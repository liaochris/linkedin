Jill Westfall's Summary

Bylined Contributor to Money magazine for 15 years, Jill has been interviewed by HGTV's The Front Door, Glamour magazine, CNN.com, Home Improvement Magazine, BobVila.com, E The Environmental Magazine (cover story) and Creators News Service. She was also a columnist for The Atlanta Business Chronicle's "Living in Atlanta" magazine in 2010.

She has worked as a ghostwriter and speechwriter for the Vice Chairman of CheckFree Corporation, the CEO of AAA, the CEO of Choicepoint (LexisNexis), the CEO of Midtown Alliance, a Lynn University dean, a New York Times best-selling author, the CEO of a Leica company, a Fortune 500, two Ernst & Young executives, and on a health/wellness book, which was translated into four languages, endorsed by Mark Victor Hansen (“Chicken Soup for the Soul”) and Usher and featured on ABC News, The Discovery Channel and CBS Radio.

As a former bylined, 15-year Money magazine contributor and longtime, bylined PEOPLE magazine contributor, she's assisted three New York Times best-selling authors on four projects (three books), five Fortune 500s, and specializes in giving business topics mass appeal.

She has reported on five covers for PEOPLE (original reporting was featured on NBC's "TODAY") and contributed two stories to PEOPLE's 30th Anniversary issue, one of which received positive comment by PEOPLE's president in The Atlanta Journal-Constitution. She has over 1,000 articles in print, written weekly for the Atlanta Journal-Constitution, reported on cover/second-cover stories for MORE and Money magazines, is an award-winning writer and has contributed to Time, The New York Times, Real Simple, Fortune, Essence, WCNN Radio, Newsday, CNN International, The New York Daily News, and Newsweek. Her original reporting has been picked up by CNN, The Discovery Channel, German television, radio stations (from Canada to South America), the BBC and talk shows nationwide. She has been featured on CNN and WSB-TV in Atlanta.
