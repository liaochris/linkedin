Bruce Brankle's Summary

Vision Integrated Graphics Group is a full service, end to end marketing solutions provider. Whether you’re looking to acquire, retain or win back customers, Vision has the resources, capabilities and experience to provide true solutions that exceed expectations and deliver an impressive return on your marketing investments.

As for myself, I have been a proud member of the Marketing and Graphic Arts Industry since 1975. I started by working in commercial print. at my family's business and 35 years and a whole bunch of technology later, I still enjoy coming to work every day to learn and teach variable data triggered marketing web to print techniques that save large organizations big money. I still do business with my first two clients from when I transitioned from manufacturing into sales and business development more than 15 years ago.

My goal is to listen carefully and truly understand the top marketing objectives of my clients, then partner with them to determine the best application of technology and reach our objectives.

Specialties

• Large, multi-channel marketing campaigns
• Fulfillment of products in a personalized and relevant manor
• Direct mail processing for lowest cost and best delivery times comparisons
• Digital printing
• Conventional sheetfed printing up to 6 colors plus coating.
• Website development to drive personalized print
• Partnering with advertising agencies, marketing departments and creatives to implement end to end, technology driven solutions such as PURLs, QR codes and email communications.
