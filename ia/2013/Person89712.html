Matt Wyburn's Summary

Footwear design service specialising in CAD, technical specification and sample production. Offering seasonal materials and forecasting packages. Designing from concept to creation, mens, womens and kids shoes.

Take a look at my site at MattWyburn.com where I blog about trends, research trips, upcoming brands and more. I'm passionate footwear, making my own custom deconstructed shoes, using my patterns, tools and expertise to reconstruct them by hand.

Matt Wyburn's Projects

  • Konichiwa Shibuya! 2013 Research Trip - Japan

    • November 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Matt Wyburn

    Japan is a fantastic hub for fashion in clothing and footwear. What makes this place breed such unique designers? Home to many amazing forward thinking brands such as catwalk Yohji Yamamoto, Junya Watanabe and on the street, A Bathing Ape, Onitsuka and Cause. But a mere snip’it of the fashion capital that Tokyo has become. I was very fortunate and able to travel there for a research trip.

  • The Cuba Project – 2013 colour & materials forecast

    • May 2012 to June 2012
    Team Members: Matt Wyburn

    During this period of 50's and 60's inspired fashion, I decided to go to Cuba for inspiration on materials and colours for 2013. It may seem an odd choice on the surface but there is no other place in the world that is as rich in culture and heritage and embodies this period of time like modern day Havana, making it a sure fire winner for forecasting packages.
