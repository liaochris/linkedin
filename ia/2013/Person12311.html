Basilio Briceño's Projects

  • DevEnv

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Basilio Briceño

    The intention of this project is to provide a complete development pipeline for programmers and help them to develop and test their applications in environments closer to the real production than their standalone local environments. It's currently based on Vagrant and Puppet.

  • SimpleCFD

    • November 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Basilio Briceño

    Mexican government (SAT) asked people to use electronically signed XML files to remove the use of paper fiscal bills. In their web page (http://sat.gob.mx) they put the rules to sign, request and provide this "new" electronic bills. This is a PHP class that intends to help people to accomplish with this requirements requested by the Mexican government.

  • Tlalokes framework

    • July 2004 to Present
    Team Members: Basilio Briceño

    Tlalokes is a modern, very well structured, MVC based, fast and easy to use PHP framework that helps developers to write professional web applications. Its focus is high availability and is being currently rewritten as binary extension.

  • AspectO

    • July 2005 to Present
    Team Members: Basilio Briceño

    Is a free (LGPL) toolkit written in PHP5 to bring support to Aspects Oriented Programming. Provides a very simple and easy to learn syntaxis. Doesn't requires binary extensions aditionals to a common PHP5 installation. Obtains the content of classes using PCRE and the PHP5's Reflection methods. Weaves preprocesing source code.
