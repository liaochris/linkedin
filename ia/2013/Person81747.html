Lisa Zamosky's Summary

Author of a new consumer healthcare book -- Healthcare, Insurance and You: The Savvy Consumer's Guide -- which explains the Affordable Care Act and how to navigate the healthcare system.

Journalist with more than 10 years experience specializing in health insurance, health reform, the business of health care, and the intersection of health and personal finance.

Expertise in helping consumers understand how to navigate the health care system, pay for health care, and make sense of health reform's impact.

Current work:
* Consumer healthcare columnist for the Los Angeles Times
* Health Reform Expert and blogger for WebMD
* Correspondent for two news services of the California Healthcare Foundation -- California Healthline and iHealthBeat

Work has also appeared on Reuters, MSNBC.com, Health.com, Kaiser Health News, in the Chicago Tribune, Baltimore Sun, NBC.com, among other publications.

Specialties: Consumer and business healthcare journalism, health insurance, health reform, healthcare costs
