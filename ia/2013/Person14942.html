Brooke Cambridge's Summary

Seen on three episodes of A&E's "Flipping Boston!"

Women Owned and Operated

Nominated for Businesswoman of the Year 2013 by Salem Chamber of Commerce

BLC Painting was established in 2006 by North Shore resident Brooke Cambridge. We are a women-owned team that serves Eastern Massachusetts, the North Shore and the greater Boston area with 14 years of experience in residential and commercial painting and home improvement projects. Whether you are looking to update one wall, one room, an entire house inside or out, or a garage, office, restaurant or showroom, we will meet and exceed your needs!
