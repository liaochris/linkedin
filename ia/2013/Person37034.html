Dorothy Madden's Summary

Since 1997 I have been a professional organizer in Rochester, NY and the founder and owner of ORGANIZE IT! I work with people who want to get organized, rid their lives of clutter, enjoy their surroundings, and gain more time for the people and activities that matter to them.

My clients are people who are ready to change. They know that their methods for organizing their time, paperwork, belongings, or workload are not working. I appreciate that change is difficult, challenging, and time-consuming. However, when clients work side-by-side with me, change becomes welcome and very manageable.

Being a former teacher, my passion lies in teaching people of all ages how to dramatically improve their lives by being better organized.

In 2004 I was the recipient of two honors. The U.S. Small Business Administration's Buffalo District, an area covering 14 counties, awarded me “Home-Based Business Advocate of the Year.” Also in 2004 the Rochester Chapter of the National Association of Women Business Owners recognized me as "Inspiring Businesswoman of the Year.”

Specialties

If you are ready to change, I will help you
- be more efficient and effective at work and at home
- reduce stress and achieve balance in your life
- find what you need when you need it
- enjoy the space where you work, live, and play
- feel, look, and know you are organized!
