Jon Barilone's Summary

I am a detail-oriented social media specialist with a strong background in content writing and editing. I started my professional career as a freelance writer and editor for niche newspapers and magazines. I then transitioned into the business world, leveraging my content background to gain experience in Marketing Communications and Public Relations while learning more about the growing field of social media marketing.

While I don't consider myself to be a "techie" in the sense that I HAVE to buy the latest and greatest gadgets, I strive to be an early adopter of up-and-coming social media platforms. I have several years' experience of heavy, daily social media usage for Digital Marketing and Reputation Management purposes.

As a social media advocate, I believe in sharing and spreading information rather than hoarding it to myself. Friends and coworkers know they can always approach me for social media and marketing advice. I've received many warm comments on the level of detail I provide, as well as how helpful my social media insights are.

I look forward to building upon my solid technical knowledge base as a social media specialist, and consistently expanding my strategic responsibilities in the future.

Specialties: Copy writing, copy editing (AP Style), online community management, social media marketing, content production and curation, webinar production, online virtual event production, search engine optimization, search engine marketing, social networking, reputation management.
