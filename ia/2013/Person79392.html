LBH Immigration Law Services' Summary

A LBH Immigration Law Services (registrada na Inglaterra como Londonbyheart LTD – No: 4930554) é uma das principais agências especializadas em vistos, cidadanias e intercâmbio no Reino Unido. A empresa foi criada em 2001 por Murilo Souto e Carina Laidens, que até hoje, além de diretores, são os principais consultores da LBH.



LBH Immigration Law Services (registered in England as Londonbyheart LTD – No: 4930554) is a London based company specializing in UK and European visas, citizenship and exchange programs. It was created in 2001 by Murilo Souto and Carina Laidens who are the directors and main consultants at LBH.

LBH Immigration Law Services' Experience

Who were are

LBH Immigration Law Services

August 2001 – Present (12 years 2 months) London Bridge

Initially, the company offered assistance in finding courses and accommodation for students. Today, LBH seeks to provide the most cost-effective plan for students with the various accredited learning institutions to which LBH are directly linked. And in order to deal with all of our clients' needs, the directors of LBH became specialized in immigration law, offering professional and qualified assistance with visa and citizenship applications.

Since 2007, our company has been authorized by the Office of the Immigration Services Commissioner (OISC) to provide immigration advice, full support and representation to our clients within or outside the UK. We can offer assistance with numerous types of visa, including Entry Clearance, EEA Family Residence Card, Student visa, Fiancé (e) visa, Marriage in the UK, Unmarried/civil partners, British citizenship, among others. See our services menu to check the full list.

Also recently our consultants became Paralegals (Member of the Institute of Paralegals). By paralegal we mean advising and assisting with the law in the same way that a solicitor advises and assists clients.

In summary, LBH offers the perfect combination of services for those that want to come and visit or legally stay in the UK.

Contact us by telephone, Skype or email. It would be a pleasure to provide you with all the necessary information so that you can make the most of your time in London. We are here to offer you all the security and assistance that you require.
