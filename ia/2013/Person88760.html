Matt Taylor's Summary

I am a freelance copywriter and travel writer. I write about destinations across New England, the United States, and the world focusing on people and places located off the beaten path. My travels have taken me from the booth at a local diner frequented by JD Salinger to a room at Camden Yards in Baltimore where an employee has rubbed dirt on baseballs for more than 30 years. My clients include the Boston Globe, the Boston Globe Magazine, and AAA Horizons Magazine. My travels can also be seen on my blog at www.mattytsmind.blogspot.com.

I also write copy for corporations across a variety of mediums including print, web, email, and interactive media. My clients include TJX Corporation, Blue Cross Blue Shield of Massachusetts, Kronos, AliMed inc., and Grand Circle Corporation. If your company is looking for a writer to craft compelling copy that engages your audiences and markets your product across multiple mediums and verticals, please contact me at www.matthewgtaylor.com

I also work as a Caregiver Support Worker for the non profit organization LUK Inc. I work with caregivers to help their adolescents replace using drugs, alcohol, and other substances with positive activities.
