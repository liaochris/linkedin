Ben Seidel's Summary

Ben has possessed a passion for technology since a very young age. He was always enamored with the newest gadget and software packages. At age 11 Ben began learning about website design and constructed his own website. By age 13, he had designed and published his first website for a corporate client. As Ben grew both in skill and experience, he found a recurring theme that small businesses' needs were not being met with affordable technology and marketing solutions.

In response to that opportunity, Ben founded Igniting Business LLC to meet his clients' growing needs. Igniting Business, located in Columbia, Missouri, is dedicated to helping businesses become more effective and efficient by implementing cost-saving and revenue generating tech and marketing systems. Igniting Business offers many services to our clients in the realm of Web Solutions, Technology Services, and Marketing Consulting. By choosing Igniting Business, you will gain access to the latest and greatest technologies combined with a knowledgeable staff at an affordable price. Ultimately, we want to see your company become a success, and we hope to serve as the spark that ignites your business into a thriving company.

Specialties: Website Design, Web Management, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Marketing Consulting, Technology Consulting and Implementation, Community Development, Content Management Solutions
