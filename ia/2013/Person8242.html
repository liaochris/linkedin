Anna Zaires (Romance Novelist)'s Summary

SUMMARY FOR CLOSE LIAISONS:

In the near future, the Krinar make the rules. An ancient, highly advanced race from another galaxy, they are beautiful, powerful, and immortal – and driven by dark desires.

A student at NYU, Mia Stalis has her whole life planned out . . . until one close encounter changes everything. Drawn against her will to the dangerous alien determined to possess her, Mia finds herself thrust into a world of explosive passion and deadly intrigue. And if she is to escape his addictive embrace, she will have to make an impossible choice . . . while the fate of Earth hangs in the balance.

Anna Zaires (Romance Novelist)'s Experience

Author of the Krinar Chronicles

Mozaika Publications

2012 – Present (1 year) Amazon, B&N, iBookstore, Kobo, All Romance

I am the author of steamy paranormal/sci-fi romance series, the Krinar Chronicles. Close Liaisons was published October 6, 2012, and is widely available on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Kobo, iBookstore, and All Romance. Close Obsession is expected to be published February 2013.

Please feel free to connect with me on Goodreads (http://www.goodreads.com/author/show/6550220.Anna_Zaires), Facebook (www.facebook.com/author.annazaires), and Twitter (@annazaires). You can also visit my website: www.annazaires.com.
