Bertus Floor's Summary

Bertus started his career in IT buying and selling second hand PC's while still attending school in the mid 90's. He left high school 2 years prior to graduating and finished his studies at a technical college while working for a small company selling computer peripherals in Gauteng. A year later Bertus managed the company and over a period of 4 years helped build the company to have 20 staff and a turnover of ZAR 24 million p/a. He founded a peripheral repair agency in 1998 and in 1999 took full ownership of Digital Peripheral Services, a peripheral distribution company.

In 2002 he moved to Cape Town and started a career at WIT, an IT service and support company. He worked for the company for 5 years, helping the company grow to a successful business employing 18 staff in 2011. In 2007, while continuing to work for WIT, Bertus founded Wobble with John Wileman. Bertus ended his career at WIT in February 2012 to completely focus on Wobble, buying his partner's shares and taking full ownership.

Bertus is based in Cape Town and has experience in the IT and internet industry since 1995. He is also passionate about travel and founded a personal luxury concierge with Melanie Zimmermann in 2007. Bertus founded TravelFlyers.com in February 2012. Wobble joined the Incubeta Holdings (Pty) Ltd group of companies 1 July 2013.
