Erron Adams' Summary

I am a forex trader and owner of www.AuthenticFX.com

AuthenticFX is an informational and the philosophical account of my trading journey. The two aims I had when starting AuthenticFX were:

- to assist traders who may be able to relate to my story from their own current state
- to network with other traders

My trading history covers almost a decade beginning with the stock market and moving into forex after a few years. I wish I could claim to have been a Super Trader but the honest truth is it's been a long hard slog for me, and I believe that works in a beginning trader's interest: I've done all the hard yards, made all the mistakes and found what works consistently, if not every time.

I know how to save the beginning trader a lot of heartache and money, and I know how a failing trader can turn it all around, having done so myself. For more details view the "About Me" page on AuthenticFX.
