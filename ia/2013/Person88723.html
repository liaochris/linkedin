Matt Fouty's Summary

Hello, I’m Matt Fouty. I am a self-proclaimed design nut. I admire minimalism and structure in design. I often subscribe to the less is more approach. My attention for detail and focus on pristine execution are both inherent and learned through practice. I have a passion for my craft which extends my interest well beyond a career path and into a hobby. I enjoy keeping up on design trends and appreciate good design in all forms.

I’ve always been adept with technology which has served me well. I work fluently with the core technologies of web design, HTML and CSS. I received a degree in Web Design at Grand Rapids Community College where I was one of only six students to graduate with a 4.0 GPA out of a graduating class of approximately 1500.

I’ve been employed as a Graphic Designer for over five years. From 2007 to early 2012, I worked for Aspen Surgical. I started there as the lone Graphic Designer and evolved into the role of Senior Graphic Designer, leading a team of three. In early 2012, I left Aspen Surgical to take a part-time position with SNAP!office as a Graphic Designer & Brand Manager. For the summer of 2013, I accepted a design internship with the Global Brand Communications team at Steelcase. In the fall, I will be entering my senior year at Kendall College of Art & Design as a Graphic Design major and Digital Media minor.

Since 2010, I have been serving on the Board of Directors for the American Advertising Federation (AAF) of West Michigan. There I am involved in decisions regarding the direction and execution of AAF West Michigan’s efforts to strengthen and preserve the advertising community in West Michigan. I am currently serving as the chair of the Communicate Committee which is responsible for branding and marketing the club to AAF members.
