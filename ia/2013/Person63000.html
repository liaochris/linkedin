Jesse Waugh's Summary

Jesse Waugh’s art has been exhibited in various galleries and at the Los Angeles Museum of Contemporary Art. His video work shot in the Amazon has been featured in a National Geographic Channel documentary, and his PRISMANIA products have been highlighted in the BBC programme “Rocket Science”. His work has also been the subject of academic study at Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, and Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island.

Jesse Waugh's Publications

  • JESSE WAUGH: Portrait of an Artist and His Strivings for Pulchrism

    • Carpophage Press
    • March 15, 2012
    Authors: Jesse Waugh

    ISBN 9781621545668

    Beginning with an overview of his film and image works, and expanding into an encyclopaedic survey of his efforts in a variety of genres, "JESSE WAUGH: Portrait of an Artist and His Strivings for Pulchrism" offers a complete guide to the accomplishments of an emerging artist.

  • Frutas de Mexico

    • Carpophage Press
    • December 1, 1999
    Authors: Jesse Waugh

    "Frutas de Mexico" is a visual reference guide of many varieties of native Mexican fruits. The rich photos is contains of unusual exotic fruits qualify the book as both a substantial scientific reference, but also as an eye-pleasing coffee table catalogue.

    Compiled and produced over a six month period on site in Querétaro, Mexico, Jesse Waugh's "Frutas de Mexico" is a visually stunning treatise on the importance of Mexican fruiticulture, which has one of the greatest variety of native and introduced fruit-bearing plant species (Angiosperms) in the world. Shooting every photograph with skill and precision, Jesse managed to produce a quality guide which not only serves as a reference but also as a beautiful compendium of the joyful color and immense variety of Mexican fruits.
