Andrew Banks' Summary

Hello, I'm Andrew Banks, a systems- and software-engineering professional, specialising in real-time/embedded systems.

Now working at Frazer-Nash Research, after over 5 years at Muirhead Aerospace (now part of AMETEK Airtechnology Group).

Owner of Internet Intuitive (an internet service provider), and ADB Ltd (vehicle for my consultancy work) both of which are involved in a number of fledgling joint venture investments.

Also partner in our family business Selby Boat Centre Ltd which boasts (one of) the largest inland-waterways chandleries in the north of England.

Degree qualified, coupled with a Postgraduate Management Diploma gives me a business grounding as well as an engineering perspective.

Specialties

Software Lifecycle Process management - ISO/IEC 12207, ISO/IEC 15288, DO-178B, DO-254, MISRA etc

Real-Time, Embedded systems, using C and assembler on a variety of target processors

Website design using HTML/PHP/CSS and MySQL
