Joseph Cahill's Summary

I'm a Graphic Designer by trade, but an Art/Creative Director by title. It's not every day people can say they love what they do for a living but I can. It's my passion and it’s in my blood and it's what I bring to the table at any job I take. I bring professionalism, mixed with a little hilarity and a touch of OCD to keep everything in order. I love to learn from others as much as I teach up and coming designers the ropes on how to succeed. We are all in 1 industry for the same reason to make incredible art & make people see things a little differently.

Specialties

Proficient in Adobe™ Creative Suite 6, Web Development AS2/3.0, Wordpress, Html 5.0, Branding, Overall Design, Department management, payroll, managing freelancers, press runs, equipment purchasing, and last but not least making companies successful.
