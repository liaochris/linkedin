The $5M platform houses current and historical student-level data for all children in the LA K-12 system and delivers the business analytics and predictive sciences capabilities necessary to measure educational policy outcomes.

• Developed and executed strategies to ensure digital readiness for all LA K-12 schools in preparation for common core state standards (CCSS) curriculum implementation in the 2014/2015 school year.

• Maintained internal customer satisfaction and service levels while streamlining IT functions and reducing headcount by 40% in response to State budget constraints.
