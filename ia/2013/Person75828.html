Kim Owens' Summary

I nurture clients through the process of understanding how the changes to business as a result of digital marketing tactics, strategies and technologies is nothing to fear. That through a thoughtful, focused, and customer-centric plan they can achieve their goals the smart way. A way that will sustain them through their own company and market changes.

Over the years, as traditional marketing has evolved to encompass digital, so has my skill set and expertise, moving onward and upward in an autodidactic fashion: Copywriting > Writing for the Web and SEO > Online Branding > Social Media Strategy and Training > Inbound Digital Marketing.

That wealth of information, lessons learned along the way (like experiencing first hand the dot com & bomb during those chaotic Silicon Valley days) and put into practice, is poured into each client project, foreseeing potential opportunities and pot holes to avoid. And through the overused-term, "Big Data," metrics and analytics reporting does indeed enable the continual fine-tuning of campaigns, tracking what's working great and what can be improved upon.

Concurrently, I operate a culture website, KaffeineBuzz.com, which covers indie music and film, along with stories on creatives doing cool things around the globe. It's been exciting to see how technology has merged with music and film over the past two decades, putting more power into the hands of the creators while disrupting the status quo. Connected to this music and culture side of my business persona is a weekly show on HoxtonRadio.com, where I feature artists and happenings from both sides of the pond.

On-going pastimes include going gigs and festivals, art opening, covering film and tech conferences, spending hours in book and record shops, dabbling in a variety of recipes in the kitchen, and having a constant eye for capturing life as it goes by, either with my DSLR or a 2-second Instagram snap.
