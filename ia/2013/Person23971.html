Christian Charles Prakas is a Boca Raton resident and Broker at Prakas & Co, a commercial real estate brokerage company specializing in restaurant, nightclub and commercial properties, located in Boca Raton. Christian grew up in the real estate business and has made it his passion and career since the age of 18. Since the age of 18, Christian has brokered commercial properties, restaurants, bars, nightclubs, lounges, and various other businesses along the way.

By the age of 20, Christian had already acquired his broker’s license and is now on track to have his Property and Casualty Insurance broker’s license by mid-2012. Christian has been named a Heavy Hitter in Real Estate by South Florida Business Journal and one of the “Top 40 Under 40” by Atlantic Ave Magazine. With so many achievements at such a young age, Christian Prakas is one to look out for as his career flourishes year after year.
