Rita Hovakimian's Summary

Rita Hovakimian is a Business Prosperity Coach, Seminar Leader and Speaker since 1989. She has worked with thousands of people in the United States and internationally in discovering their unique contribution, their authenticity and their power. Rita has coached over 400 entrepreneurs and coaches, including executives in major corporations in being successful in achieving their visions. Her methodology is action-oriented and results-focused. She is an advocate of attaining all of this in an elegant and effortless manner.

Rita is distinct and effective as a coach from having managed her own successful entrepreneurial business for 19 years. She empowers people to trust themselves and their intuition, enabling them to take their leadership to new levels of effectiveness. Among Rita’s abilities are her compassionate listening skills, her extremely intuitive sensibilities, and her dedicated and unwavering stand for each client to achieve success and produce extraordinary results.

Rita holds a Bachelor's Degree from San Francisco State University. She is certified as a Professional Coach through New Ventures West. She lives in San Francisco with her husband Robert Weinberg.

Specialties: Coaching and mentoring high achieving business women to have rapid breakthroughs so they can create 6-figure plus incomes from a Spirit Driven place.
