Bill Winter's Summary

I have lived and worked in family business, owned a beverage manufacturing company, Managed subsidiaries of listed companies, lectured at tertiary level in business planning, been contracted as an interim CEO and engaged as a successful business turnaround specialist.

My reputation is based on bringing value to private companies, advisory boards and NFPs through an ability to ask the right and sometimes, the tough questions.

Privately owned companies often lack the skills and knowledge for board disciplines and governance that ensures compliance with the Corporations Act 2001. This applies for all generations around the table. A real challenge is to implement corporate level policies and processes into private business and NFPs whilst maintaining the passion and core values that made them successful in the first place.

Specialties:

Experienced external board member in private /family Companies and NFPs

Improving board governance and procedures

Facilitator, strategic planning worshops

Conference workshop facilitator

Private Business, challenging business models, implementing change
