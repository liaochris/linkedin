David Tomen's Summary

David Tomen, owner of Swift Current Marketing, is an internationally-known marketing strategist, consultant, speaker, trainer, and author.

David's mission is to help business owners and marketing directors in small and medium-size businesses achieve immediate revenue growth by making some simple but radical changes to their marketing strategy and mix.

Strategies to bring offline and online marketing together to reach your customers wherever they are. And have them take immediate and trackable action on what you have to sell.

Print, radio and TV ads that you can track, email marketing, social media, and content marketing that draw new clients to your business. Immediate followup techniques that bring existing clients back to spend more.

David's email marketing efforts saw Simpleology's email subscriber list grow from 280,000 to nearly one million in less than two years. Leading marketing efforts with business owners in mobile, recreational yachting, self-publishing, self-help, online education, and more.

David is a member of the Email Experience Council, Marine Marketers of America, Microsoft BizSpark, and Google for Agencies partner.

Swift Current Marketing provides marketing strategy, coaching, consulting, training and mentorship programs for all-size businesses, authors, professionals, and non-profits.

If you or your company need help expanding your customer base, David can help you.

Don't wait to turn around your marketing. Often the budget can be found in taking money from non-producing ads and investing it in a sound strategy that can be tracked, and you'll see a rapid increase in revenue as a result.
