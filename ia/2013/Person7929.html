Angkor World Foundation

January 2006 – Present (8 years 1 month) Siem Reap City, Cambodia

Everyone wants the best for their kids, because children are our future; a future that we hope will be better and brighter. But what about the kids who for one reason or another don’t have loving parents, don’t have warmth of homes, and do not have the privilege to be taken care of by a family and must live in a state supervised facility.
Every kid deserves normal living conditions, happiness, love and care. The better their life is, the more hope to build a brighter future for them and all of us.
What We Do
We try to help the children improving their living condition by give them place where they are welcome and where they can be as large family. They have a safe home, food, clothing, Medical attention and drugs to stabilize the children’s condition, Education, Complying with the Cambodian Government’s emphasis on children remaining in school and being supported in their studies, Vocational training, computer and English class.

To ensuring that as individual interests become apparent and expressed that we explore all alternatives to ensure that the children can fulfill their dreams to be effective and contributing members to Cambodian society. We also have a sub-purpose and that is to advocate for the rights of children and adults who have HIV, breaking down the discrimination and educating those who do not understand this virus.
Our vision
A world where every single child has the opportunity to receive a quality education. Only then will it be possible for poverty to be conquered once and for all. Only then can we improve the living standards for millions around the globe.
Our mission
We are dedicated to transforming the lives of the world’s most disadvantaged children by focusing on literacy and efficiently.
