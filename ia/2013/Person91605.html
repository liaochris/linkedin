Joseph Dowdy's Summary

Would it be too simple to say that I help people make money on the Internet?

I help my customers leverage the best tools which will help them make money... It could be a WordPress website. It could be using Google AdWords. It could be sending my book that I wrote about Internet Marketing. It could be making a YouTube video for their business. It could be helping them optimize their SEO. It just depends on what is needed.

There is no one-size-fits-all approach to making money on the Internet; I always make sure people understand that anyone who makes millions on the Internet is NOT going to do it just because they use the Internet. There is no "silver bullet" or "winning lottery number" to making money on the Internet. It's a matter of starting with what you have to offer and finding a way to reach people who want what you have. Let's talk about what you have to offer and how we can find people who want to connect with you...
