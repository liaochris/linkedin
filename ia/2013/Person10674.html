Shannon K. Byrdsong's Summary

The Byrdsong Team works closely with first time home-buyers throughout Georgia & even those buyers that may be buying their retirement home or vacation home! We are here to help you reach your real estate goals. We also work with investors & builders and we specialize in Elegant Homes sales. My team and I also assist sellers in stopping foreclosures & ruining their credit by walking them through the short sale process & negotiating offers with the bank for them. Learn about the great 1st-time homebuyer programs, including $100 down payment with HUD homes & grant programs! www.byrdsongteam.com
