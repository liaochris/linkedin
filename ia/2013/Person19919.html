Carla Hunter's Summary

Carla Hunter is the President and Founder of Career Span, Inc. a career resource firm established in 1999. Her primary focus and passion is to empower professionals for the rigors of a 21st century workforce that offers amazing opportunities for success.

Career credentials include:

A certified Global Career Development Facilitator (GCDF) instructor with over 100 professionals trained and credentialed in the state of Kentucky.

A co-author of the nationally approved GCDF curriculum "Intersection: Where Career Theory, Models and Resources Converge" (2008, 2013).

The author and developer of "Finding Your Place in the World of Work" (2011) a career interest inventory now in iPad app development (to find out more visit: www.finding-your-place.com).

A Master Career Counselor (MCC) by the National Career Development Association.

A Nationally Certified Counselor and Career Counselor (NCC, NCCC) by the National Board for Certified Counselors.

A Board Certified Career Coach (BCC) by the National Board for Certified Counselors and the Center for Credentialing and Education.

A Credentialed Career Manager (CCM) by Career Thought Leader's Institute.

The project leader of the Workforce Opportunity Project 2010 now available online at www.workforceopportunity.com

A private practice counselor and coach in career development for individuals and companies.

A workforce development consultant with WIA programs focused on increased performance of service delivery. The most rewarding work for Carla has been with the Eastern Kentucky Concentrated Employment Program. Such an innovative and inspiring organization (www.ekcep.org). Carla has been their workforce consultant since 2004.

Carla is also a career development blogger sharing trends, resources and ideas in her blog at www.mastercareercounselor.com
