Karthik Purna Subramanian's Summary

As a Principal Technical Solutions consultant, Karthik Purna Subramanian works on a team with four other engineers to provide technical guidance to team members in QuickTestProfessional (QTP) test automation and scripting projects. In this capacity, he has also developed valuable teamwork skills in addition to highly technical skills in the creation of complex test automation. Prior to this, Karthik Purna Subramanian was a product expert at the Mercury Interactive Corporation where he learned to provide excellent customer service, resolve complex technical problems, and manage a large number of customer expectations and deadlines. Some of the technical certifications he holds include the IT Service Management Foundation certificate, the Mercury (HP) Certified Product Consultant (QTP) certificate, the Mercury Certified Product Specialist certificate, and the Sun Certified Programmer certificate.

In addition to his devotion towards continuing education, Karthik Purna Subramanian has presented numerous times in a professional setting, most notably at the HP Meet the Expert Series and at the HP Software Universe conference in Las Vegas, Nevada. This type of presenting and knowledge sharing are a key part of Karthik’s passion for his work. He excels in working on a team and prides himself in his ability to work in a global environment. He also enjoys mentoring young engineers who are on the onset of their careers.

Some of the many technologies that Karthik is proficient in include coding languages like Java, C, C++, and .Net. He also has expert level knowledge in scripting, HP mercury tools, and databases like SQL and Oracle.

In his spare time, Karthik enjoys learning about he latest technologies, collecting gadgets, and practicing yoga.

Specialties

QuickTest Professional (QTP), WinRunner, Service Test, Java, .Net, Windows Systems Administration, VMWare, Web Services and Web Development.
