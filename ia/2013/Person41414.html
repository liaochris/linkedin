Emma Prew's Summary

Graphic design graduate (2012) from University College Falmouth with a passion for typography, editorial design and design for wayfinding.

Currently working as a Junior Designer at emc design. Also, a bit of an unofficial graphic designer for Cornwall Film Festival and FXU (Falmouth & Exeter Students' Union) and official graphic designer for the band Peter Parker's Rock n Roll Club.

Aside from design; a keen sea swimmer, admirer of the natural world and live music fan.
