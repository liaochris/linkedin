Deb Reiser's Summary

Deb Reiser is a Milwaukee-based user experience designer and information architect with over a decade of web industry experience. She helps clients design impactful and easy to use websites, applications and social experiences.

Her project experience has made her adept at working with a variety of team structures including marketing, creative, interactive, industrial design, software and mechanical engineering groups. She brings to projects a well-rounded and respected voice on user experience and information architecture issues. Core to her work is attention to detail, reliability, and ability to grasp really fuzzy concepts and fly with them.

Deb is a strong communicator and comfortable leading or being an active member of a cross-functional or cross-discipline team to create successful solutions on time and within budget. She’s flexible in her work approach having worked in both Agile and more traditional waterfall methodologies.

Prior to starting her independent practice, Deb worked with top-tier companies and some of the most recognizable brands including Harley-Davidson, GE Healthcare, Kohler, Manpower, Sony and Visa. Her project work includes consumer product and corporate websites, e-commerce and transactional websites, rich internet applications (RIAs), extranet and intranets, and user interfaces for consumer products and medical equipment.

Deb lives and plays in Milwaukee, WI and is available for local or remote work.

Specialties: Core competencies include user experience definition and design, information architecture, mobile and responsive sites and applications, user research, persona development, competitive assessments, content strategy definition, heuristic evaluations, usability evaluations, taxonomy development and project coordination.
