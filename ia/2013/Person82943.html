Luann Adams' Summary

You may be seeking professional counseling for personal concerns, such as depressed mood, anxiety, anger, or complicated grieving.

Or perhaps you are struggling with a difficult relationship – marriage, family, dating, or parenting, for example. It could be that you’re simply seeking enrichment of an already good relationship, considering brief solution-focused life coaching, or in need of premarital preparation counseling.

Whatever your situation, I am available for a free initial phone consultation. We can discuss your options, consider your questions, and address your concerns. With the help of our comprehensive questionnaire, we can prepare for the next step in your journey.

Please feel free to ask any questions you may have. I’m looking forward to meeting you.
