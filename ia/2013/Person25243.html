Chad LaFarge's Summary

I'm a Missouri-licensed Broker/Salesperson and REALTOR® in the Columbia Board of REALTORS®. I also guide newscasters down the path to incorporating Google Hangouts in their broadcasts, host On Air Hangouts and provide Production and Technical Director services for Hangout on Air events. I'm a professional Web Developer with over nineteen years experience building data-driven transactional web-based applications. I founded VSpotNow, LLC, providing SMS and Voice Ad Spot services to sellers-of-things, and have self-published two books in the "What You Need NOW" theme: "What You Need NOW for Search Engine Optimization" and "What You Need NOW to Prepare Your Home for Sale". Proud member of the NRA, and a Google Top Contributor for Hangouts.

Specialties: Google Plus Hangout Production, REALTOR®, Broker/Salesperson, Web/Application Development, Database Design/Development, Self Published Author, (D)HTML, AJAX, ASP, CSS, JavaScript, VBScript, Visual Basic, SQL, Photoshop
