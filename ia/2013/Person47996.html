George Rosenberg's Summary

I advise owners and senior management of public relations and integrated communications firms on how to grow and develop their firms and run their business more effectively.

I am fortunate to work some of the fastest growing and best-known small to mid-size firms in the country, helping them develop a vision and strategic plan for their agencies and then working with them on leadership, strategic planning and operational issues, as well as coaching key members of the firm in leadership and management development.

I also coach executives and entrepreneurs outside of the agency business, many of whom are in the communications field, to achieve greater success in their careers, help them launch their business or assist them in managing career transitions.

My experience incudes 17 years with Burson-Marsteller, seven years as President and CEO of Cohn & Wolfe, two years as an executive recruiter, partner in two small firms and also owner of my own PR firm.

Specialties

Visioning and strategy development; strategic planning, organization structure, operations for PR agencies and integrated communications firms; executive coaching for entrepreneurs
