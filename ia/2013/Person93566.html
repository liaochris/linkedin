Michelle Quillin's Experience

Social Media Marketing Professional

New England Multimedia

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

November 2009 – Present (4 years 1 month)

Michelle runs New England Multimedia with Scott Quillin, and serves the company as their social media marketing representative. She uses social media, blogging, content writing, relationship-building, and creativity to drive traffic to New England Multimedia, and teaches others how to do the same for their own brands.

Since 1999, New England Multimedia has been creating strategic marketing assets for the internet, specializing in website development, audio and video marketing, and now targeted social media communications and relationship-building. We work directly with Entrepreneurs, Start-Ups, and Small- to Medium-Sized Businesses to even the marketing playing field with creative strategies to reach target audiences.

This is the new era of marketing, and it's unlike anything you've seen yet. Get on board!
