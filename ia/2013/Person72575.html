Kas Sobey-Knabb's Summary

I’ve always believed in the power of the mind. The time old adage of “change your thinking, change your life” is so true. But, how do we get the mind to believe that? Beliefs, words, memories and so much more, can and do affect and create our entire life and experiences! The mind and body are inseparable. A person has to want to change, and do it. Put the energy into it and we’ll find the connection needed.
I have been a student of mind/body/spirit work since the middle 1980's. I really began to be very conscious about my education with Silva Mind Control in the early 1990’s with Burt Goldman, now known as the American Monk. I learned much about really stretching the mind through explorative and innovative imaging.
I’m now a Board Certified Hypnotist through the National Guild of Hypnotists and guiding every day people towards their inner wisdom, their inner harvest. I am also a Master NLP practitioner and have completed training in several other parallel modalities/techniques. I help guide and support people to get in touch with the various parts of their self. To choose to adapt new behavioral changes, overcome issues/fears and enhance areas that seem to have dried up (or haven’t been turned on yet). For me, it is all about creating wholeness through understanding our inner aspects and to bring balance where it is needed.
When I was led to this gentle but powerful process called SoulCollage®, I felt I had once again, found a transformative, profound piece of creative connection. This is more art with an intuitive collage process for Self-Exploration and Discovery and Fun!
