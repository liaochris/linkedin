David Hayman's Summary

@ SUPERSONIC, David will be focusing his attention on broadening music supervision services for exciting long-form branded transmedia projects, new media ventures and branded entertainment productions like the recently completed Cross-Canada Coca-Cola Beat Bus Tour, The CIBC Run For The Cure Concert with Walk Off The Earth and The Nestea Recruit Program.

David has been a music supervisor for ten years and has created soundtracks for many major feature films (Bang Bang Club, Cairo Time, The Whistleblower, Young People Fucking…), video games (Shaun White Skateboarding, Tech Dech Live…) worldwide broadcast television series and a plethora of major advertising campaigns (Coca-Cola, Dove, Mazda, Nissan, Hershey's, Blackberry, Ford, Budweiser, Telus, Bell….).

David is currently the music supervisor for the hit dramatic series Rookie Blue (ABC/Global), Less Than Kind (HBO, Mohawk Girls (APTN) and Saving Hope (NBC/CTV). David has big plans for Supersonic Creative Inc as the young company forges many great relationships with the best studios in Canada, the US and France and with the company’s army of Jr Music Supervisors.
