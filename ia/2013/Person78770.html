Laura Brehmer's Summary

I have been a perfectionist my entire life, from my intricately illustrated stories in elementary school to my 3.8 Sociology major GPA in college to my meticulously organized Pinterest boards today. I have passionate and varied interests, the strongest among them being blogging, social media, travel, wine and food (especially cheese), and event planning and styling.

I consider myself “Bicoastal,” as I spent my childhood in New England, grew up in Calfornia, and attended college in Madison, New Jersey. I am passionate about fabulous life experiences, from food and wine to travel and simply making the most of every day.

I enjoy blogging, wine tasting, and spending time with my husband, Tim, and our two dogs. I have traveled extensively in the US, Canada, Europe, and South America.
