BARTON AGENCY & BACKGROUNDS PLUS's Summary

The Barton Agency was first Licensed in Vermont in 1974 as an investigative and security business. Several changes have taken place over the years and the business now provides a full range of services to the Human Resources divisions of companies, large and small through its subsidiary Backgrounds Plus as well as carrying on the investigative services.

Peter Barton, owner's working career included the Vermont State Police where he moved from trooper to Investigator for the Attorney General before leaving to become a Security Officer at a major bank, Investigator for the Bennington States Attorney and Chief of Police of Bennington. At the Barton Agency Peter works directly with clients in the need of specialized investigative and security services and directs other employees in their professional service to clients.

As professional background screeners Barton Agency and Backgrounds Plus provides our clients with information they can use to make good hiring decisions or conduct internal investigations to retain employees and dismiss those who do not meet company standards. We have the ability to search all states and countries for criminal information on these applicants.

Barton Agency & Backgrounds Plus are licensed, bonded and insured in Vermont, New Hampshire, Massachusetts and Florida.

Our staff is primarily made up of prior law enforcement personnel and experienced private investigators.
