Leigh Edwards, BA, CPC's Summary

A. Leigh Edwards, a Certified Life Coach and Holistic Living Consultant, is Founder and CEO (Chief Energy Officer) of DivineGoddessCoaching.com and GoddessGreen.net.

As a Life Coach and Energy Practitioner, she works with spiritually-conscious, heart-centered women to help them create prosperity (defined as something uniquely different for each individual) mentally first; how to use invisible ideas to produce visible, tangible results – from the inside out - Mind|Body|Spirit.

As a practitioner of holistic health and discerning earth-lover, her focus is on environmental medicine: that which is concerned with the effect of toxins in the human body and environment (air, food, water, cosmetics, cleaning products, etc.)

Through GoddessGreen.net, she works with conscious clients to holistically align them with their individual health intentions by sharing her experience and knowledge of eco-friendly solutions, products, and services that make going natural and green easy and effortless.

The DivineGoddessCoaching.com blog provides a wide range of thought-provoking knowledge, strategies, tools, and actionable techniques, as it pertains to Spiritual, Metaphysical and Personal Growth; all intended to help women recognize and align with their true intentions and most heart-felt desires.
