Ilka Flood's Summary

Born and raised in Germany I came to the US in 1980 when I married my husband who was in the Army. When my husband left the service we retired at the last place we were stationed at the time which happened to be beautiful Colorado. We have 3
wonderful sons and one very spoiled boxer.

I am one of those creative types and have a degree in Advanced Computer Graphic Design. I enjoy 'doodling' on the computer just as much as with 'ordinary' pencils. My creativity was influenced by my grandfather on my mother's side, who was a great artist. Other than that I love spending time with my family, traveling and the finer things of life.

More than 10 years ago when my husband lost his job from one day to the next without warning I took a closer look at network marketing. After opening my closed mind, I could finally see some of the unique benefits the network marketing industry offers people who want more out of their lives. I found not only are there great tax advantages in having a home business, but it also offers ordinary people the chance to earn extraordinary incomes. I love the freedom this business gives me. I never liked waking up to an alarm clock.

I've discovered the secrets to making money using social media and enjoy sharing it with my friends.

I am ready to help if you are ready to work to achieve your dreams.

Specialties

I am very driven, self-motivated, caring and compassionate. If you have the desire to achieve your dreams I am right beside you to make sure it will happen.
