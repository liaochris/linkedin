Kent Harvey's Summary

For almost twenty years Kent has been in the film industry and he works a Director of Photography and Camera Operator. He has circled the globe filming on the highest mountains, in extreme conditions, and on high-profile commercials, docs, television shows and feature films.

Kent's work encompasses varied imagery usually filmed under intense situations, settings, and with the most gifted people in the industry. Kent’s experience climbing and filming on 6 of the famed “7 Summits” including Mt. Everest twice, Mount Vinson, Aconcagua, Elburs, Denali, Kilimanjaro and his experience filming in extreme conditions as well as in exotic locations like Antarctica, Asia, and South Amercica has given Kent a broad worldview with a global, human perspective. He is able to bring a unique skill set to any production whether it be in a remote location or on a Hollywood stage by being adaptive, open-minded, thoroughly prepared and unflappable behind the lens.

Specializing in premier action and lifestyle cinematography, he has shot commercials for Cabela's, Intel, Ford, American Express, Eddie Bauer/First Ascent, Victory motorcycles, Arctic Cat, Ski Doo and many others. In addition, Kent works on major Hollywood feature films including most recently, Captain America 2: The Winter Soldier, Lone Survivor, The Bourne Legacy, and Gangster Squad.

Kent is member of IA Local 600 & the distinguished Society of Camera Operators (SOC) and is an Emmy-award winning cinematographer. His talent and broad skill set allow him to be a very sought-after, professional and well-respected DP/OP in the business.

Kent is a long time climber, mountaineer, skier, kayaker, & mountain biker and is able to capitalize on these skill sets when he is behind the lens shooting action.

Specialties: Director of Photogrpahy & Camera Operator shooting commercials, feature films, documentaries and branded content. 20+ years climbing & mountaineering, skiing, whitewater kayaking, & mountain biking experience.
