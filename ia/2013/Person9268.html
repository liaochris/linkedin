April Eight's Summary

April Eight (aka April Combs) writes and performs her stories full of songs for family audiences nationwide. Her touching and hilarious one-woman show, "The Butterfly Adventurer - A Story Full of Song" features a golden butterfly, ice-skating fairies, chanting goblins, a pixie peddler, one fearsome dragon and a heroic gnome named Thom.

April Eight's successful Songs for Peace Music + Arts Camp brings kids 6-11 together for songwriting, yoga and creative art projects. Our joyful group spend a happy week writing and recording our “Songs for Peace” Camp CD. Kids gain skills in creative writing and thinking beyond the cliche while exploring narrative storytelling through a variety of song forms. They also get pretty great at creative collaboration and taking turns in leadership roles. And all in the name of community building fun!

Singer, Songwriter, Storyteller

April Eight is the fairy child of musician April Combs. One evening while April was all snuggled up with her dear little daughter, a band of pixies came marching right across the pages of the book they were reading. April wondered what this band might sound like and, of course, it came right to her! She could hear their silly, wild and sweet songs in her head. Why the very moment April put pen to paper and hand to strings, new songs came pouring out.
