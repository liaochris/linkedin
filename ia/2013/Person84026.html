Madelaine Hatch's Summary

From the day she first entered a TV newsroom with her broadcaster father at the age of 6, Madelaine Hatch has been a news junkie. Today she puts that passion to work for clients who are looking for a better way to tell their story.

As the principal of Granville Communications, Madelaine provides public relations and marketing services to public and private sector clients. Whether it’s a video, website, advertising or media relations, clients depend on Madelaine’s creativity, enthusiasm and results-oriented approach.

When a client wants media profile, Madelaine designs and executes local, national and international campaigns that deliver front page coverage. When a client wants increased visibility with Canadian business leaders, Madelaine develops creative approaches that incorporate speaking engagements and YouTube videos. When a non-profit client undergoes a complete rebranding, they look to Madelaine to lead the process.

With over 30 years of diverse business experience, Madelaine is known as a steadfast, reliable and dedicated professional who is at her creative best in a fast-paced work environment. Both a team player and a leader, she motivates and empowers others to perform at their very best.

A passionate traveller who loves adventure, Madelaine’s favourite life experiences include skydiving with her son, hiking the West Coast Trail and living in Australia for a year. She brings this same spirit to her work with clients – always looking for new and better ways of achieving results through communications.
