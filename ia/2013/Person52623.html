Jeff Miller's Summary

My name is Jeff Miller and I design things. I’m the owner and conceptual artist behind HellothisisJeff Design. I’m also a Christ follower, devoted husband, creative craftsman, image architect, organizational pro, and visual storyteller. Over the past decade, I’ve worked for two top-notch Indianapolis design agencies, one world-renowned museum, and at one of the fastest-growing churches in the nation. My past work has helped inform, excite, and engage audiences while providing clients with a cohesive and branded voice.

I love creating images that awe and inspire. I always look for ways to add value to a project and have found that a holistic approach is usually the most effective. I’ll be part of the creative process from beginning to end, using my experience in everything from initial brainstorming to coordinating final printing to ensure the project is a win.

Most importantly, I value the relationship I have with every client. For me, success is when you come away at the end of the project even more excited about what we’ve created than you were at the beginning. Along the way, I’ll do my best to deliver an enjoyable and hassle-free experience as we partner together to accomplish your goals.
