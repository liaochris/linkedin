Jenny Sill-Holeman's Summary

My personal and professional objectives are essentially the same: to continue to grow in body, mind, and spirit and strive to live up to my full potential, and to help others do the same. I accomplish this primarily through the use of Reiki, guided imagery and meditation, intuitive guidance, and ritual.

My approach to my healing work? I do my best to provide to my clients what I would like to find myself!

My specialties: Hypnotherapy and guided imagery; Reiki; intuitive counseling; spiritual direction; rites of passage and healing rituals. I teach Reiki - all levels - and I also teach Intuitive Development.
