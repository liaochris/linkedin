Adol Esquivel, MD, PhD's Summary

As Assistant Vice President for Performance Measurement Systems and Comparative Effectiveness Research I am responsible for:

- Overseeing the selection, management, integration, and coordination of all St. Luke's Episcopal Health System (SLEHS) clinical performance measurement systems.
- Overseeing the development and implementation of the SLEHS data governance program to ensure data standardization, integrity and re-usability across applications, and data availability via the enterprise data warehouse.
- Ensuring the use of adequate analytic methodologies integral to the development and transmission of internal and external clinical quality performance reports and the creation of hospital quality and safety trending systems.
- Managing the design, deployment and promotion of the use of standardized business intelligence tools across the organization to support clinical and operational activities.
- System wide clinical abstraction services, clinical outcomes databases/registries, and quality/statistical analysis of priority patient populations.
- Ensuring the transition from current manual clinical abstraction to automated capturing and reporting processes using available health information technology across SLEHS.
- Leveraging the use of SLEHS data warehousing infrastructure and business intelligence tools to develop and build a comparative-effectiveness research agenda through collaborations with other researchers.

Specialties

Meaningful Use of the Electronic Health Record (EHR) and Quality Measures.
Health Systems Analysis
Implementing and evaluating Health Information Systems.
Planning, implementing and evaluating Tele Health applications.
Designing qualitative and quantitative interventions to improve the delivery of care to patients using information technology.
