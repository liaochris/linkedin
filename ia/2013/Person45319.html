John Whaley's Summary

With an educational background in Sociology (and a focus on Nonverbal Communication and Social Interaction), John Whaley is the type of innovator that puts users at center stage. His perspective on design is one of empathy and diversity, tying humanities to industrial design.

John has designed everything from fashion accessories to consumer electronics, and industrial products to lighting fixtures. His skillset encompasses the entire design process from ethnographic research to concept development and packaging design. His portfolio includes design work for Samsung, Qualcomm, Peerless Lighting, Foxconn, Fluidmaster, Trend Micro, Zuvo Water, Milkshake Labs and others.

John's latest creation, Instacube, was a smashing success on Kickstarter. The real-time photo streaming device ships globally in March 2013.
