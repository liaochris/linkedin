Kashif Zaman's Summary

My recipe for digital success is simple: fearless creativity, flawless execution and relentless optimization based on actionable insights, all nurtured in a culture of perpetual change.

During my 15 years in digital marketing for clients like Sears, Whirlpool, PBS Kids, Craftsman, Kenmore, Lean Cuisine and Stouffer's among others, I have built teams and lived life-cycles of many retail sites, marketing campaigns, social platforms, mobile / web apps. I’ve played as the creative director, technical architect, web analyst, marketing strategist and project manager—sometimes all at the same time. Today, I lead the digital team of über achievers at ARS.

Specialties:

DIGITAL MARKETING

- Full-funnel omni-channel strategy for brands and retail
- Blue ocean strategy—tools and methods

WEB ANALYTICS

DIGITAL ARCHITECTURE, DESIGN + DEVELOPMENT
