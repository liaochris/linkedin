Kristi Fowler's Summary

Kristi Fowler is a certified Life Coach who helps people move past their blocks and fears to create an intentional and inspired life – “Create the Life You Love”! One-on-one coaching, teleseminars and live workshops. Guest speaker for business, service, and civic groups [or others that fit better]. What she brings to her coaching clients, worldwide audiences, and readers:

- More than 20 years’ counseling experience to hundreds of individuals and families, guiding them through life challenges to lives of productivity and peace.

- ‘Walking the talk’ – Kristi’s own story of having triumphed over two major tragedies in her life touches the heart and spirit of all who hear it. And they say, ‘I can do it too!’

- Experience in speaking to thousands across the country on issues of life challenges – often delicate topics that most people shy away from, but that Kristi addresses with candor, wit and compassion.

- Practical methods, tools and techniques that work - to help people move past their fears, stay motivated, (fill in others?), supported by her expert guidance and attentiveness.

- Amazon Hot New Release and best-selling author of “How Could This Happen? Finding your way when tragedy strikes.”

- Recognized expert featured regularly on TV, radio and print, including CBS and Women’s Radio Network.

- Certified Life coach, also holding dual Master degrees in marriage and family therapy and theology.

Her message: "You CAN create the life you love! You’re smart and capable, but you know that something’s blocking you from living the life you were meant to have. I can help you to identify those blocks, fears, and doubts, and learn to move past them to new personal power and possibilities."
