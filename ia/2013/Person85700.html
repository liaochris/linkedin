Maria Ross' Summary

Storytelling is an art, but Maria Ross has it down to a science.

From dominating the kindergarten classroom to delighting theatre patrons to directing brand strategies, I’ve been entertaining & educating audiences from the moment I could walk. And talk. And write.

Prior to creating Red Slice, I crafted branding and marketing strategies for Silicon Valley start-ups, global software firms, Internet companies, entertainment powerhouses and consumer businesses — including Business Objects (an SAP company), Discovery Networks and Monster.com — and created communication and training strategies for Fortune 1000 clients at Accenture.

These days, I ferret out the features that make businesses completely irresistible…and then translate those qualities into strategies that stick...for clients around the world.

Brand amplification is my vocation of choice, but I’m also a speaker, actress, wine columnist and author. My book, Branding Basics for Small Business has been called "a roadmap leading small businesses in the right direction" by MarketingSherpa. And my newest book, Rebooting My Brain, tells the story of my brain aneurysm and near-miraculous recovery — with tongue-in-cheek humor & levity (yes, really).

Ready for a torrent of credentials? Brace yourself — here they come: I’ve been featured on MSNBC, NPR, ABC News and in Entrepreneur, Seattle Business, and Columbus CEO. I’m a frequent guest writer for Microsoft Small Business UK, Sharp Skirts, Entrepreneur.com, AMEX Open Forum, CRAVE. I’ve offered practical and punchy insight on talk radio shows and delighted live audiences at the New York Times Small Business Summit, Puget Sound Business Journal, Chamber of Commerce, Small Business Administration, Ladies Who Launch, CRAVE, Women Business Owners and Savor the Success.

And among other philanthropic efforts, I’m a brain injury awareness advocate and I let my dog-fanatic freak-flag fly, with pro bono branding support to the Seattle Animal Shelter Foundation.

Red Slice is...
…a digital elixir of stories, advice & strategies to ignite your brand & delight your mind.
…a marketing & branding consultancy, where unforgettable stories become irresistible brands.
…a provocative sandbox for entrepreneurs who crave real business-building juice, with an inspiration chaser.

Specializing in solopreneurs, start-ups and small to midsized growth businesses, I work with businesses to craft an irresistible brand strategy, My clients attract new customers, penetrate new markets, increase sales and grow their businesses to the next level.

I conduct branding sessions called The SLICE in which clients gain a strong brand strategy and plan, a clearly articulated message, copywritten brand elements (like About copy, taglines or bios) and a step-by-step roadmap for how to bring that brand to life visually, verbally and experientially.

My mission is simple: To engage, inform and delight your audience and keep them coming back for more.

I am also a business and inspirational speaker and a published author with two books:
