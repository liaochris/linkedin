Glenn Curry's Summary

I've worked in various roles in the industry for 15 years. Most recently I completed FX on my good friend Gia Milani's debut indie feature "All the Wrong Reasons".

Other past projects include "The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey" & "Adventures of Tintin" at Weta Digital, "Alice in Wonderland" at Cafe FX and "Watchmen" at MPC.

Specialties: FX TD | Generalist, Layout, Character Animation, Cloth/Hair, Storyboards, Post-secondary Educator
