Eric Feitel's Summary

Seasoned advertising professional with over 13 years of experience. Extensive experience in the digital space as an Art Director/ Creative Director and digital strategist for various advertising agencies. My past clients and pitch victories include American Airlines, The North Face, Nationwide Insurance, Bausch + Lomb, Clairol, Texas Tourism, Supermedia, Wii, Eukanuba, Prilosec, ESPN, Hasbro, NFL Network, USA Figure Skating, Verizon, Leap Frog, Sport Chalet and many others.

I provide simple solutions to difficult problems.

Specialties

My background is balanced between working with global and regional brands in long-term, high-level, strategic relationships and conceptualizing high impact, quick turn, integrated campaigns and user interfaces. In addition, I am experienced in managing and mentoring large teams, setting up infrastructure, pitching and winning new business.
