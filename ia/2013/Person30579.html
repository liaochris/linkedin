Daren Mehl, CISSP, CCSFP's Experience

IT Risk Management Consultant Principal

Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Minnesota

Nonprofit; 1001-5000 employees; Insurance industry

December 2011 – Present (1 year 6 months) Eagan, MN

I support Blue Cross security goals by providing critically needed security consulting services based on over 15 years in IT and security. My current focus is integrating the HITRUST Common Security Framework (CSF) into our culture and IT processes. I consult in the secure software development life cycle (SDLC) and Integrated Delivery Methodology (IDM) processes. In 2012, while on the GRC team, my focus was building our identified risk management process and providing a depth of knowledge for securely engaging new vendors and providing critical security consulting to ensure compliance with corporate policy and regulations.
