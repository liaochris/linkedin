Mike Domanico's Summary

I founded Atlantic Maintenance in 1988, to provide dependable, self-performing facilities maintenance services to retail chains and commercial facilities on the East Coast, from New York to Miami. We self-perform contract, project, and emergency services, 24/7, including: construction, renovation, lighting maintenance, carpet and tile installation, flooring maintenance, plumbing, door and lock services, fire code corrections, signage installation, signage maintenance, parking lot maintenance, landscaping, painting, window and other emergency services. Discover why some of America’s best run national and regional retail chains and commercial operations count on Atlantic for all their facility maintenance services.

For a quote or review of your needs, please call or email today.

Specialties: Self-performing complete retail and commercial services including construction, renovation, lighting maintenance, carpet and tile installation, flooring maintenance, plumbing, door and lock services, fire code corrections, signage installation, signage maintenance, parking lot maintenance, landscaping, painting, window and other emergency services.
