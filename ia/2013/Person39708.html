Ed Rigsbee, CAE, CSP's Summary

Specialties

Ed Rigsbee is an internationally recognized expert on strategic marketing alliance development and implementation. As a sub-specialty, Ed works with not-profit associations and societies helping them to develop member recruitment and retention campaigns. He frequently serves as a strategic guide for non-profit board of director meetings and strategic planning retreats. Ed Rigsbee is the ROI Guy.

Ed Rigsbee, CAE, CSP's Experience

President

Rigsbee Enterprises, Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

November 1981 – Present (31 years 11 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

Rigsbee Enterprises, Inc. & Rigsbee Research assist organizations to access their strategic ROI advantage through collaboration. This is done through direct long-term consulting, multi-day workshops, and keynote presentations. Since 2001, Rigsbee Research has conducted active qualitative research projects to help membership organizations determine the yearly sustainable real-dollar value (ROI) of membership and is a CAE Approved Provider. Additionally, Rigsbee Enterprises develops and promotes business mastery events for professional speakers, consultants, and solo practitioners.

Keynote Speaker

Developing Strategic Alliances

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

January 1991 – Present (22 years 9 months)

After joining the National Speakers Association as a professional member in 1988, Ed launched his full-time professional speaking career in 1991. He currently delivers keynote presentations globally on topics of collaboration; "Business Growth through Alliance", "The ROI of alliance Development", and "The Human Side of Alliance Success".

His secondary topic is the ROI of Membership where he instructs association executives how to prove membership in their organization is a good business decision...through proving the real-dollar value (ROI) of membership.
