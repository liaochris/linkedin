White Team Systems Administrator

Central Illinois High School Cyber Defense Competition

January 2012 – April 2012 (4 months) Normal, IL

This is the Central Illinois High School Cyber Defense Competition's first year and I was hired on to be the systems designer and administrator. My duties were to create each teams network with various servers and desktops, each exposed to various security vulnerabilities and exploits for the high school students to fix while being attacked by our Red Team hackers. The network environment was created and hosted in VMware vSphere. During competition my duties were to make sure that the system was running stable for each team, and to reset machines to their state at competition start, at the request of individual teams. The competition went smoothly and was very highly received.
