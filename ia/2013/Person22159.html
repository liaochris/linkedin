Charles A. Kush III's Summary

Charles Kush is an inspirational leader and decision maker with extensive experience building and growing businesses, maximizing profitability, reducing costs, and streamlining company operations.

Charles is the Vice President of Information Technology, E-Commerce for The Children’s Place, where he leads e-commerce technology for the largest pure-play children’s specialty apparel retailer in North America.

As the COO of eFashionSolutions, Charles provided overall strategic, administrative, and operational leadership to the foremost supplier of end-to-end e-commerce solutions serving the fashion and apparel industry.

As Founder, President and CEO of Creative Products, Charles built a B2C online mass merchandiser and directed all aspects of marketing, sales, and operations for the company.

At Beechwood Data Systems, Charles served as Program Director for the Northeast Region, where he led teams of professionals in designing and deploying telecommunications software solutions. Beechwood was successfully acquired by Capgemini during Charles' tenure.

Before Beechwood/Capgemini, Charles served in numerous management roles within AT&T. He established stringent standards and practices for software development, project management, and systems management to streamline business practices and realize significant cost reductions.

Prior to AT&T, Charles served as a Manufacturing Project Engineer for ITT Electronic Systems, where he directed the conversion of advanced military product designs into production, ensuring outstanding production quality to keep the US Military safe in combat. He received two separate Presidential Citations for his work.

Charles is also an independent executive consultant covering the e-commerce, technology, media, and telecommunications markets, where he is regularly sought after for his expert-level strategy consulting. In his spare time, he enjoys fitness activities, travel, investing, and researching the latest technologies.
