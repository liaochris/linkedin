William [Biff] Kennedy's Summary

More than twenty-five years radio promotion and marketing experience. The first half of my industry career employed by CBS/SONY/Epic Records, and the past 18 years serving as President of Charterhouse Music Group, a Philadelphia based independent AAA/Progressive radio promotion, artist and label consultation, and artist management company.

Extensive background in marketing recorded music, and recording artists to the radio industry and retail consumers. Proficient at soliciting media exposure, and working with creative people in both broadcasting and the recording arts. Adept at writing proposals, estimating project needs and documenting efforts analysis. Highly effective at negotiation and problem resolution with considerable time spent selling song concepts and artistic visions. Building events around tour appearances and media support, developing individual cities as keystones to record and concert sales markets.

For the past three years I have taught several classes at Drexel University, specifically Recording Industry operations, and development of radio marketing, ethics and mechanics classes as Independent study groups, and the University’s only Artist Representation class each semester.

My goals would include more teaching opportunities, consulting with artists who need organization, direction and development, and representing the best bands in the music business.
