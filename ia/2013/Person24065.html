Christina Bourque's Summary

I’m a graphic designer specializing in layout, print, logo and branding design. Recently I relocated from Florida to California in search of a career in Graphic Design since I just completed my M.F.A in Media Design from Full Sail University. I spent 8 years working as an engineer for several construction companies, and I believe this has given me a unique perspective on design. Working in the design field has taught me to be quick, but also thorough and to not sacrifice the quality for speed. I’ve been successful at multi-tasking and working with several clients at a time under very strict deadlines. My passion is advertising and magazine layout and design work, but I’m not about to let other opportunities go by if they can better me as a designer.



Specialties: Print Layout, Print Design, Magazine Layout and Design, Branding and Identity, Social Networking, Copy Writing
