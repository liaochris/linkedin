Carri Bugbee's Summary

I’ve been managing, strategizing and writing for advertising, public relations and marketing projects/campaigns for over 22 years. I embraced and evangelized for social media marketing long before most people thought social networks were viable marketing channels. Now I develop strategic plans to integrate social media with other marketing activities, create and curate content, advise on tools/platforms and best practices, plan operations and compliance, train personnel (including executives), forecast future opportunities and more.

I've been exploring the intersection between TV and social media since 2008 when I started tweeting anonymously as characters from Mad Men, most notably @PeggyOlson. That garnered me a Shorty Award in the advertising category for being an outstanding content producer on Twitter. I currently write about social TV for Social Media Monthly Magazine and curate related content via Twitter at @socialTVtrends.

I speak about social media and social TV at conferences, which have included The 2nd Screen Summit, O’Reilly Twitter Bootcamp, Cool Twitter Conference (keynote), OnHollywood, OMMA Global, SXSWi, 140conf, NordicGame, AllFacebook Expo, Social Media Masters, WebVisions and more.

I was a contributing author for "The Big Book of Social Media Case Studies, Stories, Perspectives" and I taught social media marketing at Portland State University for 2.5 years.

I've been featured or quoted in the Wall Street Journal, BusinessWeek, The New York Times, AMCtv.com, CNET, Clickz, The Daily Beast and other publications. Links to those stories and related video interviews are available at CarriBugbee.com

Specialties

Strategic marketing campaigns using social media engagement, PR 2.0, advertising, community management, and search marketing for businesses and organizations. Category work includes entertainment, hi-tech, film/TV production, creative services, education, health/fitness, retail, travel/hospitality and packaged goods. I also partner with other marketing and creative consultants or firms to provide social media support services for them or their clients.
