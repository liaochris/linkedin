Michael Feigin's Summary

The Law Firm of Michael J. Feigin, Esq., located just outside New York City in the state of New Jersey, handles all aspects of Intellectual Property - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights.


Experience with large clients, small clients, and individuals including AT&T, Lime Medical, TelTech, GeoPhysical Systems, MainGear Computers, The Display Connection...
