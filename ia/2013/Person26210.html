Colber Adamian - Thomas' Summary

Colber Adamian – Thomas has spent 30 years as a business, asset and project optimisation specialist: advising leading clients on over 60 major programmes and 140 projects, across 20 sectors in 17 countries.

Colber operates as an interim, independent and in-house consultant and has represented and partnered global organisations, leading private sector businesses across UK, Europe and EMEA and leading public sector bodies, local authorities, housing associations, government-sponsored public bodies, and not-for-profit, charities and third sector organisations in the UK.

As a professional trouble-shooter he is a highly respected problem solver and an effective change and turnaround specialist. Colber introduces methodologies such as lean and PRINCE2, delivering business strategies whilst supporting senior management teams and freeing key stakeholders to achieve their targets and interests. Delivery on time and within budget is a given, even under the most testing of circumstances.

In more straightforward circumstances Colber is called upon to provide an additional resource, focusing deliverables within preferred timescales, budgets and standards. He has established start-up and spin-off businesses, developed micro-businesses and SMEs and diversified larger businesses to secure exit strategies.

Colber’s skills and expertise have been recognised by a number of leading institutions including The Royal Academy of Engineers, LSE and ICMCI. He holds several professional fellowships. His client portfolio includes; BBC, RTE, Meteor, Shell, Texaco, T-Mobile and Orange (now EE), Worldcom, Nortel, Telefonica, O2, Nokia, Deutsche Bahn, Alcatel Lucent, Siemens, ntl, Vodafone, AEA, Motorola, Etisalat, Balfour Beatty, Swalec and Corus, London Boroughs of Barnet, Hackney, Hillingdon, Lambeth, Newham, Octavia Housing, Hackney Homes, CityWest Homes and City of Westminster, SEEDA.

He has led projects in London, Cardiff, Dublin, Madrid, Stockholm, Frankfurt, Warsaw, Gdansk, Dubai.
