Christina Martínez's Summary

My GREATEST ASSET is to anticipate and solve problems for clients before they become real.

Leadership, Communication & Human Relations expert renowned for intuitive ability to help people better understand themselves and others to maximize communication, strengthen relationships, and empower teams to success.

ABOUT ME: International Inspirational Speaker, Executive Coach, Master Trainer & Advanced Certified Human Behavior Consultant utilizing Personality Assessments

GOALS: To help my clients excel at what they are passionate about while coaching them to overcome barriers to their success.

SERVICES: Keynote speaking engagements, executive coaching, and customized learning & development programs.

CORNERSTONES OF SUCCESS: Communication, listening, and understanding others (feelings, motives, needs, wants) are the principal keys to build a thriving workforce and nurture client relationships. Most companies invest their dollars on hard skills/technical training not recognizing that interpersonal skills and service excellence are the core of all businesses & organizations.

PERSONAL LEADERSHIP is the new COMPETITIVE EDGE! My programs encompass a holistic approach to learning & talent development recognizing that human potential is infinite. Learning how to program your brain for success while understanding the psychology of a winning attitude are core components to personal development. As Brian Binnie, first civilian astronaut of Spaceship One said, "The sky is not the limit!"

PROFESSIONAL BACKGROUND: Human Resources (recruitment, learning, and talent development), Communication, Marketing, Public Relations, and Wellness.

INDUSTRY BACKGROUND: Finance, Healthcare, IT, Education, Aviation, Direct Sales, Social Services

COOL THINGS THAT FUEL ME: Training for the NYC ING Marathon and attaining a Private Pilot Certificate (in progress). The higher you fly, the greater perspective you have on your life and your company's big picture.

Specialties

Leadership & Talent Development, Communication, Service Excellence, Human Evolution/Life Mastery, Work-Life Balance & Holistic Health Education Specializing in Clinical Aromatherapy
