Chuck Manganiello, CPA's Summary

Chuck is the Founder of AuditSense which provides targeted high value training and consulting services to CPA firms with the goal of improving their overall efficiency and profitability.

Over the time Chuck has provided training and consulting services to CPA firms, he has:

 Provided training to approximately 15,000 professionals at CPA firms in the areas of Audit Effectiveness and Efficiency, Staff Training, Soft Skills, Personal Development, and IDEA data extraction audit software.

 Led initiatives to increase the productivity at his clients.

 Facilitated approximately 900 Engagement Strategy Sessions with audit teams.

 Integrated the use of IDEA software into many of his clients.

 Assisted in overall audit department strategic planning.

 Been a speaker AICPA, State Society, CPA Association and Industry Conferences



Chuck is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, the Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public Accountants and National Speakers Association. Chuck is a licensed Certified Public Accountant in Florida and lives in Tampa, FL.

Specialties

Audit Efficiency and Effectiveness, CPE and consulting services for National, Regional and Local Accounting Firms, Auditing, Risk Assessment, Core Level Audit Staff Training, Clarified Auditing Standards, Ethics Training, IDEA software training, Engagement Strategy Sessions, Meeting Facilitation, Course Development, Presentation Skills, Soft Skills, Conference Speaking, Audit Efficiency, CPA Association training, Client Service, Business Development for CPA's
