Elizabeth Maybury's Summary

Hello!

I'm a graphic designer with experience in a wide range of areas including branding, packaging, outdoor, POS and publication design. I've had the opportunity to work with a variety of clients including O2 Ireland, Telefonica and Telefonica EPSC, Kellogg's, Bord Bia, Mars, Johnson & Johnson and KBC Bank.

I particularly specialize in artisan food branding and I've been lucky enough to work with some of Ireland's finest food producers including Mella's Fudge, Hick's Pork Butchers, Crepes in the City, Corleggy Cheese, The Real Olive Company, Midleton Farmers Market, Frank Hederman and more.

I'm currently completing an MSc in Creative Digital Media in DIT and expect to graduate in December 2013. This has given me the chance to gain further experience in UI design, app development, motion graphics, web and coding.

Areas of study include:

- Javascript, jQuery, jQuery Mobile, HTML, HTML5, CSS
- Corona SDK (Lua)
- UI Design
- UX Design
- Agile Development and User Centered Design (UCD) including usability, accessibility, functionality, affordances, extensive user-testing, project requirements.
- Motion Graphics/Interactive
- Ludology and gamification

You can read my course blog at http://lizmaybury.blogspot.ie The blog also features some of my design work and previous case studies.

Please contact me if you would like to discuss any design projects.

Check out some of my work at:

http://www.behance.net/elizabethmaybury

Elizabeth Maybury's Projects
