Anthony Butler, Sr's Summary

Comfortable with working on a project from development to implementation and granted the unique God given ability to interface with various levels of organizational resources. (Fancy way of saying I understand how to talk to people in a way which respects their opinion and encourages their active participation while staying focused on my needs.)

I enjoy working in laid back or high stress environments and step up as pressure rises. I believe in knowing the big picture in order to make sure the smaller ones are focused. Can't stand procrastinators and detest negative mindsets.

Love all things warm except tea... prefer it sweet and icy. Willing to travel and comfortable in any neck of the woods as long as there is indoor plumbing and a place to hook up my WII.

Bio - E3

Founder and Executive director of the E3 Business Group, Inc. For the past 22 years has worked with hundreds of entrepreneurs and shared his E3 philosophy to thousands of people in the US and Europe. His concepts for marketing and relationship building have been used in a wide variety of businesses and helped independent reps and leaders from MLM businesses like ACN, We Care, Jeunique, Pre-Paid Legal, Ardyss, Body Bi Vi, Organo Gold and others.

His company, The Computer Spa was the first winner of the Fresh Ministries Entrepreneurship Award in 2005 and he has been honored by over 40 organizations for public speaking, advocacy, and leadership.

His most recent accomplishments include being selected by both the Republican and Democratic parties as a representative of small business to meet First Lady Michelle Obama in April of 2012 followed by being honored as one of the first civilians awarded honorary membership into the National Society of Pershing Rifles - Alumni Chapter. In February of 2013 he was named 2012 Member of the Year by the National Sales Network of Jacksonville. He currently is in the process of gaining accreditation for his So You Want To Be An Entrepreneur Course.
