Alex Hisaka's Summary

Alex Hisaka is the Online Marketing Analyst at Desk.com, part of the Salesforce.com family. Alex plans, creates, produces relevant and valuable content such as blog posts, ebooks, guides, white papers and infographics to attract, acquire and engage a clearly defined and understood target audience, with the objective of driving profitable customer action. She uses social media marketing, SEO and inbound marketing to distribute consistent and ongoing information to her audience.

Before Desk.com, Alex worked in the advertising industry as an interactive copywriter for Edelman Digital, Publicis Groupe, and Sapient Nitro.
