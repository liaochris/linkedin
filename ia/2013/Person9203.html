Antoine POIGNANT, MD's Summary

EuroHealthNet Group is a founding member of Argon Healthcare Network & DRAFTFCB Healthcare Global Alliance Partner.

Our mission is to create successful healthcare communications programs, driven by superior creativity and grounded in a permanent search for simple, persuasive insights.

We advertise for over-the-counter medications, nutritional products, prescription drugs and “healthcare services”.

We also provide products/services to educate and inform a broad audience of healthcare professionals and pharmaceutical company personnel : Medical Eucation, Nurses Education, REPs training and education.

We believe that great healthcare advertising is great advertising, pure and simple and that bringing a brand to life is possible only with a genuine and strong insight on which to build a brilliant ad idea. We put ideas first.
