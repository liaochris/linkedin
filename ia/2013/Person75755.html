Kimberly A. Herbel's Summary

I'm working on several projects right now. In addition to my day job, I am producing Butchlesque - a New Year's Eve Red Carpet Event at the Gulfport Casino & Ballroom in Gulfport, Florida. With that comes lots of opportunities to connect with the community that appreciates this forum of expression! Great energy & lots of publicity will fuel 2013 and this special event.

I also serve as a Legal Assistant at Fergeson Skipper law firm (since 2002), where I provide support to a board certified specialist in tax law.

I am President of Femmelicious Productions, LLC where I develop & stage programs which enrich and celebrate gender diversity & unify the LGBT community. I am currently seeking some strategic partners for several events in 2013. Please contact me to inquire about ways we can serve the community together.

I am also President of KAH Consulting where I focus on business and social media coaching and mentoring. I enjoy helping small businesses utilize social media strategies to grow!

I am the author & publisher of The Financial Excellence Blog, where I write about personal finance, as well as about the topic of being a successful landlord. This has also led me to write & distribute "Tenant Screening Toolkit: a Self Defense Manual for Landlords", available on Amazon.com.

I am also a member of Toastmasters International and was recently re-elected Vice President of Public Relations at my local club. I am enjoying the process of becoming comfortable speaking before an audience.

Specialties: Entertainment production, Project Management, Event Planning, Production, Community Engagement Specialist, Special Events, Fundraising, mentoring, coaching, teaching, collaboration, publicity, press releases, writing, investing and consulting.
