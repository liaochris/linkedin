Patrik Lönnström's Summary

I've recently return from Amsterdam, where I attended Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam's Information & Knowledge Management (Business Administration) Master program. Since I've returned to Stockholm I have been working on finishing my master thesis on ERP / ES implementations, or specifically on success perspectives in different phases of Enterprise System implementations. Simply put: the perceived success of an Enterprise System implementation project depends on who you ask and when you ask them.

New technology in general and digital media in particular interest me greatly. I am a keen user of social media. Innovation and entrepreneurship is key to the success in the globalized world and the digitalization of the world contributes greatly to both of these. Spreading knowledge is now easier and more convenient than ever, thanks in no small part to social media.

Since 2007 I’ve served as a server administrator for the volunteer-run SwiftIRC, a Internet Relay Chat network, where I have experienced both the rewards and pitfalls of non-profit, volunteer-run organizations and remote teams.

Patrik Lönnström's Projects

  • Research Project on New Ways of Working

    • oktober 2012 till december 2012
    Teammedlemmar: Patrik Lönnström, Elise Rydemark, Matilda Kristoffersson, Carel Coenraad, Djivarro Jordan

    During this research project, we investigated the dynamics and work-routines at a Seats2Meet.com location in The Netherlands. The project consisted of qualitative semi-structured interviews with key-informants and observations at the site for our main data collection.

    The central research question we addressed during this project was how new ways of working affects the work practices of the knowledge workers at our research location.
