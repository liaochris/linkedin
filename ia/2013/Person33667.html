Debra Purdy's Summary

Precision Screw Machine Products. Small lots or production runs, we specialize in high tolerance Aerospace and Military Parts. Steel, Copper, Brass, Stainless, Aluminum, and Plastics. Our Services include: MIL-STD-45662A Quality Assurance Program (CAGE Code: 311Y8), Prototypes, Production Machining, Multiple Operations, Reverse Engineering, Vibratory Debur, Design Troubleshooting, Consistent Quality, and On-Time Delivery.
