Jibran Jamshad's Summary

Decade long experience in Pakistan IT industry, worked in multiple capacities with several organizations. After my BCS from CBM I started as a Software Engineer at Infinilogic and then got promoted as a Database Administrator within the first year.

After getting 2 years of work experience & MBA, I left the job to pursue some more academic challenges and was shortlisted for Masters in Software Systems Engineering at Melbourne University. Soon after coming back to Pakistan I joined e-Bizsoft as a Technical Team Lead and was responsible for one of their mainstay Products, in a very short span I was able to wear multiple caps and started looking after multiple initiatives.

In 2007 I was selected as a part of Microsoft Innovation Center (MIC) team and moved to Microsoft Pakistan as MIC Technology Consultant where I was looking after the Microsoft Programs for Academia & Start-ups. After 3 years at MIC I was promoted as the MIC Manager and started managing the entire operations of MIC Pakistan. Later, along with MIC I was made responsible for Microsoft Customer Immersion Experience in Pakistan as well where I was evangelizing Technology by helping sales teams to leverage the right solutions to their customer.

Currently I am part of Enterprise & Partner Group at Microsoft Pakistan where my job is to strategize the technology implementations for large/major customers in Pakistan including Government, Military, Telecom & Higher Education.

Last but not least my association with Education Sector has been very close and I have spent a few years delivering lectures and coaching Computer Science students and have been on the board of multiple Universities and Associations.
