Dave Hendricks' Experience

Chief Operating Officer and CFO

LiveIntent, Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

December 2009 – Present (3 years 6 months) New York, NY

LiveIntent is a venture-backed startup that has built an ad platform for buying and selling email ad inventory exactly like display.

LiveIntent is technology platform, not a remnant ad network. We've built a platform to help publishers bring revenue one click closer via real time ads served on open to email newsletter recipients. We do not send email, we serve an advertisement in real time upon email open. We provide advertisers with the easiest way to reach a publisher's most engaged audience: through their email newsletters. Before LiveIntent, it was simply not possible to buy and sell email ad inventory at scale, or to leverage the enormous amount of 1st and 3rd party data to make these campaigns successful.

Among these exciting innovations, we are also building our company almost entirely in the cloud: Echosign, Evernote, Egnyte, Quickbooks Online, Salesforce.com, RackSpace, Tibco's Spotfire, Akamai, Broadview VOIP, Wordpress and a raft of other tools that didn't exist 5 or 10 years ago are helping us run lean, mean and fast. It's amazing what you can build when you combine great ideas, great people and great tools.
