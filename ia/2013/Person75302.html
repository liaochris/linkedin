Kevin Traynor's Summary

I am an experienced marketing director, digital evangelist and strategist. I am a highly dynamic, creative and entrepreneurial senior executive with a proven track record of driving innovation, change and business results through effective use of digital marketing and social media platforms.

I possess excellent communication and presentation skills; and have an infectious enthusiasm for digital marketing. I have proven ability to inspire “buy in” to digital marketing at all levels within organisations.

I am an accomplished presenter and public speaker having spoken at many industry and digital marketing best practice events.

I have more than 7 years of senior level online and digital marketing and strategy experience working both client side and in agency environments plus an additional 5 years experience in both management consulting and regulatory roles. I have proven ability to lead and deliver projects in both B2C and B2B marketing and work equally well as a leader of, or team member in cross-functional teams.

I have extensive leadership, managerial and motivational skills with an outstanding record of business development, customer acquisition and conversion using digital marketing and social media.

I also possess superior analytical skills with process and operational improvement talents. I am a resilient, innovative and resourceful results orientated professional with excellent negotiating skills.

I have experience in the following areas: digital strategy definition & planning; web strategy & online business models; social media strategy & policy design and implementation; building online communities; email marketing; search strategy and planning; blogging; website useability; SEO; PPC; social commerce and developing mobile applications as well as delivering training on all of the above areas to staff, senior management and clients.
