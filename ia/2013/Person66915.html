John Pribble III's Summary

"Because the purpose of business is to create a customer, the business enterprise has two—and only two—basic functions: marketing and innovation. Marketing and innovation produce results; all the rest are costs. Marketing is the distinguishing, unique function of the business." ~ Peter Drucker

I am a marketing and innovation strategy consultant. My skill is developing consumer and brand insights, then translating those insights into meaningful criteria for companies to position and design products, systems and communications that improve both top and bottom line performance. The results of my work help align a company efficiently along its core brand values, and are innovative, differentiating and exciting in the market. Target clients are primarily manufacturing, consumer packaged goods, technology and retail.

I have a rich hands-on background including advertising, retail, new product development, identity and visual language design, packaging and communications, public relations and Internet technology.

I also own and manage the German Village Guest House. German Village Guest House is a popular alternative to downtown Columbus hotels in an acclaimed historic neighborhood. Modern design, cleanliness and luxury details distinguish this innovative concept which was named the 'Best Hotel in Columbus' by 614 Magazine in the 2012 and 2013 reader polls.

Specialties: Design strategy, design thinking, brand planning, product development, advertising and communications, packaging, identity, marketing strategy and brand strategy

I've been on the creative end as an award-winning copywriter, managed a lot of printing and media buying, run an award-winning newspaper, taught advertising and marketing classes at undergraduate and graduate levels, managed hundreds of marketing and multidisciplinary design projects.
