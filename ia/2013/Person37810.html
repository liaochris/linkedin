Andrew Matthews' Summary

I'm a designer who is looking to re-locate his family to San Francisco. To have a new adventure in a new country, working on products and experiences that matter to everybody. I'm passionate about everything I do. I love my family, design and technology.

I'm transitioning from 13 years of agency experience. Ranging from small boutique to large international companies. I love teamwork and collaboration. Sketching first, iterating and spirited conversations. I love to make great things. I'm a stickler for pixel perfect details. Especially at 1x.

In short I love design, sneakers, Volkswagens and skate culture. The time I don't spend in front of a computer or gadget I spend with my wife Jen and my daughter Emily.

Specialties: Art Direction, Design, Code, Flash Actionscript, Illustrator, Photoshop, Motion Graphics, After Effects, Compositing, SFX.
