Christie Garcia's Summary

Christie Garcia is a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach and President of Mindful Choice Coaching. After 9 years of business experience working in the health care industry as both an Executive Recruiter and Sales Territory Manager she decided to follow her heart and start her own business in Career and Leadership Coaching.

Christie’s mission is to work with her clients to create the awareness and choice that inspires them to unleash their inner leader and start living their full potential with passion and confidence. She loves to work with individuals and organizations to uncover what is holding them back so they can start accomplishing their goals. She brings fun and modern leadership styles into the work place to create a culture that encourages open communication, improve personal connections, and create balance both professionally and personally.
