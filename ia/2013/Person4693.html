Alison Zarrella's Summary

Alison (Driscoll) Zarrella is a social media strategist with over 7 years of experience in the digital landscape, including web copywriting and social media marketing. She has advised a wide variety of clients on social media strategies, most notably in the consumer packaged goods, automotive and travel industries. She is the co-author of “The Facebook Marketing Book” (O’Reilly) and currently works as a social media manager in Las Vegas while running her own social media consultancy.

A 2006 graduate of Boston University, Alison holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Communications with a concentration in Advertising. While at BU, Alison was a member of the Gamma Phi Beta sorority and served as their Public Relations Vice President.

Alison has been credited as a Facebook expert in both radio and print interviews, and has been featured as a guest speaker at college courses at Boston University and Emerson College. A frequent conference speaker, including CES, SXSW and PubCon, Alison has also contributed to industry publications such as Mashable.

Specialties: advertising, marketing, writing, search engine optimization, SEO, web content development, copywriting, ghost-blogging, script-writing, social media, social networking, widget development, viral marketing, Twittering, Facebook, Twitter, blog, blogging, social media marketing, interactive copywriter, online, web marketing, content strategy, content marketing, Pinterest, Foursquare
