James F. Robinson's Summary

Filmmaker and visionary new media innovator with strong international experience and relationships. A proven leader in getting results and innovating new forms of media and bringing new creative services to market. Founded CinemaElecrtic Inc. and built a mobile distribution network in more than 50 countries before taking the company public.

Robinson wrote, directed and produced the independent film STILL BREATHING starring Brendan Fraser, Joanna Going, Ann Magnuson, Lou Rawls and Celeste Holm. The film was released theatrically in the US by October Films (now Focus Features) and internationally by Paramount (UK), Disney (Australia), Delon (S, Asia), Fox Latin America, Helkon (Central Europe), Arum Pd. (Spain), Cinema Service (Korea) and Ashe Continental (Africa) and others. Lakeshore International handled foreign sales duties.

STILL BREATHING premiered at the SXSW Film festival and went on to play in many other festivals, including Seattle, Chicago, Santa Barbara, Palm Springs, Nantucket and others. Robinson was nominated for the Grand Prix des Amériques at the Montreal World Film Festival. Fraser won the Best Actor Award for his role in the film at the Seattle International Film Festival.

Robinson's early work has been seen on PBS, The Disney Channel, The Discovery Network, The Family Channel and a variety of European Networks.

Robinson designs state-of-the-art for top-end corporate and non-profit client, in addition to providing communications consulting/production and executive presentation coaching.

He has also been a keynote speaker at MILIA-MIDEM (FRANCE), MIP-TV, NATPE, SXSW, MOBILE ENTERTAINMENT MARKET LONDON, INTERNET WORLD, iHOLLYWOOD MOBILE ENTERTAINMENT SUMMIT, 3G WORLD CONGRESS AND MARKETPLACE (HONG KONG), STREAMING MEDIA WORLD, iTV WORLD, STREAMING MEDIA WORLD EUROPE, CTIA WIRELESS IT & INTERNET, MOBILE COMMERCE WORLD ASIA and other key media and technology industry events.
