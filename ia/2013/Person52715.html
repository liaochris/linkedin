Henning Schaefer's Summary

Henning Schaefer is a Media Artist with Resonant Legal Media’s San Francisco office. Specializing in 3D animation and design, Henning has worked in the litigation field for more than 7 years supporting matters ranging from intellectual property to construction, insurance and medical topics.

Henning utilizes 2D and 3D animation, custom illustration and still graphics to develop a story and enhance his client’s message to the court. Through his skilled use of these graphics techniques, Henning can create custom technology tutorials from storyboard development to completed piece with voiceover and interactivity.

Prior to joining Resonant Legal Media, Henning worked as a Senior Motion Graphics Artist for Expert Visuals. As a manager, at Think Twice Inc., of small creative teams, he was responsible for finding freelancers to support the in-house team.

Henning has worked on cases involving such companies as Intel, Rambus, PG&E, Firestone, Microsoft, Panasonic, Dow Chemical, Coca Cola, Blue Cross/Blue Shield, Samsung and WalMart. Noteworthy matters include: City of Modesto, where 3D animation was used to show how toxic chemicals contaminated the ground water and Intel – where Henning developed a self-running tutorial with professional voice-over to explain the involved technologies.

Henning studied civil engineering and received his Master’s in Media Computer Science from the University of Applied Science in Berlin. The topic of his final thesis was 3D Animations for Litigation. Henning speaks fluent German and English.

Specialties

To deliver such sophisticated work product he uses a variety of graphics applications, including 3dsMax, Illustrator, Photoshop, AfterEffects, Powerpoint, Premier, Acrobat, Poser, Solidworks and TimeCoder.
