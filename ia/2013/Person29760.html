Daniel Livingston's Summary

I am a professional certified dispensing optician with complete experience in all aspects of private and retail optical work. I am skilled with full optical lab lens manufacturing including surfacing and edging, paraoptometry, and medical insurance billing, and experienced in optometric practice management. I love eyewear and enjoy designing and building perfect pieces for my customers.

I started in optical 13 years ago at LensCrafters. I had the fortune of working with several very experienced opticians and lab technicians, and from them I absorbed an extraordinary amount of information about optical design, frame fitting, and lens fabrication. I spent seven years with LensCrafters, designing eyeglasses and grinding lenses everywhere in the North Denver area from Boulder to Westminster, Broomfield, Longmont, and Thornton.

I decided I was interested in optical as a career and pursued a position in private care where all of the advanced techniques and state-of-the-art technologies in the industry would be available to me. I joined Family Vision Center in 2007 and have enjoyed running the optical and staffing it with optician apprentices I train myself.

My particular optical passions are in the "geek chic" and 40s-60s vintage eyewear trends, hand-made bespoke frames, custom magnetic sunglasses, and the most advanced digital lens technology.
