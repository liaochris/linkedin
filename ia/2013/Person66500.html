John Inhulsen's Summary

I am a Shareholder in the Grand Rapids, Michigan office of Foster Swift. My focus is within the firm's Commercial Litigation practice group, specifically in the areas of business and contract disputes, real estate litigation, construction litigation, employment litigation, and criminal law. However, I partner with many clients in providing legal solutions for a broad array of corporate and personal matters.

In the community, I currently serve as Chair of the March of Dimes Board of Directors, Vice Chair of the Broadway Grand Rapids Board of Directors, and sit on the Kent County GOP Executive Committee, MSU Alumni Club of West Michigan Board of Directors, Adrian Public Schools Foundation Board of Directors, Kuyper College Business Leadership Advisory Board, and the Grand Rapids Bar Association Finance Committee.

In the past I have served on the East Grand Rapids Schools Foundation Board of Directors, United Way Campaign Cabinet, the Helen DeVos Children's Hospital Campaign Cabinet, Van Andel Institute Junior Professionals Board, and the MSU Michigan Political Leadership Program Host Committee

Civic Recognition:
In 2013, I was selected by the Grand Rapids Business Journal as one of West Michigan's top 40 Under Forty business leaders, and by the Adrian Public Schools as a recipient of its Outstanding Alumni Award. In 2008, I received the Business Review of Western Michigan "Emerging Leader" Award.

Professional Recognition:
I have been awarded with the Michigan Super Lawyers "Rising Star" designation in both 2011 and 2012, and as a "Super Lawyer" in 2013.

Specialties: Commercial Litigation, Real Estate Litigation, Construction Litigation, Insurance Defense, Employment Litigation, and Criminal Law
