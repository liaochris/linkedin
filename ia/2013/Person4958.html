Allison Heller's Summary

Experienced in research and provision of policy advice on urban and social planning and social sustainability issues. Interested in stakeholder engagement to facilitate organisational behaviour change and the growing shift to quadruple bottom line approaches to sustainability in the corporate, government and non-government sectors.

Specialties

Sustainability-focused policy development, including in urban development, human services delivery and resource efficiency; social sustainability and sustainable urbanism (SU) theory and practice; social impact assessment; stakeholder analysis and engagement; urban/estate regeneration models; density and tenure mix; affordable housing delivery models; homelessness regulatory frameworks; application of liveability principles in development planning; land release/ disposals strategies.
