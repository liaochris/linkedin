Dennis O'Hara's Summary

Whether the economy is failing or succeeding, one need remains constant: the need for exceptional customer service. In fact, this need only grows more crucial in a down economy. Sometimes, exceptional customer service is the only differentiator of your products and services.

At American Communications Centers, an inbound call center, we understand how providing exceptional points of communication can make or break your success. We know that one-time customers can be transformed into loyal clients when extraordinary service is the norm. We’ve seen the firsthand effects of word-of-mouth (long before the emergence of the Internet), when one negative opinion can literally change the face of your business.

My sales and marketing career began at the age of 18. Since then, I’ve started 3 successful businesses and have experienced the power of these words time and again: “They don’t care how much you know until they know how much you care.”

In this world of "so what" sales people and no-human-required robodialers, American Communications Centers truly cares about helping you make a great impression on your clients.

We are not a telemarketing company, but a professional inbound call center, where customized programs and live agents offer the steady, reliable element that can most impact your success: a live, warm, well-trained human answering your calls.

My goal is to ensure we help our clients make this impact every single day, guided by my personal motto, “Leave the place better than when you arrived.”

If finding the right inbound call center is on your mind, give me a call and let’s discuss the details.
