  • The YES Foundation

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Michiel van de Weerthof, Marleen de Puydt, Klaas Burger, Marleen de Puydt

    In the current cultural climate the artist seems to have left little influence on his doings. THE YES FOUNDATION is not interested in could-have-been-should-have-been. We are searching for the YES in why artists do what they do, and making the YES tangible for others.

    Artists and institutions are struggling with their relationship to funding and public. At once the public is puzzled by the esteem of individual artists. THE YES FOUNDATION believes questions of artists and audience increase in relevance when put together.

  • Out of Control // Soundtrack

    • August 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Michiel van de Weerthof, Wietske Vogels

    Out of Control is a circus theatre play about an inventor with a childlike curiosity yet childish need of control using wordless theatrical story-telling, circus acrobatics, choreography and a goldberg machine-like mise en scene. So the show asked for a soundtrack that could both drive the story & movements, be an evocative moodboard on which the story was as well as be open and certainly not too predominant.

    The soundtrack combines the sounds of classical instruments and electro acoustic glitch with everyday objects, fieldrecordings, appliances and toys used as musical instruments. (sewing machines, elastic band, water, vuvuzela's, daxophone, balloons, vacuum cleaner hose, squeaky floors, rulers, drainage pipes, beatboxing)

    Listen @ http://soundcloud.com/weerthof/sets/out-of-control/

  • Kleine liedjes zonder melodie

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Michiel van de Weerthof, Doeko Langhorst

    Dichter Doeko L. doet op Artibosch een optreden samen met performer/liedjesbouwer Michiel van de Weerthof. A match made in hell, maar wel in ónze hel. Dichter en scenarist Doeko L. maakte een serie korte gedichtjes die zó liedjes zouden kunnen zijn. Fijnzinnig en spits, zoals we van hem gewend zijn. Hij verstaat als geen ander de kunst van het weglaten: de melodie, in dit geval. Weerthof- die begin dit jaar succesvol zijn muziekproject ‘Hoi’ lanceerde – bouwt zo kwaad als hij kan een spinsel van muziek en performance om de onaffe liedjes heen. Gesmeed door twee bijzondere performers, snijdt deze cocktail scherp hout.
