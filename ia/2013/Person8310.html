Anne Jacobson, MPH, CCMEP's Summary

I am a freelance medical writer and certified continuing medical education professional (CCMEP) who specializes in CME. I work closely with medical education companies to develop needs assessments, program content, and outcomes reports in a range of therapeutic areas, particularly cardiology and oncology. I blog and tweet about my experiences at www.cmewriter.com and @cmewriter, respectively.

Specialties

CME/CE programs, including newsletters, monographs, and slide kits
Needs assessments and CME grant submissions
Medical conference coverage
