Marcelo Lewin's Summary

I’ve started my career as a web developer and have always stayed a very technical and hands on person. Through the years, I’ve held many positions in the technology industry including that of a Senior Web Developer, Project Manager, Product Manager, General Manager, Director of Interactive Media and Entrepreneur.

I'm very comfortable working both as a team lead, as a member of a team or even solo with very little direction. I'm also very comfortable interfacing with upper management, clients, developers, designers, producers, video production and other creative folks. I can easily translate business requirements into technical specifications and have a knack for organizing different ideas from various sources and converting them into a product delivered on time and on budget.

I pride myself in having a great blend of both technical, creative and business savvy. I'm as comfortable coding in front of a computer as I am putting together a project plan, setting up a social media campaign, doing a product presentation in front of a large audience, prepping for a video production or managing a large team of creative professionals on a large web development project.

One of my greatest strengths is the ability to take an idea from conception all the way to production. I've created three personal website products, all of which have been acquired by other companies. I've also worked for various companies creating and managing large IT projects and web products including Walt Disney Imagineering, NBC.com, Toyota.com and J.F. Shea.

I’m highly motivated, very technical and have great written and oral communication skills both in English and Spanish. I also love to present on various technology related topics and have presented at Cal Poly Pomona for over 12 years.

I’m passionate about creating digital products! I love the process of starting with just an idea and a blank piece of paper and ending with a product that thousands or even millions of people will end up using.
