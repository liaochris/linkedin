Keith Muth's Summary

I'm a co-founder of Kinsights Inc. (http://kinsights.com). Kinsights is the smarter and easier way for parents to get answers to all of their parenting-related questions. It's a community for parents backed by what we think is the best pediatric PHR (Personal Health Record) on the market. Kinsights (formally WeSprout) was part of the first class of Rock Health, an incubator for companies working in the digital health space.

I enjoy teaching and knowledge sharing. I've taught web design and development at the Center for Digital Imaging Arts at Boston University in Washington, D.C., and I'm currently teaching web development classes at General Assembly.

My technical leadership at Corporate Executive Board helped organize mountains of industry research. I was was a key member of the front-end development teams at Viget Labs and Army.mil. I also worked on various military contracts doing other forms of media production ranging from video editing to print design.

I graduated from James Madison University with a degree from the School of Media Arts and Design with a concentration in interactive media. In my free time I enjoy staying on top of the latest web trends and technologies through knowledge sharing and community involvement.

Interests:
- Working with teams where a variety of skills needed, and being involved in all parts of the process.
- Teaching and sharing knowledge and teaching others.
- Helping people publish their own ideas with various content management systems.
- Staying on top of the lastest technologies.
