John Audette's Summary

As an Internet marketing pioneer, helped to define the concepts of Internet marketing. Invented the tactic of search engine optimization and the phrase "Search Engine Optimization" in 1997. Founded one of the first interactive agencies, Multimedia Marketing Group (MMG) in 1995 (sold to WPP). Started one of the first online press release services, the Internet News Bureau, in the mid-1990s (sold to internet.com) and the I-Sales Discussion list, an institution that grew into Adventive, the world's largest email discussion list publisher.

Co-Founder with son Adam Audette of AudetteMedia, a boutique SEO marketing firm dealing with Fortune 500 clients that was subsequently sold to RKG, one of the nation's preeminent PPC and SEO marketing firms.

Past and current clients have made well over $1B in ROI.

Currently semi-retired and engaged in the restoration of vintage Porsche lights.

Specialties: Marketing consulting, internet marketing, company building, macro-strategy
