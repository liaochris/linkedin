Ayanna Nahmias, PMP's Summary

Certified Project Management Professional (PMP) who has been actively involved with public safety communications projects since 2004 and public safety broadband since 2007. In my role as Program Manager for the DC Public Safety Wireless Programs department, managed the technical team in writing investment justifications for the Urban Area Security Initiative (UASI), Public Safety Interoperable Communications (PSIC), and the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act (ARRA) grants successfully securing for the District $200M, $11.8M and $25M respectively.

Have managed multimillion dollar federal, local and regional grant funds from award to project close-out, and fully understand the complexities and unique dynamics associated with the implementation of multi-jurisdictional, federally-funded projects. Was actively involved with and oversaw many of the tasks associated with the Public Safety Broadband Network (PSBN) deployments in Washington, D.C. and the National Capitol Region (NCR), including the selection and oversight of contractors and vendors.

Possess extensive knowledge of Public Safety Broadband Network (PSBN) and federal grants, and have sat on the government side of the table in the deployment of these projects and possesses a deep appreciation for the unique challenges facing Statewide Interoperability Coordinators in improving their Regional Interoperable Communications Systems.
