Chad Chiniquy's Summary

Chad Chiniquy is the CEO of Search Control, an online reputation management firm. Chad was drawn to the world of Internet search when his experience in the real estate market opened his eyes to the importance of search engine “real estate.” Chad previously owned a successful mortgage company for 20+ years, until the real estate market starting dipping during the mid-2000s. Chad noticed how organic search impacted buying decisions made by people using search engines, and knew that he had to focus his attention within the budding search engine marketing industry. By networking with business partners involved in the industry, Chad was recruited by the owners of Search Control to head up the company, and become the leader in a growing industry. Currently Chad manages the company out of the San Diego, CA sales HQ, and travels nationwide to educate celebrities, businesses, politicians, and individuals how to utilize search engines for your advantage. Learn more about Chad and his company at www.chadchiniquy.com

Specialties: Search Engine Control, Search Engine Marketing, Search Engine Optimization, Reputation Management, Brand Control, Real Estate
