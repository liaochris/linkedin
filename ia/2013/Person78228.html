Landon Elmore's Summary

A seasoned designer, brand strategist, web developer, and marketer with a proven track record of innovation, quality execution and efficient project management, Landon has developed a thorough understanding of multi-channel marketing through his experiences helping clients enhance their revenues and produce a measurable ROI. He is adept at analyzing current strategies and recommending refinements to meet client goals, including increased customer acquisition, retention and response.

With roots in traditional graphic design, Landon has been a leading new media producer since establishing his studio in San Francisco in the early 1990s. Landon has the ability to transform challenging concepts into practical reality with his unique combination of talents and experience. His high level design and illustrative capabilities are complemented by deep technical knowledge, strategic marketing skills, and solid business experience.

Specialties

Team building, project and client management. Strong business acumen. Seasoned online and offline designer. Proficient in a variety of software packages and most browser-based applications. Knowledgeable in cross-platform variables and issues. Keen understanding of what it takes to see a new media project through from concept to completion. A senior level team member comfortable with strategic planning for entire campaigns or some subset of projects - ability to design, produce and deliver.

Landon Elmore's Experience

Creative Director

Landon Elmore Design and Marketing

January 1993 – Present (20 years 10 months)

Landon Elmore Design & Marketing provides integrated creative and content direction, design, technical strategy, campaign and project management, and account supervision for corporate marketing efforts. Whether serving as an in-house resource or a turnkey agency, we are a highly organized "team player" with honed management skills and a keen eye for budgets and deadlines. Our clients rely on LEDM to plan, manage and implement their online (and offline) marketing programs.

While we have a long history in traditional print media and direct marketing, our focus is on creative online marketing efforts for our clients, including websites and microsites, social networks, html email campaigns and search engine marketing.

We specialize in ongoing corporate communications planning, management and implementation for small to mid size companies, ensuring consistent branding and messaging across multiple formats. We believe in a "Phased" approach to online marketing efforts, particularly websites, rather than a "build it and leave it" mentality. By structuring our development in this way, we can start simply and act quickly, then continue to refine your marketing programs over time, at a great price.

We’ve had success operating as a “virtual in-house creative team” for our clients, who contract with us because of our ability to provide expertise in design & writing and programming. From our wide circle of talented specialists, we build a team tailored to your specific needs, for a fraction of what you are mostly likely paying now.

Landon is an active member of the Direct Marketing Association (DMA) and is involved in a number of digital media, design and direct marketing organizations around the U.S.

Creative Director

Beach List Direct

January 2005 – February 2008 (3 years 2 months)

Founded in 1994, Beach List Direct, Inc. is a leading full service direct marketing firm with extensive experience in developing multi-channel direct marketing programs.

Over a four-year period as Creative Director, Landon was responsible for designing and implementing all corporate branding and marketing initiatives, and all direct marketing agency services available to clients, including direct mail design and production, website and landing page design and development, search engine marketing, email marketing, alternative media, and campaign management and analysis.

Corporate marketing activities included: branding; website redesign, formatting and content management; managed-list datacards and management of those properties in online directories for list professionals; custom landing page design; search engine optimization, paid search; online creative review and tracking; email newsletter campaign; online advertising; print advertising; trade show booth.

Creative Director

BondRewards, Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

October 2003 – November 2004 (1 year 2 months)

Landon served as Creative Director during design and launch of several web-based initiatives for this Bay Area company. Worked closely with senior management to define, design and implement online publishing and marketing programs for this web-based shopping portal, including: website re-design and production, creation of fully-functional cobranded versions of these sites for partner’ membership, site merchandising (including affiliate management), site promotion (including banner creation, jump pages, print ads, and email campaigns/promotional calendar), branding design and support, and search engine marketing and optimization studies. Also supported efforts of PR and Ad Agencies to represent their efforts on the site(s), and created all corporate print collateral including sales and marketing materials.

Art Director

Studio-V

January 1998 – January 2002 (4 years 1 month)

As a founding member and art director of Studio-V, a new media studio in San Francisco, Landon created compelling interface design and program architecture well suited to the intuitive needs of the user. Creating a consistent message across all media was the goal.

As Art and Design Director for "Peter Norton's PC Guru," the industry's first Web-ready consumer edutainment CD-ROM, released in January 1998, Landon managed a team of 15 artists, designers, and animators working with the editorial and engineering teams to create an interface that is both beautiful and effective in motivating the user to navigate through the disc content, ultimately accessing the companion website for content updates.

As Art Director and Project Manager for the design of a new retail e-commerce website for Golf and Tennis World.com [the West Coast leading retailer of pro-line golf and tennis equipment] Landon and LED guided the development of their on-line catalog containing over 20,000 products, as well as the design and production of the site interface, product photography & presentation, back-end programming and client management. The site become the preeminent destination for on-line consumers in this very competitive market, and was bought 5 years later by GolfSmith.

Art Director

Radio-V

January 1999 – January 2000 (1 year 1 month)

Landon teamed up with Michael Gosney, CEO, of Verbum, Inc., a pioneering new media producer since the mid-1980s, to create Studio-V, while at the same time beginning development on Radio-V.com. Radio-V serves as an internet commerce and culture channel for the electronic music global scene. Landon created all aspects of the interface design, and currently serves as Vice President of Design and Production.

Co-Founder/Designer

X-Farm

January 1996 – January 1998 (2 years 1 month)

X-Farm was developed as a collaborative design and production studio in San Francisco, in which Landon Elmore Design was a founding member. Members of the studio contributed to various aspects of projects, from web-design in the first days of the internet boom to 3D animation and authoring, sound design & production and print design & production.

Graphic Designer

Levi Strauss & Co

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; Apparel & Fashion industry

January 1994 – January 1996 (2 years 1 month)

Landon Elmore Design was created in conjunction with the creation of a renegade design department at LS&Co. spearheaded by Levi's Art Director Michael Allen. This group developed new ideas around a "Concept Marketing/Merchandising" initiative. This new approach to marketing involved creating entire campaigns for product lines around concepts created to depict the product in real world scenarios, and translated into all the elements a shopper would see in-store... posters, counter cards, video, product labeling & packaging, and fixtures.
