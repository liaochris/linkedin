Dennis Hendrickson's Summary

As an independent insurance agent, I represent a number of insurance companies and intermediaries that allow me to find the right products that most accurately meet the requirements of my insureds. Knowledgeable of the complexities of the insurance market and insurance law, I'm able to advise my clients about appropriate amounts and types of insurance coverage for their particular business and personal needs.

Since I'm not locked into using any one insurance provider, I'm able to seek out the most competitively priced options with broader coverage than the standard markets have available. When clients come to me for their insurance needs, I do the shopping for them.

Over time, a business' coverage can evolve and insurance carrier appetites will change. My independence allows me to stay actively involved and aware of any new programs and coverage options that may become available throughout the year.  This allows me to continually review a client's options at the time of their renewal to make sure they are aware of any new options that may have become available.

We work with those in the banking, finance, and investment community providing an additional service to strengthen relationships with their existing clients and help gain new ones. Our agency has access to all carriers serving the affluent market, allowing us to consolidate insurance programs while broadening coverages at the same time.
