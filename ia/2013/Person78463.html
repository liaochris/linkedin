Larry Lund's Summary

Real Estate Planning Group (REPG) solves real estate and urban planning problems. We work with teams during the creative process to ensure that the ideas are economically sustainable in the marketplace. We help answer the question is their sufficient market capacity and how to make the project better.

Our clients are municipalities, developers, and retailers, as well as architects and planners. We help in the conceptual stage and provide support in the public and private review process.

REPG combines the tools of primary research like focus groups, surveys, and GIS technolgies in our analysis combined with our real world experience in economic development with a goal of identifying consumer wants before they become needs.

Larry Lund is the sole proprietor of REPG and has 35+ years experience.
