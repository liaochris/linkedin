Henry Olson's Summary

Proven innovator with deep expertise across the SaaS solutions and services lifecycle. Veteran of multiple startups with leadership experience in products, technology, services and general management. Industry experience in manufacturing, distribution, financial services and healthcare. I love the energy of Silicon Valley startups and believe strongly in the innovative capability of small agile teams with a shared mission.

I’ve invested my career in meeting the challenges and opportunities of enterprise agility - creating business value through agile systems and solutions while retaining compliance, control and efficiency.
