I set up the first website in NSW, Australia (1993) and since then have worked around the globe in a wide variety of roles, principally on digital media productions. My goal is to help with the evolution of story-telling processes through interesting and successful work with talented people. I have extensive corporate experience and a track record in innovation through start-ups as an artist, entrepreneur and technologist (Mod Productions, MOD Films, thequality.com, Rack&Pin).

Specialties: Creative and technical leadership, production and delivery of complex projects, big picture vision, bridge-building, digital production, marketing, technical architecture, digital strategy and policy, troubleshooting, interactive product and service design, invention and prototyping.
