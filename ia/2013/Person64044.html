Jimmy Greco's Experience

CEO

Airgo Music Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Music industry

January 2005 – Present (8 years 10 months) New York

Airgo Music provides universal services for today’s independent recording artists. Services such as: artist development, production, physical and digital distribution, live performances, publishing, marketing and promotion are all managed within our unique system.

For over 20 years founder and CEO, Jimmy Greco had worked as a producer for the major record labels. In 2000, he noticed the music industry shift away from that which it was built on: the artists themselves. The web based, digital age of music broke the control for the majors, Greco saw this as the perfect opportunity for an independent company to compete and return the focus to the music by placing the emphasis on proper management and artist development.

In 2004 after years of planning, Jimmy Greco launched Airgo Music and has since sought to embody his vision of ‘the record label of the future.’

Airgo is an acronym for “Artists’ Independence Revolutionizes Greater Opportunity”

Airgo Music developed a streamlined, artist-centric company with a versatile and modern day approach.
We see our artist as partners who have a “hands on” contribution to their projects, and whom now can have a direct connection to their fans through their social websites. Our goal is to provide the fundamentals services every artist needs. We start with finding the best songs, then help them to define who they are as an “Artist”, We then focus on live performances, the production, publishing, promotion and marketing, as well as the importance of managing finances, to name a few. We help to guide and manage the complete independent artist system”

We have assembled a powerful management team to nurture and develop an independent artist’s set up and launch.
