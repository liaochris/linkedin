James Sullivan's Summary

My name is James Sullivan and I currently work as a mechanical engineer for Lockheed Martin Space Systems Company. I recently graduated from Drexel University's co-op program, where I accelerated my degree to obtain both a bachelors and masters degree in mechanical engineering.
