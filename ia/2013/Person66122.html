John BouAntoun's Summary

A technologist who excels at just "making it work" for the business and a passion for getting the most out of my team. I live and breathe e-commerce, both from a strategic and technical delivery perspective and enjoy seeing the uplifting impact of my team's delivery on my client's bottom line.

Experienced at strategic planning and execution as well as technical direction, I am able to use my technical, process and people management expertise to engineer an environment that fosters high-performance.

I come from a Computer Systems Engineering background, having honed my technical and architectural capabilities in my early freelance days.

Later I discovered a passion for management which started with consulting positions and project management, then blossomed more deeply into people management, settling on high-performance team leading and developing technology strategy that matters to the business.
