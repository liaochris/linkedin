Che' Rippinger's Summary

Add Social Media Marketing & Public Relations to the mix of skills on Ché’s extensive résumé. She works with her husband Bill Owen of Elevated Color consulting with various businesses on their strategy, integration & SEO.

Many know Ché from her relationship humor work, as a professional cartoonist for 20 years--published in Playgirl Magazine and many others. She also wrote and illustrated her "Dating and Hand Grenades" column for The Sunday Denver Post.

Business Owner, Instructor & Performer:
A lifetime dancer of many forms, including jazz, ballet, belly, hoop, sword & fire, Ché's Hip The Hoopla humorous hula hoop dance fitness classes initiated from community requests. Business planning and classes commenced in 2011. Ché teaches at the well known tribal belly dance studio Red Elephant Workshop, in Denver's historic "Sante Fe Arts District."

Since starting Hip The Hoopla, hundreds of (hula) hoopers--of different levels and walks of life--have enjoyed Ché's brand of hoop dance, variety-packed positive learning sessions (with Laughter Yoga), and professional performances.

Ché has the unique distinction to be known in a few circles as "The Belly Dancing Cartoonist" (not done at the same time). Now she's the "Belly Dancing Hooper." She created Hip The Hoopla with her unique style of humorous hula hoop dance fitness classes. Ché is Hoopnotica and Laughter Yoga Leader certified.

Before Hip The Hoopla, Ché helped manage the 23-year family-run southwestern gift shop El Rancho Trading Post with her mom Cam (who passed away in 2010). There, Ché was web master, marketing gal, and even produced a variety of Evergreen, Colorado made products for their store. They also ran a health food store with a deli for 6 years in Evergreen.

Ché now uses all of her expertise & business skills to incorporate in her own marketing and helping others, as well with Elevated Color. She also volunteers with several peer and professional groups helping with advice, strategy and projects.
