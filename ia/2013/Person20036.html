Carlo Romero's Summary

Carlo Romero is a licensed real estate salesperson for Douglas Elliman Real Estate in New York. Specializing in Manhattan luxury apartments, including new developments and resales, Carlo represents home buyers, investors, sellers, and landlords on all sides of residential real estate transactions. When representing sellers, he has achieved record prices. As a sales associate for several new development condominium buildings, he works closely with developers throughout the process of planning, pricing, and marketing new projects. He is also the broker specialist for Twenty9th Park Madison, 39 East 29th Street, in the emerging NoMad neighborhood.


Before joining Douglas Elliman, Carlo worked with Elika Associates as an exclusive buyer's agent, focusing on home buyers and investors purchasing real estate in New York.

Prior to working in real estate, Carlo worked in fashion as a model and photographer, representing clients including Diesel, theory, Versace, Marc Jacobs, DSquared2, Maison Martin Margiela, and Viktor & Rolf.

He started his career in finance as an Analyst in Sales & Trading at J.P. Morgan and a Research Analyst in Mergers & Acquisitions at Houlihan Lokey.  

Carlo holds a Master's in Public Policy from the University of Southern California, and a Bachelor of Arts in Letters from the University of Oklahoma.

Originally from San Diego, California, Carlo has lived in Manhattan since 2008.
