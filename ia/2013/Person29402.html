Daniel Bester's Summary

Daniel Bester is an ITEC-qualified Swedish massage therapist; he has a VTCT qualification in Sports massage and is also qualified to practice Shiatsu massage and Indian head massage.

Daniel has trained at the ISIS beauty academy and is qualified to offer hair removal by threading and facials.

Daniel works mainly from his home, which is situated between Woking and West Byfleet in Surrey.

He offers his treatments at his home in a peaceful, private road which backs onto the Basingstoke canal.

As well as working office hours,Daniel is available during the evenings and at weekends.

Specialties

Daniel offers depilatory services: that's male waxing (and threading) to you!

He also performs Intimate Male Waxing Services: BS&C etc.

For more details visit www.danielwaxing.co.uk
