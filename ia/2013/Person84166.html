Maggie Kennedy Braff's Projects

  • CPMC Wishes for Wellness

    Team Members: Maggie Kennedy Braff

    We were tasked with refreshing the format of California Pacific Medical Center's popular award ceremony honoring outstanding doctors. After carefully reviewing all the areas that worked and didn't work of the previous years' ceremonies, we landed on the idea of creating three branded larger-than-life videos on stage, and incorporating the insight of grateful patients and hard-working honorees. The featured patients were thrilled to stand on stage to hand the awards to the doctors being recognized.

  • Oakland Museum of California's Golden Gala

    Team Members: Maggie Kennedy Braff

    We assisted in planning an elegant, tented fundraising event to help the Oakland Museum of California raise additional money to benefit the museum's programs and exhibits. The Golden Gala featured a lobster dinner, a live auction, which included a ride on one of the giant cranes at Port of Oakland. Attendees enjoyed themselves while donating nearly $200,000 in less than 30 minutes to support this beloved institution.

  • The Great Baby Romp

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Maggie Kennedy Braff

    While we don't often have events where the majority of attendees are under 30" tall, we were so proud to have supported The Great Baby Romp, an event where parents came together to experience some of San Francisco’s best child enrichment programs (in one convenient space!).
