Jeff Deibel's Summary

Before joining Jam Productions in late 2005, Jeff spent his time traveling the Midwest region as a brand ambassador for Verizon Wireless utilizing experiential marketing techniques to build strong, lasting relationships between brand and consumer. Leaving the nomad life behind, he took a position with Jam Productions to handle all forms of online communication and promotions. Over the course of the past six years he has cultivated an engaged, concert-going subscriber base in the Chicago and Minneapolis markets, designed and developed web platforms for Miller-Coors, Visa Signature and Beerhoptacular, overseen the development of business-to-business web applications, and managed Jam Productions social media outlets. From the interstate to the information highway, Jeff brings nearly a decade of marketing experience to every project he undertakes.
