Daniel Dittenhafer's Summary

I have a passion for optimizing business operations through software automation. Having worked with computers from Commodore 64 programming BASIC in elementary school to now with Microsoft Windows 7/8, Server 2008/2012 and Google Android, I enjoy designing and developing software systems as well as managing other engineers doing the same. I bring more than 16 years of professional, full-circle software lifecycle experience with business process management software and enterprise systems. I am results-focused, a team player, and a technical leader with a background in planning, building, and launching successful software products and custom business applications.

Expertise: Software design & development with Microsoft .NET Framework v2-v4, C#/C++, SQL Server 2005-2012/Azure databases, ASP.NET, XML, HTML, W3C DOM, JavaScript, jQuery, WinForms , TFS, Git.

Past Experience with: Oracle 8i/9i/10g, Win32, SourceSafe, integration with Onset, Novus, Sutron data loggers, SCADA systems and Google Android.

Related skills include project management, team leadership, business analysis, requirements gathering, carbon data management, IT budget management, and SAP FICO/SCM implementation
