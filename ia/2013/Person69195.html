Josh Iverson's Summary

Josh Iverson is an accomplished online advertising and new media sales and marketing professional with many years of media sales experience in both management and individual sales. He has built successful advertising programs in the boomer, soccer-mom, and college market demographics. Josh started his first rep firm, ezADit, while at BYU which was sold to Y2M and is now owened by Viacom. He started iMediaSalesTeam in 2009. He was made managing partner of Reptide Media Inc. in 2010.

Josh covers the East Coast and is the founder and principal of iMediaSalesTeam, the national online ad sales team for Legacy.com Sites, Ancestry.com Sites, Thirdage, Patheos and Eons.com and other top boomer category sites. In 2011, About.com (owned by the NY Times) has also contracted iMediaSalesTeam to cover the New England/Boston Region. Also in 2011, Josh became the managing partner of Reptide Media a sister firm to iMediaSalesTeam with a focus on representing sites with college and age 18-30 users.

An expert at helping advertisers leverage new media to create unique campaigns, Josh collaborated with clients (Apple and Paramount Pictures) to craft the first ever social networking campaigns (on Facebook.com). Later on, he was the top contributor at United Online/Classmates.com (the oldest social network).

As a hobby, Josh promotes Yerba Mate a South American tea-like beverage, from Argentina/Brazil and sells to Yerba Mate fanatics, specialty tea-shops, Yoga Studios, and online at facebook.com/miyerbamate and www.miyerbamate.com

Specialties: Boomer, Online Advertising Team, Sales Management, Media Management, Vendor Negotiation, Media Sales Team Management, College Marketing and Advertising, Online Advertising Sales, Strategic Partnerships, Business Development, Revenue Yield Management, Cross Media and Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Rep, Representation, Revenue Yield Management, Facebook.
