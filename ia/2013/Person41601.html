PETER ENGLER's Summary

COMBINING MY ADVERTISING, EXECUTIVE RECRUITING AND CAREER COACHING EXPERTISE, I HELP EXECUTIVES CREATE "THEIR SPECIAL, POWERFUL STORY", BUILD AN ACTION PLAN TO REACH THE RIGHT PEOPLE AND OPPORTUNITIES WITH THIS STORY, AND TAKE A PROACTIVE APPROACH TO ACHIEVING THEIR "CAREER SUCCESS".

I ALSO WORK WITH CEO's AND MANAGEMENT TEAMS TO BUILD MUTUAL RESPECT AND DEEPER UNDERSTANDING OF ONE ANOTHER TO IMPROVE BUSINESS RESULTS.

Bucknell graduate, Navy Pilot in Vietnam, Advertising executive at major NYC and San Francisco advertising agencies (FCB, Saatchi, J Walter Thompson), senior marketing executive at Citibank, Ampex, and Interactive Network, retained search partner at LGES, senior executive coach at Right Management Consultant and DBM, San Francisco.

I created HIGH PERFORMANCE EXEC to help CEO's focus on what they do best, and get their management team better organized to handle the rest. The result: happier, more productive CEO's and Management Teams.

As founder of ENGLER CAREER GROUP, I work with individuals at all points in their careers to help them better understand themselves and their objectives, build effective Career Plans and Take Action to go after their ideal job or new career directon. I provide "The Right Strategies for Career Success".
