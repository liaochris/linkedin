Donna Schonhoff's Summary

La Donna Weddings Officiants & Ceremony Coordinating Services was established in 1986 as La Donna Wedding Expressions, when it opened as a unique wedding accessory shop in Warren…one of the first in Macomb County, located in southeastern Michigan.

Owner/operator, Donna Schonhoff, prior to opening the shop, had obtained experience in floral design and specialized in silk bridal bouquets. It was during this time that she started offering wedding invitations to her clients and is now a specialist in this area.

In 1986 Ms. Schonhoff joined the Association of Bridal Consultants, the only international trade organization for wedding professionals, and became Michigan’s first State Coordinator of the organization. She held that position for 13 years before stepping down to devote more time to her business.

Ms. Schonhoff’s experience also includes teaching bridal consulting classes through several Macomb County school systems. East Detroit Schools approached her in 1998, to create a career course on bridal consulting that could be taught through their Continuing Education Program.

On February 13, 1998, Ms. Schonhoff was ordained as a non-denominational minister by the Universal Life Church. The original purpose was to enable her to serve as a backup for ministers scheduled for weddings at the chapel. After the closing of the chapel, she continued to book Ministers and Officiants for offsite weddings.

At La Donna Weddings Officiants & Ceremony Coordinating Services, as it is now known, traditional and contemporary non-denominational religious ceremonies as well as customized civil and spiritual ceremonies are offered that include the input and personal touches of the couple being married.

Specialties: Officiating, Invitations, Wedding Accessories, Seating Charts, Wedding Coordinating, Gown Cleaning and Preservation, Jewelry.
