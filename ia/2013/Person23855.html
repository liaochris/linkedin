Christian Acuña's Experience

Founder, Creative Director & Cosmetologist

Christian Acuña International

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Cosmetics industry

January 2000 – Present (13 years 9 months) Sydney Area, Australia

Christian Acuña is a highly-trained and qualified skin care therapist and cosmetologist who after several years working with numerous international skin care brands, discovered there was a definite need for a range of skin care products that was not only specifically designed for men but also free of the harmful additives, synthetics and chemicals found in so many of the products currently available.

During the process of researching and developing his organic skin care line Christian unearthed several native Australian ingredients that contained powerful antioxidants, such as the Kakadu Plum. The Aboriginal people have long been privy to the wealth of nutrients and health benefits derived from these unique plants and berries and it was with this knowledge that Christian Acuña created a range of skin care that is both pure and organic yet still actively treats the unique needs of men’s skin.

Ongoing research and development of ingredients, formulas, and packaging ensure that Christian Acuña Organic Skin Care is the premium skin care range for men available on the market.

Owner, Skin Therapist, Cosmetologists

The Men's Grooming Salon

2000 – Present (13 years) Sydney Area, Australia

The Men’s Grooming Salon is reminiscent of a private gentleman’s club catering exclusively to fine men’s grooming. A comfortable and masculine urban retreat located on Sydney Woollahra’s Edgecliff Road, the ultimate grooming experience.

We offer personalised face rejuvenation treatments for men exclusively designed by Skin Care Therapist, Cosmetologist and Grooming Guru, Mr. Christian Acuña. His treatments are unique and cannot be found anywhere else. Doctor Jeremy Cumpston leading non-surgical cosmetic doctor in Australia complements the organic approach of Christian’s face treatments.

The Men’s Grooming Salon also offers a selection of full body massages and waxing hair removal treatments for men.

Without a shop front, many of our exclusive clients appreciate the extra degree of privacy afforded to each visit, especially those in the public eye. All treatments are uniquely tailored to each person.
