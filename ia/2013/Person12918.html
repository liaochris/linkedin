Ben Hayes' Summary

1) I create beautiful, intuitive user interfaces for web applications and websites. Contact me to see some examples.

2) Excellent integration across disciplines in your web team is essential if you want high quality production values. I work hard to ensure that my designs are not just pretty graphics but become the polished face of a real product that people can enjoy using.

3) I understand the importance of branding and of designing for emotion. A brand is so much more than a logo. If a brand already exists, I will ensure I have a thorough understanding of it. Where further work is required, I can run small branding workshops with stakeholders.

4) I believe user research and testing are vital to producing products that really work for people. Testing does not have to be expensive to yield valuable results.

5) Copywriting and labelling is central to good interface design. I’m good at it.

6) I usually code up the interfaces I design. I have 7 years experience coding HTML and CSS. I build elements using HTML5 and CSS3 that degrade gracefully on older browsers. I always give appropriate consideration to web standards and accessibility issues.

7) The best way to keep the highest quality design values right through into production is to let the designer ‘own’ the code for the UI. (hint: most back-end programmers do not understand the nuances of typography). I love working alongside back-end developers. I am in my element editing view templates in Ruby on Rails, JSP, Freemarker, PHP, WordPress, Drupal, etc.

Specialties

Web usability, interaction design, user experience design, copywriting, graphic design, HTML, CSS, Javascript, working on view templates in MVC frameworks
