Jennifer Reising's Summary

As a seasoned communications consultant, I develop and carry out distinctly tailored communications programs to help non-profit associations and companies meet their organizational needs.

My specialty is in health care. I help to educate patients and healthcare professionals about diagnostic and treatment options for serious diseases to ultimately improve the care of patients.

In addition, I communicate to Fortune 500 companies about innovative healthcare organizations who are positively changing the way how employer healthcare is delivered. My business-to-business expertise includes building new employee communications programs.

I also have an expertise in helping companies successfully communicate during a crisis.

Specialties

Strategic planning, securing national media placements, writing press releases, newsletters, articles and web content, crisis management, medical writing
