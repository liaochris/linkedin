Martin McDowell's Experience

Locum Veterinary Surgeon

Pet Doc Martin Ltd. (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Veterinary industry

January 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) England & Wales

I am an enthusiastic, diligent and organised small animal veterinary surgeon, who has been a small animal practitioner in the UK for the last 5 years. I am involved in daily routine and non-routine surgical and diagnostic procedures as well as consultations and emergency out-of-hour duties.

I have a keen interest in internal medicine, feline medicine and diagnostic imaging, especially in ultrasound. I have attended a CPDs in the areas of basic fracture repair and level 2 abdominal ultrasound scanning.

Specific skills:
Routine surgical procedures: bitch/cat spays, castrations...
Soft tissue surgical procedures: caesareans, simple and more complex lumpectomies involving implementation of skin tension reduction techniques (removal of various skin neoplasia), enucleations, removal of Meibomi gland adenomas, grid keratotomies, various trauma injury management, GDVs, general abdominal procedures involving splenectomies, cystotomies (feline/canine) with one case involving urethrotomy.
Basic orthopaedic operations: correction of a distal tibial fracture in a 7-year old cat with a intramedullary pin and medial plate, correction of a compound femoral fracture in a <1 yr old cat.
My other skills are in general management of emergency bleeds, intoxications, blocked cats, medical management of bladder neck tumours in bitches, canine and feline type 2 diabetes mellitus management, chronic and acute renal failure and many more.
