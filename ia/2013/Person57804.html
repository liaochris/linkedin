James Scanlan's Summary

One of the founders of Beartrap Games, launched in October 2012.

I am a sound designer and composer with 11 years professional experience working in sound and 6 years game industry experience.

I was the Audio Designer at Cohort Studios for 3 years working mainly on titles for PlayStation3 and PSP.

Experience working on a diverse range of projects from massive scale global events like the Beijing Olympics Opening Ceremony (asst recording engineer) to multi award winning films, games and animations (1 BAFTA win, 3 further BAFTA nominations, 12 International film awards).

Completed game projects on PS3, 360, PC, PSP, iOS, Web & Android. Highly skilled in game audio design, asset creation and composition.

In the years leading up to starting Beartrap Games I focused on my long running passion for post-production film sound & game audio, mainly through titles for Sony PS3/PSP, the BBC and films for the festival seasons.

Additional work with National Trust on building digital recreations of their 17th century harpsichords (Benton-Fletcher collection) at Fenton House, Hampstead, London.

Also published a paper for Audio Engineering Society on adaptive audio for games.

Specialties: Sound effect creation, Asset management, Highly experienced in audio design tools (FMOD, WWISE, Scream, Logic, ProTools), Audio recording and mixing, Foley artist
