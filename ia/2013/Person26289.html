Colin Deeb's Summary

Technology has been one of my passions since I was very young, and over time that hobby has become a way of earning a living while I pursue my artistic goals. I have been fortunate enough to have been trained by some of the top Apple service professionals in the country, and to have worked as a technician at the busiest and most rigorous Apple Store in the Western Hemisphere.

My goal is to provide every client with a timely and cost-effective solution that will continue to work after I've left the building. I also do my best to educate my clients and their employees about the technology they are using, in addition to explaining the causes and solutions to troubleshooting issues that come up. I prefer to empower my clients to resolve issues themselves in order to avoid repeat consultations, which saves both time and money.
