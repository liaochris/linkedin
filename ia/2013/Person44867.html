The package also includes Bloomberg Trading Solutions branding throughout the game. For example, 'power-ups,' which let users get to the top of leaderboards, are branded.   • Yes Virginia, the Musical

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jonathan Fishel, Matt MacDonald, Vanessa Scanlan, Ricky Bacon

    In 2010 Macy’s worked with JWT to reintroduce America to the story of “Yes, Virginia There is a Santa Claus” with a ground-breaking network animated special. In 2012, recognizing the decline of spending for the arts in our public schools, Macy’s, in partnership with JWT, created a stage musical of the story and made it available for free to public schools around the country.

    We were responsible for creating a website to support teachers and students in learning to put on this play and we gave them all of the materials to make them successful. The website included and interactive script, set and costume design hints, a musical score and videos from the original production team and theater luminaries. The result was more than 1,300 productions nationwide in its first year.
