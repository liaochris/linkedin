Cory Pampalone's Summary

My name is Cory Pampalone. I am an independent director and storyteller with a lifelong affinity for the powerful and persuasive medium of cinema. I graduated from the College for Creative Studies in Detroit, MI in 2001 with a BFA in film and digital media and for the past 10 years, have worked for various agencies from LA, San Francisco, Pittsburgh, NYC, Detroit, DC and Columbus. I am here to address the growing demand for honest, conceptual, high quality, visually compelling video storytelling content in a rapidly changing digital age. I am currently represented by Storytellers Anonymous, Kingsley, and We Are Vacancy

Specialties: pre visualization, creative direction, writing, branding and identity, cinema language, STORYTELLING, directing, shooting, editing, making client happy
