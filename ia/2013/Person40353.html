Eleanor Howe's Summary

I'm a PMP-certified Virtual Project Manager with a broad base of technical experience, purveyor of fine Vintage goods, public speaker, and sometime voice over talent.

Experienced in enterprise software implementation, training, and technology consulting, I've worked in the public sector, academia, healthcare, and the telecommunications fields with people at varying levels of tech savvy. I'm interested in part-time project management consulting as well as corporate training, adult learning, and applying the art of project management to the world of estate sales and vintage or antiques consignment.

I'm interested in projects and ventures in the following areas:

*Rescuing Projects gone awry

*Web-Based Educational Resources

*Renewable Energy and Power Grid Management

*Mobile application development for vintage, antique, flea market, and fair vendors

*Elder care and enrichment

Selling vintage goods is my passion, and I love rescuing obscure and wonderful oddities from the past and introducing them to a new world. If you've been searching for something in particular, are thinking of downsizing, or need that certain something interesting for your home, please don't hesitate to ask.

Specialties: SOW and RFP creation, vendor management, technology needs assessment, truth-telling, documentation, training, public speaking, event planning
