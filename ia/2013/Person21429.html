Celeste LeCompte's Experience

Freelance Writer

Celeste LeCompte

April 2011 – Present (2 years 7 months) San Francisco / Guangzhou, China

As a writer, I’m focused on providing savvy, in-depth reporting on sustainability, technology, and cleantech for both deeply entrenched practitioners and those new to the sector. I thrive on complex topics that feed my natural curiosity. My particular areas of expertise are clean energy, energy efficiency, transportation, waste management, sustainable business, carbon markets, and innovation and entrepreneurship. I also do work on social and environmental justice topics. My geographic focus is on the Western U.S. and China.

I’ve written everything from in-depth magazine features on biomimicry to Q&As with the former CIA chief and leading women VCs, from news stories and news analysis to trend stories on food carts and the “slow bike movement.” I’ve written or contributed to a variety of research reports for PricewaterhouseCoopers, GigaOM Pro, Kachan & Co, and others. My work has been published by BusinessWeek, Outside, the New York Times, the GigaOM Network, the San Francisco Chronicle, Sustainable Industries magazine, and others. I am currently editing a book forthcoming from Oxford University Press.
