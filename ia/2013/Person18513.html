Bryan Jamison's Summary

ABOUT ME
We offer financial protection and insurance to families and business owners to keep their lives in Good Hands and safe from Mayhem. Our Agency has been a part of the Georgetown Community for over a decade, and we are always excited to work with you. Allstate recognized our agency as a Premier Service Agency for our outstanding service to customers.

PRODUCTS & SERVICES
I’m proud to work with a company who’s been serving satisfied customers for over 80 years. Customers count on outstanding financial strength and superior claims service to help protect what they value most. Allstate delivers on their promise. In fact, their outstanding financial strength and superior claims service received an A+ (Superior) rating by A.M. Best. Quality service, strength and satisfaction – that’s something I’m glad to be a part of.

MEET THE TEAM
As your Allstate agent, I can help you understand how the choices you make – such as the car you drive – affect your insurance rate. I’m committed to helping you get the appropriate level of protection and make it easy to do business with Allstate.

PERSONAL PHILOSOPHY
Have fun with what you're doing.

FAVORITE ALLSTATE MEMORY
My greatest memory of Allstate is going for my interview to get my own agency on my 23rd Birthday. I was nervous for one of the few times in my life. I ended up with the opportunity to become one of the youngest Allstate Agents ever!

IF I COULD LEAVE MY COLLEAGUES WITH ONE KEY MESSAGE, WHAT WOULD IT BE?
Always take care of those who take care of you. Take care of your customers, love and support your family and friends, and they, along with life, will in turn take care of you.

WHO IS MY PERSONAL HERO?
My late grandfather. He supported me since day one, pushed me to be the best and most honest I could be in business and in life. He taught me how to be a deep down true person, how to always keep my head up, and how to manage life when times get tough.
