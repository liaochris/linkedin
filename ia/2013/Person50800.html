Guided Inspections (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Real Estate industry

July 2005 – Present (8 years 4 months)

Guided Inspections is a Round Rock based Real Estate inspection company that offers services on existing residential, new homes, condos, multi-units, pre-listing inspections and warranty inspections.

My goal is to make you as comfortable as possible with the sometimes daunting task of purchasing a new home and to help alleviate some of some associated stress. Superior customer service is a top priority; before, during and after the inspection. I strongly encourage that you be present and involved during the inspection, if possible, and ask lots of questions. You will receive your inspection report within twenty-four hours of the inspection, complete with color digital photos for added clarification. Reports can be e-mailed or downloaded from www.guidedinspections.com. After you receive your report, I will be available to review and explain the items within the report with you in a way that is easy to understand. I want to make the inspection process easier on you by clearly explaining any deficiencies found in the home rather than bogging you down with unclear technical jargon. Everything identified during the inspection will be included on the report in great detail.

When visiting www.guidedinspections.com, see what others have had to say about my services at the testimonial page. You can also view a sample inspection report in order to get an idea of what is covered in the inspection and how the information is presented. The links page is also a great place for information on your home, constuction practices and common household hazards. Check back regularly as this technical information is updated often. There is also a price schedule on the website for your convenience; however, I encourage you to call for a price quote as I periodically run special offers and discounts from time to time.

Call today for a free price quote and let Guided Inspections "help you discover your new home."
