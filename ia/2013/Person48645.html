Gilly Balmer's Summary

Gilly Balmer has been working as a Recruitment Specialist for over 12 years within the Irish market. Having successfully recruited middle and senior management staff for a large variety of organisations, Gilly established her own business in 2006 to work independently and as a Recruitment Partner to both Commercial Businesses and NPOs. Specifically Gilly specialises in the following disciplines: Accountancy, Marketing, Sales, Communications, Fundraising, Law and Office Management.

Gilly offers a unique approach to market by providing clients with a menu of Recruitment Services whereby clients can choose to pay for a single service at a fixed cost as an alternative to paying for the full recruitment process. Some of these services include – Job Spec Preparation, CV Selection, Panel Interviewing, and Reference Checking.

Gilly also offers the full traditional recruitment process as well as retained consultancy assignments for executive level recruitment. All retained assignments are project managed by Gilly who begins the process by developing a deep understanding of the philosophy, culture and strategic objectives of each client organisation taking time to understand their priorities and concerns at every stage throughout the recruitment process.

Gilly is passionate about her business and takes pride in having placed many key decision-makers in positions of significant influence. She works to minimise time, cost and risk to your business. Candidates are carefully handpicked using a variety of selection, screening and interviewing techniques, thereby recommending only the highest caliber candidates to clients.
