Services include: Strategic candidate sourcing programs including internet searches, targeted networking, building of specialized "pipelines" and utilizing social networking sites.Utilization of Applicant Tracking Systems such as Taleo, Virtual Edge, PeopleClick, BrassRing & OpenHire. Experienced (18 years) professional hiring/recruitment support designed to save companies time and money.

Specialties: Specialities include full cycle recruitment and implementation of applicant tracking systems. Experienced supporting recruitment of large corporations, small to mid sized firms,and start - ups.
