Aneta Hall's Summary

There are two reasons for B2B buyers to use social media: 1) to find answers to business problems and 2) to tap into network of peers.

In order for your B2B company to use social media effectively you must engage with customers and stakeholders by showing your expertise, solving problems and building trusted relationships.

I specialize in social media strategy & enablement programs for large B2B brands. My approach follows Forrester’s POST methodology of
- understanding your target audience social media usage,
- assessing your company’s organizational readiness before formulating achievable objectives
- developing a strategy that is based on empowering your subject matter experts to take on an active role in your brand’s social media program.

In my work at Pitney Bowes I focus on global social media governance & policies as well as administering the Pitney Bowes social media education & certification program. Lastly I continuously strategize in maintaining a global social media program for ongoing engagement and thought leadership.

Find out more about my work at http://anetahall.com
