Sharon Kaitner's Summary

Instructor of web design, coding and structure. Progressive enhancement, standards-based, responsive design. User centered design. M.Ed. in Instructional Technology with an emphasis on multimedia and computing applications. Currently teaching courses in Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Captivate, HTML5/CSS3, interaction design and computer applications.

Curriculum development, faculty development, online instructional method, executive account management, multi-million dollar project management, user-centered testing of products, and web analytics are all part of my experience, training and skills. My interests are music (in particular Polkacide), the brain and what's new in sci-fi technology. Visit my website, http://casabasa.com, for a glimpse of my life.
