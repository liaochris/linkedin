Lela Donelson's Summary

I started my career with Southwestern Bell Telephone Company. When I separated from Southwestern Bell the telecommunications industry was in the midst of explosive growth. During the next several years I worked for several high profile companies when they were in their start up phase. ROLM Texas which later became IBM-ROLM and is now part of Siemen’s was one of those companies. In 1985 I started my own telecommunications company which sold and serviced Inter Tel and Iwatsu systems. In 1990 I sold that company and worked as a Sales Manager or Senior Account Executive at several other companies. In 1997 I went to work for Nextel which was a newly emerging cellular company where I was the Training Manager in Houston. I revamped existing employee training materials and developed additional material. Training has been my passion since then! My depth and breadth of experience with start up/entreprenurial companies and major corporations gives me insight into the training needs and requirements of business at every stage of growth and of every size.

I was certified by Huthwait to train their globally renown sales training, SPIN and by Achieve Global on their highly acclaimed customer relationship modules. I am also a PSS (Professional Selling Skills) expert.

Specialties

I specialize in Google Apps, telecom and software training and training development for small to mid sized businesses, individuals and organization.
