SEO Analyst

2013 – Present (less than a year) Las Veas, NV

As an SEO analyst at Caesars Entertainment I work closely with key stakeholders in identifying and strengthening SEO practices that enable stronger alignment with ranking factors for optimal search engine traffic growth. This involves all aspects of SEO on-page and off-page optimization. Working closely with digital marketing, content, PR and social media teams, I lead and communicate SEO best practices including HTML coding best practices, meta data optimization, internal/external linking, and URL structure as well as educating key stakeholder involvement in these different areas.

Facebook and Google Marketing Consultant

Director of Internet Marketing

Legisima Fashion Group

February 2007 – February 2011 (4 years 1 month)

I was responsible for all aspects of online marketing for the Legisima Fashion Group’s family of Websites. Director of Internet Advertising

Internet Advertising Firm

January 2002 – January 2007 (5 years 1 month)

I was in charge of the development and management of the entire suite of online marketing products the company offered. These included search engine placement, website optimization, conversion consulting, pay-per-click advertising on our network as well as management of Google AdWords and Yahoo Sponsored Search, affiliate marketing, website translations, web design, online press releases, and email list management. In this capacity I was responsible for a support, technical, and research staff of over one hundred employees.

Web Project Coordinator

Lowestfare.com

March 2000 – November 2001 (1 year 9 months)

One of my main duties at Lowestfare was managing a multi-million dollar partnership with NBCi.com. The marketing aspects of this partnership included direct email, advertising placement on the NBCi Internet site, and commercials on the NBC television network. I also oversaw the development of web projects from inception through project completion including an affiliate program and an online travel community. My responsibilities included project identification, contract negotiation, developing client relationships, and management of programming projects.
