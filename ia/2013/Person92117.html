Michael J. Boccieri, PMP's Summary

Creative, collaborative personality; a dedicated team leader with 8+ years video game production experience, and over a decade developing interactive entertainment/educational products: ranging from e-learning products to classic compilations, to online multiplayer games, to handheld/mobile, to current-gen AAA console titles. Project Management Professional certified.

I am interested in leadership opportunities in video game management, leading teams from pre-production through post-mortem. My strengths include deep knowledge of the art/animation development pipeline, strong cross-discipline communication/collaboration, strong game design skills, development triage and risk mitigation strategy, mentoring, and waterfall/agile methodologies.

Specialties: GDD/TDD creation, project budgeting & scheduling; hybrid-agile production methodologies. Cross-project and cross-discipline oversight and integration. Risk assessment, mitigation and contingency planning; development triage. Rapid prototyping. Management & Media relations. Producer training & management. Publisher/vendor relations. Depth and breadth of what makes games fun.

MS Project, MS Office, TTP, JIRA. Maya, 3ds max, Zbrush, After Effects, Flash. Xbox/PS3 publishing platforms.
