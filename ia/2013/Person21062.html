Cathy Lieberman's Summary

Cathy Lieberman is the Principal at CDL Business Consulting serving nonprofits and small businesses. Her engagements focus on financial and strategic planning, development/fund raising, board development, coaching and organizational effectiveness solutions. She is a results-driven professional with a consistent, measurable record of promoting growth and maximizing operational efficiency.

Cathy Lieberman has extensive executive level non-profit and corporate experience. Her strength lies in motivating and guiding teams to implement plans supporting the organization’s mission and vision. Her clients benefit from her proven success in non-profit and for-profit management and leadership.

Cathy has facilitated retreats to develop marketing and financial plans and is a frequent speaker and presenter. Her article, Strategic Planning For Organization Success was published in the Philanthropic Journal and the American Schools Foundation Alliance Newsletter.

Specialties: Strong business background with emphasis in leadership and organizational development, problem solving, presenting ideas, influencing decisions, and improving processes to increase productivity and reduce costs. Areas of expertise include strategic planning, team building, budgeting, financial reporting, project management, process design and change management.
