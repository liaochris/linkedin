Aron Schoenfeld's Experience

CEO & Co-founder

Vlassified.com

Marketing and Advertising industry

October 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months)

Vlassified is a business that uses video as a medium for people to advertise and promote themselves, their company, goods, products and services. In today’s environment of social networks and Internet use, people want information to be short, sweet and to the point. They also want it in a medium that brings it to life so it isn’t just another post on a wall or picture on a page. People want information that tells them a lot more than can be illustrated through lines of text.

Vlassified solves all of these issues by using a platform that makes video the new means of communication. With Vlassifed, you can see between the lines. Video has never been as easy to create as it is now with webcams on every computer, small handheld video cameras such as the flip, camera phones and forward facing cameras on tablets. By leveraging these tools and making your videos max out at 15 or 30 seconds, you will create a message that is clear, concise and gives the viewer an inside view into what you have to offer.

The site will allow users to create video profiles that can be added to personal websites, email signatures or posted on your social media pages to make yourself come to life. When someone looks for you, they can get 15 seconds of the real you so that the first 10 minutes of your conversation doesn’t go to waste.

Businesses and classifieds will be able to make 30 second videos where they can explain their products, goods or services and sell it to the viewer. Maybe there is something unique about what you do or offer, you want to show off a special feature on the car you are going to sell or even showing a real view of the game from the seats your are going to sell. With pictures and words, these items may be hard to communicate – but with video, you will get your point across better than you ever have before.
