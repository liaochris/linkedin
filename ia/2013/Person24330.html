Christopher Bigelow's Summary

My services for businesses: Do you need to motivate people to understand and act? With over twenty years of full-time experience in marketing and corporate communications, I can help you create compelling content that promotes customer/client education and brand loyalty. I'm skilled at writing in many online and print formats, including brochures, catalogs, advertisements, websites, magazines, newsletters, press releases, email and direct mail, social media, flyers, and manuals. I'm also skilled at AV scripts and executive communications.

My services for individuals: If you've got a book-length story to tell or concept to teach, I can help you create your book and publish it. I've published seven books of my own, and I've helped produce more than thirty books for other authors. My book services include ghostwriting, evaluating and editing manuscripts, coordinating page layout and cover design, and handling print and eBook production, distribution, and promotion. From earliest concept to going live on Amazon, I can help you with the entire process or just with specific aspects.
