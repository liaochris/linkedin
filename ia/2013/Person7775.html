Angela Styhler's Summary

Experienced medical and science writer with expertise in journal abstract and article writing, white paper preparation, slide kit preparation, poster presentations and physician presentations. Excellent written communication skills in English and accomplished in taking scientific work and providing clear and accurate medical and scientific communications that appeal to a wide range of audiences. Excellent collaborative skills, flexibility, and ability to work well with team members across several departments. Expertise in diabetes & obesity , inflammatory diseases, immunology and infectious diseases.

Specialties

Although I have a wide range of experience in preparing medical and science communications, my expertise lies in the preparation of balanced, non-promotional scientific manuscripts and review articles as well as slide kits with speaker notes. I have expertise in searching for data in the scientific literature and preparing the data to be clearly communicated through the written word or through presentations.
