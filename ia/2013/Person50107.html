Greg Faherty's Summary

More than 15 years of professional resume writing experience, with 10,000-plus resume orders completed since 1997 while maintaining a better than 99% customer satisfaction rate. Extensive experience in technical, scientific, clinical, business, educational, and creative writing. Senior writer and consultant for several of world’s largest web-based resume firms. Meticulous, knowledgeable writer who can work in a wide variety of subject areas. Detail-oriented proofreader with strong aptitude for spotting and correcting errors. Excellent communication, customer service, and business skills. Published author in multiple fields.

Specialties: Areas of expertise include, but are not limited to, information technology, sales and marketing, executive, management, education, executive-level management, human resources, pharmaceutical and medical, education, and international business. Able to prepare high-quality resumes all fields, from administrative assistants, astronauts, and athletes to CxOs, engineers, and zookeepers. Additional services include federal resumes, international and academic CVs, and corporate biographies.
