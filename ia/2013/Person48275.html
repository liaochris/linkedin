For over 25 years, I have worked for iconic brands and ad agencies that include SAP, HBO, IKEA, Purina, Kodak, Fox School Of Business, Digitas, imc2 and Whitman-Hart, and have a deep and unique experience that helps me connect the right customer with the right message at the right time on the right channel:

SOCIAL MEDIA. I helped increase retailer Meijer’s Facebook fan base from 60K to 350K in a year and Rita’s Italian Ice's fan count by 100K in two weeks. I also created a social media campaign and lead generation platform for SAP.

SOCIAL SELLING. I led building a social selling program build that's increased the social selling IQ of thousands of B2B sales executives. KA-CHING!

CENTER OF EXCELLENCE. Always comfortable in front of a whiteboard, I have led strategy and subject matter expertise ... in the Mad Men world of digital advertising agencies like Digitas, imc2 and Whitman-Hart/Band Digital, as a professor at St. Joseph’s University and as the NA SAP Social Media Center of Excellence lead.

B2B & B2C MARKETING. I've filled consumers cereal bowls with Ralston Purina cereals, tuned viewers into simply the best television programs on HBO, helped furnish the homes of brave and (not so beige) IKEA customers and helped companies run like never before at SAP!

ENTREPRENEURSHIP. The entrepreneurial bug hit me right when I finished my Finance MBA. I started and ran a 5-person virtual advertising agency working with small businesses in North America.

COACHING: I’ve coached some of the best teams ever - a number of marketing groups at HBO, the direct marketing group at IKEA ... and some great girls tournament softball and basketball teams! I love helping and teaching others to be the best that they can be!

If I can ever help you with a business problem, speak at your conference or connect you with a solution or contact, please reach out to me!
