Brittany Fitzpatrick's Summary

I'm Brittany Fitzpatrick – “Brit” for short.

I'm Co-Founder & CEO of MentorMe. MentorMe uses technology to help youth organizations, colleges and universities run high-impact mentoring programs. Check us out at http://beta.getmentorme.com.

Before founding MentorMe, I served as the Communications Coordinator for Ronald McDonald House Charities® of Memphis, where I increased the organization's social media engagement by more than 100% and developed and executed a social media campaign that increased social media donations by more than 300% (both within less than 2 years).

I’ve been named among the Top 10 Young Professionals in Memphis by the Memphis Chamber of Commerce. I've been profiled as "Something Good in Memphis" in the Commercial Appeal and as a Memphis Standout in the Memphis Daily News.

I have a Bachelor’s degree in Public Relations from Howard University and a Master’s degree from the University of Memphis in Public Relations and Digital Media.
