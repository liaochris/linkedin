Mich Hancock's Summary

Mich Hancock is the owner of 100th Monkey Media; a social media company that specializes in developing, creating and managing a social media presence for local and nationwide businesses.

Mich is a dynamic presence on the social media scene. Her clients have enjoyed great success due to their online presence with appearances on Oprah, The Food Network, The Travel Channel, Local News and Business Shows, growth into new markets and territories, expanded demographics and partnerships.

Specialties: Social Media Strategies, Original Content Blogging, Email Newsletters, Online Marketing, Specializing in Marketing via Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, Pinterest, Instagram, Blogs and more!
