Martin Dolphin's Summary

Martin is a Boston-based management consultant with over 15 years of experience in the IT industry with strong expertise in Information Security, Project Management, IT Internal Audit support, IT Governance, Risk & Controls, and post deal integration.

Martin has deep security skills including operating systems security, VPN, perimeter defense, endpoint security, data leakage protection, Intrusion Detection & Prevention, Ethical Hacking, Public Key Infrastructures (PKI), and Wireless solutions. Expertise in assessing regulatory, security and privacy risks and developing remediation actions.

Martin has been managing projects throughout his tenure from developing the client relationship, understanding the client need, developing the proposal to providing project oversight and delivering the project.

Industry experience includes Financial, Health Care, Automotive, Government, Retail, and Manufacturing.

Martin has excellent oral and written communications skills and is the technical editor of a book and contributed to several articles. Martin has presented on numerous topics for internal and external audiences including being a lead instructor for Ernst & Young's eXtreme Hacking course.

Specialties

Specialty skills include information security and privacy, compliance, audit, Program & Project Management, Business & IT Strategy, Mergers & Acquisitions and the management of resources, technology migrations, and process enhancement.
