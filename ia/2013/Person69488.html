Joyce Brewer's Summary

After more than 10 years in front of the camera as a TV news reporter/anchor and receiving an Emmy award for covering Hurricane Katrina, I'm now a professional storyteller for national brands and locally-owned businesses.

I have two distinct companies designed to create social media conversations and captivating video content.

JoyceBrewer.TV is a media consulting company to "tell the story of your business with a media pro!"
I help growing businesses become the star of their own video.
I write, produce, shoot, conduct interviews, edit and distribute video content.

Specialties: Video Production & Marketing, YouTube channel creation/promotion/optimization, Press Releases, Wordpress, Social Media Management, Media Coaching & Preparation


MommyTalkShow.com is an online parenting talk show "Connecting Atlanta moms with businesses they'll love to TALK about!"
I create video reviews and online commercials for family-friendly products, as well as host on-location Atlanta events and interviews.

My most watched YouTube video was viewed more than 1,000 times in 2 months! It featured an interview about the benefits of chiropractic care for expecting mothers & newborns. The video has been shared on blogs and social media.

I've worked with national brands: Purex, Walmart and LearningRx and Walgreens.
Locally owned family-friendly businesses who've been featured include Bean Work Play Cafe in Decatur (co-working & play space) and HippoHopp Duluth (indoor play space.)
