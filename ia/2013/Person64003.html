Jim Mercure's Summary

Jim has 17 years of experience as a User Interface Designer, Web Designer and Photographer with several major hi-tech firms in the Seattle, Washington area. Most recently he has worked for the Haydrian Corporation as their Senior User Interface Designer developing XM3 an Anti Money Laundering Solution for banks and financial institutions.
http://www.jimmercure.com/

Prior to this he worked as a Senior User Interface Designer for Paul Allen's first e-commerce venture Mercata.com developing the "We Commerce" business model.

Jim also worked with world famous photographer Art Wolfe where he successfully completed an internship and worked as an Associate Workshop Instructor.

He has 3+ years working for Microsoft and designed their first enhanced music CD, which was prominently featured in the Windows 95 launch advertising campaign. He also worked as a on several popular CD-ROM titles such as Encyclopedia of Baseball, Basketball, Encarta and Road Map.

Click the link below for a video clip of my work on an infomercial hosted by Anthony Edwards and Bill Gates.

He went on to work with Cranium's co-founder Whit Alexander creating the most detailed interactive world atlas ever created, Microsoft's Encarta Interactive World Atlas.
