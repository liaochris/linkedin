Rocco Freer's Projects

    • December 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Rocco Freer, Andrew Borchardt, Chuck Cory, Robert Bidwell, Frank Baker, Alycia Demey, Tyler Wyse, David Knapp, Paula Saxton, Virg Diver

    Every year the Rotary Club of Blissfield joins hands with the Knights of Columbus in Blissfield to distribute over 100 "Basic Needs" and Food baskets to families in need throughout Lenawee county. Rotary takes care of the "Basic Needs" baskets while the Knights take care of the Food baskets.

    The "Basic Needs" baskets include:
    Toilet paper, Laundry Detergent, Dish Soap, Cleaning Supplies, Paper Towels, Kleenex, Asprin, Motrin, Band-aids, First aid ointment, Tooth paste, Tooth brushes, Pepto, Cough drops, Shampoo, Chapstick, Bar soap, Body wash, De-orderent.

    Along with the "Basic Needs" baskets the Rotary club also provides 20-25 "Baby Basic Needs" baskets, which include:
    Diaper rash ointment, Baby shampoo, Baby oil, Baby bath soap, Baby lotion, Diapers, Diaper wipes, Teething gel and a children book.

    This project has been going on since 2009 and has been a huge success within the community and surrounding areas. If you have any questions or would like to donate time or products please feel free to contact me at borchie9@gmail.com.
