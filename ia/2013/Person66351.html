John Follis' Summary

John founded Follis Inc in '93 after his agency, Follis/DeVito/Verdi, became the second most award-winning in New York. Award-winning creativity, combined with digital expertise (links below), has made Follis Inc an effective marketing resource for businesses for two decades.

BIG IDEA VIDEO, John's current focus, is meeting the growing need to sell products and services more quickly and effectively through the use of smart, creative, high impact online video. Have a look at: bigideavideo.biz
