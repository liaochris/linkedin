Daniel Casey's Summary

An award winning freelance photographer who specializes in family, portrait, event, architectural, and lifestyle photography. I have a humble studio setup and also shoot location type settings. I shoot all digital with some of the finest equipment in todays market. I love people and specialize in people images. Some of my best work is Boynton Beach Police Calendar 2008' as well as some commercial work for several different clients such as Polo Ralph Lauren. I love shooting people and capturing their character most of all. I can shoot from behind the scenes (candid) or walk right up to you and capture you unaware. I love my work and my job. If you ever need someone to shoot your wedding, Bar Mitzvah, Sweet 16, Quinceniera, executive or a headshot. I am available for all sorts of people imaging needs! My work has been published in magazines, many websites and shown on television. I have studied with a few of the best photographers and professors in the arts medium industry and have an extensive client base.

Specialties

Photography is my full time occupation.
I specialize in people pictures as well as inanimate objects. I have been known to shoot architectural, food, jewelry, product, vegetation and a plethora of other interesting things for money but my main joy is capturing people when they are being themselves. I really enjoy my work and invest a great deal of time learning the trends as they change. My equipment is clean and top of the line and I have a fantastic editing facility for final prints.
