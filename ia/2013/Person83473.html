Luke Pura's Summary

My name is Luke Pura, I'm a Chicago based graphic designer, recently graduated with my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from the University of Illinois at Chicago.

In 2005 I started to dabble in graphic design, and have been expanding my knowledge of design since, while freelancing on the side for local businesses and musicians.

I specialize in print design. I have been taught intensive typography and grid design, with a personal focus on rhythm and harmony. I am slowly working my way into the realm of web design, having created this site myself. I am constantly looking to further my knowledge of design and to work with people from all walks of life.
