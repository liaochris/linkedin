Frances Archer's Summary

I help my clients develop and publish engaging online content to generate awareness and reinforce relationships. If you're an entrepreneur or small business owner and are wondering where to start your online marketing efforts or how to make what you already have work better, let me help you:

+ create a blog that clearly establishes your business savvy and passion for your work
+ develop content for Facebook business pages that engages your fans and generates comments, clicks and page shares
+ publish e-newsletters with high open/click rates
+ integrate all marketing communications with effective editorial calendars
+ blend innovative online and offline tactics for spreading the word about your great business

First step is getting measured. Does social media fit in your budget? Do you have room in your schedule for additional work? Can social media help you reach your goals? Like the best clothing, social media is not one size fits all. My goal is to discover the social media tools that best fit your needs, so you can "wear" your social media in good health!

I have experience in these fields: wine, interior design, nonprofits, special events, personal organizing, fitness training, lawncare, professional coaching, real estate, and home remodeling. I work with individuals, agencies, small businesses, and nonprofits.

Specialties: Strategic thinking | Social media content development | Online community management | Website content creation | Writing and editing | Creating compelling stories | Speech writing and Powerpoint presentations | Research | Business blogging | Facebook business page updates | E-newsletters
