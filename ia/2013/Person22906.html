Stephanie Himonidis' Summary

With a radio career of over 10 years in formats ranging from Pop to Regional Mexican, and winner of two Emmy Awards for outstanding work in television, Stephanie Himonidis, also known as "La Chiquibaby," is currently one of the most popular communicators in the west coast, tune in to her radio program Monday through Friday from 10:00 am to 3:00 pm on KLVE 107.5 FM in the area of Los Angeles, California and La Nueva 103.5 FM in Las Vegas, Nevada.

"La Chiquibaby" is an experienced DJ who began her career in broadcasting in Guadalajara, Mexico, where her talent and popularity was recognized immediately and gave her the opportunity to be the voice of "Super Stereo Network," owner of the most important radio stations in the city.

Later she moved to the United States where she started to work in the city of Fresno, California, for Infinity Broadcasting (now CBS Radio) and Azteca America.

In 2004 she joined the Univision Radio family in the city of San Diego, and the following year formally began in television as the host of the morning program "Despierta San Diego."

In 2009 she was invited to be part of the celebrity judging panel of Univision reality competition program “Viva el Sueño.“

“La Chiquibaby" has participated as special guest and host on several programs on Univision and Telefutura Networks, such as "Don Francisco Presenta " "El Gordo y la Flaca", “Escandalo TV” , ”Lanzate” and KMEX-TV/Univision-Channel 34 in Los Angeles, "Sal y Pimienta" and she collaborates on a weekly basis for “Despierta América”.


She has also participated in several ad campaigns in the United States and Latin America for brands such as Universal Studios, Disney, Verizon Wireless, Metro PCS, Mahatma rices, Jumex juices, Barcel, Ford, KIA , Toyota, Proctor and Gamble, Post, Bansefi, Maxwell Coffee, Pinol, TIDE and most recently multi-market campaigns for IFE and Chevron.

Specialties: On air Radio and TV host.
