  • Stripped Down Marketing

    • Bookbaby.com
    • May 1, 2011
    Authors: Kelly A. Meerbott

    After spending more than thirteen years reading, researching, observing and working in marketing in many major markets across the country, I’ve made one discovery on a consistent basis. No matter the size of the company, no matter what the product or service, no matter what the demographics, marketing is an ambiguous term.

I had no idea how differently each person viewed marketing until I began presenting marketing seminars through the Virginia Department of Business Assistance (VDBA). While speaking to hundreds of business owners across the Commonwealth of Virginia, I can honestly say: no two definitions were alike.
