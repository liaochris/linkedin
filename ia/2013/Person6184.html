Anastasia Ashman's Summary

New York, Los Angeles, Kuala Lumpur & Istanbul media, publishing & entertainment industry veteran || writer/editor of a Silicon Alley internet industry & e-biz magazine, a critically acclaimed book and a group blog for global citizens || producer of events || 14 year global worker & expatriate in 4 countries || entrepreneur now in San Francisco

Cross-disciplinary content & culture professional with an intrinsic grasp of today's full stack marketing trend: social media, content marketing, transmedia storytelling, SEO, analytics, community building, etc.

Your go-to global woman for 21st century personal branding & community building. I equip individuals & groups with an effective framework of customized digital strategy and an award-winning practice to reach offline goals through your online presence.

I'm a visionary for global work/life fit we can achieve using social & mobile tech & our own media. Speaker & consultant.

A native Berkeleyan, I’ve been on a global rollercoaster: fired in Hollywood, abandoned on a snake-infested island off Borneo, married in an Ottoman palace, interviewed by Matt Lauer on the Today show.

I bring it all home as cofounder of GlobalNiche.net. We're a digital literacy training company teaching content marketing/personal branding/platform-building. We offer training content & support in a virtual community environment so you can find your best digital tools and incrementally build a professional online presence to achieve work/life fit.

I’m the cultural writer/producer behind internationally bestselling book Expat Harem and the online discussion community about identity adventure, global nomadism, location independence, and the hybrid soul.

An “accidental anthologist”, novelist Elif Shafak called my first book ”one of the 5 best books on Turkey“. Forbes.com named me in the top 20 best Twitter women for entrepreneurs; business coach Evan Carmichael put me at #46 in the Top 100 women entrepreneur experts to follow on Twitter".
