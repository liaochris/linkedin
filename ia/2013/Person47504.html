Greg Dorsey, GRI, CBR's Summary

Greg is a top producing business owner with over 16 years of experience in the real estate industry. He is the Founder and President of First Choice Realty, Inc., a full-service real estate company serving Boston, metro-Boston and all of Massachusetts. Greg is a licensed Massachusetts Real Estate Instructor, and is licensed to teach others to obtain a Real Estate License. Greg is highly regarded in the real estate community as one of the most skilled real estate professionals in Boston and Massachusetts.

· Oversee premier full-service real estate company, successfully building it into a multi-million dollar company within its first 5 years. Expanded business by purchasing and streamlining real estate firms into successful industry leading agencies, and launching a professional property management company to better serve our clients.
· Lead a staff of 30 highly competitive salespeople and leasing agents, and manage all day-to-day operations including accounting, database and web design, construction and development, financial records and sales.
· Consistently improve annual account customer satisfaction by designing and implementing agent training programs to maintain the highest quality staff that leads the industry in sales and retention.
· Manage the process of purchasing and renovating and re-designing distressed properties, and leasing or selling for profit.
· Oversee a staff of vendors and contractors covering all trades relevant to property development, maintenance, and capital improvements.
· Analyze and implement relevant technology and provide staff training to ensure it is being utilized efficiently.

Greg Dorsey, GRI, CBR, founded First Choice Realty, Inc. in 1999. Mr. Dorsey leads the FCR staff with over sixteen years of industry experience. He is the President of First Choice Realty, Inc. and general manager; and has earned the highest industry accreditation's including GRI (Graduate Real Estate Institute 90 hours of classroom instruction) and CBR (Certified Buyer Representative). Greg is also a graduate of Dr. Karrass, Effective Negotiating course.

To date Greg has successfully launched both a full service real estate company with sales & rental divisions, and a property management company to better serve his clients needs. He is a licensed home improvement specialist (construction supervisor candidate) and has successfully brokered thousands of real estate transactions; and specializes in investment property sales (including buildings, multi-families & condo's), helping first time buyers, apartment rental seekers and professionally managing buildings and owners investments to increase their ROI. Greg has also successfully listed and sold short sale & foreclosure properties and helped buyers and sellers both in Boston and many suburbs throughout Massachusetts.
