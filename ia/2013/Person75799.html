Kim Linder, CSA (Certified Senior Advisor)'s Summary

I'm passionate about helping caregivers listen to their inner voice, be authentic and make conscious decisions during every stage of the caregiving process. In addition to my 10+ years as a professional caregiver, I have personally served as primary caregiver to my in-laws for two years. My career path has provided invaluable insight into the very real struggles, guilt, challenges, joys and sadness that caregivers often experience. And because of my personal journey, I understand and embrace the healing power of self-discovery and empowerment.

As an advocate for seniors, my grass roots experience as a Marketing Director and Executive Director of an Assisted Living Community helped me to become an integral part of the senior living community. My passion to enhance the lives of seniors motivated me to develop monthly educational and wellness programs to illustrate my belief that many seniors want to be seen and heard rather then become invisible as they age. My work in this arena naturally led to my passion for helping the many caregivers that I have encountered and interacted with over the years.

You can listen to me every week on my radio show "The Caregiver Hour." This weekly radio show supports caregivers by providing encouragement, advice and helpful resources. Recognizing that many caregivers want to be authentic while setting healthy boundaries, I try to guide them towards a place of purposeful wisdom. Listen to the show by clicking the following link: http://thecaregiverhour.com

In addition to being a radio host, I'm also a Certified Senior Advisor (CSA). I've gained extensive knowledge about aging and the important health, social and financial issues that affect the majority of seniors.

Specialties

Networking, taking risks, empowering people, connecting people, holistic marketing guru, certified senior advisor

Kim Linder, CSA (Certified Senior Advisor)'s Experience

Host

The Caregiver Hour (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) Tampa/St. Petersburg, Florida Area

The Caregiver Hour is a radio show hosted by Kim Linder, founder of Senior Holistic Living and creator of The New Age of Caregiving Symposium. Her aim is to support caregivers by providing encouragement, advice and helpful resources.

Recognizing that many caregivers want to be authentic while setting healthy boundaries, Kim seeks to guide them towards a place of purposeful wisdom. A place where they can look at the big picture and make conscious decisions rather than reacting to the fear and panic that often set in after someone in their life falls, becomes ill, or experiences another type of crisis.

Focused on everything from the practical daily responsibilities to the emotional hard-to-discuss issues that caregivers face, The Caregiver Hour is a valued resource for primary and secondary caregivers. So whether you’re regularly caring for someone, or providing relief and support to a caregiver who is, this show will guide your path in a way that’s accessible, engaging, and most of all, useful to those in need.

Please join us, listen in, and let us know what you think. And if you like what we’re doing and know someone who could benefit, please pass the word along.

Founder

Senior Holistic Living, LLC

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

2010 – Present (3 years) Tampa/St. Petersburg, Florida Area

As a trusted resource, Senior Holistic Living will guide you, the caregiver, through the process of listening to your intuition and responding accordingly. We employ a holistic approach to caregiving that honors the whole person – physical, mental, emotional, social and spiritual. As you become emotionally grounded in these elements, you’ll be able to tap into the wisdom and calm judgment residing within you. Our passion lies in helping you receive these thoughts and use them to create a caregiving experience that fits who you truly are.

One-on-one Coaching

Kim Linder, founder of Senior Holistic Living, offers one-on-one personal coaching sessions ($60 each) via phone or in person.* After assessing and prioritizing your needs, she’ll work with you to create an actionable plan. These 60-minute sessions are appropriate for either ongoing support or for a limited time during a crisis.

Caregiver Programs at the Workplace

We offer onsite support, guidance and training for caregivers at the workplace. Our corporate workshops benefit the employee caregivers by reducing stress, providing much-needed resources, and illuminating the five steps to creating balance in their lives. The sponsoring companies also benefit in the form of improved employee productivity, reduced absenteeism, and increased company loyalty.

Professional Healthcare Caregivers

The delivery of quality caregiving services often relies on an interdisciplinary team of trained professionals – including professional healthcare caregivers. We offer engaging, enlightening and energizing group seminars and presentations designed to address their unique needs.

If you have questions, would like to discuss whether our approach is right for you, or to book a speaking engagement, please send us an email or schedule a complimentary 10-minute consultation.

* In person appointments provided only in the Tampa Bay area. Additional travel fees may apply depending on your exact location.
