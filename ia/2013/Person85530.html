Margaret Byrnes FIHI's Experience

Owner

RED D

Public Company; Myself Only; Staffing and Recruiting industry

February 2008 – Present (5 years 10 months) Ireland

Margaret has a strong management background in international hospitality operations with a key emphasis on excellence in customer service with companies such as Hilton International, Sarova and Forte Hotels.
She has significant experience in coaching and mentoring directors, owners, senior management and line personnel. Margaret’s forte is in designing & delivering bespoke training programmes and facilitating group learning. A key strength of Margaret’s is assisting in the mobilisation of new high profile hospitality businesses and providing expert coaching for existing organisations facing challenges in service and organisational excellence.
Building on her practical experience, Margaret has supplemented her knowledge by achieving qualifications in Hospitality Management, Counselling, Training and an MSc in Human Resources. She is pursuing a Masters in Psychotherapy.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Red D also provides a dynamic recruitment service to the Hospitality Industry, specialising in middle to executive management & Executive Chef placements.
With over 20 years experience, at Red D we believe in building long term relationships founded on mutual respect, integrity and confidentiality
We believe in simplicity and not over complicating things.
We simply source the best candidates available for our clients and the best positions available for our candidates.
