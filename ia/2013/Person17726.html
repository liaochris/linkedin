Brian Schilt's Summary

Brian is a senior software engineer specializing in web applications built on the Drupal platform. Since 2000, Brian has worked with many programming languages before adopting Drupal in 2008. His professional experience came from supporting the Federal government, education industry and the fast paced ad agency industry which allowed him to gain expertise with both front end development and back end programming. On his spare time, Brian gives back to the Drupal community by maintaining modules and participating in the Phoenix Drupal User's group.

Brian has expert knowledge in Drupal module and theme development, views integration, batch scripting, feeds, display suite, entities, features, debugging, Drupal APIs and more...
