Carla Paton's Summary

Carla Paton is a certified life coach and owner of Life Journey Now, a coaching practice which helps individuals pursue their passion and achieve fulfillment in life and at work.

Carla brings over twenty years of diverse business expertise and experience. Encouraging people to accomplish more and reach farther has been a focal point throughout her career. Since receiving an engineering degree in 1987, she has held various positions in project and program management. Carla exhibits sound judgment and decision-making skills and is an excellent communicator and facilitator. Her ability to recognize the natural talents in others has been an asset for team-building and inspiring people to perform at their best.

Upon completing a certification in transformational life coaching, Carla continues to enjoy opportunities which allow her to integrate her diverse skillset to create environments which encourages people to rise up to their fullest potential.

Specialties: Areas of specialty include Program Management, Life Purpose and Destiny Discovery, Leadership and Life Calling, Life Transition and Performance Coaching.
