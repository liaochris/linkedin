David Hildebrand's Summary

At Westfield, I lead one of the premiere design and strategy teams in the real estate industry. For two years, I’ve enjoyed developing best-in-class, shopper-first user experiences for Westfield’s U.S. portfolio of digital properties.

Recent highlights include:

• Leading a total reinvention of The Westfield App (iOS and Android) making it the most advanced and highest-rated app of any mall developer.

• Creating WestfieldStyle, a dynamic world of social and video-based fashion content.

• Relaunching the digital presence of Westfield UTC in San Diego which includes the first-ever online and onsite Pinterest wall.

Looking forward to 2013, I have the honor of designing the online presence for Westfield’s flagship World Trade Center project, the launch of WestfieldStyle 2.0, and setting a new standard for how the industry connects with their customers, whether it's on the couch, in the mall, at the gym, or on the go.
