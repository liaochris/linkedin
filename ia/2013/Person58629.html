Jan G. van der Hoop, RHB's Experience

President

HiringSmart

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry

November 2006 – Present (6 years 8 months)

We improve business performance (growing revenue and improving efficiencies while reducing waste and expenses) through the improved matching of people to roles.

The link between engagement and business performance is well documented: engagement is the most reliable predictor of business outcomes over the long term. But the factors that must align in order to set the stage for 'engagement' are four critical aspects of employee fit: Fit with the manager, with the job, with the team and with the organization.

The mechanism is simple, and immutable: Fit enabes Engagement, which enables Business Performance

When our clients base their attraction, selection, talent management and succession planning decisions on fit, they see almost immediate improvement in their key metrics.

I oversee strategy and business development, and also support our Authorized Resellers and Strategic Partners in growing new markets.

I also write, speak and lead workshops internationally.
