Charlotte Maines' Summary

I am a member of the class of 2013 in the full-time MBA program at UCLA Anderson where I serve as the President of the Anderson High Tech Business Association. I interned as a Product Manager in the Kindle group at Amazon.com in summer 2012 and will be returning full-time as a Senior Product Manager in 2013. Over the summer, I scoped the impact and launched the design and development for a product to be tied to September 6th new Kindle launch and presented and recommended product concept and implementation strategy to Jeff Bezos and Senior VPs. I also used data software to gather customer information and perform financial and segmentation analyses and conducted and analyzed surveys distributed to over 85,000 customers. I used this information to create a financial model to test the underlying assumptions in target customer behavior and develop most effective strategies for engagement.

Until 2011, I served as Development Project Manager for Fisher Property Group, Inc. where I managed hotel development projects from start to full entitlements for the ground-up development of a hotel. I accomplished this by coordinating and managing the team of engineers, consultants, and attorneys, and interfaced directly with the local jurisdictions. I developed the company website, designed all external marketing materials, and managed its digital presence.

Prior to joining Innkeepers, I was a PCG Associate at UBS Financial Services, Inc. in New York City, NY. While there I led an equities initiative to launch and market a multi-million dollar DIV product providing cross-asset exposure and sold 5% more than any other associate. I received a B.A. from Dartmouth College in 2002.

Specialties: Product Management, Project Management, Social Media, Product Marketing, Development, Market Analytics, Feasibility Research, Product Design, Cross-functional Leadership, Contract Negotiation, Obtaining Approvals
