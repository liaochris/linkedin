Lisa A. Cohen's Summary

Lisa is the President and Chief Operating Officer of Momentum Investment Partners, LLC, d/b/a Avatar Investment Management. In this role, Lisa oversees Avatar's day-to-day operations and corporate relationships and partnerships.

Lisa is also the CEO of Momentum Partners, a leading advisory firm to financial services and investment management industry leaders committed to driving growth and innovation. In this role, Lisa works with executives to address evolving distribution channels, innovate new products and services, identify partners and collaborators, build strategies and business plans, and more.

Lisa is a motivational leader who inspires and supports contrarian thinking, a frequent industry speaker and moderator, and regular media contributor. Momentum Partners is a service company signatory to the United Nations Principles for Responsible Investment.

Lisa’s background includes more than a decade of experience as a trusted advisor to senior financial services leaders, and equally as much time as a senior level industry operating executive. Lisa was a SVP and Managing Director, Product Management, at Evergreen Investments, now part of Wells Fargo, and was a Director with responsibility for investment and retirement product management and marketing with John Hancock Funds and John Hancock Financial Services.

Lisa is a graduate of Smith College and has completed Harvard’s Executive Education program in Investment Decisions and Behavioral Finance. She is the co-author of Wealthbuilding, published by Dearborn Financial Publishing, and is an active member of the Boston Club.

Reach Lisa at 617-500-9795 and by email at lcohen (at) momentumpllc (dotcom).
