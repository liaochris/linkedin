Laurie Kalmanson's Summary

UX, IA, IXD, Content strategy: What do your users need? I've been a UX lead, consultant, manager, cross-team leader, and individual contributor at agencies, Seth Godin's startup, enterprise apps and web shops since the first dotcom boom in NYC, working collaboratively with clients, c-level managers and teams to create digital experiences that delight loyal users while matching business goals to things people want to do.

I walked into Yoyodyne Entertainment / Seth Godin Productions (acquired by Yahoo!) and out of newspaper reporting when the internet went commercial, and never looked back.

Today, I work across web, iOS, social, e-commerce, dashboards, healthcare claims processing, intranets and B2B.

Methods: Listening and talking; customer journeys, hanging things on the wall and drawing on them; personas, scenarios, sitemaps, storyboards, wireframes and user flow diagrams; landing pages; A/B conversion testing; trying things to see what works.

Clients/Projects: The Actors Fund, American Express, AT&T/Downtown Digital, Bideawee, BlueCross BlueShield, CBS.com, Click3x, Clickfire Media,The Climate Project, Columbia/Presbyterian Medical Center, ConEd, Continuum Health Partners, Cummins, Deloitte, Dilbert/United Media, Emdeon/WebMD, Ford, HealthStream, Hugo Boss, iostudio, Mercedes-Benz, Macquarium, Moontoast, Munchkin, PassAlong Networks, Pedigree/Woofer, Pitney-Bowes, SESAC, Standard & Poor's, T-Mobile, Time Inc., Yoyodyne / Seth Godin Productions (acquired by Yahoo!), ZirMed.
