Fred Hann's Summary

Fred is a designer, business person and a domain expert in bringing design thinking to re-frame problems in landscape and residential real estate industries. He embraces complexity and confusion to produce beautiful simple and elegant solutions. Known for causing things to happen & provoking people to do their best work. Master’s degree in Landscape Architecture from University of Guelph supplemented with business management courses through the P.J. Gardner Institute for Small Business, Memorial University of Newfoundland & Labrador. He is member of Canadian Nursery Landscape Association; Canadian Society of Landscape Architects. Fred has led an award winning landscape design & architecture consultancy company in Atlantic Canada. He is the former O/O of a design-build company (homes & landscapes) Siteworks Inc..
He has contributed his time and expertise in community service on various public boards including the arts, health and education. Fred enjoys reading fiction and non-fiction, travel, design, biographies, economic history and business. He is passionate about creating beautiful and sustainable exterior spaces. He likes to spend his down time with friends hiking, sea-kayaking, and sailing - ocean cruising.
He is currently based in Ontario with projects in St. John's, Newfoundland, Toronto, Calgary and Vancouver.
