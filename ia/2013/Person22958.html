Christen Mau's Summary

I guide in branding and strategic communications, building visibility through digital technologies, including LinkedIn®. It’s about making results visible in profits and in Google.

I create guidelines and legal standards for the online presence of employees in relation to employer and employee branding. The basis is the Danish legislation and LinkedIn® guidelines.

I optimize and clarify on the basis of skills and achievements to evoke incentive to buy and generate leads. I help you stand out.

With the purpose and intent in mind, I guide in:
• Personal branding
• LinkedIn®
• Social link building
• Digital Strategy
• Networking and development of strategic relationships
• Employer and employee branding
• Strategic communications
• Search engine optimization (SEO)
• Social media
• Content Marketing

I blog ‘in Danish’ at “Djævlen sidder i detaljen” (The devil is in the detail) about branding and strategy through digital technologies primarily LinkedIn®. I have among others written about guidelines in relation to the Danish legislation and content marketing, how to be found in searches, and about optimizing your LinkedIn® profile to avoid the consequences of completing the English language variant in minority languages.

Christen Mau's Publications

  • Sådan: Sociale Medier

    • WLFMcph
    • March 1, 2010
    Authors: Christen Mau, Caroline Jensen, Sandra Laxager

    A guide on how to use social media for marketing, communications and branding, including a description of Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. You get the associated explanations on issues ranging from culture, opportunities and pitfalls. It guides to SRM strategy, i.e. the freedom of employees to use social media during working hours in a way that is beneficial for the workplace.

    We advise in proper use of the Internet, and how to achieve visibility. You learn how to contribute in search engine optimization, and how to use alarm systems and third-party applications. The point of departure is traditional marketing theories.

  • Sådan! Gør dig bemærket - Øg din synlighed med LinkedIn.

    • WLFMcph
    • October 4, 2011
    Authors: Christen Mau, Caroline Jensen, Simone E. Lehmann Brandt

    A guide on how to use LinkedIn facing HR consultants in companies and professional recruitment consultants, and a guide to personal branding and attainment of visibility (SEO) in searches on LinkedIn as well as in external searches for example Google.

  • Sådan! Målret din jobsøgning – Netværk dig til et job med LinkedIn

    • WLFMcph
    • February 1, 2012
    Authors: Christen Mau, Ole Bach Andersen, Mathilde Holm Hvidt

    A guide on how to use your network when looking for jobs. On LinkedIn you can gain knowledge about companies and their employees, and not the least, gather your network in one place. Get an overview of your relations and the network of your relations, and open up hidden opportunities for getting in touch with the companies and industries that you are interested in. Step-by-step the manual shows how to create and optimize your personal profile - and how to network your way to your next job.
