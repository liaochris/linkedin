Michael Huth's Summary

Image Forward has been helping companies establish their presence in the Internet market since 2002. Founded by Michael Huth in 1999 as a sole proprietorship, 3 years later he successfully formed Image Forward LLC, and became the CEO. He has a 23+ year history in the software technology industry and combines his experience and innovation into client solutions.

Image Forward specializes in helping businesses become easy to find on the Internet, specifically through Google, Yahoo & Bing keyword searches.

Specialties

Internet marketing and advertising for franchisers, franchisees and individual business owners. Internet Presence Management and Social Media Management.
