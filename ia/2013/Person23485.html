Chris Lien's Summary

Seasoned Project and Program Manager with thirteen years of project implementation consulting experience. Founder of OmniTek Consulting in 2005, Chris effectively utilizes hard and soft skills to execute client projects on time, within budget and with high quality. Extremely proficient in all stages of systems development life cycle (SDLC). Highly adroit with operating Program Management Office on small to large implementations and internal client initiatives. Able to communicate at multiple organizational levels, from VPs to system developers.

Specialties include vast project implementation with CRM, Supply Chain, HR, and web self-service technologies in industries including pharma/biotech, government and commercial.
