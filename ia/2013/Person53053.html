Chris Higgins' Summary

Long story short: I'm a writer who also works in the mobile technology field. My latest book is "The Blogger Abides," and "Breakfast on Mars" is coming in mid-2013. I work with Cloud Four and Second Rise on mobile web projects, and I write a daily column for Mental Floss.

Longer version: I contributed to This American Life ("Held Hostage," Act III, 4 June 2010). My writing appears regularly in Mental_Floss Magazine and every day on the mentalfloss.com blog. I've written a series of historical fiction books for middle readers, for an upcoming museum project in the southwest.

In my copious daytime hours, I manage app development and mobile web projects at Cloud Four. In my former life as Technical Director at Night & Day Studios, I developed (or oversaw development of) Peekaboo Barn, Peekaboo Wild, Savage Love, Cocktail Compass, Quibble, Quibble Kids, Big Fat Lies, Life in Short, Nick Jr.'s A-Z with Moose and Zee, and a bunch more.

Prior to all this, I was a Product Manager at Kavi Corporation. I was with Kavi for almost eight years, working through a series of positions related to web software development, management, marketing, and so on. I left Kavi to focus on writing, and it has been an interesting couple of years!
