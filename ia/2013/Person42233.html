Eric Levenhagen, CPA,CTC's Summary

Want to know how to enjoy a return on investment of your tax fees? Eric Levenhagen's clientele enjoy an average 100% return on investment during their first year of tax planning! By building and implementing proactive strategies for optimal tax reduction, he provides his clients with year-round confidence for continued financial growth.

As one of Certified Tax Coach's founding members, Eric Levenhagen is trailblazing a path for business owners to dramatically slash their biggest expense--taxes! He brings over ten years of experience and specialized training to his clients' business analysis and tax preparation.

Mr. Levenhagen stands out as the only Certified Tax Coach in the state of Iowa. He is a self-taught man who has gained his expertise through experience, research and the "school of hard knocks." His business and personal life are grounded by his overarching value for family, which he considers his greatest achievement.
