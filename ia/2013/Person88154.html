Mary Lyn Miller's Summary

Mary Lyn has spent the past 20 years helping thousands of clients worldwide effectively find new directions, re-invent their lives and work and transition into lifestyles and businesses that are rewarding, fulfilling and profitable.

A former Fortune 500 executive, she was diagnosed with terminal cancer and her personal journey toward passionate and purposeful living is documented in the well known book, "Chicken Soup for the Surviving Soul." The life affirming process she developed from this experience has been featured in Marie Claire, the Chicago Tribune, Career-builder and more. She is the author of “The 8 Myths of Making a Living (and the Truth of Making a Life,)” and “90 Day Magic: How to Create the Passionate Life and Business You Really Want in 90 Days or Less.”

If you or your staff have lost the fire, the inspiration, the zest for what they are doing, Mary Lyn’s “Fired Up Success System” offers 5 crucial elements to re-ignite motivation, light the inner fire and set goals that create a blaze of success.

If it’s time to thrive, create the change you desire (but probably don’t know how to do yet), and have the support you need so you don’t do it alone, it’s time to get fired up!

Specialties: career change, small business owners, women in business, motivation, inspiration, goal setting, coaching, mastermind training and speaking.
