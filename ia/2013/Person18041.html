Sriram Chandrasekaran's Summary

Entrepreneur with 15 years of experience in Industry; Founded Broadvision Perspectives - a business consulting firm that works with large corporations in challenging areas of Strategy, Brands & People - in 2008. Broadvision Perspectives has delivered on engagements worth over USD 3 Million to date, for clients including the Mahindra Group and Procter & Gamble, and has created the popular "War Room" program in India. Founded Studio56 - an Art & Technology studio in 2011. Studio56 has delivered on 49 creative projects spanning design, animation, VFX and IPad apps, for India's top advertising agencies, film producers and corporate clients. Studio56 is currently developing 2 animated television series & educational IPad apps for children in America and Europe. Serve as an Independent Director in Source Natural Health & food Supplements Limited.

Graduated with honors in Mechanical Engineering from BITS Pilani and MBA in Marketing & General Management from Indian Institute of Management, Lucknow; Served the Tata Administrative Services - Tata Group's leadership cadre - from 1998 to 2004 and Microsoft Corporation from 2004 to 2007.
