Louise Hodgins' Summary

Omelo specialises in contemporary convex mirrors. Designed in-house, we hand finish every frame to order, with bespoke colours and finishes available on request.

We have a high-end customer base, including interior designers, private individuals, businesses and hotels throughout the UK and overseas.

Our latest press coverage includes Livingetc (March 2011), Homes & Gardens (April 2011), Good Homes (May 2011 & March 2012) and Ireland’s Homes Interiors & Living magazine (August 2011).
