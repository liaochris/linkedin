Dianne Normandeau's Summary

My goal is to give the best professional immigration advice and services according to individual needs. I can help find immigration options and solutions that were not obvious. I provide ongoing support throughout complex and sometimes lengthy immigration processes.

My working experience and rigorous tests of knowledge and skills in the Canadian immigration field has gained me certification with the Canadian Society of Immigration Consultants (CSIC) and the new regulatory body designated by the Candian government the ICCRC, Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council. As per new Quebec provincial laws for Immigration Consultants I am Registered with the MICC (Quebec Immigration), a Consultant Recognized to practice before the Quebec government.

A Registered Immigration Consultant is your assurance of quality legal assistance to prepare your application and represent you before the Canadian government for your immigration needs. As a Registered Immigration Consultant I am equipped with the latest information on immigration law, procedures and practices and go about my business according to a strict code of conduct.
