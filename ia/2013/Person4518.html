Alicia Greenberg's Summary

::CLASSIC CREATIVITY, CONTEMPORARY THINKING::
alicia@aliciagcreative.com

Alicia’s passion for creative expression began in elementary school where her finger paintings and multi-media dioramas often brought her the designation of “Artist of the Week.” She followed her artistic intuition from childhood to the present and takes pride in a successful and rewarding career as a Graphic Designer. Graduating with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from a conservatory arts college in New York led Alicia to landing a graphic design job at a high-powered advertising firm in Manhattan. Working in the fast-paced, advertising capital of the world set her up for success. Dealing with a variety of clients, including prominent names in fashion and resorts, in a deadline-driven environment helped her to gain a strong understanding of branding in print design and strengthened her capacity to work well under pressure.

Alicia’s personal and business environment shifted when she moved to Colorado and accepted a graphic design position for a marketing firm in Denver. Working on various collateral material and with clients in the education industry broadened her design skills and ingenuity.

Alicia’s quest for new challenges led her to southern California where she teamed up with the marketing department of a fashion forward jewelry company. She art directed various online projects such as e-marketing and social media campaigns in addition to designing the company’s print and online magazine. Executive staff have consistently recognized Alicia’s positive attitude, creativity, reliability and team accomplishments.

Alicia uses her talents to accommodate the design needs of clients on a freelance basis. A classic background in fine arts guided Alicia’s creativity to the contemporary thinking of today’s technology aesthetics.
