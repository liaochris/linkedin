Barbara Ivey's Summary

I am passionate about all things related to productivity, from email management and personal organization to improving processes to reduce waste.

My background includes 15 years as a corporate Librarian. I worked with senior executives and professionals in high tech and financial services companies.

As my responsibilities and job scope increased, I found the most challenging information management obstacle I faced daily was my very own desktop. I had moved from a being a Librarian trying to solve the world’s organization challenges, to a business professional addressing my own daily productivity challenges (multiplied x 5 since I was a virtual employee as well). I was doing the right work – but I was drowning in email, old versions of documents and to-dos.

Soon I recognized it wasn’t only me. Many professionals I met also struggled with information overload. I realized that there is a huge gap between what professionals need to do to stay productive in the 21st century, and the piecemeal 20th century practices we have in our toolbox to get it done.

I started Optimized Offices in Fort Mill, SC in 2009 to address that gap. Optimized Offices helps professionals with no-hassle ways to organize their days and simplify their processes. We bring Lean thinking and tools in the non-manufacturing setting in what I call Lean for Professionals.

I work with people every day who spend too much time on irrelevant email, are too busy to address the process problems standing between them and their clients or spend hours looking for papers or electronic files. Lean for Professionals opens up a whole new way of thinking for them and gets them started making improvements right away.

Today, I share what I’ve learned in speeches, workshops, Executive Optimization Coaching, the Lean for Professionals blog, and Kaizen Events across the country.
