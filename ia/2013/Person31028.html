Dave Cornett's Summary

"Combining form with functionality."

SNOW are a trusted RIBA Chartered Practice based in Liverpool employed locally and throughout the North West of England and North Wales by a wide variety of sectors.

In addition, we maintain that small is beautiful because of the benefits to you. It’s not about the usual reasons… better response times, customer service, you can take that for granted, it’s about detail, design proposals that incorporate the commercial aspects of construction, engineering and cost.

SNOW are currently providing architectural services on projects with a contract value of up to £15m (with larger projects currently at feasibility stage). Our current live projects range from extensions to client’s homes, restaurants, hotels, industrial units, student accommodation and mixed use schemes.

Dave is also a featured "Expert" in Select Magazine, a property magazine for the Liverpool area.

Dave won the "Best Construction Services Provider" award at the 2009 BNI Gala Awards. and has been shortlisted for the "Property Services" award at the Downtown Liverpool In Business "Liverpool Business Awards" 2010 awards.

Dave was shortlisted for the "Property Professional of the Year" award at the Downtown Liverpool In Business "LiverCOOL" 2009 awards.
