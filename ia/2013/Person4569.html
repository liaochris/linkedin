Alida Hernandez's Summary

Born in New York City on February 16, Alida Hernandez is a first generation to Puerto Rican born parents Ismael "Izzy" Hernandez and Maria Caceres. She is a single mother of two wonderful boys living in Deerfield Beach, Florida. She is a Christian who is adventurous, happy and full-of-life person. Currently she speaks English, Spanish, and Brazilian Portuguese. Even though her talents were not the same as her parents, she developed many others. She began dance classes at the age of 7 and continued until her mid-20s. She studied piano for my years but was not as strong as she liked. Music is in her blood and has developed a gift of discovering excellent artist talent. Also in 1983 she attended modeling/makeup artist school "D'Rose" where she starting her modeling career from 1983 to 1988.

She completed her Bachelor of Science degree in Technical Management and attended Keller Graduate School of Business with 75% of her course study completed for my MBA. With her strong abilities in math and technology she began working in the Information Technology field in the mid 80's she was involved in the acquisition and installation of the first personal computers at CBS, Inc. in New York City. I also worked in companies such as Bear, Stearns and Prudential-Bache, Disney, Pacific Theatres to name a few. She branched out on her own with my company "Alida's Promo & Eventz".

Alida’s Promo & Eventz offers artist management, publicity, and video production. The company looks to promote their Artist in a variety of venues to enhance and further their careers. Alida PROMO works closely with many media outlets including Americateve, Telemundo, Univision, Gentv and others. Stay tune to see our video production events on "EnlightenTV" and "AlidaPromo" via Ustream.tv
