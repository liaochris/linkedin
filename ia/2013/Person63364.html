Jason Fincanon's Summary

With experience working with the Flash Platform since graduating from The Art Institute of Dallas
in 1998, I have spent the majority of my career building Flash-based websites, games and
applications while working for employers ranging from interactive advertising agencies to branded
entertainment and advergaming companies. During my time in these industries, I have had the
opportunity to work on projects for clients such as Patron Tequila, GameStop, Hyundai, Fruit of the
Loom, National Pork Board, Travelocity, Florida Department of Citrus, Nokia and many others.

Outside of work I try to devote some extra time to expand on my knowledge and skill set by
studying other areas such as HMTL5, Java for Android and Objective-C.

Moving forward, I am looking to utilize the skills and experience I have acquired over the past
several years while also allowing me to continue to learn and grow professionally. In the near
future, I would like to advance to a leadership role or management position.
