Who We Are
VEM Global ™ is Vertical Engine Marketing. We are dedicated to a powerful, innovative trend in online marketing.

We draw our success upon more than a decade of Internet research and development. We base our work upon studies commissioned through Doubleclick, Forrester and many other marketing data centers, as well as our own niche Website development which spans more than a decade of email, phone and Web research and testing.

We also own technology that easily manages and maximizes our network of thousands of global and niche Websites.

What We Do
VEM Global ™ gives your business the kind of power you’ve always felt was just out of reach in your marketing plan. We channel your business and your message into vertical search – the most intensely focused and accurate strategy for gaining, tracking and maximizing qualified leads.

Vertical Engine Marketing places you exactly where you want to be on the Internet: on hundreds of unique vertical Websites that allow you to tailor your strongest attributes and key points of difference to your most passionate potential customers.

Through our exclusive partner network, we can broadcast your business to the most specific local markets. The most valuable regional markets. And the most visible national and global markets.

Whether local to global or the other way around, VEM Global also provides detailed, accurate tracking and ongoing yield management throughout.
