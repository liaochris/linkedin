Ken Kraemer's Summary

I create digital experiences. From rich, powerful ecommerce and publishing sites to innovative, advertising-based marketing campaigns, I help my clients engage with their audiences at a deeply human level. My product development-based design approach helps me quickly and effectively find the best opportunities for my clients to integrate their brands and products seamlessly into their audience's lives.

I have done this for Target, GE, Coca-Cola, Emirates, McKinsey & Company, MasterCard, Hartz, The Robin Hood Foundation and Citi, to name just a few clients.
