Jon Brouchoud's Summary

Jon is a freelance entrepreneur and award winning designer, with over 15 years of experience in professional practice, specializing in the use of virtual environments in education, health care, government, entertainment, consumer, and enterprise industries.

His work has been featured in Architectural Record, New York Times, Business Week and the Chicago Tribune. He has completed projects for Autodesk, the U.S. House of Representatives, Rutgers University, University of Alabama, Kelley School of Business, Santa Barbara Community College, and many more.

His specialties include Unity3D, Maya, architecture and concept design, 3D modeling, Oculus Rift VR development, animation, rigging, interface design, terrain and landscaping, level design, graphic design, Revit, Second Life and OpenSim.

See archvirtual.com for more portfolio and client references.
