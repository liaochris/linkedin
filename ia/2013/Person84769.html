Manya Kaczkowski's Summary

Our world is amazing--and I want to tell you all about it. I offer a bit of an escape to my readers--mini-vacations in the pages of a magazine or on a computer screen--to inspire, educate, and maybe even make you chuckle a bit at some of my escapades.

I've done features on everything from tornado hunting to zip lining to fly fishing, and have met some interesting folks along the way. Whether I'm writing about an artist who spends his life carving violins in northern Wisconsin, or dishing on the best restaurants in San Antonio, or documenting a trip on a historic steam train through the Rockies, you can be sure that something unique will be revealed—and you'll want to experience it for yourself.

Specialties: Food, drink, travel, culture, profiles and lifestyles, along with book writing/editing and catalog writing/editing.
