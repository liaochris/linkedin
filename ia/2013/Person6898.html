Andrew Findlater's Summary

Andrew Findlater is the Principal of SELECT Public Relations, a senior corporate communications professional with over 15 years experience.

Andrew provides clients with sound strategic counsel in relation to corporate reputation, issues management, employee communication and external communication campaigns. He routinely works with the C-suite in anticipating issues, developing key messages and providing media and presentation skills training. His background also includes an in-depth understanding of corporate social responsibility, social media, sponsorships and event management.

Andrew’s recent client experience includes work for adidas, American Express, Black & Decker, Canada Dry Mott’s, DeWalt, Porter-Cable and Wal-Mart. His former clients also include Coco Paving, Genworth Financial, GMAC Residential Funding, Ivanhoe Cambridge, Molson, Mortgage Intelligence, MySpace, the Ontario Power Authority, Richardson Partners Financial, Seniors Money, and Thomson-Reuters among others.

Andrew has managed logistical planning and media relations efforts for a variety of high-profile international events, including the visit of Pope John Paul II to Canada for the 2002 World Youth Day. In addition, he has served as a publicist for professional racecar drivers such as Greg Moore, Dario Franchitti, Patrick Carpentier and Paul Tracy, touring the Indy racing circuit around the world.

Before embarking on his consultancy career, Andrew was a general news reporter with CBC Radio in Ottawa. He also worked as a freelance reporter for Broadcast News, covering the Ottawa Senators in their inaugural season.

Andrew is a graduate of Carleton University's School of Journalism with an Honours Bachelor of Journalism, majoring in Political Science.

Specialties

media relations, litigation communications, social media, brand management, strategic planning, speech writing, feature writing, presentation skills training, media training, event management, sponsorships
