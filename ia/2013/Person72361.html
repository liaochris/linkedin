Karla Parra, MPA's Summary

Bilingual MPA graduate of the #1-ranked Maxwell School of Syracuse University, currently working out of Accenture's Dallas office as a Change Management Consultant. Always striving to incorporate my passions in Corporate Social Responsibility, Community Relations, and Employee Engagement to effect positive change in my local, national, and international community.

Past work includes U.S. and global Communications experience at General Electric. MPA academic experience includes coursework in NGO Management, Policy Implementation, Organizational Management, Budgeting, Quantitative Analysis, and Economics. As part of a final Capstone consulting team project, collaborated with CARE USA to analyze and recommend improvements on organizational accountability towards global impact measurement.

I have great passion for Corporate Social Responsibility; implementation and monitoring of poverty reduction, education, and women-empowerment tools; U.S.-based Hispanic social media engagement; immigration and bilingual education initiatives.
