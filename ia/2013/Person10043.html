Art Steinmark's Summary

Art Steinmark is a highly experienced, versatile copywriter who is comfortable with technically complex or challenging subjects and can write in a range of styles (long, short, formal, informal, academic, funny) to meet the creative strategy.

A team player and conceptual thinker, Art worked exclusively as a freelance marketing communications consultant, creative director and copywriter for over 20 years supporting agency clients and direct accounts. He has extensive experience developing creative communications and campaigns for many of the most prominent companies in the Greater Philadelphia-New Jersey area, including work on pharmaceutical, industrial, B2B, and consumer products and accounts.

In addition to DTP/DTC tactics shown below, recent experience includes SEO copywriting, WordPress content management, HTML email, Facebook business pages and posts, and copywriting for other SMM outlets.
