Jerome Mark Mikulich's Summary

Jerome Mark Mikulich is a Entrepreneur and former Platinum Recording Studio Engineer featured in Billboard Magazine as a Recording Artist & Producer. He founded Playroom Records & Productions, which was a staple in the Chicago music scene throughout the 1990's.

Today, Mikulich is the Founder & CEO of 3 Cord Marketing LLC, a full-service mobile advertising, sales and marketing firm. Mikulich recently founded CityByApp™ LLC, the licensed mobile app development subsidiary of 3 Cord Marketing LLC. CityByApp LLC develops mobile apps for municipalities on  Apple, Android and HTML-5 mobile devices.

Current CityByApp LLC projects:

CityByApp Temecula! (Released on June 19th, 2013)
CityByApp Murrieta! (Release Date: Mid-Aug 2013)
CityByApp Menifee! (Release Date: Mid-Sept 2013)

Learn more at: http://www.citybyapp.com
