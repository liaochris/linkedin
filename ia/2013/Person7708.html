Angie Mattson's Summary

I help you find your stuff faster, create more time for the things that matter (work OR play), and help you use your team more effectively. In short, I bring sanity back into your workday.

I sincerely believe being a business owner is about doing less, well (not figuring out how to do more, while trying to squoosh it all in to the limited time available).

My work includes:

* Changing the way you manage your time and calendar using our Time Map Game Changer Method (TM)

* Giving your office a complete facelift - including banishing those piles, archiving old files, re-working your office set-up, and cleaning off your desk for good

* Teaching you how to eliminate an over-stuffed email inbox using our 5 Steps to Easy Email Management (TM) program (we'll literally take you from 1,000's of email to a manageable 25 or less AND show you how to maintain that)

* Getting your business organized and systematized using our 5 Essential Business Systems (TM) program - it's an operational roadmap that drives your business towards happier clients, increasing revenues, and a less-stressed-you

My goal is to help you get unstuck and free up your creative energy to do great work,make more money, and have the life YOU want.
