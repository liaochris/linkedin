Mary Carty's Summary

I'm an award winning entrepreneur with a background in fine art, education and startups. Working with leading international companies, arts and cultural institutions, universities and non-profits over the past ten years; while building a startup and running an online agency, has taught me a few things about making it in an online world.

Creativity and curiosity are my twin passions. My background has given me a unique insight into building and marketing companies, products and startups. I love to learn and to share that knowledge with others. I truly want the next generation of entrepreneurs to make less mistakes than I did, build faster, scale faster and tell compelling, unique stories.

At heart I'm a storyteller. In speaking, lecturing and training my clients and individuals, I have learned that great stories, well told, always win out.

I have to thank a million people for helping me grow, develop and be successful on this journey. And so, I am always happy to lend a hand, introduce, and advise others who want to make things better and solve problems.

County Arts Officer

Meath County Council

Government Agency; 501-1000 employees; Government Administration industry

January 2006 – April 2008 (2 years 4 months)

My role was diverse and far reaching. At all times I worked tirelessly to provide leadership and best practice in developing, implementing and evaluating all Arts Service policy, programmes and projects; constantly keeping the end user, citizen of Meath in mind. Here is a short summary of the kind of projects undertaken during my tenure as County Arts Officer.

Responsible for formulating and implementing arts policy and programme for Meath County Council in Arts and Health, Public Art, Youth Arts, Artist Development and Outreach and Education along with individual art forms.

Provided mentoring, training and support for artists, arts organisations and community groups across the County.

Development, programming and management of two County wide multi-disciplinary Arts Festivals each year, Bealtaine, Festival of Arts for Older People, and The Children's ArtFest.

Initiated and developed The Lit Lab, an innovative writing programme for emerging and mid-career writers and poets, in partnership with Cavan County Council Arts Office.

Lead Curator and programmer Toradh Art Gallery, Ashbourne, Co. Meath.

Responsible for budget management of the County Arts programme.

Representing Meath County Council at local and national level.

Building partnerships with all stakeholders to provide sustainable arts provision for organisations, individuals, artists and communities in Meath.
