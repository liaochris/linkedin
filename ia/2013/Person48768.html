Giorgos Chronopoulos' Summary

Hi, my name is Gio and have been working professionally as a digital artist for over 11 years with a client list including Microsoft, Ebay inc, Paypal, Ford Motors, Nike, News International Newspapers Ltd and more.
For News International Newspaper Ltd I worked creating illustrations for The Sun Newspaper and The News of The World.
During that time I also worked on two 3D comics for The Sun Newspaper and The Sun Online called Striker and The Premier which where daily 3D comics which involved the creation and depiction of realistic human characters which has given me a great deal of experience in depicting the human form and to the strict and unforgiving daily deadlines of a newspaper.

I recently launched a webcomic/graphic novel called The Talos Project (www.thetalosproject.com) which is the latest example of my work in creating digital human characters and environments. The story, all artwork and the website itself were created by me. The Talos Project is a personal project I work on in my spare time and is something I love working on as it also allows me to try out new techiniques and improve my workflow without the constraints of normal production timelines (although I plan to release the first book by Nov).


I'm able to complete projects from the initial concept ideas through to creating all art assets 3D or 2D, animation, rigging, rendering and any photography needed, to final images for print or animations for web or broadcast. Maya, Mental Ray, Mudbox, Zbrush, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premier, and Photoshop.

I'm currently Art Director at GioChronoStudios creating digital Illustrations and animations with a focus in the area of 3D character animated commercials and sales videos.

-Gio

Specialties: Concept Design, Modeling hi and low poly, Texturing, Shading and Rendering (Mental Ray), Character Creation, Rigging, Retouching, Digital Painting, Website Creation, Animation, Video Editing.
