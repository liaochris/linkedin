Geert Nijkamp - speaker internationalization's Summary

My activities are divided in Spain, the Netherlands and Uganda.

In the Netherlands I speak about ‘internationalization, exports & internet’. What will be future developments and how can we anticipate on them?
Next to that I provide my knowledge to and exchange ideas with decision makers in internationalization in order to develop future proof policy.

I am worried about the declining position of Dutch and European exports. But I also see great opportunities how to improve these exports by a better use of internet. Development in internationalization is the key.

Therefore I have written a book "The influence of the internet on Dutch exports. How can we stay in the lead?", and developed the app iEXPORT (BB/iPhone/Android).

Between 2000-2012 I have supported and trained on an individual basis over a 100 companies/organizations. SME's + Top 500 in (international) internet marketing / online international business development. In almost all branches.

Together with my family and others we support the foundation 'Orphans in Uganda'. With an enthousiastic team over there and in the Netherlands we support orphans to get a better future by giving them a chance to go to school. www-wezenvanuganda-nl

In Spain we guide high tech knowledge workers to a new job in the Netherlands. www-hightechkenniswerkers-nl

Next to that we run a company in importing/exporting professional event products.

I live with my wife and three children in Colmenar - Malaga.

Don't hesitate to contact me. Maybe I can help you.
