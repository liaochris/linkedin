Kent Hutchison's Summary

Kent has 20 years of experience working in employee development and training. A global leadership subject matter expert (SME), he is currently a senior partner with C.J. Baxter Group, an employee development firm based in Nacogdoches.

Kent is a recognized international leader, facilitator, and trainer. He is a past cluster facilitator at the nationally recognized Leadershape Institute (www.leadershape.org); as well as the prestigious multi-state Rotary Lone Star President Elect Training Seminar (www.lonestarrotarypets.org). He regularly facilitates retreats for community organizations and businesses; and is a frequent seminar leader; and is sought to keynote professional conferences, banquets, and dinners. Each Spring you will find Kent facilitating public workshops/programs at both Lamar State College-Orange as well as Stephen F. Austin State University (SFA); and occasionally at other community colleges or universities.

This fifth-generation Texan is very involved as a community volunteer. A former volunteer fire fighter, Kent is a previous board member and committee chairman for both the Nacogdoches County Chamber of Commerce, and the SFA Alumni Association; as well as past Rotary Club president and assistant governor; and has served on several Rotary district committees. Kent currently serves on the Girl Scouts of San Jacinto Council.

Specialties: Global Leadership Development, Front Line Supervision Development, Team Development, Customer Service Training, Keynote Speaker, Workshop/Seminar Leader, Non-Profit Strategic Planning
