Glenn Sorrentino's Summary

I'm a UI Designer for Salesforce.com on the User Experience Services team supporting client-facing and internal products across the company.

Past experience includes working with NewCity in Blacksburg, VA as a web & mobile designer. We had clients like Penn State, CSU, Imperial College London, & Viacom. In the earlier days of iPhone development we launched an internal venture called NOMAD Mobile Guides - an iPhone app that exclusively partnered with national parks to deliver offline ready, rich, thorough & interactive guides for park goers.

I left to freelance, which I did until I came to California. I did some cool work for CES 2011, helped clients find mobile solutions for their companies and ideas, and even taught a class on UX Research and Design to the designers at Bandy Carroll Hellige, an ad agency in Louisville, KY with clients like Four Roses Whisky & The Kentucky Lottery.

When arriving in San Francisco I joined Zinio, a digital publishing company offering titles like National Geographic, Bloomberg Business, Juxtapoz, Dwell, The Economist... around 7,000 titles, and ~75,000 SKUs. I supported their mobile UI & UX design - covering iOS, Android, Win8, RIM & OEM devices. I helped the team of PM's & engineers to shape our in-market app in an agile working environment. I led the design of future versions of the Zinio application, expanding the brand from an e-commerce solution to a platform of personalized reading and discovery of relevant long-form journalism.

Specialties

Mobile Design, Product Design, User Interface Design, User Experience Design, Information Architecture, Brand Development, Business Strategy
