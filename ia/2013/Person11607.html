Abang Edwin Syarif Agustin's Summary

Abang Edwin is recognized as an online community management pioneer on internet scene in Indonesia. He begin his journey on social media on 1998 since the terms of social media doesn't exist yet (not even web 2.0). Start experimenting by built several succesfull interest-based online community such as Music enthusiast, Volkswagen lovers, etc using mailing-list as a platform. When Tim O'reilly introduce the term "Web 2.0" which is described what online community is, he start moving forward into social networking by experimenting with almost all platform that we called social media.

His interest on human behavior on online community makes him even more into strategic part of community development specifically on social media.

He is a goal directed, result oriented with a strong social media and community management background, self motivated and innovative community manager.

His main objective is to be always the best in what he do by focusing on the quality of his work and being a part of the team success.

Specialties
