Elaine King, MBA, CFP®'s Summary

Elaine is an international author, speaker and certified financial planner (TM). Her award winning books include; Family and Matters Next Gen, La Familia y El Dinero Hecho Facil, Saltarin y el valor del dinero and Tu Plan de Vida en USA. She is also President of Family and Money Matters Institute, a non for profit organization.

Elaine has more than 16 years of experience advising high net-worth families in wealth & legacy planning. Her holistic approach includes both the qualitative and the quantitative aspects of wealth, focusing on trust based client relationships. She has constructed and led teams comprised of certified financial planners, estate and tax attorneys as well as financial analysts and statisticians. She pioneers a value based approach to improve competencies resulting in improved profitability
