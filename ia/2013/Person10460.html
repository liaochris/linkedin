Tina Marie Jones' Summary

Business Consulting * Training * Coaching * Public Speaking * Retreats * Workshops * Masterminds

Tina Marie Jones is a powerful force when it comes to personal development, leadership and communications. As she so bluntly puts it “I am not one to get over anything; I go through it!” Both personal and professional success require confronting obstacles head on and taking risks. What looks like a risk can actually turn out to be our biggest YES. The many years of experience, hundreds of clients testimonials and deep connections to people who are typically very hard to get to... having Tina Marie on your team opens doors to your dreams you never knew were possible. Whether your organization is challenged with improving team dynamics, inspiring creativity and motivation or increasing sales, engage Tina Marie. She has been named one of Houston's Most Dynamic Women, won accolades as a Female Entrepreneur of the Year, noted as one of the Top Rising Speakers (SpeakerWiki). Tina Marie Jones‘ clients include corporations, executives, non-profit organizations and everyday people seeking their own power. Tina Marie Jones is also the creator and host of the critically acclaimed Holistic Living Radio Show which receives 90,000 global listeners per week. Through her radio and television shows, Tina Marie has interviewed influencial leaders such as Deepak Chopra, Mary Morrissey and John Demartini, The Secret. In her spare time, Tina Marie takes her message to the prison system to teach those inside to embrace entrepreneurship and discover their potential. Her novel Hi Sexy! is in print along with other titles for positive human development.

Specialties: Corporate training, Business Consulting, Giving talks that have you think outside the box (and basically burn the damn box), Lifestyle Coaching, Philosopher, Adventuress, Mental frontier explorer, Professional "Door Opener", Author and person with a new opinion that you can use in your favor.
