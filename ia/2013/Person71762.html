Kerry Law's Summary

12 years' experience as a PR & Communications professional generating high-profile, positive national, regional and international media coverage, plus editorial content (print and online) for clients in the sectors Arts/Culture, Environment, Leisure/Tourism, Charity/Not-for-profit, Fashion, Retail, Eco/Sustainable Lifestyle, amongst others.

I favour an intelligent, media-savvy and creative approach to communications.

Specialties:
- Strategic communications development
- Media relations
- Crisis PR and reputation management
- Product launches
- Event PR
- Social media management and integration
- Media training
- in-house/off-site press office/team management
- Copywriting plus editing/proof-reading
- Feature writing and blogging

I'm also the Founding Editor of online eco travel magazine Goodtrippers - visit http://www.goodtrippers.co.uk - involved in writing and editing content, commissioning content from guest bloggers, managing the related social media feeds, and monetising the site (along with a bit of design and coding when needed!).
