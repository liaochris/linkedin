Ken Arendt's Summary

Ken is currently a UX Designer at Education Elements. Education Elements helps schools design the right blended learning solutions for them, and works side by side with leaders and teachers to embed online content and data into daily instruction. Ed Elements has also developed a streamlined technology platform (the Hybrid Learning Management System) that that makes it easy and effective for leaders, teachers and students to blend online learning into their school.

Prior to working at Ed Elements, Ken was a Project Manager at Striiv, Inc. Striiv's walkathon, games, and challenges are designed to motivate and help people spend more time together while getting fit, giving back, and having fun.

Prior to working at Striiv, Ken worked at Space Machine for over 11 years and was in charge of the company's product planning and development. Ken has experience and expertise in the design and development of human machine interface for car navigation software on SmartPhones, tablets, PNDs, and for in-dash AVN head units in collaboration with tier-1 clients for the automotive industry.

In the past, Ken had also developed various websites and e-commerce stores for the corporation & the company's products, and supervised logistical operations at Space Machine. Ken had also worked closely with ODM partners from Korea, Taiwan, and China to bring together high-quality hardware devices, components, and accessories.

Ken's prior experience includes, product development & planning, project management, graphic & visual design, web design & e-commerce, UI & UX design, industrial design, 3D modeling, rapid prototyping and packaging. Ken holds a B.F.A. in Industrial Design with a minor in Graphic Design from the University of Illinois at Chicago.

Specialties: Proven Software Project Leader, Software User Interface, User Experience and Visual Design on Mobile Devices & Tablets.
