Mason Duchatschek's Summary

Thought leader whose creative ideas have been featured in Selling Power magazine, The New York Times, Entrepreneur magazine, Newsweek and numerous other national publications and media outlets. Co-author of the book "Sales Utopia: How to Get the Right People, Doing the Right Things, Enough Times" with Allen Minster. Business expert who knows how to solve common business problems before they occur.

Meeting challenges related to people:

Knows how to help companies prevent hiring disasters, increase sales, minimize labor costs, increase individual and team productivity, and turn human resource information into a corporate asset and competitive advantage.

In the bestselling book "Good to Great" the author Jim Collins advises business executives to get the right people on the bus and into the right seats. The "bus" of course is their business. Teaches executives HOW to do that using pre employment tests (psychometric tests) and employment assessments.

See YouTube.com/ReverseRiskConsult, Facebook.com/ReverseRiskConsulting and Twitter.com/ReverseRisk

Meeting challenges related to business development:

Knows how to leverage web video marketing, YouTube marketing, mobile marketing, Twitter marketing, Facebook marketing, Pinterest marketing and other social media tools to help people attract qualified leads & convert them into clients online.

Keynote speaker, social media strategist, social media expert and social media training resource. See YouTube.com/buildatribe, Facebook.com/buildatribe and Twitter.com/buildtribes.

Meeting challenges related to drug free teens, schools and community drug prevention:

Donates free home drug tests to schools, law enforcement and parent coalitions to give kids a "socially acceptable" excuse to reject peer pressure (that often gets worse when kids say "no" to drugs). Works in partnership with hundreds of local communities across the United States.

See YouTube.com/TestMyTeen, Facebook.com/TestMyTeen and Twitter.com/TestMyTeen.

Specialties
