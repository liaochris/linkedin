Dina Emser's Summary

I provide private and group coaching, training and keynote speaking to share compelling information and to support established and emerging leaders in the following ways:
* to gain confidence in their unique strengths and learn to use them to high advantage,
* to reprioritize high payoff activities to significantly increase productivity while reducing stress,
* to clearly define goals and design a strategic career roadmap to accelerate professional growth,
* to improve communication skills to address workplace challenges while improving team performance and moral.

Specialties

Leadership coaching for established and emerging leaders.
Leadership teams in professional service firms and schools.
Small business owners who want to increase their leadership and service.
Parents who consider their leadership at home to be a high priority.
