Jacqueline Lichtenberg's Summary

Currently, my novel series, Sime~Gen is being developed as a Video Game by Loreful. http://ambrovx.com & https://www.facebook.com/ambrovx for information.

I'm a professional freelance fiction writer. I am creator of the Sime~Gen Universe, primary author of Star Trek Lives!, founder of the Star Trek Welcommittee, creator of the term Intimate Adventure for the newly identified plot archetype, winner of the Galaxy Award for Spirituality in Science Fiction and the first Romantic Times Awards for Best Science Fiction Novel. For many years, I headed the Science Fiction Writers of America Speaker's Bureau and am a veteran public speaker.

My work is now in e-book form on Amazon Kindle and via Wildside Press on fictionwise.com, in audio-dramatization and on XM Satellite Radio. I have been sf/f reviewer for The Monthly Aspectarian since 1993. With Professor Jean Lorrah, I teach sf/f writing online via Kabbalah, mysticism and Astrology. Bio and Bibliography at www.simegen.com/jl/

Specialties: Current trends in mixed genres such as Paranormal Romance. I write Vampire Romance and blog with 6 other Alien Romance writers. I've edited an anthology and am currently involved in feature film scriptwriting.

Here is a web radio interview with Jacqueline Lichtenberg on writing craft.
http://www.blogtalkradio.com/pwrnetwork/2013/09/10/crazy-tuesday
