Kevin E. Henderson, MBA, PMP's Summary

Kevin E. Henderson, MBA, PMP is a dynamic and skilled business and information technology professional with over ten years experience in various business and information technology roles. Mr. Henderson has worked in the technology and consulting industries for several years in various roles to include software engineer, IT process/compliance consultant, and technology manager. Kevin is currently employed with Microsoft as a Senior Technical Account Manager in the Premier Services division. Kevin is also the founder and owner of Henderson Alliance, LLC a boutique Business and Information Technology Consulting firm servicing small to mid-size FORTUNE 100 companies. Mr. Henderson is also a faculty member of the University of Phoenix where he teaches business, general management, and technology curriculum to students pursing Bachelors and Masters degrees.

Kevin received his Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics from Winston-Salem State University. Kevin went on to further his education at Wake Forest University obtaining a Masters of Business Administration (MBA) degree.

Kevin holds several professional and technical certifications to include the Project Management Institutes Project Management Professional (PMP) certification, Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP), IT Supplier Manager Certification, and he’s also a Six Sigma Green Belt (SSGB).

Kevin serves on the executive board of the National Black MBA Association Charlotte Chapter as Vice President of Operations and member of several operating committees. Kevin is also a member of several other civic and professional organizations in the Charlotte, NC area.

Kevin resides in Charlotte, NC with his wife Rashanna where they have lived the past five years. Kevin enjoys golf, reading and active investing in the equity and currency markets.

Kevin can be contacted via email and on the web at Kevin.Henderson.MBA@Live.com and www.kevinehenderson.com respectively.
