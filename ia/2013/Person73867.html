Kelly Ann Collins' Summary

Kelly Ann Collins specializes in viral media, social media, content management and strategy, public relations and marketing.

Her experience includes work with USAToday.com, USDA, the House of Representatives, The Associated Press, Lifetime Television, Chicago Bulls Charities, The Washington Times and American Idol.

From 2005 to 2008, she worked on content strategy, push communications, e-newsletters, partnerships, microbrand creation and internal public relations for AOL Co-Founder Steve Case's Revolution Health, which is now a part of Everyday Health. She also founded Glittarazzi, a pop-culture and pop-politics website (4.2M visits per month), which went from a local blog to a national online destination within three years.

She has also developed and helped to implement promotions, partnerships and marketing plans for several brands – such as Vitamin Water, Absolut Vodka, Veuve Clicquot Ponsardin champagne, VH1 Save The Music, Charity Buzz, Red Bull, Popchips and Change.org.

Kelly Ann is a well-known influencer in Washington, D.C., community and beyond. She's appeared in Guest Of A Guest and New York Social Diary for her social endeavors. She also became an advocate for telecommuting when CNN featured her on its homepage several days in a row in 2013.
