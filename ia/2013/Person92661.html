Michael Long, MPA, CAE's Summary

With over 13 years experience in leadership positions in national, regional, local trade associations across business development, marketing, engineering, medical and manufacturing industries, Michael has a thorough understanding and diverse perspective of association management.

Michael specializes in helping association leaders realize strategic clarity, providing financial, programmatic, and marketing communications guidance, and developing cost-effective innovative approaches to emerging challenges associations face in engaging with their stakeholders.

Michael is well-versed in innovative approaches to member, Volunteer, sponsor, nonmember constituent and younger generation engagement.
