Joseph F. Fournier II's Summary

Over 20 years of learning and performance experience has given me the opportunity to work with some amazing individuals to produce interesting solutions to performance problems. Most, but not all, of my experience has been with larger corporations.

I'm particularly interested in social learning, creativity, invention, and innovation…and of course, leveraging technology as a multiplier for learning.

I usually speak at a couple conferences a year, and thrive on the exchange of ideas and the process of collaborative innovation. I'm a published author, and I'm currently working on a couple more books that should be complete in 2013.

My recent learning pursuits include the science behind creativity, and unconscious thinking, learning, and behavior.
