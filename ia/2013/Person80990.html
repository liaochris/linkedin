Linda Devine's Summary

As a born entrepreneur, Linda Devine has established several very successful businesses in the field of Holistic Health and Promotions. As a Success and Wellness Coach Linda helps women reach their full potential as individuals and become flourishing and profitable entrepreneurs using Law of Attraction techniques and other positive and valuable exercises. In her role as Event Coordinator, Linda has organized a very successful annual Wellness Expo to provide a venue for practitioners to present their talents to the public and for individuals to experience first hand the benefits of alternative health options.

Linda started mentoring and coaching professionally in 2005 to help other women deal with the sometimes overwhelming prospect of starting a new business, building self confidence and leading healthy lives.

Prior to establishing her coaching business, Linda spent over 20 years in sales & marketing and management positions in both the corporate and non profit sectors. Her experience both in life and in business has provided her with a caring and compassionate demeanour to assist others.

Linda has always enjoyed being a key player and using her creative talents to generate interest, raise awareness and overall benefit the organization being represented. With her enthusiasm and positive attitude, Linda brings excitement and energy into any new challenge that comes her way.
