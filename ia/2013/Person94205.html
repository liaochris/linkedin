Mike Hambright's Summary

Professional Real Estate investor, mentor and coach that has bought and sold hundreds of houses in the past few years alone. While I love rehabbing houses, watching the transformation and working through challenges, what I love most is the flexibility and freedom that investing provides my family.

It's made such an impression on me, that I'm now also recruiting and mentoring investors to join the HomeVestors family from coast to coast, and am personally mentoring more than 50 franchisees across the country. Franchisees in the HomeVestors system (We Buy Ugly Houses) have purchased over 50,000 houses, making us by far the largest home buying company in America. I look forward to helping others achieve their goals through real estate investing. We Buy Ugly Houses, Real Estate Investing. Learn more at: www.uglyopportunities.com

Specialties: Innovative thinking, strategy development, operational and process improvement, opportunity identification and exploitation, challenging status quo, getting things DONE (execution)! We Buy Ugly Houses - Real Estate Investor, Real Estate Investing, real estate investing, wholesaling real estate, rehabbing real estate, flipping properties, real estate mentoring and real estate coaching.
