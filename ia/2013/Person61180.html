Jeff Lloyd's Summary

Jeffrey Lloyd is an engineering manager with over 30 years’ experience in engineering for mining, industrial and utilities applications. He has a degree in electrical engineering and specialises in application of systems and information technology to engineering, industrial and business processes.

His objective is to lead professional multidiscipline engineering services business through practical knowledge management, business process management and continual improvement practices.

Specialties: Jeff specialises in engineering management and team building with a focus on technical and practical quality management. He has a background specialisation in electrical and automation engineering, complex systems project planning and development of technology-based engineering services. He also has experience in engineering technical process analysis and software tools development.
