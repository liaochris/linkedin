Krista Neher's Summary

Having spent almost 6 years in marketing and Finance at Procter & Gamble, Krista has deep roots in traditional marketing and brand building, having worked on some of the biggest and most successful brand launches.

Krista continued to build her experience in marketing strategy and brand building as the VP Marketing at an Internet start-up. At the start-up, Krista became one of the first social media marketing professionals, before social media was even a recognized term. She ran one of the first corporate Twitter accounts, a popular corporate blog and also experimented with marketing on MySpace, Facebook, LinkedIn and YouTube. Krista became a respected pioneer in social media and digital marketing, attracting over 40,000 users to the website only 4 months after the launch.

Krista went on to create Boot Camp Digital, an organization that specializes in social media training. Krista has provided training to countless large businesses like P&G, GE, Macy's and the United States Senate as well as small and medium companies. As a social media educator she also created one of the first accredited social media certification programs in the world. Boot Camp Digital now trains small and large organizations through our Boot Camp training programs and Social Media Masters Circle.

In addition, Krista is a sought after professional speaker proving presentations to corporations, trade shows, conferences, businesses and conventions. She has spoken internationally on a variety of topics including social media, brand building, personal brand building, start-ups, entrepreneurship and college student use of social media.

Krista is also a best-selling marketing author. She wrote the Social Media Field Guide in 2010 to help businesses navigate the complex social media landscape. Krista is also the co-author of Social Media Marketing: A Strategic Approach, one of the first textbooks written about social media marketing.
