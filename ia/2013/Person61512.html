Jelena Woehr's Summary

I manage community and social media for the world's largest publishing platform of its kind. Our community includes more than half a million freelance writers. We develop your unique voices and give you the opportunity to share stories with Yahoo's audience of millions.

Best career moments so far: Launching Yahoo Contributor Academy, telling the Yahoo Contributor Network story as a keynote speaker at Barkworld Expo 2011, and discussing millennial work habits with Peter Shankman in a super-session at BlogWorld East 2012. I also manage social media strategy, develop educational materials, support sales, manage content programs, and much more.

My past experiences include a successful foray into Web journalism with Disaboom.com, including covering the 2008 Democratic National Convention as a credentialed blogger. The Disabled Politico blog, to which I contributed extensively, was selected for archival in the Library of Congress. In addition to serving as Senior Political Writer, I also worked as Senior Social Marketer, overseeing content promotion and social strategy.

I've also enjoyed several opportunities to explore executive communications and social strategy. I am particularly passionate about helping extraordinary leaders to grow their personal brands. Mentorship and inspiration drive personal and business success; therefore, connecting great executives with industry audiences can create viral motivation and inspiration.

Current obsession = Promoting an industry-wide understanding of difference-driven symbiosis between community management and customer care. (If you know what I mean, we should probably connect and talk shop.)
