Chawn Crawley's Summary

•Following a career in telecommunications, I took my long-time hobby of photography and moved it to the center stage by establishing "Port City Photography", which quickly became Oswego's premier portrait studio.
Recently, I changed the name to "Chawn Crawley Photography" in order to be more easily identified outside the immediate area.

•While I continue to provide a range of portrait services, I've begun to specialize in Professional Headshots, as well as Corporate Portraiture.
These two disciplines are, in my opinion, some of the most misunderstood and extremely relevant forms of photography available today.

•My website, www.chawncrawley.com is a location I keep frequently updated; including a blog which I use almost exclusively to educate and inform working professionals of the need to maintain high quality images for successful marketing.

Specialties

The Professional Headshot/Executive Portrait is a crucial aspect of every individual's personal branding. I use the most advanced technologies combined with a bit skill, but the real trick is that I create an atmosphere that's fun and allows the client and me to have fun while we're doing the shoot. It's that "rapport" that we build that yields the signature images your viewers remember-even after seeing a single time.
