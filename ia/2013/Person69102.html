Josh Page's Summary

I work with people to help them create a complete visual style for their business, employing a pragmatic approach throwing out the "design for design's sake".

Its not about whether I like it or you like it, but will your customers relate to it?

With over 15 years of visual communication and strategic brand design experience working in London and Auckland for local, national and global brands, I have had the opportunity of working with a large range of clients including guinness, jamie oliver, l'oréal, citibank, farmlands, warehouse stationery, harvey furnishings, briscoes, pgg wrightson, living and giving, palmers, meadow fresh, manukau now, porsche nz polo open, blunt umbrellas, West Auckland Trusts, trusts arena.
