Lorna Sixsmith's Summary

My aim is to show people how to blog and use social media effectively for their business and with enjoyment. I set up Write on Track in 2011 having achieved finalist status with my Garrendenny Lane business blog in two national awards. I have also achieved significant free press coverage for my online business and having taught at secondary and tertiary levels, I now enjoy teaching business people how to generate more traffic and sales for their website and business. We deliver a number of courses and also offer one to one training, copy writing and ghost blogging.

I co-founded KLCK Bloggers Network in 2010 with the aim of helping business to start and improve their blogging. I co-organised the Bloggers International conference in 2011 and am one of three organisers of the Blog Awards Ireland for 2012.

I love social media, blogging and networking online and see these as essential tools for successful businesses.

Specialities: Online marketing, social media marketing, blogging, search engine optimisation strategy building, Linked In, Twitter, Pinterest, social media training
