Katie Howison's Summary

Katie Howison -- a graduate of Michigan State University and a devoted Spartans fan -- began her Public Relations career as a copywriter for a mid-Michigan advertising agency before taking a leap of faith to form her own business, Kate Communications, in Midland, MI.

After 15 years of successfully serving local businesses, foundations and global corporations, Katie came to a career crossroads and seeking change, decided to work directly for a Fortune 40 company to help drive business results and reputation. As a senior leader for Executive Communications and for Manufacturing & Engineering, she coached top executives, wrote keynote speeches, prepared press materials, coordinated publicity, and developed and implemented a variety of internal and external communications plans among other activities.

Katie recently returned to her entrepreneurial roots, re-opening Kate Communications where she specializes in strategic communications planning, executive speechwriting, high-quality copywriting and image-building.

Katie's goal is to help individuals and businesses succeed with compelling communications that are tailored to their needs and that deliver meaningful results. She is focused on solving challenges with creative solutions that promote the right messages to the right people at the right time. And, she is committed to developing simple yet well-integrated plans that offer the best combination of marketing tools -- traditional and social media -- to drive success.

Katie has a bias toward excellence, integrity and teamwork, and is known for providing dependable and friendly service. She is always open to new opportunities and to collaborating with other experts to further drive results.
