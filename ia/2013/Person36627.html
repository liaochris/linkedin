Don Dingee's Summary

The new stories of social computing are fascinating to me. Combining mobile devices, embedded computing, wireless sensor networks, and social business, the area of social computing is transforming society and the way people connect.

I'm bringing my experience in social computing to share stories, and help audiences grasp the possibilities. I'm available for speaking engagements, contributed editorial and guest blogging, interviews, panel and webcast moderator roles, conference programming, and more items where you need a creative voice on social computing topics.

I work with B2B companies developing strategy, content, and online and social media programs. As an experienced product marketing leader, I use the Pragmatic Marketing framework to set the tone and guide activity. I've got extensive experience in customer and prospect interviewing to help uncover product needs and develop the right requirements.

In addition to my blog, I'm a blogger at EmbeddedSoftwareStore.com and SemiWiki.com, and I'm currently working on a new book to be announced soon.

Specialties

Author, speaker, broadcaster, editor, online strategy, social media, evangelist, technology strategy, marketing management, change agent, white papers, webinars, content

Don Dingee's Patents

  • Bidirectional data interface for a processor embedded in a self-propelled vehicle

    • United States Patent 5,214,584
    Inventors: Don Dingee

    In a self-propelled vehicle such as a shoulder-fired missile, a bidirectional data interface between a missile processor and an external apparatus includes a pair of function signal paths which extend partly outside the missile. A first component of the interface uses one of the signal paths to couple reprogramming information to the missile processor in response to a prompt from the processor. A second component of the interface responds to a periodic signal on the second signal path to receive missile processor data which the missile processor connects to the first signal path in synchronism with the periodic signal.
