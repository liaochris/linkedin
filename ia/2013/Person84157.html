Chad Magendanz's Summary

Over 20 years experience in software application development, including popular products such as Microsoft Office and Windows. Specialty in rapid prototyping for interaction design and user research, with the technical depth to use modern frameworks like Windows Presentation Foundation where the results can easily migrate to the shipping product.
