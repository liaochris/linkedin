Eric Gibble's Summary

Accompanied by experience in immigration policy analysis, direct and grassroots lobbying, new media outreach, and web content management, I am more than just a proven communication professional.

Upon graduating from Cabrini College in 2011 with a BA in Communication, I was determined to advocate for systemic change from inside the beltway. I participated in the one-year Associate Program at NETWORK Lobby to make this a reality. I coordinated logistics for the national Nuns on the Bus campaign, lobbied lawmakers for immigration reform, and mobilized hundreds of progressive Catholics across the country prior to the 2012 election.

After NETWORK, I worked as an Account Executive with the Hill Group, a strategy and communication firm in Bethesda, MD. In this position, I executed logistical and technical contract tasks for various offices within the National Institutes of Health.
