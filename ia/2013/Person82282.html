Julie Frey's Summary

I have been in the field of Massage Therapy work for over 13 years now and through my journey, I have gained a great knowledge through experience as well as education. I have had the opportunity to work with chiropractors, athletes, and business men and women, who have all played a part in helping me to become the therapist that I am today. My goal as a therapist is to help my clients learn to live a healthier lifestyle through the many benefits of massage therapy. My mission in life is to make people feel better, by reducing stress, pain, and giving them a overall sense of well being.

Specialties

In my practice I have worked with people of all types and I specialize my work to fit the individual's needs. The techniques used are myofascial release, neuromuscular therapy, deep tissue, trigger point therapy, stretching, and swedish massage strokes.
