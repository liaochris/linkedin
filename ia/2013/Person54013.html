Huishu Jia's Summary

A London based designer & artist, just got a MA degree in Design Products under Onkar Kular, Dash MacDonald and Jimmy Loizeau in Royal College of Art. I'm interested in a wide range of designs, especially about emotions, facial expressions and sound.

I'm also an enthusiast about motor sports, following every race through broadcasting. I've also been to Shanghai International Circuit for Chinese GP, Barcelona for Spanish GP, Silverstone for British GP and Brands Hatch for DTM. What's more, in order to be even closer to this sport I became a marshal at Brands Hatch.
