George Kongalath's Summary

Innovator at heart - Looks for the silver lining in the darkest of clouds. Very top line focused, because if there is no top line - it doesn't really matter how much you squeeze the operations - you just won't exist.

Coming out of the recession - cost efficiency has to be the watchwords of the day. Model Based Testing is of key value to the Agile methodology.

Created Ideabytes to provide MBT launch support - globally. Have put together an experienced team to deliver the efficiencies that companies are looking for.

Driving innovations that bring returns in short cycle. Believe a product life cycle has to be 90-180 days - where profitability is the highest. After this one better have the next hot tamale.

An avid toastmaster and passionate speaker. Aspire to motivate entrepreneurs and small businesses to make it big. Creating club number 3 Ideabytes Toastmasters - in Hyderabad, India.

Very passionate about photography and high quality lenses. Started posting my pictures on www.Flickr.COM. Search for lmcgeko. Feel free to download for pleasure. Copyright for prints and commercial use. RAW files Nikon NEF's available.
