Emma Hignett's Summary

I am a voice over artist working out of my own, fully equipped studio with ISDN. I deliver high quality voice overs for clients around the world.

Clients include Microsoft, Bing, Yahoo, Virgin Money, Paypal, Alton Towers, Samsung, Transport for London, Tommee Tippee, Mothercare, VISA, Cisco and many more.

I am well known as the 'Voice of London's Buses', London's Overground Trains and the London Rivervus, heard by more than 9 million people who daily in London's. I've recorded an estimated 45,000 announcements for this system.

As well as transport announcements, my voiceover work ranges from corporate films to commercials, from telephone systems to public address systems. For more information, please visit www.emmahignett.com.

A quick note on how I got here - I spent 13 years as a television and radio presenter, and am known as a television weather girl in Wales and the West Country and for presenting breakfast shows on Red Dragon FM in South Wales and Capital Gold in London. Amongst the highlights of my broadcasting was the opportunty to co-host a radio show with Jerry Springer across the Capital Gold network.

As well as operating my voice over business, I have a public relations consultancy.

Specialties: British female voice over, corporate voice over, voice over for film, voiceover for commercials, voice over recoring, public relations, broadcast PR, crisis management, media training, radio and television broadcasting, event hosting/ compere.
