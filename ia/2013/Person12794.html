Ben Buie's Summary

Howdy! My name is Ben Buie (pronounced /ˈbo͞o-ē/ like in the ocean). I’m passionate/obsessed with technology and entrepreneurship. I run a website & YouTube channel called MyNiceTie and am recovering from a recent failure to build the MOST fun place online to discover cool, new products--HappyFreebie.

MyNiceTie is pretty cool because we use 20% of all revenue to microfinance entrepreneurs in third world countries. As of May 2013, MyNiceTie has generated 18.5 million YouTube views, and has helped micro-finance 401 entrepreneurs in 40 countries.

After launching MyNiceTie, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from BYU in business with an emphasis in entrepreneurship. With the experience I gained from MyNiceTie I launched a web development company building Magento ecommerce websites for clients. Unfortunately, I ran out of runway and had to fall back on my degree with a job as an analyst at Payless Headquarters in Topeka.

After seeing how a big, traditional corporation operates, I decided to transition back into the startup life with an MBA from CU-Boulder. While at CU, I worked to improve and expand the entrepreneurial ecosystem by leading the Graduate Entrepreneurs Association, starting a club called StartupCU, and helping create a space for entrepreneurship at CU.

On a more personal level, I’m a dad of two energetic boys (2 and 4) and therefore, a wrestler on the living room floor, a husband to my lovely wife, a player of guitar, a biker (the pedal-bike-ride-to-work kind), a devour-er of Reece’s Pieces, an artist who is starving for time to practice, a supporter of freedom and liberty, a fluent speaker of Russian, and a meticulous coder. Oh, and I make amazing rolls. :)
