Debbie Benami-Rahm's Summary

With over 20 years of Human Resources experience, Debbie Benami-Rahm has worked with
start-ups, Fortune 300 and Fortune 500 corporations to provide talent acquisition, talent management and talent development building talent pipelines, increasing organizational effectiveness, enhancing employee performance and driving dynamic business results.

Debbie has managed and led strategic sourcing across all levels of organizations from entry level to senior executives. She has provided companies with top talent, including early identification of high potentials, through college recruitment and internal talent management programs in the financial services, entertainment, banking, hospitality, automotive, telecommunications, healthcare, higher education, airline and nonprofit industries.

In 2008, following her passion for the entertainment industry, she expanded her diverse portfolio of corporate talent acquisition and talent management experience to include freelance work on the casting side of the Film/TV industry, while running her own HR consulting business.

Debbie has provided highly confidential executive coaching including assessments, debriefings and follow up development plan consultations for private and corporate clients to increase talent retention.

In addition to her HR expertise, Debbie is an acclaimed keynote speaker and Professional Member of the National Speakers Association, who has provided her signature "Tap Your Talent"™ speech to universities, corporate leadership retreats and professional association audiences around the country. She is recognized as a career expert by the media: print, digital and radio. Her articles and career expert interviews have appeared in dozens of print and online publications including wsj.com.

Specialties: Talent Acquisition. Recruitment. College Recruiting. Talent Management. Talent Development. Executive Coaching, Career Coaching. Leadership Development. Training. Career Development, Professional Speaker.
