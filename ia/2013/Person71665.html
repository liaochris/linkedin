Kacey Donston's Summary

15 years of experience in all aspects of website development, promotion and optimization. Extensive web development experience, with over 350 websites built. 12 years of webmaster and website maintenance experience and the current webmaster in charge of all updates for over 100 websites and clients. In the SEO/SEM area, I have gained hundreds of page one and even #1 listings for websites. Effective link building is a crucial aspect of the SEO process and I have extensive experience with all types of link building including attracting natural links.

For small, home and local business clients, I have created professional looking, easy to maintain websites at a reasonable cost.

Current clients include published authors, renowned health and life coaches, leading companies in their niches, as well as e-commerce stores offering a wide range of products, including jewelry, wedding invitations, eye wear, furniture, industrial supplies, gourmet foods, electronics, office supplies, pet items, gifts and handmade items.

I also create effective sites for local businesses and offer local search engine marketing.

Developing high quality informational sites is also a specialty, and I have developed and promoted sites from idea to completion, in many niches.

I also create informational and business apps for the iPhone and Android platforms.

Social media marketing also offered, and I help clients create and maintain their social media presence.

I develop e-books for the Amazon Kindle Marketplace and also help clients get their books onto Kindle with proofreading, formatting and promotion services specifically for Kindle.

I work everyday with popular web languages and software programs, including HTML, PHP and MySQL and have extensive expertise with content management systems and popular shopping carts and scripts, including Wordpress, Magento, Shopify, OScommerce and more.

Want to develop your idea for a website but have no idea where to start? Contact me for a consultation!
