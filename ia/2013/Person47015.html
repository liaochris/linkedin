Garth Graham's Experience

Director

STRATMOR GROUP

March 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Fort Lauderdale, FL

Garth Graham heads STRATMOR’s marketing strategy and execution practice, which focuses on lead generation and lead management methods and practices primarily for the consumer direct and retail mortgage origination channels. Garth has over 25 years of experience in sales and marketing, from Fortune 500 companies to successful startups, including management of two of the most successful e-commerce platforms.

STRATMOR has been in business for 31 years serving exclusively the mortgage banking sector of the financial services business. Our five principals bring deep exposure to the business from all vantage points. When you hire STRATMOR, you get years of experience typically at a fixed price based on the scale of the engagement. What you will not get is a runaway project with billable hours exceeding the value of the engagement. Our specialties include:

Strategy Consulting - helping banks and independent mortgage bankers determine profitability, risks and opportunities for various business channels.

Mergers and Acquisitions - STRATMOR has participated in buy or sell transactions in the industry. We help companies choose the right investment partners, leveraging our years of experience and rich understand of profitability and operational metrics.

IT Consulting - we have participated in over 25 LOS procurement projects in the the past two years. Get unbiased insights on all the major LOS providers BEFORE you invest.

Peer Group and Benchmarking - STRATMOR is best known for its ongoing partnership with the MBA in conducting the MBA/STRATMOR Peer Group Benchmarking Program, providing insight to top mortgage firms since 1998.
