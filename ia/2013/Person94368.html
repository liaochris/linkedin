Mike Maney's Summary

I'm a devoted father and husband, a former Calvin Klein underwear model, and a stimulator of tech influencers. Like Meatloaf says, two out of three ain't bad.

I'm guessing, however, you aren't here to see pictures of my kids, or to listen to fictional tales of the wild parties I attended with Marky Mark. So let me tell you about that last part.

My name is Mike Maney (@the_spinmd). I founded a communications consultancy called maney:digital (maneydigital.com). The firm was created in January 2013 to help startup founders and corporate executives navigate the outer bands of their business strategy, helping them engage communities and influencers their existing PR and marketing programs don't reach.

The foundation of maney:digital is based on more than 20 years of experience. Over the past two decades, I've:

* Been a member of the agency tribe, both big (Ogilvy, GCI, Rowland) and small (Poppe Tyson, Zer0to5ive).

* Been part of great Fortune 500 corporate communications teams (IBM, AT&T, Alcatel-Lucent, Unisys) where I've led global communications strategies for high profile, culture shifting emerging business divisions and served as lead counsel to their senior executive teams.

* Rolled up my sleeves to drive successful communications programs for several hot startups (Cloudability, Mashery, MindTouch, ProgrammableWeb, SourceForge).

* And I've had the luck to work alongside some of the brightest minds in technology, including Dennis Ritchie, co-creator of Unix; John Patrick, a pioneer of IBM’s early entry into the web and Linux; Larry Prusak, one of the founding fathers of knowledge management; Jeff Taylor, founder of Monster.com; and Rob Malda and Jeff Bates, the brains behind Slashdot.

I co-founded Corporate Rebels United, advise Carvoyant and FullContact, and am the president of Mobile Monday Mid-Atlantic.

If you want to see why I'm not a professional underwear model, check out these videos of me speaking at various events: http://maneydigital.com/media
