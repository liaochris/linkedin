Michael De Pippo's Summary

Michael De Pippo is a Freelance art director and graphic designer, currently living in Toronto, Canada. He has been working in both traditional and digital advertising for close to 10 years, with experience at agencies such as Ogilvy, Grip Limited, Leo Burnett, BBDO, Sid Lee, Wunderman, Juniper Park and more.

Michael has recently started participating in group exhibitions at different international art galleries, as well as creating original poster art for properties such as the Muppets, Disney/Pixar, Peanuts, Looney Tunes and more.

Specialties: Ideation, Art Direction, Graphic Design, Poster Design, Illustration, Typography, Branding and Identity.
