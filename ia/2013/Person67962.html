Jon Leeke's Summary

Lead 3D visualisation artist, Part 1 RIBA, with a keen eye for detail. Photorealistic internal and external 3D rendering using Cinema 4D with Vray. Extensive post production skills using Photoshop, including photomontaging and experience with After Effects for animation compositing.

Project management skills with good team working ability and leadership. Innate sense of design and creativity enjoying innovative thought and efficiencies. Ability to take a process from conceptual massing to create fully detailed, high-end image production.

Specialties

- Architectural CAD packages (Vectorworks) for 2D drawings and 3D modelling
- Vray - create materials and textures, scene lighting within efficient render settings for optimal render times
- Cinema 4D
- Post production using Photoshop
- Architectural experience - interpret architectural drawings - plans, sections and elevations
