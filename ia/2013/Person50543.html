Divya Burton's Summary

A strategic thinker that builds strong foundation for a new brand and enhances the growth for existing brands by developing a customized marketing mix, using key components such as Public Relations (PR) including Press-Release writing, Content Development and Strategic Social Media Planning (SSMP), specific to the client's industry & target market.

Uses over 15 years of strong planning & analysis background to understand the brand's competition and unique selling proposition (points), before skillfully executing innovative "Think Outside the Lines | Not Just the Box" strategies.

After an in depth understanding of the client's vision for growth and success for their brand, clearly defined project initiatives are managed (Project Management Planning) to stay within deadline and budget.

Goal: To provide hands on consulting for brand development, target market positioning, building emotional brand equity, engagement and loyalty. Concentrate on the details and develop a high revenue generating brand with a consistent message and promise.
