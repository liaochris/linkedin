Advertising & Marketing Director

Lil' Orbits

April 1993 – January 1995 (1 year 10 months)

Lil' Orbits manufactures mini donut making machines and sells them as a business opportunity. In a nutshell, the main focus of my job was to generate as many qualified sales leads at the lowest possible cost.

I decided how to spend the advertising budget, negotiated advertising space in magazines and other media, created the advertisements, and tracked the advertising results, wrote the direct mail package that was sent to the inquiries, including the product catalog. I was also the company publicity person, i.e. write and submit press releases.

Lil’ Orbits also exported these machines, along with other products, so my job also included placing international advertisements.

It was also my job to help figure ways for the mini donut operator to sell more donuts, so we could sell more mix. (Love that residual.)

During my time I created ads that generated more leads per dollar than any other ads produced in the previous 20 years of my employ. (Had to throw that in.)
