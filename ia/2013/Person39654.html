Ed Mullen's Summary

Currently practicing law and public policy advocacy in Chicago, IL. Representing clients in discrimination, employment, real estate, insurance, consumer protection, and contract matters, and advising several non-profits on formation, trademark and contract issues. Also consulting on the campaign of Linda J. Pauel for Cook County Judge and serving as campaign's fundraising chair. Available to represent clients and for legal-related contract projects.

Specialties: Commercial and consumer litigation (intellectual property, insurance coverage, professional liability, securities, real estate etc.); civil rights; legislative advocacy; public policy; public communications and advocacy.
