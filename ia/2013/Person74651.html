Kerry Brooks' Summary

Kerryellen Studios is an innovative design practice delivering sophisticated elegance to the spaces we inhabit.

Based in Margaret River and Perth, and an honour graduate of Curtin University for Interior Architecture, I provide comprehensive design services including interior architecture and design, furniture design and commissioned artworks.

My projects are inspired by an international background infused with an Australian finesse, a preference for natural materiality, refined details and an integral awareness for functionality.

I engage the client in all phases of the design process, thus ensuring a uniquely tailored project sensitive to the individuals' particulars and reflecting my concept of timeless distinction.
