Alvaro De la Cruz's Summary

I was born and raised in Germany, where I lived until February 2007. I moved to the US and settled in Miami, Florida where I attended St. Thomas University and majored in International Business and graduated with honors. Obtaining an education in the United States and also living here, I have had the opportunity to grow and develop both personally and professionally.
Growing up in Germany I was fortunate to attend one of the well-known private schools which had embedded a strong educational and ethical values that still remain with me today. Furthermore, being raised by UN diplomat parents gave me the opportunity to learn about the international affairs such as politics, economics, and social events at first hand which have been advantageous in my life and while doing my University Degree in International Business.
While pursuing my University Degree at St. Thomas I co-founded a non-profit organization to help German citizens have a smooth transition to the Miami/Florida culture during their business or leisure visits. The organization, GermansinMiami.com was an instant success and was featured in documentaries on two of the major German TV Channels, Pro7 and VOX.
