Eric Irvin, CISSP's Summary

Eric has more than 13 years of security and network experience, his focus is providing strategic direction to both mature and optimize the security posture, policies, procedures and processes of enterprises, which will minimize security risks and increase the operational efficiency of enterprises.

Eric's experience is in business and security objectives of Fortune 500 companies, and enhancing the long term strategic plans in financial, retail, energy, healthcare, government and manufacturing industries. Listening to and understanding enterprise business objectives, Eric has the ability to translate objectives into actionable, repeatable and reliable solutions.

His experience also has included directing the efforts of companies and client personnel on integrated Security audits, assessments of internal controls in connection with Sarbanes-Oxley Section 404, HIPAA, PCI, Graham-Leach-Bliley, attack and penetration security assessments, application source code vulnerability analyses, system implementation security/control reviews, and business process effectiveness reviews.

Eric has been the product specialist for different products for different vendors across MSSPs, IDS/IPS, SIEM, Cloud Providers, and others across service providers, channel, and enterprise opportunities.
