Mette Walsted's Summary

I am an experienced and passionate User Experience Designer with strong skills in visual and interaction design, human-centrered design processes, leadership and organisational change.

In my current position I am leading the UX team at e-conomic, striving to simplify the user experience for a highly diverse customer base.

With a background in visual communication I have been working with design in various contexts, touching upon a diverse range of projects, clients and companies. I have been freelancing, working in design agencies, collaborating with large-scale private and public sector organisations and now working within a multi-national agile product design environment.

I am passionate about UX design and the way we think about and work with design for digital user interfaces, always focusing on user needs and the complex psychology of us humans. I have come to believe in empathy, positive energy and team work as some of the main drivers for crafting great design that works for people.

Specialties: Visualising concepts and user insights, conceptualisation, UI design, interaction design, product development, leading design processes, analysing user behaviour, sketching, workshop facilitation.
