Jay Bower's Summary

Jay has broad strategic and implementation experience in the integration of brand, digital and direct marketing. He's led strategic and tactical engagements with over 275 companies ranging from Fortune 500 multinationals to start-ups. Previously, he served as Vice President and co-founder of the e-business consultancy Prescients, where he helped craft online strategies for PriceWaterhouse Coopers, Gartner Group, Media Metrix, Cendant (Century 21) and Verizon. Previously at Blau/Brann/EuroRSCG, Jay helped IBM develop web-based relationships with its largest corporate clients. At Bantam Doubleday Dell and Newbridge Communications, he directed continuity and customer relationship marketing (CRM) programs. Jay has spoken at numerous trade conferences and has been quoted (and misquoted) in Fortune, Forbes, USA Today, Entrepreneur and trade magazines from Direct to Ad Age. He is a published author (an unreadable book about software - Amazon sales rank 3,369,043!) He has a B.A. in Philosophy from Colgate University, an M.B.A. from The Stern School of Business, New York University, and completed the e-B2B Program at Harvard Business School.

Specialties: The integration of brand, digital, direct and social media marketing strategy and execution; analytics; search engine marketing; customer acquisition and retention; lead generation and conversion.
