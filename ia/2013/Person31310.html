Dave Sliwinski's Summary

Dave Sliwinski is a content specialist based in Charlotte, N.C. He has extensive experience working with both corporate and mid-level organizations to analyze, create, organize and manage their marketing and communications assets.

Dave has provided copywriting, content management, content strategy, and editorial design services to the financial services, automotive, engineering, environmental, land development, property management, hospitality, nonprofit, and publishing industries. In addition to his current role serving the financial services industry, some positions he has held include writer and magazine editor for a newspaper, marketing manager for an ENR 500 engineering firm, and breakfast cook for a neighborhood diner – each equally valuable in his education.

Specialties: He has extensive experience applying his copywriting and editing, graphic design, publishing layout, communication, sales, and technical skills to his clients’ projects. Dave is comfortable navigating the business landscape, and is proficient in industry-standard design and business software, as well as several enterprise CMS solutions.
