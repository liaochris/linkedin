Alex Visa's Summary

I am a marketing & communication professional with a strong focus on digital communication in general and online performance marketing in particular (SEO, SEM, Analytics, RTB, DSP, etc.)

In the current ever-changing consumer landscape, it is our duty as marketers to look at optimizing all levels of the consumer interaction with our brands.
From Zmot to 2-mot we have to plan, monitor and continuously optimize the consumer journey, and make sure we always offer the best in class product and customer service. This is (and will continue to be) the only secret to success.

In my 15 years sales, advertising & marketing experience, I had various roles, both client and agency side working with top clients like Orange, Vodafone, Rogers AT&T, Expedia, Club-Med, American Express, ING Bank, GM, Renault-Nissan, BMW, Ferrero, UPC, Nestle, etc
