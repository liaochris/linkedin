Gregory Herman-Giddens, JD, LLM, TEP, CFP's Summary

I am the founder of the estate planning, probate and tax firm, TrustCounsel. Our goal is to provide our clients with exceptional service in the areas of wealth planning, protection and management. At TrustCounsel we actively maintain knowledge of the current laws and best practices through attendance of national educational conferences and membership in leading professional organizations. Our main office is in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, with additional offices in South Florida (Miami) and New York City.

TrustCounsel offers services in many areas including Estate Planning, Probate, Taxation, Trust and Estate Administration, Asset Protection, Elder Law (including Medicaid Planning) Guardianship, Special Needs Planning, Business Formation, Charitable Gift Planning, and Non-Profit Organization Law.

I have been practicing law for 25 years, with over 20 years experience in Wealth Planning, Protection and Management. I am licensed to practice law in North Carolina, Florida, Tennessee and New York, and have qualified as a solicitor in England and Wales.

Over the years, I have been a frequent speaker, and have published articles in several publications including Taxation for Accountants; Taxation for Lawyers; North Carolina Lawyers Weekly, and North Carolina Bar Association Publications. TrustCounsel is the publisher of the nationally recognized and syndicated North Carolina Estate Planning Blog.

I volunteer my time with the Chatham County Chamber of Commerce, North Carolina Legal Aid and the North Carolina Center for Non-Profits Pro Bono Panel. I was the 2010 President of the Orange County Bar Association, and was selected as a 2011 North Carolina Super Lawyer in Estate Planning. I am currently serving as Secretary-Treasurer of the 15-B Judicial District Bar.

Credentials: Board Certified Specialist in Estate Planning and Probate Law. Certified Financial Planner. Registered Trust and Estate Practitioner.
