Maria Semple's Summary

I am an experienced prospect researcher, trainer and sought-after speaker on the topic of prospect research, email marketing, and simple social media strategies. I work with non-profit organizations, financial advisors, and business owners interested in finding and connecting with more prospects.

I am the author of two downloadable e-Books in my "Panning For Gold" series. These e-Books are packed with websites that will help both nonprofits and financial advisors connect with more high-net-worth individuals.

In 2009, I became a Certified Expert Presenter with Constant Contact and one of their Local Experts in 2012. I am available for group presentations and one-on-one consulting for email marketing. I specialize in leveraging your email marketing campaigns into social media sites, too. Free 60-day trials for Constant Contact at the following link:
