Dave Ferguson's Summary

I help business owners and independent professionals to face their frustrations, overcome their obstacles and optimize their opportunities.

With a passion for helping people to achieve more than they think possible, I help people to leverage their strengths to reach their goals. They see an immediate return on their investment.

"What's amazing about the process is that people begin to realize the progress they are making. That progress changes their perception of what is possible. That's an energizing experience for both of us. The pace of change increases, the possibilities multiply. Suddenly the challenges don't seem so great."

Ideas - Action - Results, Guaranteed!

Specialties

In person and tele coaching, graduate credit program for education professionals, strategic retreats for the owners/partners of closely held companies, and sales workshops. All coaching is individually tailored to to fit the needs of each client. Some programs provide professional CPE and CPDU credits for teachers and CPA's licensed in the State of Illinois.

Dave Ferguson's Experience

President and Head Coach

Lake County Business Coaching, Inc.

Public Company; Myself Only; Management Consulting industry

April 2005 – Present (8 years 9 months)

I provide business coaching to the owners and key management at small and mid-sized firms in all industries and market sectors. I also coach independent and commissioned professionals - financial services, education, medical, consulting and real estate.

I help them to leverage their strengths and talents to create the plans, and then the strategies, to reach their goals. Then I help hold them accountable for their results. I want them to see a real return on the investment they make in coaching and most see a return of 400% to 900%.

Lake County Business Coaching is the publisher of my books and was formed to hold and operate The Growth Coach Office 75493 in Lake County, IL. It does business as The Growth Coach.

Dave Ferguson's Publications

  • The Business Owner's Bible - Operating on Faith

    • Lake County Business Coaching, Inc.
    • December 31, 2011
    Authors: Dave Ferguson

    This is an inspiring collection of commentaries on overcoming the challenges business owners face every day. It’s about conquering fear and celebrating success, written by a small business owner who has helped hundreds of business owners improve their results and rediscover the joy in owning their business. The book provides an opportunity to reflect on what business is and how it needs to work to be successful. Moreover, it helps every business owner to recognize how their business can contribute to their life and not become something that takes it over. It’s a reflection on what works and what doesn’t, how to recognize opportunity and how to build on success. It’s about building a business that gives you income - and joy.
