Mike Kujanek's Summary

Mike Kujanek is the co-founder and Senior Vice President, Managing Director of SearchMarketers.com. Mike is responsible for top-level paid media relationships between SearchMarketers and its clients, comprising its capabilities in paid search, SEO, mobile, and display media. He also supports all business development efforts.

Mike was previously Sr Director of Global Paid Media at Covario, the back-to-back winner of the 2011 and 2012 OMMA Agency of the Year award in Search Marketing. He supported Covario's business expansion on the US East Coast and Europe.

Before Covario, Mike Kujanek was a Sr. Group Search Director at WPP's MediaCom Interaction in New York, NY. As a group lead, Mike oversaw paid search account teams, providing thought leadership in strategic development and tactical execution to achieve client goals.

Mike began his career in Search at Google in advertising sales. He has also worked in Google's Engineering department, supporting the human-based quality evaluation team in Santa Monica, CA for English and German languages. Prior to joining MediaCom in July 2008, Mike was Did-it Search Marketing's agency liaison for Business Analytics. He communicated new developments in the search landscape and proposed testing opportunities for all Did-it clients.

In the course of Mike's work, his clients have spanned all industry verticals, including Audi, Volkswagen, Dell Small Business, DIRECTV, Dell Home & Home Office (Consumer), Lenovo, Nikon, Erie Insurance, Intel, Brookstone, LetsTalk.com, Register.com, Revlon, Allianz, Shell (Pennzoil, Quaker State, Rotella), The SCOOTER Store, W3i-Ringtone.com, Warner Bros, Movies on Demand/CTAM, DC Comics, large affiliates and several small businesses.

He is fluent in German and competent in French, and graduated from International University Bremen in Germany with a degree in History and Integrated Social Sciences. Mike lives on Long Island’s south shore.
