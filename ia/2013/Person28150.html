Hello!

My name is Octavian Cioaca and I’m working as Opportunistic Pragmatic Front-End Engineer. Since 2006 I’m taking immediate advantage, often unethically of every CSS file.

One important side of my workflow is to review products development strategy, establish goals and lead face-to-face brainstorming sessions with A-Level executives on the marketing, technical and design sides of client's business.

Frequently I’m taking ownership of the delivery, timeliness and quality of the module assigned while working on multiple projects at one time.

My written and interpersonal skills are strong when interacting with clients via phone, email and in-person. I provide constructive criticism around wire-framing, creative, test setup and customer communication skills.

One person will always have a deep impact on the way I’ll approach my work. THE END USER!
