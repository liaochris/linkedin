Jay Marshall's Summary

I am a developer at heart, an architect by trade, and a Senior Technical Marketing Manager by day for VMware. I've spent more than a dozen years in enterprise Java and have most recently been developing in the "mobile web" space (HTML5). My passion for technology has helped launch multiple startups, legacy modernization projects, and bleeding edge application development and delivery initiatives.

My cross-industry experiences, along with a career working not only in corporate, but in the partner channel, as an entrepreneur, as a consultant, and directly for an industry-leading software vendor gives me valuable insight into all sides of the business cycle when it comes to IT. I consider this one of my biggest strengths. I am not purely solving technical problems or "geeking out" over technology for technologies' sake. I truly consider myself a business innovator and strategic thinker.

My greatest (professional) joy comes from working with motivated people who are truly looking at future business solutions through applications delivered in the new paradigm of cloud computing; along with all of the application development changes that go with it (tools, frameworks, continuous integration, elastic scale, DevOps, etc.). I have helped multiple Fortune X companies shape their vision and start them down this path. Maybe you're next?
