Marja Lee Freeman, The Employment Lady's Summary

Motivational Speaker and Trainer for my own company,
M.L. Freeman Consulting, LLC for 18 years. As an independent contractor, my area of expertise is Employment Training to include areas related to: Navigating the Job Market Resumes that Work!, Turning Conflict into Cooperation: Working with Those You Don't Like, Team Building to Succeed, Strategies for Those Re-entering the Workplace and many more.

I am successfully in partnership with other speaking companies: LDI --Leader Development Institute and Skillpath Seminars.

I currently serve as Board President for the Laurie Mitchell Employment Center for a non-profit employment training designed specifically to cater to the needs of those battling Mental Illness, transitioning from Incarceration or addiction. I was the very first employment specialist to work and building the employment center which started with two employees 1995. They have now been in existence for over 10 years, employ 15 employees and have won countless awards for being a pioneer employment facility. I currently work in consultant capacity networking with the community on their behalf.

I also have worldwide training and international travel experience in which I have toured for 4 continents and 11 countries speaking to over 20,000 participants.
