Ethan Chazin's Summary

I am a business strategist, executive coach, author, teacher, business consultant, radio celebrity, career coach to entrepreneurs, start-ups and business owners. I've helped 12,000 people find employment and 2,500 people plan, launch and grow their businesses.

I help my clients achieve their goals, whether it's finding their DREAM job or planning, launching, and growing a wildly successful business so they can achieve their dreams.

HOW?
By finding where their passions and greatest strengths collide and unleashing their full life's potential!

WHY?
I was laid off eight times (NEVER for performance) in my 20+ year career in Corporate America. Despite contributing to all of my past employer’s success, I was seen to be EXPENDABLE. In 2009, I had a life-changing vision to apply my passion for helping others with my strong branding, marketing and communications skills.

I authored the book: “Bulletproof Your Career in Turbulent Times,“ as a means of teaching people from all backgrounds and walks of life how to create a compelling personal story and ensure that they never become OBSOLETE in today's global contract workplace.

Since 2009 I have dedicated all my efforts to helping people develop their personal brand (Unique Selling Proposition), market themselves for career success, or if they choose to launch their own business.

As the Founder and President of The Chazin Group (TheChazinGroup.com) I apply my experience leading teams through significant transformation across many industries by best leveraging their employees for success. I help others by sharing my expertise in strategic planning, marketing, sales, new business development, client relationship building, communications, employee enrichment, and strategic planning. I've served nearly ALL of the Fortune 500 as clients.

Check out my interviews on 'Mind Your Own Biz' radio program:

I will help you to achieve your life's calling!
