Matt Hawkins' Summary

For the past ten plus years, I've been involved in video games on virtually every end of the spectrum.

My main forte is journalism. I've been everything from a contributing writer to full-on editor for various outlets, and for a wide range of audiences. I'm no stranger when it comes to covering mainstream news, as well as the beats that industry professionals gravitate towards.

Though my true passion is game culture. Specifically those who create music, film, and artwork, because they are inspired by their love of games. Aside from writing about it, I'm also an active participant, as a part of the Attract Mode collective.

Most recently, I have begun curating art exhibitions, again centered on video games. On this end, my mastery of social networking has been a tremendous benefit, to help spread the word, which is also how I connect with my audience. I've also been making games once again, after a hiatus.

Plus I speak at conventions as an invited guest, again on the subject of gaming. Though aside from games, I have an almost encyclopedic knowledge base of technology, film, television, animation, toys, pop culture, and the internet subculture.
