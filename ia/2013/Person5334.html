Amanda Butler's Summary

As the Founder & CEO of Keenawah & Associates, Amanda Butler specializes in helping individuals break the restraints of a life that doesn’t work, address beat up and blame to release the blocks and shift from survival to thrival and from victimhood to freedom. She teaches how to release, forgive the past and become more connected, confident and empowered so you can redesign and repurpose your life.

As a change agent/healing catalyst, she assists you to remember, reconnect and realign so you can redesign and repurpose your life to be more fulfilling, confident and successful. She is an expert in transforming lives through re-patterning energy and developer of a simple, powerful technology, The Diamond Co-Creative System™.

The System provides the pathway to accelerate awareness, to heal core issues at their root, to align you with your True Self and Highest Potential and to manifest what you desire. It is unique because unlike other methods it gives you the technology, the processes, the tools to co-create your next steps.

As an expert in assessing the energetic patterns and issues that limit or block people in achieving their desired results she guides them in re-patterning processes; she helps to heal and resolve co-dependent and dysfunctional relationships once and for all.

She is an Empowerment speaker and teacher at conferences, churches and other public events and has been interviewed on TV and radio. Amanda is an author of published articles and newsletters and has co-authored the book, You Will Overcome.

On her Freedom for the Soul radio show, she guides listeners through the entanglements and distortions that occur in co-dependent and dysfunctional relationships. She helps you understand how you co-create the types of relationships you do and what to do if they don’t work for you. The most important aspect of the show is teaching you how to love, honor and respect your Self through reconnection, realignment and redesign of your energetic patterns.

Specialties

She has helped hundreds of individuals become aware of and heal co-dependent and dysfunctional relationships within themselves, with others and even within companies. She is considered a "De-Entanglement" Expert. You can hear her every first Tuesday, 3-4pm EST on her show called Freedom for the Soul online at www.NewsfortheSoul.com.

Amanda Butler's Publications

  • You Will Overcome

    • June 1, 2010
    Authors: Amanda Butler

    With hundreds of thousands of men and women across the country having fears about losing their jobs or having lost it, most are concerned for their financial well-being while battling anxiety, depression and inertia. Fear of being thrown out of work is so widespread that equal percentages of higher- and lower-income workers, 47%, worry about losing their jobs. Nearly 71%, say they know someone – a friends or a relative – who has lost a job in the past six months, according to AP-GfK 2009 poll.

    Local author and teacher, Amanda Butler knows all too well about this journey because she lived it 12 years ago. With her experience, she has answered the call to help people refocus their lives and achieve new goals regardless of the economy or their situation. As a local Michigan entrepreneur, Butler with others from across the globe joined together to share their journeys of success and triumph over personal and/or career tragedies and challenges in the new book entitled, You Will Overcome.

    In this book, innovator, teacher and developer, Amanda tells her story of suddenly leaving the corporate world of over 20 years as a Corporate Executive without a Plan B to undertake transitioning her life into that of True success and vision. She now teaches these transition skills and processes in a series called The Diamond Power Within™ with a system she developed that she utilized to assist her in her own journey – The Diamond Co-Creative System™. This powerful series is designed to heal the past and empower people to build a new and powerful foundation that transforms their lives and futures.
