Philipp Kutter's Summary

Montages Lean Analysis provides your needed productivity gains in business analysis tasks.

Our methodology leverage the proven business practice to work based on example. We provide a lean, open tool set based on Microsoft Excel and Open Source Eclipse Modeling technology.

We recruit graduates from Finance oriented fields, as well as traditional banking apprenticeship with grown excellence in Banking. These talents are trained in our methodology and work as practical analysts for our clients.

I am coaching our Lean Montages analysts and responsible to coordinate the different streams from strategy over methodology and tooling to talent sourcing and project acquisition.

Unlike other business analysis, modeling, and architecture players, we actively maintain domain knowledge about Banking business areas and standard solutions inside our company, and push our knowledge proactively into every organization we advice.

Win your business performance struggle with Montages Lean!

Philipp Kutter's Experience

CEO

Montages AG

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

March 1997 – Present (16 years 8 months) Zurich, Switzerland

Founder and CEO of the ETH Spin-Off Montages AG.

Montages AG was founded 1997 as a pioneer in Domain Specific Languages (DSLs) design and implementation frameworks. Focusing on lean and reuse aspects in methodology and tooling, the Montages Lean Analysis approach was created and is actively used for business analysis int the financial industry.

The Montages Lean analysis methodology is based on two pillars:

- example based: the proven business practice to work based on real cases and examples is systematized, and a practice and methodology to use standard, macro-free Microsoft Excel to manage, evolve, and analyse these cases is offered by Montages Lean Analysts.

- these practical examples are then inseminated into business models of the underlying concepts, which can be refined to be directly executable. Those executable models allow to validate not only the existing cases, but as well the intended future scenarios. Montages Lean Modeling offers the most scaling "what if" analysis approach, leveraging Open Standards and Open Implementations for Modeling for Business Analysis.

Montages delivers Lean to the clients in four steps:

Lean Talent Sourcing
Graduates from Finance oriented fields, as well as young talents with grown excellence in Banking are trained in our approach to work as practical analysts.

Lean Lean Project Offering
Existing experience and templates come with the analyst, coaching and quality assurance is provided by very experienced consultants.

Lean Maintenance
Previous clients get updates to new materials and findings.

Lean Knowhow-Transfer
Montages promotes both application of Montages Lean Approach by internal employees and transfer of our consultants to the clients organization as needed.
