John Sindoni's Summary

Born and raised in Salem, Massachusetts and current resident of Nahant, Massachusetts. Native Americans called the area Nahant, meaning "the point" or "almost an island."

As Hospitality Sales Representative with ABnote North America I've been given some rewarding challenges to acquire a significant amount of new RFID and smartcard business in the hospitality industry and to inform hoteliers of the significant threat of RFID key card credentials with cloned or recycled MiFARE chips and the implications of using "compatible" modules in the hospitality contactless card market vs. genuine NXP or Infineon MiFARE chips.

ABnote is the largest card manufacturer in North America and it's nice to know that they have my back when it comes to reeling in some rather large hospitality customers. There's no place I would rather be because of their support and the way they service their customers.

Specialties: Lodging and hospitality industry, hospitality facilities engineering, travel and tourism, restaurants, casino and gaming industry, RFID access control technologies, plastic card manufacturing, RFID technology, electronics, social media and internet marketing, web development, marine industry, cruise lines.
