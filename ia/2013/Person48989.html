Glen Green's Summary

Glen Green has more than 21 years of production and design management experience in multimedia, application development and information systems. A hybrid background in digital strategy, production and technology enables Glen to bridge the worlds of Marketing & Communications, User Experience and Information Technology.

Currently, Glen is a strategy, I.T. and user experience expert leading creative, branding, information design, interactive design, program management and strategic vision for front-end Web/e-business, Intranets, Extranets and Custom Made Applications (CMA).

Glen specializes in providing executive-level leadership demonstrating the value of design driven by form and function, art and science.

Specialties: IT Management, User and Customer Experience Strategy, Digital Marketing, Creative Direction, Information and Interaction Design, Usability Expertise, Project Management, Business Analysis, Multi-media Expertise
