Susan Katz's Summary

Do you wish you could grow your business without doing all the work?

Do you already know what to do but just wish you had someone that would keep you accountable to doing it?

Do you feel like you are always playing catch up and can't seem to get on top of the work?

I am a business growth advisor and executive coach passionate about and dedicated to helping business owners, executives and business professionals achieve increasing levels of professional and personal success.

I started a business in 2008 during the economic downturn and through passion, skilled and genuine networking, a commitment to delivering value, and a clear focus on how I wanted to help people succeed, I built a thriving business.

Many of my clients have dramatically grown their revenues, repositioned their careers or learned to be more effective within their existing organizations as a result of working with me. For example:
• A service based business increased revenue from $400,000 to $1M in less than two years
• A successful accountant changed partnerships leading to a much more fulfilling career
• A talented attorney learned how to communicate effectively with clients, helping her to save her position within her law firm.
I am energized by facilitating and leading organizations to develop greater strategic focus and exponentially driving greater organizational growth.

I have a keen understanding of key business and behavioral drivers that lead people to greater professional and personal success.

Though I've received numerous awards and recognition for my work, including 2009 Baltimore Regional NAWBO Rising Star, 2010 National Growth Coach of the Year, 2011 and 2013 MD Top 100 Women and 2012 MD Top 100 Minority Business Enterprise I'm most excited about my client’s successes. They are the catalyst for what I do everyday.

If you are interested in connecting send me an email. Connecting and building relationships is how we all grow our businesses!

Specialties

I work with executives, small business owners, business professionals, and business teams on developing strategic focus,effective business development and human capital alignment. Organizations who have the right people in the right seat doing the right work and a clear focus will experience dramatic growth. We utilize a proven and guaranteed process that is designed to drive greater profitability.
