Dr. Robert (Bob) Wright's Summary

Considered by many to be one of the leading thinkers in human development, Dr. Bob Wright (www.drbobwright.com) is an internationally recognized visionary, educator, program developer, leadership and sales executive, best-selling author, speaker, entrepreneur, consultant, and executive coach. Recognized as a top executive coach by Crain’s Chicago Business, he has coached CEOs across the country from leading-name public companies to entrepreneurial startup businesses and has helped thousands of people across the country transform their careers, relationships, and lives. Dr. Bob Wright has spent over twenty years training, coaching, leadership development and consulting national and international young professionals business professionals and leaders from small entrepreneurial start-up businesses to Fortune 100's on topics such as business success, personal development, corporate growth, performance management, change, and effectiveness.
He is an outstanding radio and television personality, delivering what author Andrew Harvey calls his “hard light.” With the release of his new book Transformed!, co-authored with Dr. Judith Wright, reveals the Yearning and Rematrixing process taken from research data that has changed the lives of countless people. His nationally acclaimed employee assistance and managed mental health firm, Human Effectiveness, Inc., was rated top in the nation by Mercer Meidinger Medical Audit as well as Arthur Andersen.
Dr. Bob Wright’s deep belief in human potential has guided his mission to bring cutting-edge and traditional human emergence technologies into practical everyday use for the conscious evolution of humanity using social and emotional intelligence—most recently founding the Wright Graduate Institute for the Realization of Human Potential and the Foundation for Transformational Leadership.

Our Mission:

We are an independent, (soon to be) not-for-profit transformational educational enterprise dedicated to improving adult education in leadership, training, and coaching. We exist to enhance current career success as well as the quality of life in all areas of human endeavor through education, research, and the generation of a dedicated lifelong learner community.


Our Vision:

To contribute to a conscious and just society of people who learn and grow and fulfill their potential in the course of daily life by developing transformational leaders through education. To this end, we support students through degree and non-degree programs that disseminate, research, and develop current cutting edge technologies in human transformation in addition to presenting the historical academic roots to these in a way that guarantees continuity and maximal development.



Our Edge, Difference, Advantage:

Our education yields immediate, meaningful results for the student and their world. Not only do our students learn time-honored theory, methodology, and research, but they also apply this to their current life circumstances to enhance current efforts for greater success and satisfaction--and to educate, serve, and lead others to their fullest potential.
