Amanda Magee's Summary

I am a bold, agile strategist with extensive experience in marketing and communications across multiple platforms. Also, a straight talking, pragmatist who isn't afraid to say that something isn't effective or that a different approach is required.

The approach we take to presenting a message, whether it's for a not for profit organization, an international company or an individual, combines pursuing the end goal and valuing the audience. The goal is not to check boxes and satisfy requirements, it's to build waves and earn loyalty.

I cut my teeth in theatre and the not-for-profit realm, beginning as a carpenter (I weld, drill and hoss lumber like the boys) and went on to be the production manager at the Williamstown Theatre Festival during the festival's Tony Award winning season (Best Regional Theatre).

It was a serendipitous bit of personal interest that led me to be adept in the social media sphere—Twitter, blogging, Pinterest, Flickr, Tumblr etc. Outside my role as a rainmaker (for my agency and my clients) I've authored a personal blog for more than 7 years.

I have a lifelong commitment to supporting the arts and children's health initiatives.

Specialties

Adopting a voice for copywriting projects that represents the client, but more importantly, that resonates with audiences.

Integrating social media components to campaigns to accommodate strategic objectives, budgets and staffing realities.

Sustaining awareness efforts to build loyalty and create new opportunities.

Shortening the distance between pitch and success.

Establishing emotional connections between people and brands.
