Barbara Bakken's Summary

Barbara Bakken is an experience entrepreneur and business coach who has a passion for empowering women to overcome adversity in their lives and to enable them to create long term economic stability and wealth generation for themselves and their families by enabling them to start and run successful small businesses which have the capacity to generate 6 to 7 figures in annual revenues.

In the last 20 years, she has worked as a business executive, consultant, and coach while raising a family of three children. She fully knows the challenges that woman face in balancing the demands of being an entrepreneur, business executive, and mother.

In the course of her professional career, Barbara has been responsible for all aspects of founding, growing, and managing businesses. Barbara has successfully worked as a home-based expert consult, as well as, co-founding a high-tech startup and growing it into an international small business enterprise with offices in three countries. Through these experiences she has learn that to succeed at any endeavor you must play to your strengths and passion, be authentic about who you are and what you want to achieve, and to partner with individuals who have the complementary skills that you need to not only survive, but thrive.
Today, Barbara is the CEO and Founder of the Huntsinger Bakken Institute, and is committed to empowering women and helping them maximize their potential in work, life, and self.

Her loves are her family, spirituality, knitting, yoga, cooking, travel, writing, and helping women understand how to use proven systems and solutions to build a highly successful business and a deeply meaningful life.
