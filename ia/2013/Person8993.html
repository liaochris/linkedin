Anton Johansson's Summary

CEO & Co-founder of Osom, a mobile marketplace for beautiful things.

I'm also co-founder of Headler, a fast growing Swedish ecommerce startup.

One of Sweden's most popular speakers about digital trends, ecommerce and entrepreneurship. I'm also advising companies and executives regarding ecommerce/digital strategy, PR & customer acquisition (with clients such as Vattenfall, Delphi, PWC & LRF).

Co-initiator of TEDxAlmedalen and blogger since 2004 (fyranyanser.se).

Previously, I've been CMO for Twingly, independent search marketing consultant and co-founder of various internet businesses - Startitup.se (Sold), Promenix.se (RIP), Modetoppen.se (RIP), Ticketz.se (RIP), Graduation.se (RIP), Billigarehotell.se (Sold) + more.

Email: anton@getosom.com
