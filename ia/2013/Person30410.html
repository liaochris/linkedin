Daniel McFarland's Projects

  • PAFA.edu

    • March 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel McFarland, Heidi Merscher

    Several years after our original redesign, which had information about both the museum and the school rolled into one website, PAFA decided to split them into two separate sites. For this project we completely redesigned the school website from the ground up.

    Both faculty members and students alike felt that their existing site wasn’t properly communicating to prospective new students. They were being seen as being a stuffy, old and boring institution when in reality they are the birthplace of American art and an incubator for artistic expression.

    The new design invites the prospective student (or parent) to experience what’s behind the pristine veneer of the museum — to walk the paint-splattered halls and see the freedom of expression first hand.

  • Subtle Bodies EP

    • August 2010 to January 2012
    Team Members: Daniel McFarland, Ryan Moys

    Val de Val released this 6-song EP in Jan 2011. I'm one of the founding members and writers in the band and played the drums on this release. I also designed the album artwork and packaging.

  • Hive Mind: The Fan Funded Sinch Album

    • August 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel McFarland, Tony Lannutti, Mike Abramson, Jamie Stem

    I play the drums for a band called Sinch. We've been on both major and indie labels, toured the US extensively and have a worldwide fanbase. For our most recent album I created a Kickstarter-like website that raised over $14k from Sinch fans all over the world. This allowed us to record and release the album that we wanted to make without any involvment from a record label or any other corporate entity.
