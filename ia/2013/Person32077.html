David K Donovan Jr's Publications

  • Industrialization Of Investment Banks - Need for a Chief Industrialization Officer

    • David K Donovan Jr
    Authors: David K Donovan Jr

    The investment banking industry needs to go through an industrialization process by building lean operating machines to compete and survive in the new world. With the current regulatory environment and the increased competition from newer players which leverage technology with no legacy baggage, banks have to rebuild their client service models. In addition, there is a need to revamp their front office to back office 'knowledge manufacturing' model. For example, the acquisition of the New York Stock Exchange, a two hundred year old financial pillar, by a twenty year young company, ICE, demonstrates that the normal operations no longer work. If you can build a better industrialized model leveraging processes, technology, and best of breed partnerships, you can build a model for the future. This white paper discusses the current state of investment banks and key challenges facing C-Suite executives today and introduces the need for a role of Chief Industrialization Officer within the C-Suite.
