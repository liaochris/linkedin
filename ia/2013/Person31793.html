David Fefferman's Summary

Product manager and Entrepreneur. By day I manage the design, construction, maintenance, and upgrading of numerous beautiful, SEO-friendly websites for iOVA Communications. By night I put on my entrepreneur pants and saddle up to build CloudChow, a food ordering mobile app making on-the-go orders as simple as humanly possible.
Other interests include graphic design, art, the great outdoors, the tech industry, cars, building Halloween props, growing carnivorous plants, and homemade root beer.

Specialties:
• Product Management: Specing, designing, building, iterating, tracking, analyzing and maintaining a cornucopia of different mobile applications and web properties.
• Search Engine Marketing: PPC, content-driven inbound marketing, conversion optimization.
• Starting Businesses: Agile development methodologies, customer outreach, team building & management, fund raising, strategic planning, small business accounting.
• Graphic Design: UX and UI development for websites and mobile applications.
• Technical Proficiency: Trello, Basecamp, Solve360, Photoshop, MS Office, Apple iWork, ScreenFlow video production.
• Learning from my mistakes.
