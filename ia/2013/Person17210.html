Brian Blau's Summary

Senior technology executive with more than 20 years experience in industry research, management, software development in the areas of social networking, mobile devices, computer graphics, video games, interactive media, and other cutting edge and engaging technologies and trends.

Technology experience includes delivering reliable, popular and robust, software and projects on schedule. Expert level experience in the software development process, includes recruitment, product development, management, testing, documentation. This also includes managing 3rd party relationships and partnerships with top software and hardware companies, universities and others. 7 years computer programming experience in C, C++. Strong Windows, UNIX/Linux development background.

Management experience as an executive producer for live events, animation festivals, industry conferences, developer events, web sites, and trade shows. This included program development, content management, staffing, fund raising, budget management, outreach, media relations, and party planning. Managed relationships with content, technology and productions companies.
