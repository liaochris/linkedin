Brad Keys' Summary

I've designed and programmed many Unity and Flash games for web (Facebook, Kongregate) and mobile (iOS, Android). I've also designed and programmed scalable backend APIs for games to integrate analytics, achievements, high scores, and other popular online features into their games.

I'm currently developing Lumos, an online game service platform that helps developers make great games with ease.
