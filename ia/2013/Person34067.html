Denise Davis' Summary

Artpix Studio specializes in the reproduction of artwork for your custom gifts, personal hobbies and professional career. We provide high quality Giclée printing, the very latest advanced inks, and beautiful archival papers and canvas.

My goal is to provide customers with high quality Giclées using the best materials at a fair and reasonable price, and to also provide a pleasant and personal atmosphere in which to do so.

Specialties
