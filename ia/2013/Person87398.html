Marsha Bolen's Summary

I'm the owner of Accelerated, Inc. a Denver company founded in 2001. We operate with the idea that every patient has the right to be provided with high quality personal health care in their own home.

Home health care plays a very important role in my professional life, but so does working with my hands and creating wearable works of art. That passion and love for design has led me to build Coe & Company, a custom jewelry design business.

I have always enjoyed crafting, but my artistic creativity makes my work even more unique. Whether I am painting, sewing, cooking, decorating or making jewelry, I feel my art can become the distinctive blueprint designed especially for others' personalities and tastes.

I was born and raised in Texas, in a family of five children. My father always pushed us to follow our own path. I learned to sew when I was about 12 years of age and began taking the patterns apart to make them my own individual design. That’s when I and others noticed my special gift.

I am very busy with my two businesses but my time spent on my craft table is a very fulfilling part of my life. I love creating, listening to music and just being still in my mind.
