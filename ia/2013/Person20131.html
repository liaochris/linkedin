Carlos Pappini's Summary

Solid knowledge of the Brazilian HealthCare Market, public and private sector, obtained through large professional experience in pharmaceutical industries, medical devices companies, HMO´s (Health Plan) and PBM´s. Seventeen years working in marketing and sales.
Experience structuring and leading teams, budget constructions and P&L management, strategic marketing planning and product launch. In the commercial area I have developed different projects focused on the key account management concept and established sales force effectiveness programs.
Huge knowledge and skills in dealing with public institutions at all levels, from federal to municipal players, aiming to reach the reimbursement of drugs and the standardization of products and treatments.
Strong competence to build healthy relationships with key opinion leaders as well as with decision makers in the health market. Business orientation is one of the main characteristics in my professional profile.
Abilities to manage different organizational cultures, start up projects and turnaround processes are a plus in my experience.
