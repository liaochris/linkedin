Julie Cecchini's Summary

For the past 19 years I have worked in small businesses, finding ways to innovate processes and market on a shoestring. As the web has evolved I have kept my finger on the pulse of new ways to get a product and service out into the public sphere.

Sixteen years ago I established a specialty food company called Terra Americana. We have developed a unique niche within the gift and gourmet industries. Our products have always been healthy and natural because I believe simplicity is most pleasing to the palette and mind.

The role I enjoy at Terra Americana involves;

* Research & development of a product line based on recipes we adapted from exploring the world.
* Manage local production and source local health conscious ingredients.
* Managing the marketing of our products through grass roots efforts, a variety of blogging platform targeted to different market sectors and our product links to over 5,000 websites in the US, Canada and Central America.

As my business has evolved I have found my best employees to be high school and college students. Their roles within the company are multifold; dirty production work to designing marketing literature. On all aspects I am working by their side. They keep my up to date with electronics and new options for marketing on the web and I impart truly valuable skills they can build upon for years to come.

As far as teaching goes, I have experience with a mixture of both day and evening students. Diversity offers a wide range of experiences to draw from. I make the most of it in a discussion based curriculum. I have learned to incorporate this into online classrooms as well. I have worked with Moodle, however my preferred platform is Blackboard Vista. Whether in person or online, every class is a thrill. They reaffirm the professional evolution I have been striving for, as well as my desire to begin work on my PHD.

Please feel free to call me should you have any questions.
