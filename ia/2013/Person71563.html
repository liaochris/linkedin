JW (Jim) Holley, Marriage and Family Therapist's Summary

I am a California Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist. I have been working in the field of psychotherapy for 8 years, and as a parent and former school teacher, I have over 30 years of experience working with teens, parents of teens, and families with teens.

Specialties: I have extensive experience working with teens and parents of teens, couples, young adults and adult individuals. I have also led many successful teen groups and parent psycho-education classes.

I am in private practice, Holistically supporting Teens, Parents of Teens, Couples, Young Adult and Adult Individuals, and Families.

I define 'Holistic' essentially as incorporating all aspects of our human make-up - physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual - in the work I do with my clients.

I work intuitively, energetically and empathically, while also understanding and dealing with the pragmatic here-and-now reality of our day to day human experience.

I love and am great at the work I do - especially with committed clients who are ready and willing to do the work.

CAMFT (California Association of Marriage and Family Therapists) is a professional organization that stays current with all aspects of the Marriage and Family Therapist profession.
