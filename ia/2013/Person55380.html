Ingrid Koehler's Summary

I get networks. I know social. I've cultivated communities of thousands, convened genuine collaborative content creation and turned consumers into advocates. If I can do that for local government performance management, imagine what I could do for your brand or employee engagement.

With over a decade's experience in public policy, performance and regulation, I know how to navigate complex environments to get stuff done in an engaging, transparent way. Having worked with performance management, regulation and corporate governance, I know how important information flows inside and outside organisations are to make the right decisions and to achieve objectives.

I'm an innovator and a taker of calculated risks. I know when it's ok to risk failure for a chance of big success and when it's not worth the game.

I'm also a cracking facilitator, a darn good public speaker and a sharer at heart.

Specialties

performance management, customer insight, social media, knowledge management, community facilitation

Ingrid Koehler's Publications

  • Connected councillors: a guide to using social media to support local leadership

    • LG Group
    • March 14, 2010
    Authors: Ingrid Koehler

    An introduction to social media for councillors, outlining how digital technologies can be used to support councillors' leadership roles, create a space for community conversation, keep a finger on the pulse of local needs and campaign for political office and important local issues.

  • Insight: Understanding your citizens, customers and communities

    • Local Government Association
    • July 2010
    Authors: Ingrid Koehler

    I project managed the commissioning, writing and publication of this guidance document to help councils and local public services use intelligence and engagement to understand the needs and aspirations of their communities more fully and rigorously.

  • A Manager's Guide to Performance Management

    • Audit Commission/IDeA
    • 2005
    Authors: Ingrid Koehler

    A quick guide to tools and approaches for better performance management in local government.

  • A Councillor's Guide to Performance Management

    • Audit Commission/ IDeA
    • 2005
    Authors: Ingrid Koehler, Malcolm Grimston

    This handy guidance document was aimed at those in political leadership and scrutiny roles to help them use performance management to support better public services. I co-wrote this with Councillor Malcolm Grimston and project managed its publication.

  • Corporate Governance: Improvement and Trust in Local Public Services

    • Audit Commission
    • 2003
    Authors: Ingrid Koehler, Mark Wardman

    An analysis of corporate governance in the public sector. With key recommendations for improvement of corporate governance to support public service improvement and the avoidance and mitigation of governance failures.

  • Building Better Library Services

    • Audit Commission
    • 2002
    Authors: Ingrid Koehler

    We analysed the current state of library services in England and Wales and advocated some clear reforms. This publication received more publicity and press coverage than any other Audit Commission publication that year.
