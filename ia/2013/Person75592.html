Kimberly West's Summary

Kimberly West & Co is the image, digital, and product brand of Kimberly West.

Image and Wardrobe Consulting: Image and Wardrobe Consulting Company geared towards helping clients to look their best and achieve a wardrobe that will fit their lifestyle. Whether it's personal styling, makeup tips, or learning about social graces, we assist with creating a "polished" image.

She is also the author of the Ebook Series, Beauty Tips Back to The Basics.

Style Life Fashion (website): The online component of the Kimberly West & Co. brand. Although Kim started blogging as a hobby due to her love for Fashion, she quickly gained a loyal following as a respected industry expert and contributor for numerous fashion websites. A Fashion Educator by trade, Kim seeks to provide the latest trends as well as educate consumers about all aspects of fashion. Kim has done fashion interviews with well known industry experts such as Julie Weiss, Clinton Kelly, Gianni Rich, Darius Gibbs, Dean Banowetz, and Lilliana Vazquez. She has also collaborated with online features for Glamour Magazine, Harper’s Bazaar, and Macys. Lastly, Kimberly worked to stream digital content and video for New York Fashion Week 2010.

Fashion and Beauty Products: visit shop.stylelifefashion.com
