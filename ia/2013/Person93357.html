Michele Wells' Summary

Michele is a versatile editor and writer with more than 15 years of experience in publishing. She has a strong acquisitions list to her name, and she is skilled in conceptualizing and developing content. Currently specializing in app writing and development for Disney, she previously edited illustrated books and multimedia products for DK Publishing, lifestyle nonfiction (entertainment/pop culture, foreign language, travel, film, food/wine, diet, health, diet, self-help, music, humor, technology, and light reference) for Penguin, business self-help for McGraw-Hill, and children's educational products for Pearson, The Princeton Review, Learning Express, and others. She has extensive experience in series publishing for children/YA and adults, a proven track record in managing multiple deadline-driven projects, and a reputation for delivering quality projects on time and within budget. Michele is also the author of several books for children, grades K-12.
