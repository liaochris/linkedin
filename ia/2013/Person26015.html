James Gels' Summary

I have been coaching basketball at the youth, AAU and high school level for over 20 years and have been assistant varsity boys and girls coach in Charlevoix, MI. Over the past ten years, the boys teams have won a number of conference titles, District and Regional tournament championships, and have been a participant in the Michigan State Semi-Finals and Finals at Michigan State's Breslin Center a number of times, including 1999, 2001, 2004, and 2007. In 2008, the boys repeated as conference and District tournament champs, finishing with a 20-3 record.

The 2004 gitrls team went 27-1, losing in the state final game by 5 points at Michigan State's Breslin Center. The girls finished the 2005, 2006, and 2007-2008 seasons as District and Conference champs with a records of 20-4, 18-5, and 20-3 respectively. The girl's JV team had three straight undefeated 20-0 seasons (2004, 2005 and 2006), and were 18-1 in 2007-2008. The combined girls won-loss record for 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007-08 is 202-14. At one point (over several seasons), the JV girls had a 76 game winning streak!

The Coach's Clipboard Basketball Coaching Web-site and Playbook

January 2001 – Present (13 years)

The Coach's Clipboard is a web-site and company dedicated to providing free, or low cost, information to basketball coaches and players, and it hopes to serve as a reference for the many basketball coaches throughout the world involved in coaching youth basketball and high-school level basketball. The web-site presents a large volume of free basketball coaching information in the form of articles, animated Flash diagrams and video clips. Many basketball offenses and plays, defenses, player fundamentals, drills, press offenses and defenses, and general basketball coaching articles and coaching forms are included.

Several excellent products are available for purchase including our "Premium Membership", "Youth Membership", the "Deluxe Basketball Coach's CD", a "Player's CD" and two downloads - the "Playbook", and the "Playbook & Animations".

In addition to CDs and downloads, the Coach's Clipboard Basketball Store has many other basketball items... books, software, coaching aids, play diagramming, training aids, equipment, product reviews, shooting DVDs, etc.

Another addition is the Coach's Clipboard DVD-Video Store, with over 1000 basketball DVDs from the best college, professional and high school coaches in the country! Please stop in and take a look.

Physician-Owner (Retired)

James A. Gels MD, PC

July 1975 – March 2013 (37 years 9 months)

I am a physician, now retired, from the speciality of Internal Medicine in Charlevoix, Mi. I graduated from Miami University (1968) and the Ohio State University College of Medicine (1972), and did my residency in Internal Medicine at Beaumont Hospital (Royal Oak, Mi), finishing in 1975. I have been an active staff member and former Chief of Staff at Charlevoix Area Hospital, Charlevoix, Mi, and was the former director of both Intensive Care and the Non-Invasive Cardiac Lab. I am board-certified in Internal Medicine, and a Fellow of the American College of Physicians.
