Mario Natarelli's Summary

Mario is a trusted advisor to executives and their companies, looking to leverage their most important asset — their brand. By first being able to uniquely understand a brand’s true challenges and untapped potential, then through deliberate and extensive analysis, he’s helped major fortune 500 companies and even entire countries tangibly transform, align their cultures and deliver unprecedented growth and value.

Trained as an architect early in his career, Mario uses these fundamentals to balance the art and science of brand building. He understands that a brand’s essence and their cultures emanate from their strategic core outwards. And, having built a successful digital marketing business that was acquired by Interpublic and became part of the formation of FutureBrand, he is keenly aware of the role that digital plays and has pioneered software as a digital operating system for optimizing and managing brands.

Over the past 17 years he has led teams across three continents, working for clients of varying size and industry focus, mostly in the areas of technology, real estate, travel, sports and hospitality. Along the way he has developed a proprietary set of frameworks, processes and tools that deliver proven results.

As important as the brands he has helped create or enhance, Mario is proudest to lead a multidisciplinary, multicultural, multigenerational team looking to expertly solve problems.

His love of architecture has inspired his work in destination, real estate and luxury categories, where he has helped shape a quarter-trillion dollars of value. He lives and plays in New York City.

Specialties:
Brand, technology and business consulting, design leadership including identity, environment, digital. Culture transformation, training and brand management.

Mario Natarelli's Experience

Managing Partner

MBLM

Partnership; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) New York City

Mario, together with Claude Salzberger, led a group of partners to form MBLM in New York City. They’ve built a marketing group looking to define a new paradigm aimed to create greater intimacy between people, brands and technology. Through a tailored approach that enhances partnering with clients, and a deep collective of experiences, MBLM is inventing new and innovative ways to add value.

With a healthy roster of foundation clients and a growing, diverse and talented team, the company is surpassing expectations and creating exciting results for their clients. MBLM is headquartered in NYC and has offices in 8 countries and close to 100 marketing professionals.

CEO North America & Middle East

FutureBrand

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

March 1998 – May 2010 (12 years 3 months) New York City, Toronto, Dubai

For more than 13+ years, Mario led a division of FutureBrand with offices in New York, Toronto, Dubai and Abu Dhabi. He was instrumental in building one of the fastest growing, most profitable teams of more than 120 professionals.

His work for clients won numerous awards and his team built industry-leading thought leadership works, such as Country Brand Index and the Gulf Real Estate Study, which led their categories and were major sources for media and clients alike. Mario was also a member of the Futurebrand Global Executive Committee.

During his tenure Mario led assignments with UPS, Intel, AMD, GM, Samsung, Marriott, Emaar, Leisurecorp, Microsoft, Bank of America and many more.

Co-Founding Partner

HyperMedia Solutions

March 1995 – March 1998 (3 years 1 month) New York City, Toronto

HyperMedia
Mario co-founded HyperMedia Solutions, a Toronto-based digital marketing company that pioneered internet designs and technologies. The business focused on the idea that by leveraging emerging technologies, marketing could become immersive, interactive and engaging. This was achieved by developing state-of-the-art selling tools for Real Estate, and software to drive market research and in-store simulations.

Within three years of inception and numerous marketing awards, the company was acquired by Interpublic and became an integral part of the forming of FutureBrand.

Architect

Graduate Architect

August 1991 – March 1995 (3 years 8 months) Toronto, Ontario

Prior to HyperMedia, Mario was an architect who enjoyed working in firms focused on small- to medium-scale residential, urban planning and urban renewal projects.
