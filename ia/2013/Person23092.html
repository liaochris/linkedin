Christopher Myers' Summary

I work exclusively with emerging growth companies as any/all of an advisor, investor, or operating officer. My background is as a founder and/or C-level operator of high-growth companies including:

>>> Founder of MAGetc, a digital publisher for mobile devices.

>>> Sponsor of the Musemaka Idea Challenge, a global startup competition.

>>> Founder of Philadev Ventures, a founder-driven startup accelerator.

>>> COO of Sivoo Inc., the Internet-video pioneer.

>>> Founder & CEO of searchbuilder.com, a dotcom SEO / SEM provider.

>>> Founder of H.I. Creative, the Southwest’s largest publication services agency.

>>> Founder of Duskenhaig, a turn-based play-by-mail game.

I serve on a number of boards and advisory committees for emerging growth companies and am a director of the Philadelphia Wooden Boat Factory, a non-profit that helps underserved youth build life skills.

I am an entrepreneur in residence at the Baiada Center, teach a course on entrepreneurship at LeBow, and advise entrepreneurship students at Drexel, Penn, and Temple.

I have been featured in MIT’s Who’s Who of World’s Leading Experts in IDTV, Media Management and Economics; was the keynote speaker at London’s Online Video & TV Conference, and have done interviews with Variety, The Philadelphia Inquirer, TechWeb, InteractiveTV Today, and Multichannel News among others.
