Amanda Fetterly's Summary

Amanda Fetterly is an visual designer gifted at surfacing consumer insights to build products and brands that delight and inspire.

Prior to relocating to the Bay Area to pursue her MBA in Design Strategy she played a pivotal role in the redesign of the multi-channel digital brand experiences at The University of British Columbia. Her ability to combine compelling narratives, strategy, and visual design generated results that improved user experience and brand awareness.

By taking a collaborative approach to complex projects she brings honesty, creativity and insight forward to generate solutions.
