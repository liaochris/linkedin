Balazs Koren's Summary

Father of three and an enthusiastic teacher, and technology lover a.k.a. geek.

While studying mathematics, I started to work as a system administrator in my former secondary school. I know how to set up and maintain linux servers and desktop machines.

After getting my degree, started to teach. 7 years teaching experience, form-master for 5 years.

In 2009, I came up with the idea of a Hungarian Android community. And we made it with 2 friends. It is now the biggest Hungarian Android website and community.

In 2011 joined GeoGebra, and did community management (events, Social Media, blog, Kickstarter campaign, ...), organizing and presenting at conferences worldwide.
