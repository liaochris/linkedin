JD Kilby's Summary

In over seven years of experience creating and developing games, I've had one goal: create experiences that will capture the attention and imagination of users everywhere.

I've used my programming and design skills in a variety of ways to accomplish this. Whether it is brainstorming and prototyping original game concepts, taking a fully-fleshed out idea and adding those small touches that make the game stand out, or simply creating tools and other support code to help other designers, artists, and developers create the best product possible, I've always kept this goal in mind.
