Chere Bork, MS RD LN's Summary

Chere LOVES anything to do with food, RDs, talking, eating and coaching. She helps RDs discover their purpose and live happily and healthfully ever after through individual and group coaching RD Mastermind Groups and speaking. She volunteers as the co-host of National Speaking Association MN Chapter Speaker Training Program helping people start speaking businesses and as a co-host of the Coach U Newcomer Sig Group helping people start coaching businesses. Her sister, Laurie Meyer, RD (Milwaukee RD TV star for 20+ years) and she authored the first 5 Ingredient Coaching Cookbook. It took Chere 45 years to absolutely LOVE every day of her life. Don’t wait so long!!

Specialties

Empowering speakers to learn how to research and develop content; align their message with their brand and image; create a business plan and marketing plan; present platform mechanics, focusing on positive energy and deliberate creation of getting exactly what you want in life and discover what is needed for authorship and product development.
