Troy King's Summary

From 2000 to 2005, I studied music education at UW River Falls. Post graduation I found an incredible lack of jobs and an abundance of qualified teachers looking for work. As I had grown up fixing computers and learning about technology, I decided to start PC Rewind, a small computer repair and support company.

As the business grew, several clients needed website solutions, so I began self educating on website development. In 2010, I decided to take PC Rewind to the next level and rebranded the company as King Web Design. While I still support existing clients with their computer needs, I now focus on web design and development as well as domain registration and web hosting.

Specialties

I specialize in the Content Management Systems Joomla and Wordpress. I have extensive knowledge and experience with HTML and CSS and produce W3C Validated code.
