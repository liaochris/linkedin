Mike Brunswick's Summary

A seasoned, award-winning, television & video producer, field producer, writer, reporter, with an emphasis on strong story-telling techniques and creativity. TV news and production background. Emmy® award-winning writer. Other major strengths: line-producing, interviewing, directing talent, post production supervision and voice-overs. Exceptional attention to organizing and detail. Passionate. Decisive. Strong interpersonal and time management skills. Great sense of humor and wit.

Everyone loves a good story. My job is to use my many years of experience as a producer and broadcast journalist to find that great story in all of us.

Specialties

Interviewing, writing, narrating.
