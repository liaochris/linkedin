Danielle Baird's Summary

entrepreneur // graphic designer // writer // marketer (not the slimy kind) // intermediate coder

Are you:
- A business owner who needs a trusted partner to manage your brand?
- A creative director who needs extra help with a special project?
- Tasked with trying to make a lot of scary Excel data look legible and pretty? (Think: class schedules, conference brochures, etc.)

I'm your gal!

With expertise in both graphic design and journalism, I can conceptualize designs and write compelling copy that will move your customers into action. Let's work together to uncover and highlight your company's core message—and leave the rest of that "fluff" on the cutting room floor. I develop long-term relationships with my clients and help them with everything they need (print, web, digital, marketing strategy, etc.)

I've also worked with corporations (Chicago Cubs, Allscripts, United Stationers) as an extension of their in-house team, when their plate is overflowing and they need an extra set of hands.

But...my favorite projects?

Complex, long documents, with lots of text. I get to use all my skills (design, writing, organizing + coding) to slay ugly Word docs and overwhelming Excel files. The result? Beautiful, legible documents, created fast.

Specialties

long document design (catalog, conference brochure, workbook, etc.); content organization; PDF design; editorial design; brand development (logos, stationery); brochures and advertisements; e-mail newsletter design and distribution; WordPress blog setup and design; social media education & consulting
