Meredith Williamson's Experience

President

Oregon Senior Referral Agency Association

May 2011 – Present (2 years 7 months)

OSRAA is a member based association for referral agencies in Oregon. All members are required to adhere to a high level of ethics in working with seniors. They must also provide personal assesments of clients and review of public disclosure files for complaints.

Committee Member

Pro Bono Committee, Oregon State Bar

December 2010 – Present (3 years)

Our committee helps bring awareness to Pro Bono work, recognize members of the Oregon State Bar who provide exceptional Pro Bono services, and help organize the Pro Bono Fair each year.

President

Choice Senior Services, LLC

March 2007 – Present (6 years 9 months)

Choice Senior Services, LLC is a full service referral agency. We specialize in assisting people in their search for senior housing and care. Our unique structure allows people to tailor their service providing everything from one-on-one assistance with our knowledgeable Elder Care Advisors to a Resource Guide and website for those who prefer to search on their own. All our services are always 100% free to the people we help.
