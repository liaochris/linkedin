Kim Butler's Summary

A serial entrepreneur, Kim Merritt-Butler has been working in the world of online marketing and e-commerce for 14 years on both the client side and the consulting side. Her Inbound Marketing strategies combine pay per click marketing (PPC), search engine optimization (SEO), local search, social media, email management, web site design and usability, and analytics to achieve optimum results for clients.

Kim also combines her experiences from two small business start-ups and over 30 years in brick and mortar and online retailing to offer a unique and varied approach to building client programs. She understands the importance of return on investment in an online marketing and web site program. Kim specializes in e-commerce websites and regional and national e-commerce inbound marketing campaigns. The URL Dr. is a value added reseller of the Volusion e-commerce platform and Constant Contact.

Kim is a Constant Contact Authorized Local Expert and speaks to organizations and groups on topics including email marketing, social media, and website success.

Kim has a teenage son attending Mercersburg Academy and a teenage daughter who can't wait to attend next year. Kim also has a Newfoundland and two adopted cats. She recently relocated to Western PA from Western MD. In her spare time she speaks and shares her knowledge of inbound marketing and the challenges of running a small business.

President / Founder

Kim's Khocolate

September 1985 – June 1996 (10 years 10 months)

Kim's Khocolate began as a candy making hobby when I was eleven years old. As a senior at Bishop Walsh High School, I hand made 18,000 candy bars for a school fund raiser. I transitioned my hobby to a business at the age of 18. I created new products from the recipe stage through packaging to production. I headed sales to wholesale accounts, to the general public through a retail store, and to corporate customers. I contracted custom designed pieces for Apple Computer, AT&T, The Rolling Stones, Range Rover, and many more. I headed up all public relations for Kim's Khocolate and got the company in national publications such as USA Today, Inc. Magazine, People, and Barron's.
