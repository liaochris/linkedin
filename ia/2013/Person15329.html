Bob Corlett's Summary

We're the search firm small organizations can afford. We staff positions at every career level and in every functional area--many organizations rely on us for all their staffing needs. There's no aspect of the recruiting process we have not taken apart, examined and rebuilt to get better results, less expensively. We removed the things you don’t care about (sales commissions and expensive lunches) to focus our attention on delivering the services you do care about (finding great candidates and conducting a rigorous selection process).

But it goes much deeper than that.

When we help you with a staffing need, we move quickly, taking you from the initial meeting to offer and acceptance in about 6 weeks--with a full slate of thoroughly vetted, highly qualified candidates.
We want to ensure that the best people are considered, even if they are not actively looking for a job, so our candidate research team identifies and contacts hundreds of candidates during the first two weeks of your search. Simultaneously, we conduct sophisticated social media outreach campaigns to give you access to a broad cross-section of candidates. Our process is faster, more effective, and far less insular than the traditional old-school approach of calling people to ask who they know. It's also more interesting for the candidates.

But finding and recruiting good candidates is only part of the solution--reducing your risk of hiring the wrong person is equally important. We'll give you everything you need to conduct a rigorous interview process, so you can make a great hiring decision with confidence. (Backed by our unconditional one year guarantee).

Our process works consistently--more than 80% of our search work is with clients who have engaged us 3 or more times, and some clients simply turn over all their staffing needs to us. Our candidate retention rates are among the best in the industry--over 85% of our placements are still on the job, delivering great results, three years later.

Specialties

We primarily work with associations, entrepreneurs and nonprofits in the Washington Metropolitan area. Our expertise is staffing mid-range to senior executive positions with salaries ranging from $60k to $200k in all functional areas including Accounting/Finance, Communications, Development, Education, HR, IT, Marketing, Membership, Operations/Administration, Outreach, Policy, Project Management, and Research.
