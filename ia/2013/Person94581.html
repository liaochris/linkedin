Mike Shanta's Summary

I love words. I love the affect they have on us all. With words you can fill a person with hope, you can move them to great passion, and you can inspire them to be their best. The right words strategically placed on paper, or fitly spoken in the right manner and at the right time can move people to take action they might not otherwise take.

Words can paint pictures, and stir the imagination. Words used unwisely can cause boredom, offence, and even anger. On the other hand, words used wisely, can excite the senses, lift the spirit and make many friends or fans.

When reading a blog post, a story or even advertisement; if the words don’t grab you immediately, you’re not going to read on. Most of us are so busy these days; if we don’t see a payoff coming we’re gone.

I can’t tell you how often I read ads that tell me how good a product is instead of telling me how the product can make my life better. I've read lots of web copy that blathers on and on about how great a company is without ever telling me how much they care about my needs and what they’re going to do to meet those needs.

I take words very seriously, and I think you should also.

I also have Associate Degrees in both Business and Occupational Studies. I have strong written and verbal communication skills, as well as, very good interpersonal, and customer service skills. I’m a hardworking, organized, methodical and detail oriented kind of guy. I’m a self-motivated self-starter and a quick learner. I work well under pressure and stay efficient no matter what the situation. I’m involved in my community and write my own blog at YourSpiritMindandBody.com.

If you need help with blog articles, web content, newsletter writing or ad copy, I would love the opportunity to work with you, and help you get what you want.

I can be reached at mdshanta (at) suddenlink (dot) net. If you would like to talk to me personally, just send me an email with your number, or to request mine.
