Claudia Anderson, DDS's Summary

I have worked in the Health Care Industry for over 35 years. My passion for working with and caring for people has led me to rewarding careers first as a registered nurse and then as a dentist. My focus has always been to care for the health of my patients as well as to teach them to learn optimal ways to manage their own health. The joy found in teaching my patients inspired me to pursue a part time teaching position at Marquette University School of Dentistry as well as to develop my Social Media DDS company that teaches dentists and dental professionals how to integrate social media into their marketing strategy. I love the journey that my careers have taken me on and I am always excited to see where the road may lead me tomorrow.

Specialties

General Dentist, Dental Clinical Instructor, Consultant for Social Media for Dentists and Dental Professionals
