Tom Heatherington's Experience

Owner/Founder

Flathead Guide

December 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) Kalispell, MT

The "Flathead Guide" (Flathead Valley Business Directory™) is a smartphone-optimized business directory and tourist information for NW Montana. Find businesses, shopping, restaurants and things to do and see in the Flathead Valley. Millions of people travel to the Flathead every year to visit Glacier National Park, Flathead Lake or ski in Whitefish. Our handy mobile-ready guide makes it easier to find what you are looking for in northwest Montana.
