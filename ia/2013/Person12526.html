Rebecca Murray Schuler's Experience

President and Founder

PR Inc

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry

April 2006 – Present (7 years 8 months)

Founded PR Inc to provide a full range of marketing, publicity, and copywriting / editing services for B2B, B2C, and B2I communications. This includes developing marketing and publicity plans, logo and branding strategies, promotional materials, as well as overseeing the development of social media and advertising campaigns.

PR Inc has provided copy and/or created marketing collateral for: Tom Brokaw and NBC News, President Jimmy Carter, Celine Dion, Roger Ebert, Thomas Kinkade, Nelson Mandela, The New Yorker, Sur La Table, G.B. Trudeau, USA Today, Bill Watterson, and more.

Notable Kansas City-based clients include: Accord Publishing, Andrews McMeel Publishing, Kansas International Film Festival 2010, Mercato, Nell Hill’s, Spencer Fane Britt & Browne, and StarInfo (a revenue-driven division of The Kansas City Star).

Director of Publicity for Andrews McMeel Publishing

Andrews McMeel Universal

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry

1999 – 2006 (7 years)

Directed annual promotional operations and campaigns for 350+ books and gift products, while managing a team of five publicists, six internship participants, and three freelance firms. Governed a seven-figure promotions budget and realized 25% savings without sacrificing effectiveness. Well versed in crisis management, opting for a proactive platform as opposed to a reactive response.

Pitched and secured national, regional, and local media appearances, including multiple Oprah Show bookings and the company's first cover of The New York Times Book Review, along with leading more than 25 New York Times best-selling campaigns.

Strong presentation skills and experience in leading sales presentations, pitch meetings, and trade shows, as well as design and development of print and online advertisements, B-roll, in-store signage, press kits, marketing presentations, and trade-show related promotional materials.

Founded a college-credit publicity internship program, and frequently selected to serve on special committees including the company’s five year restructuring plan, 30th and 35th anniversary promotions, and Kansas City’s hosting of the 2004 Reuben Awards.

Rebecca Murray Schuler's Publications

  • I Love You Even Though . . .

    • Andrews McMeel Publishing
    • December 21, 2010
    Authors: Rebecca Murray Schuler

    Humorously heartwarming and slightly subversive insights on romantic bliss—an anything-but-saccharine ode to the ever-evolving male / female relationship.

    Admit it, sometimes we just don’t get where our better half is coming from. Are 23 pairs of black heels—each varying in heel height, number of straps, and degrees of self-inflicted foot pain—really necessary? Does a $5000 Bose stereo system qualify as “home improvement?”

    Inside I Love You even though…, co-authors Rebecca Murray Schuler and Christine Wiesedeppe-Regan offer nearly fifty sarcastic insights on romantic bliss paired with charming, edgy line drawn illustrations.

    Perfectly timed for Christmas and Cupid Day gift-giving, guys and gals will find that all the important relationship topics are covered: lawn mowing, ESPN’s Sportscenter, her propensity to collect shoes in varying hues of black, his ability to quote entire scenes from The Godfather and always forget three items on the grocery list.

    While Schuler and Regan indicate that debates will rage over the culinary merits of stuffing a beer can up a chicken’s bottom, as well as the fine line between talking, asking, and nagging, one thing remains certain, unequivocal, and without fail—that thing is love.
