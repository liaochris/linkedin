Angel F. Cocero-Cordero, MAUD, AIA, AAH, LEED AP's Summary

Professional architect with over 20 years of experience. Involved in all aspects of the project development process with solid knowledge of every phase from design throughout construction. Background includes designing and budgeting of projects; preparation and evaluation of construction documents to meet client’s economic feasibility plan; conducting client’s presentations; construction contract negotiation and administration; construction management; construction supervision and inspection; and the management of design-build projects, guaranteeing that the built product meets the most demanding specifications established in the design intent.
