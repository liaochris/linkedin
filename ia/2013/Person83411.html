  • Sentinel Visualizer

    • 2004 to Present
    Team Members: Luke Chung

    Sentinel Visualizer is an advanced data analysis platform that helps analysts find hidden relationships among people, places, and events. Initially applied toward counter-terrorism, Sentinel Visualizer is used by communities in the defense, law enforcement, insurance fraud, pharmaceutical, academia, human resources, investigative journalism, and other fields where complex relationships are managed. Sentinel Visualizer was partially funded by InQTel, the CIA's venture capital arm.
