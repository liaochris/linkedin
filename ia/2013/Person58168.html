Jane Ducarreaux's Summary

A writer and editor with 19 years’ commercial experience in business-to-business and contract magazines, PR, corporate, internal, employee, graduate and client communications – the last 13 running my own business.

A communication specialist, skilled in leading multidisciplinary teams to deliver client and business objectives. Experienced in all aspects of initiating and delivering multimedia communication programmes for internal and external facing audiences in the UK and Europe.

An excellent communicator, used to working with individuals at all levels of business, from board level down.

A strategic thinker, able to see the bigger picture as well as the fine detail.

Deadline driven, commercially focused, committed to project delivery; on brief, on time, and on budget.

A self-motivator, equally happy working on my own as with a team.

Extensive experience in procuring, briefing and managing creatives, PR and advertising agencies, photographers and other specialists.

Specialties

Communication strategy and planning: Writing - conceptual copy, sales, marketing, internal communications, corporate and recruitment copy, reports and proposals, Journalism; Ghost writing; Editorial management; Project management
