Eric Boehm's Summary

Eric Boehm is Partner with Borden Ladner Gervais LLP in the Corporate Commercial Group, with an emphasis on outsourcing, technology and intellectual property transactions.

An experienced corporate and commercial lawyer focusing on long-term outsourcing and other commercial arrangements. Eric has extensive Canadian and international experience advising on complex commercial, information technology and intellectual property transactions.

Recognitions include:

● Selected by peers for inclusion in The Best Lawyers in Canada 2013 (Information Technology Law)

● Lexpert's Canadian Legal Lexpert Directory - Repeatedly Recommended in Computer & IT Law and in Technology Transactions (2012)

● Intellectual Asset Management's IAM Licensing 250: The World's Leading Patent and Technology Licensing Lawyers 2011 - leading lawyer globally

● Law Business Research’s Who’s Who Legal, The International Who’s Who of Business Lawyers 2010 - Leading lawyer in Internet and e-commerce

Specialties

Counselling clients in outsourcing, technology, commercial, payment cards (credit, debit, corporate and consumer), mobile commerce, financial services, e-commerce, privacy, open source, trade-mark and patent licensing, manufacturing, export controls, media, mobile apps, marketing, health and intellectual property contracts.
