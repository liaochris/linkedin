Jacob Hoehne's Summary

We're a brand-building, creative agency. We work with companies that love their brand and want to take it to the next level.

In Italian, you add "issimo" to the end of a work to make it the "best" or "ultimate."

Here's what adding Issimo can do for you:

BELLISSIMO - Eye-catching work that makes your brand beautiful on film, TV, DVD, print, or Web.


GRANDISSIMO - Big ideas that deliver big results. A work of art with a return of investment.


BRAVISSIMO - A wonderful experience with friendly people who live to make you look good.

Specialties

- Narrative Spots: Issimo is about cinematic storytelling - sharing your brand's story in a compelling and entertaining way.

- Cinematography: Issimo is about making things beautiful. Travis Cline, Director of Photography, brings the image to life. His reel includes various feature films he has shot in the last few years.

- Motion Graphics: Issimo is about making a big impact. Whether it's 3D, 2D, or compositing, we have the talent and the tools to make your vision come to life.
