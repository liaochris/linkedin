James Milligan's Summary

I own and run Milligan.TV, a media production company based in the North of the UK, providing production services to a range of clients. I've been a freelance cameraman for 5 years, am an Associate Member of The Guild of Television Cameramen, and also volunteer as their Social Media Officer and Forum Administrator. To find out more about my work as a freelance cameraman, visit www.milligan.tv

I'm the Technical Co-ordinator for PSTV, the University of Central Lancashire student television station, in charge of technical planning and production. On most events I setup all the cameras, cabling, lighting, and production desk, working alongside other crew on larger events. Whilst events are running, I usually work as vision mixer, the output of which is typically streamed online to students around campus. On pre-recorded/non-mixed shows I'm normally found behind a camera.

I am currently studying at the University of Central Lancashire for a degree in Computer Network Technology. I am a proficient PHP programmer, working mostly with a MySQL backend, as well as being competent in HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery (including jQuery-UI). Additionally, I have some experience developing C# applications, and some limited experience with C++ and VB.NET. I am most at home on a Windows machine, but have had exposure to different Linux distributions.
