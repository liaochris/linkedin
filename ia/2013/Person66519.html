John Crawford's Summary

-Created and developed a small Audiovisual start up company from ground zero to annual revenue of 1000K. Roles include marketing, sales, financial planning, vendor negotiations, design engineering, design, technical instruction, troubleshooting, live event production, and janitor. My current goal is to expand the company to a position where it is self sufficient and self sufficient while progressing from the profile of a "mediocre" company to a "good" company.

I have designed AV systems for a variety of clients; however, I specialize in church systems. Projects have ranged in size from 5K to over 300K, and have included sound systems, video systems, acoustic analysis/treatment, theatrical lighting, control systems, and a variety of related custom projects.

I have designed event systems for concerts, revivals, dramas, dance performances, and elite weddings (lightscaping). Those events have ranged from less than 1K to over 30
I am currently certified as "CTS" (ICIA/Infocomm), THX Level 2 (THX), C-SI (NSCA)
-Proficient in Audio DSP systems programming including the Biamp Audia/Nexia, Media Matrix X-Frame/M-Frame/Nion, Sabine Navigator, DBX Driverack, and QSC DSP-3/4 platforms
-Proficient in Audio Test and Measurement systems including Ivie 33/45, and Smaart Live/Acoustic Tools
-Proficient in Audio and acoustic analysis and prediction software including Ease 4.x and Ulysses
-Proficient in entertainment and permanently installed rigging--all rigging provided by Auburn AV under my supervsion maintain a minimum design factor of 10:1 for permanent installation and 5:1 for temporary events.
-Proficient in graphic design software including Corel Draw, Photo Impact, and Photo Paint
-Proficient in Microsoft Office, 2000, XP, Vista, Project, Visio, and Small Business Accounting
