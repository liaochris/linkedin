James Coyle MA MIoEE's Summary

JCR Network Services is a growing business offering business support and consultancy to both public and private clients throughout Ireland and the UK. The company, which is located in Eglinton (Londonderry), began trading in 2009 and has over 40 years of experience in the Business Consultantancy sector.
With a range of sectoral knowledge and experienced directors, JCR Network Services delivers a range of business diagnostic, sales support and mentoring to their clients. The services are intended to improve sales capability, enabling customers to leverage the maximum benefits from their sales and marketing strategies.
JCR Network Services delivers a range of business diagnostic solutions that improve business capability and are designed for businesses that wish to make their business model fit to meet today's critical challenges.
Designed to leverage your business strategy, the range of solutions cover the areas of sales, marketing, customer experience and operations.
