Denise Garcia Brady's Summary

It is my goal to help as many people as I can through fitness to live a happy and healthy life.

My journey began while learning to coexist with personal health issues through Yoga, inspiring me to teach Yoga. I have my 200 hour Yoga Certification, as well as, Personal Training and Group Fitness certifications. I offer private, semi-private and mixed level sessions, while continuing my education in pursuit of my 500 hour Yoga certification.

I have completed three Tough Mudders in one year, along with one CMC. I enjoy time with my family, rock climbing and hiking.

I hope to never stop learning, as everyday you learn something new.
