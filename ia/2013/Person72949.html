Kathy Davis' Summary

Kathy Davis has 40 years of experience in both interior and graphic design. Her wide skill set led her to collaborate with some of the nation’s top architects and designers. Davis has worked on interior and graphic design and development for major and small public and private projects including: educational, civic, hospitality, corporate, housing, medical, and historical.

In 1991 Davis founded Kathy Davis Associates, Inc., which specializes in interiors, signage & graphics, and color & materials development for educational facilities. The firm focuses on creating schools which will become the center of the community. She wants to ensure clean, healthy, and nurturing learning spaces. Kathy Davis strives to create lasting designs for both current and future generations.

Kathy Davis incorporated her leadership and design skills as a key team member with leading architects on projects such as the Denver International Airport, San Bernardino Center for Individuals with Disabilities, Radisson Park Suite Hotel in New Orleans, LA, and ships for Lykes Brothers Steamship Company.

Davis has worked on many award winning educational designs including Lynwood High School, Eastlake High School, Shadow Hills High School, Juarez-Lincoln Elementary School Modernization and Arroyo Vista Elementary School. Some of her current projects include Palm Desert High School, Horizon Alternative High School, Raymond Cree Middle School, Western Municipal Water District, and Herbert Ibarra Elementary School.

Specialties

New Construction, Modernization, Reconstruction, Interior Design, Exterior Color/Materials Development, Signage, Wayfinding & Graphics
