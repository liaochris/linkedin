Dana Rubin's Summary

I'm a communications consultant who helps business leaders and public figures define themselves in the marketplace of ideas. The content I create translates knowledge, ideas and insights into powerful reputation and brand-building tools.

My areas of expertise are executive and leadership communications, speeches and presentations, messaging, media training, and presentation coaching. I know how to write the words and create the messages that will get printed, broadcast, tweeted, quoted, and make an impact.

My career spans years as an award-winning newspaper and magazine journalist for publications such as Reader's Digest, Texas Monthly, and Institutional Investor; TV news producer for Dan Rather Reports; and corporate speechwriter and communications consultant to public and private companies in the US and abroad.

As a coach, I've worked with executives, diplomats, politicians, authors, religious leaders, and other public figures. I help prepare speakers for presentations at conferences and business events. I teach a class in persuasive communication to MBA students at the NYU Stern School of Business and lead CLE workshops in public speaking to lawyers. I've been a guest lecturer in public speaking at Georgetown University and other institutions of higher education.

My clients have been quoted in all the major media outlets, and speeches I've written have been delivered at some of the world's most influential forums. Commencement addresses I've written have been delivered at major colleges and universities across America.
