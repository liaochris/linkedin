COACHING

My coaching involves the ability to understand you: to live where you live, think how you think, & understand your perspective. From this benchmark, I will place another perspective on top of yours stretching & growing you to gently see a different way. As a certified John Maxwell coach, I can see what you are going through & going to go through. Coaching is foreseeing, paving the way, coming alongside & adding value to you.

Individual Coaching - Executive and Career Transition

When I am working 1:1 with a client, it is my number one objective to help you reach your purpose, vision & goals by working through John Maxwell’s programs specific to your needs. Together, we will create a strategic plan that is a powerful way for you to gain clarity through a weekly process including interaction & accountability.

Group Coaching

When I am working in a group setting, although it’s not singular in nature, having the entire group’s input in thinking magnifies the richness of the discussions & results in developing solutions that are above & beyond what any individual can create. The cohesive bond that is formed empowers each person to develop the leader within & shine in a safe environment that nurtures the group as a whole. It is an experience unlike any other & follows the same premise as an academic think tank.

A History:

J Gernetzke & Associates, Inc. was founded in 1989 as a traditional executive search firm serving the retail industry. We have been involved in recruiting management personnel at all levels – manager, director and executive level – and across all functional organizational lines.

We have recruited nationally and have completed field and corporate assignments across all functional lines including: Operations, Marketing/Advertising, Merchandising/Merchandise Planning, Physical Distribution/Logistics/Transportation/Engineering, Finance/Accounting, Real Estate, Loss Prevention, Human Resources, Information Systems.
