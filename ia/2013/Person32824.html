Kerry Davis' Summary

Curious, highly-engaged multimedia journalist with an affection for beat journalism done with creativity. My goal is to find the interesting characters and feature stories within tech news, looking to find the human side of what can be a very technical and dry area.

Specialties: Video journalism, production, photography, audio collection, writing, interviewing and voice-over techniques. Proficient use of: Soundslides, Audacity, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Photoshop, html, CSS, CMS and linear and non-linear editing systems.
