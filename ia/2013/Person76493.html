Koen Smits' Summary

To adapt an (/the architect's) idea, bringing a solid lighting concept to the table that not only improves the esthetic and emotional quality of the building, but also reduces costs on installment, power supply and maintenance. Also, providing independent consult on subjects like the design and development of luminair design, light measurement setup, material analysis, light sources, etc.

Specialties: Lighting design, luminair design and analysis, lighting measurement, specular reflector development, return-on-investment analysis.
