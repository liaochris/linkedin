Lia James' Summary

I'm a connector and social media is one of my favorite tools. I live for connecting people, places, and things. Drawing connections between people, brands, services, products, and concepts is what drives my passion as a connector. I thrive off of digging for channels of communication and information that will enhance relationships. Using this information to then educate on the power of social media networking and the art of communication through these networks is key to successfully achieving the desired goal.

I want to take brands back to a time when genuine relationships were an essential part of business -- back to a time when the locals knew their butcher, postman, and corner market owner. Now, with the use of social media, businesses are capable of building these genuine relationships on a larger scale. Imagine having “locals” worldwide. Let me help you accomplish this.

Please feel free to contact me: lia at liajames dot com

Specialties

Social media marketing, social media communication training, social media strategy development, public relations research, social media mentoring, skill development, monitoring industry trends, tracking brand buzz, professional writing, and creative writing.
