Carol Garborg's Summary

My passion is to empower Christian parents to grasp both the privilege and responsibility of passing along faith principles to their children in a way that sizzles and pops with meaning.

I'm an author, parent educator, and speak who has over twenty years of experience working with children and families from a variety of ethnic backgrounds.

My goal is to train families, churches, and organizations nationwide in how to bring families together and coach and empower them to "do" faith family-style.

Specialties: • developing and writing out-of-the box ideas to drive home faith values
• communicating spiritual abstracts in a way that engages all the senses
• teaching spiritual formation in an intergenerational setting
• training others to use the intergenerational format
• motivating and coaching families to work together in the faith journey
