Lynda Buitrago's Summary

My specialty is functional nutrition, using metabolic testing and nutritional protocols to give your body the support it needs to perform at its best.

Hatha Yoga has been a part of my life for about 12 years and I am a yoga instructor certified by Yoga Alliance (RY-500). I specialize in gentle forms of yoga to benefit people of all ages and abilities.

I would be honored to include you on my journey and help you to begin a transformation of your own.
