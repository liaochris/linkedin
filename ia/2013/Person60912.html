Jeff Ferst's Summary

I am an abstract painter. My work has been termed life affirming, juicy and flagrantly flamboyant. I paint textural works in a bold palette on canvas. The work are lyrical - landscapes and imagery combined to create vibrant paintings on canvas and paper in oil - the works are tactile, organic and very much autobiographical. Much of my recent work is focused on the Sonoran desert in Arizona - the colours, the textures and the amazing sunsets.

Besides my original studio work I also do commissioned work as requested. Prints are also available and can be ordered individually for home decor applications or in multiple quantities for larger hospitality installations.

Specialties: I have been involved with various organizations over the years from the London Arts Council to the Waterloo Arts Council, to chair of the City of Cambridge Exhibition and Public Art Committee & Centre for the Arts, Cambridge Arts Guild Board member, etc, Most recently I am an elected member of the Society of Canadian Artists and the Ol Painters Assn of America.

Among many accolades and awards such as the bronze Medal from the Art Students League in NYC, my work has been selected for the State department Art in Embassies program.
