Melissa Potvin's Summary

Melissa Potvin is a Sr. Specialist in Global Business Operations and is dedicated to supporting the Global Presales Organization of 1,000 Professionals.

Melissa joined CA Technologies in 1999 and spent 8 years working in Professional Services. As a Delivery Director, she managed large teams of Consultants, Project Managers and Architects while supporting many different territories including Long Island, NYC, New England, CT and Financial Services. When Melissa left CA in 2007 she was Vice President of Services Operations for the Northeast.

After a few fun years home with her children, Melissa went back to work at Fatwire Software where she was the Director of Global Operations. Fatwire was later acquired by Oracle and Melissa returned to CA in early 2010 working in Mergers and Acquisitions. As part of the Acquisition Intergration Team, Melissa assisted with numerous Sales and Front Office integration efforts.

Prior to CA, Melissa spent five years at McKinsey & Company where she was a Project Manager and Lotus Notes Developer, and later managed a Global 24x7 call center.

Melissa has a Bachelor of Art (BA) degree in Public Communications from SUNY Buffalo State College and a Masters of Business Administration (MBA) from Dowling College.

In her free time, Melissa is an avid blogger and writes frequently about Work & Family Issues, Beauty and Style and enjoys sharing Organizing tips. Melissa was recently featured on NBC Today Show and Nightly News with Lester Holt in response to an article she wrote about Telecommuting..
