Barbie Miller's Summary

I am a Graphic illustrator, Designer & Writer creating work that is always evolving, but forever rooted in a Skate/Punk meets Picture Book narrative and modern graphics.

Writing and illustrating books for Children is my greatest passion and joy.
I am constantly inspired and excited to make art of all types.
Prior projects include: greeting card design, band poster design, surface design, handlettering, comics, zines, puppets, videos and pretty much anything that might involve a wildly creative touch and an adventurous spirit.

I am proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Fireworks), Microsoft Office Suite 2007 but as mentioned enjoy using a variety of artistic mediums and am open to anything.

My 13+ years of experience in the Stanford University Benefits dept. has honed my ability to plan, execute, assess, organize and manage projects successfully so that I am able to apply that clear thinking and dedicated work ethic to any creative project while maintaining design sensibilities.

Specialties:
Highly conceptual, an ideas gal, I lead by example (work hard and be nice to people), crafting pixel perfect digital design, unique illustrations and in general I'm hilarious.
