André Natta's Summary

I enjoy working in the field of community revitalization, particularly as it pertains to using the ever expanding world of social media as a tool to strengthen what's already there in terms of communication and the sharing of information in communities.

I also enjoy writing about how our ongoing digital revolution is having an effect on the development of cities. This influences my publishing and managing an online publication for Birmingham, AL that provides in-depth perspectives on the region and the people that make it what it is.
