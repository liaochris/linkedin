Licensed NYC based freelance hair artist & men's groomer Nedjetti is available for Editorial, Ads, Film/TV, Print, Red Carpet, Award shows, Runway, Theatre, Celebs, and personal projects as well as for tv, radio and print interviews.

Nedjetti is honored to be a 2013 Black Celebration Awards finalist for her dedication and contribution as a beauty professional within the African American community.

Nedjetti owner of Hair by Nedjetti Salon based in Bloomfield, NJ who also work at Mane Advocates in NYC is an int'l natural hair educator & expert, competition judge & LOreal Soft Sheen-Carson platform educator.

Nedjetti has ran her seasoned fingers through the tresses of Ilyasah Shabazz, Marsha Ambrosius, Jada Pinkett-Smith, Cedella Marley, Bree Williamson, Beth Ann Bonner, Melba Moore, Eva Marcille, Genevieve Gorder, James McDaniel, Darlene Love, Francisco Lachowski, Mos Def, Nate Parker, Uzo Aduba, Danielle Brooks, Clarke Peters, Adepero Oduye & others.

Nedjetti's sculptured hairstyles has graced the pages of over 50 fashion and beauty magazines including TIME, O MAGAZINE, ESSENCE, UPSCALE, PAPER, BRIDES NOIR, SOPHISTICATES BLACK HAIR, COLURES, FIASCO, PRESTIGE INT'L & THE NEW YORK TIMES.

Nedjetti was Dept Head Hair on "Red Hook Summer" directed by Spike Lee & "You're Nobody 'til Somebody Kills You", written & directed by Michael "Boogie" Pinckney with Spike Lee as executive producer.

Aug '11 Nedjetti was key hair to Opera vocalists Laura Aikin, Myrto Papatanasiu and Sunhae Im for their amazing performances at Jazz Lincoln Center for Don Giovanni with conductor Ivan Fischer. The cast was featured on the Weekend Art cover of The New York Times.

Nedjetti exercises her 4 innate p’s throughout her walk in life, professionalism, promptness, precision and personality & she'll bring a ray of light onto any creative job.

Let's create some magic on set :}
