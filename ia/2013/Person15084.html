John G. Singer's Summary

An impact player in the health and pharmaceutical sector: new strategic concepts, business innovation, systems thinking. Pioneered 'strategy at a system level' as a unique platform for growth, innovation, and market integration. Developed methodology to design new forms of marketing and consumer-driven growth based on principles of complex adaptive systems.

Guest speaker on system-level competition at leading organizations and schools of business, including the RAND Corporation and Cambridge University, and an adjunct professor on strategy and innovation at ESCP Europe in Paris and McGill University in Montreal.
