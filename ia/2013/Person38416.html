Jeni Swerdlow's Summary

• DRUMMM Rhythmic Events provides innovative, hands-on drum circle programs that facilitate teambuilding, wellness, and celebration for corporate and community events.
• Founded in 2000 by San Francisco Bay Area percussionist and art therapist Jeni Swerdlow, DRUMMM’s events have captivated hundreds of thousands of participants at events in the United States and abroad.
• An interactive DRUMMM program is a fun way to invigorate your team, inspire creativity, stimulate communication, and get everyone working together toward a shared group goal.
• We bring all the instruments. No musical “talent” or prior experience is necessary to participate fully in a DRUMMM event.

Specialties

Corporate Teambuilding, Experiential Presentations, Health & Wellness, Entertainment, Festivals
