Jeff Evans' Summary

Private parties and company events
are more fun with magician Jeff Evans


Invite Jeff Evans to your event and you’ll
- hear screams of laughter when he performs the “world’s scariest card trick”,
- see looks of amazement when one of your friends does magic to their own surprise, and
- feel great when your guests thank you for planning such a successful time!

Many Fortune 500 companies call on Jeff to perform for their events. Boeing, Microsoft, Pepsi Cola, and R. E. I. are just a few of his repeat clients. Like you, they know that they can depend on Jeff for top-quality entertainment that makes people glad they came.

But don’t let the corporate talk scare you – Jeff is one of the busiest and best in the business for private parties and family celebrations, too!

Jeff lives in Olympia, Washington and performs throughout the Pacific Northwest and beyond with his air travel-friendly show.

To learn more, call Jeff Evans in Olympia at (360) 412-5064 or toll-free (877) 412-5064. Or, send e-mail to jeff@amazementproductions.com.
