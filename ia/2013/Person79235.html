Laurie Gronlund Mortenson, Career Coach & Resume Writer's Summary

Ready to embark on the career journey of your life? Looking for something more than nine-to-five? I help job seekers move into interesting, fulfilling jobs that complement their lifestyle. Wherever you are—young professional, mid-career, new graduate, relocated spouse—it’s never too late for a satisfying career.

I’m a career coach | résumé writer | founder of LegWork Résumés and Career Services, a one-stop career management shop. As a career consultant for REA Career Services, I provide job search and relocation assistance to folks in transition. My credentials include a Master of Education in Counseling, Certified Career Management Coach (CCMC), Nationally Certified Résumé Writer (NCRW), and 10+ years of career management and academic advising experience.

My résumés are featured in Barron’s fourth (newest) edition of “Résumés that POP! Designs that Reflect Your Personal Brand” and JIST’s fifth (newest) edition of “Gallery of Best Résumés.”

As a life-long learner and information junkie, I’m a frequent participant in professional development programs sponsored by Career Coach Academy, Career Director’s International, and The National Résumé Writers’ Association.
