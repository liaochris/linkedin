Ann Pillman's Summary

I strive to create understandable and clear technical documentation that is accessible to a variety of audiences. My technical background (BS EE) and work experience as an engineer and software developer makes it easy for me to "talk the language" of the subject matter experts. Then, I take that information (which is often highly technical) and extract the relevant user-focused aspects to create end-user documentation.

* Task-based user guides, quick reference instructions, installation instructions
* Training aids and instructor training manuals
* Online help (Web-based and other forms)

* Product specifications and requirements documents

Ann Pillman's Patents

  • Process for correcting across-the-head nonuniformity in thermal printers

    • United States Patent 4827279 A
    • Issued June 16, 1988
    Inventors: Ann Pillman, Rick Lubinsky, James Schmitt

    A method and apparatus of the present invention corrects the nonuniformity in the printing density between the printing elements of a thermal print head by first printing across a transparent receiver with each element of the head activated with equal inputs (flat field). The print transmittance values are read from the transparent receiver using, for example, a microdensitometer, and an adjustment factor for each heating element is computed and maintained in storage to be combined with the number of heating pulses assigned to each of the respective heating elements as they perform their normal printing function.
