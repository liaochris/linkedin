Bobbi Jo Kitchen's Summary

An extraordinary beauty, Bobbi Jo exploded onto the video modeling scene as a lead in the Young Buck music video for the single, “I know You Want Me”, featuring Jazze Pha. As a model/actress, she has since been recognized by artists and casting directors as a woman who definitely stands out above the rest. Since appearing in the video, it has been a non-stop grind for this charismatic, blue-eyed beauty, who has since been spotlighted in magazines such as XXL Magazine and Source Magazine, Blackmens magazine modeling Gucci w/ Trey Songs, also a known face for Coogi Clothing’s Women’s Line. She appeared in music videos for Lloyd Banks, Hot Rod, Prodigy of Mobb Deep and most recently playing 50 Cent’s Baby Mother in his new video “I Get Money”. She is most recognized for being the first professional winner of BET’s Model Mission Show.Coming from Bushwick Brooklyn, New York to a single mother, Bobbi had to grow up quickly and learn how to take care of herself at an early age. Spending 10 years of her youth in foster care, she moved around a lot during her younger years, and It is partially because of her rough upbringing, that she strives to succeed in whatever she does. At the same time she refuses to compromise her integrity and standards.
Currently a student at SUNY Old Westbury in Long Island, NY, Bobbi Jo definitely believes that education is just as important as being noticed in the entertainment industry. With a degrees in Media& Communication major w/ the English Minor. Be on the look out for Bobbi Jo’s feature in the “Notorious BIG” Movie and her Principle Role in the Spike produced“Perfect Age of Rock and Roll”. She most recently shot, "The Urban Beauty Hair Commercial" to be aired on NBC, CBS, and BET southeast.Most recently She worked on set of "Sex in the City 2" , "Wall Street 2", and Jay-Z's Roca wear Commercial, and modeling for OUTRE's 2010 Hair Line Campaign.
