Angela Watson's Summary

Hello and thank you for visiting my profile page. Working as a Coaching and Training Consultant, Executive Coach, and Speaker, I am passionate about helping individuals and organisations achieve their potential.

I work with a wide range of clients assisting them to increase their impact and influence, and embed a coaching culture in their organisations.

Organisations I have worked with include the Thames Valley Leadership Academy, Berkshire Healthcare NHS Trust, NHS South of England Courses and Conferences, as well as Merton Education Authority, and United Learning. I am also a postgraduate lecturer delivering Institute of Leadership and Management coaching skills programmes for individuals and organisations.

I have many years experience in leading teams to implement change for the real benefit of patients - including the set up of 'one stop' cardiology services; 1:1 coaching services; and leadership development programmes to develop capable and competent improvement leaders in NHS General Practice. My work has been published in the International Journal of Clinical Leadership and ILM Edge Magazine.


Through Pawz4Thought, I take my Golden Retriever Harry to work with me to help business people enhance their communication and leadership skills. We demonstrate how we work together using verbal and non-verbal communication and groups and individuals learns how to apply similar principles to their own business, work or life situations.


I encountered my form of 'dog whispering' whilst training with Obedience Champion, Neil Short, and quickly identified numerous links with personal and business development.

Hear more about my work via a streamed podcast interview hosted by Infinite Pie http://www.infinitepie.co.uk/podcasts/ipt-ep-39-angela-watson/#tc-comment-title

Follow me on twitter @angelawatsonuk and @pawz4thoughtuk
