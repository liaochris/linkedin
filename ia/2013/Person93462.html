Michelle Greer's Summary

Well-rounded internet marketing specialist with experience across disciplines. Proven track record of building earned media campaigns and strategies both in house and as a consultant. Experienced, ROI-driven product marketer in B2B and infrastructure space.

Early adopter of social media technologies with hands-on experience and results using various social media platforms. Winner of the first ever Texas Social Media Award in 2009. Previous marketing consulting clients include HP, eHarmony, Indeed.com, and The Science Channel. Contracted as Social Media Trainer for Breeze Marketing and Door Number Three agencies. Previous campaigns have been featured in the Huffington Post, Mashable, Yahoo News, O'Reilly Radar and several books about social media. Speaker at TechCrunch Disrupt 2010.


Specialties: agile marketing, social media monitoring and measurement, product marketing, branding, copywriting, website analytics, blogging, email marketing, search engine optimization, and software sales.
