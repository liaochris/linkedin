Anna Buglaeva's Summary

I specialize in business development, marketing strategy and content creation, in other words parts of business vital to a company’s success.

If you are reading this, you understand the importance and value of content, its delivery and reach. Creating a fairly valuable product and dumping information about it using a variety of social media outlets is arguably easy. However, engagement in business development driven by the creation and execution of a solid marketing strategy, which revolves around easily digestible, fun and interactive content is not your average walk in the park. Here is where I utilize my unique skill set in market research, marketing strategy, business development, logistics and planning.

My industry of choice is Real Estate, an industry that is focused on supplying one of Earth's most important goods - land. Aside from its critical importance to our existence, real estate is a rather challenging industry that requires in-depth knowledge of a variety of fields and studies as well as ability to work with individuals from all walks of life. A challenge that I welcome.

I am particularly keen on investment real estate. I work with US based institutional investors, smaller portfolio holders, as well as foreign investors interested in the US real estate.

Types of Investment: Single Family, Commercial, NPN*, REO*, PN*
Purpose of Investment: Buy and Hold, Flip, Fix and Flip, Rental Income
Rental Type: Long Term Rental, Short Term Rental, Vacation Rental
Average price per property: $100,000.
Location: FL, GA, NC, TX, OH, IN, VA, MD, AZ
*These are offered on nationwide bases.
