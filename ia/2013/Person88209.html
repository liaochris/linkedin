Mary Shea Wilson's Summary

Mary Shea Wilson is an award winning Real Estate Agent with over $50 million in closed transactions. Mary joined Benoit Mizner Simon & Co with seven years of experience in residential real estate in the Wellesley area. Mary's knowledge of the marketplace gives her clients the advantage when searching for the perfect home or marketing a specific property. She is accurate, thorough and tuned in to the delicacy of both pricing and timing. Mary has been a Wellesley resident since 1995 and has many contacts and connections in the area. She holds a Bachelor of Arts from Villanova University.
