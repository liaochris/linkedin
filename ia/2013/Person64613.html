JM Bonthous is founder & CEO of Seamless Social, a Los Angeles-based online marketing agency. Seamless Social helps clients build community and generate revenue online.

JM's expertise includes online growth marketing, YouTube & video marketing, lead generation, content marketing, inbound marketing, social media marketing, influencer marketing, and community building.

He holds a PhD in strategic management from the University of Paris, France, has authored two books on business intelligence and taught business transformation at the Stern School of Business at New York University.

Seamless Social has a particular expertise in B2B and the marketing of complex products. The firm partners with clients to:
• Identify untapped opportunities in their markets.
• Generate leads, score them, and nurture them until they are ready to be passed to the sales team.
• Build community and engagement for customers or users
• Grow revenues and brand equity.

Seamless Social also serves as outsourced CMO for technology startups with disruptive technology solutions and deep business domain knowledge.

Seamless Social is creator of MyChannl, a powerful market intelligence mobile app.developments in their ecosystem.

Seamless Social operates as a distributed agency, bringing the best specialists to each job. The agency is customer-centric, results-driven, metrics-focused, budget-friendly and develops long-term, partnering relationships with clients.
