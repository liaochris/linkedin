Dak Dillon's Summary

Always looking for the next opportunity and fostering relationships with my clients, I help provide my vision for those I work for.

That vision is driven by a deep desire to explore colors and how they can combine to interact. This vision is part of my movement and drive, pushing me to new projects and new adventures.

Though my career started with web design, I’ve taken many different paths: graphic design, marketing, photography and video production.

Each one is part of my creative world, which I deliver every day. To learn more about me, visit my website at www.dakdillon.com

Specialties

Adobe Master Collection CS5, Photo Mechanic, Final Cut Pro, 3Ds Max, Sketchup, ability to transmit on deadline, studio and location lighting, website design & development, video production and lighting, SEO, social media marketing, web analytics, 3D rendering, AP style
