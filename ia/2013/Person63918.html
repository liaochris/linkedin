Jim Higley's Summary

After a successful 23-year career in commercial real estate, after a life-altering detour with prostate cancer, and because of a life affirming opportunity to experience 24/7 parenting with my three children during a critical time in their lives, I now focus my full-time efforts on being a forward-thinking, action-oriented and visible leader in both the cancer and parenting communities throughout the world. I use my multi-media platform as an author, columnist, radio show host, and social media influencer to support cancer causes - especially prostate and testicular cancer - and "DadVocate" for raising emotionally and physically healthy kids.

Specialties

Motivational Speaking to groups of all sizes, Brand Spokesperson, Television and Radio Appearances, Social Media Promotions, Parenting Expert, Cancer Advocate. Outstanding Writing and Communication Skills, Marketing and Branding, Public Relations, Collaborations and Partnership Development, Grassroot Movements, Thought Leader, Social Media Marketing, Organizational Management, Financial Reporting

Jim Higley's Publications

  • Bobblehead Dad: 25 Life Lessons I Forgot I Knew

    • Greenleaf
    • June 1, 2011
    Authors: Jim Higley

    Jim Higley was a forty-year-old bobblehead. Just like those collectible figurines with oversized, bouncy heads, he’d put on a smiling face and bobble through his hectic, overflowing days.

    Higley’s bobbling came to a screeching halt with the diagnosis of cancer, surgery, and a summer of healing. More than a cancer story, however, "Bobblehead Dad" puts you in a front row seat as the author discovers the illuminating parallels between events in his childhood and his adulthood.

    Written in an informal but eloquent style, "Bobblehead Dad" keeps you laughing, crying and-most importantly-thinking. Higley’s distinctive storytelling rhythm, combined with a knack for handling heavy topics with an embraceable voice, quickly draws you into his experiences-while launching you on your own journey of self-discovery and reflection.
