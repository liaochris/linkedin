Beverly Bergman's Summary

I am a speaker, teacher, conversion strategist and award-winning content developer.

Known as The Queen of Copywriting, I help entrepreneurs and experts to attract more of their ideal clients and make more money.

I use marketing strategies focused on:

* Marketing content that optimizes your message and compels people to buy from you
* Increased click-throughconversion rates for greater results

After 18 years of copywriting and brand development and management for corporations in San Diego, I started BB Marketing Solutions to help small business owners have their marketing messages make a greater impact by using more compelling language.

Shortly after, I started working with a San Diego coach, Dr. Barbara Warren. Inspired by her coaching and my experience editing her books, I began combining my desire to help others reach their goals with my passion for creating marketing, writing and editing projects -- and Copywriting For Coaches was born.

I speak, teach and consult on creating highly targeted marketing content that converts both online and offline. I create training products that teach others how to write persuasive marketing copy. One of my tools is the “Money Matrix Hourglass” system that I developed for exponentially growing any business via Squeeze Pages. My E-book: "Squeeze Page Juice: How to Create High Converting Lead Generation Opt-In Pages" is due out soon.

What sets me apart from other marketing copywriters?

• 18 years of experience in financial services marketing
• 5 years of experience focused on coaching professionals
• Past president of San Diego Professional Coaches Alliance (SDPCA).
• Students tell me that "the light goes on" when they work with me
• I focus on direct response copy that will make people buy from you.

With over 25 years of experience in marketing and copywriting,I am proud that most of my clients come by referral and organic searches.

Specialties

Speaking, teaching and writing about marketing content that converts for landing, squeeze or opt-in pages, sales pages, websites, PPC ads, audio and video scripts, press releases and more.

Great affiliates for us are graphic designers, virtual assistants, marketing coaches, speaking coaches, video specialists and financial advisors.
