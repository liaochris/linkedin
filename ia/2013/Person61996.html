Jennifer A. Stewart DVM's Summary

Experienced veterinarian providing consultations in veterinary medicine and surgery for small animals. Population medicine and public health is also a topic in which I provide consultation and guidance.

Specialties

I have a special interest in veterinary dentistry, veterinary ophthalmology, and veterinary dermatology, and veterinary behavior especially as it leads to a better understanding of an animal in a household to improve the human-animal bond.
