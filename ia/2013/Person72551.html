Kasia Wyser-Pratte's Summary

I am passionate about creating strategy-driven work that results in engagement between brands and audiences. With love for design, technology, and storytelling, I look for ways these three can can be merged and expressed via social means and digital interaction.

I have a fifteen-year proven track record in coming up with winning ideas, building and mentoring creative teams, collaborating with diverse subject experts and fostering an inspiring work environment. My success is driven by bringing innovative ideas to live and dedication to producing world-class creative work. I am senior enough to mentor but willing and able to roll up my sleeves.
