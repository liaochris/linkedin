  • [Lead Instructor at] NEXT Madrid Abril 2013

    • April 2013 to May 2013
    Team Members: Mario López de Ávila Muñoz, Manuel Caño, Borja Prieto, Wilhelm Lappe, David Casas, Javier Criado Nesofsky, Santiago Rodríguez Llorente

    NEXT is a comprehensive curriculum rooted in Steve Blank's Customer Development methodology, built around in-person interactions with potential customers, and delivered by an accredited NEXT instructor.
    NEXT is a five-week program that combines a workshop-style classroom structure with independent assignments.

    Each week, participants will complete required video lectures provided by Steve Blank and supplemental readings from the course Instructor.

    In addition, participants will meet for a weekly, three-hour workshop led by the Instructor. During workshops, participants will present their customer discovery insights and receive critical feedback from the course Instructor, team of mentors, and their peers.

    This curriculum thrives on active, in-person customer validation, and participants will be expected to engage with customers.
