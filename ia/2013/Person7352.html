Andy Birchwood's Summary

I'm a creative digital designer and digital design strategist. I create and interpret brands into technically elegant website and app design frameworks that developers love, and visual experiences that users enjoy.

I started in interactive TV, developing interfaces and consumer content for small screens, and moved to designing websites for the broadcast industry including Flextech, Disney (Jetix) and the BBC (CBeebies/CBBC). I've dabbled in iPhone games (Fantastic Mr Fox, Scott Mills Flirt Doctor), and lately I've moved from fluffy fun to hard business at TUI (Thomson & First Choice), where I manage a team of 5 UI designers alongside a strong team of UX professionals.

I believe in sweating the details - for large companies, a strong digital design framework is a primer for digital growth and a big contributor to brand equity.

Specialties: Digital brand strategy, design strategy, standards compliant web design and front-end development (xhtml/css), iPhone app design, UI design, Flash animations and websites, branding and identity design.
