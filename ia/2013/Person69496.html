Joyce Dungee Proctor's Summary

Joyce has over 20 years of business management, leadership, human resources, staffing and coaching experience. She has spent years inspiring people around the world to make positive changes in their work and personal life that have empowered them to move beyond contemplating change to make the changes necessary to pursue their passion and fulfill their dreams. A much sought after professional speaker, certified career coach, and published author of the, Take the Limits off series one: Nine Ways to Stop Talking and Start Doing. Series two will be released fall 2011. Joyce teaches a variety of courses at Take the Limits off Career Services a Seminars by Joyce company. She is available for one-on-one, group and corporate coaching experiences. She also offers outplacement services.

Specialties

Leadership Development, Team building, Business Management, Full Cycle Recruitment, Career/Business Coaching, Soft Skills Training, Out Placement Services, Networking, Consulting, Public Speaking, Mentoring, Sales and Published Author
