Edith Moricz, M.B.A., RFC's Summary

For more than 2 decades, I have been helping small businesses & non-profit orgs significantly diversify their revenue streams and increase their levels of corporate sponsorship.

• Financial consultant for private retail clients (I created financial security for families through my work at Merrill Lynch and Fidelity Investments).

• Fundraiser (I raised over $7Million for local & national charities).

•Creator and Chief Editor of www.BeyondSuccessOnline.com (helping small businesses maximize their growth by sharing my 1:1 interviews regarding the Best Practices of 200 + successful global entrepreneurs)

•Adjunct Faculty Member (teaching advanced fundraising, non-profit management and budgeting at Cambridge College Graduate School of Management and Bunker Hill Community College).

•Creator of non-profit management curriculum (covering A-Z on fundraising, budgeting, corporate sponsorships, special events, social media marketing and more) ; to assist students in understanding NPOs and guide non-profit leaders in exceeding their development goals.

• Authored "The Young Adult's BluePrint for Success : How to Land Your First Job Before Graduation" (guiding teens toward successful adulthood & landing job before college graduation)

I encourage leaders of non-profit org and small businesses to contact me for assistance with revenue diversification, corporate sponsorships & special event planning. I also create customized non-profit management workshops geared toward your budget and your fundraising needs. My workshops will propel your organization toward immediate rapid growth.

Specialties: Asset Growth Management, New Business Development, Non-Profit Development and Marketing,Customized Fundraising Workshops, Special Event Planning,Corporate Sponsorships, Grant Writing, Estate Planning, Client/Donor Relations,Communications, Web Site Design, Social Media Marketing & Communications
