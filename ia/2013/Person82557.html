Lori Vande Krol's Projects

  • Project Digital Sanity

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Lori Vande Krol, Jocelyn Coverdale, Tracy Parks

    As the saying goes, Project Digital Sanity is “an idea whose time has come.” In fact, Project Digital Sanity is the answer to the cry of professionals world-wide struggling to maintain or regain digital sanity in this fast-paced, ever-changing information deluge.

    Was your first day on the job the last day you had an empty inbox? Knowledge workers process 167 messages each day and spend 30% of the day creating, organizing, reading and responding to email.

    Wouldn’t it be ideal to be able to find what you need when you need it? 50% of initial searches for digital information are unsuccessful on the first and second attempt.

    Is Digital Sanity Possible? Are you one of the 49% of folks feeling you can’t handle the volume of information you are called to manage? Have you wondered how to clear your backlog manage your digital files, optimize email processing, and keep information secure?

    Project Digital Sanity's unique coaching and training program offers proven methods, tools, resources and training to:

    -Reduce digital clutter;
    - Create intuitive organizing structures for digital information;
    - Integrate methods and procedures to manage workflow;
    - Leverage built-in features to process email and get out from under your “inbox”; and
    - Discover tools and processes to move toward a more paperless office.

    Imagine what will be different when your digital desktop, files, documents and email are under control!

    - You’ll be positioned to execute projects efficiently;
    - Prioritize your day to focus on highest value activities;
    - Collaborate effectively with peers, work groups, clients and customers;
    - Feel less stressed and more in control;
    - Leave the office at the office with increased peace of mind.
