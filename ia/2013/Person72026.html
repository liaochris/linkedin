Karen Baker Ruppel, MS, SPHR's Summary

Power 2 Perform Consulting LLC is located in Northeast WI. We offer Organizational Development, Leadership Development, Coaching, and Human Resource Consulting. Industry experience includes; retail, hospitality, manufacturing, education, insurance, telecommunications, and not-for-profit.

Products and workshop offerings include; Everything DiSC® products (personal assessments), True Colors® (personal assessment), and SendOutCards (an online greeting card and gift company).

~Everything DiSC ®offers facilitator kits, comparison reports, and profiles for sales, management, leaders, and teams. The Everything DiSC products service both individual and team development. Coaching, workshops, and teambuilding services are available.

~True Colors® is easy to understand, remember, and apply. Gain an in-depth understanding of your unique personality spectrum. This product services both individual and team development. Coaching, workshops, and teambuilding services are available.

~SendOutCards is an online greeting card and gift company where everyone is able to act on their promptings and share gratitude. The online system offers a contact manager to keep us organized, Picture Plus 2.0 for picture storage, over 15,000 card images, and great gift products. You design the card and SendOutCards will stuff, stamp, and ship your cards and gifts. Stay in touch with clients, family, friends, and prospects efficiently and timely.

Services include leadership coaching where we focus on business results and performance while conducting purposeful discussions to drive improved performance through personal awareness. Help your leaders maximize their potential, build commitment and hold themselves accountable.

Our Human Resource consulting is targeted towards competency development, internal communications, rewards and recognition, employee relations, organizational planning, and more. Let twenty plus years of experience help you and your organization.
