Kyle John Ijichi's Summary

When I was 5 years old I jumped into a swimming pool and taught myself how to swim. Stepping into the unknown has always been my preference, even throughout my education and career.

I have 7 years of experience working for tech startups to a tech giant, and everything in between.

Today, as a recruiter for Yelp, I educate, guide and represent both candidates and clients through the hiring process. I help connect the right candidates to the right roles, and the reverse. I love contributing to, and working towards, something bigger than myself, and surrounding myself with people who can relate.

I’ve worn many hats in my career - manager, director, advertiser, administrator, tutor, graphic designer, salesman and technical support. As a result, I have the ability to manage multiple projects from various perspectives. However, the most important skill-set that I’ve gained is the ability to be flexible and, especially, patient.

Throughout my experiences the phrase “sink or swim” has given me great perspective on that crazy 5-year old version of myself and everything leading up to now.

Reach out to me if you're interested in any position on Yelp's job board, or if you need a wedding DJ :)

Specialties: technical recruiting, communication, client-relationship management, graphic design, non-profit management, operations management, social-media, start-ups, education policy, sales, negotiating, presentations, writing, relationship-building, sense of humor (usually).
