Laura D. Field's Summary

Creativity is a word, when properly described, is that of creating something new and viable that causes the stimulation of the intellect and emotions of the receivers’ visual receptors. Combining creativity with the compassion for people and life, you have a person who enjoys the challenge of making something meaningful to themselves as well as those around them.

The ideal position for Laura would allow her to combine her positive writing skills along with her solid business skills,w/analytical and supervisory skills. Her preferred interest is in healthcare, yet knows that her compassion for people and treating them well, can be used effectively in many avenues.

Laura seeks to work within an environment where people value and respect one another, with an outlook of seeing the positive in life.

Currently Laura is working on an inspirational book that takes a look at life with obstacles and overcoming them with a change of attitude and forgiveness. Healing begins within, yet with the healing, one is able to look within themselves and understand their responses to daily events. Laura strives to make everyday an opportunity to grow, always open to learning something new, while making a difference in life itself.
