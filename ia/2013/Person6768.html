Andrew Armour's Summary

I am the founder of Benchstone - a consultancy focused on helping businesses to plan, build and improve key marketing projects & high value relationships.

I provide interim consulting, project management, workshop facilitation and training courses - including MarketingCafe, CollaborationCafe and tailored marketing team and project seminars. My clients include; Versatile Connections (WiFi projects), The AA, Powwownow, 4CM Communications, Krems Business School (Vienna).

Prior to launching Benchstone I held senior marketing and agency roles in the UK and internationally working for major media brands such as Walt Disney, OfficeMax, TVNZ, PRS for Music - and more recently Hibu (Yell.com). I hold degrees in both Law and Marketing and was educated at Auckland Business School, Bournemouth University and Henley Business School.

I live in Hampshire and work in London, where I am based at Adam Street Club and The RSA - both close to The Strand (Embankment / Covent Garden).

If you'd like to catch up for an informal chat over a very good cup of coffee (or tea...) - please do get in touch either from here or via the Benchstone website. http://www.benchstone.co.uk/Contact/
