Lucy Sawin 0207 776 9742's Summary

I joined Futureheads in August 2012. I work as a resourcer in the digital design team and I help to build relationships with candidates and clients. My role is to find the best talent in creative roles and source great people!

I am very interested in up and coming trends in digital design and commercial art. If you are a designer with lots of ideas and you are seeking new opportunities please contact me with your portfolio at lucy@wearefutureheads.co.uk.

Before I embarked on a career in recruitment I was studying Fine Art at Kingston University. During this time, I took a year out to study abroad in Michigan. I was very inspired by the culture and working abroad. I love to experience many styles of art and design and meet new people!
