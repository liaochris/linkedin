David Ansley's Summary

If you need to deliver health and medical information to consumers and make sure they understand and act on it, then you need me on your team. I specialize in all the steps of that process: business planning, product development, content strategy, publishing systems, content architecture, writing and editing, web publishing, social media, print publishing, and metrics.
