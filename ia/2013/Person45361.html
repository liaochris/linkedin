John Fortner's Summary

Having gained over 20 years of diverse experience and successes, I feel that I am doing my best work when I'm bridging the gap between the best talent and the teams that need them. Beginning as a scrappy agency recruiter in Seattle, I worked my way up the ranks, winning sales contests and opening new regional offices along the way.

Having built a strong base of experience in the Staffing Industry, I moved to the corporate side, working for companies, such as, Intel Corp, Nike, Juniper Networks, and now Cisco Systems.

I have natural curiosity about human behavior and technology, a thirst for knowledge, and a strong desire to help people, which is why I love what I do.
