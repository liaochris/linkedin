Dr.Joyce Knudsen, Ph.D.'s Summary

My FIRST career was in modeling, pageants and charm schools. It has been said that people change careers three times during their lifetime.

It was time for my SECOND career: IMAGE. I believed that Image was, not so much about appearance, but introspection. My specialty is self-esteem. I realized that the number of people with low self-worth is staggering. That disturbed me. These people have tolerated the verbal insults of people throughout their life. I've seen CEO's break down and cry in their offices, because of something a parent or teacher said to them 20 years ago! I understand. I've been there. How I dealt with this was to become an author and write a self-esteem series called Successful Failures. The 2nd Edition is now #7 on Amazon..

I am.a person who defies all limitations, who worked on my own self-esteem and moved on to undeniable success in both my personal and profession life. If you feel bad inwardly, know that this is a temporary condition. You can and will achieve if you work hard, as I did., if you allow yourself to work with a professional who has not only been there, but has the credentials to help you achieve your dreams. This, my friends, will be my pleasure.

My THIRD career is mastering Social Media, Radio Shows & Writing. Being a radio talk show host came out of a love of interviewing people on both a TV show in Detroit; now a radio show called The ImageMaker® Media Presents. I enjoy helping successful people by providing an outlet for CEO's Musicians, Celebrities and Authors for Promotion.. This isInternet-based and is streamlined, worldwide on YouTube.

All three careers are intertwined and have helped me grow throughout the years. One just led to the other. Specialties: Elite Image & Coaching Programs (work with me; not a computer!) Radio Host and Writing Self-esteem books. I am Internationally Published by David James Publishing.

I love making other people's dreams come true.and paying it forward.
