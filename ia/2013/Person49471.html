Jeff Good's Summary

After 20 years of for profit experience with organizations like Procter & Gamble and General Mills, I moved in 2005 into the not-for-profit world through the YMCA and later Junior Achievement. With the foundation of Goodworld Consulting in late 2010 and leadership of the Charity and Non-Profit real estate practice at Cresa since 2011, entirely new ways to create breakthrough opportunities and solve problems in the charitable sector have opened up. Now my passion, skills and experience benefit charities big and small right across the GTA.

Through Cresa's tenant-only advisory services and along with other real estate experts on this team, I guide my clients through an objective process whereby they become well educated with respect to real estate decisions and transactions. I ensure they maximize the overall value of their real estate decisions, and provide my clients with a high level of hands-on service, knowledge of their sector, experience, attention to detail and a focus on building long-term relationships.

I'm also very involved in our community, volunteering with Scouts Canada, as a Charitable Board member, and as a long-time Park Cleanup coordinator in my neighbourhood. As a proud father of two great teens I'm also very clear about why the working with great organizations matters so much: I want to leave the world a better place for all of our children.
