Matt Walton's Summary

An experienced digital product owner who has been developing and delivering complex web, mobile and tablet products for TV, radio, magazine and book businesses for over 12 years. Skilled at bringing together and leading cross discipline teams of editorial, technical and creative people using agile project management techniques and adept at communicating with a range of stakeholders to ensure the timely delivery of a coherent, high quality and innovative final product.

Find out more at mattwalton.co.uk
