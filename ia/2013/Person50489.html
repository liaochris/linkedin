Greta Sebald's Summary

Greta Sebald Creative, Inc. is a graphic design firm with a twist. My driving force at GS Creative is graphic design. However, it gets better, because the twist is services in the areas of marketing and writing which complement and enhance the graphic designs. I believe this strong combination of skills positively impacts every aspect of work I do. I continually strive to take fresh, creative approaches toward all concepts, design and execution.

With all of the experience gained since starting in 1995, one of my greatest strengths at GS Creative is my ability to provide expertise at so many levels. I offer complete project management from the initial concept to the final press check. Of course, I work with clients to provide the customized services they require. In addition I have a network of talented professionals such as photographers, translators, imprint specialists and printers to complete any project. I can arrange for outside services and manage the entire process for you, making you free to focus on the many other tasks you have to do.

My end goal is to be creatively effective. I am only successful when my clients are successful. Below are a list of just some of the clients I've worked with:

Specialties

Greta Sebald Creative is the founder of Teeny Peanut Designs. Teeny Peanut Designs offers custom graphic designs and expertise for adoptive parents in search of a baby. I create all of the items needed in their adoption journey, including profile letters, books and websites, outreach materials and coming home announcements. I also wrote the book, 'Teeny Peanut Insightful Ideas' to help them develop the most effective materials for domestic adoption.
