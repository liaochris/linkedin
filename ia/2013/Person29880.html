Daniel Rebman's Summary

I have worked in a variety of formats and mediums, and enjoy everything from publication design, poster design, packaging, information design, branding, illustration, type design, and photography. I enjoy working illustratively and try to incorporate illustration into my work as often as possible. I always try to create something new with every project I do, in an attempt to get a fresh look for every piece I design. Most of my work tends to either lean towards a more organic and hand-made quality, or a more stylized and geometric look. I am very interested in working with new and different mediums, and enjoy incorporating them into my work.

Specialties

Proficient in: Photoshop CS6, Illustrator CS6, InDesign CS5, Linoleum Block Carving, Wood Carving
