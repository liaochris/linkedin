Joshua Feinberg's Summary

Do you need more clients for your IT consulting, computer repair, or managed service provider (MSP) business?

I help SMB IT channel companies use proven inbound marketing to attract the right website visitors, convert visitors to leads, close sales with new clients, and delight clients for long-term retention.

With 57% of the typical B2B sales cycle over before most decision makers contact **any** vendor (Source: CEB/Corporate Executive Board), my #1 goal is to make sure that **your** firm gets found in the right places, by the right decision makers, at the right time.

I bring 23+ years of IT channel-related experience in marketing, selling to, and servicing clients in SMB IT consulting, enterprise IT, computer repair, education, financial services, telecom, and retail.

I’m Inbound Marketing Certified (HubSpot) and a former MCSE. Many know me as the author of the Computer Consulting Kit.

Before that, I was a columnist for the Microsoft Direct Access/Certified Solution Provider channel programs (MPN predecessors). I’m also author of the groundbreaking Building Profitable Solutions with Microsoft Small Business Server (Microsoft Press).

Many SMB IT veterans still remember my role as Contributing Editor to Selling Windows NT Solutions Magazine and occasional contributor to Windows NT Magazine and Inc.com

Although I have a BA in Economics from a top-ranked state university (Rutgers), my experience outside the classroom during those years as an IBM Collegiate Team Leader was infinitely more valuable: learning about campaign planning, segmentation, copywriting, content marketing, competitive positioning, developing strategic partnerships, and motivating a sales team.

On the personal side, I have a terrific wife, two young children, and am a Miami Marlins fan.

I’ve worked with SMB tech providers all over the USA, the UK, Canada, Australia, and many other nations around the globe. Please Connect with me to see how I can help your company find more great clients!
