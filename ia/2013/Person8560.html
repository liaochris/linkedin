Artist, CEO

Ann Rea, Inc.

Privately Held; Myself Only; Arts and Crafts industry

January 2006 – Present (7 years 8 months) San Francisco, CA

Ann Rea is a business savvy San Francisco-based artist known for her colors inspired by natural light.

As CEO of Ann Rea, Inc.

Ann's work is increasingly collectible and acquired by a growing list of collectors and celebrities across northern America and Europe.

Her talent is commended by Wayne Thiebaud, an American Art icon, and it continues to receive national media attention, most recently on the "Fine Living" network and in "The Wine Enthusiast" and "Fortune" magazines.

Ann Rea, Inc. offers profitable programs for the wine and interior design trade and select fine retailers.

Private collectors can acquire fine art prints, existing originals, and accessories on annrea.com or commission Ann to create a custom canvas scaled to suit their space.

The first of a series of books "'Colors of Terroir', an artist's tour of Sonoma", a collection of Ann's Sonoma vineyard paintings, will be published next year.

Ann Rea also is the founder of ArtistsWhoTHRIVE.com and as a marketing strategist she coaches and consults with artists across the globe one day a week.

"Take home a piece of the Wine Country", visit annrea.com.
