Andy Mowat's Summary

I am focused on improving the productivity of companies and executives through the leverage of people, process, and technology.

Years ago, when I hired my first personal assistant, I quickly understood the power of a remote workforce. At Elance, the leading platform for online employment, I drove development of the platform, sales/marketing, and partnerships necessary to support a global workforce in the cloud. Now at Prialto, I have combined my experience building business processes and managing a global talent pool.

Prialto helps companies and executives offload administrative processes by leveraging People, Process, and Technology. Our experience with sales teams, executives, and professional services firms, allows Prialto to quickly customize solutions to Member's needs and integrate our teams of Productivity Assistants to save you time and ensure consistency.
