David Leckie's Summary

Thanks for reading my profile - I am just as interested to know what it is that you do, so please let me know and get in touch.

In short I save company's time, streamline efficiency and reduce costs by looking after their most important asset - their staff.

Horrible, but a true fact: 55% of executives will die from a stress related illness, one in four will experience a mental health issue at some point in their lives. Perhaps this is why we've been asked to help an amazing but humbling 28,000 plus clients and counting.

I'm very proud to have started Mind Matters 'from scratch' in 1993 to make it into what it is today, a team of over 480 professionals supporting NHS, corporate and private clients in both the UK and Netherlands.

Mind Matters provides work-related Counselling, Critical Incident and Mediation services using CBT and Solution Focused therapeutic approaches. We provide bespoke training courses in Solution focused approaches and Stress Management strategies. We are recognised experts in the field of Critical Incident support and provide UK wide support coverage to organisations such as

Asda,
BUPA,
Disney Stores
Unilever
Pepsico
Marussia Formula 1 team

We have been been fortunate enough to have over 28000 clients referred to our organisation

This is why we are classed as one of the best in the UK at what we do.

Individually, I am an experienced radio broadcaster - have previously worked for BBC as co-presenter of 'Problemline', a long running weekly phone in. I'm keen to develop radio opportunities further should the right one arise. Finally, because I have always liked having more than one string to a bow, I'm a qualified (CELTA) teacher of English as foreign language and enjoy this as a 'sideline' to my proper job!
