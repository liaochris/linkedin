Dan Roberts' Summary

I am the Director of Business Development and Venture Screening at Tallwave, the largest digital/web venture accelerator in Arizona.

Previously I was the Chief Operating Officer for Maverick Angels, an international network of early-stage investors, service providers, and entrepreneur mentors. I have devoted my career to working with start-up entrepreneurs and funding sources to connect resources and commercialize high-growth ventures. My dedication to entrepreneurs extends beyond the U.S. borders with my work as an entrepreneur coach and innovation consultant with one of the largest banks in Europe. I sat on the Executive Committee of the California Coast Venture Forum (CCVF), a non-profit organization whose mission is to mentor, advise and promote growth of sustainable start-up companies. As a member of the G2G International Cleantech Incubator team, I was a mentor to early-stage cleantech entrepreneurs. I have served as a presentation judge for the Cleantech Open, the largest cleantech business competition in the world. I am also a founding manager of Mendocino Roots Charitable Foundation, a scholarship fund focused on providing financial support to underserved youth in Mendocino County that are pursuing higher education.

I have worked with hundreds of entrepreneurs to improve their investment pitch, while leveraging my extensive network to provide introductions to key resources. As an investor, I have deep experience in the angel investor due diligence process.

My journey to becoming an Eagle Scout further developed my strong work ethic and focus on preparedness.
