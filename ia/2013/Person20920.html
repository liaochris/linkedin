Cath Duncan's Summary

I help people who are struggling with grief to learn to live wholeheartedly after loss at http://www.rememberingforgood.com/. I train helping professionals to use creativity to support bereaved people over at The Creative Grief Studio: http://griefcoachingcertification.com/. I also co-curate the site about the option to bring stillborn and deceased babies home at http://whenyourbabydies.com/. I'm currently working on a Social Work Masters research dissertation on the topic of constraints in the grief of bereaved fathers, and I am a Research Assistant on an Intercultural Trauma book project.
