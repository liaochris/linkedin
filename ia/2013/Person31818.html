David Foster, EFO's Experience

Fire Chief

Village Fire Department

September 2010 – Present (3 years) Houston, Texas Area

Manage department providing fire prevention, fire protection and advanced life support transport services.

Fire Chief

DeKalb County Fire Rescue Department

January 2003 – February 2010 (7 years 2 months)

Direct and provide leadership for a Metro Atlanta Fire Rescue Department with $72m budget, 921 personnel, and 26 stations, 201,300 responses during 2008.

- Reduced response times by over half.
- Reduced fire loss by half.
- Reduced overtime by over $2 million annually.
- Increased return of spontaneous circulation, in cardiac arrest patients, to twice the national average.
- Developed and implemented a multi-tiered, public/private parternship Emergency Medical Service.

Assistant Fire Chief/ Operations Division

Gwinnett County Fire and Emergency Services

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Government Administration industry

July 1998 – January 2003 (4 years 7 months)

Managed Operations Division, 23 fire stations with over 850 personnel for the largest fire district in the State of Georgia, over 440 square miles. Served as acting Fire Chief and Gwinnett County EMA Director effective 9/11/2001.

Fire Officer

Eppley Fire Rescue Department

May 1990 – July 1998 (8 years 3 months)

Assisted in the development, implementation and management of the Eppley Fire Rescue Department. Managed A shift operations, supervised department training and infectious control programs. Acted in the position of Fire Chief in his absense. Received the City of Omana Life Saving Citation in 2001.
