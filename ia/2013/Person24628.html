Chris Trevor's Summary

I manage, consult, mentor and muse on back office, customer service and information management issues across charities and higher education, and have been in this field for about 23 years.

My track record includes delivering significant additional philanthropic funds and savings through matched funding, gift aid, efficiencies and insight in fulfilment/response processes, segmentation and analysis; recruiting, training and managing effective staff and teams, devising and implementing change, restructuring for growth, successful data migrations and mergers; and listening, learning from my mistakes and everything else, pursuing excellence, imagination and simplicity.

I have some new tricks and some old tricks, my approach to work is very much underpinned by philosophical perspectives and methodologies, accommodating psychology and collective dynamics as critical elements in effective business functions, along with principles of linearity and integrity in digital and analog processing mechanisms.

I also believe that resilient ethics, courageous and honest use of evidence, transparency and intelligent questioning will always deliver tangible returns, while expediency and ‘just do it’ inevitably deliver risk and reversal.
