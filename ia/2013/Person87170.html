Markus Baier's Summary

With my Selfmotivation, steady willingness for experimenting new ways of visual expressions as well as communication from analog to digital techniques in order to receive something unique, beautiful and unexpected I design special pieces of art in my very own ways.

All the time looking for new projects, challenges and collaborations on every creative field I want to push my limits over and over again.

Every single minute I love what I do and enjoy my life!
