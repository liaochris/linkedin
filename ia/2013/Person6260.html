Founder

Happy Curve

Public Company; Myself Only; Internet industry

June 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Oslo and Singapore

Concept Development, Design and Coding of online survey tool Happy Curve.

Happy Curve (http://happycurve.com) is a tool for continuously getting feedback from clients, customers or co-workers, so that you can improve your products/processes faster. By giving users a continuous feedback loop and using a set format for responding, both responders and people analyzing the feedback can compare days/weeks/months and continuously improve.

The tool was developed over a year, and was used and tested internally at Netlife Research in Oslo, Norway. It is now open to the public.
