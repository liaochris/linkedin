Helena V. de Vengoechea's Summary

Helena V. Studio specializes in still-life and portrait photography. If you have a product you would like photographed for your company or you would like memorable moments captured of your family, give us a call at 917-749-2499.

Additionally Helena V. is diligently working on a photo project titled The Museum of Messages. In brief, these photographs are images of messages that people have written or designed in landscapes, mostly urban, about the environment, love politics, humor, loyalty and freedom of self expression.

If you'd like to review the studio's work you may click on the website links below.
