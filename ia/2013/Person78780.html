Laura Gaines' Summary

Shalla Wista Studio is a company I founded in April 2006. I feel very blessed for the vast growth and support I have received. I currently operate out of a closed studio in Crestline Village located in Birmingham, Alabama. Everything on my lines are handmade here in the studio. My design assistants and I are constantly working on our whole sale line, which we target for placement in specialty boutiques and the individual line available in our shop on Etsy.

I have a fully operating staff in the studio everyday, where we all work hard and have learned to play hard too!
