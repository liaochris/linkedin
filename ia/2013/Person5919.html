Amy Coulson's Summary

Currently work part time as a freelance web developer, and full time as a student at Georgian College in the Web Animation and Design Co-op Program. I consider myself as a digital media strategist, where my main role is to produce user experience and accessible solutions for a different number of web-based tehnologies.

It was the faculty at Georgian College that taught me how to work with clients and deliver solid websites to meet their needs. It also educated me firsthand in the progression of trends and practices in web design over recent years.

It is with extreme joy that I get to work in a fun, creative environment where ideas are shared and new ones come alive on a constant basis. Please, don't hesitate to contact me for your next project, advice, or trade ideas over a cup of joe.

"A passionate artist with a penchant for all things digital."
