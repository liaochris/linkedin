Lynne Brodie's Summary

Lynne Brodie has developed, driven and closed multimillion dollar sales initiatives for Fortune 500 companies in the IT industry across a range of commercial industries and the Public Sector including: healthcare, banking, financial services, transportation, manufacturing, aerospace, homeland security, and housing.

An expert in Human Potential, she provides leadership empowerment workshops and coaching for organizational development and growth, cultural change and strategic planning.

She has a record of achieving trusted advisor status and delivering exponential growth, minimizing risk, and providing superior customer service. She is described as a focused leader; providing clarity and focus on demand to those around her.

She has experience in delivering best practices and enterprise solutions for organizational effectiveness. Operating in multinational, global environments and with entrepreneurs alike, she has maximized value through business, leadership, strategic partnerships, business governance and organizational transformation.

Lynne has many accomplishments in building and fostering client relationships, forecasting and sales of software and professional consulting services of ERP, cloud computing, in-memory computing, SAP and Oracle, Business Intelligence and Infrastructure. Finally, she has accomplishments in business development and is especially effective nurturing consultative sales processes to a successful conclusion.


She melds an exemplary corporate business, educational and entrepreneurial background with integrity, thought leadership and confidence to foster teamwork.
