Kelly Nash's Summary

I am fascinated by people.

I enjoy examining how environments influence behaviors and shape relationships, and conversely, how relationships and behaviors create environments. I see design and design thinking as ways to bridge differences and create new opportunities for growth and change.

With undergraduate and graduate degrees in Communication and Interaction Design and through my professional experience, I have formalized my skills in user experience, which include human-centered research and how to effectively facilitate and advocate.
