David Mason's Summary

Dr. David Mason is committed to bringing you better health and a better way of life by teaching and practicing the true principles of chiropractic wellness care. Located in the heart of Franklin, Tennessee, the leading health professionals at Mason Chiropractic Health & Wellness are dedicated to helping you achieve your wellness objectives -- combining skill and expertise that spans the entire chiropractic wellness spectrum.

Patients seeking treatment at Mason Chiropractic Health & Wellness with Dr. Mason are assured of receiving only the finest quality care through the use of modern chiropractic equipment and technology. Dr. Mason and the staff have a genuine concern for your well-being!

Specialties

At Mason Chiropractic Health & Wellness, we take great pride in providing the finest chiropractic care to our patients. A sampling of the different chiropractic services we offer in our Franklin office include:

Chiropractic Care,
Massage Therapy,
DTS Spinal Decompression,
Nutritional Counseling,
Corrective Exercises,
Spinal & Postural Screenings,
Lifestyle Advice,
Physiotherapy,
Custom Orthotics,
KinesioTaping
