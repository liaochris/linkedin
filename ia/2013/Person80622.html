Lawrence Lewis' Experience

Owner / Operator

Indian Elections BC

2002 – Present (11 years) British Columbia, Canada

We know that your election cycle process is important and critical to the future of your Nation and the well being of your community. We have conducted dozens of elections and referendums – and not a single one has been appealed successfully – in fact 98% have never been appealed at all. We are expert at implementing and executing elections, interpreting custom codes and treaty election acts, and providing a modern enhanced election that reaches more members and engage your community more effectively.

SEE PROJECTS SECTION ABOVE for most recent elections projects ranging from Treaty Code to Custom Code to Indian Act Election Regulations.

Reach more Members during your next election or referendum event using the full power and utility of the world-wide-web and modern social media. Most of your Members, especially those ages 16-55 are already connected through FaceBook, mobile phones and email – we can harness those modern methods of communication to reach more of your Members during your next election or referendum.

Here’s the best part – in most cases this service is “INCLUDED at NO EXTRA CHARGE” in our estimate/proposal. During your next election event you can rely on us to reach more members through our modern enhanced election services and save some serious $$$ too!
