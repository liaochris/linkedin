Alexander Snelling's Summary

Alex has been at the forefront of Apple's desktop editing revolution with Final Cut Pro and regularly trains, presents and consults directly for Apple, bringing a unique understanding of the symbiosis between technology and creativity to the ever-growing marketplace.

In 2010 Alex supported the entire Final Cut Pro post-production workflow at the FIFA World Cup.
Recent clients have included Apple, Burberry, Red Bull, SKY TV, BBC, Colour & Paint, Nikon, HD SLR Movie Maker, Red Gallery London (#R3D), Kelli Ali, Tasha Pyne.

Alex has also been writing, directing & producing for twelve years through his company Slack Alice Films. Feature Documentary, Tantric Tourists won London's East End Film Festival in 200 and was released in early 2011.

Before Slack Alice, Alex cut his teeth as an editor, specialising in music and has worked twice with the legendary director Julien Temple with the Sex Pistols and the Scissor Sisters.
