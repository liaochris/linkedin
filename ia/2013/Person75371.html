Kimberly Graesser's Summary

I create brands, websites and marketing material for a variety of clients, specializing in churches and other non-profit organizations. I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science in Multimedia Authoring and Communication from Sam Houston State University and have over a decade of experience in designing websites and graphics, giving me the skills required to design and develop your web presence.

My goal is to develop a personal relationship with my clients in order to deliver the best possible services at a competitive rate. You can always count on me as a friendly and expert resource for your design and technology concerns. If you would like to talk to me about what design and development I can offer you please email me at kgraesser@flying-dreams.com

Specialties

My strengths include creating clean, contemporary designs, focusing on usability, navigation and projecting your message clearly.

I specialize in websites, editorial design, logo design, brochures, flyer design, stationery & much more.
