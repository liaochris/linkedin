Karen Faulkner's Honors and Awards

  • Design/Build Installation Grand Award

    MALTA (Metro Atlanta Landscape and Turf Association)
    • November 2009

    For projects judged as greatly exceeding all criteria for the category.

  • Distinction Award-Landscaping project under $50,000

    Urban Agriculture Council (UAC)
    • December 2012

    A gorgeous transformation of a small sloped yard earned The Inspired Garden a coveted Distinction award from UAC. This space was evaluated on quality workmanship and creativity that exceeded the judges expectations for the category. The space contained a curving retaining wall, stack stone firepit and seat wall on a flagstone patio, lighting, irrigation, and lots of beautiful plantings that had interest all year long. It was a challenging space that now affords the homeowners with lots of entertaining and a cozy space for themselves to enjoy a glass of wine by the fire!

  • Southeastern Flower Show 2013 Small Space Award

    Southeastern Flower Show
    • March 2013

    This award recognizes outstanding design and innovation in a small space garden at the Southeastern Flower Show. Participation in the SEFS is by invitation only and those that are recommended as exceptional design/build professionals are accepted as participants.

    The theme of this year's garden was An Old-fashioned Tea Party featuring a raised planter bed full of English-inspired plantings, natural boulders, a bubbling urn and an 1800's cast iron bistro table and chairs from England acquired from Trish Land's furniture and gift store in Duluth GA. It was a favorite of many of the attendee's and we had a great time taking pictures of our 'guests' dressed up in hats, pearls and fascinators posing with their tea cups!
