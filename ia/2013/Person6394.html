Andrea King's Summary

My bachelors is in Business Marketing with several years experience in different marketing positions for small businesses. More recently, fitness and nutrition has become my main focus and passion. What sets me apart from many others is my passion and enthusiasm for betterment of mind, body, and soul through education, healthy practices, and positivity. I believe in a holistic approach to nutrition, and fitness by getting to the source of my client's pain, frustrations, and imbalances.

I make people's lives better. Through offering effective personal training and nutrition services I help my client's make their fitness dreams a reality. I strip away all the clutter and nonsense and only use what works. I guide my clients to a healthy lifestyle through holistic practices and expertise.

If you have always wanted to change the way you look and feel but haven't been able to, contact me. I can make it happen for you!

Specialties

Creative and dynamic fitness class instruction, corrective and functional training, holistic approach to personal training and nutrition, grass roots marketing, writing, online marketing.
