Marni Derr's Summary

I specialize in Joomla! web site development for individuals and small business, with an emphasis on consulting, training, and social media integration.

Senior level technical writer with over 17 years experience in software and hardware. Right now I am focusing on creating tutorials for all things web driven. New technologies, Joomla and Joomla extensions, WordPress, HTML 5 and CSS3. I am dedicated to helping others learn what they need to learn to be successful on the web.

If you are an individual or small business that needs a web presence and would like training and/or documentation on Joomla, or custom solutions training, feel free to contact me.

If you are a developer of Joomla extensions or templates and would like to hire a technical writer to produce documentation for you, please feel free to contact me.
