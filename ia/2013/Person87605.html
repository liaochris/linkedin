Shelli Martineau's Summary

I'm a passionate, experienced, & creative Social Media Community Manager looking for full time or freelance work.

I've been described as "the digital equivalent of someone who knows how to work a room." The reason I'm a successful community manager and strategist is because I love technology, I love figuring things out, I love talking to people, and I invest personally in the work that I do.

What I'm good at:

Strategy. I understand the difference between strategy and tactics. I'll ask about target audience, objectives, measurement and understand that "having Facebook" is not strategy.

Community Management. I write extraordinarily creative posts, questions, etc. that get people talking, but still fall within brand voice, objectives and strategy.

I am a strong contributor to brand, messaging and overall social media strategy. Consider including me in brainstorms.

I am an excellent teacher and therefore a wonderful trainer. I developed most of the training materials and handled most of the client training for Conversify, a Global Digital Agency.

I'm fun and personable and always a good pick me up at that Monday morning meeting when no one feels like being at work.

Plus, I have real social media agency experience and I also come with great references.
