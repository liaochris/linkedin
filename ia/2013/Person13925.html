Brandie Feuer's Summary

Brandie Feuer is an interactive marketing professional with more than sixteen years of experience in advertising, interactive marketing and emerging media. Currently, Ms. Feuer is Director of Marketing, Social and Mobile for Bath and Body Works, America's #1 Specialty Beauty Retailer with over 1,600 stores nationwide.

Throughout her career, Brandie has worked with top tier brands including Planet Hollywood, Luxor Hotel & Casino, MGM Resorts International, Starwood Hotels & Resorts, The Las Vegas Sands, Cirque du Soleil, Tropicana Las Vegas, the UFC and The Miami Herald.

While in Las Vegas, Brandie launched several innovative programs, including Vegas’ first viral game and a mobile marketing program that integrated with a hotel’s property management system. In addition, Brandie has garnered top awards, like USA Today's and Mashable’s Top 40 Brands that Twitter and Las Vegas’ Best Twitter Persona for her efforts in social media. More over, she co-authored the book, Connect! Marketing in the Social Media Era and is an Internationally recognized speaker, having given numerous talks in four countries on emerging media.
