Drew Neisser's Summary

Drew Neisser is CEO & Founder of Renegade, the NYC-based social media and marketing agency that helps inspired clients cut through the nonsense to deliver genuine business growth. A frequent speaker at industry events, Drew’s been a featured expert on ABC’s Nightline and CNBC. You can find Drew’s articles on FastCompany.com, MediaPost and TheDrewBlog.

In the last few years, Drew helped Renegade charge up the prepaid card market launching Magic by Magic Johnson via events & social, elevate the Empire State Building's social presence, unleash the AXA Gorilla on Twitter with audio tweets and a virtual retirement party, create the Optimist Network for Optimum Business, guide Toasted Head to become one of the most popular wines on Facebook, introduce young adults to Harlem Liqueur and make a splash for Davidoff Cool Water on Facebook.

At Renegade, Drew hatched numerous award-winning campaigns for a long-list of blue chip clients. His ideas for HSBC, Panasonic and IBM were all recognized by BRANDWEEK as Guerrilla Marketers of the Year. Among these is the legendary HSBC BankCab program, a restored Checker, that has been delighting HSBC customers since 2003 with free rides (and now informative tweets).

Drew’s creative accomplishments include naming and launching the Toughbook for Panasonic and penning numerous taglines. These include “Like money. Only Magic.” for Magic by Magic Johnson prepaid MasterCard, “Where Family Comes First.” for Family Circle Magazine, “Fire things up,” for Toasted Head wine and “Great tech support. Good karma.” for iYogi.

Drew lives in Manhattan with his wife and the agency’s mascot, a French bulldog named Pinky. A native Californian, Drew dreams of becoming a surfer but is a long way from hanging ten.
