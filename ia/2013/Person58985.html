Jason A. Howie's Summary

I am an unabashed marketing geek, with both a love for both the analytic trends and the creative possibilities in social media marketing.

As a personal interest I have regularly built and maintained websites since I was 15. Through this I have always had an interest in developing my self-taught skill set and exposing myself to new creative ideas. Eventually I turned my hobbies into professional career in Web and Social Media Marketing. My knowledge of social media marketing, SEO/SEM, and digital marketing strategy has led to proven results. Clients have enjoyed, higher SERPs, larger and more active social media communities, and website redesigns that emphasize SEO and mobile web best practices.

A capstone to combining my personal interests with my professional was the completion of my MBA in Marketing and currently I work as the Manager, Marketing and Sales Support for Gold Mobile. Outside of work I can regularly be found blogging on topics such as mobile and social media marketing and amusing my other creative interests by photographing areas I visit.
