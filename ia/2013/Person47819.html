Geoff Welch's Summary

I co-own Date-Line Digital Printing in Fairbanks, Alaska where I live, mostly indoors, with my wife and daughter. In 2012 I built Date-Line’s Thanks Fairbanks project which distributes thousands of free thank you cards to area residents each year. It’s the kind of crazy idea that just might make a difference.

I am a drummer, a terrible guitar player, and a huge music lover. I’ve written and recorded a few dozen songs in my basement studio and music, like writing this site, is basically therapy for me.

I love spending time with my girls, watching Yankee games, nerding out with tech, and recording a biweekly podcast called It Gets Loud In Here with my BFF Lauren Hatty. I also contribute a monthly article to FairbanksAlaska.com called the Small Business Insider, serve as the leadership team president at my church, and am a member of the Board of Directors for the Greater Fairbanks Chamber of Commerce.
