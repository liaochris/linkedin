Janet Galore's Summary

Veteran producer, director and program manager working in emerging technologies and applications. Likes to push both creative and technical boundaries, working end-to-end to create engaging user experiences. Experienced with managing teams, budgets, specifications, schedules, clients, and creative people. Award-winning Flash animator and director; author and contributor to several technology books on Flash and streaming media.

Specialties: experience and UI design, creative direction, information architecture, program management, 2D animation, presentation design, illustration, technology strategy, cross-team relations
