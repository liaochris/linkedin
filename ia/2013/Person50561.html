Jason Grigsby's Experience

Co-Founder

Mobile Portland

March 2008 – Present (5 years 10 months)

In late 2007, my co-founders at Cloud Four and I realized that there wasn't a group in Portland dedicated to promoting and educating people about mobile technology. We started Mobile Portland to fill that void.

Modeled after Mobile Mondays, Mobile Portland has grown from it's modest roots into a vibrant community of over 1,000 people. Our monthly events average nearly 100 people.

Current efforts include converting the organization into a non-profit and establishing a device testing facility.

Co-Founder

Cloud Four

October 2007 – Present (6 years 3 months)

Cloud Four was founded by four former Kavi employees to focus on mobile web development. Cloud Four has been recognized as one of the leading agencies working on mobile web development.

In addition to my role as an owner and founder, I assist clients with their mobile strategy. I help them determine what platforms to target, what technology to use and how to build a product that is a joy to use.
