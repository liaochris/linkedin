Jared Comis' Summary

I am a designer who is passionate about creating great experiences and interactions. I design mobile apps, responsive websites, and applications.

As a designer I make it my goal to advocate for the user through user centered design principles. Check out my projects in my portfolio (jaredcomis.com) to see some examples of my work.

I currently work as UX designer at the University of Washington. My projects include designing MyUW mobile, a student portal, and SpaceScout (a webapp, iOS app, and soon an Android app).

I also recently received a MS in Human Centered Design and Engineering from UW where I focused my studies on interaction and user experience design. I also performed research in social media, interaction design, and crisis management with several groups at UW.

I am always looking to connect with UX advocates, so please feel free to contact me here or on twitter/app.net: @jaredcomis

Specialties: Prototyping, UX Design, Usability Analysis, User Testing, User Centered Design, User Research, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Responsive Web Design, Content Strategy, Mobile UX.
