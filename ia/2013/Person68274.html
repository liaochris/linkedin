Jordan Nugent's Summary

Jordan Nugent is a Marketing and Social Media specialist dedicated to innovation in online media. She began her career at an online magazine featuring lifestyle, fashion, and technology content aimed at career driven, online savvy mothers founded by Seattle’s social media queen, Jenni Hogan. In 2012, Jordan launched JMN Creative, an editorial and social media agency. She’s been involved with companies like Lululemon Athletica, Target, The Center for Pediatric Dentistry, Jenni Hogan Media, and Microsoft on various marketing campaigns.

Jordan graduated from the University of Washington with a bachelor’s degree in Communications. She’s passionate about professional development and volunteers with Springboard Academy, a Seattle-based professional development organization.
