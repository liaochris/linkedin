Corine Aartman's Summary

Specialties

I am an all-rounder in fundraising and communications, with a talent to inspire people. Engaged and goal-driven. Flexible, multi-lingual and with an eye for human relations. I can operate in small and large organisations, with boards, multi-disciplinary teams and across cultures.
