Mickey Lasky, CEH GCFA GCFE GWAPT GREM RHCE's Summary

With more than 12 years of data and Internet security experience, Mickey Lasky can bring a unique perspective to any organization. With experiences ranging from large and mid-size corporate environments, Federal government, and higher education Mickey Lasky focuses on providing results in security services such as incident response, computer forensics, firewall and IDS/IPS design and deployment, security audits, and security awareness education. Industry recognized certifications including SANS GIAC Certified Forensic Analyst, EC Council Certified Ethical Hacker, Check Point Security Engineer, and Red Hat Certified Engineer combined with professional journalism experience allows Mickey to produce clear, concise deliverables for even the most complex of security concepts.

Specialties: Firewall design, implementation, and operations. Incident response. Digital forensics investigations. Information security evangelism. Security operations.
