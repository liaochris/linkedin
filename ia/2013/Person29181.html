Dan Livingston's Summary

30 years of project accomplishment through small team leadership (in business development, Medicare, Medicaid, insurance, aircraft, satellite, and general business environments).

24 years of managing large projects with emphasis on establishing the vision, designing the plan, counting the cost, and bringing the work to closure on schedule.

Skillful negotiator, superior interpersonal skills, able to nurture teamwork from the most ardent resistors of change. Gifted writer and communicator, comfortable in front of large groups of people, willing to adapt to changing objectives.

Solid ability to accomplish project goals in highly stressful and ambiguous work environment incorporating changing priorities, inconsistent resource access, unstable workloads, and constant requirement adjustments.

Proven history of technical project management skills addressing design of hardware components, design of software components, and systems projects.

Specialties: Proposal Management, Capture Management, Project Management, Portfolio Management, Independent Verification and Validation, and Process Development
