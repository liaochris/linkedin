Joe Henning's Summary

Joe Henning is an experienced IT professional with more than 15 years experience. With a focus on developing IT solutions through process improvement, business integration and enterprise architecture to meet current and future business requirements. Experience in building and implementing public, private & hybrid cloud based computing solutions (IaaS, SaaS, PaaS). Excellent communicator at all levels of the organization, builds lasting relationships with employees, co-workers and vendors. Strong ability to lead, provide direction and motivate teams to produce results. Experience as a member of a number of international teams with multi-national companies implementing global IT solutions.

Joe has also implemented a number of global ERP, HCM, ecommerce and warehouse management systems.

Joe has been in a number of technical leadership roles at a number of organizations. He is an excellent communicator, he focuses on building relationships and forming partnerships across the organization. Joe has also been a member on a number of multi-national cross functional teams at a number of international companies.

Joe has an Executive MBA from the Wisconsin School of Business at the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a BA with an International Business focus from Cardinal Stritch University.

Specialties
