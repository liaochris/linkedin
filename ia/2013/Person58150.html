Jane Akshar's Summary

Imagine living the dream ... I did. After 23 years working in the banking and insurance industry, providing consultancy services to the financial sector, due to a change in my personal circumstances I left the UK and emigrated to to Egypt.

I now have a property portfolio of over 20 apartments/villas and a holiday rental business .

Do YOU want to live the same dream, sunshine, local life, ancient history and out of the rat race. Either buy from us or have a holiday with us, a wonderful holiday, a romantic cruise or living here for ever we offer it all.

Email me jane.akshar@flatsinluxor.co.uk and let me transform your life

Specialties: Egyptian Holidays, Egypt Property, Ancient Egypt, Family Holidays, Holidays to Egypt, Egypt Tours, Egypt Travel, Egypt for Kids, Property, Retirement Property, Overseas Investment, Holiday Rentals

Jane Akshar's Publications

  • Hidden Luxor

    • E-book, Lulu, Amazon
    • May 2011
    Authors: Jane Akshar

    This unique book is a distillation of everything I love about Luxor, why I had to move here and why I have never got tired of visiting the sites. So much more than a guide book, every site in Luxor described, suggest itineraries, advice and lots more. How to enjoy the sites, avoid the crowds and get off the beaten track.

    Hidden Luxor is primarily aimed at people who are visiting Luxor for the second time. They will have done the normal tourist trail: Valley of the Kings, Queens, Hatshepsut, Karnak and Luxor temple, and now want to know what else there is to see.

  • Jane's Jaunt

    • E-book, Lulu, Amazon
    • January 28, 2013
    Authors: Jane Akshar

    10 years ago Jane Akshar embarked on a great adventure, emigrating to Luxor, Egypt. For the first year she kept a diary online to let reassure friends and family and let them know how she was getting on. Read how she copes with the very different life in rural Egypt with its unique culture and traditions, starting a new business and career, adapting to the life and marriage to a Muslim Egyptian and being a co-wife.

    • E-book, Lulu and Amazon
    • July 1, 2012

    Breasts : Sexy, Sacred or Secular is Jane's award winning dissertation after completing the 4 year Manchester Certificate online course.
    The topic was a result of living in Egypt, seeing attitudes, hearing about village beliefs it made her curious about the Ancient Egyptian attitudes towards the breast. The dissertation covers all aspects of this study.

  • Jane Goes to Egypt 1979

    • E-book, Amazon
    • November 18, 2012
    Authors: Jane Akshar

    Back in 1979, when I was 24, my Dad’s boss said to him. “Silly question Alec, you don’t know anyone who would be interested in going to Egypt do you. My sister is organising a trip and someone has let her down." Of course he did, me! I had loved Egypt since I was 9. This was my very first trip to Egypt, I kept a diary and I still have the photos. I have put it all together, now I have been living in Egypt for 10 years and have some new information and laugh at my old self.

  • Counting the Cost

    • E-book, Lulu and Amazon
    • February 23, 2013
    Authors: Jane Akshar, Valerie Warner

    In 1993 Jane and Ayman Akshar were interviewed by Michael O’Donnell for the radio program Relative Values, they told about their meeting with the Church of Christ, Ayman’s conversion from Islam, their subsequent life within that cult and their marriage and birth of their daughter. This book covers that period and what happened next. Ayman’s work as an exit counsellor, Jane’s IT career and chairmanship of PCG, the dynamics of their marriage and the subsequent illness and death of Ayman. Jane then remarried, moved to Egypt and became a Muslim. This fascinating story of two religious journeys in opposite directions will enthral you. It is a story of deceit and lies on a group and individual level.
