Ken Pirok's Summary

I work in the Risk and Compliance practice at Protiviti, and I specialize in the assessment of credit risk. Recently, I have worked on loan review and lender due diligence projects for financial institutions.

I am also the author of Commercial Loan Analysis, Managing Credit Department Functions, and The Lender's Toolkit. Check out my blogs, which are listed as "Websites" on this profile page.
