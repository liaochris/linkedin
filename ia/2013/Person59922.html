Jason Ng's Projects

  • Halbjorn's Wrath

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jason Ng, Brandon Alexander, Nigel Loster, Brant Stutheit, Marcelo Blanes, Luis Rodrigues, Jaime Sainz

    Halbjorn’s Wrath is a 3rd person shooter for P.C and Xbox 360 where players take the role of Halbjorn, an angry bear shaman, and defend their forest from the slimy invasion of the Drinarr; an intelligent aquatic race that is sinking the forest into the ocean. Fight your way through the Drinarr with Halbjorn’s powerful spiritual attacks and teleport. When Halbjorn really gets mad show the Drinarr who’s boss with his rage mode and drive them from the forest. Halbjorn’s Wrath has a unique art style, rarely seen in this genre.

  • Bullet Ride

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jason Ng, Donald Mensah, Hampus Gross, Pedro Arimany, David Hinds, Byron Munsie, Alessandro Ciavola Pennelli, Andrew McDermid, Michael Korotyaev

    Bullet Ride is our final project for our year of the Game Design program at Vancouver Film School.

    The game is about playing as a man riding a bullet, making sure the bullet reaches its destination. The path is filled with obstacles such as: people moving in slowed down motion, the interior of an apartment and birds. High speed, adrenaline and lots of OH SHIT moments.

    Keep a look out for Bullet Ride, developed in the Unity 3D Engine.

    Project Manager: Pedro Arimany
    Art: David Hinds
    Programmers: Byron Munsie and Alessandro Ciavola Pennelli
    Level Design: Hampus Gross

  • The Human Project

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jason Ng, Jorelle Miranda, Manuel J. Ibanez, Jaime Sainz, Greg Polak, Luis Rodrigues

  • Deja Vu - Wwise Implementation Project

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jason Ng, Luis Rodrigues, Beso Kacharava, Jaime Sainz

    DejaVu is an action-survival puzzle game created by me in a team of 4 other great sound designers. The concept behind our game was to create a sound driven experience where the story can be told by audio. It was our goal to create a story reminiscent of games like Amnesia, Silent Hill and Limbo.We created all the sounds from the game, and this is a walkthrough of the in game implementation of the audio using the Wwise middleware tool.
