Joshua Heyer's Summary

It starts with a need. A fundamental problem that, at its core, has a very simple solution. That’s where the fun begins for me. To break it down to the minimal components where design can really begin. Marrying creativity with logical thinking to develop a solution unique to the need that creates an impactful—and hopefully lasting—impression. The medium is irrelevant. The outcome will always be the same with me – the right one.

Whether it’s a logo or marketing collateral; video production or motion graphics; user interface design or web development; live event production or environmental graphics—I create experiences that are borne out of the perfect solution for the problem presented.

I love to solve problems. I love to create experiences to remember. I guess it makes sense that I do what I do.
