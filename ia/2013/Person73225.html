Kat Wildish
Ballet
high-caliber dance instructor, currently teaching multi-level ballet and pointe technique for children and adults. Her performing credits include New York City Ballet, American Ballet Theatre and numerous international companies. Kat is a recipient of the Ford Foundation Scholarship under which she attended the School of American Ballet. Her classes are very danceable, with a classic sense of line, musicality, placement, and style. Not just a warm-up , but are anatomically sound and designed to improve the body. All parts of the dancer are exercised to promote harmony of movement according to each individual's level of training and ability in a non-competitive atmosphere. She teaches an integrative, fundamental maintenance syllabus for all types of dancers. Kat is also an ABT® Certified Teacher, who has successfully completed the ABT® Teacher Training Intensive in Primary through Level 7 of the ABT® National Training Curriculum.
