Jillian Pedersen's Summary

I am currently a Social Media Manager for Leverage PR. My position there requires me to respond to the various factors needed to provide a business with the tools it needs to be prominent in the social media community. I also design and manage project aesthetics. Having extensive experience in art and design including branding, print, web and film compositions, I enjoy creating clean, modern and professional work. Using attentive client consultations and marketing techniques, my goal revolves around transforming a vision into tangible completion.

My past accomplishments include branding and designing for several local companies in Central Texas. Spending time in the print sector creating and enhancing for businesses, as well as, bringing those projects from design to full circle completion on printed ensembles. Building functional web pages and blogs according to specific guidelines and operations to be performed. Creating a company brand from the ground up including images, fonts, and color schemes associated. Working at various news stations, editing film for rolling video and reporter packages, creating a wide range of graphics for news casts including over the shoulder shots and full screen images, updating breaking news stories on the website, and managing social media accounts keeping news current.

In addition to my role at Leverage PR, I am currently active in several design communities in the area to constantly grow in the knowledge of the industry. I teach various small business owners how to utilize design programs to complete tasks such as creating newsletters and update websites.

Specialties: Print Design, Business Cards, Brochures, Postcards, Flyers, Invitations, Posters, Ad Slicks, Web Design, HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, Flash, Actionscript, Illustration, Video Editing, Adobe Premiere and After Effects, Social Media Management and Marketing
