Dave Biskner's Experience

Principal

Franklin Hamilton Consulting

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

March 2002 – Present (11 years 8 months)

Franklin Hamilton is a management consulting firm. We help businesspeople get their time back.

Through an innovative approach to program management, Franklin Hamilton assists those professionals who struggle with personal bandwidth challenges. Our typical client is a business owner who spends 12 hours a week selling their product, and would rather use that time to run the company. With Franklin Hamilton, the client can shift some or all of this sales burden to our capable field consultants. Doing so allows the business owner to use the time to work on their company and not in their company.
