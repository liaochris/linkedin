Stephanie Heinze's Summary

Creativeshark Marketing & Design is a small design studio in San Diego. We service mostly small business clientele, from California to across the country. We've helped many small businesses launch their brand, define their identity, and realize their dreams. Let us deliver the same success strategy to you!

The Creativeshark behind the studio is Stephanie Heinze, a hybrid Creative Director and Graphic Designer. She is self-taught and has been working and freelancing in the industry for over 20 years, in all areas of Graphic Design, Web Design, Marketing, Creative Writing, Technical Writing, and Photography. Stephanie's creative mantra is "Learning and maintaining a creative craft is truly a lifetime experience and the evolution and growth it affords is priceless!" Having long been a career student of life and experience, she is also currently taking online Web Development classes to seam together her specialities and areas of expertise.
