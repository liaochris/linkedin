Chris Bazett's Projects

  • VentureMap

    • January 2011 to January 2012
    Team Members: Chris Bazett, Patrick Wedgeworth, Divya Iyer, Hima Parvataneni

  • ClearLab Project

    • May 2011 to September 2011
    Team Members: Chris Bazett

In partnership with the Federation of American Scientists, curriculum developers from K12 Inc., and science teachers around the country, I produced a 90-page design document describing the systems, content, and art requirements for a browser-deployed simulation game that uses 3D virtual slot-car racing to help high-school students learn the principles of physics. The product is in full production, and a successful launch will critically shape future product lines at Muzzy Lane Software.

  • BC Place Construction Tower

My recommendations led to cost savings of several hundred thousand dollars.
