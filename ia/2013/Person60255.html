John Christly, CISO's Summary

John is an experienced technology leader and leading IT security professional who works with organizations to implement innovative and secure solutions.

He is a true "out of the box" thinker who finds unique ways to solve complex IT and business problems.

He has a proven history of building teams, creating relationships, increasing revenue, and being a trusted resource that can be relied upon.

John is widely recognized as an expert in the field of Mobile and Computer Forensics, Cloud Computing, Big Data, Healthcare Information Systems, Fraud Investigations, and Vendor/Employee Vetting.

He has previously held leadership positions for such organizations as eTERA Consulting, AvMed Health Plans, and Memorial Healthcare Systems (which is the 5th largest healthcare system in the U.S.), as well as IDS Telcom and The Golf Network. He has also owned his own IT Consulting firm (OMC Systems) since 2008.

His experience includes several industry regulations including HIPAA, HITECH, FERPA, FCPA, and PCI.

As a highly accomplished author, Mr. Christly’s articles have appeared in publications such as Security Magazine, Executive Insight Magazine, 24/7 Magazine, and the ISSA Journal.
