Emily Brown's Summary

Emily writes for Education Daily. She covers K-12 issues involving federal and state policy; funding; career-technical education; and science, technology, engineering and math education, to name a few topics.

As the founder and key contributor to The Career Pioneer, Emily writes posts and articles on the topic of and related to career exploration, as well as personal and business development. She infuses humor, inspiration and practical advice to guide a growing readership into discovering their passion, along with their true career path. She also features material from guest bloggers from around the globe. http://www.thecareerpioneer.com

As the owner of Write House LLC in Washington, DC. As a freelance copywriter and brand strategist Emily has written hundreds of articles, blogs, print marketing collateral and website pages for small businesses and solo entrepreneurs, ensuring messaging consistency, brand integrity, proper organization, grammar, sentence structure and punctuation. She effectively identifies specific audiences, and through research from both online and print resources tailors copy accordingly. Her attention-to-detail in proofing copy for consistency and accuracy is unmatched. Clients have included an award-winning web design firm, solo entrepreneurs and small business owners, several professional service companies and government contractors.

At Bloomberg News in Washington, DC, Emily wrote articles and news-content synopses as an intern and later as a full-time employee. She covered politics, along with health and science with a focus on the U.S. Food and Drug Administration, medical device companies, and pharmacy benefit managers. She authored stories based on market trends, and covered company acquisitions, mergers, management changes, shares, SEC filings, business profiles and forecast trends.

Connect with Emily by email at emily@writehousellc.com or by phone: (202) 294-6357.
