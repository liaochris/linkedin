Susan Pedrick's Summary

I have more than sixteen years of experience as the development associate at a women-oriented community center in San Francisco called The Women's Building. The focus of my position was raising money from individuals. I am looking for an opportunity to contribute to a fund raising team that operates in a supportive, collaborative atmosphere. My passion is to raise funds for organizations providing direct services to at risk populations. In addition to issues affecting women, and community building, my interests include the environment, seniors, domestic violence, mental health, and all forms of fighting isolation. In addition, I have had excellent experiences applying my skills to arts organizations.

Specialties: I have extensive experience:
--Hiring, training and supervising groups to meet a common goal--raising money from individuals.
--Managing information, from paper capture to administering complex data.
--Authentic communication with groups and individuals, in writing, on the phone, in person and in front of audiences.
--Annual drives, special events, publications and special asks, including two capital campaigns.
--Cat herding: Let me at those unruly committees!

Hired as the data coordinator, five months later the Executive Director spontaneously promoted me to the fund raising staff. I used a list of approximately 3,500 names to bring in approximately 500 gifts twice a year for total of about $60,000. I did this using direct mail and following up with telephone calls. I hired, trained and supervised phone banks. I administered all the donor and prospect data using a complex fund raising software package called DonorWorks.

Whole Life Expo

January 1987 – May 1992 (5 years 5 months) San Francisco

Hired as the receptionist, two weeks later the producer asked me to become her assistant. I was the liaison with approximately 200 speakers per show. I kept track of all comings and goings in a database, giving me the data experience that would get me my initial position at The Women's Building. I designed forms, contracts and information packets. I wrote biographies and presentation descriptions. On site, I continued my role as liaison with presenters and managed teams of volunteers.
