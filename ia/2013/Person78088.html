Ed Lallo's Summary

Newsroom Ink: Putting Your Content in Context to Connect

In today’s digital age every size organization has taken on the role of publisher. To tell your story well, you need good content.

Content that’s aligned with your business to reach the right people.

It doesn't matter what business you're in: big, small or nonprofit. Or, what media you use – social or mainstream. Things simply got to line up to work.

So, why not use good business sense to create, curate and communicate your organization’s story.

• Line it up with your strategic agenda.
• Map it to how your operations run.
• Produce it in ways to maximize your value.

The idea is simple: Invent and invest once. Use and profit often.

That's what we do at Newsroom Ink. We put your content – be it paid, owned or earned media - in the right context. We create a clear line of sight so people see the value in what you do.

Quite simply, we make the links to get you the ink - no matter the media.

www.NewsroomInk.com

Specialties: Using the power of storytelling, Newsroom Ink's award winning brand journalists create, curate and communicate influential news to a wide variety of audiences. Compelling videos, photos and stories enhance the reputation of the organization and its brand, while connecting the organizations PR, communications and marketing functions and creating a continuous conversation with an ever-increasing audience.

Publisher/Executive Editor - Gulf Seafood News

Gulf Seafood Institute

September 2013 – Present (1 month) Austin, TX

Gulf Seafood News

Uniting seafood communities of five Gulf States, the Gulf Seafood Institute’s mission is to protect the Gulf’s unique culture while elevating the Gulf Seafood brand with consumers, customers and policy leaders through advocacy, education and science.

As the official online newsroom for the Gulf Seafood Institute, it is designed to give insight into the stories of the Gulf's seafood family. Target audiences include: media, fishing communities, political leaders on local, state and national level, the worldwide seafood industry, culinary experts and chefs and general public.

Responsibilities include: newsroom budget, editing, story selection, writing, photography and video.
