Mercedes Ulaszewski's Summary

I am a graphic designer at heart with the eye of a photographer and the intuition of an illustrator with a flair for typography.

I’ve been told that I never stop smiling and that I brighten peoples days. I’ve been called a quick learner and a hard worker.

I’m a perfectionist who never misses deadlines. I’m not afraid to ask questions and never fear criticism or feedback.

I graduated from R.I.T in May of 2011 with a BFA in Visual Media. Visual Media focuses on aspects of photography, graphic design, print media, and management .As well I have minors in print media (mainly focusing on process and the business aspects) and art history, and a focus in graphic design and certification in management development

I was hired by CL!X Portrait Studios in their corporate office doing graphic design and SEO work. In addition I have now been appointed to take care of the franchise facebook and google plus pages. I am also their official blogger for the franchises bring helpful tips, DIY crafts, and just general fun information to readers. I am also still doing freelance and volunteer work on the side.

I am currently in the process of applying for my master degree in Design Management.

Typography, Page Layout, Graphic Design, Studio Lighting , Photography (Digital & Analog), Photo Editing, Matting, Framing & Hanging, Event Promotions, Organization, Social Media, Lightroom (3 & higher), Adobe CS (3, 4 & 5), Photoshop, Bridge, Illustrator, InDesign
