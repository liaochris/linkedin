Mark Klinkenberg's Summary

I am experienced in real estate development, economic development, municipal finance and business taxation issues. I am also experienced in counseling real estate developers with regard to economic development, including the use of incentives such as tax increment financing, community improvement districts, transportation development districts and other special taxing districts. I have extensive experience in general business and contracts law and have worked with several small businesses and tax-exempt organizations on business, non-profit and tax matters. I have experience representing debtors in disputes with creditors. I have a Master of Laws (LLM) in taxation.

Specialties

Real estate law, leases, contracts and contract disputes, general business, business formation and business succession, charitable organizations, state and local tax, corporate and partnership tax, debtor representation and assistance, minor domestic issues, traffic.
