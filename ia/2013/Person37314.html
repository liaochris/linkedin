Doug Mitchell's Summary

I spent the early part of my career selling and creating value while simplifying complex technologies for business owners.

In the late 90's I jumped into small/stuck stage companies transitioning into leading marketing and business development efforts into companies large and small.

For the last 7 years, I've been in leadership positions creating and executing marketing strategies, building partnerships, and delivering results. I'm equally at home in the C-suite or conducting trench warfare mano a mano.

I'm a seeker of knowledge and I strive to create value for others from an abundance mindset. I know what it takes to get things done and that no one gets there alone.

  • Grow Your Own! - Explode the Labor Shortage MYTH & Cultivate an Endless Supply of Talent

    • Douglas Mitchell
    • July 19, 2012
    Authors: Doug Mitchell

    (This is an ongoing LeanPub project so signing up now will ensure you get all the future content as the book evolves. The book will never be "done.")

    This book takes on the assumption that we have a skilled labor shortage in the United States. We can barely get through a week long news cycle without hearing that we have a need to redistribute tax payer dollars to encourage STEM education paths, how jobs are sitting unfilled in manufacturing, and how we must increase the amount of H1B foreign worker visas to compensate. I call bull. I contend that companies have outdated recruiting methods, suffer from generational biases, and are playing the victim. There are plenty of workers available when companies take full control of their labor supply.

    This book will present the arguments on both sides, examine the data, and deliver interviews and case studies from economists and companies who are growing their own labor force.
