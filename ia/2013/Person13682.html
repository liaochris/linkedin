Beth Calhoun-Leipold's Summary

I'm excited to announce that I've started a new business bringing event management solutions to corporate, professional and non-profit event planners. If you are using numerous spreadsheets to track information and payments but would like an easier way to oversee the details, evoplanning is the solution for you.

If you don't want to learn how to create an event and navigate the internet to put a registration link on your website, let evoplanning do that for you. We offer a turn key solution to set up your registration form in an online format. Log into your dashboard to view all the event statistics from number of registrants to dollars outstanding to who wants what meal.

We customize the registration process to replicate your event logos and colors for a seamless experience for your attendees. We also integrate with your merchant account. Funds are deposited directly into our account. Run multiple reports into spreadsheet format with the click of a mouse.

We offer a better more cost effective way to manage data so event planners can manage the event.

Specialties

Event Planning Management, Event Planning Software, Registrant Data Management
