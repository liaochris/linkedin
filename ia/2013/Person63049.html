Jessica Delgado's Summary

Currently, I work full time as an Internet Systems Analyst for Pensacola State College, located in the panhandle of Florida. Periodically I also do freelance design work in my spare time. My responsibilities in my current position include web design and mid-level back-end web development tasks.

I finished my degree in Web Design at The International Academy of Design and Technology in February 2010. I have a passion for beautiful design, well-kerned typography; and simple, elegant, standards-compliant, hand-written code. As a result, I am knowledgeable in HTML/XHTML, CSS, jQuery, Javascript, ColdFusion, PHP, Classic ASP, VBscript, C#, and SQL. I have also recently been learning and familiarizing myself with the updates being proposed in HTML5 and CSS3.

I am skilled with field related programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, and InDesign. Also, I have done quite a bit of work with the Wordpress CMS, as well as basic work with the Joomla CMS and with custom Tumblr layouts. I am familiar with MVC architecture and have designed several sites within the CakePHP framework. I have also worked in the ASP.NET MVC framework as well.

Specialties: Website production, XHTML, CSS, image and illustration design, photo manipulation, Javascript, PHP, Coldfusion, SQL (as implemented in both MySQL and Microsoft SQL Server), visual merchandising, customer service, writing.
