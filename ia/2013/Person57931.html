Jamie Arindaeng's Summary

WHAT SHOULD YOU KNOW?

Have you ever wondered what types of advertising or marketing channels to use to promote your business and help capture your market? What about how much money should be spent on advertising? These questions should be answered before any advertising is put into place.

Customers respond differently to advertising channels so it’s important to first clearly define who your customer is. Once your customer is defined a strategy plan should be put into place. The strategy plan should include what media channels will be the most effective in helping to now capture that customer. Media channels can include things like, trade shows, brand identity, video, or web-based strategies such as search engine optimization.


WHY VIM IS DIFFERENT: PLAN. PRINT. PROMOTE

Visual Impact Marketing (VIM) is an affordable marketing/advertising agency that helps small to midsize companies with customizable channel solutions.


VIM helps to better define your target market by first developing a marketing strategy plan using your company’s 4P’s-product, price, placement, and promotion. VIM then helps to assist in executing that strategy plan through many advertising channels—design, print, TV, radio, 3-D, video, web, and more!

Through its network of strategic partners, VIM offers its customers a convenient, one-stop shopping experience that makes advertising easy and affordable.
