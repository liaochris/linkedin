George Mentz JD, MBA, CWM®, QFP, MFP®'s Summary

International Lawyer, Celebrated Author, Law Professor, Keynote Speaker, Syndicated Writer, International Business Owner, Executive Development services in: Finance & Management in 50 countries. Mentz is a 2 Time Winner of a National Faculty Award for Superior Performance in recognition of Excellence in Teaching. Mentz has consulted with the US, China, Brazil, & UAE governments along with the United Nations & World Bank.

The first person in the United States to achieve "Quad Designation" Status as a JD, MBA, QFP, licensed financial planner, and Certified Financial Consultant. Prof. Mentz has published 20+ Books and has been featured or quoted in the Wall Street Journal, The Hindu National, El Norte Latin America, the Financial Times, Forbes, Reuters, AP, The China Daily, & The Arab Times. Prof. Mentz has been elected to the advisory board of the Global Finance Forum in Switzerland, The Royal Society of Fellows, and the World E-Commerce Forum in the UK. Mentz was President of the Law Review at Loyola for the Tax & Estate Planning. He holds a faculty appointment at the Graduate LLM Law Program and has held faculty status with several business schools globally. Prof. Mentzes' companies have worked to enhance educational standards in over 40 Countries around the world. Prof. Mentz has recently been awarded a National Faculty Award, a Distinguished Faculty Award, a Meritorious Service Medal for Charitable Service, a Team Faculty Award, an award winning author, & has taught over 200 college, law school, and graduate courses at accredited programs.

Mentz serves on the Advisory Board & Standards Boards of the: IFI India Finance Institute, AABFS Arab Academy of Banking & Financial Sciences, ACCE Chartered Economists Association, Latin Capitulo para Financiero, & The IAQFP Intl Assoc for Qualified Financial Planners.

In his early career, Mentz, a political scientist, was a coalitions organizer in Washington DC advising and writing on behalf of a Former US President.

Specialties

Lawyer, professor, author, educator, motivational speaker, training, educational quality, recruiting expert, HR hiring and screening, quality control, curriculum management, certified online professor, certified course developer.
