Mayank Garg's Summary

Mayank Garg is a strategy, finance and economics enthusiast for any business setting. He is very passionate about creating new products and constantly improving existing ones. He recently joined Enova International as a strategy and marketing analyst.

Mayank graduated from Purdue University in December 2012 with Bachelors in Industrial Engineering with honors and highest distinction. He has gained a strong background in analysis, strategy, communication and leadership and he thrives on new ideas, continuous learning, teamwork and problem solving. He also continues to research in the field of macroeconomics and global policy issues.

Mayank continuously looks for new opportunities to explore and is always open to new business ideas. He is very passionate about working with start-ups.
