Julian Fris' Summary

Julian Fris is the Founder and Director of Neller Davies - a management consultancy specialising in Facilities & Hospitality Management solutions for a range of businesses and institutions in the UK, Europe & Americas.

We work directly for clients or in collaboration with others and the Neller Davies team comprises of freelance specialists who have all been successful senior executives in the industry. The business is not sector specific, support and advice is provided to the Business & Industry; Healthcare; Education; Government & Travel; Leisure & Entertainment markets.

BSc MBIFM MIOH FCSI

Key specialties:

- Developing & implementing strategic solutions
- Generating new business opportunities
- Developing and managing outsourcing programmes
- Strategic projects and cost reduction programmes
- Troubleshooting & resolving problem areas
- Innovative solutions
