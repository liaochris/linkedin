Ben Grossman's Summary

I'm a marketing and advertising strategist with a specialty in digital, social and emerging media. My ability to formulate insight-driven, distinctive points of view has been recognized globally and featured in leading publications, presentations to Fortune 100 audiences and widely distributed thought leadership pieces.

I pride myself on a unique background that blends my passion for business and marketing strategy with my immersion in technology, consumer behavior and design. Over the course of my career, I've worked to set strategy and develop initiatives for brands around the world including Bombardier, Subway, Brother International, Voxx International and Eaton Corporation.

I'm passionate about emerging marketing and consumer lifestyle trends, and like to explain them in clear-cut ways, so I also have been invited to speak frequently. I've been tapped to present at the Consumer Electronics Show (the largest tradeshow in North America), for Microsoft (the second largest software company in the world), and at other trade events.

Outside of the office, I enjoy embracing my love for chocolate chip cookies, using Instagram to appear a better photographer than I really am, and attempting to beat out thoughts of my clients' business challenges while doing yoga (I typically fail).
