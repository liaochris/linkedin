James Oliver Smith Jr's Summary

I am currently involved with designing web deployment and document management tools for writers to facilitate the publication of ebooks on Amazon's Kindle Direct Publishing platform. I am also a writer of poetry, creative nonfiction and fiction. My primary tool-development language is Perl on Open Source software platforms and libraries. Due to my deeply compromised vision (pigment dispersion glaucoma) I have had to radically change how I do all of my work, using a variety of special hardware and software tools with significant alterations in the configurations used on systems with backlit screens.

Specialties

Writing. Ebook design. Digital publication of ebooks. Software tools (Perl, Open Source) for document management and web deployment.
