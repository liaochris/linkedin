Mark Ferretti's Summary

20+ years managing projects, IT and business personnel developing business applications, systems, and business operations. I have managed hundreds upon hundreds of projects and personnel and never had a project failure. This is due in large part to integrating Business Process Management (BPM) in the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC), bringing business and technology together in the definition, design, development, quality assurance, deployment, and production of integrated business systems.
