Eve Siegel, M.S., PCC, CPCC's Summary

• I am a Certified Life Coach through the Coaches Training Institute (CTI) and credentialed (PCC) through the International Coaching Federation (ICF). My friendly, interactive style supports professionals like you make successful career & life changes with clarity, confidence and passion while balancing the many-faceted demands of your life. I also coach students in the CTI certification program; mentor coaches renewing their ACC credential throught ICF; and mentor new and experienced coaches to enhance their skill and confidence.

My particular focus areas include helping professionals:
• Make fulfilling career change
• Handle the demands of career and life transitions
• Envision retirement
• Manage your time/energy to live the life you want
• Empower your actions by connecting with your authentic
voice
• Manifest your mid-life creative potential

With over 30 years' experience as a coach, somatic therapist, and non-profit director, I delight in helping professionals work and live their lives the way they truly want.

• Coach/Coach Mentor-- Sessions worldwide by phone. Coaching sessions & Body-Energy Experiencing (body awareness work)-- El Cerrito office (near Berkeley), CA, Skype, phone.
Free consultation for professionals committed to making vital career and life changes.

• Certified Somatic Therapist-- For over 20 years, I have offered effective touch/body-awareness support and solutions for stress and discomfort associated with navigating whole-life transitions (health, career, creativity, relationships). Office sessions in El Cerrito (near Berkeley), California.

• Experienced presenter-- Interactive teleclasses and events for professional and personal development based on my book, Success with Soul.

• Author, Success with Soul-- Loving Your Livelihood, Living in Balance. Published writer of coaching and somatic awareness articles.

• Former program director/coordinator for San Francisco School Volunteers, United Way.

Specialties

• Individual/group coaching to support professionals like you make successful career and life transitions
• Individual coaching for people wanting help in finding their authentic voice & creative paths
• Individual/group coaching for helping people create lives that balance professional, personal, and family demands
• Coaching/mentoring for students in the Coaches Training Institute (CTI) certification program
• Mentoring for coaches
• Teleclasses on personal and professional development topics

Phone and Skype sessions internationally. Office sessions available locally. Free consultation for people seriously interested in making positive career and personal changes. Go to www.kailaslifecoaching.com for access to my blog and free reports about creating changes that add value and energy to your life.

Body-Energy Experiencing supports professionals like you in career, relationship, and personal transitions who are experiencing stress, tension, and anxiety-- all of which are normal for people going through change. I use body-energy awareness techniques with coaching clients. I also offer additional, mindful-bodywork sessions if you wish to add greater depth, ease, and opening in your body as you make the changes you really want in your career and whole life.

  • "The High Cost of Ignoring Your Calling-- or Why to Work and Live Authentically!"

    • Coaching World-- ezine for the International Coach Federation (ICF)
    • June 2011
    Authors: Eve Siegel, M.S., PCC, CPCC

    Why is it so important to work and live in ways that are expressive of the person you really are? This article offers compelling reasons and examples of why this is so-- and how people often shortchange themselves in choosing careers and ways to live.

  • Success with Soul-- Loving Your Livelihood, Living in Balance

    • Ebook, self-published
    • March 15, 2013
    Authors: Eve Siegel, M.S., PCC, CPCC

    Having work you love is a vital part of a life that is rich and balanced with caring relationships, health and fitness, creativity, spirituality, and personal time. Success with Soul offers insights, support, and inspiration for opening your vision and overcoming obstacles. Here’s a professional coach’s guide to creating a livelihood, and a life, that authentically expresses who you are and what you are here to do in this lifetime.

    Success with Soul is based on Eve Siegel’s work integrating career/life transitions coaching with body-energy (somatic) experiencing. Accessibly written, it includes client stories, personal thoughts and experiences, illustrative quotes and questions, and coaching-somatic awareness exercises for exploring the elements of successful transitions to heart-centered livelihood and living in balance.

    This ebook is available at Amazon.com for Kindle and iPad/iPhone mobile readers at Amazon.com and at BarnesandNoble.com for Nook mobile readers. It is also available as a pdf download from www.kailaslifecoaching.com/success-with-soul/.
