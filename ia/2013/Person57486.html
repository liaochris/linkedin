James Hand's Summary

jameshandus.com was established by James Hand as a small freelance web design company which originated in Hampstead, NC and was later relocated to Pompano Beach, Florida. The primary focus of the company is the design and maintenance of websites. The business began as the result of interest in the internet and internet technology as well as knowledge gained at Visionair (formerly Vision Software), in Castle Hayne, North Carolina, and while being a key member of the Project7 Web Development Group.

Specialties

Access, CSS, DHTML, DreamWeaver, FireWorks, Flash, HTML, IIS, PhotoShop, PowerPoint, Swish, Wordpress, Graphic Design, Internet Research, Product Marketing
