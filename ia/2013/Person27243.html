Nick Halverson's Summary

I started Strategic Communication Partners to assist midsize businesses ($100 million - $1 billion in sales) plan, strategize and execute communication plans (social media, press releases, customer insights, etc). Additionally, given my experience in writing business plans and starting new business, analyzing market conditions and putting together marketing plans is also an expertise of mine. In my career I have spoken to groups from small senior management teams to groups of 1,500 sales people.

I have worked and lived in Australia, New Zealand and Costa Rica (in addition to the majority of my time in the USA) launching and managing two different companies. I now spend approximately 35% of my time in Costa Rica managing a 150 acre real estate development project. I have clients from nine different countries, and with social media and 3G cell networks, I can manage the marketing clients and real estate clients with relative ease regardless of my current location.

Previously, I spent ten years in international business development, marketing, and a small stint in investment banking in sell side mergers and acquisitions.

I have worked closely with companies such as Wal-Mart, Castrol (BP), Kellogg's, Fujifilm and Yamaha in developing and managing marketing and sponsorship programs for them.

Specialties: International business development, strategic planning and implementation, marketing and management, project management, foreign business start ups including all aspects of the business, day-to-day oversight of accounting, website development and deployment, negotiations with vendors, investor relations and sales training in varying market conditions (New Zealand, Costa Rica).

Improving my Spanish skills.
