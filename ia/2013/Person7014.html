Andrew Kornblatt's Summary

In the various roles I have held over the years, I have generated some incredible accomplishments. These great milestones included creating social media strategies to build active communities and networks, developing international educational events, developing outreach plans and campaigns that leveraged social media for San Francisco Agencies and green candidates. I also developed legislation that bans plastic bags in San Jose, organized teacher's protests and presences at budgetary hearings in San Diego, and put together events like a live art show and an indoor easter egg hunt for a small at-risk San Jose community.

I have done all this while juggling a life as an artist, a political activist, a blogger, a singer in a band and a writer. I am slightly obsessed with tea and can cook up a storm.
