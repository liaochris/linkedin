Lachlan Donald's Summary

I have more than 12 years' experience building disruptive applications that change how people think and work.

My experience has mostly been with startups. Specifically, I've focused on making marketplaces with dual audiences run smoothly, and applying technology to meet the varying expectations of users, the business and investors.

In 2008, I was the first employee of 99designs—now the world's largest marketplace for freelance design. I've been involved in every area of its operation, including engineering, product design, finance and marketing. Before that, I helped SitePoint power a global, passionate developer community, and lead a team that developed the SitePoint Marketplace, which has since evolved into Flippa.com.

I'm happiest when working with talented engineers and product people to create simple, beautiful things.

Specialties
