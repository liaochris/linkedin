Miguel Premoli's Summary

During my 21 years of Human Resources experience in Colgate-Palmolive, Wal-Mart Stores, Neoris Technology and PepsiCo, I have been able to:

• Define, together with other members of Executive Committees, the Business Strategy, Objectives and Goals of the organizations I worked for.
• Design and implement Human Resources Strategies to support Business Goals.
• “Coach” General Managers and Peers to become more effective Leaders.
• Lead Change to achieve transformational initiatives.
• Deploy leading edge HR practices, programs and interventions.
• Participate in Acquisition and Integration processes.
• Design organizational structures to increase speed of execution and productivity.
• Gain international experience in North America, Latin America, Europe and Asia.
.

Specialties: HR Generalist, Organizational Development, HR Strategy, M&A, HR Information Systems.
