Art Director and Creative Strategist experienced in conceptualizing, directing and producing corporate identity systems, web/interactive user interface, print advertorials, exhibit displays and motion-based presentations, for integrated marketing campaigns and events.

EXPERTISE:

PRINT DESIGN
Conceptualize and design print based materials such as: conference marketing collateral, environment graphics/displays, annual reports, brochures, corporate identity collateral and other specialized communication driven print media.

WEB/INTERACTIVE DESIGN
Develop front-end visual aesthetics of user interface (UI) for web/interactive media, migrating print content (text and images) to interactive platform, designing and delivering post-production multimedia files into finalized presentation and managing web assets (updating/repurposing) such as text, graphic, video and audio content online.

Maxine Morris' Education

Pratt Institute

MPS, Design Management (DM)

2013 – 2015 (expected)

Design Management Program (DMP) is a two-year program created to bridge the disciplines of design and business management. It is tailored to provide an executive education more focused than an M.B.A. on the special needs of design leaders managing design firms or managing design teams in creative industries. The curriculum is designed to develop strategic management skills in six study areas related to design management: operations management; financial management; marketing management; organization and human resource management; management of innovation and change; and management of local, regional, and global suppliers, distributors, and markets. The courses provide participants with an integrated focus on the role of design in the creation and management of strategic and sustainable advantage.
