Wendeen Eolis' Experience

Commander

Hope's Champion Task Force

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Legal Services industry

2001 – Present (12 years) HQ- New York City / Washington DC

EOLIS companies endorse and support my public service inititiatives, notably including my role as the leader of Hope's Champion Task Force.

In 2001, following the events of 9.11 I spearheaded a public service initiatives designed to assist displaced businesses, disaster operations recovery, and personal counseling programs. These efforts led to the development of a task force that emphasizes emergency preparedness planning and disaster operations recovery particularly with respect to the vetting and provisioning of emergency legal services required--both in the United States and abroad. This non-military position focuses on close cooperation with representatives of international, federal, state and local agencies, and civic and corporate entities.

Chairman and Chief Executive Officer

EOLIS; Eolis International Group, Eolis Institute for Leadership,Wendeen Eolis Enterprises

September 1967 – Present (46 years 4 months) HQ - New York

In 1967 I founded EOLIS as a pioneer in attorney placement and reports on legal marketplace conditions, serving major firms and companies. In 1969 I founded the first attorney placement and retainer search company exclusively for practicing lawyers and have specialized in search and related consulting services to the legal profession and clients of legal services ever since. Responding to the changes and growth in the legal profession my practice includes diverse services offered by distinct EOLIS entities under the umbrella of Wendeen Eolis Enterprises, Inc.

Eolis International Group as a multi dimensional legal consultancy that specializes in top level attorney searches, counsel reviews, attorney career transitions and employment / separation agreements.

Eolis Institute for Leadership emphasizes career coaching, negotiation training and decision strategies for attorneys in all manner of professional, political, government and business positions.

In addition, I advise government agencies, politicians and the C-suite in connection with leadership objectives especially as they relate to legal affairs.

1st Assistant and Senior Advisor to the Governor (New York)

State of New York

Government Agency; 10,001+ employees; Government Administration industry

January 1995 – December 1995 (1 year)

In 1994 I was recruited by Governor George Pataki as an independent thinker and advisor during candidacy for the Govenorship and joined his administration as First Assistant and Senior Advisor to the Govenor during his first year in office. My portfolio included confidential and special projects including rent deregulation, private and tribal gaming issues, and other special projects following which I was an outside advisor consulted by various state agencies in connection with the assessment of lawyers and law firm relationships.

Special Advisor to Rudolph W. Giuliani (New York Mayor)

City of New York and private practice

January 1988 – October 1993 (5 years 10 months)

During the first year of Rudy Giuliani's administration, I served as a City Hall Special Advisor on personnel, communications, and law department matters.
Earlier, I had advised Major Giuliani concerning career alternatives in private practice and public service, and served on his mayoralty campaigns in 1989 and 1993, and as a member of his transition team.
