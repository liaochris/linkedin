Erica Jennings' Summary

Owner and founder of JENNINGS DESIGN, a design firm based in Plano, Texas.

Services include graphic design, web design and development, book design and layout, ebook production, A/V editing and production, and project management.

Specialties: Book design, book cover design, layouts, ebook design, business cards, logos, corporate identity, direct mail, postcards, flyers, invitations, newsletters, brochures, signage, promotional materials, media kits, CD/DVD packaging, PowerPoint templates, print advertisements, website design and management (CSS/HTML/Joomla/Wordpress), banner ads, social media brand management, e-mail marketing, consulting, and project management for small businesses, nonprofits, and other entrepreneurs/solepreneurs.

Publishing services include: Project management; hiring editors, proofreaders, writers, illustrators, and/or photographers; designing layouts and covers for both print and EPUB/e-reader devices (including Kindle, iPad, and Nook); printing; and providing additional marketing support in the form of media kits, websites, and other promotional items.

Experience with a wide variety of book genres, including: arts and photography; audiobooks; biographies and memoirs; business and investing; children's literature; Christian; cooking; fantasy; general fiction; health and wellness; inspirational and motivational; mystery and thrillers; parenting and families; professional and technical; religion and spirituality; and self-help.

Knowledge of the following industries: architecture; athletics; consulting; direct selling and party plan; education; health and medical; media; personal development; publishing; sales; and self-help.
