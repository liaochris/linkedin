Alina Steinberg Baugh, Psychotherapy

July 2001 – Present (12 years 2 months) Burlingame

Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist practicing in the field of Counseling for almost two decades. With broad experience in group as well as individual therapy, Alina Baugh enjoys providing counseling services for clients at her private practice office in Burlingame in San Mateo County, California.

With treatment approaches that combine the most effective in psychotherapy, including Cognitive Behavioral, or CBT Therapy, Family Systems Therapy, Family/Marital Therapy, Relational Therapy, and Solution Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT), Alina Baugh has a strong compassion and empathy for her clients. She provides a trusting and safe environment for them that encourages empowerment, self-discovery, confidence, and a renewed sense of purpose. With Alina's expert listening capabilities and dynamic feedback techniques, it's no wonder that her clients, some of whom considered themselves in "a desperate state" before seeing Alina, are thrilled at the peace, balance, and emotional well-being they found through their psychotherapy sessions with her. Comments such as, "she is a great listener and gives me great peace," and "Alina opened my eyes to issues I didn't even know I had," are a testimony to her transformative skills with clients.
Specialty areas of psychoanalytic treatment for Alina Baugh's scope of services include: Addiction, Alcohol Abuse, Anger Management, Behavioral Issues, Career Counseling, Depression, Divorce Support, Domestic Abuse, Drug Abuse, Body Image and Self-Esteem, Family Conflict, Life Coaching, Parenting, Peer Relationships, Relationship and Trust Issues, Substance Abuse, Spousal Conflict and Stress Related to Infertility.

Alina offers free phone consultations for new clients, and can be contacted through her website, www.alinabaugh.com.

Like me: https://www.facebook.com/AlinaBaughMFT
