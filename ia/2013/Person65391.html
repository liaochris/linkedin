Joel Bander's Summary

I have 25 years of progressive litigation, immigration and law firm management experience, including criminal or personal injury jury trials, management of numerous simultaneous successful wage and hour class actions.

I have successfully processed thousands of immigration applications, as well as obtaining post-conviction relief and successful criminal pleas to avoid adverse immigration consequences.

I have also created a mortgage assistance program that has saved homeowners millions of dollars on their mortgages.

I am a skilled presenter, lecturer, public spokesperson, and investigator. See video on www.banderlaw.com web page.

I have traveled extensively in 43 states and 22 foreign countries for both business and personal reasons. I have been to every California county except Modoc. I have been to the Philippines over 25 times in the last ten years. Lifelong St. Louis Cardinals fan and avid baseball fan, and have been to 13 different major league stadiums, love to hike, be outdoors, bicycling, history (U.S Grant aficionado), politics music lover, play piano. I have been to 220 Grateful Dead shows in 11 states. Married for 25 years and have a 10th grade honor student son.
