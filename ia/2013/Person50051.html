Greg Blanton's Summary

I have working in various technical, engineering and managament roles within the Video and Film equipment rental industry since 1984.

Starting my career with 35/16mm film and 1" videotape equipment at Victor Duncan Inc., ending my rental career at Bexel working with direct to disk HD digital media content and high speed HD video systems.

My journey has led me to an independent position as a broadcast and live event workflow consultant. Speciallizing in managing extremely demanding large scale productions and offering solutions that unify the field and studio elements of all production, operations and engineering needs.
