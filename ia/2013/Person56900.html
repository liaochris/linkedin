Jade Walker's Experience

Overnight Editor

The Huffington Post

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Online Media industry

September 2011 – Present (2 years 2 months) Greater New York City Area

Produced hundreds of breaking news stories, including the wars in Afghanistan, Iraq and Syria, the Arab Spring, the Occupy Wall Street movement, the 2012 election and Olympics, Hurricane Sandy and its aftermath, the assassination of Ambassador Chris Stevens in Benghazi, the resignation of Pope Benedict XVI, the manhunt for the Boston Marathon bombers and countless mass shootings. Updated liveblogs for Hurricane Sandy, the 2012 election, the OWS movement and the end of the world. Posted breaking news and promotional items on Facebook and Twitter (@Huffingtonpost). Created slideshows and sent breaking news alerts.

Overnight Editor

Night Owl News (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Online Media industry

February 2011 – September 2011 (8 months) Hinsdale, NH

Wrote a comprehensive roundup of the world, U.S. and entertainment stories that appeared on the Web overnight. Updated the Night Owl News Website using WordPress software. Generated more than 100 public opinion polls. Posted breaking news and promotional items on Facebook and Twitter (@niteowlnews)

Overnight Editor/Producer

Yahoo! News

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; yhoo; Internet industry

September 2007 – January 2011 (3 years 5 months)

Produced thousands of breaking news stories, including the wars in Afghanistan and Iraq, the earthquakes in Haiti and New Zealand, the devastating floods in Pakistan and China, the massive BP oil spill in the Gulf of Mexico, the outbreak and global spread of the H1N1 virus, the assassination of former Pakistani prime minister Benazir Bhutto, the 2008 and 2010 elections and the 2008 Olympics in Beijing. Created a database of advance obituaries, complete with articles, videos, slideshows, links and other multimedia elements. Launched and penned The Starting Point, a daily blog offering readers a snapshot of the news stories that occurred overnight. Featured breaking news and promotional items on Facebook and Twitter (@yahoonews).

Overnight Editor/Producer

The Associated Press

Nonprofit; 1001-5000 employees; Media Production industry

April 2005 – September 2007 (2 years 6 months)

Produced thousands of breaking news stories, including the wars in Afghanistan and Iraq, the 2006 midterm elections, Hurricane Katrina and the 2006 Olympics in Torino. Produced the Homepage, U.S., Business, World, Entertainment, Sports, Technology, Politics, Science, Health and Odd News pages on Yahoo! News.

Overnight Editor/Producer

The New York Times Website

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; NYT; Newspapers industry

February 2000 – March 2005 (5 years 2 months)

Produced thousands of breaking news stories, including Sept. 11th and its one-year anniversary, the wars in Afghanistan and Iraq, the 2000 Bush/Gore election, the sinking of the Russian sub Kursk, the bombing of the U.S.S. Cole and the D.C. sniper case. Produced the Homepage, Obituaries, Portraits of Grief, A Nation Challenged, The War in Iraq, Week in Review, The Olympics, Fashion/Styles, Weddings and Pictures of the Times. Member of the Web team that won the 2002 Pulitzer Prize in the Public Service category for A Nation Challenged, a special section published online and in print after the Sept. 11th terrorist attacks.

Editor-in-Chief

Inscriptions Magazine

August 1997 – February 2002 (4 years 7 months)

Assignment editor for 50 freelance writers. Edited 70-page weekly magazine and produced Website. Wrote writing-related articles and book reviews.
