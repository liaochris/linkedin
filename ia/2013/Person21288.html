Chris Atwood's Summary

I'm an integrated marketing professional with experience in business development, nonprofit development (donor and corporate), digital/interactive strategy specializing in direct marketing (social/email), blog development, content curation and outreach, public and media relations strategy, traditional media advertising planning and execution (TV/radio/print), and publicity.

I've worked on a wide range of accounts both business-to-business and business-to-consumer in logistics, energy, major consumer/public events, healthcare, retail and retail restaurant.

With more than a decade of experience in web development and management, five years working in advertising and marketing at the agency level, and more than five years as a contract graphic designer, I've become an interesting mix of digital marketing, public relations and marketing consultant where I can help clients create and implement strategies to transform their businesses and take action.

Specialties

Website design (HTML/XHTML, CSS, PHP, jQuery/Javascript); Writing (Feature stories, PR writing, Ad copywriting); Editing (AP, MLA); Adobe Creative Suite (inDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Dreamweaver); Project management; Print coordination/production management; Digital coordination/production management; Social Media; Event coordination; Primary research tactics
