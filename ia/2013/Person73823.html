Kellie Poulsen-Grill's Summary

I am a happiness expert. My company is Whirlwind Publishing and speaking services. I'm a professional speaker and a member of the National Speaker Association. I speak on creating happiness on a daily basis and conquering fear and having no regrets in both business and personal life. I am an author and I have co-authored the life altering non fiction book on hope, healing, and love entitled "Send Me A Sign".
