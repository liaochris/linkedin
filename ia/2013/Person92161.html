Michael Albee's Summary

Michael blends a contemporary style with a touch of whimsy to create memorable graphic art pieces. His love of fashion and art, drive his ability to keep current with market trends as well as maintain an editing eye when composing new works. Armed with a sketch pad or pen tablet and a cup of coffee … he has the unique ability to whip up graphic genius in just about any medium. Web Designer, illustrator, graphic artists, painter ... no matter what he is doing ...

Michael Albee's Projects

  • If A Frog Had Wings - Children's Book

    • January 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: Michael Albee, Beth Ryan

    Hopson is a charismatic frog who appreciates his fellow amphibians and his life on Pebble Pond. When Hopson overhears Uncle Croaker speak, at a frog town meeting, that “frogs are disappearing”, he becomes concerned about his fate and the destiny of Pebble Pond. In his search for answers, to the disappearing frogs, he tumbles into in a world preserved by earth fairies. One particular fairy named Asiana, helps him discover the fate of his earth, his amphibian relatives, and his purpose for being. Hopson discovers his self worth, and demonstrates to his family and friends on Pebble Pond how all creatures must contribute to a cleaner earth!
