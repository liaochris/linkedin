Anthony Alvarado's Summary

Anthony has worked with Ayres Associates for over 12 years now doing everything from running and managing hydrographic survey and mapping projects to designing bank protection for Sacramento River levees to hydraulic modeling and sediment transport. He also helps keep our office up to speed on GIS applications and software and have a lot of experience utilizing ArcGIS for engineering water resource applications including HEC GeoRAS, working with imagery and geodatabases, and generating map sets. He has been managing projects now for the past 5 years and has completed the 2nd level of the internal manager training program.
Anthony loves the variety of work that he does and enjoys management with the administrative and logistical side of things. He loves being in the field and opportunities to train others.

Specialties

Geomatics Engineering, River Engineering, GIS, Cartography, GPS Survey, Hydrographic Survey & Mapping, Geomorphology, Bank Protection Design, Levees, Sediment Transport, Trimble GPS Handheld & Field Form development
