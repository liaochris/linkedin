David Ryan's Summary

Highly experienced and motivated practitioner, with a background in serious and organised crime investigations across international frontiers, and an immersion in Hostage Negotiation and Crisis Negotiation techniques co-ordination and management.

A skilled subject matter specialist with a raft of engagements and deployments across policing, public and private sector. An effective change manager, results driven and a team player with a network of industry contacts. Able to identify business opportunities, whilst plugging loopholes in systems processes and procedures.

Able to see how you can protect your people, your customers, your property, your business, your profit and your brand and do it consensually Trades Union or Trades Association engagement.

DRC are specialists in “Tiger Kidnap” resilience, response and return to normality issues and other specialist crisis interventions, with big brand international clients across the Banking & Financial Institutions, Retail, Global Logistics, FMCG, Vaulting, Cash in in Transit and Precious Metals and Construction.

Specialities: Communication skills as a negotiator and crisis manager. Able to influence and nurture others into options for change. Strong leadership capabilities, for mentoring and supporting individuals on commercial crisis management teams. A competent public speaker, orator, MC & media spokesperson. A strategic thinker with strong commercial awareness, and a high ethos of confidentiality. Able to compartmentalise components of a strategic plan, & influence practitioners /operatives to deliver. Can highlight gaps in the policy which will impede effective response and reassurance.
