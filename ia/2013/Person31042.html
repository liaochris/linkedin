Dave Deib's Summary

What are YOU doing to maximize lead generation for your company?

We all know regardless of how good your product or service, if potential customers can’t find you, they will continue to buy from your direct competitors.

As a born marketer, salesman, marketing strategist and business coach, my focus is to provide information that increases lead generation and creates success. In 2005, I launched US Web Authority to help individuals and companies succeed in the global market while receiving full-service internet marketing, website customization, SEO optimization, online strategies, tools and ongoing support.

With the advent of social media platforms, competition rates among private enterprise have soared exponentially. I have seen people and companies thrive with effective marketing strategies or wither because they didn’t differentiate themselves from their competition.

As a result, I have launched Biz Match Plus to complement what you are already doing to drive more targeted traffic to your business. As an online supportive community for hundreds of members, we provide opportunities for businesses that want to leverage the power and resources offered by an exclusive web platform. As you use our program, you increase your network of prospects which convert to happy customers and clients.

By choosing a complimentary (Basic) membership or fee-based (Premium) subscription, you will get a full-page listing featuring your business with a SEO Lead Generation Platform.
