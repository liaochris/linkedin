Matthew Werner's Summary

Specialties

Custom Furniture, Handcrafted Furniture, Marquetry, Music Stands

I work closely with clients to design and create custom furniture that meets their unique needs. My specialty is marquetry, and many of my pieces are embellished with scenes inspired by nature. My work is influenced by Asian themes, particularly Japanese design aesthetics.

Sole Proprietor

Matthew Werner, Handcrafted Furniture

1998 – Present (15 years)

Custom design and manufacture of unique handcrafted furniture. Specialty in marquetry inlay. Please see website for examples.

Work is sold through fine furniture galleries throughout the west coast, or built to order for individual customers. I participate in the annual Open Studios Art Tour during October, which this year brought over 1,000 visitors to my shop.

I design, implement, and maintain my own website. I also publish a monthly E-Newsletter (sign-ups on website).
