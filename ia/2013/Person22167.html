Charles Koch's Summary

I have been involved in consumer product and parts and service sales and marketing for over 25 years. Originally was based in New York w/ MTV Networks-Time Warner. Moved into the consumer product marketing and advertising field where I was involved in all aspects from, Media placement, advertisement Creative and Client Product positioning. Moved to Florida in the early 90's continuing in media and marketing. Introduced to the 3D design world about 16 years ago. Started 3xis Development Inc., 10 years back as a specialty company in product development but more importantly as a resource for low volume/short runs and Rapid Manufacturing services.

Specialties

Product development, prototype manufacturing and Rapid manufacturing services. New product market positioning
