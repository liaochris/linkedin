Ben Wilkoff's Summary

Teacher, Learner, Leader and Entrepreneur. Skills in collaboration, community creation and putting diverse technologies together and making them work as a cohesive system. From implementing single-sign on systems for Moodle to blogging and community management since 2005, I have always focused on creating learning spaces for others and then reflecting on that process. I'm Interested in any opportunity that challenges me to lead change and create authentic learning experiences. Whether that is in an EdTech startup, school district, or professional development endeavor, I have skills and experience dealing with solving existing problems and figuring out new ones to tackle in order to move forward.

Ben Wilkoff's Projects

  • Reflective Practice Vlogging Project

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Ben Wilkoff, Gallit Zvi, Sheri Edwards, Darren Kuropatwa, Ginger Lewman, Timothy Villegas

    So, here is my train of thought.

    If Learning is Change, then a Change List is really a Learning List.

    As in, everything that I could list out to try and Change in education, would really be a series of things I want to Learn and talk about.

    With that said, I am now embarking on something that I hope many of you will join me in doing.

    I will be doing a Video Blog of the “Learning is Change List” as often as I can this year. This vlog will be a way for me to accomplish the kinds of reflective practice that I lacked since I finished the 365 Questions that Google Can’t answer at the end of 2010. That is an awfully long time to go without actively doing reflection for my daily learning.

    It is also an incredibly long time to go without actively trying to cultivate or engage in a community of fellow reflective practitioners. So, not only will I be vlogging on a semi-daily basis, I am calling out to anyone with a webcam and an interest in being a part of a supportive community to join us.
