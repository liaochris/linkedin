Jackie Prendergast's Summary

Jackie is a Management Consultant, Trainer, Coach, Mentor and sometimes author who has a firm belief that people drive business success and that getting the people aspects of a business right will mean the difference between surviving and thriving.

She is owner of Consulting Excellence which she established in 2007. She works with small and medium sized businesses, equiping them with the strategies, tools, skills and practices to realise the potential of their people and ensure an organisation culture that actually works. Jackie also mentors small business on behalf of Dublin City Enterprise Board, providing them with the support to help them get ahead in their business.

Prior to setting up Consulting Excellence Jackie spent a number of years as a HR Manager and also worked as an Advisory Officer with the Labour Relations Commission, giving her a unique skills set in HR, I.R., Employee Engagement, Training & Coaching. Having been brought up in business (and working there for some time) she is very familiar with the challenges that SMES face and of course establishing and running her own business has rounded those skills even further.

Jackie's previous experience has included major change programmes such as organisation restructuring, Changes to Work Practices, New Pay Deals, Team Development Programmes, private coaching projects and a range of other training and consulting projects.

Jackie is a Fellow of the CIPD and Associate Member of the Intstiute of Management Consultants & Advisers, She runs an online Network Group for SMES on LinkedIn - SME Links Ireland to promote thought, collaboration and business and blogs and writes articles around her areas of expertise.

Specialties

Consultancy: Providing SMEs with the help and support they need to put in place the practices and behaviours that will make their organisation culture work, address their people issues and help achieve success

Training: Providing bespoke training to develop team and management skills

Coaching: Working one-to-one and with Executive teams to realise potential

Mentoring: Supporting SME owners, providing the ear to listen, asking the tough questions and helping to clarify their thinking
