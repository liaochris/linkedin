JM Auron's Summary

As a Professional IT Executive Resume Writer, I'm committed making my client's careers better.

My clients lead in innovation and business vision. They're very smart people, smart enough to write a decent resume. They're also smart enough to know that decent just isn't good enough.

Because every resume needs to tell a story with a happy ending: "I need to talk to you. When can we set up an interview?"

But IT resumes—whether for executives including IT Manager, IT Director, and CIO / CTO, or for senior technologists—are the most complex projects a professional resume writer can take on.

That's because every IT resume needs to speak clearly to two very different audiences.

Your resume needs to clearly demonstrate your business value to a non-technical HR professional, and simultaneously present your technical depth. Most IT resumes succeed in only one of those areas. Either the resume 'dumbs down' your technical achievements for readability, or the resume is too dense to be understood by a non-technical audience.

I specialize in crafting IT resumes that do both. Seamlessly, clearly, and effectively.

I'm able to do that because I'm one of the few professional resume writers in the industry to focus exclusively on IT resumes. This enables me to understand not only the technical details of your career, but to communicate the real business value you've delivered.

My unique, highly personalized approach adds value to my client's careers. I serve as a trusted, strategic partner, and leverage my exceptional communication, interpersonal, and interviewing abilities to help my clients clarify their careers.

Finally? I stand behind my work. I'm the only professional resume writer that I know of who offers a full, 100% money back guarantee.

I'm happy to say that, in my career, not a single client has taken me up on that offer.

If you'd like to discuss how I work, feel free to connect with me here - or through my website.

I'm fascinated with the transformation that technology has brought to all our lives. This fascination, coupled with my commitment to helping exceptional candidates take the next step in their careers, impelled me to launch Quantum Tech Resumes - a professional resume writing service dedicated to helping the "best and brightest" drive dramatic career growth.

I deliver exceptional IT Resumes, Cover Letters, LinkedIn Profiles, Professional Bios, and Thank You letters for CIOs, CTOs, IT Directors, IT Managers, Software and Infrastructure professionals, and Program / Project Managers who are committed to taking the next step in their careers.

My process is highly personalized, to ensure that I craft career documents that truly capture your unique value proposition.

I never use questionnaires to learn about your career. My process is based on in-depth, comprehensive phone interviews. In addition to ensuring that I have the most complete information possible to craft your resume, my interview will help you gain critical clarity on the value that you've delivered in your career. My clients often end our interview by telling me that they hadn't realized how much they had actually accomplished until after we'd spoken.

But my commitment to personal service goes deeper. I never subcontract out my resume projects. You can be certain that you will be working directly with me from our first conversation through project completion.

You can be confident that a Quantum Tech Resume won't just be a cookie cutter copy. It will be a clear, customized, and compelling presentation of your unique career.

My resumes are strategic - and get attention. Just today, a client emailed me to tell me that she's gotten 5 calls on the resume I crafted for her - in the first morning since she posted. That's what I like to hear…
