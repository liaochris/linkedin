Janis Pettit's Summary

Twenty-three years of experience as an entrepreneur owning four successful businesses--10 years as a small business growth and marketing consultant and coach. Extensive experience as a motivational speaker. Articles published locally and internationally. Hosted a business cable talk show in NY and was named to "50 Women of Achievement under 50".

With over 10,000 small business subscribers, we have helped hundreds of small and solo business owners worldwide by focusing on the key strategies and systems that will help them create a highly profitable lifestyle business. Focus areas include marketing, social media, systems development, sales, business strategy and planning and achieving independence and life balance.

Services include:
- small business growth and marketing coaching and mentoring
- Online marketing services and social media planning
- Tele-seminars, webinars and video training
- Group coaching, mentoring and masterminds
- Live presentations and keynotes
- E-courses and E-books
- Joint ventures

Specialties

Ways to your knowledge and expertise into a lucrative business, marketing strategy/planning and implementation, online lead generation and conversion, social media planning, growth business model and systems, sales strategy and skills training, entrepreneurial skills, business planning, expansion strategy

Our niche - small and solo business owners, micro lifestyle businesses, knowledge based businesses and independent service professionals.
