Bill Thorness' Summary

More than 25 years as a writer and editor in Seattle, extending into teaching in recent years.

Writing: three non-fiction books, feature and news writing for newspapers, magazines and online media.

Editing: books, magazines, web content, more.

Publishing: editorial leadership, budgeting, staff management; website and new publication development; consulting.

Corporate: annual reports, white papers, ghostwriting, speeches, presentations, web content, newsletters and event publications; conference organization, management and speaking.

Teaching: Instructor in the University of Washington Certificate in Editing program; presenter on edible gardening regionally.

Specialties

Healthcare, Property-Casualty Insurance, Bicycling, Bike Touring in the Seattle Area, Edible Gardening, Urban Farming, Lifestyle topics.
