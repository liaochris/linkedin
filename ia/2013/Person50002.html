Specialties

Since 1971, Green Sign Company is a Midwest leader full sign service custom sign manufacturing company. We range from modern Electronic Message Centers, custom build signs and full color vinyl graphics & award winning designs to complete sign service and repair. Green Signs' Education

Various Siminars and Conventions

Sign Industry

Shawn Green has been elected to the position of President of the Midwest Sign Association (MSA). The MSA is a six-state regional trade association with 50 years experience in serving custom, commercial and production sign companies, product manufacturers, reps and supply distributors in Michigan, Indiana, Ohio, Kentucky, West Virginia, and western Pennsylvania.
Shawn will take office in January and serve through the end of 2012. Shawn served as Vice President in 2010 and Secretary of association in 2008 and 2009, and as Treasure in 2006 and 2007. Prior to that, Shawn was a MSA Trustee in 2004 and 2005. He was also a past president of his local Kiwanis and is a member of the local Chamber of Commerce and the International Sign Association. Shawn also received the prestigious First Place Award from the Signs of the Times Magazine’s 2001 Electric Sign Contest, eclipsing all entries worldwide, for Best Freestanding Original Logo Sign.

Activities and Societies: Midwest Sign Association, Kiwanis, United States Sign Council, ISA
