Linda V. Eriksen's Summary

Linda became a Realtor at SKBK Sotheby's International Realty in 2002. Linda has devoted her real estate career to helping families, couples, and individuals with perhaps their most important financial decision: the purchase and/or sale of a home. Her real estate practice is focused in Oakland County Michigan, with a specialty in the Birmingham/Bloomfield area. Linda regularly provides marketing plans and advises Sellers on the best way to sell their home for the highest possible price. Similarly, Linda provides Buyers with the necessary education and area knowledge to make an intelligent purchase. All Linda's clients benefit from her area expertise, energy, professionalism, and commitment to an industry she genuinely loves!

Specialties: luxury real estate, Birmingham homes, Bloomfield Hills homes, negotiation, marketing plans.
