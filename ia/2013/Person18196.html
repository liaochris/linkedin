Bart Jansen's Summary

Hello my name is Bart Jansen and I work as a designer in New York, NY. My work ranges across disciplines, from product to packaging design and from initial concept to final product.

Born and raised in The Netherlands, my professional experience extends from Europe to the United States. I design for smaller independent companies as well as major cooperate accounts including: branding, structural packaging and consumer products.

Every good design needs a solid concept. Starting from there, I always work with the end-user in mind, a sharp brand sensibility and great care and attention to detail.
