Lisa-Jane Meates' Summary

I have always been very entrepreneurial and creative with a keen interest in Photography and Business, particularly in Marketing and Advertising. My degree in Photography for Fashion and Advertising almost seemed tailor made for me. I love meeting new people, sharing experiences, working together and learning from one another.

My retail experience and sales background gave me a great insight into the marketing strategies used by businesses to enhance their sales both on the shop floor and online. I have 7 years of experience working in customer service based jobs which has improved my skills of recognising the needs of the customer and using my initiative to satisfy those needs to the best of my abilities.

My role at Leopard Digital involves, using both creative and technical means, implementing marketing and advertising strategies through social media to improve our clients online presence, maximising visibility, therefore reaching more customers and increasing sales. In addition to my Marketing Executive role, new duties have been recently added to my position to include the responsibility of a Business Analyst. This includes frequent meetings with the Directors of the company, taking note of the current strategies and amending any changes, providing recommendations for improvement and presenting findings in a visual format.

Outside of the office, I work as a freelance photographer and Etsy shop owner. Please click the link below to view my photography work.
