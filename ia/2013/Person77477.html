Kun-I Chang's Summary

Kuni Chang, born in Taiwan, is an art director/motion graphics designer based in New York. A moviegoer, toy collector, comic reader, and outdoors lover, he sky dives for idea inspiration and uses house cleaning for relaxation.

Kun-I specializes in art direction, design, animation, and is known for his intriguing rotoscoping style. His short films and art projects have received numerous nominations and awards including Promax/BDA Award, Bitfilm Festival FX MIX Award, San Diego Comic-Con Independent Film Award, and recognized by design magazines such as Stash and IDN. Major clients include MTV, VH1, Super Bowl, AT&T, Bank of America, and Ford. He is currently an senior art director at Click3X, New York.
