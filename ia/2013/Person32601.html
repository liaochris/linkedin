David Sonatore, LMSW's Summary

Long Story Short...

Telling David’s story backwards would reveal an abundance of seemingly separate paths, which have all since culminated into one single symbiotic mission -- helping others achieve well-being and quality of life. As a career/life coach, David was trained by renowned author and popular life coach, Dr. Martha Beck (The Oprah Winfrey Show & O Magazine). David enhances his contributions to this burgeoning field with his experience as a licensed LMSW, where he’s grown a compassionate private psychotherapy practice inspired by his time spent in clinical settings ranging from the counseling of inmates in Harlem, NY to the treatment of outpatient clients at St. Vincent’s Hospital’s adult psychiatric clinic in Greenwich Village, NY. David is also an Equine Facilitated Therapy practitioner and completed his apprenticeship in Tucson, AZ with equine therapy pioneer Linda Kohanov (Tao of Equus). David continues to conduct Equine Therapy workshops throughout North America bringing people from both the private and corporate spheres together with horses to inspire innovative, impactful and expedient personal and professional growth. Using his platform as a freelance writer, radio guest and presenter, David shares his unique insights, perspectives and solutions to help individuals create lives lived by design, rather than by default.
