Lars Schmidt's Summary

I’m a fierce HR advocate with a strong track record in building, developing, and leading progressive teams. I’m a change catalyst who believes strongly in blending best practices, technology, and relationships to develop and drive talent initiatives with limited resources; leveraging innovative recruiting techniques and building proactive and sustainable global talent programs.

My approach to talent acquisition is deeply rooted in relationship-based embedded recruiting; empowering recruiters to align themselves with the clients they support. I believe when recruiting is approached as a transaction, it will fail.

My global experience spans a variety of industries including technology, web, media, non-profit, eCommerce and consulting sectors.

I created @NPRjobs / #NPRlife and founded #PubJobs to leverage social media to raise awareness of career opportunities across public media.

Founder and blogger on Amplify Talent (http://www.AmplifyTalent.com).

Connect with me on twitter.com/ThisIsLars, about.me/ThisIsLars, twylah.com/ThisIsLars

Specialties: Global Talent Acquisition Leadership, Executive Recruiting, Mentoring and Coaching, Employment Branding, Social Media, Talent Attraction, Marketing, Public Speaking, Employee Development, Employee Engagement, Employee Communications, HR M&A Integration, Performance Management, Sourcing Strategy, Business Development, Human Resources, Blogging

Lars Schmidt's Experience

Senior Director, Talent Acquisition & Innovation

NPR

Nonprofit; 501-1000 employees; Broadcast Media industry

February 2011 – Present (2 years 7 months) Washington D.C. Metro Area

In this turn-around role I was brought in to lead the rebuilding of the talent acquisition function across NPR as part of a broad HR organizational rebuilding effort. My focus is providing leadership and advocacy for company-wide talent acquisition initiatives that align with NPR’s strategic mission and core values. We achieved this by re-imagining and revamping the NPR recruiting model from a reactive transactional approach that was based on job postings, to a progressive proactive model leveraging social media, recruitment marketing, brand advocates, and employment branding.
