Colleen Deng's Summary

Hello! My name is Colleen and I'm a graphic designer from Brooklyn, NY. I'm blessed to say that I truly love what I do - I'm a creative problem solver. As a creative who loves working alongside passionate individuals, the best part about my job is being able to make ideas become a reality through design.

I'm currently a Designer at Columbia University, for the Office of Alumni & Development. I work with an amazing team to create print & digital communication pieces that informs the Columbia community of upcoming events and opportunities to give back. I also provide support to our 20 schools and Global Centers. One of my biggest projects this year includes Giving Day, an 24-hour fundraising effort, which encourages the Columbia's alumni, friends and students to give back, connect and join in on live events. Last year, our team helped raise over $6.8 Million to support various schools and programs through the use of the website (http://givingday.columbia.edu/) and social media.

In my previous position as the Art Director for the New York Knicks, I launched several season identities and created marketing collateral, which included packaging, websites, billboards and tickets. I also founded a NYC startup called OMG Foodie, an online guide to restaurants around NY. While working on OMG Foodie, I created a fully-responsive design with the web team, doubled as a photographer/videographer and had the pleasure to collaborate with 50+ local businesses. Some of the other brands I've worked with also include The NYC Food Truck Association, The New York Liberty, The New York Rangers, The Nom Nom Truck and The University of Michigan.

When I'm not designing I'm constantly looking for other creative outlets. My other passions include photography, letterpressing, exploring new eateries and collaborating with local businesses.
