Brady Paselk's Summary

I've been working with home, auto, and business insurance since 2009, and I love it. Hometown Insurance is the Agency I write with and it is located inside the SpiritBank Event Center where I was formerly the Box Office Manager. Primarily we write insurance in Tulsa, Oklahoma and the surrounding area, although we have expanded to Texas in 2012 and have an office in California as well. Personally, I try to stay flexible with meeting times and locations and am willing to meet my clients at their home, work, or at a random coffee shop or gas station. Many people have trouble knowing exactly what information to give us for a quote, so it is not uncommon for me to help sift through a client's stack of insurance papers to find the appropriate documents.
In the past, I had always struggled to understand my own insurance polices. Finding an agent that I could trust was also a big struggle. Actually, finding an agent that didn't seem sleazy was hard enough. Because of this, I strive to be both trustworthy and reputable. It is not easy to earn respect in the insurance industry because everyone expects you to be less than fair. But that is what I have set out to be: fair, kind, reputable and trustworthy. We might not always have the best prices, but you can rest assured knowing that we have been straight forward and honest with you. We will take the time to explain the ins and outs of your policy with you, and strive to earn your trust.

Specialties: Insurance Specialties: Auto Insurance, Home Insurance, Business Insurance and more in Tulsa, Ok and the surrounding areas. I am able to quote with 15+ different companies which almost always ensures a better rate than what you are paying currently.

Former Box Office Specialties: Building shows in the ticketing system, creating content and maintaining the SpiritBank Event Center website, and, of course, selling tickets.
