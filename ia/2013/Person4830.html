Allen Burt's Summary

Allen is the founder of Offmap.com and managing director of BlueStout.com.

Offmap.com is a next-generation travel company that creates spectacular adventure vacations priced 70% lower than traditional tour companies by working with local boutique outfitters and using custom guidebooks on your phone and iPad.

BlueStout.com is a boutique digital agency that crafts stunning designs and user experiences (UI/UX) for compelling brands and startups.

In a prior life, Allen ran strategic financial and economic analysis projects for large technology and media clients like the Chicago Tribune, Bayer AG, Pfizer and Sprint, and helped start and build a boutique IP consulting division for a Chicago-based valuation firm and investment bank.

Allen has significant international experience including international business and political science studies in Luxembourg, the BUNAC work program in London, starting and managing a literacy non-profit campaign in Laos, and extensive travel throughout Europe, Central America, South America, Northern Africa, and East Asia.

Allen writes on the topics of entrepreneurship, social enterprise, economics, travel and their intersection with mobile/internet technology on his blog, AllenBurt.com, and for such publications as the Matador Network and Huffington Post. He and his company have been featured in various media outlets including WIRED, The Matador Network, Huffington Post Travel, Gadling, Wend Magazine, the San Francisco Chronicle and the Los Angeles Times.

Allen holds a BS in Business Administration and Economics from Miami University (OH).
