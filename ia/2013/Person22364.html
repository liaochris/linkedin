Charmelle Mills, GRI's Summary

I am a full time real estate professional in the "The Mother Lode" covering Tuolumne and Calaveras Counties since 1994. Representing Buyer’s and Seller’s in all facets of real estate. Specializing in new construction and land development, residential homes... and most recently bank owned and short-sale properties.

I love and enjoy helping my clients reach their real estate goals -- whether it’s for your primary or perhaps secondary residence, or purely for investment purposes. Please allow me to answer all of your questions and help you feel comfortable about your real estate decisions. Working together, we can achieve your real estate goals successfully.

I also look forward to connecting with other professionals to build a Business to Business Directory leading to a strong referral network.
