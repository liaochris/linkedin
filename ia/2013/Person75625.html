Kimberly Hayworth's Summary

Kimberly Hayworth is an Instructional Designer for blended and online learning in the Office of the Vice Provost for Online Learning at Stanford University. She consults with faculty from all disciplines on strategies for integrating technology into teaching, learning and research and helps faculty to plan their online and blended courses to maximize student learning. Kimberly specializes in instructional design, online learning and digital video.

Specialties

Multimedia project management and consulting, curriculum development, video production, Final Cut Pro, iLife apps, DVD authoring, podcasting, audio editing
