James Mai's Summary

I'm a lifelong student of business and have dedicated my career to understanding and executing profitable strategies. My career spans nearly 15 years and has been focused on strategic growth initiatives, which began in executive search and internal headhunting. Over the years, I began building upon my core competencies to add to my "arsenal" which was whatever could help facilitate aggressive growth strategy...headhunting from competitors to build, building and mentoring sales teams, developing strategic marketing direction, online traffic generation, operations management, and overall revenue strategy.

I've enjoyed the challenge and the "fight" of bootstrapping and building from concept, it's was rewarding, These days what drives me is seeing driven people, hearing their stories and watching them bring their dreams to fruition. If I can be a part of that process, it's well worth it.

The journey has been awesome; started a few ventures, closed a few ventures. I've lost everything a few times and survived three market crashes, completed restructuring and eventually turned around my current firm and taking the company into international markets in 2008. Eventually we began equity deals with established partner firms building upon their infrastructure, profitable business units. In 2011 assumed role of Managing Partner @ Bristol Bates and I personally manage 3 engagements/operating ventures.

I've been fortunate enough to have joined forces with some dynamic entrepreneurs and founders who have become not only partners, but also friends - some of which I have been with for over 8 years. I'm very open to connecting with others - everyone has a story and I enjoy hearing and learning about people in general, let's connect!
