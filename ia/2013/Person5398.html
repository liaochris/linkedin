Amanda Montgomery's Summary

Since recently earning my Master's Degree in Emerging Media & Communications from the University of Texas-Dallas (December 2012); I have been actively seeking new opportunities to work with organizations that are committed to producing outstanding brand experiences within various emerging media networks alongside their clients and colleagues.

My background includes international work experience with numerous agencies and a variety of clients regarding how to best employ social media to help resolve various challenges concerning an organization's brand identity, market research, media relations, sales strategies, customer service, business development, etc.

Prior to finishing my graduate work last Fall, I earned my Bachelor's Degree from the University of Alabama (May 2009) with a concentration in Advertising, Minor in Creative Writing & an additional certification in Global Studies.

It is imperative for the modern communications practitioner to seize every possible opportunity that encourages and challenges their advertising, marketing and media skills.
