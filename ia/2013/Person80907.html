Mark Jenkinson's Summary

I am a graphic design professional with expertise in many fields of graphic design.

For the past 16 years I have been helping my clients to go gourmet, pump gas, imbibe duty free, recognize donors, direct visitors, educate palates, capture memories, go green, play the odds, send season’s greetings, eat out and procrastinate going the gym. (there’s always tomorrow, yes?).

I bring a wealth of experience with an understanding of environmental and commercial identity design, retail design strategies, multi-channel branding, brand asset management, wayfinding & signage, exhibit and interpretive graphics, donor recognition programs, design for print and construction techniques of environmental brand programs.

My client experience is as diverse as the spectrum of disciplines I work in and range from the small to the large (and everyone in-between); from architects and design firms, to institutions and corporations, to towns and cities, to independent organizations and individuals.

Owner and Creative Director

Limepickle

February 2004 – Present (9 years 9 months) London & Philadelphia

Limepickle was built around the premise of providing big agency expertise (which we have) in a smaller, more accessible and approachable format. We choose to work with a select group of like-minded partners with whom we can create exciting, conceptual work, ensuring that the client’s specific expectations are not only met, but exceeded by our solutions.

Eliel Sarrinen's quote: “Always design a thing by considering it in its next larger context - a chair in a room, a room in a house, a house in an environment, an environment in a city plan.” is something we always strive to achieve. We don’t just look at what we are asked to design. We look at the usage, the environment, the delivery method, and the relationship (and impact) it will have with your existing communications. This helps us tailor specific and suitable responses. We are currently busy working on projects with the Philadelphia Union & Sons of Ben, as well as providing pro-bono services for several local charities.
