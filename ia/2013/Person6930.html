Writer, media maker, and innovation evangelist at the intersection of tech, business, culture, and public policy.

I delight in connecting ideas and people at gb.tc (formerly known as the Greater Baltimore Tech Council). Visit http://gb.tc to get a sense of the work we're doing to cultivate a community of innovators in Baltimore.

Andrew Hazlett's Projects

  • Create Baltimore

    • January 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Andrew Hazlett, Sarah Jones, Tracy C. Gold, Elizabeth Eadie, David Troy, Scott Burkholder, Lindsey Davis, Dennis McIver

    In early 2010 a group of Baltimore-based changemakers gathered under the premise of collaboration. They undertook an alternative approach to the traditional conference. Instead of one-way presentations by predetermined speakers, the participants themselves determined the most pressing area concerns and community needs on the day of the event. The results were beyond all expectations: cross collaboration resulted in new ideas, professional silos were broken down, and conversations were honest, informed, and diverse. Three years later, CreateBaltimore has made a name for itself as one of the premiere events for Baltimore innovators, young minds, and professionals willing to challenge themselves.
