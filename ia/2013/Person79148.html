Laurent François' Summary

I'm the owner & managing director of French Ideas, a London-based boutique digital agency, specialized in Social Media and Digital Activation. We're part of a bigger collective, RE-UP, including veterans of advertising industry, creativity marketers and artists.

What makes us different:
- a business approach of digital marketing & Social Media: we sell ideas that are thought to reach critical masses of users (for brands, start-ups, social commerce briefs etc.)
- we're from diverse cultural backgrounds, which lead to more untypical ideas and solutions
- hybrid positioning between strategic planning & creativity: we sell packaged ideas, from ideation to the relevant tools/networks/media

We work closely for early-stage start-ups and innovative projects, but also for bigger organizations & companies. I'm also interested in fashion and photography.

Before launching French Ideas, I launched a business unit within Roularta Group, (a media group which owns L'Express in France), called SocialERS, dedicated to implementing a new value chain and new sources of revenue for online magazines & publishers.

I was the first Head of 360° Digital Influence for Ogilvy in Europe (now "Social@Ogilvy"). With my team, we've been implementing Social Media strategies for clients like Tom of Finland, Vodafone, Grazia, IBM, Nestlé, etc. The goals were very diverse, from Social Media monitoring studies (using for instance Radian6) to live digital activation.

I'm also teaching at ESCP Europe (Master's in Marketing & Creativity) and writing for Social Media Today (I'm a proud member of the advisory panel). I regularly give conferences on Social Media & innovation (Social Media Week, Digital Shoreditch etc.).

In the meantime, I blog on fashion for Hit Bag & Le Boulevardier, which are great hubs for creative projects.
