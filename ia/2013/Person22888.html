Chip Mitchell's Summary

My mission is to enable business and organizational leaders to achieve their highest purpose in the marketplace by providing experienced insight,creative solutions and best practice training.

I engage business professionals to examine their personal values and goals first, then build out their business plans and strategies on this foundation.

My philosophy of business consulting is to help clients implement their own ideas by filling in knowledge gaps and providing critical feedback.

My background includes strategic planning, business coaching, non-profit board leadership, marketing, team building, quality improvement, cost measurement, disaster planning, technology planning, sales, and labor program administration.

Specialties

I specialize in providing best fit management solutions and coaching for business, non-profit, and government organizations.
