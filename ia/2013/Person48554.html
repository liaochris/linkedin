Tony Lee's Summary

GIGER MEDIA (pronounced 'guy-ger') is a creative image business providing graphic design, website design, illustration, print, and photography services, established back in 1995 based in the UK in Lincolnshire.

Passionate about design, our aim is to bring all your creative needs together, on the web, in digital format, and in print. Although we are based in Lincolnshire our client base covers the whole of the UK, and even overseas.

Since 1995 GIGER MEDIA has worked with people, businesses and companies of all shapes and sizes: from sole traders and self-employed, to limited companies, local, national, and even international. Small scale static websites to larger on-line projects with database driven websites, and e-commerce solutions, start-up business marketing materials to large scale print projects for exhibition and display, and data CD/DVD production.

Unlike many other web design companies we are a full member and Registrar (not reseller) of Nominet UK (the governing authority in the United Kingdom for .uk domain registrations), and we are also an Accredited Registrar with CentralNIC.

We are members of the DBA (Design Business Association), the FSB (Federation of Small Businesses), and are a SagePay Partner.

And unlike many other creative studios we have our own 'client only' hosting and mail servers – we do not use reseller programs or shared servers for our clients sites and email.

Specialities: Essentially, we bring all your creative needs together - graphic design and illustration, print and output, display and promotional materials (pop up banners, large scale vinyls etc..), CD / DVD print mastering and duplication, people and product photography, website design and development, site hosting and email, domain registration and management.
