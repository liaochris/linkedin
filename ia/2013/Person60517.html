Jeanette Pearson's Summary

Jeanette began studying yoga at the age of seventeen. She became a dedicated practitioner of yoga in Santa Cruz, California, where she underwent a teacher’s training process beginning in 2004 and subsequently received her yoga certification in Warrior Yoga in 2006, studying under the instruction of founder instructor, Sensei Jeremy Corbell, and his top student, Sensei Kevin Snorf. In a commitment to her continued growth as a yoga instructor and practitioner, she underwent a 200-hour teacher training process through YogaWorks under the guidance and instruction of Stacy McCarthy and Jessica Seabern.

Throughout her yoga journey, Jeanette has had the opportunity to study multiple styles of yoga including Hatha, Warrior, Ashtanga, Anusara, Iyengar, Vinyasa, Synergy, and Bikram yoga in various settings including - Northern and Southern California, Turkey, and Thailand. She has had the privilege of studying under and being influenced by several extraordinary instructors including Michael Fukumura, Reverend Tom Kelly, Jessica Seabern, Stacy McCarthy, Peri Ness-DeFay, Sensei Jeremy Corbell, and Sensei Kevin Snorf.

In addition to guiding yoga classes, Jeanette is a life coach which has gifted her with the tools to support transformation both on and off the mat! She is also fortunate to be the Manager of the Haute Yoga studio in Solana Beach. She feels honored and blessed to be a part of such an incredible community of yogis!

Outside of the yoga studio, Jeanette’s interests include dancing, decadent meals, rock climbing, backpacking, hoop dance, and sharing boundless laughter and joy with the world!

Jeanette’s classes offer a dynamic flow series that focus on cultivating presence, consciousness of breath, and joy.

Specialties

finding balance and integration, personal coaching, wellness coaching, relationship coaching, and becoming an authentic leader.
