Joseph Bou-Younes' Summary

Passionate product leader with a love of design. 10 years of experience successfully creating and launching complex web-based and mobile products. Combined quantitative, qualitative, and design intuition to identify new features for a mature product; re-started growth, and increased volume by 15% Y/Y. Anticipates implementation problems, makes product trade-offs, negotiates solutions, and launches products that succeed.

Rebuilt and re-launched personalized home page with 20MM users. Increased new user conversion rate by 27% while protecting existing user base. Thrives on motivating teams of technical, business, and design professionals to successfully meet new challenges.

Specialties

Leading the design, development, implementation, launch and marketing of web and mobile products. Quantitative analysis of product performance, A/B testing, and site optimization. Development and delivery of product requirements. Agile software development. Online advertising. Engineering background; strong understanding of the design and implementation of web-based systems.
