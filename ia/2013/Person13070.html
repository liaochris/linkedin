Benjamin Spear's Summary

After more than a decade working in design and branding, I know that logos and websites aren't enough—success comes from understanding how business, market, and audience converge. I help my clients harness the power of holistic thinking to lead and win.

I offer analysis, strategy, and brand design for clients in the entrepreneurship, education, media, and nonprofit sectors. I create successful outcomes through deep understanding, thoughtful consideration, and an innate sensitivity to cultural trends and emerging values.
