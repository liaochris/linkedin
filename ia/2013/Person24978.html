Cindy Marks' Summary

Award winning interactive magazine design! CatStone Press has been specializing in digital publishing for over 3 years (we are old-timers) and consistently receive glowing feedback from both clients and readers. We stay on top of the latest developments in a fast changing industry and bring the best in technology along with focused personal service to every project.
