Yvon Lantaigne's Summary

I am a dynamic & versatile Graphics Professional with extensive experience in print, packaging, environmental and dimensional signage projects. My experience is coupled with strong organizational & management skills as well as a technical proficiency in numerous related computer applications, peripherals & network protocols.

My experience began 36 + years ago, which by no means makes me a historian, but does recall a time when simplicity was a virtue and craftsmanship was a mark of excellence. From the beginning, my focus and first love was typography and paper grades. How words appear on a page, is, I still believe, one of the most important elements of design and strongly affects the impact of a message. Type, used well, conveys more than the words can ever say – it expresses ideas and images that are bigger than themselves. This concept still applies today, when one considers how recent advances in web typography enable designers to communicate with their audiences at superior levels.

From my first job as a typographer, hand-setting lead type and assembling forms for the press in a small commercial letterpress shop, to performing comprehensive type mark-up and creating custom headlines by various means during the phototype years, to later harnessing the sophisticated power of computer technology, I believe I have developed a very good eye for typography.

Perhaps it is no surprise that some 30 years after my initial training, I would come full circle and get back to designing the analog way, hand-setting type in lead and wood in my letterpress print shop. I've since taken a special interest in type design and the history of the written word, gaining added knowledge and appreciation for the art and craft of printing and papermaking.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Print Production, Letterpress Printing, Digital Photo Post-Production; Image Manipulation & Compositing, Signage; Environmental Graphics, Wayfinding, Exhibitry, Packaging.

Yvon Lantaigne's Experience

Proprietor, Custom Letterpress Printing

Fox & Found Press

August 2007 – Present (6 years 2 months)

Some 30 years after my first industry job hand-setting lead type and assembling forms for the presses in a small commercial letterpress print shop, I've now assembled a sizable collection of antique & vintage letterpress equipment and type into a comprehensive printing studio.

Fox & Found Press is dedicated to providing custom letterpress printing for discerning designers, creative agencies, artists and individuals, while creating personal original works in limited editions. This year, I've started attending Craft Fairs & Farmers Markets in an effort to sell some of my printed works as well as handmade books, journals & sketchbooks.

The printshop now boasts 5 printing presses:
– 3 flat-bed cylinder presses; a beautifully restored Vandercook SP15, a sturdy Challenge 15KP, a rare Vandercook 05 proof press
– 1 impressively well preserved 1905 8"x12" C&P Oldstyle platen press
– and the recent acquisition of a Morgan Line-O-Scribe 14"x11" Sign Machine

The platen is suitable for cards & stationery, while the proofing presses will handle sheet sizes up to 15"x28" for broadsides & posters. The Morgan is handy for proofing linoleum & wood carvings, and will be ideal for giving demos.

The type collection now exceeds 360 fonts, 65 of which are the larger woodtype variety. I have galleys & cases full of decorative ornaments & borders along with well over 450 printer’s blocks. A sizable set of wood and linoleum carving tools is used to add custom hand carved touches to select projects.
