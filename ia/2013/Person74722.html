Kevin Owens' Summary

I am able to develop long-term scenarios, define the main customer issues, identify possible solutions, design the solution, define the requirements, manage the project and launch the product. For the past 13 years I have been progressively building my skills in strategy, product management, product marketing, project management, design and implementation. These skills have been built through a combination of direct experience, graduate work at USC and self-study.

My direct experience has included work in multiple industry verticals throughout the US, along with significant international work. I understand the challenges of working with distributed teams and launching products simultaneously in multiple countries and regions. In addition to multiple industry verticals I have worked with B2B(Business to Business), B2C(Business to Consumer), SaaS(Software as a Service), eCommerce, Social Media and Interactive solutions.

I know what it takes to successfully launch products and services and am continuously looking for ways to improve the process and my own knowledge. I am a consummate student of strategy, product management and design, along with organizational behavior. My passion is in creating truly innovative products that are both profitable and easy to use.
