Ken Browne's Summary

Ken Browne Productions, produces film/video products for business, institutions, non-profits, and government agencies. Since 1986, we have produced award-winning productions, for broadcast TV, film festivals, professional meetings, gala events, and websites. Our work has generated significant positive results for our clients. Projects are detailed at www.kbprods.com.
