John Nicodemus' Summary

A very broad background in all aspects of management and leadership development for an eclectic group of companies from Fortune 10 to start-ups. Over the years I have been on both sides of the employment situation. I have hired many people for a wide variety of positions and have suffered through the many frustrations associated with seeking employment.This along with mentoring several job clubs for unemployed or under-employed people has led me to my passion. In mentoring job seekers, I crystallized my thinking on the subject to the point that I have also written a book on resume writing and job search techniques titled "So Do You Want a Job, Or What? Dirty Secrets of Resume Writing and Job Hunting".

Selected Accomplishments include:

• Led four different financially-troubled companies back to solid profitability by:
o Establishing logical and efficient organizational structures
o Building effective management teams
o Improving manufacturing efficiencies to impact both top and bottom line results
• Increased production efficiency resulting in gains of 125% and a gross margin increase from -8% to 15%.
• Directed the shift of 90% of a Company’s production offshore increasing the gross margin from 7% to 21%.
• Reorganized the operations staff of a non-profit company allowing it to pay down more than $5 mm of debt
• Identified and corrected quality defect in the formulation for an extrusion product, resulting in an immediate 80% improvement in quality resulting in a cost savings of $1.5 million.


I help individuals get a GRIP on their search for employment by providing unique assistance from writing a resume that WILL get noticed, to setting up a job search campaign, to keeping job seekers positive throughout the search.

I continue to do individual and business coaching as well.

Specialties: All aspects of the job search, outplacement services, and individual coaching on job search issues.
