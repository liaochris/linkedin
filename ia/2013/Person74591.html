Ginny Kenyon's Summary

I have been in Community based services since 1975, starting with 10 years in the field as a visiting nurse. I earned my Masters in Nursing in 1986 and was hired out of school as the Associate Director of Community Health in a County Health Department. After 5 years I returned to Home Health as the Director of a small for profit branch and eventually became a regional director for the same company. After leaving there I was hired by a group of investors to start up a private pay agency. I left there in 1999 and continued my career in home health and community based services as a national consultant. I have my own firm and continue in the consulting role on a national basis.

Specialties

home health, hospice, private pay and assisted living
