Dustin Ellison's Summary

Dustin is an expert in implementing complex elearning initiatives for a broad range of corporations such as Target, Nike, Ameriprise, 3M, Boston Scientific, Lifetouch, Deckers, Pentair, AmericInn, and Hazelden Foundation. His passion lies in delivering learning innovations that better connect team members to the knowledge and skills they need to excel in their positions.

His experience includes designing engaging elearning courseware and blended learning, managing international development teams, and delivering corporate-wide instructional design best practices. Dustin also specializes in interactive elearning development, such as custom elearning courses, rapid development templates, high-end learning simulations, and rich internet applications. He is known for his energy, creative solutions, and the ability to simplify complex concepts on any subject.
