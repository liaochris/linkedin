Jeff Fagin's Summary

I'm a seasoned business executive with over 3 decades of Fortune 500 management experience working with companies like Xerox, Honda, and Bank One. I've also run and grown multimillion dollar nonprofit and for profit organizations Prior to starting my career I earned an Honors undergraduate degree in business and a Masters of Business Administration. I also earned my CPA and have my insurance license. A passionate financial evangelist, I’ve studied business, management, entrepreneurship, marketing, investments and personal development for over 40 years, and I teach these principles, strategies and tactics to my students every week at my Phoenix 2% Club.

I set an intention to surrounded myself with bright, successful people, smarter than me, and it's what I continue to do this day. The 2 qualities I look for in every individual I work with is the ability to do the job, and the ability to get along with people.

In the past 7 years I’ve built a highly successful financial services business and have hundreds of clients who look to me for financial guidance. I understand their needs and educate them on their options so they're clear on their objectives.

Today I focus all of my efforts to the building of Cloud Income Properties. With e-commerce on a global basis now an accepted fact of life ... owning and controlling cash flowing e-commerce businesses is a strategy today that can create financial freedom quickly.

If your goal is monthly cash flow and financial freedom I invite you to contact me and see how we might be able to work together.
