Di-Anne Di Re's Summary

Di-Anne Di Re, founder of Media Resources Enterprise, is a business communication author, speaker, coach and trainer in the areas of branding, public relations, marketing and social media strategy. She is the National Association of Professional Women (NAPW) Public Relations woman of the year 2012/2013, the recipient of the WOAMTEC Community Leadership Award, Mrs. Corporate America 2012 and founder of Global Partners Resources Foundation. Di-Anne has the expertise: professionalism, and people skills to help businesses communicate to the public. She obtained a bachelor’s degree in Communication with concentration in Public Relations, and Marketing, graduating Magna Cum Laude from Ashford University; she has a professional broadcasting certification for Radio/TV from the Connecticut School of Broadcasting, and is a news contributor for WOFL Fox 35 TV Orlando, FL. She assists communication campaigns to promote her client's interests in the areas of public relations, marketing, social media, and video production.

Di-Anne's passion is to empower businesses and individuals to achieve best results through public communication.

To arrange speaking engagements, book signings, tour events, interviews, online chats, telephone chats with the author contact 407-579-9903, info@diannedire.com or visit her online at diannedire.com.
