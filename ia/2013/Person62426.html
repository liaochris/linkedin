Jeremy Mansfield's Summary

Brand Aid Design Co. is directed by Founder and Lead User Experience Director, Jeremy Mansfield. Jeremy is an award-winning industry veteran who has designed and lead creative teams across dozens of sectors including Furniture, Real Estate, Architecture, Health Care, Technology, Auto, Retail, Trade, Music and Publication. Jeremy works closely with each of Brand Aid's clients to define campaign goals, strategic direction, and to determine which of our consultants' or recommended vendors' expertise is right for each individual project.

Prior to founding Brand Aid Design Co., LLC, Jeremy was the E-Business Art Director for one of the largest furniture companies in the industry and his creative team was responsible for the creation, maintenance and marketing of over 15 retail websites. Jeremy's experience with literally dozens of different clients in a wide variety of industries allows him to quickly assess an organization's strategic branding needs. In addition to his knowledge of branding, Jeremy also relies on more than 15 years of experience in web design & development.

Specialties: Usability, Information Architecture, Responsive Web Design, Mobile Apps, HTML5, CSS3, Brand Identity, Print Design, Art Direction, Creative Direction, Project Management, E-Commerce
