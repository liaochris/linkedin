Jonathan Jay Holley's Summary

I’m a licensed architect with seven years of experience spanning a variety of civic and institutional project types including recreation facilities, higher education classroom buildings, early education centers, and a public library.

I offer a strong design eye, refined detailing ability, expert production and Revit model management skills, and the ability to effectively communicate ideas in just about every medium: the written word, BIM models, Kerkythea renderings, computer code, Sharpie on trace -- you name it.

I enjoy working with colleagues who share my dedication to progressive building design and commitment to backing up beautiful renderings with tight construction documents.

Specialties: Design, Detailing, Communication, Revit
