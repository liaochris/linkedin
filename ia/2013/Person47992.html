George Righter's Summary

RighterTrack / AIP, Inc. was founded in 1998 by George Righter who holds a marketing degree from Penn State University, and an MBA from Rider University. George has an extensive background with label systems and labels. He managed a label manufacturing plant in the 1980’s, and has been offering bar coding applications and custom solutions including tamper evident security since the early 1990’s.

The RighterTrack product line includes label printers, label software, bar code scanners, hand held data collection devices, bar coding software, and custom labels. A large part of the specialty label offering includes an array of tamper evident and security label products and solutions.

RighterTrack's commitment to providing personal, top-notch service, counsel and long-term mutually beneficial relationships with its customers and suppliers has enabled steady growth since the company’s inception.
