Amélie Alleman (+1800)'s Summary

Akros Solutions (Greek; meaning summit, edge, peak, pinnacle ) is an exciting new Belgian recruitment agency focusing on the permanent and freelance ICT market

Akros was created in 2009 by experienced recruiters who understand their business and whose professional approach will bring long term solutions to their partners.

The financial crisis has highlighted the need to bring a qualitative and focused service to our clients allowing us to return to the basic premise of a recruitment agency, that is to say, placing the right candidate to the right job saving valuable time and money to our clients and candidates.

We achieve this by:
• having a deep and thorough understanding of the market and it’s technologies
• a network of qualified candidates
• an in-depth qualification process developed by us
• a professional follow up and communication at each stage of the recruitment process
• our flexibility and proactive approach
• one single point of contact with a company director
• offering the “akros” solution

As our name suggests our aim is to help our clients and candidates reach the very top of their business and career.

Spécialités : IT permanent recruitment

September 2009 – Present (4 years)

Akros solutions is active on the BeNeLux Market and has for target to bring a personalised and qualitative ICT recruitment solution to their clients.

Our services and added value :

- IT Permanent and Freelance recruitment, based on a qualitative approach via one single point of contact specialised in the area of your company.

- Full F2F candidate report and reference check sent to the client before the 1st interview in order to only organize meetings with candidates that match perfectly your requirement.

- Screening of the technical skills as well the personality to be sure there is a match with the company culture

- Information and advice on the market, rates, skills, ...

- Help on key points concerning recruitment processes and candidate issues. For example : How to select your PSL ? How to improve the retention of the candidates ? How to integrate properly your freelance candidates within the company ? ...

We are not a "CV box" ... our aim is to become your long term ICT recruitment partner.
