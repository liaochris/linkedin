Duane McCalla's Summary

Everyone wants a great deal on a real estate, especially now when it's the time to buy! The real estate I sell are in the pre-foreclosure stage and I look for pre-approved buyers who are ready to close on a house after the bank accepts a discount on the mortgage debt owed. I specialize in pre-foreclosure mortgage debt negotiations, commonly known as a Short Sale. When you work with me, you will get a property below market value and with equity.
