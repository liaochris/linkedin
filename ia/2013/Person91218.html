Director, Information Technology Business Development

London Economic Development Corporation

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Education Management industry

February 2013 – Present (8 months)

The London Economic Development Corporation (LEDC) is a partnership between the City of London and the private sector. Our goal is to attract new investments and expand existing investments in our community. Led by a Board of distinguished business, educational and political leaders, the LEDC will assist you with all your location and investment decisions. We work one-on-one with businesses, to facilitate the process of locating in London.

The LEDC is committed to improving the economic well-being of Londoners through the growth and expansion of local businesses and the attraction of new businesses to London. We aim to add to the strengths of the London business environment making our city an attractive place to live, work and operate a business. This includes: business expansion and retention, advice and assistance, along with the attraction of new business.

London has more than 300+ Information Technology companies with over 8000+ employees focused in the development of a diverse range of products and services: automation and enterprise management software; financial, mortgage, financial, and asset management software; interactive cable technologies; digital interactive gaming; web development; and digital interactive media firms.

I am here to help, support, serve these amazing tech companies doing so much good, great, and wonderful in our City of London Ontario. I am here to assist and support any growth or expansion initiatives in either space, location, or talent. I work closely with the area universities and colleges to support and facilitate the ongoing growth of such companies utilizing our wonderful student population in London. Also, I build and foster relationships nationally and internationally to identify both expansion and investment opportunities for London.
