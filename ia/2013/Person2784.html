Allison Jochim's Summary

Graphic designer and art director with a passion for creative type and work that is interactive and engaging.

In my five years of in-house and freelance experience, I've worked alongside some amazing creatives - from photographers and stylists, to industrial designers, to graphic designers and product developers. I love working with a skilled team to produce innovative, beautiful design that moves a company forward, and truly engages its audience.

I also love working one-on-one with small business owners, to produce inspiring, effective projects that solve problems and surpass expectations!

Making excellent design with excellent people - what could be better?

Specialties

typography, innovative print work
