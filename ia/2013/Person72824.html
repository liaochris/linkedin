Kathleen Fealy's Summary

As a search strategist specializing in organic search and a HFI-Certified Usability Analyst, I work with clients to develop or refine their online marketing strategies, implement holistic search engine optimization (SEO) and balance the needs and motivations of their customers to improve their web traffic and conversion rates. Other services include SEO and usability reviews providing a plan of action, training in-house teams on the basics of SEO and usability and conducting site clinics to teach local business owners how to improve their traffic and conversions.

Speaker at SES Conferences and IMC NY on SEO and usability and published in various industry magazines. Currently Co-Chair of the Education Committee of Search Engine Marketing Professionals Organization (SEMPO).

Looking to work with businesses or in-house teams on their organic search and usability projects.

Specialties

Search engine optimization (SEO), local search, social media, SEO and usability audits, conversion page testing, landing page optimization, persona development and copyediting. In-house training is available for SEO and informal usability testing. Available for in-house contract work to help with project overflows.
