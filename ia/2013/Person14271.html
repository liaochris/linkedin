William Feeney's Summary

Bill is an accomplished Graphic Designer and Art Director specializing in the development of communication and marketing concepts for corporate, institutional, and healthcare organizations. He provides clients with the highest level of effective, clear, creative solutions to meet their marketing communication and product development needs. Through his strong hands-on design and conceptual problem solving abilities, Bill has collaborated with marketing and creative team members to develop successful solutions to their marketing objectives. Bill has successfully managed and coordinated design projects from concept to completion, consistently meeting deadlines and budget requirements. His exceptional collaborative and interpersonal skills have served him well in the management and direction of staff/team members.
