Eric Edelstein's Summary

Eric Edelstein is a Financial Analyst turned Serial Tech Entrepreneur, and occasional Angel Investor, based in Cape Town, South Africa. *

He has co-founded many of South Africa's largest Internet marketing & product companies such as the incuBeta group (Click2Customers, TrafficSynergy, & Yola), Springleap & evly.com.

He is currently CEO of social payments company, ZunguZ.com

Eric is passionate about all types of online marketing, but especially search (PPC + SEO), affiliate and social media marketing.

He speaks frequently on a wide range of topics** at conferences & events***, and writes for business & technology media.

Eric was included in Mail & Guardian's "Top 200 young people to have lunch with", and Old Mutual's "Top 36 South African young entrepreneurs".
