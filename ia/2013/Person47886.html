George Everitt's Summary

Applied Relevance builds Taxonomy Management tools for Enterprises. Our software integrates with SharePoint 2007 and 2010, Oracle SES and UCM, Apache Solr, Coveo Enterprise Search, MarkLogic Server and many other enterprise search and enterprise content management systems.

Our unique approach is to combine taxonomy management tools, fast document tagging and faceted navigation with foundation taxonomies from our partner WAND, Inc. The result is extraordinary relevance for end users.

Specialties

search, classification, categorization, parametric selection, profiling and alerting, query processing, search optimization and integration, facets, faceted search, faceted navigation, datafacet, taxonomy management, semantic web
