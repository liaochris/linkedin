Laura Wheeler's Summary

Active since 1999 in developing website solutions for small and micro-businesses. Highly experienced in all areas of website operations and construction for startups and very small businesses. We have developed new strategies and guidelines for working with small businesses, and now train aspiring webmasters to offer high value services at low prices, and to make a healthy profit while doing so.

Watch for our new training division to come to your city! Website Success Seminars launched in February of 2010, and now travels to cities across the US to deliver Training Workshops for Website Owners and Aspiring Webmasters.

Specialties

microbusiness web solutions, dynamic websites, shopping cart, ecommerce, website sustainability documentation, technical instruction writing for microbusinesses, in-house microbusiness website design training, recession survival consulting, site monetization consulting, search engine optimization, graphic design, copywriting, web marketing, and web business startup consulting

Laura Wheeler's Experience

Co-Owner

Firelight Business Enterprises, Inc

February 2007 – Present (6 years 8 months)

Have operated our own business since 1999, but incorporated in Feb, 2007. Currently I am the chief technician, planner, and project manager, CEO, and President. I wear a lot of hats in our very small business, and share many of them with my husband, who works along-side me.

We serve the microbusiness and very small business population. This is our chosen clientele, they are our favorite people to work with.

I am personally skilled at web design, dynamic site install and configuration, site maintenance, graphic design, copy writing, search engine optimization, budget web marketing, and in site sustainability issues and documentation of site policy and procedure.

Sometimes it is very hard to explain why what we do is so different. People with small businesses really don't have any idea of what we really mean until they start to work with us, and then they understand how much we do that they didn't know they needed to have done.

Web Development Enrichment Course Instructor

University of Wyoming Enrichment Program

September 2007 – May 2009 (1 year 9 months)

Teaching non-credit certification courses through the University Enrichment program. Includes writing and developing my own curriculum to teach students how to build a website, create quality content, promote the site, and maintain it long term. Courses have included building a CMSMS website, creating a Joomla website, Search Engine Optimization, Web Monetization, Site Security, and Web Marketing. UW offered the only Small Business Website Professional Certification Program in the nation, for which we have the privilege of teaching and creating the curriculum. Discontinued when UW shut down their entire Enrichment Department due to budget cuts.
