Laresa McIntyre, CMA, MBA's Summary

My passion has always been business and, specifically, accounting and finance. As a financial executive with over 20 years of experience, I have provided sound reporting, analysis and guidance to companies in a diversity of industries including manufacturing, event management, publishing, insurance and retail. In each step of my career, I have brought a heightened sense of what is needed to make a business successful, and have provided the leadership and drive necessary to make it happen. In addition to harnessing this expertise for my current employer, I share my thoughts through my blog, The Finance Compass, which you can access by visiting http://financecompass.wordpress.com.

I am a Certified Management Accountant (CMA) and have earned my Masters of Business Administration degree.

Specialties

Financial Analysis & Reporting ~ Budgeting & Forecasting ~ Strategic Planning ~ International Business ~ Integrating Acquisitions ~ Redesigning Business Processes ~ Operations Management ~ Project Management ~ Systems Implementation ~ Staff Management & Leadership
