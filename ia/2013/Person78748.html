Laura Creekmore's Summary

We work with organizations to create, license and manage web content to meet their business goals. Creek Content specializes in content strategy, day-to-day content management, and information architecture. Our team has extensive online community management experience, though we find that solutions for community management problems often direct us right back to questions about the content.

We work extensively in health care and other highly regulated and complex fields, offering consulting, training and speaking.

We'd love to talk to you about how we can improve your organization's web content strategy and process.

Specialties

Content strategy and marketing, information architecture, online community management, writing, editing.
