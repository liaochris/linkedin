Jorge Diaz's Summary

My experience includes 3D production and design for clients like Coca Cola, Walmart, Microsoft, JP Morgan, MasterCard, Barnes & Noble, Hershey's, and the US Navy. Working within animation, print, and design, I've had the distinct pleasure of collaborating on many different projects with mixed media resulting in TV commercials, sales pitches, e-learning courseware, brochures, and award winning marketing collateral that have redefined standards within my client's areas of industry.

Although creative problem solving is what brought me to work in digital arts, it is the collaborative effort and shared success of the teams I work with that keeps my passion for creative production going. I aim to work with teams of all sizes and levels of expertise, where the common goal is creating innovative and beautiful work.
