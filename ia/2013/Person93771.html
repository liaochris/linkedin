Matt Chandler's Summary

I started in real estate as an agent when I was 19 years old and a full time student. When I was 20 years old I started focusing on the investment side of real estate and started my company Midwest Wholesale Properties LLC. We focus on distressed sale properties such as REOs, Short Sales, probates, and helping homeowners avoid foreclosure.

Currently our company works on 50+ properties at a time around the Oklahoma City metro area. We have 4 full time employees that work at our home office in Edmond Oklahoma and then 2 other employees that fix and work on our homes.

Our goal in business is to help people out of stressful home selling situations and rehabilitate distressed homes to stabilize neighborhoods.

Specialties

real estate investing, foreclosure prevention, short sales, distressed note purchases, managing people, creative negotiating solutions where everyone wins
