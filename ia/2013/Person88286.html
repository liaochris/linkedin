Megan O'Connor's Summary

My goal is to aid in balancing the client's health through initiating communication, education and safe space to achieve peace, awareness and connection of mind, body and spirit for a therapeutic experience. I specialize in therapeutic deep tissue that is both relaxing and effective. It is a combination of Swedish strokes to warm up the tissue and deep tissue techniques to relieve soreness and tension in the muscles.My varied work experience and interests allows me to tailor the massage to each individual and their body work needs.
