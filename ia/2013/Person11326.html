Chris Lee's Summary

I enable companies to use Customer Relationship Management (CRM) technology to improve sales, run more effective marketing campaigns and provide better service by: centralizing customer and prospect information, customizing systems to hold the business's specific information and tuning those systems to support/enforce the company's business practices.

I focus on the client's needs to help select the right solution: usable, easy to learn and appropriate for the business. At CSI, we work with a variety of CRM vendors in order to ensure we implement solutions that best match customer needs.

We work with customers across the nation from large organizations such as NYSE, AIG and DMB all the way down to small, local businesses.

As a member of the professional services team, I perform business analysis, write technical specifications, tailor solutions, implement systems and train technical and end users. As a partner of the company, I am also involved in strategic business decisions that involve sales, marketing and operations. As a result, I understand my clients' perspective and needs regarding CRM.

Specialties

Customer Relationship Management (SalesLogix, ACT!, Infusionsoft, Salesforce.com, Microsoft Dynamics CRM), data conversion, eMarketing automation, virtualization, technical training, business process analysis.
