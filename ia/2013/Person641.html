Aaron Street's Summary

Aaron Street advises Minnesota technology startups. He is a multi-talented business and organizational leader with broad experience leading high-profile public and private projects in Minnesota. He has spent the past five years as an executive leader in operations and management of complex organizations, including university research institutes and technology and services startups. He also brings over ten years of leadership in state and national politics and public policy development.
