John Harper's Summary

Thanks for visiting this page.

My background is in producing and promoting events, both ticketed events and branded/sponsored private events. While I was finishing my undergrad at the University of Miami, I landed a job as assistant to Michael Farrell (a veteran booking agent), and helped him book the Rolling Stones in Latin America, Asia, and Australia/New Zealand. After that, Michael Cohl hired us on full time to run tours for The Who, David Gilmour, and Genesis. We were then brought on to Live Nation Artists (which did 360 deals with Madonna, U2, Shakira, Jay Z, and others), and then migrated to S2BN Entertainment. There, I worked with Mike Luba to executive produce and promote Yo Gabba Gabba Live, Super Why Live, and other tours.

Most recently, I launched my own company, CPCTY ("Capacity"). I presently consult for S2BN and AEG Live, and produce concert tours and special events for clients including Apple and Milk Studios / MADE Fashion Week. The most recent CPCTY event was a rave in Bushwick for the launch of the Moto X phone and the closing of MADE Fashion Week, which drew 2,000 people and tons of positive feedback.

I am an owner and executive producer of Blackout, an interactive/experiential theater event that has garnered significant popular, critical, and celebrity acclaim. The New York Times called it "the extreme theater event of the year."

When I'm not producing events, I build web applications with Ruby on Rails (a web development framework popularized by Twitter, Hulu, and LivingSocial, among others). Projects currently in production include a custom content management system powering his tour web sites and a ticket sales application for pre-sales and friends & family VIP orders.
