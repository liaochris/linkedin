John Villella's Summary

Hello, I'm John Villella, a NYS Licensed Home Inspector and owner of All Pro Home Inspection. All Pro Home Inspection is a multi-inspector firm serving Buffalo, NY and all the surrounding communities within Western New York. With over 20 plus years as a licensed general contractor I have brought a large amount of first hand knowledge regarding building systems and components into the home inspection industry. I have personally completed over 6000 property inspections with a customer base that includes buyers, sellers, homeowners, insurance companies, lending institutions, and governmental agencies. Every All Pro inspector is licensed in NYS, and has completed several months of being personally trained by myself and I'll stand behind every inspection our inspectors complete. I am building a home inspection business that is unlike any other in Western New York. You truly have to Experience the All Pro Difference...Where the Inspection is Just the Beginning.

Our Company Philosophy...

We give our customers the most personalized service possible. We want you to be an educated buyer and feel confident with your purchase decision from the moment you call to schedule your inspection and until you decide to sell again. We always treat our customers the same way we would want to be treated, with respect and concern for your time and hard earned money.

We understand that two of your most important assets are your family and your home. That is our motivation to educate you regarding the condition of your home, so you can enjoy both your new home and your family.
