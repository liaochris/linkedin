Jason Butcher's Summary

I’m a successful entrepreneur who has started, managed and operated more than two dozen international business ventures in Canada, the USA and the Caribbean over the past 15 years. As an owner and CEO with an ever-growing number of firms such as ROYALWOOD Capital Corp., Xoom Marketing and Raincity Marketing Group, I’ve built marketing, technology and media companies into profitable, multi-million-dollar operations employing diverse teams of professionals.

Conceptualizing, creating and operating many businesses in different markets has taught me effective leadership, operational management skills, how to use technology and systems to reach corporate goals and most importantly, how to recognize and exploit new business opportunities.

Capital, Financing and business planning
As a Partner with ROYALWOOD Capital Corp., I help companies access sources of capital to grow their business and identify purchasers for when they’re ready to sell. My clients are able to reach investors and obtain financing in the tens to hundreds of millions of dollars.

Marketing, Advertising and Business Intelligence
Through companies including Xoom Marketing and Raincity Marketing, I can offer a full range of marketing, advertising and product or business intelligence services. I also provide access to market data lists of investors, gamblers, online purchasers and other niches.

Merchant Processing Consulting
I can assist merchants with local and international payment processing solutions providing access to domestic, national and international alternative solutions payment processing service providers.

Outside of my CEO roles, I enjoy mentoring fellow entrepreneurs through groups like the Blu Chip Network, Internet Masterminds, youngentrepreneur.com and Social Fluency. I am also a volunteer with the Canadian Auxiliary Coast Guard and am a member of Virgin United and Tedx.

Specialties

My core specialties include developing businesses concepts from ideas into successful operations. My expertise and skills include business management, sales, lead generation, marketing strategies, business development, strategic partnerships, business growth and expansion, developing and intensifying new markets, product development, production, print and design management and finance and investment initiatives.

    Christopher Chong Joins Blue Chip Productions
    Online media veteran brings extensive experience in video marketing, production and syndication.
    VANCOUVER, BC (Feb. 12, 2013) - Online media veteran, Christopher Chong, has joined Vancouver video marketing startup, Blue Chip Productions, as Vice President, Strategy, Sales and Marketing.

    Chong has previously served as VP of Sales and Marketing for TripTelevision, CEO of Chalk Media Corporation and Director of Business Development for Blast Radius.

    Wasting no time in his new role, Chong is rolling up his sleeves and starring in a new video designed to flex Blue Chip’s integrated video marketing muscle. Using a mix of SEO and email marketing, the one-minute video, which was produced in-house, and has no viral content to speak of, has managed to rack up more than 200,000 views in less than a month.

    “There has never been a better time for organizations to start leveraging video in their marketing and advertising strategies,” said Chong. “This simple video is a testament to Blue Chip’s integrated approach to video marketing, one that includes production, post-production and search optimization that delivers audiences.”

    Blue Chip, which bills itself as a “high return video marketing company,” has produced videos for clients including CWS industries, Leavitt Machinery and stocktips.com. 

    “Blue Chip is squarely focused on delivering on the promise of convergence between web, television and mobile,” said Nicholas Shepard, President, Blue Chip Productions. “Christopher is an expert in leveraging video strategies across multiple channels and mediums, and we can’t wait to share his unique expertise with our clients.”
