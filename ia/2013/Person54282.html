Nick Davis' Summary

CURRENT:
I am a WordPress developer / WordPress consultant; UX designer and co-founder at eSanctuary SAGL (based out of Ticino, Switzerland). I am also a director at local TV start-up YourTV Ltd in the UK.

I love helping people use commonly available technologies - like WordPress - to build great businesses.

PAST:
I have over 10 years of experience in writing, marketing and working as a consultant to help companies communicate their ideas as clearly and simply as possible, usually via digital media. The majority of my background is in the digital/online sector but I also have experience in print publishing.

Companies I have worked for include:

- ACCA (the global accountancy body);
- BAA (one of the world's largest transport groups);
- BBC;
- ITP Publishing (largest publisher in Middle East);
- ITV (UK's largest commercial broadcaster);
- Tesco (one of the world's largest retailers); and
- Tiscali (former #2 ISP in Europe).

In 2010 I started a new company, eSanctuary, which combines my experience of over a decade in media (and particularly digital media & digital business development) with the skills of and knowledge of an excellent team. Our main focus is on building WordPress websites, but we also offer website design, UX design / consulting and app development (working with trusted partners).

Specialties: copywriting, project management, WordPress consulting, WordPress development, UX design
