Dan Gallo's Summary

I started The Allasso Group in 2006 after selling Mentor Communications Group, a Marketing Communications and eLearning EPSS software products & services company.
During my 25+ years running small businesses serving the Fortune 1000 I conceived & launched Mentor®, an EPSS software application which ultimately served over 50 million users in the B2B & B2C markets. Mentor delivered 700+% ROI for clients by speeding assimilation of re-engineered business processes & new enterprise software deployments, dramatically reducing Help Desk utilization & removing training costs. I also developed worldwide partnerships with global technology leaders, built an international reseller network & launched a line of Mentor retail products.

In the early 1990's I was at the forefront of the adoption of interactive multimedia into mainstream marketing communications plans for the Fortune 1000, developing the earliest interactive sales tools, including the earliest interactive training applications & commercial web sites. I combined high-tech deliverables with my work with Wall Street Investment Bankers as a Communications Consultant on over 100 IPO’s & Debt Offerings including Duracell, Owens-Illinois, GM Hughes Electronics and Revlon. Working with the CEO/COO/CFO, I developed the key business messages for institutional investors, included scripting, directing/producing media & staging events throughout the USA & Europe.

Beginning in 1985, I produced, wrote, directed and designed dozens of major corporate events, product launches, national sales meetings & annual shareholder meetings.

During my career I started a half-dozen companies, led four successful start-up & expansion capital campaigns & initiated eight acquisitions/mergers & business unit sales.

I leverage all these experiences in my consulting practice, serving Fortune 1000 & SMBs with a cadre of other highly accomplished senior executives & expertly skilled consultants.
