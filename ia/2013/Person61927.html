Jennifer Lyons' Summary

I was born and raised in San Diego, and I developed a passion for user experience design during my studies at UC San Diego.  I graduated June 2013 with a B.S. in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction. I have taken multiple classes on user-based design, and completed several projects covering all aspects of the design process; from contextual interviews, to affinity diagrams, to wireframing and on to prototyping.
As a person, I am very playful and creative. I am always fiddling and striving to find new ways to look at things. I am looking for a flexible, collaborative environment where I can be free to speak my ideas, and work closely with others so that I can continue to be pushed towards innovation.

I am currently in the market for a full-time or contract position in San Diego, preferably with an agency or an established company.
For more information on my body of work, check out my online portfolio at www.jennylyons.com

Jennifer Lyons' Education

University of California, San Diego

Cognitive Science, Human Computer Interaction

2008 – 2013

Grade: 3.741

The beauty of cognitive science comes from its interdisciplinary nature. Students in this department all learn a basic understanding of neuroscience, psychology, physiology, and computer science, and can choose to specialize in whatever most strikes their interest. UC San Diego was the first university to pioneer this major, and has been at the cutting edge ever since.
I spent 3 of my 5 years at UCSD specializing in neuroscience, where I learned the inner workings of the human brain and its effects on the mind. Starting my 4th year, I began to study the distributed nature of cognition, and eventually transitioned to an emphasis in human computer interaction. It was here where I learned about UX, and how valuable an understanding of human behavior and cognition can be to the development of a product.

Activities and Societies: UCSD Sailing Team, CSSA, SandCHI
