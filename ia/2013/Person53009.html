Howard Silverman's Summary

In 1983, I received my B.S. in Finance from the University of Florida, and subsequently after that I earned my Juris Doctorate from the Dickinson School of Law in 1986. Upon graduating, I embarked on my new found mission to be an advocate for injured accident victims. Over 25 years later, I'm still driven by my passion to tirelessly represent clients based on my belief that the general public deserves the same quality legal representation available to large companies.

As the founding partner and lead trial attorney at Kane & Silverman (and Haggerty & Silverman P.C. in Lancaster, Pa), I've dedicated my life to helping those who have suffered catastrophic injuries and economic damages due to the wrongful conduct of others. Our expert Personal Injury Attorneys in Philadelphia implement a team oriented approach to every detail relative to a case thoroughly. As a result, we've represented thousands of clients, securing significant financial compensation on their behalf.

In addition, I have a reputation in the legal community for being professional, ethical and for aggressively representing victims of serious personal injuries and injustices.
