Kurtis Smith's Summary

Kurtis Smith is the Founder & CEO of The K-Method Group, Inc., & creator of the K-Method Success Academy, an online learning center dedicated to teaching selling professionals how to build, maintain and manage a book of business. With a diverse background as a United States Marine, Business Consultant, Trainer, Sales and Management Professional, he brings with him over two decades of experience and know-how in the areas of process design and implementation, as well as business development and customer relationship management strategies.

- As a Thought Leader – Kurtis is constantly thinking outside the box to create new solutions to age old challenges in order to develop workable strategies to address the challenges dealerships are wrestling with today.

- As a Change Agent – Kurtis has the ability to deconstruct complex business concepts, challenges and ideas in order to develop solutions that can be transformed into simple, tangible action plans that can be taught, executed and measured.

- As a Training Curriculum Developer – Kurtis is the author of course content and training material focused on personnel, processes and performance improvement that include videos, workbooks, worksheets, process charts and more. For more information about the various training services and programs offered, visit http://kmethodacademy.com.

- As a Technologically Competent Professional – Kurtis is versed at leveraging the various types hardware and software components that are available for the development and delivery of training course-ware. This includes the use of course authoring tools such as Articulate and Camtasia as well as Learning Management Systems for the delivery of training such as Sum Total Systems, Kajabi and various WordPress themes used for membership sites. For more information about Kurtis’ professional profile, visit http://thekurtissmith.com.

Kurtis Smith's Publications

  • Lessons From The Concrete Garden

    • Outskirts Press
    • January 10, 2011
    Authors: Kurtis Smith

    Lessons from the Concrete Garden is a by product of over a decade of research, development and field testing in order to identify and document the specific skill sets, characteristics and knowledge requirements of a highly skilled and professional salesperson. It shines a spotlight on the automotive industry and its struggling salespeople. It provides real answers and proposes real solutions to improve the effectiveness and competency of these individuals through the implementation of specific systems and processes. This is a must read for anyone involved in selling, training or business development.

  • The Sales Professional's Blueprint: The Ultimate Training Program for Dealership Selling Professionals

    • K-Method Publishing
    • July 2, 2013
    Authors: Kurtis Smith

    The Sales Professional’s Blueprint is a breakthrough for those in the dealership selling environment and all selling professionals for that matter. This book is the Blueprint and the perfect tool to act as a roadmap for anyone interested in selling as a profession. It takes you on a step by step personal development journey and teaches you exactly what you need to do and the skills you need to acquire to succeed as a selling professional today and beyond. This extremely important book has been over a decade in the making to identify and document the specific skill sets, characteristics and knowledge of a highly skilled selling professional, so that it could be transformed into a template that anyone can follow. It is packed with real content and is accompanied by an actual training program that teaches the learner exactly what to do; when to do it; and how to do it as well as why it needs to be done. All you have to do is to follow the road map and be transformed.
