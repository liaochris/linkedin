Katherine Baryluk's Summary

Regency International Estates is the exclusive affiliate of Christie's International Real Estate in the Turks and Caicos Islands. We specialize in the sale and marketing of important properties worldwide.
A registered Broker with Harvey Kalles Real Estate, Ltd., Brokerage in Toronto, Canada. Harvey Kalles is an exceptional firm, they are a company that among many other achievements has received the Bentley International Property Award recognizing Harvey Kalles as the best Real Estate company in all of Canada. Katherine comes with an excellent pedigree in Luxury Real Estate. Currently a member of FIABCI (The International Real Estate Federation), CREA (Canadian Real Estate Association), RECO (Real Estate Council of Ontario), TREB (Toronto Real Estate Board) and TCREA (The Turks & Caicos Real Estate Board). She is recognized by “Who’s Who in Luxury Real Estate”, the Board of Regents, Christie’s Great Estates and Leading Real Estate Companies of the World. Prior to 2007, Katherine had a successful career with RE/MAX Hallmark in Toronto, Canada.

Outside of real estate, Katherine is an avid golfer with a Bronze Medal from the Ontario Colleges Athletic Association and a former Ski Instructor with the CSIA (Canadian Ski Instructor’s Alliance).

After living all over the world, including Poland, Germany, Italy, Canada, and the USA she resides on Providenciales in the beautiful Turks & Caicos Islands.
