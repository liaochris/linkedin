Hannah Crum's Summary

Since 2004, Master Brewer & Educator known as The Kombucha Mamma.

What is Kombucha? An ancient homemade elixir from the Far East, consumed by people all over the world for over 2,000 years. Fermented tea that requires a SCOBY culture to brew.

I consult in person and via skype worldwide. Expertise in Traditional/Batch Brew, Continuous Brew, Small Scale Restaurant Brewing & Large Scale Commercial Brewing Operations.

My EHow Video series is the most popular Kombucha Video series on the net. I am passionate about my belief that Kombucha offers a means for reconnecting to process in our lives.

Fluent in Mandarin Chinese & Spanish, I offer private tutoring services.

Specialties

- Kombucha Brewing Supplies
- Kombucha Consultations
- Continuous Brewing
- Commerical Brewing
- Education

Kombucha Expert. Fluent speaker of Mandarin Chinese & Spanish. Current passport/world traveler (Europe, Asia, Middle East). People person. Committed to sustainability.
