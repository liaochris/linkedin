Jeff Wolf's Summary

High-tech industry executive with broad leadership experience, up and down the IT supply chain, across many markets, applications and scientific domains - now focused on applying high performance computing technologies and computational science expertise to achieve transformational, high-impact solutions for industry.

International management experience building and leading marketing, business development, sales, research, development and operations organizations. Strengths in leading multi-disciplinary and multi-cultural teams to solve complex technological challenges.

General management experience with semiconductor materials, manufacturing and device technologies, systems-on-chips (SOCs), components, embedded systems, supercomputers, design automation software, intellectual property, technology transfer and services end-products.

Applications know-how and experience in applying high-performance computing (HPC) technologies in computer, multimedia, networking, wireless, pharma, industrial automation, defense, communications, consumer product, transportation and energy markets.

Eight years board of directors experience.

Specialties

Developing businesses, strategic partnerships, innovation clusters, consortia and ecosystems; forming collaborative development partnerships; evangelizing and launching new technologies, products, corporate initiatives and companies; strategic planning; messaging and brand development; global multi-channel sales organizational development; public and media relations; business turnaround and organizational revitalization; merger & acquisition assimilation; sales training; mentoring.
