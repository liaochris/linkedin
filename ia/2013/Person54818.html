IHER Energy Services Ltd.'s Summary

IHER Energy Services (IHERES) was established as building energy rating training and consultancy company in 2007. However, from the mid 1990s, the business activities of IHER Energy Services were conducted within Energy Action Limited, a registered charity based in Dublin 8.

Michael Hanratty, our Managing Director, is an electrical engineer with 20 years experience working for the ESB, until he began working as an energy marketing and training consultant in 2003.

Bill Sheldrick, our Technical Director, is amongst the leading building energy experts in Ireland and the UK.

Bill Sheldrick was a founder and continuous member, assessor, trainer, monitor and examiner under the National Home Energy Rating (NHER) scheme in the UK, and was involved in pre-launch field trials of the NHER scheme before it was launched in June 1990.

Bill Sheldrick has provided technical consultancy services in Ireland since 1994. Since then, Bill Sheldrick has been delivering energy assessments of dwellings and communities, as well as training in energy surveying and auditing skills across Ireland, on a continual basis in conjunction with Energy Action.

In order that Ireland would have energy assessors in place for the phased introduction of the EU Energy Performance of Building Directive from 1st January 2007, Bill Sheldrick was contracted by Galway Mayo IT to deliver the first Building Energy Rating assessor courses in Ireland in December 2006. Furthermore, in June 2008, when SEI needed to urgently train 50 new dwellings assessors to survey existing dwellings for the launch of the Home Energy Saving Pilot Scheme, Bill was contracted by SEI to deliver four courses around Ireland.

IHER Energy Services was one of the first FETAC approved Building Energy Rating trainers in Ireland, and has run over 25 courses since May 2007, training more than 400 BER assessors from across all four provinces in Ireland.
