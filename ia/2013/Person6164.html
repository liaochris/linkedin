Ana Neves' Summary

I have been working on knowledge and cultural change management for some years. My passion for these topics is reflected in my results. I produce and maintain a monthly, online KM publication where I host current topics and interview influential people in KM. I am tenacious, pro-active, and responsible. I am able to adapt to different work settings and my considerable experience has been gained in both the public and private sectors, in the UK, Portugal and Brasil.

Through Knowman, I am now sharing my experience and skills with organisations looking at knowledge management, social networks and social tools to increase their profits.

I have recently organised Cidadania 2.0 (http://cidadania20.com), a 1-day event exploring how public sector organisations and NGOs may use social tools to improve dialogue and better engage citizens.

Specialties

Knowledge management, communities of practice, organisational learning, social networks, social tools, internal communication, cultural change management
