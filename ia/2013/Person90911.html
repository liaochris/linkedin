meg button's Summary

Meg Button is Digital Creative Lead at Star Content Studios. Meg is a multimedia digital strategist who has made her mark in the agency world producing creative work for clients such as General Motors, Royal Bank and MasterCard. Working on the Home Depot US account, Meg worked to build brand communities, implementing social programs including the Home Depot Garden Club which grew to a peak of 6 million members.

Meg has worked on behalf of brands to create social media strategies to engage consumers and she’s also worked as a social media influencer and blogger on a number of campaigns for brands such as Sauza Tequila, LOFT and Forty Creek Whisky. Last year, Meg spoke at Social Media Week on the topic of Brand Influencers exploring both the side of the brand and the side of the influencer.

Building her own personal brand online has led Meg to some interesting places. She’s the Co-­Host of a podcast called The Zach Bussey Show, available for download on iTunes. With a huge passion for the city where she lives, works and plays, Meg started her own event planning and hype building company called PartyPeopleTO (short for Party People Toronto). At their first event, the company’s hashtag had over 1 million impressions on Twitter and became a trending topic in Toronto and Canada.
