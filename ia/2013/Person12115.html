Terry Rohrbach's Summary

Over the past 15 years, my passion for great design has turned Base Art Co. into an award-winning, nationally known graphic design studio. Base Art Co. is best known and respected among its clients and peers for brilliant creativity that is accessible and solves business problems. The vision for Base Art Co. grew out of my philosophy that every challenge has the opportunity to produce great design and that each piece must work to promote and enhance the client’s brand.

Clients include The Columbus Foundation, Pelotonia, Limited Brands, Bath & Body Works, Resource Interactive, Alliance Data, Kaufman Development, CCAD, Vintage Books, Simon & Schuster, and Penguin Group.

Specialties: Brand strategy & development, design & writing, web & interactive design and deployment.
