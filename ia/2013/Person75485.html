KiSheyna Durham's Summary

KiSheyna is a college graduate of Clark Atlanta University with her degree in Mass Media Communications: Public Relations. Well-versed in media, She has hands-on experience from strategy building, to implemetation,execution and content creation. She also creates custom social media strategies that expand brands into the digital space, and balance traditional promotion with direct interaction.

KiSheyna can offer an in-depth perspective on the ways in which communication lays the foundation for the successful organizing of people, events, and media. She has excellent oral and written communication skills and is also experienced in customer service, graphic designing, and working in teams.

KiSheyna enjoys the constant evolving medium of social media, and spend a lot of time on Facebook,Twitter, Tumblr, Instagram & more outlets for constant viral action. She also keys herself up-to date on the lastest social media trends & catalogs her insights by blogging.

She is highly motivated and creative communicator looking to continue her thriving career in the social media industry. She is an idealist, looking for an opportunity to make the world around herself a better place through creative thinking, strategic planning and most importantly, engagement!
