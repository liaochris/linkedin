Amanda E Clarke's Summary

Amanda is an award winning entrepreneur, Non-exec Director, speaker, facilitator and writer with the ability to enthuse and inspire. She utilises the world’s best tools & technologies for enhanced performance, including Accelerated Learning, Coaching, Absorbing Information and Behavioural Communication.

Amanda is highly skilled in recognising behaviour, values and competencies, and links this with personal, team and corporate performance in all sizes of business.

A small sample of clients past and present, include McDonalds Restaurants, GE (Hotpoint), BurgerKing, Whitbread Breweries, AXA, The Home Office, London Borough of Southwark, Manchester City Council.

As author of three e-books, co-author of a number 1 best seller, developer of the DebtFree & Happy program and creator of Accelerbrain™ Training Systems, Amanda’s media coverage includes appearances on Sky’s Channel M, and she has featured in articles in many UK newspapers & magazines. She regularly appears on BBC radio and shows in the UK & USA.

Accelerbrain Training Sytems is the international arm of her business enterprise, having extensive experience of working with clients in Europe, the Middle East, and USA.

As a Non-Exec Director, she specialises in effective systems management and building strong relationships to bring an independent perspective to the business along with a broader and external view of matters. Her excellent people skills and understanding of the human challenges of growing an SME give her an edge as she can play the role of ‘devil’s advocate’ to your senior executives.

Amanda has substantial experience in how to profitably grow small to medium sized businesses as well as how to raise money for investment through private equity. She built her own business franchising model and can provide valuable insights to Franchisors.
