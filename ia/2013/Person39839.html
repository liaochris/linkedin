Edward Devine's Summary

I perform Environmental Assessment, Vapor Encroachment Screening, Property Condition Assessment and Comprehensive Building & Property Inspection, Capital Expenditure Reports and also offer consulting services in these areas.

My clients are investors, and developers; property managers and facilities directors; real estate buyers, sellers and owners; real estate investments trusts; municipalities; airport tenants; marinas; architects and engineers; brokers, bankers and other lenders; attorneys and insurance companies.

I founded this company in 1985 as a local architectural design firm and soon after added building inspection services and then environmental testing, laboratory and assessment services.

Since 1985 we've grown into a national provider and proudly serve individual property owners, local, regional and national banks and other lending institutions, investors, multi-national corporations and municipalities, and have to date delivered thousands of inspection and assessment services. We use no subcontractors, As a principal of the firm I complete every facet of every project I accept.
