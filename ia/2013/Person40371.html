David Manek, CPA, CFF, CFE's Summary

Phone: 312.583.6841
Email: dmanek@navigant.com

I lead teams within Navigant’s Legal Technology Solutions Practice where we help companies and legal counsel involved in large, complex, data-intensive investigations between private parties and compliance matters before regulatory agencies. Our services include:

• Managed services for the EDRM life cycle
• Processing, hosting and production
• Assisted review and Relativity technical support
• Outsourcing or co-sourcing support
• Identification, collection, preservation and analysis of financial and operational data
• Data visualization
• Expert testimony
• Database analysis tools for validation and legal review
• Expertise deciphering disparate data systems
• Forensic collection and legal hold

We advise companies and legal counsel on how to manage their e-discovery and data analysis service needs efficiently and cost-effectively. I am fortunate to co-manage one of the largest electronic discovery production engagements in recent history.

Within our Practice we focus on enterprise wide data collection, processing, analysis and production of two types of electronically stored information in an ediscovery litigation environment:

1) Unstructured data - traditional "free standing" documents such as emails, email attachments, Microsoft Word documents, Excel documents, PDFs, etc.

2) Structured data - data extracted from a database or information system

We use low cost electronic document management systems such as kCura’s Relativity review tool. The Legal Technology Solutions Practice also develops in-house litigation technology platforms such as our proprietary structured data privilege review tool.

Navigant is a high speed e discovery vendor serving many of the Fortune 100. Our Legal Technology Practice has hubs in New York, Washington D.C., Chicago and Los Angeles.
