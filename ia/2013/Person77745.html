Led a team of eight consultants to help Heinz:

* Achieve visibility of all expenses and truly understand cost drivers for fixed and variable expenses globally
* Set aggressive but realistic savings targets based on deep understanding of all costs and their price and consumption drivers
* Re-write corporate policies to put in place cost reduction measures to realize savings targets
* Develop a zero-based budget with input from 500 budget owners globally
* Achieve savings targets set for 2014
* Roll-out a monthly tracking and monitoring process globally to highlight monthly budget variances and identify the individuals accountable for resolving the variances
