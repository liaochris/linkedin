Bill Ainley's Summary

HortMatrix LLC services the horticultural trade with a focus on production planning, material requirement planning and number tracking to illuminate opportunities to save and to manage risk. Also provided are services in areas such as availability and store scan analysis. Services are rendered via data generation through proprietary algorithms and database engines. No products are sold. Business is generated based on consultation, process design, collection of data, processing of data and service. User interfaces and output data is customized to best fit the exact needs. Solutions from the ground up can be created. Methods lend well to filling gaps in existing systems or end to end solutions from sales plan to production line to distribution floor. Work has included strategic consultation outside of the green sector. Starting with the strengths of your team I work hands on where needed and otherwise coach & lead to results.
