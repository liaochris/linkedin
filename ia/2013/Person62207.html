Jennifer Wolfe, Esq., APR, SSBB's Summary

Jennifer Wolfe is an executive leader and proven entrepreneur with multi-disciplined expertise in digital brand strategy, innovation, and intellectual property coupled with the ability to lead multi-stakeholder groups and identify future trends to drive strategic growth. She has partnered in technology start-ups, provides management consulting and intellectual property strategy to Fortune 500 companies, offers leadership as a member of the board of directors of emerging growth companies and offers expert testimony in trials related to brands and intellectual property matters. She was recently elected to the Generic Name Supporting Organization of ICANN where she works to develop policy for the Internet.

For the second year in a row she was named one of the top 300 global intellectual property strategists by IAM Magazine. She is a two-time past president of the Greater Cincinnati Venture Capital Association, a certified Six Sigma Black Belt in process improvement and nationally accredited in public relations. She was selected as a member of the 2012 class of the Direct Women Institute.

Wolfe’s most recent book, Domain Names Rewired, predicts trends in the next generation of the internet. She also co-authored Brand Rewired, on innovation, branding and intellectual property strategy in 2010, which was widely recognized as visionary thinking by global brand executives.

In addition to her independent work, she serves as the Co-Managing Partner of Wolfe Sadler Breen Morasch & Colby, an intellectual property and corporate counsel firm she started at the age of 28 and grew via organic and merger based growth into a premiere intellectual property firm with offices in Cincinnati, New York City and Spokane.

In response to market demands, Wolfe launched a digital brand agency, Wolfe Domain, and is working with top global brands in media, entertainment, retail and consumer goods to develop strategies for the next generation of the internet.

Specialties

Digital brand strategy, strategic planning, top-level domains, intellectual property strategy, innovation, corporate counsel for emerging companies
