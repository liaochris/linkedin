Kelly Joseph's Summary

For more than a decade, Kelly Joseph has provided high quality website/graphic design services, as well as internet marketing and digital media consulting to the Central Massachusetts area. Beginning in the corporate world, it became apparent that transitioning to freelance/self-employed status was the key to her success, where her dedication to the craft and ability to provide the absolute highest customer service level could be put to use.

Kelly has worked with clients of all sizes in a broad range of fields (medical, academic, construction, art & music, retail sales, manufacturing, publishing and real estate) to assist them in getting their business vision accurately and artistically translated to web and digital formats.

Able to provide the same excellent service and accountable results to clients both nearby and remote, she prides herself on being a good listener and, as a result, has been able to maintain a very high level of client satisfaction.
