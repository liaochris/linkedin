Margaretta Noonan's Summary

Following a 30 year corporate career with major global firms, I now speak, write and consult on how companies can maximize their most precious asset: their people. I take the lessons that I've learned through a wide variety of experiences and apply them in practical ways to organizations that want to make the most of their investment in talent.
