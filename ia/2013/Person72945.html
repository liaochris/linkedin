Kathy Condon's Summary

An Award-Winning Author

I was thrilled when USA Book News sent me an announcement to tell me they named my first book “It Doesn’t Hurt to Ask It’s All About Communication” a “Best Book Finalist.”

Intergenerational Communication

The next stage of my career was learning about communication between the generations. We now have five different generations in the workplace. What were the common denominators that can help strengthen communication in the workplace? My studies revealed the answers and provided a platform for me to speak and train on the subject so real communication can take place.

Interviewing Made Easy

Thankfully, I have been honored to have developed a way to make people understand the simple things that they need to do to make a lasting impression on the person interviewing them. I’ve noticed that if I can meet with people face-to-face, on Skype or on the phone they will walk into any interview much more confidently. Nothing better when they call and say, “I got the job.”

Face-to-Face Networking – Building Relationships with the Business Community

Finally, it was time for me to put in writing all the “little things” I had learned about teaching people how to do face-to-face networking. When my book, “Face-To-Face Networking It’s All About Communication” was finished, even I was surprised by the response to this simple guide. These easy to apply tools, apparently struck a core with readers. The book soared to the forefront of other networking books on Amazon--I am most grateful for the outstanding reviews,
