Mike Brannon's Summary

Principal and Managing Director of Landers Brannon - a human-sized interactive design and development studio driven by passion and meticulous attention to detail - and fearless leader to a consortium of talented designers, developers and interactive strategists.

An advocate of clean, user-friendly design, a keen eye for understanding clients' vision and passion for delivering on users' expectations. With fourteen years of professional experience, eleven as a business owner, Mike has developed both the design acumen and business knowledge necessary for our clients' success. As an interactive consultant, he’s been involved with over 300 projects, and has clients that range from Fortune 500 companies to government and non-profit agencies.

Specialties: End-to-End Web Services, Interactive Strategy, User Experience Design, Web Design and Development, ExpressionEngine, WordPress, Branding, User Research and Testing, Information Architecture, Web Marketing, Graphic and Visual Design, SEO, SEM and Project Management.
