Glenn Hernandez's Summary

Specializing in motion graphics, visual effects, 3D animation, 3D Stereoscopic, Look Development, keying, compositing, motion tracking, particles and web media.

As a native of NYC, I graduated from New York Institute of Technology with a degree in Computer Science and became involved with computer graphics when I started working for the New York Mets. Having both artistic and technical skills, has given me the opportunity to work in various media and with a broad range of clients.

Through the years I have acquired and mastered a broad range of skills in motion graphics, visual effects, 3D animation, Stereoscopic, web development, interactive & print advertising. Past work includes everything from commercials, corporate presentations, in-game live graphics and interactive media.
