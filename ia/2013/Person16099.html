Brad Gentile's Summary

Brad is an accomplished content producer with over 20 years experience in video production, planning and developing product marketing strategies and concepts. Brad's specialty is offering cost-effective marketing strategies via video/dvd, understanding consumer needs and developing compelling content.

Brad has excellent production management skills including directing live, multi-camera productions; studio and field productions and producing content from script to screen. Brad has conducted work throughout the US and internationally and has received numeous accolades by his peers for his creativity and professionalism. He has also presented at the Lift Leader Expo and AEM conferences on "How to effectively market with video". You can visit his website at bottomlinevideo.com

Brad is an award-winning DP, producer and editor. His projects include documentaries, entertainment, product marketing, training and educational productions. Brad's skills have been utilized by several international organizations such as Troika Entertainment, G Creative Advertising and Design, Grove Worldwide, Manitowoc Crane Group, American Rental Association, Compressed Gas Association, Discovery Communciations, ESPN, Coldwell Banker, Innovative Event Productions, Ivanhoe Broadcasting, Austrian TV, ABC, NBC, FOX, FUJI TV.
Brad has also been a camera op for NFL, NBA, WNBA, NHL, MSL, MLB, PGA, LPGA, Preakness, NASCAR, US Figure Skating, professional boxing, bull riding, lacrosse, indoor soccer, steeplechase racing, and major collegiate athletics.

-Skilled in live and taped multi- camera field, studio, and event productions including documentaries, television series, corporate industrials and educational content, training and safety, marketing and entertainment, medical and sciences, associations and non-profits, live broadcast news/sports.
-Skilled script writer and Final Cut Pro editor.
-Experience in ENG, EFP, single and multiple camera field and studio production and lighting.
