Author of "Family Terror"

Today violence against women has become an epidemic. We should call it for what it is. . .FAMILY TERROR!
Today’s approach is increasing the danger and number of victims. I discuss in this book new methods and actions to start eliminating FAMILY TERROR.
From being the victim of an especially sadistic abuser, I explain the signs that could have warned me to stay away from this man from the very beginning. Had I known what to look for, I could have avoided a horrendous amount of pain and suffering.
The laws are already in existence to give protection to the victims of FAMILY TERROR. What is needed is an awakening of the legal system to enforce these laws to protect women and their children.
This country punishes the victims of FAMILY TERROR and gives the TERRORIST a free pass to continue the behavior.
The criminal behavior of the Family Terrorist needs to be handled like any other crime. This crime should include time behind bars and restitution to the victims enabling them to rebuild their crumbled lives for themselves and the children.
Children should not be a witness to these crimes and the experience of the legal game not protecting them or their mothers. Never again should we send a message to our children that they are not important enough to change the system that we have endured for all these years.
