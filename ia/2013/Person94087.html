Mike Cantrell, MBA's Summary

Mike has 18 years of progressively responsible IT experience in the Tennessee Valley area. He serves as Director of Information Systems at Benchmark Rehab Partners in Ooltewah, TN. As Director of IT, Mike oversees the implementation, support, and use of all information technology at over 150 locations across the United States. He also is Chief Information Officer for WebOutcomes.com. Mike earned a BS in Computer and Information Systems from Western Carolina University, and an MBA in Management Information Systems from Tennessee Tech University. Mike lives in Cleveland, TN with his wife and two children.
