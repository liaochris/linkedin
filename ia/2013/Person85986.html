Marilyn Ellis' Summary

My primary goal is to maintain my reputation for excellence. I wish to share my gifts and talents to improve the well being of my clients. I help businesses and individuals navigate through their busy and challenging lives. Since I built my own successful business, as a Professional Organizer and Certified Life Coach, I help Entrepreneurs and Small Businesses do the same! I help them Organize their thoughts so together we can uncover strategies to build and grow their businesses.

I am also a Senior Move Manager and Certified Senior Advisor. I have discovered that working with Seniors and their families is one of the most rewarding things I have ever done. I help move my clients forward by either downsizing ,moving to a senior community by helping them stay safely in their homes.
I have also added Estate Liquidation to my list of services and enjoy working with others who serve seniors.

If you feel stuck on the rocks, lost in the fog or surrounded by sharks, I can shine my harbor light on you and bring you safely and serenely home.

My greatest reward is when a client says, "I don't know what I would have done without you".
