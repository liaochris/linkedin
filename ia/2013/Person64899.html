Jo Anna Shaw's Summary

My mission is "To facilitate Heart, Meaning, Integrity and Excellence in Work-Life."

The greater purpose of business is to develop people. Optimal results are the outgrowth of people working together, effectively.

Even if your company is not supporting your personal and professional development at the level you desire, you can still enhance your skills and live by your values. In fact, becoming healthy and proactive in relationship to work-life issues is a measure of your personal and professional growth, and fulfills a greater purpose.

It is possible to feel peaceful and empowered working inside organizations or in your own business — as they are currently functioning — and, as you ride the waves of change.

Businesses:

-- integrate self-directed systems that optimize individual, team and organizational bests
-- be congruent so they get results instead of resistance
-- develop, sustain and enhance a culture of positive intent and professionalism

Individuals address:

-- Blocks to Success
-- Individual and Group Communication issues
-- Stress
-- Wellness

My clients are:
-- Executives
-- Career Professionals
-- Aspiring and Professional Speakers, Trainers, Coaches and Healers
-- Small Business Owners
-- Writers
-- Students

They choose me because:
-- I walk my talk.
-- I have a strong business background as well as a long history working with people who have learning, physical and emotional issues.
-- They get results.
