Dawn Boshcoff's Summary

"I thrive on working with other talented individuals to get results. My expertise is demonstrated while managing people, projects, partnerships and new business opportunities."

Dawn is a new media PR specialist, professional writer and trainer who serves an international portfolio of clients through her companies BOSHnewmedia.com and SM Strategy Group.

For over a decade she has helped individuals, entrepreneurs and companies to position their brand and create digital dialogue to get found, get noticed and get business online.

Inspired by technology, BOSH publishes creative content in all digital forms using the latest technology and software to communicate on the web. Digital dialogue, click communications and multi-media publishing of CLEARLY Content - creative, interactive and engaging.

Specialties

brand positioning, SEO, social media, PR, social networking, digital strategy, interactive marketing, web content, blogging copywriting, ad copy, A/V script, case studies, digital press kits, e-books, e-commerce, e-publishing, FB pages, interactive new media, internet radio, landing pages, forums, media releases, mobile apps, online video, podcasts, QR coding, slide presentations, training tools, webinars, brand profiles, e-mail campaigns, blog monitoring, SM monitoring
