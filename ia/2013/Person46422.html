Furkan Kıraç's Projects

  • Platasis

    • July 2003 to Present
    Team Members: Furkan Kıraç

    First national license plate recognition and parking automation software widely used all around Turkey.
    Recently Metrocity also decided to use Platasis for ticket free car park automation.

  • Model Tracker

    • 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Furkan Kıraç

    3D Model based real-time tracking framework.
    Framework is able to train and classify Random Decision Forests (RDF) and Random Regression Forests. RDF version has been demonstrated in ICCV'11. Trained forests are also used in the demonstration by Cem Keskin and Eray Berger in Gesture Challenge Workshop Demo Session in CVPR 2012. The presentation won the 1st prize.
