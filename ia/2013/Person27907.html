Cristina Allen's Experience

Naturopathic Doctor & Owner

Dr. Cristina Allen, ND

2010 – Present (3 years) King West Chiropractic Health Centre

Dr. Cristina Allen, ND was granted acceptance early into the Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine in Toronto, Ontario after competing three years of a Bachelor of Science Degree in Biology at the University of Calgary. She graduated from the Naturopathic Doctor program with adidtional training in a subspecialty in pediatrics, parenteral therapy, and mesotherapy.

She is dedicated to a lifetime of continuing education, focusing on her particular interests in pregnancy and pediatrics, as well as the impact of diet and nutrition on health. Dr. Cristina sees both paediatric and adult patients regularly and has experience treating a wide range of conditions including those seeking cosmetic treatments, anti-aging, autism, ADHD, polycystic ovarian syndrome, diabetes, high blood pressure, stress management, and multiple sclerosis.

During her time at the Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine, Dr. Cristina had the opportunity to work at Anishnawbe Health Toronto, a multidisciplinary health care center providing care for the Aboriginal community. This opportunity allowed her to learn first hand, the importance of basic and health care.

Her approach to health care is therefore simple: to treat conditions as least invasive as possible, while looking at the individual as a whole. Prevention is the key, and is achieved by optimizing the individuals health.

Dr. Cristina is proud to welcome patients to a practice where empathy combine with education and experience to provide the best health care. Her philosophy of service to the patient creates excellence in both patient care and clinical results.

Food Lover, Chef, Writer, & Teacher

Arts + Science

June 2007 – Present (6 years 4 months)

First and foremost, Cristina is a food lover. She is currently writing a cookbook on gourmet foods that can be consumed on a detox, so foodies like her can still enjoy food while eating a hypoallergenic diet!

Cristina hosts gourmet cooking classes monthly at the Health Loft with different themes, such as hypoallergenic, vegan, gluten-free. The classes are a complete dining experience, starting with a cooking class where you'll learn about the ingredients for the recipe, how to use them, and what their health benefits are. The class if followed by enjoying the meals that we prepare together which will be paired with a drink special. At the end of all of it, you get a take-out package to go, so you can enjoy the meal in the comfort of your own home.
