David Courreges' Summary

David C. Courreges is the president of Courreges, PC. His practice focuses on all aspects of government law including governmental and legislative affairs, administrative law, association law and mediation. He has over a decade of direct experience in government, working as the legislative director for two high-ranking state representatives, general counsel for two non-partisan legislative caucuses, as well as a consultant for several entities dedicated to the research and promotion of sound public policy.

Specialties: Contract legislative and governmental relations consultant.
