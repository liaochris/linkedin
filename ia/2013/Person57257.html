James K Barath, CMPS's Summary

I am a Certified Mortgage Planner by designation; Loan Officer to first-time home buyers; Mortgage Loan Originator by federal law; Mortgage Broker to borrowers prior to 2007 and Mortgage Banker by industry insiders.

REALTORS® and clients have also been known to call me their FHA Home Loan Expert, VA Home Loan Specialist, USDA Home Loan Expert, FHA $100 Down Expert, FHA 203k Rehab and Renovation Home Loan Expert and the go to for Reverse Mortgages.

Whatever you call me, I'm here to help and guide you through the mortgage planning process.

Mortgage Planning is about building relationships, not just conducting a transaction. As such, it requires a defined system of accountability in order to work effectively. The Mortgage Planning Process consists of the following five steps:

1. Establish and Define the Client-Planner Relationship
2. Analyze and Evaluate Your Financial Status
3. Develop and Present Mortgage Planning Recommendations and/or Alternatives
4. Implementing the Mortgage Planning Recommendations
5. Monitor the Mortgage Planning Recommendations through an Annual Mortgage and Equity Management Review
