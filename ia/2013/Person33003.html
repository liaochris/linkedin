David Beare's Summary

My passion is the practical engagement of people in organisations towards achieving ethical and sustainable objectives. This involves leadership of the highest order but also the provision of management structures which support the direction and strategy which underlies excellent organisational performance. My experiences of in the Army, the oil industry and in smaller business management have given me a set of experience and tools that help fufil this. Corguv is therefore a business that helps others toward better corporate governance:

CorGuv is a company that deals with providing the structures necessary for effective corporate governance. We provide measurable value to organisations by concentrating, not only on formal stuctures, but also the commercial and ethical standards necessary for successful business. Our focus is therefore on implementing systems that deliver sustainable value over long periods. We offer the following key services:

> Contract Compliance - Health Checks, Forensic Auditing.
> Joint Venture Audits –getting value from their investments.
> Internal Control Systems – Designing and implementing reporting and risk management systems.
> Project Audits – ensuring projects are managed well and deliver benefits.
> Project Management Governance – consistently getting benefits from projects.

We have set new standards in Contract Compliance Services which improve post-award contract performance and save large sums of money normally lost in transaction costs or poor processes. We can deliver this service in the form or consultancy, programme management, or a full outsourced service. We can offer part-time Audit Management Services for companies that do not have dedicated audit resources. We are so confident of providing cash benefits that we will enter into risk/reward deals where appropriate.

Our motto is:
Doing Things Right, First Time, On Time, Every Time
