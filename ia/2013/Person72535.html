Kase Wickman's Summary

Kase Wickman is a journalist with experience in print, radio, video and multimedia reporting. She graduated from Boston University in May 2010, majoring in journalism and American politics.

Since graduation, she has established herself as a features writer in New York, specializing in profiles, Q&As, and in-depth reporting. Her work has appeared in The Awl, The Hairpin, Vulture, The Riverfront Times, BUST, The Christian Science Monitor and The Boston Globe, among many other publications.

Specialties

writing, editing, AP style, political reporting, regional reporting, food writing, editorials, multimedia, wearing purple, podcasting, blogging, audio editing, social media, livetweeting, entertainment reporting, profile writing, feature writing, SEO
