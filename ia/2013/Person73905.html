Kelly Donovan, CPRW's Summary

Are you ready to land your next job? How about getting promoted? Earning what you’re worth? Enjoying work more? These are some of the benefits my clients have experienced.

I’m a career communications strategist and certified resume writer who partners with successful executives, managers, senior professionals, and entrepreneurs. Does that sound like you?

My focus is on helping you uncover and convey your unique value and accomplishments, paving the way to powerful, modern career documents, confidence, and compelling messages to be used across all your networking efforts, job interviews, and social media platforms.

I stay current on trends and emerging best practices that many of my peers don't understand yet, so that you can have an edge.

Based in Southern California (Lake Elsinore), I work with clients nationwide.

Additionally, I provide outplacement services for employers, and I speak on the topic of "Selling Yourself in Today's Job Market."
