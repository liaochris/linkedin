Karen Unland's Summary

After years of immersion in the editorial side of journalism, I have turned my attention to understanding the business side.

I have the privilege of learning on the job, through the Capital Ideas project based at the Edmonton Journal. We are working on finding new ways for a news organization to serve the information needs of its community.

We have started with Edmonton's entrepreneurial community, with the idea that everyone within it knows something about business and wants to learn more. If we can help connect those people so they can inform each other, we will have a transformative effect on our city. And, in the process, I am learning a great deal from the entrepreneurial Edmontonians that this project puts me in touch with.

Specialties

Teaching, consulting, connection-making, editing, news judgment, communication, workflow-organizing, public speaking.

Interests: entrepreneurship, small business, startups, lean startup, economic development, innovation.
