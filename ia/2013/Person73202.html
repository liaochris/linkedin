Katrina van Oudheusden's Summary

So my background is in cooking. I was a Sous Chef with Disney for almost 12 years. Learn a lot from this amazing company.

During my time there I discovered Network Marketing. I also owned a restaurant called, Tony's Original Wings and Grill for about 9 months. Best education I have ever received.

I took a hard look at my life and decided I wanted more that the Disney job, I wanted the freedom lifestyle. I wanted financial freedom and time freedom.

Thus began my journey to learn attraction marketing. I became a student of Magnetic Sponsoring and began applying what I had learned in all the courses I bought. I began taking action and the results have been life changing.

I am now the Founder of Small Business Mastermind University. The school for small business owners by small business owners. Get hands on training to marketing strategies, taxes, health care, lawyers, and so much more.

Our goal is to empower the small business owner with tool they can apply in class and as soon as they leave class.

I also continued my involvement with Reliv International, a direct sales company. They are on the cutting edge of nutrition and I am proud to be associated with them.

Because of my association with the network marketing industry I have become a Blogging Coach. I teach network marketers how to use blogging as a fast business building skill. It is better to stand out and be a leader.

So my passion is around teaching and helping small business owners and network marketers achieve their dreams.
