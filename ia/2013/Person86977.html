Mark Porter's Summary

Mark Porter is a 20 year veteran of the real estate industry. By age 23, he averaged 52 transactions a year with a 10 day market time and a 99% list to sales price ratio. Mark’s animated, humorous and engaging teaching style will leave you with practical tools and the motivation needed to take action and implement.

A Broker, Investor, Consultant and Instructor who has made a career out of helping individuals and companies unleash their potential. Mark and his company, Mark Porter Live, bring dozens of training courses and resources to thousands of professionals across the world. You too can benefit from his specialization in Planning, Systems and Technology!

Mark’s efforts contribute to the successes of the largest and most prestigious designations in real estate, GRI – Graduate REALTORS Institute and CRS – Certified Residential Specialist.

Mark has been a featured speaker for numerous industry organizations & conventions, including: Century 21, Coldwell Banker; Keller Williams; Prudential; RE/MAX; Hewlett Packard; Kodak; the Women’s Council of REALTORS®; the National Association of REALTORS

You can find more about Mark at www.MarkPorterLive.com

Mission Statement:
“We unleash core productivity and quality of life principles to sales professionals!” It is our obligation to create an engaging environment for education. We thirst for what must to be mastered, to discover the systems that result in profitability and new levels of life for those who wish to seize the opportunity. Integrity, hard work, and habits are not just words that we take lightly.

Mark Porter's Experience

Broker Owner

Castle Hills Real Estate

July 2013 – Present (4 months) Lewisville, TX

Broker Owner of Castle Hills Real Estate, we help the Castle Hills Subdivision area community buy, sell and invest in real estate.

Castle Hills, named "Community of the Year" by the Dallas Home builders Association Awards, is a majestic 2,500-acre master-planned golf course community, located in North Dallas, west of the Tollway and Park Blvd., near Carrollton, west of Plano. Convenient to major employers, shopping, recreation, and entertainment, Castle Hills is one of the premier residential golf communities in North Texas.

Castle Hills Real Estate is the only real estate office specializing in the Castle Hills Subdivision of Lewisville TX. We are specialists and we are good at what we do. Our focus on the buyers, sellers and investors in and around the immediate area is an advantage for you and we do it very well! Do you need a specialists?

If you need and REALTOR in the next city, state or even in another country, we refer you to a like-minded specialist in that location. Give us a try, you will not be disappointed.
