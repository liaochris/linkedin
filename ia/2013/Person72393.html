Karl Glotzbach's Summary

I am a creative and strategic marketing professional with high-level experience proposing, selling and implementing business-to-business solutions for Fortune 1000 clients. My work history: developing programs which drive business performance.

I’m a strong relationship-builder with a passion for new business development who loves the thrill of the chase. I am competitive and love to win.

I have experience developing a variety of business solutions, including: performance improvement/incentives, recognition, promotional merchandise, lead generation, training and corporate travel management. I love the idea side of business. I am an excellent communicator both in person and through media. I recently (Q2 2013) completed a course in Internet Marketing Essentials at St. Thomas University focused on SEO, paid search (Google AdWords), email marketing and social network marketing.

My ideal position is in corporate wellness. I am enrolled in the Wellness Coaching program at the Mayo Clinic. I start in late June (2013). My intent is become a certified wellness coach. I plan to combine this new knowledge and credential with my existing skill set to promote healthy habits in the workplace. Healthier, more engaged employees cost less and produce more, a double win for their teams and employers. I am going to be a part of that movement.

I strongly believe in the importance of maintaining a positive attitude and I like to have fun at work.
My goal is to be where the action is and leave things better than they were before I got there.

Specialties: Client discovery, proposal development, presentation production and delivery, business development, relationship management, program design, creative strategy/execution, pricing, profitability forecasting, project management, contract management, website development, lead generation, branding.
