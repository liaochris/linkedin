Bryan Jurewicz's Summary

My passion is for helping entrepreneurs, companies and investors build businesses that achieve significant growth and return on investment. I believe in leveraging a balance of entrepreneurial drive and leadership vision to create disruptive innovation that maximizes shareholder value. My background is in high growth technology environments with proven accomplishments optimizing business models, fundraising, accelerating revenue, establishing strategic partnerships and delivering liquidity.

Since 2001, I have been achieving impressive results in challenging early stage and complex turnaround situations. As CEO, I built GradeBeam.com into an online B2B construction communication network that connects over 500,000 contractors across North America. I led the company from concept through the .Com Bubble and Great Recession to be an industry leader before selling the business to Textura Corporation with attractive returns for all investors. I then successfully transitioned GradeBeam.com into a division of Textura which subsequently initiated a public stock offering.

I have seasoned leadership experience to generate impactful results and fast paced success. I thrive in positions providing strategic direction while getting my hands dirty and applying a determination for achieving winning objectives. I serve in executive, co-founder, advisor, consultant and board member roles to help mold new concepts into revenue generating companies.

CEO

GradeBeam.com

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Construction industry

February 2001 – October 2011 (10 years 9 months) Chicago, IL

I built GradeBeam.com into a B2B Construction Communication Network that connects over 500,000 companies across North America. I led the GradeBeam.com board strategy, developed the business model, and was the primary decision maker for all operations. I built the management team, expanded into markets across North America, achieved profitability within tight budgets, and successfully negotiated the sale of the business. I also directed the development of the GradeBeam.com contractor database and online tools for subcontractor selection, vendor qualification, document file sharing, bid coordination, messaging, and reporting.

Under my leadership, GradeBeam.com evolved from concept to market leader. GradeBeam.com has been recognized multiple times as a “Hot Product” by trade publications like Constructech and Construction Executive magazines for innovative and industry-leading technology. In 2008, I built relationships with and presented to most of the Chicago angel and venture capital firms. I ultimately secured funding as the 2nd investment made by Hyde Park Angels’ and financed the further growth of GradeBeam.com. I am proud to say that we doubled the size of the business in 2009 and again in 2010.

In 2011, I focused the company on reducing customer acquisition costs by developing strategic partnerships. I structured agreements with several construction industry associations, rebranded the service, and built a reseller partnership with Reed Construction Data. This collaborative approach led to conversations and acquisition interest from Textura Corporation. I then led the presentations, valuations, negotiations, due diligence, and exit for the GradeBeam.com investors who benefitted from significant return on investment. The purchase of GradeBeam.com by Textura Corporation strengthened the value propositions and allowed for the expansion of offerings available to clients of both organizations prior to Textura going public in 2013.
