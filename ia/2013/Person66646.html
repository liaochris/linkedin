John N. Marchesini's Summary

Media and entertainment companies can no longer depend solely on content creation and distribution to sustain their businesses. At The Hollywood Reporter, we are in the business of developing digital products that bundle high value content with functionality delivered to our readers whenever and wherever they want it.

John leads digital product development at The Hollywood Reporter where he is responsible for the planning, execution and implementation of the Company’s digital technology strategy. For the past three years, he has presided over a period of unprecedented growth in The Company's digital product portfolio, including website visitors/pageviews, mobile apps, and social media accounts.

John has a proven track record of successfully conceptualizing, launching and managing consumer-facing products for desktop, tablet, handheld and social platforms. He possesses an excellent understanding of great user experience (UE, UX) and how it's powered by underlying technology.

Prior to working in media and entertainment, John led digital marketing initiatives in consulting engagements for Fortune 500 and Global 2000 financial services companies. With 10 total years of experience, John is a proven leader within the product, technology, and marketing departments.
