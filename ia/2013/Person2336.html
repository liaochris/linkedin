Andrea G. Forte's Summary

Andrea G. Forte is a senior researcher with the Chief Security Office at AT&T.
He earned both his Bachelor's and Master's Degrees in Telecommunications Engineering at the University of Rome "La Sapienza" in Italy.
The paper based on his dissertation work on fast handoffs for real-time media in IEEE 802.11 wireless networks was published at ACM MobiWAC 2004. Later commercialized by SIPquest Inc. (now Counterpath), the fast handoff algorithm was awarded the 2004 Internet Telephony Product of the Year and the 2005 Frost & Sullivan Excellence in Research Award.

Specialties

His research interests include network security, mobility, real-time media, location-based services, location obfuscation and privacy, wireless networks and signaling compression, Internet of Things.
