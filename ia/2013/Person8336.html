Annelize Bester's Experience

Founder

Annelize Bester | THUTO

January 2006 – Present (8 years) Los Angeles

I am a Photographer, Filmmaker and Humanitairan in support of Education's role in achieving sustainable, coordinated and inclusive human development.


THUTO (also known as The THUTO Foundation and Think Tank Thuto) is a registered U.S. 501 (c) 3. (www.thuto.us)

Our 2013 forever fundraising campaign is for The Children of Botswana Education Fund. To learn more please visit www.thechildrenofbotswana.org. The site was created to help promote not only an Education Fund but also the Humanitarian Photography titled and created in support of the disadvantage children of Botswana, Africa.


History: THUTO started off as Think Tank Thuto. Latter is now an International Planning Committee for TAISBA [The Anglican International Schools of Botswana, Africa]. The committee has planned a Pre-K to Secondary Anglican Boarding School for Mathangwane, Botswana.
