Joshua Jordison's Education

Vanguard University of Southern California

Dropped-Out & Never Looked Back (Best Decision Ever Made), Double Major in English & Business | Minor in Music

2004 – 2005

Dropping-out of college was the best decision I've ever made. I'm not knocking college; but, it's not for everyone. I was tired of theory and wanted to go make things happen. Carrying a GPA at the top 5% of the student body and being bored sucks.

I can count on two fingers, since dropping-out, how many times I've been asked about my educational background... and one of those times occurred because a friend realized we both had dropped-out of the same college.

I've always been self-educated (I love to read and study). Who knows, perhaps I'll return to college some day, though it would only be to expand my knowledge in some particular area - it has not and would never positively or negatively effect what I'm able to do in business.

Activities and Societies: During my short stay, I was involved in numerous on-campus activities, including playing guitar in the orchestra. I received a scholarship for guitar but soon discovered that I was much more interested in the music business, rather than being a professional musician.
