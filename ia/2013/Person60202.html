j.christopher Boue's Summary

I have acquired expertise at several levels, tip of the iceberg currently :

visual effects supervisor : abroad | in-house
visual consultant : pre-production | creative solutions
2D (compositing) supervisor or just senior film artist

But above all, my approach remains very artistic :
in holding just about every existing position during my 20 years as a vFx artist, I have mastered most techniques in this field, eventually allowing me to supervise the entire process.
I now focus on and feed in the artistic aspect of the film/production, helping directors create no matter the constraints, veiling the technical aspects to a backstage discussion.

Specialties:
visual effects design and supervision :
working closely with film/art directors to shape their vision
pre-production guidance | on set-location shooting presence | post-production overview

Film compositing :
integrating CG and live beyond theory, I foresee details that are paramount to creating continuity between shots, making looks and moods, building for a smooth film edit.
