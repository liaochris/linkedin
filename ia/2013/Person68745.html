Joseph Melville's Summary

Joseph is a highly-motivated, driven I.T. professional based in Hook, Hampshire; employed as a graduate test analyst with Fivium, a dynamic I.T. company based in Covent Garden, London.

In July 2013 Joseph graduated from the University of Reading with a First Class Honours BSc degree in I.T. with Management.

Joseph is enthusiastic, hard-working and dependable. He is also a fully self-taught web specialist and runs Joseph Melville Freelance Web Design in his spare time, creating professional, sensible and robust web solutions for small and medium-sized businesses across the UK.

In the past Joseph worked on the web and results technologies underpinning the London 2012 Olympics, providing testing assurance on high-profile websites before the Games and managing technology infrastructure during the Games.
