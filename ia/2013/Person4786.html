Allan Martinson's Summary

Technology investor & serial entrepreneur.

Allan is the Managing Partner at MTVP, a technology investment company.

He also manages a portfolio of other technology investments.

Allan has been investing in and managing the largest internet & high-tech success stories in the Baltic states:
- Baltic News Service (after >20 years of existence still the dominant news agency in the region)
- MicroLink (the largest Baltic IT and technology group)
- Delfi (the largest portal in the Baltics)
- SAF Tehnika (the first listed technology company in the Baltics)
- MTVP (the leading technology investment company in the region)
- Cherry Media (the largest daily deals company in the Baltics)

Through MTVP, Allan has invested in the Baltics, Finland, Russia, Ukraine and United States. In addition, he has been also making angel investments and co-founding several internet startups

Specialties

Internet and new media, IT services, software products, mobile applications, telecom services, telecom equipment, business model innovation
