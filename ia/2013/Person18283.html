Bruce Mubayiwa's Summary

If not Africa then where, If not us then who, if not now then when?

I am a Content Marketing Strategist passionate about positive transformation in Africa using LinkedIn, Social media and Technology. My creative and artistic flair is tempered by a basic appreciation for structure and process.

As an Ambassador of Africa I try to promote the continent as much possible . There is incredible talent, resources and potential in Africa. I have a keen interest in helping Africans to inspire Africa. I am involved in various projects to help Africa tell its own story in business and chess.

My focus areas are Content Marketing & LinkedIn Strategies for our corporate clients. As an Official LinkedIn EMEA Talent Solutions Partner I work closely with LinkedIn to understand their Talent Solutions. This enables our clients find the right LinkedIn solution for their Talent strategy.

An accountant by training with a background in governance, risk and capital management, I spent a few years in banking before venturing off into the digital and networking world. I understand the numbers but much prefer talking to people.

I have worked with global brands such as IMEX presenting at the Future Leaders Forum 2013 in Sandton. I have been interviewed on the TV show Good Morning Africa about LinkedIn and Social Media. Good Morning Africa is part of the Africa Magic Channel watched by over 200 million people across the African continent.

Business Expo School 2012 and SME Africa 2012 saw me present a keynote on LinkedIn. I also sat on the Advisory Council of SME Africa 2012. My style of delivery in keynotes is spontaneous and dynamic with minimal use of slides.

My interviews have been featured on Radio and the Destiny Man Magazine talking about LinkedIn, Life and the Power of Social Media.
