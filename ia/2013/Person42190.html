Eric Hill's Summary

I am an experienced design leader and product manager with a record of success in designing and delivering user-centric and cohesive user experiences across mobile and Web platforms and their relevant marketing and sales assets.

Visual/Brand/UI/UX/Game Designer for the app franchise Badly Drawn Faces, featured by Apple in iTunes, October 2012. Badly Drawn Movies was also featured by Apple in iTunes in December, 2012.
