Ken Crotts' Summary

My professional life has been spent pursuing a lifelong passion for real estate. I don't mean just selling homes but everything about them. As a child my erector set was my parents’ house repairing almost everything on it. As a young man I naturally went to work with a builder doing everything from foundations to roofing. Even now I love the smell of freshly turned earth and new lumber on a construction site. Today I spend my off time woodworking, rehabbing properties and doing landscape design.

At age 23 I quit a great job with UPS and got my real estate license. Everyone thought I was nuts but I had to follow my passion. Over the next 22 years I became a top selling Realtor in the South King County market place by working hard, getting the best education on current topics and adjusting my business plan to meet market needs.

Adversity breeds opportunity. We are in the midst of the most adverse market in the past 30 years without a doubt. My goal in this market is to help as many foreclosure and short sale homeowner find solutions while building a constant flow of ideal investment properties for myself and my clients. For the smart investor a unique opportunity exists to acquire cash flowing properties at significant discounts to build a long term wealth portfolio. I am a graduate of the Mike Watson Institute of investing and have extensive training and experience in the acquisition, project management and disposition of investment properties.

This is the most exciting time ever to be in Real Estate!

Specialties

I have developed specific expertise in land development, new construction, residential listings and sales, hard to sell residential properties, investment analysis, foreclosures and short sale properties.
