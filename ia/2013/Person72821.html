Kathleen Warner is a Senior Advisor to the Startup America Partnership/UpGlobal. She was a member of the founding team of the Startup America Partnership and served as its Chief Operating Officer. As COO, Kathleen was responsible for business operations and management of resources. Kathleen launched the Startup America regions initiative in early 2011 and also led a number of the Partnership's strategic activities, including Startup America Women, all focused on heightening engagement with, awareness of and support for high growth startups.
