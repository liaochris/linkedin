Marla Dee's Summary

My Passion is teaching the Clear & SIMPLE way to get and stay organized. I founded Clear & SIMPLE in 1999 to support my clients in simplifying their lives by letting go of the stuff, beliefs, patterns and people that keep them stuck.

I created the Clear & SIMPLE Systems, a step by step approach, to teach the skill of organizing. Since only 10% of the human population is born with the organizing gene, most people need to be taught the skill. The systems teach them where to start, what to do and how to keep it up. They also make organizing fun!

In the last decade the Clear & SIMPLE Way to get and stay organized has spread around the world through our educational product line. In 2007 my audio book, Get Organized, the Clear & Simple Way, was released and is sold internationally, including ITunes and Amazon.

Lastly, in 2003 I developed and have taught the Professional Organizer Training Program. This extensive program gives new organizers everything they need to create and launch a successful organizing business. We have trained students from 8 countries.

Other: I have many years of training and experience in Feng Shui, meditation and energy work. I support people in clutter clearing on all levels!

MY GOAL TODAY is to find a Venture Capitalist or new partner to help me take C&S to the next level. We have a much needed and powerful message.

Specialties

speaker, trainer, published author, organizing consultant and mentor to other entrepreneurs.
