Arjan van der Poel's Summary

I want to help others to become better in what they do. A considerable part of my life I have been doing this in sports (basketball, skiing, scuba diving) and in service management. It thrills me to see how people with ambition can achieve their goals and be successful.

As a management consultant, I feel most related with service organisations. I have experience with service management concepts and processes. I like to assess service organisations and to design and carry out service improvement programs.

As a management trainer I am there to facilitate the learning process, to create the conditions in which development can take place, to stimulate, to present a mirror every now and then, to support in difficult times and to celebrate when there is success.

As a development coach I want to make people and organisations aware of the possibilities of personal development and help to stimulate talent and ambition. I like giving workshops on philosophy, integral psychology and the power of the subconscious.

For me learning is fun. I like to study, to practice. I always encounter interesting topics, that I want to know more about. Or learn new skills. I am often reflecting on my own feelings and behaviour.

And for a long time I have the ambition to do research on a subject that touches upon both personal development, performance of organisations and the rapidly changing society we currently live in.

Besides being Dutch of European, I consider myself a lucky person living on Planet Earth. But from time to time I am worried about the current status of our globe and the consuming behaviour of its inhabitants. What will be left of our planet fifty years from now? What do we leave our children?

Do you want to read more about me? Please visit my personal website (see above).

4. Het verwerven van financiële steun voor lopende en nieuwe projecten.

5. Het bevorderen van alles wat bijdraagt aan de voorgaande doelstellingen.

El Fuego heeft duurzaamheid hoog in het vaandel staan. Zij streeft ernaar blijvende verbeteringen aan te brengen bij de projecten.
