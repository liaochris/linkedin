Candy Kelly's Summary

I am a top-notch REALTOR®, with 17+ years experience serving both buyers and sellers in SW Florida and, more recently, in NE Ohio, as well. I'm proud to represent Keller Williams Chervenic Realty, serving the Greater-Akron and much of the Cleveland area, including Summit, Medina, and parts of Cuyahoga and Wayne Counties.

I have focused my practice primarily on residential consultation and sales with referral services internationally through the combination of the vast Keller Williams Realty international network and that of the national Women's Council of REALTORS®.

Prior to my real estate career, I used 20+ years of expertise and administrative experience at the executive/administrative level to support individuals with intellectual and other developmental disabilities.

That combination of experience led me to diversify my business to include "Candy Speaks Business!," molding her consulting expertise to cover pertinent topics such as:

1) small business consulting, covering topics such as staff training, strategic planning, and business plan development and
2) public speaking on a variety of entrepreneurial and networking subjects.

Specialties: I specialize in real estate sales and consulting for both buyers and sellers, winning REALTOR of the Year awards in Florida. Using my broad international referral base, I pair prospective buyers or sellers with top-notch professionals specializing in properties of interest in the community(s) of their choice.

I have a unique, 20+ years of non-profit expertise assisting individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities to access housing and support services.

Additionally, I consult with individual professionals, small businesses and non-profits on strategic planning, management training, and other pertinent topics to assist their growth and accomplishment as described in her website: www.CandySpeaksBusiness.com.

I am available as a luncheon/dinner speaker for local organizations.
