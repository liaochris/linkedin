Kim Larocque's Summary

Kim is currently leading two companies in the internet marketing space. Her first company, Launch Manager Extreme, works directly with with entrepreneurs and guides them through the process of launching their products and programs online. From start to finish, Kim manages and streamlines the process of launching online including the creation of squeeze and sales pages, email marketing and social media campaigns, media buying and online advertising (including paid advertising), online events (preview calls / telesemiars / webinars), and facilitating the follow up process designed to close more sales.

Her second company, Eagle Eye Media, took off when Kim identified the need of performance based marketing solutions for her clients' launch campaigns. Eagle Eye Media works specifically with entrepreneurs who are trying to develop a brand and reach their target audiences. While performance based marketing strategies are the core of Eagle Eye Media, we also provide comprehensive internet marketing packages in order to suit the needs of our clients. We specialize in lead generation campaigns using PPC marketing campaigns.

Specialties: Product/program launches, internet marketing, PPC advertising, social media marketing, virtual events, and eCommerce.
