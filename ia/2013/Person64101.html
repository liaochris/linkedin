Jim Nolan's Summary

I am an immigration attorney with over 20 years experience with many Japanese & German corporate clients. My goal is to help foreign people live and work in the U.S.

Every 2nd Thursday I offer FREE telephone consultations to answer questions about immigration. You can register in advance at the link above & I look forward to talking to you.

I have appeared on CNN and Bloomberg News television and on many radio programs discussing immigration law and given seminars and talks to many business organizations on immigration topics.

I particularly enjoy helping artists get visas to allow them to perform or exhibit their work and live in the U.S.

On Japan U.S. Creatives, www.japaanuscreatives.com, we have created an on-line place where people interested in Japan & America can find one another and exchange information about Japan and what they are doing. Membership is free and introduces to many fascinating people and events involving Japanese American activities.

Specialties

U.S. immigration law, Japanese-American relations.
