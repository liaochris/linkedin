Matt Conahan's Summary

Strategy, optimization and analytics expert skilled in leading organizations in the collection and use of analytics and testing data. Demonstrated leadership in guiding programs and teams that focus on analytics and optimization. Well versed in working with C-level executives to help them understand the benefits and return on their investments in analytics and optimization programs. Proven ability in helping companies achieve significant wins via:

• Carefully designed/monitored Web analytics and optimization implementations
• Methodical analysis of Web analytics and competitive intelligence data
• Meticulously crafted A/B, multivariate tests and autmated merchandising

Early days involved putting the largest college newspaper in the United States online (before many major metros had a Web presence! ;), helping the Washington Post build their Web presence and then working with a pioneering media start-up in Chicago (U-WIRE).

The "middle" years were spent with AOL helping them build their community publishing products (AOL Hometown and Groups@AOL), helping citizens in a developing country (Belize) build their communities as a Peace Corps Volunteer, and assisting non-profits with their community building needs.

In recent years I've led User Experience Research and Site Optimization for StubHub, an online ticket marketplace, driven optimization and analytics for several media companies, and enjoyed guiding high profile banking, hospitality and automotive company analytics efforts from the agency side.

In each of these roles I found the data related to how people interact with products and communities (offline and online) to be the most intriguing aspect of my work and I continue to enjoy doing so in my career.
