Stacie Clary's Summary

For 24 years I have held management positions in organizations focusing on sustainable agriculture and food systems, environmental, affordable housing/homelessness and HIV/AIDS issues.

I am a certified coach who works with those wishing to develop their leadership skills, grow professionally and enjoy their work as they promote social change.

I also contract with organizations on communications and outreach, meeting facilitation and event management.

Specialties:
• Strong team leader who works collaboratively to identify and meet specific goals and objectives
• Goal-driven and detail-oriented
• Effective communicator with facilitation experience and training in coaching/mentoring
• Experience organizing conferences and networking events
• Capable of implementing successful communication, outreach and advocacy efforts
