Annie Strack's Summary

Growing up in her family’s hotel and restaurant businesses, Annie learned the ins and outs of the hospitality trade at an early age. She worked her way through college by waiting tables and tending bar, and in the off-season she satisfied her adventurous streak by working as a Game Warden and a Wildfire Fighter. Later, her husband’s career in the National Park Service led to moves throughout the country and overseas, while she continued to move up the corporate ladder in the hospitality industry. When she lived on Guam, she seized upon an opportunity to change careers and manage a retail business, and she traveled extensively throughout Asia as a buyer.

When she left the corporate world to become a full-time, professional artist, she used her decades of international business experience to quickly propel her art career to the top. In addition to being a successful best-selling artist, Annie shares her vast knowledge and experience through her books, classes, and DVDs to help other artists reach their goals.

Specialties: Art Business, Art Marketing, Art Writing, Grant Writing, Watercolor Painting, Maritime and Seascape Painting, Landscape Painting, Architectural Rendering, Art Instruction, Art Photography, Art Business and Marketing Consultation
