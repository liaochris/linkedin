Dave Wellman's Summary

I started my first business in 1987 as a home-based design and layout company. I was the Pastor of a church in Park City, Utah at the time and owning a business that I could run from home made more sense than working for a boss on their schedule.

That first business led me into the world of entrepreneurship and I have been here every since. (Which is kind of an odd position for a Pastor, especially among my peers!)

I have been successful in some businesses and not so successful in others (I'm told by the experts I'm not supposed to talk about my failures.)In both cases I have learned valuable lessons in both that I know I can pass on to you.

Success is an interesting term (one best defined individually) and my job, my goal, and my plan is to help you define it and then help you design it!

If you are looking for a slick presentation and lots of charts and graphs don't hire me. But, if you want a life-style design coach, someone who will work with you to discover, design, and deliver success around your life-purpose, it's worth a cup of coffee to talk!

Specialties

Life-style design, home-business, entrepreneurship, micro-business start up, inbound marketing, success design and delivery

Over the past few years I have become increasing concerned about the whole economic system. It has been over-balanced toward the large multinational corporations. Local business has had to take a back seat to these large conglomerations and, in my mind that is a bad thing!

The Wellman Group, Inc. was created to help entrepreneurs Design Success. In order to do this, we take time with our clients to discover what success means to them, then to design a life-style business around that discovery, and finally to deliver that definition of success by providing a strong business model for both them and their customers.

At The Wellman Group, Inc. we believe that your business should support and enhance your life purpose, not BE your life purpose!
