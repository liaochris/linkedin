Allan Ross' Summary

A.J. Ross Creative Media was founded in 1991 by Chairman and CEO Allan J. Ross. Mr. Ross’ career in advertising spans over 30 years. Mr. Ross began his career as a musician, playing saxophone for corporate jingles, music scores and commercials. Allan has gold records for The Spinners, Evelyn King, Diana Ross amongst other artists.

His love of music resulted in the creation of a successful music production company AJR Music whose client list included renowned corporations such as Gallo Wine, NBC Sports, Crystal Light, Friehofer's,Turner Classic Moviesand CNN. As Mr. Ross' successes grew, so did the demand for additional services.

As a result, the music production company became a division of what is now a full-service marketing and advertising agency. The Agency has experience in a variety of industries including health care, real estate, senior housing, corporate and professional services. AJ Ross's goal is to do the finest and most effective work for its clients success.

Specialties

Branding, packaging, new media, promotion and marketing creative solutions for print and web, media planning, Public Relations and media buying.
