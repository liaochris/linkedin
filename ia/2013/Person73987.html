Kellyn Flanagan's Summary

As a Human/Computer Interaction professional, I am a human-centered designer and user advocate who comfortably resides at the ever shifting intersection between art, technology, psychology, and digital strategy. I push the boundaries of human and technology interaction, while giving users an individualized and positive digital experience.

I have the rare ability to be analytical and creative at the same time, which is why Information Architecture, HCI and UX Design has been such a successful fit for me. I dedicate myself to fully understanding every aspect of a business and its users, and then finding the fine balance between accomplishing business objectives and meeting as many user needs as possible. As a practicing front-end developer, I also have a rare ability to communicate business objectives with technical teams and understand technical abilities and limitations.

Design is not just simply making things look good, but understanding the psychology and human factors behind why people gravitate towards certain design aspects or interfaces, and how business goals are accomplished through design.

As an UX Architect, I have worked in the following verticals: Financial, Automotive, Beverage, Petroleum, Telecommunications, Electric/Gas, Hospitality, Non-Profit, Pharmaceuticals, Veterinary Pharmaceuticals, Education, Environmental/Green Products, Cosmetics, and Security.
