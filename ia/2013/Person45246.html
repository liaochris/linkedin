Randy Austad's Summary

At www.FollowYourCalling.com, we provide web-based tools to help people discover who they are, what their life purpose is and where they can find their greatest career satisfaction. Our primary tool is The CALL that we originally created for Focus on the Family.

We also train coaches, churches and ministries.

Specialties

I have been trained and worked with hundreds of assessment tools. I am a frequent speaker/trainer and writer on spiritual gifts, job fit and employment testing. I have a wonderful relationship with Profiles International and Focus on the Family.
