PR, Marketing and Strategy – Through storytelling and social media innovation

We work within the nonprofit and start-up business sector in technology, training, evaluation, fundraising and marketing both on and offline. Helping businesses launch or campaign driven launches by helping them to manage technology to meet their mission.
