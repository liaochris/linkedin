Adam Prochaska's Summary

Adam is a shareholder at Harding & Shultz, and concentrates his practice in the areas of civil and business litigation. He has represented both individuals and businesses in a wide variety of civil matters including discrimination, retaliation, personal injury, and contractual cases. In addition, Adam also has significant experience and knowledge in First Amendment matters involving both library and school litigation.

Adam has tried or co-tried more than ten trials, and has been involved in multiple arbitrations before the American Arbitration Association. Adam has been selected to speak on a variety of topics including both First Amendment and personnel issues.

Specialties

Civil & Business Litigation, Labor & Employment, Personal Injury, Insurance, Products Liability, and Library Law.
