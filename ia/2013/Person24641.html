Christy Geiger's Summary

As a business trainer and coach for Synergy Strategies, I am a strategic partner for movers and shakers who are responsible for performance and bottom line results to:
- maximize vision, ideas, and efforts
- develop cohesive plans and effective strategies to save time, energy and money
- use their gifts and talents to work productively in a job they love and are passionate about

With a background in counseling and HR, I understand people well. My vision is to help others see and achieve their vision. On going training and certifications (DISC profile administrator, CTI Coach, Guerilla Marketing Coach) has armed me with a great tool kit to support my clients with resources to advance company performance and personal success.

To understand more, go to www.synergystrategies.com, explore the free tools, sign up for Business Strategy E-zine or request a FREE Strategy Session.

Specialties: Leaders - Executives, Businesses Owners, Managers & Independent Professionals (people responsible for goals and their bottom line, i.e. CEO/Pres, COO, sales agents, real estate agents, home based businesses, insurance and financial planners, writers, freelance contractors, business start-ups, entrepreneurs, pastors, etc.) OR Career Seekers (those looking to select or transition to an job/industry that is rewarding, using their gifts/skills and is a great fit with the life they want to design).

Christy Geiger's Experience

Our coaching and training programs challenge people to think big, use their talents and maximize their skills to live successful and passionate lives. Outpatient Therapist

October 2001 – September 2006 (5 years)

Worked part-time as an in-home family and youth counseling agency as Outpatient Therapist to counsel with children who were dealing with different behavioral and learning issues (ADD/ADHD, oppositional defiance, anger management, borderline personality disorder, depression, etc.) and adults who desired improvement in their emotional and behavioral health. Responsibilities included assessment of client, working with them on a weekly or bi-weekly basis, setting goals for focus and improvement, developing a treatment plan, monitoring plan, writing case notes, paperwork, treatment team meetings and overall working with client to in creative, therapeutic and direct ways to identify patterns, address negative habits/beliefs in order to enforce healthy thoughts/beliefs and implement new structures to accomplish treatment and personal goals.
