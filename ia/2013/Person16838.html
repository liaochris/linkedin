Brenden Wright's Summary

Great opportunities, career advancements, and successful connections most often result from good timing, coincidence, and who you know.

Brenden is an executive search consultant with more than a dozen years of proven success within the semiconductor capital equipment, high tech, pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. Brenden’s recruiting experience also includes leading the global talent acquisition functions for public, not-for-profit, and privately held organizations. His recruiting and selection expertise spans multiple functional areas and focuses on corporate officers, executives, and highly specialized scientific and engineering technical contributors.

Before beginning his career in executive search and talent acquisition, Brenden served in the US Marine Corps as a Nuclear, Biological, and Chemical Defense Specialist. He was promoted meritoriously twice and was honorably discharged at the rank of Sergeant. Brenden received his MBA with a concentration in management from Loyola University Maryland and he is a lifetime member of International Honor Society Beta Gamma Sigma. He began his undergraduate studies while serving on active duty and completed that work at the University of Phoenix, receiving a BS in Business Management.

Brenden currently resides in the Washington DC Metro area with his wife, three children, and two dogs.
