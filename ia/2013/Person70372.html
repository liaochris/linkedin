Judy Brosky, The Moxie Mentor's Summary

MOXIE is an attitude. It's where savvy, committed, smart, and highly-skilled meet spunky, shameless, gutsy, and brazen in juuuuuust the right proportions.
The Moxie Mentor empowers Female Financial Advisors find their Moxie, sell like a woman, enjoy the process, and make more money. How lucky are you to have stumbled upon this!
As a result you achieve more success in this male dominated industry. By my calculations, 99.9% of FFAs were trained by a man, to sell to a man, like a man...and then the men are baffled when we women don't succeed. We need the tools to help us sell intuitively. We embrace a different style of selling, consultative and holistic, and then when we add a little Moxie...we soar to previously unimaginable heights!!
Isn't it amazing that science finally has proof that our brains don't work like a man's brain? Geez, we could have told them that without the scientific studies.

Selling my practice and retiring after 27 years as an FFA, I spent over half of those years as an independent advisor, working with another equally moxified woman advisor as my business partner. Our client base consisted of business owners and high net worth individuals. She and her son still work the business.
I decided to hang up my licenses and have some fun while I mentor existing female financial advisors on how to achieve even greater success in this male dominated industry. With the tools, scripts and confidence to approach this career like a woman, you begin to awaken your sleeping Moxie attitude. It's there...even tho you may have let it go into a deep sleep. It's time to schedule your wake up call. Discover new ways to make your business soar. Position yourself as the Expert.

Specialty: Targeted solutions for FFAs to fit your current life stage, move your career to the next level, have your ideal clients seek you out as an expert, make more money, and have more time and freedom to enjoy life. What a terrific life you can have!
