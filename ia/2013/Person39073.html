Daavi Zain's Summary

I am a seasoned marketer with particular expertise in analytics and optimization. I began my career as an engineer, and after earning an MBA in marketing, have found a way to marry those worlds. My background includes day-to-day management of an $80mil business, building and executing advertising campaigns in all forms of media for Fortune 500 companies, and measuring & optimizing countless online/offline marketing initiatives. I have the added experience of having managed all advertising channels simultaneously, giving me a real appreciation for their interplay and synergies. Currently, I consult with a variety of companies to improve and grow their businesses through sound, measurable tactics.

I have been doing analytics work for over 10 years, and have enjoyed watching the industry evolve during that time. Projects I worked on in the early days like attribution analyses and media-mix modeling were all done manually the hard way. Today, there are many tools out there for the modern day analytics manager, and some of these are great. But having slugged it out in the trenches early on benefits me every day. I love this work, as it continues to challenge me with increasing sophistication. And I get the pleasure of helping companies learn and grow.

Specialties: Marketing analytics & optimization, multi-channel marketing, acquisition/retention marketing, fractional attribution, media-mix modeling, Direct Response and ROI-based advertising (DRTV, Print, Radio, SEM, Affiliate, Display, Email, Direct Mail), Web analytics (Google Analytics Qualified, Omniture Site Catalyst, WebTrends), call center analytics, A/B & multi-variate testing, Big Data, SalesForce.com, database marketing, segmentation, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), product management, P&L management, forecasting.
