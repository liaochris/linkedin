    We are a group of entrepreneurs who know that the millions of small businesses are run from homes all across the world. Many of the home business owners are running it while doing a full time or part time job hoping to make it big enough to quit their jobs and become a self sustaining business. Hoping to pay themselves a paycheck that is at least equal or greater than the paycheck they receive going out to work for someone else. The problem is most don’t make it. Their home business can give them a little extra money but it won’t be enough to have them quit their job. The reason is they don’t have the time, staff, knowledge or resources. From the mechanic who wants to be a network marketer selling coffee to the banker who has invented a gadget to revolutionize outdoor cooking to the Mary Kay beauty consultant to the part time personal trainer/realtor. These are people with real dreams but need help to bring it to reality.

    If they had a staff of people to do some of the business development, marketing, customer service and other important aspects of the business it may have a better chance. Now we are talking about an operation which includes an office, and equipment and staff . That doesn’t fit the model because as someone who is just starting out, we don’t have the money for that kind of operation. What if I told you we can do it all without the office and the equipment? You may say, “ okay but we still have to hire people and they will be hard to find and costly and where will they work?” That’s where we can help.
