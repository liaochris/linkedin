Alain Mayrand's Summary

Composer of film and concert music.

Scored for the Jackie Chan film “The Legend of Silk Boy” which was a top rated Critics Choice soundtrack for that year.

Orchestrator and conductor on the Neill Bloomkamp film "Elysium" starring Matt Damon and Jodie Foster. Orchestrator on "Ender's Game" starring Harrison Ford.

Named Composer to Watch by film music magazine.

Composer-in-residence with the Vancouver Metropolitan Orchestra.
