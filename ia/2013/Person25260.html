Claire Dickson's Summary

My Coaching Focus: Getting individuals "unstuck" - be it in a work, career or team situation - re-focussed, re-energised and performing, in these times of unprecedented organizational change and complexity.

My Clients: Clients are international, across all functions and from a range of sectors: banking, manufacturing, retail, FMCG, telecommunications, logistics, pharma, consulting and European business schools.

I work with senior managers, next generation leaders and their teams to help them discover what is possible, personally and for the business and to realize their goals and sustain the change.


My Background: An experienced HR Director, operating at senior management level, with a proven track record of delivering business results in a diverse range of markets and in matrix multinational companies, for 30 years.

Having operated at Director level for 15 years with significant people leadership responsibilities, I understand the pressures and challenges facing leaders. I have a comprehensive understanding of leadership development, soft skills development and organizational change, combining both the strategic and practical application.


Both in my corporate roles and as a coach, I have a track record of developing and delivering coaching and training interventions that are what the business/individual need, that work, last and of building high levels of trust and respect wherever I have worked .

Additionally, have deep expertise in the area of career transition and outplacement, having worked through 2 major economic down turns. Know how to manage and leverage practically "Brand Me" in tough times.

.
My Style: Described as being encouraging, inspiring, challenging and stimulating, with an open and engaging style.Gets results!



My Motto: My experience, intuition and authenticity help people get "unstuck" to realize their hopes and conquer their fears. Come and get your backpack, filled with "goodies" to sustain you on the journey!
