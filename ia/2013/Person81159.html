Lindsay Brown's Summary

Lindsay was born in Stamford and raised in Norwalk and New Canaan. She attended New Canaan High School, and was a member of New Canaan Mounted Troop for over 10 years. She graduated from Gettysburg College in Gettysburg, PA with a BA degree in Business Management, and minors in both Visual Arts and Art History. She also studied abroad in Florence, Italy.

Before relocating to Stamford and joining The Higgins Group, Lindsay was a Leasing Representative for Subway's real estate division at its World Headquarters in Milford. Previous to that, she worked for News America Marketing, a News Corporation company. She also is currently an independent stylist for Stella and Dot jewelry.

She enjoys skiing, horseback riding, photography, painting, traveling, and spending time with her rescued dog Lilly.

She will go the extra mile to make sure that your needs are fulfilled, and provides excellent customer service. She is passionate, honest, creative, and trustworthy.

Member of the Stamford Board of Realtors, Consolidated Multiple Listing Service and the National Association of Realtors.
