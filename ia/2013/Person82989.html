Luca Lisci's Summary

Luca Lisci is born in Milan, 1970. Visionary and creative mind fed by humanities, attended high school of informatics then university faculty of architecture: a route leading to experiment with different approaches and techniques from programming languages to drawing and painting, three-dimension computer graphics to sculpture, photography and videomaking to virtual art. Since then, while working in the field of visual, media and concept design, Luca followed his avant-guard art research exhibiting as digital artist, often partecipating in performing arts events. Some include: Zaragoza, Spain at Expo in 2008 - Rome Italy at Teatro Nazionale in 2009, Tempio di Adriano in 2005, Ara Pacis in 2009 - Reggio Emilia Teatro Regio - Valli- in 2007, - Santander, Spain, Milan, Italy at Teatro Arcimboldi in 2007, Hong Kong, China at Kwai Tsing Theatre in 2010, Macao, China, São Paulo, Brazil at File Festival in 2010. Using interaction design as a method, he has been involved in media project teams as Art and Creative director, designing several media projects and brand communication strategies for globals like TAR magazine, Ferrari, Salvatore Ferragamo, London Stock Exchange, DIAGEO.
