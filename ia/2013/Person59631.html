Jasper van Orden's Experience

Partner / Creative director

Rodesk

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Design industry

January 2012 – Present (1 year 11 months) Rotterdam Area, Netherlands

Rodesk is a creative interaction agency from Rotterdam which I started with my partner Laurens Boex in 2011. We believe that conversation between people and brands will increase the involvement of the audience and creates meaningful interaction. Unilateral communication does no longer exist in a changing world where social media and customer engagement are critical for organizations.

We strive to make the internet a more interesting place by developing innovative identities, concepts, websites, apps, data visualizations and other interactive marketing means. Our goal is to become the authority in User eXperience strategy & design.
