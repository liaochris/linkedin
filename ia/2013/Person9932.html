Art Hill's Summary

I am an Addy award winning Art Director and Web Designer, with years of experience. I have a strong passion for design and with my dynamic ability in several web based applications I am the ideal candidate for your position..

I pride myself by continually studying both the latest design techniques and marketing trends. This helps me offer clients a wide range of looks, and strategic approaches. I am proficient in all of Adobe Creative Suite, HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Actionscript, JQuery, Javascript, Flash, Viral Marketing, Website Content management systems and many more.
