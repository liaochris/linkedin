Hans Wissema's Experience

Managing Director

Wissema Consulting EOOD

1986 – Present (27 years) Sofia, Bulgaria and The Hague, the Netherlands

Wissema Consulting advises governments on Innovation Policy and Higher Education Policy and enterprises on strategy and organisation. In addition, we offer mentoring services to individuals. We published the book Towards the Third Generation University (Edward Elgar Publishing) recently and are preparing new texts. In Wissema Consulting I continue activities carried out earlier in The Wissema Group in the Netherlands.
