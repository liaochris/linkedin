Joy Bonham's Summary

Who do we be? Where do we be? What do we be? How do we be? Why do we be?

First and foremost, I am an Ontologist. I study the nature of be-ing and the essence of life from a …
• scientific perspective: analyzing context, methods, properties, and attributes that define reality.
• creative perspective: exploring our unique instincts and child-like wonder.
• humanistic perspective: embracing our emotions, culture, nature, friendships, and community.
• spiritual perspective: gaining wisdom from immanent and transcendent experiences.
• coaching perspective: grounding our experiences in methodologies and best practices.
• psychological perspective: bringing the many aspects of self into balance and alignment.

I am also an entrepreneur, small business owner, software engineer, and webmaster.
I am an author, educator, coach and spiritual guide.
I am a fifth-generation Californian and love laughter on a warm sunny day.
Finally, I live in perpetually gratitude for the gift of life and mindful of how short it really is.
