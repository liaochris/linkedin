Ana Carolina Lanuza's Summary

Ana Carolina Lanuza is the owner of Leverage Investigations, Inc. She is a bilingual Licensed Florida Private Investigator with experience in criminal defense, background investigation, missing persons- especially children, skip-tracing, infidelity, surveillance & ID Theft (Protection, Detection, Restoration and Investigation). Her goal is to grow Leverage Investigations, Inc. into an internationally well-known private investigation agency. She was elected the President of the Florida Association of Private Investigators (FAPI) for 2012/2013. She served as Board Director for FAPI (Palm Beach, Broward & Miami-Dade areas) 2011/2012. She is currently studying for the CFE credential.
