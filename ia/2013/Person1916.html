Christopher Ruigrok's Experience

Operations Manager

A.D.R Bulbs, Inc

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Wholesale industry

January 2005 – Present (8 years 8 months)

A.D.R Bulbs, Inc has over 65 years of experience wholesaling and marketing Dutch flower bulbs in the USA and Canada. Our bulb farms, C.J Ruigrok & Zonen (founded in 1910), are one of the oldest and largest flower bulb growers and exporters in Holland today with fields spanning nearly 200 acres in the heart of the Dutch flower bulb district. C.J Ruigrok & Zonen is one of the few remaining companies that keep the entire flower bulb process in house (growing, exporting, wholesaling). All our bulbs are inspected in Holland with state of the art equipment and stored in optimum conditions and facilities on both sides of the ocean. A.D.R Bulbs deals only in bulbs of the highest quality and size. During the fall and spring bulb season we stock an extensive selection of tulips, daffodils, hyacinths, paperwhites, crocus, amaryllis, alliums and many other products in our southern New York warehouse. In addition, we offer same day shipping on all orders received during the fall and spring seasons! A.D.R Bulbs, Inc is a family owned and operated business currently run by Walter, Simon and Chris Ruigrok.
