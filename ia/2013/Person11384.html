Baba has presented in, literally, hundreds of schools and other institutions both private and public all over the world. He is recipient of a certificate of recognition by the California State Assembly for his use of creative use of storytelling and commitment to community in working with troubled teens. Baba was also awarded the Distinguished Arts Award, Artist of the Year, City of Long Beach California. This awarded was presented to Baba by the Mayor of the City of Long Beach California and administered by The Public Corporation for the Arts. Baba has also received commendations for his artistry from both the US Senate and US Congress.

Baba the Storyteller is currently touring countries in South America sharing his unique brand of musical storytelling.

Musician & Technology

Griot's Voice Records

1994 – Present (19 years) ubiquitous touring

Baba the Storyteller delights audiences the world over with his incredible mastery of the ancient 21-string West African Harp known as the Kora. Baba recently released his debut CD "Ancestral Strings Acoustic Kora" on the Griot's Voice Record label.

Essayist and Feature Writer

Seminar & Workshop Host

Baba the Storyteller

1994 – Present (19 years) The World Over

Baba the Storyteller hosts workshops, seminars and professional development for educators of all levels. Specializing in instituting the Art of Storytelling in classroom management and curriculum instruction, Baba has received accolades from educational institutions all over the world (contact for International Letters of Reference).
