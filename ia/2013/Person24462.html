Christopher Latragna's Summary

For the first half of my career, I lived and breathed video production. I rarely spent a weekend without a camera on my shoulder, and the rest of the week ripping that footage apart and piecing it back together.

For the last several years, I've found happiness combining these video skills with motion graphics and interactive design. If it moves on a screen, be it a computer monitor, TV screen, or a trade show proscenium, then I can make it.
