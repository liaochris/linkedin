Jon Mueller's Summary

My aim is to encourage people to listen and communicate.

I am the General Manager of 800ceoread, a business book company that connects people and ideas. We run 800ceoread.com, ChangeThis.com, KnowledgeBlocks.com, the LeaveSmarter event series, and a variety of other information resources for people looking to gather or spread good ideas.

I’ve spoken about creativity and new business approaches at Loyola University (NOLA), Viva! Festival (Montreal), Author Pow Wow (Austin), Weslyan University (Middleton, CT),WMSE’s Radio Summercamp (Milwaukee), PRSA (Milwaukee), and MARN (Milwaukee).

As a musician, I’ve performed throughout North America, United Kingdom, Europe, and Japan at places such as New Museum (New York), The Arnolfini (Bristol, UK), Musée des Beaux-Arts de Montréal, (Montréal, QC), and the Guggenheim Museum (New York).

My writing has appeared in Pear Noir, ChangeThis, The Shepherd Express, OnMilwaukee, Amoeba Records Hollywood, and I also contributed to "The 100 Best Business Books of All Time" (Penguin/Portfolio) by Jack Covert and Todd Sattersten.

Specialties: listening, team development and management, marketing communications and strategy, social media strategy, product development, event creation and marketing, project management
