Kevin Graham's Summary

Kevin Graham is a professional, high-energy, focused sales executive who thrives on working with clients to improve sales effectiveness.

IBM ECM delivers high-value solutions that can help companies transform the way they do business by enabling them to put content in motion: capturing, activating, socializing, analyzing and governing it throughout the entire lifecycle.

Empowered Sales offers custom programs for sales success, sales consulting and professional speaking. We leverage deep personal experience, gems from industry icons, classic sales methodologies and innovative practices to empower client sales organizations.

Highlights from Kevin Graham's sales career include:
*President's Club status in three Fortune 500's
*Successfully achieved revenues of $1B+ per year
*Led groups of more than 100 associates

Two key areas Empowered Sales helps companies:
1) Solving voids in the sales process
2) Recommending pivots based on changing market conditions

An award-winning author, Kevin is a contributor to NSA's latest book, Speak More!, and has been published in Consulting Magazine, Selling Power Magazine, Agency Sales Magazine, the Business Journal and other print and radio segments.

Kevin serves as President of the National Speakers Association's Greater Los Angeles Chaper and is actively involved with several other local non-profit organizations.

Formerly, Kevin held a Series 7 (Full Stockbroker Registration) and has been licensed as a California Real Estate Broker for over 20 years.
