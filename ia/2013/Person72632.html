Kate Fitzsimmons' Summary

"I believe that crafting stories that effectively communicate the heart, vision and mission of an organization is second only to the important work done in service to that mission. To be truly successful, communication efforts require acceptance and support from the entire organization. The story comes from the people engaged in the work – whether it is the person answering the phone, the board member attending a community luncheon or every single person in between – especially those people who change their lives because the organization helped them gain the tools to make it happen.The craft is in telling a story in a way that motivates people to act."
