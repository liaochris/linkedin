Co-Founder & COO

Sport195 Inc

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Online Media industry

March 2012 – Present (1 year 6 months) New York, NY

Sport195 is a Global Social Network for Sport

Our mission is to empower and unite people globally through sports.

There are three pillars to Sport195:

￼For the Fan - Provides the fan one platform to follow and engage all of their sports
￼interests from anywhere in the world while providing them the ability to engage with the
￼content and others through sharing images, video, making comments, etc.

￼For the Athlete - Allows an athlete, regardless of level, whether youth or professional,
￼to build a profile specific to sports showcasing their athletic involvement and
￼accomplishments.

Providing Digital Infrastructure - Provides a sports infrastructure solution for schools,
clubs, teams, leagues, countries and local communities to manage their sports within
one environment. Providing a platform for game recaps, schedules, scores, pictures,
game video etc.

Co-Founder and Vice President of Business Development

New Media Strategies

Public Company; 51-200 employees; MDP; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 2000 – March 2012 (12 years 3 months)

As a Co-Founder of New Media Strategies (NMS), Aaron helped pioneer the social media marketing and measurement industry, and has helped NMS grow to one of the largest social media agencies in the world. Aaron has generated relationships with top brands and industry leaders while providing corporate strategy to ensure NMS continues to lead the ever evolving social media marketplace, including developing the first methodology for tracking and measuring social media in 2000, which became norms in the industry.
