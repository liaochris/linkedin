Leigh Fazzina's Summary

So I'm a seasoned healthcare PR pro with 19 years experience in communications development, management, strategic planning, program implementation and measurement. I've worked on the pharma, hospital, clinical trials/patient recruitment, medical society & agency sides identifying comms/PR opps. & developing & implementing a broad range of strategies & programs that raise awareness, create a positive image, & strengthen a brand. I’m an innovative strategist with a record of award-winning initiatives & programs (read about below).

While I was proficient (& passionate) in working with the news media (& believe this work is truly an art), my work in the comm arena naturally segued into the social media space over the past 7 years. I believe strongly that social media communications is most effective if drawn from a media relations background. I was one of the first to put a national healthcare social media communications program in place, & I'm now developing & managing such programs that work in tandem with comprehensive communications/media relations programs.

Volunteer? Yep!
•. I've held a leadership role w/ the PRSA Health Academy’s Exec Committee since 2003. I’ve been a driving force for the healthcare communications industry for many years always giving 110% to advance the industry & the profession. I'm the 2012 President.

• I’m a Sr. Judge of the PRSA Bronze Anvil Awards/ Judge of the Silver Anvil Awards. A great way to learn what makes for winning, mediocre, & weak entries.

• I’m a prof. mentor to young PR pros of PRSA. Young pros seek advice all the time & need our help. Several pros helped me along the way & I believe in “paying it forward.”

Lastly, I believe the work I do as a healthcare comm. pro helps people live healthier lives & this work truly moves me (…& I love teaching it to others). While I am passionate about my work, focused on my career, and work hard - I only work to live, not live to work. I know how to relax & play hard. Life is good.

Specialties

Social media communications: providing education, auditing, strategizing, planning, implementing, monitoring, and measuring. Media relations, broadcast communications, media training, communications strategy development and implementation, crisis/advocacy communications, brand communications, event management, & multicultural communications.
