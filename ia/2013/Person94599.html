Mike Sorola's Summary

Picture = 1,000 words
Video = 24,000+ words per second

I’m a multimedia producer and digital media professional. I was known for two things as a kid: being the class clown – or morale leader, as I called it, and being the best storyteller. Not much has changed since then. I still tell stories.

With the web moving so fast, I utilize a combination of marketing, communications, social media strategies, and adult education principles coupled with multimedia channels such as written articles, audio, photos, and video to create an experience for audiences and target demographics. From content conception and development through distribution, I research and identify the best approach to have the most impact. My experience and workflow translates to media with a high production value, more traffic, and ROI.

No one-man band here. My time in Marines has taught me how to work and move as a unit, aka teamwork. It also taught me how to be resourceful, remain task-oriented, and master execution. Startup or just fast-paced, I adapt quickly, contribute early, and support the overall goal with attention to detail.

We should get in touch. If there’s anything I can do for you or if you just want to chat about tech, trends, comedy, or media, send me an email at mike@mikesorola.com.
