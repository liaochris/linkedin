Joberate Inc. - Talent Pipeline Solutions

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Human Resources industry

May 2010 – Present (3 years 8 months) San Francisco Bay Area

Joberate was created by recruiters for recruiters.

We help companies across the world to attract the best talent. Our adaptive offering is designed to meet the changing needs of our clients, which allows us to operate as a service provider or project-based consulting partner or as a full-service talent attraction outsourcing provider. Our constantly developing product and service offering is a combination of integrated marketing and sales tools designed purely for recruitment. We have already helped our clients in +50 countries, so we have a deep understanding of the different cultures and various industries. We are not a media or a marketing agency as our focus is not to create fancy campaigns, but to help you to make your recruitment process work better as a whole. We believe that the people and therefore recruiting the right talent is the most important part of the success of any company, and our dedication is to help you to succeed in it.
