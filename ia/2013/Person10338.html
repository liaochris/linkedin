Ashley Smith's Summary

Innovation. Leadership. Smarts. Service.

Embarking on my 6th year as a REALTOR®, I am determined to foster my personal professional development. I am constantly engaged in further education, city transit and community planning workshops, marketing and social media skill-building courses, as well as community and career-minded networking events.

My goal is to build as many quality relationships as I can. I admire connectors, those who bring the right people together to make something amazing happen. I strive to be one of these people.

Particularly interested in neighbourhood planning and sustainable growth, I take pride in staying in the know with new and future development, and am skilled at pin-pointing opportunities for investment, building, and property innovation.

Social media and marketing are integral aspects of my business. The ability to build and seek community (whether neighbourhood or industry), has offered me a unique way to enhance the exposure of my listings and projects, not only by showcasing them, but also by making great new connections with others who have an expanded network.

I have produced and taught social media for real estate courses for new REALTORS® at our 3 main Vancouver-based Royal LePage offices, as well as been a keynote speaker for a Real Estate Board of Greater Vancouver members' educational event, "Social Media 101".

I currently sit on the Real Estate Board of Greater Vancouver's Board of Director's table and also serve as a Director with the Westside Division of REBGV.

As a past Director-at-Large, I participated in developing the strategic direction of our board (11,000+ members). As a Geographical Director, I look to foster and promote professionalism and leadership, encourage succession planning, link members, provide valuable educational and social events, and to be involved in local industry matters, including City of Vancouver government relations (areas such as zoning).
