M. Cory Burgess' Summary

With 16 years of experience with graphic and web design (more than 11 of which has been spent in newspaper and magazine publishing), I started my own company, Metroscape Media, in late 2010 to bring my creative skills to the companies that need to visually make a professional impression.

My specialties include:
• Publication design (covers and editorial)
• Logo design
• Advertising and promotional design
• Website design
• Copyediting

M. Cory Burgess' Experience

Owner

Metroscape Media

December 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months)

As owner of Metroscape Media, I am a freelance print and web designer with two ongoing clients (The GLCCB in Baltimore and The Center in Las Vegas) while also designing websites, logos and marketing materials for other companies and organizations from time to time. I also have a few ideas up my sleeves for my own web projects I hope to launch in 2013.

Art Director and Webmaster

The GLCCB

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

October 2011 – Present (2 years 1 month) Baltimore, MD

Following a successful redesign of the Baltimore Pride website in 2011, I was asked to redesign The GLCCB's two other websites and serve as Art Director for the bi-weekly Gay Life newspaper, which became the monthly Gay Life magazine in June 2012. Additionally, I designed the first Baltimore LGBT Visitors Guide, co-published by The GLCCB and Visit Baltimore in April 2012.

Webmaster

The Gay & Lesbian Community Center of Southern Nevada

August 2007 – Present (6 years 3 months)

Since 2007, I have maintained The Center's website, which I completely redesigned in 2007 and 2012. Throughout each year, I have also designed numerous marketing materials such as fundraising packets and Honorarium promotional pieces.

Editor/Creative Director

Stonewall Publishing, Inc.

March 2004 – December 2010 (6 years 10 months) Las Vegas, NV

Initially hired as a graphic designer focusing on print ads for the Las Vegas Bugle just prior to it becoming QVegas magazine, I was quickly promoted through the ranks to become Art Director, Assistant Editor and then Creative Director in May 2007, with a further promotion to Editor beginning in October 2007. Throughout the years, I designed countless advertisements and the majority of covers and editorial layouts for QVegas, Out Las Vegas, The Findlay Voice, Findlay Vision, Windermere Homes & Living, and Anthem Country Club magazines as well as several annual non-profit event programs. As Editor, I additionally planned QVegas content, determined ad placement, copyedited content, and photographed subjects for covers and articles when necessary. I additionally designed and maintained QVegas.com, updating it monthly with new content.

Advertising Designer

The Tullahoma News

April 2003 – February 2004 (11 months)

As an Advertising Designer, I designed camera-ready ads for five area newspapers published each week by Lakeway Publishers, owners of The Tullahoma News.

Advertising & Publications Designer

The Tullahoma News

September 1996 – February 1999 (2 years 6 months) Tullahoma, TN

In 1996, I began my career in the publishing industry by hand-building camera-ready newspaper layouts using waxing/cutting/pasting methods. Over the next couple years, my duties switched to computer-assisted creation of print ads for five area newspapers and layout designs for Lakeway Publishers' special publications, The Fundlander and Fact-Finder.
