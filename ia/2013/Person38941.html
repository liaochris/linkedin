Dylan White's Summary

Dylan is currently living in La Quinta California looking for bands, models, and public figures to shoot and take to the next level with iconic images that he can provide, and which is an essential element in becoming the next superstars in our culture. His parents are professionals in the business and he’s grown up around the arts his whole life. Dylan has the training, and the talent, to “make magic” through the medium of photography and as a photographer he looks for beauty, mystery and the bitter sweet parts of life that sometimes go unnoticed. The undertones of human emotion is what makes a photograph fantastic and Dylan strives to achieve that amount of detail and passion in every photograph he captures. Dylan’s vision is to impact the viewer and to capture the truth about what means to be a human being through the medium of photography.
