Eric C. Mueller's Experience

Managing Partner

ECI Business Solutions

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Financial Services industry

January 2013 – Present (1 year 1 month) Scottsdale, Arizona

Small Business Consulting Services. ECI Business Solutions is the consulting arm of Electronic Commerce International, an industry leader since 2000 in the field of merchant processing and financial services. ECI Business Solutions is a full services consulting firm. ECI Consultants focus on reliable sources of capital infusion to assist entrepreneurs with the marketing, financial, operational and other costs of running a successful business. Unlike traditional banks that rely on the personal credit and collateral of the business owner, ECI's banking partners look at the history and performance of the business itself as a primary area for consideration. Capital resources typically range from $5,000 to $250,000. Additionally, strategic and cost effective marketing channels are identified via Digital Marketing mediums such as website redevelopment, search engine optimization, social media, blogging etc. Customized Smartphone applications with mobile media platforms are also integrated into these strategies to optimize revenues and profitability. EMV Compliance issues are identified and strategic merchant processing solutions are designed to ensure business owners are staying ahead of the curve with rapidly evolving technology. Financing Programs are designed for the customers of our Business Owner Clients. When a business has a higher ticket item product or service, their customer will now have the option of a 3, 6 and 12 Month Payment Plan.

November 2008 – Present (5 years 3 months) Rolling Hills Estates, California

In 2008 Eric created Wealth Architecture Group, Inc. Wealth Architecture is uniquely designed and ideally positioned to optimize client’s financial literacy and lifestyle objectives. Eric is passionately committed to developing a personalized game plan for success towards wealth accumulation and asset preservation. This is accomplished through synergistic partnerships with an exceptional portfolio of strategic niche companies. The primary areas of concentration are in the insurance/financial services, real estate investment and educational development solutions arenas. A comprensive and powerful real estate investment "Implementation Coaching" program with Turn-Key Solution Systems with a strategic partner company is strongly recommended.

Regional Sales Director
