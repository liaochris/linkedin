Frank Ruiz's Summary

I have a degree in computer science and have worked for a variety of companies over the years. I started out working for Sacramento Public Works as their procurement specialist, then moved on to Level 2 support for a Dental Software company and then to IT Admin for that company. I was in charge of a call center with 50 cubicles. Then I moved on to support websites from building and maintaining them to programming up databases and forms as well as scripts. Currently I support Outback Solutions with clients worldwide as well as BNI Connect which is also a worldwide company. Not only do I build and maintain websites, but I train others all over the world to do the same.

I have been a mobile DJ since 1985. I have been classically trained in the radio business and I have even been an engineer as well as an on-air personality. I have also been the sound guy for bands and performers including "Tribute to Santana", Bob Newhart, Dennis Agajanian, Jeff Martin of "The Martins" to name a few. I have done everything from weddings, barmitsvas, Birthdays, 65th Anniversaries, Corporate events, Class Reunions and also 3 day conferences across the nation. I periodically subcontract for Lights West (a major concert logistics company for the West Coast). I have also done voice-overs for audio and video productions.
