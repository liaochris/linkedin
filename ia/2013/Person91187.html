Melisa Hatfield's Summary

Real estate is a people business, no matter how you slice it! It revolves around money, facts and figures, but more important are the human relationships at it's core.

I am the President and Team Leader, and I'm an expert at what I do. I understand the minutiae of the business, but can also see the larger picture, which is one of the things that has made our Team a consistent top producer over the years. I work with a team of seasoned professionals who compliment my strengths, making my transactions smooth and seamless.

In this changing market I am always educating myself on how to help our clients the best. To assist home sellers with a possible short-sale or to prevent a foreclosure I've recently earned the prestigious Certified Distressed Property Expert designation.
