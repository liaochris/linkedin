Joey Silvian's Summary

Joey Silvian is a creative professional with a wide variety of experience in producing social media programs, corporate advertising, video, training programs, software and large marketing programs. He has held marketing and creative positions at Twentieth Century Fox, Mattel, Sandy Corporation, and BBDO. Joey currently owns VIA (Virtual Interactive Agency), a full-service marketing company.

Joey has created numerous high impact programs for major corporations and serves as a consultant for high level, intensive project management, strategic marketing, organizational development and process improvement. He has recently acted as interim digital marketing manager for Warrior Sports and BBDO.

Joey assists startups by helping companies early in their development to analyze and create their market. He has also helped business incubators and accelerators by creating new programs for organizations such as Michigan Economic Development Corp., Ann Arbor Spark, MidMIchigan Innovation Center, Battle Creek Unlimited and Techtown in Detroit. He has a B.B.A. in Marketing and an M.A. in Education Psychology from the University of Michigan.
