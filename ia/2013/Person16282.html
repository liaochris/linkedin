Bradley Taylor's Summary

195Places is the “go to” resource for both technical and industry strategy needs. By applying the extensive industry knowledge of founder Brad Taylor, a Senior Solutions Architect, specializing in Product Development, 195Places “cross pollinates” best practices in social media/networking, page design, personalization, SEO, mobile and content use from one industry to the next. This forward thinking has empowered customers with the ability to be leaders in their respective verticals for new ideas and has allowed them to stand out as world-class organizations. In addition to being skilled at anticipating problems before they can develop, the first-rate interpersonal skills of 195Places consistently foster smoother running teams, happier clients and better sales.

195Places CEO, Brad Taylor, has helped grow current partner “Clickability” to receive numerous industry awards, including the Software and Information Industry Association’s CODiE Award for best content management solution in 2008 and 2009, as well as CRM Magazine’s 2010 Rising Star CRM Service Award. In addition, Brad has been an integral team member for large web based companies like WebMD and has worked with multiple companies such as the Smithsonian, NBCBay Area, Tribeca Film and Voice of America.

195Places is committed to making a long-term partnership with your entire company to meet all of your development needs, now and in the future. Please feel free to contact us either by email or by telephone to further discuss what will become your next industry-changing project.
