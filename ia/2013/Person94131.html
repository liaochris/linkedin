Mike Ellis' Summary

I'm Mike Ellis, co-director of a new digital consultancy called Thirty8 Digital. We provide web consultancy, web sites, ideas and training. We understand content, we understand people and we can help you make your web presence shine.

My interests are in mobile technologies, social media, ubiquitous computing and innovation. I'm particularly interested in how organisations (particularly those which are traditionally quite slow at embracing new approaches to technology) can be empowered to do so.

From 2007 - June 2011 I worked for a Bath-based company called Eduserv who are a not for profit IT company. I worked within the Research & Innovation Group, looking at new technologies and how these impact on the way we communicate and collaborate.

From 2000-2007 I was Head of Web for The Science Museum in London. Before that I was Production Manager at Waterstone's Online; prior to that, I ran a small business providing web and print design consultancy.

I live in Bath with my wife and two sons.

Mike Ellis' Publications

    • Facet Publishing
    • April 2011
    Authors: Mike Ellis

    My book is a guide for anyone looking to build or maintain a cultural heritage web presence. It caters both to those who are single-handedly trying to keep their site running on limited budget and time as well as those who have big teams, large budgets and time to spend.
