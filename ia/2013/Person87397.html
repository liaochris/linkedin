Marsha Barnosky BCC, LMSW, ACSW, ACHP-SW's Summary

Board Certified Coach supporting individuals experiencing grief or a major life transition

Marsha specializes in training and consultation for managers and HR professionals on issues related to grief at work. Areas of expertise include: having difficult conversations with employees who are grieving, transitioning a grieving employee back to work, supervising a terminally ill employee as well as ways to balance productivity with compassion. Grief at work services also include discrete grief coaching for executives who've experienced a loss as well as coaching for the associated management team members. Marsha also provides grief coaching to individuals and groups related to death, divorce, and other major life transitions. Check out my blog " The Grief at Work Blog at: http://marshabarnosky.com/grief-at-work-blog-2/

Specialties: Training and consulting to business on grief at work, transition grief and loss coaching for individuals and groups, Public Speaking, Traumatic Incident Reduction
