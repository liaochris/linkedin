Kevin Marchetti's Summary

Kevin Marchetti is a Renaissance Man. After being employed as a welder for 15 years Marchetti was laid off. As a result, he returned to school, earning degrees in Drafting and Design, Civil Engineering Technology. As demand for his work grew his clients spread the word about the original, custom designs using all types of metal – Steel, Stainless, Aluminum,Cast iron, Bronze, Copper and even in Gold - creating functional art in everyday items such as furniture, balcony rails, staircase handrails and ornamental fences.

Friends and clients nicknamed Marchetti “the renaissance man” because of his artistic versatility and wide variety of abilities.
In late 2002 the fledgling business had outgrown the carport and the enterprise was moved into its present location on Pace Blvd. Renaissance Man also has 7 full time employees and several part-time workers ensuring individual attention to each project.

Specializing in custom handrails, spiral staircases, fences ,furniture and other functional art the company works on a vast array of projects, What ever the need, whether it is for custom furniture, artistic original lighting, an intricate treasure box, a soaring sculpture, or wrought iron fences and gates, Renaissance Man can take any vision and craft the reality.

Specialties

Custom Metal Fabrication, product design, we can build anything out of metal! From Industrial to Architectural to Sculptural... Please visit our web site for more info and see the photo gallery. renaissancemaninc.com
