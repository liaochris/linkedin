Kevin Au's Summary

Kevin has over five years experience creating interactive media. He started his career at a boutique agency in Toronto, Canada, where his clients included Bank of Montreal and CanWest Global. He then joined Sudden Industries in New York City, and quickly expanded his role. Initially hired as a project manager, he’s also added User Experience responsibilities over the past two years.

Working at Sudden has given Kevin the opportunity to work with some of the country’s largest organizations and businesses, including NBC Universal, Viacom, Pearson Publishing and The American Legion. His expertise has guided a variety of projects, including websites, apps and games.

Clients include:
NBC Universal - Steve Harvey TV, Viacom - SpikeTV - Ink Master, Pearson - Poptropica, Funbrain, Funbrain Jr., The American Legion, Lindy's Sports Magazine, National Cable & Telecommunications Association, RLJ Entertainment, WQXR Radio.
