John Espirian's Summary

Over fiften years of experience as a technical author, editor, test analyst and manager. Now working as a freelance technical writer and editorial consultant at espirian.co.uk.

Member of the Institute of Scientific and Technical Communicators (MISTC). Member of and principal webmaster for the Society for Editors and Proofreaders.
