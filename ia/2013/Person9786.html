Jim Arnold's Summary

I utilize my years of campaign experience at the national and state level and public advocacy experience to help groups and individuals impact public policy.

In addition, I'm an adjunct faculty member teaching government classes to college students, and I travel around the world with an international non-governmental organization working with political parties and elected officials in emerging democracies.

Recently, I began investing in real estate, renovating houses in downtown San Antonio, and hope to expand that endeavor to Austin and elsewhere.

Specialties

Lobbying executive and legislative branches for non-profit and corporate clients, managing coalitions, increasing public profiles for clients and teaching/training.
