Nandu Jayakumar's Summary

Senior Architect, Advertising and Data Group, Yahoo! Inc.

I design and build data-intensive products, and big data systems. My work spans data management, business intelligence, and analytics. The primary business domains in which I have worked are online advertising, and the understanding of users on web/mobile applications.

My experience spans a broad range of activities, with a focus on analytical data management and large-scale systems. I built Yahoo's first multi-petabyte analytical data repository, and a fully distributed data integration engine to work at that scale. I also designed Yahoo's state-of-the-art web analytics system and hold multiple patents/awards for it. At Stanford, the focus of my research was an in-memory data store at datacenter scale.
