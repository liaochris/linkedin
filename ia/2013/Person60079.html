Josh Berthume's Summary

Comprehensive strategy, brand development and creative direction - in Swash Labs I've built a modern advertising agency, deeply rooted in emerging media and storytelling. We help businesses, professionals, and political organizations build effective media strategies, develop powerful brand identities, and adapt to new media platforms.

Essentially, I help our clients understand how people interact and think, and to tell compelling stories that build real relationships.

Specialties: Extensive experience developing, deploying, and managing media and advertising campaigns, emerging media adoption strategies, brand identification and development strategies, social media content development and comprehensive communications deployments.

Additional work in behavioral economics, open source intelligence and market analysis, statistical analysis and data modeling, political communications, emerging media, political risk analysis, complex quantitative research, linguistics and semiotics.
