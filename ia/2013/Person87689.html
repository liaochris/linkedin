Martin Klappacher's Projects

  • Of Light & Shadow

    • Januar 2011 bis Juni 2012
    Teammitglieder: Martin Klappacher, Andreas Stallinger, Florian Jindra, Patrick Topf, Martin Kenzel, Michael Fuchs

    Of Light & Shadow is an award winning sidescroll puzzle-platformer for PC/Mac with a twist; the game combines two areas in each level, light and shadow, as well as two protagonists in one playable character – Mr. Light and Dr. Shadow. While either can only survive in light or shadow areas respectively, the player has to constantly switch between them and make the right decision about whose abilities to utilise.

  • Don't Drop!

    • September 2010 bis September 2011
    Teammitglieder: Martin Klappacher, Stefan Randelshofer, Iva Randelshofer, Doris Wimmer

    Don’t Drop! is an edutainment game for Android/iOS in which the player takes over the job of a building contractor. The main objective is to take care of your workers by securing them with the proper safety equipment to prevent them from falling down from heights. The game was developed by a small team of students of the Salzburg University of Applied Sciences.

  • Pratenkampf! Prototype

    • Januar 2012 bis Juni 2012
    Teammitglieder: Martin Klappacher, Franz J. Lanzendorfer, Alexander Cerny, Philipp Huber

    Piratenkampf! is a casual one vs. one player duel game which can be played against friends or AI. It’s playable cross-platform on browsers/mobile. You are a pirate captain stranded on a very small island and you want to sink your counterpart. Therefore you have to collect resources and combine them cleverly.
