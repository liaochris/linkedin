Bryan Cazan's Summary

As a Regional Account Executive for UPS Mail Innovations I am responsible for business development in the Los Angeles, San Fernando, and Santa Clarita areas. In this position I consult customers on effective mailing solutions. Coordination of customer implementation and maintenance with Operation, Transportation, and multiple sales organizations.

As President and founder of www.gpsmotoriders.com I have been able to startup a business from concept to launch. This site allows motocycle riders of all types to upload and download GPS files for the purpose of sharing "Rides" with other motorcycle riders.

Project Manager for Supply Chain Management company at the corporate (North America) level. In this industry I have led projects in process improvements, rate negotiations, and purchasing. I have also participated in new market penetration, business development, and business analysis. Other projects include business solution proposals, and supply chain implementations for clients. My career in arvato has allowed me to gain experience in a broad range of capacities including Human Resources, Operations, Sales & Marketing, and General Management.

I have also worked 5 years as a independent contractor within the Film & Motion industry. In this industry I worked on film sets as a grip to set lighting, camera mounting, dolly gripping, and rigging gripping.

Specialties

My specialties include project and team management on client supply chain implementations, and USPS Mailing Solutions. Also, business plan development, and implementation for dynamic database driven websites.
