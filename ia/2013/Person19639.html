Candace Cotton's Summary

Candace Cotton self-branded herself as "Candi Cotton" as a high school teen when she recognized the opportunity to capitalize on her name for success in the entertainment industry. Her inherent knowledge of the importance of personal branding and the power of the internet has perpetuated her accomplisments as a model and aspiring motion picture actress. Overcoming adversity to acheive outstanding accomplishments, Candace is the CEO of Candi Cotton, Inc. Utilizing herself as the prototype (and premier) client, Candace developed the modeling and acting agency, Candi Cotton, Inc., to ultimately assist young ladies aspiring for success in the entertainment industry, by mentoring them in the perfection of their craft (acting, modeling, etc), as well as the motivational aspects of success such as: Self Esteem, Initiative, Integrity, Time Management, etc. Candace intends to use her degree in Psychology and her experience working as a Therapeutic Behavioral Therapist to successfully mentor her clientele to shape their character in preparation for a life and career of excellence. Candi Cotton, Inc. also features a developing clothing line, "Candi Love". Candi Love includes Candi Cotton personally designed hoodies, tshirts, hand bags, and hats, (for men, women, and children), with a 80's vintage edgy style to all the items. Candi Love sales also include, posters and calendars. Candace has had the opportunity to do a project with legendary singer "Kenny"Babyface" Edmonds; and has worked with various music industry directors, and actors. Her debut feature motion picture, was aired on cable, on demand and is currently available on DVD in all video stores.
Miss Cotton also has a very extensive and impressive modeling portfolio, and a documented database of over 1 million interactive fans.

Specialties

Candace has proven herself as a highly motivated self-starter with an unrivaled sense of initiative. She completed her BA in Psychology from Ohio State University in just 3 years. Candace also was the intern editor for Psychologies Magazine for over 3 years . Candace Cotton is an inspiration to young ladies in particular and women in general. Her integrity and professionalism makes her stand out amongst her peers as a role model and source of inspiration.
