Lisa Cutler's Summary

Lisa has an extensive background in People Development, spending many years in the corporate environment. With roles predominantly focused on building, leading and coaching successful sales and account management teams, Lisa knows how to get the best out of people.

Lisa's career has spanned across Europe, the United States and Australia, working with global companies such as IBM and ANZ.

As a personal and professional development coach, Lisa's passion and focus is assisting people to develop and master their unique resources and personal brand; to build a strong leadership skill set; and to define career or business direction.

Lisa does this through one-on-one coaching and group workshops. She also specialises in working with women in leadership through her highly successful course - The Butterfly Program.

Lisa works with individuals and with organisations who are dedicated to developing their female leadership talent.

Lisa lives in Melbourne with her husband and their young twins.
