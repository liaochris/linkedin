Josie Levine Ph.D's Summary

A marriage and family therapist based in Oakland, CA with a PhD in clinical psychology since 1993, and a mindfulness meditation practitioner since 1987, I skillfully weave together both practices to help individuals and couples live with contentment.

My personal experience of living a healthy lifestyle with Type 2 Diabetes prompted me to focus my health coaching practice in helping others do the same.

Ph.D in Clinical Psychology 1993. California Institute of Integral Studies, San Francisco, CA.
Doctoral Internships completed at UCSF/Mt. Zion HIV Mental Health Program and C.G. Jung Institute, San Francisco, CA.

Specialties:

www.drjosielevine.com - Mindfulness-based psychotherapy in person or online via Skype. Available to all California residents.

Consultation groups for therapists and coaches wanting to work from a perspective of mindfulness. Available in person or via Skype.

www.mindfullymanagingdiabetes.com -blog for living mindfully and managing Type 2 diabetes.

Health Coaching. More about this on my blog.

As your health coach, I'll help you develop new, effective and healthy routines or habits. Creating an intention to improve how you take care of yourself is an act of self-compassion. Bringing all my expertise to your circumstances, together we’ll create a personalized routine to move you forward in your life, step by step.
