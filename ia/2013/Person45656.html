Frank Dolski Associate Broker, MBA's Summary

My professional experience as a Realtor, Associate Broker and Certified Relocation Specialist has been very rewarding. As the top producing agent for the Coldwell Banker Hearthside Realtors Franchise in 2006, 2007 and 2012 and the fact that I ranked 10th in the state of Pennsylvania for all Coldwell Banker Realtors in 2009 as well as my greatest achievement being ranked the #1 Coldwell Banker Affiliated Realtors in the state of Pennsylvania in 2012, It is clearly evident that I have a strong passion for this business as well as for people. Reaching this level in only eight years at Coldwell Banker to me is a reflection of the dedication and respect that I have for my clients.

My Mission Statement:
My promise to my clients is to make their real estate experience an exceptional one! Dedicated to providing outstanding service to Buyers, Sellers and Investors is essential to my business.
What sets a Real Estate Agent above the rest? For me it's the outstanding compliment of my repeat and referral business. It has been my commitment to provide trusted experience, hard work, enthusiasm, integrity and results! It is my job and sincere privilege to have great success for my Clients!
There is a difference!
Coupled with the support and brand recognition of Coldwell Banker International and the local support of Coldwell Banker Hearthside Realtors, our service is second to none!

My continued goal is to even better service my clients as the majority of my business results from referrals, relocation, former clients or targeted marketing. Utilizing the Internet through my personal website, social media and over 1000 advertising websites is critical. The fact that the Coldwell Banker Hearthside franchise continues to support the agents with better tools to effectively grow their business is essential. I am a full service agent and we are a full service company!

Specialties

Representation of buyers and sellers for residential and investment properties. I work with a variety of clients on resale and new construction. As an Accredited Buyer's Representative, I provide effective buyer representation. Negotiating and attention to details are critical aspects of any Real Estate Transaction. I am also e-PRO certified, which enable me to better utilize the Internet. I am also a Certified Relocation Specialist and a Previews Luxury Home Specialist.
