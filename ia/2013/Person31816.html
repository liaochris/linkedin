David Formby's Summary

So, if you’re reading this it's either because you know me or you’re looking for someone with the skills to help market and build your business – either way, thanks for popping by.

If you don’t know me; then I have nearly 20 years of experience in the marketing and advertising industry, working with leading agencies in senior roles to guide clients, develop integrated strategies and deliver campaigns that help to build customer relations and client revenues.

My skills have been developed working on brand development, proposition formulation, traditional advertising, sales promotion, customer relationship strategy, retail engagement, digital marketing, social media, social advertising, PPC, SEO and website strategy and build. I offer an integrated approach that focuses on understanding my clients business and getting the basics right before delivering great campaigns.

I’ve worked in partnership with brilliant marketers’ through-the-line in the B2B, B2C and the third sector working across finance, sports, renewables, public sector, motor, pharmaceuticals, construction, retail and many more.

In late 2011, I decided it was the right time to venture out and build a business which utilises the skills I and my associates have to deliver the very best of creative ideas. Combined with valuable insights that build clients businesses, our aim is to provide the very best service and creative that inspires and more importantly delivers both business and cultural change - after all we are judged by results and it’s our responsibility to deliver these successfully.

Three Thinking Co has now launched and we’re building it into a full service integrated agency – so watch this space.

I am passionate about getting the basics right for our clients so they can concentrate on developing and building successful businesses.

So, if you want to reconnect or want to find out how I or one of our team can help you, then drop me a line.

Specialties

Branding and identity, proposition development, marcomms, advertising strategies, campaign development and online marketing and business development strategy.
