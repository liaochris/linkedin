Anne Perry's Summary

Hello! I'm the happy founder and editor-in-chief of Business Heroine, the leading magazine and media company for purpose-driven entrepreneurs.

After spending the my early adult years burdened by debt and suffocating in jobs that drained the life out of me, I knew a few things to be true:

1) The 9-5 hamster wheel set up is not what life is meant to be about

2) There is a way out

3) I was going to find it and leave a Cheerio trail for others like me

Determined to find a way out of this all-too-common prison, I immersed myself in business courses, learning as many guerilla tactics as I could get my hands on, and studying the people who had blazed the trail I was inspired to take – that of the self-made, freedom-based entrepreneur. I finally cracked the code and took full advantage of my newfound freedom as a work-from anywhere entrepreneur, running my business from the beaches of Belize, from islands off the coast of Panama, and while traveling around the United States in a motorhome for nearly a year.

I built Business Heroine as a platform to broadcast the voices of women who use business as a tool to express their pure gifts and create positive change in the world. Out of admiration for she who, despite all obstacles, decides to break free of the mold and design life according to her own design, Business Heroine is dedicated to the entrepreneur who said she would, and did.
