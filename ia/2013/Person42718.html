Erin Ashford's Summary

Erin Ashford was trained as a documentary photographer at Academy of Art University in San Francisco. She now has a studio in Seattle, but shoots frequently in San Francisco, Atlanta, and Los Angeles, with reoccurring and previous clients such as the Thiel Foundation, Porsche, Neiman Marcus, Elie Tahari, Fendi, Jeffrey Fashion Cares, Fernbank Natural History Museum, Atlanta Contemporary Arts Center, Dogpatch Films, and Schwarzkopf Professional.
Erin Ashford is also a mixed media artist whose personal work focuses primarily on subcultures. She has documented Queer culture in the South, street rod culture in the Bay Area, construction workers in Georgia, and her continued work with Few and Far: an all-female art collective.
Her visual dialogue with diverse subjects is what makes her work stand out. The images are important but it's the relationships that are developed before that image is captured that matters to her. Erin also donates her time working with events held by the Georgia Coalition Against Domestic Violence, American Foundation for Suicide Prevention, and frequents local protests to heighten awareness of important local issues. When she is not traveling she can be found at her studio in Seattle. You can view or purchase her artwork at: http://www.etsy.com/shop/ErinAshford
