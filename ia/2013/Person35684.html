Dina Petrakis' Summary

Remodeling Consultant/ Renovation Coach with broad experience in project management of higher end single family residences and loft rehabs for office and residential use; kitchen and bath design and remodeling, and whole house renovation.

Assist client decision making in remodeling and real estate investment, providing objective information and guidance with the big view to the client's goals and building their wealth through real estate .

Act as owner's agent or project manager in construction projects, advocating the client's interests with architects, general contractors, subcontractors, and suppliers.
