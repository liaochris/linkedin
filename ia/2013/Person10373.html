Ashtan Moore's Summary

Ashtan Moore is the IT Director and Digital Media Specialist at the DC-based firm McBee Strategic; he helps to leverage digital platforms for the greatest gain, manages internal and external search engine optimization and content development strategies, serves as project manager for all bespoke design and web development, and is the director of technology infrastructure.

Ashtan is also the Chief Technology Enabler at ArtJamz, a start-up art-party franchise founded in DC in 2010, where he plans the technology infrastructure and a variety of digital initiatives, and the CEO of Moore Simply, a lightweight business consulting and digital development company.

Previously, Ashtan was the founder and CEO of Moore Enterprise Solutions, a tech-focused services company, where he built the company from the ground up to ultimately have full featured information technology, graphic and web development, and digital marketing branches. While building this company Ashtan worked on a variety of projects ranging from digital consulting and execution to international crisis remediation support. He thrives in any environment in which creative problem solving is a necessary component for success.
