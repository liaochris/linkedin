TJ Morris' Summary

My experience dates back professionally to 1999 when Walden books gave me my first illustration job. I drew coloring books for them for a while, and after that just kept growing into more and more complex creative work spanning Illustration, Animation, Sculpture, Digital Work, Art Direction, and ultimately Senior UI Design.

My goals are simple. I want to get paid for my artistic ability. Ultimately conceptual art for an interactive and/or regular media company.

Specialties:
I work across a broad range of mediums, Digital and Analog, but specialize in Digital Graphic Design and problem solving. I enjoy taking someone's idea and bringing it out for all to see. I am able to interpret the chaos of the business mind and heaving a client say "Wow, that's exactly what I'm talking about, how'd you do that?"

I also have a great deal of experience working with many different kinds of people.
