Bryan Thomas Schmidt's Summary

I am a novelist and editor with extensive experience in both fiction, nonfiction, technical writing and screenwriting. I have worked on documentaries for A&E, History Channel and NBC as well as software manuals and church ministry curriculum. Very good at writing for people with multiple learning styles and from multiple cultures and translating technical language into language anyone can understand. Skilled social media user and marketer. Skilled at fundraising and nonprofit management. Goal: To find a way to use my experience creatively to support people and companies I believe in.

Specialties

I have extensive experience with legal documentation and some medical documentation knowledge as well. I am skilled with computers and knowledgable about technology and the internet. I can help you move toward the next millennium in document and resource management and risk management. I am also skilled with publicity, grants, social media marketing and website design.
