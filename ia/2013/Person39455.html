Eddie Weinhaus' Summary

Eddie runs a Management Consultancy focusing on building companies, growth and process improvement. (EnableR / Business Enablement).

He currently manages projects and teams on behalf of CEO's, Boards and other Executives. EnableR as a consultancy focuses on organizational change.

As part of his Management Consultancy, Eddie builds and runs two "Process" Apps. One app is for Executives, successfully implemented as a workflow and process management solution for businesses with 5-500 employees. The second, as yet undisclosed is for parents managing their family. (Eddie is a single father of 5 children.)

Eddie re-engineered the local news gathering process for major media companies as the Founder and Chief Architect at Journatic with roles covering Technology, Operations, Finance and HR. Eddie built the scaling processes and software architecture for BlockShopper as Founder, expanding to 30+ metro markets.

Previous experience includes: The Boston Consulting Group, American Electric Power, Private Equity, Derivatives Trading, Founder of Oak Willow Builders.
Education: MBA - Booth (University of Chicago), B. Sc. London School of Economics
