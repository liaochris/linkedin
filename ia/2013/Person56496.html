Jackie Dotson's Summary

I started out in my career as a writer and newspaper editor, spent eight years in the restaurant industry and later went on to become a psychotherapist. I now combine all of these skills in my current work as a business therapist, freelance writer, teacher, facilitator, podcaster, and Director of Operations Riff Raft Enterprises, also known as CDK Creative.

I have an insatiable interest in business and entrepreneurialism, specifically how to responsibly use business to make the world better. I love to interview people, and in my podcast I interview business people on this very subject.

I love using my skills as a licensed therapist to help people adjust to change and help business partners resolve differences. I really like challenging old ways of thinking by developing creative solutions to old problems.

I'm New York City born and raised, currently living in Sacramento, California.

Specialties: writing, blogging, podcasting, psychotherapy, business therapy, marketing, moderating and facilitating
