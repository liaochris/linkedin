Kristyn Hammond's Summary

A 2012 graduate of Tarleton State University's Department of English and Languages, I earned my MA in English with a concentration on the Gothic (thesis). Currently, I teach freshman composition and composition and research. Teaching interests include composition, western literature, woman’s literature, and literacy studies. My teaching experience includes basic writing, writing center, English language tutoring, freshman composition, and composition and research. My research interests include classical studies, British Romanticism, Romantic Gothic tradition, the American Gothics, Postmodern Gothic, feminism and gender studies, cultural criticism, classical rhetoric, and composition pedagogy.

Additionally, I have worked as a freelance writer, publishing SEO and keyword optimized web content since 2007 for such clients as Demand Media, Blissfully Domestic, and Suite101. I write on a wide variety of topics, including social media, writing, culture, and education.

I'm an avid blogger, currently self-hosting two active blogs with the help of WordPress. When I'm not teaching, I'm working toward finishing my novel. I currently reside in a small town in Central Texas with my amazing husband, Matthew, my two shih-tzus, Anakin and Chewbacca, and a fussy tabby cat named Galileo.

Specialties: composition pedagogy, cultural criticism, gender and feminist studies, rhetoric, Romantic Gothic, postmodern Gothic, the American Gothics, British Romanticism, technology and composition, new media, internet, SEO, blogging, literature, fiction writing
