Amy Castelluzzo's Summary

I began working as a Clinical Iridologist in 2010 and thanks to BNI (Business Networking International) in New Paltz, my practice has grown very steadily.
I am located at 231 Clinton Avenue in Kingston, NY where I work very closely with Malinda Sanducci, who practices Japanese style acupuncture. We welcome other wellness professionals to look at the space!
As an Iridologist, my goal is to teach people to be pro-active about their health and learn how to incorporate positive dietary habits, daily breathing and movement exercises and physically pleasurable activities such as massage into their busy lives.
