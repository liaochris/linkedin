Grace Ventura's Summary

As a Transformational Life Coach, Master of Transitions, Healer, Yoga Instructor & Mindful Mama of 6 amazing children 17-29… I have a deep calling to work with women to gracefully move through the "transitions or unknowns" in life with courage and laughter!

Anxiety, stress and worry escalate especially during the "transitional" times of our lives.....both beginnings and endings. These feelings become overwhelming.
Re-Discover your inner guidance to make decisions, feel deeply nurtured, loved and access your inspiration and creativity.
I work with individuals help to create a relationship that is life giving & supportive both with themselves and others.

Get ready to greet your day with Gratitude and Vibrant, Expansive Energy!
What if you had a personal oracle or magic lamp that you could rub? What would you ask for in yourself, a relationship, career, family or home? Let’s Create it!!

I have taught Self Care & Mother-Daughter Workshops, Yoga, Meditation, Four Agreements & Transformational power journeys to the Pyramids of Teotihuacán, Mexico. My extensive pilgrimages to India, sacred sites in Mexico & Greece have inspired profound awareness & creativity including an upcoming book, Mother How Can This Be? Unanswered Questions as Children but were Too Afraid to Ask. I am the author of Four Alignments of Self Care: The Journey of Aligning Body, Mind & Spirit to Cultivate Excellent Self Care, which inspired several workshops.

My commitment is to my personal journey of awakening and to humanity. I can not imagine living any other way no matter what shows up. Yes, I fall down and stumble...trip over myself. And often am amused! It is not an easy path but one that is truly the journey of my soul.... Full of love, truth and so much gratitude for the richness of life.
