Franklin Maggi's Summary

Archives & Architecture was founded by the late Glory Anne Laffey, Historian in 1989, and established as a partnership in 2003. The company, headquartered in San Jose, California, provides historical resource consulting services in the fields of urban planning and preservation architecture to public agencies, private businesses and individuals throughout Northern California .

Our staff includes, architectural historians, public historians and preservation planners. All our professionals meet or exceed the Secretary of the Interior’s Professional Qualification Standards and are listed with the California Historical Resources Information System (CHRIS).

Specialties: Preservation Planning, Landmark Nominations, National Register Nominations, Historic Property Evaluations, Cultural Resource Surveys, HABS/HAER/HALS Documentation and Photography, NEPA:Section 106 and Caltrans HRER Reports.
