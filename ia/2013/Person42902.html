Errol Stewart's Summary

Portfolio: http://errol.dunked.com

“A designer knows he has achieved perfection not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.” — Steve Jobs

"Good design is innovative • Good design makes a product useful • Good design is aesthetic • Good design makes a product understandable • Good design is unobtrusive • Good design is honest • Good design is long-lasting • Good design is thorough down to the last detail • Good design is as little design as possible." — Dieter Rams

I am a Product Designer, Interaction Designer, Visual Designer, and like to think I'm a Content Strategist, but will defer. This means that I'm a UX Designer, as far as I understand. Whew.

I believe that a great user experience is a bit of science, a bit of data, a bit of art, and a whole lot of passion. We sweat over the details so the user doesn't need to. We are a critical component to the success of the business we serve.

I'm good at:

• being inspired by folks smarter than myself
• end-to-end UX, UI, IA, content, and brand strategy
• being an ambassador between business, design, and development
• fitting into the full-cycle development process; agile; scrum
• interpreting business requirements, metrics, and user research to inform design
• organizing information
• wireframes; detailed, annotated
• complex UI permutations
• style guides
• specs
• user assistance writing

and I get excited talking about:

• web standards
• responsive design
• leveraging JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3 to inspire UX
• copywriting
• a well designed form
