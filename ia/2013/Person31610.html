David Chesnut, III's Summary

I have successfully transitioned from a top-tier, traditional media copywriter and ad agency creative director to digital copywriting. That means I have the conceptual, strategic, and creative writing skills of a senior level copywriter, plus hands-on experience as a successful, award-winning Creative Director.
But wait, there’s more!
Now I’ve acquired the skills and know-how to successfully execute digital and online direct marketing programs using eCRM, email, web content, blog content, and all those other keywords.
Speaking of strategies...
When I was a rookie copywriter, working at two of the best creative and brand building ad agencies in the country, I had incredible mentors. So my concepts are strong, creative, and strategically on target; and my writing style changes to fit the audience.
Today more and more of my work is found online – most recently I’ve been a contract copywriter for several e-commerce companies, creating direct marketing materials. I wrote a lot of eCRM materials — especially emails and landing pages.
I’m now looking for challenging full-time or contract copywriting or associate creative director positions in an environment that appreciates a good idea and a crafty, persuasive sentence.

• Also: print advertising, direct marketing, promotions, online strategy & content writing, radio & TV production, collateral, name & logo development.
Clients (direct and indirect): AT&T, CIBA Vision, Coca-Cola, Home Depot, IBM, Pharmacia-Upjohn, Piedmont Hospital, SunTrust Banks, Turner Broadcasting, Vlasic Pickles

Specialties

Good copywriting from short emails to long form collateral. And a whole lot of other stuff: Web content, radio commercials - writing and production, consumer advertising, healthcare marketing, concept, copywriting, promotions, creative direction, strategic positioning and brand building, corporate communications.
