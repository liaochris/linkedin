Adrian Smith's Summary

Since its formation in 2009, emulus communications has become a trusted marketing and communications partner to clients across a wide range of industry sectors, from health to media and broadcasting.

With nearly two decades’ experience in communications, emulus works across every discipline to ensure programmes are delivered on time, on budget and to an exemplary standard.

As well as delivering projects independently, emulus communications also has an excellent record of working with in-house communications teams to provide hands-on project management, additional resource, or simply an alternative perspective.

Where particular skills or experience is needed, emulus invites support from a small number of carefully selected agencies, creatives or freelance copywriters. This is particularly valuable for larger, more complicated projects and those with particularly perilous deadlines.

For more information about how emulus communications may be able to help you, or samples of our work, please call 07702 089713 or email adrian@emulus.co.uk.

Experience includes: internal communications, marketing (including branding, advertising, web sites, social media), investor relations (including annual reports), Corporate Social Responsibility and media relations.

Skills include: project and event management, media relations, copywriting and editing, speech writing, qualitative and quantitative research, social media.
