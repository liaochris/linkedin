Ben Blackburn's Summary

As a Market/ Business Development and Process Improvement Leader for over 20 years, Ben Blackburn has a proven record of identifying and penetrating untapped global markets for growth areas in telecommunications and enterprise mobility. In addition to his acumen in business development, he is also a recognized leader at driving cross-functional teams focused on quality initiatives and establishing a corporate culture of continuous improvement.

Mr. Blackburn has worked for companies ranging in size from start-ups and joint ventures to $30B corporations. His domain expertise lies primarily in high technology and telecommunications. Through his broad experience in go-to-market operations, he has attained proficient knowledge in the enterprise, retail, government, healthcare, and transporation/logistics sectors. From a business model standpoint, he has established and grown businesses in B2B, B2C, and subscriber revenue streams.

Mr. Blackburn has also established an expertise in business process improvement over the past 8 years. He is a Six Sigma Black Belt and has driven continuous improvement and process redesign programs within Motorola for most functional areas, including Supply Chain, Sales Operations, Go-to-Market optimization, and Corporate Governance. Through his Six Sigma project leadership, he was able to achieve over $40M in Operating Earnings (OE) savings over a period of 4 years.

Mr. Blackburn's career has been truly global in scope for both Market/ Business Development and Process Improvement. He has domain expertise in Latin America with over 15 years of Business Development in the region. He has broad experience in the EMEA regions through his Process Improvement work. He is also fluent in Spanish.

Mr. Blackburn holds an MBA from the Thunderbird School of Global Management and a BA from Oral Roberts University. He attained his his Six Sigma Black Belt from Motorola in 2006. He is also a Certified Public Accountant (CPA).
