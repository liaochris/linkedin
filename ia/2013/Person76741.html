Peter Tsuru's Experience

Owner

Kraze Apparel

2007 – Present (6 years) Kailua, HI

Kraze Apparel’s mission is to present customers with unique designs and clothes that energizes any type of activity. Whether it is surfing, skateboarding, playing on the beach, or cruising the malls, Kraze Apparel wants you to feel comfortable with durable clothing that will help you look fresh.

The word "Kraze" is not pronounced "crazy". Kraze is a play on words from the word "craze". It describes our style of designs, meaning trendy and enthusiastic.

Owner

Island Style Screen Printing

March 2008 – Present (5 years 10 months)

A locally owned screen printing company that offers high-quality service for our customers. We screen print on T-shirts, long sleeves, tank tops, and etc. We do our best and print all of our designs manually which takes hard work, skill and concentration.

We enjoy working with small and large businesses who can't afford large quantity minimums or are just starting off to get their name out.
