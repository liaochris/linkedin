Moorhouse Software Services

Computer Software industry

February 1991 – Present (22 years 7 months) London, United Kingdom

Providing comprehensive face-to-face and remote control technical support services to companies ranging from sole trader to international corporate.

Clothing manufacture/distribution, Optician wholesale, Jewellery Stock Control, Employee performance questionnaire/analysis, Property lettings, Bonded Warehouse Administration, Food Manufacture/Distribution, Dividend Management (Stockbroking), Newspaper Syndication/Royalties Management, Courier/chauffeur tariff calculation, Security Key generation (Halma/Castell), Building Industry, Hospital Security, Accountancy, Human Resources.

Generic software work: Data extraction from systems held in a variety of formats, website scraping, email extraction, decoding and recovery. Network monitoring and packet analysis.

Email systems consultancy, setup, administration and recovery using non-Microsoft solutions. Troubleshooting email problems (domain name problems, spamming, relaying, sending, receiving, archiving, positively identifying the source of Rootkits, Trojans and other Malware embedded in infected corporate systems using non-invasive techniques).

Experience with ISDN and an understanding of ISDN charging tariffs in relation to email sending and collection. Setup and maintenance of email systems using Lotus/IBM cc:Mail e.g., a subsidiary of Hearst Corporation - Hearst Entertainment; and also Novell Groupwise.

WAN/LAN Network supplier, setup and support.

Consultancy and implementation of systems to migrate and maintain company "production" and "business critical" data stored in spreadsheets into Databases.
