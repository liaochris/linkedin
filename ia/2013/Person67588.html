Jonathan Lyons' Summary

For over 20 years Jonathan Lyons and Lyons Digital Media have been helping small and medium sized businesses reach and communicate with their audiences. The business has followed technology trends from digital design and print to Internet based applications, websites and social media and and we are now CPanel certified.

It's not just posting Facebook content, but real conversations. My Klout and PeerIndex scores stay in the upper 40s to 50s. I have used social networks from Friendster to Twitter. I am also a regular contributor on Quora. If you really want to connect and build a community, talk to me.

Specialties

I am CPanel certified and I have an Addy for brochure design and a Communicator Award for book design. I bring over 20 years of graphic design and communications experience to our projects. I have extensive experience with open source web applications and years of practical knowledge of social media. Hear us at http://bit.ly/oRSHqZ on the Social Media episode of Perspectives.

Jonathan Lyons' Projects

  • FACDL

    • December 2011 to December 2012
    Team Members: Jonathan Lyons, Barbara Forbes-Lyons, Karen Groves, Pam Gibson, Philip Kovoor

    Website development, data migration and design for the Florida Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers. Large sections of content creation were crowd sourced to the membership and automated by the web applications. Additionally the website has been optimized for mobile use and access.

  • Health Law Section of the Florida Bar

    • April 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Jonathan Lyons, Barbara Forbes-Lyons, Philip Kovoor, Karen Groves

The section has the ability to publish content easily and the Health Law Section Journal was migrated from print to an online edition. A new phase is being planned which will all the Section to restrict website content by membership status.
