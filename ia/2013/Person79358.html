Michelle Laycock's Summary

Owner of Laycock Designs, a Graphic Design firm for home-based business owners in the Direct Sales Industry. Blog Designer specializing in Wordpress, Typepad, and Blogger theme designs and customer membership/payment integration.
Social Networking graphic designs for Ning, Grou.ps, Twitter, YouTube, Facebook. Print business designs to match blog including logos, business cards, rewards cards, ads, product packaging, vinyl signage.

Ad Designer for several online communities including Splitcoaststampers, Paper Craft Planet, Christian Paper Crafts Magazine, and Papers and Pixels Magazine.

Ad Designer for several magazines in the paper-crafting industry including Canadian Scrapbooker, Stamp & Scrap Arts, Crafts & Things, Technique Junkies.

Product Designer of rubber stamp art and Spellbinders dies for Our Daily Bread designs, llc. Past Stampin' Up! Independent Demonstrator and a Concept Artist for the Scottish Borders Stamping Design Team.

I volunteer as a foster mom and cat adoption volunteer with the Kindred Kitties non-profit animal shelter. As of Sept 2007, I have rescued and found loving homes for over 200 baby kittens and mom cats.

Volunteer Webmaster for the KindredKitties.org website and Kindred Kitties Facebook Admin.
