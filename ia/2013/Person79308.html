Lawrence Citarelli's Summary

The very basis of my foundation is geared toward aligning the right people who share common goals, beliefs, character and philosophy. I am hoping this is why you and I have connected and our work together will be fruitful on a myriad of platforms. We have all been blessed with the successes we enjoy. Being humble in my successes, appreciative of my opportunities and grateful that I am able to help those who are less fortunate - especially the children drives me. Every one of our proprietary projects provides for a certain portion of the ROI(return on investment) to go directly back to the less fortunate children of the community we are doing business in…We are also engaging the use of LEED Certification, energy efficient and alternative energy solutions. Financially prudent Real Estate developments that are philanthropically oriented, environmentally responsible and creating jobs - Everyone Wins!

Mr. Citarelli possesses over 20 years of RE investment experience. In the past 11 years Mr. Citarelli has forged & is the President & CEO of a multi award winning organization - The L III Group, First Hampton International RE & Citadom Holdings, LLC - His acquisition & development firm which all continue to receive accolades.

First Hampton International Realty(FHIR)is a full service concierge style, boutique firm that offers a multifaceted approach the Real Estate. The services include a Luxury Auction and Asset to Asset Trading Platforms that are the perfect blend of traditional RE services with a personalized flare that is unrivaled in the industry and employs cutting edge technology, SEO, 60+ websites and 17 language translation..

The finance, economic and psychology degrees along with an insatiable work ethic, street smarts and strict adherence to disciplines continue to add to his successes.

Mr. Citarelli has recently been awarded a congressional award for his international business dealings which are specifically tied to helping less fortunate children.
