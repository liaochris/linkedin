Melissa Gillespie (Bottomley)'s Summary

I have been working in public and community relations and marketing communications (internal and external) for more than 15 years. For the past several years I have been a partner at Innova Communications, a boutique public relations, social media, and marketing firm based in Southern CA. We create and execute campaigns to increase our clients' domestic and international brand awareness and sales. Current industry specialties include: luxury consumer goods and real estate, translation/localization, and sports business/sporting goods.

Since I was very young, I have wanted to write and work in the publishing industry. In addition to my work at Innova, I handle the managing editor duties for RMS Media Group's luxury coastal publication, Ocean Home magazine (@oceanhomemag), covering the latest residential and commercial oceanfront developments world-wide. You'll also find me at RMS' other publication, regional lifestyle pub for all things North of Boston, Northshore magazine. There, I handle community relations, events, social media, and e-marketing (@northshoremag).
