Julie Theroux www.timetofaceit.com's Summary

JULIE THEROUX is an entertaining television and radio personality, writer and speaker who has built a solid reputation as the very funny go-to expert in the study of human behavior and communication through the art of face reading and body language.

Julie has appeared as a television guest on ABC, Sonoran Living, CBS and KTVK 3TV. She is a popular radio interview guest. Julie is currently working on writing a unique daytime TV talk show that encompasses unusual forms of entertainment relating to mind, body, spirit. The show features a mix of unusual talent, authors, music and her entertaining ability to read people.

She is the author of three books, including Beginning Face Reading. Beginning Face Reading is considered the leading reference for information on face reading and her next edition is due in Fall 2011. Julie has written freelance for AZNet News, Daycare.com and others. She has been interviewed for numerous articles, including Cosmopolitan Magazine, Arizona Republic and Scottsdale News.

Julie is an enthusiastic keynote speaker. Clients include the State of Texas, US Department of Agriculture, Astra Pharmceuticals, Corporate Express, staffing firms, lawfirms, political groups and law enforcement.

Julie founded the Face Reading Online University, which began as interactive seminars. Because there was so much demand for her seminars from a larger audience, she made this certification program available to everyone through her website, www.aboutfacereading.com. Ebooks are also available.

Julie just likes to help people. She considers herself a student of human communications who was lucky enough to build a career as a Face Reader. She believes that face reading evokes feelings of connection. A sense of being connected is the ability to experience the true human condition, which is the one thing all people want and this is a quality which all people share. She calls Phoenix, Arizona home.
