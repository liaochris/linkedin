Kevin Scharff's Summary

As a dedicated and passionate team leader, I specialize in production management and risk mitigation, from initial planning and resourcing to scheduling and delivery. I constantly strive to orchestrate project development in a creatively and financially successful production.

I have studio management level credentials and am most recently charged with cultivating a next generation development environment for highly skilled senior artists, across all game development disciplines at Sony Santa Monica Studios.

I have supervised development teams of over 100 internal staff and contractors across numerous technological and creative disciplines, while working with executive studio management and external partners to instill a successful development production.  

I have extensive experience in implementing agile production methodologies, including team migration, integration and iteration.

I am a seasoned public speaker and actively engaged in the gaming community. I have over 15 years of experience in interactive, film and television projects.

Specialties:
• Studio leadership and mentoring, staffing and hiring for projects.
