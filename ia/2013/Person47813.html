Geoff Stack's Summary

Building on ten years of professional practice as a Reflector, Refiner and Reconciler in the fields of design, enterprise, ecology and education, Geoff creates spaces where deep listening, grist-filled conversation, and emergence of often-surprising solutions becomes the norm.

Geoff Stack's Experience

Strategy Consultant

Stack Strategy

September 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months) Baltimore, Maryland Area

"The People Side of Sustainability"

Within the private sector, Geoff has worked with Green Teams at General Motors, MarquipWardUnited, Green Bay Packaging, Lion Brothers, Acadia Windows & Doors and Kirk-Habicht Company to spur sustainability action by co-facilitating a series of customized workshops organized by the Regional Manufacturing Institute of Maryland and funded by the US Department of Energy. He has also delivered Green Manufacturing courses to participants from McCormick, Sherwin Williams, AAI, E-Structors and Cobham as a contractor to the Maryland Technology Enterprise Institute.

Stack’s public sector clients have included Morgan State University, Enterprise Community Partners, Baltimore City Public Schools, Prince Georges County Public Schools, Green Street Academy, the Alliance to Save Energy, The Natural Step International and the Technology and Innovation in Manufacturing and Engineering (TIME) Center at the Community College of Baltimore County.

March 2005 – June 2007 (2 years 4 months)
