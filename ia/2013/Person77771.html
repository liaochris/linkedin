Kyle Lagunas' Summary

As the Talent Acquisition Analyst at Brandon Hall Group, Kyle keeps today's business leaders in touch with important conversations and emerging trends in the world of work. From in-depth research and market analysis to engaging webinars and blogs, he offers a fresh take on emerging key practices in sourcing, recruiting, assessing, hiring and retention strategies. Previously, Kyle was a contributor on SHRM's We Know Next and TLNT, His work has been featured in Forbes, The New York Times, Business Insider, and Information Weekly.

Specialties: Industry research and reporting, brand ambassadorship, public relations, content marketing, networking, blogging, community builder, copy editor.

The work is outrageous, well-researched, and a little bit dangerous.

As Relations Chair, it's my job to keep Paper Chairs in touch with our community--and vice versa. I plan and coordinate fundraisers, support promotion of productions across various channels, and lend my operations and marketing experience to the creative vision of the company.
