Eileen Seitz's Summary

Professional full time artist since 1968, traveling around the planet focusing on the tropical areas...In 1972 I created my artcard line as a vehicle to reach the masses.. In 1986 I self published my work into posters, limited editions and have continued selling and framing my work for the hospitality industry as well as the general public.. Hotels, resorts, hospitals, restaurants, cruise ships, television sets, movie sets have purchased my work for their interior design projects.. In 2002 I started to create my hand tufted 100% new zealand wood rugs and in 2007 my giclee prints on canvas were selected for the remodeled and new construction Pollo Tropical Restaurants in south and central Florida and even branched into a few restaurants in NJ.
Since the end of 2008 I have begun my handpainted textiles which can be found and purchased from my website. www.eileenseitz.com

Specialties

Starting with watercolors and oil paintings, I also have the ability to design all the matting and framing for one piece of artwork to projects that have equaled 1,987 prints for a large hotel complex in the Caribbean.
