Laura Palmer's Summary

Laura Palmer helps individuals, organizations, and high-level executives identify and overcome their limiting beliefs, freeing them to reach high levels of personal and professional success.

The need for Bridgenosis® became clear to Laura while practicing labor and employment law for seven years, including a brief stint as a political appointee. The emotional cost of conflict was sad, but the real fiscal costs were shameful. She began to envision a future where conflict within and between organizations could be turned into an opportunity to create a new perspectives to achieve greater success.

Vision: We believe in the unbridled success that comes when individuals cast off self-limitations. We believe in a future where internal and external conflict no longer hinders the performance of people or their organizations.

Mission: We are dedicated to identifying and removing limiting perspectives, both in individuals and organizations. Our cutting edge tools tap into the creative mind and enable you to shift your mindset from conflict to success.

Core Values: Empowerment, Compassion, and Perseverance

Laura welcomes the opportunity to help your organization to achieve maximum results in the face of obstacles. Contact Laura: 202-709-6013 or laura@bridgenosis.com for more information and a free consultation. Sessions are available in-person and via Skype videoconferencing.
