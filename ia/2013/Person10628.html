Athelene Gosnell's Summary

For Senior Communities: My product, Amika, helps senior living communities provide an easy way to keep residents connected. By providing simplified hardware, software and in-person involvement, seniors are able to leverage the power of technology. My approach does this without adding additional work to the staff of the community. Communities can use Amika to provide connections within the community, ongoing activities to residents, and a new way to interact with those outside the community. Tech Interpreter is all about making technology, particularly the web, understandable for the average person. Find out how a geek to human interpreter can help your community by visiting www.techinterpreter.com/amika.

For Executives: Tech Interpreter, also offers coaching to business executives who understand how important it is to be fluent in the language of technology but don’t want to spend a lifetime getting there. For an executive, not having a base level of proficiency in technology is like not being able to read a balance sheet. Too many resources are dedicated to technology to ignore its importance. Tech Interpreter can provide executives with a boot camp like service to get you up to speed in just 12 hours. You don't have to be a CPA to be a successful executive. But you do need to understand the balance sheet. You don't have to be a geek to be a successful executive. But you do need a framework to make good technology decisions. Invest 12 hours into your success. Email me to learn more. athelene@techinterpeter.com.

I have spent about 30 years helping people use technology in a way that is meaningful to them. I've been a tech interpreter, translating geek-speak, for all of those years. I look forward to translating for you!
