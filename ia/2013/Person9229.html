Amanda Appiarius' Summary

Hello!

I am a Visual Designer with strengths in many areas of expertise and experience at over 8 different companies. My strongest skills are in UI & UX design for Web & Mobile, Illustration and Visual Marketing. I am a fast and determined worker who loves to take charge and run with ideas!

- Lead Visual Design experience. Oversee and manage UI & UX design, company identity, photography, visual marketing and other design needs. Work easily with other teams including copy writing and engineering. Consistency and clean design are important.

- Complete experience with the design process : wireframes - design - iterations - asset creation - communication with the engineering team to smooth out bugs and perfect design.

- Creative skills include but are not limited to : creation of original icons, web banners and advertisements, badges, tee-shirt designs, photography, comics, business cards, invitations, typography and more.

- Digital Illustrator of characters, people, food, life-like or cartoon subjects, and more. Experienced with quick sketching and fine art as well.

-Possess some skill with animation, film editing, Cinema 4D, HTML and CSS.

Check out my portfolio, illustration site or food blog to find out more about me!

Specialties: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Flash, Actionscript, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Maxon Cinema 4d, After Effects, Microsoft Office, digital photography and scanning, drawing, illustration and painting. Mac and PC proficient.
