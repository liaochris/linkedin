Dustin Ledden's Summary

I worked for Family Technologies Programming Visual Basic, Delphi, ASP since 1994 as a Junior programmer and developer with responsibility for a web ordering and download component of the companies flagship software package.

In 2000, started Integris Consulting as a software consultancy with specialty in web integration of software projects.

Worked under contract to Embla AS, Scandalon, Fancher Chair company, BioMerridian, Biological Technologies International. On a variety of software products.

In 2004, the direction of my company changed from software development to video production and web design. I now specialize in helping graphic artists reach their web design goals and consulting on independent feature films.

Please see my website for information on web design: www.integris1.com

In 2005, I became a managing member of Filmweavers LLC. We been involved in five feature films and numerous, short films, event, and promotional videos. Among other jobs I have worked as producer, director, assistant director, foley artist, fight choreographer and effects coordinator.

IMDB credits: http://www.imdb.com/name/nm3048971
More credits: http://www.filmweavers.com/films.htm

Filmweaver's first feature film won Best Feature and was nominated for Best of Festival at SAICFF 2010.

In 2012, I worked on my first union shoot as Effects Supervisor and Fight Coordinator on the feature film: Beyond the Mask.

My responsibilities involved choreographing multiple fights between the main character and an array of adversaries, one-on-one to ten attackers bare hand and weapons. I worked with a Stunt Coordinator to ensure safety on a number of stunts including minor explosions, fire, firearms, weapons, rigging, falls, and water. I aided the production team to plan shot selection and stay on schedule and budget for fight and stunt scenes.

Currently each of the feature films I've worked on have been picked up by national networks and become profitable.
