Glenn A Curry's Summary

glennslinkedin(at)gmail.com Generic invites rejected. Tell me WHY you want to link.

Formal education in Electronics at MATC, Majored in Broadcast TV.

First half of my work experience was in Retail Audio, mainly as a Rep for companies such as Kenwood, Marantz, Maxell, AR, Pioneer (Auto), Audio Technica, Cerwin Vega, ... Covering 6 S.E. USA states.

Moved back to WI in '81 and into Pro Video sales. Worked for Avonix, then opened Roscor's 1st branch office. Board member, President of ITVA (now MCA-!). Sold to major B&I and TV stations.

Then went back to Manufacturers Repping. Started with Magni Systems, (TV test and measurement, 1/3 of the US) then went to Symbolics (AI computers, computer graphics, E. 1/2 the US) and then DFx (dedicated highend graphics systems).

Started Tech-noid Engineering and Marketing (T.E.A.M. Work) '89 to sell Broadcast Electronics, Computer Graphics (SGI based including Wavefront, Alias, Parallax, XAOS TOOLS largest dealer), custom engineering and Marketing work.

Then lured away to data sales in the expanding Telecom market. But we all know what happened there.

Started Efficient Technologies LLC with my son in '05 as a boutique B&I IT company.

Now providing the best level of support for the largest assortment of IT hardware and software in the industry as a Regional Sales Manager for GHA Technologies.
