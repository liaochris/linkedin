Alan Parisse, MBA, CSP, CPAE's Summary

Alan Parisse has been delivering keynote speeches on leadership, sales and change and presentation training to executives, managers, sports stars, financial advisors, wholesalers and sales professionals for over 20 years.

Parisse's insights, experience and wit enable him to deliver substantive, timely and challenging information in ways that inspire audiences, coaching clients and workshop participants to adopt his ideas and make them their own.

At The Speaking Intensive speaker presentation coaching workshop, Alan combines compassion and candor with a clear intention to bring out the best in each individual. His decades of experience with cutting edge ideas for new and better ways to deliver messages that get results, Alan’s speaking workshops have been proven winners for his clients.

The first speaker to come out of the investment business to be inducted into the National Speakers Association’s Hall of Fame, Alan’s ideas have been quoted in numerous business publications, including The Wall Street Journal, Business Week and Barron’s.

What sets Alan Parisse apart?

* Named One of the Top 21 Speakers for the 21st Century by Successful Meeting Magazine.
* First person from the investment business to be inducted into the Speakers Hall of Fame.
* Presentation coach to executives, financial advisors, sports stars and sales presenters for two decades.
* Co-author of “Questions Great Financial Advisors Ask …” called “a must read …” by industry publications and consistently ranked in the Top Ten Books for Financial Advisors.
* Guest lecturer at the Stanford Business School, UC Berkeley Graduate School of Business, UCLA Graduate School of Management and the University of Pennsylvania Wharton School of Business.
