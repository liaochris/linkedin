Joe Kern's Summary

Joe Kern started his career in with RTI International (Research Triangle Institute), as a contractor to U.S. ARMY. In this role Joe, provided support to Headquarters, Training, and Doctrine Command (TRADOC) and the Army Research Office to examine the use of virtual reality and synthetic environments in Army training. Joe then joined GFI Software in 2007 and managed the GFI Support Team responsible for the company’s’ Network, Security, and Fax offerings. In 2009 Joe moved into a Channel Manager position responsible for growing the GFI MAX hosted product suite.

While in this position, Joe has been working with channel partners to roll out services based on the GFI MAX RemoteManagement™ platform, helping them build profitable recurring revenue streams. Because of his work with the IT channel, Joe has gained significant insight on the pitfalls of business transformation strategies, and provides a common sense approach to building your business.

Joe has presented GFI MAX and the Building Blocks to Managed Services™ program to IT Groups throughout the country and participated in industry discussions and events.
