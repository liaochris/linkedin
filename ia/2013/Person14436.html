Bill Schaffler's Summary

I am an Experienced Financial Strategist and Operations Executive with a track record of creating company value by developing and executing innovative ideas at both the strategic and tactical levels. My professional experience includes progressive success at large and small companies, start-ups, leading efforts in business turnarounds, and leading integration efforts at companies after a merger or acquisition.

Bill Schaffler's Experience

Executive Vice President of Finance and Business Intelligence

Groupe Le Duff America

Privately Held; 5001-10,000 employees; Food & Beverages industry

October 2013 – Present (3 months) Dallas, TX

Le Duff America is the U.S. affiliate of French-based Groupe Le Duff.

Groupe Le Duff started with a single French café-bakery in Brest, France in 1977 and quickly grew into an international corporate and franchised family of restaurants centered around its authentic French fare and the core brand, Brioche Doreé.

Today, the combined Le Duff Family of brands is recognized as the second largest bakery-café company in the world.

Groupe Le Duff America currently owns and operates North American café brands totaling approximately 700 units. The brands include la Madeleine Country French Café, Bruegger’s Bagels, Brioche Dorée, Timothy’s World Coffee, Michel Baguette, Au Pain Dore and Mimi’s Café.

At Le Duff America, my role is primarily focused on financial management and operational improvement through the use of analytics, benchmarking & market intelligence, as well as growth opportunities for the organization by working to lead outstanding return on investment across our portfolio of brands.

President, COO & Partner

People Report & Black Box Intelligence

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Market Research industry

August 2007 – September 2013 (6 years 2 months) Dallas, TX

People Report is an interactive technology platform providing Human Capital Business Intelligence through benchmarking to the Service Sector Workforce.

Black Box Intelligence is an interactive technology platform founded in 2009 (a sister company to People Report) providing weekly, monthly and quarterly Sales and Operational Business Intelligence to the Service Sector.

Both brands collect data and report interactively every week. As of 2013, their products covered over 35,000 retail units nationwide, over 1 million employees and over $40 billion in annual sales among those units and employees.

At Black Box Intelligence I had the experience of partnering to lead the venture from an idea to a vibrant company in the software-as-a-service sector. Our client base grew from a handful of restaurant locations to over 15,000 locations in four years. Although my work here was focused on Business Intelligence and Software, my roots in and knowledge of the Restaurant Industry grew broadly as my clients were C-Suite executives from across over 150 organizations during that time.

Chief Financial Officer & Treasurer

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Restaurants industry

July 2002 – August 2007 (5 years 2 months) Dallas

La Madeleine Bakery Cafe is a Fast Casual concept operating over 60 locations in 7 States. The brand is owned by Group Le Duff, the second largest bakery cafe operator in the world.

In addition to its retail locations, La Madeleine also operates 2 manufacturing facilities dedicated to the production of wholesale breads, pastries, soups, sauces and other dessert items.

This is a great brand and it was an incredible experience working there. I had the opportunity to work in a company that had incredible customer loyalty because of great food and a unique dining experience. But it needed financial and operational changes in order to begin to grow again. We accomplished much in 5 years and put the brand back on solid footing.

Check them out at www.lamadeleine.com

In addition to supporting the TGIF brand, I was also involved in the planning and expansion of the Carlson restaurant portfolio as we developed new brands (7 in all) in a new division of the company called Emerging Brands, as well as the acquisition and integration of the fast casual concept, Pick Up Stix.

This is a company built on a great culture and famous brand. This is where I learned so much about how to run a great restaurant and had the privilege to work for great leaders who taught me so much of what I apply today in my work.
