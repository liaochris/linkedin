Dan DeRoeck's Summary

I possess a rare balance of strong technical and business knowledge, that is then fueled by my passion to make a substantial difference for my clients. My primary focus and expertise is leveraging SEO and SEM best practices to attain a strong Internet presence. Helping my clients succeed is my #1 goal.

My SEO philosophy is to attain a strong organic presence using multiple marketing channels. User engagement is at the heart of my strategy. I believe web analytics is imperative for an iterative approach to improvement of key metrics and conversions. A solid content strategy, combined with powerful SEO and link building methods makes my client Internet presence shine.

I am the founder and CEO of CSE Web Solutions, a total SMB [small and medium business] Internet marketing solutions provider. We are a strong advocate of open source platforms for low cost of ownership and effectiveness, this allows us to pass on savings and increase ROI for clients.

My company provides a wide range of Internet marketing services including Global SEO, Local Search Marketing, Social Media, PPC [Pay per Click], Content Strategy/Management and Website/blog development.

I also own and operate several web properties and a regular contributing writer for Web Pro News.

In a previous position, I was an IT Director for an automotive tier one supplier for 15 years.
