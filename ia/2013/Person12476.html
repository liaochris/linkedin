Beatrice Whelan's Summary

Email me at beatrice.whelan@sage.com.

I am an award winning digital communications, online PR and content marketing professional with a strong background in B2B marketing, search engine optimisation and social media. With 10+ years experience in multimedia development and digital marketing; I hand coded my first website in 2001, started blogging in 2004 and have been designing and optimising websites and blogs since 2006.

I currently work at Sage Ireland as Social Media & Content Specialist where I am responsible for managing the online reputation of this FTSE 100 company in Ireland. This includes setting our social media policy and strategy, content creation for social media channels, managing our brand profiles and the Sage Business Experts Programme in Ireland. I am also editor of and contributor to the Sage Ireland blog and a variety of digital content such as the various e-books and landing pages available on Sage.ie.

I have guest blogged for the Huffington Post, Tweak Your Biz and the Digital Marketing Institute and have also contributed to Business Plus Magazine and other prominent publications.

I am a passionate advocate of social media and the role of content in social media strategy and am a frequent speaker and writer on the topic.

Specialties: Social Media Marketing, Search Engine Optimisation (on and off page), Content Marketing, Business Blog Optimisation, WordPress Customisation, Social Media Training, Online PR, Google AdWords Management.
