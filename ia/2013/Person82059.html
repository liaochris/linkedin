Lloyd Brown's Summary

I am an Executive Adviser helping small and medium sized businesses improve their profit margins. I focus on the demand for your products and services, determining price sensitivity and opportunities for improved profits. Many companies that I work with are too busy to do the detailed transactional analysis and marketing research required to update their pricing strategies and tactics. With 25 years experience in a variety of roles & industries, I am the resource they rely on to improve their profits and company valuation.

Part of my process is an audit of my clients’ Competitive Strategy. How do you leverage your strengths, deal with your weaknesses, and adapt to changing market conditions? My clients report improved sales, improved profits, and better strategy integration after I help them with those items.

I work with companies ranging from several million dollar in sales to those over $100 million in revenue – what I look for are firms who want to implement changes and improve their performance. Learn more at my website, www.inflectstrategy.com, or follow my updates on twitter (@inflectstrategy). I am a very enthusiastic member of the Executive Resource Group, www.execresources.org, a network of service providers to medium sized businesses.


Specialties: Marketing Strategy and Implementation, Pricing Strategy and Tactics, Brand Management, Logistics, Quantitative Business Analysis, Business Strategy, Manufacturing, Product Distribution, Decision Support Services
