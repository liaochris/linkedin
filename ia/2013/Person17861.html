Brian Cavanaugh's Summary

Brian Cavanaugh is a Senior Mortgage Banker assisting potential Homebuyers with purchase mortgage financing and current Homeowners with mortgage refinancings in Massachusetts. During his 11 years of experience in Mortgage Banking in Massachusetts Brian has helped thousands of families with affordable and financially responsible mortgage financing solutions.

Named “25 Most Connected Mortgage Professionals” by National Mortgage Professional Magazine

Brian Cavanaugh is the Preferred Mortgage Lender for Partners Healthcare / Brigham & Women's Hospital, Fidelity Investments and Boston Medical Center

Brian Cavanaugh is a nationally-recognized mortgage expert. He writes the popular mortgage blog Smarterborrowing.com

Residential Mortgage Services, Inc. is a New England based and locally operated mortgage banking company. Since 1991, RMS has grown from its corporate office in South Portland to include numerous branch locations in Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Connecticut, Rhode Island and Maine.

We are a fully licensed direct lender and an independent provider of a vast selection of home financing options for 1 - 4 family home purchases & refinance transactions. Programs are available for owner occupied primary residences, second homes, condos, and investment properties. We also offer non-conforming portfolio products. RMS is a direct endorsed lender for FHA, VA, USDA/RD, and Mass Housing (MHFA). RMS is also a member of the National Mortgage Bankers and the Better Business Bureau.
