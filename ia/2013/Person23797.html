Chris Syme's Summary

About CKSymeMedia Group:
Chris Syme is the founder of CKSyme Media group, an agency experienced in crisis and reputation communications including PR and social media training for athletics and higher ed. We also offer a number of training modules in the areas of crisis communications, brand advocacy, and social media management. See the media link below for information on our popular "Practice Safe Social" workshop-- teaching the responsible use of social media to build brand advocacy and protect reputation. The workshop is perfect for training student-athletes, coaches, pro athletes,businesses, and higher ed advancement teams. Please see our website for more information including our syndicated blog at http://www.cksyme.org. We also have a company page on LinkedIn (CKSyme Media Group).

Our clients include higher education, college athletics, professional sports programs, nonprofits, and businesses. Chris Syme has national speaking experience on social media strategy and training, branding, crisis communications, and reputation recovery. Book Chris to present at your conference today. Email chris@cksyme.org.

Chris Syme's new book, Practice Safe Social, has just been released! The book is a concise manual on teaching the responsible use of social media.Check it out on Amazon.com. Click the link on the graphic below to get more information.

The CKSyme Media Group authored three different studies on the use of social media in crisis in 2011-13 with the Council for Advancement and Support of Education (CASE) and the College Sports Information Directors of America (CoSIDA). Results are available on our website at http://www.cksyme.org/social-media-research/. Chris Syme's first book, Listen, Engage, Respond: Crisis Communications in Real-Time, was published in May 2012 and is available on Amazon.

We do offer a fee waiver on an initial consultation for new clients. Contact us at chris@cksyme.org for more information.
