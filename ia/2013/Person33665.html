Debra Murphy's Summary

I am a marketing coach and mentor passionate about helping small business owners take control of a critical business asset – their online digital footprint - and use it to increase visibility and generate inbound leads. Through expert guidance and support, they achieve success by learning, incorporating and executing marketing activities that take their business from mediocre to marvelous.

Although experienced across all traditional marketing channels, I specialize in inbound marketing, a combination of search, social media and content marketing, enabling small businesses to create a larger digital footprint that increases their visibility and generates more quality inbound leads.

I enjoy sharing knowledge, inspiring success and motivating business owners individually, in groups and by speaking at events.

I post regularly on my Masterful Marketing blog covering small business marketing. My published work includes "Creating a Simplified, Integrated Practice Marketing Plan" for "The Business of Healthcare" three volume set edited by Kenneth H. Cohn, M.D. and Douglas E. Hough, PH.D. and "Cultivate Market Perception" for the co-authored work "Create the Business Breakthrough You Want: Secrets and Strategies from the World's Greatest Mentors" featuring business giants Brian Tracy, Robert G. Allen, Bob Proctor and Mark Victor Hansen (Chicken Soup for The Soul) and endorsed by Dr. Stephen Covey and Ken Blanchard.
