Mark Hemmis' Summary

As the Senior Web Designer for QAD my role is to design for web and mobile at a global level to accurately reflect corporate brand. I create concepts that drive campaigns, increase web traffic and conversions, and develop graphics that reflect QAD’s commitment to our customers.

Prior to QAD, I was the lead creative at AR & Co. Marketing, where my role was to help our clients achieve a new level of recognition by providing the tools needed to maximize the potential of their businesses. I achieved this by providing high-quality graphic design and identity services, formulating and implementing targeted social media strategies, and designing dynamic websites that were properly optimized for search engine results.

Currently, I'm self-developing my newest passions in my free time: user experience design, user interface design, and mobile application development. Outside of work I like to travel and generally be active anywhere without a roof. I also enjoy a good espresso.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Web Design, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Branding, Creative, Email Marketing, Typography, Photography, Social Media, Professional Writing, UX / UI Design, Mobile App Design.
