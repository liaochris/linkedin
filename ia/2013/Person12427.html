Understanding the business of Banking

Banks make money when customers deposit money into their bank. The bank will then invest a significant amount of your money into real estate notes aka; mortgages. Now SAM investors have an opportunity to make the same investment at a significant discount because real estate prices have dropped significantly and note holders are willing to sell at discounted prices.

Insurance companies offer annuities that yield 5-6 percent and they also take customer deposits and buy 1st trust deeds. SAM wants to educate investors and offer them the same investment vehicle that the largest corporations in the world buy. SAM buys a 1st trust deed (mortgage) with a loan to value of 60 percent or less. Therefore if today’s property value is $100k we will buy the 1st for no more than $60k with a yield of 12-14 percent.
