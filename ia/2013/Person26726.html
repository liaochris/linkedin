  • 6 Tips and Ways to Sell Your Home Fast!

    • Connie McGregor
    • April 29, 2013
    Authors: Connie McGregor

    6 tips and ways to sell your home fast!

    http://wp.me/p2PIEz-Zb

    List your home on a Friday. Homes listed on a Friday sell faster (81 days on the market, on average) for a price closer to their asking price (99.1 percent of the original asking price, on average) than homes listed on any other day of the week.

    Put your home on the market between April and June: April is the best month to list. Homes listed in April sell for closer to their original asking price, with a 99.2 percent sale price-to-list price ratio, compared with a 97.3 percent ratio for homes listed in December. The period of March through June had the largest percentage of homes that sold within 90 days

    Price your home right the first time. In the first week that a listing goes on the market, it receives nearly four times more visits on real estate websites than it does a month later. How do you know how to price things right to sell a home quickly? Be reasonable about comps—the prices that houses nearby have sold—and price your home competitively, if not slightly below market.

    Use professional photos in your listing. Listings with photos taken with professional photography equipment sold for prices considerably closer to their asking price than those photographed with point-and-shoot photos.

    Get rid of the clutter. A smart suggestion is to rent one of those PODS, and move all storage items from the garage and basement into them.

    Have your home professionally staged. Having sold, shown and held open houses, I always find when homes are staged professionally they sell faster! Spending a couple thousand to have the house staged at its original price probably does pay off in the long run.
