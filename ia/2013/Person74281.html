Ken Mattsson's Summary

My entire life has been about connecting dreams and actions. I’ve always been interested in either getting clarity about what do I or others really want, and making action steps and enrolling others into making it happen. I’ve ended up putting these together by connecting dreamers to desired outcome and accomplishers to hidden desires.

From childhood, I’ve always been the type to rally the troops to a goal and motivating them to make it happen. At age 13, I organized over 25 different dance organizations in 6 states to coordinate events and share information, which resulted in my being the guest speaker to a dance organization in front of 300 people at age 15. Through writing in magazines, networking, & planning, I increased attendance at events all over New England. That’s where I learned about motivating others and getting things done.

From helping authors improve their books, to instructing people on how to use software better, I listened to discover people's real goals, and then help them see tangible actions to take to make it happen. I’ve found that both steps are equally important, as dreams without actions go nowhere, and actions without dreams can get you into places you don’t want to be!

For the last 10 years, I’ve specialized in helping clients in their careers connect their spirit and passions to action steps resulting in happiness. Sometimes it’s guiding someone who doesn’t know what they want. Other times, it’s doing the “dream archaeology” to uncover what passions people really have. Sometimes both. Whatever it is, it’s joyful to ride in the co-pilot’s seat of their lives.

I have a passion for connecting with people and getting them to see their true value, and then showing them ways to make it happen. In a personal setting, it tends to be more of the former. In a business setting, it tends to be more of the later. Whichever setting, I never know what will be presented, and that makes the work a joy.
