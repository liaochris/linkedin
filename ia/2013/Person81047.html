Linda Lackey's Summary

Linda Lackey is highly regarded and well-known in the real estate industry for her expertise and professionalism. A top realtor in Santa Monica, Pacific Palisades and Brentwood for more than twenty years, Linda ensures her clients, ranging from established professionals to developers and celebrities, receive the best possible return and results.

Understanding that finding the right realtor can be challenging, Linda prides herself on providing clients with a high level of customer service, including thorough attention to detail, knowledge of the local market, and sensitivity to the needs of both buyers and sellers. Her areas of experience include residential transactions involving single family homes, townhomes and condos as well as investment properties. Linda’s approach to the business of real estate has earned her an impressive 80% referral rate and a position in the top 1% of Coldwell Banker realtors.
