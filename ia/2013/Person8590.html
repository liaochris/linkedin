Ann Zuccardy's Summary

OVERVIEW

Published writer, speaker, and creative communications professional.

25+ years experience as: social media consultant, presenter, marketer, technical writer, blogger, copywriter, food writer, editor, public relations professional, mentor, teacher.

HIGHLIGHTS

2013 - PR and Marketing Director (volunteer) - Miss Vermont Scholarship Organization

2013 - VP of Public Relations (volunteer) - Toastmasters of Greater Burlington, Vermont

2012 to present: CEO of AZ Communications, LLC. Provides copywriting and social media coaching services. Currently writing a book about traumatic brain injury (TBI). Frequent speaker/presenter on social media marketing.

2008 to 2012: Communications Specialist at National Life Group. Worked in corporate communications at National Life Group, a Fortune 1000 financial services company, where I was the managing editor of a weekly employee email newsletter. I created and managed a corporate and community garden at National Life Group in 2010. In 2011 we donated 500 lbs. of produce to local food non-profits.

1996 to 2011: President of Vermont Shortbread Company, an international specialty food company I created.

I am committed to working with companies and causes whose values are in alignment with my own. I am a five-year reading mentor with Everybody Wins! Vermont, a national literacy program and a volunteer on the board of directors in charge of marketing, social media, and PR with the Miss Vermont Scholarship Organization.

For more information about my social media and professional writing and speaking activities, visit my About Me page: http://about.me/ann.zuccardy
