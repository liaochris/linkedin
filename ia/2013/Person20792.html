Casey Stillman's Summary

As a small business owner and serial entrepreneur for the past 20+ years I made a ton of mistakes!

My first entrepreneurial experience began at the age of 19 when I started my FIRST business, since then, I served 8 years in the United States Marine Corps, I then went on to start 6 of my own businesses, SOLD 2 of them (one of which was part of a National Real Estate Franchise with 2 locations that I operated for over 5 years located at the Jersey Shore).

In 2007, I sold my real estate offices to REMAX of New Jersey and became a Regional Publisher of a B2B Success Magazine (called Performance Magazine) and started a small business consulting firm. My firm is comprised of a group of specialized service partners that provide consulting services to small business owners and independent professionals.

We specialize in small business growth strategies that begin with the development of a solid company infrastructure, then, through strategic planning, we assist the small business owner in the development and implementation of ON and OFFLINE marketing campaigns, integrated sales and follow-up techniques; then complete the whole process with the development and automation of internal systems and processes that ensure the success of the business!

I often credit my achievements to learning from my failures!

Specialties: I consider myself to be a creative thinker, a patient listener, results oriented, very organized, honest, ambitious, a natural sales person, a gifted visionary and highly self-motivated; a true team player. I am also fortunate to have the unique ability to see a solution when others don't! Finding the right answer, in the right situation, at just the right time is my key to success!
