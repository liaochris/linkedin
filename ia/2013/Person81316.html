Linnie S. Carter, Ph.D., APR's Summary

Entrepreneur. Educator. Everywoman.

Linnie S. Carter, Ph.D., APR (pronounced “Line-knee”) has almost 20 years of experience in public relations, communications and fundraising.

She has held leadership positions at many organizations, including three community colleges and two major universities.

Carter is known for her extensive knowledge of public relations, communications and fundraising; focus on excellence; unparalleled work ethic; and infectious laugh. She is also a change agent and has the uncanny ability to take “messes” – including organizations, situations and projects – and transform them into greatness. Due to her leadership and experience, Carter’s employers and clients have achieved accomplishments ranging from significantly increased public awareness and unprecedented media coverage to record student enrollment and surpassed fundraising goals. According to one of her mentors, Carter is a “Tri-B” – she has brains, brawn and beauty.

Carter earned a doctorate from Old Dominion University and master’s and bachelor’s degrees in mass communications from VCU. Also, she earned the Accredited in Public Relations (APR) designation from the Universal Accreditation Board.

P.S. “Linnie” rhymes with “tiny.”
