Gemma Manning's Summary

I am a strategic marketing and communications professional with more than 12 years experience in a broad range of industries ranging from the pharmaceutical sector to the management and financial consulting sectors to telecommunications, IT, tourism and retail.

I established Manning & Co. to fill a gap in the marketing consultancy market. We provide a top class, end-to-end strategic and integrated marketing service without charging over-the-top fees. With a tagline of ‘Measurable Marketing Management’, our aim is to provide strategic yet practical, effective and measurable marketing, brand and PR solutions.

Why are we so passionate about delivering measurable marketing results? During my 6 years of in-house marketing management I was the client of many marketing/PR consultancies and was surprised at the lack of reporting, tracking and measuring of marketing campaigns. I was also disappointed at the lack of innovation of agencies in general.

My aim is for Manning & Co. to work as an extension of the client company, providing the required support and expertise to senior management or the business owner directly and be 'innovative' - always producing new and exciting solutions.

With full-service strategic marketing experience from devising high level strategies through to implmentation of programmes, we understand all aspects of not only marketing but sales too.

Manning & Co. also aims to play a role in elevating the importance of the marketing profession and the value it can deliver businesses. Marketing is often an after-thought and doesn’t receive the attention it warrants. We want to do our part to change this and educate businesses as to what marketing really means and the value it can bring. To a large extent marketing is still associated with advertising and promotional goods. In today's environment, marketing means a whole lot more than just this and needs to be considered as an integrated approach - rather than just a once off campaign.
