John Markowski's Summary

Having launched Dream Builders & Remodeling in 1999, we have been assisting families in the Capital Region area build and renovate their homes ever since. With an ever growing collection of projects, we take the stress out of remodeling and bring our clients ideas to life! In 2007, we introduced CurbScapes of New York, our landscape division, to assist with the yard/landscaping beautification at the close of our construction projects. With our signature product, landscape curbing, we have brought a product to the market that has enabled homeowners and businesses alike to have an affordable alternative to constant plant bed maintenance while making their property really stand out! Be sure to visit our web sites to see more information about the products and services we offer.
