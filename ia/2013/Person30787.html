Darren Marks darren@hypnosisappstore.com's Summary

London and Hertfordshire based hypnotherapist and NLP master practitioner. He worked as a hypnotherapist for The South East Cancer Help Centre for many years and was invited to demonstrate hypnotherapy for The Prince of Wales during a visit of the Prince to the centre. He was elected a Fellow of The Institute of Clinical Hypnosis in recognition of contribution to the Institute and the profession as a whole.

A member of The General Hypnotherapy Register who have awarded the Senior Qualification in Hypnotherapy Practice. Also a member of The NHS Directory of Complementary and Alternative Practitioners and a featured therapist of The Hypnotherapy Association. A certified hypnosis instructor for the International Association of Counsellors and Therapists and a member of The International Medical and Dental Hypnotherapy Association.

Specialties

Helping people to let go of negative emotions and limiting beliefs.
