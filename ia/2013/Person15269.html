Bobby Dyer's Summary

I am a principal mechanical engineer at On Demand Therapeutics where I develop miniature implants for treatment of eye diseases. I advanced my interests in the medical design field while working in the BioInstrumentation Laboratory at MIT where I participated in the development of implantable electrode arrays for the brain as well as a novel drug delivery device. I received my Bachelors and Masters degrees in mechanical engineering from MIT in 2001 and 2003.

While not tinkering with devices you can find me participating in one of my many other extra circular activities such as photography, painting or woodworking. For more information on Bobby please visit my website at www.bobbydyer.com.
