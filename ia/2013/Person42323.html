Eric Pier's Summary

www.ericpier.com/projects


My name is Eric Pier.

I am a San Francisco based Senior Art Director with substantial industry experience working on 360 degree integrated campaigns. Focusing on brand development and marketing collateral for advertising in both digital and print media. I conceptualize, design, and project manage to execute rich campaign experiences.

I have worked on projects for companies such as: S&P Dow Jones, New York Presbyterian Hospital, Casa Dragones Tequila, The Wall Street Journal, Barnes & Noble, The W Hotel, DSquared, The Lowell Hotel, Audio Network, Bessemer Trust, New York Institute of Technology, Altegris Investments, MetLife, Forward Investments, TIAA-CREF, Fireman's Fund Insurance, and more.

Specialties: Website Design & Architecture, Online Strategy, Site Organization, Email Campaigns, Outdoor Advertising, Branding, Logo & Identities, Packaging, Print Ads, Annual Reports, Brochures/Folded Matter, Business Cards/Letterhead, Postcards/Mailers, Posters/Trade Show Displays, Event/Exhibition Branding, Direct Mail Marketing Campaigns, Identity Packages, Photo retouching, Video direction/ editing, Custom Facebook, and Flash animation.
