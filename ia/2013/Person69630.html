  • The New Wheel ... #FLATTENSF

    • October 2011 to April 2012
    Team Members: Jacob PEP Pepper, Zachary Maricondia, Chris Cerrato

    San Francisco has 72 hills totaling over 30,000 feet of vert. In other words, cycling is all but impossible for anyone outside of the dedicated spandex set.

    The New Wheel was looking to change this, and to find a way to show how their electric bicycles could effectively flatten SF and make the entire city accessible by bike.

    The posters feature 4 iconic SF landmarks portrayed in international orange, the color of the Golden Gate Bridge. The design calls for the interconnecting posters to be hung at a 30º angle, simply and elegantly demonstrating the effect of a New Wheel eBike.
