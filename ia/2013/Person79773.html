Lee Pettijohn's Summary

My whole goal in life is helping people. More specifically, helping people make things easier. Being an entrepreneur, I have a huge heart for business owners which is why marketing is one of my favorite areas of expertise. Marketing in today's world takes a wealth of knowledge.

Fortunately, I have been involved with different marketing efforts for several years now. Some of the areas include graphic design, web development, social media marketing and search engine optimization. Each of these has so much information that it's almost impossible for one business to be an expert in all of them. I am glad to say that the knowledge base I have for each of these areas is between intermediate and expert. What I am not able to do, I have a great set of resources to fall back on.

If your business is looking for an experienced marketing consultant who can not only inform you of marketing strategies but can put them into place, please contact me. I have also done projects for cities as well and enjoy the challenge of bringing a group of businesses together in unity and making a difference on a large scale.
