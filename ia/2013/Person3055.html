Alan Blakely, AIAP's Summary

Alan Blakely is an internationally recognized architectural and commercial photographer who creates photographs of People, Products and Places™ for advertising, marketing, public relations and editorial applications. Alan is considered to be one of the top architectural and commercial photographers at work today with a career that spans three decades.

Alan is the founder and director of the Association of Independent Architectural Photographers™ (AIAP), Real Estate Photographers of America & International™ (REPAI) and a regular contributor to magazines and online forums in both the photography and architectural trades.

Alan's business headquarters is based in Salt Lake City, Utah and he travels throughout North America and select international destinations on assignment for corporations and magazines.
