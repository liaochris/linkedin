Mia Shin's Summary

I represent Sellers and Buyers of luxury highrise condominium lifestyle. My areas of expertise is in The Westside of Los Angeles, Wilshire Corridor and Century City, where I reside and understand the value of living in a full service building. I also represent like properties in Beverly Hills, Downtown Los Angeles, Marina Del Rey, Ocean Avenue in Santa Monica.

I also represent developers of highrise condominiums. I offer my knowledge of the market conditions and I consult on the planning and development stage, I was the Broker of Record for Crescent Heights of America.

I also represent clients who have luxury properties who want to sell their properties to move into their Luxury Highrise lifestyle. I am currently affiliated with The John Aaroe Group of Beverly Hills. We have the ability to market the properties to an international audience of investors, I have traveled the world and have appreciation for all different cultures and nationalities.

My Team of experienced agents and staff, together, understand the needs of our clients, the entertainment industry's need for temporary housing, privacy and discretion and can offer our clients a variety of real estate needs, investments, luxury estates, condominiums, for purchase and redevelopment as well as acquisitions for buy and hold.

I was raised in Asia, New York City, Santa Monica and in La Jolla. My educational background is the Fine Arts, Interior Design and in the past 25 years, in Real-Estate, following in the path of my father's passion in all the Arts, Architecture, who was a Great MIT Engineer, the reason for my move to the US. My exacting standards and diverse interests carry over into all aspects of my daily life which includes, buying and selling as well as improving properties for myself and for my clients.

I look forward to meeting you, getting to know you, earning your trust and fulfilling all your expectations with your real-estate needs.
