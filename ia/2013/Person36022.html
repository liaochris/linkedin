Tadeus Mucelli's Summary

Main:
DJ | Music Producer | Producer and Culture Manager

Biography Summary:

Multi-talented Tadeus Mariano Mucelli Motta, Art Name Dj Tee or (3nity), was born in 1979 in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. He’s a Dj and producer in many fields of electronic music: he’s taught the art of music production by means of a computer at ACOT and has curated various editions of FAD, a digital art festival organized in his city. In addition he founded a label, Digital Conteudo Records, with which he produces a music spectrum that ranges from pop to avant-garde, as well as being tied to a visual and digital art magazine, CODE. As a producer, since 2005 to today.

Tadeus Mucelli (DJ Tee) é DJ, produtor musical, artista audiovisual e gestor cultural atuante na cidade de Belo Horizonte desde 1996. Seus principais trabalhos estão direcionados em dois festivais do Estado de Minas Gerais, FAD e BPM, ambos focados na linguagem eletrônica de novas mídias.

Além disso, possui carreira ativa como produtor musical e audiovisual com diversos trabalhos lançados no Brasil e Exterior.

Atuou também como professor de Produção Musical Eletrônica na faculdade Estácio de Sá em Belo Horizonte.

Tee é fundador do Selo Fonográfico independente Conteúdo Records( dede 2004), um dos pioneiros no Brasil quando o assunto é Netlabel

New business and technology combined with culture (audiovisual).
