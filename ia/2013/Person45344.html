Sherry Shriver's Summary

Sherry Shriver is one of the most highly regarded real estate professional in the North County Coastal area. Both her clients and her peers can attest to her thoroughness and expertise. Her word is gold and her abilities are limitless! She is the consummate professional in her dealings with buyers and sellers; is extremely knowledgeable and takes pride in a strong work ethic. Her enthusiasm and energy level are boundless.

The Leader in Luxury Real Estate!
Sherry Shriver specializes in the North County Coastal areas with an emphasis on Rancho Santa Fe, Del Mar and Solana Beach. She represents some of the most magnificent properties in the county.

She knows the area inside and out and is a savvy professional with the client’s best interest at heart. Sherry has been a real estate professional since 1977.

Personal History
Sherry grew up in Northeastern Ohio in the Akron area where she was among the youngest real estate agents in the state back in 1977. She moved to San Diego in 1979 to continue her real estate career. Sherry is a graduate of UCSD.

Sherry loves the arts and is a subscriber to The Old Globe Theatre and The La Jolla Playhouse, San Diego Symphony and The Birch Aquarium. She’s a compassionate Animal Advocate and some of her charities are the ASPCA, the local and national Humane Society, the Helen Woodward Animal Center, PETA and PCRM (Physicians for Responsible Medicine).

Rancho Santa Fe’s Expert in Luxury Homes!
Sherry knows the market, she listens to her clients, acts in their best interest, counsels where and when needed and negotiates the best deal possible! She is well versed in real estate procedures, forms and disclosures and is able to move smoothly through transactions. Sherry’s empathetic nature gives her clients the security and freedom to make good choices.

Specialties

High-End Real Estate, Property Marketing, Negotiation, Rancho Santa Fe and Del Mar real estate.
