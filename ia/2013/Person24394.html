Christopher Fields, MLHR's Summary

★Social Media Human Resources Consultant ★ Expert Resume Writer ★ HR Content Writer

HR is an awesome profession. I love being involved in HR. As a Consultant I help companies, young HR professionals, friends and associates with human resources advice. I particularly love employee relations issues, things like harassment charges, unfair workplace and hiring violations, compliance problems, and writing EEOC claims, things of that nature.

I began my HR career as a Corporate Trainer with Generalist duties. In 2011, I made the best decision of my life which was to start the CostofWork,com blogsite. Because of that decision, I have created a great reputation as an HR Content Writer and Social Media HR Enthusiast working with SmartRecruiters.com, eSkills.com, Work4Labs.com and now defunct Jobsite.com.

May of 2013, I launched the ResumeCrusade.com website focusing on using my HR knowledge, social connections and resources to deciphering the complex job market and search. I've helped hundreds of people attain better jobs and career paths, and I love it.

As an ambitious person, I am always interested in new challenges and opportunities to utilize my skills and abilities. I want to be more involved in driving organizational culture and branding through HR and social media. Community Management or Social Network Brand Strategists sounds great to me.
