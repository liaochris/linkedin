Dan Gray Sr's Summary

I've been a dry cleaner, pretty much all my life: a graduate of Indiana University with a Degree in Business Management. Having started as a pant presser, to running a small mom & pop shop, freelance and consulting, and managing a multi-million dollar operation; my skills at being a top-notch dry cleaner are simply to be the best.

And then I got involved with the "net"! With nothing more than a simple desire to help my collegues and give back to the industry, what it has given to me, I created what has now become the most popular Dry Cleaners Directory online today... www.drycleaners-directory.us

Now there are also two additional Directory's for Coin Laundromats and Leather Specialist.
www.laundromat-directory.com / www.leather-directory.us
