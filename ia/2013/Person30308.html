Dan O'Leary's Summary

Brand designer specialising in concept creation and development of all aspects of visual brand identity. Over fifteen years' experience in print, packaging, environmental graphics, signage and wayfinding in the UK and Canada.

Specialties: Development of brand creative platforms and identity systems, branded environments, three-dimensional branding, packaging, retail graphics, signage & wayfinding, naming, brand strategy, identity guidelines & application, advertising art direction.
