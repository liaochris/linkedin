Linda Zelm,'s Summary

My business is focused on small to mid sized businesses of 500 employees or less who could be eligible for SBA Financing. Over the past 30 years, I have my passion for finding ways to assist small businesses when they need a location for their operations. Using 30 years of knowledge and negotiation skills, experience with documents, relationships with developers and owners, I have consistently worked out deals for small businesses to enhance their image, lower costs and drive customers to their doors.

I am always thinking "How can I help this small business owner make the best possible choices thus ensuring that the small business owner is getting the best deal and at the same time, preventing mistakes that would keep him/her from growing?"

Specialties: Small Business agency representation for real estate location needs taking a transaction from the business plan through the opening of the office or store and follow up. Includes site selection, negotiation, analysis of lease versus buy, coordination of construction, county and city requirements, leases and purchase agreement preparation, liaison with landlords, sellers and brokers, securing financing, lease renewals, demographic studies, multiple location strategies and general consulting.

Although my major strength is retail, I have negotiated deals for small businesses in a variety of business activities needing warehouse, production or office facilities.
