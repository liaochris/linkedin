HARBOR STYLE Magazine is the authentic community magazine for the Greater Charlotte Harbor area of Florida's Gulf coast. With offices in the historic riverfront village of Punta Gorda, the magazine celebrates the lives, heritage, and future of the people who call this magnificent part of paradise "home". People, events, nature, food & wine, sports, homes and style..."The Harbor is Happening" every month in HARBOR STYLE.
