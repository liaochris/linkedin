Mark E Tisdale's Summary

Task oriented problem solver with ten years of experience managing projects in credit marketing services at Equifax. Have worked both in data processing with project fulfillment as well as consulting directly with customers gathering their requirements.

Left Atlanta four years ago and have taken an extended career break from project management while traveling and concentrating on growing my business as a visual artist.

Always interested in discussing new opportunities in either field. On the project management side of the house, I am especially interested in non-profits or challenging positions where I can grow new skills. On the visual artist side, I primarily work on marketing my art prints through multiple venues. I am open to new opportunities to get my work in front of people, such as licensing or working with interior designers and art consultants.

Specialties: High Volume Data Processing, Rudimentary Programming, Process Analysis, and Customer Consultation.
