Karen Kramer's Experience

Adjunct Faculty

Center for Creative Leadership

Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

March 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months)

After nearly 17-years as a full-time employee, Dr. Karen Kramer is now an adjunct faculty member for the Center for Creative Leadership (so she can devote full-time to her own life coaching practice). As an adjunct, she:
• Facilitates the Leadership Development Program (LDP) for mid-level managers
• Facilitates the Maximizing Your Leadership Potential (MLP) program for newer and project managers, and
• Facilitates, mentors, leads small groups, and conducts the outdoor activities for the Young Women’s Leadership Program (YWLP) for teen girls entering their junior and senior years of high school

Teen & Young Adult Life Coach

Finding Your Pearls (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

September 2009 – Present (4 years 1 month) Greater San Diego Area

Dr. Karen Kramer is the founder of Finding Your Pearls Life Coaching. Since 1993, she has inspired diverse individuals ranging from teens and college students through those new in management up to the executive level.
• Karen’s goal each year is to positively impact the lives of teens and young adults (age 12-30) through one-on-one life coaching, presentations, and workshops.
• As a Certified Teen Wisdom™ Life Coach and an adjunct faculty member and executive coach for the Center for Creative Leadership's (CCL®), Karen brings to her work both the professional and personal aspects of life coaching.
• Some of the topics she covers in coaching include relationships, teen-parent communication, grade improvement, college and career planning, stress and time management, and marriage and family planning.
• Her approach to coaching incorporates assessing one’s self, setting goals, taking action, receiving support, gaining accountability, and sustaining results.
• As a life coach, she is a trusted mentor, guide, and cheerleader to help teens and young adults become their best self by efficiently and effectively achieving the goals they want to accomplish.

Senior Enterprise Associate

Center for Creative Leadership

Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

May 1993 – September 2009 (16 years 5 months)

Dr. Kramer’s interest in business and leadership led her to join the Center for Creative Leadership’s (CCL®) San Diego Campus in 1993.
• During her tenure with CCL, she held a number of positions including Senior Program Coordinator, Revision Project Manager for the Leadership Development Program (LDP)®, Program Support Manager, classroom and small group facilitator, executive coach, and Program Manager for the Leadership development Program (LDP)® and the Foundations of Leadership (FOL).
• Karen has experience working with individuals, teams, and organizations as she has designed and delivered leadership programs for new managers through senior executives.
• Open-enrollment leadership development program she has worked with include LeaderLab®, the Global Leadership Program, the Emerging Leaders Program, Leadership Development Program (LDP)®, Looking Glass Experience (LGE), Foundations of Leadership (FOL), Assessment Certification Workshop (ACW), and the Young Women's Leadership Program (YWLP).
• She also facilitated programs in Spain and Singapore, spent six weeks at the Brussels campus as an internal consultant to improve efficiency, and has been involved in research on generational differences and the importance of emotional intelligence in leadership.
• She co-authored Emerging Leaders: An Annotated Bibliography and has written several articles on emotional intelligence.
