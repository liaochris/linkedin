Greg Mason's Experience

Director

Aquarius Design Solutions

Privately Held; Myself Only; Graphic Design industry

March 2002 – Present (11 years 6 months)

Greg Mason has been producing first class graphic design solutions for employers and clients since 1990.

During this time Greg has worked in a number of corporate markets, defining and sharpening his skills as a designer to take projects from initial concept to finished product seamlessly. He has also been a successful small business owner and director of creative agency Aquarius Design Solutions since 2002.

Greg has worked with and understands the unique time pressures and quality requirements of larger businesses. This includes anything from compelling creative design, right through to sophisticated multi-page newsletters and documentations that reflect corporate brand values.

Greg is very passionate about all aspects graphic design and believes in the motto ‘you only get one chance to make a first impression’. As such, he strives for creative excellence for his clients each and every time.

Greg’s skills reach beyond helping clients with just ‘design’ solutions; he constantly uses ‘outside the box’ thinking to present new and/or innovative ways to bring products to the market.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Business Marketing, Corporate Identity Creation, Brand Management and Print Management Solutions.
