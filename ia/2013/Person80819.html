Lori R Sacks' Summary

I specialize in guiding business owners towards a place of authentic identity from which they can passionately pursue their goals and dreams.

As a business coach, I guide clients towards a refined identity that offers the opportunity to come from a place of authenticity and to connect to their own ideal clients and target markets.

My primary areas of focus with clients are: launch new products or services, increase customer or client base, identify new niche markets, and re-brand an existing business or coaching practice. I also work with Direct Sales professionals to help them create a personal brand and to communicate more successfully with clients and their teams to encourage growth and increase residual income.

I have chosen to branch out into other areas of business and not only work with Direct Sales Professionals as a business coach to increase their residual income, build personal brands, and clarify issues related to slow growth or team stagnation but I also become a part of some carefully selected Direct Sales opportunities.

I had been a small business consultant for over 15 years. For all those years I specialized in working with start ups in contract negotiation, joint ventures, distribution channel management, branding and identity, online presence and marketing. It was a natural evolution professionally to combine my life coaching experience and training and to transition my consulting practice into a Business Coaching practice. My background in online marketing, copywriting, online research, search engine marketing and search engine optimization makes me uniquely qualified to help a client make their brand cohesive across all channels and to build brand integrity alongside brand identity.

Specialties: business growth, branding and identity, Directs Sales coaching, search engine marketing, internet research
