Mark Goren's Summary

Mark teamed up with Nexalogy Environics in March 2011 to drive its research and insights team. Since, he's implemented processes to transform the approach and deliverables emerging from Nexalogy’s services division while forging new working relationships with agencies to help their clients execute on insights derived from social listening. In April 2012, after Nexalogy was named Ford Canada's agency of record for Social Media Measurement, Analytics and Reporting, Mark has lead the account, working with Nexalogy's colleagues at Environics Research Group to deliver industry-leading social media research, intelligence and analytics, while merging social media metrics with traditional research methodologies to evaluate the success of the client's owned online channels.

During his career, Mark has worked at communications agencies and with clients of his own, helping them get up to speed on social media tools, technologies and tactics, while preparing them to add social media programs using research as a strategic base.

Mark has also lead workshops and presented at different social media conferences dating back to the fall of 2008. These presentations include social media case studies, primers, listening platform overviews and workshops.

Earlier in his career, Mark was an award-winning copywriter at agencies in Toronto, Ottawa and Montreal.

Specialties:
• Social media intelligence – custom reports, insights, links, benchmarking
• Social media listening/monitoring program design: Setting up boolean search queries to maximize collection of relevant data
• Social media training – Group and one-on-one training sessions tailored to your business needs
• Web strategy development – Determining how your web presence and website work together to grow your brand
