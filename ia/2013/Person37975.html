Dr. Jen Faber's Summary

Dr. Jen is the president of Kinetic Health Alternatives, a team of Concierge Wellness Doctors. Dr. Jen has treated Hollywood producers and Broadway stars to Lady Gaga’s Tour, the Washington Ballet, MSNBC, The Washington Post, Motley Fool, and National Geographic. She recently spoke alongside Oprah Winfrey’s Personal Trainer and author Tonya Lewis Lee. Dr. Jen is also the wellness guru for IamModern.com and has been featured in the Washingtonian, Modern Luxury Magazine, Washington Life Magazine, and more for her innovative approach to wellness.

“As the founder of Kinetic Health Alternatives, I wanted to look at health, wellness, and productivity in a new way. My goal is to treat and prevent the chronic aches, pains, and stresses that we “just deal with” every day. I also wanted to break out of the traditional office setting and bring treatment on-location to patients. This includes their home, office, on tour or any place that is convenient.”

Dr. Jen received her B.S. in Kinesiology and Social Psychology at the University of Wisconsin and her D.C. at Palmer College of Chiropractic. She began her clinical career in Morocco as one of fifteen resident doctors in Palmer’s Clinic Abroad Program, which is geared towards providing humanitarian assistance to under-served populations. Dr. Jen went on to complete her residency at Back to Living, the leading alternative medicine wellness center in Wellington, New Zealand.

Specialties

concierge wellness, progressive corporate wellness solutions, preventive health care strategies, alternative medicine and natural healing, pain prevention, health transformation workshops
