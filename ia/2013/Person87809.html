Martin Tindall's Summary

Martin Tindall's career started with a core focus in technology and corporate compliance. After completing studies in Technology, Law and Accounting, Mr. Tindall spent several years in the United States, Untied Kingdom, Australia and New Zealand creating teams and developing technology platforms for international trade and finance.

Holding key executive positions over the past 15 years with both private and publicly listed companies, Martin Tindall's appointments have included; Managing Director, Chief Information Officer, Financial Controller, Vice President, Head of eBusiness and Director of Special Operations. In each of these roles, specific focus was in creating solutions to complex problems of technology, finance, structuring and operations. Tindall has served on the Board of Directors of many of these companies providing strategic advice and business connectivity.

Today, Martin Tindall is an Executive Director of Kronos International Investments Limited, and is responsible for international financing and capital management for projects and partnerships. In this position his key focus is the acquisition of capital by leveraging US employment-based visas (EB5) and investment by foreign nationals into US projects.

Martin Tindall is involved with the development, incubation and financing of several Kronos projects and partnerships. In addition providing valuable oversight of technology platform development and implementation. These projects and partnerships include Blue Sky Biomass, South Pacific BioFuels, World Property Company, EB-5 Assist, United Tree Company, Alpine Leisure Properties, Magnifico Giornata and Voice of North America.

Martin Tindall’s core skills and studies incorporate; corporate structuring, operations, systems engineering, business development, process improvement, application design, accounting, contract preparation, and incubation of new businesses. These skills allow for a fresh approach to virtually any challenge or opportunity.
