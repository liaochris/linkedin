Elaine Andres' Summary

I am a dedicated advocate and project manager excited by non-profits and social enterprises. With a proven track record of taking on greater levels of responsibility and a demonstrated ability to respond flexibly and effectively in unpredictable environments, I find myself a relentless learner. I strive to always look critically (but not hopelessly) at good intentions and find ways to translate them into actionable plans.

I am compelled by a wide range of issues from HIV/AIDS, development infrastructure, gender, entrepreneurship, and post-colonial/decolonizing discourses--broadly united by a desire to help undo systems that fail their communities and take steps to create new ones that work.

I am passionate about development and creating strategic partnerships to help good organizations grow and scale their impact. My experience in the non-profit sector and my work within the start-up ecosystem of San Francisco and the Silicon Valley uniquely position me with an interdisciplinary skill set to move quickly, optimize my resources, leverage my network, and navigate whole-community solutions to challenges that arise.

I enjoy the thinking that gets done on a long destination-less walk, good conversation, books, writing, making and listening to music, cheese, golf, and innumerable other enjoyable things. I can be reached at ekandres@gmail.com.
