Mark Black's Summary

Mark is a Professional Inspirational Speaker and Author who uses his powerful personal story to inspire people to shed their self-imposed limitations to realize their potential.

Mark works with multi-national corporations, associaitons and educational institutions across North America to inspire their people to be their best. To learn more about what Mark can do for you, go to: www.MarkBlackSpeaks.com

Mark is also the author of an inspiring self-help book, Live Life from the Heart: 52 Weeks to a Life of Passion and Purpose. The book is available on his website: www.MarkBlackSpeaks.com

Specialties

Helping people in organizations live and work with passion and purpose.
