Anand "Lucky" Jesrani's Summary

Anand “Lucky” Jesrani was raised in the North State in Red Bluff, CA where he attended school in the Antelope School District and Red Bluff High School. He received his higher education from the University of California, Davis and his Juris Doctor from the University of Pacific, McGeorge School of Law.

After passing the California Bar Exam on his first attempt, Mr. Jesrani returned to the area and began working for law firms in Redding and Chico. After gaining valuable experience from larger firms, Mr. Jesrani acted on a life-long goal of starting his own law practice. Because of his strong connection to the community through his association in civic organizations, Mr. Jesrani chose to establish the Law Office of Anand “Lucky” Jesrani in Redding, California. Mr. Jesrani brings to his practice the level of experience, service, and proficiency one would expect to find in a large firm, but without paying large-firm rates.

Mr. Jesrani is very active in philanthropy and service to the Redding community. He spends much of his free time volunteering with the Active 20-30 Club, a service organization for young professionals, dedicated to serving underprivileged children in Redding. He also enjoys the outdoors and on any given weekend he can be found at Shasta Lake, local trails, or the golf course.

Specialties

1) Business, Real Estate, & Commercial Litigation — breach of contract, collections, corporate/partnership disputes, purchase/sale disputes, easement/lot line cases, Realtor/Broker Liability, and landlord-tenant disputes.

2) Transactional Real Estate and Business Matters

3) Bankruptcy — debtor representation and creditors' claims in Chapter 7 & 13 proceedings

4) Probate Litigation — Breach of fiduciary duty; claims against the estate; Elder Abuse

5) Estate Planning — Trusts; Wills; Probate
