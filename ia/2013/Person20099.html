Carlos L Irizarry's Summary

Bilingual (English/Spanish) Electrical engineer with deep hands on knowledge of manufacturing processes in diverse industries that include: Industrial 3 phase Molded case Circuit Breakers(MCCB) in GE;diesel locomotive manufacturing in GETS, diesel generators with FG Wilson and Bethor Aeon Power,and Alstom Wind ECO-100 S1 wind turbines.

My most recent experience included the total technology transfer of complex, state of the art, wind turbine manufacturing that culminated in the successful completion of the first Alstom Wind nacelle assembly plant in the Western Hemisphere. Project included the indirect management of an European (Spain) engineering staff, as well as US engineering assets.

The Alstom Wind project included:
* Creating a project plan in Primavera P6 to manage all associated tasks to complete the project.
* Selecting, validating and negotiating with US and European manufacturing fixture suppliers the purchase, installation and "scopes of work" required to install and start-up all fixtures, insuring compliance to US regulatory requirements (UL, NEC, OSHA, ASME, etc). This was pioneered under my tenure in Alstom Wind.
* Managing all manufacturing documentation to accurately comply with standard American English, and modifying them when required for local practices, as well as insuring their import in SAP as routing instructions.
* Assist Sourcing in finding substitutes for parts and consumables not readily available in the US.
* EHS champion during all phases of construction and production.
* Provide engineering leadership solutions to all engineering problems that surfaced prior to start of production.
* Assisted QA, Sourcing, Logistics, EHS until competent managers were hired.

Areas of experience include:Metal Stamping, Plating, Resistance Welding, Digital Controls,Tool Room, Final Assembly, Lean and 6 Sigma application in DMAIC.

Hire, mentoring, progressive discipline, termination experience in various industries.
