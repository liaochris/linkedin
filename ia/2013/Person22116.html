Eric Johnson's Summary

Eric Johnson is an experienced software developer and network engineer. He is a Microsoft Certified Developer, a title received in 2002 while attending TechSkills technical school, where he also later taught software development classes. He has been practicing software development for over eighteen years, the last twelve of which have included arcade game development. Eric has a thorough knowledge of distributed software philosophy and design, and practical real-world experience building scalable solutions.

Eric began working with Tournament Games Inc. early 2001. Prior to Tournament Games, Eric worked for Softek, Inc., in Lebanon TN, where, for 6 years, he developed and supported specialized software used in the medical and warehousing industries. Also, since Softek was the areas only ISP at the time, he developed websites for customers, as well building and maintaining networks for in house and customers usages.

Specialties: Game theory, design and software implementation, creative confusion, and nonsensical quotes. C++, C#, .NET, STL, ATL, ActiveX, Visual Basic, PHP, MVC, jQuery, Razor, Cloud Computing, Unity3D
