Dan Murphy's Experience

Co-founder

Subledger.com

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Services industry

October 2012 – Present (1 year) San Francisco Bay Area

We are moving to a 'pay per use' economy, the $100B 'Sharing' economy (Zipcar, Uber, 99designs, TaskRabbit etc) is only the start of this trend taking effect.

'Pay per use' is compelling for the customer, but its complex accounting logic (event based revenue recognition) and high transaction volume is difficult to administer with today's ERP and subscription billing platforms. This leaves a company with no option, but to build and maintain their own in-app accounting functionality.

In the same way Zuora made it easy to offer a 'subscription' pricing model, Boocx makes it easy to offer a 'pay per use' pricing model with GAAP accounting integrity.
