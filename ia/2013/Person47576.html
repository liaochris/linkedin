Joseph Geierman's Summary

Joseph Geierman manages people, operations and projects in corporate real estate and facilities. A graduate of the Georgia Institute of Technology's Building Construction and Facility Management Master's Degree program; Joseph is responsible for 366,000 square feet of Class A office space in a corporate headquarters setting and has direct accountability for firm-wide outsourcing, maintenance and service contracts.
