David Levitt's Summary

David Levitt has been a mortgage lender in Metro Detroit for over 19 years and has closed over $250 million in loans for his customers.

A veteran lender at many regional and national banks, David joined Main Street Bank in January 2012 in order to provide better service and smoother transactions for his clientele. Main Street Bank is licensed to originate mortgages in all 50 states and provides the service big banks won't..

David’s goal is to help homeowners – current and future – make educated financial decisions.
To quote Dave Ramsey:

““When getting help with money, whether it is insurance, real estate or investments you should always look for someone with the heart of a teacher, not the heart of a salesman.”

Mr. Levitt makes a point of clarifying his customer’s short and long term goals. He then provides the proper guidance and information for them to make sound decisions, consistent with their goals.

He specializes in mortgage and debt analysis and shares the best ideas in how to optimize mortgage financing.

As a Metro Detroit Mortgage Loan Officer, his target clients are First-time home buyers, move-up buyers, Financial Planners, CPA’s, and Real Estate professionals.
