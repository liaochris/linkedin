Nathan Smith's Summary

A CREATIVE PROFESSIONAL . . .

Marketer, writer, graphic designer, photographer, musician -- Nathan Smith's creativity takes many forms. But there's more to him than creativity.

Unlike many creative types, Nathan strikes equal balance with his logical / analytic side. Twelve years experience in strategic thinking, systems improvement and project management have proven his ability to both dream big and manage the details.

At his core, Nathan is a problem solver. He anticipates what lies ahead, carefully considers the obstacles and finds a better way. Whether in art or strategy, he continually finds himself in the role of "sensemaker", one who creates clarity from chaos.

. . . WHO GETS RESULTS

Nathan has served as a Graphic Designer / Writer, and Creative Manager for Portland Rescue Mission, a non-profit agency bringing hope and total life recovery to hurting men, women and children affected by homelessness, addiction, hunger and abuse. He currently leads as Interim Director of Development Ministry.

There he uses his marketing saavy, communication skills and eye for design to help the Mission communicate more effectively. His most recent initiatives include developing a comprehensive e-marketing strategy that integrates traditional direct mail marketing with website, e-mail, blogging and social media.

Nathan also provides key creative direction in all advertising, print media, video production and fundraising events. His passion for clarity and commitment to excellence continue to deliver successful projects and events time after time.
