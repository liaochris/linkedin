Cinnamon Pollard's Summary

Cinnamon Pollard is one of Australia’s leading digital consultants, specialising in digital start-up and early stage businesses.   With over 15 years online, mobile & social media experience, working with some of Australia's biggest brands and most successful start-ups, Cinnamon turns ideas into businesses that work.

If you are interested in starting your own business, or if you already have your own start up and need advice and support on how to optimise and grow it contact Cinnamon today!

Prior to setting up her own consultancy business Cinnamon launched Everguide, an innovative mobile events guide personalised based on location and personal preferences. Before that Cinnamon worked with the Starlight Children's Foundation and the Federal Government to build Livewire, an online community for sick kids.

At Fairfax Digital Cinnamon built TheVine.com.au from the ground up. TheVine is now the largest youth & entertainment website in Australia.

In the past she has overseen large scale Internet and Intranet developments for government departments, not for profits and private companies including Austereo, Australian Broadcasting Corporation, Telstra, and the Department of Housing.

Specialties: Digital strategy, start ups, online, mobile, social media, iPhone, Android, business planning & financial modelling, funding proposals, content strategy, partnership negotiation & sales, marketing, brand development, user centric product development, project management, thought leadership.
