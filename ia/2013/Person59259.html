Jason Huang's Summary

My program of research is motivated by an underlying curiosity about individuals’ adaptation to the workplace. Specifically, my research interests consist of three themes: (a) personality and the dynamic individual at work – how individuals adapt to work situations beyond their personality traits; (b) training and transfer – what factors facilitate individuals’ change of behavior; and (c) cultural influences on individual level phenomena – how culture shape individuals’ adaptation.

Research Interests:
