Julias Shaw's Summary

Julias Shaw has a long history of entrepreneurship, marketing, software development, engineering management, consulting, and hypnosis. He has been consistently focused on delivering customer and business value through marketing and development excellence.

Turning prospects into fanatical customers requires both deep insight into human desires as well as the ability to build innovative products. Towards those ends Julias has found advanced technologies such as Ruby and Clojure and ubiquitous platforms such as iOS and Android used in conjunction with modern internet marketing and the psychology of influence to be a powerful combination.

Specialties: Agile & Lean Software Processes including Extreme Programming (XP) & Scrum, Project Management, Lean Startup, Product Development, Metrics, Semantic Web, Objective-C, iOS, Clojure, Ruby, Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Web Analytics, Six Sigma, Object Oriented Analysis and Design, Java, Web Applications, and Distributed Systems.
