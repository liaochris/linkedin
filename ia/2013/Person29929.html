Foxy Fitness

September 2010 – October 2010 (2 months)

Foxy Fitness is a personal mobile fitness company based in Toronto ON. They provide one on one, in-house fitness training and healthy living consultation. I was approached to handle all aspects of the website design as well as executing the domain registration and hosting packages. Original design work was done in Photoshop and Illustrator with the site being constructed on top of the WordPress CMS. The actual construction of the site was done with HTML and CSS and then injected with the relevant PHP code allowing it to run on top of the WordPress framework. Due to the mobile nature of the business it was decided that WordPress would be the simplest and fastest method for site updates as the client could perform content updates from anywhere she could log into the WordPress online system.
