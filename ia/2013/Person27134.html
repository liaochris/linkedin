Corrado Mora's Summary

Civil and Commercial Mediator. Mediation Advocate. Attorney-at-Law (Avvocato).
CEDR Accredited Mediator. CIArb Accredited Mediator.
MCIArb and Member of the Education and Membership Committee of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, London.

Member of the Panel of Neutrals of CAM - The Chamber of National and International Arbitration and Mediation of Milan and of the Chambers of Commerce of Florence and Verona. Mediator of the OVMF - Organismo Veronese di Mediazione Forense (the Mediation Centre of the Bar Association of Verona). Community Mediator of the Mediation Centres of the Municipalities of Reggio Emilia and Bologna. Former Mediator of the International Chamber of Arbitration and Mediation of Grosseto.

Teacher - Advanced Negotiation and Mediation Class (University of Trento, School of Law).
Trainer (accredited) - Mediator Skills, Mediation and Negotiation Techniques, Mediation Advocacy.
Lecturer and speaker on Mediation.
Included on the CIArb Approved Faculty List (AFL) - Mediation.

LL.M. degree in "ADR - Alternative Dispute Resolution Procedures" (University of Siena, 2006-2007).
Deep theoretical and practical knowledge of ADR methods matured by national and international training (CEDR, London, UK; WIPO - World Intellectual Property Organization, Geneva, CH; ERA, Trier, DE; CENTRAL-DIS, Cologne, DE; King’s College, London, UK).

Member of CIArb (Chartered Institute of Arbitrators) - MCIArb (Mediation), AAA (American Arbitration Association) – ICDR (International Centre for Dispute Resolution) Young & International, ArbAut - YAAP (Young Austrian Arbitration Practitioners), LCIA's Young International Arbitration Group – YIAG, ASA -40 (Association Suisse de l'Arbitrage), CEPANI (Belgian Centre for Mediation and Arbitration), ICC Young Arbitrators Forum (International Chamber of Commerce), EADM (European Association for Decision Making), SJDM (Society for Judgement and Decision Making).

Specialties

Alternative Dispute Resolution methods (Negotiation, Mediation, Mediation Advocacy, Arbitration).
