Jeff Routzahn's Summary

Results oriented manufacturing professional with extensive experience in all aspects of finishing including chemical pretreatment, painting and clear coating processes. Proven ability to drive improvement in plant operations through strategic planning, coaching, teamwork, and an effective use of Lean Six Sigma tools. Uses knowledge and experience to lead positive change and to generate significant improvements. Demonstrates expertise in process engineering, manufacturing engineering, quality engineering, and operations management. Skilled in developing and implementing programs and process enhancements designed to reduce operating costs and increase efficiency. Thrives in a constantly changing work environment. Certified Six Sigma Greenbelt.
