KEITH BALDWIN's Summary

I am proud to say the Spikes Trophies has been voted 2013 Manufacturer of the year by the Greater Philadelphia Chamber of Commerce. Our brand promise is Speed & Dependability, with a commitment to same day personalization services.

I am managing partner of Spike's Trophies, Ltd., with over thirty five years of experience with Spike's. Spike's Trophies, established in 1929, is a Recognition Resource company that assists in the recognition of People, Brands and Organizational Identity through the use of Awards, Promotional Products and Signage products.

I am currently on the Board of Directors & Membership Chair of Award Associates of America.

My wife Beverly and I reside in Medford, NJ. We have two children: Kasey and Jake.
