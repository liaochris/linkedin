Leslie Truex's Summary

Lifestyle career design enthusiast!

Leslie Truex truly knows how to combine comfort with making a living. For over a decade she has been working at home, often in her pajamas, and helping others do the same. She has telecommuted as a school program coordinator, social worker, sales woman, and real estate agent. In recent years, she has worked as a freelance writer, career design coach, speaker/trainer, and author.

In 1998, Leslie started sharing her real life experiences and tips to other work-at-home wannabes through her website Work-At-Home Success. Today the website is recognized by telework and small business associations as a quality resource for work-at-home information.

She has appeared on The Daily Buzz, CNN.com and in a host of other media outlets. She is is a go-to expert on home-based careers and has been featured in Redbook, Women's World and other print media. She coaches, teaches and speaks on telecommuting, small and home business, marketing, and writing related topics.

She is the author of "The Work-At-Home Success Bible," "Jobs Online: Find a Get Hired to a Work-At-Home Job," and "Lifestyle Career Design." Her digital publications work-at-home courses through Work-At-Home Success University hosted at WorkAtHomeSuccess.com

Leslie's background is in social work and includes experience in:

Adoption Social Work 1990-1993, 2002- 2010 (telecommute), 2011-current
School Social Worker 1991-1994
Parent Educator in Public Health Clinic 1994-1998

She has also worked as a Realtor and in business-to-business sales.

Specialties: Career coach, telecommuting jobs, work at home, small business, home business, online business,marketing consultant, writer, author, speaker, trainer
