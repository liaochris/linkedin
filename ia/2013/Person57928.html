Jamie Allen's Summary

As Creative Director of J Allen Design, LLC, I have turned my God-given talent of graphic and web design into a way to make a living. Serving my profession also as a Senior Designer at Mass Transmit supplements my craft.

Being a visual communicator, I constantly look for ways to create unique solutions that my clients have come to appreciate. Keeping up with the latest design trends yet maintaining the firm foundation that my years of training at West Virginia University is what has brought me thus far.

Throughout my career as a designer, I have worked for large corporations, government agencies, small and large advertising agencies as well as freelancing. The long-standing relationships that I have built with my clients as well as colleagues have been critical to my success and ability to continue to fuel my creativity.

In addition to my career as a designer, I am a dedicated family man, Christian and public servant. Whether it’s changing diapers, coaching a team or helping sell Girl Scout cookies, I manage to keep work and life balanced. My compassion for people gives me the extra energy needed to reach out to those less fortunate, misguided or simply in need of a helping hand.

My sense of humor, love of life and dedication to my craft is what makes me tick. Oh yeah, a little bit of hip hop, r&b, jazz and Sports Center mixed in there too!
