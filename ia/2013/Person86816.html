Mark Luckinbill's Summary

MARKETPLACE LEADER: FINANCIAL SERVICES SALES AND MARKETING

20 years learning how to help companies and financial professionals deliver the right financial products and services to employees.

My financial services roles have specialized in the sales and distribution of 403(b), 457 and 401 retirement plans for employers of all sizes. This includes large, complex institutional business development and training, managing and coaching organization leaders and financial advisors. Notable wins include closing a single sale in excess of $100mil and three years meeting a territorial sales objective in excess of $450mil.

OWNER: TECHNOLOGY, MEDIA AND SPORTS COMPANY

RaceReach.com, a technology and media company created in 2011, is a community of thousands of connected athletes and a suite of apps for event directors and clubs. RaceReach technology is used by over 20,000 athletes (and growing) each week via computers and mobile devices.

The platform is a series of apps that install on existing websites to help club and team leaders communicate more effectively and make group admin easier for leaders (and make clubs more fun for members). The apps help athletes find great race deals, great events and new friends and training partners. Our most popular product provides event registration services and a unique online ordering tool that creates and distributes mobile ready order forms to sell apparel or other goods and services.

SUMMARY

I continue to seek opportunities to work with smart, high energy people who like to invent, market and sell new products using novel approaches in fast changing industries.
