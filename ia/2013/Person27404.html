Khuzaima Dohadwala's Summary

We provide Electronics and Computer Recycling Services to Individuals, Businesses, Towns, Cities, Counties, State Agencies, Federal Agencies, Transfer Stations and Landfills.

We are certified R2 (Responsible Recycling) and ISO 14001 compliant de-manufacturers of electronics. We ensure that your electronics are recycled appropriately within the country and will never end up in a landfill or improperly discarded. By recycling your obsolete electronics, you are doing your part to protect the environment and ensuring the reuse of valuable resources.
