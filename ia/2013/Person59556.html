Jason White's Projects

  • Kelley Blue Book

    • May 2012 to July 2012
    Team Members: Jason White, Chad Hutson, Matt Daly, Lauren Shawe

    For Kelley Blue Book’s first-ever national broadcast campaign, which was conceived to roll out the new KBB.com, agency Doner wanted visual fanfare fit for a huge launch event, so Leviathan helped combine the spirit of all three: a :60 spot featuring 3D website graphics projection mapped onto a gorgeous museum. Wait...what?

    Responsible for live action direction and production; design and animation; projection mapping; editorial and visual effects, Leviathan provided a single integrated resource from start to finish. Music and sound design was provided by our good friend and collaborator, Waveplant.

  • Amon Tobin: Wooden Toy

    • July 2012 to August 2012
    Team Members: Jason White, Chad Hutson, Matt Daly, Bradon Webb, Brandy Olsen, Vello Virkhaus

    A continuation of our collaboration with electronic artist Amon Tobin, V-Squared Labs, and new friends Redmoon, this project reflects many capabilities of design-focused production studio Leviathan: concept development, live action production, animation, compositing and experience design.

  • Leviathan: HP "Play Big"

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jason White, Lauren Shawe, Derek Weglarz, Chad Hutson, Brandy Olsen, Bradon Webb, Tim Sepulveda, David Brodeur, Gareth Fewel

    HP was approached with a simple idea. Start with several touchscreen computers. Next, add a team of designers, composers, and technologists. Then, build touchscreen synthesizers that control 3D projection mapped content. The result was an elaborate, complex, massive audio-visual collaboration.

  • Leviathan: Amon Tobin

    • April 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Jason White, David Brodeur, Derek Weglarz, Sam Gierasimczuk, Bradon Webb, Brandy Olsen, Chad Hutson, Jimi Filipovski, Tim Sepulveda, Dan Tiffany

    Leviathan worked with frequent collaborator and renowned VJ Vello Virkhaus on groundbreaking performance visuals for electronic musician Amon Tobin, creating ethereal CG narratives and engineering the geometry maps for an entire stage of stacked cube-like structures.
