Ben Kok's Summary

Acoustic Consultant specializing in all aspects related to high quality sound reproduction in large and small rooms, with and without the use of sound systems.
Reference projects are:
National Concert Hall - Dublin, Ireland ; Main auditorium of the 'Muziekkwartier' - Enschede, Netherlands; Theater am Goetheplatz - Bremen, Germany ; Studios for the Amsterdam Conservatory, Netherlands ; NFTA (National Film academy) - Amsterdam, Netherlands; Music Academy – Haarlem, Netherlands ; CCTV - Beijing, China ; Timberline studio - British Columbia, Canada; Polyhymnia listening suites - Baarn, Netherlands ; CIRMMT listening labs - Montreal, Canada ; Studio for Muziekgebouw Frits Philips – Eindhoven, Netherlands ; 3FM studio – Hilversum, Netherlands ; TV-studio for Northwestern University – Doha, Qatar ; Soundhouse – Schiedam, Netherlands ; RTL studios - Hilversum, Netherlands ; Eurosound mobiles - Herveld, Netherlands; etc.

Multiple papers have been presented on my work for the AES and other professional societies on Variable Acoustics, Speech Intelligibility, Sound System Design, Loudspeaker Design and Studio Design.
