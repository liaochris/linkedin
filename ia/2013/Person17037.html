Brett Clay's Summary

Brett Clay is the author of the award-winning book, "Selling Change", named the BEST BUSINESS BOOK of 2010 by the Independent Publisher Book Awards.

A veteran of two decades in international sales and marketing management, he is the founder and CEO of the innovative Change Leadership Group, a management consulting and training firm that helps companies grow their businesses by developing their sales, marketing, and leadership capabilities.

Brett is also the host of The Actuation Zone Radio Show where he discusses business trends and today’s hot issues with thought-leaders—authors, analysts, scholars, journalists, and practitioners—who are not just adapting, but are creating and exploiting new opportunities, by leading change.

Brett is a leading expert in change leadership. His award-winning book, Selling Change, has been called "the go-to handbook for leadership in this decade," by Independent Publisher Book Awards.

Jeffrey Gitomer says, "This is the first POSITIVE book about change in a decade...at a time when we need it most! Buy it today and implement it as fast as you can."

TJ Walker says, "I recommend that you buy this [Brett's] book, follow him on Twitter and go to his web page frequently--I do!"
