Kimberly Patterson's Summary

Yes I'm an HR professional but don't hold it against me. The industry and the people in HR can sometimes get a bad rap -- I get it. My style of HR is far from being the "policy police" nor do I endorse using cookie cutter solutions for people issues. I like companies who are unconventional and willing and comfortable tackling the gray areas in the workplace.

As an HR pro turned consultant, my experience has allowed me to build HR foundations in businesses that are customized based on their needs and individual culture. With 20 years of progressive experience, the value that I add to businesses includes staffing, training, HR start up, being an ‘on call’ HR Director to solve employee relations issues all the way to creating a performance management system, business leader coaching and benefits makeovers. Even better when we can incorporate technology along the way.

Of course I also do everything that organizations need -- compliance audits, handbooks and anything else that requires the I's to be dotted and the T's to be crossed.

If you're a company who promotes a healthy and innovative culture with employees who are happy to come to work, I’d love to meet you!
