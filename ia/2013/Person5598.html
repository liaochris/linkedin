Ameen Ahsan Muliyathil's Summary

Business Strategist, Consultant, Entrepreneur & Coach.

Business Consultant based in Calicut (Kerala, India), specializing in preparing feasibility studies, project reports/business plans for new ventures and projects.

Corporate Strategy Adviser who has done his masters in Finance & Investment from Exeter University, UK (Exeter University is ranked among Top 1% Universities in the World).

Winner of the International Portfolio Management Award in 2001, an award by INVESCO (UK) and BFS Securities (UK) in association with the Exeter University. He was the Team Leader of this group.

More than 11 years of mixed business consulting and management experience in GCC and India.

Worked as the Strategy Advisor for a leading Dubai based company; Associate Consultant at a European strategy advisory firm (MESA) based in Abu Dhabi; and as General Manager for a leading confectionery factory in UAE. Presently providing strategy advisory services in India since 2007.

Consulting and management exposure in diverse sectors in different countries (Request a detailed profile, for more details).

Business and management articles published in Gulf Madhyamam Newspaper, Dinar Standard (a New York based online publication), and Dhanam Business Magazine.
