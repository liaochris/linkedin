Diane A. Curran's Summary

Celebrating 4+ decades of Marketing Experience, and still fascinated by business! My company provides marketing, branding, writing and design services to independent business owners and professionals in practice. We present what is unique, compelling and valuable about them and their business to increase their presence in person, in print and online. We are a great fit for those who want to grow beyond their current size, or expand to serve new audience segments.

Offering a rare combination of big picture vision, with roll-up your sleeves practicality. Benefiting owners, ceo's and entrepreneurs to focus well beyond the daily rush that too often overwhelms and interferes with effective business development, real-world planning and marketing.

I offer a creative window to see into a client's original excitement about their business, with support for a fresh take and expanded vision of what is possible on their pathway for success.

Specialties: Providing the Art & Science of your WOW! Factor. Creating a memorable presence for you... In Person, In Print and Online... to reinforce your value and vision to your intended audience.

Services include strategic planning, market positioning & pricing, audience targeting, brand development, logo design, advertising, high-end CMS web sites (from start-up to upgrades), writing and editing in many media, and client/prospect communications programs.

My newest services include streamlined programs in private Tele-Consulting in marketing for the Independent Business Owner, and consulting for professionals considering membership portal development.

Artist & Owner

Fine Arts by Diane A. Curran

September 1966 – 2011 (45 years)

I realized early on that art in many forms would always be essential to my life. After being blessed with a very early and extensive public school education in painting, drawing and the full array of art history, I set about creating my own visual language in symbols. I continue to be blessed when people collect my art for their own enjoyment, with one woman and group showings, and with continuing experimentation in my two main media of pigment pencil and digital drawing. In addition, my poetry and essays remain a quiet passion, with the occasional public reading or performance to fulfill the thespian in me.

Sr. VP

Hub Mail Advertising

February 1972 – August 1987 (15 years 7 months)

I began my career with an extraordinary opportunity to enter the field of direct marketing just as it was moving from pioneer-status to mainstream media choice. Working there for over 15 years, and promoted 7 times, I acquired in-depth experience in virtually every aspect of direct marketing from concept to program completion, from sales to department management, and beyond, to results analysis and program design. I worked with literally hundreds of major national companies and regional organizations in consumer, B2B and Not-for-Profit organizations.

It was a fantastic start to a still-satisfying career. After this, only California and the life of an entrepreneur/consultant would do for my next major adventure!

Diane A. Curran's Education

University of Massachusetts, Amherst

N/A, Liberal Arts/History/Community Planning

1968 – 1971

Took the classic non-linear entrepreneur's path...

I quickly realized in college that the pace and prospects of an academic career plan were not making sense for me. So I switched directions and jumped right into advertising to learn what worked first-hand at a much more accelerated pace.

Don't get me wrong... learning remains a distinct passion of mine in a wide range of topics. Whether through trade groups, endless self-study, or courses taught by those who created their own success, I keep a constant eye out for new skills I can acquire.

My natural appetite for information is matched by an inclination for real-world testing of new ideas and tools to discover and determine what works best.

Accustomed to juggling 35-50 projects at once, from the moment I entered advertising, I quickly learned the value of staying observant and flexible as trends, patterns, business climates and technology tools always change constantly. Love it!

Activities and Societies: N/A. Having participated as an officer in many organizations during in high school, I decided to wait and take up these duties later in trade groups once I got into business, which has worked out quite nicely, indeed.
