Gerard Bianco's Summary

Gerard Bianco is a playwright, author, jewelry designer, artist and filmmaker.

As an author:

He wrote the mystery/thriller novel, The Deal Master, 2006 and a play titled, Discipline, 2012. Both have won awards.

His lessons, exercises and advice on the art and craft of creative fiction have appeared on the web and in the new book, Now Write! Mysteries: Mystery Fiction Exercises From Today's Best Writers and Teachers. (Tarcher, 2011; Edited by Sherry Ellis and Laurie Lamson.)

As a jewelry designer:

Gerard has designed and created over a thousand of pieces of fine jewelry throughout his 40 year career. His work has been featured in print and his designs have sold around the globe.

As an artist:

Gerard is an award-winning artist and has worked in the painting and illustration field for over 30 years. He has had clients in the entertainment industry and in big business, as well as commissions from the United States Navy. His portraits hang in museums and in private collections throughout the U.S.

Film: As a filmmaker-

Writer and Executive Producer of The Deal Master Trailer, filmed in New York City with a cast and crew from around the globe. Link: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ilXHaugenQ

Gerard lives and works in South Portland, Maine. His website is: gerardbianco.com
