Douglas Audirsch's Summary

Douglas Audirsch has been in Learning and Development for more than 15 years. Most recently, he created a training and development department for Encore Health Resources which has a 100% virtual workforce. His efforts to provide training to a geographically dispersed workforce of traveling consultants resulted in leading a 3 day educational corporate retreat, launching a cloud-based LMS, acquiring 3rd party training content, and developing in-house blended training solutions incorporating gamification strategies.

Prior to joining Encore, Doug provided training consulting to Continental Airlines during their merger with United Airlines, BP, as well as performing various training functions at Administaff, Devon Energy, and Microsoft. Doug graduated with high honors from Life Pacific College in San Dimas, CA.
