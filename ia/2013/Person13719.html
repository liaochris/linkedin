Beth Katz's Experience

Principal

KatzDesignGroup

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

2003 – Present (10 years) Phoenix, Arizona Area

KatzDesignGroup, LLC is a full service interior architecture firm specializing in commercial tenant improvements, healthcare design, and restaurant interiors. KatzDesignGroup enables business success by aligning with corporate culture and objectives; efficient design, utilizing green resources, and enabling rapid adaption to our changing economy. Using this blend of form and function, we create modern sustainable interiors.

Beth Katz, Principal, brings over 20 years of commercial interior design experience to KatzDesignGroup and is NCIDQ certified. She is a graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison, BS-Interior Design. Ms. Katz served on the executive board of the New England Chapter of IIDA for several years prior to relocating to Phoenix in 2003. She was a member of the Boston Architectural Center adjunct faculty and served on the Interior Design Advisory Board for Collins College in Phoenix, AZ. Beth is an active member of the Greater Phoenix Chamber of Commerce, Local First Arizona, AZCrew, Chai5, and an Ambassador for UMOM. KatzDesignGroup is City of Phoenix DBE and SBE certified.

Beth Katz's Summary

KatzDesignGroup is an interior architecture firm specializing in commercial office interiors, healthcare, and restaurant design. KDG assembles consultants such as licensed architects, licensed engineers, graphic designers, furniture dealers and art consultants to work as a team with our clients. We aim to educate and empower our clients so that informed decisions can easily be made throughout the design and construction process. With KatzDesignGroup, you will always have a principal on your project, your project will always be completed on time, it will be performed at extreme attention to detail and you will always have confidence that information won’t get lost in a pipeline of junior designers.

Beth Katz's Projects

  • Yogurtini

    • July 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Beth Katz, Vikki Green

    Yogurtini hired KatzDesignGroup to design the interiors for its National franchise. KDG customized their interior by using a unique blend of glass mosaic tile, glass bead wallcovering, custom martini glass signage, back etched mirrorplex for the bathrooms and metallic polyurethane banquet seating. We have worked on multiple locations for the franchise.

  • ABLE Financial

    • July 2011 to March 2012
    Team Members: Beth Katz, Navin Pathangay, Julie Ginsberg

    Elegant contemporary financial institution. Great installation of glass, decorative materials, and green products.

  • Edufit

    • June 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Beth Katz, Navin Pathangay

    Since 1998, EduFit Personal Training Studios have provided individualized fitness programs to their Arizona client base. Different from circuit or group training concepts, EduFit provides convenient appointment-based personal training services in their uniquely designed studios. KatzDesignGroup designed the fourth location for EduFit in Ahwatukee, AZ. Design features rubber flooring, energy efficient LED track lighting, custom rubber tile design.

  • YMCA-Scottsdale

    Team Members: Beth Katz

    After 25 years of the black and red logo, YMCA rebranded. KatzDesignGroup recolored the entire facility bringing it from bland to energetic.

  • Arizona Orthopaedic and Sports Medicine Specialists

    Team Members: Beth Katz

    Arizona Orthopaedic and Sports Medicine Specialists hired KatzDesignGroup to update their existing clinic. These five orthopedic surgeons are the “all-stars” in the Phoenix area taking care of Phoenix’s sports elite. KatzDesignGroup redesigned the reception, waiting areas, restrooms, and kitchen and re-schemed the exam rooms and common corridors.

  • Dearing Acting Studio

    • 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Beth Katz, Mike Hall

    The Dearing Studio is dedicated to enhancing life through acting. Their program is open to actors of all ages and levels of experience. They teach skills to each student that will help them in all facets of life. Their students have worked on projects like Grey’s Anatomy, Two and a Half Men, The Young and the Restless, Dexter, Cold Case, America’s Most Wanted, Nickelodeon’s Brain Surge, and many more! KatzDesignGroup has created an exciting new studio conducive to all aspects of the Dearing’s practice. We have a performance stage, a cycloramic wall for filming, an editing room, classroom, and a kick back actor’s lounge. The interior design features hand painted floors and a custom lighting design system.

  • Walden Media

    Team Members: Beth Katz

    Chronicles of Narnia, The Tooth Fairy, Hoot, Holes, and Winn Dixie are notable “nutritious” family friendly movies that Walden Media has produced over the last few years. Their Boston space features a specialized media room, viewing lounge, and current movie poster installation.

  • Health Dialog

Special design features included inlayed glass tile in the reception doors, boardroom table and credenza. All dining/meeting spaces are unique with decorative lighting, specialty flooring, and glass backsplashes. Each of the call centers has its own regional design but incorporates corporate standards.
