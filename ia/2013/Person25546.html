Clayton Bruster's Experience

Artist and Photographer

The Art of Clayton Bruster

1999 – Present (14 years) Hacienda Heights, CA

I have photographed all types of work from products to fashion-Glamour-Print modeling, fine-art, figure study and even some erotic photography. I have worked with about 300 models to date and can't remember ever having a bad shoot. I have worked in a black and white art lab, created cyan-o-types, van dyke browns & gum dichromates. I've also hand oiled black and white work, used water colors and pen and in with the alternative processes and used a plethora of toning techniques. Most of my work now is digital art, digital pixel punishing, compositing of multiple images and including digitally created components. Working mostly with Photoshop but other programs occasionally.

I have two online galleries if you'd like to see my work:
