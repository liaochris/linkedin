David Ostrow's Summary

I have been a physical therapist for 27 years. I owned a private practice, and during that time I became the 3rd franchisee in the FitGolf (then Body Balance for Performance) System. In 2003 I purchased the company with a partner and have been in charge of this company since.

Specialties

Helping people improve their ability to excel at all levels in golf and in their franchises. I believe my greatest strength is my ability to look at bodies of data and understand what they mean and how to improve them.

Outcomes with our clients and patients are what matters most. If you want to get better at golf or feel better, our job is to get that outcome for you.
