Joanne West's Summary

Artist and owner of freelance photography business - Joanne West Photography. Offering a combination of corporate and small business photography services including event coverage, photos for business brochures, websites and advertising as well as personalized portraiture for individuals, families and pets. Fine art and travel photography is exhibited at juried shows and galleries, also displayed and offered for sale in local shops,busineses and thru the internet. I am marketing large photo reproductions of Arizona and popular international destinations giclee printed on canvas suitable for office, business institutions and home decor. Related experience includes a 21 yr career at GM Desert Proving Grounds starting as Test Driver/Technician and ending as Supervisor of Photographic Services. I currently hold an Arizona Community College Teaching Certificate and lecture and lead photography classes and heart-based playshops.

Specialties

Freelance photographer excel at making the ordinary extraodinary - accept a wide variety of assignments from city life to technical products. Large photo library of Arizona and travel photos that can be licensed for commercial use. Personalized portraiture of people and their companion animals. Specializing in capturing the beauty of places, the sacredness of the moment and special events including unique weddings and committment ceremonies.
