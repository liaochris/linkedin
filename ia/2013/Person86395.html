Mark Castellano's Summary

Mark Castellano is Vice President of Business Development at RenewTricity, a utility-scale renewable energy producer. Mark's clients include large energy consumers, third-party resellers, financial service entities, and building retro-fitters. He manages relationships and business development in North America.

Mark has over twenty years of experience in sales, marketing, support, and development of products and services in the telecommunications, financial, semiconductors, and energy sectors. His past employers include distinguished companies such as AT&T Bell Laboratories, Merrill Lynch, and Texas Instruments.

Mark's technical background and broad experience enables him to connect with customers at all levels, understand their needs, and come up with creative solutions to close business.

RenewTricity services the following markets:

- Large consumers of electricity. Offtakers purchase the electricity at a fixed price through a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA), with no equipment connected at their premises, and no capital expenditure.

- Third-party resellers. These companies generate and re-sell electricity and can purchase/sell renewable electricity directly from RenewTricity with a PPA.

- Financial services. Trading desks that engage in electricity trading (spot, short/long-term) can purchase electricity from RenewTricity.

- Renewables. RenewTricity can engage in long-term contracts for Solar Renewable Energy Certificates (SRECs).

- Building retro-fitters. Companies that engage in making buildings more energy-efficient can stabilize their energy savings cashflows by signing PPAs with RenewTricity.

- Joint ventures. RenewTricity can develop an entire project turnkey for purchase, or purchase a shovel-ready project and prepare it for construction.

- Real estate. RenewTricity is actively looking for opportunities to develop net-metering and brownfield/landfill projects.

If you have interest in any of the above, please contact Mark.
