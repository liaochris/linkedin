Tiffany Marlow's Summary

Throughout my career, I have gained extensive experience in sales and marketing. I have been working since I was 15 for a wide variety of companies mainly within the service & retail industry. This helped me to develop well rounded customer experience working with all types of personalities & selling to a wide variety of clientele.

Throughout college I started to work for more independent advertising agencies & gained extensive experience working efficiently & independently. After graduating with my B.S.- Business degree w/emphasis in Marketing in 2007 I started working for a small web development firm in San Diego. As the Director of Marketing I had my hands in all aspects of the web development & marketing business.

I worked with a wide variety of clients with various products & services. I learned to design, code html & css giving me the ability to build websites & newsletters (200+). I also covered all the clients online marketing needs to get websites ranked on the first page of google so their target market could find them easily.

This was a great stepping stone that lead me to start my own web development & marketing business in July of 2010 proudly named Klutch Web Marketing & Development. The major goal of my business is to offer clients a well rounded marketing campaign so that their target market can find their company's website in all various mediums on the internet.

From managing & building web marketing campaigns & websites, to analytics research, to developing sales plans and communications strategies, I have covered a broad range of marketing disciplines. This has left me with broad marketing experience and strong online marketing campaign instincts.

My main expertise is Website and Internet Marketing, particularly SEO (Search Engine Optimization), SEM (Search Engine Marketing), SMM (Social Media Marketing) and PPC (Pay Per Click) with a specialized focus toward Social Media Optimization, Google Organic Search, and Analytics.
