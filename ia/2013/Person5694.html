Amie Hyman, MA, LSW's Summary

Amie Hyman received her Master’s degree in social work from the University of Chicago and worked with human service agencies in direct service and administrative capacities for more than 20 years. Her experience includes all aspects of business and fiscal management and administration as well as counseling and case management for children and adults.

In 2004, Amie created a successful home business with emphasis on sales and marketing as well as management/financial consultation and assistance to nonprofit agencies. She is a licensed social worker, an AARP trained money manager volunteer, a member of the National Association of Daily Money Managers, the National Association of Healthcare Advocacy Consultants as well as the National Association of Social Workers.

She has been attending to the medical, personal, tax, financial, real estate, insurance and mobility needs of her 85-year-old mother since 2005 and developed Heartfelt Solutions for Seniors as a result.

Specialties

Compassionate, resourceful, diligent, efficient and thorough. Detail-oriented and organized, Amie has an uncanny ability to get answers, resolve disputes and get things done on behalf of her client.

She is a passionate advocate for her older adult clients with comprehensive knowledge of geriatric services and networks within the Chicago area.
