John DeRoo's Summary

Director of Electronics at Pixtronix. Manager of FPGA design and verification at Sun Microsystems. Digital logic designer with significant experience in all phases of large-scale ASIC and FPGA designs in both small work groups and large multi-site teams. Design team management. ASIC & large-scale FPGA architecture & implementation using Verilog, VHDL, & SystemC.
