Julie B Cosgrove's Publications

  • P.R.A.Y.I. N.G.:Power and Purpose to Your Prayers

    • lulu.com
    • November 10, 2009
    Authors: Julie B Cosgrove

    PRAYING is a Biblcally based step by step guide to bring purpose and power to your prayers and to enrich your relationship with others and with God. Questions at the end of each chapter make this book an excellent Bible study guide.

  • Song Notes: Devotionals from the Book of Psalms

    • lulu.com
    • April 16, 2010
    Authors: Julie B Cosgrove

    A collection of original devotionals featured on two Christian women's websites, Song Notes is a fresh look at the Psalms, proving their message is as pertinent today as when scribed centuries ago.
    A great bedside table book to read whenever and however much you want, or for use as a Bible study guide.

  • What Can She Tell Us?

    • lulu.com
    • March 1, 2011
    Authors: Julie B Cosgrove

    Journey with eleven unamed women as they encounter the Holy bursting through into their mundane. Each woman's tale is discussed with ample space for reflective notes. Great for individual and group Bible studies,for men and youth as well as women.

  • Between the Window and the Door

    • Abbott Press
    • January 31, 2012
    Authors: Julie B Cosgrove

    “When God closes a door, He always opens a window.”
    Maybe you’ve heard that, and maybe you believe in your heart it’s true—or at least you always did. But when life changes unexpectedly, it can be difficult to understand what God has in mind for us. What if we wait and the window doesn’t open? What then?

  • Focused- a Novel

    • Cross Words Press
    • January 13, 2012
    Authors: Julie B Cosgrove

    Christina is at a loss- her parents have passed on, her son has flown the nest, and her husband is often absent- even when he is snoozing in front of the TV in his favorite recliner. Where does her life go from here - especailly when her old highschool beau resurfaces as charming as ever?
    A heart warming, and at times humorous, look at marriage after kids and finding your focus again. Perfect for any woman who watches her prince charming develop a paunch and an attitude ,and then wonders what God's purpose is for them both.

  • Squeeze More God-Time Into Your Day

    • Cross Word Press
    • July 1, 2013
    Authors: Julie B Cosgrove

    Is God tapping on your spiritual shoulder, "Hey, got time for me? Can you pencil me in?"
    Time with God is quickly becoming a dinosaur concept in this techno-global world that demands our instant response 24/7/365. Yet our minds, bodies and souls depend on it, daily. So do our relationships. It is, after all, our soul- purpose. Discover tips and triumphs that might just work for you.
