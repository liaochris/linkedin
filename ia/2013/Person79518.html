Leah Hopkins Henry's Summary

I am an award winning visual artist who specializes in portraiture. Portraits include children, adults, groups, pets and architecture. My background has included antiques and design. Interested in creating more corporate portraits.

Specialties: Realistic Oil Portraits for Family, Corporate, Groups & Archtecture. Capture the spirit of a loved one in a oil portrait to cherish for generations
