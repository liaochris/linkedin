Carol Hartzog's Summary

"Spotlighting you in the News" is what we do best over any other media relations company. Primarily made up of former journalists, the company knows the editors/news directors and knows how to improve your image in the print and electronic media. As writers, the company's scope includes news placement, copywriting and website/newsletter content. The team writes in a manner that is succinct, typo-free, precise, accurate and ethically and legally-sound -- critical elements for any content. Hartzog's managing editor skill-base brings projects to a quick completion. And deadlines? A piece of cake.

Specialties

Media relations -- Carol Hartzog Communications not only targets the right media with the right angle, the company has established relationships that smooth the pitching process.

Public relations -- We have it covered: From website content to newsletters, from well-written story pitches to an experienced company team which includes longtime journalists and public relations experts.

Community relations -- Carol Hartzog Communications is in the know as to who YOU need to know.
