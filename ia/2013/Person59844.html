Jay Hartlove, CRCM, MSF, CPL's Summary

Experienced Compliance Officer and Business Litigation Paralegal continuing his career of assisting business obtain its objectives in a compliant manner. A creative, organized and collaborative thinker who developed compliance programs that were successfully reviewed by regulatory agencies. Has particular expertise in writing policies and procedures, advertising regulations, risk assessment, and contract analysis.

Having spent many years working in foreclosures and business litigation picking up the broken pieces of business that had not worked, it is important to me to help business get it right the first time. Coming from a litigation background, I am adept at finding liabilities and weaknesses and proactively correcting them.

My best jobs have employed all my talents. Writing, developing, and improving interactive compliance library tools allowed me to apply my years of risk management experience to get to the heart of how regulatory changes affect my customers. Being the regulatory compliance officer for a bank, especially working with marketing issues, allowed me to bring together my business and legal training, my creativity, my training talents (experienced teacher and public speaker) and my love of words (published novelist).

Specialties: Process development, testing and improvement, project management, mediation and collaborative decision facilitation, contract analysis and negotiation, technical writing, legal research, teaching, public speaking.
