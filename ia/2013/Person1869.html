Will Roffé's Summary

Hi,

I've worked client-side in roles such as Sales Manager and Marketing Manager, before jumping the fence and enjoying ad agency life; first in media and client service, before training as a copywriter in the industry's Axis Ad School in 1992. (12 students were accepted that year.)

I have worked across many industry sectors throughout Australasia, for leading Ad and DM agencies, including:

Saatchi & Saatchi :: Saatchi & Saatchi Direct :: Colenso :: Ogilvy :: McCann-Erickson :: DMB&B :: Lavender :: Frank :: Robbins Brandt Richter :: .99.

I collaborate with agencies and brands to help translate their authentic stories into compelling experiences (for both online and offline environments) that capture the hearts and minds of their customers. My focus is on making brands more human so that they can properly engage and thrive in this ever-changing environment.

I am passionate about people, social media and technology, and have been studying many things web-related since 1995.

Today I'm having fun with multiple business interests with Adland being my primary focus.

Specialties: Copywriting, Advertising, Art Direction, Strategy, Concept development, Direct Marketing, Social Media, Design, product development, Creative Direction, web development and more.
