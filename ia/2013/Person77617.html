KVNY Commercial Real Estate's Summary

KVNY Commercial is a division of Knickerbocker Village New York. For more than a decade worked with clients to maximize the value of their commercial real estate transactions across New York City and beyond. We represent landlords, tenants, buyers and sellers in all aspects of commercial real estate, including: real estate investment management, sales, financing, research and advisory services. Our commitment is to provide superior capital market expertise and highly competitive products, which enhance the firm’s ability to serve its clients in their endeavor to create and preserve wealth.
