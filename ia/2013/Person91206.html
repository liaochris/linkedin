Melissa Cappleman's Summary

I am a Certified Debt-Free Coach.

I empower singles and couples who have more month than money to design a transforming blueprint and live debt-free. I am able to knowledgeably coach people on many topics, including:

Budgets/cash flow planning
Getting out of debt
Foreclosure/forbearance/bankruptcy
Retirement planning
College planning
Insurance
Collectors
Investing
Estate planning &
Charitable giving

What is unique about my debt-free coaching program is that it offers a common-sense approach to personal finance and debt-reduction. None of the concepts are new. They don’t contain complicated formulas, as some financial advisors in the market use. I’ve struggled personally with my finances for many years. When I saw that I could apply these sensible principles to my life, I began to dig my way out of debt. This is the first time in my adult life that I’ve had a budget that actually works. As a result, I’ve become passionate about helping others get out of debt. Please don’t misunderstand me. I do not sell any products. The only thing that I have to offer is my coaching services. I will work with you to create a sensible plan that works for you to get out of debt.

When we have a crisis in one area of our lives, the other areas suffer. The way we deal with our money is the way we deal with our life. When we feel stuck and overwhelmed by debt, we don’t feel like anything we do matters, so why try? My debt-free coaching program will give you hope and concrete tools to get out of debt.

I’m pleased to say that as of March, 2010, I am completely debt-free! I no longer feel shackled by my financial situation. That hope is what I’d like to offer to you. Please contact me to schedule your complimentary 45-minute sample coaching session. I’m happy to share with you how you can design a transforming blueprint to live debt-free.
