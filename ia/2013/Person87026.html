Mark Root-Wiley's Projects

  • White Center Chamber of Commerce: Visit White Center / Growing a Global Village

    • June 2012 to September 2012
    Team Members: Mark Root-Wiley, Kathi "george" Wheeler, Jade Blackwater, Ryan Castoldi

    White Center Seattle is a unique and diverse community with a hard working history. In recent decades White Center has become a welcoming gateway for immigrants and refugees who enrich the neighborhood with growing families, businesses, and cultural institutions. The top languages spoken here include English, Spanish, Vietnamese, Khmer/Cambodian, Tigrinya, Amharic, Arabic, Somali and Samoan.

    More than 200+ local retail businesses provide essential neighborhood services within walking/busing distance. VisitWhiteCenter.com is a welcoming online space for neighborhood events, retail business profiles, and local news. New content includes information about neighborhood goods, services, and history. This website can be used by the White Center Chamber of Commerce for years to come to promote local retail businesses and events.
