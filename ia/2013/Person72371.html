Karl Bischoff's Summary

I am COO/Chairman of PhinneyBischoff, specializing in integrated brand design, including research and strategy, design, interactive, and ideation with a special emphasis on a transparent and inclusive approach to client collaborations.

PhinneyBischoff design expertise encompasses a wide-range of services including Web sites and multimedia, ideation, corporate identities, marketing collateral, brochures, packaging and annual reports.
