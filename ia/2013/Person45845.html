Frank Robinson's Summary

Island Events is a top rated firm of corporate meeting, event and incentive planners with over a 100 year combined track record of diverse experience within incentives, events, destination and hotel / resort management.

We offer a team that applies superior organization, coordination and staff leadership skills; communicates and works from a place of understands the power of meetings and special events and the role they play in aligning senior leaders and staff with significant business goals.   

We exceed all stakeholder expectations by utilizing metrics to evaluate satisfaction rates and consistently run at (97%) with desired ROI.  

Island Events manages the details of meetings and incentive locations, content, DMC services, agendas and budgets.

We broker for our clients a financial savings, we research options, evaluate and negotiate all contracts, while leveraging vendor relationships to maximize services and minimize costs - we provide value and affordable luxury.   

At Island Events we recruit, hire, train, motivate and mentor staff to meet the Island Events brand & experience standards.

We are a solution oriented team of skilled professionals who thrive on trouble-shooting and pre-emptive planning  

We offer in-depth and relevant experience in all aspects of Destination & Event Management:
- Incentive group travel logistical planning, budgeting & operations management
- Unique and relevant theme event design, costing, planning & production
- Show Produciton (technical & creative elements)
- Hotel Logistics & Contract Negotiations (F&B, Rooms, etc.)
- Transportation management and logistics
- Print Development
- Entertainment Management
- Full Program Staffing
- Vendor Sourcing
- Budget Development & Management

Specialties

Developing and guiding the creative process of designing, planning and executing memorable events.
