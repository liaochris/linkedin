Martin A W Smith's Summary

A former Royal Marine, Martin began his career in therapy in 2001 when he enrolled on a Diploma course in Sports Massage Therapy, IIST, and Personal Training with Premier. A keen sports person himself, playing rugby for over seven years, Martin’s interest in sports injuries was partly self-motivated.

Working at Histon Football Club and a Sports Injury Clinic in Cambridge was Martin’s first taste of what has developed into a passion. With the thirst for personal development and knowledge, Martin furthered his education with a degree course in Sports Therapy at London Metropolitan University. During his degree Martin gained valuable experience including placements with GB under 16’s Ice Hockey, and a year’s placement with London Irish RFC. Upon graduation in 2008 Martin was offered a voluntary position at London Wasps RFC.

Currently Martin is working closely with high level Mixed Martal Arts Fighter, several Cambridge University Sports Societies and Cambridge Triathlon Club all looking to develop and gain the best experience and knowldge to become a more rounded sports therapist.

He specialises in pre & post injury management, remedial-recovery therapy, functional fascial taping and myofascial soft tissue therapy.
