Malcolm O. Munro's Summary

Attention: CEOs, VPs, and HR Professionals

Are you:

Disappointed by your managers’ inability to get things done?
Futilely trying to get more out of your managers but find they lack technical and interpersonal skills?
Frustrated by your managers’ inability to manage employee performance?
Inundated with organizational conflict and petty politics among your managers?
Trying to identify future managerial talent but not finding any?

If so, you’re not alone. Many organizations today are running up against increasing demands and finding their management team is simply not up to the challenge. Operating in a fluctuating economy doesn’t help nor does a lack of skilled managers to keep the team together.

Guess what? I can help you!!! I can help you diagnose your challenges through consulting, grow your managers through training, and develop your team through coaching.

So, if you’re the owner, senior executive, or HR manager of a small, medium, or large-sized business or organization and you’d like to …

Improve the effectiveness of your managers,
Develop the technical and “people” skills of your staff,
Keep your team motivated and productive,
Maximize your profits by investing in your people

…then make sure you contact me as soon as possible. Why? Your organization’s success hinges heavily on the effectiveness of your management team. If they’re not functioning, your business is doomed!

I am committed to helping you become a more effective organization by leveraging people skills and management development.

  • The Greatest Boss Who Ever Lived: Management Lessons from the Life and Work of Jesus Christ

    • Main Line Press
    • February 1, 2011
    Authors: Malcolm O. Munro

    Could You Pull This Off?

    If you were told to expand your organization into a world-wide force and market your message to millions of people, not just now, but for at least the next 2,000 years, could you do it?

    Your Challenge:

    No advertising budget

    No Internet

    No social media

    No postcards

    No sign spinners

    12 member team would include one back-stabbing traitor

    11 guys who had no formal qualifications or education whatsoever.

    Impossible? Maybe.


    But not for Jesus Christ

    In just three years He changed the course of history and established an enduring organization that only grows stronger each year. He did it without modern technology, air travel, or fancy corporate presentations. His team was ordinary at best and selfish and divisive at worst.

    Yet in spite of His divinity, His management practices were quite ordinary. How did He do it? Just seven simple (but not simplistic) principles:

    Defer to the Leader

    Focus on the Inside

    Don’t Micromanage

    Have Your Team’s Back

    Always Teach

    Celebrate

    Show Empathy

    In this 104 page book, management expert Malcolm O. Munro deciphers these lessons by showing you how Jesus did it, and then makes practical application to the 21st century workplace.
