David Jacquet's Summary

The InfoSec Group provides Information Security services to the financial and health care industries, but also to government agencies and general companies.

Services include Penetration Testing (both internal and external), Security Training (technical and non-technical), Web Security Reviews, Social Engineering, Public Speaking and Content Development.

David also owned and operated Certified CSI, LLC, a Computer Forensics company.

Dave has over 15 years of experience in IT and holds many certifications, such as the MCSE, the CISSP, the CEH and the CCE among many others.

Prior to working in IT, David worked as a College Instructor for such institutions of higher learning as MIT, Bowdoin College and the Naval Academy.
