Maurice Preter MD's Summary

Maurice Preter, MD is an experienced psychodynamic psychiatrist, psychopharmacologist, neurologist, and medical-legal expert in private practice on Manhattan’s Upper East Side. He also has an active international clinical consulting practice and is the founder and principal of Global Medical Advisors (GlobalMedicalAdvisors.net), an international medical concierge and private health advisory service. He recently joined the advisory board of www.Simbiot.net, a next-generation genomics data analysis and storage company.

Educated at the Universities of Munich, Germany and of Paris, France, he received his postgraduate specialty training in neurology and psychiatry at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine in New York, and is dually board certified in both specialties. He has researched and written about a wide range of neuropsychiatric topics, including stroke, sleep disorders, epilepsy, panic disorder, psychological trauma, and the history of neurology in China. Dr. Preter is a member of the psychiatry faculty of Columbia University’s College of Physicians and Surgeons, an associate professor of neurology at Downstate Medical Center, and internationally active as lecturer, medical educator and as clinical and medical-legal consultant. His particular interest is in the diagnosis and integrated treatment of conditions that overlap between neurology, psychological health and general medicine (e.g., chronic pain, dementia). Dr. Preter holds physician licenses in New York, Paris, Shanghai, Beijing, and Germany, and is fluent in English, French, and German.
Please refer to www.psychiatryneurology.net and www.GlobalMedicalAdvisors.net for detailed info.

Specialties: Clinical neurosciences, neurology, psychiatry, second opinion, international private medical care, Concierge medicine, forensic/medical-legal expertise, conscious pain and suffering, international expat care, medical business in the New China, academic relations, medical tourism, biotech startups.

Our mission is very simple: to be your lifetime healthcare partner, available to you 24/7 to ensure that you will have the best care when you need it, and where you need it.

TODAY’S HEALTH CARE CHALLENGES
GMA has continued to grow as the challenges of the health care system remain entangled in bureaucracy. For the past couple of decades, medical care in all countries has become overly reliant on the super-specialist model, and on the use of technology as the primary tool for examination and diagnosis. The experienced, well-rounded, thoughtful, personal physicians for that critically important first step in the diagnostic process, have become a true rarity. Although such doctors still exist, and we find them for you, this physician model has for the most part been replaced by the technician model – test, test, test. Where expertise-based medical care (knowledge capital-intensive) has been replaced by machine-based disease management (facility capital-intensive), broad diagnostic skills may get lost in the process when you need them most. In addition, with the mass influence of the pharmaceutical industry, the health care system suffers from an often unacknowledged commercial conflict of interest. These commercial interests influence medical education, leading to a relative unawareness among many doctors of frequently superior treatment modalities that are dismissed as “low-cost”, “older” or “complementary-alternative” and are relegated to the fringes of medicine.
