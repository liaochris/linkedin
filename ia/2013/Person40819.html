Ellen Freedman, CLM's Summary

A law practice administrator and management consultant for over 30 years, Ellen Freedman helps lawyers and law firms improve their bottom line. Through her company, Freedman Consulting, Ellen assists attorneys on an as-needed and outsourced basis. Through her work with the Pennsylvania Bar Association, Ellen is considered by many to be the single most valuable member benefit to help attorneys run their practices more profitably and/or efficiently.

From start-up through every phase of development, Ellen is known throughout Pennsylvania and the United States as a top level, efficient, and easy to work with adviser to small and mid-sized law firms.

The areas in which Ellen assists law firms and/or individual lawyers, include:

Strategic planning
