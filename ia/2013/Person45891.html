Fran Vincent-Muscat's Summary

Fran Vincent is the author of MySpace for Musicians: The Comprehensive Guide to Marketing Your Music Online and a digital marketing and music-industry consultant. Before starting her own consultancy, she spent six years as the marketing and public relations specialist for Warner Bros. Publications (WMG), tackling the company’s pop music marketing and overall public relations strategies and execution.

As a consultant, publicist, and writer, Fran has worked with clients around the world including: Prince “BlkMagic” Damons; Out of the Ark Music, the UK’s top children’s music publisher; PowerFX, the leading provider of audio samples and loops; Detroit producer and songwriter Andieveboi, who has written with Amp Fiddler and other noted artists; Walter Beasley, Billboard Top 20 Contemporary Jazz artist, performance consultant, and educator; the University of Miami Music Publications catalog featuring high-end performance sheet music and instructional titles; Electronic Musician, the nation’s premiere magazine for home studio musicians; and InTune Magazine, a monthly glossy, whose covers are graced by today’s hottest artists, and reaches every middle and high school music classroom in the nation.

Through her marketing work, Fran assists clients in building a positive image in their industry, and even takes some into uncharted territory as they break ground in new markets. Clients rely on her to help them craft viral and offline marketing campaigns, position them as experts in their fields, gain greater visibility among customers, fans and their community, and navigate the entertainment industry’s players and pitfalls. Fran’s results-driven PR/marketing campaigns, creative and artistic direction, strategic counseling, relationship-building savvy, and writing and editing skills make her an essential addition to her clients’ in-house teams.
