Abraham Galvan's Summary

Abraham Galvan joins Coldwell Banker as an Associate Broker and is a market-savvy professional whose client's satisfaction and best interests are paramount. His desire to serve his client's particular needs coupled with his market expertise and superior negotiating skills set him apart. Plus he has 7 years of mortgage experience. Abraham knows what it takes to prepare a property and create a marketing campaign that ensures maximum exposure, brief market time and strong offers from only the most qualified buyers. For his Buyer clients, Abraham takes a keen interest in finding not only the ideal property for his clients demands but, more importantly, a property that proves to be priced fairly - one with minimal risk and optimal return on investment. Whether buying or selling, Abraham provides you with a special attention one expects of a dedicated real estate professional whose top priority is protecting the interests of the most discerning clients. He is praised most often by his clients for his enthusiasm, his patient approach and unwavering loyalty. Abraham has been a Realtor for 11years and prides himself on helping his clients achieve their goals of home ownership. He handles all types of real estate transactions. No home is too small to receive his utmost attention. Abraham strives to go above and beyond his client's expectations in service. Abraham lives in the Upland area with his wife and his active 2-yr old son. When it's time for relaxation, he grabs his golf bag and heads for the links. (Beginner)
Over the last 11 years, Abraham has lived and owned properties in many different San Gabriel Valley neighborhoods. His experiences and passion for real estate have enhanced a natural ability to expertly guide buyers and sellers through their home purchase or sale. Abraham’s energy and positive attitude makes him a pleasure to be around. That is why a great deal of Abraham's business is due to referrals from satisfied clients.
