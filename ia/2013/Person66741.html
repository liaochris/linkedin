John Muehling, CSM's Summary

I am an accomplished marketing executive with over 15 years of experience in both strategic and operational marketing. Having started my career in sales, I have firm understanding of the dichotomy between marketing and sales and have played a key role in helping organizations overcome that challenge by deploying integrated marketing strategies that deliver high-quality leads, resulting in above-average conversion rates. I have spent the better part of the last 15 years developing an extensive knowledge of demand generation marketing and honing my analytical abilities. I am now utilizing my operational experience with SalesForce.com, my expertise as a Certified Marketo trainer and my SCRUM Master techniques to manage and deliver marketing automation solutions, which help LeadMD clients propel their organizations to stronger revenues and higher profitability.

Specialties: B2B and B2C Marketing Strategies • Demand Generation Programs • Marketing Automation • Salesforce Automation • Data Analytics Expert • Social Marketing Tactician • Cross-Channel Marketing • Database Marketing • Content Marketing • Email Marketing • Brand Positioning & Management • Budget Development & Management
