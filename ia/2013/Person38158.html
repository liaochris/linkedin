Mike Miller, PhD's Summary

I am a Licensed Psychologist and Licensed Independent Chemical Dependency Counselor. I provide individual, marital, and family, therapy to adults, adolescents, and children (age 6 and up). I have over 30 years of experience. In addition to private practice, I have also worked with diverse problems in a variety of settings, including community mental health centers, a Cleveland Clinic Family Health Center, a managed care mental health program, schools, a college counseling center, residential settings for the mentally handicapped, and the Veteran's Administration. Working with mentally handicapped individuals has deepened my beliefs that all, regardless of functioning, have the ability to grow and that all are deserving respect.

Specialties

I specialize in treating anxiety disorders, having advanced training with OCD and Trichotillomania. I am also very experienced in treating depression, couples issues, ADHD, and alcohol and drug abuse.
