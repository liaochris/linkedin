Beau Gunderson's Summary

I consider myself a developer, a hacker, a tinkerer, and (most importantly) a software gardener--someone who guides the process and judiciously prunes when needed.

My career thus far has been characterized by an arc towards data: its retrieval, transformation, storage, analysis, and visualization.

I strongly believe in continuously learning new skills and supporting my coworkers.

I received 3% of the vote in the general election--enough votes to spoil the election (though who knows in which direction). 3% of the votes mirrors the approximately 3% of voters in Washington State who identified as Libertarian in 2004.
