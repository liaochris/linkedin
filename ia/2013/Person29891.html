Daniel Rivera's Summary

Daniel Rivera is a credible and influential storyteller who excels in writing for film, television and online journalism. He is a native New Yorker ( the Bronx and the Upper West Side) who has covered musical acts in the genre's of hip-hop, rock, and adult contemporary. Daniel has written features about artists such as Darryl "DMC" McDaniels, Skyar Grey, Kat Deluna and Making Movies.
