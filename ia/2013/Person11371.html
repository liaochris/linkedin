Bee Lee Vang's Summary

I’m a talented artist and a former Marine. I have over one year of game development experience, over two years of volunteer service, and over 4 years of solid foundations in fine arts, 3d modeling and animation training and education. Above it all I enjoy making art. I was involved in creating artworks (particularly 3d modeling characters, hard surface, organic models, prop models, environment assets, painting textures, rigging, and animating characters) for game titles such as:

1) Pachinko Sabotage,
2) Doomsday Zombies,
3) Armor Tank Assault 2,
4) Paper Dinosaurs,
5) Liberico

I am available. The Department of Veteran's Affair's representatives (VA) have been advocating on my behalf. One of the benefits of hiring a veteran is that The VA has a hiring incentive that will help the employer by paying 50% of a veteran's wage if the veteran matches the employer's qualifications or If hired and in need of more training the VA will pay for the veteran's training preferably while on the job. I am also interested in an informational interview and feedbacks.

As a former US Marine I have earned several military medals, including the Good Conduct Award and the rarely awarded Navy Achievement Medal as a Supply Sub-Chief. My military service was from 1999 to 2003 when I received an honorable discharge from the US military and I'm a father of two children. During my service, I have painted murals in the Marines and designed t-shirt logos. I graduated from the Academy of Art University in 2009. Also, I earned a Master Development Certificate in 3D Art in Video Games in 2012 from Ameon Incorporated.
