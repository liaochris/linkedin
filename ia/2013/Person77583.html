Kusumba S's Summary

They are those who look at things the way they are and ask why…
… I dream of things that never were, and ask why not ?

Kusumba S, a technocrat and a visionary, founded his dream company in 2000, with a vision to provide next generation enterprise network products and solutions.

After his stint as a “Radio Officer” in Merchant Navy, started his career in 1994 as a Systems Engr. in a small company. He has christened some of the best technologies & owned companies in several technology domains. He is well known personality in Telecom and IP Business across the Asia Pac.

With an experience of more than a decade in designing, implementing and managing Wide Area Networks, ISP networks spanning various countries, he has incubated many businesses around Internet & each of them excelled in its own perfection.

Currently is engaged in rolling out one of the first ever built Digital Signage Networks that address various concerns of advertisers and media owners across the World. He is the Managing Director at Kiosk Mint. Kiosk Mint is a new age Digital Signage Network hosted on the Internet... many called it as "Adsense'ng Digital Signage Industry".

He is a consultant for various ISPs and large corporates around the region. He has delivered excellent services as an “IT Consultant” to AP Police and was instrumental in creating AP Police Data Centre, a facility which is one of its kind in the country today. Several IT initiatives have resulted in adopting IT infrastructure by AP Police.

His strengths are his vision, belief in himself, passion for technology and his ability to inspire others. He has a contagious enthusiasm, never ending drive and determination. He is a realist with a positive mental attitude and thinks work should be fun. Initiative, innovation and commitment are his hallmarks.

Kusumba is a hobby pilot and flying has been along side his career all through. Ham radio as a hobby given him the most required being a Technology Person.
