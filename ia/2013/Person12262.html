BAYSHORE BOOKS's Summary

BayShore Books LLC is a general new and used bookstore specializing in orders for schools and non-profit organizations. We are the leading distributor for numerous independent publishers, so are often able to offer generous discounts as well as exceptional service. We also offer discounted proof reading services to independent authors and publishers.
