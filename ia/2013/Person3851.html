Alexandros Mylopoulos' Summary

A web analyst with a business mind and a solid international background having studied and worked in four EU countries.

I have been involved in almost every aspect of digital marketing including paid search, email marketing, SEO, social media and usability. This experience is now precious in my role as a Senior Web Analyst, helping me to provide actionable insights beyond plain reporting.

I have also seen the other side, that of a website owner, in my duties as an E-commerce Manager at bBooks.gr, an online bookstore. There I have practiced hands-on web merchandising, order fulfillment, customer service, stock management, pricing & discounting.

Member of the Young Advisors group of Neelie Kroes, European Commissioner for Digital Agenda.

Interested in partnerships and career opportunities in the domain of online marketing with emphasis on web analytics.

Specialties: Digital marketing, e-commerce management, web analytics (Omniture Site Catalyst & Discover, Google Analytics), website optimisation, email marketing, search engine optimisation, paid search marketing (AdWords), usability analysis, social media marketing.
