Mark Grindeland's Summary

I am the Chief Marketing Officer at TeleTech Holdings. In addition to my responsibilities as CMO, I am responsible for driving Corporate Strategy, and our Product Development Team.

I am also the co-founder and Chairman of ShesConnected Multimedia Corp. ShesConnected has been exclusively focused on Social Media since 2007.

I have deep experience in Advertising, Marketing, Customer Experience, Management Consulting, Mobile, and Social Media at executive levels.

I have had the wonderful experience of working with some of the world’s leading consumer brands and Agencies. I have been deeply involved in driving strategic leadership in the ever changing digital media landscape.

I was also the co-founder of m-Qube Inc. the premier provider of mobile messaging and data solutions in North America. I was also the Chief Executive Officer, Wunderman - Europe, Middle East, and Africa region, and prior to that was a Senior Vice President at Digitas leading the Planning & Analysis Group, and Relationship Manager for clients.

I have worked with leading companies on marketing strategies including; American Express, Citibank, Bank of America, USAA, Morgan Stanley, FedEx, IBM, Ford, Nissan, Hyundai, General Motors, Ericsson, Rogers Wireless, State Street Bank, AT&T, T-Mobile, BSNL, Vodafone, O2, Bell Mobility, Cingular, MTN, Sony, Chubb Insurance, Prudential, Sears, Loblaws, President's Choice, P&G, Harcourt, Houghton Mifflin, US Airways, Alliance Air, Mitsui, and others.

Specialties: customer experience, BPO, digital media, mobile, wireless web, social media, community management, database marketing, market strategy, I work across multiple vertical categories in B to C and B to B including: telecommunications/cable/media, financial services (banking, mutual funds, insurance, credit cards, commercial lending, and leasing), health care, retail, CPG, airlines, consumer electronics, and professional services (management consulting).
