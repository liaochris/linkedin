Mark Oborn's Summary

"Mark spent 25 years working as a dental technician with 15 of those years running is own business.

He also has a Masters Degree in Business Administration (MBA) majoring in marketing, innovation and creativity.

He is also a certified Master NLP Practioner and NLP Master Coach, Master Time Line Therapy Practitioner and Master Practitioner of Hypnosis.

Having moved away from technical dentistry, his businesses now include a highly creative marketing company specialising in helping dentists find new patients with online marketing (www.markoborn.com) and a new venture helping 'small business' owners prepare for, and achieve success in their lives and work (www.mysuccessfullife.co.uk).

Mark's passion is to be fun, giving, challenging and stimulating and you have his permission to kick him if he's not!"
