  • Developing an International Space Station Curriculum for the Bootstrapped Learning Program

    • IEEE Aerospace Conference, 2010
    • March 6, 2010

    • September 2005
    Authors: John Mohammed, Jim Ong, Jian Li, Barbara Sorensen
