Bobby Finch's Experience

Creative Director

beeuneeq Ltd

May 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months) Hatch End, Pinner, NW London

beeuneeq Ltd is now a multi-disciplined, experienced design studio specialising in design, branding, advertising and marketing solutions across all all areas of communication including off-line print solutions & exhibition/signage solutions and new media solutions such as web, tablet and mobile site design and online marketing.

As a small team, we use our different creative disciplines to communicate our clients’ message in this ever changing world where you have to stay one step ahead of the competition. We are small enough to care but big enough to cope as they say.

If you are a Start-Up, Micro, SME, Corporate or even Retail business or just have a great idea
for a product, business or service, we can help you communicate your branding, design, marketing and advertising on-print, on-line, on-everything. Design as uneeq as you...

In April 2011 beeuneeq Ltd took over the administration of The Graphic Village and built a photo/graphic studio along side our graphic/design studio which we now use for our own photoshoots and also hire out to other creative professionals and photographers.

Owner

beeuneeq

December 2004 – April 2009 (4 years 5 months) Hatch End

I moved into Hatch End in November 2004, and rebranded from RCF Design to beeuneeq and at the same time employed a full time designer.

As the business grew we required more space so extended the size of the studio in the summer of 2006 and then further again in the summer of 2008 as we were now a team of 5.

We re-branded the building to The Graphic Village and the business was then incorporated in May 2009.

Owner

RCF Design

November 2000 – November 2004 (4 years 1 month) Harrow

I decided to become self employed when I left University in the Summer
of 2000, and worked on a free-lance basis in-house at WD&P Limited using the trading name RCF Design.

After 2 years I became VAT registered in November 2002 and set-up my own home studio.
