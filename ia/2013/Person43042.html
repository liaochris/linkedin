Henny Williams' Summary

I have been operating Essential Bookkeeping Solutions since 2000, servicing the Illawarra’s small business community. I assist small business owners by providing trusted and reliable bookkeeping services and have extensive experience from working in accounting based roles for more than 20 years.

I am passionate about bookkeeping and helping my clients achieve their goals. I implement effective procedures tailored specifically to each client and provide an insight into their businesses’ financial position and performance through effective reporting and analysis.

Being a registered BAS Agent I am qualified to prepare and lodge Business Activity Statements and ensuring that small business owners are compliant with the Australian Taxation Office an associated agencies.
