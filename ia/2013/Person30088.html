Dan Kruse's Summary

I have worked in both large and small studios, with and without deep technical support. I am self motivated and capable of working on my own or as a member of a team.

I have been using Maya for over 11 years now, focusing on Lighting, Lookdev and Compositing. I have extensive production experience with Nuke, Arnold, V-ray, Mental Ray and Render-Man. I also have experience setting up physical based lighting pipelines. I started my career in vfx doing audio and video editing. With Camera Tracking/Layout as my first feature film role, I have a clear understanding of production pipelines from beginning to end.

After Effects, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Photoshop.
