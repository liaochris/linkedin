Jon Mulholland's Summary

With a background in web development, I'm an experienced Technical Architect and Digital Delivery Lead, passionate about using agile methods to partner with business teams and deliver technology projects that create amazing user experiences.

I love working with web technologies (HTML, CSS, JS, RESTful APIs) tools (Heroku, GitHub) and the Salesforce platform.
