Dale Himes, Principal's Summary

Dale Himes is an experienced planning professional in the AEC and Telecommunication industries offering support services to growing firms needing to develop new business, successfully manage projects and business operations. He has worked on a variety of assignments in Oregon, Washington, California, Arizona, Colorado and Mexico City. Dale’s key focus is to provide effective project communication and problem solving skills to businesses and government needing assistance in the following areas:

Project Management & Administration
• Development of Scopes, Schedules and Budgets

• Managing Multidiscipline Project Teams including Subcontractors
