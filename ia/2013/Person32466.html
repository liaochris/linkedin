David Rencsenyi's Summary

I am a 3d artist, working primarily in Maya and Zbrush, but using several other softwares, such as Photoshop, Nuke, Mari, etc.

I've been working in the 3d industry for over 4 years now. I spent the last year living in London, working on various projects, including TV, commercials and advertising.

I am looking for further opportunities within a company as a modeler, or 3d generalist.

Specialties: 3d modeling in Maya and Zbrush, texturing in Photoshop and Mari, rendering (Mental Ray and Vray), lighting.
Efficient in: rigging, animating, ncloth and dynamics in Maya, compositing in Nuke, Illustrator, Realflow, Mudbox
