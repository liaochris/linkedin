Barry Zweibel's Summary

It’s not easy to be at your best day after day … after day after day. Conflicting priorities, sudden crises, impossible deadlines, people acting…well, weird – it’s a lot to deal with. And because there’s always so much to do, you, likely, just don’t have the time to be anything less than your absolute best.

Yet sometimes, even the best and brightest – people just like you! – get tripped up, slowed down, and sent sideways. Sometimes, like you, they get derailed, discombobulated, and lose their gumption.

My specialty: Fixing that.

Should we talk?!
