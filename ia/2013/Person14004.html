Brandon Harrop's Summary

I am an enthusiastic graphic designer residing in Salt Lake City. My passion for design ranges from very contemporary, modern designs through to the more alternative, vintage style. Apparel design seems to have taken the lead for me with little to do on my part and more of the client appreciation and word of mouth. The saying 'less is more' is a key inﬂuence in my design as I have a very simplistic, minimal style.

I am passionately creative, quality driven and motivated to create the best design solution. Combined with an indepth approach to research and analysis, I am able to focus on the target audience and engage the design in a way that meets the brief. Hardwork and determination are key qualities that makes my designs successful and I believe my eye for detail, typography, and high standards ensures a thorough design process and solution. While graphic design is my emphasis, I have also established some technical knowledge in HTML and CSS website development.
