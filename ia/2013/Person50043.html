Greg Beckham's Experience

Broker / Manager / Realtor CA BRE 01093185

Beckham & Associates Realtors

May 2012 – Present (1 year 5 months) Clayton, CA

Since 1990, I have worked in the real estate and mortgage industry, helping more than 1000 clients and their families buy, sell and finance their homes and investment properties.

When I started my career in real estate, I set out with a singular mission – to offer clients and fellow industry associates the absolute highest level of service and care possible. I do this by being available, and always returning your calls.

My business practice and philosophy is simple – listen carefully to your needs and provide full disclosure and ethical advice with your best interests in mind. My diverse and extensive real estate and lending experience is at your service and makes me a valuable resource to you for all of your real estate needs.

Here’s a little more about me:

I started my career in real estate in 1990 working as a loan officer for a national mortgage banker. Shortly thereafter, I went to work as a mortgage broker and started my own real estate and mortgage company in 1994, one of the first to handle real estate and loan transactions in a single office. In 1995, I became one of the top 2% of listing brokers in my market area, listing and selling over 100 homes.

My experience as a producing and managing broker, overseeing staff and agents, makes me a seasoned veteran in the real estate industry, and a valuable asset in the quest to buy or sell your home.

Now, as broker of Beckham & Associates my background in finance and real estate, along with cutting edge industry and market knowledge, makes me a single point of contact for all your real estate needs.

When I’m not helping buyers and sellers to buy or sell their homes, you may find me delivering seminars on personal growth and prosperity, promoting my most recent book, Manifest Destiny: How The Stories We Tell Ourselves Shape Our Lives, writing and recording music or enjoying life with my family.
