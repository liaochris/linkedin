Dorina Lanza's Summary

Known as the Billion Dollar Professor by colleagues and friends alike, Dorina Lanza is a sought-after Master Coach, Business Mentor, and Speaker on the topics of Wealth Creation, Entrepreneurship, Marketing, Leadership, Influence, and Motivation. With a combination of twenty years’ technical/corporate experience, twenty years’ entrepreneurial experience, and extensive academic accomplishments to her credit, Dorina is uniquely qualified to live out her passion for coaching and mentoring successful professionals into creating leveraged income streams and engaging in lucrative joint ventures and strategic alliances.

Dorina has become known for her ability to create a compelling vision that breaks new ground. Her ability to communicate that strategic vision and be a role model inspires others to realize it in their own businesses and lives. Dorina engages, motivates and inspires teams with her unique blend of vision, wit, story telling and humor. Dorina works one-on-one as well as with groups of all sizes. Her technique is equally effective with individuals as it is with corporations and groups large and small.

Specialties: wealth creation, entrepreneurship, strategic influence, strategic neuro-marketing, motivation, leadership, branding

Interests:

Dorina is a Certified Pistol Instructor, Tactical Range Officer, Ski Instructor, and Pyrotechnician. She is also a competitive sailor having sailed in the IOD World Championships. She is known as an art collector and has lent a number of her pieces to the Boston Museum of Fine Arts, DeCordova Museum and others. She is also a collector of gems, jewelry and silver and was a silver and goldsmith. Her pieces and designs have been featured in numerous galleries.

Groups and Associations:

Dorina is also founding President and Chairman of the Board of Beta Gamma Sigma [BGS] Boston. BGS is the international honor society founded to recognize and encourage scholarship and accomplishment in the field of business studies and has approximately 625,000 members worldwide. She is a member of ASMALLWORLD, Senatus, CBPI, CBP Elite, the Executive Lounge Club and is an Officer of the Executive Praetorian Club.
