Marcin Milczynski's Summary

Marcin is SAP Sales and Distribution consultant with five-year experience in full-cycle SAP implementation projects, most as a member of international project teams. He is goal oriented, focused on delivery of solutions on time and of expected quality. Specializing in consumer products (food/beverages) and pricing areas, he is familiar with Order-to-Cash process oriented approach to SAP implementation. Moreover he has also wide technical skills, including knowledge of ABAP programming, that allow preparation of both functional and technical design of enhancements in SD and DSD area.

Marcin has successfully completed as leading/expert consultant 3 full-cycle corporate SAP template creation, 2 full-cycle SAP implementation, full-cycle mobile solution built and 2 implementation projects as well as multiple improvement/maintenance projects in SAP environments.
