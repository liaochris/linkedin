Stock Market Companion and Homeschool Family Investor - are dedicated to teaching individuals and families how to:

1. Identify and measure excellent companies for the purpose of investing AND employment.

2. Learn the language of business and investing

3. Learn how to successfully and significantly grow their own money using stocks - on their own.

Our S.I.M.P.L.E. Stock Investing Method is being used by families and individuals across the USA and internationally.

We offer three products to help accomplish these objectives -

1. Global / Business Finance and Stock Investing COURSE

2. Fun and Exciting Science of Stock Investing TOOL KIT

3. 15Minute Market Update - School of Finance and Investing ONLINE.

Since 1995, Eric Stasak has grown $70K to $519K, resulting in an overall return of 641% and an average annual return of +22%. He avoided the 2008/2009 financial crash and helped others do the same. He led investors back into the market in March 2009 for significant returns.

The Stock Market Companion was built to share this understanding of successful stock market investing with the public. Since its inception in October 2009, SMC 15Minute Market Update and Watchlist published stock entries and exits has continued these very strong gains - for the benefit of many.
