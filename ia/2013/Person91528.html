Mike Merell -'MOE''s Summary

I am busy and super grateful about it!

Currently I am wrapping up as a Character Animator at DisneyToon Studios for Disney/Pixar's 'Planes', which gets a wide theatrical release in Aug 2013.

Next up, I am excited to transition over to the 'Tinkerbell' franchise where I will be an Asst. Animation Supervisor.

I am also currently writing and developing my second live-action feature/project entitled www.27Eds.com.

There is link to watch my first feature film 'Desert Vows' for free here:
http://desertvows.com/hd-digital-download

I am a veteran CGI Character Animator with almost 14 years and counting on Walt Disney Company feature films.

I spent 11+ years at Walt Disney Feature Animation as a character animator and supervising animator on Chicken Little, Mickey's Philharmagic, Atlantis, Hercules, the Hunchback of Notre Dame, and the Lion King DVD.

I have worked with Starz Animation Toronto(now ARC) on Disney's 'Gnomeo and Juliet', Warner Brothers, The Weinstein Co., The Orphanage, Incessant Rain in Nepal, & Juicebox Animation.

I started my career at one of the original computer animation companies, Metrolight Studios, with Oscar winner Tim McGovern working on films, theme park rides, and commercials.

Proficient in Maya, Softimage, Final Cut Pro, Photoshop.
The past: Alias, Wavefront, Prisms, BPIX, Cubicomp.

Specialties: Filmmaker, Director(Live Action & Animation), Supervising CGI Character Animator, Senior CGI Character Animator, Producer, Writer, Editor, extensive Animation Production Development, Artist Painter, Husband, Father, in no particular order.
