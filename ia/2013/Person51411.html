Sindre Hamlander's Summary

With more than 9 years of experience in the IT sector in Norway, the UK and Greece, I am an experienced Solution Architect. This includes years of hands-on and BSS/OSS architecture experience from multiple Telecommunications IT Projects, at both mobile and fixed voice providers, being very much involved and driving both pre-sales and large transformation projects at CSPs (e.g. to enable rapid rollout of new services in n-Play product offerings).

Core Telecommunication Competencies:
Pre-Sales, Mobile and Fixed Line Product optimizations and n-Play offerings, Product-, Service- and Resource- (P-S-R) Modelling, NGOSS (using eTOM; SID information models), Operations restructuring and analysis, functional and non-functional design and implementation of solutions (considering up-front; continuous operational costs and other needs – e.g. Central Order Management, Provisioning, Assurance, Network and Billing Integration), Design Authority Mgmt, Customization (enhancements giving CSPs a differentiating edge), Testing and validation of business cases, Data Migration, Mentoring, Training

Product competencies:
- Siebel – Product Modelling and Siebel Integration
- RODOD – Rapid Offer Design and Order Delivery
- RSDOD – Rapid Service Design and Order Delivery
- OSM – Oracle Communications Order and Service Management
- OSM COM – Central Order Management
- OSM SOM – Service Order Management
- OSM TOM – Technical Order Management
- OSM O2A – Order- to-Activate Cartridges
- UIM – Oracle Communications Unified Inventory Management
- AIA – Oracle Communications Application Integration Architecture
- AIA O2B – Order-to-Bill PIPS (Process Integration Packs)
- AIA AABC – Agent-Assisted-Billing-Care PIPS

Other Technology/Tools:
JBossAS, JBossESB, Drools, Spring, JMX, SOAP, Tomcat, Mule, SIP, JPA, Hibernate, SQL, J2EE, JMS, JSP, Portlets, JUnit, JWebUnit, EasyMock, XQuery, BPEL, XSL, XPath, Bash Scripting, JProfiler, Eclipse, JDeveloper, Linux, Cloud Computing (e.g. AWS EC2)
