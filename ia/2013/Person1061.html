Anita "AC" Clinton's Summary

With over a decade of creative design, marketing and editorial experience, Anita “AC” Clinton has proven that whatever she sets her mind to accomplish, IS POSSIBLE. She believes that the life she lives is the result of the reality she creates. Likewise, she is passionate about helping others envision and actualize what is truly possible in their lives. She is a transformational leader focused on building relationships and connecting others to their passion and purpose in life.

“We were all created to achieve great things in this world. We each have a unique assignment here on earth and we possess various gifts and talents that are to be used to manifest that purpose. Within it are our dreams, visions, and desires – essentially through purpose – true happiness and fulfillment in life is found. Unfortunately those who discover and actualize their purpose tend to be the anomalies, and I’m here to change that.”

Anita is the Founder/CEO of Anita Clinton Enterprises, LLC, a boutique business consulting firm whose mission is to assist consumers, entrepreneurs, organizations and other small business professionals to meet their primary objectives and take their vision from concept to reality. She is also the Founder/Director of the The Game Alliance, an upcoming non-profit organization that seeks to assist college-bound student athletes WIN at the GAME of life through athletics.

Anita previously served as the COO of wakeUPgirl, a multi-media organization with a mission to inspire women to wake up and live their dreams; and the Creator/Editor-in-Chief of wakeUPgirl Magazine. Proceeding wakeUPgirl, Anita worked in the mortgage industry for seven years. In her quest to become an “expert” in the real estate field, she served as the Editor-in-Chief for Invest With Passion, a real estate investment magazine. Her profound knowledge of these industries affords her the ability to help others connect and launch their dreams and purpose.
