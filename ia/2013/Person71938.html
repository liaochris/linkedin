Kaprun Rentals' Summary

About Kaprun Rentals

At the beginning of 2009 we began to achieve our dream, our own apartment in Kaprun. The dream of our own place in one of Europe's finest ski areas became in the spring of 2009 a reality.

But there was more:
Encourage by our enthusiastic fellow apartment owners, and the love of entrepreneurship, we decided to challenge ourself and started our own holiday rental organisation.

And no less than a half year later "Kaprun Rentals" was born. What began as an initiative of renting out our own apartment became in less than a half year later a fully operational holiday apartment renting organization.

Through quality service and exclusive apartments for rent, we hope to offer our guests as much enthusiasm for the various holiday options of "the Europe Sport Region".

Hopefully we can welcome you as one of our guests during one of the 4 seasons holidays in one of our exclusive apartments in Kaprun.

With kind regards, Melanie Treffers & Hawé van den Brink
