Luke Rennie, FICF, LUTCF's Summary

My professional experience includes working with 1000 members of the Knights of Columbus, who are in the Rosemount, Minnesota #5569, Hastings, Minnesota #1600, Vermillion, Minnesota #5199, Burnsville, Minnesota #6374 and Farmington, Minnesota #2400 councils.

My passion for people has helped me achieve a high level of success and trust with those I am entrusted to serve. I have earned the Fraternal Insurance Counselor Fellow (FICF) designation, I just completed my course of study for the LUTCF (Life Underwriter Training Council Fellow) designation.I have Qualified for the Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) in 2009 and 2010 and am on the board of directors for the Minnesota Fraternal Insurance Counselors Association.

Specialties: I specialize in providing advice and guidance to Catholic families so they are able to achieve their financial goals, while protecting them with life insurance, long term care insurance, disability income insurance and fixed annuities using the highest rated company in North America, The Knights of Columbus, in a safe financial environment.
