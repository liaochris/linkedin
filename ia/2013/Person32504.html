David Romberg's Summary

I am a documentary/narrative filmmaker, who is currently working towards completion of my MFA in Film in Media Arts at Temple University in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

In addition to my studies, I am working on my first Feature-length Documentary titled "Man of the Monkey". The film takes place on the Brazilian Island of Ilha Grande. I travelled to Brazil to complete this film during the summer of 2011.

I am currently raising funds for the film's Post-Production, which is getting ready to begin in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The expected completion date for this project is January/February 2013.

Further information about the film can be found at my company's website, http://manofthemonkey.com.

Specialties

Documentary and Narrative Film.

My films that are currently in production include:

"Man of the Monkey" (Feature-length documentary, fundraising for post-production)
"Mi Querida Buenos Aires" (Developing the short film into a feature-length documentary)
"Umbra" (Script complete, fundraising for pre-production)
