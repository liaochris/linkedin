BV Lawson's Summary

Short story awards/finalist include Center Press Masters Literary Awards, Armchair Interviews, Deadly Ink, Mysterical-E, Crime and Suspense, Press 53 Open, three-time Derringer Award nominee and a 2012 Derringer winner, and American Independent Writers 2010 story competition.

Short-story credits include dozens of magazines and anthologies including Cantaraville, ESC! Magazine, Mouth Full of Bullets, Static Movement, Powder Burn Flash, Plots With Guns, Cynic Online, Rose and Thorn, Woman’s World, Fringe Magazine, Absent Willow Review, Grimm Tales, Needle, Pulp Ink 2, and When the Veil Drops.

A Scott Drayco mystery novel manuscript was a finalist for the Maryland Writers Association awards and made the longlist for the Southwest Writers Mystery Novel Award.

In addition, I've penned public radio and commercial television feature scripts, as well as articles for regional and national publications such as The Washington Times, Sunday Journal, Insight, Mystery Readers Journal and over a dozen special-interest magazines.
