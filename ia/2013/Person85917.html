Marie Nayaka's Summary

I live and breathe social media. I love the potential, the scope for creativity, the accessibility and the fantastic results that any brand can achieve. But, believe me, I know that when working desperately hard to grow your brand online it’s all too easy to get tangled in a web of inconsistent social media activity. Where’s it going wrong? Madness is doing the same thing over and over again, each time expecting different results.

I’m here for people who take their businesses seriously and want to be in control of how they’re perceived in all markets, across all channels; people who view customers as more than just “Likes” or followers on a page. It doesn’t matter if you are selling products or services, my courses are suitable for aspiring or established businesses, either with an existing social media presence, or one waiting in the wings.

Being fortunate enough to work with amazing and inspirational people from all around the world has taught me firstly that everyone has the same questions, goals, fears and fantasies no matter what they’re selling: “How do I get more Fans?”, “I’m going to use social media to build my brand”, “What if no one likes me?” and “I just know I’m going to get 10,000 authentic new followers by Wednesday”…and also that it’s easy for expectations to slip out of proportion with reality, which is often right about the moment when frustration, fear, denial and meltdown descend.

That’s why my blogs, videos and courses cut through confusion and serve to provide the direct solutions to the questions you really want answered…so you can get on with the business of selling more products, making more money and, loving what you do.
