Bob McKamie's Summary

A Question and Answer Session with MUTT online owner Bob McKamie

Q. What is MUTT Online?
A.. MUTT Online is a free art site for writers, poets, photographers and artists. MUTT is the ultimate networking site for all who want to succeed in all forms of art.

Q. MUTT is an interesting name! What does it stand for?
A. MUTT stands for Making Us Think Together.

Q. How Unique! How can members of the site best utilize it?
A. Members may post a profile, photo gallery, classified ad or compete in online contests.

Q. Is MUTT online just for artists?
A. No, not at all! Profiles are also available for editors, literary agents, publishers and art galleries. We just added food and architectural photographers, as well as literary publicists to our categories. We even have a social media strategist to help members with getting more publicity.

Q. What special features do you offer members?
A. We feature a member of each category monthly. MUTT also has a “Golden Nugget” section, where members can gain worldwide exposure. There are also informative tutorials and articles by some of the leading professionals in all of these categories.

Q. I saw something on the site called a "MUTT to MUTT" challenge...what is that?
A. Artists can challenge each other to popularity contests using their galleries. These are called "MUTT to MUTT" challenges.

Q. What commission do you collect on artwork sold on the site?
A. MUTT charges no commissions on any work sold by members and all sales are strictly between the buyer and seller.

Q. Is there anything else you’d like to add, Bob?
A. Don’t be afraid to promote yourself as an artist and maximize your exposure. Go to

http://www.muttonline.com/members/

to show others the work in which you have poured your heart.
