Dwight Hill's Summary

Company Website: http://www.theretailadvisory.com
Email: dhill@theretailadvisory.com

Dwight Hill is an accomplished retail executive / consultant offering a unique blend of 23 years of industry and consulting experience to retailers through The Retail Advisory LLC. His experience includes leadership positions with Deloitte LLP, Neiman Marcus, Michaels Stores, and Zale‘s. He has a proven track record of leading design and implementation of continuous improvements for retailers that streamline operations, improve the customer experience, and drive profitable results.

His industry and consulting experience spans multiple formats among Fortune 500 retailers including; luxury/specialty, department store, grocery/mass, and convenience.

He is eminent in the marketplace through authorship of whitepapers on the topics of Omni-Channel Management, Customer Experience and Retail Labor/Workload Planning. Dwight has an MBA from Southern Methodist University.

He is currently assisting large $3b+ retailers and brands with cases related to global benchmarking, labor management, litigation consulting, and partnership strategy.
