Stephen Krefman's Summary

I believe in an interactive approach to Intellectual Property client representation.

The best IP portfolio comes from integrating IP procedures and activities with those of business and product development. This approach helps inventors focus on developing and protecting innovation that truly advances the business, technical and IP strategies. In the case of consumer facing products, that means developing an IP portfolio that provides a proprietary position in technologies best meeting identified compelling consumer needs.

I have extensive experience providing IP and eCommerce counseling, prosecuting patent and trademark applications, drafting and negotiating complex international technology development and transfer related agreements. I have worked closely with engineers and business people and have personal experience in both roles.

I can help you in your efforts to develop an IP portfolio that provides a sustainable competitive position in the market defined by your customer's needs, rather than merely protecting narrow features of your commercialized products.

Specialties
