Giles Hutchison's Summary

I have twenty years experience in technology development and its commercial exploitation including; directing software and hardware development teams, authoring of commercial agreements including technology licensing, sales and partnership agreements. In addition I have built teams for two start-up ventures, growing the first from two to twelve people, prior to selling it in 2000, and the second venture grew to fourteen people, raising considerable angel and institutional funding. I am a clear strategic thinker, paying attention to detail as well as operational and growth aspects. My skills are highly suited to start-up technology business that requires senior business development, operational and/or technology management roles.
