Gary Miles' Summary

Gary Miles helps technology-based start-ups and growth ventures identify and realize new business opportunities. In his roles as a management consultant, venture capital manager and operating executive, Gary has successfully driven growth through raising equity funding, developing innovative business plan strategies and guiding senior managers in the process of revitalize existing businesses and building new ones.

Gary is a global business leader with special expertise guiding Israel-based start-ups, accelerating growth of US-based B2B ventures and advising leading international corporations on innovation and new business opportunity management. He combines a broad range of business experience with a deep technology background. Gary is recognized for having strong analytical and communication skills, with an exceptional creativity and passion for identifying and realizing new business, market and technology opportunities.

Gary is an experienced Corporate Director and executive advisor, having served on the Boards of private corporations in the FinTech, mobile, IT and Cleantech sectors. Gary is an invited speaker at industry conferences and events and he has taught MBA and technology courses at the Hebrew University in Jerusalem, Tel Aviv University and Santa Clara University in Silicon Valley. Gary earned his PhD in Computer Science from University of Southern California.


Specialized expertise:
• Helping start-ups develop business plans, raise equity capital
• Identifying, evaluating and managing private and VC venture investments
• Corporate Director and advisor to executives in early stage private ventures
• Accelerating growth through organic product development, new markets and partnerships
• FinTech, Mobile, IT, Software, Cleantech and Life Science sectors.
