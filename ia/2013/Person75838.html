Kim Pichanick's Summary

Professional Profile

Ms. Pichanick is a thought leader who works with senior level management and leadership teams to develop integrated communications strategies that insure consistent messaging (internally and externally) to stakeholders, employees and the media. The experience gained from her tenure with Aleris International, Inc. (Aleris), a global recycler and manufacturer of aluminum, while in hyper growth, and subsequent leadership role in the high-end business advisory sector have enriched her with insights and perspectives valuable to a wide range of clients and companies.

Ms. Pichanick served as Communications and Investor Relations Specialist for Aleris from December 2004 to November 2007. Ms. Pichanick worked with Aleris’s senior management developing U.S. and global communications initiatives and strategies. In 24 months, Aleris grew from 2,100 employees to 8,600 employees and increased annual revenue from $2.4 billion to $8.6 billion. Aleris’s global acquisition strategy resulted in the completion of seven large acquisitions during this period. Aleris’s stock publically traded on the NYSE was acquired by a private equity firm, in December 2006. Ms. Pichanick worked with Aleris’s senior management team transitioning to a private company in the investor relations, media relations, marketing, and employee communications areas performing key functions.

In December 2007, Ms. Pichanick formed KPN Advisors, LLC, a high-end business advisory firm. As KPN’s Managing Director, Ms. Pichanick has worked extensively with owners, senior managers, and professionals in a variety of industries and business verticals.

Ms. Pichanick has developed professional teams to execute KPN’s foundational business premise of applying “State of the Art” technology tools and solutions to solve traditional business problems and has a keen understanding regarding how professional disciplines fit together for client value.

Ms. Pichanick served as Communications and Investor Relations Specialist for Aleris International from December 2004 to November 2007.
