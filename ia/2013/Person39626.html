Ed Marsh's Summary

A creative and technical content professional whose goals are making a difference and having fun doing it.

In each of my roles, planning and creating content was integral to successful projects. I bring a data-driven, Web development and design approach to content.

I am a do-er, not a manager. I like lists.

I have worked in multiple- and single-writer environments. I am a well-rounded communicator, with experience in content management, technical support, graphics editing and creation, template design, eLearning, and usability.

I started coding HTML by hand in 1995, and CSS in '96. Bare-metal experience with DITA. Founding member of the Drupal content management system (CMS) community northern NJ chapter. I assisted in creating training documentation and at the NYC Drupal newbie camp.

Award-winning newsletter editor for the NYC Metro chapter of the Society for Technical Communication (STC), since January 2010. Find out more at stcnymetro.org.

Software knowledge includes Adobe Creative Suite, Captivate, FrameMaker, RoboHelp, XMetaL, XML Spy, Drupal open-source Content Management System (CMS), WordPress, and MadCap Flare.

Specialties: HTML, CSS, XML, DITA, eLearning, Photoshop, FrameMaker, Illustrator, Captivate, DreamWeaver, InDesign, content management, help authoring, technical writing, and some Drupal theming.
