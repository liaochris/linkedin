James Caan's Summary

I founded the Mayfair based private equity firm Hamilton Bradshaw in 2004 and joined the panel of heavyweight investors for Dragons' Den in 2007. In January 2011 I chose to leave the show to focus on my charity work, current investments and growing Hamilton Bradshaw.

My passion is building businesses. I have been building and selling businesses since 1985 and I think it is important to invest in people rather than just products. I'm a great believer that it is people who create a successful business through their passion and conviction.

I work with entrepreneurs through my private equity firm Hamilton Bradshaw and the advisory growth division, HB Prime Advantage. I enjoy joining them on their journey to success and seeing them fulfill their potential.If you are looking to grow your business please visit their website www.hbprimeadvantage.com. Alternatively, if you have a business proposal, please visit the entrepreneurs section on the James Caan website.

Unfortunately I don't always have the time to respond to all the requests and messages I am sent. However, if you are looking for comprehensive guide to securing a job, check out Get The Job You Really Want. Or if you are a business owner looking for advice you can download the James Caan Business Secrets app for iPhone at this link: http://itunes.apple.com/app/id430824440?mt=8

Specialties: Business start-ups, investment, acquisitions, buy-outs, SMEs, private equity, portfolio management, recruitment, jobs, careers

    • James Caan
