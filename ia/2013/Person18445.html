Bryan Coe's Summary

Bryan Coe is the Founder of Blackbird e-Solutions. He has worked internationally as well as for foreign companies in the USA. His prior professional experience includes positions at Google, 1&1 Internet, Black Box Network Services – Germany and Band Digital Inc.

Bryan has been involved in Social Media since before Social Media was a buzz word. His first blog was in 1999 when he was a university student in Germany. While in Germany he refined his fluency in German and later he was naturally drawn to SEO and the Social Media industry.

In 2005 Bryan founded Blackbird e-Solutions combining his passion for the internet technology and linguistics. The company specializes in Localization, SEO, Social Media, Paid Advertising (PPC) and the tools behind the scenes that track success. He has received great reviews from clients for his ability to guide them through the process of developing an internet presence.

Bryan has been recognized for his expertise in the fields of Online Marketing, Localization, SEO and Social Media and has been asked to speak at organizations such as the Lancaster Chamber of Commerce, SCORE, Affiliated Staffing Group and the Knowledge Management Group of Philadelphia.

As a leader and an organizer outside the office, he heads a German group called Netzwerk Central PA and is assistant organizer of the sister group Netzwerk Philadelphia. He is also heavily involved in the soccer community in Central PA, where he runs the soccer website: CentralPASoccer.com.

Specialties: German, social media, web design, website localization, internet, internet trends, technology trends, SEO, Search engine optimization, wiki tools, erp software, translation, SEM, search engine marketing, pay per click, ppc, German to English translation
