Dale Denney's Summary

PHOTOS: http://bearpawoutfitters.com/photos.html
VIDEOS: http://bearpawoutfitters.com/videos.html
TESTIMONIALS: http://BearpawOutfitters.com/testimonials.html

My wife Tara and I have been professional outfitters and guides since 1977 and are both equally involved in our full-time hunting business. We hunt areas in Idaho, Montana, Utah, and Washington so that we can provide our hunters with a higher quality hunt for a large variety of game than if we hunted only one area. Bearpaw Outfitters offer "Highly Successful" hunting trips for Antelope, Bear, Buffalo, Bull Elk, Cougar, Cow Elk, Coyote, Fishing, Moose, Mule Deer, Predators, Sheep, Turkey, Upland Birds, Waterfowl, and Whitetail Deer.

Tara and I really enjoy visiting with and getting to know our hunters, most leave as friends and many have been hunting with us for 5 to 20 years or more. Our guiding options include your choice of a personalized guided hunt, semi-guided hunt, or an unguided hunting trip if you are more of a do-it-yourself hunter. Depending on the hunt, your accommodation choices may include the hunting lodge, hosted tent camp, drop camp, or the motel.

We lease more than 100,000 acres of top quality private hunting lands and we also hunt many of the best public land areas. We’ve had hunters from throughout the United States, Canada, and many countries around the world. TV News and TV Hunting Shows have featured "Bearpaw Outfitters" on numerous occasions.

OUR GOALS ARE SIMPLE
Continue to improve the quality of our hunts and always provide the best hunt possible to every one of our hunters.

Specialties

Personalized Hunting Trips, guiding, outfitting, guided hunts, unguided hunts, hunting lodge, hunting camps, drop camps, hunting advice, deer hunting, elk hunting, bear hunting, antelope hunting, buffalo hunting, cougar hunting, turkey hunting, moose hunting, sheep hunting, coyote hunting, predator hunting, upland bird hunting, waterfowl hunting, trout fishing, walleye fishing, recreational trips, outdoor recreation, consulting, and can assist with travel arrangements for the outdoor person.
