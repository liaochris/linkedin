John Peek's Summary

Peek Brothers Painting Contractors specializes in both the interior and exterior re-painting of existing homes, churches, historic and commercial buildings. Occupied spaces are not a problem as we go to great lenghts to protect surfaces we are not painting such as your floors and furniture!

People often ask, "Do you do both interior and exterior, cabinets and natural wood decks?". Yes we sure do!...and we do it with the proper products that will be more beautiful and durable than you can imagine.

As part of our services, we do most all repairs so you do not have to hire multiple contractors to get the project done. We do our own stucco repair, most wood replacement, rot repair, drywall repair and install new baseboards for you. We are the one stop to get it done properly and beautifully!
