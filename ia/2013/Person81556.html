Lisa Jackson's Summary

20 years' successful track record helping organizations drive stronger performance, productivity, and innovation through culture alignment, in the face of increasingly complex and rapid global change.

Author of 2 books "Transforming Corporate Culture: 9 Natural Truths for Being Fit to Compete" and "Culture Builder Toolkit - A Guidebook for Measuring and Aligning Culture to Strategy."

Culture change expertise includes:
--In-depth culture assessment and alignment of culture to strategy
--Fostering clarity and alignment during a change in strategy
--Addressing root cause barriers to strategic implementation
--Culture training to build change champions and collaborative work teams
--Simplifying and improving decision making,
--Culture merging during an acquisition - what to change, what to keep the same.

Specialties:
Professional keynotes on corporate culture
Cultural assessment
Training, coaching, and consulting for:
*Executive Team buy-in to culture
*Strategic and business alignment, through culture
*Collaboration across global workplaces
*Facilitation and presentation training and coaching
