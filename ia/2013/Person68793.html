Joseph Szymczak's Summary

JOSEPH SZYMCZAK EXQUISITE SOURCING + DESIGN is a hybrid design and supply company serving the evolving global market for interior design and FF&E projects sourcing needs. By combining a spectrum of professional ID services of a large organization with the leaness and agility of a boutique we have responded to the dynamic (r)evolution of the designer and smart buyer relationship. We make your sourcing and presentation job easier by providing cost effective solutions with superior ROI.

Every budgeted design dollar counts more than ever. Through strategic alliances with a cultivated portfolio of residential and contract furnishing producers and an international network of skilled artisans and workshops, we deliver lower cost procurement or turn-key services than the competition. Lean flexibility and on-line support guarantees we will respond quickly with proposals for each prospective project or inquirey.

I am the President of JSES+D LLC and have successfully managed large contract ID projects throughout the Arabian Gulf since 1998. My design practice began earlier in Grand Rapids, Michigan - known as the furniture capital of the USA. I know the business end of ID project management by way of working with “Rush Projects Divisions” of high profile Arabian Gulf consulting and development companies. With this unique experience I am positioned to effectively approach both sides of the design equation. You get affordable world class ID support and access to my global partners to get what you need where and when you need it.

Our success is built on valued relationships. JSES+D is always looking to expand it’s creative network. Chicago, Illinois is home base with supporting offices in Milan, Italy and Dubai UAE to serve our international clientele. Our growing family of partner companies include such premium brands as Clive Christians, Christopher Guy, Bolier & Company, Dorya, Baccarat, Paramount Nepal Weavers, and many others.

Specialties

FF & E residential and hospitality interiors, procurement, sourcing and design
