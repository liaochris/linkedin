Dan Bucko's Summary

Dan is currently the Associate Creative Director for Emerging Business at Petco where he works with Unleashed, Pooch Hotel and Petco Foundation on creative projects and campaigns.

Prior to Petco, Dan worked as the Creative Director for Parallel 6 - a fast growing Mobile Technology, Social Media and Creative Agency. Dan helped establish & manage the creative department, process, sales, and strategy. He worked with clients & staff to establish a creative vision and then executes on all creative campaigns.

Prior to Parallel 6, Dan was Senior Art Director at Geary Interactive in San Diego. Dan was in charge of strategy, integration, project management, design and multimedia for numerous high-profile clients including Trump Las Vegas, Peer1, WD-40 Company and brands, Webster Bank, BumbleBee Tuna, Island's Restaurants, Kyocera and M&T Bank.

Prior to Geary Dan was directing integration interaction at Modern Solution in Carlsbad, an agency tasked with developing new business solution strategies for Modern Postcard & The Iris Group. Using a mix of web, technology, multimedia and creative design strategies, Bucko has helped create revolutionary marketing programs in the vanity medicine industries. He has developed a “spiral marketing engine” using multi-channel, permission marketing philosophies. The engine intelligently tracks lead interaction and analyzes and organizes data into lifestyle segments that facilitate rich reporting and educates clients on key demographic and psychographic profiles.

Prior to Modern Solution, Bucko has won a Webby Award as the Modern Postcard Web Manager and was Principal/Creative Director at his own firm PurMedia, developing creative & technology solutions for Schwinn, Mongoose, Pacific Cycle, Huffy, Avexus, & Journey’s.

Dan has a BA from UC Santa Barbara, is a DMA member and has taught Web Design at UCSB Extension.

Specialties: Design, web, multimedia, animation, brand, creative and image executive with global experience.
