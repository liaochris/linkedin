Justin Renninger's Summary

I'm an interaction/product designer with a love for typography, simple and usable interfaces, and beautiful design and illustration. My skill-set allows me to be deeply involved in a product's lifecycle, from inception through concept to the end product. I focus on working in an iterative design process to craft products that are based on cycles of user feedback.

Justin Renninger's Publications

  • The New Big Book of Layouts

    • HarperCollins
    • October 19, 2010
    Authors: Justin Renninger

    An exciting collection of the latest and most visually engaging layout designs and design ideas for amateur and professional graphic designers.

    Organized to encourage creativity, serendipitous discovery, and inspiration, The New Big Book of Layouts includes techniques that can be used to enhance any layout design--be it in print or online. It provides insight into the elements that make layouts effective, and covers a range of styles--from traditional to cutting edge--selected to help designers and design students make their work more creative, more readable, and more visually engaging for an audience. With more than 750 outstanding layouts featured in a robust visual gallery with detailed descriptive information and design analyses, this book provides a thorough look at what goes into today’s most effective layout designs and how designers and design students can improve their work.
