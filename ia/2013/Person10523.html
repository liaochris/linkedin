Piotr Pawałowski's Projects

  • HIPERMED (Celtic)

    • July 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Piotr Pawałowski, Gabriel Dosso, Flaminio Minerva, Jordi Garriga

    HIPERMED will design an open telemedicine platform based on a unified Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) providing media services, SIP-based control plane services and network services over the Internet Protocol (IP).

  • EXPERIMEDIA

    • September 2012 to November 2013
    Team Members: Piotr Pawałowski

    EXPERIMEDIA will develop and operate a unique facility that offers researchers what they need for large-scale Future Media Internet experiments. Experiments will be conducted at testbeds offering live events and real-world communities to accelerate the adoption of the Future Media Internet.

  • TEFIS

    • April 2012 to April 2013
    Team Members: Piotr Pawałowski

    TEFIS will support Future Internet of Services Research by offering a single access point to different testing and experimental facilities for communities of software and business developers to test, experiment, and collaboratively elaborate knowledge.
