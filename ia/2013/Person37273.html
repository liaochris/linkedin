Douglas Reiser's Summary

Co-Founder and COO of ClaimKit LLC, an industry-leading data consulting firm for sureties and claim professionals. ClaimKit launched it's innovative claim management software, DocKit, in 2013. Doug is also the founder and principal of Reiser Legal PLLC, a law practice focusing on alcoholic beverage law.

Doug is also a published author on green building (twice by Thomson Reuters) and the founding author of widely read legal blogs, BuildersCounsel.com, BreweryLaw.com and The Electronic Claim (claimkit.com/blog).

Doug is a member of the Louisiana and Washington Bar Associations, a LEED AP, and a former Chair of the WSBA YLD CLE Committee. He formerly served as part owner of a bi-coastal law practice and director and general counsel to ZLien.com. He is an award-winning beer brewer, avid snowboarder, BBQ chef, and Cleveland sports fan (sadly).
