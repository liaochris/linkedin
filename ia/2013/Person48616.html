Gill Arnold's Summary

I am a digital recruiter with over sixteen years experience of growing and managing successful and profitable recruitment businesses, from my time as a manager of Recruit Media’s digital business to my current role as a Director of Futureheads Recruitment Ltd. My practice is based on delivering a highly professional, ethical service to our customers and I am fortunate enough to have worked with most of my core clients and candidates for many years.

Specialties

Freelance, contract, permanent recruitment of digital specialists including user experience professionals, digital content managers, project managers, producers, account managers, creatives, & front end developers. Individual staff, freelancers, temps, contractors and project teams. Consultancy, requirements analysis, proposal & bid writing, budgeting, project management.

Gill Arnold's Experience

Managing Director

Futureheads Recruitment Ltd

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

September 2009 – Present (4 years) London, United Kingdom

Futureheads is an exciting digital recruitment business launched in September 2009 by four highly experienced and successful digital experts. Respected as passionate advocates of a professional, knowledgeable and no-nonsense brand of recruitment, we forge long-term partnerships with our clients, understanding their business drivers, helping them define their requirements and ultimately offering them the best candidates to suit their businesses. We bring strong ethics to our relationships and business transactions and invest time and energy in helping our candidates exceed their career expectations, nurturing alliances that continue for many years. We offer a sound knowledge of the digital industry, a vast network of contacts and an impressive track record in sourcing and securing the best talent in user experience, project management and creative & front end technology.

Manager of Digital Creative and Business Management

Aquent

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

May 2007 – August 2009 (2 years 4 months)

Aquent's acquisition of Recruit Media in 2007 brought me to a larger, international organisation where I continued to manage a team of passionate and highly successful digital recruiters, with P&L responsibility. Employing deep expertise across digital creative and front end development, project and account management and user experience, we worked in partnership with our clients across a number of sectors from top 100 digital agencies through publishing, telecoms and corporates to public sector organisations. As well as training and developing my team and ensuring the quality of our delivery and profitability of our business I continued to recruit temporary and permanent digital experts for my clients, predominantly in the not for profit and public sectors.

I enjoyed a long and rewarding career with this boutique recruitment business, initially joining as a resourcer during the early days of "multimedia" technology and ultimately managing a 12-strong team of digital recruiters across creative & tech, project management and user experience roles for public and private sector organisations.
