J. Michael Godfrey, DMin, PhD, PCC's Summary

I help clergy and business professionals be more, see more, and achieve more in:

• Making significant changes
• Improving relationships
• Becoming better leaders
• Making bigger and better decisions
• Reducing stress
• Transitioning to a career they really want and love
• Getting ahead professionally
• Setting bigger, better, and more rewarding goals
• Reaching goals faster
• Dealing better with
-The complexities of life
-The influence of technology
-Uncertainty
• Simplifying their lives

I have extensive experience in conducting highly-rated, award-winning mentoring, coaching, and/or training experiences for more than 1000 individuals during the past 9 years.

As a coach, I am credentialed by the International Coach Federation with more than 1000 hours of coaching experience. After working with me, my clients say they are more confident, more successful, and more skillful in their lives and work than ever before. They are clearer about what they want to do with their lives and achieve a healthy work/life balance.  They understand themselves and others better; they manage their own lives better; and they have more satisfying, meaningful relationships with others. In addition, their business thrive and they make more money.

I am extensively trained and experienced in public presentations and person-to-person interactions. Known as a uniquely gifted developer of potential in individuals and organizations. Consultant in conflict management, Senior Consultant for the Birkman method, team building, and organizational behavior & development.

Specialties: mentoring, coaching, coaching for clergy, consulting for clergy and congregations; coaching for business professionals; life coaching; Birkman method consulting, consulting for team building, and organizational behavior and development. public and interpersonal communication.
