Kyryl Bystriakov's Projects

  • Wallpaper Wizard

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Kyryl Bystriakov, Ivan Ablamskyi, Petro Bondarevskyi, Julia Petrus

    Wallpaper Wizard is now close to 200,000 customers, reached Mac App Store's USA "Top 10 paid applications" multiple times.

    Wallpaper Wizard is a wallpaper changer for Mac available from the Mac App Store. It was shaped and designed with the "blue ocean strategy" in mind - to become the "one and only" wallpaper utility for Mac. Available soon after the Store was launched, Wallpaper Wizard became "de facto" wallpaper changer beloved my many - comments and reviews were written by customers ranging from 14 years old to 84(!). My responsibilities were - user experience, prototyping, user interface layout, design supervision. Initially I participated actively in marketing from the product point of view, implemented few Mac App Store specific promotion techniques and supervised all product-related processes.

    Currently, I do not have any affiliation with the project.

  • Focus

    • June 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Kyryl Bystriakov

    Focus climbed into Mac App Store's USA Top 10 paid applications during it's launch and appeared there multiple times (reaching 3rd spot at some point), was featured by Apple in "New and Noteworthy" software, appeared on the Mac App Store banner and the company also received a few offers to sell the project.

    Focus is a simple app for Mac which allows you to add lens blur effects to a photo with a single touch. As a product manager, I proposed it's current name, shaped it as a need-based, streamlined application with the minimal set of features, transparent usage hints, sharing mechanism and etc.

    Currently, I do not have any affiliation with the project.

  • CleanMyPC

    • December 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Kyryl Bystriakov, Mykola Lando, Petro Bondarevskyi, Oleksandr Kosovan

    CleanMyPC was featured in numerous online and printed magazines, including Microsoft-supported "The Official Windows Magazine", received multiple awards for it's design and streamlined user interface.

    CleanMyPC is a Windows version of the world-famous CleanMyMac by MacPaw. I am still involved in the project as a product manager, my responsibilities include everything related to the product, including but not limited to - user experience, usability, design and development supervision, decision on product changes and strategic directions, conversion improvement and split testing, upsells and even product-related copywriting.

    Currently I am also involved with CleanMyMac development.
