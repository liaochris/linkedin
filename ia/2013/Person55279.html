I belonged to a NASCAR support Group SINCH (Staten Island NASCAR Hopefuls) we were a support committe dedicated to helping NASCAR and ISC build a track on Staten Island several yeras ago. We did everything we could to get the word out about the benefits of having a NASCAR track in Staten Island NY, we held many functions, fundraisers, and spread awareness about the positives of NASCAR. Unfortunately, things were put on hold but NASCAR didnt forget our efforts and invited us to all the major events held in NYC such as Media week and Champions week every year.. which has allowed me to initially meet and get to know some of the top people and drivers in the sport. I knew for a long time that I really wanted to be involved in this sport, so I didnt miss an opportunity to meet and greet with the elites... the invitations extended to different tracks where I met more and more drivers, crews, teams, and key icons in the industry. Thankfully for facebook I have continued to network and meet great people involved in the sport and it has helped me make the move from Staten Island NY to Race City USA and I am so glad to FINALLY be here. What lies next for me.. I have no idea but I am here and I am ready for whatever oppertunity comes my way. I have met so many great ppl and I know how lucky I am for it and I am very grateful that I have been so fortunate. I am hoping it all pays off and I intend to do everything I can to make my NASCAR dreams come true. I am so happy to be known and well liked by the people I admire most.
