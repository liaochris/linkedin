Matt Forman's Summary

Over the last 10 years I have been working in the information technology field. My primary focus is the support of small business and local government, in regards to their networks, infrastructure, and technology driven business practices.

Since September of 2006 I have owned and managed The Local Circuit in Denton, TX. We are a growing business that provides computer, I.T. and document scanning services. From simple break fix repairs to full network management and file cabinet conversions to full document management projects we can cover it all. Since taking over the business, our gross sales have increased about 150%. I have a passion for local business development and enjoy working closely with those around me to make that happen.

Outside of the business world, for the last couple of years, I have worked alongside friends and supporters to provide fresh water in third world countries around the world. My partner in this endeavor, and I have spent time in places like Haiti, Kenya, and Malawi to raise money and install fresh water well systems and purification units.

Specialties: Business Management, Project Management, Information Technology. Microsoft Certifications, CompTIA A+ and CompTIA N+ Certifications
