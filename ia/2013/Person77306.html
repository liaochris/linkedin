Krystina Nguyen's Summary

A firm believer that business practices can be applied to the public good, my professional experiences have lied in economic and international development domestically and abroad. I have acquired a strong skill set in communication amongst diverse cultures through academic and professional stints in Europe and Africa respectively. My articles, interviews, and press releases have been featured in The Orange County Register, Good Generation, StoryCorps, Do It While You're Young, Blog Expat, and the University of Southern California News.

My interests lie in microfinance (domestic and international), economic development, international development, nonprofit management, project management, consulting, writing, and philanthropy.
