Anthony Mongeluzo's Summary

I am the President and CEO of Pro Computer Service LLC and pilotLight studios. Every Friday at 6:15 you can see me on Fox 29 with my Tech Talk Segment

Pro Computer Service is a nationally award winning IT consulting and service firm that offers our clients a true fixed monthly fee for IT support. We have offices in NJ,PA,DE, and MD. Our clients include industries of all types and beyond the normal help desk type service we are experts in Virtualization, Clustering, SAN Arrays, system redundancy, Linux, Unix, and have a key niche in the manufacturing industry with Progress, QAD, and MFG Pro. We also provide telecommunications consulting to help to reduce costs or in many cases recovery money through an audit of your current phone bills.

pilotLight studios develops custom applications to support companies who really utilize the web or need things created that currently don't exist. We are experts in the Django framework.

Great clients for pilotLight are companies who:
depend heavily on the web, have needs that go beyond off-the-shelf software solutions or have a unique idea for something that hasn't been done before. We also do custom integration with existing software platforms (MAS500, Microsoft CRM, Sales Force).
