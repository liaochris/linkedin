Chris Reighley's Summary

“It’s kind of fun to do the impossible.”
- Walt Disney

And as recently as 1995, it was impossible to imagine the kind of global marketplace that exists today. But I’ve been intrigued by the possibilities from the very beginning and my career has mirrored the emergence and explosive growth of eCommerce.

When it was in its infancy, I used my experience in traditional business and marketing as a foundation, and successfully tested the eCommerce waters by building my own internet-based business and eventually growing it to annual sales of three-quarters of a million dollars. And as the industry really began to expand, I adjusted my horizons and stepped out initially as a consultant, then as a Director of eCommerce, working to define the realities of doing business on a global level.

In fact, what’s difficult today is trying to imagine life without the global marketplace made possible by eCommerce and now I’m looking ahead to tomorrow’s impossible-to-imagine scenarios. I recently began working toward my PhD in eCommerce and am eager for the opportunity to utilize my business background and my ability to imagine the possibilities to develop solutions to more and bigger eCommerce challenges.
