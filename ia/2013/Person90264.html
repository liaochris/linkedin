Michael (Mike) Blewitt's Summary

Creative but pragmatic Business, Human Resource (HR) and Learning/OD leader with experience in a variety of industries, including: Hospitality (Hotels and Food Service), Real Estate, Private Equity, Investment Banking and High Technology. I help companies build strong cultures (important since this is what guides employees actions when no one is looking) and improved leadership and management bench strength. I focus company HR processes and practices to help companies become the preferred employer in their industry, build increased employee commitment, while being certain employment related costs are competitive and employees receive high value from the HR function and programs.
