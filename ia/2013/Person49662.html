Greg Quilop's Summary

As a Senior Software Engineer, I have the ability and drive to create solutions. Working within an agile development cycle allows me to be part of a dynamic team and best solve problems of user stories to help achieve the goals for the company.

Coming up through customer service into a development role has given me insight into other departments and appreciation for the work that others do. I have written technical and functional specifications and consulted with business analysts to write business specifications.

For example, faced with a business need to create an auction system, the task was to create one similar to E-Bay but within a very fast timeline. I suggested that instead of a very complex system that would require a lot of development and QA resources a more simple Silent Auction approach would achieve the same goals that would fit the given time frame. I played a very active role creating the requirements, developing, and testing and this system is the most active system currently in use.
