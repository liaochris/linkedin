Marilyn Oliva, President / Marketing Strategist's Summary

I am the founder and President of MPower Marketing Strategy and a specialist in marketing strategy planning, with a focus on online marketing solutions. I spent more than a decade in senior marketing management positions for large international corporations in the New Jersey and Florida markets prior to launching MPower.
My broad marketing experience working for billion dollar companies such as Citrix Systems, American Express, AT&T, and Research International helped me to understand the enormous need for a more customer focused and strategic approach to marketing. This knowledge, coupled with my natural creative skills, led me to develop an empowering approach that provides a combination of marketing assessment, research insight, strategic marketing planning, and marketing coaching appropriate for all types of organizations, regardless of size or budget.
I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and a Master of Business Administration degree, both from Rutgers University. I also hold an Associate of Arts degree from the New York School of Interior Design.
