Daniel Mease's Summary

My name is Daniel Mease. I am a New York based Designer, Artist and Photographer who specializes in vivid visualizations, compelling designs and stunning photography. I have worked with a variety of clients ranging from high-end retail – including: Tiffany & Co., Louis Vuitton, and Harry Winston – to large developers – including: Blackstone and Equity – to several smaller clients – including: Fieldlines Architecture, Seed Design, St. Paul Lutheran Church & School, and the Theater of the Oppressed NYC. Drawing on extensive skills in Adobe and Autodesk products, I am instrumental to design development and visual storytelling. My academic foundation was built at Kansas State University where I studied Interior Architecture & Product Design.
