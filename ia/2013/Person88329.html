Jeffrey Howard, Business Consultant & Speaker's Summary

Jeffrey Howard is business coach and consultant focused on working with other coaches, consultants and visionary entrepreneurs. Building his core brand as the MasterMind Mentor International, Jeff specializes in launching visionary entrepreneurs into their ideal expert platform, laser-focusing on their key client or customer, and developing product lines, followed by strategic product and event launches.
