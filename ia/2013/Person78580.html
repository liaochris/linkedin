Tim Larson's Summary

I am an accomplished professional developer that excels at analyzing business problems and designing and delivering technical solutions to solve them. I am skilled at training others and can clearly communicate technical matters with people not well versed in technology. Over the past 15 years, I have worked in both Microsoft and Open Source development environments, working with PHP and .Net using both MySQL and SQL Server, but lately I have focused on development in .Net.

I specialize in integrating disparate systems through web services and APIs, and I am skilled at diving deep to resolve bugs that are tricky to diagnose.

I am particularly fond of using TDD and IoC, and I am working to better familiarize myself with various design patterns to improve the quality of my code.

Specialties

ASP.Net, Azure, IIS, Linux, Microsoft SQL Server and Stored Procedures, Sharepoint Development and Administration, jQuery, AJAX, Dynamics NAV (C/AL, C/SIDE), TFS, Subversion, Git

I am also familiar with ASP.Net MVC, PHP, Perl, MySQL, and Android development, but I do not have much recent experience in these areas.
