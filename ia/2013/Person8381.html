Anne Piedmont's Experience

President

Piedmont Research Associates

August 2010 – Present (3 years 5 months)

After almost 19 years as Research Director at the Roanoke Regional Partnership, I have gone out on my own. I can provide community research and statistics, competitive business intelligence, and writing services. More to come!

Director of Research

Roanoke Regional Partnership

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Civic & Social Organization industry

December 1991 – August 2010 (18 years 9 months)

Hard to believe I did this job for so long. It was a great ride. I watched it change from file cabinets and fax machines to the Internet. I provided the research and statistics that help tell the Roanoke Region's story. I answered questions from companies and consultants looking to make a decision and include customized research in proposals and presentations. Roanoke Region is a great place to sell to the rest of the world.

Account Executive

John Lambert Associates

March 1989 – November 1990 (1 year 9 months)

Managed accounts in the Roanoke, VA area, including the Roanoke Regional Airport, Roanoke Gas Company and Total Action Against Poverty. My duties included writing news releases and brochures, and arranging news conferences and special events. One of the highlights of my time at JLA was working on the opening of the new terminal at Roanoke Regional Airport.

Editor of Current

American Electric Power

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; AEP; Utilities industry

1984 – 1988 (4 years)

I wrote and laid out the monthly employee magazine, Current, I did on-air segments for the video magazine, as well. I also was a member of the the emergency response team for the Donald C. Cook Nuclear Plant and participated in several drills.

Reporter

Danville Register

1981 – 1984 (3 years)

I started as a reporter covering healthcare, the arts and education. Then I became what would have been known as "the society editor," with responsibility for the feature section. This involved layout and feature writing -- two skills that have served me well the rest of my career. I also can still write a heck of an obituary. This was a great job with lots of interesting people.
