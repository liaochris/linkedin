Janet M. Taylor's Summary

SBA 8a Certified firmTotally Organized, LLC was started by Janet Taylor in 1994 with her experience in Management, Marketing and Administration. As the Chief Executive Officer and Lead Consultant the firm’s clients include Les Brown, The City of Philadelphia, and Hewlett Packard. Janet is also a regular workshop presenter for IKEA, the Free Library of Philadelphia and has been a presenter for the Philadelphia Federation of Teacher’s Annual Conferences since 1999.

Janet's tips have been featured on HGTV's Mission: Organization and KYW1060. She publishes a monthly newsletter and host a weekly radio show.

Specialties: Administrative Support,Office Organizing, Project Management, Trainings, Home Organizing.
