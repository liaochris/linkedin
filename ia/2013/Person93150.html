November 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months) Toronto

I joined PortfolioAid in 2010, inspired by the company's mission to improve the accountability and resilience of Canada's retail brokerage industry. I've been tasked with transforming processes at this software start-up in pursuit of selling to Canada’s federally regulated entities.

I've outlined this successful strategic initiative in the presentation titled "Fit for Service" below. Our processes are audited annually under Section 5025 Type II using the "trust service principles" of availability, confidentiality, privacy, processing integrity, and security.
