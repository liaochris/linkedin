Faye Oney's Summary

Customer communication is the new marketing. I believe that social media can play an important role in the marketing mix. If our customers are in the social networks, we should be there too. By communicating with our clients and prospective clients in the social space, we can address their concerns, educate them about our industry, provide exceptional customer service, and add value to our brand.

The value I bring to my company:

- Passion for learning. Learning shouldn't end once you earn your degree. I feel it's important to learn something new every day about the industry you work in - especially those industries affected by constantly changing technology.

- Willingness to accept responsibility above and beyond the scope of my job. When I am given responsibilities other than what I was originally hired to do, I welcome them as new learning opportunities; and look forward to the challenges they bring.
