kathleen bonham's Summary

Realtor specializing in Vintage Home properties in Sonoma County, the Northern California wine country. Skilled and Experienced in Vintage home renovations and certified as an ecogreen realtor. Expertise - working with clients to maximize their profits by taking the time to present each property at it's optimal best. How is this done?...Through the Art of Placement, less is more....thus highlighting the inherent quality and character of a home. This is my stock in trade. There is a cognitive dissonance when the decor of the house conflicts with its provenance or architectural character. This applies to all houses...from the simple to grand! That is why the "routine staging" that one frequently encounters is not only ineffective but can in fact detract from the homes appeal. Each home is unique and I have spent years learning to assess the traits that give a residence its distinctive character.
My many tools include professional photography, room by room organization and elimination, viral marketing and advertising all contribute toward a successful conclusion.

Specialties

Vintage Home Specialist, Styling and Designing to Sell, Green-Eco Realtor.
