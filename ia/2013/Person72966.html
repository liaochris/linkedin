Kathy Gottberg's Summary

I have been writing real estate related information since 1982. In 1989 I began writing newsletters for California Escrow Offices to be used as promotional materials for their clients (mainly real estate agents). I use my own background in real estate, along with my research to write the monthly newsletters. I work with companies throughout California and have clients that have used my services for over 15 years.

I am the author of three published books.

Three years ago I co-founded and created a website to serve as a resource in the Coachella Valley of Southern California for all things green and sustainable. I have used my writing, research and promotional skills to help the people of my local region learn and utilize all products and services presently available.

My latest adventure is creating a website blog (setting up the website entirely by myself) and filling it with ideas, tips, suggestions and personal experiences about how to live a SMART (sustainable, meaningful, artful, responsible & thankful) life 365--or every day of the year!

Specialties: writing, research, promotion, organization, motivation, real estate and escrow, green and sustainable
