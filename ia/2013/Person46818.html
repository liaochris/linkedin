Greg Verm's Summary

Our goal is to provide the Finest Fishing Charters on Galveston Bay and Gulf of Mexico, meeting the needs of everyone from a half day fishing adventure for a family of three to catering a corporate event entertaining employees, customers, and vendors with a multiple boat charter. Shallow water back bay flounder gigging to blue water offshore fishing for giant Red snapper and everything in between - we will go above and beyond expectations. From leaving your child with a BIG smile on their face from reeling their big catch to making sure your valuable customers have the trip of a lifetime - Fishing Galveston TX Charters is the Finest Fishing Charters on the Texas Gulf Coast.

Greg Verm's Experience

Owner/Captain

Fishing Galveston TX

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Sports industry

March 1990 – Present (23 years 10 months) Galveston Bay and Gulf of Mexico

My experience began at a very young age roaming the bays and bayous of Galveston with my Dad and Grandfather. At the age of 16, I purchased my first boat and began learning new areas and techniques to expand my knowledge of the Galveston Bay system. I love being on the water, and sharing my knowledge of the great outdoors with others. I especially enjoy when kids realize the fun and serenity of the sport of fishing. I help this come to light. I have great patience and make sure kids get hooked on fishing.
