Dr. Daniele Trevisani's Summary

Dr. Daniele Trevisani achieved the US Government Award "Fulbright Scholarship" as distinguished European independent researcher in Communication Studies and Human Factor, and obtained a Master of Arts in Mass Communication (with Distinction) at the University of Florida (USA).
He currently works as Freelance Trainer and Coach, Writer, Researcher, serving Individual Clients, Organizations, Leaders, and Business Firms. As Trainer, he worked for over 200 Companies and Organizazionts including United Nations and NATO. He trained also 6 World Champions in Kickboxing, Boxing and Martial Arts, as one of the main world experts in Mental Training for Mixed Martial Arts. His activity includes the generation of new models for training and coaching. Writer of 8 books in psychology, communication, human potential and HR, he combines a strong academic background with practical business experience and sports experience.
Beyond the MA, his Study Curriculum includes:
- Laurea Degree (BA+Master) in DAMS - Performing Arts and Communication Science (University of Bologna, Italy), graduation with honors
- Professional Master in International Marketing (IFOA Institute, Italy)
- Training in Intercultural Communication (American University of Washington DC, USA)
- Training in Drama and Theatre (University of Hull, UK)
- Specialization in Psychology and Psychometrics (University of Padua, Italy).
His current activities range between:
(1) managing advanced training and coaching programs
(2) research on active training techniques, with focus on cross-disciplinary integration,
(3) coaching and consulting in the fields of personal and organizational development
(4) research on Human Factor and Performance
