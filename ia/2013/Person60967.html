Jeff Janowski's Summary

The media business was once a very specialized portal to disseminate information. Now, with the popularity and the high speed infusion of the internet among society, the media business model has become challenged to the point that it must change to keep up with the pace that the internet has set with twitter, facebook, craigslist and other sites that have helped evolve information distribution.

I am fascinated and excited to be part of this great time in our life to help mold and be part of the next generation of information journalists that have an even greater array of tools to help broadcast the full story.

Specialties: Digital photography, final cut, adobe creative suites, multimedia editing and producing.
