J Genovese's Summary

Joseph Genovese, P.K.A Genovese is a native of Yonkers, NY. In High School he started writing music, uncovering a talent that would make history. In the mid 90s Genovese became a household name in the mix-tape hip-hop scene. His street anthem "Genovese's Thesis", featuring Styles P left a mark on the rap game. The song is a classic to this date. In 1999 Genovese became the first Italian/American hip-hop artist to sign to a major record label, inking a deal with Universal Records. Through his career as an artist he's worked with people such as:
Nas, Notorious BIG, Swizz Beatz, Jermaine Dupri, DMX, The Outlawz, Rodney Jerkins, DJ Clark Kent, DJ Kid Capri, and many more. Genovese is still a staple in the Hip-Hop community, he also now manages and consults talent.

Genovese is currently involved in the following:

*VFL Enterprises - a company that manages and consults the careers in the Sports & Entertainment business

*Sports Agent for SMWW

*Part time Basketball Scout for well renowned coach John Coffino

*Co-Owner of "2 Pals Talkin' Sports", a sports based blog/podcast available on many Social Media platforms.

*A proud volunteer at School on Wheels program in the Skid Row section of Los Angeles, tutoring and mentoring homeless children. My "Music Saves Kids Lives" fundraiser provided a Music Program for children at the Skid Row Learning Center. Voted Tutor of the Month for November 2012.

Specialties:

*Writing - My music has been featured on MTV shows, "Son of a Gun", "True Life", and "Moving In"
*Producing - TV sitcom written by rapper/actor Fredro Starr
*Managing & Consulting - Jamie Sangouthai (Khloe & Lamar Show), Rich Soil Clothing & Music
*Basketball Scout and Coach - Scouted 2012 NBA Summer League, Assistant Coach at 2012 International Exposure Basketball Camp (hartsportsmanagement.com) for men and women. Assistant Coach at the NBA D-League Bakersfield Jam tryouts 2012.
*Social Media Management - Albany Legends 2012
