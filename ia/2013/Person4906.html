Jim Thomas' Summary

An experienced, articulate speaker,with two messages and a workshop the times are demanding, now more than everive. More than 65 audiences have heard his conference keynote address, “The Integrity Imperative,“ and given it resounding approval.

Jim Thomas is author of the informative book, Integrity: The Indispensable Element and is founder of Alliance For Integrity, LLC.

Since 1984 Jim has been writing and speaking on the subject of Integrity. In addition to two books books, his writings include published articles and newspaper essays. His work has appeared in Vital Speeches of the Day. Fortune magazine quoted him.

Attendees will hear and learn that a culture of integrity enhances reputation, builds competitive age, yields greater loyalty among customes, vendors, staff, and employees.
