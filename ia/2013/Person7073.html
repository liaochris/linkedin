Andrew McIntyre's Summary

As far back as I can remember, I have always been interested in business, watching my father run his own and being keen to help was something I grew up with. By the age of 13 I decided I wanted to make money of my own and run my own company. I began importing games consoles and selling them in the UK on eBay.

At 16 I became the Managing Director of another business as a result of taking part in Young Enterprise. The company ran for a year and was very successful and as a result received six awards. Due to the exposure this company gave me, I was later awarded a full scholarship to the University of Buckingham to study BSc Business Enterprise.

Apart from business, I have a keen interest in sport. At 15, I was awarded ‘Sports leader of the year’ and became the 'Sports Ambassador for the Mandeville School Sports Partnership’ which consists of 37 schools. This was a great opportunity in my life as I met many of the British Olympians from Beijing and best of all, got to teach and encourage sport across Buckinghamshire. My role was to encourage as many young people as possible to take part in sport and to promote the Olympic values in the run up to London 2012.

Although I have run several mini enterprises, I have also tried to gain as much experience in the work place as possible. I have held a range of jobs in different sector, many at the same time. The skills i have learnt within these roles include; Management, Marketing & Communications, Event Organising, Import/Export, Website Design and Development and SEO to name a few.

I was also lucky enough work within the Operations Team for the largest McDonald's in the world at London 2012.

I currently own a Website Design and Management company and also work within Enterprise Marketing Solutions, a full service marketing agency in Aylesbury.

I hope to continue to advance within online technology and digital marketing with the aspiration of one day becoming a big name within the online world.
