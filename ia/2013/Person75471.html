Lance Reis' Summary

I am the owner of www.kickass-designs.com a web and graphic design business located in Corvallis, Oregon. My name is Lance Reis, and I am the founder and owner. I started this business years ago with the desire to aid those looking to further their own businesses and professional endeavors. As a small and emerging business owner, I know the hardships associated with getting a business off the ground. Kickass Designs is here to help such businesses as well as those that are more established. Our services include: website creation and modification, photo editing, and custom logo, business card, and flyer creation. We work to meet our clients at whatever stage of business promotion they find themselves. Our competitive rates and personalized services are adaptable for each of our client’s diverse needs. Customer satisfaction is our highest priority, and we strive to send each client away with the greatest quality service, value, and tools for business promotion.
If you would like to learn more about what Kickass Designs can offer your business, please contact us to arrange a consultation. We are available to have an in-person meeting in Corvallis, Albany, Salem, Eugene, Portland and the surrounding areas. If you are outside these locations, we can talk via phone or email. Thank you for your interest in Kickass Designs, and we hope to hear from you soon!
