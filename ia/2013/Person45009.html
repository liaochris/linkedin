Marc Fleisher's Summary

I am a divorce settlement advocate. I help people reach separation agreements without court intervention. I do this either by representing them as a collaborative lawyer, as a negotiator outside the Collaborative model, or by working with them as a third-party mediator. My goal is to reach fair and workable resolutions through a process that is no more stressful than it has be, nor less dignified than it can be

A leader in the field of Collaborative Practice and divorce mediation, Marc co-founded the New York Association of Collaborative Professionals and was a charter member of its board. He has taught divorce mediation to law students, lawyers and mental-health professionals since 1995, primarily at Brooklyn Law School and the Ackerman Institute for the Family. He will be teaching the first New York City law school Collaborative Practice course in the Spring of 2010.
