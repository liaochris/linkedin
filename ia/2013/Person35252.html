Diane Fischer's Summary

As President and Owner of L & D Mail Masters, Inc., Diane’s extensive background in direct mail has enabled her to mold L & D Mail Masters into a technologically-advanced direct marketing company whose mission is to help their clients:

✉ Develop high response, lead generation programs
✉ Differentiate themselves with targeted, custom designed direct marketing campaigns.
✉ Combine the power of direct mail marketing with interactive media channels
✉ Evaluate, tweak & measure the success of a campaign in real time
✉ Manage print and communication services

In the past 25 years, Diane has expanded L & D’s abilities to become a well-respected, full-size direct mail marketing resource. L & D’s clientele include insurance companies, financial institutions, universities, national sales organizations and other industry leaders.
