Christine Gerbstadt, MD, MPH, RD's Summary

Christine Gerbstadt, MD, RD

Physician and healthy lifestyle coach! I see food and exercise as the first medicine. I love looking into the future, and as Thomas Edison said, "The doctor of the future will give no medicine, but will interest patients in the care of the human frame, in diet, and in the cause and prevention of disease."

Doctor's Detox Diet provides books, speaking, print and television media to a variety of audiences to help them achieve better health through lifestyle enhancements.

My new book, Doctor's Detox Diet The Ultimate Weight Loss Prescription (www.Amazon.com/books February 2012) is the science-based plan to purify your body and transform your life with foods and spices you love.

The Doctor’s Detox Diet is designed to heal and strengthen your body’s natural detoxification system, or SKILL© — Skin, Kidney, Intestine, Lymphatic, and Liver.

These systems work together and independently to remove toxins and waste, fight inflammation, and speed-up metabolism to burn existing fat, keep it off, and create the new body you have always craved.

Using everyday foods, herbs, and spices loaded with specific nutrients, vitamins, minerals, and phytochemicals, you can improve your SKILL© to finally drop those excess pounds, get trim, prevent illness, reverse the effects of aging, and maintain optimal health.

Specialties: I am the world's ONLY Board Certified Medical Doctor who is a Registered Dietitian and Spokesperson for the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.
