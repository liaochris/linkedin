Betsy Muller's Summary

Betsy Bartter Muller is the founder and President of the Indigo Connection, LLC and best-selling uthor of the trademarked Energy Makeover book series. Betsy is also an Executive with Nuskin/Pharmanex, the world's leader in antiaging technology.

As a leader, speaker, coach and group facilitator, Betsy's 30 years of professional experience in a wide range of business roles provides her clients a wealth of ideas for solving problems and improving their profitability and success. Beyond the traditional, she has also integrated energy healing techniques into her coaching to allow her clients immediate positive changes. She is the best-selling author of Energy Makeover - A Conscious Way to Stay Young, Have Fun and Get More Done, the ONLY ACEP and EFTUniverse Certified EFT practitioner in Ohio, an ordained Minister, and is certified by the Association for Comprehensive Energy Psychology as an Energy Health Practitioner. She is also a professional member of the National Speakers Association.

Betsy's expertise includes professional speaking, group medical practice management, publishing, business-to-business sales, human resource management, marketing, social media, and wellness education. Her previous employers have included both large and small businesses: PPG Industries, BP Oil Company, Southwest General Health Center, and OsteoMed II Inc. She has taken numerous continuing education courses in professional speaking, online marketing, social media, platform development, energy psychology, energy coaching, holistic health and leadership. She participated in Michael Gerber's E-Myth Mastery program while serving as General Manager at OsteoMed II. She completed NSA Ohio's ProTrack program for professional speakers in 2012. Because she believes that the human spirit is the place where success originates, she teaches spiritual business and life principles while striving to serve as an example of these principles in action.

Specialties

Certified Energy Coach, 2XCertified EFT Practitioner, ACEP Certified Energy Health Practitioner. Retreat Facilitator, Non-Denominational Minister. Rapid self-care results coaching with Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT) and cutting edge Energy Makeover methods.

I empower clients, groups and organizations to apply simple energy self-care solutions to improve health, relationships, wealth and personal satisfaction. Ideal clients include female leaders, entrepreneurs, sales pros and authors.
