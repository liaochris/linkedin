Felix Holzapfel's Summary

Located in Cologne (Germany) an Denver (USA) we are baking for companies in Germany and the USA - but instead of biscuits we are serving innovative marketing strategies.

Our delicacies range from Guerilla and Viral to Online, Social Media, Mobile & Cross Media Marketing.

We work for large companies like ADAC, Germanwings, Henkel, Sharp, Swiss International Air Lines, TÜV (the German DMV) or Vodafone as well as for small and medium sized companies like Dialo.de, Handytarife.de or Sparhandy.de.

Our marketing agency conceptbakery was founded in 2002. In May 2007 we additionally launched a new model for the creation and distribution of web videos named moviebakery.com. Since that time we produced more then 100 web videos for our clients and became one of the Market leaders in the area of online commercials in Germany and the USA.

Keywords:
guerrilla marketing, viral marketing, web video marketing, online marketing, social media, mobile marketing, crossmedia campaigns.

Our philosophy:
Being more flexible and dynamic than competitors, only working on what we believe in - that makes us passionate about our work.

Beside my job at conceptbakery I'm also a tutor in the fields of Guerilla, Viral, Online, Mobile and Cross Media Marketing, for example at the Marthin-Luther University in Halle, at the University in Jena and Weimar or at different marketing conferences. Additionally I'm author of the book "Guerilla Marketing - Online, Mobile & Crossmedia" and coauthor of the book "Leitfaden Online Marketing" from Dr. Torsten Schwarz.

And last but not least im supporter of SouthAfricaProject.org. This project combines a team of international specialists - stretching from well known marketing, web 2.0 and social media experts to psychologists, financial experts and organizations already working successfully in South Africa.

Specialties

guerrilla marketing, viral marketing, web video marketing, online marketing, social media, mobile marketing, crossmedia campaigns
