Hannah Morgan's Summary

I am an energetic, compassionate conveyor of no-nonsense advice and truly enjoy helping people understand how to look for work better. For over 10 years, I've been helping job seekers learn to market themselves as they search for their next opportunity.

Reputation Management:

I strongly believe that each one of us has the power to build the right online image. The tools are in place to give you visibility, you just need to learn to use them. That's were I can help. Creating online visibility which mirrors your in-person reputation is key to your career survival and success.

The best way to learn about job search is to follow me on Twitter @CareerSherpa I love using social networking tools (Twitter is my all-time favorite!) As an introvert, these platforms make it more comfortable for me to network and meet new people- plus grow my business.

I co-founded Career Navigator, a five-day intensive boot camp for professional level job seekers currently offered through New York State's One Stop Career Centers. So far, well over 2,500 job seekers have participated in the program.

Presenting:

Delivering presentations and workshops is one of the best parts of my job because it provides the opportunity to get people thinking about how they manage their careers and online visibility.

Writing:

Sharing the latest job search trends and techniques is a passion of mine, whether on Twitter or on my site, Career Sherpa.net. Additonally, you can see my articles on
• US News & World Report's On Careers
• Job-Hunt.org
• YouTern's Savvy Intern
• The Undercover Recruiter
• Lipsticking
• HerRochester and more.


Specialties: Career consultant, writer/blogger, speaker on career management/job search topics: networking, interviewing skills. Deliver training on social networking (LinkedIn, Twitter, Blogs and Facebook), personal branding and personal online reputation management, communication skills and self promotion.
