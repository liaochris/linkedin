Andrea Stenberg's Summary

I'm passionate about helping small businesses and solopreneurs get more clients using Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter & other social media tools. You don't need a giant list of contacts, just the right strategy.

Do you have a handful of LinkedIn contacts and are wondering what to do next? Please join me for the free webinar "I'm on LinkedIn, Now What? 5 Ways to Grow Your Business Using the #1 Social Network for Professionals"

On this call I’ll share ways business professionals use LinkedIn as an important business tool. You’ll learn how to:
* Use LinkedIn to get more clients
* Be smarter, save money and make better business decisions using LinkedIn
* Build credibility and be seen as an expert in your field.

Reserve your spot at http://thebabyboomerentrepreneur.com/free-training/

In addition to coaching, I work with businesses to develop a social media marketing plan. I can analyse your current efforts, point you in the right direction, devise a strategy and train your team to implement the plan.

If you don't have the staffing to run a great social media campaign, I also manage a small number of social media campaigns for clients. Contact me to see if we're a fit.

To get a taste of my style, check out The Baby Boomer Entrepreneur blog. The blog helps Baby Boomers with information and advice about marketing their small business with traditional marketing strategies combined with social media and other online tools.

You might also like to get a free copy of my ebook "37 Interesting Things to Post to Facebook" http://socialmediaforbabyboomers.com/

Specialties: I'm passionate about helping coaches, consultants and baby boomer entrepreneurs use LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter to get new and better clients. Social media marketing, social media marketing plans, social media strategy for small business, how to use LinkedIn, online marketing, Facebook fan pages, Facebook advertising.
