Jay Suthar's Summary

As a designer I deliver functional visual design solutions of many flavours, including websites, print collateral and product concepts, taking a strategic approach at all times.

I have contributed within technology companies by actively applying design to connect products, services and values of the business to its customers.

I develop. I enhance. And strive to create compelling and immersive experiences for users, delivered in a logical format.

I have significant exposure in front-end web development; designing and developing bespoke branded online experiences that are both appealing and technology-driven, enabling businesses to communicate better and support their value proposition.

I am passionate about design at all levels - infatuated with details and technical awareness, driven to learning new skills and generating fresh ideas. Love working as part of a dynamic team, with a strong sense of collaboration.
