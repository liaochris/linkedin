Louise Griffiths' Summary

Finegreen Associates - www.finegreen.co.uk - are a market-leading recruitment consultancy providing a professional and flexible recruitment service to a wide variety of public & private sector clients throughout the UK. Our high calibre, experienced candidates are fully referenced and available for all levels of Permanent, Interim and Fixed Term Contract opportunities.

Our interim management service offers clients flexibility to recruit highly qualified individuals on a short term basis for a variety of project, consultancy or line management roles.

Our permanent recruitment campaigns and executive search services have an outstanding track-record of generating genuine excitement and interest in client roles whilst diligently matching skills and experience against an agreed job description and person specification. Unlike many of our competitors we offer clients the flexibility of paying us on either a 'success only' or a 'retained' basis. Our value for money philosophy means that our fees remain the most competitive within the industry.
