Don Sceifers' Summary

I have a 30 year career in technology. From client server to object oriented architectures to SQL design and implementation, I have had the pleasure of experiencing the full ride of just where technology has been and where it is going.

I have worked in hardware and software. I worked as a developer for a database company, on a cell controller for Cincinnati Milicron, on several workflow applications at Lexis Nexis and have created several web based applications for companies like OneNet Communications and HarvestInfo. I enjoy solving business problems where it makes sense to use technology. I do not subscribe to the belief that anything is "impossible" and have often had the opportunity to prove it. I also have over 10 years of experience as a consultant working for companies like GE Consulting, SSI and Systems Evolution.

I currently am focusing on Perl & PHP as web languages, but also have worked extensively in ASP. I have a lot of C & C++ in my career as well. I used Perl to develop a business process system, going way beyond typical uses like system scripts and simple web pages.

I also own Sceifers Unlimited, a web based distribution company for consumer health & beauty products. I also promote several business products and services, including a great Gift & Incentive program that relieves a lot of the work involved in keeping your employees motivated and feeling appreciated.

Specialties

design and development of business solutions., open source evangelism & implementation, development of incentive systems for small businesses.
