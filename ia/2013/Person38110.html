Dr. Marcella Wilson's Summary

Media Coach | Lifestyle Tech Expert

As a tech expert, I have lent my expertise to Fox News, MSNBC News, various other online and print media. I have appeared in Redbook magazine and numerous news television programs. I coach professionals, experts, business owners, hosts and those interested in TV careers how to get on TV and get more media publicity. I offer advice from my experiences, conduct webinars, online training, workshops, coaching sessions in order to educate others.

For a free 1-hour coaching session, please email me at info@marcellawilson.com

Dissertation Coach | Graduate Student Mentor

I mentor master's and doctoral students throughout the graduate process. I offer one-on-one or group coaching to graduate students. I also conduct dissertation bootcamps (online and in-person), workshops and seminars discussing all aspects of graduate life. I enjoy mentoring graduate students and am excited to work with them. I have a down-to-earth coaching style that produces results.

For a free coaching session, please email me at info@marcellawilson.com.
