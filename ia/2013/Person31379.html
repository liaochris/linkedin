David Edward Castro's Summary

I am a user interface designer and developer. I studied graphic design and marketing at the University of Nevada Reno.

After school, I opened Monzter Web Design Studios. My specialty was in helping entrepreneurs start their own businesses. From the mundane tasks associated with starting a legal business entity to crucial marketing decisions such as logo & brand development, color scheme, web site development & post production strategy, I was key in helping entrepreneurs like myself to get their business off of the ground.

Within a couple of years, I was able to secure work with a start-up where I designed interfaces for desktop & mobile applications. After that enjoyable experience, I honed my skills and have focused on the area ever since.

I enjoy working in teams. I can quickly understand people and how to best work with them. My strongest asset is my creative mind, ability to think outside the box and tenacious work ethic. Couple me with a good software developer and the possibilities are endless.

I have a passion for new technology and love working with start-ups. The best things in life are hard to get but worth the effort.
