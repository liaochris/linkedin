Bob Tankesley CPA CFP's Summary

Bob is the owner of Private Wealth Counsel and is a personal financial advisor to high net worth individuals, retirees and successful business owners. Bob provides comprehensive financial, tax, and risk management strategies, specializing in the areas of retirement transition planning, wealth protection and tax strategies. He has created and implemented targeted financial solutions for families, retirees and business owners as a financial advisor since 1995.

Bob is licensed as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) by the state of Georgia and is a Certified Financial Planner (CFP) (R) practitioner. Bob is also pursuing Level II of the CFA exam and the Qualified Plan Financial Consultant (QPFC) designation.

He is a graduate of the University of North Carolina at Asheville having received a dual undergraduate degree in Accounting and Financial Management, Cum Laude. He is also a graduate of Appalachian State University in Boone, North Carolina having received a MBA degree with an emphasis in Finance.
