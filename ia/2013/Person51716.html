HARLAN YORK's Summary

The First Ever Immigration Lawyer of the Year in New Jersey, as named by "Best Lawyers."
• Attorneys - Immigration Cases in NJ NY PA CT & beyond
• AV Rated ("Preeminent") by Martindale-Hubbell
• Former Chair, NJ State Bar Section on Immigration

• Listed in "Ten Leaders of Immigration Law, NJ"

• Newsweek Nationwide Top Immigration Attorneys
• Superb ("10.0"), Avvo
• Super Lawyers
• US News and World Report Best Law Firms, Tier One

Mr. York has appeared as an Immigration Law Expert on CBS This Morning and Primer Impacto, both televised nationally, and frequently speaks on immigration topics on Univision, Telemundo, NBC, and PBS. He is fluent in Spanish.

He has lectured at Columbia Law School, for the Federal Bar Association, ILW.com, Lawline.com, NJ State Bar Association, NJ State Bar Foundation, NJ Institute for Continuing Legal Education, at New York Law School and Rutgers Law Symposium.

Specialties: Harlan York and Associates handles all immigration matters, particularly Deportation and Detention, Federal Appeals, Visas, Permanent Residence Applications, Waivers, Deferred Action, Citizenship, Employment and Family Based Immigration, and Applications for Victims of Violence. The firm concentrates also on cases for Artists, Entertainers, Athletes, Researchers and Professors.

Mr. York has also provided expert opinions to plaintiffs' and defendants' attorneys in civil matters as well as counsel to criminal defense lawyers, in cases relating to immigration.
