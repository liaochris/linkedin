Jeff Bonano's Summary

I've spent the last 12 years holding a camera and learning how to use it. Now the proud owner of Bonano Productions and Liquid Light Media, I've taken my interests to a level where I can help others get the video they have been looking for. I have spend endless hours training in Motion Graphic Design, Non Linear Editing, Audio Science, Camera Operations, Lighting, and much more. Because there are so many ways to do 1 thing, I always look for new ways to learn how to do the same thing or how to apply old skills to new things. Also with years in Sales and Customer Service, I'm comfortable in helping clients developing new ways to sell their product or services through video. And if it's something I've never done before, I take every effort possible in researching the best way to create a cost effective solution for any situation.

Specialties: Adobe CS4-CS6 Production Software including Premier, After Effects, Sound Booth, Encore, and Photoshop. I am also confident with Adobe Illustrator, Muse, and work with Adobe Business Catalyst. I'm also familiar with Pro Tools and Sony Acid. Comfortable with the use of Pro and Pro-consumer video cameras and effective understanding of proper lighting. I currently am equipped with a Sony HVR-Z5U and DCR-VX2100. I also have Portable Green Screen equipment, Audio Equipment, and Professional lighting for up to 5 point lighting all available for mobility in filming.

Along side with my interest in motion design and video, I have spent over 17 years in either a leadership or management role. I have indulged in years of various management trainings that include certifications in Leadership and Management, Advanced Leadership and Management, and am currently studying Six Sigma. I have learned many ways of dealing with various personalities and have studied under both LIFO and DISC personality approaches. I am familiar with the studies of Geert Hofstede and enjoy sharing my knowledge with others.
