Eve Pearson's Summary

Eve Pearson, MBA, RD, CSSD, LD, is a wellness expert and the founder of Nutriworks, a nationally recognized sports nutrition practice. As a frequently requested speaker, Eve draws upon her diverse experience as a Registered Dietitian (RD), Certified Specialist in Sports Dietetics (CSSD), past certified personal trainer through the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM-CPT), and nutrition counselor.

Eve works with professional, collegiate, youth and recreational athletes. She believes if you’re active, you’re an athlete! Some of the organizations and teams Eve consults or has consulted for are: Leukemia & Lymphoma Society’s Team in Training Programs, YMCA, Michael Johnson Performance, Integrated Athletic Development, Major League Soccer’s FC Dallas, Instinct Volleyball Club, Southern Methodist University's Track and Cross Country Team and many local triathlon and running clubs. She has also consulted for several individual athletes from the Nike Oregon Project and the National Football League (NFL).

Eve earned a B.S. degree in Coordinated Dietetics from Texas Christian University and an M.B.A. from the University of North Texas. Eve is very involved in numerous professional organizations including: Sports, Cardiovascular, and Wellness Nutrition (SCAN); Nutrition Entrepreneurs (NE); The Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics and its state and local affiliates; and Toastmasters International.

Eve has been named Competitor Texas Magazine's #1 Sports Nutritionist 3 different times and was Recognized Young Dietitian of the Year in 2011 through the Texas Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.

On a personal note, Eve is an athlete. Her youth sports included gymnastics, softball and a 14 year span as a cheerleader, competing nationally at the All-Star and Collegiate level. As an adult, Eve has finished Ironman, 50 mile and 50K trail runs, and most recently qualified and ran Boston marathon. She truly practices what she teaches.
