Mario Nissan's Summary

Mario Nissan is CIO and Partner of Flock, a company that started 3.5 years ago as a digital marketing agency, and has expanded its mission by intersecting Marketing & Technology to enrich customer experience with brands and therefore generate value for them.

Flock has several business units, including: Flock Agency, Flock Free Marketing & SMB Marketing, Flock Big Data, Flock Publishing, Flock Films & Flock Labs.

Flock = Marketing + Technology & Big Data / Data-driven marketing!

As a Flocker, Mario is in charge of the company’s technology strategy, IT operations, performance improvement, and the Big Data, Free Marketing & Flock Labs business units. He also looks out for the company’s financial and tax strategies.

Mario has been recently named President of the SMB Digital Advertising committee at IAB Mexico.

In this last years, he also founded and is a board member of Swarm, a company that uses the latest crawling and semantic analysis technologies to deliver market research from social media; and he has mentored many Internet Startups in Mexico.

He is passionate about how technology is redefining the way we live and enhancing the overall human experience.

In the past, Mario was CEO of Templeo.com, Grupo Televisa’s job site; CEO of Tecnosinergia, a company specialized in security and wireless infrastructure; and CEO of Maz, a company that built and hosted websites back in 1996.

Past experience:

IP Networks, IP Telephony, VoIP, GSM Gateways, IP Security and Surveillance, Network Video, WLAN, LAN, WAN, CCTV, Software Development, Project Management, Technical Support, Training.

Currently focused on:
Big Data, Performance Marketing, Digital Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Consumer Research, Social Media CRM, Marketing Planning, Usability and Conversion, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Corporate Finance, Customer Relations, Technology Strategy.
