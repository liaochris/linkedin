Erik Deckers' Projects

  • Blogging for SEO

    • May 2010 to April 2013
    Team Members: Erik Deckers, Paul Lorinczi, Julie Ross

    A client in the construction industry who owned an online store, and had a competitor that copied everything he was doing from his store — the competitor had actually scraped all the content and photos from the site, changed the name, and marketed everything as his own products. He asked if we could help increase his online brand, beat the competitor in search, and overcome the theft of his intellectual property.

    Through a content marketing strategy using blogging and SEO, we were able to increase his online brand awareness, and help him win search for over 100 different keywords. In the course of a year, he was able to reduce his PPC budget and increased organic traffic to his store. His year-over-year sales grew 6% on $200 million in total sales, or an additional $1 million per month.

    The BAM! This conference was geared toward entrepreneurs, businesses, marketing and public relations professionals.
