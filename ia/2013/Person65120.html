Jodi Coppens' Summary

Jodi Coppens has a reputation as a dynamic and effective accounting software consultant and trainer. She's spent most of her career in corporate accounting, most recently as the CFO of a $15 million distribution company providing goods and services to the lodging industry.

9 years ago she felt she wanted to use her skills and experience to help more than one company, so she founded AdvantEdge Accounting Solutions. With her extensive background in analysis and financials, she is able to laser focus on the "bottom" line and unravel the mysteries of accounting AND technology to assist small to mid-size companies with their "real-world" accounting software, procedural and training needs.

Technically trained as an Advanced QuickBooks ProAdvisor and MAS 90/200 Authorized Partner , she can analyze a company's accounting software needs, recommend a solution that can be customized to fit their business and then provide training on how to use the software.
