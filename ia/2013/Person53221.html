Tim Brennan's Summary

I believe it’s time to change the way hiring is done.

After spending 13 years at Hostess Frito-Lay where I worked in Sales, Management, IT and Human Resources, I developed new insights into the differences between top performers and bottom performers and how to find, hire and keep the best people.

As a result in 1998, I founded the Brennan Group of Online Companies, with the goal of helping North American businesses improve their results through better hiring and employee retention processes. In eight years, We built a network of over 40 partner companies and hundreds of clients across North America including The UPS Stores, Humpty Dumpty Snackfoods and Westmont Hospitality. Then in 2006, I joined forces with Jan van der Hoop to launch HiringSmart - a revolutionary approach to hiring that helps companies change their bottom line results.
