Geremy Cepin's Summary

Geremy Cepin is a Managing Director with Koltin Consulting Group, where he specializes in Executive Retained Search. Geremy is retained to identify, assess, and recruit Partners, C-level executives, Vice Presidents, and other senior talent.

Geremy has a proven ability to develop and execute strategies to attract and retain world class talent in a highly competitive and complex market. He has extensive knowledge and expertise in all areas of talent acquisition obtained through partnering with U.S. and global business leaders to align talent strategies with organizational goals.

Geremy’s success in completing searches for world-class talent has come across multiple industries including:

• Business & Professional Services
• Asset Management
• Financial Services
• Consumer Products
• Real Estate
• Industrial & Manufacturing

Geremy has earned a reputation for his tailored and thorough approach in assessing a candidate's skill sets and values for fit with company strategy and culture. Clients praise his ability to get to know their business intimately and invest in long-term relationships. Geremy is also known for being easy to work with, consultative, insightful, responsive, reachable, and for providing a pleasant client experience.

Geremy has built his practice around an advisory approach. Most, if not all, relationships start based upon an advisory touch enabling clients to gain external perspective on market trends and best practices in talent management – sourcing, recruitment, interviewing, selection, hiring and retention – while Geremy learns about their organization, top priorities, goals and objectives.
