Andrea Lynn's Summary

Since I could hold a pen and stand on stool to help cook, I've always been writing and cooking, although it took some years to combine these talents professionally. I am the author of two cookbooks, The I Love Trader Joe's College Cookbook and The Artisan Soda Workshop.

I have more than a decade of experience as a writer, concentrating on profiles and lifestyle subjects like food, health, nutrition, as well as being a versatile recipe editor.

Additionally, I have a culinary degree and logged time in a professional kitchen before becoming a freelance food writer and cookbook author. I worked as an editor at two food publications, where my duties included recipe editing, recipe development/testing, writing, interviewing, and blogging, among other responsibilities.

Currently, I am a freelance food writer and recipe developer. My recipes have been featured on Serious Eats, Chile Pepper Magazine, Better Homes & Gardens' bookazines, Kiwi magazine's cookbook, Allergy-Friendly Food for Families, and others. I have edited recipes for MarthaStewart.com, Art Culinaire, Chile Pepper Magazine and cookbooks like Made with Love, plus tested recipes for Food52.com and cookbook authors. In addition, I have ghostwritten recipes and blog posts for various chefs and corporate clients.

Need an experienced food writer or recipe development? Contact me at andrealynn27@gmail.com.

Specialties: Recipe Editing, Cookbook Proposals, Cookbook Development, Recipe Testing, Food Writer, Recipe Development, Lifestyle Writing, SEO, Ghostwriting.

A variety of clips showcasing my writing and recipe development is available via Contently: https://andrealynn.contently.com/
