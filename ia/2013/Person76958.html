Kristen Bobst's Summary

Kristen Bobst, a native of Jacksonville, Florida, graduated summa cum laude in 2005 from the University of Florida with a degree in English. She then enrolled in Trinity College, Dublin earning a Master of Philosophy degree in Anglo-Irish Literature. In 2010, she completed the University of Southern California’s prestigious MFA program in Writing for Screen and Television where she honed her skills as a professional storyteller. She currently works as a freelance writer.

Specialties: New Media, Writing for Animation
