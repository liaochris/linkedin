Andrew Maul's Summary

Hi, I'm Andrew. I'm a designer specializing in video and board games; I'm also a writer and freelance web designer. My biggest strength is my ability to convey an experience, whether it's to a player, reader, or user.

I have a wide array of both technical and interpersonal skills, and experience in creative, lead, and management positions. I've worked as a level designer, project manager, contributing author, and am currently working in education as the lead curriculum specialist at a university.

My hobbies include a good mix of design-related projects, as well as photography, reading, and playing video games. I'm currently working on a board game and a novel in my free time. Please let me know if you think I may be a good fit for your team, or if you just want to say hello!

Specialties

Video Game Design, Level Design, Web Design, Board Game Design, Writing, Graphic Design, Curriculum
