Michael Nibert's Summary

I am a Registered Respiratory Therapist (RRT), credentialed by the NBRC in 1978, dedicated to helping maintain Quality Care in today’s complex medical environment. I earned my BacheIor of Science in Respiratory Therapy (BSRT) in 1982. I fell in love with the field of Respiratory Care (RC) in 1971 at the age of 18 and continue in a Leadership capacity with 42 years experience. My years of service provide you with the experience you need as a partner.
As a RRT, I have taken an active role helping Respiratory Care Practitioners (RCP's) stay current with compliance issues, the latest in quality care including evidence based medicine & best practices to include new service lines.
I can assist you in helping patients as you navigate the changing CMS directives for reimbursement targeting quality care core measures and readmission issue penalties.
I specialize in pediatric and adult critical care and have experience in neonatal critical care. I use my years of experience in providing quality care for patients and helping educate other health care providers in related medical fields in these service areas: invasive and non-invasive ventilation including HFOV, Biphasic Curiass Ventilation (BCV), cardiopulmonary rehabilitation, home care, cardiopulmonary diagnostics, electroneurodiagnostics, asthma education and tobacco cessation.
I am also a Respiratory Educational Consultant for some of the top Respiratory Care manufacturers. I have current affiliations with the following companies: ® Aerogen and ® Roche Laboratories. I often provide clinical support for sales teams at trade shows, conventions and events. I support them with both clinical training and evaluations. I educate the sales team on clinical approaches to help customers and drive agressive business margins.
