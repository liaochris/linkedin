Mathew DeBlanc's Summary

Mathew has spent the past 14 years serving in pastoral ministry in the greater Houston and Dallas/Fort Worth Metro areas. He has traveled by invitation nationally and internationally speaking to thousands, developing leaders, and equipping people for everyday life. He is known as an empowering leader and innovative strategic planner with high integrity and a keen eye for detail, possessing an intuitive ability to inspire people to reach their potential.

As an avid entrepreneur, Mathew also leads a speaking and consulting company based in North Dallas, co-founded a work-life care based company, and enjoys investing. His passion for people is surpassed by his love for his wife of 13 years and 2 children.

CORE COMPETENCIES:

– Proven ability to build rewarding, long-term relationships by using outstanding communication and listening skills. Establishes quick rapport resulting in high levels of confidence and trust.

– Recognized as a visionary leader and highly effective communicator with keen understanding of people.

– Strong aptitude to drive new opportunities within an organization.

– Intelligent leader capitalizing on natural leadership talents with a propensity to initiate positive changes in people, teams, and organizations.

– Confident interaction with individuals at all levels.

– Skilled in defining continuous improvement processes, presiding over lively brainstorming sessions, developing and executing targeted initiatives, building consensus, recognizing and accelerating peer’s strengths, and building powerful, successful teams.

– Proven ability to effectively handle multi-task levels of management responsibility with minimal direction from superiors while supervising personnel and providing team leadership, motivation, and development in a supportive, cooperative team environment.

– Success-driven attitude with ability to easily grasp new concepts, methods, and techniques.

– Reputation for strong work ethic and uncompromising morals.
