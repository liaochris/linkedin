Lauren Ebbecke's Summary

Lauren Ebbecke is the CEO and founder of Extraordinary Wellness: Changing the World from the Inside Out, a company committed to the personal growth and empowerment of women choosing to consciously create their lives while making a difference in the world. She helps working women go from a stressful life to a successful life by assisting them in connecting with their inner soul to create mind & body wellness.
