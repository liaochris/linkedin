Chance Brown's Summary

My interests include designing, implementing, and maintaining enterprise-wide Talent and Organizational Development solutions. This includes managing global systems, processes and programs designed to increase employee collaboration and performance.

With over 10 years experience working in global corporations as a Learning and Development professional and systems specialist, I’ve had the opportunity to lead a wide range of employee development projects - both technology related and non-technology related.
