Mike Lupidi's Summary

These days it seems like 2 people are doing the work that 5 people were doing 10 years ago! Between the time constraints and the need for good people, it's hard to focus and give a solid effort toward finding the right people.

We help growing companies that need top talent and who don’t have a full-fledged recruiting department. Think of us as your source for "Top Talent" to fill your critical positions.

We follow the Golden Rule of Recruiting practices. Our "Partnership" fee agreement provides you with a good hedge against business downturns or poor hiring outcomes. I know we can help you.

We have the resources that enable us to provide services and hiring solutions for you more quickly, effectively and efficiently - including direct hire, temp-to-direct and contractors for short-term needs.

I love this work!! My goal every day is to surface the best qualified people I can to fulfill the demanding requirements of my hiring managers. We would welcome the opportunity to address your staffing needs going forward.

We are continually evolving and gaining recognition as the preferred search partner in three niches:

• Packaging Machinery / Equipment
• Food Processing Machinery / Equipment
• Conveyor / Material Handling Systems

In these niches, we place:

1. Technical Sales Professionals

2. Field Service Technicians

3. Engineers - all disciplines

4. Project Managers

By the way, we can place you as a direct, temp-to-direct [“test drive” scenario] or as a contractor for shorter-term needs.

Specialties: Packaging Systems, Packaging Equipment, Packaging Machinery, Conveyors, Material Handling, Shrink Packaging Equipment, FFS, VFFS, Stretch Wrappers, Tray Erectors, Supply Chain, Distribution, Materials Management, Logistics, Palletizing, PLC, Food Processing Equipment
