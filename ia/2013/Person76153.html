Kit Werremeyer's Summary

40 years US and international experience in marketing, sales, claims and dispute resolution, and commercial contracting of large EPC construction projects associated with the oil, gas, refining, chemical, petrochemical, energy and water sectors.

Specialties: Southernstar Consultants provides the following services:
1. Two day training course: "Understanding and Negotiating Construction Contracts"
2. Half and One Day Seminars: "High Risk Commercial Terms in Construction Contracts"
3. Advice and assistance on doing business in Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, the Philippines, Vietnam and Burma (Myanmar).
4. Contractor advocacy: assistance with negotiating fair and balanced construction contract terms and conditions for private US and international construction projects.
