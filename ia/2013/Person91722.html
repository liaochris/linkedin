Marilyn Freedberg's Summary

I have over 20 years of consulting experience creating effective performance solutions for many big-name companies in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I enjoy bringing both my background and imagination to create that simple, elegant solution for my clients. Simple & elegant also = cost effective.

Specialties: innovative and practical applications of instructor-led, computer-based, and blended instructional methods to create successful performers for industry leaders like the Gap, Wells Fargo Bank, and Cisco Systems.

Particularly experienced in financial services, IT, call center, sales, and retail industries.

I and my team have turned that expertise into affordable semi-custom leadership classes that improve productivity. Ability's LEAP (Leadership Pathway) program provides a customized solution without a custom price. You can see more at www.abilityPS.com/products.

Classes are results based, with a knowledge and performance assessment of the related skills - well after the class. It's the improvement in performance we're after.

The program starts with the individual contributor. Classes like Personal Leadership and Time Management help employees take charge of their own productivity and success.

Higher levels include skills in managing people and work, focusing on the application of those skills in areas such as Leading People and Managing Performance.

Originally created for premium nationwide retailer Ghirardelli Chocolate, our Building Retail Success (BRS) program is truly unique. Hourly retail employees quickly become expert at giving that special guest experience that keeps customers coming back.

No classrooms are needed, and many of the activities can be done without a trainer. The program includes assessments tied to promotion for new hires (Basic level) and higher levels of expertise and responsibility (Silver and Gold levels).

Both programs utilize our semi-custom approach to provide custom results at reasonable prices.
