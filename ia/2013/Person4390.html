Alfonso Rodriguez Lecuona's Summary

Alfonso is the Managing Director (MD) of Cambridge Studies Ltd and Silicon Cambridge Ltd. He works as a senior consultant in the areas of innovation, education, entrepreneurship, ICT, R&D and knowledge transfer. Before this he was the online & marketing coordinator at NEC - Philips UK, Cambridge Research Park. He studied a Master in International and European Business Law (LLM with Distinction) and holds degrees in Business (BA Hons) and Law (LLB Hons). Alfonso has been a registered lawyer in Spain since 2007.

During his three years at the Canary Island Institute of Technology (European R&D Centre), he gained vast experience in Project Management (Information Technology Entrepreneurship). Alfonso has been involved in the Judge Business School Entrepreneurship programme since 2004 and in 2007 was awarded with a distinction certificate (CfEL - Cambridge University). He was appointed Vice-President of the Cambridge University Technology and Enterprise Club (CUTEC) and worked also as Marketing Advisor and External Relations officer for the Club from 2008. Since then, Alfonso has organized and coordinated more than 12 trade missions, tailored entrepreneurship training programs and technological bridges in Europe and helped more than 200 startups and entrepreneurs to expand their links across the continent. He has recently become an official member of IdeaSpace Enterprise Accelerator (at the Entrepreneurship Centre, Cambridge University) from where he aims to bridge the gap between academia and entrepreneurial initiatives using innovative approaches.

Specialty skills: Innovation Consultant, Entrepreneurship, Startups events, UK-Spain Trading, Internationalization, IT Consultant, Online & Digital Marketing, SEO, International Business Law, ADR.
