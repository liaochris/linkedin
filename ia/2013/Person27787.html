Chris Eberle's Summary

Chris Eberle is a seasoned GM with big company and startup experience. Leveraging his background in direct response marketing and CRM, he has built and led teams in sales, marketing, and operations and has been responsible for multi-million dollar global businesses, multi-million dollar client relationships, multi-million dollar product P&Ls, multi-billion dollar revenue delivery, and global teams of 100+. He has negotiated deals with some of the largest players in online products and media, and acquired, upsold, and retained customers in B2B, B2SMB, and B2C advertising, SaaS, consumer products, and subscription businesses.

He doesn't usually refer to himself in the third person.
