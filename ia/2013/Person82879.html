John Moore's Summary

John Moore founded Legal Resources LLC as a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) after retiring with 22 years of Distinguished & Honorable Military Service and recognizing a need for highly skilled investigative professionals in the South Puget Sound Area. As demand increased for competent legal services, Legal Resources LLC focused on three distinctive specialties; Service of Process, Courier/Delivery and Mobile Notaries Public. We also specialize in the location of persons, especially evasive persons. We offer many more legal services, and are adding more resources everyday to meet the needs of our clients. Whether in Thurston County, Statewide or Nationally... Legal Resources LLC has the "RESOURCES" to solve your needs. Ask about our service guarantee "Served in Three Days or the Service is FREE!" and receive 10% off your 1st months invoice.

Specialties:

Investigations Specialties: Skip Trace, Covert Surveillance, Digital Photography, HD Video,
Child/Person Locate Services, Witness Statements/Interviews, and Insurance Investigations.

Training Specialties: Military Science, State of Washington Criminal Justice Training Center (CJTC) and Department of Labor (DOL) PS/PI/BRA certifications, Tactical Firearms, Executive/Personal Protection/Security.

Executive/Personal Protection/Security Specialties: Domestic, International, Corporate, and Family Protective/Security Services.
