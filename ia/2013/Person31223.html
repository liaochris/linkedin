David Murray's Summary

Social media and marketing professional fully immersed in social web and digital communications. The process of integrating new media methodologies with established marketing practices is something I truly enjoy. This results in successful business solutions that “push the norm” while maintaining the integrity and goals of the assigned objectives.

As an active member of the Detroit community, I founded the Social Media Club chapter of Detroit. I've also helped organize events like Brand Camp University, and served as co-chair for FutureMidwest, the region’s largest digital and knowledge conference.

I've been quoted in Wall Street Journal Online, and have been featured in David Meerman Scott’s blog, Web Ink Now; and his book, The New Rules of Marketing and PR.

Best practices and methodologies of how social media works are two things I excel at, and I take great pride in teaching our clients not only how to do social media, but why they need to be social. I believe that companies who communicate the best will win in the social space. Communication doesn't mean marketing. This requires connecting with effective content that people will want to engage with, and share with their peers.

Sharing what I have learned is my true passion and working by the mantra, “leave no regrets”, I am constantly motivated to learn new technologies and to push my creative energy in any project I am involved with.

If you are struggling with your social media initiatives, or are looking for additional guidance, please don't hesitate to connect!
