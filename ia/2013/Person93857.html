Miguel Salcido's Summary

Miguel Salcido is a veteran search marketing executive, having held Director and VP positions at full service online marketing agencies such as GearyLSF and eVisibility. Miguel is the CEO & Founder of Organic Media Group LLC, a content driven organic search marketing agency. Organic Media Group offers SEO, Social Media, and Link Building consulting services driven by content marketing. Find Miguel on Twitter, @miguelsalcido.

His many years of agency and consulting experience have allowed Miguel the opportunity to work with hundreds of different sites on their organic visibility. Working with many SMBs as well as companies such as State Farm Insurance, Extended Stay Hotels, McDonald’s, Kyocera, ClearWire, Business.com, Bumble Bee WD-40, GM, and others has afforded him the opportunity to experience a vast range of circumstances and challenges over the years.

Miguel has been featured in both print and online publications such as Visibility Magazine (print+online), DM News, E-commerce Times, iMedia Connections. Miguel has also been a speaker and keynote at various Internet Marketing events, in addition to running many webinars over the years.

Specialties: Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Link Building, Social Media Marketing, E-commerce, Comparison Shopping Engine Data Feed Optimization, Shopping Cart Optimization, and conversion optimization.
