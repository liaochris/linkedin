Erland Howden's Summary

I'm a passionate social change campaigner, currently leading financial and mobilisation strategies for Greenpeace Australia Pacific's coal and Great Barrier Reef campaign.

I've been a professional campaigner for over six years as well as running my own photography and graphic design business.

My campaigning expertise is in community organising, mass mobilisation, corporate and finance campaigning and political strategy. My photographic and design work crosses genres, but with an eye for clean, subject-driven, high-impact visuals.
