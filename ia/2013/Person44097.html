Farah Adams' Summary

Before returning to University to study law I was lucky enough to get a broad range of experience in the commercial/corporate world. There is nothing quite like working in the hospitality & tourism sector to hone your co-ordination, business and customer services skills, or the IT sector to sharpen your mind when it comes to using technology to work smarter and not harder, or in the Financial Services sector where on hindsight I learned how not to do things and to pay close attention to risk management, given today's economic climate! All of these skills have proved to be invaluable to me now as a solicitor dealing with and handling clients cases on a daily basis.

Specialties

Wills & Powers of Attorney, Property, Employment, Hospitality & Tourism Law, IT software expertise, former Council & Board member of the Law Society of Scotland. and convenor/member of various Law Society Committees.
