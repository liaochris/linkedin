Helen M. Trencher's Summary

My experience is eclectic. I have combined my formal training and development background, my Masters and Bachelors in education, and love of performing into consulting, performing, and educational experiences.

Currently, I am a self-employed creative educator and consultant who has researched, developed and provided a variety of programs and performances: theme based programs for children and families incorporating rhythm, story and song; creativity workshops for teachers and business professionals; therapeutically-based rhythm and hand drum programs for care centers; and targeted training design solutions using sound adult education principles.

In addition, I am a working actor who has appeared in local and regional commercials and independent films. And I have also researched, wrote and perform a one-hour living history on the early years of Golda Meir (a founding mother of the State of Israel), entitled: Golda Meir: Choices & Changes.

MY GOAL: To use my skills, education and creative focus to create/collaborate on new and engaging projects!

Specialties

training design/development/facilitation; hand drumming (Middle Eastern, African, Latin); acting and performing.
