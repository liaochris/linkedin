Wiser Now, Inc.

Public Company; Myself Only; Consumer Services industry

2003 – Present (10 years)

The hallmark of Wiser Now products is light-hearted practicality.

Our primary focus is having fun while building brain power through Brain Aerobics workshops and Brain Aerobics Weekly, an online, subscription-based, downloadable publication that offers trivia quizzes, word games, discussion topics, imaginative exercises and more.

Wiser Now had its beginnings in improving Alzheimer's care. Check the website for Kathy's monthly newsletter, Wiser Now Alzheimer's Disease Caregiver Tips, and two books on AD, Alzheimer's Basic Caregiving - an ABC Guide, and Activities of Daily Living - An ADL Guide for Alzheimer's Care. She also develops (and sometimes leads) multi-media training on a broad range of aging issues for clients in the US and Australia.

Kathy is also the author of Getting to Know the Life Stories of Older Adults and loves giving workshops on creative ways for people to quickly build trust and connect with one another -- while having fun, of course.
