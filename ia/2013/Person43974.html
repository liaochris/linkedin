Steve Davis' Summary

Own and operate FacilitatorU.com, a virtual university offering training, tools and resources for group leaders. I design and deliver live and virtual trainings in the areas of virtual facilitation, learning facilitation, meeting facilitation, facilitative presenting, and integral facilitation and collaboration. I train, coach, and assist people with their business and group leadership challenges, speakers who want to better engage their audiences, and trainers who want to be more facilitative.

Key Offerings:

Facilitation and Meeting Skills Training to help:
o Trainers be more facilitative and learner-focused.
o Leaders to be more collaborative.
o Speakers to be more engaging.
o Participants to be more involved.

Program Coordinator and Instructor

Coso Community College

January 1994 – January 2000 (6 years 1 month)

Coordinated and facilitated the development and delivery the Master Worker Project for underrepresented students.
Facilitated a collaborative process between business, industry, government, and education to develop a new curriculum involving 16 different workplace competencies that included: communication and conflict resolution skills, critical and creative thinking, problem solving, time management, stress management, workplace standards, and basic business principles.
A unique feature of the program involved its focus on "emotional mastery," which addressed assessment and development of personal qualities such as: self-esteem, respect, responsibility, integrity, teamwork, and motivation.
Completed design and delivery of three offerings of the curriculum to 70 students over a three-year period.
Researched and wrote two successful grant proposals to support new program efforts.
Documented the curriculum and collaborative process in a final report entitled, "The Master Worker Guidebook," and disseminated it to all 106 community colleges in California.
Received the 1997 "Innovation of the Year" Award from the League for Innovation.
Served as Master Worker Board Member, a community guidance, assessment, and certification team.
