Dyan Connolly, SPHR, CCP's Summary

I provide HR/OD/Talent Management leadership and consultation to business leaders and line managers on the strategic and people-related matters impacting their business. I work collaboratively with business leaders and managers to drive comprehensive and progressive organization development strategy by developing programs and tools to address the needs of the business and align employees with the culture, vision and objectives.

Main Responsibilities:

Business Partner - I master the business, diagnose people implications and recommend actions around people strategy. I serve as a key member of the leadership team to assist in driving business results.

Talent Management and Development - I work with client groups through the key talent programs of competency assessments, performance appraisals, succession and career planning and well as rewards planning and global workforce planning.

Change Agent and Org Development - I partner with and coach business leadership team to facilitate organization and culture change.

Leadership Team Effectiveness - I diagnose, design and facilitate leadership team interventions. I coach executives and coordinate coaching activities for leaders. I identify competencies required to improve business results and create plans to help employees to achieve these competencies.

Project management - I lead implementation of core HR programs into the business insuring an effective, sustainable people strategy over the long term.

HR Generalist - My work includes succession planning, employee relations, manager coaching, total rewards, organization capability and effectiveness, legal compliance, senior level recruiting, performance management and appraisal, training and career counseling.
