Eric Deniau's Summary

I have a broad experience of managing technical teams in the broadcast industry, with a strong background in software and systems engineering for TV & multimedia applications.

Combined with a sincere enthousiasm as well as a deep respect for human values, this gives me the ability to lead high-tech technical teams in international environments. Also, my sense of organisation as well as acknowledged abilities for team work allows me to establish and develop complex technical partnerships.

I am highly motivated by the engineering, setup and operation of complex and innovative products and systems in the domain of TV broadcast and multimedia.

Specialties

- Motivate and organize high tech teams towards ambitious goals
- Interactive TV, video processing and broadcast systems architecture
