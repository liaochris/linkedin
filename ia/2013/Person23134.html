  • 50 Hidden Gems of Greater Western New York

    • Pandamensional Solutions, Inc.
    • December 16, 2012
    Authors: Chris Carosa

    A handbook for those too proud to believe "wide right" and "no goal" define us.

    Discover the Secrets in Your Own Backyard.

    For too long the nation has dismissed the Greater Western New York region as a mere punch-line. That ends now. 50 Hidden Gems of Greater Western New York, in a light-hearted and entertaining manner, reveals some of the most delicious underexposed treasures our region offers. These people, places and events not only help define Greater Western New York, but have often helped define America as well. Don't, however, underestimate the power of this collection of witty stories. Rather than a mere historical review eulogizing the faded glory of what once was, 50 Hidden Gems of Greater Western New York take you on a expedition celebrating the rich character of our region. In doing so, the discerning reader will discover perhaps the most powerful hidden gem of them all: the key to Greater Western New York's future success.
    Along the Journey, You'll Discover:

    - A controversial early-American whose actions led directly to the creation of the U.S. Constitution.
    - Our region's claim to be the origin of America's favorite fast food (and, no, we're not talking Buffalo Wings).
    - The home of the Grand Canyon of the East.
    - The fascinating story of the mysterious Lost Tribe of Western New York.
    - A Rochester-born woman who holds this Hollywood first - that almost wasn't.
    - The truth behind the story of New York State's smallest town's eerie tragedy.
    - The time a small Western New York hamlet seceded from the Union - and its ensuing oversight.
    - How the bumbling British Monarchy helped mark the borders of Greater Western New York.

Who Are We? We are a Registered Investment Adviser headquartered in wonderful Western New York. We specialize in providing exclusive private independent investment counsel to individual trustees and fiduciaries. Established in 1996 by Christopher Carosa, CTFA & Gordon Stanton, we recently moved to our offices to Mendon, New York. A leader in using techniques derived from Behavioral Economics Theory to help diminish downside risk and reduce fiduciary liability, our principals offer veteran experience. We offer the Next Generation in Investment Management.

What Do We Do? We are the first & only regional investment adviser to employ techniques derived from Behavioral Economics Theory to help prestigious companies, organizations & individuals achieve their Lifetime Dreams and reduce their personal fiduciary liability. Because of the market’s volatile unpredictability, investment decisions concern most people. We help them answer two types of questions: First, we bring them closer to their desires by helping them discover what is most important to them. Second, we lead them away from tricks, traps & troubles by helping them avoid the Seven Deadly Sins of Professional Money Managers. We’ve created a personalized process that helps individuals and organizations think about – and answer – these questions in ways they have never before considered.

Why Should You Do Business With Us? By providing real value to you, we can best help you achieve your goals – whether they are achieving your Lifetime Dream or reducing your personal fiduciary liability (or both). Others have trusted us to do this for them and we feel strongly we can do it for you. We realize, before we can proceed, you need and must be comfortable with who we are – what our values are and how they support your desires. The end result is we truly believe we can help you achieve your goals. We have the expertise and the specialization you are seeking. We have demonstrated it for others & we can do it for you.
