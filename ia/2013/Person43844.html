Fabian Hernandez's Summary

► I've been working with 3d graphics since 2004 and focused in games from 2006 when I start my BS in Game Development, after that I even go further with a visual effects course, also I've been attending to the Game Developers Conference since 2006 to improve my skills and learn new technologies for the industry. I'm self-motivated with passion about every aspect of 3d graphics and game development.

► Environment Artist, Level Designer and Technical Artist have been my main jobs positions in two of the most important Game Studios of Mexico, I also freelance for international game and animation studios. Most of my work was: supervising the technical aspects of the 3d art to achieve the best quality and performance for the proyect, designing and implementing pipelines for the game art creation and integration with the game engine, learning new tools to improve the processes in production, serve as a bridge between diferent areas of the studio, creating 3d models, effects, shaders / materials, lighting and textures for the games, design technical solutions for the game and trying to achieve the target look and feel for every project. I have experience with Unreal, Cryengine, Gamebryo Lightspeed and Unity so I can adapt quickly to new tools and requirements.

► From a 3d model to an art pipeline design I got a big picture of game development, 3d art creation and realtime 3d graphics processes, with a strong technical background in 3d graphics and videogames technology, this multi role give me the flexibility to work in many levels of production. Strong solve-problem and comunication skills with focus in team work are my strengths. I can work under presure, tight timelines and complex projects. I'm always looking to improve myself.
