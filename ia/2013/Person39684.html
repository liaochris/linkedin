Edouard Gomez-Vaez's Summary

I am currently looking for a business partner in order to (re)found a B2B/B2C startup around collaborative consumption (upcycling, crowd-funding...) : one having online marketing or business development skills, and ideally at least six years of experience in a pure-player company or a position close to the web activities. Expected to be willing to invest at least 15 months with no remuneration needs. And a good presentation !

I found Wiithaa ("Let's remake it!") in 2012, an upcycling social network enabling designers to offer their products, projects and workshops to people and companies interested in re-using objects without paying the full price of recycling.

I worked as the software development team lead at Wonderbox after having worked as project manager for PriceMinister. I proposed and implemented there agile methodologies such as Scrum, starting this early as 2008.

Previously, I had specialized within Accenture in technical architectures and then grown into management roles.

In the middle of my Accenture experience, I took a nine-month leave-of-absence to pursue two semesters of university level industrial design classes. I learned how to draw, design, and define new uses for everyday domains.

I am interested in activities where I can use and develop my competencies in consultancy, technology and design – in the broader sense of the word: how to find new solutions fitting loosely defined briefs.
