Dr Wayne Nowland's Summary

I have held senior-level Scientific Research, Technology Management, Executive Management and Corporate Board Appointments in a variety of industries throughout Australia and North America over the past three decades, and presently manage my own Strategic Research & Consultancy Business.

Some notable achievements include:

1. Creating the system design concept for a Canadian Government Multipurpose UHF Satellite System while working as a Research Scientist in the late 1970’s at the Canadian National Communications Research Centre in Ottawa.

2. Pioneering the planning, procurement management, launch and operation of cutting edge Space & Ground Segment Satellite Communications Systems, for Australia’s National Satellite Operator (AUSSAT: 1980-1987).

3.Managing AUSSAT's commercial business operations and strategy development prior to its sale to Optus Communications (1987-1992).

4. Leading the development of the business plan and international joint venture arrangements in 1994 that led to the Optus Board approving the rollout of Australia’s first interactive broadband network for carriage of Telephony, Pay TV and future broadband services.

5. Spearheading the introduction of Broadband Internet services in Australia through pre-commercial trials on the Optus Broadband Network to homes in northern Sydney during 1996.

6. Being publicly recognised during the 1980’s and 1990’s as a leading Australian authority and pioneer in space systems technology and satellite communications, one of Australia’s top 50 movers and shakers in Australian IT&T, and one of Australia’s Information Superhighway Pioneers.

7. Providing strategic business consulting for start-up ventures during the past 15 years, including business planning, financial modeling, strategy development, corporate mentoring, executive management, and board membership.

8. Performing scientific research, technology evaluation and web publishing services for business clients and entrepreneurs since 2009.
