Clinical Director, Dual Diagnosis Partial Hospitalization Program

Westwood Lodge Hospital

January 2012 – Present (2 years) Westwood, MA

Leadership of a comprehensive outpatient hospital program serving patients with psychiatric and addiction diagnoses.

Program Description:
The Dual Diagnosis PHP program is a short-term, intensive, outpatient treatment program which meets Monday-Friday from 10a.m.-3 p.m. An Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) is also offered for those not meeting PHP level of care, with less flexible schedules, and fewer hours available for daily treatment. IOP patients attend for half-day sessions. PHP includes:
• Psychopharmacology evaluation and management
• Comprehensive dual diagnosis programming and case management by masters level clinical professionals
• Educational and therapeutic specialties encompassing a wide range of recovery topics from relapse prevention to mood management, coping skills, crisis/safety planning and more
• Self-empowerment, strengths-based therapy model, tailored specifically for group members and designed on a daily basis to meet client-stated needs
• Creative group designs varied to accommodate diverse learning styles
• Family sessions
• Random toxicology screening
