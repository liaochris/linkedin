Jiang-Ming Yang's Summary

Jiang-Ming Yang is Development Director of Commerce & Identity Platform of Electronic Arts. Commerce & Identity Platform of Electronic Arts is one of the largest platform to power hundreds of games played by hundreds of millions of gamers, including FIFA, Medal of Honor, Battlefield, Origin, Play4free and so on. The commerce & identity platform is responsible for providing foundation services to game studios including identity management, authorization and single sign on, account, subscription, billing, payment , virtual currency, provisioning, entitlement, risk and fraud at 24x7 with 99.99% reliability.

Before joining Electronic Arts in 2012, Jiang-Ming Yang was a Senior Software Development Lead in Test of Commerce Platform (CP) group of Microsoft Corporation. The platform provides the account management, billing and subscription management, reporting for over 180M customers and 80M transactions/day of the popular Microsoft online service including Xbox live online, Windows Phone, Windows Store (Windows 8), Hotmail, Bing, Azure, O365 etc. His work is leading SDET engineers on infrastructure of Platform, designing and driving the infrastructure changes.

He was with Web Search & Mining Group (WSM) of Microsoft Research Asia from 2007 to 2009. His previous research interests includes user-created content analysis and lock-free consistency maintenance. He has published over 30 papers in premier academic conferences including WWW, SIGIR, SIGKDD, SIGGROUP, MM, CIKM. He has also filed over 10 us/international patents.

He received the M.S. and B.S. degree in Computer Science from Fudan University, China, in 2004 and 2007, respectively.
