Alisa Oyler's Summary

Group facilitator, organizational consultant, curriculums / manuals designer and skilled trainer with fifteen years of experience working in the humanitarian sector, from small community-based groups to large international institutions. Driven by a commitment to create inclusive and just social systems, with expertise in the Technology of Participation (ToP) methodology and practical approaches to participatory learning, planning, and decision-making.

Specialties

participatory planning, training design, capacity building system design, multi-stakeholder process management, participatory strategic planing, peacebuilding program design, negotiation and communication, coaching and mentoring, PRA, PLA, systems change, ToP, program management
