Dwain M. DeVille's Summary

Since 1994 I’ve worked with over a hundred start-up and growing companies developing a keen awareness of the pitfalls the entrepreneurs who run them encounter on their road to success.

My innovative Navigation Process provides entrepreneurs with a down and dirty, tattooed guide to getting where they want to go in business. It plays a critical role in helping companies – small and large – overcome the challenges of building a successful business.

My practical, hands-on approach helps entrepreneurs deal with issues within their own style, culture and timetable. I help leaders recognize problem areas and coach them to improve their critical thinking skills and decision making – key elements to successful execution.

Easy-to-implement, the Navigation Process helps Business Leaders to:

Gain that all important competitive edge, set their sights higher, solve problems faster and achieve their business goals in less time.

Focus on the right direction for success in business AND life. They aren’t mutually exclusive.

Develop winning tactics that turn your business into a high-performance profit machine.

Harness the power of their team and turn them into business leaders who ‘think for themselves’.

Enhance their communication and drive their business into the fast lane.

My client list covers the spectrum of business from small enterprises to major corporations, (including the Fortune 100) to major universities.

I advise CEOs and their companies through one-on-one coaching,high-level mastermind groups, 3-D Navigator online workshops, and The Retreat On Wheels, my mastermind program for entrepreneurs who also ride.

While much of my work focuses on helping entrepreneurs and small business owners, my work for larger corporations includes team building, succession planning, mergers and acquisitions, and communication issues.

If that describes you or someone you know, check out www.DwainDeVille.com or email me at DMD@DwainDeVille.com for more information.

Specialties

Business Strategy and Tactics, CEO Coaching, Business Planning, Mentoring, Leadership Training, Business Growth, Group Facilitation, Motorcycling, Social Media and Professional Speaking.

“The insight and guidance I’ve received is invaluable. As result of working with Dwain my revenue doubled. It’s the most significant strategic business relationship I have!!!” - G. Mears, CEO

WaterMark serves as a vital resource center to the leaders of business today. It’s built a unique business community where world-class resources in areas such as business coaching, leadership development, business strategy, tactics and planning provide business leaders with the tools necessary to compete in a rapidly-changing, highly-competitive marketplace.

As a key player in the many businesses I work with, clients rely on me to bring a no-nonsense perspective and expertise that adds value to their organizations. My ‘roll-up-your-sleeves’ style goes well beyond the typical consultant’s role and has earned the respect of CEO’s and other executives in large and small organizations alike.

Entrepreneurs must approach issues in their own style and I help key players in the organization to become more effective in daily execution and communication – hallmarks of high-performance and profitability.

Clients come to me for expertise in leadership development, transition planning, financial management, mergers and acquisitions, high-impact communications, and executable business plans – the linchpin to an entrepreneur’s ability to achieve their vision of success.

My Navigation Process provides business leaders with this crucial road map. Similar to a strategic plan, it focuses on the end goal, but also provides the critical tactics and tools for successfully managing the trials and tribulations between ‘here’ and ‘there’.

As a published author and dedicated reader of business theory, my clients also count on me to translate cutting-edge business theory into practical, easy-to-implement tools.

The Retreat On Wheels

Public Company; Myself Only; Management Consulting industry

April 1994 – Present (19 years 5 months)

"The environment is exactly what the doctor ordered for corporate executives to be able to roll up their sleeves, exchange ideas and provide the stimulus needed to conquer adversity and excel."- Dave S., CEO and Biker

10 riders - 3 days - 1 open road = A Full Throttle Business Experience!

I’ve hatched some of my most creative business solutions while on the bike. Don’t know exactly how it works, but it does.

That’s why I’m looking for an elite group of entrepreneurs, who want to hit the road with me, get some wind in their face, clear their heads and come back with no BS answers to running their businesses like they own em!

This three-day business workshop unites top-level executives, who love to riding for learning sessions, networking opportunities and group rides. Open to business owners and C-level managers, the retreat offers a rare opportunity for a full throttle business experience.

Peer-to-peer exchange is one of the best ways to brainstorm new ideas, and getting some head-clearing seat time on new roads is better than looking at pie charts in a conference room!

We’ll drop the kickstands and combine talents to hone our competitive edge, set goals higher, solve problems faster and succeed quicker.

It’s a fresh perspective and change of pace in a dynamic environment where participants can come together to share knowledge and skills about the challenges of growing a business.
