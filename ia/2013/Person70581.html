Julian Grenfell's Experience

Director and Owner

JK EW Consultancy Limited

Defense & Space industry

December 2010 – Present (2 years 8 months) Sleaford, United Kingdom

EWOS is one of those terms that means "all things to all men"; though not exhaustive EWOS can be said to comprise EW data management, the analysis of the data, mission data set (MDS) design, build, and test, electronic countermeasure (ECM) design, build, and test, in the RF and electro-optics and infrared (EOIR) parts of the electro-magnetic spectrum. JK EW Consultancy Limited has a track record of EWOS project work for customers in, and outside, the UK.

JK EW Consultancy Limited offers consultancy in matters EWOS to defence OEMs and defence organisations in the UK and overseas.

EW training is also an important underpinning of EWOS. JK EW Consultancy has a portfolio of basic and advanced courses in topics ranging from EW (EOIR and non-communications RF) through to EW data management, receivers, countermeasure techniques, missile guidance, war, doctrine, concepts of operation (CONOPS), and EW effectiveness assessment.
