Benecia Ponder's Experience

Speaker, Author, Personal and Executive Coach

Benecia Ponder Enterprises

Public Company; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

March 2006 – Present (7 years 8 months) Greater Atlanta Area

Benecia Ponder Coaching supports high-powered, ambitious leaders as they pursue even greater levels of success. I support corporate executives, attorneys, doctors, service professionals, sales managers, entrepreneurs, entertainers, and other community influencers in their decision to live more passionate, purposeful, and powerful lives.

My clients reap the benefits of the depth of my professional expertise and personal life experiences. As a former attorney and business development strategist, I offer my clients a thought-provoking and strategic approach to facing personal and business challenges. After overcoming life’s setbacks to achieve high levels of personal and professional success of my own, I am able to provide my clients with the keen insight of someone who’s “been there”. Trained by the Coaching and Positive Psychology Institute, I utilize the cutting-edge techniques and tools provided by the research and evidence-based field of positive psychology.

My coaching focuses on sustainable results that can be replicated over a lifetime. I help my clients align their lives with their core vision, develop strategies for attaining greater levels of success, while providing the accountability and support they need to stay motivated. In addition to operating at peak performance, my clients gain the emotional intelligence they need to enjoy their success.
