Marjorie Kaufman, CFA's Summary

Asset management and investment banking executive with over 25 years experience in asset raising, marketing and investor relations. I specialize in raising institutional assets and helping asset managers evolve through development of infrastructure and marketing/client service platforms.

During the past ten years I've raised over $5 billion in hedge fund capital for long/short equity, macro and credit strategies. My successful track record in raising capital is attributable to long-standing relationships with institutional investors and consultants; deep understanding and analysis of a strategy's investment approach, risk elements, liquidity and performance attributes; and executing strategic marketing plans to effectively reach consultants and investors.

Through my roles on management and operating committees, I've participated in the design and implementation of risk management and portfolio reporting systems, compliance protocols, fund terms and policies, and disaster recovery plans. I've also led initiatives to institutionalize operations, reporting and compliance for hedge funds and traditional asset managers.

My background in M&A and LBO at Bankers Trust and Pru-Bache as well as my experience in the asset management business help foster informed and sophisticated relationships with institutional investors and fund managers.
