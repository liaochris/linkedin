Chris Morena's Experience

Owner

Mostunts Entertainment LLC

2007 – Present (6 years)

Mostunts Entertainment strives to entertain with safe and top quality auto stunts within your budget. We have teamed up with Tim Chitwood from the world famous "Joie Chitwood Thrill Show". From car jumps to stunt driving, speedways to fairs and more, our team is dedicated and experienced to deliver a custom show for you.

Performer

Mostunts Entertainment performs at various dates for the Crash A Rama Series. We bring in our 2 wheel stunt driving act, high speed reverse spins and a car jump or pipe ramp. We have teamed up for several years and have plans to expand bringing in more stunts to future Crash A Rama performances.

Auto Stunts

Wheels Of Destruction Thrill Show

June 2012 – August 2012 (3 months) Myrtle Beach Speeday

Every Wednesday during the summer Myrtle Beach Speedway hosts "Wheels of Destruction Thrill Show" during June, July & August. MoStunts Entertainment and Tim Chitwood perform 4 auto stunts during the 2 1/2 hour thrill show including a car roll and the show finale a 150' + car jump. The show also includes drifting, FMX, street bike stunts, bus racing, skid plate racing and more.
