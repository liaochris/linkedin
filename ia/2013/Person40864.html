Ellie Cook-Venezia's Summary

Ellie Cook-Venezia is a strategic business partner who has wide business | professional interests and is accomplished in many areas. This Renaissance professional evolved through experience in management, admin, business / marketing / staffing plans, finance, pricing strategy, marketing, social media, public relations, web 2.0, trade show preparation and human resources. While conducting a personal branding mini-survey Ellie contacted over 50 of her professional colleagues. Asking them to supply her with a few words that came to mind when they saw or heard her name, top responses were energetic, organized, smart and professional.

Ellie keeps her finger on the pulse of her community by being a member of the Greater Monadnock SHRM chapter, and was seated on their Membership Committee for 8 years. In 2010 she was seated for a 2-year term on their BoD as a Board Member at Large. She is seen at Chamber of Commerce functions and local fundraisers. In 2013 Ellie was recruited onto the Advisory Committee of Healthy Monadnock 2020.

Ellie is frequently contacted by friends and colleagues for assistance regarding social media marketing, inclusive of LinkedIn®. She is moderator of the GMSHRM LI Group and most recently was hired by River Valley Community College to run several social media workshops for their WorkReadyNH Club.

Ms. Cook-Venezia resides with her husband, Edward Venezia, and Alazhay their golden retriever in a rural area outside of Keene, NH.
