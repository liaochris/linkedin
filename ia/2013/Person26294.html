Colin Dyer's Summary

Colin is President and Chief Executive Officer of Jones Lang LaSalle Incorporated, the leading global real estate services and investment management firm. JLL helps its clients create value by owning, occupying and investing in real estate. The firm has more than 200 offices worldwide and operates in more than 1,000 locations in 70 countries.

Colin has overall responsibility for guiding JLL’s strategic direction and growth. He also chairs the Global Executive Committee, the firm’s senior internal management committee. And he is a member of the Jones Lang LaSalle Board of Directors.

Since he joined JLL in 2004, the firm’s revenues have more than tripled – to $ 3.9 billion in 2012 – through organic growth, nearly 50 strategic acquisitions across the globe, and the addition of 100 offices and thirty new countries to its geographic footprint.

JLL provides management and real estate outsourcing services to a property portfolio of 2.6 billion square feet. The firm completed $63 billion in sales, acquisitions and finance transactions in 2012. LaSalle Investment Management, its investment management business, has $47.7 billion of real estate assets under management.
