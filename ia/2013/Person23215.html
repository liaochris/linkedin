Chris Denina's Summary

This newspaper reporter turned corporate communicator can do it all, whether it’s designing a poster, drafting a companywide e-mail announcement or writing and shooting a funny video skit.

In a sense, he’s a marketing department of one. He’s part copywriter, part graphic designer. Part web manager, part project manager. Part photographer, part videographer.

He loves figuring out ways to put a fresh spin on the same old message and learning new things, especially in the areas of internal communications, public relations and marketing.

He also enjoys building websites, taking pictures and, as a former humor writer, referring to himself in the third person.

Specialties:
♦ Web: Builds websites, organizes content and creates multimedia.
♦ Writing, reporting and editing: Crafts original content, following the Associated Press Stylebook.
♦ Graphic design: Designs posters, website banners and digital signage.
♦ Video: Writes scripts, and shoots and edits video.
♦ Photography: Shoots events and makes portraits.
