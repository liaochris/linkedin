Jonathan Ball's Summary

All aspects relating to the application of Geographical Information Systems, with a particular interest in Landscape Planning, research and 3D visualization.

Over 15 years experience in GIS, particularly in the ESRI 'ArcGIS' environment including the 3D Analyst, Spatial Analyst and Model Builder extensions. Extensive experience of producing robust, automated geo-processing scripts using ESRI's Python API.

Extensive experience of renewable energy (windfarms in particular) including site specific work using 'WindFarm' and 'WindPro' relating to EIAs and large-scale strategic analysis using GIS.

Experience of project and line management (including Scrum and Kanban) in a various multi-disciplinary commercial environments.

Additional experience in GDAL/OGR, PostGIS, Eclipse (especially PyDev), Perforce, Jira, Blender, Photoshop, GIMP.

Specialties

Developing bespoke and novel solutions to geographical rules-based modelling and spatial analysis plus 3D visualisation of geographic data.
