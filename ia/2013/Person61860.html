Jennifer Karin's Summary

Jennifer Karin is a marketing communications expert and award-winning writer with over 25 years experience in a variety of industries, including consumer goods, healthcare, high technology, and not-for-profit.

She has developed many business-building programs for successful national and international brands, including Apple Computer, Boston Popcorn, Guest Quarters Suite Hotels, Hewlett-Packard, Home Portfolio, Malden Mills Polarfleece, Zoesis Studios, Seven Hills Foundation, and Motorola.

Jennifer is also an author of four books, and is currently polishing her fifth.
