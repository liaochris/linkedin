Evan Klassen's Summary

Entrepreneur-Author-Singer-Show Host-Network Marketing Professional

Born in the mid 80’s in Asia as 1 of 9 kids, Evan learned early on to be responsible and appreciate life. Growing up in the world of poverty and civil war, helped him to learn about the opposition this life has to offer. Clean water and bread were not always available and at times his family’s life was in jeopardy due to the intensive circumstances of war.

Evan grew up in a blue color family. His family moved back to Germany where he started working for himself at the age of 11 and started developing his dream for entrepreneurship and music.

At age 21 he was introduced to Network Marketing and fell in love with the opportunity for personal growth and financial independence. To date Evan has invested over 120 thousand dollars into Books, Seminars, Events, Audios Trainings and Personal Coaching. He believes that the best investment you can make is an investment in yourself. In 2006 he moved to the United States where he started a Real Estate Business. Through many struggles and persistence he grew the business to over $22 Million Dollars in Revenue in a period of 4.5 years.

His passion for music and business gave Evan the opportunity to travel over 20 different countries and allowed him to touch Ten’s of thousands of lives. His excitement and love for people brought him back into Network Marketing in 2008 where he built a sales organization with over 14 thousand people in 23 countries.

Currently Evan is leading the creation of a revolutionary organization with the purpose to inspire people to grow and reach their maximum potential in life through Entrepreneurship and Personal Development.

Beginning of 2013 the “Immigrant2Millionaire” Show was born,to inspire Entrepreneurs Worldwide.

Evan had his ups and downs He even went personally through depression. Today Evan shares his story of Failure and Success and inspires people all over the world.

Evan gives glory to God for any of his achievements.
