Heather Fonseca's Summary

- As a doll designer I work with toy companies on a freelance basis to create new fashion dolls and other girls toys.

- I specialize in fun, fabulous, fashion illustrations, creating both digital illustrations on Adobe Photoshop and traditional hand-painted drawings.

 - As owner and creator of "The Style Confessions", a fashion blog, I create all editorial content, both visual and written. I also engage in various social media content and have a broad knowledge of social media strategies.

Specialties: Fashion illustration. Doll Design. Style Blogging.

Heather Fonseca's Experience

Doll Designer and Fashion Illustrator

Heather Fonseca Design Studio

March 2003 – Present (10 years 8 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

I create innovative new toy concepts as well as design and illustrate head-to-toe looks for existing doll lines. I have provided illustration and toy design services to numerous clients including MGA, Jakks Pacific, Spinmaster, Playmates Toys and more. I also collaborating with Funosophy to invent the Pony Royale toy line.

See all my designs and illustrations please seem by website at http://heatherfonseca.com

Fashion Blogger

Instructor

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Retail industry

September 2010 – June 2012 (1 year 10 months)

As a member of the faculty at Sewing Arts Center in Santa Monica I teach students how to choose and make basic alterations to commercial patterns, understand the qualities of different fabrics, assemble a quality sewing kit, become familiar with the home sewing machine, and sew garments.
