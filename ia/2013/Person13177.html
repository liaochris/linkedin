Ben Morrow's Summary

With experience in software, hardware, and design, Ben is uniquely suited to build businesses focused on stellar user experiences.

He has co-founded several companies including Morrow Lawncare, ViVX design, GreenGrid, 5th Fleet, and Liquid Enterprises. In the 12-member Global Investment Club he participates in growth stock trading decisions. He received a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Arts and Technology from the University of Texas at Dallas, and he also completed the University's rigorous pre-med curriculum. He had the honor to be the sole student speaker at the undergraduate commencement ceremony and studied business and culture abroad in China, Tibet, and India.
