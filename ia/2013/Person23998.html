“Think Sales 101” workshop provides insights for the entry-level, skilled trade, executive, young, and seasoned professionals.

For First Time Entry Into A Sales Career:
The workshop is a perfect introduction to what sales is all about. Many existing and new companies are looking for qualified sales representatives to promote, demonstrate, and sell their products or services.
By having an understanding of what sales is all about, you will have the confidence to properly present yourself to a potential employer and to sell your abilities.

For The Professional Experienced Sales Person:
Even the most experienced sales professional can develop poor habits or forget what they learned, that got them to where they are.
Sales techniques and customers have changed in today’s market. The one constant we can always count on is: “change.”
Knowing that it is currently an employer’s market, with many job seekers to choose from, employers re-evaluate their staff. They are only interested in keeping representatives who profitably contribute to their company’s bottom line, along with those who help to build their product’s reputation and distribution.

For The Business Owner / Entrepreneur:
As a business owner, or potential new start-up, you may be your company’s sole sales representative. You may have a professional sales & marketing staff. In either situation, it is most important to be on top of up-to-date sales and marketing techniques.
Business owners need to be more familiar with the sales process, no matter what their product or service is.

Great sales, marketing, and business techniques can be implemented in your business, everyday life, and your relationships.
