Catherine Rocheleau's Summary

As a leadership and change strategist, I coach & consult with business leaders and business owners who are stuck, frustrated or working too hard for mediocre results. My team and I help businesses establish the structures & systems that drive business results!

I've faced challenges of growth, change and redirection during my 20 years as a business owner, business leader and as a non-profit executive. This experience and on-going learning and professional development as a business and executive coach, organizational change professional provides the foundation to our products and services.

I truly believe businesses and not-for-profits can achieve sustainable leadership if they balance people, profit and planet (the triple bottom line). Balance the competing priorities of head, heart and ego present a major challenge for many. I help resolve your own conflicts and identify positive action plans with measurable results.

As part of our More Than Green (TM) Program, 5% of all sales are donated to the "Girls to School" project, established in partnership with the Create Change Foundation.
