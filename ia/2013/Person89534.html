Matt Planer's Summary

I have been working in sales for over 13 years. I have always been very competitive by nature, which has led to great success in my field. I have worked for two major companies, one as on online product specialist and the other as a automotive/major account executive. As silly as it sounds I really enjoy marketing and advertising which is what led me to started my own company Media By Matt. However, I also focus on website development advertising consulting and social media management.
