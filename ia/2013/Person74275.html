Ken Lopez's Summary

I'm a photographer based out of Orlando, Florida.

I like to tell stories through pictures, and I enjoy working with people on location. I strive to convey a certain level of depth in my images. No matter what, the emotion has to real, because that's what makes or breaks an image for me.

I have a range of experience in Commercial/Advertising, Editorial, and Public Relations photography.

Whether it's being thrown into a live situation that I have no control over, or creating an image from scratch, I work my hardest to create the most compelling images that I can.

I love this job =o)

I also proudly serve as a Leading Chief Petty Officer in the United States Navy Reserve. I work with a great group of individuals that come together and support an Active Duty command based in Charleston, SC.

Specialties

Editorial Photography, Public Relations, Stock Photography, Advertising Photography, US Navy Reserve
