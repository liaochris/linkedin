Chong Kim's Projects

  • Survivors Connect

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Chong Kim, Stella Marr, Rebecca Mott, Bridget Perrier, Natasha Falle, Holly Smith

    A leaderless international online network for the survivors of trafficking/prostitution. Joining our voices makes us stronger and harder to ignore. We come together for support, friendship and activism. We are sisters and survivors. Nothing will break the bonds between us. We are Survivors Connect.

  • Street Dreams documentary

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Chong Kim, Mike Crowhurst, Jason Bray, Liam Griffin, Tony Kirwan, Heidi Rurade, Tanya Dahlenburg, marlene alastra, Katie Wallis, Aaron Nebauer

    Fresh, original, deeply confronting and ultimately inspiring, Street Dreams has been described as a benchmark documentary in the area of human trafficking. Through harrowing interviews with underage sex slaves and undercover behind-the-scenes footage in filthy brothels, Jason Bray and Mike Crowhurst tear down preconceptions of some of the regions’ most infamous party districts to reveal an industry that thrives on manipulation and destruction. Very few films can capture the raw emotion and heartache of modern day slavery as intimately and as powerfully as this.

    • New Hope Publishing
