Katrina Wrobleski, Assoc. IIDA's Summary

Creative and technical interior designer with experience in all project phases, from programming to contract administration. Highly organized, with a strong work ethic and flexibility in office environments has led to the successful completion of complex projects including Chicago Marriott Downtown Magnificent Mile and Skipjack's Seafood Emporium at Patriot Place.

Currently on the path to acquire LEED Accreditation and NCIDQ Certification.

Construction Documentation, Purchasing & Procurement, Material Specification, 2D Hand and Computer Drafting, 3D Computer Modeling, Computer Rendering, Computer Animation, Hand Rendering, Typography, Logo Design, Layout Design.

Software expertise in the following: AutoCAD 2004 - 2011, Autodesk Architectural Desktop 2004 - 2006, VIZ Render 2004 - 2006, CAP 2020, SketchUp, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere & Dreamweaver, Microsoft Word, Excel, & Powerpoint
