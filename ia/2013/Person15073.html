Mike Shipman's Summary

I'm a full time freelance photographer and writer, a former wildlife biologist.

My photography focus through Mike Shipman Photography (mikeshipman.com) is commercial (annual reports, corporate & model/actor portraits, interior/exterior, products, industrial), editorial (environmental, agriculture, travel, general interest), and stock photography.

Through Blue Planet Photography (blueplanetphoto.com) I offer photography classes, workshops, private one-on-one instruction world wide, and fine art commissioned portraits and decor prints.

I can travel anywhere in the world. I love what I do and enjoy working/collaborating with clients.

I'm an Idaho Commission on the Arts Teaching Artist, available for residencies in Idaho schools and with Idaho nonprofit organizations.

I'm available for group presentations.
