Ainsley McDougal's Summary

Professionally I have a varied background in General IT and graphics design. Over the years I've honed my skills as a Project Manager, IT Consultant, and Designer.

Currently, I'm studying to earn my Project Management Professional (PMP) designation.

As co-founder of Digital Pure, I oversee all projects, social media coaching for clients, and website development.

Over the years I've created graphic designs for a variety of groups, but there are two particular projects of which I'm particularly fond. First, it would have to be designing the logo and clothing worn by the bronze medal winning U.S. Bobsled team during the 2002 Olympics. Second, would be having the opportunity to design a commemorative beer stein for a government workgroup at a "super secret" agency. While these two projects were very different in nature, they were incredibly fun and were a great exercise in "thinking outside the box" to meet each clients' needs.

On a personal note, I am a voracious reader of classic fiction (Tolstoy, Asimov, Dickens), non-fiction, business books, poetry and anything that piques my interest. My hobbies are: cooking (gluten-free), photography, and learning new languages-currently learning French.

Specialties: Design skills for print, web, media and logo. Corporate branding. Social media. SEO. WordPress website design, customization and implementation. Project Management.
