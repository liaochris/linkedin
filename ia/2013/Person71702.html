adam kahn's Summary

Adam has been creating advertising since 2006. Before advertising he was groomed by film, tv, and interior design companies. Adam focuses on cultivating ideas to be medium agnostic and working with creatives to think about big ideas beyond 30 seconds.

This year Adam took home a Webby's award for "Best Mobile game of the year" for The Adventures of Timmy: Run Kitty Run as well as 5 nominations.

Adam's client list includes: Tanqueray, Ketel One, Captain Morgan, Crown Royal, Nokia, Hugo Boss, Pringles, Hess, Grey, Mercedes-Benz, Dairy Queen, Walmart, Banana Boat, Electus, etc.
