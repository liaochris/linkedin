Kathleen M Raven's Summary

After working in daily newspapers, Kathleen M. Raven returned to the University of Georgia to pursue graduate degrees in conservation ecology (M.S.) and health and medical journalism (M.A.). Kathleen is especially interested in using her multimedia skills to cover public and mental health, sustainable agriculture and climate change. Kathleen holds an A.B.J. from Grady College and has won fellowships from the National Academy of Sciences Keck Futures Initiative and the Online Journalism Association, as well as two first-place awards from the North Carolina Press Association.

Specialties

Researching, writing, investigative reporting, still and video photography
