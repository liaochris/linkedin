Kevin Briody's Summary

Digital marketing professional with a deep background across technology and product marketing, digital and social media, on teams ranging from startups and non-profits to agencies and multinationals. Particularly fascinated with digital engagement strategy - the unique blend of disciplines that leverages paid, earned, and owned (content marketing) media to drive business success for brands.

Currently working on digital, content, platform and engagement strategies for Pace in Greensboro, NC.

Previously at Ignite Social Media, Microsoft, a non-profit, and three startups - 1 crashed hard, 1 still kicking, and 1 successful exit.

Specialties: Digital and social media marketing strategy, digital engagement strategy, influencer engagement, online/offline community marketing, innovation, product marketing, marketing communications.
