Harald Hynell's Experience

Senior Product Designer

Bosign AB

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Consumer Goods industry

2004 – Present (9 years) Stockholm, Sweden

Harald is Bosign’s founding designer. His passion for form, construction, material, and innovative product solutions are key to the Bosign’s design philosophy. He finds his inspiration in the physical and emotional aspects of design; how a product feels in your hands or still impacts you after several years’ use are key to his sculptural and holistic approach. He has designed several groundbreaking products for Bosign, including the Surfpillow and the Laptray. His fascination with product design is infectious and he tackles design with humility. Nothing pleases him more than walking into a store and picking up a product that provides a brilliantly simple solution, is elegantly designed and constructed to last.
