James Innes' Summary

I'm the Founder and Chief Executive of The CV Centre (UK, Ireland, New Zealand and South Africa), The Resume Center (USA) and The Resume Centre (Canada and Australia) – the world's leading CV and resume consultancies.

With 15 years' experience heading up these organisations, I have a wealth of expertise in employment, jobs and careers matters.

I regularly participate at recruitment fairs and conferences as a guest speaker, as well as working on TV and radio. I am also the author of five best-selling careers books - ‘The CV Book’, ‘The Interview Book’, 'The Interview Question & Answer Book', ‘The Cover Letter Book’ and 'Ultimate New Job'.
