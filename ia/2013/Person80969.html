Linda Bucher's Summary

Hi, I’m Linda Bucher. Once upon a time I was where you are. Now, on the other side of great adversity and change, I’ve found my purpose in guiding and teaching others.

As a master certified life coach I help people get clarity, align with their vision, conquer resistance, shift perspective, plan a path and make dreams a reality. Most significantly, I help them heal.

As a coach mentor, I help professionals become clear and confident, enhance their client relationships, augment their coaching skills and grow their businesses into what they dream they could be.
