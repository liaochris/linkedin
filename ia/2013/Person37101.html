Douglas Barone's Experience

Writer

Writer

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Writing and Editing industry

September 2008 – Present (5 years)

I'm a writer living in New York City, just blocks from where I grew up in Greenwich Village. I have a B.A. from St. Lawrence and a graduate degree from Loyola University. I've studied literature at The New School for Social Research and writing in The New School's writing program (with Sharon Mesmer, Sidney Offit, Josh Gaylord, Joseph Salvatore, Robert Boyers and Justus Rosenberg). In 2009 and 2010, I attended Pulitzer Prize winner Phil Schultz's Writers Studio, and for four years now, since 2010, I've been selected to study with Rick Moody at the New York State Writers Institute. I'm now one of Rick's private students.
