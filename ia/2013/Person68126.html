Jon Waddell's Summary

Jon Waddell is a technologist and executive with extensive experience across multiple industries and technology disciplines with 20 plus years of experience. He has worked with startup organizations and established Fortune 500 companies bringing strong leadership and vision skills to both. Jon’s core skill is to work together with his customer and solve whatever challenge lies before them. His architecture expertise goes across today’s platforms and multiple disciplines including the latest cloud technologies.
