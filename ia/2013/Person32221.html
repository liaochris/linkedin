David Mantelman's Summary

David Mantelman began his professional career at Impact Sales & Marketing, Inc. as an outside sales representative. Within 14 months he had moved his way up through the ranks of the company to become the Director of Sales & Marketing. As the director he pioneered many new brand strategies that allowed the company achieve over 500% growth in sales in 3 short years.

A new opportunity presented itself to David in the fall of 2009 as he shifted gears into the home health care industry. David brought with him a wealth of knowledge that he had gained at Impact Sales & Marketing and parlayed that into immediate success as the Vice President and Director of Market Development.

As Sienna Home Care, Inc. continues to grow David hopes to help establish them as an industry leader and a fixture in the community.
