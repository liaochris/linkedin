David Bembry's Summary

I've worked as a general contractor as well as maintaining a 40hr a week job as an operating engineer / painter for the better part of the last 35 years. I've practiced all of the building trades at one time or another. I'm also an entrepreneur and have several businesses online which include a record label and a web design entity. I've been working online since the mid 90's. I've been known online as the internet R&B artist Chivalry for just over 15 years.

Just a few years ago when Microsoft introduced it's hosting services, Bembry Business Online was founded. I looked at what Microsoft was doing and logically figured that they would at some point want newly set-up businesses to showcase. In short, I put together what I'd learned over the years into a site that would show others just how simply and easily anyone could set-up a business online with little to no cost. I used the latest in web design templates, created the business and set it up in the next 48 hours. Microsoft did notice and I was selected as one of the first to be showcased. I impressed the the Giant! I was scheduled for a video shoot but soon after I was contacted by a representative (Dean Nicolls) who informed me that upon closer inspection, that they saw me more as their competition! The video was cancelled. I wasn't really disappointed at all. The reality was that a single broke Dad sitting in his bedroom had intimidated Microsoft! Good One! : )

I majored in computer sciences at Wayne State back in 79' and have always been into technology and trends. I've been back and forth to school over the years for several things including electronic engineering and multimedia production I'm very good with computers from repair to a multitude of software applications. I may change careers and go over to IT in the coming years. Computers are a major part of my everyday life. I also intend to get my degree in the near future in Business Administration with an emphasis on Internet Marketing.
