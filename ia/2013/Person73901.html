Kelly Diedring Harris' Summary

Kelly Diedring Harris is the Chief Executive Communicator and owner of KDH Communications, a full service public relations, communications and marketing company perfecting the power of publicity for unique and interesting clients.

KDH Communications specializes in public relations for the pet industry and also represents clients across all industries.   Kelly has a Masters Degree in Mass Communications and is passionate about providing top-notch public relations services to companies of all sizes.

Kelly is also a television spokesperson and guest host for the Home Shopping Network, a DRTV infomercial host, a guest expert, and a television host for a variety of other television projects - both locally and nationally. Kelly regularly hosts live television shopping segments, documentaries, television shows and DRTV infomercials.

For more information about Kelly and KDH Communications, please visit www.kdhcommunications.com.

Specialties: Television Host, Product Spokesperson, Infomercial Host, DRTV host, Guest Expert, Public Relations, Media Relations, Pet Lifestyle Expert, Marketing, Media Training, Community Relations, Government Relations, Client and Interpersonal Communications, Writing, Editing, Social Media
