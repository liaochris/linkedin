Elaine Endsley's Summary

Plan for tomorrow, live for today.

I am a dedicated and passionate Realtor, and licensed California Real Estate Broker and a real estate consultant. For over 25 fabulous years I have been guiding and clarifying the values of clients to realize their dreams of buying or selling a home,. I listen to what is important to my clients, when I know what is important to them, I begin to add value to their lives and to be their consultant by providing my help and advise so that my clients make the best choices.

I not only work with first time, and move up buyers and sellers, I also specialize working with seniors and their families to provide help during "Next Chapter”. If the decision is to sell the house, I will provide you my assistance to choose the best way.

I am associated with a group of professional that specialize with seniors. "Point of Life" is the name of our organization. We have a team of professionals, (Legal, helath, tax structure, home investment, and more. We will provide you with a broad array of options to make the process as easy as possible.
