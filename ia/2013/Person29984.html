Daniel Vogelzang's Summary

Daniel is an entrepreneur, senior marketing leader, brand strategist, design thinker and interactive director. He currently delivers brand marketing strategy and design solutions as an independent consultant for clients across the country.

Daniel was a partner of Soldier Design, an influential consumer experience company, and served there as Director of Brand Strategy and Operations for over 8 years. Currently and through his previous work, Daniel has facilitated many offsite strategy retreats for senior executives from start-ups and cult brands to Fortune 500 companies. Through his methodology, he has translated results into salient brand strategy, while leading design and marketing execution. He has led strategy initiatives, delivered brand insights and produced marketing campaigns for companies such as Gold Toe Moretz, Canada Goose, Nike, Under Armour, New Balance, Warrior Lacrosse, Burton Snowboards and Targus.

Specialties

Facilitating and creating global strategic marketing direction, branding and design, team and vendor management, producing interactive experiences, business operations, finance and entrepreneurship.
