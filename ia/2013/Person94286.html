Mike Kincer's Summary

Mike is a career professional with over 20 years experience in a vast array of commercial systems and military avionics as a systems / software engineer, project manager, father, husband, coach and mentor.

Known for his perseverance, Mike is executive leadership's repeated choice to engage the team and enact results. Mike welcomes new challenges, learning new technologies and integrating them into real-world solutions. He has solved tough problems and successfully turned troubled projects around.

** Chief Engineer / Systems Architect / System of Systems Engineering / Cross-Functional Team Leader **

Mike has played many roles, wears many hats, and has demonstrated technical and customer savvy every step of the way. He has worked several US Navy, Army, Air Force and International Postal programs from cradle to grave. His contributions have had positive influence on the award and execution of numerous multi-million dollar contracts.

** Entrepreneur | Visionary | Forward Thinker **

Fueling his entrepreneurial spirit and motivations, Mike has consulted with a startup firm and contributed to the intellectual property portfolio with innovative media file distribution and advertising models. He has successfully conveyed concise and articulate top-level concepts at all levels of understanding to potential investors, patent attorneys and agents.

** Versatile | Multi-faceted | Diverse | Well-Rounded **

Mike continues to increase his breadth and depth of knowledge in many areas. With a background in Electrical Engineering, Mike minored in Biomedical Engineering in his undergraduate studies. Since then, he has delved deeply into software design, from assembly to high-level languages C, C++, Java, and C#.

Mike has been using Linux for over 20 years.
