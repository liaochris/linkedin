Peter Letizia's Summary

Peter now serves and President and CEO of F&T Water Solutions, www.ftwatersolutions.com. We are a water treatment company specializing in electrocoagulation and chemical free integrated technologies. F&T offers an advanced water treatment system, Variable Electro Precipitator™ (VEP), in conjunction with other pre and post treatment components to provide portable and fixed based solutions to our customers.

F&T provides the experience and expertise to produce innovative solutions to treat frac flowback, produced and industrial wastewater by leveraging electrocoagulation and supplemental separators, polymers and absorbents as needed for a complete remediation solution. Our systems are capable of treating between 2 to over 1,000 gallons per minute (GPM).

Whether you have an immediate wastewater issue at your plant, need to plan and install a complete wastewater treatment system for your facility, or need experienced assistance with a specialized industrial application, we can help. F&T Water Solutions professionals are hands-on, real world designers and operators. We have experience in designing and building sustainable and cost effective wastewater treatment plants since 1986. Each system is based on each customers unique set of specifications and requirements. We specialize in providing customized pre-assembled, small footprint, wastewater treatment systems that simplify field construction activity. Our systems includes all instructions, user manuals, training and support necessary to effectively operate these treatment systems.

Specialties: Electrocoagulation, Electroprecipitation, Industrial Waste Water Treatment, Fracking Waste Water Treatment, Clean Water Technology, Green Technology Company, Identity Access Management, Multi Factor Authentication, Data in Motion Encryption, Biometric Identity Management. Peter has held speaking engagements about Identity Management and Data Security in front of Congress.
