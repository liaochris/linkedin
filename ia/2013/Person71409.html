Justin Thomas' Summary

As an IBM Cognos Technical Specialist I help global businesses to design, build and integrate their Business Analytics server platforms.

Customers who benefit from my expertise may have some of the following requirements:

- Design, install and roll out of IBM Cognos Software
- Upgrade existing versions of their IBM Cognos products
- Problem resolution within an existing IBM Cognos product environment
- Technical Audit / Review of existing IBM Cognos BI, Planning, TM1 or Controller software
- Enhanced support with pro-active maintenance

IBM COGNOS TECHNICAL DESIGN AND INSTALLATION

During the critical phase of designing and installing your IBM Cognos software environment you are effectively laying the foundations of your whole Business Analytics environment, the importance of using an experienced Technical Consultant at this stage cannot be underestimated.

UPGRADES AND MIGRATIONS FOR IBM COGNOS SOFTWARE

Special care and must be taken during the upgrade of an existing IBM Cognos environment, be it to new servers in a new datacentre or a move to the latest version. It is important to work with a consultant who has experience and knowledge to competently build the platform and to migrate and upgrade your data ensuring a smooth transition.

PROBLEM SOLVING

The technical specialist role requires superb problem solving and analytical skills to help with efficient fault resolution. This is an area where I am highly qualified to assist customers using the wealth of experience gained whilst working with a wide variety of IT systems since 1996.

An example of when to engage an IBM Cognos Technical Specialist could be where an existing support case is logged with IBM but a faster, more focussed problem resolution is desired. Having an experienced technical consultant on-site can expedite problem resolution in most cases.
