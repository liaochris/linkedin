Joseph Murphy (joe@bizooma.com)'s Summary

Email: Joe@bizooma.com
Office: 904-226-9123

I began my career in IT developing robotics maintenance technology for the auto industry in Detroit. After that I turned my attention to web development and Internet marketing.

Anticipating the growing need for mobile solutions, I started InKnowvator, Inc in 2002 and began creating mobile applications and marketing solutions.

In 2006, InKnowvator, Inc. was purchased by a Silicon Valley firm and I then spent 4 years serving as their CTO and VP of Internet and Mobile Marketing.

Specialties: With my new company, Bizooma, Inc. I'm changing my focus and challenging my team to develop highly specialized mobile and Internet applications for small business and non-profit entities. Some of the areas we're engaged in take advantage of location based services, QR codes and augmented reality applications.

We've increased our use of video in content marketing initiatives thus prompting us to create a production company, Distracted Films. Apart from client-video projects, we participate in film competitions and created a reality show concept that was picked up by a West Coast studio and was shopped to major cable networks. We currently have another show in production and will provide more details at the appropriate time.

I also write technical and marketing articles for Jacksonville Business Advantage
and I'm the Jacksonville Android writer for Examiner.com.

I'm currently taking on new clients and am interested in consulting engagements.
