Martine Gerard's Summary

Whether you seek your first home, pied-a-terre or next investment, or are selling a prized property, Martine will satisfy your every need with utmost attention to detail. Her European background and extensive experience in the international travel industry enable her to work well with buyers and sellers from both New York and around the world. She has been so spot-on in her marketing of properties and selection of homes for clients that on several occasions, clients from overseas have purchased sight-unseen because of their confidence in Martine's expertise.

Originally from France, Martine made New York her home right after college. She has lived in Soho, the Upper East Side, Battery Park City, Tribeca, and in Harlem for the past 12 years.

Martine is a member of the Real Estate Board of New York and a Certified Negotiation Expert.
