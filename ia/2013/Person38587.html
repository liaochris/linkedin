Daniel Tunkelang's Summary

I'm an engineering manager at LinkedIn, leading LinkedIn's efforts around query understanding.

*** WE ARE HIRING! ***

Talk to me if you're passionate about search and want to join a great engineering team working on unique information retrieval challenges. Check out my slides below to learn more about what our team is doing.

My previous career history in a nutshell:
- Built and led LinkedIn's product data science team.
- Led local search quality team at Google mapping local businesses to their home pages.
- Founding employee and Chief Scientist of Endeca (acquired by Oracle in 2011 for $1.1B).
- Wrote the first book on faceted search: http://bit.ly/facetedsearch
- Created annual symposium on human-computer interaction and information retrieval (HCIR).
- BS and MS degrees from MIT; PhD in Computer Science from CMU.

If you're a recruiter, please understand that I love my team at LinkedIn, and I'm not looking to move. Also, I do not connect with recruiters other than those I work with at LinkedIn.

And if you're one of my connections, please consider endorsing me for karaoke.
