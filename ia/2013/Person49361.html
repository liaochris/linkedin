Denisha Marino's Summary

Providing companies with accurate financial data, Denisha enables business owners to make confident financial business decisions. Armed with a multitude of trouble-shooting skills acquired throughout 30 years experience in the fields of banking and accounting, she helps businesses enjoy clean, orderly audits and returns at tax-time.

Specialties

As an Advanced Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor, Denisha specializes in providing bookkeeping solutions, support, and training to small and mid-sized companies in the professional services industry. Her clients include businesses in the fields of financial services, property management, insurance services, consulting, engineering, medical/health services, and non-profit organizations, among others.
