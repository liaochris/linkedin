  • Thirdeye Visual Link Analysis System

    • June 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Ertuğrul Saruhan, Mustafa YILDIZ, Zeki Baris Dogan, RIDVAN TANIK

    Thirdeye visual analysis project is improved for police departments, financial organisations, gsm operators, intelligence services and companies that has market data.
    Thirdeye finds and visualizes hidden relations in huge data sources and provides analysis tools in order to show you criminal groups, relations between this groups, possible leaders of these groups and visualize social relations between people.
    It is also designed for market data. You can find hidden relations between products and customer relations. In this way you can manufacture and market right product to its possible customer.
    Thirdeye is established on server client architecture. No setup is required and you can use it on any browser. It can reach any data sources such as databases like Oracle, Mssql, Sybase etc., excel files and also webservices.
