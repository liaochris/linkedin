LeAnne Wagner's Summary

I'm a designer and educator with degrees in both graphic and interaction design. I specialize in up-front strategic thinking and research, user interaction and experience (UI/UX), mobile interfaces, game design, and identity development. Many of my clients are tech start-ups and non-profits who are looking for a partner with a full suite of capabilities.

I'm also active and interested in working on design-related research projects. My areas of interest include: education (21st Century Learning), gaming, at-risk youth, and technology.

I'm always interested in hearing about new opportunities and accepting new clients. I love working collaboratively and prefer to work on site when it makes sense. Please visit my site for examples of my work: www.leannewagner.com

Specialties

web design (HTML, CSS, Flash AS3), mobile design, game design, design research, prototyping, branding

Primary Investigator on Creative IT Grant Project

National Science Foundation

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Government Administration industry

October 2010 – Present (3 years) New York, NY

I am a lead on a research project called Urban Game Design: Tool for Creativity, Collaboration, and Learning Among Youth. It is a National Science Foundation funded research project under their Creative IT initiative. The purpose of the study is to better understand the potential of new media for fostering creativity among youth. Through iterative game design youth participants learn new ways to link media to physical locations via mobile technologies. As a primary investigator in this three year study, my responsibilities include the authoring of curriculum, facilitating workshops, iPhone application design, and data analysis.
