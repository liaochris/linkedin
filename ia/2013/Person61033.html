Jeff Lundwall's Summary

I have worked in the media and media/technology industries since the mid‘90s. I have held sales positions at CMP and helped launch The Industry Standard, the first business magazine for the internet. Later I co-founded three content sites where I managed sales and business development. I was also involved in early web video and widget development. Before launching Mercury Group, I was Associate Publisher at Condé Nast Digital, where I helped grow staff and revenue 5x over four years.

Specialties

Historical context of print and online marketing/advertising as well as media technology with keen understanding of business landscape.
Strong contacts with agencies/clients/vendors/publishers.
