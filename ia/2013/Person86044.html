Universiteti Marin Barleti's Summary

Marin Barleti University, also known as, Universiteti Marin Barleti, is a private institution of a higher education in Albania which bears the name of a historian and humanist of the 15th century. Marin Barleti is the author of the historic accounts on the Skanderbeg epics.

The University was founded in 2005 and within a few years since the establishment, the University has been a success story in the ever challenging attempt at raising the standards of a higher level of education within Albania.

The University has coupled together experienced and well-respected professors with young professors who have earned their degrees abroad. This strategy has proven to be a winning formula, and because of our continuous effort the University keeps on to growing wiser and more modern, both at the same time. Each passing year the University grows stronger.

Marin Barleti is a fully accredited higher education institution. With over 200 professors and staff members, our departments are always looking at creating new partnerships in order to enhance the quality of our programs and to provide our students not just with textbook knowledge but also with a fresh worldview that will make them citizens without borders.
Marin Barleti University (MBU) was established in 2005 by Marin Barleti Ltd, which is part of DUDAJ Group. The University is governed by a board of some of the most respected names of the Albanian elite, including the Former President of the Country, Mr. Rexhep Mejdani.

Specialties
