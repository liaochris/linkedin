Responsibilities have included, at various stages of our constant growth:

- built and maintained system-level images for all servers, primarily Ubuntu Linux (but also Red Hat)

- involved in many new-service architectural planning sessions to advise on appropriate technologies, failure scenarios to handle, and large-scale considerations
- developed and managed our video ingestion service to handle high-traffic video uploads via a variety of methods (WebDAV, HTTP POST, FTP, Aspera, MRSS pull-based systems)
- implemented and managed several caching layers, from CDN edge and mid-tier configurations, HTTP caching proxies, and app-specific content caches (many technologies, but mostly Akamai CDN, Varnish, and Redis)
- mentored and transferred most of these responsibilites to peers as we scaled the organization
