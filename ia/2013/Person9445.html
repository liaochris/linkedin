Areya Simmons' Projects

  • LIFE PATH

    • March 2010 to March 2011
    Team Members: Areya Simmons, Lauren O'Malley, Meg V. Snead, Kurdice Neal, Ashley Sahler, George Wentworth, Adelle Drahos, Gail Byrd, Alex Previty, Brenda Morie, Segun Olusanya

    Fia remembers her previous life.

    In that life, she was a young girl named Rebecca. Through a series of unfortunate events, Rebecca died at age 8 in a fire, and her father was partly responsible. Twenty-seven years later, having been reincarnated, Fia is 19 and trying to find the father from her old life. Having no support from family or friends, Fia has hit several dead-ends in her pursuit. After moving to the hometown she lived in during her previous incarnation, she seeks out the help of Life Path, a group for those with memories like Fia's. Through the support of fellow member Aleta, Fia recalls memories she didn't even know she still had. These recollections help bring her ever closer to reconnecting with her father, and what may be their last chance to finally heal.
