Gina Poertner, CHES's Experience

Physiologist, Bike Fitting Specialist, Triathlon and Cycling Coach

Life Balance Sports & Wellness (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Sports industry

January 2008 – Present (5 years 9 months) Based in Kansas City, world-wide clientele.

At Life Balance Sports & Wellness we custom design nutrition and training plans with one-on-one coaching to meet your individual goals. Whether you're a competitive athlete or a "weekend warrior," Life Balance Sports & Wellness can help you achieve your unique dreams.

For cyclists and triathletes, Bicycle Fitting and Positioning will boost your performance and enhance your recovery. Increase your power output, speed, strength, and flexibility with this comprehensive process.

I am a Certified Health Education Specialist focusing on exercise physiology and sports nutrition with over 20 years experience coaching the various disciplines of running, cycling and triathlon. I work with athletes of all levels of ability from beginner to elite.

As a triathlete, runner and life-long competitor, I understand the unique needs of athletes. I have served on the sports medicine staffs of AAU Junior Olympics, National Gymnastics Championships, National Indoor Track & Field Championships, and individual world class, Olympic, Paralympic, and professional athletes. I am a certified Official for USA Cycling and USA Track & Field and continue to officiate local, national, and international events.

Learn more about training and nutrition through my articles published on Ironman.com, PezCyclingNews.com, Active.com, and LavaMagazine.com.
