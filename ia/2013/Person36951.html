Dorcas Borgers' Summary

As senior legal counsel I work on an interim basis for a variety of clients, ranging from multinationals to small startup companies. Often in heavy industry, which has my special attention and where assignments are fulfilled in other industries, they correspond very well with my expertise.

My expertise lies particularly in the areas of corporate law, corporate governance and contracting. With this background I work as general counsel, corporate counsel, secretary to Boards and Supervisory Boards as well as senior legal counsel.

The variety resulting from these different interpretations of my function as legal counsel ensures my professional life to be very interesting and varied. My experience to date very well reflects my skills as well as the direction I want to continue in my career and personal development.

Please do not hesitate to contact me for further information when you are looking for a legal professional.
