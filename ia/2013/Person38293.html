Dr. Sabrina Schleicher, Rural Business Strategist's Summary

So you've read the E-Myth and The Pumpkin Plan (if you haven't, you should!). Even though you're proud your business has survived the critical first 5 years and you're generating steady revenue

...you're tired of feeling pulled in a 100 different directions and being the bottleneck in your business...

...and you know you *should* be working ON your business to systematize and scale. You've gotten started lots of times, but get pulled off course by the day-to-day demands in your business...

...problem employees drive you crazy. If only you could find & keep great-quality people...

This is exactly what I help my clients with.

I specialize in showing overworked rural business owners how to solve their #1 challenge: "How to find & keep great quality people!" so you can grow your business and multiply your profit.

It's all about the 3 F's: Freedom, Flexibility and Fulfillment.
If your business isn't supporting your life, it's time for a change!
