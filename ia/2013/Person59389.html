Jason Minick's Summary

This Spring marked the start of the 8th year for Minick Photography! After over 20 years of editorial and commercial photography I still love what I do. My goal continues to be capturing compelling and storytelling images, maintaining a streamline workflow, and forming long term partnerships with my clients.

My career in corporate, commercial and news photography has spanned more than 20 years, as I covered and captured historic and community events throughout the area. Early in my career, I served as a freelancer, staffer, and then chief photographer at a daily paper in Lewistown, Pa. I later accepted the position of chief photographer at The Sentinel in Carlisle, Pa.

I have covered a multitude of news events, including visits from Presidents, candidates and dignitaries. My true love, however, was for “community journalism,” which allowed me to capture everyday life on a daily basis. That work has earned me numerous state and regional awards throughout my career. In 2001, I spent 10 days traveling across war-torn Bosnia, documenting an American relief group’s efforts to help the Bosnian children cope with the aftermath of war. My work has been showcased in newspapers, magazines, and media outlets around the world.

I took that passion and style and created my own commercial and editorial photography business. My clients call on me for my photojournalistic style to capture candid moments while also making subjects comfortable during those times when creative portraits need to be taken. Some of those clients include: Philadelphia University, Harrisburg Area Community College, Harrisburg University, Gettysburg College, PPO&S, The Associated Press, Holy Spirit Hospital, TCG Advertising and Design, Carlisle Area Regional Medical Center, Sacunas, Highmark and others. In addition, I also offer web based video production services.

Please contact Minick Photography for rates and availability at jcm@minickphotography.com
