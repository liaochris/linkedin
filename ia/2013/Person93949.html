Mike Arcuri's Summary

Mike is an inventor and product leader at heart. His professional passion is creating new products: forging designs that are simple and enjoyable to use, working day in and day out with great teams, and interacting with the market to make these ideas into reality. He gets his biggest thrills leading product advancements, learning from the market as it reacts, and seeing things really gel when a great product/market fit is achieved.

Mike has built products and led teams for more than 15 years, and focused on early stage startups for the last 6. Prior to his current roles he led marketing and production for Seattle Startup of the Year, Zipline Games, founded a TechStars mobile gaming company, started a mobile product and marketing consultancy, and served as a mobile products and internet services executive at Photobucket and Ontela. Prior to his start-up work, Mike spent nearly a decade leading teams to develop new products and improve some of the most-used software in the world at Microsoft (Excel, Outlook, SharePoint, Frontpage, Office). Mike has 11 patents to his name and 7 additional patents pending.

Skills:
• Product management: team leadership, product planning, interaction design, usage metrics and goals, conversion goals and measurement, art direction, program management, release management, project management, algorithm and technical design, user research, technical documentation
• Marketing: goal-setting, mobile app monetization, mobile app customer acquisition, positioning, messaging, content creation, branding, public relations, marketing and advertising analytics, product naming, and promotions
• Engineering management: aligning output with the business, hiring, agile/scrum, spiral, and waterfall
• IP portfolio development and management

Specialties

1. Developing product strategies for early-stage startup companies and executing on them to bring great new products to market
2. Making relentless forward progress toward product and marketing goals with any size team and budget
3. Designing products for massive markets and millions of users (Vibrant Chocolate, Ontela and Photobucket's mobile products, Microsoft Office's user interface)
4. Refining and reinventing category-leading products (e.g. Microsoft Excel, Outlook, Frontpage)

Zipline Games

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Games industry

March 2011 – November 2012 (1 year 9 months) Seattle, WA

Zipline games is an active mobile games studio and the maker of Moai, the mobile platform for pro game developers.

Moai is the first platform for professional game developers building modern social and mobile games who want to build better games faster by using a single, game-friendly programming language on the client and server. With a cloud environment for both game data and server-side game logic that scales out automatically, resists downtime, and is replicated, backed-up and monitored by the Moai team, game developers can keep focusing on core game play without becoming operations experts or web service architects. The same scripters can now add games feature spanning both client and server.

Moai is also the first open source mobile game SDK built around the industry-standard Lua scripting language. For game developers, open-source means never getting stuck shipping a lesser title due to platform limitations. Moai’s integrated animation, physics, text handling, scene scaling and other capabilities significantly speed up game prototyping, programming and game design iterations.

Developers don’t need to wait until they start building their next title to gain some of these benefits, Moai also provides gaming-optimized, RESTful web services for cloud storage, leaderboards, achievements, and more so any game can be extended to the cloud regardless of the client technology used. Everything about Moai is tuned for game development with the goal of better games, faster.

Learn more at www.getmoai.com and www.ziplinegames.com.
