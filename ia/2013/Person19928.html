Carla Schuchman's Summary

I have been professionally working in the art field for 17 years. Mostly in decorative painting such as faux finishes, and murals. More recently I have been commissioned to create custom canvas paintings. I also have my most recent work represented by The Island Art Gallery here in Pawleys Island, SC.I have several websites that eventually I may combine, but for now, carleysense.comis one I post to the most, along with Facebook updates. I had been calling Carley my wonderful spirit part of me, but she is me, growing and learning, and sharing life's experiences with you.

I am a care giver now and have been almost full time for three yrs for my love, Smokey, who had an accident then became very ill. We are fighting to make it through and have an awesome testimony to share. The journey itself is a wonderful testimony. I also care for him elderly mom for the last seven years. If not for the help of others God placed in my life, I don't know how I would have got this far.

Although life has changed in a split moment, and I am doing things I never imagined, we are blessed to be here and I want to encourage others to not give up, even when I have my own days of throwing in the the towel and asking God to take me home. There is a great job to do still.
I am working on recreating myself and finding work that keeps me closer to home were I am needed. I also have completed my first year a online at APUS college. Not sure my direction as I was before, but still trying to fit that in.
I am blessed beyond my imagination and enjoy encouraging and lifting others up as I walk on this journey called life.
