Mary Ahern's Experience

Professional Artist

Mary Ahern Artist

1990 – Present (23 years) Northport NY

Art Naturally is the marketing and sales entity representing the Artist, Mary Ahern.

Mary Ahern creates Botanical Fine Art by combining 30 years of traditional painting experience with 25 years working in the Computer Graphics industry. She creates Plant Portraits and Still Lifes in both traditional and in digital forms.

Extremely high-resolution reproductions of this work are available in a wide variety of sizes and media. Treatment options range from Canvas and Fine Arts Papers to an economical line of Designer Prints. Framing is optional and available.

Sizing is a strong and flexible option for these Fine Art reproductions. From mural sized canvas to small print groupings the work is designed to offer the greatest adaptability for any corporate or residential design need.

Drawing upon her years as the business owner of Online Design, a graphic design firm, Mary Ahern is predisposed to working at solutions with Corporate buyers, Architects and Interior Designers.
