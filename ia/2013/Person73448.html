Kelly Jones' Summary

I have over fifteen years’ experience in the Information Technology field with expertise in web application solutions and infrastructure administration. I have worked in various roles from developer, to architect, to project lead and have worked in various environments including state government, non-profit, large corporate and small business. I am accustomed to learning and working with cutting edge technologies.


I started my IT career in Windows systems administration, focusing on Active Directory and workstation OS/application deployment. I then moved into development with .NET, and have since gained knowledge and experience with ASP.NET, ASP.NET Ajax, SQL Server, and SharePoint. Since I have experience from both the development and infrastructure areas of IT, I have the ability to design and troubleshoot the entire application and infrastructure stack.


I also have a background in teaching, in both technical settings as a Microsoft Certified Trainer and in non-technical settings. This experience has allowed me to refine my communication skills with management, IT staff, and end users.
