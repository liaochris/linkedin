Jessica Towne's Experience

VP

Clark & Towne, P.C.

March 1995 – Present (18 years 6 months) Lawrenceville, Georgia

Jessica Towne began practicing law in Gwinnett County (northeast metropolitan Atlanta) in 1989 as an Assistant Solicitor, prosecuting DUIs and misdemeanor crimes. In 1995 Ms. Towne entered private practice, opening Clark & Towne, P.C. With Gwinnett County offices centrally located in Lawrenceville, Clark & Towne Attorneys is a criminal defense law firm dedicated to helping Atlanta area drivers accused of DUI and associated charges such as vehicular homicide, driving on a suspended license, habitual violator DUI, and driving under the influence of drugs.

Ms. Towne is rated AV by Martindale-Hubbell, which is the highest rating for legal knowledge and ethics.

Ms. Towne has represented more than 1200 drivers accused of DUI, Reckless Driving and other serious traffic offenses in the Atlanta metro area. She accepts a limited number of non-DUI clients, but does no civil work.

Ms. Towne is active in local attorney activities, serving as President of the Gwinnett County Bar Association in 1999-2000, President of the Criminal Defense Section of the Gwinnett County Bar Association from 1998-99, and Vice President of the Georgia Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers from 2006-07. She volunteers with the Georgia Mock Trial program. On the national level, she is a member of the National College for DUI Defense since 2000, serving as the delegate from Georgia for the NCDD from 2002-06. She is also a member of the National Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers and NORML.

Ms. Towne's website contains in-depth DUI Information detailing what you need to know about DUI in metro Atlanta. Her free book “How to Choose a Georgia DUI Lawyer” guides you through the process of selecting the best lawyer for your individual situation. Get your free copy now at http://www.clarktowne.com/
