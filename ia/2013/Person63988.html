Jim Maisano's Summary

Jim is attorney practicing law in New Rochelle, NY and concentrates his practice in these areas:

-- Commercial, Business & Collection Litigation
-- Real Estate Closings
-- Wills, Estates & Probate Actions
-- Personal Injury Litigation
-- Government Relations
-- Election Law

Jim was re-elected to his 8th term on the Westchester County Board of Legislators in 2011. His district includes the City of New Rochelle and Town of Pelham. First elected in 1997, Jim has held various leadership positions and currently serves as the Minority Leader.

Jim recently practiced law at the New York office of two large international law firms, Crowell & Moring (2007-2011) and Buchanan Ingersoll & Rooney (2003-2007), and concentrated his practice in the areas of commercial, business and collection litigation, financial services, creditor's rights, real estate, antitrust, intellectual property and government relations.

Jim’s legal background includes running his own firm with an emphasis on commercial litigation, real estate, personal injury, wills and estates, and criminal defense in New Rochelle (1995-2003), and practicing at New York City firms in the areas of insurance defense litigation (1994-1995) and commercial and entertainment litigation (1992-1994).

Jim served in the U.S. Marine Corps from 1984 to 1988.
