Mike Barnes' Summary

I provide advice on all aspects of hosted/cloud/managed voice services, unified communications (UC), and collaboration, and the social media aspects of modern business communications. I can lead or participate in requirements capture, design, vendor assessment, documentation, training and marketing.

I have nearly 20 years experience in hosted voice services, initially global TDM virtual private networks (with BT, MCI and AT&T) and for the last 12 years with IP technologies - in particular Cisco hosted UC.

I believe we can make the world a better place through better communication - better for employers, shareholders and employees.

Read my new book, "An Infinite Number of Monkeys", to learn how to build communications platforms which improve the effectiveness of you and your people by speeding the flow of information within and between organisations.

Specialties

Mike Barnes' Experience

Independent Communications Consultant

Linked World provides communications consultancy services, through which I offer my expertise in Unified Communications/Collaboration and emerging corporate use of social networking tools. My speciality is Cisco UC, with experience in older technologies such as SS7 and of large projects in the hosted/managed space. I can help you with buying decisions, making the most of your investment, and in training, documentation and other forms of knowledge transfer.

SE

Cisco Systems

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; CSCO; Computer Networking industry

January 2001 – March 2010 (9 years 3 months)

As an SE on the BT account, I was responsible for the infrastructure voice projects in which Cisco was engaged with BT. Primarily this was the MMVoIP and VoIP Port product suite, providing hosted corporate voice and UC services, but also for the SP interconnect product IP Exchange. During much of the 9 years I was the main technical interface to BT, and was instrumental in moving the relationship from one of a "box buying" CAPEX-based model to a true service wrap with OPEX-based pricing.
