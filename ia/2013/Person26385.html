Colleen Jolly, PPF.APMP's Summary

Colleen is an accredited proposal professional (PPF.APMP) and APMP Fellow with 13 years experience working with clients in diverse industries such as defense, aerospace, healthcare, environmental and child welfare. She has been with 24 Hour Company that entire time, starting as an intern in 1999 and becoming an owner in 2004. She manages a team of graphic design, desktop publishing and editing professionals acting as 'Air Traffic Controller' interfacing with clients, vendors and staff in a high-pressure environment.

Colleen has substantial responsibility for budgets and employee policies including domestic partner policies, paternity leave and anti-discrimination policies. She created the charitable giving policies at her company and participates in human resources functions; conducting all first round interviews for new staff as well as reviews for existing staff. Additionally she leads 50% of attributed sales activity in the US and overall growth.

She is a frequent speaker, trainer, workshop leader and writer on visual communications and general business topics and has spoken in the US, UK, Germany, the Netherlands, South Africa and Australia.

Colleen spearheads all international expansion and opened the first International office in London in 2008. She maintains all operational, legal, 100% of sales and marketing efforts in the UK and is exploring Australia as a new market.

In her free time, she serves on the board of APMP International as COO as well as the board of the Capitol Hill Arts Workshop in DC. She also is a docent and mentor at the Corcoran Gallery of Art in DC. Colleen gives back to her alma mater conducting interviews for incoming potential freshman at Georgetown University. Previously she served on the Board of the APMP NCA Chapter, danced with a Bollywood troupe Dhoonya Dance for six years and was charge of layout and cover design for the association's Journal for 10 years. She is an award-winning fine artist and recognized business leader.
