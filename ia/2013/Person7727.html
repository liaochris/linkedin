Angela M. Fisher's Summary

AMF Entertainment, Inc. is a New York City-based celebrity booking agency. Our team has years of celebrity relations, television, and event experience, as well as strong relationships in the entertainment community. AMF works closely with entertainers, television networks, film/production companies, and corporate clients on bookings and creative strategies to draw attention, generate ratings, and drive revenue. We pledge our dedication to follow through, communication, and collaboration.

AMF's mission is to create innovative celebrity partnerships, bookings, and experiences for television, film, and brands. We are passionate and committed to delivering results, while providing unparalleled customer service to our clients.
