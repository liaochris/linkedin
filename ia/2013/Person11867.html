    Team Members: Barrett Conrad

  • HBABuildersRisk.com

    • November 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Barrett Conrad

    Hartwig Moss Insurance Agency's new insurance program partners with the states Home Builders Association to offer improved rates for their members. The program initially utilized a monthly paper reporting process. CotingaSoft brought that process online with a web application that allows builders to report their construction starts online. The builders also receive monthly reminders and paperless invoices. The software has allowed HMIA to keep up with their growth in four additional new states.

  • ExpertTrivia

  • Love Letters from New Orleans

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Barrett Conrad

    Epilogue Publishing came to CotingaSoft to create the iPhone version of their recent title--Love Letters from New Orleans. The book celebrates the unique elements of New Orleans through art and poetry. The app utilized existing art assets to create a fluid digital book which captures the spirit of the physical version.

  • inFront's AllClear Mapping Component

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Barrett Conrad
