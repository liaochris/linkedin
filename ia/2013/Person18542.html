Bryan Robinson's Summary

I'm a user experience specialist focusing on UI Development and product strategy.

In the past five years, I've focused on many aspects of creating websites from content to development, from writing headlines to creating wireframes.

With this varied experience, I know the importance of well-written content, well-constructed code and the deep, deep importance of understanding what your users want and not leaving them behind.

My hope is to use my experience, my degree in Philosophy -- offering me an extensive framework of critical thinking, logic and an understanding of user psychology -- and my skills to help brands find the light of the online world.
