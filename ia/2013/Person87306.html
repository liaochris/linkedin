Marlene Neumann's Summary

If you're looking for fresh ideas for corporate incentives and interior décor, a fine art photograph makes a unique alternative. My exquisite images make the perfect gift for your valued clients and a special expression of appreciation for an employee. The images I create add a subtle, natural decor to boardrooms and transform corporate environments, hotels, lodges and homes and form part of numerous international corporate and private collections.
Over the past 27 years I have developed my own unique darkroom process for my black and white film photographs that result in a original subtle feeling.
I believe that nature is a mirror to the beauty within ourselves. Each of my images is a profound experience for the viewer. My work brings the natural things in life into your home or your office - this surrounds people with inner peace, tranquility and harmony in their environments.
I have a passion for life and light, this is evident in my photographic work and in my approach to teaching “Creativity through Photography” and Inspirational workshops. My teaching is filled with enthusiasm, energy and has a vibrant style that transforms hundreds of lives each year in my exclusive workshops.
Through the lens I capture that split-second emotion inherent in a landscape or an object before the light changes. I photograph what I feel, not what I see.
For more information visit my website http://www.marleneneumann.com or watch one of my online video’s http://www.youtube.com/marleneneumann

Marlene Neumann's Honors and Awards

  • Marlene Neumann's Achievements

    MARLENE NEUMANN – Is not only recognised as a MASTER FINE ART PHOTOGRAPHER, but as a designer, visionary, inspirational speaker, philosopher, teacher, philanthropist. Marlene's creative vision and celebration of life is expressed in many forms and she has received many accolades and commissions. Marlene's greatest reward is witnessing the profound impact her fine art photography has on people and their environments - tranquility, healing, a sense of space and wonder. The perfect decor complement.

    Marlene has been featured in numerous publications, newspapers, and received many awards. She has also showcased her work at many exhibitions including having her work exhibited at The Louvre. She exhibits her work annually at South Africa's premier Design and Decor Shows - Decorex JHB and Homemakers Gauteng..
