Joe Zlomek's Summary

Journalist, photographer and videographer since 1971, with international awards and recognitions. Marketer since 1989. Course author and PA-licensed instructor for real estate since 2001.

Specialties

Specializes in reporting, photographing and editing news about Philadelphia and its suburbs, real estate issues, technology-based industries, business marketing, and travel.
