Douglas Gintz's Summary

Douglas Gintz has served as Chief Technology and Information Officer at Targeted Medical Pharma since January 2012. Prior to joining TMP, Mr. Gintz held the positions of Chief Executive Officer at Global Web Applications LLC and Director of Strategy and Planning at Nova Development Corp. Mr. Gintz has broad experience in delivering technology and content solutions to a wide audience for over 26 years. Specializing in emerging technologies, he's developed adaptive manufacturing compliance systems and native web applications for Fortune 500 companies, health care, nonprofits, start-ups, and professional services. Mr. Gintz has hands-on experience across product cycle from concept, design, planning, programming, leadership to marketing. Mr. Gintz earned a Bachelor of Arts from California State University, Northridge with an emphasis in Design.

Specialties: advanced applications programming, web design, new media content creation, advertising, presentations, strategic planning, internet marketing, branding and print collateral
