John E. Brown's Summary

I have been a municipal law and public attorney at Best Best & Krieger LLP since 1975 and have represented a variety of California public agencies including cities, redevelopment agencies, special districts and school districts. Currently, I am the city attorney of Ontario, California and the Town Attorney of the Town of Apple Valley, California. I have served as the city attorney for cities throughout California since 1976.

I also act as general counsel for a variety of other public agencies, including Elsinore Valley Municipal Water District for more than 30 years and Hi-Desert Water District. In addition, I am the general counsel of the March Joint Powers Authority and the March Inland Port Airport Authority, the local reuse authorities for the former March Air Force Base in Riverside County.

In the summer of 1972, I served as the acting city manager of Unalaska (“Dutch Harbor”), Alaska.
