Sandy Alexander's Summary

Sandy Alexander is an anti-fraud professional specializing in forensic accounting and fraud examination. Mr. Alexander has substantial case experience with asset misappropriation, bribery, conflicts of interest, breach of fiduciary duty, money-laundering, credit card abuse, and off-book schemes.

Mr. Alexander's background includes both Big 4 public and private-industry audit experience where he earned expert credentials in fraud examination, financial forensics, and information systems audits. He is an active member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners, the High Technology Crime Investigation Association, and the Forensic and Valuation Services Division of the AICPA.
