Anthony Tumminia's Summary

I bring four decades of experience in the areas of commercial and residential development, civil engineering design, construction management and client project management experience. I assist developers and franchise owners with the selection and coordinating of professionals required to obtain your development approvals while managing the costs associated with that effort. My association with Insite Engineering, LLC allows me to broaden the scope of services that can be offered.

As Founder and CEO of Design Development Consulting, I am a recognized leader within the New Jersey construction industry. I provide myclients with superior development and permitting guidance to help them manage all aspects of development approvals including soil investigations/permeability testing. I have worked on hundreds of projects within the Garden State.

This experience includes development projects for Franchises, Restaurant Chains, Convenience Stores, Banks & Financial Institutions, Commercial and Industrial Development, Churches & Non-Profit Organizations, Medical Health Care Facilities, Retail Establishments and many more. I am a proud U.S. Air Force Veteran and was part of the 554th Civil Engineering Squadron, which was based in Tan San Nhut Air Base, Vietnam. While on a second tour in Viet Nam I also served with the 823rd Red Horse (Rapid Emergency Deployable, Heavy Operational Repair Squadron, Engineering) Squadron; and was charged with the relocation design plans and living quarters development prior to the squadron move to DaNang Air Base, Vietnam.

Specialties: Civil design, permit, planning & contractor oversight
