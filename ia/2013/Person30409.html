Dan Simmons' Summary

Dan Simmons has been recruiting high-impact professionals since 1991. Since 2001, he has focused on recruiting sales and technical professionals, as well as corporate executives in agriculture across the USA and Canada.

A sampling of Dan's completed searches would include:
• President of a feed ingredients company in the mid west
• President of regional poultry company
• Director of R & D for major animal nutrition company
• Business Development Manager for forage protectorant company
• Purchasing Director for a Regional Feed Company
• Manager of Dairy Nutrition for a leading regional feed
• Dairy Technical Support Manager, (PhD) for a national company
• Senior Analytics Specialist for a leading Forage Lab
• Director of Operations for a Regional Feed Company
• Swine Nutritionist for a major pre-mix feed company in Manitoba, Canada
• Field Sales positions in Dairy, Swine and Equine feed across US
• Regional Operations Manager for a major integrated food company
• Sales reps for manufacturers of pro-biotics, DFM, fatty acids & minerals
• Feed Mill Managers for mills across the nation


Specialties: agriculture, recruiting, retention, dairy jobs, beef jobs, swine, poultry, equine, animal agriculture, animal science, animal nutrition

Dan Simmons' Experience

Recruiter/Owner

Continental Search

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Human Resources industry

August 1996 – Present (17 years 1 month) Oceanview, DE

Conceived and developed a state-of-the-art recruiting and staffing firm integrating current technologies with old-fashioned headhunting. Current focus is agriculture, animal nutrition and meat processing.

Continental Search has filled positions in 39 states, the District of Columbia and the Provinces of Manitoba and British Columbia in industries from animal agriculture to utility location Services. Since 2002 Dan has concentrated on animal agriculture. Dan celebrated his 600th career placement in the summer of 2013.
