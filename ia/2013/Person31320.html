Dave Wilhelm's Summary

I'm an Interactive Designer, by title, but my work involves so much more. I lead a small team in creating digital branding experiences for small and large, local and national, B2B and B2C clients.

I have a passion for creativity and design. I am a dedicated problem solver committed to turning complex ideas into simple, beautiful and functional solutions that makes the user’s experience memorable. I have a strong command of visual design including composition, layout information hierarchy, typography and color.

I stay on top of new and developing technologies and I am continually learning and improving my development skills (HTML/CSS).

Specialties: Interactive Art Direction & Design, User Interface (UI) Design, Interaction Design, Desktop, Mobile and Responsive Web Design, Site planning, Information Architecture, Wireframes, Visual Design, Art Direction, Advertising, Typography, Illustration
