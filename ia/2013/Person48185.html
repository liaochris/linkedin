Gerben Zaagsma's Summary

I currently work as a research fellow at the Lichtenberg Kolleg, the Institute for Advanced Studies of the University of Göttingen.

I hold a Ph.D. in modern (Jewish) history from the European University Institute in Florence. My thesis will be published with Bloomsbury Academic in 2014-2015 as 'Jewish volunteers, the International Brigades and the Spanish Civil War (1936-39)'.

I am an experienced researcher, lecturer and web developer with an active interest in Jewish History/Yiddish Studies, digital humanities and digital history. I also act as developer and editor of the internet portals European History Primary Sources and Yiddish Sources (see links).

In addition to my work in Jewish social and political history, I am particularly interested in the methodological implications of using new technologies in historical research and writing. Within this context I am currently guest-editing a special issue of BMGN - Low Countries Historical Review on digital history which will be published in december 2013.

Specialties: historical research; teaching; digital humanities; digital history; web development; content management systems (Drupal and Wordpress); copywriting; (web) editing.
