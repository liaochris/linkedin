R&D Researcher / Engineer

Mainstre.am

June 2010 – Present (3 years 5 months) Toronto, Canada Area

In conjunction with my photography and film production company, since 2010 I've been involved in a research and development program for a media distribution company, or companies. The primary action during this process was the distribution of original photo and video content to hundreds of blogs, dozens of magazines, newspapers, and news websites, various social networks, and Flickr; using a diverse set of methodologies and content licensing. In knowledge gained the work is considered a success, but further to this I achieved significant reach. On Flickr alone, over 1 million views were measured during the R&D campaign, and it is safely assumed that the total views via the combined distribution network is far beyond that figure.

Since the summer of 2011 a software-as-a-service business model has been explored with various internal technologies tested. That year potential partners were explored but not formalized. Exploration of partners and investor relationships continued in 2012 with a group in France with mixed results. In the first quarters of 2013 it is expected that an entry software service will be launched under the brand name Mainstream, and a formal offering will be made to early investors.
