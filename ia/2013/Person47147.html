Gary Lee Smith's Summary

2013 marks my ninth year as a Realtor. I have always practiced in Palm Springs and I know the area market very well. I review new listings every day and I personally visit the ones that I think my clients would be interested in purchasing. I stay close to other Realtors in my area and know the current market trends. I attend education classes on a regular basis and stay updated on changes to California DRE laws.
.
I have represented hundreds of buyers and sellers.

Specialties: Palm Springs, Rancho Mirage, Palm Desert, Cathedral City, La Quinta, Indian Wells and other desert locations. I'm looking for quality listing opportunities that are realistically priced. I have represented many buyers over the years and I enjoy a high ratio of referrals from my clients.

I'd like to help you buy or sell a real estate property. Earning your trust and continued patronage is the foundation of my business.
