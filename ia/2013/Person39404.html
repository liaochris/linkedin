Ed Correia [LION]'s Summary

Ed Correia is the founder and CEO of Silicon Valley's Sagacent Technologies, Inc.

He has extensive experience in best-of-class technology solution design, deployment, and management in small and mid-sized business environments (or 10 to 250 employees).

Business owner, executive, technology expert and speaker. Ed’s passion for technology solutions stems from a delight in efficient, productive and secure systems; not only of technology, but also of people and processes within businesses.

Specialties

With 35-years as an IT professional, Ed brings a unique blend of business management experience, operational know-how and computer industry knowledge to Silicon Valley.

*Computer Network Management / Managed Services
*Anti-Virus & Spam Prevention
*Onsite & Offsite Data Backups
*Business Continuity Planning & Disaster Recovery

*Hosted VoIP Phones
