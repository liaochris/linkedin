Martin Meyer-Gossner's Summary

Martin Meyer-Gossner is a digital web entrepreneur and web business strategist based in Munich. Martin is founder of The Strategy Web™, founder of Lancalot™ and co-founder of silicon.de, one of the first leading B2B IT business decision maker communities (2006 sold to CBS Interactive). For years, Martin held responsibility for sales, marketing and business development in leading IT publishing houses in Europe. In his role he built innovative marketing and sales concepts around media, research, events, CRM and customer loyalty programms. His expertise leads him to speak regularly at business events and to train companies on modern (social) web strategy.

The Strategy Web is expert in change and interim management consulting for C-Level and department leaders in marketing, sales and customer service with responsibility in web strategy, digital strategy as well as modern Social Web and mobile communication topics.

Specialties: Media strategy, web strategy, digital strategy, internet strategy, mobile strategy, digitale brand management, Social Media, Community Building, inspiration- and innovation management, evaluation and project management of web projects, consulting for media companies and start-up business.

More information at: http://about.me/meyergossner
