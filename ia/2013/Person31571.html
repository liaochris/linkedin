David Burt's Summary

I'm a marketing professional with over 15 years of experience in communications and public affairs, focused in the areas of information security, privacy, and online safety. I have extensive experience in the area of online safety technologies, with publications cited by both the United States Congress and the Supreme Court of the United States. I'm passionate about issues in the intersection of technology and society, with a strong interest in digital media. As a former IT manager with both a CISSP and a CIPP, I possess deep technical knowledge.

Specialties

Social media, Digital media, Advocacy, Public Speaking, Strategic Consulting, Creativity, Branding and Positioning
