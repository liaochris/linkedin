John Nunn's Summary

John Nunn is a solutions architect and managing director of Tortrix Ltd. A software development company specialising in Microsoft technologies. Tortrix has extensive expertise in Cloud Computing and Social Media Integration. They are a Microsoft Partner with a Gold Competency in Application development.
John has over 20 years commercial experience as a software developer working on a number of commercial solutions to help pay for college and university. He also organises and runs the Midlands Developer Network a user group a user group based in the midlands helping developers get the most out of the technologies and tools available to them.


Specialties: Touch Computing, Social Networking, Data Visualization, Web Services, ASP.net, C#, .net, IIS, Software Architecture, SharePoint, CRM Solutions, Virtulisation
