Bruce Feldman's Summary

Throughout a career dedicated to leasing and managing commercial and residential properties, I gained my expertise by handling thousands of leasing transactions for owners of commercial properties. Beginning with converting a family-owned industrial building into a neighborhood retail center and later serving as a senior executive officer for a multinational corporation subsidiary, my career has been marked by innovative solutions, diligent negotiations, extraordinary attention to detail, and an unblemished record of attracting the right tenant to the right space at the right price.

In forming Bruce Feldman Associates, LLC in 2009, I shifted my position from one side of the negotiating table to the other. My many years of exclusively representing landlords is uniquely suited to advocate for the interests of tenants. Tenants are able to leverage my inside knowledge of landlords' operations to gain a critical advantage in lease negotiations.

A graduate of Pace University’s Lubin School of Business, I began my real estate career managing and leasing a 50,000SF family-owned commercial center in the Five Towns area of Long Island.

Beginning in 2002, I assumed leasing responsibility for forty Manhattan buildings owned by Jakobson Properties. I also served as Executive Officer and Principal Broker for Monstermoving.com – a unique real estate relocation operation formed by TMP Worldwide’s Monster.com. Most recently, I was the exclusive leasing and managing agent for Empire Realty Management’s fifty-five commercial and residential buildings in Manhattan, Long Island, Bronx and Westchester.

I have successfully negotiated with a long and diverse list of retail, office and residential tenants including McDonald’s, Subway, Washington Mutual, St. Jude’s Children’s Research Hospital, Verizon Wireless, Quest Diagnostics and many more.
