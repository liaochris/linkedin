Adam Schallau's Summary

I'm a photographer specializing in creating images of the landscape, people, and culture of the American West. My photos are available as fine art prints on a variety of surfaces including traditional papers, canvas, and aluminum. I have an extensive collection of images in stock photography archives which are available to be licensed for calendars, travel guides, magazines, websites, and more.

I also guide workshops and personalized photo tours at Grand Canyon National Park and other destinations across the Southwest.


Artist Statement:
“I have never been so moved as I have been by the American Southwest. The land, the light, and the people are remarkable. I consider myself fortunate to have my fine art prints in people’s homes and businesses, and to share my passion through workshops as I very much enjoy teaching and sharing my favorite locations. Thank you for taking the time to view my photographs, they are an expression of not only the subject, but who I am as an artist. I truly hope they have touched your life in a meaningful way.”
