Gauntlet Commercial Real Estate Capital's Summary

Capital Markets:

Focusing on investment transactions with a minimum of $1 million, Gauntlet Commercial Real Estate Capital ("Gauntlet") arranges senior debt, mezzanine, note purchase, and equity financing. With an aggressive approach and substantial expertise in debt and private equity placement, the professional team at Gauntlet pledges their knowledge of real estate and capital markets to structure solutions specific to the acquisition, repositioning, and permanent financing for all property types.

Investment Sales:

Gauntlet advises active individual, middle market, and institutional clients on the acquisition of core and value-add commercial real estate properties throughout North America.

Distressed Asset Resolution:

Gauntlet tailors to the current financial environment by delivering efficient and cost-effective asset management strategies. Gauntlet garners a sui generis platform for accessing private equity to restructure debt obligations, to minimizing downside risk by seeking disposition avenues and other value-added services.

Specialties

Gauntlet’s investment banking business provides advisory services to real estate investors and operators on significant financing, work outs, acquisitions, dispositions, distressed debt and other strategic transactions.

Interests:

Gauntlet's interest is to identify, structure, and execute transactions on behalf of individual, middle market, and institutional clients throughout the United States and in select international markets. Gauntlet originates fixed-rate term loans, bridge loans, second mortgages, mezzanine loans and preferred equity transactions, typically targeting cash-flowing assets. It will go up to as far as 85% of the capital structure in a transaction if the company’s principals feel strongly about the operator and the real estate. Gauntlet targets office, retail, industrial, multifamily and hospitality but will look at all sectors.
