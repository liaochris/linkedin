Deborah Hamilton's Experience

President

MediaMonde, Inc.

April 2002 – Present (11 years 9 months)

MediaMonde, Inc. is a full-service marketing and public relations firm with extensive experience in compelling content creation. We are committed to helping clients consistently deliver the right message to the right audience using the right media. MediaMonde was founded in 2002 by Deborah Hamilton, veteran marketer, communications visionary, accomplished writer and unabashed stickler for details. Some of our longest-running clients include Colbert Packaging Corporation, a packaging manufacturer founded in 1959, and Highwinds Network Group, Inc., a global content delivery network provider.

Senior Public Relations Manager

Parascript

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry

July 2000 – October 2001 (1 year 4 months)

Parascript is a developer of cursive, handprint and machine print recognition technology. Leveraging digital image analysis and advanced pattern recognition, its software enables business automation in areas like forms processing, postal and financial automation, and fraud prevention; and supports cancer screening in medical imaging. Parascript’s award-winning technology draws on a proven track record and processes billions of document images annually. Fortune 500 companies, postal operators (including the U.S. Postal Service), and major government and financial institutions rely on Parascript products.
