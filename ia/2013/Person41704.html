Viktor Richardson's Summary

My current mission is to design for the web and it's users, and not for the companies' urge to expose themselves. This will form a win-win outcome.

I simply make interfaces usable and easy to interpret. I am a UX focused designer specialised on the upcoming HTML5 standard, including semantics, CSS and JavaScript with jQuery.

Internet is, and will certainly remain, my workplace and development platform. With which method and result it will occur, I let the future and my own curiousness decide.

I gladly see myself in a role where I can combine developing production work with meeting and interacting with other people.
