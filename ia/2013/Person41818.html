Sean Rasmussen's Summary

Seeking a Grails (or Spring-like) contract, ideally starting in early / mid June.

Recently completed a successful contract contract at Motability Operations, doing Grails / Groovy / Java, followed by a brief stint at News International Group.

You may find my CV in both Word and PDF formats, just below.

I've already worked in England in 1998 and 2000, where I had two short-term contracts in London. I have an Ancestry Visa.

Most likely it would be Java, as this is my strength, especially Grails which I've used several times, professionally and privately. I'm fond of Groovy, because it's elegant and expressive - and I always like to do more with less.

Incidentally, I've had brushes with the following as well: Erlang, Python, Ruby, Android, Mule).

I'm serious about making software that doesn't suck; I read books about unit testing, enterprise design patterns etc .. for fun; my most reliable pleasure comes from building something that makes something easier;I do this stuff even when there's little or no money.

As such, I prefer an environment where coding is taking seriously, seen as a craft. As a contractor with many short term mandates behind me, I've always tried to leave the place better than I found it. In my experiences, more projects founder in the absence of etiquette than technical ability.

And, while I appreciate there will always be support activity around coding, I am a coder and expect to be coding for about 75% of the time; this is how to use me best.

Looking forward to hearing from you ...

Specialties: Java (Spring, Hibernate, Maven, Grails, Groovy, JUnit, Mockito), iOS SDK. Testing, especially Unit Testing, TDD.
