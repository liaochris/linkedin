George Altman's Summary

In 1996, I co-founded Intentional Communication Consultants with my partner, Louise Altman. We combined our experience in business management, psychology and non-profit advocacy to focus on supporting organizations to develop their most valuable resource - their people.

Our work is committed to:
* Helping to develop greater openness, trust and accountability in relationships
* Expanding social-emotional competencies because how we manage our emotions and social relations is correlated with personal well-being and optimal work performance
* Equip employees with the latest information from neuroscience to enhance their self-knowledge and integrate this practical information into work performance and management practices
* Develop deeper listening skills and appreciation for differences in work and communication styles
* Help people understand and make productive use of conflict to relieve stress, improve communication and build greater trust regardless of differences
* Support employees in refining their critical thinking for improved decision making and expanded opportunites for enhanced creativity
* Develop greater resilience and flexibility in managing change
* Focus on how systems can better support optimizing people development and engagement

Through team development, small group facilitation, group learning and coaching, we work with organizations and individuals to customize and personalize their learning experience.

Some of our learning program include:

*Developing Emotional Intelligence
*Working Mindfully ~ Strategies for Greater Clarity, Focus & Balance
*Leading with the Brain in Mind ~ How Organizations Can Integrate Neuroscience into Management Practices
*Building Trust & Collaboration in Workplace Relationships
*Thinking About Thinking ~ Enhancing the Critical Thinking Process
*Transforming Conflict into Opportunity
*Building Resiliency ~ The 4 Rooms of Well-being
