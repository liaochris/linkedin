Michael Bethune's Summary

Michael Bethune has emerged as a leading / relevant Christian preacher, speaker and seminar presenter. Known for his contagious “hope-filled” messages”, he is an expert in the area of teaching, motivating and inspiring individuals towards overcoming life’s obstacles through an accurate understanding and application of biblical scripture, thus helping them to begin maximizing their fullest “God-given” potential. His personal story of life threatening struggle, perseverance and resiliency serves to further authenticate and concretize the importance of his message and ministry. Known for his passionate and precise delivery, Michael consistently impacts the lives of thousands with proven, practical and poignant content that helps many to overcome the obstacles that they face in their lives! In 2004 he was chosen to be a chapel speaker for the NFL. Since that time he has presented before several NFL teams to include the 2008 Super Bowl Champion New York Giants, Denver Broncos, Los Angeles Rams, Atlanta Falcons, Carolina Panthers, San Diego Chargers, and the Tennessee Titans. He has also presented before Coach Greg Schiano and the International Bowl Champion Rutgers University Football Team.

If you’re searching for a powerful preacher or speaker to present the next keynote address or training at your group's convention, retreat, meeting, conference or seminar, who’s realistic, relevant and ready to motivate and inspire individuals to overcome the obstacles that they face then look no further, Michael Bethune is definitely your man!

    In this thorough yet accessible resource for churches and others that are genuinely interested in helping the less fortunate, Michael Bethune presents a unique and candid look at the relationship between the church and the less fortunate as he addresses many of the issues and concerns that others have been reluctant to confront. He believes that the church and other organizations that serve the less fortunate can and must do better when it comes to how it is that we treat those that frequent our soup kitchens, clothing pantries, shelters, etc. Bethune asserts that many of today's churches have strayed far from the model of compassion that Jesus embodied and exemplified throughout His earthly ministry, a model that embraces and reaffirms the humanity of all people regardless of their social, educational, and economic location. As a result he has created a 12-stage process designed to move churches back in the right direction, back towards compassion.
