Leah Nordquist's Summary

I am a Learning and Development professional with 20 years experience in all aspects of the discipline, from analyzing needs to delivering the solution. I am known for creating and leading the implementation of training and performance improvement strategies that are directly linked to business goals and objectives of the organization. My work has had a measurable positive impact on revenue growth, cost reduction and employee satisfaction.

I have applied my expertise within the retail, financial services, insurance, publishing, healthcare management and data networking industries in both the business line support and product development functions. I bring to the table a unique understanding of Learning and Development as a business that can be a strategic advantage to the company if positioned and directed correctly. As both a Sales Consultant and Training Manager, I've sat on both sides of the desk, so to speak, and understand the integral role training professionals assume in an organization.
