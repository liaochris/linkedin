Lakita Spann's Summary

Implements solutions based sales strategies that uncover new business opportunities and manage account growth for the AT&T Business Integrated Solutions sales group. Supports territories with the largest, most complex business customers within a territory. Growth of territories is achieved primarily through acquisition of new wireless and wired customers and incremental sales of strategic wireless and wired products and services to existing customers. Knowledge of wireless and wired products, accessories, service features, pricing plans, roaming and voice features. Prepare and present professional corporate business proposals and executive presentations. Partner with Company Owned Retail Wireless Stores to provide support and training for business wired and wireless products. Partnered with Network Management employees including Network Managers, outside technicians, Engineers and External Affairs for sales referrals. Assist Account Executives with prospecting, premise visits and developing relationships at customer locations.
