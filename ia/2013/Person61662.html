Jen Miner's Summary

Co-founder, co-editor, writer at the multi-award winning travel blog TheVacationGals.com. Former Luxury travel columnist at Suite101.com (2006 - 2011). Founder and co-chair of the Los Angeles Consortium of Online Travel, whose blog LosAngelesOnlineTravel was named one of the "11 Best New Travel Blogs of 2011" by msnbc's Chris Elliott.

Speaker and/or panelist at conferences such as BlogHer, Type A Parent, BlogWorld & New Media Expo, the Los Angeles Travel & Adventure Show, and TBEX.

Specialties: Writing: Family travel/romantic vacation/girlfriend getaway articles, destination overviews, travel gear reviews.
Speaking: Travel, travel blogging, SEO, travel tech.
