Chris Wylde's Experience

Managing Director

CWMC Ltd

April 1997 – Present (16 years 5 months) Northants

CWMC is a specialist claims auditing and management consultancy firm specialising in operational review and business improvement services to insurers, their suppliers and intermediaries.

I set up the business in 1997 and have always focused on making a dramatic difference, so much so that we are now one of the leading independent insurance claims, compliance and audit specialists.

We are a team of experienced and respected industry practitioners, leaders and senior managers. We don’t apologise for a few grey hairs. Each of our consultants has at least 20 years' in-depth knowledge and understanding of the insurance, claims, compliance and audit sectors so that clients benefit from sound advice and swift, sensible implementation.

CWMC’s many longstanding clients trust us for our pragmatism, common sense and results-driven approach. Our flexible pricing structure, including fixed fee per claim arrangements, is based on results.
