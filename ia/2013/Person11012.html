Peter Cook's Summary

Peter C. Cook is President and owner of Automation Design + Entertainment, Inc. of Portage, Michigan. He is a CEDIA Certified Electronic Systems Designer as well as a CEDIA Level I Installer and a THX certified home theater designer. He has over 20 years experience in the sales, design, installation, project management, and ownership of a custom electronics design and installation firm located in southwest Michigan. He has designed, installed, and serviced residential electronic systems in mid to high end custom homes for well over a decade. He brings a wealth of experience, knowledge and practical expertise to the architectural, home building and interior design communities.

His company has been chosen as a prestigious CE Pro Top 100 Dealer on three separate occasions and has earned the exclusive HAI Five Star Award Dealer for the past ten years running.

Peter and his projects have been featured in numerous magazines, newspapers and trade journals including Electronic House, Residential Systems, Robb Report Home Entertainment Magazine, Home Systems Installer, Wall Street Journal and New York Times to name a few.

Peter's educational experience includes being an instructor for several CEDIA Expo and Regional trainings over the past decade. He has also taught various outreach events from Chicago(CRAN-AIA) to Dallas, as well as Las Vegas and San Francisco. He was a planning member of the 2007 CEDIA Management Conference Action team, was selected as the Dean of the Business College for the 2007 CEDIA Expo and most recently was selected as Chairperson of the CEDIA Electronics Lifestyles Forum event in Dallas in 2008.
