Kevin Stein's Summary

Kevin Stein is a media and marketing executive with a reputation for creative communications strategy, multi-channel marketing, and designing brand platforms. He has a history of successful business and content development in both traditional and digital media, with specialization in social media and video marketing.

Unique background includes network, cable, and syndicated television and film, both as a producer for Paramount, Columbia Pictures, VH1 with senior management experience at HBO as VP, Original Programming, CBS as Director of Late Night Programs & Interim VP, Specials & VP Development, King World Productions.

Marketing agency experience as GM at DMI Media and Marketing Solutions & Chief Strategy Officer for Buzztone, a digital and experiential marketing firm, where he worked with brands like AARP, BP, Mattel, Johnson & Johnson, Bacardi, Subway,The Gap & Starz.

Expertise includes alternative distribution channels, social media, mobile, & music branding strategy for consumer and media brands & retail entertainment. Recent consulting for Disney Kingdom Comics, New York City Board of Education, Wavefront Technology Solutions, Saatchi & Saatchi.

Media, technology & digital marketing consulting for AOL, Sony, Time, Microsoft as a digital expert for digital youth cultures. Co-authored classic history of rock and roll for Rolling Stone. Numerous productions include MTV, The Who, PBS, NBC Sports, CBS News, PRI, Zappa Family Trust, Bob Dylan.

Studied English Literature & Cultural Anthropology at Boston University, did fieldwork in Brazil for the World Health Organization. Followed with graduate work in Clinical Psychology at Swarthmore College & Harvard University. Teaching experience at Harvard Alumni College, Cal Arts & various media studies courses at Cal State University, Long Beach.

Non-profit accomplishments includes launch NASA’s Challenger Center For Space Science Education & co-founding the Jimi Hendrix Foundation, which he ran from 1994-96.
