Larry Lourcey's Summary

Larry Lourcey is a professional photographer, located in Plano, Texas. He specializes in portraiture and fine art photography. Lourcey creates images of your family or your children that will be cherished for years to come, because they go beyond showing what you look like - and capture your personality.

From business headshots to senior portraits, your portrait could not be in better hands.

Larry is also a sought-after lecturer and teacher. He has spoken at photography conventions across the U.S. and his wildly popular YouTube Channel has had over a quarter of a million views! http://www.youtube.com/LourceyPhotography
