Felix Portman's Summary

Felix has been an investor in New York and North Jersey area since 1994 as well as managing his own properties. In 2000, he started investing in properties in the Philadelphia area.
In 2003, Felix purchased Onyx Realty, a small company with little portfolios and attached it to his own. Within the next 3 years, Onyx grew into one of the largest companies in the Philadelphia area and became Onyx Management Group, a full Real Estate brokerage, managing residential and commercial properties as well as Condo and Homeowner Associations. Onyx Management Group, Inc. is a full service property management company. Since 2004 Onyx Group has been successful in the property management industry; managing 1000+ units .Onyx Management provides personalized services for every client to meet their individual wants and needs. Each clients unique arrangement is negotiated between the Board of Directors, Homeowners, Landlords, Investors and Onyx Management.

Sales, Brokerage, Property Management, Rentals, Leasing, Investments, Conveyancing, Consulting.
