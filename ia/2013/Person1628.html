Adam Sherlip's Experience

Executive Director & Founder

The Hockey Foundation

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Sports industry

June 2009 – Present (4 years 3 months) Greater New York City Area

The Hockey Foundation is a non-profit that has been established to "share happiness & change lives, one puck at a time."

I founded the organization after my experience with the Islanders so that I could harness my idealism and not concern myself with ticket sales or team performance. The Hockey Foundation uses ice hockey as a means to support the quality of life & empower youth in remote/underserved regions of the world. Our pilot program is in the Himalayan region of Ladakh, in the Indian state of Jammu & Kashmir.

The Hockey Foundation has donated over 300 pieces of second-hand hockey equipmented, run on & off ice coaching clinics for over 700 players, male & female, from 4 to 45 years old. We have enlisted the support of volunteers from around the US, Canada, Europe and India to achieve these goals & continue to grow our programming and reach with minimal resources, a testament to the lean economic times & limited resources of public donors!

501(c)(3) status is pending IRS approval, application submitted in Jan 2013, incorporation as NYS not-for-profit was in Jan 2010.

Head Coach, National Team

First head coach of the Indian National Ice Hockey Team, which participates in the IIHF Challenge Cup of Asia, a developmental tournament for un-ranked Asian hockey clubs.

I've been working with players & coaches from India to ensure sustained growth & development of the national program at all levels, for male & female players.
