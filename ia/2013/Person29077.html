Dana Tucker's Summary

Dana Tucker graduated from Western Kentucky University with a bachelor's of General Studies. She studied a broad range of courses including broadcasting, speech, and corporate communications.

She has worked in advertising for the last 20 years helping clients to create brand awareness, develop marketing strategies and drive traffic into their locations to increase overall profitability.

With over two decades of advertising, marketing and sales experience, Dana Tucker has embraced the new media revolution. Showcasing the artistic abilities of her talented husband, she has helped him launch a faux design business, Bella Tucker Decorative Finishes, designing company websites, marketing, blogging, writing for popular industry publications and blogs and establishing a national reputation in the home industry for their progressive use of product and technique.

Digging into the science of internet marketing, Dana has also represented Nashville’s top adult radio stations for the past ten years including their 2009 launch of on-line digital marketing. In 2012, she launched Forest Home Media with partner, Nancy McNulty. Redefining the traditional advertising and public relations agency, Forest Home Media, located south of Nashville TN, is merging successful advertising elements of the past with the new media revolution delivering a powerful Marketing Fusion. Forest Home Media specializes in the integration of traditional advertising campaigns with new digital media to produce results oriented marketing campaigns including: search engine marketing, website development, content creation, reputation management, social media marketing and traditional media placement and production.

Dana and her husband Brooks own Bella Tucker Decorative Finishes in Nashville, TN. Dana has a personal passion for helping others achieve their design vision through paint, color, pattern and texture.
