Desiree Scales' Summary

Do you feel you can't keep up with the latest online marketing strategies? Are you worried technology is passing you by? Are you hesitant to show people your website and/or business cards because they look dated?

My passion for technology led me to create Bella Web Design, Inc. in 1998. We are a company dedicated to helping entrepreneurs optimize their online marketing through web design, branding, social media and inbound marketing. I have personally consulted with businesses such as Microsoft, Ingenico, Arby's, Delta Air Lines and Marriott.

I have been a featured online marketing and web design expert for USAToday.com, CBS News, 11Alive, NBC Nightly News and FOX News.

My company can assist you by helping find ways to use technology to promote and market your business and increase sales so you can focus on running the parts of your business you love.

I specialize in:

• Custom and template website creation
• Infusionsoft set-up, campaign building, design and strategy
• Wordpress support, setup and hosting
• E-commerce site management
• Newsletter & Blog: support, setup
• Database Design
• Social Media Consulting
• List building
• Search Engine Optimization
• Marketing Automation

I am a registered Infusionsoft Certified Consultant (ICC).

Bella Web Design also advocates helping non-profits with technology. Our division Bella Cares creates free websites for deserving non-profits every year.

About Desiree:

She started Bella in 1998 following a successful career as a flight attendant for United Air Lines and an analyst for Delta Airlines. Since the company’s launch, Desiree created a track record of success and profitability which has helped over 2,000 companies build and maintain a strong online presence.

Desiree is also available for sound bytes, speaking engagements and training sessions. Contact her directly at 770 509 8797.
