Erick G. Cardona's Summary

I am a professional UX, UI and Ix Designer, Digital Artist, Graphic Designer, Illustrator and Front-End Web Developer for Web and Applications. I have 7+ years experience.

I create project objectives and approaches for websites and app specifications through the Waterfall approach and agile approaches. I have worked hand in hand with expert Business Analysts, Certified Project Managers and Proficient Developers to gather Business Requirements for proper stakeholders. I prefer team members with at least 5+ years in their respective field and Project Managers certified by the PMI.
I develop approaches to gather User Research by researching the scope of the application in relation to potential users through contextual inquiries, surveys, focus groups and usability testing. I also develop Persona’s for proper representative users which is an invaluable approach to put the team and the client in the user’s shoes. After I define the web development needs, storyboarding takes place to find out prioritizing of the application and development functions. I tend to use Prioritization Worksheets. The next point is Designing the proper site map and task flows that pertain to the flow of the site being designed. Once all the requirements are set for website application design, I create wireframes with my favorite graphic editor of choice, Adobe Fireworks. I develop concise wireframes with complete annotations for developers. As wireframes are set in, I use them for quick prototyping for functionality. I create detailed UI designs for myself as a front end developer and others for design, development and deployment.

I have proficient administrative and social skills. I am currently a Partner and E-commerce Account Executive for Soccer Locker USA, LTD online Stores. We cater to sports clubs and associations who buy uniforms and soccer equipment. I communicate with customers, coaches and institutions to get their orders in on time from Adidas, Nike and Puma.
