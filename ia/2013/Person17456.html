Brian Bass, CPSM's Summary

Twenty-seven years of experience with professional services firms marketing, software consulting and entrepreneurship. Industries worked in range from engineering/architecture consulting, software solutions, consumer products, and early-stage start-ups.

Seasoned copywriter with wide array of experience encompassing marketing and public relations, technical, branding reinforcement, and creative humor.

Experienced Deltek Vision CRM specialist with expert first-hand industry knowledge and portfolio of over 200 clients. Expertise in process planning, adaptive organizational behavioral change, system configuration and design, and end-user training.
