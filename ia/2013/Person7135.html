Andrew Pebley's Summary

I help people transform into more productive, more self-accountable, and better performing individuals. Through mentoring, coaching, training and development, I assists individuals, teams and organizations in building and sustaining motivation, managing performance, and improving overall processes.

My extensive background includes modification of training modules for mid-sized businesses in Southern New England. I've provided data and training implementation advisory of performance management system for Yale University. An initiative of the Obama Administration, I have also developed a "Service Bridge to Work" Training program for non-profit organizations.

My unique coaching process provides structure-based tools that help clients grow and develop the gifts within, increasing creativity, focusing attention, and improving work place activity.

Specialties: Training Coordination, Training and Development programs, Organizational Development, Building and Sustaining Motivation, Program Evaluation, Needs Assessement, Impact Assessment, Efficiency Assessment, Employee Development, Process Improvement, Performance Management, Management & Leadership Coaching, Mentoring
