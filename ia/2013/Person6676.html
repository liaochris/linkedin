André Lopes Vasconcelos' Summary

Since I've finished college I've been working in both the creative and strategic sides of Marketing, namely in its more direct and interactive forms, first as a copywriter at Proximity Portugal, then as a strategic planner and project manager at Perspectiva and after that as a senior concept writer at DoubleYou.

Currently I am back to Portugal, as Proximity_DIGITAL's Creative Supervisor.

Even though I am a creative at heart, I've been developing and perfecting my knowledge in Information Architecture and strategic planning, in order to broaden my scope of the world wide field of Interactive creativity.

My main goal is to continue working in the field of Interactive and Online creativity and strategy, by providing both creative input and strategic insight at the highest level. Always pushing boundaries and aiming at true innovation.

Specialties

Creative concept development, creative copywriting, content management, strategic planning, information architecture, presentation preparation, multilingual translations (portuguese, english and spanish).
