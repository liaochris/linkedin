Kirsten Weiss' Summary

kirsten_weiss2001@yahoo.com

Why is good marketing like alchemy?

The medieval alchemist’s goal was to distill a raw material to its pure essence through a magical catalyst.

In marketing, what makes you great is the raw material. The pure essence is how you benefit your clients. And your magical catalyst is emotion – the benefit you provide must resonate at an emotional level. Emotion is the element that drives your prospects and clients to action.

Many businesses struggle to get leads online. I’ll help you integrate these ingredients and develop a web presence that generates sales, and builds your brand.

I graduated with honors from Thunderbird with an MBA in International Management, and a specialization in global marketing. So I joined the Peace Corps, working in the marketing department of the Estonian Tourism Board. Key strategies from the tourism marketing plan I developed remained in use 15 years on.

That experience launched a fourteen-year career working in the fringes of the former Soviet Union, Afghanistan, and the Middle East. I managed a company with 70 employees and over 7,500 clients in the Republic of Georgia. On a shoe-string budget, I created a country-wide campaign in Afghanistan that generated a 400% spike in lead generation for participating firms. My clients have included global institutions such as the World Bank, IFC, and USAID.

In 2008, I returned home to San Mateo, CA. Now, I work on local and international marketing projects, enhancing the web presence of my clients through strategic branding and marketing, developing web content, and implementing social media programs.

I’ve never met a dessert I didn’t like, and a guilty pleasure is watching Ghost Whisperer reruns . And after learning to love my inner Walter Mitty, I became a mystery author, and have published three novels under the misterio press imprint.

Go to my website, http://marketingalchemy.net for a quote or to get access to my free B2B social media course.
