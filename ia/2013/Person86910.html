Mark Nutting's Summary

Received the NSCA 2009 Personal Trainer of the Year award.

I have been in Personal Training and Health Cub management for 32 years. Though born and raised in Maine, I worked for 5 years in Boston and 15 years in New York City. While I am still a Personal Trainer had been and Group Ex instructor at Saco Sport & Fitness in Saco, ME, the past 10 years have been focused on creating a specialty in business coaching, branding, sales, and marketing of services. This expertise has created opportunities for me to present at conferences nationally and consult with companies on increasing revenues. I'm currently writing a book on the Business of Personal Training as well as a Webinar Series and live trainings.

Specialties

Personal Trainer education, business coaching, branding, sales, and marketing of services.
