Helma Lieberwerth's Summary

Helma Lieberwerth is the founder of Level Five Quaning Enterprises* and inventor of the unique Quaning methodology. Helma’s career is rooted in the international IT-world and small businesses. She is an internationally certified NLP Master Practitioner, NLP trainer, trained in business management, systemic work, energetic medical science and shamanism. In the years she worked as an independent trainer/coach in business, she guided thousands of people to work and live more effectively and with better results. She also trained trainers and coaches to do their work at their best.
She is known for her enthusiastic way of guidance, where personal development and growth are at the core heading for personal and professional success.
Since Level Five Quaning Enterprises has been establishment, Helma looks after teaching and presenting on her methodology, quality compliance and further development of content and methodology. A book about the philosophy and methodology Quaning is on it’s way.

*Level Five Quaning provides software that allows coaches and consultants to do analyzes and to offer solutions directly at a quantum level. This leads to faster and more sustainable results to their customers and in their own business.
