Mark Ragsdale's Summary

I was a multi-franchise, multi-location automobile and motorcycle dealer who sold the last of my stores in August 2008—Just before the crash.

I have written the first book which provides a detailed critique and expose' of the automobile industry without villifying car dealers. The book, titled Car Wreck (Langdon Street Press, Softcover, 229 pp., $15.95) is consumer-oriented.

FACT: Buyers Can Lose up to Half their Money in the First Week of Ownersip.

However, Car Wreck de-emphasizes the importance of consumer negotiating skills at the dealership level. There simply is not enough money in it for them compared to the other BIGGER Stuff I teach about in the book.

Rather, Car Wreck educates readers on the penalties of depreciation and long-term finance commitments. It covers the myriad contentious relationships between dealers, bankers, and automakers. And places a mirror squarely in the face of our federal government.

Therefore, Car Wreck is a great read for dealers and other industry professionals, AND, is an ideal handout for family members, employees AND CUSTOMERS at the dealership level.

Specialties

Teaching in an amusing, entertaining, and memorable way. Speaking in an engaging way. Writing about complex, interrelated topics in an understandable and enjoyable format. I cover a lot of ground quickly and truthfully.
