President / Founder

Children's Holiday Magic Project, Inc.

December 2003 – Present (9 years 11 months) San Diego, CA

Mission Statement: To brighten the lives of children of all ages through the gift of songs and stories.

The first CD was produced in 2003, when professional voice actor Jeff Gelder was tasked in a Landmark Education class to come up with a project that would enhance the community and ultimately take on a life of its own. Holiday Magic, a Gift for Children of All Ages CDs are created each year and distributed to over 26 Children's Hospitals worldwide for the kids who must spend their holidays in the hospitals. In 2010 Jeff founded the official organization called Children's Holiday Magic Project, Inc. www.holidaymagiccd.org

Children’s Holiday Magic Project, Inc.(CHMP) is a tax exempt 501(c)(3) not-for-profit corporation
