Brad Lucas' Summary

I am the founder and CEO of Beacon Hill a high tech software development consultancy in New York providing custom software solutions to the Financial Services and New Media industries.

I have twenty years of professional experience developing software for a number of industries. I am unique in that in addition to being a respected business manager and technical leader I am still active in developing software on a daily basis. My passion for programming exists to this day and I find enjoyment in building systems of value that work well.

My background has led me to building a company that provides custom software development to growing firms. We have helped small companies that are growing fast and established companies with over extended resources, that needed systems built quickly and reliably. My expectation has been that client firms need the experience and quality that I am able to deliver both personally and from my team. Also, by building a company with a software development focus and a high level of accountability our clients are more able to focus on their own core competencies.

Prior to starting Beacon Hill I worked for a number of well known New York banks including Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Lehman Brothers and Bear Stearns. Before moving to New York I enjoyed building systems for the portfolio management division at Fidelity in Boston. Also, my passion for building interesting applications has also driven me to be a co-founder in two Internet startups, Anywhere Enterprises and Fanned Out.

I am interested in connecting here on LinkedIn and will accept invitations to connect (LION / TOPLINKED / Accept All Invites / Invite me / Will not IDK / Open Networker / 27,500+ connections).

I can be reached by email at brad@beaconhill.com.
