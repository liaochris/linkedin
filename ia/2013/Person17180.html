Brian Bachofner's Summary

Software & Intelligence professional who's Results oriented, customer focused with proven experience in a variety of Direct sales, Alliance management, Channel and Sales Enablement roles. Skilled Field Marshall at creating and aligning teams behind a strategic objective, while ensuring that revenue targets are met. Ability to impact change and build successful organizations augmented by excellent verbal and written communication, relationship management and leadership skills.

I currently focus on Alliance Development & Management for InsideView's Industry-leading SAAS based CRM Intelligence solution. My primary focus is to earn mindshare and drive successful partnerships with complimentary Software ISVs, Sales Training, and Marketing Solution partners. With over 10+ years in the B2B Data and Sales Intelligence market, I've seen the transformation from phonebooks, to lead lists and now the Social Web as a vital way to engage with customers.

I'm also contribute and manage content for Social Selling University, the premiere resource for insights for CSOs and individual contributors looking to deploy Social Selling Initiatives. In my role, I work with leading experts to deliver thought leadership and establish InsideView as a key component in any Social Selling Initiative.
