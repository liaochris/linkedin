Brett Greene's Summary

♦ Business Development Director | IT & Creative Staffing Provider | Marketing Strategist | Connector | Community Builder | Speaker | Mentor

Brett Greene connects companies and people to resources that help them to thrive. He is helping capitalized startups and mid to large sized companies with website and mobile app development, design and talent recruiting.

He is a passionate Senior Talent Acquisition Leader & Recruiting Strategist working with a dynamic network of IT professionals including project managers, senior software engineers, web developers, business analysts, SDETs, DBAs, and architects.

Brett is a seasoned marketing strategist who has been engaged in digital communication services since 1999. He has zeal for developing strong partner and client relationships, turning data insights into action, and connecting people, companies and communities.

Industries Brett has worked with include: technology, ecommerce, mobile and cloud-based applications, events, entertainment, and government. His clients have ranged from the Los Angeles Mayor's Office to startups and Fortune 500 companies.

Brett has worked with the investors and founders of Klout and mentored Founder Institute, LOHAS and Techstars companies. He is also co-founder of Seattle Tech Meetup (Seattle's largest monthly event) and Director of House of Genius Seattle. Brett blogs at Huffington Post, Social Media Today, All Things WOMM (WOMMA) and Blind Influence.

He has spoken nationally for organizations including Blog World, Puget Sound Business Journal, PodCamp, LOHAS Forum, Davinci Institute, Seattle Design Center, Ignite Boulder, Colorado University, and Denver University's Daniels College of Business MBA Program. You can also connect with Brett and his 79,000+ followers on Twitter at @brettgreene.
