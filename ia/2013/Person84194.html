Maggie Varney's Summary

A licensed cosmetologist and entrepreneur with forty years experience in the beauty industry. In October of 2003, Maggie founded Wigs 4 Kids, a non-profit organization that provides wigs to children with cancer and similar appearance related challenges that cause hair loss. Wigs 4 Kids established a Wellness Center where the organization expanded its programming to include medical and educational services. Maggie has a Degree in Business and is a Hair Restoration Accredited Instructor. The Detroit News bestowed the honor of Michiganian of the Year upon Maggie in 2010 for her work with Wigs 4 Kids.

Maggie has been inducted into the Macomb County Foundation Hall of Fame to be recognized for the year 2011, and was chosen by Art Van to be a recipient of the 2011 Art Van Hope Award.

Just recently, Maggie’s full-service, environmentally-friendly salon has been recognized as one of the “101 Best and Brightest Sustainable Companies” in Michigan for 2012 by the MBPA. Maggie continues to be an exemplary active member of the community and was nominated for the Athena Award through the Macomb County Chamber.

Maggie Varney's Experience

Founder & CEO

Wigs 4 Kids Wellness Center & Salon

2003 – Present (10 years)

Wigs 4 Kids is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization that reaches out to kids and young adults with hair loss. This program is available to any child or teen through age 18 in Michigan.

Our main focus is on cancer patients and includes other young people with similar appearance related challenges that cause hair loss such as alopecia, trichotillomania, lupus, hydrocephalus, burns and other disorders.

Wigs 4 Kids provides custom cut and styled wigs and services at no cost to kids and their families and accepts donations from caring individuals, companies and organizations. Wigs 4 Kids is funded solely on the basis of contributions. Any financial support received presents a greater opportunity to help kids in need of our assistance.

In June of 2010, the organization established the Wigs 4 Kids Wellness Center & Salon. The Wellness Center provides a safe, nurturing environment for the children and their families. Wigs 4 Kids is continuing its current mission, while expanding programming to include Medical and Educational support services as well as a Cosmetology Training program for licensed cosmetologists.

We are located at 30126 Harper Ave. in St. Clair Shores, MI. We invite you to stop in at any time for a tour of our facility.

Most recently, Wigs 4 Kids has been honored as one of the "Best and Brightest Wellness Champions" by the MBPA for the year 2012 for our focus on the health & well-being of our guests, team and the environment.

Program Education Facilitator & Volunteer

Look Good...Feel Better

1990 – Present (23 years)

Each two-hour, hands-on workshop includes a 12-step skin care/make-up application lesson, demonstration of options for dealing with hair loss, and nail care techniques. Held at comprehensive care clinics, hospitals, ACS offices, and community centers, local group programs are organized by the American Cancer Society, facilitated by LGFB-certified cosmetologists, and aided by general volunteers. Patients in various stages of treatment receive make-over tips and personal attention from professionals trained to meet their needs.
