Joel Haffey's Summary

Over the last thirteen years I have worked to develop and execute successful online marketing strategies for both B2B and B2C companies. A primary focus of this experience has been in PPC where I have managed a variety of national and international ad campaigns on major search advertising platforms including Google AdWords, Microsoft AdCenter, Yahoo! and Facebook. I have a record of success by adhering to exacting campaign creation standards to achieve the highest degree of relevancy and accountability. I am also experienced in SEO and have defined specifications for site architecture, navigation, content formatting and other elements related to on-page site optimization. Additionally, I have consulted both in-house and freelance clients on launching successful inbound link-building campaigns. Through my experience in search engine marketing I have crafted a multi-disciplinary understanding of the needs and expectations of large and small businesses, and how online marketing has the potential to deliver on well defined strategic objectives. I strive to leverage this experience by contributing to an effective team dynamic to achieve meaningful results.
