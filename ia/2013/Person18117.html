Biography

Brook Ellingwood is Director of Interactive at KCTS Television in Seattle, and an experienced leader of media staff and projects.

He has focused on change management and staff development as key factors for success in digital experience and content strategy. Brook's multi-platform and multi-channel understanding has solved problems for broadcast media and online retail companies. His early background working for digital media startups as well as live performance and event producers informs his approach to future-proof product development.

Brook is a graduate of the Master of Communication in Digital Media (MCDM) program at the University of Washington.

Specialties: Organizational Process and Development, Creative Media, Customer Experience, Cross-Channel Customer Experience, User Experience, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Content Management
