Luke McCarthy's Experience

Rugby Programme Manager

Cricket for Change

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Sports industry

September 2013 – Present (2 months) London and UK

The aim of Dallaglio Foundation Rugby for Change is to have a profound impact on the aspiration and self-esteem of young people to make positive life choices. It is a street engagement initiative that uses the inherent values of rugby to help young people at risk change their lives. The programme will empower young people to build positive aspirations, take responsibility for their actions and feel valued in their communities. The programme is currently delivered in London, Leeds, Newcastle and Liverpool, and the intention is to expand to more cities in the leadup to the 2015 Rugby World Cup.

I am responsible for:
* overseeing delivery of the programme
* managing a team of coaches
* managing relationships with delivery and strategic partners
* measurement and evaluation to demonstrate the impact of the programme
* strategic development of the programme and wider national roll out

Luke McCarthy's Projects

  • Sport for development mapping

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Luke McCarthy, Dominic Cotton, Lucy Allen

    Identifying the locations of sport for development projects in the UK, with the aim of providing better information on which to make decisions in the sector. This will include identifying areas where there would be benefit in establishing new projects, facilitating more effective partnership working (across different sports or between sports projects and the youth sector) and informing funding bids.

    The project is supported by Ordnace Survey who are able to both map the positions of projects and also provide information on the distribution of these relative to indices of multiple deprivation.
