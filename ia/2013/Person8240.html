Anna Wedekind's Summary

I am a Minneapolis-based graphic and web designer with a passion for international design. I strive to create clean, innovative and functional design and am very detail-oriented. I work efficiently and excel at prioritizing and finding ways to improve processes.

Branding and identity, web advertising, typography, print publications, marketing materials and layout.
