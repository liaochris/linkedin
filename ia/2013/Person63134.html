Jessica Murr's Summary

I have spent the last 10 years observing businesses all over the north state and have been a small business owner since 2006. I understand the hardships many business owners face and recognize the strength it takes to stay in business. My experience in social media marketing comes from the school of hardknocks and for that reason I have unique insight to the successful use of social media marketing for small businesses. I am constantly searching for new information and technologies in an ever-evolving industry so that other small business owners may benefit from my experience.

Specialties

Start up marketing strategies, social media integration to existing businesses and non-profit organizations, e-commerce development, market research collection and evaluation, internet marketing best practices.
