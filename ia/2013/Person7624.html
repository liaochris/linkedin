Andy Zain's Summary

Entrepreneur with 15 years of experience in building Internet and mobile business in South East Asia.

Pioneer in mobile VAS business, launched the first content provider and ringtone service in Indonesia. Helped in establishing services for top global brands across mobile/Internet industry, including Disney, Yahoo!, EA, Gameloft, FIFA and fast growing startups such as mig33, Vuclip, Snaptu, UC Mobile, and Payfone.

Founder & organizer of MobileMonday Indonesia, a networking forum of 400 mobile centric companies in Indonesia. Director of Jakarta Founder Institute, mentorship & incubation program for startup in Indonesia. I actively help and angel investing in several high growth startup in the region.

Quoted by the Economist, Asian Wall Street Journal, Jakarta Post, TechCrunch and other local/regional publications and blogs.

Specialties

Go-To-Market strategy, business development, mobile/Internet/new media, emerging market.
