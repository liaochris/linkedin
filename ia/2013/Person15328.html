European Directories

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

November 2005 – September 2008 (2 years 11 months)

Completed assignment and returned to Australia.

"European Directories" is a pan-European local search and lead generation company providing local commercial search for consumers and lead generation to advertisers through a variety of solutions in print, online, mobile and voice media.

The company has market-leading positions in the Netherlands, Finland, Denmark, Austria, Sweden, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Poland and Gibraltar.

Some 5,000 people are employed across the group servicing over 750,000 customers.

The business was formed in July 2005. In 2008 European Directories acquired Ditel in Poland and Gouden Gids in the Netherlands.

Day-to-day operations are conducted at a country level by operating companies which are located in each of the countries.

A small Group Office co-ordinates key initiatives across the Group, especially in online, and promotes best-practice behaviour amongst the operating companies.
