Dennis Vreeke's Publications

  • The Renegotiation of Strategic Alliances

    Authors: Dennis Vreeke

    This study provides an empirical investigation of the occurrence and determinants of renegotiations of governance mechanisms in strategic alliances. While some scholars have argued that managing inter-organizational relationships over time is usually more important than crafting the initial formal design, research has mainly focused on the ex ante design stage. In fact, the existing literature on renegotiations of alliances is very limited. Nevertheless, several dimensions were found to influence post-formation dynamics in strategic alliances, including alliance characteristics, contract characteristics and alliance process characteristics.

  • Effects of Multi-tiered Loyalty Programs on Consumer Behavior

    • May 21, 2007
    Authors: Dennis Vreeke

    To finish my undergraduate studies (Honours Bachelor of Science in International Economics and Business), I wrote a thesis about multi-tiered loyalty programs. This paper examines how loyalty programs with different status levels affect consumers’ expectations, satisfaction and spending patterns. Several hypotheses were tested by distributing surveys to 214 respondents.
