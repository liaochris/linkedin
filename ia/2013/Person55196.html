Christopher Lowe's Summary

ICA (Independent Corporate Access) is the first Corporate Access consultancy in the world. It focuses on bringing high quality Corporate Access opportunities to a community of leading investors across the globe. The aim is to bring qualified, real investor demand and opportunities for Technology, Media and Telecoms stocks and act as
an intermediary between the investment opportunity and the potential investor. The focus of the business should grow to encompass all sectors, in the next 2-3 years.

The consultancy is a non trading platform, is focused on partnering with a number of leading investors to maximise the quality of access for companies of choice. This outsourcing and information sharing opportunity between ICA and said investor, will enable higher quality opportunities for the Investor community. These opportunities will be delivered at very cost effective manner.

The Corporate community will receive high quality information, intelligence and access opportunities with investors that only have real investor demand to meet with them. Preparation, excellence in organization and detail, as well as continued flows of interest from a rapidly investor base will determine growing opportunities. This, in time, will encourage companies to dis-intermediate brokers and other parties in providing Corporate Access. These institutions feel it is necessary to use research as a connection to generate access. This is not the case and Corporate Access can be delivered successfully without this.

The consultancy has first mover advantage and is the first consultancy of its type to challenge the monopolistic dominance that Investment Banks have upon this niche and fast growing revenue generator, in today's financial world.
