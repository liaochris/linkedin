Fotis Siafarikas' Summary

As a founder of a successful and uprising company (App Camelot, http://appcamelot.com) my goals remain the same. I want to work with as many talented people as possible. I have setup a network of highly skilled professionals that work remotely from the convenience of their homes, or anywhere else they like.
