Joel Safran's Summary

After earning a B.Sc in Management, Joel went on to become a planner and problem solver in the graphic arts industry. Years later, a 5 year mid life retirement was spent in the Caribbean on a 32 ton sailboat. He lived an anchor in various small Caribbean harbours. He operated and maintained this vessel. It was his home for 5 years. He also had earned a US CoastGuard captain's certificate as a "100 ton Master".

Once settled in St. Petersburg, Joel was encouraged to enter this profession. From the very beginning, he chose to specialize in corrective therapies that would be effective in dealing with complex chronic conditions.

After 11 years as a Licensed Massage Therapist in St. Petersburg, Florida, Joel moved to Toronto, Ontario, Canada. A long distance relationship had resulted in marriage to his Canadian wife. To become a registered Therapist there, it was necessary to satisfy their 2200 hour educational requirements and to pass their very difficult practical and written exams.

After 14 years of treating mostly complex chronic conditions, he returned to The States and has settled in Charlotte, North Carolina. Over the years the following therapies have come to define the focus of his practice.
