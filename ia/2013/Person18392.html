Bruno Teboul's Summary

Bruno Teboul graduated from University of Paris Est-Val de Marne (Master in Philosophy of Sciences), and from Ecole Polytechnique, EHESS (Post-Graduate Diploma in Cognitive Sciences) and HEC
Executive MBA. Bruno is currently PhD. candidate and Tempory Teacher in Management Sciences (Marketing) under the direction of Professor Pierre Volle at University Paris-Dauphine. His academic research focuses on the impact and penetration of Neuromarketing as a new discipline. Bruno has an experience of 18 years in the Retail and Digital functions in Operational Management and Executive Management in large groups or start-ups such as Boulanger, Office Depot, QXL, PPR, La Poste, Brandalley, Galeries Lafayette, Carrefour, Devoteam...
Bruno, is an expert in Marketing, CRM, and he is a Digital Evangelist.
He is a french author of several articles published in Les Echos.fr and Le Monde.fr and of one book titled "Absolu Marketing": Web 3.0, Big Data, Neuromarketing", Editions Kawa, avril 2013.
