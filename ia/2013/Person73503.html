Keenan Payne's Summary

Hey there, my name is Keenan Payne. I am a web designer and developer based out Missoula, Montana. I have been working professionally for over four years now, and have experience in the whole creative process when it comes to developing web sites and web applications for small and large businesses.

I have experience in Photoshop and Illustrator designing websites, logos, banners, and posters. I can also take those web designs and turn them into pixel-perfect, fully functional websites using HTML and CSS (5 and 3 when appropriate) that are search engine friendly, and very user-friendly. I have also worked with the WordPress content management system for five years now, and have experience developing themes from scratch, modifying existing themes, maximizing search engine optimization, as well as developing rudimentary plugins from scratch.

I am also competent in many programming languages such as PHP, JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, as well as database applications such as MySQL, SQLite, and SQL Server. I love building applications from the ground-up and am always looking to expand my skills in programming.
