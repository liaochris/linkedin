  • The Guide Online Auction Section

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Andrew Dymski, Gray MacKenzie, Brandon Jones

    We worked with the team at the Guide to create a local hub to host information about auctions happening in the Central Pennsylvania area. The site had to be editable by individual auctioneers and easy for them to navigate.

    What resulted was a customized Wordpress solution that allowed the Guide staff to set up participating auctioneers with customized roles within the site. These custom roles allowed them to only edit portions of the site that pertained to their business.

    Auctioneers can now publish posts to the auction site in real time where they can share pictures of their auction offerings as well as YouTube videos containing their latest updates.

    This site changes the game in the auction industry because it is focused first locally and it empowers auction owners with an unparalleled ability to generate their own content on a collective platform.
