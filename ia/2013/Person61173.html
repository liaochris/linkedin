Jeffrey Davis' Summary

Jeffrey Davis at Speak Clear Communications is a former Adjunct Lecturer in Public Speaking, Articulation and Accent Reduction who has lectured at St. John's University and Bronx Community College. He started Speak Clear Communications in 2005 with a stated purpose of helping struggling speakers to become audience-focused, relaxed, purposeful, and articulate.

There are four different teaching tracks to choose from; planned speaking, impromptu speaking, accent reduction, and vocal coaching. Individual, private lessons as well as group and seminar packages are available. On-site and off-site coaching is available. Jeffrey has offices in New York City, Red Bank, New Jersey, and Iselin, New Jersey.

Specialties: Jeffrey and his Speak Clear co-workers have a speciality in working with non-native English speakers.
