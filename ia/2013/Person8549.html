Annoesjka Novák's Summary

Annoesjka is a trained epidemiologist with over 20 years experience in the pharmaceutical industry in clinical drug research, health economics & outcomes research, patient-reported outcomes, pricing, reimbursement and funding at both an international and in the Netherlands.

She has covered a variety of indications (oncology, chronic systemic infections, HIV, diabetis) and is mostly specialized in the reimbursement of expensive hospital therapies. Through her experience Annoesjka is able to quickly scan the options and requirements for your product and assist you in making a masterplan towards optimal reimbursement and funding.

Specialties

Strategic analysis, pragmatic solutions, coaching of professionals.
