Elyshia Brooks, MBA, DBA (ABD)'s Summary

As the Founder-President/CEO with Elyshia Brooks, Inc., I, Elyshia Brooks am a professional branding, marketing and business coach/consultant who develops and implements marketing strategies and solutions to assist businesses and organizations in achieving their marketing & sales goals and objectives.

My 15 years of the marketing industry career includes direct marketing, public relations, marketing and business strategy, advertising, brand management and event planning.

I earned my bachelor’s degree in Marketing from Albany State University and my MBA in Marketing from Strayer University. I am currently a doctorial candidate at Walden University pursuing a D.B.A.. in Marketing and Business Strategy to be complete in 2013.

Elyshia Brooks, Inc. specializes in marketing and business strategy, brand management for small to mid-size companies, and non-profit organizations. I believe that marketing education is an integral part in the development and success of a successful business or organization; therefore, I travel conducting marketing, branding workshops and seminars across the country.

Addionally, I speak on many Marketing and Leadership Topics.

Topics Include but not Limited To:

* Lean Marketing Strategies™
* Creating a Marketing Plan in 7 Steps™
* Marketing Communications
* What Does Your Brand Say About You?
* 100 Marketing Tools to Increase Your Business or Organization
* Marketing for Fun or Profit
* Planning a Top Notch Event Every Time
