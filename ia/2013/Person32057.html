David Juniper's Projects

  • DKJ Tutor

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: David Juniper, Lynda Williams, Michelle Coulter

    I am starting a new project offering free video tutorials in web design and development! I am reading Salman Khan’s One World Classroom and it inspired me to continue pursuing an educational path congruent with my work at DKJ Studios. Khan’s main thesis so far in the book is that Internet- based education offers the flexibility, the dynamism and the variety so desperately needed by today’s students. The classroom is largely outmoded, he says, and will be either replaced or greatly enhanced by an independent online learning strategies.

    Unlike the traditional classroom model, Internet-based education offers students the opportunity to become much more fully involved and active in their own education. Learning then becomes something they do, rather than something that is done to them. A critical distinction!
    I love teaching, and the DKJ Tutor project will enable me to continue my passion in a new and fascinating way. Starting next week, I will post a 5-10 minute video lesson every Tuesday on an aspect of web development and web design. The lessons will be available from this website as well as off of Youtube, and will be entirely free.

    The purpose of these lessons will be assist student who are self-learning as well as those taking classroom-based web design and development courses such as the ones offered by BCIT and NAIT. If you are a student struggling with a concept, please contact me at tutor@dkjstudios.com and I will see if I can address your needs in a video lesson. Likewise, if you are an instructor and see students struggling with a particular concept let me know and perhaps I can help shed some light.

    The site was developed using the WordPress content management system.
