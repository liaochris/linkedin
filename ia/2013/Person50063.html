Greg Bussmann's Summary

Greg Bussmann is a digital marketing professional specializing in Content Marketing, Social Media, Online Advertising, E-mail Marketing, Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Web Design. Greg has extensive experience in the IT, data storage, printing and office products industries.

Greg is a native of Saint Louis, MO, and currently lives in Ballwin (Saint Louis, MO).

Greg is the Digital Marketing Manager at PMI Computer Supplies, a B2B IT products distributor based in Saint Louis, where he is responsible for PMI's overall online presence, including digital content creation, maintaining the corporate blog, search engine optimization (SEO), social media, paid online advertising and email marketing, among other duties.
