Heather Pennington's Summary

In 2007 I graduated Summa Cum Laude from the University of Southern California with a BA in Theatre and English Literature.

After graduating, I worked on a plethora of jobs in the film industry as well as writing, editing, and tutoring. Some of my experiences include editing screenplays; tutoring students from China, Korea, Japan; voice acting; and working in the communications department of an environmental organization.
