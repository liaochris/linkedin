Dan Mall's Summary

I love projects that can challenge my skills, clients that don't settle for the same old out-of-the-box solutions. I want to work with people that thoughtfully questions my layouts, argue with my color preferences, and demand higher contrast, all for the purpose of achieving a vision.

Smart interactivity is my type of work: edgy and completely unforgiving, the kind of stuff that makes me love sitting in front of a computer with a sketchbook until the sun comes up.

Specialties

Art direction, conceptual thinking, user experience, information architecture, graphic design.

Technical skills include expertise with Photoshop, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flash development, Flash animation
