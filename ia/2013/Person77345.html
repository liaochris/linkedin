Karl Saynor's Summary

I'm digital strategy and user experience director. What exactly does that mean? Well, in my 15 or so years working with clients, it invariably means lots of different things. However in general it means that I:

1) Work with board-level executives helping them define and implement digital strategy
2) Maintain a razor-sharp focus on delivering against business objectives and users' needs
3) Quickly build credibility with clients at all levels as a digital expert and UX champion
4) Challenge the status-quo and the accepted way of doing things
5) Develop a strong and genuine rapport with all members of a project team

Like most who have been consultants in this industry for some time, I've tackled a wide variety of industries, platforms and technologies. I would say however that my specialities are eCommerce, enterprise and complex systems, mobile applications and above all, establishing (and delivering against) a strategic experience vision.

Having a combined management consulting and digital agency background, I bring a broad and adaptable skill set. I like to think I bring a refreshing clarity and intellectual rigour to the commercially-critical business of strategy and user experience. I'm articulate, personable, and commercially-astute and excel in distilling complex problems into straight-forward solutions.

I particularly enjoy working with fellow web entrepreneurs: I like helping them transform an idea or vision into a detailed product strategy, definition and user experience. My work in this area is often used to support early stage and Series A funding rounds.

Specialities: digital strategy, user experience, user experience strategy, digital product strategy, business models, monetization, startups, usability, information architecture, accessibility, experience design, ia, ux, interaction design, user journeys, personas, web strategy, start-ups, entrepreneur, head of ux, ux director, mentoring, leading teams
