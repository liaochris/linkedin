Don Ehrhardt's Summary

Fifteen years of experience in Information Technology, starting when I was 14 years old as the first young person to work at University of Mississippi Medical Center’s Information Technology Department (One of the largest IT environments in Mississippi). I have a strong background in user training, end user and desktop support, documentation, network, security, systems and telecommunications troubleshooting. Experience in major enterprise network and Data Center operating hardware and software. Recently I have been an Assistant IT Manager for a twelve store region in Yellowstone National Park with Delaware North Companies Inc., Operations Manager for Utah Transportation Group's companies in Montana, one of two Network Systems Administrators for SunGard Higher Education at a site with around 6,000 users. One of the world’s leading companies, SunGard annual revenue exceeded $5 billion, was ranked on the Fortune 500 and is the largest privately held business software and IT services company.

Currently I am a Network Engineer at Blue Cross Blue Shield of Mississippi, the largest insurance provider in Mississippi. The Blue Cross Blue Shield Association serves over 90 million people - Nearly one in three Americans.
