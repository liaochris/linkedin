Angela Giese's Summary

I love making amazing websites that others will use, love, and find useful. I focus on user experience and ease of use. Users should be able to visit a website, instantly understand what it is, and quickly navigate to find great information/products/services. My goal is to de-clutter, speed up load time, and get visitors to the right information quickly.

Unless instructed not to I make my builds responsive so they work on smartphones and tablets. It's ridiculous nowadays to not have a website work on mobile.

Content Management Systems like WordPress and creating Web templates are a specialty of mine. Reusability and ease of use are a huge focus, especially for entrepreneurs who don't have a team of designers and developers on staff to help them with their business.

I focus on clean and “minimalist” code, making the most use out of the least amount of code. My code is clean, tabbed, and commented for ease of reading and editing later on by myself or other developers. I sometimes make a game out of how much I can do with as little code as I can.

Specialties: HTML, XHTML, HTML 5, CSS, CSS 3, Javascript, jQuery, PHP, MySQL, WordPress, Web Development
