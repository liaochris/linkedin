Jimmy Hendricks' Summary

Jimmy Hendricks is Co-Founder and CEO of Deal Current, the nation’s fastest growing private label daily deal software company. Deal Current helps local media publishers implement their own revenue-generating daily deal platform in 30 days or less. Under Hendricks’ leadership, Deal Current has achieved rapid market penetration in 100+ cities serving respected media leaders including American Consolidated Media, Journal Broadcast Group, Buffalo News, and the Pittsburgh Post Gazette. He has closed high profile industry-changing partnerships with industry giants like Triton Digital Media. Deal Current and Jimmy Hendricks have been noted or quoted in Wall Street Journal, Fast Company, MediaPost, Kiplinger, San Diego Business Journal, Crain’s Chicago Business, and more. Hendricks has previously presented at industry conferences across the U.S. including: BIA/Kelsey, CommNexus, San Diego’s Young Entrepreneurs Conference, and UCLA’s Entrepreneurs Conference. A 2010, 2011, and 2012 San Diego Business Journal Most Admired CEO and San Diego Daily Transcript Top Influentials nominee, Hendricks brings 10 years of experience in sales strategy, product development, advertising sales, and team building to the Deal Current team. Prior to founding Deal Current, Hendricks was an industry executive for Active.com in San Diego, an online registration company with over $300M in annual revenue. Jimmy Hendricks received a Masters in Accounting from University of Missouri.
