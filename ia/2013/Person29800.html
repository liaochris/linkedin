Daniel Migliorelli's Summary

UX with academic background on Graphic Design and Visual Arts. UCD advocate. Methodical, yet down-to-earth. 5 years as full time freelance managing small teams and projects. Former Lemon Wallet UX.

Expert:
[wireframes, lo-fi / hi-fi prototyping, visual mockups / comps, design documentation, UI html5/css3 prototypes and production visual framework set, small teams and small projects management]

Also part of my daily work:
[Iterative prototyping, use cases flows, user research surveys, user testing (user journeys, qualitative and quantitative testing, etc)]

Want to learn, never done:
[Personas, large scale UX analysis and strategy]

I´ve been designing webapps and websites since I was a student, I´ve designed mobile interfaces since the pre-iphone era (J2Me). I´ve been 5 year full time freelance, managing my own dev team and being part of bigger ones too. In 2012 I´ve joined Lemon Inc., as Web and Mobile UI Designer, later on, that turned into UI/UX. Inside the Lemon team I advocated to start a long and slow change from a deductive-subjective design process to a inductive-objective one. In order to achieve this, I´ve worked on expanding the Lemon Design team set of tools and knowledge about UX. This will allow the team to get closer to a User-Centered Design process.

I'm concerned about web open technologies and standards that allow everyone to reach content or functionality in an easy way with a pleasant experience.
