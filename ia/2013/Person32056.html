David Tubman, Jr.'s Summary

David Tubman was appointed as Counsel to Commissioner Doyle at the Federal Maritime Commission in July 2013. David previously founded a law firm in the San Francisco Bay Area and spent 12 years in Washington, D.C., where he was legal & legislative counsel and chief of staff for a maritime labor union.
