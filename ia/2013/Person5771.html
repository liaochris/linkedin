Ami Woods' Summary

A third generation Michigan advertising creative, I have been working independently since 2006 in Northern Michigan. Prior to being independent, I was extremely fortunate to have a 10+ year career in professional sports & entertainment and facility management.

Throughout my lifetime, marketing and customer service have always carried through as a focus, no matter what the position.

Working now with an array of unique businesses, I am grateful for each client that shows me their trust and in return, I promise everyone complete satisfaction.

Specialties: Small business consulting, marketing support, social media, service and team training, and copywriting.
