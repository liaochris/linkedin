Leilah Publications' Summary

http://leilahpublications.com/
leilah@leilahpublications.com

Leilah Publications is a publishing company for the 21st century, not the 20th.

Leilah Publications brings a global vision of art & writing for the 21st century, producing & investing in cutting edge, avante-garde artists, writers, musicians, and visionaries. The company was founded on 13 October 2006 to publish the visions of prodigies, leading us into the 21st century under a new generation of visionary artists, lyricists, writers, actors & actresses, and poets.

Leilah brings an unconventional vision of art and literature to a new generation. The focus of publishing books is to present a story, exhibit information, and form a connection between writer and reader. Leilah transcends traditional publishing in the 21st century, extending creative reach across cultures, blending print media with social media and technology. Leilah extends our creative and visionary arm to social blogs and media, internet radio, theatre troupes & stage performance, self-publishing, and independent art galleries.

We are accepting submissions for:

*Children's books
* Fiction and Science-Fiction
* Theology and Religious Studies
* Lyrical & Spoken Word
* Poetry
* Politics
* Social Commentary
* Short film production
* Stage & Theatre screenplay & production
* History and Culture

Submissions must include:

One page business proposal & abstract
Printed manuscripts must be MS Word format
Art prints & illustrations accepted format: .jpeg, .pdf, psd., .pse., .png

Leilah Publications accepts all formats of digital media. Digital manuscripts and abstracts on CD/ROM, or DVD disk or external drive must be submitted to our physical address or via e-mail at:
