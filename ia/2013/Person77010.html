Kristi Cash White's Summary

"People are generally doing their best with the tools they have available." It is my goal through extensive education and experience to walk alongside people as they find better tools for their life journey. I especially find a great deal of hope and joy in exploring this avenue with children through play therapy.

Specialties

"You always have a choice." Inspired by amazing experts in human nature, such as Alfred Adler, Victor Frankl, and modern play therapists, I have formed "Choice Language", a training tool on effective communication with children.
