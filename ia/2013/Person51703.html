Haris Krijestorac's Summary

I am a B2B marketer and technology product manager with a background in information systems.

I strive for a holistic approach to marketing, aligning technology, processes and people for measurable success. I understand the components of online marketing, and focus on making them work together effectively.

My experience spans from enterprise-level marketing to startups. I have a particular interest in optimizing websites and other technology-oriented marketing tools to achieve measurable business impact.

Specialties:

B2B online marketing - emphasis on leveraging organic (non-paid) channels for lead generation and lead nurturing.

Marketing technology - knowledgeable of the landscape of marketing software and tools, and the value they each add. I am also knowledgeable of other 'technical' marketing fields, such as SEO and website optimization.

Marketing processes - product management, prioritizing and planning new features, project management
