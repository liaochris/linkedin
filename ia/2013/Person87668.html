Martin Coyne's Summary

Martin Coyne is a real estate executive with over 25 years of demonstrated success in directing operations for Industrial, Retail and Office properties in the US, within large portfolios. Martin is recognized for having a unique combination of leadership, analytical, problem resolution and persuasive skills to achieve results in the public and private sectors. His exceptional background, which includes experience in brokerage and asset management, combined with his solid business relationships have contributed to his ability to achieve full occupancy to maximize revenues over multiple economic cycles. Martin’s aggressive marketing style resulted in consistently strong tenant retention and repeat broker business.

More recently, Martin worked for AMB Property Corporation as the SVP, Southern California Regional Manager. Over the past 20 years, he built and grew the Southern California business platform from 300,000 SF to over 20 MSF. As the leader of this $100 million operating business, Martin managed AMB’s largest portfolio in the company. He recruited and led teams in property management, construction management and leasing to successfully manage and promote the assets. He interacted and collaborated extensively at the senior level in the company.

Martin’s accomplishments at AMB include people management team building, leasing vacancy from incubator to 900,000 SF spaces (with concentration between 10,000 – 100,000 SF), directly acquiring and disposing real estate assets, developing ground up as well as repositioning existing properties to enhance value. In his asset management function he played a central role in customer development with principal to principal tenant meetings to attract, renew and/or problem solve and gain the trust of customers for long term repeat business.

Martin left AMB in June 2011 as a result of a merger of AMB into PLD.
