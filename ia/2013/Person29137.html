Dan Brady's Summary

Since 1987, when I established Dan Brady Painting & Wood Restoration, I have been providing superior-quality interior and exterior painting for both residential and commercial projects. I have developed a reputation for dependability, personal service and attention to detail that keeps my customers coming back to my company again and again. I regularly attend regional and national painting conventions and trade shows in order to stay on the cutting edge of not only the latest equipment, but also preparation and application techniques, and the newest trends and products in the industry.

In 2009 I established Dan Brady Painting Tricks of the Trade Painting Videos. I currently have two DVDs out on the market, How to Paint Your Room Like A Pro and How To Remove Wallpaper Like A Pro. These DVDs provide professional tricks of the trade for do-it-yourselfers.

I have also been featured on The Learning Channel's Trading Spaces program where I worked with my friend Carter Oosterhouse. I also appeared on Carter's HGTV show Carter Can.

I am a member of the Home Builders Association of the Grand Traverse Area, the Traverse Area Association of Realtors and the Traverse City Area Chamber of Commerce. I am a past president of Business Networking International and serve as president of Painting and Decoration Contractors of America. I also serve as the board chair of the local Habitat for Humanity affiliate

Specialties

residential & commercial repaint projects, drywall repair, wallpaper removal, wallpaper installation, light carpentry, decorative painting, floor coatings
