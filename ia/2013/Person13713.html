Beth Ann Hilton's Summary

I am a boutique agency owner specializing in music and conscious entertainment, who welcomes the challenges of balancing strategic planning with hands-on project management. Successful at both corporate and entrepreneurial efforts, my strengths include public speaking, career coaching, media representation, and business development.

My career has spanned various PR & Marketing positions in music, DVD, film and fashion. As president of The B Company, I interface with music labels, writers, indie artists, fashion designers and filmmakers, designing customized publicity, marketing programs, promotional partnerships, and business development campaigns. We enjoy the challenge of launching the "new and unknown."

I welcome the opportunity to connect with individuals in various industries and those who offer specialty services, too. We seek to network with socially-conscious companies and individuals. Our intention is to add healthy food, beverage and product marketing to our range of clients in the coming year. All referrals welcome and appreciated...we treat your people like they're our people.

Specialties: Publicity, strategic planning, negotiating, niche marketing, promotional partnerships, branding, distribution, product extensions and bundling, relationship management, website management, Internet marketing & social networking. Clients: Music, film, fashion, books. Markets: holistic, conscious, health, wellness, consumer, B2B. Genres: new age, ambient, world, jazz, classical, gospel, crossover; international, domestic.
