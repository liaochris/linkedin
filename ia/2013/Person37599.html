Dr. Bob Weathers' Summary

I currently work as curriculum developer for California Southern University. In this position, chiefly administrative, I supervise the creation of new curriculum (courses, programs) in our university's masters-level and doctoral programs in marriage and family therapy, and clinical psychology, respectively. In addition, I am actively involved in accreditation, staff development, and quality assurance across all programs in our School of Behavioral Sciences.
