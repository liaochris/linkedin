Martin Neef's Publications

  • Understanding the Marketing Department’s Influence Within the Firm

    • Journal of Marketing
    • March 1, 2009
    Authors: Martin Neef, Peter Verhoef, Peter Leeflang, Auke Molendijk

    Publication of my graduation project on the influence of marketing in organizations.

    Main conclusions are:
    - Influence of marketing is positively related to market orientation, which is positively related to firm performance
    - Accountabiliy and innovativeness of the marketing department are major drivers of the influence of marketing
    - Marketing lost 2 of the 4 P's: Price and Place are determined by other departments.
