Karel Murray, CSP's Summary

How do you fit in time for self-improvement and learning new things when the thought of taking on one more responsibility feels crushing? Giving positive advice and information presented in an engaging, collaborative way is the way I guide you to the solutions you need to make your career decisions.

I open and close conferences nationally and internationally with a memorable energy and impactful insight about personal accountability through my “nothing but the truth” delivery style. Attendees soon discover that they are learning how to be innovative and more open minded when it comes to problem solving, communication and leadership – laughing all the way

I'm honored to have achieved the National Speakers Association; the Certified Speaking Professional (CSP) and Distinguished Real Estate Professional Designation (DREI) from REEA.

Now -- A Quick Background on Who I Am
------------------------------------------------------------
As a past human resource professional and Broker Manager of a real estate company, I started Our Branch, Inc. speaking nationally and internationally. I'm the author of three books: Straight Talk: Getting Off the Curb, Hitting Our Stride: Women, Work and What Matters, and Conquering the Witch Within: Intergenerational Work Place Strategies that Creates Real Results.

Facilitator / Owner

Equinox: Executive Women Mastery Enclave

July 2013 – Present (3 months) National

Let's face it - it's lonely out there for women who have reached executive levels in organizations they work for or are entrepreneurial owners of businesesses they created out of a personal vision. This is the driving the new concept of this online collaboration. The name Equinox is about alignment and Enclaves are a place where you can feel balanced and supported. From this place of comfort and trust, the decisions you make will be valid steps in reaching the goals you have established.

An Enclave consists of five to seven women who work in harmony for the attainment of a definite purpose that is defined by the group. The idea is to utilize the experience and perspective of other women who are influencers in their field of business. By creating powerful connections professionally, you can accomplish more in a concentrated period of time due to the aspect of accountability, truthful feedback and genuine interest in helping you carry out your life plan.

A full function mastery group essentially brings to life the Zen saying: "Leap and the net will appear". Your minstermind counterparts are the net, ready to freely offer their emotional and intellectual support.

To learn more about Equinox: Executive Women Mastery Enclave, send an email to karel@karel.com.
