Karl Lanocha's Summary

After 20 years as the Medical Director for psychiatric services at a large general hospital, I have established a small, private practice devoted to the care of patients with treatment resistant depression, anxiety, and certain other neuropsychiatric disorders.

After FDA approval of the NeuroStar TMS Therapy System in October 2008, I became one of the first providers in the nation to offer this revolutionary treatment. Since then, I have performed over1,500 treatments for more than 60 patients.

Not only is TMS Therapy an innovative treatment in itself, but it also offers me the opportunity to work one-on-one with my patients to provide them with a comprehensive and guided treatment plan to not only get them better, but get them fully well once again.

In May 2010, I attended the 163rd American Psychiatric Association and I had the honor of being a member of an expert panel regarding the clinical application of TMS Therapy.

In February 2011, I was the keynote speaker at the NeuroStar Leaders Conference, which was attended by nearly 300 TMS Therapy providers.

I will once again have the opportunity to present on TMS Therapy when I lead a seminar at the Northeast Regional Nurse Practitioner Conference in May 2011.

Specialties

Neuropsychiatry for the treatment of depression, anxiety, OCD and certain other neuropsychiatry disorders
