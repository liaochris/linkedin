Justin Thiele's Summary

Justin Thiele is co-founder of Glider, where he leads marketing. He earned some early battle scars co-founding Mugasha, an online music service, which ran for three years. Needing a break, he joined AppSumo as an early employee before going to GadgetTrak, a startup building theft recovery software, as the Marketing Director. While there, he built CameraTrace, a spin-off for tracking stolen cameras.

He enjoys biking, exploring the Oregon coast, and seeing live music.

Specialties: Product, Growth Hacking, Marketing, Development, Community, UX, Branding, PR.
