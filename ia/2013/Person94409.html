Mike Monahan's Summary

The best approach to inspiring greatness is to lead by example. Whether I'm facilitating workshops, coaching individuals, presenting to groups or working with businesses, everything is based on my personal experiences that are easy for people to relate to.

Since 1998, I've been lead facilitator for Life Success Seminars Inc., a Cincinnati-based nonprofit that focuses on fostering individual greatness. I facilitate Life Success' monthly Basic Seminar, where people learn the core principles to leading a fulfilling and powerful life. More than 11,000 people from 48 states and 11 countries have participated in the Basic Seminar in Cincinnati.

In addition, I've developed and facilitated many seminars and workshops, including Balancing Relationships, Leadership Training, Passport, The Plan and Blueprint.

As the executive director and CEO of Life Success from 1994 to 2013, I helped the organization achieve tremendous growth. Under my leadership, LSS alumni donated $1.5 million—the total cost—to build the West Chester Conference Center, which is the home of Life Success Seminars and a conference facility available to the community.

In 2011 I founded ThinkMonahan, LLC. My goal as an author is to touch more lives by reaching a larger audience through my books, speaking to groups and coaching individuals.

Specialties: Facilitating experiential seminars and workshops for large and small groups; coaching individuals and small group business development; best selling author and public speaker.
