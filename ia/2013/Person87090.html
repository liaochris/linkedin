Mark Sokolowski's Summary

I am a finance and accounting professional with extensive experience in all aspects of finance, accounting and treasury. Most of my experience has been concentrated in the manufacturing and distribution industries, with both public and private companies as well as large and small companies. I have managed staffs as large as 50 people, and have been in positions where I was an individual contributor.

I have been fortunate enough to work and live in multiple locations as well as overseas while running a large manufacturing facility in the Philippines, this was one of the most challenging yet rewarding experiences in my life.
