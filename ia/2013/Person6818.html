Andrew Casal's Projects

  • Meetup Notes

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Andrew Casal, Jason Pearson

    Whenever there is a speaker at a Meetup, people usually post a comment afterwards asking if the speaker can post the slides or videos that went with the presentation so that the experience can be shared and preserved. Most often the speaker doesn't read this comment, possibly because they don't have a Meetup account. The goal of Meetup Notes is to provide a way for anyone to post content associated with a Meetup event and have that content dynamically embedded into a page. We used the Meetup API and embed.ly service.

    Won Best Meetup API app at Battle of the Braces PHP Hackathon
