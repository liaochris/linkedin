Anders Brownworth's Summary

Currently I’m developing “republic wireless”, a WiFi / cellular hybrid smartphone available in the United States. Previous to that I created “Phonebooth”, a cloud based IP PBX platform for the SMB market. I played a key role in helping Bandwidth.com get started and set it's technological direction.

As a technologist, I get to watch disruption theory play out in real-time. It is at the same time both a motivating and sobering force but the opportunities it presents always seem to win out in the end. The ability to disrupt and make the world a better place is what gets me up in the morning!

Specialties

Identifying potentially disruptive technologies, refining them and building a successful business around it.
