Mary Schnack's Experience

Founder

Up from the Dust

March 2006 – Present (7 years 8 months)

Our tag line is Supporting the Global Growth of Women Microenterprises. We import items from women artisans in developing countries to open US distribution channels and help them develop their businesses. Exhibit at women business owners’ conferences in the US and developing home party network.

President

Mary Schnack & Associates

1992 – Present (21 years)

Mary Schnack & Associates are public relations consultants with more than 30 years of experience in journalism, public relations, crisis communications, media relations/training, writing, and marketing communications. Clients include sophisticated organizations, from small businesses to corporations,and government agencies to non-profits. We engage in a collaborative decision-making process with clients, bringing expertise, talent and judgment to outreach campaigns.

Mary Schnack is recognized as a dazzling speaker and trainer worldwide. Her topics are interesting, thought provoking and offer solid, useful information. Mary offers real world perspective drawn from her first-hand experiences, and not just textbook examples from other people’s work.
