Jai Stone (JaiStone.com) is a Entreprenuer, Blogger & Branding Expert with nearly 20 years of experience. She has worked with some of the most recognized brands in the world, erecting her own branding firm in 2001.

As one of the premier brand expert in the southeast, Jai Stone has created programs for companies like Turner Broadcasting, SunTrust Securities and The Coca Cola Company to name a few. And her personal branding clients ranged from professional athletes to Hollywood actors. After 11 years, Stone took a hiatus from the marketing world to pursue her own purpose and passion.

In 2011 Stone took the blogasphere by storm quickly becoming known for her unique perspective and authentic voice. In 2012 she launched Emotional Nudity (EmotionalNudity.com), a lifestyle brand that focuses on women's empowerment.

As a highly syndicated blogger Stone is a regular contributor to Essence.com and has had content featured by Madame Noire, Huffington Post and The Root.

Jai Stone is recognized with accolades such as Best of Atlanta (Atlanta Tribune Magazine), Trailblazer Award Nominee (Greater Women's Business Council), Creative Style Award Nominee (Atlanta Business League), The Cambridge Who's Who, Top 50 Black Business Women Online, The Rising Star Award (Young & Powerful Group), and Who's Who of Black Atlanta.
