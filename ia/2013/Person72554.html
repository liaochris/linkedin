Kasim Lasi's Summary

I am extremely passionate about my work from the initial idea in my mind till its visual manifestation onscreen, on paper or on production. I always follow brand guidelines & produce exceptional visuals that communicate exactly what the brief required. With over eight years of creative experience working with clients, not only meeting their exact needs but exceeding them with my highly flexible design skills, fresh ideas with a 'can do' attitude and my passion for all things creative.

Specialties: Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash (Timeline animation) and InDesign (CS5/CS6) are applications that I am proficient in and use on a daily basis, as well quick hand drawn sketching (Scamping), storyboarding, typography, conceptualisation & layout design.

Being creative with Briefs, Branding and identity, Graphic/Web/Digital design, Brainstorming concepts, Flash based animation.
