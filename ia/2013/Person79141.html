Laurent Brouat's Summary

Founder and Consultant at Link Humans, I consult and train companies on how to recruit on the internet.

We train companies like SNCF, Michelin, Mazars, Adecco to recruit on Linkedin, Viadeo, Facebook and Twitter.

- I train professional recruiters on how to recruit and source on Social Networks (Linkedin, Viadeo) et on the internet (X Ray, google searches...).

- I help companies to develop a strong presence on social platforms according to their goals and employer branding strategy

- I lecture also at Business School about HR/Recruitment on the internet (social networks, mobile)

- I co-founded the 1st Recruiting conference in France, #rmsconf and the 1st Unconference, #TruParis
