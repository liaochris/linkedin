Jason Dinesen's Summary

I am a Licensed Public Accountant based in Indianola, Iowa (just minutes south of Des Moines). After leaving a career in radio in 2007, I shifted my focus to accounting and started my own business in 2009. I thoroughly enjoy helping people alleviate their fear of - and frustration with – tax preparation and planning.

My professional memberships include the Indianola Chamber of Commerce and the Iowa Society of Enrolled Agents (where I currently serve as President). While I serve clients in and around central Iowa, I also work with individuals and businesses throughout the country because of my “Enrolled Agent” designation. As an Enrolled Agent, I'm authorized by the U.S. Department of Treasury to provide services – including advising, tax preparation, and audit representation – to clients throughout the United States.

As an entrepreneur, I understand the challenges my clients face. I find it gratifying to deliver them the peace of mind of knowing their accounting is in order – and to save them time so they can focus on running the other important aspects of their companies.

On a personal note, I was born and raised in Harlan, Iowa, and have a degree in corporate communications and certificate in accounting from Simpson College in Indianola. I am married to a truly wonderful wife who is a professor of Spanish, and we have two young sons who keep us plenty busy.
