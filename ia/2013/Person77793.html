Kyle Milnes' Summary

I am a charismatic entrepreneurial leader with a passion for technology. I believe in fostering environments where people can leverage their talents, grow and enjoy what they do.

I strive to evolve; recently I changed direction away from climbing the corporate ladder, choosing to focus on personal and open source projects and mobile technologies. I also provide a wide range of technical IT consulting services.

In the last decade I have come to specialise in Internet orientated solutions and operations. This has taken me around the world and provided me with fantastic perspective and experience in my field. I have also generalised in many IT disciplines, spanning the popular platforms and technology landscapes. I take pride in my expert technical foundation and I take time to keep up to date.

My Rapid Application Development and Deployment (RADD) approach is continually evolving, which incorporates, patterns, processes, infrastructures, frameworks, toolsets, testing techniques and other valuable learning I have acquired over the years. With this approach I can enable teams and projects to be extremely fast and successful at delivering and reacting to business challenges and demands. My approaches have been strategic in helping the organisations I have worked within; operate with agility and the ability to offer a competitive advantage to their customers.

I have evolved due in part to the many brilliant colleagues I have worked with. I have been mentored by inspirational people. Much credit goes to my early life and support network. I am so grateful for the people and the path behind me and I'm always excited to see where the path will lead next...
