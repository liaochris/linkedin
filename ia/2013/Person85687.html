Mariah Gillespie's Summary

Mariah Gillespie holds a masters degree in Journalism & Communications from the University of New South Wales (Sydney, Australia) and a bachelors of music from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. She spent 2 years as Social Media Manager for a web design agency in Sydney, where she built a social media department from scratch and grew it to become one of the company's core areas of expertise. She now lives in San Diego, California, working as Social Media Manager for Quantum Learning Networking, continuing to build her social media business and teaching music lessons (piano).

Mariah has spoken at numerous events, including the ATC Social Media Conference, where she led an unconference session on search and social, and the HR Summit 2011 (Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane), where she was a main conference speaker on the topic of understanding, maximising and leveraging social media in recruitment and employer branding. Mariah has also spoken at the Social Media Summit in Sydney as well as the RCSA (Recruitment and Consulting Services Association) international conference in Port Douglas. Throughout 2011, she hosted a series of hands-on workshops on social media across major cities throughout Australia. In 2012, she continued to host social media workshops and also spoke at a social media in government event in Melbourne.

Mariah has helped some of the largest recruitment agencies in Australia utilise social media platforms in the recruitment space (Chandler Macleod, Adecco, Drake) and has also helped brands such as Crown Casino to develop their social media strategy. In addition, she has worked with musicians, artists, fashion designers, tackle shops, retailers and other small businesses in the social media space. She is a facebook developer and can create customised facebook applications for facebook pages.
