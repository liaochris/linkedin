Hundley Matthew's Summary

Out of college (1993) I jumped into TV life at Guy Gannett-owned KGAN (CBS) in Cedar Rapids where I was guided into the marketing fold under the wing of then VP Bill Anderson.

1996, an invitation was issued to join the marketing crew at (then Hearst-Argyle owned) WDTN (ABC) in Dayton, as promotions producer/web guru. Had a great manager, Kim Peters, and a great team to work with at this station where Phil Donahue got his start. Highlights include interview w/Phil Collins, bear hug from Richard Simmons, hosting Barbara Bush, and partnering w/the National Folk Fest.

In 1998, an opportunity was brewing which would allow us to move closer to family and join the upstart ad agency ME&V (Mathis, Earnest &Vandeventer). While still in Dayton I had written Mark about how important it was that agencies latch onto the Web. So at ME&V I put theory into practice as we developed websites for companies in the "Silicorn Valley" which included Waterloo Industries, Burke Meats, Ag Services and The Iowa Donor Network.

KWWL-TV (NBC) was the next major stop on the career path. Then owned by Raycom Media I signed on as Marketing Manager to work with a great team led by Dale Woods (now at WHO-TV in Des Moines).

When KWWL hit the auction block, I decided to dive full time into my MA full time. So the transition from television to theology was made; and the family and I moved to Saint Louis to pursue full time studies at Covenant Theological Seminary. Currently. I graduated in 2008 and, after several temporary posts found my calling as a Marketing Campaign Specialist for Joyce Meyer Ministries.

Along with the great day jobs above, I have had many opportunities along the way to speak to civic groups on web and marketing topics; I have also enjoyed opportunities to teach as an adjunct and speak as a guest lector at Wartburg College (Waverly, IA) and Lindenwood University (Saint Charles, MO).

Specialties

Market Research, Audience Research, SEO/SEM, Web Analytics, Social Media Strategy, Web Site Development, TV Script Writing, Voice Coaching, Entertainment Design, Radio Spot Production.
