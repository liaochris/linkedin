Michael P. Noens' Experience

Festival Director

Blue Whiskey Independent Film Festival

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry

February 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months) Palatine, Illinois

The Blue Whiskey Independent Film Festival (BWiFF) is owned and operated by CNGM Pictures, a not-for-profit production company based in the greater Chicagoland area. The festival seeks out bold and innovative storytellers; films that utilize the basics in a new and exciting way, presenting independent cinema that is fueled by the filmmaker's passion for the art without large studio support.

CNGM Pictures

Lead a team of 4-6 staff members in the growth and development of the production company, promoting character-driven independent motion pictures through multiple mediums.
