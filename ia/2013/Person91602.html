Meshach Weber's Summary

Media innovator, technology consultant and creative marketer. Founder of Firebrand, a leader in the field of web development, web marketing, and creator of the first true Edit In Place, WYSIWYG Content Management System (CMS.)

Meshach specializes in innovative technology solutions, integrated marketing, social media, conversion-oriented designs, PPC, SEO and SEM. Advanced video solutions for digital signage and web-based video delivery also a core competency.

Design, Web Development, System Design, Usability, User Experience Design (UX), User Interface Design (UI) and Content Strategy. --Generally bridging the gaps between design, marketing, technology and business needs with a proven record of strong ROI.

It's about making things work, prosper, look good and make sense.
