Mark Porteous' Summary

I spent 20 years in sales working to create prosperity so I could pursue my passions. Then I realized I had it all backwards. Now I help others create prosperity through the pursuit of their passion. I do this primarily through group training.

Max Your Life Personal Empowerment Training is designed to help you follow your passion and create the life of your dreams.

The Transformers Mastermind Program is specifically designed to help emerging visionary leaders:
1) Clearly articulate your message,
2) Easily attract your ideal audience and
3) Monetize your unique gifts.

Our number one intention is to support heart-centered visionaries to quickly build a thriving business so they can create greater positive change in the world.

I have learned that being a successful, conscious entrepreneur means having a balance of heart and mind. Many “conscious entrepreneurs” are socially conscious, but struggle to be conscious in managing their business or simply do not have the resources and information to attract clients and optimize their income.

The Transformers Mastermind Program offers effective business tools and a complete blueprint to achieve your heart-centered mission.

I host the Personal Transformation Summit (www.PersonalTransformationSummit.com) teleconference series online with 40 amazing presenters offering FREE workshops on personal development. I am also a member of Conscious Evolution Coaching through The Shift Network which is coaches coaching coaches, so that we can help change the world by changing ourselves.
