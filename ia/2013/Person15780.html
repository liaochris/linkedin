Andrew Borene's Summary

Executive and licensed attorney with more than 12 years of professional experience in business, law and government. A former U.S. Marine officer, Andrew also has special expertise in the national security enterprise, information workflow, government relations, capital markets and building world-leading strategic relationships between public, private and academic partners. Named as one of Minnesota’s 50 leading business figures in Minnesota Business magazine’s (Real) Power 2013, at a company named #44 on Fast Company Magazine’s “The World’s 50 Most Innovative Companies of 2013”.

An articulate advocate and leader who can stay on message and effectively improvise. Andrew has had professional media training and is experienced with television, radio, print and public speaking. He has appeared live on CNN, ABC News, Fox News, CBS, National Public Radio and BBC. A published author and editor, Andrew has written Op-Ed pieces in The New York Times, The Hill, The Washington Times, Foreign Policy and other publications. He is also firmly established as an editor on legal topics related to human rights, humanitarian law and U.S. national security.

Specialties: leadership, general management, business development, law and policy advocacy
