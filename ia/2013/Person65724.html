Joe Scangamor's Summary

Key Accomplishments:

CFO at Mindshare both during its formation in the USA 12 years ago and currently as we navigate today's rapidly changing digital media landscape. Starting back in 2000 as CFO, oversaw the rapid growth of MindShare in North America as it became one of the most successful media planning and buying companies at that time; consistently grew revenue and profit margin.

Financial and operational leader in the consolidation of WPP’s media companies as GroupM CFO, Americas overseeing media billings in excess of $30 billion.

As Global COO and CFO at WPP's Enfatico, was a key member of team tasked by WPP to design and build the “Agency of the Future” based on current and emerging digital technologies, utilizing the latest analytical and research tools, and positioned to capture efficiencies by outsourcing to low cost international markets.

Facilitated the sale of a privately held company to Aegis Group plc and as EVP and CFO launched Carat USA which became the largest independent media firm in the country.

Expertise:

I have successfully combined three areas of expertise: accounting, media/advertising and operations.

(1) Accounting - my core accounting experience covers financial reporting, general ledger, payroll, fixed assets, audit and compliance, revenue recognition, internal controls, financial planning and analysis, and mergers & acquisitions.

(2) Media / Advertising - my understanding of media and advertising goes well beyond “the numbers” and includes a keen understanding of digital, search, content development, social media and key growth areas.

(3) Operations - throughout my career I have always managed or worked closely with all aspects of operations (including human resources and talent development, information technology, and facilities management).
