Renee King's Summary

I'm a lifelong dreamer who lives in Atlanta, Georgia USA and runs the travel blog, A View To A Thrill. Always wanting to explore the world, I threw caution to the wind and followed my heart in 2002 and never looked back.

In 2008, I made a commitment to share what I knew about traveling on a budget with the inauguration of my blog, A View To A Thrill. I knew that there were others who had the same desire to experience other cultures, but little knowledge about how to bring it into fruition.

When not traveling, I work full time as an administrative assistant and spend a fair amount of time watching TV (roku rocks), reading thrillers on my beloved kindle and tending to my beautiful English cottage garden. Currently, I'm seeking press trip opportunities and can be reached at aviewtoathrill@gmail.com.

No one is better than you…they may have more resources, but that doesn’t automatically disqualify you from seeing the world.
