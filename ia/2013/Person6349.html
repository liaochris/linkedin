Andrea Ahl's Summary

Andrea Ahl is a Louisville, KY based Makeup Artist; specializing in film, photography and events. As a licensed cosmetologist; she does hair, makeup and wardrobe styling for individuals, photographers, film & video productions and special events such as weddings, parties and derby events. When hiring her for your project she not only brings talent but education as well, she has spent many years studying color and art and found a unique way to bring its presence into what she does. It is important for her to be thorough in her job and provide the best services for her clients. Not to mention her clients love her professionalism and knowledge of her craft. She’s easy to get along with and uses your lifestyle and desires to create the right look for you.
