Bijo Alex Thomas' Summary

Bijo Alex Thomas is an SOA Specialist in Global Business Solutions Center (GBSC) within IBM. The practice is responsible for building and managing reusable software assets for varied industry sectors as well as cross industry sectors, which can be used as part of larger solutions.

He has 8 years of experience in India as well as in United States in development of distributed applications using SOA, Java, J2EE, UML as Team Lead / Sr. Developer / SOA Integration Developer that span across Government, Life Science and Pharmaceutical Industry and Cross Domain Technical Assets. He has architectured complete solutions using SOA best practices. He has experience leading cross-cultural, international, all client staffed development team by working closely with the business, analyzing and refining the requirements while offering innovative suggestions which adds value to client’s business and process.
