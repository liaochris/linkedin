Gabrielle Fontaine, PB's Summary

Gabrielle assists Internet savvy entrepreneurs and self-employed professionals to get their books under control, save taxes, and maximize cash flow. She is is an Advanced Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor and certified FreshBooks Bean Counter. Gabrielle specializes bookkeeping software training and support via the Internet.

Gabrielle is also the author of several bookkeeping business training books available on Amazon.com, two popular bookkeeping blogs – QB QuickTips and The Freelance Bookkeeper, and various online training programs for freelance bookkeepers.

Specialties: QuickBooks / FreshBooks training, consulting, and support; freelance bookkeeping business training & coaching.
