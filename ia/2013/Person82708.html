ChangeCorp is a mobile learning company providing solutions in both emerging markets and developing countries. We work on training people on skills they need for everyday life, such as life skills, business skills, financial literacy, business/entrepreneurship, vocational training, health, language literacy and digital competency. Learning messages are delivered via SMS (text), via mobile data or via a smartphone app. For more solutions, go to: change-corp.com

Louise M. Guido | ChangeCorp's Projects

  • eLife Tablet for Teachers

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Louise M. Guido | ChangeCorp, EnableM, Fundacion Pies Descalzos, SAP

    Foundation for Social Change launched a ground-breaking program - using mobile learning to scale its successful eLife lessons so that teachers could use these lessons in their day classes.

    In three schools – Barranquilla, Cazuca (Bogota) and in Quibdo, the FSC team trained over 40 teachers on life skills, communication skills, financial literacy, vocational/livelihoods training and business/entrepreneurship.

    The teachers use the eLife Tablet App to access the eLife lesson plans such as Life Skills, Communication Skills, Financial Skills, Business, Vocational Training and Entrepreneurship, as well as outside resources such as web sites and local information. This program greatly enhances the learning experience inside the classroom – using mobile technology!
