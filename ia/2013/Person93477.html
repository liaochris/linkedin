Michelle Hollingshead's Summary

Michelle Hollingshead is a Certified Coach and Leadership Development Consultant, whose mission is helping individuals and organizations leave a meaningful imprint on our world. She is committed to human beings realizing their potential with a focus on conscious leadership, authentic communication, and culture transformation. An energetic facilitator and course designer, Michelle is a champion for personal and organizational change. She brings her experience as a multicultural educator, corporate trainer, and entrepreneur to her coaching and consulting work.

Michelle’s passion and professionalism for guiding individuals and companies through transition is seen in every partnership she takes on. She has successfully developed and delivered several corporate training and coaching programs and consults on a variety of change management initiatives. As a coach, Michelle works with individuals, teams, and organizations to realize their goals while keeping their values aligned with their vision.

Michelle earned a Master’s of Education in Curriculum Development from Lesley University, graduated from Coach University’s Advanced Coach Training program, and received her coaching credential through the International Coaching Federation. Michelle currently serves as the President of the Ohio Valley Professional Coaches Alliance. She is an award winning teacher, yoga enthusiast, and proud wife and mother of two.
