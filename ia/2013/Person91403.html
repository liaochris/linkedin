Melvin Simpson's Summary

I'm an Senior Consultant at Biglight, The Specialist eCommerce Agency, working with a range of major brands on eCommerce strategy, optimisation, technology and online marketing.

Prior to this I was Head of Internet at Ministry of Sound, driving strategy and implementation across all online channels. I was responsible for eCommerce performance as well as managing the development and digital design team.

Previously I was Head of eCommerce Operations at zavvi, tasked with developing and executing eCommerce strategies to make zavvi.co.uk one of the leading online stores in UK entertainment.

Before that I was a consultant/project manager on various new media projects, including Virgin Digital; Arsenal FC; FA Premier League; 2 entertain and reelintime, a startup video archive and social networking site.

I was involved in two startup internet/technology companies, and before that I spent five years in communication agencies, founding my own in 1997. My early career was in entertainment publishing; music and video.

Specialties

Experienced senior executive with focus on eCommerce, internet and entertainment. Accomplished team and project leader, proficient in marketing and operations, with strong interpersonal and communication skills. Has achieved successes in client and agency environments, demonstrating strategic and tactical abilities.
