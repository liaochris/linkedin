James Barton's Summary

Working in the exciting and vibrant IT sector for the last 18 years my focus has always been on developing exceptional relationships with partners, suppliers and customers to ensure that value is given to all the stakeholders.

My most recent focus has been in international sales productivity and enablement from initial consultancy through to the design and delivery of technical and sales training, coaching and mentoring services largely focused in the IT sector.

With an IT and sales background, I am skilled in delivering a range of creative, high energy sales and technical training and coaching as well as developing leading edge programmes based on the very latest research and sales best practice for delivery across the globe.

Recent projects have included
- end to end design and training delivery of SalesForce.com adoption programmes across EMEA and Asia Pacific
- design and delivery of a Sales Excellence Bootcamp and Academy
- development of a benchmark Sales Leadership programme
- creation, editing and production of a range of Sales Best Practice videos
- creation and delivery of a 'how to sell to the IT community' programme
- creation and delivery of ISMM accredited Partner Sales Programme
- design and development of specialist sales 'Playbooks'
- design and development of the GORWTH sales methodology model

I hold formal qualifications in Personal and Corporate Coaching, PRISM Neuroscience, DISC performance profiling and NLP, along with a collection of technical certifications including ITIL v3, VMware, Cisco and Microsoft.
