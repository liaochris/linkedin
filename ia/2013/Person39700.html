Edree Allen-Agbro's Summary

When my business coach encouraged me to analyze the greatest results I have helped my clients achieve over the last 30 years, I discovered that I had become the “Go To” person for helping them resolve conflict with less pain and more understanding.

It was a bit of a surprise because leaders hire me to coach them, to provide training in inclusion and diversity and to teach better communication skills to everyone. Yet the data was there. At least 80 % of the success my clients have had all these years has been in resolving conflict and turning it into long-lasting and effective cooperation, easily and quickly.

My passion has always been to help people understand the true nature and potential of conflict, to stop avoiding it or making it worse and to tap into its constructive creative power to GET RESULTS

I typically work with clients who are...

MANAGERS AND LEADERS of small organizations or departments who are tired of spending 50% or more of your time dealing with unresolved conflict. We can help with my TRANSFORMATIONAL CONFLICT RESOLUTION for in-house or virtual training, in-house conflict dialogue interventions and coaching to help you lead a culture where differences are handled constructively for better ideas, solutions and results.

EVENT PLANNERS & ASSOCIATIONS looking for an engaging and inspiring keynote speaker (conflict resolution and inclusion topics) for your next meeting or conference.

WOMEN LEADERS IN TECHNOLOGY AND EDUCATION who are intrigued and motivated to solve the particular challenges of women and conflict in these two fields.

COACHES AND CONSULTANTS who want to get more comfortable, confident and effective in their ability to help their clients handle conflict effectively.
