Dan Debreuil's Summary

Dan Debreuil is a successful business owner with marketing expertise. Dan is committed to providing exceptional services in strategic marketing plan development and implementation to equip his clients to flourish in a highly competitive market place

By following an easy 3-step process. Dan can help you achieve business clarity and understanding through:
1. The Power of Knowledge;
2. Establishing profit-based objectives;
3. Planning for success.

Dan holds Bachelor and Master of Science degrees in Agriculture from the University of Manitoba. Prior to d-Commerce Business Consulting, Dan worked 11 years for two of the world's leading agricultural firms. This provided a deep understanding of international and regional markets and how important strategic planning can be for exposing your business to untapped opportunity.

Specialties

Strategic planning for businesses' connected to the food industry.
Business development for small family run enterprise.
