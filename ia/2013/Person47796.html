Geoffrey Palmer's Summary

I'm a serial entrepreneur with twenty years experience in operations management, marketing, technology product design, and development in emerging companies, mid-sized businesses and large corporate environments.

My passion is to utilize emerging technologies to solve business problems.

I have built and sold several companies, including Information Marketing Businesses to Simplex and Nurse.Com to Tenet Healthcare. Co-founder of uLocate, a start-up that became WHERE, Inc., which was acquired in 2011 by PayPal.

I am currently the co-founder of On The Spot Systems, a startup that provides mobile solutions for companies that believe better data leads to better business performance. On The Spot's mobile data collection technology helps businesses gain advantage in the marketplace by providing them with location-specific data that makes a difference in real-time.

Specialties: Taking an idea from conception through the development of the product or service, development and execution of the business plan, growing the company, culminating in a sale or acquisition.
Specialties include technology product design & development (software and hardware), operations management and marketing.

About On The Spot Systems, Inc.

On The Spot® Systems leverages mobile technology to provide location-specific data that enables businesses to make better decisions that improve the performance and quality of products and services. Our service is a real-time business intelligence platform that organizations use to gather customer insights, deliver service feedback, or easily complete field data collection needs wherever and whenever it is required.

Our Survey On The Spot service enables service focused organizations to capture in-the-moment customer and employee feedback cost effectively. Utilizing iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, and smartphone technology, our surveys provide on-site insights including photos and text commentary. The system also features service alerts, digital coupons and rewards, and automated data reporting via the web.

http://www.onthespotsystems.com


On The Spot Systems is proud to be a member of TwelveByTwelve (12x12), a fantastic organization in Boston. 12x12 builds on the premise that CEOs of successful companies and experienced venture capitalists generate and are constantly exposed to great new business ideas and entrepreneurs. http://www.twelvebytwelve.org/
