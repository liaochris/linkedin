Help Yourself Associates Ltd.'s Summary

I am an Motivational Speaker & Trainer. I train & speak to corporates & non-corporates alike. I am also the founder and Director at Help Yourself Associates Ltd. A training company that provides flexible skills across key business areas

We have developed concise, effective corporate training and business coaching that gets to the point and delivers the essential knowledge and skills to your key team members, quickly and memorably. This means the results are retained, and more importantly, are quickly disseminated throughout your whole organization for rapid results.

All our training is performed by highly experienced and qualified trainers, with many years of senior experience on the client side so you can be assured of high quality and we guarantee satisfaction. You can review the same by reading our many client testimonials received from both companies in the corporate and SME sectors. These are available on our website (www.helpyourselfassociates.com or at www.jillianhaslam.com). A corporate brochure & complete course directory is also available on the site.

Whether you are based anywhere in the UK, or internationally, we can deliver. Training is usually provided on-site within your business premises, or upon request we can advise on sourcing suitable venues. Corporate training and workshops can cater to small groups to several hundred delegates. All training materials are provided and all attendees will be provided with a student manual and course certification.

Specialties: Leadership Training, Leadership dealing with complacency issues, Time Management, Talent Management, Effective Feedback, Conducting performance reviews, Corporate Interviewing skills, Corporate etiquette & grooming, Presentation & speaking skills, Conflict Management, Delegation skills, Personal development, Change management, Team Building, Campus to Corporate (preparing young graduates for corporate life), Effective Communication, Self Development, NLP (all four modules).
