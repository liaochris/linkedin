Mary Flaherty's Summary

Mary directs content strategy and content development at RainToday.com, an online magazine for B2B marketing and sales. She writes, edits, and produces content for blogs, case studies, how-to guides, podcast interviews, online training programs, and research.

Her career spans more than 20 years in higher education publishing and research, as well as professional services marketing.

Prior to joining RainToday.com, she directed publishing and global distribution as associate director of Harvard’s Kennedy School of Government Case Program, the world’s largest publisher of case studies on public policy and management. She was a research associate at Harvard Business School and assistant director for new program development at Harvard University, where she coordinated the Conference on the Internet and Society.

She has extensive experience in marketing management in both the for-profit and nonprofit sectors—she served as director of marketing for organizations including Bentley College’s School of Continuing Professional Studies, a regional law firm, and chamber of commerce. Mary has taught undergraduate marketing courses as an adjunct instructor and holds an MBA from Bentley College.

Specialties

B2B content including blogs, case studies, enewsletters, research reports, how-to guides, ebooks, podcasts, online training. Writer, researcher, strategist.
