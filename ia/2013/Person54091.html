Andrew Hurov, LEED AP's Experience

President/CEO

Hurov's Pocket Pro Apps

March 2011 – Present (2 years 7 months)

Hurov’s Pocket Pro Apps was founded by Andrew Hurov, a Construction Project Manager, specifically for the construction industry. As a Project Manager, he knows first hand how much time, paper, and emails are used to communicate simple everyday tasks. So our goal is to utilize today’s technology to allow the field to be more effective and productive while on the go. No more searching around for a computer, fax machine etc., we provide the necessary tools right in the palm of your hand! All you need is a cell phone or tablet and you’re good to go!

Our apps are designed around everyday typical time consuming forms and procedures required in the construction field. They allow users to process items on the go and not get bogged down behind a desk. We understand some field personnel are a little intimidated when it comes to operating electronic devices and programs, which was taken into account during design, so rest assured -our apps are VERY easy to use.

Most importantly our apps are super affordable. They will save you time –which will in turn save you money, save you trips to the office, allow you to focus on field issues and will help your bottom line. Again, we’re from the construction industry and we want to provide fast, easy and effective tools that will allow the industry to focus on building while saving time and money.
