Michelle Auerbach's Summary

Michelle Auerbach is a consultant specializing in the places where communication, storytelling, leadership, and organizational growth overlap. Her services range from messaging and positioning consulting to ghost writing, to training and coaching on leadership, communication, emotional intelligence, presentation skills, and getting the story straight. Her clients include Johnson & Johnson, Google, the Leisure Trends Group, the US Navy, McDonalds, Redbrick Health, AL Productions, Field, Fisher, Waterhouse. She frequently works with other consultants to assist their clients in storytelling.

Michelle Auerbach is also the author of The Third Kind of Horse (2013 Beatdom Books). Her writing has appeared in (among other places) The New York Times, The London Guardian, The Denver Quarterly,Chelsea Magazine, Bombay Gin, and the literary anthologies The Veil (UC Berkley Press), Uncontained (Baksun Books), and You. An Anthology of Essays in the Second Person (Welcome Table Press). She is the winner of the 2011 Northern Colorado Fiction Prize.
