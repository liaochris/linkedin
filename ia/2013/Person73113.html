Katie O'Rourke's Summary

Katie O’Rourke is a 20 year veteran of the Long Term Care (LTC) insurance industry and Managing Partner of California Long Term Care insurance Services. They are the largest independent LTC brokerage in California. Katie has been a speaker at local and national insurance forums for Long Term Care planning. She works with consumers, businesses and associations and trains insurance professional on the need for Long Term Care planning

Katie O'Rourke is from the New York area, but has lived in California since 1987. Katie is a graduate of Indiana University, School of Business. She has worked in the insurance industry since then as both a manager and a salesperson. Katie's commitment to the long term care industry was motivated by the hardship her family faced when her grandmother needed care in an assisted living facility.

Specialties

She has specialized in long term care planning since 1991, but also has an extensive background in training and office management. Katie and her husband, Michael, live in San Francisco with their daughter.
