Stephanie Mensing's Summary

I focus my practice in the areas of employment law, business law, and commercial litigation. I have experience representing individuals and businesses at all stages of litigation before state and federal courts and administrative agencies in Pennsylvania and New Jersey.

In addition to helping clients with their litigation needs, I enjoy working with start up businesses and assisting both individuals and businesses with their contract needs, including reviewing, drafting, and negotiating contracts.

Specialties
