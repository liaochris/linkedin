Kevin Geraldez's Summary

Over 15 years I have provided my services to a wide range of clients and projects. I still continue to provide my talents to companies looking for that edgy style, "cool factor" or corporate look. Also helping with suggestions and collaborate with marketing idea's. Understanding the lifestyle, "trends" of yesterday and today are one of the best tools to have.

Specialties

* Graphic Design - Branding, print media, toys, apparel, vehicle wraps, stickers etc.
* Product Design - Die cast, toy vehicles.
* Illustration - Automotive renderings, cartoons, product illustration, Store decor etc.
* Apparel Design and Separations - Simple to complex screen print, burn screens, print.

Kevin Geraldez's Projects

  • Battle Machines

    • 2007 to 2009
    Team Members: Kevin Geraldez

    Creator of the look and detail of this project, biggest inspiration was the movie back in 1979 MAD MAX. After designing the style of vehicles to use we worked with movie car designer Harold Becker who was at the time designing the cars for DEATH RACE. Battle Machines was the last project I worked at Jada Toys.. I was pleased to hear it did well and is still out there today.

    Team Members: Kevin Geraldez
