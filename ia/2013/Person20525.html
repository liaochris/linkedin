Carolyn Glasser's Summary

With over ten years experience as an interactive designer, developer and art director, Carolyn Glasser conceives and executes thoughtful and engaging interactive experiences while supporting brand + strategy and offline efforts. With a programming background, she's very hands-on and enjoys collaborating with and overseeing creative, technical teams and vendors. Experienced working on large brands like Intel, Samsung, Princess Cruises, Lexus and Aston Martin, Carolyn has also been fortunate to have worked with art institutions and non-profits, designers and architects. Carolyn is a designer grounded in clean typography + color palettes and traditional print work. When she has the chance, she enjoys woodblock and screen printing.

Specialties

flash, html, front-end web development, asset creation, information architecture
