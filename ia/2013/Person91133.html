Melanie Smith's Summary

Accredited member of the International Society of Appraisers with a specialty in fine and animation art.

Co-owner of Seaside Art Gallery in Nags Head, NC. We exhibit over 3000 original works of art, animation art and antiques.

Note: Seaside Art Gallery was founded in 1961 by Chester & Barbara Smith. Chester and Barbara Smith are my parents and with them, I have been attending art auctions, such as Parke Bernet (Sotheby’s) and Phillips in New York since I was 10 years old. I was and have been an active participant in the daily operations of Seaside Art Gallery.
Organized and hosted numerous art shows.
Acted as judge for art shows in eastern North Carolina
Performed as auctioneer in charity art auction event.

Specialties

fine and animation art appraising, art consulting, organizing and promoting art shows, judging art shows.
