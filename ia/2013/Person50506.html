Gretchen Pisano's Summary

Gretchen is an executive coach and strategist, the co-founder of the P.link Coaching Center for Excellence (2012) and Sounding Board Ink, LLC (1995). Working from the nexus point of strong business acumen, coaching expertise, graphic facilitation and ground-breaking theories emerging from the field of positive psychology, Gretchen Gretchen helps people (individuals, teams and companies) embrace change and improve their ROI in life and business.

Gretchen’s ability to think strategically, approach situations with intellectual rigor and intense curiosity, design and create thinking models and visual tools, and facilitate group interactions gives her clients the best of both worlds: insight and action wrapped into one.

Specialties: positive psychology (MAPP, UPenn 2010), executive coach and strategist.
