Melissa Danielle's Summary

Since 2005, I've enjoyed a career as a health coach covering plant-based nutrition, traditional foodways, local food systems, and self-reliance intersecting them with self-determination, personal development, collaboration, and cooperation.

Highlights include acceptance into LEAD NY, launching Bed-Stuy Bounty on the Wholeshare platform, and winning NYS Ag Society's Cap-Creal Journalism award.

Skills & Expertise: farmers market management, CSA management and outreach, community organizing, customer service, social media, public speaking, emergency preparedness, community food education, panel moderator, Microsoft Office, MAC-friendly, big picture thinking.

Personal: Kundalini Yoga, traditional photographic processes, ceramics (handbuilding), home cooking, outdoor recreation.

  • Bed-Stuy Bounty

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Melissa Danielle

    Launched in 2012, Bed-Stuy Bounty offers cooperative-style ordering of fresh, local, organic, real food directly from regional farmers and distributors to Bed-Stuy residents. We have partnered with several neighborhood businesses who host our weekly pickups, a mutually beneficial relationship that recognizes economic development and empowerment through collaboration and cooperation.

    Bed-Stuy Bounty runs on the Wholeshare.com platform, an online ordering system that makes it easy for groups to place bulk wholesale orders directly from farmers and distributors. We are a privately owned buying club that reflects and supports cooperative values. This model has proven to be the most accessible to our members, as membership supports overhead costs, reduces the need for workshifts, and keeps food costs low.

  • Green Smoothie Challenge
