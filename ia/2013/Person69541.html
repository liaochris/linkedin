Joy Rasmussen's Summary

Listed as one of the top 5% producing Realtors in Steamboat Springs, Colorado. For over 10 years, Joy has specialized in luxury real estate for the full time resident or saavy investor. She is a past Listing Broker for the following developments in Steamboat Springs, Colorado: The Porches (64 unit luxury townhomes), Howelsen Place Downtown (37 condominiums with commercial space) and Alpen Glow Downtown (14 condominums with commercial space) of Steamboat. Instrumental in developing marketing plans and launches for multi-million dollar developments, she has throughout her career recruited and trained staff to assist in the sales effort of residential townhome and condominium developments.

If you are seeking Real Estate investments that cash flow, Joy is a full-time investor in the Self Storage and Mobile Home Park Industry. She also has several investment properties in Steamboat where the 2nd home market and tourism industry fuels her cash flow portfolio.

Specialties: Cash Flow Investments, commercial, multi-family, mixed use developments, branding and identity marketing strategies, single family homes, ranches, cabins, negotiation for Buyers/Sellers, sports destination marketing, ski destination real estate, vacation real estate, Steamboat Springs, Colorado, Self Storage, Mobile Home Parks.
