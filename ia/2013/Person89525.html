Matt Perelstein's Summary

We're finding that the ability to balance our left-brain intellectual intelligence (IQ) and our right-brain emotional intelligence (EQ) is one the keys to success in life. We learn IQ in school and at home, but our feelings are squashed, rejected and ignored, so our EQ ain't so good. Without emotional training, our childhood pain stays locked in our body and effects us, often unconsciously. Unresolved pain and anger leads to unhappy campers -- anxiety, abuse, depression, addictions, suicides, crime, mental health, violence, even war -- are all emotion-based issues. So, I'm taking it on as my mission to show the world what's possible with emotional awareness and training.

Love is a teachable skill.
Empathy is a teachable skill.
Happiness is a teachable skill.
Success is a teachable skill.

Help me reach out and teach EQ to the masses... for all of us.
