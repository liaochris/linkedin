Jordan benShea's Summary

Jordan benShea has almost twenty years of experience in marketing, writing, and strategic thinking. Jordan is the Owner of boutique marketing firm SkyView Projects, offering traditional, digital, and social marketing where "we believe your strength is your story." Jordan is also the Founder of the blog Sustainable Socially, highlighting how to use social platforms as a new paradigm for creating a sustainable planet. She regularly is engaged in discussions on social media for social good, how marketing strategies are driven by the brand story, and innovative ways to humanize high value audience interactions.

Jordan is a proud board member and Secretary of the Community Environmental Council where she recently completed a Board Leadership Institute seven month course. Jordan is also on the Casa Esparanza Marketing Advising Committee, the Fairview Gardens Development Committee, and plays a strategic role in the annual Empty Bowls fundraiser for the Foodbank of Santa Barbara County.

Specialties: Strategic Marketing, Digital Marketing, Innovative Marketing Initiatives, Brand Development, Brand Strategy, Brand Management, Social Media Strategy, Social Media Management, Social Media Integration, Non-Profit Marketing, Non-Profit Strategy, Cross-Platform Marketing Initiatives, fresh ideas to create direct results
