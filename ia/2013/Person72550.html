Katarzyna (Kasia) Hein-Peters' Summary

I have more than I am willing to admit years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry - mostly in marketing, but also in sales. I also worked as a coach and a consultant. I launched a few medicines and vaccines, in developed and emerging countries. I understand the dynamic of public and private markets. Currently I am preparing launch of the first dengue vaccine.

I am seeking the best innovation path to increase vaccination. I am interested in cross-cultural leadership and fascinated by the power of coaching.

Katarzyna (Kasia) Hein-Peters' Projects

  • Break Dengue

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Katarzyna (Kasia) Hein-Peters, Céline Schillinger, Nicholas Brooke, Martina O' Regan, Helen Dunnett, Kaori S.

    We want to build a global alliance that will connect all initiatives and emulate new ones to reduce and eventually stop dengue in its tracks. Through our combined efforts, we will contribute to ending the suffering that dengue causes around the world.

    We will:

    - Connect: Break Dengue will connect people and places, linking the different pieces that make up the dengue puzzle, giving us all a clearer picture of the disease.

    - Share: give those on the ground a voice, letting them share best practices and educate other stakeholders in other parts of the globe.

    - Measure: keep track of progress.

    - Act: turning good ideas into projects that help us stay one step ahead of dengue. Either by implementing or incubating them

  • HBA Lyon Event Sept 19: Investing in Africa and Digital & Healthcare

    • September 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Katarzyna (Kasia) Hein-Peters, Andrea Murison, Adriana Cristina Toma, Vanessa S. Lopez, Christine Maï

    Join us as to see how HBA (Healthcare Businesswomen’s Association) could help you to develop on a personal and professional level! This second HBA event in Lyon is a great opportunity to meet with experts to get a broader view on recent industry trends and discover new markets beyond your usual business scope: Together let’s explore the potential of Africa & discover healthcare initiatives in digital. The event is kindly hosted by ALCIMED Lyon, a strategic marketing and consulting firm.

    Africa, the next priority on your corporate agenda

    Learning Objectives:
    1. Discuss how African countries are among the fastest growing economies as shown by GDP growth, increase of health expenditures, life expectancy improvement, and IMF forecasts.
    2. Explain how Africa is clearly a potential healthcare business opportunity
    3. Identify important challenges when addressing this market such as the lack of infrastructure, or poor reliable market data.
    4. Find alternative approaches / business models to grasp the African healthcare market

    Digital in healthcare

    Learning Objectives:
    1: Define the scope, benefits and hurdles of the use of digital in the healthcare industry
    2: Present successful digital initiatives in healthcare and in other sectors
    3: Introduce key takeaways to build-up a digital strategy
