James Bowling's Summary

I am a Cloud Infrastructure Architect/Engineer, VCAP5-DCA, VCP5-DCV/IaaS, VCP-Cloud, VTSP, VMware vExpert, Houston VMUG Leader and virtualization enthusiast located in Houston, TX with over 13 years experience. I received the 2009 COMMON/IBM Power Systems Innovation Award for Energy Efficiency for my design and implementation of the United States Bowling Congress (USBC) Datacenter in Arlington, TX. My experience ranges from designing, deploying, and maintaining virtualized infrastructures while utilizing different types of technology to automate and scale resources.
