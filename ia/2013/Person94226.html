Mike Henry Sr.'s Summary

My passion: helping leaders grow leaders. Leaders exercise their influence and skills at any position or level in any organization. Every organization needs leaders to own their performance and make the organization competitive. Sound leadership fundamentals help you deliver value in every circumstance.

I help leaders grow as leaders and grow other leaders within their business or non-profit organization. Leadership agility applies across industries, however, I have experience in multiple industries and functional areas to help your managers learn to take responsibility, make informed choices and self-develop and grow as leaders.

Areas of specialty:
Leadership Development
Leadership Coaching

Leadership Development
I developed the "Leader From The Pack" Curriculum designed to develop management agility in new and growing leaders. The training teaches leadership in four dimensions, your team, your peers, your suppliers, and your leaders.

Group Leadership Coaching
You can be part of a small group of professionals working through the dimensions of Leader From The Pack via either face-to-face or remote group or individual coaching.

Specialties

Skilled at developing leaders at all levels of an organization, affecting change through win-win team building and relationship management, an affinity for ideas, a why not attitude and a bias for action. Combined track record in technology and operations leadership provides cross-functional relevance and impact. Creative, inquisitive, driven to implement the best solutions and exceed expectations with character, integrity and quality as a central focus.
