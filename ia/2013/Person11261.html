Ayanna Phillip's Summary

A talented and dedicated entrepreneur. She is now an Executive Assistant & Product Specialist for the Science Department at AIG. We’re leaders in property casualty insurance, life insurance and retirement services, mortgage insurance, and aircraft leasing.
