Max Morse's Summary

Max Morse is a media consultant based in San Francisco, California. Max’s passion for visual storytelling has taken him across the globe on assignments for news services, magazines, newspapers as well as PR and advertising clients. He has worked for media companies including Sports Illustrated, ESPN, Reuters, The New York Times, Getty Images and USA Today. He has produced PR and advertising projects for companies such as Canon USA, Visa and AT&T.
