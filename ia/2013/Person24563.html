Christopher D. Southgate, Ph.D., J.D.'s Experience

Patent agent

Sughrue Mion

Partnership; 201-500 employees; Law Practice industry

May 2010 – May 2013 (3 years 1 month)

As a registered patent agent with the law firm, Sughrue Mion, in DC my work focused on intellectual property and patent-law issues in the field of biotechnology. In addition to preparing and prosecuting patent applications, I assisted with due-diligence reports on freedom-to-operate and patentability analyses in the field of molecular and cellular biology.

As a scientist with more than 12 years post-graduate research experience, I conducted studies on eukaryotic gene regulation, AIDS and other lentiviruses, mouse gene knock-outs, transgenesis, regulation of angiogenesis, mammalian molecular embryology, hematopoiesis, signal transduction, nucleic acid sequencing, nucleic acid synthesis, sequencing instrumentation, structural genomics, protein biochemistry, epigenetics, RNA interference, and, more recently, human adult stem cell biology.
