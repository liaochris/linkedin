Abby Leafe's Summary

Qualitative research consultant with over a decade of experience helping companies solve their tough business problems. Particularly deep experience in B2B, financial services, IT, and travel & tourism. Consistently seeking out ways to utilize technology to improve qualitative data collection and analysis. Passion for user experience research.
