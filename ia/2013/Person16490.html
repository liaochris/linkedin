Brandon Ishida's Summary

I am currently pursuing the B.S./M.S. Combined Bachelor's and Master's Program at UC Santa Cruz with a B.S. in Robotics Engineering. I plan to pursue an M.S. in Computer Engineering with a Designated Emphasis(DE) in Robotics and Control. I envision demand for robotic automation will accelerate at breakneck rates in the coming decades and I want to be in the job markets that grow the most in the 21st century.

I have experience working at SPAWAR where I was exposed to some system programming on microcontrollers and GPS navigation as well as wireless communication protocols. I am currently interested in jobs related to naval defense, transportation, energy infrastructure or aerospace industries.

My greater life aspiration is to be part of the next generation of the digital age and contribute in the development of new autonomous systems which are involved in every major industry as well as developing industries. My ultimate dream is to play a part in developing large scale, high impact technologies that will improve or innovate society and quality of life for everyone on the planet.
