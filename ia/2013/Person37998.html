Dr John Curran's Summary

I am a business anthropologist, founder and director of JC Innovation & Strategy (JCIS) in London. Previously I was Director and Co-Founder of 7bn Insight & Agency which success led us to be finalists in the IDSA Medical and Scientific Design (US) awards while also working with some of the leading FMCG, Pharma, Retail and media brands in the world.

My professional expertise lie in generating creative and innovative business opportunities for global and UK clients and work in partnership with them so that the opportunities are a central part of their business, marketing and design strategies.

As one of the first anthropologists in the UK to use anthropology in the business sector I have over 15 years of experience shaping the use of cultural analysis to inform business strategy for some of the worlds biggest brands.

I combine my professional background as a Business Anthropologist with my previous experience working as a organisational consultant. Therefore, I understand consumers, audiences and users while also understanding the culture of my clients and their organisations. It is this combination that provides me with the ability to bridge insights with business needs so to generate innovative direction for their markets, brands, products and services. Key to this is that the innovative journey is based on a 'Partnership Model' where I work closely together with my clients and their teams.

I have extensive experience working across different industries for companies such as Coca Cola, TESCO, Novo Nordisk, Johnson&Johnson, Smith & Nephew, BBC, Channel 4, Samsung, Kohler, Intel, Hallmark PLC, Cancer Research UK, Design Council UK, Department of Health UK, NHS

Academically, I lecture on Design & Anthropology at The Royal College of Art (RCA) and on Innovation Management at Central St Martin's College of Art and Design

Dr John Curran's Experience

Director & Founder

JC Innovation & Strategy Ltd (JCIS)

January 2013 – Present (10 months) London, United Kingdom

JCIS is an innovation and strategy consultancy in London. We create business opportunities for our clients by understanding their complex questions relating to their markets, consumers, brands, products and services. We achieve this by combining expertise in the social sciences, research, marketing & advertising, design and management consultancy.

What we do -
1/ Identify and map core cultural, consumer and market codes.

2/ Provide strategic direction so to challenge and develop existing codes and create new ones


How we do this -
1/ Understanding the Consumer World – their everyday lives, communities and behaviours
- Ethnography, Trends, Market Analysis, Group Facilitation, Previous Research Analysis, Strategic White Papers

2/ Understanding the Internal World – the clients organisational culture so to understand how best to embed insights and generate, in partnership, strategic goals
- Co-Creation & Executive Facilitation, Learning Labs, Internal Interviews, Internal Ethnography, Brand & Product Portfolio Audits

JCIS's approach is based on Cultural Analysis & People Centred Innovation - Developing insight and strategies through understanding the behaviour of people, organisations and markets. Central to this is understanding 'Why' and 'How' behaviours are shaped and formed in the way they are.
