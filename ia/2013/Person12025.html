Bart Hoekstra's Summary

I'm an Open Innovator, all-round geek and generalist. I love working with amazing people and organizations on remarkable products, businesses, causes and futures.

As an Open Innovator I work with awesome organizations or independent people, that are trying to achieve remarkable things or tackle the challenges and problems of the future. I do so in all kinds of ways, but always in a design-thinking approach, going beyond that what is requested and creating that what is needed.

As an all-round geek I'm continuously working with cutting-edge technology that makes my life easier, more fun, more efficient and healthier. I love hacking (improving) my life with technology and a good conversation about ethics.

As a generalist I use all the knowledge from different fields that I have into creating valuable connections between those fields. True innovation happens (and new stuff is created) when different disciplines meet. That's a connection that I love making.

Also, I am deeply passionate about education and creating a more sustainable and resilient world.
