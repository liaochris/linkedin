Justin Foster's Summary

Co-founder and CMO of Klowd.com - the creators of the SlideKlowd Audience Engagement Platform. In addition to my business ventures, I am a speaker and author on branding, marketing, and generational/technology trends. I am the author of "Oatmeal v Bacon: How to Differentiate in a Generic World". My second book, "How to be Awesome: The 7 Elements of Human Bacon" covers personal branding and will be available Summer 2013. Outside of work, I'm a husband, dad, football geek, coffee snob and Texas Music aficionado. You can follow me on Twitter @fosterthinking.

Justin Foster's Publications

  • Oatmeal v Bacon: How to Differentiate in a Generic World

    • Aloha Publishing
    • March 1, 2011
    Authors: Justin Foster

    Oatmeal is boring. Bacon is interesting. This metaphor brings clarity to why some brands dominant and others survive. "Oatmeal v Bacon: How to Differentiate in a Generic World" makes the case that earning attention and market share starts by being a truly unique and interesting brand. Humorous, thought-provoking and full of useful ideas, "Oatmeal v Bacon" will transform the way you market and advertise, develop products, create customer experiences, and build a culture. Most of all, "Oatmeal v Bacon" will give you permission to be yourself and build a brand around being truly DIFFERENT.
