Harriet Libov's Summary

A Top Producing Agent in the Armonk brokerage in 2012, 2011, 2010 and 2008. Consistent Platinum and Gold Award Winner from Houlihan Lawrence. I represent buyer and sellers in Armonk, Chappaqua, Bedford, Pleasantville, and Mount Kisco. If there's an area I do not cover, I refer to top professionals in that area so that you are always working with a local expert.

Twenty years ago, I made the move from Manhattan to Armonk, and as I learned, buying real estate is not just about buying a house, but also choosing a community. Having renovated a home, participated as a parent in the Byram Hills schools, and sought for my family its best quality of life through local recreation, shopping, and entertainment, I am proud to share with my buyers and sellers invaluable personal experience.

I am passionate about my community, learning new ideas, strategies and information technology. Sponsoring www.Armonkhomepage.com, is the newest way that I am reaching out to the the community. The website connects homeowners with local schools, recreational programs, restaurants, businesses and services, and community organizations, including links to websites we use every day. It is absolutely free, and a valuable tool for all! I hope it will become the homepage for all who live and work in the Armonk community.

Specialties: Skilled negotiating, Marketing, Information technology, Educating and informing consumers, Professional approach to building relationships within the real estate community.
