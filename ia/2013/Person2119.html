Ad van der Rest's Summary

Ad van der Rest - 07793 593862 - ad@vanderrest.com

I undertake interim management assignments, typically as Interim Human Resources Director, Interim Head of HR, Interim HR Project Manager or HR Consultant.

I usually deliver HR assignments in Berkshire, the Thames Valley and West & Central London.

The type of assignments include:

• HR change and crisis resolution
• Restructure, mergers and acquisitions
• Employee relations project management

Achievements:

HR CHANGE AND CRISIS RESOLUTION.

Took two businesses that had lost their HR Directors in difficult circumstances, through HR restructure, successfully rebuilding their HR teams, resolving multiple Tribunal cases and restoring HR’s effectiveness and credibility in the business. I address HR crisis, HR change, HR ‘health-check’ and HR gap assignments if during transformation (not B.A.U.).

HR IMPLEMENTATION OF RESTRUCTURES, MERGERS AND ACQUISITIONS

More than a dozen business unit acquisitions successfully managed and integrated. Stock deals and TUPE acquisitions, both in and out, from 2,000 employees to a handful in size. I address the full project-cycle, from due diligence, group consultation and works council consultation, implementation & mop up.

EMPLOYEE RELATIONS PROJECT MANAGEMENT.

As Interim Director and Project Manager, implemented or resolved multiple redundancy, absence, poor performance and grievance cases (over the years individual cases into 4-figures). Multiple Tribunal cases won out-right, with claims mediated, mitigated and settled avoiding hundreds of thousands of pounds in costs. I address Employee Relations in an effective ‘plain-speaking’ and courteous manner so that even in difficult situations people are treated decently and the commercial objectives of the organisation are achieved.
