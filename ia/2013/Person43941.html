Fabrice Talbot's Summary

Before calling San Francisco home, I lived and worked in these great European capitals: Paris, London, Amsterdam, Sofia, and Barcelona – ok, the latter is not a capital but it combines world-class soccer team (go Barca) and top-MBA school (IESE, Barcelona).

I started as a software engineer in 1998 and quickly graduated to product management, building Internet software products for startups and Fortune 500 companies, with a B2B focus (Enterprise Content Management, cloud-based collaboration, and business workflow). My product management expertise ranges from strategy, roadmap, PRD, MRD, UI and UX design to partnership development, client engagement, and product evangelist.

I’ve had multiple leadership roles, orchestrating software development (Saas or on-premise), managing technical and cross-functional teams of various sizes -5 to 40+ people, both internally and offshore, and implementing cost-effective internal operations.

As an entrepreneur (x2 founder), I am a result-focused individual, with a great analytical mind (MS in Applied Mathematics) and a contagious desire to build useful and usable products. I bring a strong work ethics, high accountability, and a good sense of humor to the workplace!
