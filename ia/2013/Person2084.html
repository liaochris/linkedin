ABOUT ADRIENNE

Adrienne has over 25 years’ experience exceeding sales and management goals with a diverse background across a variety of sectors; including technology, digital media, healthcare, online marketing, finance, music business, advertising, travel and restaurant marketing.

Adrienne is the founder of Thinkfree Media, a Digital Marketing Agency located in Houston, TX. Thinkfree Media is a self-operating business. Adrienne serves in an advisory role.

Adrienne is currently pursuing a B.S. Degree in Biophysics at The Honors College: University of Houston with an emphasis in Neuroscience and a minor in Creative Writing. She is the Associate Producer of the Houston 24 Hour Film Race and an advocate for StandUp For Kids, a non-profit organization dedicated to helping homeless and at-risk youth to stabilize and improve their lives.

Summary of Accomplishments:

Accomplished sales professional, responsible for generating over $2.1 million in advertising sales for top-tier technology and digital media enterprises with a track record of consistently earning top-performer status in highly competitive sales environments.

Deep expertise of the Sales Process, with an emphasis on steps of selling, the sales cycle, pipeline management and forecasting.

A natural leader and seasoned Sales Manager with emphasis on driving revenue by utilizing strategic operational planning and developing talent with a proactive approach to training, coaching for performance, motivation and goal achievement.

Business growth consultant to SMBs, with a specialty in increasing profit through analyzing needs and goals of the business and reevaluating current strategies and techniques.

Analytical thinker with extensive experience in selling to key decision-makers; including Fortune 500 CEO’s and Senior Executives.

Adrienne resides in Houston, TX and is also interested in science, technology, health & medicine, filmmaking, writing, music, learning, teaching, motivating and helping.
