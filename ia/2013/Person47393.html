Gayle Palmer D.O., Osteopath's Summary

I have completed at least one weeks post-graduate education every year since I began practising in 1991. This has been in osteopathy, personal development, homeopathy, Reiki healing, spiritual healing etc. I will continue to do this. It's great to meet and interact with your friends and colleagues on a regular basis.

Having built the Living Elements Clinic in 2003 and created a multi-disciplinary practice I have more opportunities to share information across disciplines and increase my knowledge base. This also vastly improves the service that I am able to give to my clients as they have reliable and professional practitioners to whom I can refer them when needed.

I am sure that the practice will continue to grow and expand and thereby serve its intended purpose of health and wellbeing for everyone.

Specialties: Cranio-sacral osteopathy, especially for babies and children; during and after pregnancy / childbirth; dental and facial deveolpment and the treatment thereof with osteopathy.

Plus much more in-depth one-to-one coaching and health activation for those who want and need more personal attention and care for ALL aspects of their health using Gayle's wealth of orthodox, complementary and alternative health knowledge - embodied in the Vital Elements for Health programmes.
