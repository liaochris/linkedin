Jonah Sterling's Summary

In his current role as Group Creative Director, Jonah brings over 15 years experience in digital design which he applies to a wide variety of visual challenges involved in creating a complete User Experience. His work experience stretches across many design challenges including: toy design, promotional marketing, PC game user interface design, B2B/ B2C, X-Internet applications, branding and collateral pieces, illustrative conceptual diagramming, high-end Power-Point presentations and web interface design. His work has been a Webby Finalist in 07, and quite frankly, his work rocks.

Jonah has a BFA in Illustration from California College of Arts.

Specialties

Excellent illustrations and conceptualization skills.
