…the CEO with a vision, to build and execute technology plans that make it real.

I use thinking tools to refine & develop vision along different time horizons, identify constraints that hold us back and resources required to execute effectively. I also value leading by example, working directly with my teams. I think with them, doodle with them and help them make decisions. I lead my teams in a bigger story, whether it is one of personal ambition or of corporate vision for the future. I increase their autonomy and professionalism, expanding everyone’s capacity for effective action.
