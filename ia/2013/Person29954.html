With the newspaper business taking a downturn in the past 5-10 years across North America, I have ventured into the world of digital media, creating a regional sportswriting public relations firm and and sports web site, to fill a much-needed and requested void vacated by former print publications.

The site, www.northoaklandsports.com, is starting to grow its numbers and we are instituting a marketing and advertising campaign to reach the large target audience of high school, college, youth/travel, minor league sports teams and other local and regional sports entities.
