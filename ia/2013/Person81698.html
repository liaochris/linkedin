Lisa Croley's Summary

As the managing member of the Paragon HR Consulting LLC , I am a dynamic, innovative and accomplished Human Resources leader with highly-developed communication and interpersonal skills capable of influencing change with domestic and international board members, senior executives and employees. As a trusted advisor, I consult with companies to develop and implement practical solutions for issues related to Human Resources, Compensation and Corporate Governance.

Specialties: HR Leadership, Corporate Goverance, Executive and Employee Compensation, Incentive Compensation, Equity Compensation, Director Compensation

Guest speaker for groups such as National Association of Corporate Directors, Corporate Board Member, and the Institute for Excellence in Corporate Governance (IECG), and a guest lecturer for the University of Texas-Dallas MBA program.

More information at www.Paragon-HR.com.
