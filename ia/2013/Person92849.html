Michael Pedone's Summary

SalesBuzz.com is a Florida-based company that specializes in phone sales training. We teach innovative and proven phone selling techniques using online training workshops.

These live webinars are lead by Michael Pedone, a world-class sales training mentor, coach and marketing expert.

Michael has helped hundreds of companies across the globe improve their sales just by using his no-nonsense phone sales techniques. Anyone can learn his sales training method and put his techniques into practice quickly.

The result? Phone sales reps feel excited about their work again and become more motivated and productive. And the companies they work for rake in more leads, conversions and profits.
