John Ace Underwood's Summary

I started my professional sales career with Motorola, Inc. 33 years ago selling 2 way communication systems. I received the best sales training both at a corporate level as well as one-on-one training and as a result, quickly rose to join the ranks of the 5 Top sales producers.

In 1984, I ventured out on my own and became a corporate sales and sales management consultant serving a variety of industries ranging from Housing, RV Automobile, Boats and the mortgage industry.

SALES PEOPLE ARE LOSING SALES BECAUSE OF WHAT WE DIDN'T KNOW AND INFORMATION THAT THEY DON'T HAVE.

Within the last two years, with my new venture, Micro Seminars, all of my effort is now directed towards sales, sales management, leadership development, recruiting, testing and hiring in the housing sector.

MICRO SEMINARS TEACHES PEOPLE WHAT THEY NEED TO KNOW, USING A METHOD WITH WHICH MAXIMIZES WHAT PEOPLE ACTUALLY IMPLEMENT.

My articles appear in various trade publications and cover a variety of topics relating to sales, sales management and sales team development and have been frequently retained as an SME on various projects.

As a consultant, sales and sales management consultant and keynote speaker, I have helped literally 10's of thousands of sales professionals increase both their understanding of the psychology of selling, and as a result, their personal sales performance.

I am the author of Scratch Selling; 18 Lessons Golf Will Teach You About Sales, which will be released as a complete video training program in the fall of 2014.

I founded Micro Seminars in January 2012 and it looks like it's going to be a runaway success, for which I am truly grateful.
