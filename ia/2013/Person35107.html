David Hyde, M.Sc, CPC's Experience

Senior Security Consultant

David Hyde & Associates

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Security and Investigations industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months)

Following a highly successful 20-year career with Cadillac Fairview, David decided to dedicate his unique blend of knowledge, skills, education and experience to his own professional consulting service.

David Hyde & Associates is an independent consulting practice with access to a national network of trusted, best in class consultancy partners.

David's approach - and that of his staff and trusted affiliates - begins with a clear understanding of the client's business - it's vision, mission and objectives. Against this backdrop, the necessary steps are taken to understand the nature of the security, crime or risk management challenge(s) at hand and to offer tangible risk mitigation solutions to optimize return on the Client's investment.

Faculty Member, Continuing Education

University of Calgary

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

September 2005 – Present (8 years 1 month)

David's students receive instruction in business and workplace crime. The curriculum integrates applied concepts of: (1) Crime Prevention; (2) Risk Management, and; (3) Security Management. Students leave class with a clear understanding of the aetiology of crime, risk management in the business context, security management fundamentals, and proven approaches to mitigate the risk of crime.

Advisory Council Member

Civic Protection Institute

March 2013 – Present (7 months)

David sits as a member of the Advisory Council for the non-profit Civic Protection Institute (CPI). The goal of the CPI is to help police agencies improve efficiencies through the vetting and quality control of private security services. CPI also provides services back to the community through crime prevention innovations and leadership training programs.

Editorial Advisory Board Member

Canadian Security

2012 – Present (1 year)

David is delighted to have been invited back to serve a second term as an Editorial Advisory Board member at Canadian Security: The Publication for Professional Security Management. In this role David will work with the Editor and other Board members on Canadian security industry awards and will advise on and contribute to magazine content in his areas of expertise.

Lecturer, Centre of Excellence in Security, Resilience & Intelligence

Schulich Executive Education Centre

Educational Institution; 11-50 employees; Education Management industry

2010 – 2012 (2 years) York University

This unique world-class program is the product of extensive consultation with senior decision-makers and executive leadership in both the private and public sector who are responsible for: security in relation to multiple threats, and, resiliency of their organizations in the face of these threats.
The product of this program is a culmination of input and delivery from persons in first response, corporate security, military, and government, all with international experience and recognition.

Member, Board of Directors (Chairman)

CRE-FDSG (Commercial Real Estate Financial District Security Group)

2009 – February 2010 (1 year)

David co-founded and co-chaired the CRE-FDSG, an association of security executives from Canada's commercial real estate sector responsible for protecting the major bank towers in the Toronto Financial District. The group was formed a year prior to the 2010 G20 Summit in Toronto with a focus on critical incident prevention and preparedness, as well as critical infrastructure protection.
