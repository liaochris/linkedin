Peter Pleser's Experience

Disk Jockey

Around The World

May 1980 – Present (33 years 4 months)

Peter Pleser a.k.a. DJ Pero is without a doubt one of the best and most respected dj's in Slovenian club scene. He got his respect in his long career of over 25 years since he started djing in the 80's. Since then he was filling the dance floors of many clubs in Slovenia and abroad. Besides working as a DJ he worked on a couple of radio stations where he had his own late night show. At one time he decided that mixing the tracks wasn't enough for him and he put some of the energy into producing his own tracks and making remixes for others. His main music style is house and he can play everything from easy deep house to tech and progresive. It all depends from the crowd in front of him while he always tries to get the feel for the people so he can rock their world.
