Ana García Colomina's Summary

Spanish multifunctional creative with more than six years experience in the advertising, marketing and communication industry online and offline.

I am multifuctional because of my multifield background, my international experience and because I am a fast learner so I like facing challenges and adapting to new situations and industries.

So I'm a social media strategist, a creative professional, a graphic designer, an illustrator... but I'm also a dreamer, in love with visual candies (in Spanish, Golosinas visuales) , a little bit crazy but organized and hard worker!

At the moment I am working as a Social Media Strategist in Madrid (Spain) for the spanish shoes company Pedro Garcia and as a freelance Community Manager for Ottodisanpietro and other little companies.

If you want to know a little bit more about me you can visit my portfolio online, you can also let your imagination fly with my blog, or follow me on Facebook and Twitter.
