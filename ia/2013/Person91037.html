Meinaart van Straalen's Summary

Dutch webdeveloper with strong Actionscript, HTML, Javascript, jQuery and PHP skills. I have 10+ years experience working for major (Dutch) brands such as Bol.com, Binck Banck, Danio, Danoontje, Activia, Karl Kani, Terre des Hommes, Blijdorp, Volvo, Robeco, Hogeschool Utrecht, Hogeschool van Amsterdam, Ilse, BenQ and many more.

I am specialized in the technical realisation of (Hyves) campaignsites, gadgets, branded games, Facebook applications and other online applications and websites.

I have used Adobe Flash for online applications such as product selectors, touch screen applications, screensavers, mobile applications and for lots of (branded) games.

I have written several "How to" articles for the open source (PHP) framework ATK.

See www.kayenta.nl/portfolio for recent projects.

I love working for advertising agencies.
