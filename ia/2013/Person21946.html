Gini Graham Scott's Summary

I have published 50+ books with major publishers and have published 30 books under my own publishing company's logo: Changemakers Publishing, which are available in paperback and e-book formats. I help others write, publish and promote their own books. Examples of my books are featured at www.ginigrahamscott.com and my writing services at www.changemakerspublishingandwriting.com.
I have written 16 screenplays, some of them optioned; one is in postproduction. I have written, produced over 60 dozen short films working with various directors, which can be seen on my website at www.changemakersproductions.com or on YouTube at www.youtube.com/changemakersprod. I have written a book; A COMPLETE GUIDE TO WRITING, PRODUCING AND DIRECTING A LOW-BUDGET SHORT FILM which was published by Hal Leonard's Limelight Editions in November 2011. My follow-up book with them is FINDING FUNDS FOR YOUR FILM OR TV PROJECT, out in the fall of 2013.
I have organized several film industry networking group through Meetup.com -- the Bay Area Film and TV Connection and the San Francisco Film Industry Group and two L.A. based groups -- the Film and TV Connection and the Hollywood Film Industry group.
I am working with clients on writing self-help, memoirs, and popular business books. I am also assisting clients in turning original ideas or adapting memoirs and novels into scripts.

Specialties: screenplay writing, book writing, proposal writing, ghostwriting, game design, songwriting, promotion, marketing, PR, focus groups, qualitative social research, interviewing, radio show hosting
