MaryBeth Smith's Summary

With over 20 years experience teaching in university, community, and business settings, MaryBeth Smith now uses the Feldenkrais Method to help people improve their self-image, function, and enjoyment in movement and in life.

Specialties: Chronic pain and stress management; insomnia; performance improvement in arts, sports and business; body dismorphia (including anorexia); change management/embodiment experiences for the gay and transgender community; Wellness coaching via whole-foods, plant-based lifestyle.

As a classically trained singer, she continues to perform and work with singers and speakers on the improvement of vocal quality, technique, health, and presentation skills.
