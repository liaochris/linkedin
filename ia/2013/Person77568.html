Kurt Wannebo's Summary

Kurt Wannebo "Broker Kurt", is the CEO and Broker for San Diego Real Estate & Investments. He started his own company in 2005 and quickly became one of the top 10 real estate agents in San Diego County for 2012. His team now closes and sells hundreds of homes a year, and in 2013 was ranked by the Wall Street Journal and Real Trends as the #1 Agent Team in San Diego for transactions, #3 for CA, and #73 in the USA. Not only that, but has amassed hundreds of online client testimonials and 5 star reviews, talking about their excellent experience with his team and services.

Broker Kurt, has also traveled all over the country training other real estate agents, and speaking at notable seminars of up to 3000 agents at a time. Broker Kurt is regularly featured in the San Diego Union Tribune as a real estate expert, featured on UT-TV weekly Real Estate TV Show - The American Dream and has been interviewed for news articles on TV, radio shows, webcasts, and by global real estate trainers. Broker Kurt has a large team of agents comprising of administration staff, marketing staff, and seller and buyer specialists, to help with every part of the real estate process.
