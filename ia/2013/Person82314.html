Tineke Looman's Summary

Coaching for me is the dialogue to create effectiveness in work and life. Through many questions and feedback and if needed, supported by business advice you will develop a different view what success and work pleasure are and what work really means to you. The result: Personal gains and business profit.
Coaching can ‘force’ you to take the time to look at yourself: your convictions and your potential. It teaches you how to connect and how to communicate better. The coaching also forces you to ask yourself questions. And it is my conviction that it is “questioning ourselves that helps to develop us”
