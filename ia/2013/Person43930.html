Dr. Fabrizio Mancini's Summary

Dr. Fabrizio Mancini leads and transforms organizations and individuals in Wellness, Integrative Healthcare and Education. As President/CEO, Businessman, best-selling Author, Chiropractor and internationally-acclaimed Speaker, he delivers results for corporate and individual growth. Fab is fluent in English-Spanish with multicultural perspective, deep expertise and broad skills including:

•Executive Leadership - creating and executing vision for growth, profitability and value
•Business Consulting - partnering to build value through innovation and market expansion
•Wellness Consulting - inspiring individuals in self-healing and healthy lifestyles
•Best-selling Author, Speaker & Media Personality - educating and motivating others to improve health and wellness

Dr. Fabrizio Mancini's Experience

International Executive & Consultant, President Emeritus, Chiropractor, Speaker & Author

Dallas, TX

Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Higher Education industry

2012 – Present (1 year) Dallas

After transitioning from President/CEO to President Emeritus of Parker in 2012, Fab expanded his focus to transforming more organizations and individuals in global wellness and integrative healthcare. He consults to businesses, governments and non-profits on vision, strategies and execution. He helps product-driven companies expand market share and has consulted in Mexico to expand new careers and reduce healthcare costs. Fab serves as an advisor to governmental and civic organizations including the White House Commission for Complementary and Alternative Medicine and the Texas Governor’s Advisory Council on Physical Fitness.

Fab is the bestselling author of The Power of Self-Healing and the video online program, “21 Days to Self-Healing.” He authored Four Steps for Living a Fabulous Life and co-authored Chicken Soup for the Chiropractic Soul and The Well Adjusted Soul. He hosts the popular radio show “Self-Healing with Dr. Fab”, and has been featured television guest on “The Dr. Phil Show” and “The Doctors Show” and other media outlets including Spanish networks CNN Espanol, Univision and Telemundo, exposing millions of people to a positive lifestyle.

President

Parker University

Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Higher Education industry

1999 – 2012 (13 years) Dallas, TX

As the President/CEO of Parker University from 1999 to 2012, Fab stabilized, grew and transformed the organization from a chiropractic college to fully accredited university meeting the evolving needs of the healthcare industry. Under Fab’s leadership, institutional assets increased, enrollment doubled, retention and graduation rates surpassed the nation, and chiropractic reached an expanded global market. Recognized as President/CEO Emeritus in 2013 and continuing today, Fab consults with the organization in advancement, admissions and Parker Seminars.
