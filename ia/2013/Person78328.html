Lara Dilkes L.Ac.'s Summary

My passion when practicing Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) is to be of assistance to you as you transition to a more healthy life.
I get so much excitement and motivation when people achieve better health from their acupuncture experiences.
It is such simple yet all encompassing medicine that every day is a new experience for me as a practitioner, and I always look forward to what lies ahead in the coming to work.
I enjoy speaking about the theory of TCM and how it translates into western terms. It is always a fun challenge to me, so feel free to ask me questions and I will do my best to make it understandable.
I can't help but be inspired by this medicine and it's truly profound effects.
I look forward to the opportunity to give you a little assistance to the road of health soon:)

In Good Health!

Lara

Specialties
