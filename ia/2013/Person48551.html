Sandy Dell's Summary

Sandy Dell is a successful independent sales representative, working in the gift and gourmet food industries selling wholesale to retail stores since January of 2001. In that capacity, she’s called on over 1200 gift retailers, turning 450+ into customers, while representing up to 35 gift and gourmet producers at a time.

She is also sales manager and co-owner, with her husband, of Gourmet Innovations LLC, a manufacturer, wholesaler, and distributor of gourmet foods and gifts, including the brands Idaho Redneck, and Gold Mountain Gourmet. Sandy also manages multiple retail and wholesale web sites in the gourmet food niche, including several in-house, plus some for specialty food clients.

Recent accomplishments include launch of the following eGuides:
1. “The COMPLETE GUIDE for Selling to Gift Shops” (http://selltogiftshops.com)
2. "How to Find, Recruit and Manage Sales Reps" (http://howtofindsalesreps.com)
3,. "Trade Show Exhibiting Secrets" (http://tradeshowexhibitingsecrets.com)

Coming soon, a new home study course, “How to Start a Home-Based Independent Sales Representative Business.”

In addition, she’s submitted and published dozens of articles online in her areas of expertise.

Sandy also authors and maintains several blogs, including:
• www.SellingtoGiftShops.com
• www.GiftRepSandy.com
• www.WildHuckleberry.com

Sandy is an experienced sales person, working previously at Roos Atkins, Abercrombie & Fitch, World Book Encyclopedia, and Avon, where she won multiple awards. She also has a technical degree in Merchandising and over three years of retail management experience, including positions as manager for a Hastings book department, a woodworkers co-operative, and her gourmet and gift store, Tastes of Idaho.
