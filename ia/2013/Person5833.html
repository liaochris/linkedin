Amrit Pal Singh's Summary

I am Amrit Pal Singh, a Communication & Motion Designer. I was born and raised in New Delhi, India. I graduated studying animation and worked for a while in India. One year later I travelled to Vancouver, Canada.

I joined Digital Design at Vancouver Film School and after graduating I stayed there and worked for design houses like Grey Group, StoryPanda, and finally a fulltime stable job with Fueled based in NYC.

In February 2013 I decided to move back to New Delhi, where I continue my work with Fueled and pursue my passion for making children’s story books by founding Mister Bumbles.
