Anthony Piwarun's Summary

I’m a search engine marketing and digital strategy professional with experience in both organic (SEO) and paid search (PPC) that strives for excellence and will do whatever it takes to be successful in my role. Having worked in account management prior to joining the SEM industry, I came in with an appreciation for good client management that has grown stronger ever since. I sincerely believe that to be successful as a digital marketing strategist, one must be fully aligned with their client’s brand, goals, and business objectives in an effort to bring forth a positive ROI and long-lasting relationship.

While I have experience putting my clients campaigns in front of their target audience through both mediums the moment they click “search”, my passion lies in SEO. Nothing is more fulfilling than working on a campaign around the clock and seeing top rankings for competitive keyword phrases with an increase in qualified traffic to boot! Challenging? Of course! But in the end it’s all worth it.

Despite SEO being an ever-evolving industry that requires keeping up with the latest trends on blogs like SEOMoz, SEJ, and Search Engine Land, I do find time to hone my skills at the part of job that separates the men from the boys: communication. For me, it’s not just important to know that you need be creative in ad copy, title tags, Meta descriptions and headlines, but it’s knowing how to effectively get the message across to clients. If after a few years of working together, one of my clients knows how to develop quality content that feeds both their audience and search engines, can effectively build buzz (and links) around a new product launch through blogger outreach, and has learned just enough along the way about SEO to be dangerous, then I think we’re all winners.

Interesting in learning more? Feel free to connect with me here on LinkedIn or follow me on Twitter - @apiwarun.

Specialties

Search Engine Optimization (SEO), paid search engine marketing (PPC), online media buying, client management, web analytics, internet marketing, interactive strategy, digital strategy and everything web.
