    Team Members: Jason Burgess

    This is an online service that allows a business owner to create printable coupons for their website. (without bugging the Photoshop guy or web designer)

    What makes it unique is the included coupon design software. Unlike any other offering in the market, I created a graphical, easy to use application where coupons can be created visually.

    The software also manages start and stop dates for coupon visibility and with just a few mouse clicks, a customer can ‘fuse’ these coupons into their website.
