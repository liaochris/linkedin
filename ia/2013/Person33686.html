Debra Zimmer, MBA's Summary

I focus the brilliant vision of business executives, entrepreneurs and thought leaders like you into an actionable plan that increases your impact in serving your clients, employees and partners.

I do this by providing you with marketing consulting, marketing coaching, marketing training programs, and tools to help you magnify your impact.

I am also a national speaker, author and trainer focused on Social Media Strategy and Marketing Management.
