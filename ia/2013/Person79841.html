Lee Woods' Summary

Online merchandising and trading expert with a keen balance of technical expertise and interpersonal skills.

I have 15+ years of retail experience including e-Commerce management, online trading and merchandising, systems training, business relationship management and technical support. Extremely adaptable, and a true team player.

I love nothing more than hitting the ground running and can pick new systems up with ease.

Subject Matter Expert regarding all aspects of online merchandising - navigation and taxonomy, analytics and data, internal searchandising, metadata and keyword search engine optimisation, customer journey, conversion and ATV improvement.

I'm senior in the web team at White Stuff - looking at all aspects of customer behaviour through our website with a commercial focus, and ensuring that the merchandising, product placement and other aspects of the journey are engaging and result in sales. Keeping to strict brand guidelines whilst coordinating seasonal promotions and launches, it's a challenging and evolving environment.

Before I joined the White Stuff team, I looked after the online proposition for Fashion Accessories, Homewares, Own Label, Food, Wine and Gifts at Harrods.com - very different from White Stuff, which shows just how adapatable I am!

And way back in the ether I was responsible for the daily operations of the original zavvi.co.uk (and Virgin Megastores before it) prior to the administrators selling it on! We weren't to blame...
