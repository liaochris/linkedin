James Lipinski's Publications

  • Protecting Public Safety Under the Cloud

    • Accenture
    • November 2012
    Authors: James Lipinski

    Budgetary strictures are affecting even public safety and service organizations. Cloud-based services offer these organizations a way to do more with less that is superior to traditional forms of inter-agency collaboration.
    Instead of collaborating with each other, public-safety organizations can achieve better results by using hosted managed services such as cloud-based software-as-a-service or business-process-as-a-service applications. Examples of systems that organizations could host on the cloud include computer-aided dispatch as a service, records-management as a service and 911 as a service.

    • State of Vermont
    • January 2012
    Authors: James Lipinski

    Study prepared for the Vermont Legislature examining 9-1-1 funding and proposing an innovative funding model that collects revenue based on the point of service rather than the point of billing.
