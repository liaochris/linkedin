Balazs Bota's Summary

Digitally focused integrated senior creative with a strong combination of conceptual thinking, designing and managing skills with 13+ years of experience providing creative services for Nestlé, Procter & Gamble, Coca Cola, Pepsi, Kraft, Honda, Ford, Renault, Opel, Telenor, BAT, NDrive, Garmin, Skanska, LeGrand, Zamil, The Central Bank of Hungary and many others.


ACHIEVEMENTS

As Creative Leader and the creator of the winner idea of the tender contributed to the win of NDrive Navigation Systems the biggest international client of Insomnia. Thereafter was responsible for the entire creative development of the worldwide digital communication, inter alia, creating and launching a new creative platform for the brand.

As Creative Director led the creation of the first locally developed integrated campaign of Opel Service. Due to its success the TVC was adapted by international markets. It increased the brand‘s revenues highly exceeding the expectations and it was awarded with an EFFIE.

As Chief Art Director contributed to the win of multimillion dollar Telecom giant Telenor the most profitable client of Ogilvy Budapest. Thereafter as a promoted Head of Art struggling with manpower problems successfully managed and motivated the team of only 5 making extreme efforts to maintain creative quality. Proving managing abilities could stay calm under pressure while simultaneously built a team of 15 and produced 25+ integrated campaigns (including a gigantic country sized image launch) delivering outstanding results. A year later won and managed the digital creative part of the brand.


MAIN STRENGHTS

Based on the leadership experience can efficiently develop and manage medium sized creative groups.

Special digital ability with sound knowledge of integrated creativity meeting the requirements of the new challenges of advertising.

Obsessed with details let alone the perfectionist designer and conceptualist attitude guaranteeing the high quality of the outcome.
