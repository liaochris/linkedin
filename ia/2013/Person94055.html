Mike Coker's Summary

Mike has a passion for building businesses, branding, marketing communication and project management.

As a designer, Mike offers a unique way of visualizing brands and products, systems and workflows alike – he has a strong understanding of how different people interact with different objects or processes and he knows how to create interfaces and experiences for the easy sharing of information and knowledge.

Mike has completed a number of different roles across the full project lifecycle – from sales and marketing, to creating strategies to resolve complex issues; from information architecture, content strategy and user experience to client presentations; from art direction to delivery; from design to development – and he has proven that he can research, consider and gain an in-depth insight of what a business and or project needs to thrive.
