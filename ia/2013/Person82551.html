As Director of Recruiting, I handle day-to-day management of the team with mentoring, performance goals, performance feedback, and train to effectively source, assess, and process talented candidates. Also conduct Manager training involving the interview and hiring process.

I evaluate and oversee management of recruiting solutions such as job postings, recruitment advertising, and hiring resources database (CRM).
