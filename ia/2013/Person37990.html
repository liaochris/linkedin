J. Michael Bennett's Summary

J. Michael Bennett, M.D., is one of the most highly respected orthopedic surgeons serving Sugar Land, Katy, and the Houston area. He is board certified and specialized in sports medicine. He uses the most advanced medical technology and the least invasive procedures possible.

Dr. Bennett has a particular interest in the treatment of shoulder, elbow, and knee disorders, as well as cartilage repair and restoration.

Dr. Bennett has gained a vast amount of experience in covering a variety of sports including football, soccer, basketball, baseball, and lacrosse. His experience in treating a large number of cases in sports medicine helped earn him the distinguished CAQ Certification in Sports Medicine.

Dr. Bennett focuses on the care and education of his patients. He is described as a compassionate listener who answers questions, and thoroughly explains options and procedures. As a result, patients are quickly put at ease.

With privileges at almost all area hospitals, he can recommend the surgical facility that best serves the needs of his patients.

Dr. Bennett continues to be involved in orthopedic research and instruction. He has a number of presentations and publications to his credit. He is a volunteer and clinical instructor for Shriners Hospital for Children and UT Houston.

Dr. Bennett serves on the American Board of Orthopedic Surgeons and is a member of several medical societies including:

• American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons (AAOS)
• American Orthopedic Society for Sports Medicine (AOSSM)
• Arthroscopy Association of North America (AANA)
• International Society of Knee Surgery, Arthroscopy, and Orthopedic Sports Medicine (ISAKOS)
• Texas Medical Association
• Harris County Medical Society

Dr. Bennett’s office will help you with all insurance matters. His staff thinks of their patients as part of their family. Their promise is to provide you with the highest quality of care using the most advanced technology and medical knowledge available.
