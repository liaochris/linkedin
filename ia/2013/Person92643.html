Michael LeGrand's Summary

Michael has always had a creative eye and an interest in art. But it wasn’t until after completing three years of Architectural studies at Drexel University that Michael discovered photography was the best outlet for his creativity. Michael began building his business and perfecting his craft in Philadelphia before relocating to Florida.

Michael brings a creative edge to the photography needs of his clients. He works on location in Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Orlando, Tampa, Ft. Myers, Ft. Lauderdale, Miami, and anywhere in between or beyond. If you have an upcoming project that you would like to discuss please contact Michael directly by phone or email.
