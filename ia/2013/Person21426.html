Celeste Ford's Summary

Experienced, DuPont award-winning TV reporter now running video production company that produces nonfiction video reports, especially for online. At First Person Video, our team is made up of former TV news professionals who make videos like the networks: smart, fast and authentic with an emphasis on information.

Recent projects include an overview video for one of metropolitan New York's largest providers of services for people with mental illnesses and developmental disabilities. Also a series of website videos for Montefiore Medical Center, and another series on researchers at a top-tier, private university. For Meredith TV, we produced 10 political reports which were carried by cable TV operators nationwide. And for several private clients, First Person Video is producing oral histories of family members with remarkable life stories to share.

Specialties:
* Producing all aspects of nonfiction video reports, branded content, and video oral histories
* Researching, interviewing, writing and story telling
* Consulting on SEO and social media strategies using video
* Coaching subjects for on-camera interviews
* Proficient in Final Cut Pro and prosumer video cameras
