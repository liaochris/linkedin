Dan McGrath's Summary

As the Managing Director of Research and Development, Dan McGrath drives innovation for the U2 Servers Lab at Rocket Software. Dan is responsible for global teams creating innovative database technology found running in some of the world's biggest brands.

A decade of working in the tech industry has lead to experience in database technology, penetration testing, security design, banking systems architecture and product management.
