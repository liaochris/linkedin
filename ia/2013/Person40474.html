Eli Bishop's Summary

+ I have worked with local and national clients with both layout / design, as well as with progressive modern digital illustrations. Some of the national / international companies I have freelanced for include: Entertainment Weekly (NYC), Akademiks clothing (NYC), GC Records (LA), Eliot Chang (NYC comedian), McMurry Inc. (PHX Marketing firm), Jive Magazine (ATL), Vice Magazine (NYC).

+ Other past design projects include: print work for multiple bands including cd covers / booklets, posters, and various promotional work. I have also done branding and package design for a coffee company, interior design for a recording studio, and posters / fliers / postcards / advertisements for a festival group whose largest festival attracts over 10k people.

+ One of my recent design projects was featured extensively the local media outlets as well as on CNN and NPR, resulting in my providing lectures about the project process, interviews with the press, and winning multiple awards within our city.

Specialties: Digital Illustration, digital collage, layout, html & CSS web design, social marketing, search engine optimization, copywriting and editing, html / web design, general creative solutions, branding + marketing
