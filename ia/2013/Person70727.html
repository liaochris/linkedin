Julie Harpring's Summary

Julie has designed interactive experiences for organizations such as Orbitz Worldwide, eBookers, Hotel Club, CVS Caremark, Motorola Solutions, the University of Missouri, and Butterball.

Her work spans multiple stages in the interface design process for desktop, tablet and mobile:
- design / customer insights research
- early concepting and sketching
- high-fidelity prototype creation
- usability evaluation
- detailed functional spec documentation

Personal goal:
Strive everyday to create experiences that are as engaging and meaningful as they are useful and usable.

Julie Harpring's Publications

  • Not Enough Cooks in the Kitchen

    • ACM CSCW 2010
    • February 10, 2010
    Authors: Julie Harpring, Jason de Runa, Moe Rafiuddin, Miaoqi Zhu

    The “Not Enough Cooks in the Kitchen” prototype is a collaborative cooking application with video conference and library archiving features that has been designed to help novice cooks receive, record, and archive advice from non-collocated friends and loved ones.

    Through our design, we hope not only to provide a medium for experienced cooks to share cooking knowledge that will help the novice gain a valuable life skill, but also to help people who live apart stay in touch and create digital keepsakes of their experiences.
