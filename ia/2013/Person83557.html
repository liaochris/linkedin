David Turney's Summary

David is a guy who asks; why?

It's driven his success as a creative marketing, communications and business leader who delivers value, drives results and animates brands via insight, vision and a customer-centered mindset.

His intuition, energy and insight is underpinned by an MBA in Marketing, which equips him with highly-tuned discipline and knowledge on the key drivers of commercial growth, success and value creation.

David's passion for all things automotive runs deep. He's a car nut and developed a love of custom cars and the culture before he could even reach the pedals. This means that he gets the vital connection between, the importance of emotions, affinity and brand engagement in achieving commercial success.

Professionally though, it's marketing that puts the gas in David's motor; telling stories that make a difference. Realising the results of the application of his skills and experience to marketing and communications strategy across multiple channels that excites markets is what really makes him tick.

Specialising in brand, marketing and communications strategy, vision, creativity, leadership, motivation, customer experience design, channel leverage and execution, David's mark and the ties that bind is Invite, Entertain, Interact, Experience, Engage, Motivate.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Sports industry

Develop the strategic marketing campaign and motorsports business of Australian off-road racing and Dakar Rally competitor Bruce Garland.

Driving a non-factory prepared Isuzu D-MAX ute and competing against multi-million dollar factory teams, Bruce, co-driver Harry Suzuki and the team achieved 11th outright and 1st diesel pick-up in 2009.

My job was to build on the teams already strong profile to create and implement a strategic plan to achieve a marketable run at the 2011 Dakar, as well as to implement a commercial, business-focused framework

The Dakar goal was achieved and even though the team had to withdraw due to injury, my strategy ensure that these things were implemented:

+ Brand and campaign the team locally and globally,
+ Develop and implement the strategic marketing plan to engage fans and increase revenue sources using digital strategy as the lead,
+ Identify, manage and activate sponsorship and supply partner relationships,
+ create a commercially focused marketing organisation, creating and discovering business and commercial opportunities.

Smart Automotive is based in Australia and has client partnerships in Australia, USA, Canada, UAE, Saudi Arabia, China, South America and Europe.

My brief was to launch the company, building its brand and market position, implementing strategies that raise a dynamic brand profile, create superior customer experiences and reduce environmental impact, while maximising profit through new marketing and product development opportunities.

I developed a solid understanding of the marketing challenges facing SME's and the dynamics of international business.
