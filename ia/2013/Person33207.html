Deana Schott's Summary

I have been blessed to work in several industries and in various capacities in my lifetime. I have over 30 years of experience in the Real Estate Industry as a Realtor and in the Music Industry as a Music Minister, singer/songwriter and music teacher.

Since moving to Florida in 1985 I have worked as a realtor in Corporate Relocation, Property Management, Residential Sales and Investment Property purchases. Buyers and Investors are my niche. I enjoy working with buyers who are downsizing as well as young couples purchasing their first home as I have an education degree and a spirit of helpfulness in my nature. I own several condos personally and have had great financial success in renting them out not only for myself, but my clients as well.

I owned my own restaurant/bar for 5 years and managed my husband's law firm for 17 years so I have extensive experience as well in business management, planning, employee management, marketing, inventory control, and office management. Whatever it took to make the business successful!

After retiring from the ministry in 2005 I have been able to enjoy being involved in non-profits in the community and draw upon my past experiences to be a partner in their success. One of my strengths is identifying and empowering people to reach their potential.
