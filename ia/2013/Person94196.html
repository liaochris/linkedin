Mike Grumbles' Summary

I help people invest in real estate in Williamson County, Tennessee including Franklin, Brentwood, and Spring Hill. Whether buying as a first time home-owner, moving up or down, or buying property as an investment I can assist you in the buying and selling of your home. I specialize in golf course community homes for sale in Brentwood and Franklin, Tennessee.

I have been active in the Real Estate business since 2004 and have used my Mechanical Engineering global life experiences to provide World Class service to my clients in their real estate needs. I am a member of National Association of REALTORS, Williamson County Association of REALTORS and the Women’s Council of REALTORS. I served as the Web Chair Person for 2008 and 2009 for the Williamson County WCR. I am one-third of The Grumbles Team. Our team is customer oriented and we focus on providing quality service and implementing the best web strategies. The Grumbles Team consists of over 40 years of real estate experience. Three REALTORS for the price of one!

The Grumbles team has experience in mortgages, relocation, and first-time ownership. We have diverse backgrounds in other professional fields and utilize those experiences to best serve our clients.
