Anna Banana Kruchten's Summary

Phoenix real estate broker Anna "Banana" Kruchten is a true expert – and that affords her clients both peace of mind and the luxury of having fun while she guides them to success in reaching their real estate goals.

A professional full-time real estate broker since 1987 and she is also an ADRE Certified Real Estate Instructor She was chosen a "Master of Real Estate" by the National Association of REALTORS® and holds numerous designations. She's become a national leader in Internet marketing and she continues to study and improve her strategies.

Since the real estate market is in continual flux, and since no two markets are exactly alike, Anna is also diligent in remaining up to date with the trends and statistics affecting real estate markets in and around Phoenix.

At the core of Anna's business is a firm commitment to taking good care of her clients. Her service always begins with actively listening to their goals and desires, then goes on to doing whatever is necessary to turn those goals and desires into reality.

And, since the purchase or sale of a home is a major life event, Anna makes the process to be as smooth as possible. She wants her clients to enjoy reaching this milestone in their lives.Her goal is made easier by the fact that she possesses a knack for solving problems, inborn negotiating skills, a reputation in the real estate community that encourages cooperation, and an attitude that seeks the positive in every situation.

She enjoys hiking, traveling, and spending time with family, including her 3 grandkids. She and her husband share their home with an 18 year old cat named Kitty, and an adorable 6 year old rescue dog Barney a Bichon-Poo!

When you're looking for a Phoenix area real estate broker who will put your needs first, go above and beyond to help you reach your goals, and cause you to have fun along the way… call Anna "Banana" Kruchten.
