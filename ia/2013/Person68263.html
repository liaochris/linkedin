Jordan McAuley's Summary

"Jordan's ContactAnyCelebrity.com service will help you find any celebrity in the world."
- Timothy Ferriss, 'The 4-Hour Workweek' (Page 222)

Known as the ‘King of Celebrity Contacts,’ Jordan McAuley’s ContactAnyCelebrity.com service helps charities and nonprofits; authors and writers; journalists and the media; and entrepreneurs and small businesses get in touch with celebrities worldwide for promotional and publicity use.

McAuley has been featured by ABC News, Associated Press, Atlanta Business Chronicle, Atlanta Magazine, Atlanta Journal & Constitution, CNN, Entrepreneur Magazine, Fox News, Investor's Business Daily, Miami Herald, National Public Radio, New York Daily News, Publisher's Weekly, Sirius/XM Satellite Radio, Star Magazine, USA Today, Village Voice, Writer's Digest and more.

He is featured in several best-selling books including Timothy Ferris’ ‘The 4-Hour Workweek,’ Dan Kennedy’s ‘Marketing to the Affluent,’ Dan Poynter’s ‘Publishing Encyclopedia,’ John Kremer’s ’1001 Ways to Market Your Books’ and Robin Blakely’s ‘Get PR Therapy.’

McAuley is the author of the annual ‘Celebrity Black Book,’ ‘Secrets to Contacting Celebrities: 101 Ways to Reach the Rich and Famous‘ and ‘Celebrity Leverage: Insider Secrets to Getting Celebrity Endorsements, Instant Credibility & Star-Powered Publicity.’

Jordan is a member of the Public Relations Society of America, Association of Fundraising Professionals, GLAAD Media Circle, Independent Book Publishers Association, Information Marketing Association and Society of Professional Journalists.

He graduated with a double-major Bachelor of Science in Communication (Motion Picture Marketing and English Literature) from the University of Miami and lives in Atlanta, Georgia.

Specialties: Marketing,copywriting,product development,online services,writing,publishing
