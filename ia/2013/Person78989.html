Laurel Hoffmann's Publications

  • Design Room Techniques - First Edition

    • Contemporary Fashion Education, Inc.
    • January 1, 2010
    Authors: Laurel Hoffmann

    Design Room Techniques and its sample book present a fundamental understanding of design room procedures. With minimal equipment, a few inexpensive tools used in the industry, and the PDF of the author's master sample book on the included CD, this sewing textbook will enable you to produce more professional results, whether you are a fashion student, work in the industry, have an entrepreneurial business, or need these skills for personal use.

  • Design Room Techniques, Professional Edition

    • Contemporary Fashion Education, Inc.
    • June 2012
    Authors: Laurel Hoffmann

    Easy-to-apply techniques for creating mass-production garments - from design to production. Understand how sample garments are made; understand how and why various fashion designing procedures are used in the industry; overall concept of how fashion designing, patternmaking, and sample making are interrelated. When you have completed this book you will understand the concepts that underlie the technology used to design and manufacture apparel. Includes a glossary.

  • Drafting & Fitting Pants and Skirts

    • Contemporary Fashion Education, Inc.
    • 2003
    Authors: Laurel Hoffmann

    Classroom tested, step-by-step, fully diagrammed instructions show professional drafting and fitting techniques. Includes fill-in charts that speed the work and help to ensure success. Most diagrams throughout the book are real patterns reduced to diagram scale. All work can be done in the home with minimal equipment. Written and diagrammed by a technical designer/patternmaker. Successfully tested with industrial design room professionals, entrepreneurs, and home makers. One of a two book set. Also buy Sewing Pants and Skirts for a sweet deal of $145. Drafting & Fitting Pants and Skirts is designed to be used alone, and/or with its companion book, Sewing Pants and Skirts.

  • Sewing Pants and Skirts

    • Contemporary Fashion Education, Inc.
    • January 1, 2003
    Authors: Laurel Hoffmann

    Classroom tested step-by-step, fully diagrammed instructions show professional cutting and sewing techniques. Learn how skilled sample makers cut and sew high-end pants and skirts to produce beautiful clothing like that sold in NYC couture boutiques. Minimal equipment needed. All work can be done in the home. Includes diagrams for preparing any straight skirt and any basic pant pattern so they can be used with the cutting and sewing instructions in this book. Sewing Pants and Skirts is designed to be used alone, or with its companion book, Drafting & Fitting Pants and Skirts.
