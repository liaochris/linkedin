Betty Robinson's Summary

Email: betty@phillyhops.com

I am the owner of Philly Hops Ltd. a Performance Alliance Company. We specialize in leadership development and "experiential" team building (learning by doing). We work with businesses, organizations & corporations, creating and producing proprietary team programs. Our catalog ranges from high-energy, fun activities like "Fast Video Challenge", or "Anything Goes Treasure Hunt" to workshops on Communication Skills and Change Management.

Our history goes way back, over 13 years, when many people didn’t even understand the value of experiential team building. Our company has stood the test of time with a proven track record. But, when you do something with passion and dedication like we do, it lasts.

We have a large, diverse catalog of programs and were one of the first companies to create and develop the genre of cooking and culinary team building. That’s not by chance! In my past life I was also an innovator and pioneer in the restaurant field, working as a professional chef back at a time when women were way under-represented in the field.

Our leaders have backgrounds in training and development and in special event management. That gives us a unique blend of skills that make Philly Hops activities seamless, exciting and relevant, allowing teams to build camaraderie and bond while having fun learning team skills.

So, if you’re looking for fun, results oriented programs, book one of our activities and find out why our customers say they are “entertaining, engaging, and insightful” and able to “translate the fun part of activities to the business imperative”.

Call me today to discuss options at 215 426-5644.
