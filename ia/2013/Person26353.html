Colin Storm's Summary

Since 2007 Colin has specialized in residential real estate inside the beltway of Virginia and in Northwest DC. Colin is also EcoBroker Certified, and is a Certified Distressed Property Expert (CDPE). With eight years of management experience in 4 and 5 star hotels, and resort properties, Colin brings top-tier customer care to his client relationships. Colin also brings to bear cutting edge expertise in today’s online real estate landscape. He is an active member of DC’s Social Media community and is proud of the national recognition his blog has garnered. You can view Colin's blog at TheBeltwayLife.com, and his social media profiles at http://about.me/colinstorm. Colin and his wife live in Falls Church, Virginia with their two young girls. He is licensed in DC MD and VA.

Specialties: • Falls Church Real Estate
• Arlington Real Estate
• "Green" homes (EcoBroker® Certified)

Colin Storm's Experience

Real Estate industry

May 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Washington D.C. Metro Area

Colin brings a combination of high touch service, graphic design training, and the best of today's technology to give his clients confidence and a real advantage against their competition. By being easy to contact, and by leveraging mobile technology to gather information in real time Colin enables clients to make informed decisions quickly. From there he is ready to complete, sign, and send contracts out on the spot, getting a buyer's offer in front of the seller as soon as possible. For home sellers Colin focuses on making sure today's buyers find what they are looking for, and more, when they find his client's listing. Being found as a seller is not the problem in today's online real estate world, being interesting is! To accomplish this Colin makes use of professional staging, professional photography and videography, and a cohesive marketing plan where online media and print media work in unison.
