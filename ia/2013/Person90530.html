Michael Costuros' Summary

I am a business development coach and transformational retreat leader for high impact entrepreneurs.

As a business development coach I work with inspiring, high impact entrepreneurs who have hit a growth plateau and are wanting to increase their revenues by 50% in 6 months or less.

Working together we discover a new strategy that preserves the qualities of the business and lifestyle that they currently enjoy while scaling their business to the next level. As important, we identify and transform the deep psychological obstacles that have been holding them back from realizing their full human potential.

Through my Entrepreneurs Awakening mastermind programs I lead groups of entrepreneurs on an epic journey to Peru that awakens them to a deeper understanding of their life's true purpose, and provides them with the clarity to bring it to the world through their business.  

Specialties: Business Development, Collaborative Business Relationships, Co-founder Relationships, and removing those deep psychological blocks that have been your Achilles heal for more longer than you care to admit.

Michael Costuros' Experience

Founder / Director

Entreprenuer Awakening

2011 – Present (2 years)

Entrepreneurs Awakening

Entrepreneurs Awakening works with entrepreneurs who want to awaken to their true life purpose, live it through their business, and make the world a better place in the process.

EA Awakening Peru mastermind program
The Peru mastermind program designed to awaken the entrepreneur participants to a deeper understanding of their life's true purpose, and clarify how to bring it to the world through their business.  

The mastermind includes a two week retreat held in the high Peruvian Andes near Machu Picchu. Modeled after Joseph Campbell's concept of the hero's journey, the EA mastermind takes the participants on a deep dive into their unconscious mind to release hidden fears and transform self-limiting believes that have been holding them back in life and business.

Many participants have a full blown spiritual awakening that clarifies and enhances their life forever. In addition to the transformation work, we enjoy exploring Inca ruins, fantastic food, and the deep trust bond that is forged between those who share this life altering journey together.

Each participant emerges from this adventure with renewed inspiration and clarity on their life's true purpose, and how to bring it to the world through their business.  


EA Personal Transformation Coaching
In addition to the mastermind I provide one on one coaching to support the changes my clients want to implement in their lives and business. This work typically includes business development strategy combined with to deep personal change processes.

Founder | liveBooks, Inc.

liveBooks, Inc.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

January 2004 – June 2010 (6 years 6 months)

liveBooks.com is the leading provider of portfolio websites for top creative professionals such as professional photographers, architects and interior designers.

Founded in 2004, liveBooks rapidly grew to over 50 employees and 3MM in annual revenues in less than 3 years.

Beyond a great product and a great plan, I attribute liveBooks' rapid success directly to the quality of relationships between members of the founding team, and the "relationship first" culture we created as the company expanded to include offices in Eastern Europe, Orange County and San Francisco.

Today, liveBooks continues to enjoy steady growth and a high trust brand that is synonymous with integrity and service.

As founder, during my 6 years at liveBooks held most key leadership roles from CEO to VP of Marketing to Director of Culture. Today my primary contribution is as a member of the board.

President / Design Director

Kitchen NYC

1997 – 2001 (4 years) Greater New York City Area

New York City at the end of the millennium was a thriving global center for the fine art, fashion, and design communities.

As conceptual artist I regularly participated in collaborative group exhibitions in New York, San Francisco, and Europe during this period.

As a website designer, I worked on many sites and special projects for the design and fine art communities, as well as commercial projects for clients such as Random House Books, Consult Paul, Women Model Management, Wonderlust Industries, and VH1.com.

I am infinitely grateful that I had the opportunity to fully submerge myself in this environment as a freelance creative at this historical period of unbridled optimism and cash flow.

It was out of this experience that my vision for the software that became liveBooks was born.
