Jeremy Eslinger's Summary

I am a poet, a writer, and a visionary born in the city of Huntsville, AL. In business I am a master of many skills and a student of many more. I desire to be creative in any facet I can and strive for perfection regardless of what I put my hands to. I currently lead a team of people in a multimillion dollar department in a major retail store. I use sales strategies, collected data, and market research to formulate action plans that grow the business and bring financial success to the company. I am highly organized and utilize time management to ensure that I get done what needs to get done in an efficient manner. I have an intense curiosity that pushes me to learn and develop myself as much as I can.

With a strong musical background, I find I'm most inspired and driven when I'm working with audio. Be it from a technical standpoint, a performance standpoint, or a business standpoint music dominates the fabric of my life. I have produced and orchestrated pieces for independent gaming projects as well as commercials and other advertisements. I have performed with artists of all genres and have also been a driving factor behind the business aspect of their music. I'm adaptable and always looking for ways to improve the processes, techniques, and abilities I currently possess.
