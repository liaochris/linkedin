Marc Blumer's Summary

As Marketing Director of Jackbox Games, I work to acquire, retain and monetize customers for our social and mobile apps on iOS, Android and Facebook.

I also serve as a Principal, Senior Consultant for Essential App Marketing providing strategic counsel for entrepreneurs and developers in all aspects of mobile app marketing.

And since I'm in the industry of driving home value propositions, here's mine:

***A proven, experienced marketing hybrid***
Seven years as a b-to-c and b-to-b corporate marketing leader. Five years as leading agency’s marketing strategist. Six years as award-winning agency creative director/copywriter. M.S. in direct and interactive marketing. B.S. in print journalism.

***A passion for ambiguity***
Built three marketing departments from scratch. Led numerous enterprise consulting engagements for sales/marketing integration, marketing automation pilots, merger communications strategy and more.

***A vision for integrating brand, direct and digital around content***
Founded agency’s content marketing practice. Developed principles and processes that seamlessly bridge inbound and outbound channels around efficient, customer-focused content engine. Led major content strategy engagements for Dow Corning Corporate, Dow Corning Construction and Nuveen Investments.

***A measurement mindset inside a creative brain***
Developed and institutionalized a multi-channel campaign forecast model for agency that sets and tests assumptions at every step of marketing funnel – aligning all analytics efforts into actionable data for measurable integrated marketing

Past clients include: Google AdWords, International Truck & Engine, Nuveen Investments, Tellabs, eBay Business, Underwriters Laboratories, Research in Motion (Blackberry), PayPal Merchant Services, Harris Bank, Labelexpo North America, U.S. Department of the Treasury, InfoComm Electronic Prepress & Print, Business.com, PayCycle.com, Dean Foods, Anexsys, Beverage Marketing and Corporate Express.

Specialties

Marketing strategy; marketing consulting; app marketing strategy for iOS and Android; content marketing strategy; market segmentation and message development; direct response strategy; digital marketing strategy; casual games marketing; lead generation strategy and marketing/sales integration; multi-channel creative direction and copywriting.
