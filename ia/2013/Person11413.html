Michal Bachman's Summary

I am a Principal Consultant at GraphAware, where I help companies of all sizes succeed with Neo4j, the world's best graph database. I also work on open-source extensions to Neo4j, focusing on large-scale graph analytics and domain-specific add-ons.

Working mostly with Java and related technologies, I am a certified Spring Framework trainer and co-author of Programming Spring (O'Reilly, est. Oct 2013).

I write clean, tested, and documented code that creates value. Occasionally, I blog and speak at conferences.
