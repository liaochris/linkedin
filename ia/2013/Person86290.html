Mark Matos' Summary

Mark Matos is Managing Director, Principal, and Chief Investment Officer for Global Wealth Consultants, LLC and is responsible for leading the firm, presiding over the organizations day-to-day, month-to-month, and year-to-year operations. Mark's responsibilities include ongoing management of client relationships, and assessing the needs of all new prospective clients as well as coordinating all of their financial objectives for further review and analysis. He is also responsible for investments, recommendations, implementation, hiring, marketing, expansion, and general oversight of the firm. His expertise is in investment portfolio analysis and management. He has a special concentration in creating and managing portfolios for ultra-net-worth-individuals.

Prior to joining Global Wealth Consultants in 2009, Mark was a financial advisor and portfolio manager to the high net worth at Fifth Third Private Bank in Naples, Florida.

Mark has lived in Southwest Florida since 1999. He earned a full-tuition academic scholarship through Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society to Florida Gulf Coast University. He double majored in Finance & Computer Information Systems. At FGCU, Mark earned admission to the Beta Gamma Sigma Honor Society. Mark graduated from Florida Gulf Coast University Magna Cum Laude. He is active in the Rotary Club of Naples.

Mark resides in Bonita Springs, Florida with his wife, Annika Matos.
