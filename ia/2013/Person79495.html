John Wolf's Experience

Owner and Head Coach

Wolf Fitness Systems

July 2007 – Present (6 years 6 months) Salinas, California Area

Wolf Fitness Systems provides comprehensive health-first fitness and performance training to residents and professionals in the Monterey Bay and Salinas Valley.

Every program we offer is focused on a 'health-first' approach to enhancing quality of life and getting optimal results. This is achieved by utilizing the Circular Strength Training System and committing our energy towards honoring the hierarchy of values of this system:

- Health
- Mobility
- Function
- Attributes
- Physique

We feel that no single fitness goal is worth sacrificing your long-term health, that reclaiming pain free movement is more important than being able to increase work capacity, that having the ability to perform the tasks of everyday life confidently is more important than increasing your numbers on any single lift or flexibility test and that creating your optimal physique will be the result exploring this process.

No matter which of our services you invest in this philosophy is observed by all of our coaches. We offer Customized Personal Training Services, Corporate Wellness Programs and our popular group coaching program commonly referred to as our Wolf Fitness Boot Camp Program.

John Wolf's Projects

  • Evolution Kettlebell Groundwork

    • June 2011 to Present
    Team Members: John Wolf

    Evolution Kettlebell Groundwork (EKG) is a revolutionary system of kettlebell training focusing on developing progressive multi-directional movement skill under load.

    The system was designed to compliment the training of conventional kettlebell training in that the movement skills are typically not overlapping. This allows the practitioner of EKG that ability to develop attributes that have not been worked towards in the past as well as offering the ability to avoid overtraining injury.
