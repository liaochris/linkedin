Leigh Anne Aston's Summary

Thank you for your interest in my profile. After a few years in the world of teaching, I took my degree in education to the business world, where I put my extraordinary ability to learn and teach to work in a corporate environment. After many years working in a cubicle, I decided I wanted more for myself and took my skills to the next level by starting my own business, Aston Administrative Services.

A highly successful administrative professional, I have excellent oral and written communication skills, strong organizational skills and am able to prioritize tasks and see projects to their completion. I have an exceptional proofreading ability and digital transcription skills and am knowledgeable in a variety of software applications including Photoshop, QuarkXPress and Adobe Acrobat, as well as Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Publisher.

The thing that most excites me in dealing with my clients is my ability to assist them in bettering their products. Sometimes this is through desktop publishing, sometimes it’s through proofreading or some other skill set. Whatever the task, it always involves listening to my client and delivering more than they are expecting. This is what gets me up in the morning and positively charged for my day.

Specialties

Virtual Assistant Services, Desktop Publishing (newsletters, brochures, flyers, postcards, programs, etc.), PowerPoint Presentations, Proofreading, Digital Transcription, Word Processing, Spreadsheets, Internet Research, Website Maintenance
