Jonathan S. Anderson's Summary

I work in MITRE's Federally Funded Research and Development Center (FFRDC) for National Security Engineering. I have dedicated more than a decade to working on tough technical challenges and programs of national importance in MITRE's C2C (Command and Control Center) and more recently in CAASD (Center for Advanced Aviation System Development).

My primary technical focus is distributed, real-time, and reliable software systems, and I have explored this technical focus in a variety of defense command and control systems; in airborne integrated ISR and C2 platforms; and in civilian aviation.

Work for MITRE aligns closely with my commitment to working in the public interest and determination to choose work with broad and lasting impact.

Specialties

Experienced programmer in C++ and (Real-Time) Java, Teaching, Mentoring, Scheduling, Resource Management, Real-Time, Embedded Systems, Real-Time Transactional Memory
