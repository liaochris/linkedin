Ian Andersen's Summary

At Selectica I connect our products, solutions, and brand with the right people, putting programs in place to give each of those people what they need, which ultimately builds sales pipeline.

For me, marketing is where business meets creative. And that's crazy, because it means in a single day I get to assist with some website design, build a pipeline plan, get on some calls with our BDRs, and optimize our landing pages. I love the combination of tactical execution, creative work, management, and business planning.

My background in web development/design and some computer science has given me the ability to relate to the technical side of marketing. This, combined with my unwavering desire to simply jump in and get things done, has led me to becoming extremely passionate about the tools and data that run marketing.

Current focuses & specialties:

Demand generation - I put in place successful programs that grow sales and marketing pipeline

Website design & optimization - I focus on the fundamental user experience and conversion pathways to grow pipeline, while creating amazing looking websites

Marketing operations - I connect all the marketing tools together to make sure I know where every lead is at all times

Analytics & planning - I can build some mean dashboards, but also connect all the dots to put realistic marketing plans in place

Business development - I train and build stellar BDRs to ensure that the pipeline I'm generating is being passed off to sales happy and qualified
