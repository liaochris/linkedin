Al Cantor's Summary

I'm a consultant with three decades of experience as a nonprofit CEO and development professional.

I have two sets of clients:

First, I work nationally with community-based nonprofits to help them reach their potential. Areas of work include strategic planning, all phases of development (assessments, campaign feasibility studies, campaign counsel, planned giving), executive coaching, training for boards and staff, and cultural assessments for organizations contemplating collaboration. I work with nonprofits in many fields, but a significant number of my clients are Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFIs).

Second, I work as a philanthropic advisor to families of means.My clients are determined to make charitable giving a centerpiece of their financial planning, not an afterthought. My family clients are focused on the impact, innovation, and effectiveness of their philanthropy.

Specialties

Strategic planning, development assessments, philanthropic advising, feasibility studies, campaign counsel, staff and board training, planned giving services, executive coaching, and support for Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFIs).
