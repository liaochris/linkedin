Jack C. Simsek's Summary

Hello there and thank you for stopping by. I know you are overwhelmed by searching, researching, searching, and so on.. I will try to keep it short, so here I go:

I have been a full time wedding photographer for over nine years and have successfully documented over hundred weddings. I have been a fashion photographer for over twelve years and covered many runway shows, published local magazines. Currently, producing custom calendars for local businesses along with other assignments; corporate events, family portraits, birthdays.

My style of photography has evolved over the years with the combination of creativity, experience and education. Natural-Photojournalistic and candid with a flair for fine art is my basic approach to photography. I enjoy capturing the essence of a moment in all it’s color and splendor. Since my childhood, I loved photography. In my childhood, my father sparked my interest by always having his camera with us. Photography for me throughout the years started as a hobby, grew into a passion, and finally became my career. I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from North Carolina State University. My studies heavily concentrated on multimedia; motion picture and light.

I love capturing the moment, with self input, within the environment; can be happiness, sadness, silence, love or just still life. I love people; that makes me very easy going, relaxed and happy. For me photography is fun, not work.

Please drop me a line with any questions, I will strive my best! Cheers, Jack C. Simsek
