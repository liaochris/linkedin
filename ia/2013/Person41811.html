Eran Eyal's Experience

CEO, Founder

Springleap

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

October 2008 – Present (5 years 3 months) Halifax, Canada Area

Design Democracy meets Marketing Muscle

Springleap is a white-labeled, embeddable social marketing design/ creative campaign platform for brands and agencies. Think of us as BuddyMedia meets a high end agency level 99designs that you can run in your own space with your look and feel - our community talent delivered into your space for you to engage your fans with.

At the end of your campaign, we can also provide exciting products and merchandise sold in branded micro-shops in our marketplace.

Each contest generates agency-level workmanship from thousands of designers competing to solve the brand/agency brief ranging from packaging, point-of-sale, logos, banners, billboards, apparel, art, merchandise , apps & icons and more.

When designers enter, they are incentives to engage their own community whilst the brand and Springleap each engage their own fan base to create meaningful engagement and consumer testing on the submissions.

Additionally Springleap handles promotional and corporate printing for many companies in who seek a top-end retail quality product for their promotions and internal corporate wear at extremely competitive rates.

Springleap clients include: Google, Nokia, Mxit, Ogilvy, AMD, (Stimorol) Kraft Foods, (Marmite) Pioneer Foods, Pick 'n Pay, Hansa (SABMiller), Lexus and many more.
