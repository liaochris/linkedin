Dustin DeKoekkoek, P.E.'s Summary

I love helping people and I measure my success as a consultant by the success of my clients. Every one of my projects, no matter how small, fits in to the bigger picture of improving the lives of people by building, maintaining, and servicing the communities that they call home.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Dustin DeKoekkoek has over 7 years experience designing a wide variety of projects for his municipal and private clients.

Dustin has experience in utility design, hydraulic modeling for water, sewer, and stormwater systems, road and site design, irrigation systems, development review and construction management.

As Project Engineer at RH2, Dustin manages the design and construction of engineering projects as well as provides on-call engineering services to his municipal clients.

Dustin is an effective communicator, in person and in writing.

Dustin’s knowledge, experience, and effective communication enable him to navigate challenges, resolve conflicts, and efficiently get the job done.

Dustin is proficient in AutCAD, AutoCAD Civil 3D, HydroCAD, WWHM, KCRTS, KCBW, WaterCAD/WaterGEMS, Microsoft Office products, Google Earth Pro, Trimble Sketchup, and has some knowledge of HTML, PHP, CSS, and other programming languages.

Dustin DeKoekkoek, P.E.'s Projects

  • Irrigation Lateral Enclosure

    • 2009 to 2011
    Team Members: Dustin DeKoekkoek, P.E.

    This project for the Sunnyside Valley Irrigation District included the design of 50+ miles of pipeline, converting open channel ditches to enclosed pressurized mains. RH2 assisted in facilities planning, design, bidding and construction inspection on a very condensed timeline.

    The pipeline provides local land owners with a new pressurized system that is completely metered from source to distribution and conserves water lost to evaporation, seepage and open channel inefficiencies.

  • Cashmere Wastewater Treatment Facility

    • 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Dustin DeKoekkoek, P.E.

    Part of a design team that designed a new 1.1 million gallons per day wastewater treatment facility for the City of Cashmere.

    The design included a new headworks with screening and a vortex grit removal unit and classifier; equalization and activated sludge basins; 40-foot circular clarifiers; open channel UV disinfection; a dissolved air flotation unit for thickening; an aerobic digester; and a belt filter press for sludge handling.
