David Booth's Summary

David first worked in the software industry in the year 2000. Since then he has held marketing coordinator, manager, and consultant positions, until rising to the challenge to lead ZeroTurnaround as CEO in January of 2009. Over the next 3 years, David:
* Grew ZeroTurnaround from $100k in total revenue (4 employees) to >$2.5M annually and 45+ employees
* 198% avg annual Y/Y revenue growth over each of the last 3 years
* Closed a round of angel investment and a multi-million USD VC round which brought shareholder liquidity

Specialties: startups, high-growth, building repeatable and scalable business models, hiring, leading and inspiring teams, strategic marketing planning, lead generation, community building, website analytics, word of mouth campaigns, PR, brand management, event management, internet marketing, business development, leadership, sales strategy, product development, fiscal management, M&A experience, raising capital
