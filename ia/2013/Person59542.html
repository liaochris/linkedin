Jason Thoele's Experience

Broker

Tholco Real Estate Group, Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Real Estate industry

February 2006 – Present (7 years 11 months)

I am responsible for the sales and marketing of homes in Kern County and Ventura County. I started my real estate career in Bakersfield with no family ties or sphere of influence and have built a very successful real estate business from the ground up. My duties as a Realtor include but are not limited to; contract negotiation, cold-calling clients, telemarketing as needed, identifying client needs and purchase criteria's, selling homes, escrow processing and paperwork and all facets associated with lending and financing of homes for property purchases. My personal staff of 11 and I are consistently ranked number one in sales month after month and currently I am ranked #1 for year to date home sales with $21m in sales for the year which is 123% of my 2009 target goal. In addition to my sales experience in the real estate industry, I have also been an active real estate investor for the past 7 years. Over the period of the past 7 years, I have acquired, managed and sold over 20 of my 34 properties in the past 5 years.

Sales Manager

AT&T Wireless

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; T; Telecommunications industry

June 1999 – June 2005 (6 years 1 month)

As the Sales Manager, I was in charge of six sales reps and one Assistant Manager. My responsibilities included but were not limited to hiring, developing and coaching successful customer service and sales people, accounting paperwork, merchandising, inventory control to include monthly audits, monthly reports to the District Sales Manager and the development of marketing strategies. I was fortunate enough to accomplish the exclusive status of West Coast Sales Manager of the month twice in the year 2000 for the Pacific Region; this was a direct result of the successful development and constant motivation of a powerful sales team that continued to produce high-ranking results. Some of the reports that I was responsible for were as follows, monthly inventory reports, monthly commission reports and reconciliation, monthly executive summary reports to the District Sales Manager and year-end Operational Review Reports.

Manager of Sales & Marketing

VIA Wireless

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; T; Telecommunications industry

April 1998 – August 1999 (1 year 5 months)

Hired as the Sales & Marketing Manager, I was responsible for a team of three salespeople. My daily responsibilities were inventory control, tracking of deposits and payments made to accounts, bank deposits and opening and closing procedures. I was responsible for monthly reports to the Director of Sales and Marketing which included marketing strategies, commission reports on sales people and profit and loss for the month. Hiring and firing was also part of my job. All this while maintaining a sales quota and keeping my sales team motivated and enthusiastic is what I did best.
