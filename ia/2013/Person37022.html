Dorothea Salo's Summary

Library educator, trainer and consultant skilled in scholarly communication, copyright, digital preservation, and research-data management. Markup designer and implementer with experience in scholarly print and electronic publishing. Library technologist with DSpace and Fedora experience as well as Python, Java, and SQL knowledge.

I am an engaging and energetic speaker on scholarly communication, digital preservation, data curation, and social software. Also a data werewolf!
