Matteo Stanzani's Summary

Chief Creative Officer with 15+ years of experience designing and producing interactive cross-media Entertainment, Advertising and Edutainment content/platforms and Transmedia eco-systems, leading in-house and outsourced creative and technical teams, providing effective, high quality and targeted design solutions, on-time and within budget, with natural problem-solving and managing risks aptitude.
Specializing in creating amazing new worlds for kids' imaginations to explore, I've entertained millions of young people by creating video games and cartoons for Magic-Kinder.com worldwide online community, which boasts 30-million+ users, as well as leading the creative teams that developed Disney Cuties online games and the Tv cartoon series Matt's Monsters.

Member of 2013 Digital Emmy Awards Jury.

ASIFA Italia, International Animated Film Association

ASIFA Association is founded in the firm belief that the art of animation can be enriched and greatly developed through close international co-operation and the free exchange of ideas, experience and information between all who are concerned with animation.

The art of animation is the creation of moving images through the manipulation of all varieties of techniques apart from live action methods.
This independent art, whatever its manner of expression - in theatres, on television, in education or children's film etc. - should make an extensive and important contribution to the world's cultural heritage, while playing its part in the search for new ways of artistic expression. It should help to promote progress towards peace and mutual understanding between all people.
