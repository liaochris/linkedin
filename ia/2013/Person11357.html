Stephanie Healy's Summary

Locally-owned, Aztec Ad Services specializes in marketing and branding businesses in the Southern Rhode Island and Southeastern Connecticut regions. We service independent contractors and businesses large and small, helping them get to the next level of success through a grass-roots campaign utilizing creative, low-cost and free market methods.

There is no such thing as over-exposure, and getting your business into the public spotlight doesn't have to cost an arm and a leg. In fact, many times, it's doesn't have to cost a dime. There are many, ways to grow your business online and throughout the community through grass-roots advertising.

Specialties: I love to see businesses jump from the traditional box of print advertising. I specialize in Social Media Marketing, helping business owners understand the direct relationship between online technology and their growing business. I deliver online strategies that will expose and brand the business into the minds of their potential clientele, while helping businesses reach their ranking potential.
