Cheryl Campbell's Summary

Founder/Owner Gates2U - 2004 - present: A certified Maryland manufacturer, A+ rating (BBB). We have shipped thousands of gates nationwide and to Canada. Serving the needs of parents, pet owners, commercial breeders, hospitals, and elder care facilities across the USA.

Co-Founder/Pres. IT GlobalSecure, Inc. 2000-present - IT security services. Currently focused on expert witness work for the computer games industry. Cheryl handles all financial and contractual oversight and negotiations.

Founding partner of assorted LLCs, S-Corps, and C-Corps (2001-present). Cheryl provided expertise in order fulfillment, fraud prevention, web marketing, SEO, ecommerce, CRM, catalog management, and other aspects of the retail and wholesale fulfillment cycle.

Founder of Hebell LLC (formerly Urban Revivals of DC), 2003-present, now in Greensboro MD. Ms. Campbell's work in restoring old homes has been featured in the Washington Times, Washington Post, Old House Journal, PaintPro Magazine. Specialties include decorative historic ceilings and restoration of antique woodwork.

Defense/Intelligence experience (1985-2001): Former Div Manager for TRW (Advanced Surveillance Systems), Engineering Manager (DoD classified programs), Chief Scientist for FAA Free Flight Program, Internet Modernization Manager for the U.S. Treasury, and Marketing Manager for Telecommunications Services for TRW for TCS (U.S. Treasury Communications System). Regularly managed teams of 40 engineers, up to 100, with line responsibility for sales, project development, overhead controls, procurement, and hiring. Seven years experience (1985-1992) in signals collection and signals analysis SIGINT as part of ESL Inc and TRW, at Fort Meade. Pioneered techniques in cross platform collection and geolocation, w/ emphasis on dense signal separation and identification.

Specialties

Contracting, JIT manufacturing, E-commerce systems, AB testing and web analytics, online and print marketing, SEO, RDFa and microformats, Quickbooks, payroll systems, PHP programming, SugarCRM, back office apps integration, shopping carts (Paypal, Yahoo Stores, Zen Cart, osCommerce, QuickCart, Magento)

National expert in wood restoration, home historic restoration, with contributions to numerous newspapers & popular journals.

Master carpenter, and designer for high-end hardwood gates.
