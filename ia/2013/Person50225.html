Greg Marcus' Summary

WRITING
My writing focuses on overworked employees, managers, and executives in the corporate world, who are unhappy but feel stuck in their current role. I have interviewed over thirty executives from all levels, and found one common denominator: excess devotion to the company comes at a high price to personal health and happiness. But it doesn't have to be that way. Remembering to put people first is the secret to a happier and more balanced life. Visit me at http://idolbuster.com

THE COSTS OF CORPORATE IDOLATRY
I believe that healthy company loyalty turns into corporate idolatry when the company becomes the most important thing in your life. This is ultimately bad for business, because it leads to chronic overwork, which brings a host of stress-related and creativity-busting consequences.

I am developing a training product that is win-win for the company to help develop engaged employees for the long term by allowing them a healthy life outside of work.

Life coaching for individuals is also available.
For more information, visit my web site: http://idolbuster.com

GENOMICS BUSINESS CONSULTANT
My business specialty is bringing new genomics technology to market as robust and rapidly adopted products. The key is understanding the customer, and getting them everything they need in order to be successful. Pricing and promotion flow naturally from having the right product for the right customer.

I AM AN OPEN NETWORKER AND WOULD LOVE TO CONNECT
