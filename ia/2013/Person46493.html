Tod Gilpin's Summary

Tod Gilpin is committed to providing the highest level of design, quality, and creativity in every project. He respects the term "return on investment".

EVERYTHING IS POSSIBLE.
Tod Gilpin is a creative thinker who welcomes every opportunity to expand his abilities. He constantly challenges perceptions, questions the normal, experiments, and researches all possibilities.

NEVER MEET A STRANGER.
By working with an ever-growing network of experts, tools, and vendors, He delivers quality and satisfaction on time and on budget.

TAKING YOU TO THE NEXT LEVEL.
Tod Gilpin can identify problem areas in your company's image or product and help you develop effective solutions to rejuvenate sales and positively affect your bottom-line.

Specialties: Tod has the ability to take any visual aspect of a company's image and turn it into a working usable tool for sales or branding. If you need creativity, that is what you will get.

Tod believes that internal marketing can be more powerful that exterior marketing. He has the uncanny ability to get team members excited about change through vision.
