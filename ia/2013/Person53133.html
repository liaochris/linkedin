Michael Denigan bigmiked@insightbb.com's Summary

Hillcrest Home Solutions is owned and operated by the Denigan family, headed by Mike, Sr. and his wife, Ella Mae. They have lived in the Florence, KY area since the 1960’s. Mike has built over 400 homes since 1985. He has also worked as a general contractor, building consultant and as a lease-purchase agent. His son, Josh, has followed in his footsteps and builds homes in the area, as well.

The Denigan family represents core values, commitment to quality, and a solid foundation. They have earned trust within the Northern Kentucky community and have an impecable reputation throughout the area.
