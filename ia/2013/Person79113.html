Lauren Peters' Summary

I’m fascinated with the potential of how design can influence our future, bringing people closer together and more connected than we ever imagined. I crave knowledge and I’m most inspired by the stories of our users and what they can offer to every design challenge.

I incorporate into each of my projects a service design thinking and user experience lens which has been influenced by my background researching and designing for product forms, environments, and user interfaces. I have experience in both physical and digital platforms and approach each of my projects with the means to fulfill user/customer goals, organizational goals, and service feasibility.
