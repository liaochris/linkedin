Richard Proffitt's Summary

Since graduating from the University of the West of England in 1996 I have been fortunate enough to put my creative skills to the test with a variety of art and design related positions and placements. I enjoy working with a mixture of people and work at my best within a small or middle-sized team.

I have very good artistic skills and a good handling of the Adobe Creative Suite. The years I spent as a website designer for the Council of Disabled People really challenged me with a variety of design jobs, both for web publication and for print.

I am a quiet enabler – meaning that I like to bring out the best in the people around me without being too aggressive. I enjoy a friendly working environment, and value other people’s ideas, visions and artwork. Also, I am a keen learner and eager to study and develop my practice.
