Chris Arkenberg's Summary

I'm basically a creative type with a huge interest in how tech and people and nature interact. My primary role is in extending the sensing capacity of organizations and helping them successfully navigate change.

Over 13 years of experience shipping software to global audiences and consulting in product innovation & strategy, forecast, & competitive intelligence for web enterprise, start-ups, and government stakeholders. Strong business knowledge & technical background in enterprise workflows, mobile apps, social networking, platform services, 3D CAD & DCC, and collaboration. Highly recommended performance in managing seed-to-launch programs, defining & executing on product requirements, and ensuring high-quality user experiences. Strong team player with excellent diplomacy & communication skills. Highly organized, efficient, and time-sensitive.

The stuff I really dig: evaluating low-level details and high-level inter-dependencies; domain research & analysis, forecasting & scenario planning; usability, user advocacy, and human-centered solutions; the convergence of computation and the built environment. My interests tend towards adaptive innovation, ubiquitous computation, urban systems, and emergent behaviors.

I also write music for abandoned skyscrapers, dabble in science fiction, and publish foresight and analysis through various outlets. I've written for Fast Company, Boing Boing, Big Think, Shareable, PSFK, and others.
