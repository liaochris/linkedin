Charles Young's Summary

With over 15 years experience in the IT industry, Charles' career has seen the industry come full circle from, mainframe/terminal servers, client/server computing returning to consolidated computing models which encapsulate new technologies such as Cloud and new working styles which are so popular currently. In his career he has worked in the commercial, government, private and public sector giving unique breadth and experience. His goal is to provide solutions and tools to improve both company and customer in an ever changing and exciting field.

Currently, Charles serves as the head of technology in a progressive school district in the Greater New York City area. He is helping with district growth and integration of new and exciting technologies. He has significant experience in educational and informational technology leadership and a vision for the role of technology in the 21st century. He also has strong interpersonal skills, good sense of humor, and great communication skills.
