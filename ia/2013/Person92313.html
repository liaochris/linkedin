Michael Edelson's Summary

Mr. Edelson is a successful entrepreneur with more than 25 years of experience in e-commerce, software and credit card and risk management systems. The holder of two U.S. patents, Mr. Edelson has pioneered a number of algorithms central to e-commerce applications used today by many e-commerce and pay-per-call companies. Mr. Edelson has a solid track record of successfully founding and operating technology companies from inception to profitable business operations. Currently as chief technology officer of ACINET, Inc. his responsibility is to align ACINET, internally and externally, with future strategic visions as set forth by the CEO and President. His core duty is to facilitate business outside of ACINET while guiding employees and other executive officers towards ACINET's objectives.

Before joining ACINET in 2011, Mr. Edelson served as chief executive officer at Network Telephone Services, Inc. in 2009. Prior Mr. Edelson co-founded Stealth Ideas Inc., a privacy software company which develops software and devices that allow consumers to surf the Web in anonymity in an effort to prevent identity theft where he conceptualized, designed, and developed the StealthSurfer, which was a modified USB device that enabled consumers to surf the Internet portably from any computer and leave no traces. He also co-founded and test-marketed BeNetSafe, a start-up company that provided a unique service to ensure the safety of children using Internet social networking sites. Before co-founding Stealth Ideas Inc. in 2004, Mr. Edelson served as chief technology officer and vice president of engineering at Network Telephone Services, Inc. where he developed credit card, private billing, risk management and fraud control systems. He also has served as chief technology officer at R.J. Gordon Company and as president of Electronic Authorization Systems, where he developed credit card processing systems for audio text and Internet applications.
