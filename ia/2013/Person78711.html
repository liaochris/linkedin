Laura Ballou's Summary

My screenplay, Twilight's Pardon, a western drama set in Texas, placed as a Second Round Finalist at the 2008 Austin Film Festival screenplay competition.

Children of Duncan, a historical drama set in 1066 Scotland, is part of the reading/reference library at the Clan Donnachaidh Centre in Perthshire Scotland.

Anna, a World War II drama, placed as a first round winner in the 20/20 screenwriting contest.

Just completed another screenplay, Brother's Keeper, a drama set in small town America.
