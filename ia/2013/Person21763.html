Chad Trent's Summary

I have been a graphic designer for since 1989. I started with rapidograph pens and wax pasteups in the late 80s and moved to digital shortly thereafter. In that time I have been fortunate to work with a diverse range of clients and to meet many memorable and interesting people. I have only worked in the design field, and hope that I can continue to say that for many years to come.

A goal of mine is to teach and pay forward some of the knowledge and experience I have gained in my two decades in the industry. I am currently a tutor for the Communication Design program classes at Virginia Western Community College.

Specialties: Graphic design, pre-press
