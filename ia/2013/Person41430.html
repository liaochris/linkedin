Emmelie De La Cruz's Summary

I am a graduate of the prestigious S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University with a degree in public relations. I have a focus and interest in the development of branding through strategic communications and digital marketing. Through my various professional experiences, I have learned to think strategically and creatively in a highly social and information-driven world. I gained an understanding of digital marketing and its importance in strategic planning as well as search engine optimization strategies and techniques. I have honed my communication, social media and project management skills and am using them to build stronger brands for organizations and individuals. I am passionate about content marketing, professional development, and higher education. My work experience spreads over various industries including nutrition, bioscience and medical technology, health care and fashion as well as B2B marketing.


For more information about me you can visit my website: EmmelieDeLaCruz.com or www.thebrandingmuse.com

Specialties: personal branding, content marketing, graphic design, social media management, HTML, email and content marketing, strategic planning, campaign development, proposal writing, public speaking, Google analytics, social media analytics, Wordpress, website design, blogging, SEO
