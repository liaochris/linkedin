Mike Osswald's Summary

I have been leading Strategic Business Analysis processes for over 15 years. My goal is to help businesses create the kind of relationships consumers desire, delivering the right messages at the right time, acknowledging their power in the buying process. My background encompasses over 20 years in marketing and management, primarily with manufacturing firms and their distribution channels, and retail and e-business clients.
