Jane Hirst's Summary

Welcome to my linkedin profile.

My experience and passion lie in people development. I love to help businesses create cultures where people really want to come to work and do their very best. This means focusing on everyone in the business, their attitudes and behaviours and how they manage and embrace change. I love to help people be the best they can be and whether that's through some form of feedback, workshops or coaching, we get to the crux of the matter, whilst having some fun along the way.

I don't have 'off the shelf' packages. I find out what you need first and then create materials to meet your specific requirements. This means that you get tailor made development to suit your organisation and achieve the results you really want. However, to give you an idea of the activities we supply have a look at my website or see the attached skills development directory below.

During the last thirty years I have worked in the public, private and third sectors. This has helped me build a breadth of knowledge in business best practice whilst boosting my skills to engage and gain commitment from people at all levels.

Specialties

Creating positive and healthy organisation cultures
Performance management, including 360s
Making A Difference with customer service
Facilitation and management of change at all levels
Creating communications that are understood first time
Being a great facilitator
