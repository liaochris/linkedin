Helen Orlando's Summary

I'm a lead user experience consultant with more than 15 years of experience in defining, designing and implementing intuitive user-centred design solutions for large scale websites, interactive television, handheld and desktop applications.

I enjoy working with disruptor start ups in an advisory capacity and thrive in an entrepreneurial environment. A senior leader who enjoys talking big business ideas but will then roll up my sleeves to dig into the details to get products delivered.

On a daily basis, I work alongside many talented & bright team members delivering usable interactive experiences. Over the years, I've had the pleasure of working on diverse projects covering many channels & brands.
