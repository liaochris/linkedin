jeffrey smith's Summary

My professional experience is in the field of editorial illustration where I have created many magazine, book, and poster illustrations for various clients over the last 30 years. Clients include Time magazine, Newsweek magazine, Forbes magazine, Sports Illustrated magazine, New York magazine, The New York Times, The New York Times magazine, Gentlemen's Quarterly magazine, Rolling Stone magazine, Scholastic Inc., Penguin Putnam Inc., BDD Random House, Reader's Digest London, Reader's Digest Canada, ESPN magazine, O the Oprah magazine, Wired magazine, Canadian Business magazine, Playboy magazine, Princeton Alumni Weekly magazine, etc.

Watercolor, acrylic ink, and mixed media paintings of Noir scenes, Murder scenes, Re-creation scenes, Sports scenes, Crime scenes, Portraits, and Fiction.
