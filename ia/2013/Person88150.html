Mary Lou Gutscher's Summary

Mary Lou Gutscher is a prospecting and marketing expert who has taught thousands of others how to generate more business, more profitably. Her articles have appeared in dozens of publications.

Entrepreneurs are her heroes! They create the wealth of innovative products and services that make our lives easier and richer and they are responsible for the progress we’ve seen in society over the centuries. Mary Lou is thrilled to be able to help them grow their businesses profitably through her company, Magnetic Business Solutions.

A serial entrepreneur herself, Mary Lou has built profitable businesses in retail, telemarketing and publishing, where she achieved annual sales of almost $2 million.

Mary Lou has coordinated and emceed many local, national and international events, and has been a featured speaker at dozens of Sales Conferences, at National Speakers Association Workshops and at the exclusive Million Dollar Round Table.

Famous for her workshops on How to Generate Quality Referrals, which are boons to anyone who has to sell something to make a living, Mary Lou also personally coaches and mentors owners and successors of small and medium-sized businesses.
