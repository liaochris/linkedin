Jeff Hardy's Summary

Jeff has worked in the creative and technology industry since 1994. With a strong background in graphic and interactive design, video editing and Internet technology, Jeff’s expertise has been honed through 18 years of experience designing and developing solutions for clients from all sectors. As an entrepreneur, he is a frequent guest speaker on Internet marketing and web design for varied audiences, and has successfully completed campaigns and media projects for a wide range of global corporate and SME clients. Jeff’s skills and talents are showcased by an uncommon balance between design and technology, demonstrated by his involvement in successful projects ranging from web design, e-commerce development, SEO/Digital Marketing and interactive design to full video production.

Jeff studied Advertising Art at Fanshawe College and earned a B.A. in Communication Studies from Brock University in the Media and Culture stream. He was co-recipient of the 1998 Niagara Entrepreneur of the Year Award, and has been a Professional Member of The Association of Registered Graphic Designers of Ontario since 1998. Not only is Jeff qualified by his peers as a specialist, he has also been officially recognized as an Internet Technology Expert by the Supreme Court of Canada (2007).

With an educational background in communication and design, web technology and audio/video, Jeff is a film, music and pop culture enthusiast.

Specialties

Advertising & marketing, web, graphic and interactive design, brand creative, video & motion pre-post production.
