Ian Gifford's Summary

Creative digital media and graphics designer, with over 10 years experience working across a wide variety of b2b, and b2c brands such as Carling, Nuby and Sage.

Specialising in responsive web design, I have a solid grounding in both on and offline design, including brand development, print design and art-working, art direction and multi channel campaign development.

I love what I do, and I'm driven by an appreciation of great design.

Ian Gifford's Experience

Head of creative

Orange Bus Ltd

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

May 2010 – Present (3 years 4 months)

As head of design, I oversee the visual and creative output for the agency across a number of key accounts including, Sage, Nexus and Pearson Education.

Key to my role is the strategic implementation of creative brand campaigns and product launches. Working closely with key senior members of the agency, I co-ordinate and execute multi-discipline campaigns across a wide mix of media and online applications.

Client liaison forms an important part of the role, from pitching and winning new business, to regular progress meetings, presentations and client workshops.


I take creative lead on each project, ensuring the best possible visual experience.

Firmly integrated within the core of the agency, I formed a pivotal role in projects from conception through to development and completion, which left me with a wide array of practical and project management skills.

Pitching for new business and face to face meetings with clients formed an important part of my position, liaising with the design team and developers to facilitate a smooth transition of work through the agency.
