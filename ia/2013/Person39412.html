Ed Hall's Summary

I’ve been doing graphic design work in the freelance and professional market for over 10 years. Apart from graphic design I’m also an illustrator, photographer and budding musician. My passion is for the arts and it flows through every fiber of my being. I bring a breath of fresh air to every project as well as fun, innovation and ingenuity.

KEY SKILLS
Corporate Identity: The importance of brands in all perspectives. Not only respecting the brand but having the ability to take it further into other mediums.

Print and Interactive: From digital to tangible, I have extensive knowledge of how to create designs for print and interactive designs on various levels.

Creative Direction & Teamwork: My understanding of the design process allows me to work on projects from concept to completion. Whether this is by myself, leading a team or working with others to achieve the final goal.

PHILOSOPHY
I have had the ability to work on projects with groups from all walks of life. Through this I have learned to fully devote myself to every project I work on. I’m willing to go the extra mile to make sure that your project is the best that it can be.

Times are tough and time is limited, that is why I’m willing to work with you not just for you to achieve the results you are looking for. Whether it’s fine tuning the design, achieving the right content, or working with a pricing that is affordable. I’m here for you.
