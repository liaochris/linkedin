Kristen Sussman's Summary

As President and Founder of Social Distillery, an Austin-based social media marketing agency, Kristen leads the company in overall growth and direction. As a self-described explorer, her team (jokingly) reminds her that without her, they'd "be on the streets." Kristen is fascinated by marketing strategy, communications and user-experience and has immersed herself in current technologies as they relate to social media marketing communication. She often speaks on the topic of social media and has recently co-authored a paper on social media strategies and measurement. Kristen received her B.A. in Journalism and Broadcast Radio/Television from St. Ambrose University in Davenport, Iowa, and her M.A. in Advertising from The University of Texas at Austin. Kristen recently received her Advanced Skill Community Management certification through WOMMA-U.

Specialties: Social Business Design, Social Media Marketing, Digital Advertising, Search Engine Optimization, Inbound Marketing, Content Marketing, Search Engine Marketing, Digital Public Relations & Word of Mouth Marketing
