Fernando Leite's Projects

  • Grêmio Hoje

    • September 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Fernando Leite

    Grêmio Hoje is a personal project about Grêmio, the football team that I support. Every day you see an important moment in the club history that happened in that day.

    The project had great repercussion in blogs, social networks and was mentioned two days in a row in one of the main radio talkshows in Brazil, Sala de Redação.

    "One of the most extraordinary things for a Grêmio supporter to navigate. Absolutely exciting." — Cacalo, Grêmio's ex-president

  • Tweet Roulette

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Fernando Leite, Mauricio Wolff

    The quickest way to draw winners from tweets. No sign up. No bureaucracy.

  • Já foi um Brother?

    • March 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Fernando Leite, Rafael Greiner

    The project's goal is to play with the characters from all the seasons of Big Brother Brazil, the reality TV show with the biggest audience in Brazil. The website shows a picture and the user has to answer whether the person has or not taken part in the program in the past.

    With this data we can measure who has been forgotten by the public and who is still alive in their minds.

    The website had over 20 thousand unique visitors in the first three days after launch and a huge repercussion in blogs, social networks and in the main brazilian web portals. Project concept by Eduardo Menezes and developed by Rafael Greiner and myself.
