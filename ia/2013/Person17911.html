Brien Moakley's Summary

Over the last 15years I have worked in the financial services industry as a website project/product manager, business analyst and business process consultant through two major corporate mergers. The projects have ranged in size and scope from small to very large.

Ideal work for me involves web/application projects that require an entrepreneurial approach, creativity, problem solving and business analysis to fulfill customer needs.

My background as an entrepreneur, website manager, project manager, business analyst/consultant, inventor and philanthropist makes for an interesting mix of skills and talent.
