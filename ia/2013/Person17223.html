►Organization Profile

The National REO Brokers Association (NRBA) was founded in 1998 by several REO brokers from around the United States, all of whom currently list and sell in excess of one hundred (100) REO properties each year (see NRBA Profiles).

The National REO Brokers Association (NRBA) is a "for-profit" organization. We believe in profits for everyone involved-especially our members.

The National REO Brokers Association (NRBA) is incorporated and licensed under the laws of Clark County in the State of Nevada.


►Our Goals

1. To provide the necessary support and tools to our member brokers, so as to make them both better qualified and more efficient in the marketing and selling of bank-owned properties.
2. To provide banks, servicers, outsourcing companies, and all those requiring the services of an REO Specialist™ an easy and efficient way to find qualified individuals throughout the United States.
3. To bridge the technology gap that currently exists in our industry and to create industry standards that will create new efficiencies and greater time & cost savings to all involved.


►NRBA Mission Statement

Our mission is to bring the best-qualified brokers together with the banks, servicers, and outsourcing companies that need them. By working together, we strive to create, develop, and maintain relationships that will last a lifetime.

The National REO Brokers Association is dedicated solely to the listing and selling of bank-owned properties.
