Lissa Rolenc, ASID's Summary

Award-winning interior designer creating interiors that influence lives. Through innovation and positive change has implemented a positive business model and design process for our clients. Have lead hundreds of simultaneous high-stakes projects by creating clear strategies for their successful conclusion. Thrives by making difficult but necessary decisions that promote marketability, positive long-lasting client relationships, and top-quality documents, for increased satisfaction. Proven ability to offer design expertise in more than 46 states nationally. I believe in unique design schemes and solutions that match your companies mission, values, and brand.

Specialties

Senior Living Design, Memory Care, Rehabilitation, Hospitality, and Condo/Residential Developments.
