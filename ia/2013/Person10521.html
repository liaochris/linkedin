Assurity Life Insurance Company's Summary

Assurity serves customers across the nation by offering disability income, critical illness, life, accident, specialty insurance plans, and annuities. The company’s products and services are distributed through its independent representatives and worksite distribution. With assets exceeding $2.4 billion and a mission of helping people through difficult times, Assurity has built very long legacy of financial strength and security. Assurity has earned a high rating of A- (Excellent) with A.M. Best Company, an independent insurance industry analyst. For more information about the range of A.M. Best’s ratings, visit www.ambest.com.

WORKSITE PRODUCTS: Accident Expense Insurance | Cancer Insurance | Critical Illness Insurance | Disability Income Insurance | Hospital Indemnity Insurance | Term Life Insurance | Whole Life Insurance | Universal Life Insurance | Section 125 Plans
