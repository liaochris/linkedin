Brock VandenBerg's Summary

TaliMar Financial is a real estate finance company that specializes in financing short to medium term residential and commercial real estate loans. The majority of loan we fund include residential fix & flips in which a borrower acquires a distressed asset, renovates the interior / exterior, and re-introduces that asset to the market. Additionally, we fund income property assets for borrowers seeking a quick close or immediate cash out.

For more information on TaliMar Financial, contact us at (888) 868-8467 or visit talimarfinancial.com
