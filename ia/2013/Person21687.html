Chad Austin's Summary

Chad Austin Marketing and Media Design became the official marketing company for the city of Hickory, NC, in 2012, for the purpose of re-branding the city under the newly-created motif "Life. Well Crafted."

Chad worked as a designer at several web design firms. He's a multimedia specialist handling everything from front-end to back-end design, Social Media, SEO, CMS, Flash™, HTML5, audio & video. Project management and leader on big projects (100+ pages and DB development), worked with Client & Sales one-on-one to bring projects to fruition.
