Jen Vondenbrink; Speaker and Coach's Summary

At Life Simplified, I take my experiences and training as an internal corporate coach and bring them to overwhelmed small and mid-sized business owners helping them regain control of their lives and their businesses.

For over 25 years, I've worked in the both the entrepreneurial and corporate worlds. My biggest lesson was burning myself out not once but twice. After that I became dedicated to help other retail professionals avoid what I'd been through.

What I offer clients is my real life experience, how I navigated the process of growth and expertise in training and development. These powerful skills combined with passion and empathy creates the unique Life Simplified experience.
