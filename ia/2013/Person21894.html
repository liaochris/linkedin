Boris Chan's Summary

Experienced in leading and growing one of the best mobile engineering teams to ship great mobile experiences for the world's most important companies. I work constantly with companies to instill foundations for great mobile design and development, especially in the consumer internet space.

Bio:

Boris Chan is a Director of Engineering at Xtreme Labs, leading their renowned and fast-growing mobile engineering team to ship great mobile experiences. His work with startups and large corporations has been featured in major publications like the Wall Street Journal, New York Times and TechCrunch. He is known as a respected expert in mobile development, innovative product development and design through working with the world's most important companies. Boris has an honours bachelor degree in software engineering from the University of Waterloo.
