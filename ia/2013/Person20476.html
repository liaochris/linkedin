Carol Sanford's Summary

I teach and coach Entrepreneurs how to grow their business, Responsibly. From a non-displaceable platform, they can then change the world to be more benefiical to all living beings. I do this through coaching and Education for Entrepreneurs, I also educate coaches of working with Responsible Entrepreneurial Businesses. Beyond Social Entrepreneurship to changing industries, society, beliefs and governing systems.

Specialties: Entrepreneurial Innovation and Grow, Living Systems Thinking; Regenerative Strategic Planning; Operationalizing Strategy; Work system redesign for effectiveness; Regenerative Leadership Development
