Anjela Mangrum's Summary

I am the Owner of a Professional Search Firm, Mangrum Career Solutions, LLC. I match top talent with desirable Manufacturing Companies nationwide. My firm was recently listed by Manufacturing Crossing as the #7 “Most Influential Recruitment Firm” out of over 6000 firms across America. Our proprietary PrecisionFX process alleviates wasted time, money, and hiring mistakes.

Over 80% of my placements come from Referrals, so I know the value of connections and an active network. Supportive relationships, partnerships and networks allow us to be a one-stop-shop for clients.

► Need help filling or finding a job? Call me - 513.753.3813

To help job seekers and those we don’t place, our website contains a vast number of job postings, career articles, and numerous tools.

► Learn More at mangrumcareersolutions.com

As an early adopter of LinkedIn, I have created tutorials, written articles and developed strategies to help expand your network.

► Invite me to Connect: anjela@mangrumcareersolutions.com

Prior to founding MCS, I worked as an Executive Recruiter for one of the top Sanford Rose Associates offices. Our office specialized in manufacturing so when the economic crisis of 2008 hit, it left me without a job. This experience led me to start my firm - to help others hurt in the recession.

I am an avid networker and community volunteer. I maintain my Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC) with the National Association of Personnel Services. I am an active member of the Ohio Recruiter Association and the NAPW. I also have industrial memberships with the SME and AME. I volunteer with my local Chapter of the Lions Club which has an emphasis on community service.

I thrive on the time spent with my husband and two daughters on a former alpaca farm on the east side of Cincinnati. I am an animal lover, an entrepreneur, a book and movie aficionado, and a “Jill of all Traits”. My life ambition is to try my hand at lots of new endeavors and strive to excel in just a few.
