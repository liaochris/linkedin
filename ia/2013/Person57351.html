Hey there, ladies and gentlemen of LinkedIn (who I'm not entirely convinced hold any position except recruitment) welcome to my profile—sit down, have a drink, and bask in my excellence...

So anyway, now that we're past the formalities, I'm James and I've been developing in Ruby on Rails since few people outside of IRC heard of it #RoRHipster.
