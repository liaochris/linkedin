Laurie Laizure's Summary

Founder of www.customizedwalls.com
Customized Walls is a new and exciting company. We print custom wallpaper and wall murals for the residential and commercial markets. Our peel and stick fabric is the best available on the market (I know because I tested them all). We have extensive color management skills and can match popular paint or pantone colors. We have thousands of licensed art, graphic designs, and photos available on our site. Our goal is to provide our clients with something fantastic and original for their home or business while having so much fun doing it! We offer Interior Design Professional discounts and will help you with your other marketing needs (Portfolio books, signage, tradeshow signs, etc.)

You can find me here:
Twitter: @CustomizedWalls
Facebook: /customizedwalls
Google Plus: https://plus.google.com/communities/105411019370258764323
Pinterest: CustomizedWalls
Blog: www.customizedwalls.com/blog, www.missmurals.com

Prior to owning Customized Walls, I had 15 years experience working with printer manufacturers, media and ink distributors, and as a successful consultant and entrepreneur in the wide format printing industry (specializing in fine art and soft signage). Testing, development of canvas, watercolor papers, ink, coating so they had the highest standard in quality, archival properties, and compatibility AND bringing them to market with all new collateral materials, sales and customer service (with a smile:). I have had years long fantastic relationships with almost all Printer, RIP, Color Management, Media, and Ink manufacturers and some of the real pioneers in distribution.

My habit of surrounding myself with people that are more talented, intelligent, creative than I am, has made all the difference in my career. Printing in the art and design business is the best job in the world and I am lucky to be in it.

Thank you for being part of my LinkedIn community and for being someone I can learn from and share with. :)
