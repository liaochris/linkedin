Christi Cano's Summary

Innovative Spa Productions provides spa, salon, and fitness consulting and development services to resorts, hotels, casinos, senior living communities, day spas, residential/real estate, development companies and architecture firms.

We collaborate on projects with other spa consulting firms, provide consulting for vendors, edit textbooks for publishing company Cengage Learning/Milady’s, source spa products for the Latin Grammy Awards and reach hundreds of spas and their consumers through www.GetSpaInfo.com, social media, blogs, and speaking engagements.

The Company was founded to provide consulting services, resources and education solidly based in first-hand spa & hotel development/openings, operations, management, vendor and technician experience.

Because development needs change over time, we are flexible throughout the process and always keep in mind what is in the best interest of the client. What this means is that we never sell, deliver or charge for work that is not needed. We are completely committed to our client’s success and work with the same drive and dedication for them as we do for our own business.

Experts who own their own companies will join the team when specific and detailed knowledge is required such as website design, architecture and interior design services, MEP requirements, marketing, and public relations. This team approach produces successful collaborative results.

Keeping client needs at the forefront of our business is the right approach. By always providing the best resources available, honest opinions and a high level of commitment to our clients, ethics and values, success is the result. Anything less for us just isn’t an option.

For more information, please visit us at www.GetSpaInfo.com

Christi Cano is the President & Founder of Innovative Spa Productions, a boutique company which provides spa consulting and development services. With 20 years in the hospitality and spa industries, she has extensive experience as a Consultant, Spa Director, Trainer, Product Vendor, and Massage Therapist.

Clients include spas/salons/fitness centers in hotels, resorts, casinos, residential developments, senior living communities and day spas.

Having worked at four & five-star spas and resorts both in the U.S. and Internationally, her “hands-on” philosophy and background provides an operational viewpoint that leads to sound decisions and advice for spa businesses.

She has been a member of the International Spa Association’s Education Committee and is a frequent speaker and published writer. She was chosen to contribute to the 2009 ISPA Conference Speaker Selection Task Force.
