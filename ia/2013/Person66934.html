John Rambow's Summary

I'm an experienced editor and writer with a decade's experience in digital media and growing online communities.
I've worked with and written for some of the biggest brands in travel and related topics, including Travel + Leisure, Budget Travel, Zagat, New York and BlackBook magazines, and Fox News.
I've also help launched several blogs, edited many, many travel guides for Fodor's, and helped copyedit the website of one of the largest law firms in the world.

• Content strategy and overall editorial development, particularly in the travel and the overall consumer categories.
• Launching and helping digital brands pivot and grow.
• Deep and nuanced knowledge of book production joined with a comprehensive understanding of modern-day content management systems and social media.
