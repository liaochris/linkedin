Benjamin Y. Seldin's Summary

Jon Steel wrote, “I have hired, among others, a killer whale trainer from sea world, a litigation attorney, a Stanford MBA, a senator’s speech writer...in the context of an advertising agency, the ability of planners to look at the same information as everyone else and see something different is invaluable.”

In no particular order, here is a sample of some things I did before my advertising career: Wrote the rules for a Kanye West promotion, wore a bullet proof vest for an investigation, started my own business, interviewed a federally imprisoned carjacker, presented an opening argument in court, negotiated a music contract, and connected a technology entrepreneur with angel investors.

As a planner, I’m able to draw upon all of these experiences and more to see something different.
