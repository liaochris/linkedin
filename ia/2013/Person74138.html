Ken Course's Summary

Content creator, web developer, program manager, and entrepreneur seeking to build new relationships.

Specialties: Multimedia content creation, program design, project management, business modeling, web development, in-person and web-based instruction.

As a long time innovator, educator, strategist, and creative mind, Ken Course has been a force behind creative projects world wide. While best known for his work with five-time bestselling author, Loral Langemeier, he has helped design, develop, and execute programs of all shapes and sizes around the world. As a key member of Live Out Loud, Ken has helped shape the action plan for two New York Times Bestselling Books, Put More Cash in Your Pocket and Yes! Energy. In addition, Ken has helped developed over ten coaching and instructional programs that have generated millions in revenue for the organization. These programs include the innovative Fast Cash Coaching program, Internet Marketing 101, the 100K Challenge, the Yes Energy Coaching Program, and many more.

As a coach, instructor, and entrepreneur in his own right, Ken has worked with hundreds of small business owners to help them build better sales and marketing strategies within their companies. As a result, Ken has spoken on stages in four different countries around the world and helped build stronger small business communities.

In addition to these areas, Ken is deeply passionate about community service and non-profit causes, having served as the Program Manager and later a board member of the South Lake Tahoe Family Resource Center. During his tenure, Ken was a key part of fundraising and grant development, helping to create sustainability and new access to key demographics in the South Lake Tahoe community for over five years.
