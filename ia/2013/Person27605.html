Craig Slayen's Experience

Principal

Winship Wealth Partners

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Financial Services industry

September 2003 – Present (10 years 4 months)

Winship Wealth Partners is a boutique financial planning and investment management firm.

The firm is dedicated to 5 Core Principals:

1.) They are about comprehensive planning; so clients can retire when they want to and how they want to. 

2.) They are prudent investors; never promising you that your portfolio will make a killing, but ensuring you won't get killed. 

3.) They are academic; combining 80 years of research on behavioral psychology and portfolio returns into their investment philosophy, to enhance long term returns and bring investor peace of mind.

4.) They are about honesty; exposing the myths and illusions in the financial press and the brokerage industry. 

5.) They focus on behavior management; because they know the key determinant of long term financial success is an investor's behavior, not market timing or investment selection. 

With the markets as volatile and confusing as they have been over the past few years, even the most patient investors may come to question the wisdom of the investment plan they've been following. At anytime, feel free to reach out to Winship Wealth for a second opinion on your clients’ portfolios or for your own account.
