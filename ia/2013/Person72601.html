Kate Bacon's Summary

Get more business via the World Wide Web

Online marketing is where many small business owners get stuck. In fact many people secretly HATE the dreaded M-word and avoid it like the plague. But you can get your business message across and stand out online while still being true to your core values. And doing it intelligently you’ll be able to squeeze it into your busy day and make connections that bring you sales or new clients. Your website and how you connect to people online is where it starts. And it doesn’t need to be complicated or involve “geek-speak”. I can help you in a number of ways:

WordPress Website design - strategy, branding, SEO, copywriting, site design & build, and training

Social Media Training - half day one-to-one training in LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest

Social Media Management - developing a strategy and managing your social media profiles

Email Newsletters - half day training in MailChimp or email newsletter management

Kate Bacon's Experience

Kate Bacon: Communications Genius - Director

Kate Bacon Ltd

Public Company; Myself Only; Internet industry

2010 – Present (3 years) Brighton, United Kingdom

I set up my digital marketing and training company out of the focus (in the last two years of running my virtual assistance business) on helping clients develop an online presence for their coaching businesses.

My aim from the beginning was to help businesses market themselves online in an authentic way - to help them develop their message to make the most of their USP (what their clients valued about their services).

Services I provide to clients encompass: WordPress website strategy, branding, SEO, copywriting, design & build, and training; social media training; social media management; and email newsletter training or management

My website and social media services are offered to small businesses. I offer training on a one-to-one basis as well as to groups - also designing and delivering bespoke training (in-house) to larger organisations.
