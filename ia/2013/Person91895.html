Mia Bolte, MA's Summary

Buddhist-inspired therapy for individuals, couples and groups.

People come to work with me for some of these reasons. They want support:

*In better understanding themselves, their challenges and how to move forward with greater
intention and focus
*To improve their relationships, including with their self (We can be pretty hard on ourselves--have
you noticed?)
*Learning more about who they are, what is important, and how they really want to be and actualize
in the world

Whatever the reason we work together, I help people get in touch with more of their inner knowing by using the best of body-centered, mindfulness and Buddhist psychological tools.

Specialties: Hakomi-body centered psychotherapy, Buddhist-inspired psychology, holistic, integrative counseling using deep, mindful approach to supporting even greater well being.

Meditation Instruction also available.

Please feel welcome to be in touch if you are looking to deepen your person process or
looking for a psychotherapist. Phone consultations are offered for no charge, and I am happy
to talk with you to determine if this approach can be of service to you.
