Steve Fishwick's Experience

Owner

CUT Productions S.L.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Broadcast Media industry

February 2003 – Present (10 years 7 months) Ronda, Spain

After moving to Spain I continued to travel to the UK for work but gradually I started to look around my new home for possibilities and formed CUT Productions as a Spanish incorporated company to produce my own programmes and content. The company remains and is run together with the London company.

Product Development

MobiApp121 (Sole Proprietorship)

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Publishing industry

June 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Spain

I'm really excited about launching this. We have spent months of development getting the product ready. I was looking for an App for CUT and the prices shocked me, so I decided to make one myself and ended up creating a company. It brings together a lot of technology skills I have aquired. Not giving up on TV and broadcast. We have plans...

Owner

CUT Productions (London) Ltd.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Broadcast Media industry

May 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months)

Independent TV Production and Post Company based in London and Spain. After successfully seeking investment to bring the operation back partways into the UK I opened a little office in Soho, got a good media lawyer and we have secured a service agreement with a major US online content provider together with a growing base of other production and consultancy work. I now divide my time between London and Spain.

Managing Director

Majestic Television

January 2005 – October 2005 (10 months) San Pedro, Malaga, Spain

I was for unknown reasons promoted or thrust into the heady world of being an MD for the company. It was an exciting time with trips to MIPCOM and connections with some major UK TV companies but there was too many emails and meetings for my liking and apart from Exec producing, no hands on filmaking. When the owners grew tired of their toy I bought out the facility and equipment to reform CUT Productions as an S.L. (Limited) Spanish based TV Production company and get back to doing what I liked best. As well as making our own content we have provided service and equipment facilities in Spain for the BBC, Endemol, All3Media group companies and SKY news and sport amongst others.

Head of Post Production

Majestic Television

August 2004 – January 2005 (6 months) San Pedro, Malaga, Spain

Majestic Television was a lifestyle and entertainment Channel on SKY 287. I went there after moving to Spain and trying to get out of TV, only to be thrust back into it. It was a well funded mainly UK Broadcast crew with a smattering of minor celebs. I built and designed the Post facility based on Avid and we became Beta testers for them as well as one of early road testers of Editshare. I trained a group of offline editors to do online too and who have all gone on to good work in UK broadcast, I'm happy to say.

Freelance Editor/Producer

CUT Productions

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Broadcast Media industry

August 1989 – June 2004 (14 years 11 months) UK and Spain

I was a freelance Editor on TV documentaries and lifestyle progammes for all the major Broadcasters, with the occasional pop promo and commercial for over 15 years. I worked on Film (the celluloid stuff) video and then became an early adopter of Avid. I started directing corporates and promos at BBC Wildvision as well as training new directors for Metroland, until that side of things grew.

Producer and development

BBC Worldwide

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Broadcast Media industry

June 1991 – May 1992 (1 year)

I worked in a unit called BBC Wildvision that was setup to exploit the vast library of BBC's Wildlife stock footage. It was based in the crypt of an old church and run by a great guy called Michael Bright. I produced some promos and their Mipcom package adding the stock footage to specially shot sequences I directed. I also developed a few ideas for different formats.

Sound Mixer/Recordist

Sychrosonics

August 1988 – July 1989 (1 year) Soho, London

This was a specialized small Audio post facility for film and TV. I did a variety of all work from dubbing editing, freelance location sound recording (BBC 40 mins, South Bank Show etc.) to VO recording and mixing for shows like C4's Media Show.

Sound Mixer

Worldwide Pictures

July 1987 – July 1988 (1 year 1 month) Soho, London

I worked here first in transfer then assistant mixer then a mixer on ADR and language versioning. I worked on a variety of film and TV productions including Nick Roeg's "The Witches".

Dubbing Editor and Transfer

Universal Sound

August 1986 – July 1987 (1 year) Perivale, Middlesex

First job after film school. Laying tracks (16mm and 35mm) transfer and was perhaps one of the last people in the UK to do optical sound tracks for 16mm film prints - I can still remember how to do X Modulation curves, if that means anything to anyone now!
