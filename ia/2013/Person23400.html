Chris Jacobs' Summary

I am currently working as the Operations Officer for the United Methodist Foundation of Indiana. In my role with the foundation, my responsibilities have been focused within the organization's Loan & Savings Ministry which works to provide churches with a reliable and affordable source of funding through stable investments made by individuals, churches, and agencies. This ministry has experienced tremendous growth in recent years, growing from $23.3mm in deposits when I started to over $29.5mm today.
