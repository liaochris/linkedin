"One of the perhaps 100 people in the Netherlands who really 'gets' the internet."

You're busy with all things 'internet' and 'new media' but also realize the money is often still in 'paper'? Sooner or later that will bring you here, to me. Together we can find out how we can give 'online' that quaint little thing called 'added value' that 'offline' still has. So: don't hesitate to email me.

I combine analytical skills with insights in 'stories' and journalism and love discovering new ways of working with (and visualizing) data to help both.

Smarter, faster and social marketresearch: R2 let's you grow.

By making smart links between your existing and new (research)data we combine what you already know with new knowledge, so you get rid of your 'blind spots'.
