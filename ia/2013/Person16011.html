Brad Baskin's Summary

For more than 20 years, Brad Baskin, a Chicago-based photographer, has helped executives, lawyers, actors and others tell their compelling stories. That’s in addition to the many advertising and PR projects he’s handled, focusing on fashion, home décor and other stylish subjects.

“I’m particularly interested in what inspires people,” says Baskin. “An executive or attorney might be motivated by something entirely different than an actor or actress. Yet, all of us have a reason for the work we do. My challenge is to capture the essence of what connects people to their work – in a portrait, a group photo or a headshot.”

Baskin estimates that he has photographed more than 1,000 people in the business, legal and acting worlds. His pictures have been used in annual reports, press materials, advertising campaigns and brochures, as well as on websites and in other venues.

“I enjoy the interaction of a photo shoot – continually trying to draw out the human side of people and helping them to be more relaxed while I take their pictures,” Baskin says. “This is equally important if I’m taking a corporate portrait, pictures of attorneys in a meeting room or a headshot of an actor or a model. I’m essentially telling their stories through the photos I take.”

Baskin summarizes his work as follows: “I’m creating an image that honors the character, the sense of purpose and the unique presence of an individual or a group of businesspeople.”

Baskin brings that same kind of attitude to other assignments, too. “My job is to help families remember unique milestones in their lives,” notes Baskin. “In addition to weddings, I also photograph bar mitzvahs, bat mitzvahs and other special events that celebrate shared cultural connections.”

Brad Baskin is available to shoot photography on location or in his studio loft at 850 N. Milwaukee Ave., in Chicago. For more information, call (312) 733-2192 or visit www.bradbaskin.com and www.lifetime-wedding.com.
