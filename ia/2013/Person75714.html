Kim Deppe's Summary

Marketing and Public Relations professional with over 25 years experience in branding, integrative marketing, online marketing, crisis communications and management. Successfully managed the launch of a completely revised website that increased online lending fourfold in its first year of operation. Managed the communications and marketing around the disintegration of a health system merger including re-establishing the brand of the original hospital system. Developed multiple award-winning campaigns that resulted in increased volume and higher brand awareness. Executive management experience working in small and large environments. Team player with a philosophy of endless learning. Accredited Public Relations professional and hold a Master's Certificate in Online Marketing Strategies.
