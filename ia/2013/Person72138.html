Karen T. Lin's Summary

As a UX & human factors consultant, I investigate how people behave with products, services, and systems in order to help them achieve goals and objectives more effectively. My mission is to transform the human experience through designing thoughtful, meaningful human-to-computer and human-to-human interactions. My hobbies include independent/grassroots projects related to food education, public safety, and compassion.

STARTUPS & NONPROFITS: I would love to hear about the problems and opportunities you are facing with your customers/constituencies. If you have a project in mind and would like a pro bono consultation with me, please get in touch via LinkedIn or at karen.t.lin@gmail.com.

RECRUITERS: I am based in the DC metro area, currently only available for standalone independent projects (usability reviews/reports, user research studies, UX design work) - teleworking and travel as needed anywhere in the US. Thank you for your consideration.
