Michael Leifer's Summary

I’m a CULTURAL ANTHROPOLOGIST & CREATIVE DIRECTOR, who builds viral programs and cultural movements. I translate human insight, trends and data sets into actionable strategies, targeting and creative, which aligns with a brand's DNA, to produce authentic human engagement. I seek to identify and foster equitable exchanges, generate long term relationships, and deliver measurable impact.

In 2000, I founded and have run GUERILLA PR, INC, a 13 yr old award-winning strategic communication shop, which has helped plan and produce hundreds of participatory campaigns, generated millions of pieces of user-generated content, and enabled thousands of communities to form, advocate and flouirsh.

During that time, we collaborated with 100s of blue-chip brands (SONY, DIAGEO, REDBULL, NESTLE, COCA-COLA, SNAPPLE, GEN MILLS, THQ, DISNEY, NIKE), agencies (OMNICOM, INITIATIVE, CALRECYCLE), tech companies (APPLE, MICROSOFT, CITRIX, YAHOO!, BLURB, ZINIO, CENGAGE, NAPSTER), investors (SAND HILL ROAD), non-profits (EARTHDAY NETWORK, EARTH JUSTICE, PAST FOUNDATION) and education institutions (CA BOARD OF ED, STEM). For those clients/partners, we designed campaigns and closed 3rd party partnerships with the right influencers, stakeholders, vendors and networks, delivering each a multiple ROI.

Previously, I founded, ran and successfully exited C3 LIVE, a Webcasting, Content Distribution and Digital Rights Management Company, whose partners were MICROSOFT, YAHOO and MTV.

In 2010, I co-founded ECODADS - a next-generation parenting network. We produce ecological literacy programs, with education board-approved curriculum. We seek to build deeper more meaningful relationships with our children, each other & the earth.

I am an active member of the MARIN ECONOMIC FORUM Tech Enterpreneur Committee, have sat on the EX'PRESSION COLLEGE FOR THE DIGITAL ARTS Curriculum Advisory Board for 10 yrs, and most importantly, am an engaged father of TWO AMAZING CHILDREN and a SMART, SASSY & SOULFUL WIFE.
