Andrea Schlapia's Summary

Andrea Schlapia is the Founder and CEO of Ironstone, which represents the culmination of her 20+ year career within the financial services industry.

Her experience began as a financial advisor evolving into a consultant coach for advisors entering the field. This ignited her passion to support others through learning and development of best practices in order to achieve substantial results.

To this end, she followed her desire into positions of senior-level practice management specialists for Dreyfus, Prudential, and DWS Investments prior to the realization of Ironstone - a boutique consulting group providing specialized solutions for a select number of clientele.

Her passion combined with her talents drives her desire to forge the lives of her clientele both personally and professionally while being a catalyst for them to achieve higher levels of accomplishments

• Ironstone clientele include professionals within Financial and Sales industries: Independent - RIA - Wirehouse channels, C-Suite executives, sales managers, key account managers, wholesaler, advisors, sales teams and their support staff.

• Ironstone has identified four key performance areas known as the Fundamental 4™, which encompass optimal strategies required to design, develop, and sustain a successful business. The foundation of our professional engagements is based on these core elements and built with a step-by-step approach to create a custom road map revolving around our client objectives. The Fundamental 4™ is the philosophy that shapes our business, ideas, and solutions.

Specialties

Ironstone is a learning and development consultancy with business acumen that translates across many industries. Our focus is practice management strategies to enhance and improve both business and personal life. We support those who want major and comprehensive improvements that look at all aspects not just an isolated area for change.
