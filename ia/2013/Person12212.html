BA TS's Summary

Business Analysis Training School has been started to create Business Analysts with case-studies driven international standard quality training to aspirants to match industry requirements.

BATS is the brainchild of all our promoters and deal this training with full zeal, enthusiasm, and social awareness of sharing the knowledge, skills, and competencies.
We have been in the training industry since 3 years now and after serving to several hundreds of individuals as well as corporate customers, now we have consolidated to online training.

The main objective of this online training is to offer our proven methodologies to everyone across the globe. By online, we can reach and help anybody at any corner of the world.

Here at BATS, we not only believe in today’s training but also the continuum learning for BA and that is the reason why we are providing an online reference library, which will be updated on a regular basis with the new items addition. This online reference library is of free of cost to everyone who takes the training at BATS and is valid for lifetime.
