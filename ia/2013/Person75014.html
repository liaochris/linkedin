Kevin Langan's Summary

Kevin began his real estate career at the end of 2008 just as the Arizona real estate market had crashed hard. He got involved in real estate investment with a community of like minded people that mentored him through the process of starting a business. In March of 2009 he partnered with Tully-Holland International, a local small to mid size real estate investment company. Kevin began servicing all of their needs at the Trustee sale foreclosure auctions in Maricopa County Arizona.

Over 2009 and through late 2010 Kevin developed a bidding service for investors purchasing investment real estate, raised private capital to do a few transactions himself, and learned a whole lot about Arizona real estate investment in the metro Phoenix area. In mid 2010 Kevin was blessed with an opportunity to work with a large private equity commercial and residential real estate investment group Chase Merritt. Kevin was responsible for the trustee sale bidding for their group, and in the 3rd quarter of 2010 we successfully purchased 75 properties.

By late 2010 Kevin joined Bud Gragg and Associates Real Estate and is focused on helping regular folks buy and sell homes. In September of 2012 Kevin and Bud Gragg partnered to found Turn Key Real Estate. Turn Key Real Estate is a Real Estate Brokerage that is focused on building a well rounded residential brokerage that offers a one stop shop for buyers, sellers and investors. Kevin is excited about the opportunities that this market brings for investors & homeowners. He looks forward to assisting others in Arizona real estate investment and homeownership
