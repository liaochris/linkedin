Implemented an IT Business Partner program that integrated strategic business and technology plans. This substantially enhanced the reputation of IT within the organization and improved the way we work with our customers.

Implemented a business-to-business e-commerce portal that enabled sales teams and independent sales reps to view reports, check inventories, and place orders 24x7.

Implemented the companies first Project Management Office which improved project delivery success and business unit satisfaction.

Developed the firm’s e-business strategy and implemented the first web-based brokerage desktop for BNY Clearing’s customers.

Established Straight-Through-Processing between CheckFree Security APL and BETA Systems. Established a link between Metavante’s Trust Accounting System and BNY Clearing that facilitated a link to money managers for separately managed accounts.

Drove the conversion process for BNY Clearing’s acquisition growth initiative, resulting in the addition of 200+ correspondents between 1999 and 2003.
