Justin Seidner's Summary

Integrated Producer and Project Manager with ten years of experience making creative directors’ dreams come true and keeping clients’ blood pressures down. And vice versa.

Select accounts: CME Group, Diageo, Enterprise Rent-A-Car, General Electric, Kraft Foods, L'Oreal, Golf Galaxy (Dick's Sporting Goods), Procter & Gamble, and USAA.

Core Competencies:

- Tactical oversight from pre-production to post-production of a wide rage of deliverables – broadcast commercials, corporate films, and web/digital projects.

- Creation and supervision of project budgets, internal/external resource schedules, and deliverable timelines.

- Collaboration with multidisciplinary teams and mentorship of junior staff.

- Key contact between client-side stakeholders and agency team to ensure project development is congruent with client expectations.

- Negotiation with production and post-production vendors, talent unions and representation, stock footage/photography agencies, and music clearance divisions to ensure best possible rates and outcomes.

- Coordination of production crews in North America, Europe, and Asia, including on-site production management, union relations, equipment procurement and transportation, visa and work-permit considerations, customs procedures, and union relations.

Specialties:

- Production and post-production supervision

- Project management

- Budgeting

- AICP and AICE budget cost consulting

- Proposal/RFP writing

- Vendor relations/business affairs

- Talent management and negotiation

- Resource analysis

- Fixing broken processes and workflows
