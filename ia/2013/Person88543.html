Matthew Benedict's Summary

Platinum Restoration is a locally owned contractor that specializes in insurance restoration projects, both commercial and residential. We offer our services to clients in Cuyahoga, Lorain, Huron, Erie, and Medina counties. Our President, Michelle Brooks has over 20 years of experience in the insurance restoration business. We make sure that our phones are answered 24 hours a day by a live operator who will quickly dispatch prepared emergency workers to your site.

At Platinum, we focus on providing efficient service and solutions to customers who have suffered severe insurance losses. Our services include complete restoration from emergency loss mitigation to total reconstruction. Our professional restoration crewmen are trained and experienced and are ready to facilitate your project no matter the magnitude. To assist in the restoration process we have a team of Project Manager, Field Supervisors, Subcontractors that can handle almost any project. We utilize comprehensive cost analysis, project management, and estimating software in all of our projects.

Our goal is to exceed our clients’ expectations with quality and attention to detail. As we strive to maintain our status as best in our field, personal attention to your project by our skilled professionals is guaranteed. Our capabilities and experience make us the ideal choice for your residential or commercial restoration needs. Whether your damage was caused by fire damage, water, wind, sewage backup, vehicle impact, vandalism, ice and snow backup, or any other occurrence, our goal is to assist you in every possible way to restore your property to pre-loss conditions.
