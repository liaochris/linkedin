Gloria Quigley's Summary

I’m not a bass fisherman, a teenage video gamer, or a new car shopper, but in the course of my career I've gained insight into what matters to each and how brands can engage with them in a way that’s relevant and effective. At the heart of my work, previously as a Brand Strategist, and now, a UX Researcher, is the art and science of uncovering human insights that guide branded interactions and build businesses along the way.

I have experience across diverse targets, product categories, business challenges, and communications platforms, an almost insatiable curiosity (tempered with a pragmatic view of end goals), and a desire to stretch thinking. I work and play well with others, and enjoy rolling up my sleeves.
