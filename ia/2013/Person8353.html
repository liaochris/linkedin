Anne-Marie Perks' Summary

I am a professional illustrator and educator working for years in the publishing industry with a focus on children's illustration. I've also worked on animation projects, editorial and product illustration, illustrative logo design and development, character design and development and package design. Published children's books I've illustrated include, The Tortoise Who Bragged, Clever Calculator Cat and was privileged to be included in the Lines in the Sand, New Writing on War and Peace. My list of projects I want to do grows ever longer, including publishing a book I've both written and illustrated, doing a graphic novel format and completing an animation that isn't a student demo.

Drawing and storytelling in a variety of mediums is my reason for getting up in the morning along with passing on that passion. I work predominately in watercolour and coloured pencils, though lately I've been experimenting with collage elements and compositing digitally. Occasionally I do work in oils, acrylics and lino cut applications.

Specialties

Illustrating and writing for children's publishing; figurative work, animals and multi-cultural themes, illustrations for education publishing, puppet making and 2D and stop motion animation.
