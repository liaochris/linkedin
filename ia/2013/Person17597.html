Brian O'Neill's Summary

Management consultant focused on helping organizations become more effective & efficient. Specialties include strategy & planning, business process design, systems design, software evaluation and implementation services.

Experienced manager who has successfully led health & human services projects, including major clinical tracking and reporting systems efforts. Developed groundbreaking idea for web-based system to track individuals receiving substance abuse treatment.
