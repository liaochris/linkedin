Emma Loggins' Summary

I am a designer, social media coach, and speaker. I specialize in Web Design / UI Design and Social Media Marketing for my clients who are both inside and outside of the entertainment industry - helping them to develop a loyal following as well.

I am a skilled designer and developer, and I also specializes in helping individuals and companies with their social media marketing and networking. From keeping in touch with clients on Facebook to business networking on LinkedIn, I knows how to create and maintain the best social network exposure for my clients.

Another thing I really enjoy is public speaking on social media marketing and design related topics. I have spoken at a number of events with audiences as large as 2,000 people. My diverse business experience also allows me to share real-life stories as I educate, entertain, and motivate audiences.

-------------------

I am also the founder and editor of the popular entertainment news site FanBolt.com, a site I designed and developed from scratch. I write daily on the entertainment happenings of the industry. With a focus on television and movies, I have built my subscriber number up to over 165,000 members.

Through the doors that FanBolt has opened up for me, I have worked with a number of large entertainment companies such as Warner Brothers, and I have had my work featured on television and in various publications (newspaper, magazines, and books).

In 2005, I was interviewed by People Magazine for having one of the best television show websites, and in 2009, Warner Brothers recruited me again to help with social marketing for several of their TV shows on sites such as Facebook, Twitter, and MySpace.

I was also a contributing writer for CNN's Geek Out Blog which launched in 2011.

----------------

At the end of the day, I have a strong passion for design, technology, and the world of entertainment, and because of that - it continually shows through in all my work.
