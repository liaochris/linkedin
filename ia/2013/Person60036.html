Katie-Lee Metzdorf's Summary

After many great experiences through my internships and graduate studies, I am eager to cut my teeth in a new career. I look forward to exercising my talents and skills. During my studies I found I have a passion and a knack for public relations, marketing, and digital marketing. My goal is to advance my career in a related field.

I participated in social media marketing as part of the Information and Media Marketing Specialist Internship at H2 Label Group through HeyStuart.
