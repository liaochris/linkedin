Mads Klinkby's Summary

Core competences in SharePoint and service oriented Microsoft .NET architecture. Vast experience developing portals and web services as well as MSSQL databases.

Graduated B.Sc. in electronics and IT and certified MCSD, MCDBA, SharePoint MCTS and MCP. Continuously updated knowledge on current and upcoming Microsoft technologies and a sincere interest in process improvement, best practices, and parallelization.

Responsible and self-motivated with an honest, open an pragmatic attitude, and feels at home in both waterfall- and agile driven teams (certified ScrumMaster).

Always interested in portals, high performance systems and Azure applications.
