Robert Monster's Summary

My current focus is building Epik.com, a leader in the domain industry serving ~35,000 customers and also managing a large house portfolio of domain holdings. I am also a part-time angel investor with about a dozen investments through my angel investment company, Monster Venture Partners.

Previously Founder, Chairman and CEO of Global Market Insite (GMI) of fast-growing global provider of market research solutions with customers in 60 countries and a global panel with coverage in 200 countries and territories. Raised $48 million in venture capital from leading investors. GMI was acquired by WPP in 2011.

Before starting GMI, I was a Global Product Development manager at Procter & Gamble. Worked with some great people in Asia, Europe, and the America, managing new product launch initiatives for the Pampers brand. Left P&G in 1999 to start GMI.

Incurable optimist but otherwise in excellent health. Voracious reader. Varsity oarsman at Cornell. Involved in both secular and Christian philanthrophic initiatives.

Specialties

global marketing, domain names, software development, mergers and acquisitions, technology management, new product development, market research, venture capital, domaining, health payments, patents and intellectual property
