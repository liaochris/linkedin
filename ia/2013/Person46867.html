Gao Pronove's Summary

I am now a renewable energy developer in the Philippines. Previously, I was a Climate Change specialist at the UN in Geneva and Bonn, a founder of 2 nonprofit organizations - Earth Council Geneva, and GHG Management Institute, an entrepreneur who built an e-learning company, and started a biofuels farm. I'm developing my own practice in the global green economy. I'm very excited about starting even more social enterprises in the nonprofit and private sectors. My work blends my passion for the environment and entrepreneurship with my interest in social development.
