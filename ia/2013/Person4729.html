Aliyah Marr's Publications

  • Parallel Mind, The Art of Creativity

    Authors: Aliyah Marr

    Parallel Mind, The Art of Creativity is not about how to draw or paint, but how to think like an artist. Aliyah Marr draws from her experience as a teacher, visual artist, poet, graphic designer, and art director to demonstrate how you can change your body, your profession, your relationship, and your life just by changing your thoughts. A powerful reference guide for artists, educators, psychologists, entrepreneurs, scientists, and for those who have an interest in a practical form of self-development. Packed with practical examples and exercises from every medium: visual art, theater, music, video, poetry, scriptwriting, and dance, this book shows you how to use art to first express, and then clarify thoughts and emotions to create whatever you want.

  • Pocket Parallel Mind

    • ParallelMindzz.com
    Authors: Aliyah Marr

    Pocket Parallel Mind is a book of inspirational quotes for creative thinkers and those who wish to creatively transform their lives. Based upon Twitter-like quips from the first book, Parallel Mind, The Art of Creativity, Pocket Parallel Mind is laid out like a calendar; one quote per day. Includes a table of contents, and a convenient index of concepts. Perfect for anyone who works in a creative field—musicians, visual artists, designers, actors, scriptwriters, mathematicians, etc. Others interested in personal development through creative development will also find this book an inspirational resource.

  • Bird Seed, Advance Your Cause, Yourself, or Your Business With Social Media

    • ParallelMindzz.com
    Authors: Aliyah Marr

    A Revolution of Birds

    Bird Seed announces that a revolution has taken place under our very noses. Social media has created a new grassroots movement. The phenomena of social media shows us that a new paradigm has evolved.

    Old paradigm verbiage
    Consumers, corporate power, mass marketing, advertising, planned obsolescence, search-engine optimization, content mills, email blasts, 30-second commercials, broadcasting.

    New paradigm verbiage
    Individualists, informed buyers reporting on products and corporations, freedom of choice, democratized media, viral messaging, conversation, democratic media, inclusion and participation, flocking behavior, community, global reach.

    Or to put it more succinctly, the old paradigm of the advertising and consumerism is being replaced by an exciting new era of individualism, free choice, and democratic grass-roots viral media.

  • Celestial Navigation

    • ParallelMindzz
    Authors: Aliyah Marr

    This book is for those intrepid explorers of human expression—artists and social change agents. It doesn’t matter what field you choose—whether in the arts or sciences—if you do it in the attitude of curiosity and passion, you are an artist, not just involved with a certain medium, but with your own personal evolution. You are not satisfied with the status quo; you find your own answers. You are what author Aliyah Marr calls a “conscious creative.”

    The journey of the conscious creative is one of adventure and courage. It is not the path often taken. It is not a “normal” life, one with a great deal of comfort, in fact, it is often a life of privation and loneliness. Can you imagine Leonardo da Vinci in a bar arguing about the latest football scores? Do you think that Shakespeare, Bach or Einstein had many contemporaries who understood them?

    Most people today would rather choose to spend their lives working a job they hate and coming home to argue with their spouses and watch TV than spend one minute wrestling with themselves in front of an empty canvas or blank page. It isn’t easy, sometimes it isn’t even fun, but ultimately, a creative life is extremely rewarding.

    Picasso said, “Why do you try to understand art? Do you try to understand the song of a bird?” I have a vision that when we are all freed to express ourselves, we will be, each one of us, like a bird that has finally learned to sing. Can you imagine the symphony of creativity that together we will create?
