Devin Fleenor's Summary

I am a warrior of light. A global shift in consciousness is approaching - now is the time for illumination.

With 15+ years in the music, visual art, web and marketing industries, I have developed an arsenal of skills and the ability to combine them to overcome nearly any obstacle. Tenacious? Yes. Perpetually curious? Hell yes. I envision fantastic things, develop creative strategies and execute them. As a managing director, I have learned to listen, lead, inspire and motivate.

I live and breathe all aspects of music, video and mixed-media production, including writing, directing, storyboarding, shooting, editing, designing, mastering, and motion graphics.

Most recently, I have been focused on interactive visual art, real-time 3D and video processing, laser show production, custom visual programming and video projection mapping.

Previously, I found myself tackling custom WordPress plugins, viral marketing, social media, content distribution, global SEO, and hybrid web strategies.

I will continue to evolve - please check back again soon for new updates.
