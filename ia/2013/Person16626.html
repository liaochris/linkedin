Dave Branson's Summary

Dave Branson has over 20 years of progressive experience in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) covering application architecture design, applied spatial analysis for natural resources, government and business intelligence sectors, digital mapping production management and delivery of GIS application services. Dave has a broad mix of hands-on technical expertise with GIS, database and development tools as well as management consulting expertise on helping clients implement spatial technology solutions.

Dave is currently working on the development of a spatial data record collection and management application framework that leverage open standards such as WMS, MySQL and .NET controls for WPF and Silverlight. The application framework focuses on development of end-user solutions that will support agri-business needs for integrated record keeping and agriculture traceability. The benefits are also being extended to other industry sectors such as oil/gas pipeline asset management where the same application framework is be applied to pipeline integrity modeling.

Dave is the founder/owner of GeoSolutions Consulting Inc. (1993) and the co-founder of FieldTRAKS Solutions Inc. (2005). FieldTRAKS Solutions develops GIS application components such as their .NET RadMAP GIS control along with the BaseTRAKS & FieldTRAKS Application Frameworks. While these components are available to other developers, the business focus is on offering implementation services and providing web-hosted subscriber applications.

For more info please see: www.fieldtraks.ca

Dave Branson's Projects

  • FieldTRAKS ARKS

    • October 2005 to Present
    Team Members: Dave Branson, Matt Fraser

    FieldTRAKS ARKS is a GIS web-based technology that can be utilized in any industry sector where there is a requirement for collection and management of geospatial data. It was initally developed to meet market opportunities in the agriculture sector and has since be extended to meet requirements for pipeline integrity management in the oil & gas sector. Its’ unique web database design enables clients to define their data hierarchy of spatial and non-spatial objects such as farms, fields and soil sample locations and all of the types of events that are associated with each object (e.g., Record a Chemical Application). Application profiles are managed entirely through database configuration which results in client applications which are easily maintained and modified to meeting with changing client requirements.
