Brock Pierce's Summary

Brock Pierce is a 10x entrepreneur best known for pioneering the market for virtual currency in online games. He has founded ten companies, acquired more than 30 others, and closed more than a dozen financing securing more than $200 million on behalf of his companies. He is currently a Managing Director of the Clearstone Global Gaming Fund and sits on the boards of IMI Exchange, Playsino, Xfire, GoCoin, Five Delta, Evertune, and Spicy Horse Games.

Pierce’s entrepreneurial activities have been sector focused in the areas of digital currency, online games, and online gambling. Pierce is the founder of IMI Exchange, the worlds leading virtual currency and virtual goods marketplace with sales exceeding US$1 billion annually, which raised over $100 Million from blue chip investors such as Goldman Sachs and Oak Investment Partners, ZAM, one of the world’s largest media properties for gamers with more than 15 million monthly users which was sold in January 2012 to Tencent, IGE, the company credited with pioneering the market for virtual goods in online games, which generated $100 million in revenue in 2006 and was sold in early 2007. He is also the co-founder of GoCoin, Playsino, Evertune, GamesTV, and Digital Entertainment Network. Pierce also lead the acquisition of Xfire from Viacom.

Pierce is an advisor to Casual Connect, GamesBeat, and Digital Media Wire. He is also an active angel investor. Pierce is an active supporter of non-profit and advocacy organizations and is a member of Clinton Global Initiative.
