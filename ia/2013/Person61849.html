Jen Jaciw's Summary

I have been working in the transportation industry since I was 18 and am now the proud co-owner of Lone Star Limousine, a successful Silicon Valley based company serving the San Francisco Bay Area.

Recognized for providing exceptional transportation services, Lone Star Limo received the 2012 awards for SF Chronicle's "Best Transportation Bay Area" as well as the Mercury News' "Best Limousine Service Silicon Valley".

We were also featured in The Wall Street Journal in 2009 for thinking outside the box by bringing in lower budget/economy vehicles when the recession hit, and then on MTV in 2011 when they presented one very special underprivileged teen the Quince of her dreams.

With a 5 STAR rating on YELP, and numerous other accolades to our credit, Lone Star Limo has been chosen as Preferred Provider for several Bay Area corporations such as Microsoft, Mens Wearhouse, Sunset Magazine and Westin.

We frequently provide specialized transportation for high-profile clients and we are in tune with the level of service they require. We are very hands-on and are able to help guide these customers through the reservations process, making key decisions without wasting their time, being flexible when last minute changes occur, and anticipating their needs with the utmost discretion.

Please think of us as your “mobile” concierge, here to provide you with a variety of services including: private and corporate charter transportation, executive airport transfers, employee shuttle, sightseeing tours, wine tasting tours, wedding service and custom party packages for women.

We serve celebrities and those who require celebrity treatment!
