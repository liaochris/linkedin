Brad Dechter's Summary

I'm a detail-oriented Art Director with a passion for digital. I see the world as a source of design challenges. I choose my wine based on the label's typography and I’m way too concerned with how my signature looks. I'm a tinkerer and a learner. Whenever I see something new online, whether it's a micro-site or a chrome experiment, I immediately go to the code and try to figure it out.

I have experience working on a range of projects, from traditional media for major brands at big agencies, to innovative digital campaigns for niche clients at boutique start-ups. Because of my diverse advertising background, I’ve had many opportunities to exercise my problem-solving abilities in a wide variety of business and creative scenarios.

Specialties: Art Direction, Design, Digital, Concept Development, Mobile, Social, UI, UX, Problem Solving.
