Jim Hopkins' Summary

Are you being asked to improve employee performance and you lack the staff to make it happen? Do you supervise a training department that is being asked to do a lot more than they know how? Will people lose their jobs if things don't turnaround soon? For over 22 years I have been helping managers determine the root causes for poor performance and facilitating complete turnarounds. I can help you solve your training and performance challenges too!

My name is Jim Hopkins, and I am a Performance Consultant. I work with both frontline and senior managers to identify all the issues preventing optimal performance in both individuals and entire teams of employees and then apply appropriate solutions that will achieve the desired results. I find that often by focusing our energies on improving the performance of the training function, we create an indispensable business partner to solve future issues.

Managers work with me for one of the following five reasons:

1. To build their new training departments from scratch
2. To mentor and develop their training staff to perform new responsibilities
3. To replace learning processes with ones that will achieve better results
4. To develop the strategic vision and plan for their training department
5. To find the root causes and solutions for current employee performance challenges

I have built training departments, designed programs, and coached individuals so that performance improvement becomes the result of everything we do. I am the Author of the Book – “The Training Physical: Diagnose, Treat and Cure Your Training Department”

If you are interested in working together, please email me here on LinkedIn and I will be sure to get back to you promptly.
