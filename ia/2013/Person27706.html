ABOUT THE FOUNDERS & FACILITATORS...
David & Amy have been married since 1985 & have 3 children and 6 grandchildren & have spent years working with couples, but that alone doesn't make them the expert.

Although they are educated with dozens of certifications, licenses & degrees, the most important credential for helping others in their relationships is the restoration of their own marriage after filing for divorce. After creating AND using over 75 tools, they renewed their marriage & founded Creating Intimacy & Respect, Inc so that they could help others do the same.

ABOUT THE COUPLES TRAINING...
Creating Intimacy & Respect Couples Training is for committed, engaged, newlywed, married, separated or divorced couples. You can learn to avoid or recover from things that sabotage or destroy your relationship; communicate in one another's love language; discover your partner's logic & emotions; resolve conflict respectfully; understand & establish boundaries; & break free the prison of unforgiveness. Training's are held quarterly in Dallas/Fort Worth, Texas.

ABOUT 1-DAY WORKSHOPS...
Each workshop or retreat provides teaching & tools that build & restore a couples relationship. Go to our website for more information, topics & dates.
