Meiko Seymour's Summary

Meiko’s passions are deeply rooted in being able to see a need, find strategic solutions to that need, and implementing those ideas to fix and fill the need. His skill to take time and think things through while still being able to get things done in a timely matter has carried him far. Meiko has worked in creative arts ministries, developing new and effective ways to minister the Gospel fervently and efficiently. He has also worked in market research where he was tasked to find solutions to developing products and social issues.

Meiko is currently serving as a team member at The Church at Chapelhill in Georgia where he oversees all aspects of Student Ministries Production including but not limited to producing weekly worship experiences, project managing, directing student leadership, overseeing creative arts, event planning, and general day to day admin. He also serves as Chapelhill's Internet Campus Director where he oversees the online campus experience including all social media marketing and campaigns for various ministries.

Meiko is recently married to his wife Simone.

He also enjoys orange juice and waffles for breakfast.

Specialties: Creative arts, Script Writing, Cast Development, Project Management, Team Building, Social Networking, Mainstream Marketing, Qualitative & Quantitative Research, Product/Name Branding, PR, Client Services and Management, Leadership Development, Student Mentoring, Worship Leading, Worship Services Planning.
