DARRELL MILES's Summary

Darrell J Miles, Author, consultant, and online Houston Marketing Expert held recent positions as "Texas State Director" of both MainStreet Chamber and The National Chamber of Commerce, National Organizations. He is a current Board Member of the National Black Chamber of Commerce.

He compiles 18 years in the Houston Region as a Workforce Industry Business Manager and later as a Staffing Regional Director. He is an expert at helping small businesses gain a dominant position in their local marketplace with Houston mobile marketing as well as other local market techniques.

Darrell's Mobile/Social Marketing Media Company - ACE Consolidated Enterprises LLC (A.C.E.LLC) specializes in helping motivated entrepreneurs and small businesses gain a competitive advantage in both online and locally with Houston marketing.

He makes sure that local marketing businesses are able to be "found" on the internet, ensures that they never run out of leads, and helps them to transform these potential clients into lifetime customers (and raving fans!).

Specialties: Mobile Advertising & Houston Marketing, Social Media Business Strategies, Public and Online Media Presentations, Manage Sales Teams, Coordinate Volunteer Teams.
