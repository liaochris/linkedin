Howard A Lim's Summary

HOW Creative has over 25 years of experience in all aspects of branding. We take our Fortune 100 world-class unique branding expertise and apply it to all size companies for accelerated growth in their goals, profits, customers, value, and equity. At its unique core, HOW Creative has two distinct but complementary capabilities; business and branding. HOW Creative focuses on defining who you are, what you stand for, what makes you unique, and why this matters to your customers over the competition. Our branding capabilities focus on how you are going to be received, heard and felt by every engagement point through every communication channel, at launch or relaunch.

HOW Creative offers a full range of strategies and services: business and brand strategies, coupled with brand creation, including company and product naming, taglines, brand identity systems, websites, advertising, product designing, store front design, packaging, etc. All these are linked with brand management.

Many of our innovative solutions set big brands apart and established new standards which have touched just about everyone, such as…

• The inaugural launching of DVDs, which took over the VHS and Beta tapes; and has become an
industry standard.

• For ABC Networks, one of the first uses of computerized off-line motion graphics in creating an on-air presence for Saturday mornings (which actually changed television viewing forever, and contributed to the birth of motion graphics).

• Led a team that gave birth to Zylan by launching one the first graphically integrated award-winning websites, setting the standards for two-level deep sites.

• Created the first all-digital ad for Fujitsu for MAC World and MAC User national magazines, an ad that put Fujitsu on the map for removable discs.

• Other fortune 100 brands he and his company have done work for include: Apple, Honda, Disney, DreamWorks, Mattel, Jakks Pacific, Paramount Pictures, and HP, just to name a few.
