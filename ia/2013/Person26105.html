Coco Duckworth's Summary

I bring my expertise in the area of communication to every coaching client and partnership I engage. At every level...leadership to management, management to sales, sales to administrative assistants, and any combination thereof...it's amazing how building an effective communication culture and setting up foundations for success help a venture grow in productivity and profitability!
I enjoy working with medium-size businesses and entrepreneurially minded companies, not only because they are often very gifted business people, but because they can usually implement postitive change quickly.

Specialties

Executive coaching, leadership coaching, sales coaching, private and group sessions/seminars available. Keynotes and seminar topics available as well.
