Rozhy Talisman's Summary

I serve as a guide to the discovery and unveiling of your talents within. Our busy life does not allow us to pause and breathe. I guide you to get centered, serene and simply be. I introduce you to principles and strategies to improve your well-being, enjoy the moment. and achieve higher performance in your daily lives.

A little bit about me:
I have written two books as co-author with Jack Canfield and Deepak Chopra in 2008 and 2012 respectively. about business and different concepts of success. I am a columnist for the Bilingual Magazine HoyDallas.I am a recording artist in both English and Spanish. I am a certified Life Coach and Business Mentor.
