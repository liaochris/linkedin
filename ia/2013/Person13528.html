Bert Goos' Summary

Bert brings a unique combination of twenty five years experience in assessing and developing the human potential and performance of leaders and high potentials in public and private companies in the Netherlands and international. He is often asked as their leadership and teamcoach, or their HR business consultant. He is an entrepreneur and inspiring person who brings his knowledge about human potential and how to develop and maintain long term relationships with stakeholders and how to do business effectively to his work. He has worked with leaders in international companies in the Netherlands in a variety of industries including food, hard and software, banking and insurance, chemical, consulting, professional services, engineering, local and state government, and universities. In 2002 Bert founded Online Talent Manager, became a developer of talentmanagement solutions and delivers e-assessment and development tools in different languages as a webapplication. The Octogram is our flag ship product!

Specialties: talent management strategy, developing competencies, webapplication for 360 degree feedback, career coaching services, (e-)assessment, management development, services for careerplanners, outplacement consultants, tests for measuring entrepreneurship, leadership potential, teamroles, building webapplications for performance management and selection and development.
