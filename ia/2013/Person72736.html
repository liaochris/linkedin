Katharine Holmes' Summary

I have nearly 20 years experience in the graphic design industry in both print and web design, having graduated from Malaspina University-College (now Vancouver Island University) in 1996. I am proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop), have excellent proofreading skills, and a love for typography.

For 11 years, I was Senior Graphic Designer at IdeaZone.ca. In that capacity, I have produced a variety of pieces for clients as well as for internal projects, including business cards, brochures, magazine designs, and posters.

Prior to starting IdeaZone.ca, I worked as a pre-press designer at Flynn Printing, Greenheart Publications, and the Duncan Citizen newspaper. All of these roles required quick turn-around, self-motivation, attention to detail, and exceptional proofing skills.

I am extremely active on several social media streams, including Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Instagram, Flickr, and YouTube. I have positioned myself as one of the top Twitter users in Victoria through my avid use of this social media network over the past five years, and I have helped organize five WordCamps and four Social Media Camps in Victoria.

In addition, I am currently City Leader for City Mom Now Victoria, a role in which I write articles for the website, and promote them on Twitter and Facebook. Prior to that, I was community manager for Social Media Camp Victoria, and editor/community manager of the Foodie.ca blog with 25 authors from across Canada writing for it. At Foodie.ca, my job was to promote the articles through Twitter, Facebook, and other social channels, and liaise with the Foodie.ca bloggers.
