I currently have 15+ years experience in implementing large scale internet solutions. I started as a software engineer and continue to co-create applications. My main focus now is on succesfully guiding projects towards succesful implementations. A larger part of my work is now done as a information analist, e-business consultant and manager at Morloff, a company I founded and own.

Morloff's portfolio includes eCommerce solutions, SEO optimizations, social communities and business process applications. It is my personal goal to have fun at what I do and to never let anyone tell me that something is impossible, before it has been tried.
