Daniel Truran's Summary

Passionate about empowering individuals to become protagonists by re-connecting them to their core values, to what is really important to them.
Inspiring them into action by linking them to other like-minded doers and to their ideas.
Creating through these connections - powerful and mindful actions - that allow them to transform organizations into purposeful, meaningful and successful entities.
Ultimately contributing to a prosperous, just and sustainable civilisation.

Specialties: creating and developing social innovation, networking and connecting, creating successful values-based enterprises, social entrepreneurship, event management, social media, communicating, keynoting, teaching at business schools.
