Alistair Croll's Summary

I'm an entrepreneur, author, and event organizer focused on emerging technologies and how they'll affect society.

I co-founded Year One Labs, which helps early-stage companies find their product and market alongside the other founding partners. Before Y1L, most of my startup work was with Rednod, a startup accelerator I founded in 2008.

I'm principal analyst for Bitcurrent, a producer of research and events relating to technology and humans. In 2010, Bitcurrent was acquired by CloudOps, where I also work as VP of product and marketing.

I've been directly involved in the launch of five major conferences: O'Reilly's Strata; Techweb's Cloud Connect; Interop's Enterprise Cloud Summit; the International Startup Festival; and GigaOm's Structure. I've spoken at a variety of other conferences, including Le Web, Web2Expo, IGT, Velocity, Enterprise 2.0, Mesh, and eMetrics.

I'm also the founder of Bitnorth, an annual camp-format conference on technology and society, and I help out with events like YULtide and Ignite Montreal. With Ian Rae, I run the RPM Montreal shared space where we hold events—such as hackathons and seminars—that grow the Montreal tech and startup community.

I've written or contributed to three books on technology: Web Operations (O'Reilly, 2010); Complete Web Monitoring (O'Reilly, 2009) and Managing Bandwidth (Prentice-Hall, 1999.) I've also contributed to a number of sites, including GigaOm, Mashable, and O'Reilly Radar. I write often -- but less than I'd like -- for Human 2.0, Bitcurrent, and Year One Labs.

Prior to Year One Labs and Bitcurrent, I co-founded Coradiant, an early pioneer in User Experience Management acquired by BMC in April, 2011. Coradiant grew from Networkshop, a company I founded with Eric Packman (a longtime friend and co-conspirator) in 1997. I've also worked for 3Com Corporation, Primary Access, and Eicon Technology.

I graduated from Dalhousie University with a B.Com (Honours) and an advanced major in Strategic Marketing.

Specialties

Product management, technology ventures, web performance and operations, high-availability applications, real user monitoring, web analytics, marketing communications, business strategy
