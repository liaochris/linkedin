Adam Loving's Summary

Adam is a software developer and entrepreneur with 16+ years professional expertise and a keen interest in social media. He has been a computer hacker since the 1980s, cited in Lotus Magazine in his early teens. Adam's passion for viral user experience design led him to create Twibes (currently ranked first in Google for “Twitter groups”) which launched to 40,000 DAUs in a weekend.

Adam's company Linksy was funded by TechStars in 2012. Prior to that he was senior developer at Gist and BigDoor. Adam is also the inventor of the Zuckerbuck (the first virtual currency on Facebook). His Facebook app "Dumb Americans" won an overnight coding contest at the CommunityNext conference in San Jose. He has also presented at several conferences on viral application design.

Adam got burnt out on programming briefly in 1996 and took a short 11 month trip around the world to visit 24 countries. From 2000-2003, he lived and worked in London as a software consultant before returning to his native Seattle.

Specialties: Ruby on Rails, CoffeeScript, JavaScript, Heroku, MySQL, Facebook Platform, Twitter API, Google App Engine.
