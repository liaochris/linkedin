Margo Carey's Summary

Margo Carey, owner of Carey Custom Computer Training, is a Computer Educator and Trainer specializing in Microsoft Office applications and related software applications for the workplace or the home. She also specializes in providing training on using the Internet, including conducting searches, using internet email, social networking, and creating your personal digital domain.

Margo Carey offers customized computer training for individuals, groups, and businesses. Training is provided in your home or in the workplace. MS Office training is tailored to your specific needs and is taught at your pace. If you are getting back into the work force or changing jobs, call us. We'll get you up to speed.

Carey is also a writer and author who makes her computer expertise available to the public through her monthly newsletter (Margo's Computer Tips), her newspaper column in the Planet North Shore (Computer Concerns? Ask Margo), and in her White Papers, which are archived on her Web site, www.CareyComputerTraining.com. Other publishing projects are in the works. She welcomes inquiries about writing for other publications.

Margo is available for speaking engagements -- as a panelist, breakout session or workshop leader, or keynote presenter.

Specialties

Training, writing or presenting about computer software or internet communication.
