Cameron Barrett's Summary

August 2013: Looking for a full-time opportunity, Director or VP of UX Design. Currently contracting.

--------------

• I was a Sr.-Level UX Design consultant at UBS Wealth Management Americas, where I was responsible for wireframes, user research, interface design and acted as a front-end design consultant with various development teams (internal and outsourced). [6-mo contract]

• I was part of the Engagement Technologies team at the World Economic Forum where I was Sr. Manager (UX Design) responsible for unifying the design of all screen-based properties from the web sites to the iOS apps.

• At NewsGator Technologies, based in Denver I designed and built social media web sites and tools for the U.S. Army (U.S.M.A. at West Point) and Fortune 500 companies. I joined ASW in April 2008 after years of consulting Fortune 500 companies in the realms of blogs, social media, and online community. My clients range from the large (Thomson Reuters, Hearst, Aetna) to the small (Bluefly, Snowboard Magazine). I specialize in integrating social media into existing online media properties. I also was part of the team that launched Borders.com, the e-commerce site for Borders Books & Music in 1998. I designed the first widely-adopted XUL-based skin/theme for the Mozilla browser and helped co-author the O’Reilly book “Creating Applications with Mozilla”.

In 2003 I led the development of online community software for the Wesley Clark for President campaign. While there I launched the Clark Community Network, an online community for supporters that gave every person who wanted on a blog (and thus, a voice in the campaign). This work foreshadowed the work done by the Obama team in 2007-2008 and the 2012 campaigns.

I also am considered a pioneer of the blog format, having defined the concept in 1999 in the groundbreaking essay “Anatomy of a Weblog” which has been published in several books about the history of blogs.

Specialties

More than 15 years experience with dozens of CMS packages, including Drupal, Movable Type, WordPress, Expression Engine, Blue Martini and many more. Expert in front-end development technologies, UX design and cross-team integration.
