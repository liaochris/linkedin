Kathy Hope's Summary

Insurance Advisors is a California based health insurance solutions provider. We represent all major insurance carriers and specialize in customizing plans to fit the needs, budget and philosophy of our clients.

For the past 15 years we have operated as Insurance Brokers. This means we work unrestricted and can keep "you" as our main objective.

What Is An Insurance Broker?
* We are not obligated to sell any one company's products.
* We represent a variety of insurance companies.
* We can select from many plans and options.
* We are not aligned with just one company.

You have a partnership with Insurance Advisors. Needs change and insurance plans change, so we "get to know" our clients in order to provide the best coverage for individual needs at all times.

We pride ourselves on our client "follow-up." I personally enjoy visiting my clients in person more than any other part of the business.

We encourage our clients to come to "us" for the answers to all questions, for the processing of individual claims, and for providing a complete overview and understanding of coverage options and plans.

Specialties

Group Insurance for Small to Medium Size Businesses and Organizations, Individual, Temporary, Medigap Insurance.
