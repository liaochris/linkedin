Leila Meyer's Summary

I'm a freelance writer based in south-central British Columbia. I write news and feature articles for trade magazines, consumer magazines, and online media. I specialize in writing about technology and education (and educational technology), although I enjoy writing about other topics as well.

In addition to articles, I write technical and business documents, including computer hardware and software manuals, online help, website content, training materials, and reports.

My background is in education and technology. I have a Bachelor of Education degree and was a grade six classroom teacher for a short time before changing careers to become a technical writer. I have written award-winning technical documentation and was President of the Alberta Chapter of the Society for Technical Communication.

Previous clients and employers have complimented me on my strengths:
• Attention to detail
• Ability to always finish work on time, even under a tight deadline
• Writing that is thoroughly researched, well organized, easily understood, clear, and concise
• Ability to understand complex information quickly and convey it to others in simple terms

Specialties:
• magazine writing (technology, education)
• technical writing (manuals, online help)
• business writing (training materials, reports, newsletters)
• editing (copy editing, proofreading)
