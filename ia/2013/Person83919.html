Mac Demere's Summary

Mac Demere is a freelance automotive writer, vehicle tester, driving instructor, accomplished race driver, 18-wheeler driver and performance driver for video productions.

He’s competed in NASCAR’s Southwest Tour and IMSA’s Rolex 24 at Daytona. He co-drove to five wins in 24 hour races and scored several other victories on road racing circuits, oval tracks and autocross courses.

He has organized, hosted and instructed at numerous consumer driving events.

He’s been an instructor for several racing schools and a driving coach for numerous racers and test engineers.
He holds a Class A Commercial Drivers’ License and has extensive over-the-road experience in 18 wheelers.

He has written for numerous newspapers, magazines and websites including Motor Trend, Automobile, Popular Mechanics, Stock Car Racing, edmunds.com, Kelley Blue Book, automedia.com,and Waterfowl & Wetlands.
He’s appeared on NBC’s Today Show, Motor Trend Television, Canadian Motoring, NPR, and numerous local TV affiliates.
