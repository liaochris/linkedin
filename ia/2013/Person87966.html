Mary A. Molloy (social@maryamolloy.com)'s Summary

I’m so happy to share my thoughts and ideas with you! My job is to help people live the life of their dreams – whether it’s their business life or it’s their personal life, my common sense approach delivers the results needed.I have worked with over 11,000 people of all ages in 39 countries to achieve their life dreams and their business goals.

Since I was young, I realized that I have several unique capabilities. For one thing, I can see the ‘big picture’ of virtually any situation or system. For another, I am very, very logical thinker and with a mathematical mind that thinks sequentially.That being said, however, I am also very creative and spiritual. Actually, I use my left brain just as much as I use my right brain. When all of this is added together, it gives me the ability to take complex things and explain them in such a way that they are understandable to everyone. After all, what is more complex than someone’s life or someone’s business (which might be their life’s blood)?

I have a proven track record of success in Business and Strategic Planning, and Life Coaching. I am the co-author of "The Buck Starts Here: Profit Based Sales and Marketing Made Easy", which won the Clarion Award for best non-fiction book. My new book' "Design Your Own Destiny" is now available at http://maryamolloy.com/.

    Authors: Mary A. Molloy (social@maryamolloy.com), Michael K. Molloy

    The book provides a common sense, logical, business methodology to Sales and Marketing. It provides useful tools that make a difference in marketing and selling a product or service from a business investment point of view.

    • TRB Press, US
    Authors: Mary A. Molloy (social@maryamolloy.com)

    What if you could have the life you always wanted? What if it wasn't as hard as you thought? What if instead of always asking What if? you decided to dive in and design the destiny of your dreams? That opportunity is here now in Design Your Own Destiny: Life Planning for the 21st Century.

    Whether it's looking for a job or trying to figure out the next steps to take in a relationship, award-winning author, speaker and life-planner Mary A. Combining years of experience working with Fortune 500 companies and clients all over the globe, Mary provides the answers to how people can make it happen. Examining the complete life with deeper connections Molloy helps people to:

    o Find work/life balance
    o Create tangible goals
    o Put plans into action
    o See results and measure success

    Whether it's changing roles, changing perspectives or challenging the status quo, Mary helps readers take control and not leave what is most important to chance. She shows readers step-by-step how to break down the complex process of changing one's life so that everyone gets a personalized and manageable way to make their dreams happen.

    Inspired by a talk she gave to twenty-five homeless women - many of whom took her Life Designs and were able to leave the shelter - Design Your Own Destiny will help you create the life you want to lead!
