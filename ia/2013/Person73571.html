Keith Barraclough's Summary

I'm a New York–based photographer specializing in portrait and lifestyle images for advertising, editorial and corporate clients.

Born in Bedford, England | Son of a Foreign Service Officer | Lived in many fascinating places | Studied Geology in college | Held a camera for the first time at age 19 | Changed my focus to photography | Have never looked back...﻿

I presently live in the SoHo district of New York City with my wife Kate.  I still travel to many fascinating places, have an ongoing interest in Geology and am constantly looking to the future.
