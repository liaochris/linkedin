Merrily Orsini, MSSW's Summary

A pioneer in geriatric care managed in-home care since 1981, Orsini leads a team at www.corecubed.com that specialized in digital marketing with a special niche in home care, hospice, private duty and aging care related services. Blending the traditional with the Internet since 1998, corecubed has designed interactive websites with search engines in mind, and is a leader in effective social media engagement. www.MOST4YourMarketing.com is a popular turn-key membership marketing program for home care, and Home Care Agency Marketplace has innovative products to start and grow home care businesses at MarketHomeCare.com.

Founder, President and CEO, Merrily Orsini, MSSW, is an award-winning entrepreneur with a background that combines skills in technology and people. She is a woman always ahead of her time. Read about Merrily's speaking engagements and her home care blog at www.merrilyorsini.com.

Specialties: consulting on trends and thought leadership in home care, integrating traditional with interactive content marketing for best results, digital marketing, home care marketing programs, eldercare consultative marketing, strategic marketing communications,
