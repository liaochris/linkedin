Greg Matinian's Summary

Greg Matinian is the Founder and CEO of Maxx Inc., the maker of FruitsMax (www.myfruitsmax.com)

FruitsMax was scientifically designed to make the taking of your daily amount of fruits easier, boost your immune system and promote the digestive health. By combining 21 fruits (prebiotics) and a specific strain of good microbes called "Narine" (probiotics), FruitsMax is a perfect SYNBIOTIC product that delivers demonstrable results.
