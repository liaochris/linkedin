Betsy Rosenblatt Rosso's Summary

Rosso Writing helps organizations tell their stories so the people and communities they serve can thrive.

We believe good writing is good writing, no matter what form it takes. We ply our editorial expertise across digital platforms such as websites, e-newsletters, and social media. We work well with traditional print media such as annual reports, brochures, and white papers. If you need to rejuvenate your communications efforts, we conduct communications audits, create communications plans, and develop messages customized to your target audiences.

When you work with Rosso Writing, you can expect
• To be listened to and understood
• Responsiveness and efficiency
• Transformation of jargon and messy language into clear, compelling prose

Specialties

We are great listeners and will take the time to understand what you do and why it matters so we can help tell your story in a clear, effective way that anyone can appreciate.

Rosso Writing offers a wide variety of writing and editing services, including, but not limited to

• Web copy
• Social media
• Programs
• Profiles, bios, case studies
• Print and e-newsletters
• Op-eds, letters to the editor
• Messages, talking points
• Communications audits/plans
• Brochures
• Annual/special reports
