Glenn Hanon GRI CRS's Summary

I began my career in Real Estate at a very young age. My grandfather was a Realtor, as is my father. I began by cleaning the office, then moved to re-filing the old MLS sheets (long before computers) then eventually obtained my Real Estate license when I was still in High School. I took a brief hiatus from real estate and worked for a company in New York and was transferred to Chicago. I moved to Oconomowoc where I began working for a real estate developer. After the project was finished I moved my career to Wauwatosa Realty in 1991. I have worked for Shorewest (the new name) Realtors now since then and have been delighted to work with such a great company. During my time here at Shorewest I have had the privilege of training over 600 agents on what it means to be “in business” in real estate. I have been asked to speak to several other companies outside of our state as well which is always a great honor. My marketing degree from UW-Milwaukee has been a great help as I think of different innovative ways to market homes for my clients. I think of myself as progressive when it comes to advertising and marketing, the 888-HEY GLEN toll free number and branding of “HEY GLEN” has worked very well in the area that I work in. I believe I have one of the best track records for selling homes in our area—98.5% of the homes I list succeed in selling. In 2006 I had a great opportunity to be the agent in an episode of HGTV’s HOUSE HUNTERS. During the taping of the show I injured my right hand and it was in a large wrapped bandage. We had to try and work around the tapped appendage—I thought for sure they would dump this episode, but it aired. I received lots of phone calls of friends kidding me about the hand. Oh well..
I love helping people achieve their dreams. I hold several professional designations such as; GRI, CRS, SFR & CSRS. This along with many Shorewest achievements I continue to look for ways to improve and learn new facets of our industry.

Specialties

Real Estate Marketing, Real Estate Sales Training, Negotiation Training, Business Planning, Business Forecasting
