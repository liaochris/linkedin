Anita Nowacka's Summary

I specialize in unique family portraits in Seattle, WA area, as well as in pregnancy portraits, wedding photography and life celebrations. I also have extensive experience in creating customized portraits for all business and social media needs.

For those interested in bringing their photography or visual skills to another level, I offer personalized photography instructions, coaching in many photography areas (creating better/ stronger images). Each coaching session is tailored to client's individual needs, requests.
