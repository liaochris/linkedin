Dave Schafer's Summary

I help corporations find their voice.

Over 10 years of writing professionally, I've honed my internal communications skills while writing newsletters, websites, scripts, benefits communications, corporate emails, fliers, CEO letters and more.

I've also written materials focusing on external audiences, including marketing campaigns, websites, press releases, media alerts, solicitation brochures, recruiting brochures and more.

But I got my start in newspaper journalism, first covering police and courts for a newspaper in Ohio. Since, I've continued to write for newspapers and magazines, providing in-depth features.

I'm looking for internal communications, commercial copywriting, newsletter and freelance writing and editing work.

Specialties

Internal communications, newsletters, business communications, websites, press releases, feature stories
