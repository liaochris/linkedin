JaJuan Burgess' Summary

I have been in the culinary, hospitality and entertainment industries for a total of 16 years combined, which aided the launch of Burgess Ventures.

My experience ranges from food production in commercial kitchens to entertainment promoting, planning and designing in/outdoor events at boutique hotels, conference and meeting planning, maitre d' at an upscale restaurant and a travel & leisure concierge for exclusive clients and corporations.

Our mission is to pamper our clients, while accommodating their needs and providing the best experience the hospitality and service industry has to offer. We vow to produce the best events imaginable, and to set a new standard with each venture set forth.
