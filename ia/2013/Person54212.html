Ian Alderton's Summary

A highly inspirational, decisive and commercially focused International CIO with over 22 years significant information technology experience across Global Rates, Equities, Commodities and Treasury.

Extensive breadth of financial knowledge includes Investment Banking, Asset Management, Wholesale Banking and Payments.

Outstanding track record of strategic leadership and visionary expertise, managing complex business change to support aggressive business expansion into new markets and geographies.
