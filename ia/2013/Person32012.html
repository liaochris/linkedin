David J Flynn DDS's Summary

David J. Flynn, DDS, of Staten Island, New York, advocates for autistic children. As a Board Member for Grace Foundation of New York, David J. Flynn, DDS helps provide social services, recreation, resource coordination, and other services to families affected by this developmental disorder. David J. Flynn, DDS also contributes to Autism Speaks, Inc., the No. 1 autism research and advocacy group in the United States. The organization, which organizes walks and other fundraisers, spreads autism awareness and serves as a network of families with autistic children. David J. Flynn, DDS's more than 10-year-old practice specializes in endodontics, oral surgery, orthodontics, teeth whitening, and BOTOX Cosmetic. Before he opened his general dentistry clinic, he studied at the New York University College of Dentistry, where he placed on the dean's list and received his doctorate in 1990. David J. Flynn, DDS belongs to the American Dental Association.
