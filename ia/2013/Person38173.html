  • Integrating People with Process and Technology "Gaining Employee Acceptance of Technology Initiatives

    • The Anton Press
    Authors: Dr. Natalie Petouhoff

    The goal of any business is to be profitable. Analyzing budgets to eliminate frivolous spending of company dollars is a preoccupation of every C-level executive. Mindful CFOs want a positive return on investment (ROI), especially for implementing and using new technology. While technology vendors advertise they can get you ROI, money is frequently spent without actually calculating the ROI.

    And the intangible or "hidden" costs associated with change are generally not considered as part of the ROI calculation. Often items like training are often stripped from the budget the notion that "soft" skills are optional.

    New technology can add money to your bottom line, but only if you know the critical elements— people, process & technology— must be integrated to obtain an optimum ROI. "In a study of 100 large companies, only 37% could point to a tangible financial impact for technology solutions."

    ROI issues often arise when change management is not figured as part of the overall technology project. When technology implementations include change management, it becomes an exhilarating experience that improves the efficiency & effectiveness of the company.

    The good news is that there are proven, effective ways to make your technology implementation successful. This book provides the critical steps & by reading it you will understand:

    • The top technology implementation mistakes / why technology doesn’t always deliver what it promised

    • How to dramatically increase implementation success a scientifically proven process: Successful People Process™

    3. How to turn de-motivated employees into company ambassadors by reducing their Reptile Talk™ (negative verbalization)

    4. A simple way to calculate the ROI of "soft skills" to justify training & motivational activities

    5. How to integrate people, process & technology to be within budget, scope, and on time, leading to larger technology ROI’s & increased customer lifetime value that yields larger profits & revenues.
