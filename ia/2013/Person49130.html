Global Business Services JLT's Summary

Global Business Services (GBS) is a professional organization that offers Advisory services, Commercial services, Accountancy services, Company Formation services and Company Incorporation services in tax efficient offshore jurisdictions around the world. Global Business Services also provides assistance on Trade Mark and IP Registrations and assists company management and compliance.

Global Business Services is an offshore service provider involved in offshore company formation as well as offshore company incorporation. It endeavors to provide enterprising solutions in identifying the right destination to setting up the company as well as forming an offshore company. Global Business Services present expert understanding connected with the setting up of a successful business.

Our team of professionals includes experts in the disciplines of finance, law and international business. We will be able to assist you in the following areas:

• Offshore Company Formation in all Major Jurisdictions
• Company Management and Administration
• Corporate Structuring and Re-Structuring
• Branch Registration of Foreign Corporations
• Establishments of Trusts and Foundations
• Trusteeship and provision of Foundation Councils
• Corporate Tax Planning
• Compliance and Risk Assessment
• Assistance in Trade Mark & IP registrations
• Accounting & book keeping services

With our vast experience, GBS is not limited to registration and forming of offshore companies. We also provide all the support subsequent to the business set-up; essential for the smooth operation and maintenance of offshore companies apart from other professional services like accounting, auditing and consultancy through it’s associate firms.
