Brent Singer's Summary

Previously the Altera ANZ Distribution Product Manager @ Braemac, I'm now starting a Design and Manufacture enterprise with the goal of producing and marketing a low cost FPGA System On Module.

I am leveraging many years of mixed signal, high speed and embedded design experience to bring our first product - the helix_4 FPGA Module - to market. I am responsible for all planning, marketing and raising funds for the venture. Yes... I'm really busy.

Specialties include a spectrum of General Embedded development, but if I was asked to simplify it to two things I'm really good at: 1) Designing complex, embedded circuit board hardware, 2) Industrial display technology and it's associated video processing.

Brent Singer's Experience

Principal Engineer

Thin Layer Embedded

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry

December 2012 – Present (10 months) Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia

Thin Layer was established to design and manufacture Embedded FPGA Modules, that specifically target 'point of entry' markets, i.e. Engineers, Students and Enthusiasts who are learning the manifold benefits of Programmable Logic. With a fantastic 'out of the box' development experience of diverse development tools and robust software support resources, Thin Layer aims to abstract the mundane reference design copying that we all hate; letting you focus only on the differentiation parts of a product.

My duties include design and support of development systems, applications engineering, pre-sales and support at any stage of a customers product development life-cycle.

Altera Product Manager

Braemac PL

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry

October 2010 – November 2012 (2 years 2 months) Alexandria, Sydney, Australia

Braemac is Australia’s largest electronic component distributor with offices throughout Australia, New Zealand, the United States, Singapore, Hong Kong and the UK. Founded in Sydney in 1986, Braemac has maintained substantial growth over the past 20 years and today employs over 100 staff in Australia and New Zealand. My work at Braemac can be summarized as: "one arm around the customer, the other around the vendor".

My duties involved close work with Altera Corp in qualification of new FPGA opportunities, establishing customer pricing and managing their support through the complete design cycle. This meant a laborious pre-sales information gathering exercise to understand the customer, their products, personnel, target market and place in it. This information would demonstrate to Altera Corp that Braemac were servicing ANZ market opportunities with consistency and high quality.
