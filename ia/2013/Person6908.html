Andrew Friesner's Summary

As a broker and agency owner of Chaser Insurance Group I focus specifically on the individual health market, developing strategy through new and typically unrealized opportunities for personal and business clients alike. Always having been an entrepreneur, I have developed and changed the strategies to address the changing healthcare market and how Chaser Insurance Group fits into improving healthcare for our clients. Through a specialization and strategy shift to use technology to market and aid in more efficient distribution we created and filled a niche as a "web broker", while building a higher level of advisory service in the individual health and Medicare market. Through InsuranceChaser.com our private health exchange, we become the 3rd largest new business producing individual health insurance agency with Anthem BCBS of Ohio in just 2 years from our formation in 2006. We have continued to keep at the forefront of health reform by not only being one of the first private health exchanges in Ohio, but also by taking the necessary steps to change with the market and changes strategies to keep up with the implementation of Affordable Care Act. We are now able to provide federally subsidized coverage through the public exchange. We hold many carrier General Agency and direct contracts with carriers like:

•Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield / WellPoint
•United Healthcare / Golden Rule (AARP)
•Aetna Health Insurance Plans
•Humana Health Insurance Plans
•Medical Mutual of Ohio

Continuing to embrace change and develop, we have more recently began to focus on wellness integration and to working with the independent broker community to build new partner resources and opportunities.
