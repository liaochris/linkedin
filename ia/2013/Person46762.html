Greg Johns' Summary

Greg has over 15 years experience in the field of interactive design and development. Championing the user experience in a wide range of interactive and online systems including; various internet sites, online billing systems, administrative systems, digital learning, mobile applications and most recently consumer television.

Greg has very strong creative and problem solving skills. His main roles in his work has been to bridge creative UX solutions with technology and has many patents pending relating to UX.

Specialties

Intellectual Property, patents, R&D, strategy, prototyping, proof of concept, IPTV, media convergence, gaming, object oriented design, graphic design, computational design art, team leadership
