Willam Henson's Summary

Hi,
I'm William Henson, owner of Alternative Pain Management- massage & neuromuscular therapy.
I specialize in dealing with clients that come in with chronic muscular pain by using neuromuscular techniques to treat the source of their pain rather than the symptoms. I have had the advantage of working under professional therapists with over 15+years experience in the field, including the President of the American Massage Therapy Association (AL chapter).
