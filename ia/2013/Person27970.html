Richard Young's Summary

Previously I've advised companies from large blue chip household names all the way down to SMB outfits. This has included advising on the strategic direction of the companies CRM & ITSM policies and often expanding into a consultation around generic IT technologies applied to the sales, marketing and operational requirements.

Here is a list of a few companies I've worked with:
HP, American Express, Bank of America,
The Economist Group, WPP Group, United Utilities,
Wolters Kluwer, Health Protection Agency (HPA),LEX Vehicle Leasing
ING Lease, The Met Office, Portakabin
Fish4, Leatherhead Food & Research


I've established 3 start-ups, 2 are still going concerns and 1 was sold and 1 launched on the FTSE AIM index.

The awards won by these companies whilst I've been the Managing Director include: Global CRM Partner of the Year, International Partner of the Year, Service Management (ITSM) Partner of the Year, Technical Innovator of the Year, consistently being a top producer (top 3) for FrontRange Solutions as well as being a top tier partner for Sage Group.

Specialties: Helping SMB establish in the UK as well as working with Enterprise and Mid-Market organizations to improve sales, customer retention and customer service.

Blend of business and technology orientated perspective for problem resolution.

Technology Innovations and applied technology to the business environment with an eye on commercials. Problem solving and strategy leadership.
