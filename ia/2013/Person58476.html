Lead Organizer (Volunteer)

YYJ Twestival - Twestival Victoria

July 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months) Victoria, BC

YYJ Twestival (Twestival Victoria) is part of the worldwide Twitter fundraiser movement Twestival founded by Amanda Rose in late 2008.

The first Twestival Global was held in January 2009 in 202 cities worldwide and raised over $250,000 for charity:water to fund well drilling projects in the third world.

In September 2009, Twestival went local, and Victoria signed on to be one of 130 cities raising money for local charities. Victoria's charity, chosen by the community of Twitter users, was Power to Be Adventure Therapy Society. Collectively we raised almost $5000 in a short six weeks.

In March 2010 we were part of the Twestival Global movement once again and raised over $8000 for Concern Worldwide. Of over 200 cities, Victoria ranked 8th in fundraising per capita and 16th in total fundraising.

2011 was a Twestival Local year. On March 24th we came together at the Bengal Lounge, Empress Hotel, to support the Victoria Cool Aid Society http://www.coolaid.org. We raised $7700 and were 17th worldwide in total funds raised, 8th per capita worldwide.

Join us September 24th 2013 - once again at the Bengal Lounge - as we raise funds for The Cridge Centre.
