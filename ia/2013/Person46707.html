Gail Ahlers' Summary

Ahlers Designs creates artwork, unique gifts and custom awards for people and companies that invest in their relationships. From concept to presentation we tailor each project to fit your mission and industry.

Let Ahlers Designs create an unforgettable custom piece for your company. One of a kind, or items produced in quantity . Keepsakes or awards of quality and style.

Some of our Custom Corporate Gifts & Awards Clients: Pepsico, Teknor Apex, Lotus, Rhode Island School of Design and the Weisman Art Museum.

Fund-raising- We have designed items for schools and non profits like Habitat for Humanity and Health Care for the Homeless.

We work with our customers to make unique corporate gifts that stand out and are remembered.

Ahlers Designs is a certified Minority Female Owned Business, producing handcrafted products since 1989 #WBENC #NGLCC

Specialties: Designs and creates custom gifts, awards or fine jewelry for people or companies that invest in their relationships, foundation donors gifts, speaker gifts, employee recognition, volunteer gifts, unique golf tournament gifts, private label, made in the USA,
