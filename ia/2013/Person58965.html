Jasmine Vesque's Summary

I am a natural-born communicator who enjoys helping people share their most important stories and ideas. I am passionate about digital communications, social media, and web design.

I currently work as the Communications Marketing Manager for the Nedlaw Group of Companies where I do everything from build websites in WordPress and social media management to content creation and public relations.

I also volunteer as the VP of Communications and Webmaster for a new non-profit organization, The Forget for a Moment Foundation. I manage the websites I've built, our social media platforms, and help spread the word about our project.

I love teaching, so I am also a WordCamp Ottawa co-organizer and speaker. Giving back to the WordPress community is important to me and helps me hone my skills.

I love meeting new people, so reach out if you want to talk technology, WordPress, Social Media, or not-for-profit.

Specialties: WordPress, Social Media, Digital Communications, Graphic Design, Teaching, Public Speaking, XHTML and CSS, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, and Adobe Acrobat Pro.
