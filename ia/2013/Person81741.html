Lisa Wheelous' Summary

My success in the past has stemmed from my strong commitment and sense of professionalism. I keep high standards for my work and am known for my ability to follow through. I am very outgoing and I work well with all personality types. I completed my training and internship in The Professional Film and Television Training Program at Chattanooga State in December 2010 and I received my certification as a location scout and as a location manager. In 2010 and 2011 I was a liaison for the Chattanooga South East Tennessee Film Commission. I love all aspects of film making and desire to learn as much as possible.
