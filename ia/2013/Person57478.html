James Grima (LION 24,589+ Connections)'s Summary

A highly connected LinkedIn User, as an Entrepreneur, I have amassed over 20 years of hands-on successful multi-unit food retailing experience opening my first retail pizza outlet at the age of 19. I cultivated the company into a chain of 12 stores, employing more than 500 staff, producing over 2 million pizzas a year. I franchised the Pizza Lovers brand in 1999, which was acquired by Domino’s Pizza five years later.

I then worked in a corporate role as Franchise Consultant for Domino’s Pizza traveling the country, applying my business and marketing skills. As Manager, Victorian Operations, I was in charge of a 30 million dollar market and was affectionately known as “Mr Victoria.”

Now, as Founder and CEO of Positive Training Solutions, qualified as both a trainer and assessor, we have trained 12,000+ food retail employees including staff from EVERY major food retail brand. Conceived in response to a gap in the market, Positive Training Solutions focuses singularly on Food Retail Franchising. Since 2004, Positive Training Solutions has had triple digit growth each year for the last 4 years and placed 16th on the 2012 BRW Fast 100 list expanding from one client to 1300+ key clients, earning those clients a colossal $50 million dollars in Government Training Incentives. I continue to push the envelope of the value proposition that I offer my clients with an unwavering entrepreneurial zest and enthusiasm that is both infectious and well, yes, positive!

Positive Training has been able to provide much needed funding to employers immediately by way of Government incentives and long term by way of staff training to improve the overall operation of the business and lowering staff turnover.
