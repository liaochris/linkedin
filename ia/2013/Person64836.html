Joan Black's Summary

Joan Black is the chief communication strategist, founder, and managing partner of The Creative Octopus, LLC, a licensed business in Nevada providing a diverse range of communication and professional writing services. The company develops website content, marketing materials and technical documentation that will reach your target audience and generate leads. We specialize in writing services and content that optimizes business development including advertisements, articles, brochures, biographies, blogs, catalogs, grants, manuals, marketing plans, newsletters, press releases, proposals, SEO copywriting, technical reports, website content and more. We also assist in preparing business documentation, negotiation letters and narratives for 10Q filings.

We offer excellent quality editing and proofreading services. We edit books, technical papers, novels and can assist in organizing information for applications. Providing exceptional customer service is our highest priority.

The Creative Octopus also provides grant development guidance and expertise, As a grant resource center, we determine clients' funding needs, ensure clients are qualified for grants applications, search for grant opportunities to match those needs, prepare and oversee grant submission, and deliver summary reports to clients. We track and prioritize grant submission deadlines, and develop well-written grants. If a grant award is received, we track and manage reporting of grants to the grantor to ensure the grantee remains in compliance with grant requirements.

Business plan development takes on an interactive approach with clients. Through an in-depth process, the final product becomes a road map and useful tool that is an asset to the business.
