Markus Lampinen's Summary

Markus has founded or been an early shareholder in 10 innovative growth companies in dynamic industries such as venture capital, advertising, licensing, distribution and digital music. He also holds advisory roles in high growth companies and organizations. Markus has personally successfully recruited over 120 people and overseen three mergers and acquisitions.

Markus serves as the CEO of Crowd Valley Inc, a US based marketplace platform provider and spinoff from Grow VC, where Markus previous served as COO. He is a global investor and senior partner at the Grow VC group. As COO of Grow VC, he recruited over 100 individuals, built up a global team on six continents and expanded operations to over 150 countries. In his earlier businesses, he has focused on responsibilities of CFO and e.g. been responsible for growth of over 270% per year, for several subsequent years.

Markus has pioneered new funding models in the US and Europe, advised policy makers (SEC, European Commission, Consob) and worked with actors in both the private and public sector (the World Bank, Kauffman), to develop the right framework for new funding models, including crowdfunding, cross border investments and private placements. He serves as a frequent public speaker on related themes.

Markus is an internationally awarded entrepreneur and part of the Sandbox Network, the foremost global community of extraordinary young achievers.

Culturally diverse background from the US, Sweden, Spain and Finland, analytical being and enthusiasm to match, Markus loves working on high impact strategies, but also being hands on in ensuring progress is made. Right at home in the high pace, competitive global world, he is particularly fascinated by challenges and opportunities, that are just looking for the taker.

I love moving forward and being a part of something "big". Learning and developing, both within a professional context and as a human being, is something I look for in my life, as well as my career.
