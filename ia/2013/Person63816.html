Jim Crenshaw's Summary

A driven individual with a passion for social media, brand reputation, social analysis, and digital marketing. My career as an eCommerce Account Manager and freelance Web Developer has provided me with a complex understanding of how functionality influences content strategies for new and influential social networks. Strategy in social marketing, among many things - is an important factor because a specific social network may not gel with the goals and content of a particular brand.

The most important part of developing a social media strategy is to understand the importance of brand management and online reputation. A social network should be an extension of the values that are inherent in a brand/company. The goal for a brand should be to choose the best social networks that allows their content to be consumed by who wants it most!

My Master Degree in Media Psychology has provided me with extensive knowledge related to how individuals are influenced by media across a variety of genres and mediums. Media psychology seeks to understand how the media and the growing use of technology impacts how people perceive, interpret, respond, and interacts in a media rich world.

Specialties:
Statistical Package for the Social Sciences (SPSS), Social Media Marketing & Analysis, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Web Traffic Analysis, Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
