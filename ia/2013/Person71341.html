Justin Priddy's Summary

While attending Cal State University Fullerton where he studied business management, Justin got his start as an autograph authenticator for a the nationally recognized authentication company Global Authentication based in Southern California. Starting in October of 2003, he would soon become the company’s Senior Authenticator and later the Director of Authentication for their memorabilia division. Overseeing the certification of over 500,000 items submitted for third party verification and signed in the presence signatures during his tenure, Justin took the memorabilia division from just over $12,000 in yearly revenue to over $250,000 in his first year and steadily increasing in subsequent years to over $400,000.

In 2006 when HBO Real Sports conducted an investigative report on autograph authentication companies, Justin was one of only two national authenticators that correctly identified the fraudulent signatures when they were submitted for review. As a featured columnist in the hobby’s oldest publication, the Sports Collectors Digest, Justin has authored several signature studies, including: Joe DiMaggio, Mickey Mantle, Babe Ruth, Lou Gehrig, Cal Ripken Jr., and Joe Jackson. After leaving his position of Senior Authenticator, shortly after Independence Day in 2007, he briefly continued as a consultant through the start of September attending the last four trade shows the company would attend in the calendar year. He currently serves as the moderator for the GameUsedUniverse.com Autograph Forum, one of the industry’s principle message boards designed toward memorabilia. With the formation of Autograph Certification Experts, LLC (ACE) in 2008, Justin brings an ecumenical acceptance as one of the industry’s top experts in autographs and is well established as one of the most consistent and accurate authenticators in the autograph industry. Along with his wealth of knowledge Justin also brings one of the largest autograph exemplar libraries to the ACE brand.
