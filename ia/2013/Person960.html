Abraham Jankans' Summary

Abraham Jankans is the CEO and a co-founder of Launchpad a cloud technology company serving public and non-profit sectors. A serial entrepreneur, he brings over ten years of private- and public-sector workforce development, human capital, job creation and technology. Jankans is dedicated to finding innovative and creative solutions to address the challenges of workforce development, unemployment and education, and promote self-sufficiency.

Prior to establishing LaunchPad Careers in 2005, Jankans spent several successful years as a sales and operations manager and recruiter for RemedyTemp, Inc. During his tenure, he worked with hundreds of companies, from real estate and financial firms to technology startups and nonprofits.

Jankans is actively involved in the community, working with organizations such as Women Helping Women, Goodwill of Orange County and the Community Action Partnership of Orange County to build better communities. His leadership has won him recognition in Orange County. In February 2009, Jankans received the Gala Award for Distinguished Contributions in Community Service & Government Relations from the 100 Black Men of Orange County and the Annual Spirit of Volunteerism Award from the Volunteer Center of Orange County.

Specialties: Workforce Development, Social Entrepreneurship, Job Readiness, Staffing, Training, Public Speaking.
