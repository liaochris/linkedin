Kristina Gardin's Summary

8th Wave provides outstanding and customized regional Event Management and Destination services.

Our region wide experience and expertise gives us the flexibility and flair to create a truly memorable occasion for your next corporate event, incentive, regional conference, launch or client hospitality event.

Increasingly, CSR initiatives are also promoted as an integral part of corporate communications and events created by 8th Wave.

At 8th Wave, we believe in experiences. It is through understanding your brand and brand message, your objectives, audience and desired outcomes that an exceptional events solution can be designed. We look forward to working with you to create your solutions.

Specialties

Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Events and Hospitality for Corporates.

Our full services include concept and program development, brand and media management, hospitality, venue selection, entertainment, incentives, conferences, summits, awards shows, teambuilding, pre and post production and full logistics. A selection of yachts and unique venues add to your choices.
