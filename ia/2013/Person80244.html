Leora Wenger's Summary

Designed websites for businesses and organizations that have helped those businesses grow. Customers love the speed with which I get their information online. All my sites are well-organized and user-friendly with visually-appealing designs. WordPress is a specialty. Responsive web design (getting your website to work on mobile) is a featured skill. Will help you integrate a social media strategy with your website.

Specialties: web design, web programming, responsive web design, on-page search engine optimization, WordPress customization, database programming, site promotion, search engine keywords, PHP, MySQL, Drupal, Drupal 6 to 7 conversion, social media training, social media lectures
