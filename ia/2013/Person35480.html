Diego Saenz's Summary

Over the last 20+ years Diego has worked as a management consultant with Accenture, as a Global Fortune 500 technology executive and a successful Internet entrepreneur. Diego has been successful working in multi-million companies with 10 employees and multi-billion companies with 50,000 employees.  

As an entrepreneur Diego built two successful Internet companies. First he lead a technology startup from the early stages to profitability to rapid growth. In 2010 this start-up was recognized by Inc Magazine as one of the 500 fastest growing private companies in America. Diego and his team also built a second Internet company than captured 10% market share in 36 months and was then sold via a successful exit to a large Swiss Pharmaceutical Company.  

As a consultant with Accenture Diego consulted on large strategic technology initiatives with a number of Fortune 500 clients including Burger King, Knight Rider, PepsiCo and Great Western Financial Services.

As a senior technology executive (CIO ), Diego headed Pepsi's Technology group for all of Latin America. In this role I oversaw large-scale technology projects Marketing, Sales, Logistics, Distribution throughout South America, Central America and the Caribbean. I also lead Pepsi’s International Bottling Systems Group whose mission was to develop the next generation Sales and Distribution system. 

Diego loves building companies, being part of high performance management teams, mentoring people and using technology to innovate and drive profitable business growth.
