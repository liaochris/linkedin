Mike Norris' Summary

Mike’s career as a leader in professional services and as a corporate senior executive has spanned all aspects of business strategy and operations. His experience has included global B2B and B2C work in strategy, capital formation, business development, marketing, public/investor relations, media, advertising, social networking/media, information technology, R&D, CRM, supply chain management, and operations improvement.

His expertise covers a wide range of industries including: media, advertising, information technology, biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, healthcare, software, retail, consumer electronics, higher education, chemicals, agriculture, discrete manufacturing, process manufacturing, retail, direct mail, real estate, financial institutions, insurance, media distribution, control systems, heavy equipment manufacturing, transportation, defense, oil, gas, energy, and utilities.

His marketing work has spanned media, segmentation strategy, research, predictive modeling, cross-channel optimization, program design/implementation, interactive strategy, and interactive design/implementation across both digital and traditional media.

From his work with Microsoft he has become a recognized expert in the development of B2B social networking programs and executive relationship programs. Through his collective professional services career he has served as account lead on Microsoft for over 6 years. His work on executive and influencer multi-channel engagement strategies and marketing programs has won his clients numerous internal and external awards.
