Daan Duijm's Summary

Daan Duijm currently owns and is a web designer for Daan Design and excellent Webdesign. The information below gives you an impression of Daan, and his talents which you might use in the future.

Via Daan Design (founded at age 20) unique and stylish websites are designed for a broad audience . eXcellent Webdesign specializes in high quality websites, including Search Engine Optimization and management systems.

After Junior general secondary education and the Higher General Secondary Education, Daan successfully graduated in 2009 of the two year (fast track) course of CIOS Goes(a sports education) . With the majors: Sport Coordinator Uniform Professions ( SCUP) & Swimming and various minors such as : A + B Swim Instructor , Fitness Instructor and Jogging group Trainer A . Internships and work experience include : Dutch Marine Corps , FitPlus , Swimming pool “De schelp” in Bergen op Zoom, SPADO Bergen op Zoom , Arendse Health Club & Fitness Palace Breda . Daan Duijm also did courses in First Aid and Rescue swimming.

In 2009, Daan started the Bachelor of Tourism and Recreation ( FBTR ) on the NHTV in Breda . Currently, Daan Duijm finished his Associate Degree Major: Recreation Manager in 2011. In his first year of study he won the province and overall Creativity award in the management game, played by all 400 first year students. He graduated with great results in many different subjects.

Currently he is working abroad on a Disney Cruise Line Cruiseship as a Fitness Director.
