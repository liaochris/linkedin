My experience in analytics, community management nuances and media trends -- coupled with keen attention to opportunities for clients -- helps me to create better organizations.

Matt Kelly, MA's Publications

  • Fortune 500 Foray: How the Nation’s Largest Insurer Leverages Social Media

    • Public Relations Journal
    • August 15, 2012
    Authors: Matt Kelly, MA, Dustin W. Supa, Ph.D.

    The challenge of social media presented State Farm, the nation's largest insurer, with a unique opportunity. Could a company with more than 68,000 employees, in an industry that is heavily regulated, adopt technology as quickly and effectively as other companies? Would being a recognized leader in the field be enough for the industry giant to ensure success in a user-generated platform? The goal of this study is not to enlighten those who wish discover a best practice in the adoption of social media into a corporation, but rather to tell the story of how State Farm has
    overcome the obstacles presented in establishing its own social media identity. The purpose of this study is to examine the steps the insurance industry’s leader took as it first became involved in social media, and perhaps to showcase how a large, heavily regulated industry leader adapted and adopted the new platform in a way that could provide a benchmark to the
    insurance and financial services industries.

  • Re-Branding the Community Manager: 7 Skills of a Community Director

    • Advertising Week's Social Club
    • February 22, 2013
    Authors: Matt Kelly, MA, Rachel Caggiano

    From the article, co-authored with Rachel Caggiano: "Not too long ago, the role of “community manager” might have been relegated to an intern. Or, the role was added to the list of tasks juggled by an inexperienced social media manager (who was also expected to answer the phones and send out the email newsletters). But today, the profession is being seen as one of the most important roles in social business, and smart brands are looking for experienced business directors with deep expertise in content marketing, digital strategy, public affairs and crisis management."
