Jonas Franklin's Summary

I am 32 years old and my origin is from Sweden, Gothenburg. Since May 2013 I live and work at LBi in Stockholm.

In 2007, I joined LBi as an interface developer. After 3.5 years I started to work as a Web Analyst and SEO consultant.

Today I deal on a daily basis with large analytics accounts for customers like Nobel Biocare, GE Moneybank, Volvo Trucks, AB Volvo and Gina Tricot.

I am certified in Google Analytics and have extensive experience in both Google Analytics and Adobe Omniture.

My work today consists of helping our clients with KPI frameworks, implementing clickstream data tools such as Adobe Omniture SiteCatalyst and Google Analytics (Both as an implementation guide and hands on adding tracking code to their web sites), analysis of statistics and creation of reports and dashboard.

I use Microsoft Excel on a daily basis to pull data from Google Analytics and Omniture SiteCatalyst to generate reports.

I have experiences in Volvo Trucks and Cardo International's major SEO projects. In these projects, among other things I was involved in research, analysis of current situation and preparation of recommendations. I have also developed global instructions and tools for Marketing Managers so that they can locally optimize their sites.

I started building web sites 1995 and have a long experience of HTML, JavaScript and CSS.

Finally, I also have experience in working with Google Webmaster tools, various aspects of social media on behalf of clients, and Social Media monitoring tools like Sysomos and Radian6.
