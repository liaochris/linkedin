Dave Mitchell's Summary

The problem is simple: You created a Linkedin profile but you are not getting enough leads or sales. You type in relevant keywords about your business on Google and you are not on the 1st page. Did you know 70% of Google searchers don’t scroll past the first page of a search? The importance of Google Ranking should be clear. You want to be found at the common meeting point … page one of a Google search.

What if your LinkedIn page ranked on page 1 of Google for "patent attorney chicago" or "acupuncture dallas texas" or "tax accountant miami"? Do you think you would start to generate some revenue or make more sales? You better believe it.

Try Googling: "Rank LinkedIn Profiles in Google" and see who shows up at the top. Me! It's no coincidence.

I am the founder of Thriveonpage1.com, an internet marketing firm specializing in increasing search engine rankings. At Thriveonpage1 we use a variety of on-page and off-page search engine optimization techniques to increase our clients' LinkedIn profile rankings in Google and other search engines.

1. Sign up – The membership is managed through your PayPal account, which you can cancel at anytime.

2. Identify your keyword phrase – First you determine which keyword phrase to rank for. The keyword phrase should be specific, relevant to your industry and something your potential clients would type into Google when searching for your services. Examples include: “Rank LinkedIn Profile Google”, “Tax Accountant Kansas City”, “Patent attorney Palo Alto“, “Atlanta chiropractor back pain“, etc.

3. Optimize your profile – Next, we provide you instructions on how to optimize your LinkedIn profile for ranking in Google.

4. We go to work – First we review your profile to confirm it meets our ranking standards and then we kick of a SEO back linking campaign utilizing our private blog network. You will be emailed a progress report every month.

I look forward to connecting and helping you get found!
