Dana Wilde (Direct Sales Speaker)'s Summary

Dana Wilde, is a brain trainer, mantra mentor, direct sales, network marketing, home party plan superstar, and founder of The Mind Aware, a company providing trainings and support for direct sales, home party-plan entrepreneurs who don’t want to sacrifice more time in order to become successful.

She’s the creator of Train Your Brain, a unique and highly successful training method designed to make things easier for network marketers and fast track success through proven mental strategies.

Train Your Brain’s techniques propelled her own network marketing team to record-breaking status, achieving over a million dollars in sales faster than any team in the history of her company.

She’s delighted and guided thousands with her presentations of Train Your Brain at seminars and workshops around the country, quickly establishing herself as a growing force in both the MLM and Personal Development playing fields. Her new company caters to home party-plan consultants and network marketers looking for access to simple, time-saving and revenue building approaches to training.

Known for her unbound energy, a bottomless curiosity to learn, and fearless optimism, Dana rewards her supporters with her own, original brand of training, a commitment to instruct, inspire and mentor with care and simplicity, continually dedicating herself to finding newer and quicker ways to improve and transform direct sellers lives and also provide the training and skills necessary for building financially thriving businesses in record-setting time.

Visit www.danawilde.com to learn more.

Specialties: network marketing, direct sales, home party plan business, training, how the brain works, speaking to network marketing organizations, helping others grow direct sales and home party plan businesses
