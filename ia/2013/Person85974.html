Rev. Marilee A. Snyder - Nieciak's Summary

Marilee is a published author in “Pebbles In The Pond – Transforming the World One Person at a Time” launched May 20, 2012; FROM TEARS TO TRIUMPH and Ancient Wisdom for NOW- Crystals and the 13 Crystal Skulls in 2013.

After her “Death Experience” in 1972, Marilee studied extensively. She is a Reiki Master/Teacher & Dar'Shem III; Akashic Records Consultant and Teacher; Certified Shamanic Practitioner providing Shamanic Journeys; Drum Circles; Soul Retrieval; Extraction, Divination while founding Sage Spirit Terra in 1996

In May 2001 she joined Young Living Essential Oils as Distributor #431711. In June 2003 she became an Internationally Certified Quantum Biofeedback Practitioner.

Rev. Marilee is a “Wounded Healer” whose mission is “To Empower You to Empower Yourself” with her enthusiasm, openness, expertise and stories.
