Damodar Chetty's Summary

Author of Tomcat 6 Developer's Guide (Packt Publishing 2009)

Technical reviewer on Core JavaServer Faces 3rd Edition, 2010.

Specialist-grade skills in Java EE web applications architecture and development. This is my niche and I am passionate about what I do. With two decades of experience, I've worked in just about every role in the software development life cycle (architect, designer, developer, team leader, and analyst).

I am skilled in team leading, mentoring, conceptualizing/executing green field projects, preparing and defending architectural and design approaches, evangelizing the primacy of the web in a business' strategic tool set, presenting informed expert opinions on technology alternatives, and communicating technology in terms that a business stakeholder can understand. I love teaching and take every opportunity to ensure that I educate my business partners with the information they need to make a well reasoned decision.

I work at my best where there is a strong people and relationship component, and a culture of trust.

Very strangely for a tech person, I don't crave to work on the latest technology. I prefer finding the right technology stack for a business problem, rather than adding the undue risk that is normally associated with being an early adopter.

I am accomplished in the use of Occam's razor and rejoice in simplifying things as much as they will go, and actively avoid gold plated implementations.

I respect the people I work with, and am a strong believer in my team, rather than on maverick/rogue programmer heroes. In almost 2 decades of working, I find that I have forgotten most of the burning issues of the projects I've worked on, but I still remember the people I worked with. I try to ensure that I leave my coworkers with pleasant memories of the time spent interacting with me.

Specialties

Full life cycle software development - from requirements gathering and analysis to design and development.

Primary interests in the areas of object oriented software design and development.

Research interests in artificial intelligence techniques for predictive learning and data mining.
