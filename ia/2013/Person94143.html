Mike Taylor's Summary

Mike Taylor started Web Based Recruitment in 2001. having spent the previous 12 years working in HR and Recruitment for major blue chip companies including IBM, Motorola and Nokia.

In his last role at Nokia Mike was the UK Recruitment Specialist with responsibility for Nokia’s UK careers web site and online advertising strategy.

Mike has been involved in online recruitment for 14 years and established Web Based Recruitment as an online recruitment consultancy to help companies with all aspects of their online recruitment activities.

Mike also has experience of running successful Online Marketing campaigns in a number of different industry sectors and is also a founder member of the ORMC (Online Recruitment Marketing Council).

A the end of September 2013 Mike launched the new Mobile Recruitment website - http://www.mobilerecruitment.com - which offers Mobile Recruitment articles, strategy and advice.
