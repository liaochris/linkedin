Eddie George's Summary

Over 25 years experience in IT, the last 5 spent in the New Finance startup scene, prior to this I worked in Retail Finance Distribution technology, Investment Banking and briefly in Energy.

I have had a broad career, working originally as a UNIX Developer right through to Project Manager, CTO, and Strategic consultant; I've worked on many projects and am familiar with many key technology systems in the Investment and Retail finance industries.

I am particularly interested in innovative technologies that can positively transform finance and I'm keen to network with others who share this interest.
