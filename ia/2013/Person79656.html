LeeAnn Gibbs' Summary

My speciality is the human side of organizations - from small teams to whole systems. From horse whisperer to people whisperer, I coach and develop the human side of change, transformation and ultimate excellence.

I provide the tools and training of the "how" of human relationships.

My background is rather unique - I have 30 years of developing horse and rider for National and International competition; 11 years as a facilitator of learning in public education,; 10 years of non-profit leadership and 9 years as a consultant, speaker and coach in educational institutions and business..

The purpose of my life is provide social transformation through individual and system evolution.
