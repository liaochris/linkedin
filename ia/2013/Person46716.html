Gail Darling's Summary

Gail Darling was born and raised in Washington D.C. but after 30 years in the southwest she is a Texan for life. In 1986 Gail founded the company that became the largest private employer in El Paso, TX at one time. She also opened Baker Darling Training Center and received Federal Contracts to re-train workers displaced as a result of NAFTA. In 2012 with the help of technology she consolidated and has since moved the home office to Austin, TX to be closer to her children. Gail holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from The University of Maryland.

Gail Darling Staffing is a certified woman-owned small business established in 1986 and headquartered in Austin, TX. Being a small business has given us the opportunity to build strong relationships with our clients. We are committed to providing your business with the most professional employees in a wide range of disciplines including but not limited to: intelligence, engineering, administrative, legal, accounting, technical biological services and more in a variety of industries. We are experienced with the Service Contract Act requirements for staff augmentation services on Federally Funded Projects and have past performance functioning as a prime or sub contractor.

Nationwide placement for over 27 years.
