Jacqueline Owensby's Summary

Jacqueline Owensby, M.P.A., B.A. (English-Journalism), Author, Speaker, Certified Christian Life Coach, Founder and President of Women In Need of Empowerment (W.I.N.E.), and Founder and CEO of Women In Need of Empowerment’s Coaching & Mentoring Program. Jacqueline has been involved in the business of empowering women for twenty years. W.I.N.E. is geared to empower women to “Live IN Purpose” by discovering who you are and why you are here. Empowerment begins with renewing the mind; perspective is powerful.

Personal Note: As a former educator, I believe we transform our thinking through educating, training and application. It is my assignment and objective 'to inspire women to connect with their divine purpose and to Live Empowered Lives,' by coaching, mentoring, writing, publishing articles, books and hosting empowerment sessions through seminars and conferences.
