Leanne Goff's Summary

Hi, I am Leanne Goff. I have been involved in real estate for over a decade and licensed as a Realtor in lovely Colorado since 2008. I focus on Boulder County real estate and also have experience around North Denver Metro. Working through the years has given me the confidence and expertise to navigate the ever-changing situations each individual real estate transaction entails. With a degree from the University of Florida in Business Administration you can be assured that I have the negotiation, marketing and financial skills to deliver the results you expect in your real estate transaction.

Specialties: Boulder County real estate, contract negotiation, internet marketing, project management
