Daniel Fee's Summary

The Echo Group is one of Pennsylvania’s premier strategic communications firms. Led by Dan Fee, The Echo Group evaluates the needs of every client, developing a strategy and implementing a plan to deliver measurable results. Dan and The Echo Group have worked with a wide variety of clients, from candidates for federal, state and local public office to defending a prominent association under national attack to a coalition of professional horse riders establishing a national association.
