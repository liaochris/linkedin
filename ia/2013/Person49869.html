Tim Kim's Summary

Fun Fact: I’m an Irish Guy with an Asian last name. I took my wife’s name and changed my name from Meany to Kim. I figured why not and being in the branding industry it might help, because who is going to forget the name Tim Kim?

I'm a Brand Strategist and Designer.

I've also seen a lot of mistakes that business make with their marketing efforts and throw a lot of money down the toilet.

I have worked with clients such as AT&T, History Channel, American Airlines and much more.

I have a passion for good design and visual communication and I'm always looking for a new challenge.
