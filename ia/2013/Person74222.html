Ken Henderson's Summary

• I solidify, unify and contribute to the collective imagination and creation of entertainment properties.

• I build production teams through mutual trust, collaboration, manageable priorities and inspiring goals while striving for continual improvement and industry leading quality.

• I take complex team development issues and distill them down to create tools and production processes that garner team buy-in while focusing on company success.

This is what drives me.

20+ years experience in art direction, concept development, production processes.

I specialize in concept visualization, team building, production pipeline optimization and fostering innovation.

I like collaborating to create immersive worlds. I like solving problems. I like creating production systems to streamline processes.

Most of all, I thrive when presented with a challenge - especially the challenge that comes from creating new worlds in a production environment full of technological and personnel issues.

What I have done:

CONTENT HIGHLIGHTS: Part of the creative team for Eisner Award-Winning Darkhorse series Grendel-Warchild. Created UI for the original Apple Multi-session CD ROM. 3D artist on award-winning Reboot The Ride (Las Vegas). Art Director for award-winning Transformers-Beast Machines. Lead production designer on Doozers from the Jim Henson Company.

CORPORATE HIGHLIGHTS: Spearheaded the R&D team and Department Head structure at Mainframe Entertainment. Implemented new methodology at LucasArts, saving more than $125,000 within its first 8 weeks in operation. Launched one of Canada’s first family-focused digital periodicals, creating industry-leading interactive advertising tools as Creative Director for Our Big Earth Media Co.

COMMUNITY HIGHLIGHTS: Since 2007, I’ve been one of Canada’s unique sports voices as the sports editorial cartoonist for The Georgia Straight.

PERSONAL HIGHLIGHTS: Dad of two great kids. Lead sleep deprivation engineer. Closet sports prognosticator.

  • Host/Producer - The Family Heritage Experience at Filberg Lodge (Robert Filberg 100th Anniversary Celebration)

    • November 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Ken Henderson, Robin Rivers, Joanna Ross, Pam Watson

    The team at Our Big Earth partnered with The Filberg Heritage Lodge and Park to create a family heritage experience as a part of the Robert Filberg 100th Anniversary Celebration.

    For the day, we created an atmosphere of history and heritage on the grounds of Filberg Lodge where families came and made crafts, ate heritage foods, learned about the Filberg history and created a legacy community wall quilt that still hangs today in the Comox Valley.

    The event drew hundreds of Comox Valley families and began a long-standing programming partnership with the Filberg.

  • Creator/Producer - The Warmth of Christmas™

    • December 2009 to December 2011
    Team Members: Ken Henderson, Robin Rivers

    Social Issue: Desire to return to traditional, heritage-based holiday celebrations.

    Solution: Partner with a heritage property to create a Christmas experience that takes people back to holidays long ago.

    The Warmth of Christmas: In 2009, kids and families dressed in pajamas and wrapped up in warm blankets around the roaring fire at the Filberg Heritage Lodge listened as traditional holiday tales were read and treats served. Then, they created heritage holiday decorations to decorate the trees and take home with them too.

    In 2011, the event was also a hit, featuring caroling with the Comox Valley Children’s Choir accompanied by Alan Jossul and traditional holiday storytelling with Our Big Earth. Families enjoyed classic holiday treats and take home a book of carols sung that evening.

    Partners included: Comox Valley Record, The Filberg Heritage Lodge, Thrifty's, Planet Kids.
