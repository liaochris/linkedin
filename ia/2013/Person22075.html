Charlie Bourland's Summary

Global business executive with over 20 years medical device experience in U.S., European and Latin American markets. International experience was obtained not only as the CEO and founder of American Medical Endoscopy, but also having studied abroad in London at the London Business School and was later assigned to a post in Paris for Stryker Corporation as Director of Sales and Marketing.

In 1995, having relocated to Brazil, I started American Medical Endoscopy in Sao Paulo, which over the last 18 years has developed into a niche distributor throughout Latin America focused on disruptive technologies which included Opus Medical (Arthrocare), Cayenne Medical, Cascade Medical (Linvatec), Orthovisc (Anika Therapeutics), Peak Surgical (Medtronic), TAG Medical (Tene Funds, Israel), Arthrosurface and Strauss Surgical.

In 2012, American Medical Endoscopy made its first acquisition of a company called Trend Solution Components in Sao Paulo, a GMP manufacturing operation with Anvisa Licenses to manufacture Medical Devices with the designation BPF. We currently have a Class 10,000 clean room. As part of this acquisition, I have relocated some employees and family to Campinas, Sao Paulo, Brazil in July of 2012. I will be responsible for the integration of our manufacturing operation now called Strauss Surgical into an import, assembly, manufacturing, distribution and exporting enterprise.

Strauss Surgical will now be focused on creating fast track OEM contract manufacturing and distribution relationships in Brazil by utilizing our Anvisa GMP License through partnerships and/or collaborations with foreign or multinational companies. We are actively seeking young companies with disruptive technologies that are ready to expedite standard regulatory processes and enter the Brazilian market with Strauss as your partner advisor.

Please feel free to contact me to discuss your specific goals and how we could possibly help you here in Brazil.

Specialties: Medical devices.
