Laura Dunn's Summary

Laura has been a dedicated practitioner for almost 15 years and teaching for almost 10 years in Honolulu, Hawaii. Her teaching emphasizes both steadiness and ease in an effort to strike a balance between the tension of opposites in life as well as in yoga practice. She teaches students to cultivate the longevity of their practice through adopting an attitude of discipline, patience, and non-attachment.
