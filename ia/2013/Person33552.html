Deborah King, SPHR's Summary

Managing an SBA 8(a), woman-owned small business(WOSB), my energies are focused on business management, development of business opportunities, and my real passion - hands on consulting projects. Since we serve small, medium, and large companies, as well as government agencies, my engagements usualy take me into projects related to:

1. Organization Development
Assessments (individual, team and organization), change management, strategic planning, team building, process/system development, process improvement, organizational transition planning, restructuring-downsizing-outplacement, interviews and surveys for collection of relevant data from stakeholders, skills assessment, competency and behavioral indicator modeling, leadership development, and executive coaching.

2. Human Resource/Human Capital Management
Handbooks, policies and procedures, workforce assessment, job analysis and performance descriptions, performance management, succession planning, compensation and rewards, process audits, investigations, Strategic Human Capital Plans, affirmative action plans.

3. Training and Staff Development
Development of competency-based training curriculums, design and delivery of various training programs including: management and supervisory, customer service, harassment and violence -free workplace, drug-free workplace, and career transitions, change management, inter-generational, project management, creativity and innovative thinking – HBDI, and delivery of personalized and customized leadership and executive development coaching.
