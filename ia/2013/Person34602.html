I conceived, planned, developed and executed the launch of a new product (PKU Calc) to help those with Phenylketonuria (PKU) and their carers.

Application development leveraged many capabilities of the Apple iOS SDK including Xcode IDE, iOS Simulator, Instruments and Interface Builder. The application uses Cocoa Touch, Core Data (encapsulating a SQLite database), HTML, Objective-C, and C.

In Australia, PKU is treated with a low protein diet. I developed the application to visually reinforce the way in which specialist nutritionists train people about the PKU diet. (i.e. using a Green, Orange and Red food analogy).

My responsibilities lay in creating the simulations within the mainframe computer based upon the training requirements of the session and manning various control room consoles within the simulators assessment centre.

I achieved the rank of Leading Seaman.
