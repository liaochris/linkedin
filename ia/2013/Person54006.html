Huib J. Joor's Summary

An event architect is an event manager that becomes involved at the early initiation stages of the event. Specially for larger public events, at the initiation stage, the event architect needs to make crucial choices and decisions related to the creative concept and design of the event. In depth technical design knowledge and full understanding of how to communicate a company´s message across a public are needed in order to make the event effective.

For the second year in a row I have organized the Zwolle Ice Sculpture festival. With over 140.000 visitors in 2 months a huge success.

On the Saturday of the last weekend of June Monumental Productions organizes the Awakenings Festival at the recreation area Houtrak in Spaarnwoude. Joor Events is being hired to do the beverage catering for the 37.500 visitors.

On the Sunday of the last weekend of June A Venue Productions organizes the Latin Village Festival at the recreation area Houtrak in Spaarnwoude. Joor Events is being hired to do the beverage catering for the 22.500 visitors.

Specialties: Public event management, event production, catering

    Team Members: Huib J. Joor
