Genevieve Douglass' Summary

My background in music led to the creation of a music house, where I realized an interest in psychology, particularly around work. From there, I earned my MA in positive psychology from the University of Pennsylvania, where I focused on motivation and vitality.

This meld of music and psychology is always opening up unexpected pathways. The current one is at WOOPAAH, where companies hire us to give their employees a space to let go.
