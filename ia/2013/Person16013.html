Bradley Christian's Summary

Consultative thought leader in the arena of customer intelligence; the art and science of enabling customer-facing organizations to secure the business intelligence needed to delight customers and drive financial performance. Experienced in collaborating with restaurants, grocery & drug stores and major retailers to develop complex measurement programs and provide best practice recommendations for organizational change management across all stakeholders; from individual location management to the executive staff.

Specialties: Development of strategy, assessment of current state, evaluation and stress test of goals and objectives ("what does good look like?"), initial implementation of programs, results analysis and recommendation.

Bradley Christian's Experience

Managing Director

Market Force Information

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Market Research industry

2006 – Present (7 years)

Market Force is the leading global customer intelligence solutions company for multi-location businesses, including major retailers, restaurants, grocery and convenience stores, financial institutions, entertainment studios and consumer packaged goods companies. With more than 125 years of combined industry experience, Market Force has pioneered the industry with a suite of customer intelligence solutions – from proprietary decision-support tools such as predictive analytics and technology reporting platforms to 600,000 field associates across North America and Europe who conduct mystery shopping, to real customer surveys, contact center solutions and social media monitoring. Its solutions enable brands to identify the actions required at the store level to increase customer loyalty and improve financial performance. Market Force was named one of Forbes’ America’s Most Promising Companies in 2011.

Co-founder and Vice President

Retail Detail Merchandising

2003 – 2006 (3 years)

Retail Detail Merchandising is a merchandising service organization dedicated to the development and fulfillment of consumer product manufacturers' and brand marketers' retail service initiatives across the southeastern United States.

Co-founded the company in 2003 and generated $2M in total revenues prior to leaving. Fully responsible for all new client development. Oversaw all client engagements, retail initiative implementation plans and contract negotiations.

Successful client engagements included Acosta Sales & Marketing, Chattem, Hawaiian Tropic, General Mills, Playtex Products, Solar Cosmetic Labs, Style Science and Winn Dixie Stores.

Vice President of Sales

Sealane Marketing, Inc.

2003 – 2006 (3 years)

Sealane Marketing is a manufacturer's representative firm selling general merchandise and health & beauty care product lines to the supermarket industry, which included accounts such as Albertsons, CVS, Food Lion, Kash N Karry, Publix and Winn Dixie.

Was named Category Captain for Suncare at Winn Dixie from 2003 to 2006.

Director of Retail Operations

Advanced Retail Merchandising

1997 – 2002 (5 years)

Advanced Retail Merchandising is a merchandising service organization specializing in retail merchandising and audit solutions for manufacturers and brand marketers with a retail presence in the southeastern US.

Responsible for the complete management of all retail service initiatives for clients. Developed and implemented retail programs to meet client needs regarding product placement, case sales increases and market share growth. Managed ten Division Managers who oversaw 300 retail merchandisers. Served clients in Albertsons, Eckerd, Kash N Karry, Publix and Winn Dixie. Clients included Foster Grant, General Mills, Georgia Pacific and Procter & Gamble.

Director of Retail Operations

Sabal Marketing, Inc.

1994 – 1997 (3 years)

Sabal Marketing was a manufacturer's representative firm selling general merchandise and health & beauty care product lines to the supermarket industry in the southeast. Sabal Marketing was acquired by Advanced Retail Merchandising in 1997.
