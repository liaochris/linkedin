Annette Rolsting's Summary

For 15 years I have assisted companies, organisations and individuals in facilitating effective communication, sound HR and organisational development in order to reach overall business goals and secure employee engagement and retention.

In addition to my work, I have written articles on communication planning and communication measurement for Danish communication magazines and handbooks, been a guest teacher at Danish School of Media and Journalism and served as a panel judge for International Association of Business Communicators' Gold Quill Award on Human Resources and benefits Communication.

Currently, I work as a communications and secretariat officer at National Food Institute, Technical University of Denmark. I am fortunate to be able to use my knowledge and experience within the fields of internal communication, corporate branding, management, organisational development and working environment.

In my spare time I work as a volunteer speaker and healthcare professional for the Danish association Bedre Psykiatri.

I hold an MA in International Business Communication as well as minor degrees in HRD, Mentoring Coaching, Organisation and Organisational Psychology.
