Brandon Carroll's Summary

I am the owner and instructor at Global Config Technology Solutions now providing the highest quality Security Training for CCNA, CCNP and CCIE Security.

Previously, CCIE Instructor with INE, providing network training.

As former CTO for Ascolta I lead the vision of IT and the services it provides within our organization to internal and external users.

I have taught a number of Technical courses such as QOS, CIPT1 and CIPT2, ACUCM and other in the area of UC.
