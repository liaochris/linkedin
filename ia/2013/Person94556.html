Mike Schechter's Summary

Mike has practiced law for nearly twenty years in commercial litigation and business law in private practice, as the General Counsel for an international publishing and distribution company, and teaching business law at St Cloud State. He helps businesses and individuals increase effective legal strategy and decrease legal expenses, and serves as a mediator or arbitrator. Specialties include contract, employment, licensing, software, intellectual property, negotiation, distribution, international, FCPA, litigation, risk management and conflict resolution.

Mike graduated from the highly-rated NYU School of Law, and clerked with the US Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit and the Mn Court of Appeals. He has published articles and earned awards, including the Minnesota State Bar Association’s President’s Award in 2003, and is a Board Member of the Minnesota State Board on Continuing Legal Education.
