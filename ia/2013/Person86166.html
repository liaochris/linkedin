Mariska Richters' Summary

Mariska's nearly 10 years experience in the digital media industry have brought her to her most recent venture, Milkbone Media. Milkbone Media is a social media management service, that helps businesses build their online presence through Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and the many other social networks on the web.

Mariska's previous experience includes operations management for web development agencies in Vancouver. She has experienced the full corporate lifecycle from startup to maturity. Her expertise lies in areas of project and business management, SR&ED and immigration and relocation for international team members.

She has a Certificate in Project Management from UBC's Sauder School of Business and a Diploma in Business Administration, and a Senior Management Certificate from BCIT for Web Design & Development.

Mariska's other passions lie in photography and travel. While her main area of interest is travel photography, she has shot for clients such as Estee Lauder, Molson Coors, ING Direct, and Shawnigan Lake School. She was also awarded photographic media accreditation for the 2010 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games in Vancouver, and will attend the Sochi 2014 Paralympics with official media access as well.

The combination of Mariska’s social media talents, management skills and photographic creativity make her an experienced, smart and well-rounded individual, capable of creative solutions in a business environment. She would be an asset to any organization she chooses to join.
