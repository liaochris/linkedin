Daniel McNutt's Summary

I am a graphic designer living in Moscow, Pennsylvania. Originally an Arizona native, I moved to NEPA in 2004.

I have over 20 years of design, and management experience and I look for similar prospects in the future. I am a strong believer in good customer service and that applies to ANY industry, especially graphic design.

Well thought out design is the best way to communicate ideas and concepts. You see it every day. On your way to work or school, it may be the stop sign you stop for, or a billboard for a business you pass on the highway. It's all graphic communication. The intermingling of words and pictures or even motion and animation can lead to an emotional response, a message of clarity, or a calamity depending on the skills of the interpreter.

Specialties: Graphic design, web design, SEO, customer service, auditing, computer troubleshooting, employee relations, interviewing, counseling, telephones, software expertise, print advertising, direct mail.
