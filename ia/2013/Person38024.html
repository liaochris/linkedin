Dr. June Rogers' Summary

I have owned Rogers Chiropractic at I-29 and Barry Road in Kansas City, MO for over 30 years. I have grown up with this community. In 1985, Barry Road, in front of Zona Rosa was a two lane road!

During the past year I have been producer and consultant for a PBS documentary , entitled: “Pain in America – The Silver Tsunami”. To see a 1 minute, 52 second trailer of the documentary go to : askdrjune.com

I have begun “Ask Dr. June”- a series of articles, blogs and public presentations devoted to the Chiropractic professional as well as health in general.

I have served this community in my Northland clinic, with traditional chiropractic services until technology introduced us to an entirely new way of helping people through computer analysis.

What makes our office outstanding is that our computerized technology gives us the ability to immediately compare the spine before and after the adjustment. This technology is becoming the standard method of treatment.

We deliver gentle and safe treatment with no twisting or popping! It is effective, accurate and precise.

After the treatment, we immediately analyze the spine for comparative results so that the patient and I can track the progress. We compare those results shown on the computer screen to the results from the previous treatment to determine the most effective treatment plan.

Additionally, I like to spend my time creating stain glass arts, scuba diving, cooking, hiking and being outdoors..

Specialties

Corporate Wellness Program: I consult with larger corporations that understand the impact of their employees' health on health care costs. I develop employee programs that elevate health awareness and empower healthy decisions.

I customize the programs for each employer and target the physical, emotional, spiritual, and intellectual components. I strategically encourage employees to engage in behaviors that improve their lives and reduce health care costs.

Dr. June Rogers' Experience

Wellness Coach & Owner

Rogers Chiropractic Clinic

January 1985 – Present (29 years)

When I was 10 years old I fell and injured my neck. My mother took me to a Chiropractor who twisted my neck back into shape. Later in life, I began getting headaches on a daily basis. I went to see a doctor who wanted to put me on tranquilizers to solve the problem. There was a small voice in the back of my head that was screaming "NO!"....and I listened!

So I called the Chiropractor I worked with previously and was adjusted again.....he twisted my head and it worked! I was impressed because my headache went away immediatley! I asked him about what was actually happening when he did the adjustment.

As he described how the spine affected all the different parts of the parts of the body, I became intrigued. Then he asked me if I would ever like to restore someone' s health by adjusting the spine. I didn't hesitate and said: "Where do I sign up?"

A typical Chiropractic patient is someone who has suffered a great deal with either neck pain or low back pain.They have endured the medical route to little or no avail and then find their way to my office being both scared and anxious. My goal is to help relieve their pain and get them on their way to a happier, healthier life!

Another aspect of my business is corporate wellness programs aimed at prevention and educating and empowering employees to make healthy choices. We provide Human Resource departments with support from nationally recognized organizations, such as: The American Chiropractic Association, The American Heart Association, The American Diabetes Association.

I can work in concert with your incentive programs or develop those programs for your company.

I supply employers with the following tools:

~Lunch room video's
~Nutritional and Vitamin information
~Question & answer sessions with local health care providers
