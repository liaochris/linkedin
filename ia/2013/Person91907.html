Matt Allen's Summary

I love both 3d art and 2d art. In my past experiences I have worked as a concept artist for several companies. I currently work for Gameloft as a 3d artist, and work daily on my personal projects.

Specialties

Low and high poly modeling using 3dmax, maya and zbrush.
I am able to build efficient low poly models. I have a firm grasp on shape and silhouette, with this I am able to create different styles. Through high poly I am able to create quality normal, ao, dirt maps.
I am capable of creating quality diffuse maps, both hand painted or photo textures.
I am also experienced with 2d art, most of my models start with conceptual art and model sheets.
