Judith Aronson-Ramos, M.D., is a Board Certified Developmental and Behavioral Pediatrician whose practice is devoted exclusively to the evaluation and treatment of pediatric patients (birth - 21yrs) with challenges in learning, behavior, development, and social-emotional functioning. Having practiced Pediatrics for over 18 years in many different settings, she is aware of the need to accurately identify children and adolescents who are experiencing problems and provide them with individualized and comprehensive care.

This private medical practice specializes in the treatment of children and adolsecents with emotional, behavioral, developmental, and learning difficulties. Areas of expertise include: Autism, ADHD, Anxiety Disorders, OCD, Learning Disabilities, Developmental Delays, Depression, and Family dysfunction.
