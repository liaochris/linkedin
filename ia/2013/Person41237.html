Emily Cretella's Summary

Passionate communicator, helping organizations discover, develop and share their brand stories.

The former director of strategy at a full-service Connecticut marketing and advertising agency, I have more than 10 years of experience developing the strategy, writing the content and managing the execution of large-scale traditional and digital marketing campaigns for clients ranging from small not-for-profits to large Fortune 500s.
