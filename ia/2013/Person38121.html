Mark Nugent's Summary

What is High Performance Coaching?

As a coach, I focus on the effective use of the current best practice tools and techniques in management and leadership which I combine with the latest thinking on the role that psychology plays in success.

With this focus, I coach my clients to attain and sustain a higher level of performance giving them a more rewarding and fulfilling career and life.

I coach my client organisations on a one-to-one and group basis, as required. All coaching programmes are tailored to the specific needs of the client.

Each individual coaching programme will have clearly agreed outcomes from the start. A programme may involve 4 to 8 sessions of between 1 and 2 hours (if one-to-one) or 2 and 4 hours (if group based). I will have a preparatory phone conversation with the participant or the group leader approximately one week before the session. The coaching session itself will focus on the issue identified beforehand and will involve defining what the desired outcome is and how to make it happen. Of course this can involve discussions around tools, techniques, mindset, behaviours and barriers. There will be clear goal-setting and a commitment to action by the participant(s).

I follow up each coaching session with a detailed coaching note which documents the decisions made, the agreed actions and any further insights I have upon reflecting after the meeting. My clients tell me this is unusual and that they find these notes very useful indeed.

Who I Work With

I coach primarily SME business owners and their senior teams. In addition, I have significant experience with senior managers in international public companies as well as with CEOs and senior teams in the third sector.

My coaching clients are successful people and like all such people see their own self-development as central to their success. They understand that coaching has a significant role to play in their continued high performance and the realisation of more of their potential.
