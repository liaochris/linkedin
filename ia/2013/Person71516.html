John Voyta's Summary

Results driven research and development executive with a proven track record of leadership, innovation, product development and building strong R&D teams. Twenty plus years of increasing levels of responsibility and contribution in both start-up and large mature life sciences reagent and instrumentation companies. Contributed at the corporate level by organizing input of key scientists, participation in strategy exercises and as a member of due diligence teams. Co-founder of Tropix, Inc. in 1986. Acquired in 1996 by Applied Biosystems (then Perkin Elmer). Proven track record of innovation with 38 issued US patents and 12 published pending US applications. Extensive history of product launches at Tropix and within Applied Biosystems involving both reagent systems and instrumentation platforms. Solid understanding of molecular biology application tools for gene expression analysis, genotyping, next generation sequencing, sample prep and RNAi use and analysis of effect. Strong background in all aspects of cellular analysis including fluorescence imaging and flow cytometry as well as technical strengths in labeling technologies, antibody technologies, fluorescence spectroscopy and all aspects of chemiluminescence.

Specialties

Leadership, innovation, product development, technology evaluation, due diligence
