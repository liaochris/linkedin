  • MaxiCalc: Realistic Calculator for iPad

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Everson Stabenow Siqueira, Fernando Sciessere, MBA, MSc

    We just launched our second iOS App. On this iPad calculator we invested an obscene amount of care in user experience, polished graphics and immersive sounds. It may seem exaggerated for such a trivial type of application - but it is not: user experience is key to mobile apps' success.

    The time we spent on details paid off and the response is being overwhelming:
    - #2 overall Paid App in Brazil
    - #8 overall Paid App in Spain
    - #15 Paid Utility in the USA
    ...in less than 72 hours and a very, very tight advertising budget (does "money for dinner" count as budget anyways?)

    Reviews are being unanimous: 5 stars, all saying good things. A few comments are really emotional: "This calculator is not only fully functional... it brings me memories of my childhood times, when I used to spend evenings with my mom on her job, and she let me play with those heavy-duty table calculators. The sound is so vivid. Thanks for that!"

    I invite you to experience MaxiCalc. The search for the perfect iPad calculator is over.
