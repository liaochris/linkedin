Adrian Sutherland's Summary

Adrian is currently named as the Strategic Healthcare Technology Specialist within CSC's Global Healthcare team.

Adrian is a solution director and enterprise architect with over 25 years architecture and design experience. Adrian joined CSC UK in 2004 and was posted overseas to Australia for two years in 2010.

Adrian has worked in several industries other than health including insurance, banking and government.
