Jerry Mackay's Summary

I've been working as a filmmaker for almost 20 years, with experience in all areas of video production. I'm a writer, cinematographer, editor, graphics and animation designer, sound designer, music composer and award-winning DVD author.

My company, Littleflick Pictures, produces all manner of digital films, including television commercials, corporate sales and marketing films, training and eLearning presentations, and short subject documentaries.

I'm also a professional narrator and voice actor, with numerous credits in radio commercials, corporate films, eLearning programs and training presentations. Clients include Amazon, McDonalds, Nike, Motorola, Intel, BP, Coca-Cola, Comcast and Aramark.
