Charles Phillips' Summary

Charles Phillips is the CEO and a Member of the Board of Directors of Infor, the world's third largest business applications company. Infor provides industry specific applications for 70,000 customers in 125 countries with over 12,000 employees. Before joining Infor, Phillips served as the President of Oracle Corporation and was also a member of the company's Board of Directors.

Prior to Oracle, Phillips was a Managing Director with Morgan Stanley and on its board of directors.. He is currently a Director at Viacom Corporation, the American Museum of Natural History, and Jazz at Lincoln Center, and Phillips Charitable Organizations.

Phillips graduated from the United States Air Force Academy with a BS Computer Science degree. He earned his MBA from Hampton University, a JD from New York Law School and is a member of the Georgia State Bar.

Before his business career, he served as a Captain in the United States Marine Corps with the 10th Marines in Camp Lejune.
