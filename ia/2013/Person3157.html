Alan Holmes' Summary

As a Specialist Consultant in core banking and payment systems, I have over seven years experience in this sector, working with members the big-four on the industry’s most complex, innovative, security and customer-critical projects.

I bring to each consulting project the following key competencies:

- in-depth understanding of core banking and payment systems
- specialist knowledge of how payment systems integrate into accounting and back office systems in financial institutions
- knowledge of best practice in systems within banking environments
- expertise in dynamic and complex regulatory compliance and system rules
- 12 years plus project management skills - managing complex, cross functional, time and security critical banking projects

My most recent project achievements include:

- NAB Private Wealth Platform: For the development of a new private wealth platform, I ensure the vendors’ cash management systems meet banking standards, ensure value control and develop and implement business processes for the cash management solution.

- UBank: For the launch of a new online bank from blue-print, I managed the design and implementation of process design; and the design and delivery of training for the new term deposit product and high interest online savings product.

- NextGen project: As Senior Process Analyst I managed the requirements development and facilitation process; and developed third party interface documentation, operational risk controls and regulatory compliance framework

Sought after as a consultant, the value I bring stems from my experience working within complex institutions, my ability to see issues from all perspectives and to work in a collaborative way that brings together stakeholders with independent agendas.

I always enjoy applying my project management and analytical skills to bring security and efficiency to organizations wishing to develop best practice with core payment systems.
