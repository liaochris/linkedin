Nancy Feldman's Summary

Interior Design and Real Estate are my passions! I have over 20 years experience in both Industries so I'm a step ahead when it comes to identifying the properties that make the most sense for purchase and/or renovation and design!

Being an Interior Design Specialist as well as the owner of multiple properties has given me a slightly different vision with which to approach any project, whether it's Residential or Commercial. I know which properties seem to be more sensible than others. Over 70% of my Design Business was referral or repeat and many of those same Clients now search me out for my experties in Real Estate Investing in South Florida.

Specialties

Real Estate Investments, Interior Design, Remodeling and Renovation. Staging homes for sale, One day make-overs, unique placement techniques.

Nancy Feldman's Experience

Vice President

1-855-Kash-Now, Inc.

2009 – Present (4 years) Boca Raton, Florida

1-855-KASH-NOW is a local Real Estate Investment company that purchases single & multi-family properties and either Renovates and re-sells to end users or flips at wholesale prices to other Investors who either want to renovate and re-sell or rent to end users.

We purchase properties in any price range, in any condition, usually within Broward and Palm Beach Counties in S. Fl. and pay cash for our deals. We also represent Clients from all over the world, Investors who are looking for properties in South Florida that will give them a better return on their Investment than is possible in their country. We specialize in creating win-win solutions for both those who have to sell and those that want to buy.

As cash buyers we close fast. We purchase REOs, Short Sales, Estate Sales and sometimes sales of property where the home owners are just ready to move on. We are well respected with over 30 established years as a South Florida business owner both in my role as President, Owner, Stager, Visual Coordinator and Interior Designer of The Art of Placement, Inc. as well as Renovator and Real Estate Investor.

I look forward to hearing from all those seriously interested in both selling and purchasing South Florida real estate.
