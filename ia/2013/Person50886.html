Guise Bule's Summary

I lead the RPv Crew, we built the very first RPv Engines for the US Federal Government.

I coined the term Risk Persona Virtualization (RPv) to describe our work, RPv is the art of physically isolating specific risk profiles into virtual containers for cyber-defence purposes. My team and I work towards the greater good.

As the 'father' of Risk Persona Virtualization, I co-developed the RPv model in collaboration with the National Nuclear Security Administration, my team and I were the first to prove the viability of the RPv model in a high-security production environment at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.

My team and I specialise in building custom RPv Engines for and we are working in close collaboration with our partners and sponsors SAIC and the NNSA (LLNL/SNL) to evolve the security through physical isolation approach to cyber-defence.

I am Chairman of tuCloud Inc, a company I founded and the very first commercial DaaS provider on the planet, tuCloud pioneered the non-persistent approach to desktop virtualization, our customers include the Department of Energy, the US Federal Government and UCLA University.

In my spare time, I am Honorary Chairman of the English Breakfast Society and Fist Of The North Star at DesktopSuperhero, a private guild for technology professionals focused on virty desktops.
