Linda Gartz's Summary

An energetic and creative writer and researcher with strong management skills to envision, develop, and produce large-scale documentaries, intimate videos, or snappy ninety-second news inserts. Writes print articles and scripts that inspire, motivate, teach, and entertain. Researcher, curator, and translator of a vast trove of family letters, diaries, and documents spanning the last century. Writing family narrative based on this extensive collection, which will be joining Chicago's Newberry Library's Midwest Manuscript collection as The Gartz Family Papers, once my research and writing is finished.

Specialties: Writing for print, television, and internet. Multi-genre writer including nature, gardening, travel, history, business, and essay. Research and organizational skills to manage and write about complex projects comprising large quantities of discrete historical information.
