President

Associated Student Government

October 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months) Northwestern University

As President, lead 150-member organization and 8,400-strong undergraduate student body. Serve as primary ambassador from the student body to administration, city government, other organizations.

As Speaker of the Senate, led 50-member Senate, served on student government Executive Board, directly advised university officials and student leaders.

Previous positions: Off-Campus Caucus Whip & Senator, member of Community Relations, Services, Student Life, Rules, and Student Activities Finance Committees.

As off-campus senator, I worked on improving Northwestern's relations with Evanston government and community organizations, and created an online legal resource center for students. As an ASG account executive, I advised, audited, and supported several student groups with $10,000+ budgets.

I'm also working to enhance the sense of community and identity on campus through better utilization of public spaces, greater student group collaboration, and strengthened traditions and shared experiences.

In coordination with my student government counterparts at other universities, I've also worked to create a new framework for collegiate student governments to share information, influence national & international debate, and provide a unified voice for students.

Director of Outreach

LEND (Lending for Evanston and Northwestern Development)

October 2011 – Present (1 year 11 months) Evanston, IL

LEND is a non-profit, student-run microlending and business training organization, serving entrepreneurs in the Evanston community.

Since joining LEND in Fall 2011, I've continued outreach efforts to our partners in local business, community, and university organizations.

Working in tandem with LEND's fundraising and client services arms, I hope to raise awareness of LEND services in the community.

Our loan services provide small amounts of capital to qualified small business owners whose strategic interest require financing. LEND Business training services help entrepreneurs master basic bookkeeping, marketing, and business modeling.
