George M. Parker CFP, CFA's Summary

George Parker, CFA, CFP, CRPS is President and Founder of GM Parker LLC an independent, wealth management company that provides highly personalized financial planning and asset management services to individuals and small business owners. He is a seasoned professional. For over 20 years he has advised clients through good and bad economic times. GM Parker LLC employs Cantella & Co., Inc for broker-dealer services.

George helps clients discover, clarify and prioritize their most important personal and/or business goals using a six-step financial planning process. Applying a four-step investment process he helps clients implement an investment plan whose objective is to amass the wealth necessary to fund all or most of their important goals.

He has counseled clients through major and sometimes unexpected life changing events, including:

• Birth of a child
• Relocation
• Job loss
• Job change
• Death of loved one
• Marital Separation / Divorce
• Retirement
• Marriage
