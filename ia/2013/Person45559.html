Francis Newman's Summary

HELPING EXPERTS LIKE YOU
TRANSFORM YOUR KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS
INTO CREDIBLE PROPOSITIONS FOR WEB AND PRINT

Thanks for visiting my profile. If we’re not already connected, then do send me an invite and I’ll gladly accept. As a LinkedIn open networker (LION 5K+) I appreciate the value of growing a large network and happy to help you do the same.

My work involves helping clients identify their hidden gems – those areas of expertise and knowledge they sometimes overlook. I’ll clean, shape and polish them, so they suit the new marketing plan.

I'll formulate the right message strategy and create visual and verbal ideas for specific web or print marketing campaigns.

My background skills are copywriting and graphic design. But I prefer to call myself a ‘message designer’ as this describes exactly what I do.
I’ve always created marketing messages using words and images as an integrated unit. This has been my approach for over 25 years.

People often ask me if I design and write websites, brochures, email marketing campaigns or new business presentations. The answer is yes - and a lot more. But brochures and websites are containers. What matters is the value of what you put in the container. And that’s what Message Matters is all about.

I'll help you identify and clarify the most relevant aspects of your knowledge and skills and structure them into a marketing proposition.

Then I’ll craft the right words and images into memorable key messages for your website marketing, email marketing or promotional brochure.

I’ve successfully helped many types of organisations from multinationals to solo entrepreneurs and inventors. For me, it’s all about working with interesting people and helping them with their business.

I’m currently working on a book project to promote entrepreneurs and small business owners through shared learning. If you’re interested in featuring in this book, then visit the Pitstop for Business website using link above.

Until next time
Francis
