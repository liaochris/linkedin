Maria Hemminger, CSP's Experience

Partner and Owner

MJ Recruiters, LLC

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

August 2005 – Present (8 years 3 months)

I am the marketing partner of MJ Recruiters, LLC. It is my responsibility to market MJ Recruiters, LLC to prospective and current clients. My goal is to develop close client relationships and to be an extension of my client in the recruitment process. I facilitate our job orders. Joanna Spaun is my partner, combined we have 25 years of recruitment experience. We have worked together for 10 years.

Our passion is being an extension of our clients. Whenever possible we meet with clients and prospects face to face. We believe in solid job orders, regular communication, consistent follow up, outlining the recruitment process at the time of the order and working with our clients until the opening is filled!

MJ Recruiters, LLC specializes in technical and professional manufacturing recruitment. We partner with automotive and non-automotive clients to supply candidates in the fields of operations/supervision, engineering and quality, materials and procurement, and manufacturing human resources, IT and accounting. Our specialty is recruiting candidates at the management level of these disciplines. We are focused regionally and have the capability to recruit nationwide for our clients. We develop clients into long term partners by understanding their culture and goals.
