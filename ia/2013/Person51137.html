Gwen Tan's Summary

Entrepreneurial Asian with a track record of creative accomplishments.
Works with only the earnest and brave.

Gwen has held content strategy, brand strategy and account management roles at Y&R and at boutique agencies Crayon Digital and Formul8. Gwen is also an award-winning artist and designer, experienced in creating products as solutions to better living.

Gwen has experience from various industries including technology, telecommunications, retail, shipping, FMCG, finance and government. She has worked on major brands including Lenovo, Bharti Airtel, EDB Singapore, OCBC Bank, and Great Eastern Life.

Specialties: communication scientist, cultural anthropologist, design snob, extreme sports, (un)common sense and courage.
