Ashley Black's Summary

After a life-threatening, bone eating bacteria left Ashley unable to function, she turned to traditional medical modalities AND alternative care to find that there was a black hole in a broken model. After many painful surgeries and having her body ravaged by high potency narcotics, Ashley took control of her destiny. She studied, invented, researched and eventually pioneered techniques that eventually restored her body and led her into a field she calls Hybrid Health.

Her techniques are so successful that she has dedicated the past decade to certifying thousands of practitioners and touching the lives of professional athletes and celebrities. However, Ashley has maintained a passion for helping hopeless and chronic pain clients that so desperately need solutions.

She is now taking her methods to the masses through an all encompassing edutainment model of delivery including an itunes model for self therapy and a national media sweep. Keep an eye on Ashley Black as her faithful followers slowly change the health paradigm one consumer at a time.

Specialties

Empowering and educating consumers on cutting edge hybrid health technologies including understanding the fascial system, the deep muscles of the spine and complex neurological sports and movement patterning.

Ashley is to the human body what Suzie Orman is to money, Dr. Phil is to relationships, and Rachel Ray is to food. Ashley isn’t a physical therapist, chiropractor, masseuse, or personal trainer yet coaches, top sports agents, olympic competitors, celebrities, and even moguls like Timbaland and Donald J. In addition to being flown across the country for her elite clientele, Ashley is a sought-after keynote speaker for sports and medical conferences—the Major League Baseball Conference, the APTA Conference, and most recently the International Conference on Plasma Replacement attended by experts from around the world.

Ashley’s “secret sauce” is her revolutionary modalities of body treatment and never-before-seen exercises that she personally developed over years of researching, studying, and inventing when she set out to defy a hopeless prognosis after a near-death experience. Her discoveries about the human body and practical application of her techniques defy the philosophy of traditional medicine, mainstream therapy, and personal training methods. Today, she is often referred to as “The Body Whisperer” because her methods help correct many common health issues, orthopedic issues, chronic and recurring pain, neurological issues and athletic injuries; as well as “mystery pains” that often elude traditional medicine. Her revolutionary techniques have created a paradigm shift in the way we approach the body, thus birthing what Ashley calls "Hybrid Health."
