Beth Marshall, CPA, MBA's Summary

"Financial overwhelm can be crippling in many ways, and it can lead to high levels of stress, a lack of sleep, and an inability to focus at work or in other aspects of your life. Once you learn the skills that you were either never taught or may have forgotten, you can become financially healthy and independent so you can stop worrying about money and enjoy life more with significantly less stress." - Beth Marshall

Beth Marshall is inspired to serve others by helping them learn how to proactively manage their financial lives. In Beth's 16 years of professional experience, she has served as an auditor, an executive business advisor and consultant, an organizational change agent, and a financial independence advocate which all blends together to offer a unique and diverse perspective and approach to her work in the financial literacy arena.

Beth's passion is to work with busy, successful and self-motivated professionals and small business owners to help them learn how to intentionally, authentically, and proactively manage their money so they can have more freedom and choice in their lives. With a uniquely-blended coaching style that offers accountability, compassion, and insightful wisdom based on her varied life experiences, Beth uses a proprietary 7-step system to guide her clients toward financial clarity and stability. Beth believes that it is a divinely human right to be able to understand your finances, and that everyone can be financially successful if they are willing to learn.

Beth is dedicated to financially empowering as many people as possible while guiding her clients toward a life of financial peace and prosperity.

Skills & expertise: Compassionate and action-oriented coaching; problem solving; building trusted relationships quickly and effectively; communication skills; designing effective savings and spending plans; financial literacy; financial decision-making; accounting and finance; organizational change; project management.
