Darryl West's Summary

As one of the most respected professionals in the business, Darryl West and his team serve a distinguished clientele with unsurpassed privacy, market knowledge, personal service, and commitment to building long term relationships. Providing local, regional as well as international high end luxury home clients with extraordinary service in the Los Angeles "Westside" and South Bay areas , including Beverly Hills, Malibu, Santa Monica, Manhattan Beach, Hermosa Beach and Redondo Beach.

Darryl West is also the owner of Powerhouse REO, specializing in the REO real estate transaction. He has been in the REO business for over 5 years, and in that time, has closed many of transactions for Mortgage Bankers, Financial Lending Institutions and Private Investors nationwide.

Winning: We are passionate about becoming the unrivaled industry leader by achieving superior results for our clients.
