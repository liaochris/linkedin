Blue Print Life & Wellness Coaching believes and utilizes coaching from a mind, body and spirit approach. Human beings are three parts. The integration of these three parts is the healthiest and most functional way to approach life. It is about balancing and maintaing the whole person. My clients are able to make healthy choices to achieve their goals from their authentic selves to have the highest quality life they deserve.

What is Life Coaching?

Coaching is the process of helping people reach deeper levels of fulfillment & higher levels of success faster, more easily, and more enjoyably than they might on their own. The focus of the coaching may serve career, business, relationships, money, life balance, spirituality or any area of life in which an individual is seeking change, growth, or new discoveries...(International Life Coach Association).

Clients seek my services during times of transition, personally and professionally, to achieve clear goals they want to attain. Weight loss, Bridal, Newlywed, Career, Parenting, Divorce and Perform Artists are a few of the populations I serve. I access the clients need, map out a concrete course of action steps and coach the client to their desired result.

I create a safe space for my clients to explore their emotional, physical and spiritual selves from an objective stand point so that the break through moments can occur and choices can be made.
