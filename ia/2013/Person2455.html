Pat Stimac & Kent Buttars' Summary

Delighted to receive the 2013 Pick for Best of Weddings from The Knot!! We have also been a finalist in the Best Officiant of Western Washington award through King 5 and Seattle Bride Magazine! We've been officiating for over 17 years at wedding ceremonies, vow renewals, memorial services and baby namings. Nondenominational minsters, we celebrate significant life moments with a ceremony exactly as you wish. To make things easier, we offer lots of ideas, guidance based on real life experience, and a calm presence.

Also, premarital preparation available with the main focus on strengthening communication, understanding finances, and further developing relationship skills.

Vow renewal, baby blessing/namings and memorial services also available.

Specialties: Meaningful ceremonies created just for you. We listen to what is important to you and write words that communicate exactly that. Premarital preparation.
