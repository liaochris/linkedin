Don Shapiro's Summary

Don Shapiro is the founder of First Concepts Consultants. Don has worked with firms in over 30 industries during the last 28 years conducting investigations to help find more ways to increase sales, margins and market share. He is also a co-author and editor of the leadership book "The Character-Based Leader" by the Lead Change Group and focuses on the leadership development issues that can improve employee performance, retention and trust.

His original research has lead to several discoveries that can boost the sales of even the best trained, most experienced, quota-busting sales pros. He has spent 28 years learning how people figure out their perceptions of value, what causes that value to rise and fall and how raising the customer's perceptions of value helps close more sales and boost market share.

Don has been speaking before audiences since the age of 14, went to the national championships in debate in College and been a member of Toastmasters for over 20 years. When Don talks, people learn, laugh and leave inspired. He gives speeches and workshops on the following topics about his discoveries and innovations:

FIND THE STEALTH VALUE TO CLOSE MORE SALES

THE CHARACTER-BASED LEADER: HOW TO BUILD TRUST IN YOUR PEOPLE

LIFE IS A FORK IN THE ROAD
Add up your choices and that's your life. Change your choices and change your life.

Don is very passionate about helping people and organizations grow. A desire to make this world a better place, make a difference in people's lives and promote real change drives Don's energetic and uplifting approach toward life.

Specialties:
How customers figure out their perceptions of value and how to apply this to increase sales.
How to boost leadership character to improve employee trust and retention.
How to improve people's choices, decisions and deal with change.
How to creatively solve the most difficult problems.
