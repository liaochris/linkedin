Alejandro Lorenzo [LION]'s Summary

Spanish photographer, visual artist and teacher based in Helsinki, Finland.
Educated in graphic design and photography in Barcelona, Spain.
Started as photography freelancer and artist in 2008.

With a varied range of clients from the Finnish Red Cross to club event organizers; from private companies to local artists and performers as well as individual customers. The work changes from event photography and journalism to portraits and promotional images. Alejandro also teaches photography at Helsingin Aikuisopisto and works on personal artistic projects.

Interested in networking, collaborations, photography teaching as a coaching or facilitation tool and in new possibilities.
