James Canning's Summary

James Canning founded CANNING Communications in 2008, a Detroit-focused public relations and marketing consultancy. In just four years, Canning has provided services to about two dozen clients; and achieved three consecutive years of revenue growth.

An award winning public relations pro, James has earned the reputation as a clever strategist with very strong media relationships, proven crisis management skills, and an unmatched creative spark.

Canning has used his experience to assist clients such as the Detroit Economic Growth Corporation. In less than 1-year secured 136 stories with 40 different local and national news organizations; and generated nearly 120 million unpaid print and online media impressions; along with 291 minutes (nearly 5-hours) of unpaid television and radio coverage urban planning initiative managed by Detroit Economic Growth Corporation. For Paxahau Event Productions and Management he has established winning media and marketing strategies that generated $4-million in sales for their Detroit Restaurant Week product; and helped to increase attendance to the Movement Electronic Music Festival from 2009 to 2012 by 29%.

Before establishing his consultancy, Canning served as lead spokesperson for the Detroit Mayor’s Office. On a daily basis he helped leaders, from the mayor's office to the department directors; navigate a gauntlet of internal and external communication situations. He was the go-to media strategist for major economic development projects, policy initiatives, and campaigns.

Canning began his career with one of Detroit’s most prestigious PR firms. Its legendary partners hired him to join their agency as a full-time account manager before he completed his final semester of undergraduate studies. While there he spearheaded public relations efforts for a national beverage company, a major pharmaceutical supplier, local non-profits, political campaigns, and government agencies and officials.
