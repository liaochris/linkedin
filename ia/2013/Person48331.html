Norman Comfort's Summary

During my career I have managed to build a wealth of marketing experience by working on high profile FMCG brands in both B2C and B2B environments. I have experience of the full marketing mix including, TV advertising, PR, digital / on-line marketing, packaging design, and new product development.
.
My career highlights include:-

- Using Note Pads to raise £100,000 for Breakthrough for Breast Cancer
- Winning Superbrand Status for Blank n’ Red Notebooks
- Launching a niche range of 100% recycled notepads using advanced production techniques to minimise the environmental impact
- Developing Kettle Chips first ever Television Advertising Campaign resulting in a very positive uplift in sales
- Managing Kettle Chips global redesign and increasing household penetration dramatically
- Building some of the most popular and well established Kettle Chips flavours in the UK
- Winning DMA Award for most innovative DM campaign producing an outstanding conversion rate for Kettle Chips

Since finishing my MBA in 2008 I have set up my own business helping people to develop creative yet robust marketing strategies, plans and customer propositions. We are currently engaged in the tourism, on-line collaboration, and food & drink markets. I also work as a board member for other organisations in which I have an interest.

Connect with me on LinkedIn if you would like to learn about current ventures or get in touch.

Specialties: Brand Management, Developing Brand Identity, Creativity, Marketing B2C & B2B, Project Management, New Product Development, Developing Insight, Strategy, and Planning

Norman Comfort's Experience

Head of Sales and Marketing

Great Bircham Foods

June 2009 – December 2011 (2 years 7 months)

Great Bircham Foods is a specialist food producer and retailer. Based in the heart of North Norfolk close to the Sandringham Estate we nurture the best breeds of animal and best varieties of produce to offer a high-end range of naturally grown or farmed foods. I provide Sales and Marketing leadership within the management team.

I am particularly focused on growing the company’s strategic partnerships with like minded food & drink organisations, building our on-line business, improving the proposition in our retail outlets, and building a longer term retail sales strategy. I also represent the company in PR, the local community, and amongst the regions food and drink community.

Marketing Director

Buy Local Ltd

February 2009 – February 2011 (2 years 1 month)

Buy Local is a social enterprise based in Norfolk which represents a wide range of businesses. The organisation is dedicated to raising awareness of the importance of local procurement by consumers & businesses to produce a more resilient, distinctive and sustainable local economy.

I provide business strategy, planning and marketing management at board level. I handle all media & PR activity, act as a spokes person for press & radio enquiries, and liaise with business leaders and politicians in the East of England on behalf of the organisation.

Black n' Red Note Pads - Group Brand Manager

Hamelin Paperbrands

January 2007 – January 2008 (1 year 1 month)

Oxford Stationery - Group Brand Manager. Managing the P/L for a portfolio of brands including the 50 year old iconic Black N' Red Notebook brand.

Other brands under my responsibility included Oxford Pink & Black notebooks that raised over £100,000 for Breakthrough Breast Cancer in 2007/8.

I developed a 100% recycled notepad range using advanced production techniques to minimise environmental impact and deliver a distinct competitive advantage for the brand bringing new levels of innovation to the recycled stationery market.

Kettle Chips - Brand Manager

January 1998 – January 2007 (9 years 1 month)

Kettle Chips – Brand Manager. During my time working on Kettle Chips I was fortunate to gain many roles, responsibilities, and experiences, these included

Annual Brand Planning – Acting as the key interface with NPD and Sales teams to develop & implement the annual brand plans.

Global brand repositioning/re-design – management of a major research, positioning and packaging development project to relaunch the brand in both the UK and US markets.

Consumer TV Advertising – identifying key objectives and implementing TV advertising campaigns for Kettle Chips.

New product development – launch of key products in the Kettle Chips portfolio. Management of product P/L and commercial assessment of new product opportunities.

Management of direct mail, advertising and in-pack promotional campaigns including a DMA Award winning “Introductory Sample” Door Drop campaign.
