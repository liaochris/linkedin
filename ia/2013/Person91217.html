Melissa Johnson's Summary

Over 15 years of blue-chip brand strategy experience working with Fortune 500 Companies including: Victoria's Secret, Nationwide, Baxter Healthcare, Procter & Gamble, Citi, Pepsi, National Football League, National Basketball Association, NCAA, Notre Dame, MillerCoors and more. CA master trainer of peak performing companies and influential individuals in business and sports through a proven leadership development system designed to unleash the champion within called Win Like A Champion: Transforming Your Mind, Body and Business. Has served as "dream designer" to the celebrities, pro athletes and influential leaders and executives. As an industry influencer and pioneer in personal brand leadership development, Johnson is a media personality contributing to CNN, Essence, Thehuffingtonpost.com and more. Launched Velvet Suite in 2006 as a breakthrough brand leadership development firm focused on transforming the world one champion at a time.
