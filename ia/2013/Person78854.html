Laura Lobdell's Summary

I'm the designer behind Printed Hues LLC, a graphic design studio specializing in surface pattern design. I license original artwork to companies for use on their products and work as a freelance graphic designer on an array of projects from logos to layouts.

A few of my specialties: pattern design, graphic design, ad design, layout, illustration, print design, branding, collaborative teamwork, and consistently maintaining an upbeat, positive attitude.

My favorite tools: Adobe Illustrator and a pen & sketchbook. (Photoshop, InDesign and the rest of the Adobe Creative Suite come in handy, too).
