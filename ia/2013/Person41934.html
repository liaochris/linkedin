Eric Auld's Summary

I am a Digital Designer with 10 years experience with a major focus on designing for the user. A lot of my design expertise is on User Interface design; my goals are to produce high quality products that achieve business goals and that are engaging for users.

Design is more than just working in Photoshop. I consider my strengths as a designer to be around stakeholder management and team relationship management. I like to be involved in all stages of projects from inception and brainstorming through to production and delivery. Including all the problems, project and scope changes that appear along the way. I find the challenge of a development and design issue incredibly rewarding when solved with all parties satisfied with the outcome.

Without thought of user and stakeholders intentions or the technical limitations of a project, the most effective designs/solutions can not be produced and implemented. Nothing is award winning or innovative until it is delivered and engaged with by the user.

Specialties: User Centered Design, Visual Design, Editorial Design, Digital Product Design, User Interface Design, Application Design

At RMA Consulting in London I worked on a 12 month contract to develop ongoing mobile applications (iPhone and iPad) for a global investment bank. The apps we developed were mobile interfaces for a large internal banking system which we delivered to employee and external clients.

In this role I was integral in the the following: brain storming sessions with the Information Architect and Product Manager on what the current and future state of the apps should/could be; the production of app design visuals; and development meetings with Developers regarding finalised production apps.

The apps we developed for the investment bank were hugely successful for the organisation. Our work showed the bank the full potential of their internal system and how their employees and clients could successfully intertact with those systems in the future. Our work effectively changed the bank’s outlook on the future uses of its internal systems.

Detica

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

November 2010 – June 2011 (8 months) London, United Kingdom

I worked with Detica on two different projects over 8 months. The first project was an intranet redesign for a global telecommunications company. This project saw me working directly in-house with an Experience Architect, an in-house development team and an off-shore development team. The goals of the project were to bring time intensive processes into an online workflow and change the employees perception of how they engage with the intranet on a day to day basis.

The second project was a redesign of a corporate website for a large multi-national company. The company needed to refocus it's direction and visual message on the internet. With another Designer and two EAs we changed the design and content structure of the company to modernise its focus and push it forward for the next 5 years. The site was changed from corporate communications to innovation and products with an editorial feel.
