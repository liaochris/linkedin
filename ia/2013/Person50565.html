Boris Grinkot's Summary

A social scientist and ethnographer turned customer experience expert, Boris helps companies improve their product strategies through qualitative and quantitative research, organizational alignment on business objectives, and design of experiments.

When he led multi-million-dollar conversion rate optimization projects, he always focused on helping companies evolve toward a culture of validated learning. He trained business leaders and project teams on generating customer insights and using them to communicate value propositions and create UX designs. Boris has also led internal transformation initiatives that included continuous improvement through cross-functional collaboration, creating customer feedback loops, systematic training, and business model redesign.

In addition to working with major brands in the U.S., Canada and the U.K., such as Johnson & Johnson, Bank of America, The New York Times, UK2, RedGate, Matrikon, Schlumberger, Qwest and NetSuite, Boris has mentored hundreds of innovators and startups on Lean product development methods and requisite economic thinking.
