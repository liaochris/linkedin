Gary Wolyn's Summary

Choosing the Right Agent DOES Make a Difference!

Helping people buy and sell their home has always been a passion of mine, and I am proud to say that I love my job! From helping buyers locate the ideal home for their family to helping sellers list, market, and sell their property, I provide not only the knowledge and skills to accomplish these goals, but also have an array of resources at my disposal to properly approximate the value of homes, help negotiate a sale, and walk you through the home buying or selling process.

If you are looking to buy or sell your home, feel free to call me directly at 585-766-7099!

About me

I received my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (major in marketing, minor in psychology) from RIT, and continually strive to apply the knowledge, skills, and techniques that I gained from there to my personal and profession life. I find that my background and expertise in business and marketing have helped to develop my career in real estate to what it is today.

In my spare time, I volunteer as the Event Chair for the American Cancer Society’s Relay For Life of Greece – an amazing community event and fundraiser held annually to honor cancer Survivors, remember loved ones lost, and to raise cancer awareness. Over the past four years, the events I have managed have raised a total of more than $180,000! (All of which goes directly to the American Cancer Society for cancer research, education, advocacy, and patient services)

I enjoy spending time with my family, being outdoors, traveling, volunteering in the Rochester community, and networking with other professionals.

Specialties

Real estate, home buying and selling, marketing, communication, customer service and relationship management, event planning and management, and non-profit fundraising events coordination.
