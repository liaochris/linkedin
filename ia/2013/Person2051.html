Adrian Tumiati's Summary

After ten successful years in digital marketing, interactive strategy, and project management, Adrian Tumiati now enjoys facilitating and managing relationships and projects for Chicago’s most visible creative agencies, brands, and companies.

With his own experience in interactive branding (including responsive website design, user experience, search engine optimization, email campaigns, and social media), Adrian’s digital marketing expertise uniquely enables him to recognize, advise, and advance creative professionals by placing them into positions that challenge and reward them.

In his spare time, Adrian enjoys exploring Chicago, running 5-Ks, camping, hiking, kayaking, and almost any outdoor activity.
