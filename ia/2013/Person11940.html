Barry Lauterwasser's Summary

Years ago while serving as Corporate Marketing Manager for ProLift I saw an opportunity to serve small to mid size dealers with their marketing function. And in 2009 I formed Symbion Marketing to do just that!

Marketing is not magic, it's not voodoo, and it's easy to understand. Everything we do, well, IS marketing. From the cleanliness to the technicians uniforms to the direct mail and formal promotional strategies we employ, we are "positioning" or marketing our material handling distributorships.

I help dealers grasp that, and look at everything they do and how they do it to be sure it's in line with how they want to be positioned. Then we take the reigns of promoting the business, and get the word out there.

Media opportunities abound for businesses. From pages like this on Linked in, to email, direct mail and other forms of promotion, there are many media sources to use to reach clients and prospects. And helping design messages to help put dealers in Top Of Mind Awareness (TOMA) is key to long-terms success, and generating short-term sales promotions helps to complement those activities.

My background in business management and the operational side of the equipment industry gives me a unique perspective as a marketer and helps to round-out my consultative capabilities. It is my goal to help businesses achieve growth in awareness, which leads to market share improvement and increased revenues and profits.

Specialties

Understanding marketing channels, the members, and their relationships. How new media can mix with traditional marketing to create a 360 degree program that gets awareness. Teaching clients to share their knowledge and become "Thought Leaders." How to help salespeople achieve results by selling smart, and efficiently.
