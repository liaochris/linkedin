David Jirousek's Summary

David Jirousek has 12 years of town, county and regional planning experience in both private sector consulting and public sector planning. Prior to joining LSL, David served as a planning consultant, County Director of Planning and Building Services, Senior Town Planner and Regional Planner.

In addition to long range planning and special projects, David has managed capital projects from concept to successful completion, including CDBG water and sewer lines, roads, buildings, parking lots and parks. In the capacity of development reviewer, David ensured development compliance by enforcing codes, inspecting construction sites and providing clear guidance to the construction community. His current planning experience covers the spectrum of the field, including development agreements, planned development concept and master plans, development plans, subdivisions, zoning map & text amendments, and all other land development applications. David is also an eff ective grant writer; most recently he secured federal funds for the Town of Ridgeland (SC) from the National Park Service Battlefield Preservation Program (2013).

Current assignments include ongoing zoning administration, master plan updates, zoning ordinance revisions, special projects and plan implementation.
