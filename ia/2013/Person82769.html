Richard Breen's Summary

FIRM OVERVIEW
----------------------------
Based in Louisville, Kentucky, Richard Breen Law Offices, P.S.C. is an aggressive law firm with deep roots in the Louisville community. Since in 1979, Richard Breen has been representing clients across the state of Kentucky and today is engaged almost exclusively in representing plaintiffs in personal injury cases.

WHO WE REPRESENT
-------------------------------
We represent individuals in personal injury, automobile accidents, wrongful death, insurance, workers' rights claims, and much more.

Specialties

Personal injury, automobile accidents, wrongful death, insurance, workers' rights claims, slip and fall, nursing home liability, tractor trailer accidents, wrongful shooting, premise liability
