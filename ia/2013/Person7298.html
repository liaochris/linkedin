Andrew Yee's Summary

Andrew Yee is an entrepreneur, project manager and consultant responsible for strategy, business development, sales and marketing operations for Prospecta an software products and SAP professional services company.

Andrew is responsible for go to market strategies for two enterprise software products, establishing strategic alliances and establishing a sales channel.

His background includes Project Management and Consulting, Solution Sales, Business Development and over 16 years of SAP professional services to large enterprise business globally.

Prior to Prospecta, Andrew was a manager with KPMG Consulting SAP Practice, one of the world’s largest management and technology companies and with Hewlett Packard, a technology company operating in over 170 countries around the world.
