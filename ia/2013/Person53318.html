Hayden Montgomerie's Summary

Hayden has ten years work experience in education and social development. He currently advises the Ministry of Youth Development on youth engagement and social media, has previously been an education consultant at UNESCO Bangkok, and spent seven years at the New Zealand National Commission for UNESCO.

Outside of work Hayden is a recognised leader and governance expert in the NGO sector. Hayden played a leading role as one of the founding members of the United Nations Youth Association of New Zealand (now UN Youth New Zealand), and leading that organisation in its early years before and going on to be National President of the United Nations Association of New Zealand (UNANZ). He is currently a trustee of the New Zealand Youth Global Awareness Trust Board, and a mentor and friend to many young people working in the NGO sector.

Hayden is a Fellow of the Royal Society of the Arts (RSA), previously serving on the Board of the RSA A+NZ, and is very engaged in the work of the Asia New Zealand Foundation through its Young Leaders Network.

Hayden is an amateur photographer and art collector, enjoys New Zealand wine and loves to spend time in cafes and restaurants with friends, dreaming of travel to new places, and developing ideas for new social enterprises.
