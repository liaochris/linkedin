Andy Jarabak - Marketing Consultant's Summary

What I Do

I am a marketing consultant focused on helping small or medium sized businesses in a variety of industries to increase profits by providing benefit to their marketing, lead genersation, sales and closing, and planning efforts. I do this by using my over 30 years of experience in marketing consulting to help businesses take advantage of what works not only in traditional marketing techniques such as direct mail and TV commercials but also on email and social marketing. Thanks for taking the time to visit my LinkedIn profile and do not hesitate to contact me about your needs or to discuss any issues related to my specialties.

I have experience in marketing consulting work with businesses in manufacturing and service industries such as private golf courses, insurance, promotional, and large scale manufacturing. I also have worked with businesses who focused on obtainng governement contracts.
