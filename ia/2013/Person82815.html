Lovely Kumar's Summary

My professional experience of 16 years has been in the areas of sales and marketing, management education, training and psychometric assessments.

My educational background which combines economics ,communication, management ,training, learning, psychometric assessments and facilitation ensures a strong understanding of both learning/training as well as business needs.

My job requires adeptness in mapping an external organization's requirements and crafting a learning/ training solution to meet that.Simultaneously, I am required to meet internal growth and profit expectations.

Since I am very passionate about training and psychometric assessments as a method of facilitating people to reach their full potential, I have obtained certifications like International Vocational Qualification in teaching & training from City & Guilds UK, Advanced certification on the Extended DISC system, PPA+HJA &TAS from Thomas Assessments, Philip's ROI methodology for assessing effectiveness of HR initiatives.

As part of a pilot project by Parijaath to introduce online courses in teaching and training in India I cleared semester 1 of Masters of Science in Learning, Facilitation and Management from the Concordia University, Chicago.

To enhance performance in my role I have undergone a bootcamp in social media marketing from DigitalVidya.

Have also attended programs by famous authors such as Skip Ross, Ron Johnson and Shiv Khera.

I possess a strong commitment to excellence,
keen planning and implemention skills and a thorough understanding of management and training.

My speciality programs are:
1. Certification on the Extended DISC System
2. Enhancing sales through DISC
3. Increasing managerial effectiveness through giving of positive and developmental feedback
4. Powerful presentations

I have gained exposure to MBTI, T-groups, Transactional analysis through attending workshops on the same.
