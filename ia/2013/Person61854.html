Jennifer Jones' Summary

Founder of award winning firm Jennifer Jones & Partners, a marketing and communications advisory firm for venture capital, corporate venture and private equity. Jennifer has more than 25 years of venture capital,technology/healthcare and social media marketing experience including 23 years in a broad range of professional services marketing creating thought leadership and partnership opportunities. JJ&P invented marketing strategies currently implemented by many top tier venture firms today. Legacy and current clients include: Merck Global Health Innovation Ventures, Menlo Ventures, Goldman Sachs, Wilson,Sonsini, Goodrich & Rosati, Dell Ventures, Mayfield Fund, GSR Ventures, Allegis Capital, Claremont Creek Ventures, Psilos Group and Versant Ventures.

Jennifer was the first marketing partner for Mayfield Fund; former Executive Vice President and General Manager of Regis McKenna Inc., a marketing consulting company best known for launching Apple and Intel; Creator/Host of Bay Area’s first technology TV show; called High Tech Visions which ran on Bay Area TV in 1988. Jennifer began her career as a broadcast TV news reporter and producer for CBS news stations in Bay Area, Los Angeles and Salt Lake City.

Creator and Host of social media video/podcast called Marketing Voices™ airing currently on ITUNES and ConnectedSocialMedia.com.

Board member of Society for New Communications Research and Advisory Board member of IBF: Corporate Venturing and Innovation Conference and Xconomy.

Specialties: Branding and Positioning; Venture Capital Marketing; Social Media/Marketing; Professional Services Marketing Leadership; Public Relations
