Chris Leydon's Summary

Inspiring people to do brilliant things through technology and digital media is my passion. I have worked with a number of pioneering technology startups and media companies to achieve these two goals.

Currently I'm a founding partner and Producer in Keyone Productions, a film production company which specialises creating creative video content for the tech industry and works with event organisers to provide reliable, high quality, live and recorded event video.

Clients I've worked with include AOL's TechCrunch, producing a series in 2011 called "The Roundabout Tapes" and The Europas (European Tech Startup Awards), The Guardian & The Observer (Documenting tech startup events) and Beet.tv for the IPTV World Summit, in partnership with the Financial Times and BBC Worldwide.

I'm also one of the organisers of Tomorrow's Web, a quarterly tech event that aims to inspire people to create, build and develop brilliant things with the web. The event boasts 150 in attendance, 3 keynote accomplished speakers and is live streamed around the world to an audience. Each event has been incredibly well received and we've managed to cultivate an incredibly engaged and talented community.

I love technology and I love video and creative media, I try and fuse the two together in my work wherever possible.
