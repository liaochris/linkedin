Jonathan Vincentz's Experience

CEO and Founder

Mondano.com

January 2012 – July 2013 (1 year 7 months) London, United Kingdom / San Francisco, California, USA

Mondano Ltd. operated Mondano.com – an online webshop selling replica designer furniture to the European market. The roles as CEO and founder included founding the company and all practicalities regarding setting up a company in London. It resulted in an operational, very well optimized, and fully automated webshop that operated out of Google Campus in the “European Silicon Valley” Shoreditch, London.
Mondano.com imported replica furniture from Hong Kong to United Kingdom based on the EU legislation on internal trading within the European Union. As the European Union legislation changed, Mondano Ltd. was immediately terminated, as the legitimacy of the business model was now compromised.

Strategic Business Consultant

Comunit Media Ltd.

January 2012 – July 2013 (1 year 7 months)

The role as a Strategic Business Consultant was created as an extension to the role as CEO at Comunit Media Ltd. to secure the optimization of the knowledge base for Comunit Media Ltd. after the transition in January 2012. The role includes strategic, operational and facilitating aspects of the continuous management of Comunit Media Ltd.

CEO

Comunit Media Ltd.

January 2010 – January 2012 (2 years 1 month) St. Julians, Malta

As CEO of Comunit Media Ltd. – a company operating websites in the online gaming industry – the daily operations included contact with authorities, lawyers, accountants, media and the internal departments in the company – especially the Forum Manager, Head of Business Development and IT. Communication was a vital aspect due to the nature of the activities as well as internal organizational restructuring was a dominating field. All responsibilities regarding the management of funds were placed in this position.

The role as CEO of Comunit Media Ltd. was performed in a Southern European country, just 200 miles off the coast of Africa, with regulations and norms relatively different from Denmark, which again demanded an open mind towards cultural differences – expecially at an office with 7 nationalities present.

In 2010 Comunit Media Ltd. experienced an appx. 30% growth on EBIT, a rising profit margin and consolidation of the market leading position in Denmark. In 2011 Comunit Media Ltd. was internationalizing the business in an attempt to build a broader product portfolio for a less risky and more stable revenue generation. As a result, the main asset of Comunit Media Ltd. was successfully sold in early 2012 for an undisclosed amount.

Besides the very operational role as CEO for Comunit Media Ltd., the position automatically included the less time consuming role as CEO of Comunit Media Holding Ltd. This position in the holding company included managing the overall strategic lines of all companies fully or partly owned by Comunit Media Holding Ltd. (amongst others Comunit Media Ltd.). Strategies were discussed and drawn from Comunit Media Holding Ltd. in areas such as markets to approach, mergers and acquisitions, cash flows, management staff, licenses and others.

Business Development Manager

Comunit Media Ltd.

September 2009 – December 2009 (4 months) St. Julians, Malta

The position as Business Development Manager was covering optimization of existing revenue drivers and development of new ones in the same as well as in related industries. This position offered an excellent insight in the dynamics and possibilities of one of the worlds fastest changing online environments. Gaining this insight allowed me to work my way up in the company from the inside, to the fast promotion to become the CEO. A position I earned after 4 months in the role as Business Development Manager.
