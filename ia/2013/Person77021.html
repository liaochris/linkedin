Kristie Helms' Summary

I graduated with a degree in print journalism the same year that the first web browser launched and since then I've had a front-row seat to one of the biggest evolutions that communications has ever seen.

As an interactive and social marketing strategist since the early days of the internet, I've connected brands with people in a constantly evolving digital space.

I head the global interactive content and social business program @StateStreet, serving as strategic adviser on social media and digital communication activities across the company, from social influencers to marketing support, client outreach, internal communications and more, as social communications are integrated into many facets of State Street's business.
