Kim Brame's Summary

twitter.com/ciproductions
I LOVE what I do, if I didn't I would not have spent 25+ years in the film/video production industry. I am very proud of the production teams that I have worked with over the years we have been honored with 52 awards, national & international.

I have had the unique opportunity to work in the following areas; major motion pictures and broadcast media including;national and regional commercials, national and international sporting events, political marketing, network news, documentaries and corporate/industrials.

I find commercials and corporate/industrials to be the areas that I find the most FUN,rewarding and fulfilling. True broadcast quality video on the internet has taken the video medium to a whole new level.

I am told that my strongest talent is in interviewing individuals. Many of these interviews are corporate executives and their clients.From these interviews I strive to deliver corporate messaging that is "real". By "real" I mean that I have "a gift" for putting individuals at ease in front of the camera and their message comes across as unrehearsed, confident and most important- sincere. I deliver real messaging from real people as easily as I do with paid talent. I have been honored to assist over 160 different companies and not-for-profit organizations deliver their message through various forms of media to the public, stockholders and employees.

Specialties: Produced videos for a variety of industries;
Medical, Manufacturing, Pharma, IT, Telecoms, Food Service & Processing, Trucking, Real Estate Dev., Mining, Agriculture, Education, Public Safety, Auto, Legal, Airlines, Boating, Intl.Shipping and Hospitality.
I have directed and produced; LIVE broadcast productions, flash projects, webinars, webcasts, training and marketing tools, commercials, VNRs video-news-releases, electronic media kits and commercials both for TV & internet.
