Karel Mc Intosh's Summary

Communications has been in my blood since I was a child. I got my first glasses at age 10, thanks to devouring classics like "Things Fall Apart" and "Wuthering Heights" - sometimes in the dark. Ah...the folly of youth.

Years later, it has served me well, translating into a talent for wordplay and communications strategy. Now, I fill my days with leading communications workshops, writing speeches, web copy, and more for companies, and creating, writing, editing, and publishing stellar content for my startup – Outlish.com.

Interested in attending my communications workshops in Trinidad and Tobago, and the wider Caribbean? You'll get:
- business writing
- social media marketing
- writing for marketing and PR
- customer service, and more.

Feel free to reach me at karel@livewiredgroup(dot)com, or call me at 749-6027 to chat about your training needs.

Lead Communications Trainer

Livewired Group

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

January 2013 – Present (9 months) Trinidad and Tobago

Livewired Group designs and delivers interactive business writing and communications training that empowers professionals and marketers to communicate effectively to achieve their goals.

For companies who want their teams to walk out of workshops feeling smarter, sharper, and more skilled than when they walked in, Livewired Group achieves this with customised, in-house training that’s delivered in insightful, memorable, super-lively and easy-to-grasp ways, and post–training benefits that support you long after workshops are over.

My role is to design and deliver workshops for various areas, some of which are perfect for everyone in your organisation. Some are specifically designed for your marketing and public relations teams.

For everyone in your organisation:
- business writing
- presentation skills and public speaking
- interpersonal skills and personal branding.
- customer service.

For your marketing and public relations teams:
- speechwriting
- copywriting
- feature writing and news releases
- social media and content marketing.

You can also request training in other communications areas. I’ll design a workshop from scratch just for you. Find out more at livewiredgroup.com.
