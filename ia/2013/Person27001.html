Corey Guilbault's Summary

Sometimes described as ‘hard to tack down’, it became difficult to summarize my career and expertise on a business card years ago. I have since stopped trying. Instead, I like to tell people that my father is a polymer chemist and my mother is an art teacher. I grew up in a true left-brain/right-brain household. I love creativity and innovation but also harbor a great respect for process and pragmatism. Great ideas need brilliant execution. The distance between “we could...” and “we did...” is tricky to navigate. I’ve spent my career learning how to bridge that gap.

Through the years I have worked as a strategic consultant, creative director, operations manager, marketer, art director, copywriter, account director and interactive producer. I’ve managed budgets, expectations and dynamic teams. I’ve also developed analytical frameworks, sophisticated digital tools, strategic plans, business models, and extremely goofy, award-winning viral video campaigns.

As an entrepreneur I learned to build efficiency into everything I did and to appreciate the value of anyone’s time and money - whether they are a client, a prospect, a consumer or a business partner.

Specialties: Ideation, operations, user experience, strategic planning, process development and management, marketing and brand building, digital deployment
