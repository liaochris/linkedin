Dave Mcilroy's Summary

• Have founded, built and sold three companies in the telecommunication and broadcasting industries. From equipment and long distance resale to cable TV / broadband ISP.
• Continuing development and operations are ongoing in the Internet Broadcast space.
• Formulated an international presence with a webcasting company starting in 1996. Clients included Fortune 500, Government and Universities
• Created the brand 777 Long Distance (which became 777online), to market a specialty phone service. Integrated computer hardware and software to leverage the public switched telephone network, enabling a unique toll free calling area.
• Co-owned and built from start up, a rental car fleet to over one thousand vehicles, established retail outlets and regional branding. Provided vehicles to the Motion Picture industry and pre-sold packages in Europe to vacationers arriving to North America later in that season.

Currently Playfullscreen is shipping a broadcasting system that produces multi-camera mixing, instant reply, slow motion, on screen graphics and a highlight editing package ready for network news servers.

Specialties
