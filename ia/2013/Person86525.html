Ozgur Alaz's Summary

Özgür Alaz (1983) is a marketing trends expert and an entrepreneur. He is the co-founder of Promoqube, a digital brand communications agency which provides brands with efficient results in the social media age and Useful Business Development Agency which develops new marketing technologies.

He started sharing his ideas in his blog marketallica.com, which is one of the oldest marketing blogs, in order to inspire “marketing people” about innovations. He became one of the most successful members of the world’s biggest trend network, springspotters.com. His articles has been cited by leading websites such as psfk.com, trendwatching.com, tompeters.com and springwise.com and he’s one of the co-writers of the books Age of Conversation and “The Leverage Effect”. Apart from organizing Likemind Istanbul, which is an event that brings people with similar mindsets together, he took part in many marketing conferences, such as Marketing Summit, Webrazzi Summit, Social Media Summit, and shared his ideas about trends and digital strategy.

Promoqube, co-founded by him, is an innovation center which works with the biggest brands in Turkey, knows social networks from A to Z, therefore, gets very efficient results. It’s the first agency that has been included to Facebook PDC program. Promoqube creates while following innovations and leads its sector. It has been developed social media communication tools such as “Sense Digital PR Platform”, “Campaigns Platform” and Winnertweet.

Useful, is a business development agency which develops new technologies and aims to change the marketing world. Carrying the excitement of developing projects which are innovative and suitable for a global audience, Useful has the ability to find new business development opportunities and actualize them quickly.
