Darrell Hornbacher's Summary

Over the last 25 years I've owned a dozen businesses. During this period I was continually frustrated with financing my ideas. There had to be a better way to get money in the hands of small business owners.

In 2005 I founded The Midas Financial Company. Since then my company has obtained in excess of $400,000,000 for small biz owners. No, we're not Wells Fargo but we're pretty damn proud of what we've accomplished.

If you are a small biz owner that needs money, you owe it to yourself to give me a call. If I can't get it done, NO ONE can! Our stable of products include: Biz loans, lines of credit, SBA packaging, equipment leasing, cc advance funding, stock/security loans, business credit, factoring and credit repair programs that work. No credit check consumer financing. In most cases there are no up front fees for our clients. I also pay the most generous referral commissions in the industry.....

Specialties: Business and personal credit. Small business finance on both the debt and equity sides. NO Credit Check consumer financing.
