Edna Dimataga-Fernandez's Summary

Edna has worked and consulted in the insurance and financial services industry since 1994. Throughout her career, she has enjoyed significant success in the areas of leadership, strategic planning, management, sales, marketing and business development. Her dynamic public speaking and presentation skills have been instrumental in developing relationships and gaining consensus with centers of influence.

She is active in the insurance industry, served on the Board of Directors of the National Association of Insurance & Financial Advisors (NAIFA), Los Angeles Chapter, and served as President from 2010-2011. During her Presidency, NAIFA Los Angeles received the GOLD Association Achievement Award from NAIFA California for excelling in 4 key areas: Member Benefits, Advocacy, Membership, and Governance. She is also a graduate of NAIFA California's Leadership In Life Institute (LILI).

She is currently affiliated with the Los Angeles Chapter of Women in Insurance & Financial Services (WIFS), the preeminent professional organization for women in the insurance and financial services industry.

She supports social causes that focus on women, children, health/wellness, and education. She graduated from UCLA with a BA degree in Economics, and received her MBA from the Keller Graduate School of Management.

SPECIAL PROJECTS & INTERESTS
After several life-changing events which included the death of her father at the age of 47 due to a heart attack, her mother's battle with breast cancer at the age of 56, and her own personal struggle with infertility, she soon developed a passion for health and wellness. In 2011, she founded The Wellness Institute, a company that provides wellness education and services focusing on the “holistic approach”.
