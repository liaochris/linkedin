Jay Harper's Summary

Sunzoo Studios is a full-service music production company based in Los Angeles, specializing in custom music for Ads, TV, Radio, and Sound Logos for Brand Clients.

Our sonic branding encompasses many tactics intended to convey organizational or product identity, enhance consumers' experience of a product or service; OR extend an organization's relationship with its audience.

We've established a reputation as a "go to crew" focused mainly on the production, song-writing, and licensing of the Pop / AC / Urban genres. Our Mission - Support our clients in the creation or expansion of their corporate culture... with audio solutions.

Our Vision - Deliver unforgettable experiences that impact the minds and hearts of target audiences resulting in increased revenue.

A List clients include: McDonald's, Universal Audio, Sony / ATV, Universal Music Group, Banshee Music, and The Ant Farm
