Johan Nieuwerth's Summary

Founder of JB-IT right after the "burst of the bubble" in 2001, which further developed in the Sqills Group today. With subsidiaries in Public Transport, Projects, Products and International branches located in Istanbul, Turkey, and Tunis, Tunisia, the Sqills Group implements high-end internet solutions to increase efficiency in three ways: increasing sales, reducing costs, and improving customer satisfaction.

Sqills was ranked 4th in Deloitte Technology Fast 50 NL in 2008, 44th in Deloitte Technology Fast 500 EU in 2008, and 7th in Deloitte Technology Fast 50 Benelux in 2009. These rankings were all due to growth rates in 2008 and 2009 of respectively 2.834% and 2.154% over the preceding 5 years.

Specialties

Innovation on the boundaries between IT and business logic; turning concepts into Proof of Concepts which can be further developed into long lasting products and solutions. Using an analytic view to translate concept and high-level representations of the "subject domain" into working technology.
