Centre For Robotic Surgery's Summary

Dr.Mahesh Desai obtained his MBBS (1966) and M.S. in General Surgery (1970) from B.J. Medical College, Pune University. He received fellowship (FRCS) from the Royal College of Surgeons of London and Edinburgh in 1973. In U.K.he obtained specialized training in Urology. He returned to India and started his practice at Ahmedabad. Soon he realized the importance of institutionalized treatment for various kidney diseases.In the year 1976 he joined hands with Dr. V.V. Desai and established Muljibhai Patel Urological Hospital (MPUH) in a rural area Nadiad in the State of Gujarat in India with the aim of providing most modern treatment for the common man.

He was the Chairman of the Department of Urology since 1983 till 12th June, 2010. He is the Managing Trustee of the MPUH and Honorary Managing Director of Muljibhai Patel Society for Research in Nephro-Urology since 16th February, 2001. Since 12th June, he is also the Medical Director of MPUH. He also holds the position of Director of Jayaramdas Patel Academic Centre.

He has mastered the most modern treatment for the stone disease and developed innovative techniques in Percutaenous Nephrolithotrispy (PCNL), Ureteroscopy and ESWL. So far he has done more than 12000 PCNL, probably the largest series in the world. He expanded the use of ultrasound in Urology and established, for the first time, the service of kidney transplantation in Gujarat. He did his first transplant in 1980. 1845 Renal Transplants have been done so far at MPUH. He organized the epidemiological survey for prostatic diseases in ageing male population in Kheda / Charotar district, Gujarat, India.

He was the then Treasurer, Hon. Secretary and the President of the West Zone Chapter of Urological Society of India and he also the Treasurer of Urological Society of India. He was the Chairman of the Endourological Section of Urological Society of India. He also was the National Delegate of Society International d' Urologie.

Centre For Robotic Surgery's Experience

Center For Robotic Surgery

Currently holds this position

Centre For Robotic Surgery (CRS) - MPUH is one of its kind in Asia - the first hospital in the country devoted entirely to Nephrology and Urology, and awarded with the ISO-9001: 2008 Certificate, certifying our quality management system for design, development and delivery of clinical services for Urology & Nephrology - including Kidney Transplantation to global standards.

In 2011, MPUH won the prestigious Indian Merchants' Chamber (IMC) Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Award Trophy. IMC RBNQA follows the stringent Malcolm Baldrige criteria of the USA for evaluation of Award applications. MPUH is the first hospital in Guajrat to have won this coveted Trophy.

We were able to meet the high cost of ultramodern equipment and other infrastructure, thanks to philanthropic gestures from society and the Trustees of the hospital. Besides investment in modern technology, the donations so received are also used towards rendering services to those patients who cannot afford the cost of treatment. The cost of ‘free treatment’ we have rendered so far exceeds Rs.10 crores.

Keeping the above in mind, it is our endeavour to provide the best possible medical care in our chosen field at affordable price. We started our journey in 1978 by imparting Health Services.

Over the years, we have added Research & Development and Post Graduate Education in the field of Urology & Nephrology - including Kidney Transplantation. The Jayaramdas Patel Academic Centre (JPAC) is considered comparable to the best such facility in the world.
