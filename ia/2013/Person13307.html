Benson Koh's Summary

As a 5-year veteran in the Real Estate industry after leaving the Army as a professional soldier, Benson has been pioneering the field with innovation and breakthroughs particularly with internet media as one of his core strength as a Realtor to deliver results.

His accolade includes distinguished awards from numerous internet portals top agent, inaugural Institute of Estate Agent (IEA) Platinum award and is a consistent top performer known to many of the same field.

Well known for his strong and logical analysis, Benson always share his views on the Real Estate trending on his high viewership blog which has been incepted since 2007. His views are often mirrored and featured in numerous Real Estate publications and is always sought after by his clients ranging from investors who just begun, avid/strong investors in Singapore Real Estate, home seekers who requires delicate attention and even business operators whom requires special analytics.

His experience includes brokering several high profile prime properties since his good number of years with global agency Knight Frank and SLP Realty. With a good portfolio of High Networth Individual (HNWI) clients from the globe. His reach of properties include regal homes in prime and niche investment segments such as shop houses and retail spaces.

A leader who believes in growing his team in professionalism from strength to strength, he partners with Bruce Lye and heads a team of more than 150 licensed estate agents in 5 Business units namely, SRI5000 Privileged, SRI5000 Find a Home, SRI5000 Business Space, SRI5000 Corporate Leasing & SRI5000 Project Marketing.

For any business opportunities or real estate portfolio advisories, Benson can be reached at benson@sri5000.com or +65 9091-5000.
