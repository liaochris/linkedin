Deb Hester's Summary

Deb Hester
deb@debhester.biz
(866) 324-0705

Candidate recruiting / sourcing since 1985. Experience includes sourcing, screening and interviewing, coordinating with hiring managers through evaluation processes, reference checking, negotiations and offer letter preparation. Sourcing background includes internet sourcing — beginning with comprehensive AIRS training — traditional cold-calling, networking and referral programs. Successfully developed, trained and managed in-house and telecommuting sourcing teams for large IT consulting firms and Deb Hester & Associates. Environments most familiar with include engineering, public relations and advertising, high-tech, pharmaceutical, manufacturing, research, health care and seniors. Past presenter on Internet sourcing techniques at Seattle’s Employee Management Association conference.

Specialties

Sourcing and interviewing for civil, electrical, mechanical, environmental, geotechnical, and structural engineering; environmental and regulatory specialists for water resources and hydro power engineering. Sourcing and interviewing for public relations agencies personnel at all levels for technology, fashion, health, automotive, travel & tourism, food & beverage, luxury, and wine & spirits practices.

• Cold calling against the leads list.
• Screening and presenting 248 appropriately-skilled engineering candidates.
• Coordinating with hiring managers, running reference checks and assisting with offer letters for new hires primarily on the west coast.
