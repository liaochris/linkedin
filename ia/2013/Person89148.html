Matthew Smith's Summary

Curiosity and an unwillingness to accept the answer 'that's just the way it is' has driven my professional career.

It began with my undergraduate and post graduate studies in Religious Studies and Philosophy. Following academia, I wanted to learn the mechanisms of the business world and I started with sales in the Insurance and Financial sector. Once I realized that sales at it's essence is problem solving, I became rather good at it. After 3 years, I left to found S & G Real Estate Investments Inc which became an award winning residential real estate company in Des Moines, IA.

My experience in residential real estate caused me to see measured inefficiencies in the way buyers, sellers & agents engaged online. That was the genesis for the idea behind RealEstateFanPages.com. Presently, RealEstateFanPages.com is providing an elegant solution to integrate real estate agent's listings on the world's largest social media platform that is Facebook.
