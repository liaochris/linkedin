Laurie Lee Dovey's Summary

Laurie Lee Dovey is a highly awarded writer and photographer, marketing and media consultant, and speaker.

During her 30-year communications and marketing career, she has had more than 1,700 magazine articles and hundreds of images published.

She also served as a magazine editor, columnist and photographer, newspaper columnist, television scriptwriter and video series host talent.

As a consultant, she's helped scores of companies develop and bring their product or service messages to media and consumers, and helped build their media relations programs.

Laurie Lee currently serves as

• The Executive director/CEO of the Professional Outdoor Media Association (POMA)
• The Owner of LLD INK, www.lldovey.com
• A partner in BLT Media
• The corporate profiles editor for the NRA's America's 1st Freedom magazine, and
• A freelance content developer and photographer
• Social media specialist

She is a sought-after speaker and executive trainer on all aspects of journalism, content development, photography, media relations, social media, marketing, and the outdoors. She also presents communications-based workshops at colleges and business/trade events.

Dovey continually mentors individuals who are committed to careers in outdoor industry communications.

The Professional Outdoor Media Association (POMA) is a group of individual communicators and Corporate Partners who believe in, defend, support and promote the heritage of hunting, fishing, shooting and traditional outdoor sports through writing, photography and other means. By doing so, members hope to educate the general public about these sports and encourage more participation in them. The organization serves the membership by helping members grow professionally, improve their skills, better their working environments and enhance their businesses.
