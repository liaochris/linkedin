Kevin Craine, MBA's Summary

Kevin Craine is a professional writer and editor, a published author, a popular podcast host, and an internationally recognized expert on document strategy design. His book, "Designing a Document Strategy," has sold in over 30 countries and he has listeners and readers worldwide.

Kevin has been the written voice for some of North America's leading companies, creating strategic content for the web, marketing, social media, and more. Kevin's client list includes many well-known brands like IBM, Kodak and Intel, along with a long list of individuals and start-ups from a wide variety of industries.

Prior to starting Craine Communications Group, Kevin was the founding editor of Document magazine, an international technology trade magazine. Kevin also served for many years as Director of Document Services for Regence BlueCross BlueShield where he managed high volume print and mail production operations in Seattle, Portland and Salt Lake City. He also spent time at IKON as an Enterprise Document Management consultant working with national and major accounts. Kevin has also been, at one point or another, an adjunct university professor, a black belt martial artist, and a professional guitarist. Indeed, his newest book "The First Time Guitar Buyers Guide" has been recently published to acclaim nationwide.

Kevin holds an MBA in the Management of Science and Technology as well as a BA in Communications and Marketing.
