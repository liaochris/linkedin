Carmen Cardoza's Summary

A freelance marketing strategist, designer, and web developer, I have pooled together a diverse background of over 13 years to offer a holistic menu of services in marketing, design, and development.

My mission is to fit together the right marketing strategies, with the right message, and the right visuals, to create dynamic offline & online connections with the right market for start-ups, small businesses, and non-profit organizations.

From project management for a small non-profit in New York City, to financial development for an international humanitarian organization, to internet marketing for an online employment ad agency serving one of the largest industries in the country - I have garnered a keen understanding of the challenges faced by small businesses and non-profit organizations confronting big ambitions in an ever changing market.

I partner with clients to streamline progressive marketing initiatives, produce original design solutions for strong brand and identity positioning, and outfit my clients with outstanding online presence. I enjoy working with start-ups, small businesses and non-profits from start to launch, and beyond.

I author CARMEN CARDOZA BUSINESS MATTERS - a blog where I document the trials, triumphs and lessons learned about freelancing. I also share design tutorials, development tips and marketing insight for every level. I also author POPPIES & PENCILSHAVINGS - a personal creative outlet blog.
