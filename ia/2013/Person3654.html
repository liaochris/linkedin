Dr. Alex Anastasiou's Summary

Dr. Alex Anastasiou is a Board Certified Psychiatrist who specializes in treating Anxiety, Depression and ADHD in the East Bay Area. He is a Diplomate of the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology and is a physician licensed to perform mental health evaluations and prescribe medications in a safe and effective manner if needed. He treats Teenagers and Adults.

Dr. Anastasiou was born in Livermore, CA and grew up in Pleasanton. His parents Dr. Mary and Dr. Stephen are Pediatricians still practicing in the area. He graduated from the University of California, Davis with a B.S. in Biological Sciences with an emphasis in Neurobiology, Physiology, and Behavior as well as a minor in Psychology. After Medical School, he completed his Post Graduate Psychiatry Residency Training at the University of Nevada School of Medicine where he served as a Chief Resident.

He has given numerous lectures to medical students and physicians across different specialties, presenting at both Grand Rounds and Resident Research Day. He also received the "Master of the PRITE (Psychiatry Resident-In-Training Exam) Award" two years in a row after scoring in the top 90th percentile among all Psychiatrists trained in the United States.
