Kevin Hutchinson's Summary

Project One is a leading programme management company with an outstanding track record of making change happen.

We specialise in leading and delivering business critical change, acting as a catalyst to achieve sustainable results in partnership with our clients.

Our people are highly experienced and passionate about change leadership. Together we have created a business environment where life is exciting, balanced and rewarding, and where integrity comes as standard.

If you would like to find out more about our service, style, values or working with us, please visit us at www.projectone.com

Specialties
----------------
Programme and change management, business transformation, coaching, mentoring and benefits delivery.


About Kevin Hutchinson
-----------------------------------
Kevin joined Project One with over 20 years experience across a breadth of sectors, during which he structured and delivered technical and business change programmes with global reach.

He is a highly analytical, end-to-end process thinker and decision-maker with a natural coaching management style.

Kevin is a Fellow of the BCS Chartered Institute for IT, Chartered IT Practitioner and Chartered Engineer. He has also been practitioner certified in MSP and Prince2.
