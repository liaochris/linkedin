Josh Gidcumb, Broker/Owner/REALTOR's Projects

  • Renovation Project Highlight: FOXY Coffee Shop and Deli

    • July 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Josh Gidcumb, Broker/Owner/REALTOR

    As the original site of the historic Hamilton County Bank, this property was most recently occupied by a local insurance company. The building was acquired for a private client seeking to build-out a new upscale coffee shop and deli in McLeansboro, IL, which required a full-gut, remodel and restoration. The client’s specific goals going into the project were to fully renovate the structure and preserve the integrity of the historical facade. Additionally, their long term goal was to ensure the longevity of the building’s structure and facade for future generations to enjoy and appreciate. The building also needed to be energy efficient, and energy conscious. It still houses the original bank vault, vault door and iron cage used for securing local funds for so many years. After all, it just made sense to preserve this part of the building’s history. Looking back on the renovation and construction process, it’s almost as if the entire project hinged around the bank vault in some fashion.
