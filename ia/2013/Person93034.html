Michael Sponhour, ABC's Summary

Accomplished communications professional with strong track record of fiscally responsible leadership, strategic management, and excellence in public relations, government relations, and community outreach. Accredited Business Communicator (ABC) with experience leading key statewide initiatives, assessing their effectiveness and explaining them to the legislature, the media and the general public. Adept in developing social media strategy and creating standards for brand identity. Strong background in managing high-profile events, crisis communication and successful news media placements.

Led South Carolina's advertising and public relations campaign for the 2010 Census, which helped the Palmetto State tie for the nation's greatest improvement in census mail response rate. This campaign won an IABC Gold Quill Award of Merit, a Southern Region Silver Quill and "Best in Show" in the 2010 South Carolina Palmetto Awards.

Specialties

Strategic planning, media relations, social media, crisis communication, writing, internal communication, communications measurement.
