Marketing and Advertising Strategist with an emphasis in Social Media and Internet Marketing:

Audra Carpenter, the CEO and Founder of the Social Marketing Center, the premier destination for online marketing product reviews, jobs, hire a pro, free software downloads, daily deals, and how to's.

Owning and operating numerous agencies over the years gives Audra access to both the triumphs and struggles of owning a brick and mortar business as well as the experience to meet the needs in today’s New Marketing Economy.

If you would like to learn more about either consulting, speaking engagements, or website review please go to www.socialmarketingcenter.com (COMING SOON).

Marketing Strategy in the New Economy

SMC began out of a need to service the traditional businesses marketing and advertising needs online. As I have evolved and my clients have shared with me what they want to be successful online, I have been developing a website that will meet these ever changing needs.

As so many have come to see, when it comes to marketing online, the brick and mortar business is different and as a Marketing Strategist we have created products and systems to meet these new economy needs.

If you want to learn how to market in the New Economy to the Brick and Mortar business then let me know. We are currently developing a ‘done for you’ training system to show you how to approach, service and grow your own marketing agency in this niche.

For more products, resources, articles, videos and tips go to www.socialmarketingcenter.com (COMING SOON).
