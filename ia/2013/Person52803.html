Henryk Stawicki's Summary

Educated and experienced as a strategic manager I am bringing change and strategic improvements to the world of design, art and culture.

Within the last 10 years I have performed responsible roles of project manager and developer, exhibition producer, event coordinator, administrator and personal assistant. I had the pleasure to develop and co-create the following projects:
- Artists-in-Residence Program at Design Centrum Kielce;
- Alternative Education Program at Design Centrum Kielce;
- Labdesign.kielce.pl portal;
- Lodz Fresh Up! Program at the Lodz Art Center;
- Lodz Federation of NGO's;
- 'Alternatywa Skatepark & Workshops' center;
- 'Alternatywa BMX' Association.

Other responsible roles include:
- Main Program Manager at the Lodz Fotofestiwal - International Festival of Photography;
- Exhibition Coordinator and Logistics Coordinator at the International Lodz Design Festival;
- Chairman of the Lodz Federation of NGO's.
