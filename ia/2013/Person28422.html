Christopher Buecheler's Summary

I am a stack-independent Front-End Web Designer/Developer with fifteen years of professional experience (more than ten of those at startups). I have worked with LAMP, Windows/.NET, Rails/MySQL, proprietary web servers, and more.

I am well-versed in all areas of front-end development including Flowcharting, Wireframing, Design, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, DHTML, and Ajax. I make frequent efforts to educate myself on the latest and greatest techniques in those areas. I am also comfortable with simple back-end coding and basic database construction and management. I get along beautifully with programmers, producers, and executives. I adore managing artists, designers and illustrators.

I have held numerous senior positions in addition to maintaining a successful freelance business in between full-time employment. Previous employers include startups Crispy Gamer, Humor Rainbow (OkCupid), and GameSpy Industries. Currently I am under contract with GoldenSpear, a startup in the mobile presence field, working with Windows and ASP.Net MVC.

Specialties

HTML 5, CSS 3, JavaScript, jQuery, DHTML, AJAX, Photoshop, ASP.NET MVC, Content Authoring (text and editorial).
