Matt Sieber's Summary

Sieber Group, Inc. is a boutique life sciences executive search firm with a focus on innovative start-up to mid-cap biotech and diagnostic organizations. Our expertise is Sales, Marketing and Medical Affairs. We recruit professionals at all levels of Executive Leadership, Sales Management, Business Development, Managed Care/National Accounts and Medical Affairs.

Specialized markets include (but not limited to) oncology, cancer diagnostics, cardiovascular, critical care, infectious disease, surgical, OR, vascular, and renal/transplant with a focus on hospital / institutional based products and services. We are always on the hunt for experienced executives and sales leaders in both the biotech, genetic/molecular diagnostic and medical device markets.

If you are with an organization that is in need of an experienced and dedicated recruiter to quickly fill strategic positions or build/expand an existing team, please contact Matt Sieber.
