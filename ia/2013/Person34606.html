Desander Mas' Summary

Desander (Des), is Vice President of Development & Operations at the New York College of Podiatric Medicine (NYCPM), and the Foot Center of New York (FCNY). His responsibilities include directing the operations of the College and Foot Center through an outstanding, professional building operations team. In addition, Des is responsible for obtaining grants and raising funds from public and private organizations and foundations. These essential development efforts contribute to providing scholarships to our medical students, enhancing the infrastructure and technology of the College and Foot Center, and providing the community we serve with greater access.

Des is also is a partner with Shields Mas Learning Resources, Inc., (SMLR, Inc.) that he established with his Carol Shields in 1994. Des, and the SMLR team, have over 25 years experience in training and development, as a developer, facilitator and keynote speaker. His consulting and facilitating in building business networks, sales training, motivational programs, customer service and presentation skills have been to organizations across the business spectrum including: Pfizer Pharmaceuticals, GMAC, International Harvester, Merrill Lynch, The City of New York, The Children’s Aid Society, The City University of New York, and The International Planned Parenthood Federation. In addition to being president of SMLR, Inc., Des is a member of Toastmasters.

Specialties

Creatively enhancing and developing NYCPM and FCNY's marketing and funding initiatives; managing a professional operations team that takes pride in providing students, professors, doctors, medical staff, administrators and community at large with responsive and outstanding support.

With SMLR, consulting clients on how to develop consistent revenue streams, and supporting marketing efforts through an effective and motivated operational team.
