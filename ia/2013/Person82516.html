Lori A. Manns' Summary

A dynamic, accomplished, knowledgeable and award winning media sales, marketing and business development professional with nearly 25 years experience.

Read what clients have to say about Lori: http://qualitymediaconsultants.com/success-stories/

After working in corporate America in the radio industry for nearly 20 years, I received hands on training and practical experience developing strategic marketing and advertising sales campaigns for Fortune 500 companies as well as small business owners.

As a sales/marketing mentor and business coach, I work primarily with entrepreneurs and small business owners who desire to have a strategy for business growth. I teach them the principles of client attraction, brand visibility and sales development that result in more profits for their business.
I will help you grow your business via magnetic marketing strategies and smart sales solutions that help you stand out from the crowd. Allow me to help you elevate your revenue and propel your business to the next level of trailblazing success.
