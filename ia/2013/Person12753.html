Benjamin Arp's Summary

I recently graduated from San Diego State University with a degree in Business Management with an emphasis in Entrepreneurship. During my time at SDSU I was captivated by the stories of entrepreneurs and their ability to take an idea and turn it into reality.

My professional experience consists of various internships, part-time and contract work that have helped me to build a wealth of knowledge in various fields. I learned about social media by developing an online presence for a football legend. I learned marketing from a 25-year industry veteran (thanks dad!). I learned about entrepreneurship from two San Diego start-ups. These experience have whetted my appetite and I'm eager to continue developing professionally.

A professor of mine once challenged our class. He said, "can you create a business that makes a difference in the world?" After taking some time to study social entrepreneurship I've taken this as the ultimate challenge. My professional goal is to create a company that perfectly aligns the pursuit of profit and social good.
