Derek Manwill, PE's Summary

Derek is a registered professional engineer specializing in seismic certification of mechanical and electrical equipment. He is well versed in certification by analysis and shake table testing per IBC, UBC, IEEE, and OSHPD OSP requirements.

Derek is an Associate Engineer at Tobolski Watkins Engineering, Inc. Tobolski Watkins is a structural and earthquake engineering and protective design (blast) firm passionate about developing efficient solutions to the complex problems of today and the visions of tomorrow. Our goal is to utilize our technical expertise to provide novel solutions to our clients and the public. We have offices in San Diego, San Francisco, Bend, Portland and Seattle.
