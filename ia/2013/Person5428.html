Amanda Spann's Summary

Amanda Spann is a formally trained publicist and an avid innovation enthusiast. She founded BRANDSPAN, an identity agency, to meet the need for digital brand building in niche communities and to offer responsible B2C interactive communications for multicultural markets. The Florida State University and Georgetown grad has been honored as one of Business Insider’s 30 Most Important Women In Tech Under 30, as one of BET’s Blacks on the Brink of Greatness, and as one of 5 future leaders in technology by Black Enterprise Magazine.

Outside of BRANDSPAN, Amanda is the co-founder and former CMO of Blerdology, a tech social enterprise to support and engage the black tech community and the first organization to host hackathons targeting African Americans. The company helps spark black interest in technology, assists aspiring minority entrepreneurs get their apps or projects off the ground for little to no cost and bridges policy and innovation to combat social issues in communities. Amanda frequently speaks and writes on the topics of media, entertainment and technology and loves to travel. Follow her on Twitter at @amandaspann or subscribe to her marketing blog at BreakingtheBrand.com.
