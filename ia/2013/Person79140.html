Laurent Bourscheidt's Summary

Accomplished senior creative executive with over 15 years of experience in creative direction, corporate identity development, integrated marketing and creative teams management and a track record of building growth and transforming brands through a diverse range of environmental, print and digital experiences.

Responsible for developing, implementing, leading creative services and brand identity work for national and international private and publicly-held organizations in both, agency environment and corporate in house creative department. prior successful experience in the area of Beauty, Skin Care, Telecommunications, Technology, Finance, Fashion, Luxury, Beverage, Entertainment, in both B2B and B2C.

Work closely with agency leadership team on global pitches, account management, strategic initiative, and creative initiative on product launches, brand launches of multi-merger companies, event design and production from 10 to 1300 guests, interactive development for corporate websites, consumer brand sites, microsites, micro applications interface, blog design, social media campaign, user interface design.

Contributed to agency growth from 2 to 50 employees worldwide by creating and maintaining a strong creative offering across all offices.

Notable key clients for B2C include:
Wild Turkey Bourbon, Aberlour Scotch Whisky, Boru Vodka, FRS - energy drink, Pernod Ricard, MacLaren Strollers, Chado Ralph Rucci, Elite International, Van Cleef & Arpels, Breguet Watches, Audemars Piguet, The Bermuda Bureau of Tourism, Maison de France, Activision

Clients for B2B include:
Tata Communications, the Tata Group, Equinix, Arbinet-thexchange, Switch and data, CoreSite, Telx, Alix & Associates, Gordian Group, Sony, Barclay’s Bank, AIG Telecom, Castle Brands.

Specialties: Branding, Corporate Identity, Environmental design, Interactive and Graphic User Interface, Social Media Design, Concept ideation, Interior Design and Branding Integration in 3 Dimensional space.
