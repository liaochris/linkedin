Jay Goldman's Summary

Jay Goldman has been providing a human side to technology for over fifteen years, as a Technologist, User Experience Specialist, and Visual Designer. His career has been focused on the interaction between people and technology, and his insights have helped to greatly improve products on mobile, web, and desktop platforms, including IBM DB2 and Mozilla Firefox.

He co-founded and led Radiant Core, was the Head of Marketing at Rypple, and is now Managing Director at Klick Health, where his teams are responsible for Klick’s growing west coast business, Klick Labs R&D, digital insight research, and emerging channel strategy.

Jay has taught courses for The Learning Annex, hosted the Mr. Mobile and Status Update podcasts for Butterscotch.com, wrote The Facebook Cookbook for O’Reilly Media, and has been proudly published in the Harvard Business Review.
