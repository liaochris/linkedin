Kenny Ackerman's Summary

Ackerman’s Fine Art, LLC specializes in important American and European paintings and watercolors from the 19th, 20th, and 21st centuries. The gallery is located in Purchase, New York, just 30 minutes north of New York City by rail or car. Ackerman’s is committed to serving the new and experienced collector in the acquisition and disposition of investment quality American & European fine art. Ackerman’s takes pride in helping our clients build and prune their collection’s and seeks to create client confidence through expertise, service and confidentiality.

Ackerman’s Fine Art purchases, sells, consigns, and acts as broker for it’s clients in accordance with their individual needs. Ackerman’s Fine Art also offers assistance to individuals and corporate clients in their fine art transactions with private parties, other galleries and at auction. Additionally, Ackerman’s handles estate sales, helps clients find the art of desired artists, and lends support with restoration, framing, lighting, storage, and appraisals.

Specialties

Purchase, Sale, Restoration, and Appraisal of American Fine Art, European Fine Art, Oil Paintings, Watercolors, Contemporary Art, Modern Art, Western Art, Abstract Art, Portraits, and Impressionist Paintings from the 19th, 20th, and 21st century.
