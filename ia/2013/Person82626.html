Lotoya Vongrechin's Summary

Lotoya Vongrechin founded V Agency in 2008 with one objective: To partner with busy people in creating and managing strategic plans that transform their visions into fabulous, unforgettable events.

A mechanical engineer by trade, with a master’s degree in business administration, Lotoya spent a decade in technical and planning positions for various Fortune 500 companies. Finally, her strategic right brain hooked up with her creative and passionate left brain, leading her to pursue a career as a savvy event planner. She saw an overwhelming need from individuals who were running themselves ragged trying to plan and pull off milestone parties with minimal staff and tight budgets. So, with over 15 years experience creating, coordinating and executing college student events, fund-raising campaigns and upscale galas—and with a wealth of uniquely appropriate skills in her tool belt—she created the one-stop shop V Agency.

“Each and every event we plan benefits from our sharp attention to detail and our passion for fabulosity!” she says. Unique services V Agency offers include extensive hour–by-hour timelines, proactive contingency plans, event sponsor activation and event invitations that set just the right tone and entice guests with what’s to come. (Check out the Events and Beyond pages to learn more.)

Results-oriented and an overachiever, Lotoya cites her expertise in fostering relationships, as well as excellent negotiating, sales and critical thinking skills. Clients love her flexibility, adaptability, and her ability to meet deadlines—as well as her engaging and personable spirit. “Everything we do is driven by our client’s vision and hoped-for results,” she says. Above all, it’s her true passion for people and event planning that makes V Agency a success

Specialties: Planning events is what we do! Social, Weddings, Nonprofit and Corporate. We also do sponsorship sales, green events, funeral planning and commitment and civil ceremonies.
