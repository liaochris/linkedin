Nancy Wesselink Phd's Summary

President/CEO of One Source Counseling and Employee Assistance Services in the metro Atlanta area. I have owned the company since 2002 and provide services to over 4000 employees in manufacturing, utility, municipal and academic settings.

Private counseling practice and see approximately 30 people a week in addition to conducting trainings and workshops on such topics as Drug Free Workplace, Harrassment/Diversity, Team Building, Dealing with Change in Organizations, and Stress Management.

Licensed social worker and certified Employee Assistance Professional and holds a doctorate in Human Services from Capella University.

Primary Goal: to continue to expand One Source's services to small businesses who need Employee Assistance designed specifically for their needs. Our services are tailored to the unique issues small business owners confront, and our ability to affect positive change in these environments is second to none.

Specialties: Small Business EAP Specialist, Public Safety, Counseling and Training, Organizational Policy Compliance and Behavioral Risk Management Assessments
