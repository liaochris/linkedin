Ian Stevenson's Summary

I am a business leader with a track record of success in delivering strategic projects, innovation, and product development. I have demonstrated leadership in every environment I have worked in, and I have the communications skills and breadth of experience to work effectively with all: colleagues, customers, investors, managers, technical personnel and professional advisors. I also have an excellent academic background which includes a 1st class degree from Edinburgh University.

My passion is bringing new products and technologies to market. I have over 15 years experience of doing this, working as an engineer, consultant, executive, founder, and on behalf of investors. This breadth of experience helps me to contribute to all aspects of building and growing young companies, and especially to help bridge the gap that often exists between a core technology and market needs.

I am currently consulting for a variety of early stage companies in the technology sector through SalientPoint, which is the vehicle through which I deliver the benefit of my skills and experience, and those of associates, to companies that need them.
