David Bown PGA AA Golf Professional's Summary

PGA professional since 1975 class AA PGA Professional & an Authorised Golfing Machine instructor & Lynn Blake Certified Instructor.

You will: GAIN

MORE CONSISTENCY so you can lower your handicap, or get a handicap for the first time.

MORE POWER AND DISTANCE which will help you ENJOY YOUR GOLF!

My goal is to make you play better golf through your understanding of your golf swing, and showing you the Master Keys for success, in a simple way, using video analysis, & my teaching aids to help you SEE & FEEL the correct alignments to consistent golf.

I will show you the best way for you to achieve CONSISTENCY and give you your personal swing map for future SUCCESS. You will be hitting the ball further, and straighter!

I have sought out the best teachers in the game of golf, forty of the top fifty Golf instructors as awarded in Golf Magazine & Golf Digest, including, Butch Harmon, David Leadbetter, Hank Haney, Jim Mclean, Lynn Blake.

I have attended several PGA of America teaching & coaching summits, & PGA of Europe teaching summits.

I have written articles for the PGA profile trade magazine, which included a David Leadbetter interview & teaching technique with video analysis.

I have studied biomechanics, the mind game & the fitness side of the game so I can help you develop all elements of your golf game.

I am dedicated to teaching, using the Facts behind the golf swing based on the laws of force & motion, which are still unchanged and will not change, and not on an opinion or an illusion of what happens in the swing.
