Chef Guy Parker's Summary

After many years in the legal field, I followed my interest in cooking to start my own personal chef/catering business.

I worked for personal chef clients preparing foods for them in their home. I also prepared foods for special events for individual clients. As for parties, I served at small parties for up to 100 people.

Since my daughter was born in April 2012, I switched gears and no longer work as a personal chef/caterer. I run a food blog called Southern Exposure Cooking sharing recipes and teaching people how to cook Southern and Cajun recipes with a twist. I also am starting up a baby food blog called Figlet, as I make all of my daughter's food.

Please check out the blog for recipe ideas, tips, gardening ideas, some discussion on organic gardening, and tools that you can use in the kitchen. I can also be found on Twitter and Pinterest at ChefGuyParker.
