Matthew da Silva's Summary

An experienced writer with a strong and extensive record in blogging, journalism, corporate communications and technical writing. Also a devotee of the social web and a thinker. I started using Facebook in 2007 and Twitter in 2009. I see many opportunities in the new media landscape as the number of avenues for participation in the public sphere increase for people who want to get involved. I'm also intrigued by the attention economy and how that new reality impacts on how people use media, and how media supports itself financially. In this liminal moment, where there are ways to do things that didn't exist even five years ago, I see new kinds of ways for the media to position itself and to ensure its continued viability.
