Lois M. Brenner's Summary

Lois Brenner is a Divorce Attorney for over 30 years, and an accredited Divorce Mediator having trained with Dr. John M. Haynes, the father of family law mediation; she is a Psychologically-trained Physician Assistant. She has been appointed by the New York State courts to provide a non-adversarial alternative for separation or divorce in difficult cases.

Ms. Brenner is the Author of “Getting Your Share,” (http://bit.ly/LBrenbook) a book on divorce published by Crown and creator of “Your Divorce Personality Guide” about how it affects your case. She has written for and has been quoted by The New York Times and New York Magazine.

Ms. Brenner has been interviewed on national television programs such as Oprah, Good Morning America and CNN as a family law expert.

Ms. Brenner is in private practice with offices in New York and Connecticut.

Specialties: Divorce, Separation, Property Settlements, Alimony, Prenuptial Agreements, Child Support, Child Custody, Mediation, Negotiation, Litigation, Public Speaking, Author
