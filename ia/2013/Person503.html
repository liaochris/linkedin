Aaron Harper, PSP's Summary

Aaron Harper is a Senior Project Control Specialist at Scheduling Consultants, Ltd. Aaron has been involved in the planning and scheduling process for more than $3 billion in total construction projects. His ability to build a project realistically is an invaluable addition to his extensive knowledge in scheduling software capabilities, including cost & resource loading, risk management, claims prevention methods, and time-impact analyses. Active as well as past clients of Aaron’s include JE Dunn Construction, GE Johnson Construction, Jaynes Corporation, The Weitz Company, Solaris Property Owner, Bryan Construction, Doyon Government Group, Heath Construction, Whiting-Turner Contracting Company, and R.A. Nelson & Associates.

Aaron’s ability to create a plan and communicate that plan to a project team has proven to be an asset on large scale projects such as a $320 million condominium complex in Vail, Colorado, a $100 million nanotechnology building in Boulder, Colorado, as well as a $142 million high school in Rio Rancho, New Mexico.

Aaron has developed and taught scheduling software training courses for general contractors to assist with basic knowledge of CPM software fundamentals. He also consistently appears as a guest lecturer at Colorado State University to discuss tips for planning a successful project with detailed examples.
