Jody Underhill's Summary

A recognized social media professional and strategist, as well as the co-founder of Upside Down Iceberg, a fully integrated Social Media Marketing agency based in West Palm Beach, FL. He works with local, national, and international corporations on social media strategy. Jody has been a guest speaker for organizations, events and forums related to social media, on how to effectively integrate social media into your overall marketing and business goals. Over his 26 year tenure with a Fortune 250 company, Jody held executive positions in Operations, Sales, Marketing and Customer Service. Jody is a Certified Business Coach and holds a Mastery Certification in NLP. Jody received his BA Degree in Organizational Management from Warner Southern College.

Specialties

Upside Down Iceberg is a cutting edge Internet Marketing Agency that utilizes a combination of videos and articles in conjunction with social media networking to position companies as experts in their respective field. We build and manage your social media presence, by creating exciting informative experiences that increase your brand equity. The Social Media environment is evolving very quickly, we focus on developing successful relationships between you and your clients.
