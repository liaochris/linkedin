Jennie Lees' Summary

I had my first taste of entrepreneurship in high school and came to Silicon Valley to be at the epicentre of innovation. I'm currently working on a startup idea in the gaming space, and happy to talk to anyone with any kind of opinion about gaming - rant, idea or question.

Former Google Product Manager and general cross-functional cat herder. Task juggler and fierce user advocate. Able to wear many hats, I do what needs to be done so that users can have the best possible experience.

Coworkers have complimented me on my passion, hustle, willingness to roll my sleeves up, and technical chops.

Passionate about meaningful mobile games and the explosion of the creation space. The possibilities from ever-present devices, rapidly advancing technology platforms and creative design can quite literally change the world for the better. Specific personal interest in fitness and behaviour change.

Outside of building stuff, I teach technical and product topics and bring technical women together to learn. I also regularly do Crossfit and participate in recreational races (obstacle and half marathons).

My avatar image is from a TEDU talk I gave at TEDGlobal in 2011, titled "Improvising Happiness".
