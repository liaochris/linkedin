Kelly Priest M.S.'s Summary

I am a social cognitive specialist and consultant for parents of children with social challenges.

I teach the “whys” and “hows” of social thinking and social communication so that my students and clients can learn to consider the perspectives of others, adapt to people and situations around them, understand the purpose and use of social skills, and more successfully negotiate with others to advocate for their own needs and wishes.
