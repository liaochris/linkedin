Jay Kladiva, IV's Projects

  • Business Message Processor

    • May 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Jay Kladiva, IV

    Conceptualized, prototyped, architected, and developed a global muti-tenant dynamic business rules processor to front-end Epicor iScala that integrates seamlessly into the BizTalk 2010 pipeline architecture. Allow rules to be written in .NET or .NET/TSQL without the need for expensive BizTalk expertise. The prototype of this system was selected over 2 half million dollar preprocessors that were currently in use in the environment and was rolled out to 5 markets in Central and Eastern Europe in 2011. The system is currently being rolled out to 20+ markets in Asia and Europe. The legacy system required the custom application and database to be copied, modified, and 6 weeks of effort to implement a new one-off solution. The Business Message Processor has reduced this to a few man days of effort. This solution utilizes the robust connectors and runtime of BizTalk while taking advantage of the flexibility, speed to market, and reduce cost of a .NET based rules framework that is database driven.
