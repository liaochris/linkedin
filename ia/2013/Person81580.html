Lisa Lessard's Summary

My professional passion is to find the perfect loan for your home purchase, refinance or renovation project. With my 16 years of mortgage lending experience, I believe that I am an expert in the field; as I have earned the respect and trust of my customers, brokers and industry colleagues. My goal is to save you time and money! Experience and dedication as a loan officer and mortgage advisor has allowed me the opportunity to build long-term relationships with my customers. As a member of the Universal Lending family of mortgage professionals, I can provide local, in-house processing and underwriting on every loan plus a broad range of loan products including FHA, VA, conventional and home improvement options. I provide constant communication with my customers and attend closing. My family has been in the mortgage industry since the late 1980's, beginning with Universal Lending Corporation. Stability, dependability and trust - Universal Lending Corporation has been a leader in providing exceptional wholesale mortgage lending services. Celebrating 30 years, they believe in their slogan: "People Helping People for 30 Years."
