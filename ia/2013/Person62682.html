Jerron Smith's Summary

I have over twenty years as both a designer and design educator. At one time or another I have worked in nearly every facet of the print, web and video production fields, producing graphics and animation for magazine, broadcast and broadband distribution.
Currently, I wear many hats; I am of course working as a freelance creative profesional, I work with Aquent Graphics Institute on a contract basis as an instructor and consultant dealing with Print, Web and Video technologies and am also an adjunct instructor at both the New York Institute of Technology and New York City College of Technology.
Prior to working with AGI, I was the Associate to the Dean of Design and Digital Media at the Katharine Gibbs School in NYC. In this position I was responsible for managing the day to day operations of the ASA degree granting programs; Visual Communications, Digital Media, and Digital Film making.

  • Are You Creative Infomational Graphic

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jerron Smith

    Genre: Info-Graphic
    Project Breakdown:
    This is an info-graphic taken from the Adobe 2012 State of Create Global Benchmark study. As with all informational graphics the goal here was to create a coherent and readable graphical display of the information while at the same time making it visually appealing and engaging for the audience.
    Process:
    The initial graph animation was created in Autodesk 3DStudio Max.
    The animation was then rendered out as individual passes as png sequences to be composited in After Effects.
    3DStudioMax camera data and null objects for positional reference were also exported and brought into AE as well.
    Text was added and animated in After Effects and final color correction was performed on all elements.
