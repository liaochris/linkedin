Online Marketing Consultant for SMEs

I've been active on the Internet for about 15 years and started Questers Online (QuestersOnline.com) to help Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) increase their business through online growth strategies. We're not here to do everything related to Internet Marketing for customers. Rather, we specialize in certain things and we do it darn well. Because we’re not all things to all men, we are able to focus and we perform extremely well simply because we like what we do and we stay up-to-date with all the latest changes online related to our specialized services.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Google Places Optimization (GPO)

SEO has been the mainstay of Internet Marketing and over the last few years Google Places has come into view. I specialize in helping SMEs in Singapore and all over the world increase their visibility on the first page of Google's search results. When this happens, sales dramatically increase. Many SMEs still do not realize the power of getting greater visibility on Google and it is my business to help them do so and my joy when their sales increase as a result.

Social Media Marketing (SMM)

Beyond the above, the Social Media (Facebook, Google+, Twitter, etc.) has transformed the landscape of the Internet in the past 5 years, giving a say to everybody in its networks. SMEs benefit greatly through engaging the people of the world through Social Media Marketing.
