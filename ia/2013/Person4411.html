Alfred Ruth's Summary

Videoplaza empowers broadcasters, publishers and networks to maximise their advertising revenues from the New IP-delivered TV. Their global client base uses the Videoplaza ad management platform to monetise video experiences across PC’s, mobile devices, tablets and Connected TVs.

Running his own web consultancy business since he was 15 years old provided Alfred with a strong managerial and technical background. Alfred holds a degree from Södertörn University, Stockholm in JMM (Journalism and Multi-Media), and prior to Videoplaza had an extensive media career covering the new technology and start-up space for some of Sweden’s most high profile publications. Alfred’s background in journalism and technology brings an incisive and analytical depth to the Videoplaza team.

With a keen eye for detail and the ability to stay ahead of the industry curve, Alfred is the brains and the principle designer behind Videoplaza’s products.
