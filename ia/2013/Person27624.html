Craig Toedtman's Summary

Founder & CEO, Resource Development Company, Inc. – a human resource consulting company, located in Philadelphia, PA. Retained executive search, job search consulting, personal branding, and career counseling.

30 years of business experience in human resources, executive relocation, manufacturing and general management, having worked at Warner & Swasey, Selas Corporation, and the Abar Ipsen Industries of TI Group plc. Held a variety of executive management positions. He received his BA in Economics from Allegheny College and MBA from Case Western Reserve University. US Army Vietnam veteran.

Craig is a certified Senior Human Resource Professional (SPHR), Global Mobility Specialist (GMS), Certified Career Management Professional (CMF) by the Institute of Career Certification International (ICCI), Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW), Certified Professional Behavioral Analyst (CPBA), EARP Fellow (European Academy of Relocation Professionals), and Myers Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) assessor. Co-founded Resource Development Company in 1981. Co-creator of www.JobMetrx.com - utilizing technology and online personal branding to support job seekers in their quest for new opportunities.

Specialties: Human Capital Management - including all facets of sourcing, recruiting, relocating and retaining. Career Coach. Executive recruiter. HR consulting. Job search services. Behavior analysis. Resume Writing.
