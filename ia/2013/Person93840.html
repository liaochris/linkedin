Miguel Lopez-Quesada's Summary

Communication and Institutional Affairs Group Director at a global industrial player operating in around 20 markets in the automotive sector.

Almost 6 years leading communication function at a global company operating in +60 countries in the digital content sector (social games, mobile advertising & content, internet, animation).

Former CEO of global PR firm advising multinational companies, politicians and NGOs on communication, crisis management, media training and lobbying operating in different continents.

Involved in teaching for over 18 years in University and business schools. Author of "Estamos en crisis" (2003), reference book on crisis management communication.

Committed member of national and global trade associations on Communication, Digital Entertainment, Management and Technology and member of the Board at charities.

Specialties: crisis management 1.0 and 2.0, digital reputation, corporate communications, corporate marketing, branded content, social media, spokespeople Coaching, advocacy and Institutional relations
