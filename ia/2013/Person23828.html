Christel Quek (郭蕙萁)'s Summary

Huff Po: http://huffingtonpost.com/christel-quek
HBR: http://hbr.org/search/Christel Quek
Site: http://ladyxtel.com

Digital native who loves great thinking and strategy. Currently, I lead social business efforts regionally for Samsung Asia across Southeast Asia, Oceania, and Taiwan as part of the global social media & digital marketing leadership team at Samsung.

What drives me is my deep interest in the momentum of human behaviour across technology. I am passionate about rethinking education and driving more creativity and purpose in our curriculum, and determined to help more folks better navigate the social era efficiently.

I enjoy penning my thoughts and currently contribute to Harvard Business Review and Huffington Post. My previous adventures include working for Havas Media Group as the Global Social Manager, where I worked on new business pitches, digital strategy, training, thought leadership and global marketing.

I've worked with brands such as DBS Bank, Intel, Toshiba, Globe Telecom, Hong Kong Tourism Board, Unilever, and KLM Airlines, and I enjoy sharing my experiences - by conducting training, mentoring, and speaking.

I've spoken at conferences such as Social Media Week, Next Bank Asia, and Ad Tech, and I've mentored startups during Singapore Startup Women Weekend in 2012. I've also worked with Google & The Athena Network Singapore & Asia-Pacific to conduct digital training for the pilot "Women Entrepreneurs on the Web" program in Singapore.

I believe that creativity is a function of insight and intuition, and that a constant thirst for learning helps keep me digitally fit (enough) to survive in this dynamic landscape. My work has been featured on Yahoo!, The Guardian, The Huffington Post, Adobo Magazine, Festival of Media Asia, Warc, Microsoft, Campaign Asia-Pacific, Mumbrella, Social Media Today, and Marketer Pulse.
