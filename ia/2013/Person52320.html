Heather Lopez's Summary

I like to say that I am in the business of being social online, offline & mobile. I am a community builder and people connector that connects brands to my audience and teaches brands how to build their own communities. I am a small business/nonprofit consultant and marketing professional, with over 14 years experience in business development and management. I possess a BA in Psychology & Sociology, a Certificate in Nonprofit Management, and graduate education in Forensic Psychology. I credit my knowledge to past experience, including founding and serving as Executive Director of a 501 (c) (3) organization, small business workshops & books, other moms, and self-teaching. My natural instincts are to help people and I do that in whatever venture I take on.

I've got my hands dipped into several pots right now:

CEO- Heather Lopez Enterprises LLC- This is the parent company to all projects and websites.

VP/COO- Downtown Miami & Brickell Chamber of Commerce

Founder & Admin of SOFLA Moms Making Sales, a group of over 1300 mom business owners in South Florida.

Founder of the Bloggin' Mamas network. We have several chapters, with new ones developing in several states. Our South Florida chapter has about 100 members.

Other sites and projects I have been known for include:
Co-founder & Organizer- Super Mom Entrepreneur Conference & Expo- A traveling conference targeting moms who have either started or would like to start their own business.

Co-founder & Organizer- Bloggin' Mamas Conference & Cruise-A family-friendly hybrid conference & vacation targeting mombloggers.

Owner, Editor, & Publisher- Happy and Healthy Mom.Com- Provides a blogazine, club, and events targeting wellness for moms.

Specialties: social commerce, branding and identity, social media marketing & management, online marketing & collaboration, press releases & business content, small business workshops, conference planning, nonprofits, focus group creation, public relations
