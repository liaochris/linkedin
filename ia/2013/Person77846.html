Kyle Ten Broeke's Summary

Kyle holds a B.A., in philosophy and political science from Calvin College in Grand Rapids, MI and an M.P.A from Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, MI. In his current position, he is responsible for staffing the Michigan State Tax Commission’s Certification Advisory Committee, managing the DNR’s PILT tax roll and staffing the Commission's Certification Advisory Committee. In addition, he assists with taxpayer complaints, assessor audits, and general data collection and analysis in support of the Commission’s activities. Prior to joining the Deartment of Treasury staff, he served as administrator for the West Michigan Sustainable Business Forum, staffing the transition from one organization’s program to a new stand-alone non-profit; establishing protocols, business relationships, and infrastructure that embraced new technology. He has many years of supervisory experience at Measurement Inc., an educational testing company. He has additional experience with internships in the Administrator’s office at Kent and Ottawa Counties. He is a trained Red Cross disaster volunteer and regularly donates blood.

Specialties: training and employee development, organizational transition, database creation and management, web development and maintenance, public relations, social marketing, multi-tasking, event planning.
