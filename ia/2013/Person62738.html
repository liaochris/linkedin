Jerry Kaltenbach's Summary

Jerry Kaltenbach is recognized as one of Ohio’s top lawyers by his peers and by the leading providers of attorney ratings in the nation. Jerry is honored with an AV-Preeminent rating by Martindale-Hubbell, the highest possible rating for ethical standards and legal ability. The AV-Preeminent rating results from surveys conducted of attorneys and judges that have seen Jerry in action and it is an honor even more rare for an attorney under the age of 40.

In addition, Jerry has been selected as a Super Lawyers Rising Star by Thomson Reuters and as a Top Lawyer by Columbus C.E.O. Magazine in multiple fields including civil litigation and business law. Jerry is rated “Excellent” by Avvo.

A veteran of jury trials and appellate arguments in both state and federal court systems in Ohio, Jerry is known for his instincts, passionate advocacy, concise communication, and a unique ability to bring parties together to resolve issues effectively.
