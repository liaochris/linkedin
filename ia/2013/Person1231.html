Anne Headen - Virtual Assistant's Summary

I'm a Virtual Assistant who's passionate about helping freelancers and small business owners maximise their time by delivering more hours in their day.

I've always been an organiser, it really is my passion. I love it and when I'm helping and supporting my clients, I'm in my element.

Not only do I provide excellent Virtual Assistant services, I can take the stress of organising your next event away from you, allowing you to relax and enjoy the day with your guests. I have organised many events which have been highly successful. I take the time to understand your needs and will ensure every detail is attended to. So whether you’re looking to organise a family party, workshop or seminar to raise your profile, through to conferences, I’m here to help, advise, hold your hand, guide you through the process and keep you informed of progress.

My organisational skills have also moved into the online marketing arena. I can help with your online business presence by:

- Designing and building a Wordpress website and blog, one which reflects your brand and aligns with your online marketing vision, strategy and goals.

- Managing your email marketing campaigns, including setting up a Mailchimp or Aweber service, creating awesome newsletters and tracking results. I can also design and incorporate an opt in form for your Wordpress website and set up autoresponders.

- Helping you with your social media activity from profile set up, developing your strategy and status updates.
