Ilana DeBare's Summary

After two decades of work in daily newspapers including the San Francisco Chronicle and Sacramento Bee, I made the (not so surprising, if you know me) leap to nonprofit communications.

I'm currently Communications Director at Golden Gate Audubon Society, the local chapter of the national bird/conservation group.

I also continue to accept a limited amount of freelance writing work, including business writing.
