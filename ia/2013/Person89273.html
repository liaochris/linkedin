Matt Hoffecker, MBA (GE)'s Summary

Matt currently owns two companies:
1. IMPACT MARKETING GROUP
2. Express Employment Professionals

Matt has more than 25 years of experience in sales & marketing and as entrepreneur in Canada, USA and Germany.

Express Employment Professionals was founded in 2006 and became Victoria’s leading recruiting firm for temporary, contract and permanent recruiting solutions.

Express specializes in flexible & permanent hiring solutions in Management, Sales, Marketing, Accounting, Finance, Administration, Engineering, IT, Health Care and HR across a variety of industries. Since 2006, Express has helped more than 500 companies and organizations find excellent people to hire, including the Provincial Government and Ministries.

In 2012/13 Matt founded IMPACT MARKETING GROUP and joined Diego Rodriguez’s Power Marketing Consultants Network – the largest and most effective international network of professional marketing agencies in the world – to be able to exclusively offer the POWER MARKETING PROGRAM.

In addition, between 2010 and 2012 Matt consulted ICC Group, a Victoria based renewable energy company with its head office in Victoria, BC with facilities in Nanaimo, BC, Pitt Meadows, BC, Prince George, BC & Waterford, Ireland.

His career started in advertising and let him to work at AC&R Advertising in New York City as national account supervisor for Seiko Watches and other brands in the late 80’s.
He was recruited to become Marketing Director for Warner Bros. Germany, distributing Warner and Disney movies in the early 90’s.

At the start of 1993, Matt co-founded MARKETEAM, a specialized marketing & publicity agency which became Germany’s #1 agency in the film distribution niche, serving clients like Warner Bros., Disney, Paramount, Columbia Pictures and German film distributors and producers.

In 2002, he sold the company and moved with his family to Canada in 2004.

Specialties:
1. Entrepreneur & Business Owner
2. Advertising, Marketing & Sales
3. Professional Recruiter
