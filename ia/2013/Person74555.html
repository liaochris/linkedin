Kenton Cowdry's Summary

I specialize in training our clients to administer complex neuroscience in 4 simple steps using the NuCalm system.

NuCalm uses 4 components: chewable food supplements, Cranial Electrotherapy Stimulation, proprietary neuroacoustic software files, and light blocking glasses or eye masks - to deeply relax the body at the mid-brain level within minutes - without drugs.

Our clients include research facilities, medical and dental practices in the U.S., Canada, the U.K. and Australia. NuCalm is currently being used for dentistry, cardiology, oncology, eating disorders, sleep medicine, psychotherapy, executive and sports performance, and by individuals with a doctor's prescription. Numerous research partners are involved in bringing the NuCalm technology to diverse markets.

The NuCalm Library (see websites) archives a growing collection of blog articles, audio interviews, videos, and published articles about stress, neuroscience, compassionate healthcare, and the business of dentistry.

I share a goal with my team members of bringing this non-intrusive, all-natural stress management tool to market and 'improving the human experience' through education and leading edge neuroscience.

Specialties: Stress, training, implementation, webinars, branding, biotechnology, neutraceuticals, neurobiology, stress intervention, anxiety disorders, dental, practice management, sedation, relaxation, Wordpress, neuroscience, stress management, webmastery
