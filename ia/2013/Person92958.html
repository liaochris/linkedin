Artiste Michael Samawi's Summary

Michael Samawi brings a unique and eclectic background to Coffee, Tea And Tulips. He left a successful career in marketing and advertising to follow his true passion – food.

Born to parents of Jordanian descent, Michael inherited his enthusiasm for cooking from his mother, who would prepare large Arab feasts every Sunday. His culinary experience began at a young age, spending time cooking while his parents were at work. As he grew up he worked his way through family-owned markets, butchers and sandwich shops. From cutting meat, to creating sandwiches, to preparing family meals, he’s been working with food most of his life.

It was his time spent living in San Francisco where he fully realized his desire to work with food. He was inspired by the city’s unique cafes, rich culture, and diverse restaurants within walking distance from his home. Eager to learn the culinary craft, Michael spent time assisting chefs at Sur La Table cooking classes, and prepared elaborate meals to entertain family and friends in his personal time.

In 2009, Michael and his wife, Lina, opened Coffee, Tea and Tulips in Mission Viejo. The tea parlor and petite bistro is a place where guests are treated like family, and are encouraged to relax in a peaceful atmosphere while enjoying fresh and delicious food. In addition to unique tea offerings, Michael’s globally-inspired menu has the ability to take guests around the world in a single meal.
