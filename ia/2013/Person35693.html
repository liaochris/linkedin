Ding Neng's Summary

Ding Neng is the owner of Ding-Neng.com, a blog which he shares his entrepreneurial adventures with others. He loves dabbling with online marketing strategies like Social Media Marketing & SEO, and have helped his company generate significant revenue from it.

He came out as the Top 20 World's Coolest Intern, an international contest organized by Standard Chartered Bank in 2010, in their search for social media savvy interns.

During his term as the President of NTU Toastmasters Club, he oversees a club size of over 70 members from 11 nationalities, supporting their journey to improve public speaking skills, and grooming the leader in them. He also initiated many firsts in the club - first ever overseas club exchange, first ever NTU TMC Pecha Kucha Night, and first member to represent NTU TMC at District Humorous Contest.

He also has deep passion in Public Speaking, Entrepreneurship, Reading, Writing and Violin Playing. His purpose in life is to live life to the fullest. As the quote goes, "You only live once, but if you do it right, once is enough."

Specialties: Blogging, SEO, Social Media, Online Business, Entrepreneurship, Public Speaking.
