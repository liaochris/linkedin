Founder

CantyMedia

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

October 1999 – Present (13 years 10 months) Great Falls, VA

CantyMedia is the umbrella for:

• Weatherbase. Built the Internet's largest database of historical weather averages for fun, to learn PHP and MySQL, back in 1999. Weatherbase is now considered one of the definitive sources of weather averages. It has been referenced in four dozen books, in 1300+ Wikipedia articles and finds more than 50,000 links via Google. It serves 150,000 unique users and 1 million impressions monthly. Optimized the ads in 2011 to increase RPM 550%. On version 3 of the design, version 1 of the iPhone app. Currently gathers six million datapoints daily for more than 29,000 unique locations, with 1.2 billion records in memory.

• Deali.sh. In beta. The first realtime search engine for daily deals and flash sales on the Internet. Leveraged the tools and algorithms we have developed for structuring messy data across disparate sites.

• Geoba.se. Launched in 2009. Geoba.se started as an internal tool for Weatherbase. It gathers and organizes information about more than seven million unique locations worldwide. Facts, statistics, news and information. Government data. Corporate data. All under one roof.

• Factba.se. Launched 2010. Combines and consolidates reference information for every country in the world, with constant updates. Data comes from the CIA, State Department, the governments of Australia and the United Kingdom, the World Bank, and from newsfeeds. Testing publishing the public domain portions in searchable eBook formats.

May all hobbies be this successful. This is how I spend my time between Jon Stewart and bedtime.
