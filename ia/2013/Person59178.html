Jason Taylor's Summary

My name is Jason Everett Taylor. I’m an imaginative and experienced Interactive & Digital Designer in beautiful Cincinnati, Ohio.

I’ve had a passion for web design and development since the day I created my first geocities website at the age of thirteen. Since writing those first lines of html, my design skills have evolved through both formal education and my desire to learn more in the field.

I have used my Design skills and knowledge of design tools to complete projects for web, mobile, social media and print. I also had the pleasure of working with great clients like The Cincinnati Enquirer, Mitchell’s Salon & Day Spa, Hugo Naturals, Bush Trucks, Kaeser & Blair and many more.
