Bart Cohen's Summary

I represent plaintiffs in complex litigation, primarily class actions and antitrust litigation. My clients include small businesses that have been victimized by price-fixing and other antitrust violations. I have extensive experience in dealing with the banking and automotive industries, as well as information technology issues. Additional details as to my background are available on my firm's web site at http://tinyurl.com/bartdcohen.

    • ABA Antitrust Section Joint Conduct Committee E-Bulletin
    • March 22, 2012
    Authors: Bart Cohen

    Two pending cases may soon add volumes to the scant jurisprudence addressing the application of Illinois Brick to joint ventures. If either one should deviate from established Illinois Brick doctrine--which provides in part that transactions between conspirators are outside the scope of the rule because they are not subject to market forces--the implications for both that doctrine and the conduct of joint ventures will be substantial.
