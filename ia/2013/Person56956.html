Jaidev Vasudevan's Summary

Jaidev is a chartered, mechanical engineer with 5+ years of consulting experience for the Oil, Gas & Energy sector.

During this time, Jaidev has worked on a range of systems including renewable energy projects, coal seam gas development, oil & gas pipeline systems, biogas production and clean coal projects. For these projects, Jaidev has undertaken project management and lead mechanical engineering roles.

Jaidev spent a year working as an Australian Youth Ambassador for Development, assisting with water and sanitation development in the Solomon Islands.

Previously, Jaidev has had wind energy experience determining business development opportunities in the Pacific Islands. This work included GIS mapping, wind rose development, site identification and load estimation.

Jaidev has a keen interest in renewable energy technologies and water and sanitation engineering in the IDA sector.
