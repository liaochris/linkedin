Boris Pluskowski's Summary

SVP of Innovation Strategy at renown E2.0 Innovation Software company, Spigit (http://www.spigit.com) - acknowledged thought leader and evangelist of Enterprise Innovation Management, Social Media/Technology and Collaboration.

I've spent the last 15 years working with senior level executives at predominately large companies to build and execute robust collaborative innovation programs that become sustainable and repeatable value-creating engines for those organisations. The result of the work I do is a program that drives the creation of new products and business models, novel cost reductions, and step-change process improvements across all aspects of the enterprise - and has included the creation of both Internal and External Innovation programs, Innovation competitions, Internal Venturing models, and Idea Development Incubators.

I'm also the author of a the Complete Innovator blog - (http://www.completeinnovator.com) - aimed at helping companies become "more complete innovators" and build savvy social businesses able to leverage the unique collaborative brainpower available to them (customers, suppliers, partners, shareholders and many more) to drive unique sources of organic growth and new shareholder value..

You can find a fuller bio of myself here: http://completeinnovator.com/about-author/

Please do get in contact, connect, and talk to me about your corporate innovation, collaboration, and social media/social business programs!

Specialties: innovation, innovation strategy, collaboration, idea management, ideation, social business, social tools, social teams, social media, social business, business transformation, enterprise 2.0, knowledge management, collaborative problem solving, organic growth, performance management, leadership, business strategy, collaboration strategy, social business strategy, Crowdsourcing, gamification, game mechanics
