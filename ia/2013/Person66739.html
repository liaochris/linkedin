John M Smart's Summary

I am a foresight development educator, author, consultant, and scholar of accelerating change. I'm program champion for the Emerging Technologies M.S. for the Univ. of Advancing Technology, lecturer in technology foresight at the Naval Postgraduate School, and president of the Acceleration Studies Foundation (ASF, http://www.accelerating.org) in Mountain View, CA, a nonprofit technology foresight community. I've been primary author on several next-gen IT foresight studies, including the Metaverse Roadmap and Open Internet TV, and am a technical advisor to NextIT (http://www.nextit.com), the global leader in interactive virtual assistant (IVA) platforms. As a systems theorist, I am co-founder of the Evo Devo Universe international research community (http://www.evodevouniverse.com), which investigates evolutionary and developmental processes of change in an attempt to improve our theories and models of foresight.

I have a B.S. in business from the Haas School at U.C. Berkeley, an MS in Futures Studies from U. Houston, and have done graduate and undergrad coursework in biological, cognitive, computer and physical sciences at UCLA, UC Berkeley, and UC San Diego.

I'm presently writing a book on the topic of accelerating change.

I co-produce ASF's occasional conference, Accelerating Change, at Stanford University. In my previous career I was co-Founder and CEO of Hyperlearning, a 50-employee educational services company, sold to The Princeton Review in 1996.

One of my long-term professional goals is to network and support the twenty-two graduate programs in foresight/futures studies (http://accelerating.org/gradprograms.html), and to develop the world's first academic program in acceleration studies. At present there is no academic program anywhere trying to understand the *physical and informational drivers* of accelerating scientific, technological, and social change. This is the most important story on the planet. Let's up our game.

Specialties

Foresight education, futures studies, acceleration studies, research and analysis of technological trends and discontinuities, horizon scanning, roadmapping, emerging issues/weak signal analysis, prediction analysis, probabilistic prediction, scenarios, images of the future, strategic planning, evolutionary and developmental systems theory. Other skills: community development, event planning, marketing, recruitment, writing, public speaking
