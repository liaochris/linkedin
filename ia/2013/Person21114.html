Founder / Owner

Pod1 (acquired by Group FMG)

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

October 2001 – January 2012 (10 years 4 months) Global

3 o'clock one morning, in a tiny London office, I coined the name 'Pod1' and started a fantastic 10 year adventure.

Driving the Creative team, Pod1 quickly blossomed into one of the UKs fastest growing digital agencies. We earned a name for ourselves delivering high impact fashion ecommerce websites and marketing.

In 2007 I moved to New York and setup Pod1 Inc. Breaking into the US market place was tough. However, through passion and perseverance I led Pod1 New York to the inc500 list of US fastest growing companies.

Over the years at Pod1 I have greatly enjoyed working directly with leading fashion designers, helping them achieve their online goals, including John Varvatos, Kenneth Cole, Catherine Malandrino, Derek Lam, Nanette Lepore, Philip Stein and Anya Hindmarch to name but a few. We also picked up more than our fair share of awards along the way.

After setting up Pod1 Group Ltd. and restructuring the organisation, I escaped what was turning into a grey corporate role to follow my passion for hands-on creativity and digital innovation.
