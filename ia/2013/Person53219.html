Mel Kleiman, CSP's Summary

"If you hire the wrong person, all of the fancy management techniques in the world won’t bail you out." – Red Auerbach winningest coach in the NBA

"The day we screw up the people thing it’s all over." – Herb Kelleher, founder of Southwest Airlines

"If you beat your competition to the best employees, those are the employees who will help you beat your competition." –Mel Kleiman

As the onetime owner of three different businesses (including the largest group of Hertz Rent-a-Car franchise locations in the U.S.), my expertise is based on my personal experience as well as over 30 years of work helping companies design systems to implement successful employee hiring programs.

I have worked with hundreds of clients including many leading restaurant, retail, and franchise concerns and have delivered over 500 custom-designed presentations on these subjects.

I am also one of only 650 speakers worldwide to have earned the prestigious Certified Speaking Professional designation and have written five books including the bestselling Hire Tough, Manage Easy. I am a regular contributor to Restaurant Hospitality, Multiunit Franchise, and Convenience Store Decisions magazines and serve as a board member for both the National Restaurant Association Educational Foundation and the Workforce Institute.

Mel Kleiman’s business experience is both theoretical and practical as the owner of three different businesses, including the largest number of Hertz Rent A Car franchises in the U.S.
In 1974, Mel founded Humetrics, an independent, human capital management think tank and leading developer of systems, training, processes, and tools for recruiting, selection, and retention of the best hourly workforce and their managers. Mel is a Certified Speaking Professional (CSP). A noted author, consultant and speaker, he has written five books, including the bestselling “Hire Tough, Manage Easy©.” Mel also serves on a number of boards. Inc. He has written hundreds of articles for trade and professional journals .
Mel helps businesses and organizations worldwide improve their hiring practices and make the best possible hiring decisions by delivering approximately 50 customized presentations a year to all types

Board Member

Workforce Institute

January 2008 – June 2011 (3 years 6 months)

The Workforce Institute is a think tank that helps organizations drive performance by addressing human capital management issues that affect both hourly and salaried employees. Through education and research,

An advisory board of academics, visionaries, and practitioners spearheads the initiatives of the Workforce Institute. See the latest additions to the board.

Our Goals

provide innovative and valued content
help employers provide employees with a better experience at work
elevate organizational perception of the importance of the hourly workforce to organizational success
demonstrate measurable business impact for organizations who implement best practice recommendations
Research

Our research will address issues impacting the entire workforce, both hourly and salaried employees. Increasing global competition, the evolution of the workforce, and the changing dynamics of the workplace make this broad view imperative and timely.
