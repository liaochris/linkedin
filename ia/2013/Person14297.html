Bill Gluth's Summary

I'm a creative thinker for exceptional businesses, sales process strategist and thought leader on the topic of how small business can change the face of work in America.

I'm dedicated to changing the face of work in America by self-empowering individuals to create, sustain and grow small business and micro business enterprises.

I've dedicated myself to providing the information, resources and ideas that provide the framework for my tribe members to become self-empowered, take control of their life and business situations and create the profitable business outcomes."

Specialties

Self-empowering businesses owners, showing them how to use their greatest gifts; their talent, knowledge, passion and experience in a sales process that focuses on creating values that enhance the lives of others.

Since 2001, my clients, on average, have gained 25% to 300% revenue growth by employing my strategy, tactics and conscious business building concepts to grow their business.
