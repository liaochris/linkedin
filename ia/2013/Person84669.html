Manny Ponce's Summary

Hi. So, I'd like to take a somewhat different approach with this space, and tell you a little about me on a more personal level.

I've lived in the Bay Area all my life and have been working professionally in the Bay Area's high tech and computer reseller industries since 1991. In that time I have developed a large depth and breath of knowledge in systems and network administration, as well as a lot of general business savvy.

My early years in the reseller channel instilled a deep sense of customer service in me, something that started in my "pre computer career years" when I worked as a bank teller in high school and college. I'm proud of the work I do, and always strive to make where I work "a better place than it was before I got there". But that's pretty much the same philosophy I have with life and the world around me.

I also like to think of myself as well rounded, being able to contemplate, conceive and produce with both sides of my brain. One example of this is in the same 20 years I've been working as a sysadmin, I have also been writing and playing original music in local bands, as well as supporting the SF/Oakland/Berkeley music scene. I've been a promoter and performer, a sound guy and door guy, an adoring fan and headliner, sometimes all at the same show.

I also love movies, and movie making. And on the both sides of the brain thing, I have movie credits on IMDB as miscellaneous crew (Systems Administrator) as well as actor (Inmate).

I'm also a pretty great cook if I do say so myself, and I know how to cater a party, which I think is another example of using both sides of my brain. Because a lot of large volume cooking is timing and technique and science along with taste and creativity and visual presentation skills.

I think all these things help make me a better sysadmin: creating, planning, reacting, refining, performing, and always learning.

Specialties: Unix / Linux, Windows, MacOS, Audio & Video Systems, TCP/IP, SMTP, HTTP, /etc
