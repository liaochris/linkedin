Christina Roberts' Publications

  • The Libertas League: The White Musketeer

    • Lulu.com, Amazon.com
    • December 20, 2012
    Authors: Christina Roberts

    The White Musketeer is a self-published fiction novel and the first book in The Libertas League series. This book focuses on the adventure and growing of Erik Seeton, as he becomes caught up in Renaissance culture, swashbuckling, Mystic Art, and his father's dark past. It's up to Erik to stop his cousin Bray from destroying his home as well, and, in the midst of that, learn what's happened to him and how to cope with it. As this is the first book, it lays the foundation and elements for later books and the expansion of the story as a whole.

  • The Libertas League: Healing Crosswalks

    • Lulu.com, Amazon.com
    • February 1, 2013
    Authors: Christina Roberts

    Healing Crosswalks is book #2 in The Libertas League fiction series. Healing Crosswalks focuses on gender stereotypes from Valentine's perspective. As Valentine struggles with his gender and the stereotypical expectations because of his gender, he also must learn how to focus his Mystic Art skill and figure out what his feelings for the anorexic, bitter Melinda actually are. This is a story about expectation, going against the grain, and internal struggles.
