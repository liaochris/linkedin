Christopher Yap's Summary

Chris Yap is an experienced global business developer and global account manager in the BPO and call center industry. Chris has managed the largest call centers as well as Fortune 500 companies including Dell in a global account management capacity.

Chris Yap is passionate in facilitating organizational's financial and value growth as well as generating new jobs and keeping jobs in the United States and around the world.

Chris Yap recently joined Pearl Logic, Inc. as a Director of Business Development. Chris found a great opportunity in Pearl Logic to live his passion to help organizations increase Net Profit by reducing operating expense in a multi-faceted approach without having to terminate or outsource employees. Pearl Logic is a cost reduction firm that specializes in reducing organizational's operating expense without employment termination.

Pearl Logic has enabled million dollar companies to billion dollar companies increase Net Profit by as much as 45%. What makes Pearl Logic different from other cost reduction consulting firm is that Pearl Logic does not charge any upfront or retainer fees - Pearl Logic is a performance based firm - Pearl Logic's consulting fee is a percentage of the amount saved as a result of it's analysis and implementation of its cost reduction program during the first 12 months. If Pearl Logic is unable to reduce your OpEx, Pearl Logic does not get paid a penny.
