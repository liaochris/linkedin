Ken O'Connor's Summary

Would you base important business decisions on data you knew to be incomplete, inappropriate or inaccurate? Of course not. But how confident are you that your data is as complete, appropriate and accurate as you need it be?

I help organisations to gain confidence and trust in their business critical data. I help organisations to identify and measure the quality of their critical data, and to correct data quality problems where necessary.

Data Quality Management presents the same challenges to all businesses in all industries. The challenges include:

What is our most critical data?
Where is the data, where does it come from?
Who owns the data?
What should it contain?
What does it contain?
What should we do if our most critical data does not contain what it should?
I have faced and solved the above challenges and more.

Specialties: Data Governance, Regulatory Compliance, Data Quality Management, Solvency II, Single Customer View, CRM, Business Intelligence, Data Mining, KYC, AML, BASEL II, Data Analysis, Data Migration, Quality Assurance, QA, Testing, Business requirements elicitation, definition and verification, IT Quality Management, Process Development, Assessment and Improvement. Specification, development and implementation of End-to-End processes.
