Jim Smyth's Summary

After starting a career in electronic publishing and leading sales for LexisNexis, I continue to love working with clients to transform their work with new technology, now with a company that converts the vast amounts of imagery that is available today into a an easy-to-use ecosystem.

Hover was built around the concept of generating highly accurate 3D data of any space with minimal input imagery. Our customers range from realtors, municipal governments, fire departments and law enforcement, to the defense and intelligence communities.

Our goal is simple—rapidly provide accurate and usable 3D data of any area for any customer.

Jim Smyth's Experience

VP Business Development

Hover Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry

January 2013 – Present (1 year) Herndon, VA

Hover maximizes the value of imagery data that many organizations already have, or can easily obtain, by creating an easy-to-use 3D ecosystem that can be rapidly updated with crowd sourced ground based imagery.

Hover is a computer vision and 3D mapping company founded by a team of former Navy SEAL operators and Silicon Valley computer vision software entrepreneurs. Our software converts imagery into photorealistic 3D models, providing users with more effective ways to communicate, plan, and make decisions.

Hover has been serving the U.S. military and Federal Agencies for several years. Our Chief Technology Officer is Dr. Ed Lu, a former NASA astronaut who led the Advanced Projects Group at Google, and was responsible for imaging for Google Street View, Google Maps, and Google Earth.

In essence, what our software does is use your existing 2D imagery to generate photo realistic, geo-referenced 3D models of buildings and other real world infrastructure.
