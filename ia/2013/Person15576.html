Debra Jones' Summary

At BodyMindBusiness, we will help you transform the way you work, think and live.

I am passionate about authentic living and personal and professional expansion. My signature is to transform lives and companies through an intuitive and holistic approach to creating sustainable, integrated systems that elevate wellness, productivity and profits.

Most of my life has been grounded in agriculture. From growing up as a farm girl to working in the malting and brewing industry to attaining a Masters in Agronomy to running a laboratory dedicated to cereal grain quality and co-owning a regional wholesale seed business for 10 years. In 2006, I made the leap from a successful career to pursuing my passion in business and life. I now empower my clients to do the same through a unique co-active coaching style, helping them them find the courage within to close the gap between the life they are living now to a life filled with true joy, well-being and purpose.

I founded BodyMindBusiness LLC in 2010, and am a graduate of Hilton Johnson’s Health Coach Training Program, which is one of the most innovative and comprehensive health coach training programs available. I am also a certified DreamBuilder Coach from Life Mastery Institute, the Premier Training Center for Transformational Coaching and am Dynamic Workplace Certified through Kent Burden’s My Life Fitness.

I’ve had the privilege of working alongside some of the most elite personal and transformation trainers and coaches in the world. I’m classically trained in Pilates and was comprehensively certified through Peak Pilates. I empower my clients to see the truth that is already inside of them so they may gain conscious control of their lives in order to express their true self and find authentic happiness, the core of real wellness.

I live in Bozeman, Montana where I am awakened daily to the beauty of nature and blessings of living in community.
