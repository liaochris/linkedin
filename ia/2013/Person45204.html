Jody Colvard's Summary

Jody Colvard is the founder of the FMG Network, an inspirational and motivational TV/Radio network, which empowers writers, speakers and experts with a global online platform to share their voices and messages through blogs and podcasting to reach their audience via Internet, Radio, Mobile Devices and Television. The FMG Network is coupled with Jody's and Declan Dunn's Dunn Direct Media Marketing System which has helped many small businesses as well as larger companies, such as One World Live, JingleGram, Intel, Priceline, Office Max, PBS, ABC, Travelocity and Toshiba share their message with the world.

Jody shares her vast experience with radio, video, image consulting, talent manager and public speaking to help people's dreams of stardom come true using all the amazing technology available to us today.

Jody's goal is to globally empower individuals on the Internet with the knowledge and tools that will allow them passion in their business, freedom in their finances and the ability to give to themselves and others, hence the motto for FMG Network: "Have Fun. Make Money. Do Good."
