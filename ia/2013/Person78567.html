Lars Larsson's Summary

Lars Larsson, I'm a sociable person, focused on delivering good solutions for my customers. Love new challenges and I'm not afraid of getting into new situation, always trying to push my limits in order to become better in my role as a consultant/IT specialist.
I've been working with the IBM Lotus brand since year 2000. Working with Lotus Notes/Domino development and administration. Developed applications for the Lotus Notes client as well as web development and now focusing mostly on Xpages. Also doing a lot of Quickr (for Domino) installation, maintenance and branding/customization's.

I'm used to work in all types of companies, one of the benefits with being a consultant is the wide range of clients. Both large multinational companies as well as smaller, local businesses.

Some of my strengths are application development, planning and implementing Domino infrastructure, setting up Domino clustering, configuring and deploying Domino web servers, server upgrade projects and server migration projects.

As well as my knowledge of Lotus Notes/Domino I also have good knowledge of host operating systems, networking and hardware.

Specialties

Xpages, LotusScript, @Formula-language, Agents, Java, JavaScript, CSS, HTML, XML ,Mailrouting, replication, cluster replication, AdminP, Domino Directory, User management, installing/upgrading/migrating Domino servers into new or existing infrastructures, troubleshooting, LDAP. Quickr
