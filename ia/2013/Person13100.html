Ben Jessel's Summary

Ben is highly motivated senior business consultant with a proven record of delivering value-driven, business strategy led technology projects for a diverse range of clients for two of the Big-4 management consultancies.

Ben's has 12 years of experience in managing technology change within large scale Business Transformation programs both in shaping IT transformation strategies as well as delivery.

For the last five years, Ben has been focused on IT strategy and delivery in the Mergers and Acquisition space; both sell and buy side for full divestitures / acquisitions as well as carve outs.

Ben has the rare quality of a technologist that has an intimate appreciation of the business drivers behind technology projects.

Specialties

Ben's specialities are best articulated through his case studies; Program Management Lead for a Major Global Retail Brand, Bank of New York / Mellon integration (International Asset Servicing), QBE, Investment portfolio management and capability setup at the Hartford, Strategic Assessment of IT Shared Services for Digital Communications at SABMiller, Business Transformation Strategy Design at London Borough of Greenwich, Outsourcing and IT Strategy Assessment at Local Government Association
