Debbie Cohen's Summary

I am committed to changing the world of work to better align the needs of today’s talent base with the needs of today’s organizations.

At all levels in an organization I connect the dots that bridge the gap between the world of complex ideas and their practical application.

I love it when unclear and seemingly unrelated pieces converge to make a picture that presses us to act in ways that have meaning.

I balance the needs and interests of individuals with those of the broader organization. Not always easy, not always popular, not always conventional. I work from a framework of complexity, possibilities and new ideas.

With an honest approach and an authentic voice, I am committed to walking the world in a way keeps me approachable.

If there's an elephant in the room, you can count on me to put it in the middle of the table, with a spirit and intent that makes it safe for everyone to be a part of the conversation.

I champion others to be their best self. My beliefs in the capacity of human potential were formed early in my career, first as and early childhood educator and then as the Executive Director of parent-cooperative child care centers for Stanford University and the Department of Interior.

My career has been a richly diverse and eclectic journey of non-profits and Global Fortune 100’s. From preschool teacher to executive leadership roles in high-tech, entertainment, media and advertising.

Specialties: Culture Curator; Coaching & Mentoring; Organizational Design - Strategic Workforce Planning & Work Redesign; Talent Acquisition & Assimilation; Professional Progression & Performance Advancement; Leadership & Management Development; Work/Life, Diversity & Inclusion; Employment Branding & Communication; Knowledge Cultivation, Capture & Distribution; Employee Relations - Collaborative Problem Resolution & Root Cause Analysis; Motivational and Public Speaking

Debbie Cohen's Experience

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Computer Software industry

Responsible for shepherding and navigating the talent, cultural, and organizational challenges the project faces during these periods of transformation and growth, and for creating an environment that is compelling to current and future contributors.
