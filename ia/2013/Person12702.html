Belinda Waggoner's Summary

Belinda owns hr-haven, inc., a unique Human Resources Insourcing company on a mission to bring great HR to the entrepreneurial masses! Why Insourcing? Because we're right there with you, inside your business, working alongside you to create great things with your culture, mission, and results.

Based in Kansas City but serving clients across the nation, hr-haven's sole purpose is to provide small and medium sized business owners with all the tools and advice they need to address their human resources and business culture needs - all at a fraction of the cost of hiring their own HR Department.

Don't need to hire us as your HR Department? We also offer stand-alone solutions such as the HR StartUp Toolkit which gives businesses everything they need to get an HR program off the ground. Once you've done that you've bought yourself a ticket to our "Toolkit Alumni Club" and you can call us, any time, just paying for what you use, with hourly help with your HR issues and questions.

Visit www.hr-haven.com for more information about the ways we can help your business - or just give us a call. We're easy to talk to and our first consult is always free - and, you'll find out that we're not like other HR Pros because instead of telling you what you can't do, we help you figure out what you can do.

Call or email today to see how your business will benefit from hiring us as your HR Department. belindawaggoner@hr-haven.com or 913.940.5391 or toll free at 855.474.2836.

Specialties

We're specialists in assisting businesses with everything to do with their people, from acting as their Virtual HR Department to providing them with the tools, processes and ideas they need to successfully manage their people and run their businesses. Let us help you develop your culture, align your employees with your business goals and help you with your business plans for growth and success.
