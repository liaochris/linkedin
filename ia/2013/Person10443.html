Heather Ashley Matejczyk's Summary

I worked at an art gallery for many years throughout high school and college. My first job after graduating was with a marketing agency. I was quickly drawn to the fast-paced, creative atmosphere and talented people. Although, I found that being the middle man did not suit me so I searched for a more hands-on position. I decided to take my marketing skills and go corporate where I found myself in the electrical industry. Marketing for an electrical distributor is very unique with products always evolving towards energy efficiency. The best part is the people! My colleagues are down-to-earth and our customers base their purchasing decisions on long-lasting relationships and trust.

As a hobby, I paint silhouettes. I apply what I learn throughout my career to develop my own brand and website www.askheatherashley.com. I enjoy painting and hope to turn my passion into a profitable past-time one day.
