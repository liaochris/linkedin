Jeff Davidson's Summary

Jeff Davidson holds the registered trademark as "The Work-Life Balance Expert®" and is the premier thought leader on work-life balance. He's written 59 mainstream books -- "Breathing Space," and "Simpler Living" among them -- on topics of time management, productivity, career effectiveness, and innovation, and has been interviewed on America in the Morning, Late Night with Charlie Rose, the Australian Broadcasting Corporation, USA Today SkyRadio, and Martha Stewart Radio. Jeff's weekly or monthly columns currently appear in:

* Accounting Web
* Association News
* Boom
* Business Edge
* Ecommunique

* Human Resources I.Q.
* Organized Executive
* Plan Your Meetings
* Real Estate Professional
* Self Growth

Jeff is an electrifying professional speaker, with 812 presentations since 1985 to clients such as Kaiser Permanente, IBM, Amex, Lufthansa, National Office Furniture, the IRS, Experient, Swissotel, Re/Max, USAA, Worthington Steel, and the World Bank. His 60 Second Book Series with Adams Media -- the 60-Second Organizer, 60-Second Self-Starter, and 60-Second Innovator -- are popular in China, Japan, Malaysia, Indonesia, Russia, Turkey, Saudi Arabia, Italy, Poland, Spain, France, and Brazil.

Jeff's worldwide audience has found his books and 3,550+ articles on work-life balance, time management, stress management, and career advancement, to be enlightening, entertaining, and even life-changing.

Jeff is widely quoted in the New York Times, Businessweek, Forbes Travel, Washington Post, LA Times, USA Today, and Wall Street Journal "Employment Weekly." Cited in 2002 as a "Consummate Speaker," Jeff believes that career professionals need to achieve their own sense of work-life balance and supports them at www.BreathingSpace.com

With his high productivity, intellectual curiosity, and participation in social and civic affairs, others regard Jeff as someone who "walks his talk."

Specialties

Work-life balance: productivity, time management, managing information and communication, stress management, overcoming procrastination, interruption management, and mastering your to-do list.
