Toni Patterson's Summary

Skye Media Group is a creative web & media design studio in Washington DC that works with small businesses, creative professionals & other motivated entrepreneurs to enhance their business through website design, marketing, and branding. We don’t just design websites – we design an entire branding campaign around your business to help it grow and prosper.

Specialties: web design, graphic design, business consulting, social media, branding, print design, logos, invitations, business cards, flyers, WordPress, blogging, Flash, event design

Toni Patterson, Principal/Creative Director
hello@skyemediagroup.com

With an innate ability to create, Toni has always had a passion for fashion, art and style. Starting out in interior design, she wanted a career direction that was a bit more flexible where she could branch off and do anything no matter the training. Fast forward several years and she graduated with a BA in Business Management and minor in Communications.

Toni is a self-taught web designer who quickly learned photo editing, graphic design and web design & development. A creative writer at heart, she also prides herself on developing original web content and copy for her clients.

Toni’s style is known as stylish, colorful, creative and a bit quirky. She loves exercising her creative muscles and tackling each design project as a privilege and opportunity to learn more. When she’s not working, she’s daydreaming about design.
