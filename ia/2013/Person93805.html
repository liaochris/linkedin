Miguel A. Llano's Summary

Miguel Llano has over eight years of sales and marketing experience. The ability to search and initiate conversations with potential customers and partners has provided Miguel with the opportunity to work with some of Rochester's largest businesses and non-profits. His competitive nature, leadership capabilities, and ability to learn have allowed Miguel to be successful in multiple industries. In 2009, Miguel finished his MBA in just under one year. He currenty works for Verizon Wireless and focuses on the Healthcare & Education verticals as a mobility solution consultant.
