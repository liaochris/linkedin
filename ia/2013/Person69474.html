Joy Alyssa Day's Summary

Specializing in large scale installations for corporations, museums and resorts. Murals, kinetic sculpture, lobby sculpture usually incorporating blown/carved glass and lighting effects. Most of our work has been for the space industry, but we would like to expand more into the holiday, aquarium and cool stuff industries.

Specialties

Glass blowing, carving, etching, sandblasting, fusing, molding, stained glass, painted glass, acrylic, watercolor painting, mural, framing, "Wall of Art" installations, design, engineering, electrical, motorized, kinetic, sculpture, carbon fiber, printing, corporate id, collateral
