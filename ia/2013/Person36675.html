Don Kosak's Summary

I want to help build a vibrant startup community in Hawaii.

A strong startup community benefits local business through fostering innovation and creating a strong skilled labor pool. Startups create new jobs and opportunities. They also bring outside cash into our local economy.

I'm an entrepreneur myself with experience building web and mobile applications.

I am working with others to create a Startup Accelerator in Hawaii. If you're an entrepreneur, an investor, or are interested in being a corporate partner, please let me know.


Highlights:
========

* I'm a hard-core software engineer and have coded in everything from assembly language to objective-c on small critical projects to global high-demand web services. My forte is web and mobile (iPhone/iPad) apps.

* As CTO for Lycos I ran projects and engineering teams across the US as well as in Spain, Mexico, Brazil, Korea and Japan. I built a strong IP portfolio and helped create and run Lycos Labs, a startup incubator.

* I'm a "maker" and enjoy building things. I love working with electronics, micro controllers (like Arduino) and sensor technology. My hobby is building autonomous robots and smart network-connected devices.
