Serenity Holidays

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry

July 2005 – Present (8 years 6 months) Solent Business Park, Fareham, Hampshire

I have worked as a graphic designer and photographer in the travel industry for over 15 years. Photographic assignments include France, Italy, The Gambia, Cape Verde, Senegal and most recently Corsica for the Calvi Jazz Festival.

I also enjoy writing for Corsican Place's and The Gambia Experience's blog sites, various publications such as 'Your Experience' magazine and my own blog - http://travelwithkat.wordpress.com/

Since 2009 I have lead the UK team for The Gambia Experience's School Development Fund supporting schools in The Gambia.

Following the experience and 'know how' I have gained through writing 'Travel With Kat' and promoting this blog on various social media platforms, I am becoming more involved with developing the companies social media strategy and I am organising their first ever bloggers press trip which I will be joining as both host and blogger.
