Amy Bellgardt's Summary

Hello! My name is Amy and I am a work-at-home mom of two boys and wife to an insanely handsome jingle writer. (seriously) In 2008, I created MomSpark.net as a way to connect with fellow moms, exchange tips and share stories. I was looking for an outlet as a stay-at-home mom and quickly found the community I needed in the social media and blogging world.

Mom Spark now consists of four writers {myself, Desiree from So Fawned, Allison from Dream {a Little} BIGGER and Ali from Jam Hands} and has a wide range of articles for moms that include recipes, product reviews, arts & crafts, travel, blogging, fashion, parenting and an active community forum. I often work with brands on ambassador programs, sponsored posts, reviews and giveaways.

I also own and manage social media marketing company, Mom Spark Media, which helps mom bloggers further their blogging paths by offering instructional blogging and social media courses, as well as unique opportunities to work with brands. Mom Spark Media has worked on large online campaigns with Lands’ End, Pop Secret, Dell, VTech Communications and many more.

You can also find me on Twitter at @MomSpark and on Facebook at facebook.com/momspark.

Specialties

Blogging, advertising, marketing, web design, logo design, social networking, social media, mom-related issues, product reviews, and moms in small business.
