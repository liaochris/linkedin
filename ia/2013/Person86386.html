Mark Bunting's Summary

Mark is an accomplished entrepreneur & leader. CEO / CMO / with GM expertise in strategic consumer & enterprise brand building, channel development, strategic advancement and team building. His experience runs the gamut from CPG, EPG, new media & digital economy visionary, web 2.0 (mobile), e-commerce, biz dev, & retail. He has been a successful leader of numerous small cap entities and contributed as interim CMO at large cap tech. He is oft described as an offense-centric & gifted marketing leader with broad capabilities:

Integrated strategic vision
Financial acumen
Brand and market appreciation
Stakeholder value creation

After learning classic branding & sales as a member of the famed Wall Street Journal, he has since built & led teams contributing to over 400 brands in Technology and Electronics, Travel, Financial Services, Automotive, Health & Retail categories. Much of his vast experience in technology occurred at SkyTV (sold to Ziff-Davis / ZD -NYSE) and includes:

Traditional brand and product development / P&L / Integrated communications / Digital platforms
Retail marketing / PR / Extensive out-of-home and other non-traditional avenues. His transactional contributions include: Public company buy & sell, Private company buy and sell side, as well as numerous Private Equity placements.

His history as entrepreneur, gun-for-hire & even tech marketing personality has accrued 28 years of high-tech marketing expertise. That position has been validated in numerous arenas such as:

Published Author: (Virtual Power – Simon and Shuster). Professorship: Technology Marketing (University of Texas at Austin). Speaker (frequent contributor at trade/professional settings and even mass media). Value Creator – numerous successful engagements with measurable shareholder value. Board Member contributions

Specialties

Executive leadership, Turn-around experience consultancy, 20 year professional relationship base in Silicon Valley and Madison Avenue. With 400+ technology marketing/management engagements over the past 14 years, marketing, integration, P&L, executive leadership and BOD tenure.

Creative Background – have worked with the Marketing departments of over 400 technology related firms since 1986, expertise in messaging, marketing and communications in the tech field is unequalled.
