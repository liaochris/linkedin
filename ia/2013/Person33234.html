Calvin Dean - Dean Digital Video's Experience

Writer/Producer/Director/Editor

Dean Digital Video

March 2003 – Present (10 years 7 months)

Dean Digital Video, a video production company in the Memphis area, produces results-oriented presentations for corporations, small businesses, non-profits, museums, Chambers of Commerce, civic groups, service organizations and other firms.

Our clients include FedEx, Future Electronics, Thomas & Betts, American Heart Association, Agape Children's Services, Lifeblood, Delta State University and others. With two decades of know-how & award winning creativity we can produce highly effective presentations for you. Let us stick our knows in your business.

Editor/Producer/Director

Baldridge Studios

March 1989 – February 2003 (14 years)

Baldridge Studios specialized in corporate communications. Over the years, I worked on projects for Kellogg's, Kroger, FedEx and other Fortune 100 firms. In 1992, I traveled to Rome, Italy to videotape Etruscan tombs and artifacts for The Etruscans - Legacy of a Lost Civilization, a Memphis Wonders Series production. I also edited the orientation video which ran at Pink Palace Museum.

Writer/Producer

Baddour, Inc.

March 1985 – March 1989 (4 years 1 month)

Wrote & produced TV commercials for the chain of Fred's Discount Stores. Worked closely with Ardent Studios in Memphis and Bajus/Jones Film & Animation in Minneapolis. Also produced Fred's annual corporate meeting for corporate managment and store managment.

Production Manager & Writer/Producer

WJTV-12 & WDBD-40

March 1983 – March 1985 (2 years 1 month) Jackson, MS

Served as Writer/Producer at WJTV-12 in the Commercial Production. I also produced NewsCenter 12 promotions, improving the newscast's ratings from a weak #2 to #1. Was hired as WDBD-40's first Production Manager. Hired and managed a staff responsible for all production.

Writer/Producer/Editor/Director

Goudchaux & Maison Blanche Department Stores

September 1979 – March 1983 (3 years 7 months) Baton Rouge & New Orleans

Served as a copywriter for Goudchaux's print ads and TV spots. Was promoted to Producer/Director/Editor at Lea Advertising, responsible for all Goudchaux and Maison Blanche TV and radio commercials. During my tenure, Goudchaux's was #1 in the nation in sales-per-square-foot among America's department stores.
