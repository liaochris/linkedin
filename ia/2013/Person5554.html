Owner

Amber Shader Photography

June 2010 – Present (3 years 3 months) Based in Newark, Delaware but please contact us for travel rates.

Amber Shader is a photographer based in Newark, Delaware who specializes in fine portraiture. She is available for travel throughout the United States, so please contact us for travel rates.

Amber has crafted her skills and studied the art of photography locally at Delaware College of Art and Design and through various workshops in New York City, Las Vegas and Philadelphia.

We know the needs of our clients are not one size fits all. Amber Shader Photography can custom tailor photography packages and unique services based on your specific needs.

All packages include a clothing consultation over the phone and an online proofing gallery of 30 edited and retouched images so clients can email all their images to friends and family and post their gallery on Facebook.

Prints and unique keepsakes like home decor, image boxes, albums, photo jewelry and custom made CDs of all digital files are also available for purchase.

We pride ourselves on providing extraordinary client experiences including unique keepsakes and excellent customer service.
