Brian Jarvis' Summary

As a freelance consultant I bring a mix of communication skills to my clients on a project basis.

After reporting for a half dozen news outlets, I obtained my master's degree in strategic communication from the Missouri School of Journalism. This included a semester in Belgium researching how Europe uses social media in comparison to the U.S. I also completed a four-month project on Procter & Gamble's External Relations team in Brussels.

I consider myself multimedia in every sense of the word—I can write, edit and produce. I blog, post and tweet. I take pictures as well as shoot video. I pride myself on producing quality content for all types of communications, from company blogs and digital position papers to interactive features and corporate training videos.
