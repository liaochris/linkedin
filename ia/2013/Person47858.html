George Coghill's Summary

Cartoon-style vector artist and humorous illustrator specializing in cartoon logo design and cartoon character mascot design.

I also create magazine illustration, concert posters, t-shirt designs, packaging design, branding, advertising illustration and more.

Skilled in both traditional hand drawn art as well as Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop and the entire Adobe Creative Suite. I also bring an extensive design background and fine art foundation to my artwork.

I work with clients both large and small, from corporations to local businesses both directly with art departments and through marketing and advertising agencies.
