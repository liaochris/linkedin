Kevin Songer, J.D.'s Summary

Sustainable and Green Stormwater BMPs and technologies, Modular Wetlands Design, Green Roofs and Living Walls.

Specialties

Plants. Native and drought tolerant plants. Green Roofs, Living Walls. Stormwater. Combining Mechanical and Biological Stormwater treatment processes to provide Landscape and Stormwater Credit in one system. Develop lightweight, non-irrigated extensive green roof systems that are cost-effective and proven to survive hurricanes, drought, freezes, high winds and high humidity.
