Loren K. Barton's Summary

Loren Barton is a freelance Media Programmer/Consultant and Designer based in Los Angeles, California.

Beginning in 2007, Loren began supporting the TMB product line with demonstrations and seminars including the Green Hippo Hippotizer Media Server.

Loren has conducted training seminars covering a wide range of production lighting and video technologies. Loren has presented seminars about media servers and projection technology at many industry conferences, including USITT, InfoComm, Broadway Projection Master Classes, Concert Master Classes and LDI. He has completed over 200 Hippo School courses in the United States and internationally.

Loren's work ranges from theatrical to live event and performance design. Loren is a graduate of Carnegie Mellon University and began working on lighting crews in theater at an early age.
