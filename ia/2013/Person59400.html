Jason Mudd, APR's Summary

Jason Mudd is internationally Accredited in Public Relations (APR), a mark of distinction for public relations professionals who demonstrate their commitment to the profession and to its ethical practice, and who are selected based on broad knowledge, strategic perspective and sound professional judgment.

His work experience includes providing public relations consulting and services to great brands like Adecco, Budweiser, Dave & Busters, FIS, Florida Blue, H&R Block, Hilton, HP, Macquarie Group, Miller Lite, Modis, New York Life Insurance, Pizza Hut, Ray Charles, Rayonier, Synovus, Tarmac and Verizon.

Jason began his career as a journalist after graduating from the acclaimed University of Missouri School of Journalism with a bachelor’s degree. After a stint as a PR consultant to what is now Arm & Hammer’s Clean Shower, he joined what is now WindStream Communications as its marketing director and a shareholder. Jason went on to produce and promote the Emmy-winning Florida Sports Awards, which recognized the top professional and amateur athletes. There, Jason worked with key sponsors and professional teams and tours, including American Airlines, General Motors and Wachovia, Jacksonville Jaguars, Miami Dolphins, Miami Heat, Miami Marlins, Orlando Magic, Tampa Bay Buccaneers and Tampa Bay Rays, and ATP Tour, LPGA Tour, NASCAR, PGA Tour and WTA Tour.

Jason has always fully understood and embraced technology. In 1994, he taught himself HTML to build his first website. By 1995, companies sought out Jason for his website design services. By 1996, he was helping companies sell products online and providing SEO services. By 2000, he and his colleagues began conceptualizing and planning for the next level of web community and collaboration, "Internet 2.0," which is now known as "social media."
