Brenda Forstall's Summary

I have worked in employee benefits as a licensed broker for 16 years. I strive to ensure business owners and their employees choose the most appropriate benefit coverage for their healthcare needs and budget. In addition, I work diligently to educate my clients on how existing and pending legislation may impact their benefits and future cost. I invest the time to research and seek new ideas that will assist in stabilizing or reducing costs to both employers and employees. I assist businesses, individuals, families and Medicare eligible individuals in navigating the complex process of purchasing health, dental, vision. life, disability, long term care, travel insurance, Medicare Advantage, Medicare Supplement and PDP prescription drug plans.
