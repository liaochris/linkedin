James Acres' Projects

  • Location Based Mobile Coupons

    • September 2011 to May 2012
    Team Members: James Acres

    In this project a location-based coupon application is designed, implemented,
    tested and evaluated. Initially as part of analysis, consumer and merchant questionnaires
    are carried out. The results are analysed to gain an insight into user
    opinion. The gained stakeholder knowledge in addition to the background research
    is then used to design, implement and test an innovative location-based
    coupon service with several unique benefits. A user evaluation is carried out
    where merchants add coupons into the website application which are then pushed
    to consumer Android devices as they enter merchant brick and mortar places. The
    superior benefits of this project’s implementation include automating check-ins in
    an efficient way and offering group deals which automatically push coupons to
    devices once enough consumers have visited the location within a time span.
