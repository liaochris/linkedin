Denise Price Thomas' Summary

34 + years experience in healthcare. Certified in Healthcare Management, Pfeiffer University.

Denise is passionate about making a positive difference in healthcare. She enjoys coaching & training healthcare professionals, building lasting relationships between physicians and patients through effective communication and polished customer service.

By focusing on the positive and addressing the areas that need "POLISH," the result is a positive patient and physician outcome.

HEALTH, HOPE & HUMOR is presented by Denise's alter ego ~ "Gladys Friday" (aka Denise) ~ Healthcare Comedienne. Two speakers in one!

Keynote speaker for numerous conferences, Denise also offers motivational & inspirational presentations as well as training for healthcare organizations, hospitals and other businesses, civic groups, churches, women's events, senior groups, community organizations and more.
