Martin Maynard's Summary

Martin Maynard is a freelance business development professional with over 13 years’ commercial experience working in the life science, recruitment and publishing sectors across the UK, Europe and North America. Having previously fulfilled both operational and sales roles in the pharmaceutical and medical device supplier market, Martin has more recently brought his experience to bear in the publishing industry. Working for more than 5 years in the medical division of John Wiley & Sons, he has been a principle thought-leader and strategist in the evolution of several major print, online and digital publishing projects. Martin is a passionate writer, social media enthusiast and online devotee.

Available (for hire) for the following:

• Sales & marketing services including UK / EU sales representation
• Publishing & social media strategy
• Freelance writing including: blog posts; full length articles; case studies; business reports
• Creation of press releases / ad copy / media kits

Founder of the #PubStuff weekly tweetup (Fri at 2 pm UK time).
