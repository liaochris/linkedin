Mark Albertson's Summary

Mark Albertson is a Regional Vice-President and Trust and Estate Planning Officer with Fidelity Investments, serving the western United States. The Trust and Estate Planning Officer acts as the primary contact for trust and estate planning services at Fidelity. As a member of a national field-based team, Mark assists Senior/Account Executives in providing training and workshops, coaching, planning and presenting client events, and working with clients of Fidelity.

Mark’s job primarily centers on meeting with clients of Fidelity Investments to deliver comprehensive estate plans. Mark assists Fidelity clients in executing those plans with their outside advisors, cultivating relationships with existing centers of influence, including accountants and attorneys, to secure new trust relationships, coaching and training advisors on trust and estate planning concepts, and reviewing trust documents and other paperwork for completeness and accuracy before Fidelity Personal Trust Company accepts new trust business.

Prior to Fidelity, Mark was an attorney in private practice focused on estate and elder law for over 20 years, and has authored numerous national and local articles on estate planning and elder law, including his recently published book, Caring for Older Relatives, published in March of 2013. Mark can be contacted at mark.albertson@fmr.com.
