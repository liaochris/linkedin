Ken Braney's Summary

I have been involved in the plastics industry for over 35 years and have continues to work in markets in the USA, throughout Europe, the Middle East and India. Have helped companies grow their businesses, start up new businesses and have developed their customer base for growth through the vast network of contacts I have built over the last 35 years.

I have for the last 12 years been involved with Thermoforming Solutions. our objectives are to ensure our customers reach their potential in the various fields they have entered. This has meant a wide range of activities which ensure the customer achieve their objectives.

I have been responsible for governance and good practice in organisations such as The Society of Plastics Engineers which is a not for profit organisation of over 20,000 professionals connected to the plastics industry. I was elected President for the 2010-2011 term and was the first non North American to hold that office, not living and working in North America. I have been Chair of both SPE Europe (one of the founders) and The European Thermoforming division of SPE. My goals are always to achieve the best possible result in any activity I undertake. I have always been a leader and am rewarded when we achieve positive results.

My activities at present are with both Thermoforming Solutions Ltd where we are working on projects both in Europe and in the Far East and a new start up called Plastics Machinery Group International Ltd. Bringing together knowledge, expertise and solutions to the plastics industry. I continue to volunteer and work on behalf SPE and have held workshops in their ANTEC Mumbai conference in December 2012 and have been asked to organise a similar activity at their ANTEC Dubai conference in January 2014.

Specialties:Executive Directorship, leadership, project management,governance
