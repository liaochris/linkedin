Allan Chao's Summary

Hi, I'm an entrepreneur and technology enthusiast. I like startups, business ideas, and high-tech anything. I tend to set very ambitious goals and do whatever it takes to achieve them. In the last few years, I've been a business owner, technical architect, perfectionist coder, competitive gamer, public speaker, university instructor, contest judge, startup consultant, and more. Anything I do, I give everything I've got.

If you'd like to get in touch, please feel free to contact me.

---------------------------------
Allan Chao is a UC Berkeley graduate with a certificate of distinction for Entrepreneurship and Small Business Management. He has over ten years of web development experience, in the areas of programming, product design, technology architecture, project management, engineering management, outsourcing, and technology patent law. He holds workshops on web startups at the Small Business Administration and he teaches a class on web technology entrepreneurship at UC Berkeley. He currently works as a consultant for several web startups, with a focus on social networks, mobile apps, and software as a service.
