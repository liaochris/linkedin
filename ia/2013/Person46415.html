Funky Koi's Summary

Welcome to the Funky Koi Pond & Water Garden Supply! The Funky Koi is a Pond and Water Gardening Supply company, specializing in bringing you the best products for your koi, pond, home and garden! We are also Water Gardening enthusiasts, and we pride ourselves on offering you products with which we have had personal experience

On the "Shop Funky Koi" Network, you will find a large variety of koi foods, such as Hikari, Shinju, Microbe-Lift, and Blackwater Creek. You will also find products to build and care for your pond such as Firestone liners, Savio Filtration, Sequence Pumps, Little Giant pumps, Airmax Pond Treatments, Winston's Crystal Clear Water and Fish Treatments, Aeration systems, Laguna Products, K&H pond heaters, Loki Nets, Pond Netting, Aquatic plants and planters, Pond Vacuums, Matala filter media and more!


Here’s why we think you should choose our store:

•Excellent customer service: We respond to your needs as quickly as possible.
•Great prices for great products: We offer competitive prices and offer a wide range of products.
•Fast Shipping: We do our best to ship your order the same day.
•Return Policy: We will troubleshoot failures and facilitate returns on defective products.
•Security: Your transactions are fully encrypted using the latest technology, and we do not view or store payment information. We use a shopping cart solution that exceeds industry security standards and is fully PCI* Certified and is on Visa's list of providers!

* (Payment Card Industry).
