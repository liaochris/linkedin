Belinda Grundy's Summary

Belinda Grundy is the General Manager of Property Styling Online, BG Property Styling, and Senior Movers.

BG Property Styling helps property owners move and style their properties, aiming to save clients the overwhelming stress of a move and get top dollar for their sale or rent out price of their property.

Since the start of her business, the need arose for Belinda to open a new arm called and Senior Movers, a specialty relocation assistance business geared towards helping seniors with downsizing from the family home to a smaller residential property or into an Aged Care Facility.

Property Styling Online helps property owners from all over the world to present their property ready for sale, the right way. They deliver ‘planned for you’ daily action plan and resources via email, designed to focus the home owner's energy on the things to do, teaching you what to do and how to do it.

A real highlight during Belinda’s first years was being an award finalist in the 2009 Working Womens Network Awards and Pittwater Council Sustainability Awards.

Belinda has a Post Graduate Diploma in Management and has subsequently studied Interior Design.
