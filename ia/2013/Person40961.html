Eloi Courchesne's Summary

WHAT I DO

I provide communications and public relations advice, writing and project management services to public and private organizations as well as PR and event planning agencies to help them when they lack the expertise, the resources or the time. I work both in French and in English.


WHAT I’VE DONE

In 14 years, I have served over 100 clients, from global corporations to local non-profits.

In the past year, I managed community relations for a $1-billion hospital construction project, led visibility efforts for a venture capital group, organized information seminars for transplant patients, wrote a presentation for nationwide public hearings, directed the filming of a healthcare video, spearheaded a dozen announcements that generated hundreds of high-impact media hits and provided all sorts of other advice and support to my clients.


WHAT THEY’VE SAID

“Eloi has helped my newly established team to reach its objectives and more.” – Senia Rapisarda, Vice President, Strategic Investments & Initiatives, BDC Venture Capital

“Eloi brings his vast experience to the planning and execution of each project.” – Marlo Taylor, Vice President, Practice Group Lead, Healthcare, energi PR

“Eloi instantly understood the issues and was a vital contributor to this strategic project of prime importance for us.” – Louise Boisvert, Director of Communications, Sainte-Justine University Hospital Centre (not a LinkedIn member)


WHAT I WANT TO DO

I pride myself in achieving results and helping my clients reach their objectives. Strategic, thoughtful and impeccable are three of the words that best sum up my style and my work. If you have communication needs, I'd love to lend a hand. Please call or write anytime.
