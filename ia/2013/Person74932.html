Kevin Heupel's Summary

Kevin Heupel is a nationally recognized expert and attorney in the area of debt relief and helps good people get out of debt. Mr. Heupel has been featured in USA Today, CNBC, and the Brian Tracy Show. His solutions include credit counseling, debt settlement, loan modifications, offers in compromise, and personal bankruptcy. The right solution depends on the person's circumstances. Mr. Heupel offers a free consultation, a money-back guarantee, and the ability for his client's get a fresh financial start with the "7 Steps to a 720 Credit Score" training program.
