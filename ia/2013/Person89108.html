Matthew Dooley, MBA's Summary

Matthew Dooley is a Cincinnati OH native residing in Newport KY whose life is about innovating, connecting and giving back.

Matthew is Co-founder and COO of KAPTURE, which is debuting a wearable tech device in early 2014. KAPTURE is an always-on, audio-recording wristband that allows you to easily save and share what was just said, after the fact.

Matthew is also founder and CEO of dooley media, a specialty agency focused on helping organizations of all sizes and types to use social media to grow their business.

Matthew teaches social media to both undergraduate and MBA students at Xavier University’s Williams College of Business. Combining his knowledge and his experience in this emerging field, Matthew developed the curriculum from scratch and is especially proud of the interactive nature of the course and the lineup of guest speakers and participating companies.
