Adam Britten's Summary

Mashable recently described a campaign of mine as what could be "the next frontier for marketers." I pride myself on developing and executing innovative, exciting digital marketing campaigns that drive results - exceeding the expectations of my employer.

My work has been featured in & praised by several prominent tech and marketing publications including Mashable, Wired, Ad Age and PR Week, as well as quick service restaurant industry publications such as QSR Magazine; I've also been interviewed by CNN Tech & Mashable on multiple occasions. I've spoken at several social media conferences & events sponsored by Twitter, JPMorgan Chase, and New York University.

As a freelance writer, I've contributed my own pieces to publications such as Huffington Post, USA Today, The Chicago Tribune, The Disney Blog, Brazen Careerist, and others.

Previously, I graduated Summa Cum Laude a full year early from Syracuse University with a Bachelors degree in Marketing Management. After that, I moved to London to obtain a Masters degree in Digital Marketing from Hult International Business School; I was the youngest person in my program. While pursuing each of these degrees, I balanced my schoolwork with professional experience - managing social media marketing for several companies from a large, international retailer to a small tech startup, among other private clients.

My specialties include: social media marketing, digital marketing, community management, writing, mobile marketing, branding, content marketing, customer service, internal communications, and blogging.

Proficient in: Omniture, Radian6, Bazaarvoice, Bugzilla, Facebook Insights, Simply Measured, Social Flow, HootSuite, TweetDeck, WordPress, MailChimp, OfferPop, Wildfire, ShortStack and basic Photoshop, Illustrator, & HTML.

Outside of work, I love traveling, riding roller coasters, visiting museums, theatre, cooking, and all things Disney.
