John Lusher's Summary

Hello, my name is John Lusher. After 20 + years of helping other organizations achieve their goals through focused sales efforts and strategic marketing, I started my own firm. We research, develop, implement and manage marketing and digital media plans. We specialize in social media and digital marketing, but our experience encompasses all forms of marketing. How Can We Help You?

I am a trainer, speaker and extensive user of social media platforms. My main purpose with most social platforms is to network, connect, and to promote others.

We enjoy a strategic partnership with The Social Buzz Lab and Erika Design to provide Graphic Design and Brand Development/Management.

I volunteer in an advisory capacity for Trust House; a transitional homeless shelter in Roanoke that serves Veterans, families and individuals. http://TrustHouse.us

I was a keynote speaker for the inaugural Orange County Social Media Summit in 2012. #OCSMS.

Personally, I am a husband, son, and a brother. I love the beach, Jimmy Buffett and Frank Sinatra music along with fine cigars. My dream is to experience as many beaches of the world as is humanly possible! Oh, and to Skydive for my 50th birthday!

I welcome the opportunity to connect with you or work with you; my email is John@JohnLusher.com. Visit my website for more information on what our firm can do for you: http://JohnLusher.com

If you wish, connect with me across the social spectrum on Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest, Instagram, Google + and YouTube.

Specialties: Relationship builder, promoting others, building networks, making introductions, marketing, sales, social media, social networking and voice talent.
