Dennis A Fletcher's Summary

I provide marketing services to developers and remarketers in the IT sector through Fletcher Marketing Services -- FMS (www.FletcherMarketing.us). We serve high-tech product delivery/support organizations including IBM business partners, Independent Software Vendors and IT service providers. Services include: Marketing and Branding Strategy/Execution; Web content development, editing and translation; newsletters/publications development and editing; advertising and PR; Social Media/Networking; events and trade shows; electronic and print marketing deliverables, White Papers; Search Engine Optimization; Market Research.

We work closely with Chris Smith at CC Communications (www.cccommunicationsonline.com) who brings a stellar career in IT publishing, news reporting and both technical and promotional writing and research. We also work with Monica Hess (TWgurl54@hotmail.com), experienced technical documentation specialist in military and IT sectors with a security clearance. Together, our alliance is called TechMarketing.us and is described at www.TechMarketing.us [under development]. We are based in Southern California and Taipei, Taiwan.

SPECIALTIES

1. PLANNING: Develop marketing strategies as well as specific targeted marketing campaigns for high tech organizations.

2. AWARENESS: Design/execute awareness and branding campaigns for newer organizations and product lines.

3. SOCIAL MEDIA: Leverage the Internet and Social Network assets to promote client programs.

4. DELIVERABLES: Marketing materials focused on decision makers.

5. CHANNEL MARKETING: Channel strategy and materials to recruiting channel partners.

Earned IBM's top Thomas J. Watson Award for Branch Office Customer Workshop I developed at IBM in 5 years I was there. Technical consultant and management consultant for 20 years, then held various marketing positions with IT vendors for another 20 years before settling into FMS and TechMarketing.us.
