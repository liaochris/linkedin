Mark Woolbright's Summary

MISSION: I believe green-space and habitat must always be returned to the built environment and I love the challenge of creating new ways to accomplish it.

Through The Living Wall Company, I am providing the very best "living walls" and living building systems for those who believe living systems are superior to traditional methods.

Through Greenwall Ventures, I create new products and innovation for our own use and for the use of client companies looking for a competitive edge. Contact me directly at 314-394-8715 to ad us to your green building team.

HISTORY: I patented a line of plantable, "green" retaining modules over 20 years ago and have been promoting that niche ever since. The retaining wall market has seen many changes in that time and I have always believed that my living wall "niche" could become the main market once products became widely available, widely known and once the sustainability movement grew to be the major influence in building systems. We are at that moment in time! I have been manufacturing and marketing "living wall" systems most of my adult life and now have invented the ultimate "green wall" or more accurately, "living retaining wall" product and coupled it with portable and scalable manufacturing technology. The new line of plantable wall modules which are being marketed worldwide by SmartSlope, LLC represents the culmination of all my experience and my wish list of product features developed throughout my career. I hope to increase awareness for the beneficial use of living structures and partner with companies looking to expand.

SPECIALTIES: Product design, innovation in manufacturing methodology, intellectual property development, sustainable building and the use of plantable building systems, concrete molding technology, marketing training, public speaking, group education regarding green building systems, vegetation selection assistance, testing and compliance for retaining wall systems and living walls.

Our target customers are "Green Building" and "Sustainability" minded project owners and specifiers interested in successful execution. LWC will rely heavily on the capabilities of the SmartSlope Living Retaining Wall System invented by Mark Woolbright, coupled with his vast experience in the successful implementation of fully vegetated wall systems. Passion for living architecture is what drives our mission and successful Living Retaining Wall fulfillment is what we offer.
