IBCME Services' Summary

IBC Medical Services is the oldest independent training institute in the UAE. Established in 2000 and owned/operated by experienced healthcare providers, IBC has been training medical and working professionals for the last 12 years; teaching 4,000 physicians annually.

• Ranked 33rd on the Dubai Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) Top 100 Companies List
• Recognized as a GCC Top 100 Company and Arabia/Turkey Top 500 Fastest Growing Company
• First company to be awarded His Highness Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum Award for Best Health and Wellness in 2007 and also a Member of the Establishment for Business Development
• International faculty and staff comprised of well experienced licensed medical professionals and very senior professors, from around the world.
IBC Medical Services is internationally accredited by:
• British Society for Colposcopy and Cervical Pathology
• British Society of Gynecological Endoscopy
• American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine (AAAM)
• Affiliated with the education center of JUREI and Jefferson Hospital Philadelphia USA
• Diploma courses are awarded by UGC and recognized by the GLOBAL OPEN UNIVERSITY India
