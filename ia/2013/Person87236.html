Mark Wieman's Summary

I am a UX consultant with a focus on interaction design, UI prototyping, and usability research for desktop and mobile websites and apps.

I have 15 years of hands-on experience designing and doing research for startups and Fortune 500 companies. Verticals served include e-commerce, social, software, entertainment, education, science, health care, and professional services.

I've worked with some great clients, both directly and through agencies like Blink, Fell Swoop, LiveAreaLabs, IF/THEN, Copacino+Fujikado, Methodologie, Wunderman, and the pioneering Sitewerks (RIP).

I can help you make your website or mobile app more effective and easier for your customers to use. Please contact me to discuss the possibilities...
