Brian Williams' Summary

My biggest thrill is to design and build something new that people love to use.

Since getting my Masters in Human-Computer Interaction (the study of how people use computers) I've spent the last eleven years doing user research, interface design, prototyping and software engineering. My first iPhone application IQ boost was an early success on the iTunes App Store. Following that, I have built iPhone and iPad applications for Apple and Ongo Inc.

Specialties: Interaction design, Cocoa/Objective-C for the iPad, iPhone and Mac.

Brian Williams' Patents

  • Graphical configuration interface for network policy

    • United States Patent 8,452,876
    • Issued September 5, 2006
    Inventors: Brian Williams, Al Papp

    A graphical user interface for configuring policies which manage traffic over a computer network.

    Sans patent speak: This was a GUI to configure 1000's of computers to control a Wide Area Network (WAN). We designed a system that allowed global policy to be applied with one rule and the exceptions to be called out where needed. Users could also pick different representations of the same policy depending on the task.
