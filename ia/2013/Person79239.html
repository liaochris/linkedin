Laurie Pehar Borsh's Summary

As a publicist, promoter and producer, I have been providing simple to complex social media publicity strategy productions and tactical programming as well as online reputation management services to clients since 2001---alongside my 25+ years of PR and marketing experience. My agency produces and handles corporate and product brand (as well as small business and agency) social media and traditional publicity programs on a strategic and tactical level.

To that end we also work for C-level executives and other senior professionals including creative executives and artists---on a very personal level (within social media channels and along with traditional media placement in some cases) to provide ongoing and consistent thought leadership visibility, personal promotion and reputation management. This personal online publicity is the key to a company brand's success within online/social media channels. We work with clients who already have started a social media publicity and promotional effort and others who have not even made the attempt.

Today’s most talented and highly successful executives, professionals (who work within company and product brands) are still either too busy or too unequipped to stay involved or truly committed to a personal social media publicity initiative—which, in turn, will support the initiative of the brand. Social Media publicity programs on a company and personal level can and and will provide ROI (on many levels), but only if it's constant, consistent and engaging.

Putting a “traditional” PR professional in place to develop the right content and messaging as well as the best audience and search engine page rank is really not so much of a luxury, but really more of a necessity these days. Brand journalism, thought leadership generation, and content broadcasting is a must-have along-side traditional marketing and sales programs, traditional press and media placement as well any kind of professional career advancement effort.

As social media continues to build into a viable and necessary “new media” channel, professionals and businesses are now required to produce and maintain robust and highly favorable digital profiles as well as consistent online publicity initiatives in order to compete for new and future business opportunities. While there are many experts offering plenty of great information and advice about the ins and outs about the use of social media for publicity, promotion or marketing and advertising purposes, there continues to be a huge disconnect for people and businesses when it comes to putting effective social media practices into place. Many, if not most, of today’s highly successful executives, professionals and company brands are too busy, uninterested or simply unequipped to stay involved and truly committed to any type of social media PR initiative–which is where LPBPR comes in to play.

LPBPR produces and manages social media PR for C-suite executives and other types of high level professionals, artists, small businesses, corporations and PR agencies. Working with clients who already have started a social media publicity or promotional effort and others who have not even made the attempt, the LPBPR brand is about putting a “traditional” PR professional and promoter in place (on a very personal level) to develop the best strategy, place the right content and messaging, and gather the best audiences and connections that will lead to better online (and in some cases, off line) visibility and image—best search engine page rank alone does not always suffice. Social Media PR management is not so much a luxury as it may be a necessity for busy professionals and business operators—especially those who are not used to handling the task of self-promotion, marketing or public relations in traditional, let alone in new media channels. We support all traditional marketing and sales programs, traditional press and media campaigns and professional career advancement efforts.
