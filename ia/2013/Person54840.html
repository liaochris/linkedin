The International Journal of Sceintific & Engineering Research ( ISSN 2229-5518 ) is an open access peer-reviewed international forum for scientists and engineers involved in computer science to publish high quality and refereed papers. Papers reporting original research or extended versions of already published conference/journal papers are all welcome.
