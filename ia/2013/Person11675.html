Barbara Gilbert's Summary

Barbara Gilbert Interiors, LLC specializes in high end residential & commercial interior design. Our team creates spaces that truly reflect our client's personality, encompassing but not limited to, classic designs with a updated twist! A respected expert in her field, Barbara Gilbert has contributed her advice to television and radio programs such as Good Morning Texas, DFW Closeup, KXAS DFW Nonstop and House Talk Today Radio. She has also served as a presenter for the 2011 Dallas Home and Garden Show and a judge of the 2011 and 2012 Habitat for Humanity Design Duels.
Recent honors include 2013 Design Shuffle top 10 award, and her work has been featured on HGTV Remodel publication. An international publication called Cheers! Wine Cellar Design, by ArtPower Publishing featured a wine cellar created by Barbara Gilbert Interiors in their book. Barbara serves as President of IDS/DFW and is an Associate ASID member as well as an IDS Associate member.
