Founder

Lookshelves.com

February 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months)

Lookshelves.com is the website for literary voyeurs.

I created it when I realized that there was no online space where one could see books in their natural habitat... on shelves, in homes. I kind of thought there should be, so I made one.

The construction of our online literary identities is formal, constructed, self-aware. What we post to our Goodreads profiles is almost always less intimate, and less interesting than what's actually sitting on our bookshelves.

Lookshelves is bookshelves to look at. Go look already.
