Linda Sivertsen's Summary

As an award-winning author, New York Times best-seller, ghostwriter, editor, & book proposal coach, my work & I have been on Forbes.com, CNN, E!, Extra, & in the NY Post, NYT, Family Circle, & Teen Vogue. I launch bestselling authors at my Carmel Writing Retreats. Call (323) 769-5153 or go to: www.BookMama.com

Books I've published (as author, co-author, ghostwriter, or collaborator), minus hush-hush titles:


THE COMPOUND EFFECT: JUMPSTART YOUR INCOME, YOUR LIFE, YOUR SUCCESS, by Darren Hardy (Vanguard Press, Nov. 2011). Collaborator. New York Times, Wall Street Journal & USA Today bestseller.

YOUR BIG BEAUTIFUL BOOK PLAN: by Danielle LaPorte + Linda Sivertsen. A digital bestseller. www.YourBigBeautifulBookPlan.com

CLOSER THAN YOU THINK: by Debbie Heneghan, with Linda Sivertsen. (Hampton Roads, April, 2012.)

GENERATION GREEN: THE ULTIMATE TEEN GUIDE TO LIVING AN ECO-FRIENDLY LIFE, by Linda Sivertsen & Tosh Sivertsen (Simon & Schuster, 2008. 4th printing). See awards below.

HARMONIC WEALTH, by James Arthur Ray, with Linda Sivertsen (Hyperion, 2008). New York Times bestseller.

DO I LOOK FAT IN THIS? GET OVER YOUR BODY AND ON WITH YOUR LIFE, by Rhonda Britten (Putnam, 2006). Collaborator.

THE WILL TO SURVIVE: A MENTAL & EMOTIONAL GUIDE FOR LAW ENFORCEMENT PROFESSIONALS & THE PEOPLE WHO LOVE THEM, by Bobby Smith PhD, with Linda Sivertsen.

FEARLESS LIVING: by Rhonda Britten (Dutton, 2001). I co-authored the proposal that led to a 4-day bidding war and a 6-figure deal. Many others followed...

LIVES CHARMED: INTIMATE CONVERSATIONS WITH EXTRAORDINARY PEOPLE, by Linda Sivertsen (HCI, 1998). See award below.

Magazine Editor:

As a contributing editor to Delta Sky magazine, I was on their "Green Team" in '07. I've since ghosted & written guest columns for The Huffington Post.

As the West Coast Editor of BALANCE magazine from 2002-2009, I interviewed celebs & wrote 24 cover stories. (See covers, articles, & testimonials at www.BookMama.com.)

Specialties

Getting clients ready for & connected with big agents, who get them 6- & 7-figure book deals.

Taking the spark of an idea from start to finish... from blip to outline to fully "cooked" book + proposal. Introducing the author to agents, & then writing/editing/consulting on the book to bestseller-status.

My fave way to get you published: Your Big Beautiful Book Plan (created w/ the brilliant Danielle LaPorte), + Writing Retreats in Carmel-by-the-Sea that catapult your writing & career.

Linda Sivertsen's Experience

Linda Sivertsen is a book proposal doctor, author whisperer, agent connector, idea-fairy, and huge-hearted cheerleader of creativity for writers of all genres. Ghostwriter | Author | Editor

Book Mama

March 2002 – Present (11 years 5 months) Los Angeles

Out of the hundreds of thousands of books released each year, 50-75% are ghostwritten. With only about 5,000 ghostwriters in the U.S., I stumbled upon a really good gig:) While the deadlines can be tight and the hours sometimes grueling, it's always an honor being entrusted to take the knowledge and experience a person has gathered over his or her lifetime, and make sense of it all, make it sing. Each book, another puzzle. Each piece, sacred. I am blessed.

Author

www.DreamItGreen.com

August 1989 – Present (24 years)

I pinch myself that I'm an award-winning, best-selling author who helps writers shortcut their process to get publishing deals, big advances, and become best-selling authors themselves. My first book, Lives Charmed: Intimate Conversations with Extraordinary People, was published in 1998. In it, I interviewed people like Arnold Palmer, Wyland, & Woody Harrelson about how they'd succeeded--and also about their environmental heroism. Since, I've been blessed to write or collaborate on many other titles. In the last few years, I've co-authored the NYT best-selling book (Harmonic Wealth, Hyperion); an environmental hit for teens with my son, Tosh (Generation Green, Simon & Schuster); collaborated on the biz bestseller The Compound Effect (Vanguard), and co-authored Closer Than You Think Think (Hampton Roads).
