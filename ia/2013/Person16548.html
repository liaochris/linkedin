Brandon Reader's Summary

I am a creative professional who is proficient with many disciplines of design including design for web, print, flash, interactive, identity, online branding, and user-interface design. Over the past seven years I have worked as a print designer, web designer, interactive designer and art director.

I create unique interactive web and print solutions to help projects reach their full potential. Innovative design solutions, extraordinary attention to detail, and the communication goals of the client are my primary focus for any project.

I have had the opportunity to work on projects for the following distinguished clients:
