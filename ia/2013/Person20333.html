Carole Sanders' Summary

Originally I trained as an accountant however in late 1980's after becoming ill and experiencing the incredible healing powers of homeopathy I started training as a homeopath.

I graduated in 1993 as a homeopath and have been practising ever since. Over the last 20 years I have gained a wealth of experience in other fields, counselling mentoring and supervising and also spiritual healing. This together with my original homeopathic training does give me great insight into the human heart and soul.

I am passionately committed to homeopathy and believe that life is too short to suffer. You can be free from pain and feel you are truly living for the first time. Pain can be physical or emotional although most physical pain has its roots in emotions.

My latest project is an excting one to bring homeopathy to the general public and provide a series of homeopathic workshops to educate and empower people - they can use homepathy for their friends and family to treat minor ailments.

My intention is also to create a positive profile for homeopathy in the light of all the pharmaceutical companies denigration of homeopathy.
