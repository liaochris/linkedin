Jessica Norman's Summary

After getting my feet wet in the newspaper industry, I now specialize in developing successful online and print identities for small businesses and individuals. My life as a journalist allows me cultivate highly efficient and highly targeted written, graphic and multimedia information. I offer a wide range of services, including brand development, graphics, web design and development, promotional materials, copy writing and editing, and consultations.

My primary focus is on non-profit organizations and small businesses, helping them realize their potential in print and online campaigns with smaller budgets.
