Florian Le Goff's Summary

As an entrepreneur with a proven track record on IT sec and health care + IT, I learnt (and continue to learn) lots of lessons on how to build efficient and safe software products while managing expectations and creating a converging value for people with different interests (investors, regulating bodies, users, teams).

I enjoy creating high quality products based on pragmatic and well designed code. I care about showing the result to the world, because a product without users is nothing. Attending conferences, giving talks, speaking and learning from the public is something close to have an infinite ROI. I work closely my user/developers communities to enhance products within short timeframes, thanks to the amazing feedback always provided by people on the ground.

As you may have guessed, I have a strong interest in regulated topics (health care, security...) and I care about managing teams, as talented professionals and not as ressources.
