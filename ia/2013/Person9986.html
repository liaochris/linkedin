Arthur Zey's Summary

I am a problem-solver. While competent to tackle abstract mathematical challenges, my true passion is in organizing and optimizing things in the real world.

My experience, education, and interests are diverse, and this allows me to find creative, efficient ways of identifying and solving problems. But most of all, it has allowed me to integrate a broad context into a powerful sense of intuition.

As an undergraduate mathematics major with an emphasis in computer science, I sharpened my analytical skills, while law school taught me how to consider and evaluate differing perspectives. Starting and running a 501(c)(3) philosophy organization for seven years taught me a tremendous amount about management and, more substantively, how to apply my mathematical precision to the humanities.

I've had a very wide breadth of experience in the technology industry over the last 14 years, including general private consulting, tutoring, IT administration, technical support, web design and coding, data analytics, technical sales, project management, product management, and product marketing.

I have a strong entrepreneurial disposition, and I love working with people. I have excellent communication skills, an eye for efficiency, and I am great at simultaneously seeing the big picture and focusing on the details necessary to bring that big picture to fruition.

Professionally, I am most enthusiastic about product and project management:

On the product side, I love designing intuitive user interfaces, coming up with killer features, and finding ways to combine existing technologies in innovative and powerful ways; on the project side, I revel in improving processes, efficiently allocating resources, prioritizing goals, and leading teams to success.

I am passionate about technology generally, but more than that, I am passionate about using technology to improve the lives of real users by serving as the creative and organizational nexus for the development of amazing products.
