Jeffrey Bunce's Summary

Since beginning part time in the sign trade after high school (1980), moving onto college and commercial art school, and starting my own sign company in 1985, I have lived through the transition within the graphics industry from the days of hand lettering and airbrush to now with complex vector designs and large format printing.

Possessing both skill sets of both yesteryear and today's commercial graphic production, accompanied with my knowledge and business ownership experience of 24 years places me in a unique position to incorporate both aspects.

For me, everything is about superior creativity, quality and service while always remaining in pursuit and delivering the Wow factor to my clients.

I've had many years in the field with my sign company, http://www.bigeastsigns.com, and have designed logo's and produced graphics for so many different type businesses and trades, all having their own very special message, that I bring to you, practical experiences and diversity for any application.


Specialties: Illustration, Logo Design, Layout, Photo to Scalable Vector Graphic, Corel Draw, Creative Consultation, Communication, Mural Painting, Oil Painting, Acrylic and Watercolor Painting
