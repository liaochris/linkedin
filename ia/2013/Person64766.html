Jo Beyersdorfer's Summary

JNB is a full-service marketing and events company that will handle every aspect of your event, campaign, or promotion from start to finish.

We are a company that believes not only in satisfying the needs of their clients, but also in building relationships of honesty, trust, and face to face interaction.

Major events we have produced include the Dirty Dancing Festival, Backstage's Actorfest LA, Social Media Week LA, and Silicon BeachFest.

Background: Through experience in multiple fields of the event, marketing, entertainment and public relations industries, JNB president and founder Jo Beyersdorfer has worked as an event producer, promotions director, and business manager for various firms in Southern California. She has worked with high end clientele including Motorola, Juicy Couture, The Gap, Grey Goose Vodka, and The Southern California Honda Dealers Association. She has produced and coordinated events and promotions that run nation-wide including street team promotions and mobile tours, as well as reoccurring and one-time events and promotions local to Southern California.

Jo Beyersdorfer's Experience

Founder and Director

The Dirty Dancing Festival

April 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) Dirty Dancing Festival at Lake Lure

The Dirty Dancing Festival was founded in 2010 in the beautiful town of Lake Lure North Carolina, where much of the classic film Dirty Dancing was filmed. With the support of Lionsgate Films, the blessing of Eleanor Bergstein (the original writer of the film), and several of the film's cast and crew, we move into our 3rd Annual festival! Founded by former dancer-turned-event producer Jo Beyersdorfer of JNB Marketing & Events, and PR-guru Michelle Yelton of The McConnell Public Relations (Michelle is also a full time resident in Lake Lure!), the festival kicked off with over 1,000 attendees in 2010. In 2011 JNB took over the direction of the festival, and still produces the event today.

Real World Marketing and Events Manager

The Southern California Honda Helpful Campaign

January 2007 – January 2008 (1 year 1 month)

In 2007, JNB founder Jo Beyersdorfer acted as lead producer and manager of this real-world marketing campaign including training and managing over 30 team members, press outreach, logistics management, and event production.

The campaign covered 57 cities and their surrounding areas across Southern California including constant street team presence performing 'Helpful' acts for the general public, schools, and communities.

Monthly larger scale events included city-wide parking meter buyouts for the public, mass distribution of summer beach umbrellas, summer movie tickets, and umbrellas on rainy days.

The campaign's first year culminated in The First Annual Honda Helpful Awards. Nominations for 'Helpful' people across Southern California were collected online, and three winners were selected to receive a free, new Honda vehicle. Winners were surprised at a press-heavy Honda-blue carpet event on the beach next to the Santa Monica pier.

Special Event Manager

Azzurra Artists in Residence Event

January 2007 – January 2007 (1 month)

On January 26th, 2007, Jo Beyersdorfer managed this special event featuring the work of its three hosts, Dennis Hopper, Joe Goode, and Peter Alexander at the Azzurra Luxury Residences in Marina del Rey.

An empty top-floor penthouse was converted into a gallery to showcase each artist's work in addition to four additional spaces in the building. Additional responsibilities included management of catering, DJ, custom lighting, host handlers and liquor sponsorship.
