Bruce Outridge's Summary

I am now President and Co-Owner of Outridge Enterprises Inc. The company has three divisions under the corporate umbrella. Spending 30 years in the transportation industry from mover's helper to driver, owner-operator, and fleet supervisor has given me vast experience and knowledge that I now use to create a unique stake for our company. Working on values that are high in ethics our company goal is to offer quality products and services that will improve people's lives. Our custom illustration and design division Bruce Outridge Productions has a strong hold in the transportation industry and specializes in cartoons, caricature, and comic art. Our second division Outridge Consulting Services offers courses, presentations, and consultation on leadership, complacency, entrepreneurship, and business with a strong focus on the transportation industry. Our third division Outridge Translation Services offer French /English translation services for the Quebec market. To view more information on our services please visit www.outridgeenterprises.ca.

Specialties

My expertise to business is as follows: 30 years transportation experience, marketing solutions, editorial creation, business startup advice, branding and identity.
My illustration experience: event entertainment, illustration and design, branding and identity, animation creation, graphic design, transportation illustration, stock illustration, web design, social media setups, social media content creation, logo design, comic illustration, marketing design.
