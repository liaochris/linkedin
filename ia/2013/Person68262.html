Jordan Masiakos' Summary

We pride ourselves in providing personalized zealous and aggressive legal representation to our clients in the areas of Personal injury litigation, Residential & Commercial Transactional Real Estate & Wills, Trusts and Estate Planning.

Specialties: The emphasis of our practice is on serious personal injury cases including but not limited to serious automobile accidents, falls from scaffolds and ladders and premises liability cases. In addition, we assist clients in the sale and purchase of residential and commercial real estate as well as businesses. We also prepare estate planning documents such as wills, trusts, and ancillary documents.
