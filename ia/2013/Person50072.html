Greg Coffey's Summary

Having completed my Masters in Business and Management I have relocated to the San Francisco Bay Area and currently looking for opportunities.

As part of my Masters I completed a minor dissertation focusing on Equity Crowdfunding, a new online strategy whereby groups of people pool their money to support a company in exchange for equity. I examined how, if at all, equity-based crowdfunded companies are creating post-investment relationships with investors.

Amongst my achievements over my Masters I came first of class in Management and Organisational behaviour and Marketing Management.
