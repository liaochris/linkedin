Keith Speegle's Summary

Are you looking for someone with a "passion for design," someone who will find satisfaction in successfully completing a variety of different type projects. I am that person! I've worn many different hats in my previous positions, and I thoroughly enjoy ALL aspects of the creative process. I’ve worked in the industry for over 15 years as a graphic designer and art director. I have experience producing direct mail pieces, handouts, packaging, websites, flyers, brochures, signing, email campaigns, presentations for trade shows and seminars, video and more. I have extensive knowledge of the printing process, and pride myself on successful but cost-effective strategies in team projects.

Keith Speegle showed an early predisposition for the arts, winning the hearts of his family with great refrigerator art such as "family goes to Disney World" and "the great little soccer player." As he grew up, he knew he was destined for a great career in the graphic arts. His dream was finally realized in 1993, when he enrolled in the Graphic Design program offered at Oklahoma State University-Okmulgee. In only his first year at OSU-Okmulgee, he became known as his teachers' favorite pupil, completing his assignments ahead of schedule with ground breaking designs, innovative slogans and creative writing unparalleled in school history!

Upon graduating in 1995, he accepted a position within the Wal-Mart Corporate Signing Department, where he cut his teeth in a real-world fast paced environment... setting up photo shoots and coordinating printing efforts, all while designing for the Automotive, Tire Lube Express and One- Hour Photo Centers. His gregarious personality and impressive talents soon attracted world-renowned companies where his distinctive style led to many successful ad campaigns. Having set a precedent, he's always looking forward to continued marketing conquests, boldly seeking that next exciting ad-venture where no man has gone before!
