Marie LeBlanc Flanagan's Summary

I am a thinker, maker and a truth seeker.

As the executive director of Weird Canada, I incorporated the organization as a National Nonprofit, transitioned the site to bilingualism, obtained a 50K grant to build a nationwide store and distribution service, recruited over 200 new volunteers (from Victoria to Nunavut), ran a successful drive to win “Best Independent Music Website in Canada” (CBC3) and introduced policy and dialogue relating to accessibility, openness, and inclusivity. You can read my to-do list here: http://weirdcanada.com/2013-objectives/

An experienced professional in both Social Enterprise Management (New Beginnings for Youth, The ARTery, Wyrd Arts Initiatives) and Communications (Weird Canada, CBC), I am experienced in leading far-reaching initiatives and achieving mission-critical objectives. My skills include strategy, solution-oriented action plans, communications, and working with diverse groups of people to define and achieve results.

Specialties: Analysis, Mapping and Actualizing Ideas, Communications, Community Design
