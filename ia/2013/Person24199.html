Christine McDougall's Summary

I have spent my life exploring the skills and processes of screwing business as usual, beginning with a deep love affair of the work of R. Buckminster Fuller, and extending to an extensive examination of our economic, business and energy systems. My work is inspired by the core belief that entrepreneurs are seeking to build businesses that are successful in all domains. Profitable, ethical, environmental, purpose driven, community and future considerate, and truly inspirational to others. My concern is that anything less than business as unusual and we are not going to make it.

I have mastery in the fields of facilitation, leadership intelligence, thought leadership, human relational dynamics, adult human development, integral and systems theory and integrity and ethics.

I am now deeply engaged in creating a global movement and tribe around screwing business as usual. The purpose, to reignite the heart and soul of business for a successful tomorrow for all stakeholders. To avoid ‘feeding yesterday and starving tomorrow.” (Peter Drucker)

I write a daily blog speaking to entrepreneurs and business owners who insist on screwing business as usual for all the right reasons while living a great life.

Christine McDougall's Publications

  • Tension and Compression- Prerequisites for a healthy business

    • Integral Leadership Review
    • August 15, 2013
    Authors: Christine McDougall

    This article has been written after years of working with businesses and leaders within business. The examples of businesses that work well with tension and compression are rare. Most of the businesses that get this right are small and agile. They have unquestioned clarity around the service their business provides and they hold this core integrity as sacred.

  • Speak the Truth, 7 Steps to Radical Truth with Compassion

    • Self Published
    • December 10, 2011
    Authors: Christine McDougall

    Speak the Truth, 7 Steps to Radical Truth with Compassion, covers the 7 critical steps required to make sure you will be able to begin and conclude a critical conversation from a place that ensures the best possible outcome for all parties. The 7 step process is a rigorous self inquiry process including addressing the following.

    *examine your own agenda, judgments, outcomes, emotions
    *ensure that any covert agenda’s or emotions are clarified prior to stepping into a conversation
    *allow enough safety and care for people to be able to step into highly toxic conversations in order to resolve conflict
    *connect to the humanity of a conversation, and why it really matters to you
    *create a clear space to be able to re-engage a more healthy relationship

    When people step into a conversation that really matters from a space that invites truth and compassion, miracles often occur. Long term painful, broken relationships are able to be healed. People are able to move on, release judgements and find forgiveness. Speak the Truth does require courage, but less than what you would need without going through this process.

    Powerful, practical and able to be used as a framework for deep connection, advanced interpersonal skills, and emotional intelligence, Speak the Truth will add value to all your relationships.
