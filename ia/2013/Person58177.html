Jane Frankland's Summary

By the time Jane was 29yrs she'd built a 7-figure business. She did it within 2yrs. Whilst starting a business from scratch is a huge achievement it's not the hardest thing in business to do. The hardest thing is turning around a company that's failing. Jane has done both, and without investment. She has a diverse background - from being nominated as a Young British Designer after graduating, to building a leading global IT security (penetration testing) firm and working for some of the UK's biggest brands.

She has a proven track record in organically growing a company and delivering increased sales fast through a clear focus on the business strategy. She's comfortable discussing business strategy with senior executives as information security needs with technical staff. She has the ability to understand and translate between technical and business requirements leading to faster, smoother and more effective engagements.

Having been a business owner for over 16yrs, Jane worked tirelessly to create a respected brand in the industry, and to lead a team and business environment that promoted success at every level. Over the years she's demonstrated natural organisational and leadership skills and has provided the tangible glue that made her own company and others work. As a true entrepreneur, she has good commercial sense and has helped develop business opportunities through her effective marketing, networking and selling skills.

She's passionate about helping companies grow through effective marketing and business development strategies along with joined up operations. She's also dedicated to helping the team that she works with develop along the way. Jane has authored many information security articles and been featured in many publications. She also writes for other markets outside of information security, including entrepreneurship. She's driven by her 3 children, an obsession to deliver outstanding quality and to make a positive difference in the world!
