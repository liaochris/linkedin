Michelle Lentz's Summary

I am an award-winning instructional designer seeking a design and facilitation position in a forward-thinking learning organization. I am a leader and strategic driver focused on organizational and team success. As a learning strategist, I drive leadership initiatives, strategic planning, employee development, program implementation, change management and collaborative partnerships designed to improve performance.

I'm actively seeking opportunities in two ways: I'd love to be your instructional strategist or training manager, guiding your company's training goals. But I'm also actively seeking amazing contract and freelance opportunities to continue to broaden my expertise.

I have spent much of the last decade as a successful independent consultant, offering instructional design, instructor-led delivery, webinars, and electronic performance support systems. Responsibilities in various roles included project management, design and delivery of training, needs assessments, mobile design approach documents, use of ADDIE development cycle, integration of Agile development principles, proposal and RFP writing, press release writing, management and contractor/vendor management.

My goals are to work hard, work smart, and hopefully play hard too. I want to challenge myself in training design and delivery, marketing and social media. I approach everything with diligence, integrity and, I hope, an everlasting sense of humor.

I simply love presenting and public speaking. Do you need a designer who can also rock at delivery? That's me. I have delivered and led numerous presentations, workshops, seminars, and tutorials on a wide variety of cutting edge instructional design and training-related issues.

Speaking engagements include ASTD TechKnowledge, eLearn! Magazine conference, ALA, and The eLearning Guild's DevLearn, Learning Solutions, and mLearnCon. Full presentation listing can be found at http://www.write-tech.com/about/presentations.html.

Specialties

Recently focused on advising on complicated projects, setting scope and deliverable expectations. Have advised multiple clients on mobile learning strategies, from tools to implementation. Technology used includes Storyline, Lectora, Articulate Suite, Rapid Intake, Captivate, MS Office, WebEx, GoToMeeting, Moodle, SAP r3, Blackboard, Adobe Photoshop and Acrobat, WordPress, Google Analytics, Dreamweaver, Madcap Flare, RoboHelp and social media tools such as Facebook, Twitter, wikis and blogs.
