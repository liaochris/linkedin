VoiceLEADER

2000 – Present (13 years)

VoiceLeader programs provide a remarkable and innovative way to inspire authentic leadership, unified teams and empowered personal presence for communication. There are onsite programs designed specifically to meet the challenges of each organization, individual coaching, and keynotes, during conferences are electrified with an emotional connection to the theme. In every program, singing or speaking is optional. The techniques addresse both vocal mechanics and the tools which bring forth powerful “inner” voices. Performance techniques identify core messages, which give far greater meaning to each word and inspire change. Participants tap into genuine passion and emotion. Personalized coaching empowers individuals to project each newfound voice fearlessly, fluently and persuasively. People cheer each other on as breakthroughs happen again and again. There is a profound sense of bonding, respect, and unifying mission. Hearts open, messages inspire and the experience is transformational.

The Natural Singer

1980 – Present (33 years)

The Natural Singer Workshop fosters deep musical and humanistic breakthroughs by combining basic vocal skills with personalized performance exercises.
The approach empowers non-singers and seasoned professionals alike. We learn to phrase, match notes, improvise and project as we find the powerful, unique inner voice which touches the soul.Within a short time, individuals experience a profound shift in their ability to express themselves. Fears melt and groups rejoice in harmony as we triumph over the issues that keep each of us from finding our true voice.
