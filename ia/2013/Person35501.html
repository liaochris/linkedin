Dieter Fourie's Summary

Hi, I'm Dieter - a web + print + designer working in Wellington, New Zealand. I do freelance web, UI, UX and print design for individuals and companies who need quality work done. I aim to create vibrant and detailed designs while keeping web-standards and usability aspects in mind.

At the moment I'm involved with a project called Beek (formally Viewnz), a virtual tour guide company that is incubating at Creative HQ, a business incubator located in Wellington. In addition, I am working on a variety of projects ranging from websites, branding, identity and print design.

Please visit my portfolio at http://www.digitaltree.co.nz for more information on my work & clients.

Specialties

Web Design (CSS, HTML, JavaScript, CMS), User Interface Design (UI), User Experience (UX), Print Layout and Design, and Brand Development.
