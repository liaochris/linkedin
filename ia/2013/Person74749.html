Kevin Baker's Summary

Enthusiasm and the strong desire to ensure that every client has a fantastic experience is the cornerstone of my business. Building a long term relationship is what it’s all about.

Being a Realtor since 2001, My business has steadily grown to what is now a thriving group of individuals able to fulfill every clients needs. Specializing in residential and residential investment property, I have surrounded myself with a great team of individuals that can handle leasing, commercial, mortgage financing as well as all facets of residential real estate.
With a customized marketing plan, we cover all the bases by providing print, direct mail and an extensive online marketing package that is second to none. Through social networking, blogging, etc I am able to drive traffic to our websites to give our sellers ultimate exposure for their property.

Sending someone you care about to someone you trust” is important when referring a client, friend or family member and you can be assured that I will take care of them as if they were my own family. As a member of the R.E.A.L. Network www.realpartnersreferrals.com I know a great group of Realtors from across Ontario and I would be happy to help with an introduction if you are thinking of moving to a different city.

Being a father of 3 wonderful kids, I am very involved in minor league sports, volunteering and coaching. Giving back with my time is very important. Living in Kitchener for over 20 years, I have come to know the community and the various resources that it has to offer.

If you are looking for a Realtor in the Kitchener/Waterloo and Cambridge area, I know that we can provide the proven results that you are looking for.


You can reach me direct kevinbaker@kwhometeam.ca , or 519-579-4110.

Also follow me on facebook @ http://www.facebook.com/kevinbakerrealestate
