Beth Stevenson's Summary

Beth Stevenson, Marketing Consultant in Local Business Website, Mobile Site and Blog creation, is a manager at LinkedUP Marketing, a website creation and consulting firm in the Orlando, Florida area. Specializing in local marketing for small businesses, individuals, and non-profit organizations, Beth has worked in internet marketing for ten years. We create clean, easy-to-navigate, custom-designed websites and mobile websites that attract your customers, keep them on-page to see what you can do for them, and have a clear call to action for them to contact you now!

Beth is excited about helping individuals and businesses connect with current and potential customers in a way that is immediate, relevant, and cost-effective. Internet and mobile site marketing brings new customers, revived interest, and the potential for increased sales through focused customer contact. Beth and LinkedUP Marketing are here to assist you with marketing so that you can focus on running your business. Learn more at http://LinkedUPMarketing.com.

Specialties: LinkedUP Marketing specializes in custom-designed marketing plans based on the specific needs of Local Businesses. Let us work with you to identify how we can help bring in more customers so that you can get back to running your business.
