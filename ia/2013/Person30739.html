Darrell White's Summary

Successfully planned, launched and in the process of building the first truly patient-centered Eye Care Business, Skyvision Centers. This included the creation of a unique business and marketing model for the integration of multiple different types of practitioners as well as the application of traditional consumer service industry principles and benchmarks to medical/surgical eyecare.

Using the subsidiary Skyvision Business Lab to explore discrete business and care propositions to simultaneously maximize both patient outcomes and client revenue.

Specialties

Superior clinical outcomes in Premium Cataract Surgery, LASIK, and medical treatment of Dry Eye. The development of "plug-in" business modules for ophthalmology practices in the areas of Premium IOL Surgeries, Dry Eye care, and the integration of multiple practitioner types into a single Eye Care Business.

Within Crossfit to translate and promote the unique character and qualities of the Crossfit community.
