David Moss' Summary

I’m a creative director, brand strategist and entrepreneur. I’m a passionate designer and collaborator: I help make brands work. For 15-plus years, I have focused on delivering marketing and brand strategies that combine high design and emerging technology. And I’m thankful for the rich client experiences I have gained through this work.

Most recently, I launched Cleveland Institute of Art’s (CIA) design and tech transfer center, two design firms and a culinary events start-up. I have extensive experience developing digital content and interactive design for brands close to home and across the globe.

Clients rely on me for innovative, engaging customer experiences—and I deliver by applying proven marketing tactics and brand strategies. Imagine what I can do for your brand…
