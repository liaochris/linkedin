Matthew Belanger's Summary

I am an Assistant Professor at Borough of Manhattan Community College, CUNY where I teach in the Media Arts & Technology Department. Previously I was President & Lead Developer at FatBits LLC where I worked as a software / web developer and new media consultant for companies large and small. In 2007 I founded Greylock Arts with my partner Marianne Petit so that we could be more actively involved in community and the arts.
