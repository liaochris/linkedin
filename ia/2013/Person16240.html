Brad Pearson's Summary

In Real Estate since 1992. I began my career as a new agent at 22 years old and focused my career on the basic principle of making my customers satisfaction my primary focus. Listing and selling hundreds of homes through focused prospecting and referrals I built a business that ultimately lead to closing over 50 transactions per year. In 1999 I was offered a position to manage a small office with 12 agents in it. I grew that office with the same philosophy I had when I was selling homes. I made my agents success my focus. This lead to very deliberate growth and within 2 years I grew the office to 180 agents and #1 market share in the city/region. I was promoted to Area Manager over several offices, the. Regional Manager, and ultimately promoted to the VP of Sales over the entire firm in 2008. Our firm out performs almost every competing firm in the markets we operate in in Riverside and Orange County in sales and average sales price.

Specialties: We are always open to meeting with agents with other companies who are looking to take their career to the next level. We are also open to working directly with Brokers/Owners, and Managers to merge their offices and companies with ours in these times of financial turmoil in our industry while providing them a very respectful way to exit while creating a new stream of income without all of the expenses.
