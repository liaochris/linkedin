Betty Johnson's Summary

Betty Johnson is a lifetime resident of Peterborough and brings a wealth of experience in both real estate and the financial industry with 25 years experience in mortgage underwriting including the past 7 years with one of Canada’s largest mortgage brokerages as an Accredited Mortgage Professional and now as a Mortgage Agent with Centum Core Financial Inc. She has been a licensed real estate sales representative since 1988, currently with Re/Max Eastern Realty Inc., and has successfully built up a reputation in our local community of honest, hard working professionalism.
Betty is an active member of many organizations such as Women’s Business Network,
Barter Group, Pro Referral Group and has recently been elected as a Director for our Chamber of Commerce.
