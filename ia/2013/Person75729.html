Kim Fenolio's Summary

I have a very broad skillset and pride myself in the excellence at which I can perform each of these skills. I am self-taught and believe that as I learn and master one skill, the next related and logical step is to push it above and beyond.

As I learned how to build websites in the early 90’s and create fabulous graphics, I realized there needed to be more. I learned how to utilize direct marketing as well as social media to increase the results of those websites and the brands surrounding them. Whether that’s been done through digital media advertising and pay per click marketing, working in strictly offline mediums or combining them all for optimal results, I have learned to find what works best and apply it wherever necessary. Taking those combinations of skills, I utilized statistics and numbers, analyzing the results and have since been able to create full and strategic marketing campaigns.

I have worked with and supervised both on-site and off-site team members to negotiate these marketing efforts. In nearly every position I’ve held, I’ve been considered a jack of all trades managing everything from staff and budgets to graphic design, web development, social media, marketing, advertising and promotions.

The contacts that I’ve made have helped create long standing and powerful relationships. I have created, written and presented on multiple marketing topics within my current and past positions, traveling the country as a presenter. I have quickly become a well-respected and sought after marketing speaker.

I know what it is to communicate and create meaningful relationships that last. I know how to market myself, a business or a brand with a communication strategy that involves the written word, spoken word or graphic appeal. Relationship building, brand building, marketing: it's all the same. We're just learning new and innovative ways to make it more efficient.

This is what I'm capable of.
