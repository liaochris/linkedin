Bob McGrath's Summary

Bob's strengths lie in two areas:

1, Event Management, including corporate meeting planning, corporate exhibit management, association trade show management, special events, road shows, and virtual events such as webinars.

2.Channel Marketing, including partner recruitment, partner marketing program management, MDF and co-op management, contract negotiations, distributor relations, and partner communications.

Bob is a former Certified Meeting Planner (1989-1999), and is detail-driven to deliver cost-effective and creative events. His expertise is in industry relations, vendor management, & contract negotiations.
