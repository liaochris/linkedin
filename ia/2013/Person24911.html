Ian Cillay's Projects

  • Cary Open Data Day 2013

    • January 2013 to February 2013

    Cary Open Data Day (Saturday February 23rd, 2013) was an event that brought together more than seventy (70) open data enthusiasts together from all over the Triangle for a full day hack-a-thon and program of workshops showcasing and deliberating issues surrounding the availability and use of open data.

  • Technology Task Force, Town of Cary, NC

    • August 2012 to December 2012

    4 month public commission to determine how the Town of Cary’s use of technology services can better serve its citizens. Specifically, the task force reviewed, evaluated, and prioritized new and emerging technologies that will facilitate better engagement, citizen outreach, and service delivery by increasing involvement and lowering costs, with the overall goal of making it easier for citizens to communicate with the Town and consume available information.

  • Code for America - Cary, NC

  • Triangle Open Data Day 2014

    • August 2013 to Present

    February 22-23, 2014: Triangle Open Data Day 2014 is a gathering of citizens in the Triangle to write applications, liberate data, create visualizations and publish analyses using open public data to show support for and encourage the adoption open data policies by the world’s local, regional and national governments.

    Triangle Open Data Day will focus on three issues:
    1. Open Data Education
    2. Hacking for Civic Good
    3. Uniting the Triangle through Data
