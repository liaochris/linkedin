Gail Pizetoski, CPA, AMS, CMCA, Broker's Summary

Highly-skilled CPA with extensive experience in many industries.

Controller for a real estate property management company.

Founder of Condo & HOA Smart where we help homebuyers reduce the risk on their investment. We perform the Due Diligence, inspections and analysis of financial statements, governing documents, rules, cash flow, reserves and more. Our Due Diligence report provides homebuyers with the facts before they buy a home in a Condo or Homeowners Association. In addition, I consult, train and provide accounting & tax services within the Condominium, Townhome & Homeowners Association (Community Association) industry. Along with published articles, interviews, seminars, resource websites and more, I have been recognized as an industry expert. Find out more at Condo-Smart.com.

Available to provide part-time CFO, Controller and Accounting services.
