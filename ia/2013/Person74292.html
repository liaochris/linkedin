Ken Montville's Summary

Selling or buying a home is a major life event. It’s a big deal. That’s why you want the help of an experienced Realtor.

What I hear from clients is that they want a real estate professional that:

● Can sell their home for the most money, in the least amount of time, with the least amount of stress
● Is knowledgeable about the real estate market, real estate market trends and effective marketing strategies
● Keeps them “in the loop” about what is happening in the process — communication
● Has a sense of humor

OK, I added that last one.

My service area includes the towns of:

- College Park, MD
- Greenbelt, MD
- Bowie, MD
- Bethesda, MD
- and other towns in both Prince George’s County, MD and Montgomery County, MD

My clients are:

✔ Homeowners moving to accommodate a growing family, job relocation or just plain “moving up”
✔ Seniors and Empty Nesters seeking to downsize to a smaller home or move to a retirement community

★ HOW CAN I HELP YOU? ★

I have been a full-time Realtor since 1999. I do this for a living. So, I make it my business to stay on top of current real estate market trends using data that is both current and accurate.

I understand selling a home is more than putting a sign in the yard and your putting it in the Multiple Listing Service. That’s why, when you work with me you also gain access to:

♦ Resources to help professionally stage your home so it looks like Martha Stewart just came for a visit
♦ Contractors that can help with repairs
♦ Savvy use of the internet and local Realtor network to expand your home’s visibility to the entire home buying universe

I stay in touch with you by phone, e-mail or text to keep you in the loop and utilize resources like DocuSign®, which enables you to sign or initial important documents from anywhere on the planet.

Finally, I bring empathy and a sense of humor. I make it my job to take as much off your plate as possible.
