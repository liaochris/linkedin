Kris Putnam Walkerly's Summary

Philanthropy expert and consultant.

My firm, Putnam Community Investment Consulting, Inc., is a leading, national philanthropy consulting firm with offices in San Francisco and Cleveland. We help foundations to increase their impact through smarter grantmaking.

Our clients have included the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation, Annie E. Casey Foundation, David and Lucile Packard Foundation, Charles and Helen Schwab Foundation, California HealthCare Foundation, The Cleveland Foundation, PolicyLink, The California Endowment, United Way of the Bay Area, Center for Lobbying in the Public Interest, Northern California Grantmakers, Sisters of Charity Foundation of Cleveland, and Hispanics in Philanthropy. You can learn more about our consulting services at http://putnamcic.com

I've been awarded the Ten Under 10 Award from the Council of Smaller Enterprises and named a Top 10 Women Business Owner of Northeast Ohio by the National Association of Women Business Owners. I frequently speak and give interviews about philanthropy, and have been quoted in the Washington Post, Washington Examiner, Entrepreneur, BusinessWeek.com, Seattle Times, The Plain Dealer, Youth Today, and many other outlets.

Additionally I serve as:
- Vice Chair, National Network of Consultants to Grantmakers
- Advisory Board Member, The Foundation Center (Cleveland)
- Board Member, Community Foundation of Lorain County

Follow me on twitter @ http://twitter.com/Philanthropy411

Check out my Philanthropy411 Blog at http://philanthropy411.wordpress.com/

Specialties: Philanthropy, nonprofit, consulting, evaluation, RFP development, environmental scan, research, initiative development, program development, writing, social media, project management
