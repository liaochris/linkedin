Roberta J. Fox, FOX GROUP's Summary

Roberta J. Fox is one of Canada's top technology consultants, analyst, thought-leaders and CIO/CTO level professional as Chief Innovation Officer of FOX GROUP Technology and Expert with UCStrategies.com.

UCStrategies Experts represent the Who’s Who of the UC world, providing key insights and analysis to help you better understand UC technologies, products, and trends. UCStrategies Experts are seasoned professionals and luminaries in their fields, with extensive experience and knowledge in their various areas of expertise.an international alliance of the top unified communications.

Her and her team are experienced technology consultants that advise Canadian business on how to acquire, select, implement and manage next generation technology solutions such as unified communications, telecommunications, network and contact centre technologies.

They are able to explain, educate and inform Canadian business on the benefits and challenges of next generation technology solutions. Roberta and her team also leverages her extensive industry experience and vendor relationships to ensure that the right technologies are selected, deployed and managed to ensure successful business results for the FOX GROUP clients and their vendors.

Roberta regularly appears on or contributes to BNN, ROB, CBC TV, CTV, CBC Radio providing analysis and insight on current and future trends in telecommunications, network and contact centre technologies.

Roberta is a senior Board level member of various telecom and technology industry associations including current Board of Governor to the Telecom Hall of Fame Foundation, member of the Community of Telecommunication Consultants (CTC), supports the Canadian Telecommunication Consultants Association (CTCA), is the former Vice-Chairman of the Canadian Business Telecommunications Alliance (CBTA), former chair of the Telecoms Committee and Information Highway Advisory Task Force of the Information Technology Association of Canada (ITAC).

Specialties

ICT, VoIP & UC Strategic plans, architectures, designs. RFP vendor selection in VoIP, UC, contact centre, emerging and mobile technologies. They also assist clients with senior level RFP development, vendor selection and negotiations and project management of complex, multi-vendor ICT projects.
