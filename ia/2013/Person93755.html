George Drexel's Summary

20+ years in print-making, advertising and sales. Outsourced provider of limited edition prints, posters and cards, working with major artists, galleries and museums. Also produced print content including marketing sales development for major businesses including JCrew, Ralph Lauren, Old Navy, Starbucks, Citibank,The John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, MOMA and The Metropoloitan Museum of Art. With a background in printing and sales management, I launched a new business in 2010 which has as its goal to be a leading new media company for business-focused, community.-based conversations that engage prospects and nurture those relationships.

Specialties: Implementing new online and video marketing strategies, cross-platform branding, business development, marketing and sales.
