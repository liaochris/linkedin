Leslie Nist's Summary

I am a mother of a daughter on the autism spectrum which means I have both an honorary PHD in problem solving and an intuitive ability to understand your needs. Combine that with a passion for helping people, an abundant amount of energy/empathy and a creative entrepreneurial spirit and you have your new best friend! My saving grace and one of my greatest assets is my ability to find the humor in all the "challenges"of life!

Because I built a company from nothing I know what it takes and I truly care about your business and helping you succeed. Nurturing is what I do best and connecting people in sales is what I enjoy almost as much as knowing I helped to make someone's day a little better.

With a degree from Penn State University I started my own company, Mallory's Marvelous Muffins in 1991. What started with a church bake sale one toaster oven and two muffins pans has grown into a freshly baked to order Muffin Gift Basket company serving the entire USA. Such a unique and universally loved gift basket has helped corporations to close major deals, reward employees, open doors with new clients, beg for forgiveness on a forgotten appointment and smooth over a glitch with an account about to go to a competitor. We help build relationships with our personal touch, delicious muffins and dedication to making you stand out. Our commitment to freshness and customer service results in long lasting relationships with all our customers, you will see.

This same passion for sales, marketing and networking led me to join Aspen Sales Group. Aspen Sales Group globally sources food in all food segments:retail, food service and industrial sales,
The Food Industry fascinates me and offers vast opportunities. I have joined Russet House the 3rd largest processor of sweet potatoes as Director of Business Development for Chain Restaurants.

It won't be long before everyone knows someone or is personally effected by Autism. The rate of babies born today with Autism is 1 in 88 nationally and 1 in 50 in NJ. That number is rising every year. Thank you for your help.
