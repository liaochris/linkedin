Gareth Harmer's Summary

A solution designer (and sometimes architect), with a strong focus on delivering new products and services that are heavily customer-centric. I’m passionate about providing the best possible experience, and strongly believe that technology is a key enabler to delighting them at every turn.
At Telefonica UK, I was focused on the Business sector – anything from a sole trader to multinational enterprises. I’d also cover a range of products, from a core mobile/cellular service to broadband, fixed lines and beyond.

Most recently I led the design on paperfree billing, in order to cut the cost of sending paper bills to millions of customers every month and reduce impact on the environment. Prior to that I did the same for a project handling a cross-sector mid-contract price increase – something that had never been achieved before. Both had to meet aggressive delivery timescales with minimal budget.
I’ve also worked as part of a team of designers on Telefonica UK’s Salesforce.com implementation, owning the design for integrating between cloud infrastructure, existing billing and provisioning systems, and external suppliers such as BT Openreach. It laid the foundation for substantial amounts of process automation, making it much easier to accept new multi-play business customers.

My design specialities include formal methods such as UML, Scrum, Agile and Waterfall. I’ve worked with a number of integration styles, including SOA, and networks including pure IP, GSM and UMTS. I also hold an ITIL Foundation level certificate. I also draw on my roots as a programmer (C++ in particular) and technical background when managing cross-functional development and delivery teams, and external suppliers.

When I’m not designing new products for millions of customers, I’m writing professionally about online games inhabited by millions of players. The insight into a high-profile gamer-centric industry helps to inform those customer-centric designs.
