Mike Alpert's Summary

I am a mentor for passionate business owners and executives. I show them how to implement powerful strategies and concepts that can be applied immediately to overcome the obstacles to success that they face in the challenging business environment. By combining my extensive business experience with the proven methodology of Brian Tracy, I can produce outstanding results for my clients. I deliver a flexible one-on-one coaching program that covers every aspect of the business, including leadership and people management. This works because I don't just talk about doing things, I actually implement them! The result is increased profits and cash flow, and a stronger, more accountable team. The benefit to the client is a healthy and profitable business, more money and less stress.

I am a Certified Business Coach and owner of FocalPoint Coaching based in Orange County, California. I have had 17 years of corporate management experience and hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from USC. I help owners of businesses of all sizes and industries reach new levels of success, prosperity and fulfillment.
