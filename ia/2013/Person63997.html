Jim McGuffey, CPP, PSP, PCI's Summary

Jim is a Security Consultant with an international practice focusing on security risk assessments and training. He is a contractor for the Department of State--Anti Terrorism Training Instructor (ATA) teaching military and civilian police agencies overseas how to protect Vital Infrastructure from attack.

Prior to becoming a security consultant, Jim served 3 years in the U.S. Army, 8 years in law enforcement, 22 years with Brink's and 5 years with Loomis holding the positions of Area General Manager and Regional Director responsible for the safety and security of several thousand armed officers and 50 high risk facilities. Jim has been retained by plaintiff and defense firms regarding security losses involving death while in custody, embezzlement, workplace violence and truck crashes. Jim was interviewed in 2012 by HLN and Good Morning America regarding an armored car loss event.

Jim has been a member of ASIS, International since 1981 and is one of 75 security professionals world-wide to hold all three ASIS International Board Certifications (CPP, PSP, CPI) which are recognized by the Department of Homeland Security. Jim provides security assessments for churches and some non-profit organizations at no charge. Check out www.churchsecurityconsultant.com

Certified Protection Professional (CPP®) - This board certification designates individuals who have demonstrated competency in all areas constituting security management.

Physical Security Professional (PSP®) The PSP® designation is a board certification for those whose primary responsibility is to conduct threat surveys, design integrated security systems that include equipment, procedures and people, or install, operate and maintain those systems.

(PCI) Certified Professional Investigator. Holders of the PCI® certification have demonstrated education and/or experience in the fields of case management, evidence collection, and case presentation.
