Kate Ripley Maliawco LMT, CNMT, NCTM's Summary

The Austin Bodyworker is Austin's premier location for clinical and therapeutic massage therapy. We strive to meet each clients needs and show them how quality bodywork will have a postitive effect on their lives.

Our business is growing each and every day. More and more, clients are educating themselves to know what could work for their lives and how to get the most out of each session with us! Our business model varies from the norm in that we offer a quality experience to both our clients and our therapists in atmosphere, fees/salaries and support systems.
Our philosophy is that when you do something you love in an enviroment that respects and supports you--it shows! And our clients notice that passion for our career path.

We are very excited about the opportunities we have here in Austin and we can't wait to see where it takes us!

Specialties

Neuromuscular Massage, Orthopedic Massage and Evaluation, Sports (pre- and post-) Massage, Repetitive Use Injuires, ART
