Adam Atherton, PE's Summary

Medical Device Product Development expert and with a proven track record leading medical device development organizations and successfully delivering new products to and sustaining approved products in the market. Specializes in development and commercialization of complex medical devices and improving team effectiveness. Generates and implements both process and product development strategies.
