Lisa is also highly valued for her expertise in alliances and has come to be known as the secret weapon in the development of game-changing strategic partnerships.

Ask any of Lisa’s references, and you’ll uncover a proven ability to size up an organization, identifying both its deficiencies and strengths, and to turn that analysis into a cost-effective, work plan. The results? Qualified sales leads, increased revenues, improved branding and the kind of company culture that helps firms recruit and maintain the best talent.
