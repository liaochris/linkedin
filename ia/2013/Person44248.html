François Beausoleil's Summary

François Beausoleil is a seasoned web developer and backend systems architect with experience ranging from small intranet applications on Lotus Notes to large-scale distributed systems collecting millions of events every day.

Using RabbitMQ, PostgreSQL, Scala and Ruby, François harvests TV-related interactions on social media for Seevibes, where he is Chief-Technological Officer. Less sexy than NoSQL, but using the right tool for the right job is how he prefers to do it.

Specialties: Rails, Ruby, Clojure, PostgreSQL, Web Development, Database Architect, Redis, Scala, RabbitMQ, AMQP
