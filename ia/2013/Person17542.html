Brian Liberto's Summary

Brian Liberto brings a wealth of experience in both negotiation and business development. He delivers a dynamic, diverse and non-traditional sales and management background to his clients with many years of experience in leadership, sales, marketing, and business development with an emphasis on professionalism and customer satisfaction.

As a real estate professional, Brian draws from his corporate experience to get unparalleled results for his clients. Having spent several years on Wall Street and as a mortgage broker, Brian knows the in’s and out’s of finance. He works tirelessly to ensure that his clients get the best outcome regardless of whether they are buying or selling.

Having a background in technology and executive account sales, Brian is a strong communicator and believes it is crucial to keep his clients up to date as much as possible throughout the entire sales process. His passion for real estate, knowledge of surrounding communities, and history of collaborating with the top professionals in the industry is demonstrated in his timely advice to assist his clients in making educated decisions when it comes to their real estate goals. More than ever, in today’s market, it is important that you understand all your options.

Brian is known for representing some of the coast’s finest properties with an exceptional skill for innovative technology. Today’s technology allows Brian to reach millions of qualified prospective buyers and sellers world-wide, in real time. Nearly 87% of home buyers initiate their home search on the internet, that’s why Brian employs an award winning website to showcase his listings. He also subscribes to online marketing utilizing such venues as Facebook,WordPress Twitter, Zillow, Linkedin, Realtytrac, QR Codes,YouTube, Yahoo! Real Estate, Luxury Portfolio, and Versailles to market his listings locally and to the world.
