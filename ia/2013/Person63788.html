Jim Butz, CAPS, CRI's Summary

Architecturally Speaking - Accessibility Design & Remodel provides solutions for improved safety and accessibility for the disabled and elderly. We also provide fine custom cabinetry and expert carpentery for the residential marketplace.

Jim has spent his professional life ensuring the quality of his client’s built environment. Jim has an extensive background as an architect restoring historic buildings, designing and building residential additions, museum exhibits and quality custom cabinetry. His experience and expertise allows Jim the ability to provide his clients with the design solutions and a level of craftsmanship that solve even the most challenging situations.
