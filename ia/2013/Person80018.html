LeMonto .com's Summary

LeMonto.com – your favorite online address for all things luxury

Who we are:

lemonto.com is one of the leading online stores for luxury. Each season our expert buying team visits the fashion metropolis of the world to select the pieces that suit lemonto.com’s philosophy of elegance and ageless style that’s both modern and right on trend.

lemonto.com now features top international designs and creations, made possible from long-established collaborations with luxury designers & merchandisers.

How we work:

After attending the fashion shows across the globe, our buying team orders the collections’ key pieces and hottest looks directly from the designers. Our selection reflects the lemonto.com philosophy of offering the best edit of pieces that are not only elegant but also modern and right on trend.

What we offer:

At the heart of lemonto.com is a shopping experience built on luxury fashion and superior service.

Our reputation as one of the leading online addresses for luxury fashion has resulted in an amazing offer of fashion’s most sought-after brands as well as key exclusives with top labels. We are the only ones to offer top quality goods.

We also offer styling tips, fashion advice, and worldwide express shipping, guaranteeing a fast and reliable delivery to all destinations. Shopping has never been easier or more exciting, especially with our signature packaging. You will also receive inside scoops into the world of luxury fashion, exclusive invitations, and much more.

Our Team:

Excellent customer care is at the forefront of our ethos. Our staff is dedicated exclusively to your retail experience. Accessible by phone or email or chat, we will answer all your questions. Our expert team of global buyers order ahead of season from the designers to ensure we are one of the first to offer key fashion looks.
