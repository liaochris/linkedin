Kevin Hardy's Summary

I have been working in the entertainment industry all my life. For the past thirteen years I have been in NYC as a freelance Lighting designer, programmer. My career path has afforded me the opportunities to explore all aspects of lighting design for entertainment. I am equally comfortable in television, theater, live concert, Industrial, special event and dance as well as opera. I have many years on the road as well as sitting on a house job. I have a bachelors of fine arts in production design and a masters in lighting design. Maybe this makes me a jack of all trades and master of none. I don't know, but I do know that I have picked up allot of good habits along the way and gotten rid of a few bad. I am always looking for new challenges and adventure, I love to travel. I love lighting...
