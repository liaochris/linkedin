Lucas Grindley's Summary

Lucas Grindley is Editorial Director for Here Media, overseeing online content for The Advocate, Out, SheWired, Out Traveler, Gay.Net plus HIV Plus magazine in print and online. He was a contributing editor to Pride Magazine and online managing editor for National Journal magazine in Washington, D.C. His work has been recognized by Editor & Publisher’s online EPpy Awards in five consecutive years. His team’s work has also been honored by the NAA 'Digital Edge' awards, the national Society of Professional Journalists and the Knight-Batten Awards. He's worked not only in magazines, but also in local newspapers and television at HeraldTribune.com. And, for a short time, he joined a start-up as interactive media director for BostonNOW, a subway newspaper specializing in user-generated content.
