Matt Hackett's Summary

San Diego REALTOR & Vice President at Gold Coast Properties. Experienced REO Agent!

A natural networker and entrepreneur, Matt is an accomplished business leader & business owner based in San Diego, Ca.. With 10+ years experience in the Financial Markets as a strategic partner & division president, he has excelled at leading vertical growth campaigns for recruitment & sales efforts for small and mid-sized companies. His experience ranges from marketing, sales, leadership training, accounting, event planning, business development & general business model development.

At the age of 25, Matt was quickly growing a San Diego based start-up from 14 to over 100 employees in just 20 months of production; effectively providing a solid platform for stability, growth and profitability as the company reached new regional locations.

Currently Matt is Realtor & Vice President for Gold Coast Properties as well as Founder & CEO of ABRA Business Networking Groups. Matt takes great pride in effectively growing both businesses for profitability, while providing tremendous value to all stake holders. Sharing knowledge through educational workshops, connecting local professionals through social events & tapping into markets that have a thirst for a unique way of networking allows Matt to utilize his skill-set to the highest & best use of his time and energy at ABRA. While at Gold Coast Properties, Matt works closely with Investment Groups purchasing and selling distressed properties. His service also caters to traditional real estate buyers and sellers.

Member of NAHREP, AREAA, SDAR, EQUATOR, Res.net CA DRE Lic#01392092. Sold REO Properties for Bank of America, Wells Fargo, ING just to name a few.
