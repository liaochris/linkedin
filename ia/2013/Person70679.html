Julie Catalano's Summary

My work has appeared in local, regional, and national publications. My most recent book is The Women's Pharmacy: An Essential Guide to What Women Should Know About Prescription Drugs (Random House/Dell, 2000).

Editorial credits include AAA World, Adweek, Amtrak, Country Home, Dallas Morning News, Dance Magazine, DeSoto, Highways, Meeting News, Modern Bride, onthetownezine.com, SA CityPages, San Antonio Business Journal, San Antonio Express-News, Scene in SA, Southwest Airlines, Successful Meetings, Symmetry, Urban Home Austin/San Antonio, Vista, more

Corporate clients include Baylor College of Medicine, Redes En Accion, USAA, Secure Horizons, Harcourt Educational Publishing, Meeting Planners International, PCI Educational Publishing, Rush Enterprises, UT Health Science Center, Pape-Dawson Engineers, Omni Hotels, more

Currently Destination Insider and blogger at Cvent.com

Specialties: Travel, food, business, health, arts, profiles, pets

Editing: I'll smooth out the rough edges on your existing copy.

Ghostwriting: Can't write? I'll work with you to deliver a well-organized, readable, and lively manuscript -- with your name on it.

Speechwriting: Can't talk? I'll help you sound smarter, wittier, and more interesting than you already are.
