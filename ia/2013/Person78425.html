Larry Elle, MSW's Summary

Over 15 years helping people identify and find their ideal career. Acknowledged expert in job search networking and the Boston area labor market. WIND Networking facilitator and career author including: "Power Networking: The Path to Job Search Success" and "Community Connections: Resources for Massachusetts Unemployed."

Larry Elle, MSW's Experience

President

Professional Development Collaborative

2004 – Present (9 years) Belmont and Canton, MA

The Professional Development Collaborative, Inc. is a 501 (c) 3 non-profit whose mission is to provide affordable trainings to professionals in transition speeding their return to work.

Director

Success Associates Career Services

January 1998 – Present (16 years) Belmont, MA

Success Associates Career Services provides career counseling services, training and outplacement services to individuals and companies. Larry also facilitates a networking group for professionals in transition based in Canton, MA, called WIND South Networking.

Director, WIND South

WIND Networking

1998 – 2012 (14 years) Canton, MA

WIND Networking is a networking group for professionals in transition begun in 1990. It is New England's oldest ongoing professional networking group and has helped thousands on New England professionals regain employment through networking.

Career Counselor

Resource Partnership
