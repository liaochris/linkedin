Janice Campbell's Summary

In addition to my work with the National Association of Independent Writers and Editors (NAIWE), I speak, write, and consult on entrepreneurship, non-traditional education, marketing, and freelance career-building.

- As a speaker, I focus on enlightening, encouraging, and equipping my audience. I speak on practical business topics as well as non-traditional education topics. I am available for conferences, radio interviews, and teleseminars.

- As a writer, I've written several successful books for my niche market (non-traditional education), as well as instructional manuals, magazine articles and other materials. In addition, I write regularly for NAIWE publications and other venues.

- As an entrepreneurial consultant, I teach writers, entrepreneurs, and introverts how to make money from home by creating multiple streams of income through a microbusiness.

Specialties

Writing, speaking, entrepreneurial consulting, career-building for freelance writers and editors, project visioning, non-traditional education, classic literature, marketing strategies for writers, introverts, and solo entrepreneurs.
