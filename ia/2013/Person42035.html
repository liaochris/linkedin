Eric Deren's Summary

Over twenty years of experience in the broadcast, commercial, and feature production industry, first and foremost as an artist and technician, then as project manager and producer. In addition to my experience with creative/production side of this industry, I also have extensive experience with the business/financial and infrastructure/hardware side of acquisition/shooting and post production & VFX. I am well-suited to complicated and specialty production situations, both on-set and in post.

In addition to this, I have been specializing in stereoscopic 3D production for live action, VFX, and animation since 1999, and S3D conversion since 2007. I have extensive experience with planning, shooting, posting, finishing, and delivering/displaying stereoscopic 3D for many different venues and market sectors. I have several 3D feature films to my credit, including the $70M Disney feature "GNOMEO AND JULIET" (2011), the $230M Sony/Columbia feature "THE AMAZING SPIDERMAN" (2012) and the Disney feature "OZ: THE GREAT AND POWERFUL" (2013).

Although the technical and business side of what I do is very important, the key to doing anything well in this industry is to remember that as an ideal, STORY and CONTENT come first; story and content is why the audience is here, and therefore story and content are why the media-creation industry exists.

At times, I am available for short or long-term projects.

My goal is to bring a fresh perspective to creative problem solving. In the end, I hope never to lose my wonder and enthusiasm for the kind of work I'm lucky enough to engage in.

Specialties

- Managing CG animation & visual effects production teams for commercials, corporate/industrial, broadcast, feature & location-based entertainment.
- Extensive experience producing & directing medical visualization & stereoscopic 3D projects
- Stereo 3D pre-production, production, post, finishing, & delivery, live action & CG
- 2D to 3D conversion
- Timelapse + motion control
- Constructing & supervising medium-sized production pipelines
- Film, HD, & Bayer-pattern production (on set and post)
