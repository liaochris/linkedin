Greg Purnell's Summary

Greg Purnell is a freelance media, projection, and lighting designer based in New York City. He is an artist, technologist, and creator with a passion for live performance design, interactive art, digital media, graphics, and print. He works on large and small scale projects for theatre, dance, music, advertising, installations, and special events.

Greg loves to fuse the worlds of digital media, light, technology, science, design, art, and human interaction. He is very creative, imaginative, and visual while also analytical, logical, and organized with a very strong attention to detail. Greg is a passionate, methodical problem solver, and is a natural collaborator with leadership abilities.

Specialized software proficiencies include the entire Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, and Premiere as well as Autodesk AutoCAD, Nemetchek Vectorworks, and Final Cut Pro. He possesses skills in drafting, rigging, advanced computer networking and communication, carpentry, electricity and electronics, photography, math, and physics, and is also experienced with more specialized industry technologies such as the Green Hippo Hippotizer, PRG Mbox EXtreme, Dataton Watchout, MA Lighting GrandMA, Qlab, and almost all ETC products.

On the side, Greg is an avid photographer, enjoys kayaking, rock climbing, waffles, technology, and traveling to distant worlds.

Greg holds a M.F.A. in Digital Media & Design from the University of Connecticut in addition to a B.F.A. in Design & Technical Theatre.
