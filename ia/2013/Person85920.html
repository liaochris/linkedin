Marie P. Anderson's Summary

Marie P. Anderson, International Model Agent and Scout, modeling industry expert, public speaker, educator and is a former fashion model, with over 30 years of professional experience. She is often described as a “force of nature” and is widely known for using her amazing “eye” to continuously spot new spectacular beauties, as she did when she discovered Cindy Crawford.

Marie is currently the Fashion and Beauty Director for Agency Galatea® Model Management located downtown Chicago. Working alongside her is Katherine Tenerowicz, Director of G&J Models Lifestyle Commercial Print. Both Agency Galatea® and G&J Models are divisions of Grossman & Jack Talent, a SAG/AFTRA/AEA franchised agency.

Publications:
Author of two sold-out, internationally published books:
* Model, The Complete Guide to Becoming a Professional Model
* Model, The Complete Guide for Men & Women

Creator and Producer:
* Audio CD seminar "The Wizard of Editorial: How to Really Become a Successful Model", which can be purchased @ www.mariepanderson.com
