Cody Damon's Summary

Cody’s communication experience spans a two decades and has its roots in television. His first gig was as a high school correspondent for his local ABC affiliate. After pandering to the camera for several years he decided to explore script writing and public relations. With the agency world taking a toll on his hairline he decided it was time to get back to the books; earning his Masters in Global Communication from the American University of Paris. Inspired by his field work in India he returned to the United States to help non-profits take advantage of digital communication tools. Cody’s ideas have spawned actionable change for partners such as (RED), NRDC, Cornell Lab of Ornithology, and Thorn. Cody also serves on the board of directors of Revere Beach Partnership.

Specialties: new media, social networking, social media, cellphone films,
account management, content management, strategic communications, leadership, logistics, marketing consulting, materials management, messaging, online marketing, international development, systems thinking, web 2.0, public speaking, copy writing, speech writing, Google Grant / Adwords management, Facebook ads management, brand strategy creation, creative direction, interactive marketing campaign, contests, concept creation
