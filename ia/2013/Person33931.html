Darrell Werre's Summary

Since 1999, the management of Delphi Systems has been directly involved in the technology support field. We have experience working directly for companies as small as the single person home office, up to a 1200 employee multi-national public company.

Delphi Systems Lethbridge opened its doors in the summer of 2007, with its focus on supplying the same enterprise class IT support to small and medium sized businesses. We realize that owners of small and medium business rely on technology as an integral part of their business, and need to have an experienced, dependable partner to ensure their systems are maintained correctly.

Specialties

systems administration, network design and implementation, vendor management, procurement, project management
