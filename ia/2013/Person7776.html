Angela Ten Clay, PCM's Summary

As a Professional Certified Marketer® with marketing and communications experience in corporate, non-profit and agency environments, I am a Senior Marketing Specialist at TMG Financial Services (TMGFS). At TMGFS, I implement and execute integrated marketing programs and campaigns across all sales channels for our B2C and B2B audiences.

I currently serve as the President-Elect for the Iowa Chapter of the American Marketing Association (AMA Iowa). I am also a proud member of the Greater Des Moines Leadership Institute (GDMLI) Class of 2013 and am a member of the GDMLI Marketing Committee as the Community Leadership Program Class Liaison. These opportunities allow me to pursue my passion for connecting and engaging people within the Des Moines community.

Prior to TMGFS, I was the Director of Marketing & Communications at America’s Family Coaches, a Des Moines non-profit, where I managed, planned and implemented integrated communications, public relations and marketing strategies. I also have agency experience from Trilix, a full-service agency, as a Project Manager and as the Public Relations & Social Media Manager. I started my career in Chicago at Weber Shandwick Worldwide, a global public relations agency, where I coordinated public relations programs and campaign efforts for multiple account teams including the Milk Processors Education Program (got milk?), Unilever: Suave and Degree Women, McCormick, Kraft Foods, National Pork Board and Yum! Brands. I also have previous marketing and communications experience from Encyclopaedia Britannica in Chicago and Interstates Companies in Sioux Center.

I encourage you to connect with me here or follow me on Twitter @AngelaTenClay.

Specialties

Digital Strategy and Content Development, Campaigns and Execution, Media Relations, Event Planning, Client/Account Management, Graphic Design, Competitive Analysis and Research

Highly proficient with Microsoft Office (including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook), Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Quark and Acrobat Reader. Also highly experienced in social media platforms including Facebook, Twitter, blog platforms and Foursquare.
