Kees Wiebering's Summary

I offer intelligent support for men and women who envision change: managers, decision makers and leaders, demanding groups and teams, creative thinkers and change makers.

I am a generalist and lateral thinker, provocative and insightful. When chaos erupts, I offer overview. When directions are unclear, I am a compass. I work in dialogue, in which I am innovative, non-dogmatic, communicative and clear.

Engage me as a sparring partner, as part-time co-thinker, as a mirror or as a communication guide. I offer support through various kinds of working methods and dialogue: workshops, facilitation, individual and group coaching and mediation.
