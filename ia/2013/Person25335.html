Clare Henderson's Summary

As a specialist travel nurse, I help travellers protect their health whilst abroad by providing preventative travel health advice, vaccinations and anti-malarial tablets. Whether you are abroad on a business trip, that long awaiited holiday or climbing a mountain for charity, no-one wants illness to ruin their travel plans and with the correct advice should be very preventable.

It can be difficult to get advice that is specific to your trip and blanket advice might mean you are not fully protected. The advice that is given at The Travel Clinic is always specific to an individual traveller as the risks associated with travel can and do vary.

Specialties

Travel vaccinations, Yellow Fever, Rabies, Hepatitis B, Meningitis ACWY, Japanese Encephalitis, Malarone.
