Erik Kassebaum's Summary

I typically serve in Managerial or Individual Contributor roles and have often performed multiple roles concurrently (project manager, account manager, technical writer, etc.). I am regarded as being adept at both preventing and solving problems.

I believe in lifelong learning and have Master of Arts degrees in both Anthropology and Business and Organizational Security Management.

I seek a position (contract or regular) that will allow me to make use of my skills in management, research and communications. I can be "cleared" for work on government projects. An ideal position would involve a mix of the following:

* MANAGEMENT: project management, technical communications, product management, new product introduction, and account management.

* RESEARCH: qualitative, ethnographic, intelligence fusion, usability, online, library, open source, proprietary, database, trend, and geographic.

* COMMUNICATION: author, speaker, teacher, WWW developer, fluent in Japanese, able to develop "survival" proficiency with other languages and cultures quickly, adept with translation software, and adept with multilingual computing.

* MITIGATION: prevent or mitigate complex problems that involve the nexus of technology, process and organizational communication.
