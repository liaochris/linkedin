Katherine Lu's Summary

I've grown up in the Midwest and will always be a maple tree girl, but I'm ready to live and work in new places (and eat the food).

My experiences revolve around the design of analytical tools for people. I have developed optimization and simulation models (Excel, AMPL, CPLEX, MATLAB), biometric instruments (mechanical parts, circuits, and software), and image processing and data visualization applications (C++, Java, JavaScript, PHP, SQL).
