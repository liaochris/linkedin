David Hollingsworth, MBA, PMP, CISSP's Summary

David is a dynamic IT executive with a demonstrated track record of leveraging technology to deliver new capabilities, measurable service level improvements and cost savings. He has a strong combination of business and technical leadership, with a broad business background, including MBA and BS degrees. David is a Project Management Professional (PMP), Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and is ITIL V3 Foundation certified.

David has led numerous successful programs for SRA, including his role as Deputy Program Manager for the Joint Staff Integrated Networks (JSIN), and Program Manager of the Joint Forces Command (JFCOM) consolidation into the Joint Staff. David also serves as an instructor and exam coach for SRA’s internal PMP Boot camp.

As Chief Engineer, Infrastructure, Applications and Security Solutions, David provided technical leadership to business capture activities from lead identification through proposal.

David was previously the Director, Enterprise Applications for Watson Wyatt Worldwide, a global human resources and financial management consulting firm. David led the conversion of Watson Wyatt’s legacy messaging system to a hosted Microsoft Exchange platform, one of the first in the industry, saving over $250,000 annually.

David has previously held IT leadership roles at Raytheon and Booz-Allen and Hamilton.

David has been quoted in Computerworld magazine and other publications, and has presented at CIO Magazine’s Perspectives executive conference.

David holds a B.S. from Ball State University, and an M.B.A. from Xavier University. He is a member of Delta Sigma Pi professional Business Fraternity, and Sigma Iota Epsilon national management honorary.

Specialties: Strong hands-on knowledge in IT Security, LAN/WAN architecture, application development, voice services, mobile computing, messaging, help desk and call center management. Extensive experience in contingency planning, Cloud/ASP/hosting and outsourcing.
