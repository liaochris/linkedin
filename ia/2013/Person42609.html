Erik-Jan Vens' Summary

I love to wander off the beaten track. I love pioneering. Figuring it out, mapping it. Discovering. In my private life this manifests itself in my love for Iceland. I've lived there, I speak the language, I know many little-known places and I still discover new special people and places.

In recent years I have more and more seen that the complexity of systems or processes has grown over the head of organizations because no prior sound design or architecture was made. In these projects I make what I call infographics. Drawings which show at a glance where precisely the strength or the bottleneck lies. These drawings help organizations to understand their systems or processes, and help them set priorities to get their grip back.
