Laura Kaufman's Summary

Laura Kaufman is the Founder of Partners in Effective Philanthropy. She consults on strategic approaches to "giving" and "getting" funds.

Laura Kaufman has 27 years of experience running foundations and non-profits. Her expertise includes: women's giving, giving circles, social media fund raising and small development offices.

Her clients benefit from her proven track record in grantmaking, integrated fund raising & marketing, and non-profit management and leadership.

She is a frequent speaker and trainer on philanthropy and fund raising.

In order to make a difference on the issues that matter to them, foundations and individual donors need to have a deep understanding of the organizations and environment in which they fund.

In order to thrive in today's competitive marketplace, non profits need to build quality relationships with foundations and individual donors and to develop cutting edge approaches to society's problems.

At Partners in Effective Philanthropy, our staff has a proven track record in both grant making and in non-profit management and fundraising. We help foundations and individuals optimize the impact of their giving and help non-profits enhance their fund raising and leadership development.

For help increasing the success of their "giving" or "getting" both foundations and non-profits turn to Partners in Effective Philanthropy.
