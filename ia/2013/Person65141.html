Jodi McDermott's Summary

Passionate, detail oriented, data-driven executive with expertise in digital analytics, web technology product management and online media.

Global business owner of comScore’s Digital Analytix® SaaS-based analytics platform with direct responsibility of managing product roadmap, strategic initiatives, pricing, cost modeling and requirements process in an agile development environment.

Web Analytics subject matter expert with experience in requirements analysis, solution deployment, analysis and business case modeling. Hands on experience managing an analytics team and analyzing the performance of display advertising, CPC, CPA and RTB campaigns. Strong ability to work across functional teams that are globally distributed.

I thrive in high throughput environments that are team oriented and take pride in building a competitive edge in businesses that compete on analytics.

Specialties:Data Analysis, Big Data, Web Analytics, Widget Analytics, Project Management, Product Management, Requirements Analysis, Financial Analysis, Business Case/Proposal Writing, Capital and Operating Budget preparation and analysis.
