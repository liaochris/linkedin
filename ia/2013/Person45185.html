Tom Jones' Summary

Starting my career "agency-side" within the advertising industry, I created an incredibly wide variety of award-winning, successful, profitable, creative marketing pieces from initial concept and copy through production and placement. Accounts I worked on have run the gamut from fast food to fast cars, medicine to malpractice attorneys and resort hotels to consumer loans of the last resort.

Following some 10 years of award-winning experience at the area's largest advertising agencies I then opened Flying Terrier Creative Company before subsequently going “client-side”—first as a marketing director at the area's largest, non-profit assisted living facility, then with the largest Continuing Care Retirement Community (CCRC) in San Antonio and, most recently, at one of the oldest and most respected CCRCs in South Texas—EdenHill Communities in New Braunfels, Texas.

Along the way, I completed an MBA in Marketing Management even as I honed my skills at relationship marketing, consultative selling and community relations— all while establishing new records in sales and success.

In short, it adds up to solid, skilled marketing experience that can profit any business in any industry.

Specialties

Outstanding creative communication skills in all media, as well as public presentation and networking skills. Think of me as an embedded entrepreneur/ economic generator—a highly motivated self-starter and team-player experienced in management of a marketing staff.

Computer skills I’ve picked up along the way include Microsoft Suite, Adobe Photoshop, Acrobat, Quark XPress, CRM (REPS) and social media software. I can also fashion a phenomenal potassium gum bichromate photographic print.
