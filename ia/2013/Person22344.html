Charlotte Renee' Reid's Summary

Renee' Enterprises specializes in helping you learn more about servicing your clients, the growth of your business, and competing with other businesses in your industry. With direct expertise in market research & travel, Renee' Enterprises prides itself on total client satisfaction.

We are driven to provide high quality services in the qualitative/quantitative research areas of market research as well providing strategic planning in all business or leisure travel and much more.

Renee' Enterprises offer all standard research methods, design, implementation, project management, analysis, presentation of results, consulting, recruitment, focus groups, data entry, auditing, web design, travel, mystery shopping, mailings, resume writing, telephone interviewing, intercepts, & advertising.

We service governmental agencies, commercial industries, financial agencies, the entertainment industry, travel agencies, and public/private agencies, as well as individuals.
