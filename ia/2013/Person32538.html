David S. Bell's Summary

An accomplished generosity consultant, senior administrator, and communicator who demonstrates a passion for cultivating relationships and encouraging the joy of generous giving with individuals who relate to faith-based communities

An executive leader with proven, successful experience in capacity building, fund development, organizational management, asset-based thinking, and staff supervision

An insightful author, nationally-recognized keynote speaker, and visionary seminar leader who connects economic and cultural trends with planned giving theory in the context of Christian financial discipleship

Ordained Elder, The United Methodist Church

Specialties

Executive Leadership, Generosity Development, Faith-Based Speaking, Stewardship Education, Fundraising Design, Organizational Visioning
