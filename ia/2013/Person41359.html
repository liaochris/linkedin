Emma Bridger's Summary

I believe that happy people make workplaces better for everyone: the people that work there, their customers, the business and society. This isn’t just a hunch, but something I’ve proven many times over through the work I do.
I’m an award-winning internal communication and employee engagement specialist. With over 14 years experience, I came to internal communication and engagement via a more unconventional route, previously lecturing in psychology on graduate and post-graduate courses.  Combining this understanding of psychology with practical business experience, and an MBA, I have demonstrated how internal communication and employee engagement can add value: engaged employees helps organisations achieve goals and create a competitive advantage.
I’m comfortable working at all levels within an organisation. My areas of expertise include culture change and major change programmes, employee and brand engagement, development programmes, psychology and organizational behavior, leadership communication and the application of new media and digital technology within organisations.  
I am a member of the “Engage 4 Success” movement, as part of the guru group. I recently led a cross-industry team to deliver content for the strategic narrative section of the taskforce website. I have designed, and now deliver, the CIPDs range of public and in-house employee engagement courses. I am also a regular conference speaker, deliver guest lectures and case studies detailing my work have been published in industry journals. I’m also a qualified, practicing coach.
I have used positive psychology in my work for many years, which has enabled me to achieve some incredible results. I’m passionate about changing the way we approach employee engagement, helping organisations to view it as so much more than just the survey. Positive psychology helps move organisations from transactional engagement to transformational engagement where long-term sustainable change can happen.

Specialties

employee engagement, brand engagement, internal communication, change management and transformation, positive psychology, organizational behavior, organizational development, coaching, learning and development, training

Emma Bridger's Volunteer Experience & Causes

      Engage 4 Success
      April 2010 – present (3 years 4 months)

      Engage for Success is a movement committed to the idea that there is a better way to work; a better way to enable personal growth, organisational growth and ultimately growth for Britain by releasing more of the capability and potential of people at work.

      We want to grow awareness about the power and potential of employee engagement. We want to provoke people to think and to learn more about it. And above all we want individuals and organisations to take action, secure in the proof that it works and passionate about its importance.

      We provide evidence, case studies and points of view about how employee engagement drives performance and productivity to achieve growth, to make the case for action. We support people in the workplace with practical tools and ideas they need to take action, and we hope to inspire people to get involved in our movement by facilitating access to likeminded communities, experts and leaders.
