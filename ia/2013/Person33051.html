Brittni Devereaux's Summary

Brittni Devereaux is Chief of Staff for the Global Retirement business. In this role, she a member of the Retirement Global Leadership Team and is responsible for supporting innovative initiatives to grow the business across all markets.

In addition to her Chief of Staff responsibilities, Brittni serves as co-chair of Mercer's Philadelphia Office Community Cares Chapter – PHL’anthropy – and helps lead volunteer and fundraising initiatives that maximize colleague civic engagement for the office. Brittni is also Co-President at The Spruce Foundation, an all-volunteer nonprofit that is redefining philanthropy and cultivates the next generation of philanthropists through community giving and grant-making in support of Philadelphia’s youth.

Prior to this role, Brittni was a member of the Client Management team in the Philadelphia office. In a relatively short time, Brittni took the lead for several critical programs - managing Salesforce.com implementation for the region, facilitating Growth Team initiatives, and serving as an integral member of the team that supports some of Mercer’s most complex global clients.

Brittni joined Mercer in early 2011, and holds a degree in music performance with highest honors from the University of Michigan and an MBA in marketing and strategic management from The Fox School of Business at Temple University.
