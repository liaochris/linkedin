Michael George's Summary

Michael is a talented creative professional with experience in management, interactive multimedia, and quality assurance. Published, skilled, and over 20 years experience have resulted in opportunities to work with such Government agencies as The Defense Contract Management Agency, The Internal Revenue Service, The Federal Bureau of Investigation, Pennsylvania State Police, and Montgomery County Maryland Department of Police.
