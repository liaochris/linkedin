AfricanProfessionals Network (APNET)'s Summary

The African Professionals Network (APNET) brings together professionals of African origin and individuals interested in Africa to network with one another, foster partnerships, and give back to the community.


Are you interested in supporting or partnering with APNET, please email info@africanprofessionalsnetwork.org. APNET has the following committees: Networking, Business, & Community Engagement (N.B.C) as well as Communication /Public Relations.
