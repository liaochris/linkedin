Jason Lopez's Summary

Jason Lopez is a second generation real estate professional and licensed broker in California. Over the past 20 years he has dedicated himself to helping his customers and team navigate the Southern California real estate market. His experience includes working in management capacities with large independently owned and operated real estate firms affiliated with Century 21, RE/MAX, and LendingTree. While with LendingTree, he oversaw the growth and development of the RealEstate.com brokerage as the VP of Sales and Managing Broker in California. During his time there he grew the brokerage from the ground up to include over 300 REALTORS throughout California.

Jason also has extensive public speaking experience and has presented at Inman Connect, Agent Reboot, as well as national and regional events for RE/MAX, Century 21 and LendingTree. His standard of care has always been to go above and beyond, and Jason's goal is to ensure his team is equipped to provide top levels of service to their customers.

Carrington Real Estate Services is hiring REALTORS throughout the San Diego region. The market is complex and ever-changing. We believe today's agents need leadership and innovation. As an entrepreneur you need to join a company that can provide real business opportunities, mentoring, and 24/7 access to resources that assist you in reaching your full potential year over year. We are that company! If you are a forward thinking entrepreneur looking for a brokerage to provide you with leadership, support, and relevant business systems, we should talk.
