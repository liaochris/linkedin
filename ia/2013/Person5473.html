Amarides Montgomery's Summary

Amarides is a designer / art director and a recent Brooklyn expatriate turned Coloradian. She currently resides in Denver and designs for Deloitte Digital | Ubermind, focusing on mobile interfaces and responsive web. She loves endurance athletics, tiny dogs, and mini hats.

To view complete portfolio, please visit amarides.com.

Specialties: Proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Flash, QuarkXpress, Microsoft Office, Macintosh and PC platforms, Photo Retouching, Drawing, Sketching, Painting, Photography, basic HTML, XML, and CSS.

Senior Art Director

Deloitte Digital | Übermind

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry

July 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) Greater Denver Area

Lead mobile interface art direction for small and large project teams. Designed mobile applications for the iPhone, iPad, and mobile point-of-sale devices. Worked closely with quality assurance, user experience, and development to see projects through to completion, as well as utilized junior designers on projects to help develop their skillsets. Traveled to client sites for pursuit presentations and participated in mobile “appathons” to pitch ideas to clients through rapid prototyping.

Visual Designer

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; IPG; Design industry

Brainstormed creative concepts with the project teams, developed original design concepts, executed designs based on established style guides, participated in visual design presentations to clients, and developed design specs for production.

Interactive Designer

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Online Media industry
