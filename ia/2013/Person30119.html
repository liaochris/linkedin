Dan Littlefield's Experience

Marina Manager

Irwin Marine at Mountain View Yacht Club

September 2012 – Present (1 year 5 months) Gilford, New Hampshire

Dan manages Mountain View Yacht Club and coordinates services for slip owners, renters and valet customers on their boats on New Hampshire's crown jewel for recreation, Lake Winnipesaukee. He is the primary liason between the Mountain View Yacht Club board of directors and the Irwin Marine Management Company.

Mountain View Yacht Club was established in 1986 and is the largest facility on Lake Winnipesaukee with 284 in water slips and 130 valet racks. The boat slips will accomodate boats of various sizes up to 47′. You must own one of the boat slips to be a Member of the Yacht Club. Slips are available for sale or rent through Irwin Corporation’s, Real Estate division. The Club owns a 25,000 lb travel lift as well as a 13,000 lb forklift to handle a variety of boat lengths. It is centrally located and has easy access to many ammenities by water and by land. The property is 22 acres with 4 rack storage buildings, 2 storage buildings, 3 bath houses, a ship’s store and office, a 75′ gas dock with pump out facility, a private launch ramp, 2 beautiful sandy beaches, a playground with 2 swingsets, a volleyball net, horse shoe pits and basketball hoop. Irwin Marine has been managing the Yacht Club since 1992 and has certified technicians on site 7 days a week in season and on site security.
