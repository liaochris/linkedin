Laura L. Brown's Summary

Improving bottom line results corporate-wide, enhancing relationships internally and externally, increasing productivity, maximizing collaboration and escalating employee engagement and satisfaction.

Using a combination of cutting edge neuroscience, clear understanding of gender dynamics, and more than 25 years of successful business experience, I am passionate about guiding women to master the gender dynamics in the workplace that lead to conflict, miscommunication, and misperceptions, inevitably hindering and even crippling women's ability to advance to positions of influence and power in their field.

Consulting with organizations of all types, coaching, and mentoring individuals and groups to understand the differences between how men and women are 'wired' and what impact that has on how we interact at all levels of an organization is the core of my work. Through individual and corporate training, small group intensives and elite executive retreats, the impact of this knowledge reaches every area of the workplace.

Whether you are navigating the executive arena, managing a team, working towards a promotion at any level, building client or vendor relationships, coordinating projects amongst multiple departments, or just starting out in your career - knowing how you are being perceived by the men and women you interact with, and understanding how they think and what drives their actions, will give you the ability to succeed on levels you previously could not imagine.

Specialties: Diversity, gender dynamics, training, coaching, management and executive consulting, business-based neuroscience, corporate growth, productivity, employee engagement, sales and revenue increases, relationship building, women's initiatives.
