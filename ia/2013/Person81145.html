Writer / Editor / Author Consultant

Penchant for Penning

July 1988 – Present (25 years 5 months)

Writer, editor, author consultant, student of the Universe -- not necessarily in that order ...

From antiques to architecture ... radio to off-roading, and women's issues, I have an inherent talent for writing about many subjects in a variety of mediums -- the backbone of Penchant for Penning ...
Where Writing is an Art Form and Editing is a Fine Polish.

PFP (LR) specializes in editing and consulting for authors and book publishers, and provides upbeat, informative articles for regional and national magazines. Throw in a smattering of marketing and PR, and I become the “Jackee of all trades.”

Assisting authors with their passion for prose and to reach their goals, is half my passion – the other half is my own writing. As my business tagline says, "Writing is an art form." I strive to create prose that will endure in literary legacies, if only for my grandchildren to enjoy.
