Linda Paulsrud's Summary

Linda Paulsrud, Founder and Owner of Summit Business Solutions, LLC, helps small and mid-sized businesses in the Kansas City area with all their QuickBooks needs. She combines her strong technical knowledge in QuickBooks with her degree and experience in accounting to provide her clients with the right QuickBooks software product, the right setup, and the right reports and accounting information they need to run their businesses effectively.
Linda serves clients in a variety of industries, including wholesale distribution/warehouse, retail, restaurants, professional services, construction, real estate, property management, landscaping, consulting, legal, computer and software, manufacturing, nonprofit, beauty services, and more.
Linda has earned her Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor designation eight years in a row and is also Certified in QuickBooks Point of Sale. Prior to owning her own business, Linda worked as a Data Architect for Sprint and Business Analyst for the State of Missouri, where she honed her analytical skills, learned multiple software tools, and continuously sought process improvement opportunities. These skills perfectly translate to QuickBooks consulting and give Linda an edge when she is serving her clients.
Linda holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from Lincoln University. She is a member of Lee’s Summit Chamber of Commerce, American Business Women’s Association, and The Sleeter Group.
