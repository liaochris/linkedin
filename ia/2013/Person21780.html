Chad Meeks' Summary

I am a second year MBA candidate at The Paul Merage School of Business at UC Irvine focused on Marketing and Technology.

I am currently seeking a full-time marketing position in the private sector focused on entertainment, direct to consumer products, or software.

I am a strong advocate for data driven decision making. I enjoy exploring data to discover consumer insights and using modern data visualization techniques to present my findings in an interesting and efficient manner.

My undergraduate degree is in Computer Science. For the past nine years I’ve been working in management consulting providing customized business solutions to the Department of Defense. The three years prior to joining Merage I served as the Chief Operating Officer, responsible for the daily operation of the company.

In this role I spent a significant portion of my time working directly with a marketing consultant to develop and implement our strategic marketing plan. This is where I discovered a passion for marketing and made the decision to pursue an MBA in Marketing and Technology.
