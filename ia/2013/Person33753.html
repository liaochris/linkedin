Dee Heffernan's Summary

My name is Dee Heffernan and I'm passionate about working with enlightened companies. Companies that have a strong purpose; Companies that value their employees and promote a culture of empowerment and teamwork; Companies that are unafraid to let their uniqueness shine.

I specialize in helping companies achieve growth and success by improving their brand power. As an integrated marketing consultant, my role is to help enlightened companies identify creative ways to stand out, tell their story, and disrupt the market with their unique value.

My approach is integrated and multidisciplinary - I draw from my core competencies in market research, sales, graphic design, copy writing, and website development to help brands expand their market reach across all touch-points, including: packaging, traditional advertising, trade publications & trade shows, sales materials, digital campaigns, websites, blogs, etc...

You can view some of my recent work samples here:
http://www.behance.net/deeheffernan

A diverse business background allows me to take a creative and dynamic approach to every business challenge. Over the last 8 years, my work has been focused on honing the following competencies:

Brand Identity Design & Development with a specialty in Brand Re-Freshes and Re-Brands
Market Research Studies, spanning all aspects of design, implementation & analysis
Ad Campaigns in TV, Print and Digital
Website Design - Specialty in Front-End architecture and design for traditional sites, blogs, and e-commerce sites
Social Media Planning & Integration - Content Strategy Development, Integration with Website Storefronts & Digital Ad opportunities

My involvement with the company began at a young age, as is typical of most any family business family member.
