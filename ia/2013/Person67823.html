November 2009 – Present (4 years) Orange County, California Area

Brand + PR + Customer = integrateME
Integrated Marketing isn't a tactic, or a service but rather a philosophical change in the way marketing messages are delivered. Marketing as we know it has changed, the consumer drives the market. The old ways of mass marketing aren't working and with new ways to communicate being developed seemingly every day, messaging and branding are becoming increasingly fragmented. integrateME assists organizations gain a competitive advantage in this time of a shifting paradigm in marketing.
integrateME believes in creating scalable models that provide platforms that increase overall marketing campaign lift. We build relationships between marketing tactics to create greater marketing synergy between individual channels (Simply 1+1=3). Delivering brand messaging that resonates instead of a message that increasingly gets fragmented. We help clients develop a strategic plan, and then executes through understanding a consumers purchase path. integrateME knows if we build our strategy around a consumer's purchase path then our messaging becomes more effective and efficient because all communication channels work together and not on a one to one basis. An integrated model helps organizations not just generate leads, but also ensure that those leads become customers, that are continually engaged, retained and become brand evangelists. It's not about using more marketing tactics, it's about how a company uses them that will win in marketing today.
