Mark Kapsky's Summary

Our business provides financing for home ownership and investment opportunities in residential real estate . Our services include mortgage loans through Residential Home Funding Corporation; matching individual private money lenders with borrowers on non owner-occupied real estate investments; and restructuring jumbo loans for upside down homeowners.
