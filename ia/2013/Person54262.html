Michael McKay's Summary

I am a final year Business and Management student at Aston Business School in Birmingham who has recently completed a placement year within the management consulting industry at PA Consulting Group in London. I'm a pro-active and creative individual, passionate about business with an extremely ambitious and entrepreneurial mindset.

From this, I have been fortunate to present to Members of the European Parliament, help Aston Business School secure the world's leading business school accreditation, contribute to a project worth in excess of £1 billion, launch and run a pioneering business society, craft a successful role as a freelancer working with clients worldwide including GlaxoSmithKline amongst others and develop a pioneering student membership scheme with the world's largest organisation of professional marketers, believed to be the first of its kind in the world.

Founder and President

Aston Marketing Society

September 2010 – May 2011 (9 months) Aston Business School

As President I was directly responsible for leading the development and foundations of a now leading business society at Aston University. Today, the society continues to go from strength to strength and works with many of the most respected names in international and national marketing including Saatchi & Saatchi, Grayling and McCann Erickson.

In my role. I was able to develop the society from a concept into a thriving organisation with an approach described by a senior source as "potentially changing the way people get into Marketing". This notably involved securing buy-in at board level from leading stakeholders within the marketing industry and creating a pioneering membership package with the Chartered Institute of Marketing, the world's largest organisation of professional marketers, the first of its kind in the UK.

Student Representative

Aston Business School EQUIS Accreditation panel

November 2010 – April 2011 (6 months)

EQUIS is the leading international accreditation for business schools, acting as an exclusive quality mark awarded to a select group of 131 business schools from over 7,000 worldwide following a comprehensive application process and rigorous assessment of a number of areas.

Invited by Aston Business School’s Course Directors, Business Advisory Board and Executive Dean I acted as a student representative of the undergraduate programme in the accreditation process as part of a small cohort of students selected from across Aston Business School and predominantly from a postgraduate background (MSc, MBA and PhD).

This involved presenting our views and answering questions from a peer review panel of international experts, including Deans from leading global business schools (inc. Copenhagen Business School) and a worldwide director of Unilever, in addition to contributing to a report reflecting students’ insights as part of the accreditation process. This involvement led to Aston Business School successfully being awarded the maximum term for the accreditation.
