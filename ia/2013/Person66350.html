John Foley's Summary

Keynote speaker specializing in High Performance for individuals, teams, and organizations. Keynote presentations use high-energy video footage from the Blue Angels to help teach the Diamond Performance® Framework, a model I developed based on my time with the Blues. Also founder and CEO of CenterPoint Companies, which provides in-depth training on the How of High Performance, and the founder of a giving-forward program that donates more than $100,000 a year to worthy charities.
