Margaret Poplin's Summary

In June of 2012 I decided to change my life for the better. I sold my possessions and drove West from Georgia. I was in a rut; I had never been really alone, completely “on my own,” so I threw two suitcases into my car, took a two-week road trip across the country, and landed in Austin, TX. Life is quirky though, so two months after I arrived in Texas I was offered a job at JSU, which I happily accepted and moved back across the country again to Alabama.

I got into design at The Savannah College of Art and Design. I received my MFA in Visual Communication (specifically Illustration and Design) but I am always continuing my education. Last year I taught myself leather-working and I am currently signed up for a JAVA and HTML5 course this year.

I left Savannah for Atlanta in 2008, with the worst possible economic timing. I spent a lot of my time working any graphic design, fine art, or illustration job I could. This has made me a very adaptable, well-rounded, and multifaceted artist who can conceptualize my client’s ideas into creative assets on both the mac and PC. I also had to be a quick learner; so I quickly adopt changing technology.

I was hired at CARE.org where I delivered PowerPoint presentations that had complex interaction and animation to explain technology deliverables. I worked in advertising. I created websites utilizing WordPress, CSS, and HTML. I made web banners for InkHead.com while running their art department, creating their brand book, developing quarterly catalogs, and delivering client specific promotional product designs that adhere to branding guidelines.

Eventually, an amazing person let me into Higher Education at AIU and, finally, I joined JSU, where I currently instruct. I teach many programs in the Adobe Creative Suite including Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign as well as Fine Arts classes like Drawing or Color Theory.

Give me a call and we’ll talk about your design solutions.

Specialties

My specialties include Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, and Flash; Graphic Design including Vector Art, Branding, and Identity; Illustration as well as traditional mediums such as realistic drawing, sculpting, and rendering from life and imagination. I am also a caring and attentive professor and I excel in education. I can teach all of the above plus color theory, design concept and layout, fundamentals, and more.
