Derrick Kwa's Summary

After quitting school at 16, I've been trying to forge my own path.I believe that the web presents a great opportunity for anyone to make a living doing what they love, and I aim to help others achieve that.

I try my best to be my own person, and not just follow the crowd. I've got a really unique mix of interests, ranging from math to music to poker. I try to look at things around me in a unique, fresh way, and to learn from everything around. I'm good at seeing how various ideas and thoughts fit together, and seeing potential ways for ideas to develop.

In terms of technical skills, I have experience in web development - I specialize in front-end, specifically JQuery, and I have some experience with PHP (CodeIgnitor) and Wordpress development. I've also done some office macro development in both Microsoft Office and OpenOffice. As for non-technical skills, I've been involved in the social media / marketing scene for a few years, and have dabbled in various entrepreneurship projects.

I am currently exploring and just seeing where life takes me, and am open to any opportunties.

Specialties: personal branding, identity, uniqueness, personal development, social media, technology, web development
