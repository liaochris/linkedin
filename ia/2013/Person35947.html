Eric David Johnson's Summary

Eric David Johnson (aka DJ Bunny Ears) is a music supervisor, DJ, director, musician, filmmaker, writer, artist, curator, futurist, photographer & producer. Though I am truly rooted as an independent artist, I have spent over thirteen years in the advertising industry, working first at Wieden + Kennedy then at Young & Rubicam New York, DDB Chicago and now at Search Party Music as the Executive Producer.

I have worked with such clients as Nike, Coke, Target, Budweiser, Reebok, McDonald’s, LG, Honda, MTV, VH1, Nokia, Campbell’s, ESPN, Bacardi, Dell, Gatorade, NHL, Land Rover, Diet Coke, NASCAR, Xerox, Accenture, State Farm, Colgate, Starbucks, Oreo, EA Games, Mars/Wrigley’s, Skittles, Starburst, TED, IDEO, etc.

I have also directed award-winning TV commercials for Nike, as well as music videos for M. Ward, Cursive and Chin Up Chin Up. Currently living in Chicago, I perform/play in the bands, Pulseprogamming and Riddenpaa, cooperate the independent record label, Fryk Beat and DJ in the crew, Silent Knights.

Professionally, I work from a music-centric POV and am a Creative/Production hybrid.

I regularly participate industrywide in panels, juries and conferences such as: SXSW, CMJ, AICPs, CLIOs, London International Awards, etc.
