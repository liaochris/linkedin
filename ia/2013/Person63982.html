Jim Loznicka, CBM's Summary

I am an award winning meteorologist, focusing on "customer driven" forecasts. My main goal, on-air, is to tailor my forecasts around our customers' work day. I do this by delivering my message in an hour-by-hour forecast format. I also aggressively leverage my social platforms to go more in-depth on weather issues that may be of concern to my customers.

My forecasts, live shots, and even my tweets have appeared on various outlets including: NBC News, WeatherPlus, ABC News, FOX News, CNN, and the Weather Channel.

My main areas of interest: Marine Meteorology and Tropical Meteorology
