Chad Holbrook, MCP's Summary

Chad is the Virtual Construction Manager (BIM) for Tonn and Blank Construction leading and managing the implementation and adoption of BIM technologies. He is also an entrepreneur, a part-time BIM consultant and the only Autodesk Revit Architecture 2012 Certified Professional in the South Bend/Mishawaka (Michiana) area.

Chad is a visionary Virtual Construction/Building Information Modeling Manager with problem-solving skills and a thirst for new challenges who has managed the coordination of over two million square feet of space totaling nearly one billion dollars in value. His experience includes leading coordination efforts on healthcare, hospitality, and industrial projects varying in size and scope. Chad is a VC/BIM professional who is confidently aware of the benefits of VC/BIM and enjoys sharing those benefits with others.
