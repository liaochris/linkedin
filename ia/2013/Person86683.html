Mark Richer's Summary

As the IT Lab manager at Naval Postgraduate School's National Capital Region, I am responsible for the forensics lab used by researchers and students at the school's facility in Arlington, VA. I am responsible for all the hardware and software used in the lab (including inventory management) and system administration for Linux, Mac OS X and Windows computers including six Linux servers. I also maintain a 60 TB corpus of data used for forensics research, two MediaWiki sites and a WordPress website.

Previous experience in recent years included IT consulting for Macs, Windows and iOS devices, working with Salesforce.com CRM, CRMFusion DemandTools and PeopleImport, and Microsoft SharePoint and Exchange administration.

For the past fifteen years, my professional experience has focused on Internet development and hosting, strategic planning, content development, user interface design (including information architecture and navigation), implementation, and deployment and system administration of online services. From mid-2007 to mid-2010, I was the Director of Internet Services for a non-profit higher education association in Washington, DC, responsible for the organization's websites, social media, and event registration..
