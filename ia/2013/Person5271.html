Alyson Wilson's Summary

Primarily work to position and develop brand, creative and product side of consumer-facing fashion and e-commerce brands. Specialize in UX/IA for next-gen shopping and fashion sites. Strong start-up background. Art direction for fashion photo shoots in studio and on location. Created and launched two national magazines. Also, founded a small handmade housewares line called Wilson, sold in at The Golden State in Venice and online: wilsonmade.com.
