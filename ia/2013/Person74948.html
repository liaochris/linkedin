Kevin Inouye's Summary

I am an acting, movement, and stage combat instructor, SAG actor and stuntman currently based in Richmond, VA. I hold an MFA in Theatre Pedagogy from Virginia Commonwealth University, Teacher Certification (with separate Theatrical Firearms Instructor endorsement) with the Society of American Fight Directors, and am currently pursuing Teacher Certification with the National Michael Chekhov Association. I have also trained with the International Order of the Sword & Pen at workshops since 1999, been a part of a professional burn stunt group, studied Japanese, Chinese, and European martial arts and fencing, and firearms.


I continue to act and offer fight direction, props rentals, and other services while working as an adjunct and freelance teacher, and I am seeking a full time faculty position teaching acting, directing, movement & stage combat.

My academic services page can be found at www.kevininouye.com
___

I provide fight direction services for live theatre & film, as well as offering prop weapons rentals & related services including stuntwork, stage combat or weapons instruction & consulting, gun wrangling, etc. I'm also very interested in the hoplogical dramaturgy, that is the analysis of scenes in the context of the historical and psychological realities of combat. Helping actors discover new truths through better understanding of violence and combat can bring out great things in their acting, and depending on the production this can be at least as important as the technical aspects of fight choreography. My background in psychology and interest in the psychological and physiological aspects of violence have helped numerous actors and directors discover a depth to their scenes they were not previously aware of.

I'm based in Richmond but do on occasion travel to take gigs elsewhere.

Fight Direction & rentals available at http://www.fightdesigner.com

Specialties

Acting, movement/physical acting, furthering narrative & character arc through dramatic combat, Swordplay, firearms, historical martial arts, combat, stunt burns, falls, props, knife, broadsword, rapier, sword & shield, axe, handgun, rifle, shotgun, spear, staff, armor, police gear & tactics, domestic & sexual violence, psychology of conflict, teaching both youth & adult classes.

Teaching areas of specialty include Acting, Movement, Public Speaking, Drama (Literature), and Stage Combat
