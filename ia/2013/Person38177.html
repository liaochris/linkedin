Dr Neil Kobrin, PhD's Summary

Dr. Neil Kobrin, PhD is a licensed clinical psychologist, LCSW and MFT with decades of experience in human transformation and emotional well-being. Dr. Kobrin is committed to the alleviation of suffering. In accordance to this commitment, as a university/graduate school president and psychology professor, he has trained psychotherapist throughout the world.

Dr. Kobrin's book, Emotional Well-Being: Embracing the Gift of Life, shows us how through mindful awareness and embracing the present moment, we can transform our life. It provides a rich illustration of how we developed emotionally and what we can do as adults to lead a happier, more meaningful life. It brings a modern day perspective to topics that have always seemed to intrigue us, such as the meaning of life, our happiness, and our feelings of isolation and separateness. Presenting ways to cope with life in the wake of stress, anxiety and misperception. It examines issues related to identity, and explores the basic question, “Who are we?”

Psychologist, Author of Emotional Well-Being, Mindfulness Workshop Facilitator, Speaker, Consultant

Neil S. Kobrin PhD

1986 – Present (27 years)

As a psychologist Dr. Kobrin's approach is humanistic, one that blends mindfulness with traditional psychology, which he calls mindful psychology. Dr. Kobrin's intent is to cultivate awareness, promote insight, and nurture self compassion and acceptance. He works with a wide range of emotional difficulties and provide individual, couple and family therapy.

Noted as a dynamic public speaker, Dr. Kobrin engages audiences through story, humor and insight. He presents on an array of topics related to emotional well-being, mindfulness and psychology.

Dr. Kobrin is an international workshop and seminar leader and is an APA Approved Sponsor and BBS Provider for continuing education. He has held the position of President at Argosy University, and several professional psychology graduate schools and has taught a wide variety of doctoral and master’s level psychology courses.
