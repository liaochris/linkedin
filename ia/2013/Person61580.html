Jennifer Crawford, AKBD, WFCP's Summary

What does a kitchen & bath/interior designer do?

As a kitchen & bath designer, I help people create or redesign the kitchen/bath space to fit them. How? Most of my clients consist of a cook and helpers or multiple cooks. I evaluate the space available and then I ask lots of questions as to what you want your kitchen to do and how. Then the appliances, sinks and countertop are placed to work for you. I might suggest a work triangle arrangement or place work spaces that allow everyone to move freely and complete whatever the task is.

As an interior designer, the goal is to make the home comfortable and fit your lifestyle and needs. This means a mix of lighting options, carefully selected furniture, great colors that you like and will work together, window coverings that provide both beauty and function, and so on. Design is all about providing the best layouts and options to work with you throughout your life. This is otherwise known as Universal Design.
