Arpit Dave's Summary

My name is Arpit Davé, and I am a first-year resident at Exempla St. Joseph Hospital in Denver, Colorado. I graduated from Rice University in 2006 with degrees in Economics and Hispanic Studies and subsequently from medical school at UT-Southwestern in Dallas, TX in 2011.

My life and values have been shaped by a wide arc of experiences, from traveling through all seven continents to community organizing and outreach, from scuba diving and trekking to meditation and photography, and so much more.

I consider each a true blessing and am grateful every day for the opportunities I have had.
