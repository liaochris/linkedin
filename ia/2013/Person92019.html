Michael Alos' Summary

I enjoy helping small to mid-market companies market and convert more visitors to customers through the use of improved best practices and strategic implementation of marketing automation technology.

I work with Sales and Marketing executives, in cooperation with IT, to identify the drivers and impediments to revenue, rigorously measure them, and implement best practices and technology solutions to put them on the path to predictable revenue.

I have spent my entire career developing an extensive knowledge base of marketing best practices and designing strategies that rapidly drive revenue. Even though I consider myself a marketer, I have expert knowledge of the last cloud technologies such as Salesforce.com, Eloqua, Marketo, Hubspot, ExactTarget, just to name a few.

My marketing strategies are focused on the three primary categories of the Revenue Cycle:
♦ Acquisition of new customers
♦ Retention of current customer
♦ Maximizing lifetime value of customers

Recognizing the strong need to offer educational content 24/7 year round, I launched www.MichaelAlos.com, where you can find whitepapers, articles & other tools and resources.

In February 2012, my latest book “Simple Small Business Marketing” was published. Take a look inside my book, visit Amazon.com (http://amzn.to/XCHf4g).

When I’m not brainstorming in strategy sessions with clients I’m kicking some butt. I’ve been actively involved with martial arts for over 25 years obtaining black belts in Taekwondo & Kenpo Karate. Lately I’ve been taking it to kids half my age by studying Gracie Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

But it’s not all about toughness & business, I’ve been volunteering with numerous cat/dog shelters & rescues in the Philadelphia area for the last 7 years.

No matter what the question or wherever you’re located, I’ll be happy to help you. Whether you have a question about marketing strategy, automation or just want to talk and introduce yourself, send me an email michael@MichaelAlos.com.
