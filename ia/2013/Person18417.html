Bryan Bishop's Summary

Great teams make great games. For the past ten years I have helped enable teams and projects as an acting US Army Platoon Sergeant, a commercial Field Projects Manager, as Producer and Design Lead on professional and student titles, and as DigiPen Institute of Technology's Student Body President. I am driven by collaborative environments and I want to continue to be a part of great teams and help build real player experiences.

I have used MS Project/Visio/Office, RT, Pivotal Tracker, FogBugz, GLPI and other custom tools, in conjunction with Lean, Scrum, and Waterfall methodologies in the past for orchestrating multidisciplinary teams and concurrent projects.

I have more than four years of experience in video game design theory, history, prototyping, testing and production.

In the past I have used engines like Unity 3, CryENGINE 3, UDK, and many other component-based architectures and editors, along with asset tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, inDesign, SketchUp, 3ds Max, and audio tools and APIs like Ableton Suite 8, FL Studio, Goldwave, Audacity and FMOD.

I work daily with development tools like Visual Studio 2008/2010 and Flash, and software versioning and revision control systems like Perforce and SVN.

I have experience with multiple languages, and have completed at least one project with: C++, C#, Lua, XML, JavaScript, JSON, and Python.
