Ms. California World Intl.

Ms. World International

August 2011 – August 2012 (1 year 1 month) Greater Los Angeles Area California

Ms. California World International 2012
Jennipha-Laurén Nielsen was crowned Ms. California World International August 12, 2011. Ms. Nielsen will compete for the title of Ms. World International slated for August 5 – 10, 2012 in San Juan Puerto Rico.

While completing her Masters Jennipha-Laurén Nielsen was elected California State University, Student Body President. This gave her opportunities to speak with legislators and public officials regarding the importance for funding higher education and providing programs for girls and minority students to explore science and technology.

Inspired by these experiences she began the work necessary to start Lipsticks & Laptops a non-profit organization that would continue her mission to foster an excitement for science and technology while providing mentoring opportunities for middle school girls in her community and throughout the state of California.

Working with Microsoft for 5 years gave her access to great programs like Microsoft’s Digi Girlz program. This was able to provide firsthand experiences; the impact outreach programs can have and inspire young people. Reaching out to Friends, the University Community, Media Professionals and Community Leaders, she has been able to maintain a strong multimedia presence.

Whether on the web, television or catching her latest Radio Show or Video Blog, it is all done with the hope of reaching as many girls as possible with this empowering message.

Jennipha will embark on a 3-month Community Service Tour, her stops are to include: India, Hong Kong & Paris.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Associated Students, Inc. is to promote the establishment of, and provide the means for, effective avenues of student input into the governance of the campus; provide an official voice through which student opinion may be expressed; provide an opportunity where students may gain experience and training in responsible political participation and community leadership; assist in the protection of the rights and interests of individual students; and to stimulate the educational, social, physical and cultural well being of the university community.

Our Vision 2010-11

To increase CSULA spirit and academic pride through service, communication, advocacy and involvement in campus wide activities.
