James Clark's Summary

Since 1991, Clark Security Group has been involved in a broad array of security assignments for corporate, government and institutional clients in the Americas, Europe, Asia and the Mideast. We have completed hundreds of assignments for Corporate 500 and smaller clients; university assignments in the US, the UK and Saudi Arabia; and government assignments in the US, KSA and Asia. Current assignments include university assignments in Connecticut, Ohio and Virginia; urban library assignments in Connecticut and Ohio, a crime study in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and several forensic assignments. Over the past 19 years, the firm has completed over thirty university assignments, more than a dozen major library and museum assignments and fifteen healthcare assignments in addition to hundreds of assessments and security program development strategies.

Ours is a holistic approach that examines all facets of security, prevailing conditions, and industry best practices to provide effective solutions. Our independence from security products and services allows us to work objectively and exclusively in the best interests of our clients without consideration for the sale of products or services.

Our goal is to provide effective practical solutions, utilizing existing resources and industry best practices.

Colleges and universities, libraries and museums as well as the healthcare industry. In addition, Mr. Clark has been retained in a number of high profile security litigation matters in Federal and state courts throughout the US.
