Kordell Norton's Summary

Kordell Norton, CSP - The Revenue Mechanic - works with organizations who want out-of-the-box marketing and business growth ideas and want to move their sales up stream . . . more consultative/trusted advisor.

A Certified Speaking Professional member of the National Speakers Association, he is the Past President of the Ohio Chapter. Author of 5 books, he has been an adjunct faculty member to 7 universities.

As a senior executive at of several multi-billion dollar corporations he has . . .

* Vice President of Call Centers with over 500 agents
* Director of Human Resources
* Sales Management at local, regional and national levels
* Marketing Director with a $31M / year budget
* An operations Executive with a $22M budget
* in charge of Organizational Development
* Introduced “No-name” generic products to the United States in the late 1970’s

Kordells warm and friendly style breaks down barriers, gets leaders to talk and creates a high energy environment. As a certified Graphic Facilitator he often takes teams of executives through strategic planning in hours instead of weeks and months.

Said one of his clients, "Watching Kordell present is like watching popcorn pop . . . without the lid.!"

Specialties: Expert on creating organization Significance, Keynote Speaker! Sales Training for Professionals, Marketing and Branding for Business Growth, Creating awesome Customer Experiences, Developing Leadership that inspires, and get results through high execution practices. Facilitator for Executive Retreats.
