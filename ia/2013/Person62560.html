Jermaine Young's Projects

  • Greater Toledo Urban League 2010 Annual Dinner

    • January 2010 to March 2010
    Team Members: Jermaine Young

    One of the biggest projects of my design career, The Greater Toledo Urban League 14 Annual Dinner project presented exciting challenges and opportunities that required tremendous focus, discipline, and organization. The project panned out to be a full marketing campaign for the two day annual dinner event which was held in Toledo, Ohio and feature nationally recognized actor/comedian Dr. Bill Cosby. For this campaign I was required to design and produce billboards, web properties, flyers, postcards, banners, yard signs, T Shirts, Hats, multimedia video presentations, and a 60 page commemorative advertising booklet. Aside from producing this array of items as a lone designer, each item had to pass approval by Dr. Cosby's agent and publicist. The event was a complete success attracting more than 4000 attendees and generating a large number of new supporters for the organization.
