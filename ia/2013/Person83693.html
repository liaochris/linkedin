Lyne Tumlinson's Summary

As Team/Leadership Coach, I help small business and association leaders transform managers into leaders, work groups into powerful teams. Through innovative experiences connecting individuals with common purpose, I facilitate emerging leaders' identifying core strengths, leading to vision and focus within a team.

A professional trained coach, I founded Lift Team Coaching after witnessing groups that were stuck, not having a vision (often focused on a “weak link” within the group), leaders struggling to move others into organizational change, and emerging leaders wanting to advance their teams and themselves.

In co-creating programs with leaders, I combine my passionate energy with vital content and intuitive coaching to help employees understand diverse personalities while identifying and expanding each others' strengths. After a recent project, an association leader noted: "Our team members treated each other with new openness and positivity, ready to actively take the organization to the next level.”

My inspiration is a vision of a world where people are excited to work together; jumping out of bed thinking, “Good morning, God!” instead of “Oh my god, it’s morning.” Building effective teams that advance toward common goals relates to my passion for making beautiful music directing our church a cappella choir. I guide each singer to find their own voice and connect them to make the best possible sound together.

With my multi-faceted interests and formal knowledge in personality, cultural diversity, and leadership development, I've had the perfect storm of preparation as a Certified Association Executive, on staff 8 yrs’ with the Golf Course Superintendents Association of America. I studied Psychology, Business, and Russian studies and earned a Master’s Degree from the Univ of Kansas, certifications from the Coach Training Institute and Reach Personal Branding and member of International Coach Federation.
