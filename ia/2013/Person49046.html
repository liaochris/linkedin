Glenn Irvine's Summary

Glenn Irvine is the National Practice Manager - Google Enterprise for Dialog Information Technology, the Premier Google Enterprise Business Partner in the ANZ Region. Dialog is Australia's leading privately owned IT Services and Solutions Provider with over 900 consulting staff nationwide. The Google Enterprise Practice works with some of the cutting edge Geospatial/Enterprise Search/Cloud/ Collaborative & Mobile Computing technologies in the Government, Defence and Commercial Markets in Australia. We are the "Safe Hands" when it comes to Google Enterprise implementations.

Glenn has been instrumental in the introduction of these technologies for his clients ranging from Public Sector Agencies to large Enterprise Corporations.

Glenn has led the Dialog Google Enterprise - Geospatial Projects area with major projects built on Google Earth and geospatial frameworks in Australian Defence and Federal Agencies.

He is active in the software industry within Queensland and Australia, and has spoken at IT conferences and produced articles on the adoption of these emerging technologies. He actively participates in the local Industry Associations and Forums.

Glenn was the Secretary for IT Queensland responsible for the media and communications portfolio, and has spoken on the Industry opportunities for ICT Students at Secondary College Futures Advisory Boards, at Griffith University’s ICT Faculty (BIT@Work Program), and a Keynote for the Qld Government's ICT Factor 2007 and Food Factor 2009.

In 2008 Glenn was appointed to the ICT Industry Advisory Board for Griffith University, and the Futures Advisory Board of Sheldon College.

In 2010 Glenn was appointed to the Qld Deputy Premier's ICT Ministerial Advisory Group and was made a Life Member of Software Queensland (Now IT Queensland).

In 2012 Glenn was appointed to the Google Global Partner Council, and in 2013 attended the inaugural Google Premier Partner Summit at Google's Headquarters in Mountainview, California.
