Dimitri William Moore's Summary

Produced short films, music videos, commercials and web videos over the last 10 years. Aside from contracts and permits I have always creatively managed the project from inception to development to building the production and post-production teams to final distribution.

I am building a collaborative film environment that executes all kinds of video media across the country. I can produce from anywhere as my mobility and resolve are my greatest assets.

My primary goals are to find talented crew (writers, directors, actors, etc.), find inspiring material and find more than adequate financial support.

Specialties

Team/crew building, scheduling, creative input, decision making, hustling to get what the project needs, logistical management

Taking Park City

October 2010 – May 2011 (8 months)

“Taking Park City” is a 30-minute documentary that follows the journey of two Columbia College Chicago film alumni, Norman Franklin & Tanya Savard, as they navigate the rough terrain of the entertainment industry via the gateway lenses of the Sundance Film Festival. The expedition uncovers high expectations, high stakes, and high drama as these burgeoning filmmakers make the most of opportunities to get noticed and make a splash with industry elite.

The documentary is guided by alumna Michelle Alegria and Susanna Negovan, produced by Dimitri William Moore and directed by Vince Singleton.

The premiere presentation of “Taking Park City” will be at 9 p.m. April 21 on Chicago’s WTTW-TV (PBS).
