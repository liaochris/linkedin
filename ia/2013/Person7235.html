Andrew Williams' Summary

Andrew S. Williams concentrates his practice on providing counsel to individuals, professionals and small to mid-size businesses, including banks, hospitals and other tax-exempt entities, on a wide variety of legal issues. His focus includes general corporate and personal legal matters involving the formation and management of private businesses and not-for-profit organizations, deferred compensation and employment agreements, succession planning, business acquisitions and related transactions, and commercial and real estate syndications.
Andy speaks on benefit and employment related topics to professional and other groups, and maintains a website, www.BenefitsLawGroupofChicago.com, which publishes employment and benefit updates, analysis and commentary. He has been recognized as one of Illinois' "Leading Lawyers."

Specialties

Andy devotes a substantial portion of his practice to employee benefit matters, including qualified retirement plans, Section 401(k) plans, employee stock ownership plans (ESOP’s), benefit distributions, incentive compensation and stock option arrangements, insured and self-funded medical and other welfare benefit plans (including COBRA coverage matters and ERISA subrogation), employment and employment termination matters, and related litigation including ERISA fiduciary actions.
