Dorothy Banzon's Summary

You have a vision. I’ll help you bring shape to it.

More than 20 years experience designing a broad range of projects for small and medium sized businesses as well as large corporations. Using careful listening and strong marketing skills, I deliver thoughtfully crafted and targeted solutions.

I’m currently working as a partner and creative director at Katalyst Creative Group where I focus on website design and branding services.

I’m also owner at D Banzon Design where I do freelance and contract design work for small businesses and non-profits.

With a focus on strategic design and branding, I prefer to work with developers, marketing strategists, copywriters and/or public relations professionals, from concept to completion. I believe in building strong ongoing relationships and partnering with clients to produce deliverables that are engaging, relevant and purposeful.

As a designer it's always been my quest to use all that I am, my life experiences, my desire to achieve balance and remove visual clutter — to produce work that motivates and inspires. I care deeply about environmental issues and doing work that makes an impact in the community.

As a result of my commitment to make a difference, I've launched a campaign to help us all move from thinking green as a concept and help us to take daily actions. Visit http://www.stopthinkgreen.com for more info and read my blog.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Branding and Identity, Website Design, Brochures, Event Materials, Annual Reports, Book Jackets, Marketing Collateral
