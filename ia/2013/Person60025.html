Jason Parker's Summary

I have always been known for my curious nature. As a child I would often be found taking apart gadgets and electronics just so that I could put them back together again. Because of my curiosity, I diligently work toward the enlightenment of myself and those around me. Maintaining a positive outlook and creative view of the world helps me achieve this. I constantly take the opportunity to explore the world of technology and stay up-to-date on the latest and greatest. I enjoy sharing my ideas and collaborating with other curious minds. In my spare time, I enjoy cooking (and eating other's creations), expressing myself through digital art, playing music, studying language, and cycling. I ventured on my first international travel recently to the Yucatán, where I saw Chichén Itzá, and hope to see the other Wonders of the World in future travels.

I worked in social media for Best Buy since the launch of the very first team focused on social media. I was one of the founding members of @twelpforce, Best Buy’s Twitter help force. Both the ECC Social Media Team and the Twelpforce project were honored by the Chairman’s Innovation Award, Best Buy’s highest accolade. My work influenced the customer experience online and at the store while maintaining a consistent and recognizable brand voice for Best Buy.

I currently am Digital Analyst and Social Strategist at BBDO Proximity Minneapolis, where I get to work with several clients to optimize their online presence and creatively engage their fans.

Specialties: Social media strategy, community management, training, and analysis; website administration; SharePoint administration; copywriting; branding and identity; and Latino marketing
