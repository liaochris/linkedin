Earle Greenberg, MBA's Summary

Founded what is now Chicago Videoworks, inc. in 1979 before most people knew what video was. Integrated and pioneered the talent agency/video audition and event videography businesses with custom equipment, a unique editing style and by pushing the creative envelope with evolving software and technologies. While most seem to use buzz phrases like "think outside the box" our company motto would probably be more like, "there is no box." Our mission is continually evolving but carries the common theme of helping our clients achieve their goals, cost effectively in a creative manner - usually through unique imagery or comedy. Experts in green screen and virtual set execution and live multi-camera webcasting for business, certain events and continuing education for the medical, legal and dental professions.

Specialties: An expert in the integration of strategic marketing, video production and video technology as a means to help our clients more efficiently and effectively convey the desired message to their target market.
