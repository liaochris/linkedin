Fred A. Brackmann's Summary

I am an Executive Coach and Group Chair with Vistage International.

I am your “wingman", the one you turn to when you can’t trust anyone else to listen without judgment, and the voice that drives you to follow through on commitments.

I'm the peer-group leader who each month welcomes you to a room of other CEO’s with hundreds of years of experience to help you solve your toughest problems and assure you that you are not alone. Our members can point to real value, cost savings, revenue growth, and leadership development as the results of their Vistage experience.

• You don’t have to make tough decisions alone.
• You are accountable for the promises you make.
• Growth is hard, but it’s not quite so hard when you have the help of your peers.
• It really is lonely at the top, but Vistage offers an antidote to that loneliness.
• Change is challenging. It gets easier with the support of others who’ve already been there.

Specialties: Leadership guru, CEO Coach, Accountability Partner, Advisory Board Chair, Dutch Uncle.
