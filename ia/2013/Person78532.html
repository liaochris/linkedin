Lars Bech's Summary

After establishing one of Denmarks first online movie- and music magazines in the age of the .com bubble I worked on content production and designing the user experience for interactive television services at the launch of digital television in Denmark around 1998.

Later I again turned my attention to the web, working mainly on designing the web user experience, self service-solutions and content management. As manager at TDC Cable TV my focus was on developing and maintaining web-solutions, web-infrastructures and web development processes to facilitate user centered-design.

At Sauer-Danfoss I first worked on rebuilding the Sauer-Danfoss web presence and infrastructure before expanding into project portfolio management of customer oriented online projects.

Specialties

web, management, project management, usability, information architecture, information design, interaction design, experience design, content management, e-business, e-commerce, webstrategy, web operations management, project portfolio management, (how many specialties can you really have before they are not...specialties anymore?)
