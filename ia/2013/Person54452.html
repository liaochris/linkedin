Ian Ozsvald's Summary

I consult on Natural Language Processing and High Performance Computing problems with Python and C++. I teach High Performance Computing at Expert Level in international conferences. I like taking on jobs that push my skill set each time, then I try to turn that into a talk or tutorial at a conference to share what I've learned with others. Client work must deliver useful value (else why would I be hired again?).

Positions held include Senior Programmer for an international R&D A.I. company and owner of my own NLP/Big Data consultancy.

Topics I've worked on in industry include Natural Language Processing, Evolutionary Optimisation and Parallel Programming (including NVIDIA CUDA), Speech Recognition and Facial Recognition.

To expand my knowledge of building and running businesses I've founded two organisations:
ShowMeDo.com in 2005 - a sort of mini YouTube focused on screencasts that teach - the site is privately owned and grows comfortably
ProCasts.co.uk in 2008 - a professional screencasting company to help companies explain their software products (sold years ago)

I support several of my own open source projects via github and occasionally commit to scikit-learn (machine learning with Python).

Specialties: Artificial Intelligence techniques including planning, evolutionary optimisation, natural language parsing, vision and speech - Programming (typically for prototyping) using Python, C++, C, Matlab on Windows, Linux and Mac - Authoring and distributing a successful eBook - Screencast video production
- International web-based community building
