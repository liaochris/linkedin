Gregg Wooden - MCSE, PMP's Summary

A creative technologist and project manager, with an in depth understanding of technology and business processes, implementing effective solutions to align with business strategic goals.

With a broad understanding of how technology impacts business decisions and technology as a strategic business asset, Gregg brings over twenty years’ experience in Information Technology to his clients. With a focus on meeting business goals through the effective use of technology, he has provided technical solutions and project management to companies across the United States. As an infrastructure architect he has designed and implemented solutions to increase business revenue, reduce costs, improve efficiency, and provide high availability and recovery. Working closely with business leaders he developed strategies for implementing technology to meet business needs.
