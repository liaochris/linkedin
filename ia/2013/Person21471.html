Ioan Said's Summary

A multi-award winning photographer and owner of Celynnen Photography, I understand the need for businesses to set themselves apart, to stand out amongst their competitors.

My priority is to work closely with people, using my marketing and sales experience to help me establish how best to photograph a company that will communicate what that company is about. I aim to create a clear identity through building a strong brand image.

My ability to create an instant rapport with clients stems from listening to what the client is saying, whilst also trying to draw out from them what they would like to achieve. While following the company brief, I encourage my subjects to relax and enjoy the experience, using my sense of humour to put people at ease.

For businesses who wish to enhance their image, the photographs need to have personality and meaning, illustrating not only into what companies do but what they are. My aim is to capture the unique intangibles of an organisation; its culture, ethos and values. My photographs are not merely decorative; they have a designated purpose. They allow clients a real insight into company operations, reflecting the personality of the company itself and those who work there.

One of the few photographers in North Wales to qualify as a British Institute Professional Photographer and Licentiate of the Royal Photographic Society, I have worked with a significant number of major North Wales, Northwest and UK wide businesses and am pleased to have received so many positive testimonials.

For example North Wales Crusaders, The Grosvenor Hotel, Chester Race Company, Wrexham Borough Council affirm that I ‘genuinely care about my projects,’ that I am ‘extremely professional,’ ‘friendly and enthusiastic,’ and ‘a pleasure to work with.’

Many of my images have been selected for publication in magazines and national press.
