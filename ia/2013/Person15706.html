Bonnie O'Brien, PHR, CTS, CPC, CSP's Summary

Human Resource Pro - Subject Matter Expert in recruiting, selection and hiring, government contracting, resume writing, job counseling. Maintaining several industry certifications:
- Professional in Human Resources (PHR) from SHRM
- Certified Temporary Specialist and Certified Personnel Consultant (CTS and CPC) - National Association of Personnel Services (NAPS)
- Certified Staffing Professional - American Staffing Association (ASA)

PHR/SPHR Instructor with the North Jersey Chapter of SHRM since 1993; taught dozens of workshops and training programs on job analysis; full life cycle recruiting (sourcing to hire); quality talent acquisition and creating job descriptions; and provided career counseling to job seekers for both private and public organizations as leader in the industry for over 20 years.

President of Placement Professionals (previously owning 2 Norrell Staffing offices) - has enabled diversification into government contracting via certifications from:
- State of New Jersey WBE (Woman Business Enterprise)
- State of New Jersey SBE (Small Business Enterprise).
- Port Authority of NY & NJ SBE and WBE
- NYC Dept. of Small Business
- WBENC

President of the NJ Staffing Alliance for 3 years (a statewide trade association) and Chair of the Legislative Committee for over 10 years she testified before State government on staffing issues and appeared as an expert witness in legal cases involving staffing and employment.

Graduated with honors - Fairleigh Dickinson University (NJ) - BS - Business (Management & Marketing) and a Paralegal Certification.

Experienced board member having sat on several non-profit organization's boards (March of Dimes, Girls Scouts, etc) received numerous awards and has been recognized by NJBIZ magazine in the "Business Leaders Who’s Who" for the past ten years.

Currently involved with the Bergen County Sheriff's Department Re-entry program, working with inmates to transition back into the community.

Specialties

Full Life Cycle Sourcing, Recruiting, Selection; Job and Labor Market Analysis, Design, Job Seeker Workshops and Job Description development.

Projects: Department of Defense (DoD); Port Authority; former military (engineers, logisticians, armorers); small arms repair and upgrades.

Customer base: diverse international and local companies including: Medical Device, Consumer Electronics, Financial, Government, Military, Insurance, IT.
