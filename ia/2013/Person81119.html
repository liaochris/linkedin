Linda Trignano's Summary

As an expert coach, facilitator, and speaker, I focus on businesses and individuals looking to enhance their management and sales skills to help them to STAND OUT in today's competitive environment.

As a results-oriented business partner, I turn work performance concepts into results that enhance your company's bottom line. Effective in collaborating with senior management to drive improved organizational solutions, I am committed to consistently delivering quality client service. My expertise includes business development, coaching, sale, human resources, strategic sourcing and hiring. I coach managers on improved leadership skills, organizational change, team building and conflict resolution for measurable business results.

As a Leadership & Career Coach, I connect the right talent with the right job. I utilize DiSC assessments to achieve outstanding interpersonal relationships and improved workplace communications.

I am a frequent keynote speaker to business & industry associations on a variety of motivating topics to such organizations as The Project Management Institute, PSG, Financial Executive Networking Group, Women in Technology. I work with in-transition individuals focusing on "ace the interview" skills to quickly landing their next career opportunity utilizing social media as well as traditional methods of job search.

A specialty area within my coaching practice is "handling grief in the workplace" programs. I bring together my human resources background, coaching expertise and grief & loss facilitation to address workplace issues around loss. Through coaching, I provide business managers & staff members the tools and understanding needed to work through difficult transitions such as loss, downsizing, illness (personal or family related) or death (coworker or family).

I am interested in work opportunities with individuals, teams, and companies who are looking to raise their level of performance. Contact me at ltrignano@trignanoconsulting.com
