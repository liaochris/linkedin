Sarah Djamshidi's Summary

I’m an entrepreneur at heart, fueled by the excitement of finding emerging companies with great potential, helping them identify and tackle challenges, and connecting them to the right opportunities and people in order to make something amazing happen.

I’ve coached high-profile, technology-based startups and early-stage companies in advanced technology areas, as well as led public-private-partnership organizations. I have experience in the government sector as well as the private sector, with companies at all stages of growth from pre-funding to venture-capital-seeking to already established with millions in revenue and ready for more.

My colleagues most often comment on my energy and enthusiasm, my professionalism and integrity, and my unique ability to think strategically and tactically at the same time. I’m known for my ability to build relationships, brands, and revenue.
