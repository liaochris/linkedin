Manuel Boden's Summary

Senior executive with an equal blend of technical, business, and managerial skills developed during experience with several Fortune 350 companies. Have held the position of senior vice president of IT—as well as divisional CIO—for several multi-billion dollar corporations.

Now, I work with senior executives in corporations to further their strategic, business, and professional goals. I provide extensive experience in helping them to manage multimillion-dollar budgets and large professional staffs. In addition, I possess the unique ability to assist them in attracting, retaining, and motivating outstanding management and professional personnel. I provide these consulting services to Chief Information Officers (CIO's) and senior Executives in Fortune 350 companies. I also provide leadership coaching to these executives. I frequently speak and write on the topics of how to be successful in the CIO and other executive positions; how to create a successful IT organization; and how to be an effective leader.
