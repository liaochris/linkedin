Dorothy Liu's Summary

Broker Associate focused on understanding her clients needs and working endlessly to help them make sure they are completely satisfied. Dorothy serves buyers and sellers in Los Altos, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Cupertino, Palo Alto and San Jose submarkets and neighborhoods.

She has lived in Los Altos for over 23 years and previously in many of the South Bay cities. She has detailed knowledge of neighborhoods and schools - many via her extensive community service.

Outstanding service is Dorothy's top priority in real estate as proven with her Platinum Awards for Quality Service Certification. Prior to becoming a Realtor at Alain Pinel Realtors, Dorothy was in marketing and competitive intelligence. Her skills in analysis, negotiation, communication strategies, facilitation have helped her provide buyers and sellers a big advantage.

The purchase of a home is a significant financial and emotional decision. Dorothy is committed to providing clients with the best information possible in buying and selling real estate. The ability to provide tech-savvy real estate tactics and offer creative sales strategies, based on rich marketing and analysis skills learned from a Fortune 500 company is the primary advantage Dorothy brings to the business of real estate.

Specialties: Dorothy has excellent listening skills as well as marketing and technical skills. She has successfully managed residential property for 20+ years. Registered Senior Real Estate Specialist, Quality Service Certified, and a licensed Broker Associate, a distinction held by less than 15% of realtors. Familiar with Feng Shui & Green Homes.

For Rating Scores for TRUE Real Estate Professionals: http://www.qualityservice.org - search for Dorothy to see her Platinum service rating to her clients.
