Craig Collins' Summary

Since first creating my social media management company, Social Media Edge, in 2008, I have managed dozens of social media campaigns for clients, coached hundreds of business owners, developed a video training course that generated hundreds of thousands in sales, spoke on multiple national stages, and developed a custom fan page application that sold all across the internet.

In May of 2011 my company was purchased by Maximize Social Media, where I’m currently the Co-Founder and Senior VP. I oversee our international sales team and manage our reseller/partner accounts. As one of the nation’s leading social media management companies, Maximize Social Media’s growth has been exponential, and I’m constantly on the lookout for qualified Sales Reps and Resellers to continue our rapid expansion.

Maximize Social Media provides complete ‘done for you’ social media management solutions. We specialize in generating laser targeted leads via Facebook ads using our Enterprise Class applications, and creating engagement strategies that promote viral growth, while building long-term client retention.
