Georgia Feiste's Summary

I am a personal growth and leadership coach, artist, writer, and workshop facilitator. My specialty is helping women leaders, executives and creatives consciously exercise their heart-mind as they intentionally build a life and career based on values and priorities. Coaching is not for the faint of heart. It calls on you to actively redefine who you are willing to be and take the steps necessary to create and respond to change.

Leaders who are clear about who they are experience abundance by focusing their strengths and passions in every area of their life. It is a matter of knowing who you are, and being willing to be in integrity with yourself and the people you interact with.

As we transition through life stages, we experience different aspects of who we are in a variety of areas - our roles, health, the time we spend in differing areas, clarity around finances, relationships, creativity, personal and spiritual growth, and what we do for fun.

My role as coach is to hold open the space for my clients so they might experiment, grow and learn with encouragement and support as they go through the process of embracing what they begin to discover about who they are willing to be. All the while, helping them develop their vision, set their goals, and accomplish each sweet step with ease and grace by holding them accountable with compassion and respect..

What I bring to the table:

* An ability to see the bigger picture, the potential and the possibilities.

* A strong sense of connectedness. I know that we all have a piece of the wisdom, we just don't have all of it.

* Strengths fascinate me, and I enjoy fitting all of the pieces of the puzzle together.

* Creative, whole-brain thinking

* Trained by Coach, Inc., Career Coach Institute, and the Academy for Coaching Excellence as a professional coach. Member of the International Coach Federation.
