Gabi Moore's Summary

Hi, I'm Gabi. I'm a Product Designer with 15 years experience designing, prototyping, and building digital products. I was born and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, spent some time in England and Australia, and eventuallly moved to Brooklyn NY where I live today. When I'm not working, I'm either travelling or playing with my Lego collection.

I collaborated with AOL Mail and Phoenix, Registration and Sign In, MapQuest, Mobile, and more. I also evangelized UX across the company, organizing internal and external events, and helping identify and fix broken experiences on AOL products.
