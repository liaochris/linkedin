Anne Bodiford's Summary

I am currently finishing my degree at the University of Central Florida. I am one of 35 students selected to be a part of the Professional Selling Program. One of only approximately 30 specialized sales undergraduate programs in the nation, the UCF Professional Selling Program is at the forefront in preparing students for successful careers in the competitive job market of today. Additionally, students are paired with a professional mentor both semesters of the program.
