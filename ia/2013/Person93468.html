Michelle Hastie's Summary

After struggling with her own weight, Michelle, owner of Total Body Health Solutions seeks to be the certified coach that inspires you to live a healthy slim life. She spent time, money and energy obsessing over her weight gain and found no answers to remove it quickly. After a tragic car accident, Michelle lost weight sitting on the couch, unable to move. She became inspired to learn why, and also removed all the rest of her weight while enjoying all of the foods she loves, and only performing the movements she is excited about. She now takes a total body approach to help remove your weight focusing on your mental, emotional, physical and spiritual health.

Specialties

Weight Loss without calorie counting and extreme exercise
