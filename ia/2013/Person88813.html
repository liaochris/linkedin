Matthew Brock, CFP®'s Summary

Matt Brock began his career in the financial services industry in 2003 as a Financial Advisor with Ameriprise Financial. He soon earned the Advanced Financial Advisor title. Matt was part of Ameriprise’s “Teaming” pilot program which encouraged advisors to work with other advisors in order to provide clients with the best level of service. In 2010 Matt, along with his business partner left Ameriprise to start their own independent financial planning firm. With Divergent Planning, Matt oversees operations, business development and directs financial planning systems.

Matt was born and raised in Southern Virginia and attended Hampden Sydney College where he earned a degree in Economics and Mathematics. He now resides in Maryland with his wife Natalie, their daughter Mila and chihuahua Peanut. He holds various licenses and designations including the Certified Financial Planning Board of Standards’ CFP® certification making him a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ practitioner.

Matt is an avid golfer and CrossFitter and loves trying new sports. He loves to read which would make most of his college friends laugh since, ironically, he only began to read with regularity after graduation. Matt enjoys spending free time with his wife and family in the Outer Banks of North Carolina (where he was also married). During the Fall he spends alot of time watching football. Luckily, his wife and daughter love football also. Matt is a lifelong Washington Redskins fan.

Matt is a past President of the Rotary Club of Potomac, past co-chair of the MAM Charity Golf Classic, and a member of Financial Planning Association. He also co-founded "Matt vs Ara", a fundraising program with monthly athletic events.

Matt is registered to sell securities with US Residents only in the states of AL, CA, DC, DE, GA, LA, MD, MT, NY, TX & VA.

Securities and Investment Advisory Services offered through H. Beck Inc., Member FINRA, SIPC. H. Beck Inc. and Divergent Planning are not affiliated.

Specialties
