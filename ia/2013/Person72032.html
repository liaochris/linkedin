Karen Blakeman's Summary

I provide training and consultancy on how to use the web and social media as research tools, how to identify and assess the quality of resources, and speak at seminars and conferences. I am also available as a freelance researcher and contractor.

Past and present clients include public and corporate libraries, information providers, investment and financial services companies, organisations in the food and beverage industry, pharmaceutical companies, trade and research associations, UK government departments.

My blog is at http://www.rba.co.uk/wordpress/ and I edit and publish a newsletter at http://www.rba.co.uk/tfttr/

I am an Honorary Fellow of CILIP: The Chartered Institute of Library and Information Professionals.
In 2002 I received the Information World Review Information Professional of the Year award.

Specialties: search strategies, competitive intelligence, business and financial information, life sciences, energy resources, technical writing, desktop research, social media, collaborative tools.
