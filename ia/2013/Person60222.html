James Dodd's Summary

James Dodd is a Sheffield based photographer and founder member of Statement Images.

In 2010, James was featured as a finalist for Magnum/Burn Magazine's Emerging Photographer Fund for his Olympic Dreams work and in 2011 was an Honorable mention for Magenta Flash Forward and a Jurors pick for Daylight/CDS Awards.

James is available for commissions and freelancing work worldwide.

Statement Images (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Photography industry

April 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months)

Statement Images is a photographic collective with a curiosity for the world which they wish to explore through imagery, whether still or moving. The primary objective of the collective is to offer a support framework for it’s members and to encourage collaborations and knowledge sharing.

The collective started in late 2008 as members became disillusioned at the quality, style, impact and the direction photography in the mainstream press was heading and formally launched in April 2009.

The statementimages.co.uk website will act as an outlet for works of members, and selected guest features.
