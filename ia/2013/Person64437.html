Jeannette Kraar's Summary

Jeannette Kraar, CEO of Performance Management International LLC, is a successful partner to clients, helping them clarify objectives and develop realistic plans for achieving their purpose within industries spanning healthcare,professional and financial services, technology, retailing and consulting within corporations, non-profits and government agencies.

Able to expand options and reduce risks through a comprehensive research-based process that identifies targets, trends, opportunities, resources, threats and contingencies within markets, organizations, professional networks and industry segments.

Jeannette and her organization have proven expertise in strategic design and implementation, succession planning, capacity building, service excellence, training and development and executive coaching.

Specialties

Kraar specializes in a goal achievement process that closes the gap between current and desired performance with strong emphasis on strategic planning, capacity building, service excellence, performance improvement and skill development.

Jeannette works strategically with clients to define and achieve outcomes that impact core areas that include: financial, leadership, quality, customer perspective and/or the ability to innovate and grow.
