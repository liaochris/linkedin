Lesley Diaz's Summary

What I do: I make genuine and enduring collaboration possible in groups and enterprises by building the skills and systems for courageous conversation and collective decision-making.

How I do: Truly collaborative work requires a genuine commitment to the ideal of collaboration; relationships based on something beyond self-interest; skilled people who can share their ideas courageously; and systems that support the collaborative ideal. I work with individuals and groups on all of these levels to achieve new habits of collaboration.

Specialties:

Courageous Conversation
Collaborative Leadership
Decision-making as a social process
A systems approach to conflict and change
Making good ideas work in practical settings
