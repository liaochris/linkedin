Katie-Ellen Hazeldine's Summary

Hello, thank you for the profile visit.

I read Tarot, operating as a consultant and advisor. I am not a fortune-teller, though my work includes forecasting. I deploy divinatory skill, sourcing insights for strategy & support, to help with personal, professional & business questions & decision-making. I may also dowse for information using a pendulum as a divinatory tool.

Why use such services? Why do people watch weather forecasts, or study the Stock Exchange?

-For a new idea or perspective
-For validation of information & understanding
-For a sense of the odds in future developments
-For positioning
-To save time, money & energy in avoiding false steps

I work via email, telephone & Audio Skype, handling any question that is legal/ethical.

I write esoteric web and app content, with short fiction and verse published in online and hard copy publications eg Literary Review & Litro magazines.

Intuitive ability is grounded in divinatory skill and academic discipline.
I hold an Arts Hons degree, Upper Second, a Post Graduate Diploma In Bus Admin/IT and hold a Cert Ed as a qualified teacher of adults within Further Education.
