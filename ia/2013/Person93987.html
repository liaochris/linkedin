Mike Newton's Summary

Mike Newton is a San Diego-based commercial photographer with a soft spot for tech startups and the local craft beer and spirits industry. Having spent +10 years in high level business development he has an intimate appreciation for how a companies image is presented can influence investors, employees, and clients.

Commercial client list includes Uber.com, Miller-Coors, Sanuk Footwear, Rouse Properties (NYSE: RSE), Takelessons.com, DealCurrent.com, Digital Telepathy, Salon Elle, Vinely.com, Quint Contemporary Art, IdeaWorks, EXP, author Ellen Dolgen, Lokk Legal, and more.

Mike also has passion for photographing food. What else sells a restaurant or cocktail program better than hunger-inducing images of its product? Clients include RX Bitters, Papa Lunas Empanadas, Fernet-Branca, and more.

Latest personal projects:

Wrote my first educational ebook, Hacking Photography, to teach new photographers how to leverage the 80/20 rule to improve their photography fast. Book launches in November on www.hackingphotography.com

Started an indie app development company in San Diego that has launched 5 photography apps on IOS devices in the past 12 months.
