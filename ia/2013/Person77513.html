Kurt Hines' Summary

Over the course of my life, I've always sought out interesting challenges and opportunities. I love jumping into projects headfirst, seeking out & fixing inefficiencies and making the lives of those I work with, and the customers we ultimately serve, better than before.

Two themes have always intertwined themselves in the various positions I have filled. That of a fixer, and of a translator. I've often jumped into projects that need to get back on track, and help to put them so. That almost always requires acting as a translator between business and technology. I've lived in both worlds separately, and lived even more in both worlds together. I excel at bridging the gap between them, to help everyone involved better understand the wider picture.

I'm looking for another such challenge now. I'm interested in anything that will challenge me, help me grow, and put my skills to best use.
