Marilyn Hanes' Summary

For 25 years Marilyn Hanes has assisted friends, clients and their referrals to buy and sell homes in San Diego County. Marilyn is a dedicated and market-savvy professional who provides her clients with superior service, responsive communication and experience that allow them to make an informed purchasing or sales decision. Her attention to detail, negotiation skills, superior people skills, broad knowledge of the real estate industry and determination to indulge her clients with first class service sets her apart from other Real Estate Agents.

Marilyn understands the power of creative and well-organized marketing. “As a listing agent, I offer my clients customized marketing plans which includes a state-of-the-art Internet based marketing approach to maximize property exposure to the most qualified buyers to get their properties sold.”

Marilyn’s fair yet firm negotiating style and her commitment to excellence has gained her respect with colleagues and clients alike. Whether it’s patiently guiding first time buyers throughout this exciting process, or following through with clients’ needs long after the transaction is over, Marilyn’s warm, caring yet efficient business style will turn you, too, into a client for life.

Because Marilyn grew up in Southern California and has been a resident of San Diego since high school, she has expert knowledge of San Diego communities. She has seen San Diego grow and flourish and her neighborhood knowledge means she is equipped to match clients to a specific area that will suit them for the long term. For the last 26 years, she has lived in neighborhoods within the Poway Unified School District, where her son attended school.
