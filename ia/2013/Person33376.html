Debbie Johnstone's Summary

I have been practicing as a full time animal communicator/pet psychic since early 2003. I work with all species of animals including donkeys, turtles, buffaloes, cows, rabbits, guinea pigs, owls, hawks, ravens and many others. However, most of my clients are horses, dogs, cats and birds. I love to assist by opening the lines of communication between a person and their animal for any purpose. My goal is to always be a clear channel so that I can deliver the intent of an animal’s message in as pure a form as possible. A major focus area for me is assisting animals and their people through healing crises, euthanasia and reconnecting after the animal crosses over into spirit. It is also most gratifying when I can help people know that their animal friends’ will most often communicate or connect to them once they have transitioned back into spirit and even return to them in another physical body.

I also love educating people on how their animal companions want to help them through any type of life situation and can take on their energy and emotions. I also do hands-on and distance energy healing work.

I continue to volunteer my time and assist the dogs, cats and horses at several local animal rescue organizations. This work is so important and also gratifying. Many times it can assist an animal to heal and relax more quickly and become ready for their new forever home when they have been adopted.

Specialties

Health questions or concerns, puzzling or challenging behavior, questions about their desires for comfort and contentment, relationship or behavior issues between multiple animals, wishes in regards to dying (euthanasia) & communication after death (each animal is unique in what they request).
