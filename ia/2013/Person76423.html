Sal Saporito's Summary

You're about to enter the world of decorative hardware, the finishing touches for all your living spaces. Our selection of cabinet knobs, hinges and drawer pulls can radically change the overall look of a room at a fraction of the cost for a complete renovation. From rustic hardware, classic brass and copper, to contemporary styles in bright colors and shapes, Knobs, Hinges and More is sure to please. Our unique collection also includes doorknockers, switch plates, appliance hardware, bathroom accessories, faucets, mailboxes...everything you could need to put the final touch to your elegant room.

Specialties

Kitchen Cabinet Knobs, Drawer Pulls, Cabinet Hardware, Decorative Hardware, Decorating
