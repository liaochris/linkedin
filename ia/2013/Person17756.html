Brian Spiegel CR CIEC CMRS CEICC CSDS's Summary

Co-owner of Spiegel Property Damage Consulting and Forensics, with his brother David Spiegel, is a family-owned business. Spiegel Certified Restoration was established in 1950 and specialized in property damage restoration in the Southern California area, including fire, smoke mold and water damage restoration.

After 45 years experience in the cleaning and property damage and construction industries, Spiegel now offers extensive consulting and forensic services to law firms, insurance companies, residential and commercial property owners, contractors, realtors and property management companies.

Our services include Inspections, Expert Witness Services, Consulting, Insurance Claims Specialists, Property Damage Assessors & Appraisers, Third Party Evaluation, Cause & Origin Investigations and Technical Services. Providing expert testimony and consulting services on issues related to property damage restoration. We also have extensive experience consulting on wildfire property damage claims.

Speaker nationally and internationally on topics related to property damage restoration and mold contamination. Past speaking engagements: Property Loss Research Bureau (PLRB) national mold symposium and Forensic Consultants Association on toxic mold litigation and mold remediation.

Appeared on several radio and television interviews, including appearance on ABC TV's Good Morning America.
