John Glowacz's Summary

John Glowacz is a Partner and Independent Financial Advisor with Cinco Ranch Insurance Services. He is recognized as one of the State’s leading experts on Safe Investment Alternatives for over 22 years.

John’s credentials include a Bachelor’s degree in Business from Lamar University, a Series 6 and 63 securities license, a Registered Investment Advisor’s License (RIA), and a Property & Casualty General Lines License. Bringing exceptional market knowledge, passion and integrity.

Prior to Cinco Ranch Insurance, John was a successful Insurance Broker and the last 11 years he has been a top 10% performer as an Allstate Agency Owner with over 3500 customers.

Despite his demanding career, John makes time to be involved with both professional and community organizations doing community service.

-He is a member of the Health and Life Underwriters Association, the Independent Insurance Agents Association and the Trusted Choice Agents Association.

-John has helped 100’s of female athletes obtain College Scholarships, using girls fastpitch softball as the vehicle in obtaining a college education.

-He has been an ASA Junior Olympic Coach and Consultant for the last 19 years.

-He also mentors At Risk kids who end up in the juvenile boot camp system, many of these kids next step is prison. These kids need someone who will not only listen, but will act as a role model while building their self esteem.

-John does 200 speeches per year for MADD, sharing his heartfelt personal story and his practical life experiences with hopes of others.

Born in Corpus Christi, Texas most of John’s youth was spent growing up in Waco, Texas where he also graduated High School. John married his wife Beth in August of 1982 and they have lived in the Katy area for the last 29 years. Raising 3 great kids and now have four wonderful grandchildren. They both believe in Church, community and serving others in need and spending quality time with family and friends.
