Dr. L. Philipp Wall's Summary

For patients with vein and vascular problems in Phoenix, help is at hand. Award-winning vascular physician Dr Philipp Wall and his team at Arizona Veins & Vascular Center are standing by to offer comprehensive and high quality care to their patients. The team is passionate about providing patients with the best quality treatment and service in a modern and comfortable environment.

Dr. Philipp Wall knew from a young age that training as a Doctor and helping others was his dream. His early intuition turned out to be spot on. Dr Philipp Wall is one of the top vascular surgeons today, winner of the Mario Collura Award for excellence in surgery, and multiple winner of the Patient's Choice award. Dr Wall is passionate about treating and managing vein and vascular problems, and has even appeared on Channel 3's Your Life A to Z with Dr. Oz to talk about his work.

From education and information to a wide range of advanced cutting edge treatments, patients will find a great deal of support and patient-centered care at Arizona Vein & Vascular Center.

Dr. Wall and his team aim to provide a full support and treatment service to help patients feel better and get back to enjoying life as quickly as possible. The portfolio of treatments available includes comprehensive care of veins and arteries, varicose vein treatments, endovascular surgery, ultrasound, treatment of artery disease, treatment for abdominal aortic aneurysm and more. The staff work closely with each patient's other physicians to ensure they receive the best treatment package for them.

Everything from the initial appointment to the care packages offered and even the ease of use of the website for requesting appointments, shows the level of dedication patients can expect. With the U.S. News & World Report Top Doctor and Castle Connelly Top Doc awards under his belt, it's clear that Dr. Philipp Wall is serious about top quality care. His patients are in great hands at Arizona Vein & Vascular Center.
