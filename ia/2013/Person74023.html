Kelly Tirman's Summary

I am passionate about making content marketing and social commerce strategies operational by building (and utilizing) highly-skilled virtual and ﬂexible workforces. These workforces provide companies the ability to scale while lowering operational costs, retaining staff, and increasing brand engagement.

I have 10+ years experience with an emphasis on eCommerce, Social Media, and Online Marketing. This experience includes building communities, operating and maintaining global eCommerce websites and enterprise content management systems for several Fortune 500 companies as part of Customer Experience (CX), Product, Project and User Experience (UX) organizations.

As a highly-skilled senior leader with proven experience, I have the unique ability to juggle big picture strategy, as well as driving out operational excellence. By actively partnering with key stakeholders, leads business transformation by changing the way companies plan, organize, and communicate. As a change management professional, I use thought leadership and, most importantly, experience to manifest change toward the agreed upon objectives. I have solid experience in program and project management leading company-wide initiatives, and delivering them on time and in scope. I am a team lead responsible for facilitating BPR (business process re-engineering) programs and other corporate transformative initiatives.
