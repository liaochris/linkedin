Michael Donohoe's Summary

Michael Donohoe is the Product Engineering Director for Quartz, a global business news site, where he overseas web and product development.

Prior to joining Quartz (owned by Atlantic Media), and a year in Seattle, he spent eight years at The New York Times. Here he worked across a number of departments, from CMS, APIs, front-end, mobile and product development. He worked on a number of open source projects, developer outreach and TimesOpen events.

In his spare time he likes to work on a number of varied projects - from a NYTimes crossword search service, Chrome extensions, playing with new APIs. His work has been featured in numerous places - Apple.com, Google, techCrunch, GigaOm, Smashing Magazine, LifeHacker, and others

You can see more side-projects here:
http://donohoe.io

Specialties: Javascript (plain & various frameworks), "HTML5", DOM, CSS3, browser extensions. Other languages, frameworks & tech as required.

Prefer JSON and JSONP based APIs over XML but its all good.
