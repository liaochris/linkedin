Diego Cortassa's Summary

Engineered the entire data center and renderfarm for Endenlab S.r.l. (a 3D production firm in Turin) with 6TB EMC storage and 80 cores HPC Linux cluster.

Engineered renderfarm for Lumiq S.p.a. (a post production firm in Turin) with 300 cores (150 Dual Xeon blades) HPC Linux cluster, cloning facility, ganglia and zabbix performance monitoring and drqueue/royal render job dispatching.

Long term consultant for Airdolomiti S.p.a. (an airline company in Triest) where I administered SUN Servers (2 Workgroup server V880), Linux e Storage Area Network StorEdge from SUN Microsystems. Migrated their systems from Microsoft to Linux and Solaris.

Managed the systems for a big Italian Internet portal CiaoWeb (Solaris Enterprise server 4000 e 3000/apache/mysql) and programmed a lot of “Perl glue”.

Installed the web server of a big Italian bank (BNL) on a Sun Enterprise Server 10.000.

Programmed few web applications with LAMP platform (e-commerce and ad hoc applications) have some demos on http://eyes.logx.it, http://gsu.logx.it, http://tom.logx.it.

Engineered and installed the network of the Comune di Orbassano (city of Orbassano, a town with 22.000 inhabitants near Turin) with 6 Server Linux (mail, firewall, web, SMB sharing, fax) and about 150 windows clients.

Teached C language to programmers of the Turin public Transport company and computer security to the IT team of Valeo Turin (a French automotive group).

Installed few international VPN networks for Contacta s.p.a., Gasenergia s.p.a., CAPAT s.r.l., Grimaldi case, Bottero s.p.a (Italy, UK, USA, China, France, Germany, Poland).

Installed, configured and managed 6 local ISP companies with Linux and Cisco machines: Net&Media in Orbassano (TO), Stefan MCDS in Asti, Elbacom in Portoferraio (Elab Island), MBM in Torrepellice (TO), GO@net in Genova and Art&Media in Torino.

Installed, configured and managed for years more than 120 Linux Servers in different companies.

Specialties
