Diana Taylor's Summary

From my website bio:
A groundbreaking, New Thought writer, Taylor offers a fun, dynamic, innovative, and inspirational message through her work at Pug At The Beach.

With her trademark style of concise, tightly structured prose designed to inspire and motivate, her willingness to reach out to others guiding them to their own triumphs, an inherent sense of optimism, and sense of fun through play, Taylor's path to inspiring writer and business leader has been a lifetime in the making.

Since creating Pug At The Beach, Taylor has back filled her English/Science degree (from Dowling College in New York) with self taught business skills gleaned from participation and/or membership in various entrepreneur focused groups including:

*MicroCredit-NH
*The Small Business Development Center
*The Women’s Business Center
*The Hannah Grimes Entrepreneur Project
*The Kearsarge Women’s Business Network
*The Monadnock Business Resource Partnership
*SAILS (Serving And Inspiring Leader's Success)
*SCORE
*Toastmasters.

Taylor writes the Pug At The Beach books and a weekly online column. Both offer innovative and inspirational messages of hope and positive living through lessons learned while walking the beach everyday.

Walking her talk, Taylor recently made her own dreams come true by relocating from New Hampshire, her home of 25 years, to South Florida.

With a varied work background as:

*Phlebotomist (the person who takes your blood)
*Pharmacy Technician
