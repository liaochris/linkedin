Jared Goralnick's Summary

Entrepreneur, speaker, and community evangelist with a penchant for finding the most practical applications of everyday technology--particularly with regard to attention management and email productivity. I hope to change the way people communicate with technology, which should strengthen our relationships and facilitate productivity rather than adding even more noise to our lives.

Since I sold SET Consulting in July, 2010, my time is spent on AwayFind, a web application that helps people to escape email interruptions--there are ten of us and we have backing from institutional investors like CAP Ventures and 500 Startups. While San Francisco is home, I also spend a lot of time in the DC and Buenos Aires areas.

As you can see from my positions, I try in earnest to help the startup and creative communities where I live, and to further research that aids in addressing information overload. I believe the best way to achieve these goals is curating events and providing education through speaking and writing.

Jared Goralnick's Experience

Founder and CEO

AwayFind

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Services industry

February 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months) San Francisco Bay Area

AwayFind is an application that provides mobile notifications of priority emails. We help people to spend less time in their inbox but to be more responsive to those they most need to hear from. Some of our best customers (we have over 2,000) are in the sales, support, and professional service industries.

AwayFind was initially a side project while at SET Consulting, as we saw our clients (like the FBI, USMS, NTT/DoCoMo) were spending half their days in their email. Once I sold SET, I moved to the Bay Area and grew AwayFind to the ten-person team it is today.
