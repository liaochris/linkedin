Joni Migdal's Summary

Joni Migdal is a consistent top producer specializing in Westside high-end properties with more than 10 years of success. She is as respected for her knowledge and marketing skills as she is admired for the superbly seamless manner in which she conducts business. Joni has earned many awards in the ultra-competitive Westside real estate market, including membership in the prestigious International President's Elite, President's Circle, and as a Previews Property Specialist. Her kindness, high ethical standards, consummate charm and and commitment to her clients truly set her apart. Her communication and problem solving skills are unparalleled.

"Joni Migdal is an outstanding agent and an incredible human being. She never forgets the two are not mutually exclusive," said Scott Gibson, President and CEO of Gibson International. "She is an essential asset to any company. We are proud to be working with her."

Joni brings to Gibson International her background as an educator and administrator, with two U. C. L. A. masters degrees, one in Gifted Education and Administration, and, after many years of teaching and teacher training, the creation of the Gifted and Talented Education program for grades 1-12 for the Culver City School District.

Joni joined Gibson International after many years with Coldwell Banker to put an even greater focus on service in a smaller, more exclusive, personalized environment.

"Being a part of Gibson International gives me the opportunity to make a greater difference for my clients and to have a positive impact on the industry because the company has been structured to allow agents increased creativity and flexibility" said Migdal. "Buying or selling a home is one of the most important events in a person's life. I take my role in that very seriously. It is important to me to approach each client’s particular needs creatively, with sensitivity, to communicate effectively and to guide each important decision ethically."
