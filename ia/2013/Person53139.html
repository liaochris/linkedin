Hillmann Consulting, LLC's Summary

Hillmann Consulting, LLC is a full service Environmental & Engineering Consulting firm providing an array of customized Environmental Health and Occupational Safety, Remediation Support, Sustainability Consulting, Environmental Due Diligence, Geology, and Laboratory services. Our impressive portfolio also includes performing Emergency Planning and First Response Programs for major disasters such as terror incidents, hurricanes, typhoons, floods, earthquakes, fires, and building collapses.

Our goal is to provide innovative and cost effective solutions for your environmental needs while maintaining our ongoing commitment to safety. Our clientele include Property Owners, Property Management Firms, Commercial Lenders, Insurance Companies, and Construction Management Firms to name a few. We service some of the highest profile clients in the most complex markets across the country.

Hillmann Consulting, LLC has been a well recognized leader in the Environmental and Engineering Consulting industry since the 1980s. We offer a comprehensive range of services and continually seek to expand our service capabilities in an effort to meet the ever changing needs of our clients. We maintain a team of multi-disciplined professionals from a broad spectrum of occupational backgrounds.

Our commitment to service is simple ~ Your Property. Our Priority.

Specialties

Green Building & Sustainable Design Services, Mold Delineation and Remediation Management, Emergency Management Services, Environmental Health and Occupational Safety Services, Geology & Subsurface Services, Real Estate Due Diligence and Construction Loan Services, and Training Programs.
We also maintain an in-house Laboratory for all of your analytical needs.
