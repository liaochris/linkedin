Catherine Azzarello's Summary

Azzcat Design is a one-person studio near Portland, OR, specializing in responsive front-end solutions for websites and applications. Catherine's design and technical expertise come together in a "Designer Who Codes".

Catherine has worked extensively with Wurl, Inc. (a Palo Alto startup) as part of their Agile team building iPhone/Android apps, responsive web apps integrated with OAuth, and wurl.com (custom responsive WordPress).

Other client work includes identity design (EyeStyles, GHC, Young's), custom WordPress site design/development and print collateral. Check out Azzcat Design for examples, live links, blog and more information.
