Jamila Chambers' Summary

Dream Releaser Life Coach. Motivational Speaker. Visionary Leader. Jamila is a multifaceted business professional with over 20 years of industry experience who has worked in various market segments including retail grocery, hospitality, agricultural, non-profit, and sales. Her corporate tenure has been hallmarked by producing results and building field human resource departments for several Fortune 500 companies.

After achieving measurable success, she found her significance through empowering others to "create a life without limits." Jamila has a transformational perspective and believes that every life is significant and that every person has a unique and strategic assignment. She founded The Dream Studio, a multi-media personal development and branding platform to equip others with tools to achieve their personal best. Jamila's message is known for its boldness, authenticity, and transparency. She delivers in a way that allows her audience to "get it" and learn how to apply "it."

Her expertise in Success Coaching, Motivational Speaking, Human Resources, and Training & Development has added value to countless businesses, organizations, and individuals. Jamila is a certified 7 Habits Signature Facilitator, Dream Releaser Life Coach, and a HRCI - Senior Professional Human Resources. She has been happily married to her husband Rufus for over 10 years and they have two beautiful daughters Winter Samone and Willow Corinne. She believes that every life is significant and encourages every person to "be excellent!"

Specialties

Strategic planning, human resources development, workforce planning and development, employee and labor relations, compensation and benefits, life coaching.
