Alvaro Rodriguez's Summary

I have been for many years in the furniture design and making trade.

I take great pleasure in working with the natural element wood and staying in contact with it daily.

I particularly enjoy the challenge of designing and building a functional piece of furniture in a modern or traditional style, which expresses quality, is reliable and does not date.

My work also responds to your wish to create something unique, practical, functional and long lasting that will complement your taste and your home’s aesthetic.

I enjoy creating unique pieces which have a harmonious mix of trend, beauty, strength and style.

This furniture is made to fulfil your needs and to last, so special attention to materials, details and finishes is given.

In this way you will be able to enjoy the comfort of having furniture made especially for you in your home or office.

Specialties

The Dream-Weaver-Bed
This is a new concept of modern design and traditional wood working techniques.

The beds are all made with natural products like wood, stone and marble. All treatments like oils and waxes are environmental friendly.

The beds are metal free. No, screws or nails or any metal connection systems are used.

This combination gives your bedroom a natural environment in which you will find the rest and good sleep you so much deserve after a long-hard-working day.
