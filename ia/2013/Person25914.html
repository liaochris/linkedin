Chris Michael's Summary

By day, I'm a pimp to the American consumer whore as a senior copywriter at Publicis Kaplan Thaler. By night, I'm a political humorist at The Huffington Post, contributor to Daily Kos, and Editor's-Pick cartoonist at Salon.

As an actual human being, I do stand-up, write sketch comedy, and am the creator of the t-shirt/cartoon haven: THEMISFITPENGUIN.com. And I recently started a blog on my adventures in urban farming and sustainable living in the big city: THECRUNCHYURBANITE.com

On the other side of the Atlantic, both the Sorbonne (Paris IV) and Humboldt-Univerität took turns trying to educate me before I graduated from Bard College with a self-made major in the psycholinguistics of humor. (Then no one knew what that meant, so I went to SVA for advertising just like eeeeeeeverybody else.)

Now, my goal is to become a New Yorker cartoonist and the next Augusten Burroughs while I figure out how the antics that landed me in the principal's office as a child will pay for a one-bedroom apartment. In cash.
