Joey Goodman's Summary

I've been involved in the Insurance industry since I was 13 years old. It was a family business that provided me an excellent opportunity to not only learn about the industry, but learn about my family and myself. Approximately a year ago, I started to see the benefits that technology has to offer for the Insurance industry, and decided to take a chance on my abilities and knowledge that I had picked up along the way. Although that path hasn't worked out as I had planned, it provided me with experiences and opportunities that have helped me grow and evolve in ways I never imagined.

After spending countless hours on my computer and the Internet for the past year, I'm looking for the best way to share my knowledge and provide as much value as I can to anyone who wants to learn. Social networking has enabled me to connect with people from all over the world and truly realize how technology has changed our lives, and how much more were have to go!
