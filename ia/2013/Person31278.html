Dave Rolinson's Summary

I am a Project Manager specialising in Data Migration; I have performed this role at Lex Vehicle Leasing in major Business Integration Projects with Blue Chip companies including Ford, HSBC and HBOS.

I am currently working as Defect Manager & Data Separation Subject Matter Expert for Tata Technologies on the ATV programme at Jaguar Land Rover; a truly massive programme to separate JLR from Ford now that it has been acquired by Tata.

I have worked extensively in Financial Services industry, particularly in Asset Management / Vehicle Leasing. I have also worked in the manufacturing sector developing MRP/II systems.

I am looking for Project Management roles in the Midlands. I am a Prince2 and MSP Practitioner and also hold ITIL V3 Foundation certification. My Project Management skills are underpinned by extensive technical skills from my background as a Developer and Business Analyst.

Specialties

I have over 16 years experience in the Vehicle Leasing / Asset Management sector and have particularly strong knowledge of Pricing and Front Office Quoting applications.

I have extensive expertise in Data Migration, both at a technical and a Project Management level.
