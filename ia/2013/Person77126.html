Kristin Kurie's Summary

The Wilderman Group is a woman-owned hospitality management company that specializes in operating meeting and event venues, and civic and community developments throughout the Southeast. Based in Charleston, South Carolina, The Wilderman Group is a full-service management firm with a team of industry experts in all areas of hospitality including, operations, sales and marketing management, food and beverage, environmental programs and an emphasis on supporting local economies and strengthening communities. Our “sleeves-rolled-up” style of management focuses on success – from the owner’s perspective. For details on the capabilities and services offered by The Wilderman Group, visit the website at www.TheWildermanGroup.com or call 843.670.5056 .

The Wilderman Group is committed to environmental stewardship and is a proud and active member of the US Green Building Council’s South Carolina Chapter, Georgia Chapter, Charlotte Regional Chapter, and the International Association of Conference Centers.

Kristin Kurie's Experience

Owner and President

The Wilderman Group

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Hospitality industry

January 2006 – Present (8 years) Charleston, South Carolina Area

Kristin Kurie is president and owner of The Wilderman Group,
and is a 30-year veteran of the hospitality industry with a concentration on the sales and marketing discipline, conference centers, and meeting and event venues. Kristin served as director of sales and marketing at Doral Arrowwood Resort & Conference Center, considered one of the nation’s premier conference centers. A recognized industry leader, Kristin successfully managed her own sales consulting practice, Kurie & Associates for a number of years and worked with many of the premier conference center management companies in the industry.

Kristin served as a director of the International Association of Conference Centers (IACC) and was active on the marketing and sustainability committees. She was presented the coveted Award of Excellence from IACC for her years of service to the association and to the conference center industry and is the proud recipient of the prestigious Pyramid Award for her commitment to education in the conference center industry.

Kristin is a committed and dedicated steward of the environment and member of Lowcountry Local First, the organization that promotes the advantages of buying from local, independent business and farmers. Through concentrated environmental practices incorporated at The Wilderman Group’s three USGBC LEED facilities, she continues to raise awareness of sustainability programs and green meetings in the hospitality industry.

As Vice President of New Business for The Wilderman Group, Kristin managed the overall sales and marketing strategies as well as sales training and the pursuit of new business opportunities. While she continues to focus on sales and marketing, in her role as President she performs the day-to-day tasks aimed at implementing the organization's focused vision.

Kristin graduated from Boston College with a bachelor of arts degree in Russian Studies and remains an avid NY Football Giants fan.
