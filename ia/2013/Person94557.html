Mike Scheiner's Summary

As Senior Vice President, Executive Creative Director at MWW Group, Mike Scheiner’s responsibility is to oversee all integrated digital and social strategies to deepen the engagement between audience and brand for MWW’s clients across its nine offices.

It is Scheiner’s role to define the brand strategy, overall creative direction and messaging that clarify a brand’s position and point of difference. That, in turn, results in solutions that are highly branded, integrated ideas and content that encompass paid, earned and owned media channels. Those expanding spheres incorporate digital, social, mobile, search, public relations, traditional and experiential media, which are then supported and refined by metrics and analytics.  

Scheiner’s career has dramatically evolved and he has been at the forefront of each new media trend. Scheiner’s strengths are rooted in strategy by creating and building on fully dimensional ideas that can bridge online and offline experiences and that always seek to answer the question: how can the idea grow? Scheiner also has a proven ability to provide thought leadership, mentor internal staff and motivate them to embrace new ideas.

Prior to joining MWW, Scheiner was Executive Vice President, Integrated Branding and Digital at Porter Novelli. As an entrepreneur, Scheiner ran his own integrated brand consultancy, Scheiner Inc. Previous positions include: Executive Creative Director and Co-Managing Director for the G2 Stamford, CT office. Creative Director of DDB NY Brand Integration Group. Senior Vice President of content operations with marketingforce.com. Vice President Group Creative Director with G2 and Art Director with Grey Advertising.

Scheiner has won several awards, including the 2008 Silver Davey Award rich media campaign and the 2004 Effie Award. He has been featured in Graphis Brochure 5 and has served as a panelist for the 2010 Social Media Week and 2012 Digital Signage to Mobile Screens as a Presenter and Panelist.
