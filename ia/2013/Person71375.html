Justin Schulwitz's Projects

  • Augmented Me

    • August 2010 to May 2011
    Team Members: Justin Schulwitz, Eric Lundby, Justin Searles

    Augmented Me is an application allowing individuals to "draw" with their hands or create interesting and unique animations over themselves and record what they make using webcam-based motion tracking. Utilizing filters and algorithms designed to be light on performance requirements due to it being web-based, the application can pinpoint the center of motion and draw an artistic image on the screen. The application is modular and constructed to allow easy extension or to add additional filters and virtual reality effects.
