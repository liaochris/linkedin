Michael A. DeMayo's Summary

Mr. Michael A. DeMayo is the president and founder of the Law Offices of Michael A. DeMayo, L.L.P. Mr. DeMayo and his attorneys and staff of over 96 have helped more than 45,000 clients across the Carolinas, with offices in Charlotte, Hickory, Monroe and Lumberton.

Mr. DeMayo is a Benefactor and Founding Member of the Mecklenburg Trial Lawyers Association, a member of the North Carolina Advocates for Justice and was selected as one of "Top 100 Trial Lawyers" by the American Association of Trial Lawyers.

Mr. DeMayo is a member of the Million Dollar Advocates Forum and the Multi- Million Dollar Advocates Forum, served on the Wake Forest University Alumni Council, is a member of the 1834 Society and served on the Board of Directors for the University of North Carolina School of Law (Chapel Hill) Alumni Association. Other memberships include: American Association for Justice; American Bar Association; Mecklenburg County Bar; North Carolina Bar Association; North Carolina State Bar; Supreme Court of the United States; North Carolina Brain Injury Association; and Brain Injury Association of America.

In 2003, Mr. DeMayo developed “Arrive Alive,” an outreach program to educate teenagers about the dangers and consequences of underage drinking and driving. Since 2003, Mr. DeMayo also has awarded over $237,000 in college scholarships to graduating high school seniors through the Michael A. DeMayo Scholarship established in 2002.

Mr. DeMayo was born in Silver Spring, Maryland. He graduated cum laude in 1987 with a B.A. from Wake Forest University. He attended and graduated from theUniversity of North Carolina at Chapel Hill School of Law in 1990.

Mr. DeMayo is licensed to practice law in all North Carolina State Courts, U.S. District Courts, and is admitted to the United States Supreme Court. Mr. DeMayo is fluent in Spanish and lives in Charlotte, North Carolina.
