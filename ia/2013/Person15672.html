Bonnie Buol Ruszczyk's Summary

I have nearly 20 years of marketing experience working with a diverse range of industries and people, most recently serving as the marketing director for a top-10 accounting firm in Atlanta. In August 2009, I left that role to create BBR Marketing, which specializes in marketing for professional services providers and firms. The beauty of my company is its flexible team approach. No two clients are the same, and neither are the solutions they need. That’s why I build the team only after we discuss your marketing goals. I work with clients on projects as small as bios and company descriptions to full blown marketing plans with all the deliverables and everything in between. I can even serve as an interim or part-time marketing director for firms that aren’t quite sure if they are ready to hire someone full time or need extra help during a busy time of year. A perfect client for me is a small to mid-sized professional services firm – accountants, lawyers, IT consultants, engineers & financial advisors.
