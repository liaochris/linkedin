Chris Apple's Summary

Apple Enterprises, http://applehome.com/, specializes in custom software for Windows XP and embedded software.

Apple Enterprises was formed as a sole proprietorship by Chris Apple in 1981 to provide engineering consulting services - 28 years of experience as a consultant. He has over 33 years of experience developing software and firmware for embedded products. He has designed microprocessor boards, Windows applications to control and test the embedded products and Installers for the end customer.

Product Development Overview

Developed IBM PC products: Http server for Netscape; remote product support for Microsoft; video teleconferencing for Voyant; Ofoto for Windows for Light Source; lap top portable for Chisholm; control TSR for Alps;

Developed software tools: MPEG2 tools for Hyundai; VxGdb interface-Sun to PC for Hyundai; 386 operating system and debugger for Digital F/X; Emulator interfaces for Microtec Research's XRAY debugger.

Designed embedded microprocessor products for: General Electric in nuclear power safety equipment (patent); Pinnacle Systems, Digital F/X and CMX in commercial broadcast equipment; Compumetric and GCA in semiconductor capital equipment; Rainin, Eldex and Critikon (Johnson and Johnson) in pumps.

Success indicator - several products for same company: 4 products for Light Source, 3 products for GE, 3 products for Digital F/X, 2 products for CMX, 3 products for Compumetric and many products for Microtec Research.

Specialties

Java, C++, MFC, Windows CE, BSP, XP Embedded, ThreadX, TI MSP430, NXP LPC2000, Renesas SH4
