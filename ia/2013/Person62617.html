Jeroen van den Eijkhof's Summary

I live to produce compelling experiences. My journey began by traveling down two parallel professional paths for several years — visual design and web development — until I realized that there was a field that represented an intersection of both disciplines.

That journey has taken me through educational and professional opportunities where I was able to craft digital user experiences such as kiosks, web sites, exhibits, mobile applications, research synthesis, back-end IT systems and even physical prototyping.

I continue to look for any opportunities I can to put my passion for UX to work solving meaningful challenges facing people using technology.


Skills
Rapid Prototyping, sketching, problem space research, HCD methodologies, persona creation, IA, wireframe creation, usability testing, ethnography, interaction design, creative facilitation and concept development.

Good to know
I am a fervent collaborator, captivating presenter, obsessive iterator, strategic thinker, trilingual European with an accent.


Clients
Microsoft Game Studios, MasterCard, Kohler, Microsoft Research, T-Mobile, Amplify, Xerox, University of Southern California Libraries, Microsoft Store, Solavei and Give For Youth.
