With a passion for the beauty industry, Michelle has experienced many sides of the business as owner, image consultant, hair care stylist, skin care consultant, educator and mentor. Michelle Moore Bell's Experience

The Wellness Group

January 1994 – Present (19 years 10 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

In 1994, Michelle established The Wellness Group, a non-profit 501c (3) health awareness organization. Their mission is to educate, inform and empower women on breast cancer and a healthy lifestyle.

Michelle volunteers her skills and talent with the American Cancer Society to help women with cancer look great and feel better about themselves.

Image & Hair Styling Consultant

SALON 21 (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Cosmetics industry

January 1987 – Present (26 years 10 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

Michelle Moore Bell, Principal of Salon 21, is an image and hair styling consultant, she has experienced many sides in the beauty business as owner, educator, licensed professional cosmetologist and mentor.
For over 30 years, she has transformed the lives of women worldwide in the areas of beauty and wellness. This passion has caused her to live her dreams inspiring women all over the globe to look and feel good about themselves and to learn how to live healthier lifestyles.
Michelle offers classes, services and products to enhance the natural beauty of women of every age. As an educator, Michelle teaches business skills, techniques and trends to the salon owner and the professional hair stylist, plus she caters to consumers' hair care concerns. She teaches the benefits of going to a professional stylist, and using professional take-home hair care products for healthy hair. She also teaches skin care to women.
She created and promoted her own line of skin care products and make-up for 17 years before she was introduced to Mary Kay eight years ago.
Michelle loves to reward her clients, which is one of the reasons she offers a referral program that provides free hair care services and products.
