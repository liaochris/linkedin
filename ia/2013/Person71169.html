Justine Ruotolo's Summary

I have been an ADD/Life Coach for the past 20 years. I work with children, adolescents, parents, and adults challenged with their diagnosis of ADD. I help my clients to embrace their gifts that are associated with ADD. I have learned how to control my symptoms of ADD so they no longer run my life. I Love to share my successes with my clients. I help my adult clients un-block energies and discover their passion and purpose.
