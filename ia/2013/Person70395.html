Judy Hodge's Summary

With over 20 years in the ad biz I have had the pleasure of working with some outstanding agencies and lots of very talented people in NY, Florida, Scottsdale and Boston.

I am a creative director with a passion for doing work that works. Building brands to fall in love with and connecting them to the people they were built for. Sort of a brand match-maker.

My work is on-time, on-budget, fresh, compelling and driven by sound and truthful brand strategy. I do work that feels good and gets noticed. I say, own mindshare and the money follows. And who doesn't love that.

I am a “roll-up-my-sleeves” creative director with mad skills and a love for talent and collaboration. I've won lots of awards so I guess I am a-okay from the industry point of view.

I love what I do. Every day. And that makes me a pretty happy person to be around. I speak fluent consumer and I know the value of listening and flexibility. Go team.

Advertising. It may not save lives, but it sure is a good gig.
