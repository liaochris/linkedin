Brian Giesen's Summary

I am part of the world’s largest network of social business and word of mouth marketing professionals – the Social@Ogilvy team. Over the past two years, I’ve built from scratch and manage a 15-person strong business within Ogilvy Australia, which has generated 100% increases in revenue each year.

I love sharing my perspective on how social is revolutionizing businesses and organisations today, regularly speaking at conferences including AdTech Sydney, IRBC Social Media & Government Conference, Web 3.0 Conference, Frocomm Digital Media Conference, Walkley Media Conference, and others. I’m also no stranger to TV studios, having appeared on SkyNews to offer my views on the changing digital environment.

I can also credibly say that creativity is a strength of mine. While I don’t yet have any piercings, I’m a huge believer in the fact that big ideas are only borne under the right conditions – when you have a thorough understanding of a business problem and when you’ve done some quick planning and research. That’s how I came up with a concept that became Australia's first use of Foursquare by a major brand that won several awards in Australia and APAC.

One of the things that makes me different from other social media marketers, I suppose, is my broader knowledge of digital marketing and communications. I know what search engine spiders like for dinner, how to write a killer subject line, and engineered an award-winning national digital advertising partnership with Yahoo! on behalf of a leading U.S. government health agency.
