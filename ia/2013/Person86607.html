Mark Gartland's Summary

Creative media executive and digital content evangelist with an award-winning portfolio and a proven track record of consistently achieving success managing message and engagement at the nexus of technology, entertainment and design.

Specialties: Creative strategy, branding and identity; Responsive design for mobile devices, interactive and print integration; Media launches and redesigns from tablets to tabloids; Content Strategy, User Engagement, Online Video, Start-ups, Social Media, Sound Design, UGC and Web 2.0.

Mark Gartland's Experience

Creative Director

Google

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; GOOG; Internet industry

2012 – 2013 (1 year) Mt.View, USA

Creative Director at Google Interactive Operations for the launch of Google Play. Successfully managed operations between multiple partners and a large staff of designers involved in the creation of interactive content and responsive design for a range of mobile devices including Android and iPad tablets and most notably Google's new Nexus 4, 7 and 10 devices. Developed templates and standards for interactive publications/apps and mentored designers on the specifics of user experience and engagement.

Creative Director

MiniMash

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

2008 – 2011 (3 years) Oakland, USA

Co-founder and Creative Director of MiniMash, a cloud-based startup that leverages the revolution in image sharing, social media and mobile devices by being the easiest way to stylize and share short movies. Achieved major milestones for all aspects of this online video platform including ideation, branding, design, information architecture, user experience, wireframes, mocks, assets, market research and strategy, product management and development, talent acquisition, budgeting, promotion and fundraising. Launched at DEMO 2010 to rave reviews from the tech media and critical acclaim from the venture capital community. Orchestrated massive promotion on the BEBO social network which increased the user base by 500%. Developed Facebook application based on the AutoMash function.

Design Director

Voice Media Group

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Online Media industry

2002 – 2007 (5 years) Emeryville, USA

Design Director for Village Voice Media (now Voice Media Group) and its group of websites and publications including SF Weekly, LA Weekly, Seattle Weekly, Westword, Riverfront Times, City Pages, Village Voice and the East Bay Express. Commuted across the country interviewing and mentoring art directors, designers, illustrators, photographers and developers. Achieved increased circulation and improved performance in major metropolitan markets by enhancing cover effectiveness and consumer engagement. Successfully integrated publications that were acquired by the company (17 total) and developed the corporate template for the online publishing at the local web editions nationwide. Awards include: 2003 SPD Award for Cover Design; 2004, 2005 MAGGIE Awards for Best Tabloid

Lecturer

San Francisco State University

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

2005 – 2006 (1 year) San Francisco Bay Area

Developed and presented course on digital publication design for the Journalism Department of San Francisco State University including leading a lab on Adobe Creative Suite and InDesign.

Design Director

Red Herring Communications

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry

2000 – 2001 (1 year) San Francisco, USA

Design Director for Red Herring, a website and publication focused on the business of technology that the Wall Street Journal described as the "bible of Silicon Valley." During my tenure Red Herring became the fastest growing magazine in the world and achieved increase in circulation and revenue through brand enhancement and improved consumer engagement. Successfully implemented doubling the publication frequency from monthly to biweekly along with the move to digital direct-to-plate printing. Mentored staff on the use of imagery, infographics, illustration and typography. Partnered with world-famous creative talent including Helmut Newton and Herb Ritts to create iconic covers including a nude Pamela Anderson. Successfully initiated redesign for the corporate branding, magazine, marketing, conferences and online publications of Red Herring. Awards include: 2001 SPD Award for Cover Design

Art Director

Verlagsgruppe Georg von Holtzbrinck

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; Publishing industry

1997 – 1999 (2 years) Dusseldorf, Germany

Art Director for WirtschaftsWoche, the world’s largest weekly German business magazine. Achieved significant increase in circulation and revenue due to enhanced product performance. Revived this legacy brand through improved product performance and consumer engagement due in large part to effective cover ideation and execution. Consigned illustration and photography from internationally know contributors on a weekly basis. Successfully art directed a large German staff including photo editors, designers, writers and editors. Founding Art Director for the launch of the online publishing at wiwo.de the online portal for WirtschaftsWoche.

Creative Consultant

Time Warner

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; TWX; Entertainment industry

1995 – 1996 (1 year) Hong Kong, China

Creative consultant for several brands in this international media group including; redesign of Asiaweek magazine including logo, overall design upgrade and promotional campaign; launch of branding for Malaysian Airlines in-flight entertainment including Going Places magazine, audio-visual and interactive entertainment.

Creative Director

Hachette Filipacchi

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; MMB; Publishing industry

1994 – 1995 (1 year) Europe and Asia

Creative Director for the launch and/or redesign of magazines in Asia and Europe including Elle, Elle Decor, Orient Beauty and Woman's Day. Creative consultant responsible for installation of computer network and digital publishing systems.

Art Director

American Photo

1990 – 1993 (3 years) Greater New York City Area

Successfully redesigned this magazine to showcase the world’s greatest photography. Championed the use of digital technology by designing and producing American Photo electronically at launch in 1990. Awards include: 1993 Society of Publication Designers (SPD) Award for Overall Design, 1993 ASME National Magazine Award for General Excellence and 1992 SPD Award for Cover Design.
