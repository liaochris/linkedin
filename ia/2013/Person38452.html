Dr. Alan Winder's Summary

Feel free to add me to your network if you think we may be able to work together or help each other in any way.

I'm a Clinical Psychologist with specialization in treating stress disorders, mental health issues relating to aging, and mood disorders related to adjustment difficulties, and am the owner and Clinical Director of a company that provides mental health services to long-term care facilities. I also maintain a part-time private practice.

Aside from psychology I also have an interest and background in finance and business:

I've co-managed a hedge fund that focuses on overlooked and undervalued companies. Activist philosophy, in a nice way whenever possible, working closely with company management to unlock shareholder value.

I've also worked as a FINRA-registered investment banker at a boutique bank in NYC, focusing on raising capital, facilitating PIPEs, and providing strategic advisory services to companies on a wide variety of topics including corporate restructurings, divestitures, and M&A.

I'm interested in academic work as well, having taught as an adjunct for several semesters at a private college.

Specialties: C-level management of Healthcare companies (specialty in Mental Health,) Deal sourcing & structuring with emphasis on creating proprietary deals for Private Equity partners.
