Kale Brewer's Projects

  • Powellhurst-Gilbert Neighborhood Multicultural Community Development Project

    • January 2010 to June 2010
    Team Members: Kale Brewer, Jessica Pickul, LEED AP, Bronwyn Richardson, , Caitlin Alexander

    The Powellhurst-Gilbert Multicultural Community Development Project is a collaboration between Connect PG, a group of Community Development students at Portland State University, and our community partner, Mark White of the Powellhurst-Gilbert Neighborhood Association (PGNA), as well as a variety of business leaders, community organizations, and other stakeholders in the neighborhood.

    Connect PG seeks to engage a variety of community stakeholders in the community in envisioning and developing a plan for the transformation of this ethnically diverse neighborhood into a locally well recognized Multicultural District of Portland, OR.

  • PDX Streetverve: Examining Portland's Neighborhoods

    • June 2011 to August 2011
    Team Members: Kale Brewer, Portland State University Senior Capstone Class Summer 2011, Meg Merrick

    The Portland Neighborhood Almanac Project is an exploratory pilot project with the mission of collecting high quality micro-level quantitative and qualitative data about selected Portland neighborhoods. The pilot will also test GIS, internet and mobile applications as tools of data collection, analysis and dissemination.
