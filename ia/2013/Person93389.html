Michelle Blanc's Summary

She is a graduate of the first class of Master of Sciences in electronic commerce, with a specialization in management, in Canada. She has been author of numerous scientific, research, didactic and “how to” publications and she animates a blog at michelleblanc.com, which happens to be one of the most influential French techno blog in the world according to Wikio.fr. Her blog is also one of the favorite of the prestigious French portal Strategies.fr. She has been involved with analysis and implementation of cutting edge e-business related technologies, management and marketing activities and strategies in a wide range of industries. Mrs. Blanc has provided research and advice to Fortune 500 as well as SMB’s, government agencies and associations. As a matter of fact, she is amongst the 50 Canadian experts consulted by Industry Canada in order to set the Canadian e-economy objective and policies for its internal and external position to such international forums as OECD. As a speaker, she started her course at the prominent Council on e-business innovation of The Conference Board of Canada and has been speaking ever since in Canada and abroad.
She is the founder and President of Analyweb, a consultancy company specializing in e-business management and marketing.She also is a co-founder and president of Yulbiz, an international business blogger gathering, which is continuously expanding and is now in 5 countries.
