Michael Musgrove's Summary

I design and develop high-performance websites and web tools for my clients and then watch them get amazing results with increased revenue, a larger market share, and a more memorable (and shareable) online brand experience.

I'm a designer and web developer who runs web design POP in Louisville, KY USA. I also ship with an MBA and English degree and a specialty in marketing strategy. I specialize in WordPress and Genesis when building sites and applications. My sites are mobile-first, responsive, CSS3/LESS, HTML5, SEO-optimized, secure and schema-ready

I'm also a published marketing and software author and have appeared in Harvard Business Review. I serve on several high-level advisory committees, I love dogs, technology, the outdoors, playing guitar, SEC football and WordPress. I also enjoy building high-quality WordPress themes to give away.

I use Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Captivate, Premiere Pro and After Effects.(CS6) For my front-end development I code using PHP, JavaScript, HTML/5, CSS/3, Sass, and LESS. My specialty is WordPress applications, with over 5 years of experience. I also know my way around a command line, MySQL and Apache and an .htaccess file.

Art and design has been a passion since I was a child taking art lessons, to taking formal courses in college, including photography and composition. To be able to combine design and developmental logic is wonderful to me.

I hope you'll contact me if you or anyone you may know wants to amplify an online presence. I guarantee my work.

Best,
Michael
