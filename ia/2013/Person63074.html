Jessica Hertlein's Summary

* Over 11 years of experience in the Health and Wellness industry

*Started training and coaching dancers at the age of 17. Her first choreography was aired at the age of 19.

*Then focused on studying the body and mind within approaches ranging from Dance Movement Therapy, Pilates and Sports Conditioning, to Psychotherapy, NLP, Hypnotherapy to treatments such as Thai yoga massage and Bowen therapy

*Her company, JH Bowentherapy, evolved out of this exploration and intense study of body and mind.

*Jessica offers her clients a comprehensive approach to help them achieve their desired state in body and mind. Setting up a lifestyle that is fun and gets you to the top of your game!

Jessica provides:

- Specialist Treatments to help with physical ailments such as neck and back pain, headaches and migraines, stress,

- Personal Training including highly specialised exercise programs, nutritional guidance and lifestyle advice

- Integration Coaching to bridge the gap between what we think we "should be doing", "want to be doing" and the thing that actually feels good to be doing! Aligning your deepest desires with your current quality of life allowing you to move forward with strength, confidence and a fit body that is able to support you on that path!

Specialties: Holistic Bodywork and Integrative body and mind Coaching, bringing various specialist techniques together, providing a comprehensive high quality approach to well being.

Holistic Treatments for stress relief, pain management, back pain / Personal Training including Pilates, Functional Training/ Integration, Life style Coaching

- In 2012 JH Bowentherapy joined forces with Healthy Planet to make healthier lifestyles more accessable to the public. the first initiative was started in Chiswick (see more below)
