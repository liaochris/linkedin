Frances Drost's Summary

"Frances Drost is a gifted servant of the King!" says Carol Kent, President of Speak Up Speaker Services. "She is an extraordinary worship leader and her passionate heart for God, combined with musical excellence that includes superior vocals and fine-tuned keyboard skills, will lead you into the very presence of God. I highly recommend her as a speaker, a worship leader, and as a concert artist."

Carol Kent is not alone in her praises of Frances. Eric Copeland, President of Nashville's Creative Soul Records, comments "Not often does a songwriter come to me with the depth of lyric, stark emotion, and just plain strong melodic sense that Frances Drost brings with her songs." And the Christian Music Industry is recognizing the unique gifting that God has placed on her ministry as well.

At the 2009 Momentum Award Ceremony in Nashville, Frances was a nominee for "Inspirational Artist of the Year" and was the winner of the 2009 Momentum Award for "Female Artist of the Year." The awards are the premier recognition program for Christian Independent Artists in America, sponsored by IndieHeaven.

Frances Drost is a concert artist and inspirational speaker who began her own company, "Musical Creations" as a way to encourage people on their journey through life. She skillfully combines music, teaching, and speaking to present a unique, professionally designed program. As both a singer and songwriter, Frances has a unique way of presenting real-life experiences and meaningful messages that are subtly woven throughout her music.

As a speaker, Frances is genuine, vulnerable, and downright entertaining. Her love for people - with all their flaws and faults - is unmistakable and her ability to captivate an audience is inspiring. Candy Davison from Sandy Cove Ministries in Maryland writes, "I sensed a heightened degree of attentiveness during your message from the ladies compared to the norm. You could hear a pin drop while you were speaking!"

Frances Drost's Experience

Songwriter

Songs of Love Foundation

2006 – Present (7 years)

The Songs of Love Foundation is a national nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization that creates free, personalized, original songs to uplift children and teens currently facing tough medical, physical or emotional challenges. Each CD is professionally produced with lyrics containing the child's name and references to all of his or her favorite activities, things, people, and pets. Songs are written and performed in any language in the musical style that the child likes best (pop, R&B, rap, rock, alternative, etc.), by a variety of talented professionals.

As each child is unique, so is every 'song of love' composition. Since 1996 the Songs of Love Foundation has reached out to over 22,500 children in over 500 hospitals and health care facilities in all 50 states. The songs have often been played to alleviate pain and trauma during painful and scary hospital treatments. The song recipients have played their "songs of love" in all kinds of situations and settings such as car trips to the hospital, show and tell at school, for family and friends in person or on the phone, and even for the doctors and nurses. The children have their "songs of love" always available to them whenever they are feeling down.
