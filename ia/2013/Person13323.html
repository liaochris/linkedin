Bent Haugland's Projects

  • Winning Without Losing - Project manager

    • October 2010 to November 2011
    Team Members: Bent Haugland

    Winning Without Losing is an open source collaborative book-project where we seek to discover how to be a successful entrepreneur and live a balanced life at the same time.

    We have build an online platform where we invite authors from all over the world to submit chapters on this topic for our community to read, rate, comment and edit. The highest rated chapters are then published in the final book.

    Winning-Without-Losing is an exiting project because it is innovative on several levels: The insights from the project are important and something we want to share with the world, and the collaborative by which we are creating the book is new and exiting. Come join the project if you feel thrilled: www.winning-without-losing.com

  • Clean Tech Open - Community Organizer

    • November 2007 to September 2008
    Team Members: Bent Haugland

    Clean Tech Open is a Silican Valley business plan competition that encourages and supports the development of clean technology companies.

    After studying entrepreneurship for years, I joined Cleantech Open to get my hands dirty in the Silicon Valley entrepreneurial world. I worked as a volunteer organizing events for cleantech entrepreneurs and investors and thereby got an invaluable understanding of the genome of this world.
