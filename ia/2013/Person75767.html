Kim HuynhBa's Summary

INDUSTRY EXPERIENCES:
23 years of experiences in quality systems, analytical development, stability, project management in pharmaceutical industry. Provided consulting services for companies that were under Consent Decree. Subject matter expert for trial, litigation cases. Provided training on cGMP topics for pharmaceutical industry since 2002 via national and international organizations such as ACS, AAPS, PittCon, CATD, IVT, Compliance OnLine.

ACADEMIC EXPERIENCES:
1. Adjunct Professor at Temple University, teaching Pharmaceutical Analysis for M.S. QA/RA Master Program for School of Pharmacy
2. Adjunct Professor at Illinois Institute of Technology teaching Analytical Chemistry for Chemistry Graduate Program and Regulatory Sciences Certification Program.
3. Adjunct Professor at Widener University, teaching General Chemistry for Chemistry Undergraduate Program

BOOKS PUBLISHED:
1. "Handbook of Stability Testing in Pharmaceutical Development: Regulations, Methodologies and Best Practices", Springer Publisher, 2008
2. "Current Trends of Stability Testing to support Global Markets", Springer Publisher, 2010

PUBLICATIONS/PRESENTATIONS:
*Wrote several book chapters and articles
*frequent invited speaker at national and international conferences.

OTHER ACTIVITIES:
- President of 2013 Eastern Analytical Symposium (EAS).
- Chair of AAPS APQ Publication Committee. Past Chair of APQ Open Forum(2008-12) & Distance Learning(2008-11).
- Steering committee of AAPS CRO Focus Group, Trace Impurities and CMC Focus Group. Founder and Past-Chair of AAPS Stability Focus Group.
- Chair of USP Good Documentation Practices Expert Panel (2010-15), Member of USP Impurities of Drug Product Expert Panel (2010-15), Member of USP General Chapters Physical Analysis Expert Committee (2010-11), Member of USP Reference Standard Project Team (2009-10), Member of USP Prescription/Non-Prescription Stakeholders (2008-10)
- Member of CHPA Impurities Breakout Group (2009-11) and Stability Group.

Specialties

Analytical Development, Stability practices, Outsourcing, Project Management, GMP and Technical Training, CMC, SOP, Conformance Standards, change control. HPLC Method Validation, Technology Transfer. Laboratory Audit and Inspection. Consulting and Expert witness services.
