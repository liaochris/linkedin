Cameron Turner's Summary

I love products that give me the opportunity to express and combat a good idea. Pen and paper is the premise, the software capabilities are only tools, never obstacles.

Worked with fortune 500 Companies: Qualcomm, Oakley, Samsung, AT&T, Cricket Wireless, Microsoft, Sony, Vistage CEO, Holiday Inn, Everbank, and many more.
