Mike Lister's Summary

I have worked in the 'Information Technology' sector since the 1970's and have seen the revolution that IT has brought to business in that time. I have specialised in designing and building IT solutions for business that enhance the business and allows it to grow.

Making systems fit the business and not business processes having to change to fit the IT is a core belief.

I bring this experience into the two business's that I currently manage: -

Mobile-e-Solutions Limited (providing solutions for mobile workers):-
• Proof of Attendance
• Lone Worker Protection
• Key Appointment Monitoring
• Remote Data Capture

Digital-e-Archive Limited (providing e-brochures and archiving services):-
• Scanning of paper documents
• Creation of e-brochures (either from scan or stand-alone)
• Electronic Document Management
• Storing or shredding of scanned documents

Additional ad-hoc services include:-
• Email campaign management
• Simple web site design & build
