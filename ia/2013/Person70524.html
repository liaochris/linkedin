Julia Gentry's Summary

Julia Gentry is the catalyst for this next generation. She is an advocate for business ownership and believes that every single person has a true purpose for their lives. It has become Julia’s number one objective to help people identify what their true calling is, while learning how to leverage that purpose in life and in the marketplace. This gives people the freedom to use their working environment more as a tool to live their ideal lifestyle and embrace an exponentially better future.

At an early age, Julia proved that nothing is “impossible” and after having watched her husband “climb the company ladder” only to receive a significant pay cut, they together, took their life into their own hands and stepped into the world of entrepreneurship. Within just a short year and a half, they had grown their short sale business into one that had bought and sold MILLIONS of dollars worth of real estate, while managing four employees, over 70+ short sale files, and were featured on 4 of the major TV stations in Colorado. From there, Julia, along with 2 other business partners are in the creation stage of launching a national business... Stay tuned!

Along with actively running her newest company, Intern America, named the "Chief Talent Scout", she is traveling the country, speaking to audiences about her experiences and teaching the basic, yet transformational principles, of what it takes to be a successful business owner and purpose – driven individual.

Julia Gentry is HAPPILY married to her husband Travis Gentry, who she considers to be her partner in crime, her best friend, and her better half. Together they are partners of Bob Harrison’s Ministry and supporters of Invisible Children, and Julia is an active ambassador for Stop Child Trafficking Now and a mentor for Rocky Mountain Micro Finance Institute.
