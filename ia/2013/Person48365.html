Les Taylor les@LesTaylor.net's Summary

Les Taylor is a highly sought after and respected Keynote Speaker and Workshop Leader, Expert in Professional Development and Performance Improvement, and author of "Moving from Activity to Achievement". Small business owners, government agencies and C-level executives use his innovative business performance strategies for improving performance, productivity and profitability. His expertise in professional development and a wide range of management and leadership related topics, help organizations build high-performing teams and individuals. His planning methodologies infuse innovation and creativity - helping organizations create profitable and predictable outcomes.

As Founder and CEO of Achievement Solutions, Inc. (dba Outperformers International, LLC) Les Taylor’s extensive background in business and law enforcement provide his clients with a unique advantage. As an Assistant Chief of Police in Tempe, AZ and Executive Director of the Arizona Association of Chiefs of Police Les helped individuals and teams teams increase performance and achieve greater results. As a keynote speaker and workshop leader, Les helps individuals and teams develop performance skills leading to lasting change. Thousands worldwide have benefited from his weekly EzineArticles, and blogs. His eBook “12 Quick Ways to Improve Performance & Productivity” is an internet favorite.

Businesses hire Les Taylor to speak and consult on a number of topics, including:

-How to build and brand High-Performing Management and Leadership Teams
-Personal and Professional Development
-How to keep your Performance Focused and on Track
-How to link strategic planning with pragmatic results
-How to capitalize on the Leadership/Management Overlap

Audiences love Les Taylor’s interactive approach, enthusiastic style and practical action-oriented focus that allow participants to leave with ideas and action steps for immediately implementable results.
