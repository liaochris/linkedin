Larry Perreault's Experience

National Sales & Marketing

Kirkwood Direct

Public Company; Myself Only; Marketing and Advertising industry

September 2009 – Present (4 years) Wilmington, MA

Kirkwood Direct is an end-to-end multi-channel direct mail & print services organization.

After dissolving Arrow Direct in August, 2009, I was searching for print and mail under one roof to complement my abilities to provide strategic and creative services to my clients. Kirkwood Direct is the right combination of customer service and quality services.

We provide data management, data acquisition, analytical modeling, commercial print, letter shop services, fulfillment/kitting, and postal optimization. Kirkwood Printing, Our sister company, is a renowned commercial printer and has been named both Sappi International Printer of the Year and Sappi North American Printer, one of the print industry’s highest honors.
