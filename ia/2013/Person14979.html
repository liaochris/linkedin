Jaime Byrd's Summary

I am a producer, director, and editor for feature films, short films, music videos, commercials, web series, television, documentaries, and cross cultural projects. I work closely with Producer Adam J. Cohen, and actor/writer/producer Matthew Montgomery.

I've won a few awards for my work including a first place award for one of my commercials, film festival awards for best editing, and an honorable mention award for my short film, Cleaning House.

The TV pilot project I produce (featuring Jay Thomas, Tim Matheson, and Sharon Lawrence) won best comedy, best director, and best actor at the Los Angeles International TV awards in 2011.

Specialties: Producing on a very low budget, editing as an art form, experimental shooter, problem solving, creative thinking, art direction, packing, cultural exchange, languages, working in foreign countries, Apple computers and products, movie historian, s p a c e and dimension, marketing, and humor.
