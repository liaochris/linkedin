Loni Holm's Summary

'Cause we all need a little Whoopie in our day!'
At Holm Made Cookies, we pride ourselves in providing you confections with the best, freshest, and most natural ingredients possible. We add no preservatives or artificial sweeteners, flavors, or coloring to any of our products; the dairy we use is all hormone-free. Everything is still made by hand, so your sweets taste like they come right from the kitchen, not from the factory.
We are now a very proud member of the Star-K (dairy) family.

Specialties: Meringues Cookies and Whoopie Pies
Our meringues, are still made by hand. A light, airy cookie that melts in your mouth.
We also make Whoopie Pies. Chocolate & PB filling, Chocolate & "Holm-Made" marshmallow cream, Pumkin & cream cheese filling,Chocolate & mint filling.Oatmeal Spice version & maple cream filling.
We have just an amazing "Wheat Free" Whoopie Pies.
We only use all natural extracts & flavorings. The eggs we use are hormone-free & cage-free.We do not use any hydrogenated fats,
