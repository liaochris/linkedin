Hunter Lovins' Summary

Millennium TIME Magazine Hero of the Planet, Hunter Lovins was named the 2008 Sustainability Pioneer by the European financial community for her 30 years of work framing the sustainability movement, setting forth the business case for energy efficiency, renewable energy and resource productivity and climate protection. A social entrepreneur, she consults to large corporations, small businesses, communities, and dozens of nations around the world. A founding professor at Presidio Graduate School, she was called by Newsweek Magazine the “green business icon.” Hunter is president and founder of Natural Capitalism Solutions and co-creator of the “Natural Capitalism” concept. Previously, she co-founded Rocky Mountain Institute and led it for 20 years as CEO. Author of hundreds of papers and twelve books, including the landmark work, Natural Capitalism, Ms. Lovins travels the world, lecturing and advising private and public sector clients including the World Business Council for Sustainable Development, Wal-Mart, Royal Dutch/Shell, the International Finance Corporation, and the government of Afghanistan.
