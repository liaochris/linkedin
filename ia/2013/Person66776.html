  • Miami Green Energy Home

    • 2012 to Present
    Team Members: John Novar

    Harper purchased the land located near Biscayne Bay in the Village of Pinecrest in 2010. His plan was to design his family home and put his sustainability formula to the test: a home that would keep his family safe during a hurricane, respond sensitively to the landscape, and produce its own energy.

    Not only are the “Green Energy Home” sustainable building materials and construction methods tailored to beautifully endure South Florida’s warm and humid climate, the home’s exterior windows and doors are 100% recyclable, “blast tested” for explosions, and tested for an excess of 250 MPH winds

    A bifacial photovoltaic “Solar Trellis” will produce the majority of the home’s energy consumption, and a beautiful blue railing of solar hot water tubes will heat 100% of water for the home and pool. Other Eco-responsible design elements include a Green Roof, 5-zone automated AC system, and High-volume/Low-speed fans.

    Prior to designing the family home, Harper carefully studied the site layout and surrounding park and Biscayne Bay Nature Preserve in order to design the shape and flow of the home to respond sensitively to the landscape, not over-run it.

    Harper hopes that once completed, the home will serve as an educational tool on how to design a strong home in a tropical hurricane zone while still incorporating aesthetically pleasing energy-producing elements.

    The Green Energy Home is set to become the first LEED Gold Custom Home located in South Florida.

  • Energy Star Certified

    Department of Energy (DOE) and the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)
    • October 2012

    Green-Energy-Products.com, LLC, a small business that offers energy efficient products and services to consumers, has been accepted into the ENERGY STAR program because of G-E-P’s commitment to improving energy efficiency in the United States and across the world. The ENERGY STAR program is a joint program of the Department of Energy (DOE) and the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). The ENERGY STAR program was developed to help promote energy efficiency to the public, help protect the environment and lower energy costs for consumers.

    The DOE and EPA have made G-E-P a valued member of the ENERGY STAR program, in part, because the company offers many ENERGY STAR-certified products on its website http://green-energy-products.com. These products have been tested and measured to ensure that they meet the strict energy saving standards of the EPA and DOE.

    G-E-P is proud to join the ENERGY STAR program and will continue its mission to help homeowners and businesses achieve optimum energy savings by reducing energy usage and emissions, without sacrificing features, style or comfort.

    ENERGY STAR-certified products offered by G-E-P include: Energy Management Systems, LED Light Bulbs and Smart Thermostats.
