Kolina Cicero's Summary

I create a thriving social media presence for my clients by inserting them into digital conversations. I keep them relevant and interactive by consistently monitoring, commenting and responding to questions. With my services, my clients' social media platforms serve as a form of communication, publicity and customer service.

I have a profound understanding of the social media and digital marketplace. Familiarizing myself with clients' personalities and translating them into the online world is my daily mission. By consistently researching the marketplace, I introduce clients to relevant ideas and introduce the digital community to their brands.

Specialties: Social Media, Social Media Marketing, blogging, SEO, Campaign Management, Adobe CS5, writing, copy editing, Associated Press Style, press releases, Mac OS X, Microsoft Office, translating English to Italian/Italian to English.
