Tina and Carlos Cancio's Summary

Welcome to balance salon spa, a boutique AVEDA exclusive concept salon spa environment, created to help you escape the stresses of every day life. We offer balance to the community through our beauty and spa services in and around Tampa, Florida.

At balance salon spa, our team of naturally skilled and highly trained stylists and estheticians are experts in their field of beauty. balance salon spa is passionate about continuously striving to stay on the cutting edge of the latest advancements in the beauty industry. It is our desire to help you look and feel your very best, as we take great pride in our quality of service.

We invite you to indulge yourself with one of our relaxing hair and spa services. Choose from our extensive menu of natural botanically based, eco-friendly AVEDA product line of spa treatments and beautifying salon services, including a 24/7 Online Concierge for your convenience. Leave the outside world behind and let balance salon spa transport you to the ultimate salon spa experience.

Tina and Carlos Cancio's Experience

Owners and Founders

balance salon spa

Partnership; 11-50 employees; Cosmetics industry

2008 – Present (5 years)

Carlos Cancio has been with balance salon spa for the last 18 years. He has over 23 years in the hair care industry. He began his hair career while attending Engineering School in Cuba. Then, upon coming to America, he continued to further his creative God-given talent, following his passion for hair. It is truly an inspirational rise from extreme Communism to living the American Dream, that you only see in the movies.

In 1994, he became an assistant and barely spoke any English at all. He assisted shampoos and cleaned up the salon. In 1996, he began styling hair as a Junior Stylist. Just a year later, he became a Main Stylist, a position he kept for five years. In 2002, he became a Master Stylist. Now, he is an incredible stylist, businessman and Owner of the salon (since 2008) along with his wife, Tina Cancio.

Carlos has successfully completed numerous business and technical training programs; including those at the Vidal Sassoon Advanced Academy in San Francisco, CA; Tony & Guy, and the AVEDA Institute Training in Miami and St. Petersburg, FL.
