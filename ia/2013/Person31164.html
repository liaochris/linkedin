David Kurns' Experience

Editorial Content Director, Successful Farming

Meredith

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; MDP; Publishing industry

August 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Des Moines, Iowa Area

As head of the editorial team at Successful Farming, I am the steward of the brand that founded the Meredith Corporation in 1902.

Successful Farming is not just an agricultural publication, it is a multimedia platform that includes Agriculture.com and other websites, the Successful Farming Machinery Show on TV, a radio show, specialty magazines, social media, a mobile platform, and more.

The staff includes authoritative editors steeped in their content categories. It also includes talented design and production staff.

In short, this is a 110-year-old franchise with a startup mentality.

Editor in Chief, Meredith Interactive

Meredith

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; MDP; Publishing industry

1994 – 2006 (12 years)

Assistant Managing Editor, SIP

June 1989 – July 1994 (5 years 2 months)

I started as a copy editor in the Special Interest Publications division within the Publishing Group of Meredith. This group was the hotbed of innovative niche magazine titles in a period of great growth. I was then promoted to copy chief, then to Assistant Managing Editor.

News Editor

Quad-City Times

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Newspapers industry

June 1982 – May 1989 (7 years)

I started as a copy editor on the night news desk. After a couple of years, I was promoted to Night News Editor, heading up the entire night copy desk operation and responsible for Page 1 and the entire News section. I also was a Design Editor, responsible for the design staff and implementing a new pagination system for the news operation. I also spent time as Day News Editor while the newspaper had an afternoon edition.
