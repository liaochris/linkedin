Jamie Russell's Summary

Currently the Sr. Manager of Digital Communications at Chesapeake Energy. My responsibilities include the Interactive, Social Media and Multi-Media initiatives for both external and internal audiences. I manage teams who are responsible for the following.

Interactive responsibilities include:
- Creative direction for user experience and interaction design.
- Mobile initiatives and responsive web development.
- Email marketing campaigns and strategies.
- Search marketing initiatives - This includes both search engine optimization (SEO) and paid search.
- Corporate intranet design, content architecture and strategy.
- Data visualization project development including social media integration.
- Project management and working with third party vendors and service providers.

Social media responsibilities include:
- Monitoring and listening.
- Promotions and paid sponsorship management.
- Reporting on social activities.
- Coordinating efforts to regional teams across the country.
- Emergency response.

Multi-media responsibilities include:
- Project concepting and script approvals.
- Video production, editing, and directing.
