Mark Windham's Summary

Windham Business Services provides accounting, bookkeeping and advisory services for start-ups, sole entrepreneurs and companies up to 25 employees. Our focus is on accurate and timely data entry and easy to read and understand financial statements. We believe those statements are a valuable tool for running your business, not just a tax prep document.

I am a Certified QuicksBooks Pro Advisor with experience in all versions of the software, including Pro, Premier, Online and Enterprise Solutions. We also partner with XERO, an exciting new option in online accounting options. We offer combined firm experience of over 25 years in small business accounting and management.

Bookkeeping is the heart of our business just as a good set of books is at the heart of a successful business. We offer full charge bookkeeping as well as programs to maximize cash flow and profitability. We also offer training and support for QuickBooks and XERO.

We strongly believe in the concept of our clients being able to work ON their business instead of IN it. We provide tools to allow them to eliminate needless tasks, make better informed decisions and be more successful. We are able to transform our clients from "shoebox" accounting to well informed entrepreneurs who are growing their business.

Specialties: QuickBooks, XERO, Bookkeeping, Accounting, Coaching/Consulting, Business Management, Cost controls, Operations Management, HR, Financial Management and Controls
