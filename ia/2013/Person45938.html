Fred Celestino's Summary

I love what I do, even when I am not doing it.

If there’s a question a designer should always ask him or herself when confronted with a blank canvas it would be: “How is my work going to alter someone else’s life today?” I believe Design’s purpose is to make people’s lives better, easier, funnier, more efficient, less painful, more economic or profitable, and so on.

For the past 7 years or so, I’ve been asking myself that question and it’s been a fun journey – most of the time.

I believe in Humans and Startups.
