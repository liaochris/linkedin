Todd Stone's Summary

As an Operations Analyst, I pull data to provide reports to show the volumes and statues of operations in the Loss Draft department to upper management. I also work with the Process Management Improvement team to assist with data and improvement projects.

As a Six Sigma Green Belt, I have competed two project one of which had an annual savings of over $90K. I will be attending Black Belt Training in 2012.

Along with process improvement projects and data analysis, I build Microsoft Access Databases to improve data gathering and management for various departments within Loss Drafts.

I am currently attending Urbana University pursing a Bachelor of Science in Business Management.

When I'm not working at Assurant, I am working as a photographer with Family Stone Photography (FSP). FSP is a joint venture with my wife, Farrah. We specialize in family photography to include babies, children, families (small and large groups), engagements, sporting events (Team & Individual Photos), and special occasion such as birthday parties, or other large events.

We had the honor of photographing the 1st Annual "Making Strides Against Breast Cancer" event held in Springfield, Ohio in 2011and are also proud to be a part of the Operation Love:Reunited organization that provides photography services to Military families.

For 2012, we will be the official photographer for a newly formed little league program in Dublin Ohio, the Dublin Bats. There are currently 120 kids involved with the program, and we're very excited for the opportunity. We got our start with sports by being invited to be the team photographer for the Springfield Angels Men's Roy Hobbs amateur team for their debut season in 2011.
