Jane Ingalls' Summary

Do you have an audience to reach with a voiceover?

Our voices are everywhere; on the radio, your TV, and on the new website you just found. We direct you via GPS, challenge you in a video game, and yes, occasionally ask you to hit pound on the phone.

I can move you to care, to learn and to feel. I entice you to buy, to play, and to think a little more deeply about what matters to you.

Most of all, I want your message to be heard.

To find out what my voice can do for your company, please listen to the downloadable demos right here on my profile.
