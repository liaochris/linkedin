Martin H. Laderman's Summary

mem property management, one of the fastest growing private companies in America (#556 on the prestigious Inc. 5000), is looking to acquire other property management companies of any size in New Jersey. Please contact me privately for further details. All inquiries will be kept strictly confidential.

In 1982, I joined a New Jersey real estate management firm with responsibility for the daily management of 25,000 residential units. I recognized the importance and need for exceptional professional property management and started mem property management in 1984.

I am proud to be the CEO and President of the premier NJ property management company.

I believe in a hands-on, personal, team approach to property management, recognizing the individual requirements of each property and tailoring our management program to meet those unique needs. With a results-driven approach and a never-ending aim for capital improvements, we continue to provide outstanding value and service to all New Jersey properties that we manage and strive to maintain the comfort, beauty, and safety of each community we serve.

I am a member of National Association of Residential Property Managers,, Community Associations Institute (CAI); and I am actively involved with the New Jersey Association of Realtors legislative activities. In 1999, I completed the “Essentials of Community Association Management" and I am currently in the process of obtaining the Association Management Specialist (AMS) and Professional Community Association Management (PCAM) Designation.
