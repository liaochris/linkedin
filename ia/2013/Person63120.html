California Bank of Commerce is a “start-up” bank, focusing on commercial mid-market clients, headquartered in Lafayette, CA. This bank is known for superior client service, innovative products, advanced technology and hyper growth.

This was a unique opportunity to further my ambitions of gaining more experience in operations, corporate marketing, client management and finance. This "startup" bank was an extremely agile, creative environment. I came on board not long after they opened, and was chosen to take on many responsibilities and departments.

After being recruited to manage operations, I quickly took to the cross-functional, collaborative environment, and was able to solve many organizational and service problems. I was challenged and performed 'beyond expectations' in business operations, marketing communications, investor relations, and general management. We enjoyed hyper-growth, year after year.

I enjoyed my time with the Bank and excelled quickly, but after four years, I made the decision to take my life and career in a different direction. I decided that it was best for me to pursue my passion in a career that was more entrepreneurial, to advance and develop my creativity and talents in strategy and marketing, within the technology industry.

Perhaps I can call it, “following my dream”. But, I examined and criticized this new focus of mine, in the light of reality. I searched and I found. And I found that it not only made sense, but it meant that I was finally mastering my talents.
