Damon Brinson's Summary

My practice focuses on the defense of manufacturers in products liability litigation, especially pharmaceutical, medical device, and industrial equipment manufacturers. I also have significant experience with commercial litigation involving contracts, financial transactions, and federal regulatory compliance.

I have experience arguing motions before the court, taking and defending depositions, writing motions and appellate briefs, managing large-scale ESI and document discovery, and drafting discovery requests and responses.

Damon Brinson's Experience

Attorney

Bowman and Brooke LLP

Partnership; 201-500 employees; Law Practice industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months)

Bowman and Brooke LLP is a nationally recognized trial firm that defends corporations in complex litigation.

Most of our clients are household-name companies involved in widely publicized product liability cases. Because of our trial experience--taking cases to verdict and winning--the firm has grown beyond its roots in the automotive industry. Today, Bowman and Brooke offers extraordinary performance in:

- drug and medical device product liability litigation
- heavy manufacturing product liability litigation
- healthcare litigation
- consumer products liability & warranty litigation
- patent and infringement litigation
- business and commercial litigation

Notwithstanding our diverse backgrounds and the various industries that we serve, every attorney at Bowman and Brooke has one thing in common: we are trial lawyers who manage the biggest and highest-profile product liability, recall and commercial cases all over the country. We defend our clients before Congress, regulators, judges and juries. Bowman and Brooke has offices in Minneapolis, Phoenix, Detroit, San Jose, Los Angeles, Richmond, Columbia, Dallas, and Austin.

I practice in the area of product liability defense, with a special focus on the representation of pharmaceutical, medical device, and industrial equipment manufacturers. In addition, I have significant experience with commercial litigation involving contracts, financial transactions, and federal regulatory compliance.
