Mike Piork's Summary

My goals are to network with foward moving individuals and help as many people as I can with their real estate needs.

I specialize in Probate, Conservatorship, Guardianship and Trusts. I am a Certified Probate Real Estate Specialist through MTI and US Probate Services. I also am a Down Payment Assistance Specialist. Come visit my website, ArizonaProbateLiquidators.org. It is a resource site to: 1) educate you on the probate process as a Personal Representative, 2) access to a FREE Directory of Services (Funeral Homes. Cemetaries, Attorneys, CPA's, Realtors, Handyman, Movers, etc...) needed in the Probate process, 3) info on timelines and forms (some are sample forms) you can use to organize your inventory. I as a realtor am interested in listing your home and get you the best possible price and in the shortest period of time. Ask me about my FREE COMPARATIVE MARKET ANALYSIS which is like no other. I call it the CMA on steroids. My site is a one stop shop of different service professionals under one roof to help you finalize the estate quickly, so you can save more money to distribute to the heirs. After all you know that the probate process is very expensive. NOTE: NO SERVICE PROVIDERS PAY TO BE ON THE SITE! I just hope to get referrals from the service providers while helping Personal Representatives save time, hassle and money. I also have a list of first-time home buyers and investors that are (some are cash buyers) waiting to buy! Sometimes they make an offer to buy everything at once: Real Estate, cars, unwanted personal items, etc... Sell it all at once to save you the hassles of probate and close the estate quickly and save a lot of money.

I am also a Down Payment Assistance Specialist that can help homebuyers purchase a home from you quickly! Check my sister website AzRealEstateKing.com (currently under construction).

Do you own Real Estate out of state? No Problem! Our nationwide network of professional Realtors can help you with your Real Estate needs!
