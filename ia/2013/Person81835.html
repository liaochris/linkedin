James Willcox's Summary

Owner and founder of Live in Languages Ltd, which was established in 1997 and has gone from strength to strength, now offering ‘homestay’ English tuition for international students.

The company accommodates individual adult learners from across the globe in ESL tutors’ homes, with comprehensive English instruction delivered through short, intensive programmes.

Clients of Live in Languages enjoy the unique experience of both living and studying in their teacher's home. By specialising in this particular area of English language tuition, the aim is to provide them with an environment where constant, natural learning takes place in a relaxed atmosphere.

Programmes are specifically designed for serious adult learners who recognise that the ideal way to learn English is to immerse themselves totally in the language. The undivided attention of a personal teacher also means that lessons can be adapted to suit specific needs. Live in Languages offers a number of Business English courses in which clients are prepared for English in the workplace.

Clients can choose to enjoy the superb benefits of the ‘homestay’ experience in diverse locations throughout the UK, USA (New York, Miami, Seattle, San Francisco and Hawaii) and Canada (Vancouver and Victoria).

Please contact James if you have a client interested in a unique homestay program with Live in Languages. He offers a generous commission for referrals. James would also be delighted to hear from you if you are a qualified ESL tutor interested in teaching and hosting an adult student in your home

For further information visit: www.liveinlanguages.com
