Brett Dupree is career path is in the software industry. He has been blessed with many great jobs. it did not spark the passion in life that he desired. Software testing is a job that he enjoys, and is blessed to have worked and is currently working in such an amazingly diverse industry.

Through years of soul searching and transformational inner work, he has noticed that his purpose is to use his intuitive gift and his genius in being introspective to work with people to unlock their hidden potentials. That is why he started a side career as a life coach.

With his deep loving heart and a strong masculine love, he works with people to live their lives with joy and purpose. Living a life intentionally on purpose is something that you can do. You are not on this planet to work at a job you hate, numb yourself with alcohol, television, drugs, joyless sex, unfulfilled relationships, and then retire and die.

Brett has discovered and is living his purpose right now as a Transformational Life Coach. It is his best way to use his intuitive and introspective gifts, further his capacity to unconditionally love, and help make the world better by working with YOU to unleash YOUR gifts, to benefit your current or future children, community, city, state, country, and the entire world.

While currently working in the wonderful world of software and professional life coach, one thing he understands is the hardship of creating a new business while currently working in a great job that pays the bills and the fear that can come from making big transitions. With joy and love he can help you make your transition as fun and as quick as possible.
