Jason Guinter's Summary

I am an enthusiastic Media Professional with a fresh approach to technology training. Born in Northern Pennsylvania I moved to San Diego after receiving a Bachelors of Science Degree from Mansfield University. My focus: Mass Media Communications, I concentrated my awareness on cutting edge technology and grasped the "digital transition" first hand while working with Tribune Broadcasting's KSWB-TV in San Diego.

After twelve years with networks like NBC and FOX I see Information Technology in its rawest form, I've trained beyond television production and developed the skills necessary to manipulate and design on a World Wide Web platform, while traveling the world utilizing the tools to do so.

I direct, among other shows, a live prime time sports broadcast following NBC's Sunday Night Football featuring current and former members of the NFL. Additionally, & most recently, I've been designated the Production Supervisor for SoundDiego TV: A music magazine show based around San Diego's rich music scene which airs after Saturday Night Live.

I am also the author of LTD: Living the Dream, and pursue freelance writing as a passion having written several dozen works on experiences when traveling to various locations around the globe.
