Anthony Kersey's Summary

Throughout my life I have been influenced and intrigued by advertising as well as marketing promotions. These are two things that really make me tick.

I initially started my career in advertising, and promotions when I was 15. My dedication and passion for a company (Flash Productions, Inc.) that my friends and I started made me fall in love with the world of business promotions in all aspects. It was difficult initially gaining recognition from the professional world as an adolescent; however our persistence, constant professionalism, dedication, and quality of our work is what set us apart in our industry.
