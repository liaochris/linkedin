Carrie Wicks' Summary

With more than fifteen years of professional editorial experience in mostly creative fields as an editorial assistant and proofreader, I seek freelance proofreading/copyediting work with publishers, authors, graphic design firms or ad agencies, and universities. Very familiar with Chicago style and Merriam Webster's, I can help weed, prune, and clean copy in preparation for wider distribution. I fix spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors; notice inconsistencies in style and typography; call out unnecessary redundancies; fact-check proper nouns; and query awkward constructions, tone, etc.

Specialties: proofreading/copyediting books (nonfiction: art books and cookbooks; fiction); indexes; design materials such as catalogs, newsletters, brochures, and annual reports; newspapers; poetry; magazines; literary publications; websites; game components and packaging; PowerPoint presentations; proposals; accreditation or other reports; education literacy materials
