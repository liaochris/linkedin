Eric Foster's Summary

Highly accomplished Information Technology manager and executive with over 17 years of IT experience and over 13 combined years of leadership experience.

Specialist in information security, risk management and regulatory compliance, especially financial services regulatory compliance, including PCI-DSS, Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX), GLBA, ISO 27001 / 27002, and HIPAA.

Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and RSA Archer (GRC) Certified Professional Consultant.

Entrepreneur with 14 years of business development experience. Prior startup experience includes MMAjunkie.com (sold to USA Today) and eScout.com (sold to PerfectCommerce) as well as advisory board experience to multiple startups including Positive Networks (sold to Microsoft).

Specialties: Information Security, Regulatory Compliance, PCI-DSS, GLBA, Sarbanes-Oxley, ISO 27001 / 27002, risk management, Archer GRC, Governance Risk and Compliance, technology architecture and planning, consulting, financial services, policy and procedure.
