Lisa Dunham, MBA, CDPE's Summary

More than 10 years of professional real estate services serving an array of clients in northern VA and MD. I specialize in assisting home buyers and sellers with residential sales to include single and multi-level condominiums, town homes, and single family homes. Whether you are a first-time home buyer or have sold many homes in the past, I have the expertise and experience to facilitate your next real estate transaction.

Short Sales and Foreclosures: As a Certified Distressed Property Expert (CDPE), I have the unique skills and qualifications needed to assist today's home buyers and sellers with the purchase and sale of short sale and foreclosed properties. If you have been thinking of buying a short sale or foreclosure or if you would like to confidentially discuss the possibility of selling your home as a short sale to avoid foreclosure, contact me for an immediate appointment.

Prior to real estate, I maintained a successful career in the field of marketing and business development. For more than 10 years, I worked with some of the area’s most prestigious architectural, engineering, and information technology (IT) firms supporting a variety of projects at The Pentagon, Ronald Reagan Building, U.S. Customs, Department of Justice, and the U.S. Army, to name a few. After a successful tenure with multiple firms, I embarked upon a career as an independent consultant where I contributed to multiple firms simultaneously by providing marketing, business development, and proposal management services.
