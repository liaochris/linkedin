Lori Absher's Summary

GROWING
ideas + strategy = results + profits

Creative thinking brings together artistic creation and the practical world of commerce. Answering the "why" question and zeroing in on unique, owned qualities provide the core foundation for all good design solutions. Clarifying these objectives, creating a list and developing a strategy—guide the design to produce the desired results.

I thrive detailing client needs to develop a concept and strategy then executing the design with color theory, typography and photo manipulation. Determining the spirit of the message and visual voice links the product to the audience. I excel in creating an owned quality in visual products including logo and brand identity with collateral, editorial publications, brochures, direct mail, tradeshow graphics, web design and presentations.

Specialities include Business, Tourism, Government + Nonprofit
