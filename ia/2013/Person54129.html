Hans van Dreumel's Summary

I am an all-rounder with a vast experience in and knowledge of Wolters Kluwers online services/platforms. Background as a developer of webapplications and content management systems, since several years in the role of domain architect.
In this capacity I'm developing, maintaining and optimizing the IT architecture within the scope of the Kluwer domain Online research.
My responsibilities are the right implementation of requirements in online projects, with a focus on quality, generic development, reuse, performance, availability, future proof design and cost effectiveness.
I am working in close cooperation with collegue domain and enterprise architects and advise (project) management about IT solutions with IT-trends, policy and other experiences in mind.
