Adam Maxwell's Summary

Though I took my first jobs in the industry in the late 80s and early 90s, working as an intern and QA tester for companies such as SSI and Interplay, I really got my start as a professional game developer in 1995, when I joined Blizzard Entertainment.

Since then, I've worked in the industry in numerous roles, from QA and production to design and senior management. I pride myself on being a strong shaper of vision, able to take a game idea through every stage of the process, from initial concept to launch and into live operations. With a strong mind for abstract game analysis and decades of experience applied broadly throughout the industry, I have a unique perspective on what makes games, and the teams that make them, great.

This perspective has given me the honor of forging some of the industry's best development teams while developing pioneering game concepts and tools across nearly all game platforms to be released in the last fifteen years.

Whether you want a strong visionary to build your team and ship your game, or a consultant to fight your fires and aid your team on its way to success, look me up. I am ready and able to help you.

Specialties

Game Development, Creative Direction, Design Team Building/Management, Game Design, Content Creation, Writing, Game Architecture, Game Tool Design, Project Analysis/Consultation
