Andrea Forro {Andrea.Forro@HDS.com}'s Summary

Seasoned, certified and highly adaptable full life-cycle senior recruiter with diverse experience recruiting in support of Commercial organizations as well as Federal, Intel, Civilian and DoD government agencies. Extensive experience in cleared/non-cleared candidate and commercial sector sourcing and placement and OFCCP/EEO compliant recruiting practices. Strong background utilizing COTS and proprietary CTS/ATS systems (Taleo, Jobvite, Brassring) and solid data mining expertise (X-ray, flipsearch, passive recruiting). Specialize in the recruitment and placement of IT, software development,networking, information security, business development and executive level professionals. Current and former clients have included Hitachi Data Systems, Microsoft Federal and Enterprise Services, NSA, DIA, DoD, DOJ, Verizon, Inforeliance Corp., N.E.W., CACI, CSC, Amazon, Carpathia, AOL, TSA/DHS/CBP and Knowledge Consulting Group.

Specialties: Successful and proven record screening and placing multiple skill-sets including: Sr. Sales/Account Managers/Global Account Managers,Data Center/Virtualization/Cloud/Network architects, engineers and administrators, application development professionals (.Net, Java, Open Source, E-commerce, UI, middleware, back-end), Network monitoring and mgt. professionals (HP, BAC/BSM, DDMI) database design and admin professionals, Microsoft technical experts ( Dynamics, Lync, OCS, Exchange, Sharepoint, SCCM, PFE's, TAM's).
