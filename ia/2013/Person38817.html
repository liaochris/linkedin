Dustin Reichert's Summary

This is my 3rd career. Originally in Restaurant Management, I left that to do what I had always longed for, which was Law Enforcement. After a great almost 10 year career in Law Enforcement, I suffered a serious injury in an incident and medically retired from the Anoka County Sheriff's Office.

My neighbor owned a DJ company and with a history and love of music and public performance, I began to work for him and found my new passion. Since the owner had other ventures going on, I was able to arrange to purchase Spirit Unlimited. After a few years, we've re-structured and up and up from there.
