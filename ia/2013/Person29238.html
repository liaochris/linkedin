Danelle Brown's Summary

Queen Bee Consulting provides business coaching services, accountability programs, strategic marketing plans for online and offline and social media implementation to small business. I also am a Certified Book Yourself Solid Business Coach, Certified Asset Based Thinking Coach and a Certified Author's Consultant. I love to help entrepreneurs get their book published.

I am also the CEO of MB Tech Inc, an IT consulting company that specializes in anything from basic troubleshooting, websites, blogs, and setting up complex systems. We work with Macs, PCs, Unix and just about any other system out there. We focus on the small to medium size businesses who do not have an IT department of their own.

Specialties: business coaching, strategic planning, marketing, accountability sessions, social media marketing plans, guiding authors through the publishing process, branding, defining target market, establishing a sales cycle, Wordpress, websites, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest training
