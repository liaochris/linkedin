Chris Gould's Summary

Chris Gould has been in HR consulting since 1996 and is considered an industry expert in social media, sourcing and recruiting. He is known for his ability to influence, execute, motivate and implement change within complex matrix environments. He has been responsible for global teams as large as 175+ people delivering 12,000 corporate hires and 85,000 client hires. He is the Chair of the Corporate Sourcing Leaders Group and was the keynote speaker at SourceCon 2010. He has also presented Sourcing Leadership workshops at SourceCon 2011 and the Corporate Sourcing Leadership Exchange 2012. Chris is a requested speaker on topics related to social networking and sourcing and his work has resulted in press interest including The Wall Street Journal and the SHRM HR Magazine. He is also the founder of the Kansas City Online Community, an online community with over 7,000 members. Chris has also been on the advisory boards for SelectMinds and Bountyjobs.

Specialties: global talent acquisition, sourcing, social media, recruiting, recruitment, negotiation, Talent Intelligence, analytics, operational strategy, new project start-ups, AIRS, CIR, BPO, RPO, internet mining, research, campus recruiting, international, change management, healthcare, employment branding, technology, Taleo, process improvement, talent communities, mobile, boolean, LION, Twitter, Aon, Hewitt, Accenture, Andersen, Chicago, Kansas City, New York, India, London, Toronto, Poland, Europe
