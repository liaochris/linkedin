Eric Eng's Summary

Design is Passion

Eric Eng is a cross-disciplinary designer working between the US and UK. Native to Chicago, Eric’s work spans the areas of identity and strategic branding, environmental, exhibition, web, interactive, video and publishing.

Following his training at the Maine College of Art in 2004, Eric continued his academic development in London. In 2008 he received his Master’s in graphic design from the London College of Communication (formerly London College of Printing). While completing his studies in London, he was assistant lecturer at LCC, BA/MA dissertation advisor, letterpress researcher at St Bride Library and consulted with various architectural practices and cultural institutions. Eric is currently based in New York City working on a variety of branding and interactive projects.

Eric’s passion for typographic form and user-centered design can be recognised throughout his work, having been published in the UK, US, Serbia and China. His interest in languages and communication have driven his engagement with international projects. His work looks to balance client-based projects with self-initiated explorations, allowing each to intersect in an innovative, process-led approach.

The wealth of musical and artistic training throughout his career have supported the belief that design is a way of life.
