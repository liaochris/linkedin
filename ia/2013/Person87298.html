Marlene Chism's Summary

My name is Marlene Chism and I'm a consultant, speaker and author of Stop Workplace Drama, which was commercially published by Wiley and Sons in 2011 and continues to open doors to meet interesting people and work with incredible companies.

The magic for me when someone picks up my book and engages me to work with them is that I get to learn about all kinds of industries from manufacturing to health care to private practice to public safety, and I get to travel to interesting places around the country.

One reason many of us experience more internal drama is due to being overwhelmed by the speed at which we operate. There is virtually no space between stimulus and response. The result is road rage, impulsive acts of violence, incivility, impatience, stress and absence of well-being. We believe in the myth. We think if we work faster and harder then there will be time to do what is really important. We see no choices, even though we are drowning in a sea of choice.

Peter Drucker, one of the greatest management thinkers of all time predicted the outcome of these changes: For the first time, literally substantial and rapidly growing numbers of people have choices. For the first time, they will have to manage themselves, and society is totally unprepared for it.

Our changing times call for enlightened leadership at all levels: From the CEO to the Supervisor, from the classroom to the boardroom.

Enlightened leadership requires of us a new way of thinking and believing; a new way of seeing and speaking; and ultimately a new way of being.

It will be enlightened leaders who promote social change in our corporations, our communities and in our classrooms.

My clients achieve profitability through clarity and alignment, dramatically improved workplace relationships, increased personal effectiveness and focus and stronger communication dynamics.

I'm interested in working with leaders from all industries, executives, and business owners.
