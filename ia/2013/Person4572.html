Alijohn Ghassemlouei's Publications

  • The Hacker’s Guide to OS X - Exploiting OS X from the Root up

    • Syngress
    • November 1, 2012
    Authors: Alijohn Ghassemlouei, Russ Rogers, Rob Bathurst

    Written by two experienced penetration testers the material presented discusses the basics of the OS X environment and its vulnerabilities. Including but limited to; application porting, virtualization utilization and offensive tactics at the kernel, OS and wireless level. This book provides a comprehensive in-depth guide to exploiting and compromising the OS X platform while offering the necessary defense and countermeasure techniques that can be used to stop hackers

  • Operational White Listing: Proactive Malware Prevention Software

    • The ISSA Journal | Volume 8 Issue 6
    • June 2010
    Authors: Alijohn Ghassemlouei, Garrett Pickering

    This article explores current antivirus methodologies, obstacles that may occur during development, and a white listing proof-of-concept plan. The current methods for antivirus protection are flawed, limited to finding malware through pattern recognition via established malware libraries. This is acceptable for home users, perhaps, but businesses need tougher options. White listing is deemed the best method for fixing the problems of current antivirus software, using a proactive approach to halt attacks before they start. The technology uses a list of approved programs that can run on client machines, whittling down misuse of company equipment and adding protection against malware.
