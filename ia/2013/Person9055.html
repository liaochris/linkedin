Anubhav Misra's Summary

Anubhav Misra is an Indian Motion Graphic Designer based in Mumbai.
He holds an Advanced Diploma degree in Animation & Visual Effects from Maya Academy of Advanced Cinematics, Mumbai. Academically he has a background in science, but he chose Motion Graphics Design as a career out of sheer curiosity. Little did he know that it would become his calling. He commands many of the required software programs used in the industry and is a keen learner ready to learn more and more tools to increase his skillset and get variety in his work in terms of look and feel and prevent it from looking monotonous over a period of time.
