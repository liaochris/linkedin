Michael D. Wailes' Summary

After over a decade in print and radio news, I decided to pursue a career in software/web development in the online/digital marketing space. The web has become such an integral part of our lives and yet the development of it is just hovering above the horizon.

I’ve taken a strong interest in marketing analysis, specifically in the area of website analytics, and have begun providing some analysis to our customers at Burns Marketing Communications. One of my short to mid-term goals is to not only develop our budding web analytics service into one of the premier services in the region, providing our clients with unparalleled and unprecedented insights into their markets, but also be a key player in the digital marketing space. It’s exciting to see how the strategic implementation of web analytics and web marketing are having such an impact on the development of the medium—from faith based design and implementation to a data centric development environment.

In addition to my duties as a web developer, I am also heavily involved with the email marketing and social media efforts of our current clients—providing analysis of campaigns and advisement of optimization methods to our team.

My heart still lies with media production and I’m finding that my current position is providing me the opportunities to carry on my passion for sharing information in a timely, yet accurate manner.
