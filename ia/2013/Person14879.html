Blake Irving's Summary

A proven and seasoned leader of customer-focused organizations, Blake Irving has consistently delivered innovative products, organizational excellence, and business results. Blake’s 30-year career in technology has been defined by developing product and business plans, building organizational capabilities to execute those plans, and scaling up the business. Throughout his career, Blake has led international organizations in technical product development, marketing, business development, strategic planning, support and operations. Blake understands how to scale organizations to deliver results world-wide. He has led and grown organizations that range in size from a handful to many thousands. He has grown revenue streams from a million to a billion, and has grown customer bases from zero into the hundreds of millions around the world.

Blake and his organizations have taken initiatives from startup to scale operations in a variety of focus areas including Instant Messaging, Internet Mail, Internet Mobile Services, Blogging, Social Networking, Video Conferencing, Internet Phone, Internet Collaboration, Secure Login, Payment and Billing Systems, and Internet Advertising Systems. Blake has also led teams in Operating System Software Development, Communication Client Software, and PC Hardware Product Lines.

Blake is known as a team builder and collaborator, working across organizations, companies and industries to achieve business results and market leadership. Blake is thought of as a pragmatic, empathetic, intuitive, and decisive leader, who attracts talent, develops people, listens well, digs for details, and makes decisions in the face of ambiguity. Blake leads by example and develops an environment of open and direct communication throughout the organization, creating tighter relationships between sales, marketing, development and customers.
