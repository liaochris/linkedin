John Mourraille's Summary

I am pleased to be a part of the Market Advisors group of companies;

Market Advisors LLC through Market Advisors Capital Group is a capital strategy group specializing in consulting with small companies to organize and fund their investment capital requirements to fuel their growth.

Market Advisors are also specialists in assisting individual investors to direct their retirement funds into specialized opportunities that are available through private equity securities. We maintain professional relationships with key Self Directed Custodians such as; AccuPlan, PENSCO Trust, Entrust and others.

We believe in investing in "Main Street America" as it is the backbone to the wealth and fiscal health for our Communities. Private Equity investments into high growth corporate opportunities or real estate based specialized assets are powerful ways for Investors to seek out significant gains and diversify portfolios.

Market Advisors Realty Inc. specializes in Real Estate investments; trustee sales, non-performing note ownership, and tax sales.

PHD College Funding provides services to Families to assist in navigating the complexities that is; "how to best pay for College and which Colleges and Universities offer the best financial packages". We are experts in maximizing the award from financial aid, whether it be merit scholarships, or need based grants.
