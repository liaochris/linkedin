Matt Hoggatt's Summary

Matt is responsible for driving the growth and evolution of Adknowledge’s Performance Marketing businesses which includes its List Management, Integrated API, and Affiliate Offers solutions. Matt has worked in Technology and Marketing Services Management for over 13 years and brings a broad base of experience delivering enterprise solutions to market. Matt received his MBA from UALR and a BS in Computer Engineering from the University of Arkansas.

Adknowledge operates the 4th largest digital advertising marketplace with unique ad formats, data, insights, targeting algorithms and creative approaches, which provides advertisers with quality leads from hard to reach places. These include social networks, display, games, mobile, apps and email. With hundreds of 'ROI Junkie' employees located throughout North America, plus growing offices in Europe and Asia, Adknowledge aims to set the standard in optimizing online advertiser ROI. More about us:

• Offices in Kansas City, Los Angeles, San Francisco, New York, Ft. Myers, Toronto, London, and Shanghai
• Annual revenue of ~$300M and very profitable
• An all-common cap table, with JMI, TPG, TCV and Nokia as minority investors
• Recognized by CNBC as “the next Google”
• Recognized by Silicon Alley Insider as the 22nd most valuable start-up in the world

Adknowledge is continuing to expand its presence in the US and international markets. We seek out the best talent from an increasingly global and diverse labor pool. To APPLY ONLINE and learn more details about new opportunities, visit our careers page @ http://www.adknowledge.com/about/careers
