Kelly Jansens' Summary

Kelly has maintained a variety of positions including project manager, account manager and sales representative with exhibit houses. She has had the opportunity to coordinate services and direct internal customers, vendors, designers and architects to successful project completion. Furthermore, she has had the privilege to work with many international companies including x-rite, Steelcase, Nucraft Furniture as well as locally based companies such as The Grand Rapids Art Museum, Spectrum Heart Center, Grand Valley State University, Bronson Hospital and The David D. Hunting YMCA.

Kelly gained a wide array of experience in a marketing department directing advertising campaigns, copy writing, graphic layout, photo shoot, video direction as well as event and trade show management.

Kelly holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Advertising and Public Relations from Grand Valley State University.

She volunteers locally and has served on local association boards for the Advertising Federation of West Michigan, Make a Wish Foundation, the Humane Society of West Michigan, Gilda's Club and Juiceball/Kids Food Basket.

Kelly is an avid snowboarder and enjoys participating in the arts and music. She enjoys spending time with her Black Labrador Retriever named "Bodie," which her company, Black Dog Productions, is named.

She also founded a company called, Green Dog Pet Accessories, which produces Eco-friendly pet accessories, pet cleaners and sells natural/healthy pet food and treats.

Specialties: Trade Show, Showroom and Special Events Project Management
