Jesse Friedman's Summary

I am currently the Director of Web Interface and Development at Astonish. At Astonish I run the web development team, a group of 8 highly talented designers and developers. We specialize in building scalable, responsive, highly effective lead generating WordPress Themes. These themes integrate with our WordPress powered Astonish Web Framework. I built the framework in 2012 and since then, it has been improved upon by several other developers at Astonish.

In addition to the Web Framework, I am the inventor of the Multi-Line Center. The MLC is an enhanced web form that utilizes an intutive algorithm, delivering personalized real time content. Basically, the MLC serves users content before they know what they're asking for. It's intent based marketing at it's finest.

The largest project of my career is currently underway and should be done later this year. We are in the process of programatically migrating 600 websites from a proprietary .NET based CMS into WordPress. It's an amazing task and something that I haven't seen done before.

I have done a lot of work in WordPress over the years, and it's not just at Astonish. I started using WordPress in 2004 and now it's the only framework I use, no matter how small or large the site is.

I am the author of the Web Designers Guide to WordPress - http://wdgwp.com/onamazon and I have a new book coming out this winter called Internet Business Strategies.

I speak all over the country on WordPress, Real Time Site Personalization and Creative Leadership.

I am a huge advocate for web standards and open source solutions. I refuse to reinvent the wheel if I can help it, especially if I can rely on a community of designers and developers working on something together.
