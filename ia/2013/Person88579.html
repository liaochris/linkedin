Matt Brunini's Summary

Founder of branding, marketing, and communications firm with specific expertise in investment and financial services. Over 15 years of experience in developing award-winning initiatives for some of the most prominent companies around the globe across a wide range of industries, including Russell Investments, AIG/SunAmerica, CBRE, and Principal Financial.

Expert in all forms of content creation and branding strategy, from sophisticated investment products and processes, to campaign development across real estate, entertainment, and consumer products. Founded agency solely focused on communications within financial and investment services at MBC Strategic (www.mbcstrategic.com).

Created and managed successful marketing and branding campaigns and tools, including strategy, project/team management, plan creation and execution, copy writing, core messaging, design, art direction, websites, web content, research reports, internal/external communications, annual reports, business plans, capital raises, and material development across print, multimedia, and web.

After launching first agency in 1998, has worked extensively with large-, mid-, and small-sized companies in multiple industries. Built an expert team that can handle any size project--from logo design and mid-market brand development to award-winning national marketing campaigns, large-scale websites and branding initiatives.

Sample projects: large-scale investment company brand development and brand extension, institutional investment messaging strategy and platforms, online training/education brand launch, investment product and process positioning and white papers, alternative investment websites, entertainment rebrands and website development, main stage presentations, sports management identity materials and advertising, real estate investment branding, investment bank branding, music and media company websites, active asset allocation white paper, hedge fund white papers, HNW investing research report.

Specialties

Investment and financial services branding, marketing, and public relations services and solutions. Marketing materials that raise awareness. Also have expertise with consumer brands, entertainment, advertising, production, B2B and B2C service providers, law firms, lifestyle, music, sports, and real estate.

Logo design, corporate identity materials, web design and development, PR strategy, sales collateral, brochures, presentations, content development/copy writing, reports, and white papers.
