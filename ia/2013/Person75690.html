Kim Bresden's Summary

White Sands Pro Media, Inc. Strategic Business & Marketing consulting. Developing a clear understanding of your ideal client, what message to deliver to them and which channels are best suited for that delivery are the basis for our services. Creating effective campaigns, enhancing your company presence online and across multiple media to extend your reach in your market are all critical to your success. Essentially, we help your business acquire more customers by integrating technology and marketing strategies so "ideal" customers can find you, learn about you, begin to trust your message and ultimately buy from you and only from you.

Once the business goals and strategies are determined, we also offer "done for you" development and continuous management services for the various components of the campaign. Web development, Mobile, Video & Social Media Marketing services are available to meet any need. Mobile optimized websites, SMS/Text, Voice messaging and "App" development enable you to reach your customers wherever they may be searching for you, your products and services.


Our tag line says it all; "New Leads, More Sales, No Hype"...

Specialties: Business Growth Strategies, Structuring and managing website optimization, video & mobile campaigns, social media campaigns. Presenting and speaking to groups/associations, platform speaking.
