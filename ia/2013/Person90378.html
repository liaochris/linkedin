Mary McCarthy's Summary

A serial entrepreneur with a desire to develop, plan and grow small businesses for myself and others.

Small business ownership can be exciting, challenging and at times all-consuming. Even if you are a solopreneur you do not have to be alone in your business. Reach out and surround yourself with experts that will help you achieve your vision for yourself and your business.

Your Management Team, Inc. is a small business resource providing consulting, business plan development and assistance finding funding options for your business.

The Women's Small Business Accelerator is an incubator program for women business owners offering office space, mentoring and education.

Our consultants use their knowledge and experience as business owners to work one-on-one with you to create strategies for success and hold you accountable for implementation and success.
