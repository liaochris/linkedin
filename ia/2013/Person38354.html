Steve Hruby's Summary

Licensed and practicing in Arizona since 2001. It is the mission of Kai-Zen to bring uncompromising service to our clients. We strive to exceed our clients expectations; in turn allowing our clients to exceed their own expectations in their pursuit for health and wellness. We are committed to leading, educating, and empowering our community to take the next steps to live higher quality, more fulfilling, healthier lives.
Our promise is that we won't waste your time and we won't waste your money. If we can't help you, we will do our best to find you someone who can.

Specialties

Chiropractic, Lifestyle programs, Cooperate Wellness programs, Nutrition, Education and Empowerment of clients.
