Krishna De's Summary

I'm passionate about guiding businesses in how to build buzz and word of mouth by integrating traditional and new media into their marketing, communications and engagement programmes.

My breadth of global experience having held positions on the board of some of the worlds biggest brands places me in a unique position to help you in achieving your strategic goals.

► Digital Marketing Strategy and Mentoring - I work with my clients and their digital agencies behind the scenes helping them understand how to develop a winning plan for integrating their inbound marketing

► Social Media Training and Consulting - you don't need a social media strategy - what you need is an effective social media plan to help you deliver your business goals. From helping you develop your social media policy through to running workshops I can help you develop your plan to profit with social media communications

► Facebook for Business Training - I work with marketing and PR teams to help them integrate Facebook into their communications plans

► Google Plus for Business Training - I educate business leaders on how to explore the opportunities that Google Plus presents to build relationships with strategic partners and potential customers

► LinkedIn For Business Training - I mentor and teach executives and senior leaders how to effectively use LinkedIn to build their visibility and how to integrate it into their business networking and business development plan

► Content marketing training including Instagram, Pinterest, SlideShare, Vine, YouTube and influencer marketing
