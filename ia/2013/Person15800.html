Member

Pinnacle Society (www.pinnaclesociety.org) is a recruiting organization made up of the top 75 performing contingency and retained recruiters in the US. In order to qualify for membership, a recruiter must have at least 5 years recruiting experience with 3 out of those 5 years hitting annual billings of 400k with one year exceeding 500k. I qualified for membership after my 6th year in recruiting.

We gather biannually at various cities in the US (and sometimes Mexico and Canada) and discuss the latest happenings in the recruiting marketplace. Our membership includes recruiters working in different cities, different markets (Tech, Sales, Manufacturing, Finance, etc) and at different levels of an organization (executive, professional, administrative) and with different tenures in the recruiting business (average recruiting tenure is 15-20 years in the business).
