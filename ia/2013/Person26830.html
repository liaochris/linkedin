Gustavo Machado's Summary

- Brazilian and Canadian citizen holding a Masters Degree in the USA, including 6 projects that have been seen over 200.000 times on SlideShare. Some projects took part of 6 international publications and one of them received a Gold Cube Award from the University & College Designers Association (USA).

- Former Professor at 4 Canadian Universities. Currently teaching in 3 MBA Programs offered by B.I. International and UNISAL (SP - Brazil), making use of contemporary pedagogical approaches: andragogy, CAV, syllabus co-creation, peer instruction, learning by doing, design thinking, storytelling, corporate games, top 3 insights, and continuing education (social media).

- Entrepreneur in the same year of Khan Academy (2006) of a non-profit online Design School , which offers nowadays 5.000 resources and over 100.000 visits around the world: DesignEducation.ca – Sharing our Future. Documents available on Scribd have been read almost 500.000 times. Over 5.000 followers on Twitter.

- 22 years of experience in Marketing and 14 years in Education. Several Design projects took part of over 20 publications around the planet. One of the Brazilian pioneers in Desktop Publishing (1988), Multimedia (1994), Internet (1996) and Design Thinking (2008). Currently working as an Innovation and Entrepreneurship Consultant and Professor, contributing to companies, schools, organizations, Government and its employees success.

Skills: Business, Consulting, Innovation, Strategy, Design Thinking, Creativity, Entrepreneurship, Business Modelling, Corporate Games, Marketing, Branding, Graphic Design, Web Design, Corporate Education, Higher Education

Clients: AMBEV, Carlsberg, Bradesco, Ipiranga, Time Warner, IBM, Compaq, Microsoft, Adobe, Edinfor, Promon, Grupo Iguatemi, Holambra, Ogilvy, Mc Cann Erickson, JW Thompson, RAC, OCAD, Art Institute, Centennial College, Humber College
