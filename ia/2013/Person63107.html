Jessica Hoek's Summary

Base coaching & training is the company that can help you motivate yourself our your employees to change and growth. It's my passion to get the best out of you or your team. I believe in starting to look at what works and expanding this. Visit the website, www.basecoachingentraining.com, to read more.

As a coach i strongly believe that clients (and all people for that matter) know deep down what to do about their problems. They only need the right kind of questions (powerfull questions) to get there. I like to ask these powerfull questions. My speciality is to get taped in to the possiblities of the client. I like to bring some humor and lightness into the coaching but am not afraid to confront clients.

As a trainer i want to contribute to the personal development of people. My speciality is to mobilize pro activity and to stimulate effective communication. Creativity and interaction are key words to describe my training courses.
