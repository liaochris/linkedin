Ian Cross' Summary

Ian is a creative entrepreneur with over 15 years of experience as co-founder and CEO of I-SITE – an award-winning Interactive Design and Marketing firm specializing in web and mobile solutions for consumer products, education, healthcare, economic development and nonprofits. Ian has led the company from a start-up to a nationally known firm with clients that include the National Dairy Council, Deloitte, PBS Kids Sprout, Downtown Alliance for New York, MillerCoors, Virunga National Park and Johnson and Johnson. He is on the boards of a number of for profit and non profit entities including The Welcoming Center for New Pennsylvanians and British American Business Council. He served as a Director of Icelandic animation firm CAOZ from 1992-1999. CAOZ since released their first full-length feature animated movie Thor: The Legends of Valhalla in October 2011.

Ian co-founded the Philadelphia Area New Media Association [PANMA] in 1996, was a co-founder of TEDxPhilly and is active as a consultant and participant in creative economy initiatives that promote the Philadelphia region for business. He serves on Philadelphia Mayor Nutter's Council of Arts and Culture as Chair of the Creative Economy Committee and recently joined the Advisory Board of Breadboard, a nonprofit that empowers individuals and convene communities around creative applications of technology. Ian has also served on the DesignPhiladelphia Advisory Team since 2010.

Ian is active in other professional and personal areas that include real estate development and supporting his wife's nonprofit MYX: Multicultural Youth eXchange. Ian is also a partner in new electronic music record label Data Garden and the owner and co-creative director of Philadelphia bar/entertainment venue The Trestle Inn.
