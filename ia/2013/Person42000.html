Eric P. Casaccio's Summary

Hey Everyone! My name is Eric P. Casaccio and I am a Los Angeles based Personal Trainer. My career in the fitness industry began with a dedication to a developed exercise program. The love of meeting my own fitness goals gave me the desire and passion to help others. Shortly after, I became certified by the American Council on Exercise (ACE) and further my education with many different continuing education courses. I love what I do and strive to make my clients look amazing and feel fantastic! So, if you're looking for a fun, upbeat, compassionate, knowledgeable trainer, look no further!
