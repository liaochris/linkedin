HPGM for BI Implementation was built from the ground up for BI. It addresses BI’s unique needs rather than following more generic methods better suited to online transaction processing (OLTP). Modular, the flexible methodology readily adapts to projects of varying size, scope, and platforms and is well-suited for projects involving complex or voluminous corporate data.

The methodology takes an iterative approach to data warehousing, addressing BI issues along business, data, technical, and metadata paths.
