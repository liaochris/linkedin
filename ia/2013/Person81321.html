Lino Martire's Summary

My practice is focused on real estate and business law. I assist my clients with their transactional and litigation needs.

Real Estate

Transactions. I am well-recognized in negotiating and drafting purchase agreements, homeowner association board resolutions, CC&Rs and commercial lease documentation, including retail, office and industrial leases, on behalf of both landlords and tenants. My experience includes representing parties to ground lease transactions, retail, office, industrial leases, subleases and assignments, property management agreements, master lease agreements, parking operator agreements and lease-option agreements. I am also a licensed California Real Estate Broker.

Litigation. I also provide litigation support for issues such as CC&R and purchase agreement enforcements, lease defaults, lease interpretation, evictions, non-disclosure, prosecuting and defending broker issues and boundary disputes and rent recovery actions.

Business Practice.

Transactions.Services which I provide include corporate, partnership and limited liability corporation entity formation, corporate governance work, buy-sell agreements, business purchase agreements and contract negotiation.

Litigation. I provide efficient and aggressive litigation services designed to prosecute or defend a client’s claim. Succeeding on behalf of the client is my ultimate goal. Examples of business litigation areas include actions involving Partnership Disputes, LLC Disputes, Shareholder Disputes, Derivative Actions, Breach of Contract, Fraud, Breach of Fiduciary Duties, Unfair Competition, Purchase and Sale Disputes, Employment Disputes, Trade Secret Disputes, Commercial Debt Collection, Financial Elder Abuse, Property Theft and Conversion and Judgment Enforcement Actions.

Specialties

Transactional and litigation specialty in real estate and general business law.
