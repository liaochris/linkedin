Derek Warner's Summary

I am an international game designer with 10 years of AAA experience. Currently I am working in Unreal Engine 3 on PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and PC at Airtight Games on Murdered: Soul Suspect under partnership with Square Enix.

In my 10 years in the game industry I have shipped five titles, Rainbow Six: Vegas, Socom: Combined Assault, MAG, Socom 4, and Unit 13. I have experience working on all major game consoles and PC.

My specialties include design document creation and management, technical spec design, level design and creation, system design, and content creation using major external editors and internal development tools.

I am always looking for new opportunities to use my knowledge, skills, and abilities to create great games and innovative designs. I am an avid gamer, and I believe that you must look at the past game designs, successes and failures in our industry, to improve and innovate in the future of our industry.

Please check out my website at http://www.derekwarner.com for more information on my current and past projects.

You can download a PDF of my resume here. http://www.derekwarner.com/files/resume/derek_warner_resume.pdf
