Jon-Lenois Savage's Summary

Born in San Bernardino, CA; raised in Visalia and San Diego, California. Jon communicates fluently in American Sign Language. Growing up with a photographer/videographer father and hair stylist/craft artist mother; their deep roots and aspirations fueled Jon’s artistic creativity for as long as he can remember.

Videographing and editing to make high-quality video clips for various Deaf Community, organizations, and business.

Late 2011, desires and aspirations to be an artist again became fueled. He began creating pop art paintings based on people and classical subjects that inspired him by using abstract, bright colors and clean cut painting style. Currently he is using his paintings/photographs as life stories that are reflected in his visual journey.

Throughout his life, he has experienced Deaf Culture-related entertainment using American Sign Language. Also, he has been heavily involved in his local Deaf community by contributing his skills to make for various Deaf community members and organizations. Jon has remained passionately devoted to entertaining people about the linguistic, social, political and cultural issues of the Deaf community.

Specialties

computer skills: Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, Apple Final Cut Pro, DVD Studio Pro, 1080P High Definition with broadcast quality equipment.
