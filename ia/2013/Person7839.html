Angella Conrard's Summary

Angella Conrard is designated as a Reverse Mortgage Specialist by the National Reverse Mortgage Lender's Association. She works exclusively with these special types of loans. Angella originates in 35 states.
Angella represents all reverse mortgage products on the market and keeps a close eye on legislation, senior issues and upcoming trends that will best serve her clients.
Angella is experienced in educating her clients and asking the right questions to help them make the right decision for themselves and their circumstance.
Angella is a dedicated professional. She is the president and founder of the National Aging in Place Council- Orange County, California. She holds emeritus status.

Specialties: Reverse Mortgage refinance and purchase. FHA reverse mortgage loans available from most lenders. I am able to provide an independent recommendation based upon the needs of my clients.
