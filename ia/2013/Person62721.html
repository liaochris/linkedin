Jerry Fons' Summary

Too many organizations spend way too much time creating the "perfect" annual plan, and then 2 months later it changes, so they never actually execute the plan. We believe it is more important to plan for the long term, and execute in the shorter term - on a quarterly basis. As a Certified Gazelles Coach, I help my clients put in place the operating disciplines to execute and grow with a rhythmic and focused pace. Here is what a few clients have said as a result:

Jerry Fons' Experience

Executive Coach

Executive Power, LLC.

June 1999 – Present (14 years 7 months) Waukesha, WI

Over the past 14 years Jerry has worked with over 1800 people in over 20 industries helping them improve their bottom line results through greater focus on strategic goals, properly utilizing the talent of their people, and creating inspiring places to work.

Certified Gazelles International Coach - Using the "Mastering the Rockefeller Habits" Planning and Execution Models, Jerry helps organizations develop the operating disciplines necessary to execute rapidly on their quarterly plans and to engage their workforce in the strategic execution of their plans.

Certified Resource Associates Coach - Using proven, proprietary, and research based development processes for the past 13+ years, Jerry has helped hundreds of individuals grow personally and professionally in areas such as personal accountability, personal and organizational planning, communication, leadership, delegation, time management, interpersonal relationships, and influence.

Certified Innermetrix Coach - Using a package of 3 proven personal assessment tools unique to the industry, Jerry provides great insights to his clients regarding key new hires, candidates for promotion, and development opportunities for key staff.

Certified Arbinger Coaching Network Member - Using the methodologies of the Arbinger Institute outlined in the award winning books, "Leadership and Self-Deception" and "The Anatomy of Peace," Jerry has helped his clientele overcome significant areas of conflict, improve their communication and influence, and to overcome significant areas of frustration and resistance in their business and personal lives.
