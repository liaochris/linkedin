David Malpica's Summary

Learning technology designer especialized in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Math) education topics and methods. Currently focusing on digital fabrication, FabLabs, 3D visualization, 3D printing, mechanical design, and integration with robotics.
