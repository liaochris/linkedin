Hugh Stephens' Summary

Social and digital media consultant with experience in:

- Social Media Strategy
- Social Media Risk Management including ISO standards
- Digital Analytics and Customer Segmentation, including ROI measurement
- Consumer Engagement Strategy
- Statistics and Data Analysis

Hugh has a passion for utilising new technologies to engage target audiences and create innovative solutions to connect consumers with brands. He has extensive knowledge across many areas including project management, consulting, business analysis, change management, strategy and audit.

Hugh has experience working with clients including public and private companies, healthcare organisations, government (local/state/federal) and not-for-profits. Based in Melbourne, he works across Australia and internationally on both large and small projects.

Hugh presents frequently at a range of public and private events, and has multiple academic publications in prominent journals such as The Lancet or the Medical Journal of Australia.
