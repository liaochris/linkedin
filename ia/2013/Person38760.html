Dušan Omerčević's Summary

My team at Zemanta is currently looking for a full-stack software engineer with an inclination towards front-end development. If that's you please add me as a contact or just email me: dusan.omercevic@zemanta.com.

Dušan Omerčević is VP of Engineering at Zemanta - Your Blogging Assistant. He pursues a PhD in computer vision at University of Ljubljana.
