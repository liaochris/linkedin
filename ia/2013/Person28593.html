Cyril Chermin's Summary

I like simple things, smart ideas that translate into different kinds of media and that communicate the relevant essence of a brand or company.

Brands are like people to me, they have character, quirks and ambition, so why not communicate on a personal level and try to surprise people you want to talk/interact with?

Some clients I've worked with/for:

Porsche, Allen & Overy, Rabobank, Bic, ABN Amro, Ernst & Young, IKEA, Nedap, AMC, Silvo, Hero, Ahrend, Frama, UMC Utrecht, PGGM, Luchtverkeersleiding NL, ASML, Ministerie van Defensie (Marine, Landmacht, Luchtmacht, Marechaussee), De Nederlandsche Bank, MUMC+, Cofely, KPN, Ordina & Vodafone.

Specialties: online & offline campaigns, concepts for events, brand activation, connecting strategy with creativity
