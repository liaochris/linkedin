Michael Knoblock's Summary

I am a graphic designer of over 10 years that grew up in a small town in upstate New York. I graduated SUNY Orange with a Major in Visual Communications and Design. My experiences include web and print design. The programs I use are Adobe CS5 including InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Wordpress, Quark and many other programs. I bring my outside the box creativity to all my work. I can work independently as well as with a team. I have no problem meeting deadlines and working until the job is finished and looks to be beyond par.
Previously I was the Visual Arts Director at The Sentinel newspaper in New Windsor, NY. I paginated the entire paper and designed print advertisements. I have also worked at Color Graphic Press designing packaging, catalogs and many comps for various media.
