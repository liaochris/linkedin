Dr. Matilda Sienko, D.C.'s Summary

I am an experienced Chiropractor with over 22 years of experience, incorporating homepathy and LSA Pro Technology.

My practice includes families. My professional approach to practice is thoroughness and attention to detail. I look at the body as a whole, not just the individual ailment. My focus is to bring the body into balance through homepathy to help patients be pain free and to feel better about themselves and their bodies.
