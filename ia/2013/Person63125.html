Jessica Castle's Summary

I enjoy writing and designing marketing materials for every industry vertical, from professional services to eCommerce Web sites.

My qualifications include a BS in Journalism from Ohio University’s College of Communication and the Scripps School of Journalism in November 2009. Besides pursuing my degree in Journalism, I also earned specializations in Marketing, Management and Spanish. As a student, I learned proper writing and editing skills through my basic classes, including a strong focus on AP Style, and also participated in classes that focused on writing for new mediums including blogs and social media.

In 2009, I started Jots from Jess as my personal freelance business, to focus on my passion for SEO and social media marketing. I have assisted numerous clients with implementing a social media marketing campaign, and have created numerous newsletters, press releases, eBooks, and white papers.

I hope to one day have the opportunity to pursue growing Jots from Jess into a full-time marketing consulting firm.
