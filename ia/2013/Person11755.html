Barbara Williams, B.S., M.S.'s Summary

After over 15 successful years in Management in Human Resources and Benefits, I began my Virtual Assistance business in 1999. My focus is in 1ShoppingCart creation and troubleshooting, bookkeeping, medical billing, as well as providing additional value, aimed at helping the client become more successful in their business and achieving that sought-after “balance” in their lives.

Now, in 2012, you will benefit from all my business as well as personal experience as I apply it to “helpmewithmyhealthcarebills.com.” I am delighted to refocus my skills and talents toward assisting others in safeguarding their healthcare dollars. I have “walked in your shoes” and I understand your feelings of overload and frustration from dealing with healthcare issues.

I have over 10 years experience as a medical billing specialist and have developed employee benefits manuals as well as assisting clients and family members through health-care crises. My standards and ethics are indisputable. My clients rely on my sense of decorum and trust me to protect confidential information.

Personally, I have a great sense of humor and love the work I do. I am a trusted, knowledgeable, and Internet savvy resource for my clients and colleagues - most of my clients have been with me for over 5 years, and some have been with me since the beginning.

Specialties:
- 1ShoppingCart (1SC Certified)
- Newsletter, brochure and flyer development and promotion
- PowerPoint Presentations
- Presentation materials for conferences, webinars and meetings
- Bookkeeping, including bill paying, client billing and reconciliation of checking accounts
- Customer Service
- Medical Billing (mental health professionals)
- Personal insurance billing and tracking (helpmewithmyhealthcarebills.com)
