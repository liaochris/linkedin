  • Features of Sustainable Online Marketplaces

    • January 28, 2011
    Authors: Chris Haueter

    One of the greatest challenges in creating a sustainable online consumer-to-consumer (C2C) or business-to-consumer (B2C) marketplace is overcoming the "critical mass" problem (acquiring enough buyers, sellers, and transactions in the marketplace that it achieves a sustainable level of growth). A fledgling marketplace must somehow attract enough sellers that potential buyers visiting the marketplace will find products and services that meet their particular needs and are within acceptable ranges of quality and price. The focus of the present research, and this report, is on a particular marketplace feature, dynamic pricing, that sellers could potentially find quite attractive.
