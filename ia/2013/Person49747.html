Graham Lovelace's Summary

Dynamic leader and excellent communicator with nearly 30 years experience in print, broadcast and interactive media as journalist, editor, consultant and entrepreneur. Founder of Lovelacemedia Limited, the media intelligence provider sold to The Press Association in 2004; founding director of Teletext Limited; founding director of Associated New Media. Graduate of Harvard Business School’s Advanced Management Program.

Specialties

Operating at the intersection of traditional media with the internet and telecoms, we provide:

ADVISORY & RESEARCH SERVICES
- Evaluating strategies, assessing opportunities, conducting due diligence
- Gathering data, commissioning original research

EVENT SERVICES
- Organising events
- Chairing conferences, moderating workshops

PUBLISHING SERVICES
- Outsourced communications and bespoke market intelligence, in print and online
- Reports, speeches, presentations
