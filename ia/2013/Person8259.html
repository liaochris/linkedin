Ann Dennison's Summary

I own Advanced Physical Therapy and Fitness serving Mechanicsburg and surrounding communities. We provide expert physical therapy care with a personal touch - all of our treatments are provided one-on-one with skilled clinicians. We specialize in orthopedic, sports and geriatric physical therapy. We provide rehabilitation of injuries, fitness evaluations and exercise program development. The exercise program development may be for individuals looking to improve performance or for those interested in starting an exercise program but not sure where to start. We do not require referrals from physicians, which makes us easily accessible.

Specialties: My personal interests are in orthopedic physical therapy with a special interest in manual physical therapy and treatment of shoulder and hip disorders.
