Lars Hemming Jorgensen's Summary

Lars Hemming Jorgensen (CIM Fellow, MA Hypermedia, PgDip Communication Design)

Lars is the Founder of Boardroom Gorillas and CEO of Trojan Gorillas, a full service creative consultancy based in London and Copenhagen. Regular appearances on BBC News, Sky, ITN, ARD and BBC World Service has cemented his position as one of the authorities of the brand, retail and digital industry.

Lars has controlled countless high profile and extremely successful branding, retail and e-commerce projects for dynamic companies such as Agent Provocateur, Bang & Olufsen, De Beers, E*TRADE, Harrods, Humanic, Johnson & Johnson, ILORI, MTV, Oasis, Omega, Oticon, Shoe4You and Stiefelkoenig.

Following an MA at the prestigious Hypermedia Research Centre and after meeting his business partner, Jim Boulton, he established Large which grew to become one of Europe’s hottest digital agencies. In 2007 sold to Story Worldwide and Lars was appointed Chief Creative Officer for the group responsible for all creative of the group's six offices. Story is the world’s leading Post-Advertising agency. In 2010 Boardroom Gorillas were born.

He is a Fellow and Course Director at The Chartered Institute of Marketing, Angel Investor and occasionally performs on the London stand-up circuit – although with slightly different material.
