Amy Laurent's Experience

CEO & President

Amy Laurent Matchmaking

January 2005 – Present (8 years 8 months)

Relationship Expert, Author & Professional matchmaker for over 7 years, Amy Laurent is the author of "Eight Weeks to Everlasting" (St Martin's Press/Griffin 2012). Through a high efficiency for locating her clients' ideal matches, Amy is consistently ranked as one of the top executive matchmakers in the country.

In conjunction with her matchmaker role, Amy Laurent holds workshops annually and is a public speaker on love, sex, marriage & relationships.

Amy has been featured in The New York Times, Newsday, Mens Health, & proudly appeared as one of Oprah's '9 Female Power Players in Business and Fashion' in August 2010.

Amy also appears as an expert on: NBC, The “Today Show,” CBS' "Early Show,” "Fox & Friends", "KTLA Morning News", CBS News "Live from the Couch", “Good Day New York,” "Fox News Strategy Room", “Dr. Manny's Health Show" & more. In addition, Amy is a dating columnist for Women's Health, Glamour, & Life & Style Magazines and blogs for the Huffington Post. Amy Laurent is the official dating expert of Stylecaster.com and is community expert for the Love & Sex Q&A board for iVillage.com. Most recently, Amy is enlisted as the Love & Dating expert for LOVEPOST.COM.
