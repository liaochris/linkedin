Hsern-Wei Ling's Summary

A practitioner, facilitator, leader and learning enthusiast. Passionate about unlocking the potential in individuals, organizations and ultimately for Malaysia.

My approach to learning & development is that it is most effective when it moves beyond the classroom - most transformative when it is designed into work environment, activity and relationships. I am passionate about doing this and not just delivering ‘great’ training.

I'm now putting these energies with PwC (Malaysia’s Top Graduate Employer 2012, 2011). At PwC, I am a core facilitator for our business skills programmes. I lead on ‘Career Milestones’– looking at staff development as they build their careers with PwC. In my time at PwC, I've been on secondment to the Global Onboarding team – designing and implementing a global onboarding framework, curriculum and experience across the PwC network for 160,000 people in 159 countries. I lead local and regional teams to align talent onboarding processes and experience across Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia & Laos.

I was selected as one of the “30 under 30” Learning Leaders at Elliott Masie’s Learning 2011. A programme aimed at developing and spotlighting the next generation of Learning Leadership.

I have an MSc in Organisational & Occupational Psychology and prior to PwC spent my working career in Consulting and Leadership Development in London.


Specialties: learning, development, talent, onboarding, facilitation, chairing, training, experiential design
