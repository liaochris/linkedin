Marc Grabanski's Summary

I build bleeding edge web interfaces.

My company, MJG International, hosts expert workshops in our classroom and produces them to be sold online through e-learning. We publish a premium training website for front-end web developers called Frontend Masters (FrontendMasters.com). Customers include: Apple, Accenture, Rackspace, Federal Reserve, Thoughtworks, University of Aukland, TST Media, Gov Delivery and 250+ companies and universities.

My background is in open source where I created the jQuery UI Datepicker project and have spoken at ~40 conferences around the world teaching about web development topics.

Events I created include "Day of JavaScript on Mobile" at Google HQ, "Frontend Masters Workshop Series" I, II & III at the MJG Workshop Center as well as many other ad-hoc web development events around Minnesota.

My deepest area of expertise is in building out UI-intensive applications. I've built out countless diverse web interfaces for software companies of all shapes and sizes.
