Marcin Guzowski's Summary

I have successfully completed dozens of substantial tasks and projects through the years, performing various roles from a developer to senior-level manager. I strongly believe in Business-IT fusion and I simply love simplicity and constant innovation.

Participation in a variety of projects gave me a decent understanding of:

- several industries: General IT, FMCG, Internet/e-Commerce, Financial Services - Factoring, Retail, Pharmaceutical OTC & Rx, Telco,

- various solutions: Supply Chain Management (SCM) & related, Sales/Field Force Automation (SFA, FFA), ERP, HR/HRS, BI, B2B, B2C (e-Commerce & m-Commerce), Master Data Management (MDM), Integration, Factoring, Loyalty Management, Location-aware mobile marketing services,

- numerous technologies: Microsoft SQL Server (MSSQL), SQL, Transact-SQL (T-SQL), PostgreSQL, MySQL, .NET, ASP.NET, IIS, C#, PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, XML, webservices & SOA, C, Perl, TCP/IP sockets, Windows, Linux, Mac OS X, iOS, Android,

- different methodologies: project management (SCRUM, PRINCE2), service management (ITIL), quality management (Six Sigma) and Routes-to-Market (RTM) approach for driving revenue growth.

I conducted many sales presentations / meetings with customers - always listening before speaking (I'm a big fan of Ernesto Sirolli and his rule "Shut up and listen!").

I used to be a noticeable IT/Microsoft community contributor in Poland during 2006-2008 (please refer to http://guzowski.info/pub,en for examples), however nowadays I tend to limit myself only to press comments or interviews (e.g.
http://www.portalspozywczy.pl/tagi/marcin-guzowski,14739.html
or http://www.pb.pl/s?q=marcin+guzowski - both in Polish).

My top 3 specialities:
1. Management, leading, launching, problem-solving, making the most of teams,
2. Data-centric and mobile solutions, databases, integrations, business intelligence (BI),
3. User Experience (UX), Customer Experience (CX), usability (mobile/web/desktop).
