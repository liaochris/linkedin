Doug Boswell's Summary

As a profit & growth expert to owner-managed businesses, my accounting, bookkeeping, payroll and process improvement services focus on reducing your expenses, lowering your taxes & boosting your cash flow. I can fix the problems with your current accounting system and its contents. I can set up a custom accounting system for you and teach you to use it, or I can do it all for you. Sometimes my services fall somewhere in-between.

I am an effective business accountant and consultant, focused on the client need for accurate and timely financial record keeping as a foundation for robust and insightful business reporting and analysis for successful growth and profitability.

Experienced in accounting systems/bookkeeping, business computer applications/information technology, marketing & business development, staff recruitment & retention, organizational development, and process improvement.

My firm is an opportunity for small businesses owners to outsource their accounting needs. We provide accounting and full charge bookkeeping services that include maintaining the general ledger, receivables, payables, payroll, sales and use tax reporting, bank and credit card account reconciliation, monthly reporting & analysis, plus the additional accounting, compliance & reporting requirements unique to your business. We function as your company’s accounting department.

Specialties: I specialize in small business accounting with industry experience including manufacturing, technology, construction & contracting, web-based & e-commerce businesses, non-profit organizations, government vendors, and many types of sales & service industries.

I work with entrepreneurs in all phases of a start-up through mature successful companies, as well as those in need of a turn-around rescue. I also help owners looking to maximize the value of their business should they decide to sell it.
