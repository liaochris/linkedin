Lee Groh III's Summary

My name is Lee Groh and I am an Interaction Designer at POSSIBLE. I am always looking for new opportunities through freelance employment so feel free to reach out with any questions or professional needs.

I graduated in 2011 with honors from the University of Cincinnati's College of DAAP earning a Bachelor's Degree in Digital Design.

As a Digital Designer, I am extremely confident that no matter what challenge is placed before me I have the skills necessary to gain understanding of my audience and then develop a creative solution which effectively communicates and captures the interest of my user.

This is the main reason why I am so passionate about design. It has the ability to translate across thousands of professions, social classes, cultures and mediums. As long as a problem exists, I believe I can creatively solve it through design.

Specialties:
Branding & Identity, Creative Thinking & Strategy, Mobile Design, Motion Graphic Animation, Research, Web & Interactive Design/Programming, Flash Development, Consumer & User Testing, Video & Audio Production
