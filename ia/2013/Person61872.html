Jennifer Kumar's Summary

Jennifer has a proven track record helping Indian business executives (junior managers all the way up to CEOs) attain a clear and thorough understanding of their American and European counterparts. Cultural awareness, behavioral and language modification, and personal awareness are all part of this process. Jennifer helps her clients to bridge cultural and communications gaps without compromising their unique cultural and personal identity.

"The coaching candidates are relaxed and communicate openly with her. In the past year, while working with over 40 of our employees, we have seen real results with how they engage with Americans in everyday situations. Her ability to stand in the US India culture gap has proved instrumental in providing targeted approaches to common problems like small talk, how to take initiative in work related discussions and even how to address difficult discussions in a non-defensive, but inclusive way. Employees who have coached with her have seen their performance reviews and customer satisfaction scores improve, paving the way for career growth." ~Annelise Piers, Owner, ContentmentIndia

"Ram has shown marked improvement in his communication skills with his client over the period. Now his sentences are short and able to communicate clearly with the client, basically ensuring that the client is also on the same page. He is all the more confident now than before when speaking to the client." ~VP Financial Analytics Company, Bangalore
