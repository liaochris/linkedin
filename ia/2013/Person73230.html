Katy Dale's Summary

Educated in and passionate about architecture, I’m taking a unique route through the profession. My interests range from the craft of building to new forms of project delivery to the effect that architecture has on human health. All of these interests evolved out of my professional and educational experiences, but always in pursuit of understanding how to design and execute quality, functional, elegant and lasting architecture.
