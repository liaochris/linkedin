Founder

FlickTribe, Inc.

May 2008 – July 2009 (1 year 3 months)

FlickTribe was basically KickStarter.com, but more specifically for the entertainment industry. I started the company because I saw an opportunity for new technology to allow creatives to fund, develop, produce and distribute content outside the constraints of the old system. Later, we evolved the company into a job site for hiring cast and crew, which never quite worked out. The original ideas behind the concept still excite me, and I applaud the folks who are working on the tough problems in this space.
