Dan Allen CPA's Summary

Since 1999, Daniel Allen (Dan) has assisted scores of individuals in achieving their dreams of starting and running their own businesses or achieving their professional financial goals.

TAX:
-Saved clients millions of dollars through tax planning and strategy
-Successfully represented clients before IRS Examinations (Audits)
-Dan has always left a client better off with the IRS or state taxing authority than when the project began

CONSULTING:
-Dan has assisted individuals with entity selection and formation
-Dan has implemented processes and controls to help client's understand how their businesses are truly performing
-Dan has conducted both financial and process audits and brought ideas and concepts to managements' and the owners' attention to save money or improve efficiency

FRAUD/FORENSICS:
-Dan has worked with businesses in fraud prevention and detection (and assisted clients in obtaining restitution through his cooperation with attorneys and law enforcement agencies).
-Dan is a CPA, Fraud Examiner, and Forensic Accountant

PHILOSOPHY:
-Dan asserts that no one hires a CPA or Consultant; people purchase peace of mind and time to focus on what they do best (no one buys a drill; they buy a hole in the wall!).

BIOGRAPHY:
Dan earned both an undergraduate degree and a Masters of Accountancy at Kansas State University. Dan has accounting and consulting experience dating back to 1999. Throughout his career, Dan has been involved in: Audit, Taxation and Consulting including Fraud Exams/Forensic Accounting..

He has written many articles on tax policy, tax code changes, and the related politics that impact individuals and businesses. Dan is a Certified Public Accountant and a Member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners with experience assisting attorneys in civil litigation as well as working with local, state, and federal law enforcement agencies on criminal fraud cases.
