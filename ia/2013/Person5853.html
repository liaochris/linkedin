Alan Mandel's Summary

I have been in the floor care industry for over 35 years. Cleaning Tile, Grout, Vinyl Composition Tile, Carpet and most other popular flooring. I have spent most of my life working with national accounts like, Rite Aid Drugs, Office Depot, Petco, Blockbuster Video, Trader Joe’s, Dillards, Radisson and the Beverly Hilton Hotel. In addition our customers include: Private Residences, local Doctor offices, child care centers, schools, restaurants, retail and commercial buildings.
