Jeff Abel's Summary

At Rocky Mountain Media Group we assist non-profit organizations and small business owners craft and tell a better brand story. A great brand story captures the essence of who you are and more importantly why you do what you do. And in today's socially connected world, a well crafted brand story is essential if you want your customers, clients, or donors to be enthusiastic advocates for your organization.

I have provided brand consulting and social media management services for small business owners and non-profits, as well as consulting for large international non-profit organizations. I was the speaker for the the branding/social media track for the national conference of a DC based non-profit, and have spoken for the local Small Business Development Center and SCORE.

I specialize in brand development, story telling, social media strategy, training, and campaign management. Rocky Mountain Media Group would love to help you tell a better story about your organization.
