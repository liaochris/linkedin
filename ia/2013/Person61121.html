Jeffrey Rasansky's Summary

Jeff Rasansky represents the victims of personal injury, medical malpractice and nursing home abuse. In addition, Jeff represents children in cerebral palsy cases and other brain damaged birth related injuries. Jeff also represents business entities in complex business disputes and handles these matters on a contingent fee basis.

Texas Lawyer listed him in its "40 Under 40" Texas' top young lawyers. Jeff has been included among D Magazine's listing of Dallas' best attorneys on numerous occasions and has been named as a Texas Super Lawyer in Texas Monthly Magazine since 2002. Jeff is a Martindale AV rated lawyer and earned a spot on the prestigious Lawdragon 500 Leading Plaintiffs' Lawyers in America list.

Rasansky earned his law degree at Southern Methodist University in 1990 after receiving his undergraduate degree from the University of Texas. Soon after earning his law license, Jeff joined a law firm in downtown Dallas where he learned the legal maneuvers used by large corporations, hospitals and nursing homes. Rasansky later joined another large Dallas law firm where he began representing people who suffered personal injury by the negligence of others.

Rasansky is also often consulted by members of the news
media to provide analysis of legal issues.

Since 1994, Jeff has won tens of millions of dollars for clients who have been catastrophically injured by the greed, carelessness and negligence of others. His legal wins have been featured in newspaper articles and television and radio reports across the southwest.

Rasansky is licensed to practice before all state courts in Texas, the United States District Courts in the Northern and Eastern Districts of Texas and the Fifth United States Circuit Court of Appeals.

Currently, Jeff is the development stages of building and branding a national presence around the toll free phone 1 800 ATTORNEY and domain/url 800attorney.com.

Specialties

Recently, Jeff negotiated a settlement of more than $10 million on behalf of an infant who was catastrophically impaired while under medical care. Jeff also successfully represented two other children, one of which resulted in a settlement of 4.5 million dollars and another for 2.0 million dollars.
