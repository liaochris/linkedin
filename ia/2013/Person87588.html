William Martin's Summary

Martin & Colin, P.C., is a successful legal team that strives for litigation excellence, satisfied clients and groundbreaking legal precedent. For more than two decades, the lawyers at Martin & Colin, P.C. have been fighting for victim’s rights through the expansion of existing laws and the establishment of new laws in many areas of life. We have succeeded in expanding the federal Pregnancy Discrimination Act to also protect women who are not pregnant, and the expansion of federal Title IX to protect college students from sexual harassment in the classroom. The law firm’s victory for college students was publicized worldwide by major media outlets including ABC News, the New York Daily News, the New York Post and the Dallas Morning News; by specialty journals such as the Chronicle of Higher Education; and by local newspapers from the Journal of Lawrence, Kansas all the way to the Tribune of Chandigarh, India.

Specialties

In personal injury and negligence cases, we obtain substantial monetary compensation for clients suffering from bodily injuries, pain and suffering, and loss of enjoyment of life.
In misconduct, employment, discrimination, labor law and criminal defense cases, we advocate for individuals battling against a corporation or a government. We also represent whistleblowers, individuals falsely accused of crimes or wrongdoing, and victims of retaliation, discrimination or civil rights violations.
