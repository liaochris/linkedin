Dan Small's Summary

Dan Small Productions is in the business of video production, video webcasting and live event production for corporations, educational institutions, non-profits and other organizations.

• We produce video for marketing, education, and advertising. We produce video for meetings, webcasts and other events.

• We provide content development services: writing, producing and all services for production completion.

• We strategize on delivery and marketing options for video programs and live events.

• We provide webcasting and live event video recording services.

• We provide technical services for webcasting, video production, and audio visual needs.

We have been in business over 20 years and we are a service oriented company. We enjoy what we do, and we hope you do too.

Specialties: Video production, Webcasting, Commercial production, Corporate Marketing videos, Corporate culture videos, Educational videos, Video of live events, Editing, High Definition production, Muliti-camera recordings with a full HD switcher, Single camera recordings, Webcasting encoding and Content Delivery, Complete Audio Visual services for meetings, Webcasting, Video and AV consulting, webcasting of live events, On demand webcasting.

We are a full service video company.
