Chris Ascough's Summary

I am a Senior Project Manger & Organisational Developer with extensive experience solving problems and delivering successful organisational change through situational assessment and team, process and technological development. I am a qualified Prince II Practitioner and also have the ITIL v3 Foundation qualification.

In achieving beneficial change I have developed and utilised skills in Project Management, Troubleshooting & Problem Solving, Change Management and Policy & Procedures. These skills are demonstrated by achievements such as:

- designed and directed the development of a industry leading trading and settlements system worth £6m
- developed a systems implementation team to customise a complex banking application. The team achieved the first implementation of the product in a project worth SFR 6m
- developed an IT change control process for a Japanese bank that led to a 75% reduction in production faults
- developed a remedial plan for a failing project in which reorganisation and staff training helped to avert a potential £300,000 overrun
- prepared plan to transform underperforming Service Support group including £2million opex savings.

I live with my wife Caroline in Merseyside and – apart from rediscovering the countryside after 25 years in London – I enjoy gardening and following American Football.

My current objective is to secure a senior operational development role in which I can use my skills to deliver organisational improvements in profit, service and skill levels. I am also available for short or longer term general business consultancy opportunities.
