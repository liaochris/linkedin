Ahmad Sultan's Projects

  • LTE DIAMETER Signaling

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Ahmad Sultan

    Signaling is the nerve system of Telecommunicaitons. It is used to setup the user services in the network. To support the growing user demands of high speed mobile services, requires as Signaling system that connects a large number of network elements, nationwide. Since the LTE spectrum is limited, wireless service providers are partnering to extned their LTE coverage. This adds further to the complexity of the Signaling system connectivity.

    Tekelec Diameter Signaling Router is a IETF/3GPP complient Diameter Routing Agent. It acts as the central hub of the Diameter Signaling system for EPC and IMS. The scope is to design and deploy the Diameter Signaling architecture for inbound and outbound roaming traffic.

  • OCS IOT and PCRF Integration and Deployment

    • October 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Ahmad Sultan

    The project scope was to support Cricket 4G LTE migration, Broadband Mass Merchant charging system was to be phased out with 3GPP Policy Control and Charging (PCC) solution. The OCS and PCRF are network elements of the PCC architecture. Interoperability Testing of Openet OCS with Cricket core network elements, as well as the integration and deoployment of the Openet Policy and Charging Rule Function (PCRF) were the project scope.

  • Enhanced Circuit Switched Fallback (eCSFB)

    • November 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Ahmad Sultan

    Sprint is deploying 4G/LTE in its network. Initial deoployment support data only over LTE. Voice and ancillary services are a must for LTE users. Since LTE coverage is limited in the first role out, and to provide economical handset opetions, voice and ancillary services has to be provided through the exisiting 3G 1xRTT Circuit Switched infrastructure. The service requirement as well as engineering and architecture of the soluition should be defined and documented to ensure proper implementation and operation of the service at release date.
