Megan Fischer's Summary

Marketing communications professional with broad range of experience including – nonprofit, agency and corporate environments. In my current position I utilize my writing, social media, public relations and research skills to assist team members and help align client’s face-to-face marketing efforts with broader business strategies.

A self-described ‘research nerd,’ I have an extreme passion for staying up-to-date on marketing / branding / social media trends and teaching people how to use new social media tools to better their personal and business relationships.

As a proud Marquette University graduate, I also help coordinate the College of Communication’s Mentor Program and enjoy connecting Milwaukee professionals with promising advertising and public relations students.
