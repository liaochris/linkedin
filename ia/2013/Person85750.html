Marian Kraus' Summary

Professional Architectural and Fine Art Photographer with 20 years of (unrelated) corporate experience, possessing sharp and passionate perception for design, aesthetics and expression, who brings exemplary work ethic and heightened sensitivity to clients' needs and goals to the set.

I am interested in collaborating with Advertising Agencies, Architects, Developers, Interior Designers, Corporations, Resorts, Hotels, Hospitals and the like to create, produce and contribute outstanding images that represent their products, services and people in an ideal fashion.

And that is not all...furthermore I am interested to beautify and transform working and living spaces with my Fine Art Photography and to inspire people to transform their lives as illustrated in my book "Inspirationals".

Specialties

photography, digital imaging, multimedia production, design, inspiration, interior decorating
