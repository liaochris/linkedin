Jason Moore's Experience

Owner

Great Property Deals

May 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months)

Great Property Deals is a private Brisbane-based company sourcing residential investment properties at below market value for savvy investors.

Founder Jason Moore has completed more than 100 property transactions in just 12 years – a successful track record credited to his sophisticated negotiation skills and focus on results and relationships.

‘Long-term relationships built on trust and integrity is the foundation of our business,’ Jason says.

‘From there we focus on finding incredible deals for our clients that just aren’t readily available without the experience and strong industry connections that we have built.’

Jason and brother Jeremy Moore draw on their large network of contacts to secure distressed properties off market, giving clients the opportunity to buy direct at wholesale prices.

Their approach means they only work with astute investors who are ready to buy when the opportunity comes.

‘Our clients are asset rich but time poor,’ Jason says. ‘They don’t have time to search for deals but are motivated to grow their portfolio. We source the deals, saving them time and making them money.’

Great Property Deals secures off market properties at wholesale prices throughout southeast Queensland.

Director

The Deposit Shop Pty Ltd

May 2005 – Present (8 years 4 months)

The Deposit Shop offered people an easy way to end renting forever and fast track their way to instant home ownership.

We offered people an easy way into instant home ownership by allowing them to buy a home with no deposit. We did this by providing them with a home and the large cash deposit so that people could purchase the home in their very own name.

The Deposit Shop has ceased depository lending until the finance industry credit policies become more favorable.

Director

Boston West Pty Ltd

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry

February 2000 – Present (13 years 7 months)

As a professional property trader, Boston West’s core business is buying properties.

We do this by providing property sellers with a fast and hassle free way of getting rid of a property they no longer need or want. We source our funding through private investors by offering them 12% pa returns secured by bricks and mortar.

We acquire properties from owners that are motivated to sell for a variety of reasons. These include: facing repossession, bankruptcy, mortgage stress, drowning in debt, divorce, unemployment, tenant problems, property requiring major repairs, deceased estate, job transfers, portfolio liquidation etc.

We are able to solve these real estate challenges in a fast and efficient manner when traditional options are inadequate or time is running out.

Our private investors use cash, lines of credit, terms deposits, inheritances, self-managed superannuation funds. This system of funding bypasses traditional lending red tape allowing us to settle transactions quickly and provide our private money investors with excellent returns.

Once the acquisition process is complete, we either renovate the properties and offer them for re-sale on the retail market or keep them as rental properties.
