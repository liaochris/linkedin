Kent Simpson, P. Geo.'s Summary

Providing technical and advisory consulting services globally to national and international clients in the energy and environmental sectors, by supporting their geoscience, environmental, and GIS investigations.

Geophysical, geoscience and geomatics consultant with more than 15 years of seismic interpretation, project management, and geospatial analysis experience, coupled with an in-depth knowledge of geoscience data and navigation processing, computer systems administration and marine geophysical exploration.

Solving geoscience, environmental, and engineering challenges by characterizing the subsurface and supporting the development of energy projects, through a diverse array of specialties and project experience:

Specialties:
• Well-site geohazard and pipeline route assessments
• Marine geophysical operations planning and project management
• Environmental impact assessments and seismic acquisition permitting
• Geotechnical studies for offshore wind farm developments and LNG terminals
• Reservoir analysis for subsurface carbon storage assessments

- Seismic interpretation (2D & 3D) and geophysical mapping
- Geomatics, geospatial analysis, and GIS software development
