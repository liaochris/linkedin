Luigi Pisciotta's Summary

I was born in 1984 in Florence (Italy) from an Italian father and a German mother and I’m living in Berlin (Germany) since 2004.

After my secondary school leaving examination in the art school “Leon Battista Alberti” with the specialization in sculpture, I moved to Berlin to study sculpture and general arts at the “Kunsthochschule Berlin Weißensee” university, where I graduated in 2010.

Right now I am attending the 7th semester of „Digital Film Design“ at the “Mediadesign Hochschule” university in Berlin.

I’m specializing on lighting and rendering with Mental Ray and V-Ray for Maya.

My goal is to master Lighting and Rendering in Maya, 3ds Max and Cinema 4D with Mental Ray, V-Ray and other rendering softwares in order to use them in movie and commercial productions.
