Gina Romero's Summary

I often introduce myself as someone who has failed in business several times since the age of 16, not because I am proud of my mistakes but because I value failure as a catalyst for success.

I have since dedicated my life to helping others succeed.

I am a brand champion, social connector and strategic community builder. My passion is entrepreneurship.

What I do:

• I enable companies and organisations to rally advocates and create raving fans.
• I help micro-enterprises to develop sustainable and profitable business models.
• I work with entrepreneurs to achieve a competitive advantage.

Why?

• I am passionate about helping people start up and grow successful businesses.
• I am dedicated to creating business with purpose for passion and profit.
• I am committed to authenticity, excellence and building meaningful connections.

How?

I collaborate with a global team of talented creative thinkers and doers. Together we drive businesses forward through a combination of creative logic, radical common sense and smart execution.

• We bring exceptional talent to interesting projects.
• We do amazing work and ensure everyone profits.
• We work with nice people who trust us to get things done.

And by the way…

Fun, creativity and a childlike curiosity are fundamental elements of my work ethic. I love what I do but I don’t take myself too seriously.

I can help...

• If you require strategic direction, a creative spin on an event, product or service.
• If you want to raise the profile of your brand.
• If your communication lacks harmony and your strategy lacks Zen.
• If you find yourself under pressure to create but the creative juices aren’t flowing.
• If you need inspiration, fresh ideas and frank advice.
• If you would like to bring an unconventional approach to your business strategy.

Sometimes I blog too: www.ginaromero.com

I look forward to connecting with you!
