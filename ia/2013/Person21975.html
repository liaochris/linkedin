Dr. Alice H. Cash's Summary

For the past 43 years I have been teaching people from all walks of life to use music as way to improve, energize and vitalize their lives. My passion is the use of music as medicine and I have done medical research at the University of Louisville School of Medicine and worked as a clinician at Baptist Hospital East in Louisville.
In 1990 I became a professional speaker in addition to teaching and performing classical concerts on piano. I have now presented keynotes and workshops around the world and have an award-winning site and 23 individual blogs.
Today I welcome all joint venture proposals and look forward to working with people from professions and disciplines.

Specialties

presenting medical and clinical CEU seminars, creating digital packages of products for healing music specialties, performing lecture-recitals (keynote concerts) for holistic, wellness and medical conferences
