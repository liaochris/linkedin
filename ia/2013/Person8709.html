Anthony Delmedico's Summary

Anthony is a bootstrap, serial entrepreneur. He is the CEO of several successful companies and has served as a mentor to many start-ups, taking them from idea stage to revenue producing reality.

As a boy, Anthony dreamed of one day owning his own business. Growing up in a single parent family did not afford him the opportunity to attend college, let alone launch a business. Yet he started several “kid businesses”. This served as a foundation for his entrepreneurial success later in life.

After high school, Anthony enlisted in the Army and was selected for Officer Candidate School. He served as an infantry platoon leader during Desert Storm. After military service, Anthony finished his Bachelors and earned an MBA degree from the Carlson School of Management. He continued to operate as an Entrepreneur while earning his MBA degree (launching small companies pay his way through graduate school).

In 1999, after a bad experience with a contractor, he launched a construction business from the basement of his home. Abelard Construction is now a $30M+ company with over 250 employees and offices in several states. Anthony is also partners in several construction companies that he helped to launch, including a female owned construction company, Abri Construction.

In 2009, Anthony's success, insight, abilities and initiative have led him to form Del Visionaries, a company to help guide young people toward Entrepreneurship. He authored "The Little Green Money Machine", an interactive book that teaches kids how to start their own business. Knowing that kids would need more than just a book, he also invented a proprietary kid business stand. Additionally, he sponsors a Kids in Biz Club and awards scholarships to kids of all ages.

His Passion/Goal:
He intends to convince government leaders to institute Entrepreneurship as a standard curriculum in the 4th - 12th grade in all schools. Support the movement: http://www.e2petition.com

Specialties

Anthony donates his time, his entrepreneurial books and apprentice kits as well as speaks to at-risk kids, students and youth groups about the importance of Entrepreneurship.

He is a a dedicated father to his 6-year-old daughter, Bella Mia, whom is the primary emotional driver for his dedication to launching books, products and programs and leaving a simple "blueprint" that teaches kids entrepreneurship.

His creed is simple:

"Fight the Global Recession...Raise Entrepreneurs!"

The "Little Green Money Machine"

Entertainment industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 8 months)

The Little Green Money Machine (LGMM) is an interactive learning system that promotes youth entrepreneurship, teaching kids (ages 8 and older) how to start their own business using a book and a proprietary business stand for kids to launch their first company.

THE BOOK
"Kids in Business Around the World" is an interactive book that offers content, ideas, and exercises to give kids the tools and the know-how to actually start and run their first business or fundraiser. In addition to an introduction to business principles taught in a fun way, this book emphasizes visualization, goal setting, money management, and social entrepreneurship, lessons not taught in K-12 today. The book also offers kids 10 business blueprints kids can run through the proprietary LGMM business stand.

THE BUSINESS STAND
Knowing that kids will need more than just a book...to start their own business, we also offer the popular LGMM business stand...where kids can setup actual business ventures from the book. The stand is like a mobile office for kids and features a lockable money drawer, built in solar powered calculator, literature storage area, dry erase banner areas for advertising and more. Taking the neighborhood lemonade stand to a whole new level!

THE APPRENTICE KIT
includes the interactive book, Kids in Business Around the World, as well as the LGMM business stand, matching folding chair, CEO cap and is rapidly becoming a national and eventual global phenomenon around the world.

KIDS IN BIZ CLUB
On our interactive website, kids may sign up and join the growing and popular Kids in Biz Club where they can enter their LGMM business idea into contests to win scholarships, trips to Disney and media appearances. Additionally, they can elect to join our nationwide LGMM sales team where they earn commissions for selling LGMM products to friends and families in their neighborhoods and communities.
