Alberto Calvo's Summary

If I’d have to define myself in one word, that word would be “balance”. I like things thoroughly thought and organized.

I’ve been working on technology for over 9 years, 4+ of them building and designing products for ad agencies such as Euro RSCG 4D or Arnold, the social network Tuenti, Youzee VOD service, and for a small mobile apps company called Spartanbits.

I’ve got authentic passion for learning and getting better. I love challenges and problem solving. Think about those problems from different angles and choose what’s best for users, but also for my company.

I’m a born team player. I like to help people from empathy and respect. Learn every day from my partners and enrich my perspective thanks to them.

I’m also really adaptable. I’ve been in charge of lots of different things during my career. I see that as a great opportunity to gain expertise in certain areas and it also helps me to see things outside of the box.

This job gives me the opportunity to make people’s lives easier and happier, and that’s what makes me love it so much. I'm ready for the next challenge!
