Susan Walker's Summary

I am a successful procurement consultant seeking new clients & opportunities to network with those whose organizations want to reduce overall spend on goods & services. I am a Certified Purchasing Manager, skilled negotiator, and have earned my M.B.A.

As the owner of SM Walker, Inc., I consult with clients to reduce spend on a variety of categories and have driven cost reduction initiatives with distributors, manufacturing firms, non-profits and municipalities resulting in hundreds of thousands of dollars in savings.

I have the expertise to look at all areas of spend – from direct materials (such as resin or steel) to indirect goods (such as office supplies, corrugated boxes, etc) to services (like security, freight, et.al.). Armed with client spend data, I perform a diagnostic to pinpoint areas of improvement. From there, customized recommendations might include supplier consolidation, identification of new suppliers, or simply taking a category out to bid. I manage the bid process from creation of an RFQ to analysis of supplier submissions through creation & negotiation of supplier contracts. I also educate clients on total cost of procurement concepts and best in class procurement techniques.

Target clients are about $50 million+ in annual revenue.

The comprehensive purchasing consulting services offered by SM Walker, Inc. focus on 5 core areas:
1) Strategic Sourcing
2) Supplier Relationship Management
3) RFQ’s and Negotiations
4) Procurement Strategy
5) Education of your staff and supplier base

As a professional business consultant, I want to make a difference for your organization by putting my procurement expertise to work for you. I work on gain-sharing whereby there is no risk to hiring as I am paid for the results I generate.

For more information or a no-obligation assessment, please contact me at CRE1@comcast.net or via phone at 847-409-3595
