Corey Graham 2.0's Summary

Corey Graham 2.0 always takes a creative approach to projects or facing a particular challenge. His willingness to pursue & actualize an idea started from a very early age & has continued throughout his life. Today, as an entrepreneur he is driven by his truly pioneering and innovative spirit. He makes use of his skills in leadership, marketing, operations, technology, the internet and social media to drive the various projects he is a part of.

In 2004, Corey co-founded “Nocturnal” a youth networking group, with the vision of bringing motivated and industrious young persons together to help each other accomplish their dreams. Nocturnal led to the next significant move in his life. It connected him with one of the directors of the young start-up Alt Entertainment Inc., owners of the trendy Caribbean youth entertainment brand, BoomTribe. Corey worked with BoomTribe in the development of their mobile content business and website, BoomTribe.com. During his time there, he filled the roles of marketer, web content manager, team leader, photographer, web video editor, TV host and more.

In 2008, Corey discovered the world of social media and branding himself as Corey Graham 2.0 has become a thought leader on social media in the Caribbean.

In November 2009 Corey went full time into developing Drenalin Productions into a social media company. He is now leveraging the skills & lessons learnt over the years to grow Drenalin Productions into a premium provider of social media services & other web related services.

In September 2010, he co-founded GramStep with Jeremy Stephen. GramStep is a youth business networking community aimed at becoming the “agent of the entrepreneur”. GramStep plans to develop the eco-system and support systems around young entrepreneurs in Barbados & the wider Caribbean.
 
Currently Corey is also volunteering with the Barbados Entrepreneurship Foundation to realize the vision of making Barbados into the #1 Entrepreneurial hub in the world by 2020.
