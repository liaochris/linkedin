John-Paul Lee's Experience

Founder & President

Kosofresh

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Food & Beverages industry

September 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months) Greater New York City Area

Kosofresh’s focus on premium products starts with sourcing the best ingredients both locally and from South Korea. Our products are catered to the domestic palate while maintaining the core and traditional essence of each dish.

Our goal is to share our passion in Korean cuisine and making these dishes more accessible to the rest of the world. We aim to achieve this ambitious goal by utilizing quality ingredients and recipes refined by a seasoned culinary team, and streamlined processes. Most importantly, we simply plan on delivering deliciousness from Seoul to you.

Kosofresh represents more than a scrumptious bowl of bibimbap; the brand represents a healthy, innovative, and fun way of life. Visit us and experience the culture of Seoul and feed your soul!

Kosofresh: kosofresh.com
Koso Catering: kosocatering.com

Founder and CEO

Tavalon Tea

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Food & Beverages industry

January 2005 – Present (8 years 10 months)

A premium loose-leaf tea company on a mission to revamp and revitalize the stagnant image of tea. Tavalon's goal is not only to create the best tea blends the world has ever known, but also to cater these tea blends to the American palate.
Tea has played a minimal role for far too long in the United States, overshadowed by the seemingly ubiquitous coffee bean empire, and mired in its own "traditional" image. The goal of Tavalon is to push tea back into the spotlight by presenting a fresh, new, accessible face for tea-- Tavalon tea.

Our objective is to become a tea company for both tea connoisseurs and newcomers to the tea world. We aim to provide premium teas and accessories to a mainstream audience
