Linda Benthall's Summary

Linda Benthall
lbenthall@UnifiedCommunications.com
713-358-8050
@lindabenthall

My current job is amazing! I am super blessed to be in the position I am in with the best company in the World so that I can provide the best possible equipment and service to my clients!

Everyday I get to assist CIO types, IT Directors and Managers with their communications needs. Usually they send an IT employee to our office for a hands-on demo. We love to do that so the right equipment is selected the first time - "try BEFORE you buy".

During the demo we assess the business needs for any specific project or company goal. We talk about everything from network infrastructure to end users to how many locations, etc. We will discuss:

>> Phone system diagnostics;
>> Global and domestic video conferencing with LifeSize and Polycom, Aver, Sony, etc;
>> Headsets users and how the headsets be used with our Endpoint Strategy and Management feature;
>> Microsoft Lync specs to discover possible adoption;
>> B2B portals to ease up working with headsets, webcams, speaker phones, etc.;
>> Replacing broken; older; phones with a plethora of choices from ShoreTel, Avaya, Polycom, Snom, and many more;
>> Educating various networking groups on the types of Communications out in the market today;
>> Audio Visual site surveys for any size conference room to optimize sound, picture, AMX/Crestron control panels, lighting, projectors, screens, cabling, project management, furniture, and basically whole room integration for maximum use;
>> Setting up hosted VoIP phone systems, including Microsoft Lync hosted phone systems;
>> Microsoft Office 365 hosting;
>> Microsoft Lync deployments with 3 day deployment and on premise solutions;

To sum up my job - I can walk into a company and consult on their communications needs with 30 years of support, knowledge and wisdom from the most sought after company in the world, Unified Communications!
