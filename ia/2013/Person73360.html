Karen Batchelor's Summary

Welcome!

I'm Karen Batchelor - a Certified Coach partnering with people DYING for personal freedom & happiness beyond the CLUTTER we call "success".

But wait - did you think you already had this?

Especially if you've climbed the ladder of success and are feeling pretty comfortable on that perch? Isn't freedom and happiness up there too?? You tell me -

☀☀☀

Is your life filled with things, situations and relationships that help your career but don't bring you joy?

☀☀☀

If your answer is an unsettling "yes", then this is the perfect time to refocus on what matters to you now. After all, our priorities and goals change as we go through life. In business, you would review this information periodically to make sure it's still valid. Unfortunately, most of us don't revisit and revise our personal goals often enough, if ever.

So for starters, stop by and take the Wheel of Life assessment to get a quick snapshot of how your priorities are affecting your life in eight critical areas. http://ow.ly/kAbbg. If you don't like what you see, the Wheel of Life shows exactly where you can start to refocus on what's important now.

Think you could use support? Let's schedule a 20 minute conversation to explore how strategy, action and accountability through life coaching can help you achieve personal goals and just plain create room in your life for -
