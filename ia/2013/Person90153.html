Maybelle Pineda's Summary

Maybelle hails from Zamboanga City, Philippines and currently holds a position as an Art Director at Summit Media Group Inc., a publishing company leading in publications, events and online media for professionals in packaging and manufacturing automation. Prior to her work at Summit, she started her career as an Intermediate Designer at Site Canvas, a digital startup focused on building applications and platforms to help build and grow digital businesses for celebrity clients Taylor Swift, Rihanna, Xzibit, Charles Barkley, NFL star Michael Strahan among many others.
