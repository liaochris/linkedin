Jennifer Gonzalez's Summary

Designers need to be strategic, conceptual, and creative thinkers. The best designers are also business-oriented and know how to devise marketing plans, engage customers, and create recognizable brands. As an experienced web and graphic designer, I possess all of these skills.

Simplicity and intuition are the basics of everything I design. Presenting simplified and elegant design concepts for complex products/services is something I have mastered and really enjoy.

I’ve had the opportunity to work with clients and employers across a wide range of industries. From a Boston-based jewelry retailer to companies in the financial and IT industries. My goals for each business are the same: to increase visibility, improve marketing efforts, and boost website traffic.

With experience in print, web and interactive, UI, and responsive design, I’m capable of taking a project from concept to delivery. I have consistently been called upon to collaborate with senior management in setting the creative vision and developing marketing strategy. I take responsibility for quality control of the final product and ensure that all business objectives have been met.

My easy, open communication style means that I work well with internal teams. My natural leadership skills give me the ability necessary to motivate and manage team members and vendor relationships.

LANGUAGES: Fully bilingual English / Spanish.

EXPERTISE: Branding, marketing, advertising, conceptual thinking, UI design, print and web design, online ad campaigns, marketing collateral, web production, staff training, and e-commerce.
