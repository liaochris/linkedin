Eric Ewing's Summary

A 20 year veteran of graphic design, video, television/film and multimedia production. While having moved into the executive level, he still keeps a current skill set and isn't afraid to get hands-on in the production process and solve problems as he believes that production is, at its core, problem solving. A member of NATPE and NAB, he has also run a company office at AFM and been the recipient of several awards over the course of his career including Telly, Communicator and Videographer Awards respectively. He also served as a judge for the Aurora Awards for several years. While being directly responsible for the creative development of Omnipulse Entertainment's productions he also assists in developing relationships with co-production partners around the world.
