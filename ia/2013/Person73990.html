Ikechukwu kelly Nwosu's Publications

  • Focusing Change to Win

    • New Catalyst Mgt. Services Ltd
    • May 21, 2012
    Authors: Ikechukwu kelly Nwosu, Nick Anderson

    The cost of a failed change can be staggering, from lowering morale to losing key customers due to poor quality. We believe that it’s time to challenge change management leaders to stand back.

    This book draws conclusions and takeaways drawn from the collective wisdom of over 1000 leaders, managers and consultants with 10,000 years of change management experience.

    Most contributor responses (89%) indicate that their organizations change anywhere from daily to annually. From this analysis, we conclude that these changes are often unique to their organization driven by 3-4 simultaneous reasons for change. Interestingly they report losing customers through, Poor Quality (92%), Sales People Not Following Up (76.5%) and Assuming They Know What Customers Want (64.5%)
