Peter Conrad's Summary

I have 25 years experience in television and multimedia production. I write, direct, and produce educational documentaries, factual TV programming, and creative web content. Please visit my website for examples of my work:

http://conradentertainment.com/

I've also managed several French translation projects (video and text), and have directed and produced video projects in French as well.

In 2011, I was the creative director for visual content on an award-winning digital media wall:

http://www.perimeterinstitute.ca/News/In_The_Media/Perimeter_Info_Wall_Wins_Communication_Award_/

When I'm not doing all of that, I teach filmmaking and videography at Humber College and the Toronto Film School.
