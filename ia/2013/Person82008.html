Laura Rittenhouse's Summary

www.rittenhouserankings.com

L.J. Rittenhouse, is a trust expert, financial strategist and innovation coach to the FORTUNE 20 and is founder and President of Rittenhouse Rankings, Inc.. This executive advisory firm shows corporate clients how to improve performance and market valuation by measuring organizational trustworthiness. The comprehensive Rittenhouse Rankings audits reveal the underlying connection between financial and strategic goals and corporate culture.

Publisher of the annual Rittenhouse Rankings annual Corporate Culture and Candor survey, Rittenhouse has show the positive correlation between corporate candor and market performance for the past seven years.

Rittenhouse is the author of Buffett’s Bites (McGraw-Hill 2010) and Do Business with People You Can Tru$t, both featured selections at the Berkshire Hathaway annual meeting bookstore. Her latest book, Investing Between the Lines: How to Make Smarter Decisions By Decoding CEO Communications (McGraw-Hill 2013), is a groundbreaking system for measuring organizational trustworthiness to determine investment potential.

Prior to founding Rittenhouse Rankings, Rittenhouse was a senior vice president at Lehman Brothers Inc., where she advised clients on valuations, securities offerings, and M&A. She has an MBA and MS from Columbia University, and is a founding member of the non-profit, Families with Children from China.

Rittenhouse Rankings mission is to establish standards for measuring organizational trustworthiness and to demonstrate how these measures are correlated with strategic execution and market performance.

We work closely with our corporate client teams to align financial goals and objectives with standards that produce informative, rigorous and winning corporate and investor communications. We have established protocols can change corporate perceptions overnight, but engage with our clients to insure that the efforts to establish a culture of trust is ongoing. The rewards of these initiatives are tangible as in improved performance and intangible as in increased engagement, collaboration, movitation and innovation.

Rittenhouse’s new nationally-released book is INVESTING BETWEEN THE LINES: How to Make Smarter Decisions by Decoding CEO Communications, which reveals her groundbreaking system to measure organizational trustworthiness to determine investment potential. She is also the author of Buffett’s Bites (McGraw-Hill 2010) and Do Business with People You Can Tru$t, both featured selections at the Berskhire Hathaway annual meeting bookstore.

Rittenhouse Rankings has also created A MODEL OF A SUSTAINABLE BUSINESS which shows the importance of balancing corporate systems to achieve ongoing top of class performance. This model is based on Capital Stewardship and Candor and has been adopted by Fortune 500 companies to create investor and corporate communications.
