Seth Goldstein's Summary

Since 1998, Seth Goldstein has designed Websites for a variety of different sized companies. Having worked hard to be extremely proficient with the design tools needed to be successful in the Web design industry (ie. Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Flash, etc.), Seth strives for excellence in every project he takes on.

In 2007, after many years working as a contract designer, Seth opened his own firm, Goldstein Media, to serve the Web design needs of small and mid-sized businesses.

As the Internet continues to evolve and grow, so do Seth's goals for Goldstein Media. In addition to Web Design, Goldstein Media has developed specialties in Search Engine Optimization, Social Media, Brand Strategy and Internet Marketing.

The goal of Goldstein Media remains simple -- to provide the complete package for our clients at the highest possible quality.

With help from Goldstein Media, we hope our clients will be able to get the most out of their investment and time on the Internet.

Currently the Goldstein Media Website and Blog rank on the first page of Google for our target keywords.

Seth prides himself on the work that he produces and the contacts he makes.
