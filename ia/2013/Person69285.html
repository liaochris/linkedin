Joshua Ramsey's Summary

Joshua Ramsey, president of Strategic Point Marketing; LLC, has developed principles and strategies for businesses for over 15 years. During this time Joshua, and his team, has successfully created and managed more than 2,000 marketing campaigns in more than 3 countries and in multiple cities across the US.
In a recent interview Joshua said, "I am tired of seeing sales people claiming to be 'marketing experts'. I want to open people's eyes so they can succeed and not just follow the advice of a sales person who is trying to make another sale."
Bottom line: Joshua does not proclaim to be a "guru" or the best marketing expert you will ever meet. He does, however, bring passion, motivation, and unique methods of business with innovation to audiences around the country every time he speaks. Spend a few hours with Joshua and you will learn strategies to start applying immediately and feel re-energized to start applying these methods to your business for immediate success!

As an Advertising Agency just a few of our specialties are: Communications, Strategic Consulting, Purchasing, Branding and Identity, Ad Creation(Radio, Newspaper, TV, ECT.), Tactical Media Buying, PPC, SEO and more.
