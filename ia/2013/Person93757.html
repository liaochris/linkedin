Wiley Middleton's Experience

Owner

Middleton Photography

Currently holds this position

General Manager

Pro Lab

September 1999 – October 2003 (4 years 2 months)

Initially employed as Customer Service for The Pro Lab in Denver, Colorado a photo printing lab for professional photographers on a nationwide basis.
Promoted to Digital Operations Manager. Responsibilities included overseeing Digital Department, imaging, procurement and human resource placement within the department. Instrumental in converting The Pro Lab from analog to digital input and output, workflows in software/hardware integration and the training resources to achieve a professional product.
Promoted to General Manager. Involved in budgets, procurement of equipment, and oversight of 80 people in the lab, which included coaching Sales, Customer Service Reps and Production personnel to achieve a common goal, "Customer Satisfaction."

Photographer/Prepress Supervisor

Taylor Corporation

Privately Held; 5001-10,000 employees; Printing industry

November 1989 – November 1997 (8 years 1 month)

Employed as In-House Studio Photographer for Taylor Corporation located in North Mankato, Minnesota. We produced 25+ four-color catalogs a year in addition to brochures and other collateral marketing materials in support of 75+ companies owned by Taylor Corporation.
Implemented Leaf digital camera systems in addition to solving storage and workflow challenges. The studio performed all digital work which included image capture, retouching, manipulation and critical color corrections necessary for catalog publishing.

Promoted to Four Color Specialist for Taylor Corporation. Procured high end imagesetters, computer systems, scanners, file servers and other digital solutions for Taylor Corporations other 75+ companies worldwide. Pre-press supervisor for Corporate Graphics and Schmidt Printing, two Taylor Corporation companies.

Video Production Manager

Hoak Media of Colorado KREX-TV

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Broadcast Media industry

September 1986 – November 1989 (3 years 3 months) Grand Junction, Colorado

Employed as video switcher and technical director for KREG Television in Glenwood Springs. Station videographer, news producer as well as commercial producer. Promoted to Commercial Production Manager for KREX, mother station for KREG and four other satellite television stations. Wrote, shot and edited commercials for air at KREX-Television in Grand Junction, Colorado. Ran studio cameras for news at 5 PM and 10 PM newscast.

Studio Manager

Professional Photo Images

November 1984 – August 1986 (1 year 10 months)

Studio manager for Professional Photo Images. Promoted to General Manager. Operated two one thousand square foot studios, two 500 square foot studios and full color printing and processing lab. Performed as studio and location photographer for commercial, portrait and wedding services.

Aviation Anti Submarine Warfare Operator

United States Navy

Government Agency; 10,001+ employees; Military industry

1974 – 1979 (5 years)

Entered the U.S. Navy and undertook basic training at Orlando, Florida. Proceeded to Memphis, Tennessee to attend Aviation-Anti-Submarine Warfare Class A School. Attended additional training as Aircrewman in the H-2 Helicopter studying Air-Intelligence, Search and Rescue Training, Radar Training and Navy Rescue Swimmer Training. Attended Navy Photography School, SERE-Survival School, Water Survival Training, First Aid and qualified with various weapons and systems. Awarded Aircrew Wings at Imperial Beach, California.
Transferred to NAS Barbers Point, Hawaii. Undertook duties as ASW operator and Aircrew rescue crewman. Deployed to Western Pacific with USS Rathburn as ASW and Flight Crew for various task force groups. Operated sophisticated equipment such as Radar, MAD and other equipment in searching for and finding Russian submarines conventional and nuclear. Honorable Discharge obtained in September 1979.
