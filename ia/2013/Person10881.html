Honors and Awards:

Augustus Raymond Segar won the ‘Best MTV’ Award category at the Mini Film Festival held at UNIMAS on April 2007. The award was given for his short music video entitled ‘One More Time’. His other 3D animated short ‘Whisper From the Past’ was also nominated for Best Animation.

Both entries were solo projects produced by Augustus in multimedia classes at Swinburne University of Technology using SONY video cameras, Adobe Premiere Pro editing software, and 3DSMAX 3D animation software.
