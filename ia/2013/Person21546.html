Byron Barron's Summary

Byron J. Barron retired from active duty as a Command Sergeant Major on 30 September 2007 after more than 26-years of active duty. He recently purchased a franchise with CertaPro Painters of Northeast Georgia and has run a successful company since 2007.

He and his wife, Marina, have a daughter, Valery (27), and a son Kevin (23).
