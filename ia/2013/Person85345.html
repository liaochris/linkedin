Marc Soares' Summary

I am a business and mathematics grad, currently working as a digital analytics specialist.

I graduated with distinction from the Double Degree program at Wilfrid Laurier University and the University of Waterloo, earning a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and a Bachelor of Mathematics (BMath) in Combinatorics & Optimization.

My co-op experience included web marketing analytics at TELUS, audit at Ernst & Young, and finance at BMO Capital Markets.

I am experienced in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am Google Analytics certified. And I routinely use the 95% of Excel functionality that most people don't know exists.

I am particularly interested in the developing fields of data science and predictive analytics. I see the "big data" world as a perfect combination of my business acumen, mathematical aptitude, and technical skills. As an analyst, I can transform numbers into actionable business insights.

I believe in continuous improvement, both personally and professionally, and I am always expanding my knowledge and skills.

Most of all, I am a problem solver who enjoys a challenge. Give me a problem you can't solve, and I will use the full range of my knowledge, experience, and creativity to develop an effective and efficient solution.
