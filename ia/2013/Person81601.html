Lisa Masson's Summary

Lisa Masson is a commercial\editorial photographer with a broad base of experience ranging from international location shoots to diverse photography work in her in Annapolis, MD studio. Her photographic specialties include delicate still life’s, striking interiors, architectural exteriors, colorful displays of food and other products, distinctive corporate brochures. Lighting is Lisa's forte' and she is especially creative with shadows and light. Since 1986 some of her clients include many upscale hotels, noted interior designers, architectural firms and advertising agencies.

Along with Lisa’s assignment work a collection of Stock Photography Images including Food, Interiors, and conceptual imagery are available for usage. Please call the studio to request a stock search for images.

Lisa also owns a Fine Art Gallery and Custom Frame Shop in Annapolis, MD., Original fine art photography is featured within this unique working studio & gallery. The museum quality imagery will capture your eye and you will question whether the images are photographs or paintings.  Lisa prints all of her own work, as well as the work of other artists, on the finest art papers and canvas media available with a large format museum quality, twelve pigmented ink printer. Her artwork appears in each Guest Room of the BWI Hilton in Linthicum, Md, as well in several other hotel properties and private collections. Lisa has a wide variety beautiful frames and can help you with your custom framing needs.

Specialties: Hospitality Industry, Interiors, Exteriors, Still life, Food and portraits, Fine Art, Art Gallery, stock photography, stock food, stock interiors,
