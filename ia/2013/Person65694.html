  • Dialog über Deutschlands Zukunft

    • 2011 to 2012
    Team Members: Joerg Rheinboldt

    The Experts Dialogue on Germany’s Future
    Experts and specialists have been dealing with the three burning issues for the future parallel to the National Dialogue on Germany’s Future.

    More than 120 experts, organised in 18 working groups, gave their best from May to July 2012.
    The work of the experts is intended to trigger a controversial discussion across the whole of society about the immediate future of Germany and to produce ideas or even specific proposals for action to be taken at political level. The experts drew up a final report that they presented to the Chancellor at the closing even at the end of August 2012.

    The three questions that they explored are:

    1. How do we want to live together?
    2. How do we want to make a living?
    3. How do we want to learn?

Joerg Rheinboldt's Publications

  • SimplySeven Seven Ways to Create a Sustainable Internet Business

    • Palgrave Macmillan
    • November 22, 2011
    Authors: Joerg Rheinboldt, Erik H. Schlie, Niko Marcel Waesche

    SimplySeven is a practical guide to Internet business models for business students, entrepreneurs and executives.

    The SimplySeven framework helps entrepreneurs and executives get started on finding the right Internet business model for their web site. It is based on the idea that there are seven business building blocks – which form the foundation of all Internet business models.

    The book contains original insight from leading Internet thought leaders and entrepreneurs. These include Joe Gebbia of Airbnb, Trip Adler of Scribd, Esther Dyson, Brad Burnham of Union Square Ventures, Mark Gorenberg of Hummer Winblad Venture Partners and Doc Searls.
