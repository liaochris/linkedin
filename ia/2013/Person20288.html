Carol Ann Brazier's Summary

Montreal based, Carol Ann Brazier is active in private practice and the corporate world with her International clientele ; her focus is assisting her clients to live enriched ,empowered lives with a sense of fulfillment and well-being.

Fascinated by the complexity of human behavior, Carol Ann has pursued her interest for over 25 years in the fields of psychology, cognitive and behavioral sciences , resulting in a multi-dimensional expertise combined with a concrete tools and applications approach to enhance and maximize personal and professional potential and well-being for individuals, families and the workplace.
