Jed Fonner's Summary

Accomplished software engineer, engineering manager and consulting director with over 10 years of experience leading the development of J2EE and BPM-based applications for large government and commercial clients. I have experience managing teams that range from 3 to 12 people and I can also work directly with clients to ensure project success. I remain very technical and love to get "hands on" with the code (after all, I began my career as a developer). I helped spearhead the adoption of Test Driven Development and Scrum methodologies at my current employer. I have multiple team-years of Scrummastering experience and I have built a variety of agile management tools for in-house use. I am now the Director of the Center of Excellence group within Appian's Professional Services organization, enabling our consultants, partners and customers with the knowledge, skills, and tools to deliver successful Appian projects.

Specialties: Java/J2EE, BPM, Agile, Scrum, HTML/JavaScript, CSS, JUnit, JMeter, Git, Eclipse
