Alphonse Ha's Summary

Alphonse Hà is a passionate strategist who believes in the fundamental need of belonging and connecting with others through engagement and the use of stories.

Alphonse guided large corporations such as Hydro-Quebec, Bombardier Aerospace, Pratt & Whitney Canada and The Gazette in the creation of their digital identity and narrative on social media.

With his knowledge in social media strategies (SMM and SMO), SEO, SEM (PPC), web monitoring and web analytics, combined with his B.A. in Communications Studies and a specialization in video production from Concordia University, Alphonse specializes in giving life to brands and help them connect with their communities.
