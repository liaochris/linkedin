Marye Audet-White's Summary

My name is Marye Audet. In 2007 I began my food writing and blogging career with a (then) small network by the name of b5media. It was love at first write.
I knew pretty quickly that I loved the entire spectrum of writing genres - from informative articles to entertaining blog posts and everything in between. I had always loved research so it wasn't difficult for me to take on increasingly challenging writing assignments that led me on a virtual walk-about. Since that time I have been privileged to help clients create engaging, informative websites that keep their readers coming back for more.
It would be nice if quality content was enough, but it isn't. I constantly study the newest information on SEO and best practices to make your site search engine friendly.
I have worked with brands like Tillamook and Sees Candies to develop recipes, review products, and increase their brand identity through blogging and social media.
As an editor I was responsible for the quality of material that was published on clients' websites, updating landing page, and handling comments and other customer interaction.
I have real world experience starting and managing a small restaurant, handling all aspects of management, menu development, and design, as well as promotion and all the other details involved.
Although I have had articles included in issues of YourTeen Magazine (USA) and Simply Green (S.Africa), my biggest achievement is a cookbook that was published by Adams Media in 2009.
My photography has been included in both hard copy magazines and as illustrations for my articles. My hope is to continue to hone my skills in both writing and photography, both on the Internet and in hard copy. I have a few manuscripts in the works and am very interested in more food related projects.
Unless there is a war, zombie apocalypse, or major act of God I do not miss deadlines... not ever. I look forward to discussing your unique needs and what I can do to help you achieve your goals.
