Edward Goldstein, CFP's Summary

As a fee-based CFP(r), Certified Financial Planner and President of Financial Life Planning, LLC, I guide a variety of professionals, families and business owners, etc. through the various stages of wealth accumulation, protection and ultimate retirement distribution in the most tax-efficient and cost effective manner. My underlying philosophy to accomplish these goals is to present each client with the most appropriate customized solution(s) and ongoing support that will ultimately provide them with the greatest value and highest probability of financial goal achievement. Several examples of these mixed and matched solutions are listed in the "Specialties" section below.

Another factor that clients have increasingly found valuable, is whenever appropriate, I will work in conjunction with a client’s accountant, attorney and other advisors to identify both coordination gaps as well as planning opportunities. Such collaboration provides clients with a clearer and more comprehensive understanding and clarity providing not just greater confidence, but significant improvement to their own financial situation.

While I have been consistently successful in implementing the strategies above, I am most proud of is the strong long-term relationships and lines of communication that I build with my clients and the many resulting compliments as to my diligence, knowledge and high level of personal integrity leading to me being named a "FIVE STAR Wealth Manager" award for the past the last three years as reported in Philadelphia Magazine

Specialties:
* Comprehensive Financial Planning
* Investment Portfolio Management
* Retirement Planning
* Life Insurance, Disability Insurance and Long-Term Care Evaluation
* Small Business Benefits, Employee Retention and Ultimate Exit Strategies
