Joshua R. Schneider's Summary

It’s a fact that no one ever reaches their true potential — the REAL greatness within themselves — without a mentor or coach. The fact is, you can’t see the picture when you’re the one inside the frame! If we need coaches in sporting events — isn’t it more important that we have a coach in this thing called LIFE?

As a professional coach, speaker and author my clients and those around me are challenged to achieve their goals and are consistently going to the next level.

I am passionate about taking businesses and individuals further than thought possible! If the level you're at is NOT the level where you belong....let's talk. If you've asked yourself "Am I maximizing my potential?" or want to standout and reach the next level let's connect and see what you can do.

Gone are the days of just going to work, performing, and going home. We can't just crush it at work; to really see the wealth we have to go to work on ourselves too.

"The true mark of greatness is not found in what a person does with their own life. It is found in helping others discover that, they too, can become great." Earl Shoaff

Don’t ask yourself what the world needs. Ask yourself what makes you come alive, and then go and do that. ~Harold Whitman
