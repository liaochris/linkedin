Julia Campbell's Summary

Hi! Happy to connect with you on LinkedIn!

I'm Julia Campbell, a Beverly native, a Returned Peace Corps Volunteer, a mom, a social media marketing enthusiast and lover of social causes.

Together we can build an effective, efficient marketing strategy for your nonprofit using your website, email marketing and social media - a strategy that will amplify your cause, engage your best supporters and help you accomplish your mission.

I've been featured on Salon, Social Media Today, Business 2 Community, Forbes and The Glass Heel. I love speaking to groups of people - let me know if you need a speaker for your next event.

To learn more about the marketing and social media services I offer, and to read my blog about all things social media and nonprofits, please visit http://www.jcsocialmarketing.com - Thanks!

Specialties: Fundraising, grant writing, social media marketing, event planning, public relations, branding, web site development, program development and management, and working with diverse populations.
