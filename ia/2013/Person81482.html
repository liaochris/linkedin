Lisa Drobek's Summary

I’m an articulate designer who is passionate about creating the best possible user experiences on the web and the most engaging messages in print. My work ranges from creating interactive user experiences and online marketing strategies to traditional publications and marketing collateral to branding and identity packages. My goal is always to combine my knowledge and experience to deliver the best creative to my employers and their audiences. My creative experience includes:

PIXELS
The pixel perfection of balancing content and usability to create the best user experience for websites, user interfaces, and online marketing strategies.

PRINT
Combining words and images to create logos and branding, marketing collateral and ads, and traditional publications that engage their target audiences.
