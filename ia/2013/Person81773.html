Larry Lisser's Summary

Almost twenty years in, Larry maintains his passion for developing and executing go-to-market strategies for communications service providers and entrepreneurs. Larry has combined his strengths in strategy development, offer packaging and sales channel creation to help many leading voice and collaboration providers get to market, succeed and exit.

Based in the Bay Area, Larry also post regularly at his own blog.

In 2009, Larry co-founded StartupCamp Communications to create the first forum focused exclusively on exposing what's next in the industry.

Specialties: - cloud communications, VoIP, visual communications, the communication vendor & channel ecosystem, market selection, messaging and channel development, Pitch deck development
