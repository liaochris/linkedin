Jackie Kooi Teravainen's Summary

Jackie has been leading projects and providing outstanding service to both government and commercial clients since 2004. She started Logical Consulting, a woman-owned small business, in 2010 and offers consulting services to both the government and commercial sectors.

She brings to her consulting practice a unique blend of technical expertise, project management skills, process improvement acumen and strategic planning know-how. This breadth of knowledge affords Jackie a well-rounded perspective, allowing her the ability to transform customers' problems and business gaps into opportunities for efficiency, improvement and increased ROI.

Jackie is a Certified HP TRIM Administrator who has supported the Department of Navy's (DoN) Electronic Records Management (ERM) efforts since 2007. She has also led several Microsoft SharePoint projects. She is certified to participate on SCAMPI appraisals (CMMI®) to support organizations that wish to establish more mature business processes.

Jackie provides strategic planning and communications support as well as workforce development consulting to companies, helping them develop and execute their strategic goals and objectives as well as proactively communicate with their internal and external takeholders to positively influence behavior, build trust and produce favorable outcomes for the organization.
