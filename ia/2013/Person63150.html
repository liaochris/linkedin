Jessica Paz's Summary

I have spent the greater part of my life creatively designing. I was born into a family of artists who always saw the world differently and challenged life daily. I was taught how to live life the way my parents did, free with an artistic spirit, and it has shaped the designer I have become today. My family, culture, music, and life itself has been my greatest muse and it shows in all of my work.

I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Fine Art with a major in Media Art and Animation from The Art Institute of Tampa. I have had the privilege of working with such talented individuals such as Emmy Award winner Dax Bedell, Creative Director at WEDU/PBS Station, Bruce Barry, creative genius behind the Emmy Award winning reality show The Wacky World of Bruce Barry, and educating the talented students at ITT-Technical College. I have done everything from 3D video animation to designing interactive touch screens for the hospitality industry.

Being a designer, teaching design to others and directing design projects has allowed me the gift and talent of being able to create beautiful works of art, but also direct others how to do the same. I possess the organizational skills not found often in today’s designers, but I never stray from my initial roots of artistry.

Not only am I a skilled graphic designer/production manager, but I am also a talented illustrator. A lot of inspiration comes to me as my graphite touches the paper and I can put those beautiful works of art dancing throughout my mind, onto paper for the world to see.
