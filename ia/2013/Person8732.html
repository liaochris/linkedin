Anthony Gemma's Summary

Born in Providence, RI, the son of Larry and Gloria Gemma, I began my career at the age of 10. With my father by my side as my teacher and mentor, I began to learn the plumbing trade when I was in the 4th grade. I attended and graduated cum laude from Suffolk University in 1992 with my BS in Legal Argument/Communications. I continued my law education at Roger Williams University School of Law and graduated in 1998 with my JD. I am currently admitted to practice Law in RI and Federal Court. In 1999, I established the law firm of Gemma & Levine and shortly thereafter, my law partner and I established Justice For All, a general practice law firm. I returned to my family owned plumbing company in 1999 as Executive Vice President and CEO. In 2000, I was appointed to the position of President, where I remained until 2008 at which time I established an integrated marketing firm called Mediapeel™. Mediapeel™ specializes in integrated marketing and new media and our product line includes web video magazines and website development, media buying & placement, the Mediapeel Real Time Traffic Reporting Center, and other traditional forms of advertising.

My team and I have embarked on many journeys including the establishment of the Gloria Gemma Breast Cancer Resource Foundation, a 501(c) 3 non profit breast cancer foundation created in memory of my mom who died from this disease in 2002. In 2006, the FOCUS 500™ Business System was developed first, to establish a comprehensive business system capable of managing growth; second, to develop a systematic approach to measurement which was strategy based; third, to have a system that was user friendly to management and employees at all levels; and fourth, to design a strategic scorecard that was easy to understand with a dashboard that could graphically display results at a moments’ notice.
