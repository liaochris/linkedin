Holly Castellow's Summary

I am a multi-talented professional seeking to utilize my skills and grow with the right company. Most of my experience and formal education is in the field of Industrial-Organizational Psychology. I spent two years working for a government contracting company as a Human Capital Consultant right out of graduate school. During this time, I took initiative to learn the Adobe Creative Suite in order to strengthen the quality of the design of the client deliverables that my team and I were responsible for producing. Graphic design has remained a hobby of mine and I would like to end up in a position that will allow me to design documents and utilize my creative side to a certain degree. I am confident that I would be a valued addition to any company given my current skills, my ability to quickly learn, and strong desire to take initiative.

Specialties: competency modeling, instructional design, survey research, graphic design, writing, editing, marketing, business development
