Bobbi Palmer's Summary

I am The Dating and Relationship Coach for Women over 40, and founder and CEO of Date Like a Grownup. I’m not just a dating coach; I'm a dating success story! I married for the first time at age 47 and have helped countless women around the world do what I did: find deep and lasting love after 40.

I didn't grow up wanting to be a dating and relationship coach. No one does, right? So how the heck did I get here?

My entire adult life has been about helping adults learn so they can enrich and improve their lives. I spent 25 years in the corporate world directing, creating and delivering multimillion dollar, world-wide training programs in a multitude of industries.

Eventually my passion waned. I felt the need to provide a service that was more natural to me…more human and meaningful. And what is more meaningful than love?

I now use my proven and powerful 6-Step Find Hope and Find Him system to help you find more than just your loving life partner. You also find a deeper knowledge and appreciation of yourself and men, and learn to break down barriers to boldly take control of your search for partnership and love.

I offer many ways to help you Find Hope and Find Him including 1-1 private coaching, telecourses and programs for your home. I invite you to visit me at www.DateLikeaGrownup.com.
