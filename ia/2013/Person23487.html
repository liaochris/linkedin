Chris Livdahl's Summary

I recently attended school to finish up a Master of Science in Computer Science and Software Engineering at UW Bothell, as well as an iOS/Mac Development Certificate through UW Extension. Shortly after finishing these programs, I landed a job in the Macintosh Business Unit at Microsoft, working on our Office for Mac product line.

My interests are in iOS and OS X app design and development, as well as web design and development. I am also interested in user experience design and visual design.

For networking, feel free to drop me an email at chrislivdahl-at-gmail. Check out my portfolio site at http://www.livdahlproductions.com.

Professionally, I've performed several different roles, including web development, program management and data analysis.

I've also played in several bands on guitar and synthesizers, and done studio production of recordings. I've written electronic music tracks, and enjoy programming drums and writing string arrangements. And during the summer I am an avid golfer.

Specialties: PHP, HTML, CSS, MySQL, jQuery, XCode, NetBeans, Logic Pro, Reason, prototyping with OmniGraffle and Illustrator.
