Erica Hargreave's Summary

Erica, Founder and Creative Head of Ahimsa Media, is a forward thinker. Her early integration into the digital space and ability to develop stories interactively, led to the creation of one of the first transmedia storytelling companies and one of the world’s few truly transmedia characters.

Over the years, Erica has written for TV, magazines, books, the web and the education sector. She has worked as a creative producer for both TV and theater, directed, and even starred in her own TV Show. Erica has also taught – both in the Community, and at Post Secondary and Grade School Institutions. Thriving on challenges and the desire to solve problems, Erica has been focusing her storytelling on emerging, digital and social media for the past few years and has been loving it.

Feeling very passionate about digital, interactive and transmedia storytelling, Erica loves sharing her story with others and what she has learned along the way.
