Janine Duff's Summary

TAKE CENTER STAGE WITH LOOK-AT-ME COPY DOUBLE-DUNKED IN YOUR UNIQUE CHARM.

I absolutely adore alliteration and colour-sorting Smarties.

And I help everyone else love what you love (whether that’s hula hooping, custom-made jewelry, spiritual counseling or chicken in a can)—all through the power of words.

No matter what industry you're rockin' out in, the words you use to describe what you do are all that sets you apart from the others crowding the aisle.

People need a reason to pick you. They need to be enticed. They need to know why you're worth paying attention to. And hey, that's what I'm here to help you out with.

My street cred…

After getting my MA in English and teaching undergraduate university writing courses, I got my first taste of professional copywriting training from American Writers & Artists Inc.

In the past 4 years, I’ve served clients all over the world including a Las Vegas mentalist act, a travel agency in Israel, Canada's #1 female entrepreneur, Kelsey Ramsden, as well as big boys like Royal Canin pet foods, Elanco and General Dynamics Land Systems. I’ve even written magazine articles on women in competitive mixed martial arts and somehow succeeded at making the trucking industry sound sexy. (Those are some mad skills, yo.)

I only work with a small number of clients each month. This is to ensure that I can provide you with all the personal attention you deserve and get your project completed in lickety-split fashion. No outsourcing. Full disclosure. Illegal amounts of fun. Pinky swear.
