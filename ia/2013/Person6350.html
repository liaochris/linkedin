Andrea Phillips' Summary

A highly experienced and award-winning transmedia writer, game designer, consultant and author. My book, A Creator's Guide to Transmedia Storytelling, is now available in stores.

I primarily work on a freelance contracted basis, and am willing to consider both long and short-term projects.

Specialties

Transmedia, transmedia storytelling, experience design, interaction design, worldbuilding, writing, continuity maintenance, ruthless execution of plans, game design, social games, marketing, general technical competence, digital culture, new media

Andrea Phillips' Experience

Transmedia Writer & Game Designer

Deus Ex Machinatio

June 2007 – Present (6 years 3 months)

Game design, writing, and experience consultation for transmedia projects, alternate reality games, pervasive and immersive games, and other net-native forms of narrative and entertainment. My work includes a variety of high-profile educational and commercial projects, including:

* The Diesel Reboot Tumblr community and missions with Moving Image & Content.
* The sensory experience The Maester's Path for HBO's Game of Thrones with Campfire Media.
* The Drunk and On Drugs Happy Funtime Hour online quest with Stitch Media.
* The browser-based trading game Floating City with Thomas Dolby.
* The Facebook-based game America 2049 with human rights nonprofit Breakthrough.
* Educational docudrama Routes Game for Channel 4 Education with Oil Productions.
* The 2012 Experience for Sony Pictures with Brooklyn Interactive (now Something Massive).

These projects have received some critical acclaim:

* Drunk and On Drugs won a Digi Award for Best in Digital Advertising in Nov. of 2011.
* America 2049 was nominated for the Games for Change Transmedia award and a Katerva award in May 2010.
* Routes won the IVCA Gold in the Web 2.0 category and the Grand Prix, in Mar. 2010.
* Routes won a BIMA in the Interactive - Content category in Nov. 2009. It was also shortlisted in Online - Website, Informational, and Children's - Interactive.
* Routes won the Prix Jeunesse International 2010 Interactivity Award.
* Routes won the Broadband Digital Award in 2010 for Best Content Partnership.
* Routes was also shortlisted for a 2009 BAFTA; a 2010 MediaGuardian Innovation Award for Use of Web Platforms in; and a 2009 UK Writer's Guild award.

Digital Experience Consultant

Neogence

July 2007 – August 2009 (2 years 2 months)

User experience and general conceptual consulting with regard to an innovative mobile augmented reality platform and MMO.

ARG Producer

Mind Candy

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Entertainment industry

March 2005 – June 2007 (2 years 4 months)

Writing, puzzle design, and production work for Perplex City, the first successful commercial alternate reality game. During the two-year run of this highly immersive treasure hunt, I wrote approximately 250,000 words including multiple blogs, websites, a weekly newspaper, a magazine, etc.

Perplex City was awarded or considered for a number of prestigious honors:

* Frozen Indigo Angel, an episode of Perplex City, was nominated for a Radio Academy Edge Innovation Award in 2007.
* Perplex City was shortlisted for a Best Video Game BAFTA in Aug. 2006.
* Perplex City received an Origins Vanguard Innovation Award in 2005.
