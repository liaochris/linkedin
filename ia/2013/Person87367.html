Michael Roberts' Summary

I love technology, change, and working with something new. My purpose for entering the Information Technology field was to be in the middle of what is probably the most fluid industry in the world today. I’m excited when Microsoft releases a new operating system or when Apple releases a new device. I thrive on deploying a new system or application. I love figuring something out that I’ve never seen before. I truly like fixing something when it breaks or solving a problem that no one else could solve. My career in IT has given me many opportunities and I believe there are even better opportunities in my future.

When I’m not at the office, I volunteer on a regular basis running a Midas PRO3 console or hand-held stage camera for a congregation of over 2000 in middle Georgia. I have also performed disaster relief cleanup in Atlanta and New Orleans as well as been a service volunteer helping needy families in my local community.
