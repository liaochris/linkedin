Barbara Bray's Summary

I help schools, universities, and organizations find strategies that engage learners and encourage creativity, joy, and innovation. My focus is in facilitating inquiry driven learner-centered environments that encourage personalized learning. My journey led me to create, My eCoach, an online learning environment in the cloud for teachers and administrators. It started with my master's project (1996) on adult learners and an assessment that automatically generated individual learning plans with a list of learning opportunities for users. I realized the need to personalize learning long ago.

I use My eCoach as a model for groups creating their own learning communities with any tools. Right now there are almost 16,000 members in My eCoach with many who love our Universal Builder. I use any tools to help teachers, students, and administrators reach their fullest potential. It is all about the learner now. I know that. It doesn't matter what tool or path if it's the right path for the learner.
