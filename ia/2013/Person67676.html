Jonathan Setty's Summary

Business Development professional with 10 years commercial experience within online/digital media! A successful background of working in both client service and business development roles with leading UK and International brands across Retail, Travel, Finance, Government and Leisure verticals.

6 years working in the web analytics industry formed an appreciation of the importance of data-driven, measurable investment online. This has been complimented further by an in-depth knowledge of search marketing (both PPC/SEO), including how technology and automation can help to optimise performance. Strong understanding of all aspects of online media, including emerging display technologies such as smarter data led and programmatic buying (e.g. RTB, DSPs and SSPs), in addition to affiliates and email marketing.

An accomplished negotiator and a determined team player who consistently exceeds challenging sales targets while maintaining a consultative, solution selling approach. A high-level presenter and communicator with skills in building and maintaining strong relationships. Ability to work independently, in pressured and demanding environments, with substantial experience of building, coaching and motivating a sales team.

Always keen to represent solutions and technologies where a true and visible ROI can be achieved.
