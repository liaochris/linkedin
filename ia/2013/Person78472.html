Larry Melnick's Summary

I'm the founder and principal of Hunter Business Development, a consulting firm that helps professional services firms primarily in the advertising agency and marketing communications sector grow. We get involved with firms in a variety of ways from Strategic Plan Development to Execution - we work to open doors to the right prospective clients.

Our strengths include new business program diagnosis and strategic plan development, brand positioning, client research, marketing content development, lead generation, lead nurturing and business development coaching.

Bottom Line: We provide firms with the insights, resources and tools necessary to create a pipeline of viable prospects.
