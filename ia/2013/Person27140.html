Joe Andronaco's Summary

Corrosion Fluid Products is proud to be a veteran owned company.

Established in Southeastern Michigan in 1968; Corrosion Fluid Products Corp. has steadily grown throughout the Greater Midwest and now operates ten (10) stocking branch operations. Our branch offices stretch as far west as Davenport, Iowa and as far east as Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and every major metropolitan city in between.

We offer the most recognized pump, valve, piping and hose manufacturers in the industry for the chemical & fluid process markets.

We also support what we sell, providing quality services such as pump repair, valve automation assemblies, pipe fabrication, welded hose fabrications and skid-mounted systems. Our experienced shop mechanics are factory certified and fully capable to handle any fabrication and repair.

We are known for quick, on-time deliveries that are achieved by our network of stocking warehouse locations. Our sales staff and operating system makes the transaction process quick for our customers.

Carrying large inventories of quality products and providing quick and competent service is part of the whole package we give you. You can be assured when you request information or place an order with us, it will be handled with "Competence and Speed!"

Our stocking warehouses are orderly, bright and extremely clean because we care about our work environment and the condition of the products we sell.

Minimizing your downtime is our objective. We provide high technical assistance, service and repairs to achieve this goal. Call any of our branches who are closest to you and we will respond quickly.
