Certified Professional Resume Writer

My passion is writing effective resumes and social media content that lead to interviews and jobs. I utilize over 20 years of experience in recruiting and executive placement to produce resumes to attract the attention of all those strangers out there and pass the "Blink of an Eye" test..

Packages for all experience levels include your resume in Word, PDF and Text, an adaptable Cover Letter and your Online Profile.
