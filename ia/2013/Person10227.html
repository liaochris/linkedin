Ashley Dugan's Summary

I am a graphic designer with both agency and freelance experience. As a freelancer I have excelled at maintaining my own business and have created lasting client relationships.

As a team member at a small interactive agency I was responsible for concepts and creation of nationwide internet campaigns for highly reputable internet companies. I worked on set for several video productions for the web, as well as set up and directed casting calls for talent. I also gained experience in budgeting, trade show design, media kits, iPhone app development, rich media, and video editing. While at the agency I was also the go-to-go girl for all vector illustrations and icon development.

Specialties: graphic design, email marketing, branding and identity, web banners, logo creation, fine art, illustration, painting, book restoration, book creation, marketing, guerilla marketing, talent casting
