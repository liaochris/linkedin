Alberto Sapoznik's Summary

Alberto specializes in Office, Retail & Warehouse Buyer & Tenant Representation. Mr. Sapoznik brings a dedicated and aggressive approach to the brokerage process. His goal is to assist his client(s) in making the most advantageous decision. Mr. Sapoznik is a Cuban-born, bilingual Broker, who is sensitive to the needs of his clients. He began his career in Commercial Real Estate in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida with Intercontinental Commercial Brokerage Corporation. After relocating to Atlanta, he joined Prudential Georgia Realty, Commercial Division (3 years). After 3 years he took the position of V.P. of the Brokerage Division with Ackerman & Co. for over 7 years. Awarded the 2005 President's Award by the Atlanta Commercial Board of Realtors. Also is proud to mention that he was awarded the 2001 Rookie of the Year award atr Ackerman & Co.
In 2009 Alberto opened his own firm: 1st Global Realty... providing a personalized approach for your Commercial Real Estate needs, specializing in Tenant Representation.

Specialties

Tenant Representation with over 24 years experience, specializing in buying or leasing Office, Retail and Office, Warehouse, & Flex space, on both sides of the "table". Is now ONLY on the Tenant / Buyer side.
