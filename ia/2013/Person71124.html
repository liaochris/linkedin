Justin Bishop's Summary

Born in Huntsville, Alabama, a city known for its contributions to national defense, space exploration, and technology, Justin Bishop and his family now reside sixty miles west in Florence, a vibrant city on the banks of the beautiful Tennessee River. The Shoals is a growing metropolitan area rich in musical heritage and renowned for world-class golf and fishing.

As a freshman in college, Bishop launched a business that provided advertising design and internet marketing services to small, local companies and non-profits, a venture that supported him throughout his collegiate years. After graduating from the University of North Alabama with a degree in business administration and entrepreneurial management, Bishop obtained his real estate license and affiliated with Coldwell Banker®, a global and local leader in residential real estate. After five years of marketing and selling existing homes and new construction, Bishop discovered his true passion – commercial real estate. He joined Pounders & Associates, the leading commercial real estate company in the Shoals area. The team at Pounders & Associates is well positioned in the local market with over 25 years of experience in brokerage, property & asset management, tenant representation, development and build-to-suit projects.

The son of a law enforcement officer, Justin Bishop is a strong proponent of public safety and believes in giving back to the community. He is a certified firefighter, emergency medical technician, and an active member of the Underwood Petersville Volunteer Fire Department, where he serves as an officer and President of the Board of Directors.

Justin and his wife, Heather, have one daughter. They are members at Underwood Baptist Church, where Justin serves on the long range planning team.
