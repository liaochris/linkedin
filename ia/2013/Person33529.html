Deborah M. Dubree's Summary

Deborah pulls powerful and practical materials for her keynote, workshops and personal training/coaching from her book: Average Is An Addiction, From Mediocre to Millionaire!

The foundation of Deborah expertise has increased money, recognition and respect for leaders, sales, entrepreneurs, customer service and even college and professional golfers along with NFL players from: Baltimore Ravens; Green Bay Packers; San Francisco 49ers; Dallas Cowboys; San Diego Chargers; Houston Texans; Cleveland Browns and more.

Clients love her ‘No BS’ attitude and training methods. They improve her clients' performance and their bottom line.

Quick Bio: With only a high school diploma, Deborah went from answering phones as a receptionist to owner and CEO of a $20 million commercial construction company. During her climb up the corporate ladder she gained a unique perspective on business and how to beat the odds to reach ultimate success. She now shares her powerful, effective and easy to apply proven systems and techniques for gaining peak performance and greater financial success.

Deborah’s wit, compassion and straight-forward style makes her a popular coach, trainer and speaker. She spoke and conducted workshops all across the United States and internationally from Canada to Australia.
