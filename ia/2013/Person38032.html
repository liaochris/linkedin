Dr. Natasha Kassam's Summary

Dr. Kassam is a board certified Naturopathic primary care physician. She treats the broad range of acute and chronic conditions seen in family practice, and has a special interest in cardiology and gastrointestinal health. She gained in-depth experience working with these conditions when she was one of a few chosen interns in the Center for Natural Medicine's Heart and Lung Wellness Program. In addition, Dr. Kassam completed a residency program with the National College of Natural Medicine (NCNM). She has worked in various practice settings, from allopathic based practices to smaller community clinics targeting homeless and other underserved populations. As a result, Dr. Kassam is particularly skilled at managing chronic illnesses.

Tailoring treatment plans for each individual patient’s needs, Dr. Kassam uses herbal medicine, nutrition, nutritional supplements, body movement, homeopathy, hydrotherapy, and lifestyle counseling. “My approach is to work as a team with my patients, allowing them the best opportunity for achieving their expectations and health goals.” Dr Kassam also enjoys working with other healthcare physicians to provide patients with the optimal level of health education and medical care.

Beyond doctoring, Dr Kassam is extremely passionate about nutrition and physical fitness. She provides nutritional discussions to the community regarding general wellness and healthy lifestyle education. As mentioned earlier, Dr Kassam is committed to body movement and cardiovascular exercise. She spends time working out, playing sports, hiking, and appreciating nature. Currently she teaches the Gyrotonic(R) body movement in Portland Oregon.

Specialties

Extensive training in cardiopulmonary medicine & gastrointestinal health.

Nutritional counseling to Center for Natural Medicine patients - advice on diet, exercise, and nutritional supplements.

Additional training in Sensation Method Homeopathy

Private Gyrotonic(R) (body movement) instruction in Portland OR.
