Michele O'Mara, PhD's Summary

I am a resource. I am information, acceptance, understanding, a gentle nudge, one who encourages, and one who validates. I am a coach (without a whistle), a source of accountability. I am a guide. I am someone to challenge you, stimulate new thinking, and encourage deeper awareness and insight. I am an educator, someone who gives homework and other growth-oriented assignments, offers levity and humor. I am someone who keeps it real.

My hope is to assist you in making your life as true to who you are, and how you wish to be in this world, as possible, so that you may experience the very best version of who you are, and love yourself for it!

Educator, Therapist, Author

Michele O'Mara, PhD

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Mental Health Care industry

May 1997 – Present (16 years 6 months)

Not your traditional therapist, that's for sure. Working with primarily lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender clients, as well as those questioning any of the above, is my absolute passion. Ask Me! is my first book - a book of questions for people wanting to really get to know each other better. Then came, Love Tips and Trips which is a guide to help couples navigate their relationships and how doing so also helps navigates your life.

Among my favorite things to do is teach the classes in my Designing My Life (.com) series. Designing My life, Designing My LIfe Relationships, and Designing My Life Happy. Teaching these classes are really my way of having A LOT of fun at work, and I love them!
