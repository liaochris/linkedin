Michelle Dunn's Summary

Michelle Dunn, author of an award winning book, self syndicated columnist, voted one of the Top 5 Women in Collections and one of the Top 50 Most Influential Collection Professionals by her peers, shares her hard-won expertise on debt collection with the titles in her “Collecting Money Series.” Michelle started and ran M.A.D. Collection Agency for 8 years and has worked in the debt collection industry for over 26 years.

An internationally-known expert on the topic of credit and collections Michelle Dunn is a leading authority on collecting money to businesses everywhere. In addition to writing and marketing her books, she shares valuable credit & debt information with business owners on her blog.

Specialties: I specialize in consulting about credit and debt collection and starting a collection agency. I write books to help people be better collectors, manage A/R and improve credit policy procedures, while limiting their risk.
