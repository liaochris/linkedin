David Guerin's Projects

  • Online Intranet (WebOffice) Development

    • January 2007 to December 2008
    Team Members: David Guerin, Anna Aceto-Guerin

    Upon joining the firm on a full-time basis, it was apparent that the team had some antiquated processes that would be greatly improved with new technology. I began by mapping out existing work-flows, performed a gap analysis, and determined that an on-line intranet system would optimize our efforts.

    We needed a system that would allow our various team members, many working from home offices, to share documents, easily access contact information, and log customer activity. We also needed to be able to set up "follow-ups" for team members and to be able to accurately bill customers for our consulting time.

    I investigated various options, but decided on customizing the on-line service from WebEx called WebOffice. I determined that its flexibility, ease of use, and modest cost would be the best fit for Clear Path.

    Significant customization of their system created a proprietary system that revolutionized the way Clear Path team members could perform. It has made us exponentially more efficient and notably more effective when dealing with WSIB adjudicators (with all necessary information at our fingertips).

    Unfortunately, WebEx has not made any significant improvements to their product since 2007. It continues to work for us, but we may choose to use another vendor in the future.
