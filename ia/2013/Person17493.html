Brian Kehrer's Projects

  • Guns of Icarus Online

    • May 2011 to November 2012
    Team Members: Brian Kehrer, Jessery Bengston, Eric Chung, Jess Haskins, Alex Jarocha-Ernst, Timothy Doolen, Emily Compton, Howard Tsao, George Simpson

    Guns of Icarus Online is a multiplayer airship-to-airship combat game. Hop on board a steam/dieselpunk airship with three of your friends and play as Captain, Engineer, or Gunner and shoot down other ships out of the sky.

  • Creavures

    • January 2010 to April 2012
    Team Members: Brian Kehrer, Jessery Bengston, Conrad Kreyling, Eric Chung, Michael Gambino, Howard Tsao, Emily Compton, George Simpson, Jess Haskins, Alex Jarocha-Ernst, Timothy Doolen

    Designed as an inviting, accessible title for families or casual gamers new to the platformer genre, CreaVures is a beautifully rendered, non-violent, classically-inspired puzzle platformer for PC, Mac, and iOS with whimsical characters, breathtaking natural environments, and a soothing, atmospheric soundtrack.

    Five brave Creavures -- tiny, mythical creatures, each with a unique natural ability -- venture deep into unexplored regions of their forest home in search of Essence, the dwindling light that gives the forest its bioluminescent glow. Control two Creavures at a time and use their talents in tandem to overcome obstacles, solve puzzles, and confront the moody, giant forest guardians in their quest to restore peace to the ancient ecosystem.

  • Interactive Graphic Novel - Fanta

    • November 2012 to May 2013
    Team Members: Brian Kehrer

    Developed an interactive graphic novel engine inside Unity, with a custom UI and 2D animation system. Technical lead for a team of 2D animators. Sole developer. Built, tested, and released Play Fanta: Saving the Source on iOS and Android - with support back to iPad 1.
