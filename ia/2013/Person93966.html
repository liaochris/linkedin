Mike Barrett's Summary

I have over 12 years experience in designing, building and delivering digital publishing solutions for both brands and publishers.

With real world experience in on-line product development, I provide consultancy to businesses on all aspects of digital strategy encompassing content, architecture, design, search, social media/user generated content and ad-serving.

At CNET (now CBS Interactive), my team created some of the most sophisticated publishing sites on the web including cnet.co.uk, silicon.com and the award winning ZDNet.co.uk.

Since starting Compound Media in 2006, I have consulted for some of the best known brands in the UK including: -

* BT
* Royal Mail
* Boots
* Marks and Spencer
* Fujitsu
* British Bankers Association

and many more including the award winning NSPCC site "Your family".

A pragmatic, disciplined approach to solving business problems is complemented by the ability to speak the language of both business and technology.

Specialties

Digital publishing strategy, social media and UGC. Product development, information architecture, design, technology, ad operations and infrastructure support for on-line publishers and brands.
