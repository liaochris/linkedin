Biography: John Asher is chairman and co-founder of Asher Strategies, Inc., a global leader in sales and marketing strategies. He also serves as the managing director of Source Companies, LLC, a growth consulting and investment banking firm focused on the SME market. Asher has a Master of Science degree in administration from George Washington University, where he currently sits on the board of trustees of the business school. He attended the U.S. Naval Academy, where he earned degrees in nuclear engineering and mathematics.
