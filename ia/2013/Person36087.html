Daniella K. Thireou's Summary

My name is Daniella K. Thireou and I am a multitasking Creative Graphic/Web Designer with a strong will to make the world, if not a better place to live in, then at least optically enjoyable .

The oxymoron combination of my obsession with perfecting every single detail and my ability not to see the forest for the trees, allows me to work effectively on a wide range of areas such as designing both for the web and for print, illustration, fashion and art.

I have been involved in the creative industry since I was a teenager, studying Interior Architecture & Design, Fine Arts, and Advertising while working as a freelance muralist. I have experience in creative graphic design, web design, fashion design, traditional and digital arts and illustration, collaborating with people from and in different countries.

I have a strong passion for design and a vast love for creative technology. I always like thinking of the glass half full rather than half empty and I believe that with a big can-do attitude you can, in fact, do anything. I am imaginative and persistent, I have an aquired taste of humour and a lot of patience, to which some refer to it as a "high indicator of tolerance".

I can speak both the geek and client language fluently and accordingly...

and I can never, ever stand still!
