  • "Natten" - short film

    • November 2010 to February 2013
    Team Members: Malte Greis, Heidi Keller, Christian Winther Bergstrøm, Casper Sloth, Lasse Mogensen, Steen Bech

    Short film with subsidy from FilmFyn and Odense Film workshop. this film is in the last period of post production, the music is nearly finish and the the last sound mix will be made.

    Synopsis:
    Henrik has been promoted to a higher position at work and his wife is pregant. His life is going well, it is almost the perfect life until one night at an office party. Henrik takes his car home, and this choice changes everything about his perfect life.
    On a small road he hits a woman who dies of the junction. Henrik runs away and tries to forget the accident, but there is something that prevents him from forgetting the accident completely.

    length: 13 min.
