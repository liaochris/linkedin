Johan Erenius' Summary

My curious and creative nature have made me a very versatile artist in visual effects productions.
I have been responsible for all FX in shots for feature films where the effects is the key element for the storytelling as well as integrate 3D graphics in live action material in commercials.

My disciplined work ethic to get tasks done in time have been well credited as well as my quite awesome personality.

Specialties: Maya Dynamics, Fluids, Particles, lighting, texturing, rendering. 3DS Max Particle Flow, FumeFX & Krakatoa. Realflow simulation. Concept drawing/development.
