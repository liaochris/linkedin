Larry Bain's Summary

6 Years in the US Navy as an Electronics Technician and Nuclear Reactor Operator onboard destroyer USS Truxtun (DLGN-35) and Vietnam Veteran. Special training in Electronics, Nuclear Power, POW class, Electronic Countermeasures, Firefighting, and Weapons Fire Control.

25 Years at AT&T in Data Systems & Repair.

15 Years running my own business in Data Systems Consulting, Repair, & Sales.

My company is called Larry R. Bain, LLC. I was told that Rule Number One is to be very good at what I do. For that reason, I obtained a CompTIA A+ Certification to prove my technical abilities on computer hardware and networks. I sub-contract for Dell, HH Greg, and Social Security. If Social Security trusts me with their computers, so can you.

Each and every one of you has a computer at home or at work. They can be slow. They can be downright nasty to you. Don't throw your PC through the window. Call me or schedule a pick-up. I can teach it to treat you like a human being.
