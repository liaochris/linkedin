Maria Kontogiorgou _MaRik's Summary

MaRIK is a senior Creative UX Designer / Consultant, creates unique user centered experiences for cross media platforms. Designs Interfaces for multi-platform devices, responsive web design for brands, softwares and on-line games. She creates User experience (UX) involving a person's emotions and logical flow about using the particular product, system or service. She highlights on the experiential, affective, meaningful and valuable aspects of human-computer interaction and product ownership.

Proficient in mind map visualisation, wireframes using tools such as Axure, Omnigraffle and all adobe creative suite. Experience creating original concepts such as animation films, interactive mind maps and illustrations to visualise the Customer User Experience and ways to vehicle messages successfully internally or/and externally of a company.

Proficient in Visual design, character design, 2D vector illustration and animation, storyboards, wireframes, roughs hand drawn sketches, animatics and film animation and direction. Strong conceptual and lateral thinking, manage a team of people from various specialties and nationalities, positive and problem solver. Collaborating with brands like Nokia, Mastercard, Unilever, Nestle, Starbucks, Vauxhall, Tom Dixon, Amstel, Nike, H&M and agencies like McCann, JWT, Ogilvy, jellyfish and likes. Fluent English, French and Greek. Experience working in international environments and clients in the UK, USA, France, Russia, Japan, Belgium, Uganda, Luxembourg, Swiss, Greece, among others.

Translating visually the creative product development strategy, style guides and product development, participating on the development of an award winning and successful brand or service. Push the limits of creativity while remaining on strategy and meeting corporate objectives. Synthesize information from multiple sources into clear visual solutions.
