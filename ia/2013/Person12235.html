Bruna Calheiros' Summary

Hello, my name is Bruna!

I breathe technology and innovation and am constantly pushing myself to explore the unknown, where technology and product development are taking us.

I have a Master of Professional Studies degree from the Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP) at New York University and a Bachelor in Marketing and Advertising from ESPM-Brazil. For more than 7 years I have been working with guerrilla marketing, social media planning, web analytics, and digital strategy, creating campaigns for clients such as Volkswagen, Olympus, 3M, Coca-Cola, Unilever, and Nokia. I also have 8+ years of experience producing online content as a blogger, which provided me with the opportunity to write, produce, and host my own TV show for MTV Brazil.

I'm currently working in New York as Interaction Designer & Digital Strategist at weeSpring, a TechStars-backed startup with the power to change the way parents shop.
