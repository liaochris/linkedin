Michael Kurz's Summary

I'm a multidisciplinarian in media creation. Many industries call that a “generalist,” but I don't consider myself "general."

My priority is to be effective and I accomplish this by being sensitive to the design, production, and distribution methods that are appropriate for the intended audience and the message that needs to come across. I combine my comprehensive USC film school education, video and motion graphics work in the Mayor's Office, web design experience, and enthusiasm for emerging story telling tools to create original and successful work.

Without a doubt, I have the technical skills required for any “new media” position. I have an abundance of experience in film and video production and I'm well-versed in the Adobe Suite and Final Cut Pro. I am more than proficient in social media communications. I work well on Mac's or PCs. Additionally, I have a mastery of motion graphics creation in After Effects, photo editing and manipulation in Photoshop, and vector image creation in Adobe Illustrator. For 3.5 years I operated my own freelance web design and programming business. This experience included making simple sites based solely on HTML and CSS, blog controlled template sites, and complex sites based on integrative Flash programming.

Aside from my technical abilities, I thrive in a media production role:
- I am proficient at coordinating media projects. I'm organized.
- I work well on tight deadlines and limited or no budgets.
- I have a strong sense of integrity.
- I have always had a solid work ethic.
- I foster collaborative relationships with coworkers.

Please review my resume and portfolio website www.michaelkurz.com. I am very excited to hear from you.
