Andrea Berry's Summary

CAREER OBJECTIVE
To use my award-winning communications skills to help an organization broaden its impact, its reputation and maintain a competitive edge in a complex, changing media landscape.

SUMMARY
My professional career is characterized by my expertise to "tell the success stories" of my employers through results-based, strategic communications and marketing plans.

As a self- starter with strong interpersonal skills, I work successfully under pressure, independently or with culturally and socially diverse teams, to bring projects to completion in cost-effective and measurable ways.

Specialties

voiceover narration, online video production, social media strategies, web design, branded marketing and communications projects, web metrics and analytics; mobile marketing and application design.
