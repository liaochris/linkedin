Lynn Dye's Summary

I build relationships by partnering with clients--meeting and even anticipating a variety of needs. Beyond completing tasks, my goal is to create value for my clients through a dynamic learning process. The more I know and understand your business, the more I can help. That's why I prefer to partner with only a handful of clients rather than accepting multiple “piecework” assignments from many clients.

My areas of expertise include email and calendar management, troubleshooting email and basic computer issues, knowledgeable in computer set-up and installation of the proper security software, setting up and maintenance of social networking sites, finding and setting up the right collaboration tools for you, creating eye-popping PowerPoint presentations, developing or maintaining spreadsheest, databases or newsletters as well as writing or proofreading, I have the expertise and tools to help your business.

I use WordPress to develop and create websites for solopreneurs and small businesses. Every business needs a presence on the web and WordPress is a great content management system in addition to a premier blogging platform.

My background includes working in the finance, medical and insurance industries. I’m experienced in working remotely and am keenly aware communication and trust are key in building a productive business partnership.

I flourish in a fast paced environment and enjoy meeting deadlines. I have the ability to grasp your business’ concepts and climb into your business to positively impact your bottom line.

Specialties: Collaboration expertise, web tools, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, WordPress, Social Networking Sites
