Bryce Evans' Summary

I'm Bryce Evans, a Product Manager and small business owner living and working in Dallas, Texas. I have a passion for user centered design, and creating clean UI around functional UX, but my new obsession is building loyalty for local brands.

I have spent the last 6 years designing digital marketing software for the Automotive Industry, and I am excited about how interactive design is intersecting web, mobile, social, and other forms of media. This convergence of technology and strategy is what led me to my next adventure... Loyal Insider!
