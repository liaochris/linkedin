John Cramer's Summary

As an actor, whether working on-camera or from behind a microphone, I tend to play the guy-next-door. Conversational, credible, and engaging, I’m like your favorite teacher/coach from school who could crack some jokes or some skulls as the case warranted. Important note: I would never crack anyone’s skull, unless it was pretend.

On stage, television, or in film, my training and experience has helped me develop an ability to listen, connect, and live in the moment that leads to engaging and memorable performances. I tend to play fathers or authority figures, hapless or had-core. Important note number two: I have cracked skulls for pretend – others’ and my own.

When it comes to on-camera or voice-over industrials, I deliver great results on your schedule. As one producer I worked with put it, my “professionalism and amazing ability to carry a strong performance” saves time. And we all know that means money. Thanks to my well-equipped home voice-over studio, I can help people out with their commercial or narration needs no matter where they’re located.
