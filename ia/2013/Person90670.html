Lyndsay Johnson's Summary

After receiving her BFA from the University of Michigan, Lyndsay was a graphic designer in the marketing department of Chelsea Rhone LLC (HealthCap/HealthCap RRG), a long term care liability insurance provider based in Ann Arbor. Two years later she decided to pursue having her own design practice, Meadow Fete Media.

In addition to her formal design education she's expanded her skills to front-end web development, user experience design, information architecture, Wordpress development, and Drupal theming. She was also a Chicago style copy editor for what is now known as the Rovi Corporation for 5+ years.

Lyndsay met her husband, 3.7 Designs CEO Ross Johnson, in the Ann Arbor tech community. In 2013 the two merged companies along with Business Development partner Declan O'Neill, and she is now acting CEO, web developer, and designer.
