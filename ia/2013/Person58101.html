Jana Secord's Summary

Most businesses can not afford the going consulting rates of the big firms, but do need assistance as the small business owner is good at their specialty and not an accountant, web designer or computer trouble-shooter.

My goal is to teach my clients how to complete business tasks in the areas of accounting and technology for themselves - working my way out of a job. Small businesses can save money and I will be here if something goes wrong or if they need additional assistance during times of turn-over or at quarter and year ends or when upgrading.

As a CPA, trainer and technology expert - we can get a lot done without calling in additional parties.

Specialties

Job Cost and Project Management experience. Creation of Crystal Reports, FRx Reports and several other industry-specific report writer packages. 26 years of implementation experience in nearly 100 businesses either from manual or out-dated/out-grown systems across a wide variety of industries.
