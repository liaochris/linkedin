Carol McManus' Summary

After leaving the corporate world, I built a six-figure coaching and consulting practice in one year by effectively using social media. Today I consult with businesses and entrepreneurs on developing a solid strategy for closing more business and nurturing referral relationships using sites including LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, GooglePlus, Pinterest, and more. I teach clients to manage their social media in 17 minutes a day and integrate their efforts to support their existing marketing.

As the host of The LinkedIn Lady Show, which airs on http://toginet.com on Tues and Wed at 4 pm EST, I bring the latest tools, tips and technologies to you so you can grow your business and improve your bottom.

My newest venture is the upcoming launch of All Business Radio Network – an Internet radio network that is designed to exclusively meet the needs of the small and medium size business market, entrepreneurs and start-ups. For information, http://allbusinessradioinfo.com

As a Certified Executive Coach I work with business owners and senior executives to achieve their goals and smash records for their companies. I serve a limited number of clients at a time so that I can deliver the attention and support you need for fast results.

I bring a background in executive leadership, field operations, sales, recruiting, and maximizing franchise relationships. Regardless of the challenges you are having today, my unique SYSTEMS will save you stress, time, and energy, and laser focus your efforts directly to the bottom line!

I'm the author of "Ten Traits for Top Performers," published/distributed through Realogy. With over 25,000 copies sold, it has helped thousands of salespeople in all professions to focus on the right things to boost their success. It is available for download in mp3 format at www.ywait4success.com.
