Mark Smith's Summary

I create well designed branding systems, websites, print communications, advertising, and architectural signage that help businesses and organizations compete. I advocate for good design that supports strategic business objectives. I partner with businesses and organizations to deliver a higher level of brand awareness to gain competitive advantage. The work I do attracts new customers to your organization and instils brand loyalty by engaging them on an emotional level.

I work with other creative professionals to build the right team for the projects I work on.

I received a BFA in Communications Design from Pratt Institute and worked as a graphic designer in New York City for 10 years before moving to Maine in 1988.

While in NYC I studied with Milton Glaser at the School of Visual Arts and worked for a number of world famous design firms including Chermayeff & Geismar. I have instructed graphic design courses at the Maine College of Art.

I'm a member of AIGA, and I produced the AIGA Maine Annual Exhibit in 2010 and 2011 - an exhibit of Maine's top designers.

In my free time I enjoy being at home with my family, and I'm big fan of art, nature, music, cinema, cooking, books, science fiction and Cutler, Maine.
