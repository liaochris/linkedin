Ellie Martin Cliffe's Summary

True, I'm an editor, but my experience is so much broader than that. Thanks to my degree in communications with an emphasis in journalism, I’ve had the privilege of editing, writing, copyediting, researching and managing in a number of media outlets, from print and digital publishing to marketing and radio.

Words are practically my obsession...typos drive me crazy, and I shout grammar corrections at people on TV. Whether I'm crafting my own pieces or helping others to perfectly express themselves through words, I know I'm incredibly lucky to have a career that I love.
