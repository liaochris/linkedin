President

LEGEND Talent Management LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

May 2011 – Present (2 years 9 months)

LEGEND Talent Management is dedicated to ensuring that the right people are in the right jobs... at the right companies! Whether we're working directly with individuals or employers, our goal is to help foster the placement of individuals in positions where they will be most productive, engaged, and, most of all.. happy.

INDIVIDUALS: We work primarily with mid-career professionals who are re-evaluating their chosen path. We have found that many spend the first half of their careers doing simply what they believed they were "supposed" to do. They took jobs right out of college, worked long hours, got promotions and pay raises, and supported their families. But at the end of the day, their only fulfillment comes from the paycheck that they receive.

We believe that people can LOVE their work and find great meaning and fulfillment in it. For those who are ready to transition from "success to significance" and re-engineer their career paths, we can assist with career coaching and assessment services that will help match you to the work that you were made for.

COMPANIES: We are here to help organizations achieve the highest levels of success with their employees. We are committed to helping companies hire the right people for the right positions, and then to helping them train, coach, and develop those employees to their highest potential.

As a Strategic Business Partner for Profiles International, we use a number of assessment tools to help create performance models for success for any position at your company. We can help you determine the "formula" for success in any given position at your company, helping you to increase your chances of hiring the right person for the job.

We can also help you coach your employees to greater success, create customized leadership development programs for them, and identify future leaders for your company through strategic workforce planning.

The RIGHT people in the RIGHT jobs at the RIGHT companies = SUCCESS
