Andrea Nakayama's Summary

Hello. I'm Andrea Nakayama. I am a Certified Nutrition Educator and Certified Holistic Health Counselor practicing Functional Whole-Foods Nutrition from my home in Portland, Oregon. I counsel adults, children, and entire families (both here in Portland and around the globe), to foster the changes that stimulate rejuvenation. Through food and supplemental therapies I work from the core, to heal the gut, balance hormones and support the detoxification organs. In these ways, we alleviate longstanding health issues that may have resulted in mood, sleep, weight, inflammatory and immune disfunction. My intent is often to educate about the physiological imbalances that may cause these undesirable symptoms, while also teaching about the empowering aspects of food for healing.

It is through this comprehensive nutrition therapy that I will help you uncover your own inner resources to invite your unique propensity to thrive. I believe that the body yearns to heal itself. My mission is to open the channels to welcome this nourishment and revitalization. My approach is based on a wide variety of dietary and food-preparation techniques. Their commonality is that they all have their roots in whole foods. With this foundation I have found that I can uncover the keys to support each individual’s needs. My guidance dovetails with other therapies, when needed. This allows clients and students to realize their greatest potential for health, happiness, and vitality.

My love of, and expertise in cooking and food preparation enables me to advise how you can easily translate my nutritional recommendations into tasteful, beautiful and nourishing meals to satisfy every craving and the entire family.
