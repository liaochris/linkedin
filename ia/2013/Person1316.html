Adam Chernick's Experience

Director of Design

The Fieldhouse Project

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Fund-Raising industry

July 2013 – Present (6 months) Seattle, Washington

- Was an integral component in the founding of "The Fieldhouse Project", a nonprofit organization that will design and fund a repeatable 1500 square foot multi-use classroom building in Nyanyano, Ghana.
- The Fieldhouse Project strives to catalyze and expand upon the scope of the efforts of our NGO partner in Ghana, the Cheerful Hearts Foundation, in regards to their work against child labor.
- Designed The Fieldhouse based off user needs and efficiency. Specifically The Fieldhouse is designed to capitalize on cost, time, construction, energy, user, and waste efficiency.
- The Fieldhouse has gone through schematic and preliminary design. Working drawings are being created in Revit and will be converted to metric units and Ghanaian nominal sized lumber for construction.

All rendering was done in Revit and post processed in Adobe CS6.

For more information regarding The Fieldhouse Project please visit www.fieldhouseproject.org
