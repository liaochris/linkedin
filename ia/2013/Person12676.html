Izabela Wojcik's Summary

Izabela Wójcik is a Polish-American multidimensional artist and ornamental metalwork and lighting designer.

For the past seven years, and counting, as the official creative director/designer for Potter Art Metal Studios, Izabela has been designing custom metalwork and lighting for top designers in the country as well as her other fabulous clients.

Izabela's first love is sculpture and painting, but she also loves to work on graphic design, and glass art. She adores getting her hands messy with clay, paint and whatever other fantastic medium she wants to experience. Her most fun piece of art is a 400 pound bronze sculpture of a tortoise she named Henry. Izabela’s artwork has been displayed at the Dallas Museum of Art.

Izabela has a degree in Liberal Arts, and simply adores any kind of creative movement; she sometimes writes poetry.

Specialties

Custom ornamental ironwork and gas & electric fixture design (which we then custom build, right here at Potter Art Metal Studios... we also work with your own designs.) Bronze sculpture, oil paintings, glass artwork.
