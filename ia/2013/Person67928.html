Joni Roberts' Summary

Joni Roberts is a MBA professor, strategy expert, international entrepreneur and catalyst for transforming business into a force for good.

She is the founder of Evolution Connection and the creator of the only Mini MBA for Changemakers™ - an innovative highly experiential 10 week certification course designed to bring great ideas into reality while embracing EC’s 7Ps Philosophy: positivity, people, purpose, products, potential, planet + profit .

Joni is passionate about supporting entrepreneurs to take action and succeed. Her workshops “Turn Your Purpose Into Profits” and “Brilliant Biz Idea?” help people transition into their authentic work and create viable business models that serve others and make a positive impact on the world.

Some of her favorite topics are how the digital age and the age of purpose are enabling individuals to step into their power and change the world one person, one community at a time.

Her consulting / facilitation style is highly engaging with a focus on delivering high value content and taking action to make it real.

Joni earned her MBA in International Business from the prestigious Moore School of Business, University of South Carolina and Wirtschaftsuniversitat in Vienna Austria. She is Harvard Management Certified in 17 topics including strategic thinking & execution, innovation, leadership, finance and marketing. As a top ranked MBA professor for Keller Graduate School of Management of DeVry University, she has taught a diverse range of topics including social entrepreneurship, social media marketing, brands with social missions, management, motivation, leadership, strategic planning, information technology, quality and legal issues which has prepared her perfectly to create and deliver the Mini MBA for Changemakers™ courses as well as offer strategic consulting services.

Evolution Connection is a Business Partner of the Charter for Compassion.
