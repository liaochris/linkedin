Anthony Marlowe's Summary

Anthony Marlowe served as the CEO of TMone from its inception in 2003 when he co-founded the company to 2007. Marlowe is a principal and now serves as the company’s President. Marlowe was recognized by Inc. Magazine in 2007 as one of the 25 youngest CEOs of the 5000 fastest growing companies in America. TMone has been on Inc's List for 6 years in a row. (2007-2012)

TMone is a domestic Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) firm with extensive and well known business-to-business (B2B) expertise. TMone also has significant prowess in consumer customer service. In challenging economic times, TMone continues to create innovative, cost effective, long term customer acquisition, retention and customer service solutions for Fortune 1000 companies. As a result, TMone is consistently ranked as one of our client’s top performing outsourced partners and an integral part of our client’s CRM strategy at every stage.

Prior to launching TMone, Marlowe served as the VP of Business Development at Infinity. While there he increased the annual BPO revenue by 50 percent. In addition, Marlowe also has held senior sales and management roles with May International and MCI. During his time with MCI, Marlowe consistently performed in the top one percent. At the age of 22, Marlowe directly led an organization of over 200 Agents, Trainers and Supervisors and became the youngest member of management to be inducted into the MCI Hall of Fame. His contributions were consistently recognized by MCI executives with several awards including MCI's “Best of the Best” and MVP for his work in Arlington, VA.

Marlowe maintains relationships with many industry leaders across a variety of industries from Telecommunications to NASCAR. In addition to representing TMone’s services, Marlowe is a sounding board for executives nationally when it comes to their outsourcing and CRM strategies.

A native of LaGrange, IL Marlowe is a Greek American and now resides in Iowa City, IA.
