Brad Chmielewski's Projects

  • Viadeo, a Professional Social Network

    • June 2012 to June 2012
    Team Members: Brad Chmielewski, Sergio Salgado, Jacob Williams

    Client: Viadeo
    Production Company: LooseKeys
    Producer: Maeve Price
    Director: Sergio Salgado
    Creative Director: Brad Chmielewski
    Animation: Brad Chmielewski & Jake Williams
    Editor: Maeve Price
    Talent: Katie Sirles, Jack Danger, Steve Mosqueda, Brad Stark, Jake Williams and Marisa Pochter

    Viadeo is a professional social network with over 35 million members worldwide. They approached our friends over at Loose Keys to produce a live action explainer video with a strong focus on brand culture. LK reached out to Furnace to help shoot the footage that would eventually have tracking, rotoscoping and motion graphics overlaid.

    LK came up with a series of vignettes of people connecting through the use of the Viadeo site. LK and Furnace then worked in a highly collaborative fashion to make sure we got the shots we needed. Not only did the footage need to be technically sound but it needed to communicate the stories in a quick and smart way.

    The final piece is a fresh look at some of the ways to use the Viadeo site and delivers a concise message through video and motion graphics. We couldn’t be happier with the result.
