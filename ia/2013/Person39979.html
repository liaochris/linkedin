Eric Egeland, CPCU, AU's Summary

He has personally created ten successful start-ups, half reaching eight figure annual revenues, and has consulted on or assisted in hundreds of projects, improvements, startups, strategies, funding’s, turnarounds, reorganizations, sales, and closures.
