Canan Özkal's Summary

I have been working on e-commerce and online business since 2004. I studied Marketing and Public Relations at the university and I have Digital Marketing certificate. I work as a General Coordinator at ETİD (Association of Turkish Internet Retailers) and a Corporate Communication Manager at akakce.com. Before that I was the Marketing Manager of Evmanya.com (the first household goods and decoration vertical e-commerce site in Turkey)
The main areas I am working in are e-commerce, b2c marketing strategies and PR. I worked as an e-commerce and marketing professional for more than 8 years in Turkey.

Specialties: e-commerce, business strategy, marketing communication, partnerships management, project management, media planning and buying, digital marketing, business development, social media marketing, e-mail marketing, vertical e-commerce models, Turkish internet market, product development, Google Analytics, public relations, entrepreneurship, e-commerce marketing
