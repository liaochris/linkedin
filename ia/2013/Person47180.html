Gary Oldenburger, J.D., LL.M.'s Summary

I served on active duty in the United States Air Force for nearly ten years. I completed my JD from Drake Law School in December of 2010, and an LLM in criminal law at Drake in December of 2011. I am now an assistant county attorney in Wapello County. I am the Violence Against Women Act grant prosecutor, I prosecute crimes of family violence including domestic assault, harassment, stalking, and child endangerment. I prosecute offenses related to domestic violence and sexual assault including burglary, kidnapping, arson, and violations of protective orders. I also prosecute sexually-based offenses and I am a member of the Wapello County Sexual Assault Response Team.
