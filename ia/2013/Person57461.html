As a professionally certified mediator my role is to bring parties together to resolve disputes without resolving to go to court and appear before a judge. The wide range and scope of mediation topics and activities may range from business contract disputes, to small claims court cases, civil harassment issues, community disputes (neighbor/neighbor), appeals of small claims court judgments, and may also address issues between family members. The OCHRC is part of the ADRA Colaborative group of public service organizations and offer free and low cost services to the community at large also. Mediations may take place in the office in Santa Ana, or in one of the State Court facilities for issues that have reached the pre-trial stage, or at other sites of mutual agreeement. Sessions have no defined time element, the objective is to work together to arrive a a fair solution for the parties.

Chief Executive Officer/COO

RDS Inc. (Refrigerated Distribution Services)

December 1979 – August 1987 (7 years 9 months)

RDS was a highly unique enterprise; specializing in the FDA, ICC, & PUC regulated transportation and storage of frozen human blood plasma, operating under long term conttracts with major pharmaceutical organizations. Frozen blood plasma was transported at -25F and was tracked with temperature recorders at all times. RDS also performed export shipping of frozen blood plasma to Europe and Japan via air freight. RDS led the way with the development of "The Warehouse Manager", one of the first PC-based WMS systems (allowing for many features previously only available on main frame systems). Equipment used by RDS incorporated dual (redundant) refrigeration systems (operating on F502 & Liquid Nitrogen) to ensure superior reliability. No claim for loss of control or damage to product was ever made against RDS. Contracts required highly reliable, on schedule services and the professionals that made up the organization met or exceeded the clients needs at all times.
