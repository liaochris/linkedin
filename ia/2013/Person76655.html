Koty Neelis' Summary

Koty Neelis is a freelance writer, notably for Portable.tv, Thought Catalog, and others with over five years experience providing a variety of content to both web and print publications including but not limited to: first person essays, travel columns, newspaper articles, blogs, featured profiles, interviews, music features, entertainment news, human interest stories, and more.

Her background and education includes experience with Microsoft applications (Word, Excel, Office, Access), Wordpress, In Design, Photoshop, HTML, C#, SQL, both Mac and PC platforms, traditional forms of journalism, multimedia reporting, copy editing, proofreading, social media marketing, AP style, writing content for web, and SEO.

Her e-book "Not All Those Who Wander Are Lost: Tales and Tips For a First Time Traveler" will be published on Amazon by Thought Catalog Digital Books this fall.
