Roberta Murphy's Summary

I am a seasoned San Diego real estate broker with a market focus on San Diego's coastal North County. I love what I do and where I live, and really enjoy showing newcomers what life has to offer in this paradise.

In a prior life, I worked as a journalist and taught communications at the community college level. It is good that real estate allows for the use of those skills!

These days, I enjoy peering over the bloody edge of the envelope to see where we are headed. Technologies and Web 2.0 are moving at the speed of light and thought--and connections occur more quickly than ever.

I suppose that is why I am here!

Specialties

San Diego relocation, luxury real estate, beach and golf properties, gated communities, second and vacation homes, real estate investments.
