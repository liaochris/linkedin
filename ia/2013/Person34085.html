Denise Hopkins, architect's Summary

Improving your world one space at a time - through architecture, interiors, landscapes.

Have you ever been in a room that made you feel fabulous? Or have an office that makes you want to go to work? Rooms like these fill you with a sense of well-being and serenity. Your home could be a sanctuary. Your work place could energize and inspire.

As a licensed architect and LEED Accredited Professional, I can provide the expertise to help you make the most sustainable choices for you, your family, and the planet. You deserve to live and work in spaces where you feel happy, comfortable, and that bring out your best. Let ImaginEco Design that for you.

Need a team leader, team member, or consultant to guide you through any or all design phases?
- site selection
- programming
- LEED certification
- architecture, interiors, landscapes
- selection of the best materials and products

Want to learn more about sustainable architecture, interiors, and landscape design? I speak to many different businesses, schools, professional groups, and social groups. My talk is customized to your needs. I guarantee you will learn, and it will be fun!
