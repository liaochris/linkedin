5thGM "Never Sacrifice Creativity"'s Summary

5thGM's goal is to promote and encourage the visual evolution of your company. Utilizing, but not limited to, techniques in advertising, branding/identity, and illustration, 5thGM implements unique, creative, and fundamentally sound strategies and campaigns to provide both a solid foundation within your target market as well as a new and highly evolved visual aesthetic.

"Never Sacrifice Creativity" is a belief stood strongly by and applied to any and all projects no matter the level of simplicity or complexity, encouraging visual evolution.

Specialties

Advertising, Branding/Identity, Illustration, Typography, Web Design, and Animation Techniques.
