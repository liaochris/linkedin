Tom Metcalfe's Summary

Currently, I'm the Producer of REACT Objects Sandbox based in the Pervasive Media Studio at Watershed.

I'm a designer, with a specialism in object design, physical-digital and design thinking. My background is both in academia and industry, having recently worked in senior positions at both rAndom International and the Product Research Studio.

I'm co-founder of Fieldguide, an occasional collective and sometimes journal for people interested in design, technology, society and the intersections where they overlap.

My collaborations and employment have resulted in my work being presented and exhibited at, amongst others, the Wellcome Trust, British Library, V&A and Design Miami. I've also tutored on Product Design Master programmes and talked and delivered workshops internationally, including SXSW, Sotheby's and NID India.

I've also been included in first instalment of the recently launched SyncList – profiling 'people doing remarkable things where technology meets the arts'.

Tom Metcalfe's Projects

  • Interactive Newsprint

    • August 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Tom Metcalfe, Paul Egglestone, Jon Rogers, David Frohlich, Kate Stone

    Interactive Newsprint is constructing a new and radical digital platform for news and
    information with a difference. It's made from paper.
    Using printed electronics technology provided by Novalia, co-design techniques and a multidisciplinary academic research team, Interactive Newsprint has created internet-enabled paper that is responsive to human touch. Our prototypes, which move away from screen- based media and into the realm of the 'Internet of Things' allow readers – via a wireless pair of headphones or embedded speaker – to hear dynamic audio content, vote in polls or 'like' stories by simply touching certain areas of a static printed page. Audio material on each printed sheet can be updated by simply emailing audio to the paper itself.
