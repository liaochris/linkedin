Kymberly Brantigan's Summary

The Charlotte Destination Group, is a corporate meeting, event and destination management company. We offer turnkey services to organizations hosting meetings, conferences and conventions in the greater Charlotte. We offer teambuilding activities and corporate incentive programs. Charlotte, NC is the hub of NASCAR racing and we specialize in motorsports, transportation, area tours, themed events and celebrity appearances/speakers. We are proud to have won the transportation bid for the Democratic National Convention (DNC) and we were also chosen to host a themed delegate welcome event for the 2012 DNC.

Specialties: We specialize in purpose-driven corporate and b2b entertainment. We are known for unique customized events and itineraries, and flawless execution and are recipients of the Visit Charlotte Service Partner of the Year Award.

We also specialize in media relations creating unique story ideas and obtaining positive exposure for our clients - getting their messages out to their target audiences.
