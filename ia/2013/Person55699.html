Paul Perry's Summary

Paul Perry is an entrepreneurial team player specializing in managing software development for web, desktop and iOS applications. For almost two decades, Paul Perry has utilized his technological expertise to enhance productivity for start ups and established companies. As the current IT Director for Precision Television, Global Technology Services and xoGen, Inc., Paul is responsible for creating and maintaining software development as well as overseeing the daily system operations of all three companies.

Leveraging technology to solve challenges and generate new business opportunities is one of Paul’s critically innate talents. Paul has consistently been a key player in the development of unique hardware and software solutions for every customer base he’s encountered.

Notably, during his three years with PixelOutpost.com, Paul was directly responsible for creating, implementing and maintaining all of the necessary systems for the fledgling start up company. As the architect for their product manufacturing systems and online functionality, Paul was instrumental in the company’s growth and success. While working at PixelOutpost, Paul simultaneously founded and launched the computer repair and consulting company, iPaulTech, which he still runs today.

Paul’s keen ability to create and implement original, highly successful solutions for contrasting philosophical and financial conditions is also apparent in his industry history. Throughout his career, Paul has used his talents to assess individual situations, create custom solutions and develop systems to increase productivity and cut costs.

He is particularly adept at collaborating and identifying process improvements with “C” level managers, IT personnel, software developers, sales staff and clients. Managing engineers from the USA, India, Philippines & Russia has also given Paul a discerning sensitivity to cultural differences that enables clear communication between program teams throughout the world.
