Alan Landers' Summary

I've spent more than 35 years as an OD and Training consultant helping organizations create learning interventions and business strategies to maximize bottom line results. I specialize in designing and leading major organizational change and providing training needed to improve organizational effectiveness. I've held the positions of VP of Training, VP of OD, and VP of Performance Management throughout my career.

I was mentored by:
Bill Pfeiffer (Organizational Development, Intervention Design & Implementation)
Larry Porter (Facilitation, Experiential Learning, Action Learning)
Stephen Haines (Strategic Planning, Consulting, Performance Management)
Joseph Weinbrecht (Strategic Training Management, Talent Development, Leadership)

My consulting clients include: General Motors, AT&T, Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, Texas Instruments, HJ Heinz, Rhone-Poulenc, Medtronic, New York Power Authority, Dun & Bradstreet, and many others.

I've delivered executive-level workshops at: Washington State University, Auburn University, Texas A&M University, Southern Methodist University, UC San Diego, San Diego State University, UC Davis, Michigan State University, and Boise State University, among others.

In January I became CEO of FirstStep Communications, LLC, a BPO that specializes in providing live leads to mortgage professionals. Just recently I became Dir. of Programs & Operations for a non-profit organization whose mission is to stop youth violence. So I am quite busy with two jobs and loving every minute of it.

Please visit my website at www.FirstStepTraining.net or www.1stStepCommunications.com. If you are interested in the non-profit, that website is www.tkf.org
