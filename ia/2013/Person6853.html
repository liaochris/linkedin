Andrew Frankel's Summary

Leknarf Labs is a software development consultancy, specializing in providing flexible engineering resources to startups and small businesses.

Without any upfront commitments, you can work with one of our developers, full-time, in your own office. We understand that your business needs can change rapidly in the real world. Unlike full-time employees, our team doesn't mind suspending projects while waiting for external providers. Unlike freelancers, our team can ramp quickly if your business grows faster than expected.

Leknarf Labs provides the level of service you expect from full-time employees with the flexibility you need from contractors.
