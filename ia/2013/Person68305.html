Joel Ordesky's Summary

Senior executive with over 15 years experience in leading and managing operations for companies with revenues in excess of 35 million. Expertise in P&L management; video, audio, other communication and computer systems; risk-management and insurance; contract development, negotiations, and compliance; and database management. Strengths in strategic and business planning, team development and building, written and verbal communication, employee coaching and budgeting. Extensive experience in expense/cost-management and budgeting, OSHA and safety, human resources compliance, labor-management relations. Analytical planner and conservative risk-taker who develops creative solutions in demanding environments. Effective in union and non-union environments.
