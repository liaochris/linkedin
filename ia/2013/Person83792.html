Lynn Ryan's Summary

My life has been an incredible journey filled with success, failure and everything in between!

On January 1, 2008 after 22 years of business I sold the business and retired, but let me be the first to tell you it was years upon years of trials and tribulation. Success and failures alike…

I started my career driving a truck for more than 12 years. In 1986, I wanted a better life for my family and myself. The question was, "What Business?"

Interesting enough, I was approached by a friend who wanted a partner in the screen-printing industry. I knew this was my answer to building a better future. Even though I had to keep my trucking job, but I could do the screen-printing shop on evenings/weekends. With a wife and two sons, I borrowed $1,000.00 and started my life as an entrepreneur.

In 1989 I quit my full time job as a truck driver and devoted my full attention to Ryan Screen Supply. The hours were long and hard, but I spent more time with my family. My dreams were finally coming true.

To make a long story short I built the company to 3.8 million in sales and over 30 employees.

In 2007 I sold my company of 20+ years and YES I retired.

Four years later I found out that I needed to work again. I have to much to offer and I wanted to help people. So I started to look for a solid company that met my requirements. What I wanted in a company was a long history in it's industry. A rock solid stable company. What I found was ORKIN.

These are just a few of the reasons I now work for Orkin Pest Control

All exterminators are not necessarily experts. Experience and training make the difference. Orkin has over 111 years of experience, In 2012 Orkin was ranked in the top 125 of all companies that train their employees. As a matter of fact Orkin ranks 52nd highest. The Orkin Man™ undergoes rigorous training on the latest technology and responsible treatments. At Orkin, we strive to provide the most effective and customized treatments for all kinds of pests. Guaranteed.

Specialties

Creating a team of energy consultants with the same passion as I have. Paying it forward and the power of a referral.
