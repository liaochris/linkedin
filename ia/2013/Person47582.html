George Jennings' Summary

Core competencies include:

• Worksite Wellness: Implementation of successful, evidence-based and sustainable worksite health promotion programs; integration of employee health management goals with overall organizational goals while engaging wellness champions and management; achieve successful outcomes of cost reduction and improved productivity through worksite health promotion programs.

• Lifestyle Change: Well-versed in the trans-theoretical model of behavioral change, client fitness and nutritional assessment, risk factor screening, and exercise planning and instruction; adept at planning and implementing customized plans to engage and motivate clients toward fundamental changes to their lifestyle resulting in dramatic health and fitness improvements.

• Weight Management: Superior understanding of optimum nutritional planning for overall health and well being, with personal history of success in nutritional modification for substantially increased health. Utilized proven guidelines to promote life-long behavioral change related to eating and nutrition.

• Education: Employ professional experience in public speaking, training development, and workshop / seminar delivery to create customized instructional approaches tailored to learning styles of groups and individuals; utilize high-energy, participatory teaching style to drive performance and encourage achievement.

• Client Relations: Proven history of consulting with and identifying needs of top business professionals, with background of success in systems design, sales and delivery; leverage professional background to rapidly establish a relationship and rapport as a trusted partner in achievement of client goals and overcome emotional barriers.

• Fitness: Broad-based proficiency in physiological assessment techniques, nutritional coaching, and safe and effective exercise instruction spanning indoor / outdoor cardiovascular, strength, flexibility, balance, and meditation for optimal health and wellness.
