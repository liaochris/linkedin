Colin Hayhurst's Summary

After 5 years helping startups, I am now back hands on, and thoroughly enjoying life at my new startup GoScale. GoScale was an evolution of StackBlaze which I founded with James Cunningham in 2011 in the UK. In May 2012 we founded GoScale, spent some time and raised some seed capital in Silicon Valley.

Previously I was a co-founder and CTO of Century Dynamics Limited, a scientific and engineering software company. Having successfully grown the company it was sold to NASDAQ listed ANSYS in 2005. Shortly after I decided to leave to pursue new challenges.

At Century Dynamics I started out developing our first product, gradually developing a team around me and eventually leading four product teams. Later took over as CEO, opened offices on three continents, lead a sales team and acquired another business.

After this exit, between 2006 and 2011, I was lucky enough to work closely with around 100 startups . I helped these early stage technology, internet and knowledge based businesses with:

- Investment readiness
- IP
- Business models
- Recruitment (I once set-up an executive search operation)
- Connections
- Mentoring

Most of all I loved mentoring entrepreneurs and they seem to have appreciated it, for reasons I'm not entirely clear about.

More recently I've been an angel investor and advisor to various startups but please note that I'm making no more investments myself.

I have worked in and with a larger number of universities in commercial and research roles. The former in entrepreneurship programmes and the latter in physical sciences.

I am a chartered engineer with experience in a several industry sectors. My passion is software and I have been programming since 1976.

Technical Director
