Linda Eisenstein's Experience

Founder

Clothes Befitting

Retail industry

October 2010 – Present (3 years)

Clothes Befitting is a Specialty Boutique Website created by,
and for, "The Real Women". Not just for the size zero's that model todays Fashion.
Womens bodies come in many different shapes and sizes. To add to the shopping difficulty factor, they are always changing through the stages of life. As in, childbirth and menopause, just to name a few. This can easily cause women to become discouraged and frustrated.
My dream is to create a smile on every face and instill a sense of confidence to every woman that shops in my store by providing her with the necessary tools to make informed fashion decisions.
My site is based on body shapes and has suggestions that go along with every piece of clothing I sell. Please join me on this journey to make shopping a pleasure. Let's have some fun-girls!

Body Type Fashion Specialist

Clothes Befitting

Retail industry

2010 – Present (3 years)

My pursuit is to be sure that every woman that shops at Clothes Befitting is purchasing the clothing that will best suit their individual body type. I do this by personally trying on and reviewing every piece that comes in and classifying it into the different shapes that would look fabulous wearing them.

Purchasing and National Sales

Yarde Metals

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; rs; Mining & Metals industry

1979 – 2010 (31 years)

Through the years, I saw Yarde Metals grow from a company of 10 to over 800 employees. Some of my numerous positions included- Inventory Control Manager, Credit Manager and Purchasing Agent. I managed the Switch Board team for 2 years and then created a Sales Support Team. The last and my most satisfying position was Inside Sales, in particular National Sales. Yarde Metals was a life long experience full of many wonderful lessons that can only be learned by living them. I thank you Craig Yarde from the bottom of my heart for preparing me for the most difficult, yet most rewarding career of all-Entrepreneurship.
Sometimes being lucky enough to work for the right Company and with the right people is worth about a million times more than any degree could ever be.
That journey has brought me to a very special place. The reawakening of a passion inside. My dream is to help women that are searching for a personal style that will make them feel beautiful, comfortable and confident in their own skin.
I am so excited to see where my new adventure will take me!
