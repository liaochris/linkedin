Louise Paglen's Summary

Email: louise@intelisure.com * Web: http://www.intelisure.com
Phone: (800) 242-7250

Insurance Broker - Licensed in NC, SC, FL, GA, VA, PA

I am the founder and owner of INTELISURE, a multi-state independent insurance agency providing risk management and insurance consulting to law firms, professionals and small businesses. The primary mission of INTELISURE is to provide confidential, intelligent, independent and trusted insurance services to professionals and small businesses. Our primary focus is helping other professionals protect their reputations and assets by recommending appropriate and competitively priced insurance programs.

We specialize in professional liability insurance for law firms and proudly represent The Hartford, Travelers, LawyerGuard and Attorney Protective. We have access to many other excellent carriers, including Liberty, Zurich, Darwin and Carolina Casualty through our national network.

NC Licensed Attorney and Mediator

I am available to associate with other counsel on insurance related matters, including appeals. As a NCDRC Superior Court certified mediator, I offer mediation services for cases involving complex professional liability and insurance related claims throughout North Carolina.

In my local community, I represent families involved in abuse and neglect proceedings. I also offer mediation services to families and individuals on an as needed basis.
