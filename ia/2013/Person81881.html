Liz Barron's Summary

"I help people to realize their potential, by taking a fresh perspective on themselves and what might be limiting them, and enable them to connect with where and who they want to be in career, business and life.

I really want to play a part in the re-energising of Ireland Inc, by enabling people to capitalise on their strengths and achieve their goals, whether that is through starting or expanding a business, developing as a leader, exploring career options, finding work or simply their true passion in life.
This involves clients choosing to change their own behaviours and perceptions, and allows them to finally be true to themselves."

Liz is a qualified coach and experienced facilitator and trainer, and with a proven track record in collaborating and communicating with clients, she has successfully engaged with many individuals and teams to help them to realize their goals.

Liz offers Career Coaching and Entrepreneur & Executive Coaching, to help people achieve their goals in work and life, and has worked with people across a wide range of roles and levels in sectors such as Medicine, Manufacturing, HR, Finance, Education and the Public Sector.

With an experienced background in IT, Liz has worked with professionals at all levels in ICT, Consulting, Project & Change Management.

Being a parent of a child with dyslexia, and having worked with many clients who feel limited by dyslexia or other learning difficulties, Liz is particularly passionate about working with all clients in a way that suits their unique learning & communications styles.

She also coaches Students from Transition Year upwards to realize their potential.
