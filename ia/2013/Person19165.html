Django Steenbrink's Summary

(More info on djangosteenbrink.nl/cv/ + infographics, files I made will be send on request.)

Hello! My name is Django steenbrink and I was born in Uden (The Netherlands) on the 16th of April, 1990.
As a kid I was already very active with painting, using my own, self-made style. I looked at the world in a different way and I was a very quick learner (that was something my teachers told me). At the age of 9, I was already busy fixing simple computer problems at my school. In my last year in that school, my teachers told me I was a special kind of a student and child. Maybe they told me that, because I always wanted to know how things worked.

After primary school, I went to Udens College. My love for the IT branch was growing rapidly. I spoke with alot of IT specialists there and some times I helped them with their work. We even set up a lan-party for charity.
At that time, I was also busy with setting up websites like ComputerhulpWW (Computer help worldwide) to help people with their PC problems (it even got posted on the schools website).
Together with a clan website, I also created a small and simple game website using HTML.
Because I had a clan, I wanted to learn how a server worked (this is what created my love for Open source), But back then it was too difficult for me. For that reason I began helping others with their websites. I loved it. In my 3rd year on that school, a friend and I created the name Byteflowstudio and placed it on a free subdomain. We had enough project ideas so we began working on them.

But both of us kept it on a low profile, because we didn’t have the age to do something really big.
When I graduated at the Udens college I chose to go to the ROC de Leijgraaf to study IT. Even these teachers thought that I was a quick learner.
In the years that followed, Byteflowstudio relaunched itself with new, creative and refreshing projects.
Currently I am busy with BFS and projects and managing there teams.
