Matt Wright's Summary

I am a senior digital creative with over 15 years experience in developing innovative ways for audiences to engage with brands and products.

Over the years I’ve worked as a Creative Director, Art Director and Creative Lead on projects ranging from desktop application designs, mobile application designs, website designs, microsites, online communications & digital campaigns.

Conceptually strong, yet with a solid grasp of UX, interaction principles and accessibility, I have worked for leading clients and brands including: BBC, Visa, HSBC, RBS, Peugeot, Virgin, Unilever, Vodafone, O2, Sony, Orange, Channel 4, BT, ITV, Reckitt Benckiser, Dulux, Sainsburys, Renault & William Hill.

Highly skilled in creative strategy, concepting and creative direction, I enjoy working with integrated teams including copywriters, UX professionals, designers and account handlers to produce highly effective, award-winning work.

Experienced at managing multiple projects to strict deadlines while collaborating with clients, guiding and advising them to a successful outcome.

I have built up a wealth of technical knowledge and understanding of many technologies including Flex. In recent years, I have gained significant experience within an Agile/SCRUM environment and thrive within the iterative and incremental approach required by them.

Specialties

Digital Design, Application Design, App Design, Agile, SCRUM, Branding and Identity, Online communications, Web Design, Art Direction, Team Creation, Interactive Design
