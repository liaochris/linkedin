Danny-J Johnson's Summary

Danny-J is a well-known and respected fitness expert, social media personality, personal trainer and speaker. She started her career as a personal trainer out of an unfortunate experience that left her paralyzed and having to teach herself the body mechanics to walk again. She found a love for helping and teaching others along with the love of nutrition and began working especially with weight-loss clients. Her biggest goal is to help others see the beauty in themselves and the confidence in themselves, even before they "lose the weight."
She is the owner of The Sweaty Betties, a fun, educational, and truthful movement to help women have access to the education and truth that the "diet" industry isn't sharing.
Her mission is to give and serve others and to continue to spread a message of awareness and to continue to travel the world!

Masters of Health Promotion and Exercise Science
NASM Certified Personal Trainer
ACE Certified Lifestyle and Wellness Coach

Specialties: Weight loss, figure competitions, public speaking, seminars, nutrition expert, fitness modeling, social media, being awesome.
