KEVIN HILL's Summary

Kevin Hill is a Licensed REALTOR®-Sales Associate for Keller Williams Valley Realty in Woodcliff Lake, NJ. I have over 12 years of experience in real estate as an appraiser & real estate agent covering the Pascack Valley and surrounding Bergen County, New Jersey.

I use all the latest technologies to market your home. Internet, social networking including Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, & YouTube, video, and blogs are all utilized to give your property the maximum exposure you need. I earned the prestigious Internet Marketing Specialist Designation (IMSD) to held find more buyers, list & sell properties successfully, and have high customer satisfaction ratings.

With my appraising background, I can analyze your local area and best determine what your home is worth in this current economic climate and price your home to sell.

My goal, is your #1 goal, to get your home sold at the maximum amount possible, in the shortest amount of time, and make the transaction as smooth as possible.

I also love working with buyers because I love to help people discover the pride that comes with home ownership and guiding them through the process and make their transaction go as smooth as possible. I especially love first time home buyers!

Parents of special needs children, I understand what you go through, as a parent of a child with autism myself. Let me help you buy a house in an area with good special needs services. I can help!

I offer all my selling clients an aggressive, 90 Point Marketing & Action Plan that enables me to sell their home in the shortest amount of time and for the most money possible.

I offer several guarantees:

-If at anytime you aren't satisfied with my services, I will withdraw your listing with me, unconditionally!

-I guarantee at least weekly communication every Monday with showing activity & feedback, neighborhood sold comparables & new listings, and internet listing activity. If I fail to do this, you can fire me!
