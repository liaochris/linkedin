Anthony C Taylor-Business Strategy Consultant's Summary

My name is Anthony Taylor, and I run a business strategy consulting and management consulting firm based in downtown Vancouver, Canada.

We work exclusively with small and medium sized businesses to help them with their marketing strategy and strategic planning.

Clients call me:
- If they are looking to grow but dont know what to do
- If they are facing new competitors
- If they need to update their marketing messages
- If they are unsure of the strategic direction of their business
- If they are facing challenges that they don't know how to solve
- If they are looking for new connections or strategic partners
- If they need someone to help them manage the growth of their business
- If they want an outside opinion on whats going on in their business
- If they want to manage the risk of a new business initiative.
