Kathryn Hawkins' Summary

I'm principal and content strategy director of Eucalypt Media, a boutique content marketing agency based in Scarborough, Maine.

What's content marketing? It's about helping a business share its values with prospects through rich, meaningful, engaging content. It's about serving the prospect instead of selling to her. It's about translating jargon into language that embraces readers. It's about helping businesses establish themselves as thought leaders. Most of all, it's about what I've always loved: storytelling.

I've helped companies and organizations including Intuit, Razoo, Bizo, GEICO, Husson University, and dozens more build their brands with content that makes an impact, increases their credibility, builds SEO backlinks, and gets noticed. (One recent custom content campaign had more than 20 million views.)

In my role at Eucalypt Media, I consult on online and offline content marketing strategies; write e-books, white papers, and other thought leadership pieces for businesses and organizations; and oversee a network of talented writers on larger projects such as custom magazines and corporate blogs. We work primarily with clients in the B2B space, education, large nonprofits, and funded startups. Our combined industry experience is vast—and if we haven't worked with your industry yet, no worries. We're quick learners.

We offer full editorial services, inbound marketing, search engine optimization, and social media marketing consulting, and we partner with a network of designers, web developers, and PPC specialists to deliver incredible value at low overhead, regardless of the project scope.

Our physical location's just south of Portland, Maine, but we're occasionally available for in-person consultations throughout Boston and the New England region, and work virtually with clients all over the US and internationally.

Interested in learning more? Connect with me at kathryn@eucalyptmedia.com.
