Bill Bronsky's Summary

Bill is the Founder of The Office for Business Architecture (OBA) and the Managing Director.

OBA brings companies together (1 to 1 Company Exchanges) to develop innovative ideas to solve specific business issues - companies gain direct experience from each other (the success stories and the challenges!). This is an innovative business model that uses a network of over 500 companies (Fortune 500 and FTSE 100). See "The Times" article - "a new kind of consulting is breaking down barriers" - at www.obaconsulting.com

OBA Performance Benchmarks and advises across a broad range of functions: Supply Chain, IT (APO and ERP), TESCM, Operations Strategy (inc S&OP and Demand Planning) and across a number of different sectors: Oil & Gas, Consumer Products, Food & Drink, Chemicals, Manufacturing and High Tech.

OBA also advises at board level on transformation programmes across a number of different sector areas. OBA has led significant turnaround programmes and programme managed a number of global strategy implementations.

The Innovation-Exchange is a peer-to-peer network that relies upon the imagination and experience of its members in tackling the business challenges of our time. It is an event-driven community that meets when it chooses and works through a creative process that unleashes the expertise in the room to find novel insights and share learning's.
