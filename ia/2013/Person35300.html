Diane Offutt, Enrolled Agent (EA), MAcc's Summary

Accountant and Enrolled Agent, with over 35 years experience in a corporate enviroment. As the owner of a small accounting firm in Woodstock Georgia, I specialize in implementation of accounting systems, training accounting and non-accounting personnel, monitoring transactions, implementing tight internal control in order to minimize exposure for fraudulent transactions. I also stress strong tax planning in order to minimize taxation in business and personal issues. As an Enrolled Agent I am licensed to practice and represent clients before IRS.

Specialties

As an Enrolled Agent, I offer IRS representation on all tax issues, both personal as well as business. My continuing education requirements as an Enrolled Agent allow me to stay abreast of the ever-changing convoluted tax laws, which allow me to better serve my clients.

As a certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor I specialize in training and monitoring on all versions of QuickBooks software. I also am experienced with Peachtree software and all MS office products.
