Lauren Francis' Summary

I'm a freelance copywriter with a niche in the B2B market. I cater to entrepreneurs and marketing managers with services that include webpage copy, blog content, white papers, social media, press releases, and so much more.

If you’re looking for a results-oriented copywriter who can create a distinctive voice to your brand, you’ve found the right one in me. Here’s what I can do for you:

- create compelling copy that will mirror your audience’s exact needs
- build irresistible desire for your product or service
- utilize keywords that will effectively persuade the reader to take action
- strategize your product or service into a sales funnel that will increase your customer retention rate

If you have a copywriting project that you would like to discuss with me, feel free to send me an email at shecopywrites@gmail.com.
