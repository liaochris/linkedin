LeAnna Sharp's Summary

I consider myself to be a multidisciplinary performing artist in the mediums of theatre, music and dance. I work independently as an actor, singer, dancer and choreographer and also as an instructor in these fields. Be it adult Salsa classes to musical theatre workshops for preteens, to private voice lessons to dance fitness classes and managing after language immersion after school programs, I am an educator by nature. I am passionate about the performing arts and providing this for all who seek it.
