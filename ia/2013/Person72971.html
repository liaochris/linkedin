    About this eBook. The Promise of Social CRM is the first of in my Simplifying Social Business series of eBooks. It lays the groundwork to help business leaders understand the opportunities and challenges of the social business model and Social CRM strategies. Along the way, we’ll explore why the social business model is so important for companies today -- and the potential impact for companies that get it right.

    I cover:
    ....1. The essence of social business ─ defining what it means, why it’s a paradigm shift for business, its benefits, and communication methods required for success.
    ....2. The Unsolved Challenge of CRM failures.
    ....3. The promise of Social CRM -- emulating the human approach, making sense of social interactions, and how to start applying SCRM in your company.

  • The Business of Social Business

    Authors: Kathy Herrmann

    The Business of Social Business is the second in my Simplifying Social Business eSeries. In this eBook, I overview the 6 primary steps to becoming a social business.

    These are:
    ....1. Understand how social media impacts the traditional functional roles.
    ....2. Understand the impacts of the social business model.
    ....3. Listen and discover.
    ....4. Define objectives.
    ....5. Design strategies (to meet objectives).
    ....6. Determine Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to measure program results.
    ....7. Calculate Return on Investment (ROI) results.
    The insight will arm you so you can begin to build your own company-specific social business model, or innovate on what you’ve already started.

  • Customer Centricity in a Social Business World

    Authors: Kathy Herrmann

    The social business model focuses on engagement, influence, collaboration, and creation with customers, partners, and employees. It’s a customer-centric business model whereby customers are the central focus of all business practices within the company. Additionally, the intent of the social business model is to create mutual value in transparent and authentic ways.

    A question begging an answer in the social business model is – What is “customer centricity” in a social business world?

    This eBook answers that question. This is third book in my Simplifying Social Business ebook series.
