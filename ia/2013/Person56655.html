Jackie C.'s Summary

I am a full-time digital marketing specialist and a part-time Youtube personality with over 140,000 channel subscribers and 12 million lifetime views. I'm also a self-confessed social media nerd.

I have worked exclusively in the agency world since graduation- first in the home services niche and later in legal marketing. While in legal I focused solely on SEO, helping all of my clients rank first page for their selected search terms and keywords in locals and organics. I am currently pioneering the digital team of an established Irvine-based ad agency that specializes in pharmaceuticals, with the occasional sport team or fashion client. My specialties are SEO, local SEO, social media marketing, content creation and copywriting.

In my free time I curate content for my beauty and health-focused Youtube channel. Through my channel I have had the opportunity to participate in nationwide social media campaigns for brands like Levis and Nestle. I am also an active affiliate marketer, and have helped drive thousands of sales to sites like Hautelook, Beyond the Rack and Madame Madeline. I am also a paid beauty blogger for the Asian American entertainment network MYX.tv
