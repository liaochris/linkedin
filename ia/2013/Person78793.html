Laura Helbling's Summary

Prevision Policy is a unique premium information service that provides top-line analytics of regulatory, policy, and reimbursement issues specifically for biopharmaceutical companies. Membership to the service provides access to a regular stream of research memorandums on the commercial impact of decisions coming out of FDA, CMS and Congress.

The company’s goal is to advance mutual understanding among policymakers, business executives and the investment community, in the belief that a deeper understanding of policy leads to better business, and a deeper understanding of business leads to better policy.
