Lord Zion's Projects

  • SPiT LiKE THiS

    • 2002 to Present
    Team Members: Lord Zion, vikki spit

    Created and marketed a band with no clearly defined genre to an audience that did not exist until we found and nurtured it.

    Initially a bedroom project, all music was written by, recorded by and produced by us. We then embarked on a co-branding exercise by creating our own merchandise company. We used this to first target then nurture our audience.

    Since 2003, we have performed live and, since 2005, our music has been commercially available in shops worldwide and online.

    In 2004 readers of Kerrang! magazine voted us the 8th Best Unsigned Band in the UK and we grew steadily from that point until 2009.

    2009 was a break through year for us with the release of our debut album via two record labels (one in the UK, one in Sweden) which saw our music touch all corners of the planet. Ultimately, it culminated in us appearing at the Wacken Festival in Germany (2010, headlined by Iron Maiden).

    Throughout our careers, we have managed or co-managed ourselves. All online activities have been created and then maintained by me. Up until our most recent album, all music was written by me. I design and create our artwork (for online, promo material, CD's etc) and negotiate terms. Vikki maintains her own online activities, produces merchandise for us and looks after all the legal accounting requirements.

    We have been published since 2005, more recently moving our publishing to Enorm/ICS (Germany). We are currently negotiating the terms for the release of our second album, recorded by Grammy nominated producer Chris Tsangarides (Judas Priest, Ozzy Osbourne). I found Chris then Vikki and I negotiated terms for production with him. It looks to be a relationship that will continue.

    We found and took on an agent in 2010 who arranged overseas tours and a tour of the UK in 2011. 2012 we signed a new deal and our 2nd album "Normalityville Horror" was released to critical acclaim.

  • SMELLYOURMUM.COM

    • 2002 to Present
    Team Members: Lord Zion, vikki spit

    We created and marketed a brand, a successful website and a full range of T-shirt designs from scratch.

    We successfully sold to our initial target market expanding rapidly in both scale, content and custom.

    Since launch, we have created our OWN niche market, incorporating various tribes and sub-cultures into one. Our customers are the same as us: tattooed geeks who love rock, heavy metal and punk music.

    Year on year, our sales grow with 2011 being our best year yet, despite virtually cutting out all traditional forms of advertising. We market ourselves online (Facebook, Twitter, Amazon, eBay) and, as our band grows in popularity, so does our merchandise - and vice versa. They are intrinsically linked and feed off - and from - eachother.

    We started off as 100% DIY and remain 100% DIY. Every design is created in-house by me and then hand screen-printed by Vikki. I do all the website updates, artwork, promotional material and order administration; Vikki prints, orders blank stock, materials, packages and completes all our accounting requirements.

    Our initial launch in 2002 was an exercise in Guerilla Marketing. In 2004 we negotiated with LloydsTSB & Protx (now SagePay) to obtain a merchant account so we could accept credit cards online. It was at this point the site was re-worked and re-launched and hits went through the roof.

    We have to-date nearly 14 million hits; our site is in the top 1% most popular on the planet. We have sold hundreds of thousands of our branded, unique and original T-shirts to tens of thousands of customers, worldwide.

    Since 2008, we have regularly attended conventions (Anime, Cosplay, Memorabilia, Collectormania, Tattoo) to expand our customer base. More recently, we have negotiated with Streamline (Nat West) to accept credit card transactions at these events via their PDQ machines.

    We see no end to the possibilities. People love to know that they are buying from people just like them (albeit in a successful rock band!).

  • FITLIKETHIS.CO.UK

    • October 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Lord Zion

    My experiences and knowledge of health, diet and fitness were often being called upon by fans and I found myself answering the same questions time and again. It occurred to me that a blog filled with answers to the most frequently asked questions might save me some time but, thanks to my other online activities and the fact that the blog is written in an accessible manner, the site has really taken off.

    In a few months, I went from a couple of hundred hits a day to a couple of thousand and growth continues. This will remain an ongoing project as I will never stop learning about health and fitness and will never stop working out myself. Not only that, I like to help others improve their lives and wellbeing.

    I see this growing into something that runs alongside and co-promotes my other brands / businesses / activities (SPiT LiKE THiS & SMELLYOURMUM.COM) as they are all part of my life and I do not shy away from nor hide either. What and who I am IS my brand and it continues to grow.

    Ultimately, I see a range of FIT LIKE THIS workout attire along with a range of nutritional products and supplements when I am more prominently in the public eye.
