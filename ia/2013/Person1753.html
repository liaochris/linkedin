Add Senior's Summary

Graduating from Manchester Metropolitan University in 2008 with a 2:1 degree in Design & Art Direction. I have over 4 years employed commercial experience and have offered a freelance design service for over 5. I have a specific interest in extreme sport/skate/snow graphics, photography and associated branding. Exhibiting at the D&AD Young Blood at Earls Court, London and winning a global T-shirt design competition have been the highlights of my career thus far. I am enthusiastic, easy to get along with, down to earth and a damn hard worker. I have an open minded, methodical and experimental approach.

My perfectionist nature allows me to take a project from concept through to a highly polished completed stage. My passion for graphic design and desire for inspiration drives me to follow numerous print and online resources, allowing me to keep on top of current design trends, technologies and techniques. I have proved to be a fast and confident worker with a huge skill set and diverse experience. My organised work ethic and good time-management skills allow me to take on multiple projects without letting the quality of my work slip.

Furthermore, I am a fast learner and efficient on Adobe Creative Suit programs, both Mac and PC. My web development skills include HTML & CSS layout with implementation of javascript based elements, I have the ambition of developing these web-skills further.

After exploring my passion for snowboarding in the Japanese alps. I am now settled in Leeds, England keeping creative and exploring the boundaries of visual communication and graphic design.
