Harlem Tae Kwon Do's Summary

Through our school and non-profit organization, One World Taekwondo Foundation, we serve dozens of families of every race, religion and nationality in the Harlem community. We are as invested in students' minds as in their martial arts skills. Children's report cards are reviewed on a quarterly basis, and good academic standing is a prerequisite for taking class; the school even features a computer-equipped homework room. For adults, health and wellness seminars and self-defense classes strengthen family and community ties while improving martial arts skills. Other programs include scholarships, tuition assistance, and international athlete exchanges whose planning is underway.
