Louis Kahn's Summary

Could your business use a faster responding, more reliable and more experienced IT/computer support staff?

Look no further! I help business owners reduce technology costs, communicate better with their clients and become more efficient with their time and money.

The fortune 500 companies spend huge sums of money on large IT departments. Here's the thing: Smaller companies need the same support mechanisms, just on a smaller scale. And believe it or not, this type of support is not outside a small business's price range! You see, that’s where we come in!

I have been working in the IT industry for over 13 years and have consulted for some of the largest organizations in the Philadelphia region, such as Peco/Exelon, Amtrak, and many others.

Every solution that we bring to the table has our philosophy built in: Reliability, Security, and Scalability!

At Kahnnect IT, we only use tried and true, industry-proven solutions! We work with the best in the business and we work with our clients' budgets in mind. Kahnnect IT doesn't view computers as toys, but as business tools. We truly partner with our clients and our goal is to become an integral part of their organization.

You don't need someone who just fixes problems. You need a firm that provides unique solutions that help you communicate better and makes your business run more efficiently.

Call Kahnnect IT TODAY! 267-229-0150
