Harris Hutkin's Summary

I have created and grown digital media products at leading consumer brands for over 15 years in both the U.S. and Australia, with experience spanning television, magazines, newspapers, radio and pure-Internet based businesses.

Since moving to Australia in 2006, I have held positions at two of Australia’s leading media companies. Currently I work at Foxtel, Australia’s main subscription television provider, where I am actively involved in defining strategy and delivering experiences, such as Foxtel’s award-winning London 2012 Olympics tablet app.

Before joining Foxtel, I helped 25 mastheads plan and adopt their digital strategy at APN News and Media, Australia’s largest regional newspaper company. As Publisher, I led product, content and production for their websites; was active in setting marketing strategy; and managed the roadmap for the digital publishing platform.

Prior to APN, I spent several years in a variety of increasingly senior roles at Time Inc., one of the world’s largest magazine publishing companies, in San Francisco, New York and Sydney.

Specialties: Digital Media Strategy, Product Development, Marketing Strategy, General Management
