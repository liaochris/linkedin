Derek Keats' Summary

I have been in higher education almost all my life, and I have had a successful research and teaching career before moving into management positions. During the past 10 years I have been in level-two (vice president equivalent) positions at two different universities, and in both have been in a CIO/IT Leadership-type role. From 2001-2008, I was Executive Director responsible for information technology and related matters at the University of the Western Cape (UWC) (http://www.uwc.ac.za), and from January 2009 to July 2011 as Deputy Vice Chancellor at the University of the Witwatersrand (known as Wits), Johannesburg (http://www.wits.ac.za). The DVC-KIM position was created to help make Wits a 'tech savvy university', and includes responsibility for the Wits Libraries, Computer and Networking Services (the IT department), the establishment of eLearning, and business intelligence services. It also includes strategic engagement with the academic programme of the university in various areas related to the creative and innovative use of information and communication technologies, including enhancing our research in collaboration with the DVC – Research. I left in July to pursue interests outside of academia. I am now running my own consulting and software development business, Kenga Solutions and also Chief Scientist at thumbzup, a payment technology innovation startup.


Specialties: IT Strategy; Innovation through IT; Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) ecosystem development in organizations; open platforms more broadly; helping to create startup IT companies and mentoring/incubating them to profitability; available for consulting on a range or policy, strategy and implementation matters.
