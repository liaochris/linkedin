Chrysteen Braun's Summary

When you start thinking about working on your home, you have a lot of decisions to make. Perhaps one of your biggest fears in remodeling is making expensive mistakes, or not knowing where to start.

That’s why it’s really important to work with a company who can help you with everything: Kitchen and Bath Design, the remodeling process itself, floor coverings, and Hunter Douglas blinds, shades and shutters.

Since I started working with older homes in my parents insurance restoration company 40 years ago, I've maintained a passion for the business. Eventually, Aegean Designing Whims was created to extend our focus from floor coverings to include kitchen and bath design.

We look forward to servicing Los Alamitos, Seal Beach, Cypress, La Palma, Cerritos, Huntington Beach, Lakewood and Long Beach.
