Gordy Johnson's Summary

My main focus is voiceover acting (commercial, promos and narration). New VO demo is posted at www.7gordy7.com.(promos and animation arriving soon!)
I have announced many of the premier events in action sports including: the men's halfpipe sweep at the 2002 Olympics, at least 10 X Games, the Dew Tour, the US Open of snowboarding and World championships in three different sports. I have extensive experience as an on-camera host and expert analyst on ESPN, NBC and The CBS Sports Network. Building on nearly twenty years of event production experience including roles as live announcer for the US Snowboarding Grand Prix SBX and SkiCross events, festival village MC on the Dew Action sports tour, tower director at the Gravity Games, emcee @ several winter and summer X- Games and as a field producer and stage manager at the US Open of Surfing.

Specialties: A positive vibe and a passion for life. I grew up in the ski and snowboard industry and have worked as an announcer, coach, instructor, rep and competitor for the last 22 years. Snowboarding is my #1 passion!
I also love mountain biking, surfing and my newest obsession is cyclocross. Photography is my artistic outlet and spending time with friends and family fills my heart.
I invite you to check out my website @ www.7gordy7.com
