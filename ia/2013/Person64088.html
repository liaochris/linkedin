Jimmy Walker, CSP's Summary

I have been a Certified Safety Professional (CSP) since 2000. I have 15 years of experience in the safety field. I have worked in General Industry, primarily manufacturing and warehousing, and facilities management. I also have worked in Construction which includes; Radiological Management, Facilities Management, Demolition, Decommissioning, Industrial Services and Soil Remediation.

I make the safety difference by: protecting the company and the company’s most important asset, its workers, so they can go home in the same condition they came to work in through EHS compliance by implementing federal (OSHA, NIOSH), ANSI, NFPA, state and local regulations, safety procedures, safety training, safety audits / inspections to prevent unsafe acts, unsafe conditions, risks and hazards.

I have been a member of the American Society of Safety Engineers (ASSE) since 1995.
