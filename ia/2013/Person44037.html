Faith Sheridan, ASID's Summary

With over 20 years of experience in the industry, Faith Sheridan Design Group provides fluency in project management and established connections with the best craftspeople and product manufacturers in the industry. Our exceptional attention to detail and design talent means you can release the process to our firm and feel confident that your design inspiration will be translated into a finished product you will love. From the first swing of the sledge hammer to placement of the last throw pillow, we oversee every detail. To us, luxury is not just the outcome of your project, but something to experience all along the way.

Faith is based in Seattle and has worked nationally. She is a mentor and design industry leader. As a contributor and member of Benjamin Moore′s Color Advisory team, she defines the Color Palette Initiative that determines future color trends. Faith is is also a professional member and past president of the Oregon Chapter ofAmerican Society of Interior Designers (ASID).

Faith Sheridan, ASID's Volunteer Experience & Causes

  • Volunteer Experience

    • Sustainer | NW Arts Project

      Junior League of Seattle
      • Arts and Culture
      September 2008 – May 2010 (1 year 9 months)

      NW Arts introduces elementary school children, the community and JLS members to the rich production of Northwest artists in this highly respected art collection. Through informal and intimate discussion of the pieces, the children experience more than a formal art history lesson.

      They are encouraged to express themselves by making their own art, and exploring their own imagination and responses when viewing art. Committee members serve as docents in the Seattle Public Schools, and train PTA members to act as docents at the Bellevue Art Museum. The collection is provided to participating schools.

    • Committee Member

      Pacific Northwest Ballet Stars
      • Arts and Culture
      October 2011 – present (1 year 11 months)

      Since its inception in 1996, STARs has become an important part of the PNB family. Its fundraising efforts have made a significant contribution to the Ballet’s ongoing financial needs. PNB STARS is a volunteer organization dedicated to the support of the Pacific Northwest Ballet through special events and member education. Originally established in honor of the Ballet's 25th Anniversary, PNB STARS is filled with dedicated, energetic members who share the love of PNB and who have together raised nearly $2,000,000 for the Pacific Northwest Ballet. Special events include the annual Season Opening Gala.”
