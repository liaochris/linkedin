Arvid Bux's Summary

Professional:
More then eight years experience in the outsourcing/off shoring business on both the client's side and the supplier side for Hewlett Packard, the world's largest information technology corporation (by revenue).

Current day-to-day work consists of ensuring that;
- Identify and work on improvements for the channels call, email, chat and social media
- Support colleagues defining and improving the Customer Experience
- Customers who are using social media to voice their (dis)satisfaction are heard and where needed assisted in solving their problem
- Identify opportunities for improvement in our current Customer Satisfaction programs
- Identify deficiencies in our current Customer Satisfaction programs

Editor for several online magazines including DutchCowboys, StyleCowboys and Google+ World. Write various articles on new web technologies, new consumer electronics and cover breaking news.
