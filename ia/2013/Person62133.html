Jenny Triplett's Summary

Co-Owner of Dawah International, LLC, a multimedia company, which publishes Prisonworld Magazine, Co-host & co-producer of a weekly radio show, the Prisonworld Radio Hour, successful blogger, requested speaker for corporate functions, wellness retreats, webinars/teleseminars, correctional facilities, non-profit organizations, schools, colleges and universities, radio shows & print magazines.

I am a social media influencer where I was invited to the Ricki Lake Show as a Social Media Influencer, have co hosted a Twitter chat (#dadchat) and carry a KLOUT score of 64. I am a featured blogger for the Huffington Post and have provided comments or have been featured in or on Dr. Phil, REDBOOK, CNN, Black Enterprise Magazine, Ebony Magazine, MSN.com, The LA Times, Reuters, Loop21, New York Magazine/The Cut, The American Prospect Magazine, Google News, Lagos Times (Nigeria), and a host of other magazine and radio shows. I am a marriage expert for Huff Post Live.

I have co-authored three books, my current release - Surviving Marriage in the 21st Century - 13 Easy Tips That Can Help You Get to 20 Years and Beyond is available on Amazon now.

Being a mother of three boys, I know the trials and tribulations of not only raising children, but African-American men who could end up as stereotypes. I am passionate about family and detouring young men and women from the criminal justice system. Some of the subjects I speak about are dysfunctional families, substance abuse, entrepreneurship, marriage & relationships, self-conscious addictions, incarceration, stereotypes and the list goes on.

My appearances, along w/my husband on Family Court w/Judge Penny & Dr. Phil garnered rave reviews as to our parenting philosophies. My appearance on The Mike & Juliet Morning show was phenomenal as I gave The Real Housewives of Atlanta a little wisdom.

Specialties: Blogging, Social Media, Email Marketing, Prisoner/Parenting Expert, Motivational Speaker, Marriage Tipster, Marketing Plans
