Zach Jones' Summary

Zach is a former director of aquatic services for the city of Murfreesboro, a budding marketing strategist and a loyal Razorback fan (not necessarily in that order). As our Director of Business Development, Zach is constantly put in situations where he has has to get outside of his comfort zone and push himself to learn the ropes of this business in a short period of time. But Zach doesn’t stop there. While most of his brain is busy becoming a marketing ninja he still manages to come up with new ideas and inventions constantly. For example, while you were reading this Zach was mentally inventing “I-Dot pens” which may very well revolutionize a market and make your life better. Or maybe it won’t.
Either way we’re lucky to have him on our team and look forward to seeing where his creative drive will take him here at Thrive.
