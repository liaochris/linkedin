Dr. Johnny E. Lovejoy, II's Summary

Lovejoy & Associates, Inc., has always been what our larger competitors are still trying to become. We've been helping Individuals, Businesses, and Government operate more efficiently through better performance enrichment methods, measures, and means.

Dr. Lovejoy also currently serves as Commissioner-Texas Commission on Law Enforcement (TCOLE), Member of the Board of Community First Health Plans (CFHP), Chairman of the Board for Community First Health Services Corporation (CFHSC), Commissioned Admiral in the Texas Navy, Commissioned Kentucky Colonel by the Commonwealth of Kentucky, and very involved in various other professional, community, Statewide, National, and International activities.

Specialties: President & CEO of Lovejoy & Associates, a Professional Performance Consultancy Firm specializing in: Business & Government Enrichment Consulting, Executive Performance Coaching, Information Technology, and Income Tax Services, established in 1985.
