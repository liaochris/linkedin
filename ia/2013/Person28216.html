Carl Trigilio's Summary

I am a professional magician of 5+ years and have performed for various close-up and stage venues. In my career, I have performed for crowds with over 300 individuals and in numerous restaurants, events, and parties.
I have coached two high school Speech and Debate teams, consisting of over 100 students at a time. Both teams have produced various State Champions and National Finalists. I also hold private sessions with individuals to help them learn the basics of public speaking and to become a more natural, confident speaker.
As a competitor of Pasadena City College and Bradley University's nationally ranked Speech team, I have earned numerous awards and championship titles, including a State Champion title in Prose Interpretation.
I have professional teaching and coaching experience in public speaking and am interested in pursuing a career in teaching and communications.

Specialties: Public Speaking, Speech Writing, Magic, Teaching/Mentoring, Graphic/Web Design
