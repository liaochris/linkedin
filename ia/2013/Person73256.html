    Loccit is a service which creates a diary of your life, based on your social media footprint.


    Loccit keeps your memories safe, forever.

    Generation X&Y are diarising their lives in incredible detail. But, it goes into lots and lots of different places. You have no idea whether these places will be here, or in the same format in years to come. It is Loccit's mission to ensure that your important memories are ALWAYS safe, always available and yours.

    Loccit integrates with all your favourite social networks and pulls in where you've been, your favourite photos, your reactions to life, business achievements, events and more. It allows you to write private diary entries and to share specific moments to specific people. After all, not every ‘status' you experience is suitable for all your friends on Facebook /or followers on Twitter!
