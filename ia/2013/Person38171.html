Nancy Mramor PhD's Summary

With over 25 years as a health, media and educational psychologist I have extensive media work as a media expert. TV appearances includes CBS, NBC, ABC, Fox and PBS. My experience includes roles as producer, on-screen talent, and on-air expert for hundreds of television and radio media and print outlets in the US and Canada. Are you a Television or Radio Producer/Media Manager seeking an expert to comment on current topics in the news media or pop culture? Contact Nancy for her expertise and media presence on breaking stories.

- Your Media, Your Life - Create High Level Health with TV, Internet and Movies
- Stress Management, Health and Wellness - Mind, Body and Spirit
- Spiritual Fitness
- Top Ten Tips for Lasting Happiness,
- Other health and media topics
