Bradford Billet CEM, OBE's Summary

Bradford E. Billet was born and raised in New York City. - During the past 20 years, Mr. Billet has acquired extensive experience and skill in Business Administration and Management, Corporate Finance and Operation, International and Governmental Affairs, Emergency Management and Security. He has held high-ranking positions in both the private and public sectors. His experiences range from directing a 3000 person municipal agency with a 180 million budget, Chief Financial Officer of international oil and gas company with operations in several countries. Advising both large and emerging companies on corporate management issues.


He has received numerous honors and commendations throughout his career and he continues to lecture and consult on Public Safety and Management issues. Mr. Billet has been nationally recognized as a Certified Emergency Manager and Continuing Legal Educator. In 2002, Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth ll awarded Mr. Billet an Officer of the Most Excellent Order of the British Empire (OBE). Mr. Billet has also led or served on numerous task forces relating to management, public safety and public health.

Specialties

Senior management Decision making, corporate operations, union negotiation, government affairs and international affairs, mergers and acquisition, mediation and arbitration.
