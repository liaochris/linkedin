Tim Knight's Summary

Tim is available to work with you through his business, The BINGU Consultancy, providing advertising & communications agencies, brands & businesses with expert digital strategy and direction.

Prior to setting-up his own consultancy, Tim was National Managing Director of TEQUILA\ Australia for more than 4 years. He grew the business from a small Sydney-based boutique digital agency into a large, integrated creative agency with specialist services spanning Digital, Direct and Brand Activations (Promotions, Events & Experiences).

The BINGU Consultancy mission: PURPOSEFUL INNOVATION.

Personal Comment:

"So much of the business we are in is about people and so many of our stories are told in the context of the people we meet and the influence they have on our lives.

My career in digital media began in 1997, in the Anthropology department of the University of Canterbury, England, where I moderated blogger comments and posts and creating fresh content for a "Amazonian Rainforest People" project. Between then and now, I have been privileged enough to work with: Diageo, Aviva, Virgin Money, St George Bank, MARS Petcare (formerly Masterfoods), Nissan, Mitsubishi, Pacific Brands, eBay and Apple, to name a few.

Since being in Australia I have held Senior Management positions at NetX and TEQUILA\. My passion for doing things differently to achieve great results, has driven the direction of TEQUILA\ Australia from 2005 to 2009."
