Anna Marie Jensen's Summary

START UP OF MY ONEWOMANCOMPANY

Free lance executive management assistance, import-export based on commission payment, translations Danish-English-French-Spanish, relocations, facility management.

Generalist in change and project management namely turn-around operations, customer and market researches with the aim of cherry-picking the profitable customers and eliminate those who are not, organization and international sales and purchasing.

I have studied Spanish at the University of Barcelona since 2011 and start studying philology Spanish English in September 2013 with the goal of becoming a state certificated Spanish-Danish translator at 60 or preferably earlier. Then by 70 or a bit later, I will sit in a self-sufficient house on a cliff with view over the ocean and translate while inviting friends from all over the World. In 2012 I subscribed at the university of EAE Barcelona to finalize my “half MBA” – Program of Executive Development - from IMD Lausanne into an EMBA, but I have not started yet due to lack of time, unfortunately.

I was born in Denmark, started working for my parents at the age of 5 and since worked in France during 20 years. The past 13 years I have worked between Spain, Denmark and Switzerland.

I have worked for multinational companies in engineering, shipping and distribution of industrial components as General Manager, Functional Manager and Executive Management Assistant. Through my work at the Danish Embassy in Paris, I know most industrial and consumer sectors including design and architecture and not least the French major retailers. I have also worked as Senior Executive in one of the most important mink farms in Europe.

I have solid knowledge of general management, company organization and sales, together with a thorough experience in turn-around operations as Project Manager or General Manager of acquired companies, and streamlining the new business units to be fully operational and profitable with the head office.
