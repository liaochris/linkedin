Becky Tate's Summary

Becky Tate is a global marketing executive, PR, and event marketing strategist with over 12 years of progressive marketing success in a diversity of firms, including 9 years of international marketing. An entrepreneur at heart, she enjoys the challenge of partnering with executives and investors to create marketing strategy for new business launches, as well as leading global expansions into new markets.

Becky offers her expertise as a Marketing Consultant through her firm Red 8 Ball. Past notable corporate roles include Director of Marketing for RGL Forensics, an international forensic account firm with 24 branches on four continents and Marketing Manager for ExpressDigital, a software company focused on the photography industry.

Becky adds energy and excitement to all of her projects and thrives on getting to know people, trying new things, and traveling the world.

Specialties: Strategy | Design | Events
