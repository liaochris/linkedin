Douglass Gaking's Summary

I direct the concert bands and jazz bands and teach general music at Northside Middle School in Muncie. I am the Assistant Band Director and Percussion Director at Muncie Central High School. I previously taught K-8 general music, choir, and band for 3 years in the Archdiocese of Indianapolis.

I have been involved in the Indiana Orff-Schulwerk Association and incorporate Orff and Kodaly strategies in my teaching. I completed Orff-Schulwerk Level I at Anderson University in the summer of 2009. I am a member of the Indiana Music Educator's Association, National Association for Music Education, and the Indiana Bandmasters Association.

I have instructed high school drumlines since the summer of 2003. I currently direct marching and concert percussion activities at the high school and middle school levels. I have designed and directed 3 seasons of winter drumline shows, and I have been involved in the design as a caption head for 7 marching band shows. My most recent project was a winter drumline show for which I composed all the music and visual design. I regularly compose and arrange music for concert band, jazz band, marching band, and percussion ensemble.

I have played concert percussion with the Indianapolis Symphonic Band since 2008. I have played drum set and congas in the Holy Cross Orchestra since 2009. I play percussion in a rock/blues quartet called the Twisted Knobs.
