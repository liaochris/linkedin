Terry Soto's Summary

Terry Soto, President and CEO of About Marketing Solutions, Inc. helps her clients dramatically improve overall business performance by optimizing their strategies to succeed in the Hispanic market.

Five basic principles drive her successful approach with her clients:

1) Frame: Use your company’s growth objectives and strategic platforms as the context and direction for your Hispanic strategy.

2) Segment: Find and quantify your company's Hispanic segment "sweet spot" for your products and services and integrate them as part of your core consumer target

3) Assess: Identify your Hispanic target’s needs and requirements and assess your strategic platforms for strengths and weaknesses across the organization, operations and infrastructure.

4) Align: Fix your organization, operations and infrastructure weaknesses so they align with the target’s needs and requirements.

5) Measure: Integrate the Hispanic metrics that matter into overall reporting so you can report Hispanics’ contribution to overall growth results.

About Marketing Solutions organizes, mobilizes and helps its client teams reframe the Hispanic market opportunity as an integrated growth platform by helping them align and leverage companywide assets to their fullest potential. We help our clients evolve their current strategies so they are integrated, relevant and succesful among their core customers including Hispanics.

Terry has an MBA from Pepperdine Graziadio School of Business and Management in Malibu, California, is from Guayaquil, Ecuador, and is trilingual and completely multicultural.
