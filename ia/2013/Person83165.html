Lucretia Torva's Summary

I have extensive knowledge of visual art and it's creation. All of my work is imbued with care and attention to detail. My art makes people say "Wow!" and has even brought tears to my clients' eyes! My work not only entertains and delights people, it also becomes a family heirloom.

My goal is to brand and market myself so as to reach many more eager appreciators and owners of my artwork, both here and abroad.

I am especially concentrating on
Classic/ Collector Automobiles and Portraits

Specialties

-Painting chrome and reflections that make people say "Wow!"

-Large mural (6x100 ft or more) and decorative painting projects (multiple rooms in multi-million dollar homes): plan, budget, coordinate, knowing how to do all of the work myself ....then delegation and supervising.

-Extremely life-like portraits.
