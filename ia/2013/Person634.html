Aaron Smock's Summary

I started my professional career as a web designer back in 2000. I was hired at Mudd Advertising back in 2003 as an web designer. To make a long story short, I ended up working as a TV Graphic Artist in the production department at Mudd. That is where I really thrived as an artist. I have been doing it ever since.

Logo Animations, Abstract Animations, Compositing, Motion Graphics, Graphical styles & concepts for videos
