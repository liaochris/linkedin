ABOUT AARON HALL

I am inspired by working with business owners and their entrepreneurial drive. As a business attorney, I partner with company owners to solve complex problems and minimize legal risk, helping them build successful companies. Full bio: http://thompsonhall.com/attorneys/aaron-hall/


ABOUT THOMPSON HALL SANTI CERNY & DOOLEY

From our office in downtown Minneapolis, our business attorneys represent company owners and their businesses in complex contracts, employment law, intellectual property, real estate, and taxes—along with litigation involving these areas. Our attorneys are licensed in Minnesota, Wisconsin, New York, and Florida.

Too many law firms are outdated, overpriced, and unresponsive. Our clients don’t want attorneys with big egos, condescending attitudes, and bureaucratic service.

Attorneys at our firm think differently. We are business minded. We are “hands on.” We value efficiency, responsiveness, and results. We partner with business owners, educating them about the process, empowering them to focus on business growth.

Our attorneys leverage experience in big law firms, with major corporations, and serving top judges, at rates below what our former law firms charged for our services.

By providing corporate counsel and legal representation, our business attorneys help companies achieve results and prevent legal problems.
