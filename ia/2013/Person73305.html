Kay Hayes' Summary

I have been an adult educator for over 25 years with experience in managing training programs, developing curriculum and training and facilitating groups of all sizes.

I am currently active in the areas of job analysis for relevant competencies and the use of assessments (personality and ability) to increase selection success and provide development activities that meet the needs of the job.

I also facilitate group processes including many aspects of strategic planning from creation of mission and vision statements to goal implementation. My unique approach uses a blend of facilitation styles combining large graphic facilitation methods with the targeted use of compression planning to assure actionable results.
