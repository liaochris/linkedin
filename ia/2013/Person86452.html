Mark Deayton's Summary

A pragmatic and commercially astute HR business partner with international experience.

I have more than 20 years experience in specialist and generalist HR leadership roles in a wide range of industries in Australia, as well as a two year stint based in Jakarta, Indonesia. My approach to HR is based on delivering outcomes and processes that match business strategies and deliver bottom line results. I sit on the practical, commercially focused side of the HR fence and I am very much aware of what HR can bring to the table from a financial viewpoint.

During my career I've worked in the steel, coal mining, forestry and paper, automotive, local government, FMCG, and B2B finance industries, and have led major change initiatives, and process improvement projects, as well as being heavily involved in a number of mergers and acquisitions, including due diligence processes.

Specialties

Recruitment, workplace relations, OH&S and risk management, change management, project management, HR process improvement, mergers and acquisitions, internal and external comms, organisation redesign, workforce planning, security
