Lu Schildmeyer's Summary

Over 30 years in the commercial Hospitality / Foodservice design industry. I have been involved with the design or remodel design of over 500 restaurants and over 1,300 foodservice facilities.

We are in business to serve the restaurant industry by creating interior environments that are pleasing and functional.

We design both the FOH & BOH areas of restaurants.

My goal with Lu S Design Associates which I have opperated for over 9 years. Is to
Help Make Your Restaurant / Foodservice / Hospitality Business More Profitable Thru Design.

For foodservice design the menu is the most important element to have to start the restaurant & foodservice design process. We have a menu driven design outlook for your business.

For interior design of restaurants & foodservice facilities we know what materials & design details will stand the test of time for durability & maintence in your business.

For lighting design we know the right types of lighting to specify for ambience, functionallity, & required lighting energy code compliance.

Specialties

Restaurant Design;Commercial Interior Architecture & Design;Commercial Foodservice Consulting & Planning; & Commercial Lighting Design for Restaurants,Bars,Nightclubs,Country Clubs,Employee Cafeterias, and other types of foodservice businesses.
