Jeffrey Milberg's Summary

With over 30 years of direct marketing experience, I bring to the client relationship the unique expertise and experience in providing a combination of call center outsourcing, marketing list selection, and consulting.

Spent more than seventeen years with the Dun & Bradstreet Corporation, the nation’s leading information and marketing services firm. As the General Manager of Salesnet, the telemarketing company of Dun & Bradstreet, I led the company to become the leading provider of value added teleservices. Managed critical technology initiatives to provide for the design of the industry’s first predictive dialing technology, and to launch one of the first automated telemarketing software systems for in-house use. Additionally, my experience has produced a wealth of knowledge in list management, data processing, and market analysis, having spent ten years with Dun’s Marketing Services Division.

Co-founded Telemarketing Concepts, Inc. in 1986, a leader in B2B telemarketing, growing to six contact centers in New York, North Carolina and Georgia. In 2000, the company was sold to a Philippine software venture and I was instrumental in the establishment of call centers in Manila. In 2005, co-founded MPI Outsourcing, an international teleservices program / vendor management company with a network of call centers worldwide.

I am active member and speaker in the Professional Association for Customer Engagement (PACE), The Direct Marketing Association, American Marketing Association, American Association of Political Consultants, Sales Lead Management Association, SOCAP and the Outsourcing Institute.

Specialties

Teleservices Outsourcing for Sales,Lead Gen and Customer Service; Direct Marketing Services including Print,Email, Text Messaging, Automated Voice IB & OB; Marketing Lists and Leads
