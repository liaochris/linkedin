I have also run an after school program called "Lemonade Day." In this club students learn the structure of a business. They build a business plan using the model of a lemonade stand. They need to develop how to present their ideas a strategies to a loan board who will evaluate their plan and then give them a loan which must be paid back to the loan board when the club is over. This loan money is to then be used on materials and supplies to run a successful business stand. Students learn the value of earning a dollar. At the end of the club students will send 20% of their earning a a charity of their choice. The rest of the money they get to keep.
