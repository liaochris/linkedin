Amy Bly's Summary

Would you like to get the most money possible out of your home when you sell? Do you want to sell quickly and avoid price reductions? As a home stager, my goal is to help homeowners and builders sell their properties quickly and for the most money by making homes look their best so they stand out from the competition. I use property styling/enhancement techniques that help buyers create an emotional connection with a home.
If you've ever walked through a model home, you've probably felt that same connection. I walk through a property with a buyer's eyes -- or can even look at photos online -- to advise homeowners on creating curb appeal by making the most crucial repairs or improvements, de-personalizing, decluttering, updating, choosing the right paint colors, removing and re-arranging furniture, providing accessories, and shopping for rental furniture or artwork as needed.
I also can show sellers what needs to be packed up and how to organize closets, bookshelves, and storage areas so that spaces look bigger and better! I bring in and hang artwork and mirrors where necessary, improve traffic flow through rooms, and create or highlight focal points in every room.
I have staged homes in this down market that have sold within days of staging, including one that sold in less than 2 weeks for $10,000 more than the agent wanted to list it for! My recommendations can help you get more money out of your biggest investment!

Specialties: Color consulting, property update/rehabbing consults to get the most return on your investment. Furniture selection, rentals, purchase; floor plans; room or whole-house redesign; interior design and interior decorating; staging homes and condos to sell quickly and for more money. Creating optimal environments that appeal to more buyers and motivate them to make offers so you sell quickly. I love helping homeowners get the most money out of their properties when it's time to sell!
