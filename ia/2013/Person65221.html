Joe Bollert's Experience

Cofounder

LipoWear. LLC

October 2004 – Present (9 years)

LipoWear, LLC is a therapeutic clothing R&D company and specializes in developing products for patients with lipoatrophy. The initial product, SitRelief Shorts, is a clincially tested padded underwear garment for selected patient and consumer groups that need a discreet sitting comfort solution. Patient groups include HIV/AIDS, cancer and seniors with lipoatrophy and sitting pain.

President

Investigator Location Services

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Pharmaceuticals industry

July 2001 – Present (12 years 3 months) 70 S.W. Century Dr. Suite 100-247, Bend, OR 97702

Investigator Location Services, Inc. (ILS), the largest study broker in clinical research, recruits investigators for Sponsors and CROs and has instant access to over 27,000 experienced investigators in all medical specialties and practice settings. ILS services are free to sponsors and CROs as investigators pay our fees.

Executive Vice President

IBRD

1976 – 1996 (20 years)

The Institute for Biological Research and Development (IBRD) was one of the first CROs to be formed. The EVP was responsible for building a nationwide CRO operation, defining and creating the functional units that lead to what is today a full service CRO.

IBRD provided high quality clinical research services that created credibility for the emerging CRO industry and became a benchmark gold standard for the CRO industry.
