Amy Victorson's Projects

  • Elite Recruiting Films Website

    • February 2012 to February 2012
    Team Members: Amy Victorson

    I designed and authored the website for Elite Recruiting Films. Originally they were using an online website template that used a ton of unnecessary and messy code. I took the site and redesigned it to give a more professional appearance and improved the usability of the site. I also cleaned up the HTML code by getting rid of unnecessary tags, and I cleaned up the CSS. I created a contact form as opposed to just adding an email link, with PHP added as a way to filter the user feedback, and also provide a way in which Elite Recruiting Films could better assess what to bid their clients.

  • The Trendy Rose

  • Gruber Painting Website Design

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Amy Victorson

    This project has been an ongoing development. Gruber Painting is a well established Residential Painting Company in the South Bay & Peninsula, but for the longest time they had no web presence until they got a hold of me. The first stage of the project was to design and create a simple splash page with the company contact information, while the website was being designed and built. That way clients could still get their needed information online. The second stage of the project was to design the look and feel of the site as well as the usability. The final stage was to build the site using a combination of HTML, CSS, PHP, Jquery and Javascript. The portfolio page on the site as well as the featured projects of the homepage are connected to a database containing the image data and all data relating to each individual project.
