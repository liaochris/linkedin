Amanda Friedt's Summary

Account Director at SS Digital Media.

Digital marketing enthusiast into Web development, SEO, Google AdWords, Google Analytics, public relations, social media advertising and mobile marketing. I am very passionate about my industry. I am an over achiever and a self-proclaimed workaholic. Continuous learning and growth is vital. I live and breathe the new age of public relations and marketing.

Previously, I volunteered for a non-profit organization called Erootha. I worked to help promote awareness and plan events.

I also worked for CM-Life for two years during my college career and for the Mount Pleasant Monthly Magazine. I was also in charge of my own beat at CM-Life. Further, I interned at MAC3TV and took part in re-branding, promotions and marketing/ public relations consultations. Also, I interned at CRDL, the Chippewa River District Library, where I was in charge of updating and maintaining social networking sites, newsletters, ad copy writing, press releases and articles for the Morning Sun.

I would like to obtain my Masters degree within ten years.

Specialties: Google Adwords, Microsoft Ad Center, Budget Management, WordPress, HTML, Ad Copy Writing, SEO Copy Writing, Project Management, Social Media, Team Management, Digital Marketing, Media Relations, Human Resources,
