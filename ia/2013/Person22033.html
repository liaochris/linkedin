Charleen Maunsell's Summary

I have a growing business following my personal passion supporting and following sustainable organic practices in elements of design and plant care service.I am self-employed providing Landscape Design and Plant Care Services to residential and small business properties. I have been trained, tested and continue to maintain current active status through mandatory yearly re-accreditation/certification with the following industry organizations: MNLA Massachusetts Certified Horticulturist (MCH) since 2011, North Shore Community College Certified Landscape Design and Maintenance Professional (XLD) since 2008, Nofa Organic Land Care Professional Accreditation (AOLCP) since 2007. I am a supporting member of the Massachusetts Nursery and Landscape Association (MNLA) and Massachusetts Regional Northeast Organic Farming Association (NOFA Massachusetts).

Prior to 2006 I had an active career in Hi-Tech for
over 25 years in software engineering contributing to real-time process control, operating system internals, network server client internals and shared file systems, graphic user interfaces on Unix, Microsoft and Macintosh platforms.
