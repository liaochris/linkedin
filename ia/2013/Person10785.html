Waitemata District Health Board

Government Agency; 5001-10,000 employees; Hospital & Health Care industry

June 2011 – September 2012 (1 year 4 months) Auckland, New Zealand

Waitemata District Health Board offers hospital and medical services to more than 525,000 people in the Auckland region. With more than 5,500 staff spread across 30 locations, the organization is the largest DHB in New Zealand.

As Communications Manager, I oversaw all communications activities for a $24 million health program delivered to 137,000+ residents, and also managed the communications for other hospital services including Cardiology, Radiology, Renal and Dental Health.

I developed a strategic communications plan for the health screening program, managed all of its media relations efforts, wrote content for the website, newsletters and other collateral, and worked closely with executives, physicians, government officials and internal teams to raise awareness of the program throughout the district.

Internal Communications Manager

North Shore City Council

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Construction industry

August 2008 – November 2010 (2 years 4 months) Auckland, New Zealand

North Shore City Council offered a range of services to 220,000 local residents for nearly 20 years. In November 2010 NSCC was amalgamated along with seven other area councils to form the Auckland Council as part of the “Auckland Super City” governance restructure.

As Internal Communications Manager for the transition to the new council, I developed myriad internal communications materials, including newsletters, all-staff emails, internal documents, website/intranet copy and PPT presentations for the Chief Executive.

Working directly with the Leadership Team, elected officials, the HR team and an external transition agency, I helped develop materials, resources and messaging to support many thousands of people going through a city-wide change over a 20-month period.

Communications Manager (contract)

Kensington Swan

Partnership; 201-500 employees; Law Practice industry

September 2007 – August 2008 (1 year) Auckland, New Zealand

Kensington Swan is a commercial law firm – one of the “Big Five” in New Zealand – with more than 250 staff in Auckland and Wellington. The firm specializes in construction, energy and resources, financial services, governance, IT, M&A, taxation and other law areas.

As the Communications Manager I managed internal and external communications activities, developed strategic plans and marketing material, organized events and provided communications support to the firm’s executives and senior partners.

Among my responsibilities were managing the website content, writing and distributing internal and external newsletters, developing ads and sales collateral, and writing and producing the firm’s annual review for distribution to all of its stakeholders.
