Elizabeth Blandon's Summary

I am one of only 60 immigration experts in the State of Florida, as certified by the state's bar association. With over 10 years of experience with immigration law, I represent clients before the Citizenship and Immigration Service, the Board of Immigration Appeals and various Circuit Courts of Appeal.

In particular, I help foreigners seekings asylum. I have published an eBook on the subject as well as an article that appeared in the national magazazine of the American Immigration Lawyers Association.

Other writings have been published in several local publications and the monthly journal of The Florida Bar.

Specialties: Immigration cases based on employment, investment, family or asylum.
