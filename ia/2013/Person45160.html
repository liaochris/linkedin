Floyd Stewart's Summary

FMS, Inc serves as a St. Louis area provider of multiple building services. I often describe our services as everything from cleaning the toilets to planning and building their next board room.

As founder of our company I have moved to a position of advisor to all of our top level associates.

Everyone know me as having Plan A, Plan B, Plan C. and Plan D. This describes my core philosophy. I NEVER want to be in a position where I cannot move quickly and effectively in another direction when needed. I convey this approach in my business life as well as my personal life. This philosophy has saved us and our customers untold dollars over the years.
