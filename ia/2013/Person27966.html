Chuck Reese's Projects

  • Siemens Pitch

    • April 2010 to May 2010
    Team Members: Chuck Reese, Jeff Leaf

    Cr.media helped create a presentation deck for Ogilvy as they pitched Siemens ad business. The deck was to include, tons of up front strategy (charts, graphs) and a bevy of creative work from ads, ads in situ, product shots, videos and TV scripts, absolutely a meat lovers.

    We also had to load the then new iPad with all the videos, team address cards and photos to present to the client. We did it all over the coarse of a few weeks, pulling all nighters and surviving the days. In Munich, Germany we polished, rehearsed and packaged a compelling show that became a huge win for the client.

  • Muhammad Ali's 70th Birthday

    • June 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Chuck Reese, Barbara Warnock-Morgan, Brad Gallant

    Worked with CKX, Muhammad Ali's licensing agency, to develop this sizzle reel in both celebration of the legend's 70th birthday and as a sales tool for licensing Ali-related images and materials. The video was accompanied by a limited edition pack of 11 high-quality postcards featuring iconic images of Ali and his quotes, Keynote sales deck featuring the video and pre-loaded branded flash drives. Keynote deck was shown to potential licensees via iPad and was loaded with video and application form onto flash drives for trade show and general Sales distribution. Video and postcard images were featured on MuhammadAli.com home page in run up to his birthday.
    Art Director: Chris Feldmann
    Creative Director: Peter Mattei
