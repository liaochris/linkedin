Kristine Tohtz, DC, DABCA, CACCP's Summary

Alternative medicine with a focus on acupuncture and chiropractic integrated for the needs of each individual patient. I was awarded a specialization in pregnancy and pediatrics at the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA). As a certified yoga instructor, I specialize in proper form and function of yoga poses to help participants avoid injuries; I also teach introductory yoga as a form of rehabilitation / physical therapy. I am the Illinois trainer for Maternity Wise, a post partum doula organization as well as a certified placenta encapsulator through Placenta Benefits. As an ACA certified Diplomate in Acupuncture, I also teach for the post graduate department through NUHS in acupuncture, pregnancy and pediatrics. I also am certified through the National Guild of Hypnotherapy to use hypnosis as one of my other tools for overall patient health.

I am in school presently to complete my master of science in Oriental Medicine.

Specialties: Acupuncture, chiropractic, pregnancy care, pediatrics, yoga therapeutics, hypnotherapy
