Becky Davis' Summary

Web designer & developer, specializing in custom WordPress themes. I like to find honest workable solutions that fit your small business needs.

My primary goal is to find a web solution that makes the most sense for your business and to make sure you understand all things that go into the process and how you can help yourself. Site development is a collaborative process!

In addition to full projects, I am happy to help you with your site maintenance, tech support or training needs.

In my quest to help the small business owner and help them understand the process, I also teach a seminar at SCORE Chicago "Designing Your Website - Things All Small Business Owners Should Know."

Specialties

web design & development, WordPress theme development, training and education, site maintenance
