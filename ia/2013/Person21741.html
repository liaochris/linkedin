Chad Harris' Summary

I can help your company have a successful relocation. Moving a business, plant, hospital, data room, warehouse or any other business can be a stressful thing.With good organization, planning, move meetings, and a proper timeline I can help in making your relocation a success.

I have twenty-seven years’ experience in the moving and storage business. I have worked in virtually every facet of the moving/relocation/transportation industry. I spent the first 19 years of my career with United Van Lines. My initial duties included working as a packer, driver, dispatcher, operations manager, sales manager and finally, general manager. After an ownership change at United, I joined IMS Mayflower in 2004. I was charged with creating and developing an office/commercial moving division.

IMS Relocation has been in business since 1992 and has become one of the largest residential movers in north Texas. Since joining their team, I have been able to use my experience to establish an excellent team specifically designed to service the commercial client. My services include relocating warehouses and businesses locally or nationwide. I also specialize in the relocation of special commodities, electronics, and trade shows. Commercial warehousing and distribution is another line of business that I provide. I also contribute to marketing efforts and customer service.

I am a native Texas and have lived in the DFW area all my life. I am involved in several connection organizations like the Metrocrest Chamber of Commerce, International Facility Management Association, International Right of Way Association and Commercial Relocation Network. I enjoy travel, gardening, and spending time with my family.

I believe my experience and direct involvement in relocation's is my best asset. I can assist you with your moving needs and I have a support team to ensure that even the most complicated tasks are completed on time and on budget.
