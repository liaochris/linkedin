Antony Van Couvering's Summary

I am the CEO and a director of Top Level Domain Holdings (TLDH), created to invest in and operate new top-level domains. I'm also the CEO of Minds + Machines, a wholly owned subsidiary of TLDH, which provides registry services to top-level domains.

I grew up in the U.S. and overseas (Africa, Europe, Middle East). I have a degree in comparative literature from Columbia; I waited tables in New York and managed a restaurant in Paris; I started an art-book publishing house out of Paris and New York; I edited and helped publish works by Joseph Campbell, a scholar of myth and religion, before and after his death, with HarperCollins. Then I found the Internet -- see my experience below.

Specialties: New top-level domains and domain names. I have a lot of experience with startups generally.
