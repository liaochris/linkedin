Edward M. Sullivan (Ed@SullivanMediaGroup.com)'s Summary

Ed’s passion for excellence and quality built the world’s most respected name in the industry for Brand, Marketing Communications and related services for Media and Entertainment companies, Pittard Sullivan. As Chairman/CEO and Co-Founder of Pittard Sullivan, he led the company’s growth and expansion by opening five offices around the world and took the company from start-up in 1986 to the most celebrated and respected company in media and entertainment branding and marketing by 2001.

He has personally overseen the brand & marketing efforts for over 50 launches and re-launches of major media companies including: CBS, ABC, FOX broadcasting, Discovery Networks, Turner Broadcasting, HBO and Disney cable networks, DirecTV satellite DTH as well as several major new platforms introductions including WebTV, AOL Plus and TiVo to name a few.

At SMG leads the Marketing and Creative initiatives for their clients. His marketing skills and creative talent has been recognized with 5 Emmys and hundreds of other industry awards. Today, Sullivan’s continued interest in digital media and the new emergence of broadband distribution has ignited his passion for helping to make his client’s visions and marketing efforts come to life.
