Jim Barth's Summary

When you expect the highest level of service, attention to detail and thorough understanding of the local luxury resort real estate market...count on one of the most knowledgeable and successful real estate brokers in the Deer Valley®/Park City area.

Having received my bachelor's degree in real estate finance and graduate degrees in law and business from the University of San Diego, I began my real estate career in 1982, and have since attained a broad professional background, including brokerage, finance, law and development.

Prior to law school, I worked with Charterhouse Investment Company as an exclusive representative for some of the nation's premier lenders structuring commercial real estate joint ventures. Arriving in Park City in 1991, I spent several years as in-house counsel with Prudential Coleman Real Estate, serving as Director of the Coleman Consulting Group, which specialized in entitlement, pre-development and brokerage-related matters.

In 1994, I became a partner with the law firm of David W. Johnson and Associates, specializing in transactional real estate matters, representing of some of the area’s most prominent real estate interests.

Transitioning to a full-time Broker in 2001, I was honored during my first year selling by the Park City Board of Realtors with "Rookie of the Year" and "Professional Achievement” awards, as well as Prudential's "Rookie of the Year" award for outstanding achievement in real estate sales and service.

The decision to become an Associate Broker with Summit Sotheby’s International Realty was grounded in the firm's stellar reputation, extraordinary marketing savvy and international network of esteemed luxury brokers, who understand the unique mindset of upscale clients and distinctive homes.

For more than a decade, I served on the Park City Planning Commission, which I chaired for two terms. I also served on the Summit County Planning Commission and was Chairman of the Summit County Water Concurrency Board.

Specialties

When it comes to buying or selling Deer Valley® Resort and Park City luxury real estate, Jim Barth & Associates provides invaluable expertise and unparalleled service to buyers and sellers alike. Backed by the global marketing network of Sotheby’s International Realty, I offer an array of resources to ensure your property is represented in the most effective way.
