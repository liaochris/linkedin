David Gilbert's Summary

Business owner, manager and consultant with a proven track record of maximizing resources, streamlining production and costs while increasing ROI. Cross industry functional expertise includes: aerospace & defense, pulp & paper, electronics, consumer goods and medical supplies. Extensive experience in discrete, repetitive and process manufacturing environments.

Project manager and senior consultant with a proven track record of ERP implementations: project planning, conference room pilots, customizations, testing, data migration and user training.
