Darrell Butler's Summary

"Making workplace tension PRODUCTIVE!"

Darrell Butler is President of Butler Consulting Group – BC Innovations, specializing in work environment productivity through personal empowerment and inclusion. Darrell is recognized as a leading resource in the field of leveraging diversity and professional development. For over a decade he has assisted major companies, non-profit organizations, and government and academic institutions with innovative strategies to transform relationships to high performance results. Mr. Butler has facilitated diversity, empowerment, and work-life balance training courses to all levels of organizations across many industries. He has also motivated audiences through keynote speeches, leadership seminars, and individual coaching.

Darrell is also committed to developing our youth through mentoring as chair of the Greater Orlando CARES Mentoring Movement, a local affiliate of National CARES Mentoring Movement.

Specialties: Diversity Management, Productive Work Environment Strategies, Empowerment, Creativity, Innovation, Leadership
