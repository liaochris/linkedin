David Banes' Summary

I have more than twenty years experience in the ICT industry on three continents and have held senior and middle management roles in technical support, software development, product marketing, IT and operations.

Born in the UK and now also an Australian citizen, I left the UK as Head of Product Management for Dr Solomon’s(McAfee) to setup Dr Solomon's North American operations in Boston, USA as VP Operations & Customer Service.

I moved to Australia as Marketing Manager for a small Australian distributor and took up the role as Regional Manager for Symantec Security Response in 1997 before joining MessageLabs as Technical Director in 2003.

I have been running my own businesses since 2005, initially a cloud services provider and now also as an ICT Management Consultant. I and am now located in the UK to the west of London.

Currently also helping manage, run and refine processes for the Kop Hill Climb (a non competitive revival of an old motor sport event) at Princes Risborough an annual event which donates surplus funds to local charities.

Specialties: Broad software product development and management in the security, communication and collaboration markets. Experience in EMEA, USA and Asia Pacific Markets. Industry and media liaison and spokesperson with print, radio and TV experience. Keen interest in classics and sports cars having competed in several long distance rallies, hill climbs and sprints.
