L. Joseph Posner, MS, JD's Summary

L. Joseph Posner, MS, JD, is a commercial real estate broker and business consultant.

Posner has been a New York State Licensed Real Estate Broker for over 22 years, and concentrates in Manhattan commercial real estate.

Posner has a diverse background of 35 years of professional and business experience, including accounting, finance, law and real estate.

Posner's work experience includes Business Consultant, Senior Vice President of a Manhattan commercial real estate brokerage firm, Managing Partner of a boutique law firm, real estate Syndication Specialist with a publicly held financial services firm, and as Tax Manager and Senior Auditor with Big 4 CPA firms.

L. Joseph Posner holds a Juris Doctor Law degree, a Master's degree in business (taxation) and a Bachelor's degree in accounting.

Specialties: Real estate, business consulting, leasing, investments, taxes, mergers and acquisitions.
