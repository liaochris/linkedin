Buzz Murphy's Summary

Buzz Murphy has touched the lives of thousands of adult learners over the past 20 years either through direct classroom contact or through dozens of online, eLearning and webinar courses he has developed.

He has specialized in "Technology Knowledge Transfer", utilizing advanced adult learning concepts and techniques to communicate complex software and hardware application concepts to business customers and suppliers, as well as internal corporate staff personnel.

In essence, “Making geek-speak into human-speak.”

He has developed an agile methodology approach to the management of learning project scope and instructional design, course development and build-out, as well as project documentation and technical writing.

Buzz Murphy has developed and successfully implemented Training as a Service (TaaS) and Training as a Product (TaaP) programs which have significantly increased revenue for clients and employers on their proprietary software products.

Utilizing specialized motivational training techniques, he has developed Transformational Technology Training. A typical application would be training users on a new technology system during a corporate merger.

Through the years, he has acquired seventeen Information Technology (IT) and training industry certifications. The most significant being Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP). He is also vendor certified to facillitate many behavioral skills, communication and management courses.

Small groups to entire organizations have been trained in such subjects as SharePoint, Lean - Six Sigma, ITIL, IT Security, MS Office Suite, Adobe Suite, as well as various instructor led training programs leading to the learners testing for “certification.”

As an avid public speaker, he has presented numerous topics before dozens of civic, church and corporate groups including the American Society for Training and Development.
