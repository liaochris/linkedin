Melvin Wylie's Summary

I am currently employed at Georgetown Law as an Environmental Custodial Manager within the Facilities Management Department. My duties include overseeing the daily custodial operations on a 5 building campus located in the heart of DC to include union, staff and contractors.

I Chair the Facilities Safety Committee and sit on the Georgetown Law Green Committee. I also oversee the Plant Vendor, Custodial and Exterior Glass Cleaning Contract. Among the many responsibilities I also organizing the move in and move out of the student dorms.

Outside of Georgetown Law I run GreenNews4U an Environmental News Blog. I report on local, national and international issues. I also bringing attention to the diversity of the green movement by interviewing people, companies and organizations across the globe making a much needed change and impact in the world.

My hobbies include writing, online gaming, building PC’s, hacking/rooting smart phones, and fishing. As an avid bicycle rider I enjoy cycling to and from work as well as taking my family out on bicycle outings around the DC area. we all need to reduce our carbon footprint!

You can follow my environmental web page at http://greennews4u.com and listen to my interviews.
