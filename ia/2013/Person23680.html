Chris Thomas' Summary

With 18 years experience in the retail design industry, 9 years ago I decided to start up my own consultancy business.

Since then, I have established a highly successful business, working on major retail projects for clients such as Marks and Spencer, Boots, Superdrug, L'Oreal, Rimmel, Collection 2000, Tesco, Asda, Morrisons and Wilkinsons.

Specialties: Design Management, Independent and Client focused solutions, Specialist retail project management.
