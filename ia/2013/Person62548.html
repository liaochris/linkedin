Jeri Palumbo's Summary

Jeri Palumbo brings years of high level technical experience ranging from live broadcast engineering of Major League Sports and Entertainment, music recording engineer-producer and arranger, IT technologist, Audio/Video editor... to Marketing and Merchandising for Radio City Music Hall. Her consummate professionalism extends beyond the technical to the positive attitude, articulate communication skills and accessible demeanor she brings to any job. Her intellectual curiosity drives her ambition to learn cutting edge technologies and her application engineering skills help technology suppliers understand the boundaries between the theoretical and the practical. Jeri continues to work in the broadcast sports and entertainment arena while exploring opportunities in Technical Product Development and Placement, Marketing and PR/Promotions.

Founder: New Orleans Animal Rescue. Jeri also worked on the Pet Evacutation Bill (passed Oct 2006), a bill making it a Federal Law that pets will be accommodated in an emergency evacuation, after the horrendous tragedies to both grieving pets and families after Hurricane Katrina.

https://community.hsus.org/campaign/FED_2006_PETS3
