Lisa Dale Jones' Summary

I help you to develop your unique message and speaking style so you can share that message publicly, and have your intended healing impact on the world.

I believe that if you don’t share your ideas, inspiration and story, the world is missing something very important! I don’t coach speech in the usual “corporate” manner; rather, I work with you to bring out your distinctive inherent message and communications style that naturally draw listeners and clients, as they experience the full you. Some of my methods are unconventional, but they’re a lot of fun and definitely bring results!

Together we’ll dig for answers to questions like these:
~What is the essence of your story?
~How can that story be developed and enriched?
~How can your delivery be totally in sync with that message?

Before forming Speaking with Soul in 2005, I spent five years running the speaker training program for the Board of Lectureship, an international speakers bureau comprising over 50 professional spiritual healers from all over the world who give over 1,800 talks per year. I coached the speakers individually, and designed and led workshops at international training conferences in Europe, Africa, and the United States. A large part of our work together was finding ways to express healing, spiritual ideas in ways their listeners would easily understand.

For nearly ten years I was a successful producer and on-air talent for Monitor Radio, the broadcast edition of The Christian Science Monitor, winning the prestigious Gabriel Award for reporting with the “ability to uplift and nourish the human spirit.” Part of my job was to train print reporters to tell their stories orally for radio.

My background informs the way I coach: as a vocalist, I use analogies from music and singing. As a reader, I look for the depth and draw of good storytelling. As a journalist, I dig for clarity and accuracy. As a spiritual thinker, I encourage clients to listen for ways to inspire and enlighten their listeners.

Everyone needs some help with public speaking, but most training focuses on corporate speaking skills. And that’s just not you! (Or me.) The coaching I do is designed to bring out your authentic self when you speak, and to enable you to tell your unique story, a soul-based transformational story that is needed to help heal the world.

All my coaching is based on the Five S’s. If you work with me, you will learn to be a speaker who is Spiritual, Selfless, Sincere, Story-based, and Skilled.

Senior Producer

Monitor Radio, the broadcast edition of The Christian Science Monitor

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Online Media industry

January 1983 – January 1996 (13 years 1 month) Greater Boston Area

I worked for Monitor Radio in two different stints for a total of ten years. In 1995 I won a prestigious Gabriel Award for feature reporting. During my time at Monitor Radio I produced two different weekend programs - one for teens in Europe that was aired on Radio Luxembourg and one for public radio in the US that aired on over 100 stations nationwide. I developed a unique "sound" for each program, raising audience levels significantly for both. I also hosted a Letterbox program on shortwave radio, reading letters from listeners all over the world. I not only produced the programs using the best radio freelancers I could find, I also trained Monitor print journalists to report for radio.
