Rodney Alberto's Summary

IMMIGRATION: We represents individuals who wish to visit, live, and/or work in the United States. We also represent individuals who seek to Naturalize. Also, some of our clients face serious immigration issues, and so we represent these clients in removal and or deportation proceedings. We are fluent in all of the requirements for both immigrants and non-immigrant visa procedures. We represent small businesses who wish to access an international pool of labor when local markets are too limited and/or do not meet specific human capital requirements. Very significantly, we are members of the American Immigration Lawyers Association.

REAL ESTATE / GROWING BUSINESSES: We represent growing business in many facets, primarily transactional in nature. This includes entity formation, dissolutions, mergers, corporate resolutions, and contract review.

As to real estate law, we work with realtors and consumers as to the purchase & sale of residential properties. We represent real estate developers in complex leasing or land use matters.

ELDER LAW, ESTATE PLANNING: We represent clients who are planning for the mature phases of their life, whose concerns include: asset preservation, long-term care, tax abatement, and small business legacy planning. This type of work starts with the basics: Last Wills & Testaments, Powers of Attorney, and extends to the complex use of Trusts to achieve personal objectives.
