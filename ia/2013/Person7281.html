Andrew Foster's Summary

I graduated from Emory University with a B.A. in sociology and Spanish and began my career in marketing and digital platforms. I strategically transitioned my academic studies of demographics into a position that enabled me to communicate directly with consumers.

At Turner Broadcasting, I was hand-selected to coordinate the marketing campaign for Steven Spielberg’s Falling Skies, the network’s highest priority and Turner Broadcasting’s first international premiere. Falling Skies outperformed key competitors and delivered 5,905,000 viewers, ranking it as cable’s #1 new series in 2011-to-date, a direct result of TNT’s marketing campaign.

Following my success at Turner, I graduated from Georgia Tech’s MBA program with a concentration in marketing and successfully led communications projects as a contractor with The Coca-Cola Company. I leveraged what I learned in my MBA program to create an internal system to increase the quality and speed at which the Chief Sustainability Officer communicates with the public. The system reduced our response time by 94% and saved my Director 8.375 working days per year.

I am currently leading digital marketing initiatives at Promove and welcome opportunities that will help to create value for the Company.
