Leslie Bradshaw's Summary

How I add value:
- I am a process engineer. I am able to bring together disparate ideas and disciplines to achieve maximum efficiency and desired results.
- I am a leader and a strategist. I believe in leading by doing and strategizing based on concrete data and experience. I've earned the respect of the teams I've worked with and the industry by testing, recalibrating and ultimately, succeeding.
- I bring positivity, solution-oriented thinking and mentorship to the teams I work on. I love sharing joy, laughter and optimism, but am also careful to temper this with input from trusted voices and learnings from previous experiences.

Major accomplishments:
- I lead innovative civic education efforts for the elections in 2008 with C-SPAN, in 2010 with foursquare, and in 2012 with Google and C-SPAN, respectively. I believe in the power of social media and technology to educate, inspire and activate.
