Jojest Thomson's Summary

Being an accomplished user-centred designer with excellent technical abilities, spanning over 13 years internet production experience encompassing Telecommunications, Banking & Financial Institutions, Media, Digital, Design & Creative Agencies to name a few! I utilise both my technical and creative skills from inception with the client, discussing, advising and understanding the requirements definition, through to specifying user-interfaces, designing the visual look and feel, addressing and running usability evaluations.
