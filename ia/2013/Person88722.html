Engagement Manager

ITN Consulting

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Broadcast Media industry

January 2009 – October 2012 (3 years 10 months) London, United Kingdom

ITN is a television and news production company, established in 1955 and best known for the production of ITV News, Channel 4 News and 5 News.

The company has built up a number of leading subsdidiary businesses including the world’s largest moving image archive and a productions business creating factual and multimedia content for broadcasters, online and mobile operators.

ITN Consulting is a strategy and operational consultancy designed to complement and extend the expertise of ITN and provide end-to-end consultancy in the media sector. In addition to responsibilities relating to the day-to-day running of the business, projects included:

• Business planning – assessing markets and developing detailed propositions, operational plans and financial forecasts for new product launches including television channels, OTT services, websites and mobile applications (Europe, Africa, Middle East, SE Asia)

• Market analysis –sizing and segmenting markets ranging from digital advertising in the Middle East, global transactional TV services, global digital models for radio stations to the UK TV facilities market (Europe, Middle East, Asia Pacific, Africa)

• Implementation and Turnaround – conducting strategic reviews, recommendation development and operational turnaround of large broadcasters or their component divisions (Africa, Middle East)

• Regulatory support – developing scenario analysis, forecasting and lobbying documents for broadcasters and telecoms operators (e.g. UK, Europe)

• Market research – scoping, commissioning and analysing extensive quantitative and qualitative research campaigns with global partners such as Ipsos and Nielsen (UK, SE Asia, Middle East)

• Sports – including commercial rights valuation and operational enhancement, bid management, sports improvement and broadcast infrastructure reviews

Quickly established position as self-starter with strong client presence. Worked alone with high profile clients such as BT and Siemens and supported on projects across media, telecoms and sport including clients in the UK, Europe and the Middle East

• For Optima Telekom, analysed key market trends and conditions in Croatia and surrounding markets in preparation for the company’s Initial Public Offering

The project included around 100 interviews and recommendations for improvement across Openreach

• For Invitel, prepared a white paper for presentation to the Hungarian regulator supporting the introduction of a fourth mobile licence

• Conducted an assessment of the impact of changes to must-carry regulations applied to cable network operators
