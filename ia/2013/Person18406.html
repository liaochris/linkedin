Jeff Bruton's Summary

I am a seasoned Art Director & experienced Web Developer (19-year career). My background is in art, design, marketing, but I have always pursued my passions for Interactive & multimedia opportunities. My career has been heavy in branding, designing logos, advertisements for print & web, email marketing, interactive design & web development. I am a talented designer, a budding 3d animator & videographer, a web programmer fluent in many client-side/server-side languages, and capable of managing multiple projects or small team. With a wide range of skills, talents & experience, I am considered a "jack of all trades", filling specialized roles with ease, or being a power-house in the right environment.

As a designer, I enjoy creating for the web, and video/animation, creating works that move, change or evolve, engaging the audience. Although I want every design to "look pretty & win awards" for creative awesomeness, my designs are ultimately framed by UX, functionality & effective delivery of information. I enjoy creating websites that deliver measurable results, proof through A/B testing, traffic analytics, SEO ranking & conversion goals.

I prefer opportunities engaging me for both design & development; however, if your needs only utilize my programming skills, know that you have a "designer's eye" behind the development of your sites. I pride myself on reproducing a "pixel perfect" version of designs created by other artists. As a back-end developer, I prefer to work with Drupal. I take pride in developing powerful, flexible sites incredibly easy to use & update. I go the extra effort to provide the most easy-to-use features & functions possible. I admit it... I'm addicted to the "wow", and love to hear clients brag about how easy their site is to maintain and update.

Please feel free to contact me if you are interested or need more information.
