Hill Slowinski, J.D.'s Summary

Hill is an award-winning Realtor and Top Producer, bringing over 30 years of professional, marketing, executive, legal, publishing, and business experience to the table. He has facilitated many residential, commercial, and private real estate transactions in DC, Maryland, Virginia, and internationally.

Hill has helped a range of clients from first-time buyers, to growing families, to empty-nesters, to individuals in transition with homes in all price ranges, from condos to single family homes to luxury estates. Hill represents only a few clients at a time in order to ensure he personally can provide full support.

Hill is a native Washingtonian and lifelong area resident – Georgetown, Spring Valley, Wesley Heights, American University Park, Glover Park, Alexandria, Arlington, McLean, Falls Church, Bethesda, Potomac and Southern Maryland (Charles County) – whose mother’s family settled Maryland and Virginia in the 1600s. Hill earned his Juris Doctor degree from George Mason University School of Law and his Bachelor of Arts degree, with pre-law emphasis, from Saint Vincent College. He lives in the Washington, DC area with his wife Maureen and their two sons and two daughters.

Hill applies the power of an exclusive affiliation with Christie's International Real Estate, W.C. & A.N. Miller's private agent network, Sloans & Kenyon, and Long & Foster’s Extraordinary Props programs to marketing properties. He gets results by closing successful, timely transactions and exceeding expectations. He can be reached directly at 301-320-8430.

"Washingtonian by Birth, Lawyer by Training, Your Realtor® by Choice"

Often cited by local and national press as an expert on the local and luxury real estate markets, Hill has been interviewed by CNN, CBS, NBC Channel 4, NBC Washington, USA Channel 9, The Washington Post, The Washington Examiner, The Washington Times, Politico, Urban Turf, The Gazette Newspapers, Potomac Almanac, and other local media.

Specialties

Hill is regularly recognized for delivering unsurpassed professional solutions, service, and options to clients seeking the advice, guidance, and support he offers through Miller and Long & Foster companies’ services. He understands this real estate market and excels in his role as case manager, problem solver, consultant, skilled negotiator, market expert, and resource for authoritative information. [See client testimonials at www.HillSlowinski.com.]
