Lea Setegn's Summary

High-energy professional offering skills ranging from community outreach and customer service to public relations and writing/editing. Accomplished public speaker who has worked as a media spokeswoman for two nationally affiliated nonprofits and as a corporate trainer. Passionate about helping people navigate the medical system and access affordable health care and health insurance. Trained by the Centers for Medicaid and Medicare in the enrollment process for the Federally Facilitated Health Insurance Marketplace/Exchange.
