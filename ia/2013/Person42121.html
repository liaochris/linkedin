Eric Higginbotham's Summary

Most software professionals will tell you why a certain technology, tool, or process is the absolute best. I won't.

I've had the opportunity to work in a variety of roles (Systems Administration, Database Administration, Development) with a variety of technologies, and have realized that technology is an instrument. No single technology, tool, or process is THE answer; people are the differentiator. Great people are exponentially more valuable than the latest version of a development environment, operating system, or database technology. Innovation in software is driven by people, and my experience is in the design and implementation of software solutions that make the best use of technology and people.

I am passionate about technology and I love working with talented teams to build incredible products.
