  • Travel with Confidence: A Roadmap to Build Your Travel Spirit

    • Prolific Living Inc.
    • April 29, 2011
    Authors: Farnoosh Brock

    The Travel with Confidence book gives you the confidence to embrace your travel dreams. It helps you overcome your natural fears and anxieties on leaving home behind and embarking on that journey to your destination – be it a hop around a few cities or over oceans and continents across the globe. In this guide, every fear and anxiety associated with travel is up for scrutiny and every excuse for not traveling has to stand up to reason.

    Travel does not discriminate. Travel is not reserved for the young and unattached; travel is not set aside for the rich or retired. Travel embraces people from all walks of life. Travel can begin anytime and need not stop until you and only you decide it needs to stop. Travel is open and accessible to anyone willing and desirous of it. Travel only asks that you give it a chance and in order to take a chance, you must first crush the fears and overcome the anxieties weighing you down.

    This guide helps you understand and eliminate the wide array of travel fears such as leaving home, jobs, children or pets behind, going through the motions of air travel, choosing the right travel companions or traveling solo, being safe on the road, managing culture shock and language barriers, and doing it all with your limited time and money.
