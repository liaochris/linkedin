Kraig Kleeman's Summary

KRAIG KLEEMAN is an accomplished Author, Speaker, and Global Sales Strategist. His first book entitled The Must-React System: A Users Guide to Cold-Calling is considered to be The Bible for sales prospecting. Kleeman is a frequent speaker at sales conferences, executive retreats, and motivational events and was a recent guest panelist with President George W. Bush at The World Leaders Forum.

KRAIG KLEEMAN has overseen more than 1 million sales presentations, as well as more than 1 million customer service & retention / renewal calls in his career. He is the originator of The Must-React System, the world's leading selling methodology. Under Kleeman’s direction, Blaire Group analysts have directly observed, measured, and documented more than 25,000 customer facing presentations conducted by sales professionals across more than 50 companies. The Must-React System is a proven methodology for sales process development. As a fully vetted methodology, it contains a special, unique emphasis on pipeline development and prospect conversions and produces significant and measurable results.

KRAIG KLEEMAN has overseen numerous offshore, call-center selection projects. His deep expertise into call-center practices, analytics, delivery mechanisms led to his Advisory and Referral Services practice. Kleeman is the originator of Call Center Scorecard, which is a weighted, quantitative call center scorecard methodology which is the centerpiece of many call-center evaluation & selection practice.

KRAIG KLEEMAN was the founder and CEO of Express Direct. Under his leadership, Kleeman led the company from $0 to $25 million in sales in less than four years. The company’s twenty-five (25) FTE sales professionals adopted The Must-React System as a rigorous discipline, which proved to be the catalyst for the explosive growth of the company’s digital pre-press solutions group. Kleeman and his board of directors sold the business to a west coast based technology firm.

September 2006 – Present (6 years 11 months) Chicago, IL

KraigKleeman.com is home to The Must-React System, and specializes in business process transformation, where the function of sales is at the epicenter of that transformation.

The Must-React System is a "Sales Process Methodology" that equips sales executives and business development professionals at building sales pipelines and gaining subsequent conversions. It adheres to a strict, well-developed set of principles that are transferable / customizable to any industry or business climate. It provides vital direction for cold-calling, tele-prospecting, sales presentations, and closing. It offers specific guidance for introductory presentation skills at all levels. The core elements of The Must-React System include:

* Introductory-Messaging: Advanced Doctrines and Precepts
* Leading with Fact-Based Research vs. Leading with Value Proposition Language
* Managing Objections with Excellence
* Research-Based Briefings: Practical Models &
Implementations
* Optimized Talk-Time: Principles & Concepts
* Best Practices for Conducting Product Demonstrations
* Closing with Excellence

Kraig Kleeman's Publications

  • The Must-React System

    • iUniverse Publishing
    • March 20, 2008
    Authors: Kraig Kleeman

    Every company that wants to continue growth needs their sales team to be proficient in finding and closing net-new opportunities. Most sales persons, unfortunately, are not good at gaining new business, much less performing even the most basic prospecting practices. There are a multitude of reasons for this phenomenon. But the biggest reason is that sales professionals are untrained in vital, lead development / introductory messaging techniques. Especially in the realm of cold-calling and tele-prospecting. Further, there is a culture of sales resistance that exists, and few sales professionals are equipped to penetrate it. The Must-React System is written help all sales professionals master the art of persuasion, and especially in the important area of cold-calling, prospecting, and sales pipeline development.
