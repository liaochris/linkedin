Christine Wagner's Summary

As a Certified Dream Builder transformational life coach, trainer and coach, Christine has a deep passion to inspire and empower you to reach your highest potential to live the life you would love living. Offering techniques, strategies and support every step of the way to close the gap from where you are now to living the life of your dreams. Christine will empower you to shine your light brightly so that you can give your unique gift to the world!

As part of her outreach activities, Christine is a trainer, coach and facilitator for Pathways for Veterans bringing experiential healing work to our local veterans.

As a Dream Builder coach, Christine can help you design and manifest the life that’s in harmony with your soul’s purpose by introducing you to a powerful PROVEN 90 - day process which you can apply to your life to overcome issues and limiting beliefs by lifting you up and helping you change your emotional responses to them. Through a personalized program she can help you release negative emotions from the past, permanently by re-programming your thoughts for positive thinking strategies to give you the mindset you need for success, happiness, harmonious relationships and freedom. Through these powerful and potent techniques, you can truly create the life you love to live.

Through principles of her own teaching, Christine has been able to fulfill her own goals in entrepreneurship by founding and managing her own successful Private Dog Boarding and Pet Sitting business that embodies her love and compassion for these beings by providing a healthy and nurturing environment for her clients and their beloved pets.

As an Intern Spiritual Practitioner, Christine continues her “high consciousness” education as a contributive member and intern at the Spiritual Center for Positive Living in Cameron Park, CA for the last 3 years. Christine assists families and their members with affirmations and positive prayers to the congregation during Sunday Services.
