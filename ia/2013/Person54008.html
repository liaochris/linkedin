Huib Schoots' Summary

I have more than 15 years of experience in IT and software testing. After my study Business Informatics I started in IT as a developer. Soon I found out that developing is fun but testing is more fun because in the 90-ies testing wasn't taken really serious yet. I bridged my career from developer to tester via a period of working as a test engineer automating tests. Software testing is my passion and I simply love the game of software testing.

I have (international) experience in various roles within testing, project and line management, trainer, coach and consultant at Logica(CMG), VX Company, Polteq and Rabobank.

Currently I work at codecentric. I like combining people, testing and agile trying to maximize value, help people grow and make testing more fun. I try to share my passion in coaching, training, writing and giving presentations on testing, context-driven and agile topics.

I am a proud member of the context-driven school of software testing. In my free time I am board member of TestNet, the Dutch testing network association. TestNet offers its 1500+ members the opportunity meet with other testers and share knowledge and experience. Within TestNet I am responsible for the special interest groups and I also participate in the event & program committee. I also participate in DEWT, the Dutch Exploratory Workshop on Testing. I am a member of AST and black-belt in the Miagi-Do School of software testing.
