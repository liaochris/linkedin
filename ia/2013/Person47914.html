George Hedley's Summary

George Hedley
Certified Speaking Professional CSP
Licensed Professional Business Coach

Helping Contractors & Entrepreneurs Get Their Build Businesses To Work!

George Hedley, CSP, ‘The Business-Builder,’ is a business building expert. He is an entrepreneur, business owner, certified speaking professional, business coach, author and the recognized authority on how to build a growing business into a company that consistently produces bottom-line results, profits, wealth and equity. He has authored several books including: “On-Purpose…On-Target!”; “The Business Success Blueprint” series and ‘Get Your Business To Work!”

He founded & built his major commercial construction and real estate development company from $0 to $50 million dollars in only 7 years! For his accomplishments, George received the nationally recognized “Entrepreneur of the Year” award by Ernst & Young and “Venture” magazine. George’s expertise is based on his real world experience: Leading & managing great people; owning & running a profitable company; and getting things done. His business success is the result of long-time repeat loyal customers, focusing on priorities that really count and management systems that always make a profit.

Today, along with managing his company, George owns HARDHAT Presentations presenting motivational keynote speeches, workshops and seminars to companies and associations. George has received the prestigious ‘Certified Speaking Professional’ designation from the National Speakers Association earned by only 7% of the members. His ‘Profit-Builder’ Programs include topics on Building Leaders, Building Profits, Building Customers and Building Wealth.

He also provides ongoing professional business Coaching, hosts peer to peer mentor groups, facilitates Strategic Planning retreats for companies, and leads ‘Profit-Builder Circles’ for business owners of construction companies. George also owns Hardhat BIZSCHOOL online university for contractors.
