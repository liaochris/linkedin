Matt Diamond's Experience

Editor-In-Chief

Takin' Mines

February 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months) Brooklyn, NY

Takin’ Mines, is a webzine/blog site affiliated with the Diamond Media 360 Netowrk that publishes audio content, music videos, editorials, interviews and reviews from industry journalists and staff writers. With an emphasis of quality over quantity, Takin’ Mines provides daily updates that cover the spectrum of the hip-hop populace as we see it...."When they wanna act up, yo, I just start takin’ mine..”

President

Diamond Media 360, Inc.

July 2011 – Present (2 years 3 months) Brooklyn, NY

Diamond Media 360 (DM360) is a one-stop, multi-dimensional marketing outfit designed to provide artists and indie labels with the tools to succeed in today’s independent music industry. Matt Diamond, Founder & President of DM360, has taken the continued success of his Coalmine Records label and calibrated a winning formula to help others thrive in this dramatically changing landscape. With major labels experiencing an alarming decline in record sales, coupled with an oversaturation of music, artists must employ new strategies to get noticed. By allowing DM360 to provide the proper consulting, new media technology, online marketing strategies and premium digital distribution services, artists and labels may begin to experience new thresholds of exposure, relevance and success.

Founder/C.E.O.

Coalmine Records, Inc.

April 2005 – Present (8 years 6 months) Brooklyn, NY

Coalmine Records is a Brooklyn, New York based independent hip-hop record label. Over the years, Coalmine Records has amassed an extensive hip-hop catalog and has had worked with several iconic and up-coming artists, producers and DJs such as Pharaohe Monch, El Da Sensei, Kool G. Rap, Talib Kweli, Large Professor, Heltah Skeltah, Dilated Peoples, Bekay, Big K.R.I.T., Custom Made, Emilio Rojas, Skyzoo, Alchemist, J.R. Rotem, M-Phazes, Illmind, Khyrsis, Mick Boogie, DJ Revolution, DJ Babu and more.
