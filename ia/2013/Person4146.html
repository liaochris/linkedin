Alex Kreitman's Summary

I have been in the media business for several years now and have gained a grasp on the future and which direction the industry is headed. My experience in both print and broadcast and both news and sales, has given me a unique view of our business.

I began working in print journalism as a sports reporter before quickly jumping on the digital train to manage the website for the Times-News in Burlington, NC. After a few years in that role, I went back to Elon University and earned my Masters Degree in Interactive Media while continuing to grow web traffic and the audience on TheTimesNews.com.

I then moved into broadcast journalism, leaving NC for SC. I became the New Media Director at WCSC-TV in Charleston, SC. At the time, Live5News.com was averaging less than 500,000 page views a month, but in a short time we improved to over 1 million, and just passed 4 million in August.

In April, I was promoted to Research Director and made the switch from the News to the Sales Department. After several months of coming up with new and innovative products and packages for our sales team to sell on air, on mobile and online, I accepted a new position as Digital Marketing Manager.

I now lead our digital selling efforts for Live5News.com and continue to help grow our website and mobile audience through digital marketing.

Our fast-changing business is adapting every day and my job is to stay ahead of those changes and keep WCSC at the top of the industry in South Carolina.

Specialties

Innovation, Web design, mobile, Flash, shooting and editing video, marketing, writing, editing, html, social media.
