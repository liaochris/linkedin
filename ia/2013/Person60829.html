Jeff Cohan's Summary

nSiteful Web Builders is a full-service Web design, development, and consulting firm based in Alpharetta, Georgia.

We specialize in designing and building dynamic, database-driven Web applications and Web sites (including WordPress) and in providing social marketing consulting and training.

Our target market consists of small and medium-size businesses and organizations for whom a professional, robust, and compelling Web presence is instrumental to their sales, marketing, customer service and operational strategies.

People and organizations throughout the U.S. and beyond hire us to help them do a better job of connecting and interacting with their audiences (customers, employees, suppliers, investors, members, fans...) using the tools, techniques and strategies peculiar to the Internet.

Specialties

Web applications. Web sites. WordPress. Social marketing.

nSiteful is a full-service Web services company with an emphasis on the human element.

We help clients get the attention, trust, and engagement they deserve through the insightful implementation of the right Web tools, strategies, techniques, and technologies.

To this end, we design and build Web sites (from scratch and with WordPress), set up and support clients' social networking properties, and guide clients in their content marketing efforts. Most importantly, and unlike many of our competitors, we help clients rediscover the businesses they are in by focusing them on the outcomes they deliver to their customers rather than just the products and services they sell. We help clients earn new customers, retain old ones, delight them all, and discover new and better ways of connecting and interacting with all of their audiences — customers, employees, suppliers, investors, members, fans.

We also help clients improve the quality of their customer service efforts and streamline their operations with customized back-office Web-based solutions targeted to their needs and goals and those of their customers.

To this end, we help clients identify the operations they perform today that can be executed more effectively and efficiently, and then we work with them to design and build user-friendly, database-driven turnkey systems to revolutionize those operations. If clients' requirements can be addressed by existing third-party systems, we'll help them evaluate the candidates and we'll steward the integration for them.
