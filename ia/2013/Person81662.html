Lisa Qualls' Summary

Lisa Qualls is a high-energy inspirational thinker and business management leader with over two decades of professional experience. With a broad knowledge across the various business silos and the processes that integrate them, Lisa has been a vital player in many global companies assisting in their operational strategies that create opportunities and sustainability. She has significant technology experience, a passion for communication and is well known for her customer-first leadership approach that cultivates relationships and partnerships.

Working with all company sizes from start-up to Fortune 5, Lisa's client highlights include: Verizon Small Business, Cerner, CenturyLink, John Deere, Kansas City Chiefs, Sprint, LiveSTRONG Sporting Park, H&R Block, The Salvation Army and Saint Luke's Health System. She has developed sales opportunities, delivered multi-million dollar next-generation technology programs and created media integration campaigns that have been recognized by Hubspot, Yahoo and Agwired.

Lisa is currently a Storage Brand consultant at IBM. She is a leader, a proven entrepreneur and a technology specialist. She has the skills that create, solve and produce.

Dedicated to giving back and making a difference in her community, Lisa was the inaugural President and Chairman of the Board of the renowned Social Media Club of Kansas City and currently serves on various non-profit boards. She is a speaker in-demand who presents on many topics such as business integration, digital tools and social media. Lisa is valuable to any company needing a technology face-lift or change in business or leadership strategy and promises an experience that will be both productive and motivating. You can find her on Twitter: @lqualls4444.
