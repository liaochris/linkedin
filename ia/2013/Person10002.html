Founder / Editor-in-Chief / Curator

HESA inprint (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Fine Art industry

January 2011 – Present (2 years 8 months) Helsinki

Hesa inprint is a bilingual (Finnish & English) blog, web magazine and printed zine. Based in Finland’s Helsinki, we aim to provide a platform for artists, writers, photographers, film makers and anyone who has a vision and voice to share their opinions, work and stories not for a pay check but for the love of creating. Each month will feature a subject matter to which anyone can then submit their work. (See how to get involved)
Hesa inprint wants to give the views of all kinds of people from all walks of life. We are not intentionally a political publication but realise that in today’s world all things can and probably will be viewed as political even when that is not its intent.
The blog is updated weekly and it features a bulk of the submitted work. The web magazine is a monthly publication that features the best of that month’s submissions and will show the content (if applicable) in both Finnish and English. At the end of each month a printed version of the web magazine will be made in to a zine, which will include a centre fold artwork that is not featured in its full form in the web magazine but will comprise the cover image.
We are a group of 3 volunteers who run, edit, translate and create HESA inprint for you, we look forward to receiving your submissions, comments and hope you will enjoy our endeavours.

Artist / Writer

Anna Puhakka

October 1983 – Present (29 years 11 months) the world

I am an interdisciplinary artist/writer, committed to making conceptual work in any medium that best represents the idea. I don’t limit myself to working in the white cube and work in response to the context of my surroundings. Using an array of mediums such as installation, performance, video, photography, sound and text I create atmospheres for the viewers to experience.
