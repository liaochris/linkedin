James Broome's Summary

I am a senior technology consultant specialising in designing and building bespoke applications on the Microsoft development stack, using agile delivery methods. I manage and lead development teams in using best-practice software engineering methods to produce quality solutions for a variety of clients and industries.

My main experience over the last 12 years has been the Web, right through from ASP, into .NET 1.0 up to ASP.NET MVC 4 and Web API. However, the I've been lucky enough to have had the opportunity to work with a whole range of solution architectures - from WCF service layers, to multi-touch and gestural rich client applications with WPF, PixelSense and Kinect, to REST based APIs with OData, to full cloud-based applications using Windows Azure.

I've maintained a strong focus on engineering practices and reducing project delivery friction since attending J.P. Boodhoo's Nothin But .NET training 'bootcamp' in 2008. This opened my eyes to the benefits of Test Driven Development and Behaviour Driven Development, both of which I champion the use of amongst my teams and projects.

In the past, I've contributed to a number of open source and community projects, co-creating Who Can Help Me? (http://whocanhelpme.codeplex.com/), being on the dev team for the Sharp Architecture project (http://sharparchitecture.net/) and creating an couple of contributory projects for the Machine.Specifications BDD framework (https://github.com/jamesbroome). I occasionally blog at http://jamesbroo.me
