Leigh Riley's Summary

A confident client facing designer, adept at producing creative concepts while guiding clients through the design process right through to producing final artwork for production.

Having worked with studios of various sizes since 2005, he has a vast and varied experience to draw upon. He's an individual with drive, passion and ambition, championing design craftsmanship over the quick fix.

In 2010 Leigh began a successful period freelancing across the UK. This eventually snowballed as he carved out a niché market for himself in infographics & data visualisation which lead the birth of Studio LeighLeigh. In a very short space of time he developed a strong reputation, working with international brands and being featured in international design journals and publications.

In late 2011 Leigh was commissioned to work in Qatar with WRG, working as a freelance way-finding specialist, providing a challenge for Leigh to work in a culturally unique environment alongside some of the world's best in live event production. Early 2012 saw Leigh join Big as a brand specialist, a decision swayed by the opportunity to test his skills with some of the world's leading brands.

Today, Leigh spearheads Studio LeighLeigh with renewed vigour and enthusiasm, offering specialist design solutions for direct clients as well as an in-studio freelance service for design businesses.

As a keen traveler, most notably traveling the world solo in 2008, life experiences have moulded him in to a mature, self aware and intelligent individual. He doesn't take life too seriously and often brings a unique perspective to the table, making him a fun and interesting person to work with.
