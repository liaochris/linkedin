Chris Costner's Summary

Chris Costner, General Manager at Dealer World, has over thirteen years of automotive retail experience and takes pride in providing his services with integrity and accountability. Chris is passionate about the automotive industry in many aspects, in particular, keeping the customer top priority and the development of other industry professionals. Chris is a true professional in call handling, automotive sales, business development,advertising, digital marketing and social media strategies.

In addition to the countless articles Chris has written for the automotive industry, he enjoys training and speaking at dealerships across the country, dealer associations and key industry conferences. Chris has held various management positions in a dealership and served as Community Editor with DrivingSales. He is an active blogger on many of the automotive social platforms and enjoys the engagement with the automotive community.

Chris does have a long history of quality customer service and follows the “Customer for Life” principle of doing business.
