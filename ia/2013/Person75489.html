Kien Leong's Summary

.
> This Is Who I Am

I'm an Operations Management Consultant. I focus on operations planning and order fulfillment for manufacturing companies.

I have a solid background in manufacturing operations, demand flow and lean manufacturing. I've led projects for transformation to demand driven manufacturing.

My current work is developing advanced planning and scheduling solutions.


> This Is Who I Help

I work with manufacturing and distribution companies with high product mix / medium-high volume. Their challenge is to predict order performance during material and capacity constraints.

My clients want to extend ERP / MRP systems to finite scheduling and constraints-based planning. This approach is systems neutral, completely free from licensing or software integration.

Manufacturing companies are large users of Excel spreadsheets. They offer flexibility, but take business processes out of the information system. My clients want to extend flexibility, improve automation and bring the process back into a system.


> This Is How I Help Manufacturing Clients

We integrate process and information flow for order planning and fulfillment. This means linking information about customer demand to real-world execution events.

The result is an integrated system and method for planning and scheduling of production, inventory, sales and operations.

I build advanced planning and scheduling systems using Excel. I write and teach on the number one ranked site for production scheduling, http://production-scheduling.com.

Visit http://production-scheduling.com and join us to learn more.

There are many free resources including downloads, templates, tips and techniques for building planning and scheduling systems using Excel.
