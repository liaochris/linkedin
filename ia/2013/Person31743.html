David Erickson - e-Strategist's Summary

I can help you define your communications goals, identify the audiences with whom you need to communicate, discover how to reach them, understand how to communicate with them, and formulate a strategy that incorporates the most effective channels for reaching them, and employ the tactics that ensure your communication goals are successfully reached.
