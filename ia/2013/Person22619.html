Chris Herring's Summary

Chris Herring is a technology leader, specializing in Web, mobile and social publishing systems and interconnected workflows. He has spent his entire career in the printing, publishing and digital media industries and joined NBCUniversal in 2007. Before he joined NBCU, Chris spent six years at Forbes, Inc. as Director of Information Systems, and then moved to Hachette Filipacchi Media U.S., where he was Director of Internet Technology. In his current role at NBCU, Chris leads multiple content management, identity and advertising product teams that utilize open source technology and an Agile development process to build tools and related services that are used by all NBCUniversal brands to create and monetize digital experiences on any device or platform. Chris also serves on Madison, NJ's long-range education technology task force.

Before working in media, Chris had several roles at small, local businesses including small-engine mechanic, printing press operator, store manager and typography specialist. He lives with his wife Jennifer and their two children in bucolic Morris County, NJ from where he rides the train to work and walks to the pool during the summer.

Specialties: Agile Product Development, Product Management, Technology Management, Content Management, Publishing, Digital Media, Print Media, Editorial Workflow, Training, Content Strategy, Drupal, WordPress
