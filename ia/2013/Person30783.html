Darren LaMarr's Summary

A Creative Services Manager, my experience runs the range of job skills within the advertising, social media and video production & post production environments. I am that unusual combination of a creative that can write a script and manage client expectations while understanding a P&L statement and keeping a department on budget.

Executive Management
• Extensive experience collaborating with senior level management on strategic and long-range planning. Built tactical initiatives geared towards supporting corporate goals then successfully implemented them while increasing department and region revenue. Created, managed and balanced P&L statements, capital, expense and revenue budgets at a multi-state level. Implemented new video technologies to streamline and improve workflow.

Human Management
• Built internal creative teams including writers, producers, editors and designers to handle creative needs for businesses throughout Oregon and Washington. Extensive experience with managing supervisors & staff, conducting performance evaluations, implementing hiring practices and other direct management duties.

Social Media
• Professional experience with managing Twitter, Facebook Pages, Vimeo, and YouTube in a corporate environment.

Writing & Producing
• Experience in advertising, copywriting, & directing creative staff in advertising on air and online.

Editing & Animation
