Keith Ecker's Summary

With an equal passion for business strategy and creative content development, I am a unique blend of right brain and left brain. My background is as varied as my interests, from stand-up comedian to content strategist. Regardless of whatever hat I wear, I remain committed to crafting clear communications and effective stories that foster engagement and a sense of community around an organization's brand.

My entrepreneurial spirit has led me to innovate in everything I do. I'm one of the founders of Chicago's vibrant live lit scene, I have created and currently teach several creative and content-based classes at StoryStudio Chicago, and I am seen as a pioneer in the emerging area of content strategy in the legal industry.

Specialties: content strategy, content development, content marketing, media relations, public relations, project management, journalism, marketing, law, business, electronic discovery, humor, theater, literature, pubishing

Keith Ecker's Experience

Content Strategist

Jaffe PR

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry

September 2013 – Present (2 months) Chicago

Jaffe PR is a full-service legal marketing and public relations agency, devoted solely to the legal industry.

I serve as the agency's first Content Strategist. In this role, I work alongside our Managing Director of Creative Services, our Marketing Manager and our Digital Strategist to strategize and develop content across all Jaffe PR properties.

I also provide my services to law firms and legal vendors. My service offerings include content assessments, content marketing framework planning, editorial planning, tone and voice consultations, content development and copywriting across all media, social media consulting, and onsite and remote trainings. I also oversee Jaffe PR's staff of writers and ensure quality control across all client content marketing projects.

Finally, I serve as the lead salesperson for the Chicago area and regularly assist with drafting proposals and contracts on behalf of the agency.
