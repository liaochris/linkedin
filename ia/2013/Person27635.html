Craig Ward's Summary

My last roles have required both front and back-end development using a variety development frameworks such as Codeigniter, Wordpress, Smarty and various in-house content management systems. I am proficient in coding accessible, semantic HTML5 and CSS3 that degrades gracefully for the older, less capable browsers and able to bring designs alive with touches of jQuery.

I have experience of using both SVN and Git version control systems, but prefer Git. I have developed using the MAMP environment, but for the last 6 months have been using a dedicated Vagrant machine, provisioned by Chef scripts for each project to make sure the development environment is identical to the production environment. I use a Mac day to day and have done for the last 5 years but I am also more than comfortable working on Windows based machines.

I have a worked on a variety of projects from building simple Wordpress themes, content management systems for estate agents to developing large back-office systems for government projects. I enjoy meeting new clients, gathering website requirements, wireframing concepts and writing proposals to clearly articulate recommendations.

Specialties

HTML5, CSS3, PHP, MySql, Jquery, Codeigniter
