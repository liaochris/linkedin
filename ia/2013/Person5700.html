CEO & Founder

IBL Studios

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

March 2004 – Present (9 years 6 months) New York City

IBL Studios is a leading American and European video / film production, web technology and consultancy firm, headquartered in New York.

We offer today’s corporations and non-profit organizations:

- Cost-effective and high-quality video and film production
- Exclusively-designed web, video and social platforms
- World-class marketing consultancy and strategy

As proven professionals with over 15 years of experience in video content production, software development and marketing consultancy solutions, we are able to provide highly specialized and personalized services to meet your changing business needs in the new online and mobile world.

In today’s challenging environment we understand that companies and organizations don’t want business as usual; they want meaningfully different strategies. They call us when looking for powerful new stories and marketing ideas, innovative and consistent video solutions, and cutting-edge web technology.

At IBL Studios, we take pride in our people and service. Our clients are some of the most prestigious brands around. We are proud to serve such notables as the United Nations, Telefónica, IESE, Information Management Network (IMN), Search Marketing Expo, Lumen Institute, CAPP USA, Spain United States Chamber of Commerce, Club of Madrid, IFEMA, Real Academia Española (RAE), Cervantes Institute in New York, ICEX in Miami and New York, Germán Sánchez Ruipérez Foundation, LIBER Books International Trade Show, SGAE, Fundación Autor, Telefónica Foundation, Telefonica USA, Baker & McEnzie, University of Navarra, Red.es and El Corte Inglés, among others.

In order to provide the highest quality products and services, IBL Studios has assembled a very accomplished team. Our Board and staff consist of seasoned professionals holding years of relevant experience in video and film production, broadcast television, software development, graphic design and usability, marketing strategy and consultancy.
