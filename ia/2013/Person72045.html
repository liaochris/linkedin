Karen Cortell Reisman's Summary

Across North America and Europe, Karen Cortell Reisman’s corporate and association audiences have increased sales, enhanced productivity, and become better communicators.

Since 1992, Karen has taught corporate and association audiences how to communicate, sell, and thrive.

♦ "LETTERS FROM EINSTEIN - YOUR EQUATION FOR CHANGE"
Karen's keynote for associations & corporations

Karen puts on a one-woman show using letters her cousin, Albert Einstein, wrote to her family in an inspirational message about resilience.

She helps audiences learn how to:
 Use "Einstein-type" thinking to grow their business
 Use Karen's equation to ignite their creativity. It's easier to understand than E=mc2!
 Gain a competitive edge

♦ KAREN’S COMMUNICATION PROGRAMS & EXECUTIVE COACHING

Karen speaks about how to Speak For Yourself® so that others listen and trust you. Speak For Yourself® programs and coaching teach audiences to:
 Craft a compelling message
 Establish trust with buyers
 Boost profitability
 Not throw up during a million dollar presentation!

♦ KAREN’S BOOKS
Karen is the author of 2 books, and she has written many articles for national magazines.

♦ KAREN’S CLIENTS
Clients include: The American Heart Association, Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), Pizza Hut, ThyssenKrupp Elevator, Elk-GAF, Intuit, Sprint, and Deloitte Touche.

♦ ON A PERSONAL NOTE
Karen has one bachelor's degree, one master's degree, one cow named Bliss, one daughter, one son, and one husband.

Karen would have been her high school's Valedictorian if it weren't for a B in Home Economics due to lumpy blueberry muffins. But, she hasn't stopped speaking since.
