Adam Barratt's Summary

** Recently finished working with Pueblo Inglés, an amazing language immersion program for Spanish people and English speaking volunteers which is truly much "more than English"!

** I've also recently started an online community called 'Mediocrity To Mastery' which is designed to help people do just that; go from mediocre living to masterful living in whichever ways that may mean to them. It's a social network that has all the great features of existing networks that we know and love along with forums and more.

This is a place where like minded people can gather to get & give support and advice on their goals, ideas & projects in a positive, forward thinking and fun environment...to live the dream in 2013!

** I like to travel, write and blog. I will write about personal development and the experiences gained from travel and day-to-day life. Most is very much based around seeking and enjoying freedom. Although there are some strong tenets and ideals contained, I aim to write from a humorous, sometimes irreverent, standpoint as I believe we should find the comedy in everything and have a little fun in the process!

As well as articles, I have written 2 ebooks (so far). You can read examples of my work at my websites listed.

** I am currently available for freelance writing projects, along with looking for collaborators for the 'Mediocrity To Mastery' movement and will be doing so from south-east Asia for the first 3 months of 2013.
