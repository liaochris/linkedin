Hannah Boresow's Summary

I have an extensive background in graphic design, the production process, and effective communication. As a graphic designer for previous businesses, I have designed consistently creative and effective signs, posters, and business identity items. I can successfully multi-task different design jobs, as well as provide friendly and knowledgeable service to prospective clients. I have developed strong problem-solving skills through my work at Indiana University, where I have successfully created unique and skillful promotional items for various organizations and events on campus.
