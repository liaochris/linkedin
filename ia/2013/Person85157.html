Marco Barzaghi's Summary

I was born in 1976. I am information technology consultant since 1998. I was CMS VM/ESA developer, then Visual Basic and J2EE. Since 2001 I specialized in Microsoft .NET. I got experience on development of desktop application for backoffice, Client-Server, interoperability between windows and mainframe, and enterprise solutions.

I believe in community life and I have been Microsoft .NET MVP for the years 2004, 2005, 2006 and 2007. I am executive member of UGIdotNET (User Group Italiano .NET).

I am charmed by OOP programming. I have interests for development of distributed applications based on web or remote services.

Specialties: Software developer and architect (.NET / J2EE). My current focus is on .NET Framework, ASP.NET, MS SQL Server, SOA, RIA, ASP.NET MVC, WCF, and Silverlight
