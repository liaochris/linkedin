Lamont Chandler's Summary

LaMont Chandler, President and CEO of Chandler Financial Group, Inc., is a specialist in the field of financial estate and asset protection. LaMont and his team have been helping clients create and implement plans for achieving financial independence for over 15 years. Plans include strategies for potentially increasing income, preserving assets, and reduction of taxes and investment fees. LaMont is committed to excellence, integrity and personal attention to each and every client's needs from his personal involvement in the initial planning stages to annual reviews.

An Advisor and a Strategist, LaMont understands that clients want services designed for simplicity, convenience and effectiveness. With the "heart of a teacher," he carefully evaluates his clients' current financial position and works with them to develop and execute customized investment, financial, retirement and estate plans. The cornerstone of his success is the careful selection of products and services tailored to the individual client's needs. LaMont holds his series 7, 63, 65, and 24 licenses and is currently on the Advisory Board with Madison Avenue Securities.

Chandler Financial Group, Inc., is a full service financial planning firm that strives to help clients avoid mistakes which drain financial resources and savings. The firm’s goal is to take the mystery out of investing by managing risk, preparing you for retirement, and preserving your wealth. They do this by spending the time necessary to educate clients on the different strategies and approaches to investing and then formalize a detailed plan that matches their goals. The firm prides itself on uncommon client service, believing in helping clients use the institutional style of investing. The same style of investing that Harvard Endowment, Yale Endowment, and many state pension funds use.
