Jacob Berton's Summary

"It is prodigious the amount of good a man can do if he makes a business of it." - Benjamin Franklin

As a strategic social media marketer, Jake has nurtured online communities on a wide variety of projects ranging from a Mitsubishi’s launch of a new electric vehicle, Kia's sponsorship of the Vans Warped Tour, to helping grow a franchise restaurant concept from a single store to over 20 sold locations in less than three years. He has taught classes on new media marketing and has helped build new brands from the ground up.

He is currently leading the strategic and creative development for the Powerade/Coca Cola mobile app to be used at the 2012 London Olympic Athlete Village as well as games for the community education/promotions of a $2.6 billion dollar infrastructure investment for a utilities company in Chicago.

Berton has traveled to over 15 countries in Europe, the Middle East and South America over the past several years and his sense of adventure and passion for exploring new cultures translates directly into a thoughtfully aggressive and unique marketing style for clients to ensure these communities are interesting, vibrant and geared towards driving bottom-line results.

Berton is the Founder and CEO of Athens Social Media, an active member of the Board of Directors for Extra Special People and a founding member of UGA’s Terry Entrepreneurship Society. He is a recent graduate of the LEAD Athens program and was featured in the Athens Banner Herald’s 40 under 40.
