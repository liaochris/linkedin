Kristan Peters-Hamlin's Summary

All legal matters related to companies' greatest resource: their human resources. I have expertise in employment discrimination litigation and investigations, as well as advice and counsel. I conduct corporate investigations and handle litigation related to fraud, trade secrets, breaches of duty of loyalty, SOX, whistleblowing, wrongful discharge, theft of confidential information, harassment, discrimination, defamation, breaches of duty of loyalty, white collar criminal matters such as computer fraud, wire fraud and mail fraud. I negotiate severance and executive compensation packages for hedge fund and private equity managers, investment bankers and other executives.

Specialties

Litiigation, labor and employment, harassment, defamation, fraud investigations, executive compensation, severance negotiations, theft of trade secrets, theft of confidential information, breaches of duties of loyalty, computer fraud, mail fraud, wire fraud, white collar criminal matters.
