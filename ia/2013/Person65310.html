Joe Gannon's Summary

For twenty-five years, Mulberry Tree Press has offered a full array of coordinated book publishing services from concept through manufacture with a special focus on design, layout, prepress, and production management. Our clients range from major publishers to small independents, and we bring experience in adult and juvenile trade publishing, academic, and STM (tech/professional) work, as well as creation of catalogs and promotional materials.

Specialties: Book design, layout, and prepress for book interiors, covers, and jackets. Nonfiction research, writing and editing. Photo and illustration research. Book production and manufacturing management. And, general book business consulting.

Joe Gannon is also the author of 13 nonfiction books with combined sales of over 600,000 copies, and teaches courses in The Theory and Practice of Book Publishing, Book Design and Production, and Digital Publishing at Hofstra University in the English Department's undergraduate Publishing Studies program.
