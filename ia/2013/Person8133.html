Anna Bassham's Summary

I am an online communications professional with more than five years of combined community management, social media management and marketing communications experience. Since graduating from the University of Kansas School of Journalism in 2008 I have had the fortune of working for a number of companies in various roles, all centered around fostering an engaged community online.

My focus has been mainly representing e-commerce and technology brands in the small business sector via social media. I also have extensive experience reporting on and representing brands in the footwear industry.

What gets me most excited right now in terms of digital media is content curation; I am a regular user of Pinterest in both my professional and personal life. There is so much information readily available to people online, but it is not always easy to sift through and digest. Curation allows for easier organization of content, plus it is a fun way for brands to create sharable posts and connect with their audience in an authentic way.

I aim to facilitate relationships and communication online in an engaging way that drives business results. I enjoy networking with professionals from any industry on a global scale.

I am also an experienced blogger and web content writer, having started my own successful shoe blog back in 2008 and maintaining it into the present day. I also work as a contract writer covering shoe topics on Answers.com. I find the work challenging and rewarding, as I have learned to adhere to a rigid style guide while still keeping my articles interesting and informative.

Specialties: Pinterest, Twitter, Facebook, social media management, communications, branding, website editing, web content writing, social media marketing, blogging, community management, affiliate marketing, fashion, shoes
