Joy Day's Summary

Does your business struggle to differentiate itself from the competition?

I offer the perfect antidote to a corporate identity crisis! Specialising in original and memorable branding, Creative Joy guarantees designs to capture your customer’s imagination and convince them that you are their only option.

‘A picture is worth a thousand words’ and nowhere is this more apt than in the world of design and branding. Memorable branding and advertising that grabs your customer’s attention, will deliver your message more effectively. By avoiding the usual stereotypes of your industry, you will stand out from any perceived competitors and get your business noticed.

Having designed for many leading national brands during my agency career before launching Creative Joy in 2009, I now work within a more local market and use my big brand expertise when working with smaller, local businesses. Your business will benefit from professional high impact designs, that project a compelling image to your customers and ultimately win you business.

Working closely with a select team of specialist printers, web developers, copywriters and photographers, I create compelling, high impact solutions, - from logos to ad campaigns, brochures and websites. Working within tight deadlines, I deliver an honest and reliable service. You will benefit from the full creative service at a cost-effective rate, enabling you to focus on your business.

My attention to detail and customer care means that I take the time to get to know you and your company’s ethos, your products/services and, most importantly, your customers. My skills will communicate your specific USPs, your unique personality and brand image, so that your customers digest and remember your messages and are compelled to act on what they’ve seen.

To see how I’ve helped other businesses, please visit my website where you will find samples of my work and testimonials from existing clients.
