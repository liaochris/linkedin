Deborah Johnstone's Summary

I specialize in creating and managing digital content that connects artists, programs, and brands to current and future clients. This includes developing, blogs, websites, SEO, social media and e-mail newsletters. I spearheaded the creation of the E-mail Newsletter division at HarperCollins Publishers and managed development of web initiatives and e-mail deployment. While acting as E-Newsletter Manager, I devised channel marketing strategies that succeeded in growing the e-newsletter subscriber base from 5,000 to 450,000.

For artists, I develop show context - creating and implementing a cohesive vision and strategy for exhibitions.
