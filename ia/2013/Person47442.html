Guy Busk's Summary

Design career where I can utilize my skills as a designer and motiongraphics artist, while conributing to a team of peers and fellow designers.

When it comes to creativity, my strenghts are in conceptual work and brainstorming ideas that take into consideration demographics, target markets, advertising strategies, and shift in the economy. I look for ideas that have a direct impact on consumer buying trends. During my creation phase, I try to limit the number of elements in each piece, so as to create a more direct connection with the audience. With the tools and my skills, I can design and create motion pieces with energy and emotion.
