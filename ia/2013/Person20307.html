Carol Clemens' Summary

Winner of the 2002 San Diego Business Journal's 'Women Who Mean Business' Award. Carol broke gender boundaries in her job by climbing them - literally. She got her start in the phone company climbing telephone poles alongside the male trainees. She succeeded in a male dominated role and has gone on to provide over 30 years of telecommunications service and support. She has designed, installed, and maintained telecommunications systems in large companies throughout San Diego. Carol provides a special skill for listening and easy, open communications. She has worked on her own time for over ten years to raise guide dogs for the visually impaired and with the Delta Society for Animal Assistant Therapy. This work directly benefits people of all ages to cope with their special challenges.

Specialties

VoIP deployment. Cisco, Avaya, and ShoreTel system administration, Project Management, Documentation, Training and User Guides. Web, Audio and Video Conferencing
Consultative, carrier agnostic authorized agent for many of the carriers in the world wide market
