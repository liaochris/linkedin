Joe Maruschak's Summary

I am a Strategist and Entrepreneur, active with early stage startup and growth stage companies in Games, Health and Safety, Education, and Biotech. I take the strategic goals of the company, and align the team around achieving those goals.

I co-founded my first company, BraveTree, in 2001. At BraveTree, we developed multi-player games for the web and for the XBox. We also developed products that were a mixture of code and art assets that acted as tutorials for those experimenting with game development. During this time, I was also very active in the independent game development community, acting as an evangelist for independent games, blogging and speaking at independent game conferences.

We sold BraveTree to it's publisher, GarageGames in 2005. I was brought on to join the executive team at GarageGames and was tasked with forming a team focused on developing our own internal games and IP, and worked with the Engine Products team to extend the lines of our educational products.

I helped to grow that company until it was acquired by IAC in 2007. I stayed on after the merger to help manage the transition and explosive growth that followed, finally leaving in 2009 to pursue other interests. I subsequently acted as a consultant and adviser to early and growth stage companies.

Recently, I have been active in the Eugene, Oregon start-up community, organizing meetups and starting up an incubator/co-working space (Fertilab Thinkubator).
