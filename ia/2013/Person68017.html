Jonny Carroll's Summary

Jonny Carroll is a national award winning photographer based in Dallas, Texas. Hence, “DTX”. Jonny is best known for his aerial commercial and corporate work but considers himself a diverse photographer in all aspects of the art and is thrilled to take on new challenges.

At the age of 26, Jonny has worked on several assignments for many large clients, some of which include: National Geographic, ESPN, CBS, Discovery Channel, Toyota, UPS, T-mobile, Fox News, NBC, US Weekly, Target, HKS architechs, Texas A&M University, the University of Houston, and many others.

Jonny started Jdonut Photography, LLC his first year at the University of North Texas. While there, he studied Art, Geography, Graphic Design, Art Direction, Advertising, Photojournalism and of course, Photography. He now has a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of North Texas. At UNT he gained an in-depth knowledge of the commercial side of the art industry and the use of light to communicate a message.

All our staff is professional, hard working, creative and well trained. Jonny photographs a variety of generas. He's best known for his aerial commercial work, but has a lot of experience in editorial media, video, virtual tours, time lapses and even weddings. The variety keeps his career fresh and excited.

Specialties

aerial, commercial, editorial, head shots, corporate, creative portraits, geography, weddings, structural, etc
