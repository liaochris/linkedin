Dina Lima's Summary

I am passionate about helping people, and I am fortunate to have the privilege of connecting buyers and sellers.

With my strong service-oriented background in the construction of fine homes, I know that people want three things from anyone they work with when it comes to buying or selling their house:

1. INTEGRITY because people want to be treated with honesty,
2. SERVICE because people want to feel that their problems and needs are heard and are taken care of, and
3. EXCELLENCE because people enjoy a pleasant experience.

That is why INTEGRITY, SERVICE, and EXCELLENCE are the cornerstone of my business.

I have a background in the construction of luxury, high-performance homes in north central Texas and understand how to deliver exclusive service, high quality, and meticulous attention to detail. I was privileged to build one of the award-winning 2005 Greater Fort Worth Spring Tour of New Homes™ properties and the 2006 Savvy Idea House® for Building Savvy® Magazine.

My experience uniquely positions me to provide my clients with my professional opinion as well as advocate for their specific needs, both as sellers and as buyers.

In addition, my social media expertise and proficiency in English and Spanish are a huge asset for reaching today’s high-tech, diverse audience to help my clients sell or buy their home.

As part of the highly reputable Keller Williams Realty organization, which ranks as one of the top profitable companies in real estate, I stay abreast of the latest housing and technology trends to efficiently assist my clients with their real estate needs.

When you work with me, you’ll be sure to receive honest, Texas-friendly service… and at least a hug or two!

I would love the opportunity to work with you and welcome your consideration in allowing me to assist with all your real estate needs!
