Bisque, Beads & Beyond

Bisque, Beads & Beyond is a fired arts studio and full service bead shop featuring paint your own pottery, fused glass, clay and beading classes. A unique hybrid of the do-it-yourself and art class trends, the studio strives to provide a entertaining, encouraging creative environment for the everyday artist.
At @ 4000 square feet and catering to residents and visitors from ages 2 to 80, Bisque, Beads & Beyond! is a major anchor in Downtown Pittsfield.
