Austin Coulson's Summary

Web Design and Internet Marketing have been passions of mine for many years. While starting out by creating websites for myself, I learned many of the fundamentals and theory that constitute professional website development. Through networking and word-of-mouth, I soon began building and marketing websites for many different businesses and industries.

My first full-fledged business endeavor was realized when I became co-founder of House on the Moon (HotM). As a start-up design, development and marketing company, HotM discovered the pitfalls and the roads to success of a service-based business.

I have also managed marketing efforts for several companies in the technology industry. I have been involved with every aspect of several businesses which gave me a broad, yet deep understanding of different business processes and how to positively affect the bottom line. I managed all marketing efforts for Memory.net, a start-up online-based memory upgrade provider, headquartered in Dana Pointe, California. By utilizing several marketing channels, I significantly increased the revenue of Memory.net.

My latest venture and my own business is ACU Web. We are doing well and have created many long-lasting relationships, including many international clients from as far away as Australia. We've had great success within the automotive and medical industries but work with all types of businesses.

These combined experiences allow me to truly consult my clients in many respects. A website is one component of a successful strategy; I know that marketing a website and using it efficiently with other business efforts and processes is key to a successful strategy.

Specialties

website design and development, internet marketing, webmaster services and website maintenance, consulting
