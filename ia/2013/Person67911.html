Jon Henschen's Summary

My strong financial services background in varied environments allows me to understand the independent channel and broker dealer environment so that I can assist reps with their desire to find a better broker dealer match.

Since 1989 I have held a Series 7 license and worked as a registered representative in both the independent and wirehouse channels. In the '90s I spent several years wholesaling mutual funds and variable annuities, which is where I began to build my knowledge of the independent broker dealer channel.

In 1998 I recruited for a large insurance broker dealer and in 2001 I founded my own recruiting firm, Henschen & Associates. From the beginning, our goal has been to focus on the independent broker dealer channel and to work with a broad array of firms that met different needs. Today, we contract with over 90 Independent Broker Dealers, with the goal of helping representatives find the broker dealer that best meets their profile and needs.
