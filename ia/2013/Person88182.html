Mary Peters' Summary

Founder and President of Care For Life, Experts in Eldercare.

Care for Life provides Geriatric Care Management and homecare monitored by nurses for seniors in the Tri-County Metro Area of Charleston, South Carolina.

Geriatric Care Management is an interactive process with families that assures long term care expectations are met for aging adults who are recovering from surgery, those with chronic illnesses and others who need companionship and help with activities of daily living.

I graduated from South Carolina with a B.A., Psychology. I also have a Masters from the Medical College of Virginia in Occupational Therapy and a Post Masters from George Washington University in Health Sciences Administration.

I love family reunions in Garden City, taking bike trips around the world, and being dragged around to Civil War battle sites by my husband!
