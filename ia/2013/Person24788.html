Chuck Frey's Summary

BUSINESS DEVELOPER

CONTENT MARKETER

I specialize in creating compelling web content - both in words and visuals that command attention and compel action. I am a creative problem-solver who can transform the "straw" of raw information to the "gold" of clear, compelling content that gains the trust, understanding and support of key stakeholders.

SPECIALTIES

My specialties include business development, strategic planning, public relations, brand building, online marketing, brand building, social media, web development, content development, content management and visual thinking.

I have over 14 years experience in online marketing, and over 10 years experience in B2B public relations. I blend in-depth knowledge of web technologies with strategic business know-how.

Founder and publisher

In May 2012, the site became part of a joint venture with Sweden-based InnovationManagement.se. As of December 2012, the site's content has been merged with InnovationManagement.se to create the most comprehensive innovation portal in the world.
