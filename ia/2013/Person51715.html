  • California Legislative Guide

    • December 2011 to June 2012
    Team Members: Harlan West, Peter Hidalgo

    Creative development, design and print management for updated California Legislative Guide. The 2010 guide earned a Beacon Award from the Cable Industry of America. HWDS provided the art direction, graphic design and printing for this legislative report/corporate communication.


  • Metrolink 20th Anniversary Report

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Harlan West, Delana Gbenekama

    For Metrolink's 20th Anniversary, we created an interactive 54-page flip book that includes a video, an interactive table of contents and external links to other supporting websites. In addition to the web version, we also created an interactive CD-Rom, posters, pin, a Facebook page and a printed book that were sent to stakeholders as well as state and local government officials. HWDS provided the graphic design, print management, programming for this transportation project that involved a printed commemorative report, online publication, webpages and CD.


  • Kaiser Permanente Baldwin Park Reflections

    • January 2003 to Present
    Team Members: Harlan West, Maria Herrington Lengerke

    Ongoing monthly healthcare newsletter for the Kaiser Permanente Baldwin Park Medical Center. This is a high-end, 4-color corporate communication for physicians, management, and staff. HWDS provides the graphic design for this healthcare publication.

  • KP Voices for Woodland Hills

    • January 2003 to Present
    Team Members: Harlan West, Sherry Crosby

    Ongoing monthly healthcare newsletter for the Kaiser Permanente Woodland Hills Medical Center. This is a high-end, 4-color, 8-page corporate communication for physicians, management, and staff. This online publication is now an e-newsletter with interactive flip pages. HWDS provides the graphic design for this healthcare publication.

  • CommuteSmart News

    • January 2003 to Present
    Team Members: Harlan West, Jill Smolinski

    This is the longest running rideshare newsletter in the nation. Originally started as Crossroads in 1990, this newsletter became CommuteSmart News in 2003. This online e-newsletter is sent to 5,000 employers in Southern California to help promote commuter alternatives to driving alone. HWDS designed an e-blast teaser page that links back to web pages for more detailed information. This publication includes marketing tips to promote carpooling, vanpool, public transit and other innovative cost-saving commuter solutions. A calendar of events and a library of more than 100 previous issues are housed on the website. HWDS provides the graphic design and programming for this very user-friendly online transportation publication.
