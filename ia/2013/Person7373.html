Andy Carne's Experience

Founder

mupix

June 2013 – Present (3 months) United Kingdom

mupix is a great new platform for sharing music and pictures in one post.

Simply take a picture, add a clip of music, add a comment and you're ready to share a mupix moment - as simple as that!

Share mupix moments from your big day, holidays, festivals, gigs or parties, inspirations for your art or simply the music to your daily life.

Pictures can be taken with the in-built mupix camera or selected from your device, and the music comes from a pool of millions of official tracks, so you'll always be able to create the perfect music and picture mix.

The hub of mupix is the feed window, which is a great place to discover new music and revisit old favourites whilst enjoying great photography or art. Every post has an iTunes buy button, so you're only a couple of clicks away from owning the music. The feed is also where you can join the conversation around posts, you can 'love' the music, 'love' the picture or 'love' the combination, plus you can share mupix moments with friends in the app and via email, Twitter and Facebook.

Start bringing your pictures to life... a picture can tell a thousand words, but music gives it soul!
