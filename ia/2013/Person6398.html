Andrea Enright's Summary

CURRENTLY WORKING ON: Solutions book content, spokesperson videos and video scripts for Angstrom Graphics, personal essays on life, enrollment packet for Denver Waldorf School, case studies for Blazent, memorizing words to Rock Me Mama Like a Wagon Wheel so I can sing it to my three year old.

I HEART: Ted talks, yoga, noosa yogurt, my husband, glee, meditation in the backyard, Lighthouse writers group,

MY 411: Commited to both writing and relationship building for nearly 15 years, I've become a master at matching the right voice to the right gig. That's why I've transitioned my decade-long marketing communications business to be a sweet spot between a high priced agency and a hardcore recruiter. I don't manage projects, but I also don't take resumes off the street. Instead, I draw from a cache of personally recommended resources I've built over the years. If you need to communicate and you want to get real, tell a story or find your human voice, call me.

CLIENTS INCLUDE: Chipotle, Great-West Healthcare, Boston Market, Hunter Douglas, Planned Parenthood, Xcel Energy, Qwest, Dex, Gaiam, Bloomingdales, easyJet, EPA, Bloomingdales and many more.

I ALSO HAVE: 27 months experience as an international development professional with the Peace Corps in Sofia, Bulgaria; my yoga certification, a lot of blogging experience,

TRAVEL: I traveled independently (couchsurfing and hitchhiking) throughout the Middle East and Africa for eight months, volunteering at an olive farm through World Wide Organic Farming Opportunities, for a Palestinian-supporting NGO in Beirut for six weeks and at campsite in Uganda for three weeks, teaching computer classes to orphans. I have gone through multiple levels on the Shambhala path and I'm also a certified Vinyasa Yoga teacher.

Specialties

online content, pithy messaging, e-commerce initiatives, intercultural communication, creative writing, marketing tactic execution, message alignment, journalism, reproductive health knowledge, expatriate living, corporate social responsibility, voice narration, journalism, ethnotourism, blogging, volunteer management,
