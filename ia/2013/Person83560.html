Rachelle McCray's Summary

Luxe Entertainment is a full service video production company. At Luxe our specialty is delivering quality, well composed videos, that make a statement. Our professionals boast some heavy credentials and have the training, experience and education to back them up. The Luxe teams works with premium, upscale brands and clients, who are looking for experts in video production. We provide expertise in the areas of concept development, reality TV show filming and pitching, small business video marketing, corporate video production, and documentary filmmaking.

Luxe Entertainment opened its doors in 2008 as a “one woman band” under the name Shelley Lynn Productions, when owner Rachelle McCray decided she wanted to bring Hollywood quality production, to her home of Arizona. She knew that not only the quality needed to be superb and professional, but she wanted to work on projects that offered more than meets the eye. Uplifting and even inspiration, content that made you feel good. Whether it was an event that needed to be covered or a documentary film that could change the world, the Shelley Lynn Productions team lived up to the expectation and high standard offering their clients only the best. After 5 years in business the team quickly evolved into a well known and trusted name.

In 2011 after a television network showed interest in one of their projects, the Shelley Lynn Productions team realized it was time for an expansion, and Luxe Entertainment was born. Luxe has the same great film professionals on staff, the same dedication, and the same vision, but knew their name and brand needed to rival and match the things to come. Welcome to the NEW expansion and permanent change of Shelley Lynn Productions…we welcome you to Luxe Entertainment.
