Karan Cushman's Summary

A 20-year branding and marketing veteran, Karan is the visionary behind the creative team at Cushman Creative. As Brand Strategist, her role is to ensure that our work drives your business forward by igniting an emotional connection between your brand and its consumers.

Specialties

We grow brands that enrich our lives.

Brand Audits, Brand Development & Alignment, Websites, Email and Online Marketing, Corporate Communications, Collateral, Annual Reports, Advertising, Trade Show Exhibits, Event Planning, Environmental Design, Fundraising Campaigns, Packaging.

Our Quality of Life focus includes these industries: biotechnology, healthcare, non-profit, education, banking, and luxury industries such as yachting, spas, travel and fashion.

Karan Cushman's Experience

Founder & Brand Strategist

Cushman Creative

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Biotechnology industry

August 2007 – Present (6 years 3 months)

Cushman Creative is a cross-media branding agency that specializes in Quality of Life marketing. We help organizations that enrich our lives and our communities build brand loyalty.

From your brand identity to online presence, we develop marketing strategies that persuade, inform and inspire people to believe in your product, cause or organization.

We grow brands that enrich our lives.

Special Events Director

AIGA Maine

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Design industry

February 2006 – Present (7 years 9 months) Portland, ME

Karan is a founding board member of the Maine chapter of AIGA (the National Association for Design), and over the past seven years she has played a critical role in building the organization's financial stability. She first served as Development Director where she guided event fundraising and helped define a Sponsorship Program. Currently as Special Events Director, Karan continues to help direct the Chapter's mission through select events that require long-term planning, such as the Adobe Digital Publishing Conference and Abstract Maine.

Creative Director

Ariel Creative

April 1998 – June 2007 (9 years 3 months) Rockport, ME

Karan led the strategic vision and creative team at Ariel Creative, a design firm with a 25-year history most known for its biotechnology, healthcare and non-profit industry focus. Karan cultivated lasting relationships with companies like The Jackson Laboratory, FMC Bioproducts, Fisher Engineering and Make-A-Wish® Maine, to name a few. For nearly a decade, she continued to grow the firm's biotechnology focus and client roster. She received numerous awards including Black Book's Top 100 Annual Reports worldwide, the Rx Club, Healthcare Marketing Report and American Graphic Design Awards. As a career philantropist, she helped Maine's lobster industry gain critical state funding by designing the state’s lobster license plate – one of the first specialty plates in Maine.

Creative Director

The Ocean Society

November 1993 – April 2000 (6 years 6 months) Greater Atlanta Area

Continuing her passion for 'all things ocean', Karan served as Creative Director for The Ocean Society. There she helped raised awareness for the world's most endangered mammal, the North Atlantic Right Whale, by helping brand and launch an early education program to Southeastern schools near the whale's calving grounds. Karan also developed the organization's adoption and corporate sponsorship programs. The Ocean Society is a 501c3 organization whose purpose is to promote the conservation of our oceans through education and raising awareness. The Ocean Society also supports organizations like the New England Aquarium's Right Whale Research Team, in their quest to learn more about this critically endangered species.

Art Director

Holland & Holland Advertising

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

December 1996 – April 1998 (1 year 5 months) Birmingham, AL

Karan helped lead the creative team in branding and marketing Birmingham's top residential real estate developments such as Liberty Park and Greystone. A boutique advertising agency known for its intense research and spot-on creative, H&H became the go-to agency for high-end developments. Alongside Stephanie Holland (she-conomy.com), Karan helped launch Daniel Corporation's premier retirement community, Somerby. The agency received numerous awards for its real estate branding work, and for marketing programs in the healthcare and wellness industries.
