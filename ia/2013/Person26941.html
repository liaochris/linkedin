Corby Carlin Winters, M.A.'s Summary

I am confident that God wants all of us to experience Christ's True and Mighty Pure EmPowerment!

I am passionate about Speaking to women of all ages,Teaching,Writing & Coaching because it's a perfect way for me to help others discover God's purpose and best for them as well as help unleash the fullness of God's hope, grace, mercy and power in each of their lives!

I love empowering, equipping and encouraging others with life long skills, tools and biblical insights to help them fulfill their dreams, their passion and their purpose...

On corbywinters.com, I write inspirational blogs and books to change lives. I write Thankful Thursdays, Monday Muse and several years back Winters started calling me "The Little Black Dress" and our sons, "The SONs of Thunder." He wrote a book with that very title, and two others books so now not only do I speak but we speak together too. Now I write The Little Black Dress' Guide To A FabYOUlous and Healthy YOU and The Little Black Dress'~ Whimsy, Wit and Wisdom blogs about health and life.
