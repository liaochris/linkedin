Jeremy Newman, MPA, LEED-AP BD+C's Summary

Jeremy is currently consulting primarily in the energy, green building and sustainability space.

Prior to consulting, Jeremy was Director of Energy Operations and Finance at Redhorse Constructors. During his time at Redhorse he developed various financial models for renewable energy projects and high-end residential real estate developments. He also led a consulting team for a stealth mode solar start up to streamline and green their installation process substantially reducing installation costs.

Jeremy previously co-founded a renewable energy company for Virgin Group. Jeremy joined Virgin from TFS Energy where he served analyzing global carbon markets and government policy. Jeremy has consulted for numerous hedge and private equity funds on carbon markets, cleantech and greening operations for portfolio companies. He has consulted for the United States Army on a sustainable development plan for Southwest Baghdad. Jeremy has also consulted for a Commercial Airline on their Carbon Offset scheme.

Jeremy holds a MPA in Environmental Science and Policy with a specialization in Environmental Finance from Columbia University. Jeremy has attained the designation of Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional (LEED® AP) with the Building Design and Construction specialty from the United States Green Building Council.

Before he began his studies at Columbia University, Jeremy served in the United States Army’s 3d Armored Cavalry Regiment.

Specialties: Knowledge of renewable energy technologies; structured credit; securitzation; project finance; understanding of payments for ecosystem services; environmental finance; environmental economics;carbon neutrality; strategic planning; leadership; renewable portfolio standards; climate change legislation and international treaties/agreements; environmental policy analysis; carbon offsets in both compliance and voluntary markets; green supply chain; supply chain analysis
