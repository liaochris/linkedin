Christofer Nelson's Summary

Christofer helps leaders and institutions develop capacity for more effective collaboration and innovation. As a coach, facilitator and program designer, he develops and implements outcomes-driven solutions for government agencies, corporations, non-profits, start-ups and large societal change initiatives. Christofer is an expert in organizational strategy, program design, leadership development and event production.
