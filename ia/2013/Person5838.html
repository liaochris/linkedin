Amruth B R's Summary

I love solving intellectually challenging problems that have a social impact and getting others excited about these solutions. Have been around with this obsession long enough to win several awards & drive commercial adoption internationally.

Currently involved in building the product & business for Guru-G, the world's first gamified platform for teaching, teacher training & certification of teachers.

I routinely find myself writing short-stories, playing made-up tunes on my guitar or building crazy prototypes that usually involve some programming. I'm addicted to traveling and long, insightful conversations.

Once upon a time, I used to be a physicist... a very good one! But then I fell in love with behaviour research & neuroscience. Things have been pretty much downhill since then on the physics side.

Amruth B R's Experience

Founder, CEO

Vita Beans

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Think Tanks industry

December 2008 – Present (4 years 9 months)

Started Vita Beans in my final year of college. We started with behaviour simulation & ended up creating game based learning & assessment products that have been adopted by schools & teachers across 12 states in India and have close to 2 million plays. We have also built several products for some of the largest education & assessment companies in India and work internationally through partners in US, Mexico & Canada. Most recently, Vita Beans was selected as one of the 11 companies from close to 1000 companies across 100 countries to join Unreasonable at Sea, a global tech accelerator run by the Unreasonable Institute, Semester at Sea and co-taught by the founder of Stanford’s d.school.

Sauvé Scholar

McGill University

Educational Institution; 10,001+ employees; Higher Education industry

August 2010 – May 2011 (10 months)

One of the 14 fellows selected from around the world for the 2010-11 edition. Worked with ATLAS LAB (Advanced Technologies for Learning in Authentic Settings) at McGill and interacted with several top education research labs in Canada & US to advance the game based learning and assessment products built by Vita Beans. Also worked with several mentors & business leaders to design a tool for automatic conversion of text narration to animation/game, a dashboard for facilitating corporate training & a model for effective, low-cost delivery of education at large scale in developing markets.

President

Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership, BITS Pilani

Educational Institution; 11-50 employees; Think Tanks industry

October 2006 – November 2007 (1 year 2 months)

Led a bunch of 50 students in creating & implementing several entrepreneurial programs ranging from an international business challenge (Conquest) to a rural micro-entrepreneurship program that helped 21 women from a remote village called Garinda start a paper bags business. We are one of India’s top five entrepreneurship centers and the cofounders of National Entrepreneurship Network (NEN).

Joint Coordinator, Conquest - International Business Plan Competition
