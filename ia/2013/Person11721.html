Barbara Newman Mannix; A Dignified Life LLC's Summary

A Dignified Life LLC serves as a resource to advocate and effectively navigate more efficiently when looking for services associated with elder care and life transition. We vet and refer to service professionals in the areas of Alzheimer's Disease, assisted living, nursing home care, elder law, Medicare and Medicaid. We also work in the arenas of special needs, divorce and end of life. We alleviate emotional grid lock through communication, setting goals and finding the expert services to attain these goals. We are the bridge to peace of mind. Seeking to expand my practice by serving as personal advisor to physicians, discharge planners, social workers, geriatric care managers, lawyers and LTC insurance specialists.

Specialties

Elder Care, Downsizing/Relocation, Real Estate Specialists, Legal/Estate Planning, Financial Planning, Insurance Specialists, Benefits Specialists, Social Worker, Geriatric Care Managers, Special Needs for Children, Post Burial Planning, Additional Personal Assistance, Organize Personal Data.
