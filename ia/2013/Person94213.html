mike harkins' Summary

Story is everything.

I've observed and worked with a diverse range of clients and individuals, from the American Red Cross. to Gatorade, to Michael Jackson, and seen the best and worst communicators, and I know this: good storytellers make the world a better place.

By sharing stories in words and images, I'm hoping to make whatever positive impact I can, and by sharing what I know, I'm working to help other burgeoning storytellers do the same, whether their stories are about life, business, or play.

Specialties: Writing, editing, and producing. Expertise on the creative and production elements of successful media and communications projects; public speaking and public appearance training; video production and multi-platform media distribution; teaching new writers; author development; publicity and media relations.

mike harkins' Publications

  • Seven Years Later - An Essay on an Extraordinary Event with Lasting Implications

    • Scribd, re-posted by BoingBoing.net
    • August 17, 2011
    Authors: mike harkins

    Over 25,000 people have read this essay about one of the more volatile and continuing challenges to society. Writer Mike Harkins recounts events leading to the day eight years ago when a quadriplegic teenager from Northern California, with the assistance of his attorney and thousands of people around the country, attempted to purchase the assets of a gun manufacturer that had gone into bankruptcy to avoid paying the teen a record $24 million judgment. A documentary of the events, titled "The Lawyer, the Gun, and the Money," is in pre-production.

  • The Way to Communicate

    • book and ebook published by Portsmouth Publishing
    • October 14, 2010
    Authors: mike harkins

    The Way to Communicate teaches how to tap into your core and build upon your inherent sense of humanity, empathy, and self-awareness to become a better communicator. From this philosophical foundation, The Way to Communicate then provides the mental, physical, and technical tools that enable you to improve your personal communication skills in any person-to-person situation, from a one-on-one conversation to a presentation for a packed auditorium.
