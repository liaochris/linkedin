Holly Katko's Summary

Business Consulting and Business Training

Clients, whether they are presidents, CEO's, department heads, employees of corporations or entrepreneurs starting their own business, are given tools to succeed professionally or personally. This involves business consulting, training, and programs designed to facilitate those results.

Our programs take businesses to the next level. They consist of:

• Sales Training
• Customer Service
• Management Training
• Life Skills

Each Business Training Component is composed of individual training and consulting modules. They can be customized for you and your company to obtain optimum results.

Public Speaking

My 20 years of experience in public speaking provides the depth clients desire in presentations. This comes from my passion to help others; whether it's speaking to small groups, major corporations or keynotes. When developing programs a client needs, customization and research are my specialties in providing all my presentations.

Entrepreneurship

I also hold a passion for entrepreneurship. I started my company in 2000 and understand the critical steps in becoming an entrepreneur. My Entrepreneur You – One Day Workshop is designed to get entrepreneurs started and confident in those early decisions and steps, also, to develop the reasons and passion.

Insurance Consulting

My expertise in insurance consulting comes from the knowledge that business owners and entrepreneurs want to have peace of mind when growing or starting their business. It is a critical infrastructure component where errors are costly. From owning my own company, I see the advantage of having expert and up-to-date information in all insurance needs.
