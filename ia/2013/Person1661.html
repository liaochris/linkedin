Education Leader / Design Researcher specializing in the application of research on cognition and learning to the design and evaluation of academic programs. Proven ability to motivate exceptional achievement from interdisciplinary project teams using collaborative leadership and data-driven decision making. Expertise managing the integration of curriculum, instruction, technology, and assessment in formal and informal learning environments.
