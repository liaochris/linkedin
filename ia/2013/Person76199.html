Kj Lange's Summary

I spent 25 years loving the work in advertising and marketing in the Chicago market. As a business owner I was part creative guru, part numbers guru.

My best day is helping someone else get closer to their goals. Most of my adult life my work has been doing just that -helping others make their goals, business or personal.

I love seeing others succeed and grow. If nothing else, then if people who come in contact with me feel like I hear what they are saying and care for them, THAT makes me successful.

Specialties

I learned in the trenches of business and the world to become a strong negotiator, quick learner, great listener. I have marketing mind and love of sales because it is a blending people and business skills, helping in a successful real estate practice.
The first house I sold, my customers said in their customer service survey, "We love her". Even better when a client says, "You really know what you're doing."
I work and study hard to be able to help people in my real estate practice.
