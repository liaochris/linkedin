Jon Bailey's Summary

As a trust officer for U.S. Bank, I support more than 40 funds with a market value in excess of $4 billion. The role of Fund Custody Account Manager reports daily cash positions, ensures trade settlement, and assists in any audit or reconciliation matters. This high volume environment requires consistent, accurate, and timely reporting to many business lines and clients. By helping asset managers, back offices, and senior fund management negotiate the securities settlement process, I have become a subject matter expert in many aspects of the trust and mutual fund businesses. A strong customer focus allows me to deepen relationships with both internal and external clients while achieving shared goals. I have continued to develop personally and professionally by taking industry specific coursework and organizing volunteer opportunities.

Specialties: Developing personal networks.
Developing pricing for industrial resale markets.
Analyzing economic data.
Team building and training.
