Kenny Norton's Summary

Kenny Norton is the Digital Marketing Manager at theScore inc. The majority of his focus at theScore is to stay in tune with web, social and mobile and help drive growth. Kenny handles all social media efforts and marketing for theScore app. theScore is home to one of Canada's leading sports websites, some of the most engaged social communities and one of the world's leading sports apps.

Prior to theScore, Kenny worked closely with fortune 500 companies as an Account Manager at Sysomos, one of the leading social media monitoring solutions and helped develop the social listening strategies for some of the biggest brands in the world. Kenny has also been a Part Time Instructor at Humber College, teaching Social Media for PR Professionals.

Kenny is passionate in all things digital, especially: apps, disruptive start ups, gaming, fantasy sports, digital marketing and social media. Kenny also enjoys public speaking and has spoken at several conferences including: NXNE Interactive, Digital Media Summit and PodCamp Toronto. In his spare time he enjoys bantering about who's the better basketball player (on Twitter): LeBron James or Kobe Bryant.
