Karen Wright, MCC, CHC's Summary

I work with executives and top talent to leverage their strengths to achieve organizational goals and with talent management executives to build leadership capacity and evolve culture. I consult to organizations who want to manage their use of coaching as a development resource. My first book, "The Complete Executive: The 10-Step System for Great Leadership Performance" was released in Sept., 2012.

My firm, Parachute Executive Coaching, was one of the first coaching firms able to provide coaching on a national scale. Parachute offers programs including leader development, team effectiveness, executive wellness and retirement coaching.

I am a Master Certified Coach, the top designation granted by the International Coach Federation, and I was the first coach to be certified in Canada. I continue to contribute to the development of the profession by mentoring and educating new coaches. Prior to becoming a professional coach I worked in Canada and the US for both publicly traded and privately held Fortune 500 companies so I have had direct experience of the leadership challenges that face many of my clients.

I received my MBA from the Ivey School of Business, and my BA in Economics from Western University. I completed coach training from two leading accredited schools and have taught for both schools. I am trained in numerous assessment instruments including Birkman and Winslow, and I worked with Dr. Martin Seligman in the first cohort of coaches trained in his positive psychology-based coaching program. I have worked extensively with numerous 360 feedback survey tools and qualitative insight processes. I am also a Certified Health Coach from the Institute of Integrated Nutrition.

I am a nationally published columnist, a frequent contributor to various media and a sought after keynote speaker. I have served on multiple boards including The International Coach Federation, Bridgepoint Health, Earth Day Canada and Sick Kids Hospital's Corporate Advisory Board.

Karen Wright, MCC, CHC's Publications

  • The Complete Executive - The 10-Step System for Great Leadership Performance

    • Bibliomotion
    • September 25, 2012
    Authors: Karen Wright, MCC, CHC

    Being a leader requires much more than just understanding how to run a business. Today's executive is under a unique set of demands, coming from multiple stakeholders. In The Complete Executive, long-time executive coach Karen Wright introduces a unique 10-step system that takes all of these aspects of leadership development into account. Leaders not only need incredible physical energy and stamina, they need a support system, philosophy, plan, business acumen, a social network, and a brand and reputation that will ensure they achieve their vision. Backed by an assessment tool, giving each leader a personal report card, this book provides aspiring leaders with a framework to address all aspects of leadership that translates to peak performance. Wright knows from experience that even when everything is going well for leaders, high achievers don't stop there, which is why The Complete Executive is designed as a resource to return to again and again for continuous improvement.
