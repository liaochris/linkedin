Linda Baldwin, CPCE's Summary

Linda Baldwin is a Certified Health Coach and loving mother, wife and grandmother. For years she has been a practicing chef, feeding clients, guests and family with her creations. She decided to become a health coach so she could use her passion for food and wellness by working children, adults, seniors and those with health concerns, of all types to improve their health and family life.
Linda received her training at Integrative Nutrition in New York City. She is certified by the American Association of Drugless Practitioners and has obtained Continuing Education Units from Purchase College, State University of New York.

Linda writes for several publications and has a blog www.intelligentgourmet.wordpress.com,which is dedicated to sharing and teaching nutritional facts as well as recipes. Linda leads workshops on nutrition and offers individual health and nutrition coaching to facilities as well as families families.

Linda Baldwin, Believes that everyone should love what they eat all of the time. " I believe food is not just fuel, and that eating healthy can be a wonderful and fabulous adventure. This is why I create gourmet meals that are ultra healthy and taste terrific , Hello friends it is time to FALL IN LOVE WITH YOUR FOOD AGAIN. " Linda works with a group of Diet & Nutrition Experts and she is a Professional Catering Executive (CPCE) whose skills have been honed by 25 years in the catering, restaurant, and event production industry. She is know as local foodie, ultra healthy prepared foods specialist, educator - columnist and creator of Intelligent Gourmet a lifestyle company, based in South Tampa since 2011. She leads a team of professionals that include chefs, and educators in the field of health and wellness.
