Gabriel Rotman's Summary

Since age 12, I created 2 Internet businesses and sold both by the age of 22. Since then I've been a business development and IT consultant for various companies. Worked at Citrix Systems for a little under a year. Now I'm on my 3rd startup called EquityLancer, which is a global equity marketplace that enables startups to use their equity as a currency, instead of cash, to obtain resources such as talent, products, services, or funds. Imagine coming to a website and being able to offer equity in exchange for legal services, an office for your business, machines for manufacturing, PR services for marketing, or programmers to develop your product. All of this, and more, is possible in our equity marketplace.
