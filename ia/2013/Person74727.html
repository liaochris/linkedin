Kevin F. Adler's Summary

I'm an entrepreneur and sociologist. I believe that we are all in this together as brothers and sisters; as such, I aspire to help people connect, realize their common humanity, and change the world together.

I am the founder and CEO of inthis, an angel-backed startup based in SF that connects people around their shared experiences, beginning with the Potlucks app. I have founded and led over 20 ventures, mostly in tech, social impact, and education, as well as the occasional basketball team in England. Previously, I led alumn.us and BetterGrads, and served as a Rotary Goodwill Ambassador in Mexico.

My first book, Catalyst, will be published by UPA in 2014. I studied social capital at Cambridge, Occidental, and UCL. I am an active startup advisor, writer, actor, and adventure runner.

website: kevinfadler.com/about
contact: @kevinfadler or kevin [at] inthis.com
