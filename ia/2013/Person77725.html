Kyle Favors' Summary

More than 20 Years of experience in the instrumentation and maintenance of thermal equipment. Past Experience includes Control Systems Analyst and Maintenance Manager for a Large Commercial Heat Treat in the southeastern United States, and Field Service Engineer for a (at the time) large instrumentation company that specialize in the heat treating industry. In 1998, Bruce Favors and I started Heat Treating Services Unlimited, Inc. (HTSU) in Simpsonville, SC, to specialize in the thermal processing industry. HTSU offers complete services for the industry from Installations to ISO:IEC 17025:2005 calibration services. In June 2002, I started CF Thermal, Inc. to be a premier Technical Sales Rep Firm to promote the leaders in the thermal processing companies in the world to my customers throughout the southeastern United States.

Specialties: Instrumentation, Furnace Atmosphere, Equipment Troubleshooting
