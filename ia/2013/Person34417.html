1. We produce written learning guides

Head Coach

San Ramon Soccer Club

Nonprofit Organization Management industry

2000 – March 2011 (11 years) San Ramon, Ca

My name is Derek Fox, I am a professional soccer coach for the San Ramon Soccer Club. I started coaching soccer in 2005 but that was not the beginning of my soccer career. When I was about 7 years old I played with all my heart and soul for the San Ramon Soccer Club, Scorpions & Hellcats; WOW that was a long time ago. The funny thing is that I am still in contact with about 50% of my soccer team from back in those days. I can say that the one thing that I have learned from the game of soccer is that your teammates are friends for life.

I remember back in the day when we only had parent coaches and two fields for the whole league to play on. We had reversible jerseys, all grass fields, and $10 shoes. The game has come a long way since then and for the better. All weather fields, paid professional coaches and people that truly care for the health of the game.

The sport is finally getting the recognition that is always deserved in the United States. It is the number one sport in the world and I am glad to see that the passion for the game is hitting the San Ramon Valley. I am honored to be apart of all the next generation of soccer players and creating those memories that will last a lifetime.

I have been involved in soccer for over 23 years as a player and now as a coach. Being a coach is one of the most rewarding things I have ever done in my life. The excitement of game day, the hard times, the great times, they all make up this wonderful game.

It has been such a pleasure to give back to the city of San Ramon and I look forward to another 25 years as a coach. So many memories are still to come and I look forward to the wins, the loses and the ties because in the end we all walk away winners.
