Lisa Stacey's Summary

Professional experienced Web Designer, Developer and Computer Training Specialist based in Gorey, Co. Wexford. With over 20 years experience working with computers and technology I love what I do and am passionate about technology and love helping businesses build their online presence by building quality Search Engine Optimised websites, Content Management Systems, Blogs, Ecommerce and custom build sites as well as assisting with their online marketing strategy. I am dedicated to customer satisfaction and providing a professional quality service and building lasting relationships with my clients.
Currently using technologies such as XHTML, CSS, JS, JQuery, AJAX, EXT JS, PHP, Mysql and Flash, I custom develop sites from the ground up as well customising Open Source web software such as Joomla, Wordpress,Moodle, OScommerce, Zen Cart, Open Cart, Magento etc. I also work with clients on SEO, Online Marketing and Social Marketing. I have a strong Server and Computer Systems Support background in both Windows and Linux systems currently using Linux Servers for my Web Development and Hosting.
Also work as a IT trainer teaching custom designed courses, Fetac awards and one to one tuition.
I am customer focused and passionate about my work and love that there is always so much more to learn in this industry.
