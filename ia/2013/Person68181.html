Joost van Gorsel's Summary

Joost van Gorsel is the creative director of MetroDC, a cutting-edge web agency based in the heart of Rotterdam, the Netherlands.

Founder of the award-winning online publication Iconique.com.

Co-founder of the succesful stock sample sale Mode Marché.

Joost van Gorsel (1980) is the creative and corporate mind behind Iconique. Although his formal education is in business, he developed a passion for design during the course of his studies. Van Gorsel founded Iconique Fashion Magazine in the year 2000 and has worked on numerous projects as an art director for a growing international clientele. Iconique.com has won several web awards, appeared in books and has been published in numerous magazines. The best of Iconique (Excess: In here life is beautiful) was also shown in the Boijmans Museum in the year 2005 during ‘Project Rotterdam’. Iconique was chosen as one of the top 10 fashion magazines by ‘Business to Fashion’ in 2008.

Van Gorsel won the Dutch L’Oréal Marketing Awards in 2003 representing Erasmus University of Rotterdam and was awarded the ‘Best Overall Business Plan Award’ in 2006 by the Development Cooperation City of Rotterdam. The creative director has a Master of Science degree in Business Administration from the Erasmus University of Rotterdam, the Netherlands. His master thesis was written on the subject of ‘The democratization of luxury goods in the fashion industry’.

The supershopping event 'Mode Marché' which he co-founded with Esmé de Smit has grown into one of the leading public fashion events of the Netherlands.

Although his background is in the fashion and luxury industry, he has a wide experience working for a diverse range of companies and industries creating campaigns and marketing strategies at advertising agency 0to9 and web agency MetroDC.
