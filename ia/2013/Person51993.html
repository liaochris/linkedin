Michael Angelo Leone's Projects

  • Maui Brewery Hawaii

    • May 2011 to August 2011
    Team Members: Michael Angelo Leone, Green Global Communities, Lyman Morikawa, Solar MAX, R&R Solar, Interisland Solar, Mr Supply, Alpha Electric, Tri Isle Trucking & Storage

    GGC installs 100 kW Solar PV system for Maui Brewery in Lahaina Hawaii. 476 solar panels at 245 watts power each panel was installed in the metal roof of their warehouse beer factory. A 100 kW power Satcon solar inverter was installed inside the warehouse to produce 180,000 kW/h of green energy per year! The project was funded by a Power Purchase Agreement "PPA" the owner of Maui Brewery paid no installation costs and will buy power from the system over a 20 year agreement at a reduced rate well below the utility rates! The PPA is a great tool for funding solar projects and GGC has developed several groups of tax credit investors in Hawaii who fund solar projects and leverage their tax liabilities to reduce the cost of installation solar systems. PPA funding is great for non profits or for a company that uses a lot of energy but does not have the tax appetite to afford a new large scale solar system. Let Green Global Comunities design and fund a solar PV project for your home or buisnes and we will provide you with a quality installation and great costomer service over the life of the system.
