Kirsten Wright's Summary

Kirsten Wright is the Social Media & PR Manager for MagnaFlow, where she focuses on driving their strategy through the digital space. In addition, she oversees all events and celebrity sponsorships. She is truly passionate about social media, and provides the intelligence for MagnaFlow's social actions, assuring that every tweet, status update, dialogue, image and video asset is ideally created and placed for their market. She is the power that keeps a MagnaFlow's social presence dynamic and positive for both the brand and their customers. She is a graduate of California State University Long Beach with a degree in Rhetorical Communications, and is a frequent speaker national conferences. In her personal time, she watches old movies with her husband and enjoys taking her greyhound, Badger for long walks.

Specialties: Social Media Marketing & Management - including strategy development, community management, campaign development and analytics reporting. Training and Speaking – including large groups, national conferences and collegiate venues.
