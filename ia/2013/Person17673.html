Brian Platte, Certified Senior Advisor's Experience

Advocate

Amerivets

2011 – Present (2 years)

Amerivets is the premier provider of education for veterans and their families about the VA Aid and Attendance benefit. This benefit can provide up to $1949 per month TAX FREE for wartime veterans to pay for home healthcare, assisted living, nursing home, or memory care. These benefits can be provided regardless of the veteran's financial situation.

It is important to note that veterans did not need to see combat in order to qualify for this benefit. The stipulations for eligibility are that the veteran serve as least one day during a period of war with a total of 90 days of active duty, that they receive any discharge other than dishonorable, and that they or their spouse now require the aid or assistance of another individual with activities of daily living. Surviving spouses of wartime veterans may also qualify for the benefit.

Amerivets not only considers the qualification planning for the VA Aid and Attendance, but also the effects such planning might have on other programs such as Medicaid. Our advocates are well trained, knowledgeable about VA and Medicaid benefits, work closely with accredited VA attorneys and local elder law attorneys, and consult other experts such as CPA's to consider tax and estate planning implications.

If you know a WWII, Korean, or Vietnam era veteran who is needing assistance to get to doctor appointments, prepare meals, upkeep their home, or who is looking at a senior community that provides care, let us help them qualify for this benefit.
