Jeremy Neuharth's Summary

I am passionate about implementing technology solutions for banks that deliver results.

In 2010 I founded Sycorr, an organization fixated on creating exceptional software for the financial services industry. Within a year we released our first product Sycorr Mobile, a mobile banking platform. In addition to our product, we offer bank focused services including: custom software development, project management, technology consulting, social media, and online marketing.

Previous to founding Sycorr, I lead various technology projects in the financial sector. These projects include multiple mobile banking initiatives, building a real-time alerting engine, the redesign of various public websites, creating a federated identity (SSO) solution, implementing a multi-factor authentication product, many system integrations, and working with marketing teams on a full social media and online marketing campaigns.

An experienced project manager, I hold multiple industry certifications and take pride in coaching teams to deliver value. As an individual I like to work in areas that consist of new challenges requiring creative problem solving opportunities on a regular basis. I like taking on large responsibilities and naturally enjoy leadership.
