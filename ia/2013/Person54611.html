Will Johnson's Summary

While working as Lighting Designer and Master Electrician for several high schools and colleges, I've also worked for the nationally known Music Theatre of Wichita (SUNSET BLVD, DISNEY'S THE LITTLE MERMAID, XANADU), Music Theatre for Young People (Resident Lighting Designer), Wichita Community Theatre (RUMORS, THE 39 STEPS), and the new Crown Uptown Dinner Theatre, presenting such regional premiers as NEXT TO NORMAL and SWEENEY TODD.

I've been awarded a Mary Jane Teall award in Lighting Design for THE 39 STEPS and a Wichita State University George Award for ASSASSINS, I've also been honored with nominations for the Music Theatre of Wichita: Jester Awards in Lighting Design for ALL SHOOK UP (2007) and WEST SIDE STORY (2009) and the Wichita State University George Awards for THE FATHER (2010).

Specialties

Theatrical & Concert Lighting, Light Board Programmer, Utilizing Current Equipment, qLab, Photoshop
