Kim Stone's Summary

Throughout my childhood, my parents dabbled in the real estate field. As a result, my interest in the real estate grew as, overtime, I worked on a career of my own. My love of decorating and interior design only continued to fuel my interest in the real estate world. With 12 years of sales and marketing experience under my belt, I decided to make the change to a full time REALTOR in 2005. It proved to be a good fit as I soared to Rookie of the Year for all of SW Virginia in 2006, my first full year in business. I have continued to be a top 10 performer in the SW Virginia Market throughout my career. My success has only furthered my commitment to helping people with real estate, whether it is a first home, long considered dream home, an investment property, or something in between. I have made it my priority to help people navigate through the emotional and sometimes complicated process of buying and /or selling a home. I thank all of my past and future clients for the privilege of working for them and sharing in their future!

My goal is to represent my clients professionally and with integrity each step of the way. I put my clients’ interests in front of my own in order to help them achieve their real estate goals.
