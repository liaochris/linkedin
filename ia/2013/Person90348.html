Michael Canavan's Summary

Michael Canavan is a design veteran with over 22 years of professional experience. He has designed product for Pepsi, Woodstock '94, Disney, Warner Bros., Hit Entertainment, NFL, NBA, NHL, MLB, Wilson, Everlast, Starter, Bum Equipment, Bill Blass, and Oleg Cassini. In addition to the intensive graphic design and product development needed for these globally recognized brands, Michael also has extensive experience in brand identity, logo design, and packaging design.

Michael is presently the Director of Design and Marketing for 24-7 International, an industry leading Travel Goods Company, based in Pine Brook, New Jersey. Their branded and private label luggage, casual travel bags, and accessories can be found at most leading retailers. The company product lines feature such notable and prestigious brands as Nicole Miller, Lucas, Oleg Cassini, Bill Blass, Kathy Van Zeeland, Gloria Vanderbilt, Caribbean Joe, and Ciao.

Michael manages branded casual luggage product development for specific in-house brands and retail accounts, all product photography and retouching, company packaging program (which includes hang tags, panel cards, color boxes, and in-store signage), marketing materials and strategies, including print ads, e-commerce support, retail presentations, and trade show signage/graphics.

Michael also serves as Creative Director for Achieve Athletics, an innovative athletic apparel company representing a brand united behind principles of pride, determination, commitment, and sacrifice.

Achieve Athletics currently is one of the main outfitters and sponsors within the SCC (Southern Connecticut Conference) of high school teams. The company’s goal is to promote the high school athlete, through support of their endeavors on and off the field, and to be the primary brand and apparel supplier to high schools around the country.


Michael is also the President and Founder of PlanetCanavanDesign, an independent design business in Somers, New York.
