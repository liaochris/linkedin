Bill Youngdahl's Summary

Bill recently cofounded 14falcons to develop a collaborative web application called Teamput. It is the most straightforward approach available for collecting your thoughts and collaborating with remote team members during meetings, or any time!
See more at: http://14falcons.com
Try Teamput at: http://ww.teamput.com

Bill also serves as an Associate Professor of Project and Operations Leadership at the Thunderbird School of Global Management.

He has designed and delivered custom executive education programs or sessions for ExxonMobil, BP, Kuwait National Petroleum Company, Novartis, Eli Lilly, American Express, Textron, The Home Depot, Mattel, General Motors, Delphi, Eli Lilly, Goodyear, SK, LG, Acer, Discount Tire, RoadSafe Traffic Systems, and Phelps Dodge.

Specialties: Oil & Gas, Pharmaceuticals, Leadership, Project Leadership/Management, Diversity & Inclusion, Executive Education

Bill Youngdahl's Publications

  • Leading Global Projects - For Professional and Accidental Project Leaders

    • Butterworth-Heinemann (Elsevier)
    • 2008
    Authors: Bill Youngdahl, Robert Moran

    Authors Moran and Youngdahl bring extensive experience and learning from industry practice to present a clear and straightforward treatment of the leadership skills and knowledge required to lead projects that are global in nature. They have written the first book of its kind to address the three essential skills of global project leaders - strategic project management, project leadership, and cross-cultural leadership.
