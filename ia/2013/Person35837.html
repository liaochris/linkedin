President

Trade Management Consulting, Inc.

1998 – Present (15 years)

TMC is a corporate and commercial barter consulting company. Innovative solutions are our specialty. We assist large corporations to facilitate the use of excess inventory or surplus capacity to finance purchases with a lower outlay of cash. We also consult and train other barter organizations to utilize better industry skill sets.

The corporate barter environment is more of a one-on-one transaction and we are generally working with a CFO. Excess or obsolete inventory or capacity is converted into trade credits that are then spent in concert with some blend of cash to acquire predefined expense items on a corporation’s P&L. The goal of these transactions are to reduce or eliminate write-downs or write-offs.
