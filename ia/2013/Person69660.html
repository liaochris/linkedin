Jim Pietrangelo's Summary

I specialize in creating standards compliant, hand-coded, elegantly structured websites using whatever technologies are required to successfully complete the project. Whether you are looking for a website for your small business or you are a graphic designer looking for someone to code your design, I can execute your project in a timely manner while demonstrating a well-honed sense of design and a firm understanding of the underlying technologies that make it all work.

I work alone or with your team. I work from ideas drawn on napkins, menus, chalkboards, or layered graphics files. After all, the quality of an idea is not dependent upon its presentation. The idea must speak for itself. My job is to help promote your ideas using the web.

When you have something to say, I will work with you to make sure that every word pulls its weight. With attention spans being as short as they are, words are a valuable resource and can not be wasted.

My portfolio is on the web at:
http://www.webcampone.com/portfolio_websites.php

Specialties and Expertise: These days I specialize in website design and development using a variety of front and back-end technologies. Over the course of my life, I have demonstrated expertise not only in web design and development, but in leadership activities, B2B Sales, strategic planning, public speaking, small business formation, finance, and negotiation.
