Jean Herman's Summary

Jean Herman is primarily a fiber artist using fabric, stitching, paint and other techniques to create art quilts that often read as paintings. She likes to say, "I paint with fabric." Jean also uses various print making techniques incorporating them into her fiber pieces. Her quilts can resemble collages, incorporating paper, photo transfers, and monoprints to create a visual image.

Jean likes working in a series; developing pieces that build on each other. Whether it's an individual piece or a series, spontaneous or deliberate her goal is the same, to create an emotional response.

Jean was trained as an scientist and went unto a career as a business executive and CEO of the Denver/Boulder BBB. She retired from business to pursue art fulltime in 2009.
