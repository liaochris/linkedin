Print Remedy Ltd

October 2007 – December 2009 (2 years 3 months)

Sales Remedy evolved from Print Remedy Ltd a business I registered in October 2007. The company was created to take on the print requirements of my clients following the liquidation of Ingersoll when taken over by the new owners. Having forged an alliance with a local printer the concept was to be client facing and business developing and pass the production to my supplier. This was a successful proposition and I was very aware that the market was changing and this business model was not a sustainable long term revenue stream.

I created Sales Remedy as a trading name in late 2008 and started to focus more on the sales support that I could offer other businesses. The outcome was that Print Remedy's offering was downscaled and I focused on the new opportunity of sales and marketing for small business.
