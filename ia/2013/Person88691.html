Matteo Garcia's Summary

I am Matteo Garcia, MBA Candidate at the University of Wisconsin – Madison, Wisconsin School of Business, specializing in Brand and Product Management. I am a recipient of The Consortium for Graduate Study in Management fellowship. I am pursuing my MBA degree to further improve my knowledge of marketing and to give myself a clear career path that I am passionate about. I chose Wisconsin for the specialization and specifically Brand and Product Management to direct myself in a brand management career path. This path will utilize my leadership and marketing experience and allow me to further develop my already strong innovation and communication skills as I work with my peers and professors throughout my MBA career.

I have experience in marketing and business development with a regional IT reseller, SynerComm, working with cross-functional teams and executives to create brand messaging and marketing campaigns. My time with SynerComm has allowed me to learn marketing and gain real-world experience in the industry with the flexibility to be innovative in my projects.

Before I worked with SynerComm, I previously held two leadership roles with Best Buy. I lead a team of up to twenty 25 multi-department employees resulting in $15M in sales. I also designed an employee development program to improve sales proficiency and employee morale – improving the store teamwork and efficiency. I gained valuable business experience with Best Buy, learning to understand and analyze P&L reports and creating business plans to control the daily operations of my store.
