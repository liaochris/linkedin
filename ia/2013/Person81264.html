Tiffany Tate's Summary

Tiffany is a fashion media professional with a broad background in new media and fashion journalism, whose fashion-focused articles are known to go into syndication. Tiffany has taken her love for fashion to the next level by creating Linger Magazine in May 2010 and adding her illustrious vision.

Since its launch, Linger Magazine continues to gain industry validation by garnering several international media partnerships with highly profiled fashion entities; such as Global Fashion Brands, Vancouver Fashion Week, Fashion Industry Association, The Fashion Model Directory, and Fashion Industry Network. In August 2013, PR Couture recognized Linger Magazine as one of the "8 Digital Fashion Magazines You Should Know" as part of their "How To Pitch" series: http://bit.ly/15d1Kcp

Tiffany's profound dedication to supporting new designer's entry into the fashion industry is so appreciated that Linger Magazine has been tapped as the official title sponsor of Baltimore Fashion Week's 'Emerging Designer Competition' that launched in 2012.

Most notably, Tiffany has been appointed as a judge for the prestigious FOLIO:Awards "Eddies and Ozzies" in 2012 and 2013, where she utilizes her skills and experience to judge numerous magazines on their editorial design and layout.

Linger Magazine has had the pleasure of featuring some of today's most stylish fashionistas: Gretchen Rossi of Bravo TV's "The Real Housewives of Orange County", Keenya Hill of "America's Next Top Model", international Maxim model Carissa Rosario, and Nicole Richie!

Expertise in business management, marketing in print and online mediums in the publishing industry, Ms. Tate has a proven track record of producing compelling feature editorials, strengthening marketing program development and client services.

Tiffany is practical, articulate and creative with a proven ability to consistently obtain high performance through research and cohesive team building efforts in order to achieve targeted goals.
