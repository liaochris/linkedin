  • CBRE Custom Cost Segregation System

    • December 2006 to January 2009
    Team Members: Emmanuel Garcia

    Cost Segregation is a legal method to generate greater tax savings than you realize were possible, using tested engineering analysis and accounting principles, whether you're buying real estate, fixing, retrofiting or updating an old building or constructing a brand new one.

    Using AutoCAD Architecture 2008, custom LISP and .NET programming, Excel Spredsheets and Access Databases, I created a system that automates most tedious tasks, reduces human errors and helps create reports in both graphical and text forms designed to withstand the harshest of IRS audits.

    I designed a system able to handle most types of projects and occupancy types, from sites requiring manual as-built measurements, scanning and vectorizing paper drawings to using BIM construction documents and digital financial data.
