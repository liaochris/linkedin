Jenae Spry's Summary

Jenae Spry is goal-oriented and results-driven with over 7 years experience in translation, localization, and project management. Jenae has been repeatedly recognized by clients for her ability to multitask and work effectively within tight deadlines while maintaining quality. Her knowledge of a variety of life sciences and legal materials and familiarity with cross-cultural difficulties and communication enable her to bring a unique perspective to her work.


Specialties: Clinical Trials, Pharmaceuticals, Medical, Medical Imaging, Service Agreements, Contracts, and other Life Sciences and Legal materials.
