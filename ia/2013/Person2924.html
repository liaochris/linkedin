Akisia Grigsby's Summary

Born from a Dominican mother and American father, Akisia Grigsby is a natural-born leader whose mission in life is to motivate and inspire others. Ms. Grigsby is the founder and CEO of Akzas/ Atomic Designs Inc, a multicultural brand-imaging agency and visual communications design studio founded in 2000 to deliver creative concepts, branding ideas, creative direction and design services to companies in the fashion, beauty, entertainment, sports, business/non-profit, financial, medical, technology and political campaigns. Ms. Grigsby has a proven 19 year tract record, serving as creative director of Sean John clothing and Bad Boy Entertainment, amongst others. Due to her extensive and numerous multimillion-dollar campaigns in the urban and Hispanic markets, she has been able to successfully access the psychology of trends and the mind of the hispanic and urban communities.

Ms. Grigsby, an alumni of Parsons School of Design, is a philosopher at heart, an artist by trade and entrepreneur by spirit. While being the product of Jersey City’s Public School System, a JC homeowner and landlord and a successful JC business owner in the most competitive areas, she enjoys her true passions: painting, drawing, designing and helping out the children of her community. While being a single mother of a successfully and nationally recognized 8th grade-student athlete, she has managed to play an important role as a leading Parent Advocate and Community Leader in JC. Some of her involvement includes being Secretary of Ps#11’s Parent Council, founder of Facebook’s Parent Advocacy Group, member of the Statewide Education Organization Committee, Jersey City Chapter, member of JC Coalition of Parent Teacher Organizations, member of JC Homelessness Advocacy Group), member of the JC Board of Education Athletic Committee and Creative Director of the JC’s 2012 Crime Stop Campaign.

Specialties

Brand Imaging, Graphic Design, Illustration, Business Management, Creative Direction, Advertising, Retouching, Copywriting, Parent Advocacy, Education Advocacy, Community Advocacy, Leadership
