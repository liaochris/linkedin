Alessandro Mancini's Experience

Mancini Legal Limited

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Law Practice industry

June 2012 – Present (1 year 5 months) Horsham and Kent

Mancini Legal is a forward thinking and progressive Law firm in the South East of England, which is both proactive and is committed at offering the best possible client care and being proactive in assisting its clients. We work on building life long relationships with our clients and we focus on offering a bespoke level of services to each of our clients.

Mancini Legal Limited was founded on 3rd August 2012 by Alessandro Mancini (Director/CEO and Solicitor) Alessandro studied Law at Kingston University and later obtained his Legal Practice Course in Guildford at The College of Law. Alessandro has many years experience in Law, and qualified as a Solicitor in August 2009 working for a practice in Haywards Heath, and developing a Dispute Resolution Department. After a few years Alessandro decided to work in the city, and was contracted to work in Horsham for a multinational insurance company, looking after legal affairs. Alessandro has also worked for an international airline offering consultancy advice on catastrophic injury and overseas travel injury.

Alessandro decided to set up Mancini Legal with the intention of running a friendly, open and approachable Law firm who is always happy to offer commitment to its clients and offer affordable legal services with exception quality and customer care. Customer care is the key to Mancini Legal and the Company strives to work with its clients and ensure that they receive the care and commitment that they deserve.

The key concepts that make up Mancini Legal is to provide private individuals and businesses the best possible legal services based on innovation, efficiency, acessibility and a proactive approach.
