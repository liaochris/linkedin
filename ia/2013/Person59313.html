Jason Lacey, CFP®'s Summary

I believe my background in education and finance, coupled with raising my own family, enable me to provide my clients with a clear vision of what is meaningful and offer unique and insightful advice.

At a certain point in many lives their finances become quite complex. Most individuals hire Lewis Lacey Capital Management because they do not understand investing and we believe listening, answering questions and keeping things simple will help. Our goal is to provide first class wealth management services utilizing exceptional training and experience, with an emphasis on strong after-tax performance and long term relationships with our clients.

Specialties: Tailored wealth management strategies designed to meet cash flow needs while minimizing fees and maximizing your after-tax investment return.

Check out my website at www.laceycap.com, where you can learn more about the many services I provide for individuals and families.
