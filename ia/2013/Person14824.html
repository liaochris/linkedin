Blaine Millet's Summary

Blaine is a subject matter expert (SME) and recognized leader in the area of Social Media, Sales and Marketing and Customer Experiences/Loyalty. He delivers ROI and tangible results in each of these areas and has consistently exceeded expectations.

He is recognized as a BUSINESS LEADER who thinks strategically and acts tactically both as a manager and individual contributor. With his extensive experience in Sales, Social Media, Word-of-Mouth Marketing, Customer Experience and Strategic Consulting, he helps organizations see the vision at 5000 feet and then delivers tangible results at ground level to improve sales and build/deepen relationships .

He is the co-AUTHOR of two books, "Creating and Delivering Totally Awesome Customer Experiences" and "Business Tools - An Executives View of Social Media" - both leading edge views of how the Customer Experience and Social Media can help organizations grow. He is also a sought after SPEAKER in the areas of Social Media and Customer Experiences - personally giving over 300 speeches in the past 3 years.

Blaine has worked in start-up through Fortune 100 organizations in addition to his own consulting practice with industry expertise in professional services, software/technology, telecom, Not-for-Profit, hospitality, and financial services.
