Fulter Hong's Summary

Fulter is on Google's Global Sales & Services management team. Prior to Google he spent five years with General Electric's Lighting Division as a Product Service Manager and Systems Engineer as a graduate of their Edison Engineering program. Prior to GE, Fulter launched E-Tours Systems, a multimedia graphics and computing services company.

Fulter has 2 beautiful daughters and is active in his Church (www.knoxannarbor.org), the Board of Directors for the local Habitat for Humanity affiliate (www.h4h.org), and the Ann Arbor Energy Commission (www.a2gov.org/energy).

Fulter graduated from Case Western Reserve University with a degree in Electrical Engineering/Applied Physics and a Masters degree in Engineering & Management.
