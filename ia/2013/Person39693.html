Edward Phelps' Summary

Edward Phelps is a marketer, designer, visionary and author.

Presently Ed is Co-Founder & Chief Solutions Architect with Business Marketing Systems (BMS), located in Atlanta, Georgia. BMS designs and implements innovative digital marketing strategies to reach people on a mass scale with targeted personalized communications. He has 25 years of award winning experience in marketing, communications design, and advertising.

Ed consults to address unique marketing requirements with services that help:
1. Plan and develop tactics that are implemented with existing resources.
2. Develop personalized communications focused on target audiences.
3. Promote effectively on the Internet with tools that are wholly owned and self-managed.
4. Establish Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and simple processes for measuring effectiveness and ROI.
5. Deliver value by offering professional services that fill skill gaps, continually educate, and empower his clients.

A multi-faceted designer, Ed's work encompasses strategy, design and technology. His projects have included the design of brand identity systems, advertising, websites, automated multi-segment email & mobile marketing campaigns, social media marketing, print collateral, packaging, environments, exhibitions, user interfaces, and software development, with many of his projects spanning across multiple media. Ed is a passionate small business supporter.

He has worked with cross-disciplinary teams, and managed contractors and vendors to deliver solutions on time and on budget. Ed's Internet marketing work has consistently delivered increased unique visits (70% or more), visit durations, click-throughs and conversions (+3.8% average). Ed is an email marketing and marketing automation expert.
