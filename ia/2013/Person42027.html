Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

GoChat

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

2012 – Present (1 year)

GoChat is a social toolbar that instantly creates an interactive community on websites & blogs. Site-visitors gain the ability to chat with each other (text and video), check-in to the website (imagine Foursquare for websites), share files (like an in-browser Napster) and other features packaged into an ultra-simplified iPhone-approach UI.

GoChat is both fun to use and effortless to integrate; add just one line of HTML and it works, no configuration or registration is necessary and the service is totally free.

End-users can engage in a group-chat (text and optional video-chat) with people who are on the same site as well as instant-message users who are on any GoChat-enabled site or online via Facebook, Google, Yahoo! or Windows Live.
