Bill Curtis' Summary

First Response ( First Aid ) Ltd formed in November 1999 and achieved Health & Safety Approval to deliver ‘ First Aid at Work ‘ courses in January 2000. Our aim then was to provide ‘Good Quality’ courses at an ‘Affordable Cost’. We have continued to maintain this aim and will do so in the future.

We conduct courses at client’s premises nationwide, including Scotland and Northern Ireland as well as delivering courses at our training venue in Aldridge, West Mdlands.

Meeting our clients needs we aim to ensure that all attendees walk away having had a learning experience which inspires them to carry this knowledge and good practice into the workplace.

My role is now that of Managing Director with the daily training being delivered by a close network of HSE approved trainers. This gives me opportunity to work more closely with clients and ensure quality control.

Specialties

First Aid at Work, First Aid at Work Requalification, Emergency First Aid at Work, Paediatric First Aid, Basic First Aid, Automatic External Defibrillation, Manual Handling, Food Safety, Refresher Training in Infection Control, Working at Heights, Asbestos Awareness, Driver Safety, Occupational Health.
