Don Seidenberg's Summary

My name is Don Seidenberg. I live and work in Amsterdam. Since the early 90’s, I have written ads, brochures and annual reports for a diverse range of companies. Now, I specialize in web content.

My goal is to create applications and sites that improve the user experience. I have written sites for a variety of purposes: a product launch for Philips, a branding campaign for DSM, a webshop for Elsevier and an intranet for ING.

In addition to writing content, I can develop your content strategy, create a navigation structure or train your team in web writing. Any questions, feel free to call me at 06-24194067 or mail me at don@wordsandideas.nl

Specialties

web writing, technical writing, content strategy, user focus
