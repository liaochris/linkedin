Kirk Hasenzahl's Experience

President

RareWire

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry

January 2011 – Present (2 years 6 months) Kansas City, Missouri Area

RareWire is a company founded in March 2010 to participate in the exciting new market that the iPad has created. The problem was that there was no software to make it easy to publish content to the iPad. So to build an app, you had to get programmers involved, it took time, and cost a lot of $$, and the end result was a custom, one off thing that wasn't scalable.

RareWire is whole new platform, a whole new app description language, and a whole new tool set that makes it easy to publish content to the iPad. Our value proposition is simply this:

RareWire broadens the audience of potential app builders from what typically required hard core programmer skills, an now opens the opportunity up to front end developers, designers, and graphic artists to be able to build very robust, native, cross platform mobile apps.

Oh yeah, we are pretty good at building apps for clients also..............................
