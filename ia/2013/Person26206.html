Coilin O'Hainey's Summary

My name is Coilin O'Hainey, for the past 25 years I've been a serial entrepreneur, with all the scars and lessons to prove it. I efficiently transform ideas into profitable scalable businesses, through ambition, dedication and teamwork.

My personality has grit character and a raw authenticity that helps me to push through adversity and build meaningful relationships. I was raised by simple people with great values like Honor, Loyalty, Hospitality, Fairness and Courage. Below are a few great lessons I learned from my Grandmother:

» A man is only as good as his word.
» A smart man learns from his mistakes, but a wise man learns from others.
» Common sense is not that common.
» Measure twice and cut once.

In the late 90's I founded a Consulting Firm that provided Sales, Marketing and Public Relations services for brands such as Guinness, Budweiser, Heineken, Jack Daniels, and Green Isle Frozen Foods. I also assisted the state-owned Telecom Éireann privatize and become Eircom, and we also help launch their internet service (Eircom Net) in Ireland.

I have spent six years as a facilitator and fundraiser, during that time I raised over Thirty Million Dollars while also providing due diligence and compliance services for investors and investment managers.

My passions are building companies, designing modified cars, auto racing and helping people succeed. fulfill their potential. I design modified cars that are clean and classy with a hint of badass, kinda like myself and Ill race anything with wheels and a way to steer
.
I admire, Branson, Cuban, Jobs and Musk. I dislike Trump and O'Leary.

I have an insatiable desire to innovate and win and I'm committed to providing my partners and investors with low risk high reward opportunity's.

I’m on LinkedIn to forge strong business relationships. Lets Connect or email ➡ Coilin@OHainey.com

  • Hainey vs the Isle of man TT & Daytona 500

    Hainey vs the Isle of man TT & Daytona 500. A Multi Part Series geared toward general Motorsport enthusiasts who can follow and participate in Hainey's Journey as he attempts to conquer the greatest motorcycle race in the world the isle of Man TT, and his preparation for and competing in the greatest American race, Nascar's Daytona 500.

    The show will document the highs and lows of the preparations required, raising money, getting sponsored and in the physical and mental training to just be competitive.

    "since the first time I seen the TT as a boy its always been my dream to compete in and eventually win at Isle of man TT. I grew up a long way from Daytona but Days of Thunder was one of my favorite movies and as I watched Cole Trickle enter the Daytona 500, I sat in my little village in Ireland and thought someday I'm going to do that".

    True stories about ordinary people achieving their dreams usually are shown after the event has occurred and the viewer typically knows the final outcome. We want to show the preparation from just an idea to all the way through competition so you, the fans, can watch it unfold every step of the way. It will create great insight into what it takes to compete in the Isle of Man TT and make it to the Daytona 500, our aim is to make the viewer feel like they are part of this journey and hopefully inspire them to follow there dream.

    "Those who know me know how competitive I am, and those that don't wont be long finding out. This journey will have highs, lows, wins and certain losses. It won't happen over night, but I'll give 110% all the way to the checkered flag".
