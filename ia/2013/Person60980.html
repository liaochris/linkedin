Jeff Kalpak's Summary

•President of Barkley Kalpak Agency (BKA), an award-winning event production and entertainment company based in New York City. BKA produces hundreds of events annually for major corporations within the US and abroad.

•Coaches, C-level executives and Senior Management for various presentations and speaking engagements.

•Produces and directs theatrical productions such as the televised Drama Desk Awards show, honoring the best of Broadway and off-Broadway. Directed celebrities such as Sarah Jessica Parker, Antonio Banderas, Sean “Diddy” Combs, and Harvey Fierstein to name a few.

•Produced private concerts with celebrity headliners including Sheryl Crow, James Taylor and Vanessa Williams.

•Produced and directed theatrical and corporate events around the world, including the Asian premiere of the Broadway musical Chicago for the Macao International Music Festival in 2005, and returned to China in 2006 to produce Guys and Dolls.

•Industry speaker and Guest Lecturer at New York University and Syracuse University. Has lectured on behalf of MPI (Meeting Planners International), Event Solutions Magazine, Special Events Magazine, Agenda Magazine, SITE (Society of Incentive & Travel Executives), RSA (Receptive Services Association) and was the past chair of the NYC Chapter of SITE.

•Served on the Board of IACEP (International Association for Corporate Entertainment Producers) and was President of TEAM Net, a national network of meeting and event professionals. Other affiliations include ISES, NYC & Co. and the Society of Stage Directors and Choreographers.

•Named Best Producer by American Entertainment Magazine in both 2006 and 2007, was selected the Entertainment Producer of the Year in 2005 by Event Solutions Magazine and was the recipient of the 2004 and 2005 TEAM Net Spirit Award.

Specialties

Meeting and Event Planning and Production, Speaker Coaching, Speaking at Industry Functions, Directing
