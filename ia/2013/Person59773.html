Jay Bernard's Projects

  • Faux Decor looks

    Team Members: Jay Bernard

    Faux Décor offers all of our customer's beautiful affordable faux finishing and professional interior painting. With hundreds of inspired faux design solutions and modern painting ideas for every room in your home, re-imagined and tailored for the way we live today. Beautiful faux finishing and expert painting can help you experience every room in a different way, and it can make all the difference in how you feel about your home.

    "We make custom painting affordable". Faux Decor offers a wide variety of elegant faux finishes designs from Traditional to Contemporary using: color washes, marbleizing, plaster effects, European plaster finishes, metallic finishes, gold & silver leafing, faux stone, wood graining and murals and more. Serving the following areas: Nashville, Brentwood, Franklin, Green Hills, Belle Meade, Spring Hill, Mt. Juliet and most of Middle Tennessee. It's easy with Faux Decor's design ideas and its creative team of United Artisans (UA).
