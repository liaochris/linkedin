Max Blumberg's Summary

Welcome.

I worked as a management consultant at Accenture in South Africa and the US before founding and successfully exiting a technology start-up followed by a PhD at Goldsmiths, University of London where I am a Visiting Research Fellow in Psychology.

Today, my company's commercial and philanthropic work falls into four primary areas, all analytically based so that bottom-line impact can be easily measured:

1. Global sales force effectiveness: Redesign of sales processes, sales force structures and cross-selling based on analytical market segmentation

2. Coaching elite performers: Taking high performers in business, sport & philanthropy to the next level using evidence-based coaching psychology

3. Workforce science: Leaders in the application of human capital analytics and big data techniques for attracting, developing and retaining high performers

4. Philanthropy: Charitable self-help programmes which empower people to help themselves

Organizations benefiting from our work include Lloyds Register, the BBC, Brit Insurance, The MOD, Barclays Corporate, and Friends Provident (testimonials below).
