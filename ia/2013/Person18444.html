Bryan Morse

Bryan Morse

April 1978 – Present (35 years 7 months)

For the past twenty five years, Bryan Morse has been instrumental in pioneering new directions in landscape design and construction. A passionate plant collector, Morse travels the world collecting new species to introduce into the landscape industry of Southern California. In 1978 Morse founded Expanding Horizons, his design and Landscape Construction Company.
Bryan has a passion for Botanical Gardens. In 1998 he joined forces with Clyde and Connie Childress to help coalesce their vision of a Jungle and a Rose Garden into what was to become Pikake Botanical Gardens. Nine separate themed gardens were built around the Grand Concourse. "Bryan is one of the most talented people we have ever found," says Clyde Childress. "He is an artist and a contractor. He made our dream a reality." Bryan was given the unique opportunity to design and build nine separate Gardens. Their efforts were recognized the world over and the Garden was recognized and honored for its innovative design and execution.
In 2003 Bryan turned his focus to a new project. Since then he has been pivotal in developing the master plan for Alta Vista Botanical Gardens in Vista, CA. He is now the president of the Board, acting director of Gardens and is the inspiration for the vision that is developing the Garden.
