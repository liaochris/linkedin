First Inspiration self published book released in 2010 entitled,"In Pursuit of the Promise."
Brief Description:
In Pursuit..... At some moment in each of our lives each of us will stop and ponder on our existence and our purpose here on earth. Some people will take this moment seriously and others will allow this moment to waste away.
If you have ever gotten to a point where you know that God has much more for your life and you want to make a change”In Pursuit of the Promise” will inspire you to do just that.
With In "Pursuit of the Promise," readers who feel lost, defeated, or those who are just looking for direction, will learn six changes that they must apply to there lives in order to receive God’s complete promise. "In Pursuit of the Promise" is based on Caleb and Joshua’s journey through the wilderness and their predestined victory. It also evaluates other promised scriptures that will motivate readers and point them in the direction where they will find life’s purpose. The author creatively tells a a story that enlightens, educates, and encourages her readers. "In Pursuit of the Promise" also reminds readers about God’s faithfulness through even the worst times.

God gave us a promise, the pursuit is ours, now what are we going to do with it?
