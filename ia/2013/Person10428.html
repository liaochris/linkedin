Adam Goodman's Summary

I help individuals in advanced financial strategies that enable them to minimize risk & uncertainty from their financial plan so that they can live without financial stress or worry and ultimately live life on their terms!

I am an independent insurance professional specializing in working with retirees and those approaching retirement.

Our Service Areas

 Eliminate any future losses from existing retirement accounts + better growth than the bank
 Create a future lifetime income stream from existing assets/private pension like plans
 Optimize old 401K/403B/457 IRA accounts when over 59 1/2 or separated from service
 Tax Free Retirement
 Life Insurance (term and permanent) Long term care solutions, Estate and Pension Income planning/replacement.


Many of the solutions our clients desire, we deliver through properly designed and structure fixed indexed products. This niche in the industry has been one of the most popular and desired solutions over the past 10+ years, even though many companies aren't providing their clients with these options, the demand for these products and the # of people using them just keeps increasing year over year.... if you are looking for a Fixed Indexed Annuity or Indexed Universal Life expert in the Las Vegas area, who doesn't represent one specific company, and will take the time to get to know what is important to you, and how best to help you...I am your guy...

Initial conversation is without cost or obligation.

Also we are hiring and looking to bring into our business family an experienced and caring insurance producer as well as marketing, PR, operations and customer service support.

Our mission is to help people eliminate financial worry so that they can live life on their terms!

If that inspires and motivates you too, + you enjoy working in an entrepreneurial environment, would love to hear from you!

Will train the right individual! Compensation = to the value you help create.
