John C Mackle's Summary

Professional manager with significant start-up and high-growth technology experience. Proven President, COO and CFO. Strong strategic leader with broad operational expertise in service businesses and international operations.

Currently serving as Executive VP to two of Palm Beach Capital's portfolio companies in the employee leasing industry, in which the goals are to restructure operations, streamline management and position the newly combined company for future growth.

Prior to Palm Beach Capital, John was President of MGM International, a firm dedicated to creating carbon credits and the trading of those credits on environmental exchanges. John directed the Miami headquarters of this global enterprise with offices in the US, Argentina, China, Brazil, Ukraine and India. Since joining as CFO and VP of Operations, John lead the company through several equity transactions, joint ventures, and strategic business development initiatives, as well as an overall company restructuring in late 2009.

John has helped build three early stage ventures into successful businesses, including LiveOps a technology-based provider of virtual contact center services. In his four years as LiveOps’ CFO and VP of Operations, John led the company through a period of explosive growth, as annual revenues increased from zero to over $18MM in less than three years, and set the company on a path to nearly $200MM in revenue today. John sourced and guided the completion of a strategic merger, as well as the company’s first venture capital raise of $22MM on an aggressive pre-money valuation.

John spent the first eight years of his career in financial advisory, valuing and consulting with many new ventures, first with PWC and later Arthur Andersen. He holds two undergraduate degrees from the University of Notre Dame in finance and history, and earned his MBA degree with honors from Vanderbilt University.
