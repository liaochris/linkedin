Joseph Kuzemka's Summary

A lifelong resident of South Trenton, Joseph is a 18-year veteran of the Graphic Arts industry. Starting work in the field at the age of 20, he has worked in all aspects of the design industry including news/media, pharmaceutical advertising and most recently as an Award-Winning Senior Graphic Designer for New Jersey Housing and Mortgage Finance Agency, New Jersey’s largest financier of Affordable Housing, where he manages the Marketing and Communications department that performs graphic design, marketing and media related duties for over 300 employees and nearly a dozen divisions and departments.

In 2007 and 2008, Joseph received several awards including multiple Graphic Design USA Awards for his work. He was also presented with the NCSHA (National Council of State Housing Agencies) 2008 Program Excellence Award for the 2008 NJHMFA Annual Report where he successfully managed to win despite dozens of other HFA’s (Housing Finance Agencies) vying for the same accolade.

Growing up, Joseph was heavily involved in the underground music scene where he not only published and successfully distributed thousands of copies of his music based “fanzine” called Nevermore but also created a record label to distribute the bands he felt most passionate about. Nevermore Records released several vinyl and CD titles including the Tie That Binds cd compilation that included national acts such as Lifetime, which sold nearly 5,000 copies.

A founding member, Creative Director and Event Director of Trenton’s wildly successful 24-hour art/music/film festival, Art All Night-Trenton, Joseph's logo design and branding have become notable fixtures in the City of Trenton as well as the surrounding areas. This has helped propel Art All Night-Trenton into an incredibly successful, volunteer-based event with 2013 bringing over 30,000 fans to the annual show. Under his tutelage, Joseph has managed to increase attendance by over 20,000 attendees as well as profits by 300%.

Specialties

Creative Direction, Art Direction, Corporate Branding and Identity, Expertise in Print Media design and boutique-style websites for small businesses, Social Media guru, extensive knowledge of Offset Printing processes and formats, amateur photographer specializing in urban settings and abandoned areas, large-scale event planning
