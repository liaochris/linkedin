Major projects included the development and oversight of a $3.3 million character education grant program; coordination of the Board of Regents' Louis E. Yavner Awards for education about the Holocaust and other human rights violations; coordination of the U.S. Senate Youth Program; creation of web-based instructional resources and professional development workshops on social studies standards, curriculum, and assessments; and teacher training for state assessment (Regents examination) development committees.
