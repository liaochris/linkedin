Anita Horsley, M.Ed's Summary

During Anita Horsley’s years as a firefighter in Atlanta, she, not only was a station Captain, but trained firefighters to be nationally certified personal-fitness trainers. She also developed, from initiation to implementation, the Health and Safety Program for the fire department of over 800 employees and then she managed the program.

As the Training and Development Specialist at the Oregon Office of State Fire Marshal (Oregon State Police), she founded the eLearning track and worked all areas of training. She publishing all core curriculum online within the first three years. Additionally she provided train-the-trainer instructor-led courses and wrote all the instructor and student manuals. Horsley was the domain manager for the LMS and program coordinator of the agency eLearning implementation team. She also coordinated the Oregon State Captivate User Group and taught Captivate both internally and at local colleges and city employees in Oregon.

Currently, Horsley is the Founder, Owner, and President of CALEX Learning Consultants, that provides instructor-led courses on Adobe Captivate and designing and developing eLearning courses for agencies. She is an author of 'Fast Track To Adobe Captivate 6' with Packt Publishing. She presents at conferences on eLearning and Adobe Captivate and provides webinars for Adobe and ASTD. She is also a reviewer for Packt Publishing. Horsley is an Adobe Certified Expert and has a Masters’ degree in Education.

Anita Horsley, M.Ed's Publications

  • Fast Track to Adobe Captivate 6 Video Tutorials

    • Packt Publishing
    • May 30, 2013
    Authors: Anita Horsley, M.Ed

    Fast Track to Adobe Captivate 6 is a set of 40 ~ 3-5 minute videos (2 1/4 hours total) that takes you through step-by-step instructions on how to use fundamentals of Adobe Captivate 6 or 7. There are many tips and tricks, so even if you've used Captivate previously, you'll probably learn a lot in this course. It comes with written instructions and takes you through creating a project from start to finish with over 40 downloadable source files!
    What you'll learn:
    Familiarize yourself with the Adobe Captivate 6 (also applies to 7) interface and global settings to improve your workflow for future Captivate projects.
    Insert new slides and manage these slides using the properties panel, slides notes, master slides, and themes.
    Easily insert characters, themes, images, Photoshop files, animations, effects, highlight boxes, and zoom areas to provide emphasis and make your courses aesthetically pleasing.
    Organize the assets in your project using the library, timeline, grouping, object style manager, and unique and common object properties.
    Insert interactive elements into your content with just a click and customize the content and its appearance.
    Rapidly create product demonstrations with high definition video and record training simulations.
    Easily edit videos and add pan and zoom, transitions, smart shapes, audio, and captions.
    Enrich your courses with audio – insert or record narration to presentations and eliminate mistakes with the easy-to-use audio editor tool.
    Learn about the variety of publishing options such as SWF, HTML5, word documents, PDFs, and Adobe Connect.
