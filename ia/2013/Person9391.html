Priyanka Jain's Summary

Priyanka received a masters in urban planning from Columbia University and has a professional degree in architecture from Sushant School of Art and Architecture. She has worked as an associate architect on several award winning projects with Abaxial Architects in New Delhi. She has also worked with NYC Department of City Planning.

Her projects have focussed on sustainable architecture and participatory planning in various international contexts including New Delhi, Istanbul, Rio de Janeiro, and New York City. Her master's thesis made a case for active voice and empowerment of marginal groups. It evaluated public property rights for homeless and day laborers and recommended guidelines for cooperative than coercive practices.

Priyanka believes in collaborative platforms that engender cooperative practices through combination of design, personal narratives, and spatial data.
