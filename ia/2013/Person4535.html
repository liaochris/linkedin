Alicia R. Reid's Summary

My objective is simple. To help you achieve your real estate related goals. From your first home through your luxury home, I'll help you strategically plan and build a portfolio of properties that will exceed your wildest expectations!

As a second generation Realtor, I was raised in this business. Real Estate has always been my second nature. The first five years after college I worked as an appraiser. This set a sound foundation for my role for the past 20 years as a managing partner in acquisitions and oversight property management of a small portfolio of manufactured housing communities, NNN leased buildings, income producing single family residences and as sole owner of an apartment building.

In 2001 I launched my real estate sales career. And through a strong work ethic, customer service priority and tremendous referral base from past customers, I have built my sales level to the top 1% of Realtor's nationally.

Think big and together we will achieve! Let me share with you my joy and unbridled enthusiasm for this amazing business! I truly believe that this is the vocation that was born to pursue and it will be my pleasure to share this with you on your journey.

Specialties

Acquisition, Marketing, Preparing homes for market, Staging, Commanding Grasp of Market Values, Unique properties, Luxury homes, ADA Accessible homes/ Universal design homes, I have personal experience owning and managing residential properties in Bellevue, Issaquah, Kirkland, Redmond and Sammamish.
