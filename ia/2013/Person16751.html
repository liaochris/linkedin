Brenda Lee Roberts, M. Ed., LPC's Summary

I help couples that are redefining their family to create solutions that work for their specific needs. Every family is unique. Schedules, extracurricular activities, relatives, family holidays and day-to-day routines can prove to be challenging and overwhelming. Solutions to these challenges can be difficult during changes in the family structure. I work in partnership with families to create positive outcomes that fit their lives.

My services include collaborative consultation, parent facilitation, adoption social studies, disputed custody social studies and Children in the Middle classes.

I practice with the conviction that creative solutions for families comes from engaging each other with respect, dignity, honesty and integrity. It comes from constantly bringing your children’s best interests into focus. I provide experienced consultation or counseling to clients in the areas of divorce, separation, parenting and adoption.
