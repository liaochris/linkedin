Andreas Stephanides' Summary

A strong qualified financial senior financial executive with international work experience in Financial Management, Administration, Strategy Formulation, Business Development, IPO’s, M&A and Investor Relations.

In my career of 35 years, 18 of which were as Group CFO and Executive Director Positions, I have worked for diversified listed companies having interests in Construction, Concession, Real Estate, Energy, Mining and Quarries. Turnover exceeded €2 Billion.

Extensive experience at highly diversified and demanding business environments in Greece and internationally, with a proven international track record in senior positions in Finance and Operations, Administration, Strategy Formulation, Business Development and IPOs. Direct and open professional character. Extensive working exposure in European, African and Middle East markets. Extensive investment road shows in Canada, US, Switzerland, Italy, Germany, France.
