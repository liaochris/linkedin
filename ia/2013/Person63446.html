JG (Greg) Hanks' Summary

A former U.S. Navy Electronics Technician, college stringer for MTV, record store manager, band promoter/manager, college radio DJ and music director, personal assistant and dispatcher, Greg is currently a married, father of two, graduate school student and founder of Southernerds, a company started to promote the pursuit of knowledge and learning in all its forms by those living in and/or from the South. He is also the Director of Marketing and Public Relations/Document Control/Technical Services Director/Occupational Safety Specialist at a small family owned company.

He graduated from Mississippi State University with a BS in Interdisciplinary Studies with concentrations in Psychology, Business and Communications and is currently pursuing his Master of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication with a concentration in Public Relations at Kent State University.

Before he started graduate school, Greg started an independent public relations company and has multiple clients in the entertainment industry in Los Angeles.

In his spare time he runs a small film society in Mississippi as well as its sister film review website with the hopes of pursuing further opportunities involved with writing and film.

Greg also has a website chronicling his recent foray back into the world of golf called Quadruple Bogey. His purpose is to try and dispel the stereotype that golf is only for old, rich guys and promote the fact that it can be fun for everyone.

Specialties: social media, promotions, publicity, public relations, writing, editing, hardware, software, IT help desk support, occupational safety, document control, military background
