Dan Dieck's Summary

Dieck Executive Search is one of North America's leading executive retained search consulting firms. Privately held since 1985, Dieck Executive Search utilizes its extensive knowledge within a diverse array of industry sectors, leveraging cross-functional talent to advise its clients, who range from multinationals to emerging companies, addressing their leadership needs.

Dieck Executive Search is a fully integrated Industrial, Consumer, Life Science, Health Care, Professional Services, and Technology practice firm, placing executives in a wide array of functions in every sector of the industry. The search consultants at Dieck Executive Search focus on senior level executive search and board appointments.

Within 6 practice areas, Dieck Executive Search is uniquely qualified to advise companies on leadership issues. The organization's proven search process allows our search consultants to identify and recruit world-class senior executives and board members. Each of our consultants brings years of search experience and knowledge of the particular issues facing our clients.

Dan Dieck is recognized globally as the top Retained Search Consultant in the Pulp, Paper, Packaging, Chemical, Coatings, Printing and related Industries.
