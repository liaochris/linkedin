David Jones' Publications

  • Who Cares Wins - Why Good Business is Better Business

    • Pearson (Financial Times Series)
    • December 18, 2011
    Authors: David Jones

    Who Cares Wins - Why Good Business is Better Business - shows you how to use social media to engage with customers and grow your business. This isn't a book about social media and the inexorable rise of Facebook and Twitter. Nor is it a book about CSR or business doing good. Instead it's actually the first book that recognizes that far from being two separate subjects, they are intrinsically interlinked. And that the most successful leaders and businesses in the future will be those who are the most socially responsible.
