Dustin C. Jones, Esq.'s Summary

In May 2013, Dustin Jones earned the "Most Admired Leaders" award from The Phoenix Business Journal for his government relations, corporate and real estate law practice, as well as his community service. In 2006, he was inducted into the publication's elite “Forty Under 40” club and in 2005 was named one of the “Valley's 24 Movers and Shakers” by the Arizona Republic newspaper.

Mr. Jones has practiced law at Arizona’s top law firms. He was a Partner at Phoenix's largest law firm Snell & Wilmer, LLP after gaining experience as an Associate at Lewis and Roca, LLP. He was also a Shareholder at the distinguished real estate powerhouse of Tiffany & Bosco, PA, where he was Chairman of its Real Estate, Land Use, & Government Relations practice, before launching his own boutique firm in 2010.

Mr. Jones served two terms on the Governor's African-American Advisory Council. He has served as local municipal Planning & Zoning Commissioner and currently serves on the Maricopa County Board of Adjustments, one of the nation’s largest and fastest growing counties. He also served multiple terms on the Boards of the West Valley Arts Council and the West Valley Hospital.

Mr. Jones's involvement with Arizona State University includes serving on the Community Advisory Board of Channel EIGHT at the Walter Cronkite School of Journalism and lecturing on New Urbanism and Form Based Codes. He serves regularly as a guest speaker with the Form Based Code Institute (FBCI), the Arizona Planning Association, Urban Land Institute and other development forums. Dustin has been a member of the Congress for the New Urbanism, the U.S. Green Building Council, Valley Forward, and Valley Partnership.

Skills: Real Estate; Zoning and Land Use; Regulated Industries; Government Relations; Capital Markets; Business Law, Corporations, Banking; Finance; International Trade, Paymaster Trustee.
