Jean Cornell RN, Ph.D's Summary

I am an RN who I has a passion for health (with more than 30 years as an RN) as well as the wisdom of our ancestors. I promote using healthier choices in our food, cleaning and personal care products.

We are exposed to more than 80,000 chemicals of which only a couple of hundred are tested and approved safe! In fact OBESITY, ADHD, CANCER and other chronic conditions like DIABETES, allergies, heart disease, are linked to lifestyle and diet! It sounds too simple to be true but 80% of these diseases are PREVENTABLE. We are unknowingly and slowly causing our children to be affected as these diseases are skyrocketing in our most vulnerable population at younger and younger ages.( more than 300 chemicals are now found in newborns!)

That means that we can halt, slow down and maybe even prevent many these debilitating problems by limiting our exposure over time. I promote a CLEAN diet , reducing chemical exposure in your home environment and provide you with choices of safer more natural remedies that are tried and true by our ancestors before industry decided to change our perception and improve our lives through technology.

By fusing the old with the new paradigms, I developed educational and coaching programs to help Moms near and far manage these issues. My mission is to reach a Moms and to reverse the bleak health projections for generations to come. I encourage a more natural and healthy approach through nutrition, products and practices that support the natural healing mechanisms of our bodies. I use a step by step customized approach that fits your budget, goals and lifestyle so that you can have the peace of mind that you are doing all you can to eliminate the risks for you and your own family by providing a safe home and releasing the frustration and worry of those things you just can't control.
