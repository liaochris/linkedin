Summer Whitford's Summary

I am a food and drink writer, event planner, educator, marketer, professional chef, and consultant with over 12 years of experience.

For 15 years I was the owner and chef for Chez Vous Production, Inc., a food specialty event planning and catering business with clients that included food and drink brands, ambassadors, TV and movie celebrities, Members of the US Congress, hospitality businesses, and other public and private clients.

My specialty is building brand loyalty by telling the story of food and drink in an "edutaining" way. Enthusiasm for my subject is the result of years of experience in restaurants, retail, publishing, and customer service.

Sharing what I have learned in books, newsletters, online, in print, and in social media is one of my passions. My writing credits include: author of Join Us At The Embassy, a book about food and drink traditions, culture, and travel to 10 countries; Senior Writer and Editor of World Class Cuisine, an award-winning TV series on Discovery Channel featuring Relais & Châteaux chefs from five countries; "book doctor" for The Tax-Deductible Wedding, a how-to wedding planning book; and creator of several popular food and drink newsletters.

I have also been a product ambassador for Champagne Office USA and the Center for Wine Origins and have developed trade and consumer curricula for the Embassy of France, resorts, community colleges, departments of recreation, and other organizations.

From tips on wedding traditions from around the world, to planning a glam event on a shoestring budget, I've got you covered.

Specialties: food, wine, beer, spirits, culture, travel, writing, wedding and event planning, marketing, branding, strategic planning, social media.

Summer Whitford's Publications

  • JOIN US AT THE EMBASSY

    • Palari Publishing
    • September 9, 2009
    Authors: Summer Whitford

    JOIN US AT THE EMBASSY highlights the food, drinks, and culture of ten countries as seen through the eyes of their ambassadors. It's written like a cultural guide and culinary travelogue and includes fun tidbits about table manners, dining customs, cuisine, wine and much more. Part food guide, part travel guide--it should be on every traveler's packing list.

  • World Class Cuisine

    • Madison Books
    • 1993
    Authors: Summer Whitford, Gail Greco

    This cookbook, the companion to the award-winning Discovery Channel television series by the same name, was written as a travel guide for world class gastronomes. It features traditional, regional specialties, and elegant dishes, prepared by renowned Michelin star and Relais & Châteaux chefs from Austria, Belgium, France, Italy, and Spain.

    Summer was the Food Stylist for the cookbook cover. She was also the Contributing Editor, and in this role she provided nine of her original recipes. In addition, Ms. Whitford tested, wrote, or edited all of the recipes submitted by culinary superstars such as Alain Ducasse, Marc Meneau, and Pierre Wynants.
