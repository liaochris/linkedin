Brandon Jansen's Summary

Brandon is a Senior Manager at Ernst & Young. He advises clients on CFO-level related issues, focusing in the high-tech, consumer products and manufacturing industries. He has advised clients on a wide variety of finance transformation projects, including financial close reduction, business and operational improvement, benchmarking/process improvement and cost reduction.

Over the past 13 years, he has advised the following clients: Chevron, Dell Computer, Electroglas, FujiXerox, Google, Marubeni Corporation, P&G, Respironics, salesforce.com, Sony, Wind River Systems, and Verizon.

Brandon is a graduate of The University of Chicago’s MBA program with concentrations in General Management and Entrepreneurship and a graduate of Miami University with a B.S. in Accountancy and MIS.
