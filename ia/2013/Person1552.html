Adam Mostogl's Summary

Adam's background is varied, including having worked in hospitality, heavy industry, education and retail, which gives him a diverse understanding of where businesses have similarities but also where they are different.

He may come across as boring because he loves strategy and procedures - but by systemising the ordinary helps to maintain quality control, and allows everyone to humanise the extraordinary. He also likes working with technology to help do things that might not have been thought possible, which explains why his iPad looks like it's an extension of his arm.

Adam currently holds two roles at Effective Naturally; being the Operations Manager to keep all of the creative minds in check, but also as a Momentum Manager to maintain the day to day rollout of their roadmaps.

Adam also runs his own business called illuminate SDF, which is focused around developing young students and business people to take on the world. One way that illuminate SDF does this is through the illuminate:nextgen challenges, where students spend a week out of school learning about what it takes to run their own business, while also understanding how to handle working in teams, stress and taking control of their learning for the week. This is delivered in Tasmania in partnership with the University of Tasmania and Australian Maritime College. Adam is extremely passionate about what young business people can do and achieve, and is focused on supporting them to take the next step in their endeavours and to Believe No Boundaries.

Adam also volunteers at Door of Hope Christian Church, helping with social media interaction and using his business mind to shape the way the church functions.

Adam still holds a strong interest in community and political development through creating engaging solutions where wider engagement is crucial. This is evident in all he does, and loves to take on new challenges as they arise.
