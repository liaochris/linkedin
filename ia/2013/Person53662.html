Hosting .nl's Summary

More than 12 years a passion for Hosting!

20 highly skilled Hosting and Online Backup specialists with a passion for Hosting are here for you!

Hosting.nl is the hosting partner for business partners/resellers and companies, from small business till multinationals worldwide. Hosting.nl is specialized in webhosting,related products and services, with focus on high quality and superior performance!. Some products of Hosting.nl are for example Virtual Dedicated Servers, Dedicated servers, Webhosting (Windows & Linux) Shared hosting, Hosted Exchange, Hosted Office, Hosted Workplace, Domain Names, Online Storage and SaaS.


MKBackup (daughter enterprise of Hosting.nl) offers online backup software and solutions for almost every operating system and many databases arranged for laptops, desktops and servers. Also thanks to our PCI3 certification and two higly secured datacenters your data is safe by MKBackup. Because our reliability, safety and good reputation we have a various customers truce, like authorities institutions, banks and several multinationals.
