Gail M. Riegelmayer's Summary

Business and Life Coach specializing in working with individuals and business owners who want to improve themselves and their businesses. Extensive experience working with successful companies experiencing "growing pains" with a need to implement employee accountability; create a strong foundation to increase profitability; develop and create more effective organizational structures, systems and processes; develop successors for the next leadership generation; and achieve work/life balance. 75% of Clients are family owned and operated businesses. All coaching services are customized to the specific needs, goals and vision of the client.

Specialties: Coaching small business owners, sales professionals, family owned and managed businesses, realtors, insurance principle agents and insurance salespeople, leadership and management, sales, and succession development. I also help enhance interpersonal and company communication.

My philosophy in coaching sales professionals is that they are the CEO/President of their own business inside the company in which they are employed. Therefore, I help them set specific goals, and develop action plans, and activity and end-results tracking mechanisms to measure their progress. I then hold them accountable to executing on their plan, work through challenges and obstacles, as well as maximize opportunities.
