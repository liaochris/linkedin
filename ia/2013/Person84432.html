Malcolm Auld's Summary

I am a highly experienced marketer living in Sydney.

Over the last decade I have launched a number of digital businesses including: a specialist marketing education company that offered Australia’s first online certificate course in digital and direct marketing; an internationally awarded marketing consultancy; and the country’s first email marketing agency.

I’ve also run three multinational direct marketing agencies, a couple of marketing departments and a supermarket - working in Australia, the US and Asia.

My book “Direct Marketing Made Easy” is one of the largest selling marketing texts in Australia/NZ, while “Email Marketing Made Easy” was the world’s first non-American book on the subject. “The Little Book of Marketing Truths” is in its 3rd edition and my new book “Marketing in a Digital World” will be released in 2013.

I have written and delivered numerous Certificate courses, seminars and workshops for industry associations, fundraisers and private companies. And I’ve trained marketing executives in 19 countries.

I like to write. www.themalcolmauldblog.com
