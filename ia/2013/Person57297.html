James Bruce's Summary

Multi-award winning creative director with over 15 years' experience encompassing visual communication, interaction design and user experience across all digital platforms. Consistently building, leading and transforming creative teams to deliver exciting, imaginative interactive multi-platform content in collaboration with companies such as BBC, ITV, Channel 4, Disney, Nickelodeon and Warner Bros.

www.jamesbruce.co.uk

James Bruce's Experience

Head of Creative, Disney Interactive, EMEA

The Walt Disney Company

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; DIS; Entertainment industry

May 2011 – Present (2 years 3 months)

Creative direction and user experience design leadership of a broad portfolio of digital products, services, games and apps. Creating high-quality interactive entertainment across all digital media platforms, meeting the ever-changing demands of user behaviour and cross platform technology.

Develop long and short-term user experience and design strategy, such as implementation of the first global experience language and associated design framework adopted for all interactive development initiatives across EMEA. Alignment with the wider commercial objectives of the business, ensuring all products meet and maintain the highest standards of brand consistency, creative quality and guest experience.

Restructured, realigned and recruited two regional teams and now lead a department of 16 consisting of art directors, visual designers, game designers, user experience consultants, information architects and front-end developers in London and Budapest. Continue to define and implement successful working practices, improving productivity and resulting in a high standard of creative output across EMEA.

Introduced and continue to oversee a collective of groundbreaking creative development studios shaping an official preferred suppliers list. Continue to build these relationships and on-board new and innovative 3rd parties working towards pioneering co-production of multi-platform content experiences.

Creative direction and user experience leadership of all digital output across non-linear platforms for both CBeebies and CBBC, monitoring and maintaining high creative and usability standards. Including creative direction and brand convergence of the CBeebies and CBBC iPlayer.

Key member of the BBC User Experience and Design steering group, instrumental in developing and influencing the strategy across all digital platforms, including the first BBC global visual language.
