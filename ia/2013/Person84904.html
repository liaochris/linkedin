Marcel Ekkel's Summary

Well versed portfolio-, program- and project- and change management professional focused on strategy execution and benefits delivery. Experienced across multiple industries with amongst others automotive, supply chain, financial services and consumer goods.

Worked extensively in Europe, US and Asia Pacific (emerging markets included) covering more than 10 countries and through that build up a skill set to bridge different cultures.

Has expertise in developing business cases, setting up governance as well as structuring major change initiatives like M&A and PMI, Transformations, Systems Implementations. Drives decisions on doing the right projects and supports doing them in the right way. Uses hard and softer techniques like facilitation-, people skills and business acumen to facilitate business change to assure benefits delivery.

Has build PMO's around the Asia Pacific region that managed portfolios and were centers of excellence, provided facilitation services and did project audits as well as health checks.

Works with startups and uses cross pollination of best practices between startups and corporates. Mentors startups on business model development, strategy and execution. Has a strong interested in design thinking.
