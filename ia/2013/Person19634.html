Candice Cendana's Summary

Proficient in positioning clients as their reputation relates to industry news; professional branding; capturing product messaging and communicating core values; crisis management; establishing close working relationships in client-facing roles; developing online communities; business development, community outreach and brand loyalty; social media and publishing platforms; content development, event production, and sponsorship outreach.

Candice's work involves traditional and digital publishing, and full service marketing communications to publicize brands--all entities from small businesses and emerging artists to corporations and high profile, celebrity artists and television networks. Candice develops long-term strategies for professionals and businesses to secure their reputation as it relates to industry news; she is most passionate about consistent brand and image management, audience engagement and reputation management, but she also has experience with viral, overnight campaigns and crisis management. Candice's work in crisis management for entertainment, lifestyle and sports industry professionals established her as a reputable communications professional. She consults several businesses in the greater Los Angeles area, both new and established brands, on best practices of public relations and social media community development. She also serves as an ongoing consultant for CBS and NBC television networks.

Passion for community drove education and career goals. Candice is Founder of HealthOC, a health initiative that focused on nutrition, prevention and natural health. She is a community advocate for Students Run LA (SRLA) a non-profit organization that trains at-risk students for L.A. Marathon. As of April 2012, she is currently focused on helping entertainment professionals and creatives as Chair of the L.A. Entertainment Professionals Network.

Specialties

Compelling literature and advertising; public/community relations and crisis management; audience engagement; pitching for media placement, heavy on social media and publishing platforms; content & campaign development; social networking, strategic partnerships, event production, non-profit advertising, customer experience, and high impact integrative media strategy.
