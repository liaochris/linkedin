Beth Lengel's Summary

40 years of experience in career and workforce development. Began career in the vocational rehabilitation field working in NC, AL, and SC for state rehabilitation programs before starting Lengel Vocational Services, Inc. in 1989. Provide staff development and training services nationwide with emphasis on Career Development Facilitation. A national CDF Instructor for the National Career Development Association. Expanding lifelong learning opportunities through online and distance learning courses with the goal of expanding services outside the workforce and education arena.
