Ami Cook's Summary

Originally from the UK, Ami has been living and working in Australia for over a decade. Ami holds both a Bachelor of Arts and Masters degree as well as certification in Project Management. In addition to being a qualified therapist, she has undertaken rigorous and extensive coach training, providing a firm grounding in self actualisation psychology.

Senior Leadership roles in large corporates and a wealth of experience in the areas of Consulting, Programme Management and Change have afforded Ami the skills needed to forge strong relationships and deliver optimal results back into these businesses as well as providing
significant experience in Internal Coaching.

Ami draws from and integrates a number of leading edge methodologies, frameworks, models and tools in the field of human potential, enabling her to deliver results that are sustainable. She has spent many years working inside and consulting to large multinational businesses affording
her the ability to design and deliver solutions that meet core business needs and enabling consistent delivery of tangible results.

Ami is committed to remaining at the leading edge of the Coaching Industry and maintains excellence through continuous personal and professional development – keeping abreast of developments in the Industry and new models and systems, which she incorporates into her
existing expertise; ‘walking the talk’ by engaging with her own coach regularly in service of her own self-improvement.
