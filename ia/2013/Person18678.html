Bryon Brewer (MCP,PMP)'s Summary

Bryon is a senior technology manager, developer, and consultant with over 15 years of progressive technology experience. Bryon is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP), Certified Scrum Master (CSM), Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP), ITIL Certified, Six Sigma Green Belt, member of PMI, Association for Computing Machinery, and Dallas .NET user group association.

Bryon specializes in helping companies improve their software development processes. As a leading expert in project management methodologies, Bryon successfully leads small and large projects using both traditional waterfall and CMMI approaches, as well as agile approaches such as SCRUM. Bryon is a leading expert in:

1) Team Foundation Server 2008 and 2010
2) Microsoft Project Server 2010
3) SharePoint 2010 / portal implementations
4) Agile project management (SCRUM)
5) ASP.NET / C# architecture and programming - enterprise level
6) Business Intelligence using PerformancePoint, SharePoint, SQL, SSRS
7) Visual Studio Team System training/coaching
8) Windows Azure development

Helping you improve your software development processes through expert project management, training, and consulting!

He has been an active participant in every aspect of a project's life cycle; assessment, design, implementation, and operations. His recent focus has been on improving project management processes within organizations utilizing hybrid implementations of RUP, MSF, and agile. Recent projects include large business intelligence applications, custom software development, SharePoint portal implementations, TFS deployments and customizations, and Project Server deployments.

Specialties

project management, Team Foundation Server, TFS, PMO, .NET development, SharePoint, enterprise applications, systems architecture, product management, agile development, SCRUM, C#, VB.NET, Oracle, SQL, SharePoint, Portal implementation and strategy, vertical experience in health care, oil and gas, retail, and government.
