Joan Dobbie's Summary

A successful Business Transformation Leader with experience delivering business transformation programs that enable significant and sustainable business benefits, through the development of solutions aligned to the business vision and strategy. Successfully bridges the technology and business worlds to ensure solutions solve real business needs.

Throughout my career I have played significant role in helping many organisations achieve their business vision and goals through the development of capabilities (people, process and technology) aligned to the business strategy.

I am passionate about enabling people and businesses to achieve their vision and believe this is best enabled through the development of individual competencies, and collaborative and innovative workplaces. I am also committed to the delivery of real, sustainable business benefits from transformation programs. As a result I bring a results-driven focus, balanced by an understanding of the importance of people to every engagement.

Specialities –
• Aligning strategic transformation programs and capability development with business vision and strategy
• Delivering large, complex, transformation programs that provide significant business benefits
• Delivering performance improvement, process improvement and cost reduction programmes
• Analysis of complex business issues and development of strategic solutions
• Developing Change Management strategies and managing change programs
• Working with clients to ensure consulting engagements deliver real business solutions
• Coaching and mentoring of individuals to facilitate competency development.
