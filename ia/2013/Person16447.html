Intern/Promotional Representative, 2010 Olympic Project Team

The Coca-Cola Company

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; KO; Food & Beverages industry

January 2010 – March 2010 (3 months)

I promoted Coca-Cola to consumers at the Olympic Pavilion, a nightly parade, and a twenty-four hour activation in Robson Square for the Paralympic Torch Relay, and I motivated crowds through enthusiastic delivery of positive key messages associating Coca-Cola with the Olympic movement, healthy lifestyles, and green initiatives.
