Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

October 1993 – Present (20 years 2 months) Fort Wayne, Indiana Area

Business901 uses Lean Sales and Marketing methods to enable its customers to build sales and marketing applications in a very short amount of time. The result is increase time to market, higher customer satisfaction and a process that can be repeated and improved in real time. Utilizing Lean you spend your time on the doing versus the plan. The simplicity of the Lean model will create clarity for your staff and as a result better execution. This approach may be a model you may end up with if you knew nothing about methodology and just had to build a plan from scratch. But how long would it take for you to get there? For start-ups and established businesses alike, Business901 provides an effective but easy to use method that has been tested by numerous companies and markets. It is flexible enough to allow you to apply your own ideas, while giving you guidance before, during and after. We focus on implementing the marketing process!

Joe Dager's Publications

  • CAP-Do: Connecting Demand to the Lean Supply Chain

    • Business901
    • September 1, 2013
    Authors: Joe Dager

    What makes CAP-Do so attractive is that it assumes we do not have the answers. It allows us to create a systematic way to address the problems (pain) or opportunities (gain) from the use of our products and services. CAP-Do is an emergent process. You may know the outcomes that you desire but that is relatively unimportant in today’s world or The Challenger Model. It is the outcomes that your customer requires and how you adapt to his/her processes to produce their needed results. This takes a willingness to discover as you go versus leading the way.
