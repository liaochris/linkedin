LaTersa Blakely, MS's Summary

LaTersa Blakely is Best Selling Author, Charismatic Speaker, and an “easy growing” Life Coach for women and at risk girls who have a desire to plant seeds of truth, seeds of faith, seeds of vulnerability, She work exclusively with women who are ready to create lives of impact, accomplishment and independence. LaTersa believes in planting seeds of success to lead an extraordinary life.

****A FREE AUDIO FOR YOU~ 5 Secrets to Declutter Your Mind~www.latersablakely.com*****

We nourish our bodies, we nourish our habits, and we nourish those we love but seem to constantly forget to nourish our own hearts and mind. You deserve to choose you; you deserve to disregard who people think you are what people said you are and embrace who you are becoming.

Her programs are designed to helping women get to the core through repotting; replanting and removing the seeds of limiting beliefs, past pain and struggle. Her boutique is a unique 9 step process that is core driven on examining your strength, your essence, your tenacity and your determination. It’s about healing the areas that hold you back from experiencing loving relationships, health and well being.

Born and raised in Holly Grove, AR LaTersa now resides in Holly Grove, AR where she is a wife and doting mother to two children. She earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Agriculture Economics from the University of Arkansas at Pine Bluff and her Master’s Degree in Agriculture Business Management from Alabama A&M University. A two-time winner of StartupNation’s Leading Moms in Business (2011 and 2012), LaTersa is the founder of Moms Wearing Multiple Hats, where she provides tips and inspiration with mom entrepreneurs who are seeking ways to balance motherhood and business. Contact LaTersa on Facebook at http://www.facebook.com/authorlatersablakely.
