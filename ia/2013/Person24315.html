Christopher Argent's Summary

I used to manage accounting and finance teams, now I work to transform them!

• I am a tenacious finance programme lead who believes in delivery with full life management of finance efficiency programmes. From business case development, resource planning, governance, benefit assurance and accountability, plus strong stakeholder management, implementation, communication and user adoption.

• I am an experienced finance business partner trained in blue chip corporates with strong finance management in fast changing business, including high profile finance process programmes and projects for retail B2C, wholesale B2B, and shared service centre outsourcing - SSC, EPM, P2P, A2R.

• My style is characterised by being a strong communicator and team player, with a holistic, forward looking vision of accounting and finance partnered with the wider business. I firmly believe in a consultative and team building approach.

• Outside of Finance programmes, I support accountancy students to become finance leaders through career development programmes, which aim to develop non technical personal skills in parallel with a highly structured career plan. I also write for the ACCA's Student Accountant magazine and run the Accountancy Student Networking Group.
