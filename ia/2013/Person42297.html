Eric Myer's Summary

Eric specializes in location and portrait photography for advertising, editorial and corporate clients. Traveling extensively throughout the world, he has photographed assignments for a wide range of Fortune 500 companies, internationally recognized advertising agencies, design firms and magazines.

Eric has received awards from Communication Arts Magazine, Graphis Magazine, American Photographer Magazine, Photo District News, AR100, New York, Chicago and Los Angeles Art Directors Clubs and many others.

Eric attended UCLA and Art Center College of Design, where he graduated with honors. He has also been on the faculty as an instructor and scholarship advisory board member and has served as a board member of the Advertising Photographers of America, Los Angeles Chapter.

A partial client list includes Microsoft, General Motors, Royal Caribbean, Disney, USAA, Nintendo, Samsung, Hilton Hotels, Coca-Cola, Smuckers, Mead Paper, Jefferies & Co., Yamaha, Apple, USC, UCLA, Qualcomm, Mass Mutual, Transamerica, Newsweek, Los Angeles Magazine, Esquire, Rolling Stone, Travel and Leisure
