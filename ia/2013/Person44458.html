Margaret A. Hofmann's Summary

Author of The Wedding Gift, a mother-daughter memoir. Proceeds benefit a poor school in a slum in Nairobi, Kenya. Available at Amazon, paperback and kindle, www.amazon.com.

Personal Fitness Trainer. Sports performance enhancement programs for women & female athletes. AQR & SPARQ trained. Golf conditioning specialist and postural analysis specialist, www.femaleathletesfirst.com.

Former collegiate Volleyball and Softball Coach, Teacher and Lecturer at institutions such as Xavier University in Cincinnati, Ohio, Thomas More College in Northern Kentucky and Capital University in Columbus, Ohio.
