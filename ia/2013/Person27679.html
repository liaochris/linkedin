Catherine Reid Day, M.A.'s Summary

Storyslices™ uses story to help people and organizations productively navigate transitions, transform situations and live great lives.

In our changing global environment, story anchors complex data, communicates key ideas, aids with motivation and serves as an efficient and often overlooked path to achieving your goals. We specialize in consulting with closely held businesses and their teams of trusted advisors to manage challenges including succession, boards, strategic business plans and leadership development.

We begin by exploring your founding story as the source and baseline for aligning, capturing and conveying core vision and values, developing intergenerational leadership and moving forward to stronger futures.

Services include:

Values based trusts
Aligning vision, values, needs and goals
Succession planning
Board development and owner planning
Family Council formation, life plans and programs 
Philanthropy centered planning and programs
Family coaching and leadership development
Story coaching, writing, publishing and production 

Projects include: a food company sorting out ownership and family leadership questions; a manufacturing company launching its first philanthropic and social marketing efforts; a company forming a family council to transfer leadership to the next generation; a construction company establishing their first owner’s board; a nonprofit transitioning from founding donor to community funding; a founder entrepreneur who is capturing his values in a series of stories to share with his successors; an artist creating a retrospective of her artwork in book form; plus many individual coaching clients.

Specialties: Story coaching, succession planning, life plans and family council formation, family leadership and teams, visioning and planning, values definition, boards and ownership, philanthropy and legacy planning, community engagement, facilitation, keynote speaking, book development, media and production, resourceful connecting.

Storyslices™ uses story to help people and organizations productively navigate transitions, transform situations and live great lives.

As an owner of a closely held family business:
Are you concerned about leaving significant sums of money to your children, that it could result in undesirable consequences? Are you reluctant to give up control of your assets, especially those you’ve worked a lifetime to build? Are family dynamics, poor money management decisions, or a family member struggling with addiction or behavior issues keeping you from transferring wealth at this time? Do you wonder who will be ready to take on leadership in your business or family?

Storyslices offers values-based planning and guides you in the formation of values-based trusts, tools that provide effective control and guidance for future generations.

“Value-based planning helps ensure our descendants develop integrity and become responsible, motivated, self-sufficient, generous individuals who look beyond themselves. It is very exciting and fulfilling to know our planning will help others for a very long time, perhaps into perpetuity.” Actual quote from client. Name withheld for confidentiality

We also work with individuals to successfully navigate life challenges and major questions including job changes, relationship issues, identity, grief and loss, financial challenges and more.
Starting with your unique story we discover the pathway to claiming your more in life. We help you answer the question: “What is it that must be done that only I can do because of who I am?”

We designed the 4 Mores™ model as a generative tool to help you step more fully into your essential self and make the most of who you are. When you work with Storyslices, you will see and be more, you will live and give more.

Contact us to unfold your essential story and claim who you are, where you belong, where you’ve been and where you want to go. The best story you’ll hear is your own. Claim your story!
