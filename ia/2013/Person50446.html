Greg Vassallo's Summary

I'm a User Experience Designer at Welch Allyn, where we're envisioning meaningful, outcome-driven experiences for the future of health care. Before that I was a Senior User Experience Designer at Moment, an independent design studio in New York. I'm passionate about designing meaningful and engaging interactive products that meet user and business needs. Before going to Moment I received a Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University, where my capstone research project included an internship at NASA.

My work focuses on generative research, design strategy, information architecture, and interaction design for web-based and mobile applications. I’ve been fortunate enough to have worked with a diverse set of great clients including: Bloomberg, ESPN, Pfizer, Tiffany & Co., Martha Stewart, Liquidnet, The Markets, and Bank of America.

I’m driven by the idea that user-centered design can improve people’s lives and make the world a better place.

Specialties

user experience design, interaction design, information architecture, product strategy, user research and synthesis, usability testing, project planning and management, service design
