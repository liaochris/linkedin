Owner

Barrister Building

June 1991 – April 2007 (15 years 11 months)

I bought this building to house my own company, Journal Graphics, Inc., when we moved the company to Denver from NYC in 1991. It had a 70% vacancy rate at the time and I bought it for $110,000 -- what we were paying for one year's rent in New York! I bought the building in my own name, and Journal Graphics was my biggest tenant, filling that 70% which was vacant. When I dissolved the company in 1997, I renovated and re-rented that 70% to other tenants and became a commercial landlord. I sold the building for an $800,000 gain in 2007!
