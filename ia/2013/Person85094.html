MÁRCIO COTS's Summary

Lawyer and legal services manager with extensive experience in advising and managing legal departments and law firms. Master in Law by Faculdade Autônoma de Direito (Autonomous College of Law) – FADISP and specialist in Business Law by Mackenzie University, with extension in Contract Law in FGV - GVLaw. Also has attended executive program in Information Technology Law, FGV – EPGE and is also specialized in Cyberlaw by HARVARD LAW SCHOOL - Harvard University - USA.

He completed his MBA in Business Management at FIAP – Faculdade de Informática e Administração Paulista (Paulista College of Informatics and Management), having extended his studies in management in the Leadership in Corporate Counsel Program 2010, at HARVARD BUSINESS SCHOOL - Harvard University - USA. He also has published several articles and books on topics related to Tax Law, Legal issues of Information Technology, Cooperative Law, Legal Services Management and Consumer Law.

Consultant for Cyberlaw issues and Management of Legal Services for well-known media. In the past decades has developed a strong work in the areas of Tax Law, particularly in the administrative area in the Municipal, State and Federal sphere. Is recognized as a reference in matters related to Law and New Technologies, and is consulted by many companies on issues such as privacy, information security and e-commerce.

In the years 2011 and 2012 was hired by Angolan´s organizations and the OAA – Angolan Bar Association to advise them in studies related to the regulation of the Internet in that country.
