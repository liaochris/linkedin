Gary A. Sanchez's Summary

Fortune 500 trained marketing manager with a foundation in brand management and customer marketing. I have a consistent history of growing sales through strategic changes, creating new programs and processes, and by developing effective, benefits-driven communications across all media types at retail & consumer products companies.

I am actively seeking new opportunities, from corporate contract work and project management to small business consulting, marketing development & implementation.

My strengths are business planning & strategy, analysis & creative problem solving, effective project management and cross-functional collaboration, budgeting and forecasting. I'm also strong in developing effective marketing communications across most media types.

I can help brand and customer marketing teams be more effective by helping develop brand consistent, go-to-market strategies, evaluate and analyze new product and channel opportunities, manage a business line, or lead cross-functional projects and initiatives.

I can help small business owners build a marketing department from scratch, establish brand positioning, develop focused marketing strategies, marketing plans and programs, and identify and implement systems and processes to deliver them.

I can be contacted at garyasanchez@yahoo.com.
