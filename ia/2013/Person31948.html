David Montgomery's Summary

Award-winning political reporter. I don't just report the news, I make myself a master of complex policy and political issues, and explain them comprehensively through articles, Twitter, blogging, audiovisual segments and more. In just a few years I've risen to become the lead political reporter for the largest newspaper in the state.

I've got a track record handling complex debates and sensitive disputes in an even-handed manner, capturing the essence of the issues without oversimplifying them. I've covered hotly contested congressional elections and immersed myself in the scrum of legislative sessions. Readers can also count on me for in-depth analysis, whether in the paper, online, or in guest appearances on media such as BBC News and NPR.

I'm also a polymath who's paid his dues covering general assignment. Politics is just one of my passions, and I love bringing my multidisciplinary knowledge to a wide range of subjects.

Though my face is perhaps not made for television, I'm not tied to dead-tree journalism and embrace the multimedia opportunities of contemporary reporting. Everyone from The Washington Post to political junkies across South Dakota have recognized my blogging and tweeting as thorough and exemplary — a must-follow for anyone who cares about South Dakota politics.

My professional career so far has been based in South Dakota — a state I knew nearly nothing about before pulling into the state capital in an overloaded Chevy Cobalt. While natives would tell me I've still got a lifetime to learn about the Mount Rushmore State, I've thrown myself into my new home and feel I know as much about the state's politics as all but a few veteran political hands. But I've lived across the country and abroad, and pride myself on taking a non-parochial approach to news and life.

I'm excited about my current work and where my career might take me, and plan on producing great journalism for decades to come.

To learn more, visit my website at dhmontgomery.com.
