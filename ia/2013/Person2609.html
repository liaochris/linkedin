Ailton Henriques' Projects

  • Insert Brasil 2012

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Ailton Henriques, João Faraco, Uno de Oliveira, Fellipe Ribeiro, Lucyano Palheta, Helen Pereira, Leonardo Faria

    Insert is a design conference which aims to give the participant a broad look at the multidisciplinarities of design applied to the world, and after having experienced it, inspire their own work and daily attitudes. In September 2012, the first edition of Insert happened in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
    The stage received 8 national and international speakers, indie designers, student and small agencies case-studies and two round-tables. The visitor's area housed sponsor booths and a great fair with books sales, posters, clothes, bags, badges and everything related to design. And on the night of the first day, a party shook up the space.

    Over 1,100 people attended the conference, and a lot of buzz came out of the first edition. Insert is now a regular event in Rio's cultural agenda, and aims to attract an ever-growing crowd of designers, artists, who are thirsty for inspiration and networking.
