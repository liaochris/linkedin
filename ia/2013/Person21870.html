Samer Chalfoun's Experience

Founder & Art Director

WRAP Spaces BV

March 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months)

Wrap Spaces is nominated for the 2013 German Design Award.

Breaking Boundaries

Wrap Spaces is committed to a new way of thinking. It is time to break through the long-established boundaries. Literally, by removing walls. But also metaphorically, by expanding your horizons. The world is changing! And Wrap Spaces is capitalising on this. It is thus the ultimate must-have for the city dweller who is at the centre of a busy life and wants to be efficient with space.

Wrap Spaces is a piece of furniture that is perfect for these urbanites. They are in their thirties or forties, live a 24/7 lifestyle, make their own choices, determine their own lives and are independent of time and space. These people no longer need to adapt to their surroundings. Wrap Spaces adapts to them! And, by way of a bonus, the system provides 25% extra living space. Or requires 25% less space – whatever way you wish to look at it.

If you want breakfast in bed, then sleep in the kitchen

The freedom to do what you want is what makes Wrap Spaces so unique. If the units are closed, you can live a relaxed life. If you want to make a strong espresso at any time of the day or night, just one press of a button is all it takes. And if you don’t fancy crumbs from your home-baked croissants in your office, change the Wrap to a comfortable home-office in the blink of an eye. Just so you can see the laptop and the coffee beans of course. Comfort serving people – that is Wrap Spaces!
