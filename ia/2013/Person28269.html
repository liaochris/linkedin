Joseph Curcillo's Summary

Joseph Curcillo has more than 25 years of trial experience. He was a prosecutor in three Pennsylvania counties and for the state attorney general's office. He has spent his entire career learning the complex and intricate body of law that is criminal law and taking it to a level where it can be explained to a jury.

Specialties

In his years as a prosecutor, Mr. Curcillo handled everything from street crimes to the most complex financial fraud investigations and prosecutions. For the past 15 years, he has lent his expertise to the defense of all misdemeanor and felonies at the local, state and federal level.
