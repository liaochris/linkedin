Becki Hyde's Summary

Well-versed in all aspects of graphic design, I currently work as a Digital Visual Designer within the Digital Solutions team at Humana, where I work alongside the interaction design team to create excellent user experiences for Humana's members, providers, agents, and employees.

I have worked as a print designer, production manager, freelance designer and web developer, copywriter and proofreader, marketing manager, and visual-design-focused user experience designer. Thanks to my breadth of work experience, I see the big picture, understand that each project operates under unique constraints, and am capable of creating order from chaos, both visually and as as it relates to the life cycle of a project.
