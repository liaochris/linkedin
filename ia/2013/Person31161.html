Dave Kreimer's Summary

Next Step Consulting consists of a team of professionals supporting the efforts of Dave Kreimer, the company's principal.

Dave Kreimer's background in psychology, and years of facilitation and marketing experience enhance his market research skills. Since 1990, Dave has successfully designed, conducted, and documented roughly 500 research projects involving thousands of focus groups and personal interviews.

The company’s most loyal clients include medical, educational, publishing, technology, and finance firms.
