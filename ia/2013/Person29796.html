Daniel Messer's Summary

I'm a Web Content Manager with extensive experience in library technologies and public library circulation methodology. I'm very interested in technology applications and solutions for library websites and public library circulation.

Beyond the library, I'm also a documentarian, film maker, artist, musician, and writer for my own studio: Sonoran Dragon Studio. I create online media and art for myself and others using digital and traditional mediums.
