"I have been asked many times, if a person who has lived here all their lives, ever reaches a point, where you do not notice how beautiful this area truly is; never is the answer." Watching the valley, and in particular Canmore grow, she is happy to share her paradise, with all new comers to the Canmore and Banff area of the Canadian Rockies. Whether you are interested in recreational property or moving here permanently, seeking the ultimate in outdoor adventure, wildlife and nature photography, a healthy family oriented mountain lifestyle, she welcomes you!

Irene has lived in the valley all her life and yet she has traveled much of the world. In 1980 she opened the first furniture store in the valley, Castle Mountain Home Furnishings. Her vision was to see Canmore grow and prosper into the vibrant family oriented community it is today. In 1997 she sold her successful business, opened Heritage House B&B and become a “stay at home mom".
The year 2006 brought new changes and she became a Realtor, to pursue her other passion of real estate. Over the years she had purchased many properties in Canmore, renovated them and sold them.
In 2009 she wrote and self published her first book of a trilogy, titled “Passion Between the Sheets of Life: An Open Heart”. The work provides an introduction to life in the Bow Valley and is an autobiography, meant to inspire people to live all aspects of their lives and relationships with the same zest and enthusiasm she is well known for.

It is through this passion that we are able to achieve greater success and happiness in all we undertake in life.

This company was formed in 2009 after months of research into published versus self published authors. After much discussion and debate, it was unanimously decided to self publish. Traditionally it has been much more difficult for self published authors to reach the market place, we do believe this is the beginning of a new era in the publishing of book. The company encompasses all aspects of publishing including copyright, editing, printing, distribution, marketing and of course the writing. If you are seeking a real challenge in life, I would strongly recommend that you try this. Self published authors still have a long way to go before they garner the respect of others in the industry. For the time being, we have more guts than glory.
