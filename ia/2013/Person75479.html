Jason bays' Summary

My biggest strength is my greatest weakness, i am a terminal daydreamer. Daydreamers look at the world and see potential and possibilities, not limitations and boundaries.

The creative process should be an exploratory process and should not be hindered by limitations but meant to challenge them. The creative process should always begin and end in discovery.

Challenging the boundaries and limits and seeking adventure and discovery in the world is where the human spirit thrives and is what the mind requires and the heart desires.

That is why I am a designer, and illustrator an artist, that is why i do what i do.

Specialties: Graphic design, Illustration, Packaging, Web Design, print production, building things out of nothing, figuring things out.

Curiosities are pushing me more into product and toy design, game design, new interactive technologies and digital publishing. Recently finished work on volume one of the Adventures of Kung Fu RObot: http://bit.ly/kungfurobot
