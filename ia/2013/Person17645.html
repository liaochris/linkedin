Brian O'Neill's Summary

Brian O'Neill has been an entrepreneur for 30 years.1984 he started his first business O'Neill's Lawn Service in Bridgewater Massachusetts at the age of 21. After that he started New England Turf-Tech and B.J.O Courier Service which he delivered lost luggage for the major airlines out of Logan Airport in Boston MA. His dream was to live in Florida and on June of 1998 he made his dream a reality. He moved to Venice FL where he met his wife Sol O'Neill and they married on January 5, 2002. They started Housemaids (www.gohousemaids.com) on January 1, 2005 and he started O'Neill's Lawn Service (www.oneillslawnservice.com) on January 1, 2009.
