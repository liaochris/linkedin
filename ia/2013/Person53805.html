Howie Isaacks' Summary

At Kinetic Technology Group, I work with customers from a variety of industries: creative, construction, law firms, trade shows, restaurants, and many others. We provide IT support services for mostly Mac using companies, but we provide support for a lot of Windows users as well. Cross platform? No problem! We love making all of your technology get along and play nicely. Our main area of focus is contract IT services. We are experts in Kerio products such as Kerio Connect (an Exchange alternative), Kerio Control (firewall). Check out kerio.com to learn more about these products. We have teamed up with Jamf Software to provide complete management solutions for laptops, desktops, servers, and mobile devices.

I have been a Mac user since 1986, and a Mac IT support professional since 2000. While working various IT jobs over the years, I have had a lot of great mentors who have helped me develop solid Mac and Windows skills. I never received formal technical training. Most of what I have learned has been learned on the job and from my own curiosity and intense interest in technology. I am passionate about helping people use their technology to be successful.

If you're looking for a dedicated, IT consultant who is obsessed with providing great service, you've found him! Don't settle for working with IT guys who don't know anything about Macs. We offer complete IT solutions that include your servers, networking, desktops, laptops, and your mobile devices. Don't forget backups. We have great backup solutions too.

We have a great iOS development team. Look for Responder for Kerio Connect, Gmail, and Exchange on the iOS App Store. The apps allow you to setup your out of office auto responder from your iPhone. We have more great ideas on the way!
