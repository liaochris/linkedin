Marietta Delene Williams' Summary

Marietta is an independent concept illustrator and caricaturist from Kansas City, Missouri. She holds a bachelor’s in illustration summa cum laude with additional honors and has over ten years of experience in creating art for people just like you! As a timid fifteen-year-old with aspiring hopes of drawing full time, she applied for a job as a caricature artist at a local theme park. That job saw her blossom into a career-oriented young adult who would eventually climb the corporate ladder. But that simply wasn’t enough! In February 2011, after seven years with Kaman’s Art Shoppes, Marietta left her job to freelance full time, giving her complete attention to those interested in a more direct connection with the work she produces.

Today, Marietta offers her concept design and caricaturing services to individuals and businesses alike. Her fluid mark-making and textured concept designs lend a traditional feel to her digital creations while her quick wit with a sharpie and a color stick creates a completed, airbrushed-looking caricature in five minutes flat. Because her professionalism, style and friendliness proceeds her, Marietta’s works have been sold to a worldwide audience, while local clients include PetCo, Washington University, Lions Club, Shtick USA, and Hallboy Comics to name a few.

Marietta still resides in Kansas City today, married to her high school sweetheart. They own a tabby, a white Siamese and a Lhasa Apso, all loving rescue pets. When not drawing, Marietta volunteers with the Excelsior Springs Friends of Animals and The Animal Rescue Alliance (T.A.R.A.), focusing on pet rescue, health and adoption. She also leads local religious circle and has created a voice for the Pagan community through her blog Witchy Words. Her favorite hobbies include retro gaming, running and thrift store shopping.
