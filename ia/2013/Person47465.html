Grzegorz Ciepiel's Summary

I am an independent entrepreneur and IT versatilist. My activities are in general twofold. First, I engage in classic IT system development projects for end-customers using SW development resources located typically nearshore in Poland. Second, I engage in partnerships with other entrepreneurs for turning promising business ideas into successful products by providing technical leadership to the projects.

Technical leadership, Entrepreneurship, Enterprise Architecture, IT Architectural paradims and patterns, Outsourcing and nearshoring of SW development.
