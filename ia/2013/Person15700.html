Bonnie Low-Kramen's Summary

Services include:

* Workshop Instructor in your city
* Corporate Trainer for Admin Staffs & Managers
* Keynote Speaker
* Private Coach & Consultant
* Bulk orders available for the book "Be the Ultimate Assistant"

I love to teach and speak about the ways to be the “ultimate assistant.” One of my dreams is to provide training so that every assistant who wants a good job has the skills and the tools to get one. And when that happens, employers and businesses benefit as well. Today’s workplace has plenty of room for improvement and I want my work to be part of the solution to make things better and clearer through training and education. The vision is for the relationships between assistants and employers to be highly productive, respectful, and mutually beneficial which is one of the reasons I am a raving fan of Sheryl Sandberg's "Lean In" movement.

In April, 2013, I led a 4 hour workshop for the 100 admins at Starbucks in Seattle. What made it extra special was that it represented the first time that there had ever been any programming specifically for the admin partners. They are now building a series of programs for the admins because they clearly see the ROI. Many other companies know it is smart business to invest in the admin staff.

Starbucks CFO Troy Alstead commented, "Thank you for an empowering and inspiring inaugural workshop. The feedback to our leadership teams has been tremendous! They are energized & highly motivated to utilize what they have learned."
