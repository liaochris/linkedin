Harriett Hardman's Summary

I'm so happy to be back freelancing again. After spending five years in corporate jobs, I'm back on my own, looking for some interesting new projects. I've put in my time at all of the industry leaders (Google, Amazon, Apple) and I'm absolutely the best at writing content from scratch or organizing and cleaning up existing information sets. I'm happiest when I'm up to my neck in unruly collections of data or rambling, disorganized content that needs to be pared and focused and clarified. I'm totally self-organizing and self-motivating -- give me a project and a deadline and I deliver.

I've worked with all of the major tools, platforms and frameworks. The list of technologies that I haven't used is pretty short. I've got a strong engineering background and I'm a quick study. I'm also obsessive about completeness and accuracy.

I'm working on the bleeding edge of new content delivery systems, where technical writing and training are beginning to blur together as people demand content that is more accessible and easier to use, integrating multimedia, text, graphics, animations... you name it. Integral to this are new online delivery tools and formats. Check out http://appsofacto.com or http://churchmusiciansrock.com to see where I'm heading with this.

Specialties: Writing, organizing and editing information for maximum usability and impact, technical documentation and training, organizing large information sets, reorganizing/editing articles and papers
