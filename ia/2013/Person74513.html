Ken Tarwood's Summary

I began my career as a self-taught web developer and general IT technician in 1999, and have since worked in roles as diverse as mobile app development, search marketing and database administration, culminating in my current leadership role in digital marketing.

I've benefited from working with lean development teams early in my career. For example, having never enjoyed the privilege of having a dedicated DBA on staff, I was forced to adapt my list of skills to include database design, management and maintenance. While some would see this type of climate as a disadvantage, I've always tried to approach these challenges as an opportunity to expand my overall industry knowledge.

I believe spending the formative years of my career "in the trenches" has uniquely prepared me for my management role. I am a firm believer that one can never be a truly effective leader unless one has worked in the same capacity as each of their employees at one point in their career. A project manager who can relate to their team members in this way can foresee potential roadblocks and deftly respond to shifting objectives.

I am now overseeing my current employer's newest digital marketing initiatives including social media marketing, mobile app development and several improvements to our CRM infrastructure.
