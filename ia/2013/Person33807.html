Steve White's Summary

Over the last few decades, I have been blessed with the ability to serve a wide cross section of clients. From launching new construction subdivisions and multi-million dollar 1031 exchanges to luxury estate, short sales & traditional resale homes, I have the ability to serve just about any type of client that I might come across.

My clients deserve honest and direct communication, transactions that are free from defects and to be respected. On top of that, they want results. That is why I work diligently to stay ahead of my competition with regards to technology. I am fulfilled when I know that I have accomplished my client's goals and objectives.
