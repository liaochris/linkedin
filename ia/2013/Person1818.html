Jonathan Carroll's Summary

I have several years of experience helping children and adults meet personal, professional and educational goals. My specialty is in assisting clients with ADD/ADHD, Executive Functioning deficits, school functioning concerns (organization, study strategies, etc.), life coaching, and Special Education (learning disabilities and social/emotional concerns). As an adult managing my own ADHD, I help my clients achieve success through a variety of effective strategies and techniques.

I received my B.A. in Communication from DePaul University in Chicago and a M.A. in Learning Disabilities with additional studies in Social/Emotional Disorders from Northeastern Illinois University in Chicago. My graduate thesis examined ADD/ADHD and the use of medication along with alternative intervention strategies.

I founded and manage Carroll Educational Group (www.carrolleducationalgroup.com) and IEP Experts (www.iepexperts.com). Additionally, I am on the clinical staff at The Child, Adolescent and Family Development Center. I am serving as the PR and Marketing Chair on the Board of Directors for the ACO (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder Coaches' Organization). My other professional memberships include CEC (Council for Exceptional Children), CHADD (Children and Adults with Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder), ADDA (Attention Deficit Disorder), LDA (Learning Disabilities Association), COPAA (Council of Parent Attorneys and Advocates) and IPA (Illinois Psychological Association).

I regularly speak at conferences and seminars highlighting current trends in education. I've been a featured guest on ABC, FOX and NBC newscasts in Chicago discussing various topics on education.

I live in Northbrook with my wife and two children.

More About Us...

Carroll Educational Group was founded by Jonathan Carroll
to help individuals of all ages improve personal, academic
and professional performance. We service the following
communities in Illinois around the Chicagoland area on the
North Shore and Northern Suburbs including Buffalo Grove,
Highland Park, Lake Forest, Lake Bluff, Deerfield,
Libertyville, Wilmette, Winnetka, Glenview, Glencoe,
Kenilworth, Lincolnshire, Bannockburn, Northfield, Evanston
and Skokie. We also have a downtown Chicago office at 30 N. Michigan Avenue to serve Lincoln Park, Hyde Park, The Gold Coast, Lake View and Ravenswood Areas.

We teach clients a variety of effective strategies that are
proven to assist with ADD/ADHD, Executive

learning disabilities, and social/emotional needs.

Support for clients is also provided through the involvement
