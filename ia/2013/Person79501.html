Leah Baade's Summary

Leah Baade is a digital media marketing professional with a specialization in high converting copywriting, search engine optimization and social media management. She guides business leaders on how to integrate content marketing, SEO, digital media and social technologies into their overall business strategy. Leah has worked across numerous sectors internationally, including finance, real estate and development, travel and even non-profit. She achieves measurable results through comprehensive social media and content marketing audits, strategic planning and ongoing advice and training. Leah is named as one of the top 25 female SEO bloggers and is an active guest blogger for many top Internet Marketing blogs.
