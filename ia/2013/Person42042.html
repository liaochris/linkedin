      SCORE is a national volunteer organization supported by the US Small Business Administration. The East Central Illinois Chapter (ECI-SCORE) is headquartered at the Chamber of Commerce in Champaign, IL, and its volunteers include experienced business owners and managers who volunteer their expertise and knowledge to help small business owners and potential business owners achieve success through workshops and free confidential counseling.

      ECI-SCORE was opened for business in 1981 as a branch of the Decatur, IL SCORE chapter. It began with 12 volunteers serving 3 counties.       Convenient — Receive advice by email or arrange a meeting with a Counselor.
