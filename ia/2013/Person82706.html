Louise Gardiner's Summary

What I do
I help companies of all sizes promote their products and services through print & the web. As well as supporting end-user clients, I am also asked by marketing and PR agencies to partner with them on graphic design and digital projects for their clients.

Corporate Identity and Print Solutions
Whether you’re starting a new business or looking to revamp your existing image, I can help you develop a brand and/or logo that is both professional as well as geared towards your target audience. In today's competitive marketplace, a strong brand is critical in projecting your product or services.

Web Design & Development
I am experienced in designing and building user friendly, highly accessible attractive websites, that take account of both the client's and the end-user's needs. I cater for businesses of all sizes and develop sites for all types of companies and organisations, ranging from simple Brochure type websites to more sophisticated database driven Content Managed websites.

Email Marketing
Marketing through email is one of the most cost effective and powerful ways of reaching your customers. HTML email campaigns can prompt brand awareness, increase sales, and encourage visits to your website. I can manage the whole process from design through to broadcasting, tracking and reporting.
