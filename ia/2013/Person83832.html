Lysha Holmes' Summary

Thank you for taking time to view my profile.

I am best known for being discerning, honest and discreet within the R2R marketplace.

My style is pragmatic, fun and transparent.

R2R is unfortunately one of the most notoriously poor recruitment sectors out there- how do I make sure what I do is different to the others?

"TRUST QUI"

Since 2005, Qui Recruitment have provided a discreet and proactive service to the Recruitment to recruitment sector across the Northwest.

If you are looking for someone for find you CV's or to place your "bum on a seat", you need to look elsewhere as that is NOT what we do!!

At Qui Recruitment we are genuinely motivated by getting the right long term fit, not a quick fee or "fastest finger first".

We prefer to partner businesses and career focused recruitment professionals who appreciate that the more discerning you are, the better the result for all. We like to think we are complimentary to YOUR own network.

Qui Recruitment is a confidential job seeker led service, effectively providing a reverse search style. We act as the clients' ambassadors when we interview potential employees who are looking for a move- all of whom have been recommended to Qui Recruitment.

We don't trawl job boards or headhunt happy people.

Very simple and it must work- all of my 80+ endorsements validate this.

YOY, over 80% of our placements were exclusive to Qui, proving we work best with committed and exclusive people!

At Qui Recruitment, we have 38 yrs+ of recruitment experience- certainly well positioned to assist recruitment professionals at all levels.

I have become a "Champion" of Working Mums in recruitment, by being one, employing one and placing them!

We only place in R2R thus have NO conflict of interest to any of our clients!

Guest blogger on The Undercover Recruiter.

Owner of "ethical R2R", "Working Mums in Recruitment" and "Interns in Recruitment" groups on Linkedin. Invitation only.
