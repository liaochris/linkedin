CP PLUS GmbH & Co. KG.'s Summary

CP PLUS is latest Global offering from CP PLUS GmbH & Co. KG.

CP PLUS is the hallmark of German Engineering & Designing.

Cost & Performance are key benchmarks of the brand, where-in Aesthetics are the Add-on Advantage. CP PLUS models are launched after rigorous Research & Development by the Tech cell of CP PLUS GMBH & Co. KG., located in Germany, with the highest quality yardstick.

CP PLUS products are manufactured under strict quality control in accordance with International Security Standards in its factories located in the Far-East.

CP PLUS offers complete array of electronic security products in terms of Reliability, Performance & Aesthetics / Cutting edge German Technology / Best Cost & Performance / Highly Qualified Technical Support Team.

CP PLUS with offices in Germany, Czech Republic, UAE, Taiwan, Hong Kong, China & India has ensured that brand CP PLUS available in more than 40 countries across the globe including Indian subcontinent, Middle-East ASIA, South Asia, South-East ASIA, East Europe, Russia, South America & Africa…

Specialties

CP Plus offers an array of Cameras, DVRs, DVR cards, Speed Dome Cameras & Lens with its best of class - Range & value proposition. CP Plus would continue to add value to its products and offerings, to support its ambitious organic growth. Hence the emphasis is always on Strategic Inputs, Business Process Optimization, Technical developments & Quality assurance.
