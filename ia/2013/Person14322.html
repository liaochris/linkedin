I am looking to further the growth companies in the Gift, Home Decor, Tabletop & Stationery Industry by assisting Industry Executives and Suppliers find the right opportunities.

Our firm specializes in recruiting executives for Gift, Home Decor, Tabletop & Stationery Industry. Our goal is to provide impact Executives for your organization. Giftjobs.biz has been serving the Gift, Stationery and Home Décor Industries for over 30 years.

We know the skills required for any senior and mid-level executive position for which your firm may have a need and we guarantee our candidates in writing. We fully understand that a company is only as strong as its people.

Our Industry network encompasses over 300,000 individuals: This means your search period is shorter, your candidate choice is better and your new executives will have a lasting impact on the growth of your Company.

The next time you find your organization needing an executive, ensure that you get the best; with the specific experience and personality that promotes a long, happy, and successful relationship.

We accomplish your search with complete confidentiality.

Specialties

30 years experience in the industry provides us with a unique understanding of the qualities necessary to fill executive postions from CEO to Director of Product Development and from VP of Sales and Marketing to Regional Sales Managers.
