Graphic Designer

Net2Phone

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; NTOP; Telecommunications industry

September 2000 – August 2001 (1 year) Newark, New Jersey

While with Net2Phone (N2P), I designed packaging, ads, brochures, displays, posters, and various marketing materials for VOIP products. N2P offered many fun projects, from ski promotions to NASCAR car wraps, but their inadequate archival process led to technical challenges. Short on server space, N2P’s Creative Department stored their archived projects on CDs. These CDs were rarely labeled. Projects also contain missing or duplicate fonts. Unsatisfied with the status quo, I initiated, developed, and implemented an efficient solution for font maintenance and job archival on a department level. I utilized software to build a database of the existing CD library and developed a universal font library. This led to quick project execution and eliminated technical issues.

I currently continue to utilize the production experience I gained during my tenure with CAG.
