Lisa Capezzuoli's Summary

Lisa is a well rounded and traveled graphic designer originally from Tuscany. She has successfully achieved an honours diploma in Graphic Design at the European Institute of Design in Rome.
However, her traveling spirit inspired her to study graphic design at the University of Canberra in Australia and at the School of Visual Arts in NYC. She also won a grant for an English course in Oxford and she worked in Germany to study German.

After working for international graphic-design agencies in Italy and Australia, namely Foote Cone & Belding in Sydney, InAREA Strategic Design and Modo Comunicazione & Artwo in Rome; she now works for the award-winning international design company Nicosia Creative Expresso Ltd in New York.
She has worked on projects for clients such as: P&G, D&G, Head&Shoulders, Kraft, Crystal Light, Kool Aid, Max Factor, Covergirl, Puma, Pantene, Dunhill, Gillette, Venus, Satin Care, Vidal Sasson, Tide, Clairol, Koleston, CVS, Olay, Banana Boat, Zirh, Tiger Balm, Campari, Safeguard and Sephora.
In Italy she has planned and designed for national clients such as: Italian Post; Italidea (a project for the Olympic games in Beijing 2008); FIVB Men’s World Championship Italy 2010; Enpam; Sara assicurazioni; Aedes Immobiliare spa; Unaprol; Gruppo Leonardo Caltagirone; Metro C spa; the exhibition “Domenico Modugno 2008”; Boeing-Italy; Minister of Environment and a project for winter Olympic games in Turin 2006.
Further she worked for “Modo Comunicazione”, an advertising company following the project for Artwo association, which produces recycle-design objects created by contemporary artists. They organized national art fairs and design courses for prisoners at “Rebibbia Prison” in Rome.

As a side endeavor she is also the Art Director of Commissione Giovani NY, (Italian Youth Commission of NY) an American-Italian non-profit organization, with the goals to preserve and promote the professional, cultural, and social networking of young Italian Americans.
