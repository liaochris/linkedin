Brandon Greenberg's Summary

#1 LinkedIn search result (globally) when querying: Dodd-Frank, Volcker Rule, and SEF Trading.

I am a Regulatory Compliance Consultant with 12+ years of experience advising financial institutions on compliance issues and assisting C-level and senior management with the program and project management of large regulatory change initiatives.

Since the passage of the Dodd-Frank Act, I have advised a number of clients on the Act, including: two potential Swap Execution Facilities, the NY Branch of UK-domiciled Global Bank, a Top 3 US Global Bank, among others. My work has centered on translating the Dodd-Frank Title VII and Volcker Rule requirements into executable tasks and developing defensible approaches to compliance against complex rules. Furthermore, I have developed Volcker Rule implementation strategies for both US and foreign financial institutions.
