David Forrest's Summary

Walt Disney and David Forrest not only share the same birthday but also a common philosophy to do things creatively and in an electrifying new way. David is the Chief Creative Officer and founder of Kinetic Underground Inc., a company involved in intellectual property design and product development.

In addition to conceptualized innovative new products and designs for properties such as Star Wars and Sesame Street David also created and managed “brand teams” to re‐imagine Abrams and Gentile Entertainment and MEGO’s classic library of properties into 21st century consumer friendly, value added Entertainment brands. Most notable is The Visionaries now in development as a major feature live action franchise from the producers of TRANSFORMERS, and The Micronauts. The Micronauts will be HASBRO'S follow up to the Transformers and G.I. Joe Franchise, and JJ ABRAMS is currently on board as the producer.
