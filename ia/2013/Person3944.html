Alex de Carvalho's Summary

Alex is passionate about creating spaces for individuals, companies, and brands to connect.

Best known as a social media professional, Alex advises organizations on their use of social networks to build brand relationships with key constituents and the community.

He is adept at business strategy, concept design, marketing communication, as well as leading the implementation of complex software development projects with distributed teams. He is also an expert in the use of social media monitoring to establish performance measurement.

Alex is the co-founder of BuzzMed (formerly VoxMed), a worldwide community for physicians and pharmaceutical companies to exchange scientific information.

Previously, he directed business development efforts at online media and mobile content companies and co-founded a leading European e-mail marketing provider. Prior to that, he advised blue-chip companies on strategic initiatives. Clients have included Air France, Chevron, General Motors, Corning Saint-Gobain, British Airways, Repsol, Visa, Le Parisien, NRJ Radio, Carat, Sanofi-Aventis, Nortel Networks, and the Finnish Venture Capital Association.

Based in Miami, Florida, Alex has helped unite South Florida's tech community by founding The Startup Forum, Social Media Club, BarCamp, Ignite, Social Media Day, and Mobile Monday events for South Florida new media professionals; he is also a founding member of RefreshMiami. Over the past six years, these organizations have hosted events for over 10,000 professionals, leading to new business connections, employment opportunities, and startup ideas.

An accomplished public speaker, Alex has spoken nationally and internationally to a variety of audiences from C-Level industry executives to high school students. In addition to his career in consulting, Alex is an adjunct professor at the Universtity of Miami and recently co-authored “Securing the Clicks: Network Security in the Age of Social Media” with MacGraw-Hill.

Specialties

strategy, business planning, business development, project management, concept design, wireframing, brand and identity, marketing, communication, social media
