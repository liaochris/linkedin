Lorina Shinsato, ND, LAc's Summary

Primavita Family Medicine is guided by the principles of Awareness, Empowerment, and Choice. I believe that you are your best Doctor and through our partnership, I bring an awareness of the areas in your life that need more support, empower you with the tools and resources to make healthy changes to your lifestyle and finally encourage you to take charge of your health, choosing what is in alignment with your core beliefs and values.

You have all that it takes within you and I serve as a catalyst to help you rediscover and reconnect yourself with your body, your community and your environment. I look forward to meeting you!
