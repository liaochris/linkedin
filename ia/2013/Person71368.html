Justin Ruka's Summary

Currently, I am an undergraduate student at Lakeland College in Sheboygan, Wisconsin. My major is Computer Science, my minor being Resort Management. I am proudly holding a cumulative GPA of 3.9, and with my Bachelor of Arts degree, I hope to enter the themed-entertainment industry.

Since I was a young child, I’ve always had a fascination with themed environments. I’ve always pondered how they were made. Because of that, I eventually started to create my own themed experiences, including “Greywood Manor” – A Wisconsin Halloween Yard Haunt. A hobby that I work on year round, Greywood Manor is a unique, one of a kind haunt set up for the Halloween Trick-or-Treaters to visit free of charge. It’s an immersive, high quality experience complete with special effects and custom-made props all woven together with a unique storyline. What motivates me to create this? It’s the guest’s reactions – the surprise, joy, fear, and fun they have while visiting on Halloween night that make it worth the effort the 364 other days of the year.

Technology is something I’ve also been very intrigued by. In my opinion, it’s the perfect complement to my fascination with the themed-entertainment industry, as it is being used more and more in both high end, and local based attractions. I have stayed relevant in the technological community by having worked as a Summer IT Intern over various years at the Northern Ozaukee School District, as well as by holding my current position as the President of my school’s Association of Information Technology Professionals (AITP) Student Chapter. I also enjoy listening to podcasts in my spare time to stay current with industry’s news and rumors, such as various shows on the TWIT Network and Tech Stuff from HowStuffWorks.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
