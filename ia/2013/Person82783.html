Lou Pizante's Summary

I’m the Chief Financial Officer of GOOD, an online platform and social impact consultancy that brings together a global community of people, corporations and organizations who give a damn.

I'm also a partner of Cobble Hill Partners, a boutique private equity and consulting firm focused on capital markets, technology and legal services. Prior to this, I was CEO of Mavent Inc., an automated compliance engine for the financial services industry, which was acquired by Ellie Mae, Inc. (NYSE: ELLI). I have also held positions at Greenwich Capital Markets, Nomura Securities, Goldman Sachs & Co. and Deloitte & Touche, LLP.

I’m passionate about education. I’m a co-founder of Escuelita Cultural, a non-profit that develops educational multimedia and programs geared towards dual language learning for children and their parents. I’m the founder and former chairman of Put It Up, Inc., a non-profit partnership with LA County and the LA Clippers that provides financial literacy training to “at risk” youth.

What excites me?

• Smart companies and organizations that look to be wildly successful by rethinking their markets and changing the world.

• Hyper-intelligent teams where I can learn from my colleagues, I can teach them what I know and we’re able learn things together.

• Leadership roles where I’m needed to execute on big ideas. There are a lot of big ideas. Execution is what matters, and I specialize in execution.
