Caleb Christensen D.C.'s Summary

Lubbock has always been home for my wife, Lindsay and I. Both of us attended Lubbock Christian University and then moved to Dallas to pursue graduate work. We are glad to be back in the Hub City where we plan to establish a home, business, and family. We were drawn back to Lubbock because of the community feel and family values as well as the potential for growth.

I graduated from Parker Chiropractic College in Dallas, Tx. Then, I had the opportunity to work in an established chiropractic office with an experienced doctor. Through this job, I was exposed to the highest quality of chiropractic and wellness care as well as exceptional office management. I am passionate about chiropractic because, not only does it help relieve pain, but it also can improve a person’s overall health and quality of life.

Lindsay and I enjoy spending time with family and friends, playing sports, and serving in our local church. I am excited about using my chiropractic education and experience in order to best serve the people of Lubbock and the surrounding areas.

Specialties

At Texas Chiropractic Wellness, we seek to provide our
patients with high quality chiropractic care that is designed to
specifically address their symptoms. Because the causes of
each patient’s symptoms are different, we offer a variety of
treatments and therapies. Each treatment is designed to relieve
pain, promote healing, and improve overall spinal function.
