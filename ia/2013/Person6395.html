Andrea Driessen's Summary

Andrea Driessen, Chief Boredom Buster at No More Boring Meetings, award-winning business owner, and columnist on meeting engagement, has been serving the meetings industry for over 20 years.

When we team up, you'll gain access to an insider speaker & entertainer network to stage events with transformational experts, compelling meeting formats and out-of-the-box engagement tools.

The result: Attendees are fully awake, radically engaged, and able to perform at their very best.

details: www.NoMoreBoringMeetings.com

Specialties: Boosting audience engagement in critical business meetings by helping meeting planners find outside talent (keynoters, corporate comedians, MCs). Improving presentation skills of internal speakers, so everyone returns to work more productive.

Integrating purposeful play & gamification into events.

Industries served include high tech, finance, construction, pharma, healthcare, associations & more. Clients include Starbucks, Microsoft, the US government, Verizon Wireless & hundreds of others.

What’s the big problem with most meetings?

Boring, unproductive and “business as usual” come to mind.

Enter No More Boring Meetings—the solution to the meeting problem.

We transform business meetings—from dull, disconnecting snoozers into engaging, rip-roaring events that boost productivity and inspiration.

Every element of our meeting design is aligned with your most important business goals. Audiences are fully awake and able to learn all they need to be more productive when they return to work. We also help you integrate social media so these tools truly ENGAGE, rather than disconnect.

We treat your mission-critical meetings like SHOWS that are as engaging as a rock concert—and as captivating as a division-winning baseball game.
