Aaron Trank's Summary

Overview:

I've been a full time Christian minister since 2008 when I left my previous career as a software engineer and web designer in order to spend more time focused on my calling. My life if lived to the fullest! To get my newsletter please go to http://aarontrank.com and request them!

Ministry:

I am currently the Director of Recruitment and Minister-At-Large out of our Jews for Jesus International Headquarters in San Francisco. I serve as Strategic Adviser to our Executive Director. Our goal is to make the Messiaship of Jesus an unavoidable issue to our Jewish people worldwide, and I accomplish that goal through mobilizing new people to serve in ministry. If you are ever in the Bay Area, and would like to hear about our work (or take a tour of our facilities) please email me at: aaron.trank@jewsforjesus.org

Speaking:

I am frequently invited to speak in churches, and at college and high school groups about the Jewish roots of the Christian Faith, and about Jewish Evangelism. My specialties are listed below. If you would like to book me as a speaker please email me at: aaron.trank@jewsforjesus.org

Other Projects:

I am involved in many other projects besides my work with Jews for Jesus. Moving to San Francisco has allowed me to connect with many other Entrepreneurs, and to collaborate with many great people on many awesomely cool projects! I still do some project management, programming, web design, branding, and consulting, but often I simply help others make the necessary connections to build their teams! Maybe we will work together some day!
