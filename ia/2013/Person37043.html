Dorothy Tannahill Moran's Summary

A delightful fusion of unique, useful insight & hard core management & career expertise. I help people discover their professional potential. Many of us feel a sense that we could accomplish so much more with our careers and yet sometimes the results don’t seem to match what we think is possible. We usually don’t have the skills, knowledge or guidance to adequately navigate our career. Sure, we went to school to obtain a degree in our area of expertise but that didn’t include finding and managing the career.
I can help professionals develop and execute strong career goals and then provide them guidance on how to handle such things as:
- How to work well with your boss and management
- Effective interactions with difficult people
- How to navigate your work culture and internal politics
- How to authentically communicate and build networks, even if you’re an introvert like me
- Develop leadership qualities that will support career growth, no matter what direction you intend
- Development of management skills to lead and inspire

With over 21 years in management at Intel and diverse staff mix, I have coached, guided and trained others at all levels to go on to achieve impressive results and careers. These people have gained promotions, doubled their salary, achieved executive status and obtained broad professional recognition. I have traversed innumerable situations making my coaching unique and well informed.

Additionally, I’m a change practitioner which means that I’m certified to guide individuals and organizations at a personal or professional level to make meaningful changes that stick. At Intel I was known for large scale, comprehensive changes including systems, process and organizational redesign. My success with these was due to focus on each individual- recognizing change happens at that level.

If you’re in management, small business or an individual contributor that is ready to impact the business and your career in a positive way, let’s talk.
