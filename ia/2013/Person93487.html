Michelle Keib's Summary

Michelle demonstrates the character, creativity, and drive necessary to bring opportunities to reality -- with spectacular results. Combining her formal marketing education with a phenomenal track record in real-world sales, Michelle has proven to be a true champion of small business throughout her career. A highly sought-after speaker on entrepreneurship, her experience spans the hospitality, contract furniture, relocations/logistics, advertising and consumer packaged goods industries, in addition to an impressive run as executive director of a not-for-profit. Michelle is a winner of the CNY Business Journal 2008 Women in Business award and a 2009-10 VIP Professional Woman of the Year as named by the National Association of Professional Women, and in 2013 was recognized by the SBA as the Syracuse Region Exporter of the Year.

Michelle is an accomplished artist whose paintings are represented by C.L. Sherman's Gallery in Newport, RI. She is a cantor in the St. Elizabeth Ann Seton choir.
