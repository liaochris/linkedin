Karen Warner's Summary

Since 1995, Karen Warner has worked as a commercial real estate broker, tenant representative and advisor, representing tenants in managing their office space requirements. She excels at covering all the details of the office leasing process without losing sight of the big picture, finding creative solutions for each business’ unique real estate needs.

Karen’s skill and experience as a commercial real estate broker has helped many businesses manage smooth transitions to new locations. Her extensive industry knowledge and unique talent as a tenant representative allows her to expertly assist clients in finding the ideal office space and negotiating the best lease terms available.

Ms. Warner’s real estate career includes experience in corporate lease negotiation, build-to-suit development, sale-leaseback transactions, and owner-occupied real estate sales. She has represented tenants and buyers in every type of office space, including call centers, medical office, and government/institutional office space.

Her current focus is on working with tenants and buyers seeking office space in downtown and suburban Boise, and throughout the state of Idaho.

In 2008, Karen received her CCIM (Certified Commercial Investment Member) Designation and she is an SIOR-candidate.

Karen lives and works in downtown Boise and enjoys skiing, mountain biking and hiking. She is married to Mark Warner and has two children and two step-children.
