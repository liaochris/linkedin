David Yahalom's Summary

I'm a CTO, DBA group manager, lead architect and senior hands-on database consultant/DBA living my passion.

I have over eleven years of experience in database & information systems complete architecture design, leading database teams and as a database consultant and senior DBA.

- Senior manager, founder and CTO in charge of the technological vision and Oracle/NoSQL/BigData delivery of Israel's largest database consulting company managing over 15 people.

- Responsible for end to end delivery of database related projects and leading teams in various complex and diverse enterprise-level environments.

- Providing support, troubleshooting and consulting for over 50 Oracle customers across the world.

- The go-to-guy for pre-sales technical discussions for Oracle Israel.

- Providing end-to-end database solutions from schema design and database tuning to infrastructure aspects (network, storage, servers).

- In-depth experience with Oracle database tuning for very large and high-TPS databases for both real time and batch environments (100s of TBs in size, 100,000 TPS) including the world's largest TELCO billing database.

- In-depth experience with schema design and ERD models for large scale applications.

- Designing and supporting critical 24X7 Oracle, Hadoop, PostgreSQL and MySQL systems.

- In-depth experience with Oracle GoldenGate for realtime BI, zero downtime database upgrades as well as ACTIVE/ACTIVE high-availability.

- Planning and implementing complex Oracle RAC and DRP (DataGuard, GoldenGate and storage replication) solutions.

- Leading Oracle Security projects including: DB Hardening, TDE, Oracle Database Vault, Sentrigo Database Firewall, strong authentication, externally identified users.

- In-depth knowledge in writing software and scripting in several languages.

- In-depth experience with multiple Business Intelligence platforms.
