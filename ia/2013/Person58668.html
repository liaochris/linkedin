Japhy Grant's Summary

I love to fuse new and emerging technologies with classic storytelling and build passionate teams of creative people dedicated to transforming big ideas into products and experiences that anyone can enjoy.

It's my focus and passion; helping individuals, brands and networks tell their story and grow their audience through digital media.

I'm currently the Digital Producer for "America's Funniest Home Videos", where I've developed and produced new shows, UGC platforms and social campaigns to extend the reach of ABC's longest-running primetime television show.

Some other fun things I've developed and produced:

- Pestami, a digital video brand for pet and animal lovers that has grown to be the #1 professional pets channel on YouTube.

- Won Intel's Idea Jam, a 48-hour contest to develop and pitch the next "big idea" in digital entertainment.

- My independently produced web series, "Foodies" was featured on the Today Show and lambasted by New York Times food critic Sam Sifton.

I love making new professional acquaintances. Feel free to reach out if you want to discuss digital media, technology or where to find a great taco in L.A.
