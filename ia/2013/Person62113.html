Jenny Munn's Summary

As an independent SEO consultant, I work with small business owners and corporate clients to set and implement their search engine optimization strategy. I also am passionate about training solopreneurs, WordPress web designers, and other online marketing service providers how to do SEO for themselves and their clients.

My main services and products are:

• Free SEO training via my monthly e-newsletter, videos and blogging
• Live Atlanta SEO training workshops
• Providing both free and premium SEO guides and books
• Consulting and training for marketers and businesses
• SEO speaker at events around town

I love SEO for many reasons. One of those being that I believe it’s a very under-utilized, misunderstood business development tactic that works with a little smart strategy (it’s how I successfully grew my freelance business). I come from a copywriting background, so my specialty within the SEO field is SEO copywriting, content strategy and keyword research.

Specialties: Search Engine Optimization (SEO), SEO Copywriting, Content Marketing, Content Strategy, Business Communications, SEO Coaching and Training, Speaking, Presentation and Communications Skills, Blogging, Relationship Sales, Content Marketing
