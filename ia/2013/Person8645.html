Constance Ard's Summary

Since launching my Information Consulting business I have had several wonderful things to occur. The most recent is the publication of my second book: Adding Value to Corporate Libraries and Information Services by the Ark Group in the United Kingdom.

I am excited to be working with several clients to help them with new startup ventures or to transition traditional business models into new arenas that will ensure their longevity and grow their revenue.

Small businesses in the Louisville Kentucky area are anxious to take advantage of social media for their marketing and customer engagement purposes. I provide those businesses a strategic approach to social media that combines excellent content, with specific actions that keep them in the forefront of their customers and potential customers.

Information and knowledge are the driving forces in today's economy. I work with clients to help solve enterprise search and content issues that creates a competitive edge.

Constance Ard's Projects

  • Gourmet De Ville Microsite

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Constance Ard, Jasmine Ashton, Stephen E. Arnold

    In order to provide our client, a small craft liqueur producer, with increased search engine visibility, I worked with my ArnoldIT team to create a sophisticated and attractive news service called Gourmet De Ville.

    As the editor and manager of the blog, I assigned articles to a team of writers who filled the site with recipes and profiles designed to position our client's product as a high-quality and versatile spirit and to associate them with powerful semantic terms such as “craft,” “gourmet,” and “artisan.”

    We also produced content to feed the client's Facebook page and other social media channels.

    The result: Articles created by ArnoldIT were indexed by global search services. After a few months, our client's key product was associated with desired semantic concepts. A Google search for the phrase “pistachio liqueur” returned links to our client in the top three positions while they were at the top of search results for “craft liqueur.”

    With the Augmentext program working in sync with the company’s traditional marketing efforts, the brand and the company’s own digital platforms became more visible and findable.
