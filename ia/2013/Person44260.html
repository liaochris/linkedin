Résumé de Frederic Brunel

Accomplished CTO, technical director and chief software architect. Over 15 years of experience in building consumer software products: 3D games, SaaS platforms, web and mobile applications. Strong experience in business, software engineering, entrepreneurship, product design, staffing and management.

I'm product-driven and I have proven track record of delivering highly-technical products in challenging fast-paced environments. I always sit between business, design and engineering and I excel at translating product vision into practical solutions. I have a strong experience leading teams of software engineers and product designers.

As the former partner and CTO of WhereCloud, I have lead the development of more than 25 exclusives mobile apps for iOS and Android for some of the biggest brands in Canada. I have also lead the development of an SaaS mobile analytics & marketing platform.

I'm looking for CTO or VP Engineer positions.
