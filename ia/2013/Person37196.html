Doug LaRue's Summary

Time and road infused blend of business, creative and technical skills. Detail oriented project manager with strategic and tactical experience producing and directing a wide range of unique film, photo, video, brand and product design projects.

My projects have ranged from feature films with academy award winning actors and documentaries in the Andes Mountains of South America to photographing sports and news for United Press International and Associate Press, producing music videos, writing and directing films, architectural design of an 18,000 sq. ft. commercial retail center to publishing an arts & entertainment magazine and producing books about the University of Texas and Notre Dame.

One of my current passions is helping to brand and re-educate the public about water conservation and pollution. I am working with a new company that has the potential to revolutionize the management and cleaning of commercial and municipal waste water.

Specialties

Concept, logo and brand Design, Illustration, Photography, Cinematography, HD and Red One 4k+ Post Production Design...

Doug LaRue's Experience

CEO

Glaze Creative

Partnership; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 1994 – Present (19 years 10 months) Austin, Texas

Glaze is a concept driven creative media studio founded in 1994 providing award winning media design for video, print and web products. Our experience in video production, corporate communications, advertising and marketing, interactive technology, graphic design, feature film production, and documentary storytelling give us an exceptional combination of skills and capabilities to strategically focus on your projects.

Our highly trained designers excel at the deisign and production of Motion Graphics utilizing After Effects, PhotoShop, Illustrator, Motion, Shake and more. We also can author and produce BluRay DVD's in-house. With 25 years of professional experience we are dedicated to quality and success!

We are one of the few companies in Texas who own a RED ONE 4k camera system, not to mention our Panasonic and GoPro HD camera equipment.

Recent Glaze Studio news includes winning a Graphic Design USA 2012 Package Design Award.

Executive Producer

1996 – 2009 (13 years)

Our Social Networking experiment that began in 1996. Our print magazine Capitol City Arts & Entertainment created one of the first web magazines or online music communities in Texas. We had great writers like the legendary Rush Evans, Sharon Jones (Editor in Chief of Mike's Feedback Magazine), Kevin Dodds (recently wrote Edward Van Halen: A Definitive Biography)...

Director of Photography

Cinestar Films and Spectrum Films

Editor in Chief / Co Owner

As a partner in the company I was a key figure in all primary business dealings including venture capitol negotiations.
