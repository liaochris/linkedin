Steve Andersen's Summary

Our best strategies for positive change are worthless without brilliant, collaborative, always-learning teams who bring powerful ideas to reality. Creating environments that unleash amazing people is what I do.

I am a proven leader of hybrid organizations with deep technical ability, exceptional communication skills, and a record of success in entrepreneurial environments. For the last 12+ years I have been leading initiatives, teams, programs, and communities toward increased impact through the use of technology.
