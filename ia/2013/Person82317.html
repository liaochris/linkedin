Alexey Chistyakov's Summary

I’ve started my design career as a freelancer in 2005. When it became clear that I’m too old for a rock star in 2006 I’ve found a full-time designer job. All the knowledge I gained through years helps me a lot to make high quality, user-friendly GUI’s.

In 2008 I’ve decided that good web designer got to know at least basics of HTML/CSS. So I’ve started to learn it and I fell in love with web-programming.

In the same 2008 (right after iPhone hit the Russian market) I started to develop GUIs for mobile operation systems. I’ve developed GUI for a lot of useful apps leaving every customer extremely happy with a result.

I always draw attention of my potential employers to the fact that my approach to GUI design a little more scientific than creative. As opposed to the most of doubtless talented designers I’m not trying to make the World better, but more convenient.

I’m fond of music, sports, astronomy, and astrophysics. I have an open mind for fresh ideas, I always in search for something cool and new.
