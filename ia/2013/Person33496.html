Deb Lawrence's Summary

I am a contemporary artist and collector. Wendy Posner, of Poser Fine Art in L.A. represents me in L.A. and was instrumental in placing one of my paintings in the private collection of one of the directors of the J.Paul Getty Museum. I also was very fortunate to have one of the curators from MOCA Cleveland come to my studio this past month for a very positive and growing visit.

Like a well-worn and well-loved security blanket, my work takes Donald Winnicott’s notion of transitional objects one step further and focuses on what we say to ourselves and others to nurture security, mitigate distress, and become separate, unique individuals, comfortable in our own skin. I work directly on the floor, the canvas draped in my lap, as I fold, crease, tear, scribble and paint, imbuing them with a veritable soul.

I'm interested in finding other good homes for my work in the U.S. and abroad so please be sure to visit my website at www.DebLawrenceContemporary.com and contact me at deb@deblawrencecontemporary.com if you're interested in talking further.
