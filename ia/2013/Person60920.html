Jeff Galindo's Summary

Jeff Galindo is a principle at Real Estate Strategies LLC. Real Estate Strategies has provided sales and marketing consulting and operations support to builders, developers, and financial institutions throw-out North America including Canada and Mexico.

Jeff has held a number of senior national and regional sales and marketing positions in the home building industry, including Senior Vice President of Sales for Lennar Homes, National Vice President of Sales & Marketing for Pulte Homes, Del Webb. And was the western regional Vice President of Operation for New Home Star, an outsourced sales and marketing company, which provides comprehensive targeted business solutions to local and regional real estate developers and builder all across the US.

With nearly 20 years of professional industry experience Jeff brings both strategic and operational expertise to bear for employers and clients, as well as vast insight into the planning, marketing and sales of all types of new home neighborhoods. From traditional subdivisions, to custom homes, to mixed-use, high-density, an urban community, Jeff has had hands-on leadership experience in theme all. In addition, Jeff has particular experience in the development and sales of Lifestyle and Active Adult master plan communities.

Jeff is a true industry professional, top-notch communicator, and works well with a wide rang of teams and organizations.
