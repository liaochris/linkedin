Mike Mehrle's Summary

Professional Networker for the assembly and installation industry. My goal is to make an impact on an industry that has been unorganized for too long.

The United Assemblers Network is producing a certification program so the technicians in the industry can demand a higher pay; and so the manufacturers of the products we assemble, install or repair have qualified techs supporting their products.

The United Assemblers Network is looking for partners that are ready to bring the industry full circle and stop the recruitment of people whop call themselves technicians... that are usually found on the free listing services (not naming names, but you know who I am talking about).

Visit our website at http://unitedassemblers.com where you can get a lot more information.
