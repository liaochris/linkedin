Christa Geno's Summary

I am a multifaceted, multi-talented Northern VA professional. I own a film production company and work in marketing at a law film. My company, City Gates Productions, specializes in media production for corporations, web commercials, Short Films, and Feature films.
Media production includes complete services: scriptwriting, research, preproduction, studio and on location production, single and multi camera production, sound and music design, and editing.
I am a SWaM certified Woman owned business and an equal opportunity employer.
Other experiences include being a production coordinator, associate producer, and marketing associate.

Specialties: Film and Media Producing, Directing, Marketing, Writing.

Christa Geno's Projects

  • Taylor Law Company website videos

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Christa Geno, Michael Pollak, Scott Bruffey

    We produced, wrote, directed, shot, and edited 5 videos for Taylor Law Co. Here's what they had to say about our work:
    As with any new business, we needed a way to bring in clients. We also had done research about marketing techniques. We realized videos were the way to help potential clients get to know each of us when making a decision on whom to hire. Christa came recommended from a fellow attorney who does criminal work as she had done his firm video. While checking out numerous other videos, we found many of them to be cheesy, amateurish, and not good quality. The prior work by Christa and her production crew, Scott and Mike, was professional, informative, and good quality.
    I am glad that we selected Christa, Scott, and Mike to do our videos because we love the final product. Neither one of us really wanted to do videos or be seen and heard on camera. They promised us that we would not be on the spot and they would make it easy for us during the shoot. They were right. Except for the hot lights (which were needed to help the quality), we were comfortable. We almost forgot we were being recorded when we were talking. Looking at the final product, you cannot tell we were uncomfortable. We look sincere and honest.
    The editing and quality of the picture for the videos is really good. We really appreciate the time and attention that was put into preparing for the shoot as well as getting the videos finalized so we could use them to market our business.
    We would recommend Christa, Scott, and Mike to anyone trying to produce a good quality video for a website.
