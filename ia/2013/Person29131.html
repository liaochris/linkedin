Dan Bloom's Summary

One of Israel’s leading marketing and public relations professionals, Dan Bloom is an expert in promoting Israeli business to international customers, partners and audiences of all types.

Dan is originally from California, where he wrote copy for New York Times Company newspapers and helped to spearhead the international marketing of one of the country’s best-selling wine brands (since acquired by E.&J. Gallo Winery).

While working in the California wine industry, Dan helped to increase sales through a wide variety of marketing efforts, generating promotions, publicity, events, advertising, packaging, point-of-sale & marketing materials, tastings, awards, press releases, distributor communications, and public speaking engagements. Media relations efforts led by Dan and the team resulted in coverage by Wine Spectator, Wine X Magazine, Wine Business Monthly and other leading industry publications and general media.

Applying his well-developed marketing, communications and writing expertise locally, Dan has helped Israeli high-tech powerhouses such as Yair Shamir's VCON, Dov Moran's M-Systems, Eli Harari's SanDisk and Fanzila (a Blumberg Capital portfolio company) to attract the attention of customers, partners and major media organizations, such as TechCrunch, All Facebook, CNN, BusinessWeek and The Wall Street Journal.

Furthermore, by virtue of experience representing companies listed on the Paris Bourse (now NYSE Euronext) and NASDAQ, Dan is qualified and experienced in writing content for the financial community. He is an expert in generating material that contributes to the success of initial public offerings (IPOs), secondary offerings, partnerships, joint ventures and acquisitions.

Specialties

Text content, public relations, marketing writing, social media content, business plans, market research, corporate communications, press releases, media & sales kits, FAQs & messaging
