Jose Antonio Gelado's Summary

Journalist with a broad experience in Internet, IT and computers. More than 10 years of experience as radio journalist on main spanish networks like Cope and Cadena Ser and other radio stations. Freelance journalist in newspapers and magazines focused on IT, gamming, Internet and cyberculture at Publico, Consumer.es, Esquire, Screenfun, Ciberpais (El Pais)...

Project manager at Ya.com (Gameloft), journalist (Inicio.com) and other online media.
Ecommerce & online project development consultant.

Blogging since 2003 and pioneer podcaster in spanish since 2004 with Comunicando. Writer and author of some books, workshops, lectures and essays about podcasting.

Portfolio codesal.com
Blog: jagelado.com

Especialidades: Online media, social networks, contents, IT journalism, podcasting and web developing.
