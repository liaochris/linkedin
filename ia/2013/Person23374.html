Chris Hoch's Summary

*NMLS ID 886582

Over 27 years as a home lending/mortgage professional I’ve been passionate about helping people get the right home loan for their specific needs. Over time, I’ve been fortunate to work with fantastic people at Home Savings of America, Washington Mutual, Chase and EverBank.

After leaving Chase I learned about blogging, social media and online marketing. I found great resources and training from other online marketers and successful entrepreneurs.

I am an avid connector who thrives on cultivating relationships. I combine two great interests - networking and marketing in my mortgage planning business and online.

On the personal side I am an avid reader. My wife is the smartest person I know, my Daughter thinks she is. I like rugby and football. Years ago I worked at the San Diego Zoo Safari Park. We live in Gilroy, CA now and I volunteer at the Gilroy Garlic Festival where garlic is king.
