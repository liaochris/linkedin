His experience includes P&L management, mergers and acquisitions, M&A, business turnarounds, mobile apps, business development, global strategic alliances and partnerships, profit/productivity improvement, operations management, industrial engineering, subcontract manufacturing and internal auditing in industries that include: consumer products, industrial products, snack food, consumer packaged goods, semiconductor, TV and stereo manufacturing, diesel fuel injection systems, point of sale displays, foundries, paper mills and packaging operations.
