Daniel Upton's Summary

Summary of Presentation on “Cutting Edge BI Implementation:
"Ways to Deliver a High Quality Solution Faster”
...to the Association of IT Professionals, San Diego Chapter

Two major contributors to the success or failure of a Business Intelligence project are the level of involvement by business stakeholders and the overall time span from project inception until solution delivery to end users, and we will address ways to substantially improve both of them. We will explore the Lean principles of ‘just in time’, ‘just-enough-design’ and ‘test-driven development’, which harmonize a reasonable level of stakeholder involvement with reasonable increments of engineering deliverables. We will also introduce the Data Vault methodology as a way to accelerate progress into the critical, otherwise lengthy phases of data profiling, staging and historical change capture. In the end, we will have identified new ways to deliver requested solution increments more frequently and more aligned with business requirements as the business wants it now (rather than last quarter or last year).
