David Noble's Summary

Generalist with two decades of experience across the entire software development lifecycle, primarily in distributed systems.

Comfortable working with business stakeholders and executives, defining architecture, leading a team, and writing code.

Previous roles include chief architect for a business process software startup (successfully acquired) and cognizant engineer for a Mars rover mission operations subsystem. Work experience spans the domains of mobile search, consumer internet, consulting, business process management, defense, and space exploration.

Received several awards from NASA and JPL for development of telemetry processing and mission operations software for space exploration.

Specialties

Cloud computing, SOA, Java (SE and EE), Ruby on Rails, Python, Perl, and Linux.

Especially interested in using technology to improve distributed collaboration.
