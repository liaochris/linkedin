Michele Boudreau's Summary

Michele Boudreau is a high-producing real estate agent serving Silicon Valley communities. Prior to her real estate career, Michele spent many years in the life sciences industry. Her experience in marketing, investor relations, and communications has proven key to her ability to run a top-notch business and help her clients maximize and protect their real estate investments.

A native Californian and graduate of UC San Diego, Michele holds a Bachelor’s degree in Biochemistry. She has been a resident of Los Gatos for almost 20 years, and has two children who graduated from Los Gatos High. Now that her children are grown, she stays connected to the youth of the community by volunteering with CASA, and serving on the Town of Los Gatos’ Community and Senior Services Commission. As a town commissioner, she also represents Los Gatos on the Santa Clara County Housing and Community Development Advisory Committee (HCDAC).

Specialties: Helping homeowners and investors buy and sell properties in Silicon Valley
