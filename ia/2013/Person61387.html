Jeffrey Soplop's Projects

  • University Energy Optimization

    • October 2009 to March 2011
    Team Members: Jeffrey Soplop, Keith Smith, Scott Sandler, Angel Sustaeta

    This is a two tiered solution - working on supply and demand side.
    On the supply side, we worked to determine the best assets to put online based on current and forecast electric price as well as current and forecast electric, steam, and chilled water usage. Savings were realized by changing the allocation of assets based on changing conditions. No other capital improvements were required outside of optimization solution.

    On the demand side, we built baselines on building energy usage. This baseline would provide expected energy usage independent of changes due to weather. The baselines were useful to determine amount of savings realized from individual building improvements. Other reports detailing building chilled water and steam usage realized significant savings.
