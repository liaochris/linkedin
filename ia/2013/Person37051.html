Dorris Burch's Summary

I empower women to embody their divine feminine essence, their soul richness, their calling. Women who are willing to embrace the courage to own their true voice.

I believe that when women truly value who they are they can become financially independent to create better lives for themselves, their families and make massive impact in the world from a healthier place leading from the heart and creating legacy for generations to come.

Specialties: Women collaborating as a catalyst on evolving to whole new space. It's about living authentically from the depth of their soul, saying YES to the power within.
