Mike Garland's Summary

I help companies increase their value and revenue with strategic thinking and application of sensible marketing. Sometimes this means applying basic marketing principles like asking for customer feedback and looking at competitive actions. Other times rebranding, building strategic partnerships and adapting to new markets is required. I work well with teams who want to become or maintain their role as industry leaders, not followers. My passion is to help create reproducible systems that:
- energize sales teams
- create customer loyalty and
- significantly increase revenue. 


Specialties: Making marketing efforts work well with sales teams.
Bringing the voice of the customer into the company with surveys, personal interaction and data analysis. 
Building relationships with partners as well as clients. 
Importing management and leadership principles into organizations of all sizes.
Coaching the sales force on how to maximize sales with sensible marketing strategy.
Tactical marketing to get results sooner than later.
