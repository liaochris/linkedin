Lydia Mann's Summary

I help companies figure out who they should be communicating with, how best to reach them, and using which technologies. I can see a project straight through from research, strategy, information architecture, user experience and visual design – or focus on any one of those areas, as need be.

Professionally, nothing is more satisfying than making a task simpler. This is at the heart of improving layout and flow of online forms, editing text for easy scanning and comprehension and stripping away unneeded elements. Experience as an early-childhood educator has proven a tremendous asset during my career, helping me break down complexities and anticipate potential gaps.

I thrive in a team-based workflow, collaborating with developers and product management to move quickly from comp to layout and production. I'm a fan of an agile, MVP approach, though I haven't yet spied a project that didn't need some documentation.

I want to do great work and build things that matter.

Specialties: user-centric website design, corporate communications, information architecture, graphics, interaction design, user experience, product management, web & content strategy, web applications, intranets, print collateral
