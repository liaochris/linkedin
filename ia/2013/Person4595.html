Alina Wilczynski's Summary

Alina Wilczynski is a concept-packaging specialist, creative director, multi-media artist, photographer and life-passion-enthusiast.

In 1994, Wilczynski co-founded Jupiter Design Group, a boutique design firm located in Northern NJ. She directed award-winning communication campaigns for clients such as The Valley Hospital, The Red Cross, Schering Plough, Toshiba, Stevens Institute of Technology, M&M/Mars, Robin Hood Foundation and host of Fox’s “More to Love”, plus-size Supermodel Emme.

After an accomplished run of 7 years, Wilczynski returned to her career beginnings as a free agent where she has re-connected with a more hands-on approach to client work.

moonkissed media, her latest manifestation, is a creative channel for message-driven marketing and design, emotionally resonant written communications and commercial photography.

Wilczynski is driven by her desire to advance the work of next-generation thinkers and renegades of creative social media who apply the power of what they do to progressive endeavors. Recent clients include Queens Council on the Arts, Institute for Long Island Archaeology Stony Brook University and Executive Coaching Group, Inc., whose founder created the marketing strategy for "Swatch".

In 2003, Wilczynski co-founded OP.LYNX, The Women’s Network of Out Professionals. She organized and hosted numerous events featuring women entrepreneurs and executives, noted filmmakers, authors, artists and athletes at such venues as MoMA and Asia Society & Museum.

Wilczynski then served on the founding Executive Committee of The LGBT Committee of the Manhattan Chamber of Commerce, where she produced "Women on a Mission: Lesbian Mavericks & Mavens in a Mastermind Session on Leadership".

Wilczynski has also shown artwork in galleries throughout NYC for over 15 years and is under limited representation by Monsoon Images.
