Joel Scott's Summary

Research scientist with experience in computer science, meteorology, remote sensing, server administration, and cloud computing.

Computer science experience includes Python, HTML, CSS, Fortran, and C. With additional experience in data analysis languages (Matlab, GrADS, IDL, Mathematica, and Maple), scripting languages (Bash, C-shell), and database languages (MySQL, NoSQL, mongoDB).

Science background includes meteorology, oceanography, mathematics, and statistics. With a focus in air-sea interaction and remote sensing (radar and satellite retrievals, passive microwave, infrared, scatterometer, and sounder).

Server administration experience includes software design, configuration, scaling, and maintenance of Linux servers and cloud-based remote servers. With multi-platform experience (RHEL, CentOS, Ubuntu, Amazon Linux AMI).
