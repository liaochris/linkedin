Cherise Selley's Summary

Selling residential real estate since 1997 throughout the Pike's Peak Region including: Colorado Springs, Monument, Black Forest, Falcon, and Peyton. Cherise is dedicated to superior real estate service from relocation to listings, first time home buyers to the million dollar client, and has sold over $190 million dollars of residential real estate in her career.

Cherise Selley formed Selley Group Real Estate, LLC in 2009 after twelve years of service in the industry. Cherise has a particular emphasis on contracts and negotiations, buyer and seller representation within the realm of residential real estate and investments.

Specialties: Certified Residential Specialist (CRI), Graduate Realtor Institute (GRI), Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR), Quality Service Certified (QSC), Expert Witness
