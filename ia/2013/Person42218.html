Eric Latham's Summary

BUSINESSES

In 2011, Eric Latham founded Intellectual Property Executives (IPE), a family-owned and operated pharmaceutical drug discovery and development corporation on the Valleybrook campus of East Tennessee State University in Kingport, Tennessee (USA).

IPE has two divisions. IPE’s Research & Development Division is based on six pending patents for platform technology that are fully-owned by IPE. These patents have led to the discovery and development of drug candidates that address the following therapeutic arenas: Hypothyroidism, Breast Cancer, Parkinson’s Disease, Alzheimer’s Disease, Cardiovascular Disease, Depression, ADHD, Ischemic Bowel Disease, Inflammatory Disease, PTSD, Wound-Healing and Tissue Repair, Hypo-metabolism and others.

In June 2012, IPE spun off its newer Health & Beauty Division, New River Naturals (NRN), which has since commercialized two lines of natural products. In 4Q 2012, NRN launched SKINTRA™ Cosmeceuticals, a line of natural skin care products. In 1Q 2013, NRN launched a natural weight-loss supplement called Garcinia Cambogia LEAN™.

BOOKS

Eric Latham is the author of two books, with a third to be published in 2013. The first, Take Steps with a Purpose, is about his walk across America, from Nags Head, North Carolina to San Francisco to increase awareness of cancer and raise money for research. The second, Entitled to a Nation, is about political, social, and cultural issues facing America.

Since completing his walk across America, Eric Latham has given more than 500 high-energy, inspirational presentations at colleges, universities, leadership conferences, corporate and civic functions, hospitals and cancer centers across the United States, Canada and Mexico.

More info at www.ericlatham.com.
