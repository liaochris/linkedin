Gary Gorr's Summary

Your Trusted Partner for Financial Advice in Toronto

Are you confused about which financial path to follow? We can help.

﻿In this constantly shifting economy, individuals and small business owners are increasingly in need of custom-tailored financial plans to help them meet their specific goals and requirements.

Our Goal:
We want to help you create and preserve wealth for yourself and for your family and make the complex simple.

A Changing World:

The world of finance is changing and we are changing with it. At Gorr Financial we must grow and adapt too and that is why on our investment platform we have gone to a Fee Based Advice delivery system.

We believe our current clients and new clients will see many benefits from this change.

We use a state of the art next generation Life Planning System-"Vision Works".

Vision Works is the premier life planning software on the market.

Life Planning is helping clients create their unique Vision of Life and then helping them achieve it. I can help clients clarify their vision of life, enter their current financial circumstances, uncover financial, estate, and insurance "gaps," and learn how to resolve them.

Mutual Funds and Segregated Funds provided by the Fund Companies are offered through Worldsource Financial Management Inc. Other Products and Services are offered through Gorr Financial﻿﻿.
