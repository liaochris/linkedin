Casey Potenzone's Summary

Casey Potenzone is a seasoned technologist with a focus on digital and web technologies and IP. His focus is driven by monetization and the new business models as they replace legacy P&Ls. His experience includes hands-on and senior management roles in technical architecture and solution design, sales and business development as well as running data centers and security operations. Potenzone specializes in taking technology to market and succeeds by having a unique vision on how it is monetized.

He currently serves as the Vice President, Digital at arvato Entertainment, a division of Bertelsmann. Previously he served as the VP of US Sales for Avangate, CIO of Uniloc, Director of Operations at Relegence, Director of Technology at MTV, as well as various consulting roles within oil, banking and entertainment. Potenzone continues to serve as an advisor to several public and private operations as well as providing expert witness testimony in the area of digital media.

At any point Potenzone has at least one to two technologies bubbling on the back burner. To date he has been awarded two patents as primary inventor, named an inventor on three others and has several additional provisional and in-process patent applications. Potenzone is a product focused techie at heart and is constantly pushing the envelope of new technologies.

Specialties: Technical solution architecture, ecommerce, master data management, solution sales and design, web technologies, product development, technology management, DRM and licensing, data center operations and digital media.
