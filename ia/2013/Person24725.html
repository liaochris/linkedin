Christopher Young's Summary

Necessity is the mother of invention. This is the cornerstone of why I founded Async Interview. After experiencing the broken and inefficient interview process from different vantage points, I took matters into my own hands and founded Async, a a video interviewing company born from a creative vision of the next generation of talent acquisition.

Today, I handle all aspects of Async’s strategic direction and innovation initiatives. Aside from Async, I enjoy solving complex problems with simple solutions and always wish I could be a Lego Designer.

Video Interviewing Can:
✪ Save time and money in every aspect of interviewing: from scheduling to initial screens
✪ Get valuable feedback from all members of the hiring team on the video interviews
✪ Keep your employer brand front and center throughout the video interview
