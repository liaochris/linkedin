Denise Gervais' Experience

Website Design, SEO, Internet Marketing Consultant

WSI Milton

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

February 2007 – Present (6 years 11 months) Milton, Ontario

At WSI Milton we use internet marketing to generate a lot more qualified sales leads for home improvements companies, contractors, landscapers, related suppliers.

It’s exciting to see the Search Engine Optimization (SEO) results. We have clients that have increased from just over 200 visits in a month to nearly 20,000!

A critical part of the success of our clients is PPC (Pay-Per-Click) ads, also often called SEM (Search Engine Marketing). Using Google AdWords properly drives relatively instant and critical amounts of visitors to our clients websites. These campaigns need to be managed properly so you don’t pay an average of $3.00 a click when it could be $0.50.

Social Media is the area that has the most hype and most questions about it. We have a training course that allows you to learn in manageable bite size pieces, over 6 months (www.yourwsiadvantage.com/social). For some of our clients we also create their first layer of content, get the conversations started and increase their following online.

Your potential clients have a conversation with you on social platforms, or find you on Google, and end up at the destination (your website). This is the fundamental place to have the right content written, the right images in place, the right layout, the right calls to action… so that the website visitor turns into a qualified lead. The internet average is that 1% of website visitors ‘convert’ – take the action step you want them to take. WSI Milton’s office average is over 5%. That is big variable of why our clients love the return on investment they get from our internet marketing services.
