Frances Munoz's Summary

Hello.

I was raised with old-fashioned values that the customer comes first. Integrity, honesty and ethics rule—hard work and excellence is a minimum standard and always work hard. As a designer, it's my duty to make sure you get a design that reflects your needs, as well as reach your audience.

My desire is to create fresh design that matters and makes an significant difference, in some way, for my clients.

My portfolio ranges from corporate, to the non-profit sector, and I have designed and built everything from annual reports to full blown campaigns and websites. So no matter who you are, what your industry is, or what your goals are, consider me for creating fresh designs tailored to your business needs.

Specialties: HTML, CSS, Graphic Design, WordPress, Joomla, Web Design/Development, Branding, Non-Profit Design
