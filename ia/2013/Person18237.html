Bruce Flanders' Summary

I love my job! The library’s professional faculty and staff work as a true team in supporting academic success, research, and learning.

In April 2013 my work responsibilities were expanded to include oversight of the academic support center and an information technology assistance center, both located in the library. These two academic support units, along with the library, comprise the "Learning Commons."

My work experience and skills encompass management, supervision, leadership, strategic planning, technology development, innovation, and marketing.

At MidAmerica Nazarene University, we educate and inspire student leaders.
