Brian Salem's Summary

The Salem Team is a full-service real estate team who will always be committed to being Northeast Ohio's #1 real estate team by helping you in all aspects of buying or selling your next home.

Our goal is always to put our client's needs first, by utilizing our professional contacts and experience, and to satisfy their desires with never-ending service.

Our utmost goal is to create life-long relationships and be first when you think of real estate!

It is all about service and commitment to families in what can sometimes be a stressful time in their lives. We pride ourselves in the fact that when we are given an opportunity to work for our clients, we give them our loyalty to see that they get not only the best that they expect, but the best that they deserve.

In our past careers and interactions with individuals in the Cleveland area we discovered the importance of timely actions and how well orchestrated plans make all the difference in the final outcome of your transaction. As full-time Realtors with Keller Williams Greater Cleveland West, we are given the best tools in the industry, from Internet and web-based technologies, to print and electronic marketing, allowing us to have full knowledge of what the market is doing in the areas that interest you, or in finding out what the market will actually bear when we are pricing your home. Our past experiences and present tools combine for a powerful combination and are why we have helped over 900 families in the last 10 years buy and sell their homes.
