Lisa Curry's Summary

I have worked in communications and marketing for theatre organizations, non-profit organizations, a dance studio, and in real estate. I take lessons learned from each project and apply it to the next, continuously expanding my knowledge and skill set.

I am currently focused on company wide communications, marketing material design, and writing press releases.

Work samples can be viewed on my personal website, lisahcurry.com

In my past position as an Administrative Assistant, I was a "Jill of all trades", ranging from office management, accounting and financial reporting/budgeting, public relations, and event planning.

I have also been utilizing my skills and passion for design and communication in my spare time, finding opportunities in my hobbies. I volunteer with a local cat shelter: designing layouts for their newsletters and creating flyers to help promote the shelter, its events, and their adoptable pets. I also spent three years on the organizing committee for the Cincinnati Lindy Exchange, an annual weekend-long swing dance event. My primary responsibilities on the committee were event promotion and website content management.
