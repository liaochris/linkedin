Jason Tucker's Projects

  • On Your Block

    • September 2010 to March 2011
    Team Members: Jason Tucker, Elizabeth Ladzinski

    "On Your Block" was a months-long project to showcase some of the amazing personalities that truly exemplify the Manhattan experience. People who own their own businesses (bakers, music teachers) to artists, to gardeners, to even a singing telegram delivery man. I chose one block in each neighborhood in Manhattan and found 5 people to interview who lived or worked on that block. The project combined reporting, photography, video, and text into one all-encompassing, interactive feature for DNAinfo.com. Jason Tucker, a talented interactive designer, came up with the design for the site. After some cuts, 49 profile video and photo stories are available to view on DNAinfo.com
