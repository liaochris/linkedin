Benjamin Wey's Summary

An alumnus of Columbia Business School of Columbia University, Benjamin Wey is a global financier and the CEO of New York Global Group ( www.nyggroup.com ) - a leading U.S. and Asia based venture capital / private equity investment firm with access to approximately $1 billion in capital, having participated in over 250 projects worldwide (select recent track record listed below) in four areas: corporate finance, investments, due diligence and strategic market entry. NYGG introduces opportunities and bridges communications in many countries.

With an 18-year track record, NYGG celebrates 15 years of office presence in Asia (1998-2013).

Benjamin Wey is a leading American expert on China, a multilingual Wall Street strategic adviser and a Visiting Professor of Finance at several top universities. With contacts in 50+ countries, Benjamin Wey has advised Fortune Global 500 companies, governments and businesses on complex projects worldwide.( See links to the various TV interviews below).
