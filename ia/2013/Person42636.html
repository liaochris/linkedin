Erik O'Brien's Summary

Headquartered just outside of Denver, Colorado, EMRjobs.com is a privately held company that’s focused on providing the most up-to-date healthcare career opportunities, specifically those within the electronic medical record (EMR) industry. For the past three years employers have turned to EMRjobs.com for assistance in finding the ideal candidate for their openings in EMR implementation, training, support, software development, sales and marketing, product management and consulting. Because the best positions call for the top applicants, qualified job seekers continue to utilize emrjobs.com the moment their search for a new career begins.

The company was originally founded in December of 2005 as EMRJobs.net and was purchased by its current owners in early 2008. The name changed to EMRjobs.com and a web site re-launch in June of 2008 revealed an updated image and enhanced functionality.

www.emrjobs.com

Specialties

We provide a niche network for companies looking to hire qualified candidates with EMR/EHR experience. We also provide a forum for job-seekers who are looking for positions in the EMR/EHR Industry.
