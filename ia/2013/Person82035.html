Matthew Llewellyn's Summary

Growing up playing classical piano and guitar, I was always focused on music. After graduating high school, I attended Berklee College of Music where I obtained a dual major in Film Scoring and Music Business/Management. While at Berklee, I scored various short films and developed my craft in various genres of film composition. Upon graduating Berklee I moved to Los Angeles and got accepted into the University of Southern California’s prestigious graduate program in Scoring for Motion Picture and Television. I have worked for a handful of composers including Brian Tyler, Richard Gibbs, David Schwartz, and Walter Murphy. In addition to helping them out, I have worked on a variety of independent films, short films, and web series. I currently reside in California pursuing a career in the film music industry.

Specialties

Orchestra, Digital Performer, Pro Tools, Kontakt, Logic Pro, Gigastudio, Finale, Vienna Ensemble Pro, Steinberg, East West, Spectrasonics
