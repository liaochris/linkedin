Carol Josel's Summary

Formerly a learning specialist with Pennsylvania's Methacton School District; currently supervise student teachers at Gwynedd-Mercy College and education consultant and presenter. Also, the founder of School-Wise Books, LLC, freelance writer, and author of three education-related books, along with a parenting tips booklet.

Carol Josel's Publications

    • 2002
    Authors: Carol Josel

    • The Scarecrow Press, Inc.
    • 2002
    Authors: Carol Josel

    • Scarecrow Education
    • 2004
    Authors: Carol Josel
