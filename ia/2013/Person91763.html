Michael Harms' Summary

A member of the TDn2K team since 2005, Michael is responsible for product and systems development, as well as overseeing the data analysis process that serves as the foundation for both the Black Box Intelligence and People Report brands.

Michael's insights can frequently be found in various publications, including a co-authored chapter of Dr. Jac Fitz-enz’s latest book, "The New HR Analytics: Predicting the Economic Value of Your Company's Human Capital Investments."

Michael remains the undisputed TDn2K cookie-eating champion and proudly wears the label "Unofficial Office Music Critic."
