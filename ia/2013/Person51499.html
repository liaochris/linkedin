Hannah Camacho's Summary

Apps do not sell themselves.

I get apps in the spotlight. Having written as a professional app reviewer at a number of respected publications, I know how the journalists in the space think and what gets their attention.

I have consulted with and propelled app developers into the top 25 overall paid apps in the US by launching a thorough, strategic PR campaign. I am obsessed with all things PR and get so much fulfillment out of helping app developers reach their full potential.
