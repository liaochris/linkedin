Jonathan Ramot, SPHR's Summary

SPHR-certified, senior-level HR business partner and manager. I have over 9 years’ experience managing HR issues for various industries and organizations, including professional/legal, academic, non-profit, and retail sectors: from sourcing and recruiting C-level executives to conducting employee investigations, appearing before administrative judges with regards to unemployment and EEOC charges, and everything in-between. Name an HR issue, and I’m sure I’ve dealt with it in some way over the course of my professional career.

Specialties: Employee and labor relations, business management and strategy, workforce planning and development, compensation and benefits, payroll management, recruitment, coaching, and training.

Fully bilingual and bi-literate in English and Spanish; I translate full documents to and from English and Spanish, and create documents and forms in Spanish for industries that employ native Spanish speakers.
