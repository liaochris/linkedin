Barry Harvey's Summary

I have developed existing and start-up businesses in the hospitality and catering sector and in commercial property.

My emphasis is always on a clear business strategy and defining goals that move the business forward and improve performance.

Barry Harvey's Experience

Business Centre Manager

Colston Office Centre

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry

January 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months) Bristol, United Kingdom

The Colston Office Centre provides serviced offices, virtual office facilities, meeting rooms and business support. Our services save our clients time and money and help to improve their business.

My key role is business development: ensuring we stay ahead of technological advances, which improve the services we can offer; providing the best and most efficient service for our clients; creating marketing strategies and opportunities; and, of course, generating new sales.

Significant investment has been made in refurbishing the building, adding new facilities, like showers, improving office environments with personalised colour schemes and furniture and upgrading the building with low cost energy features.

Even in this undoubtedly difficult climate, we have taken on three new clients this year and are looking forward to further growth as we offer more services for our clients.

Proprietor

September 2002 – June 2007 (4 years 10 months)

Re-designed and opened cafe in prominent office building in city centre. Providing high quality brekafast and lunch, using local suppliers and well trained staff to give fast turnaround for busy office workers.

We also provided buffet lunches and hosted various events, including networking events for the IOD. Generated profit within 18 months and closed due to changes within building.

Host for wine tasting events

Your Silent Host

September 1999 – June 2007 (7 years 10 months) Bristol, United Kingdom

For dinner parties, garden parties, wine tasting and other private events, we will ensure you are free to enjoy the occasion.

We will take care of every aspect of service, help you theme and plan events, as well as provide catering should you require.

Instead of missing your event because you are running it, you are free to enjoy your party and mix with your guests while we ensure everything is perfect, acting as your silent host.

General Manager

Butcombe Farm

April 1998 – March 2002 (4 years)

General Manager of tourist board accredited hotel and holiday cottage accommodation. Butcombe Farm is a listed 14th century manor house with guest rooms, holiday cottages, pool and gym.

Developed marketing strategy and customer service to double turnover within first three years. Improved profitability led to reinvestment in the business, creating and implementing a rolling maintenance and refurbishment programme and developing further marketing initiatives.

On a personal level, I and my wife - it was l 'live-in' position - went to great lengths to care for and nuture the farmhouse and its' grounds and provide a warm, friendly and relaxing environment for our guests.

We provided regular meals for guests in the family dining room as well as home made, single portion meals. We organised and hosted birthday and anniversary parties, several local dinner and Christmas parties and even a wedding - our own!

Various management roles

Hospitality Industry

January 1993 – March 1998 (5 years 3 months)

Before taking over Butcombe Farm and then starting the Galley Cafe, I had run a wine bar, a hotel restaurant and a conference centre. I had been a somelier in a michelin star restaurant, ran a wine shop and taken lectures for the WSET Wine Certificate.

I have planned and organised more weddings than I care to mention. And it's hard work - as anyone in the trade will tell you. But it's also great fun. I am passionate about the combination of theatre and efficiency that leads to great service.

I help create great memories for my clients. Taking them through the process, getting them to the day, making sure the event looks great and runs how they imagined it. That is job satisfaction that is hard to beat.
