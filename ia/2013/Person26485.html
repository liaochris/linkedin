Odell Stunkard's Summary

After serving as CAO of a privately held third tier sub in the 10,000 sf+ residential market for over nine years, I came to Hearing Haven LLC as CFO in January of 2009. Since coming on board we have now opened three additional companies (one internationally).
I have co-authored a book entitled "Success Simplified" with Steven Cover, Dr. Tony Alessandra and Patricia Fripp due to be released in late November 2010 and am releasing my first private book under the title "Coloring of Gold" that is due for release in late February 2011.
I have now added public speaking in my field of expertise of "How to Build a Business" and am actively marketing this enterprise.

Specialties

Turn arounds, business building and reorganizations.
