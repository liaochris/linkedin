Emily Aiken's Summary

I'm a brand strategy consultant. I have extensive experience delivering value during the start-up, re-positioning and market extension stages of companies, products and services. I effectively develop and direct action in both new and old media market channels. I've helped launch emerging technologies, entertainment networks, software companies, design firms and personality brands. I speak the language of the consumer and am proficient at aligning a brand across all platforms, whether it is a social media prescence, a graphic user interface or an investor dog and pony.
