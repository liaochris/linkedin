Kris King's Summary

Currently I teach K-12 technology based courses. I am also in the final stages of my Doctoral Degree in Instructional Design and Technology. While technology integration and incorporating the Internet in the classroom is very important to me, my area of research and concentration is in Online Learning, specifically Virtual Schooling.

Online learning is very important to me as it allowed me to secure a better career by allowing me the opportunity to pursue my Master's 100% online while I worked full time. This topic is more than area of interest, it provided me with a lifestyle change.

My goals are to continue studying and researching online learning at all levels. As online courses are becoming the "norm" at the post-secondary level, they are new at the K-12 level and there are many factors that come into play at this level. I want to contribute to field on online learning/virtual schools for K-12 learners and their families. There are so many reasons why this type of learning can benefit students of all ages.
