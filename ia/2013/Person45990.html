Dr. Frederick Mindel's Summary

My name is Doctor Frederick Mindel and I have owned my own practice in New York City since 1987. Originally a trial lawyer, a back injury in 1983 led me to a chiropractor whose advanced approach changed my life forever. Inspired by my rapid recovery and new outlook, I returned to school to become a Doctor of Chiropractic.

For almost 20 years, I have studied with experts in different areas of natural healthcare, in order to provide a truly comprehensive approach to healing. The result is a rare integration of major methods of restoring and maintaining health.

Alternative Medicine Magazine has selectedme as their choice for Holistic Healthcare Practitioner in New York City.

Specialties

Holistic NYC focuses on discovering and correcting the underlying causes of health problems rather than merely treating symptoms. Our unique holistic approach enables us to get to the root of your problems. You can eliminate most symptoms, rather than think you have to live with them or use drugs or over-the-counter medications for temporary relief.
