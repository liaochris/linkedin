  • Prezi Hackathon

    • janvier 2013 à Aujourd'hui
    Membres de l'équipe : Jérémy Dagorn, Gábor Török, Cassus Adam Banko, Kateryna Sadovnycha, Attila Gazsó, Jose Enrique D'Arnaude, Pierre Ingmansson

    During Prezi Hackathon in 2013, we formed a team to solve a huge problem of ours: track who is in the office currently. Our product vision was that if you're keen to hack something on Saturday morning, you should be able to fire up your browser on your phone to see if there is any fellow coders who might be interested joining you.

    We came up with two ideas for the implementation. First we wanted to track folks when they enter the office. We did some experiments with image recognition using OpenCV and the security camera at the main entrance. It was fun but not so reliable.

    The other half of the team created a website—whoisintheoffice.prezi.com—based on Django and a couple of Python scripts which we used to parse the DHCP log for requests coming from devices. The idea was that if you're in the office, you're most probably connected to the office network.

    We happily use our second solution since then in the Budapest office.
