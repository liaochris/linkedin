Jonathan Good's Summary

Jonathan Good is a Senior SRM Solutions Consultant at Oracle. He provides technical and functional support to prospective clients and customers for Oracle’s Social Relationship Management platform.

Before joining Oracle, Jonathan was the founder of HelloSocialMedia.com - a social media and marketing agency that focuses on the creation and execution of social media programs, including blogging, community development, Internet marketing, design and web development.

He helped businesses integrate social media into their marketing, branding and PR efforts to help them create more awareness and buzz, generate sales and leads, increase ROI and have a positive impact on the bottom line.

Prior to founding HelloSocialMedia.com in 2009, Jonathan served as the vice president of marketing for Greenwala.com, overseeing the web development, branding, community building and strategic partnerships to further the company’s mission. In 2007, Jonathan created and launched dezumo.com in 2007. Dezumo was a social networking community for creative people, such as artists, writers, graphic designers, photographers and musicians.

Jonathan also co-founded WunderMarx|PR™, a public relations, social media and branding agency.

Before launching WunderMarx|PR, he directed brand management, Web, creative and communications at two publicly traded semiconductor companies - Conexant Systems and Mindspeed Technologies. Jonathan amassed in-depth experience with corporate spin offs, IPOs, mergers and acquisitions, internal communications, leadership, management, branding, corporate marketing, technical writing, and website strategy.

Jonathan has served as president of the Orange County chapter’s of the International Association of Business Communicators (IABC) as well as AIGA, the Professional Association for Design.
