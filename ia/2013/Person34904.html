Dev Singh's Summary

With over 10 years of immersive study and global experience in what distinguishes World Class ideas and leaders from the rest, Dev Singh works with executives, entrepreneurs and teams around the world, to cut through the self-created, unconscious and elusive barriers that keep them from unlocking their highest potential.

Having spent the past decade studying and training under some of the world's most prolific and successful leaders in entrepreneurship, marketing, influence and leadership, Dev has also worked with hundreds of entrepreneurs and business organisations around the world; from small social enterprise and technology start-ups from London to Toronto, to senior executive teams of major global corporations across Europe.

As a Certified Executive Coach and Master Practitioner in Neuro-Linguistic Programming, Dev is known for empowering other leaders, entrepreneurs and professionals to transform their businesses and lives in deep and long-lasting ways, and for making every learning experience incredibly practical and fun.

You can learn more about Dev at www.devsingh.net.
