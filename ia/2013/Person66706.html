John M. Hose's Summary

John M. Hose was first attracted to the financial business for the income potential and flexibility of the work schedule. He soon found that people need dedication to service and integrity to assure their financial future. He has always been driven with a desire to help clients design a strategy to have their money outlive them.

After working several years focusing on wealth management and retirement planning, John used his experience to create Lifetime Insurance and Financial Essentials, Inc. where he is now the Owner and President. Lifetime Insurance and Financial Essentials, Inc., or LIFE, is a Christian faith-based company that strives to apply the Golden Rule when working with clients. Their mission is to provide sound, life stage appropriate financial guidance and top notch service to clients and their families for LIFE. Their goal is to provide clients and their families with the essentials needed in the insurance and financial services market.

Lifetime Insurance and Financial Essentials, Inc. strives to provide the most current products in both industries and to provide service to all clients’ needs in a timely and efficient manner. Lifetime Insurance and Financial Essentials, Inc. wants to educate people about their alternatives and assist in implementing the appropriate solutions so that they can make the right decisions for LIFE.
