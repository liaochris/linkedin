Joey O'Connor's Experience

Owner

Joey O'Connor Enterprises

January 1998 – Present (15 years 11 months) Greensboro/Winston-Salem, North Carolina Area

Joey O'Connor has been in online property development since 1998. Starting with his personal golf businesses, he has now branched out to own over 70 websites/networks covering specific industries, employment listings, and local business.

In 2005, Joey founded and became the editor of Golf Club Business (GolfClubBusiness.com) - an online golf publication catering to the golf industry. In its' few short years, Golf Club Business has grown to be one of the largest online golf publications currently having 50,000+ pages of content covering over 700 golf companies with over 3,000 listings to their golf directory.

In late 2012, Joey decided to develop a new online publication dedicated to his hometown area - Greater Greensboro Business (GreaterGreensboroBusiness.com) - where he is also the editor. EmploymentGreensboro.com and GolfInGreensboro.com are also part of the network.

In late 2013, Joey decided to develop a new online publication dedicated to his love for the Myrtle Beach / Grand Strand Area - On The Grand Strand (OnTheGrandStrand.com) - where he is also the editor. JobsinMyrtleBeach.com and DirectoryMyrtleBeach.com are also part of the network.

For a complete listing of all of Joey's online properties, as well as complete information on Joey and his other projects, please visit http://www.JoeyOConnor.com
