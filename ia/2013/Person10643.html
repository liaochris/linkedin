Atif Hussain's Summary

I have worked in the IT industry for six years. I have worked in Education for nine years during which I have been a Head of Department, Key Stage 3 Co-ordinator, acting Deputy Head and ICT Curriculum Specialist. I am now a Director of Educational Technology and an IB examiner for ITGS.

Education is where my passion is. This passion motivates me to lead schools towards development. One such development is using Educational Technologies to enhance the teaching and learning in schools.

I have a Post Graduate Certificate in Leadership and Management in Independent Schools. I am also a Google Certified Teacher (amongst the first internationally selected ones).

Specialties: A highly motivated and educational visionary possessing a unique combination of skills and a
commitment to leading schools towards catering for the needs of the 21st century learner. An
honest and trustworthy team player with excellent communication skills and an ability to bring
together various members of the school. A principled professional dedicated to achieving the
highest standards whilst providing a safe and inspirational teaching and learning environment.
