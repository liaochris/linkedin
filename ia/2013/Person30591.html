Daria Trifu's Summary

Born in Brasov, Romania, Daria Trifu began her film career in the year 2000, few months after moving to Canada, as a graduate of Bruno Pischiutta's exclusive Film Acting Courses in Toronto.

In 2003 she was a speaker on the "Peacefulfish & Variety Presents Global Film Finance" panel in Cannes alongside Barry Poltermann, Claude Fenioux, Jonathan Bing, Christopher R. Auty and Cassian Elwes.

Daria Trifu is the Assistant Director of the feature film MAYBE (2003); Producer & Executive Producer of the documentary BRUNO PISCHIUTTA: FILM DIRECTOR (2009), Executive Producer of the feature film THE COMOEDIA: ENGLISH VERSION (2009); Producer & Executive Producer of the feature film PUNCTURED HOPE (2009), Producer & Executive Producer of the feature documentary FILMING AFRICA (2012), Executive Producer of the BRASOV FEATURE DOCUMENTARY (2012) and Executive Producer of the feature film SUICIDE (2013).

In 2003, the feature film MAYBE was an Official Selection at the Bahamas One World Film Festival and the film received ‘The Visionary In Film’ Award for Director Bruno Pischiutta. The Critically Acclaimed feature film PUNCTURED HOPE (budget USD 5.8M), directed by Maestro Bruno Pischiutta, was an Official Selection at the World Film Festival in Montreal and it was qualified for nomination consideration at the 2010 Academy Awards® in the category of “Best Picture”. That December, the film was nominated by The Political Film Society in Hollywood, CA in two categories: “Best Film Expose” and “Best Film on Human Rights” of 2009 – other films nominated in the same categories include AVATAR, THE HURT LOCKER, INVICTUS and INGLORIOUS BASTERDS.

Daria is the Founder, President & C.E.O. of Global Film Studio Inc., Vice President & C.O.O. of Toronto Pictures and Publisher & Editor-in-Chief of "Daria!" Magazine. She is also the President of the Toronto Pictures Film Academy of Ghana and a 'Celebrity Ambassador' for Humanity Healing Foundation (USA).
