Kris Nothwang's Experience

Owner

Oral Restorative Prosthetics

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Hospital & Health Care industry

January 1985 – Present (28 years 11 months)

I started out writing billing software, as there was nothing commercially available. I then began doing the pickup and delivery for the newly expanded dental laboratory. From there I learned how to do preparatory work, wax and finish crowns and copings, all phases of removables and more. I quickly learned all that I could about attachments and implants. I have attended seminars and C.E. courses from Nobel Biocare, Lifecore, Straumann, Neoss, Keystone, Attachments International, Bego, 3M ESPE, Ankylos, Deka Laser, Dentsply, Ivoclar, IMTEC and many others. My wife and I purchased the lab in 2008.

District Technician

Chuck E. Cheese Pizza Time Theatre

1983 – 1985 (2 years)

My main duties were to keep the video games, pneumatic characters and kitchen equipment operational for 3 Northern California locations. I was solely responsible for turning around the Santa Rosa locations game room from an embarrassment to the only thing keeping the parent corporation from closing the location.

Site Manager

Take-1 Family Fun Center

1982 – 1983 (1 year)

Managed one of the two locations for this video arcade until the owners closed the businesses and moved away from the area. I also did many of the repairs to the games.
