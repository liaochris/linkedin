Trevor Hudson's Summary

Trevor Hudson is a technologist with a drive to help people live healthy lives. A designer and engineer, Trevor helps healthcare companies and startups market their products and develop their online presence. His varied skills in media, technology, marketing, and teaching help him to make an impact in many different places, though his passion for health pervades all of his work.

As an engineer at eHealth, he is supporting the implementation of Health Insurance Exchanges, a crucial part of the Affordable Care Act and part of a personal goal to bring healthcare to those in need. He transferred from a role of optimizing eHealth's website for search in order to take on automating resource intensive parts of eHealth's business process.

Trevor is also a Co-Organizer at Health Technology Forum, an organization that facilitates monthly Meetups and conferences discussing health and technology. At over 3000 members, Health Technology Forum proves to be a significant place for sharing ideas and networking around digital and mobile health. Trevor built the HTF website and manages all the IT for the group. He has also personally organized Meetups and facilitated panels of distinguished speakers.

Trevor's design agency, Älva Creative, is now accepting clients. For video, infographic or ad design, web development, data science, photography, and SEO/marketing consulting, please contact him at trevor(at)alvacreative.com.
