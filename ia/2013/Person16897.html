Brent Darnell's Summary

The most successful people have learned to understand emotions in others and make true emotional connections. This is the core message of Brent Darnell International; we help technical-minded people understand and better utilize their emotional intelligence to reach their highest potential.

Brent Darnell is the international best-selling author of "The People-Profit Connection" and a leading authority on Emotional Intelligence. He is President and owner of Brent Darnell International, a training and consulting company that maximizes companies’ success by tapping into the “soft” skills, or the Emotional Intelligence of each employee. Brent was recently awarded with the prestigious honor of being named among Engineering News-Record’s Top 25 Newsmakers for his transformation of the construction industry’s Alpha Males.
