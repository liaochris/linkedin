Benny Haryanto's Summary

Experienced, talented, dependable production designer with strong understanding of all phases of production, design and project management. Excellent in website, mobile, print and packaging production from implementing concept, creating assets, image retouching and manipulating to photography.

Ten plus years experience in multiple industries ranging from advertising, technology, communication, entertainment, manufacturing to fashion. Clients ranges from Microsoft, HTC, AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, Pantech, Tire Buyer, Toyo Tires, Vertafore, PF-Flyers, Aravon, Frito-Lay, Marvel, Disney, Nickelodeon to Eddie Bauer.

SPECIALTIES: Graphic production wiz for web, mobile, print, packaging and streamlining production tasks. Experience handling full-ad campaigns graphic production services in multiple areas such as collateral, out-of-home, large format display, newsprint, magazine, brochure, bus advertising, store display, website and online.

Recognized as an energetic team player with positive and professional attitude. Ability to multi-task and manage several projects with multiple deliverables. Persistent, deadline driven, detail oriented, flexible, adaptable and motivated to learn new things. Has strong problem solving skills and creative mind.

Graduated with Honor “Magna Cum Laude” from The Art Institute of Ft. Lauderdale F.L.
