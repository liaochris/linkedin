Alex Adams' Summary

After beginning as a documentary filmmaker, Alex moved into the world of design, animation and interactivity. Over the past 15 years, he has developed a reputation for creativity and finding new ways of bringing excitement and functionality to emerging media formats. Although known for his sharp aesthetic sensibilities, Alex has great skill and experience in all aspects of media production from developing information architecture through post-production of finished elements.

Some of his more notable projects include producing the interface design, logo and branding for TiVo's first Personal Digital Recorder. Alex was also responsible for producing the interface architecture and design for DIRECTV's NFL Sunday Ticket – an application that won an Emmy for outstanding achievement in Interactive Television. He also produced seven videos for the launch of Microsoft's IE9. The purpose of the videos was to create excitement and teach new capabilities of the software.  

Video samples of all of these can be seen on his website – http://alexadams.net
