James Hartje's Summary

My name is James Hartje, I am currently CEO & Founder of Stocks on Wall Street LLC.

From a young age, I have always had the drive & ambition to succeed. My passion for finance is what has taken me on this career path & my goal is to work my way to the top of the finance world.

In 2009, I started my own successful online investment advisory firm called Stocks on Wall Street. As SWS Founder & President, the site grew to all-time heights & currently attracts over 500,000 readers per month. SWS generates strong revenues through affiliate marketing, PPC advertising, & private ad sales.

SWS's popularity opened the door to many other publishing opportunities as my articles can be seen all over the web on major sites such as TheStreet.com, Investing.com, CNBC, Forbes, Yahoo Finance, Google Finance, The Motley Fool & Seeking Alpha.

Before SWS, I held two different internships with HSBC working at their London HQ. First, I was an intern for HSBC's Equity Capital Markets Department, where I directly reported to HSBC's Head of Global Banking. I then went on to intern for HSBC's Sales & Trading Department. Previously, I also worked for Ameriprise Financial as a Client Analyst for two high-net worth Financial Advisors.

Currently I'm on track to graduate from Arizona State University in 2014 with a degree in Economics & Minor in Political Science, finishing my degree online. While at ASU, I was actively involved on campus holding many leadership positions including Treasurer of Sigma Nu Fraternity, Student Facilitator/Teacher, President/Founder of the Affiliate Marketing & Entrepreneurship Association, Investment Banking Industry Scholar, & Social Chair for the Financial Management Association.

Current CFA Level 1 Candidate

If you wish to get to know me, please email me at jameshartje@stocksonwallstreet.com
