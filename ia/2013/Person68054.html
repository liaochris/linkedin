Jon P Spitz's Projects

  • Buffalo Startup Weekend

    • May 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jon P Spitz, Dan Gigante, Dan Magnuszewski, Mark Branden, Jeffrey Ross, Charlie Wertz, Nicholas Barone, Lisa Primerano, Katie Krawczyk, Thomas Murdock, Mitchell Patterson

    Startup Weekend Buffalo is an intense 54-hour event where local entrepreneurs, developers, designers, marketers, product managers, startup enthusiasts and anyone interested in building a business will come together to share ideas, form teams, and launch a startup. Startup Weekend Buffalo is a subsidiary program of Startup Weekend, a global non-profit organization with a mission to connect local entrepreneurs and foster the development of new companies.

    Since its inception in November 2012, Startup Weekend Buffalo has connected over 300 local entrepreneurs, developers, designers and startup enthusiasts with mentors and resources to launch Buffalo’s next startups. To view highlights from the inaugural Startup Weekend Buffalo, visit http://bit.ly/13iNDlK.

    For more information, visit www.buffalo.startupweekend.org
