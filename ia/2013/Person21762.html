Chad Kreutz's Summary

Business leader at QuinStreet (Nasdaq: QNST), one of the largest Internet marketing and media companies in the world. Chad's roles at QuinStreet include 1) head of marketing and sales for the Home Services vertical and 2) General Manager of the Small to Medium-sized business category (VendorSeek.com and QuoteCatcher.com).

Prior to QuinStreet, Chad was Vice President of Sales & Business Development and then President at QualitySmith.com and Glass.net.

Experience includes full P&L responsibility, M&A, strategy development, building scalable business models, new product development, leading 100+ employees and forming highly functional teams.

Specialties: online marketing strategy and operations, lead generation, e-commerce, strategic partnerships, customer acquisition, email marketing, B2C and B2B marketing and sales, key account management, SEM, SEO, affiliate marketing, call center, local marketing, brand strategy, customer retention and remarketing, vendor management, social media, contract negotiation, talent recruiting, product development, C-level sales, M&A.
