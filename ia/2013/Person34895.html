Devon Wyland's Summary

I am a passionate, enthusiastic, and driven designer who graduated from UC Santa Cruz in June of 2012 with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science: Computer Game Design. I use my knowledge from my degree to think up interesting game concepts, design user experiences, and analyze the data of the latest popular games.

My background includes game design, playtesting, 3D modeling, management, programming, and research. I am passionate about the development process and enjoy organizing, scheduling, and monitoring the process toward a tangible end goal. I look forward to keeping a positive work environment by encouraging my team and clearly communicating cross-team. Read more about my background on my portfolio.

My career goal is to become a lead designer or project manager, leading my team to the development of the next big title. I am working toward this goal by growing my experiences and researching all genres of games and mobile development.

Specialties: Game Design, 3D Modeling, Management, Graphics, and Web Design.
