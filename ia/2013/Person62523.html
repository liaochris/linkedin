Jeremy Wilker's Summary

Grabbing a camera and creating something dramatic is immensely enjoyable and in the past several years has even been recognized with several awards. I'm a cinematographer and producer of feature (and short) narrative and documentary films and have partnered with corporate and non-profits to create branding and educational stories as well.

When I'm not on set shooting a movie, you can find me on another set as a digital technician for commercial photographers.

I'm also passionate for all types of media -- whether print, video or web -- and while consuming it is nice, creating it is much more satisfying.

I've been working with video in one form or another since 1990 (Video Toaster, anyone?). My IMDB profile is here: http://www.imdb.com/name/nm3206492/

I have been online in some form since 1987. If you want to hear about my many years in the print and prepress world, just ask.

Specialties: Video production and cinematography, digital asset management, print production, web development, photo retouching and compositing, speaking, training, and other aspects of print and video production. Fluent in Adobe Creative Suite and Final Cut Pro, among many others.
