Greg Wong's Summary

Greg Wong is an integrated art director and social catalyst fostering movements for brands and innovation. With expertise in crafting narrative concepts and visual communications, Greg activates meaningful engagements for real world impact.

Greg is currently freelancing as an Art Director for various digital and branding agencies. Previous agency experience includes Saatchi Wellness, McCann Erickson, Futurebrand, Wunderman, Exhibitgroup Giltspur and the Longitude. Greg has consulted to a wide range of Fortune 100 corporations, small business boutiques and non-profit organizations across the country. Greg has degrees in mass communications from U.C. Berkeley and graphic design from the Pasadena Art Center College of Design.

Extending beyond creative art direction, Greg consults to launch new movements for social innovation. Greg serves on the Board of Directors for Restore NYC, a non-profit that is dedicated to the holistic life rescue of internationally sex-trafficked women in New York City.

Due to the confidential nature of my latest digital projects, my work samples are viewable upon request.
