Angela Winter's Publications

  • “Many Thanks: Gregg Krech on the Revolutionary Practice of Gratitude”

    • The Sun
    • December 2004
    Authors: Angela Winter

    An interview about gratitude, Naikan, and Japanese psychology with Gregg Krech, director of the ToDo Institute in Monkton, Vermont.

  • “The Science of Happiness: Barbara Fredrickson on Cultivating Positive Emotions”

    • The Sun
    • May 2009
    Authors: Angela Winter

    An interview about positive emotions, flourishing, and upward spirals with Barb Fredrickson, Kenan Distinguished Professor of Psychology at UNC.

    An excerpt of this interview is reprinted in the textbook America Now: Short Readings from Recent Periodicals (Bedford/St. Martin’s), edited by Robert Atwan.

  • “Across the Universe: Stanislav Grof on Nonordinary States of Consciousness”

    • The Sun
    • August 2009
    Authors: Angela Winter

    An interview about holotropic breathwork and nonordinary states of consciousness with Stan Grof, psychiatrist and founder of the International Transpersonal Association.

  • “Are You Happy Now?”

    • Utne Reader
    • September 2009
    Authors: Angela Winter

    A condensed version of my interview with Barbara Fredrickson (“The Science of Happiness”), reprinted in Utne's twenty-fifth anniversary issue.

  • “No Sweat”

    • The Sun
    • May 2010
    Authors: Angela Winter

    An essay about body image, relationships with men, and running from exercise.
