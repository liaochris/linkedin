Andres Varela's Summary

Helped establish LEO TECH. In less than three years the team grew from four to 75+ staff. One customer. One project.

Now that's changing. When we shipped in the second half of 2012, we had funding for the next few years through upgrades and maintenance. A new entrepreneurial CEO and a desire to retain key personnel has me on the look out for projects which can keep the team engaged.

Pro bono, very commercial, or anything in between, we believe that we can provide extra technical muscle (or sometimes *be* the technical muscle) for all kinds of organisations.

Our main expertise is in high frequency, high volume secure transactions across the web and a range of proprietary POPs and CDNs. A private cloud, if you will. Our technology is primarily cross-platform: HTML5, CSS, jQuery and Java and our methodology is agile based –focusing on Grails rather than Ruby. In terms of IT Services we can monitor your systems 24/7 from own private NOC, handle hosting or disaster recovery or consult and implement on anything from your server and office network choices to administering them in person or remotely.

If you're not too disturbed by only a fledgling credentials deck, and would like to come and visit the best internet studio you've never heard of, get in touch through InMail or direct at andres.varela@leotech.com.sg or check out www.leotech.com.sg

About me: Production, marketing and project management skills with extensive experience of brand and product management, information architecture and site design, both as an individual and within teams. A genuine pioneer in the UK (now based in Singapore) I have been in the industry since its beginnings, and so have had the opportunity to work in a range of areas before definitions and demarcations existed.

Specialties

Production and marketing management for online gambling, e-gaming, gaming, fundraising and consumer websites. Have worked within marketing and advertising agencies for international brands, as well as client-side within corporates handling Intranet development for hundreds of thousands of users worldwide.
