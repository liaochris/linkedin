Ayres Landal's Honors and Awards

  • KUAT: Striking Flavour, the challenge

    GOLD - Wave Festival 2012
    • April 2012

    Role: Design Supervision, Production Management and Motion Design.

    We had one goal: to make the web as striking as Kuat's flavour. Marcelo Adnet, a famous Brazilian comedian, accepted the challenge and together, we recreated frame by frame web's most viewed viral videos to make them even funnier. Then, we created a talk show on which Adnet interviewed webcelebs to try to find out what is the secret to become "Internet famous".

  • ROYAL: Simple Tastes Better

    BRONZE - Wave Festival 2012
    • April 2012

    Role: Design Supervision and Production Management.

    When it comes to the relationship between mother and child, everything seems very complicated. Thinking about that, we created more than a repositioning campaign for Gelatina Royal: we developed a never-before-seen study about mothers, called Moms' Confessions. This research generated a documentary and the results provided import ideas to the new brand positioning, which were seen with the launch of a new product: Royal Duo Magic, a jelly to be prepared by mothers and children together. After all, simple tastes better.
