Jim Richardson's Summary

Jim Richardson is an International Executive Coach who helps business leaders recognize their sharp edges, become more self-aware, and increase their personal effectiveness as leaders.

Jim draws on more than 30 years of management expertise, organizational knowledge and consulting experience to deliver performance-based development programs and executive coaching services for business leaders and their teams. His work has focused on optimizing leadership performance, team effectiveness, change strategies, employee engagement, service excellence, organizational design, quality improvement and talent management.

Jim has worked closely with clients in a variety of industries including: telecommunications, travel & hospitality, manufacturing, retail, marketing, healthcare and banking. Jim’s diverse background in business and human resources enables him to help individuals, teams and organizations build key capabilities and change behaviors to achieve business goals. He also understands the challenges and nuances necessary to create cultural change within an organization to engage employees and inspire them to perform at the highest levels.

Specialties

Executive & Leader Coaching, Leadership Effectiveness, Executive Strategy & Alignment, Organizational & Team Alignment, Employee Engagement, Emotional Intelligence, People Development, Team Optimization, Facilitation & Team Building, Building Internal Coaching Capabilities
