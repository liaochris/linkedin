AJ Williams, CMP's Summary

AJ Williams, CMP: Principal & Creative Director
As founder and creative force that drives Creative Events, AJ Williams builds upon her strong eleven-year track record in the industry by executing exceptional experiences that are unforgettable. Before founding Creative Events, she worked for Donald Trump's Miss Teen USA and USA pageants for the NE region handling promotion, marketing, production for almost 7 years. She was a featured writer for a major NE based magazine. AJ is known for creating the “WOW” factor – always thinking outside the box. In addition to her creative background, AJ has traveled extensively around the world, developing a multicultural culinary palate that she incorporates into the events she plans. Her love of culture, food, fashion, and wine, together with her dedication to the attendee experience and her passion for event planning, makes her events truly unique and successful. Today, AJ’s clients look to her ability to deliver diverse and remarkably imaginative environments uniquely
tailored to their vision.

Clients include: Hill Holiday, BeanTown Jazz Festival, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Harvard Business School, RoxComp "We Take Your Health to Heart" Gala featuring Jennifer Hudson + Queen Latifah, Lexus Herb Chambers, Cytyc Corporation, and more...

Specialties

Fundraising for non-profit events, event publicity, event logistics, graphic design, event strategy, decor, and event design.
