Anne J. Kaplan's Experience

President and Principal Owner

Brookfield Educational Consultants

2005 – Present (8 years) Brookfield, CT

Brookfield Educational Consulting Services is your partner throughout your student’s entire high school experience. From the first day of ninth grade until your student is accepted to college and has chosen his home for the following year, we will work with you to make sure that your student shines for the college admissions committee. Support includes counseling related to college choice and application, college transfers, and appropriate post-secondary placement.

Services include:

- Initial consultation with family and student –advice related to extracurricular commitments, course selection, test prep advice and goal setting

- Ongoing meetings and communication on an “as needed basis”
Compiling an appropriate list of colleges or post-secondary options, including advice related to working with those institutions

- Interviewing techniques

- NCAA advice for student-athletes
Support throughout the college application process, including brainstorming ideas for college essays*
