Malcom Reed's Summary

Building THE Online Resource for Serious BBQ.

My name is Malcom Reed from Killer Hogs BBQ. I've been cooking competition BBQ for over 12 years. And, in the past 3 years I'm been building a pretty strong Brand online through social marketing, weekly newsletters, radio interviews and websites.

We currently compete in 12 - 14 BBQ contests each year, KCBS and MBN... and we win consistently.
