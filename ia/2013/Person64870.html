About Barrett Values Centre
Barrett Values Centre measures values, culture and consciousness. Together with our global network of over 4000 consultants and change agents, we provide tools that open the door for new conversations to help leaders build and sustain happier and more successful teams, organisations, communities, schools, and nations.

The core products of Barrett Values Centre are the Cultural Transformation Tools (CTT). The tools are used to map the values of over 6,000 organisations and 3,000 leaders in 80 countries. The tools are used in corporations, NGOs, government and municipal agencies, communities, schools, and nations to foster group, leadership and personal transformation. CTT is available in over 50 languages

In her current role as director of TLEX, Joanna has grown TLEX nationwide and into leading institutions and Fortune 100 companies. Experienced in leadership, collaboration and innovative social ventures, Joanna sits on the board the International Association for Human Values (IAHV).

TLEX Program - Transformational Leadership for Excellence, enhancing personal and organizational excellence, communication skills, teamwork, creativity and innovation. Facilitates change from the inside out, enabling leaders to focus on what is really important and expand their perspective.

TLEX is a program delivered by IAHV, an international entity, headquartered in Geneva. It is an NGO in special consultative status with the Economic and Social Council of the United Nations. The national chapters of IAHV work in close collaboration with each other, and have done so since the founding of IAHV in Switzerland in 1997. IAHV is truly a growing international movement, not merely an organization. It is focused on promoting peace and harmony in the world through a re-kindling of those basic human values that are at the core of every individual, culture and tradition.
