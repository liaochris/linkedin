Esther Dyson's Summary

I'm currently setting up HICCup, an initiative to set up citywide health intervention programs in several cities. I will post more details soon. Meanwhile, constructive criticism, contacts, advice and the like are welcome. I'll shortly set up a LinkedIn group... in the meantime, email me at edyson@edventure.com to be added to a mailing list (for infrequent use).

I spend most of my time exploring new space, health care and IT start-ups and technologies, writing about them and actively (and with full disclosure) investing in some of them. I love what I do and I love all the travel it entails! (You can also find me on Dopplr, if you are lucky enough to have an account! Before you ask, I have run out of invitations.)

[Re LinkedIn: I find it a valuable tool, but I respond only to personal messages that prove that the writer is writing to me rather than to an address book.]

Specialties: the Accountable Net, IP and telco policy, identity management, social software, Central and Eastern Europe, start-ups, semantic tools, mapping tools, foundation governance, board experience (esp. "corporate regime change"), commercial space, general aviation
