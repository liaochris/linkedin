Gail Sophia Edgell's Summary

Gail Sophia Edgell is the co-founder of Holistic Marketing Mentors and Health Educator at Designs for Health.

Holistic Marketing Mentors assist (mentor) holistic wellness professionals with the foundational components of their business, increasing profits, attracting clients and finding additional revenue streams. This may include partnering or additional business resources. Most important systematizing their business so they can reap the profits without stressing and always having to be there. She often consults with holistic businesses around the country assisting them with best practices, system and program design. Her extensive experience stems from working in corporate wellness for over 25 years and implementing hundreds of wellness initiatives.

As a health educator at Designs for Health, Gail supports wellness practitioners in Southeastern Pennsylvania to increase revenues through innovative nutritional therapies which in turn help patients get the results they want more quickly. She also provides practice management tools and science based education to help them excel in their practices.

Her mission is to see alternative medicine be the next conventional medicine in less than 10 years. She truly wants to help holistic professionals thrive and her be their "voice".
