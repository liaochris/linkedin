Ariane Tom's Summary

Graduate level education is an essential component of my personal and career development. As a Ph.D. student in Dr. Zhenan Bao's research group in organic electronics, I am eager to acquire the depth of knowledge necessary to develop bioelectronic materials/devices for various applications, particularly for use in the brain (neural systems analysis, bionic devices, neural repair/therapy). I am a recipient of the DOD National Defense Science & Engineering Graduate (NDSEG) Fellowship, and am currently supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

My work experiences have provided me with a keen awareness of how advances in technology impact biotech and energy sectors, as well as science policy. Past research activities include innovative use of technology to address problems in both medicine and energy poverty in the developing world.
