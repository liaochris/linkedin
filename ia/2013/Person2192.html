Subhas Gupta, MD, CM, PhD, FRCSC, FACS's Summary

Dr Subhas Gupta is an internationally recognized plastic and reconstructive surgeon, educator and researcher. He provides world-class comprehensive care for the aesthetic and reconstructive patients of Southern California and from around the world. He has performed numerous pioneering procedures in plastic surgery, and is a pioneer in minimally invasive aesthetic techniques and surgery, facial endoscopy, facial injectables, complex reconstruction, skin substitutes, wound healing, and microsurgery.



Dr Gupta holds a PhD in medical informatics with expertise and active research projects in patient safety, wound healing, scar control, advanced technologies in wound care, nutrition in surgery, technology assessment, surgical quality assessment, and healthcare economics. He has been well recognized for his contributions to research in cosmetic surgery, minimally invasive techniques, laser surgery, informatics, outcomes assessment, and wound healing with funded fellowships, research grants, and paper/presentation awards from numerous specialty and national societies. He has published over 80 scientific articles and serves on several scientific review and editorial boards.

* Broad-based surgical training
* Specialized Plastic Surgery training in aesthetic surgery, laser surgery, hand surgery, complex wound reconstruction
* Leading surgical educator
* Administrative surgical leadership
* Patient Safety in Surgery
* High reliability surgical team design
* Medical and Regulatory Affairs management

Specialties: Aesthetic Surgery, Plastic Surgery, Cosmetic Surgery, Hand Surgery, Medical Leadership, Medical Affairs, Anti-aging medicine, Facial Aesthetic Assessment, Clinical Trial Design
