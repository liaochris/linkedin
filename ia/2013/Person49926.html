Owner

Bay Area Design Center

1990 – 1996 (6 years) Oakland, CA

As owner and General Contractor of this full service design/build firm, the team included designers and construction crew members ready to meet clients' needs.

With a highly organized and skilled approach, we managed kitchen remodeling projects from concept to completion. Our remodeling services included:
- Demolition
- Cabinetry and installation
- Countertop fabrication and installation, including appliances
- Lighting and plumbing installations
- Wall removal and/or new walls
- Kitchen islands and fixtures
- Replacement and newly added windows

We provided professional kitchen design with quality cabinetry and installation, located in Oakland/Piedmont, California. Our walk-in retail Design Center displayed the latest ideas and trends in kitchen designs and accessories such as lighting, fixtures, and faucets.

Our goals included understanding our clients' needs, then presenting them with new ideas, working with them to get their dreams on paper, and putting in the kitchen of their dreams - on time and on budget.
