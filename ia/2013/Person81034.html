Linda S. Jevahirian's Summary

Legal Recruiting and Placement:

At Legal Search & Management, Inc. we combine more than 20 years of professional-to-professional networking with a detailed interview process to locate and recommend experienced attorneys, paralegals, legal secretaries, legal administrators, paralegal managers, contract administrators, information technology managers and litigation support managers.

Career Coaching. Business Coaching:

Our philosophy at Legal Search & Management, Inc. is that everyone deserves a gratifying and successful career. We help you establish SMART goals, and design and implement a targeted strategic plan to establish and advance your career or business.
