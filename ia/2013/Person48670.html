Gina Carr, MBA's Summary

Gina Carr is the Tribe Builder. She helps passionate people build powerful tribes of raving fans.

Connect with Gina via gina@ginacarr.com - Linkedin Open Networker (LION).

A marketing maven, Gina works with business leaders to attract more customers using social media. Her clients include franchised businesses, thought leaders & business owners.

She is the Co-Author of the 2013 McGraw-Hill book: Klout Matters: How to Engage Customers, Increase Your Digital Influence, and Raise Your Klout Score for Success.

Gina's personal mission is to inspire and empower people all over the world by connecting them with the people & ideas that can make their hearts sing.

Beginning in 1987 with a note-taking service for Georgia Tech students, Gina has since started a number of successful businesses including several specialty magazines, a diversified real estate company, a cable television show, a mortgage brokerage company, a business brokerage firm, and a franchised chain of community magazines with annual gross revenues of approximately $3 million which she sold at the end of 2009.

She is an expert on influence marketing and entrepreneurship - especially digital marketing - social media and mobile marketing.

She is recognized as an accomplished entrepreneur, franchise expert, and marketing consultant. In 1996, she was recognized by the Georgia House of Representatives for her contributions to her community. Gina is well known for her advocacy work on behalf of homeless pets.

She has been published in Speaker Magazine and is a regular contributor to Technorati.
