Chief Traffic Officer

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Online Media industry

April 2012 – Present (1 year 9 months) New York, NY

My responsibilities are over all traffic driving activities as well as Product development functions for all of our digital properties. These responsibilities span over 100 million visits on a monthly basis generating 350 million monthly page views. We are #3 (usually) according to comScore in the Technology content vertical with several large technology and science web properties including: Space.com, LiveScience.com, TomsHardware.com, LaptopMag.com, TopTenReviews.com to name a few.

The audience of all of our properties have grown since my arrival, largely due to my understanding of what the audience is looking for, developing a content strategy to capture and fulfill that users need - and finally, getting that user to come back. My first 18 months has seen 2 of our properties (LaptopMag and BusinessNewsDaily) triple their traffic levels. The audience for our leading science property, LiveScience has seen traffic more than double. After the acquisition of BestofMedia, we were able to double the traffic to TomsGuide within 2 months of the acquisition.

In the last 2 months we have launched versions of our science properties that are optimized for use on SmartPhones with several more product enhancements coming soon.

That is what a Chief Traffic Officer does --> I drive users to our online entities.
