Julie Robbins' Summary

My communications career began with a position as a project intern with CNN’s live coverage of the 2000 Presidential Election. This performance quickly led to an appointment as an associate and then NE Regional Producer at CBS national in NYC. In 2004, ABC News and Daytime Emmy award-winning Good Morning America recruited me to serve as a show producer. There, I led a team of broadcast professionals through a fast-paced, deadline-driven editorial process; gaining recognition for producing highly-rated show segments.

I then joined the elite primetime editorial team of Fox News Channel (FNC). At Fox I created feature, hard news and documentary segments seen by the audiences of FNC, their national affiliates and sister network, Sky News, in Europe. In my role as a primetime cable news producer with FNC, I covered an extensive mix of local, national and international news stories and events ranging from New Year’s Eve Specials in New York City’s Time Square, interviewing leading politicians and overseas Prime Ministers to sports stars and controversial news makers.

In 2009, I founded my own organization, IdeaWorks. As the Senior Marketing and Creative Strategist, my team and I craft and execute thoughtful marketing and publicity campaigns for subject-matter experts, businesses and established not-for-profit organizations.

Also a Partner in EcoCOOL Technologies U.S. and U.K., we specialize in significantly reducing commercial refrigeration and cooling costs through the application of a unique catalyst, a ‘green’ product delivering excellent ROI, fast payback and a significant impact on reducing operating cost.

In 2012, I became a founding partner of IdeaActive, a new type of Performance Management Company. Unlike traditional agency models, IdeaActive designs and implements marketing roadmaps fully integrated with clients' business plans while focusing on measurable growth.
