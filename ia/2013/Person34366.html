Deon Staffelbach's Summary

While working for several large and small agencies in my career I've been fortunate to create broadcast, print and interactive assets for many wonderful clients increasing their sales and global brand awareness.

Clients I have worked on in the past range from world wide leaders in technology to world wide leaders in ice cream and a whole lot of clients in between, here's a short list:

Adobe, Cisco Systems, Dallas Museum of Art, Dallas Symphony, Earthgrains Breads, Hewlett Packard (HP), Intel, LaCie, MPower Prosthetics, Nestle Ice Cream, Samsung, Scottish Rite Hospital for Children, TGI Fridays, Visa, Zale Lipshy Hospital, Zales Jewlers.
