Jason Corrigan's Summary

Visionary marketing and organic search manager with five years of experience in developing complex, wide-scale search and social strategies that connect brands and businesses with qualified audiences. Search development campaigns include: Duracell.com, Febreze.com, Swiffer.com, and others.

Professional experience also encompasses marketing management in television, radio, and print media and seven years of experience in journalism, copy writing, and editing for major news publications with Hearst Media and Journal Registry.

As a firm believer of Kennedy's "Ask not what your country can do for you...", I have raised over $100,000 for various breast cancer and domestic violence non-profit organizations within New York State.


Other Experience:

-Directing and managing all public relation programs for the SUNY school system.

-Creating original legislation that concerns various civil liberties.
The "Tri-Lateral Agreement" was adopted by the National Apartment Association in 2009 and recognizes specific rights for tenants and property owners considering zoning ordinances and municipal legislation.

-Providing consultation for the allocation of election grants throughout Otsego County.

-Networking with various political/military agencies in developing landmarks, memorial services, and media conferences.
