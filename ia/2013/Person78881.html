Laura Stein's Summary

Laura has pursued design throughout her career in several creative forms, giving her a unique breadth of knowledge and understanding of the field. With training in both Boston and Toronto, Laura’s talents range from interiors to styling to graphic design. Her interior decorating work has been nationally recognized with multiple industry awards. Laura has also been honoured with the 2011 Consumer Choice Award for Best Interior Decorator in the city of Toronto.

Laura's diverse experience has led to a broad range of projects, including work on HGTV's Designer Guys and Style Dept., as well as photo styling for House & Home magazine. Laura has appeared on CTV's Canada AM, CTS's Real Life, and has been featured as an industry expert in the Toronto Star,Telegraph Journal, National Post, Sun Media and in several other newspapers, magazines and online media across Canada and the United States. Laura also writes a monthly Q&A column for the Toronto Star's web site www.yourhome.ca.

Drawing from her varied artistic background, Laura approaches each new project with a unique perspective. She is dedicated to creating exceptional, distinctive, and uniquely personal interiors for her clients. Laura's focus is on superb quality, fresh design, and lifestyle-based functionality. She has built her business on a reputation of exceeding clients' expectations and guiding them on a stress-free journey to a beautiful home.

Laura's personalized attention to each client ensures that everything is taken care of, from sourcing fabrics and furniture, to hiring trades, to the finishing touches.

Laura is an accredited interior decorator and a member of the Canadian Decorators' Association (CDECA), a national organization of accredited interior decorators and designers.

Specialties

Laura Stein Interiors offers interior decorating and design services to residential clients, specializing in complete redesigns from start to finish - for a single room or an entire home.
