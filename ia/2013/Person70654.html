Executive Director

Family Life Services

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Individual & Family Services industry

March 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months) Colorado Springs, CO

The Board of Directors appointed Julie as the Executive Director for Family Life Services (FLS) in 2009. FLS is a residential community for single moms and their children designed to help families build a foundation for a new start in life. FLS also offers professional counseling services to the community on a sliding scale rate. Julie's role at FLS encompasses:

-Leadership to grow 70+ year old ministry to increase visibility, public awareness and community partnerships.
-Directly responsible for strategic planning implementation, program expansion, fundraising and brand re-alignment.
-Grant writing to establish capacity for future growth of programming and outreach models.
-Establishing internal infrastructure to strengthen accounting, human resources and expand board governance policies.

Director of Center Advancement & Training

Care Net

2006 – 2009 (3 years)

Care Net is a non-profit ministry located in Washington DC that serves as the largest affiliate network for supporting over 1200 pregnancy centers in North America.
Julie worked to revise existing training model to assure the growth and maturation of local pregnancy centers’ capacity for achieving industry best practices.
During her time there she created improved non-profit management process and procedures for affiliate centers, provided consultation with affiliate leadership on questions pertaining to board issues, strategic planning, marketing, donor development, and human resources.
Julie also served as a national speaker and created a successful integrated marketing training program to educate Care Net pregnancy centers on multi media marketing principles and execution.

Various Marketing Positions

Focus on the Family

Nonprofit; 501-1000 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

1997 – 2006 (9 years)

During her nine years at Focus, Julie served in a number of marketing positions including Associate Campaign Manager, International Account Executive, Special Projects Coordinator and Marketing Coordinator for Youth Culture.
Julie also served as the Sanctity of Human Life Manager. While in these various marketing and strategic planning positions within the ministry she contributed as the campaign manager for the development and launch of the Focus on the Family Truth Project™, and the Option Ultrasound™ program.

Office Manager, Dean of Women's Office

maintained high level of contact with student body and community representatives, and managed crisis intervention process from resolution to follow-up.
