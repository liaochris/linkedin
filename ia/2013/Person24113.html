Christina Stormson's Summary

My business experience includes Owner/Operator of CertaPro Painters of Las Vegas (2005-2009), Regulatory Compliance Manager for Players Island Casino (Lake Charles Louisiana), and Webmaster for California Building Performance Contractors Association.

Pink Iguana Services grew from my experience as a franchisee and small business owner with CertaPro Painters. My CertaPro franchise, which I had from 2005-2009, produced upwards of 2 million dollars in sales and hundreds of residential and commercial painting projects. Growing from an owner operated painting company, to a successfully managed multi partner business with over 20 employees.

With my past experience in construction, regulatory compliance and advanced experience in website development and management I, in a sense, partner with our clients to provide support in multiple business areas. Pink Iguana has become the go to company for over 70 businesses, by becoming the strategic partner for website management, SEO, content development, PPC and mobile services. We have worked exclusively with other franchisee's in the painting industry and are now being sought out by other small business owners.

I am an avid and lifelong learner with certifications in Human Resource Management, LEEP-AP (Green Building Professional), a HERS (Home Energy) rater, and a licensed painting contractor. I earned a Master’s Certificate in Internet Marketing from the University of San Francisco, Certificate in Small Business Website Development from the University of Washington along with a Market Motives Master Certification for Conversion Optimization (getting visitors to take action).

Specialties: Strategic business consulting, website development and management, online and offline marketing and advertising, mobile websites development for franchsisee's in the home services and small business industries, integrating online and offline marketing strategies and focusing on converting visitors to leads.
