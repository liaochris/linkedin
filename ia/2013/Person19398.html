Heather Biederman's Summary

Calluna Graphic Design and Cedar Creek Web Design are all about working with companies to provide them with remarkable service that brings them results. Plain and simple. As a business, you can have the most fantastic Web site or marketing collateral but without a plan to execute it and use it properly, you end up with nothing but an electronic cost burden and a stack of useless paper.

My job is to create visual harmonies that speak to your audience and urge them to contact your business to buy your product or hire your services. Creating strong, long-lasting relationships with you, my clients, enables me to know you and your business—know where you are now and where you want to be in the future.

Most clients work with me exclusively, however, in some cases I contract with other graphic designers, photographers, copy writers, marketing specialists and web developers should the needs of my client surpass my specialties: company branding and identity; website design and web development; HTML E-mail marketing; customer loyalty mobile apps and custom mobile app development; brochures, booklets and other print collateral for small businesses.
