Michael Borthwick's Summary

Since leaving the outside sales role (2007) I have spent 5 years managing the internal sales support team for two regional offices. Currently managing 11 +/- Sales Support Associates in Philadelphia and Harrisburg. Working closely with SVP's to recruit, retain and train new hires.
