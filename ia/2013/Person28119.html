Chia-Lin Simmons' Summary

Named on Huffington Post as “27 Women In Tech You Need To Follow On Twitter” in 2011 and "Top 100 Under 50" by Diversity MBA Magazine in 2012, I thrive on the challenge of inventing, initiating, and growing business opportunities from a blank slate, or turning around a business by driving adoption to ever changing consumer interests.

As a senior executive, I am passionate about delivering cutting edge technologies that change people’s lives and allow them to enjoy their entertainment whenever and wherever they choose.

I do that by building borderless, high performing teams, opening new markets, growing companies, customer bases and adding value and engaging stakeholders with companies such as Audible (an Amazon company), Harman International, AOL - Time Warner, CNET, Wired, Playphone and more.

With full P&L responsibility, I have worked on amazing products that transformed how people purchase goods in their lives while always growing bottom and top line revenues for organizations. I've increase revenue by 80% in existing marketing channels, reducing costs by 70%, tripling the number of new revenue channels for a variety of different organizations.

From Series A startups to Fortune 500 companies, I continue to be an early leader in mobile, e-commerce, digital media, gaming, social media, and currently connected cars, with customers / partners in Silicon Valley and beyond such as Amazon, Audible, Harmon International, Apple, Time Warner, Fox, Nokia, CBS, NBC, the four major music labels, Wall Street Journal, Facebook, Twitter, Yelp, Rhapsody, MOG, Rdio, NPR and more.
