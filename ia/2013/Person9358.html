Arash Shiva's Summary

I'm a designer that loves creating products at the convergence of design, technology and business. Recently I've been leading the re-design of Groupon's eCommerce mobile apps used by millions of customers worldwide.

I'm also a serial entrepreneur having started two successful startups and two design firms over the last 12 years.

My design philosophy is simple - small teams, fast iteration, clarity over style. Design is the product, so it should lead development. I enjoy the whole process from big thinking to tiny pixels.
