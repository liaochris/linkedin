Jason Claiborne's Summary

Mr. Claiborne is President and co-founder of Augustus Publishing. He runs the day-to-day operations of the company: sales, marketing production and creative services. Mr. Claiborne’s creative talents coupled with his keen business acuity have earned him a reputation as a leader among his colleagues. Mr. Claiborne has extensive experience in establishing brands and creating innovative marketing and advertising vehicles.

Mr. Claiborne played an integral part in the inspection of Integrated Beverage Group, Ltd. in 2004. IBG is a diversified manufacturer of sports, nutraceutical and recreational beverage products. With innovative products like Power Ice, a frozen sports hydrator and Throat Cooler, the only nutraceutical ice for dry or scratchy throat conditions. As a partner and creative director of IBG Mr. Claiborne has created all of the companies brand identities and eye catching package designs.

Jason is founder of Sublime Visuals, a full-service multimedia design and production studio. Mr. Claiborne's award winning creative talents have attracted numerous clients such as Business Week, Ad week, Harley Davidson, Lucky Strike, The Verve Music Group, Zomba Recording Corporation, Jive Records, Verity, Silver Tone Records, and MCA Records. Mr. Claiborne has over 10 years of experience in the music industry, which lead to the development of a variety of client, work in areas of new media design; multimedia and Internet based commercial products that compliment an already diverse set of artistic skills. Mr. Claiborne's creative background stems from a long family lineage of artists, which include his great uncle Norman Rockwell of the popular “The Saturday Evening Post” publication, first published in 1916.
