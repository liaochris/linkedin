Riggio Design: Interiors | Gallery | Showroom

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Design industry

June 2008 – Present (5 years 6 months)

Erica Riggio, founder of Riggio Design, has over 9 years of design experience. Ms. Riggio earned her Master’s of Fine Arts in Interior Design from The George Washington University. She also attended MICA, Maryland Institute College of Art, in Baltimore and received a certificate in Architectural and Interior Design Studies.
Ms. Riggio has traveled across the world to study design. Most recently, she visited over six cities in Italy studying art, art history, architecture, and interior design. Her intent is to study every facet of design within her reach.
Molding and creating art via found objects, within existing structures, and by utilizing new materials is her passion. Creating chic, stream-lined, timeless, and comfortable spaces define her aesthetic. She is equally dedicated to historical preservation. It is her goal to pay respect to historic structures by seamlessly and strategically blending the historic with the new. She carries these philosophies into her interior design.
She is dedicated to providing good design within everyone’s reach. Her commitment to the environment is strong. On almost every project principles of green design, most notably the reuse of materials, are incorporated. Ms. Riggio resides in Hyattsville and is committed to working with and for her community.
Her most recent venture is a collaborative effort called 5710Creative: a creative design studio. This unique, artistic space is home to two other arts-driven businesses as well. Her interior design studio, gallery, and showroom are located within this exciting new space. Her gallery, formerly known as Design Studio Art Gallery, is now Riggio Design: Interiors, Gallery & Showroom. 5710Creative is located in Hyattsville’s new Arts District and holds exhibits featuring the work of local area artists.

Partner (Gallery)

Design Studio Art Gallery

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Arts and Crafts industry

August 2008 – December 2010 (2 years 5 months)

Art, in any medium, is paramount to gaining a better understanding of the world in which we share and live. Art keeps us healthy, art retells the past and acts as a teacher, and art captures the emotion, the spirit, and the energy of the moment and essentially of life. With an emphasis on promoting and supporting the arts, Design Studio Art Gallery became the perfect vehicle to deliver the message that the arts are still alive and still essential to social, cultural, and personal advancement.
Creativity is never underestimated, never taken for granted, and always welcomed! Winston Churchill said it best when he remarked that “We shape our spaces; thereafter they shape us.” We truly believe that one’s environment is essential to shaping one’s existence in every capacity. And art is always part of one’s environment. Design Studio Art Gallery’s commitment to art and design has been the catalyst for this new venture. Design Studio Art Gallery was established in 2008.
It is owned and operated by Interior Designer Erica Riggio. Erica Riggio is a graduate from The George Washington University and has an MFA in Interior Design.
