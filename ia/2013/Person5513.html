Amanda Maxine Benson's Summary

SHORT INTRODUCTION
I find innovative ways to grow and utilize the resources at hand, helping people and companies understand, adapt and take strides towards a more transparent mindset. I consider digital communication an important tool in creating a strong foundation for sustainability and lasting business success.

'STATING MY CASE'
As professionals in communication, we practice in an environment populated by 'advisors', 'consultants', 'specialists' and 'experts'. The semantics of the titles, combined with the areas on which these titles are applied, contribute to an exotic array of “species”. This undoubtedly makes our trade a fascinating one, but may also leads us to pose the questions; how do you compete when your rival is a “rainbow colored tiger”, and how do you delineate the “darwinistically” strong species from the weak?

Well, I’m no different from the others; fluffing my feathers and sharpening my claws. With my war-paint applied I’m ready for battle; to take on that psychedelic tiger at any time.

But my war skills are not what particularly set me apart. No matter what area you’re in or what title you uphold, the only way forward is... well, forward. The trick up my sleeve is - just do it! I always make sure to stay ahead, I move to where the plains are fruitful and where my pack will prosper. My focus lies on uniting individuals, because as I see it, we’re always stronger together. Whether it’s friends, family, coworkers or clients; it is my belief that a trust enriched social network is what forms the strongest of safety nets.

Sharing generates transparency, and by feeding each other with knowledge we can together, as a society, grow and prosper in a sustainable way.

Let's all be the darwinist survivours of tomorrow!

Amanda Maxine Benson's Patents

  • Application: SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR COLLECTING AND ACCESSING PRODUCT INFORMATION IN A DATABASE

    • United States Patent Application 20100114957
    • Filed April 5, 2006
    Inventors: Amanda Maxine Benson, Melissa Heller, Greg Benson

    In spite of the many advances of the Information Revolution, the vast majority of modern commerce remains largely unchanged in regard to buyer-vendor visibility. The lack of transparency into one another's intentions or operating details forces the parties to base decisions on assumptions or incomplete information. Although widely accepted, this absence of real-time access to information leads to enormous inefficiencies throughout the economy. Consumers pay higher prices and get less than optimal products. Producers build, stock and ship products based on imprecise projections. Advertisers spend enormous sums on media with little ability to measure results. All parties suffer greatly from the lack of a system that offers bi-directional transparency between the supply and demand elements of the economy.

Amanda Maxine Benson's Publications

  • "Gilla Läget"

    • May 2012
    Authors: Amanda Maxine Benson, Jennie Gerum, Louise Nobel

    The purpose of this paper is to investigate Facebook users' attitudes towards being actively involved in corporate communication on the social media site through the ’Liking’ of business Pages and Content. We have chosen a deductiveonset and primary data was collected through the conduction of a survey. The result of our study partially demonstrates how the user's needs for self-expression, sharing, global affiliation, connection, and transparency are met through corporate communication on Facebook. Individuals may express opinions and feelings, gain means of communication, stay updated about their surrounding world and get free access to information through the Liking of Pages or Content.
