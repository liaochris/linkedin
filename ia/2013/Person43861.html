Fabian Tilmant's Summary

New Media marketeer, consultant, manager and strategist with a focus on Convergence and a 12+ years track record in Innovation across Media, Entertainment and Telecom universes. Board Member, Entrepreneur, Startup Mentor, Researcher and Creator of tools & methods with the simple purpose to make Innovation meaningful, profitable and efficient.

Founder of the #Leanovation Method in search for partners to Make It Happen, helping both Startups and Incumbents innovating efficiently in the uncertain game that are today's ecosystems.

Passionate by Media, Mobility, Cities and the possibilities that technology enables through Telecom and ICT evolutions. Humbly believing that Design Thinking, at the crossroad of Business and Creative Thinking, is the key to tomorrow's success.

Based in Belgium, temporarily relocated to Manila, The Philippines.

Specialties: Design thinker, system thinker, analyst/creatist/synthesist, digital strategist, creative and pragmatic.
Think-outside-the-box-and-lateral-thinking-minded. Connecting the dots, being people, projects, concepts... Solution-focussed and vision-driven. Team leader and team player. Inspired and inspiring. Ideal mix between left and right brain.
