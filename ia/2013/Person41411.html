Emmanuel St.Germain's Summary

As an experienced mortgage professional I give you the personal attention & respect you deserve. I understand you are a major commitment in buying a new home, refinancing a mortgage, or cashing out your home equity. These are major financial decisions in a client's life. So I make a commitment to you.

* I will help you qualify,
* I will help you apply
* I will help you get approved for the right mortgage loan for you. Not anyone else!

Let me illustrate how I solve problems for my clients:

Problem: Client attempts to refinance property but upon review of their credit scores, they are unable to qualify for a loan at this time.

Action: Thorough scrubbing and review of credit and explanation to client how credit works and what can be done, client writes letters to bureau’s to remove and delete some accounts and collections

Result: 90 days later, client’s credit is sufficiently high enough to refinance and take advantage of the markets currently low interest rates and save hundreds of dollars per month. My long term approach to clients for life, allowed us to be patient and in constant contact with the client until we were able to accomplish all of their goals.

Problem: Client is attempting to purchase a townhome in Miami with an FHA Loan in an association that is already FHA approved. On day of closing with a different lender, loan is denied due to the condo questionnaire that the lender requested at the last minute. Clint clearly distraught and feels helpless.

Action: I do my due diligence and speak directly to my underwriter’s to confirm that an FHA approved association does not require us to get a condo questionnaire. Client drives up from Kendall to meet me.

Result: 15 days later client closes on home and is ecstatic.
