Diane White's Summary

After working almost 23 years for the State of Texas, primarily in supervisory and project management roles (which I thoroughly enjoyed), I decided to pursue one of my long-time interests...real estate investing. My husband, Rick, and I bought, remodeled, and sold several properties on our own, but after only 2 years I had had so many people encourage me to get my real estate license that I decided to go for it! After 8 years as a licensed REALTOR, I'm having the time of my life!

It is definitely an up and down industry, but I get so much pleasure out of helping people meet their real estate goals, that it's worth it to me.

Professionally, I also enjoy leadership, mentorship, and management roles. On a personal note, I'm drawn more and more to develop the spiritual and relational aspects of my life. When you put all of it together: I love the Lord, I love people, and I love real estate...what a life!
