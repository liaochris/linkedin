Elena Cartasegna's Summary

I offer leaders the authentic insight and pragmatic results-orientation that come with having been in their shoes. For more than twenty-five years I've lived and worked around the world with more than fifteen of those years in corporate senior management before becoming a consultant, mentor, coach and facilitator to thousands in global companies including Telefonica, O2, BASF, SEB, Nordea, PEAB, Randstadt/Vedior, Nike, HP and FMC Technologies among others.

I've built and led multinational divisions across Europe and the Americas. I've reported to, advised and collaborated with C-Suite executives since 1988. I know first hand the challenge and fun of attracting, training and managing large, culturally diverse teams, while steering P+L, envisioning and delivering exceptional product and exceeding performance expectations in a fast-paced, competitive context.

My eleven years with Nike and W+K deeply inspired me as I collaborated with some of the greatest leaders, high performers and creative masterminds of our times. The more famous of those alongside whom I've worked include professional athletes Michael Jordan, Charles Barkley, David Robinson, Andre Agassi, John McEnroe, Wayne Gretzky, Sergei Bubka, Laird Hamilton and Bo Jackson; filmmakers David Fincher, Spike Lee and Joe Pytka; W+K chairman and creative director Dan Wieden, the man who came up with Just Do It.

I've worked with widely respected visionaries and leaders in their respective fields related to human and organizational potential and development including among others Dr Don Beck, CEO and founder of Spiral Dynamics Integral, author and longtime advisor to Nelson Mandela and Desmond Tutu; the late Sri K Pattabhi Jois, world-renowned Ashtanga Yoga master and the primary source responsible for yoga's contemporary popularity; Genpo Roshi, CEO and creator of Big Mind and Brian Bacon, chairman and founder of Oxford Leadership Academy.
