Kevin Urban's Summary

Specialties:
Career coaching for artists and entrepreneurs, defining career and life goals, creating business and marketing plans, helping actors create/define their brand.

Bio:
As an actor, entrepreneur, teacher, and career coach, Kevin stays busy keeping up to date on the latest business and technology trends. His continuous research makes him a great resource for everything from Apple Updates to Industry Buzz to the latest Star Wars developments. A former competitive swimmer, Kevin understands the art of setting goals and achieving them through patience and practice; these same principals help make Kevin, co-founder of The Savvy Actor, the empathetic and motivating coach he is. Informed by his love of business models and design, Kevin’s talent to collect and synthesize information in an effective way helps clients connect the missing dots in their career. With a flair for the visual, Kevin’s strengths lie in packaging and design with a clear plan of how to combine talents and business acumen. Kevin leads clients toward individualized and specific-to-the-actor goals with a playful, outside the box perspective. An actor on stage and film himself, he understands what it takes: a little bit of coffee, a whole lot of passion, and actionable, guided steps to feel truly empowered to succeed.
