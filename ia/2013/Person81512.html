Lisa Gale Peery's Summary

After 20 years of practicing law, I decided to open the Law Office of Lisa Gale, PLLC in March of 2011. My goal is simple. I want to offer the type of service that creates an ongoing relationship with you and your family,where I can be a resource for any of your legal needs. As you buy your first home, have your first children, get ready for retirement, and all of the milestones along the way, there are legal issues that affect every areaof your life.
