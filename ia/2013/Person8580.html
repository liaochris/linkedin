Ann Totten's Summary

I have twenty plus years experience in Corporate America leading technical teams and traveling representing and presenting educational sessions for the company. I also have broad experience in building teams, mentoring, and executing strategies. More recently, I gained direct experience with building joint ventures and online marketing.

A few years ago, I found myself at a crossroads professionally. While I have a rewarding career in Corporate America, I also had a strong urge to become an independent business owner. I wanted the financial independence to have options that are not available when working full time for someone else. I knew that I needed to become an internet marketer/entrepreneur while still working in corporate America. I am a baby boomer and I, like so many in this demographic, found myself trying to find the perfect second career for me.

I made the decision to become an expert in online marketing. I felt that I would afford me the means to transition into the lifestyle that I desired and eventually transition into retirement comfortably. Why couldn't I work part-time while building wealth and building an asset that provides the kind of residual income that I knew I wanted? Why wouldn't the internet be the right tool to use for my second career?

Recently I happened to align myself with a few very successful women entrepreneurs who are sharing their internet marketing skills and leadership with me.

I'm excited to be starting my second as a corporate woman with these successful women.

I have lived in New York my entire life. I am married to Bill and we have 4 children, ages 22, 20, 18, 16. I enjoy time with my family before all else.
