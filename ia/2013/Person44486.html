    • October 14, 2011
    Authors: Fernanda De Abreu

    This work is intended as a study of niche audiences in digital communication, using the online community of sport fishermen as a model. This study exposes this niche's particular characteristics and determines that, in order to communicate efficiently with sport fishermen, one must first get to know them.
