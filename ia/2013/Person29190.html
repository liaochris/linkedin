Dan Counts' Summary

With a 10-year career in the information technology space, Dan started as a software developer having obtained degrees in Business and Computer Information Systems. Later he became an account executive for a number of software companies and consulting firms in the business intelligence software space. As an independent consultant Dan later provided Fortune 2000 companies with evaluation services for Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) software. These services provided a framework for CFOs and Financial Planning and Analysis managers to evaluate software designed to automate the planning, budgeting, and forecasting process.

As an executive recruiter he assists consulting firms & software development companies with the search and placement of highly talented professionals such as: Consultants to Practice Directors, Sales Executives, Sales Engineers, Risk Security and Privacy professionals, Software Developers, and IT Managers. His unique background as a software developer, consultant, and sales executive has proven to be a valuable asset for clients during the search process, matching client needs with candidates.

Providing a consultive partnership approach to the search process.

Specialties: Hyperion Consulting services, Risk Security and Privacy, Consulting Financial Applications, Business Intelligence Systems, Hyperion Implementations HFM, Planing Essbase, etc..
