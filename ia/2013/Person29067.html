Dana M. Nelson's Summary

Wife, mother of two, business owner, master gardener, 4-H Tech Club leader, speaker, and dynamic networking organizer don’t quite capture Dana’s energy.

Dana loves social media – Twitter, Facebook, Linkedin and more. She wants to harness the power of social media to build stronger businesses and better communities, one relationship and conversation at a time.

Dana is actively involved with numerous community organizations including
WNIN (Ready To Learn Advisory Committee), 4-H Technology Club, YMCA, Girls in Bloom, and Patchwork Central. She is also a Purdue University Advanced Master Gardener.

Dana has spoken locally as a guest speaker on several occasions on numerous diversified topics, focusing on Communications, Networking, Fitness & Nutrition, and a host of computer related topics.

Dana is passionate about helping others. Driven with that spirit, she is constantly finding new avenues to fulfill that passion. She firmly believes that when you actively seek to help others, they will in turn want to help you.

Specialties
