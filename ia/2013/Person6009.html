Through educational programs, consutations, and Master Gardener volunteers, we bring the community research-based information that enables recognition and adoption of best practices for water resources, pest management, and other horticultural topics. Adjunct Professor, Department of Landscape Architecture

University of Florida

Educational Institution; 10,001+ employees; Higher Education industry

January 2012 – May 2012 (5 months) Gainesville, Florida Area

Planting Design studio for 3rd year and Master's students.
Principal project: Fisher House at the Malcolm Randall VA Center, Gainesville FL
A planned ‘home away from home” for families of veterans receiving critical care was a moving inspiration for this studio project. Students presented work in several stages to VA officials and staff, the Gainesville Fisher House Foundation Board, medical and therapy advisors, and past residents of Fisher House facilities. Feedback from these stakeholders was incorporated in design changes as the project progressed. The Fisher House project was coordinated with a Landscape Construction course for a comprehensive examination, and generated cash awards and an internship for students.
