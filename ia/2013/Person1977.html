Adrian Hoe's Summary

I am a software engineer by experience. For those who are looking for one with formal qualification in computer science, you may want to stop here if that is what you care the most.

I have been in this IT industry for over 20 years and have accumulated various amounts of skills in ranging from programming languages, databases, processes to system analysis and design. Today I do mostly backend and system design for both web and native applications. For native applications, my choice of platforms are OS X, iOS, Linux and Solaris. For coding, Ada is my main and all-time favorite choice, though I am also capable of coding in other languages, which you can see as below.

Lately I have been working on projects with RESTful architecture design and API development. No SOAP, no tears!
