Darrell "Coach D" Andrews, CSP's Summary

Darrell “Coach D” Andrews, CSP (Certified Speaking Professional), is the founder and Chief Passion Officer of Darrell Andrews Enterprises. He is known by many as America’s Passion Coach. He is one of the few CSP's worldwide. He is a regular keynote speaker and trainer at conferences and events worldwide. His areas of expertise include finding your life's passion, becoming a professional speaker,, school and youth success and general employee/personal motivation. He is also a personal coach focusing on turning passions into careers, small business success and becoming a speaker. He is the author of the nationally recognized book, "How To Find Your Passion And Make A Living At It". He is also the author of the books: "The Purpose Living Teen-A Teen’s Guide To Living Your Dreams" and "Believing the HYPE-Seven Keys To Motivating Students of Color." He is currently working on two new books. His personal story of triumph, "5 Garbage Bags And A Dream", is one of the top rated stories in the book, "Chicken Soup For The African-American Soul."

Coach D has been featured on many radio and television programs such as the Tavis Smiley Program, Fox News, MSMBC and The Black Enterprise Report. For a two year period he was a popular monthly segment guest known as America Passion Coach on Comcast Cablevision’s Your Morning Talk Show. He was formerly a top producing sales executive and regional field trainer for fortune 500 health care company Abbott Laboratories out of Chicago, Ill. At Abbott, he was known for his contagious motivation, on-going success and ability to bring out the best in people.

He has helped thousands of people worldwide find their passion and purpose, build speaking businesses and achieve entrepreneurial success. Call his office toll-free at 1-866-426-2243 for a no-cost personal consultation.

Specialties: Motivation, Career Coaching, Finding Your Passion, Youth Development, Becoming A Speaker, Training, Consulting, Keynote Speeches,
