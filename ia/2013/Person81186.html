Lindsay Manfredi's Summary

Creative, out of the box, blogger, marketer, business/brand developer, sales girl, musician, non-profit co-founder, w/ a communication arts degree w/ an emphasis in business, specializing in blogging, writing, social media, business & relationship development. Two-time TEDx speaker, TEDxBloomington 2011 and TEDxIUPUI 2013.

Specialties: Writing, networking, communication, business, and making win/win situations.

I have been, and currently STILL am in love with social media and writing. In May of 2009, I created my company, Linzstar Inc, out of Indianapolis, to help grow businesses online and create amazing relationships for people as well as for myself. My blog was voted 26 in the Top 50 Indianapolis blogs and came in 2nd for the Most Influential Social Media Dame in Indiana in 2010. I have guest lectured at IUPUI three times and have given multiple seminars on social media strategy. In July of 2010, I was voted one of Indy’s top 10 people to follow on Twitter and spoke on ghost blogging at Blog Indiana 2010. I was able to sit on a panel for The Combine 2010, and have also spoke at TEDxBloomington in May 2011 and TEDxIUPUI in 2013. Along the way, I’ve continued to self-educate with help from amazing conferences, friends, and fellow writers.

I’m extremely involved in the Indianapolis music scene as a musician that has played in numerous bands. My current band, Kaleidostars, is releasing our debut album in 2014. My old band, Neon Love Life earned a spot on the 2011 Hot List in Indy, and our album was the number one Indiana album in 2011. I’m also one of the four founders and organizers of Girls Rock! Indianapolis, a nonprofit rock n roll camp for girls’ ages 8-16 which focuses on building positive self-esteem and female camaraderie using music as the platform.

I have had 15 years plus of management and marketing experience, as well as freelance writing for the past five years. I thrive off creating and love to see others succeed through processes I am a part of.
