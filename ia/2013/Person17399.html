Brian Hackett's Summary

The best "social networking" will always be face-to-face.

That's why we connect senior executives to share ideas and experiences in candid and confidential forums.

The Learning Forum is a network of senior executives who join together for direct, peer-to-peer dialog and sharing of "what works". We focus on Leadership Development, HR Strategy, HR Operations, Workforce Planning, KM, Innovation, Foresight and Security.

We also run executive level workshops for top teams using Gettysburg, Normandy and other key historical sites to teach the timeless lessons of leadership and human nature.

Apex Performance is the leading firm in neuro-leadership. Co-Founded with Dr. Louis Csoka, Apex has a proven model to teach the core skills that connect personal performance to leadership.

See www.apexperform.com and www.thelearningforum.org

Founder

The Learning Forum

January 2000 – Present (13 years 7 months) Greenlawn, NY

The Learning Forum is a consortium of senior executives from G500 firms who share best practices in face-to-face, confidential roundtable forums. These high trust and high content forums bring together the senior-most executives who are responsible for Strategy, Organizational Effectiveness, HR, IT, Innovation and Foresight.

Most councils meet three times a year. These groups are not sponsored by consultants or vendors. The member companies determine the agendas and ground rules.
