Felix Herrera Priano's Summary

My professional skill of 20 + years is set around four basic lines:

- Education & Research: As an academic (university) and as a specialized trainer for large corporations and government agencies. Speaker at nearly 100 national/international courses. Technical & research publications and author of books on technology subjects.

- Management: CIO and IT management at the Communications & IT Center (29.000 + users), University of La Laguna (1996-2002). Administration management (vicegerente) and member of the University Government. Promotion, management and implementation of national/international projects with a volume over 12 MEuros. Responsible of corporate security.

- Advisor & Consultant: With 10 + years experience in Public entities (Universities, Councils, Government) in multidisciplinary areas: industry, information technology, telecommunications, information society, health, enterprise security, e-admin, e-gov, project management, strategic planning and administrative modernization.

- Active participation: Interlocutor for the National Network of Research and Development (RedIRIS). Contact person of the Corporate Network of the Canary Islands. Member of the national Technology Commission at the Rectors' Conference of Spanish Universities. Member and technology advisor of committees and subcommittees of the Canary Islands Government and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry. Member of the Official College and the Spanish Association of Telecommunication Engineers. Involved in the promotion's group of Telematic Engineering (Madrid) and as a 4 year board member of the Canary delegation. Participant as ICT representant (Madrid, Brussels, Guyana) of the INTERREG III Program and priority actions for the Régions Ultrapériphériques of the EU. Member of committees of major corporate IT & modernization projects of the Canary Islands' Government.
