Michelle Gamble Risley's Summary

Michelle Gamble-Risley founded MGR Consulting in 2006, which has now become 3L Publishing. She is CEO of 3L Publishing (www.3LPublishing.com) that opened in July 2009 and currently has 35 titles in its catalog. She is a seasoned veteran of the communication business, with over 20 years experience in public relations, marketing and custom publishing. She began her career in 1988 working respectively for the California departments of Justice and Water Resources. In 1991, she joined California Computer News and eventually took over the role as publisher, working on strategic communication programs to increase the publication's market presence and overall success. She then joined two new start-up companies called The Center for Digital Government and The Center for Digital Education where she served as executive editor and director of custom publications and developed an array of marketing and communication programs and services. After a brief position as director of strategic communications at a marketing agency, she left to form her own agency.

Gamble-Risley specializes in helping new and emerging writers develop their manuscripts into highly marketable books. She has written and published dozens of custom whitepapers, reports, success stories and case studies. She is also an accomplished writer, with articles appearing in regional and national magazines, including Government Technology, Converge, ENR News, T.H.E. Journal, Comstock's and Sacramento Magazine. She also wrote the screenplay Virtual Seduction that aired on Showtime.

Michelle has just finished her first novel, The California Girl Chronicles: Brea and the City of Plastic. This racy, sexy and romantic novel is a coming-of-age story set against the glamorous world of Hollywood. Follow the romantic adventures of our smart but misguided heroine.

She also published Smash, Vanity Circus and Second Bloom.

Specialties

I am a seasoned veteran of the marketing and public relations business but also a professional publisher. I now have four books completed and available. Now you can get every service a traditional publisher offers only through a specialized hybrid service that offers extremely high royalties. We are also a qualified eBook publisher.
