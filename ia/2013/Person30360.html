Dan Rice's Summary

For the past 17 years I have been driven by the challenge of organizing and coordinating inclusive teams of people to work together to produce better and faster results. I have worked in Information Technology, Sales, and Marketing in a handful of industries throughout my career including Aviation, Telecommunications, Finance, Insurance, and Retail. I have a passion for Software Development, Mobile technologies, Agile and Lean Methodologies and Online Marketing. I am a driven Scrum Servant and Agile Coach that has a passion for enhancing the software development craft and firmly believes there are better ways to build products…but…understands that Agile is not a one size fits all solution. I believe there is a place for Agile practices in all organizations regardless of the organizations maturity and that learning the spirit of the law of Agile is as important if not more important than the letter of the law.

During my career I’ve been fortunate and privileged to have the opportunity to work with some of the best companies and the brightest teammates anywhere. I love what I do and wake up every day with a drive to pursue greatness.
