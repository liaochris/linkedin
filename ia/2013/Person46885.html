Sandra Garcia's Summary

I have been freelancing as a Communications and Public Relations Specialist since 2005 in various capacities including non-profit capital campaign fundraising, event promotions, corporate sponsorship, and public company marketing.

In 2008, I started my business to help events and companies get media buzz and exposure (Publicity) through media relations campaigns. In 2012, I launched Conscious Public Relations Inc., specializing in Social Media Management and Publicity campaigns for large-scale events and ethical companies. Conscious Public Relations Inc. rewards all successful referrals 10% cash back up to $300.

I also do one-on-one Public Relations (PR) and Social Media Consulting, and copywriting & editing services for resumes, news releases, websites, and blogs. Please feel free to contact me for my hourly rate.

I AM LOOKING FOR medium-sized businesses with a communications budget for a large-scale event, product or service launch, or a kick-ass story that is worthy of news headlines or engaging people to take action.

I am also the Chair of the Board of Directors for the Vancouver Short Film Festival, established in 2005.

Specialties: Publicity, Online Public Relations, Event PR, Marketing Consulting, Media Relations, Copywriting, Copy editing, Proofreading, Branding, Art Direction, and Social Media. Whatever gets you eyeballs!
Industries: Events, Wellness, Environment, Social Enterprise, Social Venture
