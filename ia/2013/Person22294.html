Charlie Pankey's Summary

Executive Business Development / Sales Professional whose experience in Media Planning, Buying and Creative Promotion provide my clients with confidence that their marketing plans are cost effective, creative and targeted properly.

I have spent 18 years helping businesses find success in marketing their business through traditional newspaper marketing, commercial printing and in the last decade online marketing. In 2010 I started Valet Marketing, a 1 to 1 marketing company focused on using directmail, social media, mobile app and mobile web plus strategic 1 to 1 target marketing to create a cycle of growth with each clients customer base.
