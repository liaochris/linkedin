What is an Enrolled Agent?

An Enrolled Agent (EA) is afederally-authorized tax practitioner who has technical expertise in the field of taxation and who is empowered by the U.S. Department of the Treasury torepresent taxpayers before all administrative levels of the Internal RevenueService for audits, collections, and appeals.
