Edward Cook, CGMA's Summary

We are Best in Class ERP Service providers specializing in improving your Business Management Systems. Our systems enable your business to reduce costs by up to 50% and improve efficiency by up to 23%. Our systems eliminate the need for excel spread sheets and provide owners / executives and all designated key stakeholders, the ability to view their business anywhere, anyhow, anytime. Businesses no longer need to perform monthly closeouts as we close out the business daily. We install dashboards that enable CEO's and designated users to see Key performance indicators and help them focus on growing their business, rather than being bogged down in their current systems. We work with small to mid-size enterprises including Engineering, Construction, Manufacturing, Franchises, Non profits, Professional Services and many more.
