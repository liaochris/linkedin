Carrie Boyko's Summary

My ongoing education about dog activities and training broadens my enjoyment of my own pack of 3, while allowing me to share what I have learned with others on All Things Dog Blog. While maintaining a natural angle to dog care, I continue to investigate the many wondrous talents of dogs and their variety of activities, competitions and games. This gives rise to a multitude of topics of interest to my readers.

I am learning to live more green and eat healthier, both to help sustain the Earth and my family's health, the dogs included. My hope is to empower others, through my blog, to eat more healthy and live greener, treading a little more lightly on their bodies and our Earth.

Specialties

My top two areas of expertise would have to be:

1. Tips for a happy, well-balanced pack, whether you have 1 or 5 dogs. My experiences include multi-animal families with cats, rabbits and birds, as well.

2. Tips for living greener and eating organic on a budget.
