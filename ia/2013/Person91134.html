I Buy and sell antiques, dolls and robots. I sell them to fund this project rather than spending the time submitting grant proposals. It affords me access to all the history, research and objects I need only temporarily.

Facebook is the initial venue for my social media exhibition now titled Antiques, Dolls & Artists are Ghosts Too. Season One will include Dolls from different eras taking on their own Facebook Profiles and learning how to use new technology in order to communicate with others from a disjointedness time continuum . The goal is to create an entertaining and existential dialog that transcends time.
