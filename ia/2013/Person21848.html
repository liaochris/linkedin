Chad Halvorson's Summary

I steer the ship (aka CEO) at thisCLICKS. We're the makers of When I Work and App Data Room. Both SaaS products are used around the world by global brands and Fortune 500 companies.

I believe that business software should be created for the end-user, not the decision maker and that businesses should be able to roll out software to their workforce in 5 minutes, not 5 months.

When I Work is thisCLICKS' mobile employee scheduling and communication platform. When I Work simplifies staff scheduling and communication for restaurants, retailers, healthcare providers and countless SMBs all over the world. With over 50,000 monthly active users and hundreds of new businesses signing up every week, When I Work is reinventing the way businesses manage labor and communicate with staff.

thisCLICKS' mobile sales app for iPad, App Data Room, has transformed the way companies and enterprises manage and distribute marketing materials to their sales force. The management of video, documents and rich media is painlessly organized and beautifully presented via App Data Room's mobile CMS and customizable iPad app.

I am also a Managing Partner at Meditech Communications, Inc, a digital marketing agency that provides video production, animation and app development services to the medical device industry.

Since 2002, I've helped Meditech grow its capabilities with web, mobile and interactive technologies. Over the past decade, I've led the development and architecture of interactive tools, apps and web software for Fortune 500 giants like Boston Scientific, UnitedHealthcare and St. Jude Medical; —plus countless small and mid-sized companies.

Specialties: leadership, interaction design, marketing strategies with new technologies, interface design, user experience, web application design, software architecture, mobile design, creative direction
