Maurice Kroon's Summary

I’m an entrepreneur with a passion for internet. I have a formal business education but I’m making websites since high school. During my masters I started Studentify, a non profit focused on helping international students that arrive in the Netherlands. With Studentify we set up StudentTutors, a marketplace where students can share skills. In november ‘12 we launched a side project: giv.rs. A non profit url shortener designed to promote charities for free.
From march ‘13 on we terminated these projects & are focusing on Yippie.

Yippie is the ideal app for any online shopper. If you’re like me, (being Dutch that is), you want to have the best deal possible. But, you don’t have the time to go to each price comparison site to look for better prices.
Yippie is an app for your browser that will automatically compare prices for you while you shop. Whenever it finds a better deal, you get notified. For the rest, it stays nice & quietly in the background. Try it out on http://www.getyippie.com

If you’re interested in joining the team send me a message.
