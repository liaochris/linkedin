Cara Pastore's Summary

I have over ten years of professional experience as an International delegation documentarian and economic development photographer specializing in annual reports, executive portraits, stock libraries, event photos, and commercial advertising photography for non-profit, travel, tourism, and entertainment industry.
I have held the staff position of Photographer for the Georgia Department of Economic Development and the Sheikh of Bahrain Economic Development Delegation. Presently I am a freelance photographer, owner and CEO of F22 Studio in Downtown Atlanta.
Clients include CDC, CIFAL, GSAE, City of Atlanta, Fulton County, Girl Scouts USA, Be Proactive Youth Foundation, Best Academy, Ogilvy PR, BMI, SESAC, Noble Investment, W Hotels, and The Washington Post.

Specialties

I know how to work the camera from both behind and in front of the lens. I Shoot for Perfection. I am an artist and humanitarian capturing images that speak to people's heart.
