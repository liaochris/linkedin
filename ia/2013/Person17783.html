Brian Thayer's Summary

Since 2003, I have used CranioSacral Therapy to help people reduce pain and eliminate the effects of stress on their bodies by tapping into the body's natural, inherent healing process.

Specializing in helping reduce or eliminate: chronic neck & back pain, headaches & migraines, sleep disorders, TMJ problems, ADD, Autism, birth trauma, emotional trauma, anxiety, depression, scoliosis, chronic fatigue, PTSD, and many other conditions.

As an Independent Beachbody Coach I provide people with free support, motivation, and accountability in the areas of nutrition and fitness to help them reach their health & wellness goals.
