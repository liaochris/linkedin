Lauren Ann Schieffer's Summary

America is forgetting how to communicate respectfully. You can’t communicate respectfully with others until you know how to communicate respectfully with yourself.

I simplify respectful communication, champion acceptance of self and elevate others to a place of influence on High Road – which is the only road to true success.

As a nationally recognized expert on respectful communication, and the driving force behind High Road Communication, I have challenged tens of thousands of people nationally and internationally to shake off mediocrity and aspire to excellence.

I'm please to say that my first book, "Road Signs on the High Road of Life" has been listed by LifeStyles Magazine in their top ten Best-of for Corporate Gift Giving and is recommended by multiple treatment facilities across the country as a “must-read” for those in recovery.

I am able to be relate to, and motivate everyone from the custodial staff to C-Level executives. I have spent my career pinpointing how to understand differing communication styles and therefore how to resolve conflict from The High Road. I show others how to expand their comfort zones to achieve personal and professional fulfillment. I have a passion for guiding others – women, men, executives, managers, employees and entrepreneurs alike – in their quest to pass through good and grasp great; to pass beyond survival and into triumph.


Specialties: Respectful Communication, Communicating in Diversity, Conflict Management, Inspiration, Motivation, Empowerment
