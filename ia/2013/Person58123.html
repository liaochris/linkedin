Jan Clark's Summary

I thoroughly enjoy owning my full-time virtual assistant (VA) business because I choose my clients and projects ensuring a diverse workload. In the thick of the technology-based world of a VA since May 2004, I have had the opportunity to explore and master a variety of new technologies, tools, and programs often when they first become available.

My clients are busy individuals and business owners who are savvy enough to know they need to stay on top of their game by partnering with a VA. They 1) procure a highly qualified collaborator, and 2) do so while minimizing the costs associated with hiring a 30-40 hour / week employee!

Projects I love to work on include: social media networking; innovative marketing strategies and implementation; logo/graphic design; business start-up; business branding; proofreading; research; and photography!

Specialties: - Confidential projects are often outsourced to me because I am "removed" from a client's actual company and there's no question about information leaking to the wrong people. Plus, I have a stellar track record!
