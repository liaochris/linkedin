Fernando Carrion's Summary

I'm a creative producer and consultant with more than 12 years of experience creating and producing audiovisual content for films, TV series, commercials, games and interactive media for some of the best international companies and studios worldwide. My academic records include a Master in Fine Arts in Spain and a Master's Degree in Computer Graphics and Animation at the Tokyo Institute of Technology in Japan. In the last years I'm working in transmedia storytelling projects for entertainment, education and social change, always exploring the amazing power of creative storytelling and games for a better world.
