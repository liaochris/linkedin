David Marshall's Summary

My goal at this time would be to expand into other industries and commerce to develop and spread a successful approach to management which far outweighs traditional approaches. I will combine a practical leadership driven approach with individual development which will allow all to contribute to company success and organisational growth. All conducted in an enthusiastic and energetic manner which will rub off on all.

I have a great deal of practical experience in identifying problems and the development of people to fulfill their own and business potential. I have developed factory practice and structure built on sound leadership principles whilst in the mist of factory movement and expansion of which all skills are transferable.
