Ken White's Summary

I am a Financial Consultant, EPC and Branch Manager of Guelph Financial with Investment Planning Counsel. Helping families reduce debt, grow their net worth and protect their estate due to death or injury.
One of our specialties is helping people with disabilities with their retirement planning. Making use of the newly developed Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP) helps families save for the individual's long-term security by offering generous government grants and bonds that when redeemed do not affect their disability payments.

Specialties

financial planner, RDSPs, TFSAs, RRSPs, RESPs, Life insurance, Long Term Care Insurance, Disability Insurance. Mortgage reduction and proper use of Lines of Credit.
