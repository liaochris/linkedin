Charles Kraus' Summary

I began my real estate practice in 1992 and have used my past sales, managerial and accounting experience from other industries in my real estate career. We specialize in Annapolis, MD residential homes and land sales and also provide services for same to Anne Arundel, Queen Anne's and Talbot counties in Maryland.

Some of my past experience in real estate includes:

● Branch Manager / Associate Broker RE/MAX Experts
● Sales Manager Coldwell Banker HRP
● Graduate Realtor Institute
● Graduate of Maryland Association of Realtors - Leadership Academy Class of 2002
● Director Anne Arundel County Association of Realtors Board of Directors (1999-2003)
● Chairman of the Anne Arundel County Association of Realtors Forms Committee (1997/1998)
● Lifetime Member Masters Club of Anne Arundel County Association of Realtors 2002 - 2008
● Lifetime Member Distinguished Sales Acheivement Club of the Anne Arundel County Association of Realtors (1996 - 2008) Treasurer (1997/1998), Vice President (1999/2000), President (2001/2002) and Past President (2003/2008)
● Host of Videos "Strategies for Selling Your Home" and "The Home Buying Process" marketed nationwide by ShowMeHowVideos.com. Purchasers of these videos include consumers, public libraries across North America, online retailers like Barnes and Noble, Target, Amazon and the Department of the Defense for their BRAC transferees.

Follow me on Twitter - http://twitter.com/charleskraus

Specialties: Providing professional residential real estate services to buyers and sellers of land, new and resale homes in Annapolis, MD, Anne Arundel, Caroline, Queen Anne's and Talbot counties in Maryland.
