Beth Hood's Summary

My husband and I run an independent web design and technology consulting company on the Big Island of Hawaii. We specialize in creating WordPress websites and teaching our clients how to have a great relationship with their website. We also help people with social media solutions, newsletters & email autoresponders, and email & web hosting setup and troubleshooting. www.websiteswithaloha.com

We are also authors of a book called "Test Drive Your Future" which helps high school and college students choose a career with confidence, clarity and purpose.

Specialties: Website design, writing, book design and layout, internet marketing, WordPress troubleshooting.
