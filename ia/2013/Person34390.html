Derek Bentley's Summary

www.designbentley.com


I specialize in 3D game environments, accurate and to scale 3D modeling, materials and texturing, realistic lighting, and rendering with a variety of packages. I have extensive experience with mental ray, and V-Ray. ZBrush, 3D-Coat, and Modo are also in my arsenal of 3D Tools. Working on game environments is where most of my experience lies, but I also have experience, and a liking to modeling and animating characters, and ambient creatures. I also excel at modeling hard surface models from either photographs, or engineering reference, as well as retopologizing from highly detailed 3D scans. I can bring a lot of ingenuity to any company with my technical skills, and always find ways to improve production pipelines through the use of existing tools, or can guide develop new tools.. Thanks for stopping by.
