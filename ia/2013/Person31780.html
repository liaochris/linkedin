David Evans' Summary

Digicraft helps Internet startups execute successful marketing strategies, receive incredible media exposure, acquire new customers, identify and enter new markets, attract investment, forge lucrative partnerships, develop new products and much more.

My day job is editing OnlineDatingPost.com, the TechCrunch of the online dating industry. Fifteen years of consulting experience with many groundbreaking and influential international web sites and Internet advertising projects. Extensive involvement with technical and marketing teams on a variety of leading-edge projects.

Past five years consulting for startup and established dating sites ($1.5B industry).

Consulting engagements with strategic thinkers and key innovators at a variety of clients ranging from startups to Fortune 100 businesses: Matchmine, Engage.com, Trufina, Backgroundchecks.com, Anderson & Lembke (Interpublic Group), Date.com, Chemistry.com, IBM, Modem Media, Tangent Design and StratsoftLLC.

Extensive market research & analysis across a variety of technology sectors: social networking and dating, telecom, data centers, fiber optic networks, WiFi, business intelligence, consumer technology products.
