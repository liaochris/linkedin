Elizabeth Lopez, J.D., M.B.A.'s Summary

As VP of our Global Network, Elizabeth manages entrepreneur relations, leads the application and selection process, and is the central node of WINs network of mentors and corporate partners. She works closely with WIN companies to achieve their goals, raise follow-on funding and connect them to the network. Elizabeth also leads the research and due diligence for vetting international start-ups prior to entering the US market and is the first point of contact with our companies.

Elizabeth has a J.D, M.B.A, with a concentration in International and Corporate Securities law and an MBA in Finance. Before joining the W.I.N team Elizabeth was working under the Counselor General for Colombia in Miami, focusing on international affairs.

The Worldwide Investor Network (WIN), is a NY-based ecosystem fully focused on funding and accelerating international tech startups from around the world. In one year we had several companies funded, established an ecosystem of partners from NY and around the world that supports all of our entrepreneurs and hundreds of companies applying to present.
