Jeff Black's Summary

I am a seasoned and versatile IT professional continuously employed in information technology since 1985. I have expertise in project management, systems architecture, application development, and database administration. I enjoy learning about new technologies and how to apply them to solve business problems. I also enjoy participating in the Open Source community and contributing to projects like Struts 2, OpenMRS, and Perl.

I presently work for Aetna in Franklin, TN as a software engineer supporting several line-of-business systems including the firm's "pricing" system.

Before joining Aetna (Previously Coventry Health Care) I had the opportunity to work with two State governments: I worked with the Texas Workforce Commission in Austin, TX and the Department of Child Services in Indianapolis, IN. In Austin had the opportunity to work on a project to integrate a new child-care benefits management system into the State's existing TWIST platform. TWIST is used to track benefit and retraining programs for unemployed workers and welfare recipients throughout Texas. With Indiana's Department of Child Services in Indianapolis I worked on the State's SACWIS system (ICWIS) which is used for the management and support of at-risk and foster children throughout Indiana.

Before traveling to Indiana and Texas I was involved with the operation of I.T. consulting practices in the Greater Nashville Area of Middle Tennessee. I have had the opportunity to serve clients in a variety of business segments, including insurance, finance, and construction.
