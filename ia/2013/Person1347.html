Adam DeGraide's Summary

Simply stated, Adam DeGraide is where rock ’n roll meets the insurance industry.
As a veteran Internet marketer and sales guru, DeGraide has made a name for himself over the past decade as an entrepreneur, record producer, and motivational speaker.
In 1997, DeGraide started his first company, BZ Results, in Providence, Rhode Island. BZ Results is a digital marketing company that helps car dealers use the Internet to grow their businesses. In 2006, with over 1,600 dealership clients and 150 employees, DeGraide and his partners sold BZ Results to Advanced Digital Processing (ADP).
After the sale of BZ Results, DeGraide started Astonish, which was recently ranked 267th on the Inc. 500 list of fastest growing private companies in the U.S.

As the founder and CEO of Astonish, DeGraide and his team are the driving forces behind a vision to help the insurance industry across the country grow their businesses by using the Internet. DeGraide and his team have been highlighted in Rough Notes in an article about Astonish titled “Unfair Advantage” as well as an Insurance Journal cover story titled, “The Face of Freakin’ Agency Marketing.” He was also featured on the cover of National Underwriter Magazine in the article entitled “Game Changers”.

Now currently serving over 800 retail brokers in America, Adam DeGraide and his team at Astonish are encouraging the insurance industry across the country to “Join the Internet marketing revolution, or get left behind!”

Adam DeGraide's Experience

CEO, Founder

Astonish

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Insurance industry

January 2006 – Present (7 years 8 months) Warwick, RI

Astonish is on a mission to change the way insurance is sold in America. They firmly believe the local distribution channel is the greatest asset of the insurance industry. They are dedicated to helping this channel (including the agencies, firms, and individuals) adapt to the modern landscape of Internet consumers in order to thrive and not get left behind.

For over half a decade, Astonish has been the leader in providing digital marketing solutions for the insurance industry. Over the past 15 years, Astonish has built a complete digital marketing system founded upon a “FIND - SELL - KEEP” methodology. That is to say, the Astonish system helps their clients:

• FIND a higher quality and quantity of sales opportunities for less cost
• SELL a higher percentage of those customers through training and technology
• KEEP more customers for life
The Astonish system leverages three main arenas to help their clients grow: digital marketing, coaching and training, and the Virtual Profit Center. In 2012 Astonish was named in Inc 500’s fastest growing companies in the nation.
