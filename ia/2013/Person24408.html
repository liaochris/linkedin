Christopher Halladay's Summary

Christopher Halladay was born and raised in West Chester, PA, and studied and prospered in Washington DC where he was a registered Lobbyist. After some soul searching he decided to leave our Nation’s Capital to pursue his true passion. He moved to Los Angeles to study acting at The American Academy of Dramatic Arts where he received his 2nd degree. Chris has also studied abroad at the British-American Drama Academy at Oxford University, UK. and he recently completed his Master’s Degree in Acting at the Mason Gross School of the Arts at Rutgers University.

Christopher has been seen in several Films, Television shows and national commercials. He is proud to have worked with many prestigious theatres such as, The Culture Project, Ensemble Studio Theatre and The Actors Company Theatre in New York, as well as A Noise Within, South Coast Repertory Theater, and The Pasadena Playhouse in the Los Angeles area.

In addition to Acting, Christopher also teaches at the University level, currently at Rutgers, Rider and Montclair State Universities. He also coaches privately.
