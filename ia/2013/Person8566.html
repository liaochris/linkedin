Ann Ronan's Summary

My vision is to live "Everyday a Vacation Day" and I want you to have this too! My work delights my heart and combines all my passions -travel, teaching and connecting others to what they love.

I am a Certified Tour Director (International Tour Management Institute) and travel consultant with Expedia. Specialties: River Cruises. Destination Weddings and Honeymoons and Group Travel - nothing like bringing the entire family together on a cruise to celebrate a special occasion.

Continue to serve my amazing clients at GrantRight, writing government proposals that have brought more than 25 million dollars in awards to nonprofits, higher education and hospitals. Specialty is federal grant proposals.
