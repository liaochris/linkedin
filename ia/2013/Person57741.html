James Pickle's Experience

MSPT, DPT, CEO

Prospect Sports Medicine & Rehabilitation

August 2003 – Present (10 years 4 months)

At Prospect Sports Medicine and Rehabilitation we treat ALL types of injuries. Our specialty is in Spinal Dysfunction and Orthopedic Injuries which are often associated with athletics, work related tasks and active lifestyles.

Our primary goal is to restore our patients to their optimal strength and to ensure their injury doesn’t prevent them from doing the activities they love. We accomplish this by using the best and most current physical therapy techniques available.

Prospect Sports Medicine and Rehabilitation patients enjoy personalized attention and receive a comprehensive education about their injury and the recommended long term treatment. When a patient leaves our care they are EXPERT in the maintenance and preventative techniques needed to enjoy their lives to the fullest.

NO WAITING and NO GROUP therapy! You won't find a busy waiting room here. We respect your busy schedule, privacy and personal needs. Prompt service, individual attention and personalized therapy is who we are and what we do.

We believe in working together for quality treatment, injury prevention and long term health.
