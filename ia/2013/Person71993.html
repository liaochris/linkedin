Kareem Fahmi's Summary

I've spent seventeen years creating successful advertising campaigns, selling them to temperamental clients, winning awards, and dealing with questions like “Can you take the joke out, but still keep it funny?”

I've crafted highly-awarded creative solutions for brands such as Nike, Ubisoft Entertainment, Sega, Electronic Arts, Bank of America, Constellation Wines, and plenty more. I've burned the midnight oil at agencies such as FCB, Ogilvy, agency.com, and Organic. And I've taken home shiny bits of metal from the One Show, the New York Festival, and other award shows.

I'm currently a partner and creative director at Factor, a San Francisco-based agency offering disruptive creative solutions for brands both large and small. With a small core team of highly-awarded principals, and an extensive freelance network of creative, strategic, and technical talent, we deliver compelling creative solutions at significantly lower cost than traditionally-structured agencies.

Kareem Fahmi's Projects

  • 'Pass the Bottle' social-media promotion

    • October 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Kareem Fahmi, Jola Hyjek, Lauren Peters, Minji Kim, Molly Maheras, Bradley Mancuso, Anne Donnard, Jamie Shiembob

    'Pass the Bottle' is a social game that lets you pair wines with your friends. Sign into Facebook, tell us who you're drinking with, and we'll suggest a wine that matches your friend's personality. We created an algorithm that scans your friends' Facebook activity to determine their personality traits and deliver surprisingly accurate suggestions. 'Pass' them a virtual bottle of that wine, and you get instant rewards.

    We created 'Pass the Bottle' for Constellation Wines, who asked us to market a selected portfolio of their wines to millennial consumers.

  • Nike "Baby It's Cold Outside" interactive film

    • October 2008 to March 2009
    Team Members: Kareem Fahmi, Dan Kowta, Brian Kiel, Tony Shumskas, Vince Quilici

    Nike challenged us to create a single rich-media ad that would allow consumers to buy the entire range of Nike Women apparel designed for winter use. We created an interactive film that featured a woman running through constantly-changing winter environments. As her environments change, so do the clothes she wears. The ad performed so well that we created a film version that aired in all of Nike's retail outlets worldwide, and appeared on the Nike.com homepage throughout the holiday shopping season.

  • Midway Games: 'So Real' TV spot

    • June 2009 to October 2009
    Team Members: Kareem Fahmi, Scott Hayman, Brett Hocker

    'TNA Impact' is the videogame version of the popular wrestling series. We cast the real stars from the television franchise, and created an offbeat spot promoting 'wrestling so real, you feel pain.'

  • Hilton: Corporate website

    • January 2011 to January 2012
    Team Members: Kareem Fahmi, David Paslay, sacha reeb, hannah lee, Rosemary Williams, Urs Ross, Tomoko Izumoto, Mas Tsujimoto, Jessica Dufour, Carlo Guardascione, Bonnie Hoag

    Hilton Worldwide, the parent company that owns ten hospitality brands including Hilton, Waldorf-Astoria, DoubleTree, and more, asked us to create an elegant corporate website aimed at hotel developers, job-seekers, investors, and other potential B2B partners. It's not easy to create a corporate site when you have ten different brands with wildly different design aesthetics and requirements, but I think we succeeded. The finished site numbered over 200 pages.
