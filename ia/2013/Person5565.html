Leslie Wardman's Publications

  • Love, Dating & The Beatles

    • Smashwords
    • November 19, 2011
    Authors: Leslie Wardman

    Love is one of biggest mysteries of all time. Some will never experience it. Some only once...maybe twice. Whatever the case, it's nice to have some sort of a central point for balancing thoughts when it comes to writing about Love & Dating. I have been pondering what would be best regarding this matter since pressure is increasing on getting a book done (apparently you're nobody until you've written one) What would keep from me saturating dialogue with satire? The Beatles. This is it...inspired by connecting the dots between love, dating and The Beatles.

    Yesterday, I met my daughter, 24, at Starbucks. We were going to watch some local, pro-circuit tennis. Starbucks had the Abbey Road CD sitting for sale next to the cash register. I picked it up and asked her to identify the band from the left to right. She immediately picked out Paul & Ringo from the middle. She was troubled regarding 'the guys' on the end. She pointed to George and identified him as "Dave." I couldn't exactly keep a poker face on this comment. Once in the car she thanked me for embarrassing her. Oh well...It was entertaining as well as thought-provoking. Here I sit 30 hours later typing what has since been inspired as "Love, Dating & the Beatles." There is nothing like having your medicine put to music and validated by one of the most famous bands ever.
