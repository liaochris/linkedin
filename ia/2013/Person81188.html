Matt Lindsay's Summary

After much debate and one year of working towards my B.Sc., I bid adieu to my plans of pursuing medicine, and transferred into the engineering program at UBCO. Instantly, I knew it was a great fit and I was excited to begin working towards a career as a civil engineer.

Now nearing the completion of my third year, I have completed three co-op work terms, gaining valuable work experience and skills in the areas of:

- Construction monitoring
- Materials testing
- Estimating
- Quantity surveying
- Project coordination

I have had the privilege of being involved in and observing many different construction projects, all over Western and Northern Canada, including highway construction in the Northwest Territories, earthworks in the Oil Sands, and investigative pipeline digs in Saskatchewan.

My specialties include technical communication, quantity surveying, public speaking, team building, MS Excel, and, like all talented co-op students, detailed and complete operation and maintenance manuals of unparalleled quality.

I always enjoy meeting new professional acquaintances. Please do not hesitate to get in touch if you would like to discuss anything such as co-op at UBC, construction and/or motorcycles.
