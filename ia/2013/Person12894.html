Ben Griswold's Summary

Ben Griswold has over fifteen years of large-scale software development experience working with financial, real-estate, insurance, clinical research, educational, medical and marketing companies. Currently, Ben is a principal consultant at Fairway Technologies in La Jolla, CA where he provides technical leadership as architect and developer to a number of west coast firms.

Ben is a community speaker, author of johnnycoder.com, open source contributor and facilitator of multiple developer code clubs. Ben is currently focused on developing MVC web applications using the Microsoft stack. He recently open-sourced SharpRepository, a .NET generic repository written in C#, and recently completed his first ultra marathon.

Ben earned a degree in Biology from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and holds numerous technical certifications including MCPD, MCTS, MCSD and MCP.
