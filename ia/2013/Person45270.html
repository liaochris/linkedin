Scott Hutchinson's Summary

Malham is a consultancy driven by Scott Hutchinson that specialises in improving business and individual performance via recruitment, coaching and training.

In Malham's second year of trading it joined the Institute of Interim Management's list of ‘leading providers’ listing the top 80 interim service providers in the UK market. As well as naming 12 "star provider consultants" the survey singled out five senior leaders of interim businesses, including Scott.

The company is driven and owned by Scott Hutchinson who has earned his strong reputation through professionalism, ethical values and a desire to do the right thing by his clients.

Scott has 18 years experience in working in both large corporate and SME business environments. The last ten years have been spent in his own consultancy business where he has helped clients such as Coca Cola Enterprises, Baxters and Tayto improve their business performance and acquire the very best talented individuals .

He has also helped smaller, exciting, growing SME businesses acquire and retain top talent that have transformed their organisations.

In addition, he has provided career advice to literally hundreds of senior professionals, helping them to fulfil their potential.
