Chris Plant's Summary

Residential real estate broker with over 20 years experience in the Denver, Littleton and Highlands Ranch area. Over 75% of my business is derived from referrals and past clients.

Across the country, real estate is on a rebound. The metro Denver market is no exception.

Home prices are increasing rapidly but there is still opportunities for buyers.

Investing in real estate inside IRA's is becoming very popular now and there is a ton of options and opportunities. I can help the investor use their tax deferred account to multiply their returns inside the IRA.

The investment real estate arena is booming. Fix-ups are getting a little harder to find, but the rental market in Denver, Littleton and Highlands Ranch is great. Cash flowing homes are available and when the market finally does come around, investors will find themselves in an excellent position with a ton of equity. I am available to assist investors in finding good rentals.

Specialties: Littleton Denver residential single family home, investment property Littleton Denver, condo sales Littleton Denver. Showing people how to invest in real estate in their self-directed IRA. Helping distressed home owners understand their options. Multi-family opportunities.
