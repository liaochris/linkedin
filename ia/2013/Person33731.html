Decor Group | Fusion | Cribs' Summary

Here is a little about Decor Fusion and what we can do for you!

Decor:Fusion is a rapidly expanding interior design company based near Manchester city centre offering commercial interior design solutions across the UK. We boast a client base that ranges from small independent hotels, restaurants and businesses to large multi-national hotel chains.

Following the success of MNB Furniture, Decor:Fusion was established as a brand to offer a professional and comprehensive commercial interior design service at a price businesses can afford. Our director created the brand on the back of many years experience in the commercial interiors industry.

Our team consists of individuals with considerable awareness in both the interior design and business world. Whether it is interior design and fit out; manufacturing solutions; global sourcing of associated products or furniture maintenance, we realize that the key to a successful business is a clear understanding of how the client organization functions, and the image it wishes to project. From this starting point we work hard to produce an interior design scheme that is both functional and inspirational whilst ensuring that it is commercially viable. We believe in providing a high quality service, which means creating a solution to suit our clients. Our team go about their work in a co-ordinated and professional manner, guided by industry association standards. We also pay particular attention to after care, because we realise how important it is for our clients to realise that we are always just a phone call away.

Specialties

Interior Design, Interior Solutions, Global Sourcing, Manufacturing Solutions, Furniture Maitenance
