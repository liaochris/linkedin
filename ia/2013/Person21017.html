O'Hannigan Cathleen's Summary

20 years experience selling homes, somewhere over 900 by now, lost count. Some years were very part time as I was involved in building and flipping. Built two homes, remodeled 7, relocated three times, so I understand the relocation side of moving inside out!

I am the best listener I have ever met, and possess world class negotiation skills, most of which I learned at the the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London.

I Love real estate, love the homes and the incredible people I meet from all over the world.

Specialties

Negotiating, listening, truly caring about my clients, old fashioned hard work mixed with innovative sales. Much experience in short sales, foreclosures, Cary and Apex Real estate.
