Geri Wells' Summary

Geri is a highly experienced Executive Coach and Consultant specializing in Collaborative Leadership. Her work with business and community leaders is focused on building their capacity to collaboratively lead positive change in ways that improve resiliency.

Geri has a proven track record helping leaders to plan, guide, and navigate high stakes change that achieves desired objectives while building resiliency. This is accomplished by improving leadership effectiveness and leadership team dynamics, creating system support and alignment, building collaborative relationships across and beyond organizational boundaries, and engaging multiple stakeholders in constructive dialogue that results in high value decision-making, planning, problem-solving, organizational learning and innovation.

For example, Geri has helped leaders of Fortune 50 companies, small businesses, public agencies, and community based organizations to effectively …
• Transition from managing assets to leading people.
• Assess and create organizational readiness for change.
• Define and frame issues in the context of complex systems.
• Identify and leverage strengths for optimum outcomes.
• Create conditions of support for desired change.
• Heighten awareness and calibrate responses to achieve desired results.
• Use the power of presence to influence and improve leadership team dynamics.
• Lead collaborative initiatives that cross organizational or sector boundaries.
• Shift critical conversations and group deliberations for more positive outcomes.
• Lead constructive multi-stakeholder dialogues that create new value and new options.
• Understand and work with organizational defenses that can destroy value.
• Shape a culture of collaboration, learning and innovation.

Specialties:
Executive Coaching; Leadership Team Development; Stakeholder Dialogue; Collaborative Thinking, Learning & Innovation.
