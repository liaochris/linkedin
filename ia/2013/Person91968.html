Micah Kasman's Summary

With 10 years experience working on both the client and agency side, I've directed numerous branding, communications, marketing and design initiatives for a wide variety of businesses, including Facebook, Joie de Vivre Hotels, Social+Capital Partnership, 137 Ventures, Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, Monterey Bay Aquarium, Cord Blood Registry, SF SPCA, Oracle, HP, Coral Reef Alliance, Alphonso and BarTrendr.

On a personal level, I believe unique experiences help drive creativity. Following one my passions, I was featured as one of sixteen final competitors on ABC's The Taste.
