Ian Mullins' Summary

Ian Mullins is the CEO of AdSpruce - The Mobile Video Ad Network (www.adspruce.com) and the CEO of Yamgo Ltd (http://www.yamgo.com), a mobile TV service provider specialising in end-to-end mobile content distribution including live TV to mobile, ad-funded content publishing and mobile social networking (http://www.yamgo.mobi). Ian has over twelve years experience in the mobile industry and is an expert in video advertising technologies and delivery of video to mobile devices . Prior to founding Yamgo, Ian was a Director for www.thegamesarcade.com, a mobile games retailer where he developed a games portal delivering over 1000 games in 40 countries. Ian began his career as an Investment Analyst for Talisman House in London before moving into IT strategy consultancy for Engage Energy in Canada and then Morse Plc in London focusing on mobile technology solutions. Ian holds an MSc in IT and Data Communications and a BA (Hons) in Business and Finance. Ian is passionate about mobile entertainment and enjoys skydiving, paragliding, mountain biking and white water kayaking in his spare time.
