Cathy Fisher's Projects

  • UMMA Mobile Tour System

    • November 2012 to April 2013
    Team Members: Cathy Fisher, Shauna Masura, Victoria Lungu

    A Drupal-based responsive website for creating, managing, and using custom museum tours. Allows docents to add supplementary multimedia resources to enhance visitors' understanding of artworks in the museum.

  • Podcasts for iPhone Redesign

    • September 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Cathy Fisher

    The semester-long project for my Interaction Design class was to either create or redesign an interface in order to address a problem. I chose to redesign Apple's Podcasts for iPhone app, which splintered off from the Music app but failed to address the unique ways in which users listen to podcasts.

  • iTAPS That

    • September 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Cathy Fisher, Megan Schwarz, Krista Soltis, Rachael Leduc, Jason Ching

    My semester-long group project for Database Application Design was building a web application that could replace the Tracking and Planning Sheets that MSI students at the School of Information have to use in order to check whether or not they've fulfilled their degree requirements.

  • Slashdot User Experience Research

    • January 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Cathy Fisher, Benjamin Olger, Nikki Potter, Chisheng Li

    My group's semester project for SI 622 - Evaluation of Systems and Services was for the venerable and long-lived science and technology news site Slashdot. They were interested in identifying usability problems with their site and targeting ways in which they could attract a younger demographic. We conducted research using a variety of methods, including heuristic analysis, competitive analysis, surveys, personas, scenarios, interviews, and usability tests.

  • Brine Weekly

    • February 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Cathy Fisher, Max Eddy, Kris Jacque, Zachery Beauvais

    A multi-media online humor magazine I co-founded with some fellow Gargoyle alums. Non-weekly.
