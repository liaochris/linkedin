Mark Miller's Summary

I am a Truth@Work Chapter President and business coach specializing in helping business owners Trust God in the marketplace.

Our world needs business owners to utilize our influence and position to impact the seven mountains of our culture by following Jesus. As we do this we will create jobs, civil dialogue and foster a an economy based on sustainable values.

This challenging world demands marketplace leaders hear God in their business. Truth@Work provides this by:
1) Gathering business leaders in biblical agreement to help each other grow our businesses God's way
2) Teaching practical biblical economic principles
3) Creating a safe place for God to show up and speak.

Prior to business coaching I was a professional services executive with expertise in building and managing high performance teams to achieve business results in the educational software space.

I love working with people to transform their lives and businesses.

Specialties

Trust coaching, facilitation, P&L management, God's Economy, Core Values coaching
