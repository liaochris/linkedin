Dariush Ghatan's Summary

In short:
Building value, driving revenue and increasing profits through accelerated innovation. Creation of corporate-entrepreneurship ventures. Identifying and driving commercialization of hidden and under-utilized assets.

Longer version:
I base my work with Googol partially on my experience as an intrapreneur (intra-organizational entrepreneur) at various large companies prior to founding Googol. I am the principal business architect within Googol, assisting a host of corporations in developing their businesses strategically, tactically and action-oriented. I thrive on creating, accelerating and realizing value and growth. I hold a bachelor of science (bsc) from the university of Uppsala and a business administration and economics degree, with advanced studies in accounting from the university of Stockholm and have as well completed post-graduate studies at Harvard Business School in Boston, MA, USA.

Before founding Googol, I worked in various positions within the Kinnevik group mostly on the European and american markets within finance and telecommunications. I have also worked within the international banking and finance sector. I founded his first company in 1988 and have since co-founded and advised numerous company start-ups and change-processes in blue-chips. I also lecture about innovation management and entrepreneurship and have been active at institutions such as Tokyo Institute of Technology (TITO), Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) executive school, Stockholm School of Entrepreneurship, IHM Business School, SIME and IAC in Daejeong Korea.

My passion, apart from the growth and innovation business, is of course firstly my family. I also find great interest in contemporary art, architecture and our nature. I love spending time in the forest, jungle, hillsides or even 80 feet below the surface free-diving. To me, compassion curiosity and courage have become lead words. I strongly believe in leadership beyond personal winnings.
