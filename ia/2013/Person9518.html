Ariella Mostkoff's Summary

I am a recent graduate of the Masters of Graphic Design Program at North Carolina State University. I am passionate about designing meaningful experiences and exploring design’s role in shaping our future and changing our present. I have a collaborative + open mindset, and systems-oriented + human-centered design approach. I embrace design as an ongoing process of exploration, iteration, and opportunity. I believe design should be delightful, serendipitous, provocative, and drop-dead gorgeous.
