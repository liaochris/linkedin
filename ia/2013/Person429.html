Aaron Olson's Summary

Aaron Chandler Olson is a Detroit based front-end engineer and designer with a passion for creating inspiring user experiences.

He is a pragmatic developer who has tempered his skills in the fast paced and unpredictable world of newsroom journalism. This experience has given him the ability to create marquee quality projects while facing tight deadlines often with shifting requirements.

His experience working with news organizations, non-profits and as a mentor for student journalists has taught Olson the importance of creating a supportive and collaborative environment in order to find the best solutions.

Born and raised in Michigan, he developed his design aesthetic while collecting Absolut Vodka ads and vintage paperbacks as a child before going to Michigan State University and growing into a die-hard Whovian.
