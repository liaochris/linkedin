Why is all that alphabet soup behind my name important to you??? ABR, SRES, e-PRO, CDPE, Realtor®. I have extra training to make your Real Estate transaction run smooth for you.

The Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR®) designation is the benchmark of excellence in buyer representation. This coveted designation is awarded to real estate practitioners by the Nat. Assoc. of REALTORS® who meet the specified educational and practical experience criteria.

SRES - Seniors Real Estate Specialists® are REALTORS® qualified to meet the special needs and concerns of maturing Americans.

e-PRO Internet Professionals
Real estate agents who have earned e-PRO Certification are Internet professionals who have completed extensive training using the Web. e-PRO Certification means the real estate professional is prepared to employ the latest techniques and services for your benefit, just like you've grown to expect from a professional.

CDPE - Certified Distressed Property Expert.
Are you in a "distressed" situation with real estate? Do you need knowledge or assistance with Pre-Foreclosure, Foreclosure, Short Sales and Loan Mods. We can help with free consulting anywhere in the US. Learn what your options are and you get to decide the course of action to take.

The CDPE education also enhances my ability to work with buyers who want to take advantage of deals in "distressed" property such as Short Sales and Bank Owned REO's and Foreclosures.
