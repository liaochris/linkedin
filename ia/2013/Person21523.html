Michael Romano's Summary

Professional land development manager with broad experience in managing all technical aspects of the residential development process.

Specialties

Plan and Manage all aspects of residential Land Development from feasibility through bond release including: land planning, coordination and management of all required subconsultants, preparation of development cost estimates and project schedules, engineering management, construction management, facilitation of final plat recording and bonding, management of post recording development issues and punch list resolution resulting in final acceptance and bond release.
