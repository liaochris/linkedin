Craig Warhurst's Summary

36 years practice experience focusing primarily on corrective care as well as developing wellness lifestyles for practice members of all ages.

Extensive experience in care of newborns to centenarians.

Specific techniques: Specific side posture upper cervical, Corrective Care, Sacral Occipital Technique, Cranial, Applied Kinesiology, Network Spinal Analysis, Diversified, Activator, Nimmo.

First line Therapy : Nutritional program directed at metabolic syndrome, hypertension, cholesterol normalizing, weight control.

Science Based Nutrition: Nutritional programs based on extensive laboratory blood analysis to improve overall health and function of all organs and glands.

Specialties: Wellness programs for adults and children. Years of experience in corrective care of abnormal spinal curves, injuries sustained in auto accidents, work injuries, and sports injuries.

Lifestyle modification as it relates to weight loss.
