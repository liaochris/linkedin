Kym Oberauer's Summary

Often described as meticulous and methodical, Kym Oberauer began his career with Empire Ridge in 1994, one of Australia’s leading Multimedia and Internet development companies of the time. For the next 4 years interactive CD-ROMS and developing custom web applications were his specialty.

In 1998 it was time to move on. With 3 like minded colleges, Digital Zoo was formed and set about becoming a full service Multimedia agency. As technical director, Kym was responsible for the design, management and implementation of numerous multimedia projects and corporate websites. Customers included Telstra Super, the MSO, Qantas and AMP. During this time Kym created the SAFTRAC Compliance Management System for Minter Ellison Lawyers.

With the success of the SAFTRAC Compliance Management System Kym was approached to join the new company formed to manage and market the system. From 2005 to 2007 he acted as the SAFETRAC technology manager, designing and managing incremental improvements to the system for a growing user base. Today the system provides quality risk management training for tens-of-thousands of users and blue chip companies around the globe.

After 2 years with SAFETRAC Kym sort a new challenge and created KSO IT Consulting dedicated to helping savvy people build a successful online business by…

- delivering customer focused results
- with an effective marketing strategy
- and the appropriate use of technology
