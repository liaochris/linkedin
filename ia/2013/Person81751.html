Lise Gareau's Summary

Life has given me an abundance of experience both personal and professional. These experiences have transformed me. I have become astute at seeing through the confusion others are experiencing without losing sight of the impact the situation is having on the whole person.

I am passionate about leadership and governance. I believe in building on solid foundations. To me, good governance is about strengthening the foundations and good leadership is about strategically guiding the organisation towards its vision.

Every system is perfectly designed to attain the results it is getting. I have the skills and experience that can help you get to where you want to go. For me, there is no greater reward than having journeyed with someone who succeeds in reaching their goal.

All services are available in French and English.
