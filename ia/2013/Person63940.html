Jim Jeffers' Summary

As a self taught-designer and developer with over ten years of professional experience in web application conceptualization, design, and implementation. I completed my bachelors degree at ASU with a concentration in business marketing. I've also completed two graduate programs. The first was a masters certificate of Interaction Design at the University of Queensland in Brisbane, Australia. The second, was the basic and advanced track d.school programs at the HPI School of Design Thinking in Berlin, Germany.

User Experience / Design Thinking:

I currently facilitate and conduct design research and paper prototyping sessions for my clients. I help clients discover target audiences and design workflows in their products to help them best address their target audiences needs while appealing to their motives. We rapidly iterate these tests on paper to determine a concrete solution that we can test on users.

Mobile Development:

I currently help my clients design and develop mobile applications and mobile web applications. I can advise clients on platforms to pursue and whether or not to develop native, HTML5, or hybrid mobile applications.

Development and Design:

I have worked as a visual designer and developer for over 10 years with a focus on web. In addition to design creation I also can produce production worthy, cross-browser compatible html/css/js builds for visual designs following best practices for semantic markup, accessibility, and progressive enhancement. Finally, I am well versed in server side frameworks such as Ruby on Rails, PHP including (Wordpress and Expression Engine), and even .NET's MVC solution so I can work well with software developers.
