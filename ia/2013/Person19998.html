Carl Hughes' Summary

I am a cloud technology consultant and IT trainer. I help business and individuals work and play online.

I specialise in Google Apps for Business and Education which is a platform where you can send emails, produce documents, manage finances and collaborate with others. There is no need to install or update costly software as it is all done through a web browser or an app on your phone. This means there is no need for backups and you can make informed decisions with information at your finger tips wherever you are.

Android Phones are my comfort zone so you if you want to understand how to use them to extend your business or your own life; I'd love to help you.

I can assist you connect and share information remotely with your team, clients and suppliers using Google Hangouts . All you need is a phone or computer with an internet connection!

Even making a telephone call can be done in the cloud as you can save loads of money with Voice over IP (VOIP) phone systems and you dont even need a physical phone...ask me how!

I am familiar with all computer and mobile operating systems including Microsoft Windows 8, Apple OS Lion, iPhones, Android Tablets and even the more quirky devices like Windows Mobile and Blackberry!

I'm not just a geek! My interest and passions lie in harnessing freely available energy, digital alternative currencies like Bitcoin and interplanetary travel!

I'd like to connect with people who know how to create zero point energy which is a way of harnessing energy from the source field much like Tesla did over 100 years ago!​
