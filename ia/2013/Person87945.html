Marv Stanger's Summary

Marv Stanger is a Senior Loan Officer who has been originating mortgages in the Washington DC metropolitan area for over 18 years.

He entered the industry working as a settlement officer in a local real estate law firm. This included working with real estate agents as well as home buyers and home sellers to ensure their closings took place on time and according to agreed upon standards. He has financially assisted thousands of clients fulfill their dream to become homeowners and has helped to strengthen their financial position.

Marv enjoys working with a number of repeat and referral business from past clients. He also looks forward to helping new clients achieve their real estate dreams and securing their financial future.

He is licensed Virginia and Maryland and is fully compliant with all new government regulations.
