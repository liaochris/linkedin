Marguerite Moore Callaway's Summary

Marguerite Moore Callaway is Founder and President of the Callaway Companies, a group of affiliated companies providing international management consultancy as well as leadership development and practical management training services. Her company's consulting activity specializes in international healthcare system development here in the USA and abroad. Her ongoing leadership development and management training programs are also domestic and international. Since 2005, Callaway has developed and delivered formal academic and practical face-to-face and online leadership/management workshops to clients and educational organizations throughout the USA and UK, in several African countries, and in China.

In 2010, Callaway created a new company, the Callaway Leadership Institute (CLI) to house our various management training and leadership learning programs. In 2013, in response to growing demand for more online resources, CLI will be offering a few leadership development webinars focused specifically on Transformational Leadership.

A published author of three books, Ms. Callaway’s most recent title, Transformational Leadership: The Seven Aspirations, focuses on the leadership skills needed to undertake essential organizational transformation.

Specialties: leadership development and practical management training, systems thinking applied to strategy development, human motivation in the workplace, and international educational collaborations.
