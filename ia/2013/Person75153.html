  • (Game Development) Relocation, A First person VR, action adventure game

    • July 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Kevin Razmus, Andre Wilson

    Relocation: Pre-Alpha Demo Game Design Synopsis.

    The Look
    The game will be highly detailed with extensive post processing effects to give a believable immersive first person experience. This game demo is being made for VR, full Oculus Rift support has been built in from the beginning, but it will also support standard monitors.

    Abilities
    The player will have the ability to fly with the Emergency Evac Suit’s jetpack. Your strength will be slightly increased by the suit, which will allow for slightly higher jumping, and running longer distances without tiring out.

    Design
    The game will be designed with an open world approach, the player is able to explore the facility at will. Certain areas will include scripted triggers that will allow for great cinematic storytelling and intense moments. Most weapons will be close range melee, with some long range available but with extremely limited ammo.

    Story
    The demo takes place on earth a few years prior to Relocation Corp. sending people to the space station. You will play as a potential client of the mega corporation, Relocation Corp. which is pioneering in off-planet real estate. They are the first to launch a full city space station, which is capable of sustaining 50k people. It has recently been launched and is currently in orbit while the construction crew works around the clock to prepare it for the first arrivals of the middle class workforce.

    You were invited to tour the spaceport test facility where the first transport ship has just returned from dropping off a construction crew. You will have access to the lounge and hanger viewing area, and testing the Emergency Evac Suit.
