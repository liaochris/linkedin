Stephen Abbott's Summary

Stephen Abbott is the owner and founder of Abbott Public Relations, a New England-based public relations firm focused on small and home businesses and political candidates.

He brings more than a decade of retail, customer relations and writing experience as well as a wealth of political campaign skills to Abbott Public Relations.

Specialties

Writing Skills include news writing and editing, news releases, fact sheets, company histories, corporate, personal and political biographies, and advertising copy writing. Business and Political Skills include public relations and reputation management campaigns, political campaign consulting, including message development, business and political campaign effectiveness audits, crisis management for companies, political campaigns and individuals.
