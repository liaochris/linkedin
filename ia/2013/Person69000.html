Josh Locatis' Summary

A creative mind is a terrible thing to waste. And Josh Locatis has never been accused of wasting his talents. Making a conscious decision almost 10 years ago to focus his skills in the gun industry, Locatis has been breaking molds and smashing boxes ever since. On any given day you’ll find a rifle, a handgun, and an assortment of gun gear and “toys” (err, props) strewn across his desk. He doesn’t do hobbies, he does research; and by research we mean camp, hike, hunt, firearms instruct, and of course shooting any gun that crosses his desk. An integrated thinker who isn’t afraid to turn and go against the grain, Locatis has brought a fresh look to the firearms industry. When the competition emulates your work, and you can’t open a gun magazine without seeing his work, he must be doing something right.
