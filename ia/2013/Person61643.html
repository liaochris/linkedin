Paul Jenkins' Summary

Who I am?
I’m passionate about all things digital. A qualified ScrumMaster and Product Owner, I’m Project Director of specialist digital development company Big Blue Door, which I co-founded in 2012.

I previously spent 8 years leading digital delivery in central government, delivering high profile campaigns for the Cabinet Office, No.10 and Ofsted.

My company
Big blue door is a specialist digital development agency, supporting enterprise-level websites and digital applications. We currently work with some of the most high profile parts of central government, including Ofsted, Cabinet Office and the Government Digital Service.

We specialise in Open Source solutions, in agile development environments. We support PHP, primarily (but not exclusively) Drupal content management systems.

What I have done?
I have worked across the UK government for Number 10, Ofsted, the COI, and the Training & Development Agency for Schools on all things online and social media related; advising on strategy, technical implementation and, of course, delivery. I am used to managing both technical and content teams, and I pride myself in being able to quickly understanding and then realising a client’s vision. I helped create Big blue door because I believe there is always room for companies that deliver cost-effective products that exceed expectations and delight clients.

Recent Big blue door highlights include:
- Ofsted’s Learner View, providing an online system for students and learners to feedback on the quality of their courses
- Ofsted’s online Annual Report tool, Data View
- Cabinet Office’s National Citizen Service website and MI system

My areas of specialist knowledge:
Digital project management
Open Source CMS technologies
Writing content for a variety of platforms, not least digital
Managing social media channels

What I do outside of work
Seeing friends, watching football, visiting family, and (attempting) to keep my wife's shopping addiction under control.
