Dwight House's Summary

I'm passionate game development and front-end web development. I have experience in both and like them equally. In both my professional work and my hobby projects, I want to push the boundaries of what's possible, acknowledging the status quo, but never being satisfied with it. I'm always looking to make things more robust and easier to use, changing the nature of the problem if necessary.

My most recent career move was to leave game development at Electronic Arts to work on web apps and technology at Ciere Consulting. That was in May of 2013. I'm enjoying myself, learning a lot, and working with the very latest, bleeding edge tech. It is an amazing time.
