Drake Nix's Summary

Over the past six years I have worked in varying positions within the graphic design world, learning and growing as each presented their own challenges and lessons.

I worked in an ad agency, creating print and web materials for clients in vastly different arenas; banks, colleges, hospitals, automotive, non-profit and churches. Every week was a new client with new projects and new challenges.

Then I worked for a web start-up, completely branding and developing the personality of a company from scratch. Once the identity was created, I designed all collateral and sales materials; website, business cards, letterhead, Powerpoint presentations, sales materials, emails and designed the web and mobile applications.

For three years I was the General Manager and Head of Design at Saratoga Wine Exchange and saratogawine.com. Our website receives thousands of hits a day, averaging 30 orders a day and we currently offer over 16,000 different wines. I designed the current site and we are continually analyzing data to make tweaks and changes the the design and function to improve conversions.

Now I am working as a contract graphic design and front-end web coder. If you have a project you would like to talk about, let me know.

Through all of this, I have worked freelance for clients in many fields, including healthcare, public relations, hospitality and entertainment.
