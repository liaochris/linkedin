Desiree Leigh Thompson, Ph.D, R.P.C, CCPCPR's Summary

When a woman relinquishes her power - her identity and her voice - she stops living with integrity. She stops being true to herself and, she loses her vibrancy for life. Non-serving behaviors arise to hide her pain of disconnection to self and others. Thoughts get foggy; emotions get confusing; and, life starts to look like a narrow tunnel of darkness. Talk, release, refocus, and restructure creates a journey into wellness. As a result, she cultivates her new life from her inner-richness of authentic self-expression and begins to live a life of integrity.

While maintaining a private practice, I am pursuing another degree focusing on psychology and natural sciences.
