Charlie White's Summary

Author, leader and mentor with 39 years of award-winning experience in all forms of media

Specialties: Edits and writes about tech, cars, Internet, social media, consumer electronics and inexplicable peculiarities. Specializes in topics that attract tremendous traffic numbers. Many mentoring successes. Extensive experience with managing Twitter and Facebook accounts with millions of followers.

More than 9,000 articles published on high-traffic websites and respected magazines. Wrote "Bloggers Boot Camp," a book published by Focal Press in 2011. Now contracted to work on a second edition of that book, with more books in the pipeline.

Other specialties: live multicamera TV directing, music videos, video editing, product photography, voice-over performance and promotion/commercial video creation.
