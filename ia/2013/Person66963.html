John "JR" Rickinger's Projects

  • SANY NA Launch-Bell Award

    • January 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: John "JR" Rickinger

    The annual Milwaukee Chapter, BMA (Business Marketing Association) yearly Bell Awards celebrating the best creative and marketing work created in 2011.

    In the multiple categories there was over 171 entries and at the awards night event there was 200 attendees from agencies and marketing companies throughout Wisconsin. The entries are reviewed and judged by marketing professionals throughout the country, and because of that, the judging has been getting tougher and tougher. They scrutinize and look at everything detail in: creative concept, execution, how integrated and well positioned everything is and most importantly results. These judges are a tough group of contemporaries.

    One of, if not the toughest categories to win or even place in is...Total B2B Campaigns with production costs and media costs under $500,000 during a 12 month period. I was ecstatic to find out I beat them all and won a best of division bell. The win was Incredibly humbling and the moment my company name and the bell win was announced, many people cheered and congratulated me making the moment even more special, especially since many know my personal back story leaving in 2010 the agency I started 10 years prior.

    What all started with a phone call in late 2010 asking me to help launch SANY America into the US market. We laid out in our initial plan of attack our goals and set the right positioning targets... then in January 2011 we started working together.

    Thank you you SANY America for trusting and supporting me, my company and allowing me to work side by side with a great team of people.
