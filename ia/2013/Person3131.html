Alan Hart's Summary

An entrepreneur and business strategist with more than 14 years of experience. I began my career by founding two companies and have since worked with companies ranging from start-ups to Fortune 500 in the areas of innovation, strategy and marketing.

I have experience in consumer goods and services, financial services, healthcare, internet, life sciences, manufacturing, media and publishing, technology, telecommunications, and travel industries. I have led projects that cover innovation, growth strategy, new product and service development, new business model creation, corporate and local marketing alignment, marketing and organizational performance, customer loyalty and engagement, service marketing, channel strategy and due diligence to name a few.

My clients have included Bell & Howell, Blue Cross Blue Shield of NC, Sirius|XM, Eaton, Sprint Nextel, State Farm, LS Tractor, Quintiles, Tauck Travel, United Healthcare, Lulu.com, WhiteWave Foods, Campbell's Soup Company, Rollins (Orkin) and WomanCare Global.

Additionally, I have led a number of core research initiatives focused on expanding the knowledge of marketers, including:
* An annual qualitative research via conversations with 100+ CMOs to identify common challenges, opportunities and best practices.
* A bench-marking study of marketing capabilities in collaboration with the Business Marketing Association (marketing.org) and CMO.com.
* A study of Twitter & the 2012 Summer Olympics looking at three panels of Twitter users - Athletes, Sponsors, and "Commoners"
* Development of new methods to measure and track brand equity
* Development of methods to quantify value propositions for B2B firms
