Matthew Moore's Summary

Matthew brings over eighteen years global experience improving business processes and developing software solutions. Currently, Matthew is at Winshuttle heading up Marketing. Previously he was at Dexterra, helping companies enable the mobile way of business, in both Product Marketing and Partner roles. Historically, Matthew founded Wingman Mobile. Matthew is an entrepreneur and manager with extensive start-up experience. His varied work career has enabled him to amass a large network across companies and geographies.

Matthew started his career at Indeva developing applications for Oil and Gas companies such as Philips, British Gas, Mobil, and Exxon. After his graduate studies, Matthew worked with Siebel Systems Inc. in their Telecommunications, Energy, and Media vertical, where he was in charge of developing service solutions working with customers like BT, Bell Canada and Telstra.
