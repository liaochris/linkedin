Lisa Griffis' Summary

► Who am I?
 I am a visual and written-word communicator skilled in breaking down complex concepts into concise messages. My journalistic techniques produce trusted, high-quality content to tell the story of your brand.

► What can I do for you?
High-level Graphic Design * Photography * Typography * Content Creation * Business Branding * InDesign * Photoshop * Print * Social Media * Newsletters * Brochures * Informational Graphics * Team Building and Collaboration

► When are my skills needed?  
When you need someone who thinks out of the box. When you need an experienced, multi-disciplined communicator. When your brand doesn’t reflect what your business really is. Or even when you need something as simple as a new logo. I use design, images and words to tell your story.

► Where do I fit in to your picture?
I’m the calm one in the storm. I put out the fires. I’m the one juggling five projects at once, but not missing a single detail. I’m the one who knows who to call and how to get the job done.

► Why tap into my eclectic knowledge?
I have produced award-winning packages on subjects ranging from tax season nightmares, to political campaigns, to preparing the perfect Thanksgiving meal.

► How to contact me?
My portfolio can be found at lisagriffis.com.
Email me at lisagriffis@gmail.com and let me help you communicate your message.
