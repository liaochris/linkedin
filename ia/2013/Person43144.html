Etay Gafni's Summary

An entrepreneur at heart, I founded several startup companies over the years. I currently own and run BrightAct – a successful boutique-consulting firm that helps executives with their mobile, UX and software products.

My specialization in UX and prototyping began at SAP, where I led UX technologies for the SAP UX team and the office of the CTO.

In 2012, I founded intuito (http://www.intuito.com) - a user research and usability testing SaaS application for mobile apps. intuito was created to allow non-technical designers, marketers and UX professionals to validate ideas, designs and requirements before a single line of code is written, and to provide product, design and marketing insights during development, while the app is in Beta as well as during run-time.
