Heidi Tolliver-Walker's Summary

Heidi Tolliver-Walker has been a commercial and digital printing industry analyst, feature writer, and content generator for 20+ years. Her industry commentary and analysis can regularly be found on What They Think's Digital Nirvana, top industry publications, private publications, and printer, vendor, and other B2B blogs around the industry.

She is known for her meticulous research and no-nonsense perspective. Her Marketer's Primer series is well respected for its comprehensive, authoritative, and forward-looking approach to critical industry topics. Educational reports in the Marketers Primer Series include: Digital Printing, 1:1 (Personalized) Printing; Personalized URLs; QR Codes; Web-to-Print; and Greening Print Marketing, as well as brandable white papers taken from the best practices sections of each.

Heidi also generates a high volume of both bylined and ghostwritten materials, including:

• White papers
• Newsletters
• Blog content
• Books
• Press releases
• Case studies
• Web content
• Articles for publication

Heidi was one the faces of the well-respected industry research firm The Industry Measure (TrendWatch Graphic Arts) before its closure in 2007. In her more than 13-year tenure with the firm, she wrote countless reports on digital printing, 1:1 (personalized) printing, Web-to-print, personalized URLs, and other hot industry applications.

Among her many hats, Heidi is currently the editorial director and copywriter for Great Reach Communications' 1:1 Market Builder program, which offers newsletters, e-grams, 1:1 postcards, blog posts, and other marketing tools for printers.

Heidi Tolliver-Walker is the author of three titles for the National Association of Printing Leadership: Designer's Printing Companion, Ink & Color: A Printer's Guide, and Diversifying Via Value-Added Services.

As a small, niche publisher (Strong Tower Publishing), she is also active in utilizing these technologies in her own business.

Specialties

digital printing, Web-to-print, personalized URLs, 1:1 (personalized) printing, variable data printing, editorial development, feature and case study writing, book development, editing
