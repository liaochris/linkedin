Bryant Stadler's Summary

With 27 years of customer service as a background, I have learned that the key to any successful relationship is the ability to listen. My honesty, ethics and integrity have always been, and will continue to be, the foundation of success in my real estate career. The long lasting friendships I have developed while working with buyers and sellers of real estate is the most fulfilling reward of my career. My commitment is to put my clients needs above my own … to treat each and every one as if they were a member of my family! My past clients are the back bone of my business and the true source of what fuels my real estate transactions.

Along with my wife, Cathy, and 2 sons, I relocated to Charlotte from Denver, Colorado in 2004. It was during this move that I learned to appreciate the experience and knowledge that a local real estate advisor can bring to the transaction. It was about this time that I began to develop a passion for the real estate industry. In 2007, I decided to sell my retail business to pursue a career in real estate. Well... 6 years later, I'm proud to say that I weathered the market crash and am still practicing as a full time real estate agent.
Please feel free to contact me with any questions you might have. I'm never too busy to assist! For some insight on my services, please visit www.BryantStadler.com
