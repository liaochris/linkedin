Anatoly Kanivetskiy's Summary

Data Science is the art of turning data into actions. This is accomplished through the creation of data products, which provide actionable information without exposing decision makers to the underlying complexity of data or analytics.

As a data analyst, I'm striving to find those gems of insightful information in a deep mines of data, faceting them to an actionable plan of incremental improvement of the business.

In a last few years I've developed a habit of continuous learning, which keeps mind sharp, toolset rich, and a learning curve on any steepness surmountable.

Passionate about math, statistics, quantitative finance, game theory, machine learning and data mining.

Strong programming background is both empowering my analytical toolset and broadening the horizons of comprehension of a business issues related to software development.

Being a part of an analytics department, I had luxury of participation in the vast range of ongoing activities, embracing the full circle of the data flow from engineering a data warehouse to data visualization and reporting, driven with an overarching goal of supporting business owners in the daily decision making by leveraging the information hidden in the high volumes of data.

Among those activities, most memorable are:

- Built a report, allowing to track and quantitatively estimate various sources of a referral traffic on a fine-grained level of a webpage/game;
- Developed various parts of a recommendation engine, developed number of versions of game ranking algorithm;
- Contributed to a data warehousing design, assisting with Hadoop cluster provisioning;
- Integrated various internal data storages into a general ETL data flow;
