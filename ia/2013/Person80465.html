Leticia Pontes' Summary

I started my professional career as a lawyer and practiced administrative, tax and consumer law. However, my great interest in different cultures led me to the Complutense University of Madrid in 2003 until 2004, when I moved to Stockholm, Sweden. There I dedicated myself to teaching languages (Portuguese and Spanish) as well as working in the retail market. I worked in sales and management for three major European companies.

Eager to get a better understanding of the codes of my new culture as well as the differences in verbal and non-verbal communication between my home culture and Swedish culture, I started my career in image consulting. I studied personal and corporate image consulting with Ilana Berenholc, a pioneer and reference in the area in Brazil and worldwide. In 2010, I started Leticia Pontes Image Consulting in Sweden.

Aiming to promote cultural and linguistic exchange between Brazil and other countries, my partner and I started our company Belaponte AB, based in Sweden, in April of 2013.

Because of my broad professional background in different areas and countries, I have outstanding experience from international organizations and business cultures. My main strengths are that I'm a very dedicated and focused professional with a strong entrepreneurial spirit. Helping people to get the best out of themselves is one of my strongest motivating factors.

Specialties: Image consulting, cultural coaching, personal development, intercultural training, cultural consultancy, language consultancy, language teaching.
