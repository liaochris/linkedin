Hello world, I am a senior digital creative with 8 years of experience in working within corporate e-commerce and agency environments. My current work focuses on improving UX/UI/AI of digital design and its strategy while keeping in mind the brand’s philosophy and coherency. My personal interests and research are growing into the fields of AI and robotics in hopes to (someday soon) bridge the gap between our digital dreams and technological reality on a larger scale.
