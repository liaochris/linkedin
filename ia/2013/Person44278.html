Fabrizio Comolli's Summary

My first professional focus is publishing and training. Born in 1964, I have a background in humanities (grad. in philosophy and in psychology/HF), but I developed my main expertise in technical publishing and training/education.

For many years I worked as a freelance (author & consultant). Next, I was in the staff of some Italian IT imprints: mainly Apogeo (as "special projects" editor: e-business books & online courses / e-learning), and Pearson Education Italia (as publisher, HPE division). At present my role include both the editorial plans and the strategic marketing (cross media and multi-channel projects) as well as media relations.

Lately, I am strengthening and expanding my interests in the area of security (both publishing and practising).

Specialties

Management of publishing projects (online/paper). Scouting of authors/issues. Networking with local/international contacts, partners, sponsors etc. New business, multi-channel projects and co-marketing partnerships. Media relations. Writing & editing. Security and self-defense: training and consulting.
