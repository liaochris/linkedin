Clara Bellino's Summary

I am a Pop - Jazz Singer / Songwriter. I am currently following up my successful album, Embarcadero Love, with my latest Pop - Jazz album This Is Happiness - Hand In Hand. This new recording features my newest hit “This Is Happiness” and a duet with the late and legendary Bobby Sharp (best known for composing “Unchain My Heart”) as well as two videos and a documentary, making for a very unique release.

For exclusive Clara Bellino releases - and to be the first in the know - please sign up for my mailing list at www.ClaraBellino.biz.

You can also find out more at my website www.ClaraBellino.com.

I completed my West Coast Mini Tour "This Is Happiness" Fall 2012 and presented an artist workshop at the Independent Music Conference in Los Angeles . Attended MIDEM Jan. 2013. Now back in the States planning my next recording.

If you would like to make sure I play in your city, please email Bookings@ClaraBellino.com.


Thank you!
