Martin Bergén's Summary

I am a skilled designer, art director and motion graphics artist. A Hyper Island graduate, digital media creative with a varied background within arts and the restaurant business.

I have worked with graphic design and art direction since 2001, most of the time self employed. Creative problem solving is one of the things I enjoy most in my job.

Professionally I aim to lead a creative team in creating intelligent communication solutions. New technology emerge all the time and I believe in being curious, agile and playful, and always try to incorporate the latest technology in what I create.

Send me an email and I can tell you more about me and my ambitions.

Specialties: Art Direction, design, motion graphics, 3D/2D animation and compositing. I also have considerable skills in copy writing, marketing strategies, project managing, being awesome.. and humble.
