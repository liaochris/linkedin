Amanda Baldwin's Summary

I came into the ‘Business Social Media’ sphere in September 2010, having been using social media on a personal level for some years. At the time I was working for the Hastings & Rother Primary Care Trust providing management assistance on a £16M project - Station Plaza Health Centre in Hastings. The Centre opened in May 2010 and has been given a prestigious building industry award for ‘Building Better Healthcare’.

Throughout my working career I’ve had extensive dealings with people from all walks of life and it’s been my ability to communicate with people that has made my working life both challenging and rewarding. My ability to communicate yet still get the job done made social media the ideal career path for me.

The skills that I have acquired through working in the Public sector, running my own retail business for 11 years and being a complaints manager has contributed to giving me ideal social media skills which I have put to good use.

GOAL:

Helping businesses to develop, manage, grow and profit from their social media online presence. Creating their digital 'Word-of-Mouth'.
