Dr. James Proodian's Summary

I am a chiropractic sports physician and health educator. My expertise for the past two decades has been in the area of physical rehabilitation, and I have successfully established myself as a spinal specialist. Many leading medical doctors in the fields of orthopedics, interventional pain management, and neurosurgery utilize my practice at Natural Healthcare Center for nonsurgical therapy in the treatment of spinal and extremity pain.

At Natural Healthcare Center, we advocate the science of functional medicine, which takes an integrative approach to treating patients by addressing their physical, nutritional, and psychological needs. I am proud to work with a team of exceptional clinicians who are all specialists in their respective fields. As patient-centered practitioners, we spend quality time with our patients, learning their unique health histories and identifying underlying clinical imbalances.

While I love the one-on-one interaction with my patients, I am alarmed by the escalation of complex, chronic illness in the United States, with its unsustainable economic and human cost. Working to change healthcare, I developed a company called Wellness at Work, which offers health education seminars and programs to local businesses and organizations on such topics as stress management, detoxification, ergonomics, and nutrition. Since 1994, I have motivated thousands of people to make positive lifestyle choices.

To continue addressing the healthcare crisis, I am taking Wellness at Work to a national level as a nonprofit organization. As a keynote speaker, I deliver seminars across the country focused on identifying the problems in today’s healthcare system and rendering tangible solutions that not only impact the financial side but also offer significant, measurable improvements by helping people lead healthier and longer lives.

You can join me weekly on my radio program, “Proodian Healthcare By Design," on Tandem Radio or visit my blog at www.drproodian.com.
