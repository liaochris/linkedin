Erik Dunton's Summary

Hi, my name is Erik Dunton. I'm a REMO HealthRHYTHMS Endorsed Facilitator and Multi-Percussionist, I believe that everyone is born with rhythm!

My proof? … Your heartbeat!

I have worked in Nashville since the mid 80's where I have been in the studio and on the road in many creative musical capacities for many artists; Michael W. Smith, Moody Blues, Charlie Daniels Band, Joe Jackson, Bebe & Cece Winnans, Steve Winwood, Whitney Houston, Anne Murray and many others.

I also believe music should be not only valued as a form of entertainment, but as an interactive element of change for achieving specific measurable outcomes in individuals and groups. Today’s world has a need for innovative programs to help us respect differences while encouraging collaboration allowing us to work together at a greater potential, and to create a healthy balance in our personal lives. To that end, my company, Positive RePercussions uses drumming and rhythm because drums are the most accessible of musical instruments.

Everyone can drum!

Group therapeutic drumming adds a key component; community. Drumming as a community produces a collaborative creative experience that has existed since the earliest of civilizations. Documented studies have shown this form of collaborative expression to improve our overall health and wellbeing.

I am a REMO HealthRHYTHMS Endorsed Group Empowerment Drumming Facilitator. HealthRHYTHMS® is an evidence-based group drumming protocol utilized as a cost-effective strategy for; inspiring successful living; offering exceptional support and personal expression; by giving people permission to play and has been scientifically shown to boost the immune system, improve mood, and reduce stress and burnout.

Positive RePercussions' programs are designed to create a musically accessible culture, where musical expression is seen as a tool for personal development.
