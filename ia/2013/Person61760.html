Jennifer Bradley's Summary

I work one-to-one with professionals ready for smarter choices that lead to a more focused and less stressful career change and job search.

Do you have some of these questions?

*How do I find out who needs people with my skills?
*How do I match what I bring with opportunities?
*How do I stand out when I network, do interviews, and in my resume and online?
*Where do I begin to job search, how do I stay on track, and how do I know what to do next?
*How should I use social media to help rather than hinder my search?

The chances are that the extensive preparation required to achieve your professional qualifications did not include training in how to manage your own career. As technology becomes more influential in the process, it can feel even more impersonal and confusing.

I can help you to compete more effectively in today's job market by working with you to:

*Discern your Unique Combination of Strengths and Skills
*Locate and Evaluate Opportunities in the Hidden Job Market
*Differentiate Yourself through Compelling Verbal and Written Communication
(Resumes; online profiles)
*Get Access to More Opportunities by More Effective Use of Networking (Online & Face-to-face)
*Stay Focused & Develop the Resilience Needed to Manage Ups & Downs

There is no one solution that is right for everyone.

As a Certified Career Coach, job search consultant, and social media strategist I can help you to make more informed choices. I have had the privilege of learning from some of the leading US-based trainers of career professionals. It is my goal to share my knowledge and experience of the methods and tools that are most likely to be effective for you, based on your goals and strengths.
