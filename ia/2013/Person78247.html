John Downing's Summary

Land Tech Surveying, LLC is a professional Land Surveying company serving the needs of Southeastern Wisconsin. Our goal is to be your #1 choice for all your Land Surveying needs. We can provide you with our services in a timely and accurate manner while staying cost effective. Land Tech uses the latest field equipment and surveying software to ensure that the client has quality results. We strive for professionalism and a commitment to long-term relationships. Our success will be measured by those whom we serve. Land Tech Surveying serves Waukesha county, Milwaukee county, Washington, Ozaukee, Dodge, Jefferson, Walworth, Racine and Kenosha county and the rest of Wisconsin.

Specialties: GPS Surveying, Boundary surveys, ALTA surveys, Certified Survey Maps, Environmental surveys, Topographical Surveys, Forensic Surveying, Legal descriptions, Construction staking, Plat of surveys, FEMA Elevation Certificates, LOMA's, Architectural surveys, Subdivision design, Road design, Preliminary, Final & Condo Plats, Land Planning and Consulting.
