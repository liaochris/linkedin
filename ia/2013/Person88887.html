Matthew D. Sullivan's Summary

Entrepreneur who worked professionally as an art director and creative director until starting a business in the paintball & apparel industry. Opened the largest indoor paintball facility in New York (North Bellmore) with a 25,000 sq ft building containing a field, lounge, apparel line, and retail store. The business currently expanded to 30 acres out on the east end of Long Island for woodsball/scenario outdoor paintball. In charge of running the daily operations, but also all the marketing & advertising pertaining to the field and store. Consummate professional who has worked in many industries and for such brands as HBO, Showtime, Meineke, Tempurpedic, Disney, 1-800-Flowers.com, Hair Club, and many more. As an expert in all forms of marketing, advertising, graphic design, print, online media, social media, illustration, painting/graffiti, and tattoo art -- considers everything a canvas.
