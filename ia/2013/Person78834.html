Laura M. Labovich's Summary

Equal parts kick-butt trainer and nurturing job search coach, I am passionate about helping my clients move mountains in their job search. As CEO of the Bethesda-based career management firm, The Career Strategy Group, I am committed to helping you LAND A JOB FASTER.

I was born and raised professionally at Walt Disney World, so I have the Disney spunk and customer service passion in my blood. After receiving my first promotion out of the polyester costume, I traveled far and wide to recruit for the Walt Disney World College Program, and led the recruitment efforts for Disney's Typhoon Lagoon and Disney's Blizzard Beach water parks. (I even staffed up Disney's Animal Kingdom for the very first time...pretty exciting!)

At America Online, Inc. I designed and launched AOL's college internship program and coached hundreds of employees—from entry-level to executive—on how to fit in, stand out, and move up in the company and in their careers!

I am the co-author of 100 Conversations for Career Success: Learn to Tweet, Cold-Call and Network Your Way to a Dream Job, and my easy-to-implement approach has garnered attention from such national news media as The Washington Post, U.S. News & World Report, USA Today, & The Chicago Tribune, to name a few.

All this to say: I stay "in-the-know" so you always get the most effective job search advice, which enables you to look incredible on paper (resumes, cover letters, bios), ace the interview, and shave MONTHS off your job search.

So, if you are struggling in your job search and in need of a lifeline, call us at 703.942.9390. We specialize in serving you with full-service, customizable solutions. Our clients range from Fortune 500 companies and small companies in the midst of a downsizing, to job seekers all over the country who are just in need of ridiculously effective strategy and advice.

Work with us to design a program that works for you (and your budget), and LAND A JOB FASTER!

Specialties

job search strategy, networking, interviewing, and salary negotiation coaching, online identity management, corporate outplacement, career research, and career marketing collateral (resumes, bios, marketing letters, marketing plans, on-line profiles).
