David Bernstein's Summary

I am a seasoned technology executive today focused on Cloud Computing, building on experience in OS, Application Framework, and Systems Software, particularly large systems for enterprise or telecommunications. I am a products executive working in companies with offers in Cloud Computing, Applications Infrastructure, SOA and web services networking equipment, and before that in the enterprise applications space using Composite Applications and SOA techniques. I have been CEO, VP/GM, CTO, SVP Business Development, and SVP of Engineering. I have a great rapport with top architecture executives in enterprise customers, as well as internal technical teams, founders, and investors.

Specialties: My special skills are in: engineering/product division management; new product architecture/development; technology partnerships/licensing; M&A, and technical side of new business plans.
