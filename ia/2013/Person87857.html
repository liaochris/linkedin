Marty Garrett's Summary

I am Certified Co-Active Professional Coach and hold dual Masters Degrees in Education and Counseling. I have completed 88 hours of course work on Organizational and Relationship Systems Coaching at the Center of Right Relationship.

I am passionate about working with women in midlife. I have personally experienced many midlife challenges including single parenting, midlife career changes, coping with a life threatening illness, and becoming a business owner. All these experiences have strengthened my belief that difficult transitions offer the opportunity for personal transformation.

The majority of my practice focuses on women in midlife who are longing for change and wanting to be the conscious creator of the second half of their lives. I inspire them to recreate their dreams, live from their passion, and connect with their inner knowing.

Specialties

Many of my clients are exploring ways to meet some of the following challenges: Divorce, retirement decisions, loss of a loved one, shifting families, and caring for an aging parent. I also work with couples who want to explore and strengthen their relationships. Above all, my clients are seeking to discover their authentic selves, and live their lives from a place of choice and freedom.
