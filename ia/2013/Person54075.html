Hunter Swift's Summary

I create specialized processes for Automotive/Dealerships to help them maximize their sales and customer service opportunities. By capitalizing on their opportunities this helps my clients improve all aspects of their sales cycle including driving traffic, marketing, proper sales and demonstration techniques. Additionally, I also specialize in helping dealerships improve their customer satisfaction, manage their social reputation, improve web-lead response times and to improve accountability.
