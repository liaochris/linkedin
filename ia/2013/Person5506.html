Ambal Balakrishnan's Summary

Ambal Balakrishnan is President and Co-founder of ClickDocuments. She is a technologist turned marketer. She spent a decade in the Corporate world in various roles that includes Engineering, Program management, Business development, Strategy and Marketing for premium and fast growing product divisions at Cisco and Telecordia. Most recently, she was focusing on marketing and positioning of Cisco data center switching business with multi-billion dollars in annual revenues.

Ambal’s book is #CONTENTMARKETINGtweet – 140 bite-sized ideas to create and market compelling content. She speaks at many events on entrepreneurship and marketing.

Ambal is the curator for TEDxYouth@MtDiablo which is organized to inspire high school students in San Ramon Valley Unified School District. Ambal is also actively involved coaching elementary and middle school students in math and science contests.

Ambal is an avid reader and hiker. Her hiking pursuits have taken her to several mountains including Mt. Whitney (which she managed to climb in 1 day).

Ambal received her Masters in Computer Science from Purdue University and an MBA in Marketing, Strategy and Entrepreneurship from Wharton University of Pennsylvania.

She lives in San Ramon, CA with her family.
