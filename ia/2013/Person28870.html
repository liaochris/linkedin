David Lutz's Summary

As a consultant and organizational trainer, David Lutz has become widely known for providing community and municipal organizations with GIS consulting and training to help aid in revitalizing their economic generators, provide mapping analysis services and improve database management.

He specializes in the coordination of GIS and database management, to help bring successful beneficial change to existing infrastructure.

His professional experience in GIS and IT spans multiple faucets of program development, strategy, data base management, and map creation in the fields of economic development, federal planning, health care, municipal planning, transportation, aviation, crime analysis, streetscape, citywide broadband service development, and urban design.

Outside of the office Mr. Lutz is a world traveler, recreational pilot and an avid triathlete where he completed his first full Ironman in 2012 and currently working toward a second in 2013.
