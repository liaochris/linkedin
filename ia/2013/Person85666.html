Maria Elena Duron's Summary

What have you done to inspire others to speak positively about your brand? Maria Elena Duron specializes in creating community, connection and commerce around your brand. She works with high performing entrepreneurs, executives and businesses on how to manage their personal brand, the conglomeration of personal brands in their company and their business brand. Duron helps them with their brand online and offline, leverage their expertise and connections and generate “the talk that yields profits”.
