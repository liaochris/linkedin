Barbara Weibel's Summary

After 70+ hours per week in corporate positions for many years, I felt like the proverbial donut - solid on the outside but empty on the inside. A serious illness resulted in an "is this all there is" epiphany and I decided that, if I could recover, I would walk away from my career to pursue my true passions of travel, writing and photography. In early 2007 I took a six month round-the-world trip and came back changed forever; I knew there was no possibility of returning to the corporate world. Instead, I concentrated on recreating myself as a travel writer, focusing on travel beyond the normal tourist experience with an emphasis on cultural immersion. Today I travel perpetually, with no home base. To read more about my perpetual world travel, visit my blog, Hole In The Donut Cultural Travel, at http://holeintedonut.com.

Specialties: Travel, photography, culture, environment, spirituality.
