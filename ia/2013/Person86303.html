Mark Anthony Dyson, MAEd/AET's Experience

CEO

Competitive Resumes

August 2008 – Present (5 years 5 months)

I write resumes, cover letters, salary history, references, Thank-You letters, Coaching, Consulting. I travel to various places to preach the gospel about keeping an updated compelling resume, effectively writing on-line profiles, and being savvy with using on-line tools like Facebook, Twitter, Linked-In, and blogging.

Resumes that focus on accomplishments and results are more intriguing than just citing "responsibilities or job duties". Creating compelling content for your resume is simply telling a story that displays your professional progression and your most significant contributions.

I also coach, mentor, and teach people to use blogging as a way to set yourself apart from the job hunting competition. The job market is a hostile environment that is forcing people to cave in or dig deep into themselves discovering what makes them tick and passionate about what they do. Blogging is such a powerful tool because of the way it builds community, and content persuasive enough for employers to find you. In addition, blogging can position you as the expert, and act as an extension of your resume.
