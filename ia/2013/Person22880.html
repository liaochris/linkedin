Chip Henderson's Summary

I am a location photographer that specializes in advertising and marketing campaigns for such clients as Pinehurst Resort, Chris Craft , PGA golf and the Ritz Carlton. We shoot lifestyle, architecture and scenics within the projects we produce. We also work with the images to produce the best final photograph with our retoucher Craig Burt of Revelation Imaging.

Specialties

Scouting the locations prior to shoot and developing a detailed production schedule to shoot from sunrise until -- to capture the most dramatic images .

Chip Henderson's Experience

Owner

Chip Henderson Photography

June 1980 – Present (33 years 4 months)

Bumped out by the starch shirts and began my own business in 1980- It might have had something to do with me meeting this cute girl that I married 2 years later or the fact that capturing the right light became an obsession that could not be understood by 9 to 5 ers.

President and Creative Director

Lightsworks Publishing

January 1985 – June 1990 (5 years 6 months)

Lightworks Publishing produced coffee table books, calendars and marketing materials to support the books for sale in book stores and via direct mail. Books were produced on various subjects and all featured photography or artwork which was the focus of the book. A book on the US Olympic Festival, Somewhere in Time by Bob Timberlake, A Blessing Shared- photography of North Carolina and The Big Click - A 24 hour period captured in North Carolina that featured 200 plus photographers were some of the titles.
