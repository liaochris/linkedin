Kim Hurst's Summary

My specialty is making the transition to more peaceful living. Home is often one's largest investment, and it is certainly the place that has the largest place in one's heart. Home should be a place that brings one peace:

* For some, peaceful living means a move to a bigger home.
* For some, a smaller one.
* For many, it means moving a bit farther out.
* For others, peace comes by moving closer to the city.
* For a few, it means finally moving out of the parents' house or the cramped rental into a first home.

I have more than 10 years experience helping people make all of these transitions with wonderful results. I consider each new contact a God-given opportunity to make a positive difference in someone's life, and work hard to see great results.
