David Jackson's Summary

I am passionate about personal development. Since you take yourself to the place you work or the business you run, personal development is leadership development. You live with others in a family and work with others in a team, so showing up as who you really are with what you do best every day is critical to your fulfillment, inspiration, and creativity.

I Plan Strategy
by combining ideas, insights, and possibilities into alternative choices. I help people craft the roadmap to their best option for their companies or for themselves.

I Built a Company
inside a company. In 2004 I built the New York Technical Exploration Center for IBM. I hired employees, managed a construction project, and launched hands-on marketing programs that resulted in $20M in revenue per quarter and made me responsible for $5M P&L per quarter. I planned and helped launch four other TECs in major cities on the East Coast.

I Coach Executives
through questions and fierce conversations. I guide people who run companies to the intersection of Passion and Work. I help people eliminate distractions and get to the point.

I Facilitate Private Advisory Boards
because none of us alone are stronger than all of us together. I gather the best leaders I can find who understand that learning never ends and giving help to others is just as important as receiving help. I blend insight, thought, and accountability into growth for every member of my group.
