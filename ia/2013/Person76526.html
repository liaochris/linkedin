Kolby Slocum's Summary

As the line between traditional and digital media continues to blur, the need for a producer comfortable in broadcast and integrated media becomes even more crucial for a successful outcome.

Kolby Slocum is a creative Content Producer equipped with a comprehensive media production background and strong managerial skills. He performs effectively in concurrent roles, such as producer, project manager and director.

Kolby applies exceptional interpersonal and communication skills to work with and foster cooperative efforts between account and creative teams. He thrives within fast-paced, deadline-driven environments while leveraging his experience in live television and breaking news to make sound, accurate decisions without hesitation.

Kolby received his BFA from New York University-Tisch School of the Arts where he was the recipient of the Lew Wasserman MCA Fellowship.
