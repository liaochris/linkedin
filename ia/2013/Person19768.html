Jaynel Dunlap BA, RDCS, FASE's Summary

Cardiac Accreditation Services provides assistance in obtaining and managing Intersocietial Accreditation Commission (IAC) accreditations, both for physician practices and hospital laboratories. We are a "full-service" consulting company and can help clients from an initial review to the completed application, and anywhere in between (or beyond).

With extensive experience in echocardiography and its associated accreditation requirements, CAS is keenly aware of both the need for data collection and submission, as well as how onerous those tasks can be and has developed a web-based program that will dramatically improve the efficiency and reporting accuracy of any testing facility, regardless of size. This program makes every level of data collection, from the initial procedure to quarterly quality assurance reporting, time-efficient and cost-effective.

CAS has also recently developed an exciting new partnership with UltraLinq, a rapidly-growing company specializing in structured reports for cardiac procedures. This partnership allows CAS to leverage all of the data UltraLinq collects and seamlessly input it into our system, making it even more efficient.
