★ Candice A Donofrio ★'s Summary

From 1979-2010 I was a Southern California/Northern AZ/internet radio air personality.
I'm in my 12th year as a REALTOR and my 9th as a Broker.
After receiving excellent real estate training at one of the best local independent companies, then moving on to an internationally known mega-office, I opened my own company in 2005.
I am the Owner/DB of Next Wave Real Estate Investments, LLC, located in Fort Mohave, AZ between Bullhead City and Mohave Valley, Mohave County, Northwestern AZ.
I am a Certified Commercial Sales Specialist via Commercial Real Estate Institute, Phoenix. I have also taught technology/risk reduction classes for CREI.
I have taken several courses toward my CCIM designation and hope to complete all in the coming year.
