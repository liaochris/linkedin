Michael Grutter's Summary

Cedar Engineering and Design Service, LLC is a full service detailing and design company that specializes in 2D AutoCAD drafting and detailing, 3D modeling and legacy drawing conversion to AutoCAD .DWG format. Please visit the website http://www.cedarengineering.com for more information on each of the services offered.

The services of CEDS are available to clients nationwide in Mechanical Design and Miscellaneous Structural Detailing.

With 22 years experience using AutoCAD (r10-2012), several years of Inventor and SoldiWorks use, drawings and models can be created to your exact specifications using your standards for text style, scaling, layering and dimension style with generic or supplied title blocks.

Let Cedar Engineering and Design Service, LLC help with your need in getting control of your backlog of work.

Specialties

AutoCAD design and detailing, conversion of hardcopy drawings to CAD and 3D Modeling using AutoCAD, Autodesk Inventor and SolidWorks.
