RYAN GOODMAN's Summary

I have been involved in the cattle industry my entire life, growing up on a progressive cow/calf and stocker cattle operation in Arkansas, and being acquainted with the cattle feeding business most of this time. I have focused on broadening my experience to operations across the country, gaining knowledge about other sectors of cattle production from the cow/calf to the feeder phase.

I have a passion for agriculture and food production, and advocate for the voice of farmers and ranchers in food dialogues with consumers. I believe the future of agriculture depends on these conversations and our ability to connect with and understand consumer concerns, while at the same time keeping consumers informed of advancements that improve food production.
