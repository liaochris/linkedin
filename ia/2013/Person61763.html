Jennifer Bukowsky's Projects

  • Murder trial

    • October 2010 to February 2012
    Team Members: Jennifer Bukowsky, Brandy Cook, Caleb Smith, Grant Wobig, Shin Cho, Aaron M. Mallonee, Karen Giboney

    Jury finds the defendant not guilty.

    The defendant among eight men who were charged with second degree murder and second degree robbery stemming from the shooting death of an individual at a gas station in October of 2010. After over a year of investigation and preparation, and a six day trial, the jury returned a not guilty verdict. We've been informed that this was the first acquittal from murder charges in Boone County 47 or 48 years.

However, we defended our client again during the retrial and he was acquitted.
