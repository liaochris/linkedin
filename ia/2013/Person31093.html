Dave Gorham's Summary

Dave Gorham is a Coach for Frame of Mind Coaching; leading the company and coaching other professionals to achieve their ultimate goals. With an extensive background in marketing, advertising and business management, Dave uses a unique approach in FOM Coaching Principles. He helps his clients create the success they want, with his passion for helping people reach their goals and most importantly, live happy, peaceful lives.

Dave is also a Top Real Estate Sales Agent and the Broker of Record for Realty Solutions, LLC. in New Jersey. Realty Solutions focuses on servicing the needs of investors and agents alike with all their real estate needs plus customizes sales for buyers and sellers in the residential market.

With a strong entrepreneurial spirit and extensive background in marketing, advertising and business management, Dave uses a unique approach. He helps his clients create the success they want, with his passion for helping people reach their goals and most importantly, live happy, peaceful lives.

Dave feels very strongly that all of his positions be focused on leadership and training. He considers his participation in outside coaching programs as a way to broaden the vision and deepen the value of the daily approach to business. As such Dave is a graduate of the FoM Coaching Certification Program, having participated in personal coaching with Kim Ades. His experience with FoM changed his life. It allowed him to achieve balance; he defined his goals and brought his desires to the forefront of his vision. Dave reaches for his goals everyday.

In his spare time, Dave enjoys spending time on the beach in Ocean City, riding motorcycles, driving his 1973 VW convertible (not so much fixing it) and being with his family. Playing recreational golf, listening to music and watching movies are among his personal pleasures.
