Web Art Director

BellSouth Advertising & Publishing Company

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; T; Telecommunications industry

January 1993 – February 2001 (8 years 2 months) Greater Atlanta Area

Hired as a temporary, I quickly earned full-time status and eventually completed several years of management development before being promoted as a web team manager. Earning several prestigious awards along the way, I was ultimately offered the role Web Art Director for Bellsouth Intelliventures, the new web arm of BellSouth.

As the first Web Art Director I developed all web design processes and procedures, and directed all web related personnel. Web personnel included 25 internal direct reports and 4 external vendor groups representing over 150 designers. During this tenure, the web fulfillment group sold, fulfilled, and provided ongoing support for over 8,000 web sites and many associated products.

Highlights:
• Introduced a visionary telework paradigm which improved product fulfillment and employee retention.
• Introduced a competitive, multilevel hiring range which saved over $140,000.00 per year in payroll.
• Created strategy to transform the design group from “exclusive roles” to a “cross-platform” trained teams.
• Developed and implemented many process innovations for all workflow and delivery processes.
