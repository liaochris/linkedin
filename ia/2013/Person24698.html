Chris White's Projects

  • DOBRA OJCA (short film)

    • January 2012 to March 2012
    Team Members: Chris White

    DOBRA OJCA (Polish, “good father”) presents one woman’s post-World War II reflection on her family’s frantic (and ill-fated) escape from their home in Krakow during Germany’s “blitzkrieg” invasion in the fall of 1939. At its core, DOBRA OJCA is a heartbreaking farewell between father and daughter, a meditation on how we may find hope in the midst of unspeakable cruelty and fear. DOBRA OJCA was adapted from the award-winning short story, “A Proof for Roxanna” by Thomas McConnell. Starring Greg Humphreys as Simcha, Kassie Bridwell as young Roxanna, Keri Keith Lumm as adult Roxanna. Written and Directed by Chris White.

  • TAKEN IN (feature film)

    • January 2011 to June 2011
    Team Members: Chris White

    TAKEN IN is a broken family feature film by independent writer-director Chris White. Fan-funded for $8,000, shot at South of the Border (I-95, SC-USA), and finished in three months. Featuring Colin Devlin’s stunning cover of U2’s “Staring At the Sun.” Every shot, every scene improvised.

  • GOOD LIFE (short film)

    • September 2010 to December 2010
    Team Members: Chris White

    The Girl’s tenth birthday. A perfectly lovely dinner at a downtown restaurant with her father. Presents, candles, cake. Tonight though, it is she who will take care of him.
