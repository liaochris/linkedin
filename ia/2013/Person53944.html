Jace Huffman's Summary

When a client needs to develop a piece of property, we look at it from many aspects.We will lay out several designs and present them to our client for input and discussion. We will point out the possible difficulties, along with the advantageous natural qualities of the site. We will attempt to maintain what is there, and blend the new layout with the existing.

We strive to keep informed of new design trends and development requirements. With high ethical fiber, we do what we can to maintain the environment with every development. We have a hands-on approach to the client-engineer-contractor dynamic.
