Lori Riley's Summary

Lori Riley, Owner of eleMENts by Beaute Spa, Lavenderology Bath & Body Products and Eco Community Seeds Magazine is a licensed Cosmetologist, CMT and a Business Major. Lori is very interested in the environment, as well as living beautifully. With that being said, she has devoted the last 8 years of her life educating people through her green businesses on sustainable and green alternatives that are stylish, luxurious, tasty, as well as practical, fun and simple to do.

We want people to become more socially, environmentally and community conscious. We also want to provide people a way to collaborate with others for a common cause.

Being green has a broad range of definitions, as does sustainable living. To Eco Community Seeds, sustainable living not only means taking care to preserve the earth’s resources and care for the environment, but also taking care of ones health, caring for the local community, and caring for communities around the world.

Most people agree that we should not use all of our resources faster than they can be replenished. Any small change made will contribute to this lifestyle goal. Eco Community Seeds offers direction and tips on small changes that can be made. After all, small changes can make a world of difference.
