Jenni Jacobus' Summary

I am a graduate of American University, with a BA in Graphic Design and a minor in Art History. Involved with design since 1999, I work with the Adobe Suite programs (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash, and AfterEffects). I am also a photographer, using both film and digital mediums. I love working in the darkroom, even going so far as to build one for personal use.

I am currently an MA candidate in the Masters of Graphic Design program at the London College of Communication (part of the University of the Arts, London). Since achieving my BA in 2009 I have worked for FSB Associates, a boutique marketing firm that specializes in the publishing industry. As part of my role at FSB I was instrumental in implementing a social media strategy for the company and its clients. I have also worked in the Creative Department at Nestlé Nutrition, under their Infant and HealthCare divisions. Working closely with their Design Managers, I acted as a Project Coordinator and a Proofreader during the packaging redesign of several popular brands. As a graphic designer for Wedgewood Communications, I worked on print and interactive campaigns for major pharmaceutical and healthcare organizations.

Specialties

Web Design (HTML, CSS3, some PHP, jQuery); Print Design; Proficiency in Adobe Suite programs: Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Flash, AfterEffects, and Dreamweaver; copyediting, proofreading, customer service, file management, materials management, photo editing, sales, typesetting, photography, Microsoft PowerPoint
