Debbie King's Summary

I am a Visual Designer with eight years’ experience and a passion for designing user-focused, fashion-forward web and app solutions.

As a technical writer turned visual/web designer, I am a detailed technologist with a strong creative side and keen aesthetic sense. I excel at creating detailed wireframes, mockups and prototypes for web and mobile apps that clearly reflect identity and brand.

I am a strong believer in “less is more”, so my designs tend to have a clean, minimalist feel. Feedback from managers and clients consistently indicates high satisfaction with my attractive, clean designs, user-friendly navigation, and clear, concise content.

I also enjoy creating print collateral, including business cards, promo postcards, logos, and brochures.

My technical writing skills lend themselves well to writing detailed personas, style guides, user flows and related design documentation.

I have been involved with a variety of product and project teams, including serving as training coordinator for a writing team of 18. I have superior project management, organizational, and communication skills.

I thrive in small-to-medium sized organizations that offer a casual but hard working culture, good work-life balance, and strong team interactions. I am particularly interested in creating designs for the health, wellness, and fitness industries.
