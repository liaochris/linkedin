Jason Fox's Summary

Dr Jason Fox is an award winning speaker and motivation design expert on a quest to liberate the world from poorly designed processes and work.

By the age of 25, he had completed a PhD, written two books and lectured at three universities.

Now, he shows forward-thinking leaders how to use motivation science and game design to boost productivity, influence behaviour, and build for the future of work.

Jason has advised the project design of a range of projects – from global strategy of multinational organisations, to helping savvy startups unlock epic progress.

When not liberating the world from poorly designed projects and work, Jason enjoys partaking in extreme sports like coffee snobbery and fruit ninja.
