  • The Scholarship Advtantage

    • June 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Cindy Rack

    College Loan Debt is an astounding $1TRILLION. At the same time, every year, there is millions of dollars in scholarship money that is available to those that take the time to find it. While it is free to find and apply, it is a very time consuming process. Most people don't know where to start, so they don't bother to apply. Those that do apply, aren't really sure how to construct a winning essay.

    Linda Stewart, a single mom, nurse and scholarship judge at a local foundation, was able to secure nearly $100K in scholarship money for her daughter, Lindsay. This accomplishment lead her on a mission to make sure every parent and student knows it is possible to attend college debt-free. It does take time and commitment, but $100K is very freeing as a college graduate to start their career without wondering how they are going to pay back all those loans.

     The Scholarship Advantage is dedicated to making college affordable by providing parents and students with tools and techniques to find, apply and win college scholarships. We teach you the same step by step strategies and process that Linda used to secure her scholarships for Lindsay.
