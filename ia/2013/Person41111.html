Erik Meert's Summary

As an influential and Strategic Finance Executive, I am highly skilled in creating and executing blueprints for successful business growth.

I have a consistent record of achieving profitability, cost control and operational improvement for global multi-billion dollar organisations that include Pitney Bowes, Xerox and 3Com.

Experience includes leading and maximising transformational programs that have a significant impact on the long term profitability of an organisation. I have talent for building and streamlining teams and instilling customer focus.

As a Dutch national I have extensive international experience (multilingual & multicultural) and thrive in fast-paced, rapidly changing environments.
