Chad Luckie's Summary

Chad Luckie is a self-driven entrepreneur who has a strong passion for internet marketing, social media, digital marketing, and business development.

Chad is also a student of marketing at Eastern Michigan University and participates in the Google Search Marketing Course. Chad was a featured student at the Eastern Michigan University Search Marketing Conference in 2011.

Chad Luckie is the owner and founder of The College of Marketing. He is currently a Senior Online Marketing Strategist at Fathom, a top 25 search marketing firm in the USA! Chad is very active in the start-up and tech industry often sharing his knowledge by speaking at local start-up business incubators. He sold his first blog when he was only 16 years old to a holding company in London.

Chad is currently working on a few new projects of his own, but is always open to meeting new people and career opportunities
