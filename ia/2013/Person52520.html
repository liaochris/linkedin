Helen Dunnett's Summary

HELEN DUNNETT - With a training in fine arts and photography, Helen started her career in 1988 as a freelance photographic stylist in London. After two years she changed direction completely joining a charity theatre company in Scotland which devised theatre productions with and performed by the homeless, young offenders and children at risk. During six years with this award-winning charity, she was responsible for public relations and tours, fundraising and was general manager for 3 years.

Helen returned to London in 1996 and joined the private sector working for a PR and event management agency as an account manager on international marketing and communication projects as well being an event director for high profile sporting events.

After moving to Belgium, Helen joined European Crop Protection Association (ECPA) in 2001 to manage ECPA’s web presence, IT infrastructure & knowledge management. Along with Helen’s curiosity of social communications and all things web, her role evolved to lead web-based communication and social media strategies. ECPA became one of the first federations in Brussels to use social media extensively to increase visibility, understanding and stimulating debate on key political issues affecting the crop protection industry.

To further her career in social communications, Helen became an independent consultant in 2009, providing strategic communication services for European and international organisations. Helen also offers personalised coaching and consultancy in web marketing and communications to individuals, start-ups and small operations.
