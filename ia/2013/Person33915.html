Delmar Johnson's Summary

With a background in human resources & career transition training, I am particularly passionate about helping women in small businesses, & women who are in transition in their career life. I primarily educate & empower women to see themselves beyond just their title & embrace their talent, skills & know how.

In 2011, I started HR Brain for Hire, (A Delmar Johnson Enterprise Brand) a place where I can help Women in their Career & Business lives become SUCCESSFUL. This is accomplished through, consulting, coaching, speaking & training.

In a nutshell:

✔ I am the person you call when you no longer what to run your business by yourself, & ready to partner with a solutions oriented human resources resource.

And/Or

✔ When you need help creating a plan for your next career move that not only fits your talent and skills, but also connects with your personality.

✓You're a woman owned business who does not want to keep doing it all alone & ready to partner with a solutions-oriented resource for your human resources needs.
