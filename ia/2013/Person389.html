Aaron Altscher's Summary

Aaron Altscher comes from over 10 years of information technology, marketing, and technical project management experience. Over the past couple years, Aaron has seen much success in creating, growing, and selling several startup dot coms. Altscher is a professor of Entrepreneurship and Business Communications at Howard Community College and holds an MBA with a concentration in Marketing and Project Management. He has appeared on NBC's The Apprentice with Donald Trump and CNBC's The Big Idea with Donnie Deustch. He has played key leadership and innovation roles in many emerging technology projects, such as Bullhorn Mobile, RateMyTeachers.com (largest teacher rating site on the internet), MyChristianT.com (founded by former NFL wide receiver Mark Clayton), EvilChili.com (acquired in Dec 2011), and Ourspace.com (acquired by MySpace.com).  Aaron is currently is focused on the growth of Handteq, a cutting edge mobile property management system.
 
Aaron has been a keynote speaker at several corporations and organizations, including the Columbia Bank, Young Professionals Network, Maryland Tech Counsel, Future Business Leaders of America, Leadership Howard County, and The National Association of Catering Executives. He has spoken at several area universities, including University of Maryland Baltimore County, Towson University, John's Hopkin's Carey Business School, Howard Community College, and University of Mary Washington.  Aaron was selected as the youngest executive in residence at The University of Mary Washington and now holds a position on the Board of Directors for UMW's athletic program.

An avid fitness enthusiast, Aaron was an NCAA All Conference baseball player and is a 2011 Ford IronmanTriathlon finisher.  

Specialties: Real Estate, Technology, Retail, Service, Start-ups, Business Coaching, Innovation and Creativity, Business Communications
