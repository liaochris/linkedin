Jaime Neail's Summary

Working with FTSE 250/100 clients, I provide software life cycle consultancy and software licence services including initial agreement negotiation/renegotiation and or restructuring of T's & C's, licence baseline support, and onward agreement compliance management. Also advising clients on vendor licence programme or product changes, impacts and considerations to cope with change in addition to following ITIL SAM best practices.

My goal is to ensure clients get the best from vendor’s licensing agreements, have a clear understanding of any obligations, avoid unnecessary software spend whilst maintaining effective compliancy. Conservatively, I'd estimate that I've driven between £30-75 million in software licence savings for clients in the past 3Yrs.

Existing clients span all industry sectors including Banking/Financial, Telecom, Services, Local/Central Government and Healthcare.
