Janis Yee's Projects

  • Award-Winning Blu+ App

    • November 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Janis Yee, Hadi Salah, Mustafa Kurdi, Ian Gerald King, Alex Lee, Rahim Dhanani, Vejay Karthic Gandier

    The award-winning Blu+ App utilizes the Blue Button+ API that can access personal health records and create a modern digital dashboard of health records including upcoming appointments, immunizations, and extensions to track diet and fitness for the average populace to be more aware and active in their own health. This proactive awareness has been proven to increase your lifespan by 5-6 years and reduce the number of medical visits annually. Additionally, your healthcare team is fully aware of your progress and the care you have completed to date in your life.

  • Vital Signs

    • October 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Janis Yee, Daniel Finnigan, Paul Jones, Martin Coulombe, Kristopher Kivutha

    Hacking Health held an event on Oct 19 - 21, 2012 which involved collaboration between I.T. and Health Industry professionals towards resolving real world issues through the use of existing and innovating technology.

    Our Project:
    Vital Signs is a point-of-care app which improves patient safety and clinical efficiency. It allows for real time updating of patient vital signs through mobile devices. This data is output as a structured text file for import into EMRs using HL7 standard.

    Find out more at:
    http://appvitalsigns.posterous.com
