Edita Kaye's Additional Information

Interests:

Edita Kaye works with authors and publishers through her website CoachKaye.com. Her authors have received million dollar book deals, appeared on national TV and have been listed on the New York Times bestseller list. Edita Kaye is the award-winning author of business and consumer books including The Fountain of Youth, Bone Builders and The Diet: A Novel. She has also written strategic marketing books including Marketing to the Senior Market, and 101 Ways to Get and Keep New Patients. Several of her books were selected by the Doubleday Book Club, The Literary Guild, and The Christian Book Club. Kaye has also written books custom books for corporations and individual business leaders. Edita Kaye also helps write well-crafted, lead-generating business materials and helps companies - especially non- U.S. based companies - to make sure that all their materials, including their websites are written correctly with no spelling or grammar mistakes.

Groups and Associations:

Edita Kaye has appeared on countless network and cable television programs, as well as being a frequent guest on a wide variety of radio shows. Kaye or her work have also appeared in magazines and newspapers including; The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, USA Today, and The Chicago Tribune. Edita Kaye was the executive producer and host of a 13-part PBS series which featured interviews with experts such as Jack Canfield, Deepak Chopra and more all focused on bringing better lifestyle options to millions of American baby boomers. She was the spokesperson for a new anti-aging line of beauty products from L’Oreal.

Honors and Awards:

She was a columnist for Woman’s World Magazine; Managing, Physician’s Management; Physician’s Weekly and Marketing.
