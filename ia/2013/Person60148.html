Jennifer Brinn's Summary

Mind + Body Wellness Leader

Jennifer Brinn is a nationally recognized mind + body wellness leader with an established private practice in San Francisco. She has trained with leaders such as Donna Eden, Sandra Ingerman and Leslie Temple-Thurston who have provided her with a deep understanding of the body's energy system and integrative healing practices. Jennifer is best known for her innate ability to intuitively identify the root cause of patterns keeping individuals 'stuck'. Over the past decade, countless individuals have experienced deeply transformative and long-term shift from her integrative formula of intuitive guidance, coaching, Reiki healing and mind + body wellness strategies.

As a Master/Teacher of the Usui System of Reiki, Jennifer has been honored to educate and certify hundreds of students in all levels of Reiki. Her students have gone on to practice and teach throughout the U.S and abroad.

Jennifer is a blogger and essayist on spirituality and healing psychology. Her work has been featured in the online publication Elephant Journal. She has lectured and facilitated workshops at Bryman College, the San Francisco Buddhist Center, and Yoga Tree. She has been interviewed by the New York Times, 7x7 Magazine, The Psychic Reader, The Bold Italic and Feel & Look Fabulous Radio Show.

As a former designer of healing gardens, Jennifer consults with interior designers, architects and landscape architects on projects requiring knowledge of healing spaces.

In 2014 Jennifer plans to launch The Human Pace Consultancy assisting teams and organizations with conflict resolution, leadership development, employee enrichment and onboarding programs.
