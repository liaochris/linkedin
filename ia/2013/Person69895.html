Jonathan Sadowsky's Summary

Jonathan Sadowsky founded Vaca Creek Asset Management to provide global fixed income and macro driven research, portfolio management and portfolio construction advice, as well as other Chief Investment Officer functions like asset allocation and risk management to family offices, hedge funds and small institutions.

Jonathan Sadowsky, Macroeconomic Researcher and Portfolio Manager, has over 20 years experience in asset management and research, leveraging a strong engineering background to focus on macroeconomic research and analysis, portfolio construction, and portfolio management. He is proficient in using all fixed income sub-asset classes to optimize risk-reward characteristics of a portfolio and maximize risk adjusted return in different market environments. Broad and deep experience in many different asset classes coupled with quantitative skills to analyze and understand cross-asset class correlations and market dynamics.

Prior to forming Vaca Creek, Jonathan spent six years as a fixed income hedge fund researcher and portfolio manager at Barclays Global Investors (now Blackrock) in San Francisco, CA, specializing in the area of global credit and rate products. He was instrumental in developing investment strategies with a focus on high level portfolio structuring and CDS/swap modeling; as well as overseeing asset valuation and portfolio risk metrics. He also devised quantitative models to analyze, structure and trade assets in credit and rates spaces.

Mr. Sadowsky graduated with a BS in Material Science and Engineering from Lehigh University and studied for his MBA at the NYU Stern School of Business.
