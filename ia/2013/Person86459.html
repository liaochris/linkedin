Mark Denison's Summary

Porter & Woodman Group is a diverse business, providing services in the following areas: Design & Print Management; Promotional Gifts; Corporate Branded Wine and Hamper Gifts; Chalkboards and Point of Sale; Wooden Packaging; Contract Packing, Fulfilment, Mailout and Distribution.

As Director of Print Services I manage the design and print requirements for a broad customer base, as well as all of the marketing material for Porter & Woodman Group's businesses.

I also contribute to areas that help to build the business; whether this involves developing new products and services, managing the ever-increasing marketing mix, potential new business areas for Sales to focus on, or inspiring others to provide unparalleled service.

Specialties

My other areas of experience include: Sales & Marketing; IT; Graphic Design; Business Management; Systems & Production Management; Project Management; Staff Training; and Presentations
