Dr. Keith Kantor's Summary

We have been spending a lot of time and research to have leads coming to us from multiple sources so we can continue our growth and hire more salespeople (health and wellness coordinators). Some areas we have tried are facebook ads, an autocalling survey, canvassing (with a seperate canvassing department), social media (facebook, youtube, flickr and twitter) and the health professional partnership program. All have worked to varying degrees and now we are fine tuning them to maximize the results. We are almost finished redoing our web sites so we can maximize google (both organic and pay per click).
The overall goal is to have multiple sources of almost unlimited warm leads to continue and even increase our growth.

Specialties

Marketing, positioning the companies for fast profitable growth. To be innovative so we constantly have multiple sources of warm leads.
