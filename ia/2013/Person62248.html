Jeremiah Jarmin's Summary

I am a member of the State Bar of Georgia and my practice areas consist of Commercial Real Estate, Lending and Corporate Law. My skills include a concentration in real estate law where I assists companies, lenders and investors with joint venture and business entity formation, acquisition, leasing, foreclosing, securing collateral in financed transactions, and due diligence. Additionally, my law firm provides consulting services to real estate investors. I have considerable experience in assisting foreign investors and companies acquire both commercial and residential investment real estate in the United States as well as acting as their escrow agent. I have served as a guest commentator discussing real estate law on Atlanta Business Radio and as a speaker at the Cornerstone Mortgage Seminar presenting on Business Law Basics for the Real Estate Investor.

CCIM Candidate. CI Intro and CI 101 courses completed.
