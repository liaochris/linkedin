Mike Ghouse, Public Speaker's Summary

Mike exemplifies a healthy balance between earning a living and serving the public.

He defines pluralism as "respecting the otherness of others" and adds, "If we can learn to respect the otherness of others, and accept the God given uniqueness of each one of us, then conflicts fade and solutions emerge". Mike offers pluralistic solutions on issues of the day.

He is a speaker, thinker, writer and an activist of pluralism, interfaith, diversity, human rights, Islam, foreign policy, India and cohesive societies. He will motivate, educate and entertain your group in creating a cohesive environment to work, socialize and function effectively.

Mike has a strong presence on National and local TV, Radio, and Print Media. His book Standing up for others is due to be released soon.

Mike was a commissioner at City of Carrollton, and has served on the boards of many non-profit organizations including Dallas Peace Center.

He is a management consultant with comprehensive experience in home building, remodeling, and investment management for over three decades. He now serves his friends through First City Homes, a building company committed to providing services for their real estate investments.

Mike was a manager with industry giants like Ryland Homes, Grand Homes, Drees Custom Homes and Bluffview Builders. Prior to that he was a vice president of Lewis Fire Equipment in Texas and was a dy finance controller at Fluor Corporation in Saudi Arabia.
