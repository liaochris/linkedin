Ken Ashley's Summary

As an advocate and advisor for companies who lease or own real estate, Ken has worked for 18 years to help business leadership translate business strategy into real estate solutions that helps not only cut cost but boost profits. He believes that real estate can enable high level business performance and, when properly executed, serve to differentiate smart companies from their competition. He applies a strategic, yet common sense approach to delivering results oriented solutions to clients. This approach allows Ken and the C&W team to develop long-term consultative relationships focused on a high level real estate strategy rather than simply “finding space.”

Ken, a top 100 producer at C&W, is also a seventeen-year Phoenix Member of the Atlanta Commercial Board of REALTORS® Million Dollar Club. He is regularly a top producer in the Atlanta marketplace and around the US. He is the recipient of C&W’s 2003 and 2009 Service Excellence Award, C&W Atlanta Deal of the Year in 2007, 2008 and 2011. Atlanta Business Chronicle awarded him Atlanta's real estate deal of the year in 2011 as well.

Ken is a member of the Society of Industrial and Office REALTORS® (SIOR) where he served as the 2009 Georgia Chapter President. Also he has earned his CCIM designation and is a member of the leading industry organization, CoreNet Global, where he serves as a regular speaker in front of global directors of real estate. He has also earned the CoreNet Global designation Master of Corporate Real Estate (MCR) and twice the Luminary Award which is given to top rated faculty. Ken serves on the C&W Broker Advisory Committee for which functions as a US board of directors for C&W.

Ken is very fortunate to work in a career that he loves and with clients who are fun and engaging. The only thing better is his 20 year marriage to Karen and their 4 great kids. Ken and his family, including an old dog, neglected goldfish and much too young of a Guinea pig live in the Atlanta suburb of Dunwoody.
