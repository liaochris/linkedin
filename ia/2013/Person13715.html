Elizabeth (Beth) Junell's Summary

20 years of professional experience in accounting, internal controls and financial statement related matters, including forensic accounting, investigations, litigation consulting, and financial statement audit and assurance. Expertise includes:

• Investigating suspected violations of anti-bribery and anti-corruption statutes, including the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (“FCPA”) and the UK Bribery Act.

• Expertise in anti-bribery and anti-corruption compliance programs, including design and implementation of policies, procedures and internal controls, as well as continuous monitoring protocols and remediation of developing gaps.

• Expertise in conducting investigations resulting from accounting and SEC reporting violations arising from either unintentional errors or fraudulent activity. Experience comes from many perspectives, including on behalf of management for companies under investigation by various federal and state agencies; for an audit or special committee and its counsel conducting an independent investigation; and for counsel in a post-investigation Monitorship capacity.

• Conducting detailed analysis in complex matters involving fraud (asset misappropriation and fraudulent financial reporting), post acquisition disputes, breach of contract claims, termination-for-convenience claims, and bankruptcy claim resolution.
