Carol Lee, BA, ABR, LBA's Summary

Carol Lee is a luxury estate agent serving clients in Newport Coast, Newport Beach and Corona del Mar. She sold over $21M in 2012.

In 2012, Lee negotiated record deals for luxury residences, residential income properties and land on the oceanfront, the bayfront, CdM and Newport Coast.

An Orange County native, Lee currently resides in Newport Coast with her husband.

For insights into what Lee can do for you, see her client testimonials on Trulia, Zillow and LinkedIn.

If you would like professional assistance in finding a home or investment property, call Carol today.

Specialties: Properties over $1M. Homes with a view. Homes on the water or sand. Pocket listings.
