Joon Shin's Summary

Mr. Shin is an Inc. 500 award winning executive, who possess continuous intellectual curiosity in the intersections of business and technology, with over sixteen years of well-rounded and seasoned leadership of complex initiatives working with many diverse teams across several industries. He is currently a Board Director at Adept Technologies Inc., a leading enterprise software corporation for businesses and governments; consults, mentors, and advises other startups; and has held senior leadership positions that demand: corporate governance, executive management, entrepreneurship, intrapreneurship, program management, business transformation, product development, business development, strategic positioning, technology management and enterprise risk management. His success is largely driven from his passion in understanding internal and external customers’ points of view, lines of businesses, and requirements in support of their goals and needs, which has resulted in millions of dollars of year-over-year realizations of benefits from business and technological innovations. Mr. Shin completed the Executive Master's in Technology Management program, a cosponsored program between Penn Engineering and Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania, and received his Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Lehigh University.

Specialties: Strategy development; executive management; business development; consulting; continuous quality improvement; employee training; new product introductions; operational excellence; technology management; business transformation; Lean Enterprise; change management; product and interactive / online development; business analytics & intelligence; business process management; organizational dynamics; risk analysis; program management; talent management; investor relations; vendor management.
