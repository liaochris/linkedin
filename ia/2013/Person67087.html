John Sonnhalter's Summary

Who Sonnhalter is...

We are a B-to-B marketing communications firm that focuses solely on the marketing to the professional tradesmen in the Electrical, Construction, Industrial and MRO Markets.
We have been in business for over 30 years and was named one of B to B Magazine's "Top Agencies" for 2009 and 2010 - one of the only agencies in the Midwest to achieve this honor.

What we do...

Jack -of-one trade...and master of it. We've always been focused on one specialty- business to business marketing-which is why we've been around since 1976.

Marketing
It's all about marketing. We have a solid reputation and a proven track record of helping clients interface with their customers.

Creative
The heart and soul of any advertising agency is its creative output.Good creative is easy to spot. The ad that catches your eye,the e-mail subject line that makes you open it. Our concepts are smart and focused. They get your attention.

PR
For impact,credibility and cost-effectiveness, it's hard to beat a tailored PR program.We maintain strong media relationships allowing us to identify opportunities for PR that will give your marketing effort more of an edge

E-Business
It's the hottest thing since...well...since the last hottest thing. We've been helping clients with their digital needs ever since Al Gore invented the internet. Whether its design of web sites or banner ads or helping you set up a blog or start twittering we can show you how digital can work for even the most conservative companies.

Specialties

Developing integrated marketing programs that target the professional tradesman,industrial and MRO markets.
