Jessica Harris' Summary

Interpreting Profession:
I have been involved with the Deaf and Interpreting community since August 1996, and established my own Interpreting Agency in 2004 providing that personal touch for businesses/clients. This is a Florida small business that knows the needs of the community and sends out individual requests based on interpreter skill, experience, location, deaf consumer/ business client communication needs and preferences. Businesses are offered an In-Service Training for their staff which provides the foundation for working with deaf clients and interpreters. We are continually looking for Qualified/Certified interpreters to represent the agency in the upmost professional manner. We promote an environment of high standards, respect, professionalism and integrity. Contact us for more information.

Health & Business Coach:
Being a business entrepreneur and a busy mom of three young children I was very impressed that I was able to lose my unwanted weight and inches with ease. I have personally lost 22 lbs & 21 inches. I enjoy sharing these products and have helped many friends, family, and referrals lose over 30+ pounds. As a FirstFitness Nutrition Wellness Coach it is very rewarding to see individuals get inspired and reach their health and weight loss goals. Nothing is better than seeing someone transform before your eyes inside and out. When someone is on their journey to health and wellness they begin to feel and look amazing, have more energy, confidence, and more. I am there to coach those to their true potential. In addition, I work with businesses to grow their business and provide a superior nutritional line of products to meet their customer’s health and wellness needs. Many of these professionals are chiropractors, personal trainers and fitness center owners. Contact me for information on these products and business opportunity details.
