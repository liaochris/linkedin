Bill Hanifin's Summary

My background has its foundations in strategic marketing, payments, and marketing technology. While I have worked across retail, banking, telco, hospitality, and airline verticals, I have particularly deep experience working with retail and financial services brands.

Over the past 8 years, our focus at Hanifin Loyalty has been to reshape the 30 year old loyalty marketing business to incorporate new business models and digital communications channels.

We are fluent in Social Loyalty, Social CRM, and Lifecycle Marketing to engage the 150 Million US consumers we refer to as Consumer 2.0, including the Millennial generation (Gen Y).

As founders of the Customer Strategy Network (CSN), we are comfortable working in international markets. CSN is a professional organization linking the world’s best independent relationship and loyalty marketing practitioners.

For insight on my business perspectives, you can read unbiased and independent thinking on all aspects of Customer Centric marketing in our blog, Loyalty Truth.

I serve as North American Contributing Editor for The Wise Marketer, a leading publication covering global news and opinion in the loyalty marketing industry, and frequently present at industry conferences.

In the past, I have been a presenter at events sponsored by SourceMedia, the Direct Marketing Association, Visa, The Loyalty Expo, and the Institute for International Research.

More Information may be found at www.HanifinLoyalty.com

Specialties: I maintain a special level of competency in financial services, payment systems and card marketing, while developing a new base of knowledge aimed to engage and build brand loyalty across Consumer 2.0, the digitally connected group which includes Generation Y.

I am experienced working in international markets including Latin America, Caribbean, Mexico, EU, and A/P regions.

Consulting delivery, project management, and hands on guidance to implement recommended strategies are key skill sets.
