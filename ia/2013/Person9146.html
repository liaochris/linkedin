Alexandre Passant's Summary

Geek, researcher, hacker, entrepreneur. I love to build, learn and share. I'm trying to make the Web a better place, and I'm having fun doing it.

Co-founder and CEO/CTO of MDG Web (http://mdg.io), a music-tech start-up based in Dogpatch Labs Dublin. Our main product is seevl (http://seevl.net), a data-driven music discovery platform.

Previously Research Fellow and Unit Leader at DERI, working on high-impact R&D projects with partners such as Google, Cisco, etc.
