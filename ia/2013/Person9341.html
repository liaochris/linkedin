Jose Aracil's Summary

Jose’s experience assessing his customers’ needs and designing highly effective solutions have been key to successfully complete numerous high profile projects. Quality, innovation and rigour at the forefront of Jose's work today driving local and International testing methodologies to higher levels.

The past 10 years has seen Jose lead numerous high profile software testing projects using different technologies such as SAP, Siebel, Tibco, spanning multiple markets including the United Kingdom, Switzerland and Germany. In this time he has developed and refined best practise benchmarks through working for companies where the quality of software plays a key role within the development cycle.

In the non-functional testing space, Jose has mastered bottleneck identification and troubleshooting as well as security assessment in multilayer systems, where a good understanding of the environment is key for success. Jose has worked as Test Manager in a number of functional and non-functional projects, Near-Shore and Off-Shore.

Jose also enjoys learning and experimenting with new media, design and photography in his spare time indulging his natural curiosity in technology and design.
