Bruce Stanley's Experience

Sr. Faculty and Graphic Design Coordinator, Brand Director, Nossi College of Art

Nossi College of Art

Educational Institution; 11-50 employees; Higher Education industry

April 2010 – Present (3 years 5 months) 590 Cheron Rd. Madison, TN 37115

As the Graphic Design, Interactive Design and Advertising Program coordinator for the college's faculty, it's my mission to lead the charge in cultivating the best professional talent to develop and mentor students. Together we will prepare them to make a difference in the design/advertising/marketing industry and give them a prideful alma mater for getting them there.

Redesigning the curriculum and finding opportunities for growth within the structure of the Graphic Design program is a focus I look forward to every day at Nossi. The evolving challenge to instruct students with current and relevant principles will continue to be my and the college's mission.

My passion has and always will be advertising and branding. I'm fortunate to use my talent and experience to help plan the marketing efforts of this college. Finding and working with other talented creatives to execute the school's branding initiatives is a responsibility that has been entrusted to me by the Nossi family.

redpepper

As a CD at redpepper I developed all processes and implemented accountabilities – pioneering efforts like, “parallel thinking” and “integrated perspective”. Directing diverse and talented creatives, from digital scientists to designers unique as their shoes, was why I showed up everyday. With offices in Nashville and Atlanta, I learned how to manage teams in different cities without inhibiting communications. This agency and its people taught me to be fearless in the face of marketing challenges.

Bruce Stanley's Volunteer Experience & Causes

      Design-a-thon
      • Social Services
      July 2010 – present (3 years 2 months)

      Design-a-thon is a volunteer crowdsourcing event put on by Ian Rhett, Civic Actions, Geek for Good and Jessica Murray, Social Media Club, in conjunction with my creative leadership to help a single non-profit org each year develop necessary marketing strategies and deliverables that they would not have been able to achieve on their own–ALL within a 24 hour event.
