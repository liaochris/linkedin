Kristin M. Davin, Psy.D.'s Summary

As an experienced clinician with over 10 years of experience, I help people create lasting, positive changes in their lives and become "unstuck" by providing clarity in some of their most challenging and emotionally-charged situations. Navigating the ups and downs of life isn't always easy, but working together, I can help people in this endeavor. The goal is to help you get healthy again and back doing what you love doing the most - living your life on your terms!

My style is open, direct, honest, empathetic, and engaging. I find that most people want to work collaboratively with a clinician to work through their difficult situations and taking this approach helps them to this end.

Eclectic in nature, I use Cognitive Behavior Therapy and Solution Focused Therapy to establish and work on treatment goals. I address underlying issues and work hard to connect the dots in your life to implement necessary changes and learn new strategies for leading a more fulfilling and healthy life.
