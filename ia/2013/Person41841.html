Edmond "Ed" Reuschling's Summary

I’m a highly experienced business consultant who specializes in strategic planning and tactical solutions, which include key process improvement, business turn-around & tune-ups and an expert in sales compliance, SIOP (sales, inventory & operations planning). My real forte is putting value to the bottom line and my passion is helping organizations reach their full potential. Currently, I am President and Owner of North Star Strategies, a strategic planning consultancy that utilizes a unique 5-phase strategic planning process that identifies an organization’s North Star, defining its vision, mission, and long-term goals, and creating a strategy statement that aligns with these principles. North Star Strategies then uses a series of analytical tools to consider every angle of your company, starting at exactly where you are today and examining everything - including your hits, your misses and what keeps you up at night. Through the next stages North Star Strategies develops the strategic plan that helps organizations get focused and get results.

Before starting North Star Strategies, I served as JLG Industries Corporate Sales Compliance Officer, Vice President of Sales Operations, and General Manager of start-up unit JLG Equipment Services. Prior to that I was President and CEO of Pangborn Corporation and served as Chairman of Pangborn India, a joint venture company that I founded. I earned a BS degree in Industrial Management from Johns Hopkins University and an MBA in Operations Research from the University of Baltimore. I currently reside in Mt. Pleasant, SC outside of Charleston.

High velocity service, a successful track record, broad based experience, and prompt delivery of results: that is North Star Strategies. Corporate Sales Compliance Officer

JLG Industries Inc

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; osk; Machinery industry

December 1995 – June 2007 (11 years 7 months) Hagerstown, Md

I joined JLG in 1995 as the General Manager of JLG Equipment Services, a fledging unit involved in the re-marketing of used & rebuilt equipment and the rental of new equipment to pull through new sales. I had full P&L responsibility and developed all policies, procedures & systems. I redesigned the re-manufacturing process to reduce delivery from 75 days to 9 days, grew the rental fleet, utilized trades-ins to pull through new unit sales & gain share, and successfully executed this multiple channel strategy yielding a growth in sales from $8 million to $75 million in just 3-years at a 60% ROA.

I transitioned into VP Sales Operations for JLG, where I was responsible for the sales of Aerial Work Platform equipment in North America while participating in the formulation of company strategy. While in this position, which included an economic downturn, JLG increased market share and ultimately increased sales by over 50% through aggressive deal structuring, new product introductions, key process improvements including the redesign of sales distribution, and sales force development.

As Corporate Sales Compliance Officer for this $3 billion global manufacturer, I successfully tackled an environment of non-standard sales transactions, a sales force of “deal makers,” and a trail of compliance infractions by designing a system that resulted in increased management visibility through an electronic, on-line approval process and a more disciplined sales force yielding increased profitability and eliminating compliance violations. I also developed, implemented, and managed JLG’s initial global SIOP (sales, inventory & operations planning) program that enabled the company to improve its demand forecast accuracy, balance supply against demand requirements, reduce finished goods inventory by over $300 million in 2-years while improving on-time delivery performance vs customer requirements.

President / CEO

Pangborn Corporation

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Machinery industry

1976 – 1995 (19 years) Hagerstown, MD

As President and CEO at Pangborn Corporation I oversaw all functions of this $75 million manufacturer of shot blast cleaning and peening systems serving the automotive, steel, fabrication, foundry, aerospace, and metal preparation industries. This was a leveraged buy-out, turn-around situation in a declining market, with a mature product line, and UAW representation. Under my leadership, we were able to re-establish our strategic direction, build a “Total Quality” work environment, deliver some of the most sophisticated products in the industry, value engineer the product line to reduce costs, pay down debt, maintain positive cash flow, and become the most profitable company in the industry. The significant improvement in operating performance enabled the company to be sold for a high multiple of earnings. Earlier in my career at Pangborn, I had the opportunity to serve in the roles of VP Operations, Plant Manager, Materials Manager, and Director Manufacturing Engineering.
