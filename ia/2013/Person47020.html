Jane Garthson's Summary

I work to create better futures for communities and organizations through values-based leadership. I've been consulting to charities, trade and professional organizations, government agencies and other nonprofits since 1992. I have successful experience as Executive Director and Board chair as well as 21 years management experience in six ministries of the Ontario government. I've served on charity, association and government boards including every officer position; and am a founder of a national professional association.

Member of and active volunteer at CSAE and Ontario Nonprofit Network. Member, Institute of Corporate Directors (ICD), TI Canada, EthicsCentre CA, Creating the Future, Boardsource, Cultural Human Resource Council.

Past Chair of Ethics Practitioners Association of Canada and Chair of Governance of Canadian Science Fiction and Fantasy Association, which runs the Prix Auroras.

Specialties: Leadership: hands-on interim and transitional leadership; governance; strategic planning; Board development; Board-staff relations; corporate social responsibility (CSR); partnership development; organizational reviews; community and stakeholder consultation; privacy; risk management. Ethics and Integrity: diagnostics; oversight; programs; assessments and audits.
