Chris France's Summary

Advance2000 is a full-service IT engineering and communications firm serving North American clients. We not only consult/deploy IT solutions at our customers offices, but we also own and operate a "private cloud" datacenter where we can bring our customers to one, highly capable yet cost-effective IT infrastructure that also includes resources such as CIOs, engineers, and business services to give our customers a competitive advantage.

Advance2000 serves several industries such as Healthcare, Architecture/Engineering/Construction (AEC), Government, Education, Legal, and Professional Services.

With over 100 employees, our own data center, and several offices, we are able to serve the largest corporations. Yet we are nimble enough to provide the same IT capabilities the "big boys" enjoy to the small-medium sized firms.
