David Harmer's Summary

• Banking attorney with deep expertise in regulatory compliance, risk management, and consumer credit.
• Nearly successful candidate for Congress from one of the nation’s most hotly contested districts.
• Broad experience in policymaking: counsel to Senate Judiciary, litigator with Pacific Legal, fellow at Heritage, published by Cato.
• Fluent speaker and writer. Veteran of numerous media interviews and debates; published works include law review articles, op-eds, policy analyses, and a book.
• Strong relationships with political leaders including key members of 112th Congress.
