Mark Daly's Summary

If you need immigration help contact me for a FREE initial consultation - Call toll free at 1-877-381-2814 or send an email to Mark Daly at mdaly@gmail.com.

As an immigration & naturalization lawyer, I help individuals and families put down roots in the United States! I have been working in the field of immigration law since 1995 and love what I do. My goal is to help the millions of immigrants who must navigate through the immigration system every year.

As an immigration law trainer, I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and experiences with attorneys just starting out and coaching them on the art of practicing immigration law. I am doing my part to reverse the trend of attorney burn out through coaching, training and designing compact, and establishing turn-key immigration law offices that more easily deliver excellent results to clients.
