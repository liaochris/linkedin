Daniel Robles' Summary

Degree in Audiovisual Communication and Master in Knowledge Society Contents, my career and vocational training have been always focused on the Internet and new communication ways through digital devices.

Digital native, I'm a multi-faceted and multi-skilled professional who can perform many functions related to most aspects involved in online communication, my specialization. What I most love doing is to plan and manage the online presence, strategy and actions of any organization, professional or business.

Besides my specialization, I have some experience in web analytics, SEO, information architecture and usability; skills that complete my profile as specialist in online communication. My training also allows me to perform offline communication actions.

I am currently working at UPV, activity that occupies most of my time. Eventually I also give lectures on various topics related to Internet and offer my services as freelance consultant.
