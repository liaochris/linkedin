Ajay Pangarkar, CTDP, CMA's Summary

Highly recognized and leading workplace performance strategist and thought-leader. Ajay is a world-renowned author on the topic of workplace performance and is a sought after international conference keynote speaker. His research and insight into workplace and employee performance is breaking boundaries and leading to new streams of thought. Ajay's company, CentralKnowledge, works closely with Fortune 1000 clients assisting them in developing innovative and effective performance and training solutions with ensuring that every employee skill is tied into meeting business and strategic objectives.
