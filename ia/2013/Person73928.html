Kelly Pavlovic's Summary

Kelly believes the road to 21st century marketing success is paved with updates, comments, likes, tweets and blogs – and the signposts are visual content like photos and videos.

Kelly received her BS in structural engineering from Case Western Reserve University and worked in that industry until 1993. After many blueprints, notepads full of calculations, and buildings which remained standing, she hung up her T-square and found her entrepreneurial spirit.

In 1994, she began helping clients realize their vision for printed marketing collateral. In 1997, she added web development to her offerings with a certainty that the digital trend of the future would add to her success.

When the social media wave hit in 2009, she had her surfboard in hand and “went for it.” Since then, along with her design projects, she has been speaking to, and consulting with, business owners, as well as training groups & individuals.

She works with organizations who are overwhelmed with the concept of social marketing, developing strategies which are consistent with their brand and marketshare goals. She helps clients find their own voice in the 21st century global conversation.

Kelly earned her professional membership with National Speakers Association in 2012. She is an enthusiastic member of the Ohio chapter where she assists the marketing team with social strategy and content marketing management.

As author, speaker, consultant, designer (and self-admitted "social" addict), the owner of Feather & Stone Consulting, Kelly Pavlovic, is passionate about social media marketing and spends an inordinate amount of time each day in pursuit of more knowledge on the topic.

Kelly is the author of Keep Calm and Get Social: The Stress-Less Guide to Social Marketing

Specialties: Social Media Marketing. Branding & Identity, Website Development, PHP/MySQL, Print Layout & Design
