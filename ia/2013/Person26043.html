Lisa Clausén's Summary

Lisa Clausén offers coaching, mentoring, teaching and speaking services to support entrepreneurs and leaders, individuals and groups, to create successful businesses and living desired, healthy and soul fulfilling good lives.

Lisa is a stand for you living your potential and running and leading your business in an authentic aligned way.

She is an expert in facilitating transformation and a catalyst for wellness and beauty to be manifested in your daily life and environment alongside professional results.

Her work supports bridging the hard and soft factors of business and life. Lisa's approach is holistic and includes tools from NLP, Yoga, Mindfulness and Feng Shui along side business, branding and money related tools. She has got a background as a consulting Financial and Business Controller in Sweden and Internationally and a Bachelor from the Stockholm School of Economics.

Lisa's core strengths according to The Clifton StrengthsFinder are: ideation (creativity and possibilities), analytics (overview and different perspectives), maximizing (quality and improvements), woo (networking and personal connections) and connectedness (holistic and interconnectedness).

With an integrative mindset her work is the doorway to the best possible results for her customers, professionally and personally. The sum of professional and personal growth is where the biggest potential for breakthroughs and new results lays.

Lisa has got a broad and rich coaching experience running from Business Coaching of entrepreneurs, Leadership Coaching, Money Coaching, Wellness Coaching, Life Coaching, Career Coaching, Job Coaching over to Rehab Coaching.
