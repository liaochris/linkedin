Matt Spaeth's Summary

I am a healer, entrepreneur and nature lover who is passionate about helping people get the most out of life. Whether it be meditating on opening the third eye, geeking out on Wordpress or shredding some pow, I'm game.

I have been developing websites since 1997 and I love using technology and marketing to help people get their message out to the world. We live in an amazing time when we each have the tools to affect so many. By leveraging this power, the possibilities are endless.

I co-founded Food Integrity Now in 2010, a blog and podcast that is dedicated to protecting the integrity of the food we eat. We started this organization after the realizing that corporate ties and political lobbying are compromising the safety of our food supply. It was watching the growth of Food Integrity Now that I realized the true power of leveraging the tools available today.

Now I use what I learned to build custom mobile-responsive Wordpress themes for clients who want websites that will meet today's needs. Wordpress and the Genesis Framework enable me to rapidly deliver professional websites which put my clients in the driver's seat. Never has there been a time where it has been so easy to put your message out there, generate new lead and stay in touch with the current ones. For examples of my work, check out my portfolio link in my contact info.

Running in parallel is Supreme Flow LLC, providing tools for empowerment, happiness and fulfillment. Through coaching and Tai Yi energy work, I assist people in recognizing what limiting beliefs are holding them back, then give them the tools to let them go. Through letting go of limits, we increase health, power and enjoyment of life. Recently rebranded from the former name, Stream of Healing LLC.

Specialties: Wordpress, CSS, HTML, PHP, Marketing, Copywriting, SEO, Graphic Design, Photoshop, Cinema 4D, Coaching, Tai Yi, Holistic Medicine, Emotional Intelligence, Philosophy, Psychology
