Judy Reynolds' Summary

Judy has been selling real estate for 31 years and has been Owner/Broker of Evergreen Realty since 1998. She has a passion for helping her clients to make their life easier and to make their dreams come true. She has the oldest web site in Central Mass, designed in 1994. Cutting edge technology has been a focal point for creating great marketing opportunities of her clients and her company. Her team of professional Realtors are exceptionally well educated and active in the real estate community as well as their local towns.

Judy has served as Director and Officer of the Worcester Regional Association of Realtors for many years, and she is currently serving as Director of the Massachusetts Association of Realtors and member of the Technology Committee. She is Regional Vice President for the Mass Chapter of CRS.

She was chosen as one of 500 people to beta test the NAR E-PRO designation, and she has beta tested the National Association of Realtor's Real Property Resources, which provided Realtor clients with the most accurate real estate information. Ask your Realtor about this service.

Specialties: Owner Broker of Evergreen Realty specializing in residential real estate sales, custom designed new construction, land development, land sales, and real estate technology.
