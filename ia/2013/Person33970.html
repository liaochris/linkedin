Paul Demers' Summary

I am a Boston-based User Experience/User Interface designer that hand-crafts beautifully simple experiences out of clean and semantic markup. I love to solve complex design problems using HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery.

When I am not pushing pixels, you can most likely find me out on a cycling excursion, feeding my self-diagnosed hat addiction, or hammering out a beat on the drums.

Specialties: xHTML/HTML5, CSS/CSS3, SASS, Compass, LESS, jQuery, UI Design, UX Design, Site Flows, Wireframing, Sproutcore, Knockout.js, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Wordpress, Identity/Branding, Marketing, Copywriting.
