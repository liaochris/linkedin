John Rorer's Summary

Four years of elected public service including serving as chairman of my county's governing board, plus decades of experience in accounting, finance, marketing and business development have led me to the realization that entrepreneurship is the key to economic development and creating jobs in an area like the NC Outer Banks which has few employers. I assist others with creating and building their own businesses as Vice-Chairman of SCORE 497 and through the Albemarle Business Center, a non-profit entrepreneurial incubator; Rorer Internet, website and social media marketing; the 10 Hour Work Week, a business system allowing you to create a business and replace your job through social media marketing; and Share the Fun Resorts - marketing of fractional resort property shares.

Outside interests include stand up paddleboarding, flying, sailing and horses.

Specialties

Internet marketing, business analysis and development and financial reporting.
