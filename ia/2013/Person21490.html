Phillip Cenere's Summary

Phillip Cenere is an experienced leader in executive education and higher education management and a non-executive director and board consultant.

He is Managing Director of Engines of Success, a consultancy venture specialising in corporate governance, risk management, issues and crises communication, CSR / sustainability and stakeholder engagement.

Phillip is a member of the NSW Council and Curriculum Portfolio Committee of the Australian Institute of Company Directors (AICD), an Associate Fellow and a former councillor of the Association for Tertiary Education Management (ATEM) and a member and former councillor of the Public Relations Institute of Australia (PRIA). He is also a member of the Australian Business Deans Council’s (ABDC) Teaching and Learning Network, ABDC International Network, the Australia China Business Council and the Economics Society of Australia.

Phillip is Associate Dean of the School of Business and Senior Lecturer (Public Relations) at The University of Notre Dame Australia (Sydney campus), having previously taught across a range of business and communication subjects at Macquarie University, UTS and the University of Wollongong, and working as a features journalist for multimedia and trade publications.

Phillip is married and lives in Sydney.

Specialties: Corporate governance, risk management, public relations, executive and higher education management.
