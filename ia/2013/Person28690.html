Tom Read's Experience

Art Director

Everlution Software

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

January 2010 – Present (4 years)

Art director, graphic artist / illustrator and design team manager at Everlution Software (everlution.com), a Web software and incubation company in Borough, London.

Creative direction and branding for all Everlution's many in-house and affiliated incubation products, from productivity software and iPhone apps to HR web applications. Lead creative on Vivo Rewards (an affiliated company), an award and incentives programme initially for schools now moving into social and charity sectors. Vivo Rewards includes a school rewards platform (VivoMiles.com), a social network (VivoMe.com), a primary school avatar creation website (VivoStars.com) and a online bank for families (VivoPocketMoney.com).

 Core responsibilities across all projects: character / avatar / assets creation, web design, illustration, UI and wire-framing. Being part of the management group consulting on, and setting features and specifications for all Everlution projects. Lead the 5 person design team as well as working alongside project, product and development managers. Also solely responsible for all printed materials for all products, ranging from brochures, newsletters, through to packaging, posters and exhibition design.

Senior Graphic Artist

EA

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; ERTS; Entertainment industry

2006 – January 2010 (4 years)

Lead creative for branding, marketing promotions, features, user experience, animation and character design for European side of the casual gaming site Pogo.com. Responsibilities included design of marketing campaigns (Flash banners, landing pages, e-newsletters, features) for the UK, France, Germany and US teams. Managing and mentoring junior designers and the LiveOps team. Character creation, art and illustration. Print design including leaflets, tee-shirts, toys and packaging. Liaising with internal and external license property holders including Hasbro and 20th Century Fox. Also included frequent trips to the US to update the management teams and participate in key long term strategic projects.

Freelance graphic design, illustration and art direction for print and online. Produced work for a variety of brands such as Cartoon Network, Warner Bros, PricewaterhouseCoopers, BBC and Starbucks Coffee Company.
