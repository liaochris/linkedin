Donovan Solms' Summary

I'm a programmer at heart, and has contributed to the open-source community in the field of multi-touch.

I co-authored the 'Multitouch Technologies Handbook' along with other experts in the field, built the first open-source based multi-touch screen in Africa and some distributed high-load messaging systems.

In mobile, I built the first mobile site that won an award at the Mobile Marketing Association's Global Awards.

These days I can be found working cirqls.com, an exciting new South African e-commerce startup.
