Buying real estate notes has always been a primary business, though we now also help companies with their short-term cash flow needs by offering factoring (accounts receivable financing). Real estate notes (also known as mortgage notes and deed of trust notes) are created when a property owner sells that property to a buyer using owner financing.

Commercial invoice factoring and medical factoring help medium-size businesses with their cash flow needs. Basically, clients receive most of their cash right after they send out the invoices to customers instead of waiting 30-60 days or longer to be paid. Factoring helps the business to meet payroll, buy inventory, etc.
