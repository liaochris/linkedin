Project Director

Vote Compass

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Civic & Social Organization industry

June 2013 – Present (7 months) Toronto

VoteCompass is a non profit organization run by a global network of political scientists and is based on the premise that an informed electorate is fundamental for a robust democracy.

Vote compass operates a democratic engagement online application & based on their responses to a series of public policy propositions relevant to a given election campaign, users are presented with an analysis of how their views compare with the positions of each of the political parties in the running for election. We partner with major news media organisations around the world (including, to date, the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation, the Australian Broadcasting Corporation, and The Wall Street Journal) to promote the application and we have to date drawn millions of users across seven election campaigns.

You can try the most recent iteration of the application (developed for the Australian federal election) at http://www.abc.net.au/votecompass/

Our projects are motivated by a desire to stimulate voter engagement in election campaigns, spread awareness of the public policy positions adopted by parties, increase accountability of politicians to their platforms, and prompt government to be more responsive to public opinion.
