Erika-Marie S. Geiss' Summary

Copy editor, proofreader and published author with over a decade of editing and publishing experience. Editing skills include AP, Chicago Manual and other styles working with a variety of small and large documents from ad copy and marketing materials to RFPs, full-length book manuscripts and professional academic articles.

Professional goals are to continue writing and editing in traditional printed and online/digital media and providing private individual and business clients with effective editing and proofreading services.

Specialties

Art & architectural history. Writing (articles & non-fiction books). Ad book and magazine design/layout, editing, editorial decision-making. Proofreading, copy editing, substantive editing, proofreader's marks and symbols, strong communications skills, teaching experience (college level), public speaking, writing (books, periodicals and blogging), AP, Chicago, MLA and other writing styles, fluent in English and Italian, proficient in Spanish, reading knowledge of French and German.
