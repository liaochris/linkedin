Erin Sweeney's Summary

I am a graphic designer with more than 20 years of professional experience in the advertising and design industry. I began my career as a Production Consultant for a training development company and eventually landed with a group of highly talented and creative individuals at a marketing and advertising agency as a Production Artist. I later decided to start my own company. Since leaving an office to be on my own, I've been able to establish myself as a freelance graphic and web designer. In addition to graphic design services, I've developed a love of Wordpress website design and offer that service to my clients. I like to focus on small business owners, and I feel Wordpress gives small business owners more control over their website. I've also become knowledgeable in social media marketing. The virtual world has exploded and many companies that once tried to reach out through traditional media, such as print advertising, are looking for new and innovative ways to reach their customers. I assist clients in establishing their web presence through the use of social media. I believe my experience in graphic design enhances my ability to assist my clients in growing their business over the internet.

Specialties

Erin Sweeney Design focuses on designing Wordpress websites for businesses that want to self-manage their own website. I provide clients with ways to grow their business and target their message through social networking, specializing in developing customized Facebook pages and constructing social media marketing plans. I offer graphic design services such as logo design, page layout, newsletters and illustration as well.
