Lauren Class Schneider's Summary

Lauren Class Schenider is producer and founder of Broadway Fantasy Camp, a program created for adults bitten by the show-biz bug and yearning to live out their Broadway dreams. Broadway Fantasy Camp also works with corporations to create individualized experiences to meet the specific needs of the client. After 3 decades in theatre, television and event production, Lauren has had diverse clients ranging from The Dedication Ceremony of the WWII Memorial, to customized plays for pharmaceutical companies, from New Year’s Eve in Times Square to the Tony and Drama Desk Awards. Broadway Fantasy Camp’s group sessions range from one hour to five days and are led by a team of theatre industry professionals including actors, directors, choreographers and musicians. Various sessions include: conversations with Tony Award winning Broadway performers; going behind the scenes at a major Broadway theater for a VIP onstage/backstage tour; rehearsing song & dance routines from the world’s best loved musicals; and living the actor’s life — from headshots and auditions (where everybody gets a part) to celebrating at your opening night cast party. Broadway Fantasy Camp gives participants unprecedented access to the world of The Great White Way, and memories that will last a lifetime.
