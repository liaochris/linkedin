Andrew Wooten's Summary

Andrew Wooten is the President of Safety Awareness Firearms Education (S.A.F.E.), and has been in the safety and security industry for the past twenty-four years.

Throughout his career, Andrew has
• Worked with individuals that have committed crimes and survivors of crimes
• Developed cost effective security programs for businesses of all sizes and corporations
• Spoken at local and national conventions and seminars regarding crime prevention, corporate and retail security and premises liability
• Consulted and appeared on many radio and television shows

Andrew’s philosophy is that, “A person’s best weapon is themselves: their mind, voice and body.” Each person regardless of size is capable of learning and adapting some form of the techniques presented in his seminars.
