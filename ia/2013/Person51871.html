Harry E. Shade's Publications

  • Rare Sense; One Day It Will Be Common

    • Trafford
    • March 1, 2008
    Authors: Harry E. Shade

    In Rare Sense: One Day It Will Be Common, Harry Shade melds the teachings of the world's major religions, "new age" spiritual leaders and common sense to provide us with a practical guide to a fulfilled and balanced life. After over 30 years of studying religious and spiritual teachings from the Old Testament to New Age spiritual leaders such as Neale Donald Walsh, Harry decided to journal some of his thoughts and ideas about those things and what he saw happening in the world. After some prodding from his wife, Harry sat down and wrote Rare Sense, initially more to help him understand what he was thinking and then to share it with the world in the hopes that he can help people on their own spiritual paths. Harry believes that for some reading this book will be a journey of self discovery, for others a confirmation of their own ideas and for others a journey into the unknown. For everyone, it is his hope that they can learn something about themselves, be inspired, obtain some practical advice as well as ponder their own place in the world.
