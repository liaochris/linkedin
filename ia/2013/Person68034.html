Jono Schaferkotter's Summary

Jono is a skilled director, animator, painter, and sand castle builder. He’s created three feature length films, in addition to an internationally acclaimed short, music videos, stunning corporate media, award-winning commercials, and much more. He's lead video teams across the US, Tokyo, Kenya, and the Heilongjiang Province of China. Recently he teamed up with long-time friend and producer, Vanessa Tomasello, and founded The Distillery - an award winning, creative agency that offers exciting media for Silicon Valley.
