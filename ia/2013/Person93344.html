    4sqLand is a website born from the insane minds of some foursquare addicts.

  • MappedInWorld

    • May 2012 to Present

    Having listened to many people talking about crisis and opportunity in the digital arena, we decided with Matteo Sarzana, Michele Sarzana and Eros Verderio to try to implement something that could help those who:
    - are looking for work in the digital environment;
    - are launching startups;
    - have funds to invest;
    - can offer a coworking space.

    that's why we created http://www.mappedinitaly.it/ a consumer generated map of digital companies in Italy.
    The project is not for profit, we want it to be the proof that if you want to do something you can do it.
    We wanted to start with Milan and then extend it, but we were forced to extend it to Italy from the start.

    The project has been so succesful that we extended it to the World.

    We donate it to the people.

    if you want to have your country listed, comment or report bugs you are welcome write to info@mappedinworld.com

    spread the word and we'll be grateful :-)
