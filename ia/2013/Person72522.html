Karyl Gilbertson's Summary

I have worked as a Designer in the printing, signage and web industries for more than 5 years. I have a thorough grasp of design principles, web standards, prepress requirements and production processes. I understand and relish the unique challenges present in designing for web and designing for print media. I have repeatedly delivered quality work to professional clients.

I received a Bachelor of Design degree in 2007, with a major in Character Design (Concept Art) and a minor in Commercial Illustration. I also received some formal Graphic Design and Advertising instruction during my studies.

I have been drawing and painting ever since I can remember. I love making art, and my goal is to bring my inspiration and creative spirit into all aspects of my working life!

Specialties

Commercial Illustration, Concept Art, Graphic Design, working under tight deadlines, setting up artwork for prepress, HTML, XHTML, CSS, setting up blog & CMS platforms.
