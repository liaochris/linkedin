Debra Hamilton's Summary

Senior learning and performance professional with end-to-end learning program and strategy expertise across a global, diverse clientele.

Highlights: Assess, plan, implement, and evaluate learning solutions necessary to execute on business objectives. Partner with key stakeholders to identify needs for leadership and professional development portfolios. Proven ability to collaborate across functional areas, negotiate and influence approaches and methodologies, assemble working groups, and manage projects and change. Highly skilled in design/development using the ADDIE model and managing pilot programs, large-scale roll outs, T3 materials and programs, learning transfer strategies, and curriculum updates.

Accomplishments include:

•Facilitated a U.S. and Global Library Advocacy skills training model that included 20 conferences and 3,150 participants, with 93% of participating libraries meeting their grant match. Facilitated a subsequent pilot program in Belgium that included 10 participating countries.

•Designed, facilitated, and supported a National Training Initiative for sales supervisors using the company’s standardized sales and coaching model; facilitated 21 workshops with 94% of supervisors achieving proficiency.

•Designed/facilitated a High-Potential Supervisory Leadership curriculum for an international manufacturer’s diverse population, implementing 360 leadership and other diagnostic assessments into the core modules.

•Created and deployed a highly customized 90-Day Leadership Onboarding Strategy that included a coaching model, performance management templates, peer coaching, and executive coaching reinforcement.

•Cultivated and enhanced a Corporate University model to deliver 40 hours of training per associate which included six tracks of learning, manager competencies for development planning, annual performance management processes, a Manager’s Playbook, and HR and executive briefings sessions to fortify the initiative.

Specialties

Certifications: Kirkpatrick Four Levels of Evaluation; Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI®); DiSC Profile; Emotional and Social Competency Inventory (ESCI 360); ASTD E-Learning Design Certificate; Master Trainer
