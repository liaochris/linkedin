An Hilven's Summary

IT Forensics and IT Security professional with a geeky edge.

Certified as EnCase Certified Examiner (EnCE) and Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator (CHFI), and planning to certify as GCFA and/or CCE some time.

Firm interest in digital forensics. Graduated as a Masters student in Digital Forensics, followed pretty much all EnCase courses, lots of selfstudy and some work experience.

Other experience includes the day-to-day configuration, maintenance, support and management of firewalls, intrusion detection systems and SSL VPN devices for customers with a need for (managed) security services. Further information security experience includes threat analysis based on alerts triggered by intrusion detection systems, and responding either through escalation or mitigation as required.
