Eric Nathey, MBA's Summary

Ever since I was a child I have always been fascinated by the impact a story can have on people, whether that story is fictitious or based on facts. As I have grown here at athenahealth I have realized that I am working to help write a real life story that could impact the healthcare environment for years to come. I am extremely excited to be a part of the ever changing story that is the healthcare industry.

Recently I have made my way into Business Development - Care Coordination here at athenahealth. I must say I am looking forward to helping create a better storyline for healthcare in the future. Here at athenahealth we discovered there was a major hole when it comes to coordination between referring organizations and receiving organizations. So, we developed athenaCoordinator, which creates a unified network between our client providers utilizing our EMR and the labs, imaging centers, specialty groups and hospitals that they are sending their patient orders to. When you become a partner through athenaCoordinator, your lab, imaging center or specialty practice simply becomes easier to do business with.

athenaCoordinator also allows for our receiving partners have a chance to grow their business amongst our sending providers and in turn, close the loop in the order system while providing the best patient care.

The story I am working to tell at this point is by far the most fascinating and impactful story I have been a part of. If you are looking to see some of the benefits of athenaCoordinator or are interested in how the story could effect you, please do not hesitate to reach out.
