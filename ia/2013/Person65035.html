João Pedro Rodrigues' Summary

User Experience Designer, Web Application Developer and a former Biologist.

I hold a Master's degree in Human Computer Interaction (MHCI) from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) and a degree in Microbiology and Genetics from University of Lisbon.

For a decade I designed and implemented web applications for clients in the telecom industry, public administration, television, advertising, law & finance and national airlines.

Being close to the end users, I was aware of the impact of usability and overall user experience on their work. That led me to take the MHCI degree from Carnegie Mellon University. Since then, I put my multidisciplinary knowledge in a user-centered design process to web and mobile software development.
