Nicolas Cornwall's Summary

Passionate about mixing in a creative way graphic design, usability and technologies to develop new experiences and tools.
After a long period of discovering design tools by myself, I decided to educate myself in one of the best design school of Barcelona, IDEP, to learn all the basis of graphic design and art direction, directly from the best designers and creatives of Spain and Europe.

After 4 years of struggling, I got hired as a web designer for a new and ambitious online newspaper project, FACTUAL.

I fought to get quickly involved in all the department of my interests, art direction, social networks, ad campaigns and succeeded in satisfying my company needs; I ended up being in charge of the design and development department.

I want this experience to be a first step before leading bigger projects and bigger teams, in Spain or anywhere in the world (my preferences are London, New York or South Asia).

Specialties

Web Design, Branding, Graphical Design, Creative online communication, Social Networks (management/consulting)
