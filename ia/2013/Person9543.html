Ariff Sidi's Summary

Ariff has 15 years experience creative problem solving in Internet and digital media. He has held leadership roles in engineering, product development, and strategy; has authored 11 patents; has an Emmy award for excellence in digital media; and has spoken at several major industry conferences including twice at ScreenDigest's PEVE.

Ariff led development of ABC.com's Full Episode Player, the groundbreaking, industry-first online television platform, where his patented innovations have defined industry best practice for online video monetization. He also led development of ABC TV On Demand, the first ABC-branded VOD product outside the US, and helped define the digital product strategy for Disney's kids channels across Europe.

Earlier experience includes leading development of web properties like Disneyland.com and DisneyCruiseLine.com, engineering roles at technology startups like Stamps.com spin-off Encryptix, stalwarts like Qualcomm, and NASA’s Jet Propulsion Lab.

Today, Ariff is Founder of Pairental, a technology start-up in the kids and family space.

Ariff has a BS in Computer Engineering from UC San Diego and an MBA from UCLA Anderson School of Management. He holds dual US and UK nationality.
