Scott Stiffey's Summary

☞ Who I Am...

I'm a patient centered doctor with a reputation for delivering the best care treatment of the traumatically injured, along with keen assessments and referrals to other specialists. I've accumulated extensive experience ranging from a team chiropractor for local colleges to treating victims of car crashes.

I've been one of the best in my field for nearly 11 years. I currently enjoy working with patients that have been traumatically injured, guiding them through proper treatment while utilizing all diagnostic imaging and other expert specialists such as orthopedic surgeons and neurologists.

I'm passionate about life, work and life outside of it. I'm a big believer in animal welfare, children, and healthy habits that lead to a long prosperous life.
