Doug Kern's Summary

I'm passionate about the intersection of the quantitative and creative, which is why I love Marketing. For the past 16 years, I've helped software and services companies simplify their communications, create buyer-focused content, scale lead generation programs and deepen customer connections.

My approach is built from a mix of scars, victories and lessons-learned from venture-backed startups and global enterprise software vendors.

For a few decades now, I've also had the privilege of helping youth sailors get a spark for the sport and participating in advisory roles for the Austin Yacht Club.

Specialties

Turnarounds, SaaS and cloud services, product launches, product marketing, persona development, media relations, analyst relations, thought leadership content, videos, webinars, contests, social programs, M&A communication programs.
