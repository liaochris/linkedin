Marisa Hoffenberg, CFA's Summary

Owner and MD of Obeo - a digital strategy agency – focused exclusively on the asset management industry.

We work with fund managers, dealer groups and financial advisers to use digital marketing to:
- engage self-managed super fund trustees
- reposition following Future of Financial Advice Reforms
- strengthen institutional and wholesale relationships

We work with Super Funds to use digital marketing to:
- increase engagement with members and financial advisers
- effectively manage employer relationships

We work with our clients to understand the role digital does and can play within their organisation and put together a strategic and integrated plan to achieve their objectives. We then work with them to implement the plan and monitor, measure and continuously improve on its success.

Our team includes senior people from both asset management and digital marketing - we understand industry dynamics, technical terms and compliance requirements.

As we don't do any production, our strategies are completely based on consumer insights and client needs, and span the full range of digital opportunities from SEM and Social media, through to websites, email and mobile.

Specialties: Digital Marketing for the Asset Management Industry

  • Cut through to Financial Advisors – Provide Utility

    • Obeo
    • November 6, 2012
    Authors: Marisa Hoffenberg, CFA

    The vast majority of Fund Manager communication to Financial Advisors is about you and your products. By focussing on customer needs and providing utility, Fund Managers are much more likely to achieve cut through in this crowded space.

    Putnam provides utility to Advisors through its FundVisualizer Tool. A free web based tool that enables advisors to create visual comparisons of more than 10,000 mutual funds across multiple criteria. Brian Kelley, director of Putnam’s sales and marketing, positioned the tool, and its inclusion of the entire universe of mutual funds, as part of Putnam’s “client first”, attitude.

    Commonwealth Bank provides utility to consumers through its Property Guide App. The app provides real time pricing and purchase price history for house hunters and a direct link to get a quote for a home loan. The app received 85,000+ downloads in 10 weeks, more than 250,000 Sessions & more than 500,000 property searches, averaging more than 70 calls per week to the CommBank home loans number.

    Fund Managers can also provide utility in much more simplistic forms such as ‘tech tips’ or educational material on your website.

    By focusing on customer utility, rather than pushing a product, Fund Managers can significantly increase Financial Advisor engagement.

  • Aqua II – To Benefit, You Need to Adapt Your Strategy

    • Obeo
    • November 20, 2012
    Authors: Marisa Hoffenberg, CFA

    One of the core advantages of Aqua II is that it opens up a direct channel to Self-Managed Super Fund (SMSF) Investors. Fund Managers need to adapt their marketing strategy by focussing on the end investor – a mass market approach. The high cost and ineffectiveness of traditional media (TV, bill boards, magazine, banner ads, ect..) have, in the past, made the end investor inaccessible to all but the largest Fund Managers.
    The SMSF market can be both efficiently and cost effectively reached through digital marketing.

    The following list of questions should help you define your strategy to access SMSF investors using online channels.

    SMSF Market

    How can the SMSF market be segmented
    What are SMSF investors’ online usage habits and behaviours?
    Digital Content

    What type of content would each segment find valuable?
    What format should be used?
    What tone should be used?
    What frequency?
    How can it be created and using what sources?
    Digital Channels (email, website, social media)

    What digital channels should you be active in?
    Should you use Facebook, twitter, LinkedIn or YouTube?
    What channels should be used for which audience segment?
    What content should be delivered through which channel and when?
    Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) /Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

    What are the target market’s key search terms?
    How should your content be optimised for search?
    Should you buy search – what should your strategy be?
    Mobile

    Should you have a mobile site?
    Should you develop an app?
    Technology

    What systems should the web environment integrate with – finance, CRM..?
    What should the key functionality be?
    Measure and Monitor

    What are the roles, objectives and KPI’s for each digital channel?
    How and where should data be held and what fields should be available?
    How can you/should you measure your ROI?
    Having trouble answering any of the above questions/ Would you be interested in researching the online behaviour of SMSF investors?
