Boris Portman's Summary

Boris Portman brings over fifteen years experience in growing web and mobile software companies from concept to multi-million dollar revenue generating market leaders. Boris' passion for entrepreneurship starts with building great teams delivering great user experience software, often raising smart money and leading to successful company exits.

His journey includes a variety of CXO and founder roles, as well as mentoring and leading user groups on related topics internationally.

Boris earned his Master's degree in Software Engineering and Business from Carnegie Mellon’s Software Engineering Institute (SEI) and Tepper School of Business, where he co-founded the Carnegie Mellon University Entrepreneurial Association. Boris also earned his Bachelor's degree in computer science, math, and business from Carnegie Mellon University.

Specialties

Mobile app development, e-Commerce, Hispanic and LATAM markets, social networking, healthcare, workflow, content management, human resources, recruiting, off-shore software development.
