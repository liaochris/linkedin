Matt Laurence's Summary

As a widely-experienced, multi-faceted Sr. User Experience designer, my aim is to provide the very best creative and technical user-centered designs to accomplish my clients' goals; to make interactive experiences a joy for users in the face of an ever-changing media landscape; to find a group of colleagues with whom I look forward to pushing some boundaries every day; to do good work for good causes; and to play as much music as possible.

I can be your guide on the road from WTF? to FTW!

Specialties: User advocacy; on- and off-line design and art direction; IA and usability; interface and web design; diplomacy and negotiation; video media production, post-production and motion graphics; diplomacy, humor and whimsy; management of freelancers and designers; project strategy; client interaction; vendor and contractor-wrangling; significant experience with all the usual tools, and a few unusual ones
