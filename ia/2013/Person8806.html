Anthony Pingicer's Summary

Anthony is currently the Lead Art Director for a nationally-published weekly real estate trade magazine. He is the point person within the company for everything art-related, including the overall layout of the magazine, client ad materials, trade-show graphics, and self-promotional materials. With over 5 years experience within an agency setting, and over 5 years within the publishing/real estate industry, his wide range of knowledge is adaptable to any industry/setting.

Most notably, in 2007, he was the Creative Director with a start-up publication entitled "Living Little Magazine." This publication was featured on a May 2007 episode of the TLC show "Little People, Big World."

No client is too big or too small, having experience working with great companies such as Nextel, Time Warner Cable, Quick Chek Food Stores, sanofi-aventis, and Stryker, as well as countless prominent commercial real estate firms.

He is always on the lookout for new ideas and new opportunities in this ever-changing creative climate.
