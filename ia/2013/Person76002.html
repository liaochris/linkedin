Kiriaki Papadopoulou Samuelsen's Summary

Government authorised translator between norwegian and greek, both ways. Interpretor between norwegian and greek. Have translated 4 general fiction books and 1 novel from norwegian into greek and some fiction texts from greek into norwegian. Experience as a librarian and as a teacher in english, greek, and norwegian as a second language, and as a teacher in intercultural communication.

Specialties

Translation of legal and official documents, general fiction and fiction. Bibliographic work. Intercultural communication. Second language teaching. Experiential learning and co-operative learning. E-learning. Adult education.

Government authorized translator

Gresk oversetter og tolk, Norway

I have worked as an independent translator on a free-lance basis since 2000. I am translating between norwegian and greek (both ways) and from english into greek. Since 2004 I have also worked as a free-lance interpreter between norwegian and greek. I am registered at the National Register of Interpreters in Norway.
I have experience on legal, official, medical and technical texts, general fiction and fiction. I have translated 4 general fiction books and 1 novel from norwegian into greek.
