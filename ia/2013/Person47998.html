George Ruge's Summary

George received his undergraduate degree and paralegal certificate from Baruch College in New York. He was a Case Manager at Proskauer Rose in NYC for 14 Years. He was then a Paralegal Manager at Akin Gump in NYC for 7 Years. George was the Director Of Operations for the NYC office of Doerner & Goldberg Court Reporting for 3 years. He is the founding partner of PARADOCS, Inc. & Director of Business Development at CaseWorks, Inc.
