Amanda Rae Busch's Summary

While on the 2004 founding staff of Berkshire Living, the award-winning arts and lifestyle magazine serving the border region of MA, CT, VT, and NY, and as a writer and editor for a decade, I have developed the interview, research, and storytelling skills to craft compelling feature articles, columns, and reviews on a variety of subjects, as well as the managerial confidence to shepherd editorial packages from inception to layout to final proofing.

I also craft advertising copy, press materials, and other forms of communication in print and online for clients of all stripes.

Next up: writing a book about my whirlwind seven-month solo road trip across America in 2011/2012, based on anecdotes I shared in real time on my travel blog, AmandaRaeWasHere.com.
