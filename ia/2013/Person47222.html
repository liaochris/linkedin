Gary Spirer's Experience

Founder and CEO/Entrepreneur

QuestionMine.com

January 2012 – Present (1 year 9 months) Austin, Texas Area

Questionmine™ video surveys enable you to discover what your prospects and customers want. Add surveys and questionnaires to video content to capture real-time research results and analytics; generate sales-ready leads; capture demographics and other important data from customer prospects.

Questionmine enables marketing staff to easily add interactive surveys to video content. Surveys layered into video content enables companies to capture customer information in a engaging and entertaining way, delivering the presentation of video material simultaneous to the capture of viewer survey data. By layering surveys into video information, those viewing the video and interacting with it guide the content they receive (video content can be directed to switch based on viewer responses). Visitor survey data is captured and reported in real-time, giving the company valuable insight into what their customers, partners, prospects want and desire, while delivering upon those desires by re-directing the video based on each user’s responses. Never before has video content been empowered with this rich survey layer.

Questionmine’s solutions can be deployed via desktop or mobile browsers. Questionmine surveys are used to advertise, train, educate, survey, and/or collect qualified leads in an environment far more engaging than flat text-based surveys. Companies can instantly respond to their prospects with content and offers that are relevant to them based on their survey responses. The user can take advantage of interactive webinar system that plays previously recorded webinars any time someone wants to watch them. A simple, easy to use administrative dashboard overlays the questions and reports survey metrics and analytics. Questionmine is integrated with multiple CRM systems and the IBM Connections platform
