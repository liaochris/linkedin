Howard Wolf-Rodda's Summary

I am an attorney based in the Washington, DC metropolitan area. My law practice focuses on representing both businesses and individuals in their dealings with the government in addition to their relationships with others in the private sector. For nearly 20 years, I have represented clients ranging from some of the largest federal contractors to small businesses.

Strategy is at the center of my work. Whether I am counseling clients regarding their day-to-day affairs or representing them in disputes, my clients' ultimate goals drive my approach to the task at hand. Negotiating the nuances of a contract, analyzing the minutiae of government regulations, or battling one's adversaries should never be done without first finding the best path to achieving these goals - after all is said and done, it's the big picture that counts.
