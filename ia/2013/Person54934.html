Ilise Benun's Summary

If you are serious about growing your creative business and need practical guidance, accountability and a sounding board, I can help.

I am the founder of Marketing Mentor, a business coaching program for creative business owners, solopreneurs and independent professionals.

I offer both one-on-one and group coaching, in person, online and on the phone. Topics covered include:
- Proposal Review, Critique and Rewrite
- How to Position Your Services
- How to Choose a Target Market

Evidence proves that if you integrate marketing into your day-to-day process, you can get the clients you want, instead of being at the mercy of whoever comes along.

I’ll help you set realistic goals and then reach them, keeping you on track and accountable for it.

I'll show you how to choose a viable target market and position yourself or your firm as the expert in that market. Then we'll choose from all the possible marketing tools you could use, which ones are the most effective (and least expensive) to reach your market.

With your Marketing Mentor by your side, you’ll get the work you want to grow your business.

Specialties

Specialize in creating marketing plans and reviewing proposals for creative professionals (designers, writers, illustrators, video producers, photographers) as well as professional service providers (lawyers, consultants, speaking coaches, personal trainers, computer experts and even other marketing consultants) -- essentially anyone offering a B2B service.
