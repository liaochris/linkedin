Andrew Buys' Summary

Barrington Hall is a professional luxury yacht charter broker assisting elite clients in their search for their ultimate dream vacation aboard catamarans, power boats and super yachts..

What separates Barrington Hall from the rest of the world's top charter brokers?
The answer is style, integrity, commitment with a vast knowledge of luxurious yacht charters in the Bahamas, Caribbean and the Mediterranean.

Choose from a fleet of private yachts from power boats to super yachts to catamaran sailing to super mega yachts with a helicopter on board. You have your choice of exotic destinations including the Bahamas, Caribbean, Mediterranean and Greek Islands on the luxury yachts of the world.

Andrew Buys' Experience

President & CEO

Barrington Hall Yacht Charters

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry

January 1995 – Present (19 years)

Gaile, my wife and I captained our own luxury catamaran sailboat on charter in the British Virgin Islands for over five years. We were the first catamaran to offer charters in the BVI, starting in 1990 - after sailing across the Atlantic from our native South Africa.

I annually attend the major yacht broker shows and over the years has met the crews, captains and by boarding the yachts gives him first hand knowledge of the yacht, the level of quality and service as well as the abilities and dispositions of the staff.

We are in the service business by providing our clients and potential clients with in-depth and personal knowledge of yacht charters and charter destinations.

With this perspective we are able to offer unique insights and experiences with clients to assist them in selecting the best vessels and destinations. For additional information, kindly contact us toll free on 800-478-2029 or outside the USA on 954-720-0475.
