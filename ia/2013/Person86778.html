Mark Layton's Summary

Recognized as a highly successful executive leader and pioneer in the web commerce technology and services arena, Mr. Layton has utilized his entrepreneurial spirit, technology and broad based financial skills along with years of high integrity and motivational management experience in his successful career as a serial entrepreneur and C level executive at several highly successful companies.

In his current role as COO of VideoPlus, Mr. Layton and his team are developing revolutionary strategic solutions, tools and products to fuel high growth to over 50 key clients in the Direct Selling Industry.

Layton has lead large teams of 1000's of professionals in successfully deploying world class end to end eCommerce and Omnichannel solutions on leading eCommerce platforms including Demandware, Oracle ATG and IBM Websphere for many of the world's most prestigious brands including; Starbucks, Procter & Gamble, Fifth & Pacific, Diageo, Lego, & L'Oreal.

Mr. Layton is a successful entrepreneur, board member, author, speaker, instructor and mentor and has been awarded numerous industry awards and recognitions over his highly successful business career.
