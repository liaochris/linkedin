Naseem Khan's Summary

An expert in the process of casting, turning, spinning and finishing process of metals and alloys.

Learnt Product Design and presentation, first hand from the masters of the industry. Worked with Product Design Teams of major Home Decor chains around the world, such as Global Views, Pier 1, Bombay Company, Hallmark Cards, to name a few, assisted them with the development of complete lines, co-ordinating colors, finishes and entire themes.

Launched LoveUrns® - a name to represent the finest Cremation Urns and related articles, in 2011.

LoveUrns® are manufactured in self owned factories in India and Europe.

Co-ordinating a joint movement with Distributors around the globe to make the brand synonymous with High Quality Cremation Urns. Consistently keeping a control over the feel, touch, look, design, packaging and total customer experience of brand LoveUrns®

Specialties

Product Design, Planning about technical aspects of production, Finishes on Brass, other Alloys. Working with many materials and producing beautiful Decorative or Utility Articles.

Management, International Co-ordination among customers, distributors ... Working as a team globally.
