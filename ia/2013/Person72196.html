Karen Rice King's Experience

Owner

Ruffled Feathers, LLc

April 1991 – Present (22 years 7 months)

I personally supervise all operations of my company. On an individual level, I see clients one-on-one for reasons such as illness, depression, stress, accidents and injuries. I also run a paid Apprenticeship Program in Earth Acupuncture Dowsing and teach a two year residential program in Core Shamanism as one of Sandra Ingerman's recognized teachers. This program meets 5 days, twice a year for two years up in the gorgeous Rocky Mountains just a short distance from the front range metro area. I help businesses and homes with mitigating stresses created by man-made challenges to the energetic, neurological system such as cell phone towers and electrical surging.
We also mitigate underground water and high mineral content in the soil, through Earth Acupuncture Dowsing installations.

Employment Consultant

Mountain States Personnel

1979 – 1984 (5 years)

I helped employers in Colorado, who did not know how to conduct a simple JOB INTERVIEW meet with folks looking for work, who I coached as well, get "married", so to speak.

Telephone Installer

Mountain Bell Communications

1975 – 1978 (3 years)

When first starting, I was out in the field in a territory spanning from 6th Avenue in Denver all the way to south of Littleton. I encountered new construction, commercial locations as well as residential homes. After an injury, I moved indoors to field repair calls and dispatch repair folks. That is when I wrote a "jingle" which won an advertising award and was played on TV and radio. Targeted for management, I left the company to publish my own book of poetry and work with Poets in the Denver Public Schools. I moved first to Eldora to write in a miner's cabin. Then, moved to Boulder in 1975, when the winter hit. I was employed by the company that came out with Rocky Mtn Skin Trip, Lip Trip, etc. They also supported my fundraising for my first poetry book which won national awards.
