David Oldland's Summary

David runs Oldland Consulting Ltd & R&D Tax Experts Ltd. These management consultancies specialise in making R&D Tax Credit claims (£9.6m of claims for 148 companies filed to date) across a wide spectrum of business sectors on a no win no fee basis.

The Research and Development Tax Credit scheme is designed to reward innovating businesses by enhancing qualifying R&D costs to effectively reduce the company's taxable profit. A non profit making SME can also enjoy cash via a Payable R&D Tax Credit.

The average first R&D tax credit claim for a client is £39,395 ranging between £5k and £300k.

David is a member of The Research and Development Society.

David has an extensive knowledge of the UK Research & Development Tax Credit scheme. The R&D Tax scheme rewards innovating businesses.

David Oldland has almost 30 years experience in senior positions. His roles have included Managing Director, Business Development Director & Finance Director. His employers have included the Rank Organisation, ILG and the Thomson Travel Group (aka TUI).

David describes himself: A successful and energetic Senior Executive. Highly customer focused, with proven negotiating skills, a good track record, a good team leader and team player. Champion’s change, a strategic thinker and an innovator. Is able to make and implement difficult decisions. An achiever.

David has: strong people skills, strong communication skills. He motivates, he has an habit of playing devil's advocate - ensuring that a subject is viewed from all angles.

Specialties: A highly successful Research & Development Tax Credit specialist. 250 R&D Tax Credit claims completed totalling £9,565,300 these average £38,261 per claim.
