A. Benno Schmidt's Summary

US Citizen
Clearance: DoD Secret (Inactive)

Benno Schmidt brings more than 18 years of design to projects in the healthcare sector. He has worked on user experience for healthcare projects in the insurance industry, the Federal Government, and the medical association sector. These projects have given him proficiency in designing user experiences for clinician support applications, patient records, insurance claims, and electronic fund transfers throughout their lifecycles.

Benno has also gained knowledge of the requirements specific to healthcare application design, such as the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), 5010 standards, and electronic health records (EHR) meaningful use.

Before working in healthcare, Benno designed user experiences for web applications in the defense, financial services, and automotive industries. He holds a BA in Political Science and English from Boston University, as well as a BA in Fine Arts, Design from The George Washington University.

Specialties: • User workflow and user task analysis
• Patient-centered participatory design
• Heuristic and formative usability testing
• XD requirements and specification documentation
• Standards compliant web application design
• Full life-cycle XD development - rapid prototyping, launch, maintenance, and retirement
• Cross-department XD team formation and management
