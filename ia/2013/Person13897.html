Bev Weinstein's Summary

Beverly Weinstein is the founder and president of Markham Media Executive Search. For over 15 years, Markham Media has been a leader in placing stellar executive talent in sales, research, marketing, business development and operations. Her client base ranges from top tier entertainment and media companies to early stage digital start-ups.

Beverly takes personal ownership of the search process from beginning to end. She has built enduring relationships with clients and candidates. Her extensive database, personal network and insider market intelligence lead to placements that are right, not just right for now. This is especially important in the shifting media and entertainment landscape.

Beverly’s expertise has been earned over a three-decade tenure in the industry.
Previously, she held senior sales and marketing roles at some of the top global media companies. Beverly was the first account executive hired to sell MTV and later became the channel’s first Sales Director. She served as the Senior Vice President of Media Services at Orion Pictures, working on back-to-back Academy Award winners Dances With Wolves and Silence of the Lambs. Beverly also worked in account executive roles at CBS, Woman’s Day and Redbook earlier in her career. She co-founded a consulting practice that was retained to do several assignments for PBS and PBS stations. She and her partner were responsible for the ad sales launch of the WB.

Beverly wrote a regular career advice column for Media Post “Dear Bev,” that focused on career topics. She also lectures on career topics and most recently spoke at a WimLink Digital Strategy seminar. Beverly has been quoted in Parade Magazine and the Wall Street Journal.   

Specialties: Executive Search, Cable TV, Advanced Television, Radio, Digital, Mobile, Publishing, Internet, Broadcast, Marketing, Ad Sales, Development & Strategy, Research
