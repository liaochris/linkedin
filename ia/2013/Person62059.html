Jenn Steele's Summary

Jenn Steele is a graduate of MIT and holds an MBA from Simmons College. Before moving into marketing and consulting in order to broaden her experience, Jenn built a career in the legal industry running IT departments for several mid-size Boston and regional law firms. During her legal career, she was very involved with the International Legal Technology Association, and was the Social Media Coordinator at their 2009 conference.

Jenn was a Premium Support Inbound Marketing Consultant at HubSpot for some time before moving into a new role as a Consulting Team Manager. As manager, she led two key teams to consistently outperform quality objectives, and defined process and operational goals for the entire consulting team. As Product Marketing Manager at Amazon, she developed and executing product marketing strategy for Amazon Simple Email Service, and then moved over to lead Amazon's efforts across the Yahoo! Bing Network. Her most recent move is to Head of Growth at RecruitLoop, a startup that is disrupting the recruitment industry.

Jenn has always been fascinated by leadership and personal interactions, and blogs about it on her Leading Geeks blog.

Specialties: IT, operations, leadership, project management, IT systems, Microsoft applications, budget management, e-billing, eDiscovery, litigation support, communications, marketing, SEM, SEO, product marketing.
