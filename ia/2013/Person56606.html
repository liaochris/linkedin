Jackson Bazley's Summary

Jackson holds a Master of Business Administration from Florida State University and a bachelor's degree in Accounting and Marketing from Florida Southern College.. He is a Certified Public Accountant in Florida and New York, and a Certified Information Technology Professional.

Jackson is an Executive Director in Ernst & Young’s Advisory Services, Media & Entertainment practice. Within the Media Research Assurance Services specialty practice, he has been serving the media measurement industry since 1994. He has served a number of clients across the media and entertainment industries, including those specializing in online and interactive advertising delivery, online/internet audience research, advertising verification services, radio audience research, print and multi-media audience research, and television audience research.

Jackson can be reached at Jackson*Bazley#ey*com, or at 425 3650 in Area Code 813.

Specialties

Jackson's primary focus since 1999 has been in emerging media measurement technologies and online media measurement; including online and interactive advertising delivery and measurement, and online audience research. He has worked extensively on behalf of the Media Rating Council (MRC) and is currently a member of several Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) committees.
