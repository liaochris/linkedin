Charles Badenach CFP Financial Planner's Summary

As an experienced financial adviser Charles' key objective is to assist successful people throughout Australia make smart financial decisions which in turn allow them to achieve their life goals and dreams.

Over the last 12 years Charles has spoken at numerous national conferences, schools, universities, public forums and businesses on financial markets. During this time Charles has also written a self help financial book titled 'Old Head on Young Shoulders" which made the National Best Seller List.

Charles has also written many articles for both industry publications and the mainstream media. In addition Charles has appeared regularly on radio, television and the social media forums (see www.youtube.com/charlesbadenach) discussing current financial issues.

CharIes has also won a number of National awards for his work in financial services including -

-Money Management Australian Financial Planner of the Year (2011)

-Financial Planning Association of Australia Value of Advice National Award in the Pre Retirement Category (2010)

- Financial Standard Innovation, Leadership and Excellence in Social Media for Financial Services in Australia Award (2012)

-Future2 Community Service National Best Practice Award (2011) for his involvement in improving financial literacy amongst the socially disadvantaged.

Charles was also the State Runner Up in the FPA Certified Financial Planner® Professional Best Practice Award (2012).

Over the last 15 years Charles has also held numerous external board or advisory board positions.

Specialties: Charles provides tailored financial planning solutions on a fee for service basis to a diverse range of busy professionals, self funded retirees and charitable foundations.

Charles is also a part time social media consultant and well known national speaker.

Charles Badenach CFP Financial Planner's Experience

Certified Financial Planner and Principal

Shadforth Financial Group Ltd

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Financial Services industry

December 2000 – Present (12 years 8 months)

Shadforth Financial Group Limited is a national financial advisory firm which now forms part of the ASX listed Shadforth Financial Group (SFW).

The company is one of the few significant financial advisory groups in Australia that is not owned or controlled by a product provider or large institution. This enables us to offer clients highly-personalised advice, and financial products and services that are tailored to each client's unique set of circumstances, and objectives.

The group has over 400 employees, 15,000 clients and 18 offices located across Australia. The group manages in excess of $10 billion in total client funds. The group is united in its commitment to providing clients with the highest standards of ethics and integrity, quality financial advice and solutions.
