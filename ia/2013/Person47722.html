Georg Fasching's Summary

In his professional mission to date, turning mobile devices into Personal Commerce Assistants, Georg has collected over 13 years of competence in mobile payments, both in the mobile telecommunications and the payments sectors.

Georg now works with start-ups, industry forums such as Mobey Forum, and big players, widening the approach and driving the mission forward through his consultancy ModPay.Co.

Previously Georg was part of Luup’s Executive Team. As VP Products he headed up the product and UI teams, looking after the company’s three convergent product lines covering corporate, remittances, and retail. Georg also worked on strategy & strategic partnerships, and was a company spokesperson.

Prior to joining Luup, Georg was a Senior Product Manager at MasterCard Worldwide. Georg led the product development of the Mobile MoneySend Service in the United States. Georg also created and ran MasterCard’s first Mobile Partner Programme, recruiting over 100 partners.

Before, Georg was Manager, Global Projects at net-mobile UK Ltd., delivering global brand’s mobile marketing campaigns for M&C Saatchi Mobile, reaching 1.5+ million users, and consulting tier one clients on mobile commerce.

Georg also held positions with T-Mobile International’s Mobile Payment Solutions business unit. His achievements include the acquisition of a banking license, enabling of mobile payments for prepaid subscribers, and the rollout of a mobile/digital wallet solution in several European countries.

Specialties: Product & Solution Vision, Strategy & Delivery in: Mobile Money Transfer, P2P Payments, Remittances, Mobile Banking, Mobile Payment, Internet Banking, Electronic Payments, Mobile Commerce (M-Commerce), Electronic Commerce (E-Commerce/eCommerce), Branchless Banking, Cashless Delivery, Cashless Disbursements, Remote Payments, Proximity (NFC) Payments

Georg Fasching's Patents

  • METHODS AND APPARATUS FOR PROVIDING CENTRALIZED WEB SERVICES FOR FUNDS TRANSFER SYSTEM

    • United States Patent US 2010/0325021 A1
    • Issued June 22, 2009
    Inventors: Georg Fasching

    A method of operating a centralized web services computer system includes providing a standard interface for financial institutions (FIs) and for funds transfer service providers that participate in a remittance system. The centralized web services computer system may also provide services such as integrating its operations into online banking or customer service representative web environments provided by the FIs. The centralized web services computer system may also provide services related to routing messages, looking up territory-related information, checking user information against blacklists, issuing instructions to FIs to debit or credit user accounts, and/or generating reports.
