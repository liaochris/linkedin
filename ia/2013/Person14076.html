Bianka McChristion-Milton's Summary

Bianka McChristion-Milton has worked in numerous high pace settings where expectations and priorities were high with multi-million dollar implementations of Epic Applications. She focuses on systems selection, project management, IT configuration work including but not limited to business process documentation, evaluation and redesign. Another major focus is configuration, testing, and implementation support of hospital information systems (HIS) for the healthcare provider industry. Bianka versatility has allotted her experience managing Multi-Billion dollar Nuclear Major Projects including the overall project controls and funding for large mergers and account acquisitions.

Her Healthcare experience include Epic Ancillary (Outpatient) and IP with a emphasis on the Radiant and Cupid aspect of the Epic software which she holds certification from Epic on. She has been a part of other aspects of Epic including CPOM, Ambulatory, Security, EMR, Chart, OpTime, ClinDoc, Meaningful Use, etc..

Mrs. Milton has experience working with numerous EPIC applications from the design, build, validation, testing, implementation, programming, security, etc.. standpoint (Radiant application the most closely) serving as a Project Lead, Senior Application Coordinator, package implementation project team member and requirements analyst. This has assisted in her increase in knowledge of how the applications can and can not work together and the best practices when implementing the applications from a AC standpoint as well as the repercussions from a PM standpoint

Epic Radiant, Epic Cupid, Epic CPOE, Epic Ambulatory, IDX, GE, Stentor PACS, AgFa PACS, Commissure, STAR registration, Cerner Millennium (PowerChart, FirstNet)

Specialties: Certified in Epic Radiant (NVT 2012) and Epic Cupid (NVT 2012) Well versed in Project Management, design, build, implement, testing, script writer, decision maker, non profits, microsoft office suite, Cerner Millennium, etc..
