Anita Wardlaw, Director's Summary

Anita is a sought after speaker and singer at conferences across the U.S. and in other countries.

Learn more about Anita at www.wordspin.net

Corporations and ministries describe Anita as upbeat, bright, adept at working with people of diverse backgrounds, good with technology, and gifted at finding creative solutions.

According to multiple awards, Anita is depicted as a personable presenter and expert writer of award-winning copy for TV, newspapers, magazines, websites, collateral material, advertising, public relations, curriculum, books, scripts, and speeches.

As a communications specialist, Anita has served diverse entities, such as: Hoffman Communications, WOI-TV, Prudential Insurance, The St. Paul, AT&T, Maple Dental, Ace Band Shop, Little Red Hen Montessori, Edina Historical Society, Iowa State University, Christian Outreach International, Mercy Medical Center, Ernst & Young, Casa de Mana, Lowthian College, & The Learning Company.

Anita taught math & wellness at Lowthian Business College, energy conservation at Iowa State University, Native American & minority group history and interpersonal communications at Golden Valley Lutheran College, and theatre, children's book writing & illustrating, and clothing design at Edina Art Center. Anita also teaches Bible topics and God's healing message of love worldwide.

Anita excels in research, writing & editing, teaching, ministry, and singing as a lyric soprano soloist. Anita has created curriculum for math, ministry, history, theatre, wellness, writing, and aerobics classes for K-12 and college students. Anita has worked as talent for AT&T, American Girl Dolls, Rice Krispies, Crest, & other entities. Anita was nicknamed "computer" in school for achievements ranging from honors chemistry to honors literature.

See Anita's TV show at northmetrotv.com, channel 14, A Fresh New Day, or on Youtube as A Fresh New Day with Anita TV. For example, view: www.youtube.com/watch?v=hFqXBCte6Gc&feature=related
