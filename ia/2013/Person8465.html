Annie Almasi Gerlach's Summary

I am an accomplished marketing communications professional with more than 15 years of experience helping start ups to Fortune 500 companies build their brands. I am the owner and founder of Almasi Media, a full-service marketing communications and public relations firm. Almasi Media serves businesses in many different industries, including accounting, business consulting, education, engineering, manufacturing, real estate, recruiting, retail and travel. Some of my clients include: Arte, Carnivale Travel, Triton-Tek, HoodiePet, Learning Resources, Nativity Jesuit Middle School, Next Step Goals (Julie O'Keeffe), Northpoint Horizons, CORE Consulting, Pho-Tronics, Patina Solutions, Wisconsin Investment Realty, and Dryhootch.

Prior to starting Almasi Media, I served as a marketing communications manager at Assurant Health where I was responsible for marketing strategy development, copywriting, editing and design direction for the company's major product lines.

I earned a bachelor's degree in journalism and a master's degree in communications from Marquette University.

Specialties: Strategic marketing communications, brand building, public relations, media relations, social media, copywriting, case studies, white papers, brochures, e-newsletters, direct mail, e-blasts, websites, press releases
