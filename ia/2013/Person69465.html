Joven Demonteverde's Summary

Joven Demonteverde is the Director of JMCorp, an accounting and corporate advisory company specialising in outsourced accounting and virtual CFO services. Joven is an integral resource providing high level corporate expertise and top tier consulting practices to SME's and start-ups.

It is this understanding that allows ambitious companies to capitalise on their resources by focusing on their unique strengths through their growth phase. With over 6 years’ experience as an accountant in both public practice and commercial industry, Joven’s expertise with financial and management accounting has led to significant improvements in business support processes and financial reporting for a variety of companies.

Joven has worked with several start-ups in establishing strong reporting frameworks and providing them with access to high-level advice. Joven’s experience in business accounting extends over several industries, entity structures, and business types.

Joven has evolved the entire accounting system of an international mobile media advertising agency, resulting in a robust and streamlined reporting function accessible 24/7 globally and meeting the strictest compliance standards.

Joven has been involved in the early stage commercialisation of a high growth internet company, assisting in the application to secure a government grant from Commercialisation Australia, and preparing the company for a subsequent capital raise.

Joven is committed to ongoing education and industry participation with current membership to the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Australia (ICAA), and a Bachelor of Business degree from the University of Technology, Sydney.

Joven's corporate life is underpinned by his vision that “Effective Financial Management from Start-Up to Exit is essential for modern organizations and their leaders to succeed."

Specialties

Corporate Advisory, Capital Raising, Exit Strategies, De-risking, Management Accounting, Financial Accounting, Bookkeeping, Accounts Clean-up and Systemisation.
