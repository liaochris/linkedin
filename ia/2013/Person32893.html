DAWN Elyzabeth's Summary

Dawn Elyzabeth is a nonverbal communication speaker and coach, specializing in reading and interpreting body language. Dawn is continuing to empower people - enhance their communication skills ultimately increasing their success and happiness.

• 7% of human communication is the words that are spoken
• 38% of human communication is paralinguistic (the way that the words are said)
• 55% of human communication is nonverbal
Dawn will help you master the 55% of human communication that is usually taken for granted, forgot, or misunderstood.

As a master trainer, Dawn Elyzabeth brings a unique perspective to this magnificent art form with a theatre and dance background. Dawn became a professional performer at age 15 and and started choreographing and directing by the age of 18.

Dawn Elyzabeth is 200% dedicated to helping entrepreneurs and corporate leaders that are ready to create success from the inside out. Whether in front of a packed house or assisting one of her clients, Dawn has an exceptional talent for creating a connection with her audience that changes their lives forever. Dawn teaches from real world experience and observations that everyone can relate to.

With over 10 years invested in researching and practicing the most powerful communication techniques in business sales and leadership, Dawn is inspiring individuals and companies to reach greater heights in their business.
