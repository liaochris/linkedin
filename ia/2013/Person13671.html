Beth Blair's Summary

Beth Blair's airline experience has made her a natural travel expert, particularly on the topic of passenger safety and comfort in-flight. As a writer Beth's articles have been featured in Aviation Security International, AirTran Airways GO, FoxNews.com, CBSMinnesota.com, State Farm’s Good Neighbor, Hybrid Mom, Draft, Arizona Star, Arizona Bride, Oro Valley/Marana, Tucson Bridal, Association News, Christian Science Monitor, Central Penn Parent, Long Island Mothers Journal, SheKnows.com, BabyZone.com. Her strong online presence includes being a PBS SproutforParents.com travel expert and penning the monthly USA Tourist News Magazine for USATourist.com. Her work has also appeared in numerous anthologies, including the Chicken Soup for the Soul series.
Media: Beth enjoys sharing her insider’s perspective and knowledge on all aspects of travel. She is frequently quoted for her travel expertise in the media, including MSNBC.com, Travel and Leisure, Chicago Tribune, FoxNews.com, RealSimple.com and ABC-15 Sonoran Living Live in Phoenix, Arizona. In August 2010 she was featured in Woman’s Day magazine.

Safety Advocate: Travel safety issues are always on the forefront of Beth’s mind. She is a member of Safe Seats for Every Traveler (SSEAT), a committee created in support of educating the public about the hazards of airplane lap children.

Blogger: Beth co-owns TheVacationGals.com where she writes about her travels and offers valuable insight and tips for hitting the road and skies. The gals also recently launched TheSpaGals.com, a luscious spa-inspired website.

Speaker: 2010 I_Blog Conference, Minneapolis-St. Paul Social Media Breakfast 31, PRSA 2012 Travel & Tourism Conference

Affiliations: Beth has been selected as first place winner in the 2010 Writer’s Market “Freelance Success Stories” Contest and is a member of SATW (Society of America's Travel Writers) and Toastmasters, CC.
