Jamie Skella's Summary

I've dedicated 15 years to the creation of digital products and services, having worked on design strategy and product direction for brands such as Telstra, AMD, Western Digital, TAB and Tattersall's.

I believe that great product design is the result of combining an intimate understanding of a business and their customers, the pursuit of simplicity and seizing technological opportunity. The desire to deliver solutions that meet both business and user-centred objectives is what drives me.

Currently working at Tatts Group, I manage teams and design experiences with a commitment to the conception and crafting of innovative digital products that make sense.
