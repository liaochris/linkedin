Dale McCann's Experience

Singer/Performer

Contract work

January 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months) South Africa

South African Born, Dale first came to prominence as an acoustic guitarist / singer playing the local music scene around South Africa and the UK.

Known for his unique vocal ability, Dale brings the charm and humility of a young “Frank” to every performance. He evokes the style of the famous Ol’ Blue Eyes with his Swinger Swagger and dashing persona he sings and dances up a storm with some of the world’s most loved songs made famous by Frank.

With masterfully recorded backing tracks, Dale recreates the sound in a dazzling show which includes state of the art PA and lighting system along with a back drop and professional props recreating that nostalgic Las Vegas stage setting.

Dale’s tribute show is available for all types of occasions & celebrations including:
Private Parties; Corporate Functions; Weddings; Birthdays; Themed Parties; Hotels
Restaurants; Theatres; Cruises; TV and Advertising etc...

Dale’s show provides full promotional back – up with posters, promotional photos,
Demo cd’s along with professional support.

This ultimate tribute act is the hottest show in town, exclusively available and guaranteed to wow audiences of all ages. The show has it all, great songs, the laughs, the style and the class.
