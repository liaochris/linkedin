Chris Lukenbill's Summary

After quite a few years in the world of website and software development I will be applying those skills to an entirely new field, aquaponic food production.

I'll be the first person to agree with you that this isn't the most likely place to find someone with a degree in computer science and a background in developing everything from database architecture to twitter widgets. However, I see my new venture into aquaponics as the result of combining both my formal education and experience up to this point with my passion for healthy local food.

Aquaponics is a form of hydroponics (growing plants without a soil substrate for nutrient delivery) where the nutrient solution is produced as a bi-product of fish farming (aquaculture).

The field of aquaponics is still in its early stages and we plan to help this industry grow using our experience from software development.
