Denise Bayer's Experience

Founder and Executive Director

The Legal Creative

January 2013 – Present (7 months) Youngstown, Ohio Area

The Legal Creative is a non-profit legal and business resource that provides assistance to artists in the Northeast Ohio/Western Pennsylvania area. The goal is to provide educational resources to empower artists of all backgrounds as they endeavor to promote their work and successfully make a living off of their art. Inspired by the diverse artistic and creative community in the “Rust Belt” region, The Legal Creative is a direct response to the lack of resources readily available to artists who often lack knowledge of their legal rights.

TLC is an immediate response to both the growing creative community and the lack of resources readily and affordably available to artists in the Rust Belt. My extensive background in both law and the arts means that I am constantly immersed in the left brain/right brain challenge: How to create a reliable business from a creative process while maintaining legal rights.
