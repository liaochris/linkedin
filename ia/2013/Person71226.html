Justin Ip's Summary

I run the product and engineering teams at AllTrails - an AngelPad / 500Startups company. I was the first engineering hire, and launched our National Geographic co-branded RoR site in January 2012. I have since built up the team to 6 world-class engineers - we now have 2 authors of Android books, a homebrew core committer, and ex-Facebook / Google / Microsoft team members.

AllSnow, our winter-sports focused app has been the #1 featured app in both the Apple iTunes and Google Play stores. AllTrails remains one of the ten "Essential Apps for Travel" in the iTunes store, alongside Kayak, Tripadvisor, AirBnB etc.

I still spend half of my time programming, and the other half on product, growth and recruiting tasks. Prior to AllTrails I was the first engineer at afar.com, a social travel property focused on experiential travel.

My background before startups is in .NET / Java enterprise tech consulting - I started my own consulting firm at age 23, which I ran successfully for 6 years before deciding to pursue a more meaningful existence in startups.
