Daniel Rivera's Summary

Born on May 23rd, 1983 in Rochester, NY now located in Orlando, FL. Daniel Rivera always had a passion for design since a young age. Striving to complete his goal to becoming a Graphic Designer he found the International Academy of Design and Technology to fill the skill sets he needed to obtain his goal. He is on his way to meeting those goals having graduated with honors, being published in Artisk Magazine March Edition and winning a scholarship from Adobe Photoshop World in 2010.

He designs with emotion and creativity to convey a message or meaning through his artwork. He found that if you design from within, you will create something amazing. Daniel feels that it is a way to express yourself so others can understand or even get inspired by you.

The drive and the love for this field of art gives Daniel a giant leap forward in the world's never ending parade of creative arts. The world has a variation of arts to choose from and Daniel Rivera has chosen his own form that he projects through Graphic Design.
