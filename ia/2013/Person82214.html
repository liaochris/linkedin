Loïc Baumann's Summary

As a major interest for everything related to the making of software and also IT in the Microsoft world.

I've gained experience through the years on the following:

Software Building: ALM with Team Foundation Server and Visual Studio, RUP, CMMI, Software Factories. Been Microsoft MVP for 3 years on ALM.

Project Management: from the formal Management Per Project to a more lean approach, I've enjoyed running small teams as the main PM and Dev Lead.

Software Architecture: requirement, design, R&D, POC, optimization, large refactoring of mainly heavy client apps, SOA or client-server based architecture.

Software development: from Motorola or Intel assembly in the early 90s, then C++ and finally C#. I started with real-time 3D programming, switched to OS/Core library development and currently working on more "traditional" stuffs. Nowadays my center of interest includes WCF, WPF, WF, AppFabric and any kind of patterns/good practices that makes our life easier and our apps better.

In 2005, convinced that IT and Software Dev will be more and more interconnected in the future, I started working on my spare time on IT Technologies such as Network (TCP, DNS, DHCP, Firewall setup), Windows Server administration, Hyper-V, Virtual Box, SCVMM, High Availability of services and infrastructures and I'm now looking to the cloud.
