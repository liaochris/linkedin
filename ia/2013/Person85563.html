Margaret Spence's Summary

Margaret Spence, CWC, RMPE – is a Board Certified Workers’ Compensation consultant, author of – From Workers’ Comp Claimant to Valued Employee – Employers Guide to Implementing a Proactive Return to Work Program. To learn about the book visit www.TheyCanReturnToWork.com

New Book Released June 17, 2013 - Injury Management Challenge: Simplifying Workers Comp and Engaging Managers & Supervisors

Founder of National Return to Work Week this week focuses on helping ill, injured and disabled employees stay in the workforce.

Margaret is a speaker, trainer and expert at showing companies how to slash their workers’ compensation cost by implementing strategies that drastically reduce injury rates, increase productivity and energize employees to work safely.

I was recently appointed to the Society for Human Resource Management - Special Expertise Panel on Employee Health, Safety and Security for a 3 year term.

Margaret is the Founder and CEO of Douglas Claims & Risk Consultants, Inc. and WorkCompSeminars.com. For more than two decades, Margaret has managed workers’ compensation claims for Fortune 500 Corporations, Public Entities and small businesses throughout the United States.

Her company is founded on one principle – Safety has to be implemented from the Board Room to the Stock Room – Employers can not longer accept injuries as the inevitable cost of doing business.

If your company has challenges managing workers compensation claims and linking human resouces best practices with workers compensation - you need my services. I work with company's who understand that safety handbooks and written policies is not enough - you have to have a blue print for managing injuries and getting injured employees back to work successfully. Workers compensation is not an automatic cash cow - in this economy if you don't manage your workers compensation cost you may not be in business for too long.

Read my weekly newsletter at www.WorkersCompGazette.com
