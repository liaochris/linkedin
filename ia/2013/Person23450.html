Chris Kirk's Summary

You're busy. Thanks for taking the time to view my profile.

I own WildBlue, a world-wide aircraft brokerage firm. 3 things you should know: 1) General Aviation is in my blood, 2) I love working with people who value General Aviation, 3) I'll outwork anyone else to make you a lifelong client.

Airplane owners who hire WildBlue often tell me:

- They're tired of writing checks for a plane they're not using.
- They'd rather not waste time with tire-kickers.
- They've been burned by buyers who mislead them, break their commitments, or don't show up.
- They feel like they’re missing out on international sales.

Conversely, buyers often say:

- They’re tired of going to see planes that don’t live up to sales hype.
- They’re frustrated by the lack of transparency in aircraft sales.
- They’re having a hard time separating fact from airport folklore.

What can WildBlue do for you?

WildBlue is dedicated to providing you with "no surprises/no excuses" service. Instead of selling every airplane under the sun, our focus is narrow to better serve those who put their trust and confidence in us.

Who is most successful working with WildBlue?

WildBlue clients are business owners, entrepreneurs, executives & professionals. They often use their planes for business and personal reasons and are pilots themselves. They use other professionals in their daily lives and appreciate the value that a dedicated aircraft salesperson brings to the table. They know how to delegate and value the input from advisors. They maintain their aircraft to high standards and demand high standards from others. They are decision-makers and understand the productivity enhancements General Aviation provides. A WildBlue client knows precisely what his time is worth and will eagerly refer our services to others for a job well done.

In the market to buy or sell a quality airplane -- or think you may be? Please call me at 888.773.4249 or +1.816.479.2723 or email Chris@FlyWildBlue.com.
