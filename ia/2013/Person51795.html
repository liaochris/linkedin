Thomas Harris' Summary

I have 30 years experience handling injury cases, the last 17 years of which I have been self employed at Harris Law Group in Sarasota, Florida. I defended insurance companies in personal injury matters for many years to begin my career, before changing to the other side to represent only injured people the latter part of my career. As a result of being on both sides for a very long time, now that I only represent injury people, I am able to deliver superior service with exceptional experience.

Specialties

Injury Law, including Auto Accidents and Slip and Fall Accidents, or any other accident or injury caused by the fault of others.
