Clara Harris' Projects

  • Knight & Noon

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Clara Harris, Kathy Weisbach

    Follow Detective Knight and her partner Dr. Noon as they crack cases from their office in a second floor walk-up in Fawklin Heights. Monthly radio detective drama in partnership with Crescent Hill Radio in Louisville, Kentucky.

  • Bake-Off of Doom

    • February 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Clara Harris

    When the sirens sound over Suburban Shoals to begin the annual Bake-Off, the mothers of The Realm know that more is at stake than the flakiest crust and the highest meringue. 4 W/4 W or M. Unit Set. Full-length Play. Semi-Finalist, Hot City Theatre's Greenhouse New Play Festival

  • Things We Say to Girls

    • September 2007 to Present
    Team Members: Clara Harris

    A three-play series written and performed by Clara Harris examining the messages and mythologies we pass along to girls about what it means to be female in today's society through the lenses of religion, reproduction, and politics. View from the Pews, Eve's Secret, Dear Mr. Congressman. @TWS2G

  • Swamp Witch Studio LIVE!

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Clara Harris

    A monthly live entertainment of radio drama and live, local music, broadcast weekly from ART+FM in NuLu district of Louisville, Kentucky, and streaming internationally through the internet. artxfm.com

  • Robin of Sherwood

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Clara Harris

    A swashbuckling adaptation of the Robin Hood ballads designed specifically for professional TYA companies. 7M/2W. Minimal set.

  • The Haunting of Sighisoara

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Clara Harris

    The women in the medieval town of Sighisoara, Romania are haunted by a horrifying spectre who bears ominous and terrifying visions to their dreams - dreams that begin to manifest in the physical world. 5W/2M/1M or W. Minimal Set.
