James Veigli's Summary

James Veigli is Australia’s leading mortgage industry consultant, speaker and founder of Broker Profits Vault (www.brokerprofitsvault.com.au).

He is a marketing, sales and broker-business expert, who has unique ideas that can revolutionise your thinking, business and income.

Some of his products include:

- Million-Dollar Cheat Sheets
- Fee-For-Advice Blueprint
- Broking On AutoPilot System
- Broker Mentoring Guide
- GOLD, PLATINUM and DIAMOND Membership Programs

He lives in Melbourne with his partner Clare and when not working can be found on the tennis court, travelling or enjoying one of Melbourne’s great coffee shops.

Keynote Presentations (brief list):

- Fee For Advice Blueprint
- How To Cross-Sell Every Client... Every Time.
- Maximum Database Harvesting
- Trail Replacement Strategies
- The "Sales Free" Sales Process
- The Professional Broker

- Why Your Marketing Doesn't Work

To contact James for keynote presentations or workshops - email Clare: mail@jamesveigli.com

For private consulting enquiries - email concierge@brokerprofitsvault.com.au or phone (03) 9024 1232 to arrange a confidential meeting.
