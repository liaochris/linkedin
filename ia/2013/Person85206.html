Marco Krasinski's Summary

Born in 1983, after graduating in the U.K. and working in New York City, I moved back to Rome to pursue my artistic career. I have exhibited with critical acclamation my series of portraits called "OSIRIA" in Italy aswell as London, Bonn and Sopot. My works are part of private collections all over Europe. I am frontman and producer of the internationally known band FOMENTO and of the dance project TUNZ TUNZ. I play guitar, bass and drums. I have been selected for the 2012 Italian edition of the “Annuario d’Arte Moderna” and I am included in Antonio Sorgente’s latest book along world famous artists such as Guttuso and Attardi.
