Giulio Laforenza's Summary

After the degree in Telecommunication engineering (year 2006), I started working in NetResults S.r.l, a spin-off company of the University of Pisa, as VoIP technical engineer. I became an Asterisk configuration expert, and developed the Dialplan of Kalliope VoIP PBX. For a few months I worked at Elsag-Datamat in Abbadia S.Salvatore (SI) as web-interface developer/Asterisk expert. Since Septemper 2008 I've been working for Edit Engineering, a company from Pisa in the Railway industry. I was involved in AnsaldoBreda IC4 project (designing and testing of DSB IC4 trains), in GE Trasportation/Trenitalia SSC/SCMT BL3 as project manager, in DMU DSB IC2 train project for AnsaldoBreda, URF ambiental tests for Knorr-Bremse, CDPTR Vivalto RAMS analysis, in the editing of the Fault Management manual of the Metropolis 172-U-SMD Traction Converter for Alstom Transport, in functional test reports for GETS BL3 (on ATR and ALn train units), in AB EMU V250 project (as system engineer), in FMECA analysis of the Sirio GE-Casella DCU for AnsaldoBreda, in Russian/EU standards cross-analysis for Alstom Transport, in ELCAD electical schemes designing for BL3 GETS, in the editing of fire/smoke detection systems of AB CDPTR and in GETS BL3 verification activity. I'm working on the Transmission System (network infrastructure) of SL/GETS MetroStockholm project. I'm member of the association DUSTLAB (Devices Ubiquitus Sensor Tecnology).

Specialties

I have a leadership aptitude, coordination and business management skills, strong creative flair and a natural propensity for interpersonal relations. I’m “troubleshooting-oriented” and predisposed to both individual and team work.
