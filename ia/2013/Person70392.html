Judy Heft's Summary

Over the past 17 years I have grown my business from daily money manager and bookkeeper to a financial concierge service. My passion is helping people simplify their lives by handling the daily activities of personal bookkeeping & financial organization.

My clients include busy executives, divorcees, entrepreneurs, seniors & time challenged individuals. When my clients travel they never have to worry about a bill going unpaid or returning home to a pile of unopened mail.

I work closely with their other professional advisors, especially during tax season, to ensure they are getting the proper deductions. In some cases I am responsible for household employee payroll.

I help clients fill out divorce financial affidavits and work closely with their family lawyer or mediator.

I have been appointed conservator and power of attorney by the probate court when clients don't have all of their cognitive abilities to make financial decisions and there are no family members available to take this responsibility.

I was born to be an entrepreneur. My passion is helping people and connecting people and that's what makes my company unique. I have an understanding ear and I listen to my clients needs. Every client is unique and each one is treated as if they are my only client. I know how to get things done. I am a professional in every sense of the word. Confidentiality is a given!

My specialties include organization of tax records for CPA's, working with clients who are contemplating divorce and need financial records updated, tracking income and expense for multiple properties and helping seniors wade through the maize of paperwork.

Although my clients range in age from 40 to 98, I thoroughly enjoy helping the elderly. They are very grateful to have someone to explain the complexities of the banking system in today's world. It's a gift to have a business that is succesful and helps people at the same time.
