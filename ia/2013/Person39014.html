Dylan Gluck's Projects

  • Transdiaspora Network

    • January 2012 to March 2012
    Team Members: Dylan Gluck

    My goal for this project was to create a framework which would allow TDN to communicate with their audience more broadly and with a higher level of granularity through a more dynamic platform. To achieve this i re-organized the site architecture to create more depth of information and broader navigability. To enable the TDN staff to update the website and provide users with the most recent information I incorporated Wordpress as a content management solution. This CMS choice allows for future scalability & to easily manage plugins and additional feature functionality. Since launch, the site has received positive feedback from both market segments, and has allowed more creative freedom for TDN to communicate their mission and value.

  • Jillian Ricciardi

    • April 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Dylan Gluck

    Jillian Ricciardi is an aspiring Stylist and student at SCAD. In 2012, Jill asked me to design her website (something i've been wanting to do for a long time). The objective was to get her work online, and create a professional portfolio which would work on mobile devices.

    I chose to use XML and simpleXML (PHP) as an alternative to a SQL Database. This enables Jill to update her website easily by simply editing a text file. The front-end is developed using the Foundation Framework, jQuery, Orbit & Fit-Text. We are currently tracking traffic flow using Google Analytics & tweaking the UI to maximize page views.
