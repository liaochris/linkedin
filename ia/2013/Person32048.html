David J. Roman's Summary

Integrity | Collaboration | Excellence

David J. Roman is fascinated with systems and dedicated to success through collaboration. He enjoys working with people, products, and processes to discover and share value with customers. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems from Arizona State University's W.P. Carey School of Business, and is CSM, PMP, and ITIL certified.

David's career objectives are to learn the art of true service, cultivate a global mindset, and innovate value delivery. A few of David's current goals are to become a catalyst to American Express Technologies' delivery transformation, earn an International MBA from Thunderbird School of Global Management, and explore the management of global service technologies.

David also enjoys playing and composing music, hiking, writing poetry, flying/sky diving, and spending time with family and friends.
