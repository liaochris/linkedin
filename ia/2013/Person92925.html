Michael Rhodes' Experience

President

Economic Development Group, Inc.

January 1989 – Present (25 years) Indianapolis, IN and Palatine, IL

Founded company in 1989 to assist growth oriented companies to attain and administer economic incentives in support of expansion projects nationwide; and to provide site selection and comparative community analyses for companies considering multiple locations for new facilities.

Key resource for senior corporate management responsible for decisions involving site selection and economic incentives. Advocate for and assist cost and opportunity focused companies to optimize capital investment project strategies and to make informed site and incentives decisions.

Represent clients and assist senior corporate management to maximize the value of economic incentives through strategic collaborations with public officials and the formation of public - private partnerships in support of client projects.

Client incentive awards exceed $251 million from 300+ programs. Provided services for 139 expansion projects in 30 states, including 31 headquarters projects. Clients have expended more than $1.6 billion on projects and have created and retained more than 36,000 jobs.

Extensive pragmatic experience with the policies, programs and practices of 200+ economic development-focused organizations and local, county, state and federal governmental agencies.

Leverages 24 years experience with the business practices and strategies, presentations and incentive programs of many economic development organizations nationwide, as an advocate and key site selection and incentives advisor for company decision makers.

Research, author and attain approval of federal, state and locally-funded incentive programs. Provide lead role in client company administration of awarded programs.

Advise officials of economic development organizations on strategies to enhance effectiveness and outcomes.

Lead and manage a successful entrepreneurial enterprise for 24 years.
