Charles Stanley's Summary

Charles is a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER® practitioner, Chartered Financial Consultant and Accredited Investment Fiduciary®. For more than twenty years as a comprehensive financial planner, Charles Stanley has served business owners, retired physicians, corporate executives, retirees and many widows with their Wealth Management needs.

Charles Stanley has been quoted or published in The Journal of Financial Planning, Investment News, The San Diego Union Tribune, The Bottom Line, The Canadian Funeral Director and the American Funeral Director magazines, The San Diego Daily Transcript, Financial AdvisorPro, The Family Business Advisor, The Christian Businessman Magazine and was a contributing author to "TASK-The Advisors Survival Kit" and "Advisors Seeking Knowledge," both published by LexisNexis and “How to Manage a Million Dollars . . . or less.” He has appeared as an expert witness in both NASD Arbitration and in the San Diego Superior Court.

Charles has been involved in the local San Diego area financial planning community having served multiple years on the Board of the San Diego Chapter of the Financial Planning Association and is a NAPFA-Registered Financial Advisor with the National Association of Personal Financial Advisors, an exclusive organization of fee-only planners. He has taught continuing education classes for attorneys (MCLE) and CPAs (CPE). He is also an active member of the Escondido Rotary Club.

Specialties: Uniform Prudent Investor Act and Fiduciary Investment, Client-centric Values Based Planning

Serving as a fiduciary and deriving compensation on a fee-only basis, Trovena Investment Advisors provides passive investment portfolios suited to your unique needs as an investor. Trovena Investment Advisors portfolios are constructed from index based, mutual fund products of Dimensional Fund Advisors (“DFA”), which are not available to retail investors.

At Trovena Investment Advisors we provide fee-only services with a commitment to conflict free investment strategies. What this means is that we act in your best interest and do not receive compensation from other sources. This model eliminates the conflicts that are present with traditional brokerage, where a broker makes money if and only when you take new positions in your portfolio.
