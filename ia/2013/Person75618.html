Kimberly Errigo's Summary

Kimberly Errigo, Master Coach, is the CEO of Essential Coaching Services, Inc, which she founded in 1998.

Kimberly's background includes 20 years of experience as a teacher and professional coach, 15 years of expertise in business administration, and 25 years of conscious engagement in the fields of personal, organizational, and international development.

Highlights of Kimberly's qualifications include:

• Over 3000 client hours of executive, life, and business coaching for improved performance, wellness, and interpersonal relationships;
• Assuming a lead role in strategic planning, compliance, human resources, and development efforts in for-profit, nonprofit, and entrepreneurial environments;
• Resolving disputes, conflicts, and complaints between clients and within organizations; and
• Producing independent, regional, and national training programs for a variety of professional audiences.


Specialties: training and development, motivational speaking, life and business coaching, executive coaching.
