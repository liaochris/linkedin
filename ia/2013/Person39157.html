Earl Cox's Summary

I am a publishing veteran with more than two decades of experience.

After spending ten years with John Wiley & Sons, I launched my own publishing and literary management agency, Earl Cox & Associates. Most recently, I co-founded AskTheMoneyCoach.com, a free personal finance advice site.

At the NYU School of Continuing and Professional Studies Center for Publishing, I taught courses on book promotion, marketing and sales. Additionally, I taught classes at and am a graduate of the Stanford Professional Publishing Program.

Here are the three core areas of my professional expertise:

* As a Literary Agent, I offer author representation, idea development, manuscript review, and career management services.

* As a Publishing Consultant, I provide all aspects of book and digital publishing services including editing, layout, book packaging, printing, distribution and SEO.

* As a Public Relations Expert, I help authors, bloggers, publishers, and corporations get their books, products, services or messages to the media. I also collaborate with P.R. and marketing firms to connect their clients with television, radio, print and social media.

My creativity is the cornerstone of my success. I can take any book, article or even just an idea, and make it better.

Specialties

Publishing, printing, marketing, book promotion, book publicity, sales management, sales strategy, distribution, acquisitions, literary management, contract negotiation, collections
