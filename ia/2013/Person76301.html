Kristen Hess' Summary

Kristen Hess is a NYC-based Food Photographer/Stylist, and Writer. She is also the Publisher of food blog TheArtfulGourmet.com, where she develops recipes, styles and photographs food, writes about food and cooking, restaurants and chefs, products and book reviews, food and wine events, culinary travel and lifestyle.

Client experience: Epicure Foods; Unilever; Bacardi; The Works Catering; Stoli, Dr. Neal Barnard MD; BirdsEye; Kitchen Consultants; Sandra Lee Magazine, The Food Group; NYS Restaurant Assoc; Downtown Magazine, Colavita, Gary Null Power Foods, Buitoni, Harper Collins, Stonesong Press, Baci Perugina, Good Food Ireland, KitchenAid, Nespresso, National Pork Board, Kraft Foods, Tassimo, Oscar Mayer, Odwalla, Stride, Domino's; EDiningNews.

Kristen has designed, produced and managed advertising campaigns for major ad agencies in NYC, Melbourne & ATL: The Home Depot Advertising, Y&R Melbourne, McCann-Erickson, JWT, Digitas, Interbrand, & 360i.

Kristen has intensive culinary, digital and food photography, food styling, food writing and culinary video training from The Institute of Culinary Education, International Center of Photography, PhotoManhattan, Parsons New School, Gotham Writer's/NY Times Knowledge Network, and PPSOP.com in NYC. She has also studied food styling with masters in the culinary industry such as Beatrice Peltre, James Peterson, Jamie Tiampo. Laura Knoop, Lara Ferroni, Ron Goldman, and Lisa Golden-Schroeder.

Kristen holds a BFA in Graphic Design and an MA in Advertising. She is a member of the International Association of Culinary Professionals (IACP), and Media Bistro.

Specialties: food photography, food styling, writing, photo and prop styling, art direction, creative direction, blogging, video production, advertising, branding, marketing communications, design, interactive media, integrated communications, social media, emerging media, recipe development, event planning and promotion, test kitchen, on-camera chef, culinary video
