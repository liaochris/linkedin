Diane Irwin, CPRW's Summary

My passion is helping you GET RESULTS in your job search. Together, we can uncover your personal value and create career documents that grab the attention of employers.

I know your pain! In the course of my own career, I have made transitions and found that each change had many challenges and frustrations. Through time, effort, research, mentoring, and the support of a wonderful network, I learned how to achieve success in landing positions that I loved. Later, holding positions in outplacement and recruitment, I found that I really loved helping others attain results in the job search process, too.

Ultimately, I became immersed in the profession of résumé writing and job search earning my CPRW certification. In additon, I regularly attend continuing ed in cutting-edge trends that will yield HIGH IMPACT in the job market. My goal is to help you succeed in your search and to be your “easy button” in the process!

My resumes and advice appear in various publications (see my website for details). In addition, I offer free career advice as a SPEAKER, TWEETER, and BLOGGER.
