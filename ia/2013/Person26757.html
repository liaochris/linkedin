Conny Liegl's Summary

--- SHORT PROFILE ---
I am a versatile professional with several years of working experience in project management, public relations and event organization in a in a fast-paced technological research environment.
I was formally trained in social media marketing strategies, e-learning technologies and theories, as well as user experience research and evaluation. I managed to pass on my know-how in adult training and worked as a freelancer on various web-related projects.

My strongest skills include efficient planning and problem solving. I readily adapt to change, work independently and exceed expectations. I am able to juggle multiple priorities and meet tight deadlines without compromising quality.
As a result of working for several computer science institutions, I am familiar with cutting-edge research in several important fields of computer science (including visual computing and human-computer interaction).

Specialties

project management, marketing, social media marketing, public relations, event organization, communications, recruiting, evaluation, web usability and accessibility, effective human-computer interaction, web design, media design
