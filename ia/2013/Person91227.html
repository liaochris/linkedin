Melissa Dobson's Summary

Melissa Dobson is a solo entrepreneur specializing in consulting on community management, social marketing and digital strategies for forward-thinking businesses in the Food & Wine, Travel and Lifestyle industries. She works to attract clients who are entrepreneurial, good-hearted and value strong relationships.

Recently, Melissa has worked closely with the team at Vin65 on strategies related to: brand awareness, establishing thought leadership and driving referrals from clients and influencers in the wine industry, much of which was executed via campaigns incorporating both new media and traditional marketing and PR. Melissa and Waterway Houseboats Ltd. work together seasonally on a series of Facebook contests in conjunction with Facebook advertisements that helped Waterway grow their Facebook fan base of engaged fans and advocates of the Waterway Houseboats brand.

Melissa established Academy of Wine Communications-Finger Lakes Chapter where she plans, creates content and manages the AWC FLX online community page and offline meetings. She pioneered awareness of opportunities in social media when she co-presented one of the first social media workshops in the Finger Lakes region. She regularly contributes to her local wine & food community via workshops and spearheading AWC FLX. Past social workshops for: (one-on-one's & group workshops) Finger Lakes Wine Country Tourism Marketing Association, Yates County Chamber of Commerce, Wine Direct Marketing Services, Keuka College ASAP, Sheldrake Point Vineyards and Seneca Springs Resort. In 2011, Melissa sat as a panelist on "Community Management from the Front Lines" for Social Share Summit.

Melissa discovered her love for the wine and food industry while working in PR at Deutsch, Inc. in NYC on the Boston Beer Co's Samuel Adams Beer account. She followed her dream to work within the industry by establishing her solo business after moving to the Finger Lakes.
