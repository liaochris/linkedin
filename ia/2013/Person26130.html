Kenny Smith (CodingWebU)'s Experience

President and CEO

CodingWebU.com

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; E-Learning industry

April 2008 – Present (5 years 6 months)

CodingWebU.com is exclusively geared towards providing quality education at affordable prices to the medical coding and billing arenas. Our experienced authors average over 25 years of medical coding and billing experience.

Our custom Learning Management System (LMS) is a self-paced, interactive, web-based training system which allows the student to easily navigate through the system. The testing capabilities within the LMS ensure comprehension of the education provided within each course. Each course is designed to provide the student knowledge that will help them be successful in their position. The courses can be used to obtain Continuing Education Hours to maintain credentials or to fulfill their organization's annual competencies.

Every course can be purchased individually or by an organization. An organization can purchase a group license fee which allows unlimited access to the selected courses for all staff. Additionally, CodingWebU's LMS provides access to the administrative site where groups can create reports and analyze staff's performance.

CodingWebU.com has several partners who offer a variety of complimentary services including books, audio conferences, and online courses. CodingWebU.com is your one-stop shop for all educational needs.

CodingWebU.com is always looking for additional members to join our team. If you are interested in becoming an authoring partner, please send your resume and the courses you are interested in developing to Kenny Smith at (484) 433-0495 or ksmith@CodingWebU.com.

Consulting Manager

A Health Care Consulting Firm

2000 – April 2008 (8 years)

As a financial consultant, he was able to save hospitals millions of dollars by helping them understand Medicare Transfer DRGs and how to correctly bill claims and ensure proper reimbursement.
