Liane Carmi's Summary

I am, first and foremost, an educator.
I study, I learn, I apply, and I teach.

My aim in life is simple: to help others achieve more than they previously thought possible. It applies to everything I do. My mission is to guide people who are ready for action in the right direction.

My top values in life include figuring things out, connecting with people, being easy-going, spending time outside and enjoying physical challenges. When something I do touches on all those values, I enjoy it - and do it well.

I am highly interested in yoga and meditation, linguistics and languages, teaching, hiking, marketing, snorkeling and self-improvement.
