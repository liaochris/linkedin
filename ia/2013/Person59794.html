Jay Clue's Summary

I'm an Artist, Art Director, Designer, and occasional Toy Designer. Originally from the New York metro area but now living in London's East End. I hold an MA in Graphic Design with Distinction from London College of Communication and currently working on my PhD research at Central Saint Martins College of Art & Design. At the current moment I am welcoming potential perm, temp, and freelance opportunities in the design education, art direction, and design arenas in both Europe and North America.

As a designer and art director my work has won a few awards over the past years - including a Webby, WW3, Davia, and Best Pancake Maker East of the Mississippi... Alright, I may have made the last one up - but I do make some delicious pancakes. On the design side of my life I am an integrated art director and graphic designer that also has experience with physical web development, coding, UI & UX design, project & team management, etc... I work in the digital media, interactive, and print arenas - with most projects spanning across all three platforms.

My client list ranges from the underground and edgy to the clean and super corporate from big corporations to tiny local mom and pop shops and just about everywhere in between - to name a few: Slipknot, Run DMC, L'Oreal, Smart Balance, Merck, Rolling Stone, Heineken, Camel, New York Jets, MTV, National MS Society, and Bill Clinton.

The research side of my life is currently based around developing new ways of creating public art interventions that integrate with and involve their local community, while utilizing the mediums of letterform based street art and graffiti. Along with how these interventions can aid community regeneration efforts. My past research has included the history of graffiti letterform and its ties back to medieval blackletter, divine proportion, and the relationships of word and image.

More info about my work, publications, research, and art can be found on my personal site - www.JayClue.com
