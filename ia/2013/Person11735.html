Barbara Raffel's Summary

Signature Hair Designs is a team of educated, passionate professionals, offering superior service in a relaxing atmosphere to each guest. We believe that true beauty comes from within. By helping guests look their best, we are also helping them to feel their best, elevating them to a level of excellence that they can then share with others.

We strive to provide only the highest quality of service and products to all of our guest. We proudly partner with:

Goldwell Color

Great Lengths USA Hair Extensions

Pureology

Yuko Japanese Straightening

Coppola Keratin System

OPI Nail

Creative Nail

Pharmagel Skin care products

Located in Tequesta Florida, Signature Hair Designs, provides a variety of hair, nail, skin, and massage services, as well as professional retail products and tools.

Barbara Raffel, owner of Signature Hair Designs and stylist of over 18 years has extensive training and education in hair cutting, coloring and hi-lighting, permanent hair straightening, hair extension application, mens styling, and keratin treatments.

In addition, Barbara has furthered her education at the Goldwell Academy of Color in Baltimore, as well as the Vidal Sassoon Academy in London. She also attends the Premiere Beauty Event in Orlando every year to keep up with all the current styles and techniques.

Enjoy the experience of feeling the absolute best about your hair.
