Brandon Redenius' Summary

I am a San Francisco-based graphic designer and illustrator. I have over five years of experience in branding, marketing design, web design, and illustration. I’m always trying to expand my knowledge base, and I’m always looking for a chance to put new skills to use.

As a child of the 80s and early 90s, I grew up on Disney, Tim Burton movies, cartoons, and comic books. My aesthetics constantly change: sometimes colorful, sometimes dark and macabre, sometimes somewhere in between! My personal interests double as inspiration, and I love making both of those a part of my work.

Because of my varied interest, I studied Studio Art for two years before moving on to getting a Bachelors Degree in Graphic Design at California State University, Chico. I like to combine art and design, and feel that concepts and standards in one can often help me in the other.

I enjoy many creative outlets, and try out anything I become curious about! I write and illustrate my own webcomic (The Skeleton Bandit) in my spare time. I’m also working on t-shirt designs. I enjoy the art of screenprinting and like to get my hands dirty!

Specialties: Album Art/Packaging Design, Illustration (in various mediums), Branding and Identity, Brand Management, Marketing Design, Web Design (HTML and CSS)
