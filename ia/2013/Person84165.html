Maggie Beall's Summary

Maggie Beall has been involved in the Frederick County real estate marketplace for over 20 years, where she has become a recognized leader in the marketplace. She began in 1989 in her family real estate business becoming both a successful realtor and an experienced Manager of the operations and marketing of real estate services. In 2000, Maggie moved into the Title business with Keystone Title, where she learned all aspects of the Title business.

After several years in the Title business, Maggie returned to Real Estate sales/marketing from 2006 to 2009, where she also managed a Rocky Gorge Development office in Frederick. During this time, she played a key role in sales, marketing and processing for the 200 unit condo conversion project, Willow Bend. More importantly, she conducted all closings for the Willow Bend community.

Maggie reentered the Title business as Manager of Vanguard Signature Settlement in 2009 where she took over full management, marketing and business operations along with the day-to-day processing and closing of files.

In March of 2010, Maggie agreed to purchase Frederick Title, an established title company in Frederick, MD where she is now running and growing the business as the sole owner and full-time general manager.

Maggie also has a strong presence in her local community and is very active in the local real estate board, FCAR. She has built many strong relationships with people and organizations through her dedication to the community and the FCAR programs that support the community. She has sat on many committees to include the FCAR Community Service Committee, Realtor Political Action Committee & American Home Month Project. Maggie continues to serve as a key figure on the aforementioned committees and in the Real Estate community as a whole.

Specialties

Residential and light commercial real estate settlements to include purchases and refinances; Deed preparation; Title exam and issuance of Title Insurance; Contract review; Short-sale negotiations; REO sales
