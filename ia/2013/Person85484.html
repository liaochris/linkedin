Marcy Twete's Summary

Marcy Twete built her career on the foundation of nonprofit women’s initiatives before transitioning to global fundraising consulting and launching Career Girl Network in 2012. Career Girl Network has reached over 500,000 women since its launch, and continues to provide information, resources, and networking opportunities to women around the world.

Marcy is also the author of the book "You Know Everybody! A Career Girl’s Guide to Building a Network That Works," released in June 2013.

Marcy brings to any team a broad and deep set of skills and experience in professional development, diversity and inclusion, community relations, fundraising, and marketing. Confident in a combination of experience, passion and talent that creates success and generates return on investment in revenue and knowledge for a target audience.

Specialties: Network building, internal and external relations and partnership creation, brand messaging, corporate citizenship and social responsibility initiatives, community relations, and direct fundraising.

Marcy Twete's Experience

Founder

Career Girl Network

December 2008 – Present (4 years 9 months) Chicago, IL

Career Girl Network is an initiative that began in Minneapolis, Minnesota in 2007 with the blog “Minneapolis Career Girl” and later in Chicago, Illinois with the “Chicago Career Girl” blog. After building blog readership in Minneapolis and Chicago, Career Girl Network expanded in 2012 to include nearly 30 writers and over 500,000 worldwide viewers worldwide.

Career Girl Network’s mission is to provide for women clear paths to career success and a large network of women to nurture, mentor, and support their goals. An online media platform, Career Girl Network gives women on the rise the opportunity to connect with other women, expand their knowledge in career resources, and ultimately rise to the top of their fields, reach down and help another woman up.
