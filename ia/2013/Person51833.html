Harry Richardson, P.E., LEED AP's Summary

Harry Richardson is a professional architectural & engineering photographer. He is passionate about photography because still architectural photographs and interactive 360° virtual tours provide a virtual reality experience delivered on a website. They are an exciting way to enable website visitors to feel as if they are actually at a location, and inspire them to do business there.

Specialties

Interior and exterior architectural & engineering photography, and interactive 360° virtual tours
