Angie Segal's Summary

Dynamic Business coach with corporate background in sales, marketing and management in the fashion industry. I strive to help business owners achieve their dreams for their business by providing guidance, support, encouragement and accountability. Successful entrepreneurs use business coaches.

My business is focused on helping others be successful. To that aim, I have co-founded a FREE monthly happy hour in Montgomery County called The ORIGINAL Third Tuesdays to help people connect and do business with each other. All are welcome to attend and you can register on the website at www.originaltuesdays.com. It is one of the most successful groups in the area, averaging 300 people at each month’s event. Due to the incredible popularity of this event, registration is now limited to 350 people and you must register in advance.

ActionCOACH is the world’s #1 business coaching franchise as ranked by Entrepreneur Magazine for the past 7 years. I am proud to be part of this organization. The systems and procedures developed by ActionCOACH combined with my 20 years of real life business experience make me an excellent business coach. I am truly vested in the success of my clients and hope that I can help you reach new levels of success in your business.

If you are interested in business coaching, I am happy to offer a free 1-hour coaching session with no strings attached for any business owner who wants to see what coaching is all about.

If you need help making a connection, please feel free to ask me for help. I am happy to make introductions or suggest partners for building your business.

You can email me at angiesegal@actioncoach.com

National Sales Manager

Gigi In Style

April 2006 – July 2007 (1 year 4 months)

Gigi In Style creates and markets trend jewelry for department stores and specialty stores in the United States and Canada. The company is headquartered in Los Angeles, CA and New York, NY. As the National Sales Manager, I led a team of salespeople and worked directly with product development. During my time there, we opened many new accounts including Forever 21, Up Against the Wall and Lord & Taylor. We were able to negotiate a licensing deal with Nicole Miller to create an upscale, trend right jewelry line and take it to market.

Sales Manager

Liz Claiborne

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; LIZ; Apparel & Fashion industry

March 2000 – March 2006 (6 years 1 month)

Liz Claiborne is a leading manufacturer of jewelry, accessories, apparel and home goods for department stores around the world. They are headquartered in New York, NY. As the sales manager for the jewelry division I lead a sales team and managed business models for department stores in the United States. Additionally, we created and launched a new line, Realities, exclusively for Lord & Taylor. I was also the original person to suggest that iPod cases would make a great accessory item.

Territory Manager

The Monet Group

1995 – 2000 (5 years)

Monet Jewelry was the pre-eminent manufacturer of costume jewelry in the United States. The brands included Monet, Trifar, Marvella and Christian LaCroix. They were located in Providence, RI and New York City. As the Territory Manager I was primarily responsible for growing sales with department stores on the East Coast. In my 5 years there, I grew sales in large, well developed accounts every year on year over year sales

Buyer - Watches and Tailored Jewelry

Woodward & Lothrop

May 1987 – September 1995 (8 years 5 months)

Woodward & Lothrop was a department store based in Washington, DC for over 100 years. They also owned John Wannamaker in the Philadelphia market. As the buyer for watches and tailored jewelry I built a strong business model by choosing assortments, determining marketing and advertising strategies, developing POS strategy and introducing new brands. During my time as buyer we were the headquarters for Swatch watches, the hottest watch on the market at that time.
