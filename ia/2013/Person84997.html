MicroSource is more than your designer, pre-press house or printing company, we are a truly Global Print Management company. With offices in New England and Hong Kong and our own in-house transportation and Logistics Department we are in a unique position to guarantee your satisfaction. Moreover, we are your advocates during the entire process.

We will help you every step of the way by listening to your vision and requirements. We will ascertain the most appropriate direction and advise you as such, with what we believe to be the best way for you to achieve your goals.

Nothing is perfect and we don’t profess to be, but what we do promise you is this, to be with you and help you navigate through the sometimes choppy waters of printing and web media, or to put it another way, we are with you from design to delivery!
