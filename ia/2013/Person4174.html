★ A bit of his background ★

Alejandro Martin Ugalde is a Senior Software Engineer and Founder/CEO of Nebuko.com, a mobile software development studio based in the Basque Country. He holds an MBA from Deusto Business School, INSEAD Value Innovation Programme and a degree in Computer Science from the Basque Country Public University (UPV/EHU).

Since 1994, he has been involved in different Internet projects funded by his own University. In 1999, he joined Sarenet, the Internet Division of at Vocento, the leading multimedia communications group in the general press market in Spain. In 2000, he began working at FranceTelecom Spain as Development Manager for the Research and Develop Unit.
In 2004, he launched Pookmail.com, a disposable email account system, which in less than 4 years has become one of the most popular on the world wide web.

In 2008, he left FranceTelecom to create his own development studio in the Basque Country. Nebuko, in collaboration with local companies, has successfully released B5MAP, a new Geographical Information System for Gipuzkoa Council's Department of Environment and Planning.

In 2011, Nebuko launched Instamblr its first iPhone commercial application, an intuitive and simple way of finding your friends' microblogs on Tumblr.

Since 2011 Nebuko is the technical solution provider for Deskontu.com and Pantori.es.

Currently, Nebuko dedicates most of its research efforts to videogames.
