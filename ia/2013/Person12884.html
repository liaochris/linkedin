Ben Godfrey's Summary

I'm a technology leader specialising in lean software delivery in fast-growth organisations.

At Wonga I'm responsible for the delivery of technology that powers great products. I've overseen implementation of new software and improved existing processes, notably devops and operations, within a rapidly expanding engineering team.

Before Wonga architected and built a wide range of projects and platforms, primarily open source and cloud-based.

I've founded 3 startups. I've done just about everything: development, product management, marketing, community management, fundraising, finance and more.
