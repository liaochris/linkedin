Kathy Morse's Summary

I am full-service strategic visual communicator, skilled in honing management directives from conception through implementation.

Background and skills range from professional photographer (both commercial and reportage) to creative and graphic designer, printing broker as well as web structure, development and growth management director, content management designer.

Currently employed at the College of Central Florida, responsible for communication consistency, use of brand, maintaining public image through print and digital advertising, social media and other digital venues as well as college external web design.

Working with corporate initiatives, I began to hone my communication skills working in a Fortune 250 firm with $9 billion in annual revenues and two major electric utilities serving 3.1 million customers in the Carolinas and Florida for over 12 years.

While there I was able to markedly raise awareness of fundamental goals in the areas of Safety, Human Performance (HU) and Diversity. This had been measured by the amount of OSHA recordable injuries and HU mistakes made at the nuclear plant during my tenure.

Specialties: Creative Director and Graphic Designer, Web Content Administrator, Web Manager, Trainer, Technical Administrator, Studio/Location Photographer, Artist/Illustrator, Social Media Director and Trainer, Print Shop Lead, Videographer/Producer Online Distribution of Media, Corporate Event Planning, Community Relations Coordinator.Emergency Response News Coordinator, Safety, Human Performance and Diversity Instructor.
