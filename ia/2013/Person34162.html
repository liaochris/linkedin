Dennis Bacsafra's Summary

After graduating from the University of California at Berkeley with a degree in Computer Science and Economics, I worked for various companies in the San Francisco Bay Area. I have also been involved with small to large-scale software engineering projects for consumer goods, finance, biotech and industrial design institutions in Silicon Valley. In addition, I hold a Master's Degree in International Development Studies from the Universiteit van Amsterdam in The Netherlands. My graduate school thesis is an assessment on the impacts of micro-finance on rural poverty.

In 2006, I started a metaverse consulting company... Avatrian. The company provides products and services for various 3D online digital worlds. It is headquartered in Emeryville, CA and has an operations base in Cebu, Philippines. At present, I am working on a project with House Research in Los Angeles developing clinical tests and online games, primarily for hearing impaired kids.

When I have free time, I enjoy sipping tea, gardening and snowboarding down a black-diamond run at Lake Tahoe. Fortunate enough to travel and visit over 150 cities in the world, I consider Barcelona as my favorite. When asked about my proudest achievement, I always recall the time when I hiked by myself to the peak of the High Atlas mountains in Morocco. It was when I realized how much more I am capable of doing.

Apart from English, I am versed in Tagalog at a native-speaker level. I am conversant in Spanish. I also have exposure to Dutch but found Germanic languages very difficult. Now, I am absorbing the Cebuano dialect slowly.
