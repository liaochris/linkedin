Brian Murphy's Summary

Since I was a elementary school I have always been involved with creative process. Early on I wanted to be a print designer working for magazines on new layouts and design ideas. I worked on newspaper and yearbook staffs for many of my school years and into college branching into radio. But then the Internet came into my life in 1994 and that changed everything - you could build anything you wanted with no restrictions.

Since then I've been on a direct target for creating environments that users and readers would find attractive and want to stay and read the content offered. Over the years I have developed skill sets in nearly all areas related to digital publication.

Specialties

Website UI Layout/Design, Digital Project Management, HTML / CSS, Photoshop, Illustrator, Ad Serving Systems (OAS Real Media, Yahoo APT) including site setup, Flash, Ellington (CMS), Javascript, PHP, MySQL, Drupal (CMS), WordPress, Django, Anametrix, Google Analytics, Omniture.
