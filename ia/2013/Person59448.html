Jason Richard Adams' Summary

Since learning the foundations of the Architectural Business in Vail, Colorado, I have worked tirelessly in Miami, Florida to develop and grow the business of my esteemed colleague, Max Strang. His designs and principles set the bar for how timeless architecture begins.

Our goal as a firm is to continue working for the Client that seeks the exquisite design unique for South Florida's climate and latitude in the world.

Specialties

Project Development, Project Construction Management, Project Team Leading, Budget Development and Business Growth Strategies, Emerging Services and Green Technology Procurement.
