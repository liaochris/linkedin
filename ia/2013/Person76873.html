Kriss Titus' Experience

Executive Director

Kissimmee Tourism Education Association

January 2012 – Present (1 year 9 months) Kissimmee Florida

Kriss Titus is an innovator of travel and tourism strategy and business development, having leveraged an outstanding career of result-focused industry leadership and a proven track record of achieving extraordinary successes with complex, mission-critical goals.

Working in travel and tourism since 1985, Kriss distinguished herself as a high performance, high profile industry leader. Serving nine years as the past Executive Director of Beaches of South Walton’s Tourist Development Council in Northwest Florida. Her accomplishments include developing a successful research-driven strategy to the branding, marketing and business development, positioning Beaches of South Walton as a premier Florida beach resort destination. Prior to her outstanding career with Beaches of South Walton TDC, Titus spent nine years as the Executive Vice President of the world-renowned Arabian Nights Dinner Attraction in Orlando, Florida.

A pursuasive change agent, Titus taps into her boundless enthusiasm, good common sense and deep business expertise to balance the needs of government, partner stakeholders, media and the tourism industry. She has a driving passion to bring the best people to the table, paint a vision that resonates and unifies everyone around the mission.

A winner of multiple prestigious state and national awards and recognition for exemplary performance in the areas of marketing, advertising, Internet marketing, public relations and environmental stewardship, Kriss Titus is a Registered Lobbyist in Florida, entrepreneur and recently launched the Kissimmee Tourism Education Association
