Lisa Lerch's Summary

Lisa Lerch operates Legal Equestrian, an Orange County, California based practice focused soley on equine related matters. Lisa has been in private practice for over 10 years and is licensed to practice in all California courts as well as the United States District Court (Southern District.)

Legal Equestrian was formed in 2007 to meet the unique needs of the equine industry in California. Nominee for the OC Business Journal's Excellence in Entrepreneurship Awards, March 18, 2009. Nominee for the OC Business Journal's 15th Annual Women in Business Awards, May 21, 2009.

Because California does not have a specific set of legal statutes that address the needs of the equine community, the courts rely on a variety of statutes including but not limited to contract law, tort law and probate law. An attorney who is familiar with the needs of the horse community and is up to date on current equine law development is able to better analyze equine breeding, buying, selling and management issues in a timely manner.

Parties often find themselves embroiled in litigation that is expensive, time-consuming and unpredictable simply because they entered into a transaction without a written agreement. The handshake deal does not work anymore.

Specialties

Legal Equestrian offers customized contracts, purchase sale agreements, lease agreements, boarding contracts, hauling release and waivers, liability releases, training contracts, business formations, horse insurance advice, authorization to treat, full service litigation of all horse related disputes, breeding contracts, horse injury disputes, and mortality disputes.
