Jo Lynn Deal, APR's Summary

With support from remarkable mentors and exceptional colleagues, I have enjoyed a career in organizational leadership and integrated marketing communications spanning 15 years. I am passionate about helping companies attract, engage and motivate audiences that are important to them. The strategies I develop focus on teaching small business owners how to use online communication for business growth, connect with the communities they serve, and generate leads. Catch me every week on my blog, Cup of Jo, where I focus on trending topics and fresh ideas in the world of small business marketing.

Join a great community with me at www.mymarketingcafe.com.

Featured in: Social Media Today, PR News, Project Eve, The Business Journal, American Express Open Forum, and The Examiner
