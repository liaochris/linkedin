Henry Chinea's Summary

Summary of professional experience.

27 years of creative juices flowing.
27 years of the wackiest ideas bouncing around my head.
2 years of freelance copywriting.
About one year of solid proofreading experience.

My goals:

Land a position as a Jr. Copywriter at an advertising agency. Learn the ropes from the seniors and the big shots. Learn enough to eventually start teaching others. Sell, sell, sell. Compete with the likes of fellow copywriters in the never-ending quest to craft the perfect portfolio.

OR...

Land a position as an agency's in-house proofreader and ensure that all of their work goes out into the world error-free.

Oh, and I'm also a DJ and huge musical nerd.

Specialties

The art of writing. The art of catching mistakes and inconsistencies. The art of convincing. The art of inviting people to think outside the norm. The art of environmental adaptation.
