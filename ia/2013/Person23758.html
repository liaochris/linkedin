Chris Silverwood's Summary

A Corporate Financier that truly empathises with the needs and demands placed upon his clients. Building a Corporate Finance Boutique with a National presence solely representing management teams interests in fund raising, restructuring, acquisition and disposal assignments.

Specialties

FUNDRAISING - Equity & Development Capital, Asset Based Lending, Acquisition & MBOs. Renewables & Technology Investments.
RESTRUCTURING - Negotiations with HMRC re historic debt restructuring/deferrals. Crisis cash management. Strategy development for buyouts from administration / pre-pack administrations.
ACQUISITIONS - Acquisition searches and lead advisory services.
DISPOSALS - Disposal of businesses to trade buyers or via a management buyout.
