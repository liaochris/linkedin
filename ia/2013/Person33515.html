Deborah Boily's Summary

I have been performing for more than 30 years both in theater and in Cabaret (club) venues. My specialty is musical theater, popular standards and French popular music: Edith Piaf, Jacques Brel, Charles Aznavour, Serge Lama, and others. I have created numerous one woman shows and have traveled the country performing them and on several intimate cruise lines. I have recorded 4 CDS: "The Song Remembers When" in 1997, "The French Collection" in 2000, "Thank You for the Music" in 2003 and "French Collection..Deux" in 2009. All are available through my website at www.deborahboily.com and www.thefrenchsinger.com. I also am a vocal coach to young adults and professional singers and teach Master Classes entitled "becoming a singing actor". Using my two French albums (see above) I developed a French teaching manual in Ebook form for high school and university French classes. It is entitled "Teaching French with La Chanson Francaise". It is available for purchase along with the CDs at www.thefrenchsinger.com.

Specialties

performing for Fund raisers, Galas, private events, in both French and English. Teaching and sharing my experience as a performer to students in private and group settings.
