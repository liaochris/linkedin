Andrea Rudolph's Summary

Andrea Rudolph is a Mindfulness Counselor and Life Coach, Founder and Director of Oryoki Zendo dedicated to raising awareness of mindful and sustainable living. She is an ordained Osho (Buddhist priest) with the Blue Mountain Lotus Society in Harrisburg, PA., and Tenzo (Cook). In her counseling practice she works with people of every race, religion, and age to bring harmony into their lives and relationships: victims of abuse and those who suffer from low self esteem, anxiety, grief and loss, eating disorders and body image problems. She is a motivational speaker, a wedding officiate, certified Mat Pilates Instructor, a professional dancer, choreographer and teacher for over thirty years, and holds a black belt in the Sui Nagari Do style of karate. She offers workshops in Mindful Eating and Living, Self Recovery, Meditation, and Relaxation, and Basic Self Defense.

Specialties

Mindfulness Counselor and Life Coach, Wedding Officiant, Meditation and Relaxation, seminars in Mindful Eating and Living, Health and Wellness, Life Management, Self Recovery, Opening to Creativity, and Basic Self Defense.
