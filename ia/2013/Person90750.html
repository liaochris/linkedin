MediatedSolutions LLC's Experience

Mediators - Bob Walsh and Mary Hirsen

Mediated Solutions, LLC

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Alternative Dispute Resolution industry

February 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months) River Forest, IL

At Mediated Solutions, LLC, we are dedicated to two objectives. First, we will guide you and your spouse through the difficulties of divorce or separation using mediation, an alternative to the traditional legal process. Second, we will help you and your spouse or partner resolve your disagreements in a way that gives each of you what you need for the next chapter in your life.

Mediation is about empowering couples to make critical decisions together, despite their differences. Attorneys Mary White Hirsen and Bob Walsh act as neutral co-mediators, using their years of professional experience and their mediation skills to guide couples through the mediation process.
