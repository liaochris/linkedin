Alexander Efros, MBA, CPA's Summary

Alexander Efros, MBA, CPA is the President and Founder of Athelon Wealth Management, LLC. Prior to founding the firm, Mr. Efros worked as an auditor in the Investment Management practice at PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP, a global leader in financial services.

During his time with PwC, Mr. Efros audited a wide range of mutual funds, hedge funds, employee benefit plans, and private equity funds belonging to the biggest and most reputable names in the industry. His clients included AXA Equitable, JPMorgan, Bank of New York Mellon, Ford Foundation, Franklin Templeton, and the Quantum Fund headed by renowned investor George Soros.

This unprecedented level of access and experience provided Mr. Efros with a comprehensive understanding of various complex investment vehicles, knowledge of the inner workings of world-class investment funds, and a unique perspective on the financial marketplace as a whole.

Mr. Efros currently holds the Certified Public Accountant (CPA) designation. He has also passed the Uniform Investment Advisor Law Exam and is FINRA Series 65 licensed.

Mr. Efros earned his M.B.A. in Professional Accounting from Rutgers Business School and his B.A. from the College of Staten Island. He is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) as well as the New York State Society of Certified Public Accountants (NYSSCPA).

Please feel free to contact Mr. Efros at 347-706-1415 or alexefros (at) athelonwealth.com.

Alexander Efros, MBA, CPA's Experience

President / Founder

Athelon Wealth Management, LLC

2011 – Present (2 years)

Athelon Wealth Management is an Independent Fee-Only Registered Investment Adviser based in New York City. We offer comprehensive investment management services tailored to fit the needs of each individual client. Our process involves creating highly customized portfolios to obtain the desired level of return while effectively managing risk through diversification, ongoing in-depth research, and periodic portfolio rebalancing.

In choosing investments for our clients, our methodology emphasizes transparency, liquidity, and value. This helps to mitigate risk, ensure that each portfolio is working most efficiently, and increase the chances of reaching client goals.

At Athelon Wealth Management, we are not brokers. Unlike other firms, we never accept referral fees or commissions from sales of financial products. Since our compensation comes directly from our clients, we have the freedom to be independent of all other investment companies and to offer only the best investment options available. The result is an unwavering commitment to our clients without conflicts of interest.

An essential part of our strategy is ongoing in-depth research. We work with world-class data suppliers that provide independent, detailed research on more than 325,000 investments. This information gives us an “inside look” into each financial instrument which allows us to manage risk more effectively. Our investment in research allows us to diversify portfolios across asset classes, sectors, geographies, and risk levels to maximize client returns.

Choosing a financial adviser is one of the most important decisions that you can make. Therefore, it is crucial to work with a firm you can trust – one which is dedicated to understanding and addressing your individual needs. At Athelon Wealth Management, your interests always come first.
