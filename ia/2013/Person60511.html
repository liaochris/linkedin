Jeanette Fast Redmond's Summary

Astute, Versatile, and Efficient Editor with a Decade of In-House Publishing Experience

• Strong familiarity with publishers’ goals, processes, and budget constraints in a rapidly changing industry--including XML workflow.
• Subject matter knowledge combined with incisive curiosity. Catholic Church knowledge, including experience with the new Roman Missal.
• Ability to see the polished potential within the rough draft, and the knowledge needed to get there.
• Holistic approach with unrelenting attention to detail.
• Efficient processes that produce high-quality results in less time.

PROFESSIONAL HIGHLIGHTS
• Wrote the USCCB Style Guide, which set the standard for editing books and other publications issued by the United States Conference of Catholic Bishops. The USCCB Style Guide has become a desk reference for a dozen Catholic publishers and organizations.

• Developed XML tagging structure and trained colleagues in its use to enable USCCB Publishing to repurpose content in print and digital editions (Kindle, ePub).

• Provided file composing services for the new translation of the Roman Missal.

• Edited 2005 Catholic Press Association 2nd-place award winner (Pastoral Ministry category): Forgiveness in International Politics.

• Provided editorial services to more than 30 successful doctoral candidates from 2001 to 2006.

• Served on committee to draft strategic plan that turned publishing office from a deficit-spending department into a $3 million operation.
