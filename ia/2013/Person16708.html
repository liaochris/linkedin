James Breko Brechney's Summary

Strong passion for radio, digital media and social networking.

Currently driving the #DIYrainbow movement online and offline. Also the afternoon voice of OXlive.com.au weekdays, producer and host of 'Jumping with James' on Sydney's Northside Radio 99.3FM.

Have been featured in various comedic capacities on Sunrise, Mumbrella, Nova 969's Fitzy & Wippa, Q&A, The Bolt Report, Channel V, Australian Idol, World Movies Channel & Ready Steady Cook.

Specialties: social media, content creation, media production, radio: panel operation; playlist design; obtaining interviews from Australian based comics and celebrities; live to air interviews in studio and over the phone; audience interaction; preparing and delivering weather and traffic reports; and recording stings and commercials.

I also MC and throw super dooper parties in and around the Surry Hills area of Sydney.
