Kelli Richards' Summary

Digital Music and Entertainment Consultancy
BIG VISIONS * BIG CONNECTIONS * BIG RESULTS

With over 20+ years of experience in the entertainment and technology industries, creating powerful connections is MY every day success story. I've been driving the convergence of Silicon Valley, Hollywood and Madison Ave as a super-connector, strategist, innovator, rainmaker and thought leader for over two decades.

I'm best known for having launched digital music at Apple, where I led all digital music initiatives for 10 years. I am also a veteran producer of mega concerts, awards shows and celebrity fundraisers. I’m a former A&R exec at EMI and former Board of Governors of the San Francisco Chapter of NARAS (the Grammys), among other board seats. I’ve co-authored several books, including "The Art of Digital Music," and the best-selling "The Magic and Moxie of Apple: An Insider's View."

At All Access Group, we focus on connecting the dots between entertainment and technology, working with disruptive technologies and emerging business models to enable broader distribution and new revenue streams for premium content. I guide my clients to use leading-edge technologies and connections to get their products to more people in simple, revenue-driven ways.

As a coach to high achieving innovators, entrepreneurs, celebrities & established artists I help them to explore new ways to experience more freedom& fulfillment and to identify what comes next in their lives, and create a blueprint/game plan for accelerating the completion of their goals & visions. I'm personally mentored by two of the most successful authors, change agents & coaches in the world -- Alan Cohen and Alan Weiss.

Working in partnership with my clients I create out-of-the-box solutions, insights and strategies AND the most critical connections to help them implement their projects -- leveraging the power of technology and high-powered alliances.

Specialties

Digital music/media entrepreneur, strategic partnerships, business development, rainmaker/dealmaker, branded content strategy, digital distribution, develop new business models and revenue streams, deal sourcing, established artists. Working with disruptive technologies and emerging business models in music and entertainment is the common theme of my work. I personally mentor artists, celebs, and innovators. Over 25 years producing award shows, concerts, celebrity fundraisers, compilations.
