Gill White's Summary

I am a freelance Creative and Writer/Director. I specialise in interactive design and large scale site specific audio visual and interactive experiences for clients in the commercial sectors of Arts, Advertising and Heritage.

Originally trained as an animator my creative adventures were sparked in Scandinavia, after several years working overseas I returned to my native Scotland. With over fifteen years commercial design experience, I have a fresh multidisciplinary approach to design and story telling, with a love for discovering new immersive worlds to explore.

A conceptual thinker with a good understanding of corporate philosophies and marketing strategy. Enthusiastic and inquisitive with a fresh multi-disciplinary approach to interactive storytelling and brand building in the online/offline world.

I work independently and collaborate as a creative and producer with talent from a wide range of creative disciplines.

Recent clients include:
Bite Global , Informatica, Sony Ericsson,The Museum of Liverpool, Next, Pilsner Urquell, ClashMusic, Scottish FA, Manchester United, Huggies, Office Shoes, Channel 4, Volvo, Carling, Tennent's, Highland Spring, Tesco Personal Finance as well as short film work and collaborations with Video and Sonic Group - Another Space.

Specialties: - Conceptualisation of branded content platforms and campaigns
- Manage the design process for large site developments and online campaigns.
- Growing design teams.
- User centered interactive design.
- Engaging and retaining audiences in dynamic, user-generated data-driven websites.
