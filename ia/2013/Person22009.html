Char Evans King's Summary

One of the biggest decisions in a person's life is to buy or sell real estate. My passion is to help them do both. I'm blessed to be a full time, full service Realtor and represent both buyers and sellers in Central Ohio.

I graduated from Ohio University, receiving a Bachelor of Business Administration, majoring in Marketing and Interpersonal Communications. Once I graduated, I entered the retail industry. My work in retail helped me develop skills that have been invaluable in my realtor career. Being able to listen to the needs of my client, manage projects and people, multi-task in a fast paced environment and learn how to display and market a product are all skills that have translated well to the real estate industry.

I was fortunate to train with a very good mentor for over three years and expand my business with the Columbus Realty Team. I'm honored to say I've been an annual recipient of the Ohio Association of Realtors President’s Sales Award and a member of the Master Sales Society. I'm a member of the Columbus Realty Team and honored to be a part of HER Realtors.

When I'm not selling houses, I enjoy spending time with family.

Specialties: Buyers, Sellers, Short Sales, Certified Relocation Specialist, Master Sales Society
