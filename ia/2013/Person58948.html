Jonathon Aslay's Summary

Jonathon Aslay aka Your Relationship Confidant: Graduate of the University of Dating. For the past 5 years I have embarked on a new venture as a Matchmaker & Relationship Coach for the Woman of Tomorrow. After 20 successful years developing meaningful relationships, offering corporations and individuals protection from loss and harm, and crafting strategic plans for security and peace of mind, I decided to create RelationshipConfidant.com. My prior career accomplishments, years of personal dating experience and listening to the stories of those around me who were seeking love, gave me the inspiration to offer a unique perspective on the world of online dating for the busy professional. I have created a practice around my passion for relationship building and putting people at ease, with a focus on all things related to dating.

I’ve helped countless people find that spark and find true love in a smile that inspires them to laughter that takes their breath away. I love what I do and nothing makes me happier than hearing those three joyous words – "Jonathon, he proposed!" or "She's the One."

Specialties

As a coach, I listen and seek to gather knowledge before ever delivering concise and respectful feedback to my clients. I also offer workshops and on:
• The 9 Mistakes to Avoid on a First Date
• 37 Great Places to Meet Single Men
• How to Become Your Own Matchmaker
• The Male Point of View – Mr. Right, versus, Mr. Right Now (Tonight)
