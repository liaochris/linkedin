Brad Roldan's Summary

Brad has 15 years of experience in networking and security solutions, having advanced Hewlett Packard’s global IP network with IPSec VPNs, and pioneering metro-Ethernet services at Telseon. At Covad Communications, Brad drove transformational technologies of traditional ATM-based DSL services to carrier-grade IP MPLS VPNs and Ethernet backhaul services. Currently, Brad provides executive-level consulting for telecom carriers in the United States.
