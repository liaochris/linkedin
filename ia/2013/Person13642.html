Beth Ann Locke's Summary

I believe that every person has an interesting story, if only we take the time to ask and learn.

Throughout my life I have enjoyed talking with people, listening and learning about what matters to them. Connecting people to others with shared ideas, dreams, or values – whether one to one or in groups – is something I relish.

I take pleasure in being a catalyst and am happy to celebrate the strengths and achievements of others. I enjoy asking, "What can we do together?" and then achieving the goal – by building connections or bridges or getting teams "unstuck."

This is why I consider fundraising to be my calling.

When people describe me, the words they use most are generous, creative, energetic, passionate, smart, thoughtful and fun.

I enjoy working with donors and helping nonprofits create strong and loyal supporters.

Let me know how we can work together!

Specialties: • Strong understanding of donor audiences and how to approach, engage and solicit via social, in person and through writing
• Solicitation of high net-worth donors and securing six-figure + gifts
• Creative adaptation of giving with social and web
• Success in individual, corporate, foundation and event fundraising
• Compelling storytelling and communication skills
• No limitations using technology to achieve goals
• Expert at Raiser's Edge and best practices for use with donors and prospects
