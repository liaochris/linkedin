Cornell Colbert, LBC, CMQ/OE's Summary

I am a American Society of Quality (ASQ) Certified Manager of Quality/Organizational Excellence, a Society of Manufacturing Engineer's (SME) Lean Bronze Certified - Sensei, a National Restaurant Association Certified ServSafe instructor and Registered Proctor, an executive coach, business consultant and quality management professional with proven bottom line results.

I achieve results by implementation of proven quality, process and continuous improvement methodologies, strategic planning and organizational initiatives, development of KRI to determine organizational improvement, and establishing and monitoring customer/supplier relations.

As an executive coach, I help individuals maximize their full potential both personally and organizationally. My scope encompasses personal coaching, on the job training, consultation and seminars presentation that can have regional or global focus in a variety of service and industrial settings.

My expertise is recognized by the ASQ, SME, the Association for Manufacturing Excellence (AME), the Shingo Prize for Operational Excellence, and the National Restaurant Association.
