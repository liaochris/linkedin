Michael E. Perez's Summary

I entered the professional field of graphic and web design in December 2000 and have enjoyed the evolving trends in art & design ever since. I have extensive newsroom and agency experience and take an artist's approach to design.

After earning my associate degree in digital media design, I spent the majority of my career designing layouts, infographics and illustrations for news media. Notable events I've covered are the 9/11 terror attacks, the space shuttle Columbia tragedy and the passing of Pope John Paul II.

In 2006, I enrolled at Texas State University, where I earned a bachelor's degree in communication design.

In 2008, I entered the PR field, offering creative services, as well as social media branding for clients ranging from fuel pricing analysts to home furnishing designers.

Specialties

Adobe Creative Suite software, front-end web design, news page and infographic design, marketing collateral (print and digital), social media and blogging.
