Bill Eisenhauer's Summary

I am a web technology specialist with an entrepreneurial mind. As such, I'm fascinated with the construction and business of the web.

On the construction side, I build websites with lean, semantic markup, CSS inspired by Zeldman, Cederholm, Meyer, and others, and unobtrusive Javascript featuring libraries like JQuery, backbone.js, underscore.js, and others. I've built sites in Java, but have focused my work on Ruby on Rails in the past 6 years.

I believe in writing beautiful, maintainable code—every line purposeful, and every method focused and concise. People want to maintain my code because its easy to understand.

I know how to create findable websites that are highly search engine optimized. I'm versed in conversion study and the virtues of effective web copy.

I'm fluent in every social media platform and know how to conduct successful relationship marketing within each.

I have always been able to bring to bear uncommonly effective verbal and written communication skills. This has opened doors to opportunities beyond pure programming. I believe these skills are uncommon for the stereotypical software developer.

I have 26 years in the software development industry and a breadth of experience. I've programmed mainframes, PCs, and devices. I've led teams. I've watched servers. I've made junior developers, teams, and products better.

I'm still learning and incredibly curious.

Specialties

web development, ruby on rails, java, application architecture and design, UML, business communication and strategy, social media, inbound marketing, agile project management
