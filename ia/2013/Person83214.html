Publication Services of America

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry

February 2006 – January 2009 (3 years) West Palm Beach, Florida Area

In the director role I soon discovered my very strong passion for well designed backend workflows and processes. Our corporation created about two full magazines a day. We were in charge of 5 different monthly, bimonthly, and quarterly titles with 72 different markets, which each market had different features and stories. The print production was rolling with a fantastic team, but online distribution was very much non-existent. I came to the company with a big task, make every magazine available online, and make one solid brand for each of the five titles. I very successfully created and set the strategic development for syndication and aggregation. Implemented amazing backend technology that automated the publication of multi-photo editorials literally with a drag-and-drop approach where metadata was at the core of the engine. I set the backend structure and directed several developers to make 72 websites consume and syndicate 50+ monthly stories to each specific market.

Here is where the fun part began, I was also in charge of several collateral materials which won several design awards.
