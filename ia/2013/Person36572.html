Donald Trosper's Summary

Donald Trosper, BSIM, CPC; Video Producer, Mobile Device Websites and Social Media Marketing Consultant, Author and Speaker. A veteran of applied computer technology with 5 decades of experience as a business consultant, systems designer, programing and technical adviser.
