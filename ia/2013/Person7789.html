Angel Batalla's Summary

With a never-satisfied curiosity for learning I am constantly striving to realize both personal and professional growth and continually develop solutions where none seemed possible, to achieve the greatest possible outcomes.

As it has been in the past, my focus will continue to be offering value to my partners, employers and clients as a C-Suite executive, member on the Board of Advisors, Board of Directors or by advising and consulting in regards to Board and Corporate Governance, Operations, Supply Chain Management, Acquisition Strategy and Integration.

I have two highly regarded qualifications endorsed by the International Corporate Governance Network and the Global Corporate Governance Forum: Accredited Director and Expert in Corporate Governance.

I consider myself a citizen of the World, having lived and worked in Spain, Nigeria, Portugal, Italy, Germany, Belgium, UK, France and the United States. This international experience, together with my innate empathy, interest and capability to understand conflicting points of view and behavioral drivers, enables me to live naturally with diversity. This has allowed me to address, resolve and bridge cross border issues to effectively deliver complex value chain restructurings.

I have built a productive and reputable international career with the focus, credibility and resolve to drive performance improvement and operational restructuring of highly challenged Global businesses and reposition them into viable market competitors.

Specialties

Value Chain, Sales, Marketing, R&D, Product Development, Six Sigma, Operations, Performance Improvement, International Business, Restructuring Programs, B2c & B2B Retail, Branded Goods & Services, Consumer, Pharmaceutical & OTC, Medical & Professional, M&A Operations, Financial Optimization, Diversity Leadership.
