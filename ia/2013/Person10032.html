Art Flores' Experience

REALTOR, CDPE, SFR & R.E.S.C.U.E. Certified

FIRST TEAM SNS REAL ESTATE

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Real Estate industry

May 2008 – Present (5 years 7 months) Diamond Bar, CA

I'm a licensed Realtor in Southern California. I've been licensed since 1988. I've been through two down-turns in the Real Estate market. I came to First Team in 2008 because they were the experts in selling distressed properties. It is a continual education process for this market and anyone who sells Real Estate must either keep up with this continual education or they become ineffective agents and they are no help to the clients they are trying to help. I've become an expert in selling distressed properties and also in helping my clients, friends and family navigate this sea of information regarding distressed properties. Whether it is a short sale or referral to a non-profit HUD approved counselor, attorney, CPA and various other professionals working in the Real Estate industry. I use my Facebook page to post specific information to the Southern California Real Estate foreclosure market to assist my clients, friends and family.

I believe that to become an expert you must first learn. I started this by our continual education in our office and at various seminars, symposiums and conferences. Next I obtained my SFR (short sales and Forclosure resource) designation from NAR. Next I became a certified R.E.S.C.U.E. certified agent through more training. Just to be able to help clients, friends and family work with a HUD approved non-profit counselor to assist in applying for a loan modification. Finally, I obtained my CDPE designation because they are the real experts in distressed properties and the lenders and servicers recognize one or all of these designations. But education alone does not make you an expert. Add over 25 years of working in the Real Estate industry as a Realtor, loan broker, loan banker and at a bank and the many successful closings of short sale and regular transactions and you find somebody with not only the education but the experience to help out his clients, friends and family.
