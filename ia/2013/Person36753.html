Donna Gilliland's Summary

I am an award-winning instructor with a 20 year background in professional speaking and business applications training. I help companies and individuals master and use Office and Social Media technologies.

If I am not teaching about social media, Microsoft Office, Google+ for Business, iPad productivity, I am writing about what I teach. I am a regular writer for OfficePro magazine, IAAP (International Association of Administrative Professionals) publication.

Teaching others how to master Microsoft Office, Google and Social Media technologies to grow their business, careers and enhance their personal life is my passion.

SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING and MANAGEMENT
While you might be well versed in social media marketing, you simply may not have the resources to be effective. I help businesses in Facebook Marketing, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest and Google Plus for Business.

SOCIAL MEDIA WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT
Before you place your employees in the social trenches make certain you have properly equipped them to become your online voice. Training is key to understanding and successfully implementing social media marketing.

OFFICE TECHNOLOGIES
It's hard to find any business now that isn't using Microsoft Office and/or Google Applications. However, the majority are never tapping into the full potential of the business applications they are using. That is where targeted training in Microsoft Office, Google and Mobile applications is key.

Software versions change but the human nature of people is to resist the change and continue to work with what they are familiar with from their older versions. Targeted training in Microsoft Office and Google business applications helps increase productivity.

iPad Productivity Training
The mobile workforce has become the operating norm for many but the masses still aren't using their mobile devices in the most productive way. Customized training in iPad productivity is available onsite or onsite.
