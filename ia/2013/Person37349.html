Doug Stryker's Summary

Currently available for contract or permanent employment. Senior-level advertising professional with 23+ years' experience in large and small agencies, building and leading creative teams while driving all phases of print, broadcast, and online projects for regional, national and global brands. Excellent time, budget and resource management abilities.

I'm an art director who can draw. I remember how to cut amberlith. My lungs probably still have Spray Mount residue in them.

But I didn't stop with the stone knives and bear claws of the advertising world. I'm a new media aficionado; an early-adopter, highly responsive to culture and always seeking new avenues for creative exploration — casting a wider net to deliver big ideas. I enjoy inspiring and creating authentic content which is media-agnostic and converts consumers into brand evangelists.
