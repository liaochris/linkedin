Nicolas Solberg's Summary

I love visual design regardless of the intended medium. I have many years of creative design under my belt, including extensive experience in both web and print design. Additionally I have experience with photography, audio, video and podcast production. I use Adobe Creative Suite exclusively for all current design work, but know many older applications, and am always eager to expand my knowledge and skills – including social media tools and technologies. I am also well versed in project management, client negotiations, telecommuting and have a solid reputation for BOTH reliability and creativity.

Specialties: Branding, web, print, logo, illustration, product & icon design.
