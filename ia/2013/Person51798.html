Harrison Hoffman's Summary

I am currently a Lead Product Marketing Manager, leading a team focused on enthusiast events for Windows First Party Apps & Services at Microsoft.

Previously, I was a Product Marketing Manager for Microsoft SkyDrive. Before coming to Microsoft, I wrote The Web Services Report, a blog covering Web 2.0 for CNET's Blog Network and was a co-founder of LiveSide.net, a technology news website covering Microsoft's consumer web products. I graduated from the University of Miami, majoring in Business Management and Organization and Computer Science.
