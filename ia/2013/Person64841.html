Joan Canning's Summary

Joan Canning established her own HR consulting company, HR Advocate, LLC in 2006 to offer businesses a sincere, personalized approach to any of their concerns or needs relating to the crucial employer - employee relationship.

HR Advocate offers the following exclusive programs to small-and medium-sized companies:

• Catalytic Coaching - Ending the performance review
• the HR Advocate Help Desk with HR Resource Library
• and Straight-Talk: Management & Compliance Training

With these and other tailored services, Joan guides employers through the complexities of employment regulations, while helping them meet the needs of their employees—their most important asset, and greatest expense. Joan uses the information gathered during the HR Power Hour to work together with employers to develop a personalized HR action plan, one that will not only enhance organizational performance, but also reduce the risk of employee relations volatility.

The HR Advocate Help Desk provides a lifeline to employers, where they get customized answers to their HR questions. Straight-Talk: Management & Compliance Training provides employers with comprehensive, applicable training programs to enhance workplace culture and goals.

Personal

Specializing in Human Resource Management, Joan earned Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Business Administration and has also earned the professional Health Insurance Associate designation from the Health Insurance Association of America. Joan is a licensed employee benefits professional and member of the Society of Human Resource Management (SHRM) and Certified Network Professionals (CNP). Prior to starting her HR consulting business, Joan worked for over 15 years in several HR and Employee Benefits roles at Owens-Illinois, Butler Capital Advisors, Sky Insurance, and Savage & Associates. Joan lives in Toledo, Ohio where she cares for her three rescued dogs, is an avid reader of American and World History, and actively participates in the community.

Specialties

Health Care Reform Consulting, Employment Law Compliance Audits, Human Resource Process Consulting, Human Resource Help Desk for HR Professionals and Business Owners, Employee Handbook Design and Audit, Anti-Harassment & Discrimination Training, Supervisor and Management Compliance Training
