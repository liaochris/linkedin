Alex Barreto's Summary

Branding is not a logo, a website, a package, or a name, though these are important, corporate branding strategy is about bringing the organization in line with its long-term vision, unique market position, and unlocking the leadership potential within the organization.

What I do is to help bring your business strategy to life, integrating your brand strategies through your company at every point of public contact and with every type of communication.

I like to know my client, my client's market and my client's clients, I do a lot of research before I come out with solutions tailored to you.

Marketing is branding and branding is marketing,

I have a multicultural background, speak three languages, lived in three countries, and have been educated in engineering and design, and grew with technology. All of these gives me the insight to understand a wide range of markets and come up with appropriate solutions.

Specialties

Corporate branding, marketing communications, brand design, print design, web and interactive design, art direction.
