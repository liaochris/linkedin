John Bove's Summary

Absolute devotion to the brand, the user experience and the bottom-line. To me, getting it right means asking the right questions, working with the right people and knowing from experience how much time and effort a project will truly require. Extremely clean design sensibility, a user enabler rather than a traditional visual designer.


• Designer, perfectionist, collaborator and generalist in all things web.
• Over ten years designing and building web sites and web applications.
• Thorough participatory knowledge of the web design product cycle, from business requirements to release.
• 5+ years experience working within the Agile development environment.
• Architected and designed an extensible international site framework that has been seamlessly deployed to 18 countries around the world.

Specialties: Interface Design, User Experience, Visual Design, Design Management
