Matt Steen's Summary

I love the local church, and serving church leaders as they pursue their calling through coaching and consulting, and Ministry Briefing.

Accomplishments: Created a monthly subscription based resource for church leaders. Designed a coaching platform serving church planters and established church pastors. Developed and implemented a multi-faceted ministry strategy for an urban church plant resulting in unprecedented engagement with the local community. Created a new culture for youth and children’s ministries at a mid-size church, tripling the size of the leadership team and growing the ministry in depth and breadth. Helped develop the systems and leadership for a mega-church’s middle school ministry that tripled in size during its first year.

Current Desire: To serve a church who knows its identity and is pursuing its vision of Kingdom expansion. My desire is to use the skills and abilities that God has gifted me with to make that vision a reality. My goal is to serve alongside people I love, build teams that embrace and engage in a challenging vision, and see God glorified through the sum of all our parts.
