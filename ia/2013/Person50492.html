Gretchen Denton's Summary

I am a liason between users and development. I have extensive practical design experience to ensure that the software being developed is easy to use and take the guess work out of what users need to accomplish their goals.

My current title is Sr. Human Centered Designer, I have over 20 years of experience in usability, business analysis, and interaction design including: 3D modeling, virtual reality, interactive multimedia, video game development, corporate branding, and web enabled applications.

I understand multi-tiered agile development environments, managed designers, projects, and manage expectations. I am effective in communicating with users, product management and development. I enjoy working synergistically in a professional and creative team environment.
