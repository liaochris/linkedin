Bob Hertzog's Summary

Located in the heart of Northeast Phoenix/Scottsdale, Summit Home Consultants is a full service real estate brokerage serving home buyers and sellers throughout the Metro Phoenix Market. We have a team of Phoenix short sale agents in place to help you through this tumultuous market.

Finding a reputable Phoenix short sale agent to handle your needs can be a long and tedious process. The Summit Home Consultants team takes great pride in the many relationships that we have developed over the past 10 years in the Real Estate business throughout the Metro-Phoenix market.

Our Motto: We promise to deliver such outstanding service that whomever you come in contact with will thank you for referring them to our team.

Our team of Phoenix short sale agents knows that there is an art to working in today’s challenging real estate market. That’s why, in 2008, we took it upon ourselves to jump headfirst into representing buyers and sellers in the Phoenix short sale real estate arena. Since that time, our Phoenix short sale agent team has helped several buyers purchase short sales and REO (bank-owned) homes, and helped countless sellers avoid the devastating effects of foreclosure, mainly through the use of short sales.

Managing a real estate transaction is not rocket science. That being said, there are many steps to the process, and having an experienced Phoenix short sale agent looking out for your best interests is critical to having a smooth transaction. There are many details that can fall through the cracks, and several items that may need to be fine-tuned and finessed in a real estate transaction.

Our Phoenix short sale agents will help you through your home buying or selling journey because we are experienced, we have been through both good times and bad in the real estate market, and most importantly, WE CARE.

To learn more about our company, please visit www.forsalephoenixhomes.com

Bob Hertzog's Experience

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry

2008 – Present (5 years)

Bob founded Summit Land Consultants in 2001, and grew the company to be one of the top land brokerages in Phoenix, closing over $250 million in real estate transactions.

In light of the recent downturn in the land market, Bob created Summit Home Consultants in 2007. Since that time, he has been involved in closing countless Phoenix short sale and "traditional" transactions. His main focus is that of a Phoenix short sale agent, helping his clients through this very challenging real estate market that we are faced with today.

In recent months, several media outlets have turned to Bob for his insight on the current real estate market, particularly Phoenix short sales.

He is a native of Phoenix, specifically North Phoenix and Scottsdale. He graduated from Sunnyslope High School in 1987, and Arizona State University with a B.S. in Business Management in 1991.

Bob is a third-generation Phoenix short sale agent, has witnessed the ups and downs of the market during his lifetime, and has very close relationships with some of the top Real Estate Attorneys, Mortgage Brokers, and Title Companies in the Valley.

Bob has been married to his wife Kim for 18 years. They have two children; Kaitlyn is 16 and attends Chaparral High School. Hailey is 9 and attends Cherokee Elementary.

Bob enjoys spending time with his family, playing golf, camping, blogging, and helping his team of experts to be the best they can be.
