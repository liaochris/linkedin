Benjamin Parisot's Projects

  • Nike+ Kinect Training

    • septembre 2010 à octobre 2012
    Membres de l'équipe : Benjamin Parisot, Ben Gonshaw, duan evans, Brian Snyder, Harinder Bajwa, James Chorley, Patrick Lendrum, Xavier JACOB, Taraneh Khosrowshahi

    Together with our partners we created the most responsive, most authentic athletic training experience available in the home.

    We also made all the videos, TV and Cinema spots, two companion apps on iOS and Windows Phone as well as the box art - in fact almost everything to do with creating a pinnacle experience.

    The game had its own Xbox 360 bundle and was the top selling Kinect game over holiday 2012.

  • Nike Women's Marathon SF Race Webapp

    • mai 2013 à novembre 2013
    Membres de l'équipe : Benjamin Parisot, James Burton, James Chorley, Franklin Nnah, Jon Flowers, Ion Ionascu, Richard Stephenson, Hollie Dobson, Karen Chiu, Tom Maton

    Nike Women’s Marathon in San Francisco, California is the largest women’s marathon in the world. AKQA were tasked with developing a web-based training app for the full and half marathon with heavy Facebook integration.
