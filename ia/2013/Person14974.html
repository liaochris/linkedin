Brian Hill's Summary

My name is Brian L. Hill. As a consultant, I help buildings and businesses achieve more from less. For me, that means achieving greater results while reducing wasted effort and resources.

There is a saying that “hindsight is 20/20.” Through my experience in the investigation of thousands of buildings involved in construction defect lawsuits, some clear patterns have emerged. While I personally do not believe that construction defects can be completely eliminated, there are ways to minimize their impact on the built environment.

After starting my first business over 25 years ago, most of my career has involved working with small professional service firms. I’ve seen first-hand what works and what doesn’t. To succeed in today’s marketplace a business needs to be agile, empathetic, efficient, innovative and willing to leverage the right tools.

Specialties: Forensic investigation and analysis, construction quality management, building performance evaluation, code and standards research, data analysis, presentation design, business strategy, content marketing, copywriting, digital media, social networking, business development, client relationship management
