OUR STORY

Bill Lacy, a seasoned river guide, and wife Maleika Lacy, a web and marketing consultant, met under serendipitous circumstances. Bill introduced Maleika to the outdoors and together they fell in love through their many adventures.

Soon after their marriage they sought out to combine their livelihoods and passion for the outdoors. So they settled in Cambria California and followed the American dream of opening a mom and pop outdoor retail shop. And that is how River Addiction was born.

ABOUT RIVER ADDICTION

River Addiction is a concept for experiencing the outdoors through water. Whether it’s camping by the lake, playing on the river, or getting out in the ocean, the idea is dress to get wet.

The business’s foundation is to provide outdoor enthusiasts with quality brand named products focusing on paddlesports, hiking and camping. River Addiction only represents companies that stand behind their products. The end result is to get outdoors, be safe, have fun and become addicted!
