Joseph Conti's Summary

Architect and designer of an artful-science endeavor; within and outside the strict boundaries of architecture. The design studio is based on a shared vision with the client & consultants. It seeks to discover and shape ideas that promote, beauty, harmony and balance. The main interest of my work lies in seeing the possibilities beyond what is present, obvious or known.

An area of immense inspiration for the work at JCA is a respect for this planet we reside on, and the way it was designed. We believe nature provides an endless array of design and sustainable design solutions for us to utilize and serve our clients. It forms the basis in which we can begin to develop 'meaningful' solutions. Nature also holds an endless source of inspiration and science that provides clues to apply to any project, big, small, new or existing, even outside the strict boundaries of architecture. It's about seeing the possibilities . . .

Project experience includes: residential, commercial, transportation facilities, education, healthcare, banks, restaurant's, post offices, industrial, spec. development, urban infill projects, sustainable building projects, site planning, passive design strategies, and documentation of existing infrastructure. In addition, some of the new areas of work include concept planning and production design for the entertainment industry.


Additional information about the practice can be found at www.Josephcontiarchitecture.com.

Member of the American Institute of Architects.
NCARB, Certified (National Council of Architectural Registration Boards)
AIA-MN Magazine Committee Member and contributing Writer
University of Minnesota, School of Architecture - Mentor Program

Specialties: Consulting, Conceptual Design, and Sustainable design strategies.
