Lauren Osterstock's Summary

Portfolio: http://laurenosterstock.crevado.com/

Currently art directing half of the creative campaigns at ShoeDazzle. Overseeing all details of my photoshoots from model casting to location selection including final layout and design. Graphic design for lookbooks, emails and site assets.

Experience in graphic design for web, print and website design (design, not programming) for eight years. My fashion e-commerce background brings special insight pertaining to conversion, navigation and UI design. My fashion background allows me to absorb a brand image quickly and work within that specific visual vernacular to create a cohesive and consistent brand experience.

Client List:
ShoeDazzle.com, BB Dakota, Dakota Collective, Billabong USA, Too Faced Cosmetics, gorjana Jewelry, Venice Organics, CoutureCandy.com and more.
