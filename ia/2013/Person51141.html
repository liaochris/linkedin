George’s palette of voicing credits grows nearly every day. Since entering the business. He has voiced television and radio commercials, non-broadcast narrations, in-store announcements, and on hold messaging for Disney Casting, Hilton, Wachovia, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, CarMax, Verizon, Double Tree, Powells Books, Embassy Suites, Whole Foods, the Tampa Tribune, the NCAA, EA Sports, and many others.
