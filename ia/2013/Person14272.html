Bill Finkelstein's Summary

I conceptualized, convinced the executive team, and headed the development effort that launched the world's first Internet Banking website. We launched at Wells Fargo on May 18, 1995 and beat next bank to market by over 6 months.

Experienced, hands on, Sr. executive, consultant and speaker with a proven track record of success, now in semi-retirement.

I am available for selected, high impact speaking opportunities, consulting engagements, or longer term opportunities. Contact me for more information at bill@williamfinkelstein.com see also: http://williamfinkelstein.com.

After covering over 25,000 miles in 7 years aboard our Valiant 50 Sailboat "Raptor Dance", my wife, Mary and I are back in Sonoma County California near Santa Rosa. See http://raptordance.com for more information on our cruise, photos, and travel log.

Specialties

Specialties include: Technology and Operations management Sr. executive and consultant with a proven track record of delivering high quality results with low cost and risk. An expert at maximizing the return on enterprise technology investments. Recently focused on Internet enablement strategies and the design and architecture of successful Internet and IT sites. An expert in maximizing usability of technology, especially Internet enabled functions - especially in the consumer market segments.
