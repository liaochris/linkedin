Michael Sandburg, MPA's Projects

  • SupremeObserver.com

    Team Members: Michael Sandburg, MPA

    Developed and maintain SupremeObserver.com, a website conceived as a public research tool related to the U. S. Supreme Court. This website offers a valuable resource for the study of Supreme Court cases. Website visitors can read or listen to recordings of thousands of oral arguments made before the Supreme Court. Some of these hour-long recordings date back over half a century. Many are as entertaining as they are instructive. The site also publishes the text of many thousands of Supreme Court decisions and dissenting opinions. Upcoming cases are summarized, and access is provided to many source document briefs. Search over 30,000 cases by party name, topic, docket number, citation, or US Reports volume number.
