Lee Gallagher's Summary

Lee Gallagher is an Author, Speaker, and Founder of Arts & Analytics, a company that is passionate about growing patronage for the performing arts through leveraging predictive sciences and technology. He has recently published the must read book, “Precision Marketing: Maximizing Revenue Through Relevance” as well as a contributing author of "Advice From The Top".

Lee is a recognized authority, global speaker, and practitioner on improving top line results by implementing data driven marketing practices. He has conducted numerous in market proof points that demonstrate the increased value and revenue associated with implementing Precision Marketing strategies.

His book and research papers have been discussed on The Street, The Hopkinson Report, Fortune, and CBS News.

Specialties: Marketing Strategy & Execution • Thought Leadership • Marketing Measurement & Metrics, including ROI • Business Management, including P/L Responsibility • Strategic Partners/Alliance Formation • Mentorship • Multi-Unit Collaboration, Social Meida, Blogging, Predictive Analytics
