Kim Warren: FSMS, FStratPS's Summary

Most recently published on Kindle "The Trouble with Strategy ..." - a fundamental attack on the appalling state of the strategy field, and what might be done about it (free Kindle readers available for any device).
I write and supply learning materials and whole courses on a powerful, rigorous approach to the strategic management of organizations of any kind - strategy dynamics (see http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strategy_dynamics and www.strategydynamics.com).
The aim is to replace the abstract and unreliable methods used in strategy teaching and practice with this rigorous and practical approach. The weaknesses of current strategy methods are widely accepted and written about by both academics and top consulting firms in the field.

Specialities: Mapping and modelling strategy and performance, for all organizations, of all sizes, in all sectors, at all stages of development, (including government, public services and voluntary).

February 1996 – Present (17 years 9 months)

After teaching strategic management at London Business School for 6 years, it became clear that the strategy tools being taught - not just at this top-10 global business school, but all others too - were of limited value and relevance. They help with choosing strategic 'position', but do not address how to manage strategy over time. I spent 5 years developing Strategy Dynamics to solve this problem, and set up this business to publish and promote it. This now takes up most of my time, though I still teach at London Business School.
