Mark Brydges, CPC, CTS, JCTC's Summary

► RECOGNIZED PERFORMANCE!
► OUTSTANDING RESULTS!
► EXCEEDING EXPECTATIONS!

The Julmark Group is an independent consultancy specializing in Human Capital Solutions. Service offerings include Recruitment & Talent Acquisition, Job Transition & Career Coaching, and Corporate Outplacement. These cross functional core competencies are powerfully integrated to maximize performance.

As an active recruiter, founder Mark Brydges has placed literally hundreds of professionals. The practical "real-world" knowledge gained through such recruiting success forms the foundation in developing leading edge job search strategies; not only for those facing transition, but for those realizing the necessity to proactively “position” in an unpredictable and rapidly changing job world. Similarly, his in-depth career coaching and counseling experience adds great value when evaluating candidates. Going beyond just the qualifications and understanding the more intrinsic qualities of a candidate can make all the difference in recruiting a “great hire” and long term employee.

Ever striving to provide the value-added guidance deserving of every Julmark client, Mark is among a minority dually-certified through the NAPS as a Certified Personnel Consultant/CPC and Certified Temporary-Staffing Specialist/CTS. He is also certified through the CPAD Network as both a Job & Career Transition Coach/JCTC and a Job & Career Development Coach/JCDC.

PLEASE NOTE: In addition to Information Technology, successful placements have been made in Engineering, Sales & Marketing, Business & Finance, Human Resources and Procurement. Job Transition & Career Coaching services (including Outplacement) apply to professionals of varying backgrounds, specialties and levels to include more Senior/Executive personnel.

(✱✱✱In no way affiliated with New Mexico based The Julmark Group, a supplier of personalized stationery products).
