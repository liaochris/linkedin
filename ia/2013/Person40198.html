Justin Kent's Summary

An Orlando native, Kent developed a love of technology at an early age, before a lightning strike catalyzed his passion into a drive to change the world. He earned two degrees from MIT and a patent on a futuristic DJ turntable before setting out on a journey through a handful of dot-com startups. His experience on the web evolved into an obsession with apps before CP+B recruited him as their first mobile-specific developer. He had a hand in virtually every app launch for the agency and his work led to a number of awards. A few noteworthy projects include a crunch-powered video game, a smile detecting billboard, and a beat-driven dancing cube of Jell-O. Now he's serving as Lead Scientist at Modernizing Medicine, creating iPad apps for future-facing doctors. When away from the office, he serenades his wife with karaoke and DJs with his iPhone.

Specialties

iPhone / iPad native application programming, patents and intellectual property, project management, public speaking, hardware / software development, DJ / VJ performance
