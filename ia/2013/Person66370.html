John Gaskin's Summary

MOTION PICTURE FILM ACCOUNTING AND AUDITING:
The film and TV production is what I love. Since 1985 I worked as a Film Accountant, also called a Film Production Auditor, working on 50 large and small film/TV productions in 6 countries (I worked for Ron Howard twice). You can find me on IMDb.
I have developed an expertise in budgeting, cost reporting, dealing with bond companies, managing contracts of every description, working with producers, production managers and studio executives to ensure the production was produced on time and on budget. Some studios and bond companies (for example, Disney and Film Finances) rely heavily on the Film Production Auditor, in some cases trusting and demanding action from my position over the Producer's.
I have worked as of VP Operations for Canada for a major payroll service, increasing the sales stats by 60% in a year. I loved the extroversion that offered me, especially dealing face-to-face with independent producers.
Then I worked full time with an Independent Producer gaining a producing credit myself and helping him get two TV series off the ground.
Since 2009 I've been back in the 'biz working, as well as delivering workshops on Film Accounting, Film Budgeting, Managing Film Production and Film Union Payroll. I have also been working industriously on a series of Self-Study Online Courses which are AICPA compliant.

My goal is to instruct full time.

Specialties: My specialty is in the area of training and smoothing over upsets amongst people of various temperaments. My book "Walk The Talk" is used by at least one mentor at the American Film Institute as well as in the Peter Parker producer program at the University of Southern California. Tom Garrett, Assoc professor at Sam Houston State Univ in Texas refers his film students to my book, as he also did when he was teaching at the Univ of Tampa. I have a several articles and courses which I have delivered, as well as a book "Walk The Talk". See www.talkfilm.biz
