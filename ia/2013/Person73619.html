Keith Fulmis' Summary

I am a professional cartoonist and illustrator with skills in both digital and traditional media. I have worked professionally for over 8 years creating well over a thousand designs for many clients from Disney, Universal Studios, Hard Rock Café, Kennedy Space Center, Harley - Davidson, Sea World and California Science Center. I create unique illustrations for character and concept design, storyboards, and company logos for entertainment, and gaming industries as well as clothing and apparel industry. I have strong people skills and work well both on my own and in a team environment. I am all ways seeking challenges both creative and technical, as well as finding new ways to streamline a production process. In addition to creating art for retail collectibles I also design art for tee shirts and skateboards featuring my original art. I am experienced in many forms of traditional art media in including, watercolor, acrylic, and oil painting, both pencil and ink illustration, stencil design, silkscreen poster design. In addition to traditional media I'm well versed in, SketchBook Pro, Corel Painter, Photoshop, Illustrator, Streamline, ToonBoom storyboard, and ToonBoom studio, and a working understanding of Google SketchUp.

If you are in need of someone creative who can work under tight deadlines, or are in need of some one who can take the lead on a project and run with it competently and bring a finished project on time and done to the clients satisfaction then I'm your man so please contact me a KeithFulmis.Studios@gmail.com to request a PDF of my portfolio.

Keith Fulmis' Experience

Illustrator/ Sketch Artist / Graphic Designer

PinUSA

May 2006 – Present (7 years 5 months)

As a senior artist it is my job to create unique concepts and illustrations that will become various types of collectibles for the entertainment industry. My job responsibilities and duties include working with sales department and the client to create a concept that will fit the needs of the client both in "WOW" factors and in cost budgets. After a concept is approver I then create the final illustration and graphic design that will be sent to the factory for production. I also assist other artists in projects that prove too complex or too large for just one person to handle as well as training new hires. From time to time I also create and pitch concepts and collectible programs to clients, which some times means that I will have to create a look and style unique to its self so that it fits the feel and needs of the client. Clients that I currently work with or have worked with include, Disney Shopping, Disney Land Resort, Disney World, Disney Auctions, Disney Soda Fountain, Disney Mall Japan, Hooters, Hard Rock Café, Nickelodeon, Universal Studios and Marvel, Dreamworks, Ocean Park China, Sea World, Harley Davidson, Lucas Licensing, and California Science Center. In addition I also create concept designs for character development, background design, prop design, and logo design for games as well as small companies, also creates art for children's books, storyboards for independent films, and commissioned paintings.
