Jenny Williams' Summary

For the last 2 decades I have worked on the digital marketing strategies for clients and agencies across Asia, America and Europe. I am now based in Australia and focused on working with marketers, agencies and media companies who are looking to implement major digital change within their organisation either through the evolution of their existing business or the creation of totally new business models.

I also participate actively in digital industry associations, speak frequently at conferences on various aspects of digital and the impact it has had on the marketing and advertising industries.

I am available for digital strategy consulting, strategic management particularly as it relates to the evolution of the advertising and marketing industry and I run training programs for both AdSchool and private clients on digital production and advanced digital strategy as well as board level facilitation and planning around Digital change management.
