Barry Knights' Experience

Owner/Manager

Australian Regional Business Development Speicalists

April 2011 – Present (2 years 5 months) Townsville, QLD

The advent of digital technology and online engagement through social networks has changed the way in which businesses do business and interact with customers and clients. It has, for the first time in history, actually changed business models world-wide. Some view this new business model as a tragedy, and many businesses are in a great deal of pain as a result of it, but at ARBDS we see it as a great opportunity. Using our expertise in areas of coaching, business development, management and strategic development, ARBDS is able to guide businesses through the mine field of digital engagement and digital business development. Our venture partnerships with companies that provide supporting expertise mean that we can offer businesses the opportunity to test new ideas in a safe environment. You do need to engage this new business model, but you don't need to walk the mine field alone because we'll be there to guide you through it.
Whatever it is that's causing you pain in your business right now, we can help you fix it. Don't live with the pain any longer; give me a call now and let's work together to make your business efficient and pain-free.

Venture Partnership Manager

ARBDS/Intelgain Technologies Venture Partnership

October 2012 – Present (11 months) Townsville, Australia

The online space is getting crowded and therefore there is a need to define your niche. ARBDS has a strategic venture partnership with Intelgain Technologies, which allows us to operate in a niche market. Apart from offering mobile, web and SEO solutions Intelgain's niche has been their understanding of business domains across different verticals. Like ARBDS, they have a strong work ethic and believe in integrity and honesty in dealing with clients which is why their client retention ratio is very high, with clients with whom they have been working with us for several years. Intelgain's portfolio includes some large international clients such as Pfizer and Australia's third largest real estate portal, http://www.allhomes.com.au

ARBDS and our venture partners believe in partnering with our clients in their project ventures and believe in mutual growth. Such a relationship is all about managing expectations and forging partnerships based on trust and mutual cooperation. This means that rather than coming in and doing a "job" for you, we actually invest ourselves in your business success.

As the Director of ARBDS and manager of our venture partnership, I am now able to offer clients a comprehensive, across-the-board digital experience. Adding Intelgain to my company's portfolio gives me enhanced deliver-ability on the digital business offerings of ARBDS. A vast array of digital solutions including databases, apps and mobile phone solutions can now be custom-built and delivered in house.
We don't offer off-the-shelf products, but customize each job to your specific needs.
We don't need to outsource our work, because the development team is part of our team.
This is a unique relationship that gives both companies greater capability and an international presence.
