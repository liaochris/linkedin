Bruce Delahunty's Summary

As Managing Director of CSM Group I believe in being multi-skilled in various engineering disciplines to stay on top. We take a holistic view of any project placed before us and provide complete design and engineering solutions within the property development and mining / materials handling industries.

Why use 4-6 consultants when you can use just 1 for your property developments, CSM Group can design, certify, project manage and supervise your developments in totality. We can provide civil. structural,hydraulic and electrical,engineering as well as landscape architecture,town planning and surveying if required.

Technology is constantly evolving in our industry so state of the art software is important.
In 2011, CSM Group launched into 3D BIM Modelling to push our innovative and detailed design solutions even further, by providing 3D service clash reports and construction phase sequence video animation thus reducing on site rework and retro fitting resulting in cost savings to the client, and a piece of mind to the contractor / builder knowing that everything will fit together with a 99.9% accuracy on site.

If you require professional advice on a Civil, Structural, Hydraulic or a Mining / Materials Handling project call CSM Group for a no obligation consultation or refer our slide share presentations of our capability statement and 3D Modelling images at the bottom of this page and or our website.
www.csmgroup.net.au, offices in Brisbane and Sydney.

Specialties: Civil, structural and hydraulic design of property developments.
3D BIM Modelling, service clash detection and construction phase sequencing with video animation.
Softwares CSM Group utilise include:
Revit Building Design Suite Premium, Autodesk Infrastructure Suite,3DS Max, 12D Model,Civil 3D, Navisworks Manage, Autocad 2008-2014, Civilcad 6.3, P.C Drain,PC Sewer, Culvert, Staad Pro v8i, Ram Concept,Tedds, Design in Hyne, Drains,Pipes, Hec Ras, Microsoft Office Suite
