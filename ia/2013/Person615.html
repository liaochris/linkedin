Aaron Salles Torres' Summary

I have a degree in Film, Video and New Media from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and hold an MBA from Loyola University Chicago.

In Chicago, I have directed corporate videos. Currently in Brazil, I work as an Assistant Director doing TV shows and commercials and Film. My independent short films were shown around the world. My first fictional novel was published in Brazil in 2009 and the second, in 2012.

Specialties: assistant-directing, branding and identity, social media, writing, fiction, directing.
