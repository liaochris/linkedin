Deb Singer's Summary

I have been committed to the sustainable product space since 1995. My passion is bringing innovative green products to market that eliminate “single use.”

A key focus for my company, Singer Sustainability, is mainstream adaption of reusable shopping bags. To that end, much of what we do is develop and deliver comprehensive reusable bag programs for retailers, most notably Whole Foods. We are continually working on product and merchandising innovations to shift the paradigm to inspire more customers to bring their own bags when they shop. Singer Sustainability also works with innovative housewares and personal products brands to help them gain distribution in Whole Foods and other key retailers.

Prior to forming my own company, I worked at at Whole Foods, where I spearheaded the Whole Living line of housewares , which catapulted the chain to the front-runner position in sustainable household products. I was also instrumental in developing the Better Bag program which focused on eliminating Whole Foods' use of single use plastic bags while offering customers reusable bags made out of PET manufactured from recycled plastic bottles.

Prior to working in retailing, I built and sold a baking company called Piece-A-Cake.
