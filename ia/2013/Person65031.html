João Gomes' Summary

12+ years in IT consultancy, delivering value to the business. I work according to my values: commitment, leanness, high standards and strong team cooperation.

Highly experienced on the remote delivery of solutions. I have managed a Solutions Delivery unit responsible for the Northern European market and have led the setup and rollout of a new development center that delivered projects remotely for the Northern European and US markets.

I have managed several development teams (up to 50 members) and am particularly keen in coaching my teams in project management best practices, developing high performance senior PM.

My ultimate goal is to deliver the highest value to business stakeholders in the shortest time frame, focusing in the solution not on the technology. This allows me to quickly apprehend new business processes and together with the users reach the solution that best fits the business needs.

Specialties: Agile Project management; PMP; Team management; Business value delivery
