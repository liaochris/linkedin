John Stowe's Summary

John Stowe has certifications through the IICRC, OSHA, EPA, FEMA, ITI & Xactware. He has the top level certifications in the water damage industry through the IICRC. Not only has he obtained the top levels in the inspection and restoration industry, he also has obtained level 3 certification, the highest user certification offered by Xactware.

He takes a simple but yet complex approach to the water damage restoration industry. "If it can get wet then it should be able to get dry." He's dried things that other leading water damage professionals said can't be dried. Mr. Stowe has dried things that some of these professionals have never attempted.

There is, and always has been, a gap between the insurance industry, the contractor, and the property owner. One of his passions and goals is to narrow this gap. He are often hired by property owners, insured individuals, independent adjusters, general contractors, restoration contractors, construction consultants, and attorneys: because of the quality of our inspection, documentation and estimating. He have over $30,000 invested in meters, inspection tools, and documentation equipment. Mr. Stowe has numerous advanced, top level, and master certifications along with years of experience and he has built a solid, trained foundation to produce meticulous and scientifically substantiated estimates. This detailed documentation is so precise he can even calculate kilowatts used by drying equipment and the gallons of water extracted through the dehumidification drying process. With all Mr. Stowes' experiences and certifications, he truly is a leader in the industry. If you take into account all the certifications held by Mr. Stowe he is likely to be one of a kind.
