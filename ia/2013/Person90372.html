Stella McCalister Gramby's Summary

SUMMARY:

Presently, I am a Remote Graphic Designer with more than 30 years experience. I am proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite programs and are deadline driven. I can work from design concept through press ready completion on projects. Also, I am time conscious and can work under deadline pressures.

My background is colorful. I started out at WHMM-TV 32 in Washington DC as a Junior Graphic Designer and was promoted to Assistant Art Director within 2 years. From there I spent many years freelancing in NYC working for clients such as “The Pushpin Group”, “Corporate Graphics”, "Woman's Wear Daily" and "American Express",. I worked for several Print Shops and then went on from there acquiring my own clients remotely.

Specialties: Logos, Corporate Identity, Promotional Materials, Brochures, Flyers, Ads, CD's and a vast variety of other print collateral.
