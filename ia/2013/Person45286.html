Greg Ford's Summary

Graduating from East Carolina University in 1987 gave me the credentials to work for a large ad agency right out of the blocks. I was fortunate to be hired by a large firm in Raleigh, NC. It was there at Rockett, Burkhead & Lewis that I truly was thrown in the fire of advertising reality. I was trained by the best in the business for understanding typography and layout design. This was a very challenging position that gave me a strong foundation and helped mold me for the future..

After one year with that agency, I wanted to move into more of a graphic design and Illustration direction. I began to freelance some in Raleigh as well as Wilmington NC. In 1989 I landed a job with Concepts Unlimited Inc. in the Wilmington area. It was there that I received my computer training for the Mac. I was responsible for design work as well as print media for that company. Three years later in 1992 I started my own company Ford Design. This was the scariest yet most rewarding thing I've ever done in my life.

Starting from humble beginnings, Ford Design has grown from
doing small design and print projects to working with large International companies. Our goal is to always go beyond the expectations of our clients. We want to give everyone the WOW factor every chance we get. Even though we work with many large companies at this time we still love the opportunity to help grow a small business.

Specialties

FDG specializes in branding, graphic design, print media, direct mail, signage and trade show booth design as well as website design/development, SEO services, Internet marketing & email marketing.
