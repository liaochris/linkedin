Iva Keene's Summary

Iva Keene is a qualified, accredited and internationally recognized doctor of naturopathic medicine and natural fertility specialist, award winning author and speaker who has helped thousands of couples with fertility problems on their path to pregnancy.

Based in Switzerland, Iva is a compassionate and highly ethical practitioner and a strong advocate of natural fertility education. Iva has taught her safe, effective IVF alternative to couples in more than a dozen countries.

The Natural Fertility Prescription is a four month home-study course that teaches infertile couples how they can take control of their own fertility and dramatically improve their odds of conceiving using safe approaches that have proven effective.

This method is shown to reverse infertility in over 80% of infertile couples and nearly double the IVF success rate from 25% to 47.1%.

The NFP method successfully addresses the most common fertility conditions including: PCOS, endometriosis, sperm abnormalities, luteal phase defect, repeated miscarriages, unexplained infertility, secondary infertility, age related infertility (providing you've got some ovarian reserve), mild forms of blocked fallopian tubes, fibroids and adhesions.

NFP is endorsed by leading medical doctors, fertility specialists and complementary medical practitioners and is recommended and endorsed by Foresight, the international pioneer for preconception care in the UK.
