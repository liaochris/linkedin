Eric Jacobson's Summary

At Ascend Integrated Media (Overland Park, KS), we partner with nonprofit organizations, associations and governmental entities throughout the U.S. to create custom print, digital and mobile content products that help them:

* grow membership
* boost event, conference and/or show attendance
* engage their communities and audiences more effectively and frequently
* drive more revenue-generating fundraising campaigns
* improve their marketing and communications activities

We also help create the content and can deliver a turnkey project, often with a revenue-share business model for our partner clients. In fact, our royalty share payment to our partners in 2012 was a collective $4.5 million.

Ascend Integrated Media specializes in creating electronic newsletters, directories, buyers' guides, annual reports, websites, fundraising campaigns, custom branded magazines, social media campaigns, direct mail, and educational/instructional guides.

Nonprofit Research: Contact me at (913) 344-1436 for a copy of a research study about how Greater Kansas City nonprofits communicate with their donors, volunteers, clients and patrons. I'll send you the free summary white paper.

Our whole team at Ascend Integrated Media is ready to partner with you even if you are outside the Overland Park, KS (Kansas City, MO) area!

Some of our nonprofit partner clients at Ascend Integrated Media include City of Kansas City, Missouri; Johnson County Community College Performing Arts Series; American Heart Association/American Stroke Association; Jazz In The Woods; American Society For Public Administration, International City/County Management Association, and about 55 other organizations.

Our latest product for the Kansas City metro is the print and online, Ascend's Golden Guide for baby boomers and their aging family members.
