Anne Petersen's Summary

Skilled web director focusing on digital interactive strategy, with emphasis on usability.

Anne Petersen leads major research universities in adopting all their digital needs: website redesigns, interactive media and content development, social media overhauls and guideline creation along with community management, CMS adoptions and implementation, usability testing, information architecture, analytics observation and application, dynamic photography, mobile sites and apps.

Along with these, she has been conception-to-creation leader for interactive maps and virtual tours, Spanish language microsites, web style guides, student blogs, as well as providing the strategic reasoning behind all of these. Her projects run the breadth of solo work, negotiating all terms and project managing with the cooperation of agencies or freelancers, and many other projects that were completed by teams she managed.
