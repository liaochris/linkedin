Jan Stradowski's Summary

Professional science and technology writer, content editor, teamwork manager, new projects ideator, social media manager, radio host.

Specjalizacje: Finding and creating interesting science topics to write about, briefing other writers and editing their work. Developing new procedures and managing workflows. Working with text, graphic concepts, print media, radio and Internet as well. Creating and developing new projects for print and internet. Plus social media marketing and communication.

I have a strong background in medicine and biology. I'm also interested in cosmology, evolution, complexity, neuroscience and new technologies.
