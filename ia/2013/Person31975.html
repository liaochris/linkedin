David Iberkleid's Summary

I was born and raised in Latin America and moved to the US for college. Four years later, I took an internship and then a job in Washington, DC before going back to school for a masters degree. I recently founded a start-up and have worked at two others in the past.

David Iberkleid's Experience

Managing Partner

WhyEquals, LLC

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

October 2008 – Present (4 years 9 months) Atlanta, Georgia

WhyEquals, LLC is an award-winning, Atlanta-based start-up helping members of the Hispanic community connect locally through an innovative peer-to-peer mobile app. WhyEquals began as a project to foster information literacy and computer skills in local immigrants. It later grew into a grassroots research and design firm committed to bring networking to disconnected communities. Today, we keep more than a quarter million subscribers informed and better connected.
