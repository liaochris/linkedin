Jayde Ashlin's Summary

I am experienced in every aspect of advertising design, from conceptualizing, branding and comp production to high-res finished art and press-checks. My background includes 14 years of diverse Web/Print agency experience and 8 years in the fast-paced Ad Sales department. I have a deep knowledge of Adobe CS6, Quark, and Microsoft Office. My creative strengths include a natural eye for composition, depth, texture and color, and using visual concept and environment to set a tone. My technical strengths include working with complex animation challenges, projects that require focus, self-sufficiency, problem-solving, and advanced computer knowledge. A seasoned professional, I bring vitality and originality to my design work, and a playful but dedicated presence to my workplace.

Specialties

Content/Concept Creation, Site Development, Global Marketing & Corporate Initiatives, Staff Management, Advertising & Sponsorship Programs, Registration Initiatives, Brand Building, and Management of External Creative Vendors.
