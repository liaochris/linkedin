Matthew Price's Summary

I am a 15+ year veteran in the Digital Media field with expertise covering broadcast, interactive, social and web-based media and team management. My expertise stems from 20 years of technical IT experience and more than 10 years of creative media experience in video production / video editing / content development for corporate, commercial, and non-profit projects. I am a technical pioneer in Consumer Neuroscience Research [aka Neuromarketing] and have spent the last 4 years using my combined technical and creative skills to develop / build / refine AV workflows and process models to advance market research capabilities. My current career focus is in emerging technology, interactive media and social integration and how these fields will change media delivery and consumption for consumers. I am interested in speaking to individuals or organizations focused on developing strategies that address these changing markets!

Outside of work, I am an avid sports fan, gamer, foodie, movie buff and I look forward to lazy weekends and spending time with my amazing wife. You can usually find me on twitter @Wizrd23 reading about new technology, the game industry, or Alabama Football. Roll Tide.
