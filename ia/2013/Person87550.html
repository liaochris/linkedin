Martina Iring's Summary

I'm a small business marketing consultant. My passion is helping small businesses who are struggling with their online marketing - either no web presence at all, or a presence that is doing nothing! It's so rewarding to see entrepreneurs able to accomplish so much online, without the huge budgets needed for traditional media. Don't have a marketing department? Need some small biz marketing expertise? Get in touch with me... http://www.martinairing.com
