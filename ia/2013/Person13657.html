Bethany Salisbury's Experience

Owner/Portrait Artist

Pet Portraits by Bethany (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Arts and Crafts industry

October 2011 – Present (2 years 1 month) Matthews, NC

So, you want to show off your beloved pet, but you also want a piece of contemporary, stylish, art décor to distinguish your walls? Look no further! As a professional illustrator and graphic designer, I bring the character of your pet to life in a way that compliments your home and will be a conversation piece for years to come!

Your custom pet portrait will be painted in acrylic on a gallery style wrapped canvas. I paint around the edges so your piece can hang as is. You choose the color and the amount of texture or flatness of the background. I want your piece to fit right in with your style! Any type of pet is game, be it dog, cat, horse, ferret, rabbit, etc. Don’t forget pet portraits make the perfect gift for your friend or family member pet lover.
