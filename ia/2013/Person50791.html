Guewen Loussouarn's Summary

I created my first website in 2000, my first CMS in 2002, upgraded it to eCommerce in 2004 while creating my first web agency (Akoufen, still running).
Between 2006 and 2009 at NetBooster, I went from web developer to Project Management team manager, exploring and connecting every acquisition & loyalty channel for brands like Le Figaro, Neuf Telecom, Intersport or Adecco.
Then, I started Napkin in 2009 with 2 friends in order to help brands like ING Direct, Bourjois, France Televisions, Beneteau or Orange Vallée take the most of user centricity, which the company still does.
After 1 year at Nurun where me & my team work on Lacoste L!VE was awarded several times, I returned to NetBooster in September 2012 to coordinate and develop the Social Media & Video activities accross the Group (500 people in 19 offices accross Europe and Dubaï).

Entrepreneur, intrapreneur, curious and open-minded, I'm now very excited about International Management in an innovative and agile group.
