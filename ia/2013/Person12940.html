Ben Irawan's Summary

Ben Irawan is a public speaker, entrepreneur, community builder and an accomplished musician. He is the editor of NEW CITY Magazine, chairman of Dads4Kids Fatherhood Foundation, board member of Australian Heart Ministries and Focus on the Family Australia, director of Kingdom Culture International, president of Arncliffe Community Life Centre and a senior pastor of Life Centre International, a church with 2 congregations in Sydney & Wollongong.

Ben has over 16 years in business and professional experience at high level management positions. He has been involved in the music industry, film & video production, advertising & marketing and also publishing. He is a very well connected leader who is an expert on the art of social media networking with more than 4,000 friends on Facebook, more than 25,000 followers of his tweets in Twitter and he has a rapidly growing network in LinkedIn.

He is passionate in the area of community building and always looking for ways to work together with people to make this world a better place for the next generations to come. He is a futurist with the ability to see years ahead of time and also a game maker who can see the potential and strength in different people and the ability to put them together into becoming a Super Team.
