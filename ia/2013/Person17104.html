Brett Roberts' Summary

I specialise in growing organisations via smart business, commercialisation and go-to-market strategies. I have a strong sales and marketing background and a reputation for innovative thinking when it comes to identifying and taking advantage of new opportunities.

My career has focused primarily on the technology sector and I have a clear understanding of disciplines including product development and commercialisation, protecting and licensing intellectual property, competitive strategy, solution-based selling, channel management and service / support models etc.

I have worked with a wide variety of commercial and government organisations and, since the early 2000's, I have been an active member of the start-up community helping early-stage companies via the Icehouse incubator, Startup Weekend events and 1:1 engagements. During that time I have acquired a considerable level of PR, communications and public speaking experience along with a substantial network of business and technology industry contacts.
