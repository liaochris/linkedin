Errol Chung CFE's Summary

I have worked as an Account Executive/Project Management for the last 10 years. Currently I am employed as a Marketing Coordinator for and Incredible Medical technologies firm in Miami, Fl. This position offers the best opportunity to use my technical, communicative, and marketing skill sets. Work 1:1 with business leaders in order to design and implement custom Social Media Marketing Campaigns.

As a consultant with Rsitez, I have worked with several agencies to design an implement strategic marketing campaigns. By working with business leaders to understand their vision of growth and creating the tools, to achieve their goal, I have had to develop several "out of the box" ways to reduce budgets, and achieve maximum impact.

I am an articulate, competent and confident professional, who has an in-depth understanding of business development and social media principles. I able to communicate with management as well as, internal/external teams to create a variety of high end tools to meet business needs in a competitive environment. I am happy to travel or relocate for needed assignments.
