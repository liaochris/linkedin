Kate Kelly's Summary

I am a professional writer and author or co-author of more than 35 books, and I am currently focused on contributing to America's story via speeches at corporate meetings or to community organizations. I also write and distribute an e-letter, American Snapshots, that provides a new perspective on today by looking at yesterday.
My goals are to increase my distribution of my free e-letter and to book speaking engagements with those audiences who love to hear little-known stories about our past. (Did you know there were electric cars 100 years ago? I can tell you who drove them and why the gas-powered engine won out.)

Specialties

speaking, writing, and connecting with audiences and readers.
I make information accessible to people who are short on time.
