Dr. Francine Baffa, BCBA-D's Summary

Dr. Baffa has a wide scope of clinical experience. For the past ten plus years, she has specialized in developing person centered plans to enhance the lives of individuals with autism and other intellectual and developmental disabilities in her private practice. As a seasoned educator, she has done extensive public speaking and written publications.

She encourages a team approach respecting the individual, their families and staff. Dr. Baffa provides counseling for couples and siblings of those with special challenges. Her approaches include Cognitive and Behavioral Therapy, Family Systems Theory, and Acceptance Commitment Therapy, and Insight Oriented Therapy.

Additionally she supervises international and domestic candidates who are studying to become BCBA and BCaBA’s. She works closely with colleagues developing multi-cultural approaches to service and support those individuals with developmental disabilities and her practice has expanded to India, Dubai, India, Europe, Canada, and Australia.
