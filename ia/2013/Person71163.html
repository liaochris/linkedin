Justine Negri's Summary

Hello. I'm a careers and business mentor, certified trainer, coach, internet recruiter and LinkedIn trainer. My aim is to work with individuals and groups to help them realise their true career potential. I also love to help promote your career/business objective by using valuable cost effective branding, social media and sourcing strategies. The core focus of my business is to help clients gain and promote maximum results for their career. I deliver tailored seminars and training workshops providing you with a multitude of workable tools to achieve your goals.

During times of career change, I aim to help you focus and explore the various options available to you (using current strategic tools) and guide you through this career change - be it back into employment or starting your own business.

I enjoy networking, meeting & learning from like-minded people, seeing true potential and transformation happen, running my own business, biomimicry, sunflowers and I always try to help others in any way I possibly can...

• Processing and managing the occupational health and visa clearance process for every candidate prior to travel
