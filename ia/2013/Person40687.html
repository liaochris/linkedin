Elizabeth Glazner's Summary

I am a media professional with more than 20 years of experience in print and online as a writer, editor and designer. A veteran of newspapers, including the Los Angeles Times and Orange County Register, I also founded the award-winning Wahine magazine and have been a correspondent for SELF magazine and numerous other publications. I currently work as a web designer and content specialist and freelance writer, editor and graphic designer.
