Kirsti Scott's Summary

Since 1993, I have led the award-winning design team as Founder and Creative Director at Scott Design, the creative agency for technology companies. I work with our team of designers and programmers to ensure high-quality design on all websites, e-mail campaigns, web banners, online apps, and marketing campaigns for our clients in Silicon Valley, the San Francisco Bay area, and nationwide

I serve on the board at the Santa Cruz Area Chamber of Commerce, on the Executive Committee and as VP of Marketing. I am one of the co-founders of 100 Women Who Care Santa Cruz and one of the organizers of TEDxSantaCruz.

I was previously an Art Director at CKS Partners, where I created logos, brochures, packaging, and more for Apple Computer, IBM, and other large corporate clients. I was also a Software Engineer, specializing in interface design, at Decision Focus Inc., an environmental consulting firm in the Bay Area.

I have degrees in Economics from Stanford University and in Graphic Design from Rhode Island School of Design. I studied in Paris at L'École des Sciences Politiques and La Sorbonne.

I love design, marketing, art, travel, technology, and things that make me laugh.

Specialties

Online Marketing, Web Design, Email Design, Branding, Digital Strategy, Interactive Marketing Strategy
