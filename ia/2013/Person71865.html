Kammy Thurman's Summary

Kammy Thurman is CEO and senior consultant at Anchor Marketing and Anchor Creative. She and her team help businesses in the health and travel industries leverage integrated online and offline channels into one complete and cohesive marketing system that virtually guarantees they become #1 in their local markets within 1 year.

Kammy has 26 years experience in copywriting/marketing/sales. Ten years of that experience was gained as the highest-selling travel consultant in the largest agency in the state. For the last 16 years she has helped businesses of all sizes -- from one-woman gift shops to mid-sized health companies to large corporations -- sell their products and services through a variety of online and offline channels as a copywriter/consultant.

Over the last couple of years, she has expanded her business from copywriting into a full-service, business-growth firm, which recently earned acceptance into the highly exclusive Power Marketing Consultants Network (PMCN).

As Certified Power Marketing Consultants, Kammy and her team employ a proprietary marketing system, called the Power Marketing Program, to help clients dominate their marketplace and dramatically increase their revenues. The Power Marketing Consultants Network is the largest, and most successful, international network of professional marketing agencies in the world. Members of the Network all share best practices and resources, participate in continual masterminds, and have all been trained to use the proprietary Power Marketing Program™.
