Denice MacDonald's Summary

Build a sound digital strategy while elevating brand awareness.

MacDonald Consulting provides strategic digital and brand consulting for the following key deliverables - independently or collaboratively with clients, agencies and other varied stakeholders:

BRAND STRATEGY: Your brand is more than a name - create the right voice and message for your organization - ensure that your brand promise meets and exceeds your customer’s expectations – offline and online.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS: Knowledge about your competitors will provide you with the information necessary to make better decisions for your digital or branding strategy.

TARGETED DEMOGRAPHIC SEGMENTATION: Build a one-to-one relationship with your visitor by effectively segmenting your target audience.

WEB CONTENT STRATEGY: Define and create content that resonates with diverse audiences resulting in long-term relationships.

WEB BRANDING PROMOTIONS: Ensure that your online brand is consistent with your offline brand. Learn how to create it once, pay for it once, but use many ways.

WEB TECHNOLOGY PRODUCT REVIEW AND IMPLEMENTATION: Not all web sites are alike. From e-commerce solutions to content management systems, ensure that today’s technology works for you and is the right fit for your digital needs.

PROJECT/ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT: Steering multiple departments and overseeing large strategic projects requires a seasoned, integrated approach including meeting deadlines; ensuring consistency across multiple disciplines or departments while staying within budget.

SEARCH ENGINE MARKETING: You cannot deliver your message to your intended audience online without them finding you first. Create the right plan for your organization.

SOCIAL MEDIA: Everyone's doing it - but do you have a plan that is integrated with all your marketing channels?
