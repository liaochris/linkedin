Katie became a licensed Certified Public Accountant after receiving her Bachelors of Science from Saint Michael's College in Burlington, Vermont. She began Monarch CPA Services in 2005.

Throughout her career, Katie has provided accounting and tax services, business advice, income tax reduction advice and financial management/consulting services for a large number of businesses. She has acquired knowledge and expertise from years of work with every business class from entrepreneurs to large, publicly traded companies with millions of dollars in annual sales and locations in multiple states.

Katie is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and the Maryland Association of Certified Public Accountants. Katie is also an active member Gaithersburg-Germantown Chamber of Commerce.
