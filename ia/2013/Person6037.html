Amy Ravit Korin's Summary

As the Midwest Community Manager for Google+ Local, I focus on building a strong local community for Google, Google+ Local and Google Maps through the development of activities and social engagements targeting Chicagoans and businesses. Google+ Local is a hyper-local, super-social recommendation engine that connects people with the places they love.

In 2009, Amy Ravit Korin founded interactiveAmy Social Media Consulting LLC, a consultancy focusing on social media and integrated marketing strategies, digital publicity and personal branding for individuals, businesses and brands, and joined Google in 2011.

Amy studied communications at the University of Michigan and earned her MBA from Loyola University’s Graduate School of Business with a concentration in Marketing & Human Resources and currently serves on the Advisory Board for Social Media Week Chicago.
Amy lives and breathes social media personally and professionally. Amy is even an online dating success story – reconnecting with the first boy she ever kissed and marrying him in 2008!
