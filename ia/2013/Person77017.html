Kristie Camacho's Summary

My first writing project was published on a laminate machine in the second grade. Since those first moments of elementary school fame, I have continued to write fiction, poetry, and creative non-fiction.

I earned my Bachelor of Arts degree in English Literature from California State University, San Bernardino and my Master of Arts degree in English from National University, La Jolla.

When I'm not managing my freelance writing business, I teach American Literature and English Composition classes.

I am available for editing and copywriting projects including blog posts, brochures, press releases, newsletters, and web copy. I am also available for curriculum development, instruction, and consulting projects.

Past clients include online marketing firms, web content developers, and small businesses.

Specialties: blogging, copywriting, education writing, proofreading, editing, curriculum development, career management, higher education instruction
