Gerrit McGowan's Honors and Awards

  • Award for Planning Excellence in International Development

    Canadian Institute of Planners
    • 2009

    Working with UNDP and the Palestinian Ministries of Local Government and Planning, Gerrit McGowan established the first comprehensive process roadmap for District planning in occupied Palestinian territory.

  • Award for Planning Excellence in Economic Development

    Canadian Institute of Planners
    • 2007

    Working with UN-Habitat, EcoPlan was awarded the 2007 CIP Award for Planning in Economic Development for the guide series Promoting Economic Development Through Strategic Planning. The training series presents an innovative method for helping local governments face the challenges, opportunities and growing responsibilities in addressing the economic health of local areas while ensuring integration with other ongoing social and environmental sustainability initiatives.

  • Award for Cultural Authenticity

    Aboriginal Tourism Association of British Columbia
    • 2007

    Working with QQS (Eyes) Projects Society and the Heiltsuk First Nation, Gerrit McGowan of Syntegrity Consulting led the design and economic opportunities analysis of Koeye Lodge on the Central West Coast of British Columbia. Koeye demonstrates a commitment to sharing authentic cultural activities, to preserving Aboriginal culture and to bettering the community overall, while offering long-term employment and economic opportunities for the Heiltsuk people through its focus on Aboriginal heritage and ecotourism.
