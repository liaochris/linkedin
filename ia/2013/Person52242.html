Heather Wietzel's Summary

InsuranceIR principal Heather J. Wietzel is an investor relations executive with more than 25 years of corporate, agency and consulting experience. With a proven track record of results, deep Wall Street connections and nearly two decades of expertise with property and casualty and life insurers, Heather provides publicly traded insurance companies with improved credibility and transparency and important strategic insight into resolving communications challenges, helping to foster stronger relationships between management and investors.

She founded InsuranceIR in 2009 after establishing and managing the stand-alone investor relations function for six years at Cincinnati Financial Corporation, a Standard & Poor’s 500 index member and one of the nation’s 25 largest property casualty insurance companies. The company’s first investor relations officer, Heather was recognized for her achievements by the investment community with her selection as a finalist for “Best Investor Relations Officer – mid to large cap” by IR Magazine in 2007.

Expertise:
Relationships – Internal and external relationships are key to successful investor relations activities. Served as primary contact for sell-side and institutional investors. Oral and written communication skills.
External Reporting and Investor Materials – Ranging from external SEC reporting (10-K and proxy), to financial news and conference call preparation to investor presentations and strategic messaging. Financial and investment analysis, including property casualty statutory accounting. Integrated communications responsibilities including crisis communications, financial media relations, customer and employee communications.
Investor Relations Strategy and Budget – Overall responsibilities for multiple plan developments and implementations. Board interactions and disclosure committee member.
National Investor Relations Institute – Member 1998 to present. NIRI Senior Roundtable – Member 2004 to present.
