Jonas Neergaard-Nielsen's Summary

Fundamental physics researcher within experimental quantum optics. Have particularly worked with generation of non-classical light and its characterization in terms of continuous variables, but with a broad interest in quantum optics and quantum information.

Specialties

Squeezed light generation, optical resonators, homodyne detection and tomography, photon level operations.
Simple theoretical modeling, data analysis and visualization, dissemination.
Mathematica, LabView, Python.
