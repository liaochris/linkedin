Kristin is the second generation of the Guglielmo family active in the Insurance agency. She is a partner with her Dad, Tony, and has been with the agency since 1993. Kristin is a Phi Beta Kappa graduate of the University of Connecticut. She graduated Magna Cumma Laude in 1992. Kristin finished her term as the president of the Applied Systems user group in Connecticut. Over one hundred Connecticut insurance agencies are members of this insurance computer group. She lives in Stafford with her three sons, her daughter and her husband Robert Forrest who also works in the agency.
