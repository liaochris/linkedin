Joseph D'Alessandro's Summary

Joseph D'Alessandro places senior-level professionals in culinary innovation, hospitality, and food manufacturing throughout the US.

At Strategic Hospitality Search and Strategic FIT Food Innovation Talent he and his team have successfully completed hundreds of executive and management search assignments in over a decade of values-driven executive and management search.
