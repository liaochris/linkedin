Marcos Gazzana's Education

Universidade do Vale do Rio dos Sinos

MA, Applied Linguistics

2006 – 2007

During my master's course I was mostly involved with Corpus Linguistics and Cognitive Semantics. I studied the meaning of particles and their contribution to the overall meaning of phrasal verbs. My study was about three specific particles: "away", "out" and "over", their prototypical meaning and related image schemas.
My dissertation's title was: "The Semantic Contribution of Particles in Verb-Particle Constructions".

Activities and Societies: Semantec - a research group interested in semantics and technology.
