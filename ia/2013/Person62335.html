Commercial Energy Efficiency and Finance Specialist

Boulder County Comissioner's Office

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Government Administration industry

August 2010 – Present (3 years 3 months) Boulder County and City and County of Denver, Colorado

Project manager for the development, launch, and ongoing administration of the Elevations Energy Loans program. This unique loan program leverages public funding to make available $35,000,000 low cost financing to help homes and businesses implement energy efficiency and renewable energy projects. Elevations Energy Loans links the EnergySmart (and Denver Energy Challenge) advisor service with Elevations credit union's processing, underwriting and loan servicing skills to bring a turnkey energy efficiency implementation service to homes and businesses in Boulder and Denver Counties. This program, currently seen as a pilot in Boulder County and City and County of Denver, has been designed from day one to be scaled out, to reach a much greater population, and make energy lending a new "norm".
