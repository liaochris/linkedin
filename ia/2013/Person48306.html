Gert Schouten's Summary

RSVO is a compact fieldwork organisation with a small permanent staff, so that you are quickly in contact with the right person, and receive answers to your questions directly. An important share of our clients consists of foreign companies. We specialise in face-to-face data-collection for purposes of market research. In this context, the supply of high-quality data to clients is of key importance, as well as our permanent and freelance staff. RSVO wants to achieve that by being professional, involved and flexible, in an open working atmosphere.
RSVO is ISO20252 certified, member of MOA and ESOMAR.

Specialties

RSVO specialises in face-to-face research, but offers several forms of qualitative and quantitative research. Whether it involves numerical insight, or discovering the opinions or the motivations of the target group, RSVO offers you a network of professionals who ensure reliable data-collection. In addition, we have at our disposal a number of completely equipped interview rooms for research on location.
