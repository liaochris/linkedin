Alex den Haan's Summary

Once media planner and sales rep for interactive media, I have an understanding of how to get the best out of online presence, while also putting this in a broad perspective.

Nowadays I divide my time between managing the relations with AcademicTransfer's member (Universities, Medical Centres, Research Institutes) and Social Media. Not only from a strategic point of view, but right there in the trenches too. Excited to be where the action is and combining my 15+ years of internet use with 10+ year of professional experience and all the exciting new things that are abound.

I have a passion for Internet and follow the developments from both a professional and personal interest.

Having sold not only services, but also hardware and software, my strength lies in building relationships that last. Working with such diverse organisations and individuals as marketing professionals, media publishers, web developers, designers, ISV's, CIO's, sys-admins, scientists, hiring managers and recruiters has taught me to communicate on several levels

I like to learn everyday and therefore are open to share knowledge and ideas.

Specialties: On/offline marketing, account management, (after) sales, customer service, job boards, e-recruiting, Employer Branding, HRM, Social Media, Personal Branding, Location Based Services, web2.0, DRM, software distribution.
