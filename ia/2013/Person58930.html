Jasamyn Roberts' Summary

Jasamyn has more than 15 years of marketing, public relations and event planning experience. She has an extensive knowledge base in integrated marketing, social media networking, sponsorship and fundraising, communications, media relations and project management. She is a creative business woman with exemplary business skills with the ability to juggle multiple projects at once, prioritizing when needed.

Her "shoot-from-the-hip" stance allows her to complete tasks efficiently and effectively. Jasamyn always has the best interest of her clientele in mind and pays close attention to details. As an intelligent, self-motivated individual, she believes in a solid work ethic and that a person’s integrity is the key to a successful existence in today’s work environment.

With previous experience as Director of Marketing and Member Services at the American Bar Association in Chicago, Jasamyn marketed and handled event logistics for the largely attended ABA TECHSHOW, ABA Women Rainmakers Conference, and several legal educational programs. She also managed the design and production of marketing and sponsorship collateral and handled sponsor relations with such companies as Mercedes Benz, Harley Davidson Motors, Jenner & Block LLC, and Robert Half Legal. In prior years, Jasamyn worked with international and North American public relations and brand communications at Marriott International, Inc. and managed event planning with the National Trust for Historic Preservation in Washington, DC.

Jasamyn's ability to nurture relationships was helpful in recruiting sponsors for the Black Cinema Cafe events held in Washington, DC, including Kenneth Cole, Ford Motor Company, Motorola, VIBE magazine, Armani Xchange, and Grand Marnier.

She holds a Bachelor of Science and Business Administration degree in marketing from Roosevelt University. She attended Phillips Exeter Academy for high school and is fluent in Spanish.
