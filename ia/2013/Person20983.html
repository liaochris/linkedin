Catherine Saxelby's Summary

Catherine Saxelby is a qualified dietitian and nutritionist who has researched and written on almost all aspects of healthy eating in a career spanning 25 years in public health, clinical dietetics, food industry and writing.

Her skill lies in translating the scientific into articles that the public can not only understand but find fascinating.

She contributes nutrition articles to blogs, magazines and newspapers. Past contributions have been to Healthy Food Guide, New Idea, Sunday Life (Sun-Herald), Healthwatch (Herald-Sun), Canberra Times, Readers Digest, Womens Weekly and Super Food Ideas.

She is the author of 10 books on nutrition including the best-selling Nutrition for Life (Hardie Grant), now in its 24th year of continuous publication with over 500 000 copies in print. Her latest book is the huge Food and Nutrition Companion, an A to Z of all things food and nutrition, written after a lifetime in nutrition.

Specialties: Nutrition articles, presentations and media including:
• radio and television
• web-based communications
• social media marketing
• magazines and books
