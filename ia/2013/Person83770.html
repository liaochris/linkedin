Dr. Lynn K. Jones, PCC, BCC's Summary

http://www.lynnkjones.com
info@lynnkjones.com

Board Certified Coach Certification #1487

“My purpose is to inspire leaders to explore new territories, so that they can maximize their strengths, ignite synergies, and chart a bold and enlightened course in their lives and in their organizations.”

Dr. Lynn K. Jones, a certified Personal and Executive Coach, is recognized for helping leaders achieve positive, measurable changes in behavior for themselves, their organizations and their teams. With over 25 years of leadership experience, Lynn coaches leaders, trains and consults organizations on leading, managing, communicating and implementing strategic and personal change.

Lynn has extensive experience assisting organizations in developing positive organizational cultures. Known for her mix of “real-world” experience, competence and insight, Lynn is able to support organizations in achieving the results that they have dreamed about.

Lynn held senior management positions in nonprofit organizations for over 25 years. In these roles she has led culture change initiatives that have transformed organizations, has been responsible for multiple successful change management initiatives, and has developed collaborative, self-directed teams that have achieved remarkable results. She has been recognized nationally for the programs that she has developed for women and girls. Lynn also provides interim leadership to nonprofit organizations who are experiencing a transition in their leadership. In this role she has stabilized organizations that were struggling due to a lack of leadership and developed systems that strengthen the organization in preparation for the new leader.

You can reach me at info@lynnkjones.com or at 805.448.7681.
