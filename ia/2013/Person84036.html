Madeline Schaider, Your Marin Realtor's Summary

My goal is to assist buyers and sellers to fulfill their real estate dreams -- to become satisfied customers and clients. As an experienced Marin County real estate broker, I go the extra mile to find my buyers the home of their dreams, whether it's on or off the market. For sellers, I am dedicated to marketing their home to a timely and successful close and to maximize their return. As a top producing broker, I am looking for opportunities to assist you. I am dedicated to putting you first, to accomplish all of your real estate needs and desires.

After more than 22 years and close to 350 closed real estate transactions, I continue my own education to learn as much about the Marin County housing market as possible, so I can bring that knowledge to my clients. Some of this information is summarized on my website/blog LIVINGINMARIN [www.livinginmarin.com]. This is only an introduction to my ability to focus on my clients--their needs and desires--and to provide in a most personal way my vast resources and honed skills in property selection, negotiations, financing, and more. My focus is to keep my clients ahead of the curve, whether they are buying or selling. I assist them to understand the varied Marin micro-markets, neighborhoods, financing options, weather areas, and our fine schools.

Real estate is my passion. Your satisfaction and success is my goal. Marin County is truly a paradise on earth; each day I am grateful to have the opportunity to live here and to share Marin with my clients.

For more information, please check out my blog: http://livinginmarin.com/blog, email me at madeline@livinginmarin.com, and follow me on Twitter@livinginmarin
