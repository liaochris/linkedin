Jeffrey Davis' Summary

PR executive with more than 20 years of news media and national public relations experience in corporate positioning and reputation management, particularly in media relations, media training, crisis communications and social media. Prior to entering public relations, served as a newspaper reporter for daily newspapers in Ohio, New Jersey and Maryland. Active nationally in the public relations industry, authors a blog on trends in public relations and is a regular speaker on public relations, particularly in the area of PR and social media.
