Lily Qian Falzone's Summary

I am an licensed California Real Estate Broker whose focus on providing financing for Residential and Commercial properties and businesses.

RESIDENTIAL LOANS:
We offer all types of Purchase and Refinance loans including government loans like FHA and VA; Conforming and Jumbo loans up to $12 Million. We also offer profolio loan products for those "out of the box" clients such as reduced documentation income and foreign nationals.

We are a direct lender as well as broker; so I can shop the best rate and program for you. While many talks about how difficult to qualify, I know the secret to closing those hard-to-do-loans and are closing them every day - fast!

In addition, I am an experienced lender on Reverse Mortgage for Seniors.

COMMERCIAL LOANS:
I help businesses obtain financing through over 120 lending sources nationwide! Instead of waiting for month to hear back from your bank (and a decline decision in most cases), we provide our clients their term sheet & initial approval in writing, in 48 hours. We are closing commercial loans every day all over the country.

Specialties: - SBA / Commercial lending with 48 hour pre-approval in All States.
- Reverse Mortgages for Seniors
- Residential in California for conventional, jumbo, super jumbo FHA and VA.
