James Warfield's Summary

I love to engage an audience.

Tease, tempt, wow. Make them laugh, get them involved, spread your butter, love your brand, sit their bums firmly in their seats and open their eyes a little bit wider. That slight turn of the head, placing one ear more closely to the speaker, that's the effect I have.

This goal isn't bound by media.

I'm a potent mix of designer, conceptor, brand creator, animator, film maker and director. The wide, world of web feels like home. I've strategised, digitised and advertised. Bring me more.

I devour new ways of thinking. I adapt, experiment, play and work hard for it. My vision inspires others and my passion makes it happen.

Design, marketing and advertising change subtly and yet massively each day. Shifting, merging, colliding. I actively develop my understanding of this curious landscape and continue to evolve with it.

—

Alongside commercial work, I create self-initiated projects as Wig-01; a partnership experimenting within art, design and ideas.

—
