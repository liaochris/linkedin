Bram Van Londersele's Summary

I was born on the 28th of May 1986 in Aalst, Belgium.
After I grew up and had a lovely time in Meerbeke (Ninove), I went to study at Ghent University.

In June 2010 I obtained my MSc in Geomatics and Surveying.

From september 2010 until october 2011 I managed Tracking projects at Ghent University at the Department of Geography.

Meanwhile, I founded and ran my own company, called 'Landmeter & Geomaticus', which is Dutch for 'Surveyor and Geomatician'.

Together with two other surveyors I founded 'MEET HET', a surveying agency with a focus on both B2B and individual land owners.

My bachelor thesis (2008) dealt about 'airborne LIDAR hydrography'.

For my master thesis I monitored moving objects by using bluetooth, so called bluetooth tracking. I was tracking the bluetooth devices at Rock Werchter in 2009.
Here for I had an alliance with 2 Dutch companies: Flucon B.V. and Realworld Systems.
