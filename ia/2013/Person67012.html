John Sassman's Projects

  • Co-Author Local Government Needs Assessment

    • September 2011 to June 2012
    Team Members: John Sassman, Melia N. Dunn, Jeff Knoche, Laura Hammett, Ian Reynolds, Karl Burhop

    On this project, I worked with a team of fellow Peace Corps volunteers who worked in Local Governments across Albania. Collectively, we researched and analyzed challenges facing municipalities and devised potential strategies and solutions based on Peace Corps volunteer perspectives. Primary findings were based on inconsistencies within Human Capital and adequate and appropriate professional development for staff at all levels. This work was conducted at the request of the Albanian Ministry of the Interior and eventually presented to key international development players in Albania such as USAID, the Albanian American Development Foundation and the national association for municipalities based in Tirana, Albania.
