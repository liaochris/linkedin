Katie Cavanaugh's Summary

Katie Cavanaugh is known as the Intrepid Success Coach, helping conscious and heart-centered entrepreneurs discover their intrepid nature and changing the world. Author, Intuitive, Mentor and Coach for Visionary Entrepreneurs, Katie helps those who are ready to align their spiritual journey with their life purpose. She is passionate about helping her clients to heal the wounds of limiting beliefs and to step into the brilliance of infinite possibility and prosperity.

Specialties: Katie is now working with a growing number of people in her Clear Path Academy, in addition to working with Corporate & Non-Profit Team Building Processes, her keynote speaking, books, workshops and events, she teaches you how to define, design and develop your heart-centered business practices as you claim your Creative Genius. It's time to reach more people and to create a life of abundance - all while living your passionate purpose and claiming your INTREPID Nature. "Together we will illuminate our healing journey and transform the world!"
