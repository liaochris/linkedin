Corey Pettit's Summary

Communications specialist with a background in communication program implementation, video production, event planning/management, and media and community management. Diverse skillset including public relations campaign development, social media management, copywriting, editing, and freelance reporting and photography.

After graduating with a degree in Hospitality Management, I realized my real love of communications and went back to school to study broadcast journalism. I was fortunate to land a job with ScrewAttack Entertainment shortly thereafter, where I served in a capacity of roles, including: news correspondent, media and public relations director, social media manager, video production assistant, and event manager and planner. I was invited back to Southwest Airlines, where I interned during my senior year of college, to interview for a position on my old team. After providing internal customer support and working on special projects for a year, I joined the Inflight Communications Team, where I've been further enhancing my experiences in the area I love most--communications.

I'm always looking for an adventure in freelance!
