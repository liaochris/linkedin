Angela Schaefers' Summary

Angela interviews people around the globe to share their inspiring stories of overcoming challenges and living life purposefully. Including non-profits who are creating change locally and globally.

Angela speaks and shares her story of overcoming challenges, including stage IV cancer, to discover her life purpose. Her speaking experiences include; non-profit organizations and community service groups as their keynote, support groups and private sector corporations.

Speaking topics include;
~the effects and dynamics of the special needs community, in the home and the community
~using effective communication to address conflict
~understanding how your values and beliefs affect reaching your career goals
~the stages of grief
~overcoming shame and guilt
~why your story matters and how sharing it can encourage and inspire others

Angela blogs, sharing her life experiences and what she has learned to encourage others. She writes at Examiner.com sharing professional networking tips in the Tampa Bay area. She writes for Inspirational Woman Magazine sharing inspirational thoughts and tips. Angela authored her memoir Grief to Grace and recently published her book Your Story Matters You Matter A guide to healing, learning from and sharing your story. She is completing her next two books; Cancer Doesn’t Come Wrapped In A Pretty Ribbon and More Grace.

Angela has previously counseled families, couples, children and individuals, provided career coaching and has trained and worked as a Chaplain. Angela has worked with and consulted with various non-profit organizations, the State of Florida’s Early Intervention Program, the county of Los Angeles (CA) WorkForce Program and the County of Orange (CA) Alternative Dispute Resolution department. Angela has provided training and developed curriculum to train lay counselors and to teach self-improvement, effective communication and conflict resolution. She has facilitated support groups for grief and divorce.
