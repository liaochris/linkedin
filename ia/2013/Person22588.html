Cheri Elson Sperber's Summary

Cheri Elson Sperber is certified by the State Bar of California Board of Legal Specialization as a Certified Specialist in Estate Planning, Probate and Trust Law. Cheri’s practice emphasizes Estate Planning, Probate, Estate Administration, Conservatorship Law and Special Needs Trusts. Cheri has a particular passion exploring the issues involved in aging and addressing the changes our society will face as Baby Boomers become seniors and brings the highest professional standards and deep human compassion to her clients.

Cheri earned her Bachelor of Arts in Music from University of California, Santa Cruz and her Juris Doctorate from the Ventura College of Law, graduating second in her class with honors. Cheri was admitted to the California State Bar on June 2, 2001.

Cheri is an active leader of the Ventura County Bar Association, having sat on the Board of Directors three separate terms. Cheri also served as the Chair of the Estate Planning & Probate Law Section. Currently, Cheri is the community legal liaison for the Wellness & Caregiver Center of Ventura County, and a Board member of the Ventura County Adult Abuse Prevention Council. Cheri is also a member of the Alzheimer’s Association Program Committee, which meets quarterly to ensure that programs are consistent with the organization’s mission.
