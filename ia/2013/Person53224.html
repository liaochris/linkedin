Hiro Chandwani's Summary

Currently engaged in Consultancy in projects management of CSP Thermal electricity generation, concentrated heat source for process industries, water desalination etc. Design and Development, Manufacturing of components and equipment to produce electricity from CSP Thermal and other renewable sources of energy.

Specialties: Have developed complete power plant equipment for generating electricity using CSP Thermal technology using Parabolic Trough mirrors for concentration. We are in the process of starting manufacture of components for above.

We have complete know how for most efficient and cost effective CSP thermal systems for power generation and CST for direct industrial heat applications.

Hiro Chandwani's Experience

Chairman

Hiro Energy-Tech Ltd.

November 2009 – Present (4 years)

I have also been doing research and development work on renewable sources of energy mainly on CSP Thermal plants. I have floated a company by name of “Hiro Energy-Tech Ltd.” We have designed each and every component of the CSP plants and are in a process to start the manufacture of critical components like parabolic trough mirrors and receiver tubes etc. shortly. We can undertake confidently turnkey projects of CSP Thermal plants at lower capital costs compared to any of the overseas technology suppliers. Apart from CSP Thermal technology, we offer services for consultancy, design and development work on renewable energy from oceans. We also offer complete solutions in water and waste water management using latest membrane technology as well as distillation processes.
I am actively involved in the development process of CSP Thermal plant technology and keep my self-updated by regularly posting my comments, giving suggestions on the subject on new developments internationally. The technology developed by us is not only cost effective but is more efficient with low operation and maintenance costs. We can solve water problem faced by CSP Thermal technology suppliers.
We have developed water desalination systems based on membrane technology as well as distillation technology using energy efficient systems.
