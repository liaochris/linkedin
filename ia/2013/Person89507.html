Software Architect and Lead Software Engineer

Rightscenter

April 1999 – June 2002 (3 years 3 months)

Led a team of seven engineers in the design and development of a high performance web application. The product allowed literary agents to input data and create presentations for titles they represent and give secure, granular access to publishers around the world as part of their sales and marketing process. In addition, the application facilitated online negotiation of rights transactions. This enterprise application also allowed customers to enter a rich set of back-office information to manage their everyday business. They could then share subsets of this information with other employees throughout their company in a secure, granular manner. The access control module restricted access to authorized individuals and kept a log of all changes to the data, providing full audit capabilities. The product was fully Unicode compatible and the code was internationalized, allowing us present the application interface in multiple languages while maintaining a single codebase.
