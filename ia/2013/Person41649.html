I believe that in the IT and software industry where technology changes so quickly, anything can be improved, optimised and refined.
This leaves space for personal challenges and room for improvement, with the final goal of delivering a superior end result.
This is especially true in the CRM sector, where every business owner aims for an impeccable customer experience, while keeping the costs reasonable.
This is the reason why the best CRM solutions are delivered in multiple phases that constantly evolve and change, leaving space for a better user adoption along the way.

What is fascinating about the CRM business is that most businesses are often similar and I have to be able to quickly pick up the small details that make a certain business unique.
At that point of discovery the CRM solution can be optimised to gather for those unique aspects and processes.
This peculiarity of the CRM sector exposes me to numerous businesses and gives me ideas, knowledge and insights of what people want, what generally works and what does not.
All the above insights and knowledge help me and InsightfulCRM to guide customers, and help us design and produce solutions that just work.
