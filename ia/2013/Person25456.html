Claudia W. Scholz, Ph.D.'s Publications

  • Forthcoming: MOOCs and the Liberal Arts College (position paper)

    • Journal of Online Learning & Teaching (MERLOT JOLT)
    Authors: Claudia W. Scholz, Ph.D.

    The author examines the lessons from Massive Open Online Courses for small liberal arts colleges (LACs) in the U.S. Are MOOCs a threat to LACs? a tool for reducing costs? or a foil to spur small institutions to improve and assert their value? The paper examines how LACs might draw on the best tools, approaches and structures emerging from the MOOC revolution in order to help students build lifelong learning communities.
