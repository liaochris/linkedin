Eric Hardenbrook's Summary

Computers and I began our relationship in the mid 1970's when the US Marine Corps trained me to be a computer repair technician. After receiving my discharge from the service I opened a woodworking and furniture business, then spent five years in production chemistry before moving my family to beautiful Bend Oregon as part of a church plant team.

In Bend I worked in a lab doing analytical chemistry before opening Hardenbrook Construction and embarking on a 20 year career building homes and developing land. Identifying profitable projects, managing large teams of employees and contractors while setting and exceeding client expectations were the training ground for my current work with Hardenbrook Marketing Solutions.

Those decades of business and marketing experience, combined with a driving desire to help businesses to connect with their ideal customer groups spurred the launch of our agency. Most of our team works remotely and each is a specialist in their chosen field. Writers, coding geeks, graphic artists, videographers, web designers, printers and more, our team of specialists provides the advice, strategies and systems that will boost lead generation, lead conversion and customer loyalty for our clients.

Even the best marketing strategies can't fully compensate for a business that is lacking in its core operations. For that reason, we begin by helping business owners to identify and deploy the inexpensive yet foundational elements that a solid marketing campaign can be built upon. This approach sets the stage for businesses to double or even triple the ROI of their marketing dollars using our proven techniques. Few business owners utilize the combination of proven marketing principles our team of experts teach and implement.

Specialties include Marketing Consulting, Strategies and problem solving. Internet Marketing, Local Search Optimization and Reputation Management are our focus.

I do a lot of reading and discussion, tracking the amazingly rapid changes in the world of Internet Marketing and the tools and techniques available for our clients. Recognizing the potential of these tools and integrating them with time tested principles keeps us from chasing every bright shiny object that comes along, focusing instead on producing lasting results.
