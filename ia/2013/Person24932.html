Cindi Spence Matt's Summary

Born and raised in Minnesota, I ventured to Omaha, Nebraska for college, receiving a business degree from Creighton University in 1993. I then returned home for law school, receiving a JD from the University of Minnesota School of Law in 1996. After law school I landed a job in Buffalo, Minnesota, clerking for Wright County District Court Judge Bruce Douglas. When my clerkship ended, I joined a local law firm and eventually became shareholder and managing attorney at Johnson, Larson, Peterson & Matt, P.A.

In June of 2010, I decided to open my own law firm in Buffalo, Minnesota, called Matt Legal Services, LLC. My goal at Matt Legal Services, LLC is to provide exceptional, experienced representation to each and every client. My passion is guardianship and conservatorship law. I also practice in the areas of civil litigation, probate, basic estate planning and family law.

When I'm not lawyering, there is a decent chance you will find me running. I am currently training for my 13th marathon.

Specialties: guardianships and conservatorships
