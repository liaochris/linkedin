Jim Tuzzeo's Summary

As a Certified Financial Educator (CFEd.), Jim Tuzzeo knows the importance of strong relationships. Trust, caring relationships, and up to date knowledge of the insurance and financial industries and markets are just a few of the elements that qualify Jim to educate and advise his clients in making the important decisions for the achievement of their financial goals and objectives. As a small business owner and corporate executive, Jim knows and understands the multitude of retirement planning challenges that face business owners, employees, and individuals. That background and experience provides Jim with the in-depth know-how to assist and guide his clients in their pursuit of financial security and independence. His experience and history in the financial field, and his attention to detail are other personal characteristics that drive him to provide the most effective and competitive strategies and solutions to meet his client’s desired goals and objectives.

As President of JT Financial Solutions, Jim has been assisting individuals and small business owners with their insurance and financial needs for over two decades. JT Financial Solutions, Inc., provides personalized financial, insurance, and investment solutions to individuals and businesses. JTFS utilizes the advanced marketing and legal resources of national insurance and investment companies for the benefit of its clients in providing the most effective case design for achieving specific goals and objectives of our clients. In creating trust through superior service and quality products, JT Financial Solutions provides its clients high quality, personalized financial and insurance strategies and solutions for wealth accumulation, growth, and preservation for ultimate financial security and independence.
