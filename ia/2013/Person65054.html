Alicia Buzan, CPRW's Summary

As a certified resume writer, I offer more than 10 years of experience writing job-winning résumés. I absolutely love what I do and gain genuine satisfaction from supporting my clients in the job search.

What I offer to you:

★ Experience. I have written 1,000s of resumes from recent college gradutes to CEOs of Fortune 500s and everywhere in between.

★ A true passion for this craft. I want to help you to succeed in your professional life. It's a gift when you can say that you love your job and I want to help you get THAT job.

★ A resume package that is specifically hand-crafted for you. No one else.

★ Confidence. A well-written résumé is one of the best investments you will ever make.

Working as a resume writer allows me to wear a couple of different hats. First, as a small business owner, I founded JobAuthor to provide clients 1-on-1 job search support. JobAuthor is supported by both the NRWA (National Resume Writers Association) and the PARW/CC (Professional Association of Resume Writers and Career Coaches). The JobAuthor team consists of certified writers and career strategists available to offer a full spectrum of writing services including: résumé writing, LinkedIn development, executive bios, editing, cover letters, recruitment resources, professional letter writing and branding strategies.

I also work with RiseSmart outplacement services to assist clients in job transition, resume preparation, cover letter creation, LinkedIn profiles, executive bios and overall support.
