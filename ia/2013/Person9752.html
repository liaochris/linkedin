Arnd Bronkhorst's Summary

Based in the Netherlands, Arnd has worked as a professional horse photographer since 1986. He covers international equestrian events, including 4 Olympic Games and 6 World Equestrian Games, as well as a wide variety of other subjects concerning horses.

He has collected his horse images in the online horse image database at www.arnd.nl. There are more than 150.000 equine images available of almost every subject imaginable that is related to the horse.

Through the years, he has asked some friends and colleagues to join him, and they contribute their horse photos and images to the database on a regular basis. They are Jacques Toffi and Frank Sorge from Germany, Frédéric Chéhu, Pierre Costabadie and Cédric Vlemmings from France, Charles Mann from the USA, Katya Druz from Russia and Paula da Silva from Italy. They all add their unique approach to this fascinating subject, thus creating an even wider array of equine images to choose from, for clients from all over the world, including all the major equestrian sponsors, international organizations and horse magazines.

Specialties

Photography of anything horse. Distribution of those equestrian photos through the online horse images database.
