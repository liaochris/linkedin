David Wolinsky's Summary

People have used the words "prolific," "reasonably handsome," and "hey you get out of my driveway!"* to describe me. I'll have to take their word for it.

All I know is I'm a full-time freelancer writer for places like NBC Chicago, Adult Swim, Funny or Die, and Maxim. I'm also a comedy-writing instructor at Second City and teach the games biz to indie developers at DePaul University. Above all else, my focus is on helping people, be it to humanizing celebrities, exploring issues affecting local entrepreneurs, or helping my students connect deeper with their own creativity.

I've got experience with writing, editing, video producing, and interviewing for digital, print, and video journalism. Currently, I'm working on a web series over the summer, developing some new classes, working on a book, and am always on the prowl for more gigs.

*For the record, I was pretending to tie my shoes to pick up a nickel next to the grate on the curb. Not intentionally trespassing. Anyway the whole story is moot because it was merely a drawing of a nickel on a paper bag.
