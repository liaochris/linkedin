Kent L. Boyer's Summary

In 2011 I began to write art and performance reviews for various publishing entities. In 2012, I began working exclusively as a journalist and online media consultant.

Between 2008 and 2012, my work contribution was in the non-profit sector in the areas of curriculum and program development and management, social media and marketing.

I have an earned doctorate from a North Central Association of Schools and Colleges-accredited college. My higher education administrative experience includes heavy program development and curriculum design (both new curriculum and phasing in updates to curriculum over a four-year period,) participation in three self-study periods and three accreditation visitations (SACS, Middle States, and professional curriculum accreditation), and professional development and coaching/mentoring for faculty trained to be clinicians.

I have taught at community colleges, 4 year liberal arts colleges, vocational schools, and ﬁrst professional degree-granting colleges.

In total my 20 years experience in program management breaks down like this:
-higher education classroom teaching (at associate, bachelor, and doctorate levels)
-Fortune 100 corporate program and team managment (individual contributor to Vice President levels)
-higher education program administrative experience (managed 6 million dollar budget, 45 faculty members and over 70 courses for a 1500 FTE graduate professional college)
-program and curriculum development and implementation for community-based non-profit agency
