My Street Cred >>>

I'm in the business of information, creative strategy & project management. I evolve with technology and seek to get ahead of industry standard. I'm a self-starter, and I'm keen at snuffing out logistical errors/liabilities/legalities before they can occur.

Primarily a creative project manager for a state industry association, I work to execute the vision of the executive director through media (publishing, photography, presentations, video, graphic design) as well as assist the meeting planner in developing new features, branding & campaigns for events. Always putting membership first, I brain storm & develop new concepts & products that will add to the value of the membership.

I am personally vested in the wellness business via B.Real Magazine and the Radiant Health Institute, where I earned my Life Coaching certification. I also teach workshops at the Rutherford Co. Farmer's Market related to green living, home & holistic health.
