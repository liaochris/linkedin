Brian Dally's Summary

Specialist in new concept formation, positioning, launch and growth. Orchestrator of the serendipity teams need to identify and maximize slope of their growth curve. I believe startup growth doesn’t happen by accident, cannot be formulaically planned, but must be earned through painstaking cycles of creative learning.

After taking a brief break between startups to get involved in the thriving NC Triangle startup ecosystem, I co-founded GROUNDFLOOR with Nick Bhargava in 2013. Previously, I led a team within Bandwidth.com through a successful product-market pivot that resulted in the concept, brand and launch of republic wireless.

Specialties: Technology Platforms: Mobile internet & applications, IP communications, social networks, embedded software; Industries: Crowdfunding, mobile/wireless, consumer internet, gaming, management consulting; Functions: product management, business development, user-centered design, consumer technology marketing

Brian Dally's Experience

Co-Founder & CEO

GROUNDFLOOR

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Financial Services industry

May 2013 – Present (6 months) Raleigh-Durham, North Carolina Area

A new peer-to-peer lending startup launching in 2013, GROUNDFLOOR (http://groundfloor.us) connects real estate developers with individual investors. Together, they enable a new kind of financing that cuts out the banks and other undesirable middlemen. Leveraging the transparency and breadth of the Internet, developers achieve unparalleled financial flexibility to build new projects, while savers and investors get access to a time-honored vehicle for building wealth.

GROUNDFLOOR is finance, raised to the power of us. The banks won't like it, but you will.

Chief Instigator

Fomentum Labs

September 2012 – April 2013 (8 months) Raleigh-Durham, North Carolina Area

Actively investigated new industries for the Schumpeter treatment. Mentored fellow entrepreneurs, reviewed potential investments and vetted concepts. Conducted consulting and contract assignments to deliver insight, analysis and powerful new ideas in product, business development and marketing.

SVP & General Manager

republic wireless, a division of Bandwidth.com

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Telecommunications industry

January 2010 – September 2012 (2 years 9 months) Cary, NC

On track to grow into a $100+ million business, republic wireless is an innovative new carrier spearheading a wireless freedom movement to return value and control of the smartphone experience to our members. Leveraging the power of the Internet and an engaged community, republic wireless offers unlimited voice, text, and data service for only $19 per month, with no contract and with no overages or extra charges.

republic wireless is a division of Bandwidth.com, whose network and solutions also power other innovative communication services such as Google, Pinger, Skype, Groupme, RingCentral, Phonebooth, and many others. I led the mobile division from inception through a crucial product-market pivot and into a successful merger with another larger division to support republic’s growth.
