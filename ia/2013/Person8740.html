Anthony Hitt's Summary

Anthony Hitt serves as Engel & Völker USA's Chief Operating Officer, where he is dedicated to the prestigious German company's emergence as a distinct provider of premium segment real estate in the North America. He is currently responsible for overseeing the roll out of E&V brand, maintaining high client service standards, broker & agent recruiting in addition to training & coaching. Hitt provides support to E&V License Partners & their shops, helping them leverage the E&V brand to build profitable brokerages & attract top agents.

Prior to joining E&V, Hitt worked with Sotheby’s International Realty for 8 years as a top producing agent, management professional and trainer. As a Realtor®, Hitt closed approximately 400 real estate sales transactions amounting to nearly half a billion dollars & was continually acknowledged for his superior service & results. The Wall Street Journal and the Los Angeles Times both recognized Hitt as a top-producing agent, LA Magazine named him one of the city's "Super Agents" & LA Business Journal awarded Hitt as their first & only "Green Agent of the Year."

Hitt developed his keen understanding of entrepreneurship & client satisfaction as a businessman in his home state of Missouri. He mastered leadership & marketing first as CEO of an 85-person technology company, as lead negotiator for several mergers & acquisitions & then as owner of his own advertising firm.

Hitt credits his business & real estate success to his philosophy to provide exceptionally personal customer service and constantly implement the most up-to-date sales techniques possible. In fact, he began his own national training company in 2005 to teach others strategic ways to “raise the bar” in the real estate industry and, even now, he mines new territory for improvement.

Hitt also authors books designed to share his knowledge. His published works on success in life and business include “Essentials of Personal Achievement,” “Taking Charge," & “Positive Impressions."
