Jon Magliola's Summary

Jon Magliola has over 15 years of experience leading the design and development of digital products. These include highly interactive entertainment experiences (including projects large and small for Comedy Central, Discovery Channel, NBC Universal, and AOL Entertainment) to information-heavy experiences (including The Associated Press, Time Inc., as well as the various properties of the original Lycos Network). Beyond that, Jon also makes time for not-for-profit clients, including the Museum for African Art and the Philadelphia Zoo.

Specialties: Product Strategy, User Experience, Design Direction, Interaction Design, User Interface Design, Information Architecture, User Research, Usability Testing
