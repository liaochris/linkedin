Michael Hartnett's Summary

I'm Mike, director of photography and co-founder of Vinegar Hill and The New American Storytellers. I've shot in 22 countries and am experienced in both film and digital workflows including RED and the Arri ALEXA.

I've done work for nationally recognized clients such as Discovery Channel, Procter & Gamble, Netflix and the National Association of Broadcasters (NAB).

I love telling stories, and I love bringing a cinematic look to everything i shoot.

When i'm not behind a camera you can find me at my piano, eating sushi, playing legos, or with my lovely wife Jocelyn.

Specialties: Cinematographer, DP, Camera op, RED, Arri Alexa, Super 16mm
