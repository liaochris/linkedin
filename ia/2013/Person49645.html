STRATEGIC Thinking...TARGETED Programs...Meaningful RESULTS


The Perry Group is a New England-based, full service, strategic communications firm that specializes in enhancing and protecting the reputation of our clients.

From Fortune 500 companies and small, family-owned businesses, to higher education institutions and non-profit organizations, we design and implement targeted public relations and marketing communications campaigns that position and define our clients to achieve desired and meaningful results

GREGG PERRY's Experience

President

The Perry Group, LLC

January 2009 – Present (4 years 11 months)

Established The Perry Group in 2009 as a full-service strategic communications firm that specializes in enhancing and protecting the reputations of organizations both large and small.

The Perry Group has assembled a team of highly experienced independent communicators from across the country - a team with diverse backgrounds in media relations, journalism, the law, politics, marketing, branding and creative arts. By bringing together a group of professionals from varied disciplines, we are able to quickly assess an organizations reputation and communications needs from multiple perspectives and offer creative solutions that deliver targeted, meaningful results.

Senior Vice President-Managing Director

Prism Public Affairs

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry

February 2005 – December 2008 (3 years 11 months)

Develop strategic plans, integrated communications programs and message platforms. Conduct media training programs for senior executives at Fortune 500 companies nationwide.
