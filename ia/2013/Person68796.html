Joseph Tate's Summary

After finishing a Ph.D. on Shakespeare in 2005 and publishing a book the same year on Radiohead, I made the transition from university-level teaching to full-time web application design and development. Since 2007, I've worked for the University of Washington's Network Management Tools Team. In 2011, I founded A Cleaner Internet, LLC where I am Chief Design Officer. Our first iOS app, clea.nr Videos, was launched in 2012.

Specialties: I specialize in making cross-platform, minimalist software experiences. I also specialize in: user-centered interface design, web production management, information architecture, SEO, web standards; HTML5, CSS 3 and JavaScript; strategic plan development and implementation; technical writing; producing and maintaining procedural documentation; team facilitation, committee service, employee supervision and management. ITIL v3 Certified.
