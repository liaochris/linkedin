Brett Handler, Dreamstar Custom Homes' Summary

A state licensed building contractor with more than 25 years of residential building experience, Brett is one of the first two builders in Palm Beach County who is a Certified Green Professional™ by The National Association of Home Builders.

Dreamstar was founded on the premise that top quality homes and unrivaled client attention are the foundations for an outstanding homebuilder. They focus on including “green” or environmental specifications into every home.

Because of Brett, Dreamstar also enjoys a stellar reputation for its community outreach programs and sponsorships. At a time when many charities and businesses are suffering, Brett is leading the charge to band together the South Florida building community by building a spacious, designer home that will impress its owners and raise thousands of dollars for The South Florida Affiliate of Susan G. Komen for the Cure®. So far, Dreamstar has raised almost $50,000 through a program Brett created called “Build for the Cure.”

Brett’s extensive, 10-year involvement with Junior Achievement led him to write and publish a book, “Dear Graduate.” The book is a unique insight aimed at high school and college graduates on how to manage a financially responsible lifestyle and how to empower a brighter financial future.

Additionally, Dreamstar organized the free distribution of 2,200 of these books to graduating seniors at several area high schools.

Specialties: New Custom Homes, Custom Renovations, Building Green
