Sascha Brossmann's Summary

MISSION

Bridging culture & technology with design to create value for people. (And sometimes using artistic means to explore and challenge where design's strategies and methods are not able to do so.)

SUMMARY

Freelance senior designer/creative director & consultant with focus on Experience Design. Visiting lecturer for Interaction Design at Kunsthochschule Berlin Weißensee (College of Art & Design) [http://kh-berlin.de] since 04/2009. Research fellow at the cluster of excellency ‘Image Knowledge Gestaltung’ (HU Berlin) since 03/2013. Mentor for IxDA. Nearly 15 years project experience (mostly direct contractor, many start-ups). Several nominations and awards on an international level. Leadership experience (primary style: coach / mentor / enabler / catalyst, mostly smaller agile teams), highly competent interdisciplinary facilitator.

Broad general knowledge and deep expert knowledge spanning design, technology and culture (“renaissance person”, rather mesh- than T-shaped). Excellent power of comprehension, keen perception and great breadth of understanding. Quickly grasps unknown topics, equally analytically and synthetically skilled, strategically thinking, passionately systemizing. Pragmatically moderated perfectionist ;-) ENTP (Myers-Briggs).

MAIN FIELDS OF WORK

Platforms & communities. ¶ Knowledge amplification. ¶ Product & service development. ¶ Design strategy. ¶ Multi-channel (visual) communication & branding. ¶ Media installations, environments, and interventions. ¶ Design education.

SPECIAL INTERESTS

Sustainable tools, methods, and environments for work & communication in the post-industrial knowledge and service society ¶ Interdependencies and feedback processes between technology and culture/society ¶ “Innovation” [1] & iterative concept/insight processes (design thinking) ¶ Shaping design education and mentoring.

[1] In my opinion an emtpy cliché. Let's rather talk about some nitty-gritty: Problem? Why? Who? What? What for? Added value?…

Specialties

creative direction, user experience, interaction design, concept, ideation, product/service research & development, design thinking, art direction, visual communication, graphics design, interface design, typography, usability, computational design, physical computing, web design, social networks, web platforms, web standards, branding, identity, publishing, semiotics, linguistics, media art, conceptual art, interactive sculpture, installations, interventions, education, mentoring
