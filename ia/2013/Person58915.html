Jarrod Tredway's Summary

Designer and photographer. Foodie, fun-lover, and pop culture fan. Florida, born and raised, but in a New York state of mind. Adventurous, to a point. Spirited, always.

An honors graduate of the University of Florida, Jarrod holds a M.S. in business management from the Warrington College of Business Administration and a B.F.A. in graphic design with a minor in art history from the College of Fine Arts. He's designed for TOMS, photographed for celebrity chef Giada De Laurentiis, and worked in the non-profit sector with Heart of the Bride for Orphans.

Jarrod has led top-performing teams in presenting to executives at Nielsen and IDEO, and has maintained a high profile throughout his academic career. He was the 2012 College of Fine Arts commencement speaker at the University of Florida as the Outstanding Graduating Senior. He now works as a global designer for west elm, a part of Williams-Sonoma, Inc.

Jarrod Tredway's Projects

  • Spice Wheel

    • February 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jarrod Tredway

    The spice wheel is a re-imagining of the traditional spice rack centered on the ideas of experimentation and exploration. Wall-mounted and rotational, the wheel features spices organized by origin, with lines mapping flavor profiles. The user simply unscrews the bottommost spice, traces the lines to the next selection, then rotates the wheel to advance. The spice wheel is a way to sample the spice world one quarter-teaspoonful at a time.

  • VOSS Global Good Report

    • May 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jarrod Tredway

    The Voss Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing access to clean water to communities in Sub-Saharan Africa and raising awareness of ongoing need in the region. Designed as an iBook, this global good report offers insight into the 2010 year at the Voss Foundation in an engaging and interactive way.
