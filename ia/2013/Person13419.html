Berkeley Wilde's Summary

Founder and Director of the Diversity Trust, social enterprise, influencing social change.

Equality, diversity and inclusion specialist since 2004. Working across the protected characteristics and groups in the Equality Act (2010), the Public Sector Equality Duty (2011) and the Equality Delivery System.

Trustee of QwesT FtM UK a national charity supporting Trans men.

Member of the National LGBT Partnership. Funded by the Department of Health and led by the Lesbian and Gay Foundation (LGF). Advising and consulting on national policy.

Equality Associate with Equality South West.

Chair of LGBT Bristol and Co-Chair of the South West Region LGB and Trans Equality Network.

Specialist in community-based engagement and research working with equalities communities.

2003-2004 working for a Brussels based non-governmental organisation (NGO) as Communication and Information Officer.

2002-2003 working on a contract, commissioned by the Department of Health exploring the reasons men don't access primary care services. Partnership working with the Men's Health Forum and a contributor to the All Party Parliamentary Group on men's health.

Health Promotion Manager at THT West and Cymru between 2000-2002.

Health Promotion Officer with Aled Richards Trust 1995-2000.

Passionate about coaching, talking therapy and physical therapy. Practitioner and teacher.

Berkeley Wilde's Publications

  • 'Equality and Diversity Toolkit’

    • Bristol City Council
    • April 2013
    Authors: Berkeley Wilde, Russell Thomas

    Working in partnership with Safer Bristol to produce an ‘Equality and Diversity Toolkit’ for substance misuse services. We have produced an ‘Equalities Standard’, ‘Good Practice Guidance’, Fact Sheets, a DVD and an online resource.

  • 'Delivering Equality': Somerset Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual & Trans Health and Social Care

    • NHS Somerset and Somerset LINk
    • November 2012
    Authors: Berkeley Wilde

    NHS Somerset and Somerset LINk jointly commissioned the Diversity Trust to research the experiences of health and social care with Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual & Trans (LGB and Trans) communities in Somerset.

  • 'Seldom Heard': Involving and Consulting ’hard to reach’ patient groups

    • North Bristol NHS Trust (NBT)
    • July 2010
    Authors: Berkeley Wilde

    I was commissioned by North Bristol NHS Trust (NBT) to produce a report and action plan on ‘Involving and Consulting ’hard to reach’ patient groups’.

  • 'Sorted Out': Bristol Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual & Trans Survey

    • Bristol City Council
    • October 2009
    Authors: Berkeley Wilde

    Safer Bristol commissioned me to make recommendations on the monitoring of gender identity and sexual orientation and to identify the prevalence and patterns of substance use amongst LGB and Trans communities, and to make recommendations to improve access to services for LGB and Trans communities.

  • A Lifestyle Choice? Myths and Facts about Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual and Trans People

    • Camden LGBT Forum
    • 2008
    Authors: Berkeley Wilde, Lou Hart
