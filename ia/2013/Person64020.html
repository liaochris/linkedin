Jimm Singer's Summary

I am a professionally trained voice over talent with more than 15 years experience behind the mic. I've worked in a variety of industries/media: audiobooks, talking toys to corporate narration, video games to radio and TV commercials. Also I am an experienced writer in many different genres, from entertainment, advertising to technical writing.

I have a degree in Business Management with an emphasis in Computer Science - Programming.

Strengths and Specialties: Voice Talent, Technical Writing, Copywriting: Radio and TV; Multimedia (Video Games; Web Narratives / Educational / Instructional); Corporate Narration.

While I continue to enjoy voice acting, I am also actively pursuing more writing projects and welcome discussions that will lead to new projects.

1997 – Present (16 years) San Francisco Bay Area

What can I say, I just ooze talent...and my clients reap the rewards. My career began as a voice talent in 1997, voicing a multitude of projects that include: radio/TV commercials, talking educational toys, video games and corporate narrations. I've since added audiobooks, interactive voice response (IVR) and on-hold (telephony) to my repertoire.

As a writer, I've penned in several different mediums as well: commercial radio copy, industrial, software documentation and marketing.

Recently I've added webmaster to my list of talents. I have been maintaining the website for the Society of Technical Communications (STC), Sacramento Metro Chapter.
