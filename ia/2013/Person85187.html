Marco Fantozzi's Summary

I'm in digital media production since 1996. The standard internet browser was netscape at that time. During these years I slowly moved from a Graphic Designer position to a Project/Marketing Manager one. From "moving pixel" to "moving people".

I've designed digital scenographies for international opera theatres as well as I've designed new routines for making prospects take action.
I've designed a program for teaching After Effects to aspiring videodesigners as well as a web-tool for easily share and sell digital content.

Now digital agencies and startups hire me for having results-driven explainer animation and effective video marketing campaign.

In 2013 i had a couple of ideas for starting an online business. By pitching them I won, in the same weekend, both the first prize at Next La repubblica and the second at Milan Startup Weekend. Now I manage a digital startup in the early stage as co-founder.

How can I help you?

Specialties: project management, UX design, UI design, video design, color correction, teaching
