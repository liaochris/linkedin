Dino Iannuzzi Mobile Expert's Summary

*** Does Your Business Need A Mobile Site? ***

Traditional websites are not made for mobile viewing, and the content will not fit the screen correctly, making it extremely difficult to mobile users to view your website. If you have Flash on your website, people who visit your site via mobile devices may crash their tablets or phones, which is a surefire way of losing that potential customer indefinitely. Even if you don’t use Flash, heavy graphics and intricate designs can also slow mobile devices down significantly.

Also, if consumers cannot tap to call you or easily find directions and store information as soon as they navigate to your mobile site, then it is not mobile-optimized. Remember, a mobile-optimized website will accommodate finger navigation as a mouse is not available on cellphones.

In addition, you’ll want to ensure that your mobile website is fully functional across all mobile platforms. Android-based cellphones may not have the same dimensions as an iPhone, and Blackberry phones won’t have the same dimensions as a Windows-based phone. An optimized mobile website will ensure that your content is readable regardless of where it is accessed from.

In conclusion, mobile websites are created as a way to turn your visitors into paying customers.

Without the convenient mobile-friendly features that most cellphone users have grown accustomed to, you run the risk of these visitors leaving your website and finding a similar company that is more accommodating. Building a mobile website is simple, affordable, and most of all, a way to keep your current customers and build trust with new ones.
