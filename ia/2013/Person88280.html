David Masover's Publications

  • Mastering Your Sales Process: How to Create a Winning Sales Process for You, Your Boss, and Your Prospects

    • February 4, 2010
    Authors: David Masover

    From Amazon: Whether you are new to sales or have years of experience, an individual salesperson or a manager looking to increase team effectiveness, David Masover's Mastering Your Sales Process: How to Create a Winning Sales Process for You, Your Boss, and Your Prospects provides the tools to develop a sales process that produces results. Two decades of global experience have provided Masover with the insight and clarity to develop his process selling solution. Experience has also convinced the author that a well-defined process executed with discipline and given the necessary support by sales management is the key to greater efficiency and profits. This book guides readers through successful process-based selling and the development of a customized, actionable personal sales plan. With a detailed, systematic approach to each of the steps in the sales process, from lead management to closing the deal, Masover has covered all of the bases in the challenging game of sales.

  • Managing the Sales Process: Six Steps to Sales Organization Success

    • July 18, 2012
    Authors: David Masover

    From Amazon: Too many sales organizations are just poorly run. Their systems are inadequate, and sales management often fails to understand how to improve these systems in a holistic way. Too often single tools are applied as solutions to a specific problem--or a collection of loosely related techniques are used, which also fails to comprehensively address the problem. A more successful approach includes an overarching framework designed to improve and streamline systems, and holding accountable everyone involved in the process. Managers need to learn how to manage the sales process from start to finish.

    Offering a comprehensive solution to the systemic issues confronted by under-performing sales organizations, Managing the Sales Process: Six Steps to Sales Organization Success will radically change your business for the better. The book presents a complete system of sales force management that begins with selecting the best salespeople, moves on to providing them with specific activities to pursue new business, outlines how to track and manage these activities, and specifies how to handle the role of accountability and consequences.

    Masover's all-inclusive strategy for system creation will help you to restructure your business for maximum effect--or to build it from scratch. Covering the basics with clarity and comprehensible language, he builds an actionable framework that enables you to apply his concepts with simplicity and ease.
