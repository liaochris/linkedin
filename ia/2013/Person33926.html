Delphi Ellis' Summary

I help people experiencing, depression, stress and anxiety to get their sparkle back. A qualified therapist, mentor and sparkly Dream Analyst, I empower people through positive recovery toolkits towards better mental health. My professional background includes bereavement and stress counselling, personal development training (for example, assertiveness and effective communication) and volunteer management.

Media appearances include presenting The Guide to Sleep on Daybreak, ITV's This Morning and the popular BBC Radio's weekend extravaganzas. Other features have included columns in Spirit & Destiny, Natural Health, The Guardian, Daily Express, Silent Voices and more.

I founded the first ever website for women (and their families) affected by ante-natal depression (stress, anxiety and depression during pregnancy) in 2004. Winner of the Spiritual Connextions Award for Service to Others and regional finalist for the Health and Social Care Awards for Mental Health and Wellbeing (2008).

Diplomas in Therapeutic Counselling (CBCAP) and Management & Leadersip (Chartered Management Institute). Registered IoEE and SEFDI trained Mentor.

Soul Counsellor | Dream Analyst | Mentor

Helping you Sparkle™

July 2011 – June 2013 (2 years) Bedford, United Kingdom

In one sentence, I help people get their sparkle back, through services focused on positive mental health and well-being.

My Listening Service provides a safe and non-judgemental space for you to talk, one-to-one and in confidence, either at my clinic in Bedford or Milton Keynes. With a sounding board and an ear to listen, relax and share in the company of a qualified, person-centred professional who can help you find your way through what matters most.

If you're someone who is ready for action, to set goals, or create an action plan for healthy living, my Development Service includes one-to-one mentoring and masterclasses, where together we can explore empowering techniques. This includes effective stress reduction - Ultimate Soul Survival. Boost your self-esteem and regain your confidence by reclaiming your positive glow.

I also offer spiritual coaching, helping you discover what it means to be you, spiritually speaking, in this world, as well as dream analysis, exploring these secret soul messages.

I am available for corporate and organisational training on the topics I cover in the private, public and voluntary sector. I am also an IoEE registered Enterprise (SEFDI trained) Mentor.

Cruse Bereavement Care promotes the well-being of bereaved people and enables anyone bereaved by death to understand their grief and cope with their loss. As well as providing care to all bereaved people, the charity also offers information, support and training services to those who are looking after them.
