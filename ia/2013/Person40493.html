Elijah Heiss' Summary

Hi, I'm Elijah!

My focus is to help earn the ROI that you want. I build and manage networks of Web sites that bring in thousands of lead conversions a month for the right customers looking to buy your products.

With over 8 years of experience as a Web professional, I'm quite fluent in the many areas of my field - from planning, designing, developing, tracking, testing, managing, marketing, and optimizing.

I've personally setup and managed hundreds of online marketing campaigns, analytics accounts, and webmaster tracking tools for clients. I'm quite familiar with the many forms of inbound marketing.

Working with both car and RV dealer sites, I spend most of my time optimizing Web sites for Search rankings and finding what best drives the right kind of traffic for my client.

Not only can I build the site of your dreams, but I can also drive traffic to it.

Through proper tagging of traffic and narrowing case studies down to the preferred funnel, I've helped increase conversions up to 400% for my clients.

I do not believe in user error – just better planning and better design. I believe in constant growth and see it my duty to never stop improving my skills with the ever expanding Web landscape.

My objectives as a Web professional:

Understand and capitalize on my client’s unique attributes and opportunities for users to connect with.

Deliver a great user experience, and serve as a steward for my client’s audience and their needs.

Research and document the needs of my client’s audience, and prioritize enhancements and new feature ideas based on audience benefit and my client’s business opportunity.

Partner with client sales, engineering and design to conceive and build great products as a team.
