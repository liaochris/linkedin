Eric Schoep, RRP's Summary

Eric Schoep was born and raised in Poway, California and has over 10 years of sales and marketing experience and 8 years of credit and collections experience. Since starting with Conrad in 2002, he has worked in the Finance Department, Collection Department, and Sales and Marketing Department. Eric possesses a strong background in web development and has earned certificates in Graphic Design. With his current position as Marketing Director, Eric has implemented many new products at Conrad including the “Client Access Web” communication portal and Conrad’s latest technological upgrade, Conradco.com.
