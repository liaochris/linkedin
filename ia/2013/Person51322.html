"Yo Pal" Hal Elrod's Summary

Hal Elrod is one of America's top corporate and college Keynote Speakers, a hall of fame business achiever, international Success Coach, a national champion Sales Manager, record-breaking Sales Rep, ultra-marathon runner, grateful husband & proud father.

Known as “Yo Pal Hal” since hosting his first radio show at age 15, his greatest triumph came at age 20 after he was hit head on by a drunk driver and found dead at the scene…

Despite being clinically dead for six minutes, in a coma for six days, breaking 11 bones and being told he may never walk again, Hal defied the logic of doctors and the temptations to be a victim, and he bounced back to prove that ALL of us are capable of overcoming extraordinary adversity to create extraordinary results in our personal and professional lives.

Hal has appeared on dozens of TV and radio shows across the country, and he’s been featured in numerous books, including The Education of Millionaires, the all-time bestselling Chicken Soup for the Soul series, Cutting Edge Sales, The 800-Pound Gorilla of Sales, Releasing the Chains, Living College Life In the Front Row, and The Author’s Guide To Building An Online Platform, to name a few.

Visit Hal's website at http://www.HalElrod.com

May 2006 – June 2006 (2 months)

Taking Life Head On is the inspiring true story of one young man whose drive to succeed against all odds catapulted him from near death to the Hall of Fame. At age 20, Hal Elrod was struck head on and found dead at the scene... Six minutes later fate decided it wasn't his time. Six days later he woke from a coma to the devastating news that he had suffered 13 broken bones and might never walk again. Defying the logic of doctors, the challenges of permanent brain damage and the temptations to be a victim, Hal bounced back.

Hal is considered by many to have already overcome and accomplish more than most people do in a lifetime. He is living proof that all of us are capable of creating miracles in our everyday lives, and in this book, he shows you how!
