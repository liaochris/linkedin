Daniel Piche's Summary

I spent the first 7 years of my career with Unisys in the area of Systems Engineering working in the Defence Industry in Montreal, Canada.

I spent the following 14 years of my career with Ericsson in various technical and managerial capacities, during which I traveled globally and specifically in the Americas.

I also managed the sales activities for small to medium size network build services centric companies (Radian and Baran Telecom) for 3 years.

I was President and led global sales activities for Cerion and Dataworkforce until November 2012.

In December 2012, I joined KMM Telecommunications. I am responsible for our marketing approach as well as handling large accounts at the executive levels.
