Mark Aiken's Summary

Freelance travel writer Mark Aiken specializes in adventure and outdoor recreation -- so it will come as no surprise that he has covered topics like World Cup soccer in South Africa, rock climbing in the White Mountains, and ultra-running. Meanwhile, his writing scope ventures far beyond travel, and he has enjoyed researching and writing articles about such varied topics as Alzheimer's disease, farmers markets at rock concerts, and asparagus. Versatile, flexible, and fun, Mark writes for local and national magazines and newspapers, he maintains two blogs, and he's at work on a novel. A ski instructor for 18 years, Mark supervises in the ski school at Stowe, Vermont -- and writes when he's not on the slopes.
