April Force Pardoe, Interior designer's Summary

I help busy homeowners create stylish and functional rooms that they are happy to come home to.

Are you a homeowner who is. . .
• Unsure about selecting furniture, accessories and paint colors for your home, so it’s still not "pulled together?"
• On the go all day with children and/or work and you don't have time to decorate?
• Exhausted at the end of the day and you need a comfortable room where you can relax and unwind?

Wouldn't you rather your home look and feel like this…
• You walk into any room and it looks complete and makes you smile
• Your family enjoys time together in your comfortable and inviting spaces
• You can’t wait to entertain friends in your stylish and functional rooms

Creating a stylish home shouldn’t be stressful. Please contact me to discuss your projects and how I can help you save time, reduce stress and create a home you love.
