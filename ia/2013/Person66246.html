  • CONTROLLER AND INTERFACE FOR HOME SECURITY, MONITORING AND AUTOMATION HAVING CUSTOMIZABLE AUDIO ALERTS FOR SMA EVENTS

    • United States Patent Application 12/771,471
    • Filed April 30, 2010
    Inventors: John Dial, Wade Cohn, Gary Faulkner, Jim Kitchen, David Proft, Corey Quain
