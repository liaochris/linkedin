George Laegeler's Summary

For over 30 years, Integrated Security Specialists' team of highly trained experts has been the premier provider of high performance security systems for homeowners, small businesses, and large enterprises. Integrated Security Specialists serves clients throughout Chicago and Southern Wisconsin, including Cook, Lake, McHenry and DuPage counties. We specialize in the installation, service, and monitoring of security and fire alarm systems, as well as access control and video surveillance.

Integrated Security Specialists' technical talents exceed normal residential-application requirements. As a company, Integrated Security Specialists thrives to the challenge of designing and installing larger, more sophisticated systems tailored to address any commercial or high-end residential security needs. We offer full system integration specifically designed to serve the unique needs of corporate, industrial and residential properties. Integrated Security Specialists guarantees a high level of customer service and an immediate response time.

For more information on Integrated Security Specialists' security services and products, contact us toll free at (888) 228-0456 or email us at sales@gotoissi.com.
