Jeremy Riel's Summary

I am a consultant and managing partner at Millennial Associates, a learning/educational design and educational technology consulting firm. I focus on building digital environments, software, and professional development curriculum to provide relevant learning opportunities to teachers and higher education faculty. It is through my work that I hope to address some of the challenges of teaching professionals and distance learning. In addition to my design work, I research how education professionals learn within digital environments and how we might better teach professionals technology skills.

Specialties: Technology education and digital literacy; educational technology and digital learning environments; teacher professional development (K-12 & higher ed); adult education; human-computer interaction; curriculum & program design and evaluation; learning environment, educational software, and CMS design
