Eewei Chen's Summary

I am a tech futurist, agilist and lean design philosopher. I have worked in the new media creative industry since 1993. Rumours are, I created the first animated gif, designed the first secure online musical distribution service, before itunes: Cerberus Sound and Vision, launched the first online music publication, The NME.com and helped set up the first integrated UK design agency, Conchango, now part of EMC Consulting.

Previously at Microsoft I launched MSN in Russia, Canada, China and Korea as well as maintaing existing EMEA sites; improving the online user experience to over 550 million users worldwide. As Creative Director at ThoughtWorks UK and then Solutions Director at British Sky Broadcasting (BSKYB) I led cross functional teams; building prototypes using Lean Startup, Lean UX and Agile methodologies to deliver B2C TV, mobile and web experiences.

I love speaking and running workshops in the design, lean start up, technology and agile communities including Future of Web Apps 2011 & 2012, Agile 2011 & 2012 co-producing the Agile 2012 UX stage and was a keynote speaker in India for UXUtsav 2012. I also mentor at start up events including Lean Startup Machine and StartUp Weekends around the world. My blog can be found here: www.eewei.com

I am currently Head of Customer Experience at News International and working on some personal projects; creating innovative digital prototypes based on emerging trends to define new market strategies before heavy development takes place. This has also given me time to finish and publish my book http://101DesignIngredients.com, organise the Agile 2013 UX track and give a few talks at Adaptive Marketing, Future of Web Apps & LeanUX NYC.

Specialties:
1. Defining future business strategy and execution
2. Speaking at conferences on Lean Startup and Agile User Experience
3. Workshop facilitation, business envisioning, execution and innovation
4. Continuous build, measure, learn
5. Knack for what is going to be the next big thing
