Bernd Eigner's Summary

I have worked in the last 17 years in many positions in different divisions. Starting as a structural designer for steel structures I moved on to design & engineering manager, estimation, assistant project manager, site supervisor, contracting manager, construction manager to Project Manager.

The last 5 years I am active working in all aspects of project management. Besides of other duties of a Project Manager I focus on planning (time schedule, manpower, equipment), procurement (subcontracting, vendor and supplier), construction (civil works, coordination with subcontractors and vendors/suppliers, erection of steel structure and installation of cladding), commercial/reporting (invoicing, cash flow, cost forecast/control, progress reporting, performance reporting) but also safety and QA/QC.

“I prefer the contact with my client as well as subcontractors, I have an "open door" philosophy, everyone can come in but I also go everywhere, always in contact with my colleagues and staff, listen and give advice and find solutions on the spot...”

Specialties

project management, construction management, site management, project scheduling, planning, commercial aspects, cash flow, project forecast, steel structures, steel bridges, design & engineering, operation management, system implementation, communication skills, ability to solve problems efficient and quick, good experience to work with people from different cultures
