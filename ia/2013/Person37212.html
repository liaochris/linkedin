DOUGLAS E. CASTLE's Summary

Douglas E. Castle works professionally as a confidential private consultant in matters of company restructurings, turnarounds, refinancing as well as creditor, vendor, employee and major stakeholder negotiations at the director level, where discretion is crucial. This work often involves structuring the terms of management buyouts, employee ownership plans,strategic business partnerships, mergers, acquisitions and international transactions. He conducts this special aspect of his work on a referral basis though The Castle Consultancy And Advisory International, an Unincorporated Division of Global Edge Technologies Group LLC.

He also works with companies in the SME category to grow aggressively through both domestic and international mergers and acquisitions, and also in proactively assisting in the development of international sourcing, markets, co-operative joint ventures and investments. These investments traditionally have involved the purchase of real estate, existing businesses, or controlling [strategic] interests in foreign companies, some privately closely held and some publicly-traded.

He recently became Executive Director of GLOBAL BUSINESS INTEL™ (GBI) - An Unincorporated Division of Global Edge Technologies Group LLC. GBI is a Private-Sector Intelligence Provider to enable companies to gain a rapid edge on their competitors in any industry through the use of focused research, private investigation and data mining technologies.

Mr. Castle received his B.S. in Economics from Stony Brook University (1976), with Omicron Delta Epsilon honors (as well as a New York State Regents Scholarship), and he received his MBA in Public Accounting from The Hofstra University Graduate School Of Business (with a graduate fellowship) in 1979.
