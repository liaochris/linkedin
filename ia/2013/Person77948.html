LaCharla Figgs' Experience

Principal

TL Figgs Nonprofit Consulting

January 2006 – Present (8 years) DC-Baltimore Region

T.L. Figgs Nonprofit Consulting is a Maryland-based organizational development consulting practice specializing in community-based nonprofit agencies. T.L. Figgs helps agencies reach their performance goals through in-person and virtual training and coaching, consultation and hands-on assistance.

Services include board development, organization assessment, grantwriting, policy review and development, management coaching, meeting facilitation, and program development. More information is available at http://www.NonprofitCoachOnCall.com.
