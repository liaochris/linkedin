Joshua Wethington's Experience

Owner

Broken Peanut Media

May 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months) Dallas, TX

Broken Peanut Media, LLC, a Dallas, TX based video production company specializes in creating: testimonial, product, internal training, and company profile videos for companies off all sizes. We strive to create the most engaging and informative video on the web.
Our approach has been well received from both clients and the industry having won three W3 silver awards, The Videography Award of Excellence, and two Lone Star Emmy Nominations in recent years.
The owner of Broken Peanut Media , Joshua Wethington, graduated from The Art Institute of Dallas where he earned a B.F.A in Digital Media Production and now has over seven years of experience in the video industry. His education and professional experience have trained him to be a "one man band" video company that is perfect for the video marketing field. Joshua divides his time between producing, shooting and editing videos on a daily basis.
Currently, he is developing two web series as well as a suite of video services geared towards video marketing.
