Mark Kerray's Summary

Since 2009 Mark has been working on a unique, patented energy efficiency idea called Chop-Cloc and in 2011 became the Chief Executive Officer. Chop-Cloc launched in March 2013.

Chop-Cloc Limited is an innovative Scottish energy efficiency company that takes a different approach to heating control. We believe that people need new, easier to use, easier to understand, easier to fit, more affordable and more effective ways of reducing their energy demand. Chop-Cloc, it's about time.

Specialties: Strategy innovation, product and service innovation, brand positioning, brand campaign development, brand identity development, corporate storytelling, and employee engagement campaigns.
