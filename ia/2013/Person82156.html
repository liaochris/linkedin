Locus Technologies' Summary

Locus provides the world's most comprehensive on-demand software to organize environmental data and information for private industry and government. Locus is the partner of choice for all organizations looking to be credible in all of their environmental reporting, compliance and sustainability needs.

Locus' suite of environmental data management on-demand software products was designed by domain experts to serve the needs of environmental professionals. Our products are offered online as a service, allowing customers to enjoy the benefits of state-of-the-art software solutions at a fraction of the cost of building and maintaining comparable systems. Locus' products provide a complete environmental information management solution to manage the range of complex environmental data and information generated during environmental activities.

Specialties: Web-based Environmental Information Management, Document Management, Sustainability Reporting, Environmental Health and Safety Solutions, Hazardous Waste Tracking, Management and Reporting, Task Tracking and Notifications
