Joshua Butler's Experience

Senior Visual Designer

Fjord

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Design industry

June 2010 – September 2013 (3 years 4 months) Greater New York City Area

Senior Visual Designer involved in the full lifecycle of digital products (initial design concepting through production). Notably, for two years I served as a lead visual designer for Fjord’s Citibank account, having developed the initial visual language for many of their mobile products, both internal and consumer-facing. Other clients where I lead visual design efforts are Audible, T-Mobile, Kingston, Brookfield Properties, Reuters, Whaleshark Media, Nokia, ESPN and NFL. I was the first fulltime visual designer at Fjord’s NYC office.

Joshua Butler's Projects

  • Citibank for iPad Application (Released July 2011)

    • July 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Joshua Butler, Will Barnwell, Andres Wolberg-Stok, Chris Brown, Christopher Hamilton, Natalie Armendariz, Prashant Agarwal

    Citibank for iPad is the first app from a major U.S. bank to depart from traditional ledger-style banking and offer graphs and visual representations of consumer accounts and transactions.
