Laura Thornton's Summary

Experienced life and health insurance agent in Canton, Ohio that can assist you by designing a policy that best fits your personal or business needs. I want to help you protect those who are most important to you with the right life insurance, health insurance and financial products. If you are looking for health insurance in Stark, Summit, Wayne, Portage or surrounding counties of Ohio, or in a surrounding community, I can help you identify and understand your unique needs and budget and then offer you a variety of options.

As an agent with Insphere Insurance Solutions, my main goal is to help individuals, families and businesses find affordable health care coverage that is designed around their needs. Therefore, I can recommend a plan fitting their unique requirements. Additionally, if a plan you choose ever becomes too expensive, I will shop around to find you a plan that will fit into your budget and needs. Don't limit yourself to an agent or agency that represents one or two companies and their plans. You, your family and business deserve to know all your options. If you want to understand more about your insurance options, please contact me today for a free consultation and no-obligation quote.

I also still do offer assistance to my network with social media strategy. If you need any help with promoting your business or growing your network, don't hesitate to ask for help. Although it is not my primary choice of career, I still enjoy helping others expand their presence.
