Jamie Miller's Summary

Jamie has over 30 years experience in manufacturing with the past 7 years spent in professional recruiting first with MRI in Cordova, TN and in 2002 with the founding of Greenhill Solutions in Jackson, TN.

Greenhill Solutions' mission is to work with hiring authorities to locate the best possible candidate for the organization's staffing needs. He focuses on managerial, technical and sales professionals and as a former hiring authority presents only the few candidates that are pre-qualified.

Specialties

Consumer good, durable goods, food manufacturing, wood products manufacturing, metal fabrication and wire and cable. Fields of expertise include engineering, environmental, Human Recources, Plant Management and Operations Management, purchasing, design and marketing.
