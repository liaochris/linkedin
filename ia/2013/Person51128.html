Gwenno Dafydd's Summary

Over thirty years of professional experience as a Broadcaster, Performer, Writer and Educator. Always 'Creates Excellence' in whatever work or project she embarks on.

Has a Stage 1&2 ILM Accredited Coaching Qualification and has worked with a huge vareity of businesses, companies and educational establishments over the years, including extensive coaching by phone, Skype and email with the President of a successful American fashion design company.

Worked as an Equality Expert on the WDA's SME Equality Project for 9 years. Has a Masters degree in Social Sciences from University of Wales, Cardiff and a Certificate in Education from Barry Teacher Training College (integrated into Polytechnic of Wales then University of Glamorgan)

Is totally bilingual (Welsh & English) and can also work through the medium of Flemish and French. Also speaks conversational Spanish.

Her goal: To develop anyone that she works with to their FULL potential in a fearless fashion!
