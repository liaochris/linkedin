Eva Sachs Founder, WomeninDivorce Financial's Summary

In divorce, it usually comes down to the kids and money. I deal with the money part. Statistics tell us that almost 1 in 2 marriages end in divorce. If that's the case, someone you know and care about may be contemplating or facing divorce.

Women in Divorce Financial is a trusting environment where women facing divorce obtain expert information and guidance enabling them to make the right choices resulting in a clear view of their life after divorce.

I work with women who are looking for help to map out the long term financial considerations of life after divorce. Using tools that demonstrate the short and long term impact of all the financial "what if's", I answer the question : Will I be OK after my divorce?

Eva Sachs Founder, WomeninDivorce Financial's Experience

Founder

Eva Sachs Divorce Financial Planning

2005 – Present (8 years) Toronto

I provide full service Divorce Financial Planning for individuals and couples in separation and divorce.

My divorce planning services are essential no matter what separation process you are in: mediation, collaborative or traditional litigation divorce.

My role as a divorce financial advisor determines how the combination of income, property division and spousal & child support can come together with your post divorce budget to help you reach your targeted lifestyle.

An “equitable split” could turn out to be less than equitable five years down the road. When you start delving into finances, the issues become complex. It only makes sense to have someone on your side who is trained to deal with such issues.

Co-Founder

Mutual Solutions Changing the way couples divorce

July 2008 – Present (5 years 6 months) Toronto

Mutual Solutions is the starting point for separating couples who want to move from where they are today to a plan for their futures.

Since divorce may be the largest emotional and financial transaction in your lives, we find that couples who work together on both the legal and financial aspects of separation with us have better outcomes. Having a shared process allows you to achieve your individual goals, develop and examine multiple options and create solutions that work well for all.
Mutual Solutions is here to help gain an understanding of all the decisions you'll need to make and how to move to a positive outcome.

At Mutual Solutions, we specialize with working with couples divorcing after a long marriage or late in life.. known as "Grey Divorce".

Watch for our book coming out December 2013: "When Harry Left Sally ...Finding your Way through Grey Divorce"
