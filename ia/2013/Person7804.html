Angel Garcia's Summary

Currently is Founding Partner at Lanta Digital Ventures, an early stage venture capital fund that invests in innovative start-ups. The capital of the fund is composed by successful founders, CEOs and senior executives of start-ups who know what it takes to build successful companies.

Angel is an experienced executive, investor and entrepreneur. He has broad experience over more than 15 years acting in an international environments both in Asia and US building up a startup project. Angel is shareholder at Fractus, a well-known growing European Technology start-up in the global telecom industry which is currently implementing a patent licensing program having collected more than $ 70 million in royalties to this day from top worldwide cellphone manufacturers.

Previously he was working as CFO and Management Consultant involved in M&A and Corporate Finance activities. Angel has attracted more than € 40 million from top venture capital firms in different companies.

He founded Elevation Ventures some years ago, a capital company to manage investments in small companies. He is acting as board member in some companies and keeping his entrepreneurship spirit investing in start-up projects.

In addition, he´s member of Barcelona Global a private, independent and not-for-profit civil society platform made up of business leaders, professionals and entrepreneurs committed to Barcelona and its future. Member of the Barcelona Mentoring Program lead by the city hall with the aim to support SME of Barcelona. Mentor in several startups initiatives such Wayra, SeedCamp or Startbootcamp.

Angel holds an Economics and Business Administration Degree together with and Executive Program at Stanford University, US.

My greatest travels were Africa, India or Nepal but more will come. I enjoy traveling, running and hiking whenever and wherever I can.
