Joyce Jagger's Summary

In 1978 I started my small embroidery business and quickly grew it into a medium sized multi-head embroidery shop. In 1998 I sold my embroidery shop and worked with the new owner as the manager and trainer until 2000.

In October of 2000 I went to work for Hirsch International as a sales rep. In 2002 I started the Embroidery Business training and consulting business, as The Embroidery Coach which has developed into a full training program for embroidery business owners through my website, TheEmbroideryTrainingResourceCenter.com. This is a video embroidery training site for new embroiderers and embroiderers that want to increase their profitability.

I have trained many embroiderers design creation and digitizing using the Tajima by Pulse embroidery software.

I I simplify the business of embroidery into easy to understand lessons. My passion is to help get the new embroiderer off to a fast start and help the existing embroiderer improve their skills so that they can provide higher quality embroidery and increase their profitability!

Specialties:Embroidery Business Marketing, Author, Business Coach, Mentoring Embroidery Business Owners, Wordpress Websites, Internet Marketing. Teaching new business owners how to run a profitable business. Training in all areas of embroidery including applications, digitizing, pricing and marketing both on line and off line. Setting up total Embroidery Business Marketing Systems.
