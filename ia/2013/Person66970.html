John R. LaPlante's Summary

John LaPlante translates the research in K-12 education, health care, taxation and budgets, and other policy areas into plain English that busy legislators and interested citizens can easily understand. He can also work behind the scenes to provide your organization with the research necessary to do its job. He combines the skills of an editor with the insight of an analyst.

"John is a rarity in the editing world. He not only brings clarity to my words, but he also sharpens my policy perspective. I'd highly recommend him." -- Christie Herrera, Director of the Health and Human Services Task Force at the American Legislative Exchange Council
