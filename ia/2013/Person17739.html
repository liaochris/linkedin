Brian S Hartley's Summary

Brian works as a Sr. Mortgage Loan Officer for the Private Mortgage Group of First Citizens Bank A privately held high wealth boutique bank, First Citizens specializes in offering high level customer service with the full spectrum of mortgage products. Niche products for high net worth individuals, physicians, and other professionals allows Brian to provide specialized solutions for clients needs. Offering traditional mortgage products, jumbo financing, conventional and jumbo construction, and conventional and jumbo renovation financing options. First Citizens offers 100% financing for Physicians.

Brian is recognized as the Atlanta Board of Realtor's 2012 Affiliate of the Year and was recognized by the Georgia Association of Mortgage Bankers as one of the top loan originators in Georgia and recipient of their Gold Award which recognizes mortgage loan originator excellence. Brian holds a BBA from the Terry College of Business at the University of Georgia. Prior to joining First Citizens, Brian worked with Wells Fargo Bank as a Sr. Home Mortgage Consultant and with Envoy Mortgage as a Sr Mortgage Consultant. Brian has an extensive history working with clients buying, building, renovating and refinancing homes, in addition to managing a team and working cohesively with a group of Personal Bankers at Atlanta Area Bank Branches.

As a Atlanta native Brian has a passion for giving back to his community, he is actively involved in the Buckhead Business Association, City Club of Buckhead, Young Executive Society of Buckhead, the Atlanta Jr Chamber, Jr Achievement, and the Fernbank Artemis Guild. In addition, Brian serves on the Board for the Atlanta Jr Chamber, he is actively involved with the Atlanta Board of Realtor's anad was awarded the honor Affiliate of the Year. He also served on the Board in 2012 and 2013 for the Cocktails for Camp Charity Event. He is a member of the High Musuem of Art, Atlanta Botanical Gardens and Georgia Aquarium.
