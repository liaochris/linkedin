Jeff Lobb's Summary

Real Estate and Technology- International Speaker, Trainer, Coach, Innovator and Ambassador.. Jeff Lobb has been a REALTOR® for over 23 years and has more than 10 years experience in the Internet and Technology fields both with Fortune 500® companies and startup ventures. Jeff's Data, Wireless and Internet experience has held Management and Executive level positions with Companies such as IDT, LEVEL 3 Communications, Wave2Wave Communications and Verizon Wireless.

He is a Technology Specialist coming from the perspective of of a REALTOR! Over 23 years of Real Estate, he has been a top producing agent, has run Real Estate brokerages and has developed training and coaching products to serve the Real Estate Industry. His knowledge base is very diverse, but Jeff specializes in Mobile, Social Media, Video and Digital Marketing for the Real Estate Profession.
Jeff has been a guest speaker at Internet/Telecom Conferences, a Trainer with EXIT's Live Interactive Training Program,& an EXIT International Breakout speaker in 2008 thru 2012. He is a Featured Speaker at XPLODE Real Estate Technology Conferences, and has been a Guest speaker of RIS Media, Inman News, Retso, Midwest tech fair, WCR and has most recently been named Inmans Top 100 Most Influential Leaders in Real Estate.

Jeff initially joined the EXIT Realty Corp. International team as the company's Technology Specialist and was Promoted to VP of Technology and Innovation.

He has a very accurate pulse on the industry and prides himself on delivering hands-on, usable solutions for today's real estate professional. His diverse business and technology background gives Jeff the vision, the passion and experience he refers to as "the perfect storm" - all experience and knowledge converging at precisely the right time for the REAL ESTATE INDUSTRY.

Jeff is Fluent in the three languages....English, Real Estate, & Technology
