Jewel Mlnarik's Summary

Having over a decade of experience in the full life-cycle of website and interactive systems strategy, design, architecture, build, launch, adoption, support, branding and sales, Jewel is a digital generalist with a specialty in growing teams and businesses- all while minding your P's and Q's: Purpose, People, Projects, Processes and Questions.

Over her career, Jewel has helped to launch and support hundreds of websites and interactive experience projects-- from the highly-trafficked and interactive Tarot.com, TravelOregon.com and Sony Pictures Television's international cable sites to artisan sites for local entrepreneurs and creatives.

At the start of the Oughts, she architected a new interactive experience for a startup that succeeded when most didn't-- and a business model which later came to be known as Software-as-a-Service. She went on to grow a virtual agency specializing in turn-key marketing and custom software solutions for small to mid-sized companies. After being invited to build a new digital product company as a branch of a well-established consulting firm in Silicon Valley, she sold her agency. A few years later, with the new product branch ramped up and PartnerPath already helping thousands, she embarked on a sabbatical where she focused on personal travel, photography and art projects. She now balances her photography and travels with projects of substance- a skill she picked up while working for an eponymous Portland company.
