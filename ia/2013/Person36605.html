Don Chinnici's Summary

As of January 2013, we branched into two full companies: Business Management Consulting and Funds Sourcing. We continue to have an equal (and growing) ability to fund your business needs; and now additionally are thrilled to exercise Business Management Consulting, enhancing your ability to increase market share and sales, and increasing your client, community, and employee relationships.

We help you to achieve your goals; working alongside you to analyze your market and competition, and positioning your organization or product to create greater sales and market share. In our experience, the implementation of only small, creative tweaks results in a big difference in effectiveness. And it's our passion in this additional viewpoint.

This can help any business or organization, from small start-up, to multinational, to multi-level. Please see our website for full detail on both Management Consulting as well as Commercial Finance needs -- and/or sign up for our newsletters: http://donchinnici.com
