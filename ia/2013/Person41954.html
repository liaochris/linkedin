Eric Benson's Summary

After more than twenty-five years of working with and studying people in action, I have seen the high performers and the low performers, the motivated and the unmotivated, as well as the happy and the stressed. Working with people across the spectrum has convinced me that people possess a vast reservoir of untapped potential. Bound by beliefs and behaviors that lock them into a perpetual cycle of “sameness”, I am now concentrating all of my efforts on providing people with the keys for unlocking their full potential and achieving as never before. I do this through speaking engagements at meetings, conferences, workshops, and retreats for professional associations, government agencies, and corporate clients.

Specialties:

Connecting with people using an engaging, high-energy style.

Simplifying the complex so people can absorb it and apply it easily.

Creating a comfortable environment to accelerate growth.

Finding relationships among seemingly disparate bits of info that lead to strong solutions.

Designing/developing/delivering programs on time and on target.
