Chris Attanasio, NV RLA 460's Summary

Attanasio Landscape Architecture, A Nevada Professional Corporation, (ALA) has a single goal on all projects: To collaborate with quality clients and other consultants contributing talent, skills and discipline directed into a cohesive project vision. ALA take’s great pride in providing our clients with projects which create a Sense of Place, engage and enrich the experiences of the people who use the, and are responsive to the environment.

As stewards of the land, ALA’s expertise in landscape architecture allows us not only to work with clients in the private sector but also in the public realm, which includes parks and streetscapes. ALA’s staff experience spans resorts/hospitality, high-rise, parks, streetscape, sports facilities, freeway enhancements and aesthetics, and landscape restoration.

Within the private sector the staff at ALA has collaborated with quality clients and architects to design significant projects which include: The Signature at MGM Grand, New York, New York Hotel and Casino, Caesars Palace, Turnberry Place, Turnberry Towers, Panorama Towers, Sky on the Strip, The Pinnacle and Planet Hollywood.

In the public arena the staff at ALA has collaborated with many local, regional and state agencies to generate projects which contribute to communities on over fifty different projects within the state of Nevada. Currently ALA is working on Hidden Falls Park, The Neon Boneyard Park and Boulder Plaza Scuplture Park.
