Steven G. Eisenberg's Summary

EnlightenMed and Lyrical Life were founded by Dr. Steven Eisenberg, a board certified doctor of Internal Medicine, Medical Oncology and Hematology. He is a founding member of California Cancer Associates for Research & Excellence (cCARE.com) in greater San Diego. Dr. Eisenberg uses music, humor and heart to transform his patients’ journeys through the minefield of cancer.

Healing through Connection. Lyrical Life was founded by Dr. Steven Eisenberg, who uses music and song writing to connect with his patients. It’s about creating and sharing the strength required to heal. It’s about listening, engaging, expression, supporting, loving, and getting through each day together.
