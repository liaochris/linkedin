Andy Vandervell's Summary

I'm a seasoned technology journalist with a particular focus and expertise in online publishing and writing. Beyond the basics of writing and editing, I'm a very experienced video presenter - I've presented hundreds of product demos from every major tech trade show going - and have even dabbled in live online video punditry. Naturally I'm dab hand at using analytics, SEO and social media to promote content, too. My professional curiosity is piqued by micropublishing and the numerous experiments in making money from online publishing.
