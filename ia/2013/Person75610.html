Kimberly Daye Hardy, M.Ed's Summary

Driven and passionate about her calling to serve youth, women and families- Kimberly is known for her strength to inspire and pioneering spirit. Kimberly's infectious positivity and keen ability to educate, uplift, and motivate have empowered audiences. As an "inspired lifestyle expert," speaker and mentor with 17+ years of strategic leadership expertise in public health, non-profit management, and youth development in educational and corporate settings, Kimberly Daye Hardy has trained and coached hundreds of professionals, teaching them how to increase productivity, create life balance, and achieve goals.

Kimberly is CEO of www.walkinginpurposeunlimited.com , a professional & personal development company dedicated to inspiring women (& youth) to realize their dreams, have true prosperity, and lead healthier lives, through embracing their unique purpose. Through her workshops, courses, and mentoring programs, Kimberly delivers "timely, practical, relevant, and insightful" leadership & lifestyle strategies and solutions that promote healthy living and teach clients how to move from potential to unlimited purpose.

Having received international recognition for co-authoring four empowerment books for women & youth, including a book dedication in Living Your Vision and Purpose, under Professional Woman Publications (KY), Kimberly is a sought after presenter, has appeared on radio shows & expert panels and serves as keynote speaker and consultant/ trainer to a broad range of public and private organizations, service providers, and educational conferences. Her most noted accomplishments include serving as project team member with Joseph Alliance, Inc. having established a village-based wellness clinic, women’s conference, and orphanage outreach program in Ghana, West Africa and now serving as board member; as well as volunteer work with AIDS Walk Philly, the SGK Race for the Cure Breast Cancer Walk, and a host of regional health initiatives.
