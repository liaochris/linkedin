Sloan D. Payne-Waters' Summary

In 2005, after devoting 100% of myself to my family and to being a stay-home mother, I launched A Gift From The Heart. The original gift concept was not a lucrative idea due to limited growth potential. Sadly, it was my father's untimely death just two months after the launch that opened my eyes to where my true talent lied.

Around my father's funeral, my mother received numerous sympathy gift baskets. Though thoughtful, they were nice at first glance, but, honestly, they were not that great. The more I studied them, the uglier they became. The cheap wicker, (over) stuffing, useless fillers and the generic content bothered me. I knew that I could create a product that was high-end, more unique and creative. I could create gifts with added personal touches that the recipient would treasure for years to come. And, I knew that I could create gifts that were sure to be more bang for my customer's buck.

Since 2005...bittersweet, yet thanks to my dad (who, I might add, was my biggest cheerleader in everything that I did) I am successfully building a gift basket business like no other. You name the theme and I can run with a unique gift basket vision..without the use of cheap wicker!. Through an interview process with my client, I get to know the recipient. If necessary, I research the theme to ensure I'm on point with my creations.

A Gift From The Heart is an experience. My artistic yet organic approach is like no other gift basket business. Why? Because A Gift From The Heart is "True Custom Gift Baskets...From Our Heart to Yours!"

Specialties
