Dean Thompson's Summary

I am a Principal Architect at LinkedIn, and a member of our Presentation Infrastructure team. We are responsible for the software frameworks and infrastructure for www.linkedin.com and other browser-based LinkedIn applications.

After a few years as research staff at Carnegie Mellon in the late 80s, I have spent my career founding and leading Pittsburgh-based startups: mSpoke (acquired by LinkedIn), Transarc (acquired by IBM); e-Transport (acquired by Descartes); Premier Health Exchange (acquired by Medibuy); and Medebiz (merged with Moai Technologies). I also spent five wonderful years at MAYA Design Group, a Pittsburgh-based interdisciplinary product design firm.

Specialties: founding and managing software startups, recommendation systems, architectural leadership, vision for adaptive computation and predictive modeling, deep expertise in transaction processing systems
