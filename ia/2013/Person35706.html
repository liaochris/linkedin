Richard Dinnick's Summary

Richard is a screenwriter, published author and journalist represented in the UK by Alison Finch at JFL Agency.

He won the BBC Writersroom search for new writers in 2012 and is currently writing for the BBC's BAFTA Award-winning Tree Fu Tom CGI animated children's TV show among others. He is also developing several ideas for TV including a fantasy drama for CBBC and an adult crime drama, which is the first of its kind.

Richard is a judge for the Children's BAFTA Awards.

He is currently in pre-production on his first web series – Light & Shadows – as writer/producer and recently served as associate producer on another independent project.

When Richard isn't writing, he speaks on the subject at Screenwriting and Media Festivals. He also teaches creative writing at a local school as an extra-curricular activity once a week.   

Richard has written prose, scripts and comics for many tie-in media stories for various franchises including Doctor Who, Stargate SG-1, Sherlock Holmes and Moshi Monsters.

Richard has a Joint Honours degree in English and Writing & Publishing and is a full member of the Writers' Guild of Great Britain.
