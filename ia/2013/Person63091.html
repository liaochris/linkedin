Jessica Lawlor's Summary

I'm currently the Marketing and Communications Coordinator at Visit Bucks County, the official tourism promotion agency for Bucks County, PA. In my role at the tourism office, I am responsible for content creation, e-communications, B2B public relations efforts, event planning and assisting with social media, advertising and marketing initiatives.

Prior to joining the team at Visit Bucks County, I worked on the communications team at Cancer Treatment Centers of America in Philadelphia where I managed hospital internal and employee communications and assisted with marketing, public relations and social media initiatives.

I'm extremely involved in the Philadelphia PR community and serve as the Vice President of Communications for the Philadelphia Public Relations Association (PPRA). Previously, I served as the Co-chair of the Young Professionals committee for the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA) and am still an active member of the organization.

In addition to my full-time job and volunteer positions, I am a freelance writer and blogger and have been published on various websites including Brazen Careerist, Ragan's PR Daily, Muck Rack, SpinSucks.com, Mediabistro, Business Insider and AOL Jobs. I also manage my own blog (http://jessicalawlor.com) and e-newsletter focusing on getting gutsy and leaving your comfort zone in order to reach your goals.

I graduated from Temple University in May 2010 with a degree in strategic and organizational communication with a focus on public relations. While at Temple, I served as president for Temple's PRSSA chapter and co-founded PRowl Public Relations, Temple University's first and only student-run PR firm.

Specialties: Public Relations, Writing, Internal Communications, Speechwriting, Blogging, Social Media (Facebook, Twitter, Foursquare, Pinterest, Foodspotting, Instagram), Editing, Branding, Marketing
