James Knight's Experience

Owner and Integrative Therapist

EnergyFlowing

January 1988 – Present (25 years 9 months) Greater San Diego Area

As an Integrative Therapist I am dedicated to the process of transformation. I enjoy embracing each moment as an opportunity to expand our awareness and tap into the infinite possibilities for self healing and empowerment.

When we are fully aligned in mind/body/spirit our energy is flowing and we express this through living our lives with courage, joy, and passion.

Over the past 20+ years I've founded several Wellness Centers and maintained a private practice for one-on-one sessions. I also teach yoga classes, workshops, host day retreats, facilitate corporate wellness programs, and lead yoga retreats around the world.

Principal Interior Designer, and Feng Shui Consultant

James Knight Interior Design

September 1998 – Present (15 years 1 month) San Diego, Nationally, and Internationally

I started my career in Hawaii in with an invitation to manage a project that included designing a spiritual retreat center and healing gardens. It was a natural fit because of my experience in the healing arts and understanding how our environment can effect us. I believe being surrounded by beauty can heal just as much as any therapy.

Since that time I have extensive experience running my own design businesses in conjunction working with other architects, building contractors, and interior and landscape designers.

In recent times I have the fortune of training with Feng Shui Master Amanda Collins. Through understanding and implementing this ancient science has inspired me to take designing sacred spaces to a whole new level.

Currently I enjoy giving Feng Shui consultations for home and business owners so they can manifest their goals and dreams.

As a specialty I work with Real Estate agents to stage home and business offices for quick sales.

Yoga Teacher

Gentle Somatic Yoga

January 2006 – Present (7 years 9 months) International

I developed a restorative yoga method from 20+ years of teaching. This style combines traditional Hatha yoga asana's (postures), exercises for re-patterning muscles from Hanna Somatic Education, pranayama (breathing techniques), meditation, and healing visualizations inspired through the study of quantum physics.

I am partners with Yoga Vista Academy and we hold advanced teacher training's for yoga teachers and Health and Wellness professionals. In 2013 we are developing a curriculum for teacher certification in Gentle Somatic Yoga.

DVD's and workbooks are available through my website and Amazon.com.

I facilitate workshops and yoga retreats nationally and internationally.

Nature Photographer

James Knight Photography

January 2007 – Present (6 years 9 months) Greater San Diego Area

I am a nature photographer, video producer, and artist specializing in Feng Shui.

My photographs are taken with a passion for beauty inspired by nature. I look for images with repeating patterns, textures and colors that mirror the interconnectedness of life.

My art has the intention to energize the interior spaces in homes, offices, boutique hotels, restaurants, resorts, spas, healthcare facilities, retail, and other public spaces.

I often collaborate with architects, other interior designers, and real estate agents to help stage homes for sale.
