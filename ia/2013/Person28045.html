Crystal Campbell's Summary

I am a business designer doing stuff. tech + people + good.
MACPfNE, Central Saint Martins + MBA, MIT Sloan and Catolica-Nova Business Schools.

We live in a media crammed world, at a time that presents the perfect opportunity for policy shapers to be centres of innovation that provoke thought without judgment, and entice action through intrapreneurship. Not by rejecting familiar tried and tested methods, but opening mindsets by exploring and offering engaged alternatives. Communicating our messages by pushing a process forward, out of the talking phase and into the doing phase. Our creative nature is innately action-oriented and can be effortlessly amplified by integrating future-shaping with tech, ecological and people based thinking and design. It’s in our DNA to DO stuff. Let's go!

Interests: digital communities, emerging markets, mobile, intrapreneurship, innovation, placemaking, ethnomining and Southern Africa.
