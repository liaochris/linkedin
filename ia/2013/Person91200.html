Missy Berggren's Summary

** We are growing the WCG office in Mpls and looking for innovative, creative folks to join our team. Now seeking a Senior Manager of (Social) Analytics. http://tinyurl.com/my5zdy8 **

Missy Berggren is a strategic marketing communications professional with 15+ years experience in integrated communications with extensive background in brand development, strategy, advertising, digital/social strategy and community engagement. A forward thinking, innovative and respected leader, she has the ability to mobilize people and resources to accomplish great things.

Missy recently joined WCG as a director for the new Midwest office based in Minneapolis where she's working with Medtronic and other clients. Previously, she led the efforts to rebrand Allina Health, a large healthcare organization comprised of 11 hospitals and more than 60 clinics, to its new name and identity. The project lasted more than two years, requiring significant vision, leadership, collaboration and most of all passion.

In addition to her work in health care, Missy is a leader in the social media community, serving as the director of strategic planning for the well-attended Social Media Breakfast Minneapolis/St. Paul and is the founder and director of the successful MN Blogger Conference.

Missy recently spoke at the Minnesota Health Strategy and Communications Network conference and moderated a panel at the Minnesota Interactive Marketing Association (MIMA). She's also a 2013 Ignite Minneapolis speaker and has more events booked throughout the year.

As a passionate advocate and with a heart for giving back to her community, Missy also serves on the Board of Directors for the Food Allergy Support Group of Minnesota. In this role she helps support families and raise awareness of life-threatening food allergies.

In the social space, Missy is known as @MarketingMama on twitter and her lifestyle blog (marketingmama.com). She also writes at Marketing Insights and Inspirations (melissaberggren.com).
