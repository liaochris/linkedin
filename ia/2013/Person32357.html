David O'Leary's Summary

Based in Brussels since 2002, I have a decade of experience in EU affairs, working in a European institution, a large trade association and a leading public affairs and communications agency.

My expertise lies in understanding the way in which the EU institutions function - both in theory and in practice - and the EU political environment.

This knowledge helps me to develop and lead effective public affairs and communications programmes aimed at political stakeholders and other audiences using a variety of tools, including social media.

I also have experience of providing training on a range of subjects, notably on the functioning of the EU and how to lobby the EU institutions.
