Jim Wills' Summary

Jim Wills is an award winning photographer, marketer, and journalist. He has worked for major clients such as The Walt Disney Company, Ultimate Electronics and Sierra Designs.

His Writings have been published in The Walt Disney World Eyes and Ears Magazine, The Boulder Weekly, The Colorado Daily and Lodo Magazine.

He has successfully operated a 5000 sq ft. professional photography studio as well as an international stock photography agency.

His work has been seen all across the country from Florida to Texas to Colorado and California.

Specialties

Jim specializes in taking great photography, marketing, advertising, writing, editing, coordinating.
His computer skills include Photoshop, Lightroom, Aperture, and most MacOS based products.
