Stephen C. Carey, Ph.D., CAE, FASAE's Summary

STEPHEN C. CAREY, Ph.D., CAE, has been President of AMMR--Association Management Consultants since July 1995. As the lead strategist and facilitator, Dr. Carey has led AMMR’s consultant team on over 100 major association management consulting, strategic planning and market research assignments with trade associations, professional societies, and other C-3 organizations in the key areas of governance, communications and marketing.

Dr. Carey is one of two trade or professional association executives to be appointed to the Board of Examiners for the Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Award for Performance Excellence and Innovation, on which he served for three years. The award is the highest level of national recognition for organizational performance excellence that a U.S. organization can receive. Dr. Carey is also a Charter member of the American League of Lobbyists and a Charter class Fellow of ASAE and the Center.

Prior to his AMMR appointment, Dr. Carey, was the President and CEO of a trade and a professional association for 14 years. He has written, published, or edited 100+ articles and several text books on association strategic planning, governance, marketing, communication and management topics. He conceived of, published and edited the first association marketing textbook entitled, Marketing the Non-profit Association and wrote the first association marketing and communications planning guide entitled, The Association Marketing and Communication Planning Guide with Best Practices and Templates. Most recently, he published the 2013 Strategic Planning and Research Guide, selected by ASAE as one of two such publications in their strategic planning inventory. Dr. Carey was also selected as one of the 12 most influential association executives by the Washington Business Journal, and is considered to be one of the industry’s leading experts on strategic, communications and marketing planning.
