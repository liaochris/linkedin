Andrew Wahlquist's Summary

I have a broad range of experience with video and multimedia, both creative and technical. I'm currently the head of technology at Local Hero Post shepherding feature films through the digital post production process. Previously as a multimedia producer and motion graphics designer for the Los Angeles Times, I created commercials and event video. As an independent filmmaker I work my own projects as a director & writer.

Creative endeavors include an audio play adaptation of GK Chesterton's "The Man Who Was Thursday" (http://www.manwhowasthursday.com) and an independent feature film "The Association" (http://www.associationmovie.com)

I also advocate for local non-profit arts concerns in my hometown as founder of the Downey Arts Coalition.

Past experience in the industry working at Phoenix Pictures (All the Kings Men, Zodiac, Holes), Mandate Pictures (The Grudge, Stranger than Fiction), Paramount, Artists Management Group, Ocean Pictures, The Canton Company.

Specialties

Independent film production, High Definition digital acquisition, Digital post production workflows, screenplay development
