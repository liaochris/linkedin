  • Linkdrop

    • August 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Jim Spoonmore

    Linkdrop is a cloud-based bookmarking application that provides a hybrid of secure, private bookmarking and social sharing.

    Linkdrop helps you save bookmarks, but it also makes it easy to share them with others, either privately or publicly through other social networks.

    Linkdrop is your private online library that can be accessed from anywhere using a computer, tablet, or smart phone. Unlike traditional bookmarks, which are typically stored only in the browser on a specific computer or device, Linkdrop can be used from anywhere through any browser, making your bookmarks completely available to you at all times. You can import your existing bookmarks from all major browsers to start your library or you can start from scratch and begin curating a new collection.

    You can easily organize, search, backup, and share links that you discover on the internet using the tools and utilities Linkdrop provides.
