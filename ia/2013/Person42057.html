Eric Facas' Summary

Eric Facas is a social entrepreneur, start-up advisor, and digital marketing strategist with over ten years of experience at leading Internet companies and start-ups.

Eric is the Founder & CEO of Media Cause, a non-profit marketing company that offers full service agency services to larger non-profits while operating a global volunteer network to provide free services to non-profits without budget.

In 2009, Eric was a founding partner of the digital business accelerator, Tallwave, and responsible for creating the companies second portfolio company, a digital advertising agency ethology, which launched publicly in late 2010 with over 25 clients.

Prior to Tallwave, Eric spent five years at Google where he played a significant role in important program launches, built new teams from the ground up, and developed sales strategies across platforms ranging from Search Engine Advertising to Social Media. His accomplishments included successfully running Google’s SEM and Agency Councils, growing strategic partnerships, and participating in the creation of the multi-billion dollar AdWords API program. Eric received an exclusive EMG/Founder’s award from Google’s Executive Management Group for his work on this particular product in September of 2008.

Before joining Google in 2004, he spent several years with America Online. While with the AOL division of Time Warner, Eric was responsible for managing the sales programs for their largest content partners such as eBay, CBS, and Monster.com. Eric also successfully integrated Time Warner's online properties including People.com, CNN.com, Money.com and Fortune.com into AOL's interactive media offerings.

Eric holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from the University of California at Santa Barbara.
