Joerie Nijhuis' Summary

During the Master's program International Business (MSc) at Maastricht University I developed key competences in areas such as entrepreneurship, strategy, innovation, marketing and finance. Also, during the track I co-founded a for-profit company called 2Dine, an organisation that stimulates the local restaurant economy by giving customers a financial incentive to discover new culinary places in their town.

During my education at Zuyd University - Communication and Multimedia Design (Bachelor of ICT) - I broadened my specialistic knowledge in areas such as new media, ICT, audiovisual design and communications. My graduation project focussed on the development of a short interactive film about cyberbullying via social media. The film informs youngsters between 12-16 years about the consequences of their actions on social media. The film has been successfully developed by Reactif, a non-profit foundation I co-founded as chairman.

I want to combine my passion for technology, ICT & media with business in order to become a multi-disciplined and creative business professional with a broad knowledge base. I've gained start-up and management experience from both the Reactif foundation and 2Dine.
