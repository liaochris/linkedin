Caio Tozzini's Summary

Graduated in Business Administration and working in the Internet industry since 2003, Caio has started his career in web development and in the last 5 years have became specialized in Performance Marketing managing PPC, SEO, Social, Email Marketing and Display Advertising campaigns for some of the largest companies in Brazil.

Technology and entrepreneurship lover, he has moved from Sao Paulo to San Francisco in 2012 to experience the startup environment at MyTime.com, a revolutionary market place website where he's currently working along with Marketing and Engineering Teams on Web Analytics, SEM, CRO and UX.

Caio is Google Adwords and Google Analytics certified since 2008, Web Analytics Teacher at E-commerce School and member of the Web Analytics Committee at IAB Brasil.

Co-author of the book "Gerente de E-commerce" (E-commerce Manager), available in Portuguese only.
