Melanie Kellogg's Summary

Media Kellogg is a Freelance TV Producer, Casting & Crewing Professional, and a Marketing Consultant with a specialty in social media. Proud to be part of an Effie Award Winning campaign!

Since 2008, Kellogg has been going by the name “Media Melanie,” bringing her advertising and marketing background to the new media space. With nearly a decade of experience in TV & Video production, along with expertise in social media strategy and implementation, Media Melanie delivers measurable results to clients and exceeds expectations with her creative drive and vision.

Specialties: TV & Video Production, Casting & Crewing, Social media implementation and management, online branding, web marketing, promotions, publicity, and public relations. www.mediamelanie.com
