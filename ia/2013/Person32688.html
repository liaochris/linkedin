David Tyler's Summary

I've been working in broadcast radio and TV for over 2 decades now. I've developed a pretty powerful signature voice for radio and TV imaging. While at the same time I've had the chance to work as a voice actor on cartoons like Arthur, Caillou, Mona the Vampire and others after being trained by Debra Toffin. Hear samples of my work here: http://www.davidtyler.com/samples

I've also started working with medium and small businesses in the Montréal area as a Media consultant. I've begun lecturing at colleges and business associations on the "Art of Communicating Ideas". Contact me to discuss how I can help you connect with the people you want to connect with.

Specialties

Voice overs for Radio Imaging, TV Promos, Commercials, Documentaries, eLearning, cartoons and video games. And marketing consultation for small business.
