Melanie Votaw's Summary

I am a professional freelance writer who has published 14 non-fiction books on a variety of subjects. I'm also a ghostwriter of books and articles, I serve as a publishing industry consultant, and I edit and/or book doctor books and articles for individuals and publishers. Additionally, I'm a professional photographer and copywriter.

Books that I have edited have won a total of 21 awards, including the 2009 Best Beach Books Festival Grand Prize, and one of my edited books has been on the New York Times Bestseller List. I welcome inquiries from literary agents or individuals for a ghostwriter, collaborator, or editor. I often work with doctors, speakers, and business people to help them create a book or book proposal to build their platforms. My main specializations are popular business, health/medicine, cosmetic surgery, sexuality, psychology, film/theater, dance/fitness, spirituality, nature/wildlife, green subjects, food/wine, and travel.

I have also written articles for such publications as Woman's Day, Executive Travel, Business Insider, Just Luxe, Your Tango, South China Morning Post, and more.

I perform copywriting services as well for businesses and individuals (when it isn't a conflict of interest with my journalism work). I have written and edited website copy, brochures, speeches, press releases, sales letters, white papers, case studies, newsletters, email campaigns, and more. The industries I have worked with include medicine, alternative health, cosmetic surgery, dentistry, law, accounting, food, fashion, retail, luxury lifestyle including yachting, and more.

I'm also a speaker available for meetings and conferences. My favorite topic is how to access your innate creativity in your career, and I also provide writing training/coaching.

I'm a member of the Authors Guild, International Food, Wine & Travel Writers Association, New York Travel Writers Association, and the Association of Ghostwriters.
