Joy Pecchia's Summary

In 2002, I exited the corporate world to launch my coaching and consulting business. Although I was making a positive impact in business, I was limited to those who worked with and for me.

Since I had been an executive for 20-plus years, my early clients were corporate leaders who wanted support on initiating the vision, mission and strategy of their businesses. I also worked with their teams on communication skills and implementation of the company’s goals.

As word of my success with corporate leaders spread, my client base increased and the dynamics of my work began to change. I'm currently working with business leaders interested in business acceleration and professional success; with HR Partners who strive to be Strategic Business Partners and Resident Certified Coaches within their organization, and with groups, providing presentations and trainings with my proven 'Speed-to-Advancement' programs.

You probably know that one of the most prominent characteristics of successful people is that they take action! They take educated risks, are decisive, and they move forward towards success faster as a direct result of this. It’s a well-known fact these people make decisions based on their gut instinct.

Is this person you? Are you ready to take action?
