Michael McKay's Summary

I am a recent Business and Management graduate from Aston Business School with professional experience of the management consulting industry from an integrated placement year at PA Consulting Group in London. I'm a pro-active and professional individual, passionate about business with an extremely ambitious and entrepreneurial mindset.

From this, I have been fortunate to:

* Present to and work alongside stakeholders including Members of the European Parliament
* Help Aston Business School secure the world's most prestigious business school accreditation
* Contribute to a consulting assignment worth in excess of £1.5 billion
* Launch and run a pioneering business society
* Craft successful roles undertaking a range of freelance and consultancy work with clients worldwide including disruptive start-up businesses to FTSE 20 companies GlaxoSmithKline and Reckitt Benckiser
* Develop a first of its kind in the world membership programme with the worlds largest organisation of marketing professionals

Consultant

Enactus Aston

October 2012 – May 2013 (8 months) Birmingham, United Kingdom

I was pleased to be the only undergraduate accepted to a team of Aston Business School postgraduate and MBA students who advise enterprising business leaders. The role provided exposure to the challenging premise of running a successful consulting practice and working with senior stakeholders to make a difference on projects including:

The organisation had poor control over its financial management and lacked suitable business planning to accomodate its future growth plans. Our insights argued the group needed better structure in place and clear planning to ensure it could attain its stated goals.

A world leading business with over 10 million customers - Positioned at the top of a highly competitive and fast moving industry, the business wanted to continue its rapid growth since its foundation in 2007. We were asked to provide insight on the future strategic direction and business development activity as diverse as entering countries such as Japan and the release of new products to increase conversion.

Enhancing student understanding of consulting - Used my placement experience to provide advice and insights to the team on future career prospects and the real world practice operations of a successful consulting firm. Members of the team went on to interview with McKinsey and other firms.

This was a PMO role working alongside the client, contractors, consultants and a leading international law firm with board level interest in the assignment.

The placement provided significant exposure to business and was the feature topic of my dissertation, awarded one of the highest marks of over 600 students.
