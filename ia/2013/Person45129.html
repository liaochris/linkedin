Edward Leonard's Summary

Dr. Leonard is a board certified Chiropractic Physician serving the Tampa community. He graduated with honors from Palmer College of Chiropractic Florida, and has an undergraduate degree from the University of South Florida (GO BULLS!).

Dr. Leonard has been freeing people from pain in his clinic in Tampa, FL. As a Chiropractor with experience, Dr. Leonard is committed to promoting the health and well being of his patients.

Additional Activities
Being privileged by the opportunity to serve his profession at the World Health Organization in Geneva, Switzerland, Dr. Leonard utilized this opportunity to discuss healthcare initiatives such as global anti-smoking a campaign with several world leaders as well as creating internship opportunities for chiropractic students at the WHO headquarters in Geneva.

Dr. Leonard regards community service in the highest esteem, serving at both the local and international levels, his contributions range from roadside cleanup efforts in Tampa to helping establish first-class educational institutions and medical clinics, and lobbying the Parliament of Uganda to provide electricity and running water to rural, underserved areas in Africa.

Specialties

Dr. Leonard uses a "whole person approach". This approach to wellness means looking for underlying causes of any disturbance or disruption (which may or may not be causing symptoms at the time) and make whatever interventions and lifestyle adjustments that would optimize the conditions for normal function. Using this unique approach, Dr. Leonard is able to help you to accelerate and/or maintain your journey to good health.
