Joshua Narofsky's Summary

Joshua came into this world knowing exactly what he wanted to be in life – a rock star. So naturally, he went into advertising. Tucked under his metal-studded belt, Joshua’s real-world advertising experience includes gnarly copy he’s written for McDonald’s, Zales, Frito-Lay, DFW International Airport, HCA North Texas and the Armed Forces Bowl, as well as a handful of casinos, DFW hospitals and transportation clients. When the advertising doesn’t follow him home, this copywriter spends time thinking up killer band names as a self-proclaimed garage band enthusiast, making sweet harmonious love to the four strings, rockin’ the joysticks in the gamer world and annoying the heck out his awesome wife of four years.

Specialties: Copywriting, creating awesome stuff and enjoying local craft beer
