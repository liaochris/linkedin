Daymion Rogers Phelps' Summary

Retail automation solution Sales Manager with over fifteen years of experience in retail store level operations, channel management, and POS system sales. By defining requirements, objectives, and business goals, and with careful consideration of the 'wish list', I match retailers to solutions that will help grow their business, keep their customers coming back, and make their jobs easier.

Specialties include: POS and Store Automation, channel and direct sales management, integration and streamlining of multiple products and varied multi-channel solutions. Retail solution product knowledge includes: Microsoft Dynamics Retail Management System (RMS), NCR CounterPoint, and LOC Store Management Suite (SMS).
