Marc Horne's Summary

Marc Horne has designed and built multiple SaaS and faux-SaaS platforms, and managed the development of numerous other software solutions for clients since co-founding HC Consulting Group in 2008.

Based in Atlanta, GA, HC focuses on software development, consulting, and marketing for the online deployment of business solutions. It is our mission to provide industry standard and scalable software platforms and solutions to small - mid sized businesses and entrepreneurs at affordable prices.

Some of HC's products and services include;

- DailyDealBuilder.com: Daily deal software platform and training solution.
- SecurePayPro.com: Online shopping cart
- DODAggregator.com: Daily deal aggregator software
- VideoSharingEmpire.com: Video sharing platform
- SNEmpire.com: Social networking platform.

Most notably, Daily Deal Builder is an industry standard deal platform used in over 30 countries. The brand is highly recognizable in the deal space, with articles and press releases all over the internet, including on DailyDealMedia.com and Forbes.

Our specialties include: internet marketing, outsourcing, business planning & consulting, research & development, innovation, business management, software development, email marketing, search engine optimization, social media, sales funnels, and SaaS platforms and technology.
