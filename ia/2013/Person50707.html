G. Simone Clay's Summary

I started drawing on the back of bulletins during church services as a kid and found my calling! At the age of seven I sold my drawings to the other school children for a dime and received a few ribbons for my art pieces. I learned the fundamentals of design at Parsons Art School in Los Angeles CA. and further my education at California State University Northridge, where I received my Bachelor of Arts degree in art.

I am now a graphic design artist with an extensive portfolio in product developing, food packaging, illustration, and design. I have a online start up business (ArtSafari101.com) and freelance in graphic design.
