Amy Heflinger's Summary

As a designer and creative professional, it is my job to make you look good. End of story.

I will be content with a design or written piece only when it is exactly how you envisioned it or how you hoped it would appear.

Revisions and edits are NEVER a problem; I want every client to be satisfied with the end result and receive a high level of personalized customer service, every time.

Dealing with a designer should be a pleasant experience with a beautiful finished product on your time schedule and budget, and I will always do anything I can to meet, and hopefully exceed, your expectations.

Specialties

Logo and identity design, nonprofit communications, and much, much more! Please check my website www.aha4creative.com to see the full suite of services I offer.
