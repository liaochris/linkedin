Emily Timm's Summary

Emily Timm is a founding partner of Levers. Before starting as a copywriter and then a project manager at Vuurr in 2012, Timm was a contract reporter, producer and copywriter for various publications and blogs, most notably MSNBC, USAToday.com and The Arizona Republic. She graduated summa cum laude in 2012 with both her Master’s and Bachelor’s degrees in Journalism and Mass Communication from the Walter Cronkite School at Arizona State University.

Timm is Google Adwords Certified and skilled with Google Analytics, Facebook Advertising, Microsoft Bing Ads, Adobe Creative Suite and AP Style. Born and raised in Scottsdale, AZ, Timm frequently spends her free time outside hiking, biking or practicing her photography skills.

Emily Timm's Projects

  • Connectalytics

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Emily Timm, Chris Conrey, Chuck Reynolds, Andrew Ryno, Jonathan Kressaty, Scott Yacko

    Connectalytics is built for online advertisers and agencies who are tired of manually merging, calculating and analyzing advertising data from Google Adwords, Bing Ads or other monetized sources every day.
    Merging cross-campaign, pay-per-click advertising data from various sources, Connectalytics allows users to monitor their online advertising performance on one platform. It combines keywords, bids, goals, etc. from designated PPC accounts in Bing Ads with click data in Google Analytics in order to provide a detailed, comprehensive and reliable report of an online advertising strategy from one sign in.
