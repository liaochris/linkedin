For three years, I had the awesome opportunity to explore what humans are about.

Neurophysiology aims to understand the electrical wiring behind what makes us think, feel & react. The field is a fun somewhat torturous mix of physics, computer science, electronics, physiology & molecular biology.

My graduate fellowship centered around a research project that addressed novel pharmacological methods to treat Epilepsy and cell degeneration in Alzheimer’s disease.
