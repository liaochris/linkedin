Product Design Manager

As a Product Design Manager at Facebook I closely mentor and develop a team of designers. This means that I set clear goals, manage project loads and allocate designers accordingly across a multitude of projects, ensuring deadlines are met and ensuring my team is set up for overall success. I further help develop my own and the entire product design team by being an active member of the recruiting team (with a focus on international hiring). I help guide the team to develop best practices for a high bar of quality, which also involves me rolling up my own sleeves to contribute as a product designer.
