Jaime Saginor's Summary

As a Board Certified Holistic Health Coach, I work with Creative Professionals who put all of themselves into their work and can end up exhausted, unhealthy, often over-weight, and out of balance. I teach my clients how to make better food and lifestyle choices so they can look and feel their best and be at the top of their game creatively. Being an ex-sugar addict, I also help other sugar-addicts reduce the amount of sugar in their diets without deprivation!

I'm also an actress, a voice over artist, and a screenwriter.

Specialties: Creative Professionals, sugar addiction, reduce stress, ideal weight, increase energy, find balance.
