Jason Buratti's Summary

Driven by passion, creativity, diligence and integrity, I provide legal services to create and protect jobs, revenues and intellectual property. Today, I work with a small number of clients to provide practical in-house counsel type assistance with domestic and international business and legal affairs. These affairs generally involve strategic planning, negotiation and deal-making, and corporate and intellectual property matters. My clients usually do not have in-house counsel and want to have the personal attention and passion that comes with having an attorney on the team. As such, we are usually looking for each other - me for the right team and them for the right team member.

Extensive legal, classical, and scientific education and experience in large and small private law firms and corporate environments gave me skills and experience I apply to merge the roles of consultant, counselor, executive and friend and deliver personal and passionate attention to creation and trouble alike. Over the years, I have counseled large and small businesses and managed and led legal teams to address U.S. and international legal matters, including, for example: complex business, patent, trademark, trade dress, copyright, trade secret, unfair competition, trade dispute and antitrust matters; fitness, wellness and health matters; music and entertainment matters; licensing matters; corporate and intellectual property strategies; business deals; and domestic and international litigaiton and alternative dispute resolution proceedings.

Jason Buratti's Experience

Vice President, Global Intellectual Property Counsel

Zumba Fitness LLC

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

January 2012 – May 2012 (5 months) Hallandale Beach, Florida

Working with amazing people around the world as General Counsel (below) and then Global Intellectual Property Counsel at Zumba Fitness was perhaps the most spectacular professional experience anyone with a business and intellectual property background could have. During my entire tenure as a Vice President with Zumba, the company grew from about 60 to over 125 countries and experienced tremendous success. In addition to building a substantive and effective anti-piracy program, my work allowed me to engage with legislative and law enforcement bodies, outside counsel, licensees, partners and colleagues to protect and enforce Zumba 's intellectual property rights. My responsibilities included managing a team that monitored, identified and resolved intellectual property threats and risks in as many as 140 markets, more than half of which were new markets first encountered during my employment. I also negotiated licensing and other deals, worked on diligence projects, planned and executed global intellectual property strategies and enforcement campaigns, and supported global brand consistency efforts across business sectors within the company and for partners, licensees and press around the world. Perhaps more significantly, I made friends in an exciting, global community that I will value for a lifetime.
