Gael Paul's Patents

  • Automated bottom-up and top-down partitioned design synthesis

    • United States Patent 8,082,138
    • Issued December 20, 2011
    Inventors: Gael Paul, Kenneth McElvain, Smita Bakshi

    An automated logic synthesis method that combines bottom-up and top-down, providing the best of the two worlds. This invention is commercialized by Synopsys (formerly Synplicity) under the MultiPoint trademark.

  • Skew reduction for generated clocks

    • United States Patent 7,500,205
    • Issued March 3, 2009
    Inventors: Gael Paul, Kenneth McElvain

    A method to convert generated clocks (e.g. clock dividers) into clock-enable structures, thus enabling FPGA prototyping of ASIC & SoC designs featuring advanced power management such as dynamic frequency scaling.

  • Methods and apparatuses for circuit design and optimization

    • United States Patent 8,307,315
    • Issued November 6, 2012
    Inventors: Gael Paul, Kenneth McElvain, Saurabh Adya

    A method to analyze and optimize designs where critical paths are grouped together by logical & physical connectivity, thus forming timing "islands" that are optimized as a whole.

  • Logic performance in cyclic structures

    • United States Patent 8,104,004
    • Issued January 24, 2012
    Inventors: Gael Paul, Denny Scharf, Rajit Manohar

    A method to pipeline cyclic structures that exhibit associativity and commutativity. In the context of asynchronous design, this method speeds-up structures that otherwise cannot be pipelined and often limit system performance.

  • Asynchronous circuit representation of synchronous circuit with asynchronous inputs

    • United States Patent 7,982,502
    • Issued July 19, 2011
    Inventors: Gael Paul, Clint Kelly, Virantha Ekanayake, Rajit Manohar, Raymond Nijssen

  • Token enhanced asynchronous conversion of synchronous circuits

    • United States Patent 8,234,607
    • Issued July 31, 2012
    Inventors: Gael Paul, Clint Kelly, Rajit Manohar, Virantha Ekanayake, Raymond Nijssen

  • Reset mechanism conversion

    • United States Patent 8,161,435
    • Issued July 20, 2009
    Inventors: Gael Paul, Clint Kelly, Rajit Manohar, Virantha Ekanayake

    Methods, circuits, and systems for converting reset mechanisms in a synchronous circuit design into a corresponding asynchronous representation are described. These may operate to convert synchronous state holding blocks that include reset signals to corresponding asynchronous dataflow logic blocks. A replicated reset token at a fraction of the operational frequency of the reset signal may be distributed to the locations of the asynchronous dataflow logic blocks. Additional methods, circuits, and systems are disclosed.
