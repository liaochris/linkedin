Marc Liechtung D.M.D's Summary

Marc Liechtung, D.M.D., graduated from The University of Pennsylvania, one of the most world-renowned dental schools, as well as having the best fellowship programs in the country.

After graduation, he attended an Advanced Restorative and Implant fellowship at The University of Pennsylvania, and graduated from that fellowship in 1987.

Dr. Marc Liechtung has been a dentist in practice for over 18 years, and is dedicated to each of his patients’ dental needs.

Dr. Marc Liechtung, chairman and inventor of Snap-On Smile, is a restorative dentist and the owner of the New York City dental practice, Manhattan Dental Arts. There he provides the latest dental techniques and services including Snap-On Smile, implants, porcelain laminates, tooth whitening, periodontal treatments, preventive care, crown and reconstructive bridge work.
