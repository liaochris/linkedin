Brandon A. Odoi's Summary

Brandon Odoi's professional resume is as diverse as his background, born to a West African father and South Florida mother. Brandon is a serial networker and brand builder with invaluable experience in three main industries: corporate media, business development and church development.

Recently, Brandon has become an established football mind in the country for scouting and reporting at the youth, high school and college levels. He's a reporter and co-founder of America's premier youth football news information platform, Football Hotbed.

Brandon has ten years of professional experience that includes reporting, producing, public relations, public speaking, marketing and writing. In 2013, Brandon entered the field of real estate as well as taking a role as an assistant minister in the churches of Christ.

Over the span of the first five years of his professional career, Brandon gained extensive reporting and producing experience from the American Broadcasting Company Television Network in national and international news coverage. He earned distinctive experience with his work producing and reporting during the historic 2008 United States Presidential election and covering other breaking news stories.

Brandon has demonstrated his abilities as an innovative and skilled collaborator having worked in diverse environments throughout his professional career.

Through his experience founding and developing a media and marketing firm (B. Green), he acquires clients because of expert knowledge in marketing e-strategy, strategic communications, website design (user experience) and video production. He has done over 250K of business working only part-time in the past four years.

In the non-profit world, as an executive director, Brandon created the framework of services for a developing charity that is now poised for its third consecutive year of grant award increases.
