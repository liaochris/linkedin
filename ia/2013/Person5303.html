Alan MacKelworth's Experience

Chairman - High Value Networking (HVN)

AMaCLimited

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Executive Office industry

March 1989 – Present (24 years 3 months)

High Value Networking (HVN) - Believing that networking at C-Level is valuable is one thing; however, conscientiously implementing processes to leverage personal and collective company networks is another. AMaCLimited work closely with our Client’s executive’s teams to deliver B2B Executive-Executive introductions in the UK & beyond. Help build robust relationships with their peers in key-customers, alliance partners, thought leaders, emerging markets, new geography, and innovative suppliers.

The effective identification and management of risks and opportunities by the executive team is necessary to ensure the delivery of the organisation's strategic objectives. The executive teams approach to executive relationships allows early identification of key risks/opportunities and the taking of action.

Client projects include:
• HVN for Business Development Unit for one of Japans “Big Four”. A new portfolio in Europe would use the unit, invest £30m in smaller software companies and in 3 years grow to £100m. Pilot was proven.
• HVN for CEO of ON-Brand Partners, a company that specialises in assisting organisations in aligning and engaging their people with the business strategy. HQ’d in Auckland NZ,
• HVN for CEO EMEA of Telstra Global based in London. Telstra Global is a leading global provider of managed networked solutions in Asia, EMEA, Australia, and the Americas.
• HVN for Global Head of Business Development at Swiss Post Solutions focused on Enterprise Digital Transformation. HQ’d in London
• HVN for CEO Rider Levett Bucknall over 2,500 staff, 100 global offices - core services - Quantity Surveying, Building Surveying and Project Management. HQ’s in Birmingham UK.
• HVN for EVP Global Marketing and Strategy at Oracle Alliance and Channels, Embedded Global Business Unit. Based West Coast USA & London UK.
