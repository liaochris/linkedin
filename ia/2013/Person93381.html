Michelle A Rahn's Summary

I am a wife, mother, grandmother, teacher, entertainer, speaker AND the woman in the 'walk-in' tub commercial. I am also Ms. Senior America 2004 and, YES, there really IS such a thing and NO, there is not a swimsuit competition!

Summing up the more serious side of my journey for the past 13 years, however, begins with a second chance at life which was the beginning of a new commitment to helping others. I suffered a heart attack following the 1999 shootings at Columbine High School in Littleton, CO. The tragedy hit too close to home. I taught one of the killers and my husband and daughter coached with the teacher who lost his life that day. Columbine changed my life and I came out of my recovery with a new dedication to life and the choices that we make.

My choice is to "shout it from the mountain tops" that we DO have the potential to be 'the best we can be' with the gifts in which we've been entrusted. It IS our
responsibility.....hmmm...what a concept......to be accountable for our actions.......these choices are decisions which promote life's path to the ultimate.....it is OUR INDIVIDUAL choice!

You may also view my all of my information, including my one sheet and my videos, on my website: www.michellearahn.com
