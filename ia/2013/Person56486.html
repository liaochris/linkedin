Jackie Bradbury's Summary

It's almost impossible to go from point "A" to point "B: without a road map, and even harder when you don't know your exact destination and you don't know the milestones along the way.

Many small business owners do the equivalent when it comes to business planning and marketing - they jump into using a variety of tactics, with no plan in place, no goal to reach, and no audience in mind.

Then, after a lot of money and time has been spent, they ask themselves, "Where did my marketing dollars go, and what did it get me?"... and have no answer.

This is where I can help. I work individually with small business owners to create marketing plans customized to their situation and their budget, and together, we devise a game plan to use the right tactics, to test and evaluate the effectiveness of those tactics, and to identify and grow their ideal customer base.

At the end of this process, small business owners will be able to concentrate very limited marketing dollars and time on tactics that are focused on their ideal customers, and have a method in place to evaluate whether or not those tactics are working for them.

Key areas of specialty: business planning, marketing planning, small business, budgeting, marketing testing
