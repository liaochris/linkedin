Benjamin Robbins' Summary

As one of the Top 50 Mobile Influencers on Twitter (http://blogs.sap.com/innovation/mobile-applications/top-50-mobile-twitter-influencers-05660), and one of the Top 50 must read IT blogs by BizTech (http://www.biztechmagazine.com/article/2012/09/50-must-read-it-blogs-2012) Benjamin Robbins is one of the most sought after voices in the mobile and real-time delivery world.

Truly living and breathing mobile is what he does. In partnership with The Guardian, Samsung and SAP, Benjamin is spending an entire year working solely from a single mobile device (http://www.guardian.co.uk/media-network/series/mobile-only).

Benjamin is also a regular contributor to The Guardian, the Enterprise Mobility Forum (www.theemf.org) and the remotelyMOBILEblog.com on the subjects of BYOD, Mobile Strategy, Mobile Device/Application/Information Management, and Mobile Security.
