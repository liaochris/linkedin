Andrew Natoli's Summary

Hi! I’m Andrew, a web developer from Long Island, New York.

Since being introduced to the internet at a young age, I developed a passion for building websites and custom web-based software. With nearly a decade of programming experience, I have worked on and developed many websites using HTML, PHP, CSS, Javascript and MySQL.

While studying computer science at Stony Brook University, I am actively working with a variety of clients through my own business which offers web and media production services. A majority of my current work is done with Desktop Solutions Software, a Long Island based company that specializes in custom PHP software.

I have additionally taken time to study and acquire a degree in broadcast media production. I started my media production career with SmithtownRadio.com, a hyper-local online radio station and news source for which I was featured in Newsday along with my team. Future work brought me to Clear Channel's WALK 97.5fm, Cablevision's News 12 Long Island and various client projects through my business.
