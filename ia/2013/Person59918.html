Jay Morris' Summary

Seasoned communications professional with over three decades of experience in journalism, media relations, public affairs, PR and marketing. Award-winning writer, editor, marketer and manager. Advisor to CEOs, boards and industry leaders.

Formed Jay Morris Communications LLC in October 2011 after successfully leading communications and marketing teams for over 20 years. Experienced in all aspects of messaging, branding, public relations outreach, conference and event promotion, issues management, magazine and newsletter publishing, speech writing and web development.

Dedicated to impeccable client service, extraordinary value and tangible, bottom-line results.

Specific skills and experience:

• Writing articles, op-eds, marketing copy, web content, testimony and speeches
• Editing and publishing newspapers, newsletters, magazines and online publications
• Conducting media campaigns and serving as press spokesperson
• Creating marketing collateral, including print and electronic
• Managing issues and coalition building
• Branding, including corporate identity campaigns
• Leading and managing teams and departments
• Counseling CEOs, boards and industry leaders
• Strategic planning and budgeting
