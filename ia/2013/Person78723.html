Laura Oldham's Summary

I started El-Be Social Marketing as a way to combine the eclectic skills I've gained throughout the years into one single job. Although I went to school for journalism, my work with small companies has given me the opportunity to learn more about social media, SEO and developing basic websites.

Now that I'm two years into this awesome adventure, I can say with confidence that I can efficiently build a solid WordPress website, concisely write search-friendly copy, and create comprehensive social media plans for clients. I love working with small businesses in fields that are new to me; I embrace challenges, and I'm a nerd for learning.

More than anything else, I love helping people. Nothing makes me happier than helping a small business build its online presence in a way that rivals its largest competitors. If you need help with your blog, setting up your Facebook or Twitter pages, or building a website, I'm your lady.
