Drew Spencer's Summary

I am first and foremost a strategic thinker and analyst. My career background is in advertising, where since 1997 I have had a focus on interactive (as we called it then) or Digital Communications and have enjoyed a reputation as a creative and innovative thinker. I have extensive experience in Digital Marketing and Advertising that I now use to advise on business strategy and infrastructure.

In 2006, I became the Digital Director at Euro RSCG Riley and was invited to join the company's Board of Directors. It was here that I learned about building board-level relationships, managing teams and running a business. During this time, I also took on leadership of the Account Planning team, established the agency's Analysis division and was promoted to Chief Strategy Officer across 3 group companies.

I have a proven track record of strong performance which includes: making consistent and significant year-on-year increases in P&L contribution; anticipating market needs and developing business plans which create new revenue streams; identifying, recruiting and motivating talented people to achieve both short- and long-term professional goals; promoting my business and my own personal brand through business through regular speaking engagements, thought-leadership and strong pitch presentation skills; and producing innovative award-winning work either directly or through my team on a regular basis.

Specialties: Social Media strategy development, implementation and measurement; Interactive marketing strategy from scoping stage through to ongoing analysis and ROI optimisation; User Experience Design; Web site interface design and development; Web usability; CRM and Relationship-Marketing strategy development; User-centric design process; Web site traffic analysis; Brand Strategy, Proposition development, Search Engine Optimisation and Marketing; Behavioural targeting; Media planning; P&L Management
