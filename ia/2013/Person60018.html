Jay Holman's Projects

  • Case Studies: Lessons Learned with Smart Inverters

    Team Members: Jay Holman

    Developing a series of case studies for a leading US-based research institute. Case studies capture lessons learned from the deployment of smart inverters at Solar PV and Battery Energy Storage System deployments worldwide. Smart inverters incorporate functionality such as low voltage ride through, volt/var control, and others that enable utilities to control or influence the impact of the systems on the grid.

  • Strategy Development for Early Stage Materials Startup

    Team Members: Jay Holman

    Providing strategic advisory services to early stage startup emerging from CU Boulder. Company's focus is on the development of innovative sintering processes for ceramic materials. Supporting the development of a business plan, including a highly targeted go-to-market strategy.

  • Strategic Sales Plan for Novel Battery

    Team Members: Jay Holman

    Developing strategic sales plan for startup commercializing a unique battery. Interviewing industry players, carrying out web-based research, and creating quantitative models to determine which customer segments represent the fastest, easiest path to revenue for the client's technology.

  • Strategic Sales Plans for Li-Ion Battery Separator

    Team Members: Jay Holman

    Developed strategic sales plans covering multiple market segments for a startup commercializing innovative Li-ion battery separators. Each sales plan identifies the most attractive segment of the market being evaluated, the drivers behind that segment's attractiveness, and a prioritized list of target customers within the segment. Evaluation takes into account cell manufacturing technologies, cell chemistries, manufacturing location, application requirements, battery costs, and additional factors impacted by battery separators.

  • Market Opportunity Assessment for Hybrid Micro-Grids

    Team Members: Jay Holman

    Working as a subcontractor to a leading software and services provider to the micro-grid industry, assessed the market opportunity for a project developer expanding its footprint in the growing hybrid micro-grid market. Determined the relative attractiveness of island countries for hybrid micro-grid development, covering select countries in Southeast Asia, Oceania, and the Indian Ocean.

  • Smart Grid Case Studies Based On Utility Grid Optimization Projects

    Team Members: Jay Holman

    Completed four case studies of utility grid optimization projects. Utility projects included volt/VAR optimization, conservation voltage reduction, and auto-sectionalizing and restoration.

  • ASES / IDC Energy Insights Community of North American PV Installers

    • November 2011 to March 2012
    Team Members: Jay Holman

    Partnered with the American Solar Energy Society to establish an online community of North American solar installers and project developers who agreed to participate in a series of surveys on industry topics. Survey results provided Energy Insights clients with ongoing, in-depth insight into the needs, concerns, opinions, and outlook of solar installers.
