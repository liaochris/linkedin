Barb Lazarus' Summary

Co-Founder & Owner of Game On! LLC :
Game On! Sports Camps 4 Girls - multi-week summer day camps located in Illinois devoted to all sports just for girls; During the summer weeks, campers ranging from K through 8th grades play it all. Game On! – where tennis, volleyball, basketball, softball, lacrosse, soccer, swimming, horseback riding, flag football, ultimate frisbee, yoga, karate & women's pro & collegiate sporting events are a way of life. Game On! is about growth as an athlete & a person, inspiring both the recreational & travel athlete. www.gameonsportscamp.com

CURRENT GOALS: Generally: Impacting young girls around the world through sport; Specifically: Advocating for quality girls sports programming across the country & around the world; partnering with individuals, businesses & organizations (who share the passion to pass on the gift of sports) to bring Game On! Sports Camps 4 Girls to young girls worldwide; contributing to building a fan base for women's professional & collegiate sports; becoming a trusted resource for parents seeking out quality developmental & advanced sports programs for their daughters. For more, see http://gameonsportscamp.com/about-us/your-owner-and-founder/

Barb Lazarus' Experience

Owner

Game On! Sports Camp 4 Girls

Public Company; 51-200 employees; Sports industry

2006 – Present (7 years)

Game On! Sports Camps 4 Girls are multi-week, summer sports day camps that inspire girls of all athletic abilities, ages 4 – 14. Established in 2007 and offering a multi-sport experience, the Game On! program is empowering, safe, spirited and fun. It’s about instruction in and exposure to a multitude of sports and role models who share our passion. With particular attention to teaching skills related to sports and life, our campers experience growth as an athlete and a person. The program promises smiles and laughter, memories and friendships, achievements and challenges, and the spirited,fun environment that girls deserve.

A summer Game On! style will include tennis, volleyball, basketball, softball, lacrosse, soccer, swimming, flag football, ultimate frisbee, dodge ball, badminton, field hockey, in-camp clinics run by collegiate and pro athletes and high school coaches and field trips (family outings) to women’s pro sports games. The program is truly unique and all about quality.
