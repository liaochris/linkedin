Andrew Forman's Summary

As an information technology professional for over 21 years, I have developed a reputation of success in software architecture and engineering and architecture, particularly in product development and project recovery. My focus has been on enterprise software engineering leadership with emphasis on architecture, system integration, security, and team collaboration. Those I work with know me as an effective communicator, a quick and expansive learner, and as a person who works to ensure beneficial outcomes for everyone.

My experience includes varied industry verticals such as aerospace, real estate finance, global trade, higher education, telecommunications and public service. As a technical leader, I am comfortable covering a variety of roles within a software project including solutions architect, development lead, system analyst, technical project manager, requirements analysis, and software QA/Test. My experience ranges from enterprise-wide projects with release budgets in the tens of millions, to customer-facing, high-stakes, client and web-based applications, and down to division and workgroup-scaled solutions covering both enabling infrastructure and user-facing systems.

Specialties: Enterprise and solution architecture, software architecture and engineering, application and systems integration, team leadership and mentoring, business and technical analysis, knowledge management, long-distance team management, technical project management, agile project methodologies, AS9100 / ISO9000 quality management systems, ARINC standards.
