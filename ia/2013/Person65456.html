Joel Blake's Summary

Hi, how are you?

I appreciate you taking the time, to learn more about who i am and what i do!

Business is about the relationships you create and nurture, which have led me to become an multi-award winning Entrepreneur, CR Consultant and Public Speaker.

I have over 15 years of experience, in helping companies become more inclusive, resulting in them making a significant level of social investment and achieving sustainable value to their business.

I am at my best when i asked to help CEO's, corporate boards and senior management staff to feel more confident to challenge themselves and understand each other better, in order to meet the needs of diverse stakeholders.

My other area of expertise is in creating private member peer learning hubs, for ambitious business owners / entrepreneurs.

Please feel free to see below to learn more about the businesses I own and that i am involved in.

Outside of business:

- Regional Ambassador for StartUp Britain.
- Non-Executive Director x2
- Founder of the Annual Midlands CSR Summit
- Chair of an educational concierge service for small businesses.
- One of the UK's youngest ever Corporate Governance Practitioners.

I am interested in opportunities to help companies develop sustainable CR activities and to create win / win relationships with joint venture partners in relation to building Business Academies, Global CR programmes and Enterprise Training.
