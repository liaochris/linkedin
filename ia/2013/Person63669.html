Jill Grimm's Summary

Our firm helps select corporations and ad agencies find talented marketing, advertising, public relations and digital professionals.

We identify, recruit, assess, and manage the recruitment process. We are able to keep a pulse on the talent available in markets throughout the US. We develop relationships that can be tapped to fill our clients’ needs, when they need them. We are in the marketplace daily connecting with top talent, offering career consulting and management to help the most talented take advantage of hidden opportunities and the right opportunities to move their careers forward. At the same time, the candidate knowledge we have allows us to understand with depth, both the needs of the candidate and the client for great long term fit.

I am fascinated by what makes people tick….especially successful, passionate people. I have an opportunity to connect everyday and get into the heads of the very successful. It is satisfying to make the connections and help our client companies and candidates grow and prosper.
