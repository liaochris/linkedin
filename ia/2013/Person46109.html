Fredrik Westin's Summary

A visionary, strategic, and highly experienced healthcare industry business executive with over 10 years of P&L responsibility and demonstrated ability as a change agent. Internationally traveled, four language fluent leader with a track record of generating revenue in start-up markets and underperforming businesses. Skilled at assessing organizational and business strengths and weaknesses, building and leading diverse teams of professionals in establishing objectives and creating and executing plans focused on differentiation, improving competiveness, and increasing value for stakeholders. Expertise in the areas of Operations, Manufacturing, Product Development, Research, Project Management and Portfolio Management.
