Jason Shum's Summary

Over 13 years of experience working on many different projects in video games, film, game cinematics, commercials, music videos and previz for film. I enjoy working on both cartoony and realistic animation and have extensive motion capture experience as well.

I am also currently an animation instructor at Gnomon and Santa Monica College. I have been an animation mentor at Westwood College and have teaching experience heading the animation classes at Heinze Institute.
