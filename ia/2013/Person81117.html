Linda Albright's Summary

Ten years of hands-on consulting experience, specializing in training and coaching executives and their teams in businesses ranging from 15-150 employees. Use of proven frameworks for simplifying and streamlining client's operations and maximizing profitability, as well as helping teams build capabilities to recognize and exploit disruptive innovations. Prior to consulting, helped launch three successful ventures (including consumer service and B-B e-commerce). Significant business development experience in both US and EU software sales.

Specialties

Executive, entrepreneurial coaching & training, SMBs, high growth and early stage ventures.
