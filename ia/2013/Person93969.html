Mike Bauman's Summary

Prior to the Building & Real Estate field I had spent 20 years in the insurance industry. First as President and CEO of Bauman Insurance Ageny,Inc and then as a Managing Partner at Insurance & Financial Services, LLP. I spent 2 years in new construction sales with Allen Builders learning the business before being selected to help lead Joseph Douglas Homes into the future. After 4 years and producing successful results. I decided to join my wife Lynn in her Real Estate franchise and launch NewHomesWi.Com. The web site was originally designed to provide new construction sales agents with resources that would help make their jobs easier and in return feed our real estate practice with referral business. It now also serves as our practices web presence. We look for huge sales results going forward. As of October 2008, I have again been asked to lead the sales efforts at Joseph Douglas Home along with co-ownership and management duties at New Homes Wisconsin, LLC and Realty Executives, Northwest. I also work as a real estate consultant to the Gerson-Lehrman Group, a world wide market research firm.

Specialties: We specialize in helping area residents find and sell real estate and build & remodel homes. We have a very deep background in building and developing. I am involved in managing a very successful building company and my partner and wife Lynn has been involved in the development of several large residential developments. We are the area's experts when it comes to residential new construction.
