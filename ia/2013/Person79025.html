Lauren Cassidy's Summary

I knew I wanted to do hair and makeup since I was about 8 years old. I used to do the hair and makeup on my high school cheer team in 1982. Hair, makeup and fashion are my thing. It is my passion. I want my guests to feel happy, excited, and modern about their hair and makeup. I opened my business in 2004. It is a home hair salon where you receive one on one attention. I specialize in cuts, colours, highlights, colour blocking, hair straightening, makeup application, and brow shaping. I do a consultation before we get started so I know the needs ans wants if my guests. It is important that I do my very best and that my guests know I care about their beauty needs.
