Kara Chaffin's Summary

In the past 9 years, Kara has sold over $150+ million in real estate. She is licensed in MD, DC, and VA, and is a member of the Greater Capital Area Association of Realtors. In 2004, Kara began her career as sales representative for NVHomes, one of the top new home luxury builders. While there, she quickly earned numerous awards and ranked consistently in the top 3 Sales people for the entire NVR Company.
In 2008, Kara partnered with Judy Lemmons, an established real estate agent in the Annapolis area. Together, they ranked in the top 5% for all of Long and Foster and Kara gained experienced working in the resale side of the business. In 2009, Kara joined the #1 producing office of Long and Foster that achieves over $1Billion of revenue each year. She has consistently produced results, earning her a place in the prestigious Gold Team of Long and Foster for an annual sales volume in excess of $6M in revenue.
As a real estate consultant, Kara is committed to working with you to achieve your personal and financial goals. You will have a professionally seasoned negotiator on your side, and a fierce advocate for your best interests.

Specialties: First Time Homebuyers, Investors, New Construction, REO's and Short Sales
