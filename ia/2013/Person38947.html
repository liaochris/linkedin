Dwight Battle's Summary

All invitations to connect are welcome: dwight@dwightbattle.com

I am an award-winning independent art director specializing in mobile and digital design, branding, and creative direction. I have been an art director and visual designer for thirteen years, and have consulted with a wide range of clients in various of stages of growth, from established corporations to early-stage startups.

I am focused on results, and creating collaborative partnerships with my team. I’ve driven research and brand strategy, created wireframes, storyboards, visual concepts, style guides and brand standards.

I invite you to view my portfolio of work at www.dwightbattle.com. I am seeking full-time art direction opportunities as well as freelance design opportunities nationwide.

Specialties: Art Direction, Visual Design, Mobile Design, User Interface Design, User Experience Design, Interactive Design, Digital Signage, Wayfinding and Kiosk Design, Identity and Logo Design, Brand Development

The result: An record number of early registrants for MPI Cascadia 2011, and a record high in conference attendance.

The result: Menashe and Sons saw an significant increase in store traffic, and an immediate increase in sales of pieces featured on their website.
