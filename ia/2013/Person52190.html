Heather Hartmann's Summary

In 2008, I began my education at Columbia College. It was here that my idea of what graphic design changed completely. I learned how much of the world is affected by design and how important our role as a designer is. It was at Columbia that I also began to learn XML, PHP, Javascript & other programing languages that I had desperately wanted to learn.

In May 2011, I graduated from Columbia College Chicago with a BFA in Graphic Design and a minor in web development. Today I work as a graphics/web assistant at Createursdeluxe, as well as a freelancer.

As a graduate, I look back at my time at Columbia College Chicago and I am able to see just how much an individual's work style can change in just a few short years. Through my work experience I gained at Columbia as well as my internship, part-time jobs and freelance, I grew as an individual. I continue on with my passion to strengthen my skill set, and I look forward to the new challenges that future employment opportunities will bring.
