Karen Halseth's Summary

I offer Life Coaching services to individuals and professionals to take them from where they are to where they want to be. We answer the question “is the life you are creating the life you really want to be living?” We look at their strengths and their blockers allowing each person to move beyond limitations into a more fulfilling life and career. Each session is individualized to meet the specific needs of the client. Sessions are equally effective in person or over the phone.

I help individuals that have a desire to change and expand. Life changes happen either by choice or by being forced into change, children leaving home for college, loss of job due to layoff, career change due to choice, general yearning for deeper purpose and fulfillment. Each session is crafted to meet the needs of the individual. With this concentrated attention and support, movement and improvement can be made on any issue. My clients report increased communication, enhanced relationships, reaching goals they once believed were unattainable along with an overall higher sense of purpose and well being.

I am dedicated to the success of each individual. I welcome any questions or inquires.

Sincerely,

Karen Halseth
