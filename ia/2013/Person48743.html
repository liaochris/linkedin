Ginger Zumaeta's Summary

Ginger Zumaeta is an unstoppable combination of strategy, analytics and creativity. After 15+ years in the media industry, Ginger has done it all. She is a three-time Emmy Award winner for marketing campaigns she engineered at NBC & Telemundo, has won more Promax Muse awards than she can count on two hands, and even launched a daily UGC (user generated content) show that went head-to-head against Oprah in Los Angeles. Ginger’s knack is in creating clear and actionable integrated marketing plans, as well as all things digital and multicultural.

Ginger is a self-described closet-nerd, early adopter and amateur hacker. She received her BA in Humanities from the University of Notre Dame, did post-graduate studies in Critical Theory at NYU, and received her MBA from USC’s Marshall School of Business. She also currently serves as Adjunct Professor in the School of Management at California Lutheran University.

Ginger is the CoFounder of Beehive Group, a boutique marketing agency specializing in Social Media and Latino Engagement. Ginger leads strategy, analytics and digital/social at the agency.

Ginger is also a published author & public speaker on topics ranging from the latest apps for business owners & entrepreneurs, to social media best practices, to Latino engagement. You can see her tech tips at http://youtube.com/technolatina.
