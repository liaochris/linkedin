Brendan Wlas' Summary

A hands on Performance Test Manager / Lead / Consultant with 10+ years of solid performance testing experience, I'm engaged to lead the testing on projects with the aim of successfully mitigating the risk of problems occurring post go-live. My projects have ranged in size and I am comfortable leading teams or operating alone.

My experience has been in the Banking (Retail and Investment), Insurance, Oil & Gas and Retail sectors and I have delivered testing for a number of large-scale global transformational and regulatory programmes, often in challenging circumstances.

Having spent a number of years in a performance testing centre of excellence, I have been heavily involved in quality assurance process development and as such I have the ability to build a team and embed new (or improve existing) processes. I have worked using a number of models (onshore, offshore, outsourced) and believe in choosing an approach that is fit for purpose.

I have experience in the load & stress testing of different technologies including SAP (Portal, NetWeaver, BI/BW, ECC/R/3, CRM, APO, EBP/SRM, PI/XI, KM, TREX), eCommerce (ATG Commerce, ATG Merchandising, ATG Customer Service, Oracle Product Information Management, IBM Sterling Order Management, Endeca, WebSphere Commerce), Microsoft (.NET, MVC, SSIS, MRS, IIS, High Performance Computing), Algorithmics, Platform Symphony, Database (Oracle, SQL Server), Sabrix, Informatica, J2EE (IBM WebSphere, JBoss), Integration (TIBCO, MQ), Tivoli Identity Management, Siebel, Citrix.
