Elizabeth Doherty Thomas, MS in MFT's Summary

*** BREAKING NEWS ***

Bill Doherty, Ph.D., and daughter Elizabeth Doherty Thomas launch The Doherty Relationship Institute, LLC. www.DohertyRelationshipInstitute.com

This is a multi-division business with various ways for therapists, other experts, and couples to engage in strengthening relationships and marriages. Please visit the website to plug in!

*** Masters in Marriage and Family Therapy - May 15, 2013

I bridge technology, business, marketing, branding, and a high energy enthusiasm for non-techie therapists to attract more clients, find more energy, and be more inspired to market.

My BIG VISION is for every therapist to feel empowered and aware of their core calling and to express their passion in a client attractive way. This also means every prospective client will easily be able to find a fantastic therapist who they feel a connection to right away.

WRITING EXPERIENCE:
I've been blessed to write for the Psychotherapy Networker magazine twice, be their official Social Media Consultant, a featured writer in the quarterly issues of the Minnesota division of AAMFT (American Association of Marriage and Family Therapy), and am writing in the Colorado AAMFT division and others. I also run a premarital blog, am a co-author on Take Back Your Wedding, a premarital relationship book, write heavily on a therapy marketing blog, and have a Psychology Today blog (that I haven't yet started.)

SPEAKING AND PRESENTING:
My largest speaking was a keynote to 1,500 marriage educators on my premarital work. I get and give a lot of energy in front of groups of people and have presented to groups of 10 to 65 people, inspiring them and sharing high quality content, not a little content with a huge sales pitch. I'm always interested in workshop opportunities to share my knowledge with therapists!
