Jesse Zanger's Summary

I've been in the news business for 15 years, starting in television and transitioning to the web.

As Deputy Managing Editor of CBSNewYork.com, I am responsible for the content that appears on the site. I manage a team that is spread across WCBS-TV, WCBS 880, 1010 WINS and WFAN.

Prior to joining CBSNewYork.com, I was part of CBS Television Stations Digital Media, creating content and managing the national and world news coverage for the websites of CBS owned & operated stations. I was also the liaison between the local digital teams and CBS News.

I was a "one man band" at the 2008 conventions. I'd shoot, edit and post quick video segments several times a day.

In 2010, I helped launch the new CBS Local sites across the country - no small effort.

I ran Fox News Channel's crawl for six years. It was given to me in an unfinished state and I was told to "make it work" editorially, shortly after 9/11. I created the style guide for the crawl, hired, fired, trained, reviewed and managed a team of eight personnel. A few articles about my time at the Fox News crawl are Google-able.

I spent five fantastic years at NY1 News, a local cable news outlet, running around town as a shooter/producer, covering every kind of story in NYC. I shot, wrote, edited and produced stories.

Finally, I am huge foodie, and will gladly regale you with recommendations of places to eat.
