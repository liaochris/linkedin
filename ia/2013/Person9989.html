Jeff Herring's Summary

I am the Founder & CEO of The Article Marketing Network and have been using Article Marketing to build my and client's businesses since 1994.

I first began using articles to build my Relationship Coaching practice in 1994 with a syndicated article column in our local newspaper in Tallahassee, Fla. This filled my practice locally, and when the column became internationally syndicated, it filled my practice from all over the world.

So I've been building my businesses with Article Marketing since 1994 and am here to do the exact same thing for you.

In 2005 I turned this Article Marketing expertise on the Internet and have been sought out by Alex Mandossian, Mari Smith, Kevin Nations and others. Bill Glazer of the Glazer-Kennedy Inner Circle has called me THE go-to guy for Article Marketing.

Let's talk about how we can help you leverage the 5 Pillars of Article Marketing:

Content Creation

Traffic Generation

Product Creation

to increase your prospects, publicity and profits in your business. Visit me at http://JeffHerring.com and http://JeffHerringWebinars.com
