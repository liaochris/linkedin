Asad Syed's Summary

Asad has years of hands-on technical experience in the area of Information Security Strategy formulation and execution, Security Architecture, Design & Security Control hands-on implementation via a phased project approach; comprise of APPs, Data and Network security architecture design experience. Risk Management experience in global fortune 500 companies with operations in multiple countries. Experiences include application of Digital Forensics in the field of high tech digital crime and investigations on multi-national projects crossing international boundaries.

His understanding and application of Security Architecture and Principles using different technologies in the present day’s dynamic culture of mobile devices, access of data anytime from anywhere, any device and cloud computing is phenomenally exuberant. In addition, he has a wealth of experience in Security Risk Management and Compliance space of SoX, PCI, HIPAA, etc. His attitude towards goals fulfillment along with his ability to explain complex security matters in a simple and clear manner makes him one of a kind in the Chicago land. With the addition of his creative writing and track record of successful project delivery and mentoring, gives his projects a very unique human touch in the Security field like non others.

Asad with his unique perspective have completed many successful projects for companies like McDonald's Corporation, Dean Witter Discover, IBM Global Services, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Northwestern Memorial Hospital, and now leading the IT Security and Architecture at OfficeMax, Chicago head quarters.

Asad currently holds or held in the past security certifications like CISSP, ISSAP, CISM.

Specialties: Security Strategy formulation, Security Architecture, Governance Structure & Security best practices development based upon ISO 27001, NIST and/or COBIT; Consulting on secure integration with Cloud Computing including IaaS, PaaS and SaaS;
