With a degree from UCLA in Computer Science in 1995, I entered the world of online marketing to build a successful business; today offering a full-service, website development, internet marketing service, specializing in SEO, mobile websites. As an Entrepreneur, with passion, I have also been successful in Real Estate, custom clothing design, and personal health.
