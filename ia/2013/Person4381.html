Alfonso Baella's Summary

Alfonso is the founder of Baella Consulting. He is also a social media strategist. Is autor of “I WANT TO BE PRESIDENT. How to use the Internet to win an election? The Barack Obama’s electronic strategy”.

He has played a key role in the launch of dozens of successful social media and Internet marketing strategies that have seen millions of visitors. He works also as a consultant with young leaders who are using social media to promoting polítical ciberactivism. It is also a lecturer and writes frequently about politics.

Alfonso worked with the Peruvian economist, Pedro Pablo Kuczynski, PPK, on the 2011 Peruvian Presidential Campaign directing his on line strategy. PPK increased from 15.000 to 250,000 fans in 60 days and jumped from 3 to 18.5% of preferences during the same period. Social media played an important role calling for hundreds of thousands of young people within and outside Peru.

Alfonso launch on december 2012 an on line movement in Peru (www.facebook.com/17M.pe) that has now 350,000 followers. Is also cofounder of a non profit organization named Lampadia.com, a Think Tank focus on promoting ideas of freedom and development in Peru.

Specialties: TV Production, Managing of Information Technologie, Social Media, Emarketing, Politics.

Alfonso Baella's Experience

Owner

Baella Consulting (ABH Consulting sac)

February 2001 – Present (12 years 6 months)

Baella Consulting is a consultancy focused on interactive communication. Its services comprise the development of an online marketing strategy, the variety of products necessary to implement it as well as a sophisticated customer relationship management, i.e. maintenance and support. The company turns its particular attention to the implementation of multimedia content and applications.

Based on its comprehensive experience within this segment Baella Consulting has developed a core competence in consulting corporate clients. In order to meet the changing and increasing requirements of those clients the company is going to enhance the gained experience in an ongoing process. The Baella Consulting team adapts to trends, e.g. in certain industries, and converts them into sophisticated solutions for its clients. Further, the company creates new designs, applications, tools, etc. and, thus, also develops new trends in online-marketing.

Currently, Baella Consulting has three offices - in Germany, Peru and USA - and provides its clients with local expertise connected with international know how.

Baella Consulting has its roots in Peru where today is the centre of the eFactory, a concept which was incorporated in order to vanish borders - borders do not exist in a world of interactive communication. Interactive communication facilitates a company's success - no matter where you are.
