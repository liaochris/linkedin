Franz Redl's Summary

Franz moved to Maine from his native Austria in 1997 where he managed a B&B Inn prior to starting his career in Real Estate.

He gained extensive experience in building construction and renovation. Before coming to the U.S., he held a management position in the travel industry.

Drawn to New Mexico by it's enchanting spirit and magnificent climate, Franz is devoted to serving the Real Estate Community in and around Albuquerque.

Throughout his career in the Tourism and Real Estate Industries he has been repeatedly rewarded for his dedication to superior service as demonstrated by the repeat business and referrals of his clients

Specialties

Residential Single Family and Multifamily Real Estate Sales as well as Investment Properties and Land. Foreclosure and Short Sale Homes in the Greater Albuquerque New Mexico area.
Residential and Commercial Property Management.
