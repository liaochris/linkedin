Margaret Norton's Summary

I've decided that it was time for me to take control of my weight. After trying some weight loss & maintenance products manufactured by It Works!, I decided to become an Independent Distributor for them. Their goal of helping individuals get healthy and age gracefully perfectly aligns with my passion to help others. As an Independent Distributor, I'll be able to use my writing, coaching and speaking by challenging and motivating others to get healthy.

I am a writer. My memoir, When Ties Break, was released in August 2010 as a paperback. The electronic version was released in 2011. I also do freelance writing. What I enjoy most is personal coaching. Life Transitions was founded to assist individuals wanting to make changes in their lives, but not knowing where to start. They need a coach!

I also give motivational talks to churches, community organizations, and women's groups. See my web site for additional information.

How did I begin all these careers? I started writing and coaching while in the mortgage business. I became frustrated with the frequent layoffs and changing work environment. I no longer felt that what I did made a difference. In 2004, I trained to be a Stephen Minister. In 2005 & 2006, I enrolled in Creative Writing classes and started writing my first book. In 2007, I trained to be a personal life coach.

In 2011, I feel that I have come full circle. Everything that I have trained for is falling into place. My sincere desire is to help others reach their full potential through my writing, coaching and speaking. Sometimes this isn't enough and that's where natural supplements and weight loss products fit in. It is possible to live your dream. You can start over again.

Specialties

Independent Distributor: Weight management, anti-aging, body wraps and nutritional supplements.

Coaching: Specializing in helping individuals handle difficult changes, as well as weight and health related issues.

Writing: Nonfiction, memoir, short stories, editorial articles, book reviews, blogs, websites, and ghostwriting.

Speaking topics: divorce, abuse, grief, writing your life story, forgiveness, weight loss, healthy living, and empowering women.

Margaret Norton's Experience

Freelance Writer

Author of When Ties Break

August 2010 – Present (3 years 3 months)

Divorce, abuse, abortion, excommunication, chronic illness, homelessness, death, bankruptcy, sibling rivalry, adultery, single parenthood, drug addiction, low self-esteem, depression—some people suffer from one or several of these in their lives; others deal with them daily. What makes the story in When Ties Break different, exceptional, and inspirational is that the author has lived through each one of these challenging, sometimes terrifying problems. Through God's help and her own courage and perseverance, Margaret not only thrived after loss but came out on top with a new energy, a renewed spirit, and a sense of worth.

Midwest Book Review:
"Loss is something that one will face over and over again; what matters is how it's dealt with. When Ties Break: A Memoir About how to Thrive After Loss is a memoir of Margaret Norton, detailing her vast experience with loss, as she hopes readers will read her tale and use her story to help them deal with their own challenges in life. Inspired by faith, When Ties Break is strongly recommended."
