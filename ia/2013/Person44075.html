Ethan Levy's Summary

Ethan Levy is a 10 year veteran as a game producer and designer. He has worked at companies big and small, including BioWare, Pandemic Studios, PlayFirst, iWin and Electonic Arts' Office of the Chief Creative Officer. He has contributed to over 20 shipped titles that have appeared on every platform. Currently, he works as an independent game developer and a game design consultant in the mobile/social space.

As part of BioWare San Francisco, he led the development of browser-based Dragon Age Journeys and Dragon Age Legends Remix and social network Dragon Age Legends as Game Producer. Dragon Age Legends was named as one of Kotaku's "5 Best Games on Facebook" and was nominated for 2 awards at the GDC Online Choice Awards in 2011. The three Dragon Age games have totaled over 50 million plays.
