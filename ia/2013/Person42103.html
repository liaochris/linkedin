Eric Harr's Summary

Eric Harr is a digital marketing and brand strategist, best-selling author, CBS television host, former syndicated columnist with The Los Angeles Times, Founder & CEO of Make a Stand, a new Social Purpose Company, and the Founder & President of Resonate Digital, a Bay Area-based integrated digital marketing agency.

At Resonate, Mr. Harr is responsible for diagnosing and establishing overall strategies (including guiding policies and coherent actions), developing KPIs, sharpening brand messaging, developing content marketing strategies and drawing out actionable insights from a range of social analytics. He has worked with clients such as HP, The Four Seasons, Lexus and The Conference Board.

He is the Founder & CEO of Make a Stand Inc. SPC, a new for-profit Social Purpose Company building a brand platform, starting with lemonade, to address the issue of child slavery. The company has been featured on CBS News, ABC News, NBC News, BBC World News, The Huffington Post and other media. (http://makeastand.com)

Mr. Harr is a former nationally-syndicated columnist with The Los Angeles Times and the author of: The Real Truth About Social Media. He is an on-air correspondent with CBS TV, an author for Random House and Rodale and the Founding Editorial Director of VIV Magazine, the first mass-market women's digital magazine (named after his daughter Vivienne): http://vivmag.com.

He is a triathlete, ranked #6 in the world in his first year and finished 40th overall at the 2011 Ironman Triathlon World Championships in 9:01:34, while raising money for humanitarian causes.

Mr. Harr founded SocialSee, the first technology to calculate a company's Return on Investment from social media in real-time: http://socials.ee. He created FlyRight, the first real-time customer service mobile app for the airline industry: http://flyrightapp.com.
