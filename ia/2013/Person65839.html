Joey Janisheck's Summary

Joey began his love with computing when he got a Commodore Vic-20 (complete with audio cassette data recorder) as a child.

Over the past 18 years, Joey has worked for startups in Austin, Dallas, Boston, Vancouver, San Francisco and New York City, as a development leader. He has also excelled in large corporate settings working on or managing fully staffed teams. Big or small, Joey is focused on delivering easy-to-use mobile & web solutions.
