Delton Demarest's Summary

I have been working in the freelance industry for 5 years. Recent and past clients include, Infinite Monkey Theorem, Phat Thai Cherry Creek, Atmosphere of Rhymesayers Entertainment, Pacifico, Indy Ink, University of Northern Colorado, Federal Customs Building Denver, Brothers BBQ, GBs Fish n Chips, Prime Element of Kamakazi Records, and numerous other local and national clients.

I am an active participant in Plein Air and Fine art events here in Denver, including the Denver Plein Air Arts Festival 2007-2011, and have had select showings with Abend Fine Art.

As a muralist I have worked on numerous large scale exterior murals, a short listing of these works begins with the "Imagine Mural" located on the exterior of the Federal Customs House that I apprenticed to Darrel Anderson, The Sun Valley Youth Center mural, located on 12th and Decatur, and the Rude Rec Center Mural interior mural that accompanies the large community pool. A map of mural locations is in the works.

I aim to gain more work in the advertising and editiorial genres of illustration, in addition to expanding my horizons to work in the sci-fi/fantasy,comic and gaming genres.

Through continuous field and observational study my hope is to lend to my illustration and mural skills by enhancing my knowledge and ability to translate both from my imagination and from the natural world.

Specialties: Oil Painting, Aerosol Painting, Acrylic Painting, Custom Design, Conceptual Artwork, Cover Artwork, Narrative Artwork, Portraiture, Human and Animal Anatomy

Lead Illustrator and Curator

Broken Bread Arts

April 2008 – Present (5 years 6 months)

Broken Bread Arts is privately owned and operated mural arts and urban arts foundation that doubles as a platform for up and coming artists, and provides a gallery both online and physical for artists to showcase their work. In addition to providing youth with a means to grow and learn in their craft, the company has served as a staple of both public art platforms and street art platforms in Denver and abroad. With this two-fold focus Broken Bread has featured COPE 2 of NYC, amongst numerous other shows all the while BB artists are producing large scale murals with the City of Denver, Federal Government, local non-profits and multitudes of private clients. Broken Bread houses both resident artists and opens doors to budding and flourishing artists of all ages.

You can also view timelapse videos of the Broken Bread Murals in progress at YouTube.Com
