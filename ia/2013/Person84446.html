Malcolm York's Summary

Malcolm is a first class leader and a highly experienced business mentor, business developer, manager and director with an excellent track record of acquiring new business and building lasting customer relationships.

Malcolm has a demonstrable track record in the sales growth of IT, telecoms, FM and technical services, embracing markets including the legal industry, public sector, local government and FTSE 500 companies. His highly determined and driven character focuses on achieving results, revenue, profit and performance.

Malcolm has the additional resource in that he is a skilled marketeer and is a specialists in guerrilla marketing tactics, strategy, planning, execution and management.

He has a proven record of recruiting, coaching, developing and motivating scalable teams, coupled with excellent interpersonal and people skills. This enables him to manage, communicate, mentor and negotiate effectively at all levels.

Malcolm's broad management experience, which includes sales, marketing, operations, project management and training, enables him to fit into any organisation looking for business growth.

His excellent commercial acumen and general business skills, along with the ability to work collaboratively and to build effective internal and external relationships make him an ideal candidate for companies looking for business/sales growth.

Malcolm has published five eBooks, and many white papers including 'Marketing on a Shoestring' and 'Social Media for B2B', and has addressed audiences exceeding 500 delegates. He is also accredited by the Law Society to deliver CPD-based marketing training for Lawyers.
