Beth Hoban SPHR's Volunteer Experience & Causes

  • Volunteer Experience

    • Executive Board Member

      Women's Skills Bureau of Saudi Arabia
      • Social Services
      January 2011 – present (2 years 9 months)

      http://wsb-ksa.com

      The Women's Skills Bureau (WSB) is a community based non-profit organization who's primary goal is to connect expat spouses in Saudi Arabia with information on employment opportunities, community activities and promote professional networking.

      WSB’s services are available to everyone at no charge. Our website, monthly newsletters (distributed to over 5,000 people) and training sessions provide spouses with an outlet to find opportunities that support their personal and professional growth.

      We rely entirely on the generous donations of community members and businesses for funding.

      The segregation of sexes and transportation restrictions are significant challenges making working and living difficult for expat women in Riyadh. Employers are challenged to recruit quality expat employees who are willing to relocate their families to Saudi Arabia. WSB provides useful information and resources to potential incoming families and new arrivals to assist in transition.

      WSB is making a difference in the lives of others and benefiting the whole community.
