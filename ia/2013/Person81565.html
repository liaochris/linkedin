Lisa Kanda's Summary

Lisa Kanda, owner and chief results officer at Elkay Corporate Advisors, is a professional speaker, business/marketing strategist; executive and personal branding coach; and social media consultant and trainer. She focuses her work on helping companies, solo and entrepreneurs and individuals break through obstacles to their achieving success by providing the tools to realize improved results for them in their professional and personal lives.

Elkay Corporate Advisors provides business and marketing strategic planning, organizational and people development, and executive and personal branding coaching services for small and mid-size companies and professional individuals.

We are also available for keynote speeches and training workshops on a variety of topics including social media marketing, leadership development, customer loyalty, and time management and for customized facilitation of your company meetings.

Lisa brings 25+ years of versatile experience in working with corporate, government, education, professional services and non-profit organizations with demonstrated skills in people development and managing projects to meet and exceed organizational goals. Her approach is strategically directed and results-driven. She has worked for local, regional, national and international companies where she has developed and implemented strategic marketing/business development and social media plans; facilitated focus groups, strategic planning sessions and staff skill development sessions; managed the national launch of an awareness building and learning campaign for the United States Department of Labor; and oversaw the launch of three technology-based international products.

Lisa is a co-host of the Blog Talk Radio show, 30 Minute Business Dig, is a contributing writer to “Jersey Shore Woman” newspaper, where she was noted as an Entrepreneurial Woman on the Move.
