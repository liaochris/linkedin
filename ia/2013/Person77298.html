Krystal Whitten's Summary

My professional experiences include working with clients and account executives on a daily basis, striving for excellence in my designs as well as helping coworkers achieve a higher standard of work. I have worked in difficult environments that have taught me to remain calm, level-headed and professional in everything I do.

My goals are to grow my knowledge, expertise and experience in print production and design programs like Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign. I want to continue to be cutting edge and in tune with the current trends in design as well as maintain the balance of classic, clean design.
