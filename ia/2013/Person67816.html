The Both And Organization (BAO)

Many of you have recently asked me about the BAO located at http://BothAnd.Org . The website development is proceeding and we are still on schedule to begin live member testing on March 1, 2013. Can you volunteer to help test? If so, please send me a message.

The BAO is a free online community for C-Level (or equivalent) Leaders of all types of organizations. Its origins are:

1. My own experience as a C-Level Officer. When I achieved this level leadership for the first time, I realized that my options for researching solutions to organizational issues were greatly limited. An unfortunate climate of many organizations is such that a C-Level Officer asking for advice is an indication of weakness and vulnerability. I see it as a sign of strength as the leader is willing to research, learn, adapt and adjust organizational culture, strategy and tactics to accommodate the new knowledge. This opinion of strength is not commonly shared.

2. I have been asked by many of my friends and colleagues, who also serve as C-Level (or equivalent) Officers, to create a network of leaders of whom we can ask anonymous questions, eliminating judgement and fear of what we do & do not know.

As a result of recent business successes, I am now in a position to personally fund this free online community. Check http://BothAnd.Org for periodic updates.
