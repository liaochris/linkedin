Lori Fenstermaker's Summary

Lori Fenstermaker, is a recruiter and entrepreneur revolutionizing the way companies source for prospective employees. Lori founded Scavado after realizing there had to be a more efficient way to mine the Internet to source for specialized employment prospects.

Lori also founded Scavado Hire (formerly Automatic). A boutique recruitment process outsourcing firm.

Her 17+ years of work on both sides of the recruitment desk and participating in thousands of search projects in various disciplines has enhanced her ability to jump in and help companies with the most needle in a haystack searches one can imagine.

Specialties

Helping recruiters and talent organizations land the best talent by creating efficiencies in the sourcing process.
