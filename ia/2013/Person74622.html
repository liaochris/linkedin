Kermit Wallace's Summary

I currently serve as the CIO of Stroock & Stroock & Lavan LLP, an AMLAW 200 law firm with headquarters in New York City and offices in Los Angeles, Miami and Washington DC. I have over 20 years of experience managing IT strategy, operations and projects.

My past positions include technical and management / leadership roles in a top international AMLAW 100 law firm, brokerage / financial services firm, Fortune 500 software company and city government agency.

I am active in the Legal IT and e-Discovery / Litigation Support communities and have been a speaker at the ILTA annual convention, the LegalTech CIO Forum, the ARK Group / Managing Partner Law Firm Pricing & Profitability conference and the PLTG IT Director Panel.

Specialties: Application development, budgeting, business continuity, disaster recovery, co-location strategies, data center management; software distribution and management; e-Discovery and litigation support; customer service and user support; information security; ITIL; network and infrastructure management; personnel management and staff development; purchasing and inventory / asset management; quality assurance; risk management; telecommunications (VoIP); training
