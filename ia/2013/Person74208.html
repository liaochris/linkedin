Ken Forster's Summary

For over 25 years, Ken has been developing innovative products, teams and global businesses across consumer and technology industries.

He has held senior leadership roles at Syngenta, The Coca-Cola Company, and Philip Morris, co-founded several industrial automation companies including ObjectAutomation, and was an award-winning pioneer of PC-based automation for Lockheed and Wonderware.

He has a multicultural business background, having developed global businesses from the United States and Europe, with a recent focus on Brazil, China, and emerging markets in Eastern Europe.

Ken serves on boards of directors and advises select innovative technology companies and international investors. He is passionate about consumer-relevant and transformational technology and active in both the US and Swiss tech investor and start-up communities.
