Bob Gray's Summary

I have always worked hard, trying to lead the Teams I have worked with to be not only successful, productive and cooperative, but also to engage and help our customers, while at the same time moving forward with the Organization's goals. I believe it is important to lead by example.

With that spirit and intensity, the passion for excellent customer service and attention to detail has become second nature.

Today's Business Environment is ever-changing. Developing, maintaining and protecting your Brand, is more important than ever... but the methods are evolving. Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn & Blogs are becoming the tools of engagement.

Combine Social Media with graphic design, web skills, photography, photo-manipulation, music and voice-over resources, BobSongs Creative Media has the means to move your Business or Organization forward.

The BobSongs Team offers tremendous amounts of energy, enthusiasm, and a diverse skill set, to help make your good Business become better.

Specialties

Software: WordPress, CorelDraw, Paint Shop Pro, Adobe Photo Premiere, Social Media software, Crystal Reports, Graphics software, Microsoft Excel & Microsoft Word.
