David M. Crisp, Jr.'s Experience

Owner

SolarAegis, LLC.

Privately Held; Myself Only; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

July 2009 – Present (4 years 6 months) Naples, Florida

SolarAegis, LLC. is a start-up venture supplying an all-natural sunscreen formulation I developed in response to my chronic issues with skin cancer and the inconvenience of having to use sunscreen regularly.

Zinc and titanium-based sunscreens are difficult to apply due to the high solids content of the emulsion. SolarAegis is specially formulated to tackle this issue. The result is a natural product loaded with 17% zinc oxide (active ingredient/mineral UV filter) but still has the characteristics of a smooth lotion for ease of application and convenient, regular use.

SolarAegis is currently being marketed online, in local specialty retail outlets in Southwest Florida and has a growing following in South Korea where sun health awareness continues to increase.

CEO

WooHoo Foods

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Food Production industry

March 2011 – September 2012 (1 year 7 months) Naples, Florida

WooHoo Foods was an early stage, start-up providing a raw, gluten-free, all-natural snack food for kids and active adults. Closed an angel raise with a lead investor in July 2011 and moved forward with an aggressive, reformulation and retail strategy in the organic, specialty foods market. Product was introduced in 17 Whole Foods stores in Florida.

WooHoo believed kids and parents need better options for healthy eating. The days of three, family-meals are gone. On-the-go convenience is in demand and the food options for this lifestyle are sorely limited.

After taking product from "kitchen to commercial" I felt I had exhausted my ability to grow the business any further. An unsuccessful attempt to bring in an outside investor group from the food industry, which would have given us much needed expertise, led me to motion the board to shut down the company. We voted to dissolve WooHoo on 9/7/12.

Lehigh leverages its technology position and expertise in polymer chemistry to provide raw material cost solutions, product performance improvements and sustainability to its customers through the production and application of engineered polymer sourced from recycled tires.
