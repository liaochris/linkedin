Andrew Eberwine's Projects

  • The Longest Day of the Year

    • May 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Andrew Eberwine, R. Sean Lamborne, Darin Graber, Brian McCosky, Dan O'Donnell, Matt Mock

    A roughneck, guitar-driven explosion of new-fangle/old-timey country and rock music began making the local pub crawls in Boulder and Denver. TLDOTY plays music that brings you back to a time when they put up a chain link fence between the crowd and the band. Taking on the task of delivering stories that tug the tattered sleeve of shifting relationships between a man and his family, religion, vices, nature, and himself. Hammering together their influences from Hank Sr. to Modest Mouse, Avett Brothers to Tom Waits, and from Hoots & Hellmouth to Black Keys,” they worked from the ground up building the tall wooden gates labelled Outlaw Americana.
