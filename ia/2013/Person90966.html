Meg Montford's Summary

Executive Career Coach helping executives and professionals convert their career development, career management and career transition challenges into opportunities for success. President and Founder of Abilities Enhanced, 1999. Experienced since 1986 as a careers professional in coaching, consulting, recruiting, and business development. Currently, provide career consulting to expats' spouses and partners in the Greater Kansas City area through Net Expat.

Website: www.abilitiesenhanced.com

Specialties: career coaching, career transition, radical career change, life/work balance, culture fit, workplace communications, job interview coaching, networking strategies, salary negotiation, resume writing, job search strategy, coach training, coach mentoring, small business marketing, social media strategy
