Laurice Close's Experience

De'Amour Designs

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Design industry

2002 – Present (11 years)

Offering freelance graphic & website design to design companies and private clientele throughout Brisbane, the Gold Coast and Australia wide. I have a trendy home office in Currumbin on the Gold Coast where we can meet or I am happy to come to you, or alternativley we can correspond through the wonderful technology of email, messenger and ftp!

I'm skilled in many areas of graphic design, webste design & pre press, from screen printing to websites, brochures to logos. I like to keep a balance in both the online world and the secret society that is print design.

Over the years I have collected quite an assortment of industry contacts from specialist printers to web hosting companies. As a result, I can offer you the whole package from concept to execution, or alternatively I can provide you with some industry insights and handy advice to get you on your way.
