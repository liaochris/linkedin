Christopher Taylor Edwards' Summary

Strategist, designer, and user experience specialist for nonprofit and grassroots communications. Extensive experience with integrated marketing campaigns and online mobilization.

Background includes nearly a decade in marketing and branding as designer, writer and artist. Seasoned with training and experience in interpretive exhibition design, anthropology and architecture history of the urban environment.

Specialties

Training in interpretive exhibit design and planning. Project management and trafficking in advertising and design. Proficient in print media applications within Adobe CS. Additional experience in HTML and Web design. Working knowledge of editing for film and video and programing in Max/MSP.
