Founder

the ccCo2 network

April 2013 – Present (7 months) Denmark

The ccCo2 network was established based on a discussion with a colleague, wanting to fight the continuously increasing emission of man-made Co2. The frustration of my colleague, was that technologies targeting a reduction in the global emission of Co2 indeed is under development, however still on an immature and small scale basis. In order to bridge the technology-delay, the idea of my colleague was to plant trees absorbing Co2 as part of their growth, and in this way store Co2 while waiting for technology to mature.

The aim of the ccCo2 network is to help my colleague succeed in his quest, by initiating various ventures getting sprouting trees in the ground.

Project 1: Trees as employee-Christmas present, implemented at Siemens Wind Power in 2013.
