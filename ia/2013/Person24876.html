Cianna Stewart's Summary

Social Media Training & Campaign Development

I am deeply committed to helping people and companies bring their stories out to the people who should hear them. I use social media to extend the initial moment of contact, connecting to what really matters, creating a lasting bond.

Since the mid-1990s, I have immersed myself in Internet and mobile technologies, exploring ways to connect with people all over the world. My background in social marketing and my innate love of technology come together in my approach to social media, where I tailor the tools to the message.

Community Building

I have built online and offline communities in the fields of health, dance, politics, and business. There is a power to connecting individuals with each other that outlasts each individual contact.


For Large Brands

At Tencue, I lead trainings and design campaigns for national and international companies looking to expand/develop their online presence, create a virtual experience which parallels an in-person event, or launch an innovative social media campaign.

For Small Businesses & Individuals

As a consultant, I work with individuals and small businesses to articulate the distinctions which set them apart, and to reinforce the kind of relationships with their customers that will last. Using the wide array of social media technologies now available, it's possible for small brands to achieve significant impact at a low cost - if they use the tools well.
