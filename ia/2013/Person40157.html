Eiman Sharmin's Summary

As an author and keynote speaker, I teach persuasive skills strategies to help those in sales, the clergy, in counseling and in law how to convince and win using persuasive techniques. I take my proven experience in the art of persuasion and coach audiences how to improve their interpersonal relationships by implementing tools of persuasion to help convince a point of view without conflict. I lend expertise how to overcome fear, understand responses in others, use word imagery and recognize labels in effective persuasive arguments. Outcomes of my workshops produce more confidence in your negotiations, increase sales, grow your practice and build credibility.
