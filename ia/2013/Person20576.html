Anthony Carrabino's Summary

Digital marketing expert, web designer and experienced software developer makes his expertise available to clients through Carrabino Inc. Anthony uses open web standards blended with neuro marketing techniques to build interactive and exciting custom web experiences for desktops, tablets and mobile devices.

Anthony's background includes working as Sr. Product Manager at Microsoft where he received several awards for marketing innovation as the digital marketing lead for SQL Server. While at Microsoft, Anthony was selected to lead a special project for Bill Gates, which was highly commended at the company and in the press.

Prior to Microsoft, Anthony co-founded a start-up called Vista Software that developed two award winning software products, which continue to sell today. Anthony also served as senior vice president at Cybersage, which he helped lead to a successful acquisition by Macromedia (now Adobe).

Beyond his digital marketing and product management expertise, Anthony has mastered a wide range of technologies and tools, and has spoken at numerous conferences across North America.
