Nancy Adams' Summary

As a freelance copy writer and editor, I've built my business on one core belief, gleaned from over 30 years in the private, public, and nonprofit sectors: Effective communication is absolutely essential to the success of any business initiative. You can promote the greatest idea, offer the most amazing product, or provide the most vital service, but you won't be successful if your readers (or customers or clients) don't get the message.

From advertising, sales letters, and web content to trade articles, product catalogs, and policy manuals, I help my clients harness the power of words to clarify and strengthen their message, appeal to their audience, and achieve their business goals. This is my core service – writing and editing for results.

Specialties: Effective communication - writing and editing for results
