Charlie Jackman's Summary

Many smaller companies and non-profits are ready to move from their dated and ineffective websites to modern Web 2.0 websites, blogs and social media platforms.

With target market identification, keyword analysis, search engine optimization, training and common sense best practices, I help these companies build sites that attract visitors and convert them to members, donors and clients.

My clients hire me to plan and build the web projects that their own teams have failed at, or that they don't have the expertise for. They need someone who can provide direction, guidance, training and support as well as a great content platform, all within their budget, and they need to understand how to use what I deliver to them.
