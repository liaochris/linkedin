James Falk's Summary

I'm a principal consultant with more than 15 years experience managing and delivering process and productivity improvement projects. These include SAP implementation in finance and insurance, and installing risk and project management office processes. I am qualified and experienced in several methodologies, including 6 Sigma, Kaizen, TQM and PMI project management.
I have a background in policy research and development in Health, Transport and Treasury which drives my commitment to public sector policy reform and political advocacy.
All roles involved me in selling projects to senior managers - including Global Director level - and managing consulting teams.
