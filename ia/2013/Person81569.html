Lisa Horn's Summary

Hi! I’m Lisa Horn, a.k.a. The Publicity Gal, and I write content that matters. From press releases and media pitches to blog posts and earned media articles, I take a journalistic approach to content creation to bring out the authentic story—and kick spin to the curb.

What makes me different from the thousands of other PR pros? I spent nearly 10 years on the staff of an international trade association magazine so I know how journalists think and what they need. Now on the other side of the editorial desk, I bridge the gap and help clients get the media coverage they desire.

Think of me as a media matchmaker. I take an audience-centric approach, looking at the needs of targeted publications and then aligning the expertise of my client sources for the perfect fit. It’s a formula that generates on-target press pitches that get results—media placements.

But I do much more than kick butt on press releases and media relations. I also write, edit and manage content for websites, blogs, newsletters, emails, social networking sites, catalogs, marketing collateral, white papers and articles.

While my services can benefit corporations, small businesses and entrepreneurs alike in any market, my niche is working with suppliers, distributors and business service providers in the promotional products industry.

If you want to boost your visibility and position yourself in the marketplace through public relations and content marketing, don’t go it alone. Contact me, The Publicity Gal, at http://thepublicitygal.com and find out why content matters.
