Fredrik Kalseth's Summary

Fredrik has a deep understanding of object-oriented programming, with a special interest in best practices and processes such as test-driven development and domain-driven design. Combining such technology agnostic skills with an in-depth knowledge of the .NET platform makes him a versatile resource on any project. He has several years of experience with agile projects, and is a keen practitioner of Scrum and XP.

The last year Fredrik's primary focus has been on mobile software development, both native and web-based, for iPhone, Android and Windows Phone.

Besides his interests for patterns, practices and methodologies, Fredrik also likes to dabble in usability, user experience and design. He believes that software shouldn't just be functional, it should empower the user through an intuitive, well designed user interface.

Fredrik regularly speaks at seminars and conferences, most recently including Norwegian Developer Conference in both 2010 and 2011, the national Microsoft MSDN Live tour (spring 2011), TechDays 2011, and .NET User Group and other events across the country.
