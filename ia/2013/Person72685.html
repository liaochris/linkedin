Kate Noir's Summary

Over the past several years, I’ve worked with different design studios and fashion houses, and I’m always trying to get more experience in the field, as I eventually hope to launch my own clothing line.

I have strong research capability and an eye for detail. I love traveling and it's a pleasure for me to meet new people and expanding my social circle.

Besides designing, making technical drawings, trends researching, creating mood boards, fabric and color charts, I am also:
- Collaborating with trend forecasting office MPDClick, fashion designers, photographers, stylists
- Organizing photo shoots for fashion designers and events
- Do public relations, social networking, website and graphic design
- Fashion blogging on ModeNodeBlog.com during my free time
