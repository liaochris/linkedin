Grace Dobush's Summary

I'm a freelance editor, writer and content strategist based in Cincinnati. I'm a polymath with a wide range of interests. My editorial experience includes everything from genealogy to graphic design to politics to craft business.

My main gig at the moment is copy editing for Roll Call, the stalwart Capitol Hill newspaper. I've also done editing work for Web Designers Depot and Patrick McNeil, and I've written for publications including Wired, The Artist's Magazine, McSweeney's Internet Tendency, Venus Zine and Family Tree Magazine.

An updated and expanded version of Crafty Superstar, my business guide for part-time crafters, was released in 2012 by North Light Books. I'm the co-organizer of Crafty Supermarket, Cincinnati's most rad indie craft show, and I love speaking at conferences around the country about DIY business and entrepreneurship.
