Alyssa Middleton's Summary

I am the founder and owner of High Wire In Heels, a membership based organization designed to help mom entrepreneurs juggle business and family without sacrificing their relationships, health or sanity.

I founded HighWireinHeels.com because I found a void in services provided to mom entrepreneurs. Websites or organizations catering just to women building businesses don’t take into account the special circumstances we have as moms and the creativity it takes to nurture both our children and our entrepreneurial babies. Mom groups in my experience had a lot of crockpot recipes and craft tutorials but nothing to help grow my business.

High Wire in Heels is a place where moms can get solid, actionable business training each month along with a community of other supportive moms. Members can advertise their business and connect with other members in joint venture projects. Our digital magazine which is released quarterly provides a holistic look at a mom entrepreneurs’ life and provides tips, tools and resources to build a healthy self, family and business.

My Experience: I am a true mom entrepreneur. I did not just wake up one day and decide to provide coaching and training to other moms in business. Rather, I have owned my own spa products company, Vintage Body Spa (since 2007 and every day I am in the trenches, either making products, serving customers, marketing, securing new sales accounts or training others how to make their own bath and body products through the Bath and Body Academy.

I have 2 small children, so I am intimately aware of the delicate dance of balancing business and family. Prior to becoming an entrepreneur I was a social worker, so I have years of experience in listening to and coaching clients to solve problems and reach their goals.

I have taken the lessons learned & connections made through the years to develop an arsenal of training resources, coaching services and products to help mom entrepreneurs start or grow their businesses.
