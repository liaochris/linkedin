Melissa Skrbic-Huss' Summary

I LOVE CREATING EXPERIENCES PEOPLE WANT TO USE!

There are two things in the world that I’m passionate about: helping others and technology. Ok, I’m passionate about other things too, but if you asked anyone about me that is probably what they would say. But the one thing that I love more than anything else is being able to help people create effective user interfaces!

WHAT I CAN DO
In the beginning of my career, all I wanted to do was design online presences. However I quickly learned that to be a good designer, it’s important to under all aspects of the web. Because of this, I’ve picked up a strong skill-set in online marketing and user conversion, design and usability, and front-end development.

Over the last 8 years, I've worked in boutique agencies to global corporations and gained the experience for managing, building and developing websites from concept to launch.

MY SKILL SET
User Experience, User Conversion, User Testing, A/B Testing, Digital Identity and Marketing, Information Architecture, Desktop Application Design, Mobile Application Design, Mobile and Web Design, XHTML/CSS, Cross-Device Testing, JQuery
