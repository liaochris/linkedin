Lisa Grissom's Experience

Founder & Managing Partner

Promising Projects

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Music industry

November 2006 – Present (7 years) Midland, TX

Promising Projects works in a multitude of capacities on projects we find interesting, with promise and purpose. Much of our work has to do with incubating, supporting and promoting live music concepts.

Some of our first projects included exclusively representing a diverse roster of artists. Today, we select 2-3 artists a year to rep on a project/step basis, primarily booking Blues artists during Festival season.

We also offer talent buying services, buying talent for both venues, as well as Festivals and events.

We conceptualized and designed Tall City Blues Fest, which we also produce in-house. And we get hired to produce entertainment for other events, as well. We handle production & event logistics on behalf of other presenters, such as non-profits doing a fundraiser concert, private events, grand openings, VIP galas, etc.

We publish the West Texas Blues magazine, a quarterly publication distributed throughout the greater West Texas area.

Our art department also designs event marketing materials including posters, flyers, guides and programs, as well as mission driven collateral material, such as informational guides and brochures for non-profits.

Other services we provide include entertainment search services, consulting and event design.

Editor

West Texas Blues Magazine

January 2013 – Present (10 months) Midland, TX

Our mission is to strum the strings of connectedness that foster a sense of Community, Enthusiasm and a Zest for life!

The Magazine reaches beyond just music into other areas of adventure such as art, leisure, nightlife, travel, entertainment, food and health.

West Texas Blues is geared primarily toward a readership aged 21-65. Our readers contribute to their communities, embody the entrepreneurial spirit, and live their lives with gusto! They inspire; they create; they relish in the joys of life and they live abundantly. Our readers travel with curiosity; they purposely seek adventure; they think in possibilities. They are a diverse cross-section of people who aspire to be better tomorrow than they are today.

West Texas Blues is a complimentary, quarterly magazine distributed throughout the West Texas area the first month of every quarter.  

The distribution area includes a the larger markets of Midland, Odessa, San Angelo, the Trans-Pecos region of Alpine/Marfa/Fort Davis/Marathon/Terlingua and Southeastern NM, as well as extending to the small surrounding West Texas towns amongst this larger-market radius.

We look for distribution points in each community that are independently owned and operated.

West Texas Blues is a full-color publication trimmed to a finished size of 8.5 X 11.

We specifically seek advertisers that embody the spirit of entrepreneurship and add vibrancy to their community by making thoughtful products/services available to West Texans.

Producer

Tall City Blues Fest

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Music industry

April 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months) Midland, TX

Tall City Blues Fest is a multi-stage blues music festival that's held in late July in Midland, TX. The Festival hosts multiple stages, educational workshops, an art auction and memorabilia raffle, food vendors, and a midnight spectator jam.

Our Mission is to create an experience like none other that brings award-winning blues & roots music to West Texas.

Tall City Blues Fest is a culturally enriching, community-centric music festival that presents a family-friendly atmosphere, offers a culturally diverse attraction in West Texas, contributes positively to quality of life and creates tourism.

Go to www.tallcitybluesfest.com for more info!
