Kelly K. Serrano's Summary

Starting my own writing/editing business was the natural progression from business/health reporting. I spent 10 years as a journalist in the newspaper industry, including seven years covering business and health. I enjoy business writing as well as helping my clients grow their businesses by presenting a professional, polished image to the public wherever information about their businesses appear.

My business offers writing, editing and communications consulting because, while most businesses specialize in something, it's usually not writing about their businesses. My background in reporting allows me to bring not only writing, editing and researching expertise to my clients, but the ability to describe their businesses accurately, succinctly and - perhaps most importantly - creatively.

My best clilents are the small- to medium-sized businesses that can't afford and don't need a full-time marketing and public relations employee. We work together to get their message out in a way that will get the attention of the public as well as the press.

Specialties: Nearly 20 years' writing experience (seven in business reporting), editing, interviewing and researching with bachelor of art degree in technical journalism/news-editorial from Colorado State University.
