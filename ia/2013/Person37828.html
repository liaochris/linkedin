Drew Peneton's Summary

After several years in the military and the corporate life, it was time to pursue the passion of developing deep relationships with the individuals in my community, aiming to keep the individuals surrounding me informed and instructing them how to make good decisions for their future.

Boots To Suits is dedicated to connecting experienced US military veterans to professional job opportunities through comprehensive employment training, business engagement and policymaker education. All services are provided at no cost to the veteran. America cannot afford to forget that a great nation owes its warriors. Boots to Suits commits to helping drastically decrease the unemployment rate of the men and women who served this country.

As an Army veteran of both Operation Enduring Freedom and Operation Iraqi Freedom II, my first-hand stories have helped educate business executives and policymakers…about the experienced positive attitude veterans can bring to the world of business.

As Founder and Chief Advocate of Boots To Suits, I promote the hire and training of military veterans with transitional resources and pro-business policies that foster positive environments for separated service members in the professional workplace.
