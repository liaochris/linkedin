Anna Hoge's Summary

I live with an immense craving to make the world a more beautiful place. Therefore I do what I can to translate my passion for design and technological wizardry into helping others achieve results with their image. I love working with individuals, start-ups, small business, and large companies; basically anyone who wishes to raise their brand to a new level.

My background is in Fine Arts and Photography, and I am self-taught in the ways of graphics and web design. I can adapt my artistic sensibilities to fit any sector or subject matter, from the hip, young fashion industry, to the classic, formal government agency. My work touches just about every sector, in just about every medium, and I wouldn't have it any other way!

Although I'm a designer by day, when the 9-5 shift ends, my alter-ego goes to work. I secretly don my superhero cape and in a flash I'm running an online retail shop for entertainment memorabilia, comics, and pop-culture collectibles. I fight crime...the crime of incomplete collections and unauthentic memorabilia!
