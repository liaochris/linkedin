Jacquelyn Wieland's Summary

Jacquelyn Wieland, founder of Solutions Provided, is a group transformation expert leading high performance teams through unlocking group dynamics, opening interaction and addressing unmet needs of the business environment. Jacquelyn delivers impact and influence in healthcare, financial services and many highly regulated industries. Our connection with individuals result in a delightfully intense experience and group impact results in measurable profits.

As a dynamic, highly experienced Group Transformation Expert in the area of maximizing human potential, Jacquelyn has worked with individuals, teams and organisations to help them succeed. She has over 26 years of expertise in B2B sales, leadership and learning and development. After graduating with an MS degree in Consumer Behavioral Studies, Jacquelyn began her career in the automotive industry with American Honda Motors, Inc. She spent a portion of her early career years at Digital Equipment Corp, during which time she won the Top Saleswomen of the Year award for the South Central Area and was on their corporate Sales training team working with a sales force of 6500. Later Jacquelyn moved to London to start the soft skills division for BPP Financial Training & Consultancy. Upon returning to the USA in 1996, Jacquelyn formed Solutions Provided and has spent her time working at a fast pace, growing her entrepreneurial training consultancy business where she designed, developed and delivered learning solutions.
