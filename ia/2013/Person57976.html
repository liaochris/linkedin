Jamie Head's Summary

As an associate of LPL Financial, I have access to an outstanding team of experts committed to providing our clients with the highest-quality guidance and service available anywhere. Also, as an independent advisor and consultant, I specialize in educating public and private companies on the design of ERISA and DOL compliant benefit plans for the executive team to help reward and retain the high achievers, as well as advising retirees and the “suddenly single”.
I believe that trust—and the accountability it implies—is the cornerstone of any successful relationship and should never be compromised. The trust we build with clients is the essential element that sets us apart from other financial advisors. Trust is far more than simply feeling comfortable with your advisor, having a plan in place or setting achievable goals. It’s the ability to set daily financial worries aside with the full confidence and knowledge that someone is looking out for you and your needs.

Many of our clients simply seek a partner who always puts their best interests first, allowing them to confidently move on with the business of living their lives and/or running their business.
Our mission is to deliver customized service that addresses our clients’ unique, comprehensive financial needs. To accomplish this, we incorporate a total wealth management approach with financial planning at the core of the services we provide.
