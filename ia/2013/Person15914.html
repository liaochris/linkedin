Merisa Bowers' Summary

I am an assertive and proactive advocate for my clients who need legal representation in family law and criminal law matters. I focus on defending and protecting my clients' constitutional rights in the criminal defense arena and working with clients in divorce, dissolution, custody, and other family law disputes.
I can be reached at (614) 753-2235. My office is located at 400 S. Fifth Street, Suite 101, Columbus, OH 43215. The office has free off-street parking and is walking distance to the Franklin County Courthouses.
Many people think they can "work it out" on their own, or that they cannot afford an attorney. However, the extraordinary consequences of dealing with a legal problem without an attorney can be far-reaching.
I believe it is my obligation to respect my clients' budgets while pursuing the best possible outcome.

Realizing the need of aggressive yet affordable representation, I established my law office in 2010. I believe it is important, through the practice of law in the courts, to insist that every right be respected for every person.
I also want to combat the perception that lawyers are inaccessible and unsympathetic. In both my civil and criminal defense legal practice, I insist on treating each client, party, and opposing counsel with courtesy, respect, and promptness.

My prior experience includes working as an administrative assistant at Dinsmore & Shohl, LLP in Cincinnati, a Federal Judicial Externship in the Northern District of Ohio, and working as the litigation law clerk at Friedman & Gilbert in Cleveland, Ohio.

Note: I am not certified as a Certified Attorney Specialist. These are areas in which my practice is focused:
Criminal Defense: drug possession and trafficking, gun crimes, domestic violence, forgery/fraud/misuse of credit card, theft, child endangering, etc.
Family and Domestic Relations Law: divorce, dissolution, custody, guardianship, parent relocation, post-decree modifications, child support, etc.
