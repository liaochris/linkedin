Robert (Bob) C. Hill, Jr.'s Summary

Hill Corporate Partners, LLC (HCP) focuses on reducing real estate expenses for its clients. Through the deliberate creation of a business professional network, HCP has created opportunities for its clients to reduce fixed costs in areas other than real estate. Legal Shield is a continuation of this practice. Through Legal Shield HCP can now provide Small Businesses (100 employees or less ) the opportunity to gain access to legal and professional consulting advice at a fixed price. Small companies in particular need access to experts in fields such as legal, human resources, marketing, sales, tax/accounting, just to name a few. In addition, Legal Shield can provide employees with access to legal advice and protection at a fixed price, thus reducing distractions at work and employee turnover.

Legal Shield & Identity Theft help our clients by:

• Increasing employee productivity.
• Reducing employee absenteeism.
• Increasing employee retention.
• Reducing, or stabilizing legal cost.
• Increasing legal services access to the business, and/or employees.
• Providing credit monitoring for families.
• Providing identity "restoration" no matter how long it takes.

We are the outsourced real estate department for “end users” of real estate whether leased, or owned using:

• General office space
• IT specialized space
• Industrial and/or service center space
• Retail space

Real Estate is the second, or third largest expense for most companies. Our focus is to:

• Insure that this fixed real estate expense is appropriate for our client’s budget.
• Assist in properly aligning operating ratios.
• Create operating savings which drop directly to the bottom line.

We team with trusted professionals such as architects, attorneys, CPA's, and construction management advisors.

With the property owners paying our commission, our approach saves our clients time and worry.
