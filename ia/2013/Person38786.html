Dustin Everden's Summary

Professional with strong organizational, analytical and design skills. I have extensive education and professional experiences in Art Direction, Graphic Design as well as Art and Design of Business. I graduated from The Art Institute of Pittsburgh, where I attained my Bachelor of Science in Graphic Design. I am a Graphic Designer and Web Developer for Full Spectrum Solutions and EverLast® Lighting in Jackson, Michigan. My competencies include: Advertising, Marketing, Marketing Campaigns, Online Marketing, Brand Identity, Brand Management, Print Design, Signage/Exhibits, Illustrator, Content Editor, Computer Graphics, Photographer, Web Design and Digital Media.

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Flash, Dreamweaver (HTML & CSS), Joomla (CMS), Microsoft Word and Microsoft PowerPoint.
