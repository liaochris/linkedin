Edward W. Romero's Summary

Dr. Edward W. Romero has over 18 years of experience in academia and business, dealing primarily in higher education in areas related to administration, agriculture, agricultural literacy, agricultural careers, diversity, minority student recruitment, retention, and student development, in addition, to his experience in the financial services industry in insurance sales and investments.

Edward is currently Founder and Manager of AgForLife LLC, a startup created to help promote agricultural opportunities for young people through career awareness using innovative technology and education. Dr. Romero is also co-founder and co-organizer of the Latinos in Agriculture Leaders Forum, a meeting place to discuss the transformation of the Hispanic Agricultural workforce of the future by bringing together government, industry, higher education, and future leaders (students).

As Chief Diversity Officer and Director of Equal Opportunity at Texas A&M University-Commerce, he is responsible for the development of university objectives and strategic planning in relation to setting the vision and providing leadership, strategic direction and coordination for all aspects of diversity, affirmative action, and equal opportunity at the University. Prior to his current role, he was Director of Diversity and Inclusion for Texas A&M AgriLife Human Resources. He also held several positions within the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences at Texas A&M University. He was the founding Director of the Office of Student Diversity, and also Assistant Dean in the College. In his early professional career, he was an agricultural recruiter at New Mexico State University.

He holds a B.S. in Agricultural Business and Agricultural Economics with an emphasis in Finance and an M.A. in Agricultural and Extension Education from New Mexico State University. He also holds a Ph.D. in Agricultural Education (Leadership emphasis) from Texas A&M University.

Specialties

Agricultural Recruiting, Diversity, Strategic Planning, Visioning, and Strengths Training (Certified Strengths Advocate: Strategic, Responsibility, Deliberative, Restorative, Futuristic)
