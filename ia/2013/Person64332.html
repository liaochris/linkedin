Jayne "JJ" Gibson's Summary

As a full time professional REALTOR® educated in all aspects of real estate practice and licensed by the State of California, I adhere to a strict Code of Ethics and am committed to providing you with the highest level of service. As a member of the Board of REALTORS®, I strongly believe in continuing education to provide my clients with up-to-date real estate knowledge and superior customer service. Thus, I hold the designations of a Certified Residential Specialist® (CRS®), Seniors Real Estate Specialists® (SRES®), Quality Service Certified® (QSC), and am a Graduate,REALTOR® Institute / GRIsm.

My extensive knowledge of the market trends, schools, and communities of Los Gatos, Saratoga, Almaden Valley, Cambrian, Willow Glen, and Campbell areas of Silicon Valley provide me with the expertise in the buying and selling of real estate in these areas. I am also proud to be a top REALTOR® in the community of Rinconada Hills in Los Gatos.

I am honored to be one of the top producers in the Alain Pinel Realtors Los Gatos office, and I take pride in myself, my career, and earning my living for nearly 36 years by servicing the needs of homebuyers and sellers.

I am known for my skilled communication and compassion, market knowledge, and relocation resources.

Specialties

Full-service REALTOR®, facilitate preparing properties for sale, including staging and executing a comprehensive marketing plan, tenacious at finding a client their perfect home.
