Keith Petri's Summary

I am the SVP, Business Development at eDealya, which is a technology startup based in Israel looking to establish a foothold in the States. Its goal is to enable businesses to better understand their customers and instantly personalize a customer's experience with their product.

Prior to my work with eDealya I founded a number of businesses focused on technology and market inefficiencies.

Specialties: General: Project management, critical thinking and analysis, problem solving, public speaking, and negotiation.

Strategy: Data collection and research, demand generation, competitor analysis across verticals, strategy and operational planning.

Technology: Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe Creative Suite 5, Fireworks, Google Analytics, Facebook Insights, Bit.ly, Adwords, and familiarity with HTML, CSS, Flash, and Linux Server Administration (LAMP).
