Obi Henderson's Summary

*******www.IECevents.com*******
View Company Commercial: YouTube: http://youtu.be/yOKJds-TtfE

Innovative Events Consultation is a new rising company in Charleston, West Virginia, with a goal to empower youth through activities that build a realistic long-term outlook on achievement and success. IEC also presents real-life scenarios that help youth prepare for their lives beyond high school. Our overriding mission is to improve our community through cultural, character, and intellect-building activities

IEC strives to create safe enjoyable fun-filled activities for the community. The primary focus of IEC is creating events under three categories which are Youth Agenda, Family Fellowship, and Mature Socialization. Our niche' is Youth Leadership Development. We provide free workshops on a wide range of topics relevant to the youth. Currently, we are in the final stages of developing a mentoring program for youth between the ages of 11and 14 called “HOPE 4 Innovative Minds IMPACT Club”.

IEC supports the old African proverb “It Takes a Village to Raise a Child”. In today’s world, our youth are inundated with a myriad of problems and often lack guidance from their families. In response to these challenges, we have launched a Youth Incentives Program (YIP). YIP rewards young people who meet their academic and citizenship goals. These include tickets to social events and gift cards from our partners in the retail industry.

Motto: IEC Creating A Better Tomorrow...Through Innovative Events
