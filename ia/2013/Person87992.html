Mary Ann Singer's Summary

Synergy Consultants offers what organizations need to reach unprecedented levels of performance:

• Focused leadership.
• Increased productivity.
• Improved effectiveness.

We make this happen by focusing on the people behind the performance. Through skilled individual and team coaching (using Mary Ann’s SYNergy™ Success System for Leaders); dynamic and inspired training workshops; audience-captivating keynote addresses, and thorough, personalized performance assessments, we help organizations invest in their long-term success.

Our wide range of programs are comprehensive, flexible and customizable to meet your organization’s unique needs and business goals.

If you’re looking to:

• Create strong teams and increase collaborative relationships
• Apply new perspectives and innovative thinking
• Move beyond previous obstacles and challenges
• Grow your business and increase ROI
