Felix Desroches' Summary

I fell into design 7 years ago and haven't looked back. Self-taught designer, specializing in awesome user experiences that solve real problems (and try to be delightful in the process). Love UX strategy, interaction design, and graphic and visual design. I'm a huge fan of mobile apps, and can speak both startup and enterprise. SaaS is definitely sexy, as is making furniture and surfboards, and passion to actually change the world through better design.

Interested in smaller companies (startups!), tech and otherwise, who are looking for a Design Director. Bigger companies needn't be shy, so long as you have attitude and personality \o/

Check me out and get in touch: www.about.me/felixdesroches
