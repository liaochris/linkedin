Al Lemieux's Summary

I have a BFA in Graphic Design from Massachusetts College of Art ('94). I am a Web/UI designer/developer, graphic designer, media producer, video/audio producer, and an instructor. I am a certified Adobe Photoshop Expert.

Check out my blog: http://blog.lemieux-design.net

Specialties: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, ActionScript, Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Final Cut Pro, and After Effects. I also have expertise in making sites 508 compliant, usability focus, web standards compliant, and SEO.
