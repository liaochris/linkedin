Jessica Scanlon's Summary

My first job in marketing was for a band instrument manufacturer in the Austin, Texas area. As a marketing coordinator, I learned the ins and outs of project management, event management, and public relations. I worked in a large marketing department with several graphic designers and specialists. The breadth of experience I gained from this first job was perfect for launching my career as a marketing director for small, medium and large companies.

It's been nearly a decade since that first marketing job. Since then, I've handled admissions and development marketing for a non-profit; communication, social media and public relations for a local small business; and I directed local marketing efforts for a large global corporation.

In 2008, I started helping Austin, TX small business owners with their marketing projects as a hobby. It wasn't long before I realized that helping small business owners with my unique set of skills and experience was much more fulfilling than what my corporate life could offer me.

Hot Dog Marketing was founded on the principal that the small business owner deserves actual marketing advice and services that he/she can afford and that make an impact. We offer a wide-range of services and make it easy for the small business owner to hire Hot Dog Marketing as his/her full-service marketing manager. We currently serve over 30 Central Texas's businesses, and we continue to grow because of our loyal customers and great referrals.
