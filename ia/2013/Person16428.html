Brandon Cox's Summary

I've been a Pastor for fifteen years and am giving my life to the planting of Grace Hills Church in northwest Arkansas and to mentoring Pastors and church planters.

We began planting Grace Hills in July of 2011 and officially launched January 15, 2012. Our dream is to reach the least, the last, and the lost of northwest Arkansas with the gospel of Jesus Christ as well as multiplying our efforts by supporting other new church plants.

I also serve as Editor and Online Community Facilitator of Pastors.com on behalf of Rick Warren and Saddleback Church. It's a role that involves content oversight, writing, mentoring and encouraging Pastors, and connecting leaders together via social media.

I'm also working on my first book, Rewired, which is all about sharing the gospel in an age of rapid social and technological change.

Specialties: Pastor, preacher, teacher, church planter, mentor, coach, editor, writer, blogger, leader, communicator.
