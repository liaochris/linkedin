Matt Gawlowski's Summary

An excellent problem solver, I have over a decade of experience in working both solo and with teams to drive development from concept through to production. I have extensive experience with 3D modeling (SolidWorks, Pro/Engineer) and analysis tools (structural, thermal, and tolerances), and have worked in both small startups and larger, established corporations.

Specialties

mechanical engineering, mechanical design, project management, structural analysis, thermal analysis, tolerance analysis, SolidWorks, Pro/Engineer, Pro/Mechanica, Sheetmetal design, Plastics design, Cast metal design, drawings, large assembly management, Intralink, PDMworks
