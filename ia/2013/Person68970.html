Josh Keegan's Summary

Owner/Director of businessfix.com.au, I am able to leverage over 15 years working within a variety of both public and private organisations to remove painful costly processes saving businesses time and money. I am able to do this utilising Business Process Management and LEAN methodology.


I am a leader and mentor who is able to engage, manage and grow stakeholder relationships as well as deal directly with Change Management issues arising from process change. Within each project, I understand the overarching needs of the business to deliver resilient outcomes both on-time and under budget. As a leader and mentor, I develop high performing teams and maximise business performance gains.

Possessing a strong customer service ethos and a strong stakeholder engagement approach, I work with organisations to turn project deliverables into business benefits.

Specialties:
• Change Management
• Project Management
• Stakeholder Management
• Process mapping with BPMN 2.0 in MS Visio and ARIS
• Business and process analysis
• Six Sigma LEAN
• Leadership/Team management
