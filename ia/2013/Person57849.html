• Negotiated leasing and disposition transactions for several large-scale, multi-state portfolios, including the $15M debt reduction of a $100M corporate real estate portfolio in CA, AZ, NV and TX.

• Consulted with multiple small business owners in locating and negotiated leases for various commercial real estate applications, including industrial, office and retail ranging in size from 3,500 square feet to more than 80,000 square feet.
