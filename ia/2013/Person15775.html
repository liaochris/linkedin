João Bordalo's Summary

I work with Internet for more than 10 years. I started as a web programmer, become a systems engineer, managed several technical teams and projects as technical director and currently I'm co-owner of Excentric, a online agency.

My personal mantra is "rich are the ones who have time to do what they like", I'm a very optimistic (and lucky!) person and I'm always looking for the most efficient way to do things (others call this laziness).
