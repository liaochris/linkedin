Douglas Pilarski's Summary

Introducing Sawyer TMS, Inc
.
Sawyer TMS, Inc. has had the privilege of serving some of the best known companies in the world. We are focused on bringing the best available talent to clients in Architecture, Engineering, and the Construction industries. We do not limit the engagements we select by geography and have done business in more than 40 countries.

Our objective is to find the ideal combination of individual and firm. Our research driven sourcing strategy yields hard to find leadership talent and is the hallmark of our services.

Our clients expect to interview individuals who are visionary strategic thinkers with strong leadership skills and real expertise coupled with a natural enthusiasm for doing the job hands on and with an eye on results. It is our job to deliver the right candidate. Our research project managers focus their candidate outreach activities on the talent you are trying to attract to your organization.
