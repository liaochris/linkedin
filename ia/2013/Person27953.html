Cristy Nix's Summary

A Happy Mom makes a Happy Home & Happy Kiddos, right?

If you feel unsatisfied in your work, disconnected with your family, or are not taking great care of your self then life can feel like a constant uphill battle. Being stressed out, overwhelmed, and feeling like there is just not enough of you to go around is hard on your and your family!


At Heart Sync Coaching, I’ve helped hundreds of working women especially busy mom-entrepreneurs transform their life by integrating their life purpose so they can grow their business, Showcase their Brilliance, and be their Best Self and an even better Mom! When you connect who you are with what what you are here to do, then you can really thrive in all the facets of your unique life and NOT sacrifice your family relations. Your way. Isn't that how it is suppose to be?

Helping you feel empowered, inspired, and that you are making a difference in everything you do is our mission. It is what we do every day!

So, If you are a mom in business and ready to make a positive change starting with yourself, then I feel confident that I can help you.

Even if you feel stuck and overwhelmed, give me a call, send me an email, or text me – you just might be surprised at what we can do together!
Call me: 512.461.7019, and we will set up time to talk.

You can make a difference and change the world. You just need to start at home and I can help you do that.
