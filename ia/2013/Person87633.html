Martin Fried, MD's Summary

My goal is to have a active Physician Nutrition Specialist Practice. To help the public with nutritional healing for a full variety of conditions. It includes celiac, crohns, colitis, diabetes, weight loss and weight gain, lactose intolerance and food allergies, anemia and iron deficiency. We handle most conditions from A to Z. We practice conventional medicine, nutritional medicine, holistic, naturopathic and integrative medicine.

Specialties: I have board certification as a Physician Nutrition Specialist and over 25 years experience in treating and teaching people about nutrition. I am also a Board certified Pediatrician and a Pediatric Gastroenterologist.
