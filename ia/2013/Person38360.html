Derek Strong's Summary

Currently, I am a graduate student and research/teaching assistant in economics at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. I am developing skills and knowledge related to economic research, data analytics, econometrics, and statistical programming. I am generally critical of mainstream economic thought, preferring instead institutional and evolutionary approaches to economic analysis.

My primary interests lie in energy economics and I have previous work experience in energy management. I am interested in the economic aspects of energy efficiency, renewable energy and distributed generation, the smart grid, and microgrids. I am particularly interested in the emerging field of smart grid data analytics.
