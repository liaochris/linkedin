Heather Stewart's Summary

New Entrepreneur of the Year 2009, Heather Stewart of HS Home Staging, has introduced an entirely new recession-busting service to Realtors: Photo Staging. Heather brings her experience in property evaluation, retail merchandising and the design and manufacture of soft furnishings to the crafts of home staging and redesign. In addition, Heather is a Graduate of the Staging Diva ® Home Staging Training Program and holds a Marketing Management/Real Estate Diploma.

Home Staging Consultations and Services, Photo Staging, ReDesign, Move-In Services, Personal Decor Shopping, Seasonal Decorating
