Jeff Reilly's Summary

Jeff Reilly has held a number of positions focused on security solutions; concentrating on all issues related to standards-based Network Access Control (NAC). For nearly 20 years Jeff has been dedicated to the development, deployment, support and education of various access control technologies across dial-up, WAN, LAN, and NAC environments. Jeff is an established access control subject matter expert with significant experience and involvement at the earliest stages of access control standards such as RADIUS, 802.1X, and TNC (Trusted Network Connect); he maintains freeradius.net, a resource for users, developers and testers looking for a FreeRADIUS implementation that runs on Microsoft Windows. He is a frequent participant at industry events evangelizing and demonstrating standards-based NAC and promoting TNC interoperability and adoption. Most recently Jeff has focused his energy on the creation of tools and documentation that have proven to empower systems engineers and their partners to effectively discuss and demonstrate the Juniper Networks Unified Access Control (UAC) Solution.
