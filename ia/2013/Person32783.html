Social Media Strategist

Social Media Marketing Director

Power Tools Inc

January 2004 – January 2008 (4 years 1 month)

Directed every aspect of marketing including operational areas that affected marketing such as point of sale, staffing and training. Spend $0 on traditional marketing and instead pointed resources towards emerging social and multimedia communication channels to drive double digit sales growth. Focused on every minor detail of service/message to push the brand up market. Stressed delivering on even the smallest details such as coaching representatives to use terms like “my pleasure” instead of “no problem.” Hired and trained staff that turned customers into clients by systematically treating each sales interaction as an opportunity to build a relationship. Fostered a fun team mentality with contests and rewards for a job well done. Built positive relationships and communication channels with all stakeholders inside and outside the business, even with competitors. These relationships encouraged teamwork, win/win competition and a focus on the metrics that mattered above all else.

Marketing & Research Director

Colliers

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; FSRV; Commercial Real Estate industry

2003 – 2004 (1 year)

Implemented marketing innovations for this commercial real estate firm across a global network of hundreds of offices that allowed for ROI measurement of marketing focused on decreased spending with increased results. Assumed a leadership roll on a team rebranding all web, print and electronic communication across North America. Lead the design and placement of ads featured in publications including The Oregonian, The Portland Business Journal and The Wall Street Journal. Built a rapport with the media that directed positive attention and publicity towards the company. Linked research and marketing by working to systematically identify sales leads in consolidation, growth, relocation and renewal opportunities using historical and real time data. Managed six figure budgets and routinely negotiated vendor agreements to save money while maintaining or increasing the services delivered.

Pacific NW Sales Director

2002 – 2003 (1 year) Portland, Oregon Area

Worked with the CEO on a daily basis to direct both development priorities and the staff of researchers. Starting from zero users locally secured tens of thousands of dollars in monthly income and hundreds of active local/national users. In addition to sales, roles included managing data quality, user experience and product iteration. Worked with users to crowd source much of the data before that term/strategy was widely adopted. In this challenging position I developed and refined my ability to decide quickly, amplify/allocate scarce resources and differentiate urgent vs important.
