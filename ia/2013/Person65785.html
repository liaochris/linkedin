Joetta Talford's Summary

Joetta Irving Talford is a real estate professional servicing the needs of buyers and sellers in residential and commercial transactions. She utilizes her marketing, project management and operations skills to assist residential and commerical clients. Her expertise includes relocations, distressed properties, new home sales, and assisting buyers and sellers through their real estate processes. Licensed in North and South Carolina. Appointed to Keller Williams' Agent Leadership Council (ALC) 2008-2009, ALC Finance Committee Chair,Certified Eco Broker since 2009. Also an avid tennis player, she's held #1 state rankings in USTA Doubles and has helped her team make it to Sectionals championships in Mobile, AL in 2012.
