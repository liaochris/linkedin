Blanka Smetana's Summary

Blanka Smetana founded Office Evolution (formerly Tidy Corners) in 2010 to help families organize their homes and offices. Since September 2012, Office Evolution's main focus is on Small Office and Home Office organizing.

Blanka is a proud member of Professional Organizers in Canada (POC) and currently serves as Chair for the POC Halton-Peel Chapter.
