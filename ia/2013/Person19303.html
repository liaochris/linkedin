Cai Yu Lam's Summary

I am an Account Manager at We Are Social Singapore, a leading global social media agency that helps brands to listen to, understand, and engage in conversations across the spectrum of social channels.

We work with clients from a variety of industries to:
• build community strategies;
• identify key influencers and build lasting relationships with them;
• promote advocacy and positive word of mouth; and
• understand the wider business impact of social media and what this means for brands.

You can find out more about us at http://wearesocial.sg, where we'll be blogging about the latest news and stories on social media in Asia and around the world.

On any other day, you can find me in a café drawing, reading, or having brunch (mhmm eggs benedict) and a cafe latte. Or two. Okay fine three. You may not remember my unpronounceable Chinese name, but you will surely remember my spectacles.
