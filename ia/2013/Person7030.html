Andrew Lea's Summary

Powerful branding, innovative thought leadership, and competitive strategy have been the foundations of the marketing and lead generation programs Andrew Lea and his team mates have developed for leading technology vendors in the financial services space. Andrew is a marketing and communications professional with 24+ years of experience in marketing of enterprise technology and professional services.

Andrew serves as Sr. Marketing Manager for Linedata Lending & Leasing North America, providers of Linedata Capitalstream, a straight-through-processing originations and risk management platform for credit and leasing. Additionally, since 2004, Andrew has served on the Advisory Board of Leasing News.org.

Andrew's articles white papers, and interviews have appeared in The Wall Street Journal Compliance blog, The Journal of Equipment Lease Finance, Equipment Leasing Today, The Monitor, The World Leasing Yearbook, The Equipment Finance Journal, and World Leasing News.

Andrew holds a Bachelor's Degree from the University of California at Berkeley and a Master's Degree from the New School University in New York City. Andrew's professional background includes his experience as a technical writer as well an advertising copywriter, author of technology and marketing white papers and articles, and public speaking coach.

He may be reached at: andrew.lea@na.linedata.com.
