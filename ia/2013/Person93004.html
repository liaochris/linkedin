Michael Sheridan's Summary

Mike clerked for two Chapter 7 Trustees and also represented clients at one of the largest bankruptcy firms in MN. He has broad experience assisting individuals and businesses with debt resolution. To date, Mike has helped his clients settle or discharge over $6 million in debt. Mike was able to work with a Chapter 7 Trustee to allow his client to keep a Cesna personal airplane through bankruptcy. Mike counsels clients in real estate transactions including foreclosures, short sales and deeds in lieu with an eye towards eliminating liability and minimizing effect on credit. He also represents plaintiffs in consumer protection litigation including the fair debt collection practices act, the fair credit reporting act and foreclosure avoidance.

Specialties

Mike focuses his practice in the areas of insolvency and debt restructuring. Mike is an experienced bankruptcy attorney having honed his skill at one of the largest bankruptcy law firms in Minnesota. Mike is able to provide his clients with much more one-on-one attention than attorneys at large firms with hundreds of clients.
