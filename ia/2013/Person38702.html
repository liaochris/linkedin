Duncan Enright's Summary

Successful and experienced publishing director, who has worked across a range of STM, professional and academic markets in both editorial and marketing positions. Twenty five years of experience in strategy, product development, management and marketing in a FTSE 50 company. Lucky enough to have worked with some of the most talented people in the industry.

Specialties: bringing evidence into the workflow, professional publishing, digital publishing, book publishing, product development, people management, marketing and business strategy, corporate governance and audit
