Kathy Cacicedo's Experience

Principal

Kathy Cacicedo Photography and Design

1989 – Present (24 years)

Commercial Photographer - Director and Producer

To view online portfolios please visit
www.kcphotographer.com

As a photographer Kathy gained international attention and the 2002 NY Art Directors Club Merit Award for her historic photo of Flight 175 seconds before it's impact on the World Trade Center as well as Best Feature Photo from the Syracuse Press Club for her photo of "The Morning After - Sept 12." Her WTC photos were published in books and magazines around the world in over 11 countries.

As a designer she won the 2002 Bronze Award for Website Design from the Art Directors Club of New Jersey for the website of latin music group CTO.

Principal

BuenaOnda Pictures

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Motion Pictures and Film industry

2003 – Present (10 years)

BuenaOnda Pictures is an award winning NJ based film/video company founded by photographer Kathy Cacicedo in 2003.

We have completed - music videos - short films - corporate video projects and a documentary project which involved teaching filmmaking to "at risk" kids.

From producers to crew, we work only with creative friends, all who share our philosophy "hard work,loyalty, passion and you can't go wrong."

Awards:
*Music video "Prendele la Mecha" was a 2 time Best Music Video film festival award winner.
* Kathy Cacicedo was honored with the first annual "Shooting Star" award from the Garden State Film Festival.
* "VAN VORST PARK" starring Frank Vincent...
WINNER "Best Homegrown Short" Garden State Film Festival,
WINNER "Best Suporting Actor Frank Vincent - NJ International Film Festival,
OFFICIAL SELECTION : Beverly Hills Film Festival, Whistler Film Festival, BC Atlantic Film Festival, Halifax, Nova Scotia, Big Apple Film Festival

Manager

Los Mamboteros

2003 – Present (10 years)

Manage USA bookings - marketing and promotion of Los Mamboteros, a Latino Urbano group from Guatemala. www.losmamboteros.com
