      Work one-on-one with inmates to guide them through a program called "Decisions For Living" that help the inmates to understand and improve their decision making process. "Decisions for Living" help individuals make sound decisions using a logical 5-step process. In addition to identifying the external facts of a situation, inmates learn to identify other variables that influence decision making: feelings, thoughts, attitudes, self-images, habits, values, and past experiences. Vita staff and volunteers help transform those with issues related to impulsivity, low-self image, and poor decision making abilities into self-determining, controlled, socially and emotionally responsible men and women.
