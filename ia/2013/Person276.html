  • 7Install Marketplace: The Hottest Tool for Software Advertisers & Publishers

    • Software Publisher - Advertisers
    • January 14, 2013
    Authors: Rene Louis Rasmussen

    7Install Marketplace offers software advertisers and publishers with a unique strategy to market their products to existing users. It’s basically a marketplace combined with a technology platform, whose goal is to introduce existing software users to new software products which they might be interested in. The twist is that the ads appear while the users are in the process of downloading a software. Isn’t that as enticing an ad as any?

    Wilmington, Delaware USA (January 9, 2013) – In today’s highly competitive Information Technology and business industry, software manufacturers and publishers find themselves in stiff competition with programmers and other software developers for an audience. Let’s take antivirus software companies as an example. Although they might be offering a free version of their software for online users to download, they would still compete with other antivirus software brands for an audience.

    1. An online user installs the developer’s software.
    2. An offer from a software advertiser will appear for the user to see.
    3. The user can either accept or decline the offer.
    4. Once the user accepts, the advertiser’s software is installed.
    5. The software publisher gets paid.


    Combining a Unique marketplace with Technology to Introduce Users to New Software Products


    What does this mean for the software advertiser and publisher? Both will benefit in terms of increased revenue! For software advertisers, 7Install gives them access to new, exclusive markets and an ultra-targeted customer base. For software publishers, you really cannot go wrong in signing up for the program because it simply is one of the best out there.

    Whether you’re a software publisher or advertiser, there are a few more features from 7Install’s Marketplace that you can take advantage of including:

    1. Cloud-based installation.
    2. High-quality publishing.
    3. Post-installation conversion tracking.
    4. Scalability.
