Cynthia Chin-Lee's Summary

Passionate about communication, written and oral. Manager of global technical writing groups, working with writers and engineers in Asia, Europe, and North America.

Worked in rapidly changing global environments with multiple projects, using a variety of tools, including Camtasia, oXygen, FrameMaker, Arbortext Editor, HTML, and XML. Worked and trained teams in China, India, and the Czech Republic.

In addition to technical writing, I've written 7 trade books, one book on social networking for adults (It's Who You Know) and 6 picture books for children, including the best-selling "Amelia to Zora: 26 Women Who Changed the World," and "Operation Marriage."(reachandteach.com/operationmarriage)

Specialties: Technical publications, tools transitions, XML, structured writing, topic-based writing, localization, training, writer management
