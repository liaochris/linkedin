Dr. Syb Bennett, Ph.D.'s Summary

Dr. Syb is a Vanderbilt educated, Harvard trained, two-time Emmy award winning television news veteran and Google Glass Explorer. With nearly 20 years of professional experience, a terminal degree and her foray into the new media world, she is in an ideal position to help lead, guide and advise others about the media industry as well as facilitating change. She helped host the first national Media Bloggers Association Conference at Belmont University called BlogNashville. Most recently, she was the Program Chair for the National Association of Black Journalists. Her duties included: programming for the annual convention held in Philadelphia in 2011 drawing more than 3,000 attendees. For years, she managed the Scripps Howard High School Journalism Program at Belmont and she was on the Online News Association Conference Planning Committee as the Chair of the Student Newsroom in 2008. She has the accumulated wisdom from a number of fellowships through the Poynter Institute, the American Press Institute and the Associated Press Media Editors. Further, she is a certified manager through the Management Development Program at Harvard University. She was also a Fellow in the Scripps Howard Academic Leadership Program held at Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge. In addition to Scripps Howard, she has secured and managed grants awarded by the Gannett and Ethics and Excellence Foundations. Dr. Syb was the founding Executive Director for the New Century Journalism Program at Belmont and is a former Visiting Faculty at the Freedom Forum Diversity Institute in Nashville, TN. She is also an alum of Leadership Nashville, Class of 2006. Her presentations include: future trends in the virtual world, Internet Safety for teens, Embracing the Multimedia Mindset, A Framework for Innovation and other tailored talks. She is the author of the Color-Full Alphabet Book.

Specialties

Facilitating Change, Multimedia Strategist & Visionary, On-Air Anchor/Reporter/Talk Show Host/Keynote Speaker, Facilitator, Consultant, Blogger, Producer, Writer, Author and more.
