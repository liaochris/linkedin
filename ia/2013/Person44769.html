Saul Fineman's Summary

I pride myself in networking many of my friends and associates together. I truly enjoy meeting new people and seeing how we can all help each other out. When you deal with as many companies as I have, you get the inside scoop on who's looking for what and what the next big thing is.

So whether you are looking for a job, some new contacts, or just want to talk, I would love to hear from you.

On the business side, I've been a graphic designer/creative strategist for 25+ years working across a large gamut of industries and companies. You name it, I've done it.

On the personal side, I've been married for 29 years to an exquisite woman who is also my business partner/soul mate and we have 2 grown children.

Lately I have come full circle and am getting back into playing music (vocals and keyboards). I was out on the road as a musician in my youth.

P.S. Oh, by the way, if you're into science fiction, get in touch. I could go on for hours.
