Flavio Maria Rossini's Publications

  • THE TELEPATHIC BLUE LITTLE ROCKET

    • TBD
    • January 23, 2013
    Authors: Flavio Maria Rossini

    A fairy-tale about a rocket "toy" that lives in a carrousel in an amusement park. He has telepathic abilities and talk to children with his mind (no adults!). He loves the children, but also longs to be a "real" rocket, to play up in space among the stars, with other real rocket friends… Story created in 1994 inspired by my first daughter Tatiana (who was 2 years old at the time), and soon to be published (2013) in the web / mobile web.

  • THE CURIOUS WATER DROPLET

    • TBD
    • February 11, 2013
    Authors: Flavio Maria Rossini

    A teaching tale about a curious water droplet that is born on a lake, then wanders around the planet to meet other droplets by the sea and by the snow mountains. On her journey, she meets many new friends, and learns a lot of new things. A nice way to learn about the water cycle... Story created in 1996 inspired by my first daughter Tatiana (who was 4 years old at that time), and soon to be published (2013) in te web / mobile web.

  • THE ONIRIC SPACE

    • Universal Art Datamedia
    • December 12, 1995
    Authors: Flavio Maria Rossini, Nelson Coelho Nascimento, Fabio Golfetti, Luiz Zico Rocha Soares

    http://www.trivision.com.br/onirice.htm Artistic computer generated video animations, with original music, composed by Nelson Nascimento, Fabio Golfetti, Luiz Zico Rocha Soares & Flavio Maria Rossini

  • Assembly Z80 - Linguagem de Máquina (TK)

    • Editora Moderna
    • March 14, 1983
    Authors: Flavio Maria Rossini

    First book in Portuguese language about the Assembly Language for Microprocessors, dealing with the then popular Z80, 8 bit processor, from Zilog, that had an equivalent and upgraded set of instructions from the 8080 / 8085 from Intel. This book sold over 75.000 copies. http://www.datacassete.com.br/livros/detail/Linguagem-de-Maquina-para-o-TK/59.html

  • Basic TK - Sinclair (Volume 1, 2 e 3)

    • Editora Moderna
    • February 16, 1984
    Authors: Flavio Maria Rossini, Pierluigi Piazzi

    A 3 volume publication in Portuguese, explaining the BASIC programming language for computers, using as a teaching and working tool the cheap and popular Sinclair computer, based on the Z80 microprocessor, that was launched in Brazil with the brand TK. This collection sold over 90.000 books.
