Kenny Atcheson's Summary

Hello...Kenny here, Founder of Creative Profit Pros, LLC - a Marketing and Advertising Consulting Company. We help local businesses use direct response marketing that pays for itself. Let me get right to the question people ask me the most, "How did you get into marketing?"

I started marketing products and services online. As my knowledge and success grew I received phone calls from business owners asking me for tips on internet marketing, copywriting, blogging, Local SEO, and more. They offered to pay me to teach them how to do the same thing and a consulting business was born. My company has earned a list of clients that range from just under $100,000 in gross revenue to tens of millions of dollars per year.

I personally have always had an entrepreneurial spirit and an interest in marketing as you will read below...

As a young kid I lived in a modest environment (government housing) in Las Vegas. I sometimes visit the area just to remind me of what you can escape with hard work and determination.

We later moved to a home in Henderson but there were still things other kids had that I wanted. It was not a surprise that a young boy mowed lawns but Dad was impressed when I made flyers by drawing my own logo and naming my thriving business "Handy Kid." We did not have a computer nor the money to make copies so I had to hand-draw the flyers and put them in mailboxes. Yes I said "in" mailboxes and that is illegal, but the Statute of Limitations protects me now (I hope.) It was about 30 years ago.

Years later during a visit to Smart-N-Final with my Mom, I saw boxes of candy bars for sale. Quick math revealed that candy bars were 25 cents a piece when purchased in bulk. I purchased a few boxes, stacked them in my locker, and made a 100% profit selling them to other students.

My desire to teach, design, and implement marketing strategies is as strong as ever and I am involved in the entire process when my company accepts a new client.
