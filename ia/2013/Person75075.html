Kevin Mihm's Summary

Kevin Mihm has been recognized in Pittsburgh as Coldwell Banker’s #1 Sales Agent and Previews Property Specialist bringing home winning results for his clients since 1998! The National Association of Realtors has ranked Kevin among the Top 100 Salespeople Nationwide! A consistent Top Producer for over 29 years with annual sales averaging over $50 Million. A long time resident of Pittsburgh North, Kevin is widely recognized as a trusted leader in his field. Kevin and his team are committed to providing outstanding service.

Specialties: Full Time – Full Service Professional Realtor Representation, ePro, Relocation Specialist, Marketing Specialist, New Construction, Existing Home Sales, Townhomes, Condominiums and Buyer Agency Representation in the Pittsburgh PA North areas of Wexford, McCandless, Franklin Park, Bradford Woods, Marshall Township, Pine Township, Cranberry Township, Mars, Adams Township, Allison Park, Hampton Township, Shaler, Ross Township, O'Hara, Fox Chapel, Sewickley, Allegheny and Butler Counties.
