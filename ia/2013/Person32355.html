  • Champlain College Housing Registration WebApp

    • January 2013 to May 2013
    Team Members: David Watkins

    As a Champlain College capstone project, this housing registration web application is a working proof-of-concept for what would allow students to research on-campus living opportunities with details regarding location, room sizes, roommate possibilities, and more. It would allow residential administrators to easily enter descriptions and upload images and floor plans for each hall.

  • Champlain College Shuttle Tracker for Android

    • March 2011 to 2013
    Team Members: David Watkins

    The Champlain College Shuttle Tracker displays locations of shuttles and buses at Champlain College that are responsible for transportation between campus, Spinner Place, Quarry Hill, and the Gilbane/Lakeside parking lots. It is an unofficial, mobile, and on-the-go alternative to the desktop web tracker, available at shuttle.champlain.edu.
