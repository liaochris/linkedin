Camille Engel's Summary

Camille Engel is a Contemporary Realist Oil Painter who pursues the aims of the Realist movement with remarkable passion and skill.

Engel’s art career has been on a steady rise. Her work has been accepted among some of the world’s top realists and into prestigious museum tours and art exhibitions from Santa Fe to New York City, winning accolades, such as the People’s Choice award at Gilcrease Museum’s 2008 American Art in Miniature exhibition.

Engel’s work hangs in corporate, public and private collections.

Accepting both small and large scale commissions, Engel works with the highest quality oil pigments on the highest quality archival support. Please discuss any questions you may have with the artist.

Reliv International

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; RELV; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

1988 – 1991 (3 years)

Redesigned and executed all packaging and marketing materials.

Before Reliv came to us, they worked with another designer who gave their vitamin marketing materials a natural, organic appearance. We were told the company failed even though the product is outstanding.

They came to us at Camille Engel Advertising, Inc. through a satisfied client recommendation. We recognized that their focus needed to be on their sales staff and designed colorful, upbeat, lively, creative marketing materials and packaging (the style and colors are still in use today).

We were told that Reliv transitioned during their first year with our new packaging to a $5 million-dollar company.
