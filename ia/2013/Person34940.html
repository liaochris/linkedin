Dimitris Fotiou's Summary

Born in Greece in 1970
Lives and works in Athens, Greece

Symposiums - Exhibitions: Greece, USA, UK, France, Italy, Netherlands, Austria, Germany, Ireland, Turkey, many online.

Dimitris Fotiou engaged in projects which focus on concepts such as communication, information, language. He processes his works by using the internet as a medium and / or sculpture. His works are either on the web or in the real space.

He has participated in many group exhibitions such as CyberArts 2005, Prix Ars Electronica 2005, WB07 - Web Biennial 2007 - Biennial on the World Wide Web and he has done two solo shows.

For more information about his work, pictures and videos, visit www.fotiou.net
