My responsibilities have included planning meetings, outreach, event promotion, and print, radio, and video press interviews. I've volunteered time by editing vector maps of the proposed off-leash areas, walking streets and greeting people, and posting fliers.

As a spokesperson for the group, I have made presentations to the regional business association and other groups, drafted language of support adopted by area neighborhood associations, written letters and communicated with local council members and park commissioners, worked with park planning staff, and collaborated on formal project proposals and presentation materials.
