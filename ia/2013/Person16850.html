Brennan Anderson's Summary

Brennan is a video game and film composer, with a passion for unique storytelling. A piece he wrote, called Chair Love, was written for and featured as the score for the short film, Chair Love, which was featured at SXSW. On April 20, 2011 and April 26, 2012, Brennan had his work performed by the Austin Symphony Orchestra as part of the first and second annual Young Composers Competition and Concert. In early 2011, Brennan started working with Toby Turner, a YouTube personality with over 4,000,000 subscribers, writing music for his shorts, as well as re-orchestrating trailer music for use in his parodies. Recently he has finished his music production studies at Pyramind, and is pursuing a career in game audio.
