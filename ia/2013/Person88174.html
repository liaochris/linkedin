MARYNÊS PEREIRA's Summary

I was a fitness instructor during 20 years and it was a great time, giving amazing classes in big health clubs such as Runner and Cia Athletica in São Paulo city. In the beginning of my career, I was a fitness studio's owner and instructor of fitness and dance classes. I traveled a lot because I had the responsability of leader of a dance group. I had been teaching for universities in Physical Education courses (Uni Ibirapuera and Uni ABC) until I opened my own business. As a sales manager at Body Systems Latin America, I learned a lot with Les Mills International and I was introduced to many people like Phillip Mills, who was my mentor during this time and will always be in my heart. I have taken a graduate course at univesity - Marketing and Human Resources - to improve my career and myself and I also had a great experience with holistics themes and Physical Education with Edson Claro and learned a lot about Lowen, Therese Bertherart, Gaiarsa, do- in, shiatsu, Reich and so on.

I worked for a big Health Club in Brasil named Bio Ritmo running Marketing, Human Resources (Human Sigma, Gallup estrategies), new developing business (Franchising) and Training sessions to all leaders from 2006 to 2009.

I am a strategic consultant, Speaker ( Leadership,Career and Sales) and Business Coach. I wrote 3 books : How To Manage Health Clubs, 2005 (sales, human resources and marketing) Careers 2008 ,How to improve yours and Leader Premium 4x results 2013.

I have been developing a method based on Neuroscience and Quantum Physics to Leadership,Careers and Sales.

During this last 10 years I have been working not only to Fitness Industry but also to big Companies as Itau,Bradesco,Volkswagen as Speaker about Leadership ,Sales and Career



Especializações: Marketing, Sales, Human Resources, Fitness Management, Holistics themes ,Business Coaching,Leadership,Career, Neuroscience,Quantum Physics
