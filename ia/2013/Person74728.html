Kevin Francis' Summary

Personal profile

I spent my early years as an officer in the British Army developing the finer arts of leadership, people management, personnel development, technical strategy and planning in the IT / telecommunications area. After arriving in NZ 18 years ago, I have been involved in a variety of industries, including telecommunications, internet, IT, Online / Digital Marketing and currently the travel media industry.

The corporate experience has been rewarding with a variety of increasingly senior management roles, culminating in Executive Management roles for Asia Online, Ericsson Enterprise and First Rate and most recently COO / CEO roles at Australian listed digital agency Q Group (ASX:QXQ) and CEO at Jasons Travel Media (NZX: JTM).


I am often described as the "make it happen" person, who consistently out performs my peers. My speciality is maximising a commercial result through a mix of leadership, excellent people management and a knack for numbers that Zintel CFO Alf Wallis described as "the most numerate GM he'd ever come across".

Leading organisations through periods of change has been a key success, often repeated. I thrive on the challenges and rewards that are the mainstay of the most senior manager's role and I enjoy the variety and intensity that those challenges often represent. Integrity, determination, humour, perspective and passion are traits that I apply whatever I am engaged in.

CEO

Jasons Travel Media

Public Company; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry

September 2011 – May 2013 (1 year 9 months) Newmarket, Auckland

No other travel publisher has the coverage, range or depth of tourism information as Jasons Travel Media. The Jasons database is the largest in the tourism industry and contains over 30,000 detailed tourism operator listings, thousands of travel articles and hundreds of maps.

Our website, www.jasons.co.nz, is the region's most comprehensive and one of its most active travel sites. It is consistently rated as one of the top 3 travel websites in New Zealand and one of the Top 20 in Australia (Source: Nielsen NetRatings). With extensive online booking for both accommodation and activities, the site is not only a fantastic travel information resource but also a one stop shop for any travellers, domestic or international.

Jasons print and distribute over 5.7 million copies of 77 FREE print directories and guides annually and delivered online information, with over 5 million visitors during the same period. The company's monthly newsletter, Jasons Explore! reaches a customer database of over 100,000. Jasons can also be found on Twitter and Facebook.

Jasons Distribution Network is one of the industry's most extensive, servicing over 4800 outlets, many with the company's own dedicated display stands. Jasons also operates VisitorPoint, the largest New Zealand-wide third party brochure distribution service - distributing millions of brochures for more than 1000+ tourism companies via a network of 2800+ displays across New Zealand.

Incorporated as a family business in 1967, Jason Publishing Co Ltd was restructured as Jasons Travel Media Ltd in 2000 to reflect the changing nature of its core business activity. In June 2005, Jasons listed on NZAX. Jasons has transformed itself from a traditional travel publisher to a leading edge travel and tourism 'infomediary'. Operating across Australia, New Zealand and the South Pacific, Jasons delivers accurate, relevant and timely multimedia information to enable travel and tourism buyers and sellers to meet and transact.
