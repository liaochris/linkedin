David Oransky's Summary

A prudent investment philosophy, sophisticated tax strategies, and thoughtful planning are essential to long-term financial success. An investment strategy should be based on sound academic principles and thoroughly supported by historical evidence. Tax implications should be considered in every decision with an effort to maximize the investor’s return after all taxes and fees. Values and goals should guide the financial planning process to align one’s financial assets with the rest of their life.
