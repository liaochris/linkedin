Michelle Bruno's Summary

As a former supplier and conference planner/trade show manager, I see the technology and evolution of the live-event industry through a unique lens. I chronicle change through articles in event-industry publications, event-tech company blogs and my own blog at ForkintheRoadBlog.com.
