Dawn Haynes' Summary

Dawn Haynes is a Principal Trainer and Consultant for PerfTestPlus, and former Director and current member of the Association for Software Testing. As a highly regarded trainer of software testers, Dawn blends experience and humor to provide testers of all levels with tools and techniques to help them generate new approaches to common and complex software testing problems. In addition to training, Dawn is particularly passionate about improving the state of testing across the industry and works toward that goal by collaborating with small groups of testers, attending peer conferences, participating in online discussions, writing articles, and speaking at testing conferences worldwide. She has more than 28 years of experience supporting, administering, developing and testing software and hardware systems, from small business operations to large corporate enterprises. Dawn holds a BSBA in MIS with a minor in programming from Northeastern University.

Specialties

Teaching Software Testing to Practitioners, Developing Training Materials, Performance Testing Software Systems
