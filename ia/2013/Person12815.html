Ben Cotton's Summary

Ben is a Content Marketing Manager, award-winning blogger and occasional event speaker who currently works for Indeed - the #1 job site worldwide.

In this role he is responsible for creating content to help support Indeed's business goals.

He previously worked for Edelman Digital where his work helped inform communications strategy for a variety of blue chip clients.

Ben is a Google Analytics qualified, digital strategist with vast experience helping brands listen, understand and engage online. This takes the form of online conversation analysis, community management, influencer engagement and online crisis and issues management.

Ben was formerly an Associate of the Centre for Public Relations Studies at Leeds Metropolitan University. The Centre is a PR think-tank that undertakes international research, training and consultancy. His work has been acknowledged in Planning and Managing Public Relations Campaigns and the Carnegie Official History of Yorkshire County Cricket Club.

Ben is interested in social media, WOM, SEO, social media analytics, network and communication theory and, generally how different generations communicate.

Ben writes for Social Web Thing - his award-winning blog looking at communications, social business and 'big data' in the validation era. The blog has been up for several awards; winning a European award at Euprera Spring Symposium and received nominations for the some comms and CRAPPs.

Specialties: Ben has previously worked in a busy press office, a boutique agency, a PR think-tank, a famous sports club and the world's largest PR agency. He has a wide range of PR experience encompassing digital, corporate, consumer, sport, education, food and B2B. Ben is also an experienced author of successful industry award entries.
