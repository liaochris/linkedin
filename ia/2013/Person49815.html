Grant Griffiths' Summary

I have been blogging since February 2005. Since that time I have used a blog to promote and market a professional service firm. Shortly after I started that blog, I started a blog called Home Office Lawyer which has been the start of many changes in my professional life.

In September of 2007, we started the Home Office Warrior as I wanted to gain an audience of not just lawyers and the home office experience, but all of those who work from a home office. It was also around that time we started the blog, Blog for Profit.

Blogging has allowed me to now take a totally different focus and we are now building a business called G2WebMedia where we design WordPress websites and blogs.

Recently, we launched Headway Themes which is an exciting framework/visual editor you can use to create and build a blog, blogsite or website on WordPress.

Specialties

Extensive experience in blogging and using it to promote and market a small business, home business and/or a professional service firm..
