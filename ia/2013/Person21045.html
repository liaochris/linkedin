Cathy Fahrman's Summary

Cathy Fahrman is a Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW), a designation that affirms she is one of the industry's leading experts in the development of strategic resumes. Since 1991, CPRWs have demonstrated their knowledge, talent and writing expertise in meeting the industry standard of excellence and serving the client's career interests.

Cathy has been a member of the Professional Association of Resume Writers (PARW) for more than 15 years, as well as a member of Career Masters Institute (CMI) for over 10 years. Membership in these prestigious industry associations demonstrates an on-going commitment to continuing education, a broad network of global professionals, and opportunities to implement emerging technologies to assist clients achieve their personal career goals.
