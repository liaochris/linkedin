Mark Lindwall's Summary

I help companies develop and execute radically new top line growth acceleration strategies to meet the strategic business challenges of a changed economy. I have deep experience as an entrepreneur, executive coach, facilitator,sales leader,sales manager, and top performing salesperson. My current role is as a collaborative thought leader and guide in sales organization performance improvement.

At Forrester Research, I'm passionately working to help clients develop new approaches to sales system planning, operational execution, sales team talent acquisition, acceleration of hire to productivity (AKA on-boarding), and improving the development and productivity of salespeople. I engage with client leaders in ongoing coaching (via our Leadership Council), cross-functional working session facilitation, brief inquiry discussions, and written findings and blogs.

My former corporate experience includes senior leadership, sales management, and sales roles at companies including McDonnell Douglas, BT, MCI Telecommunications, and META Group.

Expertise: ■ Business Development ■ Change Management ■ Executive Coaching ■ Facilitation ■ Team Development ■ Leadership Development ■ Negotiation ■ P&L Management ■ Sales Leadership ■ Sales Management ■ Strategic Planning and Execution ■ Strategic and Tactical Sales Process ■ Training and reinforcement
