James Ferguson's Summary

My goal is to align myself with a small to mid-size Tier 1 or Tier 2 automotive supplier that can benefit from my leadership, subject matter knowledge and 25+ years management experience -- resolving challenges and taking the company to the next level of profitability.

As a born leader and expert in the OEM aluminum wheel industry, I bring the ability to quickly lead companies to success. After identifying the get-it-done people and building strong teams, together we cut through the red tape, implement cost-effective solutions and foster continuous companywide improvements.

I am a loyal, tireless leader – one who does whatever it takes to get the job done. With a strong sales and marketing background, my communication skills are welcome at all levels of the organization. People like working for me.

For a startup, stalled or plateaued automotive supplier company, bringing in an executive level manager, CEO, COO or VP such as myself gives you access to proven talent. Creating and maintaining a profitable bottom line is one of my key management strengths.

Executive management experience includes:
> Serving as CEO of Borbet Alabama, grew the manufacturing-based business 50% in one year-- turning the bottom line from red to black within five months.
> As Sr VP Global Sales, leading mfg from one to nine plants -- largest producer of cast aluminum wheels.
> P&L responsibility for overseeing the production of alloy suspension components.
> Served on two Global JV BOD's
> Cutting waste and building efficiencies through implementing lean and 6 Sigma principles.
> Traveling internationally – fostering my appreciation of all ethnic groups and cultures.
> Commitment to high integrity and professionalism.

I am inspired and passionate about making a positive difference every day I come to work.

In my free time I enjoy traveling, exercising and coaching water polo at AU, as well as mentoring individuals both at work and in club organizations.
