Mackensie Smith's Summary

"We're all stories in the end."

This simple statement has served me well as a metaphor that each story is personal, no matter the level of intrigue. There are characters that enter and leave, but the connections form a base for a magnificent plot that is truly unlike anything ever written prior.

The most beautiful thing about this story? It's quite possible the most wonderful "choose your own adventure." You are both the author and the protagonist.

This concept has led me to follow my career passions in the art of communication. Clear communication drives creativity and new ideas to advance this funny thing we call life. A focus on public relations is a natural fit for my vivacious, enterprising and outgoing personality. Mix in an affinity for journalism, quality networking and endless idea sparks and you have a storyteller waiting to share that next great tale. I have a love of learning that has led me through a number of business experiences, with a healthy mix of mistakes and successes along the way.

I don't believe we ever truly "grow-up," but rather "grow-in" to our own characters. Keep learning, keep maximizing opportunities and embarking with a perpetual zeal for the story.
