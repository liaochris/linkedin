Lisa Montanaro's Summary

Through my work as a Productivity Consultant, Success Coach, Business Strategist, Speaker & Author, I help people live successful and passionate lives, and enjoy productive and profitable businesses and careers. I offer coaching, consulting, speaking and training designed to help people and organizations achieve success. By assisting clients with focusing on the actions that are truly important and will make the biggest impact, clients find themselves taking steps which turn into habits that lead to success.

I coach and consult with individuals in one-on-one and group formats, as well as with corporations and organizations, to help each individual and each organization find balance, success and joy at home, in business, and in life. My online learning platform, LMG University, provides courses globally to improve people’s lives, businesses and careers.

I offer speaking programs on a broad range of topics, including organizing and productivity, business and entrepreneurship, leadership, career transitions, branding, and life-work balance. All of my speaking programs can be customized to meet an organization's specific needs, and can be offered in the form of live keynotes and workshops, online webinars, and teleclasses. I am honored to be a member of the National Speakers Association (NSA), the premiere organization for professional speakers.

I publish a free e-zine, Create Your Purpose-Live Your Passion, for success-minded individuals. When you subscribe, you will get my valuable digital toolkit, Achieve Success with Purpose, Passion & Productivity. Visit http://www.LisaMontanaro.com/toolkit to claim your free toolkit. My book, The Ultimate Life Organizer, was published by Peter Pauper Press in May 2011. For details, visit http://TheUltimateLifeOrganizer.com.

Owner & CEO

Lisa Montanaro Global Enterprises, LLC

2002 – Present (11 years) Davis, CA but serving the globe

Drawing upon my experience as a performer, educator, mediator, and lawyer, I founded LM Organizing Solutions, LLC® in 2002, and then expanded my brand to Lisa Montanaro Global Enterprises, LLC in 2012 to fully capture the holistic approach my work takes in improving personal and professional development at all levels.

I have had the privilege of helping thousands of people live successful and passionate lives, and enjoy productive and profitable businesses and careers through coaching, consulting, training, speaking and writing about my breakthrough methods for personal fulfillment and professional success.

My online learning platform, LMG University, provides programs globally to help people improve their businesses, careers and lives.

I am honored to be an inaugural Certified Professional Organizer®, a member of the National Association of Professional Organizers (NAPO) and a member of the National Speakers Association (NSA).

A sought-after speaker, I have presented professionally to audiences throughout the United States, have been interviewed by television and radio hosts, and am a frequent presenter and guest expert for teleclasses and webinars.

My practical programs, enthusiasm, humor and energy inspire participants to take action and implement new techniques designed to improve their personal and professional effectiveness. My work has been featured in the media, and my written content has been widely published online and in print. I am the author of The Ultimate Life Organizer: An Interactive Guide to a Simpler, Less Stressful & More Organized Life, published by Peter Pauper Press.
