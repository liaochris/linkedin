Lee Thomas' Summary

"Most good decisions come from experience, and most valuable experience comes from bad decisions."

I view business as a Great Game - a very serious game that has players, strategies, rules, boundaries, basic principles and measurements. Attracting the right players, having the right "game plan", making the right game decisions, overcoming the competition and enjoying the rewards of success are all a part of the Great Game of Business!

I am a skilled, experienced, Business Coach/Advisor that can assist you to take your business to support the lifestyle you want and deserve. I do this in a very unique and cost effective manner.

*I specialize in helping people IN business become "unstuck" and more successful in their business.

*I also help people in career transition determine if they should go into business, and what the "RIGHT" business might be!

* With almost 40 years as a professional and a multiple business owner, I know how to self prepare, acquire, run, and exit businesses. I have been "in the trenches", made the mistakes, paid "my dues", and can enhance your business success through my experience.

*I am also on a mission to help people get into the RIGHT business. To get into the WRONG business, can be an economic, emotional, and psychological NIGHTMARE. I know, because I have been there.

Contact me to be sure you are on the right PATH to your business SUCCESS.

Specialties: Small Business Coach/Advisor, Franchise Business Consultant, Serial Entrepreneur
