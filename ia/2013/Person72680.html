Kate Naito's Summary

I am a highly experienced ESL and English tutor with a passion for the English language and a love of teaching. My "specialty" is teaching advanced-level adults who are looking for a challenging and personalized way to learn English.
I treat each tutoring client as an individual, identifying the client's specific interests and language goals. Then, I carefully plan each lesson to help the client reach his/her goals as quickly as possible.
I have helped clients improve their English in many areas: casual conversation, business conversation, academic writing, pronunciation, grammar, vocabulary, and more. I also address cultural issues that many foreign adults must face.
I am a native American English speaker with no regional accent.
Visit www.eslnewyork.net for more information.
