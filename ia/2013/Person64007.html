Jimm Fox's Summary

I’ve led media projects for the last twenty-five years. Video, multimedia, interactive, rich media - the technology and delivery mechanisms have changed but the keys to success have not - tell a good story, make it engaging and communicate from your customer’s perspective.

I have been lucky to work, live and travel internationally throughout my career. One Market Media exists to help companies communicate their unique value in an engaging and persuasive manner to customers around the world.
