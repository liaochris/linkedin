Marsh Scott's Summary

Marsh creates stainless steel sculptures by commission for corporate, medical, hospitality and residential clients. She also creates installations and site-specific work for public art projects.

Marsh is currently creating abstract paintings using fresco as a media.

Specialties: Fresco, encaustic, oil and mixed media abstract paintings.
Stainless steel theme based sculptures. Suspended, free-standing, and wall mounted installations.
