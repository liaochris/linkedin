Ellen Silverman's Summary

Accomplished Information Technology professional with over 15 years experience in business analysis and technical writing for financial services. Product knowledge of equities, fixed income and derivatives. Excellent knowledge of trading and market data systems. Strong understanding of content lifecycle relating to risk, regulatory and audit requirements. Ability to navigate dynamic, global and multi-cultural environments.
Expertise includes writing functional requirements, system requirements documents and use cases, technical writing of operational support manuals, policies and procedures, quick reference guides, white papers and presentations, analyses of current state and future state workflows, processes and gap analyses, development of QA test case documentation, familiar with SharePoint security concepts and taxonomy.
