John Beck's Summary

Nov 2008 - Finalist in San Jose Business Journal's CFO of the Year Award - CFO for Hire division.
Highly experienced finance and accounting professional delivering results over 29 years in industries including hardware, software, semi-conductors, medical devices and construction manufacturing. CPA with “Big 4” foundation providing highly ethical leadership, operational process improvements, and streamlined administration to organizations ranging from a $50 million public company to matrix managed reporting strategic business units to early stage venture funded and bootstrapped startups.
