Erin Jourdan's Summary

10+ years of online and offline marketing experience with a focus on SEO and Social Media Campaigns. Strengths include: hardworking with an eye for detail in project management, excellent written and oral communication skills, extensive experience working with large corporations and small businesses, passionate about targeting brand voice in social media campaigns, broad technology skills and a self-motivated adapter of new software and programs.

In my spare time (yeah, that is nights, early mornings and weekends) I have been using my MFA in Creative Writing as a teacher. Mostly it has involved teaching writing classes focused on the creative imagination. I focus on writing from personal experience and am not interested in "getting your work published" but instead, using writing as a therapeutic modality---a way to learn and see your own life. I wrote an ebook called "Stunt Writing 101". I also use my creative writing education to teach writing to children at the nonprofit 826 Los Angeles. Fun!

Specialties: teaching, writing, strategy, marketing and all marcomm materials, managing pr companies, press releases, sales materials, websites from inception to launch, imovie.

I just finished taking Illustrator 1 and Photoshop 1at UCLA extension and am currently taking Web Design 1.
