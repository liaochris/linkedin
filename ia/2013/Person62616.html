Jeroen van Bree's Summary

I support managers in their tasks of organizational design and strategy development. For this I draw on my more than fifteen years of consulting experience, my research skills, as well as my broad interests.

A subject that has had my particular interest in the past few years is video games, specifically the potential of game design to enrich organizations. I have developed a more open, active and ultimately more effective approach for organizational design and strategy development called Game Based Organization Design, for which I was recently awarded a PhD at Nyenrode Business Universiteit.

Specialties: video games, game design, gamification, organizational design, strategy development, game based organization design
