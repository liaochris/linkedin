Peter -Coach Pete- D'Arruda, RFC's Summary

ARE YOU SEARCHING FOR SECURITY & FINANCIAL INDEPENDENCE?

For over 21 years I have utilized strategies to educate my clients to achieve their financial goals and lifetime dreams. Cash flow is what determines the amount of freedom you have. Having a future cash flow plan will reduce the fear of you ever having to say "If only..." in our uncertain financial future.

By using money protection strategies from market and tax loss, I am able to help my clients realize their financial dreams sooner, by finally being in charge of their personal cash-flow.

The past 7 years, I have hosted a nationally syndicated radio show, THE FINANCIAL SAFARI, now on over 100 stations nationally. This year, we have added THE FINANCIAL SAFARI TV SHOW and well as a show with CPA's and Tax Coaches called TAX GUYS RADIO.

Specialties: Reduce fear of the money world.
Protect & preserve family assets.

Tax reduction principles & strategies.
