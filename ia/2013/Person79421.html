LC Galleries' Summary

Born and educated in Northern California Steven Lipary attended Diablo Valley College where he earned his Associate in Arts degree. He furthered his education by attending California State University of Sacramento where he graduated with a Bachelors degree in Business Communication and a minor in Business Management. Lipary has garnered the respect given to a longtime collector and dealer as seen by the numerous accolades he receives in the field of authentic autographed sports memorabilia and collectibles.

As a court appointed expert by the State of California Mr. Lipary is able to provide individuals, the courts, auctions, charities, insurance companies, and corporations Certified Appraisals of their collections and court testimony when needed.

Lipary started his business in 1980. Lipary Collectibles has grown from a small town business with one location to multiple locations doing business all over the world. As owner of LC Galleries and Lipary Collectibles Lipary continues on his quest to provide collectors and dealers alike with the most knowledgeable and highest quality customer service.

Specialties

Limited Edition Fine Artworks & Authentic Autographed Sports Memorabilia / Collectibles. We Arrange Player Signings & Appearances for all Events. Appraisals & Authentication by Court Approved Expert.
