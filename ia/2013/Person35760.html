Dipak Bastakoti's Summary

Dipak worked as Trek Guide in the Himalayas of Nepal for more than 10 years. He then started to guide in the mystic and artistic cities of Kathmandu, Bhaktapur.

Dipak then worked in Singapore for 2 years as outbound tour executive where his duties were to organize tour to India, Tibet, Bhutan, Srilanka and Nepal along with ticketing worldwide.

Currently, Dipak is a Director of a Company named DJ's Tourism Services based in Kathmandu Nepal. The company operates and organized various Trek and Tour Programs in the Himalayas.

For more www.djstourism.com

Everest Base Camp Trek, Everest View Trek, Gokyo Lake Trek, Annapurna Round Trek, Annapurna Base Camp Trek, Ghorepani Ghanrdruk Trek, Langtang Trek, Gosainkunda Trek, Manasalu Trek , Gorkha Trek are some of the major trek programs by the company.
