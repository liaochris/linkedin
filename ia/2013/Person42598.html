Erik Hauser's Summary

My experience is vast, and the breadth and the depth of my work can't be fully explained on a 1d webpage. I HIGHLY recommend calling me to discuss - humanize your process

I'm a globally recognized thought leader in all things experiential, understanding all aspects of consumers' behavior & the consumers' journey in our "new Digital Reality". I use these key insights to optimize the consumers' pathway to purchase.

I specialize in experiential strategy, experiential methodology,new media & new technologies.

My thinking assists brands to stretch their thinking so far ahead that the curve isn’t even visible in their rear view mirror.

I'm a firm believer that a brand is a summation of their experiences they provide consumers. There are various modalities of experience, but each is integral in understanding the consumer/brand relationship.

After running my extremely successful boutique shop, Swivel Media, in San Francisco, I moved on to be the VP, Executive Creative Director for HAVAS. Currently, I'm focusing on epicenteRx and helping brands internationally.

I created the social media home of Experiential Methodology - (http://experientialforum.com) in 2004. It's within this community that I began to lead the way in teaching true, media-agnostic experiential methodology worldwide. Today the EMF has tens of thousands of members.

I believe to properly service clients in contemporary culture an agency must have one foot planted in the digital world and the other planted in the physical world. This strategy ensures that brands deliver multi-mediated experiences where and when the audiences will be most receptive to receiving and retaining the brand experiences.

SPEND YOUR CLIENT'S MONEY WHERE THEIR INTENDED AUDIENCES SPEND THEIR TIME

I'm an international speaker, writer, blogger, media commentator on all things experiential, digital and I'm looking for a new opportunity
