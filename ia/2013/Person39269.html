Edward Boches' Summary

As one of Mullen's four original partners I helped define the agency’s creative standards, established its public relations group, integrated digital design and production into all of the agency’s operations, and launched its growing social influence practice.

In 2010, in order to help the agency develop even more innovative ideas and to influence its ongoing transformation, I gave up my long-held position as chief creative officer and become chief innovation officer. Now I focus on emerging technologies, social platforms and changing consumer habits.

In 2012, after 30 years at Mullen, I decided to work part-time at the agency and join the faculty at Boston University's College of Communications. As a Professor of the Practice I now teach advertising, creativity, digital strategy and social media.

Since there are 24 hours in the day, I also serve on the board of directors for Springpad (where I was interim CMO for the first half of 2012), speak frequently on industry and consumer trends; and write an Ad Age 150 blog called Creativity_Unbound.

You can find me on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Springpad and most other online platforms as edwardboches.
