Christina McDonald's Summary

Christina has been trading options in both the US and Australian markets since 2004. She specialises in income strategies and shares her expertise by providing options training and mentoring programs primarily for stock investors and fund managers who wish to learn how to manage risk and generate consistent income from their stock portfolios in all market conditions.

Christina was a senior organisational change management consultant with IBM, assisting large organisations move through Business Transformation and large-scale IT Systems implementation. Christina has authored training materials and trained many users on various aspects of information technology and helped clients set up e-learning systems.

Christina has also been responsible for the strategic direction of web site content to provide the greatest business value for an international organisation. In the earlier part of her career, she has designed and programmed various business applications, and implemented packaged-based solutions for a number of large corporations. She has worked in Malaysia, Singapore, Hong Kong, USA and Australia for companies such as Accenture and IBM.
