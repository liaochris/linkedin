Brad Murray's Summary

Brad Murray is a senior developer and system architect with extensive experience in the internet and traditional systems world. His greatest expertise is with the Microsoft platform from development tools to databases, but he has also worked with Oracle back-ends.

Brad can spec out object-oriented applications and the systems necessary to run them in the production environment. One of his strengths is in doing the extra analysis and design up-front to make sure that the application will be able to grow into as many potential future directions as possible with minimal cost increases.

Brad has a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Pennsylvania and has been working exclusively in the technology sector over 20 years.

Specialties: Hardware: Server architecture, SAN/NAS, load balancing
Software: SQL, VB, C#, dotNET, IIS, XML, HTML, Javascript, AJAX, CSS
