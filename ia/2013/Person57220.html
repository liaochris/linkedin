James Abron's Experience

Principal

Domestic Mediation LLC

November 2009 – Present (3 years 9 months) Livonia, Michigan

Domestic Mediation LLC specializes in family related cases. Our principal, James Abron has mediated private divorce cases, post divorce disputes and domestic PPO's. He understands the damaging effects of divorce, having witnessed the effects on his own family.

He is a qualified State Court Administrative Office Domestic Relations mediator under Michigan Court Rule 3.216. Mr. Abron previously worked as a pro bono mediator for the Wayne County Friend of the Court. He is currently a Motion Day mediator in the 7th Judicial Circuit Family Court in Flint, Michigan.

Principal

AbronADR Services

Partnership; Myself Only; Alternative Dispute Resolution industry

2010 – Present (3 years) Livonia, Michigan

AbronADR Services is a full service conflict resolution firm. We specialize in labor, workplace, engineering, architecture, environmental, land use, and construction related cases due to the unique background of our principal. Mr. Abron has gained extensive experience on general civil and commercial cases while mediating cases in both private practice and the of Michigan. Mr. Abron has been accepted as a construction expert witness in several county courts in California.
