Laurel Papworth's Summary

Specialties: workshops/education/training on virtual economies, monetizing social media, building and managing online communities, 5 steps of a social media campaign, 9 steps of a social media strategy. How do deal with Negative Criticism online, Social Media for Government. Risk Management of social media. ROI and COI of social media. Monitoring and Measuring social media. Working with Key Influencers. The Do's and Don'ts of Social Media.

Laurel was named by Forbes Magazine in the Top 50 Social Media Influencers globally, and Marketing Magazine named her "Head of Industry, Social Media" for Australia. Author of Social Media Revenue, Laurel has consulted globally including with Singapore Government and Middle East Broadcasting, and in Australia, with Westpac, Sony, local, State and Federal Government. Laurel is the CEO of The Community Crew managing massive gaming and online communities including Facebook and forums for reality TV shows such as Junior Masterchef. Since 2005, Laurel has been lecturing on online community management at the University of Sydney, AFTRS, UWS etc and she has been managing virtual communities since 1989. Laurel’s blog laurelpapworth.com has been named by AdAge in the Power150 media blogs worldwide and she is in the top 10 bloggers for Australia as well as100 Most Powerful Women on Twitter.
