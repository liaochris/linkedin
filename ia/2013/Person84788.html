Mara Hoover's Summary

Mara Hoover is on a mission to empower her clients to take control of their finances today. Through her workshops, seminars, and books she urges clients to gain financial literacy, confront their fears, eliminate the guilt and move forward with a plan that works. Since the age of five, Mara has been balancing a checkbook, calculating compound interest and investing in her future. While getting her degree at Pepperdine University she spent a year in Buenos Aires where this “gringa” mastered Spanish in “rapida” fashion.

Spending 7 years in the male dominated construction industry she not only learned how to curse like a drunken sailor, more importantly she learned the power of organizing, networking, planning and taking control. By the time she was 23 she had saved over $80,000. Of course, when the 2008 recession demolished the construction industry, her savings and her pride Mara was seeking a safe harbor. Resonating with the message and the methods of Five Rings Financial she joined as a Financial rep and in two short years she jumped to become the youngest Executive Vice President, she grew her business not just based upon exams or specific accreditation but upon the common bond of her clients looking for a safe and protected harbor of understanding. Today she manages over 122 accounts.

Having discovered the secret of success for her six year marriage Mara wrote the comical book My Grumpy Husband. She also wrote the more serious book Anchor Your Plan: A Six Step Guide to Smooth Financial Sailing. As an advocate of goal setting in all walks of life Mara has been to 5 of the 7 continents including Antarctica.

Consult with Mara about how you can achieve everything on your bucket list through creating your financial plan!

My Grumpy Husband

January 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months)

My Grumpy Husband is a book series in progress. The theory of the first book (released October 2011) is based on the concept that men are very simple creatures and assumes that often times women think they are more complicated than they really are. Men truly only need a few things to keep them content with their lives; however, if one of those things get out of balance THERE IS A BIG PROBLEM. Your seemingly mature, composed, grown-up husband is quickly converted into a GRUMPY HUSBAND. I believe that most of these basics apply to all men, not just husbands, but to ex-husbands, boyfriends, teenagers, etc…

… As wives, women, girlfriends, and even mothers, we have seen this breakdown occur with our men over and over again. However, there has never been a unified effort by women to identify the reasons why, what I like to call the “Grumpy Trigger Points.” This book is an effort to identify the basics, compile them into one source, and share them with women around the world.
