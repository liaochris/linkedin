Faisal Butt's Summary

I inject entrepreneurialism, drive, and global perspective into the ventures I launch or invest in. In a 13-year career trajectory, I have lived and worked on three different continents. After a decade of globe-trotting, in 2009 I established a base in London, and am now actively looking for high-growth ventures to invest in.

Highlights include:

• serial entrepreneur: In partnership with acclaimed entrepreneur James Caan, I invest in and work with founders/management teams to scale up high-growth real estate ventures.

• investment savvy: In the past 24 months, I have personally invested in and launched five new ventures in the UK.

• international business leader: As a founding Executive Director at TiE, I launched and built a forum of high impact entrepreneurs in South Asia.

• value driven and socially conscious: In 2008, the University of Oxford awarded me the Skoll Scholarship, a highly sought after scholarship awarded annually to 5 promising “social entrepreneurs” out of 400+ applicants.

Specialties: My area of expertise is starting, developing, and growing start-ups and early-stage SMEs.

Extracurricular activities include:

In 2013, I became a columnist for Business Matters, the UK's leading SME business magazine. I write a column every month on entrepreneurship, venture capital, innovation, and challenges that we entrepreneurs face in starting and scaling up our businesses.
