Your Personal Brand DNA® program is for those interested in making better career choices to set them up for success by being their best self.

As a certified performance analyst (only 25 other people certified worldwide), I use and interpret powerful tools developed with nobel peace prize nominated research and technology to help people understand how they make choices and in turn predict their performance with precision.

Equipped with this knowledge and my guidance, people are able to make better role and work environment choices as well as understand the key drivers to how they generate value by simply being their best selves at work.

Your Personal Brand DNA® program outcomes include:
• a detailed profile of job/roles and work environments that play to your true strengths and mitigate or neutralize your blockers that will help you perform at your best naturally
• an awareness of whether or not you will be most successful in a managerial or leadership role so you can chart your career road map to set you up for success
• the ability to quickly and accurately make choices about how well you'll fit in any job/role or work environment
• the ability to proactively pursue roles, projects and organizations in which you'll naturally thrive
• the ability to stop wasting time pursuing and working in jobs/roles and projects in which you have to work hard to perform - and which can sabotage your career advancement
• an understanding of the key drivers of your performance and personal brand which you can use to position yourself for best fit roles and environments
