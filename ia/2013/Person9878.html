Arsham Mirshah's Summary

Arsham Mirshah is a co-founder and Technical Director of Marketing at WebMechanix. Arsham has been on the cutting-edge of marketing technology for more than 5 years, and has established a name for himself regionally as a thought-leader & visionary.

His specialties include technical strategy, web analytics, A/B & multivariate testing, infrastructure, systems design & development, and high-level technical project management.
