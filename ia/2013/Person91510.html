Meredith Marlow's Summary

Meredith Marlow has been described as the next big thing in Interior Design. Her interiors have received acclaim and numerous industry accolades including both a SOPHIE and SANDI award. The spaces she designs are sophisticated, refined and unique. They are both softly modern and classically clean lined. She states, "We are in the business of beautiful spaces and pride ourselves on being able to interpret the unique style of each of our clients. Customer experience is always number one. We see ourselves as advocates for the client as we guide them through the process of creating beautiful and functional spaces." It is this approach that has earned Meredith both the trust of her clients and a reputation for being at the top of her field.
