Jeroen Tamsma's Projects

  • PPS (PPP) tender ‘Hart van Zuid’, Rotterdam

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jeroen Tamsma

    The Municipality of Rotterdam runs a large, complex and innovative tender for the development of the Zuidplein and Ahoy Event Venue area. The project includes both urban development for 20 years and a number of high-end real estate projects.
    As a member of the Tender Team of Municipality I contribute to the extensive tender dialogue with the competing contractors. Furthermore I focus on optimizing the internal processes of the special project department of Municipality related to the tender dialogue.

  • International Criminal Court, The Hague

    • March 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jeroen Tamsma

    Since leaving Brink Groep I continued my tasks as senior project manager for all external processes on behalf of Brink Groep and ICC. Amongst these tasks are:
    • Leading the permit procedures, including guidance of neighbourhood stakeholders
    • Negotiating a long-term contract for an Aquifer Thermal Energy Storage (ATES) with Eneco
    • Aligning the plans for public space by the Municipality of The Hague and the ICC project

  • Quality audit Headquarters Ministry of Defence, The Hague

    • May 2012 to January 2013
    Team Members: Jeroen Tamsma

    Towards the end of the renovation project of the Headquarters in The Hague the Government Buildings Agency assigned Laanbroek Schoeman adviseurs (LSa) and Valstar Simonis for a quality audit. I led the team on behalf of LSa. During the months leading to the official hand-over the (interim) results of the construction work were monitored. Providing constructive feedback in time to avoid difficulties in a later stage was key. This audit was not just assessing the final result but helped to make it a success.

  • Tender consultancy for TBI

    • March 2012 to April 2013
    Team Members: Jeroen Tamsma

    For tenders of different combinations of TBI construction companies I joined the tender team to add more client-focus to the production of proposal documents. Healthy discussions balancing construction knowledge, cost aspects and assumed client needs led to inspiring deliverables to accompany the proposal.
