Julian Bryant's Summary

Hey! Thanks for stopping by. So I'll tell you about myself...

I'm an iOS developer. I like exploring how mobile devices and technology can change the way we work and play. I usually have at least one app I'm working on personally (quikstats.net) as well as any work I'm doing for clients.

I volunteer a lot of my time with Startup Weekend. I really enjoy the idea that people can pursue their dreams through entrepreneurship and spending time helping build entrepreneurial communities and turning people on to the idea of shaping their own futures has been a fun experience.

I also went to law school and became a lawyer (CA Bar). I think it is a great skill-set to have although it only took me a brief stint as a corporate lawyer to figure out spending my days reading & writing contracts was not for me.

I also like to travel. I spent about 7 years living in Japan and am pretty advance Japanese speaker. I also spent some time living in Mexico, Spain, (intermediate Spanish skills) and Thailand as well.
