Kiley Dorton's Summary

Director of Product Development at ACT Bridge, responsible for the vision and development of our enterprise, cloud-based applications and technologies.

Employee #3 at the startup, joining the team in 2011. Prior to joining ACT Bridge, studied as a graduate assistant at the New Media Institute at the University of Georgia. Earned my undergraduate degree in cognitive science with a certification in new media.

• 2+ years work experience with agile & lean product development
• 3+ major software development projects designing and architecting applications
• 12+ website development & design projects completed, from concept to launch
• 17 years serving as resident tech help for all my friends, family, and random people in Starbucks

Favorite work process: meet with customers, identify a need, dream up a cool solution, facilitate a lean product requirements session, build user stories and wireframes, define technical requirements, build a quick-and-dirty prototype to prove the concept, gather feedback from real customers, iterate on the product until we reach a minimally viable product, release it, gather feedback and study usage, iterate until we've successfully solved the problem.

I've got a passion for discovering creative ways to use technology to improve people’s lives. Outside the office, I love to spend time with my wife and daughter and sing/beatbox in a semi-professional a cappella group in Atlanta.
