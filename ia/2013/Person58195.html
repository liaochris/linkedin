Jane Langille's Summary

Health affects everyone, but what does your content say to readers looking for relevant, accurate information?

Writing about health, wellness and active living is my passion. Whether I am writing stories about health news and medical innovations to captivate a general reader or crafting compelling content for a business manager, I know how to put the reader first and tell your story — on target and on time.

Drawing on my unique blend of journalism skills, consumer marketing experience and business degree, I have a keen appreciation for the difference between evidence-based claims and spin. My client list includes Fortune 500 companies, Canada’s largest national newspaper, Canada’s largest single-site hospital and academic sciences centre, health care organizations, health care brands and consumer and trade publications.

My health care and business clients appreciate that I have branding, marketing strategy and consumer research from their side of the desk, drawing on my years of experience championing brands for Procter & Gamble and Christie Brown & Co. My journalism clients appreciate my ability to simply and accurately communicate complex health and medical concepts in stories that captivate their readers.

I consider myself fortunate to have interviewed over 150 doctors, researchers, scientists and health experts about a wide range of health news and medical innovation topics including: new drugs, genetics, global health, biotechnology, medical devices, fitness, nutrition, obesity, diabetes, cancer, heart health and more.

Let’s talk! Contact me to discuss your next custom content project at jane@janelangille.com.

Specialties: Extensive experience writing evidence-based stories about health news and medical innovations for consumer magazines and websites. Advertorials, articles, feature stories, website copy, business reports, case studies, blog posts, newsletters.
