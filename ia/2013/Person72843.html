Kathleen E Lipscomb's Summary

Kathy is currently focusing her consulting activities on healthcare advocacy; utilizing her extensive health care industry experience to assist patients and families navigate through the complicated healthcare system:
*Reviewing and explaining coverage options and personal financial responsibilities to patients and their families
*Actively review, coordinate, and organize client medical bills and related paperwork, prescription and medical provider information
*Guide clients through the assessment of post-hospitalization and rehab options, including evaluating home care, assisted living, and nursing home alternatives
*As an unbiased third party, helping eliminate some of the family stresses of dealing with financial and family issues: caring for elderly family members, families geographically distant with an ailing relative, families caring for children with special needs.
The best time to make decisions about your personal healthcare and your loved ones is when it is not an emergency situation. Having the necessary information documented and available in advance will save time and stress for all involved.

Kathy had previously provided executive operations management expertise to corporate leaders of small to mid-sized businesses; for-profit, not-for-profit, and government regulated organizations. The focus of her consulting had been on developing and implementing organizational efficiencies and formalizing internal business practices.

Specialties: Review and analysis of medical benefit programs, Organize and Coordinate billing and related documentation, Communication, Planning, Budget development
