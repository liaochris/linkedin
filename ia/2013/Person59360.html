Beyond Off Grid is a documentary film that explores why we should strive to reduce our dependency on the modern control grid, and how this can be accomplished. The modern economy and way of living, with all of its conveniences and comforts, is not resilient to calamity, and not conducive to spiritual blessings. True freedom is found by seeking the old paths.
