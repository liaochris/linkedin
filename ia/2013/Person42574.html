Erik Elken's Summary

Erik is a Sports Anchor/Reporter at KEZI 9 News in Eugene, OR. While at KEZI, Erik also did play-by-play on ROOT Sports, calling Pac-12 baseball. Prior to KEZI, Erik worked for NBC at the Vancouver Olympic games, ESPN, several regional sports networks, plus local radio and television stations.

Erik specializes in sports television and radio play-by-play, hosting, anchoring, producing, and reporting. He is a graduate of the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University with degrees in Broadcast Journalism and Sport Management.

You can learn more about Erik at his personal website: www.erikelken.com
