Dee Vance's Experience

Co-owner

Carefree Moves

May 2009 – Present (4 years 5 months) San Francisco Bay Area

Moving doesn't have to be stressful. CAREFREE MOVES has a staff of over a dozen dedicated women who are professional packers/organizers and most of whom have been with the company since it was established in 1995. A team of two or three women come to your home and, if needed, help you get organized before the move with what to keep, toss, donate or consign. They then professionally pack all of your possessions with meticulous care. We pack up the existing residence, leave the house empty or stage it, unpack your boxes with care, and organize your new home's kitchen and closets. We even place the furniture, hang the pictures, and...remove and recycle the boxes and paper.

We are a full service company with 17 years experience in the Bay Area.

owner

One Mother to Another Life Coaching

January 2000 – May 2010 (10 years 5 months)

LIFE COACHING FOR MOMS

Do you feel stuck? Being stuck is not knowing how to get moving - you have no direction, no action plan - you just stay in the same place, as if you are sinking slowly into quicksand. Many moms feel this way. What usually keeps you stuck is fear – fear of not knowing how to get unstuck.

You already have your own built-in wisdom and insight...my job is to help you rediscover it and unleash it. Life coaching is about your present and your future and helps you find the steps you need to guide you forward toward achieving greater balance, fulfillment, happiness, and personal growth. It is less about where you've been, and more about where you want to go.

Life coaching does not give you all the answers…what it does do is ask the right questions to help you find your own answers. Then it helps you take action. Coaching is about you creating the life you want to live!
