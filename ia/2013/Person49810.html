Grant Keller's Summary

I help small to medium businesses grow through web technologies, and help designers and agencies turn their ideas into realities.

For the business owner that needs a site that looks nice and gets the results they need, I can be a one-stop shop or an addition to their team. By working backwards from your goals for your website (and other online properties), we can then build a great-looking and easy-to-use site to get you there.

-- Website Design
-- WordPress Development
-- Custom forms/info gathering systems (for customers or internal)
-- Email Marketing
-- E-commerce/Digital Products
-- Site Security/Virus removal
-- Consulting for overall strategy or individual problems


For the graphic designer or agency, I can help you make that great design actually work. Why waste your time and energy messing around with WordPress and code, when you could be doing more of what you love: creating? WP and the web are continually evolving, and it can be hard to keep up with current best practices. When working with me, that's no longer an issue. And if your project could use an extra boost in the strategy and marketing areas too, I'd be glad to help out.


Previously, I've worked in video post-production as an editor, motion graphics artist and colorist. I'm always up for discussing new projects!
