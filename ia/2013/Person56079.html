Dana Toland's Summary

High-energy, entrepreneurial, creative executive with a passion and proven track record of developing and executing high-level corporate events that build brand awareness and market share for clients. One of ITEX's specialties is to help our clients identify, negotiate and contract with the ideal meeting venue for their program at no or little cost to them.

We also specialize in helping clients evaluate their existing program goals and procedures to ensure that they are utilizing the best tools/practices while maximizing their spend to realize the best ROI.

Specialties: Fifteen plus years of business-to-business marketing experience with a concentration in creating and executing high-level meeting and events with proven ROI.
