Laura Morask's Summary

Criminal Defense attorney in private practice with twenty five years of prior experience as a trial prosecutor, with experience in litigation, DNA and forensic analysis, as well as motion practice and appellate work. Practice is focused on trial litigation in the areas of criminal law, DUI trials, summary suspension hearings, and Professionals with matters before Administrative Boards. I am passionate about your representation. I was formerly a prosecutor with over a hundred and fifty felony jury trials in Cook County. In my new Law Practice I have dedicated myself to the same high standards of a personalized and dedicated approach to whatever your legal problem or need may be. As a twice elected trustee of five communities, I also have experience in all levels of local government.

Specialties: *Criminal Defense, Jury trial litigation, Dui, traffic and misdemanor defense. Accessible personalized service as a private attorney with free consultation. Experience in litigation, gang crimes, DNA and other forensic analysis. I have also served as a guest commentator for Fox News, WGN news in Gang crimes and DNA analysis.
