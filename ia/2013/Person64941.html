Joanne Owens' Summary

SOLARWIND MARKETING
Joanne Owens is the founder of SolarWind Marketing & Design, LLC, a marketing and graphics design company that provides creative solutions to professional services firms and businesses who want to build client loyalty and develop new business.

MORRIS JAMES LLP
Joanne currently serves as the Marketing Director for Morris James LLP, a leading Delaware law firm.

INDUSTRIES
Other industries being served by SolarWind include law firms, financial services, consulting firms, non-profits, construction, food services and other small to mid-size businesses.

BACKGROUND/EXPERIENCE
