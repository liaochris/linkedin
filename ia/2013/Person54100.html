Husna Alikhan's Summary

Husna Alikhan is the founding principal of Alikhan Law Office, LLC, a law firm practicing immigration law, estate planning, family law and consumer bankruptcy. The practice has grown exponentially and we expect to have further growth within the next year. We are also expanding our internet presence through web based marketing.

Attorney Alikhan is licensed to practice law in the State of Nevada, the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, and in the U.S. District Court for the District of Nevada. She is also licensed to practice law in the Province of Ontario in Canada through the Law Society of Upper Canada.

Specialties

Immigration Law, Family Law and Domestic Relations, Consumer Bankruptcy and Estate Planning through Family Limited Partnerships and Living Trusts
