Mark Laporta's Summary

If you can’t find the words to motivate your audience, you may be on to something. After all, motivation isn’t about words. It’s about crafting a message that rings true—simply because it delivers real value.

Think you need a writer for that? I disagree.

You need a day dreamer, a humanist, a creative able to paint the big picture from revelatory facts, hard truths and simple pleasures.

Not because it’s clever. Not because it’s sexy, sassy, witty, gritty or coked up on its own brand of phony down-home wisdom. But because your message is worth the attention of people as savvy and wise and committed as you.

Strategy? Research? Statistics? They’ll drive you all the way to the address, but what will you say when your customer answers the door?

Call me at 917-617-3560. Together, we can get behind the words to craft a sustainable, credible message that motivates your audience to make your brand an integral part of their lives.
