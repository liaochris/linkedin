Aaron Koper's Summary

Graphic Equlizer Productions started in 2000 as just a recording engineering company.
By 2001 - 2002, the company developed and branched off not just as an Indie recording label, but as a booking agency and promotions and marketing agency, as well. Today we book bands from inside Michigan, outside of Michigan and around the world. We are currently working on so many projects. You can see updates on our MySpace page and other gorilla marked website.
Currently, we are working on a major project with wide range of local, national and international bands, artists and talent world wide that are known and unknown world wide.

We are expanding daily. If interested, ask how you can get on board with this awesome opportunity to work with Graphic Equlizer Productions.

Specialties: Booking, Promotions, Recording/Sound Enginering, Tour Management, Band Management, Press Pack Making Services and more.

We also part of the MusicXray Team and also DJ/Host on Thursday's 9pm - 11pm EST on www.rawradiox.com
