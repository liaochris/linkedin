Traci Bisson's Summary

Traci Bisson is the founder of The Mom Entrepreneur and owner/founder of Bisson Barcelona, LLC. She has over 15 years experience in marketing and public relations, and has worked with individuals and companies in all stages of growth.

Traci earned her Bachelor of Science degree from the University of New Hampshire.

Traci is a popular blogger who advocates for family life in the state of NH through her blog, Enjoying Life with Kids in NH, which highlights the reasons why families with young children should consider making New Hampshire their home. Her blog, Neighbors Helping Neighbors, talks about the different programs that are available for residents who want to help in these tough economic times but are not sure how.

As a published writer, Traci has written articles on blogging, book publishing and promotion, how to get free publicity, marketing, business planning, writing news releases and other topics. Her articles have appeared in many publications including NH Mirror, Country Business Magazine, Xlibris (NH Writer’s Project Newsletter), Portsmouth Herald Newspaper, ProSales Magazine, Women’s Business Boston Journal, NH Business Review, The Value Proposition Newsletter, Business Beat, Business Network International Newsletter and Arrington’s Bed & Breakfast Journal.

Through her various positions as the Marketing Manager/Director of international companies, Traci has interfaced with people around the world from many different cultures. Her travels have taken her to Taiwan, China, Hong Kong, Puerto Rico, Canada and throughout the United States.

Traci is dedicated to her family and her work, and spends her days living life to its fullest. Her two beautiful sons and very supportive husband bring balance to her life.
