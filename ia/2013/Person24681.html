Chris Wakefield's Summary

I have been a full time professional artist since January of 2001. I am represented by several galleries mostly in the state of Alaska and am looking to be represented by more galleries nationwide and perhaps internationally.

I am also the host of a live radio show "Pop Culture Radio". It celebrates contemporary popular culture in America. I interview musicians, artists, comedians, writers, actors, etc. live on the air about their work.

Specialties

I specialize in contemporary art, watercolor, multimedia, acrylic, one shot and oil. I do mural work both indoor and out. I would also be interested in doing illustrations for hire.
