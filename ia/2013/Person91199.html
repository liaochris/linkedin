Melissa Banuchi, ATC's Summary

Whether it’s through coaching, consulting, or a combination of the two, Melissa has enjoyed partnering with individuals, teams and organizations to bring about positive change. Keeping things simple and precise, making acute observations and developing meaningful and lasting relationships are just a few of the skills she’s appreciated for. Melissa quickly establishes trust and credibility through a relentless commitment to excellence and delivering measurable value in every aspect of her work.

Her background includes key leadership positions in operations, sales and marketing; and after extensive training, she has earned her ATC as an executive coach. Melissa served on the Board of Directors of Shattuck-St. Mary’s Schools, and was the Chair of their Advisory Committee. She has outstanding facilitation and communication skills and has been the keynote speaker at a number of conferences. When not working or spending time with her family, she can be found applying her creativity to personal projects or immersed in research trying to satisfy her insatiable curiosity.

Specialties

Focusing my experience, skills and passion on leaders, their teams and their business reaching the state of being best.
