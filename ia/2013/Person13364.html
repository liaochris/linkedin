Founder and CEO

Grand Slam Garage Sales

June 2004 – Present (9 years 7 months)

Grand Slam Garage Sales offers a variety of services. The company started by running garage sales for people from start to finish. While that's still a main service, GSGS now also offers moving services, pressure washings and trash clean outs.

GSGS also buys and re-sells items via retail and online. The company was founded by Ben Weissenstein in June of 2004 and since then has done over 500 jobs in the Houston area, 6 figure annual revenue and has huge expansion plans for the future.

GSGS has received national and international media including Dr. Phil, Entrepreneur Magazine, PBS, About.com, Italian Millionaire Magazine, Houston Chronicle and more.
