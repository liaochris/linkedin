Research Analyst

City University of New York, Office of Special Programs

April 2012 – September 2012 (6 months) New York, NY

With Dr. Monique Jethwani-Keyser, collaborated to conduct 5 focus groups and numerous online surveys aimed at evaluating the effectiveness of SEEK and College Discovery’s Summer Leadership Academy. Retrieved data from IRDB to create a fact-book, and update deliverables used to inform SEEK and CD directors, as well as the Assistant Dean, of student’s academic progress relative to their non-program peers. Based on results, wrote literature review and proposal to design the evaluation of SEEK and College Discovery during the upcoming academic year.

Freelance Consultant

June 2011 – April 2012 (11 months) Washington, DC

Revised Building Blocks for Father Involvement, a Head Start publication that educates Head Start practitioners on the importance of involving fathers and strategic steps to create and maintain fatherhood programs. Updated literature review on how fathers influence their preschool-aged children’s well-being. Collaboratively developed interview protocol for and conducted interviews with Early Head Start practitioners on the use of the Building Blocks and functions of fatherhood programs within Head Start.

Owner/Editor

Owner/Editor responsibilities included editing articles submitted by guest bloggers, managing up to 18 regular Writers, maintaining SASSY social media presence.

Senior Research Assistant

Center for Research on Fathers, Children and Family Well-Being

Developed and maintained electronic database of all program commitments and ensured accurate system and data integrity.
