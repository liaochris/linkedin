Matt Crabtree's Summary

www.mattcrabtree.co.uk
www.positivemomentum.com

Following a career in sales, management and board positions for a range of businesses including Pitney Bowes, AT&T and Barclays Bank, in 2003 I decided to establish a business that we have called Positive Momentum Limited.

In essence we help businesses transform their approach to Leadership, Sales and Service but with a resolutely no nonsense style. Whether it's speaking at a conference, delivering a training seminar, providing a programme of one to one coaching or even actively leading transformation efforts we have a uniquely focused, enthusiastic and practical approach that is constructed around the specific needs of our clients.

Our team of world-class consultant partners are all ex middle to senior ranking corporate leaders who excelled in their own right and are now united in their no nonsense approach to helping clients across the globe.

Interested in becoming a Positive Momentum partner? Take a look at http://www.positivemomentum.com/joinus.html

Clients include Microsoft, Royal Bank of Scotland, Cisco, Bupa, France Telecom, Barclays Bank, Global Crossing, Experian, Red Bull, Thus, Sainsburys, Prudential, Liverpool Victoria, COLT, AT&T, Shell, Kraft, OMV, Santander, Selex Gallieo and Orange.

With a rapidly expanding global team we have extensive experience of working with clients across the globe and are delighted to engage with any business wishing to upgrade the way they interact with their people or customers.

I am also proud to be working with The Princes Trust, a British charity that helps disadvantaged young people to establish themselves in business and lead economically independent lives. Find out more at http://www.princes-trust.org.uk/

Specialties: Leadership and Management, New Business Sales, Major Account Development, Facilitation, Recruitment, Maintaining Energy & Optimism, Networking, Conference Speaking
