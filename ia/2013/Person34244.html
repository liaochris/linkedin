ClearWindowTech Inc.'s Summary

20 years of experience in residential and light commercial window replacement, patio doors, glass repair and replacement. Only carry the best windows and patio doors.
Custom wood and custom vinyl windows for less than most window replacement companies.
Helped build The Renewal by Andersen Franchise in St. Louis. Sold over 4.5 million in residential windows in 5 years.

Light commercial window jobs successfully completed:

-Hickory Sound Apartments- 99 large insulated glass with lowe, rotten wood removal, capping. completed 2008
-M.S.D. 3000 South Hampton- 68 vinyl picture windows completed 1996
-Brooking Park Retirement Living- 480 elite double hung completed 2010
-250 S. Brentwood- 53 large insulated glass with lowe completed 2010

One of my goals when I started in replacement window sales and service was to be an asset to clients by delivering high quality products, professionally installed. I give clients an insider perspective with valuable solutions to each client's individual needs. Honest answers, no pressure, under priced. Window repair is a great way to save money and increase thermal performance. Replace glass, patio door rollers, screens in sliding patio doors and windows.

Specialties: A local window company, window replacement parts, broken seals repaired . Window repair, window replacement, sliding glass door repair and replacement. Custom vinyl windows and doors. Andersen window replacement/parts.
Proudly serving St. Louis since 1983.
