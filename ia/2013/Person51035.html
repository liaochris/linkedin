Guy Covins' Summary

Has 26 years experience working in a variety of marketing roles. Previously employed by ICL (now Fujitsu) as the Marketing Programmes Manager responsible for the development and implementation of the company's Government Marketing Communications programme.

For the last seventeen years has worked as a freelance copy writer with clients from a broad range of industries, especially technology companies, to provide the concept and content development of all key marketing communications activities.

Based on the success of his work with clients he was made a Fellow of the Institute of Direct Marketing (FIDM) in 2004 and a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Marketing (FCIM) in 2007.
