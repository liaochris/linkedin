Brad Trivers' Summary

Brad Trivers is an information systems veteran with over 20 years in systems support, analysis and application development.

While attending the University of Waterloo he started out in technical support for computer algebra systems at MapleSoft (http://maplesoft.com/).

After graduation Brad spent 9 years in a business systems analyst and management role at Newbridge Networks/Alcatel (now http://alcatel-lucent.com/) working with tools to support the Research & Development teams including systems for project management (AutoPlan/MS-Project), change requests and issue management (custom BMC Remedy ARS implementation), component information/in-bound supply chain (SAP and i2 Technologies) and several other smaller internal applications.

Since 2004 Brad has been an independent consultant with his company Sunrise Solutions Inc (http://sunriseweb.ca). Along with developing web solutions for clients including custom content management, automated testing, change request management and contact management systems, Brad spent almost 6 years as a Senior Systems Analyst contracting with a federal government agency and working primarily with mainframe-based applications.

Since 2010 Brad has focused on web development using WordPress-based solutions to build his clients' web presence and enable their business processes. Brad's goal is to continue to create systems to enable business processes guided by the KISS and Pareto (80/20 rule) principles.
