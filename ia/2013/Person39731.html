Ed Sward's Summary

The person behind Interlink Marketing Corp and SMG/interlink. SMG/interlink represents and sells Otis Instruments fixed location gas detectors and confined space monitors, Honeywell Sperian and BW Technologies personal gas detectors, Gasco calibration gases, Mesa Laboratories-Bios DryCal flow calibrators, Sper Scientific industrial test instruments including sound, pH, water quality, manometers, anemometers, indoor air quality monitors, ACD electrochemical calibration gas generators, Dwyer intrinsically safe manometers and GrayWolf/Solomat indoor air quality monitors.

Web site is www.smglink.com.
