Jennifer Derryberry Mann's Summary

Jennifer Derryberry Mann teaches yoga, facilitates women's circles for self-care, and --as a birth advocate-- encourages women to embrace birth as a rite of passage. She is a certified Forrest yoga instructor who specializes in prenatal yoga and postnatal mama-baby Yoga Bonding. Jenni is also the editor of Belly Button Bliss: A Small Collection of Happy Birth Stories (Fairview Press, 2010), which earned positive press from Mothering magazine, Foreword Reviews, Babble.com, iVillage.com, and the International Childbirth Education Association. Jenni has been a speaker at the Lamaze International Annual Conference, the La Leche League of Georgia Annual Conference, and the Empower Your Pregnancy online conference.

Jenni draws on her magazine editing & writing background at Mamahhh.com, where her {breathe, mama} blog is a meditation on navigating the labyrinth of motherhood one breath at a time. Jenni’s writing has also appeared in FitPregnancy, Pregnancy, iVillage Pregnancy & Parenting, Spirituality & Health, and Natural Solutions magazines.

A mother of two, Jenni and her firstborn appear in the DVD Mom & Toddler Fitness: Tone, Strengthen, and Play (The Balancing Act for Life LLC, 2007). While pregnant with her second daughter, Jenni appeared in the DVD Be Blooma Well Prenatal Yoga (Blooma Media LLC, 2010).

Before Jenni began exclusively freelancing in 2006, she was an editor at Lifetime Fitness' healthy-way-of-life magazine Experience Life (2004-2006), a copy editor at the American Nuclear Society (2003-2004), and a b-to-b magazine editor at Vance Publishing (1995-1998).
