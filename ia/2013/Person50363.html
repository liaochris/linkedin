Fidelity National Title

December 1977 – Present (35 years 10 months) 1277 Kelly Johnson Blvd, Colorado Springs CO 80920

Fidelity National Title Insurance Company acquired Security Title Guaranty Co. and its management team in August, 2008. Security Title Guaranty Co. had been closing “The American Dream” in Colorado since 1946. We specialize in providing title insurance and protecting new owners and lenders in their residential and commercial real estate transactions together with settlement services nationwide. From closing a vacant lot to a large commercial office building, involved in complex 1031 tax deferred exchange, Fidelity National Title's knowledge, experience and expertise is the foundation of our paramount customer service. “Across the street or across the nation, we can facilitate your real estate transaction.”
With full service title plants in El Paso, Teller, Pueblo, Fremont and Custer County, we have the ability to provide a variety of specialized title research projects. This information has proved to be beneficial to governmental entities, municipalities, engineers, surveyors, rights of way acquisitions, easement searches, cellular and radio tower entities, aggregate companies and a variety of other businesses.
