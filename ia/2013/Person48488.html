Chery Gegelman's Summary

I am a VERY ordinary person, an entrepreneur, a speaker, an author, a consultant and current expat that has been blessed with repeated opportunities to lead system-wide change from the middle and the edge of organizations.

I believe:
1. That most of the answers organizations and communities are seeking today are lying dormant inside of their employees, their customers and their suppliers just waiting to be discovered and unleashed.
2. That each of us is capable of making an extraordinary difference regardless of what our title is.
3. That anyone with a leadership or management title has a great responsibility to listen, to model the behavior they would like to see, and to serve those they employ.

I write and speak about leadership, organizational development, change, and the expat journey. (Any wisdom or understanding I bring comes from failures and successes, wise mentors, the people I worked with, and the underserved grace of my Heavenly Father.)

Some of my speaking topics are:
1. Turning Unwanted Change into An Adventure!
2. Leading From Who YOU Are, Not WHERE You Sit.
3. Navigating Transformational Seasons of Change: The tug-of-war between “what is” and “what could be.”
4. Leading Organizational Change from The Middle and The Edge of an Organization
5. Dreams, Dragons, and Nerve Centers.

I am one of 21 authors from 3 countries that connected virtually, discovered a shared passion and wrote a book: The Character-Based Leader, Instigating A Leadership Revolution… One Person At A Time. Some of my most popular articles are listed in my profile below. You are welcome to join the conversation on my blog. http://consultgiana.com/

Please let me know how I can serve you!
