Glenn Wallis' Summary

I have worked as an Executive Coach for the past nine years, and been lucky enough to work with great people in a variety of organisations, including Fortune 100 companies, executive agencies of the government and individuals, including elite sports people.

A major new area of focus is taking my coaching experience into those organisations who were early adopters of coaching and providing the "next steps" in developing sustainable in-house coaching.

I am delighted to confirm that I have gained a place on the Coaching Doctoral Programme at Oxford Brookes University, starting in October 2012.

Specialties

Executive Coaching, Coach Development, Leadership Development, In-house Coach and Trainer Quality Assurance
