Emily P. Barrett, RN, CHPN, HTCP's Summary

I am a Certified Hospice and Palliative Care Nurse as well as a Healing Touch Certified Practitioner. I use essential oils to aid in the healing process. I specialize in supporting clients and their loved ones through the dying process. I have supported many clients with cancer through the difficult period of receiving chemotherapy and/or radiation. I have worked with adults, children, babies and animals. Often my clients have found Healing Touch and Aromatherapy helpful where other treatments or medications have failed. Healing Touch and essential oils are complementary to your ongoing, traditional medical treatments.

I also coordinate clinical aromatherapy classes in the San Francisco Bay area. I am a distributor for Young Living Essential Oils and would be happy to share my knowledge of the oils with anyone who is interested.
Peace!
