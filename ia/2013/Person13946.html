Bruce Bower's Summary

I am an emerging market equities portfolio manager with a specialty investing in Russia and the former Soviet Union. In eight years, I have produced top-decile returns managing long-only mandates and have worked with long/short mandates.

Also, I have extensive experience marketing to institutional investors -- endowments, foundations, FoFs, family offices- in the US and Europe. Lastly, I manage a team of 3 research analysts. I am always open to growing and developing as a professional.

I love markets and improving as a professional. Come read my blog, http://www.howoftrading.com, where I share thought about the "how" of being a good investor and trader. Look out for my upcoming book!

Lastly, I invest actively in private businesses across the US and the UK (I am dual citizen). Get in touch if you have a good idea!
