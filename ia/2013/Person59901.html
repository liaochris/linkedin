Jayme McRee's Summary

Jayme McRee has over twenty years experience in marketing communications, market research, business planning, and analytics. He holds a Bachelors Degree in Business Management from Northeastern University in Boston.

Jayme has filled executive roles at Fortune 500 corporations including Harte-Hanks CRM, a Global Direct Marketing firm; provided sales and business management services as a consultant for Sandler Training; and owned his own marketing consulting firm, Active Marketing Consultants, LLC.

Jayme's experience includes campaign development for multiple global companies, among them, Pfizer, Boehinger-Engelheim, Conseco Finance, Mazda and Mercedes Benz.

His experience also covers multiple business sectors including retail, education, manufacturing, information technologies, publishing, pharmaceuticals, finance, high-tech, state government, and municipalities.
