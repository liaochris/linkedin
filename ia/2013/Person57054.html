Jake Denham's Summary

Visualisation Artist and Graphic Designer working with Palmer Johnson Yachts currently based in Monaco. 5 years of professional experience specialising in interior and exterior rendering, advertising and marketing for private aircraft, hotels, banks, homes and yachts. Master of Arts in Design with a Scholarship for Academic Achievement and Development Potential from the University for the Creative Arts. Various international publications such as The Financial Times, The New York Times and Boat International. Highlights include two Creative Innovation awards sponsored by Apple and Sony and recent inclusion in Expose 11, a book of the best CGI from around the world.

Current role in the international design team involves utilising specialist knowledge of multiple 3D design, visualisation and editing software to create interior and exterior visuals. Communicating with press and approving marketing material and articles. Overseeing the development and content creation of the companies interactive iPad app. Creating and managing the company website. Using advanced knowledge of technology to problem solve and write software guides for use within the team.

Keen on adventures and the outdoors regularly hiking. Recently climbed Mont Bego, one of the highest mountains in the Mecantor region of the French Alps to raise money for a Children's Charity. Also keen on photography and creating texture packs of locations around the world which are free at www.jaked3d.com. The website is also an outlet for learning and teaching new ideas and technology.
