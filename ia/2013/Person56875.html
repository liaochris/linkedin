Jacqui Letran, NP, CMHt's Summary

Jacqui Letran is a Nurse Practitioner and Certified Master Hypnotherapist with offices in Irvine and Costa Mesa, California. Jacqui earned her Masters of Science in Nursing and has been practicing as a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner since 1996. She owned and operated her private medical clinics for six years, where she was responsible for the diagnosis and medical management of hundreds of patients with depression, anxiety, stress and related emotional health concerns.

Frustrated with traditional psychotherapy, pharmaceutical treatments and the current “sick care” system, Jacqui researched the most effective, results-driven, and time-proven methods for holistic healing. Jacqui’s dedication for providing the best care for her clients led her to complete a masters-level education in clinical hypnotherapy, Emotional Freedom Tecnhique, Neuro-Linguistic Programming and Relationship Coaching.

Combining her experience as a nurse practitioner with the best of these healing methods, Jacqui now practices as a hypnotherapist. Jacqui enjoys a tremendous level of success in assisting her clients navigate and overcome the pain of their past, identify and removing current obstacles allowing them to move forward with clarity and excitement for their future.
