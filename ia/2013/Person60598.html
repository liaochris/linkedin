Jeanne Lashmett's Summary

My professional experience is extremely diverse;I have worked in the corporate interior design department for Hellmuth, Obata & Kassabaum(one of the world leaders in interior design), taught and headed up the interior design department at a local St. Louis business college, spent many years as a designer for Brown Shoe Company and spent several years in sales of furniture and interior design services at Resource One, the local Knoll dealership in Springfield, Illinois.

At i3 DesignGroup, we possess a purposeful passion for interior design excellence and comprehensive client satisfaction. Our vision for interior design shows in our project implementation, project managementa and impactful interior design concepts.

As Interior designers, we possess a keen eye for detail. Our design skills are complemented by solid experience in client and project management, creating a vision for the client, and construction document assembly . We also specify and select colors and materials as well as furniture and fixtures.

We proudly offer the following services:

Residential Interior Design
Commercial Interior Design
Corporate/Office Interior Design
Hospitality Interior Design
Retail Interior Design
Exhibit Design
Staging/Display Homes

Specialties: Commercial and Residential Interior Design, Branding and Identity, conceptual design, ability to sketch visions for clients as they are sitting down with us-we really do bring visions to fruition!!
