Mike Schwarzer's Summary

Operating at Your Highest and Fullest Potential!

"Thank you so much for what I can only describe as an exhilarating weekend. I am now much more consciously engaged in communication and focused in every aspect of my daily life since completing the program." Michael Butler, Managing Director, Ivan Butler Funerals, Klemzig, SA


Are you still trying to solve today’s complex problems with yesterday’s mindset and mental capabilities?

Mike’s focus is on helping people and organisations operate at their highest and to their fullest potential.

Operating in highly complex environments?

People and organisations today operate in highly complex environments where technology and consumer demands are changing faster than many minds can follow. The world is still trying to solve the challenges of today and tomorrow with the thinking and mental models of yesterday.

A new way of thinking required!

Dealing with the complex issues and challenges of today’s and tomorrow’s world requires us to move from linear to non-linear thinking. That means developing our ability to engage the complex and systemic facets of our mental capabilities that give us the flexibility and responsiveness to play in today’s marketing place.

Mike’s expertise and programs are strongly influenced by advanced cognitive behavioural technologies. These include the cognitive behavioural sciences, systems dynamics, structural dynamics and self-actualisation psychology.

Through these technologies people learn how to use their mental capabilities in new and innovative ways and develop behaviours and qualities that are flexible and supportive of tomorrow’s challenges

Turning Meaning into Performance!

Our approach and programs are highly engaging, interactive and experiential, helping participants to close the Knowing-Doing Gap. Moving an idea or concept into behavior – turning meaning into performance!

Mike Schwarzer's Experience

January 1997 – Present (16 years 8 months)

Mike is a Performance Strategist & Modeler. An experienced presenter, facilitator and coach, with an ability to connect and align people and organisations. He helps them to fine tune their performance wizardry by 'bringing out the best in people' to bridge the gap between where they are and where they want to go!

The nature of Mike’s approach makes it suitable for most areas in business that need to maintain their performance ability. Including Strategic Thinking, Vision, Leadership, Customer Service & Sales.

A former 5 Star hospitality specialist with the Hyatt International Hotel Group, he has presented to and worked with clients from the private and public sector that include the Hilton Hotel Group, SKYCITY Casino, Accor Hotel Group, Beyond Imagination Entertainment, Hughes Limousines, Roche Pharmaceuticals, City of Salisbury, DFEEST, DFC, DENR, TAFE SA, South Australian Cricket Associaton, Telstra and ANZ Bank, Adelaide and Bendigo and Bank, Rural Bank, Toop & Toop, Brock Harcourts, The Professionals, Orana,Guide Dogs SA.NT.,RAA and Adelaide Crows.

Mike has served as the Convenor of the AHRI SA Organisational Design & Development Network and as the former Executive Director of the SA Chapter of the National Speakers Association of Australia.

He is an Internationally Certified Trainer (having trained with the Dr. Michael Hall, the developer of Neuro Semantics and Colin Cox, the only Master Trainer in this field) & Master Practitioner in Neuro Semantics and Neuro Linguistic Programming, has an Advanced Diploma in Human Resource Management and the Cert. 4 in Training & Assessment.
