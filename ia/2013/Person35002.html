Douglas Grabowski's Projects

  • DivaDocs

    • May 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Douglas Grabowski, Stephanie Bruno, Colleen Tully, Susan Bruno, Peter Flournoy, CPA/PFS, Justin Moore

    DivaCFO, LLC, an emerging women’s finance company, will release their new iPhone app, DivaDocs into the App Store later this summer (2012). A virtual scavenger hunt for vital documents with rewards along the way, DivaDocs empowers women with the first step to a financially sound future.

    “DivaDocs is unlike any app on the market. For the first time, personal finance and organizing will be fun,” said Susan Bruno, CPA, PFP/PFS and DivaCFO CEO. “Women can have fun organizing and uploading their documents; and the retail rewards are given as they accomplish milestones throughout the app.”

    DivaDocs features nine different categories, which range from DivaDebt to DivaTax, Users are prompted to, “Find It! Learn It! File It!” for all vital documents associated with each category.

    DivaCFO, LLC was founded in 2010 by a mother-daughter duo to educate women about their personal finances.
