Amanda V. Duncan's Summary

In her current role, Amanda is part of the North America Communications Team at Nokia, responsible for a combination of internal and external communication activities.

Prior to joining Nokia, Amanda worked at global PR firm Waggener Edstrom Worldwide, where she focused on analyst and public relations for the Microsoft Cloud, Developer and Intellectual Property Teams. Prior to that, she spent three years working for Wells Fargo & Co in San Francisco handling internal marketing and communication initiatives for the International Financial Services Group.

Originally from Montana, Amanda graduated from Seattle University with a bachelor’s degree in Communications. Outside of work, Amanda enjoys skiing and continuing the Duncan family tradition of shooting sporting clays competitively.

Specialties: Strategic communications, branding, event planning, strategic business planning, targeted marketing campaigns, analyst relations
