Cameron Chilton's Summary

Mr. Chilton possesses over fifteen years of experience in the fields of graphic design and information technology working in a variety of industries: IT consulting, financial services, risk management, data analytics, education, and traditional media. Some notable companies Cameron has worked with include the global IT services firm CSC, the global financial services company AXA, the global risk management firm Marsh, and the risk and data analytics company Verisk. He is both a talented graphic designer and user experience/software expert with an innate sense of organization and informational hierarchy. He also possesses expert level skills as a front-end developer using HTML, CSS, Javascript/jQuery, and graphics-creation programs to create high-fidelity, clickable application prototypes for application design and wireframing. Cameron is also able to develop standards-compliant code for production releases and is usually involved in the build and maintenance phases of software/application development projects. In the context of software/application development, his most beneficial strength is the ability to gather, categorize, and synthesize data and business requirements into user-centered solutions that improve client engagement and overall project success.
