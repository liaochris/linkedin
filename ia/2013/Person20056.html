Carlos Augusto Freitas, CAPM, PMP, ITIL's Summary

Carlos Augusto is a one of the first in Brazil and Latin America to receive a CAPM(Certified Associate in Project Management) Credendial of Project Management Institute (PMI), the largest project management organization in the world with close to 500.000 members and certified professionals in 182 countries.

He is responsible for CAPM Credencial Brazilian Group. The group is also responsible to support more than 1300 professionals around the Brazil with information about CAPM Credencial.
He is author of the first book in Portuguese for CAPM certification (focus on project team members and project managers without experience).

Team member and represent Brazil Standadization Organization (ABNT) in the ISO PC236 for development of Project Management Standard (ISO 21.500) and ISO meeting Rio 2010.
