(IES) Institute for Energy & Sustainability's Summary

IES MISSION: The Institute for Energy & Sustainability (IES) is central New England’s green business zone. Our mission is to attract clean energy technology and renewable energy companies to the region. We are building on the region’s assets and reputation as an incubator of innovation. Our priority is to create an environment for those seeking economic opportunity and advantage in this sector and to support the growth of jobs establishing a zone of world class clean technology companies

(IES) Institute for Energy & Sustainability's Projects

  • Sustainability As A Service

    Team Members: (IES) Institute for Energy & Sustainability

    IES Sustainability As A Service is a one-stop shop for a wide diversity of sustainability products and services. Through contracted partnerships with member companies, IES is able to provide lead generation, referrals, and business to business services and support designed to encourage the growth and expansion of energy and sustainability business and practice.

  • IES Marketing and Communications

    Team Members: (IES) Institute for Energy & Sustainability

    The Institute for Energy and Sustainability core mission is attract and develop energy and sustainability jobs, projects and business. We have been able to represent trends, news, case studies, events and innovation for the cleantech economy through a variety online and print news placements, academic journals, radio, print and online advertisements, marketing materials, social media, event hosting and peer to peer sharing.

  • Virtual Incubator Program

    Team Members: (IES) Institute for Energy & Sustainability

    About
    The Institute for Energy & Sustainability’s Virtual Incubator Program (VIP) provides a range of services and development opportunities to early stage clean technology organizations. IES works with them to strengthen business development strategies, engage and retain a larger customer base and expand marketing and promotion opportunities for their organizations.

    Partner Network
    Our vast partnership network with community leaders, investors, service providers, university contacts and businesses provides early stage organizations with the contacts needed to help get their business growing. We are able to leverage our partner agreements and relationships to deliver the highest quality clean technology business development tools in Central MA through quarterly receptions, individual meetings, and an array of workshops.

    Access to Mentors
    IES has reached out across Central Massachusetts and combined our Institute’s mission and resources, with our partner networks and the expertise and resources of our mentor network. Access to IES’ mentor network is an exclusive benefit of our VIP program and provides free consultation and business advisement from industry leaders in multiple sectors from banking and finance to business development, technology testing, and media and marketing.

  • Energy This Week

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: (IES) Institute for Energy & Sustainability

    IES Executive Director, Vincent DeVito and Senior Program Advisor Chris Noonan, co-hosted a weekly one-hour radio broadcast, Energy This Week, that covered a range of news and commentary on trends and topics in energy and sustainability. Energy This Week was sponsored by Energy Monster and broadcast on WCRN 830AM each Wednesday from September through December of 2012. Check out past podcasts by following the project URL above.
