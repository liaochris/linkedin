Lora Brawley's Summary

I’ve worked in the nanny care field for over 22 years as a nanny, industry leader, trainer and consultant. Nanny Biz Reviews is my home spot for everything nanny. There you’ll find the only industry-wide business directory including user and expert reviews and an extensive resource library for parents and nannies providing everything needed to find, hire and keep a great nanny (or job).

Through my consulting services, I provide a unique blend of common-sense strategies and intuitive guidance to the nannies and parents I work with.

Specialties

Nanny contract / work agreement development and negotiation, in-depth nanny interviewing and referencing, caregiver and parent training, childcare choice consulting
