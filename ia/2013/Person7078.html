Andrew Mellen's Summary

Andrew Mellen, Inc. provides one-to-one and one-to-many training, coaching, and consulting to individuals and companies nationwide.

Clients range from Fortune 100 Companies including Time, Inc. & Genentech to the U.S. Departments of Education and Homeland Security, as well as award-winning artists, authors, CEOs and overwhelmed moms and dads everywhere.

Andrew is the #1 best-selling author of "Unstuff Your Life!" (Penguin, 2010), and frequently appears on national TV, radio and press as an organizational expert and author.

Andrew's motto, "More Love, Less Stuff!®," exemplifies AMI's commitment to helping corporations, small businesses, entrepreneurs and individuals eliminate the obstacles that stand between them and a sustainable life of exceptional productivity, meaning and joy.

Over 17 years of field-tested experience has proven that the best solutions are practical and easy to use consistently.

There's nothing magical about good time management and staying organized -- the willingness to be momentarily uncomfortable or inconvenienced will pay tremendous dividends over time and seems an easy compromise. Pay now or pay later -- it's always more expensive on the way out.

Specialties: Organizing, speaking, writing, productivity, time management, project management, strategic planning, sustainability of resources: human, natural and other.

Andrew Mellen's Projects

  • Unstuff U

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Andrew Mellen, Paula Thrall, Mark Pfohl

    In January, 2013, we launched Unstuff U, an online membership site where we offer monthly courses on the biggest clutter hotspot topics like PAPERS & FILING, EMAIL & DIGITAL FILES, SENTIMENTAL OBJECTS, TIME MANAGEMENT, PHOTOS, PROJECT MANAGEMENT, etc.

    There's also a Library with resources, forms and templates for organizing everything from travel to online passwords.

  • Extend Your Reach

    • 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Andrew Mellen, Susan Piver, Charlie Gilkey, Jennifer Louden

    Master teachers (and online pioneers) Charlie Gilkey, Jennifer Louden, Andrew Mellen, and Susan Piver are coming together to offer a one-day Intensive in Seattle and New York that promises to deliver the knowledge, skills—and a clear action plan—to begin teaching online.

    Extend Your Reach is designed for anyone who currently teaches, coaches, or offers a service and wants to exponentially expand their potential audience—by learning how to teach online.

    Until recently, sharing your passion and skills as widely as possible typically involved planes, trains, and automobiles. You had to show up in-person to help and motivate others.

    Online technology has changed that.

    Now it's possible to reach more people than ever before without leaving your home or office. The only limits to what you can offer are your technical proficiency, creativity, and imagination.

    But exactly how does one do that?

    This program answers that question. This 12-hour information bootcamp includes detailed presentations, small group breakouts, conversation, food, fun, and the delight of camaraderie—so often in short supply for solopreneurs and small business owners!

    Limited to only 25 participants in each location to ensure that each attendee gets in-depth time and attention, registration is by application and qualified participants will be selected on a first-come, first-served basis.

    If you've been thinking of teaching online but unclear on how, when where or even what, this is the training event for you.

Andrew Mellen's Publications

  • Unstuff Your Life! Kick the Clutter Habit and Completely Organize Your Life for Good

    • Penguin, Audible.com
    • August 3, 2010
    Authors: Andrew Mellen

    A comprehensive and entertaining step-by-step guide to organizing everything from your office to your garage, while realigning yourself with your core values.

    Simple and direct instruction on how to free your life from clutter, disorganization and distraction and get time back in your life for what really matters, whether that's attending your child's soccer match on time, baking cookies for the church bazaar or solving global crises.

  • YES Is The Answer: (And Other Prog Rock Tales)

    • Rare Bird Books/A Barnacle Book
    • May 14, 2013
    Authors: Andrew Mellen, Seth Greenland, Rick Moody, Charles Bock, Wesley Stace, Jeff Gordinier, Tom Junod, Paul Myers, Beth Lisick, Margaret Wappler, Nathan Larson

    Progressive rock is maligned and misunderstood. Critics hate it, hipsters scoff at it. Yes Is The Answer is a pointed rebuke to the prog-haters, the first literary anthology devoted to the sub genre. Featuring acclaimed novelists Rick Moody, Wesley Stace, Seth Greenland, Charles Bock and Joe Meno, musicians Nathan Larson and Peter Case, and organizational expert Andrew Mellen, Yes Is The Answer is the first book that dares to thoughtfully reclaim prog-rock as a subject worthy of serious consideration. So take a Topographic Journey into a 21st Century Schizoid land of Prog-Lit!
