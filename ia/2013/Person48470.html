Glenn Howell's Summary

Had a great time during my 15 years of working law enforcement. I gained varied experience in patrol, investigations, court security and detentions working for specialized and county angencies. I worked as a department crime scene technician and field training officer,conducted applicant interviews and background investigations and facility security surveys. In addition to my LEO background I have 10 years physical security experience including fixed site, patrol, alarm response and facility security. In both areas I have trained staff in the operation and use of computer systems and software.

I happily provide law enforcement technical assistance to writers and novelists.

I have strong interpersonal skills and enjoy meeting challenges head on and surpassing personal goals.
