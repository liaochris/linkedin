Joshua Zerkel's Summary

I specialize in helping businesspeople find easy ways to get control of their space, information, time, and technology so they can boost their productivity and effectiveness – and their profits. I consult and train individuals and groups or teams. I'm passionate about helping people learn how to use technology-related solutions to enhance their workflow, communication, and generally make their work - and their lives - easier and more effective.

Some of the projects I've worked on include:

- Showing busy attorneys at the Public Defender's Office how to better juggle their myriad cases and tasks
- Developing complete training programs and home study courses, including creating online, offline, live, and mixed learning
- Leading webinars on many topics related to productivity for both professionals and the public
- Training teams how to enhance their individual and team workflow

I've built an extensive online and offline community of people who are interested in learning ways to get and stay organized and productive. I'm a Certified Professional Organizer® and am also the two-term past President of the San Francisco Bay Area Chapter of the National Association of Professional Organizers. I've worn every hat in my business, including marketing, social media management and community building, finance, hr, and all administrative functions. I love learning new skills, applications, and tools, and sharing them with my community.

I've been featured on National Public Radio, CBS News, ABC News, the San Francisco Chronicle and Examiner, Organize Magazine, Online Organizing, the Etsy Blog, and many others, I'm a regular columnist on the Franklin Covey website, I blog for Extra Space storage, am a Biznik Ambassador, am a part of the Neat Fleet from the Neat Company, and I'm the world’s first Evernote Ambassador on Productivity.

Although I don't consider myself freakishly organized, I do admit to spending some of my free time alphabetizing my comic books.

Specialties

Office/workplace productivity and organizing, paper and information management, space/storage planning, time management, effectiveness and efficiency, scheduling/prioritizing, systems and process improvement and documentation, instructional design and development, public speaking, leveraging technology for workflow improvement, community building and education

Joshua Zerkel's Publications

  • Evernote at Work

    • January 15, 2013
    Authors: Joshua Zerkel

    Evernote is a great tool for organizing and keeping track of your personal information, but did you know it's an incredibly powerful tool for getting things done at work? In fact, if you know how to use Evernote, you can streamline many tasks and be much more productive - but you have to know how!

    Evernote @ Work is the bestselling, step-by-step guide to the many ways you can use Evernote to make your daily work easier and more productive.

  • INTEGRATE: Evernote

    • August 2012
    Authors: Joshua Zerkel, Lindsey C. Holmes

    Have you been wondering how to get more out of Evernote?

    INTEGRATE: Evernote walks you through how to use Evernote in your daily work, and specific ideas on integrating its third-party integrations to extend its functionality and boost your productivity.

    In this book, you'll learn how to use Evernote and its 3rd-party Trunk parnters to:
    Streamline and automate your workflow
    Go paperless
    Corral your contacts
    Manage your marketing
    Share information
    Consolidate your financial records
    ...and much more!
    INTEGRATE: Evernote is designed for people who are already somewhat familiar with using Evernote (it's not for absolute beginners). If you're looking to enhance and upgrade your Evernote use, or are curious about how it can be super-charged to maximize your workflow, INTEGRATE: Evernote is perfect for you!
