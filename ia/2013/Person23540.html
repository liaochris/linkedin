Chris Horner's Summary

I have over 16 years of professional, consultative selling experience, working with both consumer and business clientele. All experience has been representing high-end products and services. 12 of those years includes working as a Sales Manager. I have managed corporate operational departments and a small call center to make them run more efficiently.

I also have over 20 years of experience in public speaking, and am proficient in Microsoft Office, Windows, Mac OS-X, iOS, and Adobe applications.

As for photography, I enjoy creating images of unique places, exotic automobiles, and images used for business marketing materials. Many of my images have won awards and have seen publication.

In-depth knowledge of financial payment systems for businesses.

As an avid auto enthusiast, I've managed to get seat time in just about every kind of car you can imagine, and have written critical reviews of them.
