Qazi Hamza Javed's Summary

Qazi Hamza Javed, the Creative Lead and Owner at Headbumped Studio, possesses an essential repertoire of technical and conceptual skills, backed by his strong academic background and ten years of work experience in Branding/Marketing, Filmmaking and Printing-related projects.

After completing an undergraduate degree in Computer Sciences from LUMS, Hamza worked on several marketing projects with the leading corporations and advertising agencies in Pakistan. Afterwards, he attained a Masters degree in Digital Imaging and Design from New York University and set up his own design and production studio.

Specialties

Character Modeling, Character Rigging, Environment Modeling, Environment Rigging, 3D Production Workflow, 3D Generalist, Branding/Marketing, Printing
