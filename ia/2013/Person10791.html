Aubre Andrus' Summary

Aubre Andrus LLC is an award-winning copywriting and lifestyle editorial business based out of Chicago with clients including American Girl, National Geographic Traveler, Mashable, WIRED, Huffington Post Travel, Six Flags, CareerBuilder, American Family Insurance, EXPRESS, ShopBop (Amazon.com), and more. I'm your go-to girl for travel, tech, geek, and kids-related content.

I've been called “an expert on branding,” “the best person for the job,” and “extremely gifted.” My clients say I'm "a pleasure to work with," "passionate," and "never miss a deadline."

As a freelance writer in Chicago, I'm allowed to do it all -- because I'm the boss and I said so! -- which means I grow as a writer every day. I've become a creative journalist who also speaks corporate thanks to the unique projects from my diverse client base. I love what I do, and I'm not afraid to tackle any assignment. My specialties include reporting compelling and timely stories for my favorite travel, tech, and lifestyle publications; working closely with editors and designers on publishing projects including books (I've written 7!) and magazine features; and creating successful marketing campaigns for big and small businesses through web copywriting, blogging, and social media.
