Joseph (Joe) Coots' Summary

Even with 20 plus years of Graphic Design experience, I love to learn new things. Im taking classes to add more to my web skills and business knowledge. I am currently getting ready to start a small graphics company to run my freelance jobs through. My portfolio website is available for you to see. Go to www.joecoots.com

Specialties: Most of my work is in Photoshop, Illustrator & Indesign and I am well versed in all three. However I am also now designing websites and doing more in interactive areas of web design. I know Flash and Dreamweaver as well as learning HTML. Im also taking photography classes and Im loving it.

Beyond the design side I am married, love travel, camping, sports, movies, art, photography, Sci-Fi and yeah Im a comic-book nerd since I was in the 4th grade. I guess you can say Im a little on the diverse side of life...
