Charlie Van Hecke's Summary

My name is Charlie and I am a sales veteran with ImproMed, an award-winning, world-wide leader in veterinary software. My technical competencies include enterprise software, SQL database applications, Microsoft Office and CRM solutions. My sales abilities were honed from classes at Pitney Bowes (Dictaphone) in Professional and Advanced Selling Skills.

A background in sales training started with being a sales instructor at Dictaphone. During that time, a Master's in Human Resource Development was earned at Georgia State University. A portfolio and in depth background can be found at www.salestrainer4U.com.
