● American Jurisprudence award in appellate practice

● selected for the 2014 edition of The Best Lawyers in America for insurance law

● ranked among the top 25 insurance coverage lawyers in Ohio by Super Lawyers in 2010, 2011, and 2012

● listed in Best's Directory of Recommended Insurance Attorneys

● wrote three chapters of the first edition of the only commercially available treatise on Ohio tort law, for Westlaw

● fellow of the American College of Board-Certified Attorneys

● member of the Claims & Litigation Management Alliance (CLM)

The Carr Law Office, LLC combines board-certified civil trial counsel and award-winning appellate counsel, with recognized insurance coverage expertise. Adam E. Carr represents insurance companies and the people they insure in civil lawsuits of all types, especially insurance coverage, bad faith, personal injury, wrongful death, and appeals. He also evaluates claims and provides written opinions to insurers, businesses, and individuals.

The Carr Law Office, LLC practices throughout the State of Ohio.
