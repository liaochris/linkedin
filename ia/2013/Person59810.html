Jay Dolan's Summary

Jay Dolan is a Senior Social Media Strategist at Capstrat in Raleigh, NC. At Capstrat, Jay helps clients develop, implement and optimize enterprise-level social media strategy for internal and external communication. He also helps businesses connect with global audiences and build online communities through Facebook, Twitter and other social media channels using paid, earned and owned social media tactics. A Facebook and Twitter developer, he consults on web, mobile, and interactive projects that connect to social network APIs.

Jay is also known for is blog, The Anti-Social Media, a social media humor and commentary blog. Posts from The Anti-Social Media have appeared on Brazen Careerist, Social Media Today, and Social Media Informer. Listed as a Top 10 Social Media Blog of 2011 by Social Media Examiner, The Anti-Social Media is a unique take on social media marketing and everyday use.

A former classical singer, Jay is passionate about the performing arts and providing accessibility to theatre and classical music. He currently serves on the board of directors at North Raleigh Arts & Creative Theatre, overseeing marketing and communications efforts.

Specialties: Social Media Strategy, Online Community Development, Online branding, Blogger Outreach, Personal Branding, Blogging, Social Networks, Online Community Management, Facebook Applications, Twitter Strategy, Enterprise Social Media Strategy, Global Social Media Strategy, social networking, internal social media, international social media strategy, social media marketing,
