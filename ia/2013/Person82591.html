Lorraine Windsor's Summary

I have over twenty years experience of working in the UK academic sector and more than 10 years IT management experience.

I love working with a range of people and believe that everyone has something of value to contribute.

I provide tailored learning solutions in Microsoft Office, as well as being a Social Media Trainer. I can also set up Social Media on a Consultancy basis, including time saving tools, to update Social Media from a single location.

It is my aim to change the way IT Training is delivered, by analysing the business needs of each organisation. To that end, I deliver "project based" or "work based" training, helping people use the tools to produce something of value to the organisation. So. if you want to produce professional reports or accounts, I will demonstrate how to use the relevant application to do just that.
