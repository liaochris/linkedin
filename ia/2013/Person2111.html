AHEC - CE medical imaging specialist's Summary

Who We Are
Advanced Health Education Center is the premier provider of corporate style education for the imaging sciences. Uniquely focused on providing our clients with courses that cross train them in a new skill, or sharpen existing skills, AHEC is on the cutting edge of imaging. Imaging technology is changing rapidly, and since 1988 Advanced Health Education Center has been committed to equipping our clients with the tools for today and the skills for tomorrow.

What We Do
We are organized around one central idea -- we'll do whatever it takes to connect our clients with the latest information on ultrasound, mammography, bone densitometry, CT, MR, PACS administration, and digital imaging. Performance improvement seminars located in over 100 different cities throughout the year make it convenient to update existing skills, network with peers, and learn from the experts. Independent study and online education accommodate even the most hectic schedules, providing user-friendly, quality CE in several modalities. Your facility may even partner with AHEC to provide customized education to your employees and community at your location.

Other divisions of AHEC: MedRelief Staffing & AHEC Cardiovascular Ultrasound Services.
