Sean Mullen's Summary

I am a behavioral scientist. My research focuses on understanding factors that facilitate long-term physical activity behavior and self-regulation among adults. My lab employs an interdisciplinary, social neuroscience framework to study psychosocial and cognitive antecedents and outcomes of regular exercise. To complement more traditional supervised, center-based physical activity interventions, we will integrate technology-based delivery methods, including the use of mobile devices.
