Dan Serra, CFP®, MS, CDFA, ADPA's Summary

My name is Dan Serra. I am a CFP (Certified Financial Planner) for high-net-worth families and individuals. I help them manage their financial life by discovering what is important to them and how financial planning can be used as one of the tools in leading a successful and fulfilling life. I am an independent fee-only advisor, taking a non-biased approach with no commissioned product sales in order to make the best decisions in the interest of the client first.

At Freed, I am actively involved in the delivery of all aspects of the wealth management services, providing advice regarding planning for:
-Retirement
-Taxes
-Insurance
-Estate Plans
-Investments
-Holistic Planning

Additionally, as a Certified Divorce Financial Analyst, I have a sub-specialty in advising those facing a divorce. I am trained as a financial specialist in the collaborative divorce process and as a financial mediator. As an Accredited Domestic Partnership Advisor, I advise same-sex partners and unmarried couples (more at www.LifePartnersPlanning.com).

I have written articles for national publications and have appeared in The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, USA TODAY, SmartMoney magazine and Investment News.

Specialties: Financial Planning, Life Planning, Tax Planning, Retirement Planning, Estate Planning, Investment Planning, Domestic Partner Planning, Divorce Planning, Mediation
