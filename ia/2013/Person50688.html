GSA XChange's Summary

Our mission is to be YOUR marketplace and service platform for government-leased properties on a national basis! Our team of highly professional, trusted advisors is truly dedicated on a daily basis to connecting buyers and sellers in this specialized market. GSAXCHANGE is the preferred choice for delivering sophisticated real estate and financial advisory services for government leased properties.

Specialties: The GSAXCHANGE team provides the following services:

Marketing & Disposition
Landlord Representation
Advisory Consulting
Market Research
Debt & Equity Placement
Appraisal & Valuation
Property Management
Lease Administration
Site Evaluation
Construction Management
LEED Requirements

Alongside our GSAXCHANGE's specialization in investment sales and financing, our team has partnered with Colliers International's Government Solutions platform to offer more services in the government sector, such as: leasing, appraisals, property management and tax appeal.

Check out the Colliers Government blog at http://www.capitolmarkets.com/ for more information about current government issues.
