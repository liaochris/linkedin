Andrew Banta's Summary

I graduated from Purdue University, where I earned a Bachelors of Science in Computer Graphics Technology, with a minor in Organizational Leadership and Supervision. Following my university studies I enrolled and completed an 18 month program at Animation Mentor, where I earned a Certificate in Advanced Studies in Character Animation. I hope to devote my career to character animation. I have taken numerous foundation courses in Maya, giving me a working knowledge of modeling, texturing and lighting, rigging and binding, and animation. At this point, I am putting all of my effort and resources into character animation and am intent on breaking into the film industry.

Specialties: Maya, Adobe Photoshop, CATIA, Microsoft Office, Adobe After Effects, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Flash, Adobe Illustrator, Macromedia Freehand, Windows XP/Vista/7, HTML/XHTML, XML.
