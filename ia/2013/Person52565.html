Helen Owens' Summary

A licensed cosmetologist, my career began over two decades ago when I lost my hair to alopecia areata. Since then I have made it my mission to ensure women have choices in hair extensions, hair replacement, and hair care that was not available to me as young woman. My vision is to ensure every woman who’s ever admired another’s hair now has the hair everyone admires. I’m available for consultations via phone, in person, or skype.

Specialties

Featured in a variety of local and national media, including public affairs programming as an advocate for increasing awareness of the impact of hair loss in women. Created and designed hairstyles and custom hair extensions for a variety of celebrities and professional athletes.
