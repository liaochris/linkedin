Christina Ray's Summary

I help people and companies design, launch and manage creative ventures.

As a consultant, I use curatorial and technical design knowledge to develop meaningful experiences, products and services for organizations and entrepreneurs who value thoughtful, sustainable and delightful innovation. I assist creative startups with strategic planning, product development and user experience design. I'm an advisor to art producers, curators, collectors and investors seeking insight into the relationships between artistic production, social impact design and emerging technologies.

Over the past ten years I’ve owned a contemporary art gallery, produced a festival and worked with groups including Eyebeam, Fidelity Investments, Intel’s Berkeley Research Lab, Microsoft, the New Museum of Contemporary Art, the New York State Council on the Arts, New York University, the PULSE Art Fair and San Francisco’s Southern Exposure non-profit arts organization and Taptera, Inc. I’m currently working with startups in New York and California.

Specialties: product strategy, experience design, content strategy, art advising, entrepreneurship, project planning, project management
