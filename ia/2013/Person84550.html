Michael Mancuso's Experience

Chief Experience Officer

Bratton Technologies

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Services industry

April 2013 – Present (7 months) New York

I am a co-founder of Bratton Technologies, a venture-backed startup based in New York City. As Chief Experience Officer, I am responsible for the user experience of the company's products, marketing and branding - all customer touch points. Our first product is called BlueLine, the first secure, global law enforcement professional network. The company was co-founded and named after with Bill Bratton, former Chief of the LAPD and former Commissioner of NYPD and the Boston Police.

BlueLine law enforcement members are able to find and engage other local, national and international peers, access expert knowledge and insights, then communicate and collaborate globally through posts as well as in real-time, through voice, video and chat. BlueLine also enables vendors serving the law enforcement community to more effectively reach the tens of thousands of individual police departments, forming a first-of-its-kind global marketplace.

For more information, see http://brattontech.com.
