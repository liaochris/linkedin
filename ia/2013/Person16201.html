Stephan Bradley's Summary

I am a licensed professional chemical engineer with over 22 years of experience in process engineering and project management. I am a Senior Member of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers, member of American Water Works Association, member of American Membrane Technology Association, and hold PE licenses in FL, MT, and AK. I have specialized in membrane filtration of water and wastewater as well as specialty water filtration processes and as a former employee of one of the top membrane manufacturers in the United States, I have unique and unmatched experience in the design of water treatment projects ranging from 0.5 MGD to 32 MGD.

I was the design engineer for the first tribal RO plant located in Montana – the Rocky Boy RO Plant near Laredo, Montana. I provided design services, construction support, start-up support, and training for tribal operators.

I recently completed the process engineering for a complicated water filtration project that received a 2010 President’s Award from the Wyoming Engineering Society.

I am a frequent presenter at AWWA and AMTA conferences at the local and national level on topics related to low pressure membrane filtration and related pretreatment.

Bradley Ventures LLC owns Simply Wine (a retail wine shop) here in Billings.
