Global Solutions India's Summary

About us

Visit our official website http://www.globalsolutionsindia.in

Global Solutions India is a leading management training and consultancy organization partnering your success and growth.

Our able and committed training / consultancy team ensures to transfer the latest techno-managerial skill to the employees of your organization / participants and do hand-holding to outperform your targets. We offer a complete range of professional consultancy and training in the field Lean Six Sigma, TPM (Total Productive Maintenance), 5S, SMED, Makigami, 7QC tools and Gemba Kaizen (Work Place Improvement), etc.

Global Solutions India have one of the most qualified and trained team of trainers / consultants. These consultants / trainers have a proven record of translating their knowledge and skills into results in all the organizations with which they have been associated.

We have tie-ups with the world’s leading CBT/ WBT companies and the products are available with us with the most competitive rates.

Several training videos on TPM, 5S, JIT (Just-in-time), Gemba Kaizen etc are also exclusively available with us.

We also provide general / customized posters, promotional items, Daruma, fuguai tags, fuguai registers, TPM formats etc to ensure that all your requirements are fulfilled under one roof.

We provide software solutions to monitor the progress and integrate TPM activities.
So, our team is there to provide the desired support and become your partner towards your path to Business Excellence.

Specialties

Training and Consultancy on Lean Six Sigma, TPM, 5S, Gemba Kaizen and Makigami consultancy. Also shop online for Daruma Dolls, TPM formats, exclusive TPM posters, fuguai tags, TPM JIT 5S and Gemba Kaizen videos.
