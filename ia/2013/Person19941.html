Carl Berthelette's Publications

  • Efficient synthesis of substituted vinyl ethers using the Julia olefination

    • Organic Letters / ACS
    • November 11, 2003
    Authors: Carl Berthelette, Simon Surprenant, Wing Yan Chan

    Julia olefination between α-alkoxy sulfones (I; R = benzyl, C6H4Cl-4, CH2CH2OMe) and a wide variety of ketones or aldehydes afforded substituted vinyl ethers in 46-90% yields. I were readily prepd. in two steps from com. available reagents in 68-80% yields. Optimization revealed that the base, solvent, and temp. were crucial to obtaining the desired vinyl ethers.
