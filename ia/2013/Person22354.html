Charly Alexander's Summary

Summary:

I have been helping business owners maximize their online presence since 2008. My role as is to take the administrative management tasks off of your shoulders so you are free to do what you do best!

I take a very proactive approach in my role by constantly brainstorming and researching ways to run your business in the most efficient and effective manner--understanding that while I am responsible for the administrative oversight of your business, your success ultimately leads to my success.

I moved into online administrative management after a long-time career in a brick & mortar office environment. I have provided administrative management to businesses ranging from small mom-and-pop companies to large international corporations.

I am certified in internet marketing, social media marketing, eCommerce, and online business management. My services benefit online business owners by:

-- Taking over the burden of the day-to-day administrative tasks
-- Brainstorming ways to streamline and optimize processes
-- Keeping up on current technologies
-- Overseeing the activities of virtual team members
-- Planning out the details of projects from start to finish
