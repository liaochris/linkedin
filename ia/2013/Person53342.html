Ian Hocking's Summary

Barrie Real estate broker, licensed in Ontario. Very passionate about being the best you can be. Knowledge with action and a keen client awareness equals a client centric experience that has lead to a business 90% driven by referrals from our existing client base.

We have a focus on residential and investment real estate and have dealt with all the major banks in connection with foreclosures as well as relocation of top executives. With our international connections across North America we now offer a relocation service focused on the client need.

Ian regularly speaks on investing in real estate and has a keen following from many investors. Sometimes it's difficult to get him to stop talking about investing in real estate, but that's not always a bad thing!

Previously ian has 18+ years of managing investment portfolios in City of London for major merchant banks and securities houses. Specialised in Emerging market funds.

Have a passion for investment properties, sale or purchase of business and higher end residential homes and estates. We also own and manage a property management company, www.hockingmanagement.com
