Anne Ashley's Summary

Stumped by a blank page? Need options? Dynamic Creative Services provider of custom print and web design for industries ranging from engineering, biotechnology, start up enterprises to industrial products. Design and produce custom work for your audience, from PowerPoint to trade shows, technical illustration and expert photo-retouching. Technical and inventive problem solver.

Design serves a purpose, and is evidence of a strategic advantage. I want to engage with participants who recognize the value of the idea well executed and its role in a successful enterprise. Strategic messaging and integrated communication is good business.

Custom visual content for print or web. Branding and identity, collateral, flash animation, illustration. Experience in hightech, biotech, energy, engineering, publishing, professional services. Working with you to achieve your business goals.
