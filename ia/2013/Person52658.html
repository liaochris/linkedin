Eric Hemati's Summary

I have been in sales and sales management since 1994, beginning as a door-to-door bookman to work my way through college. My primary specialty is helping startup ventures generate revenue in undeveloped territories within a 90-day period. My secondary specialty is mentoring sales managers and salespeople into top-level performers by establishing success habits that they will take with them into any future position.

Specialties: Vision Building, Branding and Identity, Recruiting, Sales Training, Group Development, Leadership Training

Software Sales

Sorcity.com

August 2001 – January 2003 (1 year 6 months)

Sorcity.com is an innovative solution that harnesses the power of the Internet to deliver buyers a much easier way to source, while giving them assurance they aren't paying above market prices. It provides sellers a very low cost means to gain new business and understand the competitive environment.

Sorcity.com does this through a Reverse-Auction environment, where buyers post what they need to buy with all their terms, then sellers can determine if they meet those needs and desire to bid through a competitive, open forum online. The goal is to eliminate waste and unnecessary cost and time that exists in today's procurement and sales cycle, and it has proven to work well for everyone involved.
