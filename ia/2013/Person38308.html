David Scales' Summary

I am a highly experienced technologist who independently manages and develops products for key clients. I have a valuable track record for delivering full-cycle award-winning Digital projects amongst organisational change and overloaded teams.

During my time as CTO of User37 I have been advising and developing for numerous organisations while based in London, including the Mayor of London, London 2012, Ofcom, NHS, EMC Worldwide and Digital Theatre.
