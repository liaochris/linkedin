Researcher

Danish Institute of Agricultural Sciences

March 1985 – May 2000 (15 years 3 months)

As a researcher, I addressed the need for a whole farm approach by developing an interactive decision support system for tactical planning in the dairy farm including feeding, crop rotation, and use of manure and milk quota restrictions. Competences in databases and programming evolved from this work and were later further developed by studies at Department of Computer Science, University of Aarhus.

I worked together with scientists involved in on-farm trials, as I provided these with interactive lap top based tools, which they used acting as advisers on the farm together with the farmer. Focus on user needs and usability issues were a must in this process, and I have ever since been the users advocate in the different ICT projects, in which I have participated in, so that now I masters a number of methods for improving usability.
