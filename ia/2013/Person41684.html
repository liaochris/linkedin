Scott Nelson's Summary

As an Enterprise Portal Architect I believe it is my mission to bring together robust application services and maintainable user interfaces that result in a satisfying user experience. I am an advocate for delivery team members having a work-life balance through improved processes and champion for the needs of users over the desire to find a quick fix while striving towards the end result of a more profitable and cost effective portal solution.

As a consultant I have consistently achieved the status of Trusted Advisor with technical and business decision makers by maintaining a user and customer centric focus in conjunction with a proactive approach to creating demonstrably beneficial solutions.

I have been designing, defining, developing, deploying and supporting web-based solutions since 1995 for companies ranging from home-based sole proprietors to Fortune 100 companies as well as local, state and Federal government agencies.
