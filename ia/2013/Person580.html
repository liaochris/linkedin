Aaron Morris' Summary

A civil litigation and trial attorney with extensive experience in State and Federal courts in California. Author of California SLAPP Law. Adjunct Professor at Whittier Law School and National University, teaching "Litigation Skills & Strategies". Writer and lecturer on the law, incuding Defamation, SLAPP, Constitutional Law and Employment Law. Featured speaker on law office organization and efficiency at such functions as the American Bar Association TechShow. Appeared on the national television shows "Leeza" and "Frontline." Frequently asked to consult other law firms on litigation issues and techniques. Retained expert witness on cases involving fee applications after anti-SLAPP motions.

Specialties

Trial attorney with emphasis on free speech issues, including defamation on and off the Internet. Expert on SLAPP law (a statute designed to dispose of lawsuits brought in an effort to discourage free speech or seeking redress). Current President of the California Defamation Lawyers Association. Other practice areas include business litigation, including breach of contract, trade secret, nondisclosure agreements, corporate or partnership dissolution, and employment law.
