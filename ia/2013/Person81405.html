Lisa Ann Schreier's Summary

Proven track record in writing, social media, communications, advertising, writing, advertising, marketing public speaking, guest service and sales.

15 years of successful work in advertising, copywriting, media buying/planning and promotional work for well known consumer electronics retailers and mid-sized advertising agencies in the Chicago area.

After 5 years experience in timeshare sales and sales management, founded a consumer education/consulting company dealing with timeshare. Author of 2 best-sellng consumer oriented books and co-author of college textbook, in addition to maintaining a blog that is considered a "must-read" for anyone interested in timeshare related issues.

Regular contributor to major media outlets and sought-after speaker to consumer groups, colleges and industry organizations.

Architect of International Timeshare Appreciation Day. November 1st of each year is dedicated to promoting timeshare in a non-selling, non-threatening way. Partnered with several timeshare organizations to get the word out, resulting in more than 800 members on the Facebook page within 3 weeks.

Appointed TATOC US Ambassador in July 2013.

Specialties: Timeshare consulting, communications, public speaking, writing, media relations, sales, advertising and marketing.

Lisa Ann Schreier's Publications

  • Surviving A Timeshare Presentation...Confessions From The Sales Table

    • 2004
    Authors: Lisa Ann Schreier

    Written for anyone attending a timeshare sales presentation or "ptich." Not an anti-timeshare book, but the first book that takes the mystery out of the product.

    Includes "Four Words To Avoid", "How To Check The Math" and "11 Things To Never Say."

  • Timeshare Vacations For Dummies

    • 2006
    Authors: Lisa Ann Schreier

    An in-depth look at the good, the bad, the myths and truths of timeshare. Includes sections on buying, trading, resources and updated information on sales pitches.

  • Timeshare Management-The Key Issues For Hospitality Managers

    • 2010
    Authors: Lisa Ann Schreier, Tammie Kaufman, Conrad Lashly

    The definitive college-level textbook on timeshare and vacation ownership.
