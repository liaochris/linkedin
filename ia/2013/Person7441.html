Andy Heys' Summary

Andy Heys is an Interim Manager / Director and Consultant with a Big 4 background, specialising in shared services, business turnaround and transformation across the Public and Private Sector. He has worked with over 100 of the best, and one or two of the worst organisations in the world. His experience crosses industries, continents, countries and cultures. He built his expertise in Shared Services, ICT and Contact Centres onshore and offshore as a consultant for Ernst and Young, Compass and PA Consulting. Andy is a frequent speaker on managing change and is the author of the book, "Influencing Change in Organisations".

Specialties: Change Management;Shared Services; Programme Management; Outsourcing; Benchmarking; Business Process Re-engineering; Benefits Realisation; Target Operating Model; Stakeholder Management; Prince 2; ITIL; MSP; Public Sector; Central Government; Local Government; Professional Services; and, Financial Services
