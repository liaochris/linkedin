Matthew Shapiro's Summary

Matthew Shapiro is President and Founder of MWS Capital LLC, and advocates for his clients and the growth of their wealth. He has shepherded client funds with a timeless approach through changing markets as their trusted adviser. Since founding MWS Capital in 2003, Matt has steadily grown accounts with a long term philosophy that focuses on our economy and high quality investments.

Since 2004, MWS Capital equity accounts have returned over 8% annually, besting the S&P 500 and Hedge Funds. Matt's optimism and eye for quality inspires investors with his positive growth style in long term holdings of select stocks, bonds and funds. While performance is important, Matt's first duty is to MWS Capital's clients as their adviser, many who were either bewildered by the financial landscape, or just jaded by the slick offerings of brokers and funds.

MWS Capital's core clients include prominent Chicagoans, professional couples, retirees and successful business owners. Matt is also exceptionally strong with successful and independent female clients who favor MWS Capital's personal approach. Most of all, Matt especially enjoys building long term accounts for everyday hard working people: the kitchen table investors who bring him some of the most satisfaction.

MWS Capital's story started with Matt's financial career as an Economics student at Rutgers College in the early nineties. Matt spent most of his time in the library absorbing the classic writings of Henry Clews, Walter Bagehot, and Philip Fisher well before the days of the internet. During this time he opened his first brokerage account at AG Edwards and eventually, Matt started a career as a stock options trader on the American Stock Exchange, then moved to Chicago to trade on the Chicago Board Options Exchange.

Mr. Shapiro's market views are often featured as a continuing guest on Bloomberg Television, the nationally syndicated show First Business, as well as both CBS and WGN talk radio in Chicago.
