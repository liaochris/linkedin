Jim Lewis' Summary

Hello!

I'm Jim Lewis from beautiful Dana Point, CA. I’ve been married for 25 years and have 3 great children ages 12, 17 and 20. We love baseball, the beach and wondering around Disneyland.

My personal interests are pretty simple: ANYTHING outdoors – ie. running, road biking, mountain biking, scuba diving, boating, jet skiing, dirt bike riding, swimming, trail running or tennis. I’ve competed in over 100 endurance races including many marathons and Ironman Triathons.

In November 1992, I left the traditional corporate world to start my own direct sales and marketing business, "JTL Promotions". Since then, I've worked with thousands of sales people and entrepreneurs from many industries. Through trial and error and simply being in the trenches we soon found what really works in today's world of selling and marketing.

During this time, I also personally built several international direct sales organizations, was my companies #1 income earner out of over 40,000 reps worldwide and a member of the "Millionaires Club" with two different companies.

Today, I mostly focus on consulting small business owners and entrepreneurs with sales training and marketing strategies through workshops, webinars and one-on-one business coaching.

My programs include prospecting with social media, 'being found' by your ideal customers on the internet, implementing mobile marketing, Facebook advertising, video strategies to dominate Google searches, advanced sales techniques and so much more.

If you want to sell more products and services in your business, I know how to make that happen using the internet.

How? I'm plugged in and work with some of the worlds BEST MARKETERS and internet "guru nerds" you could ever imagine. I live and breath this stuff every day, and bring this knowledge to my clients businesses to create market domination.
