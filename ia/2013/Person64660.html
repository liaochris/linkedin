J Michel Metz's Summary

High-tech companies have a problem: the ability to take the greatness of your company, products and people and get people as enthusiastic as you are. What you need is someone who is technical enough to understand the specifics and personable enough to communicate to all audiences - from the geekiest of the propellerheads to the "big picture" visionaries of C-level execs.

I am just such a person; I've worked with Fortune 100 companies developing and delivering product- and company- evangelism through a variety of media. Working with companies such as Apple, HP, Cisco, VMware and EMC to deliver product, technology and strategic vision in data center technologies.

What's next? Global domination! (Well, at least the global domain). I'm going to take my passion and talent to drive forward technology that I believe in and create a stronger global awareness and presence.
