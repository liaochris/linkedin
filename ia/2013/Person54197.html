Iain Aitchison's Summary

Iain is a Director at London-based product strategy consultancy Plan. After joining the company in 2006 he specialised in product experience strategy and social trends before leading the growth of Plan's consumer goods accounts.

Iain graduated with a first class BA(Hons) in Design from Glasgow School of Art, before going onto take a Master of Design at the Design Academy Eindhoven, The Netherlands. A Fellow of the Royal Society of Art, London he is also a visiting lecturer on Glasgow School of Art's Master of European Design programme.

He has consulted to clients including Lenovo, Samsung, Yamaha and many global FMCG brands.
