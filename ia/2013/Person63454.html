John Glynn's Summary

As a Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist (CMPS) with extensive experience in the mortgage industry, I specialize in helping my clients find, structure and manage mortgage debt as one piece of a larger financial strategy. I provide my clients with detailed home loan and debt structuring guidance that compliments their long term financial objectives, including college savings, retirement planning and other investment goals.

With an understanding of financial planning concepts that relate to taxation, investment strategy, and estate planning, I work to create a personal strategic mortgage solution for my clients’ diverse financial needs. I collaborate with advisors in other financial fields, and introduce other specialists for guidance as needed. The ability to assess and advise on all aspects of a client’s financial situation can minimize financing costs and tax exposure while building net worth – a benefit often missed when focusing on home loans exclusively.

As part of a holistic mortgage and financial planning solution, I offer a wide range of services customized to each clients’ financial needs. For homeowners, those looking for a first home and real estate investors alike, I focus on mortgage strategies that guide my clients through diverse and often challenging situations, including college planning, divorce and elder care.

I also provide a wide range of commercial services to Corporations, Realtors, Home Builders and Financial Planners, including:
• Realtor Education Workshops
• Homeowner and credit management workshops
• Builder Preferred lender solutions
• Financial planning workshops (FPA Board of Standards accredited)
• Employee real estate benefit plans
