Dennis Stone's Summary

Enterprise web implementation for clients including marchFIRST, J D’Addario & Company, Nuveen Investments, Stellato & Schwartz, Los Nopales Restaurant, OrderZone (W.W. Grainger Company), MyFootPath, Crag Burn Golf Club and DePaul University College of Law. As the web manager at DePaul University College of Law, my role is to build and execute strategies for the college that engages the community via the digital landscape.

I am a highly motivated, self-starter that excels at planning and executing strategic projects, developing and leading cohesive teams, as well as facilitating effective collaboration. In my spare time I instruct web development courses at DePaul University College of Digital Media and currently consult with organizations on how to best increase their digital footprint.

Key strengths include user experience, website development, information architecture, digital design, production & delivery of projects. Manage and mentor team members on various projects to ensure that projects remain on-strategy, on-time and on-budget.

Specific experience includes leading cross-functional design, content and development teams for various digital projects, management of interactive marketing projects for web & social media initiatives planning.

Functional activities include website development, information architecture, wireframing, interaction design, and project management. Project management responsibilities include day-to-day project planning and managing project delivery, resource and staffing planning.

Specialties: website development, user experience, information architecture, visual design, interaction design and database design.
