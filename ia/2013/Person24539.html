Christopher Rodriguez's Summary

I really enjoy selling real estate in beautiful Southern California. My firm has developed and implemented one of the best marketing and sales platforms available. Our system both tailors to sellers trying to save money on real estate commissions and active buyers who would like the best possible price!

Specialties - Home Loans( In house lending), Home Sales and Assisting homeowners with cost free options to avoid foreclosure.. Making a decision to do a short sale is never easy but making the decision to avoid foreclosure is! We have a loan center and specialize in VA financing.

Certifications and Awards- Combat Veteran and have been awarded the Purple Heart for injuries suffered in Fallujah, Iraq.

Interests - Kick Boxing, Fishing, Hiking, Free diving and most important spending time with my family

About Me- I was born in Chicago Illinois and after high school joined the United States Marine Corps. After serving 2 tours in Iraq and suffering injuries in combat I was Honorably discharged after 4 years and I decided to take on the Real Estate and Lending profession full time. Since 2006 I have managed our office and have assisted Buyers and sellers achieve their goals. I enjoy what I do and thank God I am blessed to do it. I live in Oceanside with my wife and large family of 6 children. I am well known for my negotiation skills specifically in the short sale and loan settlement field. To date I have successfully negotiated over 300 short sale approvals and loan settlements. I have an amazing team behind me and we strive to ethically and morally achieve our goals. Integrity, professionalism, dependability and teamwork are the backbone of our working environment..

Specialties

Residential home sales, Short Sale Negotiations, Real Estate Investments, VA/FHA Mortgages and conventional financing, Hard Money and Private Money.
