Fred Caballero's Summary

I'm a @Techhub member based at Google Campus London, currently co-founder, CEO of Bizpora: a platform that helps you meet the right people in your next business trip, highlighting your extended network and making it easy for you to reach out.

In 2008, I co-founded Channelship, a web & social media agency in Dublin and London.

I lectured at the Institute of Digital Marketing (theidm.com), The Digital Marketing Institute (DMI) in London and guest-lectured at Dublin Business School.

In 2011 I participated as a CNN analyst for the Technology show, "Clix".

I have co-founded Bloggertone.com (Rebranded as Tweakyourbiz.com): a space that allows people in business to access and share useful business information and opinions, constantly updated by the collaborative effort of selected business bloggers.
