Kevin Temple's Summary

ESG works with CSO's who are in high growth or recovery scenarios and recognize the potential for sales leadership and sales methodology to improve their situation. I've delivered impressive results to a variety of companies and my combination of sales methodology and leadership models have a 100% success rate. (While most sales training only companies fail to deliver measurable results 85% of the time.) This approach combines the critical sales methodology applications that will benefit your productivity with the leadership plan required to accrue the results. I have engaged many opportunities on a pay for performance basis which I recommend to ensure we are both on the same agenda.

I started this journey when I was a sales leader at Cadence. We were faced with a retracting business and had to lead the sales team from a disastrous year back to 35% growth within a few quarters. The largest deal sizes increased 5X, discounts dropped dramatically, and our services revenue grew from $2M to over $93M that year.

Here are some additional examples of my consulting projects:

Imprivata: Transactions/rep increased 20%, services bookings 300%, ACV 19%, for an overall growth rate of 47% during the height of the recession. See video below.

Jive Software: 32% increase in average contract value (ACV).

Good Technology: 250% increase in average contract value within 90 days.

IP Infusion: 89% increase in ACV.

WindRiver: 19% ACV while selling against a free linux option.

Cisco: 300% increase in pipeline outside of IT sponsorship.

Dell: 26% increase in their attach rate for servers, storage and services. Now a $14B business

Tom Brigiotta (below) from Imprivata talks about the results I helped his team achieve... during the height of the recession. He said it was the most fun he's had as a sales leader. Perhaps I can help you put the fun back into your sales organization!

If you're serious about generating results, then call me for a dialog.

We see the implementation through to success with reviews, coaching and mid-course corrections where required. Our model is successful 100% of the time, and we prefer shared risk/reward engagements. We put our skin in the game because you did when your took on your assignment.
