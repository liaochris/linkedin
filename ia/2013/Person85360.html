Marc Meyer's Summary

I've focused on Social Media and Digital solutions for the last five years. Over the last 11+ years, I've been using digital and search strategies, as well as content marketing strategies in an effort to enhance the digital marketing, e-commerce & CPG fortunes for Fortune 500's as well as SMB's. These efforts focused on the areas of lead generation, brand awareness, buzz, content marketing, reputation management, and thought leadership.

I am the co-founder of a 2 year old old Twitter based tweetchat community that focuses weekly on the business innovations of social media. I had previously created and moderated a brand based community with over 5000 members.

Prior to, I've held positions ranging from web developer and manager, internet marketing specialist, software sales rep., & sales manager, to positions of VP of sales & marketing, Director. of Internet Marketing, Director. of Social Media and Search Engine Marketing, to Director. of e-commerce.

I've humbly been mentioned as one of the top social media thought leaders on Twitter, one of the top social media strategists to watch in 2010 & 2011 and I was nominated as one of the top 100 internet marketers of 2009.
