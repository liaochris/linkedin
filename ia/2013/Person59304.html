Jason Kraft's Summary

Jason Kraft (jason.kraft@gmail.com)

I'm an information technology analyst with expert experience in SAP Business Warehouse and SAP Portals, specializing in front-end web interface design for analytical reports and dashboards, BW performance optimization, back-end data warehouse management, and query design. I'm also an independent web designer and technology consultant.

My wife and I started a new business venture in 2008 offering allergy-friendly pastries (nut-free, egg-free, dairy-free, gluten-free, vegan) to the SF Bay Area. We sold the business in 2011 when we moved to southern California. Check it out at http://www.allergyfriendlypastries.com.

I also wrote a book for SAP Press about the NetWeaver 7.1 Business Warehouse system, with an emphasis on front-end web application development, workbook design, and query design: http://www.sap-press.com/products/SAP-NetWeaver-BW-7.x-Reporting-%E2%80%94-Practical-Guide.html

Specialties: SAP Business Warehouse (BW), Tableau, Business Intelligence, SAP Portals, HTML, CSS, ASP, JavaScript, VBScript, graphic design (Photoshop), interface design for usability, computer networking, database systems (SQL Server, MS Access, Oracle, data modeling), Windows and PC hardware troubleshooting, UNIX knowledge
