Jon Coifman's Summary

Jon Coifman has provided successful strategic communications and public affairs counsel on issues and ideas for corporate, non-profit, and government organizations, helping all sides navigate the intersection where private enterprise meets public interest. He has deep specialization in energy, environment and clean technology, as well as a a broad background in economics, legal affairs and public policy.

His specialties include:
