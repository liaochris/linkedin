Mazarine Treyz's Summary

I love to teach and write. I’m passionate about teaching people how to change the world.

I'm the author of the Wild Woman's Guide to Social Media, published October, 2012. This book got a 5 star rating at Nonprofits.About.com.

I also wrote The Wild Woman’s Guide to Fundraising, published November, 2010, and named one of the top 10 nonprofit books of 2010 by Beth Kanter, nonprofit blogger and CEO of Zoetica.

In April 2013 I wrote Get the Job! Your Fundraising Career Empowerment Guide.

Specialties: Professional speaking, Public Speaking, Keynote, Workshop Presenting, Training, Webinars, Digital Marketing, Web 2.0, Writing, #nptech, #fundraising, events, copywriting, e-newsletters, grantwriting, corporate communications, art, arts marketing, sponsorship, strategic partnerships, Lean applied to marketing and fundraising, blogging, branding, encaustic art, social media

Mazarine Treyz's Publications

  • The Wild Woman's Guide to Fundraising

    • November 18, 2010
    Authors: Mazarine Treyz

    The Wild Woman’s Guide to Fundraising is an engaging, fun book which teaches you how to make your passion for a cause turn into profit. Topics include; Fundraising in a Downturn, Dealing with Conflict in the Workplace, Net-raising, Generational and Cultural Differences in Fundraising, the Lean Development Office, and more. Also includes practical, step by step how to chapters for each fundraising task, and a resource CD which is like a fundraising office in a box.

    If you care about a cause or nonprofit, you need this book.

  • The Wild Woman's Guide to Social Media

    • October 20, 2012
    Authors: Mazarine Treyz

    The Wild Woman's Guide to Social Media will help you get the attention your business or cause deserves. Two years in the making, this hands-on, how to book helps you learn how to create a social media presence that rocks, and how to measure your results too! Available at http://wildsocialmedia.com

  • Get The Job! Your Fundraising Career Empowerment Guide

    • April 19, 2013
    Authors: Mazarine Treyz

    Do you want to have a wildly successful fundraising career? Start here.

    How to create a compelling cover letter that gets results
    How to relate unrelated experience on your resume.
    How to ask good interview questions to find out if your boss will be a bully.
    Case studies of how people stepped into leadership roles at nonprofits.
    The three conversations you MUST have with your boss to be successful.
    How to negotiate for a higher nonprofit salary, AND MORE!
