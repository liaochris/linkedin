Debra Desmond, SPHR's Summary

Debra Desmond is a certified Executive Coach, HR/OD Consultant, Leadership Development professional Trainer/Facilitator, and Public Speaker

As an Executive Coach, Debra is a sounding board and Trust Advisor to corporate and entrepreneurial executives and middle managers. She offers insight and perspective into the behavioral and developmental shifts required in interpersonal, relational aspects of clients' professional and personal lives. Debra’s work helps clients deepen their self awareness and identify clear developmental goals that enhance the value in their roles. This involves facilitating conversations and interpreting clients’ suggestions about what and how to change while also encouraging the discipline needed to sustain it positively. Debra engages executives in powerful conversations to improve organizations’ competitive advantage by helping them improve their own leadership capacity, deepening their learning, resulting in decisive decision making and compelling movement. Clients state they experience the deepest appreciation of themselves from the various assessments Debra is certified to administer. Here they view their natural competing conflicts and sources of stress that limit them. Executives who have worked with Debra have solved problems by strengthening their emotional intelligence competencies, built and repaired relationships, prioritized, dealt with burnout, made career transitions, and pushed projects to breakthrough levels.

This methodology has also made Debra an in demand Career Coach who helps executive and middle management clients develop a plan of action to find the job they want and determine how to land it. She is the Executive Coach Writer of articles for Examiner.com as well as an Expert Author of Ezine articles on the subject of leadership.
