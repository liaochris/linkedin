Dion Herrera, PharmD's Summary

My focus is to: a) empower patients (or care-givers) to be proactive and responsible for managing their medications and health, and b) assist physicians on patient-specific medication therapy issues.

The outcomes of this dual-focus is to minimize patient losses (health and financial), and to free up physician "face time" to see other patients (increase revenue). This approach also creates a "ripple" effect which benefits third-party payors by reducing their patient coverage costs (increases their ROI).

At the core of our services is a current patient health history and medication therapy review which is then compared to the physician's care plan. By collaborating with the physician in fine-tuning a patient's medications and recommending adjustments in lifestyle (if applicable), we aim to help people live longer and healthier.

Specialties

I am nationally certified in Immunization Delivery and Medication Therapy Management. In addition, I hold patient-care certifications in Diabetes and Cardiovascular disease state management therapies with an additional license as a Consultant Pharmacist in the state of Florida.
These certifications have prepared me to provide each patient with a personalized health-care experience tailored for their lifestyle and benefit.
