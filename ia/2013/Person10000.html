Justin Jenkins' Summary

With over 25 years experience as an Artist and Illustrator, I have a broad range of knowledge that carries over into any creative project. From illustrating books to my private collections of paintings and drawings, my passion for the process of creating stems from my curiosity about life and the need for me to analyze with my mind and make these visions a reality with the help of my spirit and soul as the magic behind the thought. I have been drawn to art since childhood and began showing in galleries at the age of 10, where I had my first sale of a lithograph. Since then, my ambitious pursuit of excellence in my own work and evolving as an artist has led me to a project called "Genesis" where I want to create 144 works of art in 12 unique collections.

Specialties: Illustration, Drawing, Painting, Mixed Media, Sculpture, Portraits, Posters, Business Consulting and Development, Writing.
