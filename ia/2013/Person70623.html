Julian Tremaud's Summary

I'm a freelance Digital Manager and Web Designer.

I've been handling all Digital Promotions for London-based record label Visible Noise and music management company Wiseblood Management since 2008. I also help promote live events for companies such as AEG Live (Backstreet Boys, Guns n' Roses, Avicii, J Lo), Virtual Festivals (Glastonbury 2011, Beach Break Live 2011, Creamfields 2011), and festivals such as Sonisphere Festival UK and Bloodstock Festival.

I have experience in database-driven Web Design, Search Engine Optimization, eCommerce and Promotions, Email Marketing, online Advertising (Google, Youtube, Facebook, etc.), Social Network Management, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), etc.
