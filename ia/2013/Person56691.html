Jake Dreyfuss' Summary

An entrepreneur at heart, Jacob is passionate about project/product management, sales and marketing management, user interface/user experience design, QA testing & has experienced the entire "start-up" life cycle from conception to acquisition.

Balancing experiences in the scrappy atmosphere of growing a young tech company are Jacob's experiences launching new markets with a $4 billion dollar publicly traded home-builder and breaking area sales records with one of Washington DC's top real estate consulting firms.
