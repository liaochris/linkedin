Jennifer Reynolds' Summary

I started my company, Sauce Goddess, because I just loved cooking for others and sharing good food. It has always been a dream of mine to take that to the next level and make unique and tasty food available to more than just my immediate circle of friends and family. Sauce Goddess Gourmet sauces and spices let me do just that.

Sauce Goddess is growing like crazy right now. We expanded into Northern California, the Pacific Northwest with regional distribution. We now have distribution in the Midwest, East Coast and Florida through a national natural foods distributor, UNFI.

In the future, a cookbook and doing a lot of in store appearances via what I call the "Sauce Goddess World Tour".

Specialties: Promotion, sales, culinary arts, cooking videos, cook books.
