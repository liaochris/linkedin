Elaine Quinn's Summary

As an organizing, time management and productivity consultant, my advice and guidance have helped my clients organize their offices, desks and files, set goals and priorities, and increase productivity in their workspaces and workdays.

Through 25 years experience in Fortune 100 companies, in sales, training and marketing management positions, I developed strong skills in productivity, organization and time management as well as the ability to guide others. Two years as a volunteer consultant with the Small Business Administration (SCORE) helped me translate these skills to small and micro businesses.

I serve on the Board of Directors of the National Association of Professional Organizers - Chicago Chapter. I am an active member of the Lincoln Park Chamber of Commerce and on the leadership team of the National Association of Women Business Owners - Chicago Chapter.

I hold the Certified Professional Organizer® credential, earned by only 5% of professional organizers worldwide

Specialties

I write for, speak about, and provide consulting services to small business owners and work-from-home professionals to help them get organized, stay motivated, and get things done.
