Cindy Moore, VP TrainRX's Summary

We've been in the business of healthcare compliance for 10+ years. We've never seen anything that will impact healthcare more than ICD-10. It's the biggest change in Healthcare in 30 years!

→ The unknowns of transitioning to ICD-10 can be stressful for providers - Education is the KEY!

Our team is traveling the country educating:
→ Members of some of the most prestigious Medical Associations and Societies
→ Healthcare Organizations - from a small Rural hospital setting to a large hospital system, we're assisting Physician Liaisons in engaging the Physicians NOW for a positive outcome
→ Private Practices large and small appreciate EDUCATION - They once thought ICD-10 was just a coding and IT issue. We've given them the tools they need to meet the deadline of Oct. 1, 2014 We are saving them thousands of dollars!!

Today, we are ICD-10 all the time and we are experts! If you are behind schedule during this difficult transition, we have the tools, resources and share partners to meet every need you have when it comes to the ICD-10 transition.

We are passionate about Healthcare! Our Executive team has 40+ combined years of experience in the healthcare arena.

→ We work closely with Associations, Hospitals, Private Practices and more to bring them practical solutions.

→ Thousands of Practice Managers across America are using the trainRX ICD-10 Project Manager Guide to walk them through Phase 1 of ICD-10.
