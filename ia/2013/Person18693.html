Brian Schaeffer, CISSP's Organizations

  • Philadelphia Infragard

    President
    • August 2003 to Present

    InfraGard is an information sharing and analysis effort serving the interests and combining the knowledge base of a wide range of members. At its most basic level, InfraGard is a cooperative undertaking between the U.S. Government (led by the FBI and the NIPC) and an association of businesses, academic institutions, state and local law enforcement agencies, and other participants dedicated to increasing the security of United States critical infrastructures.

    All InfraGard participants are committed to the proposition that a robust exchange of information about threats to and actual attacks on these critical infrastructures is an important element for successful infrastructure protection efforts.

  • Philadelphia Regional Computer Intrusion Task Force (PRCITF)

    Chief Architect / Board Member
    • February 2008 to Present

    The PRCITF is a joint effort between the FBI and Defense Criminal Investigative Service (DCIS) charged with the implementation and operations of the Computer Intrusion Threat Assessment System (CITAS). CITAS is an early warning and intrusion detection system that enables law enforcement to be proactive in identifying the persistent cyber threats targeting our national infrastructure.

    May 2010 – Nominated by the FBI, and earned the Philadelphia Federal Executive Board “2010 Excellence in Government” Gold Medal award for Group Private Sector Involvement by volunteering to “provide expert technical assistance to create a fully operational system”.

  • Cyber Incident Detection and Data Analysis Center (CIDDAC)

    Chairman / Organizing Board Member
    • June 2004 to March 2006

    CIDDAC is an early-warning network of companies that anonymously share information about intrusion attempts to their computer networks. The CIDDAC network also provides a mechanism for law enforcement to analyze the data collected to create threat profiles in an effort to hinder future cyber crime.
