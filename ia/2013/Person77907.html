Konstantina Zoehrer's Summary

Konstantina Zoehrer is a social innovation/social entrepreneurship practitioner and researcher. Currently she is building with a multidisciplinary team 180º, a R&D social impact venture which is implementing social innovation solutions for socioeconomic development.

Konstantina is specializing on unemployment- employment, social integration issues with a strong focus on informal structures and is participating frequently in public policy working groups and as a contributing editor in various publications. She is a strong believer in entrepreneurial mindset and bridging bottom-up to top-down techniques.

Before her entrepreneurial journey, Konstantina worked as a communications consultant for various organizations and businesses, with a focus on connecting online to offline and stakeholder engagement.

 She is a member of WEF Global Shapers Athens Hub, where she serves as a deputy curator for social entrepreneurship since April 2013, and a member of Sandbox Network.

Recently she started also working on her research on public policy frameworks for social entrepreneurship with a special interest for informal structures.
