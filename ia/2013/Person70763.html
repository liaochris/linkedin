Julie Mann, MSIR, SPHR, CCP's Experience

Vice President of Human Resources

Webasto Thermo & Comfort North America, Inc.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry

October 2012 – Present (1 year) fenton, mi

As Vice President, Julie utilizes a business partner model to work with the Executive Team and German Headquarters to translate business strategy and present / future goals into HR actions. Whereas Julie has responsibility for all facets of HR such as benefits, payroll and employee relations, Julie seeks to add maximum value through her ability to attract, acquire, deploy, retain, develop and motivate talent which is a key strategic focus and paramount to organizational success. In any given moment, you will find Julie pondering such questions as...How do we define talent? Are we surrounded by the absolute best talent? How are we sure? How do we retain / develop our talent? And of most importance, how can HR add value as a strategic business partner vs. an administrative overhead function.

Webasto was founded in 1901, continues to be privately held, and employs more than 10,000 employees worldwide in over 46 countries.

Webasto is the global leader in idle reduction technologies for the transportation industry. Webasto’s technologies are designed specifically for vehicles used in nearly all segments, including passenger cars, cargo vehicles, off-highway equipment, school and transit buses, defense vehicles, commercial trucks, and recreational vehicles. Webasto’s engine-off technologies such as fuel operated heaters and cooling systems help companies save millions of dollars annually on fuel costs, thus reducing dependency on foreign oil. In addition, they play a significant role in reducing emissions (Green House Gases) globally, protecting public health, and creating job opportunities. Finally, operator comfort, whether in a commercial vehicle or a personal vehicle, is greatly increased with the use of Webasto pre-heaters, supplemental heaters, and cooling systems.

CEO & President

JMann Consulting Group, LLC

Public Company; Myself Only; Human Resources industry

January 2009 – October 2012 (3 years 10 months) Lansing, Michigan Area

Julie was the CEO & President of three companies which she founded after a successful career in Corporate Human Resources: JMann Consulting Group, The Rock Star Factory and in January 2012 launched a non-profit organization, Rock Star Warriors.

JMann Consulting Group provides on-call HR Manager Services for small & medium sized businesses, while The Rock Star Factory was a professional placement firm specializing in matching companies with rock star quality employees. Rock Star Warriors was formed to act as a conduit to work with our veterans to transition their skills acquired through their military service to the skills companies are craving in today’s workplace to create their competitive advantage to get jobs and have sustainable careers. The S.T.A.R. for us = Sustainable Talent And Retention!

Julie is a past statewide radio show host of, "Talk About People", which aired on the Michigan Business Network (www.michiganbusinessnetwork.com) six times weekly. Additionally, Julie was a weekly panel expert on 1240AM on the Mid-Michigan Business Rap, hosted by Michael Patrick Shiels. Julie hopes to return to the radio very soon as she loves sharing her HR knowledge with others!

Also, Julie regularly authors articles and is a repeat speaker for Michigan HR Day and is a sought after speaker on Human Resources/People and Veteran Employment topics across the State.

JMann companies realize that for many small and midsize businesses, the fight to stay on top can be won or lost in the Human Resources Department. These companies need to attract and retain the best and brightest employees whose decision making may boil down to their Human Resources practices. We also know that if you have employees/people, you have people questions and need HR help - we are here to be your HR Manager On Call!

CEO

Rock Star Warriors

February 2012 – September 2012 (8 months) Lansing, Michigan Area

With Julie as CEO, Michigan Veterans Law and The Rock Star Factory, two Michigan based companies have joined forces, forming a non-profit entity called RockStar Warriors.

RockStar Warriors works with our veterans to transition their skills acquired through their military service to the skills companies are craving in today’s workplace to create their competitive advantage. Our veterans have performed a service to us and to our country selflessly and heroically and RockStar Warriors will provide them with the services they now need to enter and successfully compete in today’s workforce.

We continue to hear from employers that companies create competitive advantages through their people yet finding the right skills for their job openings is not always as easy as it appears. This competitive advantage which is robust in the state of Michigan because of the high number of unemployed veterans, presents employers with a job seeking population that brings vast amounts of skills such as loyalty, work ethic, leadership, teamwork and the ability to exceed goals as they have done for our country.

RockStar Warriors will be providing our veterans with the ammo needed to translate and communicate their military skills and experiences to the skills that companies are seeking today. Veterans, equipped with employability skills and job seeking tools, will also create the competitive advantage for our veterans to successfully be able to tackle the job market which is critical to finding and keeping the job.

Through creating Veteran Friendly Employer Alliances, RockStar Warriors will also seek to clear the pathway for our hiring companies to connect with our veterans who are trained and ready to attack the job seeking market and bring their elite military skills to the workplace.
