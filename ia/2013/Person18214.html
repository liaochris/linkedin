Bruce Black's Summary

Provides bespoke editorial and marketing services for clients selling online, ensuring quality and consistency in search engine shopping channels.

Create and publish customer-focused profiles that really sell products and encourage retailers to participate and build and publish customer recommendations.

Our clients are manufacturers and distributors of premium products focused upon delivering a consistent brand online and determined to optimise sales and share of this lucrative market. We only ever work with a single client in a market sector and pride ourselves on delivering competitive advantage and higher sales levels.

Extensive experience in publishing communications, the last 10 years as a senior partner in BCB&, the award winning communications agency.

Experienced in coaching senior management to successfully present complex content in an easily assimilated style. Managing and producing communication projects including magazines, newsletters, websites and digital content for large and small organisations. Development of print publications to interactive online and tablet formats. Writing for a wide range industries, groups and ages including science and technology, automotive, sport, charities and management.

Measurable track record of managing multiple projects concurrently and high impact multi-stakeholder communications through a wide range of channels. As a credible and diplomatic influencer, I present facts and data to gain the buy-in of others, leading to successful outcomes. A skilled relationship builder I develop strong and long-lasting alliances with internal and external stakeholders to maximise not only on-going organisational directives but new business development strategies.
