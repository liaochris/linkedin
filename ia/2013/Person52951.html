Hevan Chan's Summary

As a designer and an art director, I have over 10 years of experience working within a wide range of mediums such as print, web, video and motion graphics. My body of work includes creating the identity for the Transmission Music Festival in Austria, redesigning the MoneyGram International website, constructing complex information graphics for litigation court cases and working with Apple and Adobe to develop training videos which enable users to successfully learn their new products.

As an illustrator and photographer, I have explored the traditional and digital formats of each. Combining my photography and custom illustrations, I worked with the acclaimed graffiti artist and teacher, Pose2, to create a short animated film educating his students on the aesthetics of graffiti. The success of this piece awarded Pose2 numerous educational grants to further his commitment to the arts.

One of my favorite hobbies is to construct audio mixes out of my ever-growing library of music. My "SoulBlends" assemble memories, thoughts and ideas into one composition which bring out my creativity and inspiration.

I bring an innovative and original approach to any project and believe the more the challenging the task, the greater the end result. I am a dedicated artist who consistently maintains high standards for my work and strives to surround myself with like-minded individuals.
