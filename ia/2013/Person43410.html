Evan Matteson's Summary

The Matteson Group are your trusted real estate advisors providing a joy filled experience in the Rockwall, Heath Texas and surrounding Lake Ray Hubbard communities.

As full-time real estate agents with backgrounds in commercial real estate management, small business development, marketing and interior design we are dedicated to a high level of service and professionalism. We understand that buying or selling your home is one of the most important decisions you make. We will create a partnership with you to help the whole process run as smoothly as possible.

Selling and buying a home used to mean a short contract with a few signatures. No more. The process of pricing a home right, marketing it to qualified buyers, ordering inspections, assistance with financing, negotiating the sale and completing the transaction has become highly complicated.

The Matteson Group not only engages our market knowledge and real estate expertise but also uses the superior resources and expertise of Coldwell Banker® Apex, Realtors. Out of the top producing real estate companies in the Dallas Metroplex, Coldwell Banker® Apex is consistently rated #1 in volume of sales in our market areas. We believe you will find these two together are the best possible team available working in your interest.
