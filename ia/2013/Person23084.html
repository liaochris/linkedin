Christopher Bishop's Summary

Chris Bishop is a Denver-based service provider of Sound Design and Live Sound Engineering. He currently resides in Denver, CO in pursuit of a Masters of Science in Recording Arts degree (IP). He currently mixes live sound, provides freelance sound design and serves at a local church as Technical Director, overseeing all audio/visual needs. He has recently served as a Freelance Offline Editor for Orion Multimedia, editing footage for 20-minute television series episodes, "Conceal & Carry School".

His goals include serving as Supervising Sound Editor on numerous films, serving as a Live Sound Engineer for artists nationwide, as well as editing full-length television and film for professional display.
