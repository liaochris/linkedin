Ayelet Baron's Summary

Ayelet is an Instigator, Author, Speaker and Consultant. She is an expert in strategy, change management, employee engagement and business partnerships. Ayelet works with leaders to transform their organizations. If you need a speaker, a master in implementing strategy and organizational change or a facilitator, Ayelet is your person. She has a unique gift for executing strategy through people and an uncanny ability to identify business problems and design results-oriented strategic plans. Her most recent projects include designing and executing strategic frameworks for the integration of communities. She recently wrote Getting Unstuck and is working on a book on strategy and trusted relationships. She wrote a chapter on Social Media and the Generations in Fuse and another on Business Strategy and Communications in the Handbook for Organizational Communication.

Ayelet was the Chief Strategist, Cisco Canada and helped position Canada as the second largest revenue generating country for Cisco. Prior to that she was on the executive team of Cisco’s Emerging Markets, and was also the chief strategist for Cisco's global mobile business. As a Cisco Leadership Fellow, she worked with 21 of the largest NGOs and developed a social networking handbook for social good. She works with youth in Africa on how to change their destiny by using social media tools and is on the board of goal4.org

Ayelet Baron's Projects

  • Underemployment Initiative

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Ayelet Baron, Tina Kapoor, Javier Vanga, , Rashad Said, Dann Bergman

    “To bridge the gap between high growth jobs and underutilized workers by identifying a ready
    pool of talent, relevant jobs and relevant training programs, while at the same time, fostering
    Board engagement and leveraging resources to design a sustainable training model.”

    The Pilot implementation working group is formed of representatives from work2future Staff, ReyLabs, SkillSoft, San Jose City College, San Jose State University, SRS Consulting, Business Process and Communication Strategy professionals.

    The group is focused on mid-level skills in ICT and Business professional services job market and skill set upgrades, certificates and high performing learning service providers. The goal is identify "narrow and focused" ICT and Business skills and Provide access to up-to-date career paths, Success coaches, Practical experience and Recommendation to recruiters.

  • Getting Unstuck

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Ayelet Baron

    Getting Unstuck is divided into sections that can be read in whatever order you want. There are no rules and no page numbers. There are many links that will take you to sources of information.
    Unlearn talks about why connections, conversations and trusted relationships are increasingly important. Unlearning focuses on shedding old ways that are no longer relevant.
    Unstick looks at what gets in our way and why we may need to choose to do the hard work.
    Unleash discusses the world of abundance and our opportunity to unleash our voice and make our ideas a reality. It’s about creating what matters to you.
    Be is about making a conscious decision to work on that hard problem we want to solve. If you feel you are not stuck, you may already be there.
    Getting Unstuck with Seth Godin is a summary of the wisdom Seth shared with us and includes some of his insights to problems workshop participants shared.
