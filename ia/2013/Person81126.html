Linda V. Hanson's Summary

Linda Hanson has over 25 years of Business Management experience. As the Senior Executive Assistant at Columbia Distributing she worked closely with Ed Maletis, his team of executives, board members, legal counsel, venture capitalists as well as banking partners during a time of mergers, acquisitions and rapid growth. Linda also played a critical role on the Software Implementation Team introducing tools which greatly enhanced the ability to satisfy customers as well as suppliers while improving sales and inventory management. Even though she already possessed a Bachelor of Arts degree, in 2002 Linda completed another Bachelors Degree in Business Management while performing the rigorous duties of her job at Columbia. The coursework included Global Business Strategies, Marketing Research, Project Management, Quality Management and Productivity, Financial Analysis and Statistics. Linda is currently the Director of Operations for The Merlo Foundation and Corporation,and Sales & Marketing Coordinator for the Merlo Corporation/Merlo Foundation and Lago di Merlo Family of brands. This is a privately held organization owning multiple businesses including the 250 acre Lago di Merlo Vineyard in California’s Dry Creek Appellation as well as the Lago di Merlo and Ca’ Bella Wine brands. Working beside Harry Merlo Sr., his Director of Finance, legal counsel and other staff members she continues to utilize her exceptional organizational skills and keen eye for detail to ensure all business and personal transactions are handled effectively, efficiently and confidentially.

Specialties: Competitive brand and sales analysis, mergers and acquisitions
Non-profits, real estate, compliance
