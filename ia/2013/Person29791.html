Daniel McDeavitt's Summary

I am currently working as an Assistant to the Dean in the College of Arts & Sciences of Indiana University, specifically in the production of the Fall 2013 Themester.
http://themester.indiana.edu/
---
Objective: Seeking full time position creating, crafting, and implementing content for a video game’s overall narrative content.
------------------
Specialties:
- Unity3D 4
- Adobe CS6
- Gaming Systems
- Heuristic Evaluation
- Narrative Design
- Production
------------------
I am geek…and proud of it. I have table-topped, LaRP’d, MMO’d, mini’d, etc. I have participated in many other forms of gaming that still continue to excite me, both personally and professionally. My goal is to be engaged and have fun in everything I do.

In 1996 I completed my unique, self-designed undergraduate entitled “Multimedia Production Design”. The degree was made possible via Indiana University’s College of Arts and Sciences Individualized Major Program, that let me adapt my original Graphic Design degree into a focus on the emerging new medias in 1994, (specifically the internet, computer based education, and game design.)

I have been attending the annual Game Developer’s Conference since 1995 and have been on the volunteer staff since the GDC at Long Beach, California in 1998. (Ask me about the experience running logistics for the party on the Queen Mary … as a conversation starter.)

Over the years, I have worked as a webmaster, designer, and media instructor for web management, heuristic design, & graphic media. I have even designed a pilot curriculum for gaming and animation program. And in my spare time, I have also written dialog scripts for games, television, radio, and motion pictures, and video productions.

Detailed information, portfolio, and more @ http://danielmcdeavitt.com
