Christian Petrou's Summary

"Every company has the potential to be world class. Technology should be affordable, evening the playing field and empowering every employee to become proactive in the business and no longer reactive to technology."

Christian E. Petrou is the founder and CEO of RVNUE Technologies (est. 2008) a full service technology company with its core business in revenue based business solutions via managed hosting and cloud computing. Christian received the Mobotix 2013 NPC (National Partner Conference) Project of the Year Award for the IPTV Surveillance Solution at the Las Vegas Luxury Hi-Rise Panorama Towers also showcased at the 2013 ISC West Security Tour and in the Las Vegas Sun / Vegas Magazine. Integrating on premise Virtual Desktop Infrastructure, Private Cloud Servers, Suprema Biometric Access Control, Distributed WiFi, Central Plant Building & Life Safety Intelligence, Christian has been sited in press releases, case studies and news media as an expert consultant in his industry.

Prior to these ventures Christian was a key member of the elite nightlife management group; The Light Group. With The Light Group, Christian managed and focused on business automation, unified communications, mobile client relationship management systems and VIP host development. Contributing to his project and personnel management success was Christian’s experience from his management role in the U.S. Broadband company from its humble beginnings in 2000 to a one hundred plus work force of broadband technicians within two years locally contracted with Cox Communications.

Christian attended Grossmont / Cuyamaca College in San Diego and Texas Tech University. Christian was vital in the transition of Grossmont College Security force to the California P.O.S.T. Certified G.C.C.P.D. Campus Police Department. Training director of incoming emergency dispatchers and community service officers, Christian Petrou held the position of Public Safety Agent.
