I am a web developer and frontend engineer with a particular interest in Web Standards, usability, accessibility and Agile methodology.

After completing my Bachelor Degree in Computer Science and Management Skills, I spend the following seven years working as a Java developer / consultant / lead for firms such as Cambridge Technology Partners, Thoughtworks and ING (Amsterdam). I worked for many high-profile clients such as Telstra, Pacific Access (Sensis), ANZ and Nationale Nederlanden and also spent some time as a contractor in Amsterdam.
