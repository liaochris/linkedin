C.J. Johnson's Summary

I have made a living entertaining people since 1986. OK, admittedly, people probably weren't that entertained in '86 - or even part of '87, but I worked hard and I'm pretty sure they were after June or July of '87!

I have performed as a juggler, magician, hypnotist and game show host/producer. Currently I focus on hypnosis and game show production for the corporate markets.

I've written more than a dozen books and CD's on the topics of entertainment, marketing and hypnosis.

Most of my business comes from people who have either seen our shows or who have booked us before. 65% of our business is repeat business - so we must be doing something right.
