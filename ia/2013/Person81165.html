Lindsay Broder's Summary

Lindsay Broder is an executive coach who specializes in helping top executives manage change – both in themselves and in their organizations. For executives managing a business restructuring, a crisis situation or a potential business or career opportunity, Lindsay helps unlock her clients' professional potential, identifying key skills clients already possess and showing them how to foster those traits to reach their goals.

Lindsay relates to top executives because she is more than simply a career strategist, consultant, coach, speaker and writer. Lindsay is an entrepreneur who woke up one day while working on Wall Street and realized that the level of success she achieved was dependent on the decisions made by others. She recognizes the advantages in innovating and disrupting; to either help executives move up the corporate ladder or manage key situations within their companies.

While this approach has shown great results for executives, Lindsay has adapted her approach to help enterprises grow by building better relationships with their employees and by boosting morale. Lindsay supports businesses in accomplishing organizational change by reminding them that encouraging an entrepreneurial spirit can drive results to the bottom line.

She is also a key partner for enterprises undergoing significant crisis issues, like mass layoffs or restructurings. She helps executives work through difficult decisions, assists to minimizing the disruptions that come from workforce reductions and deals with institutional morale issues that arise from crisis situations.

The success Lindsay has helped her clients achieve has made her an expert on issues of careers and employment. She is a frequent guest on television and radio, writes for media outlets and is a sought-after speaker at conferences and corporate events.

Lindsay works with clients across the United States, Canada and Europe.
