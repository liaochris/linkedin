Trevor Stolebarger's Summary

I shoot people. Preferably brides. I also run an amazing company called Media All Stars that has raised over 3 million dollars for high school sports! I love to volunteer for Make-A-Wish and am passionate about encouraging professional photographers to volunteer their services with non-profits and our military. I love my Creator. I love that He made me creative. I don't think Jessica Alba is ugly. I'm not afraid to admit that I listen to and sing along with Taylor Swift. One last thing to note, I am Iron Man.
