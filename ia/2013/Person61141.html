Jeffrey Asselstine, CFA's Summary

If you are looking for a role in MENA (Middle East and North Africa) or if you are a company looking for excellent people feel free to email direct to jeffrey@nelsonparkadvisors.com.

Our firm initially focused on financial services, but we are now expanding outside of this sector to include roles in Telecommunications, Oil and Gas and Real Estate.

Also visit out website: www.nelsonparkadvisors.com and signup for our newsletter to ensure that you are immediately notified on all new roles as they become available as well as reviewing our outstanding roles.

Am a career international banker having lived in and worked in 8 different countries in my 18 years. These areas include: Canada, Hong Kong, Oman, Dubai-UAE, London, Lebanon, Russia and Qatar. During this time I have worked in personal, private, corporate, commercial and investment banking. Overseas now for almost 20 years, of which 12 of those years have been in the Middle East.

Presently hold the role of CEO and Managing Director of the NelsonPark Group as well as Past - President of the Canadian Business Association in Qatar, and remain on the Executive Committee of the CBAQ. A Board Member for the American School of Doha, serving as the Chairman of the Strategic Development Committee of the School as well as on the Finance Committee.

I have created a group - "NelsonPark Advisors - Recruitment" that you are welcome to join if you wish to kept up to date on new roles within financial institutions in the Middle East.

Specialties

Executive recruitment for the MENA Region is our primary business, however we have recently expanded our business and team with the recruitment of Nevine Kamel, Dain Stubbs and Sunanda Dasgupta who have joined us.
