Kathleen Hein's Summary

I’m a blend of project manager and creative thinker, nerd and innovator, design and development. My superpowers all kick into action when I’m keeping technology, developers, creative, interactive, new media and software programs and projects on track. I’m the one with the clipboard who knows how all the parts fit together, from concept to strategy to design to technology.

I’ve managed online content and new media projects for one of the world’s leading museums, overseen software implementation for an online funding system for a nonprofit, developed and managed campaigns for infomercials, built branding for a major law firm, and created workflow processes for the power company’s high-volume website.

Sometimes I’m in charge of getting software produced and implemented, sometimes I’m in charge of design. I’m always in charge of getting things done.

I’m a versatile balance of technical and creative, with a strategic perspective and a tactical approach. People will tell you that I’m unstoppable, high energy, filled with ideas and a mind full of all the new cool technologies and tools. I’m known as a connector between people.

What my roles always have in common is that my job is to complete projects with flawless execution--I make that happen by quarterbacking the development and creative process from end to end with a smile the whole way.
