Gail Weiss Gaspar, Certified Career Coach's Summary

Gail is a Certified Professional Coach who lives and spreads the conviction that it is both possible and optimal to reinvent ourselves with a renewed sense of hope and courage as it relates to wealth, health and happiness -at any stage in life.

Gail Weiss Gaspar, MA, ACC, CPCC has the experience, ability, intuition and skills to meet each client where they are and guide them with a step by step action plan to where they want to go.

Gail's coaching success is greatly influenced by her professional history as Organizational Development Consultant and Trainer, Health Promotion and Marketing Manager, Board Officer and Member positions, university recruiter, fund-raiser and community leader.

Specialties

Career Re-Invention; Mid-Life Identity & Motivation; Work & Family Relationships; Decision Making; Law of Attraction
