Michelle Mendoza, SPHR's Summary

I am Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR), and the founder of Connect to HR, LLC. Connect to HR, LLC is a boutique Human Resource Consulting firm. I have extensive experience in all aspects of Human Resources, both as a corporate professional and as a consultant.

Some key areas of expertise include: employee relations, compliance, policies and procedures development, recruitment, and employee development. I help my clients develop and operate Human Resources functions at companies with 5 to 50 employees across a variety of industries. I am fluent in Spanish and can assist employers with services such as translation of documents, training and delivery of information.

My goal is to help companies maximize their most important resource – their employees! In addition, I help organizations meet their unique HR and legal compliance needs by providing HR services that are customized to their particular situation.

Please visit http://connecttohr.com for more details.
