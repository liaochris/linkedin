Wayne Pagani, MCCS, MCIS, MCRS's Summary

"connecting people with people, resources, and opportunities"

Career Management and Professional Development services.

For over 16 years, I have had the privilege of coaching people from all walks of life. I excel at providing in-depth services in the career development field complemented by sound management experience in the corporate world.

As a talented career strategist, I have a passion for not only serving people but also enhancing the industry at large. To this end, I have dedicated myself to industry leading organizations such as the Career Professionals of Canada where I currently serve as a Senior Advisor and the National Certification Chair.

During the course of my career, I have been honoured and humbled with several awards from CPC and other prestigious associations for my contributions in our field. This includes the 2011 Career Management Alliance Mentor Award honouring those individuals who have guided others in tradecraft, entrepreneurship, and community building.

From 2007 to 2010, I was the recipient of the following Awards of Excellence from CPC recognizing outstanding contributions as:

~ Canadian Career Leader
~ Volunteer Contributor to the Organization
~ Canadian Online Profile Developer
~ Canadian Resume Contributor - Best Technical Resume Category (2X)
~ Canadian Resume Contributor - Best Career Change Resume
~ Canadian Resume Contributor - Best Executive Resume
~ Executive Directors Award for Volunteer Contributions

In 2010, I was also fortunate enough to be a nominee with Career Directors Internationals' 2010 Toast of the Resume Industry (TORI) award for Best Resume in the Career Change Category.

Positioned at the forefront of Canada’s capital, I have been featured in the Globe & Mail, Ottawa Business Journal, and the Nepean Weekender.

Passionate about networking I support people through the full scope of career transition going above and beyond the call of duty to serve clients.
