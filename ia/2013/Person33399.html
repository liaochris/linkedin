Debbie Reardon's Summary

Debbie Reardon has been a top-producing real estate agent in the Colorado Springs real estate market since 1993. Nothing gives her more joy than the relationships she has formed with her real estate clients and the satisfaction of knowing that they have had a positive real estate experience. Debbie obtained her real estate license in December, 1993. She was Rookie of the Year for Mcginnis GMAC in 1994 where she worked until 1998. In 1998 she joined Prudential Professional Realtors where she received many production awards over a 7 year period.She joined the Platinum Group Realtors in Colorado Springs in September, 2005. The Platinum Group’s business philosophies are in line with Debbie’s – and here she is able to continue to provide her valued friends and clients with the same level of customer service they have come to expect of her.

Debbie’s own personal experience with relocation, as well as facilitating the relocation of many clients, has helped her to understand the emotions that are involved in all types of moves – whether local or state-to-state, Debbie knows that each family member is affected and she sincerely wants to make it as stress-free as possible for everyone involved. After working as a single real estate agent for 14 years, Debbie formed her real estate team – Reardon Partners – in January, 2008.

Debbie Reardon has been ranked as one of the top five real estate agents in Colorado Springs since forming the Reardon Partners in 2008.

Specialties: Residential Real Estate Sales In El Paso County, Colorado, Sellers Agent, Relocation, Buyers Agent, Home Listings, Real Estate Listing, Real Estate Marketing
