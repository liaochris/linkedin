Jimmy Glenn's Projects

  • The Astonishing Tales of Kid Occult

    • March 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Jimmy Glenn

    The Astonishing Tales of Kid Occult is a graphic novel following Billy Robinson, a.k.a. the Kid, 15-year-old sidekick to Professor Occult, the world’s preeminent mystical master. At least Billy always believed him to be. The Kid’s confidence in that fact is shaken as the series opens and Professor Occult and the Kid are arrested for multiple counts of assault and murder. As they are arrested Professor Occult suffers a massive heart attack and is rushed to the hospital, and the Kid is detained for questioning. During the course of his interrogation, the Kid’s confidence in the sanity and piety of the Professor begin to falter. Billy escapes and finds the Professor at a nearby hospital in a drug induced daze rambling, seemingly about nonsense, and demanding that Billy run for his life.

    The story follows the Kid as he travels across the country: encountering the Professor’s former allies, avoiding capture, meeting a new generation of heroes, and finding that Professor Occult was not held in the highest regard by most members of the superhero community.

  • Centenary College Fulbright Gateway Orientation 2012/2013

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Jimmy Glenn, Michael Iris, Trish Mahaffey, Bassim Abbassi, Prof. Cheryl Veronda, Lauren Bergey, Devon Paffendorf, Joshua Walker, Kate Booth

    Fulbright Scholars from almost 50 countries will come to Centenary College as part of the 2013 Fulbright Gateway Orientation. The United States Bureau of Educational and Cultural Affairs (ECA) sponsors the orientation as part of its Fulbright Program, the U.S. government’s flagship international exchange program supported by the people of the United States and partner countries around the world.

    The purpose of the orientation is to prepare students to adapt to academic and cultural life in the United States and to promote a shared and successful Fulbright experience. This activity is administered by the Institute of International Education (IIE). The Fulbright Gateway Orientation will be held at Centenary College, August 5-9, 2013.
