Kevin W. Grossman's Summary

My immediate passion and interest is to improve and elevate the employer-candidate-employee experience, which is why I volunteered to be on the CandE Council for the 2013 Candidate Experience Awards and why I volunteer each month for Hirewire.org, a a local organization to help job seekers in Santa Cruz County with career development and job search advice.

I'm currently the Director of Content Development at Peoplefluent, the leading provider of talent management solutions designed to support the entire workforce. My domain expertise and familiarity with the HR and recruitment marketplace for the past 14 years includes working for and with job search, talent sourcing and acquisition, video interviewing, assessment, background screening, training and development, and talent management firms, helping them to grow and excel.

For the past 25 years I've held multiple leadership positions in the human resource and recruiting marketplace, B2B and consumer technology marketplace and higher education. I'm a marketing strategist, business development and sales professional, industry evangelist, online community developer, entrepreneur, analyst, advisor, manager, philanthropist and writer.

I've also been a top social influencer in leadership, human resources, talent management and recruiting, as well as a prolific "HR business" blogger since 2004. I co-founded of the TalentCulture "world of work" community that includes the highly popular weekly #TChat Twitter chat and radio show. I've also authored multiple articles on marketing, business development, HR, recruiting and leadership, and my first business book titled "Tech Job Hunt Handbook" was released in December 2012 from Apress. I speak at HR and recruiting industry events and also moderate HR/recruiting/marketing related webinars and roundtables.

Lastly, I'm a proud father and husband, I enjoy reading and write regularly about responsible parenting and domestic violence awareness.

Kevin W. Grossman's Projects

  • #TChat Radio

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Kevin W. Grossman, Meghan M. Biro, Tim McDonald, Megan Burkett, Kathleen (KK) Kruse, Brent Skinner

    Join the popular TalentCulture World of Work community as we return to the internet airwaves with #TChat Radio! Since November 2010, our weekly #TChat events have attracted talent-minded professionals from across the Twitter universe to discuss hot topics in today's world of work. We focus on a spectrum of issues, including career management, hiring trends, leadership, learning, talent strategy, recruiting, social workplace issues, technology trends and more. There's always something important to discuss when it comes to our daily professional lives. The #TChat Twitter audience has rapidly grown to include thousands of business leaders, HR and recruiting thought leaders, technology innovators and social marketing leaders who connect each Wednesday from 7-8pm ET (4-5pm PT). We experimented with Internet radio simulcasting in 2011, and now we're creating a separate 30-minute radio program on Tuesday nights at 7:30pm ET (4pm PT)! Each Tuesday, TalentCulture Co-Creators and #TChat Radio Hosts Meghan M. Biro and Kevin W. Grossman interview compelling guests about topics that align with the week's Twitter #TChat theme. But wait — what exactly is #TChat? Why should you spend your Tuesday and Wednesday nights online with us? It's not just two events. It's a community devoted to advancing the human side of business. And the lights are ALWAYS ON our Twitter stream. So it's not just a radio show and a Twitter chat event. It's a living hashtag that connects like-minded people. And you can connect with them ANYTIME for talent-related information, ideas and support. It's easy! Just log on at Twitter and search for hashtag #TChat. See you there! (And hope to see you here on Tuesdays, too!)
