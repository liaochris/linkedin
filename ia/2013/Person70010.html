Jami S. Oliver's Summary

I currently manage and run my own law practice in Columbus, Ohio. I am a litigation attorney, handling pharmaceutical and product defects, injury & death cases, and employment/whistleblower cases.

I am past-President of the Ohio Women's Bar Association, past-President of the Central Ohio Association for Justice (fka, Franklin County Trial Lawyers Association), and am a Trustee for the Ohio Association for Justice (fka, Ohio Academy of Trial Lawyers).

Litigation Counsel of America, Trial Lawyer Honor Society, Fellowship, 2008; Columbus’ Top Lawyers, Business First, March 26, 2004; Million Dollar Advocates Hall of Fame, 2005 ; Ohio Super Lawyers, Rising Stars—Top Lawyers Under 40, 2005, 2006, 2007; Ohio Super Lawyers, 2009-2013, including Top 25 Women Lawyers in Columbus, Top 50 Women Lawyers in Ohio; Strathmore’s Who’s Who, lifetime; Madison Who’s Who, 2006; Lawdragon 500 Leading Plaintiff’s Lawyers in America, 2007, 2008.

Specialties: Plaintiff’s Products Liability Law, Plaintiff’s Pharmaceutical and Drug Litigation, Class Actions, Personal Injury Law, Wrongful Death Law, Insurance Law, Employment and Wrongful Discharge Law, Civil Rights, Whistleblower Law and Qui Tam, Business Litigation. * Disclaimer: Ohio rules do not permit an Attorney to represent himself or herself as a specialist in a particular area of practice.
