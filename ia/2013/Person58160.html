Jane Catherine Rozek's Summary

I’m a narrative non-fiction writer who digs deep for the truth in contemporary issues. I like to challenge readers to look at things from a celestial viewpoint. I write to those of open mind and brave of heart.

I love to help people work out the answers to the basic questions we all have in our lives: “Why are we here?” What’s the purpose to life?

By stripping away religious terminology from traditional Christianity…I’ve discovered an ancient “Celestial Proposal” that emerges in stunning clarity. It is this focus – that makes life a fascinating game to play. Join me in trailblazing a new 21st Century Christianity.
