Gary Rowe's Summary

Application design and development on a wide variety of projects in an assortment of industry sectors (airline, automotive, b2b directories, charity, government, services, telecommunications).

I specialise in the creation of robust, scalable enterprise web applications for use either internally or public facing. Most systems are written using Java with extensive use of open source technologies to reduce costs, improve maintainability and provide long term solutions.

I also provide consultancy and implementation to allow companies to migrate away from legacy systems to improved architectures based on solid design principles working with open source frameworks. I define a legacy system as one that does not have a comprehensive automated test suite and is largely opaque to developers attempting to make changes.

Agile/XP: Scrum, Kanban, pair programming, automated testing, DRY, pragmatic approach, continuous integration.
