Denny Kurien's Summary

TALENTED CREATIVE: Digital Art-Director with 14 years of experience in both traditional and digital advertising, design, and animation. Very passionate about the Big Idea.

STRONG LEADER: Well-versed in developing creative talent and working collaboratively with agency partners internally and externally. Supervised large teams of designers and developers to provide high-impact creative (from concept to execution) across the print, web, television, and mobile space.

HYBRID THINKER: Comes from a fine-art background with a Masters Degree in Advertising, Bachelors Degree in Graphic Design, and is a 3D Animation alumni from Vancouver Film School. Prior to joining the business of Advertising, he used to work as a Visual Effects Artist & 3D Animator for movies such as Resident Evil, Dawn of the Dead & Cube Zero.

ENTREPRENEUR: Runs his own creative boutique, Denny Kurien studios (http://DennyKurien.com), with clientele like Smoke's Poutinerie and Chartwells (Compass Group Inc.). He also develops his own apps for iPhone, iPad, and Android and sells them online through his company PopcornGrenade.com

BROAD CLIENT EXPERIENCE: Has worked on a number of high-profile multinational brands, including Panasonic, AXE, Motorola, Wind Mobile, VISA, and the Government of Canada.

DIGITALLY SAVVY: High degree of technical knowledge. Special emphasis on user-interface design, motion-graphics, social media integration, interactive media (Flash & HTML 5), web-banners, mobile app design, and 3D / 2D animation production (for television & web).
