Brian Albin's Summary

http://www.brianalbin.com

I am a technology professional helping reduce risk of companies inundated by information overload.

My diverse achievements have introduced operational efficiency and cost savings on behalf of major financial service companies. Experience includes collaborating on information management policies and procedures with legal (compliance/litigation), business operations, records management and technology groups. This framework can used to identify gaps in technology and select strategic solutions to fill them. Additionally, it helps identify content with low business value and increased risk/costs associated with potential litigation.

An effective information management framework will reduce risk, increase operational efficiency and can also be used to generate revenue.
