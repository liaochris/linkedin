Gina Abudi's Summary

Professional speaker, management consultant, facilitator/trainer, published author.

Provide management consulting focused on projects, processes, people and technology strategy and management. Project work has included development of strategic learning programs for high potentials and others, management and individual contributor assessments, leadership development programs, identification of high potentials/succession planning, onboarding programs,business impact and ROI, mentoring programs, creation of collaboration portals for project teams, process improvement initiatives,development of project management best practices, Centers of Excellence, and PMOs, customized curriculum development, training workshops. Visit Abudi Consulting Group at http://www.AbudiConsulting.com to learn more how we can help your business meets its goals.

Gina frequently speaks and is a keynote at numerous conferences, forums and at corporate and industry events on various project management, process management and leadership and general management topics. Gina's blog includes articles, white papers and case studies in a variety of strategic areas: http://www.GinaAbudi.com.

Co-author of The Complete Idiot's Guide to Best Practices for Small Business. Publisher: Alpha Books, October, 2011

Contributing author to Gantthead.com's Project Pain Reliever, October, 2011

Published in a variety of online resources and magazines, including PM Network, Executive Brief, and Vistage and Microsoft's Small Business blogs.

Adjunct Faculty: Hesser College, New Hampshire and Granite State College, New Hampshire
