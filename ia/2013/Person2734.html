Allison Emery's Summary

I'm a thinker, writer and designer. Constantly inquisitive and curious, I research, ask questions, ponder, explore and seek. I bring more to client projects than purely a knack for making things sound and look pretty. I bring an entire thought process that I've honed to perfection that truly helps to not only address the current project but introduce other possibilities for projects down the road.

I'm a brainstorming and big picture gal, who is constantly aiming to learn more—about clients, business, design, web, social media, you name it. I've always believed that if you no longer have the drive to learn, then you must think you know it all. Clearly, no one knows it all. My drive for learning has led me down an unusual path to the line of work I'm in now and I see this as a benefit. I've always instinctively been a writer. I see and think in words, definitions, spider charts and tangents. I really had to learn to be a designer, to acquire that skill set and to challenge myself to have the eye for design I have today. I'm a hybrid creative, best of both worlds.

Specialties: Adobe Creative Suite, Keynote and PowerPoint, Pages and Word, FontLab, AuthorIt, Leadership, Branding and Identity, Research, Concepting, Photoshoot direction, Strategic thinking, Project management, Presentations, CopyWriting, Technical Writing, Legal Writing, Public Speaking, Training Development
