Markell Morris' Summary

I’m a national certified counselor, teacher, trainer and program developer.

Individual Career Services:
My clients are professionals in technology project management, higher education/student affairs, marketing/business development, and PhDs transitioning to industry.

Use the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator, Skillscan, Strong Interest Inventory and Career Values Inventory to help people discover their career aspirations.

Created The Fast Track Your Career Online Class for people who are frustrated in their careers and looking for a change, want to change careers but don’t know how to get started, have tried to change careers but nothing seems to work, and who don’t know what other career opportunities exist for them.

Speaking, Training and Employee Development:
Design and deliver training programs to 1) support employee retention and career development initiatives and 2) provide insight, techniques and strategies to foster employee engagement, personal growth and professional development

Training Areas include:
a) Techniques for Establishing Your Network and Building Professional Relationships
b) Beyond First Impressions: How to Stand Out in Interviews
c) Resume Writing: How to Get Your Foot in the Door
d) Leveraging Your Transferable Skills for New Opportunities
e) Fast Track Your Career: Three Steps for Finding Work You Love
f) Time Management and Planning

Teaching:
Design and deliver in-person and online career planning and professional development classes for undergraduate and graduate students in business, education, psychology, humanities, nursing and hospitality management.

Career and Job Search Materials:
Wrote 64 page career guide, Fast Track Your Career: Three Steps for Finding Work You Love (print and pdf formats), 8 page ebook, 3 Secrets to Creating Luck in Your Job Search, 6 page ebook, 6 Tips For Discovering The Work You Love By Working With A Career Counselor, and numerous job search articles.

Specialties

virtual career counseling, career assessment, MBTI, Strong Interest Inventory, Skillscan, training and development, present on career topics, design career programs, executive and career coaching, interview preparation, job search planning, career transition, resume writing, personal branding
