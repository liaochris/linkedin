Guy Zbarsky's Summary

KeepGo.com is an international internet provider for mobile devices. We provide travelers with 3G internet access when they are traveling abroad.

Our prices are about 5-8 times less than data roaming prices provided by local network carriers.

By doing that we allow our customers to avoid expensive voice and data roaming fees from their carrier and enjoy 3G internet everywhere.
Our customers rent a SIM card with us for their destination countries before they travel. The SIM card can be used with any unlocked GSM device, including cellular modems, MiFi, smartphones and Tablet PC's.

We offer unlimited and/or pre-paid internet access in more that 198 countries. It can be used with or without voice access.
For more information, please visit our web-site: www.KeepGo.com
