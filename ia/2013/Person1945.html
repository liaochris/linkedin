When it comes to children: I create custom potty training plans, when it comes to parents and childcare providers I potty train their mouth and mind to get the results they want, and when it comes to the Corporate World you got it! I get in between the terrible two's and bring a cutting edge plan to get your Company to the next level and teach you how to retain top talent by changing management style not people. Let's POTTY!


As an avid traveler, parenting class teacher, life coach and gourmet food aficionado, I am also actively seeking to publish restaurant reviews, cutting-edge articles about culinary culture, parenting, healing and self help at www.adrianavermillion.com


My messages combine hope, love, truth about life and my own real life experiences to help families, man, woman and children develop a deeper relationship with others and live a TRUE and NOW Life.


My heart's desire is that others may know the joy of being alive in ALL ways.

As a CEO at AIS I have the opportunity to set a strategy and impart vision to our team, make decisions that will better not only the company as a whole but our clients as well, I set budgets, form partnerships, and hire a team to steer the company accordingly.

One of my funnest duty as a CEO is to build culture. Work gets done through people, and people are profoundly affected by culture.

I love team building and this is one of my projects for AIS in 2013, to build a team that will lead the company into great heights.
