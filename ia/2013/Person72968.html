Kathy Gruver, PhD.'s Publications

  • Conquer your Stress with Mind/Body Techniques

    • Infinity
    • June 17, 2013
    Authors: Kathy Gruver, PhD.

    Stress is a killer in our society with stress-related illness contributing to up to 80% of our doctor's visits. But we have to remember, stress isn't really the problem. It's our reaction to the stress and THAT we can control. This book teaches you tips and techniques you can use to decrease your stress response, invoke the relaxation response and have better health. Book includes studies on mind/body methods, chapters from experts in the field and easy ways for you to take control and conquer stress.

  • Body/Mind Therapies for the Bodyworker

    • Infinity
    • December 21, 2012
    Authors: Kathy Gruver, PhD.

    If you are a bodyworker or health professional interested in adding mind/body therapies to your practice and your life, this is your book. Learn about the stress response, placebo effect and fundamentals of mind/body communication. You will understand the specific ways these methods can enhance the healing of your clients and learn multitudes of modalities you can add to help more people, increase your income and experience the new paradigm of the mind/body connection. Though written for the professional, it is absolutely appropriate for the layperson who is interested in mind/body connection. Foreword by Ruth Werner, author of A Massage Therapists Guide to Pathology.

  • The Alternative Medicine Cabinet

    • Infinity Publishing
    • March 27, 2010
    Authors: Kathy Gruver, PhD.

    Everything you need to know about how to incorporate natural health into your life. Learn about alternative remedies and how you and your family can utilize them for better health and less reliance on prescriptions and surgeries. A must have for anyone curious about alternative medicine. Topics include healthy pregancy, women and stress, cancer, obesity, reiki, homeopathics, massage, depression, headaches and much more. A fun and easy read that you'll refer to again and again.

  • Therapeutic Massage at Home; Learn to Rub People the RIGHT Way

    • Healing Circle
    • May 15, 2009
    Authors: Kathy Gruver, PhD.

    Nothing says I love you like relaxing massage. It's even better when you can do massage on your partner. Sometimes couples are afraid of hurting themselves or their partners or just want to make sure they are doing it right. My new instructional massage DVD, Therapeutic Massage at Home; Learn to Rub People the RIGHT Way, teaches couples to do massage for home health and it's the perfect item for date night! Light the candles, put on some relaxing music and enjoy the massage of your life, catered to you by the one you love.

    My DVD demonstrates anatomy, suggested hand positions, areas to avoid, a full back and neck massage that you follow along with plus beautiful original music, massage terminology and health and wellness terms. There's also a section on how to use the bed or the floor if you don't have your own massage table. It's great for the beginner who always to learn massage or for someone that wants to hone their skills and save their thumbs. I share my 20 years of experience to make sure people are doing massage safely and effectively.
