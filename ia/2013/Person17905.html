Production Concept Artist, Rigger & Animator

Creative Submersible Research

November 2011 – May 2012 (7 months) Portland, OR

I produced all of the visual assets for the iOS application,“Puncho Fighto,” by Creative Submersible Research (Portland, OR). The project involved digital painting, character design, matte background design, UI design, key-framing of all of the game’s animated sequences, and extensive tight-knit collaboration with the creator and lead programmer, Oliver Smith.

Using Mr. Smith's proprietary software, we were able to rig and animate marionette-like characters into a 2.5D environment, and create a novel game experience that has seen around 50,000 downloads across the globe.
