Experience

Various

2003 – 2007 (4 years) MENA

Before immigrating to United States, I was heading up the WiMAX telephony product development division at a multi-billion-dollar Abu Dhabi Group backed telecommunication venture, spread across MENA. I was part of the core team that launched the world’s first and largest 4G Mobile WiMAX (802.16e) network, with Motorola, Inc.

We deployed the largest all-IP NGN backhaul and transport network with mobile WiMAX access and IMS Core. Everyone thought that the future of mobile telephony had arrived. It looked like the biggest tech revolution. In 2007, iPhone came, grabbed everything by the balls and forever changed the world.

In my early career, at the age of 21, I grew a New York based telecom startup from 15 to 100+ employees in less than a year. I was responsible for starting, scaling, growing and managing multi-country captive-offshore BPO operations for telecom providers based in New York and Florida.
