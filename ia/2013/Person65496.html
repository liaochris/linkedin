Joelle Reeder's Summary

Joelle Reeder is owner and brunette half of Moxie Design Studios. She designs fabulous, functional websites, and provides brand and social media consultation for small businesses, authors, professional bloggers and all around cool people.

Joelle is also co-author of pioneer "femme-tech" book, "The IT Girl's Guide to Blogging with Moxie" (Wiley, 2007) and has spoken on panels at industry events such as SXSW Interactive.

For media inquiries, speaking engagements, or literary inquiries please contact Joelle Reeder at joelle@themoxiegirls.com

Specialties: web design, blog design, art direction, front-end web development, social media, logo, marking and branding consultation, copywriting, Wordpress, Expression Engine
