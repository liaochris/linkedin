Jonathan Grubb's Summary

I'm a seasoned tech product manager and designer with 15 years experience building consumer web and mobile apps. I've created products for the world's top technology companies, including Yahoo!, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Vodafone, Verizon, Sprint, AT&T, and Samsung. I co-founded Get Satisfaction (4m users, $20m funding), and ran product management for Lookout Mobile Security (20m users, $1bn+ valuation).

I also co-created the RVIP Lounge, a mobile karaoke bar housed in a customized RV. RVIP has hosted events at SXSW, Sundance Film Festival, LA Film Festival, and TED, with sponsorship from HBO, Coca-Cola, Conde-Nast, Starwood Hotels, and Google.

My deep expertise is in core mobile apps like search, security, backup, sms, and email. My broad experience is in everything else it takes to run a startup or a product team.
