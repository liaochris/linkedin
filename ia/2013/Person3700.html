Alex Kim's Summary

I am a senior strategist with a unique mix of creative, technology, and business experience/understanding that companies need to engage today's digitally-centric and mobile audiences.

Working with a diverse set of businesses ranging from incubator stage start-ups to Fortune 500 companies, I lead multidisciplinary teams in interactive strategy, design, and engineering/application development to create unique brand experiences that are emotionally-engaging and utilize leading-edge technology.

My roots stem from working in the agency environment including USWeb/MarchFirst through to SolutionSet. As a founding partner of SolutionSet, a web and technology company, I was responsible for providing creative and technology solutions for clients such as eBay, TiVo, and BEA Systems, prior to its acquisition by Haggin Marketing in 2008. SolutionSet was also #23 on the Inc. 500 list of fastest growing private companies in America that year.
