Katherine Bock's Summary

I am a "recovering attorney" and professional certified coach who expands career possibilities for professionals wanting to live lives of purpose and passion in alignment with their talents, strengths & values. Clients tell me that I help them achieve extraordinary results with less effort by clarifying what it is that they really want and then co-creating a strategy to achieve what they most value.

I have helped over 1,000 individuals over the past eight years to identify and articulate their unique value proposition to propel their performance. In addition to coaching and consulting with individuals and groups,I teach Career Management Coaching at UTD and have taught a highly regarded career management course in the Executive Education program at the University of Texas at Dallas. As part of my volunteer activities, I teach a job search workshop at the Dallas Christian Women’s Job Corps.

I hold a graduate-level certificate of Executive and Professional Coaching from the University of Texas at Dallas and am certified in various assessment tools including the Birkman Method®, The Winslow Report® and Core Clarity® In-Powering Teams program and am a certified TOPs group facilitator and have attended the Sedona Method Coach Training Level I and am a newly certified Laughter Yoga leader. I hold the ICF Professional Certified Coach designation from the ICF.

I earned my law degree (with blood, sweat & tears) from the University of Houston and have an accounting degree as well. The majority of my career was spent in the financial services industry with the organization that is now JP Morgan/Chase and prior to that worked with Browning Ferris Industries, Delta Airlines and Southwestern Bell Telephone Company.

I am a proud 2001 graduate of Leadership Texas and belong to various executive-level and professional groups and am active in my community, having served on a variety of non-profit boards.

Specialties

Executive and Leadership coaching, career management, career transition, Certified Birkman and Winslow consultant, Core Clarity certified facilitator, Forton Group Leadership Coaching facilitator, Strengthsfinder, Career Anchors, Values in Action, Career Reflections. Maximizer, Input, Relator, Positivity & Individualization strengths.
