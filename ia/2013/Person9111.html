Antonio Olmo Titos' Summary

I am a software engineer with a strong academic background and nine years of international experience in project management, UX, web development and mobile development.

Some areas that interest me especially are internet standards, open systems and free software, interfaces and usability, digital imaging, 3D and data visualisation, and mobile computing.

My professional goal is to bring my skill set to an organisation that uses the best tools and applies the best practices in the industry. In return, I seek to further develop my abilities by being exposed to new challenges and excellent professionals within that team.

More than anything else, I enjoy learning and trying new things — studying subjects that are novel to me, or that I find particularly inspiring or useful (either for my work, or just to better understand the world around me). I make a point of staying active by regularly setting new goals and challenges for myself; be it testing some new technology, training for a sporting event, or taking up a foreign language.
