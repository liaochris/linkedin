I am a digital production manager living in Yorkshire, England. I love everything digital, innovative, entrepreneurial and technical.

I'm a passionate and dedicated 3D artist, with ten+ years experience in Marketing, Advertising, PR industries; creating Broadcast, online and offline digital campaigns, for agencies and clients globally.
