Joseph Ord's Summary

Joseph Ord founded AMOSO Properties LLC with the intention of buying a few rental properties to help create some income while the Commercial Real Estate market was down.

Joe found that buying run down properties and fixing them up to quality standards and renting them to be fun, so he kept buying the distressed properties. Not only is he having fun, but the return on investment is phenomenal. He started talking

Joe quickly realized there is a whole segment of the investor world that is currently under served. Busy professionals who want to take advantage of the great returns available but are just too busy in their careers to deal any aspect of real estate investing, much less land lording.

Specialties

Investment Property Identification, Renovation Managament, Transparent Communication.
