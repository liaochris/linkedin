Jerry Ashton's Summary

Coming from a "question authority" style, I seem to have become an industry gadfly in finance, credit, collections and customer service and write regularly for the Huffington Post and published at times (when I don't get too offensive) for insideARM, the industry's go-to website.

When invited to do so, and if the project is of interest, I also provide independent consulting in the application of "Relationship Management" to the Order-to-Cash process and recovery of delinquent A/R.

My 30-year-plus experiences encompass medical collections, media, training in inter-personal communication skills, and consulting to senior management on credit/collection and customer service strategies.

For companies requiring my interventionist skills, I serve at that key juncture when my client has lost any meaningful (i.e., effective) communications with their customer and the account is being considered for referral to third parties such as collection agencies or attorneys.

My "Diplomatic Intervention" approach, sharpened over years of learning and understanding how to motivate positive action (Get the Money and Keep the Good Customer), results not only in monies recovered, but the salvaging of worthwhile relationships as well.

This is a skill and approach which will come more and more in demand given the economic train wreck we call the "Great Recession."

As a sidebar, my attempts to both chronicle as well as work within the Occupy Wall Street movement motivated me to creat an Internet-Radio program called "15 Minutes of Fact" which is found at WGRNradio.com and most recently to help launch their now-famous "Rolling Jubilee" (rollingjubilee.org).

Oh yes - and presently completing my book-in-process "Written Off - America and Americans (www.WrittenOffAmerica.com) and enrolling some great LinkedIn connections as well as Twitter and Real-Life folk as contributors to what will be a combined Survival/Activist handbook to be published in March, 2013.

Specialties

My credentials in credit and collection consulting, training and outsourcing come from years of "in-the-trenches" work and then starting a consulting and outsourcing firm (CFO Advisors, Inc.) which serviced clients such as Johnson & Johnson, Gannett and Hearst, and R.R. Donneley and generated million-dollar-plus for several years.

"Written Off - America and Americans" will serve as a survival and activist guide for both creditors and debtors in dealing with today's ongoing financial challenges.
