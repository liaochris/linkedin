Samantha Clarke's Summary

I straddle that funny sweet zone between creative minx and strategic thinker. As a compulsive visionary, with an insatiable appetite for branding and innovation, style & shoes, female empowerment and consumer psychology I believe that the impossible is merely the possible waiting to happen (with lots of hard work and determination).

More about what I love to do:

- Helping women turn up the volume on their style and presence for success through personal style rejuvenation days and services. My goal is to help every woman make a positive statement about who she is everyday and invest in herself & clothes that allow her to do this. I believe your style is the ‘spokesperson’ for your values- it dictates your character before others do it for you.

- Author of the DIY Wardrobe Success - a fun, inspiring guide to help professional women everywhere understand their own signature style and build an authentic wardrobe thats allows them to sell themselves with confidence.

- I'm also wired with the strategic and consumer insight bug! I love helping clients with inspiration and insight for NPD, Brand Development and Target market understanding, through trend ideation workshops and developing intelligent research reports. In the past I've worked with large brands through Zukuri Consulting. This has knowledge has been subsequently morphed and channelled into Meet Your Niche; a research introductions agency.

- I noticed a gap in the market for insight reports packaged for entrepreneurial-sized budgets. Working with a small collective of analysts, Meet Your Niche produces succinct reports to introduce, small start-ups and businesses, creatives and young entrepreneurs, to their "Niche", "Trends" or "Competitors".

- I continue to inspire and be inspired by young entrepreneurs taking part in entrepreneur panel discussions, youth mentoring and speaking engagements.

So, what's your story and how can we help each other?
