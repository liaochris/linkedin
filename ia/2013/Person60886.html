Graduated BFA from University of Michigan School of Art & Design, Photography - specialization Photojournalism. Served as staff photographer for the Milwaukee Courier and Milwaukee Community Journal.

Partner Group 4 Productions/Corporate Media Services - photographer, editor, animator, designer of corporate communications for Consumer and B-B clients.

Founded Envision, a fully-digital communications company, in 1993, specializing in presentations and applications for sales and marketing. Worked with regional companies in B-B and B-C markets, including Briggs & Stratton, Miller Brewing, Falk Corporation, Sargento Cheese, and Manpower.

Recruited to Manpower advertising staff in 2000. Established Marketing Infrastructure Department to manage Manpower's North American Web properties, reporting to VP of NA Marketing.

Brought Manpower's North American sites in-house, architected site design, page templates and CMS. Developed advertising/promotional programs with vendors such as Google and Monster. Implemented web analytics program with WebTrends.

Coordinated online marketing programs, including professional webinar series and email marketing programs focused on SMB market.

Designed and produced online advertisements for U.S. and Canadian operations, placing ads and corporate profiles on all leading and specialty job boards.

Went off the reservation again in 2008, back to the freedom and glamor of the freelance lifestyle!
