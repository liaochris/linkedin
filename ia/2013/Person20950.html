Catherine Fenske, Spiritual Mentor's Summary

I have been coaching people for many years in goal-setting and personal and spiritual transformation. I like building relationships with spiritual, like-minded people who believe in the Law of Attraction and The Law of Prosperity and Abundance, and who believe that anything is possible. My goal is to attract many, many spiritual people to my practice and coach each one to find freedom, joy and happiness. It is my intent to teach others how to create their reality of choice and to live the life they desire and deserve.

In my practice I use the Law of Attraction as well as other Universal Laws and principles. I also am a Six-Sensory intuitive healer and practitioner and use my skills in this area for intuitive healing. I have been trained by Sonia Choquette in this modality. My teacher and mentor, Alan Cohen, facilitated the desire to become a Spiritual Mentor and has been my guide on my spiritual path.

I write a weekly column called "Joy to You" in the Grand Junction Free press: www.gjfreepresscom. I have been writing this uplifting article based on spiritual principles and transformation once a week for the past year. This has been very rewarding for me as it allows me to use my creativity and serve at the same time.

I have a blog, http://fromblocked2bliss.wordpress.com and am an active blogger. This also gets my creative juices flowing and allows me to serve others who are also on a spiritual path. Writing for me is essential. It is part of my purpose here on this planet and the way I can reach so many and share my musings and inspiration.

Specialties

Spiritual Mentoring, transformation coaching, listening, building relationships, personal support and encouragement, intuitive healing through readings and healing work, Law of Attraction coaching, teaching how to use spiritual principles in every day life. Avid writer and blogger. Provide spiritual support.
