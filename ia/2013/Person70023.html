Jeff Spar's Summary

Senior IT operating executive with more than twenty years Information Technology and global business transformation leadership across media, entertainment and publishing companies.

Deep information technology, database marketing, production, operations, and digital web and device transformation expertise .

Currently Chief Technology Officer at The Metropolitan Museum of Art. Directly before joining the Museum, served as Independent Consultant to CxOs helping to improve the strategic use of IT. Spent 10 years as SVP & Chief Information Officer at The Reader's Digest Association, a complex global $3 billion media, marketing & entertainment company with 5,000 employees across 60 countries and locations. RDA was publicly traded on NYSE until 2007 private equity transaction led by Ripplewood Partners.

Before RDA, 10 years Strategic Management Consulting experience at McKinsey & Company, Inc. Served clients on a broad set of business problems leveraging extensive IT expertise. Member of McKinsey's Business Technology Office creation team.

Specialties

• Aligning IT spending
• Global ERP solution
• Business Transformations
• Outsourcing & offshoring
• IT-enabled business innovation strategies
• Web, Mobile, iPad and internet products
• Due diligence and integration management
• Global shared service solutions development
