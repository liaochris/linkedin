James Freed's Summary

I am an Exhibit Designer with over 15 years experience in the industry. I enjoy working with clients to develop and design compelling educational exhibits. My firm can provide a full design team for your project, or I can work as an integrated part of your design team to realize the project goals.

I've recently completed a MA degree in Education in May of 2013. My thesis, titled The Nexus of Ecological Literacy, Social Justice and Secondary Education, studied and documented student performance, and curriculum efficacy in a course called Sustainable Practices, at a north Bay public high school. I plan to leverage this new knowledge and experience in both my exhibition planning/ design work and my teaching. I continue to teach art and design at the undergrad and graduate level, part time, as I have for the past 17 years.

I am also an exhibiting Fine Artist and enjoy painting in oil and watercolor, as well as drawing.
My work is represented by the Graton Gallery. All of these skills and experiences integrate to create a rich and wonderful creative life that I feel most fortunate and grateful to live.

Specialties: Conceptual development and visualization, hand drawing and traditional illustration skills, physical models, work in Photoshop, Sketch Up, Illustrator, Vector Works, and InDesign, interactive exhibit design, integration of interior architecture with exhibits, sustainable design practitioner and educator, broad knowledge of natural history and science, working as a leader of highly motivated design teams.
