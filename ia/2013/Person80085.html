Len Romano's Summary

Co-owner of Ripe Inc., an award-winning branding & design firm with a local, national and international client base. Brand strategist and soul music expert. Partial to proper chocolate and coffee. Born and raised in Sheffield, England – to Italian parents – and now a bona fide American.

The sole reason Ripe Inc. exists is to create an authentic picture of your business and show how it positively affects people's lives. Ripe Inc. transforms businesses into powerful brands that rise above the competition.

Specialties: Branding, graphic design, marketing communications & materials.
