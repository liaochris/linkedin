Daniel S. Hobbs' Summary

I've personally been an investor in peer lending loans since 2007. I left my role in cross selling equity derivatives to institutional credit clients in June 2012 to focus my time and energy on the peer lending space, particularly around the opportunities for institutions needing yield in a supply-constrained fixed income environment.

Peer Lending Group's focus for clients is three-fold:

• Comprehensive education: Clients should be comfortable & knowledgeable with the investment, operational, and legal risks & opportunities in this new, high-growth asset class. An unbiased knowledge base is a strong foundation.

• Scalable, better risk-adjusted returns: Proprietary statistical analysis from multiple data sources can create alpha in a portfolio of peer lending loans. Technology has enabled credit risk modeling to grow much more sophisticated.

• Tools and analysis: The specific and near real-time information generated from a portfolio of peer lending loans should be an input into any firm's larger investment approach. We're building the equipment to do it.
