Anna Gudmundson's Summary

As Head of Services at Vertu, Anna Gudmundson is responsible for the overall service strategic direction and roadmap of the services and application portfolio, which spans global markets, cross-platform, and both on- and off-device services.

Anna is part of the TPO (Total Product Offering) strategic steering group, defining the future strategy, product roadmap and portfolio.

With a background in telecommunications and digital marketing, Anna has a strong expertise in Mobile, involving brand communication, CRM & loyalty, customer experience, wide technical expertise, strategies & concepts, innovation, data & consumer behaviour, product development and management.

Anna was previously part of the management team at MePlease, a ground-breaking Mobile and Social Media marketing company, which specialises in providing solutions to build valuable relationships between consumers and consumer brands. There she was responsible for strategy and positioning for different brand sectors.

Anna, a Swede by origin, and fluent in four languages, has lived in Nigeria, Zimbabwe, France, Spain and has lived in the UK since 2004.

Specialties: Mobile, Marketing, Telecommunication, Program Management, Strategy, Business Analysis, Wireless, Business Development, Luxury
