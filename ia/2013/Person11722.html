Barbara Newton's Summary

I have been a licensed real estate agent since 1978, beginning in Atlanta, GA. I've been involved with multiple facets of the real estate industry, includng general real estate sales & consulting, residential mortgage loan origination, internet education for REALTORS, new home sales & marketing, land sales and much more. The knowledge I've gleaned over all these years has become invaluable to me as a REALTOR.

My primary goal is to utilize all of my skills and experience for the benefit of my clients, whether that client is a first time home buyer or one that has bought and sold mutliple properties. I want to make sure their real estate buying or selling experience with me is one in which they find I sincerely care about them, that I am truly thankful for their trust in me and my abilities, that I will work tirelessly for them, and that they can feel completely confident in referring me to their friends and family.

Referrals are the lifeblood of my profession. Due in large part to the referrals I receive from my clients, I have been fortunate to remain an active real estate agent at a time when so many agents had to move on to other posiitons during a challenging real estate market. My clients become, and remain, a part of my extended "family", and I cannot express how deeply I appreciate them. I hope I will have the opportunity to become the "go to" REALTOR for many LinkedIn members.

Specialties

Knowledge and experience in both real estate & the mortage industry, powerful negotiation skills, strong marketing skills, Internet marketing know-how, contacts in the mortgage, insurance, construction, and home repair & improvement industries, an innovative mind, an insatiable curiosity and thirst for learning...and I really enjoy my job.
