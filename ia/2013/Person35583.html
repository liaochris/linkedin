David Sparks' Summary

I started building websites in 9th grade when my family got our first computer and have been since. I've been professionaly employed to work my magic since 2006. I started working at archer>malmo as an Interactive Developer. Recently began taking on the responsibilities of Information Architect. My responsibilities include, but aren't limited to: discovery proces, competitive analysis, stake holder interviews, site maps, user testing wireframes, clickable prototypes, tech requirements, presentations and more.

I'm an expert in Axure, Balsamiq, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Coda, HTML5, CSS Browser behaviors, HTML Email, Social Media. I love UX/UI design and jQuery. WordPress is my personal CMS/Blogging platform of choice. I use many tools to get the job done. I'm Detail oriented and I hate being the link in the chain holding up production so I work very hard and very fast.

My happiest moments so far have been a couple of occasions where I developed html5 & CSS layouts to find that they worked in all browsers right out of the gate, first try. Didn't need any debugging or tweaking. Gave myself a few high fives haha.

Specialties

IA, Axure, Wireframes, HTML5, CSS, HTML Emails, Browser Behaviors, jQuery, WordPress, UX, UI, Photoshop, Dreamweaver.
