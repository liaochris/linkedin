Barbara B. Appelbaum, MBA, MAT's Summary

I am a wellness coach, consultant, speaker, published author, and founder of Appelbaum Wellness LLC who has dedicated my life to help motivated professional Baby Boomers wake up to living their lives, see clearly what they want and achieve optimal wellness. I practice an integrative approach to health and wellness in which I explore how all areas of your life – mind, body, spirit - are connected and examine how they fit together affecting your overall wellbeing. I am deeply committed to helping you learn to be proactive in your healthcare versus reactionary in your sickcare so that you can feel great in your body and in your life. My greatest wish is to never hear a person say, “I should be taking better care of myself.” Start living a healthy life now!

Visit my website today to subscribe to my FREE newsletter and receive a FREE special report, "25 Insider Secrets for Optimal Wellness." Also check out my newly published book, "Live in Wellness Now: A Proactive Guide to Living Well" at www.liveinwellnessnow.com.

Be Present | Be Purposeful | Be Well

Specialties: Health & wellness coaching & consulting, life & corporate coaching, integrative approach to nutrition, life transition, aging, purpose/balance/choice, realizing the GIFT™ of adversity, core energy/energy leadership coaching, mindset, success and empowerment.
