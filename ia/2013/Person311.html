Martin Maschke's Summary

20 years of professional photo retouching and image manipulation experience.

My knowledge and background is based on nearly 20 years of experience working in professional PrePress environments - and since 2007 owning my own small business.

Within these years a vast amount of pictures of all kinds came across my screen. Almost anything imaginable – and sometimes seemingly impossible – was done to perfection and to the clients utmost satisfaction.

Here is a list of world-renowned companies that I was doing retouching work for (this list represents only a small part of my clients):

- Max Planck Science Foundation (http://www.mpg.de/english/)
- Fraunhofer Science Foundation (http://www.fraunhofer.de/en/)
- Langenscheidt Publishing (http://www.langenscheidt-education.com/)
- BÖWE (http://www.boewesystec.com/systec_en/home-en.html)
- Thieme Medical Publishers (http://www.thieme.com/)
- Milwaukee (Heavy Duty Power Tools)
- Hamamatsu

and many many more were convinced by the high level of quality produced here and we were honored to provide our services to them.

We would be very happy to add you to our list of satisfied clients.

To see my portfolio visit my website:
http://www.A1Retouching.com/portfolio.html

Specialties

• all kinds of picture/photo retouching (print + web)

kaltnermedia

August 1997 – September 2007 (10 years 2 months)

At kaltnermedia, a multi-media graphic design company in Germany, I was responsible for providing the highest quality in any picture used while working there for 10 years.
Clients where world-renowned companies such as the "MaxPlanck Society", "Fraunhofer Society", "Langenscheidt Publishing Group", "RÖSLE" (highest quality cookware), "GRÖMO", "THIEME publishing" (an international scientific and medical publisher), "Hirschvogel Automotive Group" (among the world's largest automotive suppliers) and many many many more!

Besides supplying all of them with the best possible digital and print-ready pictures I was also responsible in maintaining the best output results on the companies EPSON StylusPro 7600 ProofPrinter. The measurable prints where proven to be above average - every day. Langenscheidt Publishing required a Delta of below 2.0 for all their proofs. At kaltnermedia I was the "go-to" person when it came to pictures...
The only reason why I'm not there anymore was because we moved from Kaufbeuren/Germany to Rochester, NY.
