Corey Kublik's Summary

Corey is a IT Professional with many technical skill sets, strong communications and over 10 years of experience working in business management.

He received his B.S. from University of Wisconsin Parkside, graduating Cum Laude in Management Information Systems. Prior to that, Corey received his Associates with Department Honors from Gateway Technical College in B2B Marketing Management.

Corey currently lives in Kenosha, WI. He is an amateur freelance designer, an avid fitness enthusiast and he enjoys going for long hikes with his girlfriend Maria and little dog Pinata.

Specialties

System analysis, system development, database administrations, project management, and IT support.
