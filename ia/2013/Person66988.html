John Rothe's Summary

John Rothe is CEO/Portfolio Manager at Riverbend Investment Management.

About Riverbend:
Riverbend Investment Management is an independent money management firm with a focus on helping clients build and protect their wealth through customized portfolio management solutions. We manage financial assets for individuals, families and institutions with the view that every client relationship is a long-term partnership adhering to the highest fiduciary standards.

Founded in 2006, Riverbend is a Registered Investment Advisor employing a formula-based, multi-asset investment process. Our investment models are quantitative, follow a long-short strategy, and adhere to strict risk controls.

We provide systematic and research-based asset management to individuals and institutions through customized portfolios and separately managed accounts using our ARTAIS Model.


About John:
Prior to starting his own firm, John was Vice President & OMEGA Portfolio Manager with Oppenheimer & Co., and began his career at Morgan Stanley where he served as Vice President-Investments.

His investment management strategies and advice have appeared in various media outlets, such as CNBC, Reuters, Investment News, Financial Planning Magazine, Investment Advisor Magazine, Financial Advisor, and on Wall Street Business Network syndicated radio broadcasts.

John is an active member of the National Association of Active Investment Managers (NAAIM) and The Market Technicians Association (MTA) and is a contributor to Seeking Alpha, Benzinga, Feed the Bull and Advisor Perspectives.
