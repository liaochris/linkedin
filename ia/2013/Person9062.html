Anu K Nousiainen's Summary

The storyline of my professional life (as well as personal life) is (and has always been) the PEOPLE.

-- If it's about services, processes or tools the ideation and development work starts with the target audience in mind. In the end, they are the ones making the business happen. This is what I believe in.

Another important storyline is the VISION.

-- From target state vision creation and engagement, you build a sustainable and dynamic action plan and so, manage the expectations and sign posts along the way. This is what I call true development.

And back to people once more: success is about TEAM WORK.

-- I love to work with different people, in multidisciplinary teams. I want to drive and improve the ways of working with teams who are committed, inspired and yet who challenge and question. This is what I embrace.

Specialties

What continues to drive me:
Things that do not flow... yet. Thus, nuflow.

I'm exploring business opportunities and concepting meaningful solutions for both the existing and future contexts - from strategy to implementation.

Futures Thinking Lecturer

Laurea University of Applied Sciences

Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Higher Education industry

October 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months)

Futures Research provides methods for understanding economic, societal and cultural changes in the business environment, which is central in anticipating alternative futures. By recognizing trends and using Futures Research methods it is possible to discover new service innovation opportunities - new value - and become a forerunner in the rapidly changing world. Indeed, uncertainty seems to be the number one concern in companies nowadays, and Futures Research helps to make changes and uncertainty more understandable and easier to approach.

There is a strong interest - personal and shared with my colleague Minna Koskelo - combining the practices of Service Design and Futures Research, since the competences integrating these two areas seem to be very rare. Follow our research based blog series "Service Design meets Futures Thinking" where we discuss and reveal a "future oriented service innovation process" as a practical outcome.

Welcome: http://sidlaurea.com/2012/09/30/service-design-meets-futures-thinking/
