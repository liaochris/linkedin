Jack Kern's Summary

Kern Accounting & Bookkeeping offers outsourced "accounting department" solutions for smaller, growing businesses that are not quite ready to hire a full-time accountant or bookkeeper. Typical client engagements start out as a QuickBooks clean-up project, and may evolve from there into a part-time controller engagement. The firm also has a staff of bookkeepers for those businesses who want to outsource this function completely, and tax preparation services are also available.

Specialties: CFO / Controller and financial consultant for small and medium size businesses, with strong emphasis on management accounting, financial analysis, and strategic financial planning. Major accomplishments included obtaining financing for companies where others had failed, and the formulation and implementation of financial and business turnaround strategies leading to major cost savings, enhanced profitability, or sale of the business.
