R. Blake Ramick's Summary

I've been fortunate to have working experience outside the bubble. I've worked for and with large Fortune 500 companies, mid-sized companies and small business owners. Plus I've done so in a wide range of working environments. I've worked in the large corporate environment of Mary Kay and been a full time Freelance Graphic Designer. I enjoy the professional interaction of dealing with clients, servicing their needs and maintaining my own financial freedoms. Although I'm a graphic designer by profession don't let that fool you. I'm an avid student of financial investing. I always have my head in a great educational financial book.

I'd classify myself as the type of person that knows a little about a lot. I like to try and do it all, and because of my interests in most anything I don't submerge myself in just one niche of life. Maybe one day I'll be well versed in a specific area. For now, I want to learn about them all.

Specialties

I'm like Heinz 57. I've got a little bit of everything. I started out as a studio artist in college and double majored in mechanical engineering. Then I switched to graphic design and engineering. I work as a Freelance Designer and Photographer while actively contributing to North 40 Partners, LLC and July Fourteenth Capital Group, LLC.
