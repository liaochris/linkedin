Deb Oskin, EA, NTPI Fellow's Summary

I'm Deb Oskin, and I'm a Clergy Tax "Subject Matter Expert"---that's 'tax talk' for "I know all the ins and outs." I even had an IRS agent once say to me, "Well, you know more about that than I do." (Wow!)

How did I become an expert? Well, after my husband finished seminary, he pastored a small country church. We lived in the church parsonage. And when I did our taxes that first year, it took every penny we had to pay the IRS. Several years later, he had moved to hospital chaplaincy, and one year as I was preparing our tax returns I discovered that I had filed the previous 2 years incorrectly. Correcting that cost us some cash yet again. You could say I've "paid" for my expertise! I've sure learned how to do it right.

The Tax Code considers Clergy to be "Hybrid Employees." Have you ever heard of such a thing? Because they are not treated like every other employee, I often find situations where they were paid---and their W-2 or 1099-MISC is reported---incorrectly. There are many variations used by Church Treasurers and other employers to pay Clergy employees, and unfortunately many of these variations are wrong. It takes special expertise to be able to prepare Clergy taxes as they are reported, while also guiding Clergy and their employers to do things the IRS way.

By the way, I welcome contact from Clergy and Church Treasurers with questions about this Complex Area of the Tax Code. It doesn't matter where you live---I have helped Clergy members across the nation, using the internet safely and securely. You can receive your completed tax returns as PDF files on a CD, or you can download them securely, or---old school---you can get them on actual paper.

I speak regularly to groups, including Clergy, Clergy Spouses, Seminarians, Chaplains, Church Treasurers & Leaders, and Tax Pros about Clergy Taxes. I'd love to speak to your group! Give me a call today at: 614.329.2966 ---or--- email me at: Deb@OskinTax.com !
