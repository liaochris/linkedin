Mark Glann's Summary

Mark Glann, the Proprietor of MG Practical Solutions, has spent over 20 years in the Information Technology and Telecommunications industry. VoIP (Voice Over Internet Protocol) & in particular SIP IP/PBX technology has come of age in recent times with the advent of better quality broadband technologies. Also with the rolling out of new Australian initiatives such as the NBN (National Broadband Network) voice and data communications will inevitably be conducted across the one medium , in most cases fibre optic topology.

Business people need to be prepared for the great changes in communication & ICT (Internet Communications Technology) which is about to occur in the coming months and years ahead.

This led him to establish MG Practical Solutions. The business vision is to provide Australians and Australian Businesses with consultancy, advice and support over the complex issues confronted by businesses concerning their current carrier/providers and communication systems generally.

Mark believes that VoIP, PSTN, ISDN, DSL, SHDSL have their purpose and every business has to be evaluated and surveyed for a suitable custom solution. Many people believe that there is only one choice of carrier/provider when it comes to communications and this can become a very costly belief indeed. Most businesses have a high cost of ownership with their communications.
