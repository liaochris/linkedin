Joe C. D'Amato's Summary

I have developed a 16-year career that started in the trenches of studio I.T. technical support. I've since had 6+ years under CTOs and Directors of Technology, managing their process, projects, and people. Since 1998, I have had roles exclusively in Information or Production Technology.

After getting my start at ILM/Lucasfilm, I’ve since held positions at two Walt Disney Studios companies as well as The Orphanage and Digital Domain. Technology and resource management are my core areas of expertise. I’ve supported immense render farms far exceeding 1000 machines and disk storage measured in petabytes.

My technology skill set is overshadowed by my aptitude for managing sizable support and engineering teams. I have developed a sophisticated ability to resolve infrastructure and production support challenges, such as how to apportion scarce resources and the importance of scalability. My experience includes working with international teams, clients, and vendors. I am comfortable making hard decisions and serving as a facilitator between competing departmental interests.

- Demonstrated success over 16 years with successive promotions at high-pressure film-production studios
- Educated and experienced in resource planning, project management, accounting, team building, and budgeting
- Managed and mentored teams of technicians, and engineers in all aspects of employment
- Certified as MCSE
- Directed large-scale system rollouts and upgrades to a widely varied user-base
- Developed analytical and communication skills – work well with executive staff and clients
