Loretta Robinson's Summary

I'm a San Francisco based Graphic Designer, recently moved to Chicago for a few years.

I have over 2 years experience freelancing for clients large and small. I specialize in identity design for small business and design for beauty and fashion.

I stay active in the design community by volunteering for my local AIGA chapter. In San Francisco I was very grateful to have been a member of the AIGASF board, having met some amazing people. Though I've graduated, I am a life learner and dedicate my free time to continuing my education and staying inspired. I'm also equally as passionate about educating others. I keep a blog: www.creatively-driven.com where I write about freelancing and tips and tricks I've learned.

Though i've been trained primarily in print, I've made an effort to learn html and css and have explored UX and UI.

The programs I use the most are:
Photoshop, Indesign, Illustrator, and Dreamweaver

Specialties:
Design for small business: identity, websites, collateral
Design research
Design for beauty and fashion
Paper goods and photography.
