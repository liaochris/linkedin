Laura Mancinelli's Summary

If I'm not conducting an auction, I'm attending an auction. I'm an auction addict and I absolutely love auctions!

Following a career of more than 20 years in human resources, I moved to Florida, attended auction school and became the Rookie Bid Calling Champion for the State of Florida.

My focus is on real estate auctions and I currently work in an executive capacity with RealEstateAuctions.com, a national firm with a goal of bringing buyers and sellers together in the fastest, most logical and most transparent way of transacting real estate. I also have my own company, ProsperityAuctions.com.

Auctions have strong benefits for buyers and sellers over the traditional, negotiated-sale business model in real estate. With consumers---meaning buyers AND sellers---demanding more and more control in virtually every aspect of life, auctions are clearly the method of choice going forward in the real estate industry.
