Brooke Duckart's Projects

  • cat WACO

    • June 2010 to October 2011
    Team Members: Brooke Duckart

    "cat WACO" is an animated music video I created for my first Stop Motion class at UWM. The video was created for a song (of the same title) preformed by Screamin’ Cyn-Cyn & the Pons; a Madison-based Theatrical Punk band. The music video uses Claymation, Stop-Motion, Puppetry and some 2D elements to tell the story about a lonely man who's filled his small home (and heart) with a family of 75 felines. His family becomes threatened when the city’s sanitation officials send a notice that they are coming to take the cats away. The story/song is dark humored with satirical references to the "Waco Siege of 1993."

    Quote - "Kittens, this is our WACO - we're going down in flames!"
