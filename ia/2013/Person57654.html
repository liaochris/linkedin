James McElwee's Summary

James McElwee qualified as a solicitor in 2007 and currently runs his own general practice which deals with all aspects of law including the following:
• Litigation including Commercial Court, general civil litigation, landlord and tenant matters litigation, debt collection, intellectual property, employment, personal injuries, family law and road traffic offences;
• Residential and general commercial conveyancing and all related transactions;
• Intellectual Property and Information Technology. Trademark Agent; and
• Corporate and commercial work.

• James has more recently been involved in dealing with NAMA and bank queries in relation to the acquisition of properties by NAMA.

Specialties

Include residential and commercial conveyancing, landlord and tenant litigation, general and commercial litigation, debt collection, personal injuries, family law, liquor licensing, intellectual property, wills and probate, corporate work and company secretarial matters.
