Jason Rafail's Summary

Over 12 years of experience in the Cyber Security arena with specific skills ranging from software tool development, team management, training, and development of policies and procedures, as well as product and business development.

Experience working in Nuclear Cyber Security focusing on system hardening and performing auditing and assessments to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements, including 10 CFR 73.54. In-depth experience with the Nuclear Regulatory Commission’s Regulatory Guide 5.71 and the Nuclear Energy Institute’s 08-09, 10-09 and 04-04 guideline, assessment processes, and requirements. Performed numerous assessments of Westinghouse AP1000 Nuclear Reactor designs, systems and components.

Extensive experience developing customer relationships and contacts both in the public and private sectors. Experience in International and Domestic business development and collaborative opportunity identification and evaluation. Specialized skills in Cyber Security include incident and vulnerability analysis and assessment, source code review and analysis, penetration and intrusion testing, web application testing, secure system design and network architecture design, as well as development of operating policies and procedures. Development and delivery of training focused on all of the areas above, as well as the establishment and development of Computer Security Incident Response Teams (CSIRTs) worldwide. Managed several teams focused on delivering products and services to industry and government sectors.

Domestic liaison for the following US Government agencies: Department of Justice, Department of State, National Cyber Security Division (NCSD), Department of Homeland Security (DHS), the Federal Bureau of Investigation and United States Secret Service. International Liaison for the CERT/CC hosting and establishing communications with over 80 government agencies (domestic and foreign) and International CSIRTs at government, university and industry levels.
