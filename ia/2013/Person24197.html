Chris Marlow's Summary

I Only Care About Getting Leads and Sales for My Clients

If you’re on a journey to find the right copywriter for a project, let me introduce myself. I’m Chris Marlow and I’m on a perpetual profit quest.

My goal has always been to bring leads and sales to my clients. And even in this day of social media and engagement… I still focus on results.

Proven results, that is. And you can see it for yourself in my Direct Response Scorecard, which is yours for the asking, at www.chrismarlow.com.

All you have to do is complete the form and I’ll send you a fact sheet of real statistics from my many years getting leads, sales and ROI for my clients.

When you visit my site be sure to check out my Case Studies page offering dozens of entertaining success stories, and my Samples page for a deeper look at my copy and concepts.

Although you’ll see a lot of Direct Mail packages as you sail through this site, I also write Video Sales Letters (VSLs), White Papers, Websites, Case Studies, Booklets, Landing Pages, Email Campaigns, Autoresponders, Magalogs, PowerPoint Presentations, Scripts & Speeches — heck, whatever it takes to get the lead or the sale!


Other notable mentions:

• Creator of the Marlow Marketing Method™, since 2003 a business-building and lead-generation system for small businesses

• Creator of the world's only statistical pricing guideline for copywriter pricing, the Freelance Copywriters Fee & Compensation Survey

• The first to coach copywriters in direct marketing principles and direct mail campaigns

1998 – 2011 (13 years) Lake Oswego OR and Palm Desert CA

I've written direct response copy for every size company, organization and institution in countless niches. One year I spent the entire year writing alternative health magalogs for FC&A Publishing; and at another time in my career, I specialized in software. I have always specialize in lead-gen or order-gen work. Or more to the point, promotions that make money!

Primary agency-side clients during those years included The Kern Organization and Creative Direct Marketing Group, both legendary for their accomplishments in the direct response world.

High level of creative concepting, results-oriented Gary Halbert-style copywriting, offer development, and strategy all delivered in a high-pressure agency environment.
