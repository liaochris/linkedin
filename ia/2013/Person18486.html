Bryan Gallinger's Summary

100% passionate about everything that I do, I am known as THE DREAM PROMOTER. I am currently working on various projects doing business development, marketing/branding and as a producer/musician. My current projects include project management, supporting an advocacy fighting the battle on sex trafficking, producing/performing rock music, restaurant/bar management, developing a new online community and running professional networking groups.

Straight out of high school, my first entrepreneur endeavors began with producing events, producing and performing music. Since 1994, I have been the percussionist and producer IDLEMINE MUSIC in addition to working with various other music projects over the years. In 1999 I started modeling and working as a tv personality. This work took me to Europe including working as an MTV VJ.

In 2001, I founded The Grassroots Network which produced a variety of multimedia channels including events music, tv, film, graphic/web design, promotions and marketing. Event production was the first channel of GRN producing concerts, specialty events, fundraisers and other types of events throughout the Greater Northwest areas and Canada.Through GRN, I produced and hosted Xtreem TV (2000-2003), an international show showcasing live music, action sports, extreme lifestyles and music videos. Also within GRN, I produced and hosted The Grassroots Radio Show (2010), a magazine-style online radio program highlighting talent, entrepreneurs and philanthropists. After some personal development and involvement with the Quixtar/Amway corporations, I began doing public speaking and coaching on areas of personal and business development. After building a friendship with a survivor of the Columbine Shooting, I started supporting advocacies by producing fundraisers, awareness campaigns and eventually as a speaker for Rachel's Challenge in 2011.

My mission is to inspire, empower, impact and elevate others around me regardless of the project that involves me!

Communication, Leadership, Event Production, Business Development, Mentorship/Coaching, Public Speaking, TV Personality, Percussion, Music Production, Marketing/Branding.
