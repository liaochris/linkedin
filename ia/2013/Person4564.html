Connie Nash's Summary

Connie Nash is a transformational coach and life purpose expert on a mission to amplify the song in your heart so you can begin doing what you love. During her 20-year personal growth and self-awareness journey, Connie discovered her own life purpose through healing her deepest pain and now successfully guides other empaths and sensitives to do the same in far less time. Using her proven system, The 5 Pillars of Personal Mastery, Connie will put you on the fast track to finding your soul’s unique purpose putting you squarely on your path to living the fulfilling, joyful and abundant life you crave and deserve. You will experience exponential growth in a safe space of pure love as she utilizes her gifts of compassion, sensitivity and intuition to expertly guide you no matter where you are now to precisely where you want to be. In 2010, she launched Align with Love, a coaching practice that helps you pinpoint specifically where you are stuck, providing you laser focused clarity about how to move forward by connecting you to your own inner guidance, empowering you to trample your fears so you can fast forward into the life you’ve always wanted. Since the age of 5, Connie has dreamed about being “on stage” as a way to teach, inspire and uplift others and is now living her dream by shining the spotlight on you, empowering you to step into your gifts and embrace your own platform of brilliance. Connie is a native Marylander and enjoys being outdoors in nature and learning from the wisdom of animals including her beloved cat.

For more information about Connie, go to http://connienash.com
