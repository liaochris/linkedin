Brian Gast's Summary

Executive life coach, team facilitator, writer, speaker, entrepreneur, and seasoned leader. I coach high-performing executives and successful entrepreneurs on becoming more effective leaders and on moving from success to fulfillment. I combine my unique experience as a CEO of public and private companies with my training as a coach and group facilitator, personal development work, and natural insight to help others gain greater influence, effectiveness and clarity. I help leaders navigate confusing business, personal and interpersonal transitions and situations. I help teams become more aligned and move their focus from interpersonal dramas to effectiveness in the marketplace.
