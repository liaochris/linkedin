Carl Grant's Summary

Carl N. Grant, III is EVP, Business Development for Cooley where he leads a ten-person business development team firm wide. He is known as a super-connector in the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Mr. Grant previously served as Executive Director-Technology Industry for PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP where he led business development in the DC Metro Region and drove unprecedented growth. Prior to joining PwC, Mr. Grant managed the Capital Attraction Program for the Fairfax County EDA, an initiative that more than doubled the number of venture capital funds in that county. Immediately before joining Cooley, Mr. Grant served as VP, Business Development for two venture capital-backed technology companies in the northern Virginia area, EYECAST Corporation and cyberCFO. Mr. Grant was also a Director in a division of Multimedia, Inc. leading up to and through Gannett's $1.7 billion acquisition of the company.

Mr. Grant served over 10 years in the Army National Guard and Reserve as an Airborne Infantry Officer, where he achieved the rank of Major. As Public Affairs Officer for the 29th Infantry Division -- a five-state, 11,000 soldier unit -- he was responsible for media relations, community relations and command information. Mr. Grant led a number of successful overseas public affairs missions during his two tours to Central America. He was awarded the Virginia Bronze Star for meritorious service as Aide to the Adjutant General of Virginia.

Mr. Grant is Chairman of the Board and President of High Tech Prayer Breakfast - DC Metro, Inc., an all-volunteer non-profit, 501(c)(3) organization that hosts an annual breakfast attended by as many as 800 people. Mr. Grant serves on the Board of Directors for Catoctin Bible Church, and was previously on the Board of Directors for Leadership Fairfax, Inc. and the ACG National Capital Chapter. He was a Commissioner for the Loudoun County EDC and is an active member of NVTC and MAVA.

Mr. Grant is a Private Pilot.

Specialties

Assisting growth oriented companies through practical business solutions, strategic business advice and high-impact targeted referrals to sources of financing, potential partners, key hires, potential buyers/sellers and other high-end service providers.
