Steve Hartman's Summary

Graphic Designer and Branding Background:
Steve Hartman is Creative Director at Falk Harrison, a branding strategy and graphic design firm and social media agency. Steve Hartman utilizes the power of solid branding & design thinking for corporate and brand communications.

Graphic Designer and Branding is not all:
Falk Harrison’s clients have a wide range of graphic design, branding and social media needs including messaging, brand identity development, books, magazines, employee communications, financial communications, event marketing, advertising, signage and social media strategy.

Graphic Design and Branding for any industry:
Steve Hartman designs for a variety of industries including biomedical, chemical, communications, legal, education, entertainment & gaming, financial, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, telecommunications, textile, transportation, and non-profit.

Graphic Design and Branding Advocacy:
Steve is a former national board member of AIGA, The Professional Association for Design. AIGA is the largest design association. Steve has taught design and visual communications at Washington University, Webster University, Maryville University and the University of Missouri St. Louis. Steve attended the premier session of AIGA Business Perspectives for Design Leaders at the Harvard Business School. His reputation has awarded him design accolades, international judging opportunities, and advisory panel roles through out the country.

Graphic Design and Branding for these clients:
Steve has consulted on branding, graphic design and social media for a wide range of clients including: The Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum, Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, Eastern Illinois University, University of Illinois, Webster University, Washington University, The Missouri Historical Society, Arch Coal, American Electric Power, Helzberg Diamonds, HBF Textiles, Macy's, Dillards, Inc., Caterpillar, Belden, Reinsurance Group of America.

Specialties

Graphic Design, Branding Strategy, Brand Identity Design, Publication Design, Advertising, Employee Communications, Financial Communications, Corporate Communications, New Product Identity, Sales and Marketing Collateral, Social Media
