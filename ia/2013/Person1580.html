Adam Pollicino's Summary

Adam founded his award winning interactive agency MissionE in 2001. Under his direction and cutting edge service suite, the MissionE team has helped companies like Disney, Kimberly Clark, 24 Hour Fitness, Hewlett Packard, Bacardi, American Standard along with over 2000 small businesses and start-ups. They have won 11 awards for their work including the DBI best practices award.

Since 2005, Adam has been responsible for incubating several Web 2.0 successes. He activley invests and consults through his seed venture firm/incubator Venture Serve.

Currently Adam is helping direct several of his firms investments including:

SearchControl.com a rapidly growing web presence and reputation management service with cutting edge SMB technology called Control Tower. Their new platform allows websites, small businesses and non-profits to manage their entire web presence from one web dashboard.

LegalACE.com a technology that allows for the automated creation of thousands of legal forms and it's charity LegalACE for Troops offering free legal services to active military.

He currently operates an active online resource for non-profits through his charity blog and donation website www.AdamPollicino.org

Adam has been activley speaking, blogging and writing about business entrepreneurship and the web for 10 years; he continues to bring valuable information and experiences to small business and aspiring entrepenuers around the world on his business blog www.AdamPollicino.net
