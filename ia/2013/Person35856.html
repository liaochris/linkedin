Marc van Vliet's Summary

Distinctive was founded by Marc van Vliet in January 2000 and has over 13 years experience in the design and development of complex data warehouse systems.

Distinctive is a small company specialized in data warehouse and business intelligence solutions in the financial industry (banking/insurance).

Our unique strength is the dual expertise:
knowledge of the financial products and processes in the industry, coupled with knowledge of information technology.

Distinctive can be responsible for the solution design in different implementation phases. We take the role as data warehouse consultant, designer or solution architect.

We also give support in master data management, dimensional data modeling, data mapping (ETL), information analysis and (commercial) data migrations.

For more information, curriculum vitae download, etc. visit our website : http://www.distinctive.it
