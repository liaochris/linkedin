Michael Law,CFP®, CLU®, AIF®'s Summary

Michael has spent over a decade in the financial services industry helping both clients and advisors reach their goals. Utilizing a consultative approach to help others build more profitable and fulfilling businesses.

Throughout his career, he has pioneered marketing and sales strategies that were the genesis for many programs adopted and implemented company-wide. Michael possesses an unparalleled ability to build iron-clad relationships with financial advisors. With his extensive knowledge and experience he assists advisors to strengthen their brand, develop strategies to acquire new clients and bullet proof relationships with existing clients. Michael works closely with advisors and investment analysts on portfolio construction, assisting in the selection of appropriate investment instruments to build the right portfolio for each client situation.

Previously, Michael was a Regional Vice President at First American Funds, the asset management arm of US Bank, where he helped increase distribution of their funds. Earlier on he was a Regional Sales Director and Product Manager with RiverSource Distributors, the asset management arm of Ameriprise Financial. While there Michael helped grow the outside distribution of fixed and variable annuity products and worked closely to rebrand the company as they moved from American Express to Ameriprise Financial and RiverSource Distributors. Before RiverSource, Michael worked as a financial advisor for several years, guiding his clients through the difficult markets following the technology bubble of the late ‘90’s.

Michael holds a Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Business Management from The University of Wisconsin - Madison. He is a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ and holds the FINRA Series 7, 63, and 65 licenses as well as the Minnesota Life and Health insurance license.
