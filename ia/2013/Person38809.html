SAN FRAN SANDWICH
is the work of Dustin McNichols, born in San Antonio of a Texan spirit and raised in the suburbs with Midwestern values. Dustin has been extremely fortunate to owe his talents to a few extraordinary people and was told once that 'No one gets to where they're going on their own.' So let's do some good and create something together. Dustin currently lives in Oswego with his beautiful wife Kate and their labrador retriever Gray.

Dustin is a designer, illustrator and solutions creator, coming off an eight year stint as assistant Art Director for an all-encompassing display, retail merchandising & print house (where he began his career as an intern). Dustin formed his studio to continue his passion for doing good work for some really great people, hoping to keep making that little bit of difference for as many as he can.
