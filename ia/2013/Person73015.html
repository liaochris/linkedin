Kathy Russell's Summary

I am the proud owner and founder of Butler Girl Services. We are a full service Concierge Company. Offering: Concierge Services, Wedding/Event Planning & Home Organizing.

By combining the skills acquired during my corporate past and my passion for planning and organizing, I made the decision, with my partner (Mike), to change my life by starting a business that allowed me to enjoy what I love doing; planning Weddings and Events and helping busy people with their ‘stuff’.

Butler Girl Wedding & Event Service provides full event and wedding planning services with creativity, organization, attention to detail unique to each client. We work with whatever size budget you have. Planning celebrations is our passion. Whether a large affair or a small intimate occasion, our creations reflect your vision and style. We absolutely love what we do and that is reflected in each event we produce. No two events are alike. We are committed to what we do and we strive not to let you down.

Butler Girl Concierge Services is one solution to today's busy peoples’ lifestyle. Our service plays an important role because after all, who isn’t pressed for time with our jobs and schedules? There just aren’t enough hours in the day to get everything done. We offer a vast array of services that include (but not limited to), running all types of errands, organizing your home, house-sitting, taking your car for service, pet sitting, shopping etc...... Yes, we pretty much do anything you need, as long as it’s legal and ethical. So, just ask us…

I also believe in giving back to the community. I volunteer at Southlake Regional Healthcare Centre, Chamber of Commerce Newmarket, Business Awards Committees & Women's Centre of York Region Events & Style for a Cause Event.
