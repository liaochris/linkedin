John Tolerico's Summary

John Tolerico is a licensed Real Estate Broker in California with the most highly regarded real estate company, Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices California Properties. Nominated Best Realtor SignonSanDiego for 2011, 2012 and again in 2013; voted by La Jolla area Agents and clients as one of the best Real Estate Agents in La Jolla 3 times; taking the top ranking in 2009.

John started in the business after graduating from the University of Southern California with a major in Business, and emphasis with exceptional accomplishment and completion of the prestigious Entrepreneur Program at USC.

There are many qualities and skills that go into being a consistent top-producing real estate professional: integrity, in-depth community and market knowledge, marketing savvy, effective negotiation skills and a high-quality professional network, all of which are hallmarks of how John works.

Properties listed with John Tolerico are given maximum exposure to buyers with his local, national and international marketing. John's advertising brings properties listed with him to the forefront of the San Diego real estate market.

That said, in his 18+ years of experience as a La Jolla real estate professional, John has also found that providing the very best service is essentially about putting his clients first. This means keeping himself accessible, being a good listener as well as a good communicator, and responding quickly to your needs.

Specialties: Areas of expertise range from San Diego's coastal properties in La Jolla, Del Mar, the 56 corridor, Pacific Beach to Point Loma and Coronado, pocket listings (homes not officially listed in the M.L.S.), including attached and residential income properties. I work with many high-profile business and sports professionals and therefore anonymity is important to me and my clients.
