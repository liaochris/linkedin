Kain Tietzel's Summary

I am a digital marketing engineer with a practical aptitude for delivering clever interactive experiences. I am a co-founder of PhotoMerchant Ltd and the founder of Everybody Internet, a digital consultancy offering creative direction, user experience, content marketing, lean marketing and interactive production for SME's, agencies and startups. My depth of experience across different industries, and at all levels of the digital production process, afford me a thorough understanding of the strategic and practical applications of available digital solutions.

Over the past 15 years I have helped businesses of all sizes to formulate and execute digital marketing, advertising and entertainment experiences that get results. While not every client is a name brand you might recognise, I have worked with Australian clients such as Audi, Fairfax, Virgin Blue, Hoyts, Department of Defence, Telstra, Optus, Yahoo7, Oakley, Southern Star Endemol, Foxtel, Goldman Sachs JBWere, Commonwealth Bank, Westpac and Channel 4, FilmFour, BBC and Lego in the United Kingdom.

I am a passionate social media practitioner, a natural presenter and have lectured in digital marketing for General Assembly Sydney, Australian Film, Television and Radio School (AFTRS) and the College of Fine Arts (CoFA).
