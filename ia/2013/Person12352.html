Brett Circe's Summary

I am a partner at Starmark. I have been president of two other technology companies, TKOnet and OmniPilot Software. I am the chief architect of my agency’s interactive offerings. A Massachusetts native, I hold a BFA in graphic design from the Ringling College of Art and Design. I was the recipient of the 2006 South Florida Business Journal Up & Comers Award in the category of Advertising and PR, and recently I earned my Certificate in Advanced Mobile Marketing from the University of San Francisco. I frequently discuss Mobile Marketing, MCommerce, LBS, Green, and things that are the latest, greatest, newest and best (and I drive a Volt).

Specialties:
* Emerging Online Media, Social, Mobile, Video
* Advertising and Marketing ROI and Effectiveness Tracking
* Web-based Software Planning and Architecture
* Process Streamlining, Creating Efficiencies through Automation
