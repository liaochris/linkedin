Michael Nix's Summary

I'm a Principal Creative Lead for TD Ameritrade, in the Advertising and Brand Management department. I do interactive art direction with a group of designers and product owners focusing on user interface, UX, and simple, clean design. Systems include the retail trading platform, Trade Architect, style guides, and pattern libraries.

I also run Pinatubo Creative LLC, focusing on interactive web design and development, branding, storyboards, creative direction, and print design. Clients include Harvard University, Ritz Carlton, Rotary International, Northwestern University, and several law firms.

Specialties: responsive web design, web development, WordPress, CSS, XHTML, javascript, web standards, typography, layout, motion graphics, storyboards, art direction, prototyping, information architecture, usability analysis, accessibility, branding, print, graphic design
