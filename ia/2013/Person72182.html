Karen Peña's Summary

Karen has worked in the Hospitality Industry for over 30 years. She is Self Employeed and is a Global Account Executive for ConferenceDirect and sources facilities all over the world for groups of 10 to 10,000 at no cost to her customers. In 2011, Karen was honored with the ConferenceDirect Platinum Award for being a Top 10 Producer.

She enjoys all aspects of both of her businesses.

One thing many people do not know about Karen is that she was a Wingwalker in the airshow industry, performing in air shows accross the country and Canada for over a decade.

In 1994 she won the Sportsman National Aerobatic Championship Trophy flying aerobatics in an airplane that her father built. Karen has created a Keynote about her experiences flying called "A Wing and a Prayer".

Specialties

Networking and negotiating hotel contracts for groups of any size in any hotel, anywhere in the world and providing a complete solutions packages for corporations who are interested in transparency and stretegic meetings management.
