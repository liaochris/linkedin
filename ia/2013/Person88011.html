Mary Bella's Summary

Founded by Mary Bella in 2000, Maestra is a Toronto-based small-business which specializes in designing interactive, multi-media websites for musicians, actors, visual artists and arts organizations.

While my post-secondary education is in music, I have worked extensively in arts administration and have been designing websites for over 10 years. My attention to detail coupled with my artistic aesthetic lend themselves to web design, a vocation I combine with a part-time performing career. I now have a growing roster of over 30 clients, ranging from solo artists to performing ensembles and PR companies.

I was Choir Manager of the High Park Choirs of Toronto for 6 years, and before that, Administrator of the Canadian Opera Volunteer Committee. I understand the needs and challenges of a volunteer-run arts organization, as well as those of a performing ensemble. I would love to help you develop an online presence that will really speak to the unique artistry of what you do.

Please see my online portfolio and visit my clients’ sites at http://www.MaestraWebDesign.com.
