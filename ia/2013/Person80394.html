Leslie Lee Sanders' Summary

ABOUT THE AUTHOR & BLOGGER:

Teaching the art and craft of writing fiction to aspiring authors and indie authors on my website and blog where I also provide insider tips and information on the book publishing industry. I’m the indie published author of over a dozen works of fiction and erotic romance since 2005. And currently published with Breathless Press and Xcite Books.
