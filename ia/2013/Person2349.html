Anthony Gibson's Summary

I am an over-compulsive perfectionist who believes in using his time as efficiently as possible. I am an effective public speaker that understands how to adapt to different audiences. I am a student, not just in college, but in life, I believe in learning from the world around me. I am a dreamer who is never satisfied and believes in quality over quantity. And I understand just how powerful perception is in this world.

I don’t get enough sleep because I can never shut my mind down. I don’t see “educator” as a future job title, but thoroughly enjoy teaching others what I know. I enjoy thinking more critically about the world around me and can listen to almost any type of music while doing so. My ideas fall somewhere between old fashioned, new age, and most practical, and I see Social Media as a potential tool for society, if we use it right.

I don’t like to talk about myself because I would rather let everyone around me explain who I am. I believe that strong ethics is something we should bring back to the world. While I specialize in Digital Media, I have a general understanding of many industries.
