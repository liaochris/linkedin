Andrew Gitt's Summary

GVU Technology (http://gvutechnologies.com) develops and distributes software. We get our user base 100% organically from the search engines that are searching for our software apps.

We monetize in a couple ways:
1) By bundling advertisers in the install process.
2) By adding 'add-on' monetization components using a Java Script and sharing a rev share.

We are in development of creating our own 'add-on' monetization components and look forward to sharing our Java Script with other companies on a rev share basis.
