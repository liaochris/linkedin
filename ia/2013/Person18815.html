Bud Vieira's Summary

I am a software product manager with >15 years experience bringing software to market for the enterprise and consumer internet markets. I have managed the process from vision and roadmap, through product design and requirements, development and release, to final installation and support. I also have extensive experience in software product design. Earlier in my career, I was a management consultant specializing in business process analysis and implementation for large companies, including design and implementation of groupware and workflow tools.

Most recently I have joined the Technical Enablement team in the Customer Centric Engineering function at Salesforce.com, where I do proactive education and spread best practices to customers around Security and Enterprise Scale for Cloud Platforms / Apps.

Customer Education and Critical Issue Response, Requirements Management, Project Management, Usability, Interaction Design, program management, high performance teams, Process Design

-- Past Experience --
