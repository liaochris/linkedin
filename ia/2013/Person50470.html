Greg Zavitz's Summary

28+ years in the Bloomington Normal Illinois area selling homes and condos to 1st time home buyers, mover uppers and investors. I also market homes for sellers by using the internet to it's fullest extent. My listings show up on every major search engine and home marketing website. I also do small commercial leasing, market and sell 2-8 unit multi family properties...

Specialties: New construction, single family homes, condos, town homes and zero lot line properties. I give free comparative market analysis on homes for prospective sellers. I heavily market all my seller's listings on every major search engine and home marketing website.
