Jason Ferguson's Summary

I have 14 years of experience working in professional theatre and entertainment. I have worked in a variety of roles such as PR, stage management, general management, writing, events and producing.

Highlights of my career include working with maestro Anton Coppola at Opera Tampa, learning how to run a theatre under Judy Lisi at the Straz Center for the Performing Arts, seeing how to produce a Broadway show while working for Manny Azenberg on shows like Movin' Out, witnessing Keith Lockhart conduct his first opera at Boston Lyric Opera, working with Sean 'Diddy' Combs at Bad Boy as he balanced celebrity with running an entertainment group, moving to London to work in the West End and with Kevin Spacey's Old Vic Theatre and Punchdrunk, producing a short film, watching children from around the world come to the Google Science Fair, having my short story be a finalist in the Glimmer Train awards, and producing the critically acclaimed Off-West End production of Ragtime.

Jason Ferguson's Experience

Director of Public Relations & Social Media

Flat Rock Playhouse

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Performing Arts industry

March 2013 – Present (7 months) Asheville, North Carolina Area

I oversee the public relations and social media for this Equity theatre located outside of Asheville. Flat Rock Playhouse is the State Theatre of North Carolina and is made up of a 500 seat mainstage and a 250 seat downtown studio space.

The Playhouse’s dual mission of producing the performing arts and providing education in the performing arts includes a ten-show professional series; a summer and fall college apprentice and intern program; performances and cabaret series by the YouTheatre; year-round classes and workshops for students from kindergarten through adults. Flat Rock Playhouse now hosts over 90,000 patrons annually and is a major contributor to the local economy and the Arts in North Carolina.
