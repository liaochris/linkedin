Gil Elegado's Summary

Gil Elegado is the founder and CEO of Suite Four Eleven, a proactive digital marketing agency whose core focus is website design / development, social media (Facebook, Twitter, Linked In, YouTube, Pinterest), and online branding.

Gil is responsible for overseeing all client services; from designing and developing websites, to creating innovative social media marketing strategies, to campaign implementation, and online brand management. Always determined, he is completely passionate about leveraging relationship-based marketing and interactive experience as a powerful tool for the growth and sustainability of businesses and brands.

Gil has a background in Marketing & Finance from the University of Alberta with vast work-experience in the areas of integrated marketing, social media (Facebook, Twitter, Linked In, YouTube, Pinterest), web development, graphics design, web analytics, seo, process development, and project management.

Gil has worked with a number of distinguished clients build and maintain an effective social media and online presence, some of which include:

- Regency Developments (The Pearl Tower, HSD Properties)
- New In Town Welcome Service by Bent Arrow & Boyle Street
- Celico Builders
- Tower Mortgage
- Cocktail & Details

Suite Four Eleven will be happy to answer ANY questions you have regarding marketing, social media, or technology. Feel free to tweet us www.twitter.com/suitefoureleven or post on our timeline www.facebook.com/suitefoureleven.
