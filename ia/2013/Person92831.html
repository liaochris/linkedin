Michael Johansen's Summary

A passionate and dedicated Safety Professional with a drive for results with over 12 years of experience in manufacturing, distribution and fleet safety, loss control and risk management. Michael Johansen's Experience

Health & Safety Manager

Dick's Sporting Goods

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; DKS; Retail industry

May 2010 – Present (2 years 10 months)

Corporate operations safety management position accountable for the Health & Safety Process and Training of over 300 warehouse associates and staff at the 750,000 square foot regional distribution center in Atlanta, GA.

Put into action strategic plans and communication that has resulted in direct reduction of the Medical Incident Rate (based on OSHA 200,000 man-hour calculation) from 17.73 to 5.30; a 70% improvement; and continuing to drive the rate well below the industry average. Successfully reduced claims dollars with a 59% improvement with sustained progress.

Management project team member in the creation and implementation of a new Behavioral Based Safety Program to drive towards Best in Class safety results. Directly responsible for the development of a positive recognition incentive program, observation program, culture shift towards recognizing safety behaviors, and serve as subject matter expert.

Coordinate and administer all OSHA and DOT compliance training and job certifications to ensure proper guidance under the Safety Management Systems including New Hire Orientations, Powered Industrial Truck Training and Certification, Hazard Communication, Energy Isolation (LOTO), and First Responder Training.

Lead and facilitate the building wide Safety Committee in coordination with the new BEST in Class Behavioral Based Safety Program.

Contributed to a successful update and revision of the company Safety Manual including project leadership of the Fall Protection, Energy Isolation (LOTO), and Job Hazard Analysis programs.

Corporate Project Team Member to modify and improve the company wide Emergency Response Plan and Procedures. Successfully updated and launched in conjunction with the Corporate Business Continuity Plan.

Maintain a strong knowledge of the Warehouse Management Systems to see that the Safety Hierarchy of Controls is developed into the Standard Operating Procedures.

Division Safety Manager

Lafarge
