Linda Cole's Summary

I founded Alexandra Publishing in 2009 to bring stories of personal courage to those who are enduring difficult times. My goal is to provide real life experience, insight and useful strategies and resources, empowering readers to meet and overcome their challenges.

My vision is to compile thousands of courageous stories from the multitudes of people who have weathered tragic personal storms and have found their way to a new life. Each Personal Courage® book will contain narratives from different contributors dealing with a specific life challenge such as Alzheimer's in a loved one or a young family losing a parent or divorce and the aftermath.

I helped co-authored my first book, Resurrecting Anthony: A True Story of Courage & Destination, which tells the story of my 12-year-old son's heart attack and brain injury. A look back over the decade since the event helps the reader see how my once perfect family survived the devastating loss of a child and how a new family has come to be.

Entrepreneurial experience- either launching new business or new division of companies. Sales, operations, financial background. Currently launching Alexandra Publishing LLC that will publish The Personal Courage SeriesTM and The Business Courage SeriesTM of books.

Specialties

Start ups. Launching new business or new divisions.
