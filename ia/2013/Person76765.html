Julien Gionis' Summary

Julien Gionis was born in 1982 in France and studied Graphic design at Akto graduating with a BFA (honours) in Graphic design.
He's been working as a freelancer since 2004, producing corporate identities, typographic projects, print design and visual communication programmes for a diverse array of clients worldwide.

In 2010 he founded FX3™ - a multi-disciplinary Design & Branding studio focused on high-quality visual communication and Brand development.

His work has been distinguished through international design awards such as WOLDA (Worldwide Logo Design Annual), HIIIBRAND (International Design & Branding Awards), EBGE (Greek Graphic Design Awards), ERMIS (Greek Advertising & Communication Awards) and also featured in several international books published by Taschen, Rockport, Index, Sandu, Smashing, and magazines such as Choi's Gallery, Slanted, PAGE, and NWP to name a few.

Specialties:
• Corporate & brand identity - Systems & guidelines
• Print design & visual communication
• Web design & development
• Brand development & re-branding procedures
• Graphic design & art direction
• Experimental typography
