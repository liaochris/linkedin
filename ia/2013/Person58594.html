Jan Shillito's Experience

Owner

Celebration Ceremonies & Weddings from thememorybox.com

October 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Yorkshire

Celebration Ceremonies & weddings is a 'real world' extension of thememorybox.com. We help clients mark the special events in their lives, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

We write, conduct and style beautiful, contemporary personal wedding, partnership, renewal of vows, naming and welcoming ceremonies in off-the-beaten-track venues and outdoor locations.

We do weddings at private stately homes, country houses/lodges, weddings at home, garden weddings, festival weddings, glamping weddings, woodland weddings, beach weddings, barn/farm weddings, green weddings and other unique locations. We are Yorkshire based but cover UK.

Unlike Registrar-led ceremonies, we don't do production line or off-the-shelf; all our ceremonies are bespoke and personal, written about you, for you. We want our couples and families to feel "this is us" whether that be a small and intimate celebration or large formal occasion.

We make your ceremony the centrepiece of your celebration and let it set the style and tone of the whole event. Our style is your style, no two couples or families are the same so why should your ceremony be any different?

An informal planning consultation helps couples and families discuss ideas. We help you say ‘I love you’ and put the things you want to say and promise into words to create a ceremony has special meaning.

Couples currently have to do 'the legal bits' to register the marriage with just their two witnessess at the Register Office at some point prior to the ceremony; they are then free to hold their personal ceremony anywhere, any time and style.

I run the company and am one of the first five in the UK to be qualified and approved by Government training body Ofqual, currently the only one in the North. I am also a fully qualified client service professional and designer.

We also offer bespoke design services for stationary collections & monograms.
