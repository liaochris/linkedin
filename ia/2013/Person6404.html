Andrea Fuzimoto MSTCM, L.Ac.'s Summary

Andréa Fuzimoto is a Licensed Acupuncturist with the California Acupuncture Board, and has been working with Traditional Chinese Medicine/Acupuncture since 1998. She is currently a Doctoral candidate of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine at Five Branches University, working on her research study about Multiple Sclerosis. Andréa holds a Master’s Degree of Science in Traditional Chinese Medicine from the American College of Traditional Chinese Medicine, one of the most prestigious programs of TCM in the country. She is certified as a Zhu Scalp Acupuncture Specialist with Dr. Zhu Ming Qing, and Board Certified in Auricular Acupuncture by the Auriculotherapy Certification Institute (ACI) with Dr. Terry Oleson, PhD. Andréa began her studies in Acupuncture in 1995 at the Brazilian College of Acupuncture (BCA/ABACO) after finishing her Bachelor’s Degree in Fine Arts at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro. In 2002 she earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Physical Therapy with emphasis in Neurology, Orthopedics and Rheumatology at the Brazilian Institute of Medicine and Rehabilitation, having received the Special Academic Laureate Diploma for the highest academic achievements of her class. In 2004 the Brazilian Board of Physical Therapy recognized her as a Specialist in Acupuncture. Andréa has her private office in Albany, CA, where patients look for her for her expertise in Chinese Medicine Neurology and careful diagnostic evaluation and strategic treatment planning.

Specialties:
- Neuro-immunological conditions (eg: stroke, multiple sclerosis, spinal cord injury (SCI), neuro myelitis optica (NMO), transverse myelitis, Parkinson's disease, neuropathies, migraines, cognitive impairment...)
- Auto-immune conditions (eg: Guillain-Barre, MS, rheumatoid arthritis, celiac diseases, IBS,…)
- Orthopedics and Rheumatology, pain management (eg: fibromyalgia, arthritis, sciatic pain, back pain,…)
- Stress management and adrenal fatigue, depression, anxiety, sleep disorders
