Ben Consoli's Summary

A multi-talented producer and visual storyteller, Ben Consoli’s credits range from director of photography, editor, director, and video producer to songwriter, composer, and former lead singer of the rock band “Violet Nine.”

During the five years he performed across the country with his band, he became aware of the need for a group to have an equally impressive on-line presence. When he changed creative gears and focused his attention on the marketing side of things, the burgeoning internet video market became the new stage on which all of Ben's talents could be showcased.

He launched a new video production business, BC Media Productions, and his ability to tell a story in video took center stage.

As owner of BC Media Productions, Ben has produced award-winning commercials and brand videos for companies like Nike, Puma, Verizon, Gillette and Pillsbury.

In 2010, he directed a highly-acclaimed commercial for Dragon Naturally Speaking, which aired during the Super Bowl that year, and, along with ads for Kaspersky Lab and Nuance, have gone on to air globally.

In 2012, Ben Consoli was honored with the prestigious Merrimack Valley Business Magazine's "40 Under 40 Award" for his entrepreneurial success.

Ben spent 2012 traveling the country and the world, shooting in Italy, South Africa and Central America for various high-profile projects for BC Media Productions.
