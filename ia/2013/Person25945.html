Chris Moxley's Summary

Started in the Risk Management and insurance placement business 18 years ago. We work with many Business' to reduce their Total Cost of Risk. Types of clients include service, construction, real estate investors, Non-Profits, & manufacturing. I was named Vice President of Professional Insurors in 2005. I obtained my CIC (Certified Insurance Counselor) designation in 1995 and CRIS (Construction Risk & Insurance Specialist) in 2005. I remain active in the Independent Insurance Agents of Oklahoma, Society of Certified Insurance Counselors and the State Chamber of Commerce.

Specialties: Non-Profit Risk Managment & Insurance, Workers Compensation Risk Management, Bonding for Construction, Manufacturing Risk Management.

Customizing Insurance, Risk Management and outside vendors for each business is very important to me. I don't think every solution fits every business so it's crucial to understand the clients need. Being on the managment team at Professional Insurors helps me understand things from the business owner viewpoint and improve my service to my clients.
