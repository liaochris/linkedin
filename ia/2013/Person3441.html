Aldonna R. Ambler, CMC, CSP's Experience

President

AMBLER Growth Strategy Consultants, Inc

May 1971 – Present (42 years 4 months)

Aldonna R. Ambler has earned the right to be called The Growth Strategist®. She has won over 2 dozen national and statewide "entrepreneur of the year" awards for the resilient growth of her own international busineses across 4 recessions...but more importantly, her clients get on, and then stay on, the published lists of the fastest growing privately held companies. A certified management consultant (CMC) and a certified professional speaker (CSP) she brings fresh ideas, insights, and energy about Achieving Accelerated Growth With Sustained Profitability®. As a strategic advisor and speaker, Ambler has earned 92% repeat business from over 800 midsized service, technology and distribution client companies. A positive force for economic development, Ambler Chaired the NJ delegation to the White House Conference on Small Business, has provided expert testimony at over 30 legislative hearings, and conferred with 4 Presidents in the Oval Office/White House about how to help midsized companies continue to grow. She is in her 5th year hosting a peer-to-peer Internet radio show, The Growth Strategist®, where the host, guests, and listeners (180,000+) are Presidents/CEOs of midsized companies (typically $20-200Mil/yr) and discuss the growth strategy of the week.

Owner

same

May 1971 – Present (42 years 4 months)

Aldonna Ambler is a powerful, influential consultant and compelling keynote speaker to diverse, midmarket companies who has spent over 30 years Achieving Accelerated Growth With Sustained Profitability® for over 800 technology-based clients as well as distributors/construction-related companies and professional service firms with 90% repeat business. Ambler is a prolific writer for professional/industry publications and is working on a book on “entrepreneurial styles”. A passionate economic development advocate, Ambler serves on many boards and committees. Ms. Ambler has been named national “Woman Business Owner of the Year,” “Business Woman of the Year,” and “Business Advocate of the Year” by state and national associations. She is the host of a weekly, peer-to-peer Internet radio show, The Growth Strategist™, featuring interviews with CEOs of rapidly growing companies between $20-200 million.
