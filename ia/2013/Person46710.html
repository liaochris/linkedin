Gail Benson's Experience

Principal and Owner

The Benson Group, LLC

January 2009 – Present (5 years)

The Benson Group, LLC, is a professional services firm dedicated to helping organizations manage their ongoing enterprise risks as they relate to technology, regulatory compliance and overall business processes. We work closely with your management team to understand your business risk exposure, develop a specific, targeted approach to evaluate how well those risks are currently being managed, and when they are not well managed, what needs to be done to close the gaps. Services include IT Audit staff augmentation, Internal Audit organizational consulting, Sarbanes-Oxley 404 work, SAS 70 preparation work, Risk Assessments, technology evaluations of start up companies, technology assessments for mergers and/or acquisitions.
