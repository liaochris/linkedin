A specialist consultancy assisting SMEs to secure R&D tax credits. We have a team of 24 consultants across the UK working with Clients and their own accountants. RandDTax deliver a contingent success fee service and offer one of the most competitive rates in the sector. We have successfully delivered more than 200 submissions for many hundreds of qualifying projects and jealously guard our 100% success track record. We have never yet had a submission rejected.

Unlike many of our competitors, who usually seek to introduce their own external tax accountant , we always work with our Client's own trusted accountancy firm. It saves costs and avoids conflicts of interest. (If you are a practising Accountant, then please do contact me for further information on maximising your Client's R&D Tax claim. I will be delighted to discuss this with you. Each of us has our specialism and there is no conflict.)

Because we don't bring in outside Tax Accountants and prefer to work with your own accountant, we have one of the most competitive rates accross the R&D Tax Credit specialist sector. Our standard fee for a new claim is just 12.5% of the successful credit awarded by HMRC.

HMRC state in their recently published statistics for the y/e 31st March 2011 that the average SME claim was more than £40K. (See HMRC/RandD tax credits website)
