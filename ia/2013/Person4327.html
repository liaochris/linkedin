Who is this Alex Vasilakos?
Alex is a rather fearless individual who loves to swim in the deep end of the creative and project management pool. Over the years, he has been an illustrator, marketing manager, project manager, art director and creative director. Alex is also extremely successful and a genuinely decent person with experience in every imaginable area of marketing and communications.

Alex Vasilakos' Projects

  • Hot Harry's Burritos

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Alex Vasilakos, Anthony Venditti, Jared Tomeck, Michaelangelo McKasty, Vladimir Kokovic

    Hot Harry’s, with 7 stores from Ohio to Massachusetts, is an alternative to gimmicky burrito restaurants. To stay competitive with the big budget burrito chains, Hot Harry’s needs to make their marketing investments go as far as possible without sacrificing quality.

    As Creative Director at Burst Marketing I help accomplish 2 goals: drive foot traffic to stores, and connect with new franchisees.

    The strategic web blueprint approach to the project established a baseline of best practices in food marketing: appetizing product shots and copy that tempts the senses. Desktop and mobile websites were developed to deliver tailored experiences to the unique context of each type of visitor, facilitating buying decisions. Prominent conversion points help Hot Harry’s broaden their loyalty base while minimizing marketing expenditures, and more clearly define retail customers and potential franchisees.

  • Albany Pine Bush Preserve

    • April 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: Alex Vasilakos, Anthony Venditti, Jared Tomeck, Shaun Webster

    As Creative Director at Burst Marketing we developed a new website to reflect the unique nature of the Preserve's mission. Visit the Discovery Center at the Albany Pine Bush Preserve for hands-on exploration of the Capital Region’s backyard!

    Over the years the Albany Pine Bush Preserve Commission has undergone significant changes and additions--including the building of the Discovery Center--but its website was no longer modern nor attracting as many visitors. IntelliSites came to the rescue, creating a site that reflects the unique nature of the Preserve's mission. Not only a special habitat spread out over 3,200 acres to protect many of the Capital Region’s plants and animals, the Albany Pine Bush Preserve allows visitors the opportunity to enjoy interactive exhibits and activities. With nearly 18 miles of nature trails, you can participate in recreational pastimes including hiking, jogging along nature paths, nature studies, cross-country skiing, horseback riding, among others!

    Check out http://www.albanypinebush.org/ to see the website Burst Marketing created to help plan your next outdoor adventure and learn about our natural habitat!

  • Adkad Technologies

    • April 2010 to November 2011
    Team Members: Alex Vasilakos, Juan Di Diego, Alyssa Quinn, Jared Tomeck, Shaun Webster, Michaelangelo McKasty

The challenge was that their website had severely outlived its usefulness, and was not up to modern day standards for design. Worse, the site began to lose revenue for the company because of its ineffectiveness.

    Burst Marketing developed a custom ecommerce site that matched their new branding, and was more professional, searchable, and user-friendly.   • Ideology Board Game Design and Packaging Design

    • November 2010 to December 2010
    Team Members: Alex Vasilakos, Zev Shlasinger

    Board game design and packaging design project for Z-man Games, a publisher of games, Ideology: The War of Ideas.

    The project consists of: Rulebook Design – Card Design – Token Design – Packaging Design – Logo Design – Map Creation – Player Cards – Icon Design

    About the Game:
    The conflict of political ideas spawned the conflicts of the 20th Century. More than any other era in human history, nations defined themselves not merely by their military or economic might, but by the ideals that inspired their ambitious achievements.

    Ideology: The War of Ideas is an exciting new strategy game by Z-Man Games. During the game, each player symbolizes one of the 20th Century’s most powerful Ideologies: Capitalism, Communism, Fascism, Imperialism, and Islamic Fundamentalism.

    Beginning with one global region completely under its sway, each Ideology attempts to influence and control the independent regions of the earth. The Ideology that acquires the highest level of global influence will dominate the world for generations to come!

    This second edition includes enhanced graphics, variant rules, and variant abilities to 4 of the 5 Ideologies in the game. Also, we added more Influence cards.
