Jason Bidwell's Summary

I enjoy my job because it involves so many creative elements: movies, computers, photography, art and sometimes music. I feel fortunate that I get paid to do something that I love to do, and this makes the long hours during production seem much easier.

My talents include an artistic eye combined with technical skills to produce photo-realistic imagery. I am knowledgeable of the digital pipeline, and I am proficient with high-end software such as Nuke, Fusion, After Effects Maya, and Flame.

Finally, I earned a master’s degree in visual effects and animation, with an emphasis on digital compositing. My master degree was a good foundation, but the majority of my knowledge has been learned from years of commercial and feature-film production experience.
