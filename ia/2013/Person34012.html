Dena Smith's Summary

Native to the DFW area, Dena has been educated in design, marketing and environmental science. She has spent over a decade in construction and is currently co-owner of a remodeling company that she helped create. Dena has excelled in real estate full time since early 2004. Her experience, creativity, attention to detail and flexible style combine to produce superior service. Utilizing state of the art technology allows her to keep her clients on the cutting edge of the ever changing market.

Dena provides residential services the entire DFW area, concentrating on the central cities of Arlington, Bedford, Coppell, Euless, Grand Prairie, Hurst, Irving, and Las Colinas. A local resident for over 40 years, and an Irving resident since 1995, she knows first hand the advantages the area has to offer. Whether you are buying, selling, leasing or looking for investment properties Dena has the solutions for you.

She is a graduate of Texas Christian University, a current member of the Council of Residential Specialists, The Independent Brokers Network, National Association of Realtors, Texas Association of Realtors, and the Collin County Board of Realtors. Dena is also a Zeta Tau Alpha alumnae and on the Board of Directors for the Nichols Park Neighborhood Association.

Dena has completed several designations including CRS (Certified Residential Specialist), CNE (Certified Negotiation Expert), GRI (Graduate Realtor Institute), SFR (Short Sale & Foreclosure Resource) and TAHS (Texas Affordable Housing Specialist).
