John Forge's Summary

Accomplished leader with record of success building high-growth marketing and go-to-market strategies, and overseeing major product development projects. Provides operations leadership to heighten top-line growth and bottom-line results. Founder

CXP

February 1975 – April 1981 (6 years 3 months)

CXP (Center for Expertise on Progiciels) was the first market and product research organization for the software package industry in Europe.
- Developed the methodology to evaluate software packages and developed a series of seminars for IT executives.
- Built the first extensive database of software packages available on the European market.
- CXP grew to include 80% of all French companies using large computers. It employed 50 people with an $8M in 1980. CXP is now part of Forrester Research.
- Led technical missions in Europe (Spain, the Netherlands, Romania), Africa (Gabon, Ivory Coast), Middle East (Tunisia, Morocco), and Asia (Malaysia).
- The organization became so well known that the word “progiciel” was officially added to the French dictionary to translate “software package” for French speaking countries.
