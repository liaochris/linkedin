Jonathan Hine's Summary

Literary and translations enhanced by professional writing and editorial experience with deep background in financial management, engineering, and public administration. Focus on book-length translations (fiction and non-fiction) and technically-oriented novels.

Specialties: Especially skilled at translating financial, commercial and legal documents for high-tech businesses.
Experienced in a wide range of scientific and military applications.
Recent work includes annual reports, contracts and legal correspondence in a wide range of technical fields. Latest book translations are Schio: Industrial Archeologyby Bernadetta Ricatti (Sassi Editore, 2013) and Beyond the Age of Oil by Leonardo Maugeri (Praeger Publishing, 2010).

Jonathan Hine's Projects

  • Schio: Industrial Archeology

    • October 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jonathan Hine

    New from Sassi Editore, Srl, this book combines stunning photography with readable text to present the new field of archeology, which is flourishing in Northern Italy as scholars race to document the Industrial Revolution before its physical traces vanish forever. The preservation efforts of the communities in the Alto Vicentino are profiled and photographed.

  • Beyond the Age of Oil: Myths and Realities and Futures of Fossil Fuels

    • June 2009 to January 2010
    Team Members: Jonathan Hine, Leonardo Maugeri, Author

    In this eagerly awaited sequel to his prize-winning bestseller, The Age of Oil, Leonardo Maugeri, the strategy director of one of the world's biggest energy companies, puts forward a hardheaded, concrete plan in simple everyday language for how to shift the world economy's primary energy dependence from fossil fuels to renewable energies by 2035.

    Dr. Maugeri covers the different issues so completely and clearly that when you finish the book, you can even disagree with his plan, and know why you do. Priceless for understanding the real issues.
