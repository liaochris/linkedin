State Program Manager - Alaska Land Mobile Radio

State of Alaska - Enterprise Technology Services

Government Agency; 10,001+ employees; Government Administration industry

April 2005 – April 2007 (2 years 1 month)

I took a two year leave of absence from StrateGen, Inc. in order to support this State of Alaska project that had been stalled for ten years.

The Alaska Land Mobile Radio system is a joint program between the State of Alaska and the Department of Defense. During the build-out phase advanced digital radio communications providing secure, on-demand, real-time service to 4,000 first responders across Alaska was deployed. The system is linked through 148 microwave and radio sites that were upgraded or built in some of the most remote and difficult geography in the United State.

* Full budget, personnel, and policy authority for the State of Alaska implementation, operation and maintenance of the Alaska Land Mobile Radio (ALMR) System.

* Responsible for the construction of 19 radio and microwave sites during the 2006 build season (April-Oct), including sites only accessible by helicopter. Current year’s capital budget $17.5 million.

* My major deliverables for the project were successfully completed:
1. Developed and prepared the Site Risk Analysis for all 148 completed sites
2. Developed the Maintenance Management Plan for the system
3. Developed the budget justification to successfully restore $14 million in state funding for the program
4. Prepared the reorganization plan document to align people with the expanded mission requirements of the system
