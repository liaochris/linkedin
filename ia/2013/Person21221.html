Carmen Caruso's Summary

I am a trial lawyer in Chicago with my own firm and have been in practice since 1983.

We have a strong concentration in franchise & dealership cases arising in all industries, and have earned a strong national presence in this field. We are among a handful of law firms in the country that regularly represent franchisees and dealers or their independent associations with considerable success.

Our diverse practice extends to the representation of business owners, officers, directors, shareholders, partners, other professionals, and executive or other key employees in sharply contested cases involving significant economic losses caused by breach of contract, fraud, unlawful employment practices, civil rights violations, trade secret and intellectual property infringement or misappropriation, or other unlawful conduct.

We also represent plaintiffs or defendants in cases involving allegations of legal malpractice or other misconduct by other attorneys.

Clients come to us when they face challenging legal problems and require aggressive, dedicated representation.

We regularly litigate against much larger law firms and we are not out-matched.


Specialties: Trials, Appeals, Commercial Arbitration, Franchise & Dealership Law, Professional Liability, Business Litigation, Complex Cases
