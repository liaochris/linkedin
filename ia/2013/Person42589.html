Erik Grob's Summary

Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) and IT Professional (MCITP) with 20 years of experience, including more than eight years working in government regulated environments.

An experienced information/data architect who can effectively design logical and physical architectures. Highly proficient in managing large enterprise level SQL Server farms; possesses strong communication and problem solving skills; a proven project leader and proactive team member. Proven team leader of database administration teams.

Received B.S. degree in Information Studies (iSchool) from Syracuse University. Graduated with honors.

Co-creator of the pharmaceutical industry’s standards and best practices for web-based clinical trial distribution systems.

Creator of www.FDARegulatory.com - "The Internet's most concise, free resource of FDA compliance information - Information which outlines ethical and scientific quality standards for designing and conducting operations in an FDA regulated environment."
