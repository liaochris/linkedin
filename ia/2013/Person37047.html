Dorothy Willetts, ASID, NCIDQ's Experience

Principal Designer

Willetts Design & Associates, Inc.

April 2006 – Present (7 years 9 months) Palm Springs Area

Founder of the interior design firm, Willetts Design & Associates, based in the Palm Springs area with a concentration in custom residential design. Willetts Design & Associates provides clients with timeless designs of the finest quality and pays attention to the smallest detail with pride, artful discrimination, and forethought. With a client inspired blend of striking furniture, exquisite fabrics, flooring, and accessories — Willetts Design & Associates brings unparalleled experience and expertise to the interior design industry. Services include: architectural detailing, custom cabinet design, landscape consultation, lighting, audio, home theater, smart home consultation, art placement and procurement for collectors.
