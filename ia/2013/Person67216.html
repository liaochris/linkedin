John Watson's Summary

I help entrepreneurs start and grow profitable small and mid-sized companies as internet marketing strategist, marketing coach or fractional CMO (chief marketing officer).

I provide strategic sales and marketing consulting and business development coaching. My focus is on-line sales lead generation, interactive and in-bound marketing, and developing integrated accountable sales and marketing programs.

Over the last 10+ years, I developed a market readiness (pre-marketing) program called Being Profitable: the earnings growth program. The program is for entrepreneurs and business leaders committed to gaining greater control over their growth strategy and marketing investments.

Typical clients are those who are;
Starting Up
Starting To Market
Investing In Growth
Shifting To Interactive, On-line or Inbound Marketing
Wanting Better Results From Marketing Investments.

    Being Profitable is a business development planning framework for business owners without the support of a senior sales and marketing strategist.
