Ben Humphry's Experience

Managing Director, UK/Ireland

nugg.ad

Public Company; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

June 2011 – Present (2 years 3 months) United Kingdom

** Who or what is nugg.ad?

nugg.ad is born out of the market research industry. It provides digital media owners and sales houses solutions to increase their revenue.

** What solutions does nugg.ad provide?

nugg.ad's solutions are focused on enhancing the media owners offerings for brand advertisers. We provide the ability to create audiences, using demographic and product interest targeting. We also offer the unique ability to measure and optimise a brand advertising campaign in real time using real and relevant measures - brand awareness, brand affinity and purchase intent.

** How does nugg.ad's targeting differ from other solutions?

Beyond traditional channel and behavioural targeting, nugg.ad provides the ability to target real demographic attributes (age, gender, income, etc.), real product interests (health food, fashion, beauty, technology, finance, etc.), real intent (planning to buy a car, planning to buy a house). In addition, nugg.ad's solution allows you to target these audiences across every user, immediately, without the need to build up audience 'pots', and without the need to buy in 3rd party data.

** How do nugg.ad's brand engagement measurements work?

nugg.ad is able to ask your audience the most important brand questions in real time: do you know this brand? Do you like this brand? Are you going to buy this brand?

This real time functionality allows us to provide two powerful functions: firstly, we can measure the uplift in any of these key branding variables, and, by comparing to a control group, we can report precisely the uplift directly attributable to the measured portion of the campaign. Secondly, we can optimise the campaign to improve any of these measures, by adjusting the exposure frequency or adjusting the targeting criteria.

** How does nugg.ad deal with user privacy?

nugg.ad is the only targeting solution to carry the Europrise privacy seal. We collect and store no identifiable user data whatsoever.
