Bianca Frank's Summary

Hello,
My name is Bianca Frank. I own a small freelance design studio in beautiful Alaska. I have worked in my field since 2006.

My overall focus is to partner with marketing professionals businesses to create a modern brand identity and apply these elements to various advertising mediums. My methods are utilized through implementation of strategies in web design, graphic design, e-commerce and web app services.

I believe that I have several competitive advantages that create an ideal fit for your company:
1. You want creative ideas that stand out and stick with your audience — I provide a unique understanding of marketing logic informed by intuitive creative thinking. My perspective allows me to craft ideas that stick with your audience and make a lasting impact with your message.

2. You want design that communicates, not decorates — My goal is to create materials that
influence your audience and further your mission, not look pretty in an awards annual. I use my understanding of strategic messaging and communication to do it. Couple that with a strong design sense, and the result is more than a good-looking finished piece. It’s an effective one.

3. You need flawless execution that produces results — My experience in project management
ensures that I will sweat the details and produce a finalized product that exceeds expectations and instills delight in your audience.

In today’s world if you want to communicate you HAVE to have a designer that will draw out the larger picture, engage and ultimately direct the viewer to act. I am that designer and look forward to speaking with you. Thank you for your time.

Sincerely,
