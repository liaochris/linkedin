Lala Castro's Summary

Lala Castro is an entrepreneur and internet visionary with a wide array of expertise in Social Media, eCommerce, Social Influencer Marketing, Community Outreach, Event Management and many other facets of business development and online marketing. Lala is also extremely active in her community, working with and supporting a number of local organizations.

Currently employed at Orange County digital marketing firm, GigaSavvy, Lala leads an innovative and fast growing Social Media department, working with clients such as Knotts Berry Farm, a well known theme park and subsidiary of Cedar Fair Entertainment Group, Zyliss Swiss Innovation and Toshiba Latin America.

Lala is co founder of #LatinaGeeks & founder of eLaLa.com. #LatinaGeeks mission is to empower and inspire Latin women by spreading knowledge of entrepreneurship, social media and technology. eLaLa.com is a social community network that embraces online media and marketing and is the umbrella company for 5 highly trafficked social community websites.

Lala's community involvement spans from larger organizations like the American Cancer Society to smaller organizations like TEDxOrangeCoast and serves on the board for the National Latina Business Woman Association - Orange County. She also enjoys giving back to the community by supporting various causes including serving as newly appointed LISTA President of Los Angeles Chapter.

Lala aims to inspire anyone who has a passion & connect them with others that have similar interests. She firmly believes in supporting her passion and sharing it with others.

Specialties: Blogger Outreach, Influencer Outreach, Social Media Strategy, E-commerce, Purchasing, Branding, Business Development, Online Marketing, Outreach & Sponsorships, Event Management, SEM, SEO, & Online Media
