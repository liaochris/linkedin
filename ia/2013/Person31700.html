David Diosdado's Summary

I specialize in direction of digital projects and strategies, from conceptualization, technical possibilities, information architecture, UI/UX and programming to art direction and social media.

With more than 10 years of experience in the industry, I've worked in almost every field of digital advertising, such as conception of ideas, the best ways to communicate them, their artistic value and the aesthetics in a wide range of media, and also programming, frameworks and technologies that will bring them to life in digital environments.

My background as an industrial designer, graphics designer and traditional painting artist, apart from giving me an impressive set of tools and techniques (like 3D modeling, rendering and FEA, vector illustration, esoteric programming languages, etc.), also allow me to find solutions and ideas from an entirely different perspective

I've worked with companies like American Express, Cervecería Cuauhtémoc Moctezuma, SHCP, Grupo Bimbo, SC Johnson, General Motors (GM, Chevrolet, Pontiac, GMC, Cadillac, Hummer and Saab), Levi Strauss, Dockers, Air France, Microsoft, HP, Mitsubishi Motors, Pioneer Electronics, Grupo Empresarial Angeles (Banco Multiva, Hospital Angeles, Grupo Real Turismo, Hoteles Real Inn, Camino Real, Quinta Real, Grupo Imagen Multimedia).
