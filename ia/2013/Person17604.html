Brian Morgenweck's Summary

REALTOR® Bergen County NJ
I’m Brian Morgenweck, Broker/Owner of Power Realty Group based in Hackensack, NJ. I’ve been a resident of Bergen County my entire life & a Bergen County REALTOR® for 15 years. I opened Power Realty Group in 2006. Since then, I’ve created the Jersey Real Estate Scene portfolio of tech-savvy, maximum exposure real estate marketing programs.
I’m the guy who’s always advising people to “Do your homework” before hiring an agent or broker to represent you in handling the marketing & transaction of what is most likely your largest asset… or your largest purchase.
Sure, I really enjoy getting to know the folks I work with & represent. But, as much as I love that aspect, I take my business & my clients’ & customers’ goals very seriously. So, it’s about getting results…the best results for you, in the shortest time possible with no stress along the way.
