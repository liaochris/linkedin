Leah Moon's Summary

I am a firm believer in finding your passion and doing what you love. I have been able to combine my passion for social media and helping people through volunteering and giving back to the community. I have volunteered with many non-profit organizations and helped with their social media strategy. Currently, I am the Communications Chair for the Dayton Area Chapter of the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA).

In my newest position, I am an Assistant Product Marketing Manager at LexisNexis. I develop, manage and execute email marketing campaigns. I also help manage social media for the This is Real Law campaign as well as other social media activities on various products.

Most recently, I was the Social Media Specialist for Universal 1 Credit Union, providing leadership and direction for all social media initiatives. I also contributed to other marketing campaigns and helped execute special events, such as HomeWorld, the largest home improvement show in the Miami Valley.

Previously, I was a digital strategist at Spectrum, a health and science firm in Washington, DC where I worked on various accounts providing insight on digital initiatives.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Journalism from Ohio University's E.W. Scripps School of Journalism in March 2008. Go Bobcats!

Specialties: Social media savvy, event planning, fund-raising, Microsoft Office, Adobe InDesign CS5, Adobe Photoshop CS5, Adobe Pagemaker, Cision MediaSource Premium, MyMediaInfo, MEDIAtlas, Listrak, LexisNexis, Factiva, MAC and PC platforms
