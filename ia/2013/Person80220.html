Leon Thompson's Summary

Philosophy = Provide structure to chaos. Build trust by valuing people. Nurture long-term, strategic relationships that illuminate vision/mission and champion brand. Understand customers in order to execute communications that educate and create competitive advantage. Manage projects with high-energy, integrity, and flexibility, while keeping “big-picture” goals and customers/end-users in mind.

I am not a one trick pony. I have achieved success by blending a qualitative, people-centered, creative, idea generating approach with a task oriented, analytical, quantitative mindset. This unique approach makes me better suited to communicate with various social styles, as well as fostering an environment that encourages engagement and innovation.

I enjoy solving problems. I love to get inside the mind of the customer/end-user to discover what motivates purchasing behavior. This knowledge can then be used to strengthen brand and exploit a true competitive advantage in the marketplace.

I am passionate that in order to create a strong brand, customer relationships need to be managed before, during, AND after the sale. I helped a previous employer to create, grow, and manage an extensive customer testimonial and content database. These success stories added credibility with customers, created good PR, and positively affected bottom line. The results of these efforts helped to increase retail sell-thru, and our company became one of only two publishers in category awarded distribution at Wal-Mart and The Home Depot.

I also know how to successfully get the most from a limited budget. Over my career, I have shown good judgment, fiscal responsibility, and a high level of integrity. I am a detail-oriented, yet a proven big-picture thinker, experienced with creating simple solutions to solve complex challenges.
