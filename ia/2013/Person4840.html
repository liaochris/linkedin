Allen Evitts, PMP, MC, CC's Summary

Project Manager, Trainer

Project managers are a company’s leaders who execute of strategic vision. Project managers are innovative leaders and project are vehicle of change advancing companies towards their executive’s objectives.

What have I done?
• 20 years as a project manager; 30 years as an instructor.

• Spoke at several PMI chapters including in Raleigh, NC, Washington DC, Norfolk, VA Savannah, GA and Ocean State in Rhode Island.

• Taught highly successful workshops in Project Management for professionals in organizations including Lowes, Syngenta, Bureau of Public Debt and the USCG.

• Led a global telecom to one of the first successful development and deployment of a GPS system to their fleet of vehicles.

• Led the enterprise wide integration testing effort (involving 200+ separate applications) for a major wireless company. The new code had in a sub 1% defect rate and was viewed as the most completely documented program to date.

• Managed a commissioning group ($1 Billion plus commissions annually) through a billing conversion which radically changed the algorithm by which employees and contractors were being paid.

• Produced/Directed over 50 plays including a production of the Pulitzer Prize winning "Wit" at which the playwright gave a standing ovation.

• Performed "Music of the Night" (from Phantom of the Opera) for over 5000 revelers on Halloween at Underground Atlanta, silencing the crowd.

• Taught youth at risk to trust their better natures and to strive to be better people and better citizens.

Specialties: Project Management, Communications, Interpersonal Communications skills, Coaching, Mentoring, Persuasion, Teaching, Training, Leadership, Cross-Cultural Communications, Problem-solving, Negotiation Skills , Risk Analysis and Management, Presentation Skills, Telecommunications, Web Technologies, Issues Management, Communication Theory, Social Media Strategy.
