Heather Cooper's Summary

Drawing on over 8 years experience, I specialize in strategic social media campaigns for brands.

I have worked with both global and local organizations to drive successful social media campaigns and coach marketing teams, including McGraw-Hill Professional, GFI Software, Lavazza, Bacardi, Debenhams, Solaris and Re/Max.

I have worked on the marketing campaigns for international bestsellers, including "Happier," "The Presentation Secrets of Steve Jobs," "The Wisdom of Wooden," "Crack the Fat-Loss Code," "The Extraordinary Leader," "The Starbucks Experience," "Built to Serve," "How to Talk to Anyone," and "The No-Cry" Series.

A Facebook campaign that I managed for the book, "The Wisdom of Wooden," serves as a case study demonstrating best practices for Facebook marketing within McGraw-Hill.

Specialties: social media marketing, Facebook marketing, Twitter marketing, social media, book publicity, author marketing, author platform development, book marketing, guerilla marketing, word of mouth marketing, promotions, social media, community engagement, real-time marketing, Web 2.0 technologies, global collaboration, integrated campaigns

  • Lavazza A Modo Mio's Facebook Caffeine Buzz

    • October 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Heather Cooper

    P. Cutajar needed a plan for launching their Lavazza A Modo Mio coffee machine into the Maltese market. In addition, we were able to make direct correlations between our content posts and sales.

  • McGraw-Hill Business Facebook Page Helps Professionals "Learn More" and "Do More."

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Heather Cooper

    We recently launched the revamped Facebook Page for McGraw-Hill Business. We wanted the Page to be a continuation of McGraw-Hill Professional's overall look and feel, while being a place that welcomed fans to explore and interact with content from some of their latest and bestselling authors.

    The "Featured" App continuously changes to feature the latest titles and offer users the chance to sign up to win a free book. Thus, along with educating users on the products and providing value through free reading samples, the Company is benefitting by collecting new leads for e-mail outreach.

    We also manage the Page's custom content and have increased interaction by over 400%. To date we have increased the number of fans by over 800%. In addition, we have driven over 1,000 unique clicks through to retail pages for the featured products.

  • GFI Software's Social Media Turnaround

    • July 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Heather Cooper

    Under my strategic coaching, GFI has completely turned around our social media assets. When we began working together, there was no real strategy in place to guide the use of social media in the organization. After writing a custom strategy that spanned the various social media channels, I began coaching the social media team on new tools and techniques for applying the new strategy.

    In the past year we shifted the GFI Software Facebook community to reflect the demographics of our potential customers, as well as increased that hyper-targeted fan base by more than 110% in 2012.

    We've also done a lot to turn content around, from being flat and uninspired to being engaging and valuable. As a result, we've increased our interaction 152% over last year and now we see some of the highest rates of interaction among our competitors.

    The team has also become a key driver of traffic to the GFI websites and blogs as a results of our engaging content. This year we drove over 85,000 clicks to our online assets. That's an increase of over 320% from 2011.
