Our Real Estate Strategy

We focus our business on a few highly profitable areas of the country, which we believe will give our clients the best returns over the long run. We do not gamble on appreciation, but instead invest only in cash flow positive properties that generate income month to month whether or not your property goes up in value.

We leverage our network of independent realtors, mortgage lenders, property appraisers, home inspectors, title companies, rehab contractors, and property management companies for the benefit of our investors, so that the buying process is as easy as possible.

If you would like to speak with me about how we can help you find the right investments for your needs please feel free to email us at invest@owenscg.com and we will answer all of your questions.
