Brooke Jibb's Summary

I’ve dedicated 13+ years of my career to working for and growing small and medium sized businesses - running the gamut from retail to real estate to eCommerce (before eCommerce was even a “thing”) - which sparked my passion for entrepreneurship. Focusing on market research, business growth strategies, on-line campaigns and implementation has shaped my belief that it’s a valuable and important exercise to learn as many aspects of a business that you can, starting from the ground up.

A “digital native,” my passion for technology has transformed into a keen understanding of how the internet works, and how people use the internet. With a focus on digital strategy, website development, SEO, analytics, as well as social media planning, strategy and implementation, I’ve worked assisted small businesses to launch successful online marketing campaigns.
