Chris Tarpey's Summary

I'm the Creative Director at Coast Media Limited, a promotional video company based in Auckland, New Zealand. I have extensive digital marketing and broadcast operations experience with a knack for video editing.

With a Diploma in Film and Television Production, I worked in the TV industry for three years. During my time at Sky Television and Prime TV, I occupied a job in Media Operations. This involved live studio shows, vision mixing, international sports broadcasts, tape-to-tape editing, advertising and quality control.

Coast Media was established in 2008 with a focus on promotional video. Our videos are social, interactive and provide real analytics about the viewers. None of this is achievable in television.

With a creative team of professionals utilising broadcast-quality cameras, audio and editing software, every project is something special.
