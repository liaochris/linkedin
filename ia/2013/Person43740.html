Shawn Collins' Summary

Shawn Collins is an industry leader in developing innovative interactive and marketing solutions. For over 10 years Shawn has consulted with Fortune 500 companies to deliver his expert interactive strategies, including extensive influence in the automotive aftermarket and pharmaceutical marketing industries. Shawn’s role as an industry thought leader yields proven results that enable high-profile organizations to reach their digital communications objectives.
