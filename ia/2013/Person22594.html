Cheri Goll, RN, MSN, NE-BC's Publications

  • Converations With Myself on the Journey of Caring

    • Pocket Journal for Caregivers
    • March 2012
    Authors: Cheri Goll, RN, MSN, NE-BC

    Patient and family satisfaction with their healthcare experience is a top strategic priority for all healthcare organizations. Hospitals emerging as top tier performers are holding their workforce accountable for understanding and demonstrating caring behaviors with every patient and family, in every situation, every time. Caregivers who develop a strong sense of awareness about their own caring behaviors are more effective in their caring of others. This pocket journal encourages caregivers to think about important elements of their own caring practice. With encouraging statements and thought provoking questions the journal is designed to inspire caregivers to pause and reflect on their own journey of caring and to embrace the importance of the contribution they make to the healing journey of others. See more at www.cherigoll.com.
