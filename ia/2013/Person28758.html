Dale Bass' Summary

I'm an award-winning user interface design and usability professional with over 15 years of web/interaction design experience. I'm currently serving as an Interaction Designer at IBM, and I've worked with major companies such as TIAA-CREF, Wachovia, Wells Fargo, Ally Bank and others on complex Internet, intranet, online banking and prominent microsite projects. Many of these initiatives have received industry awards and recognitions.

I've formally served as a UX Lead at TIAA-CREF, a Fortune 100 financial services company and a leading provider of retirement benefits nationwide; Most of my time there was spent working on their new Online Banking platform.

I hold a Master of Science degree in Human-Computer Interaction from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, the nation's oldest technological university.

Specialties: User Experience, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Web Usability, Mobile, Photoshop CS6, Dreamweaver CS6, Illustrator CS6, HTML5, CSS3, Axure RP Pro, iRise, Cognos.

Production of:

• Wireframes
• Site maps
• Lo-fi and hi-fi visual comps
• Rapid interactive prototypes suitable for usability testing
• Competitive analyses
• Heuristic evaluations
• Personas
• In-person or remote usability studies
• Corporate user interface and design standards/guidelines
