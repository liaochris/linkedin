Chris Chan's Summary

I am a practical and pragmatic Agile Practitioner and Coach working with organisations as they adopt Agile and Lean methods. I have extensive experience as a coach, consultant, team lead and software professional with more than 15 years of IT experience.

My passion is working side-by-side with teams to help them become more effective and deliver value to the business. I have a particular interest in coaching organisations and teams to bring about positive change to build learning organisations. My ethos is to be pragmatic, listen and apply incremental learning.

With a background working for large corporations, I am proficient coaching at different levels within organisations to help them understand and address their important challenges. I specialise in helping executives, managers, teams and individuals to apply lean thinking and to adopt and improve agile methods, principles and practices. I have the ability to facilitate organisational change through addressing technology, process, behavioural and transformational aspects of agile adoption and I am a practitioner of the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe).

I am active in the agile community and an organiser of local agile meetup groups.

Specialties: Agile, Agile Project Management, Agile Coaching, Consulting, Team Coaching, Change Management, Scrum, Extreme Programming, Kanban, Rally, Lean Software Development, Application Development, Scaled Agile Framework, Cynefin, Design and Architecture, building great teams.
