Global Passages LLC is an international trade consulting company. Our vision is to promote international understanding and cooperation through developing successful global business partnerships for small to medium sized business owners. Global Passage's mission is to assure that your business attains maximum profitability with the least amount of risk while initiating and/or expanding your import/export program

Global Passages provides the following customized services:
• Global product sourcing/product development
• New market research and strategic planning for entering overseas markets
• Establishing pricing methodology for products imported and exported
• Initiating best practices for establishing a global supply chain
• Implementing logistical solutions for ocean and air freight movement
• Consulting on product modifications to take advantage of reduced duty rates
• Advising on how to take advantage of Foreign Trade Zone programs(FTZ) to reduce costs
• Streamlining and simplifying internal company procedures for explosive international growth
• Finding and implementing technological solutions to grow business globally
• Building and maintaining US Customs Compliance programs
• Writing procedural manuals to meet the needs of import/export regulatory compliance programs
• Establishing 3rd party lab testing programs for consumer product manufacturers to address regulatory and quality assurance needs
• Training programs for import/export compliance
• Developing and Implementing Customs-Trade Partnership against Terrorism(C-TPAT) programs for importers
