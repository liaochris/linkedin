Ebonie Allard's Summary

"Entrepreneurship may be defined as the pursuit of opportunity without regard to resources currently controlled" (Stevenson,1983)

My background is as a Props Buyer and Stylist for TV and Film, as well as an Office Manager, Project Manager and Freelance PA for a range of different employers and HNW private individuals.

In July 2009 I set up My Girl Friday Ltd to provide a Creative Freelance, short term or part time comprehensive PA and VA service to both Individuals and Businesses.

The Company offered traditional assistant services, virtual assistance, project management, social media, marketing, PR, and business strategy to a number of businesses and individuals. My role included delivering these services to my own clients, as well as running the Company, managing a pool of freelancers, and dealing with the confidential and often delicate matters of individuals lives.

In January 2012 I decided to close My Girl Friday Ltd and instead concentrate on my own Freelance and Project work. I took time out to write my first novel and continue to work with private clients, and businesses on events, or projects by request.

As of January 2013 I have availability for ad hoc and project work, I am particularly interested in working with Entrepreneurs at all levels, enabling & assisting them to achieve to their greatest potential.

Please email me with any questions or enquiries.
