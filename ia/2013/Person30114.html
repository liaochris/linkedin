Dan Hughes' Experience

President

Field Inspection Services

January 2004 – Present (9 years 11 months) Knoxville, Tennessee Area

Field Inspection Services is a family owned company located in Knoxville, Tennessee. FIS was founded in January of 2004 with the primary focus of doing field service work on a contract basis. During the past 8 years we have visited over 1000 bank and retail locations completing commercial site signage surveys, compliance audits and retail merchandising from New England to southern Florida and west to California. We have also completed over 1000 ATM software upgrades, window sign installations and cleanings in the same area.

FIS currently performs mortgage inspections, insurance loss inspections, insurance risk inspections, collateral inspections and merchant site inspections for several large companies in the state of Tennessee. As the owner of Field Inspection Services I have over 30 years of experience in sales, marketing and management with other companies prior to starting FIS.
