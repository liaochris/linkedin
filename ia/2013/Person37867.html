Dr. Frank Kaden, D.C.'s Summary

Frank E. Kaden, D.C. is a Hermosa Beach Chiropractor who enjoys treating patients from all walks of life. He provides gentle chiropractic services including Diversified Technique, Brimhall Six Steps to Wellness, Pain Neutralization Technique, Cold Laser, Percussor and Adjustor to improve the lives of the South Bay residents of Hermosa Beach, Manhattan Beach, Redondo Beach, El Segundo, Torrance and Palos Verdes. Whether you are looking for relief of back pain, neck pain, sciatica, fibromyalgia, headaches and migraines, or overall natural health and wellness without the use of drugs or surgery, we provide the finest chiropractic care available in a family friendly setting. We also offer weight loss programs and homeopathic bio-identical hormone replacement. For more information visit us at www.kadenchiropractic.com Come in today!

Specialties: Applied Kinesiology, Pain Neutralization Technique, Instrument Adjusting, Diversified Technique, Cold Laser Therapy, Anti-Aging Therapy, Weight Loss, Personal Injury, Insurance
