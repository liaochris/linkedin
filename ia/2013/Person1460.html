Adam J. Michel's Summary

As a Creative Director, I guide talented designers, developers and producers through sometimes turbulent landscapes to produce award winning work.

Over the past 16 years, I’ve collaborated with exceptional designers, developers, producers and even a few puppets. Working with top tier companies, I’ve created varied content including websites, games, applications, videos, animations and ePublishing for multiple platforms such as mobile/tablets, interactive TV and the web.

I enjoy fostering talent and realized early on that the best work comes from a motivated and passionate team. A sense of ownership through collaboration is the ideal, as is including the client in the creative process.

A typical project under my creative direction involves varying degrees of research, strategy, ideation/concept development, UX/UI/IA brainstorming and the resulting wireframes, storyboards, functional specs, and of course, design and art direction. User testing, talent direction and prototyping often factor in. My experience also encompasses client facing communication such as proposals, feedback and presentations.

I gravitate towards emerging technologies yet stay mindful of the client, brand integrity, and target audience. Great work is just not quite as great without that client gush; it means almost nothing if it doesn’t resonate with the user.
