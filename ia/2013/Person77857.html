Kyle Vena's Summary

Brief philosophy

The top schools can only teach the technology and trends of that time it can maintain someone’s design ability for a season. As soon as the tech industry changes, it’s the person’s character that has to fall in sync.

Is the competition of the design world going to cause a person to use a company of mentor to step above, or is the character of a person great enough to see design process as an entity; a conglomeration of ideas, & people to help shape them into existence to create not just another advertisement, another marketing genius... But an evolution of people together bringing forth an experience, process that bring those at the other end of it to an enlightened moment. A belief that there is quality & value not just in the product, but the expression in creation of design.
