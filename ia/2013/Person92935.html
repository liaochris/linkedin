Michael Robinson, Ph.D., Master Club Manager's Summary

Michael Robinson, Ph.D., MCM
Robinson Club Consultants
860-653-7104
Michael@RobinsonCC.com
www.RobinsonCC.com

25 years in the management of private membership clubs. Additional experience in executive conference centers, restaurants, country inns and resorts.

Specialties: Executive Recruiting, Organizational Consulting, Governance, Executive Compensation, Member and Guest Services, Interim Management, Management Development, Executive Coaching, Hospitality Staff Training, International Wine Workshops, Chapter Education Programs.

Early in his hospitality career, Michael worked in resorts, executive conference centers, country inns and restaurants. In 1980, Michael began his club management career as General Manager of Tumble Brook Country Club in Bloomfield, CT. He subsequently managed the Locust Club of Philadelphia, Portage Country Club in Akron, OH and The Victoria Club in Riverside, CA. Michael completed his 25-year span of club management experience in 2005 as General Manager of Meadow Club, Fairfax, CA.

Michael has made a practice of sharing his knowledge and giving back to his professional association by conducting educational programs and by mentoring aspiring club managers. Within the Club Managers Association of America (CMAA), Michael has served as Connecticut Chapter President, on numerous national and state committees and earned the coveted Master Club Manager (MCM) designation. There are fewer than 20 Master Club Managers worldwide.

Michael currently serves as President of Robinson Club Consultants, organizational consultants and executive recruiters for private clubs. Robinson Club Consultants has placed over 200 club managers, golf course superintendents and golf industry professionals nationwide. Additionally, Robinson Club Consultants advises clubs on governance issues and provides interim management services.

Our passion is the management of Private Membership Clubs. Please call for a complimentary confidential phone consultation.
