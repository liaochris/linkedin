David Kanter's Summary

As an industry-leading list and direct marketing professional for over 29 years, I have provided e-mail, direct mail, insert media, co-registration, and online marketing services to 1,000's of domestic and international companies, political mailers, and non-profit organizations.

My target markets include: Active Travelers, Automotive Enthusiasts, Catalogs (B2B & B2C), Continuing Education, Fundraising, Insurance, Political Campaigns & Causes, Speculative Investors, and Trade Shows and Conferences.

I am a DMA and SRDS recognized list and insert media broker and manager. The DMA refers to me and a few others as "Data Innovators" or "List Leaders."

My articles and blog posts appear daily under News & Views at www.acculistusa.com and at www.beyondvoterlists.com, and have been published in MultiChannel Merchant and Direct Listline along with other media.

My success stories in customer and donor acquisition include e-mail, direct mail, insert media, co-registration, online display advertising, and multi-channel marketing campaigns.

Numerous clients retain me as their primary list broker or direct marketing consultant. I can also provide expert advice on merge-purge, list hygiene, copywriting, radio, response analysis, predictive modeling, and print & mail production.

As a guest lecturer, I have taught at Loyola University and given numerous presentations to various marketing and other professional organizations.

My clients know how passionate I am about their success, and how I can combine experience, competitive analysis, and cost-effective strategies to help them achieve their objectives.
