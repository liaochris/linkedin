Francine Pickering's Summary

Francine Pickering is a marketing consultant with extensive marketing experience in bluechip and small businesses as well as the HE sector.

Since founding Clarity Marketing, a Nottingham-based marketing consultancy, in 2003, she has been advising small and growing businesses on marketing strategy,planning and implementation, primarily in the B2B service sector.

She has worked closely with Ingenuity, the University of Nottingham's local business network - www.ingenuitygateway.com.

Francine has experience as a business mentor and coach, having worked on business support projects including Mentoring for All, and Women for Women, as well as mentoring entrepreneurship students in creative problem solving and new venture creation at the University of Nottingham.

If you are a business owner who could benefit from some professional marketing input, contact Francine for a no-obligation conversation. Or find out more about how Clarity Marketing could help you at www.clarity-in-communication.com

Specialties: Marketing strategy, brand strategy, marketing planning, promotional planning, social media strategy, marketing mentoring and training, business coaching and mentoring.
