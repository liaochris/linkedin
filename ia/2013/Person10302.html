Ashley Guberman's Summary

I am a catalytic force for positive change. With over 20 years in software and QA management, I’m committed to building healthy, high-performing teams whose focus on quality is deeply connected to their own personal passions. I believe the key to doing that is to connect what people care about to why they do the work that they do.

What people care about drives their conversation. Conversations drive commitment. Commitment drives action, and action is the only thing capable of driving results. Put that action in the middle of functioning organizational practices and then people can lead healthier lives. I help build those practices, allowing people to make bigger promises and deliver results of value to customers.

By way of background, a sample of my achievements includes:

* Managing a team of 17 people, including implementing a performance review process that focused on increasing their technical skills.
* Developing top talent to foster values that create and support high-performing teams.
* Consistently focusing on quantifiable results (adoption, performance, and cost-savings) for change efforts.
* Master’s degree in Behavioral Science, focusing on communication skills within organizations. Thesis on communicating feedback effectively.
* Partnering with HR to create leadership development training for executives and mid-level managers.
* Certified mediator through the King County Dispute Resolution Center.
