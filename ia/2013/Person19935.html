Carla Van Walsum Ph.D's Summary

Carla Van Walsum PhDc, has created a thriving holistic psychotherapy and personal development practice in South Florida, helping hundreds of clients make quantum progress through her unique combination of psychological, spiritual and metaphysical approaches. Her extensive study of European, American and Eastern philosophies provide the foundation for her work.

Her clients appreciate that Carla is able to get to the heart of the matter quickly. Using clinically validated techniques and powerful alternative approaches, she empowers her clients to focus on what can be done to make positive life shifts rather than having them focus on what’s not working in their lives. Carla is an expert at identifying and assisting people in releasing emotional blocks and traumas in a safe, loving environment without judgment. Many are able to heal acute and life-long patterns of emotional pain, struggling relationships, family burdens and stress.

Carla uses her compassion, intuition and vast experience to provide her clients with the tools they need in order to thrive in life, work and relationships.

Carla will help you understand and resolve your most difficult problems in an entirely new way. She uses Systemic Constellations (no, not about stars) : a deeply profound, immensely validated and moving method that reveals negative and self-destructive impulses that are embedded in the unconscious brain, expands understanding, heals and restores peace of mind.

Clinical psychology research shows that traumas are stored in the limbic brain for at least three generations and they distort our behavior, health or life and that of the future generations.
Carla's work is helping you to find the cause,- to release the effects ,-and to restore balance and harmony.
