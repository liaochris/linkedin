Founder

Various Startups

2003 – Present (10 years)

I have been a founder and co-founder in multiple startups since 2003. I cofounded an exterior house painting business in 2003. From there, my partner and I moved into the web startup world with BidOverMe.com, a site to connect those in need of home improvement with the contractors in their area. I developed a business plan, hired a web developer, managed the development of the website, and implemented a marketing strategy. I managed all business activities and development on a daily basis. From there I branched out on my own to several different markets and fields with the launch of three web 2.0 sites. I took over the web development and did everything start to finish for each site, including concept design, code development, graphics/interface design, marketing, advertising, and day-to-day management. With one of the startups, I hired marketing and sales personnel to help it grow.

My startups have ranged from group communication tools, to social recommendation sites, to improving ways to find and get in contact with local businesses. During the 2010 elections, my startup partnered with KMSP Fox 9 news to power online political debates between Minnesotan candidates.

Worked in the MR&D Recording Head Reliability group. Designed modeling tools for air bearing designers. Developed and subsequently maintained a suite of modeling tools such as a hard particle scratch (HPS) simulation, a hard particle collection / deflection model, a 3D visualization post-processor for HPS, a motion video post-processor for the deflection model, a complete live database of all HPS results (hpsLive), an OPD image processing tool, and an advanced Stiction model for lube-head interaction.
