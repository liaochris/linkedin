Herman Zandt's Summary

Entrepreneur in spirit and manager by profession, Herman Zandt (The Netherlands/1971) has focused most of his business life on international business ventures. Through cross-border co-operations with small to large organisations, various ambitious projects have been successfully completed within the challenging areas of the regulated financial and aviation industries and e-commerce and new media.

In 1995, Herman started his career by setting up his consulting company that has been the key driver behind numerous international projects ever since.

Thanks to his strong belief in quality without compromise and his knack for turning challenges into accomplishments, Herman has moved seamlessly into setting up financial institutions in Malta and navigating his way through aviation management – two sectors that are heavily regulated and therefore require a strong focus on detail and compliance.

Holding the triple-accredited Henley MBA degree for Executive Management and being well-versed in corporate legal matters, Herman has attracted various foreign investors to set up their operations in Malta. Herman continues to hold various non-executive directorships with enterprises domiciled in various jurisdictions. These positions give him scope to use his honed skills to full extent, especially for providing strategic direction, setting up company operations from scratch and guiding organisations through turbulent times of fundamental change (mergers/acquisitions/turnarounds). As Accredited Mediator (CEDR), Herman acts as neutral third facilitating parties settling their business disputes outside Court ( www.brevis.eu ).

Outside his professional life, Herman is active within Rotary International as Past President (RY2012-13) of Rotary Club La Valette Malta (District 2110) and Honorary Member of Rotary Club San Cataldo. As passionate aviator with Private Pilot License (JAR-FCL PPL(A)/NR), Herman takes time off to discover new destinations in South Europe and North Africa.
