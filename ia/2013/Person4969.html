Allison Madison's Summary

Allison has led strategic initiatives specializing in maximizing profitability, organizational analysis and supply chain solutions for Fortune 500 companies and major manufacturers spanning more than 20 years. Her work in retail, manufacturing, automotive, consumer electronics and other food and dry goods industries focuses on process improvement, financial performance, measurement, assessment and profit enhancement through Kaizen, lean, six sigma, and business process reengineering methodologies. Allison is a trained facilitator and implementer of organization strategy, cash flow analysis, and operational issues in both manufacturing and service industries. She is available for speaking engagements.

She has extensive experience in analyzing operational processes in accounting, sales and operations, in addition to successfully leading merger and acquisition teams. Her work with analyzing and segmenting customer groups, their requirements and implementing solutions led to increased revenue and margin, improved supply chain and improved customer satisfaction. She has worked with CEOs, CFOs, Presidents, VPs and Managers to understand the issues they face in delivering effectively to their organizations. As an experienced professional, Allison is particularly skilled in helping management teams improve their day-to-day processes in operations, manufacturing, finance, logistics and various lines of business.

Specialties

Lean methodologies, key performance metrics, supply chain optimization, cash flow improvement, sales process improvement, design of new processes, transitions due to mergers and acquisitions, and customer retention. Speaking engagements.
