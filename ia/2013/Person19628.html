Candice .'s Summary

Possessing a stake-holder mindset, Candice has a successful career spanning 20+ years with an extraordinary hands-on commitment to driving revenue growth and bottom line results for franchise organizations, non-profits and businesses in the: commercial real estate/construction, healthcare, private sector, printing, chemical, insurance, manufacturing and publishing industries.

Her roles as Executive Director, Vice-President of Marketing, Business Development Strategist and hands-on Creative Director on both the client and agency side affords her a rare and unique level of insight, experience and business acumen.

Candice's areas of expertise include:

- next level, turnaround consulting to improve efficiencies of overall: operations, marketing, sales, client retention, new business development, and cost savings analysis for non-profit organizations and small to medium size businesses

- complete lifestyle and customer experience marketing for brands in the commercial real estate, home building, luxury and healthcare industries

- developing infrastructures for improved communication and collateral management for multi-location companies and franchise organizations.

She maintains a proven track record of improving the bottom lines of her clients through her assessment and identification of: potential growth opportunities in relevant business channels, new approaches to product application in horizontal and vertical markets, and by developing consistent compelling creative marketing applications across all media platforms to deliver optimum results.
