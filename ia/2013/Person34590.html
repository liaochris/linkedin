Derrick Van Mell, MBA, MA's Experience

Principal

Van Mell Associates

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

1990 – Present (23 years)

We provide two services: Question-Based Business Planning and Board-Level Project Planning. We help executives and directors meet their fiduciary responsibilities of long-range planning, fiscal management, risk control and continuity, operational review and community goodwill. We are independent and objective.

Question-Based Planning is a plain-English alternative to traditional "strategic" planning. In five 2-hour meetings we produce a 1-page plan in the form of a goal tree; this is followed with monthly progress review meetings. We provide clarity, focus, discipline and a plan leaders are proud to share.

Board-Level Project Planning integrates the summary information needed to make defensible decisions about major capital projects: project objectives tied to strategy, financial and location analysis, concept design, a cost/benefit summary and internal and external communication. We perform feasibility and lease/own studies.

I've always been fascinated by how ideas transform into helpful products, processes and projects. People love to work successfully with others in a noble cause, and I feel it's my job to provide the logic, ideas and encouragement they need.

I mistrust the jargon I learned as an MBA and believe asking good questions in plain English is the best way to help managers and directors discover their important common goals.

Question-Based Business Planning is our contribution to bringing effectiveness and respectability back to planning, a key ingredient of leadership. Planning should never be an empty ritual; it should produce results to be proud of.

People might know of us from our work on major capital projects. My hero is Winston Churchill, who once famously said, "We shape our buildings; thereafter, they shape us." His courage, compassion and limitless curiosity have been an unfailing inspiration.

Specialties

We work in several industries: healthcare and health research as well as manufacturing, finance and services. The diversity of experience keeps us energized and on our toes. It's exciting and valuable to take an idea from one industry and impress people in another!

    Jaeckle Distributors is a successful, family-owned, mid-market distributor of flooring products with facilities throughout the Midwest. In 2001, Van Mell Associates helped President Jeff Jaeckle plan a large new distribution facility. In 2010, Jeff asked VMA to create a Question-Based Business plan. The VMA team used its Question-Based Planning approach to provide Jeff and his managers a plan which has produced measurable results in each management area: market share and sales growth, operational efficiency, HR engagement, focus for their business intelligence investments—and, most important, substantial margin gains. A key aspect of VMA’s approach is that it provides its clients the best and most effective (and cost-effective) use of each associate’s deep experience. The one-page “goal tree” (see Articles at vanmell.com) provides the kind of focus and clarity that leaders must provide. One unusual result: Jeff knew he had to educate his entire staff about key goals, so he created a series of humorous videos, one of which can be seen at the Project URL.
