Annette McEvoy's Summary

Annette is a dynamic entrepreneur, marketing and strategy consultant and board director with a global, profitable business creation track record in consumer and beauty products. She has deep experience in development and special skill in translating where the customer is/going to innovative marketing, assortments, and store concepts. Clients include retailers, food marketers, product development companies and trend and strategic consultancies most notably Anthropologie, Urban Outfitters, InterParfums Inc., The Dannon Co., the Zandl Group and Fletcher-Knight innovation and trend consultancies.

Annette is known for being on the founding team for Limited Brand's Bath + Body Works, building the international Clairol business from the ground up, and most recently putting The Gap back on the map in personal care. She also, as Principal of Envision Retailing is known for building beauty businesses for specialty retailers and her innovative research for leading consultancies on topics ranging from soft drinks, health & wellness, convenience stores to university re-positioning. Most recently, Annette cofounded Epic Beauty Co., a private label turnkey manufacturing beauty business.

Specialties: Beauty supply, Business development and strategy, all facets of vertically integrated personal care retail operations, marketing, negotiations.
