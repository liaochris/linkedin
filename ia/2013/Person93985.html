Mike Bloch's Summary

Extraordinary customer service is a top priority to me. I work aggressively and relentlessly behind the scenes to negotiate favorable terms for my clients and solve any challenges that come up during the process of buying or selling a property. By breaking down the process into simple and clear steps, my goal is for my clients to have a smooth and pleasant experience.

My first career was as a local elementary school teacher. I take that same passion for helping others into my real estate career. I pride myself on my communication and listening skills. My strength is assisting others in clarifying and accomplishing their goals. Michael is bilingual and is proud to offer his services to Spanish speaking clients.

My goal in working with clients is to develop long-term relationships and earn their referrals and repeat business by being their trusted Real Estate advisor. I have built my business one client at a time through a combination of hard work, honesty, and focus on ethics and professionalism.

Visit me at www.MikeBloch.com for more information.
