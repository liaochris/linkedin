Basil Beighey's Summary

Since graduating from Penn State in 1981, my career has been divided equally between entrepreneurial endeavors and corporate marketing positions.

My corporate experiences include a ten year stretch with Royal Philips Electronics, Europe's largest electronics firm and a four year term with eSecuritel, a telecom services company specializing in wireless handset insurance and subsidiary of Brightstar.

At Philips I worked in Marketing Communications, media buying, collateral design, collateral production, and event planning both in the U.S. and Europe. I also ...

- Formulated and enforced our identity
- Managed all ad campaigns
- Managed creative aspects of trade show exhibits and special events
- Prepared many multimedia sales presentations
- Produced all video production
- Managed relationships with our PR firm and outside creative vendors
- Served as liaison to our international marketing organization
- Managed a $2 million marketing budget

At eSecuritel I was responsible for the design and production of all marketing collateral. I also ...

- Designed and produced all printed collateral
- Designed and produced all point of sale materials
- Created many sales and marketing presentations
- Designed trade show exhibits
- Designed and produced many digital assets
- Produces all videos

Early in my career, my entrepreneurial endeavors included owning two restaurants for a period of 12 years. During that time I managed as many as 25 employees and achieved per store yearly sales of over $750,000. I sold both restaurants for a profit. Owning businesses teaches efficiency and management skills – project management, people management and vender relationship management. Profit is a residual and you don't get paid for mistakes; so you learn to work lean, fast, and accurately.

Currently my goal is to drive a marketing or creative team as a Creative Director, Art Director, Marketing Manager/Director or Marketing Communications Director.
