Andrew Pucci's Summary

I spend my daytime hours as a user experience designer at Telerik. I work with clients to help them understand how people are using their products and develop designs to increase usability.

I am a recent graduate of the Information Architecture and Knowledge Management Master's program at Kent State University. I graduated with a concentration in User Experience Design.

In my spare time, I help attract and retain young professionals to the Akron area as a board member of the Young Professionals of Akron, Downtown Akron Partnership Junior Leaders and Greater Akron Chamber.

Local professional community is also important to me. I recently was accepted on to the NEOUPA board as the webmaster. I was also recently elected to Chair for NORASIST.
