Enzie Shahmiri's Summary

I am a classically trained artist, who specializes in figurative paintings with a focus on portraiture of people and pets. My non-commissioned paintings and themed series are on permanent exhibit at the Artist Eye Gallery in Laguna Beach, CA. My work is collected worldwide and can be found in private collections in the USA, Europe and the Middle East.

I work mainly from photographs and paint realistic portraits of people and pets in oil on canvas, using only professional grade supplies.

There has to be a difference between a copy of a photograph and a painting that get's past down from one generation to the next. Therefore, I love to place my subjects in thought out and highly detailed background settings to convey a narrative specific to the sitter's personality.

My goal is to convey a message with my work and to bring joy to those families who have trusted me with creating a portrait of their loved ones.

Over the years reference photos I have taken for my work have also captured the interest of publishers and Getty images. My photos have been printed in books.

Specialties

Child and Family Portraiture, Pet Portraits, Posthumous Portraits, Figurative Paintings, Classical Realism
