Coral Gundlach's Summary

I am a CRS level top producing real estate consultant specializing in residential real estate. My geographic focus is Arlington, Falls Church, Alexandria, McLean, Vienna, Fairfax and Loudoun county Virginia. My focus is on helping clients define their goals so we can achieve them together. I will also look at the big picture of your situation and help you decide whether buying, selling, renting or refinancing make the most sense to reach your goals.

When marketing a home for sale or rent, I will do everything possible to get you the best price and terms with extensive marketing, expert staging and strategic pricing.

McEnearney Associates, my brokerage, constantly analyzes market data and knows the trends and how to use them to benefit our clients.

I also work extensively with all kinds of buyers -- from first time to repeat, I will navigate you through the often confusing process of buying a home: from helping you find the right neighborhood to negotiating price, inspections, advising you through the mortgage process, making buying a home the exciting and enjoyable experience it should be.

Specialties

Negotiation, Marketing, Buyer Representation, Seller Representation, Customer Service
