Bruno Pedro's Summary

Bruno Pedro is a Web and Cloud Computing Technologist with over ten years’ experience working on the industry.

Bruno has been developing Open Source and commercial applications since 1995. He's an O'Reilly author and a regular speaker at industry related events.

On his spare time he enjoys exploring the Internet of Things with the Arduino platform.
