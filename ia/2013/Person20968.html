Catherine LaPointe's Summary

I am a Syracuse University alum with a BFA in Illustration. Since entering the workforce I have taken turns working full-time, part-time, and freelancing, and am currently freelancing while looking to expand my expertise in a full-time setting.

I manage a freelance career as a book cover designer and illustrator, often working with small publishing houses and self-publishers. I also take on clients, both locally and nationally, for print and branding design contracts. In the downtime between all these activities I run an online shop selling fun hand-painted footwear and other items.

Specialties: illustration, design, layouts, Adobe, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, storyboards, books, publishing, book covers
