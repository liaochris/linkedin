Dana Hollish Hill's Summary

As an exclusive buyer's agent and licensed real estate broker in Virginia, Maryland and the District of Columbia, I serve as Vice President and Associate Broker for Buyer's Edge Company, Inc. where I have been working since 2001.

Specialties

Specializing in helping clients who are looking to purchase a home in the DC metropolitan area including DC, Maryland and Virginia. I also work with real estate investors, move-up buyers, parents purchasing for graduate students and domestic and international relocations.
