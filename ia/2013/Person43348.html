Eva-Lotta Lamm's Summary

I am a User Experience Designer with a passion for structuring and visualising information. My strength lies in creating simple, elegant and engaging designs, that present complex information in a clear and understandable way and support users in getting things done in a straightforward and easy way.

Having worked inhouse as well as within an agency’s design team and as a freelancer, I have experience working on the complete design cycle from analysis and research via concept development, specification, prototyping and visual design through to implementation.

Specialties

User Experience Design & Information Architecture (Analysis, scenarios, sitemaps, wireframes, prototypes)
Interface design (High fidelity mock-ups and templates)
Information Design (Typography, grid design, creating visual hierarchy)
Visualisation and Mapping
Standards based, semantic (X)HTML and CSS, basic knowledge of Javascript

Software: Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Flash, Visio, Omnigraffle, Office. Basic knowledge of After Effects and sound editing software
