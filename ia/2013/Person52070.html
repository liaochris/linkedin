Henry Blas' Summary

As a Visual Branding Coach, Henry Blas helps small businesses present themselves in a clear, concise, and consistent way to their clients and prospective clients all the time, every time. Small businesses cannot afford to lose clients because their message is unclear, different from one touch point to another, or they just didn’t stand above their competition. Henry makes sure this won’t happen. Henry specializes in offering graphic design solutions to small, micro, and new businesses in the form of integrated identity systems; print and digital design services. Henry prides himself in his approach that “Good design is good business” (Thomas Watson, co-founder of IBM) and doesn’t hesitate to educate small business owners how graphic design can help their business grow. Good graphic design is an investment and an essential part of a businesses success.
