Benjamin McElroy's Summary

McElroy Films LLC offers a comprehensive lineup of professional, AWARD-WINNING video production services for corporations, universities, schools and non-profits in Boston, Massachusetts and the New England area. We work with clients to create distinctive, visually stunning videos that have a track record of proven-results. Whether we are creating a web marketing video, internal communication, product how-to videos, or campaign TV and radio ads, we are dedicated to create stories that will inform, inspire and impact your audience.

We excel at providing HD video production services at a fraction of the cost of traditional video production companies, without sacrificing quality. McElroy Films prides itself on its client-driven results and boutique-like service by dedicating each project the attention it deserves. Every project is customizable from start to finish and budgeted according to your needs. We translate the newest innovative video technology into understandable terms and can guide you towards possibilities you may not know exist.

We look forward to partnering with you to create videos that will not only promote your brand, awareness, cause and mission, but will prompt your audience, funders and community to action.

Specialties: HD Filmmaking, Corporate, Educational, Non-profit, Training and Instructional videos.

Additional services include: Independent Filmmaking, Wedding Cinematography, VHS to DVD transfers and Online video hosting, and Music Video Production.
