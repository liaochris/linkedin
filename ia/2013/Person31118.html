Dave Hill - The Re-Engineered Engineer's Summary

DAVE HILL IS THE RE-ENGINEERED ENGINEER
We deliver top-shelf content on communication strategies for data-driven, smart people in memorable and funny ways.

One of the toughest problems with employing and investing in smart people is that it can be difficult to understand the important information they are conveying.

We help you re-engineer your engineers to:
• Communicate, influence and engage others, even those without a technical degree.
• Master the art of stating clearly a situation's impact on operations, people and the bottom line.
• Become exceptional leaders who know how to develop their people and create thriving workplaces.
• Maximize the outcome of any presentation from a conference call, executive meeting, training or speech.
