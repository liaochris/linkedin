Bruce Wilson's Summary

I am a science and medical writer with a keen interest in evidence-based medicine, clinical trial design, biomedical ethics, mental health, social psychology, and social ecology.

Since 1995, I have worked as a CME writer, conference reporter, health columnist, and journalist for medical trade magazines. I have a BSc in biochemistry and continue learning as an autodidact.

My current interests are in editing manuscripts, books, and book chapters, and writing special reports, white papers, and health or environmental journalism. I can travel anywhere on short notice.

In medical areas, my strengths are in oncology, hematology, cardiology, kidney disease, neurology, affective neuroscience, psychology and psychiatry. I am currently branching in to environmental writing, conservation, and "writing that matters."

Specialties

Editing, special reports, white papers, and journalism.
