Kamran Agayev A. (OCM)'s Summary

I’m an Oracle ACE Director and Oracle Certified Master DBA with 7 years of experience managing UNIX- based Oracle Databases.

As a working IT professional, I have experience in a wide range of mission critical technologies, specializing in database administration best practices for backup and recovery.
