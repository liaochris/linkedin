Jason Woodward's Summary

Born and raised in Jackson Hole, Wyoming, my friends call me "Woody". I graduated from the US Naval Academy (1999, B.S. Ocean Engineering) and earned an Executive MBA from the University of Wyoming (2009).

Served 9 years on active duty as a U.S. Naval Officer and helicopter pilot; South America counter drug operations, Arabian Gulf in support of the Iraq War, and Search and Rescue in Washington State. Currently serving as a Naval reservist with the United States Naval Academy Information Program as the Area Coordinator for Washington State.

In the civilian sector I have focused on small business development and sales opportunities. Initially in the real estate industry for 8 years and now the medical device industry.

My wife Amy and I have two lovely daughters, Lola and Reese...and a couple of Jack Russell terriers.

Specialties: Sales, Business Development, System/ Process Development, Team Leadership
