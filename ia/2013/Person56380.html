Jessica Boucher's Summary

I love technology, and I love bringing it to the people.

Having graduated from Babson College, continually listed as one of the best business schools by BusinessWeek and the Financial Times, I grew my career roots at intelitek, one of the leaders in e-learning and blended technology learning solutions. I served in the Technical Support department and I was soon promoted to Project Manager, where I managed multiple e-learning and assessment projects.

I now serve as a Product Manager for Jobscience, the leader in cloud-based talent management software. Our products, which handle recruiting, sourcing, training and managing employees, run on the Salesforce platform and on mobile devices. I am involved day to day with spec writing, managing requirements with our programmers, and completing projects on time and to high satisfaction. I am currently working on mobile solutions and Heroku implementations.

In my spare time I serve as the Chief Volunteer Coordinator for the FIRST Robotics Competition, where I recruit and train hundreds of volunteers annually. I have been honored to manage the Einstein field, where the World Championship rounds are viewed by thousands of fans, more than any other Volunteer Coordinator. I am the youngest person to ever achieve the highest level of volunteerism within FIRST. I am also finishing my Masters in Instructional Design from UMASS Boston this December.

Specialties: Technology marketing, social media, software marketing, corporate/non-profit relationship management, CRM, website functionality, programming, Adobe CS4, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, assessment, e-learning, project management, product management, copyright, software development management, robotics, technology, volunteer management, volunteer recruitment
