Austin L. Church's Summary

I got my M.A. in English from The University of Tennessee, Knoxville, in May 2008. Guess what? You can't make a living as a poet, at least not for the first forty years.

I worked for six months as a writer, account exec, and social media maven at Morris Creative Group before going freelance.

My one-man show, Bright Newt, is two years old now, and I love what I do: storytelling, copywriting, internet marketing, brand development, and other kinds of business development.

Are you excited about a new brand or business idea? Get in touch! Do you feel like your business is broken? Get in touch! Marketing and branding should be fun.

Specialties

Writing, blogging, social media, press releases, copy writing, creating web content, editing, public speaking, teaching, facilitating conversation, and analytical and problem-solving skills.
