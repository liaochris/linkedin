Christine Beenhakker's Summary

I am a coaching & counselling therapist for burn-out, stress and work related problems.
As an autonomous communications consultant I give various trainings on the subject of NLP, stress & burn-out prevention and communication skills (also in company).

MISSION STATEMENT: Be the change you want to see in the world.

GOALS:
To help clients find a new coping strategy and a life balance with more happiness and well being.
To support employers and employees, managers and executives to cope with stress related problems at work and reduce long-term absenteeism.
To improve communication processes in organisations with more mutual understanding.

OFFERING:
- personal coaching, counselling and psychotherapy
- management coaching and team coaching
- training courses burn-out prevention, NLP and professional communication skills (also in company)
- peer counselling

TOOLKIT:
- NLP Master (Neuro Linguistic Programming)
- Cognitive Behavioural Therapy
- Social Panorama (NLP based systemic work)
- Yoga therapy & Mindfulness
- EFT (Emotionally Focussed Therapy)
- Psychological Assessments (i-Screen, DISC)

I have a clear, personal and solution-focused approach.
My personal values are safety, respect, trust, openness and equivalence.
My coaching style is empathic – analytic.
