Dan Frost's Summary

President of a residential and commercial painting company with over 25 years in the fine art of painting. We work with architects, interior designers, builders, businesses and home owners to ensure that the client is satisfied with a complete design of their living and or working space. We are known for achieving the finest finish possible. Our attention to detail is without peer. Our reputation is based on completing our work in a timely, orderly, polite and conscientious fashion.

Specialties

Interior/Exterior painting and staining; wallcovering removal and installation; wall and ceiling repair; and decorative finishes.
