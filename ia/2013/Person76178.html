Kevin James Bennett's Summary

Kevin James Bennett is known across the industry as one of the most product-driven, business-focused artists of a generation.

— Michael DeVellis, Executive Director of The Powder Group

Emmy® Award winner, Kevin James Bennett's expansive cosmetic knowledge, eye for innovation and innate ability to anticipate trends makes him one of the most sought after experts in the cosmetic industry.
His development, marketing and sales promotion abilities have been fine-tuned by decades of experience and are enriched by world-class artistry and educator status.

In 2005 Kevin created IN MY KIT® (www.inmykit.com), the first online cosmetic buying guide dedicated to professional makeup artists. The website quickly became known as a "pro's product bible" and gained international recognition as a "beauty insider's best kept secret".

In 2010 Kevin launched Makeup Art + Design Enterprises Inc, a cosmetic consulting company to showcase his aggregate skill-set.
M.A.D.E. is focused on innovation, offering comprehensive services for brands, retailers and private clients. Our experience is extensive, which allows us to deliver intelligent, imaginative solutions in areas of product development, brand strategy, product concept/design, copywriting, sales promotion, social media integration and education.

The M.A.D.E. client list includes brands under the L'Oreal, Coty, LVMH, Johnson & Johnson and Proctor & Gamble banners, as well as independent cosmetic companies and entrepreneurs.

Beauty Industry Affiliations: CEW, AMA, PBA, AACS, CEA
Other Affiliations: IATSE, NATAS
