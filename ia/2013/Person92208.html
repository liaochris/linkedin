Michael Conley's Summary

My passion is bringing unique products to the market that help businesses sell their products or services. The first successful product is my specialty notepads. These are spiral-bound 40 page notepads with YOUR business card on the front and a 12-month calendar/tipping chart on the back. We can even provide the business cards with a volume order. These are great for using at a networking event or a trade show.

We have also added a magnetic, dry-erase calendar perfect for fundraising opportunities.

Please take a look at samples of our work on www.specialtynotepad.com.
