Arte Merritt's Summary

Arte Merritt is a serial entrepreneur who has more than 12 years mobile experience and 16 years Internet experience in roles ranging from CEO to product management, business development, sales, marketing, and technical roles.

Arte was the founder and CEO of Motally, a leading mobile-analytics tracking and reporting service. He led Motally through a successful exit with Nokia acquiring it in August 2010. Arte built the first version of Motally, got the initial customers, and raised a Series A round as a sole entrepreneur.

In addition to Motally, Arte co-founded two other companies and a professional organization, and created numerous websites, mobile apps, and even non-technical products.

Arte's main area of focus is mobile. In addition to Motally, he's worked with mobile companies like Yahoo Mobile, Helio, and a wide variety of mobile start-ups. He co-founded the MIT Wireless Forum in Boston in 2001, an event series focused on mobile.

Arte has a degree in Finance from MIT and studied Mechanical Engineering there as well.

A full resume is online at http://www.amerritt.com/resume.php
