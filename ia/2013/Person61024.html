Jeff Livak's Summary

My passion lies in creating sustainable brands by identifying core business objectives with timeless design principles that align a company's visual communication with their defined business strategy. I create brand systems that go beyond the logo into the entire visual experience of the target audience.
I am interested in user interaction and the psychology of the user's experience.

Specialties

Brand design, strategy and implementation from logo to launch. Creating memorable brands by implementing an engaging user experience through various touchpoints.

Conception to completion including file prep and press checks. Experience with textile screenprinting, offset and digital printing.

Adobe Creative Suite including Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign as well as Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint.
