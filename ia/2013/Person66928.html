John Quillen's Summary

Life Sciences IT Consultant
with 20+ years of experience

My technical background coupled with management experience, business acumen and general interest in working with people allows me to translate business drivers and needs into terms that can be understood by developers and vendors. Conversely, I can also identify and explain to business leaders where the risks and technical challenges are that their needs demand and map those needs into a clear, concise technology plan - and deliver.

Knowing what software can and should do affords the ability to quickly analyze a product and match it with business needs. Synthesizing stakeholder discussions, I can drill into a vendor demo to separate slideware, hype and flash from reality.

Consulting/consulted at BMS, Shire, J&J, AstraZeneca, performing business analysis and project/program management. Experience includes:
