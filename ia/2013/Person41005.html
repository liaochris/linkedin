WELS Parish Assistance

Nonprofit; 501-1000 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

January 2002 – June 2010 (8 years 6 months)

Pastor Stroh directed WELS Parish Assistance – a consulting ministry that became a blessing to about 500 congregations with the assistance of both full- and part-time lead consultants and over 100 associate consultants. He also conducted the Turnaround Churches in the WELS research project.

Senior Pastor

Peace Lutheran Church ~ Sun Prairie, WI

February 1985 – January 2002 (17 years)

Pastor Stroh served as a WELS parish pastor for 24 years during which, under God, he launched a new congregation, served as senior pastor in a turnaround congregation, and was privileged to serve in a number of district and synodical positions.
