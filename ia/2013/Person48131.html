Gerald Willoughby's Summary

I have over twenty years of progressively responsible staff and managerial experience in Human Resources and have provided contract recruiting and HR consulting services since 1987.

Possessing a strong background in Employment, College Relations, Policy Development, Workforce Planning, Operations, Outplacement and Transition Planning, I have consulted to a variety of companies including LavaStorm, Incentive Systems, SmartTime Software, Children's Hospital, Wildfire Communications, AT&T, Granite Systems, Powersoft Corporation, net.Genesis Corporation, Kronos Incorporated and Rosemount Corporation. I am an alumnus of Advanced Internet Recruiting Strategies (AIRS I & II) Training.

As a Contract Recruiter and Human Resources consultant, I have:

• Designed and executed national recruiting strategies incorporating the use of direct sourcing, college recruiting, referral programs, media advertising, internet, job fairs and contingency placement.
• Recruited software engineers, systems, sales and marketing, operations, finance, MIS, senior managers, administrative support, healthcare professionals and customer service representatives.
• Consulted to line executives & management team on change activities having organizational impact.
• Led employee focus groups to capitalize on information aimed at increasing employee retention.
• Consulted to senior management and line executives to structure total compensation plans.
• Served as a resource to employees and managers in helping to resolve employee relations issues.

My prior experience includes full-time positions as Staffing Manager, Corporate Human Resources Representative, Technical Recruiter, Senior Staffing Consultant and Compensation Analyst for The Miriam Hospital, Analog Devices, Bank of Boston, M/A-COM, Inc., and Sun Financial Group.

Specialties: Technical recruiting
