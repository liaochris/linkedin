Dr. Elaine Young's Summary

I am a professor of digital marketing. I have experience and in-depth knowledge of interactive, digital and social media marketing techniques, strategies and tools as well as marketing, public relations, media relations, and internet strategy.

My professional experience has given me an appreciation for the importance of teaching students how to engage with the communities they are in, and how to apply successful business techniques to support causes and engage various constituents. I have developed and taught courses in digital marketing, digital marketing metrics and analysis, managing online communities, internet strategy, online visibility, non-profit and social marketing, social networking, marketing, and marketing management. I also teach Marketing at the MBA level.

I have consulted with the Graduate Studies Department at Champlain College to develop an integrated social media marketing presence to help promote our awesome Master's Degree Programs, which has resulted in a fully trained team on LinkedIn and Facebook, increased engagement metrics and activity on both channels. Preliminary results show that the project has helped to increase visibility of the Master's Degree Programs.

I have been asked to speak to numerous groups about social media marketing, digital communication, social networking and marketing. I have held training sessions for staff on specific tools including Twitter and Facebook. As a speaker I'm engaged, passionate, and am quick to read the audience.

I specialize in social media marketing, digital marketing, curriculum development, course development, experiential learning, problem-based learning, service learning, internet marketing, marketing management, organizational management, non-profit and social marketing, generational differences, online visibility, search engine optimization, social networking, web analytics. I am available to speak on any of these subjects.
