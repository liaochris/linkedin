Brad Eich's Summary

Brad Eich
Real Estate Professional and Relocation Specialist:

I understand that selling and/or buying a home is the most personal and costly transaction that most people will ever undertake in their lifetimes. Because it can be a very stressful and sometimes emotional experience, I am committed to devoting myself and my resources to serving the needs of my clients and making each real estate transaction as trouble-free as possible.
I have been an Evergreen and Denver, Colorado resident since 1976 and a Residential Sales Specialist of Denver-Metro-West/Foothill properties since 1997. I previously worked with Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage and currently work as a RE/MAX Alliance affiliate and owner/president of Ike Realty, LLC (Investment Properties). I hold a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) with a specialty in Marketing. I’m a Certified Relocation Specialist (CMRS) and Certified Mountain Area Expert (CMAE). I'm continually studying our changing Real Estate market to better inform my clients. I earn yearly awards for high levels of production and outstanding performance.
My mission is to provide my clients with the personalized service and commitment required to meet their specific real estate needs before, during, and after each transaction. Therefore, I respect my fiduciary relationship with all my clients, subscribing to a high level of ethics, integrity and loyalty. Dedication, due diligence, persistence, conscientiousness and follow-up are traits that have earned me repeat business and the rewards of extensive referrals.
Your success in selling and/or buying is my success. As such, that success begins, ends, and continues with you.
Whether you are looking to sell or buy your personal dream home or investment properties, I would be happy to meet with you and discuss your particular real estate goals. Our first meeting will be a personal, no-obligation consultation.
