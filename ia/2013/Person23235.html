Christopher Drumgoole's Summary

Chris leads the Product Management function at Vocanic, Asia’s largest full service Social Media agency. Based out of the firm’s Singapore HQ, Chris is responsible for leading the product life cycle of Vocanic’s key products and platforms that play a central role to Vocanic’s social media strategy.

Vocanic’s flagship product is VSocial - the Enterprise Social Media Control Center. Chris is responsible for leading all product management activities, from strategic planning, to customer satisfaction, to tactical execution of the development. More information about the product can be found at http://www.vsocial.com.

Earlier, Chris served a broader role of leading the technical project management activities with Vocanic for more than two and a half years. In this more tactical role, Chris’ primary responsibilities included leading campaign -tethered software project lifecycles (planning, cost estimations, resources, scheduling, delivery), and overseeing quality assurance activities. Chris also served as a technical consultant when planning long term social engagements with clients.

Prior to Vocanic, Chris spent more than 6 years in Beijing where he worked for a software outsourcing company focusing on developing and managing an offshore team of more than 30 software engineers for the firm’s clients in the United States. Responsibilities included recruiting, training, regular performance reporting, P&L management, resource planning, scope management, and task oversight and prioritization.

Chris is a Project Management Professional (PMP) certified Software Project Manager with 10+ years project management experience in the software outsourcing and social media industries.

Specialties: product management, lifecycle management, social CRM, social media, offshore software development and consulting, information technology solutions consulting, project management and control, open source, linux, business continuity planning, BCP, disaster recovery, PMP
