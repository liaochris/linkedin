Kat Black's Summary

I've got my paws in many digital pies.

I'm a VJ, artist, author and digital media consultant. Married to Jasper Cook, the other half of VJzoo - we've performed in Spain, Singapore, the UK, France, NZ, New York, San Francisco and around Australia. I also ran the Byte Me! Festival of digital content in Perth, Western Australia in 2007 with speakers from Dreamworks, EA Games, Weta Digital and Weta Workshop as well as local animators and 3D companies with an international reputation.

We have also done Public Artworks, including in collaboration with West Australian Ballet and other cultural organisations.

iPhone apps now out - see littlewhitebook.net and illumiQ.com

Specialties

Art, VJing, social networking, iPhone and web 2.0 content development, tarot, design, digital collage, casual games, festival co-ordination, business match-making, exhausting people with my bouncy enthusiasm, especially when in an overcaffeinated state (uh... that's most of the time)

Web Developer

Built and ran websites, including mass-traffic sites such as retrokat.com/medieval and fork-you.com and more. No longer build website for clients, but still run a few of my own.
