Billy MacLeod's Summary

Billy has a history of being a top producer who has always kept his opportunity pipeline full with a top rate of conversion to close. Billy has always been assertive, and his ability to negotiate and close is proven. Throughout his life, Billy's public speaking ability has opened many doors and closed many opportunities.

Billy has also fought for Dallas residents on a whole range of social issues including Urban Renewal, Economic Development, and Public Safety; and has engaged the Dallas City Council on issues such as the construction of the Homeless Center to funding for the new Animal Shelter in Dallas.

Billy has aligned himself with foundational community groups such as the Texas Campaign for the Environment and helped start Freedom Release Reentry Services, an innovative Prisoner Re-entry program in Downtown Dallas. Billy found that working with formerly incarcerated men and women and helping them learn the life skills necessary to re-enter society successfully was some of the most rewarding work he had ever been involved with.

Billy's sales experience is solid as a rock, but his advertising sales experience key to his overall skill set. The final pieces to the puzzle are his non-profit and military experience, which will always be some part of his life.

Billy has been a candidate for Dallas City Council in District 2 near Downtown Dallas 3 times. In May of 2011 he ran against Deputy Mayor Pauline Medrano and garnered 29% of the vote. He is proud to have served as a personal adviser to many small businesses throughout Dallas and he has a record of engaging City government on their behalf. Billy is very excited to working for Cumulus Broadcasting diving into the future of broadcast media sales.

Billy has a history of developing strong client relationships and a proven track record of leading and producing aggressive growth results, capable of identifying, pursuing, and closing complex solution sales.

Specialties

Billy's specialties include Branding, Event Marketing, Non Traditional Revenue, New Media/New Technology, Long Form On-Air Programming, and above all Creative Ideas that translate into Results for clients. Billy has focused his off time on community advocacy in and around the Downtown Dallas area and he currently lives in The Cedars just South of Downtown.

Billy is incredibly proud to have honorably served in the United States Navy on Active Duty from 3/14/89 until 11/11/92 and he then served on Active Naval Reserve from 11/11/92 until 2/11/96.

After graduating from Navy Supply School in Meridian Mississippi, Billy served in the US Navy Seabee’s Naval Mobile Construction Combat Battalion 7 serving in Gulfport, Misissippi, Roosevelt Roads Puerto Rico, Okinawa Japan and from there was deployed into the Persian Gulf region into the Kingdom of Bahrain. Billy was deployed to the Persian Gulf during Operation Desert Shied and Operation Desert Storm, receiving 2 East Asia Service medals. He was involved in Hurricane Hugo Relief Efforts earning a Coast Guard Commendation Medal for his involvement. Billy also played club Rugby in Okinawa Japan and played for the All Island Combined Forces All Star Rugby Side. Billy was honorably discharged with distinction on December 11, 1996 and immediately returned to Stephen F. Austin to complete his business degree.
