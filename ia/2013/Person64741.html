Joel Shapiro's Summary

I am an owner and partner in two organizations (JMS Technical Solutions & J. Boye). The missions of both companies involve connecting digital and technical professionals to achieve success with their projects, advance their careers and provide value to their organizations.

Born and raised on Long Island and spending my 20's living in Brooklyn, I now reside in Buffalo, NY with my wife, son and dog.
