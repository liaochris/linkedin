Jennifer Weaver's Summary

I have been a Realtor, Certified Residential Specialist, and Relocation Specialist in the greater Houston area since 1979. I list and sell homes primarily in Ft. Bend County (Sugar Land, Missouri City, Richmond, Stafford), Katy, west and southwest Houston, and Pearland. I also work in the near northwest area of Copperfield/290. My years of experience and knowledge of a large portion of the Houston area are helpful to families relocating new to the city who need to make a housing decision quickly.

Specialties
