Kyle Sexton's Summary

Business and organizations are looking for answers to their questions about the future. Kyle Sexton has new ideas about pricing, programs, organizational culture, member involvement and much more.

Kyle Sexton is marketing strategist and served as director of business development for the Salem Area Chamber of Commerce for more than a decade. Kyle was recognized as the 2007 Chamber Staff Person of the Year for the Western Association of Chamber Executives.

A Preferred Partner of the American Chamber of Commerce Executives, Kyle now consults with chambers and economic development organizations throughout the U.S. and Canada. His organizational consulting experience lends itself to insightful thinking and creative solutions to transforming your chamber's brand.

In 2008, he created ChamberPeople.com, the largest online social network dedicated to chambers of commerce. He is also the creator of the Member SalesEngine, an online membership sales module for associations.

His innovations have been featured in the Wall Street Journal, and his book, Remembership - New Thinking for Tomorrow's Membership Organization, is fueling transformations in many North American membership organizations.

Specialties

Membership Development professional with experience in business development, sales, marketing, branding, voice-over, audio production, web design and development, advertising and public relations. Sales and marketing systems for chambers of commerce and other membership-based organizations.
