More about Lora:
Lora lives in Round Rock, Texas, with her husband, Jake, and two crazy, lovable mutts. Although Round Rock is her home, Lora is also passionate about Austin, just 23 minutes down the road.

At work, Lora loves selling homes, specializing in listing property in both Williamson and Travis Counties (Greater Austin area). She also works extensively with homebuyers.

Creator of rheumwarrior.com, an outlet of hope for those diagnosed with rheumatoid arthritis, Lora writes and speaks on courage and inspiration within the RA community. She also intends for her work to forward pioneering research on autoimmune diseases.

From a Marine family, Lora was born and raised all over the US. She studied Business and Journalism at Univ of AL-Tuscaloosa (Roll Tide!) and graduated from Univ of NV-Reno.

Catch her chasing the sunrise during her daily, early-morning gym workouts, riding the wake behind her boat on Lake Austin, exploring great foodie and fashion joints, and savoring a delicious glass of red wine.

This Texan-heart loves to travel and is head-over-heels for Aruba. Lora also loves speed: fast cars, fast motorcycles, fast boats ~ all had her at "hello." Also a football fanatic, so nevermind if she does an end zone dance after helping a client close on their home...

Lora participates in ASCEND, the prestigious emerging leader program through the Round Rock Chamber of Commerce, is a graduate of their 2012-13 Leadership Class, and serves as a Chamber Ambassador.
