Bill Smith's Summary

William A. Smith, Investment Advisor Representative & Founder of W.A. Smith Financial Group, has nearly two-decades of experience helping financially responsible individuals with retirement planning, estate planning, wealth preservation & financial planning. For Smith, providing optimal & sound advice to clients is his #1 commitment & priority. He currently has a Series 66, insurance license & is a Registered Financial Consultant with the International Association of Registered Financial Consultants.

Bill is constantly sought after by the local & national media. On a national level, he has appeared in The Wall Street Journal, Smart Money Magazine, Yahoo!Finance, AOL Finance, Forbes.com, Senior Market Advisor & on CBS Channel 19. Bill also hosts a popular radio show titled “Retirement Matters” where he interviews and debates with the nation’s top industry professionals. He is the author of “Knock-Out Your Retirement Income Worries FOREVER: The Ultimate Training Guide for Developing Financial Security”, which was published in December 2011. Outside of his profession, he enjoys spending time with family. He & his wife Erica have three children & the five of them enjoy spending time together as a family, boating on Lake Erie & supporting each other at numerous sporting events.

W.A. Smith Financial Group specializes in reliable income plans that clients cannot outlive & reducing unnecessary estate & income taxation. Their seasoned team of advisors & support staff are committed to providing the best service in the retirement planning industry today. They do this by strategically helping clients lower tax bills, reduce investment fees, protect money from future stock market declines & develop income plans that they will never outlive. W.A. Smith also provides education about key issues clients face when it comes to retirement—like creating guaranteed income, managing wealth in any economy, applying the right amount of investment risk & making sure the estate is in order.
