Arthur Silverman's Experience

Principal

Arthur Silverman Issue Communications

2006 – Present (7 years) Washington D.C. Metro Area

ASIC is a media and marketing consultancy specializing in strategic communications on controversial public policy issues. Our mission is to raise public awareness, build citizen engagement and produce concrete results in support of our clients' progressive policy goals and their own institutional growth.

I offer a proven track record of success with executive, legislative, regulatory and judicial initiatives, as well as broader public awareness campaigns.

Services include:
-- Strategy, concepts, messaging and targeting
-- Skilled copywriting, editing and content creation across platforms
-- Earned media materials, events and outreach
-- Campaign coordination and coalition building

Sample clients: AFSCME, Center for Science in the Public Interest, Environmental Defense Fund, Google, Greenpeace, MALDEF, NAACP Legal Defense Fund, National Council of Churches, National League of Cities, Solar Energy Industries Association, Tigercomm, VVH Consulting
