David Marble's Summary

An extreme generalist with a passion for people, important problems, and thoughtful answers.

I've spent most of my career in product management, development, and operations for early-stage tech companies. I look for opportunities to build and spread technologies that improve quality of life for a very large base of people, such as those that:

• improve and automate where possible everyday processes (scheduling, finance, product/service/event discovery, people discovery, medicine)
• open up new modes of communication between individuals, groups, and the masses that may lead to increased efficiency, understanding, and meaningful living
• help us form or enrich meaningful relationships
• improve the quality or efficiency of legal, financial, and medical services
• drive down costs of accessing legal, financial, and medical knowledge and services
• provide access to better education or create a better learning experience
• facilitate better allocation of resources and attention to things that matter

Specialties: product management, web and mobile application design and development, startups, business development, research, writing, speaking
