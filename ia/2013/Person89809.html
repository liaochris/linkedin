Maurice Velasquez's Summary

Team Real World is an organizational development and corporate training team that helps clients create alignment, leadership, management excellence and effective team dynamics. Team Real World offers the unique combination of consulting and training, thus helping to create a connection between the executive team and the training deliverables.

Traditional training does not usually work because of how it is being delivered, namely in long, all-day sessions, covering lots of generic material in single offerings, usually in lecture style with big manuals or lengthy PowerPoints. Unfortunately, as soon as the sessions end, the material is easy forgotten....because there was any re-enforcement or practical application built into the training.

This approach does not work to create long-lasting results.

Enter Team Real World.

Training is more effective when delivered in short sessions one week apart, mastering smaller amounts of material and practicing with real-world scenarios in an engaging group environment.

Results are achieved quicker in this method. Our team listens to what you need, we get to know your people, we tailor solutions unique to your team and we teach your team tools and habits to help them improve.

....and we make it fun! We make all our sessions engaging and relevant, applicable and long-lasting.

Our "Tools for Success" approach include focus in the following areas:

* Executive and strategic planning
* Leadership and management excellence
* Middle management effectiveness
* Personality Alignment
* Decision-making and problem-solving
* Project management and execution
* Team Dynamics and efficiency
* Train the Trainers and Computer Training

Call us and request a "test drive" to see how operate and if we are a good fit for your team and organization. Ask us about funding for all our services through the LA Workforce Commission's training grants and funding.

Contact us at 225-772-4357 (HELP) or email us at testdrive@teamrealworld.com.
