Heidi Dearinger's Summary

As Business Development Coordinator with EHL Insurance I wear many hats and manage a wide variety of responsibilities and independent projects. The majority of my time is spent in the following areas: creating custom graphic design elements, presentations, newsletters (print and electronic); website design & maintenance (SEO); marketing, communications & advertising (traditional and digital campaigns); designing custom lead tracking database systems, relationship management, team training (sales and service), sales reporting, special events & team facilitation.

I manage EHL's blog, serve as team captain, write articles, edit and provide feedback to writing team, provide custom graphics and photographs for posts, advertise and post content to social media channels, and manage RSS feed.

I have been a small business owner since the mid-90's. At Mysticool, I primarily focus on creating custom beaded jewelry and as time permits... provide graphic design and web design services.

Volunteer work and serving my community is a big part of my life. I have volunteered as a group facilitator, graphic designer, and marketer for a variety of non-profits in Kitsap County and King County. One of my most fulfilling volunteer roles is as a certified sexuality educator for middle school aged youth.

Specialties: I have a social service and parenting education background focusing on child abuse and domestic violence prevention and intervention. I majored in psychology, thoroughly enjoy contemplating human behavior and get great energy from connecting with people and helping them with the challenges they face.
