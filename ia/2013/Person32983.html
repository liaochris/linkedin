Dazzie McKelvy's Summary

Over twenty five years of experience developing successful sector strategies and creating lasting change in the workforce market. Comprehensive experience - from building and managing an award winning, innovative nonprofit, to managing a statewide workforce program. Focused on building collaborative solutions with employers, educators, and community organizations, to address workforce needs, and improve the lives of individual workers.
