Energetic, Poised, Humorous, Smart,
Professional, Conversational, Powerful, and Entertaining…

…all words that have been used to describe Jaclyn Rowe’s speaking style as she pursues her dream of motivating and moving others to live an extraordinary life.

As a national speaker and a Certified Personality Trainer, Jaclyn impresses on others the value and significance of understanding themselves, working to improve relationships, developing great communication skills, and striving for excellence in character and integrity.

Jaclyn began a professional speaking career in 2005, and had the opportunity to motivate and encourage nearly 80,000 high school and college aged students in her home state of Missouri alone through Monster.com’s Making It Count programs ranking quickly in the top 10% of speakers in this national program.

She is a co-author of the book “Ready, Set, Succeed; Making Your Dreams Come True.” Jaclyn’s unique perspective is rooted in her personal journey from country cowgirl to television personality. Growing up in a rural community instilled in Jaclyn the value of family ties, honest work, and deep moral roots.

After graduating with honors from Potosi High School, Jaclyn went on to graduate Summa cum laude from Missouri Baptist University in St. Louis where she earned a Bachelor’s degree in Communications and a Writing Certificate in English. Jaclyn, her husband, Nathan, son, Peyton and daughter, Chloe reside on a cattle ranch in beautiful southeast Missouri.

Whether Jaclyn is your keynote speaker or workshop presenter, she is sure to lead you on the way to life extraordinary!

Jaclyn Rowe's Publications

  • Author

    • King Hezekiah, Examining a Life of Bold Faith
    • August 29, 2013
    Authors: Jaclyn Rowe

    Many have written about and studied the lives of King David, King Solomon and Queen Esther. Most churchgoers can tell you King David was called a man after God’s own heart despite his affair and conspiracy to cover his mistake with murder. Many can tell you King Solomon asked for wisdom, built the temple and had more wealth than we could fathom and that Queen Esther was chosen “for such a time as this” to save her people. However, few are as familiar with the account of an equally inspiring individual, King Hezekiah.

    This five-week study will take you on a spiritual journey through history, examining the life of a young and bold King. You will be encouraged to open your Bible and look purposefully and intently for God. You will see how God uses His people to expand His kingdom and you will find hope. You will learn that no matter your background or your circumstances, God is able to turn things around. You will remember afresh that His love for your heart is compelling. You will discover that there are no Biblical heroes comparable to the God of the Bible.

    God is the hero of every story—including yours.
