Adam Mills' Summary

Adam Mills is an energetic, dedicated and approachable professional who always keeps the needs and interests of others foremost in his mind.

He graduated with honours from the University of Guelph with a bachelor of commerce degree specializing in marketing. Adam later spent five years working for two international high-tech companies managing large-scale marketing projects. His creativity and strategic approach led to increased product awareness, lead generation, and sales volume in both instances.

At the age of 24, Adam purchased, renovated and sold his first home. This experience was invaluable in providing him with a personal and detailed understanding of the many factors that are so vital in successful real estate transactions – staging, curb appeal, minor repairs, market valuation, and financing. Adam has lived in both rural and urban Ottawa (Centrepointe, Manotick, Centretown, and Barrhaven) giving him a broad understanding of the Ottawa real estate market.

Adam’s dedication and professionalism will effectively serve the needs of both buyers and sellers alike. Buyers can be confident that the home they purchase meets their personal criteria, is fairly priced and generates a positive return when it comes time for resale. Sellers will know that their home is accurately priced, effectively represented and sold in a timely manner.

With his expert communication skills and fluency in both English and French, Adam will devote himself to his client’s satisfaction at all times.
