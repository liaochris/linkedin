Kevin Keplar's Experience

Director of Technology / Digital Media Instructor

Wichita State University

Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Higher Education industry

July 1998 – Present (15 years 6 months) Wichita, Kansas

Currently, I am the Director of Technology for the Elliott School of Communication at Wichita State University. In that capacity I am responsible for the day-to-day operation of all computer and audio /visual equipment in our department.


In addition to my role as Director of Technology, I am an instructor for the Elliott School, teaching courses for our integrated media curriculum. My instruction strong points center around media and technology including, but not limited to; radio, audio and video production, web design and use of social media.

Recording Arts Instructor

Bethany College at Mindfire Academy

January 2013 – Present (1 year) Wichita, Kansas Area

Adjunct instructor for Bethany College at Mindfire Academy in the Recording Arts emphasis area. Courses include; Introduction to Multimedia, Basic Recording Techniques, Sound for Picture, Avid Pro Tools Certification and Pre & Post Music Production.

Freelance Media Production

SOUNDSFISHY.NET

2003 – 2012 (9 years) Wichita, Kansas

SOUNDSFISHY.NET is the name of my personal audio production company. My main focus is voice over work for radio, TV and the web. Additionally, I have done post audio work for film and network television. In addition to audio work, I also do freelance work in web design, media consulting and social media and marketing. Local and national client list available by request.

Programming, Promotions & Sales

Back in the year 2000, I was recruited by Dan Smith, the owner of KANR radio, to help him build a new station. Over the course of the three years we worked together, I helped by working on air, cutting commercials, promotions, marketing and sales.

Programming & Promotions

My first full time job after graduating college was working in radio. I was employed full time as a night time on-air talent and quickly moved up the media ladder. By the time I left KICT (T-95) I was hosting afternoon drive, was promotions director and assistant to the program director.
