Derek Huether's Summary

OBJECTIVE: Take the hand waving and voodoo out of Agile. Provide disciplined coaching, strategic enterprise transformation consulting, and project and portfolio management services.

You want to know my background in around 100 words?

I am the voice behind The Critical Path blog and author of Zombie Project Management (KDP 2011). Over the last 25 years, I’ve been a U.S. Marine, Start-Up Founder, Project Manager, and Federal Government Project Management Office (PMO) Advisor. Those titles align with helping start-ups, private corporations, educational institutions, and government agencies. As a Certified PMP (Project Management Professional) and PMI-ACP (Agile Certified Practitioner) through the Project Management Institute and both a Certified ScrumMaster and Certified Scrum Product Owner through the Scrum Alliance, I seek to identify customer needs and wants and then propose the best approach to deliver value and meet their goals.

Specialties: Agile Transformation, Agile in the Enterprise, Portfolio Alignment, PMO Activities, Servant-Leadership, PMBOK, SOX, SDLC, Waterfall, Agile, Scrum, Kanban, Visual Control Systems
