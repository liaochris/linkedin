Charles Bennett's Summary

I am Head of Change Delivery for NextTen, Member of the Board for iCMG plus board advisor to a number of other companies. I passionately believe that Customer Centricity and its alignment to the wider change agenda e.g. fixing broken process (Advanced BPM) is going to contribute more to company success over the next three to five years than any other item on the strategic agenda! This is not just my thinking – empirical evidence is emerging plus the leading independent researchers such as Gartner and Forrester have recently made similar observations.

My approach is to use the latest advances in customer centricity, Business Process Management (BPM), Enterprise Architecture and programme delivery to drive rapid business performance change and innovation in companies of all sizes from Fortune 500 and FTSE companies to start-up.

I have unlocked in excess of 200 million USD business benefits for my clients. I have led multi-million dollar change initiatives from business concept to delivery – mentoring, training and delivering change from practitioner to CEO/Board level, across 30 countries, into financial services, insurance, outsourcing, public sector, pharmaceuticals and IT/Service industries.

I enjoy thought sharing with other professionals. I am regularly asked to contribute to change conferences as both Chairman and Keynote speaker and have authored numerous articles. I also participate on a part time basis on European University and Business School MBA Programmes.

Specialties: Business Transformation, Advanced Process Management (APM), Outside-In thinking, Business Development, Marketing, Trainer and Coach, Program Management (MSP), Project Management (PMP, Prince 2 and APMP), Change Management, Managing Strategic Alliances, General business management including P&L responsibility. Business strategy/direction definition, business differentiation and raising funding. Negotiation and Contract Management.
