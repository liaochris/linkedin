Joshua Hammond's Summary

Josh Hammond is Vice President, Director of Marketing and New Business Development at Zócalo Group – Omnicom’s award-winning word-of-mouth, social media, and digital marketing agency.

Prior to joining Zócalo Group he was a Senior Digital Strategist, and later the Director of Marketing, for NM Incite – a joint venture between Nielsen and McKinsey & Company. Josh has also worked as a public relations account supervisor at Northlich, an agency focused on corporate, crisis, and digital communications.

Josh has also held a variety of political and government affairs positions in the Midwest and Washington DC, working with: U.S. Senator Dan Coats (IN), U.S. Senator Dick Lugar (IN), and U.S. Congressman Steve Buyer (IN-4). In addition to his time on Capitol Hill, he lobbied for the high-tech industry, working for: The Computer Systems Policy Project and Varian, Inc.

He's a graduate of Hanover College and holds a Masters of Science in Interactive Media from Quinnipiac University. Josh is also an accredited (APR) member of the Public Relations Society of America.

Contact him at joshua.hammond (at) gmail.com or @joshuahammond on Twitter.

Specialties: Word of Mouth, Social Media, Digital Strategy, Defensive Branding, Corporate Communications, Ideation, New Business Development, Marketing, and Strategic Account Planning
