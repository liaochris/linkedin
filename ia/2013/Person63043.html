Jessica Crystal Green's Summary

My professional objective is to build a career in the field of libraries and archives, with a focus on cultural heritage preservation, especially in the fields of literature and the arts. Between my studies, work experience, and enthusiasm, I will try to build solid understanding of how to organize, preserve, and make accessible archival records of all kinds.
