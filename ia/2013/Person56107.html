Chris Webb's Summary

I am a passionate and pro-active 3D artist with years of experience in the Architectural Visualisation industry, both London and now Scandinavia.

Specialties include: modelling, lighting, texturing, Vray & Mental Ray, Photoshop, photographic people, camera matching, plans, sections & cutaways, network & distributed rendering, animation & rendering, basic real-time skills, html & CSS design & coding, marketing, industry networking.

Chris Webb's Experience

3D Illustrator

Tengbom

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

March 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months) Stockholm, Sweden

At Tengbom I work closer with architects and their projects. I work on a range of projects at different stages; concepts, development, sales images. Sometimes the images are completely for in-house purposes, sometimes they are to win a client or a competetion.

I have enjoyed bringing my 3D studio experience to the in-house studio at Tengbom. I assist with their marketing, run seminars/tutorials on good workflows and encourage the studio to critique their work in weekly team meetings. I have also been building a reference library of 3D illustrations for Tengbom.

3D Artist

Hayes Davidson

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

November 2011 – January 2012 (3 months) London, United Kingdom

At Hayes Davidson I took a massive step up from being just a visualiser. We were considered artists and encouraged to think like photographers and story-tellers. HD ran weekly seminars & tutorial sessions, and they benefit from an in-house photographer passing on his invaluable advice. It was highly stressed that we consider composition and tone, and would never start a new project without a good reference image to aim for.
