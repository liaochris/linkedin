Sonia Chopra's Summary

~ Available for your next project, let's talk! ~

Award-winning design professional Sonia Chopra has been creating since she picked up her first crayon. A fan of efficiency and pixel-perfection, Sonia is determined to provide the best possible creative solutions to achieve client's business goals—on-time and within budget.

Raised in northern New England, Sonia earned a Master of Fine Arts in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design in NYC, and a Bachelor of Arts in Architecture Studies from Rice University in Houston.

Sonia's expertise is leading creative teams in user experience and design for large-scale website, commerce, and mobile projects. She excels in communicating vision and best practices to clients and project teams; delivering digital solutions that are visually engaging, usable, technically driven experiences.

Sonia has collaborated on creative projects for a number of clients including The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf, Tissot, Burlington Coat Factory, One Hope Wine, Advanstar, 1-800-Dentist, AC Moore, C&H Distributors, Gifts That Give, and NYC Department of Health. Her work has gained press in The Wall Street Journal, NY Times, and Good Housekeeping.

Sonia owns and operates FreshSparks, a full-service design studio offering website and mobile design & development, web hosting, and online marketing services. She was also the Art Director at Guidance, and Creative Director for Kahani (an award-winning children's literary magazine).

On the off chance Sonia is away from her Apple devices, you can find her running next to the Pacific Ocean, refining her cooking skills, or at a music festival.

  • Shop the Floor

    • February 2013 to Present

Guidance delivered highly-customized websites with Drupal and Magento that provides exhibitors and attendees the tools to explore product lines before, during and after the show.

  • Kraco.com

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Sonia Chopra, Josh Lusby, , Muliadi Jeo, Jason Meugniot

    Kraco, the leading manufacturer and distributor of aftermarket automotive floor mats and accessories, required an updated website that provided a user-friendly, branded online shopping experience on a modern commerce platform with powerful merchandising tools.

    Guidance delivered a Magento Enterprise website that greatly improves Kraco’s merchandising capabilities. The site’s new feature-rich product detail page includes tabbed navigation for product details, features, specs, associated videos, and reviews. This page further supports conversion with an “Add to Compare” feature that offers users a side-by-side comparison with other selected products and it is integrated with Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest to encourage viral marketing of the product.

    The new site also features a video library with “how-to” and preview videos, and offers users the ability to purchase virtual gift cards.

  • Environmental Lights

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Sonia Chopra, Dustin Howard, Jan Zands, Muliadi Jeo, Jon Provisor

    Environmental Lights, the leader in sustainable, energy-efficient LED lighting wanted to migrate to a new commerce platform that would be flexible, easy-to-update and provide for rich product detail pages to highlight the 150 attributes per product.

    Because of the information-rich product detail page, the project required a streamlined product data import so that all of the 1,700 products could be quickly exported from the client’s database.

    The project also required data migration from two highly disparate commerce platforms - a .NET shopping cart platform and a PHP shopping cart platform. These migrations included a complex transactional history for past customers, including previous orders placed as well as a full address book migration.

    In addition, a highly complex product shipping/rating/freight engine was required.
