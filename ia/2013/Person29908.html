Daniel Ryan's Summary

Daniel J. Ryan is an established business lawyer in North Andover, Massachusetts, who has represented businesses from inception to exit in a variety of industries. A graduate of the University of Michigan and Boston College Law School, he has practiced corporate and transactional law at large, international law firms, first in Denver, Colorado, and then in Boston.

His experience includes a wide range of corporate matters with both startups and mature companies, including work as outside general counsel to several companies, and he has been noted for his exceptional ability to establish and maintain relationships with clients and other professional colleagues at all levels of an organization.

He has presented on corporate and business law topics both internally to attorneys and as a recurring guest speaker to MBA students at MIT Sloan School of Management and the University of Colorado.

Specialties

Business law, entity formation, business planning, venture capital, mergers & acquisition, franchise law and regulatory compliance, licensing, stockholder agreements, buy-sell agreements, succession planning
