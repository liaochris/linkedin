Louis Lizzadro, CCIM's Experience

President

Lizzadro Properties, Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Commercial Real Estate industry

October 2004 – Present (9 years 1 month) Oak Brook, Illinois

Lizzadro Properties Inc. was founded in 2004 by Louis Lizzadro, CCIM to persue investments in commercial real estate. We acquire stable Industrial and Office properties through SEC Reg D Group Investment Syndications, creating value for our stakeholders by focusing on owning, managing and leasing commercial real estate located in the greater Chicago area. This helps us to continually improve our depth of experience and knowledge by concentrating on this specific area.

With the challenges over the past five years and those that we have yet to encounter through this economic cycle, Lizzadro Properties and our operating partners have worked diligently to provide efficient and profitable operating properties. Using a conservative approach to managing assets and developing strategies to mitigate risk, we strive to improve our investment performance.

Lizzadro Properties is now looking toward the future and expanding opportunities to serve those who chose to join us on this adventure. We are developing programs where we will be able to consider assets in other markets such as Southern California, Kansas City, Phoenix, Houston and Dallas/Fort Worth. All of our interests surround areas based on logistics patterns that follow primary distribution and manufacturing operations.

Managing Broker

Lizzadro Real Estate, LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Commercial Real Estate industry

April 2006 – Present (7 years 7 months) Oak Brook, IL

Louis L. Lizzadro is the Founder and Managing Member/Managing Broker of Lizzadro Real Estate, LLC (LizzadroCRE.com).

Lizzadro Real Estate is a boutique real estate brokerage firm that has been licensed in the state of Illinois since 2006. We have represented many clients in all forms of commercial transactions such as land, industrial, office, multifamily, forestry and retail. These projects have covered a wide scope, such as; acquisitions, leasing, dispositions, environmental remediation management, commercial forestry programs, property management, tenant build-out, development, reclassification, entitlement, land management and condo conversion with a combined value in excess of $55,000,000.

This organization currently holds a Brokers License in Illinois with a license applied for in Indiana. We are also working on licenses in the near future in the states of Florida, Michigan, California and Arizona.

Lizzadro Farms Inc. owns and operates timber harvest land, which currently contains more than 900 acres of productive timber in the Upper Peninsula of Michigan, Keweenaw County. Treasurer

Gratiot Lake Conservancy

November 2007 – Present (6 years)

The Gratiot Lake Conservancy (GLC) is located in Keweenaw County in the Upper Peninsula of Michigan. An important mission of GLC is to conserve and protect Gratiot Lake and the land within the Gratiot Lake watershed. A 307 acre nature preserve at the Lake includes over 10,000 feet of shoreline.
GLC recently acquired Bammert Farm, a 466 acre parcel located about 2 miles from Gratiot Lake. Plans for Bammert Farm include wildlife habitat restoration, archaeological research focusing on its history as a farm supplying Phoenix and Central mines. Sustainable forestry and educational projects are also planned.
Through educational programs and materials, the Conservancy encourages good stewardship of the watershed and an understanding of the history and ecology of the area.
The Gratiot Lake Conservancy is a 501( c )3 non profit organization. Your support of GLC is welcome.
