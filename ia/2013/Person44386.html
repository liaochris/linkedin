Felicia Slattery's Summary

My life's work has been about communication. That is why I formed my first business, Transformative Journeys Life Coaching in early 2006-- to help others expand their communication skills for greater joy, success, and fulfillment at home and at work. In 2007 I developed a second brand, Communication Transformation to help small business owners effectively communicate with their clients and prospects.

I still enjoy teaching in the college classroom, and have added to that teaching workshops and seminars for corporations, associations, and private groups. I have also developed my own group classes as well as work with people in private coaching.

Because my academic and teaching background in communication is so diverse, I can help with every area of communication including interpersonal communication, business and professional communication, public speaking, mass communication and more.

Specialties

I specialize in helping my clients create effective marketing communication messages, as well as mastering their public speaking skills in order to bring in more prospects, more clients and more cash flow.

I also have a service where I work with c-level executives to help them improve the communication and speaking skills in order to boost corporate morale and achieve higher productivity from their workforce.
