Robin Reynolds, SCCC's Summary

SCCC PROPERTY OWNERSHIP & MANAGEMENT

Santa Cruz Commerce Center is an enterprise of the Ak-Chin Indian Community and is developed, operated, and managed by the Ak-Chin Industrial Park Board.

The Industrial Park Board also oversees the development and management of the nearby Ak-Chin Regional Airport (formerly Phoenix Regional Airport) and the executive office suites at Santa Cruz Office Center.

COMMERCIAL PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

• The largest, fully improved commercial/industrial property in the City of Maricopa, Arizona area.
• More than 50 acres of immediately available leasable land
• Build to suit options available for well-qualified tenants
• Shovel-ready lots ranging from 1.2 acres & up
• Zoned I-1 & I-2
• Ideal for light manufacturing, green industry, tech businesses including data centers

INDUSTRIAL PARK LOCATION

On the far east section of the Ak-Chin Indian Community
Between the cities of Maricopa & Casa Grande, Pinal County, Arizona
Fronting the Maricopa-Casa Grande Highway (Highway 238) between Murphy Rd & Antone St
2.5 miles from the Ak-Chin Regional Airport (formerly Phoenix Regional Airport)
18 miles to Interstate 10 corridor
13.5 miles to Interstate 8 corridor
40 miles to Phoenix Int'l Sky Harbor Airport
70 miles to Tucson, AZ
