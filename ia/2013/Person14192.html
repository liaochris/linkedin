Bill Anderson's Summary

Bill Anderson's leadership, strategies and hands-on management approach have propelled organizations to the forefront of the financial industry for over 20 years.

Prior to launching TheAnderson4um, Bill served as Partner, COO and CIO of Brown Brothers Harriman, the largest and oldest US bank - from 1994 to 2007. There, he was responsible for 2,000 employees across American, European and Asian locations. Under his leadership, BBH's global operations and technology were completely transformed and now set the standard in the industry, In fact, global surveys indicated BBH's technology went from last to first in less than 4 years.

He served as Chair of the firm's Operating and Technology committees, and as a key member of the Finance, HR and business line committees. Bill's legacy is one of the reasons BBH continues to be profitable and a world leader even in difficult economic times.

From 1989 to 1994, Bill was a SVP, Chief Administrative Officer, and Chief Information Officer in the Retail Brokerage Division at Kidder, Peabody & Co. where he was responsible for overseeing these functions at 55 branches. Prior to Kidder, he was a Project Manager at Electronic Data Systems.

Bill Anderson's vision of the Anderson4um is to improve the leadership and management skills of business executives around the world. We provide real-world information, thought-provoking insights and proven methodologies...all with the goal of helping business leaders improve performance, gain a competitive edge and accomplish their goals.
