Hector M. Sanchez Jr.'s Summary

Film Director:

My job as a director is to develop a vision for the finished film, and define a practical route for achieving it. During pre-production, I make crucial decisions, such as selecting the right cast, crew and locations for the film. Then I direct rehearsals, and the performances of the actors once the film is in production. I also manage all the technical aspects of filming, including the camera, sound, lighting, design and special effects departments.

Camera Operator:

As a Camera Operators I perform a vital role within the camera department on films, commercials, and any other production video. I primarily support the Director of Photography (DOP or DP), and the Director, by accurately carrying out their instructions regarding shot composition and development.

Hector M. Sanchez Jr.'s Publications

  • Darren Ockert: A Mini Documentary Wins the Enzian Film Slam

    • writeclickcooklisten.com
    • February 18, 2013
    Authors: Hector M. Sanchez Jr., Tender Branson

    The ten minute film was directed by Hector M. Sanchez Jr. and it follows Darren (and his dog briefly) around his South Florida home and into the studio as he records his new album, Short Story Long (due out this Summer). It hits topics such as Darren’s music career, his style of writing and what it is like to be an openly gay artist. “For me, it just felt really important to be myself…even though it felt really scary” he states in the film. He is inspired by Adam Lambert who was able to make it big while being openly gay and, to a lesser extent, Ricky Martin. Although he thinks Martin would’ve done just as well if he had been out from the start. The documentary premiered last night at the Enzian Film Slam in Orlando and walked away with the top honors.
