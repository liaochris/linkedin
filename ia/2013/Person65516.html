Joël Maranhão (biowatts.org)'s Experience

Founder / Software Architect

Biowatts

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Oil & Energy industry

May 2010 – Present (3 years 4 months) Baden-Baden, Germany

An Online International Platform
for Anaerobic Digestion Projects

http://biowatts.org

Biowatts is an Internet platform to address practical questions around biogas generation and anaerobic digestion technologies. It contains an anaerobic digestion calculator built with a business rule engine and an extendable database of substrates. It also comprises a forum add-on and an online biomass marketplace for biogas plants.

Biowatts is a platform with the ambition to address this challenge by providing a set of interconnected tools that will allow a free exchange of information by linking biogas stakeholders worldwide. Biogas professionals, investors, scientists, farmers, researchers, the food industry and Governments can all use Biowatts to draw a picture of a biogas plant project and have the community react and help to take the correct decision. Biowatts is not another informational website but rather an online workshop to discuss, illustrate and expose your biogas project and ask questions on specific issues.

After hard work and pooling users about their practical needs, we built the current platform and made it available in English, German, French, Italian, Russian and Spanish. Very soon it will also be available in Chinese, Swedish, Turkish and Portuguese. It’s on the web and free of charge.
