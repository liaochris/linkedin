Ken Horkavy's Summary

Hello! Welcome to my LinkedIn profile. I bring big energy wherever I go. Just ask my linkedIn friends. I inspire through words and action and turn the energy up! :)

⌘ Current Goal 1: To incorporate my focus on permaculture/small scale farming/gardeners and social media access to develop a system that distributes locally grown food easily to the ones that need it most. It is easy to donate excess food to a shelter, but distributing food to the locals that can use it is better.

⌘ Current Goal 2: To remain happy and to inspire others.

I have done many exciting things in my life. I am grateful beyond words and humbled by my experiences. Each day brings new opportunity and lessons in living a fulfilled existence.

While living in Miami, New York and Los Angeles I worked in entertainment as a business developer, producer and all around liaison for getting things done. Often operating as a project manager in business terms, I bring together talent and skilled professionals to accomplish goals set by a project originator.

When my family needed me, I left it all behind, went home and stayed there until the difficulties passed. That was roughly from 2008-2010.

In 2011 I began to plan my next great adventure. I bought a motorhome and decided to travel the country to take it all in while I'm at a relatively young age. There is nothing like traveling with no destination in mind!

I am available to consult on business developments, websites, SEO, social media, fusion marketing and business development. I have a keen knowledge of print media, online media as well as a broad understanding of developing markets in any professional target group. I can bring my varied background and knowledge to the forefront to help your business grow or find your niche in an increasingly competitive world.
