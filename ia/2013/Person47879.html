George Dube's Publications

  • 81 Financial and Tax Tips for the Canadian Real Estate Investor

    • John Wiley & Sons Ltd.
    • March 2010
    Authors: George Dube, Don Campbell, Navaz Murji

    For the many thousands of real estate investors across the country, here at last is a practical, compact, and easy-to-understand guide to accounting and tax-saving strategies. The book is designed to introduce money-saving strategies as well as determine what properties offer optimum equity growth.

    How can this book help you? Whether you have already have real estate portfolio, or are considering investing, take the time to read through this book to identify tips that might apply to you. Then, speak to your tax advisor to see whether you can implement them to create and protect your wealth.

  • Legal, Tax and Accounting Strategies for the Canadian Real Estate Investor

    • John Wiley & Sons Ltd.
    • August 2010
    Authors: George Dube, Steve Cohen

    This book begins and ends with the premise that buying property in Canada can be a smart, safe and successful way to invest your money. However, like most things in life, success requires hard work. You need to do your homework, understand what you are buying, and know the pros and cons of various decisions. Most importantly, you also need to know how to structure and maintain your investment. That’s where we come in.
