Kami Arnold's Summary

Arnolds for Awards, Inc provides personal service for every client whether they are a team Mom ordering soccer trophies to a commissioned award for a state Governor.

Arnolds for Awards, Inc. is a locally owned and operated awards and recognition business based out of Shingle Springs, CA that is celebrating over 40 years in business. Arnolds for Awards, Inc has earned a reputation as a superior partner to many individuals and companies by dedicating ourselves to the highest level of customer service.
We pride ourselves in offering quality products at competitive prices and always quick turn around.

When it comes to promotional products, we offer free consultation sessions for our customers to fully understand their marketing strategy and campaign, and then customize products to promote, reinforce and remind customers of the intended promotional message. By offering these specialized services to our clients, Arnolds for Awards, Inc consistently exceeds our customers’ expectations.

We provide our clients with creative solutions to their marketing problems, both domestically and overseas, whether it be for an employee incentive program, employee appreciation gifts, trade show giveaways, new service launches, new product launches, even those rush delivery situations.

Call us today.
