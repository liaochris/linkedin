Joon S. Han ✰ Strategist ✰'s Summary

For over 23 years (and counting) I've loved helping smart leaders like you lock in your strategic edge through competitive innovation, relevant technology, organizational efficacy and savvy marketing so hit us up for as many free resources as you can.

I have an extremely ginormous soft spot for social entrepreneurs (and similar folks passionate about conscious capitalism, triple bottom line, venture philanthropy, ecopreneurs and such) and consistently help out as many as I can as an advisor, champion and trainer and for a select few every year - also as a mentor, sponsor and investor.

I've served a wide gamut of organizations and businesses as they worked smart (and hard!) to make the proverbial jump from good to great and I always love me a good challenge.

Hey if you're a nice, decent, well-meaning, kind-hearted person striving to succeed in the mean, cut-throat, back-stabbing, dog-eat-dog world of business then go ahead and grab your copy of "Get Ahead by Giving Back" + come to our training programs + dig into all of our podcasts + connect with me here on this site... look forward to seeing you soon!

We ramped down new client work through Better San Diego (a Strategic Alignment Company) several years ago to focus on helping as many small businesses and non-profit organizations as possible regardless of their budgets (and now typically only take on 3-4 new clients a year). We do this through hands-on workshops offered via national organizations or our own private training events so stay on the lookout for my team visiting a town near you! I do a fair amount of Keynote speaking for this purpose as well (I've been a professional speaker since 1990).
