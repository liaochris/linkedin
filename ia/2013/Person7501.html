Andy Peel's Summary

Hello, hey, hi. I’m Andy, a creative copywriter who does more than just string a few sentences together. I can also make pretty pictures using Photoshop, lay things out in InDesign, start from scratch in Illustrator, use CMS and even code with HTML and CSS.

I’m digitally minded but crave the ‘big idea’, sound strategies and beautiful designs. I've created everything from TV campaigns to banners (and all the home builder brochures you can think of in between). I've won awards for apps and had my face (and website) appear in an advertising book too.

I’ve worked in some great regional places, like Leith and Big, but have now joined forces with Polly Skeats-Beswick to carve out a career in London.

In my spare time, I blog, run Ad Job Wall, which uses social networks to source junior, intern and placement roles in the creative industry, and I’m part of the Young Creative Council - who help students and grads take their first steps into advertising.

If there’s one thing I’m missing, it’s owning Bernard’s watch, but I’m hoping it’ll turn up on Kickstarter soon.

Oh, and I’d drop everything to work with Iain Tait, wherever he worked.
