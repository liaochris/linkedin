Jon Cassill's Summary

Creative and Design professional with a passion and love for great design and achieving results. An experienced team leader and seasoned professional with a deep skill set, I am always eager to learn, to share and contribute to the success of a team.

Designer

Tiffany & Co.

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; TIF; Luxury Goods & Jewelry industry

1990 – 2003 (13 years) New York, NY

I began freelancing for Tiffany & Co. in 1987 and joined the Company as a staff designer in 1990. As a Designer relatively new to Luxury products I was privileged to work with John Loring, several accomplished art directors, and the product development and merchandising groups in creating designs and producing artwork for products running through virtually the entire spectrum of Tiffany merchandise; ranging from china and crystal to silver, leather goods, fragrance and accessories, from black and white intricate rendering and technical documents to full color art, 3D visualization and model making.

Additional responsibilities included the evaluation, planning and implementation of new software and technology to benefit the Design department including administrative systems and training; and served in a department liaison role in Y2K awareness and transition. In 1998 I partnered with the Corporate Design Director and IT on an Intranet project; researching and creating a working internal website prototype focused on facilitating the interchange of client art for Custom Design projects.
