Chris Soong's Summary

As a member of Booz Allen Hamilton's Core Services & Information Services Leadership Teams, Chris is responsible for the delivery solutions supporting the firms Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence programs. His team provides the competitive business intelligence, reporting, operations and infrastructure necessary to successfully manage the fast growth and operations of a company with revenues of $6+Billion and 25K+ employees worldwide.

Chris has held previous Executive level positions at Sprint Nextel where he was the Director of Enterprise Applications Development. While at Sprint, Chris was responsible for the IT delivery solutions supporting Sprint’s $8 Billion 4G WiMAX initiative. Additionally, Chris was responsible for defining and delivering Sprint’s strategy for Enterprise Applications including Enterprise Applications Integration and the Enterprise Data Warehouse / Business Intelligence. Prior to Sprint, Chris held various positions within IT organizations, including serving on the integration team responsible for merging Sprint and Nextel, two Fortune 200 companies. He has held previous management positions at NeuStar Inc, Step 9 Software Corporation, and American Management Systems (now CGI). Chris is a recipient of the 2011 CIO Magazine’s ‘Ones to Watch’ award and the 2007 Outstanding 50 Asian Americans in Business Award. In 2012, Chris received Computerworld magazine's Editor's Choice award for Best Business Intelligence project.

In his free time, Chris volunteers with the National Capital Area chapter of the March of Dimes where he sits on the revenue committee. He is a team lead with the United Way Campaign, and works with the Food Allergy & Anaphylaxis Network (FAAN) and the Susan G. Komen Race for the Cure. Additionally, Chris served as a judge for the NPower Greater DC Region Technology Innovation Award (TIA).
