Jeff Veillette's Summary

I'm a hockey blogger who approaches the concept from a couple of different directions.

Currently, rather than sticking to a single venue, I try to contribute to as many different places as possible. Whether it's for myself, helping out a friend, or an opportunity to be gained, I'm always up for writing something new. Most notably, I write Toronto Maple Leafs and Toronto Marlies content for Nations Network.

I'm also capable of running my own sites and web operations. I started blogging on my own platforms at 14, and am capable at all levels, from creating content, to graphic design, web development, advertising sales, social media, promotion, and more.

If you're looking for someone to write hockey for you, or want to partner up with a presence in the Toronto hockey section of the web, do not hesitate to leave me a message.

Specialties: Social Media, Writing, Blogging, WordPress, Web Marketing, Twitter, Social Bookmarking
