Marc Seiler's Summary

I would like to start off by saying that software development is my passion. I love every aspect of it. From debugging an existing system to engineering a new one from scratch. I love figuring out how things work and making them do things no one thought they could ever do. Its not just programming I love either. I love setting up a server and getting apache or nginx running and seeing that “It works!” page because it usually means the beginning of great things :-)

I have spent my career mostly in web development. I felt from the beginning that it was the future. I felt it back when I first started experimenting with the url’s of a website. Changing the variables around to see what affects what. I felt so much power changing those simple variables and it has pushed me to do great things with several languages.

I have developed and implemented many different types of web based applications and websites. I have built everything from nationwide time clock systems to my best friends personal blog; including developing custom shopping carts as well as using what's already available. I have set up hundreds of osCommerce sites and just as many WordPress sites. I have created every kind of plugin you can think of for WordPress and even developed my own mini framework to make the task easier.

I have learned so much about real world programming over the years and I am very passionate about my work. I look forward to continuing to learn and grow for many years to come.
