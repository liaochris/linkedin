Chel Hamilton's Summary

When it comes to using hypnosis people are usually pretty skeptical. I get it. I used to be a total skeptic about hypnosis too. Until I saw hypnotherapy in action for myself. And I found myself amazed by powerfully and quickly hypnotherapy helped people overcome issues.

Then I was like "Wow! Why aren't more people doing this!?!?" Enter the story of Freud, and hypnosis: http://chelhamilton.com/how-hypnosis-got-bad-reputation/

Probably the thing that I hear most from people when they find out I'm a hypnotherapist is "That's fascinating. I always wondered about hypnosis, but honestly I doubt I can be hypnotized."

Of course the fact that they say this while now avoiding all eye contact with me always makes me smile!

Did you know scientific studies have shown between 90-95% of the population can go into hypnosis? And that contrary to popular myths the smarter you are the MORE hypnotizable you are?

As a "whole mind" expert in dealing with anxiety, addictions, insomnia, fears, stress, and self-sabotaging mental programing I see mostly adults and teens, but often see clients even as young as 8. During sessions we may utilize any combination of Direct Suggestion Hypnosis, Guided Imagery Hypnosis, Ericksonian Hypnosis, and Neuro Linguistic Programing, along with Cognitive and Strategic Intervention techniques.

To learn more about if we might be a great fit feel free to visit me at www.ChelHamilton.com.
