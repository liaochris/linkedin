Dan Pellegrino's Summary

Dan has over 14 years of experience in corporate strategy coupled with small business acquisitions and private equity investments. Dan currently specializes in business acquisition and divestiture of small businesses between $2M and $20M, where he has a deep expertise in business valuation, decision analysis and risk evaluation, including deal structuring for business of all sizes. He has the unique distinction to have worked on deals ranging in deal size from $68 Billion (Pfizer’s acquisition of Wyeth) to deals as small as $100K.

Dan also has a breadth of knowledge across scores of industries with his deepest expertise in Manufacturing, Distribution, Life Sciences, Financial Services, Education, Health and Fitness, and Online Properties. Dan is also founder and CEO of South Peak Capital which provides a unique tool called LIBSIS (Life Insurance Backed Structured Installment Sale) that allows retiring business sellers to receive more cash from the sale of their business over time while also minimizing risk by leveraging a key man life insurance policy.

Prior to his focus on small business M&A, Dan spent 8 years in management consulting specializing in corporate strategy with Deloitte Consulting and SDG Management Consulting where he focused on pure business strategy.

Dan's creative thinking is seen in the proprietary financial product called LIBSIS (Life Insurance Backed Structured Installment Sale) which he developed for South Peak Capital.

Specialties: Dan has a deep understanding of business needs due to his diverse background, including, finance, operations and marketing, where his past clients have typically been large multi-national corporations as well as small to mid-size enterprises.
