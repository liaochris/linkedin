Louise Masin Sattler's Summary

I always have considered my professional profile as an educator- first and foremost. Whether it is to help children and teachers in my role as a School Psychologist, answer parent questions as a contributor to Education.com, as a disaster preparedness trainer for First Responders with the objective to help keep people with special needs safe, or as media expert to help give organizations a 'voice'. I am fortunate to have a "knack" for languages, thus I am multilingual.

My professional goals are simple.. to help others. I enjoy challenges and tackling new opportunities which is why I am the consummate "multi-tasker".

As an entrepreneur, my company, SIGNING FAMILIES is geared at helping bridge communication bridges - one SIGN at a time. I consider working with children and adults with special learning or sensory challenges a privilege.

411 VOICES is my passion- working with a collective of women who are focused and dedicated to helping our communities through traditional media and social-digital media. We are visionaries who have the voice to reach millions.
