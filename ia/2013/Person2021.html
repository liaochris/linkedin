Adrian Parr's Summary

I am an experienced multimedia professional with a strong mix of technical and managerial skills. I have more than thirteen years’ experience working in various busy London full-service digital media agencies. I consider myself an all-round digital media expert with a focus on HTML5, CSS3, Canvas, jQuery, JavaScript, ActionScript 3, Adobe Flash and related technologies. I am a hard worker who is very easy to get along with.

I have worked on interactive projects across a wide range of sectors, including education, government, television and advertising, receiving more than 20 major industry awards. I am passionate about the industry and active within the online community, and always ensure my knowledge is up-to-date.

Specialties: HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, JavaScript, Responsive design, Adobe Flash, Adobe Flash Builder, FlashDevelop, FDT, ActionScript 3.0, Adobe AIR for Desktop and Mobile, Adobe Edge Animate, EaselJS, Canvas, Adobe Director, educational activities, management skills, project scoping, technical writing, scheduling, recruitment, online games, cross-platform CD-ROMs, advertising banners, marketing microsites, video and audio compression and delivery, working with designers and backend programmers, SCORM.

I have a keen interest in a wide range of creative technological solutions including Processing, OpenFrameworks, Cinder, Arduino, 3D Projection Mapping and reactive installations.
