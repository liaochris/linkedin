Görkem Öğüt's Projects

  • AGRINET - Golestan Agricultural Inventory and Web Based GIS Project

    • April 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Görkem Öğüt, Musa Semerci, Cem Eraslan, Deniz Şanan, Burak Karip, Sinem Demir

    Agricultural products play an important role in Iran like whole worldwide. The government gives special importance to agricultural sector. Golestan is one of the greenest province that located in the northeast of Iran. Proportional to the area, Golestan province has the first position in Iran on the agricultural value scale. Administrators of agricultural sectors are looking for ways to promote from traditional cultivation to advanced cultivation using any related new technology. Land administration therefore cultivation management has a priority in Golestan province.
    From software aspect, an extensive study has been realized and finally Netcad engineering solutions in agricultural management and lands administration has been preferred by organisation.
    Netcad and Netcad’s Iranian Exclusive Representative Parsineh Tarhe Atieh (PTA) have completed first web-based agricultural GIS system project successfully in the Golestan province. In this project, around 140.000 parcels were surveyed, digitized, edited, and entered to the geodatabase. With this project, you can easily find not only where is he/ she is living, where is his/her farms, gardens, fish farms, cattle and poultry but also products and their amounts from the farmer name or national id number, This project is equipped with Netcad core module, Netcad 5.2 GIS. NetGIS/Server, NetSurf and Netcad/RS.
    Data acquisition, processing and management and training the managers, engineers, and Jahad Agriculture Organization experts of Golestan province has been carried out correspondingly The client is highly satisfied with Netcad solutions, especially having the CAD, GIS, and RS in the same environment. Now they admire Netcad as the first company rescuing them from different and heterogeneous software packages and providing a comprehensive agricultural information system.

  • OSTANNET - Fars Province Web Based GIS Portal Project

    • May 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Görkem Öğüt, Deniz Şanan, Musa Semerci, Cem Eraslan, Burak Karip, Sinem Demir

    Iran consists of 31 provinces. Each state has own management. Center (Tehran) government (ministries, general directorates, etc.) has offices in each state in parallel with the central structures of state. Each of these governmental bodies are producing their own data and there is a huge gap between the data production methodologies of each. It has been aimed to construct a unified structure by the end of this project.

    In the scope of the project 19 different data source coming from the different provincial government offices for different principles including geology, geography, climatology etc. have been collected. All the data structured have been modified according to the data enrollment methodologies. Geo database structure has been design and a proper enrollment methodology has been created for the sustainability.

    After creating the methodologies and structures, server installation has been accomplished. Data transfer, topological editing, duplicate data correction operations will be accomplished by the and of the project in accordance with the localization. Training will be handled at the last 2 weeks.
