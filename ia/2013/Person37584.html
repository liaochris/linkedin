Ben Drillings' Summary

Currently I am the director of The American Israeli Medical Association; which is an organization of healthcare providers that share common interest to network; help the community and exchange information between healthcare providers in United States and Israel. In addition, I am the director of A2Z Health Wellness Centers as well as managing doctor at Ben Drilling's Chiropractic Centers in Thousand Oaks and Reseda.

Ben Drillings' Experience

Director

American Isreali Medical Association

2008 – Present (5 years)

The American Israeli Medical Association (AIMA) is a networking organization of health care providers that helps to exchange information between the health care providers in Israel and the United States. We provide helpful health resources to the community, as well as promoting healthy lifestyle and medical options to the community.

Director

A2Z Health Expo

December 2008 – Present (4 years 8 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

The A2Z Health Expo is an all-inclusive training and networking event that will be conducted twice a year in Southern California. Catering to Health and Wellness providers from all backgrounds and experiences, our mission is simple:

Help The Integrated Clinic of the 21st Century Succeed.

Our emphasis is upon sharing news and updates concerning regulations within our industry, innovations that can help you and your business grow and techniques that you can learn and apply immediately. Keynote speakers and a plethora of training workshops will fit the needs of everyone.

Additionally, our 1 day Super Events allow ample time and opportunity to network with others from the Health and Wellness industry. Product Showcases and Job Fair information will also be available at each event.
