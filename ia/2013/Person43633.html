Elizabeth Weaver Engel's Summary

I have 15 years experience in association management. Although my primary focus has been in membership, marketing, and communications, my experience has been wide-ranging, including corporate sponsorship and fundraising, technology planning and implementation, social media and Internet strategy, budgeting, volunteer management, publications, and governance.

Prior to launching Spark, I consulted in online campaigns and marketing and Internet and social media strategy for Beaconfire Consulting, and in a wide range of subject areas in association management in the not-for-profit consulting practice at RSM McGladrey, Inc. I've also served in a variety of positions for associations, including director of member services and technology, director of marketing and sponsorship, vice president of marketing, and acting CEO.

Engagements have included strategy consulting; research; recommendations reporting and presentations; campaign planning and execution; development of methodologies; staff, volunteer leader, member and prospect interviews; and project management for: marketing analysis and strategy; member surveys and analysis; Internet and social media strategy; fundraising campaign concept, production, and execution; a variety of web projects; departmental analysis (external relations, advocacy, IT, etc.); and organizational benchmarking.

I combine a focus on finding and implementing unconventional solutions with a broad understanding of the association sphere. Throughout my career, I have excelled at increasing revenue, public presence, and member satisfaction while decreasing costs through a focus on the efficient and effective use of staff and technology to serve organizational goals and constituents, and the public.
