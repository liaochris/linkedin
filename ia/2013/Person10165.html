Andrew Satter's Summary

Andrew Satter is a pioneering video storyteller based in Washington, DC. His mission is to produce visual stories that inform, engage and entertain. Satter's work has won numerous journalistic awards and his short films have appeared in festivals across the United States.

Satter is experienced in all aspects of video production and project management. As the Senior Video Producer for the Center for American Progress, he has helped establish the Center as a leader in advocacy documentary and explanatory video storytelling. He shoots in multiple formats, including HDSLR, edits in Final Cut Pro and Adobe Premiere, and works independently and on teams to conceptualize, research, script, edit and track original video projects.

Previously Satter worked for the Prague Post, the Arizona Daily Star and CQ Roll Call. He teaches the course "Mastering Video Production Basics" for the Compelling Image.

Please contact Andrew for inquiries about freelance assignments, speaking engagements or just to say hello.
