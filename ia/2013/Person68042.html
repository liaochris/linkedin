Jon Ostler's Summary

Jon heads up Beyond Internationals (ASX:BYI) Digital Marketing Division BeyondD. BeyondD provides digital marketing services and media to a range of clients from entrepreneurs and start-ups to global corporations. BeyondD helps companies establish and execute digitally led sales and marketing strategies and has a strong client list across financial services, retail and travel.

Jon has founded, operated and sold a number of successful web related businesses including a web design business, an online restaurant guide, performance ad network and most notably First Rate, a leading specialist in search and performance marketing.

Jon sold First Rate to Q Ltd an ASX listed digital marketing group in 2007 and ended up running the group in 2011 as Group CEO before selling most of the groups operations to Beyond in 2012.

Jon is originally from the UK where he graduated top of his class with a 1st class degree in Communication Engineering (BEng) before taking up a technical management role with JARIC a military intelligence department of the UK Ministry of Defence.

Specialties

Start-ups, Public Speaking, Business and Digital Marketing Strategy, Company Mergers and Acquisitions.
