Julia Goldberg's Experience

Morning Talk Show Host

Hutton Broadcasting

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Broadcast Media industry

April 2013 – Present (10 months) Santa Fe, New Mexico Area

Host of the Julia Goldberg Morning Show, 8-10 a.m., Monday-Friday on 101.5 FM, The Voice of Santa Fe. The show features news and opinions, with daily guests and call-in commentary from the Santa Fe community.

Contributing Faculty

Santa Fe University of Art and Design

Educational Institution; 51-200 employees; Education Management industry

August 2011 – Present (2 years 6 months) Santa Fe, New Mexico Area

I teach classes in both the Liberal Arts and Creative Writing departments. These classes have included writing roundtables focused on the history of social movements; a seminar on dystopia in literature; technique courses on fiction and non-fiction writing. I currently team-teach journalism sections for writing and photography students, which include an online publishing component for student work. Check out their stories and photography at: journalismcollaborations.wordpress.com Starting in Fall, 2013, student writing will be published regularly on jackalopemagazine.com, a website I will design and oversee.

Santa Fe Community College

For several years, I have taught the spring Intermediate Fiction course at SFCC, which focuses on the elements of fiction writing, and provides workshop opportunities for students. I also have taught English 225—Composition and Rhetoric—at the school.
