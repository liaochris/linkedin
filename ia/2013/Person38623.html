Duane Murphy's Summary

Duane Murphy is the president of Duane Murphy & Associates, a leading business consulting company specializing in steel tubulars and founded The OCTG Situation Report in 1986.

During his 30-year corporate business career, Mr. Murphy has had operational experience in procurement, planning, and senior marketing capacities with several major FORTUNE 500 companies. Mr. Murphy's oilfield-related operational experience included senior management responsibilities with major ERW and Seamless Mills.

Mr. Murphy also was former president of The American Marketing Association, Co-chairman of The Council of The Machinery & Allied Products Institute, Chairman of The Specialty Tubular Producers Committee of The American Iron & Steel Institute and served on the API Technical Products Committee.

The OCTG Situation Report is the one of the leading authorities devoted to covering the North American Oil Country Tubular Goods market. For 26 years the subscriber-only monthly publication has thoroughly examined the OCTG sector providing a summary of current events and forecasted changes combined with in depth market research and unmatched critical analysis crucial to leading industry decision makers.

Subscribers receive timely and vital analysis: information that is geared to help you make calculated and concise decisions that invariably affects your company's products, production and bottom line.

We invite you to peruse our site at http://www.octgsituationreport.com to learn more about how our Report can work for you and your company.
