Alex Isaly's Experience

Lead Trainer/Spokesperson for KettleWorX

KettleWorX

May 2013 – Present (8 months)

Pivotal 5, a proven leader in mass-market retail fitness and wellness solutions, partnered with Alex Isaly as the new lead trainer for their popular globally known KettleWorX training program. Isaly brings more than 15 years of industry experience to the KettleWorX fitness brand. Alex will be the face of the KettleWorX revitalized home-exercise program, while also supporting its group-exercise platform for commercial fitness facilities.

Isaly Fitness

CEO of Isaly Fitness Innovations, Inc.

May 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months) Orange County, California Area

As Founder and CEO of the Isaly Fitness Innovations, Inc., Alex spends his time consulting and training on wellness programs to fitness professionals and enthusiasts. Alex is also the Founding Creator of the group exercise program called R.I.P.P.E.D. – The One Stop Body Shock. Alex is now the face of the KettleWorX revitalized home workout program, and will be supporting the KettleWorX group exercise program for commercial fitness facilities.

He has extensive experience in the fitness industry as a Presenter, Master Trainer, personal trainer and an endurance athlete. Alex has an incredible talent for motivating his clients with his passion and knowledge of fitness. As the fitness industry evolves, so does Alex; he is consistently expanding his knowledge of the subject and innovating new creative systems because of the industry. He has been featured in several national, local and industry publications, as well as, broadcast networks and fitness infomercials (i.e., SHAPE magazine, Muscle & Fitness magazine, UFC Gym, Body by Jake, CNN, FOX, NBC and ABC).

Some of his other projects include: working with Andre Agassi, Gil Reyes and Dan O’Brien with BILT by Agassi and Reyes, developing educational programs; a fitness/nutrition expert for Monavie on the RVLution (Ditch the Diet); Ambassador to NIKE for the Kinect Trainer; and fitness/nutrition expert for Proactive Health Solutions.

With over 35% of U.S. adults and 15% of children/adolescence, national obesity is at all time high and on the rise. As a father of 2 girls, and through his own personal struggles of staying fit, he is passionate about educating and creating programs to change people’s lives to live a full and healthy lifestyle. Alex believes that one of the greatest gifts in life is the ability to help and mentor others. He feels blessed to be in an environment where health and wellness is the focus.

Founding Creator of R.I.P.P.E.D. - The One Stop Body Shock

B3 Fit, Inc.

January 2008 – May 2013 (5 years 5 months) Orange County, California Area

In January 2008, Alex founded and created the group exercise program called R.I.P.P.E.D. - The One Stop Body Shock. He took a fitness concept and developed it into one of the fastest growing fitness programs in the industry. In just a couple of years after the launch of the program it went viral and is now being taught throughout the country in all 50 states, including Germany, Canada and Mexico. Over 5,000 fitness professionals and enthusiasts have become trained and certified to teach the program.

R.I.P.P.E.D. is a sports-based, fun, high energy exercise format that caters to all types of fitness levels from beginner to elite by encompassing several components of fitness….Resistance, Intervals, Power, Plyometrics, Endurance and Diet/Nutrition.
