Michelle Pajak-Reynolds, BFA, MBA's Summary

As the CEO and Creative Director at Michelle Pajak-Reynolds Studios, I leverage my creativity and business skills to create innovative couture jewelry pieces for my clients. My distinctive collections have been featured on the runway at NY Fashion Week along with numerous exhibitions, publications and red carpet events. I’ve dressed celebrities including Sophia Bush (Brooke Davis in One Tree Hill), Nona Gaye (Zee in The Matrix movies), Debora Lyra (Miss Brazil 2010), Dina Manzo (HGTV's Dina's Party), Lisa Ryan (Gratitude Expert in The Keeper of the Keys), and Jillian Barberie (FOX NFL Sunday). My long-term plans include designs for film and theatrical productions.

My unique approach to design and materials and business skills enables me to excell at collarborating with photographers, videographers, stylists, and event producers in determining artistic project strategies. I am also focused on forming alliances with retail partners, publications, writers, and industry professionals specializing in the self-purchasing female luxury goods consumer.

Awards: Distinguished Sales and Marketing Award 2013, Sales & Marketing Executives International
COSE Arts Business Challenge Award 2012 (2nd Place)
Old Becomes News Design Challenge 2010 (Winner), Manufacturing Jewelers & Suppliers of America

Specialties: Intense passion for entrepreneurship and all things luxurious and beautiful including jewelry, fashion, art, design and fine food. Enthusiatic observer and student of new business development strategies and the interests and needs of women and what influences their decisions.
