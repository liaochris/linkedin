Adrian Carrasquillo's Summary

I am a Breaking News Reporter at BuzzFeed, the leading social news organization online. The goal of our team is to figure out how breaking news can and should work on the social web.

Previously, I was the Manager of Social Media and an editor and producer for NBC Latino. As part of the founding team, we worked to fundamentally change what people understood as Hispanic news and elevated the conversation to better reflect the nuance, richness and depth of the U.S. Latino experience. I crafted and implemented a unique social media strategy that helped make us the most engaging offering in the Latino news space through a focus on zeroing in on your passions and evoking emotion on social. Whether our stories outraged you, inspired you or made you laugh, we were succeeding if you cared enough to engage with the content. Part of my role was also to help the editorial team build up their individual social media presence because of the importance of connecting with readers as people and not just a big, faceless brand. I also reported and wrote original multimedia stories and served as the assignment editor on weekends.

Previously, I was a Producer/Writer for Fox News Latino. I produced, wrote stories and managed the homepage. From day one I created and implemented social media strategy.

Before that I was a Web Producer at MyFoxNY.com, the online arm of Fox 5 News in New York.

When I was still in college, I was a paid intern/web editor for Blogform Media and I was involved in the launch of TubetheVote.com, a first of its kind site that contextualized the historic 2008 election through social media and YouTube. I worked on the project as it went from conception to reality, writing and serving as an editor for college classes that submitted content for the site.

I received an online journalism concentration at Stony Brook University where I learned what it means to be a journalist from world class faculty.

Specialties

Producer, editor, reporter, researcher, strong writer with understanding and knowledge of social media and social networking trends and applications.

Avid, Final Cut Pro, video production, web content tools, content management systems, basic html, photoshop and more.
