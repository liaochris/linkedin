JOHN ZGOURIDES's Summary

I proudly stand to fight for those who cannot fight alone. My track record shows many successes representing individuals and small businesses in many types of disputes, ranging from negotiated resolutions to jury verdicts and appeals. As an attorney, counselor, and mediator, I have a broad spectrum of tools for helping people in legal crises (see http://www.zgourides.com). My clients honor me with referrals, and those new clients honor me with yet further referrals.

Since 2002, my peers have honored me with the highest of Peer Review Ratings: "AV Preeminent, 5.0 out of 5.0" in the Martindale-Hubbell Legal Directory (see http://www.martindale.com and http://www,lawyers.com).

I am Board Certified in Personal Injury Trial Law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization (see http://www.martindale.com and http://www,tbls.org).

I am licensed by the Supreme Court of Texas. I am also licensed in many federal courts, including: U.S. Court of Appeals (5th Circuit); U.S.District Court, Texas (statewide - all divisions); U.S.District Court, Colorado (statewide); U.S.District Court, Arkansas (statewide - all divisions); U.S.District Court, Nebraska (statewide).

Specialties: Board Certified -- Personal Injury Trial Law, Texas Board of Legal Specialization.

JOHN ZGOURIDES's Experience

Civil Trial Lawyer in The Woodlands, Texas

John Zgourides, Attorney at Law

2013 – Present (less than a year) The Woodlands, Texas

John excels as a civil trial lawyer, representing individuals and businesses in a wide range of matters.

John represents and counsels people and businesses concerning personal injury matters. After 8 years defending individuals and businesses as an insurance defense lawyer, he frequently represents individuals and families in personal injury cases. John also defends individuals and businesses in personal injury cases when insurance coverage is contested or lacking. In addition to defending these types of matters, John also works as "coverage counsel," advising individuals and businesses on how to interact with their liability insurers and retained defense counsel.

John represents individuals and businesses concerning real estate matters, advocating by negotiation, mediation, and jury trial.

John prosecutes and defends cases under the Texas Deceptive Trade Practices-Consumer Protection Act and the Texas Property Code.

John represents and counsels people concerning probate matters, including wills, powers of attorney, living wills, and guardianships. He drafts these documents and represents people in court in these matters.

John represents and counsels people concerning divorce, child support, and child custody.

John has served as the mediator in hundreds of civil disputes, and served in many more as the advocate for a litigant. John trained with the American Arbitration Association, and has served as an arbitrator, and much more frequently served as an advocate for a litigant in arbitrations.

Senior Trial Lawyer

Sammons & Berry, PC

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Law Practice industry

2008 – 2013 (5 years)

John's practice at Sammons & Berry, PC concentrated on recovering damages for those with catastrophic losses. John handled a large number of Federal Employers Liability Act (FELA) cases for railroaders around the country. John has served as lead counsel in many cases throughout Texas. He has represented the interests of men and women in federal courts in Arkansas, California, Colorado, Louisiana, Kansas, Nebraska, New Mexico, and Wyoming. John left this firm in good standing and now accepts referrals from Sammons & Berry, PC.

Managing Attorney

Zgourides & Arnold

2003 – 2008 (5 years)

In 2003, John went to work trying cases for Grover & Bruce, PC. The firm morphed into Pitts, Williford & Zgourides, and then to Zgourides & Arnold. In 2008, while retaining its name, the firm became the in-house litigation department for American Century Casualty Company, a Texas insurance company with policyholders in 13 states. During these years, John's practice was in insurance defense trial law, defending people covered by liability insurance in personal injury lawsuits filed against them. John also represented ACCC in direct actions and in commercial disputes.

Trial Attorney

Archer, Shrode & Soule

2000 – 2003 (3 years)

John defended insureds of the Farmers Group of Insurance Companies in personal injury lawsuits. John also represented the Farmers Group of Insurance Companies in direct actions.

Managing Attorney for Litigation Department

1999 – 2000 (1 year)

Richard Pena is a former president of the State Bar of Texas. John was honored to lead this personal injury firm's litigation department. The main office is in Austin and satellite offices are in the Valley.

Trial Attorney and Mediator

Zgourides & Zgourides

John worked with his father, Gus Zgourides, during his formative years. John's father has been licensed in Texas since 1955. Working with his father, John tried and mediated cases for almost 10 years.
