Dan Eggenschwiler's Summary

First things first, here's a little help with the last name. Eggenschwiler. Pronounced "Egg-en-sh-why-ler". Now that that is out of the way...

I am a freelance graphic designer and photographer with a passion for print work and branding. I have been doing freelance work for almost six years. I love working with clients to develop and build an identity from the ground up, and integrating that throughout all levels of the company. I am fully proficient and comfortable with the Adobe Creative Suite as well as Microsoft Office, have a good understanding of business and the non-creative side of design, and will always give my all to find the best solution for each project and need.

I am also passionate about helping the underprivileged around the world and love to use my design and photography abilities for greater causes than just the consumer world. Any work for a Non-Profit is always welcomed!
