Allen Hurst's Summary

My career began when I graduated with a Computer Science degree from Texas A&M (Whoop!). I’ve spent my career consulting with clients, first as a Java and .NET developer, and most recently as an Agile coach and project manager. In 2007 I joined Improving Enterprises and led projects in our College Station office until 2012, when I relocated to lead our consulting business in Houston.

I’ve enjoyed the experience of learning, working, leading, and mentoring in a wide variety of environments. Along the way I have strived to recreate the positive experiences and learn from the negative ones. I’ve found a fondness for helping our customers improve through agile values and practices, rapid delivery of quality software and, most of all, working with our customers to identify ways that technology can make their business more effective.

When I’m not delivering for clients, I enjoy getting the word out about Improving, making new friends around town, and speaking at user groups and conferences. I’ve spoken at a variety of conferences including Agile 2012, Houston TechFest, and AgileDotNet. I’m also the leader of the Aggieland .NET Users Group and a producer of Improving Podcasts. To learn more about me, visit my personal website at ahurst.com.

Specialties: Agile development, Scrum, XP, .NET development
