Joni Snowden's Summary

A big believer of enjoying what you do, I believe you can be a professional on the job while having fun at the same time.

When the doors first opened in 2007, A Novel Space provided executive assistant and human resources support to companies in the entertainment industry.

In 2009, looking for a way to blend the corporate and administrative knowledge with the love of books, writing and publishing, A Novel Space opened the door to working with writers by becoming a certified Virtual Author's Assistant.

In late 2011, services expanded again with the addition of a Virtual Speaker's Assistant certification to the list of credentials.

Today, A Novel Space provides publishing and speaker support services ranging from administrative to marketing for fiction & non-fiction authors and speakers.

Specialties: Fact checking and other researching, Research potential publishers, Prepare the manuscript to publishers requirements, Coordinate promotional activities, Organize live book events, Create virtual book launches and book tours
