Alexandra Conway's Summary

With the creative eye and attention to detail of a traditional artist she finesses her way through edits and color grades with the delicacy of a paintbrush on canvas - with the foundation of double degrees in Film and Fine Arts from UC Santa Cruz. Her love for the arts began with painting and dance for personal expression, and evolved into colored pixels and motion on a big screen for large audiences.

While building a career in film and video in the Bay Area she has worked on everything from documentaries, indie films, music videos, commercials to corporate productions and with CLAi she is a great asset in the crafting of programs from concept through final output. Her experience extends to various on set roles, art direction, pre-production and small-scale producing, but her passion lies in the challenges of the edit - where it all comes together!

Alexandra specializes in film & video editing, as well as color grading & correction. At CLAi, Alexandra can also be found handling our graphic and web design, helping concept and manage projects, all while ruling the office with the subtlety of an iron fist.
