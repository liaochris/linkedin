William McDonald's Summary

Currently a consultant in the satellite communications industry, leveraging extensive experience. Worked for over 35 years in the industry with SES Americom (formerly RCA Americom and GE Americom). Began as earth station technician, served as a station manager and a regional manager, followed by 7 years as principal systems engineer, with fequent experience managing integration projects. Education includes AS in Electronic Engineering and BBA from University of Guam, and MBA from University of La Verne in California.

Goal is to take on challenging projects and improve the client's bottom line by completing projects ahead of time and within budget, getting it right the first time. Customer satisfaction is the primary focus.

Specialties: Engineering, integrating, and testing complete satellite earth station communications systems including transmit and receive RF, IF, and baseband systems, digital commmunications systems, and monitoring systems. Project budgeting, satellite link analysis, technical documentation, program management, and industry research.
