Jacqueline C Agentis' Summary

Fine Portrait, Lifestyle, Editorial Photographer/Artist located in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania USA.

An interest in photography existed for many years before receiving a BFA from Kutztown University; and then working as a staff photographer for AT&T Bell Labs. Holding several corporate jobs in years since, I have never stopped shooting. These days, photography is finally full time.

My work has appeared in a number publications, including the NY Times. I have received many awards, and have been involved with both solo and group exhibitions. My commercial and Portrait client list includes local businesses as well as those from Florida, Colorado, Kentucky and New York, London, and Manchester UK.

LIke many before me, I was mesmerized by the exquisite black & white images of Ansel Adams, then Henri Cartier-Bresson, Paul Strand, and Margaret Bourke-White; my creative interests evolved from drawing and printmaking (etching) to Photography, the love of my life.

Finding the extraordinary in the ordinary is my photographic aspiration. Whether capturing the beauty of color, texture, and architectural features in the reminisce of a factory; or the relationship of people with their environment and each other in a portrait or street photography...the extraordinary and meaningful is there to be found.

If we are open, there are many signs of the progress of humanity; the good, the bad, and the ugly. As we move two steps forward and one step back hopefully we learn and evolve. Without learning, we cannot move forward.

Specialties: Lifestyle photography for commercial and stock. Headshots for Model portfolios. Aerial photography via Helicopter. Portraits in a natural environment/pose, as organic as possible. Digital photography is now my preferred medium; however, I do use my Hasselblad film camera on occasion.
