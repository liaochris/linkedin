Lars Lindgren's Summary

13 years of experience working internationally with business strategy implementation through HR initiatives and Learning solutions, including on-line learning and technology based solutions.

Educational Background: Natural Sciences program with focus on Environment and Health. Master of Social Science in Human Resource Management majoring in Organizational Development and Pedagogy. Studied Business Administration and Computers Science in the US.
Certified ICC coach.

Passionate about working in the converging areas of Business, IT & Learning in a way that benefits the triple bottom line of people, profit & our planet.

Currently working with HR strategy and Organizational improvements at the Stockholm City Environment and Health Administration to help make the greatest city in the world even greater.

Previous experience include being CEO of one of the largest eLearning companies in Sweden, Minerva Corporate Learning Partner, through a transition period of considerable growth and profit improvements.

From start-up to global managing director at the BTS web-business unit, focused on innovating how companies change, learn and improve with the help of IT solutions.

Spent 2 years in San Francisco to drive the on-line business in the US. Responsible for integrating an acquired team in Philadelphia into BTS Global on-line Business Group.

Worked as responsible for evolution of overall technical infrastructure and platforms and later for establishing a production team in India.

Have Project Management experiences from many international development projects, done both virtually and on location in the US and Europe.

Guest lecturer at the Stockholm School of Economics for MBA students and final year students at the Business Administration program. Running Business Acumen classroom workshop sessions.

Specialties: Strategy Implementation through Learning & Development, Talent Management, Coaching, Internet Technology, Sustainable Development.
