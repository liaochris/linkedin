Mike Langford's Summary

I have a rare combination of skills and experience that bridge financial services and social business.

Financial Services:

As a financial services industry veteran with over 17 years of experience in roles spanning customer service, finance and investment advice and management. I have worked at world-class firms such as Fidelity Investments, State Street Corporation, The Pioneer Group and BFDS.

I have worked in both retail and institutional businesses and have a deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities that exist at their intersection.

I spent significant time in channel management for phones operations and web development at Fidelity where I developed a keen appreciation for the intricacies customer service and sales in a regulated environment.

I have also run a successful independent Registered Investment Advisory practice for years where I became an early adopter of social media tools for growing and managing my book of business.

Social Business

I have been a leader in the social media industry for years.

As an entrepreneur and investor I created Tweetworks, a conversational platform for Twitter users.

I also used social media tools such as LinkedIn, Twitter and Facebook to create a robust business network.

I am an active content creator with an passion for web video. I am the co-creator and co-host of NomX3 a popular web series about social media and technology that takes place over lunch.

Specialties

Video Production, Social Media, Social Business, Strategy, Speaking, Entrepreneurship, Finance, Investing, Investment
