Brian Alcorn's Summary

I’ve been leading digital design initiatives for 14 years, helping set the strategic vision and establish the design foundation for dozens of consumer web sites, mobile, tablet and web applications, knowledge management systems, social media tools, corporate intranets and extranets, and e-learning experiences.

I specialize in getting things done inside large, complex organizations, with particular expertise and interest in serving financial services, health care, and media companies.

I do this work because I believe technology should be in the service of people, not the other way around.

Above all, I pride myself on delivering outstanding client service. Happy clients who feel that I helped them and their team succeed are how I measure my own success.
