Mark Gebbie's Summary

Gebbie Press publishes the All-In-One Media Directory, listing all US Radio/TV stations, Daily/Weekly newspapers, Black/Hispanic media and Trade/Consumer magazines.

We've been in business since the year 1955 and are known for quality directories at affordable prices. The All-In-One Media Directory is now in its 38th annual edition. We offer our media contact data in a print version, on CD and also as PRPro, our online press release service.

Founded by my father Con Gebbie in 1955, I took over the business in 1972 when he passed away and have been editor and publisher since then. As chief cook and bottle washer, I've done all our marketing, advertising, direct mail, research, internet, production, graphics, copywriting, publicity and accounting.

Gebbie Press Media Contact Directory

January 1979 – Present (34 years 10 months)

Just ONE well placed press release can generate tremendous interest in your products or services. Every day editors set aside space for press releases about products, services, events and news - why not yours? We offer three ways for you to contact the media.

PRPro Online Press Release Service Creating a media list and sending press releases has never been quicker, easier or more affordable.

The All-In-One Media Directory (Print version) is a compact yet comprehensive 8 1/2 x 11 reference work - media contact information for more than 24,000 media outlets in one place.

Comma-delimited and Excel media lists. Our media contact data easily imports into your database, mailing list, contact manager, spreadsheet or other program, ready to go to work for you.

A name you know. Gebbie Press has served the public relations field since 1955. Founded in New York City by Con Gebbie, former PR man for Pan Am and Shell Oil, his first offering was the "Gebbie Press House Magazine Directory", listing all company publications. In 1970 the first "All-In-One" Directory was published; it has been published every year since that time. Gebbie Press was the first publisher to provide media lists and media contact databases in electronic format.
