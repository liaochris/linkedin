Jon Byrd's Summary

I have a unique ability to understand the flow of systems, whether those systems are electrical, plumbing, mathematics or software. This ability allows me to understand and architect complex software applications.

The trick to developing large software applications is quite simple actually, you must first acknowledge that large systems are simply made up of smaller systems, like building blocks. Then understand that it's more difficult and beneficial to make something simple vs complex.

Compile this with an organized and teaching personality and you'll find a well documented and easy to follow code base for other software programmers to follow and maintain. I'm well versed in Object Oriented Programming techniques and well adept to a clean user interface.

My passion for coding began more then ten years ago. I began studying C++ in school then Perl and ASP in my spare time. Although I no longer program in any of those languages today. I've taught myself PHP, which is the base language that I practice today.

As an internet focused software engineer, I've developed a variety of intranet and point of sale systems. My work has allowed my customers to not only save hundreds of thousands on software maintenance and business efficiencies, but I've also allowed their businesses to take on a larger customer base without added costs.

Specialties

My specialties are focused on developing website systems that are both used by internal staff from and administrative standpoint, as well as, your customer base from a presentation and sales perspective. The results are full featured software applications that allow their companies to manage the fine details in fast paced environments.

My skills do not stop at programming, I also specialize in marketing and sales. My focus is to make your company more efficient in every area.
