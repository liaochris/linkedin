Dan Embree's Summary

Dan Embree, M.Ed., NCC is a Systems of Care consultant and Wraparound Trainer for private organizations and public systems seeking to implement evidenced-based and promising approaches to serving youth and families. He focuses on strengths-based, family-driven, culturally competent, team-based work at the system and practice level.
