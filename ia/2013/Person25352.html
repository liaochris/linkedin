Clare Rice Dreyer - Yes-I want MORE Success Coach, NLP Coach's Summary

Clare is the founder of her signature system "YES! I want MORE!" created in 2013, Starting over Success Strategies established in 2012 & EVOLVE3 Consulting, LLC originally established in 1992.

Clare Dreyer is a Success Coach & a gifted inspirational speaker. She is a contributing author of the #1 bestselling book, “Get Your Woman On.” She is also a Certified NLP Coach & Certified Dream Coach®. Clare's ideal clients are choosing to Maximize their Opportunities, want to deepen their Relationships and Experience all that life has to offer them! Clare helps her clients develop a practical approach to creating the next steps that will put them on their pathway to having and giving MORE!

This work is important to Clare because she realized that she even though she has lived a wonderful life (with some bumps in the road), she still has deep and meaningful living and work to do! At age 57 she chose to quit her day job to follow her dream of helping others get MORE of what they want out of their lives!

Because of the revelation that can be too easy to get comfortable and potentially complacent she developed a process to help her & others get moving in a positive direction. Her 7-Step COURAGE Formula helps others move forward with their lives more quickly with SUCCESS!

Clare makes people feel heard, supported, encouraged, energized and inspired! Clare's clients say she has an amazing ability to get to the heart of the matter quickly!

Clare is a Certified NLP Coach, Certified Dream Coach™, Master Practitioner in the NLP, Certified Professional Behavioral & Values Analyst, a Certified Gallup Strengths Coach & Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR).

Clare received her B. S. in Business from Florida State University & has an extensive coaching & management background.

Clare’s passions are exploring, traveling & new adventures. She believes you can create new and exciting adventures no matter what stage you of life you are in!

Specialties

Personal Development, Professional Development, Consultant, Executive Coach, Trainer, Professional Speaker in the areas of Starting Over, COURAGE, Success with Clarity, E3 Success Strategy, Communication, Team Success and Resilience, Human Resources
