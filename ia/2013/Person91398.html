Melvin Pérez-Cedano's Summary

Melvin Pérez, MSIT-SE, CSDP, is a Senior Fellow at Construx Software where he focuses on software design, software construction, software process, and configuration management. Prior to joining Construx, Melvin completed his Ph.D. coursework at Rochester Institute of Technology as well as an earlier Master of Science degree in Software Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University. Melvin was co-founder of CAM Informática, a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner software development company and one of the largest software providers in the Dominican Republic. Melvin ran the technical division at CAM Informática for more than 10 years.

Melvin has a history of transforming dysfunctional and immature organizations into performance and quality-oriented environments. He has an outstanding back-ground and leadership in promoting and implementing company-wide initiatives for establishing software development processes, tools and standards across the entire software development life cycle. He is experienced in architecting and leading the development of commercial and custom software applications. Melvin is expert in Agile practices including code cleaning, refactoring, and unit testing.

Melvin is also well versed in UML, RUP, and CMMI. He is bilingual in English and Spanish. He has lectured extensively throughout the Americas and is a former IEEE Distinguished Lecturer for Latin America.
