Jesse Haley's Summary

Producer, Digital PR & Design, at Elevate Communications, driving the firm’s digital marketing and social media programs from the conceptual stage through building and development to execution. I specialize in web design, development and analytics, social media, blogging, online marketing, Search Engine Optimization, and business-to-business and business-to-consumer lead generation.

Throughout my career, I've worked with local, regional and national businesses to build, transform, extend and establish brands and services online and across the social web. Prior to joining Elevate, I served as Director of Marketing & Media for RE/MAX Destiny, building localized and targeted social media communications and digital marketing programs for one of Boston’s top residential real estate agencies and its 50 agents.

I also spent two seasons as Creative Director for the Boston Blazers, helping to launch and establish Boston’s new professional lacrosse team through innovative brand, digital marketing and social media programs.

I have also worked as a consultant, producing creative digital and social media campaigns for various clients, including Friendly’s, Boston’s Museum of Science and the San Francisco 49ers.

I am a graduate of Boston University’s Center for Digital Imaging Arts.
