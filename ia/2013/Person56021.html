Andrea Cornwell, ASID, ASBALAID's Summary

I am a registered interior designer, and principal/owner of iSpace, LLC, an award-winning interior design firm, located in Fayetteville, Arkansas, offering a full scope of commercial and residential interior design services.

With 22 years of experience working with several, successful architectural firms, I decided to venture out on my own, and I formed iSpace, LLC in 2002. Armed with a vision of creating a sense of harmony between architecture and interior design, I have been fortunate enough to build a dynamic network of dedicated professionals, and together, we have established iSpace as a leader in interior design and space planning.

As a complement to the traditional design services, iSpace specializes in offering “value-added” services that assist our client throughout the building or renovating project. From beginning to end, we are there to help our client maneuver through the process, and turn chaos into calm.

In addition to receiving numerous awards and recognitions over the past eight years, I’m very proud to say that iSpace was voted “Best Interior Design Firm in Northwest Arkansas” by the readers of CitiScapes Magazine for three consecutive years. What a thrill that has been for our team, and we are so grateful to our clients, colleagues and associates for bestowing such an honor upon us!
