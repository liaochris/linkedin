Jean Bauhaus' Summary

Jean is a skilled web and graphic designer, specializing in logos and brand identity and Facebook graphics in addition to clean, uncluttered and elegant website layouts. In addition, she is a front-end developer specializing in custom WordPress content management theme development. Along with her husband, she operates a freelance web and social media design studio, Bauhaus Online Creative.

Prior to freelancing, Jean enjoyed a long career as an administrative professional. She has over a decade of experience that includes roles such as receptionist, department administrative assistant, membership administrator and project administrator. Industries she has worked in include oil and gas, gas pipelines, engineering and construction, and law enforcement safety training. Most recently, she ran an online virtual assistant business known as The Task Wrangler, the pre-cursor to Bauhaus Online Creative.
