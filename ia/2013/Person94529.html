Mike Rohde's Summary

As a user experience and visual design professional, Mike is focused on web and user interface design. Works closely with clients and colleagues to solve design challenges Experienced in the development of user interfaces, app and in-app icons, logos and corporate identities.

As an author of The Sketchnote Handbook (Peachpit Press, December 2012), he's a leader in the international sketchnoting community.

As an illustrator, Mike has helped bestselling books REWORK, The $100 Startup and The Little Book of Talent communicate ideas with readers.

As a sketchnoter, Mike provides live, real-time sketchnotes of events, meetings and experiences. Past clients include GE, SXSW Interactive, An Event Apart, Summit Series, Tribal DDB and JESS3.

Specialties: UI, UX, user interface design, experience design, web design, information architecture, corporate identity development and design, branding, icon design, web usability, concept sketches and drawings.

Sketchnoting live events, meetings and information. Writing for Rohdesign.com since 2003, founded Sketchnote Army.
