Anina E. Hathaway's Summary

I am a graduate from the Art Institute of Colorado with an Associate’s degree in Graphic Design (with honors). Also, I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts and Bachelor of Arts—Art History degrees from University of Colorado at Boulder. I have also run my own graphic freelancing business, Hathaway Graphics, for the past five years designing logos, business cards, websites, and other marketing materials for several local businesses.

Through my college and work experiences, I have developed strong capabilities in graphic design as well as the fine arts. I am a team player, and my qualifications include both creative skills and technical skills, including an in-depth knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite on a Mac, which I am an avid user. Due to my wide knowledge of, and experience with, graphic design and fine art, particularly with conceptualism, symbolism, and a wide variety of design programs, I believe I would make an ideal candidate for your company.
