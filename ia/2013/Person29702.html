Danielle Jett's Summary

I'm a visual communication specialist with a passion for creating clear, information-rich graphics. I worked several years in print infographics in newspapers and magazines, but my current focus is in interactive data visualization tools and user-interface design. In my infographic design, I have a strict philosophy that a data visualization is only useful if it communicates the relevant information better than text alone. That said, I do have an affinity for data art.

I also have a healthy addiction to working with start-ups.

Specialties: Information analysis and design, data visualization, infographic design, user-interface design, data mining, explanatory diagrams, visual storytelling, strategic marketing, financial media, project management.
