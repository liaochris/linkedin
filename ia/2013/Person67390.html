jonas coomans' Projects

  • neoScores

    • October 2008 to Present
    Team Members: jonas coomans, Bob Hamblok, Bart Van der Roost

    neoScores (a product of Frank Starling Ltd.) is the leading HTML5 Sheet Music Web Application which renders musicXML on the client device with only JavaScript and an HTML5-Canvas.
    The main goals of neoScores are:
    - to become the leading tool for Publishers to allow them to organise and sell their online scores.
    - to become the leading tool for performing musicians to enable them to use their tablets as a music stand (on stage) with all benefits of the online (and offline) world.
