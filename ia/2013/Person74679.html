Kerstin Recker Alexandre's Summary

Kerstin Recker is working to create partnerships across the globe through information and digital technology, marketing, and social enterprise innovation. She is an integrated marketing maven and business development profession as well as the founder of Global Forces, a non-profit organization that seeks to fight energy poverty and illiteracy in the developing world through sustainable solar solutions.

For over ten years, she has worked with Fortune 500 companies and technology start-ups in disciplines like interactive media, integrated marketing, business development, and account management. Her successes have benefited global brands like American Express, Coca-Cola and T-Mobile.

Kerstin’s passion for business and international affairs drove her to complete her Masters of Science in Global Affairs at New York University with a concentration on International Business and Economic Development. Her interest in international business and economic development have led her to Mexico, Cuba, and South Africa, where she has been able to conduct first hand research.

In 2012, Kerstin launched Global Forces "Solar for Students" campaign that delivers portable solar products and educational materials to students in developing countries. The goal of the organization is to generate awareness of energy poverty and provide solutions to a cause that plagues over 23% of the world’s population.

Specialties: international business & development,business development, marketing, B2B/B2C, digital & social media, market entry analysis, product positioning, project management, CRM, content management, corporate social responsibility, social impact, public private partnerships, solar energy
