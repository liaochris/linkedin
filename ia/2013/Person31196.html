Dave McClain's Summary

Digital-Focused Executive Creative Director with extensive background in defining overarching creative vision, strategy, and direction. Comprehensive experience working with clients, leading agency teams, and delivering award-winning creative solutions.

Substantial experience building client relationships and growing accounts, including helping acquire agency of record contracts from multiple clients. Highly proficient in leading internal and client-facing presentations of concepts, strategy, and design. Adept at leading ideation and brainstorming sessions, and applying a strong understanding of industry innovations, trends, and history.

Directed and managed a team of up to 90 creative professionals in four separate locations. Considerable history of mentoring and providing leadership to team members. Experienced in serving in a Creative Lead role within multi-agency blended teams, and partnering with client-side in-house creative teams.

Passionate about creating exceptional work, growing client relationships, and elevating the proficiency and culture within internal teams.

Partial client list: Nike, ESPN, NHL, Sony, FX Networks, Activision, Intel, Qualcomm, Vizio, Broadcom, Microsoft, Dwell, Fox, Nissan, Infiniti, West Coast Choppers, Lexus, Toyota, Mistubishi, Sea Island Resorts, Hard Rock Hotel and Casino, Outrigger Hotels, Yonex, Disney.
