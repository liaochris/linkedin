David Duncan's Summary

Thinking about marketing your products and services online – but don’t know where to start?

Connect with me and learn how to create and implement a digital marketing strategy that increases our clients' website traffic by 40%.

David Duncan is a Digital Marketing Consultant with WSI, he specialises in Online Reputation Management, and is the Area Representative for WSI in the South East of the UK. At WSI we help our clients to use the internet to market their businesses online. We work with business owners to help them make the most of the internet to generate more sales improve their brand reputation and communication

David helps his clients in three main areas :-

• To generate more leads for their business
• To enhance their online credibility and brand reputation
• To improve communication with clients and prospects

At WSI we help you to market your business online -

We design and develop great websites, but for us it is just the start of the journey. What we then work with customers to do is:-

Search engine optimisation or more often called - SEO - this is working to ensure our client site rank highly in search engines, people find you when they are looking for your services.

Social media optimisation (SMO) - how do businesses leverage sites such as Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn to grow.

Social Selling - how to harness the power of the social channels to find new clients

Paid Search Advertising or pay per click - working with Google and other search partners to drive traffic to a website for conversion.

Email marketing campaign management for our clients - one way to maintain a relationship with clients that you may not see from one year to the next.

Online brand creation and reputation management in order to keep a positive company profile on the internet.

If you would be interested in finding out more about WSI or would like to join our team, please email me at david@wsigotwebsolutions.com or call 0845 345 2592 now.
