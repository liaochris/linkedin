James Burkhardt's Summary

As one friend says, "James is a difficult man to introduce because he is multifaceted". He launched SnT Radio playing 4 Decades of "The Greatest Christian Music" over the internet in May 2009. Go to http://www.sntradio.com, and give it a listen!

A self-described “connector”, James has over 30 productive years in Marketing, Sales, and Communications which began in 1976 as a retail manager for Tyndale House Publishers. He’s written and produced hundreds of radio and TV commercials and been recognized by WHO’s WHO in the Midwest since 1990. He reached his first of several executive level positions at the age of 29 and started his first entrepreneurial venture in 1987 after a successful Sales and Broadcasting Management career at KBRT-AM in Los Angeles, and WCRM-FM, WFYR-FM and WCFL-AM in Chicago.

James is an accomplished musician who plays and teaches voice, guitar, and bass. He’s midway through a Master’s program from Boston’s prestigious Berklee College of Music in Music Business and Production. As a “Berkleemusic.com Ambassador”, James has the distinction of being a one of 20 individuals worldwide officially sanctioned to represent and promote Berklee’s online school in blogs, chatrooms and anyplace else where musicians congregate.

It was the opportunity to intern with a 3 time Grammy Award winning and US patent holding Mastering Engineer Bob Katz which enticed James and his wife Terilyn, to relocate to Orlando from Chicago in the summer of 2006. After his internship, he joined Salem Communication's Conservative Talk WORL 660AM where he hosted "FOCUS Orlando" prior to launching SnT Radio.com.

Developing songwriters for his YonderStar Music publishing company and producing cutting edge Contemporary Christian Worship is his passion behind ProtoStar Digital Designs. Currently, he plays guitar and bass on the worship team at The Atlar/St. Cloud Vineyard and leads worship when asked.

In his spare time, James likes to cook gourmet meals.
