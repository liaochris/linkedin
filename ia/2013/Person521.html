Aaron Jentzen's Summary

I’m a freelance writer and multiplatform journalist, currently based in Pittsburgh, Pa. My arts, entertainment and technology coverage has appeared in Paste, The San Antonio Express-News, The Houston Chronicle and several alternative newsweeklies.

After serving as Pittsburgh City Paper’s music editor from 2006-11, I went back to school, completing master’s degrees in Professional Writing and Investigative Journalism at Carnegie Mellon University. Through this program, which included a semester studying in Glasgow, Scotland, I developed skills in digital media and online information design, researched best practices in digital news and launched an ongoing investigation into state arts funding.
