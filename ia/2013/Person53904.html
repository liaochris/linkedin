Carl Thorne's Summary

Why Use Us?

Experience. We learned the hard way. If a computer system can fail, we have probably seen it before and it happened on our watch. If you can do something dumb to a computer, we have been there, too. We try very hard to learn from our mistakes and we are constantly learning new things from training, seminars, books, magazines, clients and client service calls. Our experience is always growing.

We like what we do, and it shows! (Sorry Delta...) We love computers and everything about them. We like sharing our knowledge. We like teaching others. We understand most people do not wish to understand computers to the level we do. We have experience consulting with people who are trying to solve business problems, not gain knowledge for a doctorate in computer science. We speak computers and we speak English.

We do not sell solutions because they are elegant or bleeding edge. We sell solutions that solve the problem and are cost effective. We are risk adverse. We are cheap. We are a small business and run a household just like you and every penny counts.

We want you to be happy. Our business growth and revenue is based on repeat and referral business. We will not be doing TV infomercials any time soon.

We do not take sides in the Apple versus Windows battle. We work on both, because we use both. Both platforms have their respective places in the marketplace.

We are not commissioned sales people. We do not sell /push computers. We make recommendations based on what makes sense for your needs and what our experience tells us will work for you. We will turn down business we think is not in your best interest. Putting money into a computer that is 10 years old is not something we willingly do.

We believe in staying up to date via education and certifications. We are A+, Network + and Security + certified. We are also a Microsoft Certified Professional.

Specialties

Integration between Win Intel Business Systems and the Apple Mac platform. We specialize in providing support to small businesses that are between 1 to 5 employees that have a need for IT support, but cannot afford or feel they need a fulltime IT employee.

Carl Thorne, founder of High Tech House Calls, has more than 25 years' experience in the technology industry. As a programmer, analyst, and consultant for companies such as IBM, Digital Equipment Corporation, Informatica, and Computer Systems Corporation. Carl has worked with clients from many industries. crafting computer configurations to solve their problems and meet their technology needs. A long time Atlanta resident and father of three, Carl is active as a volunteer at The Westminster Schools and Trinity Presbyterian Church.
