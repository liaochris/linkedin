Jamie Gripton's Summary

Jamie has more than 10 years of exposure to all aspects of Recruitment including strategic resourcing & performance management, talent acquisition at all levels, vendor management, succession planning, compensation & benefits, employee communications, employer branding & employee value proposition development. The industries predominantly covered include Banking, Financial Services, Insurance, FMCG, IT&T & Professional Services.

Jamie is a firm believer that effective Talent Acquisition requires the alignment of HR strategies around business objectives, strategic partnering with line management & other key stakeholders, & a highly-engaged workforce that is committed to delivering those goals. Primarily Jamie has experience managing talent acquisition firms covering Graduate placements through to C-Level Executive Search. Key placements have included the CEO of a Global Banking Group, CIO of a Magic Circle Law Firm, Global Marketing Director at a Big4 Accounting Firm, Global Head of HR at a Global Insurance Co.

Until May 2013 when Jamie relocated with his family to New Zealand, he was the Managing Director for RMI a Human Capital Risk Firm in Singapore; Jamie now holds a non-executive advisory role at RMI. Prior to RMI, Jamie was the Managing Director of LMA Recruitment across Asia Pacific (Part of Empresaria PLC); a specialist recruitment firm covering the Banking & Financial Services Sector. Jamie has led large outsourcing projects, delivered training/assessment centers and performed search assignments for large corporate and SME’s alike.

Jamie has a Bachelors Degree in Business & Finance, and is a trusted business partner to many of the top companies in the world.

Specialties: Developing talent strategies and driving change across business. Particular focus on the management and mitigation of risk when looking at human capital. Core skill set is building strong and mutually beneficial relationships at CxO level.
