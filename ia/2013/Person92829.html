Michael Papuc's Summary

San Francisco Attorney Michael Papuc has been practicing law since 1987. The Law Office of Michael Papuc, located on Montgomery Street, was founded in 1995. His practice emphasizes insurance bad faith, mechanics liens and collections, employment harassment and discrimination, trade secret theft, non-competition agreements, personal injury. He began his career as a lawyer at Kornblum, Kelly & Herlihy, a midsized law firm. He served as the company’s litigation attorney, with a focus on insurance and bad-faith defense practice. Michael Papuc followed up by taking on employment with Thornton, Taylor & Downs, another medium-sized firm in the city, where he acted as an Associate for five years before opening up his private practice.

He earned a B.A. in Economics fom Brooklyn College in New York City and a J.D. from the University of California Hastings College of the Law in San Francisco and began practicing as a lawyer. Michael Papuc served in the United States Marine Corps eserves while attending college. In his free time, Michael Papuc enjoys biking.
