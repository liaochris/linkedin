Luciana Cadé's Summary

I'm a journalist with experience in corporate communications, public relations, mainstream journalism and implementation of communication projects of the NGOs.

I've coordinated editorial projects, such as three consecutive annual reports of Ceará Cooperative of Anesthesiologists, three annual reports of Unimed Fortaleza, as well as other entities such as the Union of Construction Industry of Ceará and the Department of Sport and Youth in Government State of Ceará.

I've developed public relations work for the Eurocentro Ceará - linked to the European Commission (Export / Import), Arx Eventos (Events), Microsiga Ceará (IT), among others.

I've led works of public relations. Emphasis for the XX International Congress of Clinical Chemistry and Laboratory Medicine (2008) and the Brazilian Congress of Sleep (2007).

I was on the team that was awarded with the 2004 Child Award, offered by Abrinq Foundation. I've also worked at O Povo journal, where I was honored, being part of a team, with the "Prêmio aNote de Jornalismo", in 2003, offered by Agência de Notícias Esperança (Hope News Agency).

Specialties

Communication consultancy, corporate communication and coordination of editorial projects.
