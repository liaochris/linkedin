David McGan's Summary

I offer your company a common-sense, practical approach for effective application of Lean methods, Quality Systems development or maintenance, and Supplier Management services.

I utilize skills and practical knowledge I gained in over 20 years of work in Lean Methods and Quality System development and management to help other companies reduce costs and improve the quality and efficiency of their processes.

Quality Expertise - development and maintenance of ISO9000, TS16949 (automotive), ISO14000. Internal Auditing for your operations or 2nd party auditing for your suppliers.

Die Casting Expertise - over my long career in various quality-related positions at Gibbs Die Casting, I have developed extensive knowledge of the die casting process and the many variables that impact the quality of a casting. Working with a manufacturing team, using data to determine root cause and correct die casting defects is a particular strength.

Perhaps I can provide this practical perspective to your company's application of Continuous Improvement and Problem Solving -- or to your suppliers.

Consider also the potential savings and quality improvement by contracting my services to manage your Supplier base through services such as potential Supplier assessment, continuing supplier assessments or assistance with their problem-solving / corrective action activities. My client base utilizing these services include a number of Small Tool & Die Shops.

Specialties: Lean Training, Kaizen Workshop facilitation, TWI Training Methods, Quality System development, Quality System auditing, ISO9000 / TS16949 / ISO14000, Vendor Selection Assistance, Supplier Development, Supplier Management, Root Cause Analysis / Problem Solving of casting-related defects, Die Casting Process Improvement, Workplace Safety and Ergonomics
