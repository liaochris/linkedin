Dr. Vicky Spring Love's Experience

Pastor

Life Renewal Church

May 2013 – Present (8 months) Costick Center, 28600 W 11 Mile, Farmington Hills, MI

The word “renew” means to restore, revive or reestablish. The vision of Life Renewal Church is to be a place where lives are changed, souls are healed, and purpose is restored. Our purpose is to gather a community of people to connect with God, glorify Him and serve others. We seek to be a place where people recover from past hurts, habits, and hang-ups that prevent them from enjoying meaningful relationships with God and each other.

Owner

Jubilee Homes, LLC

August 2006 – Present (7 years 5 months)

Buy, rehab, sell and manage single family homes in suburban Detroit areas.

Author and Speaker

Victory Jubilee Publishing

November 2003 – Present (10 years 2 months)

Author of several books, including Stop Robbing Peter to Pay Paul, Credit Repair Made Easy ebook, Changing Your Money Mindset and co-author of 101 Great Ways to Improve Your Life, along with Jack Canfield, Richard Carlson, John Gray and others.

Speaker at churches, conferences and retreats. Have spoken at both Christian and secular events. Christian topics include: Push Past the Fear, The Fork in the Road, Break the Spirit of Debt, The ABCs to Financial Victory, Overcoming Our Issues, Break the Spirit of Debt, Healing for Damaged Emotions, and Honoring God with the Tithe. Secular topics include: Budgeting is Not a Four-Letter Word, Using Credit Wisely, Foreclosure Options, and Keys to Successful Business Ownership.

Associate Minister

Family Victory Fellowship

November 1997 – May 2013 (15 years 7 months)

Minister over Adult Education. Dean of Ambassador Bible Training School, a two-year intensive study of the Bible. Preach and teach the Word on Wednesday evenings and Sunday mornings as needed by Senior Pastor Larry T. Jordan. Prayer, counseling and hospital visitations as needed.

President

Victory Financial Corporation

July 2001 – June 2008 (7 years)

15-year veteran of the mortgage industry; worked as a mortgage loan officer and owned mortgage company for several years.
