John Hodgins' Summary

A results-focused communications manager, John Hodgins helps leaders engage with the right audiences to build relationships, enhance reputations and achieve goals through digital campaigns. He excels at uniting program and policy experts with talented creative professionals to develop effective communications products – and at leveraging his extensive social media experience to help nonprofit teams succeed in a changing communications environment.

Specialties

social media, digital communications, search engine marketing, print and web design, publications management, strategic planning, project management
