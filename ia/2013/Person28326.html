Curtis Johnson's Summary

I have been practicing as a tax accountant and business advisor since 1997. I specialize in financial consulting and tax planning for small to medium sized corporations, partnerships and limited liability companies along with the individuals who own these businesses.

I began my career with a regional CPA firm working with high net worth clients in the individual tax, business tax and small business administration groups. I went on to Morris & Bryson, LLC, a legal and financial services firm, where I managed the accounting, tax, and financial services arm of the firm.
