John N. Cohen's Summary

Art Galleries refer to me as a photographer, but Photographers refer to me as an Artist!

"..regarded as one of Britain's most original photographers" The Times.

"A woman's face was exquisitely metamorphosed with the cup of a yellow tulip - a Femme - Fleur Picasso might have perpetrated had he taken to photography” Arts Revue.

John's pictures really make quite a statement! They add a new bright dimension to any wall. Sophisticated, original, always a talking point, they intrigue, yet remain intelligible to all.

Limited to editions of only 8, they have great investment potential too! Not yet one of the big names, but his exhibitions to date are impressive. Now is the best time for astute buyers to buy, considering how very special John's photography is. http://www.artist-john-cohen.net

John designed and published 'The Cohen Collection' of Asian antiques (645 colour photos) of fine Japanese and Chinese antique; netsuke, inro, ojime, snuff bottles, pendants & jade carvings. All are fully described by world renowned experts Neil K. Davey + Robert Hall. An essential beautiful reference work for the serious collector and very easy to use. Also John's father's out of print book 'In Search of Netsuke and Inro' by George Cohen is included at http://www.jncohen.com

John has written a number of articles for new collectors providing, invaluable information for collectors of Asian antiques. He describes how to avoid the most common mistakes when buying Asian antiques, particularly netsuke, inro, snuff bottles and jade. http://www.jncohen.net/antiques/articles.htm

A Free 'Special Effects' pure Photographic Course is available about his way of 'Painting with Light'. It still is amazing to see what can be done, without any expensive equipment, computer, or darkroom, with both film or digital cameras, everything is achieved with projectors - the projector really is a magical magic lantern! http://www.jncohen.net/photmagi/actual.html

Specialties

Large Limited Edition Prints, Special Effects Photography, Lover of Asian antiques such as Japanese Netsuke, Inro and Lacquer as well as Chinese Snuff Bottles, Pendants and Jade, enthusiast of Jensen Classic Cars of the 1960's.

John N. Cohen's Experience

Painting With Light

jncohen (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Photography industry

May 2009 – Present (4 years 3 months)

I am currently producing large (150cm longest side) original pictures created as limited editions for sale worldwide. Each of these pictures were first created by my unique method of 'Painting With Light', since then I have improved, or in some cases created a totally new digital version. They are all mounted on aluminum bonded with 6mm acrylic glass and appear to float off the wall (no frame is really required). You can choose any size you wish for each picture (but they are restricted to just 8 regardless of the size).

John N. Cohen's Publications

  • The Cohen Collection: Antique Netsuke, Inro, Chinese Jades and Snuff Bottles

    • Actuality PhotoDigital
    • October 9, 2000
    Authors: John N. Cohen, Neil K. Davey, Robert Hall

    First published as a CD-Rom but now available at www.jncohen.com it consists of a wonderful interactive, reference catalogue of a very fine collection of Japanese and Chinese antiques consisting of; 82 Snuff bottles, 74 Pendants, 25 Jade & hard stone carvings, 57 Netsuke, 62 Inro (all complete with Ojime and Netsuke) also a number of exceptional lacquer boxes.

    It is unusual both for being in this digital form and for combining such very fine specialised Japanese and Chinese antiques.

    Neil K. Davey and Robert Hall, the world's leading experts, have provided detailed descriptions with interesting, important new reference notes that together with the 645 colour photographs (including close-ups of signatures and seals) make this an easy to use, fascinating, and beautiful way of sharing such works of art. Many of the antiques have never been published before and this reference study will prove invaluable to any serious collector. Originally published as a CD-Rom ISBN: 0953934101

    This website of THE COHEN COLLECTION also includes the out of print 1974 publication 'IN SEARCH OF NETSUKE & INRO' by George A. Cohen.
