Jeff Parker's Summary

Jeff Parker founded Food Crew as an outlet for his dynamic blend of his culinary passions – including creating stellar food and beverages. Having an impressive multi-faceted culinary career that includes working as a chef, restaurant owner/operator, caterer, cookbook and recipe developer, and food stylist, Jeff Parker has proven himself as an intuitive chef with keen culinary and food trend insights. His experience enables him to handle the unique challenges that arise in developing and styling recipes for television, cookbooks, and print media.

Today he operates Food Crew, Inc., a culinary services venture specializing in recipe and cookbook development, test kitchen activities, food styling and culinary production for television and print. Jeff has developed nearly 3,500 food and beverage recipes, including 20+ cookbooks.

Jeff has recently been featured as guest talent on culinary and lifestyle television.

Specialties

Recipe and Cookbook development, Food Styling, On-Camera Hosting. Versatile in many cuisines and cooking styles.
