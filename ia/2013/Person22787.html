Kevin Bachman's Summary

New home construction and residential design / build remodeling is a complex process with several moving parts. Chicago Construction Works brings an educational background in design and construction from the University of Illinois at Chicago. We also bring 15 years active work experience involved with home building and commercial tenant improvements. Our portfolio also includes complex kitchen, bathroom renovations, and large scale multiple unit residential rehab renovations. Our continual education in the construction industry includes attending NAHB Builder's Show each year, learning about new products, materials and installation procedures. We surround ourselves with the best installers with the highest integrity who have been working in the industry for more than 20 years.
