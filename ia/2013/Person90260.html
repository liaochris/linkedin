Michel Blankenstein's Projects

  • 3DC

    • September 2009 to July 2010
    Team Members: Michel Blankenstein, Rakesh Chinnoe, Tom van den Tweel, Ronald van Zon, Eric van Vliet, Valentijn Verhallen

    3DC stands for 3D - Digital Communication.
    I did this project with 5 other students in the third year of college. The goal with 3DC was to create an 3D game where players were getting to know the 'Faculty for Communication and Journalistic' and the education 'Digital Communication' by doing assignments and solving puzzles.
    For this project the education manager Dhr. W. Spee was the client.

    At the start of the project we tried to work with the engine 'Irrlicht'. Because this was only a graphics engine and not a game engine it didn't met the requirements. We then started using Quest 3D by advise from and ex-student who works with Quest 3D a lot. After some research we concluded there was only a commercial version of Quest 3D. The client couldn't get a budget for the engine so after some more research we found out that the just released 'Cryengine 3' (Far Cry 2, Crysis 2) was free for educational usage.

    Proceedings:
    My primary role within this project was Projectleader.
