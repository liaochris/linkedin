John Tayman's Experience

Founder & CEO

Byliner.com

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry

June 2011 – Present (2 years 7 months) San Francisco

Byliner works directly with the world's best writers to deliver great stories to readers.

Hailed by Fast Company as "One of the 10 Most Innovative Media Companies in the World," we're the leading publisher in the "e-shorts" category, the fastest-growing segment of digital books. These quick-read stories are written to be read in two hours or less, and are available on phone, tablet, or computer as part of the Byliner subscription service. Byliner Originals can also be purchased individually through digital bookstores such as Amazon, Apple's iBookstore, and Barnes & Noble. Since our launch in June 2011, we've had more than 30 bestsellers--including the first e-short to reach No. 1 on the New York Times Best Seller list. Our authors include Jon Krakauer, Nick Hornby, Margaret Atwood, Amy Tan, Lawrence Lessig Ann Patchett, Buzz Bissinger, Richard Russo, Chuck Pahlaniuk, and many more.

In addition to commissioning original work, our editors collect and curate a rich library of more than 20,000 fiction and nonfiction stories by the world's best writers, allowing readers to easily find, read, save, and share new and classic stories by their favorite authors. Subscribers get access to premium content from Byliner's by-invitation community of writers, including thousands of rarely-seen or exclusive stories from the world's best authors.
