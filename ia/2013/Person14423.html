Bill Ricard's Summary

Lord, help me to remember there's only one today.

William L. Ricard
He is the President of Citizen Helpers Inc.(citizenhelpers.com), which was founded in 1998 through a personal experience in his life. Mr. Ricard had a grandmother who suffered through the effects of Alzheimer's disease.

Mr. Ricard is responsible for day-to-day operations of the company that currently has clients through out the Capital Region of the State of New York with a home base in the City of Cohoes.

Mr. Ricard is also the Chief Consultant for a remodeling business, Compete Remodeling Services + More, LLC which was started by Mr. Ricard’s, father Ron who is still actively involved in the businesses operation. The website for the company is www.CRCRS.com

Mr. Ricard has been associated with many volunteer organizations and endeavors including the Cohoes Caretakers and the Cohoes Chamber of Commerce. He has also been appointed to positions representing all Cohoes citizens and has served on the Historic Preservation Commission and was also a participant on the Charter Review Commission, Character Counts Committee and the 470 Corridor Study. In many of these groups, Mr. Ricard played a key role. As President of the Cohoes Chamber of Commerce, he was the CO-architect with President Lyn Taylor (Albany-Colonie Regional Chamber of Commerce) of a successful merger between the Albany-Colonie Regional Chamber of Commerce and the Cohoes Chamber of Commerce. In addition he has been the past vice-chair of the Historic Preservation Commission. Mr. Ricard has coached his daughter's softball clinic team.

Mr. Ricard graduated from Hudson Valley Community College with an Associates Degree in Marketing and from SUNY Brockport with a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration. He is a graduate of the Alzheimer's Association Train the Trainer program. He is married and the father of two children.
