Beth Levine's Summary

Multi Media Promotions is a certified woman owned boutique promotional advertising agency with over 30 years of print and promotional product experience.

We bring images, products and ideas together to help companies form a lasting impression by putting their name and logo on everything and anything.

We will help you promote your company with print material, giveaways, awards, gifts, incentives, lumpy mail, imprinted apparel. We also offer a full range of large format printing, banners, table covers,signage and column wraps.

We have partnered with tradeshow and signage companies to offer you the best in tradeshow displays and management, signage, banners and more.

We are known for helping companies promote with sustainable items and apparel within all budgets in addition to the tried and true products. Our marketing kit is totally sustainable including the folder made out of seeded paper that grows into flowers along with sustainable tips, and eco-friendly items.
No budget is too small and no turnaround too tight. We will make it happen for you.

Our clients range from sole proprietors, small businesses through Fortune 500 companies both locally and nationally. Our clients know that they can depend upon us to find the perfect item, within budget, on time with 100% satisfaction guaranty.

Good referrals for our company are advertising and marketing directors, purchasing agents, HR, event planners government agencies and schools.

Specialties: Sustainable promotional items and apparel
