Barry Egbert's Summary

I am a Software Consultant, Developer, and Entrepreneur living and working in the Salt Lake City, Utah area. I'm currently working on a contract at the Department of Veterans Affairs, managing a group of developers and designing and developing a medical information system to store our Nation's Veterans Electronic Medical Records (EMR).

The majority of my work experience has been developing Java Enterprise applications using the various technologies available. I'm a progressive thinker. I like trying new things and new technologies. I don't mind working on the bleeding edge but tend to choose technologies that have been proven. I have a great passion for what I do and consider myself one of those lucky individuals that likes going to work each day.

My goals are to continue developing my skills, provide excellent technical leadership and direction to the organizations that I serve, take advantage of opportunities for professional growth, and provide a superior value to my clients.
