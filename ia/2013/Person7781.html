Angie Wright, RLA, ASLA's Summary

Angie is a registered landscape architect in the State of Texas since 1998. She has a range of experience that includes residential, estate, commercial and institutional projects. Angie enjoys working with clients to design a garden space that meets the client’s needs while complementing the building architecture and existing site features. The interior and exterior spaces on a site should complement one another. There should be seamless flow from all spaces. The architecture of the garden should complement and work with the building architecture.

Having traveled extensively her entire life and studying art and architecture in Italy while in school, Angie brings the knowledge she has gained by experiencing landscapes old and new around the world.

Angie is the owner of vaughn wright design studio, a landscape architecture firm dedicated to producing high end designs that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. She will work with you to make sure your needs are met and that you have the highest quality of design.

Angie has spoken at the following events:
“Innovations in Landscape Design”-TNLA Conference
“Solutions to Common Garden Problems”-Dallas Garden Club
