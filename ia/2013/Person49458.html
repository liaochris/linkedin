Robyn Goodpasture's Summary

As a Fitness Expert, Personal Trainer, Weight loss and Body Image Consultant, I have dedicated my life's work to bringing total transformation in the lives of my clients through physical and mental training. Living and loving and speaking about what I teach concerning weight maintenance, building lean muscle, I am passionate about helping people lose weight once and for all. As a champion figure competitor I understand the discipline it takes to truly change the female body and reach its beautiful potential both inside and out.

Along side of my husband, also a personal trainer and coach, I am a co-owner of FitStudio365 a personal training studio in Roanoke VA. I am a mother of three beautiful children and a woman of faith and strength whose personal mission is to help women step into their own greatness and have a healthy positive image of themselves.

Specialties: Robyn is an NFPT Certified Personal Trainer and Women's Fitness Expert. In her previous life before selling her Spa, she is a Master Esthetician, Waxing and Nail technician Instructor, Permanent Make up Artist and Trainer, Electrologist.
