Derek Paul is a commercial real estate broker and consultant that specializes in industrial, retail, office and land for sale and/or lease in Southern California focusing on the Orange County and Corona markets.

Beginning his career in August of 2008, Derek learned to cut his teeth in the toughest market seen in 30+ years and has flourished when many have failed due to his pitbull like tenacity and ability to stay on the cutting edge of technology & marketing. For the first two years of his career Derek focused almost exclusively on leasing industrial properties developing his negotiation skills and expanding his knowledge base representing both Owners and Tenants. Since early 2010 he has been using his growing expertise along with Don Paul’s extensive knowledge to represent owners & buyers in the sales & acquisitions of commercial properties.
Utilizing effective communication and marketing plus the ability to be completely mobile allows him to close deals fast and remain accesible at all times to his clients. His sophisticated lead tracking system and quick response times give him a competitive edge in todays fast paced world.
