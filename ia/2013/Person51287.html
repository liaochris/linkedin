Haj Gueye's Summary

Thank you for visiting my LinkedIn profile.

I am the Founder and Chief Creative Officer of HAJ Designs Inc., and known to the fashion world as Haj. I have traveled the world in response to the fashion needs of some of the most celebrated personalities in business, entertainment, and sports. I am well-known for my imaginative designs and the unmatched fit of my tailor-made clothes.

I was born in Senegal and raised in Paris, and I am a third generation designer. I trained under the strict guidance of my father, Barra Gueye, a well-known designer who created apparel for French and West African aristocracy as well as World War II troops. I have worked throughout Europe until 1987 when I moved to the United States after gaining notoriety in Paris as the winner of the Young Designer of the Year Award for three consecutive years.

Each order that HAJ Designs fills is individually processed and cut by hand to the client's measurements and specifications. While mass production has become the norm everywhere, our craftsmen cut, trim, and stitch every garment by hand - using machines only to enhance their skills.

It is the mission of HAJ Designs Inc. to provide our customers with the finest tailor-made garments at a competitive price in the shortest manufacturing time possible.

With a credo of uncompromising excellence in materials, quality, design, fit, craftsmanship, and personalized service, I vow to create "The most perfect suit for men and women the world has ever known."
