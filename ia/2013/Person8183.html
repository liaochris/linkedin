AnnaLee Conti's Publications

  • Frontiers of Faith, the Story of Charles C, and Florence L. Personeus, Pioneer Missionaries to Alaska, the Last Frontier, 1917-1982

    • AuthorHouse
    • February 1, 2003
    Authors: AnnaLee Conti

    Grandma Personeus was a storyteller and kept everyone entranced with the accounts of their early days in Alaska: 20-foot snowfalls, avalanches, a hair-raising dog sled ride, miraculous healings. Many people kept asking her to write their story. One day, she placed in my hands a bundle of her written accounts, saying, "I'm too old to see it through. You do with this as you see fit." I wrote the story and was able to read it aloud to my grandparents before they died in their late nineties. The book is available through my website, as well as at Amazon, Barnes & Noble, and other online bookstores.

  • Till the Storm Passes By (first book in Alaskan Waters series)

    • Ambassador International
    • April 15, 2013
    Authors: AnnaLee Conti

    Historical Christian fiction set in Alaska in 1953.
    "Mommy! Wake up!" a little girl screams. But the woman on the beach lies cold and wet and still. Evie Parker is plagued by a recurring nightmare from her childhood. Who is the woman in her dream? What does it mean? A deathbed confession compels Evie to leave her home in Rhode Island and travel to the Territory of Alaska, where she struggles to unravel a past shrouded in mystery. Can she come through storms, both natural and emotional, to open her heart to true love?
    The novel explores the power of secrets, love, and forgiveness.

AnnaLee Conti's Projects

  • The Alaskan Waters Series of Christian historical fiction

    • September 2009 to Present
    Team Members: AnnaLee Conti

    The genre of the Alaskan Waters series is historical Christian fiction set in 1953 Alaska, where I grew up in a missionary family. In 2002, I published "Frontiers of Faith," the story of my grandparents, Charles & Florence Personeus, the first Assemblies of God missionaries to Alaska, 1917-1982. As I was researching that book, I came across stories that triggered my imagination, and the idea for my Alaskan Waters series was conceived. "Till the Storm Passes By" is the first book in the series.

  • A Star to Steer By

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: AnnaLee Conti

    This historical Christian novel is the second in my Alaskan Waters trilogy and is the prequel of "Till the Storm Passes By." Tales of the booming fishing industry in faraway Alaska in 1920 lure Norman, a teenaged Norwegian fisherman, to immigrate to America, leaving behind his fiancee, Kristina, who promises to remain faithful. Norman thinks of Kristina as his "star," but can she save him from himself and the beautiful but conniving Cecilia? The rough draft is completed, and I am doing the revisions now.
