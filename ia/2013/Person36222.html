Dana Mitroff Silvers' Summary

I’m a web strategy and implementation consultant with experience launching digital products in museums, nonprofits, and educational institutions. I bring a human-centered approach to every project.

I develop and facilitate custom design thinking workshops for organizations ranging from national museums to local universities.

My expertise is based on several years at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art, where I oversaw the research, strategy, design, and production of the award-winning site, www.sfmoma.org. During my tenure, I spearheaded and drove the website redesign and mobile site launch and championed and put into practice Agile software development practices and human-centered design.

Areas of expertise:
- web strategy
- project management
- vendor selection and management
- building and leading cross-disciplinary teams
- user research and experience
- facilitation of design thinking workshops
- coaching on how to integrate design thinking methodologies into daily practice
