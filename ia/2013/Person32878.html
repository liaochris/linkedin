Dawn Block's Summary

I'm Dawn Block. I’m a full-time, full-service Realtor ® and lifelong resident of the Elk Grove Village and the surrounding suburbs. My local expertise and well-rounded real estate background will benefit you whether you are a first-time home buyer, a seasoned home veteran, investor or a returning client.

I have been in the Real Estate business since 1987. I have extensive knowledge of the real estate market which will fully maximize your real estate experience. I have a marketing plan that wil expose your home to a high volume of potential buyers. I have extensive negotiating and communication skills.

My Goal is to listen to my client and see what their real estate needs are and then assist them to reach their goals of selling or purchasing a home.

Specialties

Communicating and Negotiating, Marketing and exposure of your home to a high volume of buyers. Servicing my client and walking them through the process of buying or selling a home. Updating my client on the housing trends.Working with Investors and ShortSale Sellers and Foreclosures.
