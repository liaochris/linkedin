Jasmine Evans' Summary

I'm a freelance education writer who works hard to get useful, enlightening information to parents, teachers, students, and administrators.

Writing Experience

Where have I been published?
In print: College Xpress, LINK magazine, School Planning & Management, College Planning & Management, Diverse: Issues in Higher Education
Online: Diversity Inc., Degree Prospects, Campus Explorer, Education.com

What is it like to work with me? I write with my readers and my editors in mind. The last thing I want is to bore readers or cause headaches for an editor. So, I meet my deadlines, polish my work before submission, and double check all my facts.

Teaching Experience

Tutoring: I have several years of professional tutoring under my belt. I've tutored every grade from kindergarten through twelfth and specialize in the humanities and social sciences. I also conduct individualized instruction sessions at Mills College for students taking the mandatory first year writing seminar.

Classroom Teaching: I have worked as a teaching assistant for the Summer Institute for the Gifted and the Princeton University Preparatory Program. I also taught English during a summer in Los Angeles at Stevenson Middle School.

College Admissions: For four years, I worked as the College Preparation Adviser for Results Academic Institute. I helped several students navigate the tricky college admissions process while teaching them study and stress management skills to boost their high school performance.

See my website for access to samples of my work and more about me: www.jasminekevans.com

Specialties: Writing, Blogging, Editing, Copywriting, Grant writing, short stories, HTML, CSS, WYSIWYG layout tools, Social media
