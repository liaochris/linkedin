Christie Mayer's Summary

I recently joined Pacific 33 architects, a collaboration of designers and architects that migrated from the highly-regarded healthcare design firm, Jain Malkin, Inc. after its closing in November 2012. I'm honored to be part of a team positioned at the forefront of healthcare design and evidence-based architecture.

I have several years of experience as an exhibit designer providing unique experiences for world-renowned museums and live animal habitats for both zoos and public aquariums. Along with visitor centers, history and science centers I have expanded my repertoire to include sports halls-of-fame and restaurant design.

I am passionate about our environment and strive to make smart design choices that utilize sustainable materials and eco-friendly fabrication methods whenever possible and have worked to achieve LEED Gold and LEED Platinum certification for facilities in California and Arizona.

Specialties: Because of my background as an Industrial Designer I bring a unique approach to interior design by incorporating a variety of special features and custom elements. I enjoy working as a conceptual designer but can also be found wearing the hat of Project Manager, following my projects' development from schematic design through construction.
