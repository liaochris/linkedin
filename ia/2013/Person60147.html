  • Call Flow Tool

    • January 2007 to June 2007

    Designed and developed an a generic tool to analyze and visualize log data for virtually any software product. CFT is now integrated into Cisco UCM and being used across the Voice Technology Group. CFT's generic approach to log parsing and analysis was issued a patent on October 26, 2010.

  • Business Center

  • Project Dust

    • April 2011 to Present

    Part of the team that has started LinkedIn's move to dust.js client-side templates. This approach offers a number of advantages, including (a) a unified view layer across a variety of different front-end technologies, (b) improved performance over server-side rendering, since client-side templates are served via a CDN and are cached in the browser, and (c) setting us up with one of the building blocks for client-side apps and client-side MVC.

  • Intern Hackday 2012

    • August 2012 to August 2012

    Organized LinkedIn's second annual Intern Hackday. 170 interns from every company in the Bay Area showed up at LinkedIn, hacked all night, submitted 44 projects, and got to present their work to an awesome lineup of judges (Rahul Vohra, Pamela Fox, Manu Kumar, Rashmi Sinha, Ben Zhao).

  • [in]cubator

    • March 2012 to Present

    I created and am now running the [in]cubator program at LinkedIn, which lets any employee come up with an idea, form a team, put together a proposal, and pitch it directly to LinkedIn execs. If approved, the team gets up to 90 days of dedicated time to turn the idea into reality.

    This is a powerful way to take hackday projects to the next level and another step in employee-led innovation at LinkedIn.

  • Play Framework at LinkedIn

    • April 2012 to Present

    Integrated the Play Framework as LinkedIn's new service infrastructure. Play is a modern web framework that combines the performance and reliability of Java and Scala, the power of reactive programming, and the productivity of full hot reload support.
