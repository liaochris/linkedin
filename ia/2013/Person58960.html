Jasmine Marchong's Summary

I am a Professional Resume Writer servicing all employment levels in every industry. I provide hard-hitting, effective resumes and cover letters which profile your skills and accomplishments in such a way that the employer wants to meet you.

I work one-on-one with all my clients, asking questions (sometimes digging deep), and taking as much time necessary to define all details essential to create a resume that will get results. During the process, I have provided many of my clients advice and assistance with job search techniques, marketing, networking, interview preparation and etiquette, and reference/endorsement requests.

Each resume is individually crafted to fit the client’s individual situation and career objective. My mission is to get you the interview, and my reward is your success story once you are hired!

Specialties

Expertise in: resume and cover letter writing for educators, executives, engineers, program managers, manufacturing personnel, tradespeople, students, and other professionals.
