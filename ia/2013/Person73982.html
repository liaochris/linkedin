Kelly Mitchell's Summary

Kelly Mitchell, CEO/Founder of Agent Caffeine & BreveTV . Huffington Post Hawaii Blogger. Co-Author, Swanepoel Technology Report. Co-Author, REAL .

Recognized by Inman News as a Top 100 Most Influential Real Estate Leader 2013 in New York City for her groundbreaking work in her nationally acclaimed podcast Agent Caffeine. She recently founded BreveTV designed to help entrepreneurs & start ups with growth, strategy, tech, balance while elevating and shining a light on women.

She is a moderator and international speaker at numerous events including REX 2013 in Fort Worth, Texas, LEXI Women's Conference in Seattle on "The Art of ReInvention", Inman Connect NYC 2013, , Inman Connect SF 2012, 2013 and Banff Western Connection in Canada where she spoke on Podcasting for Real Estate, Reinventing Your Marketing and moderated a panel including Phil Soper CEO of Royal LaPage and Prudential Canada on Professionalism. She revels in sharing war stories of successfully traversing 4 industries, sharing victories, learning from failures and how-to's. She is an actively licensed real estate professional with 8 years of award-winning experience.She's a licensed agent/partner in Honolulu and has been a consistent award winning vet of real estate (top 3-5% of Oahu agents), prior to her return to tech & media.

She draws from 30 years of local Hawaii expertise and over 20 years of strategic marketing, business development and digital media. As a Honolulu resident for 36 years, she has extensive knowledge of the Hawaii market. She has successfully launched four startup companies, and provided consulting services for many top companies/brands in Honolulu.

She holds a B.B.A. in Marketing from Chaminade University of Honolulu,.

Specialties: Strategy, Digital Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Software Sales, Technology, Mobile, Sales & Marketing, Business Development, Start Up, Operations
