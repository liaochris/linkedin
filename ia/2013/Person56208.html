Ivan Nikkhoo's Summary

With over 29 years of industry experience in various senior capacities internationally, Ivan is a Managing Director at Siemer & Associates, a leading global merchant bank focused on digital media, Internet, and mobile technologies. In that capacity, he is responsible for origination, business development, deal sourcing, conversion, and executing client mandates.

Ivan is also a partner at a venture fund focused on early stage investing in emerging technology companies, and serves on the boards of several technology companies.

Prior to Siemer, Ivan was the Head of the Technology Practice at Barrington Associates, a division of Well Fargo Securities. Prior to WFS, he served as Portfolio Manager for N3 Technology Fund, a long/short hedge fund. Prior to N3, he was the GM at SOA Software, responsible for M&A, strategic alliances, and joint ventures. Prior to SOA, Ivan was the President of Vertex Systems focused on technology strategy, implementation, and IP monetization. Prior to Vertex, he was the Director of European Operations for TLD Systems in Paris, France, providing software for European space and military projects.

Ivan is a member of YPO, a Wexner Heritage Fellow, and was a finalist at the E&Y Entrepreneur of the Year Award. He serves on several boars boards including Digital Jersey, LAVA, LA Chamber of Commerce, USC Marshall Partners, Junior Achievement of Southern California, and Cedars-Sinai Medical Center.

Ivan is a frequent speaker at industry events including the INC 500, OnMedia, AlwaysOn, LAVA, among others. He is a regular guest lecturer at the USC Marshall School and has been featured in numerous publications including Fortune Magazine, LA Times, and the LA Business Journal.

Mr. Nikkhoo received his MBA from USC Marshall School of Business and his Bachelor’s of Engineering from McGill University. He has completed executive education programs at HBS and Stanford. He is a General Securities Principal licensed with FINRA/SIPC (formerly NASD).
