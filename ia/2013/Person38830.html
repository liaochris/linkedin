Dustin Steward's Summary

A firm foundation of creative design from California State University at Long Beach helped me to transition from theoretical design to real-world implementation. Since obtaining my bachelors in graphic design, I have worked extensively in the field of textile manufacturing, production and overseas development.

The fashion industry, which is both fast-paced and dynamic, has shaped my work ethic and philosophy. To challenge myself and to stay relevant in a changing industry, I constantly study trends and seek out ways to push the creative and technological boundaries of design. I have also been actively involved in a broader spectrum of design fields, including Web Design, Social Media and Online Marketing .

These combined experiences have also instilled a strong knowledge of brand development and an ability to follow work from its conceptual stages to production. I’ve utilized my knowledge of the commercial market to engage in several Start-Up companies wherein I served a number of roles.

Specialties: Creative Direction, Branding, Print design/production, Hardware and Labeling systems. Online Marketing Strategies including Social Media with the implementation of Photography, Video and Motion Graphics.

Labeltex Mills

Apparel & Fashion industry

April 2009 – December 2012 (3 years 9 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

StudioX was formed in 2008 as a subdivision of Labeltex Mills Inc, one of the strongest Fashion Trim Manufacturers in the Los Angeles area. Our core strengths include branding, packaging and mechanical development of trim products. With locations spread all over the world, material sourcing and new technique exploration is daily practice. We emphasize the ability to design and produce sophisticated products in both soft-goods and metals. Much of our trim designed in LA, and manufactured in our factories are considered the highest quality and most innovative in the fashion market.

The goal of the department is to continuously expand the boundaries of trim production, explore new techniques and incorporate digital media and devices.

This year StudioX has undergone many beneficial adaptations including facility expansion, adoption of multimedia software presentation and an evolved web and social media presence. In the years coming you can expect far more advances in digital media, manicured portfolios and streamline client communication tools.
