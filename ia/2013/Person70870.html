Julie Roehm's Summary

Julie Roehm is the Chief Storyteller, SVP of Marketing for SAP. Previous to this, she founded and ran a marketing consulting firm providing expertise in all facets of the business strategy and marketing execution. Julie Roehm's past experience includes work for automotive, retail, financial services, telecommunications, start-ups, private equity and VC's, media companies and agencies.
