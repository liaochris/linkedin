David Harrington's Summary

Most of adult life has been spent working for Walter P Moore and Associates in Tampa, Florida, USA. I have worked on numerous structural engineering projects all over the USA and a few overseas. The bulk of that work has been using AutoCAD with a high level of customization. Recently I have been using Revit Structure and rather enjoy that as well. Additionally I use 3D Studio Max for visualization needs and can program a bit in AutoLISP.

My only other side occupation has been writing. I've done technical editing and writing for over 15 years on several publications and written a few AutoCAD books as well. Currently I'm co-authoring a book on Revit Structure as well.

Specialties

My primary expertise is structural design, with added elements of system management, problem solving and technical writing.
