Jeffrey J. Phillips, PMP's Summary

Jeffrey J. Phillips is a program management, product planning, and business development executive who has worked at AT&T Bell Labs, NEC, Sony, Microsoft, Pace, and Technicolor. He has directed cross-functional, multi-cultural, and geographically dispersed teams developing and deploying products and solutions to happy, engaged, and satisfied customers. His career includes best-in-class products such as Microsoft interactive television middleware, Sony VAIO personal computers, and NEC PowerMate personal computers. His startup experience includes Hager Telecommunications, Inc., where he developed ADPCM and SBC voice response systems; Urocket, where he designed hardware and software for an Internet music appliance; and TransformTec, where he founded a systems software consultancy, technology brokerage and product development company.

Jeff started his career in the early days of the UNIX operating system at AT&T Bell Labs after earning his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Wentworth Institute of Technology in Boston, MA. He has a strong technical background with field-proven commercial expertise, and translation-level fluency in English and Spanish.

Specialties

Strategy, Planning, Development, Team Building, P+L Management
