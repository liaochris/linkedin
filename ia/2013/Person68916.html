Josh Eyre's Summary

Josh seeks the sublime in the links between man and object. Regardless of interaction; be it with a 3-dimensional object, or a 2-dimensional surface, a pleasurable experience is paramount. Not a moment goes by in a day where we are not touching, squeezing, pushing, pulling, or reading an object. His goal is to make these interactions lovely, sensual, easy, purposeful, and, ultimately, memorable.

Specialties: Josh draws exceptionally well and clearly communicates ideas on paper and digitally. He is highly proficient in numerous creative and technical software packages and quickly and competently tackles new ones. After dealing with many demanding clients as a staff designer and freelancer, in high pressure situations, Josh is adaptable in changing environments and ready from the start to deal with presentations, any client or internal interactions, and multiple projects on tight deadlines.
