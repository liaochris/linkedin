Mary Kelly, PhD/Leadership Speaker/Economist's Summary

Motivational leadership and business speaker to improve profits, growth, and success. Provides economic forecasting and business projections.

Author/co-author/editor of 10 books on business, leadership, productivity, and personal finance, Mary and the team at Productive Leaders improve profit growth through keynote speaking, training and coaching.

Call Mary to speak at your next conference, meeting or event, or to have Mary EmCee your banquet. She is also really funny. Push your business forward with new ideas and strategies. Call Mary!

Find Mary at:
Mary@ProductiveLeaders.com
719-357-7360
www.ProductiveLeaders.com

Mary’s passion is helping people and their organizations reach their full productive potential. As a practicing economist, Mary finds efficiencies, solves challenging problems, and motivates people to implement change.

Mary LOVES being a motivational leadership speaker!

Through engaging, humorous, and action-packed presentations, breakout sessions, focused training, and one-on-one consulting, Mary transforms corporate cultures and increases productivity.

President of the Colorado Springs Express Network, Mary also serves on the finance committee for the National Speakers Association and as a board member for the Colorado Speakers Association.

 Mary taught at the U.S. Naval Academy, the U.S. Air Force Academy, and Hawaii Pacific University.   She is the head coach for the U. S. Air Force Academy Equestrian Team.

The proceeds from her book sales go to animal rescues (All Breed Rescue, Take Me Home Pet Rescue, and Best Friends) and to orphanages in SouthEast Asia (Together We Can Change the World).

She served 21 years on active duty in the Navy (retired as a commander) with specialties in Human Resources, Logistics, Intelligence, and Security.

Her PhD is in Economics.

Specialties: Leadership and economic development, marketing strategies and communications, motivational keynote speaking, management and executive coaching.
