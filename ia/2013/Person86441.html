Founder, Career Innovator, Counselor and Consultant

High School and Beyond.com

January 2002 – Present (11 years 9 months)

I have worked with a variety of high school and career professionals in helping them learn about career development and offerings at their schools around the United States. Integrating technology and 21st century skills into the their programs helps improve the connection they have with the world outside of academics and makes for a solid and success career program. In addition, I have trained over 100 individuals to become Career Development Facilitators to be leaders in their school with career development programs and knowledge for their schools. I have worked with career professionals from across the United States in sharing best practices and expanding the CDF network.

As the Career Services Educator, I have offered services to students, teachers and parents over the past 12 years. I am always looking to find ways to integrate new programs and ideas related to 21st century career skills into the curriculum and programs offered to the students. Helping students connect career skills to their learning leads them to greater success in school and beyond.

Career Counselor, School Counselor and School-to-Career Coordinator

Amity Regional High School

August 1998 – October 1999 (1 year 3 months)

During my time at Amity, I held a variety of positions. I was in charge of resurrecting the career center and developing a program for grades 7-12. A survey was created to find out what was already offered and what the students needed and slowly pieces were put together for the foundation of a successful program. In my first year, a Partners in Education committee was formed where we had over 118 businesses commit to working with Amity to provide successful career information and experiences for the students.
