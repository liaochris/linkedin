Michael Mayes' Summary

Architect turned Product Leader. Building the future of web-based enterprise marketplaces for web and mobile. Focused on design, usability, user experience.

Architects work with clients, developers, builders, contractors, etc. They build models and draft plans to communicate a vision. My role in product design is to be the architect of the web and mobile experience. To bring the vision to life.

Product design means more than writing a spec. Its idea > prototype > test > tweak > implement > learn. I believe Product needs to understand the code; to be able to talk with users/clients; to see the design. To do it all.
