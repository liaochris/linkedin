Geeke van der Sluis' Summary

Seven years experience as a PR & Marketing Communication professional within the Telecom and IT industry in The Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg.

Built strong relationships with media organisations, telecommunication journalists (online/offline), bloggers, influencers and third parties like Consumer Unions. Worked at two different PR agencies (tech & lifestyle oriented) for national and international clients such as Samsung, Vodafone, Dell and Verizon Business.

Currently, I’m responsible for Marketing PR within the Marketing Communication department of Vodafone Netherlands. As Marketing PR Manager I build communication campaigns for the Consumer and Enterprise Business Unit. Advising about communication strategies, implement and execute PR activities (i.a. setting up media training, interviews, writing press releases), set up content strategies + content creation (corporate blogs, animation videos, infographics, social releases) and work on campaign activations (online/offline).

Other activities: budget management, close cooperation with Event-, Creative-, Media-, and PR agencies, brief boardmembers on upcoming media interviews and work on medialoan program.
