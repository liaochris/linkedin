Carl Willis' Summary

I am the President of Simplicity Marketing LLC a for profit enterprise that utilizes network marketing and E-commerce to further the non-profit work of church planting and the development of church leaders. I am also the creator of The Cheapskates Guide to Online Marketing, Why You Should Buy Physical Gold And Silver, the Create Your Blog Now course and The Marketing Classroom. I love coaching people to greater levels of success in their business.

In addition to my marketing work, I speak frequently at international conferences for pastors and church planters. I have authored and self-published two inspirational books (The Troubles of a Righteous Man and Lessons From the Storm).
