Carlana Charles-Young's Summary

Thank you for visiting my profile!

I'm Carlana Charles and I work with small businesses, solo-professionals and entrepreneurs. As a savvy small business owner and virtual service provider I am passionate about the growth and development of small businesses and the entrepreneurial spirit and since 2006, when I started my first business catering to the support needs of small businesses and entrepreneurs I have been privileged to support scores of small businesses, entrepreneurs and professionals grow their businesses and leverage their time much more effectively.

The services are designed to propel the growth of small businesses and give owners to time and support to focus on the revenue generating aspects of their businesses. When you enlist my support, you benefit from my wealth of knowledge and resources. Modern businesses need a wide range of services and support. Oftentimes, this can result in the small business owner feel overworked and overwhelmed. My team and I help them to develop a different approach to running their businesses by taking care of tasks and responsibilities that do not make up the core of their business.

Specialties: Administration, Virtual PA, Project Management, Video Marketing and Video Creation, Social Media Marketing, Internet Marketing, Customer Service Support and Virtual and Online Events.
