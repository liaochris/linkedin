Dale Fox's Summary

As the CEO of Tribogenics (www.tribogenics.com), I lead an exceptional team that is revolutionizing X-ray technology across a $20B market space encompassing major industries such as recycling, mining, military, medical imaging, and security. Tribogenics has developed a technology that eliminates the need for high voltage, allowing us to create miniature, low-cost X-ray sources that drive products and solutions unattainable with existing X-ray sources. Tribogenics technology is based on a DARPA-funded initiative that originated at UCLA and the company is venture-backed by prominent investors, including Peter Thiel’s Founders Fund.

I’m a seasoned startup entrepreneur and inventor with a proven record of turning ideas into revenue generating businesses. Successfully launched five companies, brought over two dozen products to market, built world-class executive and advisory teams, raised numerous rounds of venture and debt funding and enjoyed successful exits. Love helping young entrepreneurs get off the ground and routinely mentor a handful of promising early-stage companies.

On a personal front, I’m a perpetual optimist and a believer in a brighter future through technology. Love golden retrievers, great food, mid-century design, amazing coffee, sunny destinations, and nearly every electronic gadget I’ve laid eyes upon.

Tribogenics is a transformative X-ray technology company developing affordable and highly portable solutions for materials analysis and imaging. The Tribogenics range of X-ray sources includes the X-Change™ cartridge, the world’s smallest turnkey X-ray source designed for use in revolutionary new XRF systems. Tribogenics was founded in 2011 and currently employs 17 scientists and engineers at a world-class R&D facility in Marina Del Rey, CA. Our advisors include luminaries in business, scientific and medical fields. The company is backed by a consortium of investors and recently closed a $6.2M round led by Peter Thiel's Founders Fund.
