Candace Moody's Summary

I'm a writer and public relations professional in Jacksonville, Florida.

I'm vice president, communications, for WorkSource, where I've been employed since 1997. WorkSource is the brand name for services provided through First Coast Workforce Development. I direct corporate communications for the organization, which serves six counties in Northeast Florida. I work closely with economic development organizations in Northeast Florida, providing labor market information and support to businesses considering locating or expanding in the area.

I did my undergraduate work at the University of Wisconsin, and joined WorkSource with over 12 years of experience in private industry. My background includes Human Resources, recruiting, training and assessment. I spent several years with a national staffing company, serving employers on both coasts and in the local Jacksonville area. I wrote a monthly column for the Jobs page of the Florida Times Union on career and employment issues. My writing has also appeared in the Atlanta Journal Constitution, 904 and Jacksonville Magazine, Enterprising Women Magazine and several national websites.

I have been married to my husband Thom, a sports massage therapist, since 1978.

Specialties

Content is the new marketing. Copy writing, interviews and creative writing. Through WorkSource, I provide technical assistance to workforce organizations in the areas of business services, marketing and public relations.
