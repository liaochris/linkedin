Constance Hoffman - Speaker, Coach, Author's Summary

Personalized group or individual training in social and business manners including dining, communication, and socialization. Focus on professionalism and building self-esteem and confidence. Learn the importance of body language and tone in your presentations. How to read the non-verbal signs to win the sale.
