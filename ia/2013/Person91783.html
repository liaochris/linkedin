Mathieu Gorge's Summary

Mathieu Gorge is the CEO and founder
of VigiTrust. He has been in the security
industry for the past 10 years. In 2003
Mathieu identified a gap in the market
to provide pro-active consultancy services
around key legal aspects of corporate security
such as compliance with international
data protection legislation as well as industry
security frameworks. He started VigiTrust whcih focuses on enabling organizations to achieve and maintain compliance with PCI DSS, PA-DSS, HIPAA and ISO 27001.

He is a regular speaker at international security conferences (RSA, ENISA, ISACA) and a well respected figure in the security industry in EMEA and North America.

Specialties

Very strong expertise in PCI DSS, HIPAA & ISO 27001
Completed extensive research work on Secure Printing - Data Dematerialization, ILM, Storage and Archiving

Councelor - Ireland France Chamber of Commerce since 2006
