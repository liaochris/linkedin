David Grewe III's Summary

The Purpose & Mission:

Built by a coach for coaches and athletes. Potential Meets Legacy (PMLegacy LLC) presents our Online Leadership Institute. Our motto is "WE BUILD CHAMPIONS", and our focus is on preparing athletes in reaching the next level through nation's most powerful platform of player development - building The 3 Dimensional Athlete. Our members are awarded two paths, on one journey, in earning college scholarships.

We are the home for all high school athletes (age 16-22) and all coaches. #gettingbetterneverstops

We have put together the most advanced and ONLY player development plan in existence today that focuses on building the character habits of elite athletes and champions by shaping mindset.

Our means of distribution is through our proactive Online Leadership Institute - open for ALL athletes and coaches at three different membership levels through a subscription-based format. For membership level info - visit this site: www.coachgrewe.com
Player Development Author:

1. eBook: 108 Ways Potential Meets Legacy

2. eBook: Champions Dictionary

3. Athletic Development Report - highlighting the knowledge, wisdom and truth in player development. It's highlights "the perfect storm" (the story of how and why a national championship was won) and the most important element in player development.

4. The 12 Principles of College Recruiting. This eBook is created in two parts. Part I is available to you after you download either our 108 Ways PML eBook or the Athletic Development Report. Part II is provided to our Legacy members of our Online Leadership Institute.

Access all FREE resources here: http://www.coachgrewe.com/freeresources/

My Passion: Created from both the success and failure of a 13-yr sport coaching career at the collegiate level, including roles as a Head Coach in the Big Ten Conference, the Associate Head Coach for a National Championship team, and an assistant coach at the University of Notre Dame.
