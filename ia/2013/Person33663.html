Debra Hammer McDevitt's Summary

DLM Innovative Arts is a small design studio owned and operated by independent graphic designer Debra McDevitt. DLM offers a broad range of marketing design services which utilize her 20+ years of experience, innovative ideas, and technical expertise.

Specialties

Debra’s mission is to convert ordinary advertising, web design, corporate identity and digital imaging, into the extraordinary.
