Eric Norton's Summary

I am a founding partner at Norton, Petersen & Furumoto LLP. Admitted to practice in California, I received my Juris Doctor from the Chapman University School of Law with an emphasis in Environmental, Land Use, and Real Estate Law. Prior to obtaining my law degree, I graduated from UCLA with a Bachelor of Science in Microbiology, Immunology, and Molecular Genetics.

I founded a copywriting business shortly after graduation from UCLA after discovering I preferred writing over lab work. It was a successful experiment and helped me discover my passion for entrepreneurialism—something I built my own practice on.

Today, as an attorney, I draw on my knack for communicating abstract ideas in comprehensible terms and my first-hand business knowhow to provide my clients with concise guidance and real-world solutions. I understand the importance of getting a job done right—and on budget—and I work closely with my clients to help them through their problems and provide solutions that work for everyone involved.

I primarily assist:

◆ ENTREPRENEURS: Let me help you start your business on the right foot. I see too many small businesses owners who know nothing about the laws governing their company, their employees, or their contractual obligations.

◆ SMALL BUSINESS OWNERS: A little legal work can go a long way to improving your business's success and ensuring the company you worked hard to build sticks around for many years to come.

◆ PROFESSIONALS: You're an expert in your field, so why not leave the legal work to us. We can help build an estate plan for your future and assist you in growing your business.

◆ INTERNET START-UPS: Though nobody reads them, I love to write Terms of Use and Privacy Policies, but I'm also at home helping the next great SaaS deal with VC funding and vendor contracts.

So if you need assistance with your venture, give me a call or send me an email. I love helping new business owners start out on the right foot.
