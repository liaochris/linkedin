Thomas Hayden's Summary

I'm a philosopher first, a hacker second, and a math nerd third. I've been writing code and building things since I was a young child, using BASIC a TANDY 2000. I worked my way through the early days of PHP3 and now work mostly in the world of analytics - my tools are Amazon Web Services, Hadoop, R, and Python.

I can build and maintain large object oriented code bases or complex scripting tools.

I love working in teams, interacting with new people, providing peer review and mentoring of younger employees. I like recruiting, interviewing, meeting new people and growing companies.

I work best in autonomous problem solving settings. Let's find a hard problem, get some technology and get to work on iteratively building the right solution as quickly as possible. Three to four month sprints are my optimal setting for projects.

In graduate school, I studied algorithmic game theory and networks -- a mixture of computer science, math, and micro-economics. I love the elegance and simplicity of math and am always trying to continually improve my math skillset. I'd love to learn more linear algebra.

I subscribe to no particular developer methodology besides focusing on impact, getting stuff done, and rapid iteration.

You can reach me via email: thayden@gmail.com

Specialties: Statistical Analysis, Time Series Analysis, Probability, Machine Learning, Decision Processes, Mathematical Economics, Incentive Centered Design, R, Mathematica, PHP, Python, Perl, C++, MySQL, Hadoop, Oracle, MongoDB, Object Oriented Programming
