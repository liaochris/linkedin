Adam Ware's Summary

Adam is one of the founders and the current CEO of SwellPath in Portland, Oregon. He has been working with digital marketing and web analytics for over a decade, in various roles and organizations. He now works with the talented team at SwellPath to help businesses measure and optimize their digital initiatives.

Specialties: Web analytics consulting, social analytics, PPC, paid search, online advertising, search engine optimization (SEO)
