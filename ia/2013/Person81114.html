Linda S. Velazquez, ASLA, GRP, LEED AP, is founder and publisher/editor of Greenroofs.com (1999) and writes and reports extensively about greenroofs. She holds a Bachelor’s of Landscape Architecture from the University of Georgia (2000) and an Associate of Arts in Business Administration from Miami Dade College (1982). She is a Member of the American Society of Landscape Architects (2000), a LEED AP, (2004), and a GRP, Green Roof Professional (2010).

Greenroofs.com is an internet news media organization: the international greenroof industry’s resource portal. Our Goal is to inform, promote and inspire the earth friendly technology of organic greenroof architecture through the free interchange of ideas, projects, news, video, travel, research, organization/government updates, and marketing opportunities. Exclusive features include guest articles, contributing editor columns, the Sky Gardens Blog, “The Week in Review,” and the WebTV series "Sky Gardens: Greenroofs of the World™."

Greenroofs.com publishes The Greenroof & Greenwall Projects Database, an international community resource; The Greenroof Directory of Manufacturers, Suppliers, Professional Services, Organizations, Students & Green Resources, with free and paid listings; the Greenroofs & Walls of the World™ Calendar series; greenroofs.tv, a video channel devoted to greenroofs and greenwalls, and the Greenroofs & Walls of the World™ Virtual Summit.

Linda’s role as Greenroofs.com publisher affords many opportunities to promote the industry; she has been published in numerous journals, magazines and online media, and has been interviewed or referenced in television, print, radio, and online sources.

Linda develops, writes and hosts the original greenroofs.tv web series “Sky Gardens – Greenroofs of the World™ (2008),” created the Sky Gardens ~ where cool green meets lofty blue Blog (2008), and is the designer of The Greenroof & Greenwall Projects Database (2004).
