The Art of Eating Through the Zombie Apocalypse

Writer

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Writing and Editing industry

February 2013 – Present (8 months) Brooklyn

Have you ever thought about what you will be eating in the event of an undead uprising? Of course you haven’t. When you imagine a world being overtaken by mindless, soulless, unrelenting undead predators, you’re probably not thinking about how it will impact your dinner plans. Naturally, your most immediate concerns become primarily focused on brain bashing and horde evasion.

And who could blame you? The demands of z-pocalyptic life will be so all-consuming, who will have time to plan a nutritious and energy-rich, let alone tasty, meal? Enter The Art of Eating Through the Zombie Apocalypse, a cookbook and general field guide for the busy zpoc survivor.

Just because the undead’s taste buds are atrophying, doesn’t mean yours need to.

Coming to a zpoc near you in the fall of 2014.
