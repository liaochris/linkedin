Jeff Kadlec's Summary

I love the coaching youth sports and have a 11 year old son that I am helping achieve "his dream" of one day being a professional athlete (my backup retirement plan).

I am presently redeveloping the sales force for Shea Mortgage and seeking out talented people. I founded and own Kadlec Holdings Group and Trifold Holdings, LLC, since 2004 I have been a real estate investor gaining a broad experience of the foreclosure process and negotiating with Realtors, contractors, investors and lenders. I have analyzed, purchased, resold and rented thousands of distressed properties from the court houses & banks in target areas for myself and other investors for a profit over the years.

As an executive for over 25 years I have hands-on operations, business management, sales, marketing and planning experience in real estate, mortgage banking and technology.

I started my career as Wall Street Financial Analysts, in my corporate career, I have exceeded with small, mid-sized and large companies at all stages of development, including KB & Ryland Mortgage as Branch Manager & Senior Staff Member of the Mortgage, Title and Sales teams overseeing a 150 person origination, underwriting and escrow division.

I have built, trained and led sales teams, developed and introduced new products and marketing programs and have extensive experience in efficiency improvement to enable organizations to grow.

I've been fortunate over the years to have built an extensive close personal network of leaders as well as up-and-coming executives which allows me to plug into at any time for guidance and clarity.

My muse since 2009 involves precious metals as I believe right now is a great time to hedge against the dollar with commodities. I collect numismatics as well as buy gold and silver bullion. I love connecting with like minded people that crave to learn how to preserve and protect their families wealth and have more desires than excuses!
