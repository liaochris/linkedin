Kazuki Takizawa's Summary

I am a Los Angeles based glassblower and glass designer who creates top-notch hand-blown glassworks and teaches glassblowing classes to students of all levels.

My work includes but are not limited to:
- Custom Wedding Goblets
- Functional Glassware (such as vases, tumblers, bowls, plates and candle holders)
- Glass Lighting Components
- Glass Installation (Residential and Commercial)
- Public Art
- Contemporary Glass Sculpture

Glassblowing classes I offer:
- Introduction to Glassblowing (a.k.a Paperweight Workshop)
- Make Your Own Tumbler Workshop
- Make Your Own Glass Heart
- Mother's Day Flower Making Workshop
- Glass Pumpkin Workshop
- Advanced Goblet Making Workshop

I have worked and taught in several different glass art facilities across the nation including Pilchuck Glass School (WA), Punahou School (HI) and Public Glass (CA). My work is represented by FreeHand Gallery in Beverly Hills, California. For inquiry regarding pricing or commissioning a project, please contact me at info@kazukitakizawa.com
