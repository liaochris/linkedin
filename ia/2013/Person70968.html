Juli Schulz's Summary

Juli Schulz is an award-winning brand marketing and communications professional with over fifteen years of experience in youth, sports entertainment and automotive industries. The 2010 recipient of Surfrider's Wavemaker Award for Coastal Impact and environmental stewardship, she is committed to creating sustainably-minded marketing initiatives, communications and experiences.

Juli thrives working with teams to develop integrated marketing campaigns for brands, agencies and non-profit companies. She is a specialist in monetizing creative ideas.

Recent projects in which she led from conceptualization to execution include: Gardein 'Cheat on Meat' sampling campaign in Los Angeles, re-launch of AMP Energy Drink in 25 markets, Rethink: Green Charity Event for the Environmental Charter Schools, Toyota national event activations, Disney's Oceans College Tour, Shangri-La/Citadel Press Event; Plastics Are Forever Youth Conference and many more.

Speaker and educator, who is continually educating herself on sustainability challenges and solutions. Juli is a speaker and teacher for Surfrider Foundation's Rise Above Plastics Program in Los Angeles.

Interested in developing innovative strategies, marketing initiatives and products that make a financial, social and environmental difference in the world.

Specialties: Experiential marketing and communications pro. Able to clearly communicate complex, high-impact, thought-provoking ideas and strategies, visually online and offline experiences. Skills include: marketing, strategy, promotions, sponsorships, partnerships, social media, website development, team building and account management. Highly organized and action oriented; thrive in a fast-paced environment.
