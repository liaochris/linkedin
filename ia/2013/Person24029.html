Animal Rescue & Veteran Support Services, Corp.© is dedicated to transforming the lives of wounded and Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) U.S. Veterans through equine interaction and job opportunities.

Veteran Rescue is dedicated to help heal wounded and Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) affected vets through physical and psychological therapies accomplished on horseback. Intrinsically, these programs will provide a health service-based need to Military vets, government, public, and corporate agencies.

We will be helping to heal our veterans who where wounded, those who are afflicted with PTSD and their families within those communities; this will be accomplished through equine therapy designed to reduce their symptoms. Promisingly, our programming will lower suicide rates with vets while providing hope and independence to all people involved in our programs through medically and psychologically acknowledged therapeutic equine therapy. At the same time, we will save rescue horses, this melding of rescued horses and military personnel who served will serve us all as Americans who care for our service people and for the majestic horse which is a part of the U.S. past and future.
