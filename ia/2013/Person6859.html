Andrew Owens' Summary

Senior aerospace engineering leader with an enthusiasm for developing advanced prototype solutions within straightforward business and technologically agile environments. Expertise ranges from detailed aerospace engineering design and systems engineering backgrounds to balancing skills in agile methodologies, risk management, and project management. A multidisciplinary and creative problem solving approach continues to differentiate holistic solutions from otherwise conventional or single discipline perspectives.

I work as an engineer within the aerospace sector; however, my long term career focus best fits the titles of Aerospace Technologist & Industrial Psychologist. The cross-over between applied science and engineering represents a unique area of continuing professional interest. I am studying specializations within the field of decision science for strategic application in small to medium sized technology companies.

I believe efficiently advancing technology requires a focused vision with a team of diverse talents. My mission is to foster the integration of core engineering talent and applied scientific practice to help achieve innovation of global significance.
