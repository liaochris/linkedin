Alex Kosiorek's Summary

For detailed information, visit http://alexkosiorek.com/

As a published engineer, producer and manager, Alex Kosiorek is specializes in projects and initiatives that promote excellence utilizing advanced production techniques. Alex’s expertise incorporates codec implementation including online streaming, surround sound, live broadcasting, direction of short form and long form productions, systems design, multimedia as well as copyright compliance.

Multifaceted and wide-ranging supervisory and leadership history, including complete oversight of live recordings, end-to-end management of production facilities construction, coordination of design teams and personnel, and close affiliation with dozens of recognized arts institutions.

With years of experience, he has completed many industry firsts including: the 1st audio engineer for the New World Symphony, instrumental in the 1st radio station to broadcast surround in the US, created one of the first high fidelity classical streams on the internet and oversaw the creation of the 1st THX-PM3 certified studio in a conservatory worldwide.

As a conservatory-trained musician and an adherent of close teamwork, Alex brings an intimate knowledge of creation to the table, enabling the visioning and completion of projects under budget and exceeding expectations.

Alex regularly contributes to trade publications, the Audio Engineering Society and the National Association of Broadcasters. He is regularly contracted as an independent engineer & consultant and is known to ensure the success of projects from inception to conclusion.

Specialties

Audio recording and mastering, video capture and production, digital audio and video editing, surround sound production and delivery, audio and video codec implementation, audio processing (including broadcast), broadcast program production, system design, and multimedia content delivery (including mobile devices.)
