Jim Herrera | Entrepreneur | Marketer's Summary

Jim is a senior executive with over 30+ years of experience at large companies - Apple Inc., Creative Labs, Arbitron - along with several startup ventures. He catalyzes marketing strategies, strategic alliances, business partnerships that increase visibility, higher lead generation and better product conversion. His entrepreneurial mindset and energy fosters collaboration and teamwork.

Jim empowers entrepreneurs - or intrapreneurs - define their business vision, purpose and goals based on their personal and professional values. He then designs and develops strategic partnerships, marketing initiatives and social network programs that enable entrepreneurs build their businesses.

Jim has developed several processes that empower entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs. These processes empower users build the business they intend because it is aligned with their insights and strengths.

Marketing Doyen
Successful marketing is the synergistic implementation of technology and human resources within a defined strategy and set of planned tactics that achieve demonstrable business goals. Jim has successfully crafted and coordinated deployment of corporate and product branding, messaging and positioning across multi-functional teams.

Strategic Alliance Expert
21st century marketing excellence is built upon key human and technology resources from within and without the organization. Jim is extremely adept at identifying key personnel within the organization and fostering alliances; from C-Level to individual contributor, Jim identifies their needs and provides a benefit to each party that encourages them to participate in the program at hand.

Superior Business Developer
Deploying successful marketing solutions requires strong relationships with customers and clients that focus on long term win-win strategies. Jim quickly identifies and prioritizes key corporate issues and incorporates them into to win-win marketing strategies and appropriate marketing tactics.
