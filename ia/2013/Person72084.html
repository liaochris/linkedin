Karen Hands' Summary

Making things happen at work can be a challenge, whether you're part of a large corporate team or running your own small enterprise.

I started out life as a chemical engineer, making big changes to the environmental performance of chemical factories and learning how to gain co-operation through teamwork along the way. I trained as a Coverdale teamwork coach to support my role as a line manager and project manager, so when I first set up Thought into Action in 2003, my clients were typically scientists and engineers who needed to develop their people skills as they rose into management positions.

I still love working in industry with technical managers and teams who can really benefit from my innovative thinking and my logical approach to working with people, but I know that owner-managers in small businesses face similar problems of broadening their skill-set as their business grows. This recognition led me to establish Thought into Action for Smaller Companies, aimed at micro-businesses and employers of small numbers of people.

These days I also offer speaking skills training as I know debilitating an effect it can have when you feel intimidated about speaking up in meetings or at networking events.

So if you've got to the point where you want to take your career or your business to the next level and you know that what you currently think and do is holding you back, please connect with me and start to aim higher!

Specialties:
Coaching you and your team to find creative solutions to the complex problems you face in your business, using my trademark Thought into Action System of teamwork coaching.
Framing change in a strategic context so that you get the big picture and take action
Facilitating a fundamental review to uncover the problems affecting your business right now
Speaking skills training to empower individuals to speak up about what they have to say.
Addressing the gender agenda through surveys, coaching, teamwork and speaking skills.
UK-based.

Thought into Action for Smaller Companies

Owners and managers of smaller companies can find it hard to step away from the front-line activities which were the reason for them founding the business, yet ultimately you cannot make every decision.

Whether you run a micro-business and need to work out what to say at networking events to describe what you do; or whether you employ a few people and need to delegate more effectively to focus on your strengths, please take a look at www.thoughtintoaction.co.uk for ideas, resources, videos and free downloads.

Phenomenal Woman speaking skills training - speak with passion and poise

Phenomenal Woman

January 2011 – Present (3 years) North West England

Do you dread having to stand up and speak in front of a roomful of people? I used to feel the same and lots of women are told they lack the confidence for public speaking. Even those of us who have become proficient through necessity will recognise that there's more to presenting than standing up and talking if we are to master the full power of influence over others.

If you recognise yourself in this description, please join the Phenomenal Woman campaign to raise the standard of speaking skills in business women around North West England.

I run monthly Speaking Clubs for women in Clitheroe and Blackburn; lunchtime seminars to inspire you to learn how to speak with passion and poise; and in-company workshops for those who can't commit to monthly speaking club membership.

Phenomenal Woman is a collaborative partnership between Catherine Sandland of White Hart Training Ltd and myself.
