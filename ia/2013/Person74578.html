Ken Watson, CMPS's Summary

Ken Watson is the principal of Ken Watson Lending, a branch of Sierra Pacific Mortgage. He holds the designation of Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist (CMPS) from the CMPS Insititute and is a member of The Core Training group. Through the use of mortgage planning and equity management tools, Ken has been advising and helping clients create wealth and increase household cash flow for over 17 years. He is considered an expert on all aspects of residential mortgage lending. He currently manages a large client base of repeat and referral clients and partners with local Realtor's, Financial Planner's and Tax Planner's in helping their clientele. Ken is well versed in conventional, FHA, VA and reverse mortgage lending products and is available to help on any mortgage needs you may have.
