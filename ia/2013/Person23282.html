Chris Forman's Summary

Over a quarter of a century of software development experience at all levels including over 10 years Team Lead experience at most of the companies I've worked for. I've also been involved in the development, implementation and monitoring of development procedures at all the companies I have worked for. Over the last few years I have come to see the value of Agile development practices and Test Driven Development, but realise that they are not the universal panacea that some of their more ardent proponents make out. As developers we need to keep looking out for new techniques and always try to adopt best practice.

I've worked for firms of 6 employees and firms of 6000 employees, and all levels in between.

I've programmed in many languages and on many operating systems over the years.

I have a interest in Usability and the user experience and like to put my knowledge of and skills in UI design to good effect.

I'm not currently looking for new opportunities.

Specialties

C#, WinForms, Silverlight, WPF, Linq, WCF, UI design, SQL, Microsoft Sync Framework, XML, Agile, C++, HTML.
