Kane Ford's Summary

I'm a creative professional living in sunny Southwest Florida, though my work can be found all over the world. While I love design work of all kinds, I excel at organizing information and making interfaces pretty (and easy to use). My typical responsibilities include:

Primary

- Interfacing with clients to identify, clarify, simplify, and distill a brand or product's essence and develop a supporting user experience strategy

- Interfacing with users to collect and analyze metrics in order to cultivate and enforce brand and product loyalty through improved user experience (QUIS, PUEU, CSUQ, USE and PUTQ)

- Combine the above research with psychology and UX/UI/IA best practices to take a consumer-centric approach to helping clients make strategy decisions, acting as a change agent that better aligns operational mechanics with goals and expectations to maximize user retention and brand loyalty

- Closely manage, direct, and work with fellow designers, developers, and product strategists to build, implement, and audit user experience improvement campaigns and develop future-proof information architecture solutions

- Generate user experience documentation such as wireframes, persona definitions, user journeys/stories, and task enumeration

Ancillary

- Thorough auditing and analysis of current UX/IA design and content in order to make informed strategic recommendations

- Assist clients with interpretation of research findings and analytics to better inform content decisions

- Create workflows and oversee development of UX/IA and content improvements

- Develop lasting plans for legacy content, partner content, and new content

- Design innovative pathways for diverting content flow to various media while supporting branding

- Help to create content and data taxonomies and metadata frameworks

- Develop nomenclature, make recommendations for messaging, and create style/data/metadata guides
