Kelly Klaiber's Summary

I currently own and operate two businesses - One Klaiber Wealth Management where I specialize in retirement planning and the other is a business my husband and started in December of 2009 - CarolinasPartyRide. Both allow me the opportunity to work out of my home so I can take care of my parents.

Our Limo-Bus is quite unique in the Charlotte area. With room for 24, 6'3" height clearance, wrap-around leather seating, in-motion satellite system and three bar areas we have become very popular with those clients wanting something special.

CarolinasPartyRide has a new name - Carolina's Executive Limo-line. We are still the same fun company but have added a corporate division.

Specialties

Specialize in working with clients near or in retirement. In 2007 one of few invited to attend a week long advanced class on advising the retirees from the Wharton School in Pennsylvania.
