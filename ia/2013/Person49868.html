Damian Brittleton's Experience

Owner

Revive Creative (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Graphic Design industry

September 2009 – Present (4 years) Stoke-on-Trent, United Kingdom

Revive Creative, a graphic and brand design service, based in Staffordshire.

Coming from a studio based background with 17 years of experience, we believe it is vital to develop the working relationship and rapport with our clients.

By taking the time to listen to their needs and working together to achieve what is required from a brief, Revive Creative can give our client the best possible high quality, considered design solution, in the most cost effective way.

Including the client throughout the design process is pivotal in making sure the brief stays in sight and we never run down the wrong tracks taking a brief too far, incurring extra time and costs.

We also offer an on and off-site freelance support service, to complement a team when an agency or studio is stretched.

We are based in North Staffordshire, making Revive easily accessible to Staffordshire, Cheshire, Manchester, West Midlands, Derbyshire and in this ‘on-line’ age, anywhere in the UK.
