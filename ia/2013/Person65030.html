Joao Pedro Canhenha's Summary

My name is Pedro Canhenha.

I've been working as an Interactive Designer/UI Designer/Visual Designer/Art Director since the year 2001.

During my working experience, I've worked with companies of Web, Multimedia, Advertising, Marketing, Film oriented products, Software Development and Mobile apps, and as such I've amassed a considerable knowledge of several crafts.

The more relevant examples of my work can be seen in my portfolio - www.joaopedrocanhenha.com.
You can also check more of my work at the Behance network: www.behance.net/Canhenha/frame.
I also have additional work that I can disclose upon request.

Some of the Websites that I've designed and programmed have been featured in the International Web Design Books "Web Design Index By Content 2008" and "Web Design Index Vol.8". I have also had sites published in several web design portals throughout the web and have had tutorials published in the magazines Photoshop PSD and Photoshop Projects. My illustrations are also available on the image bank, ImageZoo. The apps I've worked on, are also currently available on iTunes/Apple Store and Android Store.

I consider myself a hard-working person and I'm highly motivated to develop projects in the Visual Design/Interactive Design, Mobile World and Motion Graphics areas. I thrive to create products of impeccable design & functionality and pristine execution.

Specialties: User Interface, Visual Design, User Experience, Usability, Web Design, Print Design, Motion Graphics, HTML, CSS, FBML, Adobe Package (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Flash, Dreamweaver, Fireworks), Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, Sound Edit, Strata 3D, Final Cut Pro, Apple Motion, DVD Studio Pro, Office package, File Maker Pro, Bryce, Poser, QuarkXpress, Adobe Edge, Adobe Encore
