Darby Felton's Summary

Darby Felton has professional experience working with people and information technologies since 1996. During this time he has performed in various related roles, including web application and software development, agile project management and consulting, systems design, administration and analysis, instruction, and training. In 2003 he founded an independent consultancy named Deftek to provide high-quality business solutions based in open source technologies. Darby holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Missouri - Columbia, is a Zend Certified Engineer (PHP 4, PHP 5, and Zend Framework), and is a member of the Zend Framework Education Advisory Board.

Specialties

PHP, Zend Framework, Flex, ActionScript, JavaScript, Apache, MySQL, UNIX (FreeBSD, CentOS, Debian, Redhat, Cobalt, AIX, IRIX)
