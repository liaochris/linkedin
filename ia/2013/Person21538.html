Ceri Booth's Summary

Known for her responsive and flexible copywriting service, Ceri works with the marketing departments of SMEs and global manufacturers. She writes both creative and technical B2B and B2C copy for all types of marketing and PR literature.

Through The White Paper Company, which specialises in writing B2B white papers, Ceri provides marketing managers with the ultimate lead generation tool. Her ability to understand and thoroughly evaluate complex problems and solutions, and make a powerful and persuasive argument through an informative white paper, makes her a valuable asset to B2B marketing teams.

Ceri has raised more than £15,000 for children's charities and was named Young Welsh Female Entrepreneur of the Year at the Women Who Mean Business Awards 2010.
