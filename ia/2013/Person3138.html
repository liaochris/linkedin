Alan Ibbotson's Summary

The Trampoline Group are on a mission to make work a great place to spend half your life.

We do this in a number of ways:

- Helping executives and organizations make positive, sustainable change through organizational development consulting, employer branding and employee engagement initiatives.

- Helping individuals and teams identify and reach their potential through a series of transformational and highly engaging workshops, seminars and keynote events.

My job as Chief Changeologist is to help managers and leaders in client companies identify the outcomes they're looking for, then engage their people in the process so that they become committed rather than simply compliant. The difference between those two things can generally be seen on a P&L statement...

Our values truly drive the customer experience : Possibility, Innovation, Action and Enjoyment — so please don't call us if you're not ready for boundless optimism, creative solutions, rapid execution and a few laughs along the way!

Our client list is representative of a broad range of industry sectors - media, entertainment, publishing, e-commerce, consumer packaged goods, health and wellness, professional sports, retail, finance, apparel, branding and not for profit.
Most of our clients are Fortune 500 organizations but we also work with small and medium sized companies.

I am always happy to make a new professional friend, regardless of whether we end up working together - so don't be afraid to call for some free advice!

Thanks for dropping by,
