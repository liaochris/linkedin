Robin Hjelmeir's Summary

I view myself as gentle and caring, but fiercely protective of my fellow co-workers (and friends). Being a web developer is my dream job. I love the challenge, the growth, and ever changing creativity in this business. I grew up a free spirit, went to college in Hawaii with the goal of being an artist working on computers. Art is my happy place. I dream of staying in this development field where ever the technology takes us.

My strengths are in front-end web development. I mainly use PHP, Ruby and recently C# languages. My passion is taking a beautiful site design and crafting that design into a beautifully flowing piece of art full of moving parts. I am strong with coding standards. I will use the latest technologies, like HMTL5, with cascading fallbacks (those darn older browsers).
