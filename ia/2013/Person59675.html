Javier Carrera (jcarrera@hiddenbed.com)'s Summary

My activity consists in finding the best ways to record data from the daily operations of the company with as little bureaucracy as possible, if necessary adapting existing procedures of employees gradually to avoid frustrations. Alongside designing automated systems that extract this data, process and convert it in useful indicators for the director or management team. These indicators may include performance of the different sections, stocks, expenses, supplier and client accounts, and status of the processes developing the products or services.

This system is hosted in "the cloud" (Internet), making it accessible from any computer or smartphone with a web browser. I can also implement order status tracking systems for customers with web access from the site of the company (like Fedex online tracking).

I'm looking to start doing this freelance in the evening hours. I want to meet or talk to any person who believes his company has any potential to improve in operations efficiency, management, tracking or traceability to bring in what I can and gain experience.
