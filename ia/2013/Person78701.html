Laura Davis' Summary

My transformational leadership coaching and training company assists individuals, teams, and organizations in growing leaders at all levels to develop talent and to create engaged and aligned workplace cultures. My associates and I provide coaching and training for executives, high potentials, and emerging leaders in business, education, and government.

I possess 18 plus years of executive leadership and team development training and coaching experience for Fortune 500 companies in a wide variety of industries. I have worked with people at all levels of an organization to help prepare them for the new world of work. I have been an ICF Master Certified Coach and Master Facilitator since 1998. I also have an MBA from Emory University and am a Board Certified Coach.

I assist clients in accessing greater self-awareness and emotional intelligence as well as in developing solid skills for leading and collaborating in today's complex, virtual, global, and rapidly changing workplace.

Using a values-based approach, I offer Corporate Coach U's "The Coaching Clinic" and many other training, coaching, and DiSC-Based solutions to assist leaders to bring out the best in themselves and in others. Leadership is about positive influence and can be taught and cultivated through a variety of tools and techniques and mindset shifts.

The mindset shift required involves a change from a strictly hierarchical "command and control" style of leadership to more of a situational leadership "Coach Approach." I also work to create powerfully effective results by working with leaders' mental models and paradigms in order to create lasting, sustainable solutions to their “people problems."

These approaches cultivate the kind of breakthrough-thinking and creativity and innovation so needed in business today. Ethical and socially responsible actions are another outcome of a more inclusive, collaborative approach.
