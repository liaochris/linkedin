James Gasson's Summary

As a dynamic, intelligent, energetic and hard-working person I have demonstrated through both the sustained achievement of academic excellence and subsequent professional engagements that I am a talented and skilled dedicate of the art and science of music production and multimedia sound engineering. Inspired by a life-long love of music, I grew to become an accomplished singer, guitarist, drummer and songwriter, before instincts guided me into sound recording and manipulation, for which I fast developed an equal passion and a keen ear for detail. The lessons learnt along this journey now afford me a great deal of artistic, creative and professional empathy when working in all sound design and music production environments. I have dedicated my life to my work and as such I am perpetually driven to continue learning, accept and accomplish new challenges, and be the best practitioner I can be.
