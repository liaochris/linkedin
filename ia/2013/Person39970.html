Ed Yahn's Summary

Ed mixes creative thinking, digital strategy, user-centered design, visual identity, and technology expertise into a unique approach to product design and brand consulting. For over 12 years, Ed has worked with businesses, non-profits, and government organizations to create integrated experiences through brand identity, websites, social media, mobile, multimedia, data visualization, exhibits, and print. His experience planning and managing all phases and aspects of product development has led to a portfolio of award-winning projects and new business through trusting client relationships.

Ed excels in growing new profitable business capabilities and building infrastructures to support them. His expertise in growth strategy, proposal writing, operations, mentoring, team building techniques, and marketing has resulted in the creation of new second teams in his last two places of employment.
