Anne Cavazos' Summary

Has more than 25 years of professional experience conducting and managing hydrogeologic and environmental projects for government, municipal, and commercial clients. Experienced in successfully representing clients to local, state, and federal regulators. Manage multi-million dollar projects, designed and managed numerous investigations of soil and groundwater conditions to assess the nature and extent of contamination from petroleum products, solvents, heavy metals, pesticides, herbicides, PCBs, and salts.

Work with non-profit groups since 2009 to encourage sustainable practices in the community, schools, businesses, and municipalities. Knowledgeable of sustainable practices including clean energy, green building, green living, organic foods, natural resource preservation, recycling, sustainable supply chain, water conservation, energy conservation, and climate change.

CEC is a Bay Area Green Business.

Anne is a California Professional Geologist, LEED Accredited Professional, and Certified Sustainability Manager.

Specialties

Contaminated sites investigation and remediation, environmental project management, sustainability consulting
