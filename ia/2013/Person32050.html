David Seibel's Summary

Founder and Managing Partner of AGS Aurora Financial Services, LLC, Dave Seibel brings a unique perspective to his clients’ financial and retirement planning. With a Bachelor’s of Science from Worcester Polytechnic Institute and a Master’s degree from Fordham University School of Business, Dave has extensive corporate experience as a senior financial executive for Fortune 500 companies and in the private sector. This experience gives him an unique perspective to his clients’ financial and retirement planning needs.

Specialties

Financial Risk Management, Commodity Trading Advisor, Foreign Exchange Trading and Hedging, Commodity Trading and Hedging, Corporate Treasury, Finance, Hedging, Trading, Financial Planning, Business Planning, Finance, Financial Reporting, Business Operations, Pension management, Administration
