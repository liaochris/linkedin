Justin Kozuch's Summary

*** UPDATE: I'm currently looking for opportunities within a marketing and public relations capacity. Hiring? Hit the connect button and let's chat. ***

Justin lives, breathes, and sleeps social media. A web developer by trade, Justin transitioned to a digital marketing focus in 2007 and hasn’t looked back.

Outside of work, he can be found on his blog at http://justinkozuch.com/ writing about the digital fabric of his world, consulting and educating brands on the role of social media in business, and picking fights on the Internet.

Justin is a member of the Toronto Transit Commission Customer Liaison Panel and volunteers his time advising their organization on strategies to improve customer service and communications from a technology and social media lens.

In the past, Justin has worked for brands as Hailo, RIM, Scotiabank, The Food Network, Virgin, and McDonalds, performing such work as microsite development, project management, writing, community management, social media strategy, and campaign development and execution.

Hailo is the evolution of the hail - a free smartphone app which puts people just two taps away from a licensed taxi, and lets cabbies get more passengers when they want them.

A Hailo hail is accepted around the world every five seconds from Hailo’s global network of tens of thousands of drivers and hundreds of thousands of passengers. Launched in November 2011, Hailo is available in London, Dublin, Toronto, Chicago and Boston - and coming next to New York, Tokyo, Washington DC, Madrid, and Barcelona. Hailo has carried 3.5M passengers and grown to annualized sales of over $100M.

Founded by three taxi drivers and three internet entrepreneurs, Hailo has raised over $50M from the best investors anywhere, including Union Square Ventures, Accel Partners, Wellington Partners, Atomico Ventures and Sir Richard Branson.
