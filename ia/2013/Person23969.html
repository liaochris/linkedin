Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Civic & Social Organization industry

Life After Hate plants seeds of hope by developing and implementing compelling face-to-face character-building curricula based on the ideals of basic human goodness, developed by those who personally and willingly sought the mantra of “kindness is not weakness” through a road that was painfully travelled.
