Billy Cox's Summary

Billy Cox is an expert keynote speaker, author, sales trainer and social media strategist. For more than 20 years he has helped millions of people take their business and life to a higher level.

Personally mentored by world renowned speaker and motivator Zig Ziglar, Cox gives corporate sales training and leadership seminars across the nation and conducts live and Internet sales training on sales and social media strategy, leadership and personal development skills. An in-demand executive coach, he also sits on the Ziglar Corp. board of directors.

Cox’s energy is contagious and he spreads real-world messages that inspire his audiences to make the right moves to improve their lives and produce immediate and measurable business results. He has fun, believes in what he does, connects with his audience and makes a lasting impact everywhere he goes.

The author of many sales training and personal development books and programs that motivate and promote self-initiative, Cox believes that in the game of life, business and sales there are no timeouts or overtimes. His motto: “You only get one chance to play the game.” His challenge: “At what level do you want to play? Do I want to wait on the sidelines or do I want to win … and succeed at the highest level?”

Find out for yourself how Cox can help you realize your dreams. Contact him at 1- 800-722-4685 or billy@billycox.com
