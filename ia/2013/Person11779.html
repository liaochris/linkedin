Barb Dusek's Summary

Through the course of my business career, I have personally experienced three pivotal points of leadership development learning that were critical to my professional success.

The first is when I assumed a new manager position and felt the excitement of greater responsibility collide with the feeling of being a bit overwhelmed. The second pivotal point of leadership development learning happened when I realized that successfully engaging these employees was my distinct obligation. The third spot, in my career, where deep learning happened came when I decided it was time to use my unique-ability, truly activate my courage and lead with the power of my potential into the second half of my career.

Along the way, I've created leadership develoment strategies for my professional success by:
* staying alert to the relationship between employee and manager
* being certified in several assessment tools
* developing a unique leadership development coaching methodology
* crafting a presentation style that energizes my workshop audiences
* weaving personal leadership development stories into my training units
* kindly challenging business owners and their management teams

When business professionals, and especially employee managers, find their pace others around us can find their groove!

If you find yourself in some of the places where I have been, know that I am here to be your partner along your path of leadership development learning. With your courage, and my perspective, we can move you further toward your own brand of leadership excellence.

Feel free to call me at 612-269-3731 or learn more at www.corspective.com.
