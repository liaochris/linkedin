Evelyn Kalinosky, CRC's Summary

As Founder & CEO of Inner Affluence™ my coaching practice specializes in helping high-achieving midlife business women who want to increase their sacred capital. For these women, the desire to play a bigger game requires a strong profit motive, but making money is no longer the goal. The goal is a search for significance, a journey to uncover the wealth of the self, a rite of passage to their highest purpose, and to a life that is as unique as their fingerprint.

After 20 successful years in the corporate and non-profit world working as a leader in fundraising, sales, marketing, management and operations, my own search for significance led me to create Inner Affluence™ to help other career women cultivate extraordinary lives of passion, power and purpose.

I received my B.S. in Human Development and Family Studies from Penn State University, where I graduated with a 3.99 cumulative GPA. I am a Certified Retirement Coach, having received my training through Retirement Options, Inc., the leader in retirement coach training with a worldwide network of over 600 professionals serving individual, couple and group clients. In addition, I am a Certified ReCareer Coach, an assessment driven coaching system that is the first of its kind in the coaching profession to address the new population of career changers and career extenders created by the new economy, and a Founding Partner in the John C. Maxwell Team of Certified Coaches/Trainers/Speakers.

I am the author of more than 2 dozen books and audios including "The Wealth of the Self: 7 Women Executives Share Their Turning Points" and a forthcoming book on women navigating through midlife. I've provided expert commentary to ForbesWoman,85 Broads, Expert Insights, as well as other magazines and media outlets that focus on business women. Currently, I appear once a month on the Laurie & Lynn radio show as a midlife mentor/expert.

Want to learn more about how to increase your sacred capital? Visit www.InnerAffluence.com.

Specialties

Helping high-achieving, creative career women who want to increase their sacred capital and create a living legacy as unique as their fingerprint.

Executive coaching, Leadership Coaching, ReCareer/Encore Career coaching, Retirement coaching, Midlife Transition Specialist.
