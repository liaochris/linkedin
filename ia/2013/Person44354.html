March 2012 – Present (1 year 6 months) Pasadena, CA

CBA Technology focuses on two elements of home and retrofit construction.

The first element is “Smart Home,” specializing in home automation audio/video, home theater, surveillance, HVAC and lighting control, and aims to help customers navigate the complex technology landscape by creating systems that offer great flexibility and ease of use.

The “Green Home” element helps homeowners save energy by providing green retrofits. Services include home energy audits and energy upgrades, as well as claiming local and government incentives to dramatically offset costs. On average, a green retrofit costs less than $10,000 before claiming subsidies, and will increase home value by $25,000!
