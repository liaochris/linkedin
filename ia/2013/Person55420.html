Jamie Inman's Summary

I am a health activist, a marriage and family therapist,writer, teacher,
and a two-time breast cancer survivor.
I am the Founder of Stay in the Pink, a resource for women and men affected by breast cancer.
I am also one of the Founders of The Giving Ribbon, a not-for-profit group of survivors and caregivers that provides information, resources and emotional support to local families living with breast cancer.

Specialties: I speak on an array of topics about mental health, spiritual growth, and breast cancer. Titles include, but are not limited to: “Losing Our Breasts, Finding Ourselves," “The Secrets of a Happy Marriage: Only the First 25 Years are Hard,” "Soul Survival," and “Finding Myself in a Psych Ward."
