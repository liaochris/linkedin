Andy Edwards' Summary

Senior level music executive with over 20 years industry experience.

Consistently recognised as a thought leader on digital media with a proven track record of correctly anticipating and adopting emerging trends in new technology and business practices from the early 1990s to date. Experienced deal maker covering: recording, publishing, merchandising, sponsorship & endorsement and digital rights.

Career includes corporate, entrepreneurial and start-up environments in Europe and the USA, with exposure to private equity/ corporate finance/ etc issues. Close colleagues have included board level executives and prominent entrepreneurs. Artist relationships cover the full spectrum from emerging artists through to stadium-level names.

Currently also working with the Music Manager's Forum (MMF); advising on industry-wide issues, government and corporate lobbying.
