Christine Barton's Experience

Volunteer

Animal Rescue League of Boston

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Veterinary industry

April 2011 – Present (2 years 7 months) Greater Boston Area

My volunteer service with the Animal Rescue League of Boston began with photographing adoptable animals for posting on the League's website and the weekly Cat Play Group activity. This role expanded to assist with special events that have included Big Papi’s (David Ortiz) visit to support the League, and the Grand Opening of the newly renovated Dedham facility. As with most endeavors, my natural inclination is to become totally immersed in the mission-at-hand. In August 2011, I began delving into animal welfare activities and helping to facilitate adoption matches. With this valuable insight, I am better equipped to capture the essence of each animal’s personality and creating promotional displays to showcase current residents looking to find Forever Homes. When a life is saved, it is a treasured moment.

Photographer / Marketing Specialist

Measured in Moments

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Photography industry

May 2007 – Present (6 years 6 months) Greater Boston Area

In the role I held as Director of Marketing, selecting influential imagery was a fundamental ingredient for promotional branding. Through my initiatives to bolster the overall caliber of design, my passion for photography re-emerged. It led to a journey of exploration through the lens and creation of my venture Measured in Moments. The mission I abide by is encapsulating the unique spirit and significance of the moments that define our lives, our history. Each setting is preserved with a sense of style that exhibits thoughtful reflection and originating from a position of grace and humility. One of the greatest goals of this endeavor is to donate my time and a portion of all proceeds to distinctive organizations whose mission is to reinforce a sense of purpose, revitalization, and sustainability.

In 2010, I expanded the scope of services offerings available under the Measured in Moments umbrella. With a wealth of marketing and communications expertise under my belt, a full array of promotional consulting and design services can be engaged.

Advisor / Contributor

Network Solutions / Communispace

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Market Research industry

November 2010 – January 2012 (1 year 3 months) Greater Boston Area

The Network Solutions Small Business Forum, moderated by Communispace, consisted of approximately 40 professionals from across the country. Director of Marketing

DiGiorgio Associates Inc. / Monitor Builders Inc.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

June 1998 – February 2010 (11 years 9 months)

DiGiorgio Associates and Monitor Builders specialize in healthcare planning, design, and construction management. My role with the firm began as an Office Manager, transitioning to Marketing Coordinator in 1999. As Office Manager, I was responsible for monitoring and administering all facets of the firm’s daily operations including staff recruitment, training, and retention. My umbrella of responsibilities as Director of Marketing encompassed strategizing, implementing, and coordinating all activities associated with the promotion of the company’s image and future objectives in conjunction with its business development and sales efforts.

Operations (Office) Manager

Mental Health Corporations of Massachusetts

1997 – 1998 (1 year)

In this position, I was responsible for the administration and financial management of a nonprofit, advocacy organization with a 90 member roster of mental health providers located throughout Massachusetts. This included administering corporate benefits, accounts receivable and payable, monthly payroll and financial reporting to the Board of Directors. One of my final duties was to coordinate the move of the office to a suburban location including assisting the Executive Director with lease negotiations.

Assistant Property (Asset) Manager

Compass Management & Whitier Partners

1984 – 1995 (11 years)

Over a period of eleven years, I served as an Assistant Property (Asset) Manager for a range of portfolios that consisted of medical, commercial, and residential buildings. My umbrella of responsibilities included extensive interaction with clients, tenants, and a diversified range of property management and brokerage staff. I trained, directed and monitored the activities of both in-house and contracted staff that supported the property management objectives for each building. From a financial perspective, I was responsible for the development of annual budgets and monthly reporting to the property owner (client).
