Ivana Kirkbride's Summary

Ivana brings over a decade of experience integrating content, technology and monetization for companies across Hollywood, Silicon Valley and Madison Avenue.

Prior to The New Media Advisory Group, Ivana was Partner and President of New Media at Generate, a production management company, where she led the development and acquisition of new media properties and the build out of Generate’s distribution networks with companies such as YouTube, MySpace, Hulu and MSN. She also secured Generate's $6M Series A round of financing in 2008 alongside CEO Jordan Levin. Ivana facilitated cross-platform opportunities for Generate's clients, managed over 60 properties in development and executive produced web serials such as The Republicrats and Pink the Series. At The New Media Advisory Group, Ivana continues to work with content creators to identify and monetize cross-platform properties. Most recently, she helped build a multi-platform company that spans print, TV, film and the web for the popular blog, Awkward Family Photos, while bootstrapping the business to profitability in less than a year.

Before joining Generate, Ivana spent five years in venture capital at ABS Capital Partners and Advent International’s Digital Media and Communications Fund, investing over $100 million in emerging technology companies. During this time, Ivana executed large, complex capital transactions and worked with entrepreneurs on strategic growth and operational initiatives. She worked closely with Global 500 corporations such as Samsung, Toshiba, McGraw-Hill, MasterCard and NTT DoCoMo and with the executive management teams at growth companies such as Cyveillance (NASDAQ: MANT), Captivate Networks (NYSE:GCI) and DoubleTake Software (NASDAQ: DBTK). While at Advent, she managed co-investment activity with Gemini Israel Funds and represented the firm on the Board of Directors at Captivate Networks, where she oversaw its successful acquisition by Gannett Co., Inc.

Specialties

Corporate development and strategy, launching new businesses, developing new products, financing and monetization strategy, strategic partnerships, high-level, complex sales and transactions, working effectively across cross-functional teams. Domain expertise in new media and web video; negotiating complex digital media deals and content partnerships; distribution strategy, deals and partnerships; online advertising, integrations, sponsorships & branded entertainment.
