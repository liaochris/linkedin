Jennifer Lancaster's Summary

As a copywriter and in marketing for 8+ years, I know it's tough to get marketing right... What a struggling small business needs is better marketing RESULTS, and less wastage.

That's why I wrote Power Marketing: An Aussie Guide for Business Growth.

As a consulting copywriter with a focus on online, I want to get your marketing bringing in results. Preferred channels are a great website, a blog, and social profile building according to industry.

Some of the industries I have ghostwritten for include: email marketing, solar power, CRMs, business intelligence, SEO, roofing, and small business marketing. My favourite subject is investing and money management.

Authored: 'Power Marketing: An Aussie Guide to Business Growth'.
