Dustin Wheeler, CPA's Summary

I am licensed as a Certified Public Accountant in Nevada and a member of the AICPA.

As a technology-driven CPA, certified member of the QuickBooks ProAdvisor program, Certified Xero Partner, and FreshBooks Certified Beancounter, I enjoy helping business owners implement new technologies and improve business processes. My expertise includes accounting software selection, setup, conversion, training and support.

I have written software reviews for the CPA Practice Advisor, a national magazine that educates accounting professionals in the area of technology, and was recently honored in its 2011 and 2012 list of 40 under 40 tax and accounting profession leaders.

Having lived in Mexico and traveled throughout Central and South America, I speak, read, and write Spanish proficiently.

Specialties: Business and Individual Income Tax, Accounting Software, CPA Firm Software, Information Technology, Systems Design and Implementation, Spreadsheet Modeling
