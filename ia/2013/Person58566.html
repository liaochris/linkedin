Jan Raposa's Summary

Jan is an accomplished user experience design professional with 15 years of experience in web, software and mobile development, product and service design, and digital marketing.

Throughout his career he has been executing and directing product and service experience strategies and defining how some of the world’s most successful corporations and startups
engage and serve their customers. Jan has delivered a varied portfolio of notable works across telecoms, finance, automotive, retail, and FMCG industries.

He works with world-class product, technology and marketing teams that deliver innovative, valuable and brilliantly executed user experiences, and introduce revolutionary applications, devices or services to the world markets.

In 2010 he has founded Moving Crowds, a network of independent partners focusing on delivering product and service innovations for startups and technology companies.

Specialties: User and Customer Research, Product and Service Strategy, User Experience Design, User Experience Management, Branding, Digital Marketing, Design Team Management, Getting Things Done, and taking really good Photographs.
