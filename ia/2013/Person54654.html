Lisa L Moore's Summary

With a portfolio of clients in 15+ industries and projects in multiple countries, Lisa has over 12 years experience developing innovative marketing & sales strategies for start-ups and existing brands. She is a self-driven, problem-solving, marketing wizard possessing a combo of creative brilliance - envisioning the big picture, along side the contrasting technical knowledge & detail mindset to develop & implement strategies. Lisa also trains clients and fulfills speaking engagements on social media trends & tactics.

During 9 years on real estate development teams, she cultivated a strong sales/marketing/advertising portfolio that includes international sales & project management. In 2009, Lisa started a boutique marketing agency honing in on brand creation, business development & social media marketing. As a lead project mgr/creative director, she has worked with over 25 start-ups defining their identity thru messaging, imagery & market placement. Lisa has a proven record of managing product/service life cycles from ideas on paper to realization.

Lisa's customer service philosophy mimics a concierge model, using CRM to profile customers to proactively provide service not to just anticipate customer needs or wants, but to CREATE an appetite for customers wanting more.

She is a respected leader & liaison for senior management teams and has worked w/ local and international government officials on numerous projects. Her breadth of business experience & industry diversity has positioned her to pursue not only marketing, but also business development opportunities for clients.

Lisa is a charismatic & ambitious personality with polished communication and interpersonal skills. As a collegiate & life-long athlete, her DNA drives her motivation and competitive traits. She attributes her entrepreneurial spirit and business acumen to her mother, a well-respected, successful business owner of 4 decades... also her hero.
