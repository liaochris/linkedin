Barie Pinnell's Summary

My whole life has led me to this very spot in time. I am the sum of all my experiences. I've been so blessed to be able to experience the world and live in a country abroad that I absolutely fell in love with.

I have been in the interior design world for 15+ years now and it's who I am! I started in retail, then into commercial, and residential through my home staging. I absolutely love seeing something go from ideation to completion. And, then, to actually help people prepare to sell their homes...and enable them to move on to what their life holds for them..oh, it was so rewarding!

I stepped away from my professional life in April 2oo9. My husband and I moved to Lyon, France, for his job. It truly was a once in a lifetime experience and life-changing. But, my love of design didn’t stop. I have always been enamored with France. I spent most of my time antiquing and delving into the Brocantes and Marchés aux Puces learning everything I possibly could about the styles of furniture to table top accessories.

We returned to the United States from France in December of 2o11. We have never been the same.

We finally landed in a place we call “Our Forever Pueblo” in the high desert in New Mexico. And with that, I have embarked on a dream I’ve had for years :: Intriguerie. Not only the blog, but Intriguerie the brocante of my dreams. Going to the brocante is the quintessential Sunday experience in France – it’s a mélange of antique market meets flea market. This is my way of bringing un petit morceau {a small taste} of France to those people that can’t make it there or have gone and long for the feeling one gets only when one is in France. At Intriguerie, I hope you’ll have that experience toujours {always}.
