Deb Dutilh, CPC, ELI-MP's Summary

I am a passionate Relationship and Communication Maven who helps savvy, successful and decisive women in intercultural partnerships around the world get back to talking, sharing and loving like they used to for trailblazing relationships.

My own maverick lifestyle has included 30 years of professional and personal development experience while living in France, raising two bilingual, bi-cultural sons, being in a cross-cultural marriage, and then losing my ex-husband following on the heels of our divorce. In my quest for understanding the dynamics of what went wrong, I noticed that all too often cultural differences are blamed for marital problems when in fact, the real culprit is the lack of communication around those differences. This personal journey and unique combination of experiences led me to discover that my purpose in life was to help people restore communication and compatibility for trailblazing relationships that are their personal haven of joy, peace and harmony.

My approach includes my Compatibility Index which reveals how your communication habits and inner cultural landscape influence your choices. Then, using my 8-Steps To A Trailblazing Relationship, you’ll learn how to create your own trailblazing relationship based on your own terms and how to communicate effectively and confidently. Whether you are single or in a relationship, the benefits of renewed self-confidence, self-acceptance and self-esteem gained from your inner journey naturally spill over into all other aspects of your life.
