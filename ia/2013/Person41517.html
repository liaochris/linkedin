Eric C. Mullenmeister's Summary

Executive with broad experience and over 30 years of high level success in the automotive mobile tool business with Snap On Tools Corporation and Matco Tools Corporation. Over 17 years Executive experience as Vice President of Sales and Motorsports Marketing for MatcoTools Corporation,a subsidiery of Danaher Corporation.
During my tenure as VP of Sales and Motorsports Marketing(1986-2003) sales increased over 250% and profits increased from a annual loss to over 50mm dollars. In addition Matco Tools Distributor population grew from 650 to over 1400 distributors during my tenure securing the # 2 position for Matco Tools in the mobile tool industry.

Last held the position of VP of Sales and Marketing for Schumacher Electric Corporation (2003-2006) a leading manufacturer and distributor of electical conversion products in Mount Prospect, Illinios where during my tenure as VP the Sales and Marketing departments were totally revamped,revitalized and restaffed the Sales and Marketing departments at SEC. In addition there was record new product development coupled with successful introductions. Also there was a complete and successful rebranding and new packaging initiative completed in 2006.

Specialties: Rebuilding and revitalizing poor performing sales organizations,sustaining success, recruiting and placing high achievers in key positions,(right person in the right seat),financial analysis and budget preparation,sales and product forecasting, new product creation and introduction, brand recognition creation, business plan creation, cost management, new business creation and development, problem solving and public speaking. Currently a successful, award winning real estate agent and investor in SW Florida.
