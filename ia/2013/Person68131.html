Jon Chin's Summary

A passionate and experienced individual who has experience in digital/social from conceptualisation to final execution. Has successfully build up a digital practice group from scratch and made it into a profitable and important contributor to the firm's business within 6 months.

Jon has the knack for 1) understanding social business 2) building communities 3) what makes consumers tick (how (and why) they behave, how they spend their money and finding out how the brands fit into their lives.

Jon's goals are twofold (1) is to make genuine sales impact on the client’s business through the engagement of insightful communication and (2) strategies that connects with the client’s brand though an understanding of how their channel consumption and how they are influenced by those channels. These goals will be achieved using a spectrum of solutions that may or may not include advertising.

Multiple award winner and has articles published in well respected publications such as Campaign Asia and many more..

In addition a regular speaker at numerous international marketing and PR conferences and associations such as American Marketing Association, Academy of Marketing Science, Australia and New Zealand Marketing Academy Conference, Marketing Institute of Singapore, and at Nanyang Business School (Guest Lecture and Panel).

He has worked with many leading Fortune 100 brands, from technology, consumer to finance brands.

Specialties

Social business, digital integration, social/digital audits, brand reputation, influencer relations, community relations and digital media, digital/social consultancy
