  • Wilshire Girls Softball

    Established in 2002, Wilshire Girls Softball has become the premier girls fast-pitch softball league in the Hollywood-Mid City area of Los Angeles. Our goal is to help our girls build strong fundamental softball skills, with a commitment to teaching and practicing the ideals of character, teamwork, good sportsmanship and fair play while having fun.

    Wilshire Softball Association is a member of the Amateur Softball Association (ASA), which runs the U.S. National Team and is the governing body of softball in the United States. Unlike the Little League organization, we have no territorial limitations for playing, so we welcome girls who live anywhere into our league, including Beverly Hills and Culver City. Indeed, last year we had over 200 girls from over 50 different public and private schools play in our league.

    Wilshire Softball Association is a qualified I.R.C. 501(c)(3) non-profit organization run by an all volunteer staff. All of our managers attend mandatory coaching clinics run by professionals, and are ASA certified. Many have five or more years of experience. We also offer players' clinics run by the coaches and players of mostly area schools, including Marlborough, Immaculate Heart, Cambell Hall, Fairfax, and Flintridge Prep. Wilshire Softball fields All-Star teams in each division who compete in post-season tournaments leading up to the ASA District and State Championship tournaments. Many of our alumni players are now standout players on their Junior High and High Schools teams. Our spring program offers four divisions: 6 and under, 8U, 10U, and 12U.
