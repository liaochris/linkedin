Frank Colin's Summary

I'm dedicated to encouraging artists, musicians, storytellers and developers who stretch the boundaries of their media.

Expertise and insight in technology as well as the media and entertainment industries - a unique perspective. I've successfully performed the entire range of product-related duties throughout my career, including product management.
