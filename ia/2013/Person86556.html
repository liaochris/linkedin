Mktg with a Flair Inc.'s Summary

Our mission is simple. To assist our clients, maximize their marketing budgets and drive the growth of their business.

We are an award-winning full service Phoenix marketing company and communications agency offering strategic planning, marketing, public relations and advertising. We specialize in supporting companies that do not have these resources in-house or whose internal resources are over-burdened.

Our team brings a fresh, exciting, inventive approach that looks beyond the traditional approaches to marketing. We consistently deliver top-quality services including strategic planning, brand positioning, public relations, media relations, publicity, research, marketing and crisis communication.
