Ann Marie Detavernier's Summary

I have been an avid reader and writer for as long as I can remember.
I have recently discovered a passion for photography.
I require coffee to function every morning.

I enjoy working with all kinds of people to share experiences, inspire creativity, and grow personally and professionally through team building exercises, community program planning, and simply sharing my life experiences through public speaking.

My personal passion for Military families has led me to create my website, Household 6 Diva in order to both connect and encourage fellow Military Spouses as well as raise awareness with those outside the Military Community about the challenges today's Military Communities face.
