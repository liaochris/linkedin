Crispin Read's Summary

I studied Multimedia Systems at London Guildhall University in the olden days when Google didn't exist and people still said 'double-you double-you double-you dot' as a prefix for a web address. Now with fourteen years professional experience in web technologies and infrastructure I head up tech and digital strategy at White Fuse Media. I'm a specialist in Drupal development, having worked extensively with that system since 2006, before coming to White Fuse I built and consulted on large scale Drupal projects, most notably Intranets for both Ofcom and the GLA. My other areas of technical expertise are in CRM, CMS, CMF integration, front and back end web development, open source technology and development management.

A keen and vocal advocate of web accessibility, I authored Ofcom's policy for accessibility in web applications.

Specialties: Digital Strategy, Open Source Technology, Communications Strategy, Usability, Accessibility, Drupal, OSS, Drupal Development, CiviCRM, Social Media, content strategy
