Jennifer H. Elder's Summary

The mission of the Sustainable CFO is to make the financially complicated simple, awesomely simple!

We can help you decode the messages in your financial statements and business operations in order to increase your level of success and decrease your level of stress. We can assist you with strategic planning, budgeting and forecasting, financial analysis and benchmarking, and onsite training - all the steps that increase efficiency and help you do more in less time.

Our consulting and executive coaching services, seminars and keynote presentations are all geared to making your business better!

Specialties: Financial training, executive coaching, strategic planning, cash flow management, financial forecasting, organization & efficiency, sustainability strategy
