Deepak Yadav's Summary

Deepak Yadav is the Chairman of Yadu Corporation, which is a young group of 27 different companies belonging to the INR 10 billion corporation promoted by Deepak and his associates. Yadu Corporation has interests in the field of power generation, sugar, paper, alcoholic beverages, real estate, hospitality, finance, media & entertainment, education etc. and more. After graduating in Engineering (B.Tech., Mechanical) from National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra with top percentile, he went on to finish his M.S. in finance from UK and finally became a Harvard Business School Alumni in 2010. In the year 2000, he established Yadu Corporation in initial years of his carrier by taking over sick/bankrupt companies, factories and projects under it. The shareholder’s wealth, value and equity present in the Yadu Corp. today, was created by himself and his team from turning around these sick Companies. Under leadership of Deepak, his (skilled and hardworking) team has today become experts in takeover and turnaround of sick/bankrupt companies in its field of experience. Having established the initial presence of his corporation (from insignificance), Deepak is the person solely responsible for establishing a new power utility portfolio (amongst other new divisions), which is the latest thrust area within the group. The power division strictly concentrates only on renewable and green power generation. It has already established 100 MW of biomass based power generation in Punjab and Bihar. The group is also developing a 30MW hydro project in the Himalayan ranges of Himachal Pradesh in India. The power division has now forayed into wind, methane (bio-gas) and solar, while integrating the Clean Development Mechanism (CDM) projects with it for generation of Carbon Credits, to add to the financial viability of projects.
