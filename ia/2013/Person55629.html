In Toone Communication's Summary

In this sea of change and challenge, the ability to effectively communicate with your clients and customers has never been more crucial. We’re poised and prepared to support your marketing interests with lean, targeted messaging to maximize your results.

Jennifer Toone Corrigan established In Toone Communication in 2004. Over the years, she has made excellent connections with gifted professionals from the marketing communications and design industries. It is from this pool of talent that In Toone clients reap great benefit.

Honored in 2006 by Providence Business News as one of the region’s most successful business people under 40 years of age, Jennifer has worked in the marketing communications industry for over 20 years. From 2003-2004, she served as president for the Rhode Island Chapter of the American Marketing Association. She has served as a member of Rotary Club International and on the Board of Directors for Spurwink RI, a local non-profit organization.

A 1995 cum laude graduate of Emerson College, Jennifer earned a bachelor of science in communications and prestigious membership in the Gold Key Honor Society. She has taught a continuing education marketing course at the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), and has addressed students at Johnson & Wales University, Bryant College and the Center for Women & Enterprise.

Specialties

Headquartered in Providence, R.I., In Toone Communication provides website design, hosting and site maintenance, social media marketing, public relations strategy and implementation, writing and publicity services, creative and industrial copywriting, speech writing, branding and logo design, print advertising, newsletter and collateral materials design, and marketing communications support.
