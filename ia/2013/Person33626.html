Debra Cahill's Summary

I incorporate acupuncture, herbal medicine, nutrition, tuina (Asian bodywork), sho ni shin, and qigong, as appropriate into each patient's treatment plan. I have placed additional emphasis in my education and clinical practice on utilizing herbal medicine in order to enhance the benefits that my patients derive from acupuncture treatments. I find that conditions are resolved more quickly and more thoroughly by doing so.
I am in my sixth year of practicing this medicine and I continue to be in awe of it's power to change lives.
