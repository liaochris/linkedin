Julia Burman's Summary

Thank you for visiting my page!

I am an Award Winning Virtual Assistant and Coach and I help female solopreneurs to skyrocket their online presence and their business growth.

With a background in corporate marketing and event management for some of the world's leading investment banks, I initially founded my company as a traditional virtual assistance business supporting the many bright, talented women who, like me, set up their own business post children. It didn’t take long for demand for my social media and online marketing support services to swamp the more traditional virtual assistance side of my business.

I now specialise in providing online marketing assistance, training and coaching to female solopreneurs and small business owners working in the coaching and wellbeing industries who are keen to take their business to the next level but are struggling to do it alone!
