Jon Edwards' Summary

Having been a science writer and press officer, I now manage a communications team that covers a range of comms activities to many different audiences. As a team we look after policy and campaign communications, membership comms, internal comms and branding, public engagement, web and social media. I'm the editor of RSC News, the membership magazine for the RSC.

I'm the lead speechwriter for our CEO and President, and have written dozens of speeches, from 90 seconds to 90 minutes, delivered to schoolchildren, scientists, government ministers and international policymakers.

I've represented the RSC many times as a spokesman across print, broadcast and online media outlets, at local, national and international levels, on issues of science, policy and public engagement. I've also had dozens of bylined news, feature and opinion articles published in RSC magazines.

Specialties

Science communication, copy writing, copy editing, magazine production, speechwriting
