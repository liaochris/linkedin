Christa Fletcher's Summary

Christa Fletcher is the Managing Editor for Campus Explorer. Prior to working for the college planning website, she was a Content Manager for Demand Media and managed the eHow Style vertical. Previously she was the Associate Online Editor for Channel One News, a division of Alloy Media + Marketing and a web intern/contributor for MarieClaire.com.

Christa Fletcher has freelanced for LeanIn.org, Women's eNews, MarieClaire.com, Babble.com, InterviewHer.com and The New Black Magazine. Her freelance work covers the following topics: women's issues, fashion, music, pop culture and travel.

You can view her work on her blog ChristaWrites.com, which she founded in 2007.
