John Goodman's Summary

I'm a graphic designer and production artist with over 15 years experience in print and web design. I've done work for Fortune 500 companies, non-profit organizations, and individuals in a variety of market sectors.

I'm also a tech-head (primarily Mac, but also Windows), and am constantly increasing my computer knowledge and skillset. (Wherever I work, I tend to become the go-to guy when colleagues have software or hardware questions/problems.)

After my position at Augsburg Fortress Publishers was eliminated in 2009, I returned to freelancing and contracting, where I was placed with — and ultimately hired as a full-time employee by — the Health Care Compliance Association in 2011.
