Jeremy McQuigge's Summary

I am a professional member of NODA - Association for Orientation, Transition and Retention in Higher Education where I serves as the Ontario Coordinator and Canadian Network Chair.

8 years of experience building signature experiences for students in transition at Algonquin College. This included overseeing programming for students and staff between grades 7 and 12 focused on career exploration.

Over the course of the last 3 years, I have presented at over a dozen regional, national and international conferences on student transition, retention and orientation.

Recently I began teaching part-time in the School of Business at Algonquin College.

In the near future I hope to bridge to and complete my Masters in Higher Education
