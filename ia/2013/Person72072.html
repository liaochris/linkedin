Karen Fredricks' Summary

Karen Fredricks is the author of 13 books on ACT! CRM and Contact Management Software including eleven “For Dummies” titles. Her works include titles on ACT! Software, Outlook, SugarCRM, Outlook Business Contact Manager, and Microsoft Office Live. She created training videos on Outlook and ACT! for lynda.com. A true CRM fanatic, she is the founder of the Virtual ACT! Users Group and holds frequent webinars focusing on CRM usage.

Karen’s company, Tech Benders (www.techbenders.com), provides consulting, support and training services for a variety of CRM products including ACT!. She has worked with numerous Fortune 500 companies and other well known entities including the PGA, the ATP, FPL and Volvo of North America. Her focus is on making companies more efficient and productive – and therefore more profitable.
