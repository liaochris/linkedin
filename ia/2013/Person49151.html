Paul Ratha Yem's Summary

A full time real estate Broker who dedicate to maintain a reputation that built on quality, service, and uncompromising ethics. I am specialized in REOs, Bank Owned properties and Short Sales. I provide BPO service for companies such as Asset Valuation and Marketing, Inc., Clear Capital, DispoSolutions, Equators, First American AMP, Inside Valuation, Integrated Asset Services (IAS), Old Republic, LandSafe, National REO, National Valuation Services, ResNet, Single Source Property Solutions, Specialized Asset Management (SAM), Summit Valuations, and USRes. I am an active member of the Asian Real Estate Association of America, the National Association of Real Estate Brokers, National Association of Broker Price Opinion Professionals, National Association of Realtors, and the Massachusetts Association of Realtors. Currently, I am the principle broker of our company with two offices in Massachusetts and Connecticut.

Specialties: I am a HUD approved selling broker, a Certified Distressed Property Expert (CDPE), Certified Buyers Representative (CBR), and REO Certified. Certified Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) and Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE).
