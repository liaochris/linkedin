Christine Mortensen's Experience

Owner

Sprk'd

Privately Held; Myself Only; Marketing and Advertising industry

May 2012 – Present (1 year 6 months) Greater Chicago Area

Creative. Content. Conversation.

After more than 10 years of working at digital marketing agencies, I decided to pursue a life-long dream of owning a business of my own. With a history deeply rooted in Visual Design and User Experience Design, I bring a unique and well-rounded perspective to online marketing campaigns. With the advent and rise of social media I turned my attention towards content marketing, realizing that without compelling content companies of the future would quickly falter in this new online landscape.

And thus, the seeds of Sprk’d were sown. We believe that in order to have the greatest impact, online marketing needs to truly speak to its audience—across all channels. To do this effectively, companies must take a holistic and integrated approach when creating campaigns so that they not only resonate—they convert!

Sprk’d makes an excellent partner as we bring deep expertise in three areas core to solving today’s marketing challenges:

• User Experience
Our knowledge of user experience enables us to envision the customer path and thought process throughout the entire buying cycle—providing key insights for effective strategies.
• Visual Design
Our visual design expertise strengthens brand messages—feeding today’s visually hungry society.
• Content Marketing
Rounding out our skill set is a deep understanding of content creation and the ever-changing social media landscape. We stay on top of current trends and changes so you can focus on your business.

Above all else, Sprk’d is committed to excellence in customer service. When you work with Sprk’d you gain a partner and advocate looking out for your best interests beyond simple tactical execution.
