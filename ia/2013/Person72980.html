Kathy Katke's Summary

Katke Risk Consulting Group provides unique, innovative worker's compensation and casualty claim management, safety, and data management solutions to U.S. businesses.

We are an independent consulting firm. Our approach to your needs insures that we have the freedom and flexibility to provide your company with truly sound business advice focused on your financial goals.

Katke Risk Consulting Group delivers consistent financial value by improving claim outcomes and worker safety. Our consultancy does not just apply best practices, but creatively helps you design the best practices that will build and maintain a successful risk management program.

Visit our company website at http://katkerisk.com/ to learn more and to follow us on Facebook and Twitter.
