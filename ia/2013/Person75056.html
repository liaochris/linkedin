Kevin McMonagle's Summary

An award-winning ad veteran, Kevin has led both agencies and clients in creating memorable brand experiences. The kid who drew a lot, he began his career in advertising from a love of art. Painting and photography led to graphic design and art direction. Kevin's work for consumer brands and technology giants fueled his passion for interactive design. He has created campaigns for the largest global companies and the smallest local start ups, and he spends his time in San Francisco and Marin making things.
