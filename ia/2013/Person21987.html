Bilal Saib's Summary

A graduate of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, Dr. Bilal Saib brings more than seventeen years of dental experience in cosmetic, restorative and general dentistry.

At Chapel Hill Advanced Dentistry, Dr. Saib practices world class dentistry in his Chapel Hill office and has been creating “Chapel Hill's most beautiful smiles” for the past 10 years. Dr. Saib sees patients who are seeking general dentistry, cosmetic and restorative dentistry, dental implants, and TMJ therapy.

The minute you walk in you will feel at ease, as if you are walking into a friend's home located in Chapel Hill's Southern Village. We are available for appointments Monday - Thursday and we do see emergencies.

Dr. Saib and his staff's patient-friendly philosophy, which they provide to discerning locals in the Durham-Chapel Hill-Pittsboro area, local television personalities, and business executives in the Research Triangle Park.

Dr. Saib focuses on aesthetics, youthful appearances and a commitment to total oral health. Incorporating advanced technology and heartfelt compassion in helping people is why he loves what he does everyday.

To read more about Dr. Saib go to http://www.chapelhilladvanceddentistry.com

Dr. Saib is also available for presentations, courses and lectures on the following topics: "The Concept of All-on-Four/Six Implant Reconstructions", "The Current and Future of Cosmetic Dentistry", "Oral Appliances for the Management of Snoring and Obstructive Sleep Apnea", "Abstract Concepts in Dental Private Practice", "International Dental Practice" and other topics that can be specialized for your needs.
