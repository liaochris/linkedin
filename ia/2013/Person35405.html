Dick Wingate's Summary

With more than 3 decades of pushing boundaries in the music & digital entertainment industries, I am Principal at DEV Advisors (affiliate of Digital Entertainment Ventures), providing advisory services to service providers, app developers, and content owners, in business development, licensing, distribution, investment strategy and strategic partnerships.

I was previously GM Business Dev for TAG Strategic, a digital entertainment consultancy.

I served as President, Media Development & Chief Content Officer for Nellymoser Inc, an early provider of rich media mobile services, running content, licensing and biz dev strategy, and worked closely with media companies such as Sony, Warner Music, MTV, AT&T, Virgin Mobile & ABC.

I helped pioneer the digital music business from its infancy as SVP, Content Dev., at Liquid Audio, Inc, playing an integral role in a 1999 IPO. I was responsible for all music licensing, label relations, affiliate mgmt & content mktg.

My in-depth experience in the recording industry includes positions as SVP, Marketing, Arista Records (BMG); SVP, A&R PolyGram Records; Dir. Talent Acquisition, Epic Records; & Dir.,Product Mgmt, Columbia Records. I have been integrally involved with the careers of artists such as Bruce Springsteen, Elvis Costello & Aimee Mann.

I have spoken at numerous industry events, including: Digital Hollywood, CTIA, SXSW, Billboard Live, CDO Summit, Jupiter Plug-In, CMJ, Midem, Bear Stearns, Credit Suisse, Business Week and have been featured in the Sunday NY Times Business section, the front page of LA Times as well as quoted in the Wall St. Journal, Time Mag, USA Today, the NY Times Mag & Billboard Mag. In 2000 I was selected as a rep of NY’s Silicon Alley to meet President and Mrs. Clinton.

I was an early music industry supporter of interactive media, & in the early 90’s served as VP, Market Dev., Intouch Group, developer of the i-Station, the first networked, interactive music- previewing kiosk for record stores.

Specialties

Music business, digital entertainment and mobile business development. Strategic and distribution partnerships. Content licensing for online, mobile and kiosk services. Seed investment for digital entertainment.
