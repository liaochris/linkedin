Professional Speaker, Consultant, Coach

Jane A. Herron

1986 – 2011 (25 years) Worldwide

Jane A. Herron is an award-winning speaker—trainer—executive coach with over 25 years of experience guiding executives, managers, small business owners and entrepreneurs in all 50-US states and 10 countries.

Jane has created a strong foundation of experience while educating, motivating and coaching participants with some of the most prominent public Training & Development companies in the World: Covey Leadership, Career-Tracks, National Seminars, and Skill-Path Seminars.

To see a video of Jane teaching the 5-Moments of Truth, head over to the website.

National Trainer for The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People

Covey Leadership Center

January 1993 – December 1998 (6 years) International Trainer

Over the course of 5-years, Jane was an international trainer for the Covey Leadership Center in Provo-UT before the company merged with Franklin. During this 5-year period, Jane taught Dr. Covey's 3-day intensives publically to corporations, academic institutions, conferences, conventions and private clients on his 7-Habits of Highly Effective People. Later Jane taught Dr. Covey's First Things First...Beyond Time Management.
