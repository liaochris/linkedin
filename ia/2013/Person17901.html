Bridgette D. Blanton's Summary

Illustration gives me a door to the world, and to the world, a door into me.

Bridgette D. Blanton's Publications

  • Whistle Zine

    • Self-published
    • March 2012
    Authors: Bridgette D. Blanton, Victoria Jenkinson, Christine Batta, Natasha Porter

    I COULDN’T WHISTLE TO SAVE MY SOUL.
    I’D JUST SIT BACK AND BLOW BLOW BLOW.
    Named Whistle because I can’t whistle,
    never could (note my little limerick above).
    So why this zine? Why now?
    The short answer: I’ve decided to start doing the things I keep telling myself I can’t do (or perhaps: can’t do “properly”). This zine is my screaming answer back to the voice that took up residence in my head telling me this is right, this is wrong, you’re not good enough, etc. I want to create a place for the art and design I’ve been holding back. I also wanted to make something tangible that I could easily share with others. But, this isn’t only about me. I’m
    looking for contributors to include the great work they too might be holding back. Email me at whistlezine@gmail.com with your work, ideas, or just an ‘ello.
