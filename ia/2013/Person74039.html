Keloid Care Scar Cream's Experience

The Complete Scar Care Solution

Dr. Michael Jones

Medical Practice industry

October 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month)

Keloid Care is an innovative fast-acting topical cream specifically designed to help improve the appearance of thick or stubborn scars like keloids and hyper tropic (raised) scars, as well as lighten any discoloration in the affected and surrounding area(s). Keloid Care is carefully formulated with Vitamin E, Hydrocortisone, and Beta Ionone - a powerful anti-oxidant proven to nourish the skin and help improve the appearance of thick, stubborn or hard to treat scars. Keloid Care is also formulated with Aloe, Coco Butter, Shea Butter and other natural ingredients commonly used to soften and moisturize the skin. When applied to the affected area(s) as directed, Keloid Care will visibly remove or reduce the appearance of scars. Keloid Care is safe and effective for all skin types. The ingredients have been specially formulated to provide maximum efficacy in darker skin tones, including African American, Hispanic, Native American, Middle Eastern, Pacific Islander and other skin types that have a higher propensity of developing keloids.
