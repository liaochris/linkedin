Brian Bierbaum's Summary

My name is Brian Bierbaum and I am the Vice President of Strategy and Growth at BlueSpire Strategic Marketing. So what does that mean? It means I do what is necessary to help the people around me to succeed in their businesses, careers, and lives.

I joined BlueSpire Strategic Marketing in 2008 after serving as founder of Net Dynasty a regional web development agency that specialized in collaborating with advertising agencies. At BlueSpire Strategic Marketing I work closely with our clients to develop marketing strategies. I began my career in my parent’s basement as a youth creating web sites during the dot-com boom and gained leadership experience while working on a web development team at United Health Group.

I earned a BSB in Management Information Systems from the Carlson School of Management at the University of Minnesota. There I contributed to the development of the MIS program and spoke at several events through college clubs and programs.

I love to help and actively serve on the emerging media task force and executive committee for the Society of Healthcare Strategy and Market Development (SHSMD), have been on the board of directors of the Advertising Federation of Minnesota (www.adfed.org), work closely with Arc Greater Twin Cities marketing committee on their social media efforts, and am actively involved with my church.

I make my home in Minneapolis with my wife Tiffany and our daughter. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family and doing just about anything outdoors.

Brian Bierbaum's Projects

  • The Thought Leaders Project

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Brian Bierbaum

    The Thought Leaders Project was an experiment in content crowding sourcing that turned into a publishing venture based on the premise that we live in a world full amazing people tackling incredible challenges in their daily lives. The premise of the experiment was based on the following core beliefs:

    - Exceptionally high quality best practices and tips, created by those working in the trenches
    - Ideas packaged with urgency and action in mind. There is no room for filler.
    - Powerful ideas and works spread. Create content that is invaluable when spread, and then package it so it’s easy to for others to share and grow from.
    - Reward those who stand up and spread these ideas and experiences.
    - Different products for different readers. A variety of pricing options and formats to match each person’s needs and desires.

    The project created two books available on Amazon.com. One focused on Hospital Marketing (http://amzn.to/HospitalMarketing) and the other on Telemedicine (http://amzn.to/TLP-Telemedicine).
