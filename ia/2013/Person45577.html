Franco Beschizza's Summary

Franco Beschizza runs his own mobile marketing consultancy. He is working with a number of brands to bring about mobile transformation and business readiness for mobile, bridging the gap between the offline, digital and mobile worlds. His goal is to ensure that they are effectively integrated with one another, and with the wider marketing mix.

He advises C-Suite executives on mobile, helping them to solve business problems with innovative mobile thinking and solutions, developing mobile roadmaps for brands, managing digital risk, and making marketing teams mobile-ready. Seeing the business from a different perspective and not being emotionally attached, allowing him to clearly see both the problems and the potential.

Franco is a guest lecturer in Mobile Marketing to post-graduate students at the International Management and Innovation Department of Middlesex University London. He writes for the Guardian online newspaper and is frequent guest speaker at industry events. He was one of the first in the UK to earn an MSc in digital marketing and to be credit by the Digital Analytics Association as a Web Analyst.

He founded and ran the Mobile Insight Team at COI, the UK’s largest communications agency. He built the success of the unit focusing on three key areas: audience insight; consumer focused solutions, and measurement and performance tracking.

As a member of the management board for COI Interactive Services he was jointly responsible for the strategic and professional leadership of the division. He led integrated campaigns for public and private sector clients. He worked on trailblazing national campaigns such as Talk To Frank, Thumbnls, Act on CO2 and other award winning consumer engagements for mobile.

You can contact Franco concerning business opportunities, consultancy, or advisory roles within the UK, Europe or North America at franco.beschizza@gmail.com or call him on +44 7809 447327
