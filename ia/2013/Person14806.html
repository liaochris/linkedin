Bruce La Fetra's Summary

I assist professional services firms (A/E/C and other service-oriented firms) confronting the challenges of growing revenue faster than staff. Many firms get stuck or plateau when the marketing and sales that brought them success is no longer sufficient to get them where they want to go next. I get them unstuck so they can thrive.

Asking the right questions and taking the time to understand customers, partners and a firm's own culture and personality creates the clarity needed to craft solutions that instill a marketing culture across the entire firm and deliver plans that focus on growth, yet remain operationally flexible.

* * *
A widely published author, I've penned feature articles for The Pricing Advisor, Pragmatic Marketing Magazine, Soft-Letter, MarketingProfs, Sandhill.com and Sales & Marketing Management.

Bruce La Fetra's Projects

  • Lifetime Opportunity Value Equation (LOVE) model

    • June 2008 to Present
    Team Members: Bruce La Fetra, Harry Max

    The LOVE model offers a relationship-based framework for understanding the interaction between companies and their customers. The LOVE model is a better way to understand customer relationships that are built on mutual relationships or trust, respect and perception of value. Unlike the linear process of the traditional "sales funnel," the LOVE model assumes a dualist set of outcomes and purchase can occur at any stage of the process.

    • The Professional Pricing Society
    • January 2008

    Software companies often fail to understand how different customer groups value their products. This leads to firms offering both excessive discounting and too many licensing vehicles.
