Joseph Francis Bihag's Summary

Joseph Francis Bihag – aka Jobi - has been at Y&R Dubai for more than a decade. That doesn’t make Joseph part of the furniture, but rather the walls that hold the agency together.

Joseph is a man who believes in paying his dues, particularly to his work, which he does for 23 hours a day, most days. He calls it the Joseph Bihag Method of Advertising, and he has been refining it for the past 15 years.

The Joseph Method has proved to be highly effective and has helped Joseph win awards across every advertising discipline from Design, Direct, Shopper Marketing to Integrated Advertising.

Currently, Joseph oversees the Digital, Design and Brand Experience divisions at Y&R Dubai and works on a number of clients like Land Rover, Ford, LG, HP, Sony Ericsson, Citibank, Kit Kat, Arnott's, Dominos Pizza, Coca-Cola, Colgate-Palmolive, 3M, Harvey Nichols, La Senza, Virgin Atlantic, Gulf News, Warid Telecom and American Garden.

His work has been honoured at Andy, Cannes Lions, D&AD, One Show, Clio, London International Awards, New York Festivals, Adfest, Epica, Effie, Dubai Lynx, Cresta, Webby, Graphis and Wolda. He was also part of the team responsible for Y&R Dubai winning the Agency of the Year three times in a row at Dubai Lynx.

In his free time, Joseph serves as an Advisory Board Member of the Department of Visual Communication at American University of Dubai and occasionally lectures at the top three Visual Communication schools in the United Arab Emirates - American University of Sharjah, Zayed University and the American University of Dubai.

Joseph is happily married with two lovely boys and the family love spending their weekends together with their three rescued dogs.
