Lana Drew Custom Concierge, LLC
dba: Student Concierge Services

Remember when your son or daughter received word that they had been accepted to college, the family was so excited and when the time came to leave home reality set in. Along with the excitement came stress, let’s face it, as parents we have been the primary support system to our children.

Transitioning from home to dorm and or apartment can be stressful. I learned this first hand by helping a student and his family locate the perfect rental. I started LDSCS because I saw a need for this service and I am so happy I did.

With a solid background in sales & marketing as well as being a successful realtor in Rhode Island for 16 years, I pride myself on getting things done.

As a result of my leadership, organizational coordinator, project manager and executor skills, I simply get things done. I have successfully helped both students from the USA & International Students ease into dorm and or apartment life and I will do the same for your student.

I am confident I will create a positive & trusting relationship with your son or daughter. My Brown University student clients have already established" Lana Drew" as the person to call for everything and I will do the same for you and your family. On call 24 -7.

Please feel free to call me 401 578 1851 or by email ldrew20@gmail.com
