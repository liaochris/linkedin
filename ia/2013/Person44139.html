Farrah Hoehne's Summary

Farrah Hoehne is an Associate Recruiter with TalentFoot Executive Search, a national search firm solely dedicated to the digital media industry. Prior to her recruiting career, Farrah spent 10+ years on the agency and sales side, where she successfully led digital media campaign strategies, managed multi-media marketing campaigns and drove ad revenues. She has worked with Fortune 1000 brands across a variety of verticals, including Bally Total Fitness, RJO Futures, DeVry University, General Motors, Midas, True Value, Allstate, and ESPN.
Hoehne’s extensive experience managing clients and campaigns for full-service and digital advertising agencies, combined with her time selling behavioral and contextual rich campaigns, has given her a rich media foundation and an insider’s understanding of how the digital media business works. This makes her extremely adept when it comes to understanding TalentFoot’s client and candidate needs and ensuring the perfect match.

Farrah's agency experience includes leading digital strategy for Charlotte's Web Marketing for both direct response and branding clients, time as a Media Supervisor for Starcom MediaVest Group where she led a team in developing and managing paid search strategies for the agency’s leading clients; and five years with TMP Directional Marketing, overseeing the implementation of SEO, SEM and Internet Yellow Pages ad placement. In addition to her time spent on the agency side, Hoehne also gained exposure on the technology side, selling online display for Collective Media where she partnered with agencies and brands throughout the Midwest.
Farrah is passionate about educating hiring managers and job seekers on the importance of aligning the most appropriate digital skill-sets with the specific business objectives at hand.
She served on the Executive Board of CIMA as the Vice President of Education managing the association’s educational and academic affairs initiatives for 5 years.
