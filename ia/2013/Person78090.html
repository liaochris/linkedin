Lalo Garcia's Summary

Back in 2000, I was working in Washington, DC for USA Today, living the life of tight deadlines a national publication demands. After 20 years of photojournalism and documentaries, I decided I was ready to branch out and open my own company.

Shooting events and portraiture is wonderful. The people are fun and the job is rewarding. I love meeting new people and I love taking pictures. I can't ask for a better way to spend my day!

I approach every shoot with an open mind, eager to design a portrait with artistic creativity and always considering the clients needs. My goal is to give the customer what they envision and then deliver more.

Specialties

My specialties are in communication, still photography, video photography, lighting, producing, tape to tape and non-linier editing.
