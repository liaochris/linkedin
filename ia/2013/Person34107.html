Denise Rutledge's Summary

I am a resume writer who organizes information so it has both visual and reading impact. My goal with every resume is to present my client as the logical candidate for the job opening he or she has applied to fill. I do this by shifting my clients away from a job function-focused resume to a results-focused resume.

I use a series of carefully crafted questions to help my clients shift their paradigm, whether they have worked a 9 - 5 job or have been entrepreneurs. When I send a client their final resume, my goal is to have empowered him or her with more than a well-written resume. I include coaching on how to modify the resume for different job openings, how to interview more effectively, and more.

I specialize in resumes for career changes, helping my clients identify their transferable skills. I am especially strong at helping individuals who have been out of the traditional work force develop a resume that breaks through barriers. My goal with each client is to demonstrate the skills he or she has to offer whether those skills were gained through traditional employment avenues or through volunteer or entrepreneurial pursuits.
