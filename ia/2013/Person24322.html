Chris Bagley's Summary

Volition Ski Co has been a labor of love for me. Bootstrapped from the ground up, I've turned an interesting hobby into a growing business. Through the use of social media and guerrilla advertising I am beginning to build a reputable and well known brand.

Prior to becoming a business owner, I have extensive experience integrating social networks with websites to maximize exposure and have successfully advised clients how to get the most out of their limited time allotted for social media management. I have also developed successful interfaces for online Collaborative Learning Systems and multiple sites for The University of Maine.

Chris Bagley's Projects

  • Volition Ski Co

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Chris Bagley

    Developed on the wordpress platform, this site was developed to provide a means for Volition to easily present rich media content in a progressive, non traditional manner. The site is designed to integrate with the Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram social platforms acting as a one stop media “hub”.

    I setup the site to automatically receive Instagram photos posted by Volition tagged with “#volitionskico”. Instagram will also publish the same photo to the company Facebook page (not a standard option via instagram) and then Facebook will then publish to the appropriate twitter account. This loop lets Volition distribute media fast and efficiently with low overhead.

    The site also has product integration with it’s shopify store. Allowing users to purchase directly from the products page instead of going into the online store. Simplifying the user experience has played a large role in growing the companies social outreach.
