Cody Willner's Summary

Real estate broker, entrepreneur, marketing manager, and web designer.

Our team helps buyers and sellers with their real estate needs throughout the entire Willamette Valley, Oregon Coast, and SW Washington.

My skills and expertise with real estate include investment properties - particularly in college towns, working with first time home buyers, marketing our current listings, creating awareness for our team/brand, and using specialized and cutting-edge technology to help and improve our real estate business.

For marketing, I focus on SEM/SEO, utilizing newest market data for projects, social media, print/direct mail marketing, and good old-fashioned networking.

WordPress is a CMS I heavily use with my websites and with my web design clients due to it's simple back-end interface and ease of content change. I've designed and edited multiple WordPress sites, as well as several static HTML/CSS sites.

I am very open to new opportunities and to help other local businesses. I like to challenge myself and try new things. Variety is the spice of life, right?
