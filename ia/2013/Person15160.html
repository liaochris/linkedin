Branden Moskwa, B.ASBE, CAPM's Summary

Branden Moskwa has over 15 years of operational and project related experience having gained business acumen and entrepreneurial knowledge by surrounding himself with world renowned entrepreneurs and business leaders. Branden has received tremendous international media attention for his successes as the Founder and President of Ads en Route Inc, Canada's mobile advertising leader in the early 2000's, setting itself apart through its use of creativity and cutting edge technologies as accelerators to growth.

Branden believes that to survive in business requires obsessive focus and relentless determination, coupled with genuine and honest interactions with all those around you. Branden marries these together in perfect harmony. He has made it his priority to build positive relationships with talented individuals and has built an appreciation for quality, creativity and customer care incorporating these values into his everyday work.

Branden acts in advisory and management rolls to select organizations he believes have the "it" factor, holds a BA in Small Business and Entrepreneurship is a Certified Associate in Project Management and recipient of numerous esteemed business awards.
