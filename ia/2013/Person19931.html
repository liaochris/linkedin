Carla Stewart's Summary

Carla Stewart is an amazingly self-driven, people-oriented woman of integrity. Mrs. Stewart has devoted her life to putting others interests first and providing exceptional service to her clients.

After experiencing the frustration of being under-appreciated as a corporate employee, and experiencing several lay-offs, she decided that it was time to take charge of her own life.

After an honest self-evaluation of her prioritizes, dreams, and goals, Carla made the leap into business ownership. And The Office Beyond LLC was born.

The Office Beyond is a goal-oriented, service-driven Virtual Assistance practice. Carla uses her VA business to provide above average technological, marketing and social media services to her multinational clientele. She focuses on bringing excellence to her clients and helping them reach their goals - both professional and personal.
