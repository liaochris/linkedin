Jackie Pressman's Summary

Created property assistance company in south of France
Chairman of large insurance brokerage UK
Creator of international art & antiques business
Creator of Carmen (heated hair rollers) UK

Jackie has formed and profitably managed several high-visibility companies in the UK and France, and had factory production in both Europe and the Far East.

Jackie has always built and directed high-visibility sales teams and is in the front line of personal high-level sales contacts and service operations, with well-known accomplishments in multitudes of high-level press exposure.

Specialties

Property search, negotiation, tax and legal purchase arrangements in France with in-depth knowledge of French real-estate laws. Creation of international new product awareness; management and brand creation.
