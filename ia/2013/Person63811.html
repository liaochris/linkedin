Jim Conlan's Summary

Communication specialist with over 30 years' experience in advertising and marketing as a writer, art director, producer, and campaign manager. Has written and produced thousands of radio commercials for hundreds of local, regional, and national clients. Has won scores of awards up to the international level for excellence in radio advertising.

Professional voice-over talent for commercial, corporate, and institutional clients, including Jiffy Lube, Administaff, Diamond Shamrock, NASA, Chevron, Shell, and Halliburton.

Master voice-over coach who conducts workshops and gives private lessons to voice talent from beginners to seasoned professionals.

Now applying this expertise to modern media, especially where marketing requires a closer attention to content.

Specialties

Copywriter, audio and video producer and director, communication strategist, campaign coordinator and team leader, marketing and branding consultant.

Professional announcer and voice actor, voice-over trainer, production of custom voice-over demos for newcomers and experienced artists.
