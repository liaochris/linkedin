Fernando Koch's Summary

Dr Fernando Koch is a Research Scientist at the IBM Research Brazil and Honorary Senior Fellow at The University of Melbourne. He received the IBM Eminence and Excellence Award in 2012 and the IBM Outstanding Contributor Award in 2013 for leadership in research.

Dr Koch has published over 50 academic papers and has more than 15 filed patent disclosures in the areas of Smarter Cities, Education, Health Informatics, and Mobile Computing..

In 2013, he has been nominated ACM Distinguished Speaker (http://dsp.acm.org/view_lecturer.cfm?lecturer_id=4803)

Dr Koch is a proven innovator and results-driven leader with large experience in information technology, software development and scientific research. In-depth expertise in Elaborating Project Proposals for both commercial and R&D engagements by leveraging from commercial and academic experience.
