Michael Schneider's Summary

Michael Schneider has a long term career in the IT industry with over 16 years of experience. After gaining first professional IT experience at the age of 17, he has worked for well known companies, like Siemens, Webwasher, Cyberguard, Secure Computing and is currently employed by McAfee. He has held management positions including responsibilities for both people and revenue.

Michael has gained a graduation from ATIW, a SIEMENS owned vocational college, and has passed a ferderal, standartized certification as Specialist for System Integration.

He has passed several vendor dependant and vendor independant certifications, including Novell CNA (1996), Microsoft MCP (2000), Cambridge Certificate in English (2001), WWCSA (Webwasher Certified Server Administrator, 2004), WWCSE (Webwasher Content Security Expert, 2005), CTSPA (Cyberguard Total Stream Protection Administrator, 2005), CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional, 2007, 2010), Pragmatic Marketing Certified Product Manager (2009)

Michael is currently employed as Senior Product Manager at McAfee GmbH in Germany, which is a wholly owned subsidary of McAfee Inc.
