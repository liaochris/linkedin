David Varga's Summary

Compose music for all areas of the media: Television, film, commercials, interactive, video games, theater, etc.
Scoring to visual, sound design, recording/engineering.
Styles ranging from:
Electronic and acoustic- classical (orchestral-large and small ensembles), rock (all styles), pop, world, hip hop, sound tracks, etc.

EMMY Award winning Editor. Have edited everything from commercials, promos and music videos to indie films, documentaries and many kinds of reality TV. Also magazine style talk shows and some sports events. I have also worked in various areas of production: from producing to camera work and audio.
