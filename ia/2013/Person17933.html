Lisa M. Francis' Summary

Lisa has 25 years of IT experience layered with system development, project management, internet marketing and new business implementation experience.

Lisa is an expert in the field of internet marketing, search engine optimization, website design & development and social media strategies. Her passion is to help local businesses expand their customer reach and effectiveness by harnessing the Internet.

Lisa's vision is to serve business owners with consulting, services and tools designed to help business owners understand how to leverage the power of the Internet to grow their business, find new customers and better serve their existing customers.

Many business owners are aware of certain aspects available online but they do not know how to make it work for them. Lisa guides and teaches them.

Specialties: website design & development, social media for business, search engine optimization, internet marketing, online marketing, new business development, article marketing, video marketing
