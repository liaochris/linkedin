Al Lewis' Summary

Marketing, Business Strategy, & Product Management

I help companies increase profits and win market share by applying proven strategies in product planning, market assessment, product launch and throughout the life cycle of their portfolio of products. I build promotion programs, partnership alliances and sales channel strategies to support profitable product plans and execute implementations for overall success. I am a leader who repeatedly surpasses goals. I make companies winners!

I have a wide range of experience in product management and marketing. I can bring fresh perspectives to business plans, develop new strategic visions, and create viable market plans and product road-maps. I understand competitive landscapes and how to identify strengths in the various addressable market segments. I can build market forecast and product line financials that are attainable targets. I am particularly good translating what a less technical market needs into requirements that engineers and software developers can build … then working with engineering to get the product features in timely manner and on budget … then translating the features back into benefits that customers cannot wait to buy. I am good a marshalling the resources for a product launch and managing a full portfolio of products throughout their life cycles. And I bring a strong understanding of positioning and branding to technology and other markets. I have board experience with marketing communications including advertising, collaterals, trade shows, and sales force training and support. I am a team player and my management style is coach. I have high energy and thrive on being excited about breakthrough products and applications.
