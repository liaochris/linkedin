Charlie King's Summary

Charlie King has been featured in leading sports and business publications all over the world. King’s focus is on teaching the 7 Essentials of Golf. His method for teaching golf is a skill-based method instead of a model-based method where everyone must conform to the model. Charlie’s Red Zone Challenge for short game is known for identifying and testing players skills and creating actionable insight to prioritize the instruction.



In addition, King’s take on golf and mind is quite frequently used in corporate events and outings targeted at building and strengthening vital business relationships. His programs help corporations identify key clients and empower the relationship through one of the programs offered.

Specialties

Sports Psychology, Golf Instruction, Golf Expert, Business and Sports Relationship Building
