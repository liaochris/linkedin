Darren Smith's Summary

I design technology that is humane.

Interaction with technology should (usually) provide the user with a positive experience. Yet, even today we waste too much of our time working out how to use stuff. It's appalling.

I have 7 years of experience researching, designing, developing and supporting web based technology. I am a designer with a business aptitude. I move easily from conceptual & strategy thinking, to detailed pixel by pixel css.

I have travelled and worked for international clients and I hold a MSc in Human-Computer Interaction & Ergonomics from UCL. My dissertation was in collaboration with Microsoft Research in Cambridge.

I am a regular attendee at Startup Weekends, experienced in startup methodologies and have worked at multiple web based startups including my own. Lean and Agile? FTW! ;)

Specialties: User Experience Design (user journey's, wireframes, sitemaps, hi-fi mock-ups)
User Research (contextual inquiry, interviewing, usability testing, task-analysis, ethnographic studies)
Front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
Graphic design.
