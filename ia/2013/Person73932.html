Kelly Hoey's Summary

Proudly listed in Forbes as one of five women changing the world of VC/Entrepreneurship, on Fast Company's "25 of the Smartest Women on Twitter" and on the 2013 list of "40 women to watch over 40", I am one of the founders of Women Innovate Mobile, the first accelerator program for women founded (or co-founded) startups focused on mobile technology or mobile applications.

I curate a lot of conversations around startups, entrepreneurship and early-stage investing. I'm a frequent speaker on building networks, social media, online engagement and the other specialties listed below. What's my strength? I get things done. I'm a strategic thinker and doer. I'm told I'm a connector. I do know I see the world in terms of relationships, networks, interactions and communities of interest which fuel ideas, engage people and create opportunities.

In this highly networked social business era your network is a bottom-line business asset.


Specialties: Online engagement and professional visibility online. Community Definition, Design & Liftoff. Community Management. Navigating The Social Media Noise. Community Engagement Strategies. Engagement of professional networking communities. Social Media Networking. Launching affinity & voluntary interest communities. Leverage of professional networks.
