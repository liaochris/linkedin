Charles Jones' Summary

I am a seasoned public relations executive with more than 20 years of communications experience who currently represents clients in national publicity; crisis and litigation communications; legal & professional services marketing; public affairs; multicultural marketing; event marketing; and media and public speaking training.

My strengths in crisis and litigation communications are solidly built on my past experience as a broadcast news journalist. Clients rely on my ability to comprehend complex matters and advise them on how to either maximize or minimize their exposure in a clear, concise and compelling manner.
