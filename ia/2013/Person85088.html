Marcin Nowakowski's Projects

  • New Shops (Molotok.ru)

    • June 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Marcin Nowakowski, Piotr Siwiński, Kasia Galek

    The project combined previously existing features, company accounts and Molotok Shops, to create a new Russian product: company accounts with access to professional tools for sellers, including among all layout templates and seller’s own domain. Highlighting items listed by companies and quick access to them via filtering enabled buyers to recognize the most credible sellers easily, which improved significantly the perception of the service’s security.
    A by-product here was a new user page, that combined previously separate pages (feedback card, About me page, the listing of seller’s ongoing offers and user’s shop) into one comprehensive source of information about the user.
    Role: UX Designer

  • Simple registration (Aukro.ua)

    • August 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Marcin Nowakowski, Piotr Siwiński, Kasia Galek

    Launching the first registration process on the Allegro platform where the data required in the registration process was significantly limited. Saving user’s contact details, that could not be eliminated for security reasons, was moved from the registration form to the purchase process where leaving shipping data has a natural context.
    On this occasion SMS verification was used for the first time on the platform.
    Role: UX Designer
