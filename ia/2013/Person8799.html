Anthony Parisi's Summary

I live and work in San Diego, CA. For many years I have worked with New Renaissance Pictures to create a variety of web serials, feature films, and television series. I think the digital revolution has made this one of the most exciting times to work in cinema. From the outset of my career I've sought to be on the front lines of online distribution and explore the ways it is changing the landscape for artists in film and television.

During my college years, I helped create and launch WebSerials.com. Our "bite-sized" approach to serialized storytelling resulted in over 4 million views, national television syndication, and our partnership with Hulu. Currently, we are in production on a new anthology series, The Seventh Spectrum, which premieres summer 2013.

For several years I also worked with Finley-Holiday Films to create DVD and Blu-ray documentaries for sale across the country in the US National Parks. Today, I work as a freelance media producer for a variety of clients providing video, still photography, graphic design, and new media consultation.
