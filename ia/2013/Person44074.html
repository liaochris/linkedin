"Famous" Alice Linesch's Summary

Silence was shattered that lovely Sunday morning by a loud ring. It was the police calling. My store had been robbed.

The three-minute drive was painstakingly long, as dared to anticipate what I might find. Broken glass was everywhere. The thief had thrown a brick through the door and made off with my big screen TV; But nothing else. A swell of relief came over me, followed by a wave of inspiration. This is an opportunity!

I did the only thing a marketer could do. I created “The Break-in Sale and Contest.” This caught the attention of some local press, as I had hoped. My colleague said “Alice you’re now famous!” and started calling me Famous Alice. He then sent my story to marketing guru, Dan Kennedy who featured me in HIS newsletter. My new moniker was catching on quickly and I realized I could capitalize on the situation. I needed a domain name, fast. By some miracle, famousalice.com was available, which secured my destiny. My personal brand was born!

My name is Alice Linesch, but everyone knows me as Famous Alice. I show people how to craft a cohesive, recognizable, consistent personal brand so they will attract the right opportunities to them.

As you know, big brands, like Target and Starbucks, spend big bucks on their visual branding to make sure we recognized them whether we’re in a store, see an ad or visit their website.

With my background in creative design and branding, I show people how to create the same level of visual appeal and consistency for their brands. Because, just like big brands, personal brands also need to be recognized to get the most impact out of each and every impression.

To find out how you can have a powerful, authentic personal brand call me at (310) 662-1694 or via email at alice@famousalice.com.
