Maria Anassutzi's Summary

Trusted intellectual property consultant for the Business Owner. Seasoned commercial procurement and intellectual property multi-jurisdictional lawyer with expertise in intellectual property and information technology law and e-commerce having spent considerable time in-house with number one mobile network in the UK. Maria is an English qualified lawyer and speaks fluently Greek and Italian. Her clients range from IT, software development and telecomms companies to entrepreneurs, inventors, manufacturing companies and service providers.

Specialties

Intellectual property, protection, acquisition and exploitation of technology including procurement of hardware, software licensing, hosting and outsourcing contracts; licensing and assignment of patents, copyrights and trademarks; content licensing and management; knowledge transfer; collaboration and research & development agreements, branding, marketing. Specialist in procurement, ecommerce, hosting and internet issues, advises on all aspects of outsourcing and facilities management matters.
