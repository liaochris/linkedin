Eric Walker's Summary

Impresario Promotional Concepts LLC and sister company, Impresario Inc (Impresario) offers a unique solution of specialized marketing, promotions, and corporate event services for our clients. And, we will gladly travel to assist them wherever they may need us!

Impresario caters to large corporate and non-profit clients, small business owners, business leaders, and an array of event talent that need effective solutions to increase profitability and build stronger brand awareness. Our company’s daily mission is to creatively produce brilliant and effective promotional events for our clients on schedule and as promised.

Furthermore, we consistently strive to uphold a reputation of excellence in the global marketplace through the selective collaboration and representation of our event talent, vendors, sub-contractors, and preferred venues. Please continue to visit our company website for updates regarding Impresario's featured products and services.

Impresario is also the executive producer of Business Mechanics 101. This energizing seminar is designed to be an “all-in-one” resource for the many obstacles that can often cause deceleration in profitability for the small business owner or business leader.

Key Business Mechanics 101 Seminar Topics Include:

~ Re-igniting your professional passion
~ Evaluating the effectiveness of your business plan
~ Executing time management skills
~ Diagnosing potential tax and legal pitfalls
~ Utilizing low-cost advertising and marketing solutions

Specialties: Some other examples of our company's specialized services include:

~ Training and staffing for in-store, retail promotional events
~ Designing effective marketing plans
~ Orchestrating the marketing for product launches
~ Planning "All Hands" employee meetings
~ Developing executive team building activities
~ Managing corporate sponsorship negotiations for ROI
~ Preparing our clients to effectively participate in tradeshows

Eric Walker's Experience

President

Impresario Promotional Concepts, LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

October 2009 – Present (4 years 1 month) Spanish Fort, Alabama

Mr. Walker is the founder and president of “Impresario” based in Spanish Fort, Alabama. His company provides specialized marketing, promotions, and corporate event planning solutions for organizations that need proven strategies to increase their revenue and market visibility. With over 15 years of tangible experience, Mr. Walker has successfully worked with project budgets exceeding $100,000. Just a few highlights of his professional accomplishments include producing a traveling fashion event for Woodward & Lothrop Department Stores in Washington DC; managing a 1000 employee product launch meeting for Nextel Communications; designing a pre-merger, team building event for a division of 750 employees of the Titan Corporation; and developing a fundraising campaign that included planning (5) fundraising events to be held throughout the United States for the National Minority AIDS Council based in Washington DC.
