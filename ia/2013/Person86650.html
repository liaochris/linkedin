Mark Harai's Summary

For over 25+ years I've been involved with startups and helping businesses increase revenue and build market value.

My latest startup - BloggerBeat.com is a community of proven entrepreneurs, business owners and professional service providers who are doing meaningful work in the marketplace.

Whether you have a new startup, or an existing business that you would like to expand, we can show you how to leverage our strategies to successfully launch, or grow your business by simply becoming part of our community for free!

New media i.e. blogging, podcasting, video, mobile, Twitter, Facebook, Google+, et al are fueling a global movement, socializing & connecting the world, and paving the way for a new kind of business owner – “The Small Global Business Owner.”

This kind of business is built on a mix of business savvy & experience, progressive social business activities, digital marketing, and business automation & outsourcing strategies that keep you working “on” your business, instead of “in” your business.

For more info on how we can help you launch or grow your existing business, you can visit: http://bloggerbeat.com/launching-digital-startups
