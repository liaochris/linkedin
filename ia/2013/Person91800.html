Marc Grossmann's Summary

A marketing person at heart, I spent 15+ years strategizing, developing and implementing campaigns for some of the largest (and smallest) companies in the Entertainment industry. Since 2005, I've expanded my repertoire into Financial Services, Licensing, Consumer Goods, Restaurants, Home Restoration, Online Startups and Bridal Services, to name a few.

In addition to my marketing skills, I take great pride in educating friends, family and clients about investing, protecting their assets and choosing their benefits wisely.

Few people know as much as I do about finances and even fewer are as honest with their clients as I am.

Whether you are looking leverage my marketing skills or knowledge about financial concepts, you'll always get a greater value than you anticipated.

Exceptional writing skills, savvy media strategist, expert in Crisis Communications, multi-tasker, self-starter, entrepreneurial, new media guru.

Financial: Protecting you and your family's assets by utilizing life insurance, health insurance, disability insurance, Medicare and more.
