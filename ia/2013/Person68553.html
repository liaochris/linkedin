Jose Luis Moya's Summary

A marketing manager in general and a project manager for online distribution and ecommerce, especially within the travel and tourism industry. Strong knowledge of (SEO, SEM, marketing, graphic design, technology, servers, JS, ajax, flash, Open Source, Web 2.0, etc) although I could probably not do an purely specialized job about any specific one of them.
