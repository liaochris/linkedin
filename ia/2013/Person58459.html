Janine Ripper's Summary

Janine Ripper is an accredited PRINCE2, P3O and MSP Practitioner, with a Bachelor of Arts in Communications and a passion for communication and organisational health.

Janine has worked in Project Management for in excess of 12 years in a variety of arenas (Government, IT, Resources, Fleet Management, and more), and in the last few years has become a PMO/PM/MSP Specialist, consulting to organisations in areas from PMO implementations and management, establishing and refining project management standards and frameworks, through to establishing and mentoring people in best practice.

Janine is a Manager and Project Management/PMO Practice Lead at Cathara Consulting. Janine manages Cathara Consultings communications strategy, including social media accounts, company newsletter through to the design and publication of the Cathara Consulting Client and Employee Handbooks.

Janine is also a mentor, mentoring people in social media right through to project management.

At all other hours Janine is a blogger, writer, photographer and wishful traveller, with a passion for mental health and wellbeing.

Janine's personal blog - http://reflectionsfromaredhead.com - is her way of changing the world one word at a time.
