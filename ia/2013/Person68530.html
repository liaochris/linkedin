Jose Freyre, MBA, CFP®'s Summary

I lead an independent financial and investment management practice specializing in the needs of small business owners and individuals nearing, or already in, retirement.

I pride myself on providing unbiased advice that's based on our clients goals and act as our client's "Personal CFO" by taking a look at their entire financial picture to provide comprehensive advice. I also work closely with their other advisors (cpa, attorneys, etc...) to address any complex financial, business, estate and investment needs.

A key area of expertise I bring to business owners is helping them plan and execute the transfer, sale or exit of their business.

Specialties: Wealth management, financial planning, retirement planning, business succession and exit planning, investment management.
