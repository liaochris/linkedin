Darish Still's Summary

Darish Still is known as an incredible source of knowledge in the areas of housing counseling, nonprofit business development, and organizational leadership. A leader, coach, trainer, and motivational speaker, Mr. Still is undeniably dedicated to his craft and a recognized expert in the fields of consumer credit and housing counseling. As President and CEO for Consumer Credit Management Services, a leading nonprofit organization, Darish leads a staff of certified housing and credit counselors, develops and implements fund-raising strategies, and develops programs consistent with the organization’s mission and funding requirements. Always willing to extend a helping hand, he is also a volunteer Director for the Broward County Housing Development Task force and the Boca Raton Housing Authority Family Self Sufficiency PCC committee.

Specialties: Affordable housing, budgets, community engagement, compliance, contracts, grant writing, management, motivational speaking, program development and implementation, and staff development
