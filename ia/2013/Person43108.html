Esther Surden's Summary

I am now the editor and publisher of a website called NJTechWeekly.com, started from scratch in mid-2011.The website covers both the tech and clean tech business in N.J. and the issues that face N.J. tech companies.

As many of you know, I am an experienced technology business journalist who has written for many daily, weekly and monthly publications. In some cases, I wrote and edited entire monthly and twice monthly newsletters. At the Institute for Scientific Information I successfully managed a staff of six writers.

With the development of NJTechWeekly, I am continuing my career in technology journalism, expanding into digital publishing.

Specialties

Writing about the business of computers, communications, wireless, telecommunications. Also editing/ writing about new, emerging technologies in these fields.
