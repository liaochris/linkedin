I'm a Hybrid.

In transportation, as many of us already know, it's a vehicle which uses a mixture of power or fuel sources. However, in my world, it means having the ability to not only create and design a report utilizing T-SQL and SQL Server Reporting Services, but understand how the data generated from these reports impacts the business.
