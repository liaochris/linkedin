Dan Hostetler's Summary

I have proven myself over time to be a successful leader in the consulting industry. My companies have been able to successfully motivate problem ridden entrepreneurs and nonprofit entities to make actionable decisions towards improving the quality and productivity of their professional lives. I have embraced the arts of servant leadership, fiscal and organizational analysis, and meaningful persuasion from the inception of my career in change management consulting but have used it exclusively to introduce my clients to alternative choices to failure. I've enjoyed sustained epochs of success but only because I have been blessed with team members with superlative talents.

I accepted the challenge and invitation from Donald J. Fletcher, in 1994, to return to Italy as a Managing Director where I shouldered Profit and Loss responsibility the better part of ten years due to the parade of broken records and performance shattering results that ensued. These outcomes were not mine alone but came as a result of my team's innate ability to bring disparate groups of private interests together in pursuit of common goals. Commonplace strife was drastically reduced, turnover was dramatically reduced and performance results soared.

In 2003, my family and I returned to the United States and I was moved to open my own change management business consulting firm, which I did with a partner. We built our company into a national concern, winning many local and national awards, and building alliances and partnerships with companies that shared our visions and values.

In 2010 I made the decision to re-partner with my former boss of many years so that we could recreate the decades of success that we enjoyed together. We made a good team then and we make an even better team now witnessed by the honor just bestowed on us as being one of Inc. Magazine's 2013 Fastest 500 Growing Companies!
