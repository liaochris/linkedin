Al Weiland's Summary

My name is Al Weiland and I am the owner/inspector for Wall to Wall Home Inspection Services, LLC. I started the company in 2001 with the simple goal of providing a great inspection at a fair price. I perform 200-300 inspections a year and speak/teach about home inspections at home buyer workshops. I have over 25 years of experience in the home construction and the inspection industry.

Specialties: Nationally Certified Home Inspector #1037, Rental Weatherization inspector #890095, licensed Real Estate Broker #40981. I am also certified through MATC in Building and Home Inspections.
