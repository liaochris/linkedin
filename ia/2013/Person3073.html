Operations Director

Spidersnet Automotive

January 1997 – October 2011 (14 years 10 months) Sayers Common

Spidersnet was the result of my need as a 'Motor dealer' to learn how to create a website to sell cars in what I suspected was a new media to sell cars. It started small, with a handful of dealers who recognised my almost instant 'Online success' and wanted to join in the fun. Spidersnet was born in 1997. Needless to say it went through many changes in platforms and technology throughout my watch. In 2005 Spidersnet was 'Aquirred' by Friday Media Group, one the leading online publishers of classified and niche-interest websites. I continued to increase the number of users, bottom line and sales staff within Spidersnet up to October 2011. With innovations during my time with Friday Media group that I was particularly proud were class leading 'Mobile versions' of dealer websites, and keeping a very close eye on the UX. The Spidersnet system had to be the most user friendly system as there are now thousands of users all updating their 'Spidersnet' websites via a really great back office.
Thanks to all the crew, who made the job a pleasure. You know who you are...
