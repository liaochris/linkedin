Christina Wagner's Summary

I started my experience in radio by co-hosting two radio shows called ‘Darn Good Deals’ and ‘Wisdom from Experience’. The show ‘Wisdom from Experience’ focused on bringing people together to solve everyday life problems. ‘Darn Good Deals’ focused on bringing local businesses together and show the public where to find deals. DGD used the airwaves, networking, and social media, specifically Twitter, Facebook & Linkedin as well as RSS feeds to drive traffic to their businesses.

That lead me to the role of Marketing Director for KFNX News Talk Radio 1100. I was able to take my knowledge of social media to promote the station, programming, and it’s advertisers. After two years, I was ready for a change of pace and a new challenge.

As the Marketing Director for Xeriscapes Unlimited I have implemented a social voice for the company on Facebook, Google+, Twitter, & Pinterest. Currently, I am working on a new website which will be dynamic and allow for a blog. The target market for Xeriscapes is primarily Home Owners Associations and Commercial Properties. http://xeriscapes.com

It didn't take long for the radio bug to bite though, after leaving KFNX I found my home at another local radio station NBC 1260. The show focused on tools that assist business owners and explored best practices in social media. I have sinced moved my show to an internet based station that offers video as well as podcasting. I am so excited about the technology they have and the energy of the other hosts! The new show will explore just about all topics I am passionate about, it would be safe to say that nothing is off limits...hence, the new name 'No Limits with Christina Wagner'.

Specialties: marketing, networking, social media, radio
