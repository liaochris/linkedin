Based on individual clients’ needs, history and lifestyle, we employ Pilates, CranioSacral therapy and Reiki techniques, as well as my expertise as a physical therapist. Working with clients in their homes, workplaces,hospitals and at my own studio. My integrated approach works from the outside in. Pilates, through physical exercise routines, constantly moves energy, supporting the ‘homeostasis,’ or the balance needed for optimal health. CranioSacral therapy balances the energy flow within our bodies, stabilizing emotions and dissipating the negative effects of stress. Reiki can harmonize the surrounding ethereal energy with our own inner power.

The fun for me is putting them all together in response to each
individual’s needs.
