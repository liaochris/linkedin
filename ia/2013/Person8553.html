Ann Parenti's Summary

I have owned an operated Parenti & Assoc. for over 25 years. Parenti & Assoc. is a manufacturing rep firm that specializes in hospitality products such as wallcovering, fabric, fabrication, digital & traditional artwork. In addition, since 2009 I have offered a seminar series called POSSIBILITIES. The seminar offers innovative ideas on communication, how to draw positive experiences into your life and a simple meditation technique. POSSIBILITIES also organizes fundraisers several times a year for local community causes. I am also singer/songwriter and have a CD on the market base on A Course in Miracles. The CD is called As Promised. I also released a few mainstream songs called I Don't Know Why and Manhuntin' Blues that can be found on CD Baby and iTunes. I have been featured as one of the authors in the books, Life Choices: Putting the Pieces Together and Life Choices: Pursuing Your Passion. I started working on a project at the beginning of 2013 that involves creating a book, CD and DVD of the musicians and artist of jazz, blues and big band in Las Vegas for the past 50 years. The production company is called Forgotten Song Music. We are expecting to release this collection in the winter of 2013. Along with this project, I am creating a new CD of some of my favorite jazz standards with a few originals that will also be introduced around the same time frame.

Specialties: My specialties are in public relations, sales and marketing, public speaking, writing, singing, songwriting and fundraising.
