Deanna Ferrari's Summary

Deanna Ferrari is an award-winning, forward-thinking communications professional and social media dynamo. She has more than five years of agency experience working both on the consumer and B2B side of PR and social media – from startups to national brands. Former clients include Rite Aid, the Pennsylvania Lottery, Culver’s Restaurants, Krups, All-Clad, Redstone Highlands Senior Living Communities, Leap2, Bryant Heating & Cooling Systems, and Nestlé (The Skinny Cow and Edy’s/Dreyer’s Fruit Bars). Named one of the Top Women in PR and Marketing to Follow on Twitter and a “Rising Star” in public relations, Deanna has assisted clients in the development and implementation of PR and social media strategies. Her well-rounded skillset includes account management, media relations, social media management (including strategy, implementation and insights reporting), copywriting, guest post writing, event planning, blogger relations, website management and more. Throughout her career, Deanna has successfully used both traditional and digital PR to amplify messages and connect key audiences. She currently leads social media for rue21, one of the fastest growing specialty retailers in the country.

Outside of her day job, Deanna plays an active role in professional organizations. She has held several board positions on PRSA Pittsburgh, served as communications lead for PodCamp Pittsburgh and is the Pittsburgh “champ” for social media organization HAPPO (Help a PR Pro Out). She is also the president of the Pittsburgh chapter of 85 Broads, where she leads a network of trailblazing women who are inspired, empowered, and connected. Deanna also blogs at “PRetty in Pittsburgh,” where she discusses social media, PR, Pittsburgh happenings, and health and fitness. Finally, Deanna speaks frequently to organizations and universities including PodCamp Pittsburgh, the Pittsburgh Technology Conference, Visit Pittsburgh's "Tweet , Like + Be Social" and more.
