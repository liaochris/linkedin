Darren G. Austin's Summary

Darren G. Austin is a wireless internet industry pioneer and passionate technology entrepreneur with more than 12 years leading product teams for major technology brands including AOL, Amazon.com and Motorola.

In 1999 he was a part of the successful exit of a venture-backed wireless business when the start-up he was with was acquired by Amazon.com. He went on to help start mobile initiatives at Amazon.com and RealNetworks before co-founding SeattleRentals.com, an online real estate classifieds business serving the Pacific Northwest.

Darren joined AOL in 2005 to lead the product team responsible for Mobile AIM. He helped grow the Mobile AIM audience to over 9 million active monthly users and helped transition the business from a carrier-centric distribution model to a direct-to-consumer model.

Darren joined Expedia in 2010 to begin building their mobile e-commerce team. He is passionate about innovative consumer experiences and is active as an adviser in the Seattle-area start-up community.


Highlights
• Consumer product executive focused on architecting the global mobile Internet revolution since 1999.

• Entrepreneur and corporate-level experience working at global internet and technology brands (AOL, Amazon.com, Expedia, Motorola, and RealNetworks).

• 14+ years in product management, marketing and business development leading cross-functional teams and managing revenue streams in excess of $55 million per year with 80% EBIDA.

• Participant in successful exit of wireless start-up in 1999. Sold 8-person business to Amazon.com for $23 million and established Amazon.com’s mobile e-commerce initiative (Amazon.com Anywhere).

• Founding member of mobile teams at Amazon.com, RealNetworks and Expedia.

• Active in the Seattle-area start-up community as an adviser for early-stage internet ventures.

Specialties
