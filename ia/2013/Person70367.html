Judith Belmont, MS, LPC's Publications

  • The Swiss Cheese Theory of Life!

    • Premier Publishing Group, WI
    • October 21, 2011
    Authors: Judith Belmont, MS, LPC, Lora Shor

    The Swiss Cheese Theory of Life uses the analogy of Swiss cheese as a metaphor for life itself. Both my co-author and I are psychotherapists and wellness speakers, and underlying the essence of the book is the notion that life is not smooth and predictable like American or Cream Cheese. Rather, life has holes, and it’s how you get through them that counts! Instead of wishing the holes away in our lives, the book’s ten self-help slices show readers how to get through life’s pitfalls without getting stuck in them! In fact, it is the holes in our lives that give us depth of character and make our life rich in meaning, just as the larger the holes in the Swiss, the sweeter the taste of the cheese. Thus, we need holes in our lives to become who we are!
    This book is the basis of Judy Belmont's wellness seminars, offering practical life skills that help people Smile and Say CHHESE!"
