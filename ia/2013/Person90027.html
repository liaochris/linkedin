Jason Harper's Summary

Analytic minded, digital marketing strategist. I have a track record of innovation, leadership, team building and business development.

MIT Technology Review referred to me as a “Social Soothsayer” in their article “A New Model for Predicting Social-Media Impact” http://bit.ly/sBcZfD

I have expertise in econometric modeling, forecasting, search engine marketing, digital marketing, social media marketing, advertising and I have experience working at advertising agencies and as a client. This expertise was best highlighted through the development of The Organic Media Model (TOMM), which estimates the budget and media mix required to achieve a sales objective and forecast ROI. This ROI is determined for each media channel including TV, Radio, PR, Social Media, Online Display, Paid Search (SEM) and other relevant media types as determined by the advertiser. TOMM utilizes digital consumer behavioral data (such as website visits and social media activity), which contributes to its groundbreaking accuracy. TOMM may also be used to forecast sales volumes based on a given media mix and spend.

The Wall Street Journal covered the TOMM approach in the article "Modeling Tools Stretch Ad Dollars: Chrysler Uses Digital-Response Data to Adjust Commercials, Drive Web Visits" http://on.wsj.com/vunZTQ

Some of the key industries I have worked in automotive, travel, software, banking, and CPG.
