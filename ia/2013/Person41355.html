Elaine Johnson's Summary

I have been designing residential landscapes on Cape Cod and the South Shore for over 22 years. I have a very strong background in creative design and project management for all phases of construction and installation. With an extensive knowledge of plant material suited to local growing conditions, I design landscapes that have lasting beauty and integrity.

On a personal note I enjoy photography, anything "Apple", yoga and walks with my springer spaniel Molly.

Specialties: Architectural features, landscape masonry, site furnishings, exterior lighting and container gardening. Strong project management skills and scheduling. Plant selection to compliment site, conditions and architecture. Conservation mitigation.
