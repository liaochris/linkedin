Jeremy Vitale's Summary

I work to secure financing for businesses, organizations, and investors in Tompkins and Cortland Counties in New York. Our lending reach extends to the contiguous counties of Onondaga, Madison, Chenango, Broome, Tioga, Chemung, Schuyler, Seneca, and Cayuga.

I consider myself to be an intrapreneur within the companies I have worked for as well as an entrepreneur. In the past I have owned a tanning business and I currently invest in income generating rental property.
