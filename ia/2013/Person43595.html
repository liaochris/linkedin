Larry Meador's Summary

Beneath the occasionally unfiltered yet often brutally honest veneer of evok’s founder beats the passionate pulse of a leader whose dedication to his team and unyielding competitive intensity for our clients’ success knows no boundary or equal.

After more than twenty years in the industry, many on the client side and ten at the helm of evok, he’s performed almost every role in advertising from brand manager to creative director to social media strategist, and in doing so, calloused an exacting approach for attaining client success that may in fact be one of our agency’s best assets.

In addition to leading the agency’s account service division, he serves as account planner, agency futurist and even gets back to his roots on the creative side every once in a while.

A graduate of the Gator Nation, Larry has served as the American Advertising Federation – Orlando President, has been named a 40 Under 40 recipient by the Orlando Business Journal and is a dedicated husband and father of two beautiful daughters.
