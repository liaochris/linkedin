Lynne Miller's Summary

Professional writer and editor with more than two decades of experience writing for trade industry and business publications, as well as general circulation daily newspapers and websites. I worked as a staff writer, reporter, associate editor and section editor for many publications before kicking off my freelance career in 2007.

As a freelancer, I have contributed articles to a variety of newspapers and magazines including the Daily News, The Real Deal magazine and Web site and QSR magazine. As a contract writer for Pfizer, I write and edit articles and manage content for the company's intranet, I also produce two weekly e-newsletters for Pfizer's New York and New Jersey employees.
(Some common misspellings of my name include Lynn Miller and Lyn Miller.)

Specialties: Business writing including retailing and real estate; feature writing, line editing, headlline writing, website management, newsletter production.
