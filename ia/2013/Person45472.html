Francesco Miceli's Summary

Civil engineer specialized in turnkey wind farms EPC projects. Worked on several multi megawatt turnkey wind farm projects worldwide.

Main tasks:
Preparation of RfQ and technical offers to international customers for more than 40 turnkey projects.
Preliminary cost estimation and firm prices.
Technical support to the sales department and to the client during the sale phase.
Monitoring and technical support to the construction department during the construction phase.

Specialties

In-depth knowledge of AutoCAD, Bentley MX ROAD (InRoads), CLIP, Ispol-Istram, Presto. Good understanding of Google Earth, gvSIG (shareware GIS Software) Emme/2, Epanet. Calculation of all civil works items (earthworks cut/fill, foundations, drainage, crane pads, electrical collection system layout, etc.).
Review of civil work project, bill of quantities and specifications prepared by external consultants for dozen of wind farms, e.g. Janda III, Cupinisque Talara, Juancho los Cocos, Punta Lima, Racoviteni, etc.

Procurement:
Preparation of RfQ (request for quotations to subcontractors) and technical offers to international customers for more than 40 turnkey projects.
Meeting with the shortlisted subcontractors to review their offers and to detect possible mistakes or deviations from scope.

Construction:
Monitoring and technical support to the construction department during the construction phase. Visits to site. Variations to the project if needed.

Miscellaneous:
Analysis of tender in English, Spanish and French.

Development of Vestas MED technical specifications for civil works, geotechnical surveys, road surveys and topographical surveys.Interpretation of Geotechnical surveys.
