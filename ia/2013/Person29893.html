Formed in 2009 by myself and Matt Dixon, Band2market is the music industry's leading independent campaign management company. Based in the UK, we offer a fully integrated and centralised UK and international service. From conception to beyond release, we cover any aspect you need to successfully take your artist to market and drive business across all sectors of media, not just album or single sales. We are passionate about helping to build careers in the UK and the rest of the world.
