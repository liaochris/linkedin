Marcus Meazzo's Summary

My Purpose: To help individuals and businesses build genuine, lovable and sustaining brands through comprehensive coaching and creative strategy in order to add excellence, high integrity and accountability to our marketplace and to the world. - See more at: http://meazzo.com

Strengths: (in order of experience): Brand and Creative Strategy, Creative Direction, Design Education, User Experience Design, App Design, Print and Packaging Design.

I began my career as the Art Director of a respected trade magazine for the dental technology industry, Dental Dialogue. Following I founded Open Door Creatives, where most of my projects included spearheading grassroot brand initiatives for national and international companies in the hi-tech, entertainment, property management, clean-tech, non-profit, and VC startup ventures. Marcus has had the pleasure of working with clients including PayPal, ToyotaOwners.com, The Groop / Jamie Oliver Food Revolution, Payoff.com, CB Richard Ellis, Energy and Power Solutions, and the Los Angeles Forum to name a few. I have also worked with multiple service bureaus as a brand consultant and design lead.

For 10 years, I have enjoyed being either in the trenches designing or leading multidiscipline teams conceptualizing a multitude of online and offline creative strategies. I understand what it takes to share the story of a genuine brand, and have a thriving passion to work with people and companies who truly have something unique to bring to the world.

Today, I am focusing on continuing to grow my personal brand as a creative strategist, lead designer and an educator on building, genuine, lovable and sustaining brands. Additionally, I am an advocate of companies who seek to know thyself, and to help align their internal reality with their external reputation and who seeks to connect with their customers on a human level.
