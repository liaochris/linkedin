Doug Eymer's Summary

For cutting-edge business organizations, eager to firmly establish their brand presence, I deliver unique marketing solutions that attract customers and drive sales.

My abilities differentiate me from my competition. I am highly successful with both business and creative processes and challenges, as well as, embracing new technologies and media as they emerge.

The foundation of my expertise has been built upon years as an agency principal/creative director, communications design firm owner, department manager within a corporate environment and my formal education as a design professional.

Specialties:
• Management and leadership of a team of creative professionals
• Conceptualization and development of integrated marketing programs and strategies
• Overseeing the design, production and implementation of individual marketing components whether they be online or printed
