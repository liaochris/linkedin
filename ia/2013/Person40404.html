Elena Frankel's Summary

Client Focused

Connecting with clients and being a trusted advocate is the best part of being a real estate agent. I recognize that buying and selling real estate is likely the biggest financial decision you will make, and I strive to work my hardest to provide clients with the best customer service needed to achieve any real estate goals. I listen to my clients wants and needs, and am committed to serving in their best interest

Local Neighborhood Knowledge

As a Chicago native, I love sharing my passion for my city with my clients. After graduating from University of Illinois with degrees in Finance and International Policy, and experiencing careers in the investment and banking industries, I found my niche in real estate. My extensive knowledge of the city, along with my financial background, allows me to give confident and educated advice on pricing and negotiating to get you a great value as a buyer or seller

In an ever evolving market, I work hard to stay on top of both market and technological trends to keep my clients well informed. By understanding changing technological trends I can work with buyers, and can expertly market listings to their full capacity, reaching the maximum number of buyers

Experience and Expertise

Having represented both buyers and sellers in short sales, I have developed a deep understanding of how the process works. This helps me be able to confidently help navigate my clients through their options from both a financial and personal perspective.

I am fully committed to being your trusted advocate and confidant, and to being a dedicated professional, and have had great success. With the backing and support of an international brand, Coldwell Banker, and working in a top producing office, I am constantly motivated and driven to work at the highest level.
