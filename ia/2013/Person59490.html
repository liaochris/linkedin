Jason Schultz's Summary

Serial entrepreneur and part time investor in web/mobile based businesses. A fan of people who create something significant from thin air. .

In school I co-founded a nationwide internet service provider and become a partner at a money management firm managing over $1 billion to create a suite of online financial services, late nineties co-founded a full service marketing firm, early 2000's built two multi-million dollar ecommerce companies, early and mid 2000's founded several consumer internet companies, late 2000's invested in and advised startups. Late 2011 began work on new startup.

Areas I may be able to add value: marketing, ecommerce, internet product/service development, new distribution channels, infrastructure, premium domain acquisitions, and lean startup practices.

Areas of interest or business: USA, UK, Germany, Spain, Switzerland.

Specialties

marketing, ecommerce, internet product/service development, lean startups, mobile apps, new distribution channels, infrastructure, high value domain names, and the like.
