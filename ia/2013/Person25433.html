Claudia Colombo's Summary

Passionate. Informed. Committed. Accessible.

I’m passionate about the healing arts and natural medicine, and this has turned me into a seeker of truths, a lover of humanity, a yogi, a natural chef and an ingredient geek. As a holistic wellness expert, I’m dedicated to guiding others (including babies and even pets!) on the journey to total health through an exploration of skincare, nutrition and fitness for the mind, body and soul.

My process begins with understanding each client’s unique history. I believe that the best, most enduring results come from this very personal connection. This enables me to create a truly customized plan that can encompass everything from skincare to yoga instruction to meal plans, whatever is called for to help clients make beneficial lifestyle changes.
