Alan Bleiweiss' Summary

World Class SEO Consulting and Forensic Audit Solutions

If your web site is not reaching enough people in your target market(s), or you are not seeing a high enough conversion of site visitors to clients / customers, My team and I can help you determine whether the problem lies in your current search engine optimization, site user experience, or a combination of both.

Forensic SEO Audits
All of our SEO work beings with a comprehensive review and analysis of existing web sites - at the code, content and site architecture level; competitive analysis; keyword phrase analysis; online presence across relevant social media; inbound link authority and depth.

SEO Action Plans
For every item addressed that is shown to have weakness, clear and concise action plan recommendations are made and prioritized so that work can be executed to improve that aspect of the overall SEO status based on client's unique budgetary and implementation constraints.

My background includes sixteen years Internet consulting experience. Clients from sole proprietors to Fortune 500, across the United States.

I am a search engine optimization and Internet Marketing expert, blogger, author and industry speaker. If there is anything my team and I can do to get you to that next level, we'll find a way to do it.
