Debora Stewart psa's Summary

I create contemporary abstract pastel and acrylic paintings. My work is an intuitive and expressive. My pastels have received awards from The Pastel Society of America, Artslant, International Artist Magazine and Pastel Journal Magazine.

I am an experienced art educator and have specific skills in working with adolescents. I have organized art camps for youth, facilitated expressive arts groups for at-risk youth and have authored an original curriculum utilizing creative expression in developing reslience in young people. I have also presented at professional conferences on the topic of Art and Resilience.

I provide workshops on developing abstractions in pastel and acrylic. I have taught workshops in the U.S. and Canada. I was invited to teach a workshop at the 2013 International Association of Pastel Societies in Albuquerque, New Mexico. I am also a visiting artist for Very Special Arts Iowa.

I am represented by Wilde Meyer Gallery, Scottsdale, Arizona, UGallery, SanFrancisco, California, Milward Farrell Gallery, Madison, Wisconsin, Artful Home, Madison, Wisconsin, Iowa Artisans Gallery, Iowa City, Iowa and Corner House Gallery in Cedar Rapids, Iowa.

Specialties: Contemporary Abstract Pastel and Acrylic paintings
