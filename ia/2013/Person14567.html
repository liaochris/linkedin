Bindy Bonnette's Summary

2013 sees me founding Ideas Rock - an independent Touch publishing and digital content development company dedicated to a love of touch screen devices and what they offer to experiential play, discovery and learning.

Im also happy to compliment my workload with other projects and am available for consulting on a freelance basis.
Flick me an email or call me if you need a hand, I am based in Wellington, NZ.

I have been 2 years in educational publishing, and 18 years delivering interactive environments and brand experiences on every side of the line. I am a mix of Strategy, UX, UI, Creative, Content development, Online, Mobile, Print, Social media, Marketing and Comms.

Consulting & freelance:
Strategy and vision work, Concept development and UX, Professional coaching, Digital publishing, digital solutions development with clients or internal teams. Available for consulting, mentoring in-house short term with your team or as an independent agency.

Specialties:
Strategic vision, thought leadership, vision setting & creative strategy, concept development, pitching, guidelines, design system development, mobile development, mentoring and skills development for creative staff, brainstorming, user flows, storyboarding & scamps.

Software and systems I use most:
Pen and paper, Free apps and open source, Creative Suite 6, Adobe Digital Publishing Suite, OS, Android, HTML & CSS, Omnigraffle pro, Axure Pro.
