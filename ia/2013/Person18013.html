Britt Carr's Projects

  • The American Dream

    • September 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Britt Carr, David Marshall, Ronda Closner, Elaine Bolle, Jennifer Whiting

    The American Dream is a mobile / tablet / desktop game that allows players a trial-and-error run at experimenting with spending and budgeting decisions over their simulated life. It's a "choose your own adventure" type game, with a dose of reality. Think: 'The Game of Life' meets 'The Sims' meets 'Survivor'

  • Jazz Bandstand

    • January 2013 to August 2013
    Team Members: Britt Carr

    A mobile game designed for Southern Methodist University students to help "Intro to Jazz" students learn the jazz greats and their signature sounds. Students are asked to "build" a band matching a particular style by dragging musicians on stage. Aural feedback lets the students know if they are correct or wrong. If they are correct, a jazz piece matching the style is played. If incorrect, the resulting sounds are out of sync and in different keys making for a cacophony of sound.
