    • @.l.interpretations / Amazon Kindle

    Tribes: They're NOT Just For 'Survivor'! is an eBook about the fascinating pulse of every brand, big and small: the tribe.

    That's a folksy name we call a community of people with shared interests who rally behind a brand or cause. They're a brand's cheerleaders at best, or Statler and Waldorf, at worst. They're a people who have become united by a shared interest and are not only any brand's eyes and ears, but the heart.

    In 'Tribes: They're NOT Just For 'Survivor'!' you'll find the following:

    - Different types of tribes and best practices to develop to engage with them
    - Creative measures to take in growing your brand
    - How to build trust while growing your brand
    - Video links of unique vantage points from current leading online marketers
    - The importance of social media integration when building a tribe
    - The importance of providing ground-breaking information that is rich in value
    - Alternative means of expanding your brand to grow your tribe
    - ... and more!

    Whether you're a novice consultant looking to establish an online presence or a well-established small business with a sizable following, 'Tribes: They're NOT Just For 'Survivor'!' surely has something for your daily customer engagement circumstances.

    Authors: Annesa L Lacey

    Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is often thought of as a business strategy that enables businesses to:

    - Understand the customer
    - Retain customers through better customer experience
    - Attract new customers
    - Win new clients and contracts
    - Increase profitably
    - Decrease customer management costs

    Ok, let's go for more appeal, shall we?

    It’s paramount for any thriving business to keep their customers happy, because satisfied customers are repeat customers and repeat customers refer new customers.

    Between software designed to help businesses benefit from customer relationships, strategic methodologies and social media integration, small brands are prime for serious growth with the right CRM strategy - no matter the economic environment.
