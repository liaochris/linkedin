Let me take the mystery out of buying or selling a home for you. By working together we will make buying or selling your home pleasant and productive.

If you are a buyer I promise
• To be open and honest every step of the way
• To communicate and keep you informed of the home buying process
• To be responsive to your needs and questions at all times
• To share my expertise of the real estate market so you can make informed decisions
• To always act as your advocate during the negotiation process
• To use new technologies to expand your home buying options

If you are a seller I promise
• To partner with you when setting a competitive and fair price for your home
• To use both traditional and new online technologies to find the right buyer for your home
• To plan and implement the most extensive marketing plan possible to get your home sold

• To communicate on a consistent basis so you always know where you are in the process
