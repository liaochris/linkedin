Matt Christianer's Summary

Highly focused and passionate I have over 13 years in the fitness industry. As an IFBB Pro and Fitness Model, I am currently sponsored by 6 Pack Fitness and have been featured in Iron Man Magazine, Muscle & Fitness, Reps, MMA Fitness, Power Systems, Musclemag, NPC Magazine, Fitness RX, and on the covers of Iron Man Magazine, Max Sports & Fitness and Fitness Plus. Most recently, you can find me on the September Cover of Iron Man Magazine and October Cover of Iron Man Magazine (Australia).

My journey into fitness began after a near fatal car accident at the age of 17. After nine years of training, I went on to compete and win my first ever bodybuilding show at the age of 26. In 2011, I became the 1st ever Pro in the IFBB for Men's Physique. Within the next two years, I achieved three Pro wins; 2012 IFBB O.C. Muscle Classic, 2012 IFBB Governor's Cup and 2013 IFBB LA Grand Prix . In September, I competed as a contender in the 1st ever Men's Physique Olympia in Las Vegas and it was an honor to step foot on the stage.

I aim to help others in reaching their fitness goals. I specialize in Men’s Physique and Women's Bikini competition prep including; strength training, diets and posing. I have helped many men and women reach their competition goals.

Currently residing in West Hollywood, I am pursuing opportunities in the fitness industry and stay tuned for the upcoming reality show Muscle Beach The Series.

Specialities: 1st Ever IFBB Pro Mens Physique, Muscle Beach: The Series, Olympia 2013, Pro Fitness Modeling, NPTI Personal Trainer, Strength Training, Mens Physique & Womens Bikini Competition Prep, Posing Clinics, Diets, 6 Pack Fitness, 6 Pack Bags, Nutrition, Weight Training, Physique.Inc, Fitness Entrepreneur, Matt "Swoll", Bootcamp clinics, Priority Principle, Instinctive Training, FMI, Mens Physique Visionary & Leader
