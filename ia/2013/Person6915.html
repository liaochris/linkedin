Andrew Girvan's Summary

A digitally native arts manager, I approach projects with twin passions for both the performing arts and technology. Working in an editorial role for the UK’s leading theatre website I thrive in a fast-paced environment meeting tight deadlines, where I am able to draw on my diverse experiences to meet a variety of rapidly changing challenges.

I confidentially manage multiple priorities and am responsible for developing and implementing social media strategy as well as managing large online projects such as web streaming the annual Whatsonstage.com Awards. I am given daily opportunities to study the West End and its audiences, placing particular emphasis on the way theatregoers use the internet to consume and purchase.

Whilst at university I staged my own small scale productions as well as taking on venue management roles at the Fringe in my native Edinburgh. I was also able to exploit my long-held passion for musical theatre, spending a three month academic placement working as Production Assistant for Perfect Pitch Musicals, gaining invaluable experience working for a small-scale commercial producer.
