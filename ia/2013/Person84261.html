Mahastee Mehdizadeh, MFT, CHt.'s Summary

I am a Licensed Psychotherapist, and a Clinical Hypnotherapist. I am called the "Stop Sabotaging Expert", I help people overcome negative beliefs, and behaviors in order to successfully achieve their goals in relationships, finances, health, and or happiness. I have been providing individual, family, group, and couples therapy for over 10 years, and have worked with people of various ages and issues ranging from age 4- 90 years of age. I have also completed a one year intensive program in Hypnotherapy, and am a certified Clinical Hypnotherapist, and NLP Practitioner. I am also certified in Psycho-Kinesiology (Muscle Testing). My husband, Arash Shahangian is a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, and Clinical Hypnotherapist, and we have built a practice in providing a powerful combination of Psychotherapy, Life Coaching, Clinical Hypnotherapy, Psycho-Kinesiology, and other powerful tools achieving amazing results.

I personally believe based on my own life experience and others that I have worked with, along with research and observation, that most of our anxiety and stress is due to our inability to say no and set healthy boundaries. Therefore I focus on helping my clients increase their confidence, set healthy boundaries, and gain a strong sense of self and control in order to create and maintain the life they want.

I also specialize in relationship therapy/coaching helping people in their current relationships and those looking for a perfect match.

What makes me unique is that I provide my clients coping skills and tools that they use on their own to maintain and reinforce positive changes. Examples are relaxation techniques, communication skills, and self-hypnosis tools that are easy, and become automatic for my clients.
