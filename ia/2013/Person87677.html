Martin Potter's Summary

A highly successful Finance Director and respected business partner, delivering insight and possessing the ability to both challenge the business and secure improved performance.

Played a leading role in the successful integration of ICI into Akzo Nobel and contributed to all major, and critical, transactions of this Fortune 500 company over a 4 year period (with an accumulated major transaction value in 2007 / 2008 of £18.7 billion).

Combines a commercial acumen with extensive international business, having working throughout the European Community and in key countries of both Asia and the Americas such as Brazil, China, India, South Korea, Turkey and the USA.

Demonstrates an assured style at the highest levels and with external constituencies, having been chairman or director in many boards and joint ventures and a period as pensions trustee chairman.

Strong track record of driving profitable growth and delivering results through effective change management and the development of high achieving organisations.

B.Sc. Economics (First Class Honours 1979), London School of Economics

CIMA Associate (Institute of Management Accountants) with first time passes.

Specialties

Strong problem solving and communications skills.
Business change, reorganisation, mergers, acquisitions and divestments. Thinks strategically while maintaining a focus on the detail. Compliance, including internal control structures. Pensions Trustee Chairman. Global process manufacturing.
