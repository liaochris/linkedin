Karen Kratz-Miller's Experience

Co-Owner

Broad Spectrum

August 1993 – Present (20 years)

Broad Spectrum creates innovative faux finishes and murals on walls and ceilings. Our finishes and murals are custom designed to complement your decor. We offer a broad spectrum of faux finish techniques from old and new world textures, fresco, Italian plasters, gilding, antiquing and glazing, to new innovative finishes. Our murals range from impressionistic Italian country sides to American folk art vistas and graphic designs.
