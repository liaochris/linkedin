Suzanne Petryshyn's Summary

Suzanne is a learning specialist and Brain Jacks is your solution for technology and learning!

Are you wanting to get online with your business or work activities and you are not quite sure how to do it? Do you want a dynamic and engaging web presence? We can help you get online to claim your web space with our Joomla! powered web packages. This option goes hand in hand with technology and learning and we can offer you complete elearning packages or website packages as a stand alone option. ALL of our packages include mobile app functions so your elearning and websites are viewable on phones, tablets and other mobile devices.

For our learning packages, we provide you with credible and cost effective professional development training designed and formatted using instructional design principles for technology. Our learning packages can be delivered in a classroom setting, online or blended using both classroom and online delivery methods. When you choose Brain Jacks, we provide you with the technology to host your courses online. We will design and upload your curriculum online in moodle, our content management system. Content can include social media, quizzes, discussion forums, videos, blogs, presentations, wikis, voicethread, videoconferencing and any other components needed to create an engaging learning environment for your audience. This design service provides you with both flexibility and affordability at all levels of your organization.

Suzanne is an internationally published author in both print based and eBook formats and has provided keynote presentations and conference sessions for international, national, provincial and local groups with topics related to change, mediation, conflict management, being online, social media and eLearning.

Join our Facebook community, follow us on Twitter and Pinterest. Visit our website for more information!

Suzanne Petryshyn's Publications

  • TACT (Teens and Conflict Together): A Facilitator's Guide

    • CreateSpace
    • February 12, 2010
    Authors: Suzanne Petryshyn

    TACT (Teens and Conflict Together) is a six-session, skills based program designed for facilitation by professionals in school and community systems who work with youth. TACT (Teens and Conflict Together) is structured to provide youth with opportunities for reflection of personal communication styles, conflict management styles and beliefs about their relationships with each other, their peers and the world around them. Skills are presented using fun and interactive games to both encourage and empower youth to employ a problem solving process for conflict management and conflict resolution.

  • Gunther the Goat and Friends

    • BookBaby
    • February 12, 2012
    Authors: Suzanne Petryshyn

    Gunther the Goat lives on a ranch with his friends. This eBook captures the adventures of Gunther with his friends. illustrations include real life accounts of Gunther and his ranch life. The companion website to this series of books includes live video footage of Gunther and his friends in their daily routine. The author, and her family, believe in sharing their ranch life and activities with children who may have never visited a ranch or may have enjoyed a visit to a petting zoo. This first book in this series is intended to provide an introduction to Gunther and his character. The books in this series are designed to be both fun and educational for children around the world.
