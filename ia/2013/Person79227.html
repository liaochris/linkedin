Laurie Lonsdorf's Summary

I am among a rare and growing breed of former residents returning to Lansing after decades of living on the West Coast. What brought me back were the amazing people, the low cost of living and a growing technology community.

My passion is helping small business leaders and communities connect and communicate with people who will grow their organizations. As a marketing consultant in Seattle, I had the pleasure of helping mom-and-pop shops, micro businesses and small startups grow their companies through strategic marketing and creative copywriting. Today I'm helping those sames kinds of businesses in Lansing and across Ingham, Eaton and Clinton counties through my work at SBTDC.

About a decade ago, I was inspired to adopt The Princess of Persuasion™ as my marketing alter ego. I wanted to create a distinctive marketing impression that was fun and memorable for the people I connected with—in person, in print and online. Make no mistake, I’m all professional, but with a twist of wacky and an occasional tiara thrown in for good measure. I came to Lansing to reinvent myself, and found that while The Princess brand no longer fits my mission, I’m still very much the princess at heart.

Specialties

• SEO content development
