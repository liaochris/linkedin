Anna DeBattiste's Summary

Anna spent ten years as an in-house human resource generalist before leaving the corporate world in 2002. She founded Tango Training in 2006, and New England Crane School in 2010. Tango Training offers customized program development and delivery for teams in the areas of leadership development, supervisory skills, human resources, teambuilding, diversity, customer service, business writing, presentation skills, and communication/interpersonal skills. Anna has delivered programs for public sector clients such as Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA), Department of Education, the US Forest Service, EPA, the National Weather Service, Defense Finance and Accounting Services (DFAS), the US Army, the US Air Force, the Social Security Administration, the Institute of Museum and Library Science, and various US military bases. Private sector clients include Metlife, Nike, Rockwell Collins, General Dynamics and Sauer Dan-Foss. She also contracts as a freelance writer specializing in training and human resource related projects, and adventure sports.

Believing that what you do for fun defines you as much as your work, Anna has spent significant time in the past decade teaching alpine skiing, competing in adventure races, and volunteering for a search and rescue team in a Colorado mountain community.

Specialties

Training specialities include team building, diversity awareness, human resource topics, management/supervisory skill development, leadership, business writing, communication & interpersonal skills, presentation skills and meeting facilitation.
Writing specialties include training programs, employee handbooks, policy manuals, public relations pieces and employee newsletters.
