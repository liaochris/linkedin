Mike Da Ponte's Summary

A entrepreneurial marketing executive with over 15 years of experience, Mike has a proven ability to build organizations and motivate teams in a fast paced, high performance environment. Mike has experience in both large organizations and start up ventures. A results oriented business executive with extensive experience in developing integrated consumer centric and brand-building strategies .

About BIMM:

Direct and Responsive...we are a Direct and Digital Response Communications company of 75 people, helping Canada’s largest and most respected brands advance their B2C and B2B acquisition and Customer Relationship Management initiatives for over 30 years.

A full-service agency. With the emphasis on service...our non traditional agency structure ensures our clients business is managed by our most senior team. We believe in an all-hands on deck approach for all of our clients.

Not just an Agency a Partner...our organization is uniquely positioned to help clients uncover new consumer insights through our proprietary analytic tools and bring those insights to life using our award winning creative team.

Award winning, yes. But customer winning first...in our 32 year history we've been recognized by our peers for delivering work that simply WORKS (30+ CMA awards and 7 International DMA Echo awards). In the end that's really all that matters isn't it.
