► Ellen Rogin, CPA, CFP®'s Summary

Ellen Rogin, CPA, CFP® is a leading expert on money and women. As a sought after speaker, writer and consultant Ellen has freed people to breathe easier about their money and focus on how they could use it to express what they are about. She is co-author of Great with Money: The Women's Guide to Prosperity.

Specialties

Financial issues relating to wealth planning for women.
