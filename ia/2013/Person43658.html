I established Excel-Expert in 2009 to help businesses to get greater control of their data

► Our Expertise

♦ Automation – By automating office tasks, we have helped companies save tens of thousands of man-hours. If you are manhandling data in anyway, you are also wasting time and we can help you.

♦ Error Prevention | Error Capture – One of the biggest sources of errors in your data happens at the point of data entry. Our error prevention systems can control and validate data as it is entered, while our error prevention systems can help identify and eliminate corrupt or faulty data that is already in your systems.

♦ Spreadsheet Auditing – A study by the University of Hawaii discovered that over 80% of spreadsheets in use contained errors, of which 90% could be classed as business critical. Our auditing service ensures our spreadsheets perform their roles correctly and that your spreadsheets are error free.

♦ Data Manipulation | Data Cleansing – Moving data between systems or reusing data for something it wasn’t intended, can be an overly difficult process. Our tools can translate data and repurpose it for new tasks.

♦ Reporting | Dashboards – The amount of data available to use in our businesses grows everyday but our visibility of the facts becomes more and more clouded. Reporting and dashboard tools will allow you to see through the numbers to see the true facts behind them.

♦ Bespoke Applications – Utilising the power of Excel, Access, Word, Outlook and many other Microsoft off the shelf products, we develop bespoke applications for far less than the traditional bespoke solutions. This is ideal if you have only a temporary need for your applications.

► What back up do we offer?

As well as offering a one year warranty on all programming we offer a unique 90 day free tweaking period.
