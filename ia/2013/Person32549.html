David Schoeffel's Summary

I am a passionate Operations Manager with over 15 years of experience. I have developed a knack for having a futuristic view of Operations Management to support start-up and ramp-up organizations, the ability to transform chaos into one cohesive unit one step at a time, and a passion for developing technology to help people achieve efficiencies and to provide consistent information to customers.

My Operations Management has spanned many activities from test and manufacturing engineering, to QA and purchasing, shipping, receiving, and manufacturing groups. I have an ability to establish and maintain systems to sustain double digit growth.

My most significant accomplishment was forging a coherent group of people together to form a manufacturing team at SurgiVet. My attention to detail, and other qualities, was instrumental in this start-up company’s growth from $4M to $14M in sales in just under 7 years. I am very proud of the fact that we enjoyed a better than 50% gross margin while shipping the average customer order in 1-2 days from time of order.

I have a Bachelors Degree in Management Systems from the Milwaukee School of Engineering. I also have an Advanced Technical Certificate in Lean Manufacturing from Waukesha County Technical College and numerous other training classes. Some of these include: Photoshop, Advance lighting techniques, Microsoft Access, and Mastering the Art of Networking.

I am also a creative photographer offering photography services specializing in marketing photography, family portraits, high school senior portraits, group or individual portrait work, and youth sports team photography. Hundreds of people and organizations have realized how I can help them look their best. I love to work with people on their photo needs and desires. View my website for samples of my work.

In May, 2010 I completed Strengths Finder 2.0 My top 5 strengths are identified as: Harmony, Maximizer, Activator, Communication, and Positivity.

Specialties

Operations Management, planning, organizing, training, and leading people in our efforts to satisfy customers. Design and implement systems to promote and sustain double digit growth.

Marketing photography, family, high school senior, group, and individual portraits. Expert in Photoshop and photo lighting techniques.
