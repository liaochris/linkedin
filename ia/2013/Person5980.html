Amy Laine's Summary

Skilled brand marketing professional with proven experience in creating and executing revenue-generating integrated marketing solutions for media companies, major marketers, independent business owners and non-profit organizations. Opened the marketing consultancy hyperlaine to work with independent business owners and startups, along with top-tier media clients.

Clients include: The Atlantic, Ars Technica and WIRED (Conde Nast), Bloomberg L.P., DailyCandy (NBCUniversal), MicroOffice, TechStars, Tarisio Auctions, The (next level) Storytelling Academy.

Speaking engagements: Cannes Lions: Storytelling for Brands workshop panelist; Panelist at American Chamber of Commerce (AMCHAM) "In Good Company" -- Storytelling for Small Businesses event and workshop, Stockholm, Sweden.

Specialties: strategic marketing, copywriting, event production, integrated marketing, promotions strategy and execution, new business and partnership development, social media, mentoring, PR and media relations
