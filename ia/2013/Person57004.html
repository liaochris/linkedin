Jaime Villafuerte's Summary

For the past 15 years I have been applying continuous improvement in the Americas, Europe & Asia for Fortune 500 companies. My responsibilities now include strategy development, organizational transformation and talent development.

My contribution to the Lean Six Sigma community includes authoring "The Lean Six Sigma Deployment Memory Jogger" and "Creating a Kaizen Culture: Align the Organization, Achieve Breakthrough Results, and Sustain the Gains."

I am a certified Lean practitioner and Master Black Belt by ASQ, AME, SME and Shingo Prize. I served as Baldrige National Quality Examiner in 2010 and I served as a speaker for national and international ASQ, IIE, ASME, and IndustryWeek Best Plants Conferences

Specialties: Strategy, gap analysis, business system design, change leadership, talent development and process improvement.
