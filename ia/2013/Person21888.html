Champagne Jayne Powell's Honors and Awards

  • Champagne Educator of the Year 2012

    The Champagne Summit
    • February 2012

    At the third Champagne Summit organised by Harpers magazine in London in February 2012 Jayne Powell (aka Champagne Jayne) beat Master of Wine Richard Bampfield and CIVC European Ambassador Laura Clay, to take out the prestigious Champagne Educator of the Year Award.

  • Author Best French Wine Book (Australia) 2011

    Gourmand World CookBook Awards
    • March 2012

    At the Paris Cook Book Fair organised by Gourmand in March 2012 Jayne Powell was awarded Best French Wine Book 2011 (Australia) for her first authored 240 page lavishly illustrated coffee table "bible" of champagne entitled "Champagne - Behind The Bubbles".

  • Market Builder of the Year

    EMAP Business Communications
    • June 1997

    At the Emap Business Communications Awards in 1997 Jayne Powell won "Market Builder of the Year" for her successful launch and development of International Risk Management Magazine & its portfolio of multi-media products (profitable website, industry awards, global conference newsletters & custom publishing division).

  • Champagne Dame

    L'Ordre des Coteaux de Champagne
    • September 2012

    At the Chateau de Bligny in Champagne in September 2012 Jayne Powell was awarded the prestigious title of "Dame Chevalier de L'Ordre des Coteaux de Champagne" by the Commandeur Pierre Emmanuel Taittinger for her services to the champagne industry as a journalist, educator and global ambassador for champagne. Since 1956 approximately 4,000 Chevaliers around the world have been inducted into this special order.
