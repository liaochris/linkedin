Doug Montgomery's Summary

While my peers here in Florida played sports and had after-school activities, in which I dabbled in Baseball for a few years in High School, I spent most of my time inside learning how to use DOS on my 486. What a prize it was. And since 1998 (http://goo.gl/DYSiZ) I've been doing my best to help shape the web into what it is today. (My next Chihuahua will be named "Lycos"!)

I've built more than 300 websites for clients and companies over the years, and soon I realized I wanted the world to see them, which is how I happened on Search Engine Optimization in early 2004. After some schooling, and some amazing mentoring from some of the most intelligent men and women I have ever had the chance to speak with, I decided it was time to begin my journey as a total-package.

So in 2013, I now provide my services to clients all over the world, from simple website re-designs, to full-blow marketing campaigns for amazing products and programs, no matter the business size.

And I freakin' love it.

I'm very thankful for the internet and the ability to consistently provide my expertise, and grow, all while doing what I love. Which is help business owners harness the power of the internet, and begin putting the word trust back into marketing.

Specialties: Web Design, Logo Design, CSS, Php, SEM, SEO, SEO copywriting, Brand Awareness, Content Creation, Social Media Management
