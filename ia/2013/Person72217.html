Karen Spiegelman's Summary

Writer and editor with two decades of professional experience across all media. Has written for magazines, copy-edited for television, and line-edited for Web publications. Exceptional editing sense and grammar skills. Specializes in humorous approach to arcane material.

Specialties

Technology how-tos, casual games, video games, production for press or Internet
