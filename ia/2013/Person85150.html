Teaching UX Optimization at NYU User Experience Certificate - Course Description: UX Optimization is the process of continuous learning through research, experimenting and analysis. In this practicum students will work with a local SME to help define business goals, KPI metrics and customer segments. Depending on the SME students will develop target customers, setup and track analytics, develop A / B or multivariate testing, and analyze data. Students will present a report with recommendations to senior stakeholders about the risk and opportunity based on stated business goals for conversion, registration, abandonment rates, support calls, time on page and reduced customer frustration. The course will introduce the leading reporting and analytics tools, the strategies behind building actionable real-time business intelligence that demonstrate what is working and what is not. This course will also give students an introduction to both quantitative and qualitative research principles and methods that are used to understand why to needle is moving one way and not the other. Students should be familiar with basic web technologies like HTTP, cookies, SEO and Google Analytics.
