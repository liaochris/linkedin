Chirag Patel, MD's Summary

Hansa Medical Groupe, LLC strives to provide elite level medical care and health management to their clients. Established in 2007, HMG is a retainer based practice model, requiring a yearly fee for membership. Membership is limited allowing the physicians to focus their medical skills and effort at an extremely high level. The practice also provides medical care to professional athletes & their families, requiring unique services & discretion.

Dr Patel has been practicing for the past 11 years. His medical schooling was at the combined 6 yr BA Biology/MD program at University of Missouri- Kansas City. He completed his residency in Internal Medicine at Northwestern Medical Center/St Joseph Hospital in Chicago, IL

Dr Patel also developed the practice, Internal Medicine Consultants, providing care to patients in NW Indiana, for the past 8 years.
