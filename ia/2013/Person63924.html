Jim Hogan's Summary

I am a passionate teacher, coach, trainer, author & public speaker. My 17 years in the mortgage business and knack for using social media strategies as a tool to grow business has given me a unique perspective to help my clients recognize the unlimited possibilities available. By connecting the dots between sales, social media, business networking and referral marketing, my clients are able to build brand awareness and own their specific target market.

I help build Social Media Platforms that support your current marketing strategies, create a Strategic Referral Marketing plan and teach the tactical skills to successfully implement your plan from all angles. Once you are able to master networking, referral marketing and social media strategies, your brand will explode.

My training and coaching skills were honed during my tenure at XINNIX, the Mortgage Academy of Excellence as VP of Training. At this boot-camp style training center, I was instrumental in course design, documentation, testing and delivery of the 6-week course.

I am a member of the British American Business Group (Board of Directors since since 2008), which is one of the largest and most active bi-lateral chambers of commerce in Atlanta. This organization has had a major influence on international networking in and around Atlanta for many years.

Early in 2011, I became a published author after my study of Improv with the Blank Stage in Marietta, Georgia. A collaborative effort, “Improv to Improve Your Business; Using the Principles of Improvisation to Foster Communication, Creativity & Innovation.” I enjoy speaking on business communication, creativity and innovation to businesses and groups throughout Atlanta.

Specialties

Referral Marketing, Networking, Social Media & Web Solutions Strategies, Sales Training, Small Business Planning and Consulting, Public Speaker.
