Carey S. Barnes' Summary

Carey Barnes is the founder and owner of CSB Consulting, LLC. Carey has over 15 years experience in sales within the Fire Fighting Community; 10 of those in the Aircraft Rescue & Fire Fighting industry. She is a former Executive Board Member of the Aircraft Rescue Fire Fighting Working Group (ARFFWG), working alongside many industry leaders.

Furthermore, she has 12 years of experience in obtaining GSA and other government contracts as well as selling to the U.S. Government including the U.S. Military and Airport sectors. In a previous 10 year position at Crash Rescue Equipment Service, she moved up within the company from Product Manager to Airport/Military Sales Manager to International Sales Manager. In each position, she has dramatically grown the product lines that she managed domestically, as well as internationally.

Carey has been successful at managing multiple product lines and developing relationships with customers around the world. She has gained much experience from marketing research to developing marketing strategies and planning. Carey has lived in Europe (Paris, France) and has created business relationships not only in Europe, but in the Middle East, Africa and Asia as well.

Carey Barnes graduated with a B.B.A. in Marketing from Tarleton State University. She is currently getting her Executive MBA from the International School of Management in Paris, France. Through her Executive MBA program, she has studied on 3 continents—USA, Europe and Asia. Carey currently represents several manufacturers in building new business within the firefighting industry world-wide and she assists companies in doing business with the U.S. Government.
