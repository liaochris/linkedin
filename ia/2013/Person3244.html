Alan Wight's Summary

An Event Producer & Meeting Designer helping organisations with the preparation, delivery and measurement of their meetings and events. Co-founder of a company based in Manchester UK established in 1992.

We design events using a proven framework starting with understand the audience and the desired outcomes, then drawing from a wide range of tools and techniques to plan an effective agenda, select the ideal venue and deliver a successful event - ideally with a measure of ROI.

Our core services are Event Production, Meeting Support, Media Production and Hotel & Venue Booking. We are constantly growing our meeting and event 'toolbox' - developing news ways of producing live meetings using conceptual and technological tools i.e. different formats including Events Apps, Live Streaming, Social Media tools, the Backchannel, Hybrid events and crowdsourcing.

At the heart of what we do we aim to provide well balanced events that balance information, inspiration and interaction.

Specialties: meeting architecture, event design, event apps, conference, events, parties, teambuild, scriptwriting, design, video production, travel, venue booking, hybrid events, backchannel, visitor attractions
