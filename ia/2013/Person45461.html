Francesc Gomez-Morales' Summary

Managing Partner @itnig. Struggling to help internet startups to grow in a changing environment.

I studied Physics (BSc) and Optics (MSc) in the University of Barcelona. Back in 2008 I quit research and focused on the unlimited possibilities of the World Wide Web, first for non-profits (NGO Intervida) and after that for Enterprises (Elogia).

In 2010 I graduated in Brand Community Management (LaSalle Business Engineering School). Since then I've been Professor of Social Media Analytics in several Postgraduate Courses. In 2011 I founded Enconexo, a network of online reputation management experts. In 2012 I joined Itnig as Partner.
