  • #MacroMesoMicro Systemic Territory Framework from the perspective of Social Innovation

    • #MacroMesoMicro
    • June 23, 2013
    Authors: Dr. Igor Calzada, Ph.D., Domenico Di Siena, adolfo chautón

    The following publication was made by Dr Igor Calzada, Domenico Di Siena and Adolfo Chautón. The aim of the publication to outline the importance of the three territorial scales, #Macro #Meso #Micro in the implementation of Social Innovation Processes in a specific Territory. The outcome is a Systemic Framework to diagnose and intervene territories from the Top-Down and to the Bottom-Up, assuming the contradictions in the system and coming up with a complex deliberation task.
