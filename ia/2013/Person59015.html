Jason B. Clubb CPA's Summary

Hands-on finance/accounting expert with experience as Auditor, Controller, CFO, and entrepreneur. Interested in helping small to mid-sized business owners and executives grow their companies by improving the timeliness, relevance, and accessibility of financial information which will enable them to make better business decisions and focus more of their time on strategic initiatives.

Specialties

All aspects of business financial management - from basic accounting/bookkeeping to the development of dynamic forecasts/projections that are tied to identified business drivers, to the design and implementation of timely and actionable financial reporting/analysis systems, to the development and implementation of workflow/process improvement initiatives, to strategic business and tax planning.

Who We Are
Uneo Business Services LLC is a unique business services firm located in Winston-Salem, NC. We bring value to our city by helping businesses succeed and by supporting community restoration initiatives. We believe that these are not mutually exclusive endeavors, but rather each one depends upon the other. Businesses cannot succeed when communities lie in ruins and devastation; likewise, communities cannot be restored without the industriousness and generosity of businesses.

What We Do
The business services offered by Uneo Business Services LLC fall into three broad categories: Business Start-Up Services, Finance & Accounting Services, and Business Optimization Services.

Business Start-Up Services
Every business in existence today had its start in somebody’s mind—a dream, a vision, an idea that was eventually translated into a plan of action and executed. We know what it takes to make your business dream a practical reality.

Finance & Accounting Services
No matter what business you’re in, you will not survive if you do not properly manage your finances. Properly recording and accounting for business transactions, actively managing your cash, developing realistic forecasts and budgets—all of these things are critical to the long-term viability of your business.

Business Optimization Services
In today’s fast-paced business environment, companies must foster a culture of continuous process improvement in order to stay competitive. Ensuring that all aspects of your business are harmonized and operating efficiently is critically important. We offer two key services to help you optimize the performance of your business—Dashboard Services and Workflow Services.
