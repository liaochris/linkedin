Deborah Osgood's Projects

  • Dropout Prevention Program: High School Aged-Youth

    • October 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Deborah Osgood, Jobs for America's Graduates, Goodwill GoodGuides, The Brentwood School, Robert Lister Academy

    MyILP for Life represents an empirically validated 6 Step approach that engages youth in a self-discovery, hands-on process of assessment, career identification, goal setting, life mapping and networking, which culminates in the development of an Individual Learning Plan (ILP) unique to their learning objectives, style and aspirations. With a stronger sense of self, a life purpose and documented goals, educational content is given a more meaningful context. As a result, the ILP becomes a facilitation tool for bringing students, parents, educators and members of the community together around a shared perspective to collaborate in the positive development of the youth.

  • Educational Gateway to Promoting Innovation

    • October 1998 to Present
    Team Members: Deborah Osgood, Government Agencies, Economic Development Agencies, Business-to-Business Influence Leaders, Chambers of Commerce, Trade Associations, Academic Institutions, Industrial Development Corporations, Entrepreneurs, Veterans, Women

    Innovation Through Collaboration: Educational Gateways leverage people, process and technology to facilitate connections between resource users and resource providers who share interests and objectives. The Business Utility Zone Gateway, for example, is connecting over 125,000 individuals each month to the resources they require to start, grow and succeed in business. The MyILPforLife Gateway is being developed to facilitate similar connections between students in grades 5 through 12 and those who offer resources and opportunities for promoting learning and career development.

  • Adults in Transition: Get It, Want It, Go for It!

    • May 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Deborah Osgood

    Stuck in a creative rut? Facing a challenge? Want or need to pursue a new career? Considering launching your own business? Whatever your objective is for your future, the Adults in Transition program represents a step-by-step, hands on process for discovering who you are, what you want and how to go for it! Through a facilitative, highly structured process, you will uncover your inherent strengths, learn how they align with adding value in ways that generate income, and launch an action plan for turning your dreams into reality.
