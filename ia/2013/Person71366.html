Justin Rubner's Summary

I'm a communications officer, writer and producer with a deep background in journalism. My reporting experience drives everything I do, from brand storytelling and public relations to producing my own TV show pilot.

On the communications side, I help tech startups with positioning, editorial, PR and messaging strategy.
My biggest role is VP of Marketing Communications for Mahala, a payments technology startup.

On the entertainment side, I'm the creator and executive producer of Breaking the Law, which investigates victimless crimes and the everyday American lawbreaker.

Previously, I was a news reporter for five years at Atlanta Business Chronicle, where I covered technology. I'm also an U.S. Air Force vet with a degree in Journalism from The University of Georgia.

Specialties: Copywriting, branding, producing, positioning, message development, editorial management, news strategy, PR and editing.
