Maria Fox's Summary

I am a first-year PhD student in Biological Anthropology at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I recently completed an MA thesis at the University of Cincinnati on the lumbopelvic anatomy of Neandertals and the biomechanics of walking with a reduced lumbar lordosis. My research interests include functional morphology, skeletal anatomy, biomechanics, and human evolution.

I graduated from UC in 2011 with a BS in Digital Design (from the School of Design in DAAP), where I completed a research capstone project entitled "Out of Africa: The Story of Human Evolutionary Adaptation".
