Bret Pawlowski's Summary

For nearly 15 years Brands In Motion and our divisions have specialized in integrated digital and mobile branding for a select group of clients. Our client list includes Fortune 500 companies and brands such as Jack Daniel's, Procter & Gamble, Paramount Pictures, Hilton and Infiniti. From gigantic digital billboards and interactive kiosks in high traffic areas, JumboScreens at races, concerts and events to connecting via personal mobile devices our company delivers unique brand content to consumers when and where they are the most receptive.

Brands In Motion's array of mobile solutions include: interactive applications, NFC, SMS, check-in / rewards,mobile sweepstakes, mobile couponing, mobile booking, voting & polling campaigns, trivia applications, loyalty programs and social mobile advertising. Additionally in 2013 we are adding a number of branded mobile games and patent pending software applications to enhance our repertoire.

Yinski is the Digital Media Agency that manages the creative development, strategic deployment and data analysis for a select group of Internet properties. Using Algorithmic Marketing and Predictive Analysis we put our fingers on the pulse of a business and create integrated digital marketing ecosystems that are focused on monetizing Internet Assets using Mobile Commerce, Social Media, Out Of Home Media, SEO, Conversion Marketing, Interactive Games and Digital Video Distribution.

Khmeritrade was founded in 2012 to create opportunities between The USA and The Kingdom of Cambodia. We are currently involved in projects relating to Trade, Technology, Tourism, Casino Gaming, Energy, Advertising & Marketing.

Brands In Motion services America, Mexico and Canada from our Headquarters in Las Vegas, Nevada. Khmeritrade Group is pleased to announce the opening of operations in Phnom Penh.

Specialties

Mobile Marketing, Digital Media Development, Software & Game Development, Content Management, Proximity and Mobile Coupons, Interactive Consumer Promotions, Guerrilla and Event Marketing, Out-Of-Home Media Placement and Digital Media Buying for LED, LCD and Interactive Kiosks. Check-In and Reward Programs for (LBS) Location Based Service, Loyalty Card Printing and SMS Promotions.
