Bob Andelman's Summary

Bob Andelman is the author or co-author of 16 books, including:

• Keep Your Eye On the Marshmallow: Gain Focus and Resilience-And Come Out Ahead (with Joachim De Posada; Berkley, May 2013)

• Fans Not Customers (with Vernon Hill; Profile, October 2012)

• "Mind Over Business" (with Ken Baum; Perigee/Penguin, March 2012)

• "The Consulate" (spy fiction, with Thomas R. Stutler; MIHI Trust, March 2011)

• "The Profiler" (with Pat Brown; Hyperion/Voice, May 2010)

• "Four Seasons: The Philosophy of a Business" (by Isadore Sharp, founder and chairman of Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts; Portfolio/Penguin, 2009), Did not author or co-author this book but came in and helped Sharp near the end of the writing process by conducting dozens of interviews with Four Seasons managers and executives and contributing additional material to the finished manuscript.

• "Will Eisner: A Spirited Life" (Dark Horse/M Press; October 2005)

• "The Corporate Athlete" (with Jack Groppel; John Wiley, 2000)

• "Built From Scratch" (with Bernie Marcus & Arthur Blank, co-founders of The Home Depot; Crown Business, 1999)

• "The Profit Zone" (with Adrian Slywotzky; Crown Business, 1997)

• "Mean Business" (with Albert J. Dunlap; Crown Business, 1996)

• "Stadium For Rent" (McFarland and Company, 1993)

Agents: Jane Dystel and Michael Bourret, Dystel & Goderich Literary Management, http://www.dystel.com

Bob Andelman is the host, producer and writer of "Mr. Media®" a celebrity and media newsmaker video and podcast show seen at MrMedia.com and widely syndicated via iTunes, Stitcher, YouTube, Frequency, BlogTalkRadio and more.

The show began its seventh year of audio and video podcasting on February 1, 2013. The show's archive includes more than 1,000 interviews.

Specialties

Ghostwriting: Collaborating with people in business, sports and entertainment who have expertise in their field but need assistance telling their story or sharing their wisdom in book form. I work with title credit or without. Books are usually for major publishers but I also work on self-published or in-house books.

General Assignment: I also have experience as a newspaper and magazine correspondent, including several years with the St. Petersburg Times, Newsweek and BusinessWeek.

Bob Andelman's Publications

  • Built Frm Scratch

    • Crown Business
    • April 1999
    Authors: Bob Andelman, Bernie Marcus, Arthur Blank

    One of the greatest entrepreneurial success stories of the past twenty years. Built from Scratch is the story of how two incredibly determined and creative people--and their associates--built The Home Depot from nothing to 761 stores and $30 billion in sales in a mere twenty years.

    Built from Scratch tells many colorful stories associated with The Home Depot's founding and meteoric rise; shows that a company can be a tough, growth-oriented competitor and still maintain a high sense of responsibility to the community; and provides great lessons useful to people in any business, from start-ups to the Fortune 500.

  • Bankers as Brokers

    • MacGraw Hill
    Authors: Bob Andelman, Betty Carlin, Merlin Gackle

    When published, Bankers As Brokers was the first and the definitive book on a new industry supporting the sale of mutual funds and annuities by banks.

  • Fans Not Customers: How to Create Growth Companies in a No Growth World

    • Profile Books
    • October 2012
    Authors: Bob Andelman, Vernon Hill

    These are tough times for all of us—but it is still possible to defy gravity and create massively successful new ventures, turn-round lumbering giants, and go for growth. The secret lies in not just satisfying customers but in amazing and delighting them.

    Vernon Hill was the founder of the hugely successful Commerce Bank in the United States and Metro Bank in the United Kingdom. He is one of only a handful of chief executives ever to be a member of the Forbes 20/20 club: to have stayed in the same job for over two decades paying more than 20 percent returns every year.
