Carlos Scarpero's Summary

I am an internet marketing consultant who specializes in LinkedIn training, email marketing, SEO and web design.

I'm a trainer and facilitator of a LinkedIn workshop called Mr. Leads Power Networking that shows business owners how to use LinkedIn effectively.
