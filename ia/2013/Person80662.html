Liam Dunphy's Summary

As a Learning Leader with TAS responsibilities at NBCS I have the opportunity to lead a team in an exciting learning space and look for continuous improvement in the pedagogical practices that support innovative learning.

As an ICT integrator and teacher of IT subjects, I can utilise my vast experience in IT to bring reality into the classroom for my students. I like to help teachers to integrate the vast wealth of ICT resources to create rich learning environments where students are engaged by the content and teachers feel empowered to facilitate it.

I believe these skills are essential to the facilitation of C21 eLearning which students need as a basic right in their education. I aim to equip students with the skills to be creative and adaptable to the uncertain and ever changing demands that they will encounter in their future. I challenge them to problem solve so that they can take on the challenges in their future. While faciliating C21 eLearning skills by being a role model for learning with my students, I hope that I can instill in my students a love for learning that will allow them to become lifelong learners as they take on the future with hope and the skills to succeed in a future that they can be proactive in choosing for themselves.

As the new Learning Area Manager for TAS, I embarked on a process of using our wonderful new learning space to explore innovative ways to improve our pedagogy. I introduced choices for students to allow them to navigate their path through Technology in Stage 4. I introduced team-teaching in our new open learning space with our focus on project/challenge based learning. We started to flip some of our learning so that students get greater exposure to experiential project based learning. My goal is to ultimately gamify the TAS curriculum starting with Stage 4 and working our way eventually to encompass Stages 5 & 6.
