Jason Gogela's Summary

I have been a web designer for twelve years and am an experienced expert in web and program usability and pushing complex relational information to clients in the civil and defense worlds. I am an accredited interrogator and briefer and have broad experience in effectively communicating with people of different educational and cultural backgrounds. My writing experience includes: strategic level DoD threat assessments, technical manuals for the decommissioning of nuclear reactors, sales copy for growing businesses, and standard operating procedure manuals for a wide variety of operations. I have a broad range of experience in computer, network, and electronic repair. I am absolutely fascinated with technology... to think that the site you are currently viewing, the systems that run the network you pulled this page from, and the computer or mobile device you are using right now all spawned as a natural evolution of something as simple as the first transistor is amazing to me. The societal implications are staggering, and I can think of nothing I would rather do than push the boundary of the technologies we may sometimes take for granted further still.
