Jeff Weinstein's Summary

My fascination advantage is witty communication, unique expertise and dramatic results.

For over 26 years, I have devoted my life to helping accident victims in the great state of Texas recover from their injuries, both physical and emotional. In that time, I have seen many unfortunate events, most of which could have been avoided if not for the carelessness of another.

Assisting in the recovery process is a great feeling. Accident victims are often lost, with nowhere to turn and often no one to turn to. If I can help these people in anyway, it gives my life and my profession a sense of purpose and a serious mission to follow.

Today, my goal is to help prevent accidents from occurring by discussing safety and injury prevention. Promoting safety and protecting rights should go hand in hand. The future of my children and the children of others certainly depends on it.

Specialties: Claims against Distracted Drivers including Claims against Drunk Drivers, Claims against Drivers using a cell phone that cause accidents, Claims against Intoxicated Drivers and Claims against Drivers that are texting and Driving
