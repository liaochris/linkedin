Beryl Preuschmann's Summary

An avid photographer and traveller, I relocated to Edinburgh in 2007. I provide a wide range of experience in Online Media and Marketing, Events, PR & Comms and Journalism.

I am an outgoing person with excellent interpersonal and networking skills and have a strong ability to project manage and identify and resolve problems efficiently, whether technical, organisational or personal relations. I have a wealth of experience in working in both an office environment and outside the office, for example I recently managed the shoot on location in Scotland for Germany’s X Factor. I have an outstanding ability to work in an International environment and I was able to prove that devising and implementing international PR and media strategies for VisitScotland's and Skyscanner's main European markets.

I am experienced in working in busy environments within the media, production, marketing and PR industry where I have been involved in project management, project launches, social media activities as well as editorial work. I have outstanding communication and negotiation skills and I am confident interacting across international borders with any stakeholders or VIPs / celebrities, having gained my natural reliance during my time working for a German TV station and working for the Scottish tourism board.

I am fluent in three languages (English, German and French) and have basic knowledge of Italian, Spanish and Russian.
