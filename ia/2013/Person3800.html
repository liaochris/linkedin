Alexandra Stein, MPT's Summary

With a Masters Degree in Physical Therapy, Alexandra Stein has 11 years of experience in orthopedic physical therapy. She believes that optimal progress in physical therapy is achieved through one-on-one patient care, and her clinical experience includes working with individuals of varied age and fitness levels.

Alexandra is the owner and chief physical therapist of AMS Physical Therapy, Inc., an out-patient orthopedic physical therapy clinic in Southern California. AMS PT specializes in post-surgical orthopedic conditions, postural dysfunction, joint pain, balance disorders, sport specific injuries, pregnancy related low back pain, and much more. AMS PT focuses on providing quality patient care and optimizing physical therapy to reach desired results.
