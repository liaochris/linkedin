Chris Bradley's Summary

I have worked in the publishing/printing industry for the past 18 years.

While working in the cookbook fundraising division and speaking with 1000’s of customers I recognized a need for a simple, fast and cost effective publishing company geared towards any writer with any type of publication. I took my near lifetime family involvement with printing and customer knowledge and created Instantpublisher.com in 2000. Instantpublisher.com has helped thousands of authors with various publications ranging from novels to company manuals.

In 2008 I recognized a new need for individual books created from digital photos -the new age photo album so to speak - and started FamilyMemories.com.

In 2010 I helped create PhotoGalley.com with a twist on the FamilyMemoires theme offering a much more user friendly experience with many more printed photo product options including yearbooks, church directories, custom photo calendars, posters and all types of book printing.

In 2012 Fundcraftprint.com was release opening up our equipment to the commercial print market. Fundcraft Print specializes in helping other commercial printers with bindery and mailing needs. Fundcraft Print also has the capability of handling just about any type of print job in house.

Specialties: Self-publishing, recipe books, fundraiser recipe books, digital photo books, commercial print, yearbooks, directories, manuals, handouts, mailing, promotional marketing.
