Michael Cooper's Summary

Michael Cooper discovered his path to fulfillment and success, through over thirty-nine years of research and development. The knowledge he has organized and classified is being applied under the “label”, Human Performance Mentors™, which he officially named the team in March 2010, and expressed through the “label”, Missing Playbook™, which he officially named the Mentorship Philosophy in November 2008.
Born and raised in Miami, FL. Student-Athlete (Football, Baseball, Basketball, Soccer, and Wrestling). Certified LifeSuccess Consultant.

Michael is an athlete and entrepreneur, mentoring athletes and organizations, at every level, in the area of personal development and behavioral change, to make breakthroughs in every aspect of life. Personal and Professional.

Michael had the opportunity to play football in college but chose to serve in the United States Navy aboard the USS Nimitz aircraft carrier, where he was responsible for over 100 men. His coaching emphasizes knowledge transfer, skills building, and habit forming in the “laws of success” tied specifically to the purpose of each individual.

Michael’s coaching provides guidance and self-management, and has empowered thousands to navigate through the turbulent storms of life. His Missing Playbook™ Mentorship Philosophy brings forth a great deal of experience, and is guaranteed to transform your life.

Specialties

Human Performance Mentors, LLC (HPM) is a dynamic, coaching and consulting team, which leads individuals and organizations in the area of personal development and behavioral change. At HPM, we lead our clients to achieve ongoing improvement in all aspects of life (E.g, mental & physical strength and conditioning, family, relationships, team interaction, academics, athletic performance on and off the playing surface, professional direction and achievement, wealth, etc.).
