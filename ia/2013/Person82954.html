Steve Lubetkin, MBA, APR, Fellow, PRSA's Summary

Experienced, award-winning producer of business audio and video podcasts, photography, multimedia.

Named a "Social Media Star 2012" for podcasting by the Philadelphia Business Journal.

Frequent speaker at industry conferences, schedule at http://bit.ly/SteveSpeaking.

Clients: ACE Group, National Association of Realtors, IABC, NAPL, Greater Philadelphia Senior Executives Group (GPSEG); NJ Bankers Association, Peter Shankman; PRSA, Rutgers University.

Corporate photojournalist and videographer w/decades of national photo credits including cover photos of Philadelphia area CEOs for Smart Business Magazine. Public photo portfolio is at http://stevelubetkinphotography.zenfolio.com/.

Senior Fellow and Advisory Board member of the Society for New Communications Research. Experienced technologist, counselor to senior management regarding the business and commercial implications of leading edge technologies. MBA thesis (1994), “Bond Ratings in Cyberspace,” was a seminal study of the attitudes of financial industry professionals toward "groupware" computer-mediated meeting technologies -- the "social media" of the early 1990s.

Fluent in written and spoken Spanish.

Host of three personal blogs and podcasts:

Blogs/Podcasts
"Lubetkin on Communications" - Commentary and interviews on public relations and journalism issues, with people making news in the PR and journalism worlds. http://bit.ly/cqpNDG

"CompuSchmooze" - Enhances monthly newspaper column about technology and Judaism in the Jewish Community voice of Southern New Jersey (http://bit.ly/661hL7, http://bit.ly/7SEWeg, or via RSS feed at http://bit.ly/acXr70).

"Middle Chamber Books Podcast" - supports Middle Chamber Book Store (http://bit.ly/aX8a2a ) with podcast interviews of authors and other creative artists.

Specialties

Extensive experience in podcast production, creating compelling B2B audio and video programs distributed via podcast technologies. Fluent in applying broadcast quality production techniques to podcasting, ensuring high quality production values. Corporate photojournalism.

Keen understanding of corporate financial communications, organizational communications, executive (speechwriting) communications, and internal communications issues.

Senior Fellow and Advisory Board Member

Society for New Communications Research

January 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months)

The Society for New Communications Research is a global nonprofit 501(c)(3) think tank dedicated to the advanced study of new communications tools, technologies and emerging modes of communication, and their effect on traditional media, professional communications, business, culture and society.

The Society’s Fellows and Senior Fellows include more than 40 futurists, scholars, business leaders, professional communicators, members of the media and technologists from around the globe. In addition, the Society’s members include a Vendor Council with representatives from more than a dozen leading technology companies and Organizational Affiliates comprising membership associations, research organizations and other leading think tanks.

For more information, email us at info@sncr.org or call +1 (650) 331-0083.
