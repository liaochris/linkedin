Huyen Tue Dao's Publications

  • Getting to Know Machine Learning

    • UX Magazine
    • December 15, 2011
    Authors: Huyen Tue Dao

    Machine Learning, a branch of Artificial Intelligence, has gone from abstract idea in science fiction and academia to ubiquitous tool used both online and in consumer devices. This article presents an overview of machine learning and gives example of how it integrated into today's technology.

  • NASDAQ Velocity and Forces: An Interactive Visualization of Activity and Change

    • Journal of Universal Computer Science
    • January 5, 2008
    Authors: Huyen Tue Dao, Adam Bazinet, Robin Berthier, Ben Shneiderman

    NASDAQ Market Velocity and Market Forces are two relatively new data products
    that attempt to capture market sentiment, something that was previously only observable if one
    was on a trading floor. Given the transient and temporal properties of the data, we were
    challenged to create a visualization that would highlight the ever-changing qualities of Velocity
    and Forces. To that end, we developed FireStox, a web application that provides unified
    representation and filtering solutions to help market researchers observe the behavior of these
    metrics for one or many companies throughout the course of a trading day.
