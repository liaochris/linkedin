Michael McGonigal's Summary

* Exclusive trauma, critical care, and acute care surgery practice for 25 years

* Enhanced and grew the Level I Adult Trauma Center at Regions Hospital from 1993 to present

* Developed a new Level I Pediatric Trauma Center at Regions Hospital while partnering with Gillette Children's Hospital, verified by the American College of Surgeons in 2009

* Working with two other trauma surgeons, created the PennSTAR Trauma Program at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania in 1987

* Author of the Trauma Professionals Blog (www.thetraumapro.com)

* Expert at trauma program development and troubleshooting

* Nationally recognized trauma surgeon

* Skilled trauma speaker who has given more than 500 trauma presentations to a variety of audiences, including physicians, nurses and EMS

* Trauma Center Reviewer for the American College of Surgeons and the states of Washington, North Carolina, Colorado, Oregon

Specialties

Trauma Center development, Trauma Center verification, trauma-related presentations for all healthcare providers, computer and web-based applications in trauma care
