Joyanne Landers' Summary

Ear-Opening Insights. Powerful Results.
Successful organizations focus on developing their leaders and the people who surround them. Joyanne Landers helps you cultivate a healthy, high-performing team. She provides you with the skills you need to identify your impact on others, manage your key relationships and deal with the tough issues.

Joyanne uses a wildly effective combination of experiential education, brain science, and interactive learning to engage your employees, support your leaders, and generate lasting behavior change.

When you are looking for a different kind of training – one that touches the hearts and minds of participants in a deep and meaningful way, call Joyanne.

She stands on a bedrock of over twenty years experience as a team builder, leadership trainer, facilitator and speaker. She has a way of creating learning that is both simple and profound.
