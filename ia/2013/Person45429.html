Alexandre Fracazo's Summary

I've always been passionate for design, especially everything related to web. I love to create things for people, good interfaces that are easy to use.

I’ve got authentic passion for learning and getting better, everyday I try to learn more and more to improve myself and my skills. I love challenges and problem solving, think about those problems from different angles and choose what’s best for users, but also for my company.

I’m a born team player, I like to help people from empathy and respect. Learn every day from my partners and enrich my perspective thanks to them.

I’m also really adaptable. I’ve been in charge of lots of different things during my career. I see that as a great opportunity to gain expertise in certain areas and it also helps me to see things outside of the box.

Along with my expert level skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, and other Adobe CS6 tools, I maintain an advanced knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3, as well as other front end development tools. This blend of skills helps develop forward thinking, user-centered design, which responds to the ever-changing advancements of technologies and code.

How My Skills Benefit Larger Companies:
I create efficient, crisp design solutions to the complex business requirements of larger organizations, resulting in stronger branding and increased user base. My experience creating and presenting functional prototypes to key stakeholders and users has been a great benefit to larger clients.
