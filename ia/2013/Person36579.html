Don Alvarez's Summary

PhD in Physics, heavy focus on user experience as it impacts business processes and business strategy, a career in the film and game industries, and the good sense to lose a pie eating contest.

Almost 20 years living and working at the intersection of analytical technology and intuitive creativity for employers like Microsoft Game Studios, Walt Disney Feature Animation, WildTangent, General Motors and the MIT Media Lab. Passion and experience building systems that improve the lives and productivity of artists, engineers, designers, and other manufacturers of intellectual property.

Coached a Lego robotics team to the national championships, created revolutionary tools for the film industry, developed user experiences for Fortune 500 companies, helped define the future of the Halo franchise, nailed Jell-O(tm) to a wall, project managed the largest telescope at the South Pole, built adiabatic demagnetization cryogenic systems, and created environments where programmers and artists work together as peers and friends.
