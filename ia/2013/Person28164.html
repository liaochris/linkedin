Chris Stover @ChrisStover's Summary

I am an anchor and reporter for the Charlottesville Newsplex in Charlottesville, Va. I work for the CBS, ABC and FOX affiliates and their combined website, newsplex.com.

As the weekend anchor, I manage reporters, help to develop content, produce three newscasts each day, and make sure the website and social media are kept up to date.

In my role as a one-man-band reporter, I am often required to shoot, write and edit multiple stories in one day on my own. In addition to my reporting and anchoring at the Newsplex stations, I have served many roles including producer, Web producer and live truck operator/photographer. I am the recipient of the Meritorious Award in the 2011 Virginia Associated Press Broadcasters Awards for spot news photography.

At Temple, I was the editor-in-chief of The Temple News, the award-winning student newspaper. I helped to bring audio and video to a newly designed website, named by Editor & Publisher and Mediaweek magazines as the best college newspaper website in the country. My work has been rewarded with two Keystone Press Awards and recognition from UWIRE as one of the top 100 college journalists in the country.

During my college years, I’ve held two news internships with two local O&O TV stations — CBS3 and Fox 29 — and one marketing internship with SEPTA, Philadelphia’s public transportation system.

A native of the Philadelphia area, I have always been drawn to the city. I have been fortunate to live and report in one of the most interesting cities in the country. I look forward to where my career takes me next.

Specialties: On-air performance (television and radio), video editing, video photography, writing (for broadcast and print), proofing, staff management
