Allison Burgueno's Summary

I am interested in full-time, corp-to-corp or contract work. As a strategist I lead investigations into how users interact in multi-channel environments, develop concepts for making experiences users desire, and educate others on how to approach informed design.

Typically, I work as the User Experience Lead with clients as part of their team, to develop user experience concepts; conduct user research, task analysis concept testing, prototype testing, and development of Information Architecture deliverables and development of metrics and support day-to-day business.

I’ve had experience with a multitude of clients. In the end, I'm passionate about User Experience. I always look to ensure that the final interaction design is flexible enough to suit clients' needs, extensible to fit most budgets, and comprehensive enough to use as leverage in presenting our recommendations.

Specialties:
UX Strategy Deliverables: Comparative landscapes, real world metaphor exploration, persona development, user journeys, concept modeling and organizational models.
Information Architecture Deliverables: Process flows, navigation models, site map and wireframes
Community interactions: profile creation and targeting, content creation and collection, peer critique and status reward, video, photos and collection building, social networking, tags and faceted navigation
User-Centered Design (UCD) Methods: including research, analysis, testing and presentation.
Multi-platform: multi channel challenges and device specific recommendations and interface planning.
