Dorethia Conner's Summary

Dorethia Conner is a personal finance/business coach and blogger who is passionate about helping people achieve their financial goals. She is often called upon as a financial expert including a recurrent feature in Black Enterprise Magazine.

Many people are operating without a specific plan for their finances. Instead, resources that could be used toward a more secure future, are used for interest payments to acquire material possessions. Dorethia teaches practical ways to become financially stable, often bucking the trend of popular thought in our 'gimme now' society.


Conner Coaching, LLC offers workshops and coaching for individuals, families, organizations, companies and youth groups. Dorethia can help you learn how to save, get out of debt, deal with creditors, reign in your spending and live a financially free life!
