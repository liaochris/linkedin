Kevin Gilbert's Summary

Today's web is not about having a digital billboard, or even just being your own media channel. It's about fostering community. It's about building, and leading, a tribe. And leadership, now more than ever, is about trust and service. Tomorrow's leaders understand this today, and they're using the Internet, and all that it offers, to gain trust by serving their community with helpful, relevant, and informative content.
