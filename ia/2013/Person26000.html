Craig Jennings' Organizations

  • Caring For The Caregiver

    Founder
    • January 2007 to Present

    Caring For The Caregiver is a not-for-profit providing assistance, support and healing for those who look after others.

    It started 5 years ago when Annie, my wife, came down with Breast Cancer. i had great difficulty settting my business aside to be a caregiver and take care of her, shop, cook, clean house.. She had great difficulty being looked after. First we fought, finally worked it out, and I decided to create an organization to keep others from being blind-sided by caregiving as I had been.

    Caring for the Caregiver has created a program of Caregivers' Fairs and Caring Circles whichexpanded to 5 locations on Long Island, and looks to far-flung communities in Florida, New Mexico, and elsewhere. We've create a program comprised of a Caregivers' Fair and a Caring And Healing Circle which we have performed in 4 communities on Long Island. At this writing, there are 5 different places where a Caregiver can find ongoing support. That fulfills our target for 2011.
    In 2012, we want to create 5 more places where Caregivers can find on-going support. In addition we are instituting a program to bring a Caregiver support program to large businesses.

    Large businesses have a lot of caregivers as employees - one out of 8.. They try to stay under the radar - one described his role as "don't ask, don't tell." "My supervisor doesn't ask when I come in late, and I don't tell anyone either." Caregivers, who are among the most capable and valuable employees frequently become highly distracted - and their focus is on their caregiving first, job second - with obvious consequences for the employer.
    In addition, health costs are much higher, so the employer has a double incentive to find ways to mitigate the problem. A Met Life survey estimates that caregivers cost their employers $2400 per year in health costs and lost productivity.

    We'll be bringing our program to large businesses on Long Island and NY to offer a powerful solution to this problem..
