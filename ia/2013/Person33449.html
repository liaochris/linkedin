I've always felt my job was to make sure my boss had nothing to do. I work with little guidance & if I don't know how to get something done I'm not afraid to pick up the phone & find out. I have an extensive background that has given me a broad exposure to all fiscal operations and their inter-departmental relationships. I have written, designed, trained, & implemented new policies and procedures throughout the various disciplines including Accounting, Treasury, Human Resources, IT, Shipping, Receiving, Inventory Control, Purchasing, etc in my previous Controller / HR Manager positions.

I am a CPA with expertise in Internal Controls, Cost Mgr, government liaison, & non-profit work, training, mentoring, etc..
