Marijo Puleo, PhD's Summary

It is said that change is constant. Managing change can be effortless. My specialty is working with leaders to create positive, motivating change efforts. I especially look for leaders and employees who want to make a significant difference and change in his/her life and in the organization.

We work together in four domains: mental, emotional, physical ("somatic") and spiritual ("purpose"). Clients generally need fewer sessions and experience quicker results because they are balanced, calmer, have more mental clarity and are able to focus and prioritize.

Specialties: Coaching, leadership and personal transformation, somatic coaching

Marijo Puleo, PhD's Experience

Managing Partner

Make Change Positive

November 2005 – Present (7 years 11 months)

Organizations are looking to strengthen their operating models and market positioning while leveraging the talent and passion of all employees. It has been our experience that employees must be passionate in managing the customer experience in order for organizations to optimize technology and process investments. Employees want to be led by strong, inspiring leaders and work for organizations that they believe in.

We bring a variety of resources and consultants who collectively have over 100 years of experience working with a variety of Fortune 200 companies to create comprehensive customer strategies, sustaining change programs and leadership coaching that contribute to the bottom line.

Carlson Companies

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; Hospitality industry

January 2005 – November 2005 (11 months)

During her tenure, her team was responsible for creating the Carlson change methodology, and creating communities of practice around several key disciplines – change management, six sigma, consulting, project management, facilitation, communications and coaching.

Over 50 change practitioners are leveraging the change methodology, and regularly participate in activities to deepen their skills sets in these disciplines while impacting the effectiveness of over 200 change initiatives.

Director Organization Transformation

Peppers and Rogers Group

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Management Consulting industry

February 1999 – December 2004 (5 years 11 months)

Marijo created customer strategies and accelerated one-to-one efforts for clients with her global change management practice.

Her team worked on in-depth strategy, privacy, and enterprise change management projects with many clients, including Boise Cascade (now OfficeMax), Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield, E*TRADE, AstraZeneca, Amgen, Cabela’s, John Deere, Merial, BMW, and the United States Postal Service.

She personally conducted over 100 workshops on how to apply the Marketing 1to1/Pepppers and Rogers Group consulting methodology. She has been a keynote speaker for DMA, Siebel and other organizations, and a contributor for Don Peppers and Martha Roger’s books on customer strategy.

Manager Marketing, Sales Info and IT Trg

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; KFT; Food Production industry

1992 – 1999 (7 years) Northfield IL

Marijo was part of the change management efforts that merged the Kraft, General Foods and Oscar Mayer companies. She worked primarily with sales, marketing, and technology teams to create an information-based culture where customer insights and data analysis produced year over year increased market share.

She was also responsible for the sales force automation project, as well as implementation of change efforts across all of Kraft Foods, winning several awards for program quality and diversity.
