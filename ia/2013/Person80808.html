Salvatore J. Liga, Esq.'s Summary

● I offer all of my clients and partners a combination of experience, technical skill, an attention to detail and an action-oriented focus which drives deals forward to completion. I have the self-discipline, ability and the drive to methodically take an idea or concept and turn it into a self-sustaining, profitable business.

● I pride myself on taking a hands-on approach to market research, financial analysis, cost accounting, operations, distribution and sales. Whenever I engage a project, I seek to become an expert in the field to the fullest extent possible. I am passionate about numbers, technology, building organizations and making money. I bring a positive attitude, as well as, an honest, independent and realistic perspective to every project I get involved in.

● As an attorney, accountant and an investor, I understand risk and how to value and package business opportunities to successfully attract lenders, joint venture partners and capital investment, from public and private sources.

● I am always looking for opportunities:

a) To create value from underutilized assets;
b) To turnaround troubled companies or companies in crisis;
c) To turnaround distressed assets, including commercial real estate;
d) To expand successful companies into new markets;
e) To help companies improve profitability via outsourcing and joint venture;
f) To license products, trademarks and services;
g) To help companies raise capital, obtain financing and find strategic partners;
h) To help mature companies sell their business or devise a viable exit strategy.

● I am extremely flexible with my time and how I engage opportunities. In the past, I have engaged opportunities as an attorney, as a consultant, as a lender, as a broker, and as a partner. I treat every opportunity differently depending on my availability and level of interest.
