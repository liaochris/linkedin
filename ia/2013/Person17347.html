Brian E. Young's Summary

Reinvented magazine and newspapers editorial, advertising, collateral and marketing materials utilizing a strong understanding of typographic and design principles.

Awarded as first place award winner for editorial and advertising design from the Maryland, Delaware and DC (MDDC) Press Association, Parenting Publications of America (PPA) and Suburban Newspapers of America.

Managed multiple simultaneous products from concept to completion including design, illustration, assigning photography and art direction, operating large format printers, evaluating vendor quotes and finalizing output to press.

Executed tasks efficiently within tight deadlines. Applied independent creativity as well as assisted under art direction.

Solved creative print design projects with advanced proficiency in Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop and other Creative Suite applications on Windows PC and Mac OSX work stations
Achieved Bachelor of Arts degree in Fine Art with specific education in computer graphics and 2-D design.

Created Infographics appearing on infographicworld.com, searchenginejournal.com, mirnabard.com, fatwallet.com, bimehq.com and more.

Broadcasted blog and podcast about design and creativity on sketchee.com
