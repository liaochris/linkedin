Jordan Hatcher's Summary

Lawyer and consultant with multi-jurisdictional training and experience in the United States and Europe on intellectual property and internet law issues, particularly on open data, open content licensing in copyright; internet regulation; and international aspects of IP and IT.

Key words: Law and technology, Intellectual Property (IP) strategies, copyright, patent, trade mark, international IP, internet regulation, internet law, open content law, and plain language drafting (no legalese please).
