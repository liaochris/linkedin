Roger C Barrantes' Summary

My career spans over fifteen years of sales experience in the hospitality and travel industry. After enrolling at the International University of the Americas (UIA) in San Jose, Costa Rica, I joined United Airlines/Lacsa (TACA) where I held Sales Representative and Flight Inventory Control Supervisor positions from 92-95.

I moved to San Diego, CA to work for The Travel Company as a Sales Coach from 1996-1998 where I received several monthly, quarterly and annually sales awards including 1994 Top Sales of the Year . Later, I joined American Express Business Travel at Intuit, Inc. from 1998-2003 and received the Annual Star Performer Award and National Pacesetter Award.

In 2004 I joined the Hilton team at the Doubletree San Diego/Del Mar as a Group Sales Manager and Business Travel Sales Manager. After the hotel was sold, I accepted a position with the Hilton San Diego/Del Mar as a Corporate Group Sales Manager (Pharma) from 2005-2007. I joined the San Diego Sales Complex in March 2007 as Business Travel Sales Manager and was recognized as the 2007 Sales Manager of the Year.

In 2009, I was promoted to Area Manager - Business Travel Sales Western Region for Hilton Worldwide representing Doubletree by Hilton Mission Valley, Hilton La Jolla Torrey Pines, Doubletree by Hilton Golf Resort and Hilton San Diego Bayfront.

In 2011, I was promoted to Director of Sales and Marketing at DoubleTree Suites by Hilton Doheny Beach and recognized with the 2012 Manager of the Year Award.

In 2013, DoubleTree by Hilton Doheny Beach-Dana Point achived the most improved 2012 RevPAR Index in the Americas and I was recognized with the 2012 Circle of Excellence Director of the Year Award for Hilton Americas - United States of America, Canada, Mexico, Latin America and the Caribbean.

Most recently, I was promoted to Director of Sales and Marketing at DoubleTree Suites by Hilton Anaheim-Orange.
