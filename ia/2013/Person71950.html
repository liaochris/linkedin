Kara Clark's Summary

Kara has ten years of experience working with lifestyle brands on the Central Coast and superior knowledge of social media, print and web design production—Social By Design.

Originally from North Carolina, she moved to San Luis Obispo for an advertising/graphic design career. Combining roles in a small business as the Producer, Photo Stylist and Editorial Designer for over three years at Central Coast Magazine enabled Kara to network with numerous local personalities and further confirmed her interest in Central Coast lifestyle, specifically wine, retail, and hospitality. For the last three years Kara has freelanced as an Art Director for Mental Marketing and Juice Media, informing social, content, and creative strategies. Kara is currently employed at Rosetta Marketing, focusing on Social CRM for global brands, and gearing up for the next wave of social content marketing—brand publishing.

While not ideating, strategizing, and curating content, Kara practices her calligraphy skills, teaches Les Mills fitness classes at local gym eqclubs.com, and can be found soaking up the luxuries of the Central Coast, which always includes a glass of local wine. Cheers to #WW!
