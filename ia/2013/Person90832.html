Megan Burkham's Summary

“Give a girl a great pair of shoes and she can conquer the world” - Marilyn Monroe

A graduate of the University Of Missouri-Columbia, I earned my degree in Textile and Apparel Management, with an emphasis in Fashion Marketing and Merchandising. College is where I realized my talent and ambition for a career in the fashion industry.

Studying abroad at American InterContinental University in London provided a landscape of new opportunities. While studying Fashion Styling and Fashion Show Production, my talent landed me on the Production Board for AIU London’s Fall 2006 Fashion Show, as the Production Stylist. During my time in London, I partook in an internship with Linda Farrow Vintage, a couture sunglass design house, as the Press/Public Relations Assistant.

After graduation I worked for Dillard’s - St. Louis as an Assistant Buyer and Assistant Area Sales Manager, before moving on to become the Merchandise Manager and Buyer for Anatomy of Style, a specialized boutique in St. Louis. I was fortunate to help grow AOS from an image consulting website, to an eCommerce business, and ultimately launched a storefront in 2008.

In my move back to Texas, I continued to build my portfolio. While living in Austin, I worked as a Freelance Merchandise Coordinator with Winston Retail Solutions, acting as a liaison between branded accounts and retailers. My next venture as a Visual Merchandiser and Stylist for Forever 21 Inc brought me to where I currently reside in Dallas, Texas.

My blog My Shoes Speak Italian, was developed after my recent position as Retail Merchandiser for the US with Silver Jeans Co., came to a close. This position allowed me to travel all over the country providing product knowledge and merchandising training to my department store accounts.The excitement and recognition of my passion to inspire those who will listen, is exactly the reason why I have chosen MyShoesSpeakItalian.com to be an outlet for innovation.
