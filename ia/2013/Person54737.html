Israel Y. Ganot's Summary

Israel Ganot was appointed to the position of President and CEO of Gazelle in November 2008. Under Israel’s leadership, Gazelle has emerged as the nation’s largest and fastest growing place for consumers to responsibly trade-in gadgets for cash, with more than 100% growth during the past 6 months. Israel has established Gazelle’s leadership position by focusing on building a world-class brand, technology, and customer experience that so far has attracted more than 50,000 consumers. For his accomplishments at Gazelle, Israel has been featured by top national media and Ernst & Young named him as a 2009 finalist for their Entrepreneur of the Year award. Prior to Gazelle, Israel held senior positions at eBay, PayPal, and Amazon. He earned his BS at NYU’s Stern School and his MBA from Harvard.

Specialties

- Starting and growing high potential consumer Internet companies
- Mergers & acquisitions (especially international deal making)

Israel Y. Ganot's Experience

Gazelle

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

September 2006 – Present (7 years 1 month) Boston, MA

Gazelle (www.gazelle.com) is the nation's leading high-end consumer electronics trade-in site, providing an easy, fast and safe way for consumers to get cash for their unwanted devices. Gazelle takes the risk and effort out of selling electronic gadgets online with benefits such as free shipping and packaging and comprehensive identity and data removal. To date, Gazelle has accepted more than one million trade-ins, helping consumers upgrade to the latest technology and earn quick cash. Based in Boston, Gazelle is privately owned and is backed by premier venture capital firms Venrock Associates, RockPort Capital Partners, Physic Ventures and Craton Equity Partners.
