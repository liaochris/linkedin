Michael Fortune's Summary

I communicate how Planet Earth functions and how it is changing: the land, sea, air, ice, and all life that thrives in these realms. I am an atmospheric scientist, writer, and editor.

Climate is changing rapidly in sometimes unexpected ways. I communicate these changes in meaningful terms to people. I inform people what the consequences are for them, and what communities might do about it.

At Oregon's Climate Change Institute I have spoken and written to foresters, teachers, students, and state and federal decision-makers.

I write three newsletters, plus book reviews, articles, and blogs. I also teach science and environmental stewardship.

If you intend to communicate a message about the changing climate or environment, and you want to base it on good science, let us work together. I have expertise in the science of weather, climate, and Earth systems.
