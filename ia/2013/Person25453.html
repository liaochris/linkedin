Claudia Robiou's Summary

I am a communication designer with 15+ years of experience.
Projects I have worked with range from web, mobile and application design, brand development, marketing and publication design.

I like to work on a clear design basis across projects and value enabling both process and creativity to solve problems. Through the years I have held a variety of roles in my work, from creative director, design manager, liason to content, technology and sales areas, as well as art direction and hands on design execution, and have been responsible for personnel and budgets.

Currently enjoying: Designing for tablet size in iOs and Android's many gadgets in that range; Reading about mental models and Responsive Design reads. I have a fascination with haikus, e.e. cummings and the sea. I love running, yoga, biking, cooking a fresh meal and time with my family.
