Kit Hughes' Summary

Our professional lives shouldn't be defined only by the companies we work for and the titles we hold.

I've decided to give you a view of my professional life, year-over-year. Whether you met me at a conference or have known me for years, the year we met is much more revealing about our relationship than any company association or title. For instance, 2007 was a pretty horrible year for me. I'm sorry if you met me then. On the other hand 2003 was a great year. I hope you met me then.

Here's everything rolled up into one package:

"For almost 15 years, playing in rock bands, creating technology-inspired drawings and sculptures, designing packaging for Anheuser-Busch and Coca-Cola, shaping retail experiences for BP and Valvoline, studying strategy and innovation at MIT Sloan, speaking about brand strategy at numerous conferences, researching users on multiple continents, crafting digital experiences for Philips Healthcare and Philips Consumer Lifestyle, designing brand-driven e-commerce experiences, running the mobile-focused strategic brand studio Look-Listen, and co-founding FindPost, has kept Kit Hughes busy. He doesn't sleep as much as he should."

Specialties: Making things look pretty, Geo-political corporate negotiation, Organizational therapy through design, Hugs, Talking to crowds of people about design, Talking shop, Helping businesses make money because of their brands
