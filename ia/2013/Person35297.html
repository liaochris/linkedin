Diane Rogers' Summary

Diane M. Rogers is the founder and principal consultant for Contagious Change LLC, and assists organizations in achieving organizational excellence through repeatable analysis, design, and execution methodologies toward the achievement of performance improvement and risk reduction goals. Diane has a diverse background, with a history of demonstrated leadership positions across multiple industries, including software information technology, semiconductors, aerospace, and nuclear power. Diane's areas of expertise include process/performance improvement, organizational change, failure analysis, human factors, and project/program management.

Contagious Change is a organizational excellence consultancy that helps individuals and organizations experience the energy of teams, engage in changing and creating positive energy toward better outcomes and results, and achieve new potentials - TOGETHER.

Specialties: Organizational Excellence, Organizational Change and Development, Healthcare Performance Improvement, Risk Reduction Strategies and Execution, Collaborative Strategies, Quality Assurance and Training, National Speaker

Diane Rogers' Projects

  • The hArt of Medicine

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Diane Rogers

    The hArt of Medicine is a program designed to create positive healthcare experiences. Committed to improving the healthcare experience, the program gets to the hArt of the experience; The interaction and relationship patients, family and clinicians have with each other.

    The program is designed to engage the clinician into an experience where they make a personal choice to incorporate new behaviors and practices into their encounters with both patients and colleagues.

    The program framework begins with a discovery phase to assess, measure and discusss the clinicians' current practice in the patient interaction. This information provides the foundation for the experiential learning modules focused on creating the therapeutic relationship.

    To ensure practice changes are effectively transferred and sustained, coaching techniques, leadership development and organizational change practices are introduced and integrated into the overall healthcare experience improvement strategy, engaging the clinician in a personal, insightful and empowering experience.

    The program consists of 5 experiential learning modules focused on creating the therapeutic relationship.

    - Engaging the Clinician
    - Beyond Courtesy and Respect
    - Listening & Attunement
    - Empathy & Compassion
    - Maintaining Empathy in Difficult Encounters

    Through experiential exercises, the clinician connects to the heart of the patient and derives an understanding of how to enhance their own empathy and relational skills.
