Michael Arlen's Summary

Core Competencies:
Advertising, Marketing, Sales, Strategic Planning, International Business, Human Resources and Law
Multi-cultural and Trilingual: English, Spanish, and Portuguese

Functional Roles:
Visionary, Innovator, Rainmaker, Creative Catalyst, Out-of-the Box Thinker, Resourceful Problem Solver, Contracts Negotiator, Consensus Builder, Team Builder and Charismatic Leader

Michael Arlen was a pioneer in the sales automation, desktop publishing and e-commerce revolutions. Early adoption of those technologies empowered his companies to do much more with far less; and generously rewarded him. He has 20+ years of Advertising, Marketing and Sales experience; and has enjoyed an enviable track record for hiring, developing, motivating and retaining high performance, cross-functional teams in fast-paced environments.

He graduated Loyola Law School with a Juris Doctorate and UCLA with a BA in Anthropology; and has extensive Human Resources and Operations experience. He sold the International Ad Agency that he founded and ran for 18 years in October of 1999, but retained control of Arlen Medical Education Products; a company which designed and sold materials that health care professionals used to learn how to treat heart attacks and other medical emergencies.

After suffering a freak heart attack of his own in 2005, he decided to change his lifestyle and the focus of his work to that of helping people become more healthy and avoid heart attacks (cancer and degenerative diseases) altogether. Today he runs NEWtritious™, a forward-thinking designer / manufacturer / marketer of highly fortified, nutrient-dense foods that are “Better Tasting & Better For You”™
