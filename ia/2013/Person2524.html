Ahmed Hazem Hosny's Summary

During 14 years of progressively responsible experience in Training and Capacity-Building, I managed or delivered training for a wide range of clients and participants in
Egypt and the Middle East.

As a TRAINER, I have personally trained, coached, facilitated knowledge transfer sessions, and gave motivational speeches to literally thousands of participants covering a wide range of demographics:
age, gender, professional and educational levels, backgrounds, faiths, ethnicities, and national origin.
Ranging from 10-day certificates down to intense 10-minute eye-opener motivational interventions, I delivered training or trained trainers “TOT” on a wide range of management, strategy, soft skills, and life skills. Organizationally and geographically, my experience covers a wide range of organizations including local and international companies, as well as international organizations and development
agencies around Egypt (Cairo, Luxor, Port Said, Siwa, Alexandria, Suez, ...), as well as in the Levant (Lebanon and Syria). I have experience delivering conventional classroom training, outdoor experiential training, on the job “OTJ” training, and simulations purely in English, purely in Arabic, as well as in the “mixed language” setup usually used in Egypt. In more multi-cultural settings, I also use French as needed.

My INTERNATIONAL EXPOSURE is equally rich: a British and French educated professional who has traveled, learned, and worked in several countries and with people from several nationalities and backgrounds.

Following the life of continuous self-education that I preach, I have also been an avid reader of the greatest management, strategy, personal development, and soft skill books.

My GOAL remains to work on projects enhancing human productivity and welfare.

Specialties: Training, Strategy, Personal Development, Soft Skills, TOT, Strategy for SMEs, Entrepreneurship, Leadership
