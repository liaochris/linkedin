Brad Lazarus' Summary

I'm a seasoned manager of professional live bands with a background in the recording industry as a radio and TV plugger for artists including Tom Jones, The Charlatans, Muse and Donny Osmond.

I manage our bands and invest in their development to help us better serve our entertainment agent and event planner clients.

Building relationships with great new bands one at a time can be painfully slow and carries risk.

We offer a collection of inspiring bands all in one place that you know are going to represent your company and enhance your reputation in the eyes of your client.

How We Can Yelp You:

- Specialists in bands with a signature musical style and charismatic personality helping you inspire your client with more exciting entertainment options.

- All bands are fully pro with thousands of hours of performance time so you can be sure of consistently high levels of performance event after event.

- Bands are coached and mentored as to client facing etiquette so you can be 100% certain they can be trusted to represent you and your business.

- All on the day event logisitics are dealt with through our office giving you total peace of mind that everything has been dealt with and the band fully briefed.

- 'refer back to you' policy - We developed an internal procedure to efficiently deal with prospects that contact us direct ensuring you don't lose the prospective business you generated.

- We developed a suite of 'agent friendly' tools including websites and anonymous promo videos. Contact me for more info.
