Fidelis Resourcing is an industry specific recruitment company working within the European packaging and processing industry focused on 4 key market sectors – Packaging Machinery, Processing Machinery, Product ID and Materials & Containers.

Our areas of expertise include sales, service, engineering and supply chain positions from management through to board level.

The company is driven with a purpose of using our knowledge, expertise and savvy to get the right resource for your organisation and build long term client relationships.

We pride ourselves on the specialist retained relationships we have built with our client base. We purposely work with a small number of non-competitive client businesses to ensure we always deliver focus, creativity and accuracy.

We know that we are only ever as good as our last recruit and we are confident of being judged on the results we deliver. We won’t make a promise to a client that we can’t deliver and we measure our success on the long-term retention and career progress of candidates, not simply at the signing of a contract.
