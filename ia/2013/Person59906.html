J Michael Long's Summary

Jay Michael is an award winning veteran broadcaster with 19 years experience. He has produced event and wedding videography since the late 1980's. In addition to this he has also produced several special interest video titles and documentaries. Jay is also a Film Festival Award Winner. Jay Michael maintains a blog for video producers and enthusiasts as well as administrates a professional network, Digital Video Professionals. With his DVD series "Mastering Movie Edit Pro" Jay educates new video producers in the art of video editing via his online community. His talent and creativity gained him the prestigious "MAGIXIAN of the Month" award from MAGIX™, the company that manufactures his video editing software. Jay has won the award twice. He is also the host of "Conundrums", an online TV show.

Specialties

Video editing, voice overs, video production, event videography, documentaries.
Magix Video Pro X, Bluff Titler, Photoshop, etc.
