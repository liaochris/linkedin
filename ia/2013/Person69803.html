JR. Forasteros' Summary

I've served as a minister in youth and collegiate ministries for seven years in both church and para-church organizations. I've also planned and implemented large events (300+ person) as a part of the ministries.

I invest much time in developing as a leader through reading (both religious and business books, blogs, magazines, etc.) and writing, as well as implementing what I'm learning into my teams.

Specialties

I am skilled in creating innovative, engaging worship and learning experiences that interests/engages adults of all ages. I focus on biblical theology, biblical & cultural exegesis, Christian leadership, and contemporary Christian issues and ethics.

I lead biblically-based, high-impact, multi-media ministry teams by training/developing lay leadership, vision casting and providing direction/teaching congruent with our vision/mission as they relate to the community’s unique cultural context.
