Lisa Hainline's Summary

Graphic Designer / Christian book cover designer and Owner of Lionsgate Book Design & Marketing, where we help Christian Authors with all elements of production, from book cover design, through editing, formatting, publishing and marketing.

Graphic Designer / Owner of Hainline Design with 35 years experience in all aspects of Graphic design and illustration, magazine design, poster and promotion, logo and corporate ID and many other aspects advertising and marketing.

Editor/Designer/Owner of The Healing Source, a website to bring people to the lord to receive healing directly from Him: (http://www.thehealingsource.org ). There is only one source of power and that is at the throne of the Lord Jesus who promises blessings of peace to all who will come...and ask. We also lead people to the throne to learn how to go deeper and abide in His manifest presence, "abiding" in Him and finding all sustainance in and through Him and the Power of His Holy Spirit.

Business: Too many companies to put here and i can't remember most of them as it was so long ago but basically advertising studio and agency work.
i've been in advertising and graphic design since 1978, working for various companies and agencies in the Milwaukee area and in recent years; out of my home. I've had my own studio and business before freelancing here in Michigan.
I've worked on various accounts and won a few design awards along the way, doing work for such companies as Coors, Miller Brewing Company, Summerfest (the world's largest Music Festival) along with radio stations, hospitals, manufacturers, game designing, display (toy to outdoor awning), and clothing retailers.

Specialties

i specialize in corporate ID, logo design, graphic design and ad layout. Book Cover design is a new specialty but catalogs and posters are also a snap for me, laying them out through directing photographers, hiring models and printers and seeing the job through completion.

I also do fashion illustration and have illustrated many ads for retailers to manufacturing and designers.

A sideline is Portraiture where i do colored pencil portraits and graphite in lifelike realism.
