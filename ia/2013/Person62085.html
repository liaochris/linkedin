Jenny Frazier's Summary

I am a Digital & Social Media Producer living and working in Boston, Massachusetts. I have over 10 years experience using social media and the web to build digital relationships both personally and professionally.

Currently, I work at Harvard Business School managing a number of digital initiatives & running the social media program.

In September of 2013, Forbes & MBA50 ranked my social media program #1 in the 2013 Business School Social Media Ranking (article: http://onforb.es/172Sw0g). In January of 2013, OnlineMBApage.com ranked my program #2 in the Top 100 Most Social Media Friendly MBA Schools for 2013 (article: http://bit.ly/GzD8SJ and within 8 months our numbers nearly doubled, surpassing Stanford the #1 on this list).

I oversee 40+ social media mangers on campus, chair the Social Media Managers Group at the School, and I’m also part of several committees and groups on main Harvard campus including the committee to create Harvard’s Social Media Policy.

In addition, I work as a consultant to several local and national companies across a variety of industries. I provides support in a number of areas from brainstorming, brand/identity development and design, print and web design, photography, as well as digital media strategies and implementation.
