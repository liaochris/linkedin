Dejha Carrington's Summary

Landmark projects, distinguished brands, leading festivals, influential exhibitions.

With more than 10 years in the industry, Dejha Carrington engineers strategic publicity campaigns for her clients. Specializing in art and culture, the strength of her experience and relationships uniquely position her to deliver value-added, high-impact services.

Dejha has launched such projects as the prestigious William John Kennedy Collection with The Andy Warhol Museum and Skywalkers by Friends With You. She has collaborated with leading exhibitions, fairs and brands during Art Basel Miami Beach week, partnered with celebrated corporations including Moët & Chandon and Société Perrier, and supported the National Film Board of Canada and City of Hollywood's Community Redevelopment Agency. This experience underscores her interest in socially and culturally relevant initiatives.

A critical thinker, Dejha plays an integral role in the creative, logistics and execution phases of each project. She is driven by the power of ideas and takes an insightful approach toward actualizing the vision and goals of her clients.

Born and raised in Montreal, Dejha is a world-traveler based in Miami.

Dejha Carrington's Projects

  • KIWI Arts Group

    • December 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Dejha Carrington, Ilana Vardy, Michael Huter, Jeff Wilkinson, Louis Canales

    After laying in storage for nearly 50 years, the archive photography of William John Kennedy reveals images of Andy Warhol and Robert Indiana with their most iconic work. Now published as a collection of silver gelatin and chromogenic prints by KIWI Arts Group, exhibitions have been held at SCOPE Art Show Miami, site/109 during Frieze New York, Art Hamptons, and at Conrad Indianapolis’ “A Fine Arts Experience at The Conrad” produced by ModernMasters Fine Art.

  • Primary Projects

    • November 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Dejha Carrington, Books Bischof, Chris Oh, Typoe, Cristina Gonzalez, Aimee Boulos

    he enterprising brainchild of Primary Flight street art collective, Primary Projects gallery was conceived as a multidisciplinary space that explores various forms of expression from established and emerging artists. Utilizing an extensive and wholly specific experience in producing exhibitions, the result is a vital presence of an intelligent, alternative arts environment with a bold sensibility.

  • Long-Sharp Gallery

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Dejha Carrington, Rhonda Long-Sharp, Jessica Warren

    Under the helm of Rhonda Long-Sharp, ModernMasters Fine Art has established a strong reputation in the print market. Working with the foundations and archives of artists, such as Haring and Dali, to ensure that all works are authentic, she has created a network of more than 25 art dealers and 2000 private collectors from 50 countries. Long-Sharp Gallery is an extension of ModernMasters Fine Art, making its permanent home in the exquisite Conrad Indianapolis, where she also directs the hotel's award-winning art program.
