Adam Eisen's Summary

Strategy and Leadership Consultant and Corporate Events Moderator.

I work closely with company and division management teams to develop and implement new strategies. My focus is on helping organizations establish a process of Strategic Learning that increases their ability to continually translate insights into action. To support successful strategies, I also help organizations, teams and individuals develop the necessary leadership skills and culture to drive their transformation.

As a Moderator, I enable clients to deliver successful external and internal events. In this role, it is a definite advantage to be able to draw upon my experience and knowledge of global business, including management and marketing roles in the telecom, IT, services and consulting industries. My focus is on both communication and content, a combination that is ideally suited for international events and internal management gatherings.

Specialties

Management Consultant in Strategy & Leadership, Conference Moderator and Facilitator.
