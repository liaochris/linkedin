Michael Powell's Summary

Logos, identity systems, and other branding essentials. Traditional communications born out of nontraditional ideas. And, over 25 years in the design trenches crafting thoughtful, focused work for a variety of clients. Michael Powell Design is all of that, and more. But mostly, Michael Powell Design is Michael Powell, Designer.

Specialties: Logos, identity systems, posters, color, typography. Strengths: Creative problem solving, attention to detail, organization and efficiency.
