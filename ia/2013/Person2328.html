Sue Sherry's Summary

Just a little about me and how I do business... I’ve been providing real estate services to Coastal San Diego for over 14 years. My continued success is due in large part to my conscientious, professional, and ethical approach to real estate. I take your wishes to heart as I would my own, working tirelessly, aggressively and persistently to ensure you get what you desire — whether it’s your first home, home of your dreams, or getting top dollar from the home you are leaving behind. I strive to educate, and listen...calling upon my intimate knowledge of San Diego’s neighborhoods, current market inventory, and the fine art of negotiation. I apply no pressure, instead I ensures my clients have all they need to make the most informed decisions.

Serving the real estate markets Coastal San Diego including Solana Beach, Del Mar, Carmel Valley, Encinitas, La Costa, Carlsbad, Chula Vista, San Marcos and La Mesa.
