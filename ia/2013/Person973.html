Andrew Bream's Summary

Andrew is a graduate of Boston University and a young marketing professional. Most recently as Marketing Director Andrew has helped create the marketing vision and voice for the Minneapolis-based start-up Bite Squad.

Bream has also had several internships in marketing, including at an interior design firm in Paris, France and an artist-centric PR firm in Boston, MA.

While at BU, Andrew was consistently on the Dean's List and graduated with Latin honors Magna Cum Laude as well as a BU Humanities Foundation Student Award.

Andrew is currently seeking a new adventure in the Minneapolis marketing world! Please feel free to reach out with any opportunities you may have.

Andrew's skills and competencies include: Social media marketing, online marketing, branding, programming, blogging, graphic design, MS Office, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, In Design, After Effects, Dreamweaver, HTML, CSS, iPhoto, iMovie, Mac OSX, and more.
