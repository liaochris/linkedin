Chris LoDuca's Summary

I have worked as a Video Director/Editor, Art Director, Studio Manager, and designer in my many years of agency experience. Moreover, by combining all of his skills, I also have been working as a Senior Digital Producer in New York City – taking projects from the scoping and discovery phase to launch and beyond. Currently, I am freelancing/consulting as a Digital Producer, Video Director/Editor, and Art Director. Specialties: Advertising, Branding, Managing, Art Directing, Multimedia, Video, Pharmaceutical, Medical, Entertainment, Promotional

In addition to my ad agency work, I have also worked in both film and theatre. My short film, Stealing Kisses aired on Chicago's WTTW as well as other PBS stations, and was featured on Crackle.com — a film website owned by Columbia Pictures. For theatre, I directed a large-scale professional production of Damn Yankees at Metropolis Theatre in Arlington Heights, IL, which was well received by critics and patrons. I also have many credits as an actor in both film and television and I below to SAG and AEA.
