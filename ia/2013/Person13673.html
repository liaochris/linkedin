Beth Brainard's Summary

Writer, instructor, and speaker. Author of Soup Should Be Seen, Not Heard! Formerly Director of Communications for divisions of Disney and Harvard and head of Beth Brainard Associates a corporate consulting firm specializing in teaching business etiquette and social skills. Created and taught a K-5 etiquette curriculum, hosted a radio talk show for parents, and contributed to the formation of Josephson Institute of Ethics' K-12 Character Counts! program. Three decades of hands-on experience as mom, step-mom, and grammy.

Beth Brainard's Projects

  • Soup Should Be Seen, Not Heard!

    • July 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Beth Brainard

    Mom's Choice Award winner Soup Should Be Seen, Not Heard! is a complete etiquette book for children 4 and up that covers everything from table manners to good sportsmanship, dressing to e-messaging in a clever, lighthearted way that appeals to both boys and girls.
    Presenting the tenets of polite behavior through the antics of the kooky Good Idea Kids, Soup! engages children and offers parents and teachers a way to begin conversations about the basics of good manners.
