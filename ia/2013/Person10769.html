Scott C. Silva's Summary

Scott C. Silva is a top-rated trial attorney in the State of Arizona:

- AV "Preeminent" Peer Rated Attorney (highest possible rating in both legal ability and ethical standards) by Martindale-Hubbell®.

- AVVO Rated "Superb" Attorney (highest rating) and Expert Legal Contributor on Avvo.com.

- practicing law for over 16 years.

- practices primarily in the fields of criminal defense, DUI defense, and personal injury law.

- has personally represented clients in over 3500 cases, including over 1200 DUI cases.

- has substantial experience in handling serious felony cases, including capital murder.

- has received Not Guilty verdicts in every type of DUI case, including Super-Extreme DUI and Drug DUI cases.

- has experience in handling high-profile cases with national media exposure.

- has been counsel of record in over 400 bench and jury trials (this number includes cases that went to verdict or were completely dismissed by the judge during the trial or immediately before the start of the trial).

- was a featured trial attorney on the ABC television show "State V.", where he was defense counsel in a first-degree murder trial.

Specialties: Criminal Law, Criminal Defense, DUI Defense, Personal Injury Law
