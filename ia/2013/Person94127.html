Mike Cavaliere's Summary

Associate editor of the Ormond Beach Observer. Award-winning book author and humor columnist. Journalist. Film geek.

Four years of experience writing and editing published work. One year of experience managing a newsroom and its weekly production schedule, from story budgeting to print. Skilled at AP style, managing freelancers/contributors and fostering relationships within the community.

CREDITS:
- Author, book, "Tracks in the Sand: Sea Turtles and Their Protectors" (published April 2010)
- Author, weekly humor column, in the Palm Coast and Ormond Beach Observer newspapers (combined circulation: 40,000/week)
- Author, movie reviews, Ormond Beach Observer; music reviews, Saint Augustine Underground newspaper
- Editor/Copyeditor, book, "Explore the Southeast Natl. Marine Sanctuaries with Jean-Michel Cousteau" (April 2011)
- Editor, book, "Climate of Uncertainty: A Balanced Look at Global Warming and Renewable Energy" (May 2010)
