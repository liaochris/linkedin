David J. Jacobson's Summary

In 1986 I began my professional media career with the production of a weekly Radio & Television program in northern CA called RealTView. The programs focused on Real Estate related issues and enjoyed a little over two years on the air. In 1986 I Founded Never Give Up media group, a full service video production company specializing in the production of corporate video, TV commercials, infomercials, documentaries for social causes, nonprofit & community based organizations.

I have also collaborated with & consulted for other production companies. In 1999 in collaboration with HeartLand Media I co-produced the multi-award winning two part series; “S.O.S.-Saving Our Schools from hate & violence.” Fully sponsored by “SEE’s Candies and hosted by humorist youth activist Michael Pritchard. This series has been shown nationally on PBS & has won numerous National awards.

In 2004 AJ Vancreveld, Dennis Keefe and I founded "Real Tour" a full service production company specializing in the production and distribution of Internet video and web site development. Real Tour produces everything required to distribute video and other media to the Internet. One of our specialties is the production of Real Tours for the real estate community, in fact this particular portion of our business has become so successful that in the first 3 months of 2008 our clients received 45000+ visitors!

In August of 2009 I moved to Colorado due to family issues. I have established a new company Words and Pictures. I continue to produce what I had at Real Tour, but am now also focusing on: Music videos, reality TV, promotional videos for colleges & small businesses, documentaries for community organizations and more. My website (which I created) www.wordsandpicturestellastory.com explains what we do & contains samples.

Specialties: Production of: Video, Web sites, Internet media, Reality TV, Promotional videos for Universities, Small Business, Nonprofits, Musicians etc.
