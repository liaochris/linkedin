gerry blakney's Projects

  • Sharp SunSnap Beats the Clock

    • August 2012 to September 2012
    Team Members: gerry blakney

    I was the lead for strategy, concepting, storyboarding, copywriting management, visual design, and art direction for the trade show video eROI produced for the SunSnap™ product release.

    As the first phase of their official SunSnap™ solar appliance product launch, Sharp Solar needed to build awareness and interest among their industry audience. To do so, they partnered with eROI to create a promotional video that premiered at one of the year’s largest solar industry tradeshows.

    For the video we didn’t just want to claim the ease and speed of the SunSnap™ solution, we wanted to prove it. So, working with RecordSetter, we filmed and timed an installation of the system and set the world record for Fastest Time To Install A Residential Solar Photovoltaic System.

  • Our Portland Story

    • June 2010 to July 2010
    Team Members: gerry blakney, Melissa Delzio

    Our Portland Story Volume 1 is the work of 77 authors, 68 designers, one editor and one founder (And creative mastermind, Melissa Delzio). I designed two layouts for Volume 1.

    The stories were collected from local Portlanders following an open call for submissions in September of 2008. It took over a year to edit those stories, pair each story with a designer, and work with the designer and author to create the resulting page. Each page in the book represents a different story told by a different author and designed by a different designer.

    This book is by no means a complete look at the City of Portland. Rather, it represents insights by 77 different people.
