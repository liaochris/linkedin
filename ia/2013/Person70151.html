Jamie Troia's Summary

There is no better time to be a technologist and marketer with a head for finance than today! The web has changed the definition of marketing, and helped turn it into equal parts science and art.

Yes, I help my clients develop innovative online campaigns to help capture more prospects and turn them into customers.

Yes, we use the latest ideas in social media, email, video, search engine optimization (SEO) and pay-per-click marketing (PPC) to attract new customers.

But... This is all done with a focus on ROI and continuous improvement that bring me back to my days at General Electric when those were the things that made Jack Welch's engine run.

It's no longer a "Mad Men" world - Mass media has given way to niche segments and targeted reach. I can help your business take advantage of inbound marketing to reduce your customer acquisition costs and increase revenue.
