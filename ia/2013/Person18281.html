Bruce King's Summary

Thanks for dropping by. Sales, marketing and entrepreneurship are my passions and if there is anything I might help you with, contact me for a time to talk. I love to speak with passionate people and, for avoidance of doubt, there is no charge for a chat.

I am UK based and work internationally presenting keynotes at conferences, delivering sales and marketing master classes and in-house training. I also work one on one with directors and key personnel as a personal coach and sales and marketing strategist.

Because I subscribe to the philosophy that success is 80% attitude and 20% skills, I utilize and incorporate personal growth strategies into much of my interaction with the people I work with when appropriate to do so.

I have 25 + years business experience and early in my journey, I was an employee with several companies and thereafter, an owner / manager of several businesses which I developed and subsequently sold. I also have considerable experience in helping companies to franchise their businesses and continue to do so.

In 1993 I was commissioned by BBC Books to write a book for them. 'Psycho-Selling- How To Double Your Income From Sales In 8 Weeks', was published by them in 1994 and was translated into several languages. It rapidly became an international best seller and launched my career as an author and conference speaker. A complimentary copy of this book is available on my website – bruceking.co.uk.

Since then I have written several other best sellers, including but not exclusively, Double Your Sales (Financial Times), Winning Sales Referrals (Soderpalm Publishing) , Cold Calling Made Easy (Crown Publishing), details of those are also available on my website.

I look forward to hearing from you soon. Why not right now?
