Joe C Burke's Summary

I have excelled for over 30 years at helping firms completely redefine their competitive market position, for leadership in their industry.
I do this in several steps, including
- understanding your products, customers, and market,
- honestly defining your competitive position
- defining the product/service mentality in your market (how your customers think)
- creating a differentiating brand and product that redefines the market and brings prospects to your sales process
- building a marketing mix & plan that clearly establishes your leadership and successfully feeds your sales efforts
- hiring/shaping a team that delivers the marketing mix/plan
- managing the transition from your old position to the new
- achieving essential goals within budget constraints
I have successfully performed transformations for many B2B firms, from raw startups to large public companies, focused on technology products and services, and in markets (e.g., commercial real estate, security, computer retailing, manufacturing) where technology was a part of the mix to achieve new positioning.
I have performed this work solo, or managed distributed marketing teams of over 100 people with an annual budget of millions of dollars. I am comfortable rolling up my sleeves (e.g., new business plan, writing the press release, coding the website, etc.), or orchestrating whole teams (hiring/training/motivating marketing & sales teams, standing before 1,000+ customers to pitch, teaching leadership teams to change).
I have been an author, a spokesman, a coach. I am intimately familiar with U.S. GAAP accounting compliance regulations, have lived in Europe and directed sales/marketing teams in both EMEA and Pac-Rim, as well as the USA. Titles I have held include: CEO, VP of Marketing, VP of Sales, VP of Operations, Managing Director, Director Channel Sales, Director Product Marketing.
Regardless what my title is, I come to transform, and I work with the whole team selflessly and equally.

Specialties

Burke has successfully built businesses in the following industry categories: CAFM/IWMS (Commercial Real Estate software), SCF (Supply Chain Finance), ERP, CRM, BI, BPM, JITM, Bio-ID, security software, computer hardware, retail (catalog) software/hardware sales, SaaS-delivery models, D/B architecture, and Consulting Services. Residence is flexible within the U.S. Boston-Washington corridor and travel can be up to 80% including international. I have lived and worked in Europe and Pac-rim.
