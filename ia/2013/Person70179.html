Juan Bermudez's Summary

Juan has worked as a technologist since 2006.

His work includes award winning applications and developments for clients like McDonalds, HBO, Sony Electronics, General Mills, Dish Networks, Sony Ericsson, Atari and Fisher Price.

In early 2012 Juan Co-Founded Nightlife Labs, building a web and mobile solution for the hospitality industry. The result was NightPro.co a cloud based solution for managing nightlife events and venues that has become the choice of world famous promoters & operators.
