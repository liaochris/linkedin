Ed Burgoyne's Summary

An accomplished agency director with over eighteen years experience in the field of advertising & marketing. I focus on building strategies and teams in an agency to increase operational efficiencies, improve agency culture, and sustain profitability.

A leadership approach that is team-driven.
I utilize my extensive project management and integrated production background to analyze issues, set best practices and lead agency-wide. I break down silos and have a strong belief in the value of true end-to-end project management. I'm known for having a level-headed demeanor, strong facilitation skills and fostering an environment where learning happens.

A proactive style that considers the nuances of culture.
I have a deep understanding of agency culture and how it can play an important role in the success of an agency. I am an advocate for the creative process, brand storytelling and true crafting. Ability to resolve and manage conflict in high-volume and time-sensitive environments.

An entrepreneurial approach to business.
I know what it takes to get work done, from agency financials to the mechanics of how to keep the organization on track. I identify opportunities for change and innovation internally in the agency and externally on client business. Not afraid to look for strategic ways to improve the business and organizational structure or redefining the business model.

Specialties: Managing director, operations director, head of production, executive producer, project management director, PMO lead. Advertising agency operations, process and workflow. Mentor, coach.

Multi-department management, agency-wide leadership, budget development, scope-of-work, hiring, resource allocations, forecasting, P&Ls, training, performance reviews, process development, accounting & project management systems.

Integrated, broadcast, digital, apps, mobile, social, local. Content, experiential, traditional, nontraditional & branding.
