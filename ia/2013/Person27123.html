Todd Hutchison's Summary

Are you dealing with people and project issues, but you're stuck? Unclear on what is next or don’t know what is needed?

Let me help give you the clarity on what has to be done, and provide the training, systems, processes and project coaching to “make it happen”.

Known as the Corporate Mechanic, as well as being an international bestselling author, certified professional speaker, and business consultant, I know what it takes to create a winning mindset that pushes through any barriers and achieves above and beyond the whelm of possibilities.

I have just been awarded the 2013 'Hall of Fame' recipient of the Worldwide Who's Who 2013 "Professional of the Year".

I am the founder and Global Chairman of People Rich, with offices in Australia, the UK and the USA. I have also authored the books; Project Coaching, Sales is a People Game, and Millionaire Motivators, as well as the Applied Project Management DVD series and the 2-CD set Power of Goals. I was also featured in the Millionaire Mentors as a leading coach.

If your organisation needs help to develop and execute your strategy, improve your business improvement, project management systems or team dynamics, then I can help you.
