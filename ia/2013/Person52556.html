Helen Oliveri's Summary

Ranked in the Top 20 Chicagoland Agents for 2012 by Chicago Agent Magazine, the Wall Street Journal and Real Trends naming The Helen Oliveri Team in the Top 250 Teams Nationwide by transaction sides for 3 years consecutively, and with over 1,200+ career sales, Helen Oliveri is a fierce leader in real estate. With a solid team of professionals, The Helen Oliveri Team is a full service real estate company that delivers results.

The Helen Oliveri Team is a business model of highly efficient "specialists" creating the best transaction for you. With your best interest at heart, our strategy focuses on making your next real estate transaction a complete success where your goals are fully realized.

Helen specializes in the Chicagoland & Chicago North Shore areas including but not limited to, Hawthorn Woods (60047), Long Grove (60047), Kildeer (60047), Lake Zurich (60047), Mundelein (60060), Glenview (60025, 60026), Morton Grove (60053), Northfield (60093), Northbrook (60062) and Wilmette (60091.) As your Lake and Cook County expert, when looking to buy or sell, look no further because Helen and her team will provide all you need to achieve your real estate goals.

We provide market knowledge, expert negotiation, top-of-the-line marketing and high-end customer service to our clientele. Our company will give you the edge you deserve to be successful in your real estate venture. We sell over 375+ homes a year and we are "Your Best Move" in real estate. Getting you top dollar, short market time and results is our goal. If you are looking to buy or sell, call us today!

Specialties: Selling and marketing residential real estate in the greater Chicago land area. Helen is the Hawthorn Woods Country Club expert and #1 broker in Illinois for Keller Williams Realty. Additional specialties include Northern Cook and Lake Counties such as Glenview, Long Grove, Kildeer, Lake Zurich and more.. Helen sells residential homes, luxury homes and foreclosure properties.
