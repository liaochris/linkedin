Chris Wachowiak's Summary

My four principal areas of expertise are Business Strategy, Entrepreneurship, Marketing and Promotions, and Event Planning and Execution.

I enjoy and am available for speaking engagements that allow me to share my successful business strategies and models with other passionate business people that are driven to succeed in their respective businesses, markets and careers.

I now operate and run two successful businesses, Kryptonite and EntreFunding, and am a managing partner for a third company, Studio Fyra.

I also write a weekly column relating towards business, entrepreneurship, and leadership in The Rock River Times as well as host a Radio Show/Podcast with Rockford College Radio called “Getting Down to Business”.
