Frank Diana's Summary

An exceptional business leader with thirty (30) years experience in technology and general management. The opportunity to lead technology organizations, business organizations, and companies, provided the experience required to ensure the effective alignment of technology with the needs of the business. Business leadership experience obtained through various C-Level positions, including CEO, CPO, CTO and CIO. Particular areas of technology leadership include knowledge management, business intelligence, text analytics, predictive analytics, prescriptive analytics, knowledge engineering, social technologies, social intelligence, collaborative solutions, customer intelligence, and risk management.

Strong visioning skills have enabled the identification of industry trends quickly and broadly, with an ability to see the implications of trends years into the future. These skills have led to the effective identification of new business ventures and the use of leading edge technology to create competitive advantage ahead of broad market adoption. Strong leadership skills enable the rapid realization of value.

A win-win approach and collaborative strengths get the most out of strategic and tactical initiatives across organizations. Excellent communication skills were developed through the many years communicating with C-Level Executives and Industry Analysts, and fine tuned through the effective use of various social media techniques. Particular strengths include articulating complex ideas in a clear and concise manner and helping business people understand technology in layman’s terms.

Frank is a frequent speaker at industry events. Please dialog with him by visiting my Blog at http://frankdiana.wordpress.com/.
