Doris Klietmann, AICI CIP, MBA's Summary

SUMMARY

Doris is an international certified image professional and certified seminar leader in Boston, Massachusetts, USA. Doris specializes in training executives and entrepreneurs managing their personal and professional image.

Doris’ professional profile, acumen and international recognition has grown consistently over the past years. She is a respected conference speaker, TV and radio presenter, author and columnist and has been published in numerous business journals and magazines. She is frequently interviewed by the media on image and branding of high profile individuals creating a personal signature brand.

Doris is applying the forces that determine brand identity resulting in career progression and executive presence.

Doris’ training approach and results delivery reflect the highest standards in personal and professional branding.

Doris is affiliated with the Association of Image Consultants International (AICI). The Premier Association for Image Consultants worldwide.
She is also a member of the Boston Club, New England’s leading organization for the advancement and recognition of corporate and professional women.

Doris is fluent in German, English and conversational French.

Specialties

Brand Management, Concept Development, Conferences, Image Consulting, Career Management, Lifestyle Management, Fashion Consulting, Social Trend Analysis
