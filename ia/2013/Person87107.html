Mark Stevens' Summary

Mark Stevens is a bestselling author and a popular media commentator (CNBC and Fox Business) on a host of business matters including marketing, branding, management and sales. Mr. Stevens is also the founder and CEO of MSCO, a global marketing firm specializing in strategy and integrated marketing solutions for such diverse clients as Nike, RE/MAX, Chartis, AIG and many small and mid-size businesses.

Known for delivering business insights with blunt truths and unconventional wisdom, Mr. Stevens shook the marketing establishment with his Business Week bestseller, “Your Marketing Sucks,” (2003) and redefined the rules of business management in “Your Management Sucks” (2006), “Your Company Sucks (2011) which identifies the four major reasons why companies fail.
Some of his other books include “God Is A Salesman,” “Rich Is A Religion,” “The Big Eight,” “King Icahn,” and “Sudden Death: The Rise and Fall of EF Hutton” (a Wall Street Journal bestseller and one of the “Best Business Books of the Year” by the Library Journal).

Mr. Stevens is also the author of the popular marketing blog, “Unconventional Thinking.” Mr. Stevens shares his unique wisdom on all aspects of business including marketing, strategic management, branding, consulting, advertising, as well as on popular issues of the day. The blog is consistently ranked in the Top 150 marketing blogs (equivalent to top 1% of all published blogs which count to over 72 million) and is described as the perfect blend of Harvard Business School and US Magazine, allowing everyone to relate to and learn about business.

Specialties: Marketing, consulting, branding, managing, innovation, public speaking, branding and identity, entrepreneurship, business

Mark Stevens' Publications

  • Your Marketing Sucks

    • Crown Business
    • April 26, 2003
    Authors: Mark Stevens

    Do you rely on things like "buzz" and "mindshare" to determine a campaign's effectiveness? If you answered "yes," chances are your marketing sucks. Mark Stevens' book can help you address the issues with your current tactics and give you the tools to design marketing that generates dollars, not just buzz.

  • Your Management Sucks

    • Crown Business
    • May 9, 2006
    Authors: Mark Stevens

    At some point in time, you’ve probably experienced management that sucks. It’s aimless, lethargic, and adrift without a clear picture of who is doing what to get where. Mark Stevens challenges traditional management philosophy and throws out the stuff that just doesn’t work. The end result is smart, effective management practices which you can adopt every day. We all manage something, right?

  • Your Company Sucks

    • BenBella Books
    • August 2, 2011
    Authors: Mark Stevens

    It’s time to declare war on yourself. This book will candidly force you to face the fact that the malaise your business suffers from is ultimately your responsibility and show you how to deal with it. There are only four reasons why a business fails, and Mark Stevens outlines how each one of them can rebound from lows and regain momentum, thrill customers, and regain a position as a source of pride and profit.
