Mauno Ahlgren's Summary

Hard worker, problem solver and always love to learn new things. I am a marketing professional with expertise on social media and website design. I am very passionate about social media and how it keeps evolving and offering us new ways of promoting and doing business. My personal mission is to encourage small business owners to take the time to learn how to use internet to work for their businesses! I repeatedly tell my clients that even though the Internet word can be very confusing at first, it is NOT rocket science. In fact, if one has managed to learn how to use VCRs, Betamaxes and cassette recorders, they are most definitely able to figure out social media.

I am a web designer and a computer coach concentrating on helping business owners to learn how to work with their computers and regular update their websites. Most of my clients are working for themselves and want to be in control of their websites, if they need to update their information or write a blog. I sit down with them and make sure they are able to do this.

I have a BBA degree majoring in marketing and international business and an MBA degree from SUNY New Paltz concentrating on business management, analyses and marketing management. I am originally from Finland and currently reside in Albany, NY. I am a real people’s person, who loves working with people and helping customers and co-workers with whatever help they need.
