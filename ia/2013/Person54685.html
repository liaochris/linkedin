About Dovell
Dovell Bonnett has been creating security solutions for computer users for over 20 years. Recently he wrote “Dovell’s Three Laws of Computers” that drives the “why” in his products and services. In order to address Laws #2 and #3, he founded Access Smart, LLC so that computers remain servants to people, and that computers securely and accurately authenticate the user before performing any tasks. With each of his innovations, the end user — the person sitting in front of a computer — is his No. 1 customer.

This passion, as he puts it, to “empower people to manage digital information in the digital age” also led him to write the popular Online Identity Theft Protection for Dummies. Within the pervasive nature of our e-commerce and e-business community, personal information, from credit card numbers to your pet’s name, is more easily accessed, and identity theft and fraud has become an issue that touches every consumer.

Dovell Bonnett's Experience

Access Smart

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer & Network Security industry

June 2005 – Present (8 years 7 months) Ladera Ranch, CA

Access Smart offers the HIPAA, FACTA, etc. compliance requirement for multifactor authentications with our password management system. Add logical access security to your information security strategy.

Founded in 2005 and headquartered in Ladera Ranch, California, Access Smart, LLC (a certified CA Small Business) is to information security through reliable user authentication prior to network access. Authentication, authorization and non-repudiation do not have to be cumbersome to be effective. That’s why our products are designed using state-of-the-art security technologies while focusing on ease-of-use and low-cost-of-ownership.

Access Smart is turning the smartcard model upside down by allowing IT managers to utilize their existing security policies, badge credentials in circulation and network infrastructure investments. Power LogOn is compatible with 50+ different card types including CAC and PIV. Power LogOn licenses have no annual subscription or renewal fees, and all licenses are transferable to accommodate employee turnover.

- Dovell Bonnett, Founder and CEO of Access Smart
