Joe Vaughn's Projects

  • Street Fighting Man

    • March 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Joe Vaughn, Dennis Przywara, Andrew James, Sara Archambault, Torben Bernhard, Jolyn Schleiffarth, Brady Kimball

    STREET FIGHTING MAN is a character-driven feature documentary that follows three generations of men as they seek to build a future for themselves in post-industrial Detroit. The film offers a nuanced portrait of daily life in urban America and unflinchingly reveals how hard it can be to build a future when everything seems to be crumbling around you. Shot over the course of two years, STREET FIGHTING MAN uses the techniques of direct cinema and cinema verite to tell a complex story of tragedy, survival, and hope.
