Laurie Smith's Summary

~~ About Laurie J. Smith, Executive Resume Writer ~~

A specialist in senior executive resume writing and career transition assistance for more than two decades, Laurie Smith’s global clientele includes CEO, CTO, CIO, COO, CFO, CMO, EVP, VP, and Director-level candidates spanning start-up/entrepreneurial to Fortune 5 settings.

A certified resume writer, nationally recognized expert on executive-level resumes, and judge of multiple international resume writing competitions, Laurie's focus is on developing resumes and supporting documents that powerfully communicate an executive’s personal brand. She also emphasizes maintaining her clients’ competitive edge, by guiding them in leveraging the latest in effective job search and career management strategies, including social media.

~~ A Personal Note from Laurie ~~

My passion has long been to equip my clients with personally branded executive resumes and other career marketing collateral that eclipse the competition, along with career management and employment search techniques that ensure fast, successful career transitions. The goal is to inspire confidence as executives navigate the turbulent waters of today’s executive employment search, streamlining the career transition process while making it motivational, empowering, exciting, and even fun.

Clients and colleagues tend to describe me as a professional who can “truly make your resume stand out from the competition,” whose “resumes are some of the VERY best in the industry,” and who “understands what is needed for executives to present themselves well in today's difficult market.” (Scroll down to “Recommendations" for more.)

It is my firm belief that career success is critical to personal fulfillment, good family dynamics, lifelong financial management, and overall quality of life, and I am dedicated to helping my clients achieve all of these things through proactive career management.

Contact me via www.creativekeystrokes.com or ✆800-817-2779.

  • National Resume Writers Association (NRWA)

    By-Laws/Ethics Review Committee (past)

    The NRWA is a nonprofit trade association for professional resume writers, founded in 1997 to increase the visibility of the industry, encourage ethical practices, promote excellence, and raise industry standards through peer marketing and training. Laurie joined the NRWA not long after its founding, and her participation has included service on the organization's By-Laws and Ethics Review committee. http://nrwaweb.com/index.php

  • Professional Association of Resume Writers and Career Coaches

    Certification Board Member, Resume Writing Contest Judge (past)
    • 1991 to Present

    The Professional Association of Resume Writers & Career Coaches was founded in January of 1990. Prior to that time, there had been no association for career professionals to exchange information, enhance their skills, or demonstrate their commitment to providing professional services to the general public. PARW/CC was also the first organization to develop and offer a certification program for resume writers, a program in which Laurie participated as Certification Board Member, Test Material Developer, Test Grader, and Grading Instructor. http://www.parw.com/
