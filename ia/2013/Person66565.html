John Krech's Summary

My goal is to change the way the world does business!

Ordering, delivering, invoicing, and payment activities consist of numerous tasks, tasks that more than half of QuickBooks customers report as taking too long. Quite frankly – these tasks SUCK – suck time.

Improving any one of these tasks alone would be a great solution to existing customers, such a solution is RightOn; however, imagine an intelligence that can automatically trigger and link these activities together. Such a solution would be a huge opportunity – that solution is autoP2P.

autoP2P is an online network which automates and combines the ordering, delivering, invoicing, and payment activities between a buyer and a supplier with business intelligence that maintains peak financial performance. autoP2P eliminates over 27000 steps for the typical small business or 8 hours per week.

RightOn Inventory is an online solution which automates inventory replenishment in QuickBooks. RightOn eliminates over 11000 steps for the typical small business or 3 hours per week.

RightOn and autoP2P are $40 billion opportunities.
