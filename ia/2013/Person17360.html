BRIAN FLEMING, MBA's Summary

BRIAN FLEMING CRE/GROUP serves commercial real estate owners and tenants in the office,retail and industrial markets throughout South Florida including Miami-Dade, Broward and Palm Beach counties.

We provide sensible advice and transaction services based on our knowledge, negotiation abilities and the best market resources in the industry.

When considering your next commercial real estate move, call us. Businesses of every type call on us to resolve issues related to property sales and leasing, corporate locations, tenant representation, and investment opportunities. We stand behind our recommendations with logical property valuations and thorough research.

Specialties:

Medical and healthcare properties including sales and leasing; corporate locations; tenant- and owner representation assignments; investment opportunities and analysis; market research including demographic studies; retail void analysis; and commercial property valuations.
