Deborah Cooney's Summary

I began studying holistic medicine in Jan 2007 and worked on a small scale until Oct 2010 when I made Tranquil Path Therapies a full time business. In my clinic I work with both adults and children, Clients can present with a whole host of different ailments ranging from physical conditions (chronic pain), environmental factors (allergies/intolerances), limiting beliefs (low self-esteem), trauma caused by accidents, death, or abuse and mental/emotional conditions (depression/fears/phobias).

Tranquil Path Therapies is dedicated to helping people remove the obstacles (physical/ mental/emotional/spiritual) that are preventing them from moving forward, so they can live a full and rewarding life.
.

Specialties

Tranquil Path Therapies specialises in the use of The BodyTalk System and incorporates Reiki and Baby/Toddler/Adult Reflexology to provide a truly holistic approach to your health. Clients that attend the clinic receive tailored made sessions specific to their individual needs, taking into account their physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing.
