Marion Youngblood's Summary

I am a Certified Life Coach, a Speaker and I also write a blog. My message is simple - If you want to find your way back to yourself and listen to your own voice and fulfill your life's purpose, you can start right where you are. What awaits you is clarity, joy, synchronicity and fun...beyond your wildest dreams!

My blog is bestihave.com and is part of my website, (marionyoungblood.com) and that is where I share thoughts about life and how to manage the abundance that is knocking at the door just waiting to be let in. We are creating our lives with each decision we make. What will your next decision be? No pressure...

Executive Director

January 1998 – December 2009 (12 years)

I had the pleasure of serving as the chief fundraiser and event producer of Light Up Durham for nearly twelve years. The mission of Light Up Durham, Inc., a nonprofit organization, was to shine the light on the exquisitely talented youth of the Durham, NC area, to bring the diverse community together and demonstrate, with the youth being the teachers, that the differences among us pale in comparison to all that we have in common.
