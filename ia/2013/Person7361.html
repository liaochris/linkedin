Andy Britnell's Summary

Andy Britnell has a training & coaching practice based in Somerset. He also runs 'See A Man About a Blog' which is a company set up to help people who are not technical get to grips with blogging and social media. He is an licensed practitioner for Insights Learning & Development, a certified master NLP practitioner & a certified hypnotherapist. He delivers corporate development programmes to local, national and international businesses, and coaches on a corporate & individual basis.

He has had a varied career, studying for a degree in Hotel and Catering Administration and then working in the music business on a semi-professional basis. Tired of being a starving musician he joined BT and over 15 years worked his way up from clerical work to international sales and service roles, in which he dealt with multi-million pound global clients & was one of the top-performing Account Managers in his sector.

Andy had also started to run workshops for his colleagues on personal development and Emotional Intelligence and was approached by BT’s training arm to take on a secondment developing and delivering sales and service programmes to an international audience, including the top 10% of sales professionals in BT.

Andy’s first taste of NLP was through the Journey self-development workshops of Brandon Bays, which illustrated the profound transformational effects of NLP on participants’ long-term health and emotional problems.

Intrigued by the possibilities, Andy completed his NLP master practitioner training and uses the principles both in his coaching work, seminars and workshops

As a keen surfer Andy is grateful for living close to Cornwall although would like to visit his favourite breaks a bit more often. He plays 6-string and bass guitar and is currently composing original material to be released next year.
