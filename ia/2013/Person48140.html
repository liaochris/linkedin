Gerard Denys' Summary

Berlin & Denys Insurance and Financial Services has a strong track record of providing excellent service to our clients, our contracted agents and our employees. We think outside of the box to design intelligent solutions to the high cost retirement, income continuation and health insurance.

We are always looking for quality agents and high performing agencies to join our team. We offer on-line training for the basics and hands on field training with our dedicated trainers.

We teach our agents to use the Florida Blue health insurance products and Healthcare Reform as door openers to other products like Annuities, Critical Illness and Life Insurance products.

Berlin & Denys Insurance is a true "one stop shop" when it comes to insurance products. We offer Health, Life, Annuities, PEO, Auto, Home, Business and other products. When all of your business is in one place we can help you more efficiently because we can coordinate all of your coverage plans.

Specialties: We are experts in the areas of group and individual health insurance, annuities and life insurance protection.
