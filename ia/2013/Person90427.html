Michael McGee's Summary

Michael McGee is a Business Consultant that works with small to medium Creative Agencies and Professional Services companies.

Initially I conduct a comprehensive review of the business, identify the profit gap, and highlight any issues and risks that relate to technology, people & processes.

The review is then complete with a list of recommendations and an implementable action plan that can be addressed by using internal resources or by engaging me to assist with the implementation and project management.

My focus over the past 13 years has been specifically with the creative industry, working with Design, Digital, Marketing, PR agencies and Architects.

Specialties

Business Consulting, Process Mapping, Facilitation, Systems Implementation, Change Management, Management Accounting
