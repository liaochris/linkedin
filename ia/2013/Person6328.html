Andrew Davies' Summary

I am a co-founder of idio, a UK-based marketing technology company. idio helps major brands and media companies deliver personalised content marketing to b2b or b2c audiences across multiple digital channels. This builds strong customer relationships and deep customer intelligence.

I have helped build idio from scratch, to its current position as a award-winning and rapidly-growing business, that is serving major clients in the UK and the US. See http://idioplatform.com for more details.

A few years before idio, I co-founded thrudigital, a social-media development agency based near Kings Cross in London, which served clients such as Universal Studios, No. 10 Downing Street, and Sky. idio has now acquired thrudigital as part of its expansion plans.

I gained early experience with large multi-nationals such as Deloitte (working in Audit and Strategy Consulting for Tech/Media/Telecoms clients) and Reuters (Equities Analyst).

I graduated with a First Class Honours degree from Warwick Business School. Also studied at Richmond University, VA, for 1 year.

I am young and have lots to learn, but have had the opportunity to speak at several conferences and workshops. I have also had articles published in several trade publications, such as Marketing Week, Media Week, Mad.co.uk and Net Imperative. I also offer consulting services in the areas I specialise in: content analytics for ECM and CRM, social media monitoring & marketing, online marketing strategy, personalisation and targeting, web startups, web 2.0 etc.
