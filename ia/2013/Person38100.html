Lydia Sosenko DDS's Summary

Dr. Sosenko is the CEO of SMART Dental Systems an organization dedicated to providing the tools, resources and accountability to increase a dental practice’s productivity while minimizing ‘business stress’. Coaching dentists, through the eyes of a colleague, she helps maximize current opportunities or develop new areas such as dental sleep medicine within one’s practice. Dr. Sosenko particular strengths include one on one coaching, team communication, leadership, marketing, and dental sleep medicine integration.

Dr. Lydia Sosenko received her dental degree from the University of Illinois in Chicago. She entered the world of dental sleep medicine in1998 and became one of the first Diplomates of the AADSM in the Chicago area. She currently limits her practice to dental sleep medicine while maintaining a general dental practice. This business arrangement, allows Dr. Sosenko the unique position of seeing daily obstacles in a general dental practice as well as specific obstacles in the practice management of dental sleep medicine. She has lectured to international attendees of the AADSM numerous times on the topics of incorporating dental sleep medicine into a general practice.

Dr. Sosenko is passionate in reaching a neglected population of snoring and apnea sufferers’ with the use of her creativity, leadership training and post doctorate training in business development and coaching. In 2012, Dr. Sosenko's general dentistry practice was awarded a prestigious small business award in her community by one of the strongest Chambers in the country.

Dr. Sosenko is devoted to the well-being of her patients and communication with her referring members of the medical and dental fields, as well as working with business owners, leaders and executives who want strategies to be successful, move to excellence and achieve profound breakthroughs.
