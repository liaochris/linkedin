Luís Filipe Brandão's Publications

  • CAD graphical interface for simulation of semiconductor devices

    • November 25, 2011
    Authors: Luís Filipe Brandão, João Prata, Tiago Ramalho

    The ASCA Project is a system for virtually modeling an object, based on semiconductors, and simulating its behavior in different circumstances. The prime motivation was to create a user-friendly, visual, point-and-click software, with a simplified interface and inspired by the actual industrial process. This paper presents a possible solution to the problem of the complexity to model virtual objects; we are going to propose an interface with a low learning curve. Our goal is also to propose a scalable, extensible and uncoupled architecture. The final result is an open-source software prototype, developed as a proof of concept, offering an interface which simplifies modeling multi-dimensional devices. The proposed architecture divides the system into two major modules the “simulation executor” and the “simulation designer” and they where designed to promote extensibility.
