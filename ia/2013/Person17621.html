Brianna Wagenbrenner's Projects

  • A Day for Southern Student Campaign

    • August 2010 to August 2011
    Team Members: Brianna Wagenbrenner, Vince Miller

    A Day for Southern Student Campaign was first established three years ago. The campaign allows students to get involved in Georgia Southern's largest fundraising campaign, A Day for Southern. Students can donate anywhere on campus by making a contribution during the campaign, which is the first two weeks of the academic year. During the last campaign we raised over $4,000, which helped contribute to the overall A Day for Southern campaign of over $1,000,000.

    I worked along side my other co-workers and Southern Legacy members to help promote and run the campaign. I personally worked with Greek Life's involvement in the campaign through speaking engagements, networking and relationship management.

  • Parent Giving

    • November 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Brianna Wagenbrenner, Vince Miller

    The parent giving project is the first established fundraising campaign directed only to parents. The campaign is separated into two giving categories: Leadership Society and Leadership Council. The campaign allows parents to become more involved in their child's education through both philanthropy and volunteer involvement.

    I researched for parent giving to establish the leadership society and leadership council's description, goals, giving amount, copy material, promotional material and campaign design. I then worked with my boss to create those materials in order to launch the campaign at the end of the Fall 2011 semester.
