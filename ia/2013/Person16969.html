Brent Turner's Summary

A digital native on the cloverleaf of product, technology, analytics, and marketing.

Everyday I solve problems with some of the world's best creators, builders, and hackers. Those who also obsess quality, embody accountability, and build strong relationships. Those with an insatiable need to always be learning. Those who build to think. Those who tolerate my morning guy ways and standing desk. Those who launch quickly, then iterate and optimize.

Through my career, my teams have built and launched numerous products, brands, campaigns, technologies, and companies. We have delivered for start-ups and upstarts, Fortune 10 companies, the world’s most respected brands, and the scrappy challengers in between.

To those who also give a damn and have something to prove: I look forward to working together.
