Loren Keith Jordan's Experience

President/ CEO

B.O.L.D. Athletics

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Sports industry

June 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Twin Cities, Minnesota

Loren Keith Jordan serves as the President and Chief Executive Officer of B.O.L.D. Athletics. B.O.L.D. Athletics specializes in helping professional athletes and their families transition into the next phase of their lives after sports. Jordan founded B.O.L.D. Athletics during the spring of 2011 after closely studying and monitoring how professional athletes were unprepared financially and in other areas of their lives for the lockout that occurred in the NFL and NBA.

Loren aspires to help professional athletes through business ventures that build a legacy by helping his clients develop into business icons within the industries they aspire to become a part of.

B.O.L.D. is an acronym that stands for "Business Of Life's Destinations."

Our team of professionals are verified experts who are highly educated in their industry and are capable of helping you achieve your goals both on and off the field. Our focus is not only to help you protect everything you have built up for yourself through sports (defensive strategy), but to bring that level of success to other areas of your life (offensive strategy), allowing you to feel secure and confident in the direction you are headed.

My main responsibilities include developing and implementing high-level strategies, making major company decisions, managing the overall operation and resources between various accounts, traveling to meet clients, maintain a high-level of customer service and overall enjoyable experience, market and brand the company and acting as the main point of communication between multiple parties who are involved with B.O.L.D. Athletics.
