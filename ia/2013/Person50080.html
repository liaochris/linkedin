Greg Davis' Summary

29 years of experience as a Landscape Designer and Landscape Contractor. I own and operate the firm of Gregory Davis & Associates with a full time crew of landscape construction experts. We are a Design/Build firm capable of Designing, Budgeting and Installing all phases of a complete landscape or a specific project phase.

Goal: To personalize our experience here in Los Angeles by creating a beautiful outdoor lifestyle at your home.

Specialties

Landscape renovations to create "Outdoor Living Spaces" that draw the homeowners outdoors to spend time being nurtured by the positive life force that is enhanced by Living Plants and Good Design.
