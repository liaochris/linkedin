Deborah E. Dorsey's Summary

Deborah has over 20 years experience in real estate, is an Associate Broker and an active member of Suburban West Realtors Association, of which, she is a past Board Director. Deborah has years of experience serving on the Professional Standards Committee, which strives to ensure that the real estate business is ethical.

She prides herself on our close working relationships with clients. These relationships are based on active listening, open communication and confidentiality. She is not interested in the quick sale; she is interested in building relationships, serving the needs of your family, and honoring your budget. As her client, you can relax in the knowledge that she will do what is best for you, and work to make your home-buying or selling experience enjoyable.

Specialties

Residential real estate sales, new construction, working with seniors, first time buyers and relocation.
