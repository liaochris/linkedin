Juan Carlos Diaz's Summary

I provide experiential marketing services to agencies and brands across North America. From turn-key productions to short term consulting projects. With a wealth of experience, contacts and resources I help some of the best agencies and brands to execute exceptional events anywhere & anytime.

Specialties:
• VIP Hospitality & On-Premise Special Events
• Immersive Simulations and Gaming Experiences
• Design & Production of marketing projects
• Brand & Product launch campaigns
• Experiential Marketing

My expertise is in Hospitality & PR Events for Beverages, Technology, Telecom, Interactive Media and CPG of FORTUNE 500 Brands. I bring 20 years of management experience in event production, grassroots, field marketing, retail promos, campus events and mobile tours in 100 North American cities. I have programmed DJ, sound & lighting, eCommerce websites, APPs, social, mobile & email marketing campaigns tied to my live event productions.
﻿
I have produced and managed VIP PR events, Nightclubs, Concerts, Festivals, Tours, Fundraisers, Fashion Shows, Theater Productions and Art Exhibitions. At age 22, my biggest production was a 2-day Live Concert with 50 bands on 5 stages for 60,000 people. My longest production was a nationwide College Tour promoting consumer brands at on-campus and on-premise events by age 24. My largest talent management and group training is 120+ Brand Ambassadors, 100+ On-Premise/Off-Premise Sales Reps and 50+ Performing Artists executing 1,000,000 brand samples.
﻿
My biggest accomplishments are marketing the 1st college smart card system, 1st transnational & national Fiber Optics systems, 1st prepaid phone program, 1st text-messaging service, 1st mobile multimedia download service, 1st mobile TV service, 1st portable digital media player, 1st PC/TV system, 1st Interactive On-Demand TV Channel, 1st Consumer Broadband Internet/TV service, 1st Military Simulator in Consumer Video Games, 1st flavored Vodka line, multiple start-up websites and events.
