Steve Casey's Summary

STEVE CASEY / CASEY DESIGN INC.
WWW.STEVECASEYDESIGN.COM
Agoura Hills, CA 91301


Steve Casey is a hands-on designer / craftsman creating fine custom woodwork for the Los Angeles and Southern California market since 1978. As well as being an accomplished master craftsman, Steve is also a nationally published author. Casey has written several articles for Fine Woodworking Magazine based on his unique and exclusive expertise designing and building custom home theater furniture, cabinetry and entertainment centers. Steve is also well known for his ability to produce one-of-a-kind fine furniture, fine cabinetry, wood sculpture and art furniture for residential and commercial clients. For over thirty three years Casey has also worked closely and collaborated with the finest professionals in the audio / video, architectural and interior design fields. Please visit www.stevecaseydesign.com for more information on Steve, his work and business as well as extensive information on how to commission new work. You are also invited to contact Steve directly by phone 818-706-3147 to discuss your next custom woodworking project.
