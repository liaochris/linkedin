Elisa Miller-Out's Summary

Elisa is CEO and Co-Founder of Singlebrook Technology, Inc., a custom web and mobile development company based in Ithaca, NY that specializes in web application development, mobile apps, integration, databases, content management systems and technology strategy consulting. Singlebrook is a certified B Corp and has a strong emphasis on social and environmental responsibility. The company's mission is: Technology for Change. Clients include the American Chiropractic Association, College and University Professional Association for Human Resources, Call2Action, CSRwire, Peeled Snacks, the Environmental Defense Fund, Mission Markets, RSF Social Finance, the Social Venture Network and Cornell University. She has been featured in articles in the New York Times, Forbes, the Washington Post, Mashable, USA Today and many other publications. She’s spoken about entrepreneurship and technology at several events including the We Live NY Summit at Cornell University and the NYSAE Technology Institute. She graduated Summa Cum Laude from Barnard College of Columbia University. When she’s not working at Singlebrook, Elisa enjoys cooking with local foods, fitness, reading and playing Suzuki cello with her two daughters.

Specialties

Singlebrook Technology specializes in: Custom Web Application Development, Web Databases, Integration, Mobile Apps, Content Management Systems, Web Design, Member Research (Survey & Benchmarking) Software, Web Technology Strategy & Consulting
