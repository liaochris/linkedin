Joe Foley's Summary

Being an unrepentant entrepreneur, my current efforts are directed toward 2 areas. I am the Director of Leading Edge Medical, a sales and marketing partnership that promotes several medical, health, wellness, and practice management products. My current passion involves a patent pending technology for medical imaging that will bring "Transparency Through Technology". I honor my career in radiology by providing approved CEU programs for my fellow radiologic technologists. I am also enthusiastic about encouraging, training and coaching small businesses that want to do business with large entities, such as the federal government. My aptitude here comes via 2+ years of experience in federal contractor training and offering a program called "Running With The Big Dogs". I'm an ardent networker and host my own medical mixer in the Houston area. An ongoing major interest is veteran affairs and I have a vision for a business development program that would be a vehicle for jobs, housing, training, entrepreneurship and wellness for my fellow vets.

Specialties:
* Sales/marketing professional and advocate for health and wellness.
* Advocate/coach for small businesses outreach to larger entities.
* Advocate/coach for business development.
* Continuing education provider for radiology professionals.
* Champion for military veterans' welfare and success.
