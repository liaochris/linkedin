Bev Brammer's Summary

In 2005, after 30 years of yo-yo dieting I decided to embark on a lifestyle change. I shed 3 stone, and more importantly, learnt all about healthy eating. I have kept the weight off and developed a healthy relationship with food. This has led me to learn more about food and nutrition. I am now qualified as a Diet & Nutrition Advisor and I am setting up my own business to help anyone who is fed up with yo-yo dieting and wants to change for the better. I understand the problems, the cravings and the difficulties because I've been there. So I offer a 1 to 1 personal consultancy together with daily help, support and advice via email or text - you never you know when you'll need a friend!

Specialties

- Personal 1 to 1 support
