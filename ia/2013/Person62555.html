Jeri Winkler's Summary

I specialize in remote real estate support with 13 years experience in the real estate industry. I am a certified RESS through the International Virtual Assistants Association, a member of Remote Professionals,a member of REVA network, the premier organization for real estate virtual assistants and a founding member of CyberstarVA. There is a secret to real estate success - The Secret Assistant!

I am a licensed realtor in the state of Virginia, with that license currently on inactive status. I work with Realtors® across the country from the major franchises, RE/MAX, Keller Williams, Century 21, Coldwell Banker, Weichert, GMAC, ERA and more, as well as with brokers that own their own companies. I am up-to-date on the latest technology and the hottest real estate programs. I know the markets across the country and knows what works. If you need to know what will work for your market call me!
