Angelica Heredia's Summary

Working in the fashion industry I held positions such as fashion show coordinator, stylist, apparel buyer, apparel & jewelry wholesaler, retail sales, and much more. I started a sportwear line, Frankee Gear, in memory of my youngest brother, Francisco (still in the works), then created my own Austin based fashion show company, Manoken Productions working with boutiques and salons around town. After much searching, I found myself making gift baskets for friends and family and became very drawn to gifting. My inspiration developed for what is now my e-commerce gift boutique - Delicia's Gifts.
I am a huge supporter of other entrepreneurs and Austin small businesses. If you are passionate and motivated you can achieve anything!
