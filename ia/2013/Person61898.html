Jennifer Lundstrom's Summary

I purchased my first home in 2002 and quickly thereafter decided that a career in real estate was a great fit for me. I got my real estate license and jumped in to the business right away. I started strong and haven’t stopped since; successfully closing over 100 transactions. I absolutely love my job and can’t think of any career that suits me better.

Portland is where I was raised. In fact, my family goes back 5 generations in the Portland area! My grandfather and my dad even went to the same grade school, high school and college as I did. I have traveled around the states and to several other countries, but consider Portland my true love.

I currently live in North Portland but I’ve lived in many different neighborhoods throughout the city. I work in all parts of town and with many different types of people. For me, it’s about where my clients’ needs lie rather than working with a particular niche, although I do tend to work with a lot of first-time home buyers.

My business partner Sam Fuqua and I work full-time and all year long, keeping ourselves very available for our clients. I’m truly blessed to have found a job that I enjoy so much, a company that fits the level of professionalism that I thrive for and a business partner that shares my “client-first” mentality. Our team is made up not only of the two of us, but also includes a few trust-worthy and hard-working mortgage brokers, a select number of inspectors, contractors, escrow officers and other professionals. We find that the client’s experience is so much better with the help of these trusted team players who share our level of ethics and professionalism.

Specialties

Specializing in old homes, new homes, tall homes, skinny homes, condos, first-time buyers, renovations, neighborhood and city-wide happenings, out-of-state relocation situations, divorcing or distressed sellers, foreclosures, investment properties, both residential as well as commercial properties.
