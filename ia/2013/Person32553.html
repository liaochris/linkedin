David Schwartz's Summary

David Schwartz’s genuine interest in people gives him the ability to make his subjects relaxed and comfortable in front of the camera. David has expertise in utilizing the right lighting techniques that create beautiful portraits that impress both his clients and photography subjects alike. He utilizes the latest in technology to provide his clients with the captured image immediately, on a digital tablet, allowing for any adjustments and selection, in real time. This guarantees that he has captured the desired image. Many of his clients select their final images for retouching right on set, saving them valuable time. They do not have to worry about sorting though online selection galleries nor waiting for proof CDs to be delivered.

David believes that his customers come first and he is wholeheartedly dedicated to delivering final products that that satisfy his customers 100%. David's growing clientele include major universities, healthcare organizations, financial institutions, advertising agencies and working professionals.

During his free time David turns his lens to documenting Historic Route 66. Since 2004, he has been devoting his time to preserving the legacy of “America's Main Street”. All the while, he creates beautiful pieces of photographic art that have been met with much interest in the Route 66 community. From 2011-2012 a retrospective of over 60 of David's "Pics on Route 66" images were on display at the Crawford Auto Museum.

Since 2003, David has been teaching community education photography classes at his alma mater. He has taught over 400 students on how to successfully compose and capture quality photographic images. David thoroughly enjoys passing along the knowledge that he has learned over the years to new budding photographers.

David would love to apply his expertise, creativity and ability to work well with people to your next photographic project!
