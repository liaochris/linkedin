Bonnie Feldman's Summary

I draw upon my 360⁰ view of healthcare to help digital healthcare companies with corporate strategy, including: market research, business development, financing, and marketing communications. My hot topics are mobile health games, online social networking and support for behavior change and Big Data and analytics.

Digital Health:My white paper: Mobile Social Games for Health, published on MobiHealthNews, built a framework for understanding emerging companies that use game mechanics and online support networks to enable patient-centric healthcare across the disease spectrum: from chronic disease to health and wellness. Big Data- Hype and Hope, published by Rock Health is my latest research.

Business Development Consultant: corporate development, market research, sales, marketing, communications, investor targeting and pitching. My business development experience includes partnerships for mobile healthcare apps, investor presentations, and custom due-diligence work with new investors/partners, and creating a dental franchise for an investment bank.

Investment Finance: first sell-side and buy-side equity analyst, then agency and in-house investor relations. On Wall Street, my nickname was the “due diligence hound dog”. I’ve worked with scores of management teams on strategic assessment of potential and emerging markets.

Communications, Marketing, Investor Relations and Public Relations: I transform complex jumbles of ideas into easy-to- remember stories with visual flair.

Data Analytics: My love for data analytics arose from my work as a health services researcher at The Rand Corporation, where I examined quality of care and health outcomes.

Medical Entrepreneur with “behavior change experience”: I’ve built two dental businesses from the ground up; managing finances, staffing, operations and business development while passionately working to turn fearful, noncompliant patients into relaxed clients focused on preventive health.

Specialties

Persistence and resilience, business development, strategic planning, market research, competitive analysis and identifying partnership opportunities in rapidly evolving markets, investor outreach,investor presentations with a cogent investment thesis using simple and elegant custom visuals
