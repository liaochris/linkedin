Malgosia Green's Summary

I was the Founder & CEO of Savvica, an online marketing company focused on international education. Savvica is funded by Educomp Solutions Ltd. (EDSO.BO), India's largest education company.

Savvica launched LearnHub.com in 2008, a higher education, test prep, and career network for Indian students. It attracts close to 4 million students monthly, making it the #1 education site in the world's largest student market.

Savvica also runs the world's most used test prep website, JumboTests.com.

In 2010, Savvica acquired its KPCB-backed rival, a fellow major Indian online education company, StudyPlaces.

Previously, I was the Director of Product Management at Affinity Labs, an online communities company. Affinity Labs was funded by the Mayfield Fund and Trinity Ventures. It was acquired after 14 months by Monster Worldwide (NYSE:MWW) for $61MM.

I have been featured in Canadian Business Magazine's "20 Young Women in Power" and Toronto Life.

I hold a B.Sc. (Hons) in System Design Engineering from the University of Waterloo.

Specialties: Execution of Internet-enabled B2C ventures, online consumer product design, online marketing, operations.
