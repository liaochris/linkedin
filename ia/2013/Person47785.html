Geoffrey Hetrick's Summary

Hi! Let's start with what I want you to see: The several professional recommendations in the Experience and Skills & Expertise sections below. They say it better than I. And they're geniuses! Go ahead...

Now, my take: I'm a wordsmith and I'm proficient in the technical and creative arenas. I produce copy and content for any platform and in any form that is crisp, clear and concise - to suit your needs. Content - words, video, audio, graphics, tables, charts, etc. - should not land haphazardly onto a platform, although sometimes it can seem that way and as if no one cared. If it matters, these elements are painstakingly planned, considered, fawned-over, scrutinized, reviewed, nurtured, improved, approved and then sent out to do their work. In sync. In harmony. In other words, someone needs to care and have control of the content - the message. I care.

I thoroughly enjoy collaborating with my clients and I become an advocate for their products and services. Your ideas effectively communicated. Accurate and on-point. Persuasive writing. Compelling copy. Reach your target audience. Get your message across efficiently and intelligently.

Specialties: Technical and conventional writer and editor. Website content management. Document management. Analyze website metrics and Excel spreadsheet data and convert into charts and graphics. Digital Photography and editing; graphics editing. Quick thinker. Connect to reality and the abstract. Think in a new way; sometimes go unconventional! See things from all angles. Communicate effectively to those who want to hear from you. Quality control; I find errors others miss. I consider things others do not.
