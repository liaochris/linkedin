Kristie McMaster's Summary

Kristie McMaster is a dynamic go-getter who takes life by the horns and inspires others around her to do the same.

Having survived neuroblastoma (cancer) as an infant, overcome a severe illness as a teenager which caused her to be hospitalised at-risk of heart failure, and in the last 5 years starting a HR Consultancy from scratch and increasing the revenue by double & triple digits year on year at the same time as having her first baby, you can start to see that Kristie is a very determined young women who does whatever it takes to move herself forward despite any adversity or challenge.

Kristie is a true leader who aspires to encourage and empower other leaders, young and old, male and female, to step up to the mark and live life to their fullest potential....no matter what!

Through her new business Green Brain Training Kristie aims to help individuals, teams and leaders explore personal and professional strengths, challenges and paths to ultimate goals and success.
