Leah Darrow-Stano's Summary

My professional experience has been in ergonomics, occupational rehabilitation, and employment. I have worked as an occupational therapist in the Seattle area since 1999. Prior to that, I worked as a staffing specialist and recruiter for the health care industry.

My specialty is helping businesses invest wisely in ergonomic modifications and/or disability accommodations for their employees. I also have extensive experience working collaboratively with medical and vocational professionals to resolve medically complex workers' compensation cases.

My role is to assist companies in providing a safe, comfortable, and efficient work environment for their employees while empowering employees to understand how their work behaviors and postures impact their comfort and productivity at work.

I provide ergonomic consulting services (both early intervention/prevention and return to work) in a wide variety of industries. I also continue to work in a limited capacity as a clinician, treating injured workers in all phases of the occupational rehabilitation continuum.

Specialties: Ergonomic assessments for medically complicated cases, pre-job accommodation/job modification services through Washington State Labor and Industries, transitional return to work programs, ADA compliance, ergonomic assessments for disability accommodation, chronic pain management, pre-employment/post-offer test design.
