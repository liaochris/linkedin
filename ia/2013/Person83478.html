  • In The Mix

    Welcome to In the Mix! I’m Luke Ryan, the Aspiring Bartender, and I’m going to show you how to make some of my favorite cocktails and how to be a better online entrepreneur—but you don’t have to take just my advice! Professionals in social media and online branding, as well as some of my favorite celebrities, will also share their success stories and some priceless advice about how to make it happen in your own business.

    Always In The Mix

    This web series combines two of my most passionate interests: mixing great drinks and talking with fascinating people. I’m only an “aspiring” bartender, since I’m new to mixology, but I’ve spent my life getting to know people from all corners of the world, and I’ve learned what questions to ask to get the most out of each conversation. That means every tip, trick, and tidbit from the leaders in marketing technology, small business organization, social media, and more is passed straight on to you, which goes a long way to making you a leader in your own field.

    Who We Feature

    My guests are selected based on the likelihood of their names coming up when I ask myself, “If I could share a drink with anyone in the world, who would I choose?” Then, I reach out to them via email, Twitter, G+, Facebook, and other nefarious means until they agree to come on the show. So far, my persistence has paid off!

    Three Ways to Enjoy

    Each episode is available in three formats: 1) the full episode, which contains both a cocktail recipe and an interview with a cool guest, 2) a video of just the interview, and 3) a just the drink video. That way, you can watch the whole show or just the part you’re most interested in at the moment.
