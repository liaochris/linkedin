Robert Krummenacker's Summary

Robert Krummenacker Biography

Bob was Partner of Algometrix and VP of Analytics at Cross Country Computer, a data management provider specializing in online and offline database applications, merge purge and related services. Bob has over 20 years of experience processing and analyzing Very Large Databases, typically in support of direct marketing applications. During his career, Bob has worked with more than a hundred clients across a wide range of sectors including Publishing, Banking, Retail and Non-Profit to build, maintain and analyze databases that have exceeded 1 billion records. For the last 12 years, Bob has been the chief architect of CrossAlyze, an advanced analytical database system designed for very rapid experimentation, data processing, and discovery of “Gems of Opportunity within the Multidimensional Desert”. In his spare time, he plays solo acoustic guitar.

Specialties

Very Large (100k - 100M+ records) Database Creation / Maintenance / Analysis
