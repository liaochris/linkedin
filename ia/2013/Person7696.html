Angela Gruszka's Summary

Angela Gruszka is a creative brand strategist and communications expert with a passion for technology and innovation. She has over 10 years of experience developing and implementing integrated marketing and communications programs in the design and fashion industries.   

Currently, she is the Director of Marketing and Communications at ABC Carpet & Home. There she oversees social media, public relations, content creation, brand marketing, and digital creative design and development. During her tenure, Angela has been instrumental in redefining ABC Carpet & Home’s brand position in the industry. She has worked proactively to build and implement tactical marketing strategies while offering insight and guidance of new and innovative platforms and technology. Applying her strategic and creative abilities, she repositioned the rug division, building several brands including Color Reform, which has proven favorable results in customer acquisition, retention, and year over year sales growth. Presently, she is working with the Director of Ecommerce to spearhead the redevelopment of ABC Carpet & Home's ecommerce platform and online brand presence.

Prior to ABC Carpet & Home, Angela oversaw the public relations for Anne Klein working closely with the Vice President of Corporate Communications and the Head of Marketing to elevate the brand image and preserve the luxury brand integrity.

In her free time, Angela enjoys traveling to remote destinations and traversing the slopes of mountain peaks around the globe.
