Eric Bogs' Summary

I'm driven by technology and by media (art, music, stories), and my passions tend to lie in the intersection of the two. I love connecting creators, curators and consumers, and by solving impossible problems with technology.

I am an entrepreneur and technical leader based in New York. I have been building web applications since 1996, and in the last 17 years I have enjoyed building and launching a variety of businesses and products at Google, Yahoo!, Etsy, Scient and elsewhere.

I enjoy product development, engineering management, technical leadership, project delivery and recruiting. Recent focus has been on building and leading product/engineering teams to rapidly build, launch, and iterate on products. Technical strengths are on web applications design, architecture and performance, as well as internationalization, mobile development and front-end technology.

My formal technical education focused on interface design and multithreaded application design at Caltech. My formal artistic education centered around typography and letterform design at Art Center.
