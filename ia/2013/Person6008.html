Amy Morais' Summary

Amy Morais has worked in digital and interactive publishing for more than 15 years, but she still recalls the thrill of seeing her first web page download into a Netscape browser. She thrives on developing creative communication solutions for clients, translating a brand message and marketing goals into engaging user experiences.
