Gaspare Marturano's Summary

Gaspare Marturano is a well respected social media and digital marketing thought leader, industry speaker and author of SocializeWith.Me or Someone Else Will (available on Amazon and Barnes & Noble). With over 20 years of experience, Gaspare is now leading Fastlane’s Digital Marketing and Social Media practice, providing best practice counsel and execution in the areas of social media policies, processes, people, technologies and content marketing programs on behalf of our valued clients.

Prior to Fastlane, Gaspare has led complex and challenging IT, sales, and marketing initiatives for many industry-leading firms, such as SIRS Publishing, PRIMEDIA Consumer Magazine Group, Mad Mariner LLC, and ProQuest Information & Learning. Gaspare has also served as Vice President of Sales & Marketing and Chief Technology Officer at TSC Consulting, Inc., where he led the company’s marketing initiatives, including product management, product marketing, and demand creation.

Gaspare sits on the Board of the Marine Marketers of America.
He is the owner/moderator of some of the largest marine networks within LinkedIn with a personal social reach well over 125,000 and serves as a Flotilla Staff Officer - Maritime Domain Awareness (FSO-DA) in the United States Coast Guard Auxiliary.

Flotilla Staff Officer - Maritime Domain Awareness (FSO-DA)

Maritime Domain Awareness (MDA) is the effective understanding of activities, events, and threats in the maritime environment that could impact global safety, security, economic activity, or the environment. International efforts to share information to enhance awareness of the maritime environment are growing, and the Coast Guard is at the forefront of many of those efforts. They are based on formal multilateral structures, informal multilateral or regional organizations, or bilateral agreements for cooperation in specific areas.

The United States Coast Guard Auxiliary operates in:

• Safety and Security Patrols
• Search and Rescue
• Mass Causality or Disasters
• Pollution Response & Patrols
• Homeland Security
• Recreational Boating Safety
• Commercial Fishing and Vessel Exams
• Platforms for Boarding Parties
• Recruit for all service in the Coast Guard

In addition to the above, the U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary operates in any mission as directed by the Commandant of the U.S. Coast Guard or Secretary of Homeland Security.
