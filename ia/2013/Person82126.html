Sean Young's Summary

Thanks for visiting. As a seasoned Colorado Senior Mortgage Loan Officer,Mortgage Broker,Mortgage Banker,Loan Officer,Mortgage Lender with over 16 years of experience, I attribute my success to one simple quality: I take care of my clients!

Whether your goal is to purchase your dream home, refinance an existing loan, or consolidate debt, I can provide you with the expertise, professionalism, and customer service that will ensure that your transaction goes smoothly. I am committed to providing the highest quality of financial services and to finding you the lowest rates available. I strive to create a lasting relationship with each of my clients, and I am proud of and grateful for their repeat business and referrals.

My team and I believe in taking the time to get to know you. We work hard to understand your financial goals then cut through the confusion and present you with relevant information so you can make informed choices.

It is our job to be the experts. It is your expectation to receive great advice and service. We want you to be confident that anyone you refer to us will get the same great advice and service.

I have great relationships with experienced Financial Planners, Realtors, CPA's and Insurance Consultants. Please feel free to ask and I can get you in touch with one of my strategic partners.

We are direct with Ginnie Mae I and II, Fannie Mae & Freddie Mac and we are approved with many unique lenders to do loans that most would consider out of the box.

We offer full HARP loans DU Refi Plus and Open Access with no overlays and can transfer Mortgage Insurance without a 2nd thought.

We offer Diamond Lane Underwriting. Meet the Diamond Lane Checklist and your loan will be underwritten within 24 hours of me turning in the loan file to the processor. If it's early enough I will get it the same day.

I look forward to working with you. For more information, please email me at loanofficerseanyoung@gmail.com or call 303-521-7169.
