Laura Doucette's Summary

http://www.homesinhamiltonontario.com Helping families buy and sell their homes, houses, condo's, and townhomes in and around Hamilton, Dundas, Ancaster, Stoney Creek, Grimsby, Burlington, Waterdown, Flambourough and Caledonia (to name a few!).

First time buyers and people re-locating are some of my favourite people to work with and help educate. My buyer package comes with a great map of the area which I use to help point out all the up and coming areas of the city.

There are many seniors and baby boomers getting out of their homes and purchasing 1 floor detached bungalows, or condo townhouses, in adult lifestyle communities such as Garth Trails and Twenty Place, in Glanbrook. Sometimes, they need extra help with getting their home staged and de-cluttered and ready for sale.

For me, Real Estate is so much more than helping people buy and sell homes. It’s more about people fulfilling their dreams, whatever they may be. Sometimes it’s about finding a first home, or a dream home after they've been in their home for a while, or putting them in touch with a great Real Estate Agent in the town they are getting transferred to, and sometimes, it’s about assisting people in coming to the decision that it’s not the best time to buy or sell.

Laura Doucette's Experience

Real Estate - Sales Representative

Sutton Group Innovative Realty Inc., Brokerage

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Real Estate industry

August 1990 – Present (23 years 2 months) Hamilton Ontario

Since 1990, I have been helping families with all their Real Estate needs in Hamilton and all the surrounding areas including: Dundas, Ancaster, Stoney Creek, Grimsby, Burlington, Waterdown, Flamborough, Glanbrook (incl. Mount Hope and Binbrook), Caledonia, Hagersville. All these areas are located in the Golden Horseshoe, less than an hour away from Toronto, Niagara Falls and U.S boarder.

My goal is to help you learn as much as possible about all the areas of Hamilton, and for me to learn as much as possible about you so I can help you figure out the best place for you to move. Many families with young children love the Victorian style homes, both semi and fully detached in West Hamilton near Westdale, trendy Locke Street, or down by the bay front near Pier 4 and the Bayfront Park. There are many single people and couples buying in the downtown core in the Lofts and condo apartments, close to the Go Station for easy commute to Toronto. The Baby Boomers…and that would include me, are finding 1-floor homes throughout all the areas, and those looking for more adult senior lifestyle are finding just what they need in Glanbrook at Twenty Place and Garth Trails. Ancaster and Dundas have so much to offer with executive estates, ravine and conservation lots, exclusive condo living, golf courses, tree lined streets, and charming small town amenities.

Whatever your Real Estate needs are, if you are looking to buy a home, sell your house, condo or townhome, find fixer upper homes or an investment property, downsizing to a nice bungalow or one floor home, re-financing, helping your kids get their first mortgage and buy their first time homes…feel free to please get in touch.

With over 20 years of extensive real estate experience, and my passion for helping people with all their Real Estate needs, you’ll be happy you did!
