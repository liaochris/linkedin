Suzanne S. Felber, MIRM's Summary

Lifestylist® Suzanne Felber started The Home Idea Factory because she wanted to be a part of a design firm that was all about empowering individuals to discover their inner design divas.

After being involved in the home building industry for over 30 years, Felber understands what it takes to design a home or interior that feels like home.

Felber is a member of the Institute of Residential Marketing with the National Association of Home Builders and is a national instructor of their IRM III class. Her designs won a National Gold award for merchandising of a home under $150,000.

Specialties

Model Home Merchandising, social media, public relations, Show house design, product development, editorial styling of product, homes, interiors, and food.

Suzanne S. Felber, MIRM's Experience

Lifestylist®

Lifestylist® Brands

February 2002 – Present (11 years 10 months) Oak Cliff, TX

Lifestylist® Suzanne Felber is one of those lucky people who truly loves what she does.  Her work has been seen throughout the country in various publications, show houses and model homes. She been featured on the HGTV program “Dream Builders”, and her work has appeared in Home Magazine, This Old House and Home Depot's StyleIdeas magazines, and in JCPenney publications.  Felber’s fresh approach to model home merchandising has been featured in successfully merchandised model homes from coast to coast. Her popular websites and blogs include www.modelhomesthatsell.com and www.lifestylist.com When not on the road helping clients, Felber calls Dallas home and is busy renovating a 1941 printing loft into her studio and living space.

The Lifestylist® Brands was created to provide innovative products and solutions to today's consumers. By being a consumer favorite in the various social media platforms including You Tube, Pinterest, and Twitter we help to position our clients to receive consumer credibility.
