Chris Craig's Summary

Dynamic cross-functional senior leader skilled at driving market share gains and sustained top- and bottom-line growth in lean small- to mid-cap organizations. Expert at unlocking brand value.

Repeated success in fast-growth businesses as well as high-pressure turnaround and private equity situations; have also worked in family-owned businesses. Hands-on global marketer who has led teams on three continents across two companies.

Strong track record of innovation in new product development, creation of new brands and reinvigoration of established brands..
