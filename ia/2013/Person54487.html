Ian Spector's Summary

I enjoy finding useful and unusual ways to solve life's little problems by means of creative business development and creative applications of technologies. I love helping organizations answer the question, "How can we make this better?" In what little spare time I have, I've been known to develop groundbreaking business ideas and occasionally galvanize cultural phenomena (see below).

I am always looking for motivated and talented individuals to work with me on future projects, or just to talk to. Feel free to shoot a message over any time.

Specialty interests: User experience design and development, clever entrepreneurship, human factors, behavioral economics, web and print design/layout/editing, comedy writing, copywriting, solving problems, etc...
