Mark Robertson's Summary

Mark Robertson is founder of ReelSEO.com, a first-of-its-kind website for the online video marketing space offering both valuable and objective insight into news, tips, trends, and overall guidance for those wishing to leverage video marketing, social media, and search engine optimization.

Mark is a passionate and experienced search engine and internet marketing professional with almost 10 years of experience in the industry. Mark is a popular speaker, consultant and passionate thought-leader within the online video and search engine marketing industries and has had the privilege of speaking at several high-profile industry conferences including: Search Engine Strategies, PubCon, Video Commerce Summit, Streaming Media West, Online Video Platform Summit, Online Video Summit, SMX Advanced, and more...

Specialties

Web Video: Video Publishing, Video Marketing, Video Advertising, VSEO, Video Technology & Mobile Video

Internet Marketing: Organic & Universal Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Paid Search (PPC), Local Search, Social Media, Email Marketing, Internet Advertising.

Web Design & Development: HTML, CSS, Wordpress, PHP, streaming video.

Automotive, Real Estate, Retail/e-commerce, National, Brand, and Employment Verticals.
