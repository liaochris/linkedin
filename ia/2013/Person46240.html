Molly Friedenfeld's Summary

Molly Friedenfeld is the founder and president of Violet Wisdom LLC, and creator of the global radio show call Violet Wisdom Radio. She is a businesswoman, mentor, Teacher of Light, author, and radio show host.

Molly is dedicated to inspiring the hearts and minds of others to unite and honor the light within every soul, illuminate the way with light and inspiration, spread goodness across the earth, and let all souls know this TRUTH – they are never left to wander alone and dearly loved.

Molly’s develops projects, products, videos, radio shows, and programs as templates for others to follow to spread goodness, love, and light across the earth.

She also guides others how to unlock the wisdom of the heart, receive guidance from their Angels, and arrive at the understanding that there is no such thing as a coincidence; everything is divinely guided behind the scenes to provide endless mini-miracles and synchronicities as we travel throughout our days.

Molly lives in Minneapolis, Minnesota with her husband Peter in a blended family unit of joyful souls consisting of Mallory, Maddy, Will, Jake and granddaughter Hailey.

Molly Friedenfeld's Publications

  • The Book of Simple Human Truths by Molly Friedenfeld

    • She Writes Press
    • April 23, 2013
    Authors: Molly Friedenfeld

    The word wisdom means ancient knowledge. It’s the kind of knowledge you feel when you look into the eyes of an elephant or stop for a moment to marvel at the deep wrinkles on its skin, both of which contain the truths of each intentional step of their feet. Wisdom is knowledge that is rich, deep, and everlasting. It’s center-of-the-earth deep. It’s center-of-the-earth pure. And it is gained through the seeking of truth. This wisdom is constant; it is never distorted, reshaped, or pieced back together to fit molds specific to a culture or its people.

    Many of life’s treasures remain constant in the state of simplicity—a lesson that author Molly Friedenfeld learned when she began her journey in search of ancient wisdom, growing her spirit and moving from her head down to her heart. Love is simple. Truth is simple. Peace is simple. Joy is simple. It is from the benevolent heart space that one comes to the realization that we are all divinely connected—that we are all one. We thus realize we are here on earth to grow our souls, and we begin to look beyond ourselves. When we do so, we come to know this truth: what is for our highest good is also good for all. We then become difference-makers, and we inspire others to find unique ways to spread around this newfound love, truth, peace and joy—transforming souls far and wide!
