Michael DeYoung's Summary

We (myself and wife/business partner Lauri) are an adventure lifestyle and landscape commercial photography team.

My style of energetic, clean, and colorful imagery (often with lots of soothing space) comes from a combination of outdoor life and professional experiences. Meteorology and its effects on light, knowledge of many things outdoors, a little wilderness guiding, independent foreign travel, workshop teaching, art directing and production all contribute to what you see in my imagery. Our clients get 100% dedication and tireless creative energy from us regardless of the project size.

A long time former Alaska resident, I have photographed extensively for the Alaska tourism industry. Based in Taos, New Mexico and Anchorage, Alaska, we specialize in adventure, landscape, lifestyle, fitness, and travel photography for advertising, corporate and editorial clients. Our images are widely published in visitor guides, corporate websites, catalogs, annual reports, magazines, brochures, calendars, cards, and many other places.

Our world is the outdoors and we like it all: desert, mountain, forest and water. While adventuring in remote corners of the world is cool, so is exploring close to home and we do that regularly. Not all of our pursuits need to be an adrenaline rush or an expedition. We thoroughly enjoys day trips too. As long as it is human powered fitness, recreation or adventure sports and their related lifestyles and surrounding landscapes then it’s all good!

Bottom line is: if it’s outside, we’re in!

Specialties

location, adventure sports, outdoor fitness, active lifestyle, travel + tourism, environmental portraits, landscapes

Michael DeYoung's Experience

Photographer

Michael DeYoung Photography (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Photography industry

August 1992 – Present (21 years 3 months)

Over ten years assignment experience producing custom imagery for tourism and corporate clients. Select clients include: Agrium, Inc., Alaska Travel Industry Association (producing many images for the Alaska State Vacation Planner), South Carolina Parks, Recreation, and Tourism, Visit Anchorage, Taos Ski Valley Chamber of Commerce, Columbia Sportswear, National Park Service-Denali National Park. Image creation emphasizes muscle powered outdoor recreation and adventure, active lifestyle, heath, fitness, travel, and landscapes.

Stock image production with 20+ years experience orchestrating self assigned shoots and supplying multiple agencies that have a global distribution network of commercial and editorial imagery. Thousands of images published in virtually all sectors ranging from books, national magazines, calendars and card lines to corporate reports, catalogs, brochures, billboards, banners and websites.

Digital photography expert: Well versed in Photoshop, compositing and high volume post processing of RAW images. Other specialties: adventure and wilderness travel logistics, producing studio quality lighting in hard to access and hard to reach locations.

Experienced in self assigned and self directed productions as well as working with other creative professionals, talent, and producers to deliver clients visual message bringing a lifetime of adventure and outdoor experiences.

Sole photographer of 4 flyfishing books with award winning author and guide Cecilia “Pudge” Kleinkauf, of Women’s Flyfishing. Two titles were Benjamin Franklin Award recipients.

Organized, produced, and conducted weekend-long workshops focusing on creative outdoor photography in Alaska.

Pro-studies workshop instructor for Rocky Mountain School of Photography.
