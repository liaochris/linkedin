Gihan Perera's Summary

I work with business owners and leaders who know the Internet is important, but they don't know what to do about it or how to use it without wasting time. They don't want to turn into Internet marketers, but they do want to deliver key messages to the right people at the right time, and they want to leverage the power of the Internet to do that.

I'm an author, speaker, trainer and consultant. Since 1997, I've worked with leading thought leaders, change agents, business leaders and entrepreneurs, helping them reach more people and leverage their expertise, on and off the Internet.

What makes me different is that I practice what I preach! I've been using the Internet since 1987 - long before many people even knew the Internet existed, and long before it was used as a marketing tool. I know how to use it as a key communication tool, not just a marketing channel.

Think of me like the GPS navigation in your car. You tell me where you want to go, and I’ll help you plot out the best path for you, depending on your circumstances and requirements, and I can work with you side by side to help you get there.

In June 2012, "Forbes" magazine rated me the #5 social media influencer in the world in one of my areas of expertise (book publishing). That recognition was based not just on my number of followers (which is relatively low), but - importantly - on the relevance and resonance with my network.

If you'd like to learn how to use the Internet more effectively to deliver your key messages, e-mail me or phone me via my Web site.
