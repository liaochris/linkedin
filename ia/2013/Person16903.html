Brent Drew's Summary

Port15Utah, located in Cedar City, Utah, is truly a "convergence of commerce." With the exception of water vessels, every form of transportation is centrally located in Port15Utah. Union Pacific Railroad, Fiber Optics, the Cedar City Regional Airport and one days trucking to the West's largest markets are just a few of the advantages that make Port 15 Utah so vital to your business needs. The importance of the approximately 800 acre park cannot be understated when you realize Cedar City offers one of the last areas that offers connectivity and reasonable land prices.

Port15Utah is the West's preeminent business park for rail-served industries and other business groups seeking a central location. Your investment will be protected by quality restrictive covenants and building standards to keep all companies looking their best.
