Amy Parnell's Summary

Consumer web product designer, with extensive experience designing personalized, mass market, high traffic websites, including LinkedIn (Profile, Homepage, Content & Community products) and Yahoo! (Homepage, My Yahoo!, and Yahoo! Buzz).

I have worn many hats during my time at LinkedIn, including Lead Designer, Design Manager, and now Principal Designer. As a design lead and Principal, I provide product design strategy and direction, UED team leadership, and hands-on design work, most notably on the Profile redesign - and now on our Education initiative. As a manager, I led the design team responsible for redesigning Homepage, LinkedIn Today, the Influencer program, and various Content and Community solutions.

My strengths include early stage product ideation and vision - gathering user insights, rough product ideals and high level strategic inputs - to quickly arrive at tangible, user centric design solutions that provide direction for product development. I'm best utilized starting a project at its earliest phases of brainstorming and white boarding, and driving it to a pixel perfect, delightful and useful consumer product.

Previous Yahoo! work includes designing forward-looking consumer experiences across connected devices including TV, mobile, game consoles and the web. My background additionally includes mobile design, marketing communications, print design, video production, environmental/trade show booth design, broadcast media and 3D animation.
