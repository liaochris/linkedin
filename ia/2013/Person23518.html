Chris Marsh's Summary

Apparel Manufacturing has been a part of my family for 45 years. Our company, Hemingway Apparel has primarily been a sewing contractor for the Apparel industry for the majority of that time. However, in the last ten years our business model has changed significantly towards manufacturing. Currently we are manufacturing for a variety of Customers including the Department of Defense. For more information go to our website at http://hemingwayapparel.com
I am also a Bi-Vocational Pastor. Striving to excel in my relationship with Jesus, and embracing the fact that He has called me to be a part of His work, is a very humbling task. You can check out my blog at http://chrismarsh.us
