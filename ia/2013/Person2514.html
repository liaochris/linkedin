Ahmed Yearwood's Summary

Ahmed Yearwood is the sole owner and founder of Y INTERACT, a New York City-based design studio specializing in interactive development, graphic design, animation, video production, social media, and mobile/app development. Celebrating 11 years in business, Y INTERACT works with clients throughout the US, Europe and Asia on a range of media design projects from concept to completion to maintenance and expansion.

Specialties

Creative conceptualization, design and construction of websites and online ad campaigns, social media strategies and execution, brochures and presentation kits, invitations and cards (printed and animated), exhibition kiosks, invitations, animated sequences, video and photo shoots, and more.
