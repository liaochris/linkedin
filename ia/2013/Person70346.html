Judith Parker Harris-Bust Life Blocks expert's Summary

Expert on getting companies and their work forces to break through blocks as they learn to communicate, produce, and create to maximum potential. Former owner of a multi-million dollar advertising agency that pioneered brand advertising for national healthcare chains. Writer, Producer and Director of over 3,000 television commercials for clients like Sears & Roebuck and Shell Oil. Preventative Healthcare advocate and teacher due to recovery from the partial paralysis and sight loss of Multiple Sclerosis by dealing with the emotional and cognitive blocks in life. (Symptom-free since 1990) Creator of a 5-step process that moves clients from BLOCKED to BLOCKBUSTER (her latest book). Interested in reaching Associations and businesses experiencing times of transition as they redefine the health of their work environment, vision and relevancy on all levels. Feature film producer on a quest to bring blockbuster results to her clients.
