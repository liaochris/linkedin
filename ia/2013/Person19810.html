Carey Baker's Summary

Part-Time Pros (“PTP”) was created in March of 2008 by Tulsa natives Carey Dunkin Baker and Brett Baker. Carey saw an opportunity to tap into the talents of degreed professional women who wanted to continue to work while also making time for family. This concept was the spring board for starting Part-Time Pros.

PTP’s mission is to be a talent acquisition firm that specializes in recruiting part-time professionals (the “professionals”) and placing them with companies (the “clients”) who have part-time personnel needs. PTP is the only talent acquisition firm in Tulsa and Oklahoma recruiting individuals who are degree-educated or have equivalent work experience to work contract or part-time solely. PTP employs professionals from the following demographics: A. stay-at-home moms/dads desiring to contribute to her/his household income, B. the early retiree desiring to supplement retirement, C. the highly skilled independent contractors who only desire contract work and, D. the college student desiring to earn extra income while pursuing their degree. PTP recognizes the desire of these talented professionals wanting to continue careers on a part-time basis and gives the clients the benefit of receiving the professionals’ services at a much reduced cost over their competitors.

Part-Time Pros vision is to provide flexibility for employers and to be a resource to professionals seeking part-time employment. PTP defines “part-time” as the desire or availability to work for any or all of the following alternative work arrangements: alternative hours, work environment (telecommuting) or contract work for a specified period of time. PTP works to identify potential clients and continues to work with existing clients in order to identify their changing personnel needs.
