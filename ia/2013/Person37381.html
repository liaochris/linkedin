doveink, llc's Summary

Intuition™ Recovery Bra, designed by women for women is a revolutionary new device that is the first and only postoperative bra to address the top five postoperative concerns of women: heal quicker, with less pain, less scarring, less irritation and adjustable fit. The Intuition™ Recovery Bra was designed with a focus on cosmetic breast augmentation, reconstruction, lift, reduction and implant removal procedures.


The Intuition Recovery Bra™ features:
•Wire-free and non-latex lining that achieves optimal benefits with compression and support therapy.

•Reinforced, breathable two-way stretch pattern to add support and assist back muscles.

•Front closure zipper for ease of use.

•Non-chaffing and adjustable straps throughout for a fully customizable fit as swelling decreases.

•Oversized lower band that is positioned below the scars for comfort and function.

•100% Silicon scar management to reduce appearance of scarring.

•Front and back cool packs that can be put directly on skin to alleviate pain and discomfort. Self-regulated thermal control technology that is set at 57 degrees to prevent frostbite. Reusable. FDA approved.

•Nipple covers that self -adhere to the body to help to soothe sensitivity and nipple tenderness. 100% Medical grade silicon. Reusable.

•Foam pillow that provides maximum comfort between the breasts while sleeping. Reticulated Poly-urethane. 100 pores per inch.

•Attachable pouches to hold surgical drains, lining is Velcro® hook receptive.

Specialties

We are medical professionals who have experienced first-hand the recovery process after augmentation surgery. After talking to doctors, family and other women who shared similar experiences, we realized the need for a bra that truly accommodates a woman’s post-operative needs. We created the answer, the Intuition bra.
