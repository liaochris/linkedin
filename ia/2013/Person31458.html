David Argy's Summary

I'm a senior marketing professional with proven results in Product Management across numerous industries. My most significant accomplishments have come as a Product Manager where I take responsibility for analyzing the company and competitive situations, developing actionable product plans, then leading the execution of the product plan across the organization.

I'm passionate about driving results for the organization while maximizing the contributions of my team.

My personal goal is to build off the successes I've had both within organizations and as the owner of a business to gain a new position in marketing management to help a company drive growth and profitability.

My current company, TrainingOnlineNetwork.com provides high quality, online training services for adult learners; concentrating in markets that improve employee safety or enhance employment opportunities for our students.

My background covers over 20 years of experience in multiple areas of marketing, operations, and management for both large and small companies targeting both B2B and B2C markets.

Specialties

Product Management - Marketing Management - Developing Product Plans - Competitive Analysis - Marketing ROI - Financial Forecasting and Analysis - Business Planning - Driving change throughout organizations
