Julie Britt's Summary

I have 30 years of experience as an editor, copy editor, reporter and page designer at daily and weekly newspapers, national monthly business magazines and trade newsletters in the Carolinas, Virginia and Washington, D.C. I am also an adjunct English composition instructor at Northern Virginia Community College.

Areas I have covered include information technology, human resource management, transportation, automotives, insurance, financial planning, health, pharmaceuticals, federal and state government technology, local government and local businesses.

Outside the day job, I write fiction and essays. Five of my short stories have been published, and I'm working on a novel.

Specialties: I have hired, trained and managed staff and freelance writers, copy editors and production staffs in a deadline-driven environment.

In addition to a bachelor's degree in journalism and a master of fine arts in creative writing, I have completed courses and seminars in web design and development with HTML and Dreamweaver; print page design and desktop publishing; and human resource management. I have experience with Quark XPress and InDesign, as well as content management systems.
