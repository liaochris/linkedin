Mari PerezLION's Summary

My career as a teacher has been long and varied. After three years in the school district of Orange, NJ, my tenure was established. The Dept. of Defense accepted my (and my husband's) application to teach in the military schools overseas. The next five years brought me invaluable experience, knowledge, and travel from my positions in Morocco, Spain, and Trinidad. After that time, my homeland, the USA, seemed to call to me, and California became my new home. San Diego, my base, was where my teaching continued, along with raising a family of 2 sons. After retirement, teaching recycled into the business world with an the emphasis on wellness. Eleven years later, this passion is still continuing.

Specialties

Wellness home with an emphasis on quality rest and relaxation, whole-food nutrition, easy fitness, clean air, and pure water, especially applicable for baby boomers and seniors.
