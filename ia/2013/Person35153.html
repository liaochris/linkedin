Diana Cristea's Summary

Diana started early her career as a visual artist when her first drawings decorated the 100 years old mahogany table in her mother's living room. Nowadays she's not punished anymore for her creations, but encouraged to put on long hours so she can get through the long list of brands needing her attention. She specializes in quick, efficient and most of the time beautiful solutions to all kinds of design needs.

Specialties

identity, branding, packaging, web & app design, book design, advertising, art direction
