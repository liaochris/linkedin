Alina Barcia's Summary

My goal is to meet all of my client's travel needs. I would like to help them experience and create a lifetime of memories while delivering top notch service.

I have sailed on more than 35 cruises, and escorted groups of up to 400 passengers. I have assisted countless individuals in their cruise and travel needs and organized many types of groups from school and family reunions to church and weight loss groups.
