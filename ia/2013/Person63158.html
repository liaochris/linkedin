Jessica Parry Probst's Summary

Jessica has extensive advanced training in a variety of manual therapy techniques, as well as women's health pelvic floor training. Jessica graduated magna cum laude from Ithaca College's physical therapy program. She is an American Physical Therapy Association credentialed clinical instructor. Jessica is also a Specialist in Martial Arts Conditioning, certified by the International Sports Sciences Association.

Jessica has over 15 years of martial arts experience and has earned a 2nd degree Black Belt in Aikido and a Brown Belt in Dan Zan Ryu Jujitsu. She also has experience in Krav Maga, Kali, Capoeira and Iaido. She looks at the body as a whole and treats by addressing the underlying problems which interfere with optimal function. With Jessica's expertise, you'll be in top form sooner than you ever thought possible.

Specialties:
- Back and Neck Pain
- Women's Health (pelvic pain, incontinence and pregnancy/post-partum)
- Chronic Pain
- Rib, shoulder and thoracic spine dysfunction
- Hip and knee problems
- A wide variety of orthopedic conditions
- Treating Martial Artists and Fighters

Jessica Parry Probst's Experience

Martial Arts & Sports PT

2009 – Present (4 years) 1712 Eye Street, NW Suite 305, Washington, DC 20006

Martial Arts & Sports PT was founded in 2009 by Jessica Probst, PT, MSPT, SMAC to provide top quality physical therapy. She has a special focus on treating martial artists -- from the casual, recreational practitioner, to the elite competitor and advanced martial arts instructor. Jessica treats people of all ages, interests and activity levels.

Martial Arts & Sports PT is set up in a different fashion from many other physical therapy clinics—Jessica treats in a private treatment room and sees only one patient per hour. In therapy sessions with Jessica, you have her undivided attention as she does advanced manual treatments and creates a tailored program just for you.
