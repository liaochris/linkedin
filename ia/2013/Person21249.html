Chris McCall's Summary

Over 20 years of experience in strategy consulting, strategic planning, mergers and acquisitions and alliance management. Worked in a variety of industries, including technology, financial services, health care energy and aerospace. Current business focus is alliance and channel management in technology and corporate strategic planning.

Currently a Principal at Touch Worldwide, a global business consulting and creative agency. Also, is an Adjunct Professor at Seattle University's Albers School of Business, teaching an MBA-level course in managing acquisitions and alliances.

Specialties

alliance management, channel strategy, mergers and acquisitions, portfolio analysis, project management, executive communications
