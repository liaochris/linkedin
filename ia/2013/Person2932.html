Alexander Kofman, CFRW's Summary

Whether you're looking for a promotion, changing careers, or re-entering the job market, I can help develop your winning resume and market you to any potential employer. I prepare private/public sector, federal government, and military transition resumes for all industries.

As a Certified Federal Resume Writer (CFRW), I am experienced in writing and editing Executive Core Qualifications (ECQs) for government Senior Executive Service (SES) executives, as well as Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities (KSAs) and narrative statements that will receive maximum points to get to the interview stage of the federal hiring process.

In addition to resume preparation, I offer comprehensive career consultations, interview preparation, job search coaching, and strategy sessions. I am talented in writing SEO-driven LinkedIn profiles and scientific, academic, and international curriculum vitas (CVs), cover letters, executive/professional biographies, and anything else required in the job-seeking process.

I am recognized as an expert in preparing technical, engineering, and information technology resumes, highlighting all of your selling points and presenting your qualifications, skills, and accomplishments to enhance your value and give you a competitive advantage in the job market. The bottom line is that I prepare resumes, which get you the only thing that matters–an interview!

In fact, I have been nominated 3X for the prestigious TORI Awards in the categories of Best Military Conversion Resume, Best Executive Resume, and Best Technical Resume for 3 consecutive years (2010-2012). I am also a published contributor in best selling career books, including the latest Gallery of Best Cover Letters.

I have a strong technical background with expertise in Information Technology. I worked in the IT industry for 10 years before launching my successful resume writing career in 2000. I am a member in good standing with Career Directors International (CDI) and associate of ProfilingPro.
