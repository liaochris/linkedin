Bob Gockeler's Summary

I had always wanted to create a family-owned business in my home town, one that exemplifies old-fashioned values. My passion has always been creating and designing spaces both inside and outside the home. I, therefore, decided years ago to leave a senior level corporate position to open a business that I am passionate about, one that specializes in kitchen and baths.

People have always marveled at my sense of style and color. I have a natural ability for seeing "what could be." I've been involved in building and remodeling for over 20+ years and that experience gives me insight into how a space could be transformed, say, by moving a wall or adding windows. Of course, I am also comfortable relocating plumbing, electrical or gas lines. My vast experience in general contracting is coupled with a keen sense of design that is a unique blend, not often found in the world of contracting. I have designed hundreds of projects from simple remodels to complete additions.

I strive to be a local go-to place for people who want that small-town touch, who appreciate the type of personal attention that you can only get by dealing directly with the owner of a company. Blending design and creativity with my years of building and construction assures my clients the best possible solutions for their home remodeling projects. I have been able to take on the seemingly impossible and make dreams come true for my clients.

For years most of my kitchen renovations were only marveled at by clients, friends and family until a project was photographed for Better Homes and Gardens. I now have the best of both worlds with Kitchen Intuitions and KraftMaster Renovations, it has given me the opportunity to not only design, but remodel a project from start to finish.
