Kristi Trimmer's Summary

I have been a small business owner since I was 20 and have a good grasp on what it takes to run a business when you might be wearing all the hats. One of the things I enjoy doing the most is helping other small business owners succeed.

I am the Social Media Director for the National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO), a Chapter President for Women's Circle Network (WCN), and the voice behind over a dozen social media accounts.

I wanted a way to take my love of wine, blogging, and promoting small businesses... thus the launch of 52WinesWeeks.com. The first part of my wine blog tells the wine's story, the 2nd part features the sponsor. This blog was so successful, that each quarter a new blog will be born. Check out 52Beers52Weeks.com and the launch of 52Tacos52Weeks.com coming soon.

Kristi Trimmer's Experience

Lifestyle Blogger, Brand Advocate, WP Site Maven & Social Media Educator

Orange Dragonfly Media

June 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months) Phoenix, Arizona Area

I am a Lifestyle Blogger and Brand Ambassador for Road Runner Sports. I blog about health and fitness, tech and social, and everything in between.

I teach small businesses how to effectively use Social Media and show which platform is the best for their business. My marketing firm, Orange Dragonfly Media, also builds WordPress websites, designs graphics around a brand's core identity, and creates stunning print and web materials.

I write and edit content for articles, newsletters, web content, and blogs. I am also a technical editor for government contracts and am vastly experienced in the proposal process from the release of the RFP through the entire submittal process. Worked on Department of Defense proposals and documents.

Orange Dragonfly Media is a full service boutique marketing firm. We can help you from logo development to building a website to maintaining a solid presence and engaging with your clients through social media.
