Marcelo Somers' Summary

I live at the intersection of product design and business strategy. I'm passionate about reinventing the way that people interact with technology while reimagining the way the underlying business foundation works. I solve complex problems by understanding users and iterating on solutions to find the best fit.

In my current role as an User Experience Consulting at Credera, I partner with companies to help them solve their biggest challenges through how their technology works and looks.

I write Behind Companies, a site that collects and comments on the stories behind the world's most interesting companies.

I founded The Syndicate, a technology and design blog sponsorship network that delivers sponsor's messages to over 87,000 readers each week.

I speak about topics ranging from Economics to Career Development. I guest lecture for the Management Honors Program at UT Dallas and have been a featured speaker at the Big (D)esign Conference.

Specialties: Consulting, User Experience Design (UX), Interaction Design, Business Strategy, Information Architecture, Front End Development, Prototyping, Visual Design, Content Strategy, eCommerce Strategy, Advertising, Wireframing, Product Strategy, Copywriting, Blogging, Speaking

Tools: HTML5, CSS, Sass, Ruby on Rails, PHP, Photoshop, WordPress, Git, Axure, OmniGraffle

The User Experience practice takes pride in designing great experiences, not just websites - experiences that can be proven and measured. We partner with our clients to design for a purpose, not just flare. Most importantly - we solve problems through design.

Media Product Manager

Sabre Holdings

Privately Held; 5001-10,000 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

January 2010 – January 2012 (2 years 1 month)

I am responsible for growing our media portfolio and developing new advertising products to reach our 280,000 connected travel agents.

In 2011, I delivered Sabre Deals & Travel Extras (http://deals.agentstream.com/), a complete re-imaging of how Sabre approaches the business and technology of in-destination activities. We built the site using Ruby on Rails technology on top of an existing social community. I was responsible for developing requirements, user stories, defining the interaction models, and managing supplier relationships.

I also manage Sabre's email marketing portfolio for airlines, hotels, and others to communicate their message to 40,000+ travel agents and 500,000+ end-travelers.

I am also involved in giving back to the company through our Young Professionals Council & Bring It. I am a mentor to new undergrads in our New Grad Leadership Program. I also lead a team that hosts "The Big Pitch" which has given over 40 Sabre employees the opportunity to pitch ideas to a team of senior executives & other employees.
