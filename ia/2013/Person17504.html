Brian Kiplinger's Summary

My name is Brian Kiplinger and I currently live in Northern California. I've been an entrepreneur pretty much since the 2nd grade when I became the highest paid candy dealer at school. One thing my own experiences have taught me is that true success in any aspect of our lives is not dependent upon companies, opportunities, individuals, whether or not someone was mistreated as a kid, if they grew up on the wrong side of the tracks or any of the other millions of excuses that can be thought of, and we've all thought of them.

Success comes when opportunity meets preparation, and since there is an endless amount of opportunity out there, it stands to reason that preparation is one of the primary suspects in holding people back. And the preparation I'm talking about goes much deeper than the preparation needed to physically handle the day to day functions of our businesses. I'm talking about being prepared by first becoming the person who provides enough value to justify the attainment and retention of the quality of life we envision for ourselves and the people we love. Can some folks get lucky and become successful without having to go through any kind personal growth? Sure it happens. But when the bottom falls out and they aren't prepared, more times than not they end up right back where they started but worse because now they feel defeated, discouraged, hurt, mad and probably even depressed.

Whether you are a seasoned pro or brand new to entrepreneurship, I want to remind you of something that is very easy to forget. Most people look at success as a means to an end, and they call that end happiness. But I'm here to remind you that happiness should always be the place we begin. When we do that, success becomes instantaneous with absolutely no end in sight.
