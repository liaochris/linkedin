Christine Lehmann's Summary

I am an award-winning health care reporter whose articles have appeared in The Washington Post, The Dallas Morning News, and Psychiatric News. I have authored more than 600 published articles on a broad range of social, policy, and health care issues. My current interests are nutrition, integrative and alternative medicine. I will be a certified nutrition therapist in October 2013. My new company is called Nourishing Foundations and I will have a new Website and blog with that name shortly.

I obtained my master’s degree in public health and journalism from the University of Minnesota in 2007. I also taught magazine writing as an instructor in the School of Journalism and Mass Communication. Since returning to the Washington DC area in 2007, I have been a writing and editorial consultant for consumer advocacy organizations, federal health care agencies, and private corporations. I worked full-time for Westat, a consulting organization, as a publisher, writer, and editor of a Web site focused on health care innovations. I also used social media to engage our audience.

Specialties: nutrition, health and wellness, health care policy, services, research, and innovations, public health, mental health, and traumatic stress.
