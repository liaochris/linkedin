Janet Handschuh's Summary

Real Estate professional specializing in second home properties and residential homes. Also experienced with large acreage tracts, farms and commercial investment. History of working with REO companies in selling their foreclosure portfolio.

My company is also located near the Ft. Drum army base, home of the 10th Mountain Division. They are still in a major expansion of the base. We're used to working with VA loans and the time pressures our soldiers are under to secure housing as they transition to a new base. As a graduate of the first HOMES certification class (Housing our Military with Excellent Service) I'm look forward to continuing to meet the unique needs of our countrys' service families.

Northern New York is blessed to be surrounded by beautiful bodies of water. To our west is Lake Ontario, the Golden Crescent. To the NW & North is the majestic St. Lawrence River. Finally all around us are pristine, glacier made lakes called the Indian River Lake group including Black Lake, Butterfield, Millsite, Lake of the Woods, Sixberry and more. We have properties for all price ranges and life styles.

Specialties

2nd Home & Resort Property, Residential, Vacant Land, Military Relocation.
