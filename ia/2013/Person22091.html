Charles J. Collins, Jr. - Architects

April 1977 – Present (36 years 6 months) Medford, New Jersey

Charles J. Collins, Jr./Architects of Medford, New Jersey, is a comprehensive architectural services firm providing architecture design services to a broad range of clients in the New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware and Maryland metropolitan areas. Established in 1977, Charles J. Collins, Jr./Architects provides architecture services to residential, commercial, historic preservation, industrial, institutional and ecclesiastical clients.
