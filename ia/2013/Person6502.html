Andreas Forsland's Summary

Andreas currently leads the brand management function within the online services division of Citrix (GoToMeeting, Podio), helping to build a portfolio of world class leading brands as well as helping transform Citrix into a legendary brand and great place to work.

Prior to Citrix, the past 18 years of his career have been sharply focused on design, innovation, customer experience strategy and affecting change through design as a global design management leader across 3 continents; Europe, North America and South America.

He has contributed to and facilitated global innovation, brand and communication initiatives and holistic customer experience initiatives for Royal Philips Electronics, under the design leadership of Stefano Marzano, Chief Creative Director at Philips Design - the 500+ multinational design division of Royal Philips Electronics.

Prior to Philips, Andreas inspired and led similar programs for Porsche Cars, Progressive Insurance, Trek Bicycles, AT&T, Coca-Cola, United Nations, IBM, Bank of America, ING and others.

Leading large and small cross-functional and globally distributed teams leading to results that have disrupted markets, created new markets and positions, and generated new and incremental value and significant jumps in brand value.

Andreas' deep love of systemic design thinking, culture, vision and an insatiable drive to make a difference paired with a balanced business sense and broad perspective is what sets him apart from the rest. He understands, prescribes and executes the necessary things to transform good design organizations into great ones.
