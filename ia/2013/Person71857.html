  • Camp Bizgym - Crowdfunding Bootcamp for Kids

    Camp BizGym is a 1-week day camp for K-12 students to create a short video on a business idea of their own to gain funding donations through online “crowd-funding” platforms.

    Participants only need to come with an idea for a business they are interested in pursuing. Students will create a script, shoot video, edit and implement a viral media strategy to get their projects funded through “crowd-funding” sites such as Indiegogo.com or kickstarter.com.
