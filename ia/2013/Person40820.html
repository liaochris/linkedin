ELLEN GOYNE BRADLEY
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY



We believe that expertise in operations and communication is your key to greater success and an important asset for your organization. Whether you're looking for strategic planning, event management, or a full-service management package, The Bradley Group is prepared to meet your needs, your budget, and your objectives.

If your professional, trade, industry association or nonprofit organization is being confronted by unique challenges that are getting in the way of your growth and you are ready to take the steps needed to move to the next level of service - then we are the organziation for you.

By providing a high-quality, professional image to your board, clients, customers, members and the outside world, The Bradley Group becomes an addition to the staff of your organization. Our objective: to handle the relevant details of your organization, while ensuring efficiency and continued success.

Our administrative services include communications to members/clients, financial/accounting services, meeting facilitation and onsite management, travel and logistic support, minutes preparation and board transition support and more.
