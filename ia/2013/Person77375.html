Biography

With over 25 years of marketing management experience, Kym has completed successful projects for many types of industries, including: manufacturing, property management, non-profits, legal, and medical services. She began this company as a way for many small and new businesses to better afford quality marketing services. Kym forges long term relationships with her clients as their “offsite marketing coordinator.”

Kym spent eighteen years as the marketing manager for a local machine tool distributor. Her duties included developing marketing plans, creating marketing collateral pieces, database management, sales administration, and also wore the hat of an IT manager. She started her company in 2004 and the following year started degrees in Web Design and Graphic Communications with completions in 2008 and 2010.

Kym currently teaches classes through adult education programs on how to become a Virtual Assistant along with classes on Social Media to Market Your Business, How to use a Virtual Assistant for your Business, Free and Inexpensive Marketing Ideas to grow your Business and Networking 101.

Kym resides with her rescues cats Patches and Pixie and her rescue dogs Molly and Nelly. When she has free time, she enjoys a variety of crafts including jewelry making. She volunteers her marketing, web and graphic skills to non-profits in need. She designed and published an award winning newsletter for a local ABWA chapter (in addition to designing their first website, and established their social media presence). She’s also a member of the Macomb County Chamber of Commerce where she volunteers on the marketing, networking, and ambassador committees. She assists local non-profits when graphic or web design services are needed on a pro-bono basis.
