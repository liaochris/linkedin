Denis McSweeney's Summary

Denis has extensive marketing and business experience in both line and staff positions. During his career, Denis has consistently demonstrated his ability to contribute to organization success through his strong analytic and leadership skills, goal orientation, broad knowledge base, high level of energy, team focus, and adaptability to changing organization needs. Being a fast learner has been a particularly important asset.

Denis’ career has encompassed retail marketing (Maxwell House Coffee), direct marketing (Gevalia Kaffe, AOL, Vonage), new product consumer research and brand management (Kraft Foods’ first ready-to-drink iced cappuccino beverage), direct marketing channel management (direct mail and telemarketing), marketing analysis (e.g., direct marketing campaign profitability), and budget management (e.g., AOL’s $1 billion marketing budget).

In 2007, Denis decided to take a leave of absence from the business world. Based on his interests in staff development and teaching, Denis took several graduate courses in organization development, and became certified to teach Marketing and Business courses in public high schools.

While enjoying the experience of teaching, Denis decided to return to the business world -- it is more exciting to "do" marketing than to "teach about it"! AARP provided the opportunity to again work in direct marketing, focused on acquisition mail.

Specialties: Brand management, channel management, direct marketing, business analysis, market analysis, forecasting, budgeting, P&L management, consumer research, new product development, staff development, mentoring, cross-organization team leadership, consumer packaged goods, technology (internet service, VOIP)
