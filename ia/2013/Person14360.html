Bill Lang's Summary

Prior to founding The Human Performance Company and Bill Lang International I worked as an executive with KPMG, McKinsey & Company, Macquarie Bank and AXA. In addition I was the co-founder of FreeOnline and Sharinga Networks Inc in Silicon Valley.

Graduate of Harvard Business School and Faculty Member at University of Melbourne.

Serving clients operating in 50 countries with over 750,000 employees and over 25 million customers.

Learn more about the Best of Breed Solutions available by visiting www.human-performance.com.au

Specialties

The Human Performance Company is a global provider of Best of Breed solutions to help organisations and their people maximize workplace success. The company’s clients use the solutions to improve sales and service results to over 25 million consumer and small business customers. Flag ship products like Scores on the Board and Full Circle Feedback are available from Independent Licensed Consultants that operate in the Management Consulting, Training and Coaching industries.
