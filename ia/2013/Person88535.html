Matt Barackman's Summary

I was born with an insatiable curiosity and a drive to understand the world around me.

I began my career in advertising, where I had the privilege of collaborating with some incredible companies, including LinkedIn and Mozilla, on their brands.

I loved the fun and the challenge of that work, but I really wanted to build revolutionary products, not just brand them.

In pursuit of this goal, I attended Dev Bootcamp during the summer of 2013 and am now looking for web development job opportunities in the Cambridge / Boston area.

I'm passionate about education and public policy and my plan for the next few years is to hone my technical skills and ultimately found my own company or non-profit in one of those spaces.
