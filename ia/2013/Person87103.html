Mark Sievers' Summary

I am a Central Kentucky based business intermediary that is affiliated with the Business Brokers Network ( BBN), and Apex Realty which is a highly regarded commercial real estate firm in Lexington, Ky. My website is www.thesieverscompany.com. I also have a website that focuses on resorts and restaurants called www.resortsandrestaurantsforsale.com . My core activities include business brokerage, franchise resales, business valuations, business consulting and commercial real estate .
, I am also a Director with the business advisory firm Freedom Square Group which provides services in the areas of consulting, troubled/restructuring, financing , CFO Solutions and strategic sourcing .

Additionally, I am a freelance writer and am a member of the International Food Wine and Travel Writers Association . One publication I write for is Business Lexington and another is Prep magazine.

I am a member of the Kentucky Assocation for Economic Development and serve as a board member and President of the Kentucky Children's Health and Fitness Fund which is a Kentucky based non-profit organization .
