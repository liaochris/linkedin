Lisa Rossetti's Summary

Positive Lives offers coaching and training, and creative workshops to improve teamwork, leadership, wellbeing, engagement and communications. We also offer story-based service improvement projects and leadership transformation story coaching. We especially work in Health and Social Care settings, and in the Voluntary Sector.

The way we communicate and connect, and how we construct our identity, has always fascinated me. I grew up in colonial West Africa, and experienced cultural diversity at a very early age, as we travelled between Cornwall and Ghana. By the age of 3, I'd travelled thousands of miles. By the age of 7, I was trying to make sense of different rules, customs and communities. I was already aware of Big Questions about identity and freedom. Perhaps that's why my coaching and story work is often about Identity, Transitions and Connection. I understand what it means to be a New Kid on the Block, needing to find out what the rules are - and importantly, where is my place here, and what can contribute?

I've been involved with the Voluntary Sector for nearly 40 years, working cross-culturally. It's an environment I understand well. As a volunteer, I have travelled to India, Nepal, USA, Europe, and Australia. I now volunteer my writing skills to an international charity. I also coach within Third Sector and social enterprise.

I spent nearly 15 years working in the National Health Service as a training facilitator in a local Mental Health Trust. I was made redundant in 2008, giving me the opportunity to work within Health & Social Care settings, but from the "outside".

I am a member of Lapidus, a charity which promotes the creative use of words for therapeutic & Well-being purposes and has established professional guidelines and ethics for this work.

I am the Founder of The Story Cafe (Stories for Well-being), which provides a reflective environment to encourage meaningful conversations, listening & communication skills and connection with others.

Specialties

Service improvement story projects,Team building, Applied Storytelling in Health & Social Care, Stories for Well-Being at Work. Leader Transformation Stories coaching.
Coaching & Creative Coaching Supervision.
