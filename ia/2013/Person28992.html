Damon Crump's Summary

Jackalope Entertainment and Damon Crump have been providing high-end film and video production services out of Waco & Dallas, Texas for 26 years now. Damon is the head guy, and works as a writer, producer, director, director of photography and editor among other positions. Damon specializes in bringing “Hollywood-Style” production quality to each production while not spending the Hollywood dollars. His goal is to combine high-end production value with a touch of creativity that enables businesses to communicate their message to their audience efficiently and effectively.

Damon has worked on over a dozen movies and short films in his career, plus numerous TV and reality shows, commercials and corporate videos.

Career Goal: To direct a James Bond or Star Wars movie.
