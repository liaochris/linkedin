Jessi Poor's Summary

Upon completion of my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration concentrating in Information Technology Management along side multiple internships, I returned to Boeing as a participant of the IT Career Foundations Program (ITCFP). The ITCFP allows for 4 consecutive 6-month positions in order to gain experience in multiple areas of IT while developing leadership skills. See below for descriptions of my experiences while in the program. I have since graduated from the ITCFP and continued at Boeing as a programmer analyst.

During my first year with Boeing, I took additional classes in Design Communication and User Experience through UCLA until I was eligible to return for graduate school.

Now in my third year with Boeing, I am enrolled in the Master of Science in Information Systems program through Northwestern University with a dual concentration in Database & Internet Technologies as well as Software Project Management & Development. It is my goal to complete this degree in March of 2014.
