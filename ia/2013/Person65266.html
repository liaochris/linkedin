Joe Dunn's Summary

Software executive at Macromedia from 1990 to 2000, working on everything from rebuilding the engineering team in the early 90's to launching Shockwave.com in 1999. Engineering VP as the company went public. Heavily involved in driving product and company strategy.

Previously: engineering and engineering management at Frame Technology and (way back) Acorn Computers.

Have managed, built, positioned, and launched everything from an entertainment website (Shockwave.com) to Macromedia Director on Windows to a tiny local area fileserver.

Specialties

Vision, management and motivation. I love motivating small teams to great things. I love taking the seeds of new ideas and seeing them grow into products and then businesses. I love seeing people succeed.
