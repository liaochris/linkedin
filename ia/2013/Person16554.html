Brandon Weiss' Summary

I am a general contractor with a focus on sustainable and green building products and methods. I like to not only consider myself a quality builder, but also a building scientist by using the whole house systems approach to building.

I pay very close attention to building healthy, energy efficient, sustainable, durable, and low maintenance homes. We make sure we build to a level beyond code so that our clients homes will be at the highest level of quality for many years, are built to last hundreds of years, and do not become obsolete when building codes get more stringent. We also pay close attention to educating out customers on how to operate their green / high performance home.

We also pride ourselves on being able to offer these high quality, green homes in all price levels and budgets. We do not focus on a certain price tier of home, our only focus is building high quality, healthy, efficient, sustainable homes.

Specialties

Green Building, Remodeling, & Construction, Healthy Home Building, Energy Efficient Homes, Passive House Certified Homes, LEED for Homes, NAHB Green Building Standard Homes

Brandon Weiss' Experience

Owner

Weiss Building & Development LLC

May 2005 – Present (8 years 8 months) Greater Chicago Area

Weiss Building & Development is a Chicagoland home builder that focuses on healthy, energy efficient, durable, low maintenance, sustainable homes. Weiss Building & Development is dedicated to giving each customer a friendly, stress free experience throughout the duration of the building process. It is the constant pursuit of perfection for each and every home that puts Weiss Building & Development on top.

The building business is in a constant state of change. It is that change that keeps Weiss B & D on top of a strict continuing education plan. We have earned the designations of Graduate Master Builder (GMB) & Certified Graduate Builder (CGB) from the National Association of Home Builders(NAHB) through this education plan.

Weiss Building & Development is truly passionate about the green building movement. We are one of the few builders that have multiple green building accreditations. Weiss Building & Development is not only a LEED AP (Leader in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional) through the US Green Building Council but is also a MCGP (Master Certified Green Professional) through the NAHB. Brandon is actually 1 of 24 MCGP's in the country and the only in Illinois.

Through studies and research, the sustainability of a green home is something that resonates with every homeowner, regardless of their environmental beliefs. Owner, Brandon Weiss, view's himself as not only a builder, but a building scientist. Building science means everything that goes into the health and longevity of a home, and green building is the forefront of Building Science. We are BPI (Building Performance Analyst & Envelope Professionals, which is a building science certification that licenses us to do home audits. We are also a Illinois Home Performance contractor.

We also do remodeling, additions, wine cellars, home theatres, basements, kitchens, and bathrooms.

President

Sustain-A-Building LLC

April 2005 – Present (8 years 9 months)

Sustain-A-Building LLC is a contracting firm that does roofing, siding, window replacements, solar distribution & installation, wind turbines, weatherization, green retrofitting, green consulting, green remodeling, LED retrofitting, skylights, sun tunnels, and mold remediation. We look at these jobs differently than the traditional contractor focusing on quality and longevity.

We do these activties with a focus on making your home or building sustainable, durable, long lasting, healthy, energy efficient, and green. This leads to a longer lasting, more valuable, healthier, virtually maintenace free, and sustainable building.

We are Graduate Master Builders (GMB- National Assoc. of Home Builders), Certfied Graduate Builders (CGB- National Association of Home Builders), LEED AP (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional - US Green Building Council), and Certfied Green Professionals (CGP- National Association of Home Builders.

Co-owner

Sustain-A-Building Belize

January 2012 – Present (2 years) Sanctuary Belize, Stann Creek District Belize

This company was founded to build high performance, energy efficient homes in the Sanctuary Belize development in Belize.

Sanctuary Belize is the only development in the world that contains 5 different Eco Systems. The goal of the development is to preserve these ecosystems which is why out of the 14,000 acres owned by the developer, more than 10,000 is set aside for wildlife preserve and will remain undeveloped. The homes in the development are to be built to a sustainable level, which is why we were invited to become a preferred builder here.

We are building healthy, energy efficient, durable, green homes. Energy is expensive in Belize, so a second home should be as efficient as possible so you are not paying unnecessarily high utility bills. Also, homes in this region must be built to building science standards to have any chance of longevity due to all the sources of moisture that exist.
