Candy Lesher's Summary

- Veteran culinary and media professional specializing in public speaking, television and writing.
- 6 years as Editor promoted to Editor-in-Chief of a food-focused magazine, garnered extensive experience in producing and coordinating editorial material.
- 3 years as Executive Producer and Host of a food and lifestyle television show, handling all aspects of coordinating and promoting a shot-all-on-location production. Show won two Telly Awards.
- 11 years as culinary personality for local NBC affiliate's live morning and noon shows.
- 5 years as chef and owner of a restaurant and brewpub in historic mining town.
- 5 years as chef and cooking instructor.
- 2 years as owner and chef of a made-to-order bakery.

Specialties

television appearances, public speaker, food and travel writer, culinary consultant, cooking instructor, chef

Design Representative

SunWest Appliance Distributing

January 2009 – February 2012 (3 years 2 months)

Design representative for SunWest Appliance Distributing (distributor of Viking Range appliances throughout the Southwest). My culinary background (chef, culinary instructor and food writer) gives me a broad spectrum of experience with which to assist those trade industries.
