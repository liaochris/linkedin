A. Ron Evangelista's Summary

Las Vegas Realtor both from a buyers perspective and sellers listing. I've been selling homes in Las Vegas since 2003 and also spent 5 years in commercial real estate in Albuquerque, NM. I have the designation SFR (Short Sale & Foreclosure Specialist) and have been a "short sale" focused Broker for several years. HBM2, (Home Buyers Marketing II) works closely with federal agencies like Fannie Mae to help sponsor "Short Sale" & REO (Bank owned) listings. Some of the Las Vegas zip codes that we focus on stretch from 89131-Northwest Las Vegas-around the west side of Las Vegas to 89141 in the southwest areas of Las Vegas homes.

I consult on a regular basis with Gerson Lehrman group, a Think Tank, in the Real Estate vertical and utilize my Executive MBA on a regular basis for REO & Short Sale business. Many investment bankers want to get an insiders view of what is happening in the Las Vegas Real Estate market, so they engage a GLG Broker-I'm one of only two in Las Vegas-and they book consultation with us. This also gives me a different perspective from an investors side.

Our talents include customizing programs for investors and first time home buyers to meet their specific real estate needs.

Specialties

We've done some of our best work with investors who have bought multiple properties on a hold, rent & watch the future basis. We also have a robust Las Vegas marketing program.
