Harro Verton's Summary

I'm a seasoned ICT professional with more than 20 years of exceptionally broad and thorough technical knowledge and experience, gained while working for companies like General Electric, Getronics and Cap Gemini.

I work as a management consultant, architect, programme- or projectmanager in the area of networking and security, datacenter and office automation environments.

Exite's clients are among the largest organisations in both the Netherlands as in Europe, including the Dutch Flower Auction, Cap Gemini, EDS, Telindus - Belgacom ICT, Alcatel and the Flemish Government.

Specialties: By day, a Project- and Programme Manager and Infrastructure Architect in the Networking and Telecomms arena. By night, a driven PHP developer, with a nack for Open Source.
