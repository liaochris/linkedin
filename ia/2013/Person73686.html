Keith Moore's Summary

Proven Executive, Marketer, Lead Generation pioneer, Entrepreneur and innovative business scaler with over 18 years of Internet product development and marketing experience.

Prior to joining CoverHound, Keith was the President & COO of AcademixDirect, Inc. that had profitably grown over 120% during his tenure and was frequently recognized as an Education Industry leader after Keith joined the company. Before AcademixDirect, Keith was CMO of iContact, where he significantly increased the company’s valuation and SMB customer growth, which led to the company raising a $40 Million Series B (from JMI Equity) in less than a year, which fueled its acquisition for $169 million by Vocus (NASDAQ: VOCS). Marketing leadership efforts at iContact included expansion of the internal Marketing and Communications teams, bringing most of the mission critical direct response marketing efforts in-house, extensive customer surveys which led to successful retention and targeting efforts, launching newly developed iContactPlus branded enterprise service offerings, and rebranding iContact to reflect the company’s new position as a highly recognized resource for email and social media marketing for small to medium businesses. The Web Marketing Association recognized the re-launch of the new iContact web site for outstanding achievement in Web Design & Development with a 2010 WMA Award.

Prior to joining iContact, Keith served as General Manager and as SVP at LendingTree, LLC. as part of IAC, and then with the IAC spinoff Tree.com, where he owned multiple business unit P&L’s, as well as managing the LendingTree Exchange.
