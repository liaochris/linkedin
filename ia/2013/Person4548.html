Alicyn Packard's Summary

She is Alicyn – a sun lit voice-over artist, actress and musician illuminating airwaves & screens, scripts & stages daily. She can be heard daily starring in the hit series "Poppy Cat" on NBC & Sprout as the cute-to-the-max bunny Alma. Alicyn’s silver lining sound can also be heard as Miss Sunshine, Miss Whoops, and Miss Naughty in Cartoon Network’s Emmy-nominated animated program, “The Mr. Men Show” as the singing voice of the Little Miss Sunshine Toy and as Zepher in Disney’s “Pixie Hollow Games.” Other series credits include “Olivia” currently airing on Nickelodeon, and starring roles in the animated feature “Chop Kick Panda,” and the animated short “An Okay Place to Eat.”  

Alicyn has also gained the attention of legions of video game enthusiasts through her as the Female Blood Elf in the international smash hit, World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade, and other games such as Prince of Persia: Warrior Within, Eat Lead: The Return of Matt Hazard, Star Ocean and Vandal Hearts.

On screen, she has starred in the award-winning indie comedy feature “Alondra Smiles,” the psychological thriller, “World Full of Nothing,” as well as originating the role of Jenn in the world-premiere of cosmic rock musical “Earth Sucks,” which the Los Angeles Times called, "daffy, smart & energized." Variety called Alicyn’s duet in “Earth Sucks” a "highlight of the show."


Her numerous promo & commercial credits include Disney, Nick Jr., Vongo, Starz, WB, Sara Lee, HealthNet, and Taco Bell to name a few. 
 

As a stand-up comic, Alicyn’s unique brand of sweet and sassy has graced the stages of The World Famous Improv, The Comedy Store, The Ha Ha Cafe, Room 5, among others. Her comedic songwriting skills are on full display as she incorporates her witty and distinctively catchy, pop-infused ukulele ditties into her stand-up routine.

In her free time, Alicyn produces the podcast “Inside Alicyn’s Wonderland,” which can be heard at www.alicynswonderland.com.

Specialties

Acting, singing, improv comedy, character development, voice & articulation.
