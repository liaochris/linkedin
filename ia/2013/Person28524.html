Cynthia Bullock's Summary

I am a Administrative Support Consultant who works with great executives by providing them with outstanding administrative and sometimes personal support on a virtual basis. My purpose is to clear the way for my clients to be able to once again focus on the genius work. I eliminate the frenetic activities and pave the way for focus and serenity. Working with me gives my clients more hours in their day and a chance to finally be able to be about the business of running their businesses.

Because I am able to handle all of those tasks for business owners that, while important, do not have to be personally handled by them, they are now allowed to revisit the feeling that bought them to become a business owner in the first place. They can take vacations, spend more time with family and friends, and live a life of joy secure in the fact that business is still being taken care of.

Some of the services I provide my clients consist of contact, email, calendar, and appointment management, correspondence, internet research, event planning, human resources, office management, e-newsletter distribution(Constant Contact, Mail Chimp, and 1Shopping Cart), auto responders, Wordpress website management, and customer service. In addition, as a certified social media specialist I am able to provide a host of social media services from setting up profiles to analytics.

My relationship with my clients is framed around long term collaborative partnering. This framework offers each of us the chance to get to know each other well and thereby allows me the opportunity to support and assist my clients better.

Specialties

High level administrative support, travel arrangements, project management, calendar and customer relationship management, event planning, organization, expense reporting, contact management, auto responders, 1Shopping Cart, Mail Chimp, Constant Contact, Wordpress website management, technology, collaboration, internet research.
