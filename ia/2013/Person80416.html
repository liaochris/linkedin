Leslie Samet's Summary

I am a Human Resources consultant with over 25 years’ experience and the highest level of professional credentials. I have been involved in the healthcare industry for most of my life. While working at the University of Florida, I directly supported three medical researchers and two clinical doctors, and worked at Shands Hospital at the University of Florida for several years in Human Resources supporting various departments.

My sweet spot is working with small- to mid-sized firms to protect their financial and human assets through the delivery of expert HR Compliance services. I believe HR Compliance should be integrated into business strategy for increased ROI and efficiency while mitigating risk and lowering the cost and frequency of asset replacement.

I work closely with clients to identify and deliver creative solutions for a sound HR Compliance strategy.
