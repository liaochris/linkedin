Ken Bryant (LION for AML Professionals Only)'s Summary

Mr. Bryant has more than twenty five years senior management experience both in the United States and Overseas as a money laundering investigator, compliance officer/money laundering reporting officer, enforcement regulator and a counter terrorism and counter narcotics consultant. He has been awarded a Master of Science degree in Criminal Justice, summa cum laude, and has held nine board certifications including two fellowships covering the areas of risk management, financial due diligence, financial operations, regulatory compliance, and Anti-Money Laundering (AML)/Combating the Financing of Terrorism (CFT).
