Jimmy Smutek's Summary

Nine years of professional experience in graphic and web design for clients ranging from non-profits to large corporate clients to small business.

Primary area of professional interest lies in user experience design, information architecture, and content strategy. Firm believer in the 'start with paper' approach.

Working knowledge of HTML and CSS with a focus on design and implementation of dynamic websites on the WordPress and Drupal platforms.

Knowledge of SASS & LESS.

Highly proficient in the use of Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

Working knowledge of InDesign, Lightroom, Cinema 4D, Final Cut X and other industry standard software packages.

Fundamental skills in photography and introductory skills in DSLR Video. Familiarity with Nikon systems.`
