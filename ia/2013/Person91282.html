Melissa Cliett Levesque's Summary

Melissa Cliett Levesque, President

With a 14 year track record as a planned giving officer, financial services executive, and senior nonprofit leader, Melissa Levesque has dedicated her professional career to assisting clients maximize their potential and fully realize their philanthropic objectives. As a vice president in the Endowment and Foundations division of SunTrust Bank, Ms. Levesque managed a multi-billion portfolio of the bank’s planned giving, nonprofit, and private foundation clients. In the nonprofit sector, she has held senior positions focusing on professional advisor outreach, donor-advised account management, and grantmaker education with the Council on Foundations, DonorsTrust, and Donors Capital Fund.

Because of her knowledge of the nonprofit sector and emerging issues in philanthropy, Ms. Levesque has been a speaker at numerous conferences, including those sponsored by the Council on Foundations, Philanthropy Roundtable, National Committee on Planned Giving, Association of Fundraising Professionals, National Conference of Bar Foundations, and local estate planning councils. Additionally, she has been interviewed or quoted on philanthropic issues by the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Bloomberg Wealth Manager, Worth, Registered Rep magazine and other national media outlets.

Ms. Levesque received her Bachelor of Arts in History from Queens University of Charlotte and her Juris Doctor and Masters degrees from the University of Georgia. She is a Guest Lecturer and Presenter at Georgia State University and Emory University, as well as President of the Savannah Area Planned Giving Council. Ms. Levesque maintains leadership roles with the Savannah Ocean Exchange, Savannah Area Planned Giving Council, Georgia Center for Nonprofit's Coastal Nonprofit Professionals Network, and Queens University of Charlotte.
