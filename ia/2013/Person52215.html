Heather John's Summary

Heather focuses her practice in the areas of venture capital and private equity financings, mergers and acquisitions, fund formation, restructurings, joint venture transactions, debt transactions, and general corporate and securities matters.

Heather is an experienced entrepreneur and an active member of New York's startup community. She regularly counsels emerging companies on the full range of their activities through all stages of growth, including formation, day-to-day business operations, fund-raising and exit events.

She is proficient in French and Spanish and has lived in the USA, Belgium, Argentina, Japan, Ireland and Canada.
