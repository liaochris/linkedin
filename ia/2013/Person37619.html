Dr. Cara Iovino's Summary

Dr. Cara has a doctorate in Chiropractic from Life University and a B.S. in Life Sciences from the University of California at Los Angeles. She has studied nutrition and traditional health care from many cultures and has over 20 years of experience in her field. Dr. Cara has worked with holistic and medical specialists and is a leading authority on natural approaches to wellness. She is a highly sought after speaker in both corporate and community venues. Her favorite successes as a Chiropractor come from helping people with various health problems get their life back; many of whom had suffered for a long time, and had nearly given up hope.

Specialties

Dr. Cara specializes in weight management, injuries from accidents, pain and chronic health problems, spinal issues, headaches, and women’s health issues. She has advanced training in the natural health care and Chiropractic care of athletes, children, and pregnant women.
