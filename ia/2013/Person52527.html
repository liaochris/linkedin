Helene Mazur, MBA, CFP®, PCC's Summary

During the past 13 years as a business coach and team facilitator I have worked creatively with hundreds of professionals in a wide variety of organizations helping them to focus their goals, build on their strengths, and take action to achieve new levels of success.

My coaching and strategic planning practice is built on a foundation of 20 years of leadership experience at Electronic Data Systems, Dean Witter Reynolds, Bankers Trust Company and Merrill Lynch - coupled with an MBA, certification as a financial planner, and extensive coach and facilitator training and certification.

Behind the scenes I have supported professionals, business owners, corporate and non-profit leaders resulting in new perspectives, focused resources, renewed energy, regained confidence, and meaningful initiatives that have enabled them to flourish.

I look forward to the opportunity to help you and/or your team to grow in fun, productive, and positive ways.   If you are ready to take on the future - I welcome your inquiry.
