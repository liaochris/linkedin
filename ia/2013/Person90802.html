Michelle Howie's Summary

Knowledgeable and over 12 years experience in Employability Support, Careers Advice/Planning, Recruitment and Information, Advice and Guidance support for business start up.

Educated to Degree level information, advice & guidance & hold a certificate in Recruitment & Employment from the REC, as well as public liability & professional indemnity insurance.

We will offer open, honest and realistic advice and guidance to maximise your potential, through Career Connect I have helped hundreds of people in to work or through a career change, through effective and motivational coaching methods.

Resourcing solutions, can be used in the form of advice and guidance, helping you set up the right interview process with adverts that will attract the right candidates, or through to a full resourcing service upon request.

Full resourcing services will be agreed at a flat rate of 10% of the starting salary offered, only if we find a candidate for you and they accept the offer. Career Connect does not cold call as this is not our core business, this service is purely carried out upon request, candidates will only be allowed to be screened and registered if applying for a specific vacancy we have been asked to fill.

Private Consultancy services are also available, in the form of delivering employment skills workshops, with partner organisations, community groups and charities, and can incorporate sector specific information in to the workshops.

Experienced in providing a duty of care to employees of businesses that are going through a redundancy situation, helping their employees to prepare for the job market.

Managing Director of The Meet & Mingle Business networking group in Bicester and Banbury. My goal is to support all businesses to network informally without the need for annual fees, presentations or having to bring referrals. Good old fashioned mingling, chatting and swapping of business cards over a coffee!

ALL WELCOME regardless of gender or business type!
