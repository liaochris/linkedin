Aimee Holland BA Hons CMgr MCMI's Summary

Let me start by saying “Sorry!” I have most probably interrupted your tea / children’s bath-time / favorite program at some point. I was a ‘cold caller’! Hideous though this confession is I hope you will forgive me this trespass and allow yourself to discover what I have done since.

Direct sales isn't for everyone, but sales are the foundation of a business and I am proud that I experienced the tricks of the trade, even if in later years I have found the need to finesse them! There were some really valuable lessons I learnt from retail and cold calling, mostly it’s all about the numbers!

I learnt very quickly that you needed different people for different jobs, we are just not all wired the same. My skills are in analysing and researching, problem solving if you will. I love numbers, making things work slicker and more profitably and understanding how business tick. So if you think we could share ideas, collaborate on your business or someone else’s feel free to get in touch.

Specialties: Numbers!
Think targets, conversion rates, return on investment, profitability and demographics.

Planning!
Preparing for investment, business planning, strategic goals, marketing strategies.

Systems!
Making it faster, smarter and accessible, making it work better for you.
