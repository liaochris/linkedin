John Hutchison's Summary

Specializing in custom residential, historic preservation, adaptive re-use and small scale commercial architecture, John Hutchison Architect, LLC is an award winning, design-focussed practice with projects located in NJ, PA, DE and MD.

Inspired by the elegant simplicity of the historic architecture of my childhood on the Eastern Shore of Maryland, my designs have won awards from both local Historical Societies, and the American Institute of Architects. My projects have been praised for “their harmony of bright, open volumes with simple, clean lines, blended with fine details based on historic building practices”. I encourage a team approach with my clients, and believe the ingredients of good architecture are 1/3 Architect, 1/3 Builder, and 1/3 Client.
