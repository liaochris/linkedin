Mark Koberlein's Summary

I am an independent application developer with over 12 years of experience building web and mobile products for companies in the Lehigh Valley, Philadelphia, and New York.

I consult with companies through my company Parker West, LLC. Parker West is an interactive product development company specializing in digital publishing and the development of web and mobile products.

I am a co-founder and managing partner of the Lehigh Valley Tech which is a grassroots organization promoting entrepreneurship & technology in Lehigh Valley. We organize monthly meetings and larger events like Hackathons and the Startup Weekend Lehigh Valley.

I am also an Adjunct Professor at Northampton Community College where I teach courses in web and mobile development, computer graphics, and 3D animation.
