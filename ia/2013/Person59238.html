Jason Herbert's Summary

The personal injury law firm of Krentsel & Guzman LLP, represents clients who have been injured in accidents or whose loved ones have suffered an injury or death as a result of negligence or medical malpractice.

Our Manhattan office is home to a highly skilled team of attorneys who work tirelessly to ensure victims are justly compensated. If you have been injured, rest assured that we will do our very best to make sure you and your family are made whole again.

We offer free initial consultations to discuss the facts of your case. Please contact our office to schedule a no-obligation conversation with a lawyer who can answer your questions and offer you sound legal advice about your options in seeking maximum financial recovery after a serious accident, severe personal injury or wrongful death of a loved one.

At our firm our focus is on you, our client. We limit the number of cases we handle so that we can provide each case with the attention it deserves.

After your car accident, construction site accident, slip-and-fall, defective product or medical malpractice injury, our staff of attorneys, paralegals and assistants will work hard to obtain maximum recovery for you. Quite simply we will not be satisfied until you are satisfied. We will prepare your case to obtain maximum recovery whether through settlement or trial.

Feel free to contact me with any legal questions.
