Tyler Esselstrom's Summary

I lead the lighting team on various feature projects as well as assisted with set construction, surfacing, and texture work. I am able to take shots from previews to completion, including Nuke compositing. I developed Python tools for the lighting team, as well as the behind-the-scenes pipeline automation.

I have solid production experience with VRay, Mental Ray, Arnold, and real-time game engines, including Unreal Engine and CryENGINE.
