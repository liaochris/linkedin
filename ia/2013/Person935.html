Daniel O'Brien's Summary

I founded OPEUS in 1999 to provide web-based software to enhance teaching, learning and leadership. I work closely with educators at all levels, listening carefully to their needs and understanding their context. This insight allows me and the team at OPEUS to design effective solutions which blend simplicity and ease-of-use with power and functionality.

I am a down-to-earth realist, with a passion for new technology and its effective use in education.

As the creator of one of the first electronic portfolio systems in 1999 I pioneered the pedagogical use of internet publishing - a trend which has continued over the years as I've created virtual learning environments, curriculum mapping tools, class-blogging systems, online resource directories and web 2.0 teaching tools. All of these have been rolled out in hundreds of schools, colleges and universities across the world.

As a "hands-on" Director at OPEUS - I oversee a team of education technologists in the start-to-finish process of product design, development and delivery. I collaborate closely with a number of key educational professionals who contribute to the “outer team” involved with OPEUS.
