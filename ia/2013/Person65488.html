Joelle Caputa's Summary

My campaigns get their attention – and keep it. I specialize in creating and promoting compelling messages and have gained worldwide coverage for my clients in the entertainment, green / solar energy, non-profit (animal rescue), healthcare and beauty sectors.

From securing music placement on AOL Radio, MTV (Jersey Shore, Real World), NBC and Showtime to interviews with Forbes and Wall Street Journal, my clips are decorated and diverse.

Throughout my career, I’ve negotiated partnerships between companies, planned and promoted events, managed social media campaigns, designed promotional materials and media kits, written for leading magazines and websites, conducted casting for reality TV shows, hosted/edited Internet videos, produced video press kits and launched a few of my own companies.

I am available for freelance opportunities.
