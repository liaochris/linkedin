Grace Barry's Summary

For over 25 years, I've been helping small businesses and non-profits on Long Island get the most from their technology.

As the founder of Barry Technology Consulting, I combine a deep understanding of the workings and needs of small business with cutting edge expertise in the latest technology options. With the resources of the best of Nassau and Suffolk experts behind me and communicating with all levels of an organization, bring technology to where it needs to be --- working towards business goals.
