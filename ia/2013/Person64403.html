Joshua Howland's Summary

I graduated in Economics from Brigham Young University. While focusing on school and start-ups, I attempted to maintain a well rounded lifestyle, spending time with my wife, serving in my church responsibilities and staying athletically active. I played on the National Champion Brigham Young Lacrosse team and have run multiple half marathons. I am currently training for my first full marathon.

I have spent the last 5 years working in the mobile app development industry. I currently am building my own software company while working as a senior mobile software developer for a local contracting firm: Software Technology Group. I have spent time working on large and small development teams on iOS and web service projects.

Prior to and concurrent with my position at Zions Bank as a Financial Analyst, I have worked with teams of students to create products and business models centered on adding value to the world. I think of myself as a pioneer and builder and believe that I clearly recognize how to produce results by assembling great teams and helping them to focus on simple yet measurable, transparent goals.

I am interested in entrepreneurship within information distribution, structured finance, and or web/software design industries. My professional experience includes iOS development, UI and UX design, general management, relationship management, investment valuation services, business analysis, and sales.
