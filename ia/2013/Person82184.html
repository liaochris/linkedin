Logan Hale's Summary

My passion has always been focused around making films and videos to entertain and enlighten.

At V3 Media Marketing, I direct a team of creative professionals that help our corporate clients grow their business and brands through the power of video. We connect our clients to their target audience by mixing smart Strategy with quality Production and tactical Distribution.

Strategy, Production, Distribution equal the 3 V's in V3 Media Marketing (V3MM).

We specialize in Promotional Video Campaigns, Viral Videos, Live Event Production and Streaming, Explainer Animations, Customer Testimonials and Interactive Media Content.

Beyond corporate video production, V3MM also produces Websites, Blogs, Social Media and more - with the goal of helping clients maximize ROI with every video finding its perfect target audience.

And for those of you wondering about our little passion project, YourLittleFilm...

YourLittleFilm has a mission to make sure all of your precious family home video memories are saved and secured and shared for generations to come. Too many of these home videos spend their time collecting "digital dust" in a computer or phone without being seen and enjoyed.

We want to bring those media memories back to life!

YourLittleFilm takes your own home videos and still photos to create cool, creative, custom little films for you to share with friends and family via email, Facebook, Youtube, etc., and to keep as moving memories forever and ever. Find out more at www.YourLittleFilm.com :)

Specialties: Video Production, Media Production, Video Marketing, Event Marketing, Experiential Marketing, SEO, Social Media, Web Design, Photography, Videography, Editing, Directing, Producing
