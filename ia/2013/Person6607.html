André Fecteau's Summary

I am a freelance multimedia journalist who covers the post-revolution developments in Egypt at the political and social level. I fell in love with Cairo during one of my earlier expeditions and I moved to the Egyptian capital in 2011 to study Arabic and learn about its people. My blog, Glimpse of Cairo, chronicles Egyptian life amidst the country’s reorganization.

I am a regular contributor to Frontline Security, and my work was also published by Postmedia News, the Canadian Geographic and various specialized magazines, including Trenz and Blackfly. I speak English, French, German (intermediate) and Egyptian Arabic (beginner).

Specialties

Photography, photojournalism, print journalism
