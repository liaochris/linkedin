James Reader's Summary

PIANIST/KEYBOARD PLAYER/
MUSICAL DIRECTOR/ARRANGER/
PRODUCER/COLLABORATIVE SONGWRITER

James graduated from Goldsmiths’ College, University of London in 1995, achieving a first-class honours degree in Music. Following studies with acclaimed jazz pianist John Horler and a further year of postgraduate study at the Institute of Education, University of London, he embarked on a seven-year stint as a College Lecturer in Music/Music Technology to undergraduate level. James also holds a licentiate diploma in Piano Performance (with distinction) from Trinity College of Music, London.

To date, James has clocked up in-excess of 20 years of professional performing/recording experience, undertaking commitments with some of the UK's finest musicians across a diverse range of popular music. James spent a two-year period as keyboard player on tour with legendary vocal group 'The Drifters' - the longest running band in pop history. Most recently, he has been engaged by impresario Francis Rockliff as Arranger/MD for a new musical destined for London's West End.

James' song-writing/production collaborations with long-term creative partner Barnaby Pinny have recently resulted in the duo being signed (uniquely as UK-based writers) to Sony ATV/EMI Music Publishing exclusively across Asia...by the same South Korean A&R team that look-after Psy of 'Gangnam Style' fame!

Barnaby and James write/produce at their own digital studio facility in Olney, UK - the 'spiritual home of song-writing' - the small English town where 'Amazing Grace' was written in 1772. One of the duo's recent song placements was 'Just Go' with KPop sensation RaNia. The March 2013 release by the girl band formed by superstar US producer Teddy Riley was produced by KNS: the multi-million selling creative team behind hits for Britney Spears, Lady Gaga and 50 Cent.

James is endorsed by Universal Audio and PMC Speakers: world-class pro-audio manufacturers.

"...some very tasteful piano playing." [Paul Carrack]
