Joel Knoop's Summary

In October of 2000 with a cliff and a waterfall, I almost lost my life. As a result, and through a series of God-given events, I discovered my career path in media production. Since then I have had the wonderful opportunity to work on 3 Emmy Award winning shows as well as 2 Emmy nominated shows, and a slew of interesting and challenging projects.

I am a highly motivated self-starter and entrepreneur in the suburbs of Metro Detroit. I specialize in media production with an advertising twist. The quintessential element in all of my projects is the presentation of the customer’s product or identity, because after all that is the bottom line.

Utilizing my instincts in advertising, I take the approach that my company is not only hired to create a video or design a graphic, but to look at the bigger picture of the corporate identity. Whether the client is a church, corporation or individual, my goal is to advise them on the best way to market their product or service as a whole.

Live Out Loud Productions Inc. has developed an extensive network of creative talent, always at the ready. Our services include: Video production and post production, commercials, training & corporate videos, video editing, 2D & 3D graphics and animations, radio spots, location audio recording, location video crews, Steadicam, website development, corporate identity and logo design.

Our clients include: Discovery Channel, TLC, PBS, ABC, People.com... and many more.

My passion is to create cutting edge media that stirs the heart and soul. My goal is to be the tipping point. To reach into the lives of my viewers and challenge, encourage, inspire and strengthen.

I have a passion for family and have an interest in history. I am interested in Christian apologetics, and challenging the church to live as Christ did. I am passionate about Christian media and using it to challenge, encourage, inspire and strengthen. If you have similar interests I would love to meet you.

Specialties

Self motivated problem solver, budgeting shoots, securing talent, editing, shooting, graphics & animation, logo design, live audio, audio recoding, camera work, lighting.
