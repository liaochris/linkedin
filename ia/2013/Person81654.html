Lisa Pendlebury's Summary

I am a senior manager with considerable background in information security, information technology and customer services, with Divisional P & L responsibility. I am a self-motivated, confident professional with over 30 years experience in the IT Industry. With wide range of skills in general management, project management, and for the past 15 years, the successful implementation of the BS7799/ISO27001 information security standard and the achievement of accreditation against the UK Government Security Policy Framework (SPF). I have experience in business process definition, team recruitment, team building and have a track record in setting and achieving goals in a timely, organised manner through the development and motivation of staff.
I am a certified Information Security Management Professional (CISMP), a member of ISACA, an associate of the Institute of Information Security Professionals, a PRINCE 2 Practitioner with an MBA and a current Government Security Clearance.

Specialties: Government Security Accreditation, ISO27001, Programme & Project Management (Prince II Practitioner), Gold Standard Consultant (LRQA Consultants Network)
