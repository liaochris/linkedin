Gordon Hewett's Summary

Gordon Hewett is a director of CMB Technologies Limited (CMBSoft) an innovative software and marketing services company that specialises in database management i.e. Customer database management software or Customer relationship management i.e. CRM, email marketing, creating a presence and sales and marketing services from the CMB's professional resources group see the website for more details at www.cmbsoft.co.uk. CMBSoft is the brand name for the website and software prducts that consist of ContactMaker and MakingContact - customer database mangement and email marketing software. There is an Aftercare service to ensure you make the best use of your software. The products are all UK developed and aimed specifically at the SME market in Surrey, South London, Berkshire, Buckinghamshire, Oxfordshire, Hertfordshire, Kent & Hampshire. The company offers free audits of your database requirements as well as your sales and marketing needs. A well developed database for Customers, often referred to as Customer relationship management or CRM for short will give you a great platform from which to build your sales and marketing plans.
