Katherine Wilson's Summary

A perceptive, eloquent and professionally qualified Chartered Marketeer with over 20 years’ management experience across both public and private sector. Applying specialist knowledge to help clients consolidate their marketing and communications activity through tailor-made strategies which raise company profile whilst reinforcing consistent brand identity.
