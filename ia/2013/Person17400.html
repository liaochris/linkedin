Brian Hagman's Summary

Brian Hagman is Managing Partner with Sanford Rose Associates – Brighton. With an emphasis on
"Finding People Who Make a Difference", Brian truly enjoys and has a passion for helping organizations build and develop strong teams. He works closely with his clients to overcome the challenges that exist in sourcing and hiring the right talent.

Prior to joining Sanford Rose Associates, Brian spent 12 years in the field of human resources where he held several leadership roles within the automotive and building materials industries. He was instrumental in various plant start-ups and turnarounds, and was as a key player in many talent management and organizational development initiatives.

Brian is a member SAE International and earned his Bachelor of Science degree from Western Kentucky University in Business and Human Resource Management. He currently resides in the Louisville, KY area with his family, is an avid sports fan, and enjoys reading and studying all things related to business and personal development.
