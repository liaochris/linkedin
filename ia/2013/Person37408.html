Brian Knopf's Summary

Dynamic management career with strong leadership, problem solving, planning, team building, coaching and project management skills focused on Quality Assurance, virtualization, security, automation, configuration management, and IT. Experience teaching developers and testers how to execute and protect against XSS, injection, CSRF, privilege escalation, OWASP Top 10, SANS 25, and other security exploit types. Certified ScrumMaster (CSM) since 2007 who has been a ScrumMaster for multiple teams. Responsibilities included recruiting, developing, motivating and retaining diverse staff, while structuring them in teams that deliver results and savings. Experienced in developing, implementing, and documenting tools, processes, procedures, and implementation plans in fast-paced, high-pressure environments. Extensive experience building test lab and data center environments using virtualization technologies including VMware ESX, ESXi, vCenter, vSphere, Lab Manager, vCloud Director, Server, Workstation, and View.

Also known as the WeMo Wizard: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BIBQz_eV9Qk

Specialties: Pentesting, building technical teams, implementing Quality Engineering policy & process improvements, teaching Scrum, secure development and security testing, Functionality, Compatibility, Usability, Internationalization, Localization, and fuzzing. White & Black box testing of C, C++, C#, VB, Objective C & Java applications. Design of automation frameworks, workflows, SDLC, defect tracking, test case management, and test labs using virtual and physical systems.
