Dawn Barclay's Summary

Dawn Barclay is an experienced trainer and coach who's work Living Moxie helps individuals find the courage and confidence to break out of frustrating life and work circumstances to be who they really are, do what they love to do and make a difference. She works with individuals, groups and solo business owners.

She excels in helping individuals in their 2nd act, who are wishing to creating a new path for themselves in life, work or creating a business.

A former careers adviser and personal/professional development trainer in the non-profit sector primarily working in employability, and herself a solopreneur for 10 years, she understands deeply the issues, problems and questions individuals have when making massive life changes.

Before delivering online coaching programs for individuals, Dawn delivered contracts for charities, non-profits and the local sector. Her work is client centered, demand led and she is committed to life-long learning. She also runs charity firewalks, helping charities without a fundraising department to raise funds.

Specialties: confidence and courage coaching, writing and delivery of personal development courses and online coaching programs. Solo business help: blogging, online marketing + content marketing. Supporting individuals to make their difference and creating community.

Discover how I can help you live the life and biz you really want: http://dawnbarclay.com/
