John Shepler's Summary

I can help you understand the technology and get better deals on telecom and networking services for your company. My business blog, Telexplainer (telexplainer.com), and suite of web sites that offer instant automated online pricing are available to you 24/7.

I'm also thrilled to be affiliated with Telarus, Inc., to promote their award winning high bandwidth business telecommunications and cloud services. With dozens of competitive services providers, we can help all organizations from the smallest retail establishments to the largest multinational corporations cut their telecom network expenses and gain more bandwidth, often at the same time.

Specialties: Online marketing, especially of telecommunications, cloud computing and other technology related products and services, with a diverse background that spans digital communications, radio broadcasting, aerospace and industrial electronics engineering, management, website building, writing and online publication.
