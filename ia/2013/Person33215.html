Dean Brenner's Summary

Dean Brenner is an experienced executive coach, with a focus on public speaking, presentation and communication skills. Dean and his firm, The Latimer Group, provide communication coaching and training services, including presentation skills workshops, business writing workshops, strategic message consulting, and executive speech writing.

Dean is accomplished public speaker who provides keynote speeches for many of his clients at industry conferences, and company and team gatherings.

Dean is a published author. His second book, "Sharing the Sandbox: Building and Leading World-Class Teams in the 21st Century" was published in May 2012. His first book "Move the World: Persuade Your Audience, Change Minds and Achieve Your Goals" was published by John Wiley & Sons in April 2007. (Both books are available for purchase at www.SharingTheSandbox.com.)

Dean served as the Chairman of the US Olympic Sailing Program, from 2005-2012. He led the organization responsible for all aspects of funding, selection and performance for the US Olympic, Paralympic and Youth World Teams. In addition, Dean also served as Team Leader for the US Olympic Sailing Team at the 2008 Games in Beijing and the 2012 Games in London.
