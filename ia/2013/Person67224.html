John Currier's Summary

Certified Public Accounting firm that helps small and medium sized businesses and their owners take the stress out of their financial and income tax lives by performing audits, reviews, preparing compiled financial statements, preparing income taxes and income tax planning. A good lead or referral is a small and medium sized business that wants to reduce income taxes, better understand their financial statements and more efficiently run their business, thereby reducing their tax burden or improving their net income.

Specialties

Work with small and medium business doing audits and reviews that will provide value to their company. Provide expert income tax advice and service to save money and make you life less taxing.
