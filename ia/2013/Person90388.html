Charles McCool's Summary

Travel consultant and trainer to consumers and businesses, teaching clients how to save money, time, and stress on all aspects of travel. Author of "Winning the Airfare Game" and instructor of adult education classes in Northern Virginia (Fairfax and Arlington counties). Owner and operator of McCoolTravel.com, LowerAirfares.com, and TheTravelCoach.com (past). Personal travels to all 50 U.S.A. states and several dozen countries on five continents.

Extensive software testing experience with a history of improving quality for mobile, internet, and software companies.
