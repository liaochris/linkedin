Caroline McKean's Summary

I am currently the Art Director at LeisureMedia360, in Roanoke, VA.

As a part of a two-person creative team at Leisure I have to maintain a high standard of organization and communication, as I actively work with 6-8 publications, project-managers, and digital projects each month.

My creative focus at Leisure is editorial design, art direction, proposal publication-design, and publication redevelopment. My latest redesigns include: Blue Ridge Country Magazine & Virginia Tourism Corp. Travel Guide.

I recently had the opportunity help create a new magazine, bridebook, a announcement-style, wedding-resource print publication. I was on the business development-side as well as doing the creative design. It's been a rewarding and incredibly successful experience. June of 2013 our team turned out a 100-page bridal resource that will continue to grow and expand to encompass the entire state of Virginia.

I am also responsible for print production in preparation, identifying and fixing mistakes within the design process, maintaining a consistent publication style, overall quality control, and trouble-shooting software issues.

I am passionate about editorial design, consistent branding, and identity development.

I currently learning HTML and CSS design, Wordpress Design, and can't wait to take a hand-letting course in the near future.

On a personal level, I volunteer with the Roanoke Valley SPCA and local arts organizations. When I'm not designing I can be found on a trail in the surrounding mountains or swimming with my chocolate lab.

Specialties: Layout Design, Brand and Identity Development or Re-identification, Concept Development, Client Brainstorm, Photo Retouching, Web Design, Developing New Outreach/Marketing Plans, Writing, Collaboration and Teamwork, Leadership
