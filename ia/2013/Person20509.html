Carolyn Caldwell, CPO, CPO-CD's Summary

Certified Professional Organizer in Chronic Disorganization and business owner following 20 plus years in health care services management. Many of my clients are severly disorganized or managing significant organizational challenges including hoarding. I am focussed on providing individual solutions to client organizing challenges to support enduring change.

I have worked with corporate clients, residential and small office, home office clients.

Specialties

Chronic Disorganization, Hoarding, ADHD, Organizing with Health Care Facilities/Services
