J.M. Grimaldi's Summary

J.M. Grimaldi is a Director of Continental Funding Group (CFG), specializing in retail, multifamily, office, industrial, mixed-use, self-storage, net-leased and hospitality properties throughout the Western United States. He is responsible for structuring debt and equity transactions and originating new business across all property types.

Mr. Grimaldi has been with CFG since 2011. He began his career in 2007 as an investment sales associate with Marcus & Millichap and member of its National Office and Industrial Properties Group. There he was responsible for exclusively listing and negotiating over $400 million of investment property transactions. In 2010 he joined the Large Loan Group as an underwriter and analyst for Marcus & Millichap Capital Corporation (MMCC), one of the largest real estate investment banking firms in the country. His clients include many major institutions, REITs, syndicators, lenders, developers and private parties.

Mr. Grimaldi is a graduate of the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) with a Bachelor's degree in Business Economics. He is a licensed real estate salesperson in the state of California.
