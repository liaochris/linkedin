Laura Tower's Summary

I offer cost effective promotional websites for small businesses and individuals who are looking for their first professional online presence. A promotional website is a perfect way for people to learn about your service or business. Each design is geared towards my client's needs. I enjoy working with people and helping them get their voice heard on the web. I communicate with my clients throughout the design process.

The cost depends on your budget and the features you are looking for. Since your site is unique in its needs, I'll give you a quote based upon your particular requirements & desires. And, if you have a particular budget, please let me know what it is - I can likely come up with a solution to fit.

I also assist the client in the promotional phase of their website if needed using SEO and other tools.

Using my own original designs, I develop content management websites and blogs. I train clients to maintain their own information within the framework of their own website without having to traditionally depend on a webmaster.

I am currently experimenting with html5 as an alternative to Flash for audio and video.

The expertise of my past career focused on applications programming for the midrange computers in New York City. I decided to enhance my career and build websites to combine my technical and creative talents.

Specialties: I offer three things that my competitors don't: original style; quality; and individualized attention throughout the design process.
