Maggie Chandler's Summary

I love what I do and I work hard to educate my home buyer and home seller clients to ensure that their Vancouver real estate experience is organized and fun. I offer savvy opinions, exceptional service, great interpersonal skills and cutting edge technology. All coupled with a long history of hyper-local experience.

My motto is "do a job well or don't do it at all" and I do mine with a high degree of energy and efficiency, with a focus on building long term relationships.

My job is always changing and I enjoy the challenge.

I focus Vancouver's waterfront condo/townhome neighbourhoods of Coal Harbour, Downtown, Kitsilano, Yaletown, the West End and False Creek which includes the Olympic Village

If you see me walking on the seawall, say hi!

Specialties: Vancouver condos, townhomes and homes
