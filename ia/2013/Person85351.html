Marc Steen's Summary

Marc Steen (marcsteen.nl) works as a senior research scientist at research and innovation organization TNO (tno.nl).

He earned MSc, MTD and PhD degrees in Industrial Design Engineering at Delft University of Technology. He worked at Philips and KPN before joining TNO.

His MBTI-profile is INFP: Introversion, Intuition, Feeling, Perceiving.

Expertise: Human-centred design; Co-design; Open innovation; Collaboration Building; Innovation management; Organization studies; Science and Technology Studies; Qualitative User Studies; Service Design; Interaction Design; Marketing Strategy; Philosophy of Technology; Ethics.
