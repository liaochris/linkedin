Diane Strand's Summary

I am the majority owner of a multi award winning full service video production company JDS Video & Media Productions, a wife and mother of three. I have over 25 years experience in video production, business management & marketing. My bachelors’ degree is from CSU Northridge in Television Production with a minor in media marketing. I began my career as a producer for theater productions. After a several years working in entertainment television for company's such as Disney, Universal & Warner Brothers, I found my ultimate passion in the marketing/marcom departments of fortune 500 companies creating corporate videos. As the Owner I strive to make JDS be the best media solution company for marketing, communications & sales. JDS is proud of a clientele that includes companies such as: Abbott, OptiForms, IBSS, Breg. Datron World Communications, ProSci, & Palomar College (list not inclusive).

JDS was founded in 2003 in North County San Diego, and in 2008 JDS expanded and relocated to Temecula, CA. In January 2010 JDS expanded again when Diane and her husband grew an after school Drama Club in the Temecula Unified School District into a full scale Professional Acting School for all ages to learn the technique and craft of Acting.

Since launching JDS Actors Studio they have enrolled over 100 students who participate acting classes, private lessons, and/or professional workshops. The Studio is able to assist actors in getting agent representation from SAG franchised agencies.

Diane is deeply involved with community business and economic development. JDS is a member of many business & community organizations:EDC of Southwest California, Temecula Chamber, & Murrieta Chamber. Diane is currently holding a Board of Directors seat on Southwest Community Development Organization, & the EDC of Southwest California. In addition, Diane chair's the marketing comm. & a member of technology comm. for the EDC and Co-Chair's of the Women in Business Comm. for the Temecula Chamber.

Specialties

Video production and multimedia from concept to delivery. Specializing in corporate training videos and corporate web messages, Website Design & Implementation, Search Engine Optimization, Email marketing through eblast newsletters, CD-ROM for Green Marketing and Communications, DVD/CD replication/duplication & packaging, Internet streaming and content delivery
