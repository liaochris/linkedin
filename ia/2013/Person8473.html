Annie Boccio's Summary

I love my job. It started as a hobby, the best way to begin a career!

My technical/creative skills combined with my customer service experience allow me to provide my clients with a web presence and social media strategy that works without over-complicating things.

I’m an “early adopter” who never stops trying new tools and technologies. Sometimes they work… sometimes they don’t. They always teach me something and introduce me to fascinating people and ideas.

I’ve presented to community, educational, and specialized organizations on topics including social networking for beginners, online security for parents, and video conversation.

I enjoy offering my skills and time to local community organizations. It’s fun, and it’s a great way to meet people.

I’m a proud mom of three sons, and happily married for nearly 25 years.
