Erin Sander's Summary

Erin Sander founded Erin Sander Design in 2009 as a full service Interior Design Firm striving to create an intelligent integration of architecture and interiors through a detailed approach to the design process. Erin Sander Design focuses on designing residential properties from the ground up and giving each client an inspiring place to call home. Erin thrives on seeing the carefully planned and executed bones of a home come to life with perfectly appointed treatments and furnishings.

Erin studied Interior Design at Baylor University with an added minor in Spanish. Erin now practices as a licensed designer in the State of Texas (#10788) and a member of the American Society of Interior Designers. Erin has been designing professionally since 2005.

Email: Erin@ErinSanderDesign.com

Specialties

Complete Interior Design, Residential New Construction, Residential Remodels, Auto CAD, Space Planning, Interior Architectural Detailing, Interior Finish Selection, Interior Furnishing Selections and Procurement

Erin Sander's Experience

2009 – Present (4 years)

Erin Sander Design focuses on Luxury Residential Construction and guides clients through the process of refining architectural plans, bringing each space to life through detailed elevation drawings, and selecting all finishes to complete the design. Throughout the process Erin is there to ensure each element is implemented as planned and no detail is overlooked.

As each space begins to take shape, furniture layouts and selection of interior art, accessories, furnishings, window treatments and rugs will be made and scheduled to install with the completion of each home.
