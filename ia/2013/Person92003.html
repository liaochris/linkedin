Michael A. Foley's Experience

Owner & Principle Photographer

Michael A. Foley Photography LLC

July 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Columbus, Ohio Area

Michael A. Foleys' world of photography is colors, inspirations and emotions. Imagine images capable of cutting thru the world of what many describe as all too often the same. His photography seeks to capture imaginative stories retold thru the eyes of breathless viewers.

Michael is co-president of the Ohio Valley Chapter of the American Society of Media Photographers. Just one reason why he has long been recognized for his prolific and fine portraiture of well‐known and influential executives, judges, politicians, philanthropists, state and community leaders.

More so, Michael uses travel and learning about new people and places to keep him aware of new trends and innovative brand techniques.

As a member of Editorial Photographers (EP), Michael enjoys working with new professionals and is a master of specialized lighting techniques. He loves brainstorming with other creative personalities to find solutions. His assignments are completed on time, in budget with premium file delivery.

And finally, Michael Foley is involved in the community and specializes in: people, food, editorial features, annual report photography, portraiture/corporate-executive and personality portraiture, residential and corporate photography.
