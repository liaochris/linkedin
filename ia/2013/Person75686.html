Kim Bookless' Summary

Thinking of publishing a book, or looking for a writer or editor? I am a publishing consultant, writer, and editor based in downtown Chicago.

Publishing Consulting Services:

Traditional book deals are hard to come by but self-publishing is within everyone's reach. If you’d like to self-publish and don’t want to do it alone, I can be your guide and your single point of contact throughout the publishing process. I’ll explain your publishing options, work with you to clarify your goals for the book, help you choose the right self-publishing service providers (cover designers, ebook formatters, etc.), and interact with the service providers on your behalf. I'll manage the entire book production process and keep you informed every step of the way.

If you're considering hiring a publishing consultant or want to learn more about my services, just drop me a line. We’ll schedule a complimentary 30-minute phone consultation to talk about your book concept and help you decide if working with a publishing consultant is right for you.

Writing Services:

I provide original writing, ghostwriting, content editing, and copyediting for:
-Books and book proposals
-Web content, articles, and blog posts
-Grant proposals and applications
-White papers, case studies, and marketing materials
-E-newsletters

I co-founded the Chicago Self-Publishing Meetup Group. Our members are aspiring authors, people who work in the publishing industry, and others who are simply curious about how to create a book. We meet in the downtown Chicago area every other month and offer our members free resources, presentations, guest speakers, and panel discussions.

Specialties

Publishing consulting, book consulting, writing web content, ghost writing, book editing, and copyediting.
