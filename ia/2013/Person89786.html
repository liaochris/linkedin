Maurice Claypole's Projects

  • The Fractal Approach to Language Teaching

    • 1999 bis Heute
    Teammitglieder: Maurice Claypole

    This is an ongoing project that dates back to my initial involvement with fractal graphics and the principles of dynamism which led me to draw parallels between chaos theory and human language. A key part of this project is identifying positive consequences for English language teaching. As of 2013 this topic has gained a new currency and I have delivered papers on the subject at conferences and events in London, Athens, Stuttgart and Cologne.
