  • Rolling Rock: Born Small Town

    • December 2008 to March 2009
    Team Members: Hafiz Huda, Ash Warren, Scott Cook, Matthew Tillstrom, J.R. Gain, Randy Smith, Susie Urschler, Matt Glarner, Jeff Stevens, Steve DeLorez

    The challenge for Rolling Rock was to create an engaging interactive site that offered a visceral brand experience and also had local appeal. The Town Gazette was the digital manifestation of the brand in the online space. Every small town has news to share, and it’s usually a little off and entertaining. Our town loved music and had a lot to share with its residents.

    We activated the campaign through a music tour, online content, email, and local bar events featuring Bloodshot Records artists and a really relaxed vibe that resonated with Rolling Rock’s indie cred. As the Digital Creative Director for the Anheuser-Busch team, I oversaw the strategy, information architecture, design, and development of RollingRock.com.
