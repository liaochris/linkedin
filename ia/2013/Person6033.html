Amy Felix Polefrone's Summary

ABOUT HR STRATEGY GROUP, LLC
HR Strategy Group, LLC specializes in helping small & mid-sized business owners with their tough employment, HR, and management issues. We focus on solving difficult HR and management issues and improving HR functions with client companies. We have significant experience in designing and conducting training programs (customer service, team building, supervisory & management training). HR Strategy Group's corporate capabilities include employment/HR advice, training & development, recruitment & interviewing protocols, designing & implementing performance management systems, benefits advocacy, employee communications, and strategic planning.

Our clients can call 24/7 to discuss their business issues. Client industries include: insurance, telecommunications, federal government, non-profits, healthcare, educational enrichment, and other entrepreneurial "Main Street" sectors.

In December 2011, we announced a strategic alliance with PayScale, Inc. PayScale is the nation's leading provider of compensation data, strategy, and consulting solutions. HR Strategy Group is a proud business partner and call provide local account management services for PayScale's MarketRate and Insight Platform solutions. More information about our partnership is available at: http://www.hrstrategygroup.com/compensation.html

We are certified by the State of Maryland as a MBE/DBE firm (certification # 11-378).

Specialties

Executive/management coaching, Human resources and employment services, employee relations, compensation, performance management, interviewing and hiring guidelines, customized training programs, strategic planning, and customized email marketing solutions using Constant Contact.
