Gary Radin's Summary

I have over twenty years of experience in design including specialty in production design, art direction, interior design, theater and graphics. My project work is diverse and includes hundreds of creative solutions for television commercials, cable network shows, corporate meeting and video sets, exhibits, live event staging and theme interiors. My creative and technical roles range from independent source to contributing partner.

My designs have been showcased for such entities as Showtime, Great American Country, Jewelry Television, Comcast, PNC Bank, Pfizer, Merck, PECO, 3M, Unisys, AT&T, Rohm & Haas and Fidelity Investments. Over the past 15 years I have also been integral in the management and successful growth of three creative service companies. Work has been published in Event Design and Broadcast Engineering magazines.

A creative spirit is part of my every day life. Strong concepts are at the core of my professional work. I think design disciplines have few boundaries - a well-trained designer can create anything. Creating something from nothing and transforming space is my life's work.

Specialties

concept development, design, art direction, interior design, set design, exhibit design, project management, proposal writing, and creative business consultation.
