For features I wrote a piece on Samuel Johnson's Dictionary and a very well-received, two-part piece on Ernest Hemingway. Also did the usual copy editing, layout and design. Special projects.

Copy editor | layout and design

The Bradenton Herald

Public Company; 51-200 employees; MNI; Newspapers industry

November 1979 – April 1980 (6 months) Bradenton, Florida

Among other things, this is where I learned to be a weatherman. Every morning at 5 a.m. I went to the roof, checked the rain guage, checked other instruments, recorded the data, then put together a full page of weather news for that afternoon's paper. I was told, "Florida people are interested in the weather!"

Teacher | publications advisor

Caldwell Ohio Exempted Village Schools (high school)

August 1976 – June 1978 (1 year 11 months) Caldwell, Ohio

Certified by the State of Ohio in English and journalism, I taught everything from basic English to literature, composition, news writing, copy editing and photography to high school students. Supervised yearbook, school newspaper, literary magazine.
