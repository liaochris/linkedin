Ilari Niitamo's Summary

I am an artist / designer / programmer based in Helsinki. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Media Engineering from Metropolia University of Applied Sciences and I am presently pursuing a Master's degree in New Media from Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture. I am also the co-founder of Pintxo Creative.

My work is often cross-disciplinary, mixing art and design with technical implementations and interactive mediums. My main strength is the ability to approach projects from multiple standpoints and experiences. In arts, I mainly practice illustration, animation, 3D and design. As a programmer, I currently work mostly in HTML+CSS, JavaScript, Objective-C and ActionScript.
