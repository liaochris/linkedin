Kate Chapman's Summary

I am a tutor and academic mentor in the Montlake/Madison Park area of Seattle providing highly personalized supplementary education for K-12 students. I have been working with students since 2007, and ground my educational theories in ongoing education and independent research as well as my undergraduate background in literature, linguistics and the philosophy of language.

I generally work with students from ages eight to eighteen, and aim to provide continuity throughout primary and secondary education by building long-term academic mentoring relationships with students and their families.

Specialties: Academic Mentoring, Standardized Test Preparation, Subject Tutoring
