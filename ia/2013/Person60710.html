Janet Dore's Summary

After graduating with a Bachelor’s degree in Real Estate, I worked for several years as a state-certified appraiser, quality assurance coordinator and office manager for a small San Diego real estate firm. With the arrival of my first child, running a dynamic household, managing personal investments, and volunteering in my community became my primary focus. In 2007, I launched a travel blog (The Wandering Housewife) that I maintain to this day. Several years later, I returned to my previous employer on a part-time basis, where I am currently a webmaster, quality assurance/social networking coordinator and writing. Forming my own company, Scribaceous, Inc., has allowed me to provide freelance writing services and Wordpress website development and publish a website (DateOxygen.com) designed to steer dating couples to the best of San Diego’s small local businesses.

Specialties: Business writing (specializing in website content, real estate, demographics, and global/macro/micro economics), creative writing (specializing in San Diego, domestic/international travel, and personal development), Wordpress website development, administrative and personal support, travel planning/research.
