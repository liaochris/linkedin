Angela Hausman's Summary

Social media marketing, tied with a good social media strategy and social media analytics reflecting the appropriate KPI's, can dramatically increase the ROI of your firm. At Hausman and Associates, a full service firm operating at the intersection of marketing and social media, we combine skills in social media strategy, social media analytics, content marketing, SEO, Website Design, and email marketing to make your marketing SIZZLE!

Our team of designers, content managers, and business intelligence analysts creates custom social media marketing solutions that include video, podcasts, webinars, mobile apps, and other tools to help clients reach their goals.

As a speaker, I give dynamic presentations to audiences from all over the US and even some from around the world. My "zen presentations" are as entertaining as they are engaging and educational. Previous topics include:

-- Creating engagement in social networks

-- Using social media analytics to improve your ROI

-- How social media can fit into your marketing without breaking the bank
