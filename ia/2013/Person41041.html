The Talent Group Recruiting

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

April 2007 – Present (6 years 9 months) Greater Boston Area

The Talent Group is a Boston-based, privately held company. We are not a one-size-fits-all mega firm. We focus solely within the advertising and marketing industry working with each client and each candidate individually — all with the ultimate goal of making the perfect match.

Our recruiters take the time to understand the nuances of your organization—the quirks, the personalities, the challenges, the long-term and short-term goals. So when you review a candidate from The Talent Group, you can be confident of a "good fit" right from the start.

Our mission is to support each client with their strategic business initiatives by providing the highest quality talent in a timely and professional manner.

We are focused on building a partnership centered around a strong commitment to excellent service. We believe in serving our corporate clients and candidates using the highest ethical and professional standards.
