Felipe Raga's Summary

Felipe Raga is Director of Online Strategy at Daemon Quest, Deloitte’s marketing & sales consultancy.

He is a seasoned multilingual IT & Internet business development executive, manager and entrepreneur with 15+ years’ experience in:
- International sales, marketing and expansion plans;
- Online marketing: SEM, SEO, re-marketing, re-targeting, online intelligence & analytics, and conversion funnel optimization;
- Multichannel customer experience & operations: in particular integrating online and offline business models and structures;
- Marketing & Sales Automation across several platforms: Demand Generation, Lead Management (Qualification, Scoring, Nurturing, Up&Cross Selling), and Workflow Automation.

Previous to Daemon Quest by Deloitte, Felipe has worked in the following sectors and companies:
- Market research: GfK Markt- und Sozialforschung;
- Commercial, financial and marketing information services: eInforma-Dun&Bradstreet;
- Telecom and advertising: Telefonica Publicidad e Información (now Yell Group);
- Software and IT Security: Panda Security;
- Food and non-food distribution and retailing: Rewe Gruppe.

He is a qualified lawyer by the Spanish Bar Association, holds a BBA in International Business & Marketing (Paris, and Heidelberg, Germany), and graduated from a Management Development Program (PDD) at IESE Business School. He is also a Google Analytics and a Nedstat-ComScore certified professional.

Felipe is fully proficient in German, French, English and Spanish, and has spent several years studying and working abroad.
