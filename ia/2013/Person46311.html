Frost Simula's Summary

I have been working in web design and development since 1994, and have built hundreds of sites and web applications using a wide variety of technologies. During that time I have been embedded with sales teams as a technical consultant and project estimator, and I have been a strategist and designer on user experience (UX) projects.

I specialize in technical and creative strategy, business analysis and requirements gathering, and user experience engineering. The focus of my career is to bridge the gap between software design and development by bringing new contexts and blend of experience to the table.
