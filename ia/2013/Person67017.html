John Bliss' Summary

Lawyer turned technologist and entrepreneur. An innovative and creative thinker, strategist and doer. I work, live and play in Boulder, Colorado where I am Founder and CEO of Captimo, a mobile + social solution for brands and research firms. In former lives, I worked at the intersection of law, politics, technology and policy. Now, when I'm not racing (or thinking of racing) my mountain or cyclocross bike, I'm heads down in the incredibly exciting and fast paced world of mobile, social and video.

Specialties: mobile, social, video, internet law and policy, privacy and civil liberties law and policy, national security policy and politics, government affairs, coalition/team building, strategy (corporate, legal, political), social, persuasion, getting the most out of life.
