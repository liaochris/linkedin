Eric T. Cook's Projects

  • Business Continuation and Disaster Recovery Plan

    • February 2012 to May 2012
    Team Members: Eric T. Cook, Jason Zebulon Merson

    Created plan to prevent and respond to various emergency situations. Business continuity planning involves recovery from emergencies and resuming normal business operations as quickly as possible. Emergency situations in this case refer to natural disasters, malicious activities, technological failures, emergencies and other catastrophic events. The primary purpose of this document is to prevent damage, prepare in advance and resume business operations after an emergency.

    The steps and procedures outlined in the plan minimize risk to staff, data and equipment in the event of an emergency. While no business continuity plan can prevent every possible disaster, this plan will mitigate and help prevent financial damages, data loss and most importantly increase the safety of the companies employees and stakeholders during a time of crisis.
