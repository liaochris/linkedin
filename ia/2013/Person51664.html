Harald Koebler, CSM's Summary

Project Manager with a unique combination of agile project management expertise, computer science education and German work ethic that achieves extraordinary results for organizations. 5+ years experience in project management and program management for 80+ projects with budgets totaling over $21M. 50+ hours of presenting, teaching and training on topics of agile development principles and coding basics for team members and project managers.

Consults and leads executive management and development teams through organizational change, with a focus on transitioning to an agile development methodology. Designs and implements custom project delivery solutions for organizations to increase profits, while establishing stability and laying the foundation for continuous future growth. Shapes and leads multi-cultural teams, both co-located and globally located, to be highly efficient and productive. Proven success working with teams. Fluent in both English and German with unrestricted work authorization to work in the United States and the European Union.

Industries include Internet/Mobile Software, Health Care Services, Automotive, Education, Film and E-Commerce. Clients include Fox Broadcasting Company, Toyota, Kaiser Permanente, Spin Master, USA Network, National Geographic, Tiger Woods Foundation, Documentary.org.
