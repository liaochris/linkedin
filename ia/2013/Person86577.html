Mark Felber's Summary

Mark is a Licensed Professional Counselor, facilitator and public speaker with an extensive educational background. If you care about your employees, your congregation, or organization members, here's your chance to offer a helping hand. Mark's 12- group seminars, offered as a community service, are free, packed with solid information, and can change the life of someone you know. Mark is also an approved CEU provider for LPC's LCDC's, and Social Workers in the state of Texas. Click on his website above then click on Free Group Seminars for more information.

He has trained in 18 models of therapy and is able to draw on his vast array of therapeutic tools in working with clients. In additional to being a Licensed Professional Counselor, Mark is a Licensed Chemical Dependency Counselor, a Certified Practitioner of Psychodrama, a Certified Imago Relationship Therapist, a Certified Solution-Focused Therapist, a Level II Advanced Grief Recovery Facilitator, a Level II Advanced EMDR Trauma Resolution Facilitator and a Level II Advanced Experiential Therapist.

His professional and educational background makes him well-qualified to offer his Road Map for Life Seminars that address a variety of mental health issues that just about everyone can relate to. His seminars on grief, co-dependency, addiction, couples therapy, trauma, toxic relationships, and many other topics have informed and captivated audiences for more than a decade.

Mark creates a holistic healing atmosphere that addresses the underlying issues of the mind, body and soul that lead to addiction and dysfunctional behaviors. He also works with the individual to establish a new lifestyle free of old addictive habits and dysfunctional behaviors. He offers a wide variety of approaches to help adults live more fulfilling lives including.

* Couples Therapy
* Mindfulness Training
* Stress Reduction
* Recovery Skills Training
* PTSD Reducing Therapies
* Life Coaching
* Self Esteem Building

Specialties

Psychotherapist specializing in adult children of early childhood trauma, alcohol and drug abuse counseling, codependency, relationship issues, conflict resolution, intimate relationship skills, grief and loss, stress management, adult children of alcoholics, depression, anxiety/fear/panic, quick, solution- focused counseling, low self esteem, family stress, infidelity, sexual abuse, personal growth, relapse prevention, dual diagnosis, emotional abuse, coping skills, and chronic impulsivity.

Road Map for Life: Twelve Seminars for Creating the Life You Want:
1. Creating the Relationship of Your Dreams: Pathways to Healing For Singles and Couples- Focuses on ways to save, improve or simply understand relationships
2.Hooked on Love: When You Love too Much (Parts 1 & 2) - Deals with codependency and the self-defeating behaviors that stand in the way of happiness.
3. Understanding the Addict in Your Life: Awakening to Recovery (Parts 1&2) -Focuses on the tragedy of addiction.
4. Healing Toxic Relationships: How to Talk so Your Partner Listens - Speaks to everyone's desire to have satisfying and loving connections.
5. Everyday Addictions That Destroy Your Happiness - Examines the things we do that on the surface seem harmless: Spending, gossiping, sleeping, etc.
6. When Love Hurts: How to Heal a Painful Relationship - Looks at the damage done through humiliation, control, intimidation and other behaviors. These insidious behaviors destroy relationships and cause immense pain.
7. The Healing Power of Experiential Psychotherapy: Where Talk Therapy Never Goes - Looks at how acting out painful experiences from the past can help people learn to resolve issues in the present.
8. Recovering from Your Traumatic Childhood: Breaking the Cycle of Pain - Focuses on the ways parents unconsciously wound their children and how those behaviors are passed from one generation to the next.
9. Grieving Life's Losses: Developing the Courage to Mourn (Parts 1&2) - Looks at all kinds of losses people face including the death of a loved one, the end of a career, the loss of a pet and many others. It also focuses on the stages of mourning and the grief work that must be done for true healing to occur.
