Jamie Gaymon's Summary

I grew up in Cherry Hill NJ, right outside of Philadelphia. I had a lot of very entrepreneurial influences growing up, and the seed was planted. I've always known that I was going to be an entrepreneur.

I graduated from the University of Richmond in 1999, and went to work in the staffing industry. I started out in operations (A/R and A/P), and hated it. I was able to transfer into recruiting, and spent the next 7 years of my career in sales and recruiting.

After one too many layoffs, I decided to go out on my own, and ran my own successful executive recruiting and recruiting consultancy for almost 3 years. The one huge mistake I made in building this business was the same mistake I know millions of small business owners have done-I tried to do EVERYTHING.

Social media is one of the biggest keys to building leverage into any business, and I'm successfully using it to build a profitable, and most importantly sustainable business so that I can spend time living life and travelling with my family

If you ask me nicely, I may even tell you how I'm doing it :)

Specialties: Networking, Lead Generation, Branding, SEO, Affiliate Marketing, Attraction Marketing, Article Marketing, Video Marketing, Blogging. Helping entrepreneurs begin, jump start and run a successful business as well as coach them to make as much money as they desire. In short, to live their dreams.
