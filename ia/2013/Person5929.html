Amy Eastment's Summary

Helping companies make their totally rad products even better!

Together, we'll improve your user experience through user research, team brainstorming, and design...but I also pitch in wherever your company needs help in order to succeed. My entire career has been with startups, and I know how important it is to figure out - and shift - priorities on short notice.

I (most often) help teams:
· Build awesome products starting with informed interaction design.
· Improve adoption with fast, critical, & actionable user feedback.
· Maximize customer retention by catching product issues now.
· Boost development efficiency by adopting Agile principles.
· Create extra bandwidth for a dev, product, marketing, or QA teams on short notice.

I also cheerlead the hell out of your team, and bring folks together through a combination of excitement about individual strengths, comparisons to Voltron in how you are addressing your customers' problems, tasty baked goods, and captioned (cat) pictures. Seriously.
