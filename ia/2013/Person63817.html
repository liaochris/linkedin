Jim Crotty, CPP's Summary

Professional photographer and owner of Picture Ohio LLC. In addition to my work with fine art nature and landscape photography and fine art print installations I also provide commercial and editorial photography services that include architectural, product, portrait and event imaging.

I live and work on nearly eight acres of fields and woods on a historic farm property in Greene County, Ohio, just east of Dayton.

Published images in Professional Photographer Magazine, National Wildlife, Nature’s Best magazines, USAToday and the Smithsonian Magazine. Installations of my fine art nature prints can be viewed at hospitals and medical centers in Dayton, Columbus, Lima and Portsmouth, Ohio.

CPP (Certified Professional Photographer) designation through Professional Photographers of America earned in 2009. A number of awards through print competitions with the Professional Photographers of Ohio.

I teach and present both introductory and advanced photography workshops on nature and landscape photography, digital editing and workflow and developing artistic vision. I shoot almost entirely with digital equipment including Canon EOS camera bodies and Canon L lenses and utilize Mac based hardware for editing, post-processing and digital file management.

Graduate of both the University of Dayton and Westminster College of Salt Lake City.

Honorably discharged veteran of the United States Marine Corps Reserve. 1983 - 1988.

Committed to discovering what truly matters in life by seeking the good light, lifting my heart and finding peace.

I’m originally from Dayton but have also lived in Salt Lake City, Utah, Hilton Head Island, S.C. and Frisco, Texas.

Specialties: Commercial photography of events, facilities, processes, products, employees, architectural projects. Editorial photography for corporate publications and regional magazines.
Digital image editing and work flow on both Mac and PC platforms. Studio and location lighting.

Jim Crotty, CPP's Publications

  • Renewal

    • Blurb
    • September 7, 2009
    Authors: Jim Crotty, CPP

    Ohio Photographer, Visual Artist and Writer Jim Crotty presents his images of the spring landscape, photographed in April and May, 2009. Select fine art nature and landscape images were captured in the Appalachian Foothills of Hocking Hills, Ohio as well as along the streams and waterfalls of Great Smoky Mountain National Park in Tennessee. In addition to the magnificent images presented, Jim also includes his essay "Life's Lessons Learned on the Trail to Ramsay Cascade," a thought-provoking journey of self-discovery while on the trail in the Smoky Mountains.

  • The Margins of the Day

    • Blurb
    • November 29, 2009
    Authors: Jim Crotty, CPP

    Ohio Photographer Jim Crotty presents examples of his best landscape photographs taken from late summer through fall of 2009, of the scenic and skyscape scenes at sunrise and sunset. Locations include Hocking Hills, Ohio; the Carolina Low Country and Great Smoky Mountain National Park. Margins of the Day is Jim Crotty's sixth self-published photography book on Blurb.com. This volume includes 50 pages of breathtaking beauty discovered during those times of day when the "good light" prevails and the creative spirit of the landscape photographer is awakened.

  • In The Peace of This Place

    • Blurb
    • January 4, 2012
    Authors: Jim Crotty, CPP

    A photographer's creative vision and interpretation of the Hocking Hills of Southeastern Ohio. Jim Crotty discovered his passion for nature and landscape photography when he first visited Hocking Hills State Park in the winter of 1980. He has since returned, again and again, to refine his art and rediscover the spirit and soul of a haunted landscape that sits on the edge of the Appalachian foothills. This volume includes a narrative essay by the artist photographer on both Hocking Hills and The Inn at Cedar Falls as well as his calming and peaceful imagery captured through the seasons from spring 2009 through fall 2011.
