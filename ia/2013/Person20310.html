  • THE SOUL OF SELLING

    Selling based on warm, authentic relationships; service; guaranteed results; and feeling good about what you do. The 3 Soul of Selling principles: Honor yourself. Honor others. Honor what you offer. Six powerful steps to the exact sales results of your choice, respecting and appreciating everyone with whom you speak, and doing good as well as doing well.

  • SELL YOURSELF WITHOUT FEELING PUSHY, CREEPY, OR WEIRD!

    • New Horizons Library
    Authors: Carol Costello, Author, Speaker, Sales Coach

    SELL YOURSELF transforms those queasy, uneasy feelings about selling yourself into a clear, open confidence that helps you handle objections with ease and aplomb, and close the sale with skill and grace.
    This easy, natural, 10-step approach is based on the three basic principles of Carol's popular selling primer, THE SOUL OF SELLING: Honor yourself, honor others, and honor what you offer.

  • CREATIVITY ON DEMAND: Write in the Zone

    • New Horizons Library
    Authors: Carol Costello, Author, Speaker, Sales Coach

    You get 3 Secret Strategies for finishing your book and practical tips for tapping into that focused, spontaneous, relaxed and imaginative place we call the Zone.
    The Zone is not for special people, or radically talented people, or even for good people. It's for all of us.
