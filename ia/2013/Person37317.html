Doug Muise's Summary

It's hard to overstate how important good design is these days. Consumers demand sophisticated product experiences that are beautifully delivered. These products need to provide real value and make people's lives better. They need to be built incrementally, with a lot of customer involvement, and designed well. And they need to be simple, easy, and gratifying to use. As a seasoned visual designer leveraging a 15-year track record crafting digital experiences for desktop and mobile users, my mission is to help design great products that drive consumer behavior, achieve business goals, and help create sustainable opportunities for businesses.

Current Challenge:
Working with SAP Labs in NYC to enhance the customer experience of SAP's business applications. We are working hard to translate big data into big solutions for SAP's customers leveraging HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design.

Skills + Expertise:
• Able to translate business goals and functional requirements into compelling creative solutions
• Excellent sense of typography, color, composition, and layout
• Solid foundation in web design methodologies, best practices, and usability standards
• Enthusiastic, strong collaborator, and positive partner for management, UX, and development teams
• Dynamic, responsive communicator with excellent written, verbal and presentation skills
• Near-expert knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite
• "Light lifting" skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript
• Adept at conceptual copywriting, headline creation, product naming

Process + Methodologies:
• Comfort with Agile and Lean UX workflow
• High degree of comfort working in small, multi-disciplinary teams
• Excel at partnering with UX designers, developers, marketing managers
• Strong advocate of CSS-based, accessible, standards-driven web design

Clients:
Adobe, American Express, Apple, Cisco, Dockers, Hard Rock Cafe, Home Depot, HP, Levi’s, Macromedia, Microsoft, Oracle, Safeway, Sega, Sprint, Symantec, Virgin Records America, Yahoo!
