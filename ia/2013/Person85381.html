Marcus Daley's Summary

Marcus has worked with small start-up companies and Fortune 500 companies including Microsoft and Intel. He has managed large, complex projects with teams across the globe including India, Japan, Philippines, Russia, Uruguay and Vietnam. Over the last decade his responsibilities have also included creating and maintaining technology centers and overseeing the associated operational functions. He is currently CTO of Market Force (MFI) an exciting, medium-sized company focused on bringing best in class customer experience information to more than 350 of the world’s leading brands.

Specialties

Managing global software engineering and operations teams. Directing multiple development projects and product plans. Creating and maintaining technology centers and overseeing the associated operational functions.
