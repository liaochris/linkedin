Edson Gardea's Summary

As president of Neomedia Design Group, I've helped my clients get over $45+ Million dollars in awarded contracts, and I've worked on over 80 projects with a collective value of over $250+ Million dollars.

Media design is about the creation of content that drives the marketing, advertising, or experience of a design, product or concept. It’s about taking media to the next level and supporting the transformation of traditional media to dynamic digital media.

My capabilities to deliver true media design are a mix of 3D Visualization, 3D Animation, HD Video Production, Motion Graphics, Professional Photography, and high-end Visual FX for Advertising, Marketing, Film, Broadcast, TV Commercials, Digital Videos, Architecture, Engineering, Real Estate, and Land Development.

I'm a media design expert for the company and I'm able to interpret any design and documentation from Architects, Artists, Business Owners, Designers, Engineers, and Real Estate Professionals for creating advanced 3D visualizations, 3D animations, virtual tours, product videos, information videos, marketing videos, and advertising materials.

In addition to being architecturally trained, in 2008 I decided to enter the media & entertainment industry to provide high quality 3D content and VFX for film, TV, broadcast, and showcase events.

Prior to founding Neomedia Design Group, I worked with several of the leading architectural firms in El Paso, Texas for 10 years; there I gained experience as a 3D artist and Project Manager. Being architecturally trained, I understand the intricacies of the design and development process of Education, Healthcare, Government, Retail/Restaurant, Industrial/Manufacturing, Business/Corporate, and Residential architectural projects.

My 3D animation and VFX skills have enabled us to pierce into the markets of Filmmaking and TV, taking Neomedia Design Group in a new direction and expanding the company's core services to fulfill the needs in those markets.

Specialties

Media Design using 3D Animation, 3D Visualizations, Architectural Renderings, 3D Virtual Tours, Motion Graphics, and Visual Effects (VFX) for Film, TV Commercials, and Videos.
