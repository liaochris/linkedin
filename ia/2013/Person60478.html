Jean-Baptiste Audrerie's Summary

Organizational Psychologist Jean-Baptiste Audrerie is in charge of SPB’s Technological Development. He oversees the R&D department’s technological watches and works closely with senior management to keep all technology processes and systems up-to-date.

Through his six years as a partner and account executive for a French consulting firm and his 17 years as a business consultant, he has acquired solid expertise in developing staffing processes and conducting executive assessment appraisals in France and Quebec.

Since 2010, he has worked on business projects involving innovation, process efficiency, the development of data management applications (big data, predictive data, client portal data) and the marketing of various technology solutions (Web applications for clients). As the manager of SPB’s CRM Microsoft Dynamics project, he oversaw everything from design, software analysis and configuration, systems integration and implementation, to identifying and using indicators to ensure the return on investment of this project. His role as a change management agent also led him to maintain an updated communication plan, to lead training sessions and to conduct post-implementation follow-ups for 90 users.

An expert on the Web2.0, innovation and collaborative tools, Mr. Audrerie is also responsible for optimizing SPB’s online presence (CMS, Website, Google Analytics, LinkedIn, Twitter and Yammer). He conducts ongoing technological, economic and competitive watches and monitors the company’s image and reputation.

For the past four years, Mr. Audrerie has been a board member of a community organization that helps people become more employable.

Jean-Baptiste Audrerie holds a Master’s degree in Industrial and Organizational Psychology (MPs) from the Université de Poitiers (France) and an Executive MBA from the Université du Québec à Montréal and the Université Paris Dauphine.
