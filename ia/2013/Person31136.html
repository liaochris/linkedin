David Jasper's Summary

During my career working with major media brands, I have developed successful digital and video products that included strategies for programming, multi-screen distribution, and monetization. Through this experience, I have worked with IT and web development teams to standardize and integrate production workflows, encoding, streaming protocols and player technology. I collaborated with business and sales executives to implement marketing and monetization strategies including advertising, sponsorship, and syndication. I have also produced, directed and edited award winning video content with editorial and marketing colleagues.

In 2008, I completed a master’s degree in media studies at The New School where I focused on video production and web design. At the end of 2012, I finished a second master’s degree in information systems at Pace University concentrating on web and mobile video strategy.

Please check out my blog: webvideotrends.com and my site: davejasper.com.
