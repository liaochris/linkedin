Brenda Rohren's Summary

I have a life-long interest in advanced education and self-improvement, both personally and professionally. In addition, I enjoy passing my knowledge on to others. As I result, I taught at two local colleges for 18 years and have mentored and supervised numerous graduate students. Mangement experience includes at United Parcel Service and in outpatient and inpatient mental health organizations (CARF and TJC accredited). As a therapist with 28 years of experience, I have provided trauma-informed clinical services in all levels of care in mental health and substance abuse facilities (i.e.,outpatient through inpatient treatment). I am also a Veteran (Navy).

In recent years, my interests led to employment as a CARF surveyor, then to founding a management consulting company, Behavioral Health Resources, LLC.
