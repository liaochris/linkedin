Anthony Parent's Summary

The IRS (and state taxing agencies) can cause some of the most intense pain in the world. Our goal is to remove this pain as easily for our clients.

We understand that there is usually complicated reasons a tax problem occurred. Divorce, poor financial control, and lack of proper planning are the most common. So before we enter into a final resolution with the IRS, we want to make sure we addressed the root cause of the misery --- so that the problem will not occur again. We give our clients the tools to emerge from their nightmare not only more financially sound, but also, wiser.

Specialties

World-wide Off-shore Voluntary Disclosures (OVDI) of undeclared tax liabilities | Tax planning | Payroll tax negotiations | Tax audits and negotiations | Representation before the Connecticut Department of Revenue Services (DRS) |Offers-in-Compromise with the IRS and DRS | Back sales-tax negotiations | Bankruptcy consultation | Tax Penalty Abatement | Audit Reconsideration | Installment Agreements | Tax levy and lien removal | Tax Court | Speaking engagements | Radio and TV appearances |
