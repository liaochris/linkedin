Herbert Erlbach, CAPM, ITILv3's Summary

OVERVIEW
As a self-taught specialist, I have worked my way up the technology industry ladder in the corporate world for over 25 years. My passion for collaborating and teaching led me to a Regional Director of Technology position where I was responsible for the training of over 300 associates in the Chicagoland territory in addition to exhaustive work on national pilot projects and the oversight of branch technology managers.

Currently, I am an adjunct instructor at Computer Systems Institute (CSI) having taught Success Strategies and job search techniques, I now teach Excel with an Accounting emphasis. In November of 2010, I earned a Microsoft Office 2010 Masters Certification which includes Word Expert, Excel Expert, PowerPoint and Outlook. I had also the distinct honor of being CSI's keynote speaker at their first graduation ceremony and at their June 14, 2013 ceremony at their new Gurnee campus.

Teach Customer Service,Small Business Management, Personal Finance and Introduction to Network Foundations as well as being a Certified Project Manager with experience spanning hardware and software upgrades, orientations and training responsibilities. Excels as an individual contributor and in team leadership roles. Highly Experienced Application Support Technology Manager that is results driven, self-motivated in IT Support and Training. Solutions driven IT Professional leading change through the creation and implementation of end-user orientation and training materials in direct support of business objectives. Effective communicator able to explain complex concepts and issues to management. Outstanding public speaking skills and ability to motivate, energize, and lead to success.

Ability to create:
- Understandable presentations on topics relating to technology and software applications.
- Quick Reference Cards and Standard Practices

Specialties: Training, consulting, contact management, customer service, documentation, forms, internet software.
