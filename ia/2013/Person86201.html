Marius De Jager's Summary

I design brands.

I like to get my hands dirty, but dirty hands bug me.
I like to see the world as a beautiful place,
but realise that sometimes it needs a little help to become beautiful.
I question the world around me but understand that the answer mostly lies within me.
I design to entertain because we all need a little lightness in our lives.
I believe that black and white is totally underrated and life without colour is far too grey.
I design for a living but cannot live without design.
