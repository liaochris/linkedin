Laura Wilcox CFP MS's Summary

Your life can change in an instant. When it does, you need the caring support and guidance of someone who really gets it. With some financial education, unbiased advice and a plan, you will be able to make wise decisions and gain confidence about your future.

I work with women dealing with divorce, death, illness, job loss, retirement and other transitions. I provide unbiased financial and investment advice for an hourly fee.

Start with a free initial consultation - an opportunity to explain your situation and get some honest feedback - without committing to anything long-term and without the fear of being sold anything.

Simply call 414.529.5599 and ask for Rose.

My rate is $200/hour. A sliding scale is available for women in need.

Specialties: Women in Transition, Financial Education, Holistic Financial Planning, Unbiased Advice, Portfolio Manager Search-Selection-Monitoring. Registered Principal, Securities offered through Cambridge Investment Research, Inc., a Broker/Dealer, Member FINRA/SIPC. Investment Advisor Representative, Cambridge Investment Research Advisors, Inc., a Registered Investment Advisor. Cambridge and Wilcox Financial Advisors, s.c. are not affiliated.
