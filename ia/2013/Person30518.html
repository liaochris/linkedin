Dan Zukowski's Summary

I head the Automotive Practice at GolinHarris, where I manage the national Toyota account. My primary role is to provide thought leadership and senior counsel to client executive management. The world of automotive marketing and public relations has never been more challenging. I believe in leading my clients forward both creatively and strategically, guiding them through the exciting changes in the media and communications landscape and helping them stay one step ahead of the competition.

I lead and work with a team of very talented professionals. We engage with our clients across the entire spectrum of marketing and communications media, including paid, earned, shared and owned. Our work includes automotive marketing, corporate communications, product communications, CSR and community relations campaigns, corporate reputation, motorsports, social media and digital marketing.

Previously, I have headed major automotive and transportation accounts including Honda, Kawasaki and Suzuki, and have worked with other leading brands including Isuzu, Hyundai, Kia and Mitsubishi. I've also worked extensively on environmental issues, car sharing, telematics, advanced technology vehicles including fuel cell, hybrids and natural gas, along with intelligent transportation systems.

Taxis, subways and buses were my main modes of transportation growing up in New York City, so it’s no surprise that urban mobility is an area of special interest.

It's a thrill every day to take on these challenges and deliver innovative programs that generate measurable results. At heart, I’m a communicator, a creator and a counselor. And an inveterate optimist. I’m only satisfied at the end of the day if I’ve accomplished something. Preferably several, significant things.

    Team Members: Dan Zukowski, Micholl Azenon, Marissa Borjon

    Created sponsorship opportunity for Toyota with Latinos in Social Media (LATISM) in 2011, and returned in 2012 with extended presence. Program included activations during and beyond the conference, designed to engage Latino buyers in a culturally relevant way.

  • Global Debut: 2012 Toyota Camry

    • February 2011 to August 2011
    Team Members: Dan Zukowski

    A critical launch for Toyota's - and America's - best-selling car had to break through competitive noise and generate excitement among consumers, dealers, and the media. We devised a simultaneous global reveal strategy, originating from Los Angeles with a massive event at Paramount Studios in Hollywood. Press events in Detroit and New York were tied in by satellite, as was the Camry manufacturing plant in Georgetown, KY where Toyota president Akio Toyoda drove the first vehicle off the line. The event was webcast live around the world, with consumers, dealers and Toyota associates invited to watch. More than two billion media impressions were generated, consumer interest spiked on car shopping sites, and the vehicle launched to strong sales.

  • Toyota "100 Cars for Good" Program

    • February 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Dan Zukowski

  • Product Launch: Toyota Entune

    Team Members: Dan Zukowski, Rebecca Lee

    Toyota intended to debut its new multimedia system, Entune, at the 2011 Consumer Electronics Show. We crafted a detailed plan to engage media leading up to and during CES, while educating auto industry analysts via a live preview webcast. Entune won two best-of-show awards, and we continued to support the launch with follow-up events both standalone and integrated with vehicle press previews.
