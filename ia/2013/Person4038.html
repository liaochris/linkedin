Alex Hogan's Summary

The most effective User Interface is one that is indistinguishable from the user's natural workflow and interaction. This Natural User Interface (NUI) allows users to emerse themselves in the content, rather than having to negotiate or learn a Graphical User Interface (GUI). Imagine the possibilities that brings.

As a User Experience Evangelist for the web, mobile and beyond, I have the opportunity to research and set into implementation some of the most interesting technological and user experience solutions in the market. My focus on digital strategies and the research that accompanies allows me to gain an indepth understanding of the business and the opportunities for experiential changes that provide measurable returns.

I've had the pleasure of working with organizations large and small some of which are; CenturyLink, Lowe's Home Improvement, IHS, Pizza Hut, Primerica, 7-Eleven, Prilosec, Olay, Max Factor, Travelocity, TDAmeritrade and IBM, in both strategic and architectural capacities.
