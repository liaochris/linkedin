Jacky Abromitis' Summary

Continuing Education for Teachers (A Division of Abromitis Online Learning) has specialized in developing online teacher education courses and programs and developing undergraduate courses. Our goal is to help colleges and universities expand their online course offerings with pedagogically sound yet affordable e-learning materials. We partner with universities in the US to offer practical and engaging accredited courses.

Abromitis Online Learning provides course and program development, consulting, and training to colleges and universities who wish to expand their distance learning skills and offerings.

Founder/CEO - Fanfic.me. Fans write stories about their favorite TV shows, movies, books, cartoons, and more. We provide archiving software for writers and readers to network around shared interests. Fanfic.me is our third generation of fanfiction software, based on 8 years of fan feedback. Fanfic.me is a startup, and we are seeking funding. The software can also be utilized in K-16 education in secured environments.

Jacky Abromitis' Experience

President

Abromitis Online Learning

January 2000 – Present (13 years 8 months) NJ & MA

Abromitis Online Learning is headed up by Dr. Jacquelyn G. Abromitis. Jacky's professional roots are in teaching. She was a classroom teacher for 18 years as an Associate Professor of English at Middlesex County College in Edison, NJ. Since 1997, her focus has been on melding education and the Internet.

Jacky earned her doctorate in Instructional Technology and Distance Education in December 2002. Jacky has been the lead instructional designer and project manager on the development of over 35 online classes in seven disciplines.

Abromitis Online Learning is available to help colleges and universities increase their online catalogs through either our Ready for Adoption courses and programs or our Custom Developed learning materials. Visit http://www.abromitis.com for more information.

In 2012, Abromitis Online Learning created a separate division for their continuing education/professional development courses, named CONTINUING EDUCATION FOR TEACHERS (www.ce4teachers.com). Continuing Education for Teachers partners with fully accredited, highly reputable universities to offer practicing teachers practical and useful continuing ed/prof dev courses, fully online.

Associate Professor of English

Middlesex County College

Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Higher Education industry

September 1989 – June 2005 (15 years 10 months)

I went from Adjunct to Assistant Professor to Associate Professor of English. I taught all developmental and freshman English courses plus several literature courses. I became very interested in what we used to call "computer assisted" learning, which eventually lead to e-learning. I was very active in departmental, divisional, and college-wide committees. Eventually, I returned to school for my Ed.D. in Instructional Technology and Distance Learning. I began working as a consultant, developing and delivering online courses and programs for colleges and universities. Abromitis Online Learning, the precursor of Abromitis E-Learning, was born. I left the college in June 2005 to dedicate myself to Abromitis E-Learning.
