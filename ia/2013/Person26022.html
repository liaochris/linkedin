Heather Ludwig's Summary

"Opening Life's Potential Through Coaching"

As a Professional Coach, I work with managers and students who want to be more effective, confident and excited about their work and career.

Want a sample of how coaching may help you? Call me 952-221-0035.

Want to read some testimonials of my work? Check out www.keytorenew.com Success Stories.

I join my client relationships as a confidante, a sounding board, a reality check, and an accountability partner. I may work with you for one situation or over a period of several months. The outcome is about the new you...with clarity, focus, a plan, presence, or even more basic, improved relationships with others.

My clients who get the most out of coaching come in ready to move beyond their past, get connected with "now", and blaze some trails forward. I enjoy working with clients in any industry as my business experience spans 20 years with a variety of companies in a variety of industries.

I enjoy working with students, helping them think about their career path and prepare for the transition from school to work.
