Steve Ganz's Summary

A southern Belize location, ideally situated on the Caribbean Sea near the Garifuna village of Hopkins in the Stann Creek District, Hopkins Bay Villas is close to it all.

As the closest mainland Belize property to the longest barrier reef in the Western Hemisphere, we offer you unparalleled access to some of the best scuba diving, snorkeling and fishing in the world. You can also explore ancient Mayan ruins, canoe or kayak on a jungle river, go birding in the rainforest, or just relax in a hammock on the beach. At Hopkins Bay Villas adventure abounds.

Our private five-star Belize villas offer the ultimate luxury oceanfront setting in a lush tropical paradise. Nowhere else on Belize’s mainland can you experience such a supreme level of comfort and luxury.

Belize is an underwater paradise. With exceptional clarity and visibility, divers reveal in vast gardens of shallow coral reef, as well as steep drop offs, shelves and walls. These azure waters also boast caves, grottos, and amazing atolls that are home to a profusion of beautiful marine life; all waiting to be explored in waters that average 79 degrees. Just steps away, certified PADI instructors and dive masters stand ready to guide you to some of the best diving sites in the world.

Whether you want to go snorkeling Belize’s Barrier Reef with its beautiful coral and vibrant marine life; take a fly or reef Belize fishing trip; or cruise one of Belize’s rivers observing exotic birds and wildlife, we offer a tour just for you. Belizean guides are the best in the business; their sole objective is to ensure your comfort and safety and to assist you in getting the most out of your adventure experience.

The national parks of Belize rain forest encompass natural habitats for wildlife such as tapir, jaguar, monkeys, snakes and crocodiles. Even though all of the above animals have been seen by our guests, we cannot guarantee that any specific wildlife will be spotted during your adventures.
