Michael Korfhage's Summary

Nashville based illustrator and designer interested in working with people from all walks of life. I am working to expand my portfolio to include not only printed media but also textiles and art licensing.

Experienced in working with clients ranging from large companies and publications to individuals and non profits. Described as self-motivated, energetic, enthusiastic and willing to learn.
