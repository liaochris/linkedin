Kay Fittes' Summary

Kay Fittes is a national speaker, trainer, consultant, coach and author on issues for women in the workplace. Kay leads women in overcoming behaviors that block their career success. She holds a certification in Leadership Training and created numerous Leadership training series for women.

Specialties: Keynote speaking for women's conferences and associations, corporate and non-profit on-site training for women in the workplace, High-Heeled Success® coaching for women, women's success public seminars

Kay Fittes' Experience

Founder

High-Heeled Success, LLC

July 2011 – Present (2 years 3 months) Cincinnati

I show career-oriented women who struggle with letting go of behaviors that hold back their careers, (i.e. over commitment, perfectionism and lack of focus) simple ways to experience career breakthroughs.

Owner

Strategies for Women's Growth

August 1990 – July 2011 (21 years)

Kay Fittes leads women to High-Heeled Success® in the workplace. Through keynotes, training and coaching she guides women in developing skills to overcome self-inhibiting behaviors in their careers.

Educational Specialist, Community Relations Liaison,Support Group Coordinator
