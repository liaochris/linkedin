Jurriejan Smits' Summary

"Translating ideas into feasible IT solutions", that's what I do generally. For me, the challenge is not to finish paperwork but to see satisfied owners AND users of (complex) IT systems.

I take freelance assignments in the following areas:
- Gathering and filtering requirements, process management
- Functional design, bussiness/application/software architecture
- Project execution, (agile) project management
- Custom training
- Hands-on activities on short notice

Key characteristics:
- Fast learning
..understanding business and politics quickly and take that
into account
- Solution driven
..focus on business goals as well as the end-users
- Creative
..in avoiding and solving problems
- Common sense
..together with experience, this guarantees a realistic view
on complex projects

Recent experience:
- Project management for implementation of a single sign on solution for a an e-learning publisher (planning of development teams, testing, customer service activities)
- Interim ICT management on a small university (develop ICT strategy plan, daily operations, process improvement, develop ICT service catalog)
- Program management (business process inventory and project definition of several ICT projects)
- Project management / consulting for implementation of large SharePoint environments

Specialties
