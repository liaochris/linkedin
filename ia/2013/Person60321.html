Joseph Cunningham's Summary

Learning is my number one goal in both school and the real world.

Began career in journalism after being recruited to the Northwestern State University student-run newspaper. Became editor-in-chief in 2009. Left there for a news director and on-air personality position at Elite Radio Group full-time. After a combined three years in radio, went back to print, but as a sports editor in south Louisiana.

Specialties include writing, editing, analysis, political news, commentary and social media.
