Damion Lewin's Summary

Professional Experience:

Career began in animation working as a 2D/3D compositor for cartoons. This opened the door for overseeing cartoon productions abroad in China, South Korea, France, and the UK. Worked exclusively with the creator of Nickelodeon's Avatar on his film, Atomic Love.

Moved animation talents into the DVD Menu industry. Art directed, designed, and animated many A Title DVD menus for Disney, Warner Bros., Morgan Creek, Universal, Bluetorch TV, and more. Won several Tellys Awards for Alias Season 1 and Season 2 DVD Menus. Won several BDA awards for the Bluetorch TV DVD menus.

Progressed into broadcast motion graphics. Creative Director on numerous network branding, show promos,and title sequences. Clients include: ABC, CBS, Fox, Sony, Discovery Channel, TLC, Animal Planet, Military Channel, History Channel, Spike TV, Directv, National Geographic Channel, Academy Awards and others.

Directed numerous green screen promo spots and title sequences. Designed packages, created 3D, and compositor on many broadcast projects.

Won 6 BDA awards for branding campaigns for TLC.

Currently, Freelancing as a Director/Designer/Animator on various broadcast and theatrical projects. Active as a Visual Effects Supervisor on green screen promo shoots and music videos.

My Goals:

To continue to direct teams and push their design and expand their knowledge of branding. Along with directing green screen and add my two cents to the infinite realm of motion graphics/visual effects in film and television.


Specialties: Directing, Creative Directing, Branding, Post Supervising, Animating, Designing, Compositing, 3D, Camera.
