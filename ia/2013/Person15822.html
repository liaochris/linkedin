Borja Berzosa's Summary

I have developed my professional experience in the online marketing world, starting in sales marketing en e-business solutions for IBM and joining Telefonica for the launch of the onine Yellow Pages, working in verticals of the online directory and in new technologies. During that time I participated in several projects of the Telefonica Group like the launch of the information number 11888 and the preparation of the white book of Telefonica.

In 2005 I joined Google working at the European Headquartes in Dublin, Ireland. While I was there I was selected in 2007 to work as European Ambassador in the American Headquarters in San Francisco with the Agency Team. On October 2009 I joined the team of the Madrid Office working with Agencies in order to develop the online presence of their customers.

In 2012 I joined the Google office in Santiago de Chile to help in the launch of the Agency Team and launch the program Google University with the two main Universities in Santiago.

Back in Madrid in 2013 I work in the Google agency team managing main spanish SEMs and working as Account Manager in Big 6.

Besides since 2010 I started working as a teacher delivering clases of Internet Marketing at the Master de Internet Business (MIB) and Escuela Superior de Negocios y Tecnologia (ESDEN), both in Madrid and Barcelona.

Specialties: Online Marketing, presentation skills, data analysis
