Justin Flechsig's Summary

I've only just moved to the Buffalo area, and I'm currently considering career opportunities within marketing, project, and brand management. I have an extensive background in computer programming, modeling, and analytics.

-----

I completed a BS in computer science at Binghamton University, where I graduated magna cum laude. Ultimately, I did not believe a career based solely in programming was for me, so I attended the Simon School of Business at the University of Rochester. While at Simon, I completed my MBA in marketing and brand management and served as a statistics and modeling workshop leader. I'm equally comfortable with the qualitative and quantitative aspects of marketing, and believe that it is necessary to excel at both to be successful.

I was most recently employed as a marketing and operations manager at KDF where I managed online and print advertising. Additionally, my role included business protocol revision, wherein I identified possible areas of operational improvement and managed their implementation. It is unfortunate to leave behind a job I enjoyed, but my relocation to Buffalo is an opportunity to explore new career paths.
