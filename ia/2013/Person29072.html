Dan & Tish Abraham's Summary

Like the Sacramento area itself, Dan & Tish are a unique blend, as well. When you combine Dan’s driven personality & energetic demeanor with Tish’s creativity, low-key style & caring approach, they are living proof of the old adage that “opposites attract.” “What we’ve found is a perfect balance,” Dan says. “When we work together toward a shared goal, there’s nothing we can’t achieve.”

Before attending college in Sacramento, Dan grew up in a rural part of Wisconsin with the local countryside as his own playground. He’s always been passionate about the outdoors—swimming, riding motorcycles, fishing—and loves that you don’t have to go far outside Sacramento to find great adventures. Tish was raised right here in this area & knows there’s no other place she’d rather be with her family. Both were instilled with strong family values & nothing is more important to them than their four children. They cherish every moment they share as a family, whether it’s a day in the park or a trip to the pizza parlor. As one of the Sacramento area’s premier real estate teams, Dan & Tish’s approach blends many key elements. Their steadfast family values & strong community spirit keep them inspired to make an impact in the lives of the people they work with. And, their perfect blend of personalities, skills and attention to detail ensure all of their clients' needs are well taken care of.

Dan & Tish are focused on what matters most to you, and their teamwork is second to none. With Dan providing the motivation leadership to reach your goals and Tish providing the detailed organization, personal care and creative solutions, you get the benefit of two dedicated professionals who understand your needs and won’t quit until the job is done right for you. Together, their combined skills give you a distinct advantage whether buying or selling. Count on two real estate professionals who are The Perfect Blend for Your Success. Call Dan & Tish today.
