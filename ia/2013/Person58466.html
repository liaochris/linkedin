Janine Toro's Summary

I am a designer, art director and an educator. I am a recent graduate of SVA's Designer as Author + Entrepreneur program. I'm passionate about all things design and sharing my knowledge of design with others. To me no matter what the project design is a tool to solve the problem and visually communicate.

Specialities: conceptualization, ideation, graphic design, page and typographic layout, design consulting, creative strategy, design thinking, design education, user experience, visual concept for web and apps including information and structure with basic skills in HTML &CSS.

Available for off-site freelance projects.
