Brent S. Rice's Summary

I have a 25 year background in real estate and real estate finance working with large and small financial institutions/commercial banks, privately-held workout firms, various Wall Street companies as well as my own business serving individuals, private groups and corporate clients.

My team provides both comprehensive and objective-based consulting and transaction management related to acquisitions, dispositions, asset evaluations, market studies, note portfolio due diligence, asset/debt recovery strategies and execution including opportunities for discounted purchases, loan modification, property management, and short sales of residential and commercial real estate. I hold a Texas real estate broker license (#0395896).

As a Private Client Agent, I work with referrals from past clients and high net worth individuals along with their financial planners/CPAs/Agents on a case by case basis for specific residential real estate matters to combine temporary needs such as short term corporate housing and relocation services to longer term property search, acquisition, listing/staging/disposition, as well as securing and optimizing mortgage financing.

I provide financing and consulting in private aviation.........from private charter, jet card membership programs, fractional ownership and/or whole aircraft ownership.

Specialties: Residential/Commercial Real Estate Transaction Management including brokerage, negotiation, financing, coordinating closings and fundings.
