Joe Tradii's Summary

In his current role, Joe leads a team responsible for defining and addressing market needs by developing differentiated products, creating compelling value propositions and marketing campaigns, providing overall strategic marketing direction, and driving solid returns on marketing investments.

Before joining Intuicom, Joe spent several years at Microsoft as Senior Brand Manager. There he created and managed various marketing initiatives across the company related to the enterprise market, cloud services and servers, leading cross-functional teams which included members from research, campaign measurement, advertising, and product management. Joe received both the Microsoft MVP Award and Team Collaboration Award.

Joe has held senior marketing and leadership positions in product marketing, channel strategy, business development, and brand strategy with Verizon Wireless, AT&T/Cingular Wireless, and LizardTech Software.

Joe is an internationally recognized authority on marketing. His ability to think outside the box and unique perspectives have been highlighted in leading industry journals including Brandweek, The Marketer, Visions Journal of Product Management and the International Journal of Branding.

During his tenure with some of the world’s most-recognized advertising agencies, his award-winning campaigns garnered Belding, ANDY, Clio, Creative Arts Annual, and ProComm honors. His campaign for Ocean Pacific Sportswear is part of the Library of Congress' permanent collection of popular culture.

Joe received his MBA, with an emphasis in marketing, from the University of California at Irvine, and is a frequent lecturer on the topics of marketing and branding.

Specialties: Top-tier award winning leader effective in boosting revenue and driving market share growth. Experience in leading high-performance teams to drive opportunity identification, managing cross-team strategic planning, and overseeing successful execution.
