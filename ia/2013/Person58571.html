Jan Richards' Summary

I work with people at companies, large and small, to grow profitability, improve the ease of operations and increase customer satisfaction while reducing complexity and costs.

We get this done in many ways, including by: 
- Visioning
- Strategic Planning
- Prioritizing
- Action planning 
- Designing and improving key business processes
- Creating user-friendly process management practices
- Implementation coaching
- Mentoring
 - Setting teams up for success on challenging, high-risk projects

Before starting my consulting business, I worked at Apple Computer, evaluating and improving key business processes in product development, manufacturing, distribution, finance and administration, and sales and marketing.

My education includes an MBA from UC Berkeley and a BS in journalism from Iowa State.

I was also a five-year national examiner for the Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Award, an award funded by the US Department of Commerce to recognize full-organization performance excellence (in business, health care, education, and non-profit organizations) and its importance for national performance and competitiveness.

Specialties

Assessment, visioning, prioritizing, strategic planning, action planning, process design, process improvement, strategic management, lean management, lean six sigma, business process improvement, service excellence, customer satisfaction, metrics, process improvement training, project management, facilitation, coaching, mentoring, change management, Baldrige, world class quality, performance excellence, leadership, knowledge management, profitability improvements, business results, author.
