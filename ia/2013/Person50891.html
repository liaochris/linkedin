James Shelton's Summary

Here at Guit Strips, you have a world of choices when it comes to custom guitar straps. We offer a diversity of options that is unmatched by our competitors. Our one on one personal attention to client relationships offers us the unique opportunity to create truly one of a kind leatherworks. As a small family business, we strive to offer unparalleled service to our valued customers.

We make it easy for you to design your custom guitar strap. Here are just some of our strap options:
•You can specify width, length, adjustable or fixed length
•Leather types
•Exotic skins
•Tooling designs (hand carving)
•Buckle sets
•Decorative additions (conchos, rivets, spots, spikes, etc…)
•Biker style
•Colors and finishes
•Smooth or suede backing / padding
•Several styles of hand stitching and lacing

Each one of our personalized guitar straps is made completely by hand of rugged 100% natural leather for a lifetime of enjoyment. All of our custom guitar straps are completely unique. While we offer many suggestions, ideas, and photos for you to brainstorm from, in the end your new strap will be as individual as you are.
