Blooming Butterflies, Inc., A Non Profit Company

February 2011 – Present (2 years 8 months) 10248 Westheimer Road, Houston, Texas 77042

Blooming Butterflies * A Texas Non Profit Organization To promote the economic independence of disadvantaged women, teens, children and senior citizens by providing professional attire, a network of support and the career development tools to help women start a new beginning in their New Life.

Blooming Butterflies is an organization offering services designed to help battered women and senior citizens find jobs and remain employed. Each client receives one suit or dress when she has a job interview and can return for a second suit, dress or separates when she finds work.

Blooming Butterflies serves women by shelter referral confidentially, and women must have an interview scheduled before receiving clothing. Many of our women come to us from a continually expanding and diverse group of non-profit organizations including homeless shelters, immigration services, job training programs, educational institutions and domestic violence shelters, among many other organizations.

Blooming Butterflies works with the referred woman utilizing her first visit as the most impressionable one. We provide a woman with a suit or clothing attire appropriate for the industry in which she is interviewing and, if available, accessories. After a woman finds a job a second appointment is set up with Blooming Butterflies for additional clothing that can be mixed and matched to make several outfits, providing her with the confident foundation for her new career wardrobe.

While providing professional attire to women, it is our goal to build the employment retention programs that will be the cornerstone of the organization. Soon after Blooming Butterflies was founded we came to recognize that a woman must first have confidence in herself and her appearance. The next step would be a woman's journey towards economic independence; remaining employed and building a rewarding career. These are essential goals if a woman is to become self-sufficient.
