January 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months) Kiev

3D Solutions is a VR consultant. We consult in the usage of modern communication tools in improving design and sales processes in real estate development to:

- attract investors
- faster decision making
- make better designs
- sell property off plan

Company is based in the Ukraine and have sales offices in Ukraine, the Netherlands and Dubai.

Our goal is help clients realize how communication budgets can be optimized using the latest (3d) technologies to reach the right spectators.

Our company has been able to create added value network in Europe and Middle East of specialists and companies that share this vision in real estate communication.It covers on one hand branding, marketing, PR, real estate agencies, architects & project managers.

We've been involved in more than 60 real estate projects.
