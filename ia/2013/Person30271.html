Danny Pouladian's Summary

Danny Pouladian currently serves as a director at Blue Line Media, a provider of national outdoor advertising media.

::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::
Blue Line Media :: www.BlueLineMedia.com :: 800-807-0360
::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::

Blue Line Media provides outdoor advertising (such as billboard advertising) in 300 cities across the United States.

The company focuses on research, analysis and placement of national outdoor advertising media.

Advertisers rely on Blue Line Media for its deep expertise in the out-of-home advertising world to build their brands and engage their consumers.

Blue Line Media works with many clients and their ad agencies nationwide -- from start-ups to Fortune 500's across a wide variety of industries, including consumer goods, healthcare, retail, education, financial services, manufacturing, transportation, software and technology.

Clients include PETA, Box Tops for Education, Humana, Circle K, the CDC, Dept of Health and Human Services, the College Board, NASA and many others.

Offered outdoor ad media includes:

- Billboard Ads
- Transit Ads
- Bus Ads
- Bus Shelter Ads
- Street Ads
- Bench Ads
- Mall Ads
- Airport Ads
- Taxi Ads
- Gas Station Ads
- Rail-Subway Ads


Learn about the outdoor media options by visiting www.BlueLineMedia.com or calling 800-807-0360.
