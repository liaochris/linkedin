Kevin Drost's Summary

Kevin is an Engagement Manager with Prophet, a global strategy firm focusing on brand, marketing and innovation.

He brings over ten years of experience developing growth strategies for clients ranging from Fortune 500 corporations to start-ups. This includes defining growth opportunities, developing go-to-market strategies, brand portfolio decisions, brand valuation, financial modeling and competitive analysis. As a musician and former record label executive, Kevin specializes in balancing creativity with rigorous analysis to drive inspired ideas that deliver real business results. Current and past clients include eBay/PayPal, ESPN, ConocoPhillips, Disney, IBM, Sony, Dun and Bradstreet and the New York Times.

Prior to joining Prophet, Kevin worked as a Managing Consultant in IBM’s Growth and Innovation Strategy Consulting Practice. Before entering consulting, Kevin served as President of Zealous Records, LLC, a recorded music company which he founded. Kevin oversaw all elements of operations, marketing, strategy and finance leading to an eventual partnership with the largest entertainment firm in the world. Prior, he designed and implemented the international distribution strategy for a high-profile record label and artist management firm. He began his career at Sony Music Entertainment in London and New York City.

Kevin holds an MBA from New York University’s Stern School of Business, graduating in the top 10 percent of his class.

Specialties: Marketing, Strategy, Production, Digital Media, Contract Negotiation, Business Development,Financial Analysis, Operations, Social Media Analytics, Predictive Modeling, Advanced Analytics, Business Case Development, Cloud based business models, Big Data
