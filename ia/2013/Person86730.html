Mark Jordan Photography's Summary

I am Mark Jordan of Mark Jordan Photography, Orange County's leading family portrait and headshot Photographer. From the pages of People magazine to the halls and walls of office and home, my portraits have expressed the life-styles of American families since 1981.

I am a Master Photographer and Photographic Craftsman. I am a member of the American Society of Photographers and the Photography Hall of Fame, where my recreation of Georges Seurat’s “La Grande Jatte” was installed alongside Ansel Adams in the museum. Only a relative few artists, worldwide, have been inducted as a permanent exhibitor.

In addition to being named O.C. Photographer of the Year, I am also an eleven time recipient of the coveted Int'l. Loan Collection Award. Prominent organizations such as Professional Photographers of America, Norway, Germany, Kodak, Fuji, and EPCOT Center (three time honoree in the "World's Greatest Photographers" exhibit) have honored my work.

A few notable companies that have engaged my services are Tiffany, Virgin, Oakley, Bijan, Toyota, Boston Red Socks, Angels, Marriott, ClubCorp, Predictive Technologies, AVCO, Viacom, Pacific Mutual, Boston Pharmaceuticals, Blockbuster, Bourns, Gensler & Associates, O.C. Performing Arts Center, and the Actors and Productions of Tony Award winning South Coast Repertory.

More importantly, every single honor and award that I have ever earned has come directly from commissioned work. I owe my success to my clients - it all began with simple inquiries and continues today because of those who honor me with their trust.

My clients tell me what sets me apart is my flair for capturing their personality, genuinely and artistically. Whether I'm photographing their family or creating their headshot, they are amazed not only with the extent I'll go to elicit something special about them, but how I make it "fun" and "painless." I think what they sense is that I am not so much passionate about photography as I am about people.
