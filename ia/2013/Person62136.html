Jenny Westerkamp's Summary

Jenny Westerkamp, RD is a holistic-minded registered dietitian promoting whole food nutrition principles through consulting, speaking, writing, and tweeting.

In Chicago, Jenny is the nutritionist for CJK Foods, a prepared meal service featuring organic, local, gluten-free, dairy-free, and superfood ingredients. Previously, Jenny was the Senior Sports Nutritionist for SportFuel, Inc. where she consulted one-on-one with professional athletes and active people, including the Chicago Blackhawks, Rockford Icehogs, and NBA Development League. In November 2012, she released You Are What You Retweet: 140 Social Media Rules to Eat By.

She is currently the President of Chicago Food and Nutrition Network and a member of Weston A. Price Foundation and the American Nutrition Association.

As a senior in college, Jenny founded All Access Internships, a website dedicated to meeting the needs of the dietetics student community now offering one-on-one coaching and in-person workshops across the country. She has written e-books including Social Media and the RD (Skelly Publishing), Getting Matched: A Guide for Dietetics Students, and Interns and the RD: Develop a Win-Win Internship Program For Your Business.

Jenny graduated with high honors from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign where she earned a Bachelor’s degree in Food Science and Human Nutrition. She was honored as the most outstanding dietetics student and also was the recipient of the Senior Honorary 100 for the top 100 seniors at the university in academic excellence, outstanding leadership, and unique contributions to the university. In 2009, she was awarded the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics Nutrition Entrepreneurs Student of the Year. Jenny completed her dietetics training at Massachusetts General Hospital.

She enjoys practicing yoga, tweeting fun nutrition facts, and eating organic eggs with bacon. She is the eldest in her family with five younger brothers.
