Felicia Thomas' Summary

My young adult novel, 80 Proof Lives, is geared toward youth ages 13-18, although adults like it, too. In fact, several schools in my area are using it in the classroom as a teaching tool. I'm writing the sequel to 80 Proof Lives, as well as a another young adult novel, a murder mystery, a legal thriller, and the other titles that have been on my heart for years. My goals also include developing a writing program around 80 Proof Lives, in which I infuse creativity into FCAT writing. My program has the proven ability to assist students in improving their FCAT writing scores. I have a passion for reading and writing; I'd like to see our youth become as passionate.
