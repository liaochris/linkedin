Marcel van Wiggen's Summary

I have 13 years experience in management in several fields of expertise. E.g. strategic management, productivity improvement, production, quality and project management. I have lead everything between small specialistic teams and a large business unit with close to 1000 employees.

Since 2010 I am working as a advisor and trainer within numerous companies to improve performance and employee satisfaction. Our training programs consist of the combination of business experience and extensive knowledge about human behavior.

Our focus is on the connection between results and human behavior. What makes people succeed?

Specialties: to unleash the potential in people, create energy within teams and to establish clear goals and actionplans to ensure that intentions are put into action.
