Ed Harrold MBA, BScEE's Summary

Ed has worked in National Defense since he was a teenager (17) and, is a past member of the US Army, and following years in the Intelligence community, plus the Space and Aviation sector. He received the National Defense Medal, and various badges for his expertise with weapons. He learned martial arts at a young age (Judo+Kung Fu), and studied Keichu-Do with his kids including lessons from the famous Karl Marx (Soke' Karl - the founder!) Ed's work brought him side-by-side with some of the most brilliant and talented people in the world. Projects include Space Shuttle, Space Station, and Intercontinental Ballistic Missile Systems. Military projects resulted in technology used by the famous General Norman Schwarzkopf in his ground campaign; some still in use today.

His career encompasses high-tech, engineering, information systems, and software development industries. Awards and Kudos include Honeywell Engineer of the Year award and recognition from Siemens Energy and Automation and Square-D Company for improving B2B & B2C with cutting edge middle-ware solutions. Ed brings uncompromising leadership, experience, and capabilities working with internal and external customers while creating, managing, and securing what they need for success - getting the right information to the right place at the right time!

Specialties: Cryptography and credential management including RSA PKCS, PBKDF2, AES, and implementations in C#, Objective-C, and Java. Mobile solutions architecture (iOS, Android), infrastructure, systems design, integration with SOAP, MCF, REST and Sync/Async web services (ASP.NET, Java, Lamp stack). Ed is a recognized leader / manager and a proven architect/developer/troubleshooter. He embraces a balance of discipline and agility while managing projects and teams with an eye towards IT governance, software engineering, operations management, technology strategy, and business process architecture.
