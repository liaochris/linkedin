Bill Wolfe's Summary

My name is Bill Wolfe and I specialize in the California brokerage sector. I began my career in the insurance industry and I’ve been recruiting for the last 20 years. My expertise is placing property and casualty and employee benefits professionals using my relationships with California's top brokerages.

Are you seeking career growth? Do you want to work for a larger broker? Do you have a difficult commute? Would a challenging environment help you advance? Are you a producer seeking a more rewarding commission structure?

I have placed over 1,000 candidates including, Account Managers, Account Executives, Producers, and Managers.

We founded Insurance Search Specialists to bring together a like-minded team dedicated to providing a personal and comprehensive search experience for our clients and candidates.

1. Our team works as one to help both clients and candidates achieve their goals.
2. Our team has actual insurance industry experience.
3. Our team is California based and meets with the clients and candidates we represent.
4. Our team has over 1,000 placements and 20 years of insurance recruiting experience.
5. Our team has developed an enviable in-house database enabling us to pinpoint the right talent.

We are Insurance people, not sales people. We are not focused on quotas or commission.

Because our team meets with our clients and understands the various cultures, we are able to help candidates meet the right firms and make great career choices.

Our success comes from those choices.
