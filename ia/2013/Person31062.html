David Fagan's Summary

Who I am
--------------------
I'm a man of habit, especially when it comes to food. I'm a music junky, my iPod is full and my guitar well used. I don't mind solitude, I can always keep busy. I could be a professional golfer, if I wasn't consistently inconsistent. I've always wished I had a superpower, but can't answer which one. I love to work out, I can spend hours at the gym. I'm a decent cook, but don't ask me to do dishes. I love to laugh, so much so that I can barely finish a joke. I can solve a Rubik's Cube without peeling off the stickers. I'm a movie fanatic, I'll watch anything. I've got more nicknames than I can keep track of. I love dogs AND cats, beer AND wine, creative AND technical. I'm my own kind of geek.

What I do
---------------------
My colleagues call me The Hybrid, because I can effectively blend design aesthetics with technical development. One self taught/learned, one formally studied. But in my experiences over the years, I've grown to wear many additional hats. Both out of necessity and desire. So whether it's creative design or complex coding, full websites or mobile apps, timely production or strategic brainstorming, I'm one who strives on challenges and breaking boundaries, and loves every second of it.

Specialties

web design and development, object oriented programming, scripting languages, mobile website strategy & design/development, iPhone app design & development, branding and logo design, team leadership, user interface design
