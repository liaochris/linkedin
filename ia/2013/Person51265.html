Johnny G | Hair by Johnny G's Summary

Johnny G is a Celebrity Hair Designer, Life Stylist, Speaker, and the Inspirational Author of "Creating Beauty From The Inside Out…Lessons on Inner & Outer Beauty" who teaches that anything is possible.

Johnny began his career at the prestigious Goldwell Academy in London and Baltimore where he achieved recognition as a Certified National Educator. Upon completion, Johnny excelled at the Toni and Guy Advanced Hairdressing Academy program in Arizona. He continues to update his knowledge, most recently studying in Beverly Hills at the J. Beverly Hills Training Academy.

He has won numerous national makeover and color awards including: Excellence in Education, Goldwell Educator; Third Place in the National TIGI Make-Over Magic Contest; achieved TIGI Top Ten Stylists nationwide; and First Place in the National Goldwell Color Contest.

Johnny has appeared as a guest beauty expert on television programs such as the Morning Exchange and Good Morning Arizona with Beverly Kidd.
.
The vision for his salon, "Hair by Johnny G", is to focus 100% on every client when they are in his chair. Johnny creates hairstyles that fit an individual’s personality, career and place in life.

As an award-winning cut and color expert, Johnny sees the beauty in his clients that they don’t see themselves. Clients often say, “Johnny G. is the first man in the world who REALLY listens.”

In 2011 Johnny wrote "Creating Beauty From The Inside Out" to help others overcome adversity by understanding the keys to “My Best Self” which includes how to positively impact the mind and the thoughts we create; how we project ourselves to the world; and our spirit, which is the deep and universal expression of our total life.

Johnny G | Hair by Johnny G's Publications

    • JG Publishers

    Authors: Johnny G | Hair by Johnny G

    The 50+ tips in this book will give you fresh new ideas about hair, color, salon-styling and products to use in order to always look and feel your best.

    Beauty is more than just physical appearance. It’s about taking the beauty that lies within your heart and soul and radiating that outward to everyone you meet. It’s about understanding the truth of who you really are. It’s about being in harmony in all dimensions of your life in order to create true beauty. That’s why Johnny believes in (and uses) a unique Beverly Hills 3D Hair Design technique that encompasses all of the elements of your life while helping you achieve your outer beauty. This technique is revealed and explained in Creating Beauty from the Inside-Out.

    Your First Step in Creating a New You
    Before entering a salon for the first time, let the hair stylist (or Life Stylist as Johnny likes to call it), understand and uncover your true personal expression of life by providing the tools to successfully work with you. (Each of these tools is detailed and described in this book.)

    A Life Stylist considers all elements of your life when helping you to achieve outer beauty. Such aspects include divorce, loss of a loved one, a new job, pending marriage, and health issues. These elements are all part of your personal life story.

    Your physical appearance also comes into play in determining your new look. Factors to consider are:

    – The shape of your face, head and body.
    – How a new cut or color will impact your natural hair, skin and eye tones.

    Through his book, Johnny will touch your heart and help you bring out your unique splendor. Get Creating Beauty from the Inside-Out today!
