Michael Short's Summary

Products and Services
We offer a wide variety of marketing services to promote your brand and drive sales for your products and services. 3rd Street Marketing gives you much more than beautifully designed creative pieces, you get proven experience and a full-service marketing partner to help your business succeed.

Web Services
3rd Street specializes in complete websites, web banners, and Search Engine Optimization (SEO). Whether your goals are to develop a new website, enhance an existing website, generate more leads, or close more business online, we have a solution to accomplish your goals.

Corporate Identities
The first step to a successful brand starts with a well designed corporate identity. From logo design, letterhead and envelopes, to business cards, presentation folders, and labels, we can help you establish an identity that defines your business and seperates you from your competition.

Flyers & Postcards
We give your flyers, handouts, and postcards the marketing perspective required to achieve results. Drawing from many years of experience in direct mail campaigns, we can help you create effective mailings that will heighten your brand’s awareness, generate leads, and lay the foundation for increased revenues.

Brochures & Catalogs
We create brochures and catalogs that will enhance your image and create response. Our experienced design team knows what it takes to create a dynamic cover, where to place your call-to-actions, and how to design an aesthetically pleasing piece that will clearly state your most important message.

Print Advertising
Developing a compelling ad goes beyond a pretty picture. At 3rd Street, we understand what it takes to create an ad that effectively communicates your message, builds your brand and drives response.
