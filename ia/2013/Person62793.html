Jerry Reed's Summary

Experience - Professional voiceover artist and public/media relations specialist. His voiceovers have been described as having a "warm tone and a natural finish." Jerry's clients have included: Welch Allyn, Ferris Industries, Amoco, Utica National Insurance, Adirondack Bank, Campbell-Dean Funeral Homes, NY State Electric and Gas Corp, Turning Stone Resort Casino, Oneida Indian Nation, Sav-On Convenience Stores, Syracuse Research Corporation, Crouse Hospital, PEC Inc. and many more.

Jerry's personal experiences are vast and cover a multitude of interests: from personal fitness to baking artisan breads, from genealogy, photography and amateur radio, to building a computer, from public relations and marketing, from foraging for wild foods to finding his way around Vancouver, Valkenburg, Valetta or the Venice of the East. Jerry has done it all and he draws on those life experiences to personally relate to any copy because he's "been there and done that."

Jerry's background includes twelve years as executive director NY Beef Industry Council and thirteen years in the field of public and media relations with the New York Farm Bureau (2 years) and Oneida Indian Nation (11 years). While with the Oneida Indian Nation he worked to raise the viability of the Nation and its Turning Stone Resort Casino. In 2006 Jerry was recognized for generating "well over $2 million in media coverage" for entertainment at Turning Stone.

Jerry's broadcasting experience includes the development and launch of a daily radio newscast airing on forty-three stations in New York, Pennsylvania, Vermont, New Hampshire and Maine. Those award winning broadcasts, RFD and The Northeast Farm Report, delivered important news for the farm and rural audience of the Northeast for nearly eight years. He has an additional 20 years as an on-air radio personality and commercial production.

Specialties: Voice acting, voice-overs, audio editing, public relations.
