Carson Lowry Real Estate's Summary

Our mission is to provide revolutionary value and innovation for buyers and sellers of residential real estate.

Any realtor can promise customer service. At Carson Lowry Real Estate we deliver it.

We use a proven repeatable system, backed by market research, to sell your home for up to 18% more money than other traditional real estate methods.

There are over 115 different variables that determine what your home will sell for. If these variables are not managed correctly it could cost you thousands. As a certified home selling advisor, I have been trained to proactively manage these variables, so you will net the most amount money from the sale of your home.

For buyers we use the "Preferred Buyer Program" to put you in a position to get a better home, for less money, with less stress along the way. We put you in control by getting you to a home that matches exactly what you are looking for first. Wouldn't you agree that the nicest homes always sell first? Unfortunately the FACTS are that a good percentage of homes are not even listed in the major websites like Zillow,Trulia, and Realtor.com.

We negotiate on your behalf to make sure you don't overpay for the home and often arrange to have your closing costs paid by the seller and a free home warranty for the first year you own the home to protect you from unexpected expenses.

Our proven approach utilizes a team of expert advisors including lenders, title companies, home inspectors, and contractors, with the focus on you getting what you want and need in your home purchase.
