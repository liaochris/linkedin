Mallory Lindsley-Çiçek's Summary

The world of mobile surrounds us day in and day out. As our society relies heavily on their phones, tablets and other devices, we are faced with creating new and innovative ideas and applications that will break the mold.

Currently I am working in the mobile industry creating inventive apps and experiences for the everyday user. Overall, I focus on bringing an avant-garde approach to the products that I create. Taking my experiences in print, web, digital publishing and marketing, and bridging them with mobile, allows me and my team to create original and contemporary applications for multiple mobile platforms.
