Mandy Messer's Summary

I've journeyed through ways to blossom the relationship between creative problem-solving and analytic thinking, since I noticed my affinity and passion for both back in 1999. User Experience Design has been my primary medium to express this relationship and I'm fortunate enough to have been an active participant in this new media field for 10 years now.

Always striving to get the most out of my time, I'm finding that freelancing in the UX Lead role gives me 2 critical things that are making me a better designer on a daily basis. First, freelancing provides the freedom to constantly explore and apply industry trends and techniques. And second, it's encouraging me to work up to my own and my team's standards, and these are many times higher than those set for me in traditional work environments.

Currently teamed up with 5 other highly-talented independent contractors, what I LOVE about what I do is that I get to produce the best digital design possible by way of combining creative strategy, technical knowledge, analytic thought processes and personable communication in a healthy team environment.

I've previously been fortunate enough to polish my skills and experience at several different agencies. I thoroughly enjoyed working with Hot Studio when they were at their peak before and during the recent Facebook acquisition; Odopod before they rebranded as Nurun SF; Modem as they went through a merge between traditional ad agency and a digital agency; and Organic as I worked closely with the wonderful people out of the Detroit and SF offices during my own lifestyle transition.

Prior to that, I was the founder of Topaz Agency, building smart web sites for small businesses; spent a year living and working out of Armenia helping to design and write activities for a web design curriculum for high school students, and have worked and taught at Michigan State University.

But mostly, I'm looking forward to what tomorrow brings. :)
