Matías Calderón Bourband's Summary

In early 2009 I co-founded guionmedio, a design/development/adv agency in the beautiful city of Paraná, Argentina, where I was born and raised.

I’m in charge of web design and leading digital projects. I love design but I am also passionate about customer relationships, leading and managing the day-to-day of projects with clients and our team.

In 2012, I co-founded La Fotoneta, a photo booth rental service for all kinds of events, with the goal of offering new entertainment experiences, mixing photography, design, art, technology and marketing.
