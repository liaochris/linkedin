Clinton Power's Summary

Clinton is the founder and website concept developer of Australia's leading counselling directory www.AustraliaCounselling.com.au. Clinton formed Australia Counselling to connect the Australian public with high-quality mental health practitioners across Australia. Australia Counselling is also the first membership group in Australia dedicated to providing ongoing training and support for counsellors, therapists and psychologists in the areas of learning how to effectively market a private practice as well as develop clinical skills and knowledge about psychotherapy.

Clinton is a trained Gestalt psychotherapist who founded Clinton Power + Associates to provide relationship counselling for singles and couples from three locations throughout Sydney.

Clinton also has a passion for online marketing and social media and provides coaching, consulting and business development ideas for healthcare professionals.

Specialties: Conflict resolution, relationship counselling, marriage therapy, small business consulting, social media training, online marketing coaching, business development consulting.
