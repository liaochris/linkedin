Gonzalo Gus Vergara's Summary

Executive Bios

Mr. Gonzalo (Gus) Vergara
Founder, Owner and CEO of Distinctive Personnel

A proud member of the distinguished Strategic Air Command of the United States Air Force until his Honorable Discharge and Service Disabled Veteran Status in 1976, Mr. Gonzalo Vergara founded Distinctive Personnel in 1983 with the vision of creating a one-of-a-kind staffing agency that acts as a true partner to employees and organizations alike. He is very active in the Hispanic Community and has assisted thousands of Veterans find jobs across America at www.distinctivepersonnel.com and www.distinctivepersonnel.com/usaveteran

As President and CEO of Distinctive Personnel, Mr. Vergara is responsible for new and existing business development; finance/capital coordination; marketing, advertising, sales and public relations management; employee quality control and development; risk management; advanced technology procurement, analysis, development and management; and operations management.

As the founder of the largest and Oldest New York State Certified MBE Hispanic-Owned Temporary Staffing and Employment Agency. Mr. Vergara is a proud member of New York’s Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) association. He has also been a member of (NAACP) and the National Minority Business Council (NMBC). His certification can be seen on the Official Directory of Minority Owned Businesses at the New York State Website: www.empire.state.ny.us.

About Our Company

Founded in 1983, Distinctive Personnel is New York’s oldest and largest Service Disabled Veteran Owned and Hispanic-Owned Minority Business Enterprise (MBE), State & City Certified Employment Service Agency with a nationwide database of over 147 million resumes. As one of the fastest growing personnel agencies in the country, we continue to challenge our clients and our candidates to reach their full potential. We are a GSA Contract Holder providing Staffing to the Federal Government and A CVE Certified Service Disabled Veteran Owned Business.

Our focus on providing our clients with the most qualified temporary, temp-to-perm and direct-hire employees, along with comprehensive support services – including payroll outsourcing,
onsite/offsite managed service providers (MSP) and vendor managed services (VMS) – has made Distinctive Personnel a leader in a highly-competitive industry. Our dedication to serving the needs of our candidates – from comprehensive benefits to competitive pay – has strengthened our reputation as the agency everyone wants to work with. 1-800-504-1148
