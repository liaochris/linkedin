Andrew Bloo's Summary

Andrew Bloo is currently the Chief Marketing Officer of BizPlanIt.com while also maintaining an active interest in BNBranding.com, Accelerance.com and numerous consulting roles with technology based start-ups. Prior to Joining BizPlanit.com Andrew served as Vice President of Marketing at Lodestar Technologies a Tarkenton Company where he provided mentoring and guidance to the companies product development and marketing teams.

Andrew has also served as Chief Executive Officer of SciTech Software, a company whose innovative-systems approach to device driver development were instrumental in providing the tools and cross-platform device drivers required by the world’s top hardware manufacturers ( ATI, HP, Nvidia, IBM, Intel, ..) and early 3D game developers with products like SciTech SNAP and SciTech UniVBE.

Mr. Bloo has also worked as Chief Marketing Officer of Jian Software where we successfully transitioned the company from a traditional "boxed" software solution provider to an online leader of business software solutions. Under Andrews leadership JIAN experienced double digit growth for 16 straight quarters culminating in a licensing agreement with Tarkenton Companies.

Andrew holds a degree in Information and Communication Studies with an option in Visual Communications from California State University Chico and has been recognized by the SIIA for his software package design and marketing work (WinWay Resume). Andrew currently lives in Central Oregon.

Specialties: Strong technical experience managing and implementing e-commerce platforms,Hands-on business building expertise, systems based product development strategies, partnership development, sales management, team leadership, product life-cycle management (from concept to business profit center and ultimately building end-of-life revenue opportunities), multi-faceted cost effective marketing strategies.
