Dave Carr's Summary

Dave has successfully worked for 15 years with organisations and individuals to develop their business and management skills through the practical implementation of training, facilitation, change management and organisational development. He designs and delivers high quality consultancy across the private, public and not-for-profit sectors. Dave is a visionary, offering new perspectives and asking probing questions to provide insight when thinking about or planning the future.

Dave is educated to degree level and is currently studying an MSc in Organisational Development at Sheffield Hallam University. In addition to his consultancy experience, Dave has over 20 years of involvement in the voluntary sector, working at every level from local volunteer groups to international networks.

Dave is known for a relaxed, interactive and inclusive delivery style that creates an environment that is constructive, but creative and gets people thinking. His approach is inspiring and empowering. Dave uses a range of questioning and interactive techniques to draw out key issues and encourage participation. He continually evaluates his work and receives a lot of good feedback and repeat bookings. Organisations and individuals benefit from his long range thinking and ability to see things from a global perspective. He creates a learning environment, packed with ideas and information.
