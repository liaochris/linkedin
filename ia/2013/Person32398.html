David Diana's Summary

David P. Diana, MA, LPC, is the co-founder of David P. Diana Marketing in Charleston, SC where his team offers creative design and marketing consulting services.

In addition, David has been a counselor in a variety of capacities and has served in management and director roles for several counseling practices and organizations. He has worked as a consultant for Coopers & Lybrand, PricewaterhouseCoopers, the Hay Group, IBM Global Services, and is currently the lead principle and co-founder of David P. Diana Marketing.

David is the author of the best selling book, “Marketing for the Mental Health Professional: An Innovative Guide for Practitioners” published by John Wiley & Sons, and is the founder of a popular weekly blog (click here to view) focusing on professional development ideas and marketing innovations.

In 2009, David was the recipient of the 40-Under-40 Award given by the Charleston Business Journal. Each year, the Business Journal recognizes the most influential business professionals under forty years old in the Charleston, SC metro area.

David serves on the Board of Life Management Center - a Charleston based non-profit providing testing and tutoring for children with learning disabilities, and he is the founder of Project Charleston, which each year raises funds and provides marketing support for Charleston based non-profits.

Prior to his current position, David served as the Director of Sales and Marketing for a large behavioral health care organization.

Specialties

business development, change management, consulting, contract management, customer relations, management, leadership training, marketing, design, mental health, sales, health care, counseling, coaching
