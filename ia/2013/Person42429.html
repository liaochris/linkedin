Eric Twiggs' Summary

I bring a wealth of knowledge and over 17 years of experience in business . Prior to starting my career, I Graduated from Hampton University with a degree in Marketing. I then began my career in Automotive as a top producing Service Advisor and quickly worked my way up the ranks to store manager where I supervised up to 35 employees and was responsible for $3.5 Million Dollars of annual revenue.

After successfully managing 2 different Automotive locations, I went on to become a Performance Consultant in the Automotive industry where I coached, trained and consulted with over 65 Store and Service Managers in the Upstate New York and Washington DC areas. It was at this point that my passion for coaching and taking leaders to the next level was birthed.

Following a strong desire to go to the next level, I took on the role of District Manager where I successfully led as many as 17 Automotive locations with responsibility over 500 employees and $50 million dollars in annual revenue. I spent 9 years as a District Manager and received numerous awards for my sales and profit results.

I currently work with business owners across the nation as a Management Consultant / Coach. In this role, I drive profits and dreams home for my clients through honest coaching and integrity.
