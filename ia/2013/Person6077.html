Amy Vulpis' Experience

Realtor®

Atlantic Properties International, Inc.

June 2013 – Present (2 months) Fort Lauderdale, Florida

Founded in 1983 and internationally established, Atlantic Properties International has led with professional service in the Real estate industry. Atlantic Properties International agents are carefully selected and are highly trained licensed professionals who are capable of handling all aspects of your transaction, based on a philosophy that the successful sale of a property begins with meticulous research.

Prospective purchasers are provided with invaluable information relating to property availability, financial lending programs, vital community information, economic statistics, appraisals, surveys & comprehensive inspection reports. Our organization will also direct you to qualified professionals, providing client protection in tax matters, immigration and real estate law as well as environmental protection guidelines

As a Seller having listed your property with Atlantic Properties International, you will benefit by our proven marketing efforts which include local, national and international marketing exposure. High profile periodicals are utilized in conjunction with our established network of national and international brokers, bankers and developers. As a member of an international computerized listing service, Atlantic Properties International exclusive listings are globally marketed to over 7,000 international real estate specialists in 52 countries.

South Florida offers an incomparable lifestyle which Atlantic Properties International invites you to enjoy. Whether you are seeking or selling an oceanfront condominium, a luxury residence, commercial investments or require a total corporate relocation, Atlantic Properties International provides an unsurpassed professional service and knowledge of the local real estate market.
