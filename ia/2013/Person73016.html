Kathy Santini's Summary

I’m a writer, editor and public relations professional with extensive, diverse experiences in communications and public relations. An example of two extremes is doing PR for a Royal Visit and working as a journalist in a war zone in Kabul, Afghanistan.

With 25 years of communications experience, I CAN HELP YOU BY:

** Developing a COMMUNICATIONS STRATEGY that identifies your project’s weaknesses, strengths and opportunities, while outlining what will optimize your communication goals.

**Managing stories in the press, by doing ISSUES MANAGEMENT and MEDIA RELATIONS, helping you to respond to negative stories quickly and promptly to limit the impact on your business or organization.

** WRITING and PROJECT MANAGING promotional and marketing materials – newsletters, brochures, reports, web content, magazines, fact sheets and backgrounders - that sell your idea or product in the most persuasive way possible.

** DOING EVENT PLANNING, BY creating, planning and managing the ABCs of your event, so that your announcement comes off flawlessly.

** PUBLICIZING YOUR EVENT, by writing and distributing press kits with news releases, Q’s and A’s, fact sheets and backgrounders to the media, so that your story gets the publicity that it deserves.

** Writing a SPEECH that has people talking days later – positively, that is.

** WRITING and/or EDITING op-eds, letters to the editor, columns, hard or soft news articles, or blogs for you, raising your profile and maximizing exposure for your organization or business.

** Reviewing your SOCIAL MEDIA platforms, to see what’s working and what isn’t.

Savvy About Success, Coaching and Education

Savvy About Success, Coaching and Education

January 2006 – December 2012 (7 years) Victoria, B.C.

My primary interest as a coach was in in the psychology of success, because success leaves clues and by modelling successful people we can replicate their success. To help coaching clients realize their personal and professional goals, I used strategies from leaders in the human potential field, brain scientists, psychologists and counsellors.

I educated readers on the coaching profession by writing columns, blogs and articles on coaching subjects for a variety of publications and websites, including: Exceptionalife Magazine, Ezine Articles, Viva Visibility Blog, Women’s Health Talk Magazine, Lifeline Magazine, Victoria’s Business Examiner, Business Matters, and In-Flight Review magazines.

My first book: Live Your Best Life Now! The Cancer Survivors Guide to Creating a Happy and Meaningful Life, showed cancer survivors how to use coaching principles to create a wonderful life after cancer.

In addition to writing about coaching, I developed different workshops/speeches for a variety of audiences, on: modelling excellence and success, financial empowerment, work-life balance, creating a fabulous life after cancer, visioning, goal setting and time management. I have national conference speaking experience.

To reach a bigger audience, I developed, and hosted a series of teleseminars called, "What Successful People Know.” I interviewed a number of experts in a variety of fields on how to be successful in their areas of expertise: relationships, finances, marketing and social media. Listeners participated from around the world.

I was a frequent guest on CFAX Radio, on The Terry Spence and Murray Langdon Shows and on Life Style, with Patty Mack.
