Carrie McKiernan-Swenson's Summary

Carrie McKiernan loves exploring how people engage in, and are fulfilled by, their careers. She has been working in one side or the other of the Employment relationship since her career began. From hiring 10% of Genentech's headcount when she was in staffing, to coaching executives and employees as they navigate their careers-- she is, and always has been, about how people choose and succeed in their work.

Currently, she coaches individuals on career transitions, employment searches, and performance optimization. As both a trained coach and a former in-house recruiter who taught managers how to develop critical interviewing and selection skills, Carrie understands how to grow careers as well as confidence. Some examples of moments when she is most in "flow"* include:

• Building candidate confidence in to the search and interview process. One executive who had been out of work for over a year made significant changes in their interview style - and was hired after the next interview!
• Coaching a leader through a career 'rough spot' - and watching them grow in skill and career satisfaction.
• In some cases, helping employees realize that the right decision was to stay, not to leave, but to improve their performance, attitude, and develop a stronger relationship with their boss-- and identifying the steps to make that happen.
• Watching a client have the "a-ha" moment of seeing themselves in their Myers-Briggs results.
• Helping clients create more powerful resumes that they feel confident sending out.

She works with clients from many industries, including high tech, biotech, nonprofit, and consumer goods. Do you need support around finding the right match, making a move, or being the leader or colleague you want to be? Contact Carrie - she'd be glad to help.

* She'd be glad to explain "Flow" to you if you haven't read it - the idea is central to career satisfaction.
