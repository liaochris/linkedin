Jerome Farnum's Summary

Senior Web/UI Designer with 8+ years of progressive experience in online, interactive, and motion graphics across multiple industries. Domain expertise includes five years of creative work in Gaming Industry highlighted by web design, video production, and marketing collateral development for Shaiya, Last Chaos, Eden Eternal, Grand Fantasia, Divina, Soul Captor, and more. Additionally, possess two years of management experience leading teams of 3-5 designers across US and international offices in the Netherlands and Taiwan. Creative and strategic thinker looking to leverage design, leadership, and business experience with a dynamic and growing organization.
