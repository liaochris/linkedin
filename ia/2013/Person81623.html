Lisa Rodgers' Summary

Throughout my career, I have been involved in sales. Whether I was working for another company or running my own. A hand injury forced me to make a difficult decision to close the manufacturing side of Cactus & Ivy in 2011.

My love affair with photography was my saving grace. I never thought of making a full-time gig out of capturing life until my life was thrown a curve ball. I'm thankful every day that I can continue to work for myself.

Entrepreneurship is an empowering and beautiful journey.
