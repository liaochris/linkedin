Jermain Smith's Summary

I am a detail-oriented and industrious Information Technology Professional. Knowledgeable in both hardware and software aspects of IT support. I have a diverse background and skill set in variety of different areas with over 20 years of hands-on experience, 15 years of higher lever support areas. I am a self-motivated, creative and artistic individual with excellent analytical, interpersonal and organizational skills and a true team is player.

I am the creative mind behind Divine Business Group, Inc. a multifaceted company with vested interest in an assortment of different areas from online marketing and branding to community service.

I am currently developing a couple branches of Divine Business Group, Inc. One of which is Cyberian Creative Solutions is a web development and online marketing business that is coming soon. Déjà vu is a green initiative we are looking to recycle old computers from local colleges and businesses – gathering these old machines and repurposing donating them to under privilege families in the Westchester and New York City area. And then there is Project Chrysalis – this is an education, training and consulting operation aimed at young adults and other community organizations for assistance with technology.

Currently I am the Technology Coordinator for the School of Education at Pace University after serving as a Server Technician for Pace University the past 12 years. I am an entrepreneur, a graphic designer and IT consultant.

Specialties: Smart Board Setup and Configuration, Hot Banana Content Management System, Computer and Handheld device service and support, Media Management, Technical Documentation Writing, IT Consultant, Graphic Designer, Adobe CS5 (Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat) Microsoft Office Enterprise, (Visio for Charts and Technical Documentation) Brightstor Arcserv Backup from CA, Bomgar (remote access software), VMWare, and more...
