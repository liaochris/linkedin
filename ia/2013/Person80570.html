Lex Hegt's Projects

  • BizTalk Events

    • May 2012 to Present

    This website serves as a calendar for BizTalk events all over the world. Follow upcoming events with RSS and on Twitter (@BizTalkEvents)!

  • BizTalk Message Decompressor

    • January 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Lex Hegt

    This tool enables you to decompress BizTalk messages/context from a screen that looks like SQL Query Analyzer.
    By simple entering a SQL query and some custom functions, you can retrieve (partial) message content and/or context. Handy for administrators/engineers!

  • BizTalk Processing Monitor

    • January 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Lex Hegt

    The most important and distinct functionality of BizTalk Processing Monitor (or BPM) is that it enables you to monitor message flow through your BizTalk systems. Most other available monitoring software, monitor for example the availability of endpoints. Of course it is important to know if your endpoints are up-and-running, but this does not guarantee that messages actually are flowing through your system.
    BizTalk Processing Monitor does give you that insight and in case of any interruptions, you got notified.
