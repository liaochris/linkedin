Becca Ludlum's Summary

I am a Southwest-based blogger, freelance writer, and small business and blog digital media consultant (http://BeccaLudlum.com). I own and manage the blog My Crazy Good Life (http://mycrazygoodlife.com), formerly OurCrazyBoys.com.

I enjoy writing about parenting tweens and teens, technology as it relates to raising children, tutorials for new bloggers and occasional recipes and reviews. You can find my portfolio online at http://beccaludlum.com. My published print work can be found in iPhone Life magazine.

I have been fortunate to form partnerships with and attend events with Disneyland, Disneyworld, GMC, Canyon Ranch Tucson, Arizona Science Center, Toyota/Lexus, The San Diego Zoo, and Sea Life Aquarium, among others.
