Jackie Jacobi's Summary

SUCCESS COMES THROUGH COURAGE!
WHAT WOULD YOU DO IF YOU WEREN'T AFRAID?

Business is really a Creative and Exploratory process: Learn - Think - Create - Try - Change. Entrepreneurs must repeat this process throughout their Business Life Cycle.

My VISION: To empower entrepreneurs to be in control of their own destiny and future.

My MISSION: To provide the information and resources entrepreneurs need to make educated and informed business and financial decisions.

More Information available at www.JackieJacobi.com
Business News at www.twitter.com/jackiejacobi

I believe that Entrepreneurship is a way of thinking that has the ability to recognize problems, analyze creative solutions, focus on new opportunities and lead with a vision toward the future.

Specialties: Developing a strategic direction that enables entrepreneurs to start and grow a successful small business while learning to build wealth not debt. I help entrepreneurs prioritize, focus, and implement the strategic plan.
