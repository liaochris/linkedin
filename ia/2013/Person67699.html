Jonathan Sternberg's Summary

Jonathan Sternberg is an appellate lawyer in Kansas City, Missouri. He focuses his solo practice on civil and criminal appellate litigation at all points of a case, including briefing and arguing appeals, writs, and related actions, as well as advising trial attorneys on procedure and strategy.

Since opening his practice immediately after his admission to the bar in 2007, Jonathan has represented businesses and individuals in dozens of state and federal appeals throughout America, successfully pursuing commercial, constitutional, insurance, criminal, estate, tax, and family cases, among other subjects. The total amount in controversy of all his cases exceeds $200 million. His clients have included everything from Fortune 500® companies to indigent people. He has presented more than 40 appellate oral arguments, including nine before the Supreme Court of Missouri, an unusual achievement for a private lawyer of his age and time in practice. He is admitted to practice before Missouri and Kansas state courts, the U.S. Courts of Appeals for the Eighth, Ninth, and Tenth Circuits, all federal district courts in Missouri and Kansas, and the U.S. Supreme Court.

Jonathan was the only solo practitioner who Missouri Lawyers Weekly selected as an "Up & Coming Lawyer" in 2009 and the youngest solo practitioner named to the Kansas City Business Journal's "Best of the Bar" list in 2013. He has been named a "Rising Star" by Missouri & Kansas SuperLawyers every year since 2010, was a member of the Missouri Bar Leadership Academy's 2011 class, and has received Martindale-Hubbell®'s highest peer review rating, "AV® Preeminent™." He chairs the appellate-related committees of both the statewide Missouri Bar and the local Kansas City Metropolitan Bar Association, and regularly presents educational programs to lawyers and non-lawyers alike.

Specialties: appeals; litigation strategy; post-judgment proceedings; post-conviction remedies; writ practice; constitutional law; municipal law
