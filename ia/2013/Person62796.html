Jerry Tribuzio's Summary

I have 25 years of experience in the mortgage business and possess all the tools needed to help people finance their new homes and assist them in refinancing their current loans and make them more affordable even if they owe more on their mortgage than their home is worth.

I have been an athlete all my life and a workout enthusiast. I feel that my drive, determination, and passion is the major factor which has made me successful in the loan industry. I know about hard work, time management, commitment, focus, and perseverance which helps me accomplish my personal and professional goals. Today, my never say die attitude is my greatest asset when I encounter the never ending challenges the mortgage business presents to me on a daily basis.

Today, I want to focus on developing relationships with Realtors, builders, and other real estate professional to help rebuild the housing market and restore a sense of community in America. In addition to assisting homeowners and buyers with their mortgage needs, I am committed to educating them about the housing market through one-on-one consultations and seminars as well as my social media and personal websites. My website firsthomebuyers.net is devoted to helping first time home buyers through every step of the first time home buying process. I am the author of “Ultimate First Time Home Buyer Guide” which is a roadmap on how you can achieve the dream of homeownership. My other website firsthomebuyernetwork.com is a preferred partner directory website for first time home buyers who are looking for real estate professionals to assist them in purchasing their 1st home. If you would like to partner with me or if I can be a resource to you in any way, please contact me by calling (847) 516-5743 or email me at jerrytribuzio@gmail.com.

I specialize in the following:
• First Time Home Buyers
• Mortgage Loans
• Refinance Mortgage
• FHA, VA, and USDA Loan Programs
• HARP Refinance Loans
