Jon Hadden's Summary

Jon is a user experience design director, speaker, and writer with over 10 years of experience designing and developing digital products for millions of users across the globe.

His philosophy is that the best design solutions simply get out of the way, allowing users to achieve their goal as easy as possible.

He is able to produce usable, intuitive interfaces from a theoretical understanding of project requirements, human cognition and behavioral psychology.

He has a strong background of typography, visual design, color theory, pattern recognition, semantic front-end development, and team management.

Specialties

Interaction Design, Visual Design, Axure & Keynote Prototyping, User Research, Usability Testing, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Photoshop, Illustrator, Cognitive Science, Behavioral Psychology
