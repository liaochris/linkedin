Mayank Patel's Summary

Do you need help with:

Application Development? Ambitious software projects often require large amounts of man-power to develop, test, deploy, and maintain. I can lend a helping hand to architect, design and implement the various modules of your revenue producing application.

Runaway Projects? Sometimes a project can go off in an undesirable direction in spite of our best efforts and intentions. A fresh pair of trained eyes on the project can be what's needed to shepherd it back into the fold.

Rapid Prototyping? Need to get a project out in front of a client or an internal team for feedback and acceptance of direction? I work fast and methodically using proven agile methods to provide my clients with high quality usable prototypes of software systems.

Automation & Integration? Mundane tasks often cost time, money, and decrease the credibility and confidence of systems, teams, and business units by introducing variable errors. Process automation results in better efficiency, more cost savings, and improved team morale.

Mentoring & Leadership? Get a no-nonsense explanation of the strengths and weaknesses of your chosen technical plan. Allow me to be a sounding board for you and your team.

I like hard problems and I enjoy it when people use creative multi-disciplinary approaches to solve problems in computation - as in this presentation - "Temporally Quaquaversal Virtual Nanomachine" (http://bit.ly/YKJd3O) - in which Damian applies the principles of space-time and relativity to computations in Perl.

Over the years I have worked both as a consultant and as an employee for a number of firms including NASA, the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), public companies, and Silicon Valley startups.

I work hard to provide value for my clients, employers, and customers. Their happiness matters to me. Results on projects we work on together matters to me. Growing our collective knowledge base and skills matters to me.

Everything else I consider a distraction.
