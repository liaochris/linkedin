Steven MacGregor's Summary

In over 10 years as a practice-based scholar, I have worked with some of the world's leading universities and companies. This has spanned research, consultancy, and teaching with the likes of Stanford, Carnegie Mellon, IESE, CEIBS, and Oracle, Telefónica, Mars, Danone, and Nestlé.

Based in Spain since 2003 I am focused on executive education, particularly the personal sustainability of leaders, and how that links to the sustainable competitiveness of the firm.

Highly skilled and in demand across a range of professional development mechanisms, from keynote/plenary speeches for hundreds of people, to more intimate teaching and coaching with small groups and individuals.

A competitive sportsman for many years, I have trained with Olympic athletes, Tour de France riders, triathlon world champions, and currently race for FC Barcelona.

Specialties: Executive health and performance; Design as a driver of innovation; Role of business in society.
