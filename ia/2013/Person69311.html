Joshua Smith's Summary

I provide smart people with the space and encouragement needed to create amazing digital platforms, and then ensure that those creations are helping small business owners to become more effective.

iOnline was founded back in March 2000 to make it easier for businesses to shift into the digital marketplace and to enhance the manner by which they communicate with customers. In the years since, this vision has been maintained as the team's capabilities and code base have increased.

We now have 9 team members on board and service approximately 500 clients from Queensland and around the world.

Some of the platforms we created and maintain are:
* iCMS - website content management system
* iCart - online e-commerce system
* elevationHQ - a simple, strategic small business CRM
* cloudPSA - an online scheduling and job management system
* xocashflow - an add-on for Xero that streamlines the chasing of overdue debts

Specialties

Web design, web development, interactive media, search marketing, email marketing, social media marketing, viral marketing, Google Apps, online video, web publishing, small business management, start-ups
