Andrew Abrams' Summary

I wear a variety of business hats. My early career as an industrial designer has me conceiving, engineering, and developing consumer products mostly in toys and giftware. That experience allows me to combine design skills with my marketing services.

As a licensing agent for artists, designers, and inventors of new product concepts, I am instrumental in guiding those creatives in their business, career, and personal lives. That activity launched my professional whole-life coaching practice.

Andrew Abrams' Patents

  • Ring Accessory For An Educational Device

    • United States Patent 285,586
    • Issued September 1, 1972
    Inventors: Andrew Abrams

    The patent covers one of many accessories for an educational system entitled Interlogs which was created by me.

  • Door Chime Music Box

    • United States Patent 760,747
    • Issued September 17, 1968
    Inventors: Andrew Abrams

    This mechanism that is activated by the opening of a door emits a musical announcement. The operation is powered by a spring-wound reel which eliminates the need for electrical connection or batteries.

  • Block Structure With A Reinforcing Filler

    • United States Patent 86,507
    • Issued November 3, 1970
    Inventors: Andrew Abrams

    This invention is an important part of the educational system I created for teaching early grade arithmetic. The product is entitled How Many Apples Counting Blocks.

  • Box Structure Convertible Into An Easel

    • United States Patent 79,129
    • Issued October 8, 1970
    Inventors: Andrew Abrams

    I designed this concept which functions as a package and easel for the parts of my How Many Apples Counting Blocks educational product.
