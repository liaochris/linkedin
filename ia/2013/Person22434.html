Chastin Miles' Summary

Chastin Miles is a native Texan and is no stranger to Texas Real Estate. His background in Sales and Marketing has equipped him with all the necessary tools to effectively be a superstar in the Real Estate profession. Chastin has a never ending strive to produce the best results for his clients. Chastin possesses an enthusiastic attitude with a strong work ethic and can be noted as one of North Texas’ top agents.

Chastin Miles started his first business at the young age a twelve; a personalized greeting card company. Astonished at the success of his custom greeting cards, and how much money he was making, he decided to expand his business into graphic design and marketing. At the age of fifteen, Miles Business Technology was born and later renamed Printafix, LLC a full service business marketing company. Printafix quickly grew to become a prominent business in the Atlanta area specializing in Graphic Design, Web Design and Printing.

Chastin attended Kennesaw State University in Kennesaw, Georgia and majored in Communications with a concentration in Public Relations and Marketing. With his professional business endeavours, Chastin has been nominated as a “Who’s Who of African-American Young Entrepreneurs”. Chastin strongly believes in giving back to the community in any way possible and in 2011 he introduced NetFusion. Miles’ vision for NetFusion is to provide resources, information, and a platform for young entrepreneur’s to showcase and grow their businesses. It is extremely important that our youth are encouraged to reach their goals and dreams especially when it comes to entreperneurship.

Miles‘ work background, education, energy, and ethics has empowered him to become one of the best producing Real Estate agents today.His mission is to provide each client with the best sales and marketing tools to get your property sold and an open line of communication so that you are involved each step of the way.
