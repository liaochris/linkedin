Jason Hreha's Summary

I'm a product strategist.

I apply the most up-to-date thinking in the behavioral sciences to product design. If you need to change the behavior of your users, or need to think more clearly about how to analyze your customer base, I can help you.

===

I'm also concerned about the current role of technology in our society:

http://gigaom.com/2012/09/02/when-did-addiction-become-a-good-thing/


===

Expert and thought leader in Behavior Change and Persuasive Technology. I combine this expertise with my skills in User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design to build compelling online apps and websites.

Specialties:
Product Strategy, User Experience (UX) Design, UI Design, Psychology, Persuasive Technology, Behavior Change
