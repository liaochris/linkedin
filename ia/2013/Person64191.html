About Opus Faveo

Opus Faveo is a venture development firm that discovers, builds and supports great companies and those who lead them. The goal of Opus Faveo is to create a vibrant, encouraging and optimistic innovation community. Like other start‐up accelerators, Opus Faveo provides innovators with services, mentoring and introductions. Unlike them, Opus Faveo becomes part of the team – doing whatever it takes to make the idea commercially successful over the long term. Opus Faveo is interested in scalable ideas, “born global” companies, experienced first‐time founders, and large company/start‐up interaction.

Opus Faveo will provide a detailed consultation to any scalable start‐up (early or later stage) for free, no strings attached. Depending on the situation, a more expansive level of services is also available – for free. And if a deeper relationship is desired, a long‐term agreement with Opus Faveo is possible in exchange for a small equity stake. In other words, there is no cash outlay by the start‐up.


Opus Faveo CI²
Corporate & Institutional Innovation

Through the Opus Faveo CI² initiative, the firm provides thought leadership, strategic guidance and venture acceleration for middle market and large companies, multinational corporations, governments, multilateral agencies, universities and research institutions. Opus Faveo uses an innovative, proprietary and comprehensive framework to enhance innovation throughout the organization and deliver measurable value to stakeholders. An Opus Faveo CI² engagement enables companies and institutions to to foster innovation in a highly cost-effective manner while limiting risk and retaining economic upside.

ES Interims (an Executive Smarts, LLC company) provides proven, well-qualified, interim professional managers (contract) or permanent executives to manage a period of transition, crisis or change within a company. Rather than taking on a purely advisory role (as a management consultant would), Executive Smarts’ executives are responsible and accountable line managers who will implement and manage a project or business.

ES Interims provides a range of advisory services for companies that lack the internal resources or expertise for specific business requirements. This includes business strategy projects, filling interim senior executive positions and participating on boards of directors.

We can immediately fill positions for board of directors, C-suite, operational, financial, technical, marketing, and manufacturing managers

ES Interims’ Professionals ARE NOT analysts, report writers, or all things to all people. We solve specific business problems, teach, mentor and transfer our knowledge to our client’s organization.
