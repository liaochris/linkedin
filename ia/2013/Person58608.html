Jan Stittleburg's Summary

My main focus is interior and exterior architectural photography for residential and commercial designers, builders and architects. I also enjoy product photography for industries as diverse as handcrafted furniture, jewelry, cleaning products, machine tools and electronics.

My graphic design experience in catalogs, e-newsletters, trade show displays, magazine layout and ads, brochures, and postcards helps your small business gain market attention. I work in Wordpress for CMS websites or Dreamweaver for customized sites.

I am available for tutoring in Photoshop and beginning photography techniques, and basic Wordpress website set-up and design.

As a student of Tai Chi and Qi Gong for over 14 years, I have attained teacher certification in both. I am open to starting new classes around the NE Metro Atlanta area. Qi Gong is a gently moving, meditative exercise good for all ages, relieving stress and building calmer and healthier bodies. Please see my website http://qimovingmeditation.com for more information.

Specialties: Over 20 years of related experience in photography, graphic design and pre-press means that your work will be showcased effectively in whatever medium it is shown, print or web.
