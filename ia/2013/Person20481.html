Carol Shepherd's Summary

Carol has a zeal for working with both technology and people. She provides the leadership and organization that enables clients to act as active collaborators in the software development process. Her communication, information design, and prototyping skills empower her to transition easily from an over arching strategy and design into technical architecture and software construction concerns. Using a variety of creative techniques she helps others to think creatively through development challenges. She enjoys actively managing activities of a project team and acting as a key participant in client meetings. She plays a key role in an organization’s software architecture research — continually asking “what if?”

(switching from the royal "She")...

I am exploring the emerging platforms that enable mobile media and interactive publishing, nearing the end of an iPad enterprise workforce enhancement project, and has recently finished teaching three graduate IT courses. I am very interested in how mobile, learning, and the education of the next generation of software engineers intersects.

My (and my business partner's) bread and butter is the design, development, and support of either enterprise workforce automation apps intended for private distribution or investor funded Apple App Store apps (say that three times fast). My partner and I have extensive experience in the disciplined support of production software. We have chosen to focus on clients that have an existing app that they need to stabilize and gradually improve and would like the flexibility and cost savings that a defined support agreement provides.
