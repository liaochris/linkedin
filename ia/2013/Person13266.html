Ben Reid's Summary

Director and Principal Consultant at Memia Ltd - helping New Zealand’s SaaS businesses and IT organisations get strategy right, scale and perform.

I'm a T-shaped “deep generalist” with a strong strategic, architectural and governance skillset, an ever-broadening knowledge of how great SaaS and cloud IT organisations should plan and operate, backed up by a firm 19-year grounding in software technology, architecture and development practices.

I act as strategic advisor and technology consultant to a growing number of high-growth potential New Zealand SaaS businesses as well as corporate IT organisations. Fundamentally I like building robust strategic growth plans and overseeing their successful execution.

I am also the 2012-2013 Chair of Canterbury Software Inc., the regional software industry body. We help to grow and support world-class technology businesses from Canterbury, New Zealand - one of the top innovation, commercial - and lifestyle - regions in the world.

Specialties:
- Consulting and facilitation
- Cloud services strategy and architecture
- Software and enterprise architecture
- Strategic technology planning, roadmaps and governance
- SaaS business modelling

- Knowledge of current and future technology trends
- Tech startup management
- ISV management and technical leadership recruitment
- Entrepreneurship and innovation
