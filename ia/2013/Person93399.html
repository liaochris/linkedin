Michelle Caers' Summary

I am the Chief Customer Officer for Savvica a higher education marketing company. I am a member of the founding team of this start-up and work in partnership with the CEO to oversee the day-to-day operations of the business and contribute to the strategic vision for the company.

As CCO, I provide the comprehensive and authoritative view of the customer and create corporate and customer strategy to maximize customer acquisition, retention and profitability. I am responsible for global sales and marketing, business development and corporate partnerships with the ultimate objective of maximizing customer and corporate value.

Specialties: Business development, relationship sales, enterprise sales, negotiation, international experience, technology and internet products and services, marketing, elearning, consulting, hiring and building a team, serial entrepreneur
