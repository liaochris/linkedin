Commercial Real Estate

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry

April 2008 – Present (5 years 6 months) San Antonio, Texas Area

We educate clients regarding Commercial Real Estate Brokerage, Leasing, Development, and Investments. We close deals through in depth financial analysis, "outside the box" thinking, and aggressive web marketing campaigns. We help business owners to learn how to purchase and/or develop real estate to own/occupy as well as aid them through the financing process with lending institutions. We aid developers in site acquisitions, financial analysis, leasing, and dispositions. We help tenants to find locations that are best suited for their needs and represent them in lease negotation and their build-out process. We develop or aid developers in the development and lease negotiation of single user net leased assets (health care, retail, office, industrial). We represent owners of net leased assets to market/sell their assets to a worldwide market of buyers through our large network. We aid buyers of Texas net leased assets to review and negotiate acquisitions of assets. We represent owners of commercial land tracts in an around the San Antonio, Texas area to sell their properties.
