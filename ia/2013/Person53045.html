Robin Mayhall, APR's Summary

Versatile communications professional with more than 20 years of experience in writing and editing, public relations planning and implementation, corporate communications, crisis communications, speechwriting, special event planning, media relations, and website and social media content development.

Specialties: Business and feature writing, copyediting and proofreading, trade publications, healthcare, publication management, public relations campaigns, media relations, newsletter writing and editing.

I also write science fiction and fantasy poetry and stories, and I've written two young adult graphic novels. "The Quest for Dragon Mountain," published in 2010, and "He Loves Me, He Loves Me Not," published in January 2013, are both available on Amazon.com.
