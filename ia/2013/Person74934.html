Kevin Hill: "The Professor Of High Response"'s Summary

Did you know you could be losing money as you read this? Chances are you’re using an advertisement that is missing the #1 factor that is responsible for 90% of the success of ANY advertisement.

It’s a factor most marketers and copywriters overlook...yet it could make the difference between boosting your profits or having a big fat ZERO staring at you in your bank account.

This #1 factor is research. This is the only way you can hit the right nerve and create a sales surge thanks to your advertising.

Without it, your advertising misses the mark and ends up costing you time, money, and opportunity. And it doesn’t matter if you have the greatest headline in the world or slick "power words" in your copy.

Unfortunately performing painstaking research is a learned skill that takes years to perfect. And that’s why most copywriters write copy that "looks" good...but falls flat because they did not perform the #1 critical factor that is responsible for the ad's success: Research!

Thankfully, performing research come easy to me. I have over 13 years of research experience as a scientist, and this has allowed me to write copy that has brought in over $8 million dollars for my clients in the past 5 years.

Because, after all, advertising is a science. I have created many profit-pulling sales letters, video sales letters, landing pages, emails, and one-time offers that have raked in many sales.

And, after all, great copy is all about research. That's what allows any direct mail letter, video script, and web copy to get the prospect's attention, grab them by the lapels, and force them to read your advertisement!
