Nikolas Hedberg's Summary

Dr. Hedberg helps patients overcome thyroid disorders, fatigue, weight gain, depression, digestive problems, hormone imbalances and autoimmune diseases. Dr. Hedberg founded the Immune Restoration Center in Asheville, NC where he helps patients become healthy again with a special emphasis on the immune system. Dr. Hedberg trains health care professionals in functional medicine at www.infectionconnection.net with a special emphasis on the connection between infections and autoimmune diseases.
