Jeremy Larochelle's Summary

Jeremy is the perfect mix of a seasoned business professional. This proven entrepreneur, who once increased sales 212% over seven years for his own printing firm, now holds an undergraduate degree specialized in music business and is currently a MBA candidate with a concentration on marketing.

Today he is an agent and marketing specialist with Mike Moloney Entertainment out of Las Vegas.

As an agent, he works with a huge spectrum of artists from national acts to club performers, variety artists, tributes, and production dancers and singers. He helps place these performers in various venues that the organization books, be it casinos, special events, hotels, cruise ships, and their own touring collection of full-scale production shows.

As a marketing specialist, Jeremy assists both MMEC and the venues they partner with in promoting the talent they represent to the right demographic through traditional and modern marketing channels. On top of that he facilitates the business to business marketing strategies of MMEC that are designed to accomplish the goals of expanding both the firm’s pool of talent and the venues they book.
