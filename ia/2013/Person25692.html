Cliff Keith - Real Estate Broker 650.597.1821's Summary

Helping clients buy & sell homes & investment properties on the SF Peninsula for the past 36 years, has given me the ability to know what is important to you when you spend the most money for a single purchase or net cash to you like you never have before in a single transaction. Location is the number one mantra in real estatet, timing dictates success or failure, but having someone with the experience and knowledge base to make sure you will take the greatest advantage of both location and timing as you make your real estate decisions. My goal for my clients is to get them the best price, the least effort and hurdles in the quickest time possible. I will use superior due diligence to make sure that pertinent facts about your new home are disclosed, and that when it comes to selling your home at a future date, the property is still very much desirable. What is most important to my clients come first: I have on occasion, advised my clients not to buy a home, or not to sell a property if I thought doing so was not in their best interest. In my 36 years as a Realtor, I've seen 4 different business cycles, but I seen time and time again good homes always sell. Because of the 36 years of experience my knowledge and expertise has given me the wisdom to know what makes a home worth owning, for both quality of life and return of investment. I am a California Real Estate Broker. I have been helping families increase their net worth since 1976! My negotiations skills alone are worth the money I receive. Every real estate agent knows the rules, however, it is the older ones that knows the exceptions.
