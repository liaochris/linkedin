Megg Sohn's Summary

Contact at meggsdesign@gmail.com.
Portfolio at www.meggsdesign.com.

An entrepreneur and life long volunteer, Megg Sohn offers 20 years experience in comprehensive branding and marketing: multi-channel advertising and promotions strategy, graphic and UX design, special events and sponsorship, retail interiors and signage, trade show and museum exhibits.

With a focused education in branding, fine art, photography and the history of art and design, Megg has a B.S. in Art from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo, with a concentration in graphic design and studied design in London at the Central St. Martins College of Design.
