Becky Harmon's Summary

Wife of Jay (28 years), mother of four wonderful children and CEO of www.Successnotsabotage.com. I specialize in helping entrepreneurs and executives create new clarity, boundaries and strategies that help them gain more peace and provision. I facilitate this from a personal and marketplace perspective.

I serve individuals who have a desire to live a purposed life and are prepared to build a lifestyle of leadership that will equip them to model an authentically BOLD identity with less stress and more grace.

In order to empower my clients and assist them in achieving consistent long-term success, I have systems and teams to help them plug into in addition to receiving personal coaching from me. This gives them an opportunity to not just gain more momentum but build a network of individuals who will support them long-term.

I also provide consulting for new and established faith-based entrepreneurs and ministry leaders. I specialize in helping them block their time efficiently, build a brand without hype, create successful teams, market authentically without a big budget and I aim in all my consults to help people double their results with simple strategies. One of the things I am most proud of is that I help my clients build a profitable business that fuels their spiritual life, marriage and family.

Specialties: Lead Generation, Church Planting, Small Business Marketing, Business Coaching, E-books, Online Marketing, Teams, Women Speakers, Free Coaching Resources, Leadership Coaching, Sales Strategies, Addiction Recovery, Conflict Resolution, Author, Entrepreneur, Half Marathon Training, Weight Loss
