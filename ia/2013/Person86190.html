Marita Roebkes' Summary

Founding partner and co-founder of Society3 with 8 years experience in social media and social media monitoring.

My business expertise spans shareholder relationship management, partner and alliance relationship management, international business affairs, as well as marketing and and product management.

Prior to Society3 and the Social Media Academy, I was the co-founder of Infinigate , BlueRoads, and Xeeme. In my role as investor relations manager I accompanied Infinigate from first investments to IPO. I worked with major brands such as SAP, Avaya, Hitachi, HP, Juniper Networks, Nortel, Red Hat, Sun Microsystems, IBM, and others.

My early career included various sales, marketing and operations roles in the Hotel, Travel & Tourism industry in Europe.
