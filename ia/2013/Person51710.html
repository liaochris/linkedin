Harlan Mittag, DC's Summary

Dr. Harlan Mittag received his bachelor’s degree in Human Biology from Stanford University in 1973. In 1981, he enrolled at Northwestern College of Chiropractic, completing his doctorate degree in 1986. While at Northwestern he was also certified to practice acupuncture.

In April, 1987 he co-founded Crossroad Chiropractic Clinic. He had a clinical practice at Crossroad Chiropractic in the western suburbs of Minneapolis for the next 19 years, until 2006. In 2006 he founded Mittag Holistic Chiropractic in St. Louis Park, MN. Dr. Mittag has completed the Diplomate curriculum in clinical nutrition provided by the Chiropractic Board of Clinical Nutrition. He has received numerous certifications in functional medicine, herbal medicine, energy healing (Reiki, Reconnective Healing, Spring Forest Qi Gong) and craniosacral therapy (Upledger). He has made numerous presentations on “Whiplash Soft Tissue Injuries” to attorneys in Minnesota, including presentations at the annual convention of the Minnesota Trial Lawyers Association. He is a provider for most local insurances, including Medica, Health Partners, Preferred One, BCBS and others. Specialties: Nutritional counseling utilizing nutritional supplements, diet and herbs: acupuncture; energy healing; chiropractic; applied neurology utilizing the cold laser.

In 2008 Dr. Mittag began learning how to build websites and sell nutritional supplements. That year he also began marketing his own clinic online using SEO and video's. Gradually friends began asking for his help with their internet marketing needs.

In 2009 Dr. Mittag began his business helping other local businesses to market themselves online. He is the CEO of Local Web Marketing Consultants which gets local businesses in front of their customers online.
