Erich Buerger's Summary

Erich Buerger is an Enterprise e-Commerce Architect at RipeVine, Inc. He has held several key positions in several e-Commerce engagements. For over 14 years he has worked in management roles on many enterprise level e-commerce B2C/B2B engagements for a variety of Retail, Consumer Product, and Digital companies. Working directly with top level management in these organizations, he has worked to strategize, conceptualize, develop, and deploy their solutions using state of the art e-commerce technologies. Most recently, he has been working with clients that are focused on improving their e-Commerce implementations effectiveness and efficiencies through user experience design, sales, marketing channels, streamlined checkout processes, fulfillment logistics, and improved payment processing. This includes strategies to leverage integration of e-Commerce platforms to Mobile, SEO, SEM, Social Media Marketing, Blogging and more. In addition, he has been involved with developing strategies to improve backend fulfillment and logistics. His strength and passion is in Web Modernization exercises to further grow his clients enterprises and leapfrog their respective competitors.

Some of the e-Business clients he has implemented for include: Amazon.com, Borders Books, RitzCamera.com, David's Bridal, Weight Watchers, FannieMayChocolates.com, Sanyo, Toshiba, Harry & David, T-Mobile, Avery Dennison, ePuck.com, BoatersWorld.com, StoryBids, RazorReef.com, Kozmo.com, VF Corporation, Target, Mattel, IHS, Nissan, Playaway, Entepreneur.com, Warner Chappell, McKesson, and IBM.

Mr. Buerger was an e-Commerce Instructor at California State University, Fullerton and holds a B.S. in Information and Computer Science from University of California, Irvine.

Extensive Experience e-Commerce platforms: IBM WebSphere Commerce, IBM Sterling Selling and Fulfillment Suite (OMS/DOM, CPQ, Visual Modeler Rules), IBM Smarter Commerce Foundation
