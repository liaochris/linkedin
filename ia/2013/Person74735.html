Kevin Alter's Summary

I am an Event, Experience and Broadcast Environment Designer with 20 years experience providing unique solutions for leading companies. I bring an innate ability for realizing creative solutions for the integration of marketing and style into the built environment as well as a true passion for exhibit and set design principles. I have designed projects for clients such as Alcon Laboratories, GM, Chrysler Corp, Coors, Nokia, and Lennox International.

Specialties: - Background in the managing, planning and budgeting of design activities
- High-quality solutions in very demanding environments
- Integration of creative solutions within a practical business model
- Establishment, perpetuation and maintenance of creative studios
- Extensive presentation capabilities
