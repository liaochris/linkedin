Janice Lindstrom's Summary

I am a Board Certified Music Therapist with over 15 years experience working in a variety of settings and with a variety of populations, including Autism and other neurological disorders. I help parents of children with disabilities make life fun again by using music to connect with their child.

I host a BlogTalkRadio show called The Music Therapy Show, where you can call in each week to ask questions about music therapy and Autism or other neurological disorders.

I speak to parent groups and support groups about how music therapy works for their specific need and offer some examples on ways to use music on your own to achieve specific outcomes.
