Deven Langston's Summary

I am a motion graphic artist with professional experience in television advertising and web development. My animation has won several awards and been featured in events such as The West Virginia Flash Animation Festival, The Dallas Video Festival and the New Genre Festival in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I ride a super sweet bike and have a pencil in my ear. I believe that we judge ourselves by what we feel capable of doing, but others judge us by what we have done. I strive close that gap.

Specialties

2D/3D motion graphics, animation, visual effects (green screen, 3D match-moving, rotoscoping), compositing, general post-production, DVD authoring, interactive Flash development, web design
