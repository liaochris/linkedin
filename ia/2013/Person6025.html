Amy Ostigny, CPSM's Summary

With more than a decade of experience, Amy Ostigny and her staff provide a diverse array of Professional Marketing Services, including Electronic Marketing and Social Media, Event Planning, Sponsorships and Fundraising, along with Public Relations, Technical Writing, New Business Development and Association Management Services.

Amy Ostigny Company has been instrumental in the membership growth of many non-profit associations, such as the U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC), International Facility Management Association (IFMA) and International Interior Design Association (IIDA). Amy is also the Managing Director of the Cincinnati Chapter of eWomenNetwork, the #1 resource for connecting and promoting women in business.
