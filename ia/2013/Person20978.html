Catherine Palmiere's Summary

Adam Personnel, Inc. is a certified Woman-Owned New York staffing and recruiting agency servicing the Tri-State Area. We specialize in the following industries: Real Estate/Investment/Financial Services/Non-Profit/Insurance. We also specialize in legal staffing the NYC. We are experts in placing accounting, administrative, information technology, bookkeeping, financial/banking and human resource professionals on a direct-hire and temporary/temp to hire basis.

I have worked with hundreds of corporations and thousands of applicants, and am a sought after speaker on hiring and professional development. I take all the pain out of your hiring process – only sending the two or three BEST candidates for your position.

I hold eight industry certifications from Certified Employment Interview Consultant to Certified Professional Behavioral Analyst along with an M.B.A. in Management. I am also a member of the Society of Human Resource Management, and Career Directors International, along with being listed in numerous editions of Marquis Who’s Who.

I have been quoted in numerous on-line and in print articles pertaining to staffing and retention. I am also the co-author of the book “Unlocking the Door to Your Career.” In addition to my own staffing business I also conduct workshops and seminars at colleges and training schools on Career Development. I’m also an Adjunct Instructor at Manhattan College where I teach in the School of Business.

I’m a native New Yorker who enjoys exercising and making sure I laugh at least three times a day, which are two things that have helped me complete two New York City Marathons.

I know I can add a unique perspective to your hiring needs. If you are looking for your next opportunity, I'd welcome the opportunity to add hope to your search.

My specialties include: Staffing and retention, interview techniques, salary negotiation, career management, career coaching, resume critique, job opportunities, and talent acquisition.
