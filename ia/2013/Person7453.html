Andy Howard's Summary

I create digital campaigns and strategies, design interfaces, engineer web technology and manage teams. I've lead digital campaigns and projects for clients including CountryLink, Royal Bank of Scotland, Smirnoff, Sydney Airport and Harley-Davidson. I've worked with digital agencies and clients in Newcastle, Sydney, London, Edinburgh and Seattle.

I've lectured in digital and integrated marketing communications to the Masters of Marketing class at the University of Newcastle and have delivered digital marketing workshops for marketing managers and business owners. I'm a Slingshot tech startup program mentor.

Specialties: Digital Strategy, Digital Marketing, Advertising, Social Media Integration, Branding, SEO, SEM, Online Media, CRM, User Experience (UX) Design, Information Architecture (IA), Cloud Computing, LAMP, JavaScript, WordPress, iPhone, Mobile, User Centred Design (UCD), Usability, Wireframing, Prototyping, Production, API, Project Management, Interaction Design (IxD), User Testing, User Research, Copywriting, HTML, CSS, Adobe Fireworks.
