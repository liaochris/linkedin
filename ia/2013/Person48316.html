Gery Deer's Summary

Nominated 2010 Pulitzer Prize for Journalism
Nominated 2010 Ohio Public Image Award for Media

Writer, Public Relations and Marketing Strategist.
Unique and diverse background in publishing, entertainment, engineering and mechanical technology. Industrial specialization in product prototype development testing, and human factors engineering, collecting data in oil refinery time/job studies.

Involved heavily in the entertainment industry - stage variety performance, artist placement, specializing in bullwhip, magic, knife throwing and comedy/music shows. Performed and consulted with television, film and web production companies including Tribune Entertainment, Universal Studios, NBC and Warner Brothers television.

Currently serve as the expert business contributor to WDTN-TV2's "Living Dayton" talk show. Provide content and guests for the show and produce the segments I am involved with. Also provide weekly column to several print and web-based publications including the Xenia Daily Gazette and Fairborn Daily Herald. Author of "DEER IN HEADLINES" editorial column, published regionally each week since 2008.

Started, operated and and sold two businesses since 1994 and currently enjoying a successful career as a freelance commercial writer and marketing consultant.

Specialties: Business communications,public relations, developing business presentations, speech writing, feature stories, press releases, white papers, e-books, and web content copywriting and editing. Also work with clients to gain media attention and exposure to potential customer base.
