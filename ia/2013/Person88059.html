Mary Elaine Kiener, RN, PhD's Summary

Mary Elaine Kiener, RN, PhD is the Founder and Creative Energy Officer (CEO) of ASK ME House, LLC. Ever the “Resource Queen” she offers simple, yet elegant resources that help clients compassionately transform stress into healing energy.

Her own life experiences have offered her countless opportunities to learn and practice the art of self-care amidst a wide range challenging circumstances. By her early-40’s, mismanaged stress within both her personal and professional lives collided, culminating in a fairly major health “meltdown”. As part of her own healing journey, Mary Elaine learned to honor and embrace her own uniqueness while finding her way forward to health and well-being.

Applying the best of what she learned along the way, Mary Elaine has created a virtual nursing practice that assists her clients to create and nurture their own healing journeys. Her services include:

* Stresswell™ -- offering clients an individualized, strengths-based approach for transforming stress into well-being.

* Juice Plus and Tower Garden -- assisting individuals and families to increase their intake of fruits and vegetables as a way to help “bridge the gap” to better health.

* Courageous Self-Care™ -- helping family caregivers, professional health care providers and community leaders learn to care for themselves as well as they do for others.

Whether interacting with Mary Elaine one-on-one or as part of a group presentation, one thing is certain: She will stimulate your mind, touch your heart and engage your spirit.
