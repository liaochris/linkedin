Dr. Ilene Ringler's Summary

Dr. Ilene Ringler has over thirty years of experience supporting business and individuals in meeting their growth goals with a straightforward, easy to use process based on personal values. She has supported many leading edge companies such as Textron, Bose, Oracle, Fidelity Investments, International Rectifier, Smartbridge, Albertsons, The 99 Dollar Furniture Store and All Season Wholesale Growers. Values are important to your business because they:

• Align your vision, mission, strategic plan and culture for maximum productivity, better-quality decision-making, and reduced costs.
• Optimize your ability to quickly discover the perfect customers, suppliers, strategic partners, and employees.
• Show customers, suppliers, strategic partners, and employees why they should choose you.
• Increase your satisfaction and relight your passion.

Specialties

Strategy – Integration of values in your vision, mission and strategy.
Innovation- Values aligned cultures based on a balance of innovation and continuous improvement.
Recruit – Meet recruitment needs are quickly and cost effectively.
Retain - Increase retention, yielding returns surpassing your investment.
Develop - Create continuous learning and development cultures.
Team - Build strong and effective teams who share your passion and vision.

Dr. Ilene Ringler's Publications

  • Motivational Fit and Organizational Systems

    • The Journal of Business Leadership
    • December 1, 2010
    Authors: Dr. Ilene Ringler

    Information about how work is accomplished through various organizational systems can support informed choices for workers and leaders who are seeking a strong motivational fit between personal motivation and organizational systems. This article describes two of these systems; open and closed, and rational and natural including cultural features of each system within an organization. The reader can use this information and the examples presented to understand how these organizational systems may increase the fit between personal and organizational culture.
