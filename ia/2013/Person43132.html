Эдуард Сёмин's Summary

Assisting people and companies to improve. International education, extensive professional experience in business advisory and lifelong self-development practices allow Edward to deliver inclusive business consulting services in the areas of:

- Commercial Improvements
- Corporate Transformations and Change Management
- Development of Productive Environments
- Internal Management Systems, IT based Infrastructures
- Due Diligence Services

Edward started as a business analyst in 1999. Edward worked as an employed professional for 10 years and accomplished a variety of projects as a consultant within the teams of several leading international advisory companies operating in the region. Edward has acquired expertise in several industries, including: finance, metallurgy, pharmaceuticals, aviation, retail, agro, gas&oil, building and construction materials. The projects included due diligence for M&A transactions, business strategy, turnaround plan development, business processes, restructuring and organizational design.

Edward has also been active in social and professional development projects. In 2003-04 Edward participated as a project coordinator of Accounting Reform in Ukraine on behalf of the American Counsels for International Education.

Currently, Edward facilitates several projects in the areas of managerial solutions, business leadership and innovations. Edward has also developed and promotes entrepreneur empowerment and personal development programs.
