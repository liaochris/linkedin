Carl Fischer's Summary

Carl Fischer is a founder of CamaPlan, a national self-directed IRA company. He is a national and state certified educator of professionals and provider of continuing education for attorneys, financial planners, CPAs and realtors. Carl is the one professionals turn to when they need answers. He speaks nationally about the advantages of using qualified plans to build wealth. His wisdom, understanding and years of experience working in real estate, tax rules and regulations, traditional and non-traditional asset classes and legal documentation make him a well respected resource in the industry. Carl was a former owner of Global Financial Resources, a mortgage broker company, and he is a third generation real estate investor and president of Presidential Properties. Carl is and has been active in national and state real estate associations. From 1977 to 1995, he launched rockets at Kennedy Space Center and Cape Canaveral Air Force Station.

Carl Fischer's Experience

Principal Owner/Certified Financial Services Professional

CamaPlan

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Financial Services industry

November 2003 – Present (9 years 10 months) Blue Bell, PA

Self-directed IRAs and 401(k) plans provide the flexibilty to invest your IRA in real estate, notes, private placements, precious metals, tax liens and almost anything else that the IRS does not prohibit. Self-direction also allows for clients to control their IRA investments and use their expertise and knowledge to build wealth and plan for their future. With a CamaPlan self-directed IRA, you are in full control and can choose from a wide range of permissible assets.

CamaPlan is a third party administrator as required by the IRS for self-directed accounts. Part of our service is keeping records of your investment transactions and reporting to the IRS accordingly. We offer personalized self-directed IRA planning and customer service, as well as free financial education through our Cama Academy to help you make the right investment choices and perform the proper due diligence.

A CamaPlan gives you access to a multitude of investments that have historically only been available to the very wealthy and the elite. It’s what brokers won’t tell you about because it has no commissions or kickbacks, and is based solely on your requirements. It’s a plan that you direct which has the maximum tax-free and tax-deferred benefits allowed by law.

Please contact me to learn more about the types of plans we offer at cfischer@camaplan.com
