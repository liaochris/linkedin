Adrianne Ahern, Ph.D's Summary

Adrianne Ahern, Ph.D. – A dedicated professional who is constantly upgrading her skills. She is committed to helping people with a wide range of problems and guiding them in living lives with greater joy, health, love, success, and friendship.

Dr. Ahern is a licensed clinical and consulting psychologist with a private practice in the Carmel Valley/Del Mar area of San Diego, California. Dr. Ahern specializes in brief psychotherapy, cognitive-behavioral techniques with an interesting blend of energy psychology tools, performance enhancement and the pursuit of excellence. She has a depth of training and a wealth of experience in helping people break free from negative patterns and rewire their brains with thoughts and behaviors that are in alignment with what they truly want to be, do and have in life. Dr. Ahern has published two books in her field of expertise, “Snap Out Of It NOW!” (Sentient, June 2007) and “Back In Charge!” (Sentient, June 2009), and produced an American Public Television 90-minute special, “Snap Out Of It NOW! with Dr. Adrianne Ahern.” (APT, Spring 2009).

Specialties

Dr. Adrianne Ahern is a San Diego CA psychotherapist and coach, specializing in a unique blend of cognitive-behavioral psychology and mindfulness based meditative tools.
Her work with her clients provides them with a life-long approach to achieving success and well-being. Adrianne's commitment is toward having the client achieve an independence and self-confidence. Her joy is in helping people discover their own creativity and achieve greater satisfaction and fulfillment in life.
