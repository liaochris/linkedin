John Glozek's Summary

Was a professional musician, played drums for 25 years. Worked at Grumman Aerospace in Bethpage for about 6 years. Had secret clearance and spent some time at NASA in California.
Have been publishing a golf magazine on Long Island for 20 years. Expanded the company (Golfing Magaizne) 5 years ago with national distribution.
Big fan of Jeffrey Gitomer - the all time sales motivating guru.

Specialties

Print and online production, layout & design, sales, administration, networking.
