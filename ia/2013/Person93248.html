Mícheál Morgan's Summary

Pursuit of mastery both in entrepreneurism and crafting technology are two constant passions that thread throughout my career. This ranges from building high availability Software-as-a-Service to the complexities of high transaction e-commerce and Nuclear Quality Assurance. Career ranges a broad spectrum of clients from Fortune 500 companies such as Ford Lincoln and Clear Channel Communications to SMBs and startups. Business experience includes starting private companies along with sitting on the board and serving as Chief Technology Officer for the publicly traded technology company, Vidaroo Corporation.

Simply, live and breathe product development. Contribute to ensuring product aligns with sales initiatives. Technical experience ranges from low-level architecture to API development along with cross-platform desktop applications. There is no problem too big or too challenging. Am most happy when responsible for a full 360 degrees of product development including creative direction, project management (SCRUM product owner), architecture and writing code on a daily basis.

Advocate of user experience with a primary focus on usability. Design drives development at all levels. An open source contributor with a keen interest in Qt C++ and Kohana frameworks. Listed as one of the top 10 most prolific Kohana module authors.
