Mike Haeg's Summary

My daily routine consists of 1.) Wake up 2.) Try to do some awesome things 3.) Go to bed

Somewhere in there, I love working alongside people and technology. Better yet, fusing them together so my company and other businesses achieve more. I like taking big picture ideas, carving our short term strategies, and getting things done. Problems are not always black and white for me, so I think it’s important to whittle through the haze and get back to basics.

I’m fascinated by the people I come across in both my work and personal life. The vision and accomplishments of others inspires me. This could be a savvy teenage entrepreneur, 80 year old retired Veteran, or my colleague two desks down. Without their expressed written consent, I try to learn from them and they become de factor mentors. If you’re reading this, that could very well be you. Hopefully, I can provide a little inspiration to someone else. If not, let’s talk about how I can be better.
