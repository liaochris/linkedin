Gillian Hutshing's Summary

As a feature film editor in Los Angeles for over 20 years, Ms. Hutshing has worked with acclaimed directors Sir Ridley Scott, Kevin Costner, Ben Affleck among others.  Ms. Hutshing just completed a film for Warner Bros. produced by David Geffen. "Sparky the Wonderfish: A Story of How Love Creates Magic" is her first children's book.  Ms. Hutshing is involved in many children's charities and literacy programs throughout the US.  And is currently working on a series of Big Word Books to teach children big words in a fun and humorous way.

Ms. Hutshing has repeatedly been a featured author and title at Book Expo America, West Hollywood Book Fair, LA Times Festival of Books, Orange County Children's Book festival, Orange County Department of Education Young Authors' Faire, and the Santa Barbara Writers Conference.

Ms. Hutshing has had numerous signings and events at Barnes & Noble Booksellers and Borders Bookstores throughout California. She is on the Advisory Board and is Booksellers Chairman for the Greater Los Angeles Writers Society (GLAWS) and Chairman of the Orange County Children's Book Fair Award Committee (OCCBF).

Book Festival To Honor Authors and Illustrators

The Orange County Children's Book Festival will honor authors, illustrators and innovators in children's literature at its First Annual Orange County Children's Author and Illustrator Book Awards. A gala awards presentation is planned for the evening of Saturday, October 1, 2011 and will be held in The Forum on the campus of Orange Coast College in Costa Mesa.

Honorees will be selected and acknowledged for outstanding work in the field of children's writing and illustration from a global pool, in a variety of categories each year. Recipients will be selected by a panel of qualified literary experts from a variety of backgrounds.
