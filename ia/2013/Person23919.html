Christian Hunter's Summary

Christian Hunter is one of America’s most successful and influential direct marketing entrepreneurs who sold his first startup for 7-figures just one year out of high school. He has since bootstrapped nearly a dozen consumer-facing, online businesses that, collectively, created over 50,000 jobs, generated roughly $1 billion in revenues, and range from still-growing names in consumer photo-retouching and pet-health sectors, to more well-known businesses like Bargain Network, and inQ.com (now TouchCommerce.com).

Mr. Hunter today provides guidance and various resources to a half-dozen developing projects and upstarts within his venture management portfolio. Most of his input is geared towards leveraging latent social marketing opportunities, building and automating evolutionary marketing systems, and developing "prosumption" networks (per Alvin Toffler's definition) as a primary means of amplifying media messaging, generating response, and developing content.

Christian Hunter is a proponent of Agile SCRUM development methodology, a Lean Startup evangelist, amateur bio-hacker, and H+ supporter.
