David Owens-Hill's Summary

As a communications professional with experience in project management, I seek new ways to bring order to an ever-complicated world. Be it cutting through the clutter of the many messages presented to every audience everyday; explaining complex, conceptual ideas to an internal audience; designing a system for encouraging sustainable capacity building; or managing the production of a communication device from concept to design, I believe in the power of change through organizational cooperation.

In each successively-responsible position I have held in the nonprofit and higher education industries, my mission has been singular: seek organizations that are doing interesting things and throw every ounce of my energy behind those efforts.
