John Ringgold's Summary

As a top Topeka Real Estate agent, by using systems and setting goals, I developed my business into a successful real estate machine. With my knowledge of technology in real estate and my patience and understanding of daily real estate issues, I then had the opportunity to take the position as the Supervising Broker at Coldwell Banker Griffith & Blair American Home in Topeka Kansas.

I was heavily involved with both the Topeka Area Association of REALTORS® and the Kansas Association of REALTORS® by participating on many committees and being elected to the leadership of both associations by committing my time to be on the Board of Directors and the Executive Committee of both associations.

Now as the President / CEO of Real Estate Business Resources, I get the opportunity to search out and share new technology tools for Real Estate Brokers and REALTORS® across the state and soon nationally!

Specialties: Real Estate Technology, Wordpress, Facebook, Video for REALTORS®, Real Estate Training, Real Estate, Foreclosures and short sales, real estate client follow up systems and plans, using technology in real estate to be profitable, Blogging.
