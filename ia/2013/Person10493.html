Karin Johanek's Experience

CEO & Chief Inspiration Officer

Aspire Consulting, Inc.

January 1997 – Present (16 years 8 months)

Karin Johanek, Aspire’s founder, CEO and Chief Inspiration Officer, is an energetic learning strategist, trainer, speaker, author and leader who has delivered measurable results for businesses around the world over the past 20 years.

Karin founded Aspire Consulting, www.aspireconsulting.com, in 1997 and since then has worked with thousands of clients to build custom solutions that increase talent, growth and results.

An energetic and committed business leader, Karin and her team help global brands achieve breakthrough results by utilizing proven best practices in organizational development, adult learning, instructional design, change management, leadership consulting, and revenue optimization.

Karin began her career with Carlson Companies holding several leadership roles with responsibility for operations and performance improvement for the organization.

Karin is best known for her energetic approach to collaborating with organizational leaders to inspire possibilities and target outcomes that lead to breakthrough solutions.

Her engaging speaking style has educated and entertained people from around the globe. Karin is often found in the space she loves - leading strategic planning sessions, piloting new programs with key leaders, facilitating workshops, and certifying other learning professionals.

Energy, expertise and value are common words used to describe the innovative strategies that Karin and her team have been delivering for years.
