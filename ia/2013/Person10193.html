John Asdourian's Summary

John has been a licensed Broker Associated with McGuire Real Estate since 1998. Previously, John was associated with Coldwell Banker Real Estate Services from 1987-1998.

An industry leader, Asdourian was a Director of the San Francisco Association of Realtors from 1999-2008, and served as its chief financial officer in 2005, and president in 2007. Asdourian was the 2008 S. F. Regional Chair of Directors at CAR.

Specialties

Sales San Francisco high-end residential and income properties, probate and trust sales.
