Elmer Kerr's Summary

PMM is Ireland's leading merchandising & marketing company with national blue chip clients such as Independent News & Media, Road Safety Authority of Ireland (RSA), Allied Irish Bank, LIDL, Castrol, Johnson & Johnson, Ulster Bank, Calor Gas, Teva Pharmaceutical, Bank of Ireland, Sanofi-Aventis, Amscan UK Party Zone, Banking 365, DeRee UK & Dyson.

PMM operates "real-time" field reporting 24/7 for clients with its competitive-edge industry award winning Mobile Researcher program including digital photography. Know what is happening in your retail network daily!

PMM offers retail field merchandising, sales development operating full-time managed client teams,marketing services, retail auditing & consultancy, mystery shopping, warehousing & distribution and bespoke training programs to the retail sector.

PMM merchandises in retail stores, Tesco, Dunnes, SuperValu, Centra, Woodies DIY, Pharmacies, Hardward & Garden Centres, Homecare, B&Q, Easons, Independent Newsagents, M&S and Topaz Filling Stations.

PMM Retail is a Skillnet approved training partner with bespoke & tailored courses in Sales & Marketing Strategies, Customer Service, Selling Skills and In-Store Merchandising

PMM has reached an historic landmark in 2013 by completing 1,000,000+ (one million!) retail merchandising calls in Ireland.

PMM, 24 years in business with 160+ nationwide dedicated field staff (full-time & part-time) servicing every town, city district and county throughout Ireland (32 counties)
