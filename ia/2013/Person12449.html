Bean Lucas' Summary

It's the 21st century: the old engineering cliché of "Fast, good, or cheap: You can pick two" no longer holds, in my experience. The quality of software, hardware and data services has accelerated very fast and progressed very far. A decade ago, a half-decade ago, we expected that technology would fail at some point to meet our business and professional demands. Today, we can all count on meeting, exceeding, and changing the nature of our demands, because technology has matured and diversified faster than our habits and expectations have.

I want to work in an agile if not extreme fashion, producing satisfying and powerful solutions for people who have work to do and goals to meet.

Nobody can afford to let technology pass them by, or impede their productivity, or drain their purse. Technology should be available, accessible, affordable, and effective. I aim to help working people and organizations experience Information Technology and Services the way it should be.

I've seen and done it all: from monolithic "Big IT", highly formal classical engineering of software, and elaborate, years-long managed projects with dizzying complexity and interdependencies, all the way to daring, seat-of-the-pants agility and highly modularized solutions, developed in near-real-time with iterative and incremental improvements.

The Internet technology alphabet soup, the network and protocol jargon, the software brand-names: I don't want to discuss all that with my clients. I want to discuss business goals and outcomes, and make supportable, extensible solutions that look like magic.

Specialties

Web Solutions is the name of the game in the 21st century. Research shows that custom development rarely pays off like off-the-shelf technology, whether it's commercial, open-source, proprietary or free. Don't pay me to write you a Web2.0 tool or a CMS, pay me to choose the most suitable, supportable, scalable and useable one available which will solve your immediate needs and grow with you as you develop new needs, and we'll write some code if there IS a good reason to customize your solution.
