Alex Beisser's Summary

I am an experienced senior Information Assurance professional leading on managing the Information Governance agenda for an acute NHS organisation. I focus on Data Protection, Compliance, Audit, Information Risk and Business Continuity Management, Freedom of Information utilising the ISO27000 standards to implement industry best practice is used to assure compliance to national legislation and healthcare specific guidelines.

I am ITIL, ISEB certified passed my CISA exam and have a wealth of expertise in access control measures to certify compliance against the ISO 27000 standards in relation to use of requirements of regulated medical data. I am a UKCHIP Level 2 registered Health Informatics Professional and I am working towards my CISM and further ISEB Certifications.

I gained extensive knowledge in the due diligence of information assurance and access control (identity management) workstreams in mergers and acquisitions within the NHS. Furthermore, I have wide-ranging experience leading the information assurance and access control work streams of several large-scale simultaneous IT Project implementations (EPR and email communication systems).

My background in IT management background helps me in showing others how to protect their and others' personal data and use it in an ethical acceptable way to support modern life, better healthcare and cloud computing.

I have a keen interest in using RFID and barcode technology to improve the safety and efficiency in healthcare. My work within the Caldicott Guardian network enhanced my understanding of the use of clinical and personal data to provide the best care to patients, vulnerable members of our society and public health in general.
