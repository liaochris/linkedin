Dana Theus' Summary

I'm a a leadership consultant and coach. I'm also a research-based advocate and motivational speaker for women's leadership & corporate change that achieves business results through gender-balanced culture initiatives.

In my coaching practice I support CEOs, executives, authors and entrepreneurs to develop personal brand and media strategies around thought leadership platforms. I also help high-potential employees develop Leadership Change skills to succeed on high profile projects. Though InPowerCoaching.com leading coaches and I provide a growing catalog of recorded and live coaching programs to help mid-level women and men get on the leadership track, and stand out in the leadership selection process.

In my consultancy I help companies find hidden productivity by leveraging best practices from women's leadership for all their employees. I also design and facilitate culture-shaping initiatives to integrate leader's personal styles with their corporate brand identity. I am certified as a facilitator in The PRIMES methodologies, and as Champion in Lean Six Sigma.

Capitalizing on a 25-year business career in branding, marketing, product management, strategic planning and public affairs I have worked for Fortune 50 companies, entrepreneurial startups, government and military agencies and non-profits. A prolific writer, my message of InPowerment resonates strongly with leaders. In 2012, I founded InPowerWomen.com to speak directly to professional women helping them access their authentic leadership potential. My articles are featured on InPower Women, Smartblog/Smartbrief on Leadership, Switch & Shift and The National Journal's Next America.

Specialties: Corporate Culture, Women's Leadership, Motivational Speaking, Transformation and Change, Thought Leadership, Leadership Development, Management Consulting, Leadership Coaching, Executive Facilitation, Personal Branding, Social Media, Visioning, Women's Empowerment
