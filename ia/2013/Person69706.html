James Patrick's Summary

Award-winning editorial and commercial photographer with additional experience as a journalist, marketer, podcast host and public speaker.

What all this means is that I am a storyteller. As a storyteller, what is unique about my position is that I have a bevy of mediums in which I can craft and share my stories.

I began my career as a journalist working as a staff writer and section editor for several newspapers and magazines which segued into a passion in photography

Focusing onto editorial and commercial photography, I have had my work appear in a variety of local and national publications and commercial projects.

Samples of my work are available on my website www.jamespatrick.com

You may also connect with me through my Facebook Page at www.facebook.com/jamespatrickphotography

Specialties

Photography, graphic design, public speaking, marketing, journalism, presenting, project management.
