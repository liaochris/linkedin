    Here’s a dose of reality: the U.S. municipal wireless market has grown from zero to nearly $400 million in annual revenues in just four years. In the next four years, the market is expected to grow by more than 35% percent—each year. More than 400 municipalities in the U.S. have either planned or deployed networks. Local governments have saved tens of millions of dollars in improved efficiencies using municipal wireless networks, and they’ve only just begun.
