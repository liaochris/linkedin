Glenn Andert's Summary

I currently run 8Interactive, where we build the 'Read Me Stories' app that delivers a new book to Sammy EVERY DAY he opens the app! It's designed to build the love and habit of reading, and skills too! It's the top reading app for kids in Education iPad in the US, UK, CA, etc.

I also invest in and sit on the boards of other technology companies.

The 2005 to 2008 years saw me sailing about 16,000 nautical miles between California and New Zealand. That activity has a whole new set of learning curves!
