Jeffrey Unger's Summary

Jeff Unger is the founder and CEO of eMinutes (http://eminutes.com), a law firm that will waive its legal fees for first time entrepreneurs. Jeff's law practice exclusively focuses on the formation, structuring, and maintenance of corporations and LLCs. He is also a frequent expert witness in corporate matters, including corporate formalities for closely held corporations, entity formation, and alter ego liability. Dividing his time between Los Angeles and New York, Jeff is a leading expert on choice of jurisdiction.
