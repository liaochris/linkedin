Jordan Cram's Summary

Jordan Cram is an entrepreneur with proven experience creating brand value, building teams, growing a worldwide enterprise customer base, and establishing sustainable competitive advantage. Work experience over the past 15 years in the Middle East, Europe and North America includes founding two multi-million dollar business in technology services and business consulting. Jordan has a BS in Architectural Engineering, Univ. of Colorado, a MBA from Columbia Business School, and MBA from London Business School. Jordan has been based in New York City since 2000.

Specialties

Entrepreneurship; business consulting, technology services, capital projects management, facilities and asset management
