Michael T. Blair, CPA's Summary

Since 1994 Blair & Associates, Inc. has helped a variety of small business clients and individuals accomplish their financial goals.

The company's experienced team act as trusted advisors to many small business and individual clients. Many of these professional relationships have grown into valued friendships.

Blair & Associates enjoys a solid client roster most of whom are Carroll County based. During the past 17 years, Blair & Associates, Inc., has refined its level of expertise in servicing the following industries: Construction, Real Estate, Manufacturing, Food Service, Professional Services, Nonprofit Organizations, and Retail establishments.

We are committed to providing tailored services across a wide range of tax, accounting, and business advisory needs to achieve each client’s unique financial goals.

Our success is based on our clients knowing that we are always available to them and that they are very important to us – especially during challenging economic times.
