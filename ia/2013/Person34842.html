Devin Cuffee, MBA's Summary

Devin Cuffee has an MBA in Marketing and has completed course work in business, marketing, law, music, and history. Cuffee is a marketing and sales specialists with a desire to see entrepreneurs and small businesses reach their goals. He has a passion for effective communication. As an entrepreneur himself, he can address the unique challenges of being an entrepreneur or business owner.

Cuffee's philosophy in marketing communications involves four key values:

• Customer Relations – Customers are number one, so he helps clients establish strong relationships through targeted content and communication.

• Marketing Strategy – Branding that’s trusted and memorable, Cuffee creates strategies that establish a loyal customer base around the targeted customer segment.

• Sales Tactics – Increase sales? Generate profits? Beat the competition? Yes, yes, and yes.

• Innovation – Cuffee thinks outside the box and creates new approaches to improving the effectiveness of marketing programs.

Devin Cuffee is a member of the American Marketing Association and National Black MBA Association.
