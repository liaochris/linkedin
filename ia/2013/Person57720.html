Jameson Moncrief's Summary

20 years of high level experience in the automotive fixed operations field, having been recognized for exceptional financial performance by both dealers & manufacturers while maintaining top-shelf CSI. Recent years have lead to the augmentation of these same skills in the independent & (specifically) the tire segment.

Having seen the enormous impact that menu presentations can have upon both financial product sales (in the F&I office) and on maintenance sales on the service drive, I began developing TireMenuManager in 2007, and completed the current version and incorporated Trinity Dealer Solutions in late 2009.

In addition to the tire-specific TireMenuManager product, Trinity Dealer Solutions, Inc also offers a wide range of services for both the franchised dealer & independent relative to fixed operations performance & warranty issues. The primary focus of Trinity Dealer Solutions, Inc. at present is the growing penetration of TireMenuManager into the replacement tire market, however on a limited basis, I offer:

On-site Dealership Fixed Operations Consulting
Service Drive Process Training
Service Advisor / Tire Salesperson Training
Repair Order Analysis
Maintenance Menu Systems
Advanced Pricing Systems
Production Systems Installation & Troubleshooting
Dealership Warranty Analysis & Audit Forbearance
IT / DMS Consulting
Advanced DMS Reporting & Templates
Application Development

If you are interested in seeing how Trinity Dealer Solutions, Inc. or TireMenuManager can help you & your organization, email me! All LinkedIn invitations from automotive or related industries are accepted!

Specialties

Trinity Dealer Solutions was incorporated to bring a fresh approach to the service drive sales process... the ability to present 100% of the product (available services) to 100% of the customers, 100% of the time.

TireMenuManager is the only software product available that has the ability to create tiered menus for tire sales "on the fly", presenting any combination of options (one, two, or three tire options), with road hazard, alignment, and tires with all products.

TireMenuManager excels where other products fail: staggered fitments, road hazards & adjustments, outside purchases, identifying up-size opportunities, and in general, helping your front line tire people become the "experts" that you know they can be. No other product gives you as much support on staggered fitments, service descriptions, and plus-size fitment as TireMenuManager, and is the ONLY product that will determine the manufacturer & plant of origin on a tire based on the DOT code.

From one of our customers & TireMenuManager users:

"Since our implementation of TireMenuManager in our store we have increased our tire sales 14% in what might be considered a fairly depressed economy. The product has transformed us from non-tire people to one of the most professional looking and acting tire outlets in the area." Randy Lucyk, President, MASE Inc.

In addition to the TireMenuManager product, Trinity also offers other important tools & services for both dealership fixed operations and the independent/tire retailer, and if we don't have it, we can create it for you. Contact us for a consultation.
