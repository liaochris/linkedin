Host/Founder/Producer

The Woman's Connection(R)

1994 – Present (19 years)

A television program I started in 1994 about “events shaping women’s lives and helping them gain authentic power on a personal or professional level”. When I started, which was a flute, Oprah, Jerry Springer and the likes were all bashing everyone and their wasn’t anything positive out their. I created this program focusing on women and how we could all connect to one another while empowering ourselves.

I started this show while I was out of work and wondering what I was going to do next. Consulting with someone from SCORE (Senior Corp of Retired Executives) I was asked to write a business plan for a television show on how to start a business. Not that I had ever done one prior but I jumped in. Mentioning to a friend what I was doing and what was going on with him, he suggested I call someone for more information about a television show. Our lunch was quite successful for me. He reviewed my 10 pages I had done and wound up with barely one. However, he told me about his show and what goes into producing. I left him and went an signed up for classes at the very beginning of MNN (Manhattan Neighborhood Network). After I started directing and doing technical work for everyone they got on my case and wondered when I would be starting my own show. It evolved from their. The women have been wonderful and it has been a win win for everyone.
