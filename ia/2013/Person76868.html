Kris Schindler, APR's Summary

I solve business problems with communications solutions.

Every day requires businesses to think more critically about communications initiatives. I consult all types and sizes of businesses (but primarily advertising, marketing and PR agencies) on how to be more competitive in today’s evolving business climate by maximizing efficiencies, leveraging team strengths and infusing the workplace with creativity. My media relations consulting centers on complex and challenging public issues management where media attention may influence public opinion and have a profound impact on outcome. I’m especially committed to helping young professionals chart gratifying career courses.

Known for providing creative, innovative, enthusiastic and forward-thinking leadership in a team environment, the driving force behind my success in delivering high-value business solutions is an interdisciplinary approach to integrated communications designs, which is fueled by my big-picture, progressive, entrepreneurial mindset. People I've worked with say they appreciate my “producer mentality,” resourcefulness and ability to identify and build synergetic relationships, which I leverage through traditional communication channels as well as within the social web.

In 2011 I followed my husband to Denver, where he grew up and has family. I've established roots in a historic neighborhood with easy access to many of the things I enjoy: books (Tattered Cover), movies (Denver Film Center), the outdoors (parks and Denver Botanic Gardens) and food and wine. The mountains are just a short drive away.

My experience crosses the globe, spans more than 20 years and includes servicing clients in consumer products & services, sports & entertainment, health & wellness, education, legal, lifestyle and travel & hospitality. Ideally I'd like to helm a team in or in close proximity to Denver. Until then, my schedule includes healthy doses of consulting, public speaking, graduate school and TEDx organizing.
