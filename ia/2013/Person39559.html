Ed Henkler's Experience

Business Mentor

COSY (Cognitive Operational Systems)

September 2012 – Present (1 year) Philadelphia, PA

COSY is developing Wayfinder, a software package that finds the location of a mobile platform. Applying the same principles we have developed in robotic navigation, Wayfinder processes video in real-time to supplement information from accelerometer and GPS sensors. It works in places like cluttered urban spaces and indoors, where GPS is not accurate and often not accessible.
One of COSY's first products, BlindNav, will permit a blind person to navigate an unfamiliar building without assistance or infrastructural modification.

Consultant

Annidis

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Medical Devices industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 3 months)

Annidis has developed an ocular pathology management system that integrates advanced multi-spectral imaging with analytic software for early detection and management of ocular pathologies such as Glaucoma, AMD and diabetic retinopathy. The system is scientifically engineered to capture health indicator data points and images from the deep retinal layers. The software integrates all data points and images into intuitive ocular health intelligence. This report is used to clinically assess a patient’s ocular health.
I am consulting with CEO and other senior leaders on various operational and strategic aspects of Philadelphia launch.
