John Randazzo COT, ROUB, OCS's Summary

Highly organized and detail oriented Military Veteran, Certified Ophthalmic Technician, Registered Ophthalmic Ultrasound Biometrist, and Ophthalmic Coding Specialist with over 23 years experience demonstrating excellence in patient care, employee supervision, training and skills. Self motivated in fast paced, high volume environments where proficiency, speed, and teamwork is required. An ideal position should include the opportunity to assist in growing a practice by expanding administrative and ophthalmic knowledge to support staff in order to deliver exceptional patient care.

He has lectured and conducted workshops in the workplace and at several major meetings for the past 10+ years including the annual meeting of the Joint Commission on Allied Health Personnel in Ophthalmology (JCAHPO) and the Association of Technical Personnel in Ophthalmology (ATPO).
