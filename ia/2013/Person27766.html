In addition, Lenox.com has won several awards from BizRate.com, Good Housekeeping, VeriSign, Webawards and others.

Stafford Design Consulting has been an integral part of the email marketing process. Working along side Lenox’s successful email marketing management and providing critical feedback and great creative work. With a huge importance on conversion, and the staff and analytic tools to track it – the work produced by Stafford Design Consulting was always under the microscope.
