Erik Radvon's Summary

Erik Radvon is an internationally recognized journalist, technical writer, communications manager, and author. Erik’s work has appeared in newspapers, magazines, comics, television, and online media.

Erik's 2009 article “Hackertown” served as a primary source for producers of the television program "The Real Story - Live Free or Die Hard." The show aired around the globe, including on the Smithsonian Channel in the United States, Discovery UK in the United Kingdom, and more channels internationally. Erik was a featured on-camera contributor to the TV show, providing detailed background information on real world computer hacking crimes.

Additionally, Erik contributes to Kabooooom.com, The Best Game Apps.com, the award winning Worcester Magazine, and leads inbound marketing efforts for a large IT services firm. He is also a published independent comic writer, having adapted Shakespeare's "Romeo and Juliet" for the anthology collection "Shakespeare Shaken."
