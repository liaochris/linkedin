Emily Klein's Summary

Experienced strategy consultant and advisor with global, mid-market and start-up organizations in the areas of organization design, change management, program management, performance management, strategic partnership development. Extensive client engagement, management and leadership experience with Department of Defense agencies, civilian agencies and private industry.

Board member to organizations including the Integrative Medicine Alliance, the Preeclampsia Foundation and the American Society for Training and Development, Greater Boston chapter. Previous experience consulting for WFD, Inc., and affiliate, LifeWork Strategies, leading innovators in implementing workforce solutions for Fortune 500 corporations. Co-author of forthcoming book, Workshift, (Palgrave Macmillan, 2014).

Specialties: Strategic planning/strategic partnership development, organization development, program management, performance management, change management, government reform legislation. Facilitator with expertise in leading and managing change, social business collaboration, managing distributed teams, and implementing flexible work/mobile work initiatives.

Emily Klein's Projects

  • Co-Author: Workshift: Future-Proof Your Organization for the 21st Century

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Emily Klein, Robyn Bews, Tim Lorman, Jason (Jay) Morwick

    Workshift is a comprehensive guide for leaders to bridge the gap between the traditional and evolving workplace. Through a stimulating blend of case studies, research, and storytelling, Workshift delivers a road map for taking organizations from awareness to adoption and provides examples of how all types of organizations can cut costs, increase productivity, recruit or retain top talent, and increase employee well-being. The authors are widely known experts on the subjects of telework, flexible work arrangements, and the virtual workplace.
