Joshua March's Summary

I'm the co-founder and CEO of Conversocial, a venture backed SaaS company. Conversocial's social customer service software is used by companies including Sephora, Groupon, Hertz, Tesco, Net A Porter, Waitrose, M&S and more to help them manage the fast growing flow of customer service enquiries they are receiving on Facebook and Twitter.

Previously I co-founded iPlatform, the UK’s leading social app development company (and the first official Facebook Preferred Developer Consultants in the UK), which was acquired by Betapond in September 2012.

From 2008-2010 I was the Chairman of the Facebook Developer Garage London, an official Facebook event running monthly for developers, entrepreneurs and marketers working on the Facebook platform, which I helped set up in 2007.

Specialties

entrepreneurship, technology, customer service, social customer service, social CRM
