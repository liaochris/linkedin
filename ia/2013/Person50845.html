So I integrated HETIC school (High School of Information Technology and Communication); a formation integrated in ECE School and validated by an engineering degree.

During these diversified formations, I acquired crossed skills in IT / Information Systems including all specialties related to the world of the Internet.

Through my professional experiences in communication agency (developer of web technologies and Project manager) and directly with the announcer / customer (SI Project Manager), I have acquired a good work culture concerning Information Technology projects.

As a technical staff - as well as outsourced in-branch services -, I justify strong knowledge of recent web / softwares development technologies. I can now rely on solid knowledge of SI production constraints and work practices in web / ICT. This allows me to have short-term and long-term overview of both functional and technical aspects of a project in order to match as close as possible with time and costs issues.
