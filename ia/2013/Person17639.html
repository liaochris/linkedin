CTO

Right Media

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

August 2003 – July 2007 (4 years)

As Right Media's CTO, I'm reponsible for the design and architecture of our various product lines. In addition, I work with our largest clients, like Yahoo, to help them get the most from our products.

I oversee a few different groups: technical operations (a 20-person team that keeps our systems running smoothly 24/7), analytics (leveraging the data we collect to predict how creatives and campaigns will perform), and Media Guard (auditing the creatives and sites in the exchange to protect advertisers and publishers).

Executive Vice President

Cetova

May 2001 – January 2003 (1 year 9 months)

Cetova developed a proprietary technology to vastly simplify financial analysis and reporting for large enterprises using ERP systems. I led the growth of Cetova from a technology prototype to a full-featured product. I sold and managed key accounts, including LVMH and Philips, de Pury, Luxembourg.

Vice President

October 1999 – April 2001 (1 year 7 months)

Logic Span specialized in helping companies use technology to improve their marketing strategies. I managed the real-time personalization practice, helping companies like American Express use their customer information to cross-sell and up-sell.
