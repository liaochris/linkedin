Max Muncey's Summary

At Quell, Max Muncey serves as a senior account executive in the automotive practice, working with may of the firm’s key automotive clients. Max provides account management, public relations strategy, media relations management, writing and event planning to clients such as Nexteer Automotive, Ricardo, Inc., and Akebono Brake Corporation. In addition Max manages and provides strategic direction to his clients’ social media channels.

Prior to working at Quell, Max served as an associate at a Detroit PR firm in its automotive practice. Max served many of the firm’s key automotive clients, including the North American International Auto Show, Polk, Osram Sylvania and Automotive Hall of Fame. In addition, Max was a lead member of the firm’s media relations and analysis team, providing clients and account teams with news and information relevant to their respective industries.

Max thrives when it comes to interacting with media and providing superior client service. He keeps the Quell team and his clients up to date on the latest automotive industry happenings with his daily “Q Auto News” e-mail blast.

Max has always been interested in working within the automotive industry. His dad has been an engineer at General Motors for more than 40 years and from an early age, Max realized that a career in engineering was not for him. He decided that a career in public relations was a perfect fit to complement his strong passion for all things automotive.

A graduate of Western Michigan University, Max earned a bachelor’s degree in public relations. He is a member of the Automotive Press Association, Automotive Public Relations Council and the Washington Automotive Press Association.
