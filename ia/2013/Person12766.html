Ben Benedetti's Summary

Ben Benedetti is currently employed by Sony Pictures Entertainment, as a member of the executive management for the studio's post production facilities. Benedetti's role is as both a facilities administrator as well as marketing and client service specialist. The facility, one of the largest in North America, provides complete post production service packages for A-List feature films and television clientele. These services include 4k digital intermediates, 4k television color correction, complete picture editorial, sound editorial/design and mixing. They also support digital remastering and deliverables for international distribution of all Sony's properties.

Ben has worked in the industry for the past twenty years as freelance producer and studio executive. He has extensive experience in management and administration of all phases of production including network, studio operations, and facilities management. Ben started his career as a post production service specialist working as an associate producer and post supervisor of many feature films and television productions, and at one time consulted as the head of post production for Disney Channel Original Programming.

He is a honorary affiliate member of the American Cinema Editors, the Producer’s Guild of America, the Motion Picture Sound Editors (MPSE), and sits on the Board of the Sales Resource Committee of the Hollywood Post Alliance.

Specialties: Facilities Administrator, Business Development and Client Services, and Strategic Operations. Post Production Service Provider - Digital Image Capture, 4K - 2K, HD mastering, Digital Intermediates, VFX, Sound Editorial, Sound Mix, Avid Systems Integration, Production and Post Production workflows.
