Cheryl Lynn Deschamps' Summary

I am a full-time professional REALTOR®, committed to excellence. My depth of experience in social helping fields has added immense value to my approach to real estate and therefore direct value to my clients. In addition, as a reflection of my past career in adult education and career development, I am active in mentoring and providing resources to others in the real estate profession.

I am very proud to represent Re/Max Nova and work with a network of agents across Canada. When my clients are transferred to another location in Canada, there is a good chance I will be able to match them with another agent I know personally!

Specialties: I am a Dartmouth to the Shore Specialist - residential relocation is my niche. I utilize technology to the utmost as it applies to the the daily practice of real estate. I take the time to give added value to my clients through all forms of accessible social media. My clients have come to expect nothing less from me. The delivery of my real estate services is from a personal and very individualized approach. My client's testimonials reference this often.
