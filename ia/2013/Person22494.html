Mark Garcia's Summary

Mark is internationally recognized for his unique perspective & ability to foresee, understand & target the needs of the changing consumer. He also has keen awareness of emerging markets and shifting trends. Mark is quickly becoming known as one of the country's leading Digital Media thought leaders

He is the author of two books and is working on his third for release in 2013

An award winning chef, he is also a strategic culinary innovation executive with a proven record of moving teams & organizations to new sales and profit levels in any type of market.

Chef Mark has 20+ Years of Hands-On Unique & Specialized Experience Spearheading; Digital, Social, Mobile and Interactive Media Marketing as well as Platform Development.
Numerous Retail Concepts, Culinary R&D, Nationwide Product Launches, Media Appearances, Licensing, and Operations are also part of his recipe box

His career spans across some of the world's most prestigious and "Best In Class" retailers such as; Wegman's, HEB/Central Market, Marks & Spencer, and Walmart/Sam's Club.

Mark was on the original concept team that created the groundbreaking gourmet market Eatzi's in concert with Phil Romano.

Chef Mark's extensive list of clients also include some of the largest restaurant & hotel companies such as Sofitel Hotels, Ritz Carlton Hotels, Brinker, Darden Restaurants, Il Fornaio, Boston Market, and many others.

Chef Mark attended the Culinary Institute of America, (CIA) in Hyde Park, NY where he graduated with Honors and received the Francis L. Roth award for Outstanding Performance.

Upon graduation he was also awarded a teaching fellowship in St. Andrew's cafew under Certified Master Chef Vince Fatigati.

He is a member of the Research Chef's Association, International Association of Culinary Professionals, American Culinary Federation, La Chaine des Rotisseurs, and National Restaurant Association
