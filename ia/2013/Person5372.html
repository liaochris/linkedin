Amanda Dennis' Summary

Over 30 years of hospitality experience with both franchise and independent management companies and hotels as well as timeshare rental programs. Proven track record in all aspects of hospitality sales and marketing including strategic planning, branding, revenue management, electronic distribution and e-commerce including channel management and social media and call center management. Highly driven, passionate, articulate, trustworthy and entrepreneurial; able to achieve results in the most competitive markets under the most challenging conditions.

Demonstrated success in developing and mentoring highly successful teams that routinely accomplish goals; finding innovative solutions that positively impact financial results, both sales and profit; creating measureable business plans that fully incorporate all distribution channels; quickly assessing a situation and launching a plan for both immediate results and long-term growth; partnering across the organization to ensure success of business plan and company goals.

Have served in various roles beginning with on-site Director of Sales and Marketing and progressing to multi-property responsibilities as regional corporate Director of Sales and Marketing and full responsibility of the sales and marketing division as Vice President of Sales and Marketing. Professional experience also includes development and repositioning of a reservations and distribution company as its Vice President; special projects related to revenue enhancement and training with a global hotel company; and, most recently, repositioning, developing and deploying a rental division for the world's largest private timeshare company. While there, combined resources of five separate departments to capitalize on existing talent, resulting in significant NOI growth YOY and continued double-digit growth in rental revenues in spite of a declining economy.

Specialties

Staff development, multi-channel distribution, account development, strategic planning, dynamic packaging, budgeting/forecasting, revenue generation, relationship management, training and measurement, call center management, traditional and non-tradtional marketing including social media

A sales and marketing approach that incorporates both traditional and modern strategies is essential. Our firm is uniquely equipped to guide you through today’s volatile hospitality environment. We transcend the traditional role of hoteliers and incorporate advanced, more effective strategies paired with today’s modern distribution opportunities. Specialties include sales and marketing, revenue management, training, staff development and mentoring, call center enhancement and asset management.

As your partner we isolate underlying issues, provide a tactful, straightforward assessment of the situation, shape strategic solutions that achieve maximum results and measure their effectiveness.
