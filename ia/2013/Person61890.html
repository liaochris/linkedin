Jennifer L. Nelson's Summary

I'm a New Jersey-based freelance writer who writes for regional and national newspapers, magazines, and websites. My specialties are health/fitness, parenting, and lifestyle.

I also write promotional and educational material including press releases, newsletters, brochures, and website copy for non-profit organizations, independent professionals, and small businesses.

I maintain a blog entitled "The Final Forty" (www.thefinalforty.com), which chronicles my personal 90-pound weight loss journey and newfound dedication to a healthy, active lifestyle.

Specialties

Editing, interviewing, researching, reporting, fact-checking, proofreading, copywriting, branding.

For further information, or to view my writing samples and client testimonials, please visit my website at www.jenniferlnelson.com
