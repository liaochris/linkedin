Beth Heilman's Summary

Hey, Let's connect!! My email address is beth@bethheilman.com.
For a girl who couldn't even copy and paste just a few short years ago, I totally love all things internet and Social Media Marketing. My specialties are in Social Media Strategies for Real Estate Professionals. It's where my heart is because I've been a Real Estate Agent working for builders and also a Realtor for the last 20 years.
The shift in Real Estate Marketing from offline to online has opened a whole new way of doing business for Realtors, Brokerages and Builders in the last few years. A few are doing it right, but unfortunately many are doing it wrong and their business (and their bank account) s suffering as a result. It doesn't have to be that way. Social Media Marketing has put the world literally at everyones fingertips. What I do is work with Realtors, Brokers and Builders as a Social Media Strategist, helping them to put their best face forward with consistent Branding, Marketing and connecting and building a lasting relationship with their ideal clients. Social Media Marketing is huge!! It's fun, and when done with a sound strategy in place, works like nothing else ever has.
Yes, you can still go knock on doors. Yes, you can send out postcards and letters. Those things still have their place, but let me ask you this...is it more effective to do all those things, hope someone reads that postcard or letter and maybe gain some business or would you love to connect with your customers and clients in real time, target the folks you love working with using a proven Social Media Strategy and have a pipeline that's overflowing? Let me tell you...overflowing clients is a beautiful thing. It's great to meet you...let's talk soon.
