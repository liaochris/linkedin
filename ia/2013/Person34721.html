Pamela Grover's Summary

Business owner since 1996, Pamela's extensive experience on the Web and expertise in graphic design, marketing and project management benefit her clients who receive top-notch customer service and fresh ideas for their businesses. Because of Pamela's transition to building her scrapbooking businesses, she is only maintaining her current clients with Design Tech Consulting.

Pamela is a traditional-turned-digital scrapbooker who co-founded The Future of Scrapping, Inc., with partner Amy Krauss. Future of Scrapping instructs others in digital scrapbooking through webinars, group classes and 1-on-1 instruction.

Tu Storia works with people who are frustrated with boxes of photos laying around and would like a beautiful keepsake to share with family and friends. Tu Storia provides a concierge-level service for memory preservation, photo organization and photo display. Their premier product is their Possibilities Album™ or custom-made photo book.

Tu Storia also works with photographers who want to focus on taking SPECTACULAR photos while Tu Storia designs SPECTACULAR albums for their clients!

Pamela & her husband, Spencer, also own and maintain investment properties in the Twin Cities. They are currently looking for a property in a warm state to invest in and then to eventually retire to.

Personal interests include relishing her weekly "Date Night" with husband, Spencer, studying the Bible, volunteering at church, investing in real estate, taking photos, and preserving memories through traditional and digital scrapbooking. Pamela attends Grace Church in Eden Prairie. She has three young children and an AWESOME husband.
