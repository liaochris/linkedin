Johnny Gwin's Summary

This is Who I Am:

I help companies understand who they are and tell their story and message to their audience customers and community with advertising, visual communication, graphic design, social media and PR.

This is Who I've Helped:

While working at agencies throughout the country on national brands I have created advertising and design for Upper Deck Sports Trading Cards, Cobra Golf Clubs, San Diego Tourism and The Walking Company. Closer to home, I have directed and created advertising for MAX Credit Union, Golden Key International Honor Society, Mobile Gas, The Hangout Music Fest, BugMaster and Alabama Orthopedic Clinic. See Hummingbird Ideas's and my advertising and design work at: http://www.hummingbirdideas.com/portfolio/
