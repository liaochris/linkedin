Dylan Fagan's Summary

Connect with me on LinkedIn or email me at Dylan@reputationmanagementnw.com and I will be happy to answer any questions that I can regarding Reputation Management, website design or online marketing strategies in general.

At Reputation Management Northwest, we believe that as a business owner in today's online world, it's vital to have a 5-Star online reputation. We are committed to building your 5-Star Online Reputation and marketing that stellar reputation to attract more customers.

Consumers read an average of 6-10 reviews online before deciding which company to choose to do business with.

>> 70% of consumers trust businesses with 6-10 reviews.<<

If your local directories listings are poorly optimized or even non-existent, you are undoubtedly losing out on new customers every day. What is just one more client per week worth to you? Five? Ten?

With the new Google+ local listings, they almost always show up at the top of the search results, often above any organic listings.

So, even if your website ranks well, and is very well optimized, your Google+, Citysearch, Yelp, SuperPages and other local directory listings may very well be holding you back.

We specialize in building your Online reputation by optimizing the myriad of local listing directories, as well as making sure that your positive reviews are highlighted and any bad reviews you have are downplayed and pushed off of the first page.

Through trial and error, and a lot of time and money, we have developed an online review portal. This is a very easy to use website that your customers will use to leave a review or complaint. If they click the "I had a bad experience" button, they are taken to a page where they enter their email, name and message. When they hit "send" it is sent right to your email to be handled and fixed..If they had a "good experience" they are taken to a page with links to any place you would like them to leave a review. This is very powerful.

Dylan Fagan's Experience

Owner/Founder

Elite Mobile Sites

January 2013 – Present (1 year) Greater Seattle Area

EliteMobileSites.com is a Seattle based design and marketing firm that caters to your mobile website design and mobile marketing needs.

With mobile internet usage set to surpass desktop PC internet access any day now it is essential that all businesses offer a mobile version of their website. Your current website is likely to perform badly on mobile devices as it has not been optimized for the various screen sizes and browsers. If you have used a mobile phone to browse the internet you will already be aware of this. When visiting the majority of websites you are unable to read or navigate them without excessive scrolling and zooming in. Worse still, some websites don’t show at all! It is very common for businesses such as restaurants and photographers to have websites built in “flash” but there is one major problem with this. The current best selling smartphone, the iPhone, can’t read websites built in flash.

By not having a mobile version of your website you are losing potential customers and profits for your business.
How does your website look on a mobile phone?

We can provide a fast and affordable solution to this problem by designing a fully optimized mobile website to compliment your existing desktop website. When a customer enters your web address on their mobile phone they will be redirected to the mobile version of your website (we provide the code needed to do this) but if they are visiting from a desktop PC or laptop they will see the desktop version. You can now be sure that no matter how a potential customer accesses your website they are going to be able to find all the information they require about your business fast.
