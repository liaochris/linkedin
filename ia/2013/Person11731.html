Barbara Pinson Lash's Summary

Barbara is an experienced Communications Coach with a news broadcasting background. She's started behind the scenes in the Detroit market, and spent most of her 15-year career in front of the camera.

Now she is sharing her on-air expertise and behind the scenes knowledge with those who often find themselves sought after by the media. She helps clients understand their communication strengths and works with them on opportunities for improvement (e.g. body language).

Athletic Media Coaching clients include the Charlotte Bobcats, the University of Arkansas and iHoops First Team.

Executive Communication Consulting clients include Greater Atlanta Economic Alliance, Metro Landmarks and Our Weekly.

Media Versed

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Broadcast Media industry

June 2007 – Present (6 years 5 months) Charlotte, North Carolina Area & Dallas, Texas Area

Media Versed is a Athletic Media Coaching and Executive Communications Consulting company that began in 2007. More on our two focus areas:

Athletics - Media Versed coaching helps budding and professional athletes, coaches, teams/organizations, front office and commentators develop and fine-tune their on-air skills, replacing distracting verbal and non-verbal communication with natural movements and flowing speech. Our clients learn interviewing techniques to answer questions seemingly effortlessly, articulating their intended message fluidly AND tools to professionally disengage from controversial interviews. Media Versed also helps clients hone their Social Media skills as we work to help them develop brand awareness and brand responsible Social Media Campaigns.

Executive/Corporate - Media Versed consulting helps business professionals hone their communication strengths, while minimizing their challenges by focusing on presentations, speeches and communicating effectively with employees. Our consulting also helps identify message/branding inconsistencies, and prepares clients to take their corporate message to the highest stage.
