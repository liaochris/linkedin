Alan Seilhammer (5,600+)'s Summary

LEADERSHIP, INNOVATION, QUALITY:

I align myself with motivated organizations that insist on providing the highest quality service and technology to the community association industry. I then guide that bank to grow into becoming a national leader.

Association Capital Bank is rapidly becoming the leading financial services provider to the community association industry. The entrepreneurial environment creates a culture of advocacy for driving management company efficiency and profitability. The bank has a dedicated team of customer servicing specialists and a greater willingness to customize service to the needs of management companies and community associations. The technology is of the highest order with an undaunted aspiration to bring greater efficiencies to the market through ever improving applications.

My History: First Associations Bank was the only bank in the country entirely specialized to provide financial services to the community association industry by virtue of its bank charter. While a member of the bank’s leadership, the bank earned the strongest possible “Safety & Soundness” rating (5 stars) available as reported by BankRate.com.
At Community Association Banc, I developed and led a team of community association lenders which became the largest and most successful national program. Most of the financing was for capital maintenance improvements ranging from $50K to $17 million. That skilled team and I executed thousands of community association loans in as many as 46 states.
