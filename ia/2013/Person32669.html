David Tompkins' Summary

A dynamic Digital Media Operations Director with 20+ years of achievement setting and executing content development and distribution strategies for premier scientific organizations and printing services firms.

A record of success, reflects my ability to consolidate and optimize the production of all types of media and marketing collateral, especially within the rapidly expanding digital and social media landscape. A consummate professional in my field, I have extensive experience driving the technical innovations required to deliver optimum end-user experiences and leading teams who have the same objective while achieving sustainable business value.

My record is also marked by substantial success as a senior project manager leading both the implementation and ongoing oversight of electronic content systems that meet the end-to-end needs of internal and external clients across various publication platforms, formats, and distribution pathways. In a sentence, my experiences have shown me what it takes to win and maintain value-adding presence in the competitive and quickly evolving digital media space.

David Tompkins' Experience

Director of Preflight Operations

American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS)

Nonprofit; 201-500 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

April 2001 – Present (12 years 5 months) Washington, D.C.

Recruited to establish and manage the day-to-day operations of the Association’s preflight design, production, prepress, and advertising functions supporting the weekly publication of the Science family of journals (140K+) and web periodicals. Manage daily operations of 50+ cross-functional resources while developing new managers and functional experts to promote preflight autonomy and adaptability. Coordinate with IT during the planning, development, and implementation of new business solutions that build efficiency, productivity, and quality across business and editorial systems. Set and execute $300K in annual operating budgets while directly supporting contract negotiations, capital investments, and HR functions. Participate as a subject matter expert and visionary on the Digital Publishing Platform Core Team, working with a skilled team of colleagues to transform the lifecycle of publishing from print centric to a cross-medium article focus.
