* Next Steps: A new roadmap to land your ideal career position.

* Assess lifelong success patterns pointing to your strongest, most enjoyable, marketable skills
* Define your ideal career position using a 10-point checklist
* Transform resumes, cover letters, social networking profiles, and verbal pitches from bland to brilliant
* Learn networking techniques to tap the hidden job market
* Diversify your job search using intermediate and advanced techniques
* Ace employment interviews using a simple, 5-question strategy
* Negotiate your best job offer; don’t leave money on the table
* Stay on-course after landing your ideal career position, to ensure ongoing satisfaction

Joellyn Wittenstein Schwerdlin's Experience

Career Coach for Executives, Managers, and Professionals in Career Transition

The Career Success Coach (private practice: formerly known as A-1 Quality Resumes & Career Services)

December 1991 – Present (21 years 9 months)

Career coach in private practice, working with executives, managers, and professionals who are ready to make a change in their employment situation, but don't know what that change looks like or what their next steps should be.

First step is for clients to have a Career Action Planning (CAP) Session to determine where they are getting stuck, stalled or confused in the process of making their desired job and career transition.

Second step is to mentor clients through an 8-module program, to help them: 1) identify their most perfect career path; 2) find the right the right job opportunities; 3) stay focused and motivated during their transition; 4) ultimately land their most ideal career position much faster and more deliberately than they could by working alone.

Please call 508-459-2854 or email joellyn@career-success-coach.com for a complimentary 15-minute consult, to determine if a CAP Session and Career Coaching Program are the right solutions for you.
