David Speltz's Summary

David Speltz is the co-owner of Wheat Ventures LLC, an angel investment company specializing in funding start-up companies in real estate, financial entities, and healthcare and internet technology. He is a licensed real estate agent in New Hampshire affiliated with Keller Williams Coastal Realty and a founder and director of the Optima Bank and Trust in Portsmouth, NH and most recently started Safe Homes Real Estate LLC, located in Portsmouth, NH.

Prior to 2006, he was the owner of a hospital turnaround company which he sold to Huron Consulting Group, a publicly traded consulting company with headquarters in Chicago and offices worldwide. He worked with distressed hospitals and managed the sale or merger of many of these hospitals to national or local healthcare entities. In 2007 he left Huron to form Wheat Ventures LLC. Wheat Ventures focuses on consulting to and investing in New Hampshire start-ups including the most recent startup, Safe Homes Real Estate LLC.
