Mya's Heart Foundation

January 2011 – Present (2 years 9 months) Norfolk, Virginia Area

The Mya’s Heart Foundation was named after Mya Christina Patillo, a beautiful angel who was born with brain asphyxia which was caused during the birth process. During her two years of life, she gave hope to many and was a clear example of what the love of God resembled. Families are most times unaware of the needs and cost that are involved with raising children who are born with birth defects. The Mya’s Heart foundation provides counseling and resources for the families and/or care givers of these precious children.

Youth Pastor

Grove Visions of Truth Ministries

June 2009 – Present (4 years 4 months) Portsmouth, Virginia

Born in Portsmouth, Virginia, Cardell Patillo, Jr. has blessed the world with an extreme love for people and an endless desire to please God. Cardell currently serves as the full time Youth Pastor on staff at Grove Baptist Church where Dr. Melvin O. Marriner leads as Senior Pastor. The youth ministry is a flourishing ministry which currently has over 900 youth on roll between the ages of five and twenty. Cardell exercises his gifts vigorously through lectures, workshops and revivals.

Cardell is an aggressive learner and has received his formal education from several higher institutions of learning. Cardell completed his undergraduate work at Norfolk State University and Liberty University. He is currently completing graduate studies at Liberty University and Princeton Theological Seminary. Cardell believes that a person’s life is put on pause when they believe they are no longer able to learn.

Cardell’s ministry is guided by I Timothy 1:12 which states “And I thank Christ Jesus our Lord who hath enabled me, for that he counted me faithful putting me into the ministry”.

CEO

Pastor Cardell C. Patillo Jr. Ministries

February 2002 – Present (11 years 8 months) Norfolk, Virginia Area

Cardell Patillo (CP) Ministries, Inc. was developed to spiritually, physically and socially serve those in the world who are seeking guidance and spiritual understanding. CP Ministries currently promotes effectual training for leadership development, church growth, youth ministry building and youth ministry sustainability.

CP Ministries has established an effective platform to address some of the many concerns facing our world today, including:

• Special needs of children born with birth defects

• Tuition assistance for college-bound high school seniors

• Working with international missions

CP Ministries currently offers motivational guides (i.e. tapes, videos, books, ministry growth technology) to address to the daily spiritual concerns of the believer.
