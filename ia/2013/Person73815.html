Kelli Calabrese MS's Summary

My commitment is to provide individuals with the tools to live vibrant, strong, energetic fulfilled years and to coach fitness professionals to reach higher levels of success in positively influencing their customers and in their prosperity.

I am the author of Feminine, Firm & Fit, Contributor to "The Power of Champions" and "The Women Doctors Guide to Health & Healing". I have been writing, presenting, training and performing spokesperson work in the fitness industry for 22 years. I have experience with all media including TV, radio, podcasting and print. I was the 2004 Personal Trainer of the Year for online training. The 2007 Personal Trainer in Denton County, TX and my Adventure Boot Camp was voted the Best Place to Work Out in 2008.

I was the Editor of Personal Fitness Professional Magazine, for 3 years and was the lead fitness expert for eDiets and eFitness for 3 years. I owned and operated a chain of health clubs for 14 years. I founded Xercise Institute, a fitness exam preparation and continuing education school where I successfully prepared over 3K candidates to become certified fitness professionals in a 9 year span.

I have experience with facility design, equipment acquisition, corporate fitness, group fitness, Personal Training Leadership, and more. Some of my corporate clients included Calvin Klein, Beneficial Insurance, BASF & Nabisco.

I have been coaching Personal Trainers how to grow their business with success for over a decade.

My personal mission is to help the general public incorporate health, fitness and wellness as a permanent and healthful part of their lives and to help personal fitness professionals grow their businesses with excellence and integrity.

Specialties

Masters degree in Clinical Exercise Physiology / Cardiac Rehabilitation, 23 fitness, lifestyle and nutrition related certifications, 22 years experience in the fitness industry. Women's tranformation specialist, lifestyle balance, childrens fitness.

I have provide online training support. I work with women between the ages of 25 and 65 who want to reclaim their lives and be their physical best.
