Susan Connelly Murphy's Summary

I have over 30 years of experience in the information technology, automotive, and education industries. What I love most is working with people to understand their business strategies and determine what information they need for success. I launched Connelly Information in 2009 to provide customized business intelligence and research services that are necessary in today's information rich environment.

This new opportunity makes the most of my communication skills and extensive business experience to understand the information needs of my clients. Utilizing expert research, analysis, and synthesis techniques, I produce a customized report to address those information needs.

Highlights

• Successful business/management consultant with experience in project development and management, sales and financial management, and employee training and development.

• Successful educator with thirteen years of teaching experience in Finance and Information Systems.

• Team player with excellent communication, administration, and problem-solving skills.

• Proven online search skills and experience in retrieval and advanced knowledge of on-line research systems and databases.

• Demonstrated commitment to understanding and meeting clients' information needs resulting in satisfied customers who generate referrals and repeat inquiries.

Specialties

• In-depth company and industry research across multiple industries.
• Market research in transportation and energy infrastructure, automotive industry.
• Training and instruction.
