Brad Binko's Summary

Originally from Buffalo, NY. Go Bills and Sabres, I've been out here for about 5 years now. I started Prism Nightlife in 2006 and enjoy every second of it. I am currently a member of the Las Vegas Young Business Professionals and always looking for ways to advance myself and my business to the next level. I have learned a lot since going to Northeastern University and the University of Buffalo years ago. There is no substitute for on the job training and real life experience. My travels have taken me through Charleston, SC where I lived for a couple years before moving out here. Living in Charleston really showed me how to slow down and enjoy all that life has to offer. (AKA stop and smell the roses) A couple mottos I have are 'Don't talk about it, be about it', and 'If you can dream it, you can achieve it'. I enjoy Las Vegas and meeting as many cool people as possible. Life is about stories so let’s go make one!
