Kirralee Ashworth's Experience

Owner

Kirralee Wedding Photographer

2009 – Present (4 years)

I absolutely love what I do as a wedding photographer, and hope that that passion comes through in every photo I take.

I've always been enchanted with the idea of finding someone who loves you so much that they would make a commitment to grow old with you.

For me photography is more than just capturing an image. It is an art form that allows me to look through a lens and see the history and emotions that tie two people together.

My photography style is a very un-obtrusive and natural. I strive to creatively document the true essence of your special day. To me all those small moments that go past in a flash are priceless - the laugh, the tear, the nervous smile – are ones that you will back in years to come with joy.

Every couple is different, therefore every wedding day is unique, and my job is to seek out the hidden gems and get them on camera.

I love getting to know all of my clients, so that I can create images that truly reflect their character. I want the process to be enjoyable for everyone, and encourage a sharing of ideas between bride and groom and myself.

I am based in Melbourne, Australia and am available to photograph your wedding nationally or internationally.

Please follow my journey as a photographer and meet couples who have shared their lives and romance with me, on my website and my blog

www.kirraleeweddings.com and www.kirraleeblog.com

Owner / Photographer

Grand View Photography

2009 – Present (4 years) Melbourne Area, Australia

Grand View Photography is my Melbourne based business specialising in architectural, interior design, landscape design, swimming pools and general real estate photography.

My get-up-and-go attitude and eye for perfection ensure that every photo shoot produces stunning magazine quality images.

I also provide expert advise on styling and optimum use of space. With every image I take, I carefully select the right angle to show of any property to it's absolute best!

If you are looking for a photographer to take images of your space or you are a real estate photographer please get in touch!

Please also visit my website: www.grandviewphotography.com.au
