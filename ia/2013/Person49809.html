Grant D Fairley's Summary

Grant provides executive coaching and mentoring for leaders in industry, sales, government, education and the community. His one-on-one sessions explore the leader's current successes and challenges to map out the road ahead. He will add the tools that the leader needs to improve their leadership and team-building skills to survive and thrive in good times and bad.

He also works with leaders during their good times to build their resources for when the tough times inevitably arrive. Grant also works with leaders facing a transition to a new position or who are in a career move.

The coaching takes a whole person approach that looks beyond the current assignment to enhancing the long-term career. His methods are encouraging, insightful and supportive. He often serves in conjunction with the company Human Resources team to assist and support their work with individual managers and executives.

For groups, Grant provides seminars, workshops and retreats that blend of information, humor and interaction to make the seminar or workship process and the product worthwhile for individuals and their companies. Our seminars take an integrative and whole person approach to make the content useful for the individual no matter where they are or whatever they do.

One of our hallmarks is the important use of stories to make the integration of principles deeper and more life-changing.

Stategic Seminars offers programs in motivation, business, relationships, leadership, customer service, teaching adults, conflict management and other topic areas. Visit our website www.strategic-seminars.com or www.canadian-executive-coaching.com for more information.

Grant D Fairley's Patents

  • SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR ACCESSING MEDICAL OR CONSULTING SERVICES

    • Canada Patent CA 2335399
    • Issued September 8, 2009
    Inventors: Grant D Fairley, Blair Lamb

    Invented in 2000, the Iasis Safeguard System is an innovative technology to allow medical services such as prescription renewals to be provided using the internet as an alternative to being physically at the doctor's office. System allows for more doctor minutes to be used with people who are actually ill, reduces contact between those who are ill and those who are not, reduces costly use of ER, reduces transportation cost and impact and reduces need for employee time off when not really required. Patent envisioned use of not only PCs but also handheld devices like PDAs, kiosks and other smart technology. Also promoted use of email and video-conferencing to extend and add flexibility of physican consultations not only locally but at a distance.

  • Method of Treating Pain - The present invention is directed to the injection of a paralyzing agent into the intrinsic spinal muscles to treat chronic pain.

    • United States Patent 6,806,251
    • Issued October 19, 2004
    Inventors: Grant D Fairley, Blair Lamb

    The patented method has been used successfully to a variety of pain problems including migraine headaches, whiplash, acute and chronic low back pain, neck pain, TMJ, carpal tunnel syndrome, tennis elbow, golfers elbow, some forms of osteoarthritis, herniated disks of the lower back and neck.

    The methodology uses BOTOX®, or other similar products. The way this system works is that the BOTOX® (botulinum toxin) is injected into the deep spinal muscles. The effect is to force the relaxation of these deep spinal muscles that have been injured over time or by accident. The forced relaxation allows for the spine to actually heal.

    Many pain conditions are caused or aggravated by shortened and scarred muscles that "pinch" the nerve roots along the spine. In some pain conditions, these scarred muscles compress the vertebrae and disks of the spine and neck causing long-term damage.

    Building on Dr. Lamb's experience in pain medicine over the past decade, He observed dramatic benefits when using this technique that he developed. He has further refined the method that has now been patented in the United States, Canada, Austalia and is a new indication for BOTOX® in the United Kingdom. The patent was researched and co-authored by Grant D. Fairley.

    Dr. Lamb has used this method successfully on many patients who otherwise would be living with chronic pain or facing back surgery. Dr. Lamb is currently developing a training program that could be used by physicians around the world.
