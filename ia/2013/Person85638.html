Maria Borg's Experience

Success Coach & Facilitator/ Owner

Thinking Forward

May 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Sydney Area, Australia

Reaching your true potential is essential to all success.

Thinking Forward was established to assist individuals in doing exactly that!

By providing insight into the latest techniques and knowledge in Recruitment and Sales, we not only provide the right conditions for learning but we inspire you to strive for change and reach new heights on the quest for achieving your professional goals.

Our goal is simple: Growth and Success through Inspiration and Learning.

As experienced facilitators, coaches and recruitment specialists with over 20 years experience, we understand that success leaves clues. People who produce outstanding results, do specific things to create those results.

At Thinking Forward, we enable you to maximise your career potential by eliminating your self-limiting beliefs. We assist you in understanding your unique traits and selling points, teach you how to sell yourself and your ‘product’ successfully and provide you with the guidance, tools and motivational strategies to unlock your true potential.

We also assist organisations in creating an effective and productive Recruitment Strategy, to enable successful recruitment at all levels within their organisation.

So, whether you are an organisation aiming to drive change and improve business outcomes, a Recruiter striving to increase billings or an individual wanting to take the next step on their career path… Thinking Forward will help you gain an unfair advantage!

Principal Facilitator

Recruitment Academy Pty Ltd

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

January 2006 – January 2012 (6 years 1 month)

From day one, Recruitment Academy has sought to improve the service delivery and performance standards of new recruitment consultants, to enhance the industry’s reputation and to build a stronger sense of professionalism from within.

As a result of our high profile, branding, marketing and our solid reputation, Recruitment Academy has organically become a leading training specialist within the recruitment industry.

Our in-depth knowledge of the industry is of a calibre that can only be obtained from on-the-ground experience in recruitment.

Our aim is to give candidates and recruitment consultants an extensive and grounded source of knowledge and programs that empower them to not only succeed in the recruitment industry but to thrive in it. We believe clients today expect their recruitment consultants to implicitly understand business, to be trained in all types of interviewing methodology, and to be knowledgeable about many industry types. That type of background is difficult to build on the job, and meanwhile, clients are paying the price for their consultants’ inexperience.

Recruitment Academy has become recognised as a leading induction and training service available to recruitment consultants entering the industry.

We offer professional, market leading induction and training services that are adaptable to your organisation’s needs. We can take newly appointed recruits off your hands, training them at our dedicated induction courses or, if you find yourself with limited resources or congested staff intake, Recruitment Academy is ready to facilitate an in-house induction course, tailored specifically to your organization, nationally.
