John Fallon's Summary

A "Triple Threat" of an Educator promoting “Presentation Literacy”, a Professional Speaker and PowerPoint expert.

I have delivered presentations at the following educational venues and for various companies: NFTE (National Foundation for Teaching Entrepreneurship), ASCD (Association for Superintendents and Curriculum Developers, SREB (Southern Regional Education Board), HSTW (High Schools That Work), EBS (Educational and Business Summit of South Carolina and YESCarolina (Youth Entrepreneurship in South Carolina).

Invite me to connect if you feel I can help you.

Specialties: Educational Keynotes, Presentation Skills Instruction / Training, PowerPoint Design, Pecha Kucha presentations, Speaking Coach, Vocal Coach and Choral Clinician
