Jessica Wolfson's Summary

I am a film producer and film distribution marketing strategist.

I have directed and produced several critically acclaimed feature length documentary films as well as series for television and for the web. I have worked in many roles from director to post production supervisor. From conception to completion of projects, I work closely with clients and networks to create programming. I manage budgets, scripts, schedules, crew, cast, locations and sets. I have directed editors, designers, animators to form the creative content of a project and have worked extensively in the post production online and sound mix.

As a film distribution marketing strategist, I have overseen the release of independent films in theatrical and non-theatrical venues, on television and DVD. Through promotional partnerships, national initiatives and digital marketing, I work to create innovative and strategic marketing plans for all releases.

Specialties

Documentary and Independent Films, Reality Televison, Web videos focusing on a variety of subjects from Book Videos and Trailers to Fashion Photography to Educational Videos and Short Films.
