Frank Lopez's Experience

CEO @ NHCA & President & Chief Strategist

Taino Group LLC

April 2008 – Present (5 years 3 months) Washington, DC

The Taino Group LLC is a business development and strategic diversity sourcing company that is focused on the development of association and emerging market entrepreneurship channels ready to fuel business ownership and procurement opportunities in the Federal and commercial contracting arena vis-a-vis major industry sectors that drive economic development across the nation and its economy.

Most recently, initiated a strategically important engagement by agreeing to serve in the Executive Lead position of the National Hispanic Contractors Association (NHCA), a National trade association with member chapters across all regions of the US that is dedicated to promoting the long term success, achievement and expansion of Hispanic entrepreneurs, affiliated state chapters, as well as its associates and corpoprate partners in the construction industry. Focused on expanding the reach of the association through development of new local state chapters across the nation, and in helping improve profitability of affilaited chapters and construction industry contractors.

The Taino Group has also been retained by the Puerto Rico Industrial Development Company (PRIDCO) to provide a wide range of proactive and strategic consulting services focused on the development and implementation of strategies to increase participation of Puerto Rico enterprises in the Federal government contracting process. Principal stakeholders from Puerto Rico, in addition to PRIDCO and a number of Industry-Specific business clusters, include the "Enlace Empresarial", an island wide private sector collaborative of all major trade associations based in Puerto Rico encompassing a wide range of sectors of industry.

Another dynamic and evolving engagement involves serving as President of the Quiles Consortium Group LLC, a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Business focused on increasing capture footprint with Federal agencies in areas associated with Information Technology and cyber-security service solutions.
