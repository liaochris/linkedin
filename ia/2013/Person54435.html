Ian Merricks' Summary

18 years working entrepreneurially, now specialising in investment, M&A, turnarounds / profit improvement. I aim to make a real impact on the industries I touch, looking to create best of breed businesses and returns.

95-97: Founded music management business, secured lucrative record contracts and chart success for international artists I promoted. Won Regional Promotions Award.
98-02: Launched a radio station, won Shell Livewire Award. Spun out radio consultancy business; launched 16 industry-backed radio stations. Recruited by Kelvin Mackenzie to head up direct sales for talkSport; added £1m revenue.
02-07: Acquired distressed academic publishing business, turned it around, recruited management team, grew to market leader, achieved strong exit to Egmont
03-04: Corporate development for FTSE100 media company EMAP, identifying acquisitions and organic expansion opportunities for the CEO.
04-09: Ran private media / tech investment portfolio, attracted external investment from VC and HNW syndicate investors. Setbacks due to advertising recession, but secured partial trade sale.
08-Present: Co-founded White Horse Capital; a TMT investment boutique. We advise on £100m of M&A transactions / fundraisings each year and work with VCs to create portfolio liquidity.
10-Present: Spun-out £3m B2B media planning & buying agency; increases advertising efficiency by 100%.
11-Present: Launched the Accelerator Academy; provides high growth training, mentoring & seed capital for ambitious early stage tech entrepreneurs. Large majority of supported companies have received investment.
13-Present; Building The Accelerator Network (& Fund) to provide end-to-end support for tech & media entrepreneurs, including a pre-accelerator coaching programme co-funded by the EU.

I speak and write regularly on early stage investment issues, advise the Cabinet Office’s £10m Social Incubator Fund, support corporate venturing through TMTConnect.me and am a shareholder at Startup Funding Club.
