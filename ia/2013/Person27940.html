Cristina Verger's Summary

Cristina Verger is a sought after event planner based in New York City. She is the driving force behind her company, Cristina Verger Event Planning & Production, LLC and an expert on private and corporate events and weddings. Bringing more than 25 years of experience to the table, she plans and executes events in the New York City metro area as well as in the Caribbean and Europe, with a focus on her home country Italy, where the company has satellite offices in both Milan and Rome.

Her strong background includes positions as the Director of Catering at the St. Moritz Hotel, then part of Helmsley Hotels, located on chic Central Park South. Subsequently, Cristina became Director of Catering at Windows on the World, the famous restaurant located at the top of the former 1 World Trade Center, and the World Trade Center Club. Following the 1993 World Trade Center bombing, Cristina moved on to become the first ever, and so far only, woman to head the private special events department at the renowned Harvard Club of New York City.
