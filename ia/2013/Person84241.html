Mike Magolnick's Summary

With every company and client I work with, my goal is the same... I want to associate my name and experience with the DNA of a great company so that in 20 years people will look back and say, "Yes, that's the company Mike Magolnick is/was with." I enjoy the freedom and flexibility of owning my own company, however I'm not opposed to consider a full-time, long-term opportunity with the right company; provided there is an appropriate cultural and business connection.

I feel very blessed that all 3 of my books have hit the Amazon Bestseller list. Not because I can call myself a "bestselling author" but because I know that I've been able to effectively help groups of people navigate the worlds of brand strategy, social and mobile media, and critical marketing.

I have extensive experience with social/mobile/digital, reputation management, corporate operations, communications, marketing, sales, brand management, strategic analysis/intelligence and business planning and often have the pleasure of speaking on these things at business meetings, conferences and seminars. (I am also available to talk to everyone through Clarity... https://clarity.fm/magolnick)

I believe in work:life balance, with extra weight on the life side. I am a full time dance dad to 3 competitive team performers and a VERY active school parent. I'm also active with American Mensa and I serve on the Board of North Texas Mensa, serving the 5.5 million+ people in and around Dallas, TX.
