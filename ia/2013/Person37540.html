Angela Elliot's Summary

Wellness Advisor and Chiropractor with a main focus in Pediatrics and Women's Health. Through a whole body approach we locate locate Nerve System Interference and help your body work, feel and function better. I also help our members with nutrition, posture, stress management, and ergonomics to ensure their success and set goals for the level of health each individual wants to achieve.
I receive ongoing training through the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association and have additional training in nutrition. I have a passion for education and strive to educate our practice members on how their body works. I also teach Biology at Globe University in Woodbury. If you want to be healthier 5 years from now than you are today, we can help you get there!
