Daniel "Danibal" Hentschel's Summary

Update: Looking for a job and/or freelance-work.

cartoons/illustrations, logo-design, DTP and lot's of web-related skills.

sound poetry, vocal acrobatics, overtones with throat and tons of jew's harps. love live looping. improvising musician.

cultural entrepreneur. curator of some quirky events (e.g FF Bijschaven, Couscous Kaas & Kousenband, Konijnendisco, the absurdist show De Grill).

Specialties: cartoons/illustration; logo design; DTP; jew's harp virtuoso; experimental vocalist
