Catherine Johnson's Summary

Consensus building Research Chemist with demonstrated expertise in advancing multiple oncology (kinase) drug discovery projects. Delivered highest quality designs by combining best practices of fragment based drug design, structure based drug design (SBDD), high throughput screening (HTS) hit triage, and outsourced parallel (combinatorial) and singleton synthesis. Proven team leader of diverse scientific groups with a reputation of fast decision-making and consensus building. Facilitated interface between Medicinal Chemistry, Discovery Platform and IT groups. Proven ability to manage multiple projects in a matrix environment and collaborate with colleagues in multi-site operations. Interested in applying project management skills to improve R&D operations.

Specialties

Fragment Based Drug Design, HTS Hit Triage, Drug Discovery, Structure Based Drug Design, Oncology, Kinases, Combinatorial Chemistry, Patent and Publication Writing, Team Leader, Outsourcing, and Cross-site Collaboration
