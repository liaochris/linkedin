Kevin Shoesmith's Summary

Kevin Shoesmith is the Founder and Directory of Factory Interactive, an experienced eCommerce development agency helping companies achieve eCommerce growth by supporting their online sales and transaction websites, systems, and platforms.

Founded in 2003, Factory Interactive is the evolution of Kevin’s deep knowledge of all things Internet and eCommerce — knowledge gained from building complex websites and applications for a host of organizations since 2000. A metrics-driven entrepreneur, Kevin exhibits a focus on measurable success and personal service that has kept Factory Interactive an excellent choice as a development firm for more than 10 years.

Today, Factory Interactive works with companies such as Telus, Fox Media, Mio, Simon Fraser University, Tourism Richmond, Livres Canada Books, Conde Nast, Kostkilp, GSI Commerce, PEER 1, Armpocket, and other companies who want a relationship-based approach from their development partners in eCommerce.

Specialties: eCommerce, Conversion Optimization, SEO, Analytics, Web Development (.NET, PHP, HTML5/CSS3, Javascript, XML/XSLT), Information Architecture, Database Development and Reporting (SQL, MySql, XML), Business Analysis, Requirements Design, Graphic Design, Web Usability, online marketing, importing, purchasing
