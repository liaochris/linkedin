Blue Turner's Summary

Blue Turner is a true Gemini.
The good twin is Pop/R&B, for all the party girls.
The evil twin is the Blues/Rocker, for all hard rockin' guys.
Mix in some kickin' Country Rock songs & you have Blue Turner.

We are proud to present the new vocal Pop/Rock/Rhythm & Blues meets Hip Hop album "Leave On Your Stilettos" available at CD Baby, itunes & Amazon.
Official Website: http://www.blueturnerguitar.com
Singer, Composer, Musician... Blue is a captivating and compelling artist who draws in his audience with insightful lyrics about life, love and the human condition.

He is also renowned for his fascinating experiments with the guitar, fused with a wildly imaginative lyrical sensibility.

Blue is currently available for studio work & touring with well established bands & artists. His catalog of songs are available to artists & publishing companies at http://www.blueturnerguitar.com
