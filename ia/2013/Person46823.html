Jose Alejandro Garcia Munoz's Summary

Jose Alejandro Garcia Munoz is a devoted animation creator focused in the development, production and distribution of original animated properties generated by Latin-American talent.

Originally educated as a character animator in Canada and the United States, he holds diplomas for Classical Animation, Digital Character Animation in Maya, Advanced Character Animation Studies, Digital Painting, Animated Series Development, and Entertainment Business Management.

As a professional, his career rapidly ascended acquiring responsibilities as a Lead CG Generalist, Art Director, Animation Director, Director and eventually Producer. Through these positions he has served a client list that includes Anima Estudios, Televisa, Porchlight Animation, Imagination Films, Kaxan Studios, Warner Bros. Animation and Rancho Studios.

Aware of the challenges that Latin-American entertainment industries face when securing distribution, he is a committed advocate of the potential held by digital media as alternative distribution channels. Through his personal studio, Gama Animation; he works to incubate entertainment brands designed specifically for these platforms. Furthermore, convinced that the future of the Mexican animation industry relies on its capacity to develop and market original intellectual properties; he currently serves as Director of Creative Development at Anima Estudios.
