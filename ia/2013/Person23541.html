Chris Michalik's Summary

I'm a creative services professional with experience ranging from small business to Fortune 500, including working with high profile clients like HP, Microsoft and Cisco. This experience allows me to be comfortable working under the stress of tight deadlines and can adapt to work with even the most demanding of clients. I pride myself in my ability to listen to clients and working with them to create materials or implement projects that will best meet their needs. In doing this, I also don't shy away from making suggestions that I believe will give the client a better end product, while making sure their expectations are still met.

In addition to my experience, I enjoy creative services and I try to expand my general creative knowledge and stay on top of relevant trends in design, marketing, advertising, technology and media. In doing this, I look to not just read about new trends, but also apply them first in small projects or as templates that can be edited for future uses.

I strive to continue working toward my ultimate professional goal of a creative director type position. Ideally, I look to stay on this path in a position where my current experience will benefit the organization, but also allows for opportunities to grow and expand my knowledge in creative services.
