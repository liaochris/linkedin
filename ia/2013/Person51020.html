Guus ter Beek's Summary

Digital art director who believes in bringing together consumers and brands through digital. Gained work experience at several international agencies, Razorfish, BBH London, Happiness Brussels, McCann & IfB.

I prefer to think of consumers as prosumers: Engaged consumers who are informed and curious and immerse themselves with all things digital. Therefore I aim to enrich the prosumers experience of brands with campaigns, applications, services and games.

At the moment I am putting my imagination to work in London, and I am open to new opportunities.
