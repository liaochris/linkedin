Jon Greer's Summary

Does your company have a great story to tell – but you have a hard time getting the attention you deserve?

If you are eager to get your story out, we can help. Jon Greer Consulting LLC (JGC LLC) creates and implements winning content marketing strategies for companies of all sizes.

"Content marketing -- from blog posts to pinboards to YouTube -- is the new PR," Greer says. "Companies that engage with their audiences through these channels reap great rewards."

Here are some recent examples of our work:
* Raised the profile of an exciting cloud computing start-up
* Trained senior spokespeople at a multinational tech corporation
* Served as ghostwriter/editor for guest blog posts on top-tier online media site
* Edited important investor communications for cross-border venture capital firm
* Researched and wrote a contract book on public relations best practices
* Edited key investor relations materials for complex publicly traded company

Current and recent clients include El Dorado Ventures, HRJ Capital, Mayfield, Morrison & Foerster, Redwood Trust, Bulldog Reporter, Research in Motion and Qualcomm.

Specialties: Investor communications, marketing communications, spokesperson training, interview coaching, investor relations, corporate communications, technology, health care, retail, marketing communications, crisis communications
