Kaustuv De Biswas' Summary

I am passionate about leveraging technology to create "platforms for exchange" at the intersection of design and education.

Currently building Sunglass.io, a cloud based platform to collect and deliver micro-innovations through a low barrier and ubiquitous channel for designers across the globe.

Specialties

Talk to me about:
- Future of design -> Tools to support it.
- Economics of very crowded places.
- Sunglass.io and what it means to be on the cloud.
