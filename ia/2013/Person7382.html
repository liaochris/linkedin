Andy Core's Summary

Everything Andy does is aimed at helping busy adults become more connected at work, productive and able to get off the "I wish I had better work-life balance" roller coaster.

By doing this, he helps organizations worldwide increase their numbers of exceptional, loyal and less costly people.

Selected as one of 2012's Global Top5 Health/Healthcare speakers, Andy is highly credentialed, award winning expert who is "sneak up on you" funny.

His success rate at inspiring people to move toward a more balanced, productive and healthier life, and STICK with it, is 13X the national average.

He provides a variety of presentations, books, articles and self-study programs on the following topics:

* Work-Life Balance
* Peak Personal Performance
* Stress Management
* Sustained Healthy Life Motivation
