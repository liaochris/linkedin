Hank Miller's Summary

I'm an associate broker and certified appraiser with Atlanta Communities Real Estate Brokerage, serving Metro Atlanta. I started an appraisal firm in NY back in 1989 and have been in real estate full time since.

I'm also a certified appraiser in the state of Georgia. The current concentration is exclusively foreclosure appraisals for banks and FNMA, the responsibility being to accurately estimate market value for their portfolios.

The current real estate market is exceptionally fluid and agents not fully invested are an impediment. Our objective is simple; to ensure that our clients achieve the best possible outcome for their individual circumstance.

Specialties

Property marketing and sales, Buyer representation, Contract negotiation, Data research, Data application, Real estate appraisal, Tax assessment appeal, Internet marketing, Investor counseling, Distressed sales
