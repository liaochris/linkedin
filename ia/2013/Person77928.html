Helene Roussi's Summary

I want to continue to develop the North American Retriever hybrid in order to produce a consistently allergy-friendly pet and service dog. I also breed Labradoodles and Goldendoodles as family pets and service dogs. I intend to continue donating several puppies a year to be trained to serve disabled individuals..

I am also a writer, and have had several articles published in various areas of interest. Some of the most recent ones can be seen here:

http://www.associatedcontent.com/user/459169/helene_roussi.html

In addition, I am massage therapist licensed by the state of Ohio, and have been in practice for 20 years. I have a private practice in Worthington, OH.

My goals for my massage clients are developed in cooperation with them, and are reviewed and revised at every appointment. This assures that every appointment addresses the client's needs at that moment. I work with clients in the office, and provide them with suggestions for ways to address their challenges outside the office as well.

I am a second degree black belt in the Ernie Reyes Martial Arts system and do my best to live my life and conduct my businesses by the Five Tenets of Tae Kwon Do:

Courtesy - I will treat every person who contacts me with courtesy, and strive to answer questions and provide information to the best of my ability.

Integrity - I will treat everyone fairly, and strive to do what is right, no matter how difficult it may be.

Perseverance - I will persist despite challenges, until the best
outcome possible for everyone is obtained.

Self-control - I will develop self-discipline in order to bring out the best in myself and others.

Indomitable will - Every event, even those that seem negative, will be seen as an opportunity.

A-SAH!

Specialties

Breeder of Labradoodles, Goldendoodles, North American Retrievers, Double Doodles. Currently focusing on the development of the new hybrid, the mini/medium North American Retriever, also called a Double Doodle. I created the name 'North American Retriever' several years ago and am one of the first few breeders in North America to breed this hybrid of a Labradoodle and Goldendoodle.

As a massage therapist I specialize in myofascial (trigger point) therapy.
