Marcia Tal's Summary

As the founder of Tal Solutions, LLC, I bring significant value to my clients by helping them create revenue and profit growth opportunities from their own data. I do this by designing and building an analytical framework and then creating processes to gain insights for developing enterprise policies, programs and products – fueling business strategies and increasing revenue and profits. I also enable businesses to be better positioned to understand their customers, provide differentiated solutions and deliver rich results. A New York-based advisory and management consulting firm, Tal Solutions serves a broad range of clients – start-ups, C-suite partners and complex global organizations.

Tal Solutions offers consulting services and analytical solutions packages to help organizations profit from the hidden value in their data.
• Enterprise Custom Consultations
• Analytic Audits and Assessments
• Data and Analytics
• Segmentation
• Price Management
• Customer Lifecycle
• Analytics Training
• C-Suite Analytical Organization

"Big Data" and analytics have played a significant role throughout my career. I was an early adopter and advocate of harnessing the power of big data, and I am an acknowledged expert in designing and leading business analytic organizations. As EVP at Citigroup, I was widely recognized in the financial services industry for creating and building the Global Decision Management function in over 30 countries. Using my decades of experience in Risk Management and Marketing, I introduced advanced analytical tools and a strong governance process into business decisions.

In addition to business consulting, I also speak, teach, advise and write about big data and analytics. I have written for The Guardian and have provided key interviews in recently published books, "Lean Analytics", and "Big Data, Big Analytics." Most recently, I was profiled in and interviewed for Mike Barlow’s O’Reilly Strata report, “The Culture of Big Data.”
