Martin Spooner's Summary

Spooners Ltd was established to help those hospitality professionals that are short of time manage their properties, finances and lives. Spooners facilitate business solutions.

Invest Dorset LLP, the financial element, in conjunction with professionals from the finance industry, actively introduces private and corporate clients of high net worth to wealth and tax planning opportunities, aiming to add wealth through tax minimisation, using HMRC tax reliefs. EIS, Seed EIS and BPRA. The Film, Music and Information Technology industries are all particularly fast-moving arenas. See: http://benedictsbrotherfilm.blogspot.co.uk/ and www.bwtonline.co.uk.

One of the benefits of working so closely with entrepreneurs from the creative world is the opportunities these friendships bring. Buy With Thrills is one such company, a new and exciting start-up that seeks funds for an innovative project that blends commerce and marketing and intends to boost revenues for traditional retailers and online retailers alike. We call it BWT and it rewards customer and merchant loyalty with 'thrills'.

Early association with Maricuda Ltd owned by Naval Architect David Aitken, this company has grown in three years to become a very innovative marine technology resource. The Atlantic Challenger, the Neptune Tsunami and Flood Escape Shelter and the Tidal Energy projects all seek global markets. The development of fast craft technology for the military, commercial and leisure markets continues apace.

Involvement in these businesses stem from 35 years in the hospitality industry where targets, discipline, training, networking, excellent relationships and a 'just do it' ethos are the keys to success.

Specialties: Setting and achieving tough time and financial targets, networking, determination, voluntary business mentoring, working relationships, family, sailing and fun
