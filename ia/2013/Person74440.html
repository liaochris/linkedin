Kenny Mendes' Summary

I currently have one goal in mind: make Box one of the most successful hiring organizations of all time.

The talent we have at Box is, by far, our biggest driver of success. As our company grows and hiring becomes more decentralized, we need to focus even more on ensuring every single person we recruit into our organization is incredible. Once those people join our team, we surround them with incredible individuals, the ability to grow, and a chance to make a tremendous impact. This combination has created one of the strongest cultures known to the technology world and I hope that the people I hire have a chance to do some of the best work of their lives at Box.

Thankfully, I'm not alone in this quest to create one of the most legendary enterprise software companies in the world. I've had the joy of building one of the Valley's best recruiting teams from scratch. My team and I ask ourselves every single day, "How do we make sure the next 500 people we hire are just as epic as the last 500?"

Hiring this many brilliant, wacky Boxers in such a short time-frame requires us to be both innovative and data-driven with our hiring methodologies. I'm proud to say that we're on the right track. Box has grown from 40 people in late 2009 to over 700 today.

I get a chance to work with some of the smartest, most accomplished people in the software world, and two of the best entrepreneurs of our generation -- Aaron Levie and Dylan Smith -- and I'm loving every second of it.
