Carolyn Brown's Summary

A serial entrepreneur, The Accountant's Office is the 5th business I have started. Helping small business owners to be more successful is my passion! I've worked with business owners from a wide variety of industries and listen to their needs and concerns. The result is that we have created some unique options for business owners to save their money, free their time and secure their peace of mind. Our goal is to help their businesses grow and prosper.

Specialties

Our team of highly skilled accountants are experts at providing outstanding accounting services and internal controls for small businesses and individuals nationwide. Our award-winning services include Your Virtual Accounting Office (a full outsource option), Your Remote CFO (remote access reconciliations and supervision), Personal Bill Paying and bookkeeping services. We are a team you can trust!

Carolyn Brown's Experience

CEO & Founder

The Accountant's Office LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Accounting industry

June 2001 – Present (12 years 7 months)

An award-winning business owner and mentor, Carolyn leads a team of highly skilled accountants. She has developed several unique service offerings that are totally virtual, totally paperless. In May 2010 Carolyn was the first woman in Arizona to ever receive a SBA national enterprise award. The honor was given for "providing services that enhance the business owner experience" and "support for the creation and expansion of small businesses." It was a humbling honor!

Mentor, speaker and author

The Accountant's Office, LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Accounting industry

May 2001 – Present (12 years 8 months)

Carolyn is passionate about helping small business owners succeed. Through her books and presentations she gives business owners solid tools to be more effective in their businesses. Her first book, "Biz-Tips: 30 Quick Tips to Make You More Effective in Your Business" was released in Nov 2010 and is available at www.TheBookPatch.com. Carolyn speaks to small business owner and mentoring groups on "The Art of Keeping Score: Playing the Game of Business to Win" and "The Power of Six."
