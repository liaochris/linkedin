Mary Brett's Summary

Owner and principal of National Industrial Supply Co. (NIS). NIS is the national leader in providing quality on-site load testing inspections and certification of lifting slings and associated hardware to ensure our customers maintain OSHA safety compliance. NIS also inspects and certifies: crane hooks, Hi-Lo trucks, Fall Protection Equipment, Below the Hook Lifting Devices, Dock Plates, Die Stands, Alloy Steel Chain Slings, Wire Rope Slings, Nylon and Polyester Slings, Round Slings, TwinPath Slings, First Sling, Stairs, Platforms, and Ladders. All inspected items are tagged with a color coded inspection tag and Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) tags are available upon request.

NIS is dedicated to providing innovative rigging equipment and services to allow our customers to track and manage all their compliance records online (24/7 access on protected website). NIS basis all of its inspection procedures on recognized established standards from OSHA, MIOSHA, ASME/ANSI and ASTM.

By offering hosted, on-demand inspection records, NIS takes the hassle and liability away for our customers. NIS takes the worry of maintaining and storing our customer’s vital inspection and maintenance records so they can concentrate on their core business and maintain a safe, compliant and productive work environment.

NIS also offers top quality rigging products; such as Round Slings, Polyester Slings, Alloy Steel Chain Slings, Wire Rope Slings, Crane Cable, Roll Off Cable, Shackles, Eyebolts, Hoist Rings, Star Points, Roller Blocks, First Sling, Dynema Slings and an assortment of lifting hardware.

Specialties

Load Testing on-site, maintaining customer’s inspection records for governing body audits, quality audits, Wind Energy Developer requirements and the Armed Forces.
