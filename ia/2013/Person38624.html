Duane Nason's Summary

I'm the organizer of Pitch San Francisco 2013, taking place on September 19th, 2013 in San Francisco’s AT&T Park – home of the World Champion San Francisco Giants.

I'm bringing together 100 of the latest and greatest Startups with over 2,000 early adopters, potential customers, influential reporters, investors, and the rest of the tech and Startup community.

Come meet founders of Startups, hear their pitches, and see demos of the next great thing. Enjoy AT&T Park, with behind the scenes ballpark tours, hang out with Giant's mascot Lou Seal, enjoy ballpark concessions, play free video games, and much more.

This is the second time for Pitch, and you will have a ball! (pun intended). Startups from our last event went on to raise $159 million in funding after attending Pitch '11.

See photos, videos, and learn more at http://www.pitchevent.com. Check it out for free with promo code "linkedin" (register by Aug 31st for discount).

Beyond having fun with Pitch '13, my primary focus is designing, developing, and deploying web-based applications - and building/supporting the companies around them.
