About Anne Dalton

Anne relocated to Tucson area in 2007 after a successful career promoting community collaboration in Charlotte, North Carolina. She served as division director for a large county mental health division, securing grants and managing community programs and outreach. As communications director for the Department of Mental health, Substance Abuse and Developmental Disabilities, her communications and community liaison work motivated public support for an innovative array of community services.

Since relocating to the Tucson area, Anne served as communications director of the Soroptimist International of Desert Tucson and managing editor of the Pima Community College's Aztec Press, the student newspaper. She currently writes for Tucson’s Connecting Communities Foundation magazine. Her work has been published in local and national magazines on and off-line. A complete portfolio of her writing and photography is available at www.annedaltonphotoournalist.net
