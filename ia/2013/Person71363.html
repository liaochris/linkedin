Justin Romito's Summary

After years wrestling with corporate networks, creating elegant Active Directory structures, and installing retail systems, it was a logical step to move on to the world of live music production. The skills I learned in the world of IT help me daily as I'm organizing shows, wrangling musicians and technicians, troubleshooting electronic lighting and audio control systems, and navigating the esoteric accounting practices of the world of rock.

I'm at my best in fast paced environments where my creative problem solving, team building, and project management skills can shine.

Specialties

Orchestrating a wide variety of digital and analog audio; lighting; video; and show controllers, permitting agencies, staging systems, and contractors to create live shows that run on schedule and under budget

MS Group Policy Administration, Numara track-IT certified technician and administrator, MS Configuration Manager administration, MS Active Directory administration, MS Exchange administration, McAfee ePolicy Orchestrator & VirusScan administration, and resurrecting dead hardware.
