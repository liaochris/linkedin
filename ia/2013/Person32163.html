David Lee Coleman's Summary

My wife and I started our first HIIT gym in July of '06, Quick Gym. We have several 24/7 locations in Colorado Springs as well as in Falcon and Peyton CO.
We also purchase and sale quality ROM Equipment.

We have helped hundreds of Colorado Springs residents exercise that were burned out on exercise programs because they take too long and results don't come fast enough.

At Quick Gym we address both of those problems with Multiple locations. Most with 24/7 access.
Providing unsurpassed results with heart patients, the elderly, those with back, knee and hip issues as the only exercise and Athletes adding it to their regimen.

Special forces, Navy Seals, World Champion Athletes, Olympic Athletes, Denver Broncos, World Class Athlete at Ft Carson and local professional boxers and wrestlers all incorporate our program in their fitness regimen.

We also work with corporate wellness programs.
