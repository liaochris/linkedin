Jurgen Kramer's Summary

I am a passionate man who displays vigor and humor. Through empathy and my clear and direct communication style, I am able to connect with and motivate people. As a people manager, I motivate others toward personal development as well as achieving results. I ensure employees’ full participation in the business plan, and I create an environment where they are co-responsible for the results, as a team.

My core values are openness, honesty and trust.

My personal passion is offshore sail racing. As the Navigator, I am responsible that the team is on the right course, which is paramount toward the goal of reaching the finish line in first place. The shortest distance between two points may not a straight line, so that I must never fail to recognize and consider the other conditions of the course such as weather, local obstacles or current.
The goal can be successfully reached only if the team can identify and properly respond to all conditions.
It is therefore most important to keep a keen eye on what is going on around you and then to act decisively and optimally, without deviating from the final goal. In this way, I achieve the best performance from the boat and the crew (as well as the organization and the team.)

My specialties are change management, program management and coaching. I have broad knowledge and experience in the Chemical Industry, Trade, Retail, FMCG, Food & Feed Industry, Media, Insurance, Logistics and Leisure.
