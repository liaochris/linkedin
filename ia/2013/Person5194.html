Alun Tanner's Summary

I am a dynamic, exceptional leader with a track record of exceeding business goals. Experienced in managing cultural diversity; as a skilled change leader, with excellent communication, coaching and interpersonal skills, I have consistently turned dysfunctional groups into high performing teams. I am confident speaking publicly and am an effective facilitator with broad international experience. After more than 30 years working on all aspects of drug development in multiple therapeutic areas of both veterinary and human medicines, I can cut through the complexity of the pharmaceutical business to bring innovative solutions to operational and strategic issues at all levels of an organization.

Specialties include:
Pharmacovigilance systems risk assessment for regulatory compliance, licensing agreements, M&A activities
Business continuity planning and implementation
Safety & risk management training for lay and industry professionals
Preparation and/or review of associated documentation including SOPs and working practices
Inspection preparedness training
Compliance audits (GVP, GCP, GLP, GDP)
Clinical trials oversight
