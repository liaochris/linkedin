Kathryn Newhouse's Summary

Concierge Madison is a homecare management company dedicated to providing trustworthy help around the house so families can be freed of the day-to-day concerns of homecare management. A personal assistant providing attention to detail, and professional discretion to assist with your household needs. Our offerings include:

Concierge Homecare Management
◊ Coordinate and be present for maintenance and repair services
◊ Assist with packing and unpacking of household goods

Concierge Homecare Interior Services
◊ Organizing specialist to de-cluttering rooms, closets, and workspaces
◊ Obtain quotes for home services
◊ Organize cabinets, drawers, and pantries to optimize space
◊ Coordinate and oversee housecleaning

Concierge Home Exterior Services
◊ Oversee landscaping and lawn services
◊ Arrange quotes for exterior maintenance projects
◊ Plant pots/urns with seasonal foliage

Concierge Personal Assistant Services
◊ Secure reservations for dining and entertainment
◊ Schedule airport and other transportation
◊ Arrange appointments for salon, spa, and personal training
◊ Shop for, wrap, and deliver gifts
◊ Drop off and pick up dry cleaning
◊ Handle shopping errands and merchandise returns
◊ Schedule auto maintenance and related transportation

Concierge Corporate Services
◊ Purchase, wrap, and deliver client gifts
◊ Buy and deliver office/break room supplies
◊ Meet and greet clients at airport and functions
