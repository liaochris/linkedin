Cove Aviation LLC's Summary

If you’re looking for high quality and personal service, you’ve come to the right place. At Cove Aviation LLC we’ll give you the attention and personal service you’ll come to expect and enjoy.

Cove Aviation has been one of the nation's leaders in providing private jet solutions. Our operational staff experience is committed to your best interest regardless of the complexity of your mission. Unlimited access to a network of aircraft worldwide, coupled with our commitment to detail make your private jet experience unsurpassed.

Our fleet includes a Gulfstream V, Gulfstream III and Bell 407. Cove Aviation has been in operation since 2009 in the (Western Pacific) California region with aircraft that are located at the Van Nuys, CA (VNY) and Teterboro, NJ (TEB) airports.
