juan marin's Summary

Juan has more than 16 years experience in the digital world. He graduated in advertising from the UPB in Medellín, he has worked as a senior designer, art director and creative director at various digital agencies (Starmedia, Cyclelogic, Zemoga, Studiocom). At the companies where he has worked, he has been involved in projects ranging from the conception and design of large sites, to the definition and production of important online campaigns for brands such as Land Rover, Colgate, Dunkin' Donuts, amongst others.
