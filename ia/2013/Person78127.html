Manager

E Network Enterprises LLC

2008 – Present (5 years)

Helping others get more customers to their business using the WWW (and that's nothing to do with wrestling) is always rewarding.

There is usually a lot of opportunity a local business owner can make in getting more customers and it doesn't take too much extra effort or cost on their part if they are smart about it. Sometimes just improving the website or building one for the business can really help.

One tip I have is to be very descriptive about what you do for people and put some pictures on your site that are from or about your business. You can take them yourself. Who cares about professional images if they don't show the prospective customer what to expect?

Think "reality". Give people an idea both written and visually about what you have to offer.

It's rewarding to hear "I'm getting so much business I have to turn some away" from the people that I have helped!
