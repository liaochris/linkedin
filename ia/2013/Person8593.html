Anoki Casey's Summary

I am a San Diego, Ca. based Graphic Artist and Web Designer specializing in print, web, and identity development for commercial, non-profit, start-up, small business, creative, health-related and educational institutions of all types. My goal is to provide my clients with high-quality design, quick service, and effective, user-focused designs that will ultimately benefit their business. I work with clients in New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Washington, and California.

Having worked with a variety of clients and in a wide assortment of situations since my sophomore year in college, I can safely say that I have always been a freelance graphic artist. In my latest years I have expanded the body of my skills into the realm of website development and online marketing. I bring the wealth of my experience in layout, design, advertising, and creativity into a working portfolio of web and printed projects.

About my services:

• I am a true Graphic Designer—not a desktop publisher with extra time—with a BFA from Rutgers University in Graphic Design and Typography and have been working in the field (both print and web) for over 13 years...

• I am a full-fledged, 50+ hour a week working design professional with all the knowledge and know-how to meet client goals and maximize worth. I work from home, and this is my only job.

I create web sites using the latest, most modern version of valid XHTML/CSS, along with Javascript, PHP, AJAX, and Flash. Which means the site will be:

- Search Engine Ready, allowing search engines to penetrate and understand the content more semantically;

- Are accessible and will always work in future and previous versions of browsers;

- Have an overall more professional look. It’s gotten easier these days to tell when a site is coded better.


• And finally, I’m fast, always available, and fun to work with. I will work with clients and their ideas to ensure they get what they're looking for.
