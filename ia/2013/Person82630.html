Lotte Rensen's Experience

PR & Event Manager

2keer.nl

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

August 2012 – Present (1 year) Arnhem

In my all-round function at full-service marketing agency 2keer.nl, I primarily focus on PR & Event management. My accounts range from local retailers up to world leading multinationals. Besides the planning & production of events, I'm also responsible for the Community Management of our online social media campaigns, for customers such as MoBA 13 (the world's most extensive fashion biennial).

I find that my previous work-experience in the arts & cultural sector allows me to come up with creative concepts which capture the true essence of the events & campaigns I produce. I am very passionate about creating brand experiences that provoke an ongoing dialogue between our clients and their target group. What drives me on a day to day base is to capture what moves people to buy a product or go to an event. Being able to influence these emotions and to give direction to the actions that follow is what makes my job the most thrilling job in the world to me!

Founder & Managing Director

Ambitieuze Meisjes

November 2011 – Present (1 year 9 months) Arnhem - The Netherlands

Ambitieuze Meisjes is een netwerkorganisatie, opgezet door en voor ambitieuze meisjes; lokale business babes met beauty, brains & balls. Ons doel is het verbinden en verrijken van jong vrouwelijk talent door middel van een uitgebreid workshop-traject, o.l.v. Grand Dames (ervaringsdeskundigen).

Met de workshops die wij aanbieden begeleiden wij jonge high potentials op hun road to success. Ons omvangrijke deelnemersbestand bevat business babes uit de meest uiteenlopende sectoren. Zelfstandig onderneemsters, ZZP'ers, Freelancers, consultants, interim professionals.
