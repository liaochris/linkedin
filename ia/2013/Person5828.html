Amril Taufik Gobel's Summary

Amril has been working over 15 years in Automotive, Chemical and Oil & Gas Industry. He held various position started from engineer, PPIC Manager. Regional Logistics Coordinator and Supply Chain specialist,

Summary of key responsibility : Develop and manage Materials Planning and Inventory Management functions for goods and materials demanded by external and internal customers. Create and coordinate integrated processes among business units Operations, Purchasing, Logistics, and outside suppliers to execute a cost effective planning/replenishment process. Lead and coordinate process improvement efforts as it relates to supply chain planning and execution systems considering global inventory.

Manage and supervision of staff tasked with day to day execution of order entry through order delivery, receipts, stock put away, picking, packing and shipping. Create material requirement plans and/or forecasts; establish key performance metrics and benchmarks relating to supply chain planning / forecasting; measure actual performance against goals on regular basis and present results to senior management. Apply expertise to specify system functionality.
