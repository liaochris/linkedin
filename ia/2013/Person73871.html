Kelly Banks' Summary

I am currently managing a design firm focused on renovations and full service interior design. I have been working with licensed design builders in the area since 2007. We have completed several major whole house renovations as well as new construction projects. I am part of a successful design cooperative and travel annually to furniture markets on the East and West coasts to stay ahead of the newest trends. My education in design is an ongoing process which I thoroughly enjoy.

My most recent expansion comes by the way of kitchen and bathroom cabinetry. I am now an authorized dealer for Bellmont Cabinents, formerly known as Pacific Crest Industries. This is a 30 year old, family run organization, headquartered in the Northwest. I worked on numerous projects where we used these cabinets and was always very pleased with the quality, construction, design, and service they provided. So when I decided to focus more on kitchen and bath renovations, it made sense to look to a line like Bellmont. They offer a nice range of contemporary to traditional, European [Frameless] cabinets with over 300 finish/material options.

Since the design industry is one that continues to grow today, I need to be creative in how I market myself and provide value added services for all of my clients. Understanding what design elements will enhance my client's home, while being a good investment for the cost, has resulted in numerous referrals. Staying on top of the never ending changes in design, also allow me to offer the freshest design ideas. This is why it is so important to attend markets and network within the industry.

For many years I focused on staging, growing the business into two divisions: RE staging and interior design-renovation services. As my design business steadily increased, I closed my staging division to allow greater focus in this area. With my recent expansion into quality cabinetry, I look forward to where this will take both my clients and my business.

Specialties

Design Project Management for Home Renovations, Interior Decorating and Design, Space Planning, Color Consultation, and Window Treatments
