Mike Dziok's Summary

Mike Dziok is a director-level marketing leader with fifteen years of experience. His work covers solution-based selling, training, customer incentives, research, media, web development, and creative.

Having filled specialist roles early in his career, Mike has some advantages over executives who haven't worked their way up a marketing ladder. Tough deadlines, price negotiations, and extracting truth from suppliers are ingrained. “Mike can't be hornswaggled.” is how one business partner put it.

Mike describes the most rewarding thing about his work as “building marketing machines” and he sums it up like this.

1. Uncover business challenges and understand how those challenges obstruct sales.
2. Dream up a custom solution and bring that solution to life. (the fun part of the job)
3. Train the sales team; they are the most important part of any marketing machine.
4. Measure the results against the plan.
5. Adjust the machine and make it better.

Mike's most recent machine is projected to generate more than $20M in new sales in 2013. He explains, “It's rooted in the most fundamental concept of sales and marketing; find your customer's pain and present an actual fix for it. When you do that, you don't have to do much selling or convincing.” Mike offered, “We are seeing customers make bigger commitments to buy new products from us because our program is so clearly tied to growth of new categories.”

While earning an MBA from The State University of New York, Mike co-authored research on website design. His work was published in The Association of Marketing Theory and Practice Proceedings, a PhD academic journal. He has won numerous awards from national trade journals as well as national recognition from his employer.

As a co-worker puts it, “Mike hates buzz words and corporate speak. If a meeting drags on for more than an hour, he will say that we are talking just to hear ourselves talk. He's fun and enthusiastic about getting things done; he's contagious.”
