Lloyd Pearson's Summary

My focus is legal directories. I do two things:

Firstly, I am a legal directory consultant. I help law firms raise their profile among organizations and publications that rank, analyze, measure and write about lawyers – directories, listings, surveys, and awards – and related media and communications issues. My consultancy website/blog has regular news, advice and resources for law firms engaged with legal directories.

Secondly, I founded NovoLawyer – an online lawyer search tool - in 2012. Designed for the fluid, globalized legal market, NovoLawyer highlights business lawyers around the world, with a focus on cross-border work. Using the unique search and refine filters, clients can find lawyers with specific combinations of practice, industry, and foreign expertise.

As well as legal directories, my professional interests are international legal markets, the business of law, and legal marketing/communications.

I have worked in the legal sector for most of my career.

My involvement in the legal directories world began in the 1990s when I was part of the team that worked on the launch of Chambers Global, a leading guidebook.

Later I became editor of Chambers Global, and worked on the first editions of Chambers USA, as well as contributing to Chambers UK.

I joined the leading international firm, Herbert Smith, in 2004. From London, I ran the press office, and worked on media relations, communications, and legal directories.

In 2006 I moved to White & Case’s global communications team in New York - becoming the first dedicated law firm directories manager in the U.S.

The role involved managing the full range of legal directories, publications and surveys across the world, and producing hundreds of submissions every year.

After moving to the U.K in 2008, I formed 393 Communications to make the directories process smoother and more effective for law firms, and to help lawyers promote themselves to the growing number of listings, surveys and awards.

NovoLawyer

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Legal Services industry

August 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) London, United Kingdom

Launched in 2012, NovoLawyer is a new and innovative online search tool and directory of international business lawyers.

At the heart of NovoLawyer is a unique and powerful search facility.

Clients and others with an interest in finding the right lawyer can search using combinations of practice, industry, and foreign expertise.

Using the refine bar, they can narrow down their selection and find the right lawyer in just a few clicks.

NovoLawyer was designed for the fluid, globalized world and helps clients and lawyers find each other in a changing international market.

Lawyers can list themselves on NovoLawyer and select the practices, industries, and countries, where they have expertise and experience.

This means they will appear in the right places when clients and referral partners are searching for a lawyer with their mix of skills and experience.

For lawyers in private practice, NovoLawyer provides a dynamic platform to present an engaging, substantive and up-to-date profile to the market and position themselves in front of clients, prospects, referral partners, and the wider market.

Lawyers can log in at any time, update their profile, and check how many people viewed their profile using a personal dashboard.

NovoLawyer currently features more than 7,000 lawyers from 180 countries – and rising every day.

Lawyers: if you are in private practice, please register at novolawyer.com
