Elizabeth Lombino's Summary

I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) with over 12 years of experience working in the fields of addiction, mental health & HIV/AIDS. Clinical Supervision and Staff Training are my primary interests and specialties.

I am a Freelance Writer, writing about all things that interest me. My pieces have been featured on Change.org, TheBody.com, and PostPartumProgress.com.

I also present clinical workshops at conferences throughout the country. I am a regular presenter at NASW-NJ Annual Conference and the Social Work and HIV/AIDS Annual National Conference. I am also a regular presenter with Columbia University and Rutgers University.
