November 2011 – January 2013 (1 year 3 months) London, United Kingdom

I was employed by Profero specifically to work within ASOS head office in London as a Software developer on the ASOS Marketplace website.

ASOS Marketplace launched in 2010 with the aim of building a Global Fashion Democracy; The place where anyone, anywhere in the world, can sell fashion, to anyone, anywhere in the world.

It was a greenfield project and was carefully cultivated inside and out to provide a great experience for buyers, sellers, its developers and business users alike.

The Marketplace team was small (less than 10 people) and dynamic. We worked as a close-knit unit encompassing product management, operations, UX design, QA and development.

We worked using agile principles, with a dual focus on honing our workflow to reflect the demands of the outside world, and maintaining constant communication and feedback. We iterated quickly - our features generally went from conception to delivery in a matter of weeks. The smallness of the team meant that everyone had a voice and if you had a great idea for a feature often it would become real in a very short space of time. Everyone involved with Marketplace really cared about it and that passion shows through in the site itself.
