Jeff Hale, Ph.D.'s Summary

The statistics are harsh — according to Gallup research, more than 50% of U.S. workers are unhappy in their jobs, and this disengagement is directly costing thousands of businesses and not-for-profit organizations up to $350 billion in lost productivity each year.

I have a passion that drives me. I’m out to change the way companies and not-for-profit organizations do business and help my clients push through the boundaries and barriers they’re facing to create healthy workplaces, where employees thrive, are valued, are connected, are empowered, and their companies prosper. I want to everyone to have a great job in a great place to work.

Making use of extensive global experience — 20+ years in diverse executive and operational leadership roles—and a doctorate in Organizational Leadership, my work as a management consultant enables my clients to be clear about why they are in business, draw on that purpose to engage their employees in meaningful work, and make sure their business processes serve their employees and clients in ways that increase revenue and profits.

For me, that’s the key to staying cutting edge, building equity, ensuring motivated and high-performing teams, staying profitable (or becoming more profitable), negotiating the inevitable shifts of a dynamic business, and maintaining a connection not only with your clients but with your team.

Leading global operations involving 600 people in up to 50 countries and managing budgets of up to $17 million has developed my expertise as a management consultant. Drawing from this experience, I’m able to help my clients gather momentum, grow, and get to the next stage — whether that involves physical expansion, revenue growth, a culture shift, or positioning the company for a profitable sale.

For more information, articles, and tips, please visit our web site at http://wellspiritconsulting.com.
