Joseph P. Smith's Summary

► About Me:

I began my Sales Management career in the Telecommunications Industry in 1998, and have served the business communities in North Carolina and South Carolina specifically since 2001. Over the last 15 years I have successfully built and managed many sales organizations in offices across a wide variety of states and markets. I have a strong track record of success in both the CLEC and ILEC sides of Telecom, and have served in senior leadership positions in both Direct Sales and Indirect channels.

► About North State Communications – the Piedmont Triad’s Phone Company:

North State Communications is one of the nation's 15 largest integrated communications companies, maintaining a robust network composed of more than 68,000 miles of high capacity fiber optic facilities, utilized to carry everything from voice and broadband Internet services to networking and IP services to critical back-up and disaster recovery data for businesses.

North State operates as an ILEC, providing services primarily in a 600-square-mile area located in the Piedmont Triad Region of North Carolina in and around the communities of High Point, Thomasville, Archdale, Randleman, Jamestown, Trinity and portions of Greensboro and Kernersville. North State also operates as a CLEC, providing advanced business communication services in Greensboro and Winston-Salem. In 2011, North State acquired Data Chambers, a SSAE 16 Soc 1 Type II data center located in Winston-Salem.

North State offers landline, Internet, data and wireless services that enable consumers and businesses of all sizes to connect, communicate and collaborate without limitations. NSC delivers the ultimate broadband Internet and video entertainment experience across an all-digital fiber-to-the-home network under its PLEX brand; offers the nation's most advanced mobile connectivity and leading wireless devices through a joint arrangement with AT&T; and provides a wide array of innovative business communication services.
