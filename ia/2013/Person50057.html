Greg Brookes' Summary

I took my first fitness qualifications as a personal trainer back in 1992 and since then have been on an amazing journey into Sports Massage and Rehab, Kettlebells, Strength and Conditioning and Fat Loss.

In 2006 I started GB Personal Training based in London and built up the company to 10 personal trainers. We were one of the first personal training company's to offer a money back guarantee should results not be achieved within 30 days. I personally educated all the trainers up to my exacting standards to offer a premium personal training experience.

In 2007 I started instructing kettlebells both for rehabilitation and conditioning. I offered seminars educating personal trainers on how to best teach and train with kettlebells.

I then took my kettlebell skills into the workplace and offered blue chips such as Dow Jones and Aviva private kettlebell sessions. I continue to teach kettlebells in the corporate environment today.

I currently spend a good deal of time educating personal trainers on how to improve their business and also achieve better results for their clients.

I also write for the press offering my, often controversial, thoughts on health and fitness and have been featured in most of the top publications.
