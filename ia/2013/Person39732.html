Ed Taylor, Internet Marketing Coach's Summary

Ed Taylor is one of the country's leading Internet marketing experts for small business. By distilling information about what works for small business relative to SEO, website conversion improvement, e-mail marketing and social networking Ed's clients typically see bottom-line improvement rates of between 10% and 300%.

Since presenting his first seminar on Internet marketing in 1994, Ed has been working with small business to improve their results. Having delivered more than 1,550 presentations Ed is a highly-rated international speaker.
