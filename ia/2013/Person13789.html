Betsy DeLage's Summary

Simply put, we are passionate about creating solutions to address an organization’s direction and marketing challenges.

Typical clients are faced with these types of situations: expanding beyond their current market area; changing or fine-tuning the direction of the business; introducing new products; unsatisfactory marketing results.

We help businesses and nonprofit organizations develop the best methods to achieve their goals and drive growth. From tactical to strategic we work with clients in the areas of marketing planning and execution and strategic planning.

Our clients are non-profit as well as for-profit organizations in a variety of industries/sectors including IT, manufacturing, engineering/architecture, healthcare and local government and located in Michigan and Ohio.

Specialties

All aspects of marketing strategy, messaging, planning and execution, non-profit strategic planning; marketing communications, go-to-market planning and execution, lead and revenue generating strategy and execution.
