Dr Ben Bruce's Summary

My name is Ben and I absolutely love what I do. In my job, I get to see some amazing changes in people’s lives, so I guess in a sense it’s not really a job, but instead a passion of mine.

When I was young, I became involved with Taekwon Do and did this for a number of years, which inevitably took its toll on my body and I found myself in the local chiropractors office with a sore lower back. I was impressed at how quickly it seemed to work, but I think the thing that impacted me the most was the other changes I started to notice. As well as my back feeling freer and less painful, I started to notice over the coming weeks that I was sleeping easier and was able to concentrate for longer periods of time at school, and felt more alert. All I was really going for was my back pain but once my chiropractor explained how the spine protected the nervous system, which is how the body communicates with itself, it all started to make sense.

This was enough to motivate me to move to Auckland and study for 5 long, hard years to complete a Bachelor of Chiropractic. When I finished my studies I joined a practice in Mairangi Bay on the North Shore and practiced there for 3 years before I went out on my own, and now have two branches of Balanced Spine Chiropractic, one in Albany and one in Warkworth.

If you are interested at all in learning more about your spine and the vital role it plays in how your body functions (not just whether you have a sore back or not..), feel free to get in touch and I'd love to teach you more about. After an initial consultation just about everyone I see is blown away by how little they know about their spine and about how important it really is!
