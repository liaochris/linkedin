Andrew Lick's Summary

Andrew Lick is an Associate Broker specializing in Harrisburg commercial real estate at RSR Realtors, Central PA's Premier full service real estate firm. A trusted resource for office, industrial, retail and investment sales and leasing. Born and raised in Harrisburg he has a wide array of regional contacts and strong ties to the community. Andrew emphasizes a dedicated approach that utilizes local market knowledge, resources and technology to consistently reach client goals. Services include commercial sales, acquisitions, tenant and landlord representations and home sales for select clients.

While working with many of the area's most influential corporations and decision makers his on going mission has been to build relationships based on high standards of integrity, service and professionalism. Notable transactions have included The Vartan Group, Hamilton Health Center, Hersha Group, Classic Communities, Fine Line Homes, Mike Murphy Rentals, M & T Bank, The Homeland Center, The Catholic Diocese of Harrisburg and The Dauphin County Redevelopment Authority. Andrew is currently on the Board of Directors at the ICI Council of The Greater Harrisburg Association of Realtors and has a long record of civic involvement. He has served as an advisory board member of The Multiple Sclerosis Society, Harrisburg Keystone Rotary Club, St. Stephen's Episcopal Cathedral, The Hill Society and The Harrisburg Young Professionals.
