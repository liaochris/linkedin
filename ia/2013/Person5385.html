Amanda Longden's Summary

There are real benefits to your business of working with The HR Company including:

- you get the services of an experienced, qualfied HR professional at a fraction of the cost of employing someone in-house.

- access to expertise in all aspects of human resources from HR planning; design & implementation of HR systems & processes; and performance management, through to drafting employee handbooks and dealing with disciplinary & grievance issues.

-minimising the risks associated with non-compliance with employment legislation.

- allowing you to get on with the job of running your business in the knowledge that the worries and concerns around employing and managing people, will be taken care of.
