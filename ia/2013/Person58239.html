Jane Rosen's Summary

Jane C. Rosen is a screenwriter, author and PR executive. Drawing upon over 20 years of experience as a public relations professional, broadcasting producer/director and media professional, she brings her humor and insight to women’s issues. She is listed on IMDB and InkTip.

Jane has completed three screenplays, Grapes, Ancestral and Alice. Her book, My Life as a Corporate Goddess, was launched at the Forbes Executive Women’s Conference, and is a humorous reflection of her corporate experience, filled with inspiring stories of women she’s met along the way.
Jane has written articles for the Business Journal, Womensbiz.us, NW Women’s Journal and co-authored Convergence Marketing: Combining Brand & Direct for Unprecedented Profits, John Wiley & Sons.

She is a member of Oregon Public Television's Community Advisory Board, (CAB).

She held a number of executive positions in television including Executive Producer, Director, and Producer of Cultural and Public Affairs. Her broadcasting career in New York, Houston and Dayton included award-winning national programs, music videos and commercials, working with Lyle Lovett, BB King, Buddy Guy, Judy Collins and others. Her documentaries range from the art of Annie Leibovitz to the life of surrealist, Max Ernst. Her corporate roles include Director of Executive Public Relations, Executive Director and Global Manager for Senior Leadership Development at NCR.

Specialties: She has placed as a finalist and semi-finalist in screenwriting competitions including the LA Femme Film Festival, New England Women in Film & Video, BlueCat Screenwriting, Moondance Film Festival, Silver Screenwriting, and twice in the Female Eye Film Festival in Toronto. Additional awards include Emmy Awards, a Corporation for Public Broadcasting Award, and The Ohio Governors Award for her contributions to cultural and educational television production.
