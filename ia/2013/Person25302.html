Claire Wagner's Summary

I’m a seasoned marketing communications manager, strategist, copywriter, blogger, and social media consultant with more than 30 years of experience. Currently, I serve as the Marketing and Communications Manager for EHC LifeBuilders, a leading provider of shelter, housing opportunities, and supportive services to people in crisis and those experiencing homelessness in Silicon Valley.

As a consultant, my non-profit clients have included Applied Materials Foundation, Catholic Charities, Destination: Home, EHC LifeBuilders, Gardner Health Services, Santa Clara County Public Health Department, Stanford Hospital & Clinics, Second Harvest Food Bank, United Way Silicon Valley, and others.

From January 2006 – December 2007, I served as Director of Communications for Catholic Charities of Santa Clara County, where I oversaw all marketing and outreach activities for this large nonprofit organization. I later returned to this position in November 2011 for a four-month tenure as Interim Communications Director during a staffing transition.

From 1995 through 2005, I worked as a freelance writer for major technology firms and was also active in the nonprofit sector. Before starting my own business, I worked at the Pearson & Co. technology marketing agency, where I held successive positions as Senior Copywriter, Copywriting Manager, Public Relations Director, and finally Operations Director, overseeing the daily business of the agency. I began my career in technical writing in the personal computer industry.

Since 2010, I’ve maintained a blog about marketing communications at http://wagnerwrites.com.
