Animator/TRA & Lead Artist

Junebud AB

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Computer Games industry

October 2008 – August 2012 (3 years 11 months) Malmö

I had the leading responsibility of the character team in the game project MilMo. Contentwise in both MilMo and Tuff Tanks, my main role was Animator but I was also working as a Technical Artist and 3D/2D Artist.

As a Lead Artist I managed a team of 3-4 people and made sure that tasks were fullfilled on time. I worked in team with the producer to set goals and estimate time for tasks. The tools used for planning was in-house developed and Hansoft Project Manager Client.

As an Animator I had responsibility for anything animated in 3D or 2D in the games we produced. This includes animating, rigging, skinning, basic scripting, particle effects, movie editing, animated ads/logos and be part of designing pipelines regarding the animation systems in game.

My 3D/2D artist tasks were to work where I was currently needed. Like help out with modeling, texturing, paint icons, clean up art assets, optimize art assets, make vector art, script content, make concept art etc.
