Larry Kacyon's Experience

Owner & Integrated Marketing Specialist

L-A Advertising & L-A Interactive

2005 – Present (8 years) Lehigh Valley, Pa.

Purchased by Larry Kacyon and his wife, Marci in 2005, L-A Advertising celebrated its 55th anniversary in 2009!

Founded in 1954 by George Lieberman, the agency was later named Lieberman-Appalucci to include his partner. In 1994, L-A Interactive is formed. And, in 2000, Lieberman-Appalucci changes its name to L-A Advertising. In 2008, L-A Interactive acquires a Pocono-based Web development firm, further expanding its operations to provide more innovative, strategically integrated and cost-effective advertising, marketing communications and online marketing solutions.

Senior Vice President & Interactive Designer

Lieberman-Appalucci (former L-A name)

1987 – 2004 (17 years)

Working closely with Apple and IBM in the late 1980's, Larry Kacyon and his team led Lieberman-Apppalucci - as one of the first advertising firms in the country - into the era of desktop publishing, Mac to PC integration and marketing on the World Wide Web.

In those early days, the agency marketed products on AOL, America Online, launched Bethlehem Steel Corporation's first Web site, developed touch-screen kiosks for Lehigh Valley Hospital, produced digital video customer testimonials for Penske Truck Leasing, designed electronic product catalogs for Air Products & Chemicals, Inc. and created a CD-ROM presentation for Just Born PEEPS' 50th Anniversary.
