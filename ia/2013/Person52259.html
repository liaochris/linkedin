Heather Head's Summary

Nationally published author with more than twelve years of B2B marketing experience.

Character:
Passion for storytelling, keen strategic vision, and love for business and the people who run business.

What I Do:
I solve content problems for marketers.

I identify, develop, and manage the best writing talent available to bring quality written content to the marketers who need it. Together, we shape brand stories, connect with markets, and help companies reach their market potential.

I also lead content planning sessions, present on topics related to content marketing, prepare experienced writers and journalists to serve our clients, and sometimes still write copy. Kick-butt copy, of course.

"Storytelling is not the best way to get things done. It's the only way to get things done." -Steve Denning, internationally award-winning author on leadership and management, in a 2011 TED Talk.
