John Bowen's Summary

Over the last 30 years I've been very fortunate to work on developing and implementing strategies in both the private and public sectors and in sales, marketing, purchasing and operations, so have a very broad business experience of making things happen and delivering results.

But I've seen so many organisations that, even though they had the right strategy and goals, just could not implement them. These days I help those sorts of businesses understand what they need to do to Turn Strategy into Profit.

I've served as an executive and a non executive director and have worked throughout the UK trouble shooting business problems as well as having experience of working with companies in France, Germany, The Netherlands and the USA.

Through my career I've developed a number of teams, some from scratch and others as a result of mergers and the like. I was lucky enough in my early career to work for a number of people who believed in me and helped me. I'm passionate about repaying that debt through developing others and that is a key reason for my involvement in the 3 professional bodies that I am a member of, and why my company, Gulfhaven, includes training and development amongst its product offerings.

I'm also a great believer in the power of networking, and am open to contacts through LinkedIn.

I work mainly in the UK and Europe from my base here in Wiltshire, but venture further afield for interesting projects. When I can head out to Clermont, FL where the poolside and my den provide a great environment for thinking and writing.

At Gulfhaven we have a strong US connection, and are proud to support the Breast Cancer movement there through the donation of free mammograms. You'll find a link at the top right corner of our website www.gulfhaven.co.uk and every click through that link helps. Thanks for your support.

Outside of work I am a judge in various categories for UK industry awards and mentor people studying for their professional qualifications.
