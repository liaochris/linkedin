Abraao Dahis' Summary

Graduated in architecture from the University Santa Ursula, in 1982; studied marketing at Cademp of FGV - Getúlio Vargas Foundation; post-graduated Master in Project Management by the Polytechnic School of UFRJ - Federal University of Rio de Janeiro; Practitioner in NLP - Programming Neurolinguístic by INAp - Institute of Applied Neurolinguístic; has a background in coaching with certification by InCoaching - International Coaching Institute.
