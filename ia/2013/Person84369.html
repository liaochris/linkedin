Makasha Dorsey's Summary

Hi. I’m Makasha Dorsey. My personality, creativity, and knack for communicating with people have enabled me to work with amazing people in various industries.

From creating research processes for consumer finance and investment divisions at credit unions and national banks to helping religious institutions develop processes for their leadership teams, I can effectively manage internal communications strategies for organizations. Adept at conducting research and finding the proverbial needle in haystacks, my clients and partners have benefited from thorough needs assessments and on-point situation analysis to fuel their external communications efforts.

As a project/product manager, I have supervised budgets ranging from $1,200 to more than $1 million dollars. With a keen ability to manage resources, I have saved my clients as much as $125,000 by streamlining processes, developing partnerships, keeping exceptional records, and monitoring results while delivering hi-quality projects on time and within budget.

The current focus of Dorsey Group is to provide communications strategies for Christian-based organizations, personalities, authors, artists, and brands. Our client roster includes an independent press, several motivational speakers and pastors, a Stellar and Dove-award winning songwriter/producer, a Texas Gospel Music Excellence award winning female vocalist, and various authors and poets. We would like to add you to our growing list of exceptionally talented customers.

Specialties

Skilled in initiating projects, creating & implementing project plans, managing budgets & procurement, marketing communication tactics, market research, pricing strategies, corporate branding, public relations, internal controls, streamlining procedures, employee morale improvement and facilitating the day-to-day operations for organizations.
