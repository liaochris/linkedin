Jason Hart's Summary

Raced, instructed and driven countless cars/tracks. Currently racing ASCS sprint cars on dirt and sports cars. Working off a contract based schedule with various “Racing Schools”, “Film Producers”, “Race Teams”, “Precision Driving Coordinators”, “Race Drivers”, “www.revitupracing.com” and “Ride and Drive Event Organizers”. Forward looking focus is to continue competing in professional level sports car racing while also working in the professional driving/coaching industry. Ironically, “Heart” is the best way to define what drives the passion to succeed at being a professional race car driver.
