John Upchurch's Summary

I have had a successful and personally rewarding IT career, including leading the technical development of large projects for finance, web, print, and broadcast television. I was a pioneer developer of computerized sports scoring systems for national televised sporting events, including MLB, NBA, NFL, NCAA, PGA, IndyCar, NASCAR, and USTA. Now, I have transitioned from the technical side of media production to the creative side, and am currently directing my first feature film, Mango Dreams.
