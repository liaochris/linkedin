Duncan Casemore's Summary

An Oracle HR E-Business Suite veteran, having worked in Oracle's own HR development team as a Principal Developer for almost 6 years and then a further 8 years helping customers implement and support Oracle HR.

An Oracle HCM Certified Implementation Specialist with deep functional and technical knowledge and broad end-to-end lifecycle experience implementing Core HR, Payroll, Self Service, Talent and Performance Management, Absence, iRecruitment and Advanced Benefits on 11i up to 12.x for public and private organizations in a diverse range of industries.

An active member of the Oracle HR community, regularly presenting at conferences such as OHUG, UKOUG, HR and PS SIGs. He is also an Expert-level contributor in the Oracle HRMS Forums.

Specialties: Oracle HR, E-Business Suite, Mobile, Tablet, Fusion HCM, User Experience, SOA, Rich Front-ends, HTML5, RIA
