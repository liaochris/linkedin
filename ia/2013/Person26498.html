Colton Day's Summary

Colton Day, partner at Melissa Agnes Crisis Management, is an SEO for crisis management expert and talented Wordpress web developer.

Colton is a strategic SEO expert who understands how an individual / consumer’s Internet and social media behavior can heavily impact a brand’s online rankings. Pre-crisis, Colton uses a wide variety of tools to deeply analyze an organization’s web presence, in order to assess ways to reinforce their search engine optimization (SEO) and empower them with a strong base for ranking high within the search engines.

In a crisis, Colton uses white-hat SEO techniques to make sure that a company or organization does not get drowned-out by all the negativity being published online, making sure that when a client, prospective client or the media searches for the organization online, that their positive messages appear front and centre, along with the organization’s website and additional online profiles.

An experienced Wordpress developer, Colton also helps large organizations develop highly-functioning internal communications platforms, with a high emphasis on internal communications for crisis management.

On his spare time, you can find Colton snowboarding, riding his Harley, skeet shooting, sailing or traveling the world with his wife.
