Jonathan Knoll's Summary

Jonathan "Yoni" Knoll is a researcher, strategist, and designer who has spent more than a decade engaged in nearly every facet of software design and implementation. He brings UX deliverables to life through his extensive knowledge of prototyping, and his skills have been brought to bear for Standard & Poor’s, The Gilt Group, Refinery29, Happy Cog Studios, and more. He’s rocked the UX casbah on projects for S&P, Mozilla, Time, Purex, Keds, Gilt, and ADP, and his solutions have a penchant for seeing the light of day.

An active member of the UX community, Jonathan is ever engaged in at least half-a-dozen UX community sites, initiatives, conferences, and activities. He is also a Strategic Advisor for Rosenfeld Media, and recently served on the Advisory Board for the IA Institute.

Jonathan has led workshops on collaborative sketching and prototyping at IDEA, Interaction, IA Summit, and Big Design conferences. He is currently focused on product strategy & design for startups and businesses with new ideas.
