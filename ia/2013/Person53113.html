Hilde Buys' Summary

As a lifelong student with many diverse interests I have knowledge in, for some people, very diverse fields. I am very strong willed, and make my decisions after a thorough analysis that surprisingly considers many possible outcomes.

Hopefully I can, in the near future, combine my interests in Healthcare, Computer Science, Legislature, Education and Gifted Children in an innovative way. I enjoy using IT and Internet, and easily adapt to the technological advantages making new opportunities possible.

My atypical past, combining studying in a new field while working as a nurse spiced with my personal interests in finding enjoyment in reading complex research and legislature has allowed me to solve problems innovatively, as well as given me stamina to not give up when faced with obstacles. Networking using Facebook, twitter, forums, blogs as well as newsgroups has broadened my horizons tremendously.

Personally I am from an International backround and enjoy working with all kinds of culturally diverse people, especially including people with special needs.

I have reasonable experience in areas such as inclusion, leadership, problem solving and innovation. As a parent I also am privileged to have an indepth knowledge about Gifted Children and legislature surrounding Education as well as Special Ed, solving at times seemingly impossible complex situations.

In my daily life I am concerned about human rights and especially allowing cultural heritage of people to shape their lives, as well as the needs and rights of ethnic minorities. I speak and write Danish, fluent English as well as Afrikaans (similar to dutch).

For my previous school projects, in fields of research as well as development, I have independently arranged customer contact, designed and developed my ideas to attain top grades. Hopefully this, as well as, my past experiences makes me ready to become a project coordinator in the future.

Specialties

As an employee I am honest and loyal and I strive to deliver a good service. I therefore like to solve problems with uncanny creativity in combination with a problem-solving approach.

I am a responsible person and appreciate delivering a good piece of work. I have a wide medical, special needs education and legislative knowledge as well as an solid IT foundation. I also have group and one on one teaching experience, and have taught my patients, colleagues and friends in many diverse fields.
