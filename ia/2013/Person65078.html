Job van de Sande's Summary

My prime goal is to accelerate the development process of sustainable technologies. I believe sustainability is the only path ahead. Business can be the key driver to speed up the process of creating sustainable societies which benefit to us all. At times we may have to look harder to see opportunities in the challenges we face, but this is in my view a matter of creative thinking and mostly perseverance.

I am a graduate in the field of MSc Innovation Management part of Industrial Engineering and Management Sciences. During my studies I specialized through extra courses into sustainable technology development.
