Jay Sterling Austin's Summary

Building design professional and project manager with years of experience in architecture, interiors and industrial design development and realization. Recently out of the academy with a Master of Architecture degree, I'm currently engaging in high-stakes work in which my technical knowledge at a variety of scales can be leveraged in architectural design and construction.

Specialties: Client development and public relations, market research and segmentation, company branding and identity (analog + web-based). Design concept development through sketching, modeling, and rendering; ongoing client communications to maintain close working relationship durin schematic design; project management, shop drawings and exploded assembly documents as the project translates to design development; CNC machining, laser- & waterjet-cutting, construction documentation, construction oversight.
