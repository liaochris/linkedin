Linda Chell Rooney's Summary

I help aspiring, new and evolving entrepreneurs successfully navigate the New Economy with start-up, branding, marketing, business & personal growth expertise.

An award-winning marketing strategist, I wake up every day, eager and excited to put my years of business and marketing expertise to work for my clients, while continually learning and mastering new ways to help them succeed.

An entrepreneur since before I was old enough to order an adult beverage, my business acumen and love of all things marketing just might be genetic. I'm the first-born of an entrepreneurial father who instilled in me from a young age that determining your own destiny is best accomplished by owning your own business. I guess the lesson "took," since I dipped my toe into entrepreneurship at age 16 with a little part-time business and decided I liked it. From there, it was a succession of increasingly serious forays into business ownership, with a couple of short breaks in between when there was something new I could learn elsewhere.

I recently transformed my current business (launched in 2002) for portability, so I could make my favorite spot on earth my "home port," with the ability to literally travel the world and work from wherever my interests lead me. Mine is truly a lifestyle business - and I help others create theirs.

What I do:

● Help turn entrepreneurial dreams into reality through training, coaching and mentorship programs
● Develop engaging and compelling marketing strategies
● Creatively and seamlessly integrate traditional and new media marketing
● Craft effective branding, social media and email marketing strategies for either do-it-yourself or done-for-you implementation

Linda Chell Rooney's Experience

President

Linda Rooney | Business, Social Media and Email Marketing Navigatrix (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

July 2009 – Present (4 years 3 months) Virtually, Everywhere

My reputation for excellence and integrity is well-known, having been earned through the creation and delivery of award-winning marketing expertise and sales training / coaching / mentoring programs, initially in the home-building industry beginning in 1997.

As the economic winds of change began to blow in earnest in 2008, it became increasingly clear there was a much wider audience hungry for my brand of entrepreneurial and marketing expertise; namely, the professionals, business specialists, creative types and others being affected in ways few had ever anticipated. The New Economy began creating a huge number of New Entrepreneurs in a relatively short period of time. To serve this growing & more diverse market, I combined my full range of business development, online and email marketing services, and training programs under the umbrella of LindaRooney.com.

Today, my client base is a diversified mix of consultants, marketers, B2B and B2C businesses (although I prefer the term P2P - People-to-People - to describe ALL business relationships), REALTORS® and, yes, home builders. I have an ever-growing following on various social media channels and regularly engage in strategic alliances with other highly regarded professionals to provide comprehensive business development and marketing solutions to my clients.

I am proud of my long record of accomplishment in providing the marketing direction for numerous home-building and remodeling companies, marketing firms, a downtown merchants association and many other types of businesses and organizations.
