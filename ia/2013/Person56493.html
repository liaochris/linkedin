ABOUT ME:
I'm the owner & chief online marketing strategist, designer, and trainer at SmartMuse Marketing (Coming Soon) - a one-of-a-kind place for new entrepreneurs seeking to start and grow a business online and for established small business owners seeking to best leverage the online marketing world to build their business empires.

I'm a broad-spectrum marketing professional and thought leader with a skill, experience, and formal education combination that mixes the art, strategy, and technology that is "marketing" today.

I've spent the majority of the past decade working and living on the cutting edge of the marketing world.

My unique blend of formal educational achievements includes an MBA in Marketing and a Certificate in Electronic Commerce, both from the Hagan School of Business at Iona College, and an M.S. in Management and Systems from NYU.


Specialties: Online Marketing Plans; Website Design; Social Media Marketing; Email Marketing; Online Marketing Campaigns; Helping You Find Your Life's Work; Helping You Build Your Dream Business Empire Online.
