Karyl Miller's Experience

TV writer-producer

freelance sitcom writer-producer

1973 – Present (40 years)

Emmy winning sitcom writer-producer: The Cosby Show, The Mary Tyler Moore Show, The Lily Tomlin Specials, My Sister Sam, Sanford & Son(co-written w Richard Pryor), Kate & Allie, Tony Randall's Love Sydney, Erma Bombeck's Maggie, Diff'rent Strokes, 3's Company, Cher and many more plus many hour 1/2 pilots, both live action and animation. Script consulting for numerous sitcoms and animation pilots.

President

Southern California Cartoonists Society

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Newspapers industry

January 2006 – Present (7 years 10 months)

Organize and lead monthly meetings for SCCS ( the San Diego chapter of the National Cartoonists Society). Appoint and supervise Executive Board, obtain guest speakers, recruit caricaturists for the San Diego Blood Bank Cartoonists Day and the San Diego Chargers Blood Drive. Co- designed and maintain club website sccs-online.org. Supervise annual Comic-Con party for National Cartoonists Society and SCCS. Plan Comic-Con SCCS new member outreach.

editorial cartoonist

American Toon

2008 – Present (5 years)

Karyl's "American Toon" editorial cartoons and rants have appeared in San Diego City Beat newspaper, The Writer's Guild of America On The Line newspaper, The Writers Guild of America Written By magazine, the San Diego Democratic Party newsletter and on the web at MillerReport.blogspot.com and CNNs iReports. Her PlanB strip is on GoComics.com

writer, artist

PLAN B Comic Strip
