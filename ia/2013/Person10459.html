http://www.TruckingSocialMedia.com

Goal: To network with others within the trucking industry in order to provide knowledge, products, and services of value to the professional truck driver. Using Social Media to promote: Honesty, Integrity, and Transparency.

Involved in many facets of the trucking industry for over 30 years now, including: driver, owner operator, operations manager, and local business owner.

In the year 2000, "The Truth About Trucking" was introduced as an immediate download e-book and then as an Audio CD, discussing recruiting,high turnover rates, driver wages, CDL training,and other aspects students and new drivers need to know.
.
My intention is to to educate students and new drivers, guiding them in the right direction in order to avoid the pitfalls that I had seen so many succumb to during my 35 years.

AskTheTrucker.com blog was later introduced to share more knowledge and insight with others, including all those interested and involved in trucking. The blog is designed to allow and share thoughts and ideas as current concerns and issues of the industry are posted regularly. The blog is opinionated, but open for debate in order to open ones mind and discuss things in an intelligent and civilized manner.

In 2008 we began the Blog Talk Radio show Truth About Trucking "Live" where we discuss many of the most controversial and current topics and have included many well known expert guests as a part of the conversations and dialogue.
Recent stats from Blog Talk Radio are included here

http://aubreyallensmith.com/trucking-online-radio/index.html

Specialties

Specialize in trucking currents events and research. Guide new drivers and students in the right direction. Discuss the in and outs of the trucking industry, including the most current issues and regulations. Network with like minded people who desire to be a part of a team where integrity is the number one priority. By doing so we can add value and respect to one another and " Raise the Standards of the Trucking Industry"
