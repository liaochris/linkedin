David Krubsack's Summary

Director of R&D with hands-on and management experience integrating diverse technologies and talents for the development of electronic based products, especially active medical devices. Takes product from research and development to production and market quickly and efficiently, following FDA QSR.

Worked directly with Alfred E. Mann integrating technologies and staff from his existing companies, as well as contractors, to develop a new commercial product. Technologies and staff include electrical, mechanical, acoustics, audiology, surgical, biological, neurological, materials, chemical, batteries, and software.

25 years of experience in Electrical, Systems, and Computer Engineering, Signal Processing, and Software, including 18 years of commercial product development and management. Products include medical devices, audio, instrumentation, telecommunications, and efficient human-computer interaction.

Self-motivated, committed to the goals of the project and the company. Pleasant, keeps team excited with enthusiasm and optimism, respectful of people. Delegates tasks, reviews work, advises. Creative yet practical. Draws out relevant issues and communicates cross-technology solutions. Comfortable presenting to both peers and executive team. Excellent written and verbal skills.

Specialties

Electrical Engineering, R&D - research and development, DSP - digital signal processing, hardware, software, analog, digital, medical device, acoustics, hearing aids, audiology, neurostimulation, neuromodulation, class I, class II, class III medical devices, FDA regulated devices, implanted devices.
