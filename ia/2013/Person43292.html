Honors and Awards:

Since 1992, Eunice has combined her up-to-date knowledge, personal experience and understanding to assist her clients in achieving fitness results and optimal health. She has been a Certified Personal Trainer for corporate, small groups, Women’s Crisis Center and private clients. She administers fitness assessments and personalized fitness programs for clients.Eunice has been featured in magazines such as Muscle and Fitness and Strength Magazine for Women. She’s won Best in Class and Overall in the Massachusetts State Body Building Competition and has appeared as a guest poser in Body Building promotions.
