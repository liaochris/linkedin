Carol Neuman's Summary

My passion is empowering communities of champions to do enterprising, innovative work.

For the last decade, my career has focused on the intersection of education, technology and communications.

My expertise lies in education policy and research; advocacy and government relations; non-profit management; communications planning; project management; and public speaking.
