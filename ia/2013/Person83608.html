Lynn Anderson's Summary

Overworked? Overwhelmed? Afraid to Speak Up? Unsure How to Lead a Team?

As an executive leader, project management consultant, coach, motivational speaker, author, wife, and mom, I know all too well how life and your career can get the best of you. Don’t let it put you down. Be a Woman Warrior, unleash the power within you, and learn to be a leader in your life. Prepare for the battles ahead and come out victorious on your terms.

I specialize in helping busy professional women to:
• Find career-life balance
• Achieve career and personal success – yes this is possible!
• Reduce stress
• Tap into and build your energy level
• Learn key leadership skills that can be applied at work and at home
• Support your life transitions (e.g. career, new family additions, job loss, divorce, empty nest, etc.)

I struggled with these issues over my 25 year corporate career. Now I combine my executive leadership and project management experience with a keen ability to hone into your challenges and help you to create new possibilities.

I also provide business services for companies which include:
• Professional Development Seminars and Workshops
• High Performance and Team Engagement Activities & Workshops
• Women’s Leadership & Diversity Training
• Executive Leadership Training & Coaching
• Customer Service Excellence & Professionalism Training
• Energetic Sales Engagement Model
• Conflict & Negotiation Skills Training
• Motivational Speaking on Leadership Topics for Your Internal Employee Engagement Groups
• Business Coaching & Team Engagement for Medical Practice Owners & Entrepreneurs

It’s time to take charge of your life and not let it take charge of you! I encourage you to take me up on a complimentary coaching session and be amazed at how coaching can change your life.
