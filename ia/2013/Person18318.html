Brett Rudy's Summary

Brett is a strategic marketer with 15+ years of experience leading organizations and brands in the development and implementation of campaigns that enhance acquisition and retention efforts. He has expertise in digital marketing, customer engagement, direct marketing and traditional marketing across verticals ranging from CPG to retail to financial services. He also has expertise with website strategy and builds. Brett has managed employees, clients, and vendor partners. Brett is an idea guy, always seeking creative solutions to business problems.

In his spare time, he helps professional athletes raise money for charity, writes a monthly column for a local magazine, and plays as much amateur baseball as possible.

Specialties: Acquisition, Retention, Loyalty, Branding, CRM, Digital Marketing, Engagement, Testing, Analytics, Promotions, Advertising, Sports Marketing, Philanthropy, Retail, CPG, Direct Marketing.
