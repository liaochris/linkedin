Jason Brower's Summary

Jason is an Enthusiastic Entrepreneur and Company Principle with a broad Software Architecture background that includes experience in industries such as aerospace, anti-counterfeiting, credit card processing and point of sale software.

Jason's Software Architecture and Implementation experience includes Research and Development, Imagineering a Product Concept, Boot Code, Device Drivers, Programmable Interrupt Controller Software as well as high level application software development based on patterns such as MVVM.

Jason is highly passionate about Engineering and Software Development with an emphasis on Microsoft .NET technologies including C#, WPF, WCF, MSSQL, Windows 7, Windows 8 and touch screen user interface design.

At Authentix, I am involved in a fulfilling and exciting line of work that protects against the counterfeiting and diversion of our clients products which range anywhere from pharmaceuticals, oil and gas, cosmetics, pesticides, alcohol and tobacco to currency.
I work directly with a small group of PhD chemists, physicists, marketing directors and engineers who pull together to make a great line of products. More specifically my duties include, but are not limited to performing customer requirements capture, operating systems selection, development and integration as well as full applications development including a GUI interface.
