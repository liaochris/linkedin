John Canfield's Summary

I'm a 23 year-old filmmaker and analyst who graduated from SUNY Geneseo in May 2013. At Geneseo I studied financial analysis and business administration while captaining the Track and Field team.

In January 2013 I finished editing my latest film, "Adopt Me". In May it had its world premiere in front of a sold out crowd at the Landmark Sunshine Cinema on East Houston Street in NYC. Whenever I work on a project I immerse myself in all roles of production and was the director, editor, writer, producer, sound designer and re-recording mixer for my film.

I'm also a photographer, web designer, and a Level 1 CFA Candidate (December 2013).
