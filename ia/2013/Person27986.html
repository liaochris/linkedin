Adrian Cropley, ABC, FRSA's Summary

I am president and founder of Cropley Communication: a global consultancy specializing in strategy development in the areas of change communication, corporate communication, training and development, as well as executive coaching. Our headquarters are in Australia

Alongside my consulting work and sitting on a number of boards, I am the Immediate Past Chair of IABC International Association of Business Communicators

I've worked in the professional communication field for over 25 years, spending 16 years in telecommunications, During that time I worked in variety of roles from technical project management to staff development and HR management and as an internal consultant on Change Management and business planning and.

I have been fortunate enough to have gained a variety of experiences in many industries from both the public and private sector working directly for three CEO's. I have a number of fortune 500 companies as clients providing a mix of HR, Change and Communication expertise, strategic communication, as well as training and development in strategic communication. I offer senior counsel to organisations on the best strategies in corporate communication, change and development.

I have a global client base, who I work with to develop key communication strategies, develop their people and quite often coach or sit on their executive board as a strategic advisor.

I have managed teams of people in various organisations through sometimes challenging situations and have the ability to build, manage and lead large team while managing large and complex budgets.

Adrian Cropley, ABC, FRSA's Projects

  • Alcatel Lucent - Act Like an Agency Program

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Adrian Cropley, ABC, FRSA, Cyrus Mavalwala, ABC

    Developed and delivered a six virtual module course over six months, for 25 Communication Professionals globally in Alcatel Lucent. Each module included readings and mini assignments to use the various tools and models, to help embed the learnings within the team. The outcomes from this program, included bringing a virtual team together with a shared way of working. It helped the team focus on developing a consultacy mindset and using the models and tools to add even greater value as strategic advisors to the business.

  • Unilever Communications Development Program

    • January 2009 to May 2011
    Team Members: Adrian Cropley, ABC, FRSA, Helene Bradley-Ritt, MBA, ABC

    Consulted with Unilever Corporate communications Manager Asia/ Africa/ Middle East, to establish a bespoke communications training program, for professional communication practitioners across the region. The program is a combination of skills based face to face programs, virtual programs, to build the strategic acumen for professionals. Two pilots were run in 2010 & 2011 which combined IABC accreditation with professional development to elevate communications professionals to strategic advisors and prepare candidates for IABC accreditation. In 2010 - 2011 13 senior professionals became accrediated, 13 more were trained to the highest standard of Internal Communications.
