Julie Larsen Wyss' Summary

Dynamic, passionate and ambitious Realtor, consistently delivering results.

Team Wyss clients receive a unique Real Estate STRATEGY, cohesive SYNERGY & incredible SUCCESS.

Buying or selling a home is one of life’s major events & can be extremely stressful. Choosing an agent you can trust is a critical aspect to make the process smooth & enjoyable.

Julie Wyss is a Real Estate Broker at Intero Real Estate Services & Broker Associate at North Star Mortgage Associates. With over 20 years experience in business, she is known for high integrity & attention to detail. Energetic, positive & dedicated to achieving clear objectives & demonstrating high values, Julie believes success means continual reinvention & adapting to fluctuating environments.

In 1994 Julie began her business career by opening a fitness center in Los Gatos. As sole proprietor, she was responsible for all sales & marketing strategies as well as management of a staff of 15. Julie ultimately negotiated the sale of the business for a profit in a declining market.

Julie has earned her reputation as “ The Selling Genius” by listing & selling homes throughout Silicon Valley, ultimately netting more money for sellers than other agents. She is passionate about helping buyers secure the best offer possible, her extensive knowledge of the area contributing to her success in this residential match making. Julie assists in navigating the real estate market, making sure she understands & meets your needs, answers your questions & respects your time and budget.

A firm believer in the power of talented, committed individuals working together to make an extraordinary difference, Julie makes her transactions exciting & completely clear, paying close attention to every detail. Relax as Julie manages the flow of information & remains connected to you, guaranteeing the smoothest transaction possible.

Julie is your advocate, your negotiator, a trustworthy professional & an innovative member of your team.
