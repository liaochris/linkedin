Benjamin Bahrenburg's Summary

I am an author, blogger, and a technologist in PricewaterhouseCooper’s International Assignment Services (IAS) practice. Over the last decade I have designed and implemented innovative Enterprise scale solutions to meet the global mobility challenges of numerous Fortune 100 organizations’. I specialize in building enterprise solutions using Cross-platform mobile technologies, geo location services, and business focused domain specific languages.

I collaborate and lead product teams to provide disruptive Enterprise client focused solutions. A proven Enterprise innovator, I have lead global product teams in the delivery of market leading solutions with industry award winning results.

  • Aidori – Relief App for Japan (Mobile App)

    • April 2011 to December 2011
    Team Members: Benjamin Bahrenburg

    Aidori is developed for all the people who have lost their families and homes and we hope the app will be of help as much as possible.

    Main features:

    - Map feature to find shelters nearby or from address.
    - Twitter viewer to show hashtag #prayforjapan.
    - Instagram viewer to show images of prayforjapan.
    - News reader to watch latest news.
    - Telephone directory of call center of missing persons.
    - Lists of links to the disaster information site.
    - Support for English, Japanese and Korean languages.

    Aidori was developed in a very condensed time frame primarily by Appcelerator, Appcelerator Japan, and the Appcelerator Titans with contributing efforts from the Appcelerator Developer Community at large.
