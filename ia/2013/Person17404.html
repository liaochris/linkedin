Brian Hanse's Summary

Welcome. After the passing of a loved one, it is important for the executor or personal representative of a decedent's estate to have tax, legal and financial counsel so that the transfer of assets to the surviving spouse or to the next generation can be handled as smoothly as possible. I'm pleased to assist with each step of this process. A carefully planned estate can make the transition easier. Taxes can be minimized, often through the use of trusts. We discuss how closely-held businesses and vacation homes can remain in the family while being equitable to all children. Lastly, in situations where there are children from previous marriages, we have techniques for protecting surviving spouses, children and including step-children, where desired. I would be pleased to hear how we might help.
