Matthew J Doughty's Summary

Matthew J Doughty is a Los Angeles, CA Music Composer, music editor, and Avid Pro Tools 11 Mixer & Recording Engineer. "I live right around the corner from the Fox/Sony lots and am ready to make Custom Music for Your Next Project!!" CREDITS: ABC TV, BECK, ESPN FILMS, FLEETWOOD MAC, THE KILLERS
http://MatthewJDoughty.com

---

As a Songwriter, Matthew J Doughty has studied under Nashville's Tom Shapiro (four time BMI Country Songwriter of the Year: Randy Travis, Tim McGraw, Trisha Yearwood... also a Belmont alumnus) and Hollywood's Marty Panzer (35 Gold and Platinum Albums: Barry Manilow, Julio Iglesias, Disney). MjDoughty has Composed Music for ESPN FILMS and Oscar Nominated Director Ron Shelton's Sports Documentary about NBA Superstar Michael Jordan titled "30 for 30: Jordan Rides the Bus" which aired on ABC TV, as well as written over 43 minutes of instrumental music for Aslyum Entertainment's (Hollywood, CA) Paranoize Music Library. In addition, MJD has Recording Engineering and Studio Production experience with bands like Fleetwood Mac, The Killers, Econoline Crush, and Stabilo Boss. He has worked alongside studio greats like Sacramento's Joe Johnston (Cake, Deftones, Papa Roach), LA Mixing Engineer: Mark Needham (Chris Isaak, Lindsey Buckingham, Shakira), and Rock Producer Sylvia Massy (Tool, Johnny Cash, Prince, The Red Hot Chili Peppers).

Listen to Trailer Music Cues, Instrumental Soundtracks and more original music @matthewjdoughty - http://MatthewJDoughty.com
