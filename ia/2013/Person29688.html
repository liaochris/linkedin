Danielle Elliott Smith's Summary

Danielle Smith is a StoryTeller. Once upon a time, Danielle was an Award Winning Television News Anchor. When the redlight was on, Danielle was at her best, relaying the days events to those viewers who were fortunate enough to watch her do what she loves, tell a story.
Danielle has turned the page on her Traditional Media tale, and is now focused on "Social" Media where she is the founder and creator of ExtraordinaryMommy.com. Now, she is expanding upon that love of intersecting 'social' and 'media', specificially through video with www.DanielleSmithMedia.com.

Danielle's list of accomplishments and relationships include:
*Featured nationally on CBS Early Show, Fox News, MSNBC, CNN Headline News, NPR
*Author of Mom, Incorporated: A Guide to Business + Baby
*Brand/Blogger relationships with companies such as P&G, Con Agra, Capital One, Chevy, Microsoft Bing, Office Max, Clorox, Fisher Price, Kraft, HP, Daisy, Earth Footwear
*Correspondent covering 2011 ProBowl in Hawaii & 2010 Winter Olympics on behalf of P&G, Fashion140 Conference-Lincoln Center in NYC, 140 Conference-NYC (sched June 2011) and the Kodak Theater in Los Angeles and the 2011 Capital One Bowl Game & Mascot Challenge in Orlando, FL
*Seminar speaker at: Blissdom, Evolution of Women in Social Media, Social Fresh, Type-A Mom, St. Louis Women In Media, Creative Alliance, Windy City Social, Parents As Teachers

Danielle is the producer and host of Bliss TV, segments of sponsored conversation that highlight the topics moms are talking about every day.

Danielle was born to communicate. Her specialty is using video to relay to your viewer, client, and/or target audience the exact message that you want to convey.

Danielle has worked with major networks and brands- on-camera and online–as a spokesperson and advocate. Covering topics ranging from social media best practices and vlogging to cause marketing by brands and brand/blogger relations, she is a sought-after opinion on how to reach a niche market.
