C Com Enterprises' Summary

Our first website project (dates back to 1998), was completed over a 32Kbps dial-up internet connection. Back then, Hotmail had not been served hot & Yahoo didn’t deliver any such emotion! Dot Com was in its nascent stage but had started blooming faster than the speed of light! Soon, hotmail became very hot, Yahoo! was pure fun and Google had started making waves! The bubble burst and Dot Com became Dot Gone, Hotmail by now was lukewarm and Yahoo was low on being yippy but Google kept making waves by their innovativeness & creativeness!

That was 1998. Today (2012), we work on a 2Mbps broadband connection, Facebook has become the third most populous entity on earth (after China & India) and the dynamics of the web space are changing by the second but our work quality has remained sturdy as ever. Over these years, we have delivered countless websites, designed & developed e-commerce portal(s), coded internet software(s) and have completed umpteen numbers of multi-media projects.

We have been witness to two waves of boom & gloom. We made hay while the sun shined and also believe that the economical recessional waves have made us wiser & stronger. Future economics doesn’t bother us any longer, but the future of Internet does find our undivided attention. Our bet is that in future countries will spend millions for upkeep of cyber armies and that internet virus threats will be treated as hazards bigger than nuclear bombs!

We are a strong team of dedicated & passionate web enthusiasts. Our understanding of the web-space, branding & online marketing is nice and we did be happy to have some conversations on any or all of the three... So whenever nearby, do drop in for some hot coffee & some even hotter conversations!
