Kevin Mattice's Summary

As a former Air Force intelligence analyst and Gulf War veteran, Kevin's entire career has been oriented around the collection, processing, analysis, and presentation of information. After 12 years in the military (the last three of which were spent training others in the intelligence disciplines), he entered the civilian world of information design.

Since leaving the Air Force in 1997, Kevin has held a number of different positions, including instructional designer, interaction designer, 2D and 3D illustrator, and technical writer. His recent industry experience is comprised of stints with various companies in the telecommunications sector, national security, online travel, and corporate consulting. Currently, Kevin works at Texas Instruments as a senior user experience architect specializing in the design of Web-based user experiences.

www.kevinmattice.com

Specialties

User experience, user centered design, usability sciences, information architecture, user interface design, user studies, usability testing, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX, Adobe Flex, Adobe Fireworks, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Axure Pro RP, Microsoft Visio, Microsoft Expression Suite
