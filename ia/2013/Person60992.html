Jeff Kortes' Summary

Owner of Human Asset Management LLC a human resources consulting firm specializing in professional search, employee retention and leadership training. Professional speaker on these topics. We use a systematic 10 step process to successfully complete searches in areas of supply chain/logistics/purchasing, operations, engineering, sales, quality, R&D, human resources, environmental health and safety.

*********************************************************

Deliver a proprietary Leadership Training Series titled Execution Focused Leadership that is focused on driving results oriented leadership without destroying your people in the process.

*********************************************************

Professional speaker on the topics of retention and recruitment, development of high performance work cultures and leadership. My passion for these topics is evident if you view the video of me giving my "No Nonsense Retention" speech by clicking on my website above. My program "Slug Proof Your Team" provides a proven method to develop a high performance work culture. Join my LinkedIn group "Slug Proof Your Team" for tips and candid discussions on how to develop a high performance work culture.

Located in Milwaukee, WI, our clients are located across the United States. Our reach is global because we are a member of our owner/membership in a world wide network of 400 independent recruiting firms so we can solve your recruiting needs no matter where they might arise in the world!

*************************************************************

Human Resources Professional/Generalist with 25+ years experience in privately held and public corporations. Have led organizational change initiatives, continuous improvement, developed strategy and policies, driven talent retention and recruitment strategy, and labor/manangement improvement initiatives. Skilled strategist as well as tactician.

Specialties

Human Asset Management LLC conducts retained and contingency searches across a variety of industries anywhere in the world. We consult to companies looking to retain their employees, and deliver a leadership training and development series to organizations. SHRM member and National Speakers Association WI board member. KEYWORDS: employee retention, talent retention, recruiter, search, trainer, training and development, leadership training, speaker, Milwaukee, Wisconsin, SHRM, Kortes, teams
