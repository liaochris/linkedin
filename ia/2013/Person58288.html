Janet Edmunson's Summary

I am an inspirational speaker, writer and health promotion professional who is dedicated to using my life experiences and inner values to support the growth and self esteem of the people my life touches.

I am an inspirational speaker on personal growth, leadership, health promotion and care giving throughout the U.S.

I have over 30 years of experience in the health promotion field and a master's degree in exercise science from Georgia State University. Recently retired as the Director of Prevention and Wellness at Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Massachusetts, I led a team of 20 to support the company's promise to "always put our members' health first."

Specialties

Speaking, Writing, Inspiration, Growth, Leadership, Health, Care giving
