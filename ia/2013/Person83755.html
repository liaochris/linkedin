Lynn Forbes' Summary

Lynn Forbes is a highly experienced digital strategist and industry leader who incorporates exceptional business development savvy with deep experience in digital content and product creation that is truly rare. This mastery combined with outstanding creativity and the talent to define goals, source talent and manage diverse teams has resulted in big wins for some of the top online brands including BlogHer, Scripps Networks and Disney.

Lynn is the co-founder of WHOA! Network - Women Honoring Our Age - a platform, a place and a perspective for women 50 and beyond who are revolutionizing and redefining the way we grow older. WHOA! is a movement to rebrand “women of a certain age”.
