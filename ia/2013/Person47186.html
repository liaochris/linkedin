Gary Papay's Summary

Gary Papay, CBI, M&AMI, SBA, CMEA

Professional Intermediary and Business Appraiser specializing in the sale and transfer of privately held mid-market companies since 1976. Gary has attained the professional designations of Certified Business Intermediary (CBI), and Merger and Acquisition Master Intermediary (M&AMI) with the International Business Brokers Association (IBBA) and the M&A Source, the world's largest international organization of experienced, dedicated Merger and Acquisition Intermediaries representing the middle market. In addition, Gary has been awarded the prestigious Fellow of the IBBA, the IBBA Chariman's Award, and the Tom West Award through the IBBA.

Gary is also a Certified Machinery and Equipment Appraiser/Broker (CMEA) with the National Equipment & Business Brokers Institute (NEBB).

Specialties

Business sales and acquisitions, divestitures, business appraisals, machinery & equipment appraisals and brokerage, consulting services. Designated an Industry Expert in the Petroleum and Propane Industries by Business Brokerage Press.
