Aurnhammer LLC's Summary

Aurnhammer LLC is a creative media, custom design and development agency specializing in branded smart device applications for the mobile space. (iOS & Android) Located in Lower Manhattan, the company prides itself on being born, bred, and operated solely in New York City. Besides technological skill in coding and programming, Aurnhammer team players bring experience in the fields of film and television production, digital arts, and graphic design. At the company’s core is a solid team of strategists, designers and programmers who work in a variety technologies and platforms to flawlessly bring these ideas to life.

CLIENTS: Sony Music Entertainment, Showtime Networks, Kohler, The New York Jets, Foxwoods Resort Casino, Lumber Liquidators, Bass Pro Shops, Mass Mutual, Getty Images, Oldcastle, The Milwaukee Bucks, Lincoln Center, Stanley Hand Tools, Russian Standard Vodka, Clorox, Century 21 Real Estate, Viacom, ClosetMaid, ThinkSmart Games and ShopKeep.

Specialties: iPhone, iPad, iPod, Android & Blackberry app Design, Development & Innovation.
