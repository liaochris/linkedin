Mikail Mokuena's Summary

I work with a wide range of local and international clients in SMEs, Professional Intermediaries, Entrepreneurs, High net-Worth Individuals and Non-profit organisations. I provide innovative and responsive corporate secretarial and corporate governance services.

These services are tailored to support businesses in the establishment and administration of their corporate structures’ affairs in the U.K. I understand the challenges faced by boards of directors, board committees, directors, senior management, non-executive directors and key stakeholders.

As a result, I provide support aimed at effective implementation of corporate strategies, alignment of values, vision, strategy and controls. Adherence, compliance with Codes of corporate governance and company statutory obligations. I take a highly consultative and pro-active approach in working closely with clients, to evaluate their corporate governance practices against international benchmarks. My services are based on in-depth experience of both complex corporate matters and regulatory compliance duties placed upon corporations and their officers in U.K.

I regularly work collaboratively with chief executive officers, finance directors, solicitors and general counsels to ensure implementation of best-practice compliance programs for my clients. I have helped clients to promptly respond to emerging compliance concerns and putting appropriate measures in place.

Specialties: Corporate Governance and Company Secretarial Services are key to my specialism in assisting international businesses with all statutory compliance and administrative obligations under the UK CA 2006.
