CreativeMynds Project, LLC is a creative arts production company focused on using various forms of performance art to inspire creativity in our audiences. Each project is a collaborative effort of urban artists bringing together our individual talents with the ultimate goal of sharing our art.
Through our exploration and development of various performance projects, specifically theatre, film, and visual arts, we are committed to producing unique work that tells the story and provokes emotion and thought from our audiences, while creating the platform and the opportunity for artists.
Creative Mynds Project, LLC is a sponsored project of Fractured Atlas, a non-profit arts service organization. Contributions for the purposes of Creative Mynds Project, LLC must be made payable to Fractured Atlas and are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.
