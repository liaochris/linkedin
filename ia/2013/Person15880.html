PRINCIPLES
Focused on the importance of sustainability in the built environment and some of the key ways in which it is measured. Sustainable cities, neighbourhoods were looked at with a critical approach. Architectural design, environmental agendas and different approaches to making sustainable buildings were examined and debated through seminars, weblogs and discussion boards.

STUDIO
Theory was very much put into practice with a design project based in Leith, Edinburgh. The process addressed complex issues of site and programme from a sustainable perspective. It dwelved into such aspects as the difficult relationship between the urban and the environmental and the role of technology in sustainable design.
The Design Elaboration course was an useful opportunity to reflect in greater depth on the design work and test it by means of environmental modelling software (Ecotect and IES VE).

DISSERTATION
Sustainable Retrofit of Office Buildings in the UK is a substantial work that demonstrated not only a thorough knowledge of research topics, but practical skills in the assembly of a complex yet coherent narrative.
