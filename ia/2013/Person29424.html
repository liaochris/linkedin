Daniel Bruce's Summary

A UK journalist and broadcaster for more than decade, I now work with organisations seeking to support the development of professional and independent news media in fragile and conflict affected states worldwide.

I have extensive and varied experience in the media development sector, from conducting journalism training in the field, to the development of new curricula, evaluation systems and donor fundraising proposals.

Specialties

 Training of Media Professionals
 Monitoring & Evaluation
 Training Curricula and Programme Development
 Institutional Fundraising & Proposal Writing
