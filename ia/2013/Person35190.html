Diana Parziale's Summary

An Art Director/Designer proficient in both print and web design, my talents lie in creating strong designs that communicate through various media whether in print or on-line. I've worked in-house within corporations and educational institutions, and have supervised a team of designers and production artists. In addition, I've worked in design studios with commercial clients from a variety of industries, including restaurants, magazines, entrepreneurs, artists, non-profits, and real estate companies.

Specialties

All areas of print design, corporate identity and branding, art direction/creative direction, supervision of design staff, web design

Diana Parziale's Experience

Art Director, Office of Marketing Communications

Boston College

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

March 2013 – Present (10 months) Chestnut Hill and Brighton, MA

Part of the management team that supports the marketing of Boston College to various external constituencies, including prospective students and parents, prospective graduate students, alumni, and the general public. The department focuses on projects that require a combination of original design, writing, and photography for print and online communications as well as signage, banners, and other types of promotional vehicles.

My key roles:
• Oversee initiatives that achieve marketing and communications goals for the University;
• Serve as a supervisor and mentor for staff and freelance designers by providing guidance on concept and strategic direction, and critiquing the quality and effectiveness of the work being produced;
• Develop concepts and design high profile projects for both print and electronic media, keeping budgets and schedules on track;
• Work with writers and editors to assist in the development of editorial direction;
• Collaborate with department staff to provide strategic guidance and address client needs;
• Guide the continuing development and application of the University brand and graphic standards for print and electronic publications, advertising, and signage;
• Oversee the design of University alumni magazines, undergraduate admission materials, and critical campaign and fund-raising publications.
