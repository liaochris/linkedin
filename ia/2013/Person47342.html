Gavin Keirans' Summary

Gavin is an experienced Management Consultant for Accenture focused on driving revenue/margin growth and return on investment (ROI), especially in the areas of sales and marketing strategy.

His career has primarily been spent working for multiple Fortune 100 High-Tech Companies, with experience also in Banking, Insurance and Consumer Goods. Gavin has managed global teams up to 9 direct reports, and led analytics to define benefits across multiple clients.

Gavin also leads two young professional Non Profit Corporations, The Philadelphia Catholic League Alumni Corps and New Leaders Council- Philadelphia Chapter.

In college, Gavin was Student Body President of Penn State University for two terms. He stays connected to his alma mater, most recently serving on the Executive Board of the Alumni Association.
