James Deas' Summary

James Deas is a business exec with broad experience in accounting, and financial management, with “hands on” experience in marketing, financial consulting, venture capital, and real estate investments.

His purpose in founding Wealth Builders Associated, LLC. with it's four divisions, (WBA Marketing, WBA Funding, Growba and Investors Pro Active), is to create specialized entities through which he and select Associates can best apply the knowledge, skills and expertise gained from first hand experience. This enables he and his Associates to capitalize on the many exceptional opportunities that exist in today’s market.

To achieve this, he has “teamed” with Marcel Spitz (see Websites), who, in addition to being an expert in marketing .. has a thorough knowledge of today’s advanced technology... and its application.

Operating as a team, they in turn utilize the services of select Associates (Professional entities that consist of Marketing Experts, Legal Consultants, Tax & Financial Planners, Investors, Private Lenders, Clergymen, Insurance and Real Estate Experts) who control vital functions of the business world. Together they find, evaluate and develop, or acquire, businesses and, or properties, with exceptional income, or potential equity value. Theirr services are made available to clients who are interested in buying or selling for themselves, joint ventures with clients and associates...or for their own use.

This combination of experience, knowledge, and talent, working for a common cause, enables them to maximize the potential profits available in each transaction. Simultaneously, it enables them to accomplish the objectives of all parties concerned for a trully “win, win, win” situation.
