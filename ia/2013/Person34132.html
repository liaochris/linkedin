Denis Pitcher's Experience

Trading Systems Developer

Orbis Investment Mgmt Ltd

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Investment Management industry

November 2006 – April 2013 (6 years 6 months) Hamilton, Bermuda

Orbis Investment Management has a 20+ year track record of market out-performance with 20+ billion in assets under management. The Trading Department is a high risk environment focused on delivering accurate results. Its remit is to manage the execution of trades sometimes worth hundreds of millions of dollars with a target error rate of 0%. My role within trading was to find a balance of ensuring a low rate of error while delivering value in the automation of various trading processes.

Responsibilities included:
- Ownership of middle office related trading processes including FX, Loan and Fixed Income
- Ownership of the tiered-fund FX hedging recommendation system responsible for generating daily trade recommendations worth hundreds of millions of dollars.
- Ownership of the live portfolio spreadsheet used by our Portfolio Managers and Analysts
- Conducting cross-team training on new technologies (WPF)
- Advancing the architecture of Trading's in-house Order Management System to make it more modular, testable and reusable.
- Ongoing systems support and maintenance for other asset classes
- Coordination of a cross-team Architecture Discussion group reviewing modern design practices and their potential application within the firm.


Previous responsibilities included:

- Assisting with the coordination of recruitment efforts
- Excel and Crystal Reports based report generation
- MS Excel and Office macros and VSTO plug-ins
