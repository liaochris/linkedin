Cristina Jackson's Summary

I have my AA in Merchandise Product Development from the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising and my BA in Marketing from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. At Novica, I am the Associate Merchandiser, where I do a variety of tasks from day to day. I am responsible for the merchandising of our online store, as well as re-organization of products and product categories, product selection for partners, product listings for partners, sample reviews, and many other tasks depending on the day.

Through my current position, I have learned the importance of meeting deadlines, and I have an innate ability to backwards plan from a deadline to create tasks and intermediate deadlines to meet my goals and deadlines on time. This type of planning is key when working on multiple projects at once.
