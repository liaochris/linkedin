Gail Kraft's Summary

Gail is a business with balance expert for strategically transforming your business. Entrepreneurs and small business leaders looking for growth, direction and balance get the value of an executive coach with more than 20 years of successful execution of strategic initiatives and change management. We work together to uncover what is important to do and what is important stop doing in order to achieve success strategically, financially, professionally and personally. This is accomplished by using process oriented approaches to problem resolution with Elite Business and Six Discipline’s coaching methodologies. The goal at Take the Next Step in San Diego is to show you how your business can grow and help good leaders become great. Find out more at www.takethenxstep.com.
