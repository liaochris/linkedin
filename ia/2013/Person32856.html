Dawid Giemza's Summary

I enjoy the process of coming up with simple solution to complex communication message. And then making it work with use of color, motion, form and typography.

I’m a Designer with over a decade of experience. Nowadays I work mostly as a freelance motion designer. I work well as part of a team or independently managing and developing projects from conception to finalization.

Specialties: art direction, motion graphics, visual effects, post-production, branding and identity, DTP.
