Bill Applegate's Summary

Bill Applegate is a Senior Loan Officer with Emery Financial Group, a residential direct lender with a core focus on purchase and refinance transactions. He has 15+ years’ experience in the lending industry and has a passion for building long-term client relationships. He has worked for Fortune 500 companies all the way down to small entrepreneurial businesses.

No stranger to complex challenges, he thrives on helping his clients. He has a passion for taking on purchases that seem impossible, or convincing an underwriter to view a refinance from a different perspective.

Bill views a mortgage, more than ever, as a financial tool that must be tied into your long and short term personal financial plan, to create a debt strategy that helps in building long term wealth creation for the client and their family.

A very active member of the Orange County community, Bill participates in the Hoag Hospital Platelet Donor Program and volunteers frequently throughout his community.

Bill is looking for likeminded people to have various discussions, share referrals to build business, turn virtual connections into long term relationships and most importantly “Pay It Forward”.

To contact Bill please send him a message via LinkedIn, www.BillApplegate.com or bapplegate@emeryfinancial.com.
