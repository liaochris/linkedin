Diane Brennan's Summary

Diane Brennan is Realtor and Host of That Real Estate Show Saturdays at 3pm on That Real Estate Show.

Diane spent 20 years in broadcasting as a News Anchor and Co-host in both Canada and the U.S. She has always been an investor in real estate and spent much of her free time following market trends and dragging her friends to open houses on the weekends. She decided to turn her passion into a career and has never looked back. She continues to appear in the media in radio, television and print sharing her knowledge about the industry she loves. She also hosts her own real estate radio show in the Phoenix Metropolitan area. That Real Estate Show airs every Saturday at 3pm on 92.3 FM KTAR. Streaming at ktar.com and podcasts available 24/7 at thatrealestateshow.co (we couldn't afford the "m").

Diane concentrates on providing people with great customer service, accurate information and solid guidance. She provides critical real estate analysis to help make informed buying and selling decisions and utilizes seasoned negotiating skills to protect and position her clients.

Specialties: Diane loves help everyone buy and sell property. She specializes in Canadians and other foreign investors
