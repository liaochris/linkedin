Amy Botkin's Summary

I typically receive referrals from attorneys, insurance carriers and self-insured employers to help job seekers with disabilities or obstacles to employment return-to-work. Along with vocational consulting and direct job placement services, my business has expanded to include life care planning services.

Life care planning services help individuals who have been involved in a catastrophic accident. A plan protects assets while assuring healthcare needs are met. Attorneys and financial advisers will have another benefit to provide to their clients!
