Joe Konopacki's Summary

I’m Joe Konopacki, owner of Insight Property Services. As a local property owner and licensed inspector, I know that decisions are only as good as the information they are based on.
After spending twenty years working in property construction, remodeling, maintenance,
and management throughout the Chicagoland area, I realized the limitations of traditional home inspections and began offering consultations to ensure your remodel is done right the first time.
The dedicated team at Insight will ensure that the comfort, efficiency, safety, and air quality of your home are taken into consideration.
At Insight, we are committed to offering high value for a fair price. Before starting home improvements, let us help you identify what you can do instead of what you could have done – saving you time, money, and frustration.
