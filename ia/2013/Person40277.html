Elaine Herskowitz's Summary

More than twenty five years of experience in the EEO field, including fifteen years as a policy attorney at the headquarters of the U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission.

Services include:
- Training on harassment prevention and EEO compliance;
- Investigations of employee complaints alleging harassment or discrimination;
- Drafting of final agency decisions resolving federal employees' EEO complaints; and
- Expert witness testimony in EEO litigation.

Specialties: EEO compliance; prevention of workplace harassment, discrimination, and retaliation.

  • Article: Not Funny: Remove the Welcome Mat for Inappropriate Jokes

    • HR Magazine
    • September 2008
    Authors: Elaine Herskowitz

    Examines the risks for an employer when management tolerates offensive jokes in the workplace due to a lack of complaints.

  • Article: The Perils of Office Romance,

    • Human Resource Executive Online
    • February 2007
    Authors: Elaine Herskowitz

    Describes potential claims arising from romance between supervisors and subordinates and sets forth suggestions to address the risk of such claims.
