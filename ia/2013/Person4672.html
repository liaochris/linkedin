Alison Leigh, MFT's Projects

  • Life Tapes Project

    • April 2003 to April 2008
    Team Members: Alison Leigh, MFT, Robert Garlan

    I led and directed a five year research project that allowed over four hundred families of cancer patients to document their personal stories in regards to their experience having and living with cancer. Our results were significant and were documented and published in three journals of Medicine and Oncology. Listening to the stories, recording them and then giving the tapes to the families was one of the most rewarding jobs I've ever completed. I remained friends with so many living legacies and learned that the best things in life (meaningful and supportive, loving relationships) are free.
