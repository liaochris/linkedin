Joey Gault's Summary

I'm a marketing strategist/consultant helping entrepreneurs and business owners understand how their website works and what they need to make it better.

There are four indexes that are important when using the web to market your business: Visibility, Engagement, Presentation and Conversion. I have numerous metrics that I use to score these indexes, some qualitative and subjective but most are quantitative. I also have a number of tactics and tools I use to successfully influence these indexes to achieve the goals set in each project. My projects are varied and different, but a great deal of them focus on impacting revenue and growing sales.

A portion of my clients are entrepreneurs and business owners looking at unsatisfactory sales reports or feeling minimal impact from current marketing efforts. I also work with creative agencies looking to better understand where to best flex their creative muscle on client projects. A third segment of clients comes from Inbound Marketing agencies looking for a fresh set of eyes on their active projects.
