Jon Gelder's Summary

Nebraska, Iowa, Montana, Washington, Idaho, Alaska, Wyoming, Oregon, Colorado, Utah – I've called them all home. I'm usually outside. I'm six foot eight and played basketball for The University of Montana Western. I graduated from the Art Institute of Seattle where I lived downstairs from Alice'in'Chains. My music collection consists of everything from AC/DC to Eazy-E, Modest Mouse to Medeski Martin & Wood. I have a respectable collection of outlaw country music. I'm into cooking, snowboarding, snowshoeing, fly-fishing, camping, rock-jumping into water and taking tons of pictures. My wife Heather is a great mom and a total creative guru. She's from Idaho and she's a killer cook. Heather loves to make things with her hands for animals, babies and friends. We have an 11 year old manchild, Jobi. Seriously, he looks like he's 17. He's named after a river guide friend in Red Lodge, Montana. He loves to draw monsters, construction sites and basketball players. He can dunk on his nerf hoop. Jobi has a knack for spotting birds of prey and ice cream shops. His favorite book is "Cloudy With a Chance of Meatballs." We have three dogs – a Chesapeake named Quincy; she jumps off of 30 foot cliffs at the lake, and two Boston Terriers named Ruby and Haines. Our four cats are Loki, Maisy Mouse, Frito the Burrito and Carlisle.

I've wanted to be in advertising since I was in 8th grade. I love what I do. I know the difference between concept and execution. I know how to execute a concept. I've got a great sense of typography. I'm not a geek but I know what makes my computer run. I know all of the relevant software. I’m a student of this business and I strive to be a truly great creative director and team leader.
