Bishop James Brown's Summary

Bishop James Brown aka "I Feel God" is the founding pastor of Victory Intl Church in Fort Worth, Texas and the First Assistant Bishop of the Non-Denominational Gospel Fellowship under the leadership of Bishop Dr. Shevoyd Hamilton.

He is called "The Internet's Favorite Pastor" and is the creating founder of the 1000churches.org Network of Christian Websites. Which in addition to the flagship website, includes: NoLack.org and several others.

Additionally, Bishop Brown is a prolific writer and speaker who is in demand for his insights on cutting edge ministry and technology. His articles, commentary, sermons, audio, & video are widely distributed across the Internet.

Born in Jackson, Michigan in 1954, Bishop Brown has always known that he was a product of the prayers of his Grandmother Sarah Fletcher. She petitioned God for a grandson and God responded through her only surviving child Ellendora Martha Brown, even though she was 40 years old, unmarried and had never had any children.

Through the love and encouragement of his Godly grandmother and mother James flourished in the harsh environment of the city of his birth. A rough child Jimmy, as he was called, loved sports & was a pretty good student.
