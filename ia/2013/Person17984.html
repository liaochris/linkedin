Brian McDonald CCIM's Summary

Brian McDonald, a National Account Manager with the National Commercial Services team at First American Title, has been a real estate investor, broker, pre-licensing instructor and asset manager. For himself and his partners, Brian handled the acquisition, management and disposition of scores of investment properties. In addition, he has been involved in a wide variety of real estate projects including commercial property tax appeals, lease analysis/negotiations and construction management. With more than a decade of experience in commercial real estate, Brian understands the complexities of the business and can help his clients through their commercial real estate transactions.

Brian holds the prestigious Certified Commercial Investment Member (CCIM) designation. He also has an Executive Master’s degree in Real Estate and Construction Management from the University of Denver where he was President of the Real Estate Club. Brian was also part of the team that won the prominent NAIOP 2008 Rocky Mountain Real Estate Challenge. In addition, Brian won the 2011 NAIOP Developing Leaders Award and is actively involved in a number of professional organizations.

Brian bought his first investment property at the age of 16 and obtained his real estate broker’s license at 22 years old. While obtaining a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration/Finance, Brian continued to invest in real estate and brokered a couple hundred properties. Brian has owned and managed several dozen assets in multiple states.

As a youth, Brian earned his Eagle Scout Award with three palms and still enjoys being involved with the Boy Scout program. He also enjoys playing the guitar and drums and anything in the outdoors including hiking mountains, watching wildlife, mountain biking and sleeping in snow caves. Brian, his wife and four children live in Denver, Colorado.
