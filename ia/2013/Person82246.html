Lolita Carrico's Summary

Long before the word “mompreneur” entered our lexicon, Lolita Carrico was already blazing trails as a true mommy media pioneer as founder of Modern Mom Inc., one of the first major online presences for mothers. With the success of both Modern Mom and the GLOSS Network for Women, her latest venture, Lolita has established herself as one of the industry’s top marketers and influencers.

After eight years of guiding it to a leading source for women online, Lolita sold Modern Mom in 2009 to launch the GLOSS Network for Women, an online destination offering content and community covering motherhood (of course), fashion, beauty, fitness, cooking, technology, pop culture and more. Within only a few months since its inception, GLOSS has been featured by media outlets like Entrepreneur.com, ABC News and Redbook, and continues to see remarkable growth in readership with more than one million views projected for the month of April 2013.

A recognized voice for moms in the industry, Lolita also serves as a spokesperson and consultant for many companies including Procter & Gamble’s Bounty and Swiffer brands, Baskin-Robbins, Got Milk, Fresh & Easy Markets and others. She is a member of Nielsen’s Power Mom Advisory Council, pens a regular column for Marketing Daily’s Engage: Moms series and is a featured blogger for LA Moms Blog.

Lolita has been featured as an expert on hundreds of media outlets like Redbook, Parents, The Washington Post, ABC News and FOX News, discussing topics such as parenting, relationships, career and balancing it all.

Specialties: marketing, marketing to moms, social networking, promotions / partnerships, business development
