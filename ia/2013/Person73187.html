Katrina Fox's Summary

I teach change makers, socially conscious business owners, coaches, authors, speakers, experts and ethical entrepreneurs how to get free publicity in the media.

Along with teaching you practical techniques and strategies on the best ways to get media coverage, I also help you to install effective and empowering thinking patterns and behaviours to boost your confidence when being interviewed by reporters.

For the past 15 years I’ve carved out a successful career as a journalist, writer and editor, with my work appearing in a range of publications in Australia, the UK and US including The Sydney Morning Herald, News Limited’s The Punch and the ABC’s The Drum among many others. I’ve also written for specialist and niche publications in HR, social housing, building and construction, property, interior design, urban regeneration, unions and advertising and media.

I worked as a columnist, news reporter and features writer for SX magazine and as the launch editor of niche women's magazine Cherrie.

I’m a published author and editor of books in the areas of sex, gender and sexuality diversity; hypnosis and self-help, and founder of The Scavenger, a not-for-profit online magazine with a focus on social change.

I’ve been a small business owner for the past 12 years, as co-director of my partner's health and wellness company, for which I now look after publicity and marketing.

In August 2013 I joined The Animal Effect as associate editor. This is a digital magazine focusing on ethical leadership in the business community.

In September 2013 I launched a blog and upcoming podcast, Inspired Action Zone, on conscious living, loving and money-making, where self-growth meets business and social change. In addition to sharing my own insights, I also feature expert interviews on how to have a great life and business while making a positive difference in the world.

Specialties: Media coaching, writing, editing, public speaking, personal development.

Katrina Fox's Publications

  • Trans People in Love

    • Routledge
    • 2009
    Authors: Katrina Fox, Tracie O'Keefe

    An anthology of essays about the experience of being in love and in relationships with significant others for members of the trans community. The book informs clinicians, scholars, and trans people themselves of the beauty and complexity that trans identity brings to a romantic relationship, what skills and mindsets are needed to forge positive relationships, and demonstrates the reality that trans people in all stages of transition can create stable and loving relationships that are both physically and emotionally fulfilling.

  • Finding the Real Me: True Tales of Sex and Gender Diversity

    • Jossey Bass (a division of Wiley)
    • 2003
    Authors: Katrina Fox, Tracie O'Keefe

    An extraordinary collection of real-life stories told by a wide-range of sex and gender diverse people. These healing tales of struggle and transformation reveal just how creative, resourceful, and adventurous the individuals in this community can be and also helps to bridge the gap between ignorance and understanding. Each story reveals the author's journey to self-acceptance and join the celebration of their new life and the road they walked to be their true selves. Finding the Real Me is a landmark book that encourages us to embrace diversity, to never fear our differences, and to remain always in awe of our amazing possibilities.
