Brandon Eley's Summary

Brandon Eley is an Interactive Director, entrepreneur, author, and professional speaker. Brandon has 12 years of experience in online marketing and e-commerce from both the perspective of an e-commerce retailer and that of a consultant.

As the Interactive Director for Kelsey Advertising & Design, a small+smart™ advertising agency with a strong focus on digital media, Brandon oversees interactive projects, online marketing campaigns and information architecture.

Brandon is the co-author of "Online Marketing Inside Out," and frequently speaks about online marketing at conferences and organizations all over the country.

Brandon is the owner of 2BigFeet.com, online retailer of large men's shoes, as well as several other websites and blogs.

Specialties: Online Marketing, E-commerce, Information Architecture, Mobile Marketing, Email Marketing, Entrepreneurship

Owner

2BigFeet, LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Apparel & Fashion industry

December 1999 – Present (13 years 10 months)

2BigFeet.com is an online retailer of large size footwear for men. We have warehouse and retail store in LaGrange, Georgia and our website services over 50 countries all over the world. We have shoes from size 14 to size 20 9E.

I started 2BigFeet.com in 1999 with a business partner, and the website launched in April, 2000. In January, 2007, I bought out my business partner's equity and became the majority owner (with my wife Tracy as my new business partner).

Since becoming the majority owner, I have been focused on aggressively growing business by building up our selection and inventory, optimizing our website for better conversions, and maintaining a high level of customer service. Since January 2007, I have increased our e-commerce conversion rate by over 500%, and revenues have increased an average of 187% per year, year over year.
