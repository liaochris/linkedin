Mary H. Ruth's Summary

By working with entrepreneurs, I continue my lifelong passion for supporting leaders and innovators. I provide a broad range of assistance, with emphasis on marketing and communications.

Communications and especially all forms of writing come naturally to me. The way words are used is endlessly interesting, whatever vehicle they ride. I work with their presentation in the appropriate context and style, focusing on the impact for your specific audience.

I enjoy helping solopreneurs design their messages on all levels in ways that are friendly, efficient, and powerful.

Especially useful currently is my expertise in social media marketing, website development, SEO, and blogging.

Specialties: Executive assistance, copywriting, editing, web content with SEO, business planning, small business administration and marketing, web site creation, social media marketing strategies, consulting, and maintenance.
