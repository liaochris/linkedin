Les Lankhorst's Experience

Star & Producer

Les Lankhorst Productions

November 2010 – Present (3 years 2 months)

The Rat Pack Live! is a high energy throwback to the golden era of Las Vegas and the days when The Rat Pack ruled the strip. Featuring world famous tribute artists, you will be swept away by “Mr. Entertainment” Sammy Davis Jr, hilarious and smooth Dean Martin and of course The Voice of the Century, The Chairman of the Board –Frank Sinatra. On selected dates, the show also includes the classic comedy of our own Joey B! Don’t miss out on such classic songs as Come Fly with Me, Mr. Bojangles, That’s Amore’, Mack the Knife, the essential My Way and much, much more. Backed by a Swingin’ Live band, The Rat Pack Live! will leave you humming and snapping your fingers all the way home!

I am a Professional Singer/Actor. I do my solo Swing/Broadway show all over the world in Concert Venues, Festivals, on Cruise Ships, Private Functions, etc.
