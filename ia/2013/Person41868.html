Erica Bass' Summary

Professional & ambitious Realtor working with buyers & sellers throughout San Diego.

As a San Diego Native I've experienced living in many communities throughout the county; From the inland East County to Downtown San Diego; I know these neighborhoods inside and out! I entered Real Estate following High School in 2004 and haven't looked back since. I experienced the pre-boom, the bubble, the crash, and the aftermath(where we are today)- in what seems like just a few short years. The constant change keeps it exciting and I'm always happy to help first time buyers or sellers navigate these waters of the ever-changing market. Whether you're buying, selling, or investing- I'm eager to help! As your local area expert with a great passion for our city, I welcome the opportunity to serve all of your San Diego Real Estate needs.

I sell properties throughout Greater San Diego, however my areas of specialization include;
Central San Diego; San Carlos, Del Cerro, Mission Valley, La Mesa.
The Southbay; Otay Mesa, Chula Vista, Otay Ranch, Eastlake.
The East County; Santee, El Cajon, Lakeside, Alpine & Campo.
South; Spring Valley, Bonita, Encanto & Paradise Hills.


If you have any questions or interest in buying or selling please do not hesitate to contact me today!
