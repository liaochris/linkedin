Jennifer de la Fuente's Summary

designer / crafter / general goofball

Are you:
- A designer who loves to design websites, but doesn’t know a lick of code (or hates code)?
- Looking for someone to collaborate with to build a function-rich site with lots of content and photo galleries and all kinds of other bells and whistles?
- A fellow designer or a photographer or an artist or someone who needs to showcase a portfolio but doesn’t want to bang your head against the wall trying to keep it updated?
- Just looking for someone vivacious, enthusiastic and fun to work with?

I can help with that.

I like big, complicated builds and projects. I enjoy the challenge and enjoy solving big creative problems.

I also enjoy teaching. I worked as a tutor in the Graphic Communications department Sacramento City College for two years, and then worked in the design lab as a coordinator. I taught WordPress and design workshops and spoke at design classes in the Sacramento area.

In a previous life, I spent almost 10 years in the newspaper industry as a sports reporter, copy editor, designer, clerk, web monkey, intern supervisor, general fix-it gal and unofficial geek to non-geek translator in the newsroom.
