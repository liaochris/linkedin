David Giltner's Summary

Technical team leader with more than 15 years experience in commercial product development and technical marketing. Skilled at taking control of a project that is not clearly defined, creating a simple and focused vision, and guiding it through successful execution. Rapidly promoted to leadership roles based on strong organizational influence and the successful release of high-reliability products for a variety of applications. Broad technical background and skilled at leading multi-discipline engineering teams in both direct and matrix reporting environments.

Specialties: Commercialization of new optical technologies, converting loosely defined customer requirements into well defined and manufacturable products, effective project management tactics, implementing good business practices such as Stage/Gate Process in an efficient manner that ensures they are respected and used rather than loathed and neglected.

Strengths Finder top 5: Ideation, Communication, Learner, Focus, Restorative
