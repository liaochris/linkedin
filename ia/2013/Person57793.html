James R. Veal, RFC, Financial/Wealth Educator's Summary

James R. Veal is founder and managing member of JRV Wealth Management Group, LLC based in Philadelphia, Pa. He has been in the financial services industry since 1995. He is a Philadelphia native and an active community leader who takes pride in giving back and work tirelessly educating his clients, city and state employees, local employers, and the general public about the strategies and importance of building wealth - specifically in the African American and Latino communities. He owns and manages his financial planning and investment advisory firm to provide services to people who are often neglected, underserved, and severely affected from a shortage of competent financial planning advice. His objective is to improve the financial intelligence and illiteracy that has plagued much of society by instituting financial educational seminars, workshops, classes, and webinars - in that will help create wealth and negate financial struggles. He does most of these forums for FREE and has volunteered as a financial educator at local colleges and universities for the past 11 years.

Specialties: Financial educator, teacher, coach, and public speaker. In addition: financial planner, investment advisory/management, retirement and social security planning, life & long-term care insurance, and tax, estate and & college planning.

Designations: Series 65, RFC, Pa Life license / Previously held: Series 7 & 66
