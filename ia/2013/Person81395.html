Lisa Brunette's Summary

I got my start as an interactive storyteller in the late 90s, when I wrote text for an informal science exhibit called Cyberville. It was exciting to engage visitors with the story of information technology and to write for an audience that would interact and "play" with the exhibit. This prepared me well for the interactive writing I did for many years on the Web, and then especially for the games industry.

I'm a highly creative, passionate leader who's found just the right fit at a company pushing at the forefront of the casual gaming industry. The most exciting question for me, and the one that drives the work I do every day is, How do we tell a sophisticated story that supports the fun of playing a game?

Specialties: Interactive storytelling, video-game writing, creative copywriting, interactive exhibit text, social media, blogging, microblogging, marketing collateral, fund raising materials, Web / online content and e-mail campaigns, feature articles, corporate history, profiles, author interviews, coaching writers, advanced editing.
