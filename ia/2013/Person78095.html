Lamar Romero's Summary

Lamar Romero is a sales and management guru with 15+ years of proven and measurable success throughout a storied and varied career. Lamar started in technology sales working for Dell in 1999, selling computers to families all across America. By 2002, Lamar was ranked Top Salesperson of the Year within in the Global Division of Dell, growing his accounts over 2000% that year, which also earned him and his mom and dad a free trip to Australia. Lamar worked in many other divisions of Dell performing inside and outside roles before finally leaving Dell in 2008 to pursue his own entrepreneurial opportunities.

Lamar founded Romero Capital and Funding LLC during the "Financial Crisis of 2009", and helped small businesses with financial needs while also working as a sales consultant, helping his clients with internal sales & operational processes. While working for a small client in 2011, Lamar was asked to assist a YC Combinator company recently relocated to Austin TX. Within days of working together, Lamar was hired as the Vice President of Strategy & Development for OwnLocal, helping grow revenue 10X during tenure with the company.

It was at OwnLocal that Lamar discovered helping small start-ups was both super challenging, and super exhilarating! During a sipping session of tequila with his wife Laura, the two decided to team up and help bring a brand new tequila to the Austin market by the name of Abandonado.

Under Lamar and Laura's passionate guidance, Abandonado exploded, becoming the #1 premium selling tequila in Austin TX during the winter months of 2012. Lamar and Laura quickly discovered they had a knack for helping start up liquor business, and founded Dragon Spirits Marketing and Promotions in 2013 to pursue this passion. Utilizing their collective experiences in technology and sales and their new love for distilled spirits, Lamar and Laura savor helping small, artisanal, high quality spirits become discovered, loved, and bought in Central Texas.
