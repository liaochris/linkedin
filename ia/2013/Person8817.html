Anthony Reece's Summary

After many moons in and around the advertising, marketing, radio, tv, animation, sound, video, web radio, infomercial, toy, ringtone, voice over, gaming, and multimedia production world, I can honestly say, I've "been there done that" and then some. (phew - what the heck is this gray stuff on my head)

Truth is I've gained most of my skills from hands-on experience, and paid for a street education no amount of money can buy at any UNV. Even my degree from Bauder College can't compare.

I'm a proud life time graduate of the "school of hard knocks" in both studio partnerships, executive blood sweat and tears, along with earning a MBA in the study of risking it all :-)

I've got plenty of scars from nightmare clients, deadlines, egotistical talent and lazy employees. (Did I say low-doe prospects too?)

Time has a way of rewarding one with both wisdom, and humility. Guess you can say, I'm now a much wiser, yet humble man in my field of trade.

Yet, I wake up everyday, still excited and hopeful of what the Lord and future has in store.
