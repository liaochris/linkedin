Amy Neeser's Summary

I am a librarian whose professional and research interests include digital scholarship, information access and retrieval, research data management, and technological literacy. I am especially interested in the intersection between scholarly communication and technology in the fields of e-science and the digital humanities.

Specialties: Fluent in German and English. Strong knowledge of computer hardware and software for Windows, Linux, and Mac OSX as well as computerized databases, MS Suite, Adobe Creative Suite, CSS, HTML, wiki markup, XML, and XSLT, RSS, CONTENTdm, Drupal, Moodle, and Serena Collage.

Extensive knowledge of reference / research services and metadata / cataloging standards (LC classification, MARC, AACR2, RDA, OCLC, Dublin Core). Experience designing, editing, and preparing material for publication.
