Jim Bryson's Summary

Founder and CEO of 20/20 Research, Inc. 20/20 is a leader in online qualitative fieldwork, software, services and support operating globally. The company has physical facilities in Nashville, Charlotte and Miami.

I was a qualitative consultant with 25 years experience before retiring to focus on running the business full time. Currently serving on the Board of Directors of the Marketing Research Association.

Founder and President of The Joseph School, a planned secondary school for orphaned children in Haiti.

Currently serving on the Board of Directors for the Marketing Research Association (MRA), Baptist Standard Publishing and the Middle Tennessee Orphan Alliance.
