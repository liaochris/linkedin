Cheri Ruskus' Summary

With 25+ years as an entrepreneurial veteran and 12 years as a Business Coach, my Definite Chief Aim is to Empower Entrepreneurs To Climb New Heights This includes:

- Bringing together the entrepreneurs of the Victory Circles as we assist the small business owners to grow their businesses and become more PROFITABLE.

- Coaching Entrepreneurs to fully create the business and lifestyle of their dreams.

- To write from my heart and soul on how we each can make a significant difference in not only our businesses but in the overall quality of our lives. This includes my weekly Victory Letter which has been the basis for two books I have authored Victory One Moment at a Time - Unlocking the Power of the Master Mind and The Victory Letters - Inspiration for the Human Race.

- To see a shift with entrepreneurs to continuously look at working "on" their business with a solid business plan as opposed to always working "in" their businesses.

Receive our Free Business Success Kit by going to http://www.victorycircles.com

Specialties: Helping others to see past their perceived obstacles and to Go For It!

Helping small business around my deep passion for writing to get them to get clear and consistent with their messages via email and social media.
