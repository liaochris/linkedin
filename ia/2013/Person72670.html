Kate Martin's Summary

Kate Martin is a graduate of the College of Charleston department of Media Studies in Charleston, SC and has worked for nearly a decade in print media.

Her reports have held public officials accountable and informed the public of critical information.

She is a member of Investigative Reporters and Editors, and of the National Institute for Computer Assisted Reporting. Kate was also IRE's first Rural Computer Assisted Reporting fellow in 2011.

Kate began her career as a freelance reporter in the Newport News, VA area, and then worked full-time at the Loveland Reporter-Herald in Colorado.

She now works at the Skagit Valley Herald, where she finds time to investigate complicated issues to bring to the public eye.

Specialties:
* Open records requests
* Access, Excel and SQL
* HTML, Google maps, Fusion Tables
* Data visualization, Tableau Public
* Extensive knowledge of online resources, databases and ability to track down information
* Familiarity with multimedia: blogging, tweeting, video creation and more
* Investigative Reporters and Editors member
