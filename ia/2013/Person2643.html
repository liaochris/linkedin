Ainsley Nies' Publications

  • Liftoff: Launching Agile Teams and Projects

    • Onyx Neon Press
    • October 31, 2011
    Authors: Ainsley Nies, Diana Larsen

    As leaders of hundreds of retrospectives and as coach and project manager to Agile projects, we’ve seen that a great proportion of the issues teams wrestle with can be traced back to a lack of clarity and alignment from early days of the project. Miscommunications, unexamined assumptions, and misperceptions create a environment in which people make decisions and take actions that unintentionally work at cross-purposes and undermine overall progress.
    Such dynamics prevent people on the project from seeing the whole system, and cause rifts and resentments in the project community. Taken together, they impede progress. We wrote this book because we want to build a wider awareness of ways to intentionally launch agile projects and teams toward success.
