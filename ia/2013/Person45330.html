Forrest Dworsky's Summary

■ Forrest Dworsky is a digital media professional specializing in creating video for Television, Web, and Mobile devices. For many years, he has used his talents to create effective content for companies of all sizes. Forrest built his first computer from scratch at the age of 12 and since then he has not stopped learning about new technology. He knows technology and he knows the target market for modern videos.

Forrest has worked on everything from reality shows, music television, live shows, instructional videos, brand identity, and instructional books.

He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Media Studies from the City University of New York Queens College. His greatest academic accomplishment is completing a four year course load of 120 credits in just three years graduating college at the young age of twenty years old. While most college students were becoming experts in watching TV reruns, Forrest was already working as a professional in Television.

To earn more, you must learn more. We all continue to grow no matter what state we are in. Having said that, Forrest is an avid reader. He reads anything he can find from business, technology, media, marketing, psychology, self improvement, communication, finance, and physical health. He doesn’t keep count, but it’s somewhere over 40+ books each year.
