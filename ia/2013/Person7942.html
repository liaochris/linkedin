Angus Warren's Summary

Warren Business Consulting is a management consulting and business training company founded by Angus Warren to provide strategic services to the energy industry, primarily in the oil and gas sector.

Warren Business Consulting’s principal consultant and associates are all very experienced and work worldwide with a wide range of clients that has typically included oil and gas majors, independents, government ministries, national oil companies, technology companies, financial institutions and professional services firms.

Specialties

Strategy formulation and implementation; negotiations; joint ventures; business planning; business processes; business development; international business; training and facilitation.

Angus Warren's Experience

Owner

Warren Business Consulting - Education and Training (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Oil & Energy industry

January 2008 – Present (6 years) London, United Kingdom

Warren Business Consulting provides an extensive suite of training, coaching and facilitation services to its clients.

Our education and training programs cover the broad range of the petroleum value chain. The emphasis is on providing attendees with a deeper understanding of the business that is immediately applicable in the work place.

Programs are interactive and delivery is through formal presentation, flipchart examples, class discussion, case studies and delegate presentations.

Training is both public and in-company and always tailored to meet the needs of the client. This includes the training materials and the format of the training. The training format can range from one on one sessions all the way up to formal 5 day classroom programmes. We work with in-house training departments to design course programmes.
