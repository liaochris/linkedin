Agnes Nowaczek, PhD Ecotourism's Summary

Dr. Agnes M.K. Nowaczek is a Professor of Hospitality and Tourism at Niagara College, School of Hospitality, Tourism and Administration, in Niagara-on-the-Lake, Ontario, Canada. She is also a Freelance Ecotourism Consultant and owner of her Eco-Travel company Ecotours-by-Agnes, working with Carlson Wagonlit Travel Source in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Agnes has completed ecotourism projects in Poland, Peru, Costa Rica, and North America, and is a recipient of several research awards and grants.

Agnes is currently pursuing several projects related to her earlier work on measuring ecotourist predispositions (Ecotourist Predisposition Scale) and ecotourist ethics (Ecotourist Ethics Scale) leading to promotion and use of these instruments among the wider sectors of tourism and ecotourism such as ecolodge operators and park managers.

Throughout her academic studies interlinked with professional work activities, Agnes has acquired a wide range of expertise and is knowledgeable in ecotourism ethics, codes of conduct, product development and marketing, programme design, policy planning, visitor management, environmental planning and guidelines, and ecotourism evaluation. Agnes has served as the Associate/Editor and Reviewer of scholarly journals and books, and published in peer-reviewed journals in the area of Tourism and Ecotourism, as well as presented in several academic and professional conferences.

Should you require professional advice in the area of Sustainable Tourism, Agnes is available for consulting.
