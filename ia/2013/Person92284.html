Mike Dory's Summary

I've spent the last decade studying the ways people communicate and working to make their conversations better. I have worked with brands, agencies, and start-ups to build social applications and platforms that connect users with their friends, their devices, and the world around them.

Prior to joining kbs+, I co-founded the social technology agency Socialbomb (acquired by Refinery29 in 2013), and served as its CTO four years. In addition to my work with Socialbomb, I've developed installations, sites, and games for Unified Field and the Institute of Play.

My personal and collaborative projects have appeared in the New York Times, National Public Radio, Gothamist, BoingBoing, Gizmodo, and MAKE Magazine.

Specialties: Skilled with technologies like Python/Tornado/Django, MongoDB, Postgres, HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery, Facebook Platform/Connect, Arduino, and PHP/MySQL.

Comfortable with Photoshop, Illustrator, Max/MSP, Logic, ProTools, Final Cut Pro.
