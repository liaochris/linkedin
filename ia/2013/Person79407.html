Brooke Fritz's Summary

As the Southeast Director, Client Relations, with Optimum Outcomes, Brooke is responsible for revenue growth, client account management & retention of clients in the healthcare cash recovery arena in the Southeastern United States. She manages a ten state territory, including Georgia, Florida, The Carolinas, Tennessee, Alabama, Mississippi, Louisiana, Arkansas, and Texas. Brooke is also very involved in continuing her professional accreditation & education in the Revenue Cycle industry.

Brooke has become a senior level sales, marketing, & media professional who brings over thirteen years of experience across many industries. She still dabbles in her entrepreneurial passion as owner of a full service marketing firm based in Nashville, specializing in logo design, branding & positioning, and overall business identity strategies for small to midsized businesses - this company is known as MintMoxie.

Background: Sales/Marketing/Media Executive with broad advertising, publishing, and marketing experience. Depth of healthcare skill sets include client acquisition, client strategic planning, and sales strategy for the account resolution arena of revenue cycle for hospitals & providers across the Southeastern United States.

Direct experience with all aspects of healthcare/revenue cycle, business development, healthcare grant writing, brand positioning/strategy, real estate marketing, consumer products & merchandising, and a heavy emphasis on media/advertising/magazine publishing/website concept development.
