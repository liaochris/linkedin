Eugene Baldovino's Summary

Eugene got his start in post production at TBWA\Chiat\Day in San Francisco where he assisted editors on award winning spots for Levis and Pets.com. Then he got sidelined into journalism, where he wrote and produced television packages for TechTV's X-Play and Unscrewed with Martin Sargent. But soon enough, Eugene re-discovered his passion for post production when he lived in New York and worked for City Lights Media and MTV. After a series of life-changing events in the Big Apple, Eugene landed in Los Angeles where he got gigs on music videos for high profile artists ranging from Akon to Lady GaGa at the post house, Sunset Edit. He is also a member of the Motion Picture Editors Guild and has worked on a number of feature films encompassing children's movies such as the Dr. Dolittle series and a horror flick by the prestigious director, Fruit Chan. This wide range of post production experience has made Eugene the picture editor he is today -- fast, technical, creative, and professional in working with clients. In the last few years, Eugene has cut an action feature, award winning music videos, corporate videos for fortune 500 companies, and has a number of short films running the film festival circuit that have won awards for his directors. Eugene's greatest strength as an editor is his love for good ol' storytelling and technical mastery that allows him to easily experiment with creative ideas from directors and producers.

Specialties

video editing, post production, tv script writing, film screenplay writing
