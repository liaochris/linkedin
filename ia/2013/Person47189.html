Gary Boyer's Summary

Since 1998 I have been involved in the mortgage business and I love it! The amazing part of my job is having the opportunity every day to help people improve their lives through refinancing or achieving their dream of becoming a homeowner.

I also believe in having some fun along the way ... that's why I have a mascot: Mort, the Mortgage Monkey. Mort is my way of injecting a little fun into the process while removing some of the fear and intimidation. One of my favorite compliments from my clients is that they called me because they knew I had a sense of humor!

Specialties

My area of expertise is working with the Gay & Lesbian community - but I don't discriminate, I'm happy to work with straight people too!
