Tom & Angela Balsamo's Experience

Publishers

Coffee News

June 2006 – Present (7 years 4 months)

Coffee News is a fun, weekly publication provided FREE to restaurants, coffee shops and all types of waiting areas for patrons to enjoy. Each week you can find interesting and unusual stories, trivia, jokes, horoscopes, as well as local happenings around town.

Coffee News is an effective and affordable advertising vehicle for businesses. It is only available where potential customers are currently spending discretionary dollars. The affordability of this publication gives the advertiser the opportunity to add something to their repertoire. There is no need to take dollars from one vehicle to attempt something new. The big difference with Coffee News is that you can block out your competition. All advertisers are guaranteed exclusivity in their issue of Coffee News. Competitors are placed on a waiting list until space becomes available.

We are currently publishing editions of Coffee News in Crofton, Bowie, Glen Burnie, and Severna Park/Pasadena.
