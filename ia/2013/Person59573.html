Jason W. Anderson's Summary

I recently left the communications and marketing division at Conservation International (CI) after 10 years to join a Rare, a dynamic, get-it-done in the field conservation organization.


To know and work with me is to realize my passion, ability to build and lead teams, problem solve and innovate. Through success, crisis and even failure, you can count on me to act with composure. When all else fails, there is always my sense of humor.

I match my passion with results. Example include:

•Designing and executing integrated communications and marketing campaigns that have resulted in double digit growth in brand awareness, revenue and online membership.

•Serving as media spokesperson, securing millions of media impressions across top US and international outlets and leading crisis communications team.

•Providing communications guidance to executive team, board of directors and top donors.

•Using social media, online, email and mobile channels to inspire donors and consumers to take action.

Whether delivering the news for CNN or saving the world at CI, I have worked for only two organizations. I have been fortunate enough to find companies that have lined up with my interests and values.

The opportunity to educate, engage and empower key decisions makers and consumers alike is what drives me. I like knowing I created something which made a difference.

These values will guide my next career choice.
