Gerald Adler's Summary

Senior Interactive Advertising Specialist for Key and National Accounts at Scripps - KNS Media Group. I work with and coach managers & senior advertising executives in increasing company online revenue - while best fulfilling the digital media needs of our agency, national, and major client accounts.

We do this by not only understanding our clients' needs and goals, but also those of our clients' customers.

Strategic business development, consultative sales management, relationship development, contract negotiation, and increasing market share are strengths. I am passionate about interactive marketing and advertising - as well as working with teams to exceed the goals of our company and clients.

Primary skill sets include generating revenue, developing a sense of esprit de corps, discussing current campaign needs and evolving goals, audience-targeting objectives, success measurements, and developing strategic plans to enhance campaign success. I apply the same principle to corporate growth.

I foster the team concept, innovation, and being a corporate ambassador.
