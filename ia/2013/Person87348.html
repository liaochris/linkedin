Marna Friedman's Summary

As I began to write my books on technology and real estate, I wanted to know what "right" looks like. So I enrolled in a real estate class. Not only did I complete the class, but realized I liked the business, and am now a licensed real estate agent. While beginning my venture into real estate, I continue to work with clients on business strategy and leveraging technology to increase their productivity, engage community and grow their business. Seasoned management professional with progressive positions of responsibility. Creative in developing programs to improve employee morale and productivity. Proven ability to manage, develop and train staff to achieve results. Organized and focused, passionate business leader with extensive experience in multiple industries. Recognized as an advisor and motivator, strong communicator and mentor with exceptional business acumen.
