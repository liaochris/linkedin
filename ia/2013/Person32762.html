The CMA and the CPA are the 2 most widely recognized accounting certifications in the USA. The CMA focuses on analysis, forward financial planning and execution of business strategy while the CPA typically focuses on tax and public auditing of past financial performance. The CMA Certification covers material in the scope of an MBA in Finance; with a rigorous testing regime similar to the CPA.
