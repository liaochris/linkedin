Brian Kaufman's Summary

Brian is an independent filmmaker who has worked in a variety of fields as an Animation Director, Traditional Animator, CG Animator, Storyboard Artist, Screenwriter, Clean-up Artist, In-Betweener, Video Editor, Sound Designer, Web Designer/Coder, and Graphic Designer.

A 2010 graduate from the School of Visual Arts, Brian has a BA in Traditional Animation. During his first year he created the opening animation to the documentary "Blowing Smoke" which won the Cine Golden Eagle Award. Since then Brian animated on the Emmy Nominated series "MAD" for Cartoon Network at Bunko Studios and is currently an Animator at Titmouse Inc New York.

Brian has produced various personal films which have screened at a multitude of film festivals including Midsummer Night Toons the Fifth, The Blue Plum Animation Festival, The Garden State Film Festival, and the California International Animation Festival.

Brian is eager to continue working in the entertainment industry and gain more knowledge of the field while enlarging his portfolio of work.
