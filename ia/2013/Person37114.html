Doug Brock's Summary

I have seventeen years of electrical wholesale industry experience and am currently a member of the Chattanooga market sales team for Kendall Electric as an Automation Engineer. I develop the market for Rockwell Automation controllers and other factory automation products. I also facilitate Kendall’s Chattanooga market quality teams and continuous improvement activities. I actively seek areas both at Kendall and outside the wholesale distribution industry to develop and hone my leadership and business management skills such as the pursuit of my MBA and volunteer activities with the TNCPE. I have my hands in a number of interesting projects and want to continue to grow and lead people and to be challenged in a role that uses all of my skills and experience.

Specialties

Wholesale Distribution. Continuous Improvement activities, the use of quality tools, and application of the Baldrige criteria. Market development in factory automation, industrial automation controls and software, and industrial electrical equipment.
