Jeremy Fiance's Summary

Jeremy is a UC Berkeley junior, young entrepreneur, and lifelong learner.

His experience ranges from working with student startups, non-profits, an angel investment group, a private wealth management firm, and a film production studio.

Jeremy is studying interdisciplinary topics with a focus on the role of entrepreneurship and innovation in economic growth. He is working to unify campus entrepreneurship culture through student groups and university initiatives.

Currently, Jeremy is leading the Northern California region of Kairos Society, an international student entrepreneur community. Kairos brings together students from across the world to tackle global challenges with venture solutions.

For fun, Jeremy enjoys basketball, soccer, martial arts, lacrosse, live music, shooting/editing short films, mentorship/coaching, and brainstorming ideas.
