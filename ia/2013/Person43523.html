Even Sandvold Roland's Summary

Even Sandvold Roland is co-founder and manager of Rotor, the only specialized employer branding agency in Norway.

He is also a recognized speaker on such topics as digital media, Generation Y, recruitment and employer branding. He has recieved rave reviews for bringing his energetic talks to companies like Google Norway, TVNorge, Amnesty, SINTEF and Orkla. He’s also presented at large conferences like Personalforum, and for a crowd of 600 at "Mobile Trender".

You can get in touch with Even through LinkedIn, at even@rotor.no or by calling (+47) 934 14 820
