Diane Windingland's Summary

As both a professional speaker and as a presentation coach, I am passionate about helping people communicate effectively in ways that build bridges that connect rather than walls that separate. Organizations hire me to help their people have better, more profitable conversations. I help people turn ordinary conversations and presentations into extraordinary relationships. I help leaders develop communication skills that leave a legacy of empowering others to lead.

In my coaching practice, I provide one-on-one and small group coaching in content development and delivery effectiveness. Get confident. Get heard. Get remembered.

  • Speech Class for Teens: 28 Speech Class Lessons Plus Handouts and Forms

    • Createspace
    • July 27, 2012
    Authors: Diane Windingland

Students will take notes and fill out assignment forms in their own copies of the book.
