Lou Solomon's Summary

At Interact, our single-minded focus is to not only help you engage others at the level of trust--but to help you find your voice and release you into your giftedness.

We teach Authenticity and Influence in Business Communication: from speaking on-camera and presenting to groups--to running meetings and simply conversing. We've been working with executives and teams at Fortune 500 organizations for close to 14 years.

When we work privately with executives, we insure success in the spotlight. When we work with teams, we give them the competitive differentiation of cut-through communication, as well as an extraordinary experience in team trust and commitment to results.

Interact Studio is located off West Morehead Street in Charlotte, North Carolina, in the historic Grinnell Building. You can come to us, or we can come to you. Most of our work is done with corporate clients, but we teach open-enrollment courses as well. See our web site for the current calendar of courses, or call us at 704.374.0423. We would love to hear from you!
