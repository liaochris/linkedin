LISA HENDRIX RN, CCM's Summary

Lisa has been a Registered Nurse for 18 years. She is also a Certified Case Manager. In addition, she has been a member of the Case Management Society of America for the last 12 years. Lisa has 10 years of experience in Worker's Compensation case management. Her favorite cases are those that involve catastrophic injury. She feels she can impact the lives of those injured workers the most. Her caring nature, dedication to advocacy and resourcefulness allows her to succeed in guiding the injured worker and their family through one of the hardest times of their lives. Lisa's strongest attribute is her ability to see the "whole patient" and identify issues thay may impede their recovery. She coordinates care of these injured worker's by referring to the best providers in the area. Her philosophy is that if you get the injured worker to the best rehabilitative specialists in the acute phase of their injury, you will have the best outcome.

Lisa has worked with elderly patients in a variety of settings such as home health, hospitals, and assisted living environments. She loves to assist families in their time of need and believes that taking into account the elderly patient’s physical, psychosocial and spiritual well being are paramount to achieving their optimum level of health and quality of life.

Lisa also values education and works hard to educate others. She loves to work with other NURSES who wish to stay in nursing, but would like to "do their own thing". Lisa loves to brainstorm with nurses to figure out just what "their thing" is.

Specialties

Superb organizational skills, highly motivated, creative and assertive.
