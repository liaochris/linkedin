Dawn Bovasso's Summary

I am a content strategist with a background in library science and user experience - particularly as it applies to the ecommerce industry. I’m devoted to making digital life intuitive, useful, and beautiful.

My doctoral research focuses on the correlation between self-efficacy and willingness to adopt emerging formats of digital content.
