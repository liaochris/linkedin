Lindsay Mann's Summary

Lindsay Mann is a Director & Cinematographer. He's been shooting shorts, commercials, and music videos in New York City since 2005. His work has been shown at SXSW and the American Pavilion Emerging Filmmaker showcase at Cannes. His commercial work plays regularly on cable channels across the greater NYC area.

Lindsay has filmed Bruce Springsteen in Asbury Park, operated for Haskell Wexler, ASC on a feature, and run film on Martin Scorsese's documentary "Shine a Light" about The Rolling Stones.

Partners Pull-Start Pictures of North Haven, Maine, are a documentary outfit that produces short and long form non-fiction films. Current projects include a short film on a worm harvester from Downeast Maine, and a series of films for the Maine Farmland Trust.

Lindsay studied Film Production and Cinematography at Boston University. He lives in Brooklyn with his wife, but their hearts reside in Maine.

Specialties: Proficient shooting 35mm, 16mm, and High Definition video (including RED and Canon 5D).
