John Murray's Summary

I am offering my skills and experience in liability claims-handling and risk management to help clients save time and money, whether they are businesses, local authorities, other organisations or individuals.
I draw on experience gained over many years in local government finance including accountancy, audit, and loans, but latterly in managing all aspects of insurance resulting in significant savings for my employer by effective purchasing, sound portfolio-management and especially by reducing the number and cost of claims, leading to reductions in premiums and other internal savings.
My aim is to bring similar benefits to current and future clients.
I am also a distributor for the Utility Warehouse Warehouse, helping individuals and businesses save money and make money on electricity, gas, 'phones, broadband and mobiles.

Specialties

Public and employers liability claims, risk management, fraud.
Also saving and making money in connection with utility bills.
