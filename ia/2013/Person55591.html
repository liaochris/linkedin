Michelle Rozovics' Summary

"Many attorneys don't listen, don't respond to their clients, don't help their clients plan for the future. Many attorneys just don't care."

Rozovics Law Firm, LLC was founded in September 2006 (subsequently merging with Rozovics & Crawford LLP in September 2012) because its founder, Michelle J. Rozovics, was continually saddened when she heard complaints from people about their past experiences with the legal profession. To avoid these pitfalls, at Rozovics & Crawford LLP we base our practice upon the belief that attorneys can be caring, personable professionals while simultaneously providing zealous, quality and sophisticated legal services.

Rozovics & Crawford LLP combines the experience of a large firm lawyer with the type of client responsiveness and efficiency of legal billing that only a small firm can provide. The result, we hope, are comprehensive legal services that will help your business grow.

At Rozovics & Crawford LLP we base our practice upon the belief that attorneys can be caring, personable professionals while simultaneously providing zealous, quality and sophisticated legal services. Rozovics & Crawford LLP combines the experience of a large firm lawyer with the type of client responsiveness and efficiency of legal billing that only a small firm can provide.

Rozovics & Crawford LLP provides comprehensive legal advice to growing businesses as they expand throughout Illinois, nationally and globally. Our practice focuses on business law for small and mid-market enterprises, patent law, trademark law, copyright law, and international law. We represent Illinois, national and foreign businesses in a wide variety of business litigation matters in state and federal courts. Frequently told that they are “not like other attorneys”, our attorneys truly enjoy finding creative and efficient legal means to help their clients achieve their business objectives.

Our attorneys are licensed in state and federal courts in Illinois and Wisconsin, as well as the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office, the United Kingdom, Wales and Scotland. We have of counsel who are licensed to practice before the U.S. Court of International Trade.
