Jennifer Bishop's Summary

Jennifer’s business has trained hundreds of businesses in LinkedIn and Social Media B2B best practice.

Jennifer has 20 years of marketing and management experience with ASX organisations including being part of the “Executive Team” during the Powerdirect/AGL Merger in 2007. Her Marketing Management experience spans Assa Abloy, AGL/Powerdirect. She has also worked in data anaytics and consulting with PMP, AXA, and Fairfax.

She has a degree in Marketing and Post Graduate qualifications in Emarketing, Management and Training. She has taught Marketing at both RMIT and Deakin Universities. She has now helped hundreds of clients leverage LinkedIn for business growth, personal and corporate branding. She is the Director of CCA and writes for three Australian Marketing publications and has been published in the U.S. and Canada.

During 2011, 2012 she has regularly guest presented at conferences and for CEO’s, including the CFO forum Australasia. She was privileged to be invited by “LinkedIn” to present alongside LinkedIn’s GM, at Melbourne’s Social Media Club.

Her articles have appeared in Dynamic Business, Nine MSN Finance, Anthill, First5000, Smart Thinking, Social Media Today and Human Capital.
