Kyle Pribilski's Summary

Executive leader, entrepreneur and technology strategist providing ideation and solutions for cloud, mobile, social, full application life cycle management including optimization and modernization in the enterprise space.

Ideated, designed and sold the Human Dynamics platform, an award winning, first-of-a-kind, SaaS enterprise solution that provided enterprise mobile & social applications to drive actionable lines of sight up and downstream for all key stakeholders. Human Dynamics leverages big data and advanced analytics in a cloud environment to create the first ever 360 degree view of a person in the healthcare setting, allowing for truly personalized health. HD connected internal and external health data and all key stakeholders allowing HD to drive improved health outcomes, real-time adherence measurement, personalized health data and records at a persons finger-tips 24/7.

Experience: 15 years of enterprise software, cloud, mobile and digital sales, business development and design as well as executive leadership. Have worked directly with healthcare, insurance, financial, retail and travel and transportation industries designing solutions and delivering services with a focus on client experience.
