Annie Skinner's Summary

Originally from the Land of Salt I am now an integral member of the design and music community here in the Crossroads of America. I find identity with the words “A Stranger To This Place” because I want everything and everywhere to pique my intellectual curiosity. I am inspired by travel, movement, exploration, and new experiences, and this drives “A Stranger To This Place” through research, observation, analysis, review, consideration and study.

I want to combine communication and technology with art and business in an environment that will benefit from my vision. I aim to effectively identify and solve meaningful problems, using creativity and critical thinking by discussing relevant theories and undertaking real-world practical projects. I will invent beautiful and intelligent design concepts that engage, provoke and inspire while gaining valuable experience working collaboratively with artists, designers, writers, research experts, business strategists, and clients in our local and national communities.

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry

We are an independently owned and operated company specializing in entertainment and entertainment marketing. We book, promote and produce concerts and special events; we are designers who operate as outside consultants for other firms to identify and solve communication problems; we are marketing and promotion professionals, helping other firms meet their marketing and promotion needs; we are professional entertainers who DJ four monthly club nights each month; and we also have run a record label and hosted a weekly FM radio show.

We have operated A Squared Industries since December 2005. Clients have included the Indianapolis Motor Speedway, The Vogue Theater, LiveNation Indiana, NUVO Newsweekly, Metromix Indianapolis, Sun King Brewing Company, and Kolman Dental. We have booked and promoted over 100 concerts and events featuring national recording artists, from Death Cab For Cutie to Queens Of The Stone Age, from The Duke Spirit to Interpol.

A Squared Industries’ mission is to “help make Indianapolis a better place to live.”

I also perform regularly as a vinyl DJ with my husband/partner Andy at our monthly dance nights LET GO!, PUB LIFE, and MANIC MONDAY!, and we also are the Official DJs of the Naptown Roller Girls.
