Kevin Tams' Summary

I'm an artist with technical ability. I run my own commercial production company 'COPAcetik Productions', roles have included producing, consulting, script developing, storyboarding, directing, camera operating, editing, graphic design, VFX, music arranging and DVD manufacture. I also freelance to production houses as Director/Editor/Camera Operator, teach training courses & have experience editing live events on a NewTek TriCaster.

I like working to tight deadlines either out in the field or locked away in an edit suite, with like-minded creative folk or on my own. Editing is my passion, but I love adding to my skillset. I work fast, I work hard, I'm easy going and I'm full of enthusiasm.
