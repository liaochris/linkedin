Founder & CEO

TestNow

January 1997 – July 1999 (2 years 7 months) El Segundo, CA

TestNow was launched to make concept testing affordable to SMB's who were below the threshold of traditional market research. For example, a new web company could have 100 users explore a website and complete surveys about the experience. The service changed the industry's expectations for speed by delivering results within 24 hours rather than weeks required for a typical focus group or survey.

• Attracted and managed 70,000 registered testers
• Sold services to 30 clients from start-ups to F500's
