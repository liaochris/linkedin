Lennart Kraak's Experience

Senior Application Developer

(Zero)70 IT Services

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

January 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months) The Hague Area, Netherlands

I'm currently working on multiple applications, using the Outsystems Platform.

The main application I'm working on is the European Claim Handling application of the Van Ameyde Group, called ECHO. This application is used by the Van Ameyde Group to handle claims (traffic, travel, injury, etc.) from various European insurance companies.

For this application I developed an Extranet environment that gives Van Ameyde's clients and their intermediaries insight in the status of their claims. It gives them the possibility to download documents and to upload new document.

Another project I'm working on is a medical intake and diagnosis application. It gives a patient the possibility to fill up a medical questionnaire at home. Based on the probable diagnosis the application provides them with laboratory forms for the laboratory tests and sets an appointment date, such that the test results are known with the specialist. This way the patient's treatment can start from the first consult, saving time for both the patient and the specialist.
