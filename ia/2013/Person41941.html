Eric Barany's Summary

Pizazz Floors & Interior Design Inc. is a full service design and build company. Pizazz has been creating beautiful home designs since 1995. Our areas of expertise include custom hardwood designs (inlays, boarders, and mixed media) Hardwood finishing and refinishing with a dust free system. Carpet, Elegant Tile, Extravagant Granite, Intricate Cabinetry, Trim, Paint, Etc... Our focus is taking a blank slate (kitchen, bath, basement, etc...) and making it beautiful. We offer a turn key product for any price range. The by product of handling home renovations is being able to do the most simple or complex job (from carpet and paint in a rental home to a very elegant $100k kitchen or bath) No job is too big or too small and every job gets that same respect and commitment no mater what price range. Every job we are on will yeild 100% satisfaction. We aim to please!

Specialties

Kitchen Design & Build, Bathroom Design & Build, Hardwood Floor Installation & Refinishing, Provide Carpet & Installation, Simple/Custom Tile Design & Installation, Granite Countertops, Stack Stone Faux Or Traditional (Fireplaces, Columns, Exteriors), Interior Design (By Room Or House), Staging Homes

Eric Barany's Projects

    Team Members: Eric Barany, Claire Barany

    There’s nothing quite like the style and sophistication of hardwood floors, and there’s no better way to add value to your home. At Pizazz Floors & Interior Design, hardwood is truly our forté and we take pride each time we enhance a home with this outstanding floor covering.

    Pizazz Floors offers every style of hardwood floors available, including these popular choices:

    Unfinished woods with a wide range of species
    Pre-finished woods: brand-named solid & engineered (multi-layer, cross grain constructed hardwood)
