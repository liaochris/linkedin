Maryann Croce's Summary

Located in Norwalk, CT: I'm a woman in a man's world (auto repair shop owner) who is changing the perception of the auto repair industry one day at a time. I look forward to networking with others who have the passion and vision to grow their businesses; while learning and sharing what's working ideas, for the benefit of our customers and/or clients, employees and community.

To continually exceed customers’ expectations of the auto repair industry; I educate them on vehicle care, safety, maintenance and repair. So they can get the most value from their vehicles. This also helps businesses improve their bottom line. Through car care events, "You Auto Know" car tips, Certified Female Friendly auto service and speaking engagements, I bring the much needed professionalism to the auto repair industry and promote it as a rewarding profession / career.

Having small business startup, development and growth experience, I look forward to continued business growth in the high tech auto repair industry with a thirst for keeping current through continued training, a solid business plan and long-term business goals, while incorporating both social media and new marketing technology to meet customer needs and exceed their expectations. I’m available for speaking and community service ventures to promote the industry, educate consumers, attract stellar employees, help other small businesses, women owned businesses and my community.

Specialties: Marketing, Social Media, Small Business Growth, Business Development, Consumer Education. Customer-focused Service, Auto Repair Industry, Women Owned Business
