Ron Watson's Projects

  • Jam in a Flash

    • September 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Ron Watson, Jason Rigler, Andrea Rigler

    Jam in a Flash is a trick by trick Disc Dog Routine Building system by Pawsitive Vybe. Standard and/or signature disc dog tricks are featured on small cards. These cards are linked to online brass tacks instruction and valuable usage info via QR codes for disc dog support and sharing on the field with friends.

    Jam in a Flash is the first collection of standardized disc dog tricks anywhere and creates a vocabulary and nomenclature that makes wrapping your head around the game of disc with a dog much easier.

    A 100 page eBook is included with 50 disc dog flash cards. The eBook is a compilation of the QR code links for offline and more relaxed reading.

    The project is in Beta through late November and is being developed by 15 passionate disc doggers of all experience levels.

  • Zero to Jam - 4 Hours to Disc Dog Freestyle

    • September 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Ron Watson, Apryl Lea, Abby Cline

    Zero to Jam is a complete disc dog training and handling system, beginning to end - zero to jam.

    Ron Watson & Apryl Lea and the Pawsitive Vybe pack along with select disc dog professionals, clients and friends of Pawsitive Vybe, introduce you to a complete handling and communication system for the game of flying disc with dogs.

    Ron & Apryl are innovative performers and Disc Dog Trainers who have helped hundreds of the best disc doggers out there learn how to better play the game of disc with their dogs.

    This project is actively seeking sponsorship, production support, and distribution opportunities. Contact Ron for more information.

  • Pawsitive Vybe

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Ron Watson, Apryl Lea

    Pawsitive Vybe is a weekly half hour dog training and lifestyle web series hosted by Ron Watson & Apryl Lea, founders of Pawsitive Vybe. The show features an amazing pack of performance dogs doing everything from Frisbee to Assistance dog work.
