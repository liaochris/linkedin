Amanda Lynn David's Summary

Currently, I am an adjunct English instructor at Old Dominion University in Norfolk, VA. I graduated from ODU with a Master’s in Professional Writing. While my aspirations lie in multimedia journalism, I have a new found passion for teaching at the college level.

My dream job is one where I express myself through words and visual design. That’s why I’m not only interested in writing, but in photography and video too. My skills range from production to editing in all three mediums. I’m a one woman show.

As my portfolio shows, I’ve spent most of my freelance work covering feature stories. I like writing about people. There is a face behind every event and it’s my job as a journalist to find it and convey it to my readers in a way that intrigues them.

My passion for a job well done has served me well throughout my professional life. As a quick learner, I like to jump directly into projects and figure things out along the way. I have a passion for learning new skills as well as mastering useful techniques. This has come in handy as I’ve been teaching myself Adobe Creative Suite.

When I’m not writing, I especially like to visit new places. I’m also a lover of all things Harry Potter and currently obsessed with oolong tea.

Currently, I live in Hampton, VA, but I’m looking to relocate for the right job.
