Jason Brooks' Summary

Jason is an Information Technology professional with 20+ years experience in business analysis & project management, and a background in supply chain / logistics, consumer goods warehousing, distribution, international shipping, and Mineral exploration.
Having worked for many years in senior roles for both corporations and in the public sector, Jason has recently been pursuing his own family business interests with Lab4 Web Design: Applying a wealth of big business experience to assist small businesses with web design/hosting and related IT consulting services.
