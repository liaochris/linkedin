Graeme R. Daley's Summary

Graeme has experience working and managing a variety of planning and design projects. His early Mechanical & Industrial Engineering training, study of Architecture along with more recent study of Urban Planning have prepared him to design in several different venues. His educational experience has included concept design for parts of machines, workspaces for office and manufacturing settings, building massing and integration of outside and inside landscape and green spaces. His recent project experience has emphasized the design of sustainable urban spaces and projects.

Specialties: Graeme has a unique set of design skills that allow him to use industry leading software to take 2D site plans and transform them into highly detailed 3D computer models. These computer models can be used to render photo-realistic still renderings and animations to literally show clients what the final product will look like. Examples of this work can be seen at www.daleyrenderings.com

Graeme R. Daley's Experience

President

DALEY RENDERINGS (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Architecture & Planning industry

August 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months) Cincinnati Area

DALEY RENDERINGS is a 3D Computer Graphics Studio specializing in 3D architectural renderings and animation videos. Utilizing eight years of 3D visualization experience, Graeme R. Daley strives to help companies improve their own business, by delivering more advanced and engaging visual presentations to their customers.

For larger scale urban developments these renderings and animation videos can help developments secure investors and project funding, attain jurisdictional support, receive permit approvals and secure tenants before construction is complete. A captivating presentation can also ensure that all stakeholders are on the same page and understand exactly what type of development is being proposed.

For individual homeowners, DALEY RENDERINGS can show the customer exactly what their new or remodeled home will look like once construction is complete. This allows customers to literally “see” the final product and make changes before construction starts, reducing costly mid-construction change orders.

A graduate of the University of Cincinnati's College of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning (DAAP), Cincinnati native Graeme specializes in Urban Planning and Design. With additional studies in Engineering and Architecture, Graeme brings to each project a rich background of practical knowledge to enhance his technical expertise. Speaking to potential clients he suggests:

Imagine the possibilities… What can we visualize for YOU?
