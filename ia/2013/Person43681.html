Bradley Kerschensteiner, LMFT's Summary

Brad Kerschensteiner, LMFT maintains an integrative practice in Miami Beach that includes Executive Coaching, Business Training and psychotherapy services.

Executive Coaching & Training
Brad works with executives and businesses worldwide to improve leadership, team effectiveness and work performance strategies. His services are completely unique in that they combine both coaching and key insights from cognitive behavioral psychology.

His clients include Young Presidents Organization in the United States, South America and Africa as well as Canyon Ranch Hotel & Spa in Miami Beach, where he provides educational lectures on stress management and mind-body health.

Bradley received his Masters in Integral Counseling Psychology at the California Institute of Integral Studies in 2003. Previously, Bradley served as the Assistant Clinical Director at G & G Holistic Addiction Treatment, Inc. in North Miami Beach.

He has extensive experience practicing both individual and group psychotherapy, having worked in the addiction field in both San Francisco and Miami.

Bradley has also provided clinical trainings for mental health agencies and substance abuse treatment centers including: Orchid Women’s Recovery Center, G & G Holistic Addiction Treatment, Florida International University, Milestones in Eating Disorder Recovery Center, GEO Care, Inc., The Treatment Center and Seaside Addiction Treatment Center in Palm Beach, Florida.

Finally, Bradley is also a member of Mt. Sinai's Elite Health Practitioner Network, the American Psychological Association and a former member of Miami Business Networking International for over two years.

Leadership Development Training

Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

October 2007 – Present (6 years 3 months) Miami, FL working with Executives and Professionals around the world

OVERVIEW:
One’s leadership style is largely determined by one’s core personality style including certain strengths and weaknesses. These blind-spots often undermine one’s best efforts to change, lead and work at high performance levels. This training utilizes the Enneagram personality system, a highly effective tool for improving leadership development and work performance.

This training guides participants through the Awareness to Action™ (A2A) process that is commonly used in corporate and business environments. A2A consists of a practical three-step process that guides participants from initial insights regarding their personality into practical, everyday applications to accelerate their performance and leadership. Among the organizations and businesses that have benefited from the Enneagram are Walt Disney, Hewlett Packard, Sony, Johnson & Johnson, the Federal Reserve Bank, and even the CIA.

HOW THIS IS DIFFERENT:
Other personality systems, such as Meyers-Briggs, DISC, Predictive Index, are helpful, however they tend to be impractical and cumbersome in their application. These systems focus primarily on surface-level personality traits, therefore failing to address the core motivations and dynamics of one’s personality type which are absolutely critical to understand in order to create sustainable change.

The Enneagram is also completely unique in that it provides an actual roadmap to successfully navigate the change process, including making positive shifts in one’s behavior, thoughts and belief system. Participants not only have increased awareness of their strengths and weaknesses but are shown practical, hands-on tools that can dramatically boost their work performance and leadership.

BIO
Brad is one of the select practitioners in the U.S. who has received advanced certification as a trainer in the Awareness to Action™ process, which utilizes the Enneagram personality system for Leadership Development and improving work performance.
