Joe Mahaffey's Summary

As a thought leader in Global Content Ecology, Joe focuses on identifying innovative end-to-end global content lifecycle solutions. Hands-on experience in business strategy creation, content authoring, content management system implementation, e-learning, cloud computing, social media, user experience, and business and IT consulting has provided Joe a diverse background for providing a unique business perspective for organizations wrestling with content convergence challenges.

  A large amount of Joe’s time is spent educating clients on value of their content assets and how to leverage the current technology to yield even greater value to their organization. For the last decade Joe’s expertise has helped his clients create measureable results in the area of global content creation and reuse. Joe is also a frequent speaker on all topics around the Global Content Lifecycle.   

Fun Facts: Joe has a passion for helping young people succeed. In addition to being a founding Scoutmaster of Troop 502 in Charlotte, NC, and plays bass guitar in the Youth band at his church.

Specialties: Content creation, management, and distribution processes. Business strategy creation, content authoring, content management system implementation, translation, localization, globalization, e-learning, cloud computing, social media, user experience, mobile UX, and business and IT consulting
