Dorothy McGuinness' Summary

Research Scientist with 15+ years of clinical research lab experience. Responsible for performing immunoassays for a research group of 5-10+ investigators and for processing study cohorts of anywhere of 100 to 1000+ samples. This included keeping detailed QC records, developing and implementing new assays and methodologies, and evaluating and testing new assays systems for the organizations research needs.

Experience includes literature searches, helping write grants and manuscripts, tissue culture, working with hazardous materials. Familiar with various lab equipment including, centrifuges, pipetting stations, plate readers, gamma counters, and their associated computers and programming.

Am a quick learner and intellectually curious with strong organizational and record keeping skills. Can work both independently and with a team. Have experience with data analysis and database management and am familiar with MS Office applications and iMedidata.

Recommendation:

Ms. McGuinness is an expert in immunoassays and her work led Dr. Bremners’ laboratory to become an internationally recognized research referral lab for the quantification of male hormones. She is excels at organization, optimization and reporting of hormone assays. Ms. McGuinness is meticulous in her work and reliable in every way. She is very well organized, and kept thousands of human samples and data generated over decades in order and available to our group. Ms. McGuinness is a critical thinker and brought her years of experience to troubleshooting and validating new assays for potential use in our laboratory. She is also willing to learn new techniques and is able to function very independently in the laboratory.
Stephanie T. Page, MD, PhD
Associate Professor
University of Washington School of Medicine
