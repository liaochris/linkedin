Detlef Eobaldt's Summary

During more than 20 years of working experience in the lighting market, i learned business from the scratch. I was involved in nearly all disciplines within a company, starting from warehouse management up to the position as a general manager. I spent nearly 11 years working abroad and I am in a management position for over 13 years now. Since 1999/2000, i am solely involved in the LED lighting segment and was able to gain international reputation by launching multiple products successfully to the international market. Brands such as LagoLED, DecaLED, V:LED, FLEX LED, DURO LED and LED SYS TEC participated on my profound knowledge of the LED market and the technologies in use. I am a creative and open minded person and show great personal interest in the development of customer focussed and innovative LED products. People who know me say that I have a "never say die" mentality and that I show strong personal desire to get my objectives.

Specialties: During the past, I was involved in many projects, including architectural and entertainment LED applications. Projects I was involved in: Shakira "Moongoose Tour", Jon Bon Jovi "Bounce Tour", several cruise ships from Holland-America line, Carnival Line and Platinum Yachts, a large number of TV Shows in Germany & Portugal, several prestigious architectural projects such as "Konzerthaus Berlin", "Kaisersaal Berlin", " 7 interactive Lightsails Kärnten Austria", and many more.
