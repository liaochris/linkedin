Dr. Ruth Bar-Shalom, Integrative Medicine's Summary

Dr. Ruth Bar-Shalom ND

I am a Physician working in the Alternative, Integrative Medicine field. I have been in practice since 1988, when I first founded a clinic in Fairbanks, Alaska.

Being a licensed Naturopathic Doctor with 24 years of experience and practice, has allowed me to serve thousands of clients who suffered from a wide range of health problems such as hormonal imbalance, diabetes, heart disease, immunological and pain disorders as well as fitness challenges. I often work with patients who have not been helped by ordinary medicine or other complementary therapies or practitioners.

Each patient I work with receives my undivided attention and guidance. I carefully analyze the patient’s symptoms and daily activities to understand what the root of the problem is. After this analysis, I am able to provide a specialized diagnosis and treatment based on scientific knowledge and time proven natural therapies. Once the diagnosis is made, we can then apply natural therapies, from a wide range of disciplines , such as medical nutrition, homeopathy, biological medicine, ayurveda and other oriental medical disciplines, etc). Continual guidance on filling nutritional needs, adjusting biochemical imbalances and necessary life style adjustments, can help in the long searched recovery from chronic disease.

Recently, guided by my willingness to fill a void in health education as well as the need to dispel myths about what works and doesn't work in natural medicine, I created a website geared to the general public: www.naturalmedicalsolutions.com . It offers information about nutrition and fitness as well as resources for health and wellbeing.

Health and Medical retreats are another way in which people from all over the world can reach us and benefit from our experience.

Founder and Medical Director. Naturopathic and Integrative Medicine

Holistic Medical Center

1988 – Present (25 years) Santa Cruz, CA, USA

Premier Center for Naturopathy and Integrative Medicine in Santa Cruz, CA.
Providing natural, integrative and holistic medicine resources to patients locally, nationally and internationally.
Why are we successful? As Naturopathic doctors trained both in conventional medicine and natural therapies we have helped thousands of patients recover from chronic illness. Whether your condition is acute or chronic or you've suffered with an illness that has persisted despite the treatment you've received, take advantage of our expertise by visiting our clinic, benefiting of our phone consultations or attending a retreat. Unlike many treatments that focus only upon managing the symptoms, we use a 2 prong approach: in-depth diagnosis including both conventional and functional medical diagnosis and use of the best in natural therapies to support your body in healing by focusing on your individuality. No two people are identical, why expect the same treatment to help everyone?
