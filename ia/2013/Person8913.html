C. Anton Ames' Summary

A seasoned and innovative executive, whose strengths include strategy formulation and execution. With nearly 20 years experience in business operations and technology, Anton has a wealth of knowledge to draw from. His work has been credited with high operational improvements and strong ROI.

Anton holds a BS in Managing Information Systems and completed his MBA at the University of Missouri-Kansas City Bloch School of Management. He also serves as an Adjunct Professor in the UMKC Executive MBA program.

Specialties

Strategic Planning - Leadership - Modernization - Training & Mentoring - Process Improvement - Security - Vendor Management - System Selection & Implementation - Turnarounds.
