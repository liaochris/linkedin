Julie Walraven's Summary

As a certified professional resume writer and career marketing professional, I understand the challenge of today's job search. Your resume is no longer just a piece of paper but a living document capturing your personality and your value for the next employer. As the foundation of the job search, resume writing allows you to focus on the value you bring the employer, discern who and what you are offering in the job market, and positions you to network with decision makers with compelling job proposal.

My "Julie" process of resume writing evolved from working directly with clients in my office to delivering a virtual model with the client participating in resume writing, cover letter writing, and LinkedIn profile writing while collaborating in a LIVE session with me. I interview you, searching for the forgotten accomplishments and help you grasp the critical elements of resume writing, job search, and career marketing.

Working with me as your resume writer and career professional, you have someone in your career who cares that you succeed in your job search
