Harry Casimir's Summary

Harry Casimir is the co-founder of Atilus, LLC. (http://www.atilus.com) a web development and Internet marketing company he started in 2005 while attending Florida Gulf Coast University. Mr. Casimir is also CEO and founder of Dixivox, SA (http://www.dixivox.com) a company he co-founded after the 2010 earthquake in Haiti that provides voice and SMS portal. Dixivox is specialized in voice interactivity and SMS solutions. Prior to start his first company, Harry was the senior student web application developer at FGCU.

Mr. Casimir is a veteran of both Afghanistan and Iraq wars with multiple awards including forward deployment.


Mr. Casimir holds a Bachlor Degree in Computer Information Systems from Florida Gulf Coast University. He is originally from Haiti and is always looking for potential investments in his country of birth.
