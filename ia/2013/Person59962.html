Jay Evan Schoenfeldt's Summary

I am an L.A.-based real estate broker with nearly10 years of professional experience brokering residential real estate. When my clients sell or purchase real estate, I am committed to ensuring they have a positive experience.

I believe a real estate broker is more than a salesperson. I educate my clients, market their property, offer solutions, lend emotional support, negotiate contracts, and work well with other brokers.

Respecting this business as a profession and treating my clients/colleagues with that level of respect is important to me; throw a little fun into the mix and call it a work week.

Not only do I broker real estate, but have been investing in Los Angeles real estate since 2002. My passion is making Los Angeles’ neighborhoods more beautiful by rehabilitating one broken-down house at a time.

I strive to preserve the character and architectural integrity of older homes, while adding modern conveniences for today’s lifestyle.

In short, I prefer to restore rather than raze, create character rather than neutralize and provide buyers with a more desirable product than the competition.

Specialties:
•Believe human interaction is as important as financial transaction, and treat all clients with integrity, fairness and respect
•Maximize resale value through simple design techniques and smart capital improvements
•Negotiate fair transactions that buyers and sellers can feel confident about
•Present equitable solutions to clients’ real estate problems, and close transactions quickly to meet sensitive deadlines
•Recognize and assess the potential of residential investment properties for short- and long-term growth
•Identify LA neighborhoods displaying upward mobility

Jay Evan Schoenfeldt's Experience

Brokerage & Acquisitions

Brick & Mortar Real Estate

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Real Estate industry

January 2005 – Present (9 years) Greater Los Angeles Area

As a licensed real estate broker, I list/market Seller’s homes for sale and help Buyers purchase single-family residences and rental properties in the Los Angeles metro area.

After six years of independently acquiring and renovating distressed residential real estate, I launched Brick & Mortar Real Estate. By adding value to single-family and multiple-unit residences, I ready them for resale or for a rental portfolio.

I work efficiently, completing projects in as little as 20 days. And, on average, my renovated homes spend just seven days on the open market before they are under contract and in escrow.

SUCCESS STORIES

• Resurrected a property that was vacant 30 + years and condemned by the city with an abatement order. Replaced all systems. Made heavy cosmetic and structural repairs. Added a bath and a master suite. Reconfigured interior walls. Worked with the city to remove the Abate Order.

• Successfully turned over units under L.A. Rent Stabilization, with Grade A/B tenants, within 4 months. Repaired or replaced most systems. Made interior and exterior cosmetic repairs. Renovated kitchens, refurbished and added baths, and restored period character to make units more attractive to prospective tenants.

• Gave tired 1920s fixers the facelifts of a lifetime. Made mid-level cosmetic and system repairs on properties, with some light structural work. Added elements of design to appeal to retail homebuyers.
