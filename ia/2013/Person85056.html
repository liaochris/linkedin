I'm recruiting for a range of openings posted on indeed.com. Position titles are listed at http://www.fmindustries.com/careers.html and include engineers, quality professionals, operations, and new positions in our Phoenix plant. Please apply via Indeed or directly to careers@fmindustries.com.

Training is provided in monthly Town Hall meetings, workshops and presentations. I have also created and uploaded a database of articles, information and presentations on a variety of topics.

My largest project has been a 10-week, hour-long series of sessions for management training. I created and implemented a new Onboarding program, a Train-the-Trainer two-part training and a session on the California Supply Chain Transparency Act (human trafficking). Additional training for Preventing Sexual Harassment, Supervisory Skills, Improving Communication Skills, Effective Interviewing and soft skill training have been created and provided.

One of the benefits of in-person onsite training is that employees and supervisors from different departments mingle and learn from each other. It helps the company when team members understand each other's challenges and needs and absolutely improves communication and collaboration.

Health and Safety responsibilities are high on the list at FM Industries. I provide information and education and lead the Safety Team. We are working on raising safety awareness in the company culture, and we stress safety in the Onboarding program on Day One.
