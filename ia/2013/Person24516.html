Christopher Palacios' Summary

Look Back To Go Forward
Christopher Palacios is a visual artist, graphic designer, and design educator. He currently lives and works in Portland, Oregon.
As a creative services professional he has 10 years of experience designing as a in-house production designer and as creative visual designer for small businesses as well as other cutting edge design studios.
You can catch up with Palacios on his website, http://clearcreative.com/.
