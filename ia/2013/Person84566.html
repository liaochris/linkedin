Mandi Susman's Summary

I have a passion for helping people and sharing my knowledge. This is something I get to do every day because I created a company, Sus4Media to use my online marketing skills to help small business owners like myself get found online. Making money from home on the internet was my goal because I wanted to be home for my family. But I also wanted to make a positive contribution to the world. So I turned to the Local Internet Marketing Association and learned how to market local businesses online.

Now, three years later, I am a published author (my second book will be coming out in November) and speaker and my company provides customized internet marketing solutions for the health and wellness industry and professional services industries including compensation consultants, attorneys and technology service providers. No other marketing partner can offer my experience and knowledge, combining over 15 years in corporate America with my background in online marketing.

I have an active speaking calendar, providing workshops to small business owners and entrepreneurs teaching them how they can get their businesses found online.

We work with our clients to boost credibility and visibility online and in the marketplace to grow your business. We will develop a marketing plan that will yield as many new clients as you can handle.

Specialties: Search engine optimization, social media marketing with a focus on LinkedIn, article and blog marketing; video marketing including video email and blog distribution and mobile marketing.

    “I’m a straight shooter,” she said. “All I want
    is for him to buy my product!”

    By Sheffra A. Williams
    A Worldwide Teleconference Community

    She sat across from me with her face scrunched up, then she leaned forward.
    "I'm a straight shooter, she said. That was before I knew that her words and words like hers would be called, "Me Cycle"ideas. It was the late 1980's.

    “I don’t care about his success. All I want is for him to buy my product.”

    She had engaged me in a relationship to support her in improving her results. Pendulum in Action, which explains the cultural shift we are experiencing from a “Me” cycle to “We,” had not been written, one of its authors may have just been born.

    Then, “Your Business Salon for a Better World,” (The Salon) a free, worldwide teleconference call where CEO’s, investors, entrepreneurs gather first to share stories then do business, would not have gotten any traction.

    Today, using tools of LinkedIn, you sit in the Salon to tell about yourself and your projects, and what you need next. You get support often with on-the-spot referrals, through LinkedIn. You have opportunities to tell your stories, which brand them in our hearts. Data alone won't.

    Margaret Tom… would care!

    Margaret Tom, real estate investor and founder of Hawaii Wealth Partners, shared her story at the Salon. It's all about investing to support what is good and sustainable. You'd want to connect with her, regardless of your industry.

    How can you “sit in” as a guest? Message me on Linkedin to get an invitation with dial in info. Not connected? Send a request; I have an OPEN link.

    Sheffra A. Williams, Founder
