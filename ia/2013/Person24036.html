Christina Bruun's Summary

As of September 2012 I'm studying visual communication in Copenhagen at The Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts.

I'm currently looking for freelance jobs in the field of design.
I work in Illustrator, Photoshop & InDesign.

Background:
In 2008 I took an AP degree in Multimedia Design and Communication.
I was immediately turned on by the world of graphic design and visual communication.
In 2010 when school ended I started my own company working with design and social media.
That lead to a position as a Social Designer at Mindjumpers - a global social media agency located in Copenhagen - where I had a chance to really challenge myself and grow as a designer.
