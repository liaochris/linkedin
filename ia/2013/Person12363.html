Bob Barry, CFP®'s Summary

Transitions and periods of change present both challenges and opportunities. For many individuals and couples, these periods of transition can be watershed events, allowing for us to set new goals, assess new realities and define our direction.

At Barry Capital, we build for our client's future making certain that the centerpiece of our work together is emerging life as the clients envision it. Creating breakthrough strategies, while seeing linkage between all of your resources, that had not been seen before, let alone, maximized, is one of the hallmarks of our work.

Our preferential pricing and experience enables us to offer high-end wealth management solutions to a wide array of clients and families.

Specialties

Fee-Only Wealth Management, Fee-Only Financial Planning, Fee-Only Investment Management and Collaboration and Conversation in pursuit of financial security and prosperity.
