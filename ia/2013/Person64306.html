John 'JJ' Jakubowski's Summary

Recently resigning his global post at Ogilvy and Mather Asia, JJ holds an Academy Award, Cyber Lions, Titanium Lions, ONEShows, Emmys, CLIOs, Andys, Addy's, GrAndy's, ADC, D&AD - JJ has simply won every major award available to a creative.
He packs a combined twenty years experience in the fields of creative leadership, strategy and ward-winning
integrated campaigns. His client list speaks volumes - BMW, Coca Cola, Hewlett Packard, Nestle, Sony, McDonalds, Microsoft, Clear Channel, Dolby Labs, Xbox just to name a few.
Holding professional degrees as an Architect and a Magician, he has held rapidly progressive positions in design, branding, identity and creative strategy.
John is a published poet, illustrator, ordained Buddhist and wannabe Iron Chef. He holds numerous degrees and honors in multiple fields and uses both big and small words.

Specialties: Creative Direction, Strategy and Leadership.
