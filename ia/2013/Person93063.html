Michael Sweeney's Summary

A high energy, client-focused, real estate specialist, with a proven track record for results, I successfully managed thousands of real estate transactions. My solid reputation for excellence & innovative marketing expertise has made Comey & Shepherd City Office unique in the market. My company is the exclusive area affiliate of Christie’s Great Estates & is one of the Leading Real Estate Companies of the World. For over 20 years, I have garnered & maintained high profile clients: individual purchasers & sellers, key builders & developers, & corporate relocations. With our talented agents, marketing team, & support staff I provide an unparalleled level of service. My expertise throughout the Greater Cincinnati Area is unsurpassed & enables me to successfully promote a property for sale, find the perfect city or suburban residence or business location, & provide premier marketing development support.
