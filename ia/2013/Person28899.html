Damian Brennan's Summary

I learn quickly and prefer to be in positions that require me to have involvement in a number of areas, including logistics, sales, marketing, accounting, software development and general management.

My current role is V.P. and CIO of a privately held niche steel plate manufacturing and trading company. We're lean and agile, and focus on providing a level of service that larger industry players cannot hope to match. We have grown from 5 people when I joined in late 2005 to over 150 in just 3 years.

As a former Partner in IMA Consulting, a boutique consulting company servicing small and medium-sized Japan-oriented businesses, I ensured that we provided our services in a manner that is both profitable to our company, and exceeded client expectations. During my time with IMA Consulting, we introduced a cloud-based HR Management tool that allowed clients to control HR activities throughout the organization. We more than tripled sales and our client base, while introducing additional new products. The bottom line result was increased profitability and strong organic growth.

Specialties

Supply Chain systems, accounting and inventory management systems, change management, staff recruitment and development, process design and defining metrics, HRIS (Human Resources Information Systems), Performance Management Consulting, HR Consulting, Project Management, Web Application Development, extensive experience with Japanese companies operating in the United States, Database Design, Management of Technical Staff, Management of Third Party Software Developers, Non-Profit Fundraising.
