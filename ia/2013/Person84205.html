Caroline "Carrie" Hochstrasser's Summary

"Good design is a renaissance attitude that combines technology, cognitive science, human need, and beauty to produce something that the world didn’t know it was missing."
- Paola Antonelli

I am a talented and devoted Graphic Designer and Art Director who thrives in a creative and energetic atmosphere. I have a growing interest in copyright and trademark matters, and a career focus on print, packaging, and branding.
