    Team Members: Michael Bhrad, EDGAR POURESHAGH, beneta betgovargezgogtapeh, Eva Poureshagh, MBA

    Created a workable SEO strategy; client was very concerned with content and ad copy as it related to aggressive optimization. We worked with them to find a healthy balance of organic guidelines and practices to produce efficient inbound traffic, vs a modernized, energetic, and friendly website framework and experience for the prospective client.

    320 currently enjoys a strong page 1 presence on Google for approximately 50 different keyword phrases, while producing a 3/4 new vs. returning user base. As we harness and further develop the optimization, we plan on seeing the inbound traffic and viewership to continue increasing.

  • ACRC Encino

    • April 2011 to June 2011
    Team Members: Michael Bhrad, Elie Haddad

    A young, energetic, and growing practice, Advanced Chiropractic Rehab Center of Encino, California, needed a web presence to capture their excellent service, while providing convincing elements of that excellent service as it was experienced.

    Client concerns were to establish a strong presence presence on Google for a primary term that was fairly competitive for a just established Chiro clinic, in an area with over 60 other competitive Chiro clinics.

    Ensight was able to establish a strong user experience, guiding the prospective patient through ACRC's gamut of services. All while allowing some piece of mind and trustworthiness in past patient experiences.

    We were able to successfully establish this while also ranking ACRC for a combined 90 + page 1 terms between Google and Bing.

    As of June 2012 ACRC ranks #2 for the phrase Encino Chiro and #3 for the phrase Encino Chiropractic ... although not ultra-competitive phrases, ACRC has done almost 0 upkeep in the last 12 months to their SEO, and because of the strong organic guidelines followed, has actually experienced a strong hold of position and increase of position over competitors.

    It has been a great experience bringing a clinic that treats NBA All-Stars, to a prominent position of visibility within the world-wide-web.
