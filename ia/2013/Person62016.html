    Team Members: Jennifer West

    I am looking for volunteers to serve on a committee to help organize and promote the 1st Annual 5K Run/1 Mile Fun Run with proceeds to benefit the Marine Corps League in Hattiesburg. The event will be held on Saturday, September 14 at The Gateway at Longleaf Trace at USM in Hattiesburg.

    The Marine Corps Leaague awards scholarships, assists local recruiters in preparing new recruits for basic training, promotes membership incentives, provides assistance through programs for hospitalized Veterans and participates in patriotic functions in the community including Honor Guard and Flag Ceremonies for Marine deaths and burials.
