Katherine Billings Palmer's Summary

I have been a professional writer for nearly 20 years at ETI in Livonia, Michigan, where I proofread and edit technical engineering textbooks, workbooks, and training software. I also write web content, design ad materials, and produce the company blog, marketing emails and newsletter.

In 2002, I started Palmerworks Web and Print Design. My husband, Richard Palmer, and I work closely with clients designing websites and writing web content. We also utilize a variety of marketing strategies to assist each small business client in establishing a viable internet identity.

Palmerworks also provides print design services, including business cards, brochures, menus, and newsletters.

The company's mission is to provide highly personalized web and print service to every client.

Specialties: Palmerworks specializes in personalized web design for individuals and small businesses. We provide affordable, professional sites, including domain name, hosting, and SEO services. Our team of award winning writers can provide your web content, tailor-made to your company's goals - all for less than $500. A one-page site package is also available.

Katherine Billings Palmer's Projects

  • GD&T Trainer: Fundamentals 2009

    • 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Katherine Billings Palmer, Brandon Billings, Mark Ramsey

    The GD&T Trainer: Fundamentals 2009 is a comprehensive computer-based training program that teaches the fundamentals of geometric dimensioning and tolerancing based on the ASME Y14.5-2009 Standard. This fully interactive, narrated course contains 29 lessons that teach the symbols, modifiers, rules, and concepts of GD&T, with quizzes that reinforce the material and allow students to gauge their level of understanding. The course is recommended for all individuals who create or interpret engineering drawings.

    The GD&T Trainer: Fundamentals 2009 is available in LAN, corporate, multi-user and single-user options.
