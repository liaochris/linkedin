Jamie Riddell's Summary

I am a digital marketing veteran now focusing on the converging businesses of search and social.

I work to deliver profitable search and social strategies calling upon 15+ years at the forefront of digital marketing campaigns.

I have created BirdSong: Social Media Reconnaissance to help brands and their agencies improve social media strategies. With on demand access to reports for any brand or their competitors, BirdSong is helping companies improve by understanding more about their social media presence, a key step before engagement and sentiment.

I work on consultation projects, helping future proof brand strategies through strategy insight and training. This work covers all forms of digital marketing, combining my experience of online marketing since 1996 and my understanding of the changing digital landscape and how it will impact brands and their marketing strategies.

I am proud to have been part of the Advisory Board helping the National Media Museum launch 'Life Online', Europe's first museum space dedicated to the Internet.

Between 1999 & 2007 I co-founded, grew and sold Cheeze Ltd. one of the UK's most successful digital marketing agencies. I now retain significant shareholding in Jaywing (formerly 2020 / Digital Marketing Group plc.)

I have seen and tried most digital marketing techniques, I invest in technology to deliver real insight and benefit.

Specialties: SEO, Social Media, Digital Marketing Strategy, Digital Direct Marketing, Social Media Strategy, Social Media Training, Digital Marketing Training, Social Media Research, Social Media Insights, Social Media Presence Metrics
