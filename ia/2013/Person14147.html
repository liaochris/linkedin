Noiseman MD's Education

20 Years of Self Study

Hiphop, Music Production

1986 – 2007

Started Pro-Mo Records and dropped Dont Fight The Flavor. With a roster of 3 acts we performed at the local showcases with the groups that came to the Bay (Naughty by Nature, Das Effex, C Funk, House of Pain, Grave Diggers, Lighter Shade of Brown, Goodie Mob, Lord Digga, Paris, Lord Finesse, Master Ace, Assassin, MC Breed, Mystic, Cisco, The Youngsters, Total Devastation, DJ Quick...).

In the 90's recorded & mixed the "Nasty Nancy" project with the help of King Tech, Muffler & Chase released on Double Dose records. After that that singed with the ILC headed by Jay King and dropped The Ruff Shit with some national promotion and release. Also dropped Noiseman Breaks & Grooves on Upstairs Records. Started Big Noise Entertainment in 96 and dropped Back to Basics with B-Flatt and Tyranny. Dropped Keepin U Open in 98, a re-mix project featuring classic hiphop MCs (EPMD, Kurupt, Big Pun, Craig Mack and Mob Deep to name a few).
