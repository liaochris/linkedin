John Bernardin's Summary

I have over fifteen years of experience in print and television advertising, direct response, outdoor, POS and interactive. My award winning work for Hyundai dramatically changed consumer perception according to a Gallup and Robinson tracking study. My theme-line "Driving is Believing" with my Winds of Change print and television campaign took Hyundai from a car company that was joked about on the Tonight Show to a serious consideration for consumers.

I enjoy approaching creative assignments from a strategic point of view. A concept I presented for Pontiac was used in many planning meetings as the example of where the company should go in 2000 and beyond. Sound strategy also helps in presentations.  My presentation to the Chicago Area Hyundai Dealer Group (sixteen groups... it was a big crowd) won back the business for Bates USA. My writer partner and I received a standing ovation.

I have worked on hundreds of shoots and have art directed large retouching and color correction projects. I was hired by Chiat/Day to art direct and manage Nissan and Infiniti's most complex consumer communication program ever attempted. It was a direct mail piece utilizing Variable Data Publishing and purls (personal urls) to customize each piece based on an individuals current model, potential migration to another model, localized dealer information and important lease expiration information. Choices made on-line by a consumer through their purl would be reflected in the next direct mail piece they would receive.

Here's a list of my client experience:
AAA Michigan, Eckard Drugs, American Greetings Cards, Giant Eagle Stores, Children's Palace, Hickory Farms, Canadian Tire, Zellers, Western Auto, Thoratec, Orange County Marine Institute, Lucky Strike/BAT International, Coca Cola, Molson Canadian, Ocean Master, Yoshimura, Polaris Victory Motorcycles and ATVs, Nissan, Infiniti, Buick, GMC, Pontiac, Hyundai and Hyundai Dealer Association of America.

Specialties

Final Cut Pro and Motion, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Quark. Experienced with Dreamweaver, Flash, ActionScript, HTML, XML and Wordpress
