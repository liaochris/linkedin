Elaine Zito, CFP, CEP, M.Ed.'s Education

University of San Diego

M.Ed., Bilingual Education

1990 – 1993

My view on language acquisition is that the brain developes in a more complete way as a result of being multilingual. The connections created in the brain, especially early on in life, can help improve thinking capacities throughout one's life.

I also designed curriculum for and taught adult business students in the evenings for over 10 years. Teaching is about sharing, and sharing is the greatest gift one can give.

These days I teach basic financial concepts. My degree in education and teaching experience prepared me well. I am grateful.

Activities and Societies: While I was completing my graduate work, I taught Spanish to English speaking elementary school students. I then entered the world of educational publishing. In 1994 a Scottsdale education firm recruited me to teach multiple subject areas via satellite TV to schools throughout the US.
