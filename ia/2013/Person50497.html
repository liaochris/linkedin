Gretchen Edwards' Summary

Gretchen Edwards develops, drives and executes strategic social media and digital initiatives targeted to the Wake Forest University community.

Previously, Gretchen served as the Coordinator for Technology Innovation and Collaboration at Wake Forest. She began her work at the institution as the Cisco Fellow. In these roles, she was sponsored by Cisco to foster the adoption and assess the effectiveness of collaborative technologies within the University community. She worked between Cisco and Wake Forest's library and IT departments.

Gretchen's professional experience and liberal arts education provide the well-rounded foundation for working with and facilitating connections among diverse constituencies across both corporate and higher education environments.
