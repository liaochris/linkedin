Josh Penzell's Summary

What's your hook? Successful training needs to be engaging, well-structured, and learner-oriented. In other words, what's in it for you? As someone with nearly 13 years experience walking the line between the worlds of entertainment and technology, I have spent my career developing and designing useful and engaging training products, services, and events that merge adult learning techniques, technology, and theatre. The result is entertaining and effective training, with extremely high learner satisfaction and performance-based results.

A certified technical trainer (CTT+/MCT), certified in both the live and virtual classroom environments, I specialize in converting boring powerpoint or demonstration-based, receptive training into highly interactive, entertaining, and successful training sessions focused not just on learning-transfer that meets basic objectives, but "passion-transfer" that engages learners in a lifetime of interest and true return on investment.

Train-the-trainer workshops I've facilitated result in more confident and effective trainers who rely less on their powerpoint slide decks, and more on the needs of the learners. An avid technologist, Webinars I've conducted on the iPad, iPhone, Office Suite, and Cloud Computing are available through various retailers (including Amazon).

I hold the CompTIA CTT+, A+, Network+, Security+, and Cloud Essentials certifications, in addition to being a Microsoft Office Specialist and an Apple Certified Pro. My training as an actor, film editor, and instructional designer, means I can provide end-to-end solutions when it comes to creating virtual and computer based training.

Specialties: Presentation skills, research-based teaching techniques, distance education, eLearning, vocal dynamics, creativity, storytelling, instructional design and delivery, Camtasia.
