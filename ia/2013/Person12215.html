Batsheva Chase's Summary

Summary
Check out www.toprecruiter.tv/season-2-episode-2 and get to know this awesome recruiter.

Hired for the TOP RECRUITER Reality series 2 years running. Nobody else can say that!

Batsheva Chase graduated from UC San Diego with a degree in Sociology. She fell into recruiting, like most recruiters do, starting at Culver Careers, the fastest growing Sales Recruiting firm on the West Coast. Within her first year she won Rookie of the Year and Builder of the Year, closing out her first 12 months in the Top 10 of the entire company. She then went on to hit the Presidents Club, as well as the Circle of Excellence, for 5 consecutive years. She was the #1 selling recruiter and #1 selling manager out of 250 recruiters nationwide.

From there, she co-founded Peak Performers Inc. Over a few years, she billed over $1 Million with a few key accounts she had developed through her cold calling efforts and stellar relationship building skills.

Batsheva returned to school in 2008 to obtain her Masters in Applied Psychology. Graduating in 2010, she had the itch to try something new. She became the Division Director at Robert Half International for the Legal Recruiting division. Within two months she was the #1 recruiter on the West Coast. She then went on to quota busting months for the next four consecutive months.

After nine months at Robert Half, Batsheva realized that her passion lies with sales recruiting. She returned to building her own business with charisma, compassion, a zest for learning, and a competitive spirit like no other.

If you are a company that is seeking Top Talent in the fields of Sales/Marketing/Management please give us a call. www.ppirecruiting.com or bchase@ppirecruiting.com.

Specialties: We recruit Top Sales Talent in every vertical including: Software, Telecommunications, Industrial, Financial, Social Media, Advertising, Social Gaming.
