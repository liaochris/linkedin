John Navin's Summary

About John Navin
A simple man who has found balance and success in
his life. Helping others discover what is important in their life...Personally, Financially and Physically. Sure you could chose any of the books or courses written by
celebrities, sports stars, doctors, or moguls, but what do they know about everyday
life? You can be sure being rich and famous won’t help you much if you are trying
to live within your budget and discover what you were called to do.
John has never been the head of a Fortune 500 company (although he worked
for one 20 years ago). He’s never cured any diseases, been in the Olympics, or
been wildly famous for much of anything. He has, however, started a successful
financial advising business, helping thousands of people organize their finances
and invest in their future. He has raised two beautiful daughters who are now
teenagers. In the past 6 years, he has lost over 60 pounds. John has run marathons,
triathlons, and in 2011, earned the prestigious title of IronMan, completing IM
Wisconsin.
His path to success was not based on an
Ivy League education, family ties, lies or a
smokescreen of charisma. His great strengths
lie in connecting with people, and providing
the possibilities for his clients to spend more
time doing what they love, living their life with
passion. Realizing it is people, their passion and
relationships that change the world.
John has the real world, real life experience
to help you through almost any trial in your life, including understanding your
faith, building relationships, having children, moving across the country, caring
for aging parents, losing weight, getting in shape, starting and building successful
businesses and defining your own goals and dreams.
He also has over 20 years of financial training and experience to round out his
ability to show you the golden path to balance in your life.

Specialties

financial planning, Individual & Corporate Workshops, wellness seminars, life coaching
