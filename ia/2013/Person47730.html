Geoff Campbell's Projects

  • 20 Tips in 20 Days for first-year students

    • June 2011 to September 2011
    Team Members: Geoff Campbell, Nadine LeBlanc, Tony Frost

    I planned, organized, and executed a video series in an effort to reach out to incoming students and decrease summer melt. The end product was 20 videos aimed at incoming students. Topics included orientation, clubs, theatre, inside information, athletics, adjustment to academics, adjustment from abroad, transition to life at the university, and study spaces. The videos have exceeded 10,000 views for an expected 750 incoming students.

In particular, this ARCUP was an opportunity to showcase The Argosy's journalist alumni with keynote speaker Michael Grange, Sportsnet Magazine, William Wolfe-Wylie, National Homepage Editor of canada.com and Dru Oja Jay, The Dominion. Mount Allison Chancellor Peter Mansbridge also Skyped in for a Q&A session.
