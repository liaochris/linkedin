Keymanna Paulas' Summary

In an industry with an infinite amount of talent and even further inundated with options for representation, Keymanna Management sets itself apart by offering the highest echeleon of professional service to a variety of reputable clients. For over a decade, Keymanna Management, founded and operated by Entertainment Manager Keymanna Paulas, has been committed to providing its talent with multi-facete
d representation, garnering mass publicity, assisting with artists' images, and offering a plethora of advice from the experiences of a seasoned industry professional.
As Chief Executive Officer, Keymanna has previously operated
under the executive wings of pioneering entertainment attorney L. Londell McMillan of The McMillan Firm and La’Verne Perry Kennedy, former VP of Urban Promotions Epic Records, also holding positions at Billboard Magazine and Epic Records. There, her ingenuity, business savvy and sponsorship acquisition skills were fine tuned and polished which enabled her to begin contributing them to organizations such as The North Star Group, a non-profit founded by Mr. McMillan, Stevie Wonder, Prince and Roberta Flack.

Providing extraordinary service to the entertainment industry for over a decade, Keymanna Management acts as a vehicle to artists, songwriters and producers, breaking barriers and taking clients to new heights in their careers.
