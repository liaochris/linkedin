Drew Massie's Summary

Experienced entrepreneurial technology and business development professional.

Lead Gen Ventures provides technology, marketing and operational consulting to lead generation and direct response businesses. We use a handful of 3rd party tools as well as our own software, including Leads2.me and Rings2.me. Leads2.me is a lead capture, lead distribution and lead buyer management software platform. Rings2.me is a call screening, call distribution and call management software platform.

Verticals: Mass Tort, Personal Injury, Home Care/Home Health, Traffic Tickets, SBA Lending and more.

Specialties: Start-Ups, Strategic Partnerships, Lead Generation, Entrepreneurship, Technology, Lead Brokering, Lead and Call Management, Lead and Call Distribution,

Technologies: HasOffers, Twilio, SalesForce, Five9, RingRevenue, CallPixels, Amazon Web Services S3, SES, Route 53, ColdFusion and more.
