Andrea Edwards Hamilton's Summary

I make websites and help people promote their business on the web. Your online visibility is about how and where people find you on the web. Whether you own your business or work for a large company I can help with ideas, training, or support that will increase your visibility.

> What I do for you:
I can be Project Manager for your website and online presence in social networks, directories, plus gather and post customer testimonials. I’ll let you know the options for staying in touch with customers. Then I can create and publish your content or train you how to get it done. I set up new websites, fix and update existing sites. I work with you on Social Media- Facebook page, LinkedIn profiles, and evaluating the other choices.

> What I know:
Any business owner, sales professional, or job seeker can have an "online presence" that can support their goals.

> My message:
Don't let competitors take your place online. You have a fountain of knowledge that people are searching for online. Do you want them to learn from your site or from someone else?

> My clients:
Real Estate, Lawyers, Insurance Agents, Construction businesses, Fitness Trainer, Chiropractor, Sales Reps, Landscapers, and you.

Specialties: Web development, Wordpress, Email marketing, Social Media, Wordpress training, ideas for marketing your business, your products, and your skills.
