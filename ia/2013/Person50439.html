Greg Tutunjian, CSM's Summary

Agile Coach, Certified Scrum Master (CSM) and Senior Technical Project Manager with more than thirteen years of experience using Scrum and applying Agile principles, practices, tools and techniques to expedite the development and delivery of technical solutions and to support the emergence and persistence of self-managing high performance teams.
