Kevin Corbett's Summary

A self-confessed "tech. geek", I’m like a squirrel drawn to anything shiny! Since my first computer--the Atari 400 in 1979, I’ve enjoyed learning, using, playing, and discovering new technologies.

I spend a lot of time looking to provide the most rich and rigorous learning experiences for student learners of all ages. Having fun AND learning--whether you’re a 16 year old high school student or a 36 year old corporate trainee, *is* possible, and the challenge I enjoy pursuing today.

Working with energizing, positive, and creative people is something that is important to me to contribute to, and be a part of. Life is short and time is valuable and I refuse to waste either.

Making connections with individuals and businesses with similar interests have been a valuable aspect of LinkedIn. Whether we work together now or in the future, I find the world continues to get smaller, so I encourage you to connect with me today!
