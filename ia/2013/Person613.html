Aaron Rutledge's Summary

Aaron is a seasoned founder with over 15 years experience in inventing, building and scaling digital platforms for businesses of all sizes. With a deep understanding of both the cognitive psychology behind digital user experience, and the technical skills to actually develop it, Aaron has helped companies bring digital innovation to life. He started building platforms in the mid 90’s in the nascent field of corporate web development with Agency.com. In 2002 Aaron and a few partners founded and built the largest e-commerce platform for the wine industry in just five years. He then returned to Madison Ave to build intelligent ad platforms for the likes of British Airways, Discovery Networks and CNN. In 2007 he co-founded the New York office of POKE, leading creative technology projects for American Express, Dell, Sephora, Johnson & Johnson, and more. In 2012 Aaron launched Valet.com with partner Josh Spear, and currently consults with early stage start-ups to help bring clarity & vision to new products in many verticals.

Aaron is actively involved in the New York City tech scene, attending meet-ups, hack-a-thons, leading workshops, and teaching classes as often as time permits.

Specialties: digital strategy, technical direction, innovation, user experience design, web development, game design, digital marketing tactics, digital audio synthesis & processing.
