Mattson Tomlin's Summary

I've worked heavily in independent filmmaking and indy filmmaking for several years. Working both on my own projects, and in independent film companies (most recently for Smuggler Films while in production of 2008s Revolutionary Road) I have seen many sides of the working world. Recently, I've started looking for musicians and companies as clients for both music videos and advertisements, as well as funding my own independent projects. I have directed three feature films, and am currently working on several small projects for SUNY Purchase. Most recently, I've begun to explore the world of advertising, and have spent the past summer shooting ads as I work on independent projects.

Specialties

Final Cut Pro editing, digital filmmaking, sound design, web authoring, advertising, film production, screenwriting, web site production, marketing, Avid editing, directing, management, casting, viral marketing
