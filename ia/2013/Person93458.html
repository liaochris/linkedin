Michelle Gower's Summary

Michelle Gower here. Most people just call me Gower. I’m known for making life a lot easier for people trying to run their businesses without losing sight of why they started it in the first place. (Learn more about me here: http://gowerpower.com/about-michelle-gower)

I work with organizations of all types and sizes to make sense of their marketing, and pride myself on being a plug-and-play gal that can step in and roll up her sleeves in just about any area.
