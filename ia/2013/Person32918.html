Dawn Mentzer's Summary

As a freelance writer, I help businesses more effectively project their value to - and better connect with - clients and prospective clients.

I write marketing, public relations and corporate communications content for online and print media. I work with marketing firms, business owners and managers in varied industries, and website developers and designers.

My areas of focus include writing website content; blog writing and editing; marketing and public relations copywriting; corporate communications writing; and social media content calendar creation.

With a background in project management and product development/management, I'm effective at facilitating efforts of multiple groups to accomplish objectives.

Specialties: Writing for blogs, websites, newsletters, press releases, and magazines; Social media coaching and content curation to facilitate audience engagement and growth; Proficient in Word Press (for blog and website content management), Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google Plus, Constant Contact (email marketing tool for newsletters), MS Publisher, MS Word, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint, MS Expression Web 3 (for website content management), & Google Docs.
