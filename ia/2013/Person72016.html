Karel Verbrugge's Summary

Since February 2011, I work at MICT as a junior researcher, with a focus both on fundamental academic research and several iMinds (former IBBT) applied research projects. Area of interest is mobile ICT user research, with a focus on behavioral patterns and habits linked to mobile media. At the moment, I am involved in two iMinds ICON projects: (1) CoMobile, a search for innovative services at the crossroads of mobile internet and online communities, and (2) SoLoMIDEM, which addresses identity management and consumer trust in a social, local & mobile context.

Specialties: Communication studies, new media research, mobile media, user research, adoption of innovations, innovation development methodology, interdisciplinary projects
