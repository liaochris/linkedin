Heather Weaver's Summary

I keep busy with my online work projects. Most of my time is spent writing in the space where technology and family life overlap. I enjoy reviewing video games and gadgets, and writing about life as a digi-mom.

I have been building web sites since 1994. I learned how to build sites the "old-fashioned" way, in plain html, before WYSIWYG software was available. I still prefer working with code over most WYSIWYG site builders, since the latter seem to add unnecessary junk behind the scenes of sites, slowing them down and clogging up the code. I'm available for website design jobs, so just send me a message!

In my offline time, I design and craft a variety of small gifts, including afghans and blankets, jewelry, and origami mobiles. Some of my pieces are carried at retail gift shops. I also donate handmade blankets and other items to the Riley Hospital for Children and Blankets of Hope for Soldiers' Angels.

Specialties

Project management, Web site design and maintenance, creative direction, content creation, WordPress blog creation, brainstorming, jewelry design, jewelry crafting
