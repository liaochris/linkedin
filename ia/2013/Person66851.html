John Parker Stewart's Summary

I am an Internationally Recognized:
• Leadership Coach
• Executive Development Specialist
• Award Winning Leadership Trainer & Speaker
• Team Performance Consultant
• Award Winning Author
• Orals Coach for Government Contractors

Over the past 35 years, I have coached and trained tens of thousands of leaders worldwide. I managed executive and leadership development for 86,000 employees at Lockheed Corporation where I designed and taught the Lockheed Executive Institutes for 13 years. I started Stewart Systems, Inc. in 1980 where I have researched, consulted, taught, and coached CEOs, presidents, executives, military and government leaders.

I began my undergraduate studies at the University of Colorado, finished my bachelor’s degree at Brigham Young University, and earned my master’s degree in Organizational Communication where I wrote my thesis at Parliament in London. I began my doctoral work and teaching at Michigan State University, and continued doctoral studies under management guru Peter Drucker in Executive Performance and Leadership at Claremont Graduate School.

I was selected “National Trainer of the Year” by the American Society for Training and Development for two consecutive years. In addition to training and coaching all levels of management at Kennedy Space Center over an eight year period, I have worked with companies including: Citibank, U.S. Air Force, Chevron, IBM, Lockheed Martin, Toshiba, CSL-Hong Kong, Xerox, GM, Kaiser Permanente, Telstra-Australia, U.S. Dept of Energy, Boeing, and many other government agencies and commercial firms.

I have researched organizations and worked with corporations worldwide and can provide you with the tools and coaching you need to be a results-driven leader.

John Parker Stewart's Experience

President and Founder

Stewart Leadership

October 1980 – Present (33 years 1 month) Portland, Oregon Area

-Asked by NASA to train and coach the team leads, supervisors, managers and executives at Kennedy Space Center over an eight year period during the 80s and 90s. This resulted in the contractor teams, receiving for the first time in KSC’s history, an award fee score of a perfect “100”. The Center Director, Jay Honeycutt, claimed this effort had saved NASA hundreds of millions of dollars. He said, “At KSC, John taught us how to manage the complex, high-risk space shuttle launch process, and to do so at almost half the cost!”

-Coached the 26 members of the senior leadership development program at Telstra, the multi-billion dollar major telecommunications company in Australia. Designed and instructed four two week sessions that taught the essentials of leading business and preparing for future career demands and responsibilities, which also resulted in the CEO requesting personalized coaching, development, and assessment for his immediate staff.

-Provided orals coaching for a large government contractor that involved a complex set of requirements and relationship issues, so challenging that the team leads were changed out four different times over 16 months. Through exceptional orals coaching, in spite of these setbacks, the team won a U.S. Navy contract worth $600 million causing the vice president to say “we had the finest orals coach in the country.”

-Designed and taught a major training program for all 1400 senior leaders of a large Silicon Valley firm on creating an environment where their employees would want to stay with that firm, while competing organizations such as Apple, Google, and Yahoo were trying to lure them away. Putting in practice the attributes of a high “Quality Manager” this firm was able to retain over 90 percent of their employees because of the new culture established by this intensive training program.
