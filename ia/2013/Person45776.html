Frank Pessia's Summary

Frank’s professional experience is a reflection of his ambitions. His work ethic and desire to create functional design solutions guides each move he makes.

While studying Graphic Design at Mercyhurst College, Frank applied his budding knowledge to Tungsten Creative Group and the Tillman Group. Combining theory with practical application gave him an edge into making graphic design work hard for businesses such as the Erie Otters, Gannon University and the Presque Isle Foundation.

True to Frank’s ambition, him and a few colleagues brought about a digital design firm. Gaining key business experience, this venture exposed Frank to the internet, which proved to be an important tool in achieving functional solutions.

A move to Baltimore, MD brought Frank to a Senior Graphic Design position at Six Red Marbles. Here, his illustration experience developed into a key skill set. Creating design solutions in print and online, Frank worked with clients such as National Geographic, Disney, McGraw-Hill and Sylvan Learning. While in Baltimore, Frank spent time serving on the board for the Associated Italian American Charities of Maryland. This organization is dedicated to charity and education, and thus was a meaningful way for Frank to give back.

A desire to be closer to family brought him to his hometown of Rochester, NY and to an Art Director position at Dixon Schwabl. Immediately hitting the ground running, Frank’s versatility allowed him to balance many clients such as Roberts Wesleyan College, Pluta Cancer Center and Tobacco Free New York. Developing integrated campaigns, website solutions and television spots was all in a day’s work.

Continuing in his professional career, Frank's self-motivations and collaborative nature allow him to continually grow his freelance business—he is always in pursuit of his next creative challenge!

Since developing his freelance business, Frank took on a creative role at Alleson Athletic to develop and maintain their corporate brand.

Specialties

Photoshop, creative concepting, illustration, brand identity, marketing campaign execution, interactive design
