Jeff Hardy's Summary

Jeff Hardy is an award winning artist, entrepreneur and creative thinker.

A Painter and Illustrator rooted in photo realism since the age of five, his talents have granted him the opportunity to sell his work professionally to numerous private collectors.

Hardy attended Syracuse University’s School of Visual and Performing Arts, Syracuse, NY graduating top of his class with a B.F.A. in Illustration.

After completing his education he headed west and established Floor 84 Studio, LLC (F84) in 2000. F84 provides creative services ranging from branding, marketing, motion/broadcast graphics to website design and development. Under Hardy’s leadership and artistic skill set, the company has found great success.

In 2008, with Hardy’s endless pursuit to be innovative and cutting edge, he created a subsidiary division, F84 Games. With F84 Games, Hardy quickly immersed himself in the video game industry while gaining respect amongst his peers, publishers and gamers the like. Development on all game platforms commenced.

With Hardy’s passion and excellence in service, F84 works as an authorized vendor for numerous major corporations including: Disney, Activision, Interscope Records, Wells Fargo and Magic Johnson Enterprises to name a few.

In addition to the fulfillment of its clients’ needs, F84 continues to create their own IP’s while developing their publishing arm and international reach.

F84’s most recent release, Survival Run with Bear Grylls, for iOS reached the Top 10 charts in the US Apple App Store as well as ranking #1 in numerous regions worldwide. Since its June, 2012, release Survival Run has achieved over 3,000,000 downloads.
