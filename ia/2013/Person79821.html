Lee Tuck's Summary

CEO and Chief Software Architect for I-Group, Inc. living and working in sunny Florida. Our team provides social B2B/B2C CRM software. We combine a social networking component to a fully functional CRM system i-linked.

What i-linked does is help you work with your team, prospects, clients and vendors in one environment. Although, we work primarily with the insurance industry including carriers, brokers, TPAs, MGAs, consultants, agents and Professional Employer Organizations, we can work with any business that wants to be more effective.

i-linked.net rips out bottlenecks in traditional business models by providing a real-time link between vendors, agents, carriers, employers and employees so they can do business in the most efficient manner possible. We strongly believe you should NEVER REPEAT THE WORK SOMEONE ELSE HAS ALREADY DONE!

As each party works together in our secure, encrypted environment, collecting and sharing information, providing fulfillment (prefilled forms, literature, contracts and other business collateral), they are supporting the efforts of one another using our Human Asset Streaming technology to enable each person they touch.

Efficiency is paramount in expansion and i-linked lets you build network and productive relationships in a common sense manner. When you simply invite and go on i-linked, you get LEAN, SAVE TIME and MONEY! Two week processes happen in two days! Two day process in two hours! We put all of your Human Assets first!

See it online at www.i-linked.net
