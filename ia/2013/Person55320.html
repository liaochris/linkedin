At the end of 2009 that contract period came to an end, and I resumed my normal freelancing work. Currently I am continuing to freelance on a regular basis in a variety of skillsets. My primary skill set is IT related stuff, as well as a variety of other secondary services that I offer.

Overall I have a total of 7+ years freelancing experience, in a variety of areas related to IT work and Misc services. I can handle just about anything pertaining to web development, application development, coding, programming, design and various other IT services (as well as some Misc services).

If there is something I am unable to do, chances are their is someone in my network who is capable of handling it.
