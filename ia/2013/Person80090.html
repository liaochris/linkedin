April Lentini's Summary

As a Web Content Strategist, I create and manage original, relevant and contextual copy that follows industry best-in-class practices.

With over 10 years experience, my work as a Web Content Writer includes writing and editing web pages and blog articles while adhering to editorial calendars with strict deadlines. I have produced and managed content for a variety of businesses -large and small and have also worked with international corporations.

I have a strong familiarity with social media, search, web analytics, ecommerce and SEO. I am very proficient at working with content management tools such as WordPress-based products as well as proprietary CMS programs.

Over the past 4 years, my role has shifted from Web Content Writer to Web Content Strategist employing a more integrated approach to web content. Working with User Experience teammates, Web Developers, Business Partners and Stakeholders, I create and manage content based on complex implementation standards.

With proven entrepreneurial skills, I am no stranger to working proactively and independently with other content professionals across the globe to help drive requirements that provide for the best possible customer experience.
