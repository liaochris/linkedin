Joe Flach's Summary

Precast Solution System, Inc. is an architectural and structural precast repair and restoration company with over 30 years combined experience. Precast Solution System, Inc. is based in Central Florida, but mobilized throughout the United States and abroad upon request.

Our services include:

• Pressure Washing
• Grinding
• Color Matching
• Sacking
• Custom Repairs
• Sand Blasting
• Patching
• Epoxy Injection
• Blockfill
