Clint Horvath's Summary

I enjoy creating art, whether it's graphic design, 3D motion graphics, video editing or even sitting on the beach sketching. I like getting together with friends and writing screenplays.

I like learning new techniques on all art forms. I recently found out that there is a name for people who are like me, which is "Renaissance Artists". This is given to an individual who not only can do all art forms but gets inspired on a daily basis to pick an art form and do it then do a different one tomorrow. They're unpredictable. I definitely have this problem.

When I'm not on the computer, I enjoy watching movies, reading pop culture philosophy/religion books and the occasional boxing class.

My goal in the art field is to be around inspiring people and create masterpieces.
