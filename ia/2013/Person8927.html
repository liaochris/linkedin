Antone Roundy's Publications

  • Riffs on a Purple Cow

    • August 30, 2012
    Authors: Antone Roundy

    The short, pithy articles that Seth Godin posts to his blog are a powerful source of
    inspiration for marketers, authors, bloggers, and many others. They suggest big ideas,
    but leave the fleshing out as an exercise for the reader.

    In Riffs on a Purple Cow, Antone Roundy picks up where Seth left off. The book is a
    collection of articles grown from the seeds Seth planted. What you'll find here is not
    "Seth Regurgitated", but a deeper exploration of ideas.

    You'll find stories that illustrate the points. And sometimes, you find Antone and Seth
    disagreeing.

    Most of the articles are about marketing, with a few other topics mixed in. Titles include:

    * How Do You Create a Mythological Brand?
    * Why It's Hard to Be Interesting and Interested
    * Fools Cash In Where Angels Fear To Tread
    * How to Find Your Own Personal Goldmine of Unique Selling Angles
    * Do Only Cheaters Prosper?
    * Doing Business in an Information-Overloaded World
    * Creating Connections Through Shared Experience
    * Does Everybody Have to Be a Social Media Expert Now?
    * Choosing Your Customers
    * Shiny Old Objects
    * Introductory Reverse-Discount Price?
    * Causal Chains and Solution Chains
    * Which is More Important: Perception or Reality?
    * A Demonstration is Worth a Thousand Words
    * One Man's Hype is Another Man's Truth
    * Mass Marketing for the Social Age
    * An Invitation to Change the World
    * ...and more.

  • Podcasting: How To Do It In 7 Simple Steps

    • January 1, 2012
    Authors: Antone Roundy

    In "Podcasting Step-by-Step", I reveal how to:

    * Come up with things to blog about every day.
    * Turn your blog posts into podcasts -- how to record, edit, and publish.
    * Make the process of going from idea to blog post to podcast as efficient as possible.

    Each step is outlined in detail, including:

    * All the tools you'll need and where to get them.
    * The entire work flow, with many screen shots.
    * Tips for getting more professional results.
