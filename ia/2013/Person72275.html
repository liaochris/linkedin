Kari Lynch - Koerper's Summary

With over 26 years of experience in the industry, Kari has become an expert in Make-Up Application and Theory. She is an outstanding artist, talented instructor and focused professional with experience as an in demand makeup artist to clients ranging from celebrities, models, Bridal groups to post surgical patents.

Kari’s scope and expertise in design, creation, application of beauty, corrective, charter, wound and burn simulation, prosthetics, make-up for photography, motion picture and television.

Trained by the industry’s top professionals, including Joe Blasco, Kari was also the Head instructor for John Casablancas Modeling Agency in Hollywood at which time she also worked as a make up artist for one of the industries top publications, The Hollywood Reporter.

Her past clientele include Actress Carol Lawrence, Actors George Burns and Ken Blanchard, MLB Players Ozzie Smith, Jim Edmonds and Mark McGuire and Author Rock A Puff.

Kari's film projects include Shorts; "Apocalypse and the Beauty Queen", "Forgettable, Packing Up", and "Troy Loan", a PSA filmed in 2011 which was awarded an EMMY for "Outstanding Work on a Public Service Announcement"

Specialties: Make Up Artist, Make Up Supervisor, Key Make Up Artist, Hair Stylist, Design, Creation, Application of Beauty, Special Effects, Corrective, Wound and Burn Simulation, Celebrities, Models, Bridal, Surgical Patients, Print, Television, Film, Motion Pictures, Photography, Make Up Instructor, Runway and Pageants, Make Up Instruction, Advanced Esthetician and Advanced Airbrush Make-up Artist, Event Planning, Catering
