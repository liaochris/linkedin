Lawson W. Brooks III's Summary

Politics and government are passions, aligning the interests and solutions of businesses with the needs of government is my mission. My clients are enterprises run by creative leaders with true business and entrepreneurial spirits.

Whether serving as an individual contributor or a completely accountable extension of staff, the end result is to initiate game changing strategies that will effectively position companies to successfully compete in the public sector marketplace.

My distinct background encompasses a range of accomplishments in both the public and private sectors. From leading the winning team on a $30 million IT contract offering to gaining access to important decision makers, to solving a complex political or business issues, I've successfully and immediately contributed to the goals set by my clients or as an employee-prenuer, my management team. Preparation, effort, and persistence yields results.

Lawson W. Brooks III's Experience

President, Business Development/Government Relations Services

L3 Public Strategies

January 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months) Washington, DC

L3 Public Strategies is a full service firm organized and designed to offer solutions and create opportunities for both public and private sector clients, specializing in the areas of business development and government relations. Using our broad base of contacts and in-depth understanding of the municipal government marketplace and the Washington, DC environment and our diverse skills and expertise, we are able to provide creative responses to the needs of our clients.

Our approach is simple – L3 Public Strategies operates as a completely accountable extension of your staff and is committed to achieving the most successful outcome possible by providing personalized quality service of lasting value. Whether it’s confidential counseling to strategically position a product, service or idea, gaining access to an important decision-maker or solving a complex political or business issue, we can help you achieve your desired results in the most productive, yet cost effective manner.
Our winning team provides our clients with the following benefits;

• The knowledge of government and how it works at all levels.
• Political experience and contacts.
• The creativity needed to develop and implement non-traditional approaches to problem solving.
• The organizational skills which allows us to utilize the most efficient approach to address an issue or idea.
