Ed Hagerty's Summary

Offers more than 20 years of strategic management, operations and technology experience representing both privately funded start-ups as well as the top international software and management consulting firms including Price Waterhouse, Oracle, Microsoft and Deloitte across 16 cities and 8 countries.

Possesses a broad range of technology and strategy skills gained through delivery roles spanning the preliminary sales cycle through to post delivery support in SaaS, shrink wrap and enterprise environments with both public and private sector customers including GE, the energy supermajors and the worlds top 20 banks.

Has extensive experience in the analysis, establishment and management of outsourced, offshore and near shore / extended development operations for both enterprise and standalone projects in multiple technical and language environments. Has additional, specialty experience in transitioning software delivery operations from start-up through to maturity phase with team sizes ranging from incubation to 200+ individuals.
