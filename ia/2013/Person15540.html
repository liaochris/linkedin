Robert Underwood CPC's Summary

Current owner and managing parnter of Underwood Recruiting, Inc. with over twenty-five years experience primarily in HR related to manufacturing and full P&L with Fortune 500 companies. Most of my background has been centered on continuous improvement and recruiting for organizational efficiencies. Our executive search firm has an established focus on talent plan development and recruiting services. With a previous background in Japanese and American culture coupling HR and Employee Development, my expertise is centered on talent assessment and recruiting at most all levels of engagement.
Multi site and corporate engagement warants a wide variety of talent strategies in recruiting regarding finance, sales and marketing, manufacturing, engineering, quality, information technology, research and development, and HR / EH&S initiativies.
We work specifically with a number of strong recruiters which specialize in each skill and many industries.

Specialties

Our core industry expertise includes furniture, metals, environmental, power, paper, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, plastics, construction, and food.
We specialize in lean competencies sourcing through manufacturing and distribution based business platforms.
Our base business utilizes both contingency and retained business structures - prefer retained for excecutive level search deliverables.
