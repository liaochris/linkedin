Dann Smit's Summary

www.caperock.tv

Cape Rock is a creative agency specialized in identity design, motion branding and content creation for leading media, sports, lifestyle and entertainment brands.

We build bridges between brands and people. Starting from clear insights and simple truths, we craft inspiring design and engaging stories for any media or screen. We work with brands, exploring opportunities to create brand signals that get you ahead in your category.

We call this ‘brand adventurous’.

We strongly believe that creative excellence delivers business results. To achieve this our organization is open, agile, independent and most importantly creative-led. Our teams are tailored to maximize the right skill set for any project, from identity design to broadcast to campaigns. This results in work that stands out and lasts.

The partners of Cape Rock are: Dann Smit, Marco-Paul de Jeu and Martijn Wolff. Individually they have worked across borders, on both agency and client side. Together they cover creative leadership, marketing strategy and production.

Specialties

Specialties: Creation of Brands, Brand Strategy & Design, Creative Concepts, Management of large scale design projects, Development of Marketing, Advertising & PR plans. International experience in event marketing and broadcast branding.

Naming, positioning, logo design, corporate design, motion design, channel flow, animation & live action direction.
