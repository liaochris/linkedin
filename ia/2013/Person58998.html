Jason Reis' Summary

Jason Reis has been a serial entrepreneur with an active mind and an enormous capacity for work; he is energized by a compelling need to “do stuff.” A skilled software architect with applications in use all over the world, Jason is also a trainer and a public speaker. He has a strong interest in teaching kids and young entrepreneurs about the art and science of starting a small business as well as programming.
