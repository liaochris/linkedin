Carol Coughlin's Summary

Carol Coughlin lives for the type of challenges that require determination, strategic brain power and the courage to sit in the hot seat, as well as venture into new opportunities. It is her passion for tough problems and growing companies that led Carol to leave her work as a CFO with large corporations and form Bottom Line Growth Strategies, Inc., an executive growth advisory company serving organizations and entrepreneurs that want to realize increased growth and profitability. BottomLine Growth Strategies has expanded, since its inception, to add COO services, Bookkeeping services to smaller companies, and Advisory Board Services to larger, privately held companies.

As a CFO advisor, Carol helps growing companies improve profits, overcome obstacles and prepare for profitable mergers and acquisitions. Her innovative spirit has led her to develop break-through processes that dramatically increase profitability.

Carol’s expertise working with companies from start- ups to $900M corporations, along with her experience across a wide range of industries and her commitment to maximizing her client’s personal wealth, has earned her a reputation as the go-to advisor when it’s time for meaningful action, increased financial stability and real change.

Carol also serves on the Board of Directors of Hamilton Bank, a publicly held Community Bank, where she also chairs the Audit Committee. A subject matter expert on financial strategies for growth oriented companies, she has appeared on Fox Business News and participated in the White House Business Council. She was also been the recipient of a number of prestigious awards, including Brava! (Smart CEO, 2012), The Daily Records’ Maryland Top 100 Women (2012), Woman in Balance (MyCity4Her, 2012). BottomLine Growth Strategies has also received the WBE designation from WBENC in 2012.
