Harvey Kart's Summary

As a niche publisher, I have published over 20 publications over the past 30 years. Currently, I'm the publisher of Western Pennsylvania Healthcare News (www.wphealthcarenews.com), Pittsburgh Healthcare Report (www.pittsburghhealthcarereport.com), and Lake Oconee Boomers (www.lakeoconeeboomers.com).

Healthcare News reports on the trends, issues, and people impacting the region’s health care industry. We reach over 40,000 professionals in print, 9,000+ through social media, and another 20,000+ monthly website visitors.

Pittsburgh Healthcare Report informs and educates the general public on health and wellness issues. You won’t find better content in any other consumer health magazine—local or national, in print or online.

Lake Oconee Boomers is a lifestyle digital publication targeted to the active baby boomers who work, live, and play in the Lake Country region of Georgia. The theme of our publication—”It’s about attitude, not age.”

The Internet continues to change our relationship with information. We spend more time reading magazines and newspapers online, whether it’s through our laptop, iPads, or other mobile devices.Through our current publications, we wanted to end static, offline reading experience and unite print, mobile and web initiatives by connecting print to the digital world. As a result, social media continues to be a critical communications tool to share articles, news, and videos with our audience.

Consider some of the statistics:

—Our website traffic has increased 45% in the past year
—Facebook likes have increased 30% since May 2013
—Twitter followers have increased by 45% since January 2013
—LinkedIn connections have increased by 25% since January 2013

For more information about our publications, email me at hdkart@aol.com or call 412-475-9063.
