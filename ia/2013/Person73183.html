Katrina Chambers' Summary

Based in Houston, TX., PRO-TENTIAL Management is a lifestyle firm that comes together with the client to cross promote ideas, resources & contacts. We offer our clientele one-on-one attention and a tailored approach, designed to promote their talents from all angles within their niche and beyond. We help our clients communicate their concepts effectively & we find creative channels to gain maximum exposure.

Pro-tential Management is a firm devoted to an exciting and diverse clientele made up of athletes, businesses, national speakers, events, and organizations. Pro-tential Management represents clients who need lifestyle and/or business management. We represent with individual attention, integrity, honesty & professionalism with a more personable approach. We want you to feel that you are just as important to us as you are to your community and respective industry. We provide prompt responses and exceptional results. To put it in perspective, we work on fulfilling the goals, dreams and objectives of our clients. The primary mission of PRO-TENTIAL Management is to provide unparalleled quality services to its clientele and provide the most aggressive business representation available. The commitment to and involvement in the client's career is 100%.
