Francisco Cámara Riess' Summary

Bilingual and bicultural communications specialist with excellent writing, editing, proofreading, translating and interpreting skills. Over 20 years of experience creating/editing/translating print and electronic content for Spanish-speaking audiences in Latin America and the U.S. Self-motivated entrepreneur with outstanding attention to detail, multitasking skills, and proven record producing high quality results under tight deadlines and within budget. Proactive team member and inspiring leader. Deep, first-hand knowledge of Latin American and U.S. cultures and idioms. Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office and other content management applications for Mac and PC.

Francisco Cámara Riess' Experience

Founder

SpanishSpeaking

Public Relations and Communications industry

2007 – Present (6 years) Winston-Salem, North Carolina

SpanishSpeaking is an effective communications firm that offers language and cultural solutions to organizations that work with Spanish-speaking clients and associates. Company has two divisions: Linguistics (translations, interpreting, editorial services) and Cultural Awareness (outreach, customized lessons, marketing support).
Ongoing projects:
~ SOCCER.COM: since 2008, SpanishSpeaking (SS) has provided editorial services to the team in
charge of producing the printed catalog and the website, translating and proofreading product
descriptions, articles, scripts, marketing collaterals and more.
~ North Carolina Department of Transportation: since 2009, SS has provided translation,
interpretation and outreach services to Limited English Proficiency populations throughout NC.
~ Compare Foods: since 2008, SS has been in charge of producing bilingual marketing materials
for this supermarket chain with a footprint from MA to SC.

Editorial Consultant

Abasto Magazine

2008 – 2010 (2 years) Winston-Salem, North Carolina

Developed the first Spanish-language trade magazine catering the Hispanic food and beverage industry in the U.S., with a nationwide circulation of 42K copies.
Managed a team of six in-house professionals and a pool of 20+ contributors around the country.
Represented company in trade events and forums to serve as a bridge between industry officials and readership.

Executive Editor

Qué Pasa Media

July 2000 – August 2007 (7 years 2 months) Winston-Salem, North Carolina

Developed news creation strategy to transform a weekly, single edition community newspaper with a circulation of 12K copies, into the largest Spanish-language publication in North Carolina, with three independent editions, 85K copies and statewide circulation, reaching 250K readers. Developed the first Spanish-language radio news department in North Carolina, serving seven stations and reaching 250K listeners.

Editor

Maya World Magazine

1998 – 2000 (2 years) Cancun, Mexico

Expanded contributors pool to improve quality of publication, with emphasis in scholars and academics from the most prestigious American institutions. Developed roadmap to launch publication online and increase readership.

City Editor

The Miami Herald Satellite Edition

1996 – 1998 (2 years) Cancun, Mexico / Miami, Florida

Developed the first locally produced Spanish-language section of the Herald's Satellite Edition project in Latin America.
