David Simard's Summary

David previously joined Marnell Companies in 2010 as Senior VP to oversee all operations of Marnell Properties. He provided the experience to identify, acquire, construct and manage assets to achieve maximum profit. David also was instrumental at Tiger Capital Management in serving as interim COO during the funds initial inception in the United States. He was responsible for structuring and monitoring the various private equity portfolios. David previously served as Division President at Storm Properties & Storm Western Development where he was responsible for all operations, acquisitions, dispositions, entitlements and construction. David has worked in corporate and real property law since 1998 and has been involved in commercial real estate since 1992. David brings with him an additional 20 years of management and project management experience. David has worked extensively with numerous Fortune 500 companies. He also served as a Real Estate Associate with the firm of Warren & Associates, PC. David received his Bachelor of Psychology degree in Industrial Psychology and his Bachelor of History degree in International Relations from the University of Windsor in Canada. He received his Juris Doctor focusing on taxation & real property from Michigan State University and is a licensed attorney.
