George Manzanilla's Summary

Los Angeles based Freelance Cinematographer / Editor / Creative Director and Digital Artist, George Manzanilla, has over 12 years experience creating motion media of all kinds for all people. Focused on the Fashion, Action Sports and Music industries.

He has been running his own company, Rundfunk Media, since 2002 and has experience in every part of the production process from concept to delivery.

While quite used to speaking about his work in all lowercase, 2013 has brought about a different mindset and he has focused a lot of energy on changing to the more accepted sentence case.
