George Csete, P.E.'s Summary

George Csete is an accomplished engineer and technical services professional with extensive experience in design, analysis and management. His background includes a unique blend of experience in engineering and information technology, spanning a wide range of industries.

Seeing a growing need for accurate capture and efficient utilization of 3D data, especially in the building industry, George founded Streambend, LLC to provide specialized support services which leverage recent advancements in laser scanning and 3D data processing technology.

Previously, as Director of Converged Networks at DecisionOne (Glodyne), in Devon, PA; George led planning, design and implementation of technology improvements, and infrastructure upgrades which greatly enhanced productivity while maintaining secure and highly available networks globally across the enterprise.

Prior to joining DecisionOne, George worked for United Engineers & Constructors (URS), Asta Engineering, The Kling Lindquist Partnership (Jacobs Engineering), and Sun Oil Company (Sunoco), on major projects for IBM, Smith Barney (Morgan Stanley), the nuclear industry (PECO), Federal Government (DOE, GSA, DOS) and the petrochemical industry (DuPont) as a Supervising Engineer and Project Manager.

As an engineer, George specialized in 3D Finite Element Analysis, design of piping/mechanical systems and Computer Aided Design (CAD). This included static and dynamic stress analysis of piping and structural supports as well as construction installation support for nuclear power and industrial facilities.

George began his career as a field engineer with Ray Consulting in northern New Jersey, performing civil engineering, piping systems surveys, and designing modifications at numerous chemical and industrial facilities.

George has a Bachelor’s Degree in Architectural Engineering and Construction Management from Penn State University and is a licensed Professional Engineer (P.E.) in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.
