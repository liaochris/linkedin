Our goal is to popularize the need for racial justice and prepare people to achieve for it. We accomplish this by providing network building, consulting, facilitation, leadership development, and training services to:

* Nonprofit organizations of all sizes
* Schools & school systems
* Intermediary organizations

A program of the Black Ministerial Alliance of Greater Boston, the Boston Capacity Tank works with our Partners, United Way of Massachusetts Bay (www.uwmb.org), Emmanuel Gospel Center (www.egc.org), and Boston TenPoint Coalition ( www.bostontenpoint.org), to provide capacity-building services to community-based organizations (CBOs) and faith-based organizations (FBOs) that work with at-risk and high-risk youth in Boston. The Tank helps FBOs and CBOs to become more sustainable and their programs to be more effective. Ultimately, the Tank’s services help youths receive needed services and access relationships with supportive adults so they can accomplish positive results in life.
