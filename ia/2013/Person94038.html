Mike Ferrara's Summary

Mike Ferrara is a Senior Associate in the Legal Management Consulting practice at Duff & Phelps. He has over 12 years of experience with information systems integration, and he specializes in SharePoint Products and Technologies. Mike is actively involved in the SharePoint community, and he is an editor for SharePointReviews.com, a respected source for SharePoint 3rd party product information. He also authors technical and business-focused articles for CMSWire.com.

Expertise:
- Extensive experience in legal DM/CMS implementations, migrations and data consolidations on the Microsoft SharePoint, Autonomy WorkSite, and OpenText eDOCS platforms
- Integration with Aderant Expert, Elite, Omega, RainMaker, ClientProfiles and other time and billing, case and matter management systems
- Expert in implementation and customization of the Autonomy WorkSite platform
- SharePoint 2003/2007/2010/2013, Office 365
- SQL administration, tuning and scripting
