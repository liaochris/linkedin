Elizabeth Glick's Experience

Chief Operating Officer

E Glick Consulting

August 2013 – Present (2 months)

As the COO of E Glick Consulting, I personally consult with real estate professionals nationwide.

As a real estate Operations Consultant, I partner with business owners and real estate agents to create strategies and implement them. I walk the talk and produce tangible results. My specialties include assessment, analysis and strategic planning; procedure development, documentation and implementation; client service, retention and referral building; and team-building, training and motivation.

With over 25 years of experience in residential & commercial real estate, I am uniquely qualified to assist real estate brokers and salespeople in identifying and reaching their goals. I understand what it takes to help agents grow their business because I've held every back office position in real estate and sold property on the front lines.

With a proven track record and the drive required to deliver real results that withstand the test of time, I've made a noteworthy positive impact. When serving individual agents and companies both large and small I set my sights on creating the right solutions for each client and I keep it simple. This enables me to help my clients, on a continued basis, to increase client satisfaction, retention, referrals and of course – revenue.

I consider personal development a lifetime hobby. I also enjoy helping other people discover and grow their own strengths, especially children.

Teaching young people their value, helping them find their voice, uplifting and inspiring people around me- these are the things I’m passionate about.
