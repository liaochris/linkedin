Andrea Stephen's Summary

For the first time in my life I found great fitness success - I used P90X to lose nearly 30 pounds. I've since added Shakeology to improve my nutrition, Insanity and Turbo Fire for even more variety to my workout. I've lowered my cholesterol 40 points and improved my overall health. I am so excited about the results I received that I want to help anyone who struggling like I was and want to get in shape, learn how to eat properly and feel better about themselves.

I have personally used P90X, Power90, Ten Minute Trainer, Insanity, Hip Hop Abs, Turbo Jam, Turbo Fire, Brazil Butt Lift, Yoga Booty Ballet, and Slim in 6 so I can provide personal feedback and assistance with these programs. I also use Shakeology, P90X Results and Recovery Formula, P90X Peak Performance Protein Bars and the Core Nutrition Pack of vitamins regularly.

I'd love to Coach you to your healthiest days! My Coaching is totally free!! I'll help you determine your fitness plan, your nutrition plan and help hold you accountable to those goals. We're addressing the two biggest needs in America: Fitness and Finances.
