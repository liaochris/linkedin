Michael Koenigs' Summary

I'm an entrepreneur, filmmaker, speaker, author, hold a patent in “Cross-Channel” Marketing Technology, and was honored with the “2009 Marketer of the Year” award.

I'm also the CEO, “Chief Disruptasaurus” and Founder of Traffic Geyser and Instant Customer. My company's products simplify marketing for tens of thousands of small businesses, authors, experts, speakers, coaches and consultants worldwide.

Some of my celebrity and bestselling author clients and friends include Paula Abdul, Tony Robbins, Peter Diamandis, Debbie Ford, John Assaraf from “The Secret”, Brian Tracy, Jorge Cruise, Dan Kennedy and Harvey MacKay.

My companies have produced nine consecutive online product launches, with the largest grossing over $9.1 million dollars. I also generated over $3 million dollars in sales during a “direct to camera” live webcast.

I executive produced “Bill’s Gun Shop”, an independent feature film that was distributed by Warner Brothers, and produced a feature documentary, “LifeWithTesla” about living gas-free with an electric car charged by the sun. I've created over 300 web sites and more than 35 products.

Originally from Minnesota, I live in San Diego, CA with my wife and son.

Specialties

Marketing consultant, business consultant, entrepreneur, ebusiness strategist, fundraiser, fundraising, brainstorming, web developer, producer, director, product developer, product development, filmaker
