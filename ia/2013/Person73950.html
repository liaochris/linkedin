Kelly Ketelboeter's Experience

Co-Founder

The Experience Factor

January 2009 – Present (5 years)

Are you asking your employees to do more with less? Is morale low around the office? Are your managers managing or coaching? Would you like to enhance internal and external service? Would you like to enhance productivity? Then coaching is your answer and The Experience Factor can help you fill in the blanks.

Our goal is to help companies and managers create a positive work experience for employees through positive and consistent coaching methods. By enhancing and capitalizing on the employee experience, companies will in turn enhance and create amazing customer experiences.

Focusing on the experience and the points of impact with both employees and customers will lead to increased retention, higher morale, increased word of mouth and wallet share along with creative employees that have a stake in your company and work to bring the mission of your company alive.

Strategic Partner

Michael Neill & Associates

2002 – 2010 (8 years)

Partnering with Credit Unions to help develop and maintain a sales and service culture. My experience ranges from client management, process implementation, staff training, management training and development and working with key executives to identify small wins and opportunities for growth for both the Credit Union and individuals.

Working with over 70 clients nationwide and in Canada, helping to introduce and implement change initiatives through effective training classes and coaching efforts.

Design, development and delivery of a wide range of participant centered training programs to include participant workbooks and leader guides. Relationship management and needs based solution cross selling with all clients. Provide on-going feedback for the transfer of training skills to the workplace to executives, managers and employees.
