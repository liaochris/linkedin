Lisa Rice's Summary

Experienced working in both radio and television as a producer, writer and on-camera talent since the age of eighteen. However, voiceover has been my career mainstay.

Your time is valuable. With twenty plus years of experience, I bring more to your project than just voice. Clients have the option of directing me live via phone-patch or ISDN yet I'm comfortable with self-direction, too. As an accredited member of the Society of Accredited Voice Over Artists, I deliver results-oriented, deadline-driven work.

Satisfied clients include companies and organizations from a wide range of business and corporate levels as well as advertising agencies, radio and television stations, non-profit groups and ministries.
