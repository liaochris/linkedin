Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

February 2011 – May 2012 (1 year 4 months) Chicago, IL

In addition, the recent launch of the Deal Wallet allows consumers to organize their purchases from across providers in a single interface.

My responsibilities in the company were to handle the strategy, execution & analysis of a comprehensive Social Media presence for DealsGoRound, as well as front end design to optimise landing pages & increase conversions.
