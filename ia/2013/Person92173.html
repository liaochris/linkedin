Michael Cawood's Summary

British Animation Director, Filmmaker, Layout & Previs Artist

Michael is a multi-award winning Animation Director with a broad skill set. He's been working in animation for over a decade in Film, Commercials, Games, Short Films and Web TV, on everything from cartoon animation to realistic visual effects. He has dedicated himself to addressing the biggest need on each project at any given time. Where possible he'll animate, but more often his time is spent supervising, mentoring, creating shot layouts and pre-viz, refining the animation pipeline, finding rigging problems and addressing story, edit, design and filmmaking issues. He has a unique strength in directing teams to create high quality visuals at low budgets with minimal resources as showcased in his award winning short film that was created by a 100 strong team without any funding.
