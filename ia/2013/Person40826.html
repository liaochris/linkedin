Ellen Hughes' Summary

I created The McKee Company in 2000. My company's motto is "We do the research so you don't have to". The McKee Company researches and publishes its findings in a logical format to save people time and money.

Some of the things I have created:
**Office Wizard, an office procedures manual template for small businesses
**Snakebit, a book that teaches people how to hire, fire and work with attorneys
**Adventures With Natural Healing, a book that explores the who, what, where, when and why of 27 lesser-known alternative health methods.

I am pleased to say my products have been reviewed in such publications as Kiplinger, Reader's Digest, Worth, and Denver Business Journal. In addition to being an author, I am a clinical hypnotherapist and trained in several other alternative health methods while working as a paralegal for 20 years. Working both sides of the brain!

Specialties
