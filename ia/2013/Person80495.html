Paal Leveraas' Summary

Paal Leveraas works with communications in HR Norge, Norway's association for HR professionals. This includes among other things media and press relations and development and execution of digital strategies.

Paal Leveraas has worked as a journalist and editor, and has more than 15 years experience from media. His experience comes from a large number of roles and activities, ranging from publishing, writing, editing and public relations, as well as starting up new companies. He is particulary proud of digi.no, one of Europe's first internet-only newspapers, which he co-founded in 1996. Since then start-ups have been one of his passions.

Privately Paal Leveraas publishes a weekly newsletter Tirsdag morgen through his website www.tirsdagmorgen.no. The site and newsletter is in Norwegian. Tirsdag morgen is mainly focused on Leadership - the art and craft of leading yourself and others.

Paal is a certified professional co-active coach (CPCC) and relationship/team coach (ORSCC).

Specialties: Media Relations, Entrepreneurship, Team Coaching, Public Relations, Communications, Leader development, Executive Coaching
