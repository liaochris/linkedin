Maria Etkind's Summary

From launching a hot new restaurant to getting new students interested in a college, my creative work crosses boundaries and languages.

I am an experienced creative director with roots as a graphic designer. I partner with corporations, small businesses and agencies to establish compelling and elegant brand and visual identity systems.

I also help organizations develop supporting marketing communications campaigns and tools in all media formats — print, web, social and advertising.

I am interested in freelance work with agencies, as well as direct work with small businesses.

I volunteer as lead gardener in the Algiers Behrman Community Garden.

Specialties: Branding and Visual Identity; Design for Print, Web & Social Media; Marketing and Marketing Communications; Printer/Vendor Oversight; Bilingual (English & Spanish)
