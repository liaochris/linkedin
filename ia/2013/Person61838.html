Jennifer Borges' Summary

Jennifer Borges is the owner of Balance Books, an accounting advisory and bookkeeping firm offering services to clients interested in leveraging the power of the Cloud. Her experience with financial advisory, CPA, and law firms led to the establishment of Balance Books in 2006. She recognized that a void – both physical and technical - existed between these advisors and their clients. Balance Books aims to fill this gap in the name of productivity and profitability.

Specialties

QuickBooks, cloud computing, SME management
