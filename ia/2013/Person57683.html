Public Company; 10,001+ employees; S; Telecommunications industry

1995 – 1997 (2 years) Overland Park, KS

Integral leader/manager with developing and implementing the Sprint PCS nationwide CDMA network. Hence, James played a key role in designing and implementing the broadband internet (wireless and wire-line) as it is today.

Engineering Manager

1990 – 1995 (5 years) Greater Denver Area

James was the primary Engineering Manager on US West's first commercial data service . The service was entitled "TLS" or Transparent LAN Service" and supported campus, multi-tenant, and government based environments. James also performed research and applied engineering on Optical Amplifiers (EDFA's), Digital Cross Connect Systems, Local Area Networks (Ethernet, Token Ring), and Personal Communications Service (PCS). James also directly supported T1/E1 and T1M1 standards bodies.
