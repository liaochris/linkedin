Don Ellwanger's Summary

Don Ellwanger serves as senior vice president for Sperry Van Ness, specializing in retail investment properties in California, Nevada and Arizona. With more than 22 years of commercial real estate experience, Ellwanger has completed more than 220 transactions valued in excess of $400 million.

Prior to joining Sperry Van Ness, Ellwanger served as vice president of the Ellwanger Corporation, a mechanical contractor company specializing in schools, shopping centers and hospitals. Under his direction, the company grew to be one of the top 200 firms in the United States. He went on to serve as a broker for Coldwell Banker Commercial and was the Number One Commercial Broker for the year 2000.

Due to his extensive background in retail investment transactions, Ellwanger has been responsible for several key transactions. His recent accomplishments include: Park Plaza Retail Center in Sacramento, Lakeside Retail Center in Stockton, Penn Valley Retail Center in Grass Valley, Village Festival Retail Center in Atlanta Georgia, Lake Mead Retail in Las Vegas Nevada, Lincoln Ridge Retail in Lincoln California,
the Antelope/Sunrise Retail Center in Sacramento and the $37 million Yuba City Market Place.

Specialties

Don Ellwanger specializes in the professional representation of buyers and sellers of commercial real estate.
