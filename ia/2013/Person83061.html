Lucas Wade's Summary

Hi,
I have over 5 years of professional experience in industrial design practices working with a range of clients anywhere from dental hygiene to automotive. I have experience working with a range of companies such as Colgate/Palmolive, OXO, All-Clad and the Groupe SEB umbrella, John Deere, and many entrepreneurs starting up.

Working with big name companies has given me the experience to manage and work closely with clients right from the start. Some of the experience includes: design process and strategies, proposal writing, design management, design research, concept generation, critical thinking, prototyping, CAD development, part development, and manufacturer liaison.

My background studies of industrial and interaction design has given me the unique experience to understand interaction design studies and techniques to branch over into the industrial design process. Striving towards solving problems at a user level and manufacturing level is one of my main goals in product design.
