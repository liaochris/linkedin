Jennifer Gomez's Summary

Jennifer Gomez, Esq. is an attorney and Florida Supreme Court Certified County Court Mediator. She currently practices law in the areas of Entertainment, Intellectual Property, Estate Planning and Contracts. Her clients include quadruple-platinum recording artists, sold out music festivals, New York Times bestselling authors, international film festivals, management companies, producers, actors, songwriters and models. In addition to being licensed to practice law in the State of Florida, she is also a mediator certified by the Supreme Court of Florida. Her involvement in the Nova Alternative Dispute Resolution Clinic has given her unique training in mediation, arbitration and negotiation.

Ms. Gomez graduated cum laude from the University of Florida and continued her education at the Nova Southeastern University Shepard Broad Law Center. At Nova, she served as the president of the Sports & Entertainment Law Society. As president, she organized many successful networking events and speaking engagements including the Annual Sports & Entertainment Law Symposium. As the vice-president of the Real Property, Probate and Trust Law Society she doubled their presence in the South Florida legal community.

Ms. Gomez worked at the management company, Long Distance Entertainment where she supported for three of the top twenty-five highest grossing concert tours of all time. She also has experience from one of the largest Estate Planning and Probate Administration firms in Florida catering to over 4,000 individuals, families and businesses.

Ms. Gomez is a Florida native residing in Fort Lauderdale. There she has given back to the community through organizations such as Legal Aid, Home Safe, Cinema Paradiso, the Palm Beach International Film Festival and the Fort Lauderdale International Film Festival.
