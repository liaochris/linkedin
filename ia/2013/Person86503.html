Mark Edward Smith's Summary

Social Media Branding Coach for MLM Entrepreneurs, Online Network Marketing Mentor at MLM Training Tools for MLM Success.
Mentoring and coaching new and seasoned online entrepreneurs with proven training tools that generate winning results. It is my privilege sharing Social Media Branding techniques that will enable you to attain the online success you deserve.

As a vocalist I’ve performed all over North America and Europe. Over the years I’ve had the absolute pleasure of singing with many talented performers: Sting, Herbie Hancock, Vinx, Vonda Shepard, David Sanborn, Sheryl Crow, Branford Marsalis. Extensive list of European jazz festivals w/ Vinx in 90's. Currently perform regularly with the LA Master Chorale and LA Philharmonic. Score vocalist on the blockbuster films: The Rite, Race To Witch Mountain, Earth, XMen-Wolverine, Sorcerer's Apprentice and Avatar.

My first TV acting gig (2002) was on the hit show Everybody Loves Raymond. That really helped get the ball rolling. Now in 2011 I have over 4 dozen TV shows and national commercials under my belt. Television credits include, Grey's Anatomy, Harry's Law, Parenthood (x3), Monk, Numb3rs, Boston Legal (x5), Secret Life of an American Teenager, Unhitched, The Riches, Standoff, How I Met Your Mother, The Shield, Studio 60, Everybody Loves Raymond and the critically acclaimed film Wristcutters…A Love Story. Recently completed filming the new feature film, Someone Marry Barry, starring Ed Helms, Tyler Labine & Damon Wayons Jr.

Became an Independent Arbonne Consultant after falling in love with the pure, botanical based, ultra premium, anti-aging skin care line. Recently lost 30 pounds in just months using Arbonne Essentials program. Living my dream as I build my global Arbonne business. Currently in the US, Canada, United Kingdom, and Australia.
