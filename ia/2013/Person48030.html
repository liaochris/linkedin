George Tuvell's Summary

I am a technology enthusiast that loves anything to do with software, hardware, security, and gadgets! I have co-founded and led the product development of two successful software companies. I have also shared responsibility in new business development, sales and marketing, and revenue generation. I focus on emerging and disruptive technologies that solve customer-centric problems.

I thrive at working with honest people I respect. I love building and participating in creative teams, and being hands on from concept to closing deals. I am currently Co-Founder & CTO of mSIGNIA, a Dynamic Device Identification Platform that helps reliably identify mobile users and eliminates the need for passwords.

I graduated with honors from The Ohio State University with a BS in Business Administration & Management Information Systems.

Specialties

Technology, from all angles; software, hardware, security, mobile devices, identity management, network and desktop security, cloud computing, product strategy, revenue generation, product marketing and business development, leading and motivating teams, raising money, writing patents, selling and closing deals, having fun and building life long friends.
