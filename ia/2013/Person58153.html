Janeane Davis' Summary

Janeane Davis is President and Executive Editor for the blog Janeane's World, located at www.janeanesworld.com that was created in order to address the thoughts, dreams and ideas relevant to the modern woman dealing with family, work and life. This blog has provided advertising content for Cookie's Kids, Verizon, Walmart, Golgate and Lexus among other brands.

Janeane is Co-Founder and Executive Editor for Pushing Lovely a website created to advance the stories of women writers. She plans and conducts individualized support meeting with writers, explains publication guidelines, requirements and standards and ensures that a quality product is put forth for public consumption. In addition, she is responsible for planning and managing educational programs that help the writers become better at their craft.

Janeane Davis is President and Chief Executive Officer of James,Davis and Associates where she facilitates encounters with clients, persons in the private sector, public officials and people in all levels of the enterprise chain in order to achieve outstanding results.

Janeane Davis currently specializes in the production of social media work for a variety of clients. By using social media platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest and Instagram she is able to gather attention and publicize attention for clients. She has written articles that have been featured with BlogHer, Project Eve, and Bloggy Moms.

Janeane Davis is a seasoned, trained, and well qualified litigator. Her education, training and experience make her well qualified to advise clients in a vast array of areas.As a business consultant and adviser she specializes in advising clients in financial planning, real estate investment, corporate organization, and corporate development for small organizations.
