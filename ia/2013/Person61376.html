Company Background:
Winshuttle is the premier provider of ERP usability solutions, providing software products that enable business users to work with SAP directly from Excel, Web forms and other common interfaces without any programming. Winshuttle focuses on a simple fact: When using SAP applications, time is money. Winshuttle's usability solutions radically accelerate SAP user transactions, saving SAP customers millions of dollars every day. These financial benefits are achieved by significantly reducing employee and contractor costs and increasing resources for addressing more strategic priorities. Thousands of customers currently use Winshuttle products to make their SAP lives easier and more productive.
