Loren Wilson's Summary

Loren Wilson is a veteran of the Internet advertising industry having spent the last 10+ years involved in building consumer-facing applications and sites, monetizing page views and operating a syndicated publisher ad network.

Currently, Loren is President of Answer Media a tech start-up in Kansas City.

Previously, Loren was the Vice President of Products at Inuvo where he led the Search Products and Partner Management groups since 2006. Loren was integral in growing the publisher network from $2 million to nearly $30 million annually. Loren joined Inuvo as part of an acquisition of Litmus Media, Inc. where he served as Director of Client Relations since 2005.

Prior to Litmus Media and Inuvo, Loren was part of the management team at tech start-up iModules Software, a SAS technology solution provider started from the purchased remnants of dot-com implosion Planet Alumni. There, he led client services and, in true start-up form, handled many responsibilities including sales engineering, product management and support. During his tenure, Loren played a key strategic role in helping to build iModules from fewer than 5 clients to more than 250 with an annual retention rate over 90%.

Loren began his career at KTEC, a private/public partnership established by the state of Kansas to promote technology-based entrepreneurship and economic development.

Loren received a B.S. in Business Administration with a concentration in Advertising from The University of Kansas.
