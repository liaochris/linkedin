Janine Sullivan Love's Summary

Things I enjoy doing: providing editorial and business leadership; managing, motivating, and mentoring; learning about new technologies and helping others to learn about them; public speaking; writing technical content; and thinking.

With more than twenty years of experience as a technical writer and editor, I have worked in marketing groups for manufacturers, served as an editor and editor in chief, moderated panels, instructed in classrooms, and traveled. I’ve set editorial calendars, managed budgets, made assignments, approved final copy, transitioned publications from print to online, mentored new editors, and worked with clients. I’ve edited highly technical and/or convoluted articles into straightforward ones that are useful for a wider audience. I’ve interviewed industry experts and written articles on the latest developments in high-tech and electronics. My fun is unraveling the technical and helping others to benefit from it, whether in a printed magazine, an online magazine, or at a conference.
