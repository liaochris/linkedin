Keith S. Aul's Summary

The INTERNET gives everyone the privilege to create a lifestyle of their dreams!!!

Unfortunately everyone is still being programmed to believe that the only way to earn a living is by accepting a JOB working for someone else's dreams and making a commute everyday to a brick and mortar business. With this belief system, most people will wind up spending the majority of their adult life traveling to and from work for a JOB that pays them very little and for a JOB they don't have a passion for.

Our educational system prepares us to become Employee's. Now, there is nothing absolutely wrong with this. However being an employee does have it's price. What comes with that price is the following:

Someone else determines your PAYCHECK!
Someone else dictates when you get a vacation and how long of a vacation you can have!
Someone else determines the Benefits you are allowed to have!
Someone else determines if you get to leave work early to take care of a family emergency or leave work to attend a child's soccer game!
Someone else determines where you get to live based on the location of your work place!
Someone else determines what kind of car(s) you get to drive which is directly related to the amount of disposable income you have!
In my opinion, LIFE is way to SHORT to allow other PEOPLE determine what you can or can't have.

There Is A BETTER WAY.....

Become an ENTREPRENEUR and surround yourself with people that will SUPPORT YOUR DREAMS.

There are many BENEFITS of becoming an Entrepreneur:

You and ONLY You get to determine every part of your life from the amount of income you earn to where you get to vacation!
You become a more valuable human being by learning every aspect of running a business!
You learn the very principles that will make you successful in all areas of your life: mind, body, spirit, relationships, emotions, and finances!
And the best benefit of all is when and where you get to earn a living!
