David Errichiello's Summary

I was born November 11th 1974 in Boonton, NJ. My family and I lived in Hopatcong, NJ until moving to Parsippany, NJ. I was educated by the Parsippany Public School System, attending High School at Parsippany Hills. While taking the everyday pre-requisite classes. I excelled in Woodworking 1 & 2, Cabinetmaking 1, 2 & 3, Building Trades 1, 2 & 3, Stagecraft 1 & 2, Drafting 1 and CAD Drawing (Computer Aided Drafting) 1 & 2. After graduating High School I had different jobs as carpenters and cabinetmakers helpers. As years went by I landed a really nice job working for Appletree Woodworks. I worked there for three years and gained lots of experience. My boss who was also named David was my mentor. He taught me more than just work. I also learned about business ethics and life lessons. In October of 1999 I left Appletree Woodworks to start my own business.

DE Services, LLC was started to help homeowners do all their home improvement projects, that you would never get done otherwise. We are full service construction company that specializes in Kitchen and Bathroom Renovations. We strive to have excellent response and customer service. When you call we answer, if we are unavailable and you leave a message; you will get a call back. If you set up an estimate, we show up on time. Since 1999 we have been working by Word of Mouth Advertising. We usually stay busy through out the year so if your looking for us to complete a large project you must plan in advance.

I am a driven and expressive person who is very dependable, organized and focused. I will always get the projects finished in a timely manner and usually under budget. The key to my success is to build business and personal relationships that turn into friendships. When somone likes you and they trust you, they'll do business with you. You will not have to sell them!!!
