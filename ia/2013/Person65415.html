Joel Constantino's Summary

What I do:
I concept, develop and manage truly innovative digital creative experiences to help brands tell a cohesive story across multiple platforms and channels. Ensuring brands are communicating with their target audience at the multiple touch-points consumers are exposed to on a daily basis.

I lead teams to create experiences that people actually want to interact with. Experiences that make engagement between the consumer and the brand valuable and memorable; helping brands become part of people’s culture, lives and communities. My teams are known for helping brands create brand enthusiasts who will organically grow awareness of the brand and their products.

Goals:
Tell great stories, incentivize participation, add value to people’s lives through the following four core values:

• Entertainment – giving the audience something that is unique and entertains them long enough to remember the brand and their experience

• Utility – giving them something they’ll use and/or need, making a valuable connection between the brand and the consumer

• Community – fulfilling the basic human need to be a part of something bigger and be accepted and loved as individuals in their social communities—Social Validation

• Inform – education and e-learnings that help people better themselves
