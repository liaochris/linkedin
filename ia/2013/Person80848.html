Lila Kelly's Summary

Lila has worked with staffing and diversity strategies and practices for 20 years. She works in the areas of: diversity assessment, strategic action planning, and training; integrating diversity into the recruitment, interview and hiring process; and centralizing and structuring the employment process. Lila has authored several books on integrating diversity into the hiring process, which can be found at http://stores.lulu.com/diversitybooks.

For several years, Lila taught a college course on Managing a Diverse Workforce. She has consulted with an equity collaboration between two school districts that were working to close the achievement gap. She conducted an assessment and provided recommendations for a strategic action plan to meet their goal to recruit and retain staff of color to reflect their student population. She also completed a temporary position as the Recruitment and Diversity Specialist for Intermediate District 287, which she held for over one year. See more details at WWW.LILAKELLY.COM, or Lila can be reached at 651-983-4489.

Specialties: Developing and implementing diversity and inclusion strategies and practices to create a culturally competent and inclusive work environment. Organizational assessment and strategic diversity action planning. Integrating diversity and inclusion into recruitment, interviewing, and hiring practices.

Lila Kelly's Publications

  • Integrating Diversity & Inclusion into Recruitment, Interviewing & Hiring

    • Book by Lila Kelly
    Authors: Lila Kelly

    This book will guide employers through a process to identify and eliminate barriers for diverse applicants, build cultural competencies, and create inclusive recruitment, interviewing and hiring practices. Techniques and examples are presented for a structured employee selection process, culturally-sensitive interview questions, addressing diversity during an interview, diversity recruiting, and inclusive hiring. Quizzes are included to test the reader’s learning. With a focus on developing individual and organizational cultural competencies, this book offers a unique approach to building successful diversity hiring strategies in organizations. This book can be used for internal training programs for your recruitment and hiring staff and as a guide to supplement Lila Kelly Associate's consulting and training services.

  • Diversity Recruiting & Resource Directory

    • Book by Lila Kelly
    • 2012
    Authors: Lila Kelly

    Why spend $150 or more per recruitment ad, with little results, when you can strategically develop a diverse pipeline of qualified applicants using resources in this Directory, many for little to no cost? Over 400 national and regional listings are included.
    Entries are included for: Multicultural, Latino/Hispanic, Asian American, American Indian, African American, Russian, Women, Disability, Veterans, GLBT, and Older Americans. General Diversity Resources also include: White men, Size, Translation Services, Films/Videos, Workshops. Diversity Assessment tools, and Books.

    Resources offer options to post job openings and internships, purchase a vendor booth, provide sponsorships, and/or network in other ways to connect with diverse communities. General Diversity Resources can help develop cultural competencies for recruiting and hiring.
