Diana Estrada's Summary

For over 20 years I’ve been fascinated by how fashion and the power of image as a tool, has the ability to influence the opinions and perceptions people have of each other, and more importantly, themselves.

I remember years ago, when I first started out as an image consultant at world renowned modeling agency John Robert Powers, how surprised I was when I saw men and women who were not models and not interested in becoming models, trying to learn how to improve their self-image through how they dressed and their body language.

It was in that moment that I realized that people were craving, and really needed help, tackling this area of their lives.

That's become my passion and guiding mission.
