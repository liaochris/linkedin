Jason D. Lazarus, Esq.'s Summary

Mr. Lazarus is the CEO and owner of Synergy Settlement Services, a group of companies offering settlement services across the country. He is also the managing partner and founder of Special Needs Law Firm; a Florida law firm that provides legal services related to public benefit preservation and qualified settlement funds. Prior to starting his settlement planning practice and founding the Special Needs Law Firm, Mr. Lazarus practiced as a Medical Malpractice and Workers’ Compensation attorney in Orlando.

He has had his written work published by AAJ’s Trial Magazine, Florida Justice Association Journal, The Florida Bar Journal, NAELA Journal, ElderLaw Report, Exceptional Parent, The Employee Advocate, News and 440 Report and The Journal of Transnational Law & Policy. He has presented on the topic of complex settlement issues for the Florida Justice Association, Southern Trial Lawyers Association, Florida Conference of Circuit Judges, Florida Workers Advocates, Workers’ Compensation Section of the Florida Bar, Mass Torts Made Perfect, Lorman Educational Seminars and countless local trial lawyer groups.
