Cara Smith's Summary

Creativity has always been an important part of my identity. From an early age I taught myself various crafts, and was never afraid to try something new, even if it meant failing.

Today, I continue to make and create both visually and with my hands. Professionally trained in graphic design from the beautiful Indiana University, I have grown and developed into a marketing professional where I am able to use my creativity to manage and market a unique company that produces several all-natural health products across various markets.

Using both the left and right brain has been a strong skill of mine, and I consider myself a jack of all trades. I like to have an understanding of all of the moving parts. Design is a strong skill and my diversity allows me to create compelling design both digitally and traditionally across various markets.
