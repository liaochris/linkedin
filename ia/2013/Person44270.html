Francesco Cetraro's Summary

I am an easygoing, friendly and loyal person that enjoys a life constantly on the move, always genuinely excited about new challenges and opportunities to learn new things.

Best defined as a massive "Domain Geek", I have had the immense luck and pleasure to spend over 8 years (and counting...) working with passion at the highest levels of the domain industry.
Through the years I managed to establish a broad contact network and to build up a very solid expertise on Domains and Hosting. I am comfortable dealing also with the more technically-advanced issues and on occasions I enjoy getting my hands dirty writing PHP code.

I joined Afilias in 2009 originally as part of the dotMobi team, and since then I have had the opportunity to work on many interesting projects. Today I am responsible for Business Development and customer relations in Europe for all Afilias' products and services, from .info/.mobi/.pro domains and new TLDs to all our mobile services like the award-winning goMobi Mobile Website Builder.

I also regularly represent Afilias at various industry events, and I am often invited to speak on topics ranging from new and existing TLDs to mobile Web at conferences and events across the globe.

Prior to joining Afilias I spent 4 years at one of Europe's leading ICANN-accredited Registrars in various roles, among other things as Registry Relations Manager looking after ccTLD and gTLD accreditations and as Product Manager.


I am a strong advocate of Open Source Software as an instrument to overcome digital divide and to allow a more equal distribution of resources among the people of the world. I see my position as an opportunity to contribute to a fairer development of the Internet.
