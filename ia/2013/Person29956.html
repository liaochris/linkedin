Daniel Stoehr's Summary

Experienced EHS Manager specializing in the hazardous waste regulations of the the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) and their application at industrial facilities. This includes providing the initial and annual refresher training for Hazardous Waste Personnel per 40 CFR 265.16.
Also provides high-quality HazMat Employee training for those who handle hazardous materials in transportation (includes loading and unloading) as required by 49 CFR 172, Subpart H.
A licensed Asbestos Inspector in Wisconsin, Iowa, and Illinois. Able to complete inspections of structures prior to disturbance or demolition per the requirements of the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP).

Specialties: Providing training services to meet the regulatory requirements of the USEPA for Hazardous Waste Personnel (RCRA Personnel) at 40 CFR 262.34(a)(4) & 40 CFR 265.16 and the USDOT training requirements for HazMat Employees found at 49 CFR 172, Subpart H.

Daniel Stoehr's Experience

Owner/Training Provider

Daniels Training Services (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Environmental Services industry

November 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months)

Daniels Training Services is a nationwide Environmental Health and Safety training service specializing in training to meet the requirements of the US EPA for Hazardous Waste Personnel (40 CFR 265.16) and the US DOT for HazMat Employees (49 CFR 172, Subpart H).
Training is available in a variety of formats: (1) Open Enrollment/Public Workshops are held in major cities throughout the US. These classes are great for the EHS or Transportation Professional who wish to learn the tips and tools necessary to maintain compliance and an overview of the applicable regulations. (2) On-Site Training is your best option if you have many employees that require training. I'll work with you to create a site-specific training presentation illuminating just the regulations your employees need to know and then come to your site to deliver it as often as necessary until everyone has received the same high-quality training. A great follow-up to On-Site Training is (3) Self-Guided Training, where, armed with the knowledge gained completing the On-Site Training, I will create a package of training materials you can use to train new employees yourself. If getting the training done ASAP is your priority, then I suggest (4) Web-Based Training. Web-Based Training is a concise training package, delivered by me to your employees through the internet; it is the best way to get your training done, fast.
Whichever training option you choose, my flat, reasonable rates make it easy to calculate your potential costs and decide which is right for you. Refer to my website or contact me with questions.
I am also available to speak at a meeting of your professional organization. If you have a group of professionals who are interested in any aspect of the regulations pertaining to the management of hazardous waste or the transportation of hazardous materials, I'm willing to speak at no charge.

Daniel Stoehr's Projects

  • Public Training Seminars

    • November 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel Stoehr

    Open-enrollment training events held year-round and nationwide. These one day events are designed for the EHS or transportation professional who needs a full overview of the regulations with assistance in applying to their operations. Attendees will learn how the regulations impact them and others based on their generator status and the types of waste they generate. In addition to training, I provide a binder of content, including links to websites, definitions, guidance documents; also the 2012 Emergency Response Guidebook and the JJ Keller HazMat Compliance Pocketbook. I will instruct you in how to use these tools to maintain compliance at your facility.

  • Onsite Training

    • November 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel Stoehr

    Onsite training is for those who want their employees to receive site-specific training tailored to just what they need to know to perform their jobs in compliance with the regulations. Training will address the requirements of the USEPA (and state regulations) for managing all waste, not just hazardous waste and the regulations of the PHMSA/USDOT for the transportation of hazardous materials. The training is delivered at your site (or wherever you choose) at a time and date of your convenience, so your expense and disruption to your work schedule is kept to a minimum. While on-site I'm happy to assist you with any regulatory issue or questions that arise.

  • NESHAP Asbestos Inspection

    • May 1999 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel Stoehr, Charlotte Stoehr

    Prior to the disturbance of any Asbestos Containing Material in a school or commercial and public building, or the demolition of any structure, it must first be inspected by a trained and licensed Asbestos Inspector. This is a requirement of the National Emissions Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) which is enforced by the US EPA or an approved state environmental agency, such as:
    The Illinois Environmental Protection Agency (IEPA)
    The Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM)
    The Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources (WDNR)
    The Iowa Department of Natural Resources (IDNR)
    I conduct the inspections, submit samples to a certified lab for analysis, and provide a report to the customer summarizing the results and informing them of their responsibilities and options.
    My rates are competitive in the areas of Rockford, IL; Belvidere, IL; Monroe, WI; Beloit, WI, Janesville, WI; the Quad Cities; and beyond.

  • Residential Asbestos Inspections

    • May 1999 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel Stoehr, Charlotte Stoehr

    If you suspect that your home contains asbestos, the best way to know for sure is to have it inspected by a licensed Asbestos Inspector. A professional inspection by licensed personnel with samples submitted to a certified lab for analysis is the best way to ensure you have the information you need to protect your family. Asbestos may be found in any home building material that is not known to be 100% glass, metal, or wood. I can provide inspection services throughout northwest Illinois and southern Wisconsin. I have performed inspections in Freeport, IL; Madison, WI; Beloit, WI; Rockford, IL; Janesville, WI; Monroe, WI; Belvidere, IL; and beyond.

  • Web-Based Training

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel Stoehr

    Web-based training or webinars are a great way to come into compliance with the training requirements of the USEPA (or your state) at 40 CFR 262.34(a)(4)/265.16 and/or those of the USDOT/PHMSA at 49 CFR 172, Subpart H. Not as in-depth as Onsite Training or Public Training Seminars, but it is fast (3 hours for either training), inexpensive ($189/person & no travel expenses), easy, and fully compliant with the regulations. Training can be made more site-specific and scheduled at your convenience if necessary. Discounts are available for groups. Check my schedule to see if one of my regularly scheduled webinars is for you.
