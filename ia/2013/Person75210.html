Kevin Stahl's Summary

Kevin Stahl is a composer, drummer, and music educator currently residing in Willow Grove, PA.

Kevin is credited with numerous award-winning film scores, game soundtracks, concert works, and television ads. Several of his award-winning film scores have been released commercially and his concert works have been performed around the country.

In 2003 Kevin co-founded Idryonis Studios, a multimedia post-production agency. Idryonis is an award-winning independent film post-production facility.

Specialties

Music Composition, Audio Technology, Music Education, IT, Networking, Computer Support, Broadcast Systems
