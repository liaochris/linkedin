Abhishek Singla's Summary

Abhishek started his career as a software developer, evolved as an entrepreneur by founding a technology company in education space, started India operations of a Canadian start-up, and enjoyed challenges of growing this start-up to become a leading company. Abhishek is passionate about entrepreneurship, web-based product development, data-driven marketing, and web analytic.

In 2008, Abhishek started the India operation of a Canadian startup, Savvica, funded by Educomp (EDSO.BO) - India’s largest Education Company. Savvica offers leading student enrollment and marketing solutions to education institutes in USA, UK, Canada, and India. Savvica acquired its primary competitor StudyPlaces, funded by KPCB, in 2010.

With over 12 million student visitors per annum, Savvica has created biggest network of online Indian students through three student portals – Learnhub.com, JumboTests.com, and StudyPlaces.com. Used by over 2.5 million students, LearnHub became the 12th most visited website of India in May 2011 (QuantCast).

Prior to Savvica, Abhishek co-founded Pathfinder eSolutions in 2005 to serve the emerging need of web-based solution for information management, office automation, and online learning in education industry.

Specialties: Startups, Internet Products, Online Marketing, Customer Analytic, Lead Management, Process Development, Leadership, and Team Building
