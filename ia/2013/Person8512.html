Ann J. Parks' Experience

Managing Partner - Executive Coach

Parks Executive Coaching, LLC

January 2011 – Present (3 years) Greater New York City Area

Ann’s primary focus is to deliver leading-edge, customized executive coaching services. She believes that to advance one’s career, a business professional must leverage his or her talent and personality to differentiate themselves in an increasingly challenging corporate environment. Parks Executive Coaching provides a framework for success. Ann capitalizes on her 25 years of leadership and management experience at select Fortune 100 companies to help enhance your professional skill set. Business leaders utilizing the Parks Executive Coaching model will benefit from enhanced management and leadership skills, more effective interaction with their management boards, peers, staff, and clients as well as a greater awareness of their personal communication styles. Ann coaches senior level executives, managing directors, senior vice presidents, vice presidents and high-potential professionals. Her services have been retained by a wide range of executives in financial, entertainment, and not-for-profit organizations.

Director, Learning & Development

JPMorgan Chase

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; JPM; Financial Services industry

Oversaw various web-based training modules linked to product, compliance, and continuing education.

Additional responsibilities included merger management. Merged two investment banking training departments, downsized and restructured, while maintaining merger synergies.
