Jill Olkoski's Summary

I provide comprehensive small business website design: custom design, search engine optimization, maintenance, content management system, blog integration, ecommerce integration, and hand holding for non-technical folks. If you need a website for your small business, we can get it done.

I attract clients who enjoy working with a small business, versus a large web design firm and who wish to form a long term relationship as their businesses grow and evolve. I also offer phone consultations on web design related topics as well as WordPress blogging lessons. I have clients from Seattle to New York, to Miami to Los Angeles and around the world.

I tend to attract many therapists, because of my Masters degree in Clinical Psychology. But my other degrees in Computer Science and Engineering and my experience working at Motorola have left me with solid business knowledge, customer satisfaction skills, and respect for quality guidelines (especially Google's).

These talents have filled my web design portfolio with a diverse array of small business owners. I routinely work with attorneys, artists, authors, contractors, consultants, coaches, farms, vineyards, financial companies, doctors, dentists, non-profits, pet services, real estate agents, rental properties, restaurants, gourmet food producers, window washers, retail businesses, travel and tourism companies.
