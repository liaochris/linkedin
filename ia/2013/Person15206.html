Bo Barron, CCIM's Summary

Bo Barron, CCIM serves as Vice President of Organizational Development for Sperry Van Ness International Corporation (SVNIC) where he increases productivity and delivers smiles to over 850 of the nation's most innovative CRE advisors. Barron is also a coach for the Massimo Group, a blogger, and a speaker.

Before joining the executive team of SVNIC, Barron served as Managing Director of Sperry Van Ness/The Barron Group, specializing in Single Tenant Net Lease (STNL) transactions, Tenant Representation and the sale and leasing of retail, office and multifamily property.

Prior to joining Sperry Van Ness, Barron served as a commercial real estate specialist for Wm. G. Barron Enterprises. Previously, he served for five years in United States Marine Corps as a sergeant and cryptologic linguist. While serving in the Marines, Barron led a platoon of more than 20 Marines and received two joint service achievement medals.

Barron holds the prestigious Certified Commercial Investment Member designation and real estate brokers licenses in the state of Indiana and the commonwealth of Kentucky.

Active in his community, Barron serves as a board member and President for MentorKids Kentucky and is an elder at Owensboro Christian Church. Barron also served 4+ years on the board of the Owensboro Family YMCA.

Barron earned a bachelor’s degree in Organizational Communication from Murray State University and an associate’s degree in Arabic from the Defense Language Institute. He is a graduate of the Cryptologic Technician Interpretive Arabic course where he received the Commander’s Award for academic achievement. In addition, he is also a graduate of the USMC Martial Arts Center of Excellence and holds the Tan and Grey Belt certifications.

A native of Owensboro, Kentucky, Barron married his high school sweetheart, the former Autumn Harley, and they are proud parents of three children.

Specialties: Productivity, Systemization, Coaching, and Training
