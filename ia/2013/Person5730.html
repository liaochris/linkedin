Amir Nathoo's Summary

I'm an engineer, so I want to create technology that solves real problems and build a great company in the process. I am an alumni of Y Combinator and St. John's College, University of Cambridge with a 1st class MEng.

James Brady and I founded Trigger.io, and WebMynd before that. Since 2008 we've built apps that have been downloaded millions of times and more recently have raised $1.8M seed investment from top Silicon Valley angels.

I started my career at IBM Software Development Group in the Hursley Lab, gaining experience as a support engineer working on-call with major banks. I was promoted and took on a variety of roles including a test team leader, resource deployment manager, and software consulting principle for content management software.
