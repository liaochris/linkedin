Jaime A. Zucker's Summary

I have over twelve years of professional and hands-on experience as a designer creating, establishing and maintaining social and corporate brands. I possess a wide-ranging knowledge of multimedia design, typography, page layout, digital, web and print production skills.

I have strong identity design talent and continually use my creativity to help develop and manage a variety of events, marketing and business development projects. I conceptualize designs and propose new branded collateral for internal/external marketing. I am passionate about graphic standards but also think outside the box. I pay strong attention to detail and use my originality to develop design concepts which offers continuity and consistency.
