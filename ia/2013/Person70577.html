Juliane Soprano's Summary

Juliane Soprano has been recognized as New England's Best Lawyers® in 2010, 2011, 2012 & 2013 in the area of Workers' Compensation based on exemplary legal work. She has also been awarded the Woman of Achievement by the Upper Cape Cod Business and Professional Women's Association. Attorney Soprano was recently Inducted into Worldwide Who’s Who for Excellence in Legal Services.

Attorney Soprano is on the lawyer referral panel for the National Organization of Social Security Claimants' Representatives, Barnstable County Bar Association, National Multiple Sclerosis Society, and Southeastern Massachusetts Fibromyalgia Support Group.

She is a member of the Workers' Injury Law & Advocacy Group and the Massachusetts Association of Trial Attorneys.

Juliane Soprano is a former member of the board of the Cape Cod Council on Alcoholism and Drug Addiction, and Big Brothers & Big Sisters.

She is a frequent speaker at legal clinics and informational seminars on the topics of social security disability (SSDI; SSI), and workers compensation.

Attorney Soprano is admitted to the bar in both Massachusetts and California.

Specialties: Attorney Soprano's concentrated practice in the areas of Workers' Compensation and Social Security Disability Law includes legal representation for numerous unions and trade groups such as the Massachusetts Nursing Association, the IBEW and the Association of Professional Flight Attendants.

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Juliane Soprano

    In July of 2012 Social Security issued a new ruling, SSR 12-2p, recognizing Fibromyalgia as a “complex medical condition characterized primarily by widespread pain in the joints, muscles, tendons, or nearby soft tissues that has persisted for at least 3 months.” This ruling acknowledges that Fibromyalgia can be the basis for a finding of disability.
