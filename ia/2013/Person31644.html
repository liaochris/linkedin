David Cooney's Summary

An engineer with 14 years experience in the design and management of building structures , Renewable operation and maintenance of Wind, Thermal and Hydro power Stations. Throughout this time I have gained considerable experience in both infrastructure and structural design, the use of the relevant design codes & best practice guidelines. Having played a key role in the delivery of projects from pre-planning to build completion I am familiar with current scottish planning requirements, building warrant approvals, SER audit requirements, Road Construction Consents, drainage/SUDS vesting with Scottish Water and current Health & Safety / CDM regulations.
Currently employed with Scottish and Southern Energy in Perth, Scotland. I am working within their Engineering Centre Team where I have civil / structural responsibility for technical & design compliance of new build Wind Farms and Thermal Power Stations.
