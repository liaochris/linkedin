Marty Coyne's Summary

I use my engineering and marketing background to help our clients in pharmaceuticals, medical technology, and life science expose new points of differentiation, develop market-shaping strategies, and invent new product and service solutions.

Because of my background, I can operate seamlessly across mixed teams of technical and marketing members, look at data in new ways to extract hidden value, and develop solutions that meet customer, technical, and business needs.

I am recognized for being able to frame issues vividly so teams can move forward while also respectfully challenging the status quo. I help “connect the dots” and translate disparate and ambiguous information into new, exciting solutions with a concise, actionable plan.

With experience in engineering and marketing at Stryker Orthopedics and Becton Dickinson, I have a unique view on both implantable and disposable medical devices used in institutional and home-based patient care settings. I have experience developing, launching, and positioning market leading implantable and disposable devices as well as drug-device combination products. I am a named inventor on ten issued patents and numerous patent applications.

I am passionate about improving the safety of injection practices for patients and healthcare workers. Most recently, I've been involved in advocacy work for safe injection practices to help halt the spread of infectious disease due to contamination of patient treatment supplies.
