Jennifer Goodwin's Summary

Jennifer Goodwin is the CEO of internetGIRLfriday.com, an award-winning internet marketing agency committed to helping others around the world understand how to leverage technology and the internet to earn more time and make more money.

Her SEO-focused agency provides businesses with services such as: Competitive Analysis, Keyword Research, Website Design, SEO, SEM, Pay Per Click, Social Media Marketing, Video Distribution, Analytics etc.

Early in 2013, Miss Goodwin launched a Support Program for Small Businesses in who want to learn her agency's 12 Step Internet Marketing formula. Membership starts at only $97/month.

Jennifer was born & raised in Massachusetts and has lived all over the US. After graduating in 1998 with an Architectural Technology degree, Jennifer started a successful drafting outsourcing company for Kitchen/Interior Designers. This was just the beginning of her internet career.

Upon moving to NJ, Jennifer began the NJ Young Professionals and has extensive experience planning networking events for up to 4000 attendees. More recently, she opened a Florida Office and is comfortable speaking to large audiences on technology, social media and internet marketing.

Jennifer loves to help businesses leverage internet technology to get the most ROI. She customizes her consultations to each individual business and really likes to get to know who she is working with. Her personal interested include holistic health, shooting, boating and travel.

Specialties: Keyword Research, Competitive Analysis, Website Architecture, Wordrpress, Sitemap Indexing, Business Directories, Blogging, PPC, Email Marketing, Social Media, Backlinking, Press & Awards, Shopping Carts, eCommerce, Affiliate Marketing, Analytics
