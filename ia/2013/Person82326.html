Lopa Parekh's Summary

Lopa is a versatile designer with diverse range of professional experience in product, exhibitions & large scale built environments across workplace, education, civic & commercial sectors in Australia & India. Her experience ranges from client consultation, design research, needs analysis, feasibility studies, concept generation, development, documentation, prototyping, project coordination through to delivery.

She has assisted various public & private sector client organizations enhance their business function, operational performance, brand strength & sustainability objectives through the development and implementation of innovative strategies, design concepts, plans and processes.

Lopa is experienced and competent across the processes critical to achieve a successful and practical design outcome. She employs Human centred, Systems thinking & Collaborative design approach to comprehend multifaceted challenges, explore opportunities and develop solutions that are contextual, innovative, socially engaging & environmentally responsible.

Lopa believes good design should engage & inspire people, instigate & foster positive behaviour change, enhance peoples capabilities while responding to the way they live, work & behave. She strongly believe in design's potential to induce positive change & holds keen interest in exploring design's role in addressing multidisciplinary challenges grounded in present & future context.
