Christine J. Rice, MBA, CPC's Summary

Located in the New York metropolitan area, Recruiting Executives Inc. is an international contingency executive search firm that has been an active member of the National Association of Personnel Services, AIRS directory, National Association of Physician Recruiters, National Coalition of Healthcare Recruiters and MDLinx as well as is committed to ongoing education and adherence of all applicable EEOC laws. Recruiting Executives Inc. is incorporated and insured in New York State.

Our successful search experience ranges from working with pre-IPO start-up companies to mid-size companies to Fortune 500 corporations. Services can be tailored to your company's individual requirements and can include individualized executive search as well as assistance in national personnel strategies. Since incorporation, Recruiting Executives Inc. has effectively generated more than $2,000,000 in gross revenues from both contract and permanent placements.

Specialized focus areas include identifying high performing Hematology, Oncology, Urology, Cancer targeted Clinical and Medical Affairs Professionals for the Biotechnology and Pharmaceutical industries as well as Hematology, Oncology, Cancer specific Healthcare Providers: Physician, Midlevel Practitioner and Research Professionals.
