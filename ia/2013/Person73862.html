Kelly A. Madison's Summary

Employee Benefits Broker/Consultant advising business of all sizes on their group medical, dental and all other areas of Employee Benefits. We are independent agents representing all major carriers operating in Idaho. With over 20 years in the industry Kelly Madison recently merged her company, Strategic Benefits with Myriad Benefits. Experienced and respected in the business community, Kelly has often been invited to speak on benefits related subjects to organizations such as; The Idaho Society of CPA's (Industry & Education Group), Southern Idaho Compensation & Benefits Association, Boise Metro Chamber of Commerce and Lorman Education Services.
