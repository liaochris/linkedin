Colin Powers' Summary

I am a Documentary Producer, Manager and Media Strategist with 22 years of management and sales experience. As Director of Production & Programming at Mountain Lake PBS, I led strategic planning, budgeting and production of media across all platforms.

I've spent 13 years as an entrepreneur producing documentary films for PBS, A&E, AMC, and corporate clients. In 2010 my company released “Skatopia: 88 Acres of Anarchy”, a feature documentary.

I have 20 years of experience in the outdoor industry including General Manager of $10M worker-owned bicycle manufacturer.

Much of my work has focused on sustainability - in business and the environment. I serve on the Board of Directors of Bright Blue Ecomedia - an educational media non-profit.
