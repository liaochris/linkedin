Adam Edgerton's Summary

I am well-rounded in all things web. With background in business and marketing plus talent and experience in project management, being a digital PM suits me extremely well. In current and previous positions I've been a natural leader, unafraid of tackling big challenges. From coordinating and supervising a team of project managers to leading large projects for marquee clients to planning a product and managing it to successful launch, I have experience in key areas that are extremely transferrable across the digital industry. Along the way, I've used my digital marketing acumen to plan project requirements, my understanding of client business objectives to close sales, and my business school education to aid with financial forecasting and high level company strategy.

Any given day I'm a people manager, a project and product manager, an account manager, a digital strategist, a salesperson, and more. I constantly seek to identify the biggest weaknesses in an organization and align my role to make sure potential shortcomings are addressed. I wouldn't have it any other way!
