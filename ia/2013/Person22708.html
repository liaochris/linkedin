Cheryl Rubin's Summary

Currently an adjunct professor at New York University in Licensing & Merchandising and a strategic consultant in licensing, integrated marketing and brand development for clients including AMC Networks' YEAH! and Seltzer Licensing Group.

Known as "Batman's brand manager" during a long career at Warner Bros./DC Comics, I am an expert in creative strategy, integrated brand marketing, promotions, brand extension licensing and intellectual property franchise management.

During my tenure at Warner Bros., I managed the growth of DC Comics' iconic brands including Batman and Superman into the global franchises they are today.

My expertise in strategic creative planning to drive and support licensed consumer products is unparalleled - I led creative teams to produce over 400 brand style guides while also managing DC's global licensing business and creative integration across consumer products and entertainment.

I also supervised DC Direct (now called DC Collectibles), a vertically integrated collectible business, maintaining #1 market share in the comic book industry for 10 consecutive years.

My broad relationships with industry leading manufacturers, intellectual property owners, retailers, creative talent and entertainment executives provide a broad base of expertise, resources and partners.

I have "done it all" experience as a licensor, licensee, brand manager, marketer, creative director, publisher, retailer and Hollywood studio senior executive.

I am also fluent in super heroes, the comic book industry, and fan targeted marketing.

Specialties: Strategic & Creative Brand Development | Entertainment Franchise Management | Integrated Brand Marketing | Global Merchandising & Licensing | Business Development | Strategic Partnership Development and Activation | Style Guide Development | Intellectual Property Development | Product Development | Fan Marketing
