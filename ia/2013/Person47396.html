Gayle Threets' Summary

Threets & Associates, a Boutique Real Estate Firm serving the Bay Area. Our team has over 19 years of experience in Negotiating, Consulting & Overseeing the details in real estate transactions. We leverage the latest technology with innovative negotiating techniques to consistently deliver exceptional customer service and the highest quality Real Estate services.

As your Trusted Advisor our mission is to help client’s attain their goal of homeownership, financial security & wealth management while simultaneously building a strong, lasting, lifelong relationship. Our purpose is to support families, build strong communities and promote education throughout the communities we serve.

We believe what separates us from the pack is not what we do, but how we do it. When the client speaks, we listen and customize each transaction to ensure we deliver premium services. We hand tailor every transaction so that we maximize the client’s return on investment exceeding their expectations in every way. Every relationship is managed by a Senior Consultant who provides personalized service, superior negotiations and strategic marketing expertise. As a result, we are regarded by our clients and the industry as one of the most innovative, efficient, reliable, consistent & personable Real Estate firms in the Bay Area.

With Threets & Associates, client’s get the best of two worlds. They get to partner with an innovative boutique firm that is flexible and results-oriented. They also get access to the unlimited resources of RE/MAX, the largest and most renowned Real Estate Company in the World. As a sub-corp operating under RE/MAX, we are linked to the biggest names in real estate from brokers to lenders. Our partnerships with these brokers & lenders are solid. When the deal’s on our table, it gets done.

We at Threets & Associates are not Salespeople, we are Consultants. We already know you want to buy, sell or invest in real property. Our goal is to provide you with valuable information so that you can make the right decisions based on your short and long term goals.

We put our experience to work for all of our client’s because it is not only about buying, selling or investing in Real Estate, but also about making a difference that transforms our communities.
