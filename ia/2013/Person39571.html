Edie Raether, MS, CSP's Summary

Edie Raether, MS, CSP, is a Change Strategist who will challenge the way you think and change the way you do business to thrive even in a tough economy.

For 38 years Edie’s keynotes have empowered over 3,500 associations and Fortune 500 companies on 5 continents. An author and expert on breakthrough thinking and the Other IQ-Innovation, Influence and Intuitive Intelligence, her “must have” change management and influencing system predicts performance and transforms apathy into action to increase sales, empower leaders, and improve customer care.
