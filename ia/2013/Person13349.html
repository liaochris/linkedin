Ben Van Hunnik's Summary

Hands on researcher/marketer with diverse experience not only gathering and analyzing data, but creating the framework and processes to efficiently, effectively and repeatedly deliver the knowledge (data) and products needed to accomplish organizational goals. Examples of systems (process and products) include:
- Systems to recruit participants into research and health projects
- Systems to ensure participant retention in research and health projects
- Automated data visualization systems
- Web analytics systems
- Social media systems
- Usability testing systems
- Film and video marketing systems

I also own Prodigy Research, which offers services to track down anything, from mobile populations in research and health projects, to your genealogy, to the story behind that unique antique in your attic. Check out the blog posts in my activities streams or at prodigyresearch.com.

Currently enjoying my role as Director of Information, Research & Evaluation at Hearth Connection.

Specialties: Marketing, Branding, Web Analytics, Social Media, Longitudinal Research, People Tracking, Internet Research.
