Edward Hoyt's Summary

I am a marketing executive with extensive experience developing and managing successful organizations. After a successful 30 year career as an owner/operator of California radio stations, my wife Darlyne and I relocated to Pebble Beach. I put my marketing skills and knowledge to work developing a real estate practice, focusing on residential golf properties.

My philosophy of marketing can be summed-up in the simple definition of the difference between marketing and selling; marketing fulfills the need of the buyer, selling fulfills the need of the salesperson. In a challenging real estate market, it takes marketing skill to cut through the clutter, and to get the attention of buyers.

In addition to my real estate practice, I have pursued and received a degree in Applied Economics from The University of San Francisco. And, I am a founding Board member of 1st Capital Bank, with branches in Monterey, Salinas, and King City.

Darlyne and I are both avid golfers, and are members of Pasadera Country Club, Monterey. I am proud to be the 2008 Pasadera Conquistador Champion.
