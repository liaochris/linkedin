Mike A. Starcher's Summary

Mike is a strategic thinker with strong analytical and problem solving skills. He has over fifteen (15) years of hands on project management experience, with ten (10) years specifically in the wireless industry and over six (6) years working with NetSuite ERP/MRP SaaS & cloud computing systems. Mike enjoys the challenges associated with start up operations and is literally a man of many hats.

Mike has his M.B.A. from Mckendree University. In addition, he currently holds a B.S. in Business Management with a minor in Marketing, an A.S. in Electrical Engineering and an A.S. in Mechanics.

Some specific career accomplishments include:

• Provided hands on leadership and management during the startup of over ten (10) new business operations including two (2) local small businesses, five (5) domestic operations and three (3) international ventures.

• Managed multiple successful NetSuite implementation projects over a six (6) year period; including the merger of three (3) separate Cellular phone companies onto one ERP system.

• Managed the development and implementation of a custom cell phone tracking system that was deployed globally at Certicell. The system was .NET based, hosted in the cloud running on SQL server 2008 and used Reporting Services 2008 for data mining.

• Successful management of multiple international projects in Hong Kong, Manila, Nogales, Reynosa and London.

• Achieved monthly savings of $120K after successful completion of a Lean Sigma Project focused on Asset Recovery.
