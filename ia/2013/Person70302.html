Jude Odu's Summary

An experienced and seasoned healthcare professional for over a decade and a half, Jude has worked in long-term care, in acute care, on the payer side, as well as on the academic side of the healthcare industry. His past work as medical records director and privacy compliance officer, supervisor for clinical analytics and decision support at a major, multi-hospital health system, senior clinical data analyst, and health information manager, among other roles, have served to enrich his experience in the healthcare field.

In his current role as a Business Analyst and healthcare subject matter expert, Jude works with stakeholders to define and document business processes and software requirements for technology initiatives at Health Care DataWorks, Inc., a healthcare business intelligence and enterprise data warehouse solutions company. This requires frequent interaction with software development teams, Product Management, Sales, Customers, outside vendors and other cross-functional team members to provide subject matter expertise wherever needed. Jude’s ability to perform his duties effectively relies on a comprehensive understanding of healthcare operations and the strategic application of Business Intelligence and data warehousing within healthcare organizations.

In addition to his extensive experience in healthcare, Jude has developed and currently teaches courses in healthcare informatics and other introductory courses in healthcare at several Northeast Ohio colleges as well as online.
