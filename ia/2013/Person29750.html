Danielle Turner's Summary

Danielle Turner, video media specialist and voice over artist, is the chick in charge at Velvet Pumpkin Productions and has been actively involved in the field of video production for web broadcasting long before it was popular. Danielle has guided her clients to the forefront of online video technologies, leading them through pioneering communication practices.

And she is much more than an eye behind the camera. Having started her career as an actress, she has transitioned that skill to voice over and hasn't looked back since.

It isn't easy finding a job where you look forward to going to work each Monday morning. But Danielle has managed to do just that. It's her greatest passion working with business owners and marketing teams to design and construct the perfect video that communicates exactly what they need in a way that engages and motivates their audiences to take action.

Specialties: Video production. Voice Over for commercials and narration. Corporate training videos (diplomas in adult education). Video post production - work with Adobe Production Premium. Camera shooting - currently working with Canon 5D Mark ii
