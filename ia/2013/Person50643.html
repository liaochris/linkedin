Rocco Bruno's Summary

I have been in the Law Label printing business (Do Not Remove Under Penalty of Law labels) as the owner of American Law Label, Inc. since 1989. We print and ship law labels all over the world with a fast printing turnaround and reasonable prices.

I also started and own Global Registration Services, Inc. in 2004. This company was the first company in the world to offer manufacturers around the world of Bedding & Furniture products (with hidden filling materials that require law labels) assistance in obtaining their U.S. licenses and Uniform Registry Number so their products can be sold in the U.S.

No other company in the world offers this range of services. And there is no other single person in the world with the vast working knowledge of bedding & furniture labeling laws and registration than myself.

Specialties

A comprehensive knowledge of individual state bedding & furniture tagging laws and registration requirements. Assistance to overseas manufacturers in applying for bedding licenses and obtaining the Uniform Registry Numbers for their law labels so their products can be sold in the U.S. marketplace.
