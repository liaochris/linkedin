Josh Klenert's Summary

An accomplished creative leader in the field of Design and UX with over 15 years experience in designing and delivering highly engaging large-scale user experiences across all platforms. Leading the User Experience & Design team for The Huffington Post Media Group across mobile and web — having recently launched iPad apps for The Huffington Post, HuffPost Live and Huffington magazine.

• Collaborative leader and strong mentor skilled in fostering creativity and innovation. Experienced building and managing teams of internal and external resources.

  • SPD.org iPad Application (SPD45)

    • November 2010 to May 2011
    Team Members: Josh Klenert, Mark Kociscin, Jeremy LaCroix, Brian Doyle

    SPD45 took the SPD [Graphic Design] Annual #45 competition bound-book and turned it into an interactive app on the iPad.
    My role in the project was to create a back-end database which drives and underlies all the fantastic visuals.

    Starting with the Filemaker Pro database files that SPD uses to collect and manage each year's compeition entries, I re-engineered the database to suit the app development needs, then exported, massaged, and cleaned up the data. This data was then turned into a working SQLite file that was included as part of the XCode project that creates the app.

    In simple terms, I was the Plumber and Electrician on the project - nothing in the pretty app's house would work without my efforts.

    The app is selling on Apple's iTunes App Store.
