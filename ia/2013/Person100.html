Benefits of Outsourcing:

Save Money:
Reduction of office overhead.
No longer have to pay for training, seminars, Disability/ WC and Health Coverage.
No more worrying about claims not going out on time due to the absences of your medical biller.
No more overtime due the work back-logged from absences.

¬ Save Time:
You and your staff will have more time to care for your patients and manage your practice.
Staff will have more time to handle day to day routine instead of spending time on the telephone with insurance companies and patients regarding their accounts.
More time to complete charts and the necessary administrative tasks.

¬ Increase Cash Flow:
Consistant cash flow due to accurate and timely filing of claims and patient statements.
Electronic submission of claims which give you a 7-14 day turn around as opposed to paper claims which have a 30-45 day turn around.
Intense follow up with insurance companies, appeals and soft collections.
Accounts receivable recovery.
