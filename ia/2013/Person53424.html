Zachary Holliday's Summary

Zach has over 15 years of experience working with the U.S. Federal Government. He manages Holliday & Associates, Inc. strategic vision while leading capture planning and acting as the "Red Team Evaluator" for our clients. He has served as a Proposal Architect, Capture Manager and Technical Writer for hundreds of technical proposals to include large design-build construction projects up to $400,000,000.00 and MATOC projects up to $350,000,000. Zach maintains a firm understanding in Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) regulations, best businesses practices and protocols, as well as analyzing competition and factoring award probability.
