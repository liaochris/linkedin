Marleen de Korver's Summary

I design both print and web, for non-profit organisations and professionals/entrepreneurs in creative en service providing sectors.

In designing and optimizing websites, weblogs or e-mail newsletters i help clients to attract more visitors to their websites through the principles of usability and user centered design.

Usability in relation to webdesign and online communication is the focus of my blog and of my free e-mail newsletter (in dutch). Tips and advice on attracting more visitors to your website, in order to find new, more or better clients (or for nonprofits: more members, contributors, volunteers).
blog: http://blog.laatjewebsitewerken.nl/
subscribe to the newsletter at http://www.laatjewebsitewerken.nl/nieuwsbrief.htm

I teach webdesign at SKVR Beeldfabriek (Stichting Kunstzinnige Vorming Rotterdam). Occasionally I teach workshops on website maintenance and optimization for non-web professionals.
