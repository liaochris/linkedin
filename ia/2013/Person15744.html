Stephanie Padovani's Summary

Stephanie is a professional wedding entertainer in the Hudson Valley, freelance writer and blogger for the wedding industry, and wedding business marketing coach with a passion for helping people.

She aims to deliver massive value to clients through her writing and performance, expanding into the arenas of newspaper, magazine and book publication.
