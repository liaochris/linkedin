✔ Believes in a 'FAIR but FIRM' debt collections philosophy...

► Short:

I am collection agency consultant who helps businesses get paid for their products or services and medical practices to get paid for their care - by matching them with the 'best fit' professional collection agencies for their accounts receivable issues.

► Detailed:

Are you frustrated because someone isn't paying you for the products or services you provided?

Are you sick of tired of sending out past due letters, emails and making phone calls - only to get excuses, 'the checks in the mail', the run around or just plain ignored?

If the answer to either of the above questions is a YES, it might make sense for us to chat...

Finding the right collection agency is as easy as it may appear. One should keep in mind that the collection agency you choose will be representing your business to your current and former customers/clients/patients, your community as well as State and Federal Regulators.

A bad collection agency can not only damage your reputation, but it some instances can create a legal liability for your business. That doesn't even take into account the recovery rate and fees that the debt collection agency charges.

We have relationships with multiple FDCPA and HIPAA compliant debt collectors to help our clients recover the money that is owed to them both quickly and affordably. All without creating liability, throwing good money after bad, or damaging their reputations.

Stop wasting time and resources on debtor's who are taking advantage of your good nature! Let the collection agencies we recommend be the bad guys, while you focus on your core business.
