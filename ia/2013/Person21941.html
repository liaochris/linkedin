Chanelle Schneider's Summary

I am seeking a full time position in the Social Media Marketing & Management industry, specifically creating and/or editing content for the Web and strategic planning of media projects from conception through release. I have a philanthropic desire to help those seeking to act on their unselfish dreams.

I have worked in the retail, clerical/administrative, event planning, social media and content production industries for the past eleven years. During this time I worked closely with the public, which afforded me the opportunity to learn about management, marketing, fostering successful client relationships, professionalism and team work. Through my experience I learned that I am especially skilled at anticipating and establishing client needs by asking the right questions, handling organizational paperwork, and creating “info-taining” (informational and entertaining) content and marketing tools to drive business goals.

I will bring to you someone who understands that social media is about building relationships through offering a unique and valuable experience. My engagement has resulted in people seeking me to contribute to their blogs through guest posts and several marketing consulting positions. I have run the weekly chat, #GenYChat, on Twitter for 3 years, breaking down communication barriers between Generation Y, Generation X and Baby Boomers. As a social media evangelist, I encourage those around me to see the value in these tools to further goals and grow as a person.

I want to make the Marketing Communications industry with emphasis on driving user interaction through genuine communication my career. As a fast learner, I will grow beyond my current entry level experience quickly, offering your company a great asset as an employee. I thrive in both independent and team-oriented work environments and can effectively represent the company in professional settings.
