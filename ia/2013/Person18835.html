Paul Beavers' Summary

Refer to our web site at www.ROOFCONSULTANT.com or www.AIRBARRIERCONSULTANTS.com

Paul Beavers is the president of Systems Building Envelope Consultants, Ltd. Paul is an expert in roofing and waterproofing associated mostly with lodging, commercial and industrial properties. He is called upon by these types of clients to source out water infiltration problems and develop the solutions to remedy. Although at times is retained for large residential projects. The services he provides start with sourcing out water infiltration issues, providing a report with the findings, issuing a scope of work providing the solution and follows up with quality control site visits confirming that the work being performed is in compliance with his scope of work and the manufacturers specifications. He likewise performs analysis for developers, investors and building owners during the due diligence process when his clients are considering buying properties. Paul is engaged as a expert often and has testified in county, state and federal courts. SBEC has been involved in numerous LEED programs and keeps itself on the cutting edge of the roofing, waterproofing and green building technologies. SBEC is aggressively involved in The United States Army Corps of Engineer (USACE) projects nationwide.

Specialties

Paul is capable of sourcing out water infiltration problems on any type of residential, commercial and/or industrial roofing and/or waterproofing system. He is also a Registered Roof Observer (RRO) and is often engaged as an expert on cases related to the proper construction and/or maintenance involved with the roofing and/or the waterproofing of a property. SBEC is aggressively involved in The United States Army Corps of Engineer (USACE) projects nationwide.
