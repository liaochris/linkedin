Jill Castle's Summary

I have over 23 years of professional and practical experience in the field of pediatric nutrition. As a former clinical pediatric dietitian and private practice owner, I currently spend my time writing and blogging, speaking on childhood nutrition topics such as preventing childhood obesity and how to feed children, as well as acting as a nutrition consultant.

I co-authored Fearless Feeding: How to Raise Healthy Eaters from High Chair to High School, published by Jossey-Bass/Wiley.

For businesses and organizations, I help with program & product development, educational materials, consultation, and speaking or writing about topics in pediatric nutrition.

Specialties: Pediatric Nutrition especially normal feeding, eating disorders, obesity, allergies, veganism, feeding difficulties, growth failure, family feeding dynamics, sports nutrition
