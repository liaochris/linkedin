Ali Jagger (AMAC)'s Summary

I AM HERBALIFE AND HERBALIFE IS ME! I run the West Kirby Wellness Centre with Josh Houston where we:

* Help people learn how to lose weight, get healthy and feel amazing
* Train and mentor other people who join our team with a view to helping others
* Work with team members who want to change their financial future forever
* Are generally living the dream and loving what we do!

Having been over 20 stone myself 10 years ago and struggling for many years trying to lose this weight I am passionate about what I do! This is my vocation ... helping others achieve their wellness and weight loss goals - you don't have to accept weight gain and let it define who you are forever. I've been there and I promise you with the right help, support and mindset ... it really can be done.
