Kenneth Seidberg's Summary

Who I Am:
I'm an Arizona Consumer Collection Law Attorney for businesses and founder of an Arizona Collection Law Firm. I'm dedicated to Collections and Creditors’ Rights, Committed to long term NET results for my clients and Capable of meeting the most demanding Client EDI requirements.

Who I Help:
My Firm's primary clients are major national, regional and state creditors/companies doing business in Arizona; specifically selling products or services to Arizona consumers. We are looking for creditors doing at least $15 million plus a year in gross revenues in Arizona and needing regular and ongoing assistance in collecting delinquent/bad debt.

How I Help:
I utilize state of the art recovery, electronic payment, computer security, data security, document imaging, investigative and information software. With over 30 years of experience I've learned to pull the best of what collections has to offer and combine it with what attorneys can offer. It's a very effective combination.

If you have any questions please visit www.azdebtcollectionlaw.com or call my office at 623-258-4397.
