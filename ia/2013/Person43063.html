Esteban Rey Torres' Summary

Highly skilled and creative media artist, knowledgeable on a wide variety of media formats applied in diverse branches of the digital industry, including video, web, events, augmented reality, e-learning and marketing. Qualified researcher whose few publications have been indexed by recognized institutions in Colombia and the United States. Cross-functional experience with more than ten years of practice as entrepreneur in interactive design and application development for the fields of advertising, education and arts. Conducted several audiovisual projects with effective leadership and proven ability to maintain creative integrity; recognized for creativity, sense of humor, flexible thinking and accuracy while adhering to strict project, budget and schedule parameters.
