Arthur Coddington's Summary

I work with an elite crew of extraordinary people who are serious about reaching for what's next in their lives. I am a thirteen-time world champion athlete. My work is informed by twenty years of pursuing ambitious goals, honestly assessing situations, adapting to change and sometimes bending reality.

I help introverts in technology embrace their strengths and thrive, and I help organizations make full use of the talents of their introverted staff.

I help athletes apply their sports experience to new challenges. Together, we unleash the power of team leadership, high intensity training and peak performance.

I help passionate progressives put their values into action and change the world.

Want to be part of the crew? Get in touch at http://arthurcoddington.com/contact/
