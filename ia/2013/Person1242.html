Andrew Davis' Summary

Nearly 20 years of award-winning results in digital, design and advertising. A very diplomatic fellow who remains calm and keeps an eye on the big picture even in the midst of absolute chaos. Excellent problem solving abilities, strong interpersonal communication skills and an innate talent for leadership. Gets clients excited and onboard. Sense of humor? Yes.

Currently I am available for full-time or freelance work.

I've recently finished several branding and site design projects including for a variety of agencies on clients like Apica, Halogen, Dell, Pizza Hut, Intel, and Corning.

In the past I've:
• Developed award-winning campaigns for Microsoft, Nortel, Nestle and Honeywell. Work included extensive interactive and streaming advertising, Global DR campaigns and more.
• Key concepter and driver of the extremely successful viral site “msdewey.com”. Using the character Miss Dewey, who comments on your searches while you search, this site promoted Windows Live Search. Comprised of almost 500 separate responses, this video-based site had over 9 million hits in the first three months it was live. Remarkably, this site was cash positive for the client, paying for itself in the long run.
• Directly managed approximately 15 creatives on a variety of Microsoft clients including Office, Dynamics, Mid-market and Small Business. Direct report to Rob Bagot, ECD and responsible with my partner for the entire creative output of MRM. The work was primarily CRM and includes developing specific efforts against the targets, as well as leveraging advertising efforts “below the line”. Responsibilities included managing all Art Directors through a period of intense growth where the department went from 12 to 60 in a 12-month period.

Specialties

Lead creative on accounts including Microsoft, Nortel, Visa, Nestle, and Sony. Experienced in Technology, Finance and CPG sectors. Equally comfortable in front of the client, developing account strategy or crafting mechanicals. Managed creative teams on tight deadlines in high-stress environments. Created and enhanced client brands from identity to campaign. A good creative manager who actively mentors juniors.
