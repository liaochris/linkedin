Kathryn Gritzmacher's Summary

At WestmorelandFlint, I develop and help implement marketing strategies for businesses. I particularly enjoy business development, B2B sales and e-marketing in the areas of lead nurturing and lead generation, content marketing and marketing automation. I love to work with my talented co-workers to come up with great ideas for my clients.
