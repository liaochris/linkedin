Kathryn Taylor's Summary

I love ideas that make a difference. I love collaborative projects that result in greater good. I love entrepreneurs who dream of a different work and social innovators who are thinking of new ways to do business. I believe a group of people can decide what they want their city to become and actively work to create that reality.

Some of the best advice I got in my twenties was to try lots of different things. I took that to heart and explored as many different avenues as possible: starting new programs in Italy for college students, leading trips at an outdoor adventure camp, serving at homeless shelters in Chicago and helping with the rebuilding efforts in New Orleans to name a few. I learned that I like influencing people and can strategically lead teams. I learned that I am a visionary but one who enjoys the planning process. I learned that I can understand the details required to move a project forward but don't necessarily enjoy spending time in the details. Most of all I learned that my passion is mobilizing people and resources on behalf of projects that make a difference. This lead me to the development world where I currently serve.
