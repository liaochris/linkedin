Greg Gunn's Projects

  • Core Values

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Greg Gunn

    Inspired by Zappos, Blind lives by 10 Core Values that embody the culture and spirit of their company. They are more than just mottos, or rules, or bullet points – they’re beliefs. If you’ve ever walked through their front door then you’ll know what I mean.

    In order to celebrate these values, I created 10 animated, mini films. Each film interprets and illustrates one Core Value in a fun and unexpected way.

  • Method - We're All In This Together

    • July 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Greg Gunn

    What is it about air travel that brings out the ...quirks, in people? This fun little animation reminds you that although you're seated next to some maniac, we're all in this together. If you fly Virgin anytime soon, you'll catch it playing in the preflight videos on your seat screen.

    This was as fun to make as it is to watch. During the production process, we developed an interesting animation technique combining cel animation with After Effect's puppet tool. I think the result looks pretty darned good, and the client loved it.

    Also, if you're a frequent AE Puppet Tool user, check out this handy script I created to make your animating life easier - http://aescripts.com/puppettools/

  • N.A.S.A. - Gifted

    • February 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Greg Gunn

    I doubt I will ever pull this many all nighters in a row ever again. However, I am extremely proud of the finished piece our small team created. Without that late night delirium contributing to the design and animation process, I don't think this video would have been as mind meltingly successful.

  • Zune - Piece of Me, Piece of You

    • January 2008 to Present
    Team Members: Greg Gunn

    Zombie Puppets. What else should I say. The lovely people at 72andSunny gave us absolute freedom to convey the idea of 'sharing' in any way we wanted. We conceptualized, wrote, directed and vfx'd this entire thing. 99% of what you're watching was created in camera with puppetry and and practical effects; that's right, zero CG.

    The characters were designed and developed with the talented Andrew Hem and then constructed by Adam Parker Smith and Carolyn Salas. The undead soundbed was provided by Chromeo. So much fun, and too this day, one of my favorite projects.

  • Humans!

    • May 2006 to Present
    Team Members: Greg Gunn

    We are really kind of jerks to this lovely earth. And hopefully one day, we can colonize space and be jerks to other planets too. 'Humans' was my thesis animation project at Otis College of Art + Design, that I made with then school pals, Casey and Reza.
