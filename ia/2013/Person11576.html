Howard Baltus' Summary

"In a failing economy, we learn to juggle and succeed by moving quickly from one project to another with no loss of enthusiasm".

I developed one of the first magic shops, the trickery.com, on the Internet almost 18 years ago. It housed over 3000 items for the beginner to the performers on television. I've also developed and created many magic items that have been on the magic market throughout the world. The Trickery was the first magic shop to offer on-line video demos of the effects we sold.

At the start of the recession I brought back to life WebAuthorings, a web design and development business. The bulk of that business is designing custom CMS solutions and custom Ecommerce solutions for small to medium businesses as well as hosting on an array of dedicated cloud servers.

Other work includes graphics, flash, video editing, dvd authoring, audio production, scoring original music for use in video or dvd presentations, powerpoint.

Specialties

html, css, php, perl, wordpress, open source platforms.
