The Boot Camp is an eight month hands on education program of the Brownsville Chamber of Commerce. Our mission is to collaborate with women small business owners to provide the knowledge, resources and plan of action needed to make their “entrepreneurial dream” a reality. As the lead facilitator, Maria Nuez Hall, coordinates all program communication, content development, training, mentoring, coaching and scheduling.
Program Vision/Goals
• To provide an innovative and comprehensive informational program for Women Entrepreneurs.
• To provide an opportunity for a solid and long-lasting network to be formed, utilized and sustained.
• To provide a venue which allows Women Entrepreneurs to be empowered, engaged and accountable to the success they personally desire.
• To provide leadership development training that will foster self-discovery and awareness for each Women Entrepreneur individually as well as the collective group.
• To provide coaching/mentoring to increase the effectiveness and integration of the program learning’s for each participant throughout the program.

Ignite Consultants Plus is a strategic partner and proud sponsor of this program.
