To 5i, convergence (UC) is a process, not a product. We bring to enterprises the skills and experience to:

Understand current business processes and ongoing business needs
Use that knowledge to create a map of desired information flows
Design an information system to deliver that flow ensuring effective communications across transparent systems.
When we engage with any end-user organisation the process begins with a studied comparison of that enterprise’s information needs, business process and the business potential of IP communications technologies. The output of that first engagement is a set of goals for converged, intelligent communication systems within the organisation.

In our experience, organisations that go through this top-down process consistently surprise themselves by setting more imaginative, ambitious and creative goals than they would have reached via the usual bottom up method of looking at what problems technology can fix.

5i works with businesses to understand how the latest technologies can help them communicate more effectively. This has the advantage of increasing individuals productivity which in course can lead to cost savings, increased differentiation and revenue gains.
