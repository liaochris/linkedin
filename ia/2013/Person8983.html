Dr. Antonio Tovar, D.M.'s Projects

  • Current Research\Academic Work

    • March 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Dr. Antonio Tovar, D.M., Dr. Phil Harris, Dr. Scott Rounds, Dr. Nancy Camacho

    This is an academic longitudinal study examining organizational ethics. The study proposes to evaluate executive corporate ethical behavior as a way to discover the different forms of applied ethics (also known as professional ethics) at play in the business environment. The aim of the study is to find a possible correlation of all aspects of business ethics and its relevance to social responsibility and ethical behavior of entire organizations.

  • Entrepreneurial Founder Effects on Firm Performance

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Dr. Antonio Tovar, D.M., Dr. Phil Harris, DBA

    This is a study that aims to explore the relationship between founder/CEO’s and firm performance. Extant literature implies that individual entrepreneurial characteristics influence organizational success. This study explores the vision and commitment of founder/CEO’s towards the firm’s success playing a critical role in the performance and achievement of organizational goals.
