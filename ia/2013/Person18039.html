Ed Riley's Experience

Owner

Broadcast Image, Inc.

October 1990 – Present (22 years 10 months)

Broadcast Image, Inc. Specializes in High Definition Video production Services for the Television and Motion Picture industry.

News Cameraman

KTTV Channel 11 Fox News

1981 – 1993 (12 years)

I worked as a broadcast news cameraman shooting general and breaking news in the greater Los Angeles area. Many of my news assignments would take me to nationwide and international news locations. I have shot many documentaries, promos, live shots and news specials during my employment at KTTV Fox News. The fast paced broadcast news environment trained me to work and think fast while maintaining a high degree of quality in my work.

Director, Associate Director

KTTV Channel 11 Fox News

1978 – 1981 (3 years)

Directed hourly live news cut-ins for the morning and evening news broadcasts. Directed the live weekend news program and Satellite News Channel's hourly news segments. The duties of director included blocking cameras and shot selection. I was responsible for calling cameras to the TD, keeping track of tape times, VTR location, live shots and graphics. I would work directly with the news anchors, technical director, news and sports producers.

I worked as associate director on the morning, evening and weekend news programs. I worked directly with the news anchors, technical director, news and sports producers and director. I was responsible for directing cut-ins, marking scripts with camera shots, tape times, VTR location, graphics, and coordinating live shots. This was a constant challenge due to the fast paced and ever changing environment of live news coverage.
