Kevin C. Hurteau's Summary

I started as a Mortgage Advisor in 1992 after graduating from the University of Rhode Island. I have always liked 3 things about this business;

1. Helping people meet their Real Estate mortgage goals whether a first time home buyer or a high end investor.

2. Every day is a new adventure, a new problem to solve or a new need to fulfill.

3. The freedom to control my own schedule and what I do with that time.

My expertise has always been with the First Time Homebuyer. The emotion behind buying your first home is something that I enjoy witnessing as a professional and as a friend. I am creative, persistent, and well informed in order to keep ahead of the changes in the mortgage industry. It has been a challenge and I hope to keep helping people realize their dream of home ownership. All that being said, I love what I do as a Mortgage Advisor and cannot imagine doing anything else. This is who I am and what I bring to the Real Estate industry.

My wife and I have 6 children and are very active in our community. We combine family activities with local charities, sports and school events to keep us busy and to make special memories.

Send me an email to connect whether you are in the Real Estate business or not. I look forward to meeting new people in the community and reconnecting with past colleagues and friends.

You can call me at 401-623-9096 or email me at kevinhurteau@yahoo.com

Thank you, Kevin

Specialties: Mortgage Advisor/Mortgage Consultant, First Time Home Buyer Mortgage Programs, FHA, VA, and USDA Mortgage Programs, New Construction Mortgages, MA, RI, NH Real Estate, etc.
