Marqise Allen's Summary

Marqise is a journalist that made the leap to public relations/communications. He had covered everything from the nitty gritty of federal health care reform and its impacts to the opening of new businesses. Now he writes and edits news releases for the Washington State Department of Health, while also penning internal memos and materials for the governor's office. Outside of his love for writing, he's also a technology and social media enthusiast with experience managing websites.
