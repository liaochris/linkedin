David Coats' Summary

Digital marketing is a confluence of science and marketing; where complex coding and data analytics mixes with the basic marketing function of creating interest in a product or service.

A scientist by education, marketeer by profession, I love the mixing of technology with business principles which makes online marketing.

Beginning in 2004 by designing simple websites, and progressing to manage a team of 22 search analysts, my career has spanned a range of experiences including providing indepth technical consultancy, devising multi-channel marketing strategies, people management, directing a department and new business development.

I love the challenges online marketing presents, but more importantly I love the interaction opportunities technical marketing brings. Taking a complex solution and making it easy to understand is where I excel. Seeing the results of a well planned marketing strategy is where I get the most pleasure.

Specialties

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO), PPC and Online Acquisition Marketing, Digital Strategy, Consultancy, Multi-channel Digital Marketing, Web Analytics, Multivariant testing and conversion management, Affiliate Marketing, Website build.
