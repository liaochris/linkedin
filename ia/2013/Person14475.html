Bill Tilley's Summary

For fourteen plus years Bill Tilley has been devoted to creating and then fine-tuning the litigation finance industry. Bill Tilley joined Themis Capital in 1999, launching the legal finance industry in CA. Mr. Tilley was instrumental in growing Themis from 12mm in loans upon his arrival to more than 135mm in less than four years. After successfully winding down the Themis portfolio in 2004 Bill Tilley became the largest provider of legal financial services. In 2006 Bill expanded by offering his financial expertise to both plaintiff’s and law firms through the settlement process and expanded offerings to include comprehensive settlement planning and financial advisory services. Bill has personally originated well over 300 million in attorney loans and structures and has participated in the origination of greater than 400 million. In early 2007 he launched the first full service financial services company that caters exclusively to trial focused law firms, Amicus Capital Services, LLC.

Mr. Tilley has been instrumental in developing risk management controls for the law firm lending market that continue to be used today by legal finance companies. Examples include case concentration risk matrices on a per firm and per portfolio basis, developed coverage ratios and monitoring systems of borrowers required to ensure loan repayment and tort case valuation process.

Continuing his devotion to trial law firms, “The Business of Law Advisory Services” was launched to provide business guidance to select law firms throughout the country. Mr. Tilley counts many of the most successful trial attorneys in the country as personal friends and has developed a network in the thousands of invaluable industry contacts which enables him to facilitate partnerships and collaborations. When appropriate for a particular case, partnering the best firms, resources and expertise can allow firms to prosecute their cases from a position of maximum strength.
