Jen Sterling's Summary

Jen is the President/CEO of Red Thinking. She is dedicated to helping professional service companies create an emotional connection with their audience; which improves customer loyalty, increases awareness, and will ultimately lead to greater revenues. She brings almost 25 years of award-winning design experience along with a contagious zest for life to the firm. She is also the former president and co-founder of Hinge, a professional services brand marketing firm.

As a self-proclaimed “volunteer-oholic”, Jen is a graduate of Leadership Fairfax and enthusiastically serves on several boards in the Metro DC area such as the Reston Hospital Center, Let's Help Kids, Entrepreneurs' Organization DC Chapter, and numerous small business advisory boards.

Her community leadership positions have included: President of the DC Chapter of Entrepreneurs' Organization, Chairman of the Board of Success in the City, Chairman of the Greater Reston Chamber of Commerce, Chairman of the Loudoun County Chamber of Commerce, Chairman of the INC.spire incubator program, Director Fairfax County Park Foundation, Director Leadership Fairfax, Advisory Board Devotion to Children, and Advisory Board Bite Me Cancer.

She is a graduate of George Washington University where she received a BA in Visual Communications with a minor in Psychology.

Jen is also a proud mother and a mentor to other women business owners.

Specialties: Brand Strategy, Design Implementation, Creativity, Innovation and Basic Business Management
