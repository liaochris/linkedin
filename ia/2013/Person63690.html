Jillian Sullivan's Summary

I write fiction and non-fiction for children, teenagers and adults. My work includes four novels, three collections of short stories, including one on mythology, over sixty short stories for children and adults, published in New Zealand and America, and a book of creative non-fiction on the art of writing (Fishing from the Boat Ramp). Teaching work includes lecturer in writing for young adults for the Bachelor in Arts and Media at Nelson Marlborough Institute of Technology, and running courses on writing and the Hero's Journey.
