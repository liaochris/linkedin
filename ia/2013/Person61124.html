Jeff Redwine's Summary

For the last 4 years I have worked in toxicology research, including GLP toxicology preclinical study management. I value building positive working relationships with others, and maintaining a positive, collaborative, and supportive professional environment. I have previous experience with virus-induced disease models, quantitative stereologic techniques, histologic techniques, and imaging/microscopy applied to rodent models of neurologic disease, as well as experience with molecular biology, transgenic mouse models, and multiple types of microscopy (deconvolution, confocal, epifluorescence, and standard transmitted light).

Specialties

GLP preclinical toxicology, histology-based quantitative techniques, neurobiology, neurologic disease
