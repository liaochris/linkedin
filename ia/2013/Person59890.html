Jaylyn Bergner's Summary

My expertise in marketing and communications spans a broad spectrum of topics and audiences. Some of these subjects have covered weapons of mass destruction to memory tips for business-minded professionals to band profiles. Specifically, my experience includes, but is not limited to, editing critical documents for the Government concerning the national security of our country; working with companies to create engaging and effective website content; generating press releases for industry-specific media; scripting a speech for Doctor Richard Brown, nationally recognized plastic surgeon, who appeared on Illinois’ The Biggest Loser; writing a music column covering the events in the Hampton Roads area; managing the public relations section of a thriving entertainment company servicing the New Jersey, Delaware, Maryland, and Pennsylvania areas; writing articles and pieces for publications, such as Rugby Magazine, Mid-Atlantic Monthly Events, bizMe, and Daedalus (collegiate); creating short stories; teaching courses on branding and the incorporation of neuro-linguistic programming (NLP) into all communication efforts to improve rapport; and writing books (two in progress).
