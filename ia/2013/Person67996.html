Jon Morse's Summary

A 17+ year Information Technology Leader experienced in Technology Sales and Delivery to small and large clients globally with focus on Software Development for Web-based and Mobile Technology, Application and Business Solution Architecture, and Enterprise Program Management. I apply my expertise as a true “Knowledge Worker” focused on the integration of People, Process and Technology for start-up to enterprise-wide information technology projects.

My technology and process-focused background as an IT Business Analyst and Project and Program Manager has served as the basis for my success to bridge business and technical teams to achieve business goals. Having direct exposure and influence in over 200+ projects has resulted in my ability to conceptualize, architect and sell solutions at the executive level.

My passion for disruptive technology has led me to create and lead a multi-million dollar Mobile Technology Professional Service Line for Experis IT where I am able to engage and build mobile solutions for 100s of corporate clients. This exposure has opened exciting opportunities for me to speak at many Fortune 500 companies in addition to local and national organizations and universities.

My passion also extends into sports and technology start-up ventures including my own golf travel-based website (TripCaddie.com) which has received acknowledgement from the International Association of Golf Tour Operators and the award winning Golf Channel television network.
