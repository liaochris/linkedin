Drew Beson drew@drewbeson.com's Summary

Drew Beson is a professional artist living and working in Minneapolis, Minnesota. His art career started at St. Olaf College as well as a spell in New York City. Art has taken Drew to Florence, Cortona, Siena, and Lucca, Italy as well as in London, England.

After graduation and a brief retreat to the North woods, Drew began working independently on his art at his studio space in Edina, Minnesota and has continued to do so for the last ten years. Recently, Drew has added an additional 3D studio space just a few miles away.

In recent years, Drew has been published or written about in every major Minneapolis publication and has also had exhibitions at several galleries including: The Minneapolis Institute of Art, Hennes Art Company, Gallery 13, Milward Farrell Fine Art in Madison, WI, and 3rd Street Gallery in Grand Forks, ND. Drew has had solo exhibitions at the Kirkland Gallery, HangIt Gallery, Gallery 122, The Women’s Club of Minneapolis, and Sotheby’s International Realty in Minneapolis, Galleria Edina, and at Gallery One at BMW of Minnetonka.

Currently, you can find Drew making and exhibiting work at his new 4,800 square foot luxury Gallery+Studio space in the Skyway level of Canadian Pacific Plaza in Downtown Minneapolis, MN.

Find out more about art purchase and leasing at: www.drewbesonart.com
