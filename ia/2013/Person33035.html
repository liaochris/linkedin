Daniela Anavitarte Bolzmann's Publications

  • How Productivity Tools Can Waste Your Time

    • Wall Street Journal
    • January 29, 2013
    Authors: Daniela Anavitarte Bolzmann, SUE SHELLENBARGER

    Sue Shellenbarger from the Wall Street Journal interviewed me on the topic of productivity tools. Her article covers may different methods that are successful for different people and the key take away is to find a process that works for you. It is a personal favorite activity of mine to find and explore the latest apps so for me this is not a waste of time. In the past, I have almost always ended up reverting back to pen and paper only to be lost in a sea of notepads. That sea of data lost to stacks of notepads was almost becoming normal to me, that is, until I found Trello. I learned about Trello when I was at Startup Weekend Chicago and have since stopped trying to find the next best thing…for now. Around the same time of learning about Trello I had also read a blog post from Joel Gasciogne and what he call the Anti To-Do list. Using a scrum style task board on Trello to manage my “done list,” as Joel calls it, is now my daily routine. I have noticed that seeing that my done list is always the longest list is a big motivator for me and I can proudly say that I have not touched a paper and pen in four months. Although my process is likely to change in the future I am loving my current productivity process.
