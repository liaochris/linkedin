Chris Simpson, CISSP, CCSK, CKM, GCIA's Summary

Retired Navy Information Professional with broad experience managing information technology. Specialized experience as project lead for stand up and daily operations of a 50 server data and as Information Assurance Manager for large organizations with experience securing information and network systems. Special interest in vulnerability detection and remediation, incident response and intrusion detection.
