Amy Vitti's Summary

With a BFA from Savannah College of Art and Design and a keen eye for design, I have over 5 years of experience as a Motion Graphics Designer and Video Editor.

I've collaborated with and designed motion graphics for universities, iPod application development teams, product marketing teams, video bloggers, and re-sale motion art companies, as well as a number of screen-cast video tutorials and voice over work. I've also been heavily involved with editing wedding videography as an entire post-production spectrum from raw footage to final DVD.

With each project I encounter, my number one goal is to give the piece the attention it deserves to make a dazzling finished product to ensure my clients are pleased with the final outcome.

Specialties

My skill set includes logo design, print and layout design, motion graphics, editing, compositing, color correction, audio tuning, logo animation, logo revision, video compression or reformatting, collaboration, flash video, web implementation, and light web design.
