D-Mar General Contracting & Development, Inc.'s Summary

Since 1972 D-Mar General Contracting and Development, Inc. has been a privately owned commercial construction company located in Clearwater, Florida. Starting in 2001 the company has been expanding its business plan from commercial remodeling and build-outs to include site development & demolition, new commercial/residential building, ADA compliance renovations, project management, generator manual transfer switches and Commercial Service Contracts and Management

Our experience and drive over the past 35 years has been to perform to the highest level of customer satisfaction. This has made us a respected company within the industry.
We strive to maintain our customer satisfaction levels while we continue to expand our business towards new construction and development.

We are positioned and ready to service you and any project that you may have. Our confident, dedicated and skilled staff will ensure a smoothly run project from start to finish.

Specialties

D-Mar General Contracting and Development is a State Certified General Contracting business in Florida and South Carolina.
