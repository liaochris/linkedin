Kathie M Thomas' Summary

I can be contacted direct via my websites - please send an introduction with your invitation.

What do I do?
I connect clients with VAs, and VAs with clients. I write about the industry through articles and blogs and run an online training program for Virtual Assistants. I speak at conferences and seminars locally in Australia and internationally by webinar to encourage women worldwide that they too can return home to work and be there for their family. My favourite topic is the Proverbs 31 Woman - a Work at Home Mum.

What is a VA?
A Virtual Assistant (VA) is a self-employed business owner/operator who provides office support services to other business operators to allow them to get on with the business of running their business. VAs operate on a remote or virtual basis from their own offices and therefore don't require clients to pay for their insurances, taxes, equipment, etc. An ideal answer for those who need admin support without having to engage an employee.

In 1994 I chose to work as a home-based secretary to be home for my 5 daughters. When the Internet entered the business world in Australia 18 months later my whole business focus changed. Before I knew it I became mentor and motivator for women around the world who wanted to work at home. Today I manage a network of VAs from many countries and moderate two VA forums totaling 2,500+ members.

Providing support for public speakers,authors, trainers & coaches and NFP organisations:
• Social Media support (Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter),
• Blog set up and management
• Database management,
• Website design and updates,
• Web-hosting,
• eBooks and printed books.

My favourite quote: "Whatever your passion is - that is your vision" - Mike Jeffs, Founder Australian Christian TV

When I'm not at my computer working, you will find me out and about with my Nikon in the Dandenong Ranges (Vic, Australia) taking nature photographs. I can also be found at local markets selling my images in prints or on items.
