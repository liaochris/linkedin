Chad Lariscy's Summary

I have nearly 20 years of experience in the Housing Industry. I have been a licensed Realtor for only 7 years and have been very successful. My personal goals are to live a God Centered Life, to be a great husband and the best father I can be. My business goals are to exceed the expectations of my customers and clients, build strong relationships and provide them with the absolute best experience in Real Estate. In 2012 my focus will be to build an extremely successful Real Estate Team and to give back to the needful homes within the communities we serve through The Mountain Life Team's non-profitable organization "A Home With Heart."

Specialties

Customer Service and providing an experience in Real Estate that is second to none. Building lasting relationships with my customers and clients. Internet Marketing and Presence. Authoritative Advice and Market Knowledge. Strong Negotiating Skills. Being an Advocate for Mountain Living.
