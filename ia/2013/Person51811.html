James Harris' Summary

I have been a professional photographer since 1979. In the early years, I traveled with my Canon A-1 and Linhof Technika 4x5 view camera photographing the American West, amassing a stock photo library of over 10,000 transparencies. I now shoots exclusively digital with Nikon equipment, looking to craft outstanding photographs. My photographs have appeared in numerous ads, books, magazines, and on magazine covers. Assignments have included such clients as the National Park Service, U.S. Electricar, the Pikes Peak International Hill Climb, and Catholic Outreach for Northern Ukraine (CONU).

I live with my wife and kids in Colorado Springs, Colorado, in the shadow of Pikes Peak, one of Colorado's 54 fourteeners.

I also teach photography classes and workshops, with an emphasis on panoramas, HDR (high-dynamic range) photography, and landscape photography. Join my workshop site at www.meetup.com/The-Quiet-Light/. My workshops are sponsored by Kolor.com (Autopano panorama software), HDRsoft (Photomatix Pro HDR software), and Unified Color Technologies (HDR Expose 2 software).
