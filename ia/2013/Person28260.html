Paul Cunningham's Summary

I've been working in the Brisbane IT sector since 1999.

My roles have include pre-sales, solutions architecture, project implementation, and operational support. In many cases I have performed all of those roles in a complete end to end piece of work.

I've worked in government and the private sector, for in-house IT departments and outsource providers, with small business and big multi-nationals.

My specialty is Microsoft Exchange Server, Microsoft server infrastructure, and some of the technology that plugs into it such as security and backup products.

I have many years of experience in design, delivery and and operational support and maintenance for organizations from small business to multi-national enterprises.

Nearly every role in my career has involved Microsoft Exchange Server operations or implementation in some way, either as a primary focus or as part of larger duties in the Microsoft server infrastructure space (Active Directory, DNS, File/Print, Clustering, ISA/TMG. SCCM).

My industry certifications include MCP, MCSE, MCSA:Security, MCTS: Exchange Server 2007 & 2010, MCITP:Enterprise Messaging Administrator 2007 & 2010 and Exchange 2013 certification.

Specialties: Microsoft Exchange Server is my specialty. I am certified in multiple versions of Exchange as well as being awarded as a Microsoft MVP.

Of added value is my exceptional quality documentation and technical writing.
