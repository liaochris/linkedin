Loretta Cella's Summary

11 years of experience in human services. Leadership Program/ Project Developer. Trained ICF Coach. Certified Canadian Career Development Practioner. Child and Youth Care Counselor. Mapper of potential, supporter of diversity.

Working with diverse populations in various parts of the world in an array of capacities, I have been able to hone in on my skills and develop a real passion for creating high quality innovative programs for young people and adults.

Community development and individual enhancement sits at the core of my work.

In 2009 the Passion Foundation became a Canadian Charity serving young women in Canada. Using my networking abilities I brought together an amazing national Board of Directors from Vancouver and Toronto who are dedicated to providing quality services for young women and their families. Special thanks to McCarthy Tetrault LLP in Vancouver, BC for their services as the Passion Foundation's law firm.
