Brian Sweat's Experience

Director of Product Management

Alterity, Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry

June 2007 – Present (6 years 2 months) Arlington, Texas

Responsible for ACCTivate! roadmap, including review and approval of all reported bugs and enhancement requests in Trac. Major release cycles include 500-700 tickets with monthly service packs in the 50-100 ticket range. Manage software release process, including 28 different installers for various products and editions.

Launched EZ Analytics for Inventory, the first Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product offered by Alterity in the Intuit App Center. Application was very profitable, leading to multiple ACCTivate! clients with virtually no ongoing sales, support or development costs.

Launched ACCTivate! international editions in Canada, South Africa, UK and Australia. These editions include full integration with the corresponding QuickBooks international edition, support for local sales tax and multiple currencies.

Maintain relationships with sales and support teams to ensure that ACCTivate! remains the best software system for small to medium-sized wholesale/distribution clients. Provide ongoing training and mentoring for new staff across all departments.

Developed relationships with many contacts throughout Intuit and other key strategic partners. Recently introduced as "the most knowledgeable guy for the QuickBooks SDK" at a conference.

Host monthly ACCTivate! webinars for prospects, customers and partners. Topics covered include new product announcements, advanced inventory concepts, integrations with eCommerce platforms, EDI and Mobile Warehousing.

Created the ACCTivate! Documenation site at http://help.acctivate.com. Authored many of the written articles. Principle editor for other contributors within the organization.

Principle speaker for ACCTivate! training sessions at industry conferences, such as Scaling New Heights and The Sleeter Group.
