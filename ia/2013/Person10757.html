Michael Calogero's Summary

The Law Office of Michael G. Calogero, LLC practices Personal Injury & Wrongful Death Litigation; Insurance Litigation; Auto Collisions; Criminal Law; Small Business Law - Risk Management / Loss Prevention; Transactional Law / Contracts; Wills & Successions, Intra-Family Adoptions and Recognition of Foreign Adoptions; Criminal Law.
What sets our firm apart is prompt, quality service (top / AV peer review rating by Martindale-Hubbell), dedication and commitment to excellence. Serving the client is of paramount importance. Listening to client concerns is order to establish the scope of representation and a goal oriented approach are key. Paying attention to the needs of the client is essential. Each member of the firm conscientiously works to serve our clients. It's not about us - it's about the client.
From April 1994 to the present (19 years), Michael has been licensed to practice law in Louisiana. He has experience and serves his clients well.
In January 1998, Michael established the Law Office of Michael G. Calogero, LLC. We determined that there is no room for compromising personal service, quality representation and client accessibility. The firm prmarily represents individuals and small businesses, people who have been seriously injured or the families of people who are killed, or who need medical treatment, for chronic pain and disabling conditions as a result of a motor vehicle collisions and other incidents. The firm also represents small businesses, primarily with loss prevention, risk management and contract / transactional legal services.
In December 1993, Michael earned a J.D. of Law from Loyola School of Law in New Orleans, LA. Distinctions: Loyola Law Review (top 5%); December 1993 Cum Laude graduate; Alpha Sigma Nu Nat'l Honor Society; Memberships: La. Assn. for Justice, Jeff.
