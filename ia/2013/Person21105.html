Catriona Pollard's Summary

Good PR is so much more than churning out press releases. It’s about asking the right questions, understanding the landscape of the media and market, and having an antenna poised to pick up on any opportunities to benefit a client’s business.

I am the owner and founder of CP Communications, a highly experienced PR company, merging traditional PR methodologies with cutting-edge social media strategies to identify your business’s uniqueness - and find the right people to share it with.

Ethical, creative and committed to performance, we deliver meaningful and long-term results that link your brand stories and core messaging to what’s happening in the real world, in real time.

I am a pioneer of combining PR with social media. My professional blog – Public Relations Sydney – is listed as the top 2 Business Blog in Australia. I am listed as one of the top 100 PR people to follow on Twitter.

My views are sought by the media on PR and social media. I am actively involved in business networks am a member of the Public Relations Institute of Australia. I regularly keynote and run workshops on PR & social media.

Catriona Pollard's Experience

Founder and CEO

CP Communications

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry

2001 – Present (12 years) Crows Nest

CP Communications offers truly strategic and creative solutions that can help you achieve your business objectives through strategic PR and social media.

CP Communications was established in 2001 to drive business success by using the power of PR and social media.

Our clients love that the CP Communications team are organised, consistent and proactive. We will always be on the phone with a gentle prod about a deadline or to pitch another idea. Integrity and ethics have always driven this business. We genuinely want to make your business more successful.

We work in a results focused way, so our clients can achieve the exposure that’s right for their business goals.

And that is where we start. We talk through not only your communications goals but your business objectives. We then facilitate a strategy session to link your communications goals with your business goals, so that everything we do assists in building your business as a whole.

The business is a member of the Public Relations Institute of Australia (PRIA) Registered Consultancy Group, a group which establishes high standards of operation and professional excellence for public relations consultancies.

Co-Founder

Social Media Women

March 2010 – March 2012 (2 years 1 month)

I developed the concept and co-founded Social Media Women which is a formal networking group that encourages and assists women to participate more prominently in social media.

Having recognised the important role women play in social media, the group aims to give women a voice in all areas of social media. It’s a place to connect and share to support each other and build a community.

With Social Media Women we have created a safe place for women to gain experience and confidence to ask questions, learn and challenge themselves to grow, evolve and aspire.
