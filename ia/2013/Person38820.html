Dustin Ryen's Summary

First and foremost I am an idea guy. Whether it's working on a new business concept or coming up with a creative solution for a client I thrive in all things creative. As far as professional experience I have worked in marketing and business development for the past 9 years. I am an experienced experiential marketer with many events to my credit including numerous concerts, trade-shows, fundraisers, product launches, and many restaurant/bar promotions/openings. I currently serve as the Marketing Director for Purple Cow Websites. I am co-owner of Event Marketing Creative with my wife. I also am a part owner of Fun Run Group LLC. I also worked for a year as an assistant community manager Yelp for the Sacramento territory.

My past clients include Yelp, 3M, Skyy Vodka, Gold's Gym, The Ups Store, Jim Beam, Patron Tequila, Red Rabbit Restaurant, and Eppie's Great Race among others. I was Valedictorian in the Internet Marketing program at Full Sail University where I received my Bachelor of Science Degree in 2011. During my studies at Full Sail I received eight different Course Director awards for achievement.

One of the most influential experiences of my career was the founding of NiiTES.com with only a $1,000 loan. That business grew to millions of photo requests monthly and several contract employees through my marketing and the hard work of my wife who jointly founded the business with me. We took on numerous clients including several major household brands. We also put on numerous sold out events and restaurant/bar promotions. At one point the business was even ranked #7 on Google for the term "nightlife".
