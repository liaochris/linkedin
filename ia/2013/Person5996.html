Amy Kraushaar's Summary

Connecting with people emotionally is the key to brand competitiveness. It is also the single most under-utilized asset of most brands. When companies truly understand how emotions influence beliefs and behaviors, marry it with data and analytics in context, and apply it across and throughout their business, they will succeed and grow.

I use a broad spectrum of creative ways to understand what really motivates beliefs and behaviors toward brands and organizations to develop better strategy that solves the business problem.

Specialties

Planning, brand & marketing strategy, positioning, qualitative research, ideation facilitation & co-creation, portfolio strategy, innovation training
