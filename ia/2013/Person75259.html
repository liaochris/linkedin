Owner/Designer/Developer

KJV Designs

January 2008 – Present (5 years 9 months)

My Name is Kevin Vreeburg and I am a freelance Web Designer living in the bay area. I started KJV Designs in January of 2008 and I like to focus on the personability side of business, where I work face to face with the client to reach their ultimate Web Design, Graphic Design or Flash Development needs.

After earning my Bachelors Degree in Business Management from Notre Dame de Namur University I decided that I wanted to be a designer. I joined a design school based out of New York City called Sessions and enrolled in the Web Design Master’s Certificate program. I completed the two-year Web Design Master’s Certificate with concentrations in Graphic Design and Flash within fourteen months and graduated with honors. I enjoy design and working one on one with clients to assist them in providing creative solutions.
