Justin Archer's Summary

I have been working in the health and fitness industry for nearly a decade now. And in that time I've managed a health food store, consulted for gyms on exercise equipment, started my own personal training business, and helped teach hundreds of people how to get themselves out of pain.

I continue helping those in pain, only now online. I hope to grow online exercise therapy as a business and lead the way in this new and exciting line of work.

Specialties

sales, management, organization skills, computer skills, health and fitness, personal coach, health analysis, nutrition, fitness equipment consulting, branding and identity, basic website and blog design, building systems for greater efficiency, postural evaluation, postural exercise therapy
