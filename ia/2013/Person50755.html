Glenn Torrez's Summary

Glenn Torrez has been a California licensed General Contractor since 1988. He owned and operated GT Construction from 1988-1996. He sold GT Construction in 1996 and he and his family relocated to San Diego, where Glenn worked as a Project Manager for Nielsen Dillingham Builders. While working at NDB he was assigned to manage the Sempra Energy Utilities account. Glenn has been able to maintain a relationship with Sempra Energy which has led him to where he is today. In February of 2005, Glenn formed PRAVA Construction Services, Inc., serving Sempra Energy, San Diego Gas & Electric and Southern California Gas Company. Providing his clients with Construction Management Services and Commercial General Contracting Services.

Specialties

Commercial Ground Up Construction, Tenant Improvements, Design Assist, Design Build, Construction Management
