Beverly Kingsley AIAM, LPRT's Summary

* Senior Programs Benefit Advisor
* Health Insurance Professional
* Employee Benefit Broker & Consultant

Beginning in the late 90's, I have dedicated my professional efforts to consulting seniors, individuals and small businesses by identify benefit programs that best meet each client's unique goals and objectives. I am passionate about guiding my clients as we identify product needs and financial parameters through the selection process and implementation of each plan. Understanding the importance of ongoing service, support and personalized education, I always strive to exceed my clients' expectations.

After founding Benfit Alliance in 2010 and most recently joining Celedinas Insurance in 2013 I have shifted my education, certification and consulting primarliy towards the senior market, specializing in Medicare Supplements, Advantage Plans, Part D Prescription Drug Plans, Long Term Care Insurance and Final Expense coverage. I also offer Dental, Vision and other ancillary coverages for seniors.

Ask me how I can assist you with:

* Medicare Supplements
* Advantage Plans
* Part D Prescription Drug Plans
* Long Term Care Insurance
* Final Expense coverage
* Dental Insurance for Seniors
* Vision Insurance for Seniors

Specialties:
* Consulting pre-65 clients on Medicare options available to them and the benefits of each
* Assisting Advantage & PDP clients during the Medicare Annual Election Period
* Analyze & Identify Needs and Objectives
* Audit Existing Plan Parameters, Features, Claims Experience & Cost vs. Value
* Solicit Competitive Carrier Proposals
* Negotiate Comprehensive Renewal Options
* Customized Employee Benefit Services
* Individual, Employee & Employer Education and Guidance
* HealthCare Reform Guidance & Updates
* Medicare Eduction Seminars
* Assistance with Escalated Enrollment, Billing and Claims Issue Resolution
