Helena Écija's Summary

Helena loves color, design, texture, type and all strokes in between. She doesn't walk out of her house without her Moleskine sketchbook, anything around her could be the inspiration for the next assignment. Always thinking of new projects, she likes to mingle with other colleagues and join design and illustration challenges.

Specialties

Product conceptualization and development, Illustration, Graphic design, Communication with China, English and Spanish languages, etc.
