Laurie Banks' Summary

Clients hire us to grow their business opportunities and increase revenues. Our management team have all owned businesses so we intimately understand your business perspectives. Thanks to 25 years of success we've developed specialized expertise in Technology, Finance, Logistics, Supply Chain, Direct Response, Telecommunications, Manufacturing and Economic Development.
