Carrie Winslow's Summary

I have over nineteen successful years recruiting insurance executives for national/international companies. My candidates have been hired at many levels for insurance operations and finance, customer service, nurse case managers, vocational rehabilitation counselors and claim analysts/managers/directors/vice presidents. Consulting firms have also utilized my services. Although specialized in the disability/life insurance arenas, I am not limited to any one area of recruiting and enjoy new challenges.

My goal is to offer my hiring clients the absolute best talent for their needs so when they hire candidates from me, it is a long-term, beneficial relationship for everyone involved. I take the time to know who my clients are looking for and where my candidates have been for the best matches! A resume is a nice outline of a person's work experience, but it doesn't tell what their peers think of them, what former employees felt about their management style, etc. That information tells you WHO a candidate is and not just WHAT a candidate has done and where he/she has been employed.

Since most of my candidates are referrals from people I have worked with or for in the past, I am privy to many details about a candidate that most recruiters wouldn't have. Better yet, I don't have to ask for details because referrals always tell me why they think highly of a candidate when they connect us!

Training to be an Executive Recruiter occurred in a Management Recruiting office in Omaha, NE. I was very lucky to be in an office that was one of the top ten offices of about 700 offices nationally. Being able to work with top recruiters was the best training I could have hoped for and it has served me well for many years!

Specialties: Relationship building, skilled negotiating, grammar specialist for resume reviews, networking, and truly caring for clients and candidates keeps my work exciting and fresh!
