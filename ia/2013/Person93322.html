Michele McConomy's Summary

Michele is an experienced leader in change management and innovation, focusing on impacting corporate performance and profitability through the creation of value generating innovation programs. She is responsible for helping global organizations develop and scale their innovation programs by focusing on enabling the right strategy, process, and technology to power innovation and value creation. With diverse knowledge and expertise, Michele has over 12 years of experience in innovation management, strategy and operations, change management, and business process improvement. Currently, Michele is working with global organizations in the industries of Entertainment, Media & Communications, Consumer & Retail, Financial Services, Higher Education, Energy, and Industrial Products to maximize their innovation capabilities and develop robust innovation programs.

Mindjet

September 2013 – Present (4 months) pleasanton, ca

Leading an experienced team of innovation strategy, process, and challenge/community management experts, I am responsible for overseeing a multi-million dollar portfolio of Innovation services and consulting engagements spanning Spigit's global clients within the US. Our teams work with clients to create an Innovation ecosystem that aligns business strategy, innovation process management, and social technology to drive growth, disruption, efficiency, and ultimately a steady source of new value throughout the enterprise. Spigit's Innovation Services are designed to drive our clients to success as they set out on their Innovation journey, leveraging our best practices and the vast experience of our team to guide them through the pitfalls and best practices of establishing, sustaining, and growing a successful social innovation program.

Along with client experience and program delivery responsibilities, I am also responsible for driving new business development of Innovation Services within the US at Spigit. Recently, I have been tasked with leading our elite team with the continued evolution of Spigit's Innovation Challenge and Program Management methodologies, our proprietary methodologies used to run successful innovation challenge initiatives through to development of robust enterprise innovation programs for many of our Spigit's clients.

Representative clients: Intel, JP Morgan Chase, Boeing Employee Credit Union, Warner Brothers, Bridgepoint Education, DuPont, QVC and many others.
