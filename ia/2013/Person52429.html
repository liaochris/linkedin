Founder

Built On Respect

Nonprofit; Myself Only; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

August 2008 – Present (5 years 5 months)

Built on Respect has been a mantra for alt-culture personality heidiminx for years. It continues to be her driving mission, "Treating people with respect, and sewing the seeds of fairness creates solidarity"

Minx has started Built On Respect to help Tibetan refugees in exile. She spent three years traveling between NYC and Dharamsala, India to teach sustainable business skills to small youth-run non-profits and individuals that are making a difference in their communities.

Concentrating on positive youth action, she works to fight cultural genocide, ageism, discrimination and raise social and political awareness.

Minx uses journalism -- combining blogs, videos, interviews with musicians, and photos on a regular basis to break down the barriers of distance and truly show support in action. She currently writes for InkedMag.com, The Huffington Post (Impact), AMP Magazine, and has also written for International Campaign for Tibet, the Tibet House (US), Shambhala Sun, and MTV Norway.

In June, 2011 Built on Respect formed a Scholarly Partnership with Rutger's University's Center for the Study of Genocide, Conflict Resolution, and Human Rights.
