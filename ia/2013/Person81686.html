Lisa Salem's Summary

I am an accomplished administrative professional, turned small business owner. My company provides remote executive administrative support and transcription services to businesses and professionals looking for a flexible, convenient, alternative solution to getting their administrative needs met without the hassle and high cost of hiring an on-site full-time, part-time, or temporary employee.

My passion lies in developing on-going business relationships with clients and providing "right-hand" support to help them manage the day to day details of their busy lives. My intention is to become a valuable asset and resource, while building a solid partnership that will contribute to mutual business growth, stability, and success.

My support services focus on the following key areas: Executive Support Services, Administrative Support Services, MS Office Document Services, Concierge Services, Office Management Services, and Transcription Services (General, Legal, and Healthcare Services).

I also provide Business Relationships Management services to help you stay in touch with clients and prospects - direct mail services, postcard mailings, greeting cards/gift services, follow up, etc.

Many corporations are exploring outsourcing their administrative functions and are finding working with an independent service provider to be an ideal alternative staffing solution as it reduces labor and overhead,costs, and provides them with staff scalability during business fluctuation periods for better cost control and support stability.

Services are offered on a short-term, long-term, or per project basis.

Specialties: Executive Administrative Support, Calendar/Schedule Management, Transcription, Travel Arrangements, Concierge Support, Office Management, Meeting Coordination, Event Planning, Voice Mail Monitoring, Email Management, Contacts Database Maintenance, Word Processing, Spreadsheets, PowerPoint Presentations, Labels Creation, Mailing Services, and more.
