Kris Camealy's Summary

Kris is a freelance writer with a passion for using her words to encourage, inspire, and lead others. She maintains an active blog while serving in her home as a homeschooling mother of four, and in her church, as a MOPS Coordinator. Kris also serves as an advocate for Compassion International which seeks to set children free from poverty in Jesus's name.
