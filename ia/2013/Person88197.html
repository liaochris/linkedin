Mary Schmid's Summary

Recognizing that the key to change begins with deeply engrained thoughts, attitudes and beliefs that come from within, Mary Schmid founded Your Brilliant You coaching services. Your Brilliant You specializes in coaching business professionals and entrepreneurs to develop the awareness, strategies, and skills they need to be successful.
