Laura Lambeth's Summary

Laura Lambeth has worked in student housing since 2007 at small and large institutions, including public and for-profit (proprietary) sectors. She has held roles that included selection, training, supervision, and evalutation of 4-22 student staff in addition to student conduct management, facility managment, crisis response, professional staff recruitment, and collaboration with various campus partners.

She completed a Master of Education degree in Higher Education Administration at Texas Tech University in 2012, successfully defending a thesis on faith and sexual identity formation in gay, lesbian, bisexual, and transgender college students at faith-based institutions.
