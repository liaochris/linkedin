Mark Soper's Summary

I’m a Javascript developer, focused on consumer web & mobile products with rich-client HTML5 interfaces and Node.js services. Two years ago I shut down Rally - a social plans/events startup I founded in Cambridge, MA - and made a shift from running my own startup to helping others succeed with theirs. During that time I’ve done full-time Javascript development work to help four early-stage, venture-backed startup teams design and develop their initial products: Aquto, Connected Sports, Shopximity (now Swoop), and Kibits. And I’ve helped several other startup teams through advisory or part-time contributions.

I look for software development opportunities where I can put into play both of my core strengths: 1) building architectural-grade Javascript for both apps and services in a startup environment, 2) using my startup CEO and CTO background, experience wearing design, product marketing, and technology strategy hats to make a founder-level leadership contribution to the team.
