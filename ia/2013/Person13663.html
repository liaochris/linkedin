Beth Hill-Skinner's Summary

"I've been able to combine my fields of study in international politics, religion, and transnational terrorism, at the most opportune time in history." Beth Hill-Skinner

I am a resource for busy executives, thought-leaders, military trainers, special interest groups, and others who need information in an easy to understand format when addressing a range of complex global issues.

I created my company, Out of the Box International®, to serve both private and public needs and to foster better understanding of international and cultural issues that are having a significant impact in today's globalized world. As my business grows it is exciting to reach an ever-widening audience with new tools, techniques, and the necessary information to fully equip those who want to understand the complexities of 21st century business, politics, and geo-strategic realities.

Never has there been a greater need for business leaders, professionals, and all Americans to understand foreign affairs, issues of globalization, other cultures, along with the realities of the international game board. Ever-changing geo-political concerns have significant implications for multi-national companies, NGO's, private interest groups and the voting public. And companies that operate solely in the U.S. must likewise be prepared to train a diverse sector of employees and to serve a culturally diverse clientele.

When you, your corporation, or your group need to get up-to-speed, on what is going on in the world, I will tailor your request to your specific needs to ensure your success. I'll do the work so that you and your team are prepared. I'll do the research, so that you reap the benefits. And I'll do the training to see that your company is well positioned and ready to capitalize on opportunity. I look forward to speaking to you about your needs. ~ Beth
