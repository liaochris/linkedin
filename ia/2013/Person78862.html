Laura Mannes' Summary

I produced tv commericals for a big ad agency in the 80's--and have been producing interiors for the past 12 years. Advertising to interior design? Very similar skill set, actually. Include the antiques business I owned in the 90's which organically segued into a full service interior design business-and you've got the full trajectory.
I bring my extensive knowledge of design styles, furniture, art and antiquities to every project- residential or commercial. Laura Mannes Design masters the mix by combining design styles in a clean, cohesive, thoughtful, completely modern and fresh way.
