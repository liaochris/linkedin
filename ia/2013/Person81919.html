Liz Guthridge's Summary

Messaging. Stakeholder engagement. Behavior change.

Leaders hire me to help them implement strategic initiatives when they're experiencing gaps between words and actions.

By working with me, leaders and their teams increase their clarity, build stakeholder support and get themselves and others to take appropriate actions that boost their performance.

As a result, leaders, their work teams and stakeholders improve their focus and build unity. They also work faster with more discipline and confidence, which helps leaders build their credibility. Leaders achieve their goals, staying on schedule and budget.

People say they appreciate working with me because of my objective perspective that’s free of blind spots, echo chambers and negativity. I also receive kudos for my listening, thoughtful questions, and as requested, creative, practical and results-oriented ideas and execution.

In working with my clients, I draw on my expertise in:
• Change management and leadership
• Strategic communication, including lean communications®
• Brain-based coaching using principles of neuroscience and neuroleadership
• Behavior design
• Measurement.
