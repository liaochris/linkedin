Senior Art Director

In my current role as Senior Art Director for Summerfield Advertising I help the organization with strategy, branding, print collateral, interface design, marketing and execution of mobile and internet initiatives as a part of the creative team.

Senior Designer

Oxiem Brand Interactions

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

February 2011 – January 2013 (2 years) Dublin, Ohio

I assumed the role of Senior Designer at Oxiem Interactive in 2011 where I was able to grow my expertise in functional and needs assessment, interactive strategy, user interface design, information architecture, and translating brands online. That lead to overseeing larger aspects of the digital process, including estimating projects, strategy, internal process, hiring and mentoring designers and production artists. My work demonstrated quantitative and qualitative success in developing local, regional and global print collateral, web videos, interactive and environmental advertising, new product launches, creation and repositioning of brands and corporate identities. Thus gaining the firm multiple awards including Interactive Agency of the Year by the 2012 Ohio Interactive Awards.

As a key member of the design/marketing team I worked closely with a large group of public relations, marketing, SEO, social media relations and c-level professionals on a daily basis to deliver our clients marketing goals.
