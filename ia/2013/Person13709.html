Beth Greer's Summary

I’m an award-winning journalist, environmental health consultant, holistic wellness educator, and Certified Build It Green® Healthy Home Specialist. My bestselling book, “Super Natural Home: Improve Your Health, Home and Planet…One Room at a Time” (Rodale), a Books for a Better Life Award Finalist, is endorsed by Deepak Chopra, Dr. Joe Mercola, Ralph Nader, etc. My work has touched the lives of thousands through my writing, syndicated radio show, consulting programs and live events.

I am former President and Co-Owner of The Learning Annex, an acclaimed national adult education company. I currently design unique, “Working Healthy” holistic wellness programs for corporations, and offer in-home consultations. I’m a columnist for The Huffington Post as well as a popular media guest, having appeared on ABC, NBC and CNN and on national and local radio shows across the US. I speak at industry expos, corporations, spas, health centers and schools.

I offer a tremendous array of valuable strategies, insights, techniques and proven methods to radically transform your health and vitality. I eliminated a tumor in my chest without drugs or surgery by making small shifts in my diet, and the products I use in my home.

Specialties: Writing, consulting, holistic corporate wellness programs, public speaking, webinars, workshops, healthy home detox audits that protect people from invisible toxins in their homes that can cause fatigue, allergies, asthma, and other health problems..

I design unique corporate wellness program that improve the overall health and well-being of employees. It is founded on the belief that when people feel healthier, more rested, more fit, and more energetic they will be more engaged, more productive, happier employees who are more likely to take fewer sick days and spend less money on medical benefits. I bring in a team of natural and integrative health practitioners, naturopathic physicians, and scientists who offer cutting edge ideas on food and nutrition, stress and sleep, exercise and mind/body, and home/office environmental health.

In addition, my Working Healthy program uses a personalized measurement tool called BIA (Bio-Impedance Analysis), a non-invasive and reliable method of quantifying body composition and metabolic status. Participants are also coached in HeartMath, a scientifically proven biofeedback technique that helps reduce stress.
