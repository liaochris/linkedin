Rebecca Burley's Summary

“Our mission is creating vitality, joy, comfort and peace. We encourage clients to pursue then realize the life they desire and goals they set by crafting a space complimentary to their unique life or work style choices.”

A Spacious Place™ has aimed to achieve this mission since 2006. That's when founder Rebecca Burley began helping clients in their homes. After only one year, the business expanded to also serve entrepreneurs and small to mid size businesses.

For the past four years A Spacious Place™ has provided personal, professional and effective services and results. We believe in helping people and businesses achieve their full potential through organization in the home and office. The organizing process does not need to be overwhelming and unpleasant. We aim to bring peace and structure (as well as fun) to the experience, reducing stress.

A Spacious Place™ is a member of NAPO (The National Association of Professional Organizers) and an active member of NAPO-New England. Founder Rebecca Burley served on NAPO-New England's Board from 2008 to 2011 and recently became NAPO-New England's chapter President. She also is an involved member of local chambers and business organizations.

In addition to directly helping clients through one on one organizing assistance, A Spacious Place™ has taught several workshops for the local business chamber, Councils on Aging and A Week of Self Discovery for Girls. This fulfills the primary objective of A Spacious Place™: to teach others to be organized and reach their full potential.. Contact us today to begin the journey!
