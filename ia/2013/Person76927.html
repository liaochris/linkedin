Kristen Day's Summary

I am a instructional designer, technical writer and website developer. My goal in any position is to create upward mobility for my end users. I understand that everyone on a team is part of a bigger picture and purpose. If my work can help others grow, we all move forward.

Instructional design and websites that place equal importance on attractive visuals and informative content are my specialty. I enjoy every aspect of the instructional design and website development life cycle. It's exciting to help a client bring ideas to life with a well-built training program or web presence.

Throughout my career I've had a mission to help others understand technology. To me, end user manuals or detailed technical documentation are fun to produce! When I train an end user and see that, "Wow! I understand this!" look on their face I know my efforts have made a difference.

Specialties: E-learning, LMS, Articulate Storyline, Adobe Captivate, Camtasia, Technical Writing, Knowledge Bases, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, jQuery, CMS, WordPress, Drupal, Joomla, Website Design, Photoshop, Photo Restoration, SEO, PPC
