Robert Knijnenburg ✔'s Summary

Engineeringsupport.eu is a trademark of the Computer Design Company B.V.

We are specialised in providing support for engineering projects & applications. Members of our team can be integrated in your project teams supporting your engineering projects. They will follow your standard procedures and practises in their daily support role.

Besides engineering services we offer complete database management solutions for various engineering applications. Starting with online user support, development of databases, interfaces between various engineering applications, creation of guides and reports purpose build for each requirement.

We are providing these services for various engineering databases applications like INtools, Smartplant Instrumentation (SPI), Smartplant Electrical(SPEL), Smartplant P&ID(SPPID), Smartplant 3D (SP3D) and various in house developed purpose build MS-Access applications.

Currently we are providing Engineeringsupport for various large scale EPC projects, for example the SATORP project we are participating in the Total/Aramco core PMC team. Our company daily activities consist of the management and support of the Smartplant Instrumentation hosted database. This SPI database is used to generate all the main instrument deliverables by 12 worldwide EPC offices.

Engineeringsupport.eu has developed all Infomaker reports including revision controlled reporting, interfaces to other databases. We will take care of all the imports including the wiring equipment of the control system supplier (125.000+ IO). We provide support to all the engineering office's during their daily engineering activities in Smartplant Instrumentation.
