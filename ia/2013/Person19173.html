Brenda Zamzow-Frazier's Summary

Founder of The Zamzow Group, Inc., an accounting services firm providing seasoned financial and accounting professionals to client companies for special projects, interim staffing, temp-to-perm and direct hire positions. My extensive experience building, strengthening and leading the financial function for corporations in various stages has propelled The Zamzow Group to one of the leading consulting firms in Southern California. We are proud to be among the Top 100 Women-Owned Businesses in Los Angeles.

In 2008, The Zamzow Group became certified by the Women's Business Enterprise National Council (WBENC), one of the most widely recognized and respected certifications in the nation.

Prior to founding The Zamzow Group, I was a financial consultant for Sony Pictures, SVP of Finance at Internet Directories USA, and served as COO/CFO of Creative Intelligence, Inc. I spent 10 years at Fox Television, highlighted by my work as VP & General Manager of FoxStar Productions.

Since founding the company in 2003, we've cultivated an impressive list of clientele including Sony Pictures, Warner Bros., Ernst & Young, & So Cal Edison. We cater to many middle-market companies and not-for-profit organizations in the Greater LA area. I am currently the Chief Financial Officer for the Girl Scouts of Greater Los Angeles.

I currently chair the Ernst & Young Alumni Association, and am a member of the Finance Committee of the Alzheimer's Association. I'm also a past President of the LA Junior Chamber of Commerce and the Angeles Girl Scout Council. I'm an active member in several community and professional organizations, including NAWBO, the LA Area Chamber of Commerce, Cal CPA Society, ProVisors, and Executive Women’s Golf Association.

A native of Wisconsin, I earned my B.A. degree in Accounting from the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire and am a CPA (inactive) in the State of California.
