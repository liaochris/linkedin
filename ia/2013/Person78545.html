Lars Kjær Dideriksen's Publications

  • First You Close Your Eyes (Documentary film)

    • Sound Of Perpetual Astonishment
    • November 3, 2012
    Authors: Lars Kjær Dideriksen

    DOCUMENTARY FILM: Danish singer Esther Maria travels to New York with her musical partner Torsten Stistrup Cubel. They must record an entire album with American producer Kramer in just five days.
    In the film we hear Esther Maria's thoughts about the subject matter of her songs and how she tries to reach the right frame of mind for the project. Kramer shares his philosophy on the creative proces and how he experienced working with her. We follow these three creative souls as Esther Maria's debut album "The Abyss" starts to take shape in a small flat in Tribeca.
    The music itself and the urban landscape of New York City play their own part in the film alongside the three musicians.
    Film website: http://www.larsdideriksen.com/firstyoucloseyoureyes

  • Working Lab Greenland (Documentary film)

    DOCUMENTARY FILM: The half hour documentary film follows lecturers from Aarhus, Denmark, doing a project on social pedagogy and theatre in Greenland.
