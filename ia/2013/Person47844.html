George Birbilis' Summary

Born in Ioannina, Greece, at 1975. Got engineering diploma at Computer Engineering & Informatics Department of Patras University.

While being at the university, started working at RA-CTI (www.cti.gr) and later on moved to its Athens branch. Left RA-CTI after some years of work on E-Slate educational microworlds platform (e-slate.cti.gr) and various other projects (including EU and Greek goverment funded ones).

Since then has been working as a freelance consultant and programmer/architect with several companies and institutions in Greece and as a technical reviewer for international book publishers. For several years now also being an independent sofware vendor, providing mostly software components via Kagi's (www.kagi.com) online services.

Apart from a freelancer, is currently a PhD student on "MultiAgent Robotics and their applications on highly redundant manipulators and mobile robot swarms" at the Mechanical Enginnering and Aeronautics Department at the University of Patras (www.mech.upatras.gr/~Robotics, www.mech.upatras.gr/~robgroup).

Work and community presence recognized by major companies like Microsoft (Most Valuable Professional - MVP Visual Developer J# 2004-today, also alpha/beta tester), Borland (Spirit of Delphi award at BorCon 2001) and Apple (alpha/beta tester).

Visit www.zoomicon.com
