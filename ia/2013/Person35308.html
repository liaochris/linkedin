Diane Rosen's Experience

Owner

Diane Rosen Interiors

January 2008 – Present (6 years) Greater St. Louis Area

With 20 plus years of experience, Diane Rosen Interiors brings to her clients a team of professionally certified and accredited architects, builders and installers to meet your projects needs.

Be it new construction, renovating or a refreshing update, we start with the Discovery Questionnaire and discuss your vision, wants and needs along with your budget considerations. We come to know your style and personality, discuss any future needs such as aging in place or special limitations.

Together we create a complete plan for you to view and review the scope of the work involved, working in a timely manor that we both know and understand. Let us deal with the hundreds of details and reduce your stress and worry, saving both time and money.

This is all about you; focusing on you and your family, your office, your home and/ or vacation retreat to create a beautiful, yet energy efficient, environmentally friendly space that you can call your own.
