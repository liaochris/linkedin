Craig Lowder (LION)'s Summary

ABOUT CRAIG LOWDER

I am a global sales and marketing leader with 23 years of experience propelling revenue growth and exceeding sales goals for start-up and established product and services organizations in the B2B, B2C, non-profit, and government sectors.

My expertise is in building sales teams capable of identifying & capitalizing on new sales opportunities and clearly communicating value propositions to diverse audiences. I am a strong relationship-builder, with an excellent work ethic and board-room level communication, analytical and conflict resolution skills.

HOW I CAN ADD VALUE TO YOUR COMPANY

With my extensive sales and marketing background I can help your company profitably increase sales as your part-time CSO, to develop and execute a sales strategy, develop sales process and performance management systems, and ensure sales execution.

MY ACCOMPLISHMENTS INCLUDE:

Of my accomplishments, a few of the things I am most proudest are:

* Increased annual sales from 22% to 142% for five companies.
* Grew revenues 157%, increased gross margins by 32% and improved profits to 8.8% (from -7.8%) of sales over a 3-year period as General Manager of a business restart.
* Successfully launched three new companies / business units & four new products.
* Led business units with sales budgets to $175 million and staff to 51 employees.

WHAT I AM LOOKING FOR

I am looking for a select few visionary business owners that need an accomplished strategic sales leader to realize their vision. I am well-suited to the following industries: Business Products & Services, Chemicals, Consumer Products, Financial Services, Government Sales, Healthcare, Information Technology, Manufacturing, Professional Services, Real Estate, Retail, Software, Telecommunications, Wholesale Distribution.
