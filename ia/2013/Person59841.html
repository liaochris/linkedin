Jay Hargis, MSHR's Summary

Organizational leader focused on aligning organizational development strategies with organizational goals and objectives. Skilled at rapid development and execution of change initiatives. Professionally focused on leadership development, organizational effectiveness, talent management, executive coaching and HR technology alignment.
