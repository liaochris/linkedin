Landon Howell's Summary

I'm Head of Strategy & Content for Fancred, a Boston sports technology startup. I joined the team in November 2012 as the first non-founder hire.

Fancred is a social platform that lets fans build their sports credibility. In December 2012, just two weeks after received seed funding and weeks ahead of our scheduled launch, Fancred was named by the Boston Business Journal one of five startups to follow.

Our app debuted in the Apple App Store in March 2013 and within 16 hours shot to #23 in the Apple App Store for free sports apps.

Fancred was a part of the 2013 Spring class at TechStars Boston.
