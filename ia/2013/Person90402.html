Clara Carter's Experience

President/Owner

Multi-Cultural Convention Services Network

November 2004 – Present (9 years 2 months) Based in San Diego, CA

The Multi-Cultural Convention Services Network (MCCSN) is a for-profit San Diego-based destination/meeting management company, focused on global site selection and contract negotiations for associations, corporations and leisure travel groups. We source the best hotels offering the best available group rates globally - all at NO COST to our clients.
What makes us unique is that we offer customized services to fit the needs of our clients' meeting, convention or travel needs, saving them money and time to concentrate on their core meeting needs. In addition, MCCSN is the only independent meeting management company in San Diego County that has fully realized the significance of responding to the needs of an often-underserved segment of the market - the ethnic conference and its attendees.

“Our goal is to ensure that our visitors have the triple “E” effect— Experiences that Exceed their Expectations.”
