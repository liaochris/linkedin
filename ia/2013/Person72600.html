Kate Ausburn's Summary

I am a writer, photographer, researcher and current postgraduate journalism student at UTS.

My writing has appeared in various publications including New Matilda, the Newcastle Herald, and Overland blog, My photographs has been published in the Sydney Morning Herald, Northern Daily Leader, ABC online, The Conversation and used by NGOs including Greenpeace Australia-Pacific and The Wilderness Society. My video footage has been used in news reports on NBN and Prime7.

My professional experience extends to managing websites, blogs, and social media content, as well as providing administrative support at team and managerial levels.
