Julie Hopkins' Summary

I create memories for people through my photography and give my clients artwork, that can become family heirlooms, as a result of our photography sessions. I was a professional ballet dancer for thirteen years. My love of photography along with a dance career, that spanned the globe, a degree in art history from Loyola Marymount University, and a passion for art, has developed my discerning eye for beauty. I hold a certificate in photography from Santa Monica College and I am published in Vogue magazine, Dance Magazine, Pointe magazine, and Back Stage (west).
