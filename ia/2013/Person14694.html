Miljöparkering.se

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

October 2011 – Present (1 year 11 months)

This site helps citizens of Malmö to avoid distinct types of parking fines, and is currently saving the users between €31.000 ($40.000) and €94.000 ($125.000) worth of potential fines every month. The site also serves as place to test new technology and patterns, before introducing them to customers.

Technology background: Behind the simple user interface you'll find a .Net CQRS+EventSourcing based solution, wrapped by an architecture based on Event Driven Architecture principles, implemented in NServiceBus 3.0.

All flow control is implemented based on commands and asynchronous handling of events, combined with Sagas (long running processes) and time-bound events.

The website (frontend) is built using ASP.NET MVC 4, twitter bootstrap, javascript, jquery, knockoutjs and signalr.
