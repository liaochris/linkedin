chief sound bender

BENDYmusic

February 2004 – Present (9 years 6 months)

Composer / Sound Designer for film, advertising, interactive and television. Andy formed Bendy in 2004 after gaining over 12 years of experience as a composer / sound designer at top music and sound facilities (Soundstorm, tomandandy, CB+Co. & Mike Recording). Andy is at home working in various capacities on a variety of projects including film, commercial, television, radio, album release, web and interactive. His musical and design attributes are chameleon like, adapting and enforcing the mood and message of the work that it accompanies. Stylistically, his work is unlimited, there is no genre, region, or sound that he won’t explore. Record, mix, re-mix, original music, sound design, foley, ADR and VO for commercials, documentaries, television, short and feature length film. AMP member company (Andy was West Coast President of the Association of Music Producers from 2006 to 2008 and currently sits on the national board of AMP), Apple Consultants Network / Apple Certified Technical Coordinator (ACTC), AFM, SAG, AFTRA, Motion Picture Editors Guild (Local 700)
