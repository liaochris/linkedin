Shari Morgan's Experience

Chef/Owner

Saucy Shallot Personal Chef Service

June 2007 – Present (6 years 7 months)

As a Personal Chef I help families gather around the table for meals, allowing singles to eat healthier, and couples to enjoy quality time while enjoying food they love. Busy, time-starved families hire a Personal Chef in order to eat better and enjoy food prepared just for them using the freshest ingredients possible and adding just the right amount of spice. First, I complete a personal consultation with my clients to customize meals, determine dinner time favorites, discuss allergies, other health issues and dislikes, and then shop for the freshest ingredients. After shopping, I cook delicious meals in the homes of my clients, package the meals according to their preferences and provide complete re-heating instructions allowing them to eat the meals at their convenience. Also, available are Dinner Parties, Romantic Dinners, Cooking Classes (group or private), cooking demos and meal planning.
