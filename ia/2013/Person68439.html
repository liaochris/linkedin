Jorik Lommen's Summary

I'm a young marketeer with a passion for marketing and a keen interest in developments in the field of Social Media and major brands that build experiences for their customers. Currently I'm working at adidas Global Sports Marketing as a Trainee, this is where my passion for marketing and sports comes together.

I always love a challenge and therefore I started my own company during my study. I focused on providing small and medium businesses with advise about how to use social media. During this time I learned that I really like working with people and love to combine both strategic thinking and the execution of plans.

Specialism: Presenting, creative-mind, customer-friendly, driven, easy-going, social media interest, reliable and love to learn.
