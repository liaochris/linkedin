Margaret (Maggie) Huft's Summary

I've worked as an illustrator and graphic artist for over 20 years, for various companies. Now I am self-employed; and actively seeking work in publishing (children's books, new age books, fantasy books, and am open to others that fit my art style) and in licensing. I have one children's book published. I also have art on display in several venues in my area. And I am a member of the local art league, where I recently served as secretary for two years.

Specialties: Black and white drawing (pencil or ink), Watercolor and watercolor over ink drawings, Acrylic and acrylic airbrush.
Also, Photoshop, Illustrator, DreamWeaver and InDesign.
