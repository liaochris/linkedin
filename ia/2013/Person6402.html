  • Which College is Right for Your Teen?

    • UT San Diego
    • September 2, 2013
    Authors: Andrea Frimmer

    The most important question to ask when considering colleges is "Will my teenager be both comfortable and challenged there?" After answering this initial question, there are then three kinds of college environments to think about: the physical environment, the academic environment and the social/cultural environment. Other considerations include your teen's chances of acceptance and the annual cost after financial aid. Apply to 10 to12 schools with the list broken down into 2 to 3 reach schools, 5 to 6 target schools and 2 to 3 safety schools.
