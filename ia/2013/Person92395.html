Michael Gass' Summary

Michael Gass, is an international new business consultant to advertising, digital, media and PR agencies. Since 2007, he has led in the use of social media and content marketing strategies to make agency new business EASIER.

Michael has worked face-to-face with well over 150+ agencies in almost all 50 states here in the U.S. and with agencies from over 14 foreign countries.

He is the founder of Fuel Lines, which has been rated among the top 100 marketing blogs in the world, according to Ad Age’s Power 150.

Michael's entire career in the advertising industry has been spent in business development. After speaking and writing about best new business practices from his own experience and requests from numerous agencies for new business advice, he decided to launch his own consultancy. That was back in 2007, the beginning of The Great Recession. Social media was still in its infancy but he saw its potential for new business. He created his blog Fuel Lines and began to write helpful business development articles. The blog quickly grew in popularity and was key to building his consultancy into an international practice incredibly from his home-base in Alabaster, Alabama.

Along with providing consulting services for individual agencies, Michael also speaks at events and conducts training for agency groups such as the 4A’s, Mirren New Business, TAAN, The Magnet Global Network, MCAN, DMA and the American Advertising Federation.

“Michael Gass is one of the country’s foremost authorities on using social media. We’ve hired Michael to run his seminar in various cities and his evaluations from the seminar participants have been extremely high. I’ve had the pleasure of sitting in on one of his seminars and aside from being knowledgeable, he is a dynamic presenter. He comes highly recommended.” Bob Linden, 4A’s Vice President of Training, Education and Development
