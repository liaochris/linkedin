Andrea D. Copeland, @PositivChannels/@copelandandi's Summary

I have been working in television broadcasting since 2003, having produced and hosted hundreds of programs. Inside Nonprofits with Andrea Copeland, Speaking with Andrea and Breaking the Chains are three programs I produce and host. As well, I'm often commissioned to do one-time special productions, moderate panels and MC community events.

Positive Channels is about producing and promoting positive television that will educate and enlighten our community. I use the power of media only for good. To enlighten, inspire, and educate. As a result, I have been invited to meet and/or interview prominent local, national and even international individuals, including The Dalai Lama, and the Haitian Ambassador to the US. These opportunities and the businesses who support my programs speak volumes as to the kind of work I do at Positive Channels.

Positive Channels... Using the Power of Word for Good!
