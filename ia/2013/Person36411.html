Dolph Kawalec's Summary

I am currently employed at Mullen.

My previous position was as a Creative Director at GlobalHue, where I helped lead the Jeep brand advertising since 2010. While at GlobalHue, I created and guided successful campaigns for Jeep Wrangler and Jeep Grand Cherokee. Most notably, the annual partnerships between Jeep Wrangler and Activision’s Call of Duty video game franchise and the Winter X Games.

I have worked for such agencies as Publicis, Leo Burnett, FCB and BBDO. At BBDO, I was integral in bringing the Wrigley Gum brand out of their tradition of jingle-based advertising. I produced the first award-winning advertising for any U.S. Wrigley gum brands ever. This led to massive spikes in sales for stagnant brands such as Eclipse and Spearmint. I also co-founded BBDO Hardware, which helped garner a new creative spirit and reputation at BBDO in Chicago.

My work has been recognized by some of the top award shows and publications worldwide, including Cannes, Clios, Communication Arts, Creativity, Graphis, London International Advertising Awards, Lürzer’s International Archive, the National Addy Awards, and the Obies.

Specialties: My focus has always been on concept, but I have the tools to make anything come to life in print, broadcast, digital and ambient. The computer is a familiar tool but also an extension of my creativity, being more than proficient in InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash, Final Cut, and After Effects – as well as dabbling in many other programs like Maya and ZBrush to sate my more base creative urges.
