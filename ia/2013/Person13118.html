Ben Kinney's Summary

After only six full years in the real estate business Ben Kinney now owns and operates one of the top producing real estate teams in the nation. In addition to his real estate team Ben owns and operates multiple Keller Williams Realty Franchises that includes 300 + agents servicing over 6 counties.

Ben is a nationally recognized trainer and speaker, a top producing agent that sold over 500 homes in 2011. Ben is also ranked by the Wall Street Journal's as one of the top 100 agents for total units sold and total sales volume in the nation for 2010.

Ben Kinney serves on the BOD at Activerain Corp and is the creator and keynote speaker for Raincamp a nationally known real estate technology conference.

Ben Kinney along with the NY Times best selling author Jay Papasan wrote and published an award winning book named: Soci@l - How to attract friends, followers, and connections to your real estate business.

Most recently Ben Kinney has created the newest real estate designation and training program - IMSD (Internet Marketing Specialist Designation) which teaches agents the tools and systems needed to thrive in the toughest of markets.

When Ben is not teaching, writing about, or selling real estate he enjoys fishing Washington rivers for steelhead, playing with his golden retriever Taupo, and cooking for his friends.
