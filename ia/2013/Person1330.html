Master of Financial Technical Analysis, Financial Techncial Analysis

2012 – 2013

Completed by way of Research Report.

PC is used to reduce dimensions and employed in such a way so as to form a linear projection; used with with a moving window technique to reduce lag, the technique creates feed forward data and mitigate edge effects.

Transformed data series forms the basis which the null and alternative hypothesis may be tested which is includes the testing of a number of techncial trading rule candidates on high frequency NZ dollar data and the transformed data series.
