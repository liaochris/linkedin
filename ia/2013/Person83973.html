Andy Mackin's Summary

Mackin Consultancy has a wide range services that have being applied all across Europe. Mackin Consultancy prides it self in looking after all its clients needs both in Ireland and in Europe which gives it an edge over other Health & Safety providers. We pride ourselves in being able to understand what the client needs even if they dont, this is extremely important when you are dealing with different types of clients for the different types of industry across different Regions.

The company goal is to become a Globle brand for top class Quality Management 9001 (2008), 14001 &18001 Compliance and EHS Consultancy & Training services. Working in Contries like USA, Canada, Middle East,Australia, etc
