David Meddings' Summary

Started making seed growing boxes for my father at the age of 6, was distracted by school and college etc and now a designer and maker of all things recycled and upcycled.

Show me the material and the challenge is to convert that to a sustainable, eco friendly product.

My aim is to establish a major brand of useful, designer led items of furniture, home and garden products and commercial contracts that will stimulate others to use our resources more wisely.

Specialties

eco furniture for cafes, restaurants and eco schools, eco marketing tools, wooden boxes, traditional oak and copper signage, new Trespa furniture
