Melissa Balkon's Summary

I have over 10 years of graphic design experience working for print, direct mail, non-profit, and agency environments. Through my experience and having started Strong Design Studios early in my career, I've been able to work with clients and projects of all shapes and sizes, but my true passion is for smaller businesses and organizations who have a need for professional design, but might not otherwise be able to afford a traditional agency.

I try to remain active in the design community, and have helped put on Phoenix Design Week 2 years running, served as the Education Director for Commpose and volunteered as a design mentor at Gangplank in Chandler, AZ. When not hard at work (which is rare) you'll find me practicing my hand-lettering, boxing at the gym, crafting, working around the house or finding something to take my compulsive organization skills out on.

Melissa Balkon's Experience

Owner / Graphic Designer

Strong Design Studios

January 2003 – Present (10 years 10 months) Scottsdale, AZ

I'm the owner of Strong Design Studios, a boutique design studio which offers a refreshingly simple approach to visual design for small businesses, startups, churches, and creative agencies. Championing an individual and small organization's cause is my passion, and at Strong Design we get to do that for our clients by focusing on saving them time and money by creating enduring design that stands the test of time. For organizations who need the talent and experience of a professional designer, but you don't have the budget to work with an agency, Strong Design Studios is a perfect fit.

Clients include: Vuurr, Made Better Studio, Pixel Farmers Interactive, KEO Marketing, Weiher Creative, English/Moon, Liquis Design, JaxDesign, Studio Sedition, BeeSoBusy, Paradise Valley Church, Jacob's Well Church, United Methodist Communications, Mimi Marie Children's Fairytale Fashion, Isospin, Pyramid Financial, Panda Home Services, United Printing & Mailing, Foster Properties, MarketingPress, Elementz Interactive and many more!

Design Strategist

Forty

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Design industry

July 2010 – December 2012 (2 years 6 months) Chandler, AZ

Up until December of 2012 I worked as a Design Strategist at Forty, where I got to help our clients revolutionize their brand experience through design. My job went way further than logos and websites. I designed meaningful customer experiences by tying all of a brand’s elements together—the identity, website, collateral, interior design, and more—and worked to communicate their personality through every touchpoint. I have always had a passion for creating harmony between aesthetics and usability, and at Forty, I got to do that every day!

Clients include: Epiphany Chocolates, Impression Foot & Ankle, Vuurr, Get Lucky the book, 80sTees.com, Volta, Jacob Bromwell, Blue Sky Airport Parking and many more!
