Mike Hayes' Summary

Mike has over 25 years experience in marketing, sales and consulting with some of the world’s largest retailers and brands.

Mike’s expertise is in Customer Experience, Strategy, Insights and Relationship Management, Loyalty Marketing, Marketing Effectiveness and Omnichannel strategies. Mike helps companies improve their performance by gaining a deep understanding of their customers, opening up a 1:1 dialogue with them, providing more relevant offers / communications and fostering longer term relationships. He leverages the strategic application of advanced analyses, best in class processes, technology tools and great ideas.

Mike brings a unique and valuable perspective to his clients, having successfully led marketing, media and sales organizations through periods of transformational growth, as both a senior executive and as an outside consultant.
