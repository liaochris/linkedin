Alice Heiman's Summary

Sales Strategist, Speaker, Coach, Trainer, Author

Why should you hire me?

1. Because I believe you can have all the sales you need to be wildly successful.

2. I will show you how to generate more leads using traditional methods and social selling.

3. Selling will become easy for you and you will enjoy finding and securing new customers.

4. You will be able to clearly differentiate yourself in the market place.

5. Closing more deals will become easy.

My clients have seen 40 to 75% increase in their sales over the time I have worked with them. I have consulted with and trained sales organizations at Schneider Logistics, Coca Cola, Hewlett Packard, Install Shield, Price Waterhouse Coopers, Mercedes Benz, as well as a myriad of small companies you probably have never heard of. It's exciting to see their sales soar.

Specialties:

1) Increasing Sales: I show companies of any size how to increase sales. I provide expertise in sales strategy, sales management, sales process and sales training.

2) Networking: My passion is getting people connected online and in person. I teach salespeople and business owners how to get connected and stay connected to people with whom they can do business directly and collaborate with for mutual referrals and outstanding results. Getting connected using LinkedIn is something everyone needs to learn. I use the BizTalk Blender event to help business people connect.

3) Social Selling: I use social media to generate leads and will show you what you need to do. LinkedIn is a powerful tool and I have mastered it so that I can show you how to prospect, get connected and stay connected. I have effectively used Twitter and Facebook as well and can show you what will work and what will not in your business.

Inspiring you to make a profound difference in the way you approach sales.
