Audrey K. Chisholm, Esq.'s Summary

Audrey K. Chisholm is a Partner at Chisholm Law Firm, LLC. Prior to founding the firm, she worked for several established law firms in the business and real estate departments. She has over 10 years’ industry experience as the founder of a successful startup company, non-profit founder and as a real estate investor.

Her practice focuses on real estate, corporate, non-profit and entertainment law. She has served as lead counsel on a multitude of litigated cases, including breach of contract claims, real estate disputes, and business disputes, and has prevailed against top law firms in her field. Ms. Chisholm has successfully represented foreign real estate investors, corporations, small businesses, non-profit organizations, entrepreneurs, landlords, commercial tenants, residential tenants, buyers and sellers of commercial and residential properties in a myriad of legal matters.

Ms. Chisholm’s practice also involves a wide variety of other general corporate, business and commercial matters for businesses at all stages of development, including entity structuring and formation matters, traditional and non-traditional financing arrangements, licensing arrangements, executive employment and other business agreements and arrangements. Her practice also extends to counseling clients on individual business and personal matters.

Specialties: Law, Nonprofit Management, Business, Real Estate, Public Speaking

Motivational Speaker & Bestselling Author

www.AudreyChisholm.com

January 2004 – Present (9 years 8 months)

Audrey K. Chisholm is an attorney, entrepreneur, national speaker and leadership expert. She was a speaker at the 2011 White House Summit on Hispanic Leadership hosted by the Obama administration. During the 2008 elections, she served on a televised panel with Senator Hillary Clinton and discussed the subprime lending real estate crisis. She was also featured in the November 2011 issue of Black Enterprise® magazine. She gave the Florida State University commencement address for an audience of over 13,000 people including the former Vice President of the United States of America. She has spoken to audiences throughout the country including businesses, nonprofits, universities and more.

She is the published author of the #1 Amazon Bestselling book in music business “Start Your Music Business™” and “Run Your Music Business™” available for purchase on www.StartYourMusicBusiness.com.
