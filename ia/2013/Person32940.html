Dawn Stebbing AICI FLC's Summary

Making lasting and authentic impressions is the goal of every business professional.

I’ve been in the beauty industry for over 22 years. I help leaders just like you make the kind of lasting and authentic impressions that elevate your image at work, home and in the community.

EXPERIENCE
• I’m one of only three certified image consultants in the state of Minnesota. AICI—The Association of Image Consulting International.
• I spent 15 years as a licensed cosmetologist.
I have 10 Years with a Skin Care, Cosmetic and Nutrition Company
• I’m trained by Stoltz Image Consulting and also studied with Aveda and the Horst Salons

WHAT I DO
What makes me unique is my ability to unveil your distinct color canvas…what already exists in you—the exact colors that best compliment your natural appearance. By working with me, you gain confidence, which sets the stage for you to go perform. Be the most powerful you. Whether you’re running your business, making a sales call, closing a deal or relaxing at the cabin.

During your personalized style appointment we’ll develop:
• Your style recipe—image descriptors that tell us how you want to be perceived. Words like classy, polished, comfortable or eccentric.
• Your style guide—a reference sheet of perfectly matched, flattering outfits you already own.
• Your shopping list—perhaps it’s the little black dress or that nice silk tie that would make your wardrobe complete. We’ll get you there, a few items at a time.
