Breandan Dezendorf's Summary

A user-oriented UNIX systems administrator with substantial experience in higher education Information Technology/Information Systems environments and proven abilities in culturally diverse settings. I am one of the few professionals I know that actively enjoys setting up monitoring and alerting systems like Nagios and Merlin. When these are tied into Graphite and Splunk, really cool things start to happen. I have significant development experience in Perl and bash, and I am learning python and ruby. I also have a lot of experience running RHEL 5 and 6 at scale, as well as managing Mac OS X. I also dabble in OpenSolaris in my free time - mostly for the magic of ZFS, though I'm looking towards Ceph as a replacement for my personal needs.

Specialties: Nagios, UNIX Systems Administration (RedHat Enterprise Linux 5 and 6, Solaris 10, and Mac OS X Server), ZFS, puppet
