Jena Rodriguez's Experience

Brand Strategist | Marketing Strategist | Coach | Visual Image Consultant | Speaker | Author

Brand With Jena

October 2007 – Present (6 years) Houston, Texas Area

Http://www.brandwithjena.com

Jena Rodriguez, Brand+Marketing Strategist/Speaker/Author, is founder of Brand With Jena, a brand and marketing coaching company that educates, elevates, and inspires women entrepreneurs in United States and Canada. Her passion is transforming their business with brand strategy, marketing, and image building expertise that will move them from so-so to WOW!

Jena helps women apply the power of branding to their business to increase profitability! She shows them why branding is THE greatest game-changer and how to AMPLIFY it for bigger results through strategic coaching, brand design and image redos. She has helped clients surpass the 6-figure mark and see increases of 600% or more in their business.

Jena has been working in brand consulting and business marketing, finance and imaging for more than 20 years. Her extensive knowledge has influenced business owners to truly understand the brand within them and catapult their success. Jena has taken many small to mid-size businesses from start-up to STAND OUT! And she can do the same for you!

Her story begins with when she worked at the headquarters of a big box brand, Express (The Limited Co.) in the fashion world. This being her first major exposure to what it truly means to create, define and sustain a brand. It is not a far jump from fashion design and merchandising to graphic design and branding. It is all about presentation, positioning, clarity and a sense of self. From there, she went into running her own freelance business as a professional makeup artist, working with individuals, brides, fashion photographers, various makeup lines (YSL, Lancome, MAC, and others), and even makeover shows on the Jenny Jones Show in Chicago!
