Duncan Stuart's Summary

Hi! I'm Duncan, and I eat complexity for breakfast.

I'm a Generalist through-and-through: none of this "not my job" nonsense - I'm supremely adaptable and experienced at cutting through complexity and ambiguity to understand your process/software/strategy problem and doing whatever needs to be done to solve it.

I can do this because I've spent 5 years in consultancy developing client-facing skills with a focus on Business analysis, while also becoming a competent web developer - primarily in Ruby-on-Rails.

I'm very happy working for dxw at the moment, but my Fridays are my own, so if you're an exciting and/or small organisation with an interesting problem that I might be able to help with, please get in touch.

--

Just to be absolutely categorically clear, I have zero interest in working for your large consultancy / city finance organisation. No, not even for pots and pots of money.
