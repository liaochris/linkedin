David Dixon's Summary

David is part of a high-powered team with SAP product development roots saving projects and delivering unmatched enterprise-scale solutions to SAP problems through product insight, expertise and innovation.

He is a featured worldwide speaker at ASUG and SAPinsider conferences (including "BPC Seminar"). He was also the only third-party expert featured at SAP's "BPC MEGA Elite" training his competitors on EPM 10.0 and the 2012 SAPPHIRE deep dive on SAP BPC and HANA. On May 15th, 2013 he co-presented with Mars at SAPPHIRE.

He co-authored the book "Mastering the SAP Business Information Warehouse" written with Bill Inmon, the "father of data warehousing," as well as white papers sponsored by Business Objects and SAP on Business Intelligence (BI) and Enterprise Performance Management (EPM). David started his career in 1995 as a Financials and Controlling (FI/CO) consultant with SAP specializing in financial consolidations, product costing and profitability analysis with particular expertise in the ERP information systems and tools.

David was an FI/CO Platinum Consultant before he left SAP in 1999 to help form an SAP BW consultancy startup that was acquired in 2004 which in turn, was acquired in 2007 for the SAP practice and culture that David built. Thereafter, David built overnight SAP BPC practices at other companies.

David is not only an executive and practicing consultant but a hands-on developer since his earliest days with SAP. His skills cross functional and technical domains as well as SAP and non-SAP. He has worked with SAP's development team in Walldorf and Palo Alto on numerous occasions in support of applications and initiatives in the EPM and BI space. He continues to have active and personal ties with SAP's product and solution organizations. He has extensive project experience in implementing complicated global solutions for Fortune 100 companies and is an internationally recognized speaker at various SAP forums and conferences.
