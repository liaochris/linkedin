Jeremy D McGowan's Summary

With nearly 25 years of operations leadership in a broad range of business sectors, both publicly and privately held corporations and in the United States Air Force, Jeremy D. McGowan is a motivational leader with a proven track record of steering organizations to the next level with his vision, practical execution, and ability to build teams across a wide spectrum of industries.

He is noted as a judicious risk taker, presenting creative and entrepreneurial solutions to challenging business issues. A catalyst and driving force behind advanced, strategic development and operational streamlining initiatives comprised of service enhancement, operational efficiency, asset protection, profit building and cost reduction methodologies.

He has an exceptional ability to forge innovative marketing strategies, build lucrative partnerships and anticipate growth across multiple sites. More importantly, he is very astute at recruiting and cultivating effective teams of highly motivated talent.

While serving in the USAF, he was attached to various deployed operating units from Joint Special Operations Command, the Drug Enforcement Agency, and the Department of Energy. While serving with the 4404th Tactical Wing (Provisional) he was responsible for conducting Vulnerability Assessments for both the Tiger Team and the Downing Commission. His duties were the direct physical inspection of various forward operating locations in the Middle East throughout Saudi Arabia and Kuwait along with the creation and presentation of vulnerability assessments and force protection reports of those sites. Through his expert performance, these assessments contributed to a directional change in US and Allied Military Operations in the Middle East.

After founding and running Ecuador's first and only alternative fuel company, Jeremy got married and had a daughter and soon moved back to the United States where he now lives in Buckhannon, WV with his wife and daughter.

Jeremy D McGowan's Patents

  • Wound Dressing Impervious To Chemical And Biological Warfare Agents

    • United States Patent 6,787,680
    • Issued September 7, 2004
    Inventors: Jeremy D McGowan

    The present invention is directed towards wound dressings impervious to chemical and biological agents therefore designed for use in chemically and biologically contaminated environments and for wound dressing designed for use in protecting attending persons from contamination of biological contagions from the wounded person, having a designated absorbent region defined by liquid resistant materials that enable the uptake and storage of bodily fluids within the wound dressing, while simultaneously containing and preventing the migration of bodily fluids from the occlusive dressing thereby preventing exposure to any contagion or pathogen that may be present in said bodily fluid.

  • Cooking Oil Transfer System

    • United States Patent Application 13555145
    • Filed July 21, 2012
    Inventors: Jeremy D McGowan, Wescott Clowdus

    A fryer oil utilization and storage system transfers fryer oil between a fryer within a structure and a storage tank located external of the structure. Adjustments to an internal pressure of the storage tank, in conjunction with an arrangement of valves can be used to control the transfer of oil between the fryer and storage tank as well as for discharging from the storage tank to a portable container. A negative pressure can be maintained within the storage tank to preclude theft. The partial vacuum retains the stored fluid within the storage tank, even when the stored fluid is subjected to an external pump. The negative pressure reduces oxidation of the oil and any particles contained therein; reduces the FFA content; and provides for a higher quality product for biodiesel and any other use.
