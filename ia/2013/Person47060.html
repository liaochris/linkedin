Gary A. Brown's Summary

I am currently running for Detroit City Council to bring a "new direction we can trust." I have been a leader in Detroit for nearly three decades which includes progressively higher leadership positions through my 26-year career at the Detroit Police Department. I have served on numerous boards and have a proven track record of being able to work with community organizations, the business and private sectors, as well as civic groups to build strategies that empower citizens to take charge of their own destinies and reclaim their neighborhoods. Together, we possess the power to become the best of Detroit.
