James P. Andersen, Jr.'s Summary

Jim is a CPA with over 30 years experience in various financial services organizations. He has played key financial management roles in a variety of start-up ventures, including a London based assignment involving initial setup and financial management operations in five countries. Jim is particularly valued for his expertise in process re-engineering and the creative use of technology to bring about administrative and financial improvements and efficiencies.

Jim founded and is a Managing Partner at Foundation Management Associates, an outsourced provider of accounting and administrative services to small non profit organizations.

Foundation Management Associates
FMA specializes in helping a non-profit's management and board eliminate the headaches associated with their accounting and bookkeeping processes. FMA does this by providing a comprehensive service, providing all their financial management needs ranging from bookkeeping to CFO, but only for the amount of time they need during the month. They also help them streamline and manage their annual audit function as well.

Small non-profits often need the services of a controller or a CFO, but can only afford a bookkeeper. This can cause constant frustration with the organization's management and board, as they often find their bookkeeper does not have the "bandwidth" to meet all their financial reporting needs. This is where FMA can help. By leveraging technology and streamlining their bookkeeping process, FMA provides their clients with a comprehensive, but cost-effective accounting service.

FMA operates behind the scenes in a virtual, paperless environment, and so they don't require the use of their client's valuable real estate space. This allows them to provide these services to anywhere and from anywhere in the world. In fact their clients have ranged from local Boston-based organizations, to a school in an out- island of the Bahamas, to a hospital located in the back country of rural Haiti.

Specialties

* Outsourced Accounting & Administrative Support Services to non profit organizations

* Outsourced accounting services in Haiti, the Caribbean and other developing countries

* Internal assistance with managing your external audit process including A-133 audits.

* Assistance with establishing simple but effective internal controls and systems in foreign locations.

* Real-time electronic documentation storage (disaster recovery) maintained in the U.S. for foreign financial transactions.
