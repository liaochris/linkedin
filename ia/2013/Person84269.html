Maher Maso's Summary

Maher Maso is currently the Mayor of Frisco, Texas. Billed as the fastest growing city in the country by the U.S. Census from 2000-2010, Frisco is a success story in municipal government. With low unemployement, excellent public safety, top-rated school districts, and billed as one of the friendliest cities in the country, Frisco is the place to be.

Mayor Maso is actively involved with all aspects of Frisco and is its number one ambassador. He serves on numerous local, regional, state and national boards and, under his leadership, Frisco continues to grow towards buildout.
