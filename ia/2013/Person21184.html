It showcased internationally recognized artists, provided a forum for new composers and arrangers, showcased individual performers and gave them the modern big band experience which is almost extinct outside of New York. The band has 4 CDS available on itunes or at jazznation.com

In addition to Festivals and Concerts, the CJO performed every Monday night at Jazz@Pearls in North Beach San Francisco where it continued until 2008 when the club closed. The band continues to perform in the Bay Area.
