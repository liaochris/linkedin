Mike Morgan's Summary

Commercial, advertising and editorial photographer based in Washington DC and Maryland.

********

My passion is creating memorable portraits and images that embody the heroism, vulnerability or humor of my subjects: evoking the sense of pride that comes from hard work, the courage of choosing to be an individual, the beauty of a well-crafted product.

I grew up on California's Central Coast and came east for college, but deeply identify with my roots: I've always felt the landscapes and solitude of places like Big Sur run through my work and have indelibly informed the way I look at the world, within the camera and without.

I very much love what I do and though I'm based in the Washington DC area, I will travel anywhere on the planet for an interesting and challenging campaign. My clients will get images that bring a fresh and creative look to their publication, pictures with an edge.

My wife Theresa and I live on Kent Island in the Chesapeake Bay with our daughter Julia. When we're not at work we can be found taking long walks together.

Specialties: Washington DC Photographer, Editorial Photographer, Commercial Photographer, Advertising Photographer, Portrait Photography, Corporate Photographer, Food Photographer, Architectural Photographer, Still Life Photographer, Still Photographer, Writer, Educator
