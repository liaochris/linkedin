Beth Browning's Summary

SEO and Inbound Marketing trainer, blogger, and consultant

I'm a transplanted Midwesterner currently living in the Greater Philadelphia area.

My particular passion is about the growing connection between search engine optimization (SEO) and social media. I love teaching and helping people understand the power of the internet and how they can make the most of their online presence to generate new leads and convert them to customers.

I have a corporate background with over a decade of Ecommerce experience, and have been working in the online space since the beginning. My experience includes Search Engine Optimization, Marketing, Customer Experience, Web Application Development Management, Web Merchandising, Website Design, Email Marketing, and Operations.
