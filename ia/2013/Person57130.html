Jefferson Akly's Summary

Jefferson Akly is an alumnus of Full Sail University, having obtained a B.S. in Music Business. He graduated with a 3.7 GPA, received Course Director Awards for Artist Management, Business Law as well as Marketing and was honored with the “Advanced Achievement Award”. Currently, he's enrolled in Full Sail’s M.S. Internet Marketing program and expected to graduate 12/2013.

Since 1999, Jeff has worked in the legal dept of the largest PRO in the U.S., ASCAP, and has received two promotions. He’s been involved with obtaining necessary documentation from publishers in order to successfully clear song counts, confirmed in ASCAP’s repertory, for use in infringement litigation. In charge of his depts’ copyright database, filing system and office administration, he’s been able to implement new processes to better streamline productivity and improve workflow, including developing training materials & training staff in the song clearance process, which is a key factor for infringement actions that bring in revenue.

As co-founder of an entertainment business he structured the company, raised brand awareness and marketing. Through this experience, his passion for marketing & branding developed. His drive to help and inspire others leads him to create a music business blog to educate all artists & music business professionals achieve success in their endeavors.

In 2011, unfortunately a friend & colleague employed his service and assistance in raising awareness for a fundraiser through series PSA videos for LCNEC (a rare lung cancer). He devised the creative approach of each PSA in a way that put the loved one’s voice and story in front of many to become aware of this rare disease. His effective techniques and creativity helped generate close to $5K.

Jeff participated in the 2013 Google Online Marketing Challenge. He led a virtual team and ran an AdWords campaign on behalf of a nonprofit. An experience he is happy he committed to.
