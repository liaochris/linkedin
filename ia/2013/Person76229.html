I am Kate Krause, founder of KK Stitches. My passion is embroidery and all of the many trends & techniques that go along with it. Don't think that you have a need for custom embroidery? You'll be suprised; the uses are endless. Check us out.

Kate Krause's Experience

Owner

KKStitches - Embroidery Services & Promotional Products

January 2007 – Present (6 years 10 months) North Carolina, USA

KKStitches is an Embroidery & Apparel Company providing exceptional customer service and attention to detail for all your embroidery needs. Embroidery is a great way to advertize, and provide advertizing as your wear your clothing. Embroidery can be done on so many items, not just clothing and hats. You can add stitches to bags, car floor mats, dog collars, garden flags, horse pads, and so much more.

KKStitches has expanded and can now provide your club, company, group, sports team, with all your promotional products, from bags, magnets, mousemats, pens, basically anything you can think of, we can put your name and logo on it!
