Aubrey Johnson's Summary

I love creating engaging User Experiences, designing elegant and interactive interfaces, and working with users to deploy the best and most immersive interactive work.

I eat sleep and breathe Photoshop/Illustrator.

I enjoy using OmniGraffle, building wireframes, and discovering how design can solve business goals.

I'm comfortable writing semantic and accessible XHTML, CSS, and integrating and customizing Javascript libraries (like JQuery).

My past experience includes running my own design company, working in an agency environment, and currently working in a fast-paced startup. I've worked with small businesses to American Express, Novartis, and Sixthman Entertainment.

My Goals are to raise the standard for how people view design and further establish its relevancy in business.

Specialties

User Experience Design, Visual Design, User Interface Design
