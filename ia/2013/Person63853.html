Jim Eltringham's Summary

As Vice President, at Advantage, Jim Eltringham crafts and executes dynamic voter contact and grassroots mobilization campaigns to deliver maximum impact for Advantage, Inc.’s clients. Eltringham has designed and implemented grassroots campaigns merging digital and offline tactics for a range of political, advocacy, and corporate clients.

Specialties: Grassroots campaigns, organization and coalition building; traditional and modern media relations; combining online activity with offline action
