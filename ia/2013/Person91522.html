Meredith Thatcher, CFM, IFMA Fellow, Prosci, LEED's Summary

30 years of success through workplace strategy and change management - essential roles to contribute to organizations providing collaborative, effective, sustainable, quality and inspiring work environments that improve the lives of occupants and advance the organizations strategic objectives. And I get to produce the occasional miracle.

I deliver IFMA competency courses in Canada and as far away as Trinidad as an authorized IFMA instructor. I enjoy being able to contribute to others achieving their CFM, FMP and SFP credentials.

Recent presentations that I have delivered, in locales near and far, include: Biomimicry: A Bold Legacy, High Heels and Hard Hats; Women in FM, Social Media Mania, Ignite YOUR Innovation; www.future.2020 Work, Worker, Workplace of the Future - Are you Ready; The 'How-to" of the IFMA Area Measure Standard; Shades of Green: Getting to Boardroom Buy-In; Risk and Resilience: Strategic Thinking in Your World; and Change, Chaos, Contrasts and Compromise - Workplace of the Future.

Specialties: Specialties include: workplace strategy, strategic analysis, sustainability, strategic planning, facility planning, functional programming, space needs analysis, space planning, defining your workplace of the future, biomimicry, functional program audit, area measure, real property advisor, workflow analysis, proximity and adjacency analysis, furniture standards, space management, integrated workplace management systems (IWMS / CAFM), training, developing and delivering presentations to audiences of 4 to 4000.
