Kevin Greenstein's Summary

I have a long and successful track record as a leader, communicator and innovator. From developing Citigroup’s first online credit card application to managing member acquisition efforts at GeoCities to founding and operating both an internationally-recognized online hockey magazine and a potent marketing service firm for musicians, I have honed both my managerial and creative skills while being guided by an undying dedication to quality and consistency. I am always seeking opportunities to utilize my deep understanding of Internet technologies, coupled with an ability to interface comfortably with clients and colleagues alike, in the challenging and exciting realm of new media.

Kevin Greenstein's Experience

Digital Media Consultant

Airocide

Brought aboard to help bring structure to Airocide's digital marketing efforts. In particular, I am focused heavily on SEO (search engine optimization), constructing a blog and developing a content strategy around it that will clearly express the relevance of Airocide's top-tier air purification device for consumers searching for solutions to allergies, asthma, and poor air quality. Fantasy/IDP Content Development

Pro Football Focus

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Sports industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 4 months) Greater Boston Area

Pro Football Focus (PFF) does incredible research/analysis into what defines success in the NFL. Each and every player (at every position) is graded on each and every play, and the result is an incredibly useful data set that has been utilized by teams, player agents, and the general sports media. Beginning in 2010, I have worked with PFF to develop a top-tier fantasy offering that leverages their data. PFF Fantasy has quickly become one of the most respected resources for fantasy football players, amassing hundreds of paid subscribers in its first year behind a paywall. In addition to my work on the content and strategy front, I have also designed the cover for each of the first three editions of PFF's Fantasy Draft Magazine.

Professor

Suffolk University

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

September 2008 – Present (5 years 1 month) Greater Boston Area

Professor in the Department of Communication and Journalism. I teach two courses, New Media, New Markets (CJN297) and Communication Design Lab (CJN247). New Media, New Markets takes a historical and sociological look at the impact of the Internet, breaking down its effects on various industries (publishing, politics, education, music, sports, etc.). In addition, each student justifies and develops a website in the topic of their choosing. Communication Design Lab focuses on giving the students the ability to communicate visual design concepts using Adobe Photoshop. Each student designs a logo, a website, a magazine cover and (finally) an advertising campaign.

Owner

Band Letter

January 2005 – Present (8 years 9 months) Greater Boston Area

Musicians have historically struggled to keep their fans in the loop regarding the release of recordings and upcoming show dates, largely because their primary focus is on making music rather than promoting it. Band Letter is a service that was designed to help make it incredibly easy for musicians to deliver big label-quality email newsletters to their fans on a regular basis. In addition to assisting with the musicians' newsletters, Band Letter also offers website development, social media marketing, and communications strategies for independent musicians. The client list includes Sophie Serafino, Jen Chapin, Bill Curtis & The Fatback Band, Ann Sweeten, Golden Bloom, Jim Boggia, Matt Marshak, Neshama Carlebach, and Sylvia Bennett.

President

Inside Hockey

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Sports industry

June 2002 – Present (11 years 4 months) Greater Boston Area

Inside Hockey has for over 10 years delivered high-quality hockey coverage to fans across the globe. Though the primary focus is the NHL, Inside Hockey also includes coverage of the minor leagues (AHL/ECHL/USHL), college hockey, major Canadian junior hockey, and international tournaments (Olympics, World Cup World Championships, World Junior Championships). In my role as President of the company, I manage a team of 10 editors, 100+ writers, and 20+ photographers. In addition, I have established partnerships for Inside Hockey with many high-profile companies, including HDNet, Fox Sports, Universal Music, Sony, STATS Inc., the New York Sun, the Wall Street Journal, and Getty. On the social media front, I have established robust presences on Facebook and Twitter, with both auto-updates and dedicated posts.
