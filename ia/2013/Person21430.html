Quinx is an international consultancy and training-company, located in Belgium and the Netherlands.
We offer innovative trainings, coaching, workshops and in-company projects.
Individuals, teams and organisations come to us because they want to evolve.

Co-founder

ARTISA Academic & Art Retreat

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

July 2011 – Present (2 years 7 months) Epidavros-Greece

ARTISA Academic & Art Retreat is a unique retreat for scientists, PhD students, artists, leaders and other professionals from across the globe. Away from the distractions of daily life, you can make progress with your work, spend (a part of) your sabbatical, design new projects, create artwork, and regain inspiration. We also offer special programs, like Silent retreat, Masterclass for visual artists and a Women and Leadership retreat.

ARTISA builds a platform for mutual inspiration, investigation, learning and sharing insights and ideas. We are dedicated to providing space for using your talents, creativity and exploring new ways to deal with the challenges we are facing.
I am co-founder and managing director at Artisa. Look at www.artisagreece.org for more info.
