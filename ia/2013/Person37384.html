Melanie Lentz-Janney's Summary

Seasoned, creative and results-oriented marketing, public relations, social media professional with extensive strategic and hands-on experience with campaigns, branding, media relations, publicity, special events and social networking. Strong background, knowledge and expertise in entertainment, film/TV, hospitality/tourism, retail, green marketing, real estate, and other related industries.

I am also a Filmmaker/Executive Producer/Producer ... My first feature documentary, "An Affair of the Heart" about Rick Springfield and his devoted fan base (I've been a Rick fan for 30 years and this project was my dream/passion) opened in NYC at the IFC Center for a 9 day run on October 10th and had its EUROPEAN PREMIERE at IDFA (Amsterdam) on NOVEMBER 18, 19 & 20! The doc was picked up by EPIX and will premiere on May 15, 2013 in the USA. DVD release will be mid-summer '13. Pre-order it now: www.rickspringfielddoc.com/yellowrickroad/store

I partnered with Sylvia Caminer, an Emmy Award-winning filmmaker, to produce the film. We had our world premiere at the Florida Film Festival where we won a Special Jury Award. We also won Special Jury Awards at the Nashville Film Festival, Boston Film Festival and Sidewalk Moving Pictures Festival in Birmingham, AL. We had our international premiere at the biggest doc fest in North America, HOT DOCS in Toronto, where indieWIRE selected us as one of the 10 Hottest Docs at Hot Docs and we were named a 2012 "Best of Hot Docs" film.

Official website is: www.rickspringfielddoc.com.

Doverwood Communications (www.doverwood.com) has handled the PR/marketing of the film from day one. "Follow the YELLOW RICK ROAD" on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, YouTube, CafePress, SmugMug & eBay.

People from all over the world are following the Yellow Rick Road ... and the journey continues ... please join us!

Specialties

If you have a dream, we can help make it a reality. Consultation services available.
