Jennifer Fanelli's Summary

Jennifer Fanelli has been designing and decorating for as long as she can remember. She began her career working for two of the top ten residential designers in south Florida. The experience and knowledge that she had gained from working with these two affluent design firms plus her love and enthusiasm for interior design eventually lead Jennifer four years later to the founding of her own design firm, Signature Design Creations in 2003. Jennifer has over 15 years of experience in the Interior design field.

Signature Design Creations is a boutique styled interior design firm that specializes in residential, commercial and yacht interiors. Jennifer creates interiors that are sophisticated, timeless and very livable. She works hand and hand with all of her clients to create interiors that are distinctly different and unique. With choice fabrics, furnishing, architectural elements, antiques and fine artworks she has crafted interiors that truly reflect her client’s ideals and taste.

Client satisfaction is Signature Design Creations number one priority. We understand that creating beautiful interiors is more than just an application of aesthetic sensibility but involves combining form, function, comfort, quality and balance through careful editing and placement of the things that you love. Every client receives the personal attention of the company’s president Jennifer Fanelli. By offering full interior design services, Signature Design Creations can handle any size project. Whether a project is new, a complete renovation or remolded, we offer a variety of design levels suited to a client’s individual needs and budgets.

Signature Design Creations offers the latest concepts in interior design and top quality products. We have spent many years building and maintaining relationships with the finest trade professionals and craftsman in the industry who care about quality, detail and craftsmanship.
