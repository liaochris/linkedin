UX Specialist

April 2010 – Present (3 years 6 months)

I gather data using stakeholder/customer/potential customer interviews, industry and competitive analyses, and online research. From this research, I create personas and perform technical/content evaluation. Once the vision and roadmap are in place, I build out the sitemap and wireframes with iterative feedback from internal team members, moving then into wireframing and content strategy.
