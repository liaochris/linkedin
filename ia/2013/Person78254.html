Lane A. Houk's Summary

Lane is a veteran and served in the US Army as a combat field medic, EMT and practical nurse. After returning to civilian life he worked as a Critical Care Technician in Intensive Care units and Emergency Rooms for 5 years.

Lane also has over a decade of experience working in and with the social (non-profit) sector in various roles and is a graduate of Executive Leadership in Non-Profits from Gulf Coast University and Leadership Lee County.

Lane spent 10 years in the banking industry managing branch lending operations and teaching mortgage sales professionals on the importance of building strong, high-trust relationships while employing effective network, offline and internet marketing strategies which helped sales professionals and branch operations under his influence achieve annual sales volumes of $20-30 million.

From 2006-2008, Lane also served as an instructor at the Hobbs Herder School of Real Estate Management educating real estate brokers/managers how to differentiate and build blue ocean strategies to separate themselves from competition.

Using his experiences in business and the social sectors, Lane is able to use his greatest asset - innovative and creative thinking - in his current role as the CEO of Think Big Enterprises and Think Big University. Lane also trains other internet marketing professionals as a guest speaker for several internet marketing forums.

Think Big University is a dynamic, online business school for business owners and entrepreneurs providing the nuts and bolts curriculum and action steps to take a business owner to $1 million in annual revenues.

Specialties: Market Analysis and Research, Keyword and Demographic Analysis, Business and Marketing Intelligence, Online Marketing, Internet Marketing, CRM Solution Integration, Business Development, Business Automation, SEO, Local SEO, Optimized Blog Development, Website Design, Website Development, Web Analytics, Mobile Marketing and Unique Customer Experience Design.
