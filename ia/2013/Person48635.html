Gillian Ormiston's Summary

There are lots of bookkeepers around so why buy from me? Well, I recognise that every one of my customers is unique and so I won’t try to sell you a ‘one size fits all’ service. I’ll ask you what your problems are and then I’ll work out how best to fix them. It may be as simple as generating invoices and chasing overdue payments or helping you to put together figures to back up a business loan application. You may alternatively need my expertise to help you submit Tenders for public or private sector projects and help you manage them when you win them.

My background is in IT project management and public sector bidding so I’ve got a lot of experience of project accounting, and project costing. When I worked in the corporate world I had a good reputation amongst my colleagues for my attention to detail and for being able to balance an unbalanceable project account, so when I decided that change was due, a new career working with numbers was the obvious choice.

Specialties

Project Accounting, Bid Management, Management Accounts, Sage, QuickBooks
