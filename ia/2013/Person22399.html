Chase Gassert's Summary

Focused entrepreneur on a mission.

I began my first company at age 14, first LLP at 16, and have been building businesses ever since.

The Chase Ideas network began back in 2001 when I was introduced to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Quickly learning and mastering the basics, I created a few small sites about my wide range of interests, and began establishing a large network of content websites, media websites, forums, and social networking sites.

This core network of websites grew quickly, and established high ranking positions for a variety of related keywords in their respective niches.

In 2003, I began teaching myself Linux and Apache (LAMP) systems administration and established my core web hosting brand, ChaseHosting.com. This allowed me to offer clients hosting along with their web design packages, and ensure a quality experience from start to finish. The web hosting company grew over time and now hosts several thousands of websites across the globe.

Over the years, I've expanded my web arsenal to Perl, PHP, MySQL, Python, Ruby, and other web technologies. I also began bringing outside consultants into the company work-flow.

Originally, most of the business focus was on small websites for hundreds of local clients, with most of the larger developmental projects going on in the background. After many years of establishing Chase Ideas in the service industry, the company priority nowadays is on high-end internal development projects, with an emphasis on the advertising network, online marketing platforms, and other public web services.

Working with a large network of other professional vendors in the web industry, I've provided services to a number of corporate clients, large business and organizations, political figures, and other important organizations around the world.

I'm an active partner in several expanding companies, both on and off the web, and play a key role in the strategic management of Chase Ideas, and the various subdivisions and services.
