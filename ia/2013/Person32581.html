David Shulman's Summary

I am a solo practitioner located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, but am able to meet clients throughout the South Florida area, and depending on the circumstances, throughout the state. My practice is dedicated solely to estate planning and administration, tax planning, and asset protection.

My prior work experience was with the Internal Revenue Service in Washington, DC, where I drafted regulations, revenue rulings, revenue procedures, and private letter rulings. In addition, I have worked in a large South Florida firm, where I drafted numerous, wills, trusts, family limited partnerships, and other estate planning documents.

Specialties

Estate Planning, Probate, Retirement Planning, Asset Protection, Tax Planning, Charitable Planning
