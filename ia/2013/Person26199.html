Glen Cohen's Summary

Glen Cohen acts as Vice President and leads the business development and marketing team at Paperless Proposal. Cohen started Paperless Software, Inc. in 2007 and has served in a variety of management and R&D roles. Cohen has 20yrs experience in the industry and is responsible for marketing, communications and all sales operations.

Prior to joining Paperless Proposal, Cohen was CEO of Glen Cohen Design a full service Advertising & Marketing firm specializing in creative services & web based technology working with a prominent client base. He holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Advertising as well as numerous certifications in software.
