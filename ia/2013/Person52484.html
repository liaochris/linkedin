Helder Luis' Summary

I’m a graphic designer currently working and living in central Portugal.
Working through notype since 1996, i like to create brands and implementing them. I also run two notype sub-brands, LAB52 and Ethertype, the first providing advanced multimedia services and the second webdevelopment services.
Nowadays i work as a consultant and UX mobile designer for Seara (Yunit group) since 2010 and have been involved in high visibility projects.
Individually I have been developing a body of work that spans from the commercial work as a graphic designer and multimedia developer to the artistic work as a mixmedia artist and playing experimental music throughout the years.
Collectively i was, and plan to continue to be, involved in some of the most interesting multimedia projects being developed in Portugal.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Branding, Multimedia, Webdesign, iOS (iPhone, iPod Touch and iPad) Development, GUI & UX, Illustration, MacOSX, PHP, MySQL, Linux, Wordpress, Drupal, Plone, MAX/MSP/Jitter, etc.
