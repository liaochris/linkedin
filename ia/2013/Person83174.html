Lucy Allen's Summary

I have over 15 years of PR experience and am fortunate to have worked with an amazing range of companies, from charities and arts organisations to international travel, finance and legal, and global retail giants.

My career has spanned agency and inhouse PR and marketing, B2B and B2C, both public and private sector.

Clients include ASDA, Morrisons, and Welcome to Yorkshire.

I work with a talented group of associates which enables me to offer additional services such as design, branding and market research.

Specialties

Short and long term PR cover, inhouse or distance working, crisis management, media releases, media liaison, media calls, forward features, articles, press briefings, photocalls, events management, copywriting and checking.
