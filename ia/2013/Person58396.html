Janice Gable Bashman's Summary

I am the Bram Stoker nominated author (with NEW YORK TIMES bestseller Jonathan Maberry) of WANTED UNDEAD OR ALIVE (Citadel Press 2010) and PREDATOR (Month9Books, coming 2014). I am managing editor of THE BIG THRILL, the International Thriller Writers' ezine. My short fiction has been published in various anthologies and magazines. I have written for leading publications, including NOVEL & SHORT STORY WRITER'S MARKET, THE WRITER, WRITER'S DIGEST, INDUSTRY TODAY, FOOD & DRINK QUARTERLY, and WILD RIVER REVIEW.

I am a speaker and workshop leader at writers conferences, including ThrillerFest, Backspace, Pennwriters, The Write Stuff, Stoker weekend, and others. I am an active member of the International Thriller Writers, Horror Writers Association, and Mystery Writers of America, and a member of The Liars Club.

I have worked in television/ film production and multimedia production. I also have a master's degree in clinical psychology and worked extensively in that field.

Specialties: *Writing for a wide variety of industries
*A working knowledge of television and film production
*Background in psychology
