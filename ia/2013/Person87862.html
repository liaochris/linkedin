Marty Hosking's Summary

Hello everyone! My name is Marty Hosking. I pursue two independent services: Real Estate and Spanish Interpretation/Translation for business, court and legal proceedings; I am in the Washington DC/MD/VA region and NOW Omaha, Nebraska.

I’m focused on meeting your particular needs and making the process of home buying and selling both seamless and easy. I come from a family of developers and have successfully partnered with an entire spectrum of people throughout my career as a Realtor. I also have connections to qualified service providers to help keep your transition hassle-free. Please read the testimonials my clients have written about my service: http://dcmarty.com/real-estate/testimonials/

As for my Spanish interpreting, I interpret for business-to-business transactions, DC/MD/VA court proceedings and legal document translation. I spent many years living in Latin America where I learned the cultural nuances associated with interpreting. I’m able to replicate the corresponding dialects of the different regions—providing the best possible translation for your business needs. Additionally, I’m State Consortium Certified, which means that I am approved to court interpret in most states including Nebraska.

Specialties

Legal Interpretation, Legal document translating (marriage documents, Real Estate consultation, condominiums, condos

October 2012 – Present (1 year) 13304 California St, Omaha, NE 68154

As a native Omahan, Marty Hosking has an intimate knowledge of the Omaha area and the nuances offered by its many neighborhoods and communities. Growing up and attending school at Creighton Prep in the Omaha suburbs, and Creighton University, Marty’s connections go back generations to include many local families and business owners. His earliest memories are of accompanying his father in his title insurance businesses (Metro Title and First Nebraska Title, now TitleCore) and real estate development adventures like Spring Green, Brookhollow, SpyGlass, and Tomlinson Woods. Six years ago, Marty moved to the Washington DC region and started his own company, Marty Hosking LLC. With licenses in Washington DC, Maryland, and Virginia he built a successful real estate business using the Buffini school of ethical, relationship referral-based practices. His work there distinguished national top producer for Prudential Real Estate. He also runs, as part of his LLC, LenguaMax Language Services and served as a NCSC-certified legal interpreter for the Washington DC region’s courthouses, local attorneys and national agencies. Now joining Prudential Ambassador this fall 2012, Marty brings a wealth of experience to Omaha from the diverse, fast paced and challenging DC real estate market. There he earned his stripes and thrived in the rough recession market while negotiating homes from the simple to the sublime---single-family suburban to urban condos, $150k homes to over a $1 mil, including short sales and foreclosures. While he will maintain his great DC business his dream has been always to contribute to the growth of Omaha. Marty is excited to be a presence and put his skills at the service of people looking to buy or sell a home in Omaha and DC. He is uniquely poised to advise clients in both places-- locals and newcomers throughout the region using his years of real estate expertise and his lifelong enjoyment of living in and around Omaha and Washington, DC.
