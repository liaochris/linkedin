Eric Lorentz's Projects

  • SoundCloud Latest Tracks for Google Chrome

    • July 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Eric Lorentz

    SoundCloud Latest Tracks for Google Chrome is an unofficial extension that gives you quick access to your latest incoming tracks from SoundCloud and notifies you of new track uploads from artists you follow. Stop checking SoundCloud for newly uploaded tracks. Let them come straight to you!

  • Economist Radio Chrome Extension

    • March 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Eric Lorentz

    Worked with The Economist to bring their SoundCloud hosted audio content to Google Chrome with a branded extension. Extension users can subscribe to, listen to and keep up with their favorite Economist audio content while browsing the Internet with Google Chrome.

  • Economist TV Chrome Extension

    • July 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Eric Lorentz

    Worked with The Economist to bring their Brightcove hosted video content to Google Chrome with a branded extension. Video content is served up via HTML5 in a pop-up window, a pop-out window or in the background with audio only. Extension users can subscribe to, watch and keep up with their favorite Economist video content while browsing the Internet with Google Chrome.
