Brian Arndt's Summary

For more than fifteen years, Brian has brought provocative and compelling ideas to clients around the world. Brian has consistently written, directed and produced work recognized for its creativity, diversity and most importantly its results for such clients as ABC, ESPN, Showtime, GMC, Ford, HSN, Good Morning America,Swissair, Cunard, Marriott, Outback, Panerai, ALLTEL, Del Monte, Baxter Medical, CAT Footwear, Wenger (Swiss Army Knives) and others.

Before starting his own company, Brian came up through the “big agency” ranks, including stints with Ross Roy Communications, McCann/SAS, McCann Erickson and Ogilvy & Mather.

As Creative Director of Zeh Arndt Creative, Brian has the good fortune of developing a variety of brands at all market levels, including locally, USF St. Petersburg; regionally, Firehouse Subs; nationally, ESPN; and internationally, Wenger.

Whether concepting new products, services or business ventures for clients, managing strategic partners, directing production crews, or collaborating with a gang of creatives, Brian thrives on bringing talented people together, leading by example, and motivating everyone to perform at their highest level of creativity.

His honors include Addys, Tellys, Sports Emmys, London International Advertising Awards, The New York Festivals, The Clio Awards, AMPIA, The Right Brain Awards, and 38th Annual American
Advertising Awards where he was named Copywriter of the Year.
