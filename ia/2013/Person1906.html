CEO

Centipaid Corporation

January 2004 – December 2008 (5 years)

Centipaid provides Micro payment technology based on its internet stamp technology, which was developed to support the unattended payments of email delivery charges. The technology is based on embedding payment and identifying characteristics securely in an image file bearing a visual representation of brand or marketing messages.

The image (called centipix) is then used to make payments by uploading the image to a payment server.

The technology model was successful in implementation on centipaid.com.

Centipaid's business model was changed to provide OEM marketing solutions based on the centipix model, to help companies increase loyalty, track usage, and provide innovative marketing initiatives.
