President/Broker

2011 – Present (2 years) Scottsdale, AZ

Elan Residential is a full service commercial real estate brokerage located in Scottsdale, AZ specializing in investor and developer representation.

Elan Consulting Group and our strategic partners serve as independent advisors to property owners, lenders, architects and facility managers. We offer a holistic approach to evaluating and designing capital improvements that optimize energy efficiency and increase profitability and property values.

Total solution services offered by Elan Residential include: (a) purchase and sale of broken condos, multi-housing communities and portfolios of single family homes (including pre-acquisition due diligence and market/competitive analysis), (b) repositioning and sale of bank owned and distressed assets, (c) development and management of capital improvement projects (including energy optimization opportunities) and budgets, (d) development and implementation of marketing and sale/leasing strategies, and (e) legal compliance/HOA relationship management.

Representative Clients: Centrum Properties, Speedwagon Properties, The Carlyle Group, Equity Residential and New South Federal Savings Bank.
