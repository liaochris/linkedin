Jim Egan's Experience

Owner

Next Level Baseball Training

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Sports industry

2008 – Present (5 years) Twin Cities

We founded Next Level Baseball Training with a simple goal in mind: to offer an opportunity for Minnesota players to make a significant impact on their game during the off-season. NLBT has never been, and will never be, an ordinary training program. NLBT is a mindset, an attitude, a work ethic. Those who have trained with us know that we are a special club made up of players who approach hard work, sacrifice, and accountability at a higher level than their peers. We don’t tolerate excuse-making and we don’t care about talent. The players who trained with us in our infancy set a standard that today’s players are driven to uphold every time they pick up a glove, bat, exercise band, or medicine ball. Our attitude about proper preparation makes us unique; the strength of our commitment to the playing the game the right way sets us apart.
