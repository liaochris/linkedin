Director

Level 7 Un-Networking

March 2013 – Present (7 months) Phoenix, Arizona Area

Level 7 Un-Networking was created by business owners who were frustrated with poor results they were getting from conventional networking techniques. Too often, conventional networking events are characterized by salespeople trying to sell other salespeople and do not offer the opportunity to build productive business relationships with decision makers. Level 7 is specifically for B2B business owners and executives, and focuses on getting the right people, in the right place, and using the right process.
