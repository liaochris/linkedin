Guillem Recolons Argenter's Summary

We all leave an impression

Yes, I have advertising DNA. After 25 years in ad agencies I realized that my passion is about branding ... applied to people.

Since 2005 I am Personal Branding Strategist and since 2010 I'm part of Soymimarca, a company of which I am co-founder with my partner Jordi Collell.

My branding know-how comes from BBDO, JWT, Ogilvy, Saatchi & Saatchi, Altraforma, TVLowCost and my own Lateral Consulting.

I’m also co-author of the Personal Branding Plan, the 1st online personal branding self-management program and co-author of the first Personal Branding Degree at the URL University,

Specialities: Personal Branding (consultancy and training), Strategic Planning, Political Communication

Guillem Recolons Argenter's Experience

Personal Branding Strategist

Soymimarca (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

October 2010 – Present (3 years 1 month)

Soymimarca is born in Barcelona in October 2010. The firm focuses on personal branding management for enterprise environments or individuals, whether college students, professionals who are temporarily unemployed, entrepreneurs and working professionals. Everyone has a reason to create or strengthen your personal brand, but it is clear that there is always a horizon of improvement with a strong brand: find work, improve on the job, lead, create new companies ...

Soymimarca management team consists of three personal branding consultants: Jordi Collell Lopez, Guillem Recolons, and Alexia Herms.

Soymimarca starting point is the fusion of disciplines of coaching, personal branding and marketing, and their origin are the companies CTCoachCode and Lateral Consulting, both with long experience in personal branding development.

Founding partner and general manager of TVLowCost Spain

TVLowCost

September 2007 – December 2010 (3 years 4 months)

TVLowCost was first launched in Paris in October 2004.

100% independent, it belongs to its managers, a rather unusual situation in the advertising industry where 85% of the ad agencies belong to 5 global groups !

It's immediate success has been tremendous and hundreds of companies have expressed high interest in this revolutionary approach. TVLC Paris already handles 80 clients and has produced over 500 TV commercials since then.

Due to this huge impact on the market, the company decided rapidly to become international and to cover all the key markets with its revolutionary approach of TV advertising. Currently, the group is represented by 12 agencies, including Spain (establised 2007), all over the world and employs a little more than 200 people.

Its philosophy can be summarized by this simple phrase : offer to the advertisers all over the world to GET MORE FOR LESS !
