Julie Gordon White Bestselling Author, Coach & Broker's Summary

After 25 years in the business world, and most recently after working with hundreds of business owners over the last 10 years, I felt compelled to share in a much bigger way the valuable strategies and best practices I've learned from selling million dollar plus businesses, as well as running my own business brokerage firm with 7 plus figures in transactions.

So from many to millions, The WELL for Women Entrepreneurs journeys towards it's mission of teaching and inspiring EVERY woman business owner who passionately wants to grow her business to a million and someday sell for a million (or more!).

In addition to The WELL, I continue to work with a few private clients to facilitate the sale of their companies through my firm, BlueKey Business Brokerage Mergers & Acquisitions founded in 2003, specializing in companies with an enterprise value between $1 to $10 million. I also oversee BlueKey Media Group - the publishing and speaking brand extension of my Amazon bestselling book, "EXIT! 12 Steps to Sell Your Business for the Price You Deserve".

Julie Gordon White Bestselling Author, Coach & Broker's Publications

  • EXIT! 12 Steps to Sell Your Business for the Price You Deserve

    • Amazon Bestseller October 2011
    • October 11, 2011
    Authors: Julie Gordon White Bestselling Author, Coach & Broker

    EXIT! 12 Steps to Sell Your Business for the Price You Deserve demystifies the selling process ensuring that business owners will be able to sell their business for the maximum market price, whether they want to sell today or ten years from today. The EXIT! book begins with the reasons businesses change ownership (or not), how to determine the best time to sell, and teaches business owners how to value their own business in 30 minutes or less. From there, the reader will discover how to get the business ready for market, how to prepare financial statements and even contemplate the cost benefit of selling a business “For Sale By Owner”, and if not, how to assemble a selling success team. At this point in the book, a business owner will have a clear overview of the process allowing the book to go deeper to discuss the difference between an asset sale and a stock sale, how to market for buyers, how to manage the offer and due diligence process, and lastly, how to exit problem-free and with a bank account full of cash! The reader’s reward for being a proactive business owner is the final section, “Wrapping It All Up”, which is a toolbox of checklists, all of the 27 broker’s secrets included in the book, sample valuations worksheets, plus additional resources to further support a business owner’s successful exit journey.
