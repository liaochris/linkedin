Adam Teitelbaum's Summary

I am a serial entrepreneur establishing and running green and sustainable businesses in the San Francisco Bay Area for over 20 years. Having started Top Banana Produce in 1990 and Adam's Organic Coffees 1992 -2002, I was a pioneer in the sustainable business category.

I have continued my interest in green business by building Eco-Depo, recycling and remanufacturing toner cartridges. Our closed loop supply chain creates added value for the consumer as well as the environment, keeping costs down by utilizing post consumer resources.

We are looking for like minded partners to help us grow our business.

Specialties

Supplier of premium remanufactured printer cartridges for all major printer brands. Our manufacturer is ISO 9001:2008, STMC certified.
Excellent networking, public speaking and writing skills. Trade show road warrior.
