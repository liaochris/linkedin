Curt James' Summary

I am a conscientious Professional in the Real Estate industry, with high expectations of myself in the realm of customer service. I focus primarily on Real Estate properties in the rural areas of Wyoming, Nebraska and Colorado. Because of my lifelong involvement in the Agricultural industry and my love for the great outdoors, I work with ranch, farm, recreational and hunting properties.
I have a great understanding of the Agriculture industry, not just in real estate, but also in the commodities with experience in hedging grains and cattle. Many agricultural or country properties have benefits beyond just owning the property. These are potential investment properties with income coming from crop sharing, bushel leases, cash leases or a combination of each and leasing hunting rights or mineral rights.
Being an outdoorsman, I work with Recreational properties, such as mountain properties including hunting properties and mining claims, along with river and lake front properties. Important to me, not only is the challenge of marketing and selling a property, but helping match a property to what a buyer is specifically looking for.
I chose the Real Estate industry because it allows me variety, challenges, and the chance to meet new people daily. I am always looking for new and innovative ideas that can help me become a better service provider for all my clients and customers. A number of years ago, I developed an online real estate auction site, to allow maximum exposure of a property to as many buyers as possible. This process allows buyers more freedom and anonymity to make offers on a property.
My business has many features, including online auctions, on site auctions, working with sellers and buyers of real estate, mineral rights research, and leasing of properties. If you see anything on my sight that you have questions about or are interested in please contact me. I always have time to talk real estate.
