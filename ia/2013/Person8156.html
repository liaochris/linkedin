Anna J H Fang's Summary

I am the director and founder of anna fang public relations, a multiple award winning PR consultancy established in 2002.

I have over 25+ years of experience in marketing communications, corporate communications and public relations in the Asia Pacific region: SE Asia (Malaysia, India, Cambodia), Hong Kong, Beijing, China, and from the United States.

anna fang public relations handles the media relations for US and foreign multi-nationals and renowned Hong Kong companies.

Projects and companies include: Right Management, Center for Creative Leadership (CCL), Development Dimensions Int'l (DDI), Hong Kong in Venice (Venice Biennale), Kwai Fung Art Group, CO2nnsulting, SHL, Coudert Brothers, Andrew Matthews (author), True Organic, Hammer Gallery, Brian Tilbrook (artist), BPW International, HKABPW, Sedan Chair Race Charities Fund, Clarins, Oxbridge Applications, Danilo Giannoni (Ebru Painter), ikindof.com, etc.
