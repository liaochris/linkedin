Kristin Finch's Summary

Kristin is an ambitious creative professional with a passion for design and seven years’ experience in Adobe Creative Suite. She specializes in corporate identity, brand development, concept-driven creative direction, as well as merchandising and marketing. Her perfectionist streak drives her to ensure every project she creates is pixel perfect, from the technical specifications to the grammatical accuracy of the copy. Kristin is currently accepting creatively stimulating opportunities in Orange County & the San Francisco Bay Area, and can be reached at her website, kristinfinch.com, or by email at kkfinch@gmail.com.
