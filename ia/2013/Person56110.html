De Bolton's Summary

De Bolton is a Work at Home Mother of two beautiful girls ages 9 and 5. She is happily married to her soulmate for over 10 years. A successful nationally award winning business owner and entrepreneur since March 2009.

Graduated from Washington Adventist University in Takoma Park, MD majoring in Business Administration with an emphasis in Marketing.

De Bolton is a nationally awarded direct sales business owner; recognized for her success every year. Ranked #1 in her state of residence in sales and ranked within the Top 30 Nationwide. Nationwide Recruiter (2nd) in 2012 for her direct sales company. Maintaining a leadership position in her company for 4 years and has trained over hundreds of women who have achieved awarded recognition. She has spoken at several Corporate events and has provided several tele-seminar trainings for her Corporate company.

She is also the published author of Consultant Organizer Success Planner and Entrepreneurs Organizer Success Planner.
