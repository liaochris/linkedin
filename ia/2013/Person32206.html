Deutsche Bank

Graduate Analyst

Deutsche Bank

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; DB; Investment Banking industry

September 2011 – December 2012 (1 year 4 months) London, United Kingdom

The Deutsche Bank graduate programme consists of two placements in different business areas over a 15 month period.

Rotation 1 - Programme and project management for Deutsche Bank's main equities trading platform. This included working directly with business stakeholders to prioritise their projects, working with project managers to estimate and budget for future programmes, and managing the relationship between our internal business clients and application developers.

Rotation 2 - Strategic organisational analysis, including management and production of a Balanced Scorecard to monitor progress towards strategic objectives across Deutsche Bank’s entire Global Technology division. This was achieved by developing and tracking KPIs, benchmarking Deutsche Bank’s technology progress against our global competitors, and statistical analysis on technological and market data to produce analytical papers.
