Kate Holder's Summary

Career Statement: Creative leader and decisive problem-solver adept at balancing strategy and detail to execute broad communications campaigns in health and wellness, politics, and the environment. A former foreign policy analyst who developed successful management and branding skills that help create profits and change lives.

Specialty: I craft compelling stories that help clients sell their products and services, promote their non-profit causes, and win elective office. I specialize in health care, politics, sports and wellness, and the environment.

As a two-time non-profit executive director, my natural leadership abilities, management experience, and intuitive problem-solving skills create added value for my clients. I have a reputation for being highly reliable and easy to work with.

I am equally adept at strategic thinking and minding the details.

I recently updated my website -- www.kateholder.com -- and created my new "Bright Shiny Blog," a different take on current events that entertains and occasionally calls to action, posted weekly.

Functional skills: Writing, editing, ghostwriting, corporate copywriting, blogging, political messaging, non-profit public affairs, branding, magazine writing, non-fiction news articles, opinion pieces, website copy, promotions, press & public relations, radio production & advertising, project management, proofreading.
