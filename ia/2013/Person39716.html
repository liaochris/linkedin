Sandy Edry's Summary

The Edry Group is led by Sandy Edry, a lifelong New Yorker who has taken the typical Manhattanite’s fascination with real estate and turned it into one of the top sales and rental teams in the city.

In just the last few years, Sandy has sold nearly $100 million dollars in Manhattan real estate, including more than 110 apartments in the Riverside Drive Condominiums.
Sandy’s knowledge of the market and devotion to his clients led to his being named Team Player of the Year 2012 at Keller Williams NYC as well as a Top Sales Agent at Citi Habitats in 2009 & 10 as well as a five-time Platinum Award winner in 2007-2011.

Before entering real estate, Sandy spent more than 15 years as a professional journalist, including seven years on staff as a writer/editor at Newsweek Magazine. Edry is a graduate of the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism and has been published in various newspapers and magazines including The New York Times, New York, Inc., The Los Angeles Times and many others.

Specialties: Real Estate: Sales and rentals throughout Manhattan, with a special focus on the Upper West Side and Northern Manhattan (including Hudson Heights and Inwood).
Writing/Editing: Travel, Technology, Real Estate.
