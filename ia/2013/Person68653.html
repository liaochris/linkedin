Joseph Levy's Summary

Global Support Services (GSS), established in 2011, provides operational expertise, assistance, and services to support Harvard University's international projects and travelers. We partner with every School and Central Administration department to simplify business processes and flexibly address risks, enabling faculty, students, and staff to work effectively overseas. GSS has a cross-functional scope, covering finance, HR, imports and exports, IT, real estate, safety and security, taxes, and visas.

I lead GSS and have primary operating responsibility for Harvard Global Research and Support Services, Inc. ("Harvard Global"), an affiliated entity founded in 2012 to manage in-country HR and financial infrastructure for overseas projects.

Prior to GSS, I worked as a certified HR professional (SPHR, GPHR), with more than ten years' experience in higher education and prior experience as an administrator in the performing arts. I have partnered with a wide variety of clients in the U.S. and overseas, spanning administrative, production, creative, IT, and research environments.

Specialties: Global operations, relationship management, customer service, staff management, employee and labor relations, problem-solving, coaching, organizational effectiveness, value-added processes and policies.
