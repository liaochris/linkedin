Michael Habib's Summary

My passion is creating intuitive user-experiences that connect people with the information and experts they need. I currently make applications that help academic researchers discover interdisciplinary connections in the scholarly literature and identify new international collaborators.

Product Manager for Scopus from 09/2009.
