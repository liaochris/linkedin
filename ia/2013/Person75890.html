Kim Walker's Summary

The Advance Consultancy specialises in delivering an increase in tangible business results by extending the impact and contribution of leaders and managers .We are passionate that the actions and behaviours of leaders and managers are the catalyst to create or destroy high performance.

Our expertise is in developing skills and creating a culture where everyone is aligned to the vision and can perform to their full potential. Our focus is to understand the impact of change within an organisation and to drive future performance; today.

Utilising our extensive expertise and understanding of what creates high performance; we deliver tailored solutions which enhance the culture, the skills and contribution of leaders and managers. Resulting in an improvement in both individual and team performance.

Specialties

Consulting in the areas of Change Management, Talent and Succession Management, Culture and Employee Engagement. Leadership and Management Development and Executive Coaching
