William G. Lethemon III's Summary

• Bill began his professional career in 1990 starting out as an intern at Merrill Lynch offices in Dearborn and Lansing Michigan. He worked as an intern learning the business for 3 years while he was finishing his studies.

• After graduating from Michigan State University with a degree in Finance in August of 1993 he was hired to be the financial planning coordinator for the Dearborn branch of Merrill Lynch.

• In July of 1994 Bill started to develop his own clientele. Using a planning based approach to helping his clients achieve their financial goals; he successfully completed the Merrill Lynch Professional Development Program. While working at Merrill Lynch he also earned the Certified Financial Manager Designation.

• In March of 2003 Bill left Merrill Lynch to Join Wachovia Securities. Bill continued his success working with clients and growing his practice, until he decided to open his own practice in the summer of 2009.
