Jim Laumeyer's Summary

From the classroom to the boardroom
Jim is a nationally recognized leader in Hr & LR ; is a Labor Arbitrator, Teacher; Presenter & Consultant.
Jim's is brings cutting edge expertise and innovation to lead any organization or audience toward responsible and successful employee engagement and management.
He is a past Chair of SHRM'S National Committee for Employee & Labor Relations.
In addition, Jim leads a team of other subject matter experts who can address any HR & LR issue or opportunity. Jim's goals are to provide HR & LR expertise and leadership that will create the workforce and environment that will be competitive advantages for any organization or entrepreneur.

Specialties

Labor Arbitration. Consultation on all HR matters, particularly design and redesign of HR processes, such as class & comp and performance management/feedback. Jim also specializes in OD assessment and development, strategic planning/staffing; work design and Org structure. LHRS provides training/developmental in HR, management & OD topics, such as leadership, customer service, coaching, motivation etc
Jim is a professional presenter who has presenter at national &, state conferences.
