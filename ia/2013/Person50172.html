Greg Huntoon's Summary

An anthropologist and writer by education, Greg converted a lifelong passion and enthusiasm for architecture and technology into a successful career as a creative director, designer, and art director. He is particularly focused on branding and user experience, with a penchant for intuitive user interfaces. Incredibly interested in the cultural and social aspects of media, Greg keenly watches how new technologies are shaping our world and its future.
