Lauren Reynolds' Summary

I set out to create a line of gemstone jewelry that would be sporty enough for everyday use, but elegant enough to be worn for a night out.

I work exclusively with Gemstones and Swarovski Crystals because they create beautiful, colorful jewelry that can make your personality and inner beauty shine at an affordable price.

My goal is to work together with you in choosing or creating a custom jewelry piece that will evoke your inner fashionista at an affordable price.
