Mary Musso's Summary

Distinctive Homes is an accomplished, semi-custom homebuilder which features unconventional floor plan flexibility to complement diverse interests and lifestyles. The Distinctive Homes experience begins with our dedication to customer service where we simplify the process. We maximize strategic partnerships in design, construction and financing to benefit our homeowners. Design and construction are driven by our attention to detail, integration of technology, commitment to unsurpassed energy efficiencies and environmental sensitivities. We are your homebuilder and neighbor. Distinctive Homes is actively involved in the community and remain personally accessible to clients. The overall experience of buying, designing, building and living in a home created by Distinctive Homes is beyond the expected.

Since 1980, Distinctive Homes has been part of the Las Vegas Nevada community using our new home building expertise to create innovative designs and quality new home construction, while keeping value at the forefront of importance for all our customers.
