Bill Einreinhofer's Summary

Chair, Broadcast Journalism department, New York Film Academy.

Successful communications/media professional, responsible for local, national and international projects. Worldwide production credits with global cross-platform experience. Three time Emmy Award winner.

I head a dynamic department that draws students from across the United States and around the world. No school offers more "hand-on" intensives than NYFA. Our graduates leave with a strong skill set, a firm grounding and knowledge of how to function as a broadcast journalist in "the real world." Our staff is collaborative and inventive, our students motivated and engaged.
