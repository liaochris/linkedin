Jason Feller's Summary

I graduated from the University of North Texas in 1994, and worked for an international industrial company until 1999 when I went to work for Keller Williams in Plano, Texas. In 2001 I started my own real estate company "Feller Realty" and have continued in residential real estate ever since, with a main focus on foreclosure representation, buyer and seller representation, and real estate negotiation.

real estate negotiation, foreclosure representation, accredited buyer representative, certified residential specialist, mortgage broker, real estate broker
