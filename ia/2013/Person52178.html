Heather Ann Havenwood's Summary

Heather Ann, CEO of Havenwood Worldwide, Co-Host of www.SuccessMagnetSeminar.com has fast become regarded as an entrepreneurial online marketing success authority. Since her first online marketing business plan in 1999, bringing together clients and personal coaches online, she has been a part of the online marketing Wild West.

In 2006 she started, developed and grew an online information marketing publishing company from ground zero to over 1 million in sales in less than 12 months, in the Real Estate Investing niche. Starting without a list, a product, a name or an offer; Heather Ann, behind the scenes molded the ‘expert’ into a successful guru with products, a name, a list and profitable joint venture relationships.

She has been named by a few as the ‘Icon Creator’ or the ‘Girl behind the Curtain’. She has promoted and coached many platform speakers helping them increase sales on stage. She has produced, promoted and managed over 350+ seminars and events around the Country. She has hosted teleseminars with many top online thought leaders such as Tom Antion, Perry Belcher, Tony Blake, David Lakhani, Robert Shemin and many others.

She is currently the author of many self-help dating and relationship products such as “Dating Up” and www.Only Date Younger Women.com. She has recently become advance in helping info marketers create Successful Joint Ventures and Affiliate Marketing campaigns specifically in the dating and real estate investing niches.

Heather Ann is now stepping out from behind the curtain to teach, train and maybe even find the next Icon she will create.
