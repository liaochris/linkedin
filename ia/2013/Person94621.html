Michael Tedeschi's Summary

Michael is an interactive user-interface, user-experience designer bringing a background in web and touch-screen platforms to every project (with a touch of print design and brand identity).

Currently, Michael has founded Interactive Mechanics, a start-up interactive design consultancy specializing in exciting and engaging experiences through web, mobile and installation platforms. Notable clients include Bryn Mawr College, NITLE, and Georgetown University.

Previously, Michael has worked with interactive and software development firms on projects for the educational institutions (University of Pennsylvania, University of Richmond, Stanford University), historical institutions (Mount Vernon Historical Society, the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art, and the College of Physicians of Philadelphia), and many others (Bristol-Myer Squibb, ColdLight Solutions).

Specialties

Interactive Design, Branding, Identity, Logo Design, Signage, Print Materials, Web Design and Development, Motion Graphics, Video Production (Post-production, production), Social Media Network, Network Marketing, Adobe CS6 Master Collection, Final Cut Pro, Microsoft Office, XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, jQuery Mobile, Mobile Design, Actionscript 3.0, PHP/MySQL, Django, JSON, XML, Drupal, WordPress, Concrete5
