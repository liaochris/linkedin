Brent M Olivier's Summary

I'm a left handed artist from Southeast Louisiana, developing my skills and pushing myself further to an ideal career here in San Francisco. Over the last few years I've built upon skills and pushed my limits as a visual artist into digital media and continue my passion for traditional mediums such as oil on canvass, sculpture, ceramics, pottery, and other mixed physical media. With tradition I believe my duty as an artist in creating work that helps lead people through each movement or period in time as such as the great historical artists did in the past. I am Currently in my last year at The Art Institute of California - San Francisco and gain knowledge everyday and improve my skills and learn to code my artwork so it's accessible to everyone on any platform.

Specialties

I Love 3D animation, Flash!!! and useful innovative technologies that bring these two mediums together to make the web more animated and captivate end users. Also, Digital illustration, Web Design, and photo manipulation are my fun specialties, along with my precious bonsai trees.
