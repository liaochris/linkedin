Justin Romack's Summary

I suppose you could call me a marketer, a communications specialist, a PR guy or something similar. But I really like to think of myself as a storyteller and dot connector—and that’s exactly how I serve thee marketing, public relations and advertising needs of businesses, organizations and professionals.

Stories are the glue that bind us together. They’re relatable and tend to strike an emotional chord, and as a result, help to humanize a brand and business. It’s all about capturing the ways your brand is empowering and transforming consumers into world-changers and superheros.

I partner with businesses, organizations and professionals to create digital content that captivates readers, tells stories and genuinely helps consumers. This often comes in the form of blog posts, email marketing campaigns and newsletters, ebooks, podcasts, video marketing, social media campaigns, revised website content and much, much more.

My philosophy is simple: Helping always trumps selling. Be helpful, be human and be communicative—and customers will flock to sign on the dotted line.

Intrigued? Let’s talk! Hit the Connect button below, and say “Howdy!”. I want to hear more about the customers you serve and how you’re leveraging content to do business better.

Specialties: Content creation and distribution; brand culture and communication; public relations; copywriting; organizational communications and leadership; public speaking; artist development and management

Digital Advocacy Strategist and Accessibility Evangelist

No Eyes Needed

Public Relations and Communications industry

February 2011 – October 2012 (1 year 9 months) Bryan, TX

No Eyes Needed is a web-based community delivering content specific to the blindness community, but from an angle that is relevant to those with eyesight, as well. Many topics include advocacy, accessibility, cane and guide dog mobility, education and employment, and independent and family living skills. The blog and ezine present content in the form of articles, discussion polls and topics, and personal stories and profiles. No Eyes Needed plans to include a monthly podcast in 2012, which will feature interviews with businesses and developers making the world more accessible through their offerings.

Drummer / Booking / Promotions

November 2010 – February 2011 (4 months)

Drummer / Management

Clairmont

September 2009 – September 2010 (1 year 1 month) College Station, TX

Banging drums was only a fraction of my involvement with independent, alternative rock group, Clairmont, whose roots had been sewed deeply in the College Station, TX, area since 2005. This includes, but was certainly not limited to, engaging with venue operators and bookers, promoting events and coordinating with other acts, creating and implementing a band budget, communicating and engaging with fans and supporters, and working to further distribute previously released content, such as the bands two albums "The Dweller EP" and "Strength Like Lions".
