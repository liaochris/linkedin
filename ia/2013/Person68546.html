Jose Marin's Summary

I have worked on both the technical and the production side of the 2D/CG pipelines with additional experience in stereoscopic 3D. I have also integrated the knowledge of photography into these fields.

More than anything, I love to learn and seek self improvement. I approach tasks and team mates with enthusiasm and professionalism. My focus has been on compositing and stereoscopic compositing primarily using Nuke, with additional experience using After Effects and Fusion.

I have been recognized for superior performance as an Operations and Logistics Manager and Martial Arts Instructor in the United States Marine Corps. I am experienced as a teacher/trainer, demonstrating patience and effectiveness when working with a wide range of personalities.

I have had the opportunity to further develop my skill set while working on projects such as: Star Trek: Into Darkness, Oblivion, Game of Thrones, Beautiful Creatures, Avengers, and Transformers 3 to name the most recent.


Specialties: Compositing, Paint and Roto, Stereoscopic 3D, Nuke, Fusion, After Effects, Mocha Pro, Photography, Production Management, Logistics and Operations
