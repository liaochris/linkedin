Web Developer & Unix Sys Admin

GNS Europe

My initial role was as a web developer / designer - using HTML / CSS / Javascript / ASP and later on PHP. After 8 months in this role I joined the technical support team as a systems administrator as I wanted to learn the more technical aspect of the internet and how it all worked.

I gained a good level of working knowledge with server operating systems (Windows/Unix/Linux), hardware, networks and DNS. I was a member of the out of hours on-call team, to support clients at any time during the day. Primarily based in Guildford, I later transferred to the London office.

During this time I continued to develop websites, and further improve my skills.
