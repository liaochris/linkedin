Albert Rocker's Summary

With an unparalleled commitment to success and a personal philosophy
underscored with competitive-edge, Albert Rocker captivates any audience from
the stadium to the boardroom.

As a Technical Account Manager for Microsoft in Irvine, California, Rocker is
celebrated for being an ambitious, results-driven intrapreneur, demonstrating
a forward thinking business acumen through an innovative approach to
entrepreneurial leadership.

While attending the College of Management at Georgia Tech, Rocker soared in
courses encompassing the complete business spectrum including a multifarious
internship with Automatic Data Processing, a Fortune 200 organization and
the most respected payroll outsourcing company in the world. Rocker was
simultaneously awarded academic honors from the All Atlantic Coast Conference
from 2009-2011, and was voted “Most Likely to Succeed” by his team while
participating in the renowned Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets athletic program.

Crediting strict military upbringing and athleticism as providing competitive-
edge, quality of performance and being sources of structure and accountability
for many of his achievements, Rocker made the heartfelt decision to forgo his
senior season of football in pursuit of an opportunity to work for the best software
company in the world, Microsoft. As a result, Rocker received a Bachelor in
Business Management with a concentration in IT and Entrepreneurship from the
Georgia Institute of Technology.

Proving to be more beneficial to his long-term, professional business goals and
while boasting a great appreciation for the culture of the company, Rocker has
successfully transitioned from the Microsoft Academy of College Hire Program
with a solid year of outstanding contributions and service to the organization.
