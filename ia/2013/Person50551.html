Griffin de Luce's Summary

I've directly advised or worked closely with TWO(!) CEOs of Apple Computer, the Chairman (and former CEO) of Microsoft, the Chairman of the SEC, the Chairman and CEO of MasterCard International and countless other high ranking executives throughout Silicon Valley and around the World. I've helped guide some of the biggest brands through exciting technical transitions and I've spearheaded strategic efforts for large organizations like JP Morgan, Lexus, IBM, Time Warner, AOL, American Express, Deloitte, and McKinsey & Company.

I ideate, design, curate, art direct, develop and manage an abundance of projects for an exciting cross section of startups, corporations, governments and universities around the world! I've carved out a niches as a CTO-for-hire, mentor, coach, recruiter or plain old cheerleader while working to productize, strategize and market position for companies preparing for acquisition, merger or venture financing. My role often varies but most typically is 1) advisor, 2) executive and/or 3) investor.

I have dedicated much of my energy to forwarding an agenda of sustainability during our lifetimes through my work with State of the World Forum, Green.org, Wind.com, Solar.com and the Lexus hybrid division. My current obsession is bringing education to all the world's people.

My contributions to the future of education and media have taken the form of consulting to MIT, Columbia University, the BBC, CBS, PBS, Middle East Broadcast Center, Newsweek, Fortune, Wired, The Writers Guild of America, The Wall Street Journal and The New York Times.

My personal mission on the planet is to co-create a friendlier, happier world by sharing in fun experiences with like-minded people! Please reach out and say "Hi." Let's see if we can play together on something fun.
