Jonathan W. Buckley's Summary

Jonathan takes a fresh approach to marketing, combining a methodical process of analytics and “systems thinking” together with the art of messaging and positioning. He has spent the last 15 years leading successful client engagements, starting as a manager in the business strategy consulting arm of a “Big Six” firm and progressing through the orchestration of many high profile company and/or product launches and ongoing campaigns. A student of innovation and market disruption, Jonathan specializes in the rapidly changing world of technology. Jonathan has held a number of posts as a marketing officer in both public and private corporations and brings his practical insight and guidance to Marketing Services engagements at Bhava.

A representative sample of retained engagements is listed below. Additional engagements are covered in references document.
