Kim Ashcraft's Summary

I am a 20 year + veteran in financial services, with most of my time being spent on the wholesale side of the business. I started The 180 Wholesaler in 2008 because I believe wholesalers are a valuable asset to an advisor's practice, though the advisor may not always recognize or have a valuable experience. By updating and enhancing the skillset of sales people in our industry we can meet the changing demands of the advisors, change long-held perceptions and deliver our clients a new sales experience

I'm on LinkedIn to network with colleagues and industry professionals. I can offer expertise in general distribution strategies and wholesaler training. I have particular interest and expertise in training new wholesalers promoted from internal sales or delivering a career-path program for internals looking to transition to an external role.

I'm always open to general discussion around industry best practices, wholesaler training and advisor trends.

Specialties

External,hybrid and internal training, Sales manager training or coaching, Sales desk career-path training, New hire orientation programs, Business basics, Innovative sales ideas
