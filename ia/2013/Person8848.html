Anthony Trott's Summary

Anthony Trott is an American Artist. He began as a sketch artist and has evolved into a Neo-expressionist painter.

His artwork often contains elements meant to promote Social Awareness and Global Consciousness. Animal Rights and Welfare, Psychological Struggles and Religion are often reoccurring themes.

An Animal Activist, and Vegetarian, Anthony often donates his artwork, including murals, to Animal Shelters to help raise awareness and funds.

Interested in the Street Art Movement and the purity of Graffiti, Anthony Trott has left many of his paintings and drawings places, such as Bus Stops, Parks, and Parking Lots, for people to admire and take freely if they so desire. This is often the last moment he knows of their existence.

Going forward, Anthony is further refining his art, finding its life and path, and helping raise awareness of the beauty in the world which surrounds us.

Specialties: MEDIA: Acrylic, Charcoal, Oil, Pastel, Pencil | PRACTICE: Neo-expressionism, Realism | SUBJECT: Advocacy, Animal, Cerebral, Emotion, Plant, Propaganda
