Jennifer Nickerson's Summary

The promise of emerging singer/songwriter Jennifer Nickerson is as large as the Dallas, Texas, where she regularly performs to audiences up to 60,000 at American Airlines Center, Cowboy Stadium, The Ballpark at Arlington and other renowned venues. Nickerson’s recent collaboration with production dynamo, Power Source Music, is furthermore building recognition in Nashville and on national country music circuits. Locally, she rapidly is adding performances and product endorsements to her roster.
Jennifer’s music transcends stereotypical labels of the past by hybridizing country, crossover and inspirational styles to fashion a truly unique sound with honest and relatable lyrics. Jennifer said, “I write from my heart and hope it connects with people’s souls. I want them to feel an excitement about living their life!” Composing songs at the piano since the age of five, she effortlessly produces musical gems that express everyday emotions and conservative values. Jennifer has written both the music and lyrics of every song on both her albums, “Girl Next Door” and “Keep On Keepin On”. While every song has a back story, “Chasing Dreams” is the recording on the Girl Next Door album that embodies the spirit of her journey to make this whole album possible.
Jennifer sings and lives for others. With a father from West Point/Army her goal, as cliché as it sounds, is to help others be the best they can be! With her fun and bubbly personality every interaction with Jennifer, whether personal or from a stage, is a great and memorable experience. Some refer to this prolific talent as the next Martina McBride or Shania Twain, while others name her the title song from her first album and most recent music video “Girl Next Door”. Predictably the name Jennifer Nickerson will soon be as familiar as your neighbor’s next door
