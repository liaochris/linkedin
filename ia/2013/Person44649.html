Michael Stillman, AIF®'s Summary

I'm an Accredited Investment Fiduciary (AIF®) with over twenty-five years experience in ERISA stewardship, wealth management, and public accounting.

My practice is focused on leading small to mid market 401(k) committees through intricate oversight functions unique to ERISA fiduciaries. The scope of these engagements mandate fiduciary status and can not be replicated by nonfiduciary vendors or suitabillity standard investment advisers.

25 years ERISA stewardship and investment management expertise.

Fiduciary Compliance Reviews – This is a detailed evaluation of 401(k) operational procedures that identifies opportunities for improving the overall function, cost efficiency, and performance of the plan. The fiduciary standards published by the Center for Fiduciary Studies serve as benchmarks for all reviews.

ERISA Fiduciary – In this capacity we provide the 401(k) committee continuous oversight of investment and administrative functions as well as participant fiduciary advice. Instead of merely serving as a consultant and leaving the scope of fiduciary commitment and liability exclusively on the committee, our responsibility is elevated to investment manager (ERISA §3(38)) and shoulders the fiduciary process on the committee's behalf. These engagements have a major impact on committee effectiveness, plan efficiency and participant satisfaction.
