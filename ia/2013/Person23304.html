Chris Geyer's Summary

I am a user experience designer who enjoys solving problems through design to create great experiences. My design process is simple and iterative, involving user research, usability testing, collaborative sketching and rapid prototyping. I take complexity and make it simple.

I currently work for the City of Raleigh improving the user experience of their website to better serve our citizens.

Looking for freelance UX projects in the Triangle. Feel free to contact me at chris.w.geyer@gmail.com or 919-480-8737 about opportunities for project work, I am not looking for any new full time positions.
