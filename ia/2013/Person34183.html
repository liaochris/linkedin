Dennis Cudd's Projects

  • Hill Country Modern

    • August 2007 to May 2009
    Team Members: Dennis Cudd, Mary, Yanina, Matias

    This 3,600 square foot custom home is located South of Austin. The design reflects the owner’s desire for a Modern style while maintaining high standards for both energy efficiency and material sustainability. Many of the custom features were fabricated on site by the family, including the rain water retention system. Construction was completed in July 2009. To see more of the Owners comments about the process please see Hill Country Modern.

  • Lambie Street Townhomes

    • January 2008 to December 2009
    Team Members: Dennis Cudd

    These East Side town homes were designed to offer a Modern housing option that was near Austin’s 6th Street entertainment district. These four units, completed in 2009, were created using Austin’s Green Building Standard & every unit received 3 stars or greater. One of the challenges of designing in urban situations is retaining a unit’s autonomy. Here, each unit was given an individual connection to the landscape in addition to its unique modern geometry that sets them apart from one another.
