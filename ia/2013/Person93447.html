Michelle Felice-Steele's Summary

I've worked on many different Web based consumer facing products and services in both project and product management capacities.

My expertise is in capturing and defining Online and Mobile product requirements and functional specifications. I am the driver behind the product who sees the initiative through the full life cycle. My specialties include defining consumer facing Online and Mobile product requirements, functional specifications, process flows, wireframes, product roadmaps and project planning.

Here’s a partial list of my accomplishments:
- Managed Mobile Websites and Mobile Applications at freecreditscore.com
- Championed the creation of Mobile optimized websites for the consumer credit brands at Experian Consumer Direct
- Managed projects at REALTOR.com to convert legacy code base from classic ASP to a .NET, and implement shared .Net web services.
- Lead functional requirements and project development for “MyLiveNation” web application; created product specifications for user registration, email alerts, and concert calendar.
- Directed development, testing, training and deployment for website redesign and Ecommerce shopping cart for major US cosmetics manufacturer.
- Designed and built a corporate Intranet for Homestore.com (now Move.com)
- Researched and analyzed web content management tools and implemented Ektron CMS system for a company website consisting of hundreds of static content pages.
- Managed web properties on behalf of small business clients: including web operations, requirements and specifications, issue tracking, testing, analytics and SEO.
