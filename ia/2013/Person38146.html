Dr. Michael Morch's Summary

Through his work as a dentist and volunteer, Dr. Michael Morch contributes to the health and well being of his local and global community. After graduating from the Virginia Commonwealth University School of Dentistry in Richmond, Virginia, Dr. Michael Morch began his own private dental practice in northern Virginia in 1984 and now operates offices in Woodbridge and Fredericksburg. At his practice, Dr. Michael Morch makes sure to treat every patient as an individual with his or her own unique medical and cosmetic needs. With this in mind, Dr. Michael Morch offers an array of dental services, many of which can be complimented by the use of nitrous oxide and anti-anxiety medications. The use of calming medical treatment in dental procedures is just one of the ways that Dr. Michael Morch is able to make sure his clients have a relaxing, positive experience that ensures future dental health. In order to familiarize young children with the dental office, Dr. Michael Morch also offers pedodontics, or pediatric dentistry, so that young children have the chance to visit the office for a relaxing and easy teeth polishing. In this way, Dr. Michael Morch introduces children to the office and sets the stage for a lifetime of good oral hygiene. For his adult patients, Dr. Michael Morch’s practice offers many services for the maintenance of healthy teeth, including implants, dentures, crowns, root canals, and a number of cosmetic treatments.

In his spare time, Dr. Michael Morch has dedicated himself to a number of worthwhile charitable endeavors. In his own community, he volunteers at his church as the musical director for the youth group’s theater program. Abroad, Dr. Michael Morch travels to Haiti as a member of Medical Missionaries, where, along with fellow dentists, doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals, Dr. Michael Morch treats members of the Thomassique community in the Medical Missionaries’ hospital.
