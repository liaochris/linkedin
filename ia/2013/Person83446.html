Luke Kohler's Summary

I specialize in using my creativity to increase my clients reach through social media, search engine optimization and website analysis and design. I work with clients to help them brand and re-brand their company to reach their customers as easily as possible and promote their image and products in the best possible light.

I have worked on the creative side of things my entire professional life. Prior to starting Impero Media, I worked in radio and audio production. In radio, I also worked as a talk show host and sports beat reporter, allowing me to become very experienced in communicating with people. I draw upon my past experiences working in this field to not only create the ideas my clients need, but can communicate them in ways that make the business relationship succeed.

With the Internet being a fast moving and ever-changing world, I pride myself on staying educated, following current industry trends, technological advances and changes to make sure that I am one step ahead of my competition and therefore, so are my clients.

I am proud of the work I've put in to gain the knowledge and experience I have and am confident that I can not only help my clients achieve their goals, but I can do so by sharing what I do with them and effectively communicating it every step of the way. My job isn't only to do what my clients need, but to teach them what they need so we can work together to create the best overall product.

Creativity plus communication is a combination that I am proud to say describes myself and my business.
