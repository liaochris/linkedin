Fran Tarkenton's Summary

As a senior citizen, I have created Tarkenton Financial to focus on one thing, improving the quality of life for senior citizens. I formed Tarkenton Financial in 2003 with a deep personal understanding of the needs, desires and challenges shared by seniors. Tarkenton Financial is comprised of a national network of insurance and financial services professionals who offer a broad range of insurance and investment solutions. These experienced professionals share my vision and have joined with me to offer personalized products and advice that are most relevant to seniors.

My past business experience includes:
-Obtained my first insurance license at 19.
-I did the first payroll deduct guaranteed issue Life Insurance Program at Cannon Mills in North Carolina. By utilizing National Life & Accident of Nashville, Tennessee, 26,000 people at Cannon Mills were insured. I took that idea to corporate America and built a large company.
-I started the practice of advertising on airplane ticket jackets in 1980 and eventually had every airline except Eastern and Braniff utilizing his system.
-I built a software technology company in the 1980's partnering with IBM and Ernst & Young. This company went from $0 in revenue to $129M in revenue within just a few years
-I also formed close relationships with several business leaders whom he greatly respects; among them, Sam Walton, founder of Wal-Mart and Jay Abraham, marketing guru. Sam was a great fan of mine and the feeling was reciprocated.
-I worked for many years with marketing genius Jay Abraham.
-I was among the pioneers behind the launch of Guthy-Renker Corporation in 1986. Today it is the leading infomercial production company. Guthy-Renker Corporation introduced television spokesperson Tony Robbins to the world and is the promotional genius behind successes such as Power-Rider fitness products.
-I also helped launch 1-800-BAR-NONE, the top sub-prime auto loan program in the United States.
