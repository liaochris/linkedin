Margaret Smith's Summary

EmployHer.com brings women together - it allows a network of business women to connect and work together on a local or national level.

Our directory is easy to use and allows women all over the UK to search, find and learn more about what you do. Whether it's running a hairdressing business from home or working as part of a larger organisation, we offer simple, straightforward and affordable advertising packages.
