James Fisfis' Summary

James Fisfis founded Chariot LLC, which advises U.S. federal, state and local elected officials, candidates and campaigns and trade organizations as general consultant, research and media consultant. Prior to founding Chariot, he served in the California State Assembly as a Political and Communications Director for three consecutive Republican Leaders and the 2002 Republican nominee for Governor.

James is also an international consultant and published author in the field of quantitative and qualitative political public opinion research. He has successfully predicted election results and political trends in third world, post-conflict and totalitarian environments in former communist bloc countries. He has also conducted political risk research projects for investors in both domestic and foreign environments.

His research and analysis has been used by United States Ambassadors and diplomats, the National Security Council, political leaders and foreign policy analysts, as well as by foreign heads of state and government ministers, the Helsinki Commission on Human Rights, and Native American tribal leaders.

James Fisfis graduated from the University of Notre Dame Law School and is a member of the Maryland Bar. He received his B.A. from Connecticut College.
