Already over 30 years, Schurgers Design is a company which specializes in limited series of glass fiber reinforced products, from one-offs to series reaching up to 500 pieces. The Company focuses on high quality special products, prototypes and concepts. All stages of the manufacturing process - from concept design, prototype model, production mold, to end product - are carried out in-house. The production processes are ‘hand lay up’, ‘spray technique’ and ‘vacuum injection molding’.
