Barry Weatherall's Summary

Creative direction, personnel and project management for multiple Aquent Studios office locations. Lead two teams in concepting, design/illustration and coordination for worldwide-use printed materials, multimedia kiosks and interactive displays, trade shows. Work closely with Rockwell Automation Brand Standards Mgr. to maintain brand consistency/integrity.

Full understanding of pre-press, 1 – 4+ color work, magazine and newspaper page design, as well as two years of packaging experience. Drawing/painting skills (fine/digital), illustration in Photoshop/Illustrator. Aquent Studios creative direction & development team lead for new media (ePub, iBooks Author, iPhone & iPad app development).

Specialties

Work extensively with high end corporate clients. Flexibility and skill in listening to customers, delivering what they want and need. Well managed projects, accurate, and appealing to audiences. Branding and identity. Professional, approachable attitude regardless of deadline pressures, outstanding performance under stress. Delivers product that advances business communication objectives. Passionate for the work.
