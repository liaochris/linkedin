Lotta Holmström's Experience

Data journalist at SVT Pejl

SVT

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Broadcast Media industry

February 2013 – Present (9 months)

Content is king, and massive amounts of data is the kingdom. As data journalist, my focus is on the stories in the data, the people and the landscape of the kingdom, the patterns you see and the anomalies that turn up as you dig into it.
In my work I get to be hands-on in most steps of the data journalistic process. Gathering data – from databases, crowdsourcing etc – cleaning it, analyzing it, doing interviews, writing stories, visualizing the data in a way that it becomes more accessible and asking our readers/viewers for their input. Often we do follow up stories based on input from and conversations with our online visitors.
For instance, I've developed javascript solutions to do complex geographical mapping, as a way to let visitors explore data and find what is relevant for them where they live.
For different projects we at SVT Pejl work together with different teams and reporters within SVT. This makes the job very rewarding as there are a lot of extremely competent people covering different fields of news within the organization.
