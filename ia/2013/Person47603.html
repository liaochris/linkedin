Gemma Butlin's Summary

A creative and innovative self employed web designer with 6 years web design/development experience in agency/industry environments. Having the ability to solve complex design problems, usability issues in interactive design and also holding expertise with production for web and print mediums.

My goals are to produce optimised and accessible websites and quality design work within a creative environment.

Specialties

Adobe Dreamweaver/ Acrobat / Photoshop / Illustrator / Flash CS4, Visual Studio, Microsoft Office, Quark Xpress, print design including banners, brochures, printed stationery, concept development/design, design for content management / eCommerce, HTML/XHTML/CSS, email design, graphic design, graphical user interface, layout design, logo design, marketing including copy writing and search engine optimisation (seo).
