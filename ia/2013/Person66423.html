John Hall's Summary

Senior-level creative with more than 20 years of experience in the concept, design, creative direction, and project management of publications, marketing communication materials, and digital media.

In over ten years of managing my own design business, I have worked closely with editorial, marketing, and development teams in a wide-variety of industries to create books, magazines, newsletters, catalogs, annual reports, websites, and a wide variety of marketing collateral materials.

My clients have included colleges and universities, arts and research organizations, financial services companies, publishers, and many other types of businesses and industries.

Previously, I held senior art and design director roles on both the editorial and advertising side of a variety of publishers, marketing agencies, and digital media companies.

In addition to my design background, I have strong written communications skills, a passion for technology, and a keen interest in journalism and social media.

With this diverse set of skills and interests, I am able to make creative decisions with a deep understanding into the perspective of the various stakeholders within an organization, as well as different elements of the audience. I enjoy being involved in projects on both a macro and micro level—leading the creative and concept decisions and/or delving into the details of production, and even coding.

I have strong hands-on skills in all the relevant print design software packages including InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator, as well as a working knowledge of HTML, CSS, various content management systems, and SEO best-practices.

John Hall's Experience

Owner/Creative Director

John Hall Design Group

August 2001 – Present (12 years 3 months) Beverly, MA

Operate a full service graphic design business specializing in print and online publication design, art direction, and project management. Projects include magazines, books, catalogs, newsletters, annual reports, and all types of print publications as well as design of marketing collateral materials and corporate identity projects. Clients include Adviser Investments, Babson College, Collegia, Fidelity Investments, InvestorPlace Media, Landmark School, Lincoln Financial, The MacDowell Colony, McGraw-Hill, Pearson Education, Rockport Publishers, The University of Northern Colorado, The Urban Land Institute, and Wolters Kluwer.

We also offer web design (including Wordpress implementation), social media, and hyperlocal media consultation services. We have developed Wordpress-based websites for area schools, churches, and political organizations; author a hyperlocal blog on our home city's school system; and have advised community groups on their social media efforts.

Design Director

Experience, Inc.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

April 2000 – August 2001 (1 year 5 months)

Instituted an in-house creative department, and directed all design projects for an online and print publishing company that targeted the college market. Led a creative team that produced the award-winning career and lifestyle magazine Experience (circulation 1,000,000), as well as the experience.com web-site and all corporate marketing materials. Also led a rebranding effort that included development of a new corporate logo, creative standards, and prototype for a web-based recruiting network interface.

Senior Art Director

Pohly & Partners

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry

May 1996 – March 2000 (3 years 11 months)

Designed and art directed a variety of magazines for a custom publishing company. Primary responsibility was Continental (circulation 400,000), the inflight magazine of Continental Airlines. Completed a redesign and relaunch of the publication, and art directed each monthly issue. Supervised a staff of junior art directors and freelance designers. Also worked on the design of several smaller magazines, and assisted on new project launches.

Art Director

Running Times Magazine

August 1994 – May 1996 (1 year 10 months)

Art directed a monthly national magazine for competitive runners (circulation 75,000). Developed design concepts for features; art directed photo shoots; researched stock photography; commissioned illustrations; managed the art budget; hired and supervised freelance design assistants. Also managed the company’s computer network, and directed the design of marketing materials, media kits, and other special projects.

Art Director

Boston Business Journal

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Design industry

January 1989 – January 1991 (2 years 1 month) Greater Boston Area

Designed the cover of the weekly business newspaper, and art directed special supplements. Also served as Assistant Art Director of the award-winning bi-monthly magazine Boston Business.

Advertising Art Manager

Phoenix Media/Communications Group

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Newspapers industry

January 1986 – November 1988 (2 years 11 months)

Managed the art department of the weekly alternative newspaper. The six-person department produced all advertising and promotional work for the newspaper and radio station. Instituted a Macintosh ad production system, which was the company’s first use of desktop computer technology. Also served as Design Director of the Boston Bruins and Boston Celtics Official Yearbooks, which were published as contract publishing projects.
