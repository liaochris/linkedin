Daniel Ensign's Summary

Daniel Ensign is a finance assistant at O' Say Can You See PAC, the federal PAC of Maryland Governor Martin O'Malley. He is a recent graduate of the University of Maryland Robert H. Smith School of Business with a degree in finance. He has extensive experience in political fundraising and communications, including media tracking, social and new media, opinion writing, and constituency communication strategic planning.

Daniel has extensive experience in political advocacy and public service. In the past, he has interned at the White House in the Office of Public Engagement & Intergovernmental Affairs, Democratic National Committee, National Jewish Democratic Council, and the American Israel Public Affairs Committee.
