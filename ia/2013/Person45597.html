François Roughol's Summary

Senior level / game designer with 8 shipped AAA games and DLCs on X360, PS3, PS2, Wii and PC.

Creative problem solver with 7 years industry experience.

Worked as senior level designer and game designer on game products for major console and PC publishers, including Ubisoft, Electronic Arts, Valve Software and Take Two.

Responsible for designing levels and features in games to providing training for an entire team, designing and maintaining production methods and managing worldwide DLC releases.

Known as an innovative, dedicated and trusted facilitator with a reputation for follow-through, thoroughness and being results driven.

Team player and committed collaborator in dynamic environments, willing to meet challenges head on until successful.

Key Skills:

- Level design; gameplay scripting from concept to gold master.
- Game engine optimization; proven track record of optimizing individual levels to entire games.
- Game design; prototyping gameplay mechanics from concept to gold master.
- Combat design; technical requirements for boss fights from concept to gold master.
- Project management of professional teams on specific features, from concept to gold master.
- Strong focus on end-user experience; using telemetry and feedback data to deliver powerful results.
- Training and mentoring of talent in both level design and game design.
- Self motivation and continuous learning of various production methods, including Agile and Waterfall.
- Communication; analysis and solution driven approach to games development issues.
- 10 years experience developing online gaming communities and associated marketing.
- Tools: CryEngine 3, Unreal 3, Source Engine, Anvil, Jade, 3dsMax
