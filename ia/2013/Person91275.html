Melissa Coleman's Summary

Melissa Coleman has over 15 years experience designing professional PowerPoint presentations, running presentations on site, designing After Effects video animation, and creation of memory and candid faces shows for corporate or special events.

Her PowerPoint design skills are top notch and will help you communicate clearly, effectively and attractively. Whether your audience is a ballroom of 5,000, or a conference room of 10, Melissa can help you stand out and get your message across in a meaningful way.

Not only is she proficient in PowerPoint and Photoshop but she also incorporates her skills in Illustrator, After Effects, and Proshow Producer to ensure your presentation is beautiful from start to finish. She combines a great attitude with outstanding organization, and excellent communication to make sure you get the service you deserve.

Melissa is used to working in a fast-paced, deadline driven environment and will have no problem taking your presentation, web site, or video from concept to completion.

She’s able to work both independently and as part of a team in a variety of circumstances.Whether you require advanced animation, insertion of video or photo retouching, Melissa has you covered. She can also create touch screen kiosk presentations or hyper-linked presentations for the web, as well as travel with you to your on site location to make sure your presentation runs the way you intended.

Melissa has worked with top level executives and keynote speakers from companies such as Target, Victoria Secret, Heineken, Best Buy, Dell, Maybelline, Loreal, Medtronic, Upsher Smith, Reebok, Domino's, Citi Bank, KFC, Allergen, Boston Scientific, Barclays, Bear Stearns, Con Agra Foods, Miracle Ear, as well as many production companies such as Media Loft (MN), Martin Bastian (MN), StoneArch (MN), Showcore (MN), Inhouse (MN), AVHQ (MN), Image Media (NY), Michael Andrews (NY), MJM (NY), TAG Creative (NY), Broadstreet (NY).

Specialties

Melissa's specialty is providing presentation services including PowerPoint design and on site operations for corporate executives and keynote speakers. She is also experienced with kiosk design and setup for trade show events, audience response systems using Turning Point, and Corporate Candid videos and memory videos using ProShow Producer. Software expertise includes Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Wordpress, Turning Point, all versions of PowerPoint from 2003 - 2010, Excel and Word.
