Richard Hooey's Summary

Forward thinking companies focus upon “What’s next?”, while successful investment firms anticipate emerging risk factors. They both rely on making prudent decisions – value-based decisions, with minimal downside risk. That’s why leadership turns to me.

I offer a unique perspective - the skill to both value equity securities for investment funds and “green light” proposed, strategic initiatives for industrial companies. I understand what corporate leaders really need and what investors expect.

My analyses and recommendations enable leadership to make well-informed decisions that reduce potential risk, and realize more profit.
