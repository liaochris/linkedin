Gabriele Columbro's Summary

Grown in the flourishing Open Source Enterprise ecosystem of the last decade, Gabriele brings on the table a wealth of expertise as Senior Open Source Consultant, Enterprise Architect and technical team Leader.

With his master thesis on Business Process Re-engineering and almost 10 years in the IT business between Consulting, Sales and Engineering, he developed full-spectrum technical and project/deparment leadership skills, especially in the context of large scale Enterprise Content Management projects for key government and Fortune 500 global customers.

His technical leadership expertise and personable skills allow him to communicate effectively at Technical and Executive levels. For this reason, he plays a key connection role between Engineering and Business, both within his company and while working at Customers. Notable achievements for Alfresco were leading and being customers' advocate in cross-department projects like Product Benchmarks and Scalability Papers, as well founding and nurturing a developer ecosystem around the Maven Alfresco SDK.

Based in Amsterdam, Gabriele is fluent in at least 3 languages and his international profile allow him to play key corporate roles and perform strategic customer engagements both in EMEA and Americas, often bridging between company branches to accomplish strategic objectives. Among others, signature customers he worked for are SAP, Cisco, European Commission, Ministerio de Justicia (Spain), Mc-GrawHill, NBCUniversal, Marriott, Accenture.

Gabriele is an open source enthusiast, Apache Committer, active blogger and speaker at technical conferences. His main interests, apart from Alfresco and ECM, are the creation of a sustainable open source product development ecosystem and highly scalable systems benchmarking.

Gab's career objective is to evolve into Product Management, Chief Architect or Development Management...or to be CTO of his own startup if he finds time to build one :)
