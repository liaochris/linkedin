Jeffrey Arnold's Summary

Mr. Arnold is the president of Arnold Strategies, LLC, a strategic advisor to early and growth stage CEO's.

With prior experience running R&D, marketing and operations for large and midsized corporations, Mr. Arnold then spent 20 years as the CEO of multiple technology and life science companies. As CEO he raised over $140 million in venture capital, IPO and post IPO financings and achieved considerable returns for investors. He has founded, grown, turned around, bought and sold companies and taken them public.

He now applies what he has learned to helping business owners and entrepreneurs improve the performance of their companies and avoid mistakes. Working with only 6-8 companies at a time, and with engagements that typically last 1 to 3 years, Mr. Arnold has:

• Helped 14 companies raise angel or venture rounds totaling $61 MM.

• Helped 5 companies obtain non-dilutive financing including debt, grants and licenses.

• Helped numerous companies refine their business models, improve their profitability and grow their business. One closely held distribution company with previously flat sales grew 4x to $50MM in sales in 4 years without external financing. Another closely held services company grew 3x in the first year of working together.

• Identified and hired numerous advisors, board members, CXO's and senior team members for client companies.

• Helped 6 companies which achieved exits totaling $720 million.

Mr. Arnold is a guest lecturer at the MIT Sloan School, member of the MIT Venture Mentoring Society, on the Grant Review Board of the MIT Deshpande Center and an investor with Boston Harbor Angels and Mass Medical Angels. He was formerly Chairman of the Greater Boston Chapter of the American Heart Association. He received a BSEE from MIT in 1972.

Specialties: Strategic and business planning, marketing, organizational development, angel and venture financing, strategic partnerships, mergers and acquisitions.
