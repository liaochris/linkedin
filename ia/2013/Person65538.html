Joel Peck's Summary

I have been in film/video/audio/music media production for over 30 years working on a vast variety of projects from commercials to indie features, winning many national/international awards. I have worked as a freelance independent to owning a fully staffed mid-sized high-quality commercial production company and location, studio and post facilities.

During these many years I developed proficiency in many skills, from studio/performing/touring musician and audio engineer/music producer, composer, Maya 3D VFX/animation artist and designer, DP, writer, director, and producer, to production facility engineering and business management. Including a brief gig as adjunct professor teaching a graduate course “Directing for Film/Video” at American University in Washington, D.C.

About 4 years ago I walked out of the theater after seeing Cameron’s film “Avatar” still holding the 3D glasses in my hands, feeling inspired and mind expanded I said to myself “This 3D stereoscopic thing is huge and it is where I want be!”

Now I have a production company and boutique state-of-the-art 3D stereoscopic studio and post facility, which is focused entirely on participating in creating the next wave of new 3D stereo content. We are currently working on a full 3d stereoscopic super science fiction, action-adventure, metaphysical-fantasy project.

I want to use and progress the new creative and business possibilities this fantastic, enhanced artistic palette of 3D stereoscopic filmmaking offers.

I am looking to partner with other like minded filmmakers, producers and distributors in bringing imaginative, good quality 3D stereoscopic content to 3D stereo lovers and new audiences worldwide.

Producer, Director, Visual & Sound 3d Stereoscopic Media Designer

artHouse3d

2009 – Present (4 years)

artHouse3dTM is a stereoscopic production company with studio and post production facility. We believe in the future of 3D stereoscopic content for home entertainment, and other home and work place applications.

Our primary business and creative objective is to collaborate with like minded filmmakers, producers and distributors to create imaginative, good quality 3D stereoscopic content for worldwide distribution.

Although 3d film making is derived from 2d traditional film making and was invented many years ago, when using today's technology we can see a fresh new art form coming to life. Ready for new creative choices and visions. We keep that at the front of the decisive and creative hemispheres throughout all phases of production.

3dStereo Production:
3d full L/R HD, 2k, 4k, resolutions. Our green screen sound stage is well equipped to shoot video ready for any CGI virtual 3d world and/or live action composite.

3dStereo Post:
Our post-production approach is also all about 3d. From matte creation, DI, color, and Maya CGI, VFX, model animation, scene lighting, stereo camera renders, to complex 3d stereo composites.

3dStereo Sound:
A 5.1/7.1 sound track is in effect 360 degrees. But, often the 3d aspect of the design is underused or overlooked. We approach and create sound designs and music scores that follow and accentuate the whole 3d vision of the project for an enhanced 3d experience for the audience.
