Hammad Rajjoub's Summary

Hammad Rajjoub is currently working in Developer and Platform Evangelism Team at Microsoft Singapore.

His job is to drive and evangelize Windows Azure and Windows 8 adoption in Singapore. Most of the times he is busy driving (often high visibility, complex and mission critical) projects with top enterprise customers. His role requires a lot of cross team collaboration and he works closely with EPG (Enterprise), PS (Public Sector) and CA (Small/Medium and Corporate Accounts) segments.

Being in an external facing role, Hammad often acts as a conduit between a customer and a partner (delivering the solution to customer). This often implies Architect advisory as well as leading the delivery of projects as a Project/Program Manager.

In his previous role he was Vice President at Bank Of America Merrill Lynch where he was leading an Options Trading Platform as well as Real-Time downstream feeds.

He is a 7 times Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) for Connected Systems.

Hammad Rajjoub's Projects

  • AswaqVision – Real Time Financial Market Information System

    • March 2006 to September 2009
    Team Members: Hammad Rajjoub, Khurram Shakir, Jehanzeb Musani, Muhammad Ehsan Khan, PhD, PgMP, PMP, Irfan Rizvi, Rizwan Ahmed, Syed Faraz Mahmood, Fazeel Shaikh, Basel Dayyani

    AswaqVision is a stock market information aggregation and dissemination service that provides tools for active investors to analyze and contribute in regional financial markets. AswaqVision is based on Sharesoft Trading Platform (SSTP).

    one of its most import component is Real time data streaming server. Streaming Server is a scalable push engine for stocks data. The server preferred method of communication is TCP/IP, but it also supports HTTP based snapshot delivery. The optimized use of HTTP makes it immune to proxies and firewall restrictions. It has built-in features for adaptive streaming that results a robust and efficient data delivery. It is one of the integral components of Sharesoft Trading platform and benefited Fail-over and Load balancing capabilities from underlying architecture.

  • Online Realtime trading platform

    • March 2006 to September 2009
    Team Members: Hammad Rajjoub, Jehanzeb Musani, Danish Ahmed, Khurram Shakir, Moiz Uddin Shakih, Muhammad Ehsan Khan, PhD, PgMP, PMP, Haikel Hichri, Rizwan Ahmed, Fazeel Shaikh, Faiq Shaikh, Basel Dayyani

    Sharesoft Platform is set of coordinated technologies that facilitate development, deployment, and management of multi-tier, server-centric real time applications. Platform is based on following components/technologies:

    • Sharesoft Application Server
    • Sharesoft Application Server Management Console
    • Sharesoft Message Bus Connector Library
    • Sharesoft Platform Invoker Framework
    • Sharesoft Real time Streaming Services
    • Sharesoft Client Application Framework

    Sharesoft Application Server is a message oriented, microkernel based application server. This is an implementation of Inversion of Control (IoC) container, responsible to provide managed but multi-threaded execution environment for domain specific business components. The platform provides sophisticated support of load balancing and fail-over.

    Application Server relies on broadcast, multicast, and point-to-point messaging paradigm to orchestrate asynchronous/synchronous message passing among services to execute administrative and business tasks.
