Jamie Leo's Summary

My practice spans multiple marketing and creative disciplines, and includes comprehensive strategic and tactical collaboration with business clients and colleagues. I deliver cross-platform branding and communication experiences, including customized development of social media/networking strategies, architecture and content, as well as expertise in Adobe CS design programs – resulting in numerous awards, ongoing participation in industry panels, and awards adjudication.

Before being recruited to GRACE Communications Foundation, clients included IBM, Wolters Kluwer, Scholastic, McGraw-Hill, United Rentals, Office Depot, American Express – and other organizations, large and small.

Specialties

Multidisciplinary creative. Award-winning design and writing skills. UX design, typography and digital illustration.
