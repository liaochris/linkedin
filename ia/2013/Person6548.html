Andreas Sundberg's Summary

Andreas is a 28 year old Swedish designer trying to make the world a more a more functional and beautiful place. Born and raised in Stockholm and educated graphic designer at Pratt Institute in New York. He mostly spend his free time reading design and photography blogs, drawing digital paintings and shooting photos. He loves to create and some day hopes to have the role as an art director at a creative company that share his visions and ideas, and is hoping to get there by working with talented and passionate people in a creative and inspiring environment.
