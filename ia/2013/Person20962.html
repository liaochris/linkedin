Catherine Howard French's Experience

Principal

Veritas Leadership Group, LLC

September 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months)

We are a leadership development company. By developing leaders we drive organization effectiveness.

We focus on effective leadership because effective leadership is the backbone of high performing organizations - whether we are leading from an office on the 60th floor, the crosswalk stopping traffic as students head to school, or a hospital hallway as we mop the floor. Whether we are mentoring youth, installing air conditioners or booking a cruise, each of us is a leader and our decisions and actions affect others. How can we not afford to be the best we can be?

The foundation of robust leadership and high performing organizations consists of
+ Sound strategic plan, compelling mission and meaningful values
+ Corporate ethics and comprehensive framework for ethical decision-making

We develop successful leaders and drive organizational effectiveness using three methods
+ Group development of specific skill sets through our training programs: keynote speeches, seminars and workshops
+ Individual development of key groups – Millennials, High Potential Employees and Derailed Executives to develop specific skill sets/mindsets
+ Facilitate communications – we have a mediator and meeting facilitators who can help resolve conflicts or lead strategic or brain storming discussions; we utilize retreats, team building experiences to not only facilitate discussions but to help leaders develop their own communication skills.

We have a passion for developing the effective leaders and improving the performance of individuals and organizations. Call us - 602.795.1363!
