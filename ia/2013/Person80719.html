Mark Buzzard's Summary

We at Liberty Bell Alarm & Home Theater are committed to providing the highest quality of products and services to create a safe and pleasurable home environment for our clients.

Our teams of experienced professionals are dedicated to the installation of systems to enhance the everyday living experience in the modern American home; from the safety of a security system, the soothing sounds of a whole house music system, the excitement of a home theater, to the informational and technological advantages of structured wiring and home automation.

Our staff is highly educated and engaged with our ever-expanding industry, helping our clients make informed decisions for their homes.

We strive to create an atmosphere for caring, intelligent people to work together in pursuit of our goal to be the finest low voltage integrator in Northern California.

Specialties

Long standing builder relationships, purchasing power, industry certifications, IT staff, large recurring revenue stream, full integration and automation of all low voltage systems including burglar alarms, 24 hour monitoring, surveillance, home theater, whole house audio, structured wiring, networking, access control, Intercom & central vacuum systems.
