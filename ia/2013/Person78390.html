Larry Beacham's Summary

Larry Beacham is an award-winning internet marketing and social media expert, as well as published author and accomplished platform speaker based in Tampa.

Formally a cable installer from Ohio, Larry entered the world of internet marketing in March of 2009. Within 18 months he became known as the go-to person for small business owners searching for leads on LinkedIn. In September of 2009, Larry published his first work, "The LinkedIn Lead Generation Blueprint," which sold nearly 500 copies on the day it was released.

Over the last 4 years Larry has established an impressive professional profile on LinkedIn, as well as an impeccable reputation for results-driven training as a personal business coach. Multiple videos, articles and blog posts boast about Larry's effectiveness and his Google presence speaks for itself.

Having coached hundreds of people in every English speaking country, Larry focuses his attention on teaching small business owners and entrepreneurs how to leverage the internet with "Purpose Driven Marketing" by teaching them how to promote their unique value over sales pitches.

Larry is extremely proficient in the following disciplines: video marketing, blogging, article marketing, CPA Marketing, webinar and teleconference marketing, personal branding, product development, and SEO.

For speaking opportunities, consultation or professional coaching, you can reach Larry directly by calling 813-562-6602 or via email: Larry@StonecoldMillionaire.com

Specialties: Marketing and advertising, social network development, brand recognition
