Yalanda Lattimore's Summary

Successfully mastered engaging audiences and after decade plus in blogosphere with award winning DryerBuzz.com, new media pioneer Yalanda P Lattimore embraced coaching others and now offers a high demand series of workshops, Blogging for Business and Nonprofits, as well as #27Answers to Create Buzz publications.

"For businesses, blogging is about closing the sale and seeing measurable results," says Yalanda. For organizations, blogging is about meeting digital demand for information and connecting to those served. Blogging for Business and Nonprofits workshops helps both entities successfully create spaces to serve and engage with peers, audiences, and customers (PAC)."

Workshops provided through partnerships and alliances and serve as excellent lead generators for sponsors.
