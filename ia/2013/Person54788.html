Igor Mróz, PMP's Summary

I've been in the IT business for a number of years working as an analyst, consultant and project manager. I worked with customers and partners in Poland, Western (I lived for more than one year in the Netherlands) and Eastern Europe and in the Middle East (e.g. Yemen).

The whole adventure with project management dates back to my university years — I was challenged with organizing a huge conference. It was a tough one, but I liked it. In the following years I did some business on my own but decided that It would be better to gain some experience in large, international projects. That's how I ended up working in the corporate world.

I'm ambitious, well organized and methodical. Self-development, learning new things and honing my skills is my passion. I am also an open and easy-going person — traits of great value for successful collaboration.

My biggest asset is the fact that I can easily communicate with and understand both technically-oriented and business-oriented people. It is truly amazing how often these two groups cannot understand each other. I help them for their mutual benefit to achieve less conflicts, stress and delays in the endeavours that I contribute to.

My business goals: to develop leadership and strategic management skills & get more experience in a non-software-development environment

My skills: please refer to the "skills learned" section after each of the professional experience entries.
