Carol Harris-Fike's Summary

Certified professional coach specializing in personal leadership coaching as well as mentoring newly trained coaches. Personal leadership is about taking charge of your life... getting back on track with your purpose in this life.

Carol was trained by Newfield Network beginning in 2005. She became a Newfield Program Coach in 2009 and International Coaching Federation Co-ordinator for Newfield in 2013. She coaches people in Asia, Europe, and across North America. She also mentors beginning coaches around the world. Carol completed Newfield's advanced ontological training program: Body and Movement in September 2012.

Author of 5 Life Energies: The Choice You Have in How Energy Shapes Your Life, available on Amazon. Carol shares her understanding (based on the latest science) of how we can choose to shift the flow of our life energy and influence our movement, our emotions, and our language. Her book includes a research chapter, stories from her clients and her life, as well as many practices she uses in her coaching. Visit her website: YourLifeFromHere.com to learn more.

Co-owner of JICT Images, LLC, Carol and her partners, Irene Kelly and Jane Kerschner, created JICT Images, a set of 72 evocative photographs that take you to the heart of the matter. JICT Images are used by facilitators of groups of any kind, coaches, and individuals to move quickly and deeply into the emotional realm. Search for JICT Images under companies on Linked In to learn more.

Specialties: Ontological coaching distinctions for long lasting change...transformation in a desired domain of life and work, Explanation of brain/body research implications for their use of ontological coaching distinctions, Support for shifting the energy within and around a person to affect emotions and language through movement and even dance. Support for beginning coaches preparing for certification. Learning-how-to-learn theories and practices to support learners of all ages.
