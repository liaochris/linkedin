Andres Benoit's Summary

My fascination for audio technology dates back to my middle school years when I began my path with DJing. From then on, I was wrapped up in its evolving technology and artistic capabilities. As my interests in audio continued to grow, I also became captivated by digital media technology. Realizing that through computers there are endless creative opportunities, I continued venturing into the artistic side of technology and started learning graphic design, video editing, and web design.

My passion for music production and audio technology lead me to the School of Audio Engineering (SAE). My commitment and devotion allowed me to graduate at the top of my class in 2004 and immediately earned me an administrative role at Florida International University’s Audio-Visual and Media Department. I became a leader and role-model for my staff and further developed my managerial and business professional skills. During my studies at SAE and experience at FIU, I continued to learn new media software programs and attended conferences such as InfoComm, the Winter Music Conference, and Adobe Max to stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technological advances.

After obtaining much knowledge and experience, I branched out and co-founded Optimal Sounds, an event services professional DJ company. I applied my creative and technical expertise to lead many of the developmental stages of the company including branding, web design, and marketing. Since the founding of Optimal Sounds in 2009, I have directed all executive affairs and public relations, while still offering event production support. My meticulous attention to detail and organizational skills have helped pave the road map for Optimal Sounds’ vision and structured a foundation that offers clients a highly professional experience.
