Lindsay Lamb's Summary

I am not a traditional writer. Until recently, I did not have literary aspirations, and I never really considered myself a bookworm. I majored in Statistics, but what I loved about statistics was not data crunching, it was using the numbers to tell an unusual story. I started my career on the account side of the agency and helped tell Christmas stories through Volvo's eyes, fix it solutions through Paper Mate's perspective, and Samsung's fans how much we cared. However, I had the creative bug so I started working on my portfolio in the evenings. I taught myself InDesign and Photoshop and got advice from two Creative Directors at DDB. Then, I submitted my stuff to Cannes and became part of the Young Lions Creative Academy.

What I love about my job is that conversations often start with, "You know what would be awesome..."
