Aaron Max Epstein's Summary

I am an accomplished web / print designer, animator and developer with certifications in Doubleclick Core Studio. Since 1999 I've been contracted to work with top advertising agencies throughout NYC. My work has been seen with such clients as Deutsche Bank, XM Radio, BMW, Related, Merrill Lynch, Tiaa-Cref, Bride’s Magazine and Sony Music to name a few. I was also nominated for best design for a fashion site by Glam.com.

I'm currently continue to work on innovative concepts for myself and my clientele, pushing the boundaries whenever possible.
