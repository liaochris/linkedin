Bernie Freytag's Summary

My professional career started in college (MVCC, 1987-89) working for a local pennysaver in their production department. I found that I was quite good at the job and pushed further into schooling for a lifetime in design (attending RIT, 1989-91). Upon leaving college I was asked to freelance at an agency near my hometown (Romanelli Communication). Again, I found this to be a good fit for my skills. Eventually I was working here full time and quickly moved up to the position of Art Director. I worked in Vermont and Boston over the years following my first "tour" at Romanelli but eventually came back and now am in the Creative Director position. So, in the last two decades, I’ve worked as a Production Designer, a Graphic Designer, a Web Designer, an Art Director and now the Creative Director. My goal has always been to produce the most creative work possible, while staying true to the clients goals.

Specialties

Logo design, billboard design, web design, creative thinking, brainstorming, branding, identity, drawing, painting, filming, writing, blogging, etc.
