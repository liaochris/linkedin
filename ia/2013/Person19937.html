Carla Visser's Summary

I'm a dynamic global career management consultant who provides services to international companies, outplacement firms, relocation companies, universities and business schools as well as non-profit organizations to help international and American executives, professionals and students manage their careers.

I have also held senior management positions in training, marketing, and business development at AT&T, Nortel Networks and Verizon Communications. Expat for Nortel Network in Paris. My programs have always exceeded their objectives and achieved more than $310M.

My core competencies are global career development, training and program development and management.
