Martin Segal's Summary

I am a business owner. My first love has always been Music along with entrepreneurship! I have loved and always been involved in music through playing guitar in live bands and DJ'ing, listening to it and living it. Back in 1999 I started a mobile disc jockey service called Performance Sound that I ran part time successfully until late 2006. I still miss it to this day and kick myself for giving it up because when I did it was just at the tipping point. Well it's all water under the bridge. I can't go back I can only move forward and move forward I shall! Enter the birth of In-Tune Music Worx!

In-Tune was born with being the best music and disc jockey business in the country in mind! A company that will change the music / DJ business forever.
