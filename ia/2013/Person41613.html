Enio Miranda's Summary

Multidisciplinary professional in marketing and communication, performing business management, strategic and creative planning and heading productions on brand experience.

Experience in branding, digital media, events, promotional marketing, publishing, new business and startups in Brazilian market.

Worked in agencies, media and final client, attending companies like Unilever, Redecard, Petrobras, Equipotel, Etna and others, creating and implementing marketing and communication solutions for institutional upscale and sales increase.

Current Executive Director for National Tourism Confederation at São Paulo head office.

Professor for Master in Branding and Promotional Design at IED - Istituto Europeo di Design, Sao Paulo. Master degree in Communication, Media and Cultural Studies at Universities of Grenoble, Florence and Brasília.

Official jury to regional stage of AMPRO's MAAW Globes Awards 2013.

Board of Brazilian Academy of Gastronomy, nonprofit cultural association to promote Brazilian gastronomic heritage.

All details, list of clients and undertaken projects, see on the link: www.eniopm.com


Specialties:

- Strategic planning - marketing, polls and metrics, brand positioning, business approach, media, sales promotion
- Creative planning - demand reading, target perception, briefing, closeness to creative and production departments, brand alignment, processes management
- Project and executive team management
- Account management, public relations, public presentations
- Business development, negotiations and start-ups
- Production of BTL - promotional events, brand activation, digital, campaigns and media
- Editorial and content management
- Analysis report on communication routines for internal and external targets
- Design and architectural issues
- Special network on food, culture and tourism industries.
