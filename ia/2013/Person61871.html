Jennifer Kovaleski's Summary

I joined the NBC Bay Area team in February 2012. Previously I worked as a digital journalist at ABC11 Eyewitness News in Raleigh-Durham, North Carolina. I also previously worked in Columbia, Missouri, as a multi-platform reporter and anchor for KOMU-TV8. I worked at KOMU-TV8 for four years. During my time there I learned every aspect of the newsroom by holding a variety of positions. I began working on the assignment desk, but ended up working as a reporter, anchor, photojournalist, and web reporter.

I graduated Cum Laude from the University of Missouri's School of Journalism with a degree in broadcast and a minor in business. During my time in Missouri I also contributed to the local NPR affiliate, KBIA-FM.

I have a passion for reporting and digging for answers. I enjoy using multi-platforms to tell stories. What I love most about my job is the ability to learn something new every day and tell stories that bring change and impact people's lives.

I grew up in states all around the country. I was born in Reno, Nevada. I then moved with my family to California, Arizona, and Texas before settling in the place I call home Denver, Colorado. In my spare time, I enjoy cooking, working out, and spending time with friends and family.

Specialties

As a reporter I can shoot, edit, and write my own stories. I am am also a journalist who can provide enterprising stories and generate extra content for the web. My skills include: Avid iNEWS, Avid NewsCutter XP, Avid Active Content Manager, Macromedia Flash, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Audition, Sony XDCAM EX-1
