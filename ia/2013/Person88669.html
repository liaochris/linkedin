Matt Edgar's Summary

My clients refer to me as the “OFFICE SPACE DOCTOR.” They have PAIN associated with their office space, so they look for me to DIAGNOSE the problem and PRESCRIBE the appropriate REMEDY to their OFFICE SPACE PROBLEMS.

Typical PAIN indicators:
♦ Frustrated by high rent rates
♦ Overwhelmed by the time and headache involved with moving their office
♦ Concerned about rising operating expense pass-through bills
♦ Worried because their office space doesn’t align with future growth or contraction plans
♦ Aggravated because their landlord is unresponsive
♦ Pressured to reduce occupancy costs NOW
♦ Angry because their commercial real estate broker provides poor service

COMMUNITY FIRST COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE looks for ways to eliminate office occupancy pain. After a diagnostic session, and execution of the right strategy, the end result is PAIN-FREE, IDEAL OFFICE SPACE.

July 2009 – Present (4 years 3 months)

Some clients refer to me as the "Office Space Doctor." My official "title" is Broker/ Owner of Community First Commercial Real Estate, a Denver-based corporate real estate/ tenant representation firm. I help companies uncover ways to make their office space work better for them, or help them fix areas of pain associated with office occupancy.

What my firm and I do is not a fit for everyone. That said, we typically works with two types of clients. The first type, frankly, is very happy with their existing office facility however, they are often visionaries and may be open to working with me because I can help them:

♦ Reduce their monthly rent payment
♦ Improve the efficiency of their current office space
♦ Find an alternative office location that better-suits their needs

The second type of client is not a fortunate as the first. They do have vision but they aren’t totally satisfied with their existing office space. They may be:

♦ Frustrated by the high cost of their lease
♦ Concerned with rising operating expenses pass-throughs
♦ Upset because their landlord is unresponsive
♦ Under pressure to reduce costs NOW
♦ Mad because their commercial broker provides poor service!

I have helped Fortune 100 companies, startups, local and multi-national firms, and every kind of business in between. Typical clients experience a 20% reduction in occupancy costs, which has a direct, positive impact on their bottom line.
