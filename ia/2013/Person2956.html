Akshay Bhardwaj's Projects

  • Fly Robot Interface

    • November 2011 to August 2012
    Team Members: Akshay Bhardwaj

    This project involves development of robust interface to control NXP LPC1768 mbed based robot using fly's vision and horizontal motion detection neurons. It is developed based on wavelet transformation along with kalman filter which is implemented on a fly motion algorithm developed during this project.

  • Preparation of Novel Three-Dimensional Scaffold and its Role in Tissue Engineering

    • August 2010 to May 2011
    Team Members: Akshay Bhardwaj

    During this project novel scaffold composed of PGA and Gelatine (cross-linked using gluteraldehyde) was made. In later phase, physical and biological characterisation of this novel scaffold was done to assess viability of scaffold.

  • Enzymatic surface modification of Poly acrylonitrile.

    • September 2009 to January 2010
    Team Members: Akshay Bhardwaj

    Project started with study of the hydrophilation action of Nitrile hydratase isolated from a specific
    bacterial strain, on Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) and hence determine the optimum conditions (eg:
    pH,salt concentration etc.) at which the isolated enzyme would work efficiently. After that we
    started modifying surface activity of Poly acrylonitrile against Nitrile hydratase enzyme.

  • Sequence analysis and structure prediction of Mycobacterium tuberculosis H37Ra 4-hydroxy-2-ketovalerate aldolase

    • August 2008 to November 2008
    Team Members: Akshay Bhardwaj

    Project goal was to predict the structure of 4-hydroxy-2-ketovalerate aldolase produced in
    mycobacterium tuberculosis(H37Ra) using bioinformatics tools. Project also aimed at studying
    where and how the key molecules in Biphenyl degradation cycle will be attached to this protein
    using docking software (autodock).

  • Faculty Profiles

    • January 2009 to March 2009

    It is an online portal used by faculties to edit their profile, displayed on main website of IIT
