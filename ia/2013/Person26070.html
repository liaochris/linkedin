Amy Wolfgang's Summary

Certified Professional Career Manager and business owner with proven expertise in the areas of life-long career management, assessments, career selection and job placement. Her company provides full-service career development and professional career coaching with a focus on 1) educating clients about appropriate career strategies to achieve goals and 2) empowering clients to make informed decisions. She holds the Professional Career Manager (PCM) certification and a Master's Degree in Educational Psychology from The University of Texas at Austin.

Amy offers a wealth of experience as a professional career coach and career counselor:
• Coached individuals at all levels from New College Graduates to senior-level Executives.
• 8 years of corporate experience including 2 years in resource management and 2 years in training and development for a Fortune 1000 corporation.
• 2 years as a Lecturer on career strategies at The University of Texas McCombs School of Business.
• Worked with and coached over 1,000+ people
• Business owner
• Frequent guest presenter on career related topics
• Experienced in Career Counseling

For Amy, career coaching not only utilizes her years of experience developing talent but also taps into her passion for helping others. Amy is dedicated to helping her clients become empowered and confident in their career search and achieve their career goals.

CEO & Career Coach

Wolfgang Career Coaching

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

June 2009 – Present (4 years 7 months) Austin, TX

I am a certified Career Coach and help clients take an active role in managing their careers. My staff and I work with all of our clients to set short-term and long-term career goals and help hold them accountable in reaching those goals. We help breakdown the overwhelming career search/job search process into manageable steps.

Our clients tend to fall into three areas:
- Clients who are in the midst of a career transition or returning to the workplace. Clients in this category tend to need help in career exploration, discovering the right careers for them, branding themselves for a specific career and creating a strategy for their career search.

- Clients who are in an active job search and need assistance with branding, resume writing, LinkedIn profiles, interview preparation or job search strategy.

- Clients who are relatively happy in their careers, but are looking forward to the next step in their career. They are looking to develop the right skills and position themselves for the next opportunity.
