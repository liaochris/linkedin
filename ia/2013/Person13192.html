Producer

KFMB

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Broadcast Media industry

April 2005 – June 2007 (2 years 3 months)

My main responsibility was Producer for the one-hour 5pm newscast on the weekends, but that was just the beginning.

Beyond being a self-starter and guiding our news coverage on weekends, I also led a team of writers while offering critical feedback to help improve the end product. This Producer role also required serious time management skills and adapting to constant change.

I was also called on to be a co-Producer for the weekday 6:30pm newscast for an extended period of 7 months. When not producing, I worked as a writer for other newscasts, and also as a video editor on the station's non-linear editing system.

Advance planning as an Assignment Editor was also a big part of the job. As the main weekend Producer, it also fell on me to set stories up in advance. That means working the phones and determining what will work ahead of time so we can be as efficient as possible with limited resources when the weekend arrives.
