ZoomOnme Studios Shaun Alexander's Experience

January 1985 – Present (29 years)

Professional Los Angeles photographer Shaun Alexander has earned his success and reputation in Hollywood since 1985.Shaun’s love and fascination for photography started when he was given a toy camera at the age of 2.
According to his father, he never left that toy camera out of his site, always taking pictures of his friends and family as he was growing up, even when he was pursuing a career in medicine. Shaun supported his short 7 years of medical school by photography, but never knew

that his hidden talents can some day to be his life and legacy. After he left the medical school, he decided to get a degree in Art and photography. His unprecedented obsession for art enabled him to graduate with honor and pursue his true passion, “FASHION PHOTOGRAPHY”
