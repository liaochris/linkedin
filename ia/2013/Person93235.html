Michal Szafranski's Summary

After spending most of my professional career in IT on business development positions, I've decided to fulfill another mission: become a full-time blogger evangelizing everyone who wants to hear about personal finances, getting debt-free and achieving wealth through hard, self-growth. I teach my Readers how to become their own financial advisors... and I enjoy it :)

I've chased my dream to retire at 40. And I did it - somehow... :) I'm working for fun giving away my knowledge, hustling, learning from others and trying to make the world a better place to live. I hope I can help you and that you can benefit from it.

If you would find my experience worthy and would like to get in touch, feel free to contact me. I'm open for all new experiences although I'm carefully choosing what to do with my retirement :)

Specialties: Key Account Management, Project Management, Business Development, Strategy Development, Product Lifecycle Management, M-Business-Consulting, Mobile Services, International Cooperation.
