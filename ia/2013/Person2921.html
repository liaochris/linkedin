Aki Pasoulas' Summary

Composer of electroacoustic and acoustic music, with a PhD in music and time perception. Board member of the UK and Ireland Soundscape Community. Extensive teaching experience in topics related to electroacoustic music, sound perception and sonic art. International presence as a composer (at ISCM, ICMC, SMC, WFAE, Sonoimágenes, Festival Synthèse, EMUFest amongst others). Co-ordinated and organised conferences, workshops, sound installation events and electroacoustic music concerts.
Composed c.300 pieces for various instruments, voice, recorded and electronic sound, multi-channel works, sound installations, music for film and for the theatre. Organised, conducted and participated in instrumental/vocal ensembles, and in electronic improvisation ensembles using laptop, various controllers and found objects.

Specialties

Electroacoustic music, particularly acousmatic music; time and timescale perception in relation to music; psychoacoustics and sound perception; spatial sound; acoustic communication; soundscape ecology, especially in relation to listening psychology.
