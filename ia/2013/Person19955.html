Carl Corry's Summary

I am the online editor for local news at Newsday, charged with delivering hyperlocal news and information to Long Islanders.

Multimedia and mobile journalism are our key methods.

I am also a Director At-Large for the Society of Professional Journalists, the largest and most broad-based journalism organization in the country.

My experience spans the print, TV and online worlds.

Before Newsday, where I started in 2010, I was executive producer of News 12 Interactive, running the Web and interactive TV operations of the seven News 12 cable stations. I also launched and ran a hyperlocal initiative covering towns on Long Island and New Jersey called News 12 My Town.

I was previously editor of Long Island Business News and an Internet reporter for Marketwatch.com.

I was also a community news and business reporter.

I’m a big lover of history, genealogy and my grandparents’ old recipes.

Specialties: Editing, reporting, newsroom management, project management, social media.
