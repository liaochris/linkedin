Frank Giammatteo's Summary

Professional Experience:
I am a professional Artist/Designer with experience both as an in-house corporate creative designer and an agency designer, developing creative solutions for B2B and B2C markets, while working directly with the Marketing departments, Art Directors and/or operating independently, prioritizing and meet due dates within a short time frame.
As a freelance Artist/Designer I enjoy working direct with clients and colleagues; producing illustrations, design solutions, web UI designs and art pieces.

Goals:
Placement in a creative environment with room for growth and the opportunity to advance. A position which job duties may include, but not necessarily be limited to creative graphic design, both traditional and digital illustration, web UI design, computer graphics, brand development, packaging design and developing other creative solutions for clientele. I enjoy working with a team or working independently.
In closing, It has been my life long pursuit to become a professional creative.
I am passionate about both art and design; as an individual who holds high personal standards, great integrity and honesty, I believe that I can be a great addition to any creative staff.

Specialties

Graphic design solutions, Illustration, web UI design, branding, designing publications and collateral material, packaging design, photo manipulation, apparel graphics, photography, professional quality mock-ups and presentation boards, pre-press production and developing other creative solutions for clientele. Other responsibilities include: Maintaining graphics server, conducting computer maintenance and repair in the art department.
10+ years of experience in graphic design industry.
