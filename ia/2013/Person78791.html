Laura Haykel's Summary

Laura Haykel is a Mom-preneur, coach & consultant who is passionate about inspiring, motivating and empowering others to step into their greatest potentials and live their greatest lives. She has founded two companies; iFlourish Consulting & Uplift 2 Uplevel coaching.
