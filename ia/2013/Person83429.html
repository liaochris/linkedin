Luke Gheen, MBA, CPA's Summary

Luke Gheen, MBA, CPA, is a long time resident of Colorado with extensive finance and accounting experience with large and small businesses in the Colorado area, including Lockheed Martin, Agilent Technologies, Plasmon Inc. and more.

Luke particularly enjoys working with small business owners who want to grow their businesses. He works closely with them to develop goals, outline solutions and offer guidance and trusted advice to help them improve their bottom line, grow their business and plan for the future. Luke understands the challenges facing small business owners and has the experience to help them succeed.

Businesses and Individuals have hired Gheen and Co., CPA, LLC when they need help with:

• Outsourced CFO and Controllership
• Business accounting
• Tax planning & saving money in taxes
• Income tax preparation
• Financial management and consulting
• Budgeting and financial projections
• QuickBooks set up and maintenance
• Starting a business
• Incorporations and entity selection
• Personal financial statements
• Cash flow analysis
• Budgets
• Financial projections
• Strategic business planning
• Business process improvement


Luke graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration from the University of Colorado in 1999 and received his MBA from the University of Colorado in 2007.

Luke is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and is chairman of the Finance Committee at Pulpit Rock Church. He loves to spend time with his wife, three sons and extended family.

Specialties: Outsourced Controllership | Hi Tech Industry | Healthcare Industry | Professional Service Industries | Law Firms | Architects
