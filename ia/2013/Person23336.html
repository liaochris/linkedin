Chris Haines' Summary

Owner /director Chris Haines is a 30+ year veteran of riding, racing and winning in Baja, and considered to be the world's #1 off-road tour operator. Chris Haines Motorcycle Adventure Company is partnered with the Honda Rider's Club of America and you'll ride the newest Honda motorcycles and ATV's.

Chris Haines Motorcycle and ATV Adventure Tours are set apart by Chris' legendary 13 wins of the Baja 1,000 as well, as receiving the highest recognition as the newest inductee into the Off-Road Motorsports Hall of Fame in October 2010.

As a testament to his accomplishments, Chris is continually recognized by both the Mexican and American Governments as an expert in all things off-road. Chris was awarded the prestigious “Amigo de Baja California” award by the Governor of Baja, California and Mexican Department of Tourism.
