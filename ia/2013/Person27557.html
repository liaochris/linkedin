Craig Dennis' Summary

I'm a designer with a passion for the web focusing on interfaces, interactions & experiences.

People say that I'm lucky to love what I do. They're wrong; it's not luck. The harder you work the 'luckier' you get and I worked very hard to turn my hobby into a career.

Each project is different but usually include producing initial sketches following onto detailed wireframes and then crisp, sharp interface mockups. I'm a designer who codes so also capable of implementing the designs with semantic, accessible markup and adding advanced interactions in javascript. I do not consider myself a developer though.

I would love to work for a year or two on the US West Coast. San Francisco would be ideal. In this industry I can work from anywhere but that is somewhere I would love to try.

I currently work for an agency during the day (and sometimes the night) and I also work on a freelance basis for some extra-pocket money here and there.

I try and make time to learn something new every day. I'm active on Twitter; sharing with similarly minded people or getting trolled. I also contribute to the community where I can on Stack Overflow and GitHub. I also write articles when I get a chance.

I'm not all work though and enjoy keeping fit. I'm currently training for Tough Mudder. When I'm not training, I'm driving. Really fast. Around racetracks. In a Nissan 350z.

I graduated with a 1st class honours degree from Ravensbourne and have interned at ITV Future Technologies and Samsung design Europe (where I was selected to travel to Korea and present to the company's directors and President).

I prefer Photoshop to Illustrator.
I have milk and one sweetener in my coffee.
My favourite film is Anchorman.
My guilty pleasure is a Passoa & Lemonade.
Sarcasm is not the lowest form of wit.
