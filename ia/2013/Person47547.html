Léon Toeset's Summary

From idea to clear-cut message
Anyone can hold a camera. But not anyone can translate an originally good idea into a clear-cut message in sound and vision. We will help you to get a clear picture of your ideas and the goals that you wish to achieve. Therefore, our job often involves more work ‘off camera’ than the shooting itself. Brainstorm sessions for instance, or developing concepts and scripts, and editing in the studio of course. To touch or to educate your audience, or to convey a message that stays on their mind, that’s what we aim for.

Professional, engaging and compelling
A corporate movie is a powerful way to show what your company is about. Core values and mission statements become vibrant and alive if you charge them with emotion and perception. In just 2-3 minutes, a film can create more impact than a presentation of half an hour. It’s a great way to enhance sympathy and loyalty. But also to easily explain products, services or processes. Once your film is finished, we will help you to share it through all available types of media.
