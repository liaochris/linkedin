Jason Cresswell's Summary

I have been living and working in South Korea since 1999. I first started as a teacher and then ran recruiting company from 2004 until 2010.

I am currently the owner of Traveler's Bar & Grill, located in Bundang and Daegu.

Our goal is to expand to other cities throughout Korea. We are open to franchise inquiries and outside investment in either equity or debt positions to finance expansion.

I am also available for consulting with companies looking to bridge the gap between Korean and Western culture.
