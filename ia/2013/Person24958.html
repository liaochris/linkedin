Cindy Hill's Summary

I love caring for dogs and cats and help their parents travel stress-free knowing their dog or cat are having fun at our special dog ranch, dog retreat with custom dog boarding and cat care.

Its fun to see shy or scared dogs gain confidence with our special manners ranch training that I have my llamas, horses and chickens help dogs learn patience around strange animals.

My goal is help others start their own small dog and cat care business. I helped a partner open another dog ranch in Snohomish using Mystic Mountain Pet Retreat setup, care and philosophy.

I have 20 years in the corp life, 5 years as product manager for Microsoft Cert Program , 10 years as product/system manager for Boeing in Electrical Systems for engineering tools; these roles will enable me to run Mystic Mt and to help others move from corp life to their own pet business.

Specialties: Dog Boarding, Dog Ranch, Dog Retreat, Dog Training, Cat Boarding, Cat Care, special pet needs, own and manage Mystic Mountain Pet Retreat since 2005.

Buy it on Amazon, search on Got Dogs and Cindy Hill: http://www.amazon.com/Dogs-What-Fido-When-Travel/dp/0615493491/ref=sr_1_15?ie=UTF8&qid=1311716997&sr=8-15

Easy tips to help you determine if you should bring Fido on your trip; leave him home with a pet sitter; or take him to a pet hotel, dog ranch, dog boarding kennel or your trusted veterinarian.

10 Reasons To Read This Book
1. Learn how Fido can be healthy and happy while you travel.
2. Enjoy your trip with peace of mind knowing that Fido is receiving loving care.
3. Know your available dog care options.
- Bring Fido with you on your trip.
- Leave Fido at home to be cared for by family, friends, neighbors, pet sitters, dog walkers or dog trainers.
- Take Fido to dog ranches, pet resorts, pet hotels, dog boarding kennels or veterinarians.
4. Determine the right care for your dog and your family.
- Review your dog’s personality, breed, health and energy level.
- Review yourself, your family and your desires for Fido.
- Review your pet care choices to ensure they are right for your dog.
5. Find out what helps Fido be a great traveling buddy.
6. Know when to have your friends or family care for your dog, when to use a professional pet sitter or when to go to a dog boarding kennel.
7. Learn how to find the best professional animal care from referrals, online searches and social media.
8. Know what to look for and what questions to ask while touring a dog boarding kennel.
9. Know what pet care facilities and options you should walk away from.
10. Tips before, during and when you return to ensure a successful experience for your family and Fido.
