Joe Miller's Summary

Joe’s work in design has received national to international recognition in more than 60 books and periodicals including AIGA annuals, American Corporate Identity annuals, The Design of Dissent, Graphis books, Just Design, Communication Arts, How, Print, and many other respected publications. He has published articles in AIGA Journal of Graphic Design, LIMN, and Studio. He has received more than 120 regional to international awards for graphic design and his work has been included in exhibitions nationwide. Corporate clients have included Hewlett-Packard, Compaq, Hitachi Data Systems, Cooper, Cadence, and Quantum. Cultural clients include TheTech Museum, Arts Council Silicon Valley, Works/San José, and San Jose Museum of Quilts and Textiles. Individual projects include a wide range of material from corporate identity to symbols, screen icons, exhibit design, environments, and print communications.

Regarded as a regional leader in design and the arts, Joe is president of the Board of Directors of Works/San José; is a member of the Board of Directors of AIGASF; is a member of the Board of Directors of Poetry Center San José; and is on the San José City Hall Exhibition Committee.

Specialties: identity design, exhibit design, visual branding, symbol and icon design for screen and print
