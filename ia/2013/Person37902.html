Dr. Holly's Publications

  • Adrenal Fatigque

    • Summer Bay Press
    • 2012
    Authors: Dr. Holly

    This book explores the various functions of the adrenal glands; the causes of hyper and hypo adrenal function and fatigue; the impact on the rest of the body; and what to do to resolve it. If you have diabetes, hypothyroid, foggy brain syndrome, fatigue, or a whole host of other issues, then this is a must read.

  • Cancer : Why what you don't know about your treatment could harm you.

    • Summer Bay Press
    • 2012
    Authors: Dr. Holly

    The book explores cancer from an allopathic perspective with all of the allopathic treatments along with their research, side effects and outcomes. Then the books explores a wide variety of other treatment options, the research and the outcomes. You will be amazed at what the research really says.

  • The Chocolate Controversy: the Bad, the Mediocre and the Awesome

    • Summer Bay Press
    • 2012
    Authors: Dr. Holly

    This book explores the origins of chocolate; the evolution of chocolate; the different types of processing of chocolate; horrors of most chocolate; the illusion of dark bitter chocolate; the one current patent that protects the over 300 nutrients in the chocolate. The book also explores all the different clinical applications and medicinal uses of chocolate.

  • Managing Your Weight: Why your body may be working against you and what you can do about it

    • www.choicesunlimited.ca/store
    • 2012
    Authors: Dr. Holly

    This book explores the history of the perception of weight and what women "should" weigh. Then the body explores a wide variety of factors and variables that can contribute to weight issues from the psychological to a huge and wide variety of physiological issues that have nothing to do with calories or metabolism. A must for anyone with challenges about their weight.

  • Depression: The real cause may be your body

    • Summer Bay Press
    • 2012
    Authors: Dr. Holly

    Historically we only had a few understandings of the cause and treatment of depression: it was either psychological, neural imbalance or hypothyroid and you could treat it with either anti-depressants, synthyroid or some form of counselling. Today we understand that there a wide variety of dysfunctions, disorders and imbalances in the body that can be the root of psychological and neurological illnesses including depression. If you have battled with any kind of psychological cognitive or emotional disorder, this book is a must read.
