Linda Wehrli's Publications

  • Classic Series: Beginning Basics for the Piano Vol. 1, Edition 7

    • Wehrli Publications
    • 2012
    Authors: Linda Wehrli, Barry Michael Wehrli

    "Classic Series: Volume 1 Beginning Basics for the Piano" is a unique study of beginner theory, ear training, finger technique and repertoire, researched and tested by the authors in their own teaching practices. These elements are brought together into one book, eliminating the need to shuffle between separate method books.

    "Volume 1" takes the student from absolute beginner to playing music on the grand staff, hands together, white keys only, with dynamics and articulation. The end result is a piano student who understands musical concepts and can apply them with skill and precision in the playing of beginner-level music, prepared to move up to the Intermediate level course.

  • Classic Series: Intermediate Basics for the Piano, Volume 2, Edition 3

    • Wehrli Publications
    • 2012
    Authors: Linda Wehrli, Barry Michael Wehrli

    "Classic Series: Intermediate Basics for the Piano, Volume 2, Edition 3" continues where "Classic Series: Volume 1" leaves off, advancing into intermediate music containing intervals, chords, key signatures, the damper pedal, 8th, 16th, tripleted and dotted note values and more.

    The end result is a piano student who has complete understanding of the notation and terms found in intermediate music and can apply them with expression and control in the playing of such music, ready to tackle the wealth of musical material available on the market.
