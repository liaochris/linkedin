Greg Lauver's Summary

My life includes a smorgasbord of unrelated pursuits: from entertainment to hard science, writing, computing, management, and more. I've found ridiculously simple solutions to expensive problems, designed and built software systems which senior analysts thought impossible, and rescued people and animals from unfortunate fates.

I'm a nuts and bolts guy and a straight shooter, but not a hotshot. Compared with schooled IT people, my accomplishments are impressive because I taught myself everything due to lack of funds and while otherwise fully employed.

Since 2007, I have promoted my mobile computing support business in Durango, Colorado. In 2010, I moved to Michigan and have resumed business here.

I've found that life doesn't have to be rocket science, and I most enjoy seeing people lit with inspiration.
