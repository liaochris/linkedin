Cheryl Robertson's Summary

I'm Cheryl Robertson, a versatile writer with accomplishments in technical writing, project management, software testing, computer graphics, animation & Website design & development—some of which have been used as examples in magazines, books & on PBS. I'm also known to be upbeat!

My background spans 20+ years in fields of wireless technology, Internet, IT & electronics; with start-ups to large companies & leaders such as Nokia, Novatel Wireless, PacketVideo & Legrand.

I’ve written for award-winning software products; for techies & non technical audiences, on very complex technology. I also have considerable ability as a Digital Artist to boot.

My written works have been printed, shrink-wrapped, stored on websites, downloaded & translated into numerous languages—including Chinese. I've written for over a million users worldwide & trained numerous individuals in the use of computer software & security issues.

The power of digital technology has provided us with a truly connected world!
