Christina Bush, CES, CAS, CMFC's Summary

Soon after graduating from Western Carolina University in 1992, Christina Bush began her career in the financial industry with Wells Fargo Investments, where she spent the next 18 years and progressed to a position as Vice President/Senior Financial Consultant assisting high net worth clients with all of their investment and insurance needs. In 2010 she decided to start her own practice, CB Wealth Advisory, where she has access to a much wider variety of products and solutions for the clients whose accounts she manages. Guiding each portfolio is a conviction that investment decisions are made with the clients' interests and objectives foremost in mind. She uses a rational approach to portfolio construction that is centered on strategic asset allocation--determining the optimal combination of asset classes designed to produce growth, and income, while minimizing capital risk.

Securities offered through Securities America, Inc., Member FINRA (www.finra.org) and SIPC (www.sipc.org). Advisory Services offered through Securities America Advisors, Inc., an SEC Registered Investment Advisory firm; Christina Bush, Registered Representative. CB Wealth Advisory and the Securities America companies are unaffiliated.

Securities licensed in CA, WA, FL, NC, and TX.

Christina also is a photographer with a passion for wildlife and exotic animal conservation. She owns Animal Magnetism Photography and is the creator of Youth for Wildlife, where she uses her images to educate and raise awareness for the conservation of animals all over the world. Her images have been featured by Animal Planet, National Geographic, ABC's Extreme Home Makeovers, The International Anti-Fur Coalition, The Paw Project and various newspapers, ads and websites across the globe. She has a monthly column in Expressions of India magazine by iCare featuring her photos and articles about various species of wildlife and conservation issues. See images at
www.christinabush.com and www.youthforwildlife.com.
