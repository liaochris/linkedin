Maxine Goulding, CDMP's Summary

As an event planning and coordination company, California Special Occasions (CSO) builds relationships with their clients first. Through a consultation, CSO talks with their clients to determine their objectives, the vision and ambiance of their event. It takes a little time, but is well worth the effort. CSO finds this process a win-win situation for all parties involved and this helps set expectations right up front - no surprises. The next step is to talk about budget. Why? Without one there is no event. CSO has a lot of experience, connections and affiliates to help work with any budget.

Having this help at hand will relieve the client of much of the stress associated with event planning. They can relax and enjoy their special day with the peace of mind that a professional Event Planner is taking care of every last detail.

Our events include, but are not limited to;
- business conferences
- grand opening of a new business
- customer appreciation events
- award dinners
- weddings
- anniversaries
- milestone birthdays
- private and holiday parties
- Bar and Bat Mitzvahs
- retirement party and more.
