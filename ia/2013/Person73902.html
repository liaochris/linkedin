Kelly D. Morris' Summary

When honored with a captive audience, we must transport them, entertain them, make them laugh, or teach them. Every project can make a difference and one thought, embedded in an open mind, can change the course of history. I am eager to learn and grow through every new project.

Specialties: I love crafting scenes, subtleties in performance and emotion enhanced by finding that elusive moment that, when placed, changes context entirely. I have a dry sense of humor and appreciate subtleties. I've spent a lot of my free time at concerts and incorporate music as gracefully as possible. I have much love for funky edits that don't feel gratuitous. I don't think cleverly placed dissolves will ever go out of style.

I will happily work as an Editor or Assistant Editor, and am focused on working on scripted features and television shows.
