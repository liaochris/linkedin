Greg Smith, MS's Summary

Greg Smith is a motivational leadership speaker, author and business performance consultant. He is a recognized authority on talent management, leadership development and employee engagement.

Greg teaches businesses how to accelerate individual and organizational performance. He helps businesses navigate change and transform themselves into great places to work that attract, retain and motivate people to their full potential.

His leadership keynote speeches and presentations are inspirational, informative and entertaining with a highly interactive style that connects with audiences. He combines story and strategy to teach audiences how to create great places to work that attract customers, drive employee retention, employee engagement and greater job satisfaction.

Smith has addressed organizations in over 26 countries. He has spoken to hundreds of state and national associations, SHRM chapters, healthcare organizations and businesses. He has consistently been rated at one of the top speakers nationally.

He is the publisher of the Navigator Newsletter, author of several books and a contributing editor to many publications and websites.

Greg works with organizations who want to select the right people for the right jobs and provides a variety of hiring, development assessments and DISC training. Retreats, facilitation, individual and team coaching provided as part of our service.

SPECIALTIES: motivational speaker, atlanta speaker, change management, leadership development, keynote speaker, DISC, 360 assessments, transformational leadership, strategic planning, facilitation, leadership speaker, keynotes, DISC certification, talent management, employee engagement, customer service, corporate retreats

  • Boosting Employee Engagement: 50 Ways to Motivate and Unleash Employee Performance

    • April 9, 2013
    Authors: Greg Smith, MS

Engaged employees stay with their employers, have higher levels of job satisfaction and make significant contributions. This 24-page book provides 50 easy to implement and low or no cost ways to motivate and energize your workforce.
