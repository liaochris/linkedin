Robert Laird's Summary

I happily reside in Brighton with my wife and two beautiful children. In addition to teaching piano privately, I am the Assistant/Youth Music Director at St. Paul’s Evangelical Lutheran Church in Pittsford. I am also a member of the Christ Episcopal Church Schola Cantorum under the direction of Stephen Kennedy. The culmination of my experience as a pianist, church musician, and teaching assistant at the Eastman School has led to a unique pedagogical style.

I am well versed at piano, guitar, and voice, and I am comfortable improvising, arranging and composing. I regularly perform and lead youth ensemble performances on a weekly basis and would draw on those experiences when leading student performances. I am knowledgeable in a wide-rage of music pedagogies including Orff, Dalcroze, Kodály, MLT, and strongly believe in experiential learning through self-discovery.

In order to coordinate a demanding schedule as a self-employed musician, private piano teacher of forty students, and church musician, I continually strive to present information clearly, concisely and efficiently, and maintain free flowing lines of communication. I maintain self-designed websites, creating a centralized hub of the latest pertinent information and teaching materials (http://robertlaird.net or http://stpaulspittsford.org/music). I believe these skills directly inform my classroom management style and speak to my ability to handle all demands of a music teaching position.
