Founder, President and Chief Financial Officer

Federated Precision, Inc.

February 2011 – Present (2 years 8 months) Deerfield Beach, Florida

Federated Precision is an Advanced Manufacturing Enterprise that conducts high-speed, ultra tight tolerance precision manufacturing for the Medical Device, Aerospace, Energy, and Defense Industries.

FP is an Information Technology driven, highly scalable company designed from a fresh approach to provide original equipment manufacturers with high-precision components and engineering services using both emerging, additive technologies (3D Printing) and modern, subtractive techniques (CNC Machines). FP utilizes the latest digital technology, combined with networked virtual communications, and the most advanced manufacturing equipment available globally.

Further, FP's commitment to hire, train and retain the brightest, customer-centric engineering and manufacturing talent available, allows it to fully utilize the company's investment in technology while forming deep supplier relationships with clients and inventors to collaboratively manufacture complex metal and composite components for industries representing multi-billion dollar markets.
