Harry R. Burger's Summary

I design products for the airport ground support equipment industry. This can include anything from wheel chocks to lavatory drain couplings to ring gears for fly wheels that go into the Tug that tows your airplane to the gate.

My second major in college was Science and Technology Studies, which looks at how technology affects people and how people affect technology, which helps me design for the needs of all stakeholders, who aren't always obvious.

I'm also searching for clients for my side business, freelance design consulting and CAD modeling, and selling my own custom products, including wax seals (think King Arthur/Ned Stark) through Shapeways (see links above).

Specialties: 3D CAD modeling (Certified SolidWorks Associate, over 10 years experience), design for manufacturing (especially sheet metal, machining, and injection molding), explaining technical subjects to non-technical people, human factors design, writing/editing, public speaking (Dale Carnegie trained)
