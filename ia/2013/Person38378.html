Tameka Bryant's Summary

Experienced business professional that specializes in building wealth through real estate and entrepreneurship.

As a current business owner, educator, coach and author, I have dedicated my life's work to assisting Real people build wealth.

Growing up in the South Bronx, I came from a rough area and learned how to be saavy with resources early on. I coupled my street smarts and advanced degrees to gain the knowledge I needed to assist others in being successful and now conduct workshops for businesses, organizations and work one on one with individuals.

My strategy to work alongside investors and businessowners in finding the best returns on their money and investments has paid off for everyone. I get the gratification of helping them build wealth while they increase their portfolios. I do this by evaluating where they are and assisting them to create a plan that is specifically designed to get them where they need to be. Then we work the plan together.

My most sought after workshops are:

Doubling Your Business in 12 months for Real Estate Agents
NAR's Employer Assisted Housing Class & Expanding Housing Opportunities
RPR - Realtors Property Resource
Diversity Training for Organization
Instructor Development - Package Your Knowledge

Specialties: Instructor Development, Employee Benefit Programs, Acquisition & Business Development
