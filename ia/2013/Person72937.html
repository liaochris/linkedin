KATHY BROWN's Summary

KATHY BROWN, founder and president of Kathy Brown TLC LLC, is a charismatic national keynote and motivational speaker committed to awakening the DYNAMIC ENERGY in each individual as well as each organization.

WHAT MAKES KATHY UNIQUE:
There is a continual thread through all of her presentations; transformation takes place through
--1. Recognizing one’s unique personal energy
--2. How that energy relates to the outside world
--3. Learning how to tap into and focus that energy toward achieving your desired goals.
Using a holistic approach, Kathy inspires individuals to play a proactive role in their personal and professional growth.
