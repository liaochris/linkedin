Marcel Bensch's Summary

I'm a passionate Webdeveloper, Marketing Freak and Gadget Guy. I love to explore new things and try out creative and unusual ways to market products, brands and projects in the digital space.

I wrote my first line of code when I was 8 years old, not knowing what I was getting myself into. At the age of 14 I found my love for Webdesign, Development and Photoshop which soon led me to sell my first website.
