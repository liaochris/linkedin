Ilyas Iyoob, Ph.D.'s Patents

  • Accurately Predicting Capacity Requirements for IT Resources in Physical, Virtual and Hybrid Cloud Environments

    • United States Patent Application 13/042,138
    • Filed March 7, 2011
    Inventors: Ilyas Iyoob, Ph.D., Zehra Bilginturk Yalcin, Manish Modh, Mohammed Farooq

    The invention allows users to accurately plan IT capacity at different levels of the physical/cloud/hybrid architecture to support business demand requirements.

    Business demand is first translated to IT application demand using the demand translation module. Then IT application demand is further separated by architecture layer and compared with the capacity of each layer in the capacity estimation module to estimate utilization of each (physical/virtual) resource within the layers. Finally, the bottleneck resource driving capacity is identified and resolved sequentially in the bottleneck identification and resolution module through simulation and optimization.

  • Optimally Sourcing Services in Hybrid Cloud Environments

    • United States Patent Application 13/373,162
    • Filed November 9, 2011
    Inventors: Ilyas Iyoob, Ph.D., Emrah Zarifoglu, Ph.D., Manish Modh, Mohammed Farooq

    The invention allows users to translate physical capacity to cloud capacity and ultimately compare and select the best fit cloud service provider.

    Physical capacity is first translated into cloud capacity using the capacity translation engine. Cloud capacity is priced based on the provider catalog to come up with a provider listing by price that is standardized as per the GCU. The provider listing is then fed into the optimization engine to find the best suitable provider.
