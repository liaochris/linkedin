Jim Sniechowski, PhD's Summary

Jim & Judith (husband and wife Jim Sniechowski, PhD and Judith Sherven, PhD) are psychologists with over 60 years combined experience as psychotherapists, coaches, and business consultants. Now on a 3/4 time retainer to LinkedIn, they provide executive coaching, leadership training, team building, and customized offsite programs among other things, and most recently wrapped up the initial roll-out of their 6-part training: “Squishy Conversations: The Emotional Side of Outstanding Management” for the Engineering organization.

Both Judith & Jim are Influencers, publishing weekly posts that cover wide-ranging topics from self-branding and professional authority to anxiety and depression as well as developing your personal pitch. They invite you to “Follow” them.

They are Los Angeles Times bestselling authors of six books, including The Heart of Marketing and What Really Killed Whitney Houston, both of which address their core area of expertise, what they call—The Fear of Being Fabuloustm.

As guest experts they’ve been on 3000 radio and television shows including Oprah, The View, CNN, MSNBC, and Canada AM as well as being interviewed by hundreds of magazines and newspapers around the world.

Earlier in their lives, long before Judith & Jim met in 1987, they were both professional actors, and then in 1994 they began what became a 12-year journey of creating and selling their own programs through internet marketing. So they bring a wide range of experience to their current work at LinkedIn.

For more information, visit their website at http://JudithandJim.com
