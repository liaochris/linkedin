Gary Green's Summary

Gary Green is a second-generation marketing innovator, a recognized television personality throughout the country, a visionary promoter, and a fearless marketer. He continues to make his presence known as a multiple-discipline, cross-industry senior executive with a sophisticated marketing, financial, operational, and technology background. He is known for over-achiever accomplishments in multiple industries including: resort/casino/hotel; technology; new-media; entertainment; real estate; and publishing.

His byline has appeared more than 1,000 times in daily newspapers and national magazines and his face and voice have been heard by more than 10-million people on national and satellite-feed radio and television. Winner of more than a dozen “Freedom Newspaper” writing awards, he has twice been nominated for the Pulitzer Prize in Journalism

A former Donald Trump Vice President of Marketing, and author of the book “Marketing Donald Trump” (recently published by Amazon Kindle), he is also author of the longer book “Gambling Man” (ISBN 978-0-615-26697-8).

His three legendary record albums are now part of the historical Smithsonian Collection in Washington DC and his songs have been covered by some leading artists (including “The Pixies”’ Frank Black).

Once he was known as one of America's most intense folk singers and rock poets. He was identified as a civil rights and union organizer and advocate of Native American rights. But first, he was an award-winning journalist; then later a pioneer of the dot-com era (where he was a finalist for “BEST OF COMDEX”). Then some identified Gary Green as one of the country's leading casino executives (or as the ultimate gambler to some — especially since his colorful appearance in the World Series of Poker a few years ago and his book giving a casino boss's inside secrets to players). No matter where you come from, entering Gary Green's world is an entire adventure to most. Visit www.GaryGreen.com
