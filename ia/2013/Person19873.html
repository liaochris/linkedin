Carla Marques' Summary

I started teaching English, like many English teachers do, at languages schools. While I was teaching at the British Council, at the Military Academy and pharmaceutical companies I had various invitations to work directly with some of these companies as opposed to through another language school and was recommended by some clients to other companies. This gave me the courage to leave the language schools and go solo. As soon as I started working as a free lancer I quickly built up my clientele and found the work far more rewarding being able to meet each individual client's needs.
Since then I have had the pleasure of working with professionals from different sectors including doctor's, lawyers, CEO's and other executives, helping them develop their English communication skills in both professional and social environments.
