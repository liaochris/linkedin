Matthew Dent's Summary

Hello, I am Matthew Dent!

Developing relationships is my greatest asset. My friends and family describe me with words such as: Intelligent, Loyal, Confident, Motivated, Trustworthy, Focused, Inspirational & Committed. I have been blessed to have been surrounded by the most encouraging and loyal friends and family.

So who am I? I say what I think and I stand up for what I believe. I do what can to help others see the glass as half full and try to laugh as much as possible. I work hard, put a lot on my plate so I can stay focused on where I am going. I like to control what I can and try to let go of everything else. I think the hardest part is accepting the fact that we don't have control. I am a firm believer in working hard and playing harder. You must take the time to do what it is that helps you relax and recharge so you can operate at peak performance. I recharge by spending time in San Jose del Cabo, life slows down, I can let go, and get ready for the next round.

I enjoy my friends and I especially like to surround myself with optimistic realists. From a young age I have been influenced by some successful individuals who taught me to think big and to never stopping growing. I believe success means having the courage, the determination, and the will to become the person you believe you were meant to be.
