Joanna Halton's Summary

I'm a digital marketing and online communication strategist, specialising in social media. Currently employed as a Social Media Strategist by McCann Manchester, which was recently voted the UK’s Best Large Social Media Agency.

Four years in the digital industry and a Digital Marketing Communications Msc from MMU partnered with eConsultancy have given me a holistic view of Internet marketing tools, allowing me to spot opportunities and weaknesses when creating and implementing an integrated digital strategy.

During my time I’ve worked on local and Pan-European campaigns for big brands such as Holiday Inn, Kitbag.com, Regis Salons, Tsingtao, Dyson and Hotel Indigo. In addition to this, I’ve worked in the public sector with Greater Manchester and Merseyside Police.

My experience in social media includes monitoring, management and analysis, as well as strategy development, policy production, training, content creation, campaign management, customer service and also implementation across numerous countries, audiences and languages.

As a creative mind with an active interest in emerging technologies and a strong focus on the end goal, I enjoy thinking of ways which brands can engage their audience, build relationships and get noticed, whatever the medium.
