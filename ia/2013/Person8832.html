Anthony Shapiro's Summary

Anthony’s practice is primarily focused on securities and corporate law, with an emphasis on corporate finance transactions and mergers and acquisitions. He has particular experience acting for international resource companies which have, or seek to have, their shares listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange or TSX Venture Exchange. Anthony has regularly advised public and private corporations and investment dealers in connection with mergers, acquisitions and divestitures, reverse take-overs, plans of arrangement, friendly and hostile take-over bids, prospectus offerings, private placements, flow-through financings, rights offerings and stock exchange listings. Anthony has also advised corporations with respect to securities regulatory compliance, compliance with stock exchange policies, corporate governance, continuous disclosure obligations, general corporate matters and shareholders’ meetings.
