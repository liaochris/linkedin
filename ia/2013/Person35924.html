Danielle Jacques' Summary

Danielle Jacques draws upon many disciplines to enhance the function, safety, and aesthetics of interior spaces. Her main concerns are with how different colors, textures, furniture, lighting, and space work together to meet the needs of a home's occupants.

Traditionally, most interior designers focused on decorating—choosing a style and color palette and then selecting appropriate furniture, floor and window coverings, artwork, and lighting. However, Danielle raises the bar in her design work and is involved in architectural detailing, such as crown molding, tile layout, and built-in bookshelves, and in planning layouts of homes undergoing renovation, including helping to determine the location of windows, stairways, and material specifications.

Danielle is able to read blueprints, understands building and fire codes, and knows how to make space accessible to people who are disabled. Danielle frequently collaborates with architects, electricians, and building contractors to ensure that designs are safe and meet construction requirements.

Danielle usually meets face-to-face with the client to find out how the space will be used and to get an idea of the client's preferences and budget. For example, Danielle might inquire about a family's cooking habits if the family is remodeling a kitchen or ask about a clients favorite color or style to pick an appropriate motif.

After collecting information, Danielle formulates a design plan and estimates costs. Today, designs often are created with the use of computer-aided design (CAD) software, which provides more detail and easier corrections than sketches made by hand. Upon completing the design plan, Danielle will present it to the client and make revisions based on the client's input.

When the design concept is finalized, Danielle will begin specifying the materials, finishes, and furnishings required, such as furniture, lighting, flooring, wall covering, and artwork.

Specialties

Danielle specializes in one area of interior design- specifications for new home construction and remodeling. She then completes the project by furnishing a turn-key home, through her company Danielle Jacques Designs LLC , creating a full service and beautiful home for her clients.

Danielle supervises assistants who carry out her plans and perform administrative tasks, such as reviewing catalogues and ordering samples.
