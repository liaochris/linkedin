Carlton Nettleton's Summary

I am a business and technical consultant combining the best of Lean and Agile to help you make better products, increase quality and add predictability to your software projects. I will focus on improving productivity, communication and quality by empowering individuals to engage with their work and accept greater ownership of the outcome of their teams. I want to help your business succeed by meeting your customers's needs and improving your technical execution.
