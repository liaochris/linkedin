Annie Hart's Summary

Cultural re-evolutionary, consultant, author, speaker and real human being, Annie Hart has been on the leading edge of change for over twenty five years. Her pioneering work in the field of human transformation has helped individuals and organizations to achieve exceptional change. She has brought her work to the fields of business, education, nonprofit, community development & youth at risk.

Annie develops skills of resiliency, innovation, creative thinking & risk taking that help people stay motivated, aligned, inspired and on the leading edge. Annie’s personal ethic is to embody principles of human kindness, generosity and collaboration as a basic business model, believing that relationships are the most important factor of all.

Her work extends beyond the usual measures of success into true human fulfillment. She has many happy and satisfied clients from around the world.
