Criss Ittermann's Summary

Do you know any "SuperWomen"? These are women who often work at non-profits, sit on a board, or volunteer, own their own business, work one or more jobs, juggle children while also taking care of their parents or siblings, and seem to be everywhere at the same time.

I help these women who "do too much" and are experiencing overwhelm use an innovative and unique set of techniques to eliminate overwhelm from their busy lives.

My technique takes the information that overlaps between Daoism (Traditional Chinese Medicine), modern human biology, shamanic techniques, and life coaching techniques and blends them into an innovative system that explains why we feel overwhelmed, what the dangers are, and how to put the brakes on and do things differently while making sure we're following our dreams and creating burning desires.
