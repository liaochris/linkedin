    I organized and produced the entire project with the help of Aaron Bourque as secondary director during filming. It was completely funded by donation or personal income.

    There are definitely many mixed opinions on how, why, and what should be done to make a difference in this world.
    Our ground breaking first episode is a head first dive into the homeless community of Madison Wisconsin. Getting up close and personal with some of the homeless people and seeing where they come from, how they got to where they are now, and what is in store for them in the future.
    We filmed it in one weekend with low budget DSLR cameras and one microphone.
    This is the first documentary we have ever done and learned so much from it.
    We hope we can make a difference and shed a different light on the homeless community.
    We are just a group of young people that love to make videos and inspire others to make a difference.
