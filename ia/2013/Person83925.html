Mace Horoff's Summary

Author of "Mastering Medical Sales: The Essential Attitudes, Habits & Skills of High-earning Medical Sales Professionals."

Creator of "Mastering Medical Sales Online eTraining." This is real-world training that transfers specialized sales techniques and sales methodologies that work.

Selling in the world of healthcare is changing dramatically. The decision-makers are changing and so are their buying motives. Your biggest challenge used to be positioning your product in a sea of competition. Today, your biggest challenge might be access to a decision-maker or demonstrating how your product or service helps hospitals and clinicians to compete and survive while dealing with accountable care and other drivers that threaten their economic survival.

Mace challenges the thinking of medical sales professionals and others who sell to those who make critical buying decisions. He guides them to be “relevant” to their customers, which is something beyond products and services. It used to be that product-pushers were merely ineffective. In the new healthcare economy, there is no time for them and they are unwelcome.

If you manufacture or represent great products or services that are sold to healthcare or other high-stakes industries and you’re looking to grow sales, talk to Mace. He’s friendly, funny, and generous with his expertise. Be prepared to hear ideas to improve sales force effectiveness that you haven’t thought of. What are you waiting for?

Specialties: Medical-specific selling skills and sales process, eLearning and ePractice for medical sales, medical sales time management workshop, sales training for medical device product and marketing managers and associated personnel to help make the marketing message congruent with the sales message.

Mace Horoff's Publications

  • Mastering Medical Sales: The Essential Attitudes, Habits, & Skills of High-earning Medical Sales Professionals

    • Healthcare Business Books
    • May 1, 2010
    Authors: Mace Horoff

    Mastering Medical Sales: The Essential Attitudes, Habits and Skills of High-earning Medical Sales Professionals is the no-holds-barred, real-world guide to succeeding in the competitive and lucrative world of professional medical sales.

    Selling in today’s medical environment presents many challenges—a complex sales atmosphere, customers with little time or desire to meet with sales people, constant cost issues, and intense competition. How can the medical sales professional capture new business, while growing and maintaining the existing business?

    Mastering Medical Sales is about what the medical sales professional must know and do in order to succeed in this rewarding career. The information presented is not a rehash of sales techniques from other books, but a comprehensive approach on selling to those who make product decisions in healthcare. Every sales call must consider the patient, the customer’s ego, institutional politics, and other factors that influence the buying decision. The secret to success in medical sales is not just one behavior or idea, but a collection of attitudes, habits, and skills that have been internalized by almost every high-earning medical sales professional.

    If you’re a seasoned medical sales representative, you’ll find fresh ideas and tactics that you’ll need to keep new business coming year after year. You will also learn ways to refine what you are already doing to achieve more predictable results.

    If you’re just starting your career in medical sales, you will have the keys to developing a secure income for yourself and your family. This information will save you from years of frustration that drive many medical sales representatives out of the business.

    Mastering Medical Sales is a must-have guide for your medical sales career. These proven approaches are the same ones that thousands of medical sales professionals have used to build lucrative, long-lasting careers, and they will do the same for you.
