Mark J. Rose's Summary

Mr. Rose obtained his Bachelor of Science in Engineering, cum laude, from the University of Pittsburgh and obtained his Master of Business Administration and Juris Doctor from Stetson University.

Prior to joining Roig, Tutan, Rosenberg, Martin & Stoller, P.A., Mr. Rose practiced civil litigation and personal injury law at a boutique law firm in Boca Raton, Florida. Mr. Rose is admitted to the United States District Court for the Southern District of Florida and is a Florida Supreme Court Certified County Mediator.

Mr. Rose is an adjunct professor of business law at Palm Beach State College where he teaches "The Legal and Ethical Environment of Business."

While in law school Mr. Rose served as a federal litigation intern at the United States Department of Justice, earned a certificate in Leadership Development, and served on the American Bar Association award winning Student Leadership Development Committee. Mr. Rose served as a member of the Barney Masterson American Inn of Court and worked as a law clerk in both plaintiffs’ and defense civil litigation firms specializing in the defense of personal injury, auto negligence, and premises liability claims.
