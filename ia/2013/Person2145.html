Adrian Salisbury's Experience

Senior Partner

Smart Virtual Tours

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

February 2013 – Present (7 months) Peterborough, United Kingdom

Ok, so it's not a new company but we've re-branded OpticalFX as Smart Virtual Tours.

We are setting ourselves out as the only smart option for a virtual tour provider.
As we look around there are very competitors that offer the options we do and that's largely because this is all we do! We aren't a web designer, although were 8 years ago, we don't advertise photography as a core business, we just create beautiful virtual tours.

Please take a look at our new website to see some of the options we offer our clients and let me know if you want to chat about your own needs.


So why change our name?
There's a few reasons for this, firstly nobody uses the term 'fx' any more. 11 years ago when I was trying to come up with a new company name it meant something but not today. Also with the way our businesses have moved online I felt it was more beneficial to have a name that clearly said what we did and would be easier for our new customers to find us.

...Also I got fed up of having to spell it when ever I left a message for someone!
