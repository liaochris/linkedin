Jay Thaker's Summary

Jay is currently a MPH candiate in Health Management and Policy at the University of Michigan, passionate about using economic modeling to make data-driven decisions in health system operations. At the University of Michigan, he serves as a research assistant to the Robert Wood Johnson Health Policy Scholars Program and works as a research economist in the Department of Health Management and Policy. He also serves as a consultant to the advanced medical device industry specializing in health economics, warranty design, and recall/performance-related issues. This summer, he will be interning at the UCLA Health System in the Operations department.

Jay earned a Master of Health Economics from the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health in 2012. He earned a Bachelor of Science in Health Studies and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from the Michigan State University Honors College and College of Social Science in 2011.
