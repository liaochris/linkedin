Kelly Conway's Summary

As a third generation Colorado native who grew up playing in the mountains, I understand how important lifestyle is when choosing a place to call home. I moved to the Yampa Valley in 2002 after spending my childhood in resort areas such as Copper Mountain and Vail, ultimately finding that there is truly no comparison for Steamboat Springs a.k.a. “Ski Town USA”.

Following my passion for the outdoors is what led me to Steamboat but becoming part of an amazing community with fulfilling career in real estate is why I stayed here. For the past 6 years, I’ve followed our local market closely while assisting some of Steamboat’s most successful agents. Now as a full-time broker with Coldwell Banker Distinctive Properties I am ready to equip my Buyers and Sellers with the inside edge needed to achieve their real estate goals.
