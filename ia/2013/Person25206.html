Where I've been:

Extensive experience as an innovative leader in the building of the home division for Ralph Lauren.

Direction of all labels including Polo/Ralph Lauren, Lauren, Chaps and American Living, responsibilities focused on style research, design and merchandising from initial "lifestyle" concepts through storyboard presentation to Mr. Lauren.

Continuing the process with the management of all stages of product development of soft goods through to first samples of bedding, towels, rugs, fabric and wallpaper, table linens, and paint. With some input on furniture and tabletop.

Leader in the launches of many new businesses and initiatives for labels under Ralph Lauren Brand including Lauren, American Living and Chaps, Paint, White Label, Suite, Children’s Home, University, Spa, Core Basics, Rugs.

Specialties: Collaboration with many divisions and partners with the common goal of delivering and extensive, intelligent and strategic product offering.
