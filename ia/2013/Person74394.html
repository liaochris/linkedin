Kenneth Wilkinson's Summary

Passionate, creative, sound editor and audio professional. Talented Pro Tools engineer proficient in dialogue, sound effects, and music editing; automated dialogue replacement (ADR), and ISDN voiceover recording, and re-recording mixing of film and television programming.

Well versed in implementation of new CALM and ATSC television program loudness standards and network delivery requirements. Musically literate with strong critical listening skills. Flexible and versatile, thrive in deadline-driven environments. Diplomatic and tactful with clients and colleagues; enthusiastic and conscientious team player.
