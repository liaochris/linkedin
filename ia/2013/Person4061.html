Alexis Antonelli Morris' Summary

Offering Axure software training classes including full-day in-person training and remote training by the hour.
_________________________________________

With 14 years of experience in user interface design, I've created compelling user experience design solutions for Lexus, Acura, ShopNBC, Activision, and many other companies large and small. Passionate about user-centered design, I've conducted usability testing and other research studies to guide conceptual modeling and inform critical design decisions for Toyota, Verizon, Disney, Samsung, Kelley Blue Book, and Edmunds. I've lead the discovery and strategy of large initiatives for Nuance Communications, CreditCards.com, and McKesson. As an Axure specialist, I've created prototypes, widget libraries, and specification documents for clients including Cisco/WebEx and JP Morgan Chase.

My academic and independent training in both business and human factors has helped me develop the right balance of advocating for user needs while staying focused on business goals. My empathy for everyone from users to my team members to my clients is what makes me successful and love what I do.

I served as Vice President and then President of the Los Angeles Chapter of the Usability Professionals Association (UPA) from 2010 to 2012.
