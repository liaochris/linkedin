Matt Bovell's Summary

Matthew Bovell has more than thirty years experience in the information technology industry. He currently runs a consulting practice specializing in webinar production. His company, Vell Group handles all the minutiae of webinars so that online presenters can focus on their presentation and not worry about the technology.

He previously worked for IBM in the areas of contract benchmarking and cost analysis with a focus on competitive costing. Matthew’s breadth of experience includes application programming and testing, offering development and technical writing. He has a particular interest in internet applications and environments and has several years of project leadership and management experience in that area. Matthew is a strong communicator and is fascinated by the role that communications plays in team and organizational dynamics.

Specialties: Web conference technologies, managing in a matrixed environment, cost analysis, translation of technical information to business or laymen's language
