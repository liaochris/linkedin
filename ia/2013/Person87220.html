Mark Walztoni's Summary

I’ve built my human capital credentials within leading technology and professional services firms and have served private equity investors and their portfolio companies in consulting roles.

As a co-founder of Lotus Leaf Coatings Inc., a company commercializing coatings from Sandia National Lab, I have served as Chief Business Development Officer since July 2010.

My human capital expertise includes 25+ years leading global recruiting, retention, and development initiatives at American Express, Ernst & Young LLP, and Thomson, supplemented by a M.A. in Organizational Psychology from Columbia University and related certifications.

My significant corporate contributions were in start-up, rapid growth, turnaround, and merger and acquisition due diligence and integration environments. I entered the entrepreneurial world as the Global VP of Human Resources for the founders of IGATE Capital Management and Computer Horizons Corporation.

I have provided human capital advisory services, (including executive recruitment, executive coaching, and team development) to twenty+ early-stage companies.

I am also a speaker on recruitment, retention, executive coaching, onboarding, merger and acquisition due diligence and integration and individual and organizational transition topics for the American Society of Training and Development, Society of Human Resource Management, Organizational Development Network, Fortune Magazine’s CIO Forum, and was an Expert Advisor in Onboarding, Mergers and Acquisition, and Leadership Development for the Human Capital Institute.

My recent clients include Flywheel Ventures, HCP (Hispania Capital Partners) & Company, Lockheed Martin, Adidas, Homewise, Business Leadership Network, TruTouch Technologies, Patni Computer Systems, Pajarito Scientific Corp, Santa Fe Business Incubator, and the New Mexico Angels. I am a board member of the New Mexico Angels and former board member of New Mexico Biotech and state International Coach Federation chapter.

Specialties

Technology licensing and commercialization, developing entrepreneurial leadership teams, mergers and acquisitions, turnarounds, and rapid growth organizations, human capital services, due diligence, onboarding, executive search, venture capital, executive coaching, human capital advisor, staffing strategy, professional speaker
