Linda Ireland's Summary

I love being owner and partner at Aveus. We are a global strategy and operational change firm that helps leaders create change that pays through customer experience driven performance.

I wrote Domino: How to Use Customer Experience to Tip Everything in Your Business toward Better Financial Performance, building on work done in past lives as a line executive and with clients at Aveus. So many leaders I meet feel trapped, thinking that investments in strengthening a customer experience are a trade-off to profits. I know the opposite is true - that organizations who use a clearly defined target experience to guide all operating decisions have better financial performance.

My passion for emerging growth and turnaround situations shows up in my experience in a wide variety of industries – automotive, multi-channel retail, technology and health services. I've led from many chairs: general management, operations, marketing, corporate development, manufacturing and governance. With some fabulous teams, I've led strategic change working through an IPO, new economy shifts and down economy shifts. All rewarding; all different experiences.

I have served on the boards of organizations in health services, the arts and leadership development. I am an active angel investor in woman-owned businesses. I've taught in the MBA and Executive MBA programs at the University of St. Thomas for over 10 years (and learned from every student, too).

I am a global wanderer. 31 countries, six continents and countless cultures - I am energized by the deeper perspective that comes with a change of people and place.

Specialties

Business, brand and product strategy. Operationalizing change. Change readiness. Business evaluation. Public speaking. Customer experience: expert at using customer experience as an operating strategy to get tangible performance payoff.
