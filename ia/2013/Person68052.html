Jon Pond's Projects

  • Mobile Device Preview

    • June 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jon Pond, Keith Pepin, Joseph Theriault, Corey Hamilton, Andrew Eddy, Cecilia Lam, Mark Metcalfe

    A conscientiously UX-designed in-browser simulator of device resolution and OS/model, this project enables marketers to validate the mobile experience for their site visitors through an intuitive, easy to use interface.

    This project involved providing for the configuration and serving of images adapted to specific device resolutions, presenting the simulation UI, and communicating the available devices and device-specific requests with the server.

    The Mobile Device Preview app established many practices for Ektron's new UX initiative and relied heavily on skills in TDD, REST, MVVM, AMD and AJAX; it leveraged technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, KnockoutJS, RequireJS, and QUnit.
