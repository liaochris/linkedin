Mark Fox's Summary

For the past five years I've had the pleasure of freelancing, lending my expertise to organizations delivering great web and interactive experiences to their audience.

As a developer I leverage contemporary tools and techniques to create responsive, maintainable, and scalable web applications and interactive experiences. I specialize in beautiful frontend and mid-level backend. What sets me apart from my developer peers is that I speak design and love collaborating with designers.

As a designer I'm interested in making the complex intuitive. I focus on information visualization, and the refinement of current UX approaches. My design practice is leveraged by my programming skills, so I focus on rapid prototyping, iteration and excel at solving design problems that are enabled by algorithmic solutions.

I happily work as a developer, a designer, or both.
Contact me for solo or team gigs.
