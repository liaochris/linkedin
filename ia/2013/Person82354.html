Gilbert Lordong's Summary

Expert analyst and designer of user-friendly GUIs in World Wide Web and other digital media.
Graduated as Master of Computer Science (focus on software ergonomics) at the Federal Institute of Technology (ETH) in Zurich and as Master of Advanced Studies (MAS) in Human Computer Interaction Design (HCID) at the University of Basel / HSR Rapperswil.
Broad experience as team leader, project leader, consultant, author and designer of web sites, web portals, intranets, CD-ROMs and touchscreen applications.
