Christine Rainero Catoggio's Summary

Christine Catoggio is the Owner of Successful Life Transitions, a Senior Care Management Company, offering G.uidance, P.lanning and S.upport for Seniors and their families and is passionate about helping families share long healthy lives and strong relationships with peace of mind and quality of life.
Known as the Life Navigator and Guide Through Unchartered Life Change, Christine helps her clients transition into new phases of life with compassion, knowledge and expertise. Specializing in working primarily with adult children of aging parents, she coaches them through the entire process, educating the family on what to expect and how to best manage the variety of emotions that will appear throughout this part of life’s journey.
Christine and her staff primarily work with long-distance caregivers, families who (do not live in Florida, while their aging parents do,) need to make plans for elder care from a distance, as well as, busy families who are overwhelmed with their own family and career responsibilities. She guides her clients from the very beginning stages of planning on the next level of care and helps them prepare as things happen, so families have a clear direction and aren’t thrown into a crisis mode, which comes about when having to make decisions in the moment. Christine is dedicated to ensuring that her client families maximize the time they have left together so they can continue to create memories without having to worry about the details.

A sought after speaker and Senior Issues Educator, she has been a keynote presenter for both live events and radio platforms. She is a contributing author in the book, “OMG, My Parents Are Getting Old” and leads a teleseminar series entitled, “Dealing with the Unexpected”.

Age in Place is a Senior Care management company, providing support and services that allow Seniors to live safely and independently in their own homes. Established in July, 2009, Age in Place is experiencing continued growth as part of an emerging industry as the elder population continues to grow. Age in Place Management was created in response to the growing demand for assistance navigating the number of resources and services available in the Senior market. Age in Place Management specializes in life transition coaching for Baby Boomers, Seniors, and their adult children as they navigate the journey of aging. Our services are designed for:
•Seniors who are living independently who need assistance managing and coordinating the services to help them remain at home;
•Families of seniors, who want to help their aging parents maintain quality of life, but do not have the time or resources;
•Long distance caregivers who need the peace of mind and reassurance that there is a local contact overseeing their parents needs.
Age in Place has developed a niche market in non-medical Care Management, offering Seniors and their Caregivers our three-pronged approach of Education, Preparation and Communication, empowering them to make informed decisions about their own life, covering their medical, financial, and residential concerns.
