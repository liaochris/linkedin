Cory Van Horn's Summary

As a detail-oriented professional, I offer extensive experiences in community tourism and development, local food systems, sustainability and marketing. Consistently providing focused leadership, I possess an effective and eloquent ability to navigate through all levels of the organization to "make things happen."

I explore the untold stories of food and communities; then narrate those stories in well-articulated ways by leveraging my skills in writing, teaching and social media.
