John Mayer's Summary

The Space Coast in Brevard County, Florida has been my home for over 20 years now and I love this area. It has so much to offer! As a dedicated professional, I am committed to giving my customers the full attention they deserve. I believe in treating people fairly and will never compromise my integrity under any circumstances.

My goal is to make every sale the start of a relationship, not the end of one. I want to be your trusted Real Estate resource for life as well as the Realtor all of your friends and family call when they need knowledgeable, trustworthy services and advice. Just like your local favorite grocer or repair shop, I'll be there when you need me and will be accessible any time to answer your questions.

Specialties

I am a buyer's and seller's agent for residential, investment / income and commercial property, specializing in Cocoa Beach, Cape Canaveral and Merritt Island, Florida.
