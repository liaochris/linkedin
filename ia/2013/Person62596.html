Jeroen Jonker's Summary

Founder and owner of De Winstfabriek, a powerhouse for improving business results for our clients. We pack 13 years of experience in strategic and operations management together with state of the art visual group facilitating skills, to work on solutions for better results in your company.

Previously; founder and partner in Nobleman, a consultancy firm focusing on improving business results. Experienced business leader, advisor and interim manager in the field of strategy & operations management. Specialism: result driven improvement of organizations, with a getting-things-done mentality. Roles: interim manager or advisor, supporting management in continues improvement.

Experiences in strategic and operations management, logistics, manufacturing, ICT-implementation.

Specialties
