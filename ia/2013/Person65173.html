Jody Wilson's Publications

  • The first cosmic ray albedo proton map of the Moon

    • Journal of Geophysical Research - Planets
    • June 5, 2012
    Authors: Jody Wilson, Harlan Spence, NATHAN SCHWADRON, Justin Kasper, Tony Case, Cary Zeitlin

    We construct the first map of the normalized albedo proton emission rate from the lunar surface to look for any albedo variation that correlates with surface features. The map is consistent with a spatially uniform albedo proton yield to within statistical uncertainties.
