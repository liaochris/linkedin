Lisa Renery Handalian's Summary

My passion for research is fueled by boundless curiosity and the burning impulse to draw connections across contexts. The storyteller in me is energized by seeking out end user experiences that make it possible to design what matters. I sparkle in cross-disciplinary environments that tap in to my life’s work as an ethnographer, teacher, writer, and citizen of the world.
