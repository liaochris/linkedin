David Covington's Summary

As Vice-President, Adult & Youth Services for Magellan of Arizona's Regional Behavioral Health Authority (RBHA), Mr. Covington is responsible for providing administrative, financial, programmatic and clinical oversight and support to a specialty network of providers that provides services for more than 80,000 adults, children and adolescents with mental health and substance abuse issues.

In addition, David leads the Magellan of Arizona clinical initiatives (Provider Outcomes Dashboards, the Programmatic Suicide Deterrent System Program, and the Integrated Health Home) to better serve those in our care who are seeking hope and recovery and to drive our integrated approach for addressing the entire life span.

Mr. Covington works closely with key partners across the state and served as President of the NAMI Arizona Board of Directors in 2011. He also serves as the Chair of the SAMHSA National Suicide Prevention Lifeline Steering Committee, co-leads the Clinical Care & Intervention Task Force of the National Action Alliance for Suicide Prevention and is a member of the Board of Directors for the National Council of Community Behavioral Healthcare. He is a nationally-certified and licensed professional counselor and has an EMBA from Kennesaw State University and a master of science from the University of Memphis.

Specialties: Previously, Mr. Covington was CEO of Behavioral Health Link and managed one of the nation's leading integrated crisis programs with a statewide crisis and access hotline and mobile clinical response. Prior to 2002, he served as Director of Public Sector Quality Management at APS Healthcare.

Mr. Covington's work in integrated software solutions, performance management dashboards, balanced scorecard approaches and sophisticated analytics have been recognized nationally as industry leading.
