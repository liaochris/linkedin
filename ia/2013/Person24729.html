Chris Zaharia's Summary

I am a co-founder and CTO of Zookal, an Australian education company primarily involved in renting textbooks to University students. My qualifications include a Bachelor of Business (Major in Accounting) and a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (Major in Software Engineering) at the University of Technology Sydney.

I have a great passion for innovation, and have built applications in many technologies within the online and mobile space. I enjoy creating new products which solve other people’s problems the best way I possibly can.

My goal is to change the world and I am constantly looking out for opportunities to do so!
