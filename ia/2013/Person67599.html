Jonathan Ment's Summary

I am a formally-trained video producer, photographer and writer who has adapted these varied disciplines into a specialty of boutique media service delivery

As a one-man show, I plan, execute and deliver a wide range of projects. While in production settings, whether assembled by me or including me as a contractor, I am a versatile member of any team or crew.

MMG works principally in New York's Hudson Valley and Catskill Mountain Region; New York City and its environs; Albany and the Capitol District; and via the Internet.

Specialties: Getting things done in spite of the odds and pulling rabbits out of hats.

Crossing the Ts and dotting the Is, logistics, planning and organization float my boat.
