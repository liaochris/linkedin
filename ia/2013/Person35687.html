Dinesh Doshi's Summary

At 25, he moved to New York where he continues to live and create his Art & Architecture.

From this varied and well traveled past has emerged his unique sense of himself and the world. Dinesh’s strong use of color and movement represent his life, dreams, fears and aspirations - in stirring and impassioned works. He often looks inward to explore a subjective expression of personal truth.

“I’m like an open book, all you need to do to understand me is read my paintings.”

Much like the art created by Paul Klee and the Blue Rider Group of the early 20th century, one can experience what Dinesh feels through his works.

“I have been painting my life on canvas”

His paintings are above all else Expressionistic - charged with an emotional and spiritual vision of the world.
