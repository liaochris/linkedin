Reynolds is the CEO and Website Developer of SocialNotz. She is a featured author at SteamFeed.com and is currently working on her first book. She is also a technology enthusiast, which led her to develop the first QR code social media directory, QwikR.me

Kim started her internet journey in 1994. The next year, the Press-Enterprise wrote an article called "Savvy Woman Behind Redlands.com", recognizing her as a leader in internet marketing and content creation.

Her subsequent projects focused on internet marketing and SEO. Recognizing that internet marketing has evolved with the emergence of social media and mobile access, Kim started her own social media and mobile marketing agency, Savvy Media Marketing, which was later re-branded as SocialNotz to emphasize the social side of business.

- Founder/Owner of the Social Media Mentors Google+ Community with over 1,200 members. Community focuses on mentoring individuals and businesses on social media content creation, best practices, useful tools as well as a variety of other topics including measure ROI and strategy development.
