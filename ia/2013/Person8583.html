Ann Baker's Summary

I'm the founder and producer of Women At Woodstock (http://womenatwoodstock.com); 4-day retreats by and for Boomer women. Our life-inspiring events include iconic speakers, mind-shifting workshops, expert consultations, entertainment geared for women 50 and over, gourmet meals, and gifts. Currently two events are held each year: July in Berkeley, California, and October in Woodstock, New York.

I'm also an internet marketing specialist with over 20 years of experience. I provide services as well as do-it-yourself training in all the essential ingredients for successful online marketing: website design, mobile websites, social media marketing, article writing and placement, media relations, Facebook pages, LinkedIn, blogging, Twitter, email campaigns, search engine optimization, and press releases. Most of my clients are Boomer women, and are located throughout the country

I also teach a two-part course at UCLA titled "Internet Catch-Up for Boomers and Beyond," a comprehensive survey of basic internet usage, security and backup, online purchasing and banking, social media platforms, and internet marketing strategies and management for Boomers in Business.

Specialties: Boomer women's issues, women's retreat planning, internet marketing; social media marketing; targeted PR lead-finding and pitching; media relations training; social media marketing strategy, implementation, and training; search engine optimization analysis, services, and training.
