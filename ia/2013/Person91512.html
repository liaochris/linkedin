Meredith Oliver, MIRM, MCSP's Summary

Meredith Oliver is an Internet sales and marketing expert. She is a professional speaker, consultant and founder of Creating WOW; a full service digital marketing company specializing in website development and search engine optimization for home builders, land developers, Realtors, apartment communities, senior living communities and hotels.

Meredith holds a Masters Degree in Communication Technology from the prestigious Rollins College and a Bachelors of Arts Degree, majoring in Psychology, from the University of Central Florida.

While she specializes in speaking to audiences in the housing industry, Meredith has spoken to a number of industries including healthcare, education, association, insurance, technology, and temporary staffing. She is a proud member of the National Speakers Association.

Meredith has spoken at a number of tradeshows and conferences including the International Builders Show with numerous appearances in the Super Bowl Sales Rally and the Sales Management Summit. Her convention seminars are consistently standing room only and rated by attendees among the most popular, entertaining and educational seminars offered.

Specialties

Website development, search engine marketing, social media marketing, email marketing, new home sales/marketing, eLead management, eMystery shopping, online sales consultant training and management.
