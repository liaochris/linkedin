Gabe Mellein's Summary

Absync Realty opened its doors in 2009 to one of the shakiest markets southwest Florida has ever seen. Yet since then, it has grown into one of the area’s premiere full service realty companies with a savvy sales team, a reliable property manager, and a tireless leasing brokerage department.

The reason we’ve thrived when other have struggled is simple–experience and dedication. Our Realtors® have combined decades of experience in Naples, Bonita Springs, Estero and Fort Myers. We know the area, we know the challenges, and we know how to achieve success in this market or any other. The Naples Daily News knows that Gabe Mellein and his colleagues know their stuff, and that’s why they feature his advice each week as their regular real estate columnist.

The word Absync was created by combining abundance and synchronicity. We work side-by-side with you, our client, to meet every type of real estate goal. We strive to make your experience buying or selling a home harmonious–so much so that we put it into our name. Our customer service and negotiation skills are open and honest.

We are particularly excited about Absync Rentals, Inc., a division of Absync Realty, specializing in full service property management and rentals. We currently manage over 150 residential rentals in southwest Florida and are growing quickly. Our operation is fully modernized, providing online payments for tenants for rent and/or other bills and a private, secure website for tenants to view leases and other important information. We handle all your maintenance requests and maintain accounting records that are accessible 24/7. Both landlords and tenants enjoy the ability to not only show properties online, but deal with all the financial details there as well.



Specialties: Sales, Leasing, Property Management
