-Wrote and managed federal grants.

Manager, Distance Learning and Telemedicine

North Country HealthCare

January 2007 – January 2009 (2 years 1 month)

As distance learning and telemedicine manager for North Country HealthCare I created several unique and innovative approaches to solving disparities in health care and health professions education. Specific duties included the creation of a telemedicine network spanning across 12 clinic locations, the creation of a social networking website to reach high school and college age students interested in health careers, and a distance learning network that provides certification, college education and continued professional education to the rural areas of northern Arizona using state-of-the-art video teleconferencing equipment.

Owner

As a small business owner I designed extensive business plans, developed market research and projections to create a project timeline to open business profitably, on budget and on time. Educator

Arizona Public Schools

1993 – 2004 (11 years)

During this time I worked to develop new technology based instructional methods that would function as core curriculum in both Tempe and Flagstaff school districts. Creation of instructor materials was an integral part of this process. My focus on technology and grant writing allowed me to create the first fully funded and staffed computer lab in Flagstaff. Working with staff members I created original curriculum at each grade level which allowed for a dynamic, comprehensive technology program to be developed.
