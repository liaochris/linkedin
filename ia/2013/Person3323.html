Albert Lo's Summary

Hello, I posses the unique skill set that overlaps between Interaction and Visual design. I provide the voice, personality & visual organisation for interaction and design for users following a UCD process.

I am increasing specialising more in the mobile space across iPhone, Android devices and designing mobile web and native apps.

Over the years I've been privileged to have worked directly or under contract with clients such as BBC, Last.fm, Orange, BMW, MINI & Expedia amongst others across various industry sectors including digital agency, telecommunications, broadcasting, retail, finance, tourism & technology.

I have also been seen in print and online:

* The Web Designer's Idea Book Volume 2
* NET magazine, issue 181
* Contributed to Smashing Magazine
* Showcased on various online CSS galleries
* Interviewed on various sites & blogs

Specialties

Web Design, Digital Art Direction, Creative strategy, Mobile UX design, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Wireframing, User Experience, Understanding of visual and client requirements, UI Design, UI prototyping, Web Standards (CSS/XHTML), CMS Frameworks (Wordpress, Textpattern)
