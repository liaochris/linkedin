William E "Bill" Gschwind, MBA Attorney at Law's Summary

My clients appreciate working with an attorney who spent over two decades walking in their shoes as a business owner before practicing law. They value my advice as a business management expert helping them navigate the turbulent waters of entrepreneurship to become the successful business owners they always knew they could be.

My formal education includes law, finance, economics, politics, and human relations. I earned my law degree from William Mitchell College of Law and my MBA from the University of St. Thomas. This intensive training gives me the foundation to thoroughly evaluate the complexities of your situation and craft effective solutions for your unique business.

My business experience includes executive management, operations, sales, human resources, and customer service. Before joining C Alden Pearson PA, I owned several businesses and was self-employed since 1994.

My purpose is to enable business owners to realize the benefits they envisioned when they started their business. My greatest satisfaction comes from seeing owners shed the frustrations that get in their way and regain control.

Business owners I work with realize more profit from their existing revenue stream by developing the necessary attitudes of business ownership. Using my processes they regain control - over their business, their income, and their time! They empower their staff to make decisions and take responsibility for the outcomes in ways that make the company better than the sum of its parts.

Owners engage my services to guide them through their journey to success. Together we develop and implement strategies to achieve their dreams by managing risk, protecting personal assets, and improving productivity.

Specialties

Leadership development, legal advice, strategic planning, financial management, negotiation, organizational development, personnel management.
