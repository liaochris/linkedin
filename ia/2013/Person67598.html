Jonathan Mendez's Summary

Jonathan is one of the foremost pioneers in digital media testing, targeting & optimization having spearheaded the rise of real-time rules based/event driven targeting technology with search & display advertising. As Chief Strategy Officer of Offermatica (Adobe Test&Target) through its acquisition by Omniture Jonathan drove a 100% overall performance improvement rate with over 60 clients including six of the top eleven brands in the world. An incredible six of his case study reports appear in MarketingSherpa 2007 Landing Page Handbook. In February of 2010 Jonathan founded Yieldbot to build technology that would apply Search's realtime matching and optimization principles to disrupt Display advertising and greatly improve Publisher monetization. Jonathan’s personal blog is an Advertising Age Top150 and as a speaker Jonathan has keynoted & participated in over 50 digital marketing & advertising events since 2005. He is often quoted on digital marketing strategy and technology in publications that include New York Times, Business Week, USA Today, Advertising Age, MediaPost, DM News, B2B & Inc. Magazine. His clients have included Amazon.com, Ameriprise, AutoNation, Disney, Citibank, H&R Block, IBM, Intuit, Microsoft, Monster.com, Sears and T-Mobile.

Specialties: * Discovering user intent and affinity to create and implement segmented online marketing strategies and optimized experiences for acquisition, lead gen and engagement.

* Using analytics and metric driven strategies with advanced marketing technology for testing, targeting and optimization.

* Creating product solutions with JavaScript, APIs & Semantic technology that use the web as a platform for distributing content, messaging and offers with more relevance.
