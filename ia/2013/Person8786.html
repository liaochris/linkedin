Anthony Molina's Summary

Safety Systems Technology, headquartered in Southern California, is a privately owned corporation established in 1988 and has a worldwide distribution network.

SST designs and manufactures fire and gas detection and control instrumentation, and has the capability to provide fully integrated turnkey systems, from design to start-up, for both new and retrofit applications, and in both the private and governmental sectors.

SST has a worldwide customer base in diverse industries such as oil and gas exploration and drilling, hazardous waste, chemical storage facilities, sewage treatment plants, ships and tankers, utilities, automobile manufacturing, refrigeration, nuclear facilities, alternative energy, and industrial manufacturing facilities.

As a company, we adhere to the highest level of professionalism, integrity, and excellence. Each and everyone in our organization from our engineers who design our products, business partners who supply us and who sell our products, and to our team members who assemble and test our products ensure every single SST product that leave our factory are built with the highest quality and reliability.

Specialties

SST, is a U.S. manufacturer of fire and gas detection systems that comprises an entire product line of flame detectors, smoke detectors, heat detectors, oxygen sensors, combustible gas sensors, toxic gas sensors, manual stations, control modules, and signaling devices that can be used in cogeneration, hazardous waste storage, sewage treatment, mining, utilities, aerospace, manufacturing, marine, petrochemical, refining, exploration, drilling, producing, and/or pipeline industries.
