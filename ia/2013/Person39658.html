Edmund Chein's Summary

Dr. Edmund Chein has used hormone therapy for over a decade to treat Multiple Sclerosis, the clearing of blocked coronary arteries, and, most notably, to slow down the biological aging process. Dr. Chein’s several valuable discoveries on the capabilities of the Human Growth Hormone in adult use, and his success in the subsequent legal battles that ensued, have allowed doctors from all over the world to employ hormone treatments for the benefit of patients internationally. The founder of the Palm Springs Life Extension Institute and the author of multiple books on the subject of aging, Dr. Edmund Chein is a pioneer in the field of hormone treatments.
