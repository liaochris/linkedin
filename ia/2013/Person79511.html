Leah Larkin Dixon's Summary

One of the few attorneys in the Inland Empire certified by the State Bar of California in Estate Planning, Trust & Probate Law, Leah L. Dixon is dedicated to providing excellent representation to her clients. Leah is also licensed by the California Department of Real Estate as a Broker. While our speciality is estate planning, elder law, probate and trust administration, we offer a full-range of legal services to its clients. Located in the Inland Empire, the Offices serve local clients in areas including criminal defense, estate planning, probate, family law, small business law, and bankruptcy. Over the internet, the Offices serve clients throughout California in drafting estate plans.

We like what we do, and our results show it. Our success comes from our ability to establish and maintain trusting, lasting client-lawyer relationships. We provide practical, cost-effective service designed to satisfy the specific goals of each of our clients.
