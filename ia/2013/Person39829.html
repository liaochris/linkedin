Edward Booth's Summary

From an early age I have been learning new professions. I don't just do my job, I look for ways to make it better.

Graduated Minuteman RVTHS in 1991 and immediately to a a position as a cabinet makers apprentice. I gain considerable insight into the design, building, and remodeling industry in my 4 short years.

Worked part-time on the front line in the gasoline retail industry starting in 1993. Soon advanced to Manager and then business owner. 15 Years later, it was time to develop a new career path.

In November of 2008 sold my business and started consulting small business owners on the Internet and web design. My wealth of business operation information is of great help to those who are just starting or are looking for ways to improve their bottom line.

I have one goal and that is to be the person small business owners turn to, to help them get that competitive edge in today's market.

Specialties

I know technology! If you have even the smallest question about how to accomplish something with today's technology or just what new gadget or software to buy, I am your source for information.
