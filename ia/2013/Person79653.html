Lee Ann Dzelzkalns' Summary

The keynote of my consulting is to promote sustainable well-being, which encompasses wholistic learning, harmonious living and spiritual well-being.

I synthesized my passions for teaching, physical movement, mindbody integration, energy work, and spirituality, when I founded L A Consulting, Inc. (mindbodyspirit integration) in the early 90's. Syinthesis® Programs were later developed to assist individuals to create a mindful and harmonious life through self-empowerment, healing into wholeness and discovering the authentic self (soul). The scope of my work in the world fosters 'The Nature of Being Whole'.

The Syinthesis® programs guide individuals and groups to cultivate and realize internal adjustments through relaxation, meditation, harmonic sound healing (The Lotus Heart Sound Journey), sacred symbols, spiritual development and nature journeys. Internal shifts gently guide the 'self' back to its' individual wisdom and strength. Thus self-transformation encourages global transformation. It is through the path of purification we enter the path of self-transformation. The lotus petals of self unfold evermore as the path of self-realization exposes the jewel in the lotus.

Whether cultivating kinesthetic awareness, quieting the mind, mindfully hiking, healing the heart, or spiritually awakening, a sense of inner harmony presents in right time. We recover our original state of unity consciousness and unfold the full expression of One-Self. We become masterful in all that we are -- in the One Life.
