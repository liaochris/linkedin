Keith Baldwin's Summary

Arena Text & Graphics helps sports teams by (1) improving the fan's game day experience (2) generates multiple streams of revenue for the sports team (3) creates new inventory and sponsorship opportunities (4) helps sports coaches motivate and reward their sports team and players.

Arena Text & Graphics is similar to card stunts - but without all the labor, shipping, setup, and mess. So simple and cheap - you can do it every game.

The Basics

Each sports team has a dark color (usually red, green, blue, or purple) and a light color (usually white). Fans going to your games wonder - what colored shirt should I wear to the game?

Fans go to our website, enter their section, row, and seat and we return their Game Day Color™ (one of your sports team colors). They wear that color to the game.

Fans can wear a t-shirt, dress shirt, sweatshirt, jacket, poncho, body paint - it does not matter. The idea is to get them to buy more of your licensed clothing.

Teams or Sponsors Give Away Free Shirts

If a sports team or sponsor wants to give away free shirts – we can do that by: (1) We can tell you what colored shirt goes in each seat and you can place them there (2) We have apps that scan peoples tickets when they come into the arena and looks up their Game Day Color™. You hand them that colored shirt.

Messages

Sponsors can pay $$$ to have their name, logo, or slogan in part of the stadium. Your team determines the text and graphics in other parts of the stadium.

Coaches can send messages to the players.

Fans can send messages to the coaches, players, and opponent.

Honor former players and coaches.

Create patriotic displays.

Sell pictures of the events to fans.

Visit our website at www.ArenaText.com or email (LinkedIn at ArenaText.com) or inmail me for more ideas and samples.

Specialties

Color coordinating the shirts of 100,000 people attending sports games and events to create text and graphics viewed by all.
