Barry Hurd's Summary

Thank you for taking the time to visit my profile.

First and foremost: I am a digital accelerator. Some people refer to my expertise as a combination data scientist, market researcher, competitive intelligence professional, and business executive rolled into one. By understanding where data and online trends collide with traditional business models I am capable of redefining performance metrics for an organization.

My main role is as the managing partner for Epiphany Metrics in support of our 123 Social Media, TradeShowSocialMedia.com, SocialMediaKit.com, SocialMediaAdvertising.com, and InfluencerAnalysis.com teams. As our projects often deal with multiple partners handling specialty scenarios in industry niches, I'm always engaged in finding new solutions for emerging problems (speaking with start-ups & working with internal corporate teams.)

Past projects and clients have included Wall Street Journal, Dow Jones, Microsoft, DEMO, NIKE, REI, Onvia, TravelClick, TMP Worldwide, Monster.com, Verizon Superpages, Intuit, RISMedia, and a variety of digital "start-up" projects.

As an accomplished speaker and author I have presented to audiences around the world. You can find some of that insight at http://BarryHurd.com

What does this mean?

It means that I have an expert level understanding of online technologies, mobile systems, market research tools, business intelligence, competitive processes, visualization tools, strategic models, and industry trends. When these are combined with an understanding of corporate investment, product development, and market leadership I have the ability to create accurate plans for industry innovation and market disruption at a variety of budget scales.
