Clayton Farr's Summary

I help shepherd the creation of digital products and surrounding experiences that delight customers and employees. I am avid advocate for users - both external and internal - a team mentor and make communication and facilitation central to my work. I provide strategic insight, fresh perspective and the design expertise necessary to help successfully marry a product's business goals and its customers' needs.

User Experience is an increasingly broad domain. Over my 20+ years working in design I've gained experience across many of its disciplines and also those of front-end development, however the areas where my skills excel are product strategy, team leadership, business and user research and crafting effective interaction and interface design through iterative prototyping.

I'm always eager to work with individuals and companies that have ambitious vision and respect for the people that they sell their products and services.
