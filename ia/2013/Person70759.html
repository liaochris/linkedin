Graphic Designer

Conrad Jupiters Casino

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Hospitality industry

1990 – 1994 (4 years) Gold Coast, Australia

Having commenced studies in Accountancy in the UK, I was initially employed in the accounts department (General Ledgers, Receivables, Payables, F&B Inventory Control), before transferring into Human Resources as Training Coordinator to help establish Conrad College. In 1989 the role of Junior Graphic Designer was created especially for me within the Marketing Services Department and I was set the task of overseeing implementation of the Apple Mac operating system. It was an incredible opportunity that I met with enthusiasm, passion and determination, and was quickly rewarded with a promotion. During what was an affluent period for Conrad Jupiters, the work was diverse and constant, VIP special events were lavish, offering amazing creative freedom. Career advancement, by means of external training, was encouraged and I trained with the likes of Russell Brown, Adobe Photoshop Developer. I also worked alongside an expert management team brought in from around the world, and absorbed myself in learning about each department and the business as a whole. 6 years later, I was selected to set up the Marketing Services department for Conrad Treasury Brisbane.
