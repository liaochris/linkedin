Ashley Johnson's Summary

I received my BS degree in Computer Science from Spelman College in 2009. Though I was a computer science major, my passion was still in the arts. During undergrad, I took art classes to satisfy my desire to be involved in the arts. However, I do love and enjoy technology and wanted to make significant use of computer science.

My junior year, I took a game design course that allowed me to take part in all aspects of creating games. This is when I discovered that I could combine everything I learned into the makings for a fun and exciting career in graphics. My interests are in the fields of user interface design, web development and technical art.

I earned my Master's degree in Entertainment Technology at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA in May of 2009. While attending Carnegie Mellon, my concentrations were in user interface design, 2D art, and web design.

I am currently looking for opportunities in user interface design or web design to obtain a creative and challenging career utilizing interactive technologies in the entertainment industry.

Specialties

2D art (digital and traditional), CSS, HTML, SQL, PHP, Photoshop, Maya, Javascript
