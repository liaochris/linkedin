  • weaving words into light

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Sarah Mercer, Bronwen Hyde

    the weavers:

    bronwen hyde is an australian fine art photographer currently based in london with a penchant for self-portraiture, portraiture, landscapes, the minutiae of every day life, graveyards and death.

    sarah mercer is a canadian photographer with a similar love of self-portraiture, as well as macro still life.

    the two met virtually around 2008 via the female self-portrait artists’ support group on flickr, going on to be part of the divine diptych project.

    in 2013 they decided they wanted to continue to collaborate regularly, both in diptych and non-diptych format, weaving words into light.
