Dr. Kyra Gerber's Experience

Owner & Clinical Director

Courtyard Chiropractic Health Centre

October 1997 – Present (15 years 11 months)

I founded this Family Chiropractic Practice in the Court at the Centenary site of the Rouge Valley Health System in October of 1997. Our team offers holistic Chiropractic care, Active Release Techniques, low intensity light therapy (laser therapy), custom made orthotics, excellent health, wellness and fitness products and Registered Massage Therapy. I work closely with Marla Weinrib, RMT who offers excellence in Massage Therapy and has been with me since 2000.

The Courtyard Chiropractic Health Centre (CCHC) has been serving the Scarborough and Greater Toronto Area since October 1997. The moment you become a patient, is when your healing may begin. Our holistic chiropractic and massage therapy team will help you with pain reduction, functional enhancement and will inspire you to reach your health goals.

We are honoured when our patients say… “Thank goodness I found you; I didn’t know that this type of care existed!” Whether it’s back pain, headaches, sports injury, repetitive strain or wellness care that has brought you to us – you will soon know that you have come to the right place! We understand your need to feel at ease and comfortable with a thorough chiropractic examination and explanation of your diagnosis and treatment plan. We offer a fun, relaxed and friendly professional environment where your questions are always welcome.
