Katlyn Wyllie's Summary

Since I was a small child, I have always found photography fascinating. After a few years of observation and knowledge on photography equipment, I discovered my passion within the art in telling a story - verbally and/or visually.

I love editing. I love the fun things I can do with Final Cut. I love getting people excited with a collection of amazing videos and photos. If I love what I do, I never work a day in my life.

Katlyn Wyllie's Projects

  • Korea Burn

    • April 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Katlyn Wyllie, Chung Shinyeob, Dani Phillips, Erin Murphy

    Started Korea's first Regional Burning Man festival named "Korea Burn." With two years in existence, Korea Burn brought in 1,400 people to a rural beach on the Western side of South Korea, Gijipo Beach, for a weekend of art appreciation, music, and the celebration of peace and love with the ritual fire conclave and burning of two wooden structures in the shape of two figures holindg hands. Katlyn was the main organizer in 2011 and worked with a famous fire-spinner Chung Shinyeob in 2012 to make the festival a success.

    We designed our own logo, website, and only used social media for advertisement. People came from all of the peninsula and some even from China and Japan to attend.

    This festival will continue and grow every years thanks to the help of Chung Shinyob and Katlyn Wyllie.
