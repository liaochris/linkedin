Dustin D. Trammell's Summary

I have primarily been employed in Information Security Research & Development in the areas of vulnerability research and exploit development, reverse code engineering, and general advanced security research of new technologies such as smart-cards, Voice over IP (VoIP), and covert communications.

Currently I am leveraging my experience in the Information Security field to incubate and manage various businesses and projects such as VBI, ExploitHub, American Information Security Group (AISG), and others.

Specialties

My current expertise is in Business Management and Vulnerability Research & Exploit Development in the realm of network communication protocols and computer software. I am currently focusing on network attack and vulnerability exploitation. I also excel in security tools design and/or development.
