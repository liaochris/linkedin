Darcy Alkus Barrow's Summary

The Marin-based husband and wife team, Christopher Sean Barrow and Darcy Alkus Barrow, have co-founded Foundation Rentals and Relocation in Kentfield, CA, which specializes in managing and leasing income properties from $3,000 - $10,000+ per month in Marin and San Francisco.

The Company also works extensively with relocation firms and corporate human resources departments to provide support for executives who are moving to the Bay Area.

As the Broker for Foundation Rentals and Relocation, Christopher Barrow oversees hiring, operations, company growth and client relations. Darcy Alkus Barrow is responsible for business development, marketing, online content and corporate image. Together they co-manage the firm's team of agents including training and mentoring programs.

"Our entire business is geared toward successfully matching property owners with potential renters," said Christopher Barrow. "Darcy and I both manage and actively work as agents. Rentals, leasing and reloation services are our core business."

Darcy Barrow, born in Malibu, previously worked at Kandoo Films in Hollywood in cable television development.

Among the community activities in which they participate are the Marin Humane Society, Wildcare and Sacramento Child Advocates. The couple also recently founded their own non-profit - The LifeBoost Charitable Fund - providing micro holisitc healing grants to Marin residents battling cancer.

For more information visit www.FoundationHomes.com

Specialties

The agency specializes in Marin County property management, relocation and rental homes, working with both landlords and tenants alike. Darcy covers properties from Sausalito to Novato, with an emphasis or corporate and private relocation and in representing homes for rent in the $7k and up range.
