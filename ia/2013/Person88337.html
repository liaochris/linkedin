Jeff Masters' Summary

Jeff possesses a broad background of hands-on as well as managerial experience in business offices. His numerous challenges and responsibilities over the years have honed an insightful managerial perspective that many professionals cannot fathom let alone claim. These advantages are significant, translating to high value, solid performance, and continued proficiency.

He has been privileged to have worked for and with prominent private companies such as Benson's, Michigan Caterpillar, Ernst Concrete, and Koegel Meats. His respective stations at each of these entities have given Jeff a unique managerial perspective that only such successful third and fourth generation family businesses can provide.

Jeff Masters' Additional Information

Interests:

Member and songwriter of The Tearjerkers, now a relatively extinct Flint-area blues band that occasionally gets out to "blow the dust off". Jeff plays guitars, and has been a participant, contributor, songwriter, and stage leader alongside other local musicians and bands in fifty venues over at least the past 17 years. He was privileged to have opened for national recording artist Jeremy Horn in 2007 in Flint. Jeff enjoys hiking Emmet County off-trail in the deeper woods where few care to wander among cedar swamps, blueberry fields, ridges, valleys, meandering streams, and hidden lakes. Whatever the weather may bring, encountering scat, fresh cougar tracks, or nearby coyotes in song as the sun disappears - this makes it all worthwhile. Jeff also enjoys golf, softball, camping, bicycling, and spending time with the family.
