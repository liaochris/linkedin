Chaz Berman's Summary

Mr. Berman has conceived and built many successful companies over the past three decades. Mr Berman is the CEO and Board Member at Grower's Secret, Inc. a life science company that produces an organic (OMRI.org) plant enhancer that is added to many other organic fertilizers focused on each plant lifecycle. Mr Berman has also founded with Mr Damien Fuentes, Lojali, Inc a stealth company with launch scheduled for Summer of 2013. He was the Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer of United Consumer Benefits Network, Inc a membership and direct response loyalty marketing company. In addition he was the Founder & Chairman of UniPrivacy Inc dba DeleteNow a company founded to help consumers avoid identity theft.

Prior to his role at UCBN, Inc and previous to his founding UniPrivacy dba DeleteNow, Mr. Berman was Chief Operating Officer (COO) of MyPoints.com, Inc., where he was responsible for the Company's domestic operations. As COO, Mr. Berman initiated a major profit-focused restructuring of the Company ahead of its acquisition by United Airlines. From 1998 through 2000, Mr. Berman held a variety of senior executive posts of increasing responsibility. Mr. Berman built the Company's sales and client resources operations (comprising 135 professionals), growing annual revenue to $64 million in 2000 from zero two years earlier.

Before joining MyPoints.com, Mr. Berman was President of three high-growth consumer companies: Goodstuffs, Footprint Marketing and WineWrights. Earlier, Mr. Berman was EVP of Sales for Willitts Designs, a merchandising design company acquired by Hallmark, Inc. At Willitts, Mr. Berman profitably grew annual sales to $32 million from $1.5 million and managed more than 200 sales professionals.
