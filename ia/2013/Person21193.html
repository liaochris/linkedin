Cindy Brunger's Summary

MSW with managerial and consulting experience in a variety of practice settings who seeks to improve client outcomes through effective program and organizational performance. Prior success in turning around underperforming programs, and launching new ones. Expertise in performance-based supervision & management, Medicaid implementation, accreditation preparation, youth development programming, and developing performance management systems. Also able/willing to assist with administrative or supervisory tasks that may be needed on a short-term or transitional basis.

Specialties: Accreditation preparation and related training; Implementation of Medicaid within residential and foster care programs; Performance Management processes to clarify expectations and provide support and accountabilty to meet contractual, accreditation and licensing requirements.
