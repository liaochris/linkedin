Jake Bondurant's Summary

I was born on April 7, 1984, and grew up in Pensacola, FL. I accepted Jesus Christ as my Savior and Lord when I was 8 years old. I felt a call to ministry in my early teenage years, but it wasn’t until I was 19 that I actually surrendered to that call to ministry. My wife, Diana Lambeth Bondurant, and I were married in November of 2004, and we’ve been serving in churches ever since. God blessed us with our son Noah in October of 2010. The early part of my ministry was centered on children and youth, and although I still love working with young people, God has created in me a passion for preaching and evangelism and a desire to lead a local body of believers in knowing, worshipping, and serving Him.
