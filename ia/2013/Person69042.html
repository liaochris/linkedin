josh pfeiffer's Experience

singer/songwriter

Josh Pfeiffer

August 2006 – Present (7 years 3 months)

Entertainer/songwriter. Pfeiffer has won outstanding musicianship awards at the Santa Cruz, Redding, and Fullerton Jazz festivals. Over the years he has had the pleasure of working with renowned music producer, Joe Chiccarelli ( U2, Jason Mraz, Shins ), and an A list of musicians, including Gigi Gonaway (Mariah Carey), Mic Gillette (Tower of Power), Stu Hamm (Joe Satriani), and Marvin McFadden (Huey Lewis). Pfeiffer was recently named a Platinum Entertainer by Grace Ormonde Wedding Style Magazine.

"Pfeiffer's deep smooth voice sets him apart. He remains true to the jazz ethos with a willingness to experiment and improvise," said Christian Gulliksen, Associate editor for the Robb Report.
