Candyce Stapen's Summary

I am also a leading authority in family travel who is frequently interviewed on television, I have authored 2,000+ family travel articles, 30 books including the Blue Guide Washington, D.C. and two National Geographic Guides, plus received numerous prestigious travel writing awards.
