Julian Stubbs' Summary

Julian Stubbs is a senior brand strategist, identity specialist and writer at UP THERE, EVERYWHERE, the Global Cloud Based Agency.

His first book, 'Wish You Were Here, the Branding of Stockholm & Destinations', was published in 2011. The book is available now on his own website - www.julianstubbs.net - and is also be available on Amazon.com. He is a creative business writer who has worked across a variety of industries.

Specialties: Brand development and architecture, Corporate Identity & Communications.
Areas: Place & Destination branding, International corporations, Industry, Healthcare & Life Science.

Julian Stubbs' Publications

  • Wish You Were Here, The Branding of Stockholm and Destinations

    • UP* Publications Group
    • November 17, 2010
    Authors: Julian Stubbs

    Wish You Were Here, The Branding of Stockholm and Destinations goes through the key elements in developing a successful destination branding programme and gives an insight into the branding of Stockholm.

    This 240 page full colour book looks at examples from New York, Liverpool, Barcelona, The Ice Hotel, Hollywood and more and examines the similarities and differences with the branding of regular consumer products and the major issues involved.

    Julian Stubbs is an English writer and brand strategist and the person behind the branding of Stockholm, The Capital of Scandinavia. In his distinctive English style he makes the journey through the branding of destinations both enjoyable and informative.

    ‘‘When it comes to destination branding, Julian Stubbs has been there and done that. Luckily for us he's now ready to pass on his knowledge. Using his first-hand experience, drawing on relevant examples, he writes in an engaging way about the brand strategies behind some of the world's greatest places."

    John Simmons formerly director of verbal identity at Interbrand in London
