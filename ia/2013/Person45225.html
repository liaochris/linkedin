Focus Edu Care's Summary

Focus Edu-Care is an off shoot of the FOCUS Group of Companies who have operations in key business segments such as Financial Services, Healthcare Staffing and Placement, Software Consultancy Services, and Education Services across many countries such as US, Europe, Canada, Middle East, Nepal and Sri Lanka. This division was launched, keeping in mind the growing need for education across the Globe. Our Goal is to offer world class education at affordable prices and to enable an individual to be on par with the best…

Specialties

Focus Edu Care is an Education Multiplex that offers definitive career courses in Stock Market Courses, Vocational Courses, Healthcare Management, Clinical Research, BPO training, etc. We have trained many batches of students in achieving successful and rewarding careers. Our Exam preparatory courses are aimed at providing admission success to students through our preparatory courses for IELTS, NCLEX, TOEFL, CET, CAT, CA - CPT, IPCC, IIT-JEE, AIEEE, etc.
