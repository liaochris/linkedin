Dylan Swan's Summary

Qualifications:
- Bachelor of Engineering (Honours), Civil
- Postgraduate Diploma (Grade: 84%), Information Technology
- Diploma with Distinction (Grade: 92%), 3D Animation

I have been working as a 3D artist specialising as a technical director since 2008 in roles from concept to final production of 3D computer generated videos, games, touchscreens, web site development, mobile phone apps, databases and virtual tours. This has required a wide range of skills that includes programming, 3D modeling, texturing, lighting, rigging, animation, rendering, photography, writing tenders, business cases and standards. I have developed many pipeline automation tools for large efficiency gains for our team and innovative products for our clients including computer game systems and 360 degree images. Before 2008 I was a Principal Analyst Programmer for many years specialising in enterprise database systems while also working as a project manager where I enjoyed communicating with clients and technical staff.

Other experience includes the development my own 3D engine using DirectX technologies to make a 3D graph software product that was published and sold in stores across Australia and online to the rest of the world. I have worked on a feature length film called Megaman for a director in New York that required rigging, animation and compositing of CG effects into live action footage. I am a passionate photographer specialising in 360 degree images, sold photos at a professional level and developed my own virtual tour travel website (360tours.com.au).

Software experience: 3ds Max, V-Ray, Maya, Realflow, Unity, UDK, Ogre, Photoshop, After Effects, Microsoft Visual Studio, SQL Server, Microsoft Access, Apple Xcode, Dreamweaver

Technology experience: Maxscript, Melscript, Javascript, JQuery, .NET, VB, C#, Objective-C, HTML 4/5, CSS, flash, video and image encoding
