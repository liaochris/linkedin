Gershon Ben-Peretz's Summary

Founded, and now president and managing director of Legal Writing Services, which is an exciting leap from the traditional Legal Process Outsourcing (L.P.O.) concept. We've taken the legal outsourcing model to an entirely new level, and are proud to be the most unique professional organization of it's kind in the world.

Each member of our legal staff graduated from among the finest universities and law schools in the U.S. We were trial and appellate lawyers in U.S.State and Federal Courts and Administrative Agencies; in jurisdictions nationwide; for between 15-30 years, prior to relocating to Israel.

And now we take on new and exciting challenges. We no longer practice law, appear in court, or dispense legal advice. Our clients are exclusively attorneys licensed in the U.S. We work together with our clients to provide them with a variety legal services; at fair and reasonable business to business rates.

Our clients are the final decision makers regarding our duties and responsibilities in every case. These should be informed decisions, and Gershon encourages an open line of communication that he is delighted to provide. Because we prefer watching our work and our client’s best interests, to watching our clock, we much prefer to agree on flat-rate fees whenever possible. Our main goals are client satisfaction and retention.

We specialize in writing criminal and civil appellate briefs. We prepare the briefs in compliance with the local rules of the specific U.S.State or Federal Court. The final draft submitted to our client, ready review, signature, and filing with the court. Our work is never complete, until our client is 100% satisfied with the final draft. We are also available to assist counsel with preparing for oral argument.

We use the finest on-line legal research and document management systems, and maintain strict security standards,which include data encryption and storage on highly secure networks and servers.

I also became qualified as an expert in the use of a variety of electronic document review software platforms, and also trained other attorneys. Clients included both private law-firms and corporate legal departments located in the U.S. and Israel, with litigation pending in U.S. federal and state courts.
