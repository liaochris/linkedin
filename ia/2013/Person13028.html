Benjamin Leary's Summary

Creating 3D art for video games with high-efficiency modeling as my primary focus, I utilize my skills to produce compelling results. I can complete entire projects or serve as a member of your team in any capacity.

Studying the principles of art since a child, my hopes are creating entertaining and attractive content for years to come. I aim to grow and improve, and while my dream of entering the industry has already been achieved, the hunger to be better and to create better content will continue to drive me.
