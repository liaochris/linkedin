Abby (Hardie) Kovach's Summary

Abby Kovach is founder and principal of Abby Kovach Financial Planning, LLC, a Registered Investment Advisory firm in the state of Colorado with offices in Denver and Boulder.

Ms. Kovach’s firm specializes in providing hourly, as-needed financial planning and advice to individuals and families, regardless of their net-worth, income or investable assets. Clients have the flexibility to engage on a one-time, as-needed, or ongoing basis.

As a Fee-Only advisor, Ms. Kovach works solely for her clients. As a Fiduciary, she is required by law always to put her clients’ best interests first. Because she does not receive any commissions, potential conflicts of interest regarding compensation are removed.

Specialties: Helping clients identify and achieve goals, retirement planning, investment advice without ongoing management, college planning, hourly planning.
