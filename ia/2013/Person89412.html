Matt McGuire's Summary

Matt founded MusicWerks Productions, Inc. in 1993 and has since written and produced music for all the major affiliates- ABC, NBC, CBS and FOX.  He also composed for the animated shows "Extreme Dinosaurs", "Pocket Dragon Adventures", "Sabrina", "Sonic X", "Winx Club", "G.I Joe", "Shaman King", "Chaotic", "Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's", etc.  He scored the movies "Titanic, The Legend Goes On", "Monster Mash", "Dennis The Menace - Cruise Control", and wrote songs for many of those shows. Matt has contributed to the scores of the epic Bollywood releases "Lahore", "Raajneeti", and "Aarakshan". Recently, Matt co-scored the critically-acclaimed film Mulberry Child with Kyle Eastwood, directed by Susan Morgan Cooper and narrated by Jacqueline Bisset. Matt does most of his work out of his custom-built recording studio in Encino, Ca. For more information, credits, music, etc.,
visit www.mattmcguiremusic.com.

Matt McGuire's Projects

  • Mulberry Child

    • 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Matt McGuire

    Written and Directed by Susan Morgan-Cooper, Mulberry Child has won several awards and recieved 3 and 1/2 stars from Roger Ebert. The film's diverse subject matter required an equally diverse score. Music that spans from China during the cultural revolution to present day Chicago was captured as authentically as possible in order to properly underscore this emotionally-charged story. Matt co-wrote the film's score with Kyle Eastwood.

  • A Christian Ebner Christmas

    • October 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Matt McGuire

    Matt produced, arranged, orchestrated, as well as performed and conducted this exciting CD from singing sensation Christian Ebner. The project was recorded in both LA and Nashville and features A-list musicians and singers from both cities.
