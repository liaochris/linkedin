Chris Buswell's Summary

Born in Sacramento, California, I am a visual communicator with a Graphic Design degree from California State University, Sacramento. What began as a mere interest in all aspects of design at a young age grew into an obsession.

In November 2011, I moved to the Orange County area in search of new inspiration and surroundings. I currently reside in Costa Mesa and work as a full-time web designer for a Fortune 500 company.

Through the course of my career, I have had the amazing opportunity to work on many projects at a corporate level, taking knowledge and experience from a variety of talented individuals along the way. My trademark is simple, clean, usable design.

Specialties

Web Branding & Identity, Social Networking, Corporate Communications, Web Advertising, Front-end Web Design, Web UI design, Icon Design, Interaction Design, Wireframes and Mobile Web Design
