Lisa Buyer's Summary

Lisa Buyer is an expert in Social PR and an advocate on the influence public relations can have on SEO and Social Media.

Ahead of the curve and trend watcher at heart - I'm a brand junkie who loves to help companies best position themselves in the marketplace. Whether it's analyzing a corporate identity system, launching a brand, creating effective sound bytes, developing a digital publicity campaign, making sure Google knows about them or sometimes even acting as the corporate therapist -- my clients turn to me when looking for the best and most professional onine branding and public relations consultation.

I recognized a need for a PR + Branding agency versed in search engine strategies and how corporate client’s benefit from the mix of traditional and online media relations; messaging, branding, positioning and reputation management are critical PR components of online media. After researching the Top PR agencies in South Florida, regionally and nationally, I was amazed to find most agencies focusing on traditional media services. Since PR is the backbone and a major driver of online media, it only makes sense to combine the power of SEO and PR under one umbrella.

Specialties: SEO/PR/Brand Consulting, Generating positive editorial coverage online and in traditional media, interactive PR, online media, SEO/PR reviews, optimized content, SEM project manager, SEO strategies, social media strategies, social media networks, analytics, online reputation management, interactive marketing.
