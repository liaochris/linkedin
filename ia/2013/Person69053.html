Josh Russell's Summary

I like to build things for the web, collaborate with great people, and see how far things can go. My approach is practical, seeing the big picture, but concentrating on the important details. Knowing at what level to abstract, are we building a platform or a tool, is it about the data, or about the users. Knowing that every detail is important to all of those things, and also the business. Not forgetting that whatever is being built, it’s always about people.

Excited by digital innovation, I always have something to give and an opinion to share. I’m Interested in how what we build with the Internet effects us as individuals and society as a whole. I believe in a flat world, and a level playing field.

Connector, thinker, enabler, pusher.. nomad. I survive on coffee, live on the Internet, and a mountain bike, but not at the same time.

Specialties

concept development, project definition, sustainability, joined-up thinking, networking, communities, structure hacking, alpha-citizenship, technology innovation, application of technology, social change, empowerment, data, people, platforms, infrastructure, open standards, protocol development, knowledge sharing, linking, preservation, all things open, best practices.
