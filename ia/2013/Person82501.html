Lori Klinka's Summary

Lori Klinka is an award-winning speaker and presentation skills coach from the Chicago area. She is best known for her entertaining, insightful and informational keynotes and workshops. Whether Lori is working with individuals or groups, her experience as an educator, producer, actress and coach allows her to provide immediate solutions for your communication issues. She has worked with Fortune 100 companies, associations and individuals, and yet when you work with Lori it’s always about you! You will see results immediately when working with her. She LOVES to see people succeed and gain confidence. She currently teaches Speaking and Acting at Columbia College.
