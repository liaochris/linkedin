Alex Mons' Experience

ICT Manager

Meteo Consult

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Information Services industry

May 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Wageningen (Netherlands)

As ICT-Manager I am accountable for a 24/7 availability on weather related services from Meteo Consult. I am responsible for the operations and for the integration of these into a new Group (international) concept. In addition to this, I am also responsible for staffing & delivery of the Dutch portfolio of new & lifecycle product and structural improvements to people, process and technology.

MeteoGroup is Europe’s largest private sector weather business with a worldwide customer base and operations in ten European countries and the USA. MeteoGroup is a full-service weather business supplying tailored products to professional, media and consumer markets, covering marine, aviation, transport and energy markets, as well as broadcasters, newspapers and digital platforms around the world. Founded in 1986, the company has an unrivalled reputation for quality and innovation in weather forecasting.
Expert meteorologists provide forecasts, support, monitoring and consultancy services 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. In addition, a dedicated weather modelling team continuously develop MeteoGroup’s in-house systems and forecasting techniques. Of the total of around 300 employees, ICT/Operations plays a key part in building and delivering the value-added data streams.

Alex Mons' Summary

Through his experience as a Naval Officer (with the Royal Netherlands Navy) and his career at Baan (an ERP-supplier, present day it is Infor) Alex matured his skills as (senior) project manager in a very wide spectrum, from IT-projects to change management programs, from all kind of communication aspects to multi-cultural environmental challenges.
Specifically in environments under high pressure Alex will be a stable resource.
Because of his experience as an officer in the Navy Alex is also able to function very well in any line function, as crisis manager or as interim.

Alex is a team player with a great sense of humour and excellent communicative and diplomatic skills. He knows how to improve team effort and how to bring people (specifically in a multi-cultural environment) to work together. He is creative, but also pragmatic and knows how to set priorities, during which he remains to keep focus on the overall view and objectives.
