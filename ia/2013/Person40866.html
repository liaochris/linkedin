Ellie Jerow, MLIS's Summary

I have a deep passion for information and the web. How could this browsing experience be improved? How could this website be better organized to help users get to what they're looking for? How could the design of what I'm looking at get out of the way of the user make this a seamless experience?

All the things that librarians do with books - organization, accessibility evaluation, collection management, helping users find the right things and use them appropriately - I do with digital information of all kinds. Information Architecture, UX design, collection and records management, and electronic publishing are just a few of the areas I've studied to prepare myself for this work.

I've been participating in the social web for over a decade - I started my first blog in 1999 and I haven't stopped sharing since. (Yes - my blogging history goes back to the birth of the term 'blog'.) In that time, I've been able to discover what works and what doesn't on a personal level. Whether you're working with a blog, an e-commerce site, social media, or another web project, I'd like to make your information work for you. So get in touch!

Specialties

Information management, information organization, information architecture, content management systems, UX design, records management
