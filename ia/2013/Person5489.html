Deborah White, LPC, CVCC, ACC's Summary

Deborah is passionate about helping Working Women create work/home balance in their lives. Deborah uses her formal life coach training and years of life experience to help clients recognize what matters most and live every day doing those. Wise decisions and choices about their daily time brings balance and contentment. With a high emphasis on time and how it is spent her clients learn how to take the overwhelm out of their lives and follow simple systems that give them hours of time back each month to focus on what is most important.
Deborah takes her certification as a Life Purpose Coach with Life Purpose Centers International and Certification with CoachVille very serious, but that does not stop her from using her natural tendency to add laughter and fun into all she does. This lighthearted approach helps her clients to relax and take a lighter look at their own circumstances making it easier to make the tough changes. Deborah attributes this zest for life to her strong core beliefs. She lives a lifestyle that is fun, honest, loving, caring, and contagious which shows through what she does and how she thinks. Which also helps you understand completely her Motto: Attitudes are contagious, is your attitude worth catching?
Her commitment to each client helps them to follow through and not give up. Deborah teaches through her example she truly walks her talk. Her joy and inspiration comes from others ah ha moments, breakthroughs and living a life of peace. People say she is a pleasure to work with.
