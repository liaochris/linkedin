Belinda Zylberman's Summary

My mission is to empower individuals to LIVE IN AUTHENTIC RELATIONSHIP.

In 2011 I founded Paikea Mediation (formerly Mediate PDX) to create this discourse through mediations, trainings, coaching and workshops. I work with individuals, couples, families (which includes ALL kinds of families) and small businesses.

My credentials: My “formal education” includes an Undergraduate Degree in Communication and Anthropology from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and a Law Degree from Cardozo Law School, Yeshiva University in New York. My “informal” education includes over 500 hundred hours of experiential education training received across the United States.

I have worked, lived, studied and traveled in 45 countries and it is the experience of taking in so many languages (spoken and unspoken) and cultures that most informs me in the work that I do with people and in relationship.

Specialties: Divorce and family mediation, basic mediation, conflict resolution, coaching, training, team building, listening and communication skills development, small group facilitation, large group facilitation, mission and vision development, public speaking, and life transitions.
