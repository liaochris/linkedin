Jon Pitton's Summary

With 20+ years’ experience, I am passionate about helping clients increase online revenue and enhance customer experiences through call center outsourcing. An expert in e-Commerce and IT Support Call Center industries means I help clients keep costs under control while they get the extra help they need.

At Avertech, we believe in relationships: those with our customers are most important to us. Here for you as IT Support, we understand your problems and provide solutions… solutions that allow you to focus on what you do best: your business.

Every business is different, so we offer customizable support services to meet your needs: IT Support, supplemental staff support, or special project servicing.

Why did I choose Avertech? It is exciting to work with an early stage company with developed strategic, cloud-based partnerships who serve clients with a youthful mentality, believing and proving that as IT professionals, they can solve any problem put to them. And, known for my follow-through in sales and service initiatives, I am driven to listen and translate client needs into meaningful programs.

As a Click-to-Call and IT Support Company, Avertech is achieving success where other help desk vendors fail—our client-centric approach succeeds because of a talented, hard-working team delivering clever solutions.

Avertech has partnered with Workday, a cloud-based ERP whose October 2012 IPO was very successful. The opportunity to acquire enterprise accounts as prospects reach a need to upgrade SAP is proving to be fruitful for the company and provides savings and increased efficiency to their new clients.

I bring a strong executive-level referral network which will help others succeed. By joining Avertech, I’m able to bring value to my conections who seek to lower operating costs and improve internal and customer service.
