Marge Jensen's Summary

Marge Jensen brings over 30 years of marketing and sales strategy to TouchStone Marketing. Marge combines her passion for creative problem solving and finding the right opportunities for her clients who are ready to take next steps to grow their business. As a marketing professional, getting the attention of the customer through the combination of message, visuals and mediums to build a relationship is not for the "faint hearted". “Creative challenges are what I live for.”

TouchStone Marketing partners with small and medium sized businesses to help them build and maintain a strong ongoing marketing effort. "Every Day Counts!". We use social media, online marketing assessment, competitive review and online advertising which will have the greatest impact on the businesses bottom line.

Since 1997, Marge has worked with a broad range of clients, guiding them through the strategic planning and creative solutions resulting in increased sales. A partial list of clients: Chevron, Abbott Diagnostics, LifeScan, Roche, Hitachi, Boehringer Mannheim, Applied Biosystems, Celera, PeopleSoft, Thoratec Corporation, Nellcor Puritan Bennett, VentureOne, Security Management Group Intl., Denali Data Systems, Telegraph Business Improvement District, Mayer Laboratories, Safeway and others.

Marge is a consummate explorer. When she not immersed in new marketing ideas, social media applications for her clients and networking, you might find her at a baseball game or sampling a new culinary adventure. She is a graduate of St Mary's College in Moraga, Executive Business School, UCSF in San Francisco and currently lives in the South Bay.
