Haje Jan Kamps' Summary

In June 2011, I set up Triggertrap Ltd. We make products that give users creative ways of triggering their cameras. See Triggertrap.com

Before that, following from the huge success of my popular Photocritic.org photography blog, I set up as a writer and editor of books about photography. Between 2009 and 2011, I worked as a full-time writer, mostly on consumer electronics and photography subjects. Check out http://kamps.org for my bibliography of book- and freelance projects.

I also have a dark yet colourful past as an agile online product/project manager. http://press.nokia.com and http://fwd.five.tv are two of the bigger ones I managed, but there are dozens of smaller sites out there with my name on them as well. In the figurative sense, of course; nobody actually thinks to add the names of the people who build websites on their websites. Perhaps sites should have a movie-style 'credits scroll' at the end? Just a thought...

Recently, I've done a fair bit of speaking for Escape the City, University of Southbank, various conferences, etc. Topics include entrepreneurship, customer support, crowdfunding, creating an MVP, lean startup, and various other bits and pieces. In general, it's getting me to shut up that's the problem.
