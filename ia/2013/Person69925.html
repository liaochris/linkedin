Jeff Schilffarth's Summary

I'm a telecommunications technology professional helping CXOs, businesses owners and managers improve business process all while driving costs down and improving revenues.

I help businesses leverage IP based phone system technology along with the Unified Communications (UC) applications inherent in that technology. These applications include Mobility, Instant Messaging, Presence and Contact Center technology.

I have 23 years in telecommunications, technology, sales and marketing.

I have served clients with the following solutions: multi-location networked solutions, Voice over IP (VoIP), IP-PBX, PBX, Key Systems, Voice Processing, Unified Communications, Call Centers, Interactive Voice Response (IVR) and ACD.

It is my goal to educate and serve clients looking to grow their businesses, improve business process and enhance communications with clients and employees by providing expertise in the field of telecommunications.

Specialties: Telecommunications, Technology, Sales, Marketing, Project Management, Solutions and Education.
