Komal Mangu's Projects

  • Danspire

    • July 2012 to August 2012
    Team Members: Komal Mangu, Medha Malhotra (Minty), DongYoung Kim, Julia Eichler

    Danspire provides an interactive dance experience. It is an interactive dance floor which encourages the user to get to know various forms of dance and also to have fun with his/her dancing partner.

  • Taters vs Aliens - Interactive Iphone/pad game

    • November 2011 to February 2012
    Team Members: Komal Mangu, , Ana Stan

    Taters and Aliens is an iphone application for which I along with my team has created a location based gaming app to encourage children to visit art museums by introducing a fun element of gaming. From the findings of user research it was very difficult to get the attention and involvement of children into visiting and knowing about art in art museums. It was also found that children of the present generation astonishingly spend a considerable amount of the time on mobile phones or tablets playing games. Hence it only seemed logical to create a location based gaming application which would encourage and involved children in learning about art. A fictional narrative of the aliens attacking the Tate Museum to steal the artwork was created and the mission of the children was to protect the art pieces from the aliens before they steal them. To our surprising finding from the user testing children were very much involved and engrossed in the activity and also learned about the artwork! It also was a medium to increase communication between the parent and child.
