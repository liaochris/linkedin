Brian Pasch's Summary

I am the founder and CEO of PCG Consulting Inc. which was created in 2012. In 2005 I founded PCG Digital Marketing, which is now lead by CEO, Glenn Pasch. PCG Digital Marketing was ranked 192nd fastest growing private company by Inc Magazine in 2011.

One of our largest vertical markets is the Automotive Industry where our company has been recognized for our success with car dealers. Those awards include:

- Top Internet Trainer
- Top Automotive SEO Company
- Top Automotive SEM Company

PCG Consulting Inc. provides vendor neutral consulting services to assist car dealers develop the strategies, processes, and reporting needed to operate profitability in an Internet centric business climate.

If you are looking for Brian Pash, Bryan Pash, or Brian Pass you may be looking for me with the wrong spelling.

If you are looking for solid advice on marketing your business on the Internet, give my firm a call and request a free consultation.

Specialties: Search Engine Marketing, Web Design, Internet Marketing, Internet Lead Generation, Internet Sales Process, CRM Workflows & Training, Budget Analysis, Direct Marketing and Reputation Management.

According to Landino “I have been a long time advocate of empowering women in the automotive industry. One reason I chose to work with PCG Digital Marketing was their leadership in providing cutting edge education for car dealers.”
    After participating in the PCG Pit Stop Conference, Lizelle took action, “I reached out to Jody DeVere because I saw the opportunity for women to strengthen their careers through PCG’s educational workshops.
