Darren Starwynn's Summary

My name is Dr. Darren Starwynn and I am an author, inventor, and integrator, who is passionate about the science and business of energy based medicine and holistic practices.

Want to achieve more masterful results, be on the leading edge, and increase revenue? As the President of East-West Seminars and The Microlight Institute, these are the things I help Acupuncturists, Chiropractors, M.D.s, and Estheticians accomplish on a daily basis.

Eastwestmed’s Research Director. Foremost expert in electro-acupuncture and Energy Medicine, and a leader in the research and development of cutting-edge microcurrent, microlight (color-light), and electro-acupuncture systems for over twenty years. Also a highly regarded writer and educator.
