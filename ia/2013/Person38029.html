Dr Kara Bishop's Summary

Dr. Bishop pulls from a large variety of Chiropractic Techniques and Acupuncture to give her patients the best of herself. She recieved her degree from one of the top Chiropractic schools in the nation and furthered her studies in Applied Kinesiology and Acupuncture while pursueing her degree. Dr. Bishop strives to make her office family friendly and a place of relaxation and well-being. No one knows all the answers, but Dr. Bishop goes out of her way to find your answers. Every 'body' is different, that's why health care must be individualized to the person not the diagnosis. Dr. Bishop does just that.
