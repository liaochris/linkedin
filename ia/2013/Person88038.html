Mary Claire O'Neal's Summary

Mary Claire O'Neal is a nationally known communication consultant and author of the award-winning book, Becoming What You Want to See in the World. For almost 20 years, she has been working with Fortune 500 companies, small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals in professional and personal development through workshops, keynotes, consulting and coaching. Mary Claire has coached a New York Times best-selling author, and one of her clients was chosen by Fortune Magazine as one of the 100 Best Companies to Work For. Mary Claire is also a Kentucky Peer Advisory Network Consultant (KPAN) for the Kentucky Arts Council.

She's had many articles published on communication, leadership, and life balance, and her book, Becoming What You Want to See in the World, has won a National Best Books Award, ForeWord Magazine's Book of the Year Award, and a national Indie Excellence Award. Her book has been published and translated into several languages and is distributed in over 25 countries. Mary Claire is an avid blogger and writes for Intent.com and Examiner.com.

Mary Claire O'Neal, CTACC is a certified coach, professional member of the National Speakers Association and the International Coaching Federation. She is the 2012 President of the National Speakers Association/KY and has serves on its Board of Directors. She's been an Award Finalist for Commerce Lexington’s Independent Professional of the Year Award for 2007 and 2008.

Specialties: Communication consulting; communication and leadership coaching; positive intelligence coaching; keynote speaker; conference presenter; customized workshops for corporations, service and health care organizations, and non-profits; articles on communication, leadership, life purpose, service and work/life balance.

Before becoming a communication consultant, her past experience included mental health educator, business owner and Equity actor.
