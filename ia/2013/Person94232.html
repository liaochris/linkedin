Mike Holman's Summary

My name is Mike Holman ( http://mikeholman.com/ ) As a Wall Street Veteran of over 10 Years I am more than happy to share my experience in the Custody space within Financial Services handling products such as Trade Settlement, Cash, Corporate Actions, income & more for years..

I have uncovered inconsistencies within the TBA (whose underlying securities are Mortgages) Reporting market resulting in FINRA/SEC ruling allowing for greater transparency (the heart of Dodd Frank). More info is at my sites http://mikeholman.com/ and http://tbasecurity.info/ as well as the following FINRA and related sites:

http://www.finra.org/Industry/Regulation/Notices/2012/P126488
http://www.finra.org/Newsroom/NewsReleases/2012/P196963
http://www.finra.org/Industry/Compliance/MarketTransparency/TRACE/Notices/P160926
http://solomonexamprep.com/news/finra/exam-alert-finra-amends-trace-rules-for-tba-transactions/

I am a Global Knowledge Certified Professional Business Analyst, Institute of Certified Bankers Certified Securities Operations Professional, New York State Teachers Assistant and trained New York State Security Officer. Of special note is that in the way of Information Security Governance, Risk and Compliance, I have used RSA Archer eGRC with Administration Access.

I have been on the phone with clients in Asia Pacific, Europe, South Africa, Western Hemisphere and Latin America at all hours to ensure timely settlement of trades in a Middle Office role as a Liason between the Client and the Support Areas globally.

I've proudly serviced Managers, Banks, Insurance Companies, etc. Clients assigned to me over the years (like Loomis Sayles, Wellington Management, Deutsche Assetment Management, US Bank, Ameriprise Financial, Jackson Asset Management, etc.)

As a New York State Registered Security Officer, I am looking to get involved with the New York Chapter of ASIS with a further desire to obtain the Certified Protection Professional ( CPP™ ) Designation.
