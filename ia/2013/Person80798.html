Bob Gallo's Summary

As a Personal Success Coach I provide personal and business success tips, articles and resources through my web site: SuccessTipsOnline.com. Achieving real and lasting success takes time, consistency, and patience and it is my wish that the information and resources that I provide on this web site can help you get there.

I’m a true believer in being the best you can be (and thinking that way too). It is my belief that true success is the result of providing value by expressing and sharing your creative gifts. It’s about enjoying “the magic that life has to offer, giving and receiving love, expressing gratitude, and being happy.

Many sales professionals and solo-entrepreneurs spend most of their time chasing new business and ignoring their clients and those people in their center of influence who could be providing the with a consistent stream of new business and referrals.

I also provide a service (SendOutCards) that many top sales professionals and business owners use to unlock the hidden sales and referrals contained in your client list and personal rolodex by positioning yourself as being the person that they want to do business with.

While most sales professionals and solo-entrepreneurs recognize the importance of using keeping in touch with customers, prospects and referral sources, they just never seemed to get it done.

Whether you are a sales professional or a business owner, there has never been an easier way to send a genuine thank you, reminder or follow-up card. This system provides you with a powerful business tool that you can use to make sure your business clients and customers know just how important they are!

If you want to get and keep loyal customers and generate a consistent steam of referrals, I invite you to visit my web site CustomGreetingCardsOnDemand.com and discover why you should be using this powerful relationship building tool to take your business to the next level.
