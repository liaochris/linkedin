Laura Rys' Summary

As a member of the New York and New Jersey bars, I have broad experience in defense-side Securities Law with an emphasis on the new Dodd-Frank Forex regulations relating to commodities and hedge funds.

I am skilled in creating and conducting risk assessments and making recommendations to mitigate those risks. My reputation and my passion is tenaciously performing thorough due diligence.

As the owner of a boutique law firm specializing in assisting NFA members with disclosure requirements mandated by both the CFTC and NFA, I am a strong believer in the benefit of performing mock compliance examinations (pre-audits) to ensure readiness for a potential NFA audit. By emphasizing a compliance culture, financial institutions can provide investors with a higher level of comfort in the handling of their investments. Additionally, firms can save money if they are are actually audited and prevent costly penalties for violations.
