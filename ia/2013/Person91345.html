Melita Thornhill's Experience

President

Thornhill Career Services

September 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months)

Over the years I have worked closely with hundreds of individuals in their personal quest for a more rewarding and satisfying career.

This experience, combined with delivering highly tailored services and support to Corporate Human Resource departments has led me to my own transition. Thornhill Career Services is a continuum of my life's journey.

As president of Thornhill Career Services I continue to be a strong believer in a holistic approach to career planning. My team and I offer a wide range of individualized Career Planning Programs to support both Corporate H.R. departments and individuals who are looking for the personal touch.

Our backgrounds are varied and showcase the expertise required to assist our clients to a successful outcome. We help develop realistic and achievable goals, enabling clients to meet both career and retirement life needs.

Thornhill Career Services is honored and proud to have been welcomed as a member of Career Partners International. Career Partners International is one of the world's largest providers of talent management solutions with more than 200 offices in over 25 countries around the world. CPI partners are of like mind, with organizations of all sizes that successfully attract, retain, develop and transition talent.

CPI partners draw upon and more importantly share with each other the expertise of more than 1600 highly skilled and experienced professionals in the areas of assessment, coaching, leadership development, retirement planning, outplacement and recruiting.
