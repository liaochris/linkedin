Kevin Tucker, M.A., Career Coach, Speaker's Summary

People are drawn to Kevin Tucker because of his proven experience in bringing organizations and individuals together.

Kevin brings to you over 20 years of experience in human relations at the HR manager and director levels as a certified HR professional, along with being a credentialed career and life transition coach.

As recently stated:

“Kevin is a dedicated professional with a strong orientation towards service and relationship building.”
~ Robert McNeil – Vice President, Human Resources, ValueOptions – Phoenix, AZ

“This process provided an excellent opportunity to jump-start future career paths despite a very disappointing lay-off. Well worth it!”
~ Carly S. - Human Resources Manager - Mesa, AZ

With his broad experience as a career coach, speaker, and author Kevin has been able to give back to the community through leading community workshops, speaking on national platforms, and writing articles for Gannett Publishing—publishers of USA Today. Through his relational style of coaching and group facilitation, he has been nationally recognized for having a personal, life-changing commitment to developing proactive strategies for a more meaningful life and career.

Kevin is an active member of the Society of Human Resource Management (SHRM) through the Human Resource Association of Central Ohio (HRACO) chapter and has served on the board of directors for the Central Ohio Coaches.

Kevin holds a dual Masters (cum laude) in Human Resource Management and Management. In addition to his Professional in Human Resources (PHR) certification, he is also credentialed by the Career Management Alliance as a Credentialed Career Manager (CCM) and has been trained by The Coaches Training Institute. Kevin also is an ordained minister. Kevin’s first book, "Putting Passion to Purpose", is scheduled for release soon.

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Internet industry

October 2007 – April 2013 (5 years 7 months)

This is a program of CareerBuilder, which provides career services to individuals that are typically at the graduate level of their education. In this role, Kevin maps out an individual plan for each student to include: overall career strategy, resume focus, networking, online presence, social media, interviewing, and tips for negotiation. He is one of the top coaches on the team, as evidenced by individuals being referred to him by name and the continued positive feedback that is received.
