Rick Stephens' Summary

Having worked as Plant Manager, CEO, Account Manager and Business Owner for over 25 years, one thing has become overwhelming clear: A company must to create, implement and constantly improve upon its business systems. These systems include but are not limited to Operations, Human Resources, Safety, Quality, Customer Service, Accounting and Information Technology.
In the field of management, I help small to medium sized companies startup new facilities and new companies, sometimes temporary taking managerial roles to accomplish this. Helping to develop the business system mentioned above. I have also taken temporary management role to restructure seriously troubled companies.

Specialties

Web based business applications for small and medium sized companies.
Plant management including Operations, Human Resources, Safety, Quality, Customer Service, Sales, Accounting and Information Technology systems.
