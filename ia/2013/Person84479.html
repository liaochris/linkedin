Lisa Malik, M.A.'s Summary

Through my seminars and private sessions, I help people become effective communicators. I developed the Maxitize Method which trains people to present themselves in a clear and comfortable manner in any situation. Whether it is interviewing skills, public speaking, video coaching or sales presentations, there are many ways to make an unforgettable impression.

Many of my clients are nervous about speaking in front of a group. I help them Overcome Nervousness, Connect with the Audience and use Speaking to Enroll more Clients.

Specializations: Body language, Speech Delivery, and Projecting the Image you Desire.

Seminars: How to make an Unforgettable Impression, Tips for Presenting your Business, Creating an Elevator Speech that Works, Use Speaking to Enroll more Clients.

I am available for public speaking, corporate training, private coaching, virtual coaching and pageant coaching. Remember it's not just what you say , It's how you say it.
