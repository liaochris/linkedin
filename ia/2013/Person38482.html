David Schor's Experience

Official Artist

The Sailing Channel. Tv

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry

January 2012 – Present (1 year 9 months)

TheSailingChannel.TV is delighted to present the Nautical Art of David Schor, one of the best active nautical artists in the world today. David has been a sailor for many years and has drawn upon his passion for sailing to create a series of original, dramatic and energetic nautical paintings in his unique impressionist style.
Much of David's work is centered on commissions from private and organizational clients in varied locations around the globe including the United States, Italy, France, Norway, England, Germany and Australia. He has been commissioned by such seagoing organizations as the Windjammer Barefoot Cruises, Norwegian Cruise Line, and Royal Caribbean Cruise Line.
David has created dozens of beautiful paintings depicting sailing and motor yachts as well as original portraits of yacht Owner’s, Captains, and their families. His art work is found in many prestigious homes, offices, yachts, and public places worldwide, as well as in many magazines, books, and on television.

TheSailingChannel is offering the full range of David Schor's nautical works, including Originals, signed Limited Editions in various sizes, and signed Open Editions. You can view David's work and purchase it online at davidschor.thesailingchannel.tv.
Select a David Schor painting to add an exciting, nautical element to your home, office, or yacht. And if you are looking for a custom work by a world-class nautical artist, TheSailingChannel can arrange a consultation with David to discuss your commission. Learn more at www.thesailingchannel.tv/davidschor

Internationally Collected Artist

The David Schor Gallery aka BODS, Inc

March 1980 – Present (33 years 7 months) Palmetto Bay, Fl, 33158

The artist for thousands of paintings around the world, David Schor is in a league of his own. Few artists possess the ability to create the variety of paintings that David has shown us so far. His confidence is such that he will create a realistic pen and ink or perhaps an acrylic so full of color that your excitement will grow with each viewing. He has also worked on many canvasses using oil.
His long list of clients is testament to the work that only David is able to bring to any home, office, or organization. He captures the needs of his clients and then goes to work taking numerous photographs of his subjects until he is satisfied that he has been able to capture the essence of his subject.
His work may be found in many homes and public places, as well as in offices, magazines, books, newspaper articles, and on television.
David obtained his Master’s Degree from George Washington University in Washington, D.C.
Approximately five years ago, he was commissioned to create a painting to commemorate the anniversary of the first Peace Conference of the League of Nations. A copy of the painting now hangs in the Castello de Vachan in San Remo, Italy, where the conference was held. A photograph of the painting , along with an article about the unveiling of the painting and the celebration surrounding the event, was featured in Italy’s national newspaper, La Stampa.
Many of our countries’ YMCA’s have prints of the “Four Values“, original paintings that David was commissioned to create for the organization. Each painting depicts a value by telling a story . The originals are the property of the YMCA.
Rotary International, in a similar fashion, commissioned the artist to create stories with his brush, and so he also made it possible for Rotarians around the world to see their valuable work through paintings.
The Rotary International’s “Four Way Test” paintings are now the property of this well known organization.

Official Artist

YMCA of the USA

Nonprofit; 10,001+ employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

January 1996 – December 1997 (2 years)

I created, for the YMCA of the USA the paintings called "The Four Values". Each original painting, three feet by four feet depicted one of the following values: Honesty, Caring, Respect, Responsibility.
The originals were published by the YMCA of the USA as Limited Edition Lithographs, posters and stationary.
I am in possession of the Artist Proofs of all four images and if contacted can make them available.
I was also selected as the "Volunteer of the Year by the YMCA of the USA.
