Keith Mabe's Summary

I am currently the Vice President of Operations for Charter Capital. We are recognized as one of the hardest working independent providers of invoice factoring for small to mid-sized businesses. We offer a complete line of factoring and related financial services. Headquartered in Houston, Texas, we provide accounts receivable financing and asset-based lending for major industries including freight and transportation, consulting firms, service providers, staffing firms, distributors and manufacturers, medical service providers.

Specialties

Invoice Factoring and Accounts Receivable Financing, Business practices and procedures, accounting, sales and marketing, Internet marketing, IT systems and processes, network infrastructure, personnel and project management
