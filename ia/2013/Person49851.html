Grant Holmes' Summary

My greatest passion lies in brand management and marketing. By extension, marketing and PR follow. With over 20 years of business experience from ownership to managing at the C-level, I've come to believe that "BRAND" is everything. While many think of brand as their logo, we know that brand encompasses everything your stakeholders come to know and experience regarding your organization.

For over 10 years my business, Unisance, LLC served clients by Listening, Creating & Delivering. I felt my strength was taking their story and effectively suggesting the best path to having their public "get" what it is they do and what they are about. This was accomplished through logo design & creation, collateral creation, writing and editing, web & social media presence and more.

I've worked in and for industries as diverse as construction, manufacturing, marketing, technical training, tourism, entertainment and software development.

My greatest strength lies in the ability to quickly assess needs, develop plans to achieve goals and implement those plans. In short, I learn complex information- Quickly. And I know how to develop plans and strategy to overcome obstacles and produce positive bottom-line results.
