Lewis Bishop's Summary

Homeowners: If you are underwater on your mortgage, I can help

In these difficult financial times, many people are struggling. If you owe more than your house is worth, I can help you find the best possible solution for your situation. I understand the impact financial stress of this kind has on a family. In partnership with a trusted law firm, I will clearly explain the available options including loan modification, short sale and foreclosure, and how they each will affect your choices in the future. Once you decide on a course of action, I will work with you to create a plan to get back on track financially. My goal is to do more than just help with your transaction, I want to help you make a new start. If you are facing a short sale or foreclosure, call me at 925-368-7738 for a free, confidential consultation.

Investors: Get access to hidden inventory of highly discounted foreclosed properties

If you are an investor who is looking to develop a portfolio of rental properties in Contra Costa County, I am an invaluable resource. As a specialist in the sale of foreclosed homes, I offer access to highly discounted inventory that is otherwise unavailable to the average investor. The majority of these properties will never appear on the MLS. Our team tours hundreds of foreclosed properties each week to determine the best possible match for each investor. I also create a database for each of my clients so they can easily track their existing investments, and view video, pictures and agent notes for potential investments. As a full-service resource, I also offer clients access to property management, remodeling and maintenance services. If you are looking to build a portfolio of highly profitable rental properties, call me at 925-368-7738.

Specialties

Leadership, organization, marketing, contract negotiation, process analysis, project management, public speaking, business planning, strategic planning, competitive analysis, communication between stakeholders, real estate foreclosure prevention, and real estate short sale negotiation.
