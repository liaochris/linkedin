Danny Rocks' Summary

I train and coach clients to improve their communications skills. This may be involve communicating Financial results using an Excel spreadsheet. Or, leading a meeting, using MS PowerPoint to illustrate the progress of your project. Or, writing a memo to the project stakeholders in MS Word.

For the past 15 years, I have been a popular keynote speaker and seminar leader. I consult for NAMM, The International Music Products Association in the area of Professional Development.

My skill set is put to good use as a meeting facilitator for corporate and non-profit organizations.

You can see me "in action" by watching one of my @ 400 free MS Excel and PowerPoint video lessonson my website -www.thecompanyrocks.com

I am also the author of five best-selling educational DVDs: "The 50 Best Tips for Excel, PowerPoint, Word, Access, and Outlook 2007. You can purchase them at my online bookstore: http://store.thecompanyrocks.com
