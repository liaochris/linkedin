CEO and Founder

Carole Spiers Group

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

February 1987 – Present (26 years 6 months) London. UK

Established in 1987, the Carole Spiers Group (CSG) is a leading provider of stress management and employee counselling. Their focus is on developing a healthy workplace culture through the successful management of work stress and organisational change and work with equal success in the UK and the Gulf.

Through its cutting-edge work stress management programmes, CSG has delivered benefits to both commercial and public sector clients around the world including names such as Abu Dhabi Marine Operating company, Accenture, Al Habib [Oman], AXA, Debenhams, Dubai Cables, Givaudan, House of Fraser, Kanoo Group [ME], Phoenix Pharmahandel [Germany], Somerfield, W H Smith and many others.

CSG consultants have introduced personal anti-stress work stress strategies into organisations that have been shown to decisively improve productivity and competitive advantage through satisfied employees working in a healthy corporate culture.

CSG Will Show You Show to Manage Work Stress Effectively, through:

* Executive Stress Management Boardroom Briefings
* Work Stress Management Training for Managers
* Stress Awareness Programmes for all Employees
* Professional Counselling and Post Trauma Debriefings
* 1-1 Executive Coaching and Mentoring for
Managers
* Motivational Keynote Presentations for Seminars

With a UK network of employee counsellors, professional consultants, trainers and coaches, CSG is uniquely equipped to advise professionally on both the human and the corporate aspects of work stress, including bullying, intimidation, violence, post-trauma, absenteeism and organisational change.

CSG is regularly called upon for professional comment by the BBC, Sky, CNN, Gulf Region TV / radio and other media, as well as being a regular contributor on work stress to Gulf News, the UK and Gulf regional press and professional journals.
