What is the process?


1) Your firm conducts a client intake.

A list of required documents and a questionnaire will be provided by D.S. Consulting based on the bankruptcy database you use.

2) After the receiving ALL the required bankruptcy documents and the FULLY completed bankruptcy questionnaire, an electronic file is sent via e-mail to D.S. Consulting.

3) D.S. Consulting prepares the bankruptcy petition & conducts a client phone interview to address missing, unclear, or contradicting information.

4) Once the interview is complete, and any changes are made, the petition is reviewed by another member of our team.
Our bankruptcy team has almost 60 years of combined experience which includes: bankruptcy petition preparing, petition reviewing, experience as a Ch.7 Bankruptcy Trustee, working with a Ch. 13 Bankruptcy Trustee, and work as bankruptcy consultants for various bankruptcy law firms.

5) After this thorough review, a second client interview is conducted to determine if any remaining changes are necessary, or to cover any important information overlooked in the initial phone interview.

6) One copy of the bankruptcy petition is now sent to both your client and to your firm for review.

a) The client is instructed to print the bankruptcy petition, review it, and mark any questions or remaining inaccuracies or areas of concern directly onto the bankruptcy petition and bring it to the sign-off appointment where any final corrections are made.

b) Your firm reviews the bankruptcy petition for accuracy and completeness, and calls the client to set up a sign-off appointment.
