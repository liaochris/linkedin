Brett Johnson's Summary

Highly collaborative, agile, and professional public servant with varied international experience in public administration. Proven abilities to lead people, manage teams, improve business process, and deliver results within the context of organizational goals. A gritty leadership style.

Passionate about coaching people to reach their potential, building enterprise-wide leadership capacity, fostering public service excellence, and managing natural resources in a sustainable and responsible manner. Experience in regional service delivery, central office policy/strategy development, and corporate service support (intra-department and government-wide).

Areas of strength are self-awareness, building networks, creating collaborative environments, engaging stakeholders to develop partnerships, supporting the success of others, turning continuous improvement ideas into action, communicating effectively, thriving in high change environments, and planning and organization skills.

Brett Johnson's Publications

  • Supervising in the Public Service: A handbook for success (2nd Edition)

    • Independently Published
    • April 16, 2012
    Authors: Brett Johnson

    Supervising in the Public Service is a short and practical handbook for new, seasoned, and aspiring supervisors within the public service. A core theme of this handbook is that supervising is not about using positional authority to control people. It is about connecting with people, understanding them as individuals, and then creating a space for them to be successful. Available in paperback and as a Kindle eBook.
