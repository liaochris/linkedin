John Heseltine's Summary

About Me

After 34 enjoyable years working in a large blue chip company (straight from School at 16) I left in 2007 to become an independent employee. I wanted to use all the skills and experience I had learned over the years. I set up Ocius Consulting Limited and have never looked back since (See LinkedIn Company page)

I see myself as an energetic, customer focused, hands-on HR specialist with experience in a wide range of disciplines including

Business and Process Analysis
HR Operations
HR metrics
HR Outsourcing
Offshoring and Shared Services
Exposure to all HR processes including Reward and Recruitment.

This is supplemented with excellent business awareness, change and project management skills. In recent years I have also been asked to use my broad skills to successfully help transform and change the procurement process and procedures of a well-known global organisation.

I like to get involved in change projects from the start to completion, liaising with people at all levels from Senior Board Director level sponsors to shop floor staff. As such I have a sound appreciation for best practice and strategy for transforming services as well as attention to the operational details which can often derail implementation of any change projects.

I am currently learning to speak Mandarin Chinese and hold valid Business VISA for India (expires July 2013)
