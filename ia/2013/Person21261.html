Chris Cowley's Summary

A proven marketer, that has over six years experience with high-profile media, music, entertainment, banking, retail, airline, travel, telecommunications organisations in Australia; and with a strong background in digital marketing and ecommerce. A highly efficient, creative and innovative thinker, that is results focused, and has demonstrated success in the planning, creation, and implementation of digital and traditional marketing strategy; market analysis; growing brands; enhancing product/service offerings; client and agency relationship management; project management; team leadership; and financial planning in a fast paced, hands-on entrepreneurial environment.

In my corporate career I've worked with some of Australia's and the world's top brands including MasterCard, Flight Centre, Jetset Travel Group, EMI, Live Nation, Disney, Universal Pictures, Paramount Pictures, MYER, Fairfax, Virgin Australia, Optus, St George, ANZ, Westpac, Channel 7, 2GB, Racing NSW plus as a freelance marketer with smaller businesses including Kenilworth Partners and Cafe Shenkin.

Specialties: digital marketing, direct marketing, social media, email marketing, search engine marketing (SEM), ecommerce, account management, business development, project management, acquisition marketing, customer retention, campaign management, copywriting, team leadership, marketing and business strategy consultation
