Anne Swigard's Summary

Every teacher has the right to feel prepared to teach any student that walks through their classroom door. Recent research has shown that educators simply do not feel that they've left their teacher preparation program with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate today's complicated and ever-changing educational landscape.

I remember what it was like, leaving my Post-Baccalaureate teacher program in 1990, and being wholly unprepared for what I faced in my first teaching assignment - a room full of divergent learners and no materials to help me teach them. It was because of that first experience that I made it my mission to acquire training in nearly every major initiative in general education, including the Common Core State Standards, brain research, 6 Traits Writing, the SIOP Model, SDAIE, Language Immersion, Bilingual Education, Effective Elements of Instruction (Hunter), Cooperative Learning, Robert Marzano's Classroom Instruction that Works, Integrated Thematic Instruction, Thinking Maps, and many more.

As a trainer of teachers in school, district, and university settings since 1998, my passion has been to help educators, no matter how long they've been teaching, become fluent in strategies that are research-driven, highly motivating for both teachers and students, and fun. The training curriculum I've written has reached many thousands of teachers and has impacted hundreds of thousands of students. My training sessions show teachers exactly what to do with struggling students and divergent learners, and how to do it well.

Alas, one person cannot be everywhere! That's why I created a network of dynamic consultant trainers that have worked and trained across the country. Our excellent participant evaluations (mean, median and mode scores of 5/5, consistenly) and more than 1,000 testimonials are evidence that teachers love the way we train - as if every student they teach is our own.

Specialties

Presenting, training, helping school districts with issues involving the Office for Civil Rights, writing training curriculum, program design for second language acquisition programs, dual language setup and training, Structured English Immersion, SEI program setup and training, Literacy workshops, hands-on Math workshops, training for mainstream teachers in Special and Gifted Education, teacher coaching and mentoring.
