Kim White, MAC's Summary

I have over 25 years of tax and accounting experience. I provide superior and personalized service at reasonable prices. My goal is to provide my clients with a very satisfying experience regarding their tax and accounting needs. I provide QuickBooks support: set up, maintenance, and staff training.

I have a Masters in Forensic Accounting and I look forward to doing more in the forensic field.

I have the pleasure of being the production accountant on the movie "Lake Effects" filmed at Smith Mountain Lake, VA, in October 2010. I worked closely with the producer, Sara Elizabeth Timmins and co-producer, Scott Bridges. My experience coupled with additional education through John Gaskin Productions LLC, is helping me attain more work in the movie field. I have two possible movie projects lining up for next year.
