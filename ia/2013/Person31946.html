David Hitt's Summary

David Hitt, of the Hitt|Kim Team at Keller Williams Realty, is very well respected throughout the brokerage community for his comprehensive knowledge of real estate, from residential sales to estates, probate/trust and investment properties. He specializes in Southern California Lifestyle Properties in Studio City, Sherman Oaks, Encino, Beverly Hills and West Hollywood. David offers buyers and sellers the highest level of professional service that produces optimum results. That is why over 98 percent of his business comes from referrals from past satisfied clients. Before recently joining Keller Williams Realty, for most of his career, David has worked for top national brokerages such as Coldwell Banker and Prudential California Realty. He has been in the real estate industry since 1996 and has been consistently recognized as a top producing agent receiving several awards. David has also worked as the Office Training Director at Coldwell Banker where he was responsible for training and mentoring new agents coming into the business. He is known for his talent for strategically marketing and pricing his listings to sell for top dollar and in the shortest amount of time. David also successfully assists his buyers with locating the right home and negotiating the best price and terms. David believes that successful transactions depend on effective communication with clients as well as providing savvy guidance throughout the process. With having lived in Sherman Oaks, West Hollywood and Santa Monica he is well versed in the workings of the ever changing Southern California real estate market.

Specialties: Sellers, buyers, single family, condos, multi-family, 1031 exchanges, estates, probate/estate/trust sales
