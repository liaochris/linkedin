David Holden's Summary

I currently complete production related field research for a variety of manufacturers of agricultural nutrient and pest management products ( Acadian, Agraquest, Agrium, Amvac, Arysta Life Sciences, Bayer, Certis, Chemtura, DOW, DuPont, Floratine Bioscience, Global Organics, Lignotech, Marrone, Miller Chemical, Nichino, Simplot, Syngenta, TKI, UPI, and Valent) both in the traditional and organic farming areas, along with biopesticides. In addition to the above named companies I have also worked with the California Avocado Commission, California Celery Commission, California Pepper Commission and the University of California. Annually completing 80 to 100 various projects divided 50/50 between nutrient management and pest management. In addition I am consulting on approximately 10,000 acres of crops with growers of avocados, berries (strawberries and blueberries), citrus, and field grown vegetables (celery, lettuce, cole crops, etc).

Specialties

- Field testing of early stage (numbered compounds) development products.
- Field testing and proofing of market ready agricultural chemistry.
- Expertise in both conventional and organic production methods.
- Specific knowledge to the marketplace in coastal California along with remaining growing areas in California.
