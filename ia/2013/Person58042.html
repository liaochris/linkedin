Jamie Vollmer's Summary

A consummate communications professional with experience crafting and implementing strategic public relations and marketing plans for Fortune 100 companies, mid-sized business and regional non-profits. In addition to my expertise in the communications field, I am proficient in event management having played a vital role in special events ranging from grand openings to galas and international trade occasions with royalty to community festivals. Whether using PR, marketing or events, I am committed to finding new ways to inspire relationships, increase reputation and cultivate business for my clients.
