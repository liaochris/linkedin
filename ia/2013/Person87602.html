Martin Doubravský's Summary

Continually and relentlessly seeking new opportunities, growth, challenges, inspirations, experiences, goals, perfection, happiness and fun.

I'm ambitious & determined, passionate and keen on pushing any limits.

I'm Martin Doubravsky, mainly focusing on modern and future web technologies and methods (such as HTML5, CSS3, javascript, UX and cognitive science) and bringing them to the present.

I love creative thinking and acting as well as I enjoy fresh and innovative ideas.

I personally view web design as a space where I can find passion, fun and innovation. The fact that through web design I can push its limits and by creativeness and innovation I can contribute to the world, this drives me forward.

I love the whole life cycle of web projects, especially the frond-end part. I always have a great time creating one, it's such a complex story!

I have experienced variety of projects - small, huge, team-based, international and distributed. I've had clients all across the world (the U.S., Australia, Czech Republic etc.)
I also love working remotely and being part of a distributed team, it's a great opportunity to travel and meet new people!



I'm immersed in:

HTML5, CSS3 (LESS/SASS), UI, UX, JavaScript (jQuery, Ajax, JSON, REST web services, MVVM pattern), usability and accessibility.
Wirefrimes, prototyping, designing in the browser.
Semantics (microformats, microdata, RDFa).

Other words I know:
Django, PHP, Nette, Java, JSP, Spring, SQL, UML, XML, SEO, SVN/GIT, CodeKit, Balsamiq, Keynote, Fireworks, Spotify.
