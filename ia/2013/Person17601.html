Brian A. Montague's Summary

Attorney with many years of legal experience and over twenty years of military service, the former including over 27 years of trial and appellate work, risk mitigation service to businesses and individuals, drafting and negotiation of customer service agreements, business acquisition and sale agreements, due diligence plans, real estate instruments, settlement and release documents, pleadings of all types, and a wide array of other agreements for businesses and individuals, and felony-level prosecutorial experience, and the latter including representation of military commanders & Soldiers in deployed & domestic environments (with two active duty stints, the most recent in Northern Iraq). My civilian experience includes corporate and individual representation, litigation, administrative, and other representation of a local airport, a local cellular and CLEC service provider, trucking companies, homebuilders, insurance companies, and other businesses and individuals, service to a US Senator, appearances before State and Federal Courts in Mississippi and the Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals, testimony before the Mississippi State Senate regarding cellular tower legislation, researched opinion letters and briefs, work as an assistant district attorney, work on behalf of charitable activities (United Way of Southeast Mississippi, Boy Scouts of America, local homebuilders association, other), commercial trucking representation, corporate and transactional work, general litigation, local government law, residential and commercial construction issues, contract drafting, review, and negotiation, estate probate and administration. My twenty years of retired military experience includes, most recently, advice to Commanders and Soldiers on military justice (UCMJ) issues, the US - Iraq Security Agreement, Escalation of Force / Rules of Engagement issues, administrative law, government contract review and interpretation, and some dealings with Iraqi tribal leaders, and claims.

Specialties

General litigation, general business and corporate law, prosecutorial experience, transportation safety, insurance defense and coverage, local government law, administrative law
