Style Maker University

Style Maker University was created by Megan Kristel founder and CEO of Kristel Closets Inc., one of the leading image consulting agencies in the country, as a resource for new and established personal stylists to teach skills they need to run a sought after profitable practice. Known for her disarming nature and down to earth advice, Megan Kristel takes the often intimidating topic of image and personal branding and breaks it down into practical skills. With the same straight forward approach she uses with her clients, Megan shares her knowledge, tips and strategies that make her a successful stylist with participates of Style Maker University.

Style Maker University teaches both the aspiring and the established personal stylist and image consultant how to take their business from the start-up phase to the $100,000 per year revenue level and beyond.

Through workshops and one-on-one mentoring Megan guides you through the process of creating your own profitable practice that leaves you fulfilled and your clients delighted.
