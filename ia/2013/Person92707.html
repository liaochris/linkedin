Michael Mayhew's Summary

Hello. I got my start marketing insurance products for Combined Insurance in 1988. Shortly thereafter, 1989, I started my own General Agency. Today we are a National Independent Marketing Organization, (NMO, IMO), providing TOP Products & Commissions to IMO's FMO's, MGA's, GA's, and Independent Broker/Agents nationwide.
