Maartje Vissers' Summary

Vision in Finance offers legal and tax counseling for Dutch residents in Spain. It informs them about the consequences of their move to Spain with respect to the rules of inheritance, as the Spanish legislation is quite different from the Dutch. Vision in Finance assists its clients in achieving (tax-efficient) testamentary solutions.
