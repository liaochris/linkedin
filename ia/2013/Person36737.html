Donna Moulds' Summary

At Mantra Training & Development we provide quality learning outcomes. The integration of adult learning principles into the delivery of all relevant programs provides results.

Innovation, Flexibility & Support = Results for our clients.

Over 8 years experience in Vocational Education and Training has provided the opportunity to successfully complete a number of internal Audits with external organisations providing a greater understanding of the regulations in which RTO's must comply with.

Donna's philosophy around education is to provide opportunity for those who want to learn and re-educate themselves for a brighter future and financial stability.

Specialties: RTO Compliance and Management, personal and professional Development, Traineeships in Business, Financial Services, Hospitality and Information Technology, overcoming adversity and thinking creatively and strategically.
