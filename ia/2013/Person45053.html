Floor Wittink's Summary

My goal is to let people experience the magic of music. Music makes us tune in to the heart, to creativity, to the information that exists beyond our minds.
In what way am I working with this concept so that it can help you to tune in?

In my performances as a saxophonist I make a musical journey taking the audience with me. I perform on stage and in living rooms, making it a personal concert.

I share my experience of playing and tuning in to music by giving Tune In workshops. I use great tools to make everybody experience this wonderful feeling without having to play an instrument.

I teach the saxophone to adults and children, let them experience their own magic musical abilities.
Currently I'm writing a book sharing these experiences.

Specialties: As a saxophonist:
Classical music including contemporary classical music, novelty/vaudeville music, musictheatre, crossover.

Concepts:
Tune In Muziekcafe
Children Music Stories

As a workshopleader:
Tune In Workhops
Saxophone workshop for adult players focusing on how to study and perform and play together music in a creative way and how to optimize your playing on stage.
