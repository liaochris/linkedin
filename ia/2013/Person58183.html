Jane Gerster's Projects

  • Gemini News Service

    • January 2013 to March 2013
    Team Members: Jane Gerster, Laura Jakobschuk, Wilson Lo, Danielle Klassen

    Shepherd journalists in the developing world to help them write articles for the Gemini site. Gemini is unique in the sense it contains stories written by journalists in the developing for audiences in the developing world and beyond, unlike Western media outlets, which parachute correspondents in in times of interest only. Tasks included maintaining a line of communication with journalists to guide them through the process and editing their stories while learning more about them.

    Part of the JOUR 4101 - Special Topics course at Carleton University.
