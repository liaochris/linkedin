Linda Stokes' Summary

Founder and Managing Partner at Academy Physicians, LLC. a boutique Staffing and Recruiting Firm. As a physician recruiter, I like to focus on working with great physicians from all specialties, helping them locate their ideal practice opportunity. Academy Physicians works with some of the top Healthcare employers in the US.

Over the years I have been a business owner, president and vice president of several technology companies. During this time I earned extensive experience in recruiting top talent. With numerous personal ties to the medical field, I converted my recruiting business to focus solely on Physicians.

We are members of the National Coalition of Healthcare Recruiters and the National Association of Physician Recruiters, which gives us access to both physicians and job openings on an international scale. The relationships we build with our clients and our candidates are close and long lasting. Allow us to demonstrate our unique capabilities to you for your next Search.

I have recently joined Health Practice Advisors, a Health and Wellness company that works directly with physicians as a complement to their existing practices to offer non-pharmaceutical products listed in the PDR to treat Metabolic Syndrome, Hyperlipidemia, Diabetes and Overweight.

I volunteer as President of ReadWest, Inc. Board of Directors. ReadWest's mission is to provide quality one to one-to-one tutoring to adults seeking to transform their lives through enhanced literacy skills. I believe that helping adults learn how to read is one of the most important things a person can do to help others improve their lives.

Being half Cherokee, I enjoy exploring Indian tribes and culture. One of my clients is the Navajo Area Indian Health Service and I help them find physicians for the largest Indian Reservation in the United States.
