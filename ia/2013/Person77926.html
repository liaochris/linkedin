Lisa Erb's Summary

I am working as free artist, curator, writer, media-jockey and lecturer
working on the ontologic (topologic) issue of relation/ distinction/ form principle, ()*.


Mostly I am interested in the form of trajectory, self referential time evolving form of transgression and the cultural and political consequences and the necessity for a sustainable structure and -over all- in the shift and change that needs to happen. A new framework of diffractive entangled processed intersectionality firstly derives a new conceptual framework for all entangled agents (world inhabitants, objects,) and also derives a new basal calculus (re-presentation) for the intersection of time and space: development, motion parallel to time.


Understanding technology in it basic sense, apart from its applied technological hierarchy reproducing sense of local dominance but close to the understandiung of holistic entity feeling. People are locked in in the derivals from those power systems. There is change that needs to be done. Facing the future.

I have worked on structural applications of the identity of relation, trajectory, depth and more poetic derivates for many years. After 10 years of APPLIED ART, I started TRUE_ Investigation (following the investigations of performed living as research on occurences and structure itself), and also letting myself follow my research in philosophic, cultural, political levels of techno-scientific, cybernetic, quant physics, mathematic and system theoretic models.

Informed by feminist art, techno-scientifc approaches, alternative forms of structure, opposition as a form principle, I think culture and the duty of an individual as artist is to bring forward the understanding for the process it is maintaining and evoking.

sciences and the arts*will combine again. Let´s get meta!

Specialties: Implications of the New Era needs & visions

more on those investigations: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5BxlG_9lfBk
