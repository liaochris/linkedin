Dennis Pishev's Summary

I very much love what I do, and every year it becomes more exciting for me.

My fields of expertise include design of graphical user interfaces (GUI) for websites and web/desktop applications, responsive design (front-end, hand-coding), animation based on jQuery/CSS3/Flash/GIF, and corporate identity (especially logo design).

Since 2002 I have been designing graphics, creating animations, and dealing with front-end development. I have accumulated experience in design and animation for web, design for print, and hand-coding web pages.

My education includes a PE degree in Visual Communication (honours) and a diploma in Web Design (honours), as well as a certificate in Marketing and Advertising.

In addition to my areas of specialization, I also have experience in WPF, and work fluently with Microsoft Expression Blend/Design.
