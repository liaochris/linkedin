Craig Mungons' Summary

Turning great ideas into effective communication is something I strive for with every project.

I consider myself a creative advocate, using my experience, expertise and production creativity to help each project rise above the clutter and be as effective as possible. My honed organizational and communication skills are very relevant for the unique set of creative, strategic and budgetary considerations necessary for consistently successful projects.

I have a well-rounded understanding of corporate communication and media-related marketing through my extensive experience helping creative directors, marketing executives and a diverse roster of clients implement their strategies to effectively impact audiences with artistically designed messages. My organizational strength in the management of time, people and resources has been one of the benchmarks of my ability to consistently deliver successful projects on time and within budget without compromising the creative concept or marketing strategy.

I’m interested in applying my talent and expertise in the related fields of marketing, media, advertising, entertainment, film production and corporate communication. I look forward to discussing where our professional interests might overlap and the unique perspective I can provide for you.

Over the years I have enjoyed life with my wife and children on Walnut Acres, our 9-acre pre-Civil War farm just north of metro Detroit where we have raised free-range chickens, turkeys, cows, sheep, goats, ponies and organic vegetables.

LATE 2008: 7-spot campaign for the launch of the redesigned ‘09 Ford F-150 pick-up truck. Director is Adrien Brody, winner of the 2002 Best Actor Academy Award for The Pianist. Brody also provided the voice-over. “Its time, once again, to arrive in style.”

MID 2012: Very dynamic :90 video for the introduction of the reengineered Ram truck. We shot this video in the Chrysler Tech Center and Design Center at their World Headquarters in Michigan. This may sound pretty basic, but it's far from it. This video is as much about heart, desire, conviction and fight as it is about horsepower, torque, gear ratios and volumetric efficiency.

LATE 2012: Global spot for Chevrolet Trailblazer through Commonwealth, a joint venture between Goodby and McCann on behalf of Chevrolet. Stunning locations near Dubai and in Oman. Completed multiple versions including visual effects in London for four global regions. Very cinematic in scope with genuine moments and big adventures – everyday people, but not everyday experiences.
