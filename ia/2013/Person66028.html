John Apfelbaum's Summary

John Apfelbaum attended the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, where he graduated magna cum laude in 1975 with a Bachelor of Arts in History. He began work as a professional philatelist, buying and selling stamps for collectors in 1971. With both an interest and a strong academic background, Mr. Apfelbaum brings considerable knowledge to his work. He has held a variety of positions at Earl P.L. Apfelbaum, Inc. in Jenkintown, Pennsylvania. Currently, John Apfelbaum serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of the company.

John Apfelbaum first became a Licensed Auctioneer with the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania's Board of Auctioneer Examiners in August 1976. His license was most recently renewed in February 2011. For more than 80 years, Earl P.L. Apfelbaum, Inc. has held regularly scheduled auctions of both rare and more common stamps for collectors. With a truly wide range of stamps available, Earl P.L. Apfelbaum, Inc. provides an excellent variety to choose from for collectors at any level. Apfelbaum, Inc. accepts bids through a variety of methods, including fax, phone, e-mail, U.S. Mail, in person, and via live Internet bidding. Working with the Stamp Auction Network, John Apfelbaum has the firm broadcast public auctions live over the Internet, allowing interested buyers to listen to the auction as it progresses and participate in the auction. Bidders must be pre-approved and verified by the Stamp Auction Network.

A published author, John Apfelbaum also maintains an active blog at www.johnapfelbaum.blogspot.com. He regularly covers topics surrounding stamp artistry, history, manufacturing and collecting. Mr. Apfelbaum's book, The World of Stamps & Stamp Collecting, serves as a detailed, full-featured guide to the world of stamp collecting, sometimes known as the Hobby of Kings.
