Lorene Schaefer, Esq.'s Summary

Lorene Schaefer, Esq., is the managing partner of Win-Win Resolve, a boutique law firm focused on helping employers and corporate counsel solve workplace conflict with internal dispute resolution programs, impartial workplace investigations and mediation. She is also the co-founder and president of Workplace Investigations Group and Workplace Mediations Group, two national directories of employment attorneys with 10+ years of experience who work as impartial workplace investigators or mediators.

Prior to beginning her workplace neutral focus, Lorene practiced law for more than 20 years, including 14 years as in-house counsel at the General Electric Company where she was General Counsel of GE Transportation. Lorene blogs regularly at www.WinWinHR.com and is a trusted media resource providing a balanced perspective on workplace issues to various media outlets, including The Wall Street Journal, Corporate Counsel, Inside Counsel and Business Insurance.

In 2013, Lorene was 1 of 7 attorneys invited to testify before the EEOC. Her written testimony urged the EEOC to implement a pilot program incorporating a structured ADR process into the EEOC's post-cause conciliation process and to modify its investigation process for improved quality.

Lorene enjoys an excellent reputation with both the plaintiff and defense bars. At GE, she was considered a “thoughtful leader” who “works hard to find ‘win-win’ solutions” and “stands up for what is right and fair.” David Sanford, a partner at the national plaintiff’s firm of Sanford and Heisler LLP, describes Lorene as an “incredibly talented, conscientious and driven attorney. Her attention to detail and relentless pursuit of justice, coupled with her dual perspective as both a former in-house counsel for GE and a plaintiff, make her ideally suited as a mediator. I would highly recommend to both sides of the bar.”
