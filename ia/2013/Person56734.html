Emergency Medical Technician / Emergency Dispatcher / Recruitment Chair

Fair Harbor Fire Department

November 2011 – Present (2 years 2 months) Fair Harbor, New York

The Fair Harbor Fire Department (FHFD) is a year-round volunteer Fire Department, which serves the Fair Harbor and Dunewood Fire Districts (which include three communities), on Fire Island, NY. FHFD provides both fire supression and emergency medical services.

I joined the Fire Department in December 2011. Since joining, I've been certified as a New York State EMT-Basic, and work as both an EMT and a dispatcher within the department.

Additionally, I am the chair of the recruitment committee, responsible for the recruitment of new members and for helping them with any questions they may have about the process. I also maintain the web services for the department, which help keep track of data vital to smooth operations.
