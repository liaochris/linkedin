Al Lee's Summary

My key area of expertise is in performance improvement for both individuals and organizations. I have decades of experience in business, from the mail room to the board room, with companies ranging from high octane start-ups to the Fortune 500, and in markets as diverse as aerospace, high tech, entertainment, and non-profits.

I specialize in maximizing "Return on Energy". Showing individuals and teams how create more energy and use it more effectively using principles and techniques based on cutting-edge science and the latest human performance research. I am the co-author of “Perfect Breathing: Transform Your Life One Breath at a Time” (currently in international distribution in four languages) and am a recognized authority in personal performance improvement, wellness and stress management.

I love sharing my experience with audiences (recent past-president of the National Speaker’s Association - Oregon), and offer programs that are designed motivate, entertain, enlighten, and most importantly - to deliver bottom-line results.

Specialties: Decades of experience as high-tech engineering manager, executive, coach, and consultant taught me the importance of the critical life skills, especially energy management, stress management, and emotional intelligence which are essential attributes of successful leaders and individual contributors.

Al Lee's Publications

  • Perfect Breathing: Transform Your Life One Breath at a Time

    • Sterling Publishing NYC
    • January 6, 2009
    Authors: Al Lee, Don Campbell

    Perfect Breathing shows you how to start taking advantage of your single most powerful source of energy - your breath. Although it is often overlooked or taken for granted it is the common denominator in everything that you do and touches every dimension of your life - mind, body, emotions, and spirit. Using the latest cutting-edge science, expert interviews, and decades of personal experience, Lee and Campbell show you why the world's top performers - astronauts, fighter pilots, Olympic athletes, performing artists of all kinds, doctors and healers from the east and west, as well as spiritual seekers from all traditions use this amazing resource to elicit the most from their minds and bodies - and how you can too.

  • Perfect Breathing 28 Day Workbook

    • Perfect Breathing
    • May 12, 2011
    Authors: Al Lee, Don Campbell

    Developing the habit of breath awareness and its companion, conscious breathing, will impact every dimension of your life. You will have access to a new level of energy and potential. You will find that you have more control over your health and ability to heal, your mental, physical and creative performance, as well as your emotional and spiritual well-being.

    By using this workbook as a guide for the next 28 days, you will take a big step toward making breath awareness and conscious breathing an ingrained habit that will become your secret weapon in the moments when you need it the most - when your health is challenged, when you need to deliver your best performance, when difficult emotions have knocked you off balance.
