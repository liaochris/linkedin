For these events an ISP grade network has to be build in a short time span, usually in less than 5 days. This done with sponsored hardware from several major vendors like Juniper (MX/EX), Brocade (MLX), HP (Procurve), and Cisco (WLC, WCS, Prime). The last few years I’ve been focussing on building the wireless network on these events. Wireless usage has become more dominant over the last few years, with over 3000 concurrent users at the CCC congress in 2012.

For OHM2013 I will be coordinating the network team.
