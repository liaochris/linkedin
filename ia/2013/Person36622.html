Don Demrow's Summary

My greatest joy is being able to contribute to the success of a company that understands the value of technical competence, near-obsessive attention to detail, and passion for excellence.

I design and develop aesthetically pleasing, robust, multi-tiered (N-Tier) online websites and applications with database-driven backends.

My primary goal is to provide design that is beyond excellent, and to develop code that is lean, and perfect in its execution, which saves money, time, and aggravation. If an interface is a joy to use, users will come back!

I have created designs, and developed for companies such as Citi, Microsoft, RTL television network, and various federal, state, county, and municipal government entities. I have created many websites and online applications for businesses of all sizes such as law firms, dental / medical practices and institutions, and many non-profit agencies.

I am not currently seeking employment, and I have just been hired by an exemplary company with extraordinary vision, that is a leader in their industry. I am very excited about the prospect of bringing my talent to a company that understands that doing it right the first time is less costly than doing it "good enough" with the attitude of "we'll go back later and fix whatever doesn't work."

This company is New York Life.

New York Life has empowered employees that are talented, but humble, who love collaboration - and who aren't afraid to admit if they don't know something. New York Life is a company where learning is encouraged, and where everyone can share their particular individual expertise, working toward the common goal of accomplishing something far beyond the ordinary.

That's what makes New York Life my dream company.

I am thrilled to be able to be part of New York Life!
