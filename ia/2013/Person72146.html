Karen Lyu's Summary

HOLISTIC VOICE COACH
Voice lessons and classes for people who think they are "hopeless cases"! Karen's holistic, personalized and playful voice coaching has improved the voices of hundreds of students - from absolute beginners to untrained professional singers, speakers, life coaches, and actors.

Karen has a BSS in Jazz Studies - Vocal Performance, studied with noted voice instructors such as Roberta Davis (Berklee School of Music) and Mira Kehoe (Guthrie Theater), and has sung for Dr. Oren Brown (Juillard School).

Since 1993, Karen has sung with Fresh Jazz, Global Jazz, Devon Evans (Bob Marley), Soli Hughes (Sounds of Blackness), Curly Martin (Etta James), Karen Lyu Quartet, Bomba, Via Brasil, Eddie Piccard Trio, Robert Robinson's MCC Gospel Choir (Peabo Bryson), vocal jazz group Jazz Transit, and as a solo vocalist. Her voice has been on NPR, MPR, Democracy Now, film, TV, radio and cable, and she won a 2007 MN State Arts Board theater collaboration grant with Maryam Yusefzadeh.

Karen is a voice expert for eHow.com, EzineArticles.com, TheModernVocalist.com, Voicematters.net, and TRCB.com.
