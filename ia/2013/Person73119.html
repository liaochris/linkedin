Katie (Qiong) Dailey's Summary

People call me "accidental entrepreneur" and I think it is very true. I am a China native, grew up in Shanghai China. I love China and I love travel. This makes it perfect for me to consulting people and helping people solve travel issues and cultural issues that they face today on vacation or in their dealing with the Chinese. I am your China resources from education to services.
