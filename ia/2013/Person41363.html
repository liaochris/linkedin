Emma Channing's Summary

I am a high energy, skilled, international professional, with many years of experience as a proactive and strategic business advisor to executive management and directors of publicly-traded and privately held companies, as well as a variety of funds. I am adept at providing rapid, practical solutions to complex business and legal issues, and I have the ability to negotiate effectively across a wide range of issues and practice areas, with a focus on keeping as much value on the table as possible. I am disciplined and confident in my ability to manage multiple projects efficiently across a variety of project time frames, and ensure that deadlines and transaction targets are met regardless of the obstacles or pressure. I have a broad spread of US securities, general corporate, global compliance and commercial experience with a client focus on technology companies, telecoms, renewable and conventional energy.

I have many years of experience conducting cross-border transactions across multiple jurisdictions, and I have specialized experience in most major European, Asian and South American jurisdictions including the BRICs - Brazil, Russia, India and China, and in particular experience of guiding US based clients through transactions involving these jurisdictions. I am admitted in California, New York, Colorado and England & Wales.

I am also the founder of the LLM Network www.llmnetwork.org, an online community that connects LLM graduates from the most prestigious US law schools, and captures an existing international network of professionals to share recommendations, experiences and to facilitate referrals.
