Kaspar Knikkink's Summary

I am a versatile IT specialist and I am experienced in many disciplines in the field of information and communication technology. My core competencies are the analysis, design and development of automated systems for various platforms with the user in mind.

Due to my analytical mindset I am able to make a thorough analysis of a problem and provide a conceptual solution within a short time-frame. Because I understand people as well as ICT I have the ability to provide clear, jargon free business level presentations of complex issues.

These characteristics also make me a strong lead developer, technical team leader and IT architect. My current focus is on projects in the field of Computer Telephony Integration (CTI) and Unified Communications (UC).
