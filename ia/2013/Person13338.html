J.L. Reid's Summary

Telling stories is one of our oldest forms of entertainment. The allure of a good yarn is so intertwined with our cultural DNA that we're almost compelled to react to, and share, the stories that engage us.

Stories are ubiquitous. Companies that successfully present their products and services through compelling stories, whether true-to-life or fictional, build compelling brands.

Compelling Stories = Compelling Companies.

Today, technology allows us to tell engaging stories in new ways. Creating stories that beg sharing is the purpose of Viral Narrative.
