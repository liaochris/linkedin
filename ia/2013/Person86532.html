Will Royall's Summary

Royall is a full service advertising agency and marketing firm with offices in both New York, NY and Orlando, FL. We market businesses' products and services to their target audience through a variety of mediums connecting people with brands. Clients include Hilton Grand Vacations Company, Holiday Inn Club Vacations, Wyndham Vacation Ownership, Darden Restaurants, Ruth's Chris Steak House, Mitchell's Fish Market, Mitchell's Steak House, Vines Grille, Ci Ci's Pizza, The Compass Group (Caffé Ritazza, Eurest, Profiles 3), Sysco, The Big Break IV, The Perfect Club (Peter Kessler), Stetson University, Caribbean Hotel Association, Fiserv, SunGard, FEMA, SSGA Global, and the Rockefeller Center.
