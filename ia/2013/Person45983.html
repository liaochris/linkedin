Frederick Goodall's Summary

I am a writer, speaker, and fatherhood advocate. I am passionate about building strong families and creating content and resources to help families thrive.


Through my company, Goodall Family Media, I offer a full suite of products including books, screenplays, video production, photography, web design, social media management, blogging, writing services, and communications training/consulting.

I am a skilled online marketer, consultant, and social media expert. In 2013, I ranked #12 on the list of Top 100 Social Media Power Influencers in Houston, TX. I’ve worked with several companies to improve their online presence and craft messages that will resonate with their target audiences. Some of my major clients include Kellogg's, Dove Men + Care, Toyota, Lexus, Ford, eBay, General Mills, Puritan's Pride, Dr. Pepper/Snapple, Pepperidge Farms, ConAgra, Disney, Chevy, Sony, HP, Adobe, Sears, Samsung, Walmart, AT&T, and Dockers, General Mills, Tide/Downy, Excedrin, VSP, Hershey's, Universal Studios, SeaWorld, and Wells Fargo.

Goodall Family Media publishes two flagship websites - Mocha Dad & Mocha Man Style. Through Mocha Dad, I share family-related stories and create content to help men become better dads, husbands, and community servants.

Mocha Man Style is a lifestyle site for sophisticated men. Our vision is to help men to be the best versions of themselves.

I have also been featured is several media outlets such as NPR, CNN, Black Enterprise, Ebony, HuffPost Live, Great Day Houston, Newsday, Sprout Social, and several radio shows across the country. I’m a regular guest on the local public radio show – Houston Matters.

My writing has appeared in The Houston Chronicle, Essence, Babble, SheKnows, Mamiverse, Voxxi, Your Teen Mag, Folio Weekly, and several others.

I have nearly 20 years of management experience at a Fortune 500 company in the areas of project management, technical writing, project controls, professional safety management, and quality assurance.
