School Governor, Chair of Governors as of Sept 2012

Fairway Primary School and Children's Centre

October 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months) Mill Hill, London

I have been serving as a governor at the Fairway Primary School and Children's Centre since October 2010, with a particular focus on Curriculum, Literacy and Music. Elected to Chair the Governing Body in September 2012, further responsibilities now include school budget and finances, Headteacher performance and effective management of the School's governing body.

Director

Areas of involvement include: Membership recruitment and retention, events planning and coordination, representation of the APRS on the Trade Association Forum, the Sponsors Alliance and the National Music Council; trade show presence, editorial contributions, etc.
