Josh Hinman's Summary

I am a skilled developer with 10 years of experience. My specialties are native iOS applications; back-end development using ASP.Net and C#; and beautifully hand-crafted HTML, CSS and Javascript.

My code is my poetry. It is indented with spaces so everything lines up nicely. It is commented just the right amount. And it is immediately understandable and maintainable by any developer who comes across it.

An app is nothing but a collection of details, and I pay attention to those details. It's not "how do I implement this feature?" Rather, it's "how *should* I implement this feature?" I understand that engineering is a constant balancing act between a list of competing goals, and code isn't written in a vacuum. I excel in making the right trade-offs.

When I'm not working, I am piloting small, single-engine airplanes, riding my bike or snowboarding in Mammoth, Whistler or Utah.
