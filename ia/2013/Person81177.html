Lindsay Ifill's Summary

As an agile product manager (scrum product owner), I collaborate with the team and stakeholders to guide the product vision, manage the backlog, and provide any answers or support the team needs to be successful. My communication skills along with a passion for digital media and software led me to this role, but it wasn't until I worked with an agile team that I fell in love with what I do.

My latest obsessions: Lean Startup, Lean UX, and finding new ways and new software that helps my team validate ideas before building features. If you have any recommendations or stories to share, I'd love to hear them.

A storyteller at heart with a background in journalism, I satisfy my writing and editing itch by writing a monthly tech column for Flying Kite Media, blogging, and crafting user stories and persona documents. I'm also a punctuation and grammar fanatic, going so far as to secretly copyedit test criteria.

Specialties: agile product management, online writing and content management, social media marketing, website management, CSPO, scrum, copy editing and proofreading
