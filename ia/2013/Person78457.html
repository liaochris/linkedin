Larry Lieb, ACE, CCA's Summary

Larry Lieb is a nationally known subject matter expert in the field of electronic discovery.

Larry has testified and is on the Federal record in Michigan on the subject of electronic discovery costs and best practices.

Larry’s practice focuses on maximizing the limited dollars his clients have available for substantive legal work whilst minimizing wasted expense.

Expert Work:

● Custodial Interviews / Evidence Map Definition
● Domestic and International Forensic Collection of Evidence
● Theft of Trade Secrets
● Electronic Discovery Stipulations
● Expert Opinion on Electronic Discovery Methods and Costs

Larry is committed to providing his expert work on Pro Bono matters as well as corporate matters.
