Kathy Deal's Summary

I love to help people! There is nothing better to do in life but to serve others. I started my life out waiting tables as a teenager and 30 years later I am still serving others. In 1999 I was approached by a client to get my real estate license and come run his sales office in Fawn Lake. After many discussions about the career change I took that step out in faith and I have never looked back. I started my real estate career working for NTS Development Company as the Sales Manager for Fawn Lake Real Estate and VP of Sales for the Developer selling their lots to folks who wanted to buy now, and build later. Five years later as the market was changing it was time to move from working exclusively in Fawn Lake to working for clients buying and selling real estate in the entire region. Since leaving Fawn Lake I have not only built my business but have built an amazing team to surround me. Many years later I was blessed with the opportunity to have my wonderful husband Scott join me by getting his real estate license and being trained by the best. With The Deal Team, we consider every client a friend and look out for them as family. We are not in the business of transactions but we are in the business of relationships. We look forward to the day we get to serve you and your family.

Specialties: Foreclosures, Short Sales, New Construction, Land, Residential sales, Military Relocation.
