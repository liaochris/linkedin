Maria Joyner's Summary

I'm a photographer turned entrepreneur with a passion for growth hacking, problem solving, and cutting edge technology. I have been quoted in the AJC, Examiner, Buzzfeed, Saporta Report, Under30CEO and more on topics including mobile apps, startups, baseball, and entrepreneurship.

After spending the past 3 years consulting for Atlanta technology startups in the social media space, I have joined Synapp.io as a full time growth hacker. As the 4th team member, I am excited about the opportunity to influence and scale a high growth technology startup. Synapp.io is part of 500 Startups, Flashpoint Accelerator, and ATDC Selects.

In my spare time, I am a startup community catalyst, an exhibiting photographer, and baseball fanatic. I reside on the board of directors of Atlanta Photography Group and my photography is in a permanent collection in Lacoste, France.

If you are interested in getting connected to the Atlanta startup community, working with Synapp.io, talking baseball, or discussing email deliverability and compliance trends, let's connect.
