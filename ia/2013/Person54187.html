Harry Zernike's Summary

I am an editorial and commercial portrait photographer. I work with clients ranging from Pentagram Design to Bicycling magazine. My style is intimate, vivid and responsive.

I endeavor to make arresting, bold images, both for clients and in my personal work. In a broader sense, I am interested in photographs as a record of our history and culture, our ideas about who we are and how we live.
I am based in New York City, and have worked on assignments around the US and internationally. I'm interested in having satisfied, repeat clients.

Specialties: I have a strong directorial ability when working with people in front of the camera. I work with state of the art equipment, and with brilliant crew.
I also have strong background in managing creative projects. I enjoy collaborating with clients, and relish every aspect of my work, from pre-production to publication.
