Dani Loebs' Summary

Welcome! My name is Dani Loebs and my passions are community-building and path finding for businesses on the web. Each morning I wake up excited to make new discoveries and connections and I find that my desire for creative collaboration is insatiable. I fully embrace the hacker culture of innovation and improvisation in order to tackle challenges. Disruption creates opportunity!

Technologies in our world are evolving at an exponential rate, and I thrive on anticipating how to adapt my business and marketing strategies before the each new wave of change hits. These same technologies have also enabled us to become closer, collaborate more, and create incredible communities in ways that have never been possible before. The industry is tumultuous and exciting while also surprisingly generous and kind. I love it.

Currently I am thrilled to begin applying my skills to Kovars Satori Academy of Martial Arts, an organization whose outstanding instructors create a better community by teaching life skills and good citizenship to all ages. It’s incredible to devote time to the causes I believe in, and I can’t wait to begin training and learning from them.

In my free time, I also enjoy Improv Comedy. I’m a member and performer at Blacktop Comedy in Roseville, CA. The skills I’ve learned from improv have proven invaluable to my professional life. I highly recommend dropping in at a workshop at your nearest comedy club if you are so inclined.

Please feel free to connect with me for any reason at all! I am an open networker on LinkedIn and have never met a connection who wasn’t valuable.

There are as many job descriptions for a social media specialist as there are social media specialists! Here is the best way I can think of to describe what I do every day:

•PROMOTION/OUTREACH: In addition to writing and optimizing social media posts, I identify bloggers and other influencers on the social web to help promote brands and initiatives. I find content opportunities in memes, trends and current events and work to position brands to be as relevant and cutting-edge to their target audiences as possible.
