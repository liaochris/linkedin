Freelance Writer and Marketing Consultant

Reading Intervention Specialist

Burbank Unified School District

Education Management industry

2008 – 2010 (2 years)

Working along with administrators, I developed a comprehensive intervention program that served hundreds of low-performing children. I wrote a 40-page research-based intervention program that highlighted national best practices in reading intervention, along with quarterly reading development benchmarks for children in grades K-5. The plan also implemented all aspects of IDEA 2004 with the goal of ending the "wait to fail" model of special education qualification in our schools. In addition, I worked in small groups or one-on-one with students in kindergarten, fourth and fifth grades on reading intervention and kindergarten readiness using strategies specifically tailored to each child's assessed needs. Duties also included working with parents and administrators to make tough decisions about students and their educational careers, attending student study team and IEP meetings and much more.

Reading and Math Intervention Specialist

Monrovia Unified School District

2005 – 2007 (2 years)

Implemented specialized intervention program that served more than 100 students per year in need-based groups based upon assessed result. Working with an aid, school administrators, special education specialists and classroom teachers, I developed individualized plans for students and grouped children based on needs, not grade level. Using IDEA 2004 and innovative forms of reading and math intervention, I fought for students to receive the services they needed, including special day, RSP and speech. I also worked provided parents with documentation and reports to help with diagnosis of disabilities.
