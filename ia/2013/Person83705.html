Lynn Anne Miller's Summary

I run 4GreenPs™, a boutique consulting firm focused on strategy, execution and results.
Check out our many CLIENT references here on LinkedIn and at 4greenps.com.
Results include:
o Tripling an energy-efficiency device manufacturer’s web traffic and growing sales via a social media campaign;
o Building a new food brand’s Facebook engagement rate to 5.3% in 1 month; growing to 9% and 3,500 likes;
o Growing a national non-profit’s targeted media coverage by 800% and message pick-up by 257%;
o Increasing donations to a healthcare non-profit by 71% during the Great Recession;
o Re-branding and relaunching a venture backed software business within 60 days after its spin-out from a diversified multinational corporation;
o Recommending new growth paths after completion of strategic marketing assessments.

From 2007 to 2011, I blogged at OrganicMania ™ and founded the Green Moms Carnival. I was named one of the top 50 "Mom bloggers" by Nielsen Media Research and @GreenMoms won the 1st Shorty Award for Best Green Content on Twitter and was named one of the 10 Most Socially Influential Moms Groups by Groupable.

From 1996 - 2006, I worked in the embedded software sector for Telogy Networks and its acquirer, Texas Instruments. I led a team that leveraged innovative SEO and PR tactics to position this early stage firm as the leader in its market niche, contributing to its $750M acquisition by TI.

Earlier wireless technology marketing roles included international business development and sales roles where I closed more than $19M of business with Fortune 500 firms.

I'm a HOYA with an MBA from Georgetown, an MA (Maitrise) in Political Science from the University of Paris I -Sorbonne, and a BA from Lehigh University. I also attended the University of Bonn, Germany.

Most proud of receiving Lehigh University’s “Alumni Award,” the university’s highest honorary alumni award and paying Lehigh back for all the scholarship money it invested in me.
