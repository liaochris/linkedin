Linda Abbit's Summary

Words matter!

I help companies and entrepreneurs tell the story of who they are, what they value, and how they can best help their prospects and customers.

My expertise is getting your company’s unique message across clearly to your target market through my writing. This leads to your connecting with more potential clients, increasing your industry-wide reputation, building stronger customer relationships, and the growth of your business.

I am a freelance writer who works with entrepreneurs, businesses and organizations, both online and in the real world. My writing specialty is eldercare, but other topics include business, baby boomers, technology, women's issues, social media, real estate, and health and wellness.
