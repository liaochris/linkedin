Robert S. Meyring's Summary

Robert S. Meyring, Attorney helps all people and families with wills, trusts, probate, estate litigation with his dedicated Trusts & Estates Law Firm. A lawyer for Estate Planning, drafting of simple wills and trusts, to complex structures, incorporations, contracts, partnerships to help family navigate incapacity and life transitions, probate of estates and transfer of estate property, setting up probate trusts and estate accounts, advising trustees and executors, filing of probate petitions, estate claims and trusts disputes in probate court, protecting family assets, preserving legacy, beneficiary designations, complex trusts.

Founder and owner of several current/past businesses Robert starts business for clients, supports with legal advice contracts & litigation.

Robert is a longstanding member of the Probate & Estate Planning section of the Atlanta Bar, Elder Law section and Small Firm section, a member of the State Bar of Georgia, the Metro Atlanta Chamber of Commerce, the Cobb Chamber, Vinings Business Association and volunteer to the Fulton County Probate Court's Probate Information Center and pro bono clients.

Meyring now offers Legal Plans to give clients attorney access, estate planning services, and timely legal support through a Legal Plan provided directly by the Law Firm. Meyring Legal Plans give member clients peace of mind with reasonable monthly fee, direct attorney access and full estate planning with Last Will and Testament, Powers of Attorney and Health Care Advance Directives.

Robert's law practice, established 2007, is a Law Firm with several of counsel attorneys and lawyers that associate with Firm on specialty cases like DUI, accident injury, personal injury, bankruptcy, divorce, real estate and a range of civil litigation and business cases.

Meyring's Law Firm office is located in Northwest Atlanta on Paces Ferry Road, in the center of Vinings Village just 200 feet east of the railroad tracks, at the start of New Paces Ferry Road.
