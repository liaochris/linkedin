Brent Holliday's Summary

Over five years of M&A advisory services and capital raising for technology companies in Western Canada. Since early 2013, founded a cross-Canada, technology focused M&A and capital raising advisory firm, Garibaldi Capital Advisors.

Recent technology transactions:
BroadbandTV $36m private placement from RTL Group in Europe
BuildDirect $21m capital raise from OMERS Ventures, others
Summify Sale to Twitter (angel investor, advised founders)
MetroLeap (MetroLyrics) Sale to CBS Interactive (advised founders)
Point2 Technologies Sale to Yardi Systems
Cityfone Sale to Rogers
Vivonet Inc $8.5m Capital Raise from Maxam Opportunities Fund
Delta-Q Technologies $17m Capital Raise from Tandem Expansion Fund

Twelve years of early stage venture capital investing for The Western Technology Seed Investment Fund and Greenstone Venture Partners, both in Vancouver, BC.

Invested in over 20 information technology companies.
Three years of start-up software management prior to joining the venture industry.

Column Writer (Tech Talk) for BC Business Magazine

Specialties: Vancouver and Western Canada technology companies, capital raising, broad understanding of technology markets, mergers & acquisitions
