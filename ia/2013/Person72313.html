Karin Høgh's Summary

Karin Høgh is the go-to-person in Denmark about podcasting and/or recording, editing and publishing of audio for independent or corporate clients.

She has 20+ years experience mainly with radio and web, editing and writing.

Specialty: Podcasting - courses, consulting and presentations about podcasting, recording, editing and publishing of podcasts

Podcasts? Audio/video programmes you can subscribe to - for free - and have delivered automatically to your computer. Or you can produce your own podcasts.

Karin Hoegh has a sound studio in Copenhagen - coSounds - you can rent for your own audio courses, audio book recording, webinars, interviews and more. Go to http://coSounds.dk for more information.

Karin Høgh is a reseller of beginners audio- and video equipment as well as accessories and external microphones for iPhones, iPads and other devices. You can expect a high level of advice when consulting Karin Hoegh befor buying these items.

Specialties: Podcasting, journalism, new media, sound editing, recording, conferences, poken, networking, publishing, new media, mobile podcasting, blogging, Podcasting in Education, corporate podcasting.
