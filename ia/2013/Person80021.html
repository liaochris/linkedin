Lena Barnett's Summary

Lena’s grandfather suddenly died when her father was just 7 years old. With no will in place, his estate was ravaged and the family struggled through some very rough years. Lena knows what can happen without planning! She wants to help her clients and client families avoid what her father, her aunt & her grandmother experienced.

ATTORNEY & COUNSELLOR at LAW: Estate Plans With Care: Lena's clients involve her when planning for the two most important things in their lives: Everything they own & everyone they love!

Estate & wealth strategies plans enable clients to focus on what they want as they determine what happens to them, their loved ones & their assets while healthy, upon disability & after their deaths while saving unnecessary expenses, taxes & stress. Estate and wealth strategies planning enable clients to leave wonderful & lasting legacies.

AV Preeminent 5.0 Peer Review Rated, Martindale Hubbell

As you will see below, Lena collaborates in a number of different ways. She collaborates with planning professionals in the estate and wealth strategies process and she practices collaborative law.

AUTHOR & SPEAKER: Reviewer or Contributing Reviewer for 6 books published by the American Bar Association.

Lena has conducted seminars for numerous organizations including AARP, Gillette, HP, NASA HQ, Nordstrom, Retired Officer's Association, MD Council, Women Presidents' Organization, National Network of Estate Planning Attorneys, Capitas Financial, Mutual of Omaha, Northwestern Mutual, Prudential & Watkins, Meegan, Drury & Co.

Regular guest on WUSA-TV 9's Mind Over Money, guest MCTV, WPGC AM 1580, WBZS 730 AM, WMAL News Talk 630 AM & co-host of The Asset Masters on WBZS AM 570.

ESTATE PLANS WITH CARE: Our clients involve us when planning for the two most important things in their lives: Everything they own and everyone they love. Lena Barnett & Associates, L.L.C. helps clients structure and implement estate, business, and charitable plans that meet their specific needs, goals, and dreams. We emphasize personalized, comprehensive, timely, and proactive estate and wealth strategies planning.

Attorney Lena Barnett also practices collaborative law which is a confidential, voluntary process which allows parties & their attorneys to resolve disputes without court intervention. Participants in the process have found they are able to preserve important business & personal relationships as well as significantly reduce the amounts of time, stress, and expense normally associated with litigation. All of this is consistent with what Lena does with estate and wealth strategies planning. Throughout the United States, and around the world, the collaborative process has been widely used in family law and is being used ever increasingly in other civil matters including, but certainly not limited to, probate, estates, and business. Lena has satisfied the requirements of the International Academy of Collaborative Professionals for practice as a collaborative lawyer.

We collaborate in a number of different ways. We collaborate with planning professionals in the estate and wealth strategies process and we practice collaborative law.

The title "Attorney & Counsellor at Law" reflects the "old fashioned values" of the trusted advisor and counsellor while utilizing the latest legal and tax developments, select professional relationships & technology to bring the best possible counsel & service to our clients and client families.

We enjoy working with clients and client families who appreciate the care we put into each case. www.lenabarnett.com.
