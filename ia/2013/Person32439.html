David Qualls' Summary

RMD International Inc. d.b.a. RMD Mobile HD is a Rocky Mountain based HD television production and distribution company. Unlike traditional production companies who commonly rent expensive trucks or equipment from 3rd party providers, RMD owns its own mobile unit and lightweight production system bringing a cost effective and high quality live solution to all types of sports, music and corporate events for cable television and high quality streaming. The company currently provides regular live production, streaming and online channel services to sports including professional equestrian and tennis amongst others and has managed US television distribution of major world events including Beijing Paralympic Games, Swimming World Championships, Track and Field World Championships, Gymnastics World Championships and Ski World Championships. RMD will travel anywhere allowing these cost efficiencies to be realized world-wide wherever an event or production is taking place. RMD in-house producers and engineers have broad knowledge and experience in the world of live and delayed (VOD) television, web and mobile production. An outside director will be perfectly comfortable with the support from professionals steeped in many years of production experience.

We have produced live and delayed events for Comcast SportsNet, The Tennis Channel, CBS Sports Network, Fight Network and NBC Sports.
