Michael Gamboa's Summary

Growing up I knew I always wanted to create something big. My Grandfather was an entrepreneur in the steel fabrication industry which created jobs, delivered a quality product and service, and allowed him to pursue his goals and provide for his family. The business is still family owned and operated 51 years later.

That is big. Listening to my Grandfather's stories and replaying them years later reminds me real success takes time, passion and a consistent effort to hone your craft.

My passion is Marketing and I wake up excited to learn something new everyday.
