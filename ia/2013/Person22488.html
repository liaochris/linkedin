Daniel Chong-Jimenez's Summary

Extensive culinary and hospitality management experience, with a specialty focus on venue-specific, contemporary and spa cuisine model development, and brand creation, development and deployment. Establishment of venue and station-specific standards and procedures, including handbooks and training protocol, daily tracking and service metrics. Development of culinary nutrition education programming for healthy lifestyles, foodservice consulting, public speaking and public relations.

Recipient of multiple culinary critic accolades, including top-rated establishment from Zagat, and "Best of Connecticut Spa" for eight consecutive years, up to the present, from Connecticut Magazine, as well as from regional newspapers and magazines.

Coauthor of Prescription for Healthy Weight Loss and Optimum Health, 2006
