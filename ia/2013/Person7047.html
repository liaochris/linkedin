Andrew Long's Summary

I am a social media specialist for my own company Know Social Media and former Academic at the University of Otago. I have a long standing interest in social media and a passion for empowering people through thoughtful technological choices. Through my work at the University (teaching and researching social media), interactions with local businesses (workshops and consultations), talking with media (The Listener, TV3) and feeding my daily social media obsession (participating, reading, blogging, doing, ...), I have developed the necessary skillset to provide high-quality, professional services.
