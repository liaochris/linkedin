Connie Miller's Summary

With a background in negotiating, developing successful marketing plans and competitive sports, I help people buy and sell luxury homes and investment property in Silicon Valley and the south Peninsula. I am passionate about real estate as a haven for people in this fast-paced Valley and as an investment vehicle where people can accumulate wealth. I believe in a direct link between the quality of life and real property ownership. My driven, goal-oriented approach to life fuels the results I achieve for my clients. In addition to real estate, my other passions are my awesome daughter and competing in the occasional triathlon.

Specialties

Known for results, keen ability to quickly understand the needs of my buyer-clients, differentiated from most Realtors by strong negotiating skills, unwavering loyalty to doing what is right for my clients, comprehensive resuts-oriented marketing plans for selling homes for the highest possible price.
