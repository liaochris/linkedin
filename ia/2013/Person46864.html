Steve Gannon's Summary

I love the insurance industry and my career! I help people every day protect what is important to them and create insurance plans that provide the necessary coverage at a price that is fair and reasonable. It is not just about selling a policy, in fact that is only a small portion of my job. My team and I provide service to our clients as their advocates. We are truly there to make certain that our clients are responded to quickly and treated fairly when they need help.

When you work with Gannon Insurance Agency you aren't just getting a cookie cutter insurance policy. We offer professional advice, policy reviews, and recommendations that match your ever changing needs over time.

As independent agents, we represent competing insurance companies. This means more choices and lower rates for you.

We aim to make insurance shopping easy for you. We will find you the right policy at the right price.

Please visit our Website to learn more!
