Claire Dowdall DipM MCIM's Summary

Experience in managerial-level marketing roles since graduation in industries including horticulture, manufacturing and industrial services in small to medium-sized companies in Wolverhampton and Shropshire. I've worked in cross functional project teams and with outside suppliers of pr, advertising, web, graphic design and product photography services and managed budgets large and small.

This experience and my passion for marketing has led me to be able to offer marketing workshops and one-to-one training to small business owners who want to learn how to do marketing themselves. I want to help solo entrepreneurs and small businesses to achieve impact with their marketing activities within their budget and in their own unique way.

Specialties

Marketing strategy, copywriting, copy editing, proofreading, branding, web planning, product management, marketing communications, PR, project management and mentoring and advice to small entrepreneurial businesses in Telford, Shrewsbury, Oswestry (and the rest of Shropshire), Wolverhampton and Stafford.

Claire Dowdall DipM MCIM's Experience

Owner

Claire Dowdall - Marketing Trainer, Writer & Speaker

July 2011 – Present (2 years 2 months) Shropshire

I'm passionate about helping small businesses to learn about marketing in a way that suits them. I believe that through a mixture of interactive and hands-on learning, both in groups and one-to-one settings, and by facilitating people to learn from each other in a supportive community, small businesses can do marketing themselves and they can have the confidence to do it well.

I know marketing works and I want to help local enterprises to see a real return on their time and money and that's what I'm here to make sure happens.

Event Leader

#focusonmarketing

June 2011 – Present (2 years 3 months) Shropshire

Are you looking for an opportunity to meet up with other business owners and share your ideas and experiences? Well that’s why I set up #focusonmarketing – an informal ‘masterminding’ group for small business owners in Shropshire and surrounding areas, who are looking for marketing inspiration from specialists as well as their peers.

We meet in the gallery space at The Hive music and media centre in Shrewsbury and run a series of themed events where we discuss a set subject, sharing tips and best practice along the way. All attendees will agree that you can’t fail to come away with something useful to apply to your business straight away. I will be there to answer any burning questions you may have, so come with your dilemmas. What’s more, there’s no cost to attend!
