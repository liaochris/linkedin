Sean Lynch's Summary

Think of Dr. Lynch as your alternative to stress. Using his experience and his clients desire, the relationship that persons have with stress, change, leading to a more comfortable, productive, peaceful and joyous life.

We serve those seeking Solutions to pain problems, Prevention of illness and injury and Enhancement of quality of life.

Dr. Lynch is uniquely positioned at the only instructor and practitioner of Zero Balancing on the Western Slope. Zero Balancing(ZB) is a hands on therapy that addresses stress, pain, held patterns of tension while enhancing feelings of well being and health.

Specialties

As an instructor of Zero Balancing I can offer continuing education to most all in the health care field including other doctors, acupuncturists, physical and occupational therapist as well as massage and body work therapist.
