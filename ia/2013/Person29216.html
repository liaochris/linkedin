Dan Dodds' Summary

This Is Who I Am:
I'm a Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 Bankruptcy Attorney and I primarily focus on matters involving Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 Bankruptcy, consumer protection, breach of contract, public education and special education. I am also the founder of The Dodds Law Firm. I'm a former teacher, high school principal and school district superintendent and I've been a sole practioner in Surprise, Arizona since 2006.

This Is Who I Help:
I help those thinking about filing Chapter 7 or Chapter 13 Bankruptcy or need help with General Litigation, Education Law or Special Education Law.

I also help CPAs, Financial Advisors, Real Estate and Mortgage Brokers by assisting their clients with Chapter 7 or Chapter 13 Bankruptcy.

How Do I Help:
I take a down-to-earth approach; I will listen carefully to all your questions and concerns. Before becoming an attorney, I spent 28 years in education. I learned the value of listening, as well as the ability to clearly communicate complex ideas. I will make certain that you understand the law and the process, as well as your options moving forward, so that you can make informed decisions.

If you want to stay informed on Bankruptcy issues please visit http://doddsbankruptcylaw.com. It's my daily blog where I do my best to keep you informed on Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 Bankruptcy. If you have a question call 623-209-8923.

Former teacher, high school principal and for fourteen years, a school district superintendent. Obtained my B.S. in Education from Oregon State University in 1970, M.S. in Curriculum & Instruction from the University of Oregon in 1977, Ph.D. in Human Resource Management from the University of Oregon in 1990, and J.D. from Thomas Jefferson School of Law in San Diego, California in 2004. Sole practioner in Surprise, Arizona since 2006 primarily focusing on matters involving consumer protection, breach of contract, public education and special education.
