Joe Bartolucci's Summary

Creativity. Technology. Innovation. I'm more interested in something hair-brained than half-assed. I hate the words "NO" and "Can't." I always make time to listen to an idea, you never know where it may lead. I like to build things, so I love to know HOW things work. I have a roll-up-your-sleeves mentality and believe no matter what level you're at, you need to get into the work. I make brands GO online. Actually, I make brands GO, look good and sound awesome. And I like to make the journey fun along the way!

I've worked in these categories: CPG, home-improvement retail, fashion retail, QSR, beer & spirits, mobile device, cellular communications, armed services and outdoor lifestyle goods.

Always looking for experiences to broaden my expertise; sharpen my creative skills; or work with visionary people, mavericks, savants, whackos and carnies. Let's talk about what's Next>>>

Specialties: Written, presented, sold work and won awards... always looking to innovate. Clients include: Cheez-Its, Wrigley Brands (Orbit, Hubba Bubba, Altoids, Big Red, Juicy Fruit), MCDs, Budweiser, Blackberry, US Army, Kellogg's, Hostess Snacks, Wonder Bread, Tropicana, State Farm, Lowe's, LensCrafters, True Value, Met Life.
