Fernando Nicolau Ferreira CISM, CGEIT, CSSLP, CFE's Summary

• Over 16 years of experience in information technology and information security projects. Responsible for the planning, the execution and the coordination of many projects in the area of information security, systems audit, technical support, local net administration, revision and elaboration of contingency plans, risk assessment, information security policies, management of changes and development of systems, applying the best international practices ISO 17799, ISO 27001, ISO 27002, CobiT, ITIL e SAS70.

• Information Security professional certified as CISM, CGEIT, CRISC, CSSLP, CITP, CFE, CobiT Foundation, and BS7799 Lead Auditor.

• Author of the books:
o “Segurança da Informação” [Information Security], Ciência Moderna Publishing House, São Paulo, 2003 (in Portuguese);
o “Política de Segurança da Informação, Guia de Desenvolvimento e Implementação” [Information Security Policy, Guide for Development and Implementation], Ciência Moderna Publishing House, São Paulo, 2006 (in Portuguese).

• One of the authors of the book “Novos Conceitos de Auditoria de Sistemas em Bancos” [New Concepts on Audit on Systems in Banks], published by FEBRABAN (Brazilian Federation of Banks) in June 2003 (in Portuguese).

• Sponsor of the portal about Systems Audit and Information Security called FERNANDOFERREIRA.COM (www.fernandoferreira.com).

• Communication Director at ISACA São Paulo chapter (2009-2011).

• Trainning Director at ACFE Brazil chapter (2013-current).

• Instructor of internal and external courses, author of articles for newspapers, magazines and audit and security web sites (such as ISSA Brazil).
