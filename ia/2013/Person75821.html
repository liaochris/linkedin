Kim N Carswell's Publications

  • Resume Branding 101: Strategies for Getting Noticed in 10 Seconds or Less

    • Asta Publications
    • March 1, 2011
    Authors: Kim N Carswell

    Resume Branding 101 is a motivational guidebook for job seekers who need help with marketing their brand value to employers. Kim N. Carswell is a conversationalist who shares 26 years worth of resume writing, recruiting, branding and training expertise in this book. She introduces the Resume Branding Strategy™ which includes five major philosophies to help people create marketable “employer friendly” resumes and career related documents before the standard interview phase.

    Readers get step-by-step exercises at the end of each chapter and gain a practical understanding of how to create a polished resume brand based on their personality, credentials and accomplishments to elevate their market value.
