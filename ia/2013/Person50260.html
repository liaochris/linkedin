Greg Nunamaker's Summary

I have the opportunity every day to communicate ideas through interaction, motion and design. Whether it be a sketch, illustration, animation, 3D model, website or physical structure. I love to work out of both sides of my brain marrying the creative with the technical and analytical. Day to day I get to solve problems and create solutions that aren't only functional, but intentional and inspiring.

Specialties

Developing front-end systems; communicating ideas with animation, design and illustration; bridging the gap between design and technical; developing concepts via storyboards and wireframes; simplifying complex ideas to maximize user experiences; adapting to new environments; finding solutions to new challenges.
