Eric Martinez's Summary

For just over 3,105 coffee fueled, iPod charged, Pantone selecting days...

Eric Martinez has been creating print, motion, and interactive solutions for youth, music, and entertainment clients using a combination of future focused ideas, modern technologies, and traditional creative tools and methods.

This new hybrid of art and design has spawn solutions that are...smirk inducing, lively, fat-free and contagious.

On good days, these solutions were recognized and/or appeared on the pages of HOW, Communication Arts, Print, ID Magazine, American Graphic Design Awards, Creativity, Logos 2000, Graphic Design, U.S.A., as well as the walls of The White House( yes, that "White" House).

Specialties

Art Direction & Design for the Web & Print, Branding & Identity, Really Big Ideas, and Good (mostly clean) Fun!
