Jane Barr CPC, ELIMP Leadership/Communication Coach, Speaker's Summary

Who:
As a coach I specialize in working with SuperWoman, the smart, savvy business woman who goes to work everyday to protect her world, yet often feels like she spent her day pushing a boulder up a mountain, by herself.

What:
Power Plays for SuperWoman, is a mid-career strategy which includes an on-line assessment that identifies exactly what is draining your energy and how likely you are to either keep pushing that boulder up a mountain or move your career ahead in a way that is fun, fulfilling and financially rewarding.

Why:
For 30 years I have lead teams of business and technical consultants in providing business process re-engineering strategies, software selections and system implementations for national retailers such as GAP, Vera Bradley, and CVS, I know what it takes to complete a project on time and on budget and I’ve brought in business worth millions of dollars to the consulting and software companies who employed me.

On the other hand, I also know exactly what it’s like to have great products and services, to do handstands in order to solve a clients concern and work long hours to make sure a project is completed on time and then... still not get the project, not get promoted, even to get the boot. OUCH!

Other Relevant Background:
In addition to being a Certified Life Coach, I also share my knowledge and experience as a Certified Energy Leadership Master Practitioner, Dale Carnegie Leadership and Communication Coach, High Power! Presentations Trainer, Savvy Relationships, and Perfect Customers.

If you are interested in picking up some tips that you can use right now, visit my blog at www.kinnexion.com

Contact me directly: jbarr@kinnexion.com or (941) 315-0109
