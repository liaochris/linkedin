Lauren A. Wolfe's Summary

Lauren Wolfe is the Interactive Marketing & New Media Manager at Higher Logic (www.higherlogic.com), a leading provider of social media applications for member-based organizations world wide. Lauren is responsible for running the creative department, trade-show/event department and the impressive Learning Series Program – and at Higher Logic, she works on all things *glitter*. Her goal is to do great things and make the world a better place...one glitter bomb at time.
