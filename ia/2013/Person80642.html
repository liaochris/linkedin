Leslie Holley, MA, LPC's Summary

Leslie Holley is a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) who earned her Masters in Community Counseling from DePaul University. Currently, she sees clients at Urban Balance, LLC in the Chicago downtown and Evanston office locations. Leslie also works, part time, at Bensinger Dupont & Associates (BDA), counseling clients who have an employee assistant program (EAP) and clients presenting with gambling addiction.

Leslie specializes in cognitive behavior therapy (CBT) and believes in an interactive strength-based approach to therapy. She is experienced working with individuals (adults and adolescents) and couples presenting with depression, anxiety, trauma, coping with life transitions and relationship issues. Leslie is a Illinois Mental Health Counseling Association (IMHCA) board member and membership committee chair. She is an experienced presenter and enjoys advocating for the underserved.
