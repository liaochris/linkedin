Hollen M. Ferrendelli's Summary

Hollen launched Think InsideOut in January of 2010 after one year of performing executive coaching under her other company, Inscite Partners. As a Partner of Inscite, in business since 2000, she and her other two partners provide consulting services with small to mid-sized companies in transition on an outsouced managment interim basis. Hollen remains responsible for the overall business development and day-to-day activities.

Hollen is a 20-year experienced professional in employee relations, operations and facilities, strategy and management. She obtained a dual Masters from the University of Denver in Organizational Leadership and Conflict Resolution. She has significant expertise with companies in transition including successful start-up, shut-down and turn-around operations and facilities management, as well as experience in executive business management and operations within several industries including healthcare, venture capital, high-tech and non-profit sectors.

In addition to extensive experience in strategic planning, restructuring and day-to-day management, Hollen has 10 years training and experience in coaching, mentoring, mediating and facilitating. Her experience spans many sectors, including working on and off-campus with at-risk youth, in the workplace with indiviaul executives and team members, as well as in the board room with for and not-for-profit companies. Hollen has transferred her business experience, training and volunteer work in to the workplace serving individuals and small companies who are in transition.

Hollen is the President of the Board of Directores for Susan G. Komen for the Cure Denver Affiliate, actively advocating, educating, and fundraising for the organization. She also supports, volunteers and is a facilitator for Colorado Youth at Risk, as well as an ambassador in the community for several other non-profits that she is passionate about including Project PAVE, RMC Health and Colorado Youth Matter.
