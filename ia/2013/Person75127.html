Kevin Day's Summary

I am a content strategist with a background in website user experience, content marketing, copywriting and project oversight. I am also an entrepreneur, having started my own content strategy firm in 2011.

My industry focus has been in the travel/tourism and outdoor recreation sectors, but I also have extensive knowledge of economic development, finance/banking and wine/food products. I am passionate about how content can support brand campaigns and trigger action among targeted audiences.

The perfect fit for my skill set and expertise includes: (a) consulting with an in-house web/marketing team on short- and long-term content-oriented projects, (b) joining an interactive/advertising agency on a dedicated web development project, and (c) freelance content creation for any of the above entities.

Specialties: Content strategy, content marketing, content development, SEO, web analytics, copywriting, new product strategies and execution, new business proposals and RFPs, public speaking and presenting, project management, social media integration, writing/journalistic research, brainstorming techniques, content management systems (including drupal, Wordpress and Sitecore).
