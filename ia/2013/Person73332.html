Kay Paumier's Summary

I work with B2B companies that are struggling to compete and grow. They want to stop working so hard for sales leads, and start winning business from their rivals.

Typically these organizations are stretched thin, but still need their products and services to stand out in the crowd. They need to be on the “A” list, to be better known, more credible and more profitable.

Companies like these are ready to go to the next level.

So together we uncover what makes them different, and then market those features online and off. http://www.CommunicationsPlus.net/PRservices.html

Here is what I do for these companies.

Publicity: I increase my clients' credibility through favorable coverage in both traditional and social media. www.CommunicationsPlus.net/PR_Results.html

Writing: I help my clients stand out in the crowd by showcasing what makes them different in written material, from website and blog copy to news releases and e-books. www.CommunicationsPlus.net/Writ_Examples.html

Online and Offline Communications: I help my clients improve their online and offline presence by enhancing their websites, improving their presentations, and developing brochures and datasheets. .

What I am looking for in LinkedIn.

•Medium-sized (30-100 people) B2B companies or divisions of larger companies that need to become better known and increase their sales leads.

•Potential partners, including marketing or communications firms or consultants such as PR professionals, graphic artists, web designers, writers, branding and advertising firms.

Specialties: Marketing communications, public relations, PR, publicity, media relations, writing (website content, blogs, news releases, feature stories, e-books, brochures, corporate profiles, datasheets) PowerPoint presentations, product and company launches.

Kay Paumier's Experience

Marketing Communications Consultant | Public Relations | PR | Writer | Trainer

Communications Plus

April 1987 – Present (26 years 6 months)

As principal of Communications Plus, I provide marketing communications services including public relations (PR), publicity, and writing primarily to mid-sized B2B companies and divisions of larger organizations. I help make companies more credible and generate sales leads by increasing awareness of their products and services through traditional and online media. Clients have included Fujitsu, Nokia, Novell, Oracle, Memjet, CableWholesale, Toshiba MRI, and Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.
