Jonathan Dietz's Summary

With more than 12 years of Technology Development and Digital Strategy experience, I have been on the cutting edge of strategy and development as well as a pioneer in digital marketing.

I have held various technology infrastructure and full stack development positions giving me a diverse and unique perspective on technology in general, which in turn helps me to understand the end-to-end needs of the development lifecycle. I have also held management and lead developer positions at businesses ranging from small boutique digital agencies, to large publicly traded companies.

The last 5 years I have spent strategizing and developing in the digital brand management and advertising space, where I’ve worked on building and maintaining high performance, high traffic websites, pioneering social strategies, and bringing creativity to many 3rd party APIs.

I have had the pleasure to work on some of the most well known brands in the world including, Activision, Pixar, NBC Universal, Dreamworks, Quiksilver, Subway, General Motors, Qualcomm, Taylor Guitars, UFL, Timex, and Invisible Children.

I am also actively involved in the open source community where I have been an organizer and presenter of monthly Drupal meet-ups and conferences.

Skills: Backend and frontend development (Full Stack), digital architecture, DevOps, social strategies, brand management, LAMP stack, Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, Git, Chef, Selenium, Nginx.
