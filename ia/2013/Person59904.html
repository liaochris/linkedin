Jayme Soulati's Summary

Jayme Soulati is a hybrid public relations professional blending digital marketing, content marketing and social media marketing with message mapping and core PR.

She is an award-winning professional blogger and author of Writing with Verve on the Blogging Journey, available in e-book and softcover.

She writes for Steamfeed.com, 12Most.com, BloggerBeat.com, Clarity for the Boss, Chief Marketer and a variety of other sites. She is a national speaker and is available to present at all types of meetings, workshops and conferences.

She is an agency PR professional with 18 years in Chicago's PR firms. She is available for hire on a project basis, to round out a marketing or PR team, on retainer, for longer-term projects, and to consult.

Jayme is a graduate of the University of Wisconsin with a Bachelor’s of Arts in Journalism with emphasis in public relations and minor studies in German.

She is a past president of the Publicity Club of Chicago (1993-94) and 2-term board member. She volunteers with social service organizations locally for women's and children's issues.
