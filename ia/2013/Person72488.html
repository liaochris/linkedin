Karrie Wallen's Summary

I am a gifted change agent and facilitator, specifically in regard to the transformation of troubled sites or organizations. I have an incredible ability to listen, understand and communicate the underlying issues pertaining to workplace conflict or blocking goal attainment. I have a knack for finding the pulse, making determinations and providing site specific suggestions for alleviating the root causes.

I have a background in education and labor/employee relations. I strongly believe in collaborative problem solving, creative and outside the box solutions, individual input and empowerment, connecting to passion as well as respect, integrity and honesty with ourselves and others.

Please feel free to contact me directly via LinkedIn mail or send me an email at karrie.wallen@yahoo.com.

Specialties: Troubled or Conflicted Sites and Organizations; Culture/Climate Transformation; Employee Relations; Change Management; Employee/Management Coaching; Empowerment Training; Conflict Resolution
