Edmund Jackson's Summary

# I am:
* An independent, data-science consultant
* with a doctorate from Cambridge in statistical signal processing
* and over a decade experience in analysing large, difficult data in many different domains
* using decades of experience as a programmer in many different languages


# I value:
* Working, clean, technical solutions,
* using a pragmatic approach to achieve good-enough results today, and better results tomorrow
* which are clearly communicated.


# I offer:
* Data science engagement
- Communicate the techniques and potential of data science
- and learn about your data
- so that we can discover new potential in your existing data
- and achieve your business goals
- or discover new opportunities

* Algorithmic design
- Design the mathematical, statistical or machine learning techniques to solve your problems

* Software development
- Work independently or with your team to create the software to bring these algorithms to reality

* Communication and support
- Communicate the functioning of the algorithms to your management and development teams
- and support them in its ongoing usage.


# Keyword Skills
* Programming: Clojure, R, Matlab, Mathematica
* Machine Learning: General, Bayesian Statistics, MCMC, SMC
