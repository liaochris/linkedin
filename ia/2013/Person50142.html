Gregg Shupe's Summary

Gregg Shupe, a graduate of RIT with a BS in Professional Photography, began Freelance PhotoImages, in 1983, and since then has consistently delivered creative, quality imagery that is exceptionally dependable and competitive.

In 2000, with the arrival of digital photography at hand, Gregg was able to create his own website launching it with a new business name of ShupeStudios.com. Gregg continues to offer the most advanced technology for his clients and willingly shares his knowledge to educate about the ever changing tools of photography.

Shupe Studios believes in working with a client's schedule to meet their needs with competitive pricing, quality results and delivering all within a client's critical time constraints.

When Gregg is not shooting, he is a devoted family man with two children and great wife, assistant Scout Master with BSA Troop 12 Framingham and avid skier at Sugarbush & Mad River Glen.

Specialties

Architectural, Product, Corporate, Food, Resort and Aerial Photography.
