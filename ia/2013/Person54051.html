Hunington Sachs' Summary

Accomplished senior executive and dealmaker with over 20 years experience advising and managing media & entertainment companies in advertising, game development, film, television, sports, music, digital media and technology. Experienced intellectual property, business and finance lawyer for domestic and international companies, including the Japanese venture capital company Transcosmos Inc. Certified securities arbitrator and panel chair for FINRA. Prior to law and business, Hunington led troops as a Captain in US Army-Europe. He’s a creative problem solver, dealmaker, and leader.
