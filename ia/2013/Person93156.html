Golden Gate Integral Counseling Center

Two-day training where I presented the fundamental concepts of AEDP for Couples drawing on the work of Dr. Diana Fosha and Dr. David Mars. Key Concepts presented: Establishing Safety, Polyvagal Theory, Bottom-Up Processing vs. Top-Down Processing, The 7 Channels of Experience, Time-Shifting, Embodied Witnessing, and Metaprocessing. Experiential exercises included a "7 channels of Experience" meditation and interactive discussion of the video work.
