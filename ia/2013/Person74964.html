Kevin May's Summary

As you can probably tell, my name is Kevin May, I grew up in Erie, Pennsylvania and quickly developed a passion for web design. What started out to just be something fun I liked to do in my free time, turned into taking classes and getting a degree and now my career.

I have worked with a few well known companies in Erie, helping them improve their websites: John V. Schultz Furniture & Mattress and Lavery Brewing Company.

I have also won 2 Addy Awards for my personal website and also the redesign of the John V. Schultz website.

I have a passion for design and I work hard every day to continue learning new things and enhancing my skills until one day, I become a Creative Director.

Specialties

Pixel Perfect Creative Designs, HTML5 & CSS3 Development, Logo/Brand Design, Graphic Design, Print Design, Digital Photography
