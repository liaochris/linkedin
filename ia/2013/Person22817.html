Alexander Kjerulf's Summary

Alex makes people happy at work!

As the Chief Happiness Officer and founder of Woohoo inc. he speaks and consults worldwide in businesses like IBM, Hilton, LEGO, IKEA, HP and many others.

He is the author of Happy Hour is 9 to 5 - How to Love Your Job, create a great workplace and Kick Butt at Work.

He also writes one of the world's most popular blogs on career and leadership issues at www.positivesharing.com.

As he’ll be happy to tell you: Happiness at work is not rocket science. It is, in fact, surprisingly easy. And if you’re not happy – what’s it all worth?
