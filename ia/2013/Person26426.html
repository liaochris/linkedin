Ellyn Small Enisman's Summary

I am a career and life coach and work with anyone going through, thinking about, or preparing for a career transition. I also work with all levels of staff within companies to develop, implement and deliver individual and group coaching for performance improvement.
Since we spend so much of our time working, I believe that career and life are intertwined. Life transitions affect work and work transitions affect life. Separating the two is challenging and I collaborate with my clients to navigate both successfully.

In addition to my practice with experienced professionals, I specialize in coaching college students and new college grads. I help them explore careers, prepare for, and execute their job search with the greatest emphasis on interview skill training. Using a self assessment that I have developed, the student and I collaborate to identify the knowledge, skills, and abilities they have gained from their education, project work, internships, work experiences, and life experiences. My goal is to help them utilize this knowledge to successfully interview and get the job they want.
My qualifications: I am an accomplished senior executive with over twenty years experience in the employment and coaching and manufacturing industries with hands on experience and bottom line responsibility for recruiting, hiring, client relationship development, operations, leadership development and training, marketing and sales management, strategic planning, and administration. I have participated in the growth of a company from one small office to a national firm. The knowledge, skills, and abilities, I have gained through my education and professional experience enables me to collaborate with my clients to uncover their own knowledge, skills, and abilities, choose their goals, and improve their performance.

Specialties

Career transition coaching with experienced professionals.
Executive coaching all levels of staff and management.
Developing and implementing recruiting and staffing plans.
Interview and career exploration coaching with college students and new grads
