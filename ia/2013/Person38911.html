Mr. Clarke is the managing partner of Clarke Real Estate Group. Mr. Clarke has been successful in the real estate acquisitions, brokerage and property management industries for over 10 years. Mr. Clarke formed Clarke Real Estate Group in 2004 in order to create a vehicle for private and institutional investors to acquire and own income-producing real estate.

Clarke Real Estate Group acquires commercial real estate properties within emerging markets with an emphasis on Multifamily, Medical Office and Self-Storage and Retail and then offer Investors different ways to participate in the ownership.

With our unique range of local market knowledge, management expertise and strong transactional relationships with owners, brokers, and lenders we are able to grow and manage these assets to achieve superior returns. Investors today are seeking alternatives that are more secure and stable and. Clarke Real Estate Group, LLC fills this demand for individual investors

Please visit our website or call our information line to learn more about opportunities as well as receive reports and guides on how to earn high returns through commercial investment real estate.
