Maria Aponte's Summary

Maria is a fashion stylist based in Philadelphia Pennsylvania. After graduating from the University of the Arts, she has been working in the photography industry for 18 years and the past 11 of the those years have been in the fashion industry. From clothing and jewelry, to room décor and styling, she has worked on advertising, commercial, catalog and fashion editorial shoots, as well as music videos. Her background also includes fashion photography, where she spent several years assessing the subject from the other side of the camera, which helped her gain a unique perspective on the bigger picture…so to speak.

Maria has worked with several notable clients, both locally and nationally, including Puma, Trump Plaza Casino, Hamilton Jewelers, Roc-A-Fella Records, Nextel and People Magazine, among others. She has also handled the creative direction for several magazine covers and editorial shoots as well.

Maria has worked as the Fashion Director for Accent Magazine, and continues to consult on multiple projects within the industry.
