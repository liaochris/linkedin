Carolyn Douglas' Summary

An Emmy award-winning broadcast news journalist, public speaking coach and communications consultant with two decades of experience in the broadcast news industry, media consulting and community relations. Dedicated to helping people and organizations tell their stories to the world - on air, online and in person - and facilitating exclusive networking opportunities.
Communications/Media consultant, TV anchor, producer, public speaker and coach. Host/Managing Editor for UWTV; Chief Communications Officer for Operation Military Family; founding board member for the Alliance for International Women's Rights; event emcee and panel moderator for Edgar Martinez's Image Source; media coach/talent for Cimira productions; Celebrate Edmonds member.
Clients and partners include: University of Washington, UWTV, KING TV, KOMO TV, Operation Military Family, City of Edmonds, ETC Corporation, Alliance Media Network, Camp Korey, The Alliance for International Women's Rights, Main Street Law Group, College Promise Coalition, Service Linen Supply, Comcast Spotlight, Synergia Northwest, Chat With Women Radio, Image Source, The Window Channel, Wilson Strategic, Northwest Physicians Network, Fabric of Life Foundation, Social Smarts program, Seeds of Compassion/ Compassionate Action Network, Washington CASH, Cimira Studios, Northwest Design and Consulting, Cathy Nilon/Ravenna Press.

Specialties: Media and community relations strategist; broadcast journalist/on camera host; public speaking coach; content producer; event emcee/panel moderator; workshop facilitator.
