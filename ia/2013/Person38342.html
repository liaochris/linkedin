Simon W Rosenberg, DMD's Summary

Since my graduation from Tufts Dental School in Boston in 1975 and doing my General Practice Residency at Boston City Hospital, I developed a Medical-Hospital analytic approach to patient diagnosis and treatment planning. This was reenforced during my practice at Harvard St. Neighborhood Health Center and my 11 years of Prosthodontic and Maxillofacial Prosthetics at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. At MSKCC, I combined my interests in Oral Medicine (oral complications of treating cancer with head and neck surgery, chemotherapy and radiation therapy), Prosthodontic Reconstruction and Rehabilitation with my interest in Computer Application in Medical Research and Practice Management. Since 1989 my private office practice has evolved from providing Patient-Centered care (kind of a Nordstroms or Ritz-Carlton approach to a luxurious client experience) with precise prosthodontic and general dental patient care into the Center for High Tech Dentistry that uses Biolase Waterlase MD and Oddesey Dental Lasers, Sirona CEREC CAD/CAM precision milled porcelain restorations that we can custom stain with our Vita porcelain oven and verify shade match with our Vita EZShade spectrophotometer. We also use other technologies such as Snap Digital Imaging (for Smile Analysis), T-Scan (Computer Assisted Occlusal Analysis for bite adjustment), and Schick Digital Intra-oral and Panoramic Radiographs (using 80-90% less radiation than film and elliminating the Lead, Silver and other chemical pollution associated with conventional fillm development.)

Specialties

Trained in General Prosthodontics (crowns, bridges, dentures and implant supported dental restorations), Maxillofacial Prosthetics (oral prosthetics to restore eating, chewing, swallowing, speech & normal esthetic appearance. I combine the art of facial & smile evaluation with scientific & clinical skills to provide Cosmetic Dentistry. Finally, I have proficiency in Internet Marketing to increase the traffic flow to my Websites and help others to achieve the same.
