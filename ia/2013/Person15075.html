Xiomara Smith's Summary

Blueprint Financial Group is a Business Development Firm located in Atlanta metro area. We specialize in practical business services such as business loan placement, loan packaging, corporate identity development, start-up business planning & consultation, and business, market research. We also have added value service referrals for hospitality industry advisement and branding through our affiliated firms; tax planning and preparation for businesses by reputable CPA referrals. We also offer B2B serivces by providing Third Party Underwriting to financial groups & institutions, start-up SBA units, venture capitalists, and even brokerage firms.

Proficient in PCFS 2000, Moody's, E-Tran, Underwriting templates and Credit Workbooks, FHA Connection, Veri-Tax, SBA GP and PLP closing procedures, and SBA 7(a) and 504 Underwriting procedures and Loan Review Memos criteria and format.

Client Service Representative, Xiomara Smith, has been in the banking industry for over 8 years and has many resources at her fingertips to help a business start up, expand, and/or re-organize its structure. With a strong SBA/Conventional underwriting background, her business banking skills are what any person needs to help support their business operation behind the scenes, while the business owner concentrates on marketing their business.
