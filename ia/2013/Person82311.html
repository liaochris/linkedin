Lookman _'s Summary

LOOKMAN as a production company was established in 2009 originally it was an author brand. We work on the challenging edge of film and TV with an eye to cross-media brands. We believe, 'a story has to stretch its audience' and the process of enhancement and development never ends. We admire transformative scenes, taking things to the limit, epic and moving stories.

LOOKMAN released two unique children's TV animation series pilots “Who is Henrietta?’ a comedy (2012) and ‘Mihte Lugh the Shining One’ an action adventure (2013) . We are now involved in co-productions following this. We are also developing 'Children from the Sea' – an extraordinary teen film akin to Twilight (2008). We also have historical movie projects and some interesting animated features maturing.
