Derrick Monroe's Summary

Thanks for stopping by - my name is Derrick Monroe & I am a full time Realtor in the Minneapolis Minnesota area specializing in the Twin Cities & suburbs.

My background is a 100% self starter & entrepreneur since a young age and I know the hard work & dedication it takes to get the job done. The Derrick Monroe Group with Lakes Area Realty consists of full time listing agents, exclusive buyers agents and a customer care transaction coordinator. DMG also offers in-house lending through PHH Home Loans & closing services - there's nothing the "big box" stores have that we don't.

We are available for short sales, foreclosure help, listings, buyers, REO & Realtor to Realtor referrals.

There are a ton of agents out there, in fact maybe too many that are part time and simply think of real estate as nothing but "fun looking at homes all day", yes, I hear agents say that all the time on why they got into real estate. DMG doesn't have any agents like that. Our team understands the job, in fact our team leader Derrick Monroe owns rental properties and has a construction background so he understands many aspects of real estate first hand, not just by seeing others do it. Our exclusive buyers agent has over a decade of managing thousands of rentals in the portfolio of a management company and has sold hundreds of homes.

DMG is here to help, we know its hard to pick an agent for yourself or to refer to a friend but we can promise you this... give us a shot and we will do our very best to make sure you're taken care of and the transaction is seamless. Our reputation depends on it and we know that.
