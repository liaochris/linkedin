Alexis Nascimento's Summary

Alexis (Dias) Nascimento specializes in integrated marketing and communications, with expertise in strategic planning, branding, public relations, message development and deployment, social media and event planning. Alexis currently works as the social media specialist for E. & J. Gallo Winery, and also provides communication consultation services for small businesses.

Prior to joining Gallo, Alexis worked for Hi-Tec USA and Magnum USA, where she successfully launched both brands into the social media space. During her time at Magnum, she quickly assumed the role of public relations manager, and developed solid relationships with both tactical editors and bloggers alike. In this capacity, Alexis increased Magnum's media exposure by 1,275% in less than a year. Her past experience also includes serving as the marketing and PR manager for Doctors Medical Center. Alexis started her career as an account manager a Merced-based communications agency, On Target Marketing, where she managed more than 20 brands. Alexis also aided in forming Central Valley Public Relations Organization (CVPRO), where she served as the chairperson, publicity chair and secretary since its inception.

Alexis resides in Turlock, where she lives with her husband Steven, and dogs Mo and Ruby. In her free time, Alexis fosters dogs in need and advocates for animal rescues/adoptions. For marketing and PR insights, news and tips, you can follow her on Twitter at http://twitter.com/AlexisNasc.

Specialties

Strategic planning; branding; public relations; event planning; media relations; interactive marketing and social media; message development and dissemination; news and feature writing; and research.
