Elizabeth Gordon's Summary

I am a game programmer with 7 years of experience. I have worked on all aspects of gameplay and had the opportunity to work on a multi-threaded game engine, and I have written server-side gameplay code in JavaScript for an online MMO. I have worked on a variety of gaming platforms, including Xbox 360, PS3, Wii, PC, iOS, and Flash.

Right now, I am working on an Android / iOS title using C# and Unity3D.

I have both an MSc and a PhD in artificial intelligence. My industry experience includes work on combat AI, stealth, perceptual systems, crowd/pedestrian behavior, levels of detail, path finding, behavior systems, and traffic car simulation.

Specialties: artificial intelligence, gameplay, C++, JavaScript, C#, Unity3D
