Matt Huber's Summary

I am a motivated, dedicated, and driven person who enjoys working on exciting projects. Consulting has been a great fit for me in my career due to the fast-paced environment. This has given me the opportunity to experience multiple industry verticals and interesting challenges. I’ve also been given the chance to manage a few team members at Cardinal Solutions and have really enjoyed managing/mentoring those individuals.

I specialize in developing for SharePoint, HTML/CSS/JS and I’ve had the opportunity to lead large and small projects. I really enjoy working with clients to figure out their needs and work towards creating a creative/cost-effective solution that will solve their problems. I pride myself on coming up with solutions that involve and understand risk, cost, and culture.

I’ve grown a lot since I started in Information Technology… learning a lot from blogs and online-resources. Since then, I've decided to give back to the community by speaking at multiple SharePoint User Groups ( Columbus, Ohio) and SharePoint Saturdays (OH, MI). I also write a blog at http://matthuber.com where I give guidance and advice on SharePoint and current web standards.
