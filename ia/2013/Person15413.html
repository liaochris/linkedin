Bob Kelleher's Summary

Bob is an author, speaker, and founder of The Employee Engagement Group (www.EmployeeEngagement.com). A noted thought leader on employee engagement and leadership trends, Bob works with leadership teams across the globe helping them improve their business results through improved employee engagement. Author of the critically acclaimed book Louder Than Words - 10 Practical Employee Engagement Steps...that Drive Results!, the best selling employee engagement book on Amazon during 2011. Bob's second book, Creativeship, A Novel for Evolving Leaders, was just released to glowing reviews in October, 2012. An an award winning speaker, (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OHsB-cjyhzY), Bob has been featured on NBC News, CNBC, CBS Radio, Fox Radio, Business Week, The Street, AMA, and is a contributing guest editor for many publications. Bob frequently speaks at conferences, forums, and strategic planning meetings, including recent talks in Asia, France, Mexico, Ukraine, UAE, Canada, England, Ireland, and Poland. Previously, Bob was employed by AECOM as Chief Human Resources Officer, one of the world's largest engineering firms with over 45,000 employees. He also was COO and EVP for ENSR, a 2,300 employee global consulting firm, and a subsidiary of AECOM. Bob's competencies include leading cultural transformations, leadership development, teambuilding, performance improvement, talent management, communication and alignment, and strategic planning. At AECOM, Bob was responsible for AECOM's global HR and OD, and spearheaded AECOM's engagement initatives.
