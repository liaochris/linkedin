Alex Krail's Summary

With 20 years of agency/freelance experience, I have created and produced award-winning print and online marketing campaigns in a variety of industries. Excellent work ethic. Proven results.

Specialties

I am very fast with creative solutions, have strict attention to detail and I am a very good problem solver. I have excellent skills in all of the major design software - print and online, staff and project management experience and good relationships with my clients and suppliers. And through it all, I have helped create fun and highly productive working relationships.
