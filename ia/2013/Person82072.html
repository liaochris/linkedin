Lauren Markon, MS Counseling's Summary

As far back as I can remember, I have been helping friends and family with their job search. I gain a personal satisfaction from seeing others succeed in their careers, as I have in mine.

In my business, I provide my clients with quality resumes and cover letters, as well as offer job search and interview skills. I also assist in developing LinkedIn profiles, setting up a job search agents or guiding client's to local area networking events or job fairs.

With a Master Degree in Counseling and 12+ years of experience as a Career Counselor in Higher Education and Outplacement, I have extensive resume and job search experience. What makes me different from other resume writers is that I meet my client's in person, to customize their resume and cover letter and give them an advantage against the competition in their career search.

I am an experienced Career Counselor with expertise in client relations. I enjoy motivational speaking, program development and networking, as well as sharing ideas and referrals with other counselors.

My business goal is to guide client's to achieve success in their careers. My personal goal is to develop more networks to build my personal business, Career Connections.

Specialties: Career Counseling, Motivational Speaking, Teaching, Resume and Cover Letter Writing, Program and Presentation Development, Referrals, Social Services, Client Services, Networking, Writing and Job Development, Linkedin Profile Development, Public Speaking, Workshop Development and Presenter, Mock Interviewing,
