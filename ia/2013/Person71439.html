Justin Wolter's Summary

Interior – Spatial Design
Graphics – Illustration

www.justinwolter.com

__________________________________________________________

I approach my work with attention to detail and a contemporary,
culturally-sensitive ethos – considering theory with practice in hand
with style and appropriateness – generating strong concepts through
to a polished execution.

My interests lie in the basics – the principles of proportion, form,
colour and texture – balancing their symbolic and emotive potential,
to create communicative and successful designs that fundamentally
meet the end-users’ requirements, whether two or three-dimensionally.

Fluent in AutoCAD, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, freehand and
technical drawing, extensive FF+E / supplier knowledge, as well as
a proficient understanding of numerous production and manufacturing
processes within the interior, furniture, print and digital graphics industries.
