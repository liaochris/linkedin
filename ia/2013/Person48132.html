Geralyn Humphrey's Publications

  • Jersey Women Mean Business - Big Bold Business (TM) Advice from New Jersey Women Business Owners

    • Woodpecker Press, LLC
    • June 14, 2012
    Authors: Geralyn Humphrey, Joyce Restaino

    This book gathers the best business advice and tips from 72 New Jersey women business owners in areas such as finance, legal, marketing, leadership, management, sales, personnel, professional development, technology, systems and production and work-life balance. I contributed Chapter 6 - "Prenups - Not Just For Marriages", about "business prenups". A business prenup (a/k/a a partnership agreement, operating agreement, shareholder agreement or buy-sell agreement) is an agreement among the owners of a business concerning how the business will be set up and operated, as well as dealing with "what ifs", such as the death or disability of an owner, and various exit strategies for the owners.
