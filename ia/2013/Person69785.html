I follow-up in a systematized manner with potential sellers and buyers of land who didn't tell me to kiss off the first time.

Lyricist

Vagabond's Rose (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Music industry

January 1996 – Present (17 years 10 months)

I've been writing since I was a 12 year-old (over 30 years)...mostly lyrics to melodies in my head. My folks took me and my brother to little country churches where we mainly sang old hymns and I fell asleep while my Dad preached. Some of those stuck with me and I can still sing my favorites today.

Harry Chapin poured his sad song, "Cats in the Cradle", out of the little green radio that my mother kept tuned to the local AM station, WWNS (We're Where Nature Smiles). Mama explained what "silver spoons" and "man in the moon meant" and why the cat was in the cradle. My Dad had a handful of eight-track tapes that were kept in his office at home. Once was an Elvis gospel album. I continually would rewind "I've Got Confidence" and "Thing Called Love" from my favorite recording of his. For Christmas in 1976, I was given Steve Miller's, "Fly Like an Eagle." All of these songs just moved me...and that's what still matters to me when it comes to music. Although, I like the way Rick James grooves too.

"Vagabond's Rose" is just a name I thought sounded cool and I imagined the "Vagabond" wandering from town to town wearing a faded red rose on his ragged tweed jacket's lapel all the while looking for contentment or peace or something that he can't quite define...God, maybe?

Until I get some of them recorded, I decided to share a few of the lyric sheets on Scribd.com just to try and get feedback. It's been humbling that folks would actually follow me on there based on lyrics with no melodies or music at all.

I've also created a Zazzle store with some print-on-demand items related to my lyrics: http://www.zazzle.com/vagabondsrose.

I just read an article related to the state of mind the author is in when he writes as being between "a mystic and madness." This pretty much sums it up.

Contact me at john@vagabondsrose.com or 678-386-4694.

I dabble in Digital Photography of autos..mostly Corvettes. My portfolio is on Behance and I have some items on Zazzle as well.
