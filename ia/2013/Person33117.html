Doug CohenMiller's Summary

I am Doug CohenMiller, a graphic designer and communications professional living and working in San Antonio, Texas. I currently work as the in-house designer and social media specialist at the International Trade Center at UTSA and do freelance design work for small businesses, non-profits as well as arts and cultural organizations. Outside work I am interested in my family, sustainable technology, the future of transportation, social justice and fiction.

I was a Big(D)esign Conference attendee in 2010 and 2011. In 2012 I made the move to conference presenter, when I delivered a presentation on "Creating Happiness as an In-House Designer." In 2011 and 2012 I was selected to be an attendee for TEDxSanAntonio.

I am versed in print and standards-based web design, as well as email design, and am particularly focused on identity and brand development. I develop clean, clear and consistent communication and manange my projects from concept to delivery of the final product.

Specialties

Graphic Design for print and web and email. Expert or near expert level knowledge of industry standard design software and standards. Knowledge of CSS, xHTML, and some CMSs. Deep understanding of design principles and theory as well as practical, day-to-day productivity.
