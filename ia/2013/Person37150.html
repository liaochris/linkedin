Doug Everitt's Summary

Helping make the extraordinary OLLI West program work for University of Denver with both technology and human solutions. The smartest and most enjoyable "retired" people I'll likely ever find employment with!

Serving up Mac OS and iOS solutions for Creative Professionals in Denver, Boulder, and around Colorado since 1989. Nearly all new clients come through referrals from satisfied customers.

Co-founder and Host of the Rocky Mountain West Conference (g rockies) on The WELL (well.com) 1994-1998. Social networking online since the 1980s, before it was called that.

Specialties: Mac OS system support, iPad & iPhone suport, cross-platform network integration, wireless network security, equipment purchase recommendation, migration to new systems, Mac OS X Server support, general IT consulting, and making software and devices "play nicely" together.
