Chip Scholz's Summary

Scholz and Associates, Inc. is a top tier leadership development company, sought out for the quality and measurable outcomes of its services.

Chip Scholz is Head Coach of Scholz and Associates, Inc. He is a nationally recognized executive coach, public speaker and author. He is a Certified Business Coach and works with CEO’s, business owners and sales professionals across North America. He has written for a number of business and trade publications, and “The Scholz Report”, a monthly e-newsletter produced by Chip, is distributed broadly. His first book project “Masterminds Unleashed: Selling for Geniuses” was released in 2009. His next book, "Do Eagles Just Wing it" is available now.

A strong supporter of the community, he has served as Chairman of the Board of four chambers of commerce including the Lake Norman Chamber of Commerce in 2003. He has been on the board and in leadership capacities in several other civic and charitable groups. In addition, he has held advisory positions in state, county and local government units, including chairmanship of the Los Angeles County Quality and Productivity Commission.

Chip currently serves as the Education Chair on the board of the International Warehouse Logistics Association in Chicago.
