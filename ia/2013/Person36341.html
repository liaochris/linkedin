Julie Reardon's Summary

As a physician and medical consultant, my specialty is looking at the whole health picture of an individual or an organization. I integrate cultural and sociological factors with both traditional wisdom and modern science to design solutions.

As a medical coach and advocate, I work to empower my clients - whether individuals or my fellow physicians - with the best health possible, while using common sense and sound experience.

I also consult with entrepreneurs and organizations as a integrative medical subject matter expert.
