John B. Marek's Summary

John B. Marek is a North Carolina-based economic development and marketing professional specializing in place branding and business retention, expansion and attraction for micropolitan and rural communities. He brings more than 25 years experience in the industrial, retail, technology and public sectors to the planning and deployment of economic development strategies, and equips communities with the tools to create meaningful, positive and sustainable change.

Since 2007, John has served as Director of Business Retention/Expansion and Marketing for Statesville Regional Development (SRD), a public-private partnership driving economic growth for Statesville, Troutman and Iredell County, North Carolina -- a Charlotte USA community. Over those years, John has sustained and built upon the region’s long-standing tradition of excellence in economic development, earning Site Selection Magazine’s #1 Micropolitan ranking for new and expanding industry in 2007, 2009, 2010 and 2012, and being named to the publication’s list of Best Economic Development Organizations in 2009 and 2012.

John also provides customized corporate training and consulting services in the areas of strategic marketing, business development and Web 2.0 to organizations across the region through ThunkTank Corporate Training, a joint initiative of Statesville Regional Development and Mitchell Community College.

In addition to two well-received books on marketing and business development, 7 Secrets of Successful Neighborhood Marketing (2006), and Breakfast at Midway: A Lean Marketing Journey (2009), John has published a variety of professional articles, white papers, instructional guides and ebooks on topics ranging from Internet marketing for economic developers to creating quick and easy custom maps using Publisher. Many of these publications can be downloaded from the WRITING page on John's website, www.johnbmarek.com.
