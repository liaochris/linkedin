Lisa Longest's Summary

Lisa is an award winning, full time REALTOR, in the Stevensville MD area who has been making her client's dreams a reality for over 10 years. She doesn't look at what she does as just selling homes in Stevensville, Chester, Grasonville, Queenstown, Centreville, Denton, Ridgely, Annapolis and other nearby cities. Lisa helps people make decisions that will change their lives for the better. Houses aren't just products to her. They are the four walls that hold the people and the memories we love the most. Houses are where we build our lives, entertain friends, and care for the family. For years now, she has helped many people make life-changing decisions about where to live. In turn, it's changed her life. That's why she loves being a Realtor.

Lisa earned her Real Estate License in 2003. Since then, she has worked with and learned from some of the industry's top agents and coaches to enhance her understanding of the market and all aspects of real estate. As a Realtor, she brings special tools and solutions that exceed the expectations of her clients. Lisa uses the latest internet technologies to market and find homes at an expert level. Lisa is a talented contract negotiator who relies on market analysis of housing trends together with a keen understanding of each community’s unique characteristics. She is certified to handle both sides of short sales and pre-foreclosure sales.
