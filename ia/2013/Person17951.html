Brigitt Thompson's Summary

Brigitt Thompson is principal of Brigitt Thompson, LLC, a marketing communications firm serving companies, organizations and individuals. For projects large and small, the goal is the same: to help clients communicate with clarity, consistency and a human touch. Brigitt helps clients clarify and conceptualize the core messages that support their brands. Her ability to tap into and articulate her client’s vision results in marketing products with depth, authenticity and an honest voice.

Clients in a variety of fields, including finance, energy, education and tourism turn to Brigitt for a fresh approach to promoting new products and services. Brigitt writes and edits copy for print and digital media, transforming technical jargon into simple, easy-to-understand language. A marketing professional since 1996, Brigitt’s background is in writing, editing and graphic design.

Brigitt has also been involved in the non-profit sector. For six years, she served as a board member for The Learning Bank of C.O.I.L., an adult literacy organization in Baltimore. She was also a board member of the American Institute of Graphic Arts’ Baltimore chapter for three years, serving as chapter president during her final year.
