Steven Mather's Summary

With over 30 years in the IT industry, Steven Mather has repeatedly participated and led businesses to achieve their technical goals. From development to leading architectural endeavors, his skill and commitment to delivering solutions that met the real needs of the business requirements has been a significant asset. Over 15 years of his experience has been at the level of architecture in a variety of disciplines and as a leader of teams and owner of the solutions. His experience spans the full SDLC from concept to shrink-wrap with a high degree of experience in large integration efforts. This experience has been in a wide variety of industries in both commercial and government organizations. His experience is in managing product delivery and governance using a variety of SDLC methodologies such as waterfall, iterative, RUP, and most importantly scaling Agile, His primary focus is on Agile-Lean practices and principles to foster continuous development and transparency. In this regard, his experience includes multiple positions training and coaching Agile, Scrum, and Agile scaled for the enterprise using SAFe. His goal is to assist businesses to achieve well-governed projects that fulfill their intended business value. He has a high interest in providing these skills at the international level.
