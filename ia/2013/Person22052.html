Charles Butler's Summary

Hi, I'm Charles! I run the Game Design program at NITH, and I'm co-founder of a startup that's trying to make it easier for game studios to handle the hard parts of developing online games.

I'm VERY interested in how games can improve our businesses and our lives (basically gamification, even though misuse has made the term somewhat cringeworthy).
