Chris Sparks' Summary

TO PR PROFESSIONALS & UPPER MANAGEMENT
I am always interested in connecting with top PR professionals & Upper Management to make sure we are providing the best coverage possible for your company & clients.

BUSINESS & REGIONAL PUBLICATIONS
The BusinessMVP.com Network of publications covers every industry. We have regional publications in 750 US Cities as well as Australia, Canada and the UK. Each regional publications has a section for each industry syndicating the content from the corresponding industry publication.

CAREERMVP PUBLICATION NETWORK
Our CareerMVP.com Network has 1,100 career publications that carry jobs, employer information, industry information and advice in 750 US Cities, 64 Provinces in Canada, 30 Cities in the UK, 28 Cities in Australia and numerous other international cities.

LUXURY & EXECUTIVE PUBLICATIONS
Our luxury and executive publication aim to offer information on the most prestigious brands and companies for the most affluent consumers and top executives. Publications include ManueverMagazine, VivaciousMagazine, EloquentMagazine, FerociousMagazine & HerPath.

MUSIC NETWORK
We are greatly expanding our coverage and offerings in the music, film and other entertainment industries. Some of them include RecordingVIP, MusicMVP, CountryMVP, CountryMusicVIP, BarstoolCowboy & GrooveTavern.

Specialties

PUBLIC RELATIONS OPPORTUNITIES

Again, PR professionals and Upper Management should feel free to connect with me.

Press Releases - We add your press releases to our network of publications exactly as they are.

Interviews - We are doing positive interviews with the top executives, recording artists, filmmakers & executives.

Reviews - Product reviews are extremely time consuming. We rarely do them. But, if we love the product, we will provide incredible coverage for it!
