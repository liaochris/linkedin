Inji El Abd's Summary

Economist working in the field of sustainable development; took part in the planning, management and evaluation of a number of development cooperation projects, grassroots projects and government projects.

Worked with programs of the World Bank, the United Nations Development Programme, the Spanish Cooperation, the European Commission and the Egyptian Government and volunteered for NGOs in Egypt as consultant and trainer.

I am familiar with innovative and traditional homegrown solutions in education, health, housing, inclusion and empowerment of marginalized groups, employment and income generation, environmental sustainability; in addition to the use of arts and sports as tools for sustainable development.

During all held posts, I have successfully conducted negotiations resulting in aligning the interests of diverse stakeholders, have introduced new activities and designed innovative fundraising strategies. I have identified new opportunities for the organization, revamped existing structures and systems towards more efficiency, maximized the organization's communication and outreach goals, and cast the organization's network wider by engaging new partners.
