Jason Ziolo's Summary

Jason has over 10 years of experience in the software/contact center field. His engineering acumen combined with a passion for working with customers makes him a natural fit to oversee RiverStar customer operations. Jason and his team are responsible for implementing and supporting RiverStar Studio and the RiverStar Professional Services solutions.

Skilled and educated in software and database design, architecture, and security, Jason worked as a Java/C++ developer with Object Oriented methodologies before joining RiverStar in 2002. Jason has his BS in Computer Science from the University of Illinois, College of Engineering.
