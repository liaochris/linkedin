Adrian Hines' Summary

I thought I was the worst in the Network Marketing/MLM industry because I had been in it for so long with 8 DIFFERENT companies and had no lasting success.

I knew the industry worked because others were having success. My only question was, "Why wasn't I?" I found out there were a number of reasons why I was failing.

I didn't know how to market correctly, I wasn't giving value to my audience, my leadership skills were horrible, and mentally I wasn't tough enough.

That was a harsh dose of reality BUT I allowed it to change my life. Now I am having more success online in the Network Marketing Industry than I EVER had offline.

Now it is my mission to help those that are struggling in network marketing, online marketing, or just marketing their business period to take their business to a global level.

Brand recognition is everything. Allow me to help you.
