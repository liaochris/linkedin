Anna Gallegos' Summary

I am a part-time student in the Master of Communication in Digital Media program at the University of Washington looking for a full-position in journalism or digital media.

I have experience as both a reporter and copy editor at top newspapers; however, my most recent position was in social media marketing. I'm looking for a job that will allow me to combine my background and degree in journalism with my social media and social metrics experience.

Specialties: Copy editing, social media marketing and deadline reporting.

Anna Gallegos' Experience

Social Media Associate

Consumer Media Network

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Online Media industry

August 2012 – May 2013 (10 months) Houston, Texas

I managed the Facebook, Twitter and Google+ accounts for three online education and personal finance blogs. In this position, I worked to promote CMN's blog content by building relationships with similar websites and engaging our audience through Twitter chats, sharing links and answering questions related to financial management.

My duties also included tracking social shares, blog metrics and trends to help the editors better gear their content. I also assisted in moderating comments on CMN's sites.

Houston Aeros/AHL beat reporter

The Overtime Page

October 2011 – May 2013 (1 year 8 months) Houston, Texas

I covered the Houston Aeros for the 2011 - 2012 and 2012 - 2013 seasons before the team relocated to Des Moines, Iowa. Asides from providing post game articles, I live tweeted games and cultivated my own Twitter following to promote my content.

Not only did I interview Minnesota Wild rookies like Charlie Coyle and Jason Zucker for feature articles and videos, but I started my own web series ("On the Other Side of the Ice") that allowed me to interview top AHL players not on the Aeros. The highlight of the series was interviewing Joe Morrow after he was traded to the Dallas Stars/Texas Stars, which was his first interview with the team.

I also assisted with copy editing and uploading content to theovertimepage.com.

Theovertimepage.com is a sports website founded by University of Houston journalism students in 2009 who wanted the opportunity to cover Houston's professional sports teams.

General Assignments Reporter

Oakland Tribune

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Newspapers industry

May 2012 – August 2012 (4 months) Oakland, California

As a general assignments reporter, I had a chance to report on crime, the Asian immigrant population and various community improvement programs. I assisted staff reporters on covering the Occupy Movement and Obama's 2012 trip to Oakland.

The highlight of my time with the Tribune was covering the high-profile Hans Reiser civil trial. My articles has been serialized on the San Jose Mercury News website.

I held this position through the Chips Quinn Scholars Program.

Metro Reporter, Web Producer, Copy Editor

Houston Chronicle

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Newspapers industry

August 2011 – May 2012 (10 months) Houston, Texas

This was an internship facilitated through the Jack J. Valenti School of Communication at the University of Houston. I spent my first semester there on the city desk where I mostly reported and wrote the area crime briefs and weekend features.

During the spring semester, I moved to the Chronicle's web production team and the copy desk. I compiled SEO content for the Newswatch and Hot Topics blogs during peak web traffic hours. On the copy desk, I wrote headlines and cutlines for the metro and national sections of the daily paper.

Editor in Chief

The Venture

May 2011 – May 2012 (1 year 1 month) Houston, Texas

I oversaw a staff of nearly 40 college students to produce a bi-monthly newspaper directed at minority college students throughout the Houston area. My staff included four sections editors who worked with separate sports, broadcast and photography departments.

My duties involved generating and assigning articles; copy editing according to AP style; holding training sessions for new/freshmen reporters; and updating the paper's website on a Wordpress platform.

Copy Editor

Atlanta Journal-Constitution

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Newspapers industry

June 2011 – August 2011 (3 months) Greater Atlanta Area

I worked on the universal copy desk writing headlines and cutlines for the metro, national, business and lifestyles section. I assisted in laying out pages for the weekend lifestyles section.
