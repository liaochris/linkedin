Mai Tran's Summary

Mai has an extensive background in business and event marketing for high-tech corporations, non-profits, and athletic entertainment clients, including Adobe Systems, Boost Mobile, Diageo, John Legend, KODAK, Microsoft, Nike, NBA New York Knicks, NBA Portland Trail Blazers, The Roots, and Symantec Corp. As the owner of CRAVE Portland and the founder/principal of /rouz/ mar•ket•ing, Mai helped drive a modern marketing, events and public relations agency dedicated to innovatively connecting small business owners with the customers they crave.
