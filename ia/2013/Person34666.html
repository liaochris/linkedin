derek burgess' Summary

When asked what I do; I tell people two things, hacker & painter. Really I do many things that involve working with my hands and my mind in unison. Everything from; fine arts, applied design, to technology, mechanics and agriculture.

I see myself as a post-materialist and creative problem solver, using recycled & discarded materials to create authentic experiences. I believe artistic expression begins with the desired experience and should consider life-span & location. I enjoy working with elements like texture, repetition and color and I believe design can be broken down into two principles, balance & contrast, the rest are ancillary.

60% of my time as a design contractor in software architecture & interface / interaction design.
I've been a curious user of hardware and software all my life. From tweaking settings to inspecting every button and function. I'm an artist and a huge nerd, so aesthetics are important but I enjoy dabbling in code and understand the structure, this helps me communicate well with engineers.

20% of my time is spent designing / building furniture for a personal collection & study. theme: recycled material & objects repurposed into unique functional furniture. Sometimes this leads to doing it for clients.

20% of my time I can be found instructing young adults on understanding the web;including structure, presentation, and minor behavior.

Specialties: creative / art direction, web & software design, user interface, interaction, developer, product design, arduino, furniture design, photography, cinematography, studio arts.

Languages: HTML, CSS, Javascript & jQuery
