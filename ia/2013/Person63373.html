Johan Farid Khairuddin's Summary

From 1999-2009, was Southeast Asia's most experienced strategic youth business & media development experts. Frequently traveled to work across the globe with Asia's leading satellite broadcasting company (The Astro All Asia Networks plc) as their Head of Regional Youth Projects.

Main role with Astro was to enhance brand positioning & enhance profitability amongst the youths through all group owned cross-media platforms, projects & business units-- worldwide.

Highlights of my 10 year tenure at Astro include speaking on Youth CSR & ICTs at the UN Global Youth Forum in Switzerland (Sept 07), the UN Meets Web 2.0 Conference in New York (March 08), the European Alternative Channel TV Forum in Barcelona (April 08), the World Congress for ICT (May 08) in Kuala Lumpur, the 1st World Radio Broadcasters Meeting (June 08) in Istabul, Turkey, the UN Global Youth Forum in Armenia (Oct 08), the 2nd Annual Middle East Corporate Social Responsibility Forum in Dubai (Nov 08) and the Annual General Meeting for the Committee for ASEAN Youth Cooperation in Manila, Philippines (Dec 08).

I was awarded by the United Nations the role of Youth Ambassador for the Asia Pacific region. Within Asia, I was also appointed e-Ambassador to the Committee for ASEAN Youth Cooperation (CAYC). And in Malaysia, I was the Youth Ambassador (CDC) for the Ministry of Youth & Sports-- in 2007-2008.

As a veteran Radio & TV Show Host with more than 12 years experience in the entertainment and broadcasting industries; my still current hands-on and award winning work in all media including my role in music as a singer, songwriter & producer have given me an international following of fans which continue to expand my own network of global & influential decision makers.

I hold an MBA with a fellowship honor (MBA F.Inst BA) from the Trinity College & University in the United Kingdom, a Diploma in Aviation from the Australian Wings Academy, Gold Coast and an Airline Transport Pilot License.

I love airplanes.
