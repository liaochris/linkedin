Diana Lynn Carter's Experience

Songwriter, Playwright and Producer

Carter House Productions

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Music industry

August 2005 – Present (8 years 1 month) San Luis Obispo, California

Carter House Productions creates original songs, music compositions, music videos, & musical plays for entertainment & education. www.slosong.com. Since 2005, we have produced media & music for broadcasting & entertainment including: commercials, TV shows, children's music, jingles, music compositions for broadcasting & theater,voiceovers & narration, & web video. However, Carter House Productions is now narrowing our focus. We are creating a new series of children's songs, plays, music videos, & contemporary music.

Owner Diana Lynn Carter is an award-winning writer and producer, a professional vocalist/musician & songwriter, and an experienced performing arts teacher. She founded Edna Valley Music, educational music, in 1997 which evolved into Carter House Productions. Carter is the author of three children's music albums: "Sing and Learn with Diana," "Exploring Our World" by Brad and Diana, and "Just Be Yourself," by Diana, and several plays. On July 22, 2012 she released a contemporary folk-rock album, "Politics and Fairy Tales."
A professional writer for 32 years, Carter was on the journalism department lecture pool at Cal Poly from 2005 to 2007. *She has also taught performing arts classes (music, theater and dance) to children in schools and at performing arts studios since 1996 and has presented staff development workshops for teachers, "Integrating the Performing Arts Into Science & Social Science" through Cal Poly Center for Arts Education, the California Science Teacher's Convention, and various school districts.

Carter's original children's songs have been approved for social content by the California Department of Education. Her children's songs and contemporary music are sold worldwide on iTunes, Amazon.com, and CD Baby. Carter is a former member of Mid-Life Crisis, the band, Carter is a lead vocalist/musician in the band, "Strummingbirds" which plays contemporary folk rock, classic rock, country, and blues.

Performing Arts Teacher

On the California Central Coast

September 1996 – Present (17 years) California Central Coast

Carter has taught performing arts classes, camps, and musical programs in schools, at teacher conferences, and performing arts studios in California. She has taught as a contracted teacher, as an artist in residence, and a musical touring artist through Children's Creative Project.

Creative Services Supervisor

KSBY
