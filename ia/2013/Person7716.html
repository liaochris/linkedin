Angela Lemond, RDN, CSP, LD's Summary

My passion for nutrition started decades ago when I went on my own self-discovery journey.

I was out of shape, tired, run down and facing serious medical problems if I didn’t change. I looked for answers in magazines, books, television programs, and prayer. The answers did not come right away and I felt more confused than ever.

For a period of ten years I experimented with food, feelings and fad-like eating patterns. I finally found solid ground in faith and proper nutrition education. I discovered what my body really needed all along.

During this same time, I fell in love with fitness and outdoor exploration. I found my body really felt good when I was regularly active and doing anything outdoors inspires to move.

I moved from a successful career in marketing to become a dietitian. My passion is in helping adults, children and families understand how they can achieve wellness through a focus on new thinking about food and movement. They are able to take actions that get results through the new thinking.

I love running. Running takes endurance, and so does living a well life. I help others set the pace for their life so the goals that we set together are achieved in a phase-like system. These little changes over time yield maximum impact in health, well-being and quality of life.

Clients, patients and families I work with tell me they are so thankful for my help. I get results because I focus on the following:

- Inspire patients, clients and families to action that lasts

- Relay practical, useable and achievable behaviors

- Put to use my strong clinical knowledge combinedwith easy application in their life

- Separate facts from fiction

- Set the stage for the behavioral aspects for feeding their family now and in the future

Specialties

Pediatric and family nutrition, feeding behaviors, weight loss, heart health, digestive disorders and immunology. Mom blogger!
