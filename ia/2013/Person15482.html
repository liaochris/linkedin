Bob Raynor's Summary

Creative, talented designer, with 10 years experience across a wide range of media. Expertise includes print and web design solutions in the B2B, Advertising, Recruitment, Education and Pharmaceutical markets.

I have a strong desire to work with smart, passionate creatives and to be part of a fun and energetic team where my abilities are put to the test and my ideas are utilized.
