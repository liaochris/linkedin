  • Salvager

    Team Members: Joakim Larsen, Stina Flodström, Lucan Monks, Danijel Piperac, Carl Palacios, Patrik Ånberg, Kx Kx

    The challenge was to make a vertical slice of a tower defense game in just 7 weeks using the Unity Engine. We created a game where, instead of vast landscapes, the player must make their way through a dilapidated hospital over-run with crazed cyborgs. A story would unfold as the player went from room to room, revealing a dark and complex story that you don’t usually get with TD games. We also included a “fort building” mechanic, where the player can drag the furniture around to barricade themselves from the enemy.

    After the project was over, the results were pretty close to how it was expected and we were very pleased with the results.

  • Djungelknuff

    Team Members: Joakim Larsen, Andreas Uhr, Elvira Björkman, Christofer Öhman, Carl Palacios, Malin Svärdsten

    We were three designers and three artists on this project, we wanted to create an experience for more than one person and make use of the touch function of the Ipad.

    We chose to go for a competetive game that can be fun in short burst's. With Lugo in mind we wanted to make it into an adventure at the same time, so the long shape of the "board" was born

  • Alex The Axolotl

    Alex the Axolotl was created as a homage to the classic sidescroller genre. Set in a aquarium, a man made rat maze our androgynous character is trying to get find his/her family.
