John-Luke Peck's Summary

I'm the manager of TerraPower's Information Technology department, helping the company develop and build its IT infrastructure and operations team. As of Q1 of 2013, we're building and deploying the company's first services environment (VMWare-based Active Directory, Exchange, Sharepoint, remote connectivity, and client device deployment), and hiring for the IT Operations team. I also oversee the on-premise HPC environment, and support our clientele.

General cluster management tasks include:

- Overall cluster management (OS, Job management, MPI and IB operations, and vendor relations)
- Evaluation, recommendation, design, and implementation of new hardware (blade server/chassis, storage, Infiniband networking)
- Hardware management (RMA/Returns, acquisition and deployment of new hardware, predominantly blades and chassis)
- OS Deployment/management (deploying new hardware/redeploying nodes, diagnosing/resolving software issues)
- Job/User management (coordinating and managing an HPC user-base of ~100 users and their jobs submitted)
- Error handling (determining the cause of and resolution for errors encountered during jobs both application and MPI related)
- Network management (Infiniband QDR interconnect, GigE management, MPI, IB Subnet management with OpenSM, and network and routing topology)
- Proprietary/Confidential Data handling, backup, and recovery strategies
