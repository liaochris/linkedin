Marc Lucas' Summary

Marc Lucas is a seasoned television executive with over 17 years working on a wide-range of programming including reality series, stage shows and live events. Having worked in almost every area of production as he moved through the ranks, Lucas is well-versed in all aspects of producing from creative development to managing budgets to executing large scaled events. He has a proven track record of consistently, delivering projects that are original, on budget and on time.

Lucas began his entertainment career working in post-production for a number of reality and variety television productions including America’s Next Top Model, Blind Date, JJ the Jet Plane, and UPN’s Manhunt. He worked up the ranks and eventually became an editor where his responsibilities included creative story producing and project management.

From 2005 to 2009 Lucas joined John Cossette Productions as an Associate Producer and was promoted to Executive in Charge of Production and eventually to Supervising Producer, serving as second in command at the Prodco. At JCP, his role involved managing all aspects of production including creative development, union negotiations, talent agreements, show budgets, insurance requirements and supervising schedules to ensure each show stayed on track. As a part of JCP’s team, he executed enormous events such as the Grammy Awards, BET Awards, BET Hip Hop Awards, BET Honors and BET Rip the Runway.

Having established a broad client list consisting of networks and production companies, Lucas continues to bring his vast experience to TV and film projects. He is well-connected within the industry and brings with him a creative mind and a wealth of knowledge about all aspects of production
