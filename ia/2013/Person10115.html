Arvind Rajan's Summary

I am general manager and entrepreneur, and over the years I've worked in a wide range of industries, ranging from hybrid/electric transportation to wireless data networks to the consumer Internet. I've been with LinkedIn since early 2008.

Prior to joining LinkedIn, I spent nine years (5 as CEO) of Grassroots Enterprise, a technology and services firm that built online grassroots marketing and influencer programs for companies, products and causes. Grassroots is now part of Edelman Public Relations.

Specialties: general management, business development, sales and marketing, international expansion, public affairs, viral marketing, word of mouth marketing programs, public relations, government relations, Internet, management consulting, international business

A great idea, strong team, but at the wrong time. We went public in 1997, and thought we were off to the races. Instead, the company went bankrupt in 2000, and the assets were bought by Schlumberger. What probably doomed the company was all of the uncertainty surrounding deregulation of the utility industry. A decade later, the space finally began to take off (e.g., Silver Spring Networks, Trilliant...)

VP, Marketing and Business Development

Solectria Corporation

April 1992 – March 1995 (3 years) Greater Boston Area

In retrospect, probably the greatest job I've ever had, though I didn't fully appreciate that at the time. The company's founders had started the famous MIT Solar Electric Vehicle Team while at school, and had become a 3-person startup in a garage when i joined. I was the "suit" at the company, given that (a) I owned a suit and (b) I had a vast 20 months of working experience.

Over the next three years, we won every electric vehicle competition around, secured millions of dollars of funding from DARPA, were on the cover of the NYT multiple times, and built and sold the Solectria Force, hundreds of which are still driving around today.

We were all in our early 20's at the time, and it was a pretty remarkable experience.
