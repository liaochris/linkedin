Ashton Harrewyn's Summary

Originally from Johannesburg, South Africa, Ashton Harrewyn is a freelance Director of Photography whose clients have included Coca Cola, Sheraton Hotel, the PGA and the University of Vermont.

A graduate of the University of Vermont with a Film and Television minor, Ashton also completed his Professional Certificate in Cinematography at Maine Media College.

Ashton played Division I soccer at the University of Vermont and professionally for the Vermont Voltage. Ashton also has a background in the kitchen at his family’s Chef’s Corner Café and Bakery in Vermont, which brings an interesting spice to his work behind the camera and on a crew.
