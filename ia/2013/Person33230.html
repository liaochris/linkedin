Dean Donaldson's Summary

Marketing strategist. Consumer evangelist. Digital futurologist.

I am a world-renowned global conspiracist who is passionate about driving creative technology to ensure personalised brand engagement progress across all media channels, whilst addressing consumer’s concerns around privacy. I bring consumer insights and meaning to converging media technologies and shine a guiding light for brands to stand out in an increasingly fragmented yet connected media landscape.

An engaging public speaker, I have delivered presentations at over a 100 industry events across more than 30 countries around the world. Evangelising about technological convergence and consumer behaviour, I help organisations to re-think and optimise their marketing strategies with a grounded understanding of current trends.
