John Lankford's Summary

Results are my passion. During the past 25 years in leadership positions, I have become known for developing talent and for implementing systems that result in consistent double and triple digit sales and revenue growth, regardless of the industry or economy.

Before becoming a Master Certified Business Advisor, and recruiting the best team in the business for Premier Development Solutions, my career included executive positions with three Fortune 500 companies.

I have presented nationally at the Disney Institute, the Worldwide Conference on Work Teams and the International Conference for Performance Improvement. As a dynamic presenter, I apply my real-world experience as a national conference keynote speaker, and have designed and implemented executive education and leadership programs with GE, the University of Michigan Business School and the Center for Creative Leadership. I have also shared my knowledge with future business leaders as an adjunct professor.

I have been tapped as an expert business source by prominent media such as The New York Times and CBS and am the author of “Superstar for Life... Career Transitions.” I have been a syndicated columnist for Bay Area Business Magazine and other publications.

I was recognized as the North American Associate Business Coach of the Year from 2007 through 2010 and the 2008 Global Associate Business Coach of the Year.

Specialties

Strategic Planning, Sales Training, Lead Generation/Marketing, Leadership Training, Team Building, Executive Coaching, Executive Talent Development, Master Business Coach, Keynote Speaking, Building Businesses, Succession Planning, Operations, Corporate Accountability Standards, Developing Emerging Leaders.

John Lankford's Experience

Master Business Advisor

Premier Development Solutions

August 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Michigan

Premier Development Solutions is a leading business development consultancy with clients across the country. The firm's core competency centers around lead generation and sales for CEOs. PDS helps corporate executives and business owners develop talent and achieve results for their company.

I created Premier Development Solutions as a natural progression of my drive for results and success. I create a fun and lively approach that motivates others to embrace new ideas, as we emphasize alignment and accountability to help ensure everyone is behind the effort to achieve clearly defined goals.

As a Business Advisor I partner with CEOs and executives who want to be #1 in their industry. My elite clients have a passion for growing their business, preparing an effective succession plan or completing the essential components to have their business ready for sale. They all want and can achieve a business that will run without them.

My work with CEOs to develop a strategic plan is followed by intense focus on execution of that plan. Many mid-size market business owners have the business acumen only to grow their business to a certain revenue level. While strategically putting the correct systems into place, much of our time is spent developing the talent inside a business. Human capital is the best resource and the most underutilized asset in most businesses. We help our clients optimize and develop their human talent and we provide easy to follow systems. The revenue and profit growth always follow.

This economy has transformed many business owners’ focus to just landing more customers. Much of my niche in the last few years has been focused on lead generation (marketing) and converting those leads into sales.

I simply target CEOs who are coachable and excited for me to bring best practices into their company.
