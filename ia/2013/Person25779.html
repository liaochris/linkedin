Clinton Lugert's Summary

I have become a creative hydra. My experience in the advertising and post-production industries as well as working for myself for several years demanded such. Consequently, I function well as a traditional and interactive designer, illustrator, photographer, video editor, copywriter, animator and cinematographer. Thus I stay very informed of new technologies and trends in all creative fields. I've found a balance for all this digital work in getting my hands dirty screen printing, modifying my vintage moped, brewing beer and a myriad other adventures.

I work extremely well independently and under-pressure and rarely need a project manager. The last few years I've worked at a very large agency where I've learned to collaborate as both a leader and member of a team while developing a reputation for being "unflappable". I understand the trust needed in delegation, as well as how to keep the creative process productive.

My current objective is to join a rabidly creative team that is evolving well as our industry shifts. I'd like more ownership of my creative projects and a greater challenge. I continually seek to create effective, award-winning work in and out of my workplace, so the caliber of my team is very important to me.

Lately I've been refining my raw conceptual skills and sharpening my pitch presentation abilities. My capacity for creative thought, perceptive intuition, communication skills, resourcefulness and very diverse skill set are my greatest strengths.
