Janet McSwain, P.E.'s Summary

Your water and wastewater treatment equipment is a major investment to the utilities. To be selected, you have to educate your clients on why your solutions is the best, and to do this you need marketing materials that speak their language.

With over 27 years as a water and wastwater engineer, I can understand your process, its features and applications, and how to translate that into benefits that sell.

Technically Accurate - Yet Persuasive

I will craft the white papers, case studies, web pages, articles (ghost writing), product brochures, video scripts, or blog articles to establish you as an industry leader, and which will convert to more sales.

You need someone who can share your enthusiasm for your equipment, who gets energized from innovation and can translate the highly technical into easy to understand copy - to meet your diverse decision makers. Explaining the complex, and conveying that innovation is one of the things I do best.

Specialties: White papers, case studies/success stories, webpages, product sheets, product brochures - all directed towards the B2B marketing of water and wastewater equipment.
