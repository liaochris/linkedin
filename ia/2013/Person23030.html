Christopher Annunziata's Summary

Since becoming registered with the Georgia Office of Dispute Resolution in 2007, Atlanta Mediator Chris Annunziata has mediated and settled hundreds of personal injury, commercial and family law disputes throughout the Atlanta metro area.

Chris' mediation clients praise his calm and confident demeanor and his ability to gain the trust of both the parties and attorneys. Drawing upon his experience to identify quickly and accurately the critical issues in the case – whether legal, practical or emotional – Chris generates meaningful discussion and moves the parties toward both a better understanding of each other's position and a successful resolution of the dispute.

His peers also respect his skills as a mediator, inviting him to join the Georgia Chapter of the National Academy of Distinguished Neutrals, the nation's premier association of mediation professionals and recommending him for an AV-Preeminent Peer Review Rating from Martindale-Hubbell.

Whether a personal injury action or a complex, high-asset divorce, you can expect Chris to devote his full effort and attention to resolving your mediation in a timely manner.

If you would like to have Chris settle your dispute, you can check his availability and schedule a mediation from the convenient online calendar - http://ckamediation.com/calendar.html. He is available to mediate your case throughout Metro Atlanta. If you have any other questions, please give Chris a call 404.551.3643 or email Chris directly.

Specialties: Civil and Domestic Mediation with experience in the underlying fields of commercial and business litigation, personal injury law, employment law, corporate law, and insurance law.
