Kristen Wheatley | Realtor's Summary

Lewiston - Auburn Maine Realtor

I have worked in the Maine Real Estate market since 2006. Our local market has changed a lot during this time and today's Realtors need to understand and know how to effectively use the latest technology tools and targeted negotiating strategies to MARKET and SELL homes rather than just simply LIST them. As a former technology coach for my previous agency, I have the experience and knowledge necessary to maximize these tools.

I love all things Maine and thoroughly enjoy helping people relocate to our great state as well as helping Mainers move from homes they don't want into homes they do want.

The Maine real estate market is wide open right now, and with historically low interest rates, there is truly an opportunity for every budget!

Licensed to sell real estate in the state of Maine since 2006

Efficiency Maine Real Estate Specialist Designation

Member, Maine Association of Realtors

Member, National Association of Realtors

I have a different philosophy about practicing real estate: I focus 110% of my efforts on delivering the most competent, time and money-saving service. This way I am able to deliver such outstanding results, you'll look forward to working with me again and think of me when your family and friends need a caring and competent Realtor.

If you, or someone you know needs my help, please do not hesitate to call.
