Bobby Ennis' Summary

Over twenty years of Business Development experience working with clients to help them succeed. My expertise is matching services to client needs in a manner that is profitable and essential for both parties.

Currently focused on providing services to individuals and corporations that believe in the power of design, structural brand integration, and understand that it pays to have a third party perspective.

Recent believers include Mattel Toys, SAP, Sony Computer Entertainment, Electronic Arts, UbiSoft, Eidos, Leapfrog, Macrovision, Nike, and Burton Snowboards.

I have a deep desire to represent only the finest products, services, and personal efforts in life.

bennis@nestenvironments.com

Specialties: Branding and identity, Marketing initiatives, Activations, In-store display design, corporate environments, trade show exhibits, consumer packaging design, and other customer facing initiatives.
