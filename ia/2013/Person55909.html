Isaac Udotong's Summary

Mr. Udotong has over 20 years experience as Chairman of Isatong Group of companies. He is a general management level executive thoroughly familiar with producing bottom-line results through directing all functional day-to-day operations, particularly in technology environments. Isaac founded Isatong Enterprises in Nigeria in 1987 as a computer engineering company and was Managing Director of Isatong Nigeria Ltd for 8 years. Isaac moved to the United States in 1995 and worked as Consultant for many Fortune 500 companies such as Harris Corporation, General Electric, Lucent Technologies, Conectiv Energy, EMC, NASA, Ford Motor Company and GlaxoSmithKline in the area of Storage Infrastructure Transformation and Business Development. In his career, he has provided consulting services to Shell Oil, Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation and National Electric Power Authority. In recognition of his leadership, Isatong won Shell Safety Performance Award in 2007. Isaac holds a B.Eng degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Benin, Nigeria.
