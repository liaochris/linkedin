Brad Stewart's Summary

Brad Stewart is the founder and director of Kingdom Warrior (www.KingdomWarrior.net) an organization dedicated to helping churches reach and equip men. Brad works with chaplains, pastors, and leaders, by helping them design, implement, and evaluate an effective ministry to and through men. As part of his products and services, Kingdom Warrior partners with Iron Sharpens Iron (www.IronSharpensIron.net) to produce regional equipping men's conferences in central and western Washington state. He is a frequent speaker and seminar presenter.

Prior to this role, Brad designed instructional materials, taught software courses, and managed project teams.
