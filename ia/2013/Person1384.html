Adam Fendelman's Summary

In a chronological nutshell, here's my professional story...

After being trained as a print news journalist at the University of Missouri-Columbia's School of Journalism, I branched into online tech/business news reporting at MidwestBusiness.com (formerly ePrairie.com). While continuing to write, my role expanded to include editing and managing columnists as well as publishing for third-party associations and organizations.

My career then extended to include co-inventing eXtreme Networking, which provided professional speed networking for businesspeople. I managed the product evolution of the patent-pending eXtreme Networking software.

I've freelanced for Time Out Chicago, the Chicago Sun-Times, etc. I was a theatre critic for Centerstage Chicago (the Chicago Sun-Times Group). I was the exclusive cell phones guide at About.com (the New York Times Company). Most recently, I was the Vice President of Technology at EDC Technology, which creates administration software for universities.

I am currently the founder and publisher of HollywoodChicago.com, which is the largest independently owned and operated daily everything entertainment publication out of Chicago.

Specialties

business development, connected devices, movie reviews, entertainment news, news reporting, news editing, film critic, interviewing, online publishing, newsletter creation, software project management, event and conference planning, Web design, smartphones, social media, QA testing, software upgrades, software integration, technology management
