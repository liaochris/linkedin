Arik Johnson's Experience

Managing Director

Center for Organizational Reconnaissance

June 2009 – Present (4 years 2 months) Madison, Wisconsin Area

Founded in 2009, the Center for Organizational Reconnaissance (COR) is an intelligence R&D lab and policy think tank underwritten by the consultancy, Aurora WDC. Coordinating and facilitating projects, meetings and publications among a worldwide network of associates and business partners, the COR is focused on helping career intelligence officers, analysts and the decision makers they serve in business and government design solutions for their most persistent, widespread and seemingly insoluble intelligence problems.

Founder & Chairman

Aurora WDC

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Research industry

February 1995 – Present (18 years 6 months)

Since 1995, Aurora WDC has been called upon to provide the competitive, market and business intelligence executives need most. We help companies of all kinds understand, anticipate and position to exploit the changes in their industry and markets and solve the business problems confronting their long-range sustainability as well as optimizing short-term performance.
