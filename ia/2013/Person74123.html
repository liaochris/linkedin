Kenneth J. Burke, CPA & FocalPoint Business Coach's Summary

Founder of Ken Burke International, Inc and FocalPoint Coaching of New York.

I am a Certified Business Coach and Certified Public Accountant with over 24 years of broad experience working with hundreds of closely-held corporations and entrepreneurs on business and tax planning matters.

FocalPoint Coaching of New York is powered by Brian Tracy's exclusive coaching process.

I'm interested in working with entrepreneurs and business executive looking to improve their: individual effectiveness as an employee or owner, business performance, business planning process and maximize the value of their business interests.

Specialties

Strategic planning, executive development, business performance improvement, business plan development, sale training and business succession planning.

Ken Burke International, Inc

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

May 2009 – Present (4 years 5 months)

Ken Burke International, Inc is a business coaching and advisory firm. KBI was founded by Kenneth J. Burke, a CPA and Certified FocalPoint Business Coach.

For over 20 years, Ken has advised hundreds of companies on tax, finance, accounting, merger and acquisition strategies, banking and strategic business coaching. In his desire to understand what makes some companies and individuals more success than others patterns began to emerge. He noticed that the most successful companies have similar characteristic, processes and are intensely action orientated.

KBI has taken this experience and insight together with the FocalPoint Business Coaching program to assist any company in their desire to improve their business.
