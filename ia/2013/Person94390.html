Mike Meagher's Summary

Businesses are designed perfectly for the results they are currently getting.

Sagency helps leaders design their businesses to win. Selecting the right people, focusing strategies and building healthy cultures are how businesses win.

Competition for talent is fierce. Sagency helps business owners and leaders get and keep people with the attributes essential to successfully execute their strategies. We focus on the who and the how: hiring the best and getting the best from people.

Talent strategy and business strategy must go hand in hand. We prepare organizations to do what’s best by building their cultures for the results they want, such as growth, innovation or other outcomes. Businesses that “outculture” their competitors will outperform them financially and vastly surmount their own past successes.

All kinds and sizes of businesses profit from our analysis and smart guidance around people, culture and strategy focus. Our direction is supported by proven and unbiased assessment tools that provide science in order to dramatically reduce chance, guesswork and blind spots.
