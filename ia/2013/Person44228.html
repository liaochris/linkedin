Faydra Koenig, MA's Summary

I was divorced three times before I was 40. It is my passion to share the secrets I learned about how to divorce with dignity with men and women who are fed up with crazy exes, emotionally disconnected attorneys and the fears of the divorce unknown. I have worked with hundreds of men and women who thank me everyday for teaching them the tools they needed to get out of their marriages with their money, their minds and their custody in tact. You have questions about finding an attorney, custody, mediation or parenting plans, visitation, financial security during divorce, child support, spousal support, the emotional effects of divorce on children and yourself. I have answers for each and every one of those issues. You need hope and I have it and I can't wait to show you how to come out of the fire and into the life you deserve.
