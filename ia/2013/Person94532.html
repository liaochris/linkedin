Mike Rose's Summary

"The Magic Kinda Guy"

Mike Rose is both a magician and a comedian, just not always simultaneously! The program designed for your event will be very interactive and include people from your company or group integrated into the performance. However, the onstage helpers are treated with respect and none are ever embarrassed or made to look silly. Instead, these volunteers become a highlight of the performance and enjoy being an important part of the show. Not just any show, but a customized comedy magic show that everyone will continue to talk about and compliment you on long after it’s over.

Mike has performed his comedy magic presentations in 25 states and Canada (not on the same day!). In addition to corporate events Mike has performed at the Vice Presidential Mansion for former Vice President Al Gore (on repeat occasions) and at the Presidential Inaugural Dinner for George W. Bush. All this real world experience means your group is guaranteed to have an unforgettable time. Regardless if you need a stage show, a professional MC for your conference, or an intimate sleight of hand performer for your hospitality suite or gathering, you will receive a customized presentation to perfectly complement your event, people and venue.

Regardless if your audience is being engaged with quick wit or watching Mike find a borrowed hundred dollar bill inside a Twinkie, they are guaranteed to be entertained (an entertained audience is a happy audience!). Although your group will be amazed by technically demanding sleight of hand, they may be too busy laughing to realize it. This multi-layering of comedy, magic, and audience interaction is what makes the show so memorable.

The bottom line: when you book Mike Rose for your function you get clean, interactive comedy magic for your sophisticated audience. Not just an experienced, multifaceted entertainer, but a new member of your team dedicated to the success of your event.

Specialties

Improvisational humor infused with sleight of hand magic. Presenting corporate messages within custom designed comedy magic presentations. Making your event memorable!

Mike Rose's Publications

  • Joe Karson - Beyond Zombie

    • Mike Rose Magic
    • January 2000
    Authors: Mike Rose

    At 210 pages, the book is divided into two main sections; Karson's biography and Karson's creations (books, tricks and commentary). It has 26 photos, most not seen in many years. Besides extensive research in New England public records, several people who knew Karson were also interviewed, both magicians and laymen. Some of the well known magic personalities interviewed were; John Calvert, Jim Cellini, Ray & Ann Goulet, Jay Marshall, Sidney Radner, Nick Ruggiero, Les Smith, Tony Spina, Ben Stone, Parker Swan, and many others. The book also features a Foreword by Lance Burton, one of the most famous "Zombie" workers alive.

  • 10 Amazing Card Tricks

    • Mike Rose Magic
    • May 30, 2012
    Authors: Mike Rose

    A new DVD that teaches you card tricks you can do with an ordinary deck of cards. No previous magic knowledge is needed to learn the magic tricks as taught by Mike Rose. The tricks are as entertaining to learn (and watch!) as they are amazing.
