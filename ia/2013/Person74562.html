Kent E. Seton, Esq's Summary

Kent E. Seton, Esq., a former collegiate All American tennis player from USC, has spent the last 14+ years of his legal career involved in representing a blend of clients, charities themselves, corporations engaged in philanthropy, high net worth families, athletes and entertainers engaged in philanthropy.

A partial past and present client list includes the American Red Cross of Greater LA, United Cerebral Palsy of Greater LA, St Vincent Meals On Wheels Foundation, the largest meals and wheels organization in LA County, Woodcraft Rangers, Movember (raised over $120 million last year), the Magic Johnson Foundation, Forest Whitaker, Leeza Gibbons, Pamela Buffet, Snoop Lion (formerly Snoop Dog), Ronny Turiaf, Ian Somerhalder (actor Vampire Diaries), Carlos Boozer, Starkey Hearing Foundation, Kreiss Foundation, Los Angeles Urban League, Prototypes, One Hope Wine Foundation, Beverly Hills Education Foundation and Valley Village.

Specialties

Representing in major national cause marketing campaigns, representing clients with complex related entity matters, hybrid enterprises (i.e. Paul Newman's Own, Tom's Shoes type orgs). Acts as "general counsel" to the LA Mission and the Starkey Hearing Foundation.

Seton Law Firm is located in Beverly Hills, California with another office in Irvine, California.
