Adam Mignanelli's Projects

  • Meet The Pros Speaker Series featuring Adam G. Mignanelli

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Adam Mignanelli

    Join the Art Directors Club and SMH magazine in welcoming curatorial maestro, Adam G. Mignanelli

    Meet the PROS is a speaker series organized by the students of the Design Club at City Tech. With the help of some of the professors of the Advertising Design department, and some networking of their own, the students have invited speakers in various design fields including editorial, digital, and advertising. The series was launched on October, 2009 with the purpose of giving the design students at City Tech a chance to meet people currently in the field. The speakers would share their experiences and suggestions on what students should be doing while in school to make it in the field as well as give them some insight on strategies and methods used in the field.
    Description
    Follow us on Twitter: http://twitter.com/#!/MeethePROS
    And we are also on Tumblr: http://meetthepros.tumblr.com/

    Adam was born and raised in Providence, Rhode Island. He graduated from Parsons The New School for Design Manhattan, and studied at Central Saint Martins College of Art and Design in London. He interned with several magazines in New York City, then worked for companies such as Roadrunner Records, Cubanica Design Studio, Zoo York, SPIN Magazine, VICE Media and their internal global ad agency Virtue Worldwide. This involved top brands such as Nike, Intel, Vodafone, Warner Brothers. Currently he is the Creative Services Manager for DIESEL, USA. Adam founded the Art and Design blog Ballast NYC, which gave birth to his independent curatorial initiative Ballast Projects, showcasing top up-and-coming contemporary artists in galleries and pop-up spaces. We bring you Art Director and Curator, Adam G. Mignanelli.
