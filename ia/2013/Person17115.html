Brett Seyler's Summary

My background is in mathematics and finance, but I've been in the games industry since 2006 in a variety of roles, from strategy / BD to development management and operations. I joined GarageGames when it was just a couple dozen people. It later grew to well over 100 employees, and we sold a majority stake to IAC. I joined Unity at a similarly early stage, and we grew the company's valuation more than 600% in <2 yrs, and raised $12M from several firms, including Sequoia Capital, to fuel new growth initiatives.

Now, I spend most of my time working with a handful of companies that I've invested in, primarily in games and related tech. I'm an active angel investor and love helping entrepreneurs any way I can.
