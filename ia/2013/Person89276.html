Matt Holden's Summary

My first experience with game design came at eight years old - armed with a stenographer's pad, I set out to write a sequel to the NES title 'Metal Gear'. Konami beat me to it, but the passion never died. Two decades later, I am co-owner of Darkened Sky Studios, and the driving force behind our Gemini engine, a J2EE/XML client/server object framework that borders on being its own scripting language.

I am a passionate, driven individual who believes that a day starts when you get up and ends when the job is done - leading me to have lived some significantly longer than 24 hour days! Having been a coder for so long, I truly can say I'm one of those who "would do it for free" - I have!

Specialties: Java/J2EE, C / C++, JavaScript, PHP, HTML5
SQL (Microsoft SQL Server, PostgreSQL, mySQL, hSQLdb)

Outside of the computer world, I'm nationally certified in retail sales, enjoy writing everything from technical manuals to fantasy novels, and speak proficient Spanish.
