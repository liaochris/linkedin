Amy Mauro's Summary

I'm an experienced web editor and producer who has worked with major brands like Rodale, Biography.com, iVillage, Readers Digest, and Lifetime.

I am currently accepting freelance writing assignments and content curation projects. Most recently I co-managed social media initiatives for a YouTube channel for Rodale and BermanBraun.

As an editor, I've overseen entire channels and verticals, planned and implemented editorial calendars, and worked on multiple site redesigns.

As a content producer, I've mastered various CMS to create engaging content (quizzes, photo galleries, slideshows, polls, articles, and channel pages). I've also managed production of several e-newsletters to support traffic goals.

My writing appears on ReadersDigest.com, Yahoo! Shine, iVillage.com, Biography.com, Lifetime.com, and on my personal blog, champagnebdays.com.

Specialties: Writing, Editing, Copyediting, CMS Content Production, Copywriting, Curation/Aggregation, SEO Writing, e-Newsletters, Website Redesigns, Video Metadata, Social Media Management, Image-Driven Social Networks (Pinterest/Instagram)
