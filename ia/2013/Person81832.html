Cheryl Brown's Summary

LIVED2TELL - FOUNDER & CEO

Cheryl is recognized for her life, challenges and leadership with helping, motivating and encouraging cancer patient; her creative coaching skills, and most importantly her inspiring testimony. She is full of life and has such a joyful countenance; a very energetic spirit and a perpetual smile along with a heart of giving.
