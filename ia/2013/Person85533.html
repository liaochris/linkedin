Margaret Chind's Summary

Using the avenue of my blog Creative Madness Mama (www.CreativeMadnessMama.com) I review Christian Fiction, some non-Fiction, and quality General Market Fiction. As a member of CFBA, Christian Science Fiction & Fantasy Blog Alliance, and as an Assistant Director and member of FIRST Wild Card Blog Tours I post blurbs, reviews, and other information of up and coming Christian Fiction titles to share with readers what is available.

As of this time I have worked with Glass Roads PR, Waterbrook-Multnomah, Thomas Nelson, Simon & Schuster-Howard Books, Zondervan, Bethany House, Revell Books, Source Books, Barbour Books, MIRA, Harvest House, Hatchette Book Group USA, Whitaker House, Tyndale, Random House, Sheaf House, B&H, Kregel, Penance Publishing, TBBMedia, and others.

Since the pregnancy and birth of my daughters I have also opened my blog up to more types of reviews including maternity products, breastfeeding suggestions, cloth diapers, babywearing themes, and more. As a stay-at-home mama and aunt to eight children under the age of nine I have a bit of experience that allows me to be rather opinionated and I love helping others find the latest and greatest.
