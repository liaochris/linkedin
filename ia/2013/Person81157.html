Lindsay Shugerman's Projects

  • SEO Friendly Library of Content

    Team Members: Lindsay Shugerman, David Tovar

    The GNeil brand website (www.gneil.com) included a significant amount of article content. Unfortunately, several barriers prevented it from helping with site SEO. It was accessible only though a drop-down, it required user registration to view the content, the content was not listed on nor linked to the site map nor product pages. There was also a significant problem with duplicate content within the library and between the library and other site pages. Metadata was identical for all or most articles. A major overhaul was clearly needed.

    But there were plans to replace the entire platform within two years, so the company was unwilling to spend a significant amount for a redesign in the meantime.

    Working with IT and another staff member, we created a new structure and architecture for the library, allowing for search and linking. We also replaced the drop-down and registration barriers to spider discovery (improving UX in the process as well.) I created a standard for content, and all articles were reviewed, unique meta-data was created for each article, and all duplicates were either removed or rewritten.

    The new library, now accessible from the home page, well linked into product pages, external sites and of course the site map, resulted in the website placing in positions 1-3 for over 200 critical keywords they had been unable to place on page 1 using the old site content. The only cost for the redesign was approximately 60 hours of professional-level staff hours and an additional 50 hours of clerical-level employee time.

  • SEO Presenter at South Florida Code Camp

    Team Members: Lindsay Shugerman
