Lloyd Medley's Summary

My name is Lloyd Medley, owner of Penarth WebSol and PT Batteries and co owner of Barber Blades and Commission Cloud.

I left school in 2007 after completing my A Levels to work on Reward Xtreme Ltd, a popular network of cashback websites, along side Matthew Brice. After successfully building the company up we sold the websites and set up Commission Cloud Ltd, an affiliate network specialising in lead gen.

In 2010 we set up Barber Blades Ltd, along with Daniel Moore. Barber Blades specialises in providing modern barbers with all the equipment and supplies they require, and is growing rapidly. Further to the products we sell, we are also the organisers of Barber Connect - the first and only UK Barber exhibition.

In 2013 I set up PT Batteries Ltd, importing and distributing after-market power tool batteries. PT Batteries is supplies replacement batteries that perform just as well as the originals for a fraction of the price.

Along side all this I also run Penarth WebSol, helping local companies upgrade their office computer set up, optimising their network setup and file systems and helping them gain an online presence.
