Karsten Rost's Education

Handelshøjskolen i Århus

Master, The Master of Science Programme (M.Sc), Business Administration and Information Science

2002 – 2005

The master thesis analyses national culture issues involving enterprises investing in global ERP packages with a multi-site ERP setup. These global multi-site ERP setups are normally based on a model company which represents a common set of functionality for all the business units. Usually, this model contains the technical platform, business strategy, software configuration, and management execution.

Multi-site ERP implementations are normally regardless of national cultures implemented in the entire enterprise. This means that among the four different levels, national cultural issues arise at the management execution level. Furthermore, this indicates that the only way to handle national cultures in global multi-site ERP implementation is to include flexibility at the management execution level.

Link: http://www.hba.dk/digital/open/135258.pdf
