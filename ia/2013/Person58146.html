Jandt Tours' Summary

For almost ten years, J and T Tours has been dedicated to providing travelers with an experience of a lifetime...one filled with excitement, comfort, luxury, and great memories. Join those who have chosen J and T Tours each year as their travel expert and experience your trip of a lifetime. In addition to excursions to visit the archaeological sites throughout Egypt, Jordan, Greece, and the Holy lands, our activities cover all tourism fields: incoming, outgoing, transportation, assistance at the airports, and privately escorted groups. Our programs are designed and organized to meet your needs and suitable for all ages, budgets, and categories. We invite you to discover the magic of Egypt ~~ the land where all time began.

Specialties

J and T Tours is an American travel agency specializing in individual and group tours to Egypt and Worldwide. With over fifty (50) packages from which to choose, our tours offer the traveler a wide variety of choices, interesting itineraries with flexible pacing, special values, and fine quality. We offer a wide variety of programs which are designed to satisfy all your hopes and wishes. With J & T Tours your dream trip can become reality
