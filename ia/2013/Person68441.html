Joris De Groot's Summary

Specialties: People Management, Digital Marketing and strategy, Usability, Project-Process-Product management, Analysis.

I've got a technical background and combine that with an in depth knowledge of digital marketing, digital strategies and people skills. A strong communicator and open for new ideas.

I started out as a software developer and around 2006, I rolled into the booming iDTV business. After a while I became responsible as a tech lead and swiftly I started as a technical project manager. I got a training in Prince2 principles and became certified.
I became more experienced in Project Management and managed many projects with success.
For a change of environment and a new challenge I decided that I wanted to train foreign students in the art of media and digital during a 7 month stint in South-Africa.
I became the digital expert/trainer at Belgium Campus in South-Africa.
After 7 amazing months, I got to have fun as a freelance digital marketing expert and project manager in Belgium at agency.com/TBWA group Brussels.
2 years later, I found myself at the amazing These Days digital agency, where I managed a large project at the client side, from project management, to process and product management, and as a business developer and digital marketing expert.
Now I'm back at the Cronos group, working for Calibrate and other groups, leading digital projects for the benefit of our customers, while covering all possible aspects of a digital project.

During my career, I've worked for clients like The Belgian National Lottery, Telenet, Belgacom, Sony, KBC, MTV, VRT, RTBF, SBS, Alken-Maes and many others. Visit silversource.be for some examples.
