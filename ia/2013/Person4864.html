Allen Madsen's Summary

I don't mind hearing about positions from recruiters, that's how I got my current job. There are a lot of new companies popping up all the time. Here are a couple caveats for getting me to respond to you:

* You should include the name of the company you are recruiting for.
* You should include the description of the position.
* Do not call me or send a connect request. Either send me an email or a message on LinkedIn.
* The position should be in Boston, MA or very near by.

My goal here is to evaluate whether I would even consider the position you're contacting me about. If you don't do these I will mark you as spam, because you are wasting my time.

What might I find interesting?

Companies that provide services to other developers. Companies whose product is the software. Companies with a focus towards business to consumer rather than business to business. Small companies (< 100). Companies that deal with a lot of data. Companies that produce a lot of open source.

A healthy mix of any those would be pretty interesting.
