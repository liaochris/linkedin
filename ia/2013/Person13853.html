Betty Taylor's Summary

Betty Taylor Design (hey, that's me!) offers creative graphic design thoughtfully crafted to communicate to your audience.

Project management and problem solving skills make me a natural at handling detailed, complex projects. Technical expertise, knowledge of effective copy writing and proofreading, and a passion for detail and organization round out my full-service approach. I enjoy working with a variety of design styles to best fit the needs of each project.

I design effective pieces that help my clients look good: to their audience (and to their bosses).
