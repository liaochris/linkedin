Barry Smith's Summary

Having been a successful entrepreneur and corporate executive, Barry is now focusing his efforts on coaching and training that increases the triple bottom line (people, profits, and planet). Leadership development programs targeting Adult Development (increasing mental capacities), Spiritual Intelligence (wisdom, compassion, equanimity), and Cultural Literacy for Religions (appreciating and understanding differences) are necessary for the increasing performance of leaders, teams, and organizations who operate in a global context. Coaching and training for religious diversity, spirituality, and transformational leadership are the workplace trends of the future. The conscious capitalism movement predicted in the book Megatrends 2010 and seen in many top performing companies is today's reality for progressive organizations. (See Conscious Capitalism by John Mackey and Raj Sisodia)

Using Ken Wilber's Integral Theory as a framework for coaching, training and consulting, Barry helps leaders gain a comprehensive understanding of themselves and their organizations. A suite of assessment tools are used based upon the needs of the leader and organization.

In addition to being a corporate executive, entrepreneur, and trainer, Barry has been a college instructor, Christian minister and life counselor. He is known for his interpersonal skills and ability to bring out the best in others. Always reaching high levels of productivity and worker satisfaction through his leadership, he attributes his success to understanding people, helping them grow personally and professionally, and empowering them to reach their stated goals and objectives.
