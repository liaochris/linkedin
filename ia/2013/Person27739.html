Creative India Journey's Summary

Please find the details of Golden Triangle Tour circuit cities as per follows:-

Delhi - Being the capital of India Delhi is well connected with all worldwide major destinations by various International flight connections. One can experience two cultures in Delhi as it is divided in two parts (Old & New Delhi). In Old Delhi you will feel the culture of Muslim Era with many old buildings and monuments and New Delhi experience modern architectures with several World heritage sites. Delhi is dotted with many beautiful historical sites like Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple, Humayun's Tomb, Akshardham Temple, Red Fort (Lal Quila), Jama Masjid, Moti Masjid, Narrow passages of Chandini Chowk (In Old Delhi), Raj Ghat, India Gate, Jantar Mantar, , Parliament & President House, Birla Temple, , Safdarjang's Tomb & Lodhi Garden etc which reflect the glory of the past.

Agra - is the second city to visit after Delhi and was the capital of India once. Agra is situated in Uttar Pradesh (U.P.). The neibouring state of Delhi, kwon for one of the Seven Wonders of the World & one of the three world heritage sites in Agra. Taj Mahal - The symbol of love is located on the banks of river Yamuna, built by the emperor Shah Jahan of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal after her death. The other two heritage sites are Agra Fort & Fatehpur Sikri, and one more monument Itmad-Ud-Daullah (often described as the Little Taj or the Baby Taj or the Draft of Taj Mahal) is the other attraction of Agra.

Jaipur - is the neibouring city of Delhi & Uttarpradesh situated in Rajasthan which plays one of the important role in Indian Tourism industry due to it's rich & heritage culture, Forts, Palaces, Castles, colorful festival, dresses as Amer Fort, Jaigarh Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar, Jal Mahal, and Nahargarh Fort and Chokhi Dhani ethnic village where tourist can enjoy colorful Rajasthani culture with various folk dances, cultural program with Royal Rajasthani cuisines etc.

Specialties

India is an immense country with many unique characteristics and known as another mini world within a world. Among the all Tours Golden Triangle has got a special place. This tour covers three main historical cities of north India which is Delhi, Agra & Jaipur
