Daniel Kim's Summary

I started out in the creative field as an art director in advertising. Since then I've broaden my talents by bringing more interactive & user experience skills in the mix.

My strategy is to really look at the insight/pains of users and make sure the simple communication/use case solution is verified before there's any production work. It's always about the users, and knowing how the communication and design works for them. Then it's just ideation and executions, and my technical skills come in handy.

My ultimate goal is to become the most brilliant creative that can solve any branding communication problems for any agency or company.

Specialties: Creative Think Tank - conceptualizing and idea creation :: Adobe Creative Suite 6 - photoshop, illustrator, indesign, flash, dreamweaver, fireworks, edge, muse, light room, premiere & after effects :: Freehand, Photography, Final Cut Pro, Keynote/Powerpoint, Logic Pro, xhtml/html/css/jQuery, basics on JavaScript, Balsamic, Omnigraffle, Axure, User Experience, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Design Patterns, User Testing (Moderating), Wireframing Lofi/Hifi, Agile production.
