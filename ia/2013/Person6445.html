Andrea Mene''s Summary

Having a strong interest in business at a global level, I got a MSc in International Management in Bocconi, an Italian top university, and meantime I got another MSc in CEMS, ranked # 3 Master's in Management in the world by the Financial Times in 2012, both with excellent grades.

I started to get experience at McKinsey & Company, ranked # 2 in the global "Top 50 Consulting Firms, 2013" by Vault, on a business project to identify the best investment opportunity in the Hungarian electricity market for a foreign European company, in a time of liberalization.

Because of my curiosity for Internet, I moved to NERONERO, with the goal of creating the main Italian OCS (office coffee service) distributorship. I was responsible for market analysis and online marketing promotion.

My passion for Internet and Entrepreneurship led me to Google, where I am a Senior Account Manager for the Italian market, consulting C-level decision makers about online advertising. My focus is on growing SMBs in the Technology / Travel / Retail / B2B verticals, also developing export strategies. I am responsible for
• business planning and review • key account management • cross functional projects

In my free time, I am a lecturer of online marketing at the CMI Institute in Ireland and the treasurer at the Bocconi Alumni Association in Ireland.

I believe in Ownership and Thinking Fast to drive success.

Specialties:
• Industries: online advertising, internet start-ups, non profit
• Areas: business planning, business analysis and modelling, e-commerce, web marketing, email marketing, social media marketing, loyalty programs, SEO, SEM, affiliation and partnerships, website building, negotiation, team management.
• Products: Search ads, Display ads, Video ads, Mobile ads
• Tools: Office apps, Graphic apps, CRM, Web CMS, Web Analytics, Affiliate apps.
