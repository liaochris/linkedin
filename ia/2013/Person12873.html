Ben Freeman's Summary

A commercial ACA with extensive experience of entrepreneurial businesses including property, consumer products and other complex operational companies gained within blue chip, smaller MBO environments as well as not for profit organisations. Success has been based on strong financial, analytical and strategic skills combined with softer people and leadership skills.

For the last five years I have developed an interim / consultancy career focused on change focused finance roles and assisting with large scale business development opportunities.

FD/CFO roles will focus on improving the MI to give the Board a clear idea of the opportunities (and issues) within the organisation. This initial review may lead to a series of initiatives including cost reduction strategies, cash control, improving processes, encouraging budget ownership etc.

Change focused roles involve working beyond the Finance team but will often start by using the same strategic finance skills to understand the need for change. A core part of these assignments is to act as a catalyst and to generate enthusiasm. It is also important for projects to be properly structured and I am MSP qualified and use Prince 2 methodology. I use broader OD skills to accommodate the people and cultural issues associated with strategic change projects.

I have a love of ideas, creative thinking and people with ideas which has led to being involved with a succession of business development opportunities. In these situations, I use my CF and Treasury experience to create business plans, investment summaries and financial models. I also focus on risks and seek to due diligence the supporting assumptions to support fund raising.

Interested in commercial roles using these skills and can be contacted on 07958 344436 or at ben@makechangehappen.co.uk
