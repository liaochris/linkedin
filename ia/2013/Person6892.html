Andrew Fearnley's Summary

Andrew is a Graphic Designer and sole proprietor of Applecross Design, a Vancouver based firm specializing in identity and information design. Working in the industry full time since 1995, Andrew has broad experience with clients in many sectors including light industrial & manufacturing, telecom & hi-tech, entertainment & hospitality, real-estate, architecture & construction and public & media relations.

Before establishing Applecross Design in 1998, Andrew held positions of increasing responsibility, with Toronto and Vancouver design firms including ComputerTime™, (a print production service bureau), Columbus Group Communications Inc. (now part of TELUS Enterprise Solutions), and Timm Williams Design. He is an alumnus of both the University of Guelph, Ontario (B.A.) and Emily Carr Institute of Art and Design, Vancouver (B.Design), and holds a Certificate in Small Business Development from the Association of Self-Employment Developers of Ontario.

Specialties

Information design, corporate identity, customer and corporate communications, research, writing, photography, film output, 4-color process, pre-press, storyboarding, XML, HTML, JavaScript, usability, CSS, WYSIWYG layout tools
