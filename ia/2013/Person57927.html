Jamie Allan's Summary

My passion centres around connecting Businesses and Sales and Marketing. Through the eighties I provided sales and marketing expertise to selected Audi/VW dealers in their local markets increasing their sales by 20% to 30% year on year. Through the nineties it was IT software solutions and won contracts to small and large organisations with values up to £1 million. In the 2000's I helped grow new and existing businesses - the largest contract being a £3 million framework with a leading Utility.

Historically, local marketing is a particular strength of mine as it combines all the elements of the marketing mix – get that right and you have a blueprint to market for any size of organisation. Recently I have helped grow businesses in London, the South-East & East Anglia especially Norwich and my focus with any client is to make things happen. I like to get under the skin of a company and come up with new and different ways to "break through the barrier" and get more business. With business being tough; and getting tougher, being different and having the right people is critical for success.

I recently helped set up ZENNYO an online business community where you can share experiences, make new contacts and help others in your local area.
