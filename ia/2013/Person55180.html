Elizabeth Todd's Summary

Elizabeth is the Founder and CEO of Income Trigger Consulting, a Social Media Management and Business Consulting Company specializing in reputation management. She helps business owners monitor and protect their brand reputation by keeping a watching brief and listening to what’s being said about them across the social channels. She helps them take actions that will ensure that the positive images always outshine anything negative.

Elizabeth started her career as an Air Traffic Controller in the Dutch Air Force where she learned to think on her feet, apply lateral thinking at the drop of a hat and, through work with NATO, learned to communicate with people of all ranks from many countries.

On leaving the Air Force Elizabeth moved to Australia where she resumed her business and computer science studies and entered the corporate world in Banking and Finance, and subsequently in other industries such as IT and manufacturing. This career path saw her taking on senior management responsibilities including involvement with development and installation of major financial and CRM systems.

After Elizabeth left the corporate world she started her own business. The first venture was to build and develop a vineyard with guest accommodation in the Hunter Valley. This provided a great opportunity to get hands on experience in all facets of starting and building a successful small business.

During that time Social Media made rapid advances and learning how to build strong and integrated strategies for her business became essential. Working with some of the top names in Internet Marketing allowed her to implement tactics that successfully connect her brands with the social media buzz.

She is passionate about sharing her experience and created Income Trigger Consulting to this end. Through her consulting services, she is able to provide the support needed by online and offline businesses.

Specialties: Social Media Reputation Management, Small Business Coach
