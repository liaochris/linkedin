Easter Becker-Smith's Summary

Easter Becker-Smith provides coaching for individuals, groups and teams, including interview coaching, leadership and life coaching. She has 25 years of highly successful business and leadership experience. Her track record as a valued and highly regarded leader, known for continuously developing her people, demonstrates the expertise she brings to her coaching services. Easter maintains a focus on life coaching as well as coaching for development. Her years in executive corporate sales and operations gives her complete understanding about what it takes to keep people motivated and moving forward, even in challenging circumstances. Her executive positions have been held in various retail companies including Boudin Bakery Café, David’s Bridal and Pearle Vision.

Easter Becker-Smith has worked within a variety of organizational contexts. She has managed upwards of 178 stores across large regions in the USA and has successfully moved her regions through multiple initiatives, such as increasing sales exponentially, reducing costs, and bringing creative new products to market. She always sees beyond the horizon and helps teams analyze issues and behaviors so that they find the most efficient way to accomplish key strategies and goals.

Perhaps her best known strengths beyond coaching are in training and developing managers and their own teams. She is both practical and supportive, and has always been acknowledged by her people as setting achievable and challenging development targets. Her ability to establish rapport with many people gains her the opportunity to create trusting and productive coaching environments. She has inspired leaders to discover new insights and learn which changes will make the most significant impact on their success at work, and even at home.

Easter has a real talent with coaching individuals and teams to resolve conflicts, in a positve and respective way, so that productivity, efficiencies, and motivation are greatly improved.

Specialties

Coaching, leadershipship development, interview coaching, life coaching, teambuilding, conflict management without drama, motivation, speaking engagements
