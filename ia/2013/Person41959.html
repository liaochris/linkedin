Eric Bishko's Summary

Having traveled the world from the ripe age of 15, Eric has experienced various cultures, art, and struggle around the globe. He has picked up much of his design and illustrative style from the difference between his empirical observations and erudite knowledge.

Eric had held a varirty of positions in the past. A former customer service representative for the New York State Department of Motor Vehicles, the former Interim Director of Promotions and Marketing at a major promotions company on the east coast that is now defunct, a Freelance Graphic Artist and Illustrator, a Former Starbucks Barista, and a Second Degree Black Belt in Soo Bahk Do Moo Duk Kwan style Korean Karate.

Goals and aspirations include keeping his dog safe and healthy, as well as seeing his company flourish. He sets high standards and is always striving to keep abreast of the latest trends in the markets, and always has the latest software and the knowledge to use it.
