Brittany Purcell's Summary

Brittany Purcell is an Associate Broker, Luxury Homes Specialist, & Certified Short Sale Specialist with Keller Williams Realty. Her real estate team serves Athens, GA and the surrounding areas. Brittany serves traditional real estate needs by listing and selling residential real estate. She also helps sellers in need by listing, selling, and facilitating short sales.

Brittany lives just outside of Athens in Jefferson, GA with her family and dogs. She earned her real estate license in 2007 and her Broker’s license in 2010. Brittany has since enjoyed each and every day of making an impact in her community through real estate, business networking, and charitable contribution.

She loves horseback riding, spending time at the lake, and being around people. One of her goals is to earn her pilot's license.
