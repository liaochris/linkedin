Deb Hansen, Certified Senior Advisor 20,000+'s Summary

As a Certified professional coach, I specialize in career coaching and college planning. I work with adults in career or other life transition. I am Certified in the Strong Interest Inventory assessment to guide adults to their most compatible career choices; this assessment also helps young adults ages 15 and up with choosing a college major and potential career options. I also provide resume writing, interview coaching services, assists with job searches and is also experienced in helping adults transition to self-employment.

BLOG
----------
Visit the Transition-U blog (http://www.transition-u.com/?cat=1) for great information on how I can help you in your career transition and for other useful tips!

MY EXPERIENCE
-------------------------
I have been coaching part-time since 2003 and full time since 2007. I earned my CPCC, coaching credential in 2005. I have worked with over 100 individuals from all walks of life on issues ranging from career, work/life balance, feeling of being stuck, lack of fulfillment, confusion, overwhelm, low self esteem, life transitions such as retirement, career change, divorce, and re-entry to the workplace.

MY MISSION
------------------
My belief is that all people are creative, resourceful and whole. My clients don’t come to coaching because they are broken and need fixing. They come to coaching because they understand that making a change can be challenging, even a change that is obvious and necessary. My mission as their coach is to understand who they are as unique individuals, to learn what they want to achieve with coaching, and to be their guide, catalyst, and witness through the process.

FREE SESSION
-----------------------
Set up a no-obligation 30 minute phone session to discuss your situation and see if we are a good fit. If either of us feels we’re not a good match I am happy to refer you to another coach.

Please visit: http://www.transition-u.com/?page_id=10 to start your FREE Session today!

Specialties

Career coaching, Strong Interest Inventory, college and career guidance for young adults ages 15 and up, transition coaching, new business start-ups.
