Colin Hynes' Summary

Before in a nutshell:
During grad school covered stories for the AP ranging from governors to noble prize winners to paralyzed pimps living in the Combat Zone. First job out of grad, all 50 people in my start-up got laid off except me. Ran the product and built it for three more years working on a shoestring for the parent company. Along the way learned some valuable "first real job" lessons. Best professional experience a 25-year-old could have. Left for the big agency. Amazing clients. Brilliant and driven co-workers. Then quick stint building mind-numbingly complex financial planning tool. Left to the land of paper clips and sharpies. Spent 11 incredible years helping make things easy. Kiosks, store signage, catalogs, web. You name it, we studied and designed it. When the dust settled, our ecom business was second biggest in the world. In parallel, got another Masters, was on a few boards, taught classes, did lots of speaking and press stuff. On to high fashion. Learned a lot. Met some awesome people.

Now:
Partner in a user experience firm with a deep bench of UX talent. Leveraging the thousands of people I have studied to make things better for some great clients.
