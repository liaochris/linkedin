Brent N. Hunter, PMP, CSM's Summary

In the spirit of professional networking and international friendship, I accept all requests to connect.

PMP-certified Senior IT Project Manager currently serving as Senior Project Manager at Blue Shield of California. Mr. Hunter's previous role at Intelligent Internet Marketing was as a Senior Project Manager responsible for all aspects of Information Technology. Previously, he was a Technical Project Manager at Wells Fargo Bank. Previously and through Robert Half International, he was a Director-level IT Consultant and Senior Project Manager helping prepare a client for major expansion, including facilitating the implementation of their first-ever instructional webinars.

Prior to this, Mr. Hunter's role was the CIO/Director of IT responsible for all aspects of Information Technology at The Surplus Line Association of California, whose systems monitored the $6 billion surplus line insurance market for the state of California. Implemented a first-ever IT Department Code of Conduct.

Mr. Hunter's previous position was Technology Manager of Enterprise DNS and Network Administration Services at Wells Fargo Bank in San Francisco, CA. Decreased expenses and increased productivity. Raised awareness of the extraordinary criticality of DNS to the $575 billion enterprise. Implemented a first-ever Enterprise-wide DNS Usage Policy.

Mr. Hunter's experience between 1994 and 2001 consisted of envisioning, founding, running, producing, directing and raising $1.8M for a first-ever intentional World Community in cyberspace.

Specialties: Experienced IT consultant and Senior Project Manager with wide experience in network infrastructure, software design and development, hardware design and development, ASP implementation and customization, excellent analytical and troubleshooting skills, entrepreneurial spirit, interpersonal abilities, team-building skills and ability to add solid business value in any organization. Social media pioneer.
