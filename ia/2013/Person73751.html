Keith Young's Summary

I am a certified life, business, personal development & accountability coach. I have been trained and certified by JTE Associates of San Francisco CA and also a certified Law of Attraction Coach by Quantum Success Academy.
I have been a coach in a national real estate training program for over 6 years and have coached over 600 personal clients and consulted with another 1000 plus clients over that time.
My goal is to empower people get what they want out of life and show them how to create the life or business of their dreams by changing their inner game and overcome the self-limiting beliefs they have that could be holding them back.

Specialties: time management, goal setting, overcoming fears and procrastination. Universal Laws that help create an ideal life or business. I also help companies with problem solving, structuring sales, employee focus and motivation.
