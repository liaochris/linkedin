Greg Maxson's Summary

As an internationally recognized illustrator and author, a variety of clients have called upon Greg to provide clear and accurate visual solutions for over twenty years. He routinely works from sketches, photos, engineering drawings, and the actual products to illustrate complex processes and products for some of the top studios, publishers, and manufacturers in the United States.

Greg is also the owner/manager of the LinkedIn group tech.ART. Attracting over 500 members since 2009, tech.ART is solely focused on the technical illustration industry.
