Derry Birkett's Summary

Web Designer since 1999 forged during the tech bubble, but didn't make a million. Worked in startups in London, Dublin, Malaga, Barcelona, Florence and now run my studio from Bologna, Italy. Still working on that elusive million.

I learn fast because I screw up a lot and make plenty of mistakes: I'm still wondering whether this is actually a good technique or not - there always seems to be more mistakes to make. I believe it was Einstein who said "stupidity is repeating the same thing over and over again and expecting different results.". So I'm hoping I'm actually doing different things.

Professionally, I'm a Digital Designer with over 13 years experience - I offer Art Direction, Web & Mobile Design, UI / UX and Front-end development. I also have skills in the startup capacity - managing teams, project management, marketing etc. Although this is not my key skill. I really enjoy researching new trends in design and technology and bringing them into my work.

Personally I love photography, salsa, travel, socialising, music & art.

I think that's enough about me.

Tweet me @derrybirkett - I'm a friendly guy.

Specialties: Web & Mobile Design & Development, Art Direction, UI/UX, Digital Strategy, Digital Marketing and Startups, Screwing Up.
