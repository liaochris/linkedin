Adam Stein's Summary

Gridium is the second company I've founded focused on the application of internet software to clean tech. The energy sector lags badly in the application of new technology, opening up big opportunities for nimble companies who can deliver compelling user experiences and quantifiable savings. Gridium aims to be a disruptive force in the commercial and industrial energy markets. Right now, our customers love us. My personal goal is to make sure they continue to love us as we grow to thousands and then millions of users.
