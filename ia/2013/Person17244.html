Brian Chiorello's Summary

ACI Worldwide is the leader in the Banking and Biller Electronic Payments Industry transacting nearly 11 trillion dollars a month. Working as a Project Manager with an average of 25 projects at any given time is a constant challenge. Projects are always in different phases, pushing through the development defects, assisting Clients with their requirements all while delivering and meeting very demanding timelines.

Previously I was a Team Supervisor of 7+ employees at ACI Worldwide formerly known as (ORCC) in the Client Services Dept where I manage over 250 Clients that process millions of dollars a day in transactions. Supporting the clients, their applications, projects and other daily issues while troubleshooting technical issues, writing SQL queries and offering directives or advice to guide our client's businesses in the ever changing online payments solutions.

Prior to my employment with ACI, I spent nearly 14 years as a Private Investigator, handling all types of investigations. Professionally trained in the art of Research,Surveillance & Investigations, this training developed and honed skills which allowed me to search and locate detail information on the less obvious, it taught me how to quickly identify discrepancies which then led to formulating and creating solutions and better processes.

I converted this training and experience into my passion for business which has led me to strive an obtain new levels in the corporate world.

Other passions include innovation, providing solutions to unique problems, film and script writing which has led to my first book being published called Going Viral. A fictional fantasy based book about how twisted life can get at times. Offered exclusively online at Amazon.com.

Specialties: Technical Account Management, Project Management, Research, Investigations, Due Diligence, Industry Data, Demographics, Competitive Analysis, Customer & Vendor Analysis, Problem Solving, Process Flow Improvements
