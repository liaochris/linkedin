John Greene's Summary

Experienced Hardware Engineer with many years of electronic hardware design, development and manufacturing support, within both large established firms, and startup environments. My skills include:

- Extensive experience with RF design, FM, AM, PM, Spread Spectrum.

- Designing microcontroller circuits for remote controls, LCD displays, consumer electonics.

- Audio and Video experience, NTSC/PAL video frame grabber, Music electronic devices, cordless phones, FM stereo transmitters.

-Extensive experience interfacing with PCB designers to ensure that RF and analog signal routing rules were followed during PCB layout and to determine layer stack-ups, impedance etc.

-Practical knowledge of designing for reduced EMI/RFI and experience with debug at test site facilities.

Specialties

All types of hardware development. Network Analyzer, Spectrum Analyzers, Logice Analyzers, Oscillocopes, RF Communications Test sets, Eagleware simulation, Pspice, FPGA design.
