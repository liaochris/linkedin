Kevin Lawver's Summary

I'm passionate about the web! I've spent my entire career consumed with building kickass web products and being part of some amazing teams. I love solving problems, writing code, launching products and leading agile teams to Get Things Done.

I spent 13 years at AOL building big web "stuff" and leading the charge for standards-based development best practices. I worked on AOL Search, built a neat feed reader, a huge social networking app, hundreds of other apps of various sizes, taught a lot of people about semantic markup, CSS and JavaScript, led some agile development teams, joined the W3C and helped the CSS Working Group, and rocked a few boats.

I left AOL to become CTO at Music Intelligence Solutions, where I got everyone up to speed on building products for the web, agile development best practices and launched some products, all while managing all the servers, deploying two products to "the cloud", and learning the ropes of startup life. I was interim CEO for a little while as the company transitioned after almost a year of not having one, but decided to leave after I wasn't given the authority I needed to perform the job.

I just started here at Rails Machine and am happy to get back to doing awesome technology stuff!

Fifteen years into my career on the web, I'm even more passionate about building products than ever.

Specialties

web standards (XHTML, CSS, XSL, XML, microformats), accessibility, ruby on rails, web application architecture, problem solving, troubleshooting, javascript, DOM, jquery, product development
