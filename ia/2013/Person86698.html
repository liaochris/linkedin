Mark Iliff's Summary

Elearning
***********************************
In 1985 people backed away from me because they thought training with computers was weird. Now they back away because they think I might be responsible for some of the junk elearning they have suffered. (They're wrong*.)

As long as good people are trying to help adults learn, I'll be trying to help them do it even better: strategy, learning design, writing, script doctoring... I enjoy it all and I love a challenge.

Web development
***********************************
When I went freelance in 2000 I needed a website. I found it was rather fun. And I think I'm quite good at it (look at clients' sites for the evidence - or even mine, which is not as shamefully tired as it used to be!).


* Well, 95% wrong. I plead guilty to scripting a fewer duffers in my time. But I never stayed at those places for a second project...
