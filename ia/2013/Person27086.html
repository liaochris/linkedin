Corky Ballas' Summary

I began dancing at the age of six years old, started out as Flamenco dancer, I then later tried out the style of American Style Latin dancing referred to now as 'Rhythm', in the Juvenile Division, in 1966 in Houston Texas .

My father, George Ballas Sr., was President of Fred Astaire corp, he owned and operated a 64,000 square foot dance studio: the largest dance studio ever in the world, called Dance City USA, with 125 teachers on payroll. There has never been a studio this large since 1964.

My father sold the studio and later went on to invent the Weed Eater, which contributes over 2 billion to the economy today.

Currently I shot a national Diet Pepsi Commercial with Sofia Vergara, and have been an actor on the 'hit' Televison show Dancing With The Stars :)

Ten Times United States Latin Champion, and 3 Times Open to the World British Latin Champion. I am the only US citizen to win these amazing titles.
