Annette Oden's Summary

Dove Award-winning producer and songwriter of kids musicals, including Aloha Christmas!, Just Run, Get to the Manger, Born to Worship & Noelle, The First! Latest musical is a fun, non-seasonal project set in a community garden published by Brentwood-Benson called Herbie and the Good News Garden. Relishing the experience as a children’s choir director and kids worship leader, Annette produced several collections like The Kids Praise Team Project & Real Kids Worship through a long-time partnership with Integrity Music. Her heart to serve as Children’s Pastor of a local church extended into the community where she co-created two parachurch organizations. SPIN! provides worship arts instruction for kids while STOMP! is a dance and drama ministry for teenagers. Annette and her husband John have three great kids and over 26 wonderful years together living in the beautiful, sunny south.
