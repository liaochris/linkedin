Jim Williams' Summary

HomeVestors Independent Franchise Owner - JKK Property Investors, LLC, and Founder/CEO of multiple real estate companies including All Property Solutions, Triad Residential Solutions, and The Triad Mastermind Group for Real Estate Service Providers and Wealth Builder.

"JKK Property Investors/HomeVestors" is an Independent Franchise that is known as America's Number One Home Buyer!

All Property Solutions is a full service, licensed brokerage that functions as most brokerage's do: Listing Property to Sell, Representing Clients as a Buyer's Agent, handling REO's, etc. Our property management division manages single family homes all over the Triad including Greensboro, High Point and Winston Salem. All Property Solutions currently manages over 200 homes and is growing rapidly due to the focus on service, efficiency, and technology that streamlines both processes and communications.

Triad Residential Solutions is a renovations company that buys, renovates and sells real estate with a purpose of stabilizing and raising values in neighborhoods throughout the Triad area. The wholesale division sells properties directly to investors.

The Triad Mastermind Group for Real Estate Service Providers trains, coaches, and supports investors to build wealth through real estate, local and nationwide.

My wife, Karen Rittenhouse, has written several books on buying, selling, and landlording that give clear, simple, step by step processes for each of these areas of real estate. www.karensperspective.com

My passion is developing steps for real estate businesses to optimize and achieve their goals and objectives.

My goal is to share my successes and personal experiences of overcoming self limiting thinking with others who are seeking to grow in mind, body, and soul.

My vision is to help others realize EVERYONE has the potential for success once they create the proper alignment with their creator's purpose for their life!

Maui Mastermind Business Coaching To Real Estate Entrepreneurs

Dear Real Estate Entrepreneur,

After close to 20 years mentoring and training real estate investors one thing has become all too
clear—most real estate investors aren’t building a business, they’re building a job for
themselves—doing things deal by deal.

Sadly, by approaching their investing with this “deal by deal” mentality they never make the
money they want nor enjoy the freedom that first drew them to investing to begin with.

There is a better way!

Once you’re learned how to do deals the next major focus for you must be on building a scalable,
systematized investment business that does the deals! The difference is enormous.

We know this first hand. Over the past 20 years we’ve personally trained and coached over
100,000 investors across the country, helping them to buy and sell over $1 billion of real
estate. We are still active investors ourselves that have personally structured and executed over $100
million of deals, everything from houses, to apartment buildings, to shopping centers, to office
complexes. ( David Finkel CEO got his start years ago buying single family houses in San Diego!)

Which brings me to the purpose of this letter—to invite you to take a closer look at a brand new
business coaching program we have developed exclusively for real estate investors who want to
learn to build a real estate business, not just settle for the status quo of chasing deals one at a
time.

The program is not about teaching you how to do real estate deals.

Why? Because the core focus of the program isn’t to teach you how to structure a lease option,
or complete a rehab. Rather the focus of the program is to give you the concrete road map to
actually build a scalable business that successfully and profitably does these things!

Jimwilliams@mauimastermind.com
