Kevin Alexander, M.A.'s Projects

  • My PKU Life

    • November 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: Kevin Alexander, M.A.

    I produced My PKU Life to raise awareness of the rare metabolic disease Phenylketonuria (PKU). The film has since gone viral within the PKU community and has been seen all over the world.

  • PKULife.tv

    Team Members: Kevin Alexander, M.A.

    PKULife.tv is a documentary web series about the rare disease PKU, produced by an adult living with PKU. Living with PKU can be a lonely experience. But we take comfort in knowing we aren't the only ones living with this disease. Stories inspire, empower, & unite. We may be scattered across the world, but the boundaries that once separated us are dissolving. We can now be connected with one another, no matter where in the world we live. My goal is that these films continue to inspire, empower, and unite all of us in the PKU community to share our stories with the world.

  • Patient Power PKU

    Team Members: Kevin Alexander, M.A.

    I am a Correspondent & Videographer for an on-going series of medical stories about the rare metabolic disease Phenylketonuria.

  • 50th Anniversary of Newborn Screening

    • September 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Kevin Alexander, M.A.

    I am partnering with the Association of Public Health Laboratories, PerkinElmer, and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention on a campaign celebrating 50 years of newborn screening in the U.S.

  • Gluten Free World TV webseries

    • July 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Kevin Alexander, M.A., Mara Alexander AADP CHHC

    A webseries to provide worldwide encouragement to those who have Celiac or on gluten-free diets. Gluten-free travel tips and more!
