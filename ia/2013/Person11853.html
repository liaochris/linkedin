Jeremy Barnish's Summary

Renowned as an innovator at the forefront of the latest technologies and methodologies, Jeremy has successfully marketed enterprise, software and cloud technologies in most of the world’s major markets, run teams with award winning accolades in the world’s top marketing agencies, and managed global marketing teams and budgets that have skyrocketed the capitalization of public companies.

"Personally, I believe it all starts with what you want to say, so I'm passionate about crystal clear messaging and content, utilizing sales to identify real world best practices, and about getting results. I've had a career that has spanned most of the world’s major markets, working in the top agencies and running global in house marketing teams in charge of hundreds of people and multi million and even billion dollar initiatives. I’ve seen at first hand that sales and marketing is most effective when aligned behind one arrowhead and aimed at a common target, no matter what the task -- whether introducing a new product or service, entering a new territory or market, or developing a new strategy to communicate better with other teams in a large organization to revitalize the business.”
