Michael Griffey's Summary

Over thirty (30) years of solid management and project executive experience in the commercial construction field; More recently he has transformed a lifelong passion for photography into a viable, energetic business. Combining skills and training developed in both fields, he has successfully merged the two in a professional manner. His expertise lies in analyzing problems, determining innovative techniques, and providing cost effective solutions. He is conscientious, highly motivated, and capable of working both independently and as a member of an integrated team. A proven leader, he's accountable for project completion and customer satisfaction.

Michael Griffey's Experience

Principal & Owner

Michael Griffey Photography, LLC.

2007 – Present (6 years) Conyers, Georgia

I work closely with you to transform your thoughts into timeless, creative images. My work includes architectural photography, personal portraits, corporate headshots, intimate and boudoir, events, products, fine art and stock photography.

Customer service and satisfaction are the cornerstones of my business philosophy. The project is not finished until you are completely satisfied.

I have participated in Local, State, and Regional competitions, and received numerous honors and awards including several Best of Shows and Photographer of the Year. My work has been published in Creative Loafing, Sunday Paper, on Boortz.com and JPG Magazine.

I have also exhibited at The Mable House Gallery, the Granite Room, Artist’s Trifecta Gallery, The ‘G’ Gallery, Big House Gallery and The Composition Gallery.

My work has been in various exhibitions such as Southern Fried Photographers, Artful Harvest, National Juried Art Competition, Art at the Rock, Atlanta Celebrates Photography, and countless festivals and craft shows.

Thank you for visiting my profile, and I hope to be of service for your photographic needs.
