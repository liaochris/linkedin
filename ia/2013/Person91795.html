Mark Grennan's Summary

Database expert with extensive and proven knowledge of how to grow and sustain database infrastructures for highly available and successful web applications via MySQL and PostgreSQL and PostGIS. Having supported such companies as Weather Decision Technologies, TelaFlora, Hertz Car Rental and Fleming Foods. I know what is needed to take a database ecosystem to the next level to support the demanding web applications being built today.

I specialize in Open Source products like MySQL, Apache, Tomcat and RedHat Linux. No project is to big or to small. I enjoy the challenges of maintaining enterprise environments with five 9's of up time.

Specialties

• Data Modeling and Data Access
• High Availability with Heartbeat, PaceMaker and ScaleArc

• Open Source application support
• I return value to the database community through -- www.MySQLFanBoy.com
