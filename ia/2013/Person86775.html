Mark Law's Summary

A world-class facilitator, trainer and career mentor specializing in consultancy skills and value management training for those working in or for complex organisations.

- Works extensively at CEO / Director / Senior Manager levels
- Developed powerful new techniques for strategy formulation & organizational design
- Facilitates innovation & cost reduction workshops

Mark collaborates with top academics and management consulting practitioners to deliver high value / high impact workshops and seminars. Close collaborators include ex BCG, McKinsey, Bain consultants and London Business School professors.

Mark started his career as an oil field troubleshooter with Schlumberger in the North Sea, Adriatic and Mediterranean. He is a Chartered Engineer, Chartered Marketer and has an MBA from London Business School. He is also a fully qualified value management / value engineering facilitator and trainer.
