Michael Kapp, CSI, CDT, LEED AP BD+C, CSBA,CPHC's Summary

Mike is a Professional Architect licensed in California with 26 years of experience. He is a member of the Construction Specifications Institute (CSI). His credentials include Construction Documents Technologist (CDT) through CSI, and Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional, specialty Building Design + Construction (LEED AP BD+C) through the Green Building Certification Institute, Certified Sustainable Building Advisor (CSBA) through the Sustainable Building Advisor Institute and Certified Passive House Consultant (CPHC) through the Passive House Institute US.

Specialties

Project Management, Sustainable Architecture, Accessibility, Envelope Integrity, Passive House design, Ecoroofs
