Jonathan Moon's Summary

I am a recent graduate of Southern Methodist University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Cinema-Television. I am an aspiring film & TV producer who is passionate about all things media-related. I am most useful in video production and editing, but also enjoy audio and photography in my pastime.

Ever since I was a young child, I have always enjoyed technology and pushing every button I could find. At the age of fourteen, I began to realize my passion for media because of my fascination with music and technology. My first interaction with production began at my church when I started observing an audio engineer mix the worship music for the services. Over the years, I learned to mix in a live situation for recordings, monitors, and eventually, front-of-house sound. Through my interest in audio, I developed relationships with the video production staff and became more interested in video and photography. After I graduated from high school, I had a clear understanding of what wanted to study.

After beginning my higher education in the Cinema-Television program at SMU, I was an intern at Igniter Media for two years—a company that produces church media resources. I have worked in Marketing and Sales for Apple, Inc., and I have worked on the sound crew, as a Production Assistant, and as an Engineer’s Assistant for ESPN. Additionally, I worked as a Media Consultant for professors at SMU, teaching and equipping them to enhance their classroom experience with technology. I spent five years as the Video Team Leader at Bent Tree Bible--a 5,000+ person church in North Dallas. In this role, I was the producer of a large IMAG team and the primary editor of all custom video content for the church.

Today I am a full time freelancer in media production. Most recently, I have been on the in-house IMAG crews for the Frisco Rough Riders ballpark and FC Dallas Stadium.

Apple Computers, the Final Cut Pro Suite, the Adobe Creative Suite, & Microsoft Office
