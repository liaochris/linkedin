Kali Readwin's Summary

I'm a marketer who believes in real, relevant conversation over buying lists and pushing products. I like to support businesses who are good at what they do already but just don't know how to communicate it.

I am an organizer, a connector of people, a fierce advocate for small business and a how-can-we-make-this-better addict. With experience in the corporate, small business and non-profit sectors, my perspective on marketing is well-rounded and founded in an understanding of both people and good business. I like to teach outstanding companies how to shine and teach new companies how to grow their community the right way.

Specialties: social media marketing, social media management, community management, relationship marketing, social media consulting, copywriting, marketing implementation consulting, small business, entrepreneurship, adult education, marketing strategy, marketing plans
