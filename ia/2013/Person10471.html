A business directory is a collection of listings that are grouped together by category. When a person goes online to a search engine and types in a keyword or category, the search engine performs a search of the internet based on those keywords or categories. If your business is listed in all the business directories typically containing your business name, a description of what you offer, keywords and your website, your business name will be generated from that information and your business could ultimately be listed on several pages of the search results.

Several online directories offer "free business listings" for business owners. Every individual directory that your business profile is placed in is another chance to be found so it's important to make sure you're listed in every directory possible.

Business Directory Marketing Strategy

Adams Social Media Connections will work with you to gather all the detailed information that will be required to complete the directories.
