Ken Stone's Summary

Ken Stone, the Soul Archaeologist, guides and supports people along their spiritual journey while returning the entire person to their naturally aligned state – physically, emotionally, and spiritually. Ken has helped thousands of people around the world including, cancer patients, war heroes suffering from PTSD, child abuse survivors, and victims of debilitating depression and anxiety. As a conduit for Divine energy, Ken facilitates the excavation of his clients’ souls, revealing their connection to the truth within, allowing the experience of calm and clarity, and discovering the source of their power.

Informed of his natural spiritual gifts in the fall of 2007, Ken let go of his ideas of what his life would be in order to accept his life’s purpose. In the process he transformed from an unhappy mortgage lender and frustrated spiritual seeker to internationally recognized Teacher and Healer. Since then, Ken has been exploring and sharing his abilities with a growing international audience in individual and group session, in-person and via remote connection – including telephone and webcast, and through recordings.

“Ken has one of the most beautiful and gentle souls I’ve ever known. He is like a master tuner of fine instruments with the ability to energetically touch the physical, spiritual, and energetic bodies and re-tune them to the Divine perfection they are meant to naturally be.” -- Rachael Jayne Groover, Founder: The Yin Project and Author: Powerful AND Feminine
