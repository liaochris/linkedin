David Clare's Summary

David is a Digital Consultant at Hotwire PR - an integrated PR and communications agency, providing an integrated portfolio of specialist services on a local, regional and global scale.

David studied Marketing and Public Relations at the University of Lincoln, writing a dissertation on the uses of Twitter in Crisis Communications. David has also completed a 10 week short course in Creative Writing at City University London.

After starting his career at sister agency 33 Digital, David moved to Hotwire PR to take on the Digital Consultant role, developing the digital offering and consultancy the agency provides.
