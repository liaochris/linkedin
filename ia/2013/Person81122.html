Linda Van Valkenburgh, MS, CCMC, CJSS, CSMCS's Summary

Linda partners with clients to plan, manage and organize individualized career campaigns. She helps clients define career goals and identify career options utilizing proven and comprehensive career planning techniques and systems; teaches job search and research strategies; and provides support for clients experiencing the stress of job loss or career transition.

Linda assists clients to discover their skills and strengths; develop their value proposition and strategic plan; target opportunities that advance their careers to the next level and supports them through a skillful negotiation process.

Clients have transitioned to such organizations as: UBS, RBS, IRS, ING, GE, P&G, J&J, LIMRA, CB Richard Ellis, PZ Cussons UK, Boehringer Ingleheim, American Red Cross, Starwood, Starbucks, McGraw Hill, Tiffany & Co, Novo Nordisk, Apple, Merrill Lynch, HomeServe, Hospira, HILTI, UniVision, Paychex, Bridgewater, Prudential, Bank of America & Bank of NY Mellon.

Linda is a Certified Career Management Coach (CCMC), Certified Job Search Strategist (CJSS), Certified Social Media Career Strategist (CSMCS) and is recognized by the International Coaching Federation. Linda holds a BA in Education & Psychology from Marymount College and a Masters degree from Fordham with additional advanced degree credits from Purdue. Linda was a speaker at the Yale School of Management lecturing on Interview Techniques; an invited Career Expert on NBC CT WVIT Channel 6; and has spoken at many networking and professional groups across the region including: New York University, PMI, NJ Department of Labor, SCORE/Norwalk, UJA/WJCS Connect to Care, BlogTalkRadio, and local Comcast TV. She is a regular contributor for ExecuNet providing “Coffee Break” & Fast Track lectures, has been quoted in ExecuNet Career Advisor articles, and a guest speaker for ExecuNet’s 6 Figure Hotline. Linda also organizes and facilitates several monthly networking meetings for the 100k and 200k+ executive.
