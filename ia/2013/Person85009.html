Marc Glasser, MS, CPP, CEM, CORE, FABCHS's Summary

Feel free to e-mail me at mglasser@rmllc.com. Feel free to send a LinkedIn invite using mglasser@rmllc.com as well.

For RM (Resilience Management) LLC and PRM (Protection Risk Management) GLOBAL, LLC service details see "Experience" section below.

U.S. Government Special Agent career spans over two decades. Directed risk management programs to combat threats including intentional (e.g., terrorism, theft, sabotage), natural (e.g., floods, earthquakes, hurricanes) and technical (e.g., utility, technology losses, hasmat) risks involving U.S. National Critical Infrastructure and Key Resources (CIKR). Conducted federal criminal, civil and presidential appointee background investigations. Served on federal cabinet level and foreign dignitary U.S. executive protection details. Served on National Response Framework response teams and FBI Joint Terrorism Task Force and Organized Crime task forces. Fusion center representative.

Holds an active U.S. Government “Top Secret” security clearance. Has worked in over 90 countries. Graduate of the Federal Law Enforcement Training Center (FLETC) Special Agent Academy.

Master of Science Degree in Crisis and Emergency Management from University of Nevada, Las Vegas and a Bachelor of Science Degree in Law Enforcement and Police Science from Sam Houston State University.

Associate or Adjunct Professor at University of Maryland University College, University of Nevada Las Vegas, Henley-Putnam University, Regis University and National University. He is peer-reviewed published. He is a presenter at national and international conferences.

Present and past board service includes: ASIS International Crisis Management and Business Continuity Council; ASIS International Academic and Training Programs Council; American Board for Certification in Dignitary and Executive Protection; American Board for Certification in Infrastructure Protection; Hotel Business Review, Editorial Board and Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Use of Force Review Board.
