Mark Jones Jr.'s Summary

Transforming uncertainty and risk into decisive action--that's what it means to multiply leadership.

For years, people have tried to burden you, the leader, with complex statistical tools that require special software or training, that make you re-organize your procedures and work-flow, that rob you of momentum and the joy of doing the technical work you do. But these tools don't answer the questions nagging in the back of your mind.

You need something that everyone in the organization can use in the day to day analysis that takes place in the trenches, something that helps them make decisions, something that helps them give you the information needed to make strategic decisions--something that doesn’t slow down innovation or technical progress, something that is elementary, fundamentally simple to apply, yet applicable to even the complex problems you encounter.

Those tools are known as ATOMs (analytical tools of mathematics and statistics).
