Erik Flowers' Summary

I believe in the power of experiences and how they shape the journeys people take through the things we create. My goal is to build processes and products that deliver delight to the people who use them.

UX is where all my passions intersect; design, business, and psychology. Bringing the three together is how truly great experiences are created.

I have spent the last 15 years building digital things that people use and experience. My purpose is to facilitate a journey that helps the users reach their goals.
