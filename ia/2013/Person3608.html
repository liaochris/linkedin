Alessandro Mottura's Summary

Accumulated more than 5 years experience in the field of high temperature metallurgy, specifically single crystal nickel and cobalt superalloys. Specialized in different modeling and analysis techniques spanning several lengthscales (from electronic structure to continuum). Published several articles in world renowned international journals. Developed close collaborations with other researchers at many US, UK and EU institutions.

Scientific techniques: FS Laser Serial Sectioning, FIB Serial Sectioning (FIB Tomography), Ab-initio Modelling, Monte Carlo, EXAFS, Atom Probe Tomography, X-ray Micro Tomography, EDS, EBSD

Programming: C++, Shell Scripting, OpenMP, OpenGL, FLTK, Qt, PHP, HTML, CSS, mySQL, Labview
