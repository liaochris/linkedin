Constantin Chirila's Summary

- During my 5 years as working as a freelance graphic and web designer I have learned how to be competitive and I developed organisational skills. I improved as a creative person, being able not only to come up with great designs, but also to understand and create a brief, to come up with structure, research and ideas. Another strong suit in this is my ability to also code my website designs into HTML and CSS and also develop websites for Wordpress platform. I have a keen eye for details, i am quite a perfectionist.
- During my 3 years working as a freelancer photographer, I understood how to judge the commercial value of a photograph and the photography demand on the market. Other than that it merged excellent with my graphic design skills and complemented each other.
- During the period I worked as a photographer and photojournalist for a national newspaper and tabloid I have understood what is a story and how to express one, got deeper understanding on how people understand, read and interact with publications and also how important is imagery in a printed product. I can say I developed a lot as a person, being able to break a discussion with a random stranger, as well as with personalities and celebrities alike. Also I have learned important skills regarding the importance of well established workflow and the indexing of photographs into archives.

Right now the most important goal is to finish my Creative Digital Media studies at the University of Worcester, UK.
Other than that my objectives are to develop into a full grown creative professional, to be able to contribute to this ever expanding and demanding market.
