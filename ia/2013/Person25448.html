Claudia Pasqualini Salsa's Summary

Graduated in Law, summa cum laude, in 1973 at the Genoa University, admitted as lawyer in 1976, attorney at the Supreme Courts specialized in Environmental law and Work safety.

In 1981, I founded my own Law Firm, with offices in Savona, Milan and Rome.

Specialties

The legal services are specialized in EU and Italian Environmental law, Work safety, Company Law. The Firm employs lawyers practising civil, administrative and penal law.
