Greg Selvin's Summary

As a software / I.T. product development specialist, I help the organizations for which I work to improve the quality and suitability of their products, align costs with economic outcomes, and improve employee morale and retention.

I am a Agilist who applies the rigor and toolkit of a SPC Scaled Agile (SAFe) Program Consultant, CSM, and PMP certified Agile Coach, Technical Product, and Program Manager. I combine business experience, deep technical knowledge, strong interpersonal skills, a great work ethic, and creative intelligence to consistently deliver demonstrable value. Moreover, I bring the skills of a management and solutions consultant to any organization for which I work as a positive agent of change. My years of experience covers Project Management, Product Management, Management Consulting, Operations, Support, Engineering, Pre-Sales, Client and Vendor Relations. I have enjoyed success managing and participating in software development (SDLC) with Waterfall, Agile (SAFe / Scrum), RUP (use case, etc.), and RAD / JAD methodologies.

Specialties: Agile coaching and transformation, program and technical project management, product management, light-weight marketing, software development (various methodologies, including Agile), middleware architecture, communications skills ranging from managing people to working with senior executives, collaboration, enterprise 2.0
