Juliana Swenson's Summary

Juliana has a passion for learning, teaching, gathering inspiration and information from her surroundings and always considering how news, fashion, culture and world events shape and influence design. This gives her the ability to generate designs that are visually versatile and consistently relevant.

In addition to being artistic, Juliana is strategic. She seeks input from her clients, adopts their goals as her own and solicits their feedback throughout the project to ensure objectives will be met. Her straightforward approach makes the seemingly abstract creative process easier, more efficient and even fun for all involved.

Specialties

Graphic design for print and web; art direction; pattern design; photo styling and photo retouching. Proficient in Mac platform Quark Express 7 and Adobe CS5 Illustrator, Photoshop and InDesign. Some knowledge of Microsoft PowerPoint and Word. Expert knowledge in current printing processes and paper brands. Excellent organizational and problem solving skills. Ability to manage projects and work within deadlines.
