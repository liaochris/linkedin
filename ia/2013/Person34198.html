Dennis Evert's Summary

A creative technology professional providing development leadership, strategy, client management, planning and execution server-side to client-side. Particular expertise in front end web technologies and workflows for traditional and mobile web development. Between my education in interactive design and my experience as a programmer, I specialize in bridging the gap between design and development to bring creative visions to life.

Throughout my career, I’ve experienced many sides of the process, including interactive design, UX wireframing, animation, 3D modeling, video production, audio editing, web application architecture and development in several programming languages. I’ve had the pleasure of executing work for many innovative and engaging brands including Nike, Bauer Hockey Equipment, Target, General Mills, ConAgra Foods, 3M, Best Buy, MolsonCoors, Phillips Distilling, J.R. Watkins, Capital One, Fifth Third Bank, Northwestern Mutual, Discover Boating, Country Inns & Suites, Chinet, Formica, Thomson Reuters, United Health Care and the University of Minnesota.
