Mark A.M. Kramer's Publications

  • Overcoming Poverty Through Digital Inclusion

    • IEEE
    • May 1, 2012
    Authors: Mark A.M. Kramer, Marco Figueiredo, Paola Prado

    This article argues that the goal of reducing and overcoming poverty can be achieved in part through digital inclusion efforts. The main challenge lies in how best to achieve this goal of reducing poverty. This article argues that it must occur in two distinct stages. The first stage is digital literacy, which we’ll accomplish with what we refer to as the Symbiotic Computer (SC)—smartphones and tablets. The second stage will be professional capacity-building, ac- complished with the more tradi- tional Personal Computer (PC).
