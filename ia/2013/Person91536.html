Mark Adams' Summary

Mark Adams is an experienced copywriter and creative director with 15 years experience including copywriting positions at agencies like 2.0 Interactive, Idea Integration, and MergingDesign.

From 2002 to 2009 Mark served as Founding Partner and Copywriter/Creative Director for MergingDesign where he worked on new business, developed brands and messaging strategies in addition to copywriting, and led the creative team on projects for companies from the Fortune 500 to mid-market and upstart clients.

Mark has written for a wide range of marketing activities including corporate websites, interactive demos and sales tools, trade shows, brochures, direct mail and email marketing, newsletters, campaigns, and other marketing efforts.

Currently, Mark consults with multiple Fortune 500 companies providing copywriting and other creative marketing services.

Specialties

Copywriting, Creative Direction, Branding, Identity, Marketing, Information Architecture, telling stories, listening to clients, understanding client needs
