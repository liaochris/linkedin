Anita Nowinska's Summary

I have three great passions in life: Art, People and being a Mother.

I Create Art which influences feeling and mood and enhances an environment. I also love to help others use their talents to earn a living.

The core of my work is my Art. Creating individual commissioned works evoking feeling & mood specifically for an individual home or office. I also hold exhibitions & shows on a regular basis &work with a select group of designers and architects. Existing available work, originals, prints and cards are available at www.nowinska.com

I have engaged in projects worldwide both for original work & for large scale hotel, hospital & office projects using my Limited Edition Prints.

I believe in working with openness and integrity and enjoying the contact with clients and suppliers alike.

Specialties: Commissioned art work, original paintings, fine quality giclee prints, greeting cards. Coaching, Career Coaching, Life balance coaching, inspirational coaching. Painting, drawing, art. Floral paintings, landscapes, portraits, interior design, collaboration,

Anita Nowinska's Experience

Artist

Anita Nowinska Art

January 1980 – Present (34 years) Farnham, Surrey & London

My passion for creativity and nature is translated into my Art.
I am best known for my large sensuous images of flowers, falling from the canvas in waves of colour and landscapes, creating memories and feelings. Tumultuous flower borders and vivid landscapes & kies re also a favourite of mine.

My work has a strong emotional effect on people and testimonials talk of influencing mood, creating relaxation or passion, generating an atmosphere and ambience in a room. I have been told by clients that commissioned pieces has revitalised the passion in a relationship, created a space for total relaxation and even replaced a regular glass of wine as a post work wind-down.

I use colour and form to create feeling and when developing a painting, work very closely with a client or designer to understand the ambience, mood and colour they want to infuse the space. The results are center pieces which bring a room together and radiate emotion. Colour has great influence on the mind and atmosphere and can lift the spirits, relax or envigorate.

When working with designers or architects, I am adept at taking a colour palette and creating work to enhance a scheme. Having both a creative and commercial head, I can work to deadlines and collaborate effectively. For larger projects I can combine original pieces with high quality prints to suit a corporate budget.

I have a range of original works available, wok on bespoke commissions and have a range of limited edition giclee prints both for the private and commercial markets.

For more information go to www.nowinska.com or contact me on 07767 420820

Inspirational Coach

Anita Nowinska- Dragonfly Coaching

January 1991 – Present (23 years) Surrey & London

There are times when the reality of where we are in life does not match up to the vision of how we would like life to be.

Often , we are so embroiled in the day to day ‘doing’ that we cannot even see a clear vision of what would bring us fulfilment, success, joy.

My work as a Personal & Business Coach focuses on creating a clear understanding of where your life or business are and creating clarity around a powerful vision to work towards. Once that is in place, we work towards achieving challenging and inspiring goals which turn the vision into reality.

I have worked successfully with a range of people as individuals and teams, from those looking for what they really want to do and achieving it, to companies and sole traders looking to work more effectively and profitably taking their business to the next level.

I have a particular passion for helping creative people turn their talent into a viable living.

My philosophy is that life is too short and too precious not to be doing something you genuinely enjoy.

For a free 30 minute consultation to discuss the possibilities call me on 07767 420820
