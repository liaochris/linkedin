Patrick Ryan's Summary

Patrick Ryan is a best selling author, executive leadership coach, leadership program developer, vision quest guide, entrepreneur, and founder of Awakened Wisdom Experiences™ Inc.

Patrick is passionate leadership and supporting the fulfillment of their visions and missions. Patrick has developed programs that support executive development, personal growth, team performance, communication and out of the box problem solving.

Great organizations have a need for consciousness development and Patrick has created an organization that provides coaching, vision quest guiding, and a way for amazing and talented people to learn how to contribute in ways that tap into the best of who they are. Great change is only possible through our willingness and collective ability to do it together.

Now is the time for us all to create Awakened Enterprises™ together. We must acknowledge the interrelatedness of our actions to the state of the world we are all living in.

As a former Buddhist monk in Burma (now Myanmar), he practiced the path of awakened living, according to Buddhist philosophy. He has also studied indigenous wisdoms with a variety of teachers.

"I live and work outside the box. I love it every time someone tells me it can't be done or points out to me that I am breaking the rules.   My journey has taken me around the world as an explorer of human and cultural consciousness, as a coach, a vision guide, a leadership program developer, Buddhist monk, and as a hungry learner from great teachers."

As a coach and vision quest guide he powerfully integrates rich life experiences with ancient teachings and modern applications to help deepen his clients’ understandings of their own experiences.

Patrick’s latest book Awakened Wisdom: A Guide to Reclaiming Your Brilliance reached international bestseller status. Patrick shares his insights and experiences from his book through his acclaimed vision quests, workshop programs, and international speaking engagements.

Patrick Ryan's Publications

  • Awakened Wisdom: A Guide to Reclaiming Your Brilliance

    • Awakened Wisdom
    • January 1, 2009
    Authors: Patrick Ryan

    Awakened Wisdom blends Buddhist, Native, and collected wisdoms into a beautifully crafted healing message for our modern culture. Patrick Ryan s own life experiences greatly inform this work, and his message to his readers is simple and profound: we are at choice when it comes to how we want to live our lives during our brief time on this planet. In addition to sage teachings about ways of understanding our Selves, he also dedicates an entire half of the book to understanding and embodying the eight states of an awakened life. With exercises, daily practices, and plenty of anecdotes, Awakened Wisdom is sure to resonate with spiritual newcomers and advanced practitioners alike. Patrick Ryan is an executive coach, leadership trainer, author, and founder of Awakened Wisdom Experiences Inc. Ryan died from a drug overdose in his late teens, only to be revived with a new understanding of the Divine. He went on to be a successful entrepreneur, a Buddhist monk in Burma, and now an executive coach, trainer, and author. As a former Buddhist monk in Burma (now Myanmar), he practiced the path of awakened living, according to Buddhist philosophy. Patrick works with executives and entrepreneurs, focusing on questions of personal effectiveness and leadership. He powerfully integrates his rich life experiences with ancient teachings and modern applications to help deepen his clients understandings of their own experiences.

    Patrick’s book Awakened Wisdom: A Guide to Reclaiming Your Brilliance reached international bestseller status. It blends Buddhist, Native, and collected wisdoms into a beautifully crafted healing message for our modern culture. Ryan’s message to his readers is simple yet profound: we get to choose how we want to live during our time on this planet. He offers sound wisdom about how we might access different aspects of our authentic selves, as well as sage teachings and practical ideas for understanding and embodying the eight states of an awakened life.

  • The Eagle's Call

    • Awakened Wisdom
    • May 1, 2002
    Authors: Patrick Ryan

    The Eagle’s Call is the true story of one man's journey through the shadow worlds and beyond toward a lighter side of life. Patrick Ryan takes pivotal turns that lead him from a self-destructive lifestyle to one of creating magic, adventure and abundance!
    His journey takes him from the streets to the very successful creation of abundance as an entrepreneur in a variety of industries. Patrick shares his own stories of creating exciting small businesses from having nothing at all. After many years of riding the roller coaster of small business life he walks away from it all while at the top of his game.

    Patrick then travels to Asia where he finds his way to a monastery in Burma and becomes a Buddhist monk. His experience as a monk takes him through an extraordinary set of experiences where he is challenged physically, mentally, emotionally and spiritually.

    He faces the cruel military of Burma as he engages with the Burmese people. He is smuggled into regions that foreigners have been strictly prevented from visiting. His experience leads him to challenge everything he has made up about himself and the world.

    The saga concludes with insights that will have you reading your own life signs as you walk, run, stumble and float your way through your one-of-a-kind journey.
