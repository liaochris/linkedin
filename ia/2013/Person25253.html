Claire Blaustein's Summary

As a writer, I have worked in many situations -- on daily deadline for a nationally broadcast radio program, for websites -- NPR.org, Cookthink, and Blogcritics -- and print sources -- BBC Music, Exclaim! Magazine and the Washington Post. Each posed its own challenges, and embracing each unique medium provided a new and exciting experience.

In my work with Cookthink, I helped take a startup company from pre-launch through the present day. Juggling the many tasks inherent in keeping a large project running with a small staff, I have enhanced my ability to balance time and projects well, keeping in mind both deadlines and budgetary needs to get the job done.

I was responsible for a wide range of tasks, from writing and editing, to collaborating with programmers and managing analytics and SEO. I also had the opportunity to become actively involved in the community surrounding the site, through social media such as Facebook and Twitter, and the international BlogHer conferences for women bloggers.

In taking the next step in my career, I hope to bring together my diversity of experience and interests with my creative approach and strong work ethic.

Specialties

creative writing and editing, project management, organization, music (various genres), food and leisure topics
