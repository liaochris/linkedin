Bettina Eckerle's Summary

I came to the United States almost 20 years ago after becoming a lawyer in Germany.  As one of a hand-picked few, I was sent to New York as part of the management trainee program at a top German bank.  I stayed to go to law school at New York University and took the bar in 1996.  The rest is history. I had the good fortune to be trained and practice at Debevoise & Plimpton, one of the top firms in the country.  My lawyer’s dream came true when I got an offer at Wachtell, Lipton Rosen & Katz, the dream of being part of the firm built by one of the true visionaries and shapers of the corporate world and learning and practicing at one of the greatest corporate law firms in the world.  I then became the General Counsel of DigitalGlobe, Inc., which set out to become the largest satellite imagery company in the world.  I built their legal and compliance function, preparing them for their Initial Public Offering.  I later became the General Counsel of Focus Financial Partners, LLC, then on its way to transform into the largest partnership of independent financial advisory firms.  I was in charge of their legal affairs and compliance.

I am a skier, yogi, traveler, lover of creativity, vision and change. And the inspirational power of beauty.

Eckerle Law is a corporate law firm. I started it to help companies in every stage of their life cycle, from formation to first round of financing to sale and IPO, and everything in-between and beyond.   With two decades of experience as the General Counsel for two market leaders in heavily regulated industries and as a lawyer at two of the top law firms in the country, I know both sides and can deal with the in-s and outs of businesses with complex legal, compliance and business issues.  It allows me to be the go-to person for a company’s legal and compliance needs.

Examples of my services are:

* I form businesses
* I develop a master plan to protect their assets

* I form joint ventures and other strategic partnerships
* I buy businesses and sell them
* I help businesses to go public and with being public
* I am a business’ go-to-person for day-to-day legal needs
* I help with a compliance program and with dealings with the regulators. . .
