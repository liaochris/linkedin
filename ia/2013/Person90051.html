Max J Crowley's Summary

As the Senior Community Manager for Uber in Chicago and member of the launch team here, I've overseen marketing for Chicago and the Midwest, including the launch of two additional product lines: TAXI and uberX. For reference, Uber is a mobile based technology platform that connects riders with reliable transportation options.

To dig a bit further, my role has consisted of everything consumer-facing in Chicago including all marketing, PR, customer support, general business development, social media and other items pertaining to day-to-day business operations. In my current role, I also manage a team of Chicago Community Managers and oversee all marketing initiatives in the Midwest. In my time with Uber I've helped scale the Chicago team from two to 20+, and also worked on a number of Uber-wide initiatives including managing operations at the Democratic National Convention and SXSW2013.

Before Uber, I spent two years as a Consulting Analyst at Accenture, working on two Financial system implementations, primarily in the General Ledger functionality. At Accenture I gained broad experience in Financial Technology and strengthened a working knowledge of SQL and daily use of Excel.

A Finance major at the Tippie School of Business, I was a dedicated Sigma Chi member, holding two executive board positions and managing budgets in excess of $100,000. I also got my first taste of marketing as the campus representative for Monster Energy Drink, coordinating distribution of 25 cases a month through marketing on campus.

During my senior year at Iowa, I started a sustainability awareness group as a branch of the Sigma Alpha Pi leadership society, working with staff and students of the Tippie School of Business in order to raise Green awareness in the building and on campus. I was also an equity analyst and researcher for the Krause Fund.

For anything Uber related, please contact me by email: max@uber.com.
