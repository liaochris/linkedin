Jacob Sharony's Summary

Seasoned professional who is able to connect the dots and get things done, visionary, creative and innovative.

An accomplished executive and scientist with over 25 years in technology and business experience.

An adjunct professor of electrical engineering at Columbia University.

Served as a faculty member of the electrical and computer engineering department and director of the Center of Excellence in Wireless and Information Technology at Stony Brook University.

Held engineering and management positions in diverse technology disciplines at Time Warner Cable, Motorola, BAE Systems, NEC Research Institute, and IBM Research.

Has over 50 U.S. patents issued or pending and has published extensively in scientific journals and conferences.

Consults and serves as an adviser to industry and government on wireless and mobile technologies. Also served as a wireless expert witness in several patent litigation cases.

Specialties: Offering independent mobile and wireless consulting services from technology strategy to system analysis and enterprise mobility solutions. Expertise in WiFi, 4G LTE and WiMAX, BLE, ZigBee, MIMO, VoIP, RTLS, RFID, M2M and wireless sensing technologies.

Deep knowledge of target markets, e.g., telecom, healthcare, logistics, transportation, government, hospitality, mobile commerce and supply chain management, combined with extensive expertise in wireless and mobile technologies ensure innovative and successful solutions.
