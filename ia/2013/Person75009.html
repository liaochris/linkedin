Kevin Kruse's Summary

Kevin Kruse is the co-author of the NY Times, Wall Street Journal, and USA Today best seller, We: How to Increase Performance and Profits Through Full Engagement.

He is a popular keynote speaker, who delivers presentations on employee engagement, leadership, communication, strategy and business excellence.

For 20 years Kevin has been a serial entrepreneur and has won awards for both fast growth (Inc 500) as well as employee engagement (#4 Best Place to Work in PA).
