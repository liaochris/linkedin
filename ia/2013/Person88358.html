Mat Denney's Summary

Creative senior based in London with over 17 years’ experience in the field of digital and looking for freelance or, if the chemistry is right, my next permanent role.

I've a wealth of experience crafting, directing and delivering a diverse set of projects through concept to execution. From immersive, technically challenging builds; socially-driven campaigns across multiple territories; and smaller projects where craft is everything. I’ve worked across a wide range of brands and markets and am equally comfortable working with big financial brands as I am with FMCG and fashion.

I'm a do-er, a thinker, a tinkerer and a bit of a creative hybrid. I didn't come through a traditional advertising background. I studied photography, printmaking and fine art, have been a games designer, founded a 3D animation studio and worked across lots of disciplines like illustration, animation, video, writing and making.

Creative Director

Agency Republic

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

October 2012 – June 2013 (9 months) London, United Kingdom

As a Creative Director with a particular emphasis on craft, I was tasked with ensuring the highest quality of our visual output. Working with brands such as PepsiCo, where I oversaw the relaunch of Tropicana.co.uk and creation of their new Trop50 site; Nivea, where I was involved in creative direction of their brand site, shop, eCRM and social activity as well as bigger campaigns such as Dare To Dip; Vodafone and Konami.

Conceptual development with the creative team was central to my role - from initial ideas, through to worked up visuals, copy and client presentation. I worked closely with the design team and Head of Design to bring a fresh rigour to our production, and with technical to produce social apps and sites designed and built using responsive, mobile-first principles.

I attended to the day to day running of the creative department and steered the rebranding, development and production of the relaunched agencyrepublic.com.

Associate Creative Director

Skive Creative

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

July 2010 – May 2012 (1 year 11 months)

It was my responsibility to guide the overall creative vision for a project, working with my creative team to produce concepts and develop their ideas. I worked closely with the strategy, UX, design, production and technical teams, to realise these ideas and bring them to life.

I was hands-on and relished being involved at every stage, from brief to final execution. My experience helped me to communicate what was required, to understand the complexities of the process and to galvanise everyone into delivering their very best work.
When required, I was happy to roll up my sleeves and contribute my own skills in illustration, video production, photography and copywriting.

I was also involved in organising the annual Cann’t Skinny Cubs event for young creatives for two years running.

Mat Denney's Education

Norwich School of Art and Design

First Class Honours, Fine Art

1992 – 1995

Three inspiring years where my love of printmaking (etching, lithography, screen-printing), drawing and photography were allowed to flourish.

It was here that I was first introduced to computers in the form of an Apple PowerMac in 1994. It came with an incredible piece of software called Photoshop 2.5 which I recall didn’t have layers - this is our generation’s equivalent of “I remember when all this was fields.” That was the beginning of my interest in digital. I graduated with a First in 1995.

Nene College, Northampton

Art Foundation (Merit), Art & Design

1990 – 1991

I specialised in photography and printmaking and graduated in 1991 with Merit. I then took a year out during which I taught an Adult Education class in Printmaking – also at Nene College.
