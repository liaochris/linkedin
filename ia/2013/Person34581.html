Derrick Olivier's Summary

I've always had a passion for the arts, but I'm also driven by that entrepreneurial spirit that has inspired me to take on business roles in marketing and management.

I worked as a graphic designer for over 5 years, and continue to use and develop my skills further all the time in IT, business, and marketing roles. I love having the freedom to take my ideas and instantly bring them to reality.

Through much of my childhood and teen years, my dad always worked as an international business attorney, and later taught international business law all over the world. As a result I got the opportunity to live in countries all over the world, including the United States, Canada, Belize, China, the UK, Cyprus, the Netherlands… the list goes on. So, I've been around.
From 2005 to mid 2008 my family and I lived in the Netherlands. At first in Hoorn, and then later in Katwijk. I attended Werenfridus in Hoorn, where I took drama and learned to speak Dutch.

While living in the UK, in Berkshire, I flourished in drama while working toward my GCSE under the guidance of Bob Hawxswell. I learned valuable lessons in method acting and pantomiming.
I grew up around computers too, so my desire for a creative output while travelling quickly married with computers, and I took off as a graphic designer. Today, I have great respect for good design.

I studied Theatre Arts in Portland, Oregon under Patrick Tangredi. He taught me about the Commedia Dell’Arte style of acting. I loved it!

I've definitely got the drive. I sing. I dance. I love to act. I love the people in this industry. When I perform, I give everything to that performance. Once of my strengths, I believe, is that I’m a good listener, and I’m normally able to give the director/AD what they’re looking for.

In my free time, I enjoy filmmaking, graphic design, swimming, hiking, traveling, learning new languages, reading up on the latest technology trends, blogging, and visiting with friends.

Specialties

Acting and singing. I still haven't decided which one I enjoy most, so right now I'm doing both as much as I can. I'm a member of Equity, and I'm always looking for new opportunities to hone my skills and try new things.

I am also a very skilled graphic designer with years of industry experience and talent to my name. My business, Olivier Designs, was founded to provide businesses and organizations with the means to create an entire corporate image. The devil's in the details.
