Frederick KAWUMA's Summary

Fred was nominated in Feb 2012 by the Government, as Uganda's candidate for the position of Secretary General of the Inter-African Coffee Organisation (IACO). He has over 18 years of experience as a CEO of various organisations, and more than 23 years of experience in the coffee industry.

The IACO general assembly, at its 52nd sitting, in Abidjan, Cote d'Ivoire, elected him as the new SG on 29 Nov 2012, to take up office in Jan 2013.

Fred Kawuma is an experienced executive, consultant, and trainer, with a strong focus on organisational and institutional development, marketing, corporate issues, and achieving organisational goals.He has considerable experience working with different organisations as well as donors (EU, World Bank, DfID, USAID) and has expertise in institutional development, commodities, policy analysis, and cross-cutting issues. He has worked with public and private organisations in the areas of organisational and institutional development, accountability systems development and is a champion of integrity in business operations. His personnel development skills have also enabled him to mentor and empower others in leadership positions and those managing newly established organisations. He is a member on a number of boards and has served on several boards of both local and international organisations. In Feb 2004, he received commendation from the US Congress for his work in the coffee industry in Africa.

Fred has a proven track record in pioneering and facilitating new initiatives. He has excellent team skills -- functioning, facilitating, and leading dynamically in and working through teams. He spearheaded the setting up of the Africa Management Forum (AMF), and organised four successful AMF annual summits. Launched an integrity campaign in Uganda, to initiate awards where a new opportunity was provided for individuals to be recognised for their success in taking a firm stand as icons of Integrity in the face of corruption in society.
