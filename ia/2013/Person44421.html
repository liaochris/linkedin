Felipe Tofani's Summary

My name is Felipe Tofani and i never know what to write here. I know you don't read this quite often but i have to be straight to the point here.

My top 3 skills:

- I read the term Renaissance Man somewhere and i think it might suit me in a way. I started designing things back in 2003 and it was the days before the web boomed. A few years later i got really into webdesign and i never looked back. Typography, graphic design, UI, lettering; this is my world.

- I am not crazy or anything but i am pretty obsessed with good design. I believe design is more than a lifestyle and i really like what i do for a living. I like my designs, layouts, UIs to be as good as possible. Time permitting, of course. We live in a world of deadlines and i like to keep my promises.

- Some people would call me a Nerd and that is almost right. I love the web and everything that comes with it. I like techy stuff, computers, apps and what makes them work. I am always looking for new references, new fonts, new programs, new sites. And when i say always, i am being sincere. I have been blogging about design and tech stuff since 2005, almost everyday. Go to Pristina.org and check it out.
