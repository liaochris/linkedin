David Gioiella's Summary

David's career spans over 18 years of award winning work. He is a pioneer in the world of reality television editorial having set the look and feel for the hit shows Cribs and Diary on MTV.

Campaigns and films he's cut and produced have been honored at the Sundance Film Festival, Cannes Lions, Promax, BDA, Tellys, The One Show and others. In addition to his advertising work David has been involved in concepting and editing on-air campaigns for the hit shows Burn Notice, Covert Affairs, and Common Law on USA. Project Runway All Stars for Lifetime, and Inside the Actor's Studio on Bravo, to list just a few.

David has also lent his editorial and producing skills to numerous campaigns for the Make a Wish Foundation, the New York Red Cross, The Evelyn Lauder Breast Cancer Research Foundation, and others.

In addition he serves as VP on the Board of Directors of the New York City chapter of the AICE, which promotes mentorship, knowledge and learning about the discipline of editing to professionals in the world of advertising, broadcast and commercials.

A partner in Northern Lights, named one of INC magazines fastest 500 growing companies in the USA. Also a partner in the design boutique Mr. Wonderful, and the bi-costal creative production company Bodega. As well as a founder of the NYC audio mixing and music composition boutique, SuperExploder.

AICE

Nonprofit Organization Management industry

January 2012 – Present (1 year 11 months) Greater New York City Area

The Association of Independent Creative Editors (AICE) is an international association which represents the interests of independent creative editorial companies and their editors.

AICE is dedicated to the advancement and promotion of the creative editor and of the increasingly important role the creative editor plays in the media creation process. For example, more than 85% of all network television commercials edited in the United States and Canada are edited by AICE members.

As a trade organization, the AICE will continue to:

* Represent the creative editorial community within the advertising industry.

* Disseminate information on all aspects of editorial and post production for the edification of its membership.

* Develop industry standards and common business practices and procedures.

* Facilitate dialogue on key issues with agencies, advertisers, and suppliers.

* Educate advertising agencies and advertisers on all aspects of editorial and post production
