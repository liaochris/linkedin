Neal Bailey's Experience

Founder / CEO

BXR Motors Inc.

July 2007 – Present (6 years 2 months)

BXR Motors, Inc. is an innovative and forward-thinking development stage company that designs, develops, builds, and markets the widely anticipated Bailey Blade Roadster.

The Company’s founder, Neal Bailey, has become an instant Internet celebrity because he committed to building the high-performance car on a shoestring budget and regularly uploaded images and time-lapse video footage detailing how the car took shape.

Today, Neal’s vision has grown way beyond an ambitious idea and led to the development of a curvaceous super car that will be available with a zero-emission electric powertrain, or a 550 horsepower, 5.4L V8 combustion engine. Extraordinary performance, outstanding driving dynamics and unique design are only some of the key attributes that will distinguish the Bailey Blade Roadster from other high-performance sports cars. The company is also looking for new ways to infuse top performance with advanced technologies aimed at reducing fuel consumption and CO2 emissions. BXR Motors, Inc. is also working on a 100% electric powertrain, which will be powered by leading-edge lithium phosphate battery technologies.

The BXR Motors, Inc. website regularly receives in excess of 200,000 hits every month and the car has been featured in various special interest publications, blogs, and newspaper articles such as “The Times”, “Der Spiegel”, and “The Rob Report”. To date, we have received various offers from prestigious publishers to cover the official unveiling of the car, along with inquiries from dealerships and customers who want to reserve a car.

BXR Motors, Inc. is a Delaware Corporation with executive offices located in Fort Worth, Texas. Our website is available at http://www.BXRMotors.com.
