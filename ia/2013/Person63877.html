Jim Fruchterman's Summary

I’m always excited about the new things ahead. In each of the areas where we work – Global Literacy, Human Rights and the Environment – the Benetech team is busy inventing the future of technology for social good. And, our newest effort is Benetech Labs, a place where we can experiment and respond to the hundreds of ideas people bring us for Silicon Valley technology to do more for society.

http://benetech.org/about-us/who-we-are/

Specialties
