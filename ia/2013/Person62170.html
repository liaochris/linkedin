Jen Sinkler's Summary

Longtime fitness writer and editor. Former editorial director of fitness content for Experience Life magazine, a publication I worked for from 2003 to the very end of 2012. Before that, I worked in marketing for three years.

Recently launched Thrive With Jen Sinkler at www.jensinkler.com, a website focused on fitness, food, gym fashion, good living and great health.

Also doing personal training, and coaching a women's-only strength class at The Movement Minneapolis (www.movementminneapolis.com).
