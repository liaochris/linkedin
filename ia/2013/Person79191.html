Laurie Ayers' Summary

Laurie Ayers is a Michigan work from home mom who started her first business in 1988. Retired from the federal government, Laurie now spends her time as a nonverbal communication, body language and deception awareness. One of her passions is in the area of crime prevention/personal safety. She is the founder of Ayers on the Side of Caution. LA is also the President & CEO of Income Wax, Inc., a scented candle business.


Favorite quotes -
