Andrea Ciobanu, MPA, JD's Summary

I practice in the areas of civil law and litigation and in particular: family law, immigration, personal injury/premise liability, civil rights, medical malpractice, and ADA compliance. I am dedicated to providing the highest quality legal services at a cost-effective and efficient manner. I pride myself on consistently delivering dynamic and innovative advocacy to my clients. I also pride myself in giving back to the community through public and pro bono service, in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to the following: Indiana Protective Order Pro Bono Project, Indiana Coalition Against Domestic Violence, Indiana State Bar Association's Pro Bono Appellate Program, Indiana State Bar's Civil Rights of Children and ADR Committees, Indiana State Bar's Wellness Committee, Indiana State Bar Association's Diversity Committee, IndyBar's Steering Committee where I also serve as a permanent lecturer, Indianapolis Bar Association's Go Green Committee, Indiana Mediation Association, PFJr Foundation, IndyGals, Indianapolis' Women's Mentoring Network, National Association of Professional Women, Keep Indianapolis Beautiful, Indiana Trial Lawyer's Association and other such organizations.

I am also a registered civil mediator, domestic mediator, elder mediator and foreclosure facilitator. I assist with civil mediation coaching at IU McKinney; I also assist in coaching elder mediation training sessions through the Hoeller Training.

As a board member on the ADR Section of the Indiana State Bar Association, as well as the Indiana Association of Mediators, I served as the joint amici representative before the Indiana State Supreme Court. As a representative of both groups, we argued that the confidentiality of mediation statements must be protected to uphold the sanctity of the mediation process.

Specialties

My passion is Civil Rights and ADA Compliance, and, in particular, issues pertaining to the deaf community. I enjoy advocating for equal access to the legal system for all.

I strive to offer quality legal services and strive to meet my goals of giving back to the public. I have had the privilege of arguing before the Northern and Southern District Courts of Indiana. I have also had the privilege of participating in Seventh Circuit Appellate and Indiana Supreme Court Oral Arguments. While my practice is that of general practice and general civil litigation, I am dedicated to making a difference through the legal process. In particular, I am dedicated to making a difference in the legal process for those that do not have equal access to the legal system. I provide legal services for the deaf community and strive to be the legal voice for the deaf community in Indiana.

My practice has grown so quickly ~ in fact, quicker than I ever thought imaginable. I could not accomplish what I do without the help of my four wonderful law clerks. The assistance of my law clerks with my heavy case load is invaluable. I am looking forward to the time when my dear law clerks are sworn in and join Ciobanu Law, PC as practicing attorneys. I feel extremely blessed and satisfied to be surrounded by these wonderful individuals who also want to make a difference in the legal community as I do. It is also refreshing to practice law with such hard working and ethical individuals.

Although this is our employment, we have collaborated on pro bono cases, resulting in positive outcomes for others ~ whether it was assisting victims of domestic violence, asylum refugees, families that could not afford appellate representation, or high conflict cases protecting children in dangerous situations, we advocated diligently.

Practicing law is more than a job to me. It is a life of affecting other individuals positively through the legal process. I thoroughly enjoy the practice of law and practicing law is more meaningful with the wonderful people that surround me to support Ciobanu Law, PC in making a difference in the legal community.

2007 – Present (6 years)
