Kellie Andrews' Summary

I am the Founder of the Huntsville Hub, the original alternative workspace provider in Huntsville, AL. I have a passion for helping small businesses grow and succeed! The Huntsville Hub caters to the needs of the local business community by providing our clients with quality shared office space, state-of-the-art technology, and a support team dedicated to outstanding customer service. The Hub goes a step further than most shared office facilities. We don’t just provide office space, we get actively involved in our customer’s businesses to help them succeed. I am creative, innovative, and help my customers come up with unique and effective ways to grow and develop their businesses.

My background is in project and operations management, strategic planning, market planning, new business development, and customer service. I am experienced in all aspects of starting, growing, managing, marketing, and expanding new businesses.

Specialties

Strategic Planning, Market Planning, Operations Management, Administration, Customer Service, Project Management, New Business Development, Cost Analysis, Database Development
