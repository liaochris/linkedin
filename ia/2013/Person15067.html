Paul Fricke's Summary

For twenty-five years Paul Fricke's cartooning and illustration have appeared in print and on screen. Specializing in comic book and pop art cartooning, Blue Moon Studios is a one-stop-shop from concept to final digital art.

Paul's art can help you to:
• grab attention
• convey an idea quickly
• tell a story
• enhance a concept
• make 'em laugh
• educate and inform
• "make it stick"

Specialties

illustration, cartooning, comic book art, pop art, superheroes, character design, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Artist and author Paul Fricke specializes in cartooning, comic book, illustration and storyboards since 1985. Blue Moon Studios is a one-stop-shop from concept to final digital art.

Conceived as an outlet for any and all creative pursuits, the company allows Paul and his wife, Mary, also an artist, to balance their art and their livelihood; to work and play and raise a family right at home.
