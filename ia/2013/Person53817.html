Julie-Ann Blackmore's Summary

First and foremost I develop people's minds to guide them in creating the body, health, relationships, skills and lives of their dreams.

It all started whenI was very young I realised I had foresight. I could see into the future, know things that others didn't, read people's minds and see beyond the material into the metaphysical. But it didn't stop there. Curious and experimental by nature I wanted to know more. Through this discovery could I find a way to end human pain? My own prediction aged 10 told me that was my purpose and young as I was I knew it was true

So how and why could I 'know' things beyond logic? This led me down a path of discovery of what actually makes up a human being. I covered many areas including medicine, science, religion, metaphysics, quantum physics, business, relationships and so on. I undertook years of research and personal development to know the things I now know to support us in our individual and collective growth

Throughout my life I was consistently trying and testing my findings on many others, not just myself, to finely tune and fully understand how to create not only the life of our dreams but also the disease free body of our dreams.

My business journey started off as a way of being able to guide individuals to make improvements to their lifes. But as I grew I knew there was more to it than that.

My knowledge needed an outlet and people needed to benefit from it so out came 15 books now published as E books, Art as visual tools, available as originals and prints, Personalised Poetry, Individual Sessions, Seminars and Webinars, Women's Fashion and Accessories (to feed the designer in me) and a lot more surprises for the future.

When you know yourself then you have true power.

If you want to find out more explore www.howtobeyou.com
