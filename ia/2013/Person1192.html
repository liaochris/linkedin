J. Jackson Sr.'s Summary

J. Jackson Sr., is an award winning, accomplished entrepreneur and author who has built a portfolio of subsidiary companies in the industries of real estate, health/ beauty, publishing and business consulting.

Jackson’s business accomplishments and experience have earned him recognition as:

- The 2009 Entrepreneur of the Year by the African American Chamber of Commerce
- Orlando Business Journal’s 2010 Forty under Forty in Business.
- 2010 ONYX Award for Excellence in Business by the LBS Foundation.
- 2013 Black Business Beacon from the Black Business Investment Fund (BBIF)
- 2013 Central Florida Living Legend Honoree

He is also a published author, whose book Cubicle to CEO – The BluePrint for starting your own business, shares with its readers, real life lessons on how to overcome obstacles and turn their passion into profit.

Specialties: Entrepreneurship, Management Consulting, Organizational Development, Strategic Marketing, Leadership Development, Professional Speaking.

Jackson believes in envisioning things that many think are impossible and showing them that anything is possible.

J. Jackson Sr.'s Experience

President

RYSE Media

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Media Production industry

May 2011 – Present (2 years 8 months) Winter Park

RYSE Media Group is the publisher of RYSE Magazine (Recognizing Young Successful Executives & Entrepreneurs).

RYSE (pronounced rise) is a multi-media platform that recognizes and showcases the numerous individuals that are excelling in their careers, whose talents and contributions are often overlooked, a vehicle to educate and empower those who are determined to RYSE to the top, because they understand that the bottom is simply too crowded.

From the first-class, glossy print publication RYSE Magazine, which is entertaining, newsworthy and thought-provoking, to RYSE Online which serves as a valuable resource providing real-time community news and other valuable tools, to RYSE Events which serves as a social network bringing the community together to celebrate each other’s accomplishments, RYSE is truly committed to our vision: To inspire others to want more, do more and achieve more so that one day they will ultimately BE more!

RYSE Signature Events include:

»»The RYSE Awards: A collaborative awards ceremony with Star 94.5 FM

»»The Women on the RYSE Honors: A recognition ceremony dedicated to celebrating successful women in business and in the community.

»»The “Walk-It-Out” 5K Walk, Run, and Zumba-thon for childhood obesity: A community based event supported by the City of Orlando which promotes healthy living initiatives and healthcare awareness.

A partial client list includes national brands such as: Pepsi, ESPN, Walt Disney World, AT&T, TD Bank, BB&T Bank, Universal Studios, the Orlando Magic, Mercedes Benz and Walmart, which featured RYSE Magazine’s Women’s issue on the front page of their community website.

Specialty: A direct connect to the Central Florida’s affluent middle-class executives and entrepreneurs.

Motivational Speaker/ Author

Words of Action Consulting

August 2010 – Present (3 years 5 months) Winter Park

After spending his childhood growing up in a community known for high crime and drug activity, Jay was determined to overcome childhood obstacles and succeed in life.

Because of his own childhood struggles, Jay truly has a passion for reaching back and preparing the next generation of leaders. He consistently gives of his time, talent and treasures to organizations that focus on youth and leadership development. He is also passionate about inspiring others.

Through seminars, published books and keynote speaking engagements, Jackson and his Words of Action provide aspiring entrepreneurs with the inspiration and empowerment they need to pursue their dreams.

Whether speaking to a group of aspiring entrepreneurs or an assembly of youth and young adults, his solid, earth-shaking message is always clear: “We all have the power to dream, we all have the ability to take action toward our dreams and we all possess the talent inside to successfully achieve our dreams.” - J. Jackson Sr.
