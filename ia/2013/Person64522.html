Jack Regan's Summary

Jack is a respected and experienced Catholic Youth Minister, who has been in ministry since 1999 and has worked in most areas of the field including residential retreat centres, school campus ministry, and on many projects and teams at deanery, diocesan and national levels.

In 2006, Jack wrote the UK's first QCA-accredited award in Catholic Youth Ministry. In 2007, he ran the national 'volunteers conference', and from 2005 he chaired the national retreat centres Asst. Directors' group. He was also part of the core team for Flame Congress in 2012, the largest Catholic youth event ever held in the UK, and is currently on the core team for WYD@Home, a groundbreaking event being held this summer.

Jack is known internationally for CatholicYouthWork.com (the world's largest Catholic YM site) and for his work with 'Rebuild My Church'. Later this year, he will also be involved in the brand new ProjectYM.com website, as the only UK contributor.

Jack's current 'day job' is in Crawley Deanery (Sussex, UK) where he is working to extend an existing school Chaplaincy role to a vibrant, large deanery.
