Jennifer Dlugozima's Summary

Jennifer is a highly versatile marketing executive, who sees marketing through a unique lens that blends online disciplines – like Analytics, Search and Conversion Optimization – with traditional marketing and public relations.

She works collaboratively across the organization - IT, Customer Service, Product Development, Human Resources - to implement strategic positioning and new services, and optimize their delivery. She has an uncanny ability to inject metrics into the organization, and use them to measure and improve overall marketing performance.

Jennifer started her career in journalism, working as a business reporter for daily newspapers. She switched to marketing, and served as Marketing Director for the Florida region of Powertel, one of the most successful GSM start-ups in the U.S. In 1998, she developed the first m-commerce trial in the US, presenting results in Nice, France and London, UK Mobile Commerce conferences. She also helped grow the region’s subscriber base from 0 to 100,000 subscribers.

Because of her database marketing skills, she was relocated to Bellevue, WA, when the company was acquired by T-Mobile USA in 2001. As Director of Customer Marketing, she was in charge of developing customer segmentation strategies to retain the company’s most profitable customers.

Later, she turned her attention to online marketing, where she taught herself PPC and SEO, while driving a family-owned business.

She worked in various consulting roles for online and offline marketing for 6 years. Then, joined eSecuritel as VP Interactive Marketing in 2010, and helped the device insurance company expand nationally and internationally.

When the company was acquired by Brightstar Corp, ranked #58 on Forbes 2012 List of America’s Largest Private Companies, she was asked to head up the company’s digital marketing efforts.

Specialties

Strategic positioning and messaging • Tactical implementation of online strategies • Go-to-Market strategy conceptualization and implementation • Analytics • Social Media and Public Relations • Paid Search and Search Engine Optimization • Customer Segmentation • Customer Experience Strategy and Enhancements • Retention & CLV Marketing • Brand Marketing
