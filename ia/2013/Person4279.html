Alex Moore's Projects

  • World Directory of Churches

    • August 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Alex Moore

    The World Directory of Churches is the first attempt to to catalog every single Christian church on the planet, regardless of language, size, creed, or denomination. Our goal is to provide an accurate and up-to-date resource for Christians everywhere, so that the whole world can learn of the diversity and breadth of the faith, and so that anyone who so desires can find a church to call home.

  • Red Colony

    • July 2000 to Present
    Team Members: Alex Moore, James Keener

    Since its founding 12 years ago, Red Colony has grown into the largest Mars colonization and terraforming resource on the Internet, spawned an independent Mars advocacy organization, and attracted hundreds of thousands of visitors. We welcome article submissions and discussion from scientists and laymen alike. The website is currently undergoing a significant reconstruction.

  • DashBrain

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Alex Moore, Geoff Barnes, Katie Jennings

    DashBrain is a real-time social media, SEO and web analytics monitoring tool. It integrates with seven APIs to track brand mentions through social media services, daily search engine positions, and trending Google Analytics data at a glance, through an interactive web application. Since DashBrain was built with responsive web design practices, it is optimized for use on tablets and mobile devices.
