Martin Dickie's Summary

Two opertaing functions

1/ Assisting company owners in restructuring and developing businesses, predominantly with the window and door industry in the UK. Specialising in financial turnround situations and new market development. Undertaking full P+L analysis, procurment and management tools to assist in ongoing micro and macro management of business at all levels of the structure.

2/ A network of door specialists undertaking Door Risk Assessments to ensure that building owners and facility managers comply with latest fire and health and safety legislation and best practice in relation to the buildings use. Assertaining finger trapping risk and fire door conditions and compliance. A free survey that will highlight any areas of need and make recommendations accordingly.

A practicle approach based on the natural usage of the building, undertaken by a network of door specialists throughout the UK.

Operating within the education, health, public sector and commercial buildings of all types.

Working in partnership, as agents and approved installers, with Fingershield, Fireco and ADC for the supply of materials and components where required.
