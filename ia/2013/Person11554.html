Frank Ballabio's Summary

Highly strategic and creative marketing executive with over 15 years experience at companies in the luxury space (current) and consumer digital service and CPG companies including Kaplan Digital Education and Colgate-Palmolive. Managed P&Ls up to $260M. Served on senior leadership team for two start-up business units achieving yearly double-digit growth. Expert in developing emotional brand positioning, discovering rich consumer insights via cutting-edge qualitative and complex quantitative studies, driving break-through creative, and integrating campaigns across mobile platforms, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, email, SEM, SEO, TV/print, and retail. Deep experience in both eCommerce and driving retail sales. Driver of CRM and customer life-cycle strategies with online and offline lead generation, customer acquisition / retention and loyalty programs. Developed multi-year new product innovation pipelines and partnered with R&D to deliver new products to market. Managed Ogilvy, Y&R, Enter: New Media, MEC, Siboney, and Uniworld. Partnered with fragrance houses such as IFF and Firmenich, innovation and research firms such as Continuum, Dr. G.C. Rapaille, and BrainReserve, retailers such as Target and Wal-Mart, licensors such as Nickelodeon and Warner Bros., and ecommerce platform suppliers such as salesforce.com. Extensive international experience. MBA from Kellogg School of Management.

Specialties: Brand Strategy, Brand Management, New Product Innovation, General Management, Market Research, CRM, Concept Writing, Workshop Facilitation, Concept Writing, eCommerce, Online Marketing, Digital Marketing, SEM, SEO, Social Media, Facebook, Twitter, Youtube, Blog, Customer Reviews, Email strategy, Product Management, Customer Acquisition, Customer Retention, Lead Conversion, Integrated Marketing Communications, Mom Marketing, Young Adults, CPG, Health, Beauty, Personal Care,Beverages, Retail
