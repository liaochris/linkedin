Terry Brock, MBA, CSP, CPAE's Summary

I work with organizations to achieve their best increasing sales and profitability. I'm co-author of the best-selling McGraw-Hill book, Klout Matters. It is about social media and how to be most effective with it using Klout as a measuring stick. I help organizations use video, audio, Blogging, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube (!) and more to build marketing and help achieve real-world business goals.
