Jennifer Dalitz's Summary

Welcome to my LinkedIn profile. I'm Jen Dalitz and I’m obsessed with seeing more women in leadership roles, in their workplaces and communities. I write about, speak about and consult on gender balance and my blog www.TheSheEOblog.com attracts up to 70,000 pvpm.

On a professional level, my deep expertise in gender diversity and organisation change drives real outcomes for my clients. I understand the bottom line impact when female talent is under-utilised. Research shows organisations with gender balanced leadership produce financial results up to 35 per cent stronger, and it's my job to help leaders realise this opportunity by finding, retaining and developing the best women in their teams.

I also work with women to help them take charge of their own careers, to define and deliver on their personal and professional goals, and to achieve financial independence.

I absolutely love my work, and am honoured to have received an Edna Ryan Workplace Award for improving the working conditions of Australian women, be in the 40 Young Business Leaders List in 2012, and to represent Australia in a BBC global debate on the advancement of women and the Millennium Goals and the 2010 Women’s Summit in Malaysia.

On a personal level, I live the day to day challenge of balancing motherhood with a demanding career, travel and a farming enterprise. Believe me, behind the calm there is often chaos!

An avid commentator, you’ll often see me quoted in media publications such as BRW, The Financial Review, national newspapers and womens magazines - and on news programs like Lateline Business, SkyNews, 10News and The Finance Quarter.

Whether gender diversity is new on your agenda; you want to supplement your internal diversity expertise; or you’re looking to outsource the development and monitoring of your entire gender diversity programs, I’d be delighted to have a chat and offer my advice.

If you'd like to connect, please send a personalised invitation to introduce yourself.
