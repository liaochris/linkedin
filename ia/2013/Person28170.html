Christopher Swanger's Summary

Advisory to gaming and hospitality companies who have disparate data sources (Big Data) without a reasonable way to make sense of the information. My focus is to have the conversations with the business stake holders to understand their obstacles as it pertains to data harvesting, correlation and visual verification through video/data association. Once we understand the problem and the business need, we create real-time connections to the data sources and use the eConnect software suite to format and aggregate the data sources while combining it with synchronized video to provide business decision makers with all the evidence they need to make the right decisions.
