Kurt Tilkin's Summary

After obtaining my law degree (matser in Law, KU Leuven), I started an exciting journey in sales and customer care. I had the privilege to work in both big established environments (Belgacom, Xerox) as in challenging startups (BT-Yucom). Having the end-responsibility of an internal contactcenter (Tiscali) and working as a manager of an outsourcer (Sitel -IN2COM) gives me a view on both sides of customer care in an outsourced environment.

Specialismen: Seasoned & flexible Contact Center Manager with a unique mix of highlevelled background and in-depth experiences in customer care and call center environments, both internal as outsourced.

Experienced in setting-up, implementing or managing complex inbound, outbound and telesales projects in several fast-moving industries.

Typically people call me a teamplayer, a dynamical people manager with and oriented with also a look to quality.
