Isaac Vail's Summary

Film composer with experience using various music production and writing programs on past projects. Currently working on an expanding video portfolio with examples of scoring for video and film. Interested in writing music for video games and television as well, specializing in underscore and mood pieces. Received a Master Certificate in Orchestration for Film and TV in June 2010.

Specialties

Composing for film, scoring, mixing, mastering, remixing, arranging, orchestration, editing in Final Cut, Logic Express, Adobe Photoshop, visualization, Mac OS X, Sibelius, creative, art, concepts, atmosphere, loop-ready content, MIDI mock-ups
