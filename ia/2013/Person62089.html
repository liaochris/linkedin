Jenny Hahn's Summary

My creative profession extends across platforms. I express ideas and emotions in paint and in pixels; creating, facilitating, sharing and empowering.

As a fine art painter working mostly in acrylic, I convey a unique uplifting quality through bold, colorful expression—whether figuratively or abstractly. I find inspiration in nature, color, meditation, yoga, biology and sacred geometry. Viewers often comment on the happiness and peace of mind the paintings bring them. My studio currently resides in Mission, KS.

My love for color and expressing emotion carries over into my freelance graphic design work. My gift is to distill and organize information in a way that leads to simple, easy to use, inspiring beauty.

I facilitate painting-for-process workshops through Creative Nectar Studio in Mission, KS, for anyone yearning to deepen their connection to their authentic self, develop present-moment awareness, learn to trust the process and get their creative juices flowing in a safe, supportive environment.

My goal is to transform lives and shape the world with inspiring art and empowered, healing experiences.

Specialties

Fine-art acrylic painting. Color theory. Workshop facilitation.
Design programs: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver. Mac platform.
