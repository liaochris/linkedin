Gerry Crispin's Summary

CareerXroads is a small international consulting practice founded by myself and my business partner, Mark Mehler.

Simply stated, we facilitate a dialogue between corporate staffing leaders through small-group invitation-only peer-to-peer meetings (CareerXroads' Colloquium) throughout the year. Our aspiration is to contribute to a growing community of recruiting professionals who are dedicated to improving their business' long-term performance through the staffing function.

My passion is to understand how firms design and build staffing processes, the technology to enhance them and the systems to manage them.

I want to know more about the playing field where candidates and employers meet; how firms tout their latest opening and job seekers‘game’their way in. Does any of it truly make a difference (to a firm's performance)? is there a better way? I'm pretty sure there is.

I'm more than an observer. I want t influence future staffing models to a) be driven by the evidence (b) offer candidates experiences that empower them to make better decisions in parallel with corporate selection practices, and (c) embed and embrace technology-based networking tools as a primary means to build 2-way candidate pipelines.

To that end, in 2011, I joined with other volunteers in the industry to form a non-profit, TalentBoard, to oversee the Candidate Experience Awards. Check out this growing movement to define, measure and honor the firms who are treating candidates as partners in the hiring process at www.thecandes.org.



Specialties: Staffing business models, Recruiting processes, Hiring strategies and tactics. Candidate Experience, Source of Hire
