Walker Willingham's Summary

Currently available to provide creative, affordable, and conscientious assistance with GIS projects, from data management and editing, topological editing, geocoding, or map production, to analysis, cartography, or web delivery.

Background in software testing combines with my GIS training, and communication and presentation skills, to provide a well rounded perspective on your project.

Also open to invitations for being hired onto creative teams dedicated to applying GIS to analysis of real world problems and/or seeking to create sustainable solutions, or to inform the sensible direction of public policy.

Especially interested in projects which seek to leverage the enlightening power of maps toward the service of conservation, sustainability, social and economic justice, or planetary healing. In the meantime I'm not above taking on laborious data intensive jobs requiring perseverance and patience.
