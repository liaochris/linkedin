Caryl is a petite gal, slightly tomboy, mostly rocker-chick, all big ideas - living in The Beaches of Toronto, running after her seemingly tireless daughters while trying to hold yoga positions that make her look like a contortionist.

By night she is a superhero (so her daughters think), but her daytime alter-ego helps her clients achieve all types of crazy goals in crazy timelines with crazy budgets alongside some stellar people that could only be described as interactive magicians. Caryl's only kryptonite? The words "We can't do that". <HULK SMASH>

Goals? To win the LottoMax and buy all the shares of Apple one can afford, spend time in South Africa playing cards with Zebras and shoot a puck around with Carey Price.

Specialties: Over 10 years of Strategy, Design, Creative Direction, Art Direction, UX, Branding, Trending and making clients smile.

Decent golf swing, good slap shot, swift jab, and awesome downward dog.
