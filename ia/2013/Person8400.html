Anne Sorensen's Summary

Strategic marketing specialist with more than 20 years experience in Melbourne, London, New York and Brisbane, across the retail, not for profit and tourism sectors.

Founder and Director of Marketing Is Us, a Brisbane based, globally focused marketing agency, servicing clients nationally and internationally. Marketing Is Us is dedicated to empowering businesses with strategic marketing services and tools.

Created the Marketing Is Us Tool Kit, a range of affordable, electronic and reusable marketing templates in six individual packages.

In 2012 commenced doctoral studies investigating value creation in social media based cause brand communities.

Specialties: Strategic business and marketing planning; brand building; holistic, integrated, innovative and cost effective marketing strategies.
