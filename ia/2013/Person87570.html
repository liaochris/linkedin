Martin Bjergegaard's Summary

15 years as an entrepreneur with several multi million companies and exits behind me - and hopefully even more in front of me:-) Together with the best friends and entrepreneurs one can imagine I co-founded the "start up factory" Rainmaking 6 years ago. Since then we have created, and invested in, more than 60 start ups. One of our most remarkable initiatives is the leading European accelerator Startupbootcamp that we build from scratch and that is now present in 5 cities. Together with Jordan Milne I have written a book called Winning Without Losing that during the next 6 months will be published in 30+ countries by leading publishers. I love to connect with inspiring entrepreneurs, authors and investors.

Specialties

Entrepreneurship in all it's forms, inspiring others through books, blog posts and speeches, coming up with fresh ideas, bringing products to market, building and leading world class start up teams.

Martin Bjergegaard's Experience

Author

We wrote a book about how to become wildly successful while at the same time living a whole, happy and balanced life. Co-founder and partner

Rainmaking

Partnership; 11-50 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

January 2007 – Present (6 years 10 months) London, United Kingdom

At Rainmaking we get business ideas, start up companies, get customer, build the team - and eventually make an exit. During the last 6 years we have launched 15 "home grown" start ups. 8 of these we are running today, 5 have been closed down, and 2 have been exited to industry leaders. Today we are 9 partners and around 100 team members in our offices in Copenhagen, London and Berlin.

Mentor and co-founder

Startupbootcamp

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 10 months)

Startupbootcamp is the leading European start up accelerator. We are present in Berlin, Copenhagen, Amsterdam, Dublin, and Haifa in Israel. Each year we invest in and help out 50 new kick ass start ups.

Founder and chairman

BetterNow

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

November 2010 – Present (3 years)

BetterNow is taking online fundraising to the next level through an integrated video, payment and campaigning web functionality. Create your own fundraising campaign in seconds, spread the word through social media and emails to your friends, and sit back while money rolls in to your favorite causes. It doesn’t matter whether you are running a marathon, climbing a mountain, growing a beard, hosting a party or just pledge to stay away from junk food for a month. You choose your challenge and your cause, BetterNow provide you with the tools and support your need to succeed as a kick ass online fundraiser. All seamlessly, completely free for you to use, and with a wealth of charities and causes to pick from. Launched in Denmark 2011, expanded to Sweden early 2012 and now rolling out internationally.

Managing Director

Frokost.dk

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Food & Beverages industry

October 2005 – December 2006 (1 year 3 months) Copenhagen Area, Denmark

In 2005 I founded Frokost.dk as a B2B online food concept. Today the company is still owned by Rainmaking, and in 2012 we are seeing a revenue of around 13m EUR and a solid profit. Each day we serve 7,500 meals for 300+ Danish work places.

Business Development Manager
