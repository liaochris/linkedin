Branon Dempsey's Summary

Branon Dempsey is the CEO, Founder and Training Director of Worship Team Training® a ministry providing workshops and online resources for local worship ministries. A Worship Leader, Clinician, Author and Songwriter, called to lead worship teams, leaders and artists in becoming authentic leaders of worship and followers of Jesus Christ. MDiv. Master of Arts in Worship Art Studies and Evangelism by Hope International University, Bachelor of Music in Composition and Performance by University of Houston and has been formally trained by Maranatha! Music

Branon Dempsey responded to God's call to ministry in 1993 to help connect people to Christ through worship ministry. God led Branon to establish For His Music (2002) and Worship Team Training (2004), through the support of great friends, partners and churches. The purpose of Worship Team Training is to inspire, nurture and encourage effective worship leading team skills, while fostering healthy relationships through the power team synergy for the call of honoring God while serving His Church. Today WTT is committed to grow along-side of church worship teams, as we serve 40+ yearly across the country.

Branon is a Training Partner with Yamaha Corporation of America | Worship Resources, Writer and on the Panel of Experts for Shure Microphones, an Instructor/Speaker at Christian Musician Summit Conferences, Regular Column Writer for Worship Musician Magazine, Featured WTT Radio Show Host, Contributor for Creator Leadership Network, News Channel Church and TCMR iLevite Magazine and CCLITV Video Training Contributor. Additionally, Branon is a writer for Religious Product News, Technologies for Worship Magazine and Mark Condon Music. Also he is the author/publisher of all WTT books & training material. Supported/Endorsed by Line 6, D’Addario, Proclaim Church Presentation Software, iSing Worship and endorsed by Promark Drumsticks and Jim Hewett Guitars.
