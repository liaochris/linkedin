Gabrijel Gavranović's Projects

  • Website SnowKeys.com

    • March 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Gabrijel Gavranović, Marcel Van Schooten, Eric van Hall, Bing Voorham, Tim Beekhuis, Niek Brinkman, Martijn van Nieuwenhoven

    The idea of SnowKeys was born out of our passion for winter sports and the mountains in combination with our fascination for the power of the Internet to bring likeminded people together. We are aiming to become the “Key” to the mountains in every possible way. SnowKeys aims to be your digital key to a perfect mountain getaway all year round.

    Our primary goal is to bring together home owners who want to safely rent out their mountain properties and likeminded people looking for an original and affordable place to stay in the mountains. This can be just a bed, but also a luxuriously catered chalet.

    What makes us really different from other accommodation websites is that we combine the information of the accommodations with real time and statistic data of snow and weather conditions in the vast majority of mountain resorts, so you can quickly and easily find the best place to stay with the best snow conditions you could wish for.

    SnowKeys will also be a vibrant community of people who love the mountains, so you can be sure that whenever you want to travel to the mountains, you will always find a friendly host to stay. And if you happen to be so lucky to own a place in the mountains, you will always be able to find friendly and likeminded (paying) guests, soon to be friends…

    Our goal is to create the biggest online community of mountain loving people where they can share their places, experiences and love for the peaks in a wide variety of ways. We are dedicated to keep improving and innovating our services to this community every single day for years to come.
