Kristen Beireis' Summary

Kristen has been marketing herself and others online since 2000. She has marketed herself as a Professional Singer/Actress in NYC online and offline for six years. While an actress, she presented herself to temporary agencies where she earned highly regarded temporary Executive Administrative positions. In 2000, Kristen and her husband started an online retail store where she did all the administrative, design and marketing work. She has also done online marketing exclusively for coaches and other personal transformation professionals since 2006.

Kristen has spent many years getting over her highly skeptical ways of thinking when it comes to trusting others with her personal and private life. It's that skeptical thinking that taught her to test and research in order to make sure she could trust someone enough to call them a friend. She now uses those testing and research skills by combining it with her marketing knowledge to find the places where trust building opportunities are being missed.

Specialties

Online business systems creation and implementation. Marketing plan implementation. Running businesses from the heart, with integrity, professionalism and individuality. Many years of following my passion and making a career of it.
