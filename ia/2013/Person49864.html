Phil Ashforth's Summary

I work closely and personally with clients, developing innovative marketing strategies, then working on delivery of agreed marketing tools and channels to generate the very best return on investment.

My experience covers corporate clients, international, retail, finance, education, manufacturing, IT, NHS, councils and much more besides.

I have worked at senior levels in each sector, yet I am equally at home working with SME's, sole traders or entrepeneurs.

With an interest in campaign development, I like to develop the range of marketing interfaces for my clients, so that all access points from the client or customer perspective are met with uncompromising quality.

I am interested in meeting new potential clients, but also in developing partnerships and in considering any other business opportunities that may be appropriate.
