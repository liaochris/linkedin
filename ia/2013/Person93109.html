Michael Trudel's Summary

There is no 'perfect brew' of competencies and experiences that can tackle every assignment, but rather a long-simmering stock of trials, tribulations and successes that meld together to create a well-rounded communications professional. This is my personal recipe for success.

With a diverse background in concept development, communications strategy and creative management gained through 20+ years of hard work and challenging deadlines, I bring a unique perspective and expertise that has helped create powerful solutions to answer many a client's business challenges and opportunities.

I am proud to be a decorated United States Navy veteran; the highlight of my six-year tour was spending 12 months with NATO as a hand-picked member of the Standing Naval Forces Atlantic enlisted staff, providing communications support for the Commandant, his international staff and all of the NATO ships assigned to the fleet. I'm a graduate in communications from Wayne State University, Detroit and I was selected to complete a comprehensive business development seminar through Washington University in St. Louis.

Specialties

My communications, creative and management background has proven most effective in digital, video and print media, including: sales presentations, corporate and marketing communications, proposal development, video production, training, public relations, experiential marketing, Web content and event marketing.
