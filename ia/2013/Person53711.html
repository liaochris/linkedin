Bruce Mann's Summary

Bruce Mann formed KB Building Products in 2006. KB Building Products is one of the fastest growing housewrap manufacturers in the United States. KB Building Products markets and manufactures BreatheEZ™ housewrap throughout the United States. KB Building Products provides custom imprinting marketing solutions to builders and building supply companies. Advertise your company with logos and contact info printed on BreatheEZ™ & BreatheEZ™ Plus building wrap. Increase the power and value of your building envelope. Visit our website for more information about custom imprint housewrap here: http://www.kb-bp.com

Bruce Mann has over 25 years of sales experience with weather resistant and air barriers and dealing with foreign companies and countries in importing processes as well as domestic processes and manufacturing.
See: www.kb-bp.com for info about our products.

Bruce Mann plays a critical role in rapid growth of an early-stage company and helps position it for profitable sale.
Bruce is a proactive, success-driven sales professional with a consistent history of top ranking. Bruce successfully launched many new products and built a strong network of distributors and sales representatives across the United States. Bruce is entrepreneurial and competitive; not afraid to take calculated risks. Bruce focuses on solutions, uses a consultative sales approach that focuses on relationship building, has an in-depth product knowledge and holds high standards in customer service and is skilled at communicating enthusiasm for a product’s value. Bruce is business savy with a good eye for market trends. Bruce has exceptional networking skills, is energized by new challenges and opportunities and willing to travel. Bruce is an excellent mentor with solid understanding of what it takes to succeed. Specializing in interests in green building and sports-related products.
