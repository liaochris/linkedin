Joy C. Daniels, M.Ed., CCC-SLP's Summary

Joy has 18 years of experience as a Medical Speech-Language Pathologist working with the geriatric population. She has recently started her own home-based private practice, Dysphagy, Ltd., where she is able to focus on her interest of helping people to "Eat. Drink. Enjoy Life." again.

Dysphagy, Ltd. is currently accepting new patients on a outpatient basis and prefers to provide "house calls."
