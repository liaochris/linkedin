Ann Tardy's Summary

From Silicon Valley corporate attorney to award-winning advocate for middle managers, mentoring expert, and adventurer, I've never met a dull moment or a person who wasn’t dying to make a difference. I have a unique skill for combining the art of management with the science of behavioral economics to interpret the stories we tell ourselves. In the process, I am constantly revealing how we are each hardwired to hold ourselves back. And in the end, I help to create a new emotional context to prepare people to execute in ever-changing environments.

At LifeMoxie, I have the distinct pleasure to work with some of the best and the brightest consultants in the business of change. We are considered one of the top thought leaders on transforming middle management. Committed to business as "unusual," we help visionary organizations unleash moxie where they need it most – in the middle.

In Summer 2011, I circumvented my own hardwiring to cycle over 4,200 miles from San Francisco to New Jersey in The Moxie Ride, a cross-country bicycle ride I created to research and document what people love about their jobs and their managers. The documentary was released November 2011. During the Summer of 2012, I cycled again from Key West, FL to Bar Harbor, ME.

I am the author of LifeMoxie! Ambition on a Mission - 9 Strategies for Taking Life by the Horns (published in 2007), and Moxie for Managers - The Secret to Evolving from Manager to Leader (published 2011).
