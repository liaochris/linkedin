Kevin Dobbs' Summary

Experienced technology executive. Nearly 20 years of success managing sales, marketing, business development, mergers and acquisitions, product marketing and operational functions for both public and start-up companies.

Highly knowledgeable regarding SaaS [Software as a Service], on-demand business models as well as enterprise-class software applications for talent management, HCM, CRM, Financials and ERP.

Adept at establishing new market categories as well as raising capital and working with venture capitalists and private equity firms.

Kevin Dobbs' Experience

CEO and Founder

Montclare

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

March 2013 – Present (9 months) Oakland, CA

Montclare is a leading provider of SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) resources including products, services and the premier online destination for all matters SaaS. Montclare develops the SaaS Top 250, the industry's leading report on the most influential SaaS companies in the world. The company also provides strategic direction designed to assist companies transitioning to a SaaS model as well as directing current SaaS companies on optimizing business models, controlling costs, improving revenues and delivering world-class software. Regularly trusted by the world's largest software companies, Montclare ensures results, which improve growth, innovation and profitability.
