Elana Rudick's Summary

In 2009 Elana said goodbye to the world of cubicles to combine two of her passions; design and entrepreneurship. And with that, DESIGN IS YUMMY was born.

Elana is a skilled and talented professional that designs with a clean and modern aesthetic. Her goal is to exceed client expectations and push the limits of her own creativity.

She also cares. Elana donates time and creative skills to non-profits such as MADA, Power of Movement, Musician's Against Parkinson's, The Weekend To End Breast Cancer, and Mazon Canada.

Specialties: Creating fresh and effective designs.

Elana Rudick's Experience

Founder, Lead Graphic Designer

DESIGN IS YUMMY

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Graphic Design industry

December 2009 – Present (3 years 9 months)

DESIGN IS YUMMY is a Montreal-based graphic design studio that specializes in creating modern print and web design. We are your one-stop marketing shop offering design, printing, and social media solutions for local and international businesses.

We believe that great design is both intellectual and visual. Since our inception in 2009, DESIGN IS YUMMY has worked closely with clients to understand their businesses, and their competition, in order to create effective promotional materials. Our specialty is producing engaging designs that cohesively communicate brand messaging for startups and well-established companies.

At DESIGN IS YUMMY, quality service is at our core. We strive to exceed client expectations by building strong relationships based on professionalism, reliability and outstanding creative results.

From full brand development to websites, DESIGN IS YUMMY has your marketing needs covered. Whether its designing logos, billboards, brochures, or managing your Facebook page, we make it our business to make yours look good.
