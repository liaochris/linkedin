Erik O'Donnell's Summary

Visual Effects work for film, television and music videos.

With four years of professional work in the VFX industry, I've had the fortune of being involved in many aspects of the field. From previz, on-set assistance to editing and final delivery. This has provided the chance to work with directors and producers and to be the lead on projects. I enjoy the camaraderie, contributions and new learning that this work provides.

Specialties: Nuke, Photoshop, AfterEffects, Final Cut Pro, Premiere Pro, 3ds Max, Maya, Dreamweaver, Boujou, Mocha, SynthEyes, Combustion and Shake.
