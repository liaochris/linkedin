Susan Scanland MSN, CRNP, GNP-BC, CDP's Summary

Antipsychotic Reduction Boot Camp Webinars and Seminars are designed for current national initiatives (CMS, AHCA, Senate Bill 3604):

• 1-2% of dementia residents die from antipsychotic use in 3-6 months
• 1 death occurs for every 11 to 33 helped
• Stroke rate increases 2 to 3-fold
• Pneumonia rates triple
• Hip fracture risk increases 20-40%
• Cognition decline accelerates
• Hospitalization rates double
• Functional decline occurs

References: info@dementiaconnection.com

Susan offers 30 years of dementia experience to assist facilities with her unique system to reduce antipsychotic use. As a Dementia Consultant for 300 secure-unit residents, Susan's rate is 5%; the US average is 23.8%.

Antipsychotic Boot Camp participants (nurses/interdisciplinary team) are prepared over four hours. Susan offers an Advanced Antipsychotic Boot Camp with case studies for prescribers and pharmacists in nursing homes.

Participants share their Antipsychotic Reduction Boot Camp feedback on Dementia Connection's YouTube Channel in the video "Antipsychotic Reduction Seminar."

Webinars can be divided into 2 two-hour programs. Live seminars are available in the US/Canada.
For additional information please call 570-586-0655 or email through Linked in or info@dementiaconnection.com

Susan offers other dementia-related seminars including a two-hour workshop on communication skills for nursing assistants, dietary and housekeeping aids to recognize the strong link between poor dementia communication skills and challenging behaviors.

Susan (National Speakers Association Member) has spoken in 41 states on other related topics: Alzheimer's, other dementias, depression, delirium, adverse drug events in elders. She has presented over 100 CE national/state annual seminars plus 500 presentations to staff and family members at senior communities. Dementia Connection provides educational marketing presentations to boost admissions to retirement or adult day care centers.
