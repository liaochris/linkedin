Jade Doel's Projects

  • LightSpace

    • August 2012 to November 2012
    Team Members: Jade Doel, Nick Gill

    LightSpace started by studying the impact of the spatial shift from traditional domestic dining rooms to contemporary, open plan dining spaces.
    This shift proved beneficial to some inhabitants but simultaneously became a detriment to the tasks associated with dining room experiences. These issues included; a lack of respect for spatial definition, decreased acoustic qualities, increased distractions and the formation of impediments on cultural and traditional values and tasks, such as the perception of dinner as ‘family time’.
    I saw an opportunity to challenge these matters through a tailor-made fixture that employed the technological advantages LED lighting presented over conventional lighting as well as experimenting with untraditional materials and construction.
