Harper West, MA, TLLP's Summary

After a career in corporate communications and marketing, I found my passion and earned my master's in clinical psychology. In my practice, I provide high-quality, evidence-based individual and family psychotherapy. I have experience working with all ages of clients, including co-occurring disorders and severely mentally ill.

As author of "Pack Leader Psychology," I have developed a powerful new concept that I use with clients to help them decode their behavior using the simple, primal wisdom of the animals. Pack Leader Psychology also provides a new paradigm for rethinking how we look at mental illness that tosses out the stigmatizing labels and confusing jargon of the mental health profession and gives people real tools to make changes in their personalities and relationships.

Because of my expertise, I am able to speak and write on parenting, relationships, career transitions, and all behavioral health issues including anxiety, depression, ADHD and others.
