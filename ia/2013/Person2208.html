Anne Cook's Summary

Efficient but creative problem solver and team player who can also work independently as a project manager. Goal is to maximize client objectives by helping develop and present information for target audiences in a clear, concise, effective manner. A key part of my approach is to establish clear and respectful communication channels for collaboration with subject matter experts (SMEs) and other stakeholders.

• More than 20 years of experience in instructional design and teaching/training, writing/editing, event and media promotion and branding (independent projects), process solutions and documentation, multimedia design, audio and video project management.
• Content generalist who adapts easily to various organizational missions and target audience requirements. Focused on editorial quality control and document integrity/usability.
• Familiar with various ISD methodologies, SCORM, and 508 compliance standards.
• Highly experienced working with SMEs to define content objectives and delivery approaches. Clients include the private sector/contractors, the federal government, and non-profit/educational organizations.
