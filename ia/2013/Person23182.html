Chris Countey's Summary

From Borgata and Aéropostale to local plumbers and universities, I’ve developed digital marketing strategies for companies of all shapes and sizes.

The Sales Team relied upon my expertise and knowledge of SEO, Social Media, Ecommerce, and Inbound Content development to qualify potential clients.

I’ve been setting the strategy for SEO campaigns for clients of many different types, from large retail businesses and ecommerce sites, to sites for professionals, and sites for small-to-medium businesses.

I’ve conducted deep level audits of sites for SEO and conversion issues. I’ve diagnosed Google penalties for Panda and Penguin, and provided recommendations and solutions for those problems.

I’ve used and introduced a wide range of software to Webimax for SEO, Project Management, Link Building, and Content Creation. I’ve acted as a information resource for the organization, maintaining a vigilant eye upon the industry for news and updates, and acted as an evangelist for improvements and updates based upon that news.
