Melissa Li's Summary

I have experience in performance arts, administrative work, and media, specifically film and video.

As a composer, I write musicals, score films and games, and write songs for myself and my band. I serve as a booking agent for myself and other bands, and hope to grow this part of my business.


My main goal is to combine creative energy, administrative efficiency, and business acumen to create ventures that excite me and have meaning, whether through social activism or personal growth.

Melissa Li & The Barely Theirs

From performing countless venues across the nation to hosting a women's rock festival in Brooklyn, Melissa Li & The Barely Theirs are known for its country-fried, sugar-high repertoire and crafty songwriting with a feminist conscience.

Melissa formed MLBT with drummer Ashley Baier, guitarist Chris Takita, and bassist Darren Lipper in late 2009. Since their formation, they have performed at Union Hall, Bowery Poetry Club, Milwaukee PrideFest, Toronto Pride, and dozens of other venues across the country.
