John Transue's Summary

For a brand to stand out and be seen, creativity is essential. My interest is in using creativity to awaken consumers and drive them to action. I believe that growth in business demands creativity, and I am an integral part of managing and growing the brand. I successfully combine business solutions with creative problem solving to grow the business.

As a marketing coordinator and graphic designer at Coca-Cola, I help create, produce, manage, oversee and ensure the organization's quality of creative product supports the high standards of Coca-Cola and aligns with our style guide for some of the world's most valuable brands: Coca-Cola, Dasani, Sprite, Powerade, Honest Tea, Glacéau Vitaminwater and many more. I drive sales by supplying key accounts with strategic advertising that support communication objectives appropriate for corresponding zones. But most of all, I make integrating creativity into business a simple process.

Throughout the years, I've held many positions and internships from art director, graphic designer, web developer, user interaction designer, content manager, to marketing, branding and advertising positions at many well-known companies and agencies. These experiences have enabled me to become fluid and helpful at integrating business with creativity.

Ultimately, I am looking to pursue a career in a creative environment and eventually I think I could make a great Creative Director. On my pursuit to a Creative Direction role, I think Marketing Manager, Brand Manager, Account Director, Content Manager, Social Media Strategist, Art Director, Graphic Designer and similar positions would be fitting. While I am currently happily employed, I am always looking to advance my career and welcome new opportunities. Additionally, if you want to talk or ask questions, please reach out!
