Lucio Scarpa's Summary

I am graduated in Economic Statistics from the University of Bologna, where I also earned an MBA at the Alma Graduate School.
My hobbies - narrations and films - appeared to be the driving force of my professional interests. Continuously evolving, those gave rise to my film production company named Kublai Film. The company is known to be be one of the most efficient and innovative film production companies in North-East Italy. Kublai Film has reached this result very quickly considering that it was founded in 2005.

Specialties

Raising money, opening up new business opportunities, leading teams, and growing businesses.
Expertise in cinema, digital media, broadcast television, internet.
