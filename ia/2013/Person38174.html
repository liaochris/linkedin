Dr. Nathalie Beauchamp's Summary

Dr. Nathalie Beauchamp, B.Sc., D.C. is a Ottawa based chiropractor, a certified personal fitness trainer, a professional natural bodybuilder, a wellness consultant and lecturer.
Creator of Roadmap to Wellness - a self pace, on-line wellness education program
Book Co-Author - Wellness On The Go: Take The Plunge - It's Your Life
Chiropractor and Owner of Sante Chiropractic and Wellness Centre
I love help people locally and globally achieve better health and wellness in their lives so they can have more energy and vitality!

I also have a passion to help entrepreneurs be more successful in their businesses as a Licensed Business Coach.

  • Orleans Health and Wellness Expo

    • March 2007 to January 2012
    Team Members: Dr. Nathalie Beauchamp, Deborah MacDonald

    The 2011 Orleans Health & Wellness Expo was recognized as one of the most well attended health events in the history of the Ottawa area. The statistics from on-line analytics revealed the attendance tripled over last year with astounding positive feedback from both the attendees and vendors. The Orleans Health & Wellness Expo will connect your business to the strong, dynamic and active demographic that seek health, fitness and wellness advice, services and products.

We invite you to be part of this natural health and environmental trend happening in our city. Healthy lifestyle choices, body-mind connection, and improved quality of life have all become market buzzwords, and for good reason.
