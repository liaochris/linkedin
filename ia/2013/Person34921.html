Dexon Software S.A.'s Summary

Dexon Software S.A. was born in 2000 in the U.S.A. and has an activity range that spreads through several Latin American countries. It develops Information Technology (IT) tools that mean to provide a basis for any business or company improvement, by offering mechanisms that support their management processes through a proper appropriation of the information, and the integration, automation and time optimization of business processes.

Dexon is a leading company in its realm, for it has some of the most important quality certifications in the IT development sector: in 2002 obtains an ITIL certification, Version 2; in 2010 it attains ITIL certification, Version 3.1 in nine processes, and the NTC – ISO 9001: 2008 certification; in 2011 obtains IT MARK certification and update the NTC – ISO 9001: 2008 certification. In 2012 it attains its most important achievement till the momento: a new certification in ITIL, Version 3.1 in four more processes, given by Pink Elephant; it means thirteen certified processes which position Dexon in the 5th place of IT development quality.

Our products can basically be divided into three aspects: IT asset management (automatic inventory, remote manager, content guardian, network devices); IT process management (Service Desk, modules); Business process management (business intelligence, document management).

Services: Business Intelligence, Business Process Management, Enterprise Content Management, ITIL, SAM, WorkFlow, VoIP, Service Desk.
