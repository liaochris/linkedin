Jiri Hyzler's Summary

Since 2000 I have been working for OKsystem company (www.oksystem.com) as Microsoft trainer specialized in server operating systems and network security. My favourite subjects are Active Directory, firewall management (not only ISA Server) and all about PKI and smartcards. I am specialist in antivirus protection and content filtering. I work on preparation and implementation security policies and procedures by ISO 17799, security audits, designing network topology and I administering company network.
My other responsibilities are consulting services and technical support for small, medium and enterprise customers.
I am member of Windows User Group community, I speaking at WUG meetings and Hands-on-Labs and I contributing to czech discussion groups.

Specialties

I have many Microsoft certifications (such as MSCE Security, MCT, MVP, ..), Symantec certifications, CompTIA Security+ and many others.
