Kevin Morales' Summary

I Grew up in Killeen Texas. My career in real estate began in the late eighties immediately after my duty in the United States Army. I became an apartment manager in Austin Texas. After several years as a manager I decided to become a realtor sales agent and continued working in Austin. I then moved to San Antonio and worked as an agent for 10 years. I recently moved back to the Austin - Round Rock area after spending a year in Afghanistan.

Possess over 20 years in the Real Estate / Construction / Office Management field in both residential and commercial properties. Supervised and coordinated the purchase and construction process from the conceptual development stage through final construction, ensuring all project are completed as scheduled and within budget. Superintendent / assistant superintendent overseeing the planning and scheduling implementation of the clients request per the contract, work as a liaison between contractors and sub-contractors. (On all real estate and construction projects)

Served as Personnel Administrative Specialist, (75B), US Army, charged with providing reports, statistics, charts and graphs directly for Company and Battalion Commanders, on a daily basis. Organized Company movement’s along with equipment for deployments and training exercises. Processed troops for deployments, relocation, new duty assignments and for military schooling.

Provided leadership to cause the creation of organizational structure and processes required to fully execute the requirements of the contract between parties. Reviewed cost, budgets and material list to ensure contractors and sub-contractors were adhering to the specification and agreement set out forth by the customer.

Specialties: Kevin Morales is a native Texan and has served central Texas as a Realtor for over 15 years. Specializing in New Home Construction, Kevin offers the kind of service that sets the standard for Realtors.
