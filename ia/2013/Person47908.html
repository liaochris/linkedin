George Hadgelias' Summary

George Hadgelias finished his degree at Kelvin Grove Teacher's College and was offered a position with Petrie Terrace State School from 1977 – 1979, before taking a position at Brisbane State High School where he was for another two years.

He began his real estate career in 1981 where he spent one year with Ray White Morningside before taking on the role of branch manager at Ray White Paddington in 1982. Ray White became a franchise business in 1986, and George took on the Ray White Paddington office and hasn’t looked back.

Under his tutelage, the office has virtually achieved the impossible – being the #1 Ray White office in Brisbane for 18 years straight.

Business owner for over 25 years, principal and auctioneer, George Hadgelias has been the recipient of the Ray White ‘Auctioneer of Excellence’ and has been voted Auctioneer of the Year.

He has received the coveted Max White prize, a prestigious award presented to principals in recognition of the outstanding contribution they have made to the organisation.

Since beginning his vision, he has lead the team to outstanding success, in not only the surrounding suburbs of Paddington, Bardon, Red Hill, Kelvin Grove, Ashgrove through to The Gap, but in fact, throughout Brisbane.

The commitment to continual training, leading edge marketing tools and fundamental business professionalism underpins his success.
