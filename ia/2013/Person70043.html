J Stephen "Steve" Wiggs, E.A.'s Summary

Colorado Tax Solutions is a full service accounting firm with a bit of twist!

Along with the usual services offered by all other accounting firms such as bookkeeping, payroll, personal and business tax preparation, we get tax payers out of trouble with the IRS by assisting them with a resolution that doesn't put them in the poor house.

So, if you or a colleague haven't filed your taxes for a number of years or the IRS is garnishing your wages, levying your bank accounts or even threatening to, give us a call!
