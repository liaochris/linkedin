John Welch's Summary

John uses service, interaction and visual design to help clients envision and implement new offerings for their customers.

In the past, he’s designed new customer experiences for clients such as ASUS, Intel, UniCredit and U.S. Bank, across sectors such as banking, healthcare, retail, and consumer electronics. Specific highlights of John’s work include a major role in designing and launching the mobile offering of a large U.S. bank, helping set the strategic direction for the online channel of a leading Italian bank, and the design and launch of a new social media service.

John holds a Masters in Service and Experience Design, awarded with distinction, from Domus Academy in Milan, Italy.

Specialties: Product and Service Concepting & Visualization, Design for Mobile, Web Design, UX Design, Web Coding, Doing Something Completely New
