Chuck Mulberg's Summary

After 20 years of industry experience Charles created Mulberg & Associates PLLC with the single purpose of helping small to mid-size companies succeed in building their business through providing the tools necessary to make better management decisions. This can be simply providing you with strategic planning or as big as outsourcing your entire accounting department. His experience comes mainly from the technology and service industries and also including aviation, retail, and others. Charles draws on experiences gained while working with small to large size companies and uses that knowledge to design the right strategy for each of his clients. That strategy is then implemented with a team of professionals. This method allows you to receive the same high level of professional services on a part-time as needed basis while avoiding the cost of hiring full-time accounting and finance professionals.
Charles is a Certified Public Accountant licensed in the state of Washington and holds a Master of Business Administration degree. He served as a member of the strategic task force for the Washington Society of CPAs (WSCPA) which has over 10,000 members. He is also a former board member and Past President of his local chapter and was recognized as the 2006 Chapter President of the year.
Also in 2006, Charles completed his first Ironman distance triathlon in twelve and a half hours. Charles knows the passion that drives business and understands that just like an athletic coach can help you succeed with athletic goals, Charles coaches his clients to successfully reach their business objectives and get you farther than you thought possible. All he asks is that his clients bring the passion necessary to make it happen. Charles will provide you the tools necessary to take your business to the next level and beyond.

Specialties

We provide business advisory services, tax planning and preparation. We also provide traditional accounting department needs such as bookkeeping, payroll, cash flow management, budgeting and forecasting. In addition, we assist you with adding value to your business with profit improvement tools including GamePlan, dashboards, key performance indicators (KPIs), and other tools.
