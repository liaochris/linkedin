Mark Allison, MFT's Summary

Mark is a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist working with individual and couples, in private practice. Additionally, he is currently on the clinical staff at The Maple Counseling Center where for the past 2 years facilitates a mixed adult interpersonal psychotherapy group. Mark has helped many individuals and couples to develop insight into themselves and deepen their ability to live a more fulfilling and authentic life.

Specialties

Premarital and marital counseling, Individuals & Couples
