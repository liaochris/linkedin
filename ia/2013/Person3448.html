Aldrich Tan's Summary

I am currently seeking opportunities involving Branding, User Experience, and Graphic Design in a fast-paced and challenging environment.

I am a creative designer whose curiosity and passion lies in Branding, Graphic Design, User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) Design. I am also adept in Team and Project Management as I enjoy a good, thorough collaborative process.

My passion drives my process and involvement with others: exploration, experimentation, and reiteration are practices deeply integrated in my workflow to ensure the best possible outcome for any venture. My involvement with the Design Community helps others come together and learn from each other - I see this as a way of giving back.

Various identity and logo projects of mine have been published in logo design books such as iHeartLogos and LogoNest. My works are featured in multiple online galleries like LogoMoose, LogoCrush, DesignWoop, and Cruzine.

I'm always up for a good chat. Lets talk! hello@aldrich.ca
