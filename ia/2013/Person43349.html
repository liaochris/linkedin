Eva-Lynne Carlson's Summary

I live for a great challenge. Specific to work, I have an innate sense for situation analysis and for recognizing salient patterns found in a mass of information or scattering of events. The unique contribution I bring is making use of this ability to quickly evaluate and associate relevant components to a bigger picture.

I am known for blazing difficult trails to unearth the source of both the cause and the solution. More often than not, they are found not that far away from each other.

This means I don't always deliver the news to a cheering audience, I also do not compromise the integrity of the actual situation in order to avoid the occasional sling and arrow. In fact, I'll go so far as to provide a clear shot, and stand in an open field next to an impossible to miss target when I bring discounted or unnoticed things up to the surface and into focus. Why? Because I _genuinely_ trust the transformative power of transparency.

Where others stop at finding a problem, I get started sourcing solutions. What others consider tedium, I consider a benefit of the job. I have never met a detail I didn't like, and maybe, sometimes, I could further benefit from a reminder that not everyone shares a friendship with the minutia.

If something has to be done, I'm the person that will drive it like a dog with a bone until that objective is done. Right.The.First.Time.

Just please don't ask me to do this at 7 a.m..

Currently, I'm completing a bazillion hour Mediator certification program, fostering rescue parrots and figuring out how to get my tweet on.
