Craig Allen's Summary

A highly-respected comedic writer, Craig has quickly become a staple of morning radio shows. Craig can be heard regularly on XM Satellite Radio. He recently made his live national radio debut on “The Bob and Tom Show” (heard by several million people daily).

Material that Craig has written has appeared in Playboy, The London Times, The Washington Post, The New York Post, the tabloid Star, and on ABC's "The View".

"Craig did an excellent job at holding our group's attention for over an hour! His material was clean, funny and original - thanks for an hour of laughs. It was great that he did his homework on the company and our products. He did a great job of poking fun at SPAM®, etc. without being offensive. I would recommend him to anyone."
~ Larry Vorpahl, President, Hormel Foods

People appreciate that Craig does his homework before an event. When possible, he tailors some material specifically to the company or industry, making audiences feel that he truly knows them. His writing skills have also made him a highly sought after roastmaster, skewering individuals and celebrities at retirement parties, 50th birthday celebrations, awards banquets and
other milestone events.

From small Mom & Pop businesses to Fortune 500 companies, audiences everywhere enjoy Craig’s standout standup!
