Alexander Palmo's Summary

Do you think a "developer/designer" is a myth? Think again!

Alex has over a decade of experience in Interactive and web development. He has worked with a wide range of clients in e-commerce, retail, consumer, education, finance, pharma and healthcare.

E-commerce: The Limited
Retail: Best Buy
Consumer: Cooper Tires, Napa, McDonalds, Wendy's
Education: McGraw Hill, IQ-ity
Finance: Bank One, Rockbridge Capital
Pharma: Eli Lilly, Genentech, Roche, Abbott
Healthcare: Ohio Health, Orthopedic Foot and Ankle Center

Alex has played a leadership role in projects from conception and planning to graphic design, programming and development. He has a wide range of project experience including e-commerce, online marketing, banner ads and games, point-of-sale plasma screens, trade-show kiosks and internationally distributed CD-ROMs.

Specialties: Demandware, Wordpress, ActionScript, PHP, HTML, CSS, Flash, Graphic Design, Photoshop, Kiosks, 3D Animation.
