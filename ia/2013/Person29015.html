Dana Bernard's Summary

My mission in life is to educate others. I am a registered nurse by trade and a teacher by passion. I love to teach in a classroom, online or informally- to those around me. I am often asked to speak formally or informally at a variety of events. My positive outlook on business and life is a treasured asset.

I founded The Learning Oasis to help others realize their full potential through personal and professional development. We love to share knowledge with others. Social media has become an integral part of our day-to-day operations. By engaging our audience we can learn what is important and how we can inform and educate today.

Throughout my career I have lead a team of talented professionals who guide and develop others as health professionals. I believe in a positive leadership style and influence others through coaching, mentoring and modeling proper workplace behaviors. I am a strategic planner and have proven my ability to execute goals in a timely fashion. My belief in an affordable and quality-driven education for all drives me to continuously improve the experience for my students.
