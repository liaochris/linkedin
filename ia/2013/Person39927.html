Edwin Delgado's Summary

When I am not reading books, writing books, practicing new ideas and concepts, I am usually at work doing my best to improve the usability and aesthetics of current or future projects.

I strongly believe that the user experience of any design takes center stage, but never should the look and feel of the interface be neglected. UI / UX should go hand-in-hand, no exceptions.

I have experience with wireframes, prototypes, web design, mobile design, application design and video and audio editing. My favorite tools are Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Balsamiq and After Effects. I am also not afraid to learn new technologies and I move from PC to Mac on a daily basis.

I believe it's not the tools that make us, but our knowledge and experience.

Edwin Delgado's Experience

Senior UI / UX Designer

Eloqua

Public Company; 201-500 employees; Computer Software industry

August 2012 – May 2013 (10 months) Vienna, Virginia

At Eloqua, I worked on managing the usability, and design of their leading software Eloqua10.

My week usually consisted of creating new concepts and ideas. Working with project managers and understanding use-cases helped me implement new features and/or improve existing ones. Joining JAD sessions and interacting with R&D allowed me to better understand all requirements before a project began. Depending on the project, I created sketches, wireframes, prototypes and/or hi-res mockups.

Lead Web / UI / UX Designer

Timberlake Membership Software

August 2010 – August 2012 (2 years 1 month) Fairfax Va

While working at Timberlake, I helped speed up the release process of project releases. This was accomplished by creating a new piece of code that was adaptable to all the designs that we created, or handed over by the clients. A design process that used to take days, was brought down to a few hours.

I helped design a new website, and worked on their branding, to modernize it, and give it it's own distinction within the AMS provider space. I worked with developers to improve the user experience of the AMS software, and designed new concepts to help it stand out.

I worked on daily tickets, and helped troubleshoot problems with the team. Direct client engagement was included in this process.

Game Designer

Opengo Game Studios

Project involving the creation of a new video game and created all original designs with concept artists for the video game graphics.
Managed a team of 4 that involved concept art being incorporated into the computer.

Web Designer

Anexo Group

April 2008 – January 2009 (10 months) Puerto Rico

During my time at Anexo I created web cemplates and coded websites on a daily basis. Projects would start out with simple requirements from clients. It would move on to simple wireframes to hi-res mockups. I would code the designs in HTML/CSS and hand over for review. Adjustments would be made after feedback was collected.

It was a very simple process, but I created many templates and worked on practicing skeumorphism at the time.
