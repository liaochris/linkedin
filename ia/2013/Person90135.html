Maya Frost's Summary

After spending the last few years living in Latin America while writing and consulting around the world, I moved to Beijing, China. I loved teaching preschoolers at a private English-immersion kindergarten there and was quickly promoted to vice principal.

Soon thereafter, I was chosen by one of China's most admired and successful entrepreneurs to oversee the English education of his daughter. I moved from Beijing to Hangzhou to live near the family. I feel so fortunate to be given this opportunity and enjoy every day with my student.

I'm most grateful for the experience of raising four happy, successful daughters who are all in their twenties. I'm lucky that my beloved fellow adventurer and husband of 27 years is with me no matter where life takes us!

I just finished my first novel. My agent is Jillian Manus of Manus Literary & Media.
