Julia Verdin's Summary

Julia is an active film producer. As well as producing her own movies through her production company Rough Diamond Productions, she also frequently gets hired as a producer or Executive producer on other film maker's projects. She is well versed in all aspects of film production and also how to get a film financed, packaged with talent and sold. She also has a consulting business and consults for individuals and companies on scripts, film projects and all aspects of film making. She is also speaks regularly on film panels and seminars for up and coming film makers and organizations including Women In Film, BAFTA, IIFF-LA and various film festivals .
