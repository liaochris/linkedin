Hal Hoadley's Summary

Internet Marketing Business

I've managed and made our Internet Marketing plan work exceptionally well - this was accomplished with good old fashion Hard Work and Testing-Testing-Testing.

It also requires that you step out of your comfort zone! There are many of us earning a very good income working our Internet marketing plan for many local businesses. A potential customer is just around the corner. Show them a solution to a problem and it becomes a win - win situation.

First thing I do is gather materials and info on any local business that is already spending money on advertising. This could be for a Flyer I get in the mail or hanging on my door, a Newspaper Ad, Fancy Magazine Ad (I love those since placing an ad in those is upwards of thousands of dollars), Billboards, Radio Station ads, Cable TV, Buses: (dentists are popular with using radio ads) bus benches, etc...

I use the latest information on Internet and Mobile Marketing gathered from reliable sources. I follow the works of Dan Kennedy, Jack Mize, Frank Kern, Bill Glazer, John Carlton, Jim Palmer, Kevin Wilke, Zig Zigler and many others.
