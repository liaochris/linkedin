Andres Goyanes, MBA's Summary

Andres Goyanes is an accomplished senior-level marketing management professional with over 15 years of experience in marketing strategy, advertising campaign planning, public relations, and project management for business development, corporate communications, online marketing, and lead generation.

In addition, Andres is an experienced copywriter specializing in the development of B2B marketing communication solutions. His experience also covers a diverse set of industries, including industrial and technical services, construction and development, real estate, hospitality and tourism, higher education, renewable energy, sustainable development, and international business.

Andres’ marketing experience covers department supervision (including marketing, business support and creative personnel), strategic planning from conceptual design to execution, contract negotiation, campaign management, the development of metrics-driven decision support systems for analysis and reporting. He has also managed projects in web/graphic design, brand development, content development, paid search (PPC), search engine optimization (SEO), and online marketing.

Extracurricular accomplishments include serving as President of the American Marketing Association of Central Florida in 2008-2009, following a decade-long tenure as a board member. Andres is also active in community and civic organizations, serving as President 2011-2013 of the Rotary Club of International Drive - Orlando.

As a respected member of the Greater Orlando business community, Andres has been invited to speak at diverse forums and events hosted by the American Marketing Association of Central Florida, the Orlando Advertising Federation, the International Association of Business Communicators, and the Orlando Regional Chamber of Commerce.

Andres holds an MBA with a concentration in Sustainable Business from Marylhurst University and a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing from the University of Central Florida.

Specialties

Marketing Management and Strategy, Industrial Marketing, B2B Marketing Public Relations, Corporate Communications, Online Marketing, Direct Response Marketing, Creative Development, SEM/SEO, Social Media, Advertising Campaign Management, Resort Marketing, Hospitality Marketing, Campaign Planning/Development, and Hispanic Marketing.
