John Klein's Summary

AV-rated attorney and medical doctor. Medical-legal expertise with an emphasis on serious injury cases, health care regulatory compliance, physician licensing concerns, and business matters. All significant legal matters considered. Licensed to practice law in the states of Nebraska and New York, and available for travel to serve as appearance counsel in Nebraska and New York for hearings, depositions and trials. Also available for medical-legal consulting relationships elsewhere in conjunction with locally licensed attorneys of record. Call us today at 402-391-1871.

Specialties

Health law, health care regulatory matters, medical insurance questions, injury cases, and a wide range of business issues. Appearance Counsel for litigation in Nebraska and New York.
