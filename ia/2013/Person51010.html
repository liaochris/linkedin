Gus Torres' Summary

A creative and interactive design professional whose experience spans all levels of the medium, from traditional design and art direction, to human interface and web design.

Strengths include a balanced mix of strong creative problem solving, solid design, illustration and animation skills, and vast up-to-date knowledge of relevant strategic interactive offerings including Flash, email campaigns, e-commerce, content management systems, and standards-based web design.

Experience includes successfully hiring and developing functional creative teams, streamlining processes, creating collaborative and fun environments across design and development teams, maintaining good client relationships and managing projects that cross print to web disciplines.

Specialties

W3C standards-based web authoring, CMS template development, creative visual design, project and staff management, strategic interactive marketing, branding and identity, corporate training and multimedia instruction, illustration and Flash-based animation
