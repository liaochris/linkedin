Ellen Anderson's Summary

My six years in architecture school taught me many things, but perhaps the most ground-breaking thing to learn was that I did not want to be an architect. My passion for communication through design led me to leave the Master of Architecture program at the University of Minnesota in order to pursue different options. I continue to have a deep respect for architecture, but have been able to separate my love of designed spaces from the architectural profession.

Each day is a learning experience as I adapt the design thinking I've been taught to new situations on a daily basis. Transforming from buildings to pixels has been an exciting adjustment.

Specialties

sustainable design, graphic design, digital design, photography, video production
