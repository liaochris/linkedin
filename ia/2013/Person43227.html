Etienne Perret's Summary

For the last 35 years, I've had the incredible good fortune to be able to live on the coast of Maine, making my extraordinarily beautiful jewelry designs for my friends. Working with just the best of materials, 18kt gold and platinum, set with colored diamonds and tahitian pearls, my experience is turning jewelry dreams into reality. I encourage you to participate in creating a precious heirloom that will last you a lifetime.

Specialties

We are the leading company in the use of colored diamonds in bridal jewelry.

Etienne Perret's Experience

creator

ceramique

January 2007 – Present (6 years 8 months) Camden, Maine

I have been very excited about the design possibilities of gem ceramic for many years. We are now building an amazing collection of wedding and engagement rings using zirconia ceramic in conjunction with gold, platinum and diamonds. I design the pieces and have the sintering process done in Asia, after which I rework the ceramics and add gold and diamonds. The collection is currently available at about 50 stores in the USA and Europe.

creator

Starlight Wedding Bands

January 2000 – Present (13 years 8 months)

We make a wonderful collection of 18kt and platinum wedding bands set with colored diamonds

Owner/ Designer/Jeweler

Etienne Perret & Company

January 1973 – Present (40 years 8 months)

At Etienne Perret & Company I conceptualize, design, manufacture and sell a collection of clean contemporary 18kt and platinum jewelry set with colored diamonds. Where other may sell the very exclusive natural color diamonds, the focus of my company is designing using treated color natural diamonds. The costs of these jewelry pieces is accessible to most Americans for special occasions.
