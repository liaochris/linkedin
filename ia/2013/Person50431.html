Greg Swindle's Summary

I assemble and lead teams to gather and analyze customer requirements, design and develop Web browser-based e-learning solutions, performance management and improvement applications, competency assessment tools, HR self-service applications, and custom analytics solutions. I am the development lead for PeopleSoft/HR portal integration at Verizon, and I have also lead development for the Verizon Telecom HR portal, a third-generation, knowledge management system designed to integrate e-learning solutions with the strategic initiatives of Verizon business units and departments. Over 60,000 employees uses these applications daily.

I also lead the Phprules open-source project, a forward-chaining inference rule engine for PHP.

I place great emphasis on usability and quality assurance. I introduced and implemented Agile Programming to quickly analyze, design, test, program and deploy systems quickly per customer requirements based on solid OOP principles, practices and design patterns.

Technologies include: Analytics, AJAX, .NET, CSS, DHTML, Domain driven design, Full software project lifecycle, GUI Design, HTML, JSP, Java, Javascript, JSON, OOP, NoSQL, PeopleCode, PHP, Python, Relational database design and development, Servlets, Software Configuration Management (Souce control, Continuous Integration with Unit testing), UML and XML.
