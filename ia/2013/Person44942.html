Terje Flaarønning's Summary

I’m basically a very technical person who takes pride in delivering next to perfect solutions whenever possible, with focus on the business needs and economy behind the project. In my mind, a perfect solution is not perfect just by being technically perfect, if it don’t bring any added value it’s not perfect. The business aspect is very important to keep in mind, especially in these days. My expertise is diverse in many technical fields as well as deep in some focus areas. Large and complex projects are something I’m used to handle and I often take the architect and technical project manager roles in such projects.

I hold a few vendor specific certifications such as Cisco CCIE Voice # 17105, Microsoft MCSE + Messaging on NT, 2000 & 2003 and Microsoft MCSA + Messaging on 2000 & 2003 among others. However my knowledge is not limited to only the certified areas as I do understand the technology, not just a single vendor’s implementation of a technology.
