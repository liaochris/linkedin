Eric Perret's Summary

Here’s some of what I like: listening, then finding the words to express what others can’t; finding creative concepts that tap into basic emotional truths; teaching stuff (even better: helping folks access their own wisdom); telling stories -- with paragraphs, with pixels, with people, with pictures. The red thread through all this: I like finding the signal in the noise. What’s really going on.

I try to use the two halves of my brain as frequently and accurately as my two feet; this has led to an intriguing blend of career ingredients. Most of the time, I work in Creative Services, working with clients to find whatever tools work best to communicate clear, engaging stories with an impact. This work is based on the belief (and science) that stories are our most powerful connectors. It requires the capacity to adapt message and medium to connect with any kind of audience.


Other times, I’m working as an Organization Development consultant, helping to optimize strategy, people, and processes in enterprises and non-profits. This work is based on a belief in human potential and learning; it requires the capacity to engage multiple ideas and possibilities without judgment, and the ability to develop that same capacity in others.

All of the time, I’m working from a foundation of cross-cultural business, communications, and technology experience.

Whoa now. How’s that all look? See my graphic CV at: http://www.http://ericperret.com/visual-cv/
