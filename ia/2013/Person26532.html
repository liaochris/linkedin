Rodney Marks' Summary

Sydney-based, Rodney is Australia's misleading corporate comedian. He has appeared at the Melbourne, Sydney and Adelaide comedy and fringe festivals, and extensively overseas on about 35 tours. He's a corporate impostor: a comedian who is a comic hoaxer who pretends to be a speaker. He infiltrates business events as an expert, and delivers a keynote.
