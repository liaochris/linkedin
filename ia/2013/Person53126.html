Hillary Feder, MAS's Summary

I have a core belief that people transform business.

As a leader in strategic relationship building for 24 years, as the CEO of Ask Hillary's, we have been providing strategic process development and products around internal employee branding, communication and marketing to executives, human resource, marketing and sales leaders.

OBJECTIVE:
♦ To increase employee productivity, client retention and bottom line profitability  through a culture that promotes humanity in the workplace.

PROCESS and OUTCOME:
♦ We use a personalized approach and collaborative style to nurture connections between people and companies. Our work creates the conditions for people to see the big picture, understand how their work contributes and feel appreciated.

CLIENTS:
♦ We focus on organizations that believe in people-centric practices in medical devices, wellness, technology software, financial services, training and education and manufacturing.

As a speaker and contributing author in The Business Expert Guide to Small Business Success. Recognized as a thought leader in employee engagement my chapter fouses on employees as a company's "X" Factor To Success. Providing insight into "how" to create a people-centric work place.

Specialties: Employee Engagement Expert, People-Centric Practices, Onboarding New Employees Processes, Launching Brands Products, Bringing Values, Mission, Vision to Life, Employee Recognition, Sales Incentives, Appreciation Programs, Memorable Meetings, Events, Strategic Marketing Communications, Corporate Social Responsibility, Brand Identity Integration, Brand Marketing, Loyalty, Corporate Gifts, Project Management, Design, Medical Devices, HighTech, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Speaker
