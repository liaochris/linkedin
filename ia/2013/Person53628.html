Horace Qiu's Summary

A certified image consultant and personal stylist with fashion as my first love. As an entrepreneur using his skills and expertise, transforming ordinary men into extraordinary gentlemen. I truly love what I do. I got some of my fashion sense from my father. He is a blue collar guy, but he also loves his casual wear. I used to make fun of the way he dressed until I got older, and realized he set the standard for how I should always present myself as a man. I was always good at presenting myself well, but it wasn't until a friend whom I was helping pick out clothes said that I should do this for a living. This was when my light bulb came on and Sir Styles was created. The fact that I’m passionate about what I do means I will always make sure that my customers are more than satisfied with their service.

My company Sir Styles offers everything from complete wardrobe makeovers to personal shopping experiences, all with the mission to boost men’s confidence and improve their overall style. Once we achieve this, we give them the confidence to get that date with a beautiful woman, job offer and just have a better outlook on life. Sir Styles welcomes all men; we will work with you to meet your needs and make sure you leave feeling like a new man.

Do you hate to shop? Do you look through your closet and don't know what to wear? This is where I come in. We will work together to create and define you through your clothes. We will buy new clothes, get a new hairstyle or haircut, and also teach you how to put together outfits.

www.sirstyles.com for more information
