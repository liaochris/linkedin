Brad Farkas' Summary

Brad is a Venture Capitalist, Technologist, and Entrepreneur specializing in mobile and broadband technologies. He has started and sold several technology startups, developed early key internet technologies (enabling the first VPN's and audio streaming systems), and served on the boards many successful startups including MCK Communications (IPO:MCKC), SignalSoft (IPO:SGSF), Compatible Systems (acquired by Cisco), Sonoma Systems (acquired by Nortel), Salsa Systems (acquired by Broadbeam), Equator Technology (acquired by PixelWorks), WiderThan (acquired by Real Networks), Vindigo Studios (acquired by ForSide), pcANYWHERE (acquired by Symantec), Thumbplay, Inc. (acquired by Clear Channel), M:Metrics (acquired by Comscore), Reaxion (acquired by Mobliss) and AtHoc.

Brad also has a strong interest and background in applied financial mathematics, and developed several groundbreaking algorithm's, which included working with Fischer Black on the next generation of his Black-Scholes model to enable valuations of fixed income derivatives. In addition he specializes in structured venture finance and recapitalizations.

Specialties

Wireless Data, Communications Infrastructure, Network Security, Broadband Video, Structured Finance.
