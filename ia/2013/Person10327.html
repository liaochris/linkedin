Ashley Quenan's Summary

After graduating with a BA in English from Kennesaw State University, I spent a few years working as a freelance editor and writer for various channels. I am currently employed by Ispa, Inc. as an editor for Lockheed Martin; I work on the flight and maintenance manuals for the C-130J.

As an avid lover of video games and the industry, I have maintained a freelancer status for indies4indies, an independent group that offers a variety of services for other independent developers. In addition to working as a writer and editor, I'm also a 2D artist: I have strong roots in traditional media, but I now mostly specialize in digital painting. My skillset is best suited towards portraits, character design, and landscapes.
