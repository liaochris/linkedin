Gloria Bannasch's Summary

I am thankful for all my work experience to date: Inpatient and Outpatient Mental Health; Residential and Day Treatment Programs for Addictions; Driving While Intoxicated Classes in Green Bay, Wisconsin and Evansville, Indiana. I am at my best when working with Clients who have a dual diagnosis of addiction and mood disorders and when working with couples. I am trained as an Interventionist and am a big believer in that process. Though not affiliated with an 12 Step Recovery group, I have been a friend to the recovering community for 28 + years. I am excited to share my experience with my Business Coaching and Personal Coaching Clients; my work with team building and Mindfulness work transform good employees and leaders to great employees and leaders. Call me for a free phone consultation and see what I can do for you. I can't wait to work with you!

I opened GJB Health Services in August, 2011. GJB Health Services is a private counseling practice serving older teens and adults. Individual, Couple and Family Therapy is offered; working with dual diagnosis of addiction and mental health issue is a specialty. We offer Intervention Services and Assessment Services as well. GJB Transitions is a new division. This Business and Professional Coaching practice will serve professionals with their enrichment and management needs on and off the job. We provide Team Building, Onboarding, Emerging Leader and Derailed Worker services to business state wide. Using phone, email and personal visits to the site, we provide a menu of services to business owners and team leaders.
