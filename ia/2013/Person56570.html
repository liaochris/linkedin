Jack Montgomery's Summary

Co-editor, writer and photographer for Buzz Trips an experiential travel website aimed at travellers and holidaymakers who want a bit of adventure.

I'm also co-director at Tenerife Magazine - an online lifestyle publication dedicated to reporting independant, experiential articles. I've co-authored the travel guidebooks Real Tenerife Island Drives and Going Native in Tenerife and run websites dedicated to both guides as well as being co-owner of Walking Tenerife - a site aimed at promoting the rural side of Tenerife and the Canary Islands, including the publication of detailed routes.

I have a wide experience of writing for different markets: specific remits (bid writing, government reports, presentations, speech writing), copywriting (fresh original copy for travel and hotel websites), travel blogging and feature writing (information based, location focussed, and experiential for print and online publications) & for fun - fiction and poetry.

I'm used to writing to specific remits, varying style of content as required, and working to tight deadlines.

Specialties

Living amongst Canarians in the north of Tenerife and immersing myself in their traditions and culture has given me an indepth knowledge of Tenerife and the Canary Islands, especially aspects that are often overlooked in the media. Because of this I was interviewed by Spanish newspaper ABC in Jan 2011.

My photographs have been used in various magazines and websites as well as on general news websites and sold through stock image websites.
I'm a qualified interviewer and am also TEFL certified.
