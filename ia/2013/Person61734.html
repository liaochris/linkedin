Jennifer Akers' Summary

Freelance writer, copywriter and editor, Jennifer Akers writes regularly for online media.

Since 2004, her work has been published on Patch, OM Times, Fowler Wainwright International, MyShelf, Motivational Radio, Happy News, Indian TV, and in local weekly newspapers.

Her articles focus on human interest stories, local events, family activities, writer resources, business, health, beauty, art, saving money, entertainment and social media marketing.

Jennifer writes features, articles, web content, book and product reviews, and blog posts under her own name, in addition to ghostwriting for business owners, other writers, and individuals.

She has edited author manuscripts, other writers’ work-in-progress, business newsletters, fliers, and web content.

Skills:
*Experience in different types of writing
*Editing
*Microsoft Word
*Experience in some HTML coding
*Content management systems
*Still photography and editing
*Social networking / social marketing

On the social platform of Squidoo, she has earned a few awards, including Lens of the Day. Award winning author Carolyn Howard-Johnson interviewed Jennifer about utilizing Squidoo for author branding / book promotions and by Blog Talk Radio about Squidoo’s power in social marketing. Jennifer was referenced as a Squidoo marketer in Howard-Johnson's book, The Frugal Book Promoter (Second Edition).

Jennifer has contributed to several blogs and communities:

*Motivational Radio: previous lead writer and publicity consultant

*Facebook Fan Page: personal Fan Page to share lenses and teach Squidoo tips, social networking and writers resources.

*Extreme Writing Now: judge of Halloween fiction contest.

*SquidLog.net: central news hub of all Squidoo topics on Squidoo, wrote articles on social networking, writer resources and making money online.

*Social Media and Online Marketing: a Facebook group, contributed tips on social media marketing, Squidoo and article writing.

Specialties

Non-fiction articles, features, blog content, web content, journalism, social networking, research, copywriting, press releases, article writing, and editing. Publications in both online and print media.

Web content, business letters, brochures, newsletters, and articles for business and individual websites.
