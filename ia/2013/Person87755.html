Martin Perna's Summary

I have two careers, as a musician and as an education technology specialist. My goal is to continue to collaborate with forward thinking individuals and companies who understand the true value of culture and education.

In 2011 I developed the Music Foundations curriculum at Dubspot, a music production school in New York City for their new online education program. The Music Foundations program consists of three eight week courses. I created and developed the curriculum as well as the bulk of the instructional multimedia for these courses. In addition, I taught (and continue to teach) these courses to students from eleven countries.

As a musician, I founded the group Antibalas in 1998. Since then we have toured the world performing in jazz, world music, and pop festivals in over thirty countries. From 1996-2002 I was part of the Desco/Daptone Records recording stable and recorded with Sharon Jones and the Dap Kings. In 2001 I founded the Latin folk-funk group Ocote Soul Sounds and have recorded 4 albums on ESL Music. I and have recorded with artists from Baaba Maal to Scarlett Johanson and regularly collaborate live with artists including the Roots, Medeski, Martin and Wood, and TV on the Radio.

Specialties

Arts and culture, world music, dance music, education technology, e-learning, curriculum development, instructional design
