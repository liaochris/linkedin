Luigi Ciuffetelli's Summary

I specialize in shooting people and have been since I picked up my first camera at the age of 12. However, since working in a market like Delaware, you have to be able to shoot anything.

I have been very lucky in my career to have been exposed to just about every medium of photography, and through hard work I have become very competent in shooting product still life, food, interiors, and many other subjects.

I have photographed healthcare professionals, open heart surgeries, cancer survivors and the buildings they all took place. I have photographed the parts to the machines that the CSI use, as well as the Police officers, Lawyers, and Judges who utilize them.I have photographed Chefs, the food they make and the restaurants they serve it in, etc, etc...

The catalyst here, is that I have had a successful career shooting an array of subjects using over 20 years of experience and knowledge.

Specialties

Lighting, problem solving, composition, digital imaging, retouching, image manipulation.

Luigi Ciuffetelli's Experience

Owner

Luigi Ciuffetelli Photography, LLC

Photography industry

January 1988 – Present (25 years 9 months)

I have been on my own for a little over 20 years now. I spent the first 10 years of my career as a Fashion Photographer in New York City, shooting for Magazines, Catalogs and Advertising.

I relocated back to my hometown of Wilmington Delwaware about 10 years ago and changed my focus to the corporate world. I still shoot a little fashion, but I mostly take photographs for the healthcare industry, editorial portraits for an array of national publications, corporate and retail advertising, websites and many other commercial media applications.

One of my most notable achievements was being asked by Vice President Biden to be his personal photographer for the five days of his 2009 inauguration.
