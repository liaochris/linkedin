Larry Arnold's Summary

Larry Arnold, President of Arnold Wealth Advisory Group (AWAG), LLC, is a native Houstonian. Larry began his career in financial services with Mutual of New York in 1994, where he founded The Arnold Group. Since then, he has become an expert in Investment Advisory Services, Income Retirement Planning, Social Security Income Planning, IRA and 401K Distribution planning, Personal Tax Planning and providing clients with the knowledge to plan for the future and have financial peace of mind.

In May, 2010 Larry formed Arnold Investment Services, LLC an independent Registered Investment Advisor, registered with the State of Texas Securities Board. He began working with professional money management firms in 2000 while serving as Sales Manager at John Hancock Life Insurance. Previously he served as Vice President for Landata, Inc., a subsidiary of Stewart Title Company for ­­­­­­7 years. This position gave him a very certain understanding of customer service and a hands on experience of selling product and delivering service.

Larry wanted to offer assistance to clients to help them achieve financial independence and improve their wealth management. He has more than 20 years of experience in the financial services industry. Throughout the years Larry has been devoted to maintaining high ethical standards while developing financial strategies for business and individuals alike.

Arnold Wealth Advisory Group, LLC prides itself on creating a strong and caring relationship with it’s clients. They offer not only their competency, but their independence as financial planners and advisors for clients looking to attain their most important core value financial goals. They focus on integrating business and personal financial planning in order to offer comprehensive financial and wealth planning for individuals looking to attain core value financial goals.
