Jason Crawford's Summary

With innovation happening at such a rapid pace, it can be difficult for a technology company to quickly and effectively communicate the benefits of its solutions to customers. Highline Communications helps technology companies boil down complex solutions into terms investors and customers can easily relate to and understand. We talk to the engineers in their own language and bridge the communications gap with marketing to help companies create effective marketing communications materials.

Highline has intimate knowledge of business strategy, which helps to align marketing materials with the overall business objectives of a company. We work with growth-stage companies to help accelerate their businesses, and larger companies to secure thought leadership roles in the market.

Projects have included:

Atomic Learning, Blackboard, Concentric, ConnectEDU, ConnectYard, CoyotePoint, Dell, eXplorance, JDS Uniphase, Kaltura, KZO Innovations, Microsoft, NBC Universal, and SoftChalk.
