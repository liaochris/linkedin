Leesa Bleile-Rosinski, CPC's Summary

Lee West & Associates, builds lasting relationships with law firms and businesses throughout the Western Region. Our mission is to seamlessly provide talent acquisition services to fulfill your most urgent and difficult to fill positions.

LATERAL Placement: We specialize in recruiting top talented legal professionals with stellar academics as Associates, Partners, and In-House Counsel.

Confidential LATERAL PARTNERSHIP services: We take the time to get to know the partners that we represent, as well as the culture of the representing law firm. We excel at making discreet inquiries regarding the placement of one partner or a practice group seeking to make a change.

CONTRACT Services: Contract attorneys are now a mainstream resource strategically and commonly utilized by the most successful law firms and corporate legal departments today. Whether you are seeking a talented team of experienced litigation attorneys for a particular case, or a knowledgeable attorney with expertise in transactional law, our account executives are here to help you successfully fill your position with a large pool of talent to choose from.

Specialties

The depth of our involvement in the search process allows us to identify outstanding candidates from merely good ones, resulting in particularly effective, profitable and long-lasting placements.
