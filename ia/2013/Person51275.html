Joanne Washington's Summary

I am Joanne Washington a Professional in Hair Replacement , a Board Certified Cosmetologist, Trainer and a Hair Loss Consultant. I provide women with information, tools and solutions that are designed to help them reverse further hair thinning and help get their hair back.. In addition, I provide hair restoration and hair additions for women of all ages and backgrounds experiencing any type of hair thinning, such as, alopecia, cancer and scalp disorders.

I am trained in Trichology, the study of hair and scalp disorders, under Rodney Barnett, Trichologist located in Dallas, Texas and David Salinger, Director of International Association of Trichologists, located in Syndney, Australia. Also, trained under Mr. Willard Brown, pioneer in designing and ventilating hair systems, Los Angeles, Ca and Jenny Rupini, Advance Hair Specialist, Modesto, Ca

For the past 10 years, using a health and wellness approach I have researched and developed nutritional supplements, hair care products and customized treatments that have helped women strengthen their body and maintain a healthy head of hair. I understand how underlying medical conditions can affect the body and hair over a period of time, therefore, it is extremely important to identify the causes linked to hair loss.

The people I usually work with are...

Women under the age of 40....for many years most of my clients with hair loss were women in their 50's and now majority of the women I see today are getting younger and younger. Common reasons, anti-depressants, oral contraceptives, chronic stress, damaging hair attachment methods and more

Working women concern about their personal appearance in need of hair replacement or treatments for hair loss

I train hair stylists how to recognize the causes of hair Loss and recommend appropriate solutions to their clients. And I teach them hair replacement. For more information contact me at hairoots@gmail.com
