George Matthew Linkert IV's Summary

Passion to make the world a better place is always my goal. From providing childcare and taking care of family members, to creating wonderful places where children can learn and create music, I have always worked toward something bigger than myself.

My latest passion is making my community a better place. So I've created "A Place in Mound" where discussions about the places and connections Mound employs can be strengthened and improved, usually through easy, and inexpensive ways.

Specialties: Leading small and large groups, Public speaking, Performing, Teaching simple and complex topics, Organizing events, Problem solving, Creativity, Resourcefulness, Empathetic, Flexibility, Tenacious! Perseverance, Leader, Trombone, critical listening, action oriented, innovative
