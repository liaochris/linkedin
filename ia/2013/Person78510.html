Larry Vandervoort's Summary

I have over 20 years of experience in the financial services industry, which includes 15 years of direct experience in the brokerage industry as a Registered Rep. My individual and company goals are to match individuals with the right firms.

Recruiting Services of Arizona (R.S.A.) is a financial services firm for Advisors. We are a recruiting and placement firm, as well as a consulting firm.

At R.S.A. we source and place qualified registered representatives with various broker dealers around the country. We also recruit executives in other areas in the financial services industry such as CEOs and Portfolio Managers. Our success is measured by the quality of the people that we introduce and place at our client firms.

R.S.A. also offers consulting services to help firms establish a distinct recruiting system.
We can assist firms in developing a centralized process and system for staffing long-term recruiters.

R.S.A. helps Financial Advisors learn how to grow their practice through Informative Seminars, as well as pump up their marketing efforts to High Net-Worth Clients through various media outlets, such as LinkedIn.

Specialties: We specialize in branch building for independent registered reps across the country.

We are experts in the areas of:

Financial Services – Independent Broker Dealers – Series 7 Licensed Reps
Executive Recruiting – Financial Services Industry
Marketing for Financial Advisors
Consulting Services for Firms

We also hold quarterly seminars for advisors. • Please go to the link below to view our most recent seminar for advisors, which was held on March 14 and 15, 2013, titled, “What Advisors Want to Know II”. We hosted over 40 advisors at this event.
http://recruitingservicesofarizona.com/seminar-what-advisors-want-to-know-ii/previous-seminars/
