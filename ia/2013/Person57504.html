Jim Hohl's Summary

I am the Co-Founder and COO of Vidaao, a marketplace for custom video production and animation services. Our marketplace connects corporate marketing and communications professionals with our network of video creatives in 18 cities. The marketplace is live at vidaao.com and our video project management platform is currently in closed beta.

My previous company, CPS Creative, was a web design, web consulting and SEO/search engine optimization firm, which I ran for 8 years. We developed a project and task management application called Tasklite (tasklite.com). CPS Creative merged with SEO PRO in March 2013 to form Visify, a web design, SEO and social media marketing firm.

I am proud of my long term involvement with Toastmasters, formerly serving as VP of Education of Pride Toastmasters and Area Governor for District 46. I occasionally perform with the New York improv school, Improvolution. I am a graduate of Momentum Education's Leadership training program (LT59).

Specialties: Marketing, web design, flash design, social networking, search engine optimization, SEO, WordPress, social marketing, web development (PHP/Apache), MySQL, HTML5, XHTML, Google Analytics, video, online marketplaces
