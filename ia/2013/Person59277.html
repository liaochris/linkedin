Jason Greer's Summary

Jason Greer is founder and President of Greer Consulting, Inc. (GCI), an employee relations/labor relations consulting firm, where he has built a successful management development practice.

Jason is an accomplished speaker, in addition to being a subject matter expert in employee and labor relations.

Jason Greer's Experience

Owner

Greer Consulting, Inc

February 2005 – Present (8 years 9 months) Greater St. Louis Area

Greer Consulting, Inc. (GCI) is a full service employee and labor relations consulting firm that works with client organizations and leaders to address challenges and solve problems caused by the complexities in the workforce. We offer services that are tailored to the unique needs of our clients in the areas of Labor Relations and Organizational Development, Executive Coaching and Staff Development as well as Professional Speaking.

Field Examiner (Board Agent)

National Labor Relations Board

Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Government Administration industry

August 2002 – February 2005 (2 years 7 months)
