Keith Bourne's Summary

Keith got into mobile in the 90’s when everyone else was chasing Internet companies. Because of this, he is one of the most experienced mobile strategy professionals in the field. Over the years, he built and refined his expertise in launching new businesses, new products, and strategically driving the growth of companies he is involved with, often into triple digit revenue growth within months. He has deep knowledge in the breadth of areas that makes launching new ventures and product lines successful – marketing, technology development and deployment, product innovation, brand management, business development, sales and field work management and support, relationship management, operational improvements, and revenue generation.

Keith speaks often and has been published through articles, papers, and interviews on many mobile related topics including mobile strategy, mobile marketing, ecommerce, and social media. Keith is co-founder of multiple network groups, including the Michigan chapter of Mobile Monday (http://mobilemondaymichigan.org/), which has grown to 4 chapters and over 2,500 members in just three years.

Keith holds a BSBA from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte and an MBA from Babson College, #4 in marketing and the most renowned entrepreneurship program in the world (#1 for 18 years in a row).

Specialties: Strategic Mobile Planning • Integrating Mobile Into Enterprise • In-depth Knowledge of Mobile Trends • Start-up Operations Development • Go-To-Market Strategy • Mobile Development Frameworks • Mobile Marketing • Cross-Functional Team Building • Mobile Security Awareness • Developing Strong Client Relations • Public Speaking About Mobile
