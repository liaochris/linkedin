Matt Dubois' Summary

Matt is CEO / CTO at d2 Business Solutions, drive2go and Intangib.ly. After studying Business Management at California State University, Fullerton, Matt found his passion for technology and has never looked back. He has worked in network security, infrastructure management, and network engineering at start-ups and large corporations alike. Matt’s key roles in the financial, medical, and defense industries, gave him an excellent background to draw upon when he developed d2 Business Solutions. Over the years, Matt has focused his attention on creating and managing innovative cloud computing solutions. He is the driving force in development and success of d2 Business Solutions. Matt is a hands-on team player and is personally dedicated to the satisfaction of customers and the excellence of each client program or solution developed by his team.
