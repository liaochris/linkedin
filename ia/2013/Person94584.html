Thoroughly enjoy working with small business owners, leaders, managers and staff to help them achieve the success they deserve.

1. 'Big Picture' (making Critical Decisions and setting goals)
2. Value (Maximizing EBITDA and cash flow)
3. Banking (Dynamic Business plans and securing financing)
4. People (Hiring, motivating and retaining a great team)
5. Tech (Getting the most from software, hardware and web)
6. Numbers (Reading and understanding the Financials)

Best Practices and BizQuiz with many Free tips for small businesses that have immediate take-home value
