Certified Spiritual Director

Spiritual Direction by Laurel Coote

June 2013 – Present (6 months) Torrance, CA

After a 3-year course of study and practicum, I earned my Certified Spiritual Director credential from the Stillpoint Center for Christian Spirituality. With an ecumenical and broad-based focus, I am trained to companion others on their spiritual journey through listening, stillness, conversation and prayer. Here's a bit about Spiritual Direction:

Sometimes referred to as spiritual guidance or friendship, spiritual direction is an ongoing relationship in which one person (the Directee) desiring to be attentive to their spiritual life meets with another (the Director) regularly for the specific purpose of deepening their awareness of the Divine's presence, so that they might respond more fully to that presence in all of life.
· The primary focus is upon the Directee’s relationship with the Divine as it is reflected in and challenged by all aspect of their life.
· The Directee assumes responsibility for his/her life with the Holy and is striving to be serious about some form of intentional prayer and reflection on their daily life and prayer.
· A sacred and safe meeting (usually of about one hour per month) in which both parties seek to enter into a prayerful space in which they can be attentive to the Divine (who is truly the Director).
· The Director will listen and may question, challenge, support or suggest as seems called for by the Spirit, but it is the Directee’s prayerful openness to Spirit that determines whatever insights are uncovered or the course of action to be taken. The director is only a facilitator in the process. The journey belongs to the Directee.

If you would like more information, or to schedule a session, please message me. For more information about Spiritual Direction, go to: www.sdiworld.org
