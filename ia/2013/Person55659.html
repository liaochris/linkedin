Gina Cozza's Summary

Professional, cost-effective administrative support is the goal of IBS. In other words, we want to make a positive contribution that benefits your organization. Equipped with 20 years work experience as a skilled executive assistant serving various fields and industries, IBS has the tools and knowledge to give you back your valuable time.

With proficiency in Microsoft Windows 2003 & 2007, Microsoft Outlook, Corel Draw, AutoCad, QuickBooks Pro & Premier, MAS90 & MAS200, IBS is able to quickly produce error free documents, perform transcription, edit and proofread documents, perform data entry, and handle your scheduling, travel arrangements, file management, client database management, marketing, advertising, and general bookkeeping needs.

Gina Cozza is a talented administrative professional with a love for learning. These talents along with her desire to help people succeed are the reasons she created IBS. With over 20 years experience in the engineering industry, serving both government and small business sectors, Gina has proven her success time and again.
