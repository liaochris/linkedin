Michel Floyd's Summary

Super "Tech Guy" with social skills and strong business acumen. Who would have thought? Rocket scientist ScD from MIT with 28 years of experience in silicon valley. Two IPOs, a restart, a .bomb, a couple acquisitions (both sides), mergers, hundreds of software releases, officer in a public company, VC pitches, and a whole lot of out of the box thinking. I'm currently Global CTO at YouGov PLC where I'm enjoying managing the global technology team and trying to drive innovation and integration over a bunch of time zones.

My goal? To be a driving force behind something great. What's great? Something that's fun for the team, rewarding intellectually and financially, and affects people in a positive way.

Specialties

Applying advanced technology to business problems. Being able to make connections between problems and cost-effective, pragmatic solutions. Strong people manager and leader. Good at a lot of stuff on the tech side: distributed systems, internet software, databases, networking, security, and user experience. Know way more than I ever expected to about finance, workflow, and business process optimization. Also very right brained on the marketing side. Very loyal, honest, and fair.
