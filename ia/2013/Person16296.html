Brad Wild's Summary

Accomplished graphic designer seeking a position, or freelance assignments, where I can utilize my creative, project management, and expert production art skills to produce a quality outcome. I am goal and detail-oriented, a strong communicator, and an effective problem solver.

In my 30+ years as a graphic design professional I have specialized in developing, as well as implementing, design objectives in both mass market and prestige channels in the beauty industry. I have created graphics for primary and secondary packaging, line extensions and maintenance, POP displays, and merchandising walls. In addition, I have been involved with floor stands, end caps, power panels, blister cards, scent strips, hang tags, magazine ads and promotional items.

I have held positions as Art Director, Designer, Consultant, and Manager of Packaging Production. Possessing a thorough working knowledge of the process from inception to completion, I took responsibility of executing the production art of all assignments I have managed. I am an expert in typography with a critical eye for detail.

Prior to the time spent in the beauty industry, I worked on a variety of consumer goods packaging including electronics, hardware & tools, cutlery & household items and cigarettes.

A Parsons School of Design graduate with a BFA in Communication Design, I have worked with Estée Lauder, Coty, Wet 'n’ Wild, L’Oréal, Lancôme, Maybelline, and have been involved with some of the most popular brands and products of the last decade including DKNY, Tommy Hilfiger, Aramis, Sean John, Michael Kors, Lab Series for Men, Cashmere Mist, Adidas, Rimmel, Celine Dion, Calvin Klein, Cool Water, Stetson, Jennifer Lopez, Nautica, Shania, David Beckham, Avon, Jovan, Oscar de la Renta, Black Radiance, Plénitude Skincare and more.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Package Design, Production Art
