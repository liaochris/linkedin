Frank Comer, PE's Summary

Mr. Comer is the President, Managing Officer, and Chief Engineer of PST and has an extensive background in the fields of Structural, Civil, and Geotechnical Engineering. Frank has the stability and depth of character gained by making many tough business and engineering decisions over his career. Frank‘s can-do attitude, get-down-to-business efficency, his forward thinking style, and his vision for the future has built PST into the small family owned strong engineering firm that it is today. Frank keeps a full schedule and would be able to provide key direction for any issues that may arise.

Specialties

Civil Engineering, Construction & Building Materials, Inspections and Construction Techniques and Project Management.

Expert Witness Testimony for plaintiffs and defendants, Forensic Engineering, Structural Evaluations, Damage Assessments, Construction Defect Investigations and Water Intrusion Investigations.
