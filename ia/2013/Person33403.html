Debbie Sadowsky's Summary

Development and Growth of my successful business of Manufacturing and Sales of Mostly Myrtle's All Natural Baked Goods. In addition to our Original Biskookys, a Gourmet product that is unquestionably "Better Than Biscotti", we have evolved into the world of baking an extensive line of Gluten Free Treats.

My extensive experience as a Pediatric Occupational Therapist working with Learning Disabled School Aged Children provided exemplary skills in activity analysis and ability to think out of the box.

Understanding the need to move forward one step at a time towards ultimate goals and to appreciate successes regardless of dimension has contributed to the continued growth of Mostly Myrtle's.

A primary goal is to provide a joyful experience on first bite of our Biskookys and other baked goods. We work closely with customers who are new to the Gluten Free life style and those with other food allergies to provide delicious treats within their food restrictions, made with healthy ingredients..
Achieving that goal requires careful and ethical business practices, a scientific approach to recipe formulation, imagination and innovation to address current food trends .

Being self supporting is a of course a primary goal, however, being able to accomplish this goal while doing something you feel passionate about is certainly as good as it gets.

Specialties: Baking, recipe formulation, product development, communication/team building, activity analysis
