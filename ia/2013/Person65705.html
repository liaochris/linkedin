JOE RODWELL's Summary

I’m Joe Rodwell, and I’m passionate about working with business owners.

WHY
I’ve been around business owners for a long time I owned businesses for 20 years and grew my second company to 70 people. I had complete responsibility over management, payroll and real estate. I built three commercial office buildings and co—owned a fourth, a 50,000 square foot commercial building. I could relate to the problems that the business owners faced because I had faced those same problems, so my objective was to work exclusively with business owners.

MOTTO
I help business owners prepare for the most important financial event in their lives, the sale of their businesses–the reality is they only have one chance to get it right!

WHAT I DO
There are three things that I do:
► Help owners create a succession plan focused on how the owner can receive top dollar when the business sells.
► Help owners actively implement the succession plan by serving on their Board of Advisors, I sit on four Board of Advisors, and through the years I have sat on several Board of Advisors.
►If owners want to cut back from day-to-day operations, I can help them hire a new CEO/President who can develop a professional management team which will yield top dollar when the business sells.

INTERESTING FACT
Throughout the years I have conducted 3,600 employment interviews, reviewed over 50,000 resumes and have had six management people that I have recruited and developed go on to own their own financial companies.

RECOGNITION
I was ranked one of “The Top 16 Exit Planning Experts from Across the Country” by Boomer Market Advisor magazine.

  • How to Create a Succession Plan to Sell a Business for Top Dollar // ESOPs & Selling A Business White Paper

    • May 27, 2012
    Authors: JOE RODWELL

While examining how ESOPs work, their advantages and disadvantages, readers learn that ESOPs do not work for all owners or for every company. They do, however, provide opportunity for some owners to leave their businesses in style.
