Michael Bastos, BSCS's Summary

I'm not like most developers, I code for work, for fun and for challenge. This to me isn't just a job, Computer Science has been apart of my life since I can remember and has driven me to making pragmatic decisions in both business and in my personal life.

What I do for work!

For my day job I work on Government Military Production Projects. I only write object oriented code and believe in testing everything before hand.

What I do for fun!

For my hobby I'm currently building out an Open Source Rails App called Ruby SAAS, it's basically all of the components necessary to start a basic software as a service application in Rails and it's completely Cucumber and RSpec Tested.

What I want to do!

I've done a ton of data processing in the past back from when I was in the military and also today, I enjoy finding human errors and issues in large data sets that few people can pick out or even understand. I love working anywhere that allows me to work in multiple languages filtering and parsing through large amounts of data in order to do something valuable. I love being challenged to do better.

Technical Skills

C++, Java, Php, Perl & Ruby (Learning Clojure), Rails, TreeFrog, CodeIgnitor w/ Composer, Eclipse, Android Dev Tools, AWS, MySQL, Postgres, SQLite, SVN, git, Agile and Scrum experience
