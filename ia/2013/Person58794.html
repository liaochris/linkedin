Jaren Angerbauer's Summary

I have been in the email compliance and deliverability space for more than 10 years, and have worked with companies of all sizes, from start-ups to Fortune 500 level. I have experience in both tactical (support) and strategic (management) roles, and work with clients to resolve email delivery challenges, stemming from issues such as a bad sending reputation, technical setup, blacklist and ISP blocking issues, etc. I also educate clients on industry best practices, and work with them to map out long-term strategic solutions that include a blueprint for ongoing success, thereby helping to prevent future problems.

I have established myself as both a technical and business expert on email, and have established relationships with individuals and organizations within the email industry, including Network and Internet Service Providers, Email Service Providers, and email software vendors for both the sending and anti-spam communities.

I have an expert level knowledge and understanding of mass email infrastructures, including network / DNS setup, email authentication, and configuration of email platforms such as StrongMail, MessageSystems, PowerMTA, Port25, and Postfix. I also have a strong background in Unix / Linux systems and database management.

I also teach a class on email technologies at Utah Valley University, and am responsible for creating and maintaining the core curriculum, as well as class discussions and activities.

I am also in the process of gaining expertise and certification in the privacy community.

Specialties

Email delivery consulting, Email deliverability, Email compliance; Email marketing; Email sender reputation, ISP relations Sender best practices, Unix, Linux and database management, Tactical and strategic business planning
