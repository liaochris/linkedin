Georgiana Comsa's Summary

I am the founder of Silicon Valley PR, a public relations firm working with data storage, networking and cloud companies. I founded the PR agency in 2005 and, since then, we have launched and helped grow many startups while keeping our focus on this industry. In 2010 and 2013, I have been recognized as a Top 10 Tech PR Pro and SVPR as a Top Tech PR agency, based on a survey of journalists and analysts.
