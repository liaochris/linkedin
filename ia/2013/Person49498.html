Gordion Akademi's Experience

Managing Director

Gordion Akademi

Education Management industry

March 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months) Ankara, Turkey

Established in 2008, Gordion Akademi is leading provider of teacher training and professional development programmes for teachers of English.
English language teachers and instructors arround Turkey choose to develop their teaching skills with Gordion Akademi’s specialist team of trainers which include some of the industry’s leading academics, education professionals and authors.

Gordion Akademi is also a Cambridge University Press Education partner, and runs teacher training programmes for teachers leading to professional qualifications such as Cert TEFL.

Gordion Learning Solutions
The new consultancy “partner” of Gordion Akademi

Gordion Learning Solutions brings together the best international educational service providers and talent and a wide range of Turkish educational stakeholders who want to make a difference in how they “perform” across education. GLSs been created to act as a “premium complete learning solution” provider for a wider range of learning and educational objectives.

Gordion Learning Solutions is also dedicated to developing creative, capacity-building projects for schools, colleges and universities wishing to “do more” with programmes of institutional effectiveness and accreditation with high profile international partners.

International Accreditation
Gordion Learning Solutions offer local professional support to maintain standards for English Language Programs which form the foundation for accreditation developed for language schools that are applying to be accredited by international accreditation boards.

Training and professional support on areas including mission, curriculum, assessment and leadership is offered especially for language schools in Turkey wishing to demonstrate that they meet the standards in ways appropriate to their settings and missions, in order to offer the best possible language instruction for their students.
