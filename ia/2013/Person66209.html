John Cooney's Summary

I am a fearless game designer with a drive to make amazing experiences and spectacular games. Over eight years I have published 90+ games, culminating in hundreds of millions of plays. I am responsible for all facets of development, including game design, artwork, programming, animation, UI/UX, and sound design.

+ Producer on team projects ranging from solo to 30+ developers.

+ Several honors, including Game Developer of the Year in 2010 from Écrans. Winner of several animation festival awards, including "Environmental Award" for MTM Film Festival.

+ Rapid prototype and iteration experience, taking games from concept to execution in hours.

+ Browser-based/mobile game development.

+ When not making games I love being outside running, bike riding, and rock climbing.

Specialties

Game design, game development, actionscript, user experience design, user interface design, rapid prototyping, mobile, iOS, browser-based gaming
