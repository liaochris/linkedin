Emmanuelle Chaulet's Summary

F. Emmanuelle Chaulet is currently a Lecturer at the University of Southern Maine Department of Theatre. An international film actress (Eric Rohmer, Jon Jost), a theatre director and acting coach, she is the author of the book "A Balancing Act: The Development of ENERGIZE! a Holistic Approach to Acting" available on Amazon.com http://amzn.to/t7ow5F

She is a Voice Dialogue Facilitator, and trained with Martha-Lou Wolff and Judith Tamar Stone.

She offers workshops, speaking engagements and private coaching for artists and creative souls.

She specializes in applying energy healing modalities to performing arts techniques, offering unprecedented ways to develop a character and revolutionary tools to rebalance emotions after performance.

Her work has been recognized by the New York City Drama Book Shop, Backstage Magazine, the Kennedy Center American College Theater Festival, the New England Theater Conference, Theatre Topics, The New England Theatre Journal, Premiere Magazine and many more.

She is a Fulbright Scholar and a member of the Association for Contemplative Mind in Higher Education. She is co-founder of ANPACT, the Association for the New Paradigm in Acting and Creative Techniques. She has been included in Who's Who in America since 2007.

Specialties: Acting, Acting coach, Theatre director, Voice Dialogue, Author, Energy work, Theatre, Film, Reiki, Performance Stress Coaching, Performers' holistic health, Performers' spiritual purpose, Bilingual English/French and dual citizen USA/France.

Starlight Acting Institute is devoted to inspire actors, performers and all creative souls to reach their highest potential both creatively and spiritually. It teaches core values for a Holistic Approach to Acting and to Life.

Founded by Emmanuelle Chaulet in 2003, Starlight Acting Institute developed its teachings through the fusion of traditional and realist approaches to acting from Constantin Stanislavski, Michael Chekhov and Lee Strasberg, with cutting edge Energy Awareness knowledge from energy field healing modalities such as Polarity, RYSE® and Reiki as well as Voice Dialogue of Hal and Sidra Stone, Ph. D.The result is a unique acting method called ENERGIZE.

With this work you will:

▪ activate your Highest Creative Self
▪ discover the multitude of Inner Characters (or Inner Voices) present in you and learn how to play with them in performance.
▪ bridge the 'outside-in' and 'inside-out' acting approaches
▪ connect with the vibratory blueprint of your character and of its story
▪ keep your instrument emotionally balanced and filled with energy
▪ heal pre-performance anxiety and post-performance stress
▪ re-define your artistic goals through your spiritual growth
▪ develop a career strategy and learn how to market yourself and your shows
▪ reclaim your creative leadership and responsibility in this world
