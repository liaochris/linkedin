Laura Lasmane's Summary

"Don't go into something to test the waters, go into things to make waves." - my philosophy.

Started my career during high-school working as a sports journalist for several sports-related websites. While studying Communication Science (BA) at the University of Latvia I involved in international project coordination. After graduation I was invited to work as External Relations Coordinator at the Latvian Institute where my experience with public relations begun. It was broadened while working at P.R.A.E. Public Relations as an Account Assistant.

After working as a Social Media Account Manager for nearly a year at Inspired Communications, I am currently holding the Senior Digital Account Manager position at Hill+Knowlton Strategies where my daily work is related to social media marketing and digital/web project management.

Recently a new online dialogue agency CLICK has been formed - my role there is to develop and execute social media content strategies, manage digital projects, and provide client support. Besides all that I prepare social media lectures and workshops that are tailored to meet clients' needs and profile.

Specialties

External relations, publicity, project managing and coordination, public relations, media monitoring, social media, customer support, social media workshops and lectures etc.
