Dale J. Stephens' Summary

I founded UnCollege.org because we're paying too much for college and learning too little. In just four months I've been featured in the New York Times, FastCompany, NPR, ABC, CBS, Fox, CNN, The Chronicle of Higher Education, The Huffington Post, New York Magazine, Washington Post, and more. I was recently lauded by Forbes as 'a successful teen knowledge worker.'

In May 2011 I was named one of twenty-four Thiel Fellows, chosen from more than 500 youth around the world. Sponsored by Peter Thiel, the Thiel Fellowship recognizes the top global entrepreneurs under the age of twenty.

Perigee/Penguin will publish my first book, Hacking Your Education, in early 2013.

I speak about youth, education, and entrepreneurship to audiences worldwide. I have spoken at NBCUniversal to the C-level executive team, The New York Times, Stanford University, Digital Life Design, and TED 2012 among others.

I am twenty.

Specialties

Project management, non-profit management, unschooling, homeschooling, higher education, long-distance collaboration, marketing, business development, JFDI, alternative education, networking, writing, speaking
