Jen Miller's Summary

I am the owner and founder of NeedSomeoneToBlog.com, a company based in Orange County, California dedicated to helping small business owners develop an internet marketing presence through coordinating 100% original website content and safe blog posts with social media posting and great stories.

I believe in the power of the written word.

I believe that a unique, focused blog can establish your authority and credibility.

I know that growing your business requires search engine visibility and I want to make that easy for you to achieve.

I provide unique custom business blogging and social media posting to busy entrepreneurs who prefer to dedicate their time to building their business, rather than writing about it.

Handling your small business social media and internet marketing is key to growing your company in today's business world, and I deliver results on a local search level, along with improving worldwide search results.

I offer a variety of services to develop your blog through DIY SEO advice to ghost blogging and original content development. Each offering is customized to meet your specific needs and aimed at delivering your message in your voice to help you develop real connections with your clients - connections that will GROW your business.

I love to share what I know and enjoy teaching small business owners and college students ways to effectively target and market themselves with online tools. Let's connect!

To find out more about me, visit my website at http://needsomeonetoblog.com/connect-with-me/ or send me a message via LinkedIn. I'd love to meet you!
