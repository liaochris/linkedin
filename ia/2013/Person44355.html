Fehzan Ali's Summary

Fehzan is the co-founder of Adscend Media and serves as the Chief Executive Officer. He is responsible for driving and implementing the strategic vision of the Company. Since inception, Fehzan has secured business with high revenue publishers and top advertisers through his deep relationships and experience in the sector. Fehzan Ali's Experience

Co-Founder & CEO

Adscend Media LLC

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

August 2008 – Present (5 years 2 months)

Adscend Media is a leader in the online advertising space, focused on developing innovative, turnkey digital content monetization solutions. Founded in 2009, we are a privately held and debt-free company headquartered in Austin, TX with staff located throughout the United States.

The company’s flagship solution is the Content Gateway technology.

For publishers, our turnkey solutions allow them to maximize revenues from their premium content, while our vast global reach and high engagement solutions allows advertisers to acquire new customers, perform market research, and ultimately grow sales. Since our inception, we have served over 1 billion relevant ads, producing hundreds of millions of clicks, and tens of millions of actionable leads.

With a commitment to a high quality user experience, Adscend Media has established itself as a leader in the digital content monetization industry. A technology company at heart, we continue to develop our content monetization platform to accommodate the needs of both premium content publishers and advertisers alike.
