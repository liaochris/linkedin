Hugo Menino Aguiar's Summary

Social entrepreneurship explorer, online marketer, web developer and an easy smile.

Currently working at Google as a scalable support geek and AdSense community specialist.

Strongly believes that the younger generation has the potential to create sustainable solutions for social problems. Passioned with non-profits and volunteering since the age of 15. Co-founder and lead of Associação Fazer Avançar a non-profit that educates, trains and mobilizes youth.

Enthusiastic with social entrepreneurship, and with creating sustainable and simple solutions to solve complex social problems. Dreams with societies that integrate and value cultural diversity.
