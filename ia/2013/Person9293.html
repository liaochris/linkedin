April Simpson's Summary

I’m a journalist with international reporting experience as well as media development and academic credentials.

I write and report quickly and accurately, while drawing from all the appropriate digital tools, including multimedia and SEO. Also, I’m a skilled feature writer, with a proven ability to craft stimulating narratives, such as a piece on Africa’s growing population of teenagers who were infected with HIV at birth – a success story for having survived, yet also facing new challenges as they grow up.

I’m passionate about telling stories on too-often overlooked people and places, and aspire to one-day report on humanitarian issues from across the globe. I’m persistent, I know how to deliver, whether it’s breaking news, a quick blog post, tweet, or analytical feature. I have excelled when I’m working on my own, as part of a team, or managing a special project.
