Joe Hertvik's Summary

I work for and with various companies to provide IT services, including:

- Project management for large-scale IT projects
- Data Center management
- Help Desk management
- IBM i (aka AS/400 and iSeries) administration, upgrades, care, and management
- Consulting services for all of the above
- Written Marketing content including white papers, product reviews, and direct marketing material (brochures, sale, seminars, and webinars)
- Technical articles for computer industry publications. Over 1000 articles to date.

Specialties:

IT Management, IBM i administration (care and feeding), high availability setup and implementation, setup of medium to large ERP systems on the midrange environment, Help Desk management, staff management, vendor management, budgeting.

Excellent writing, presentation, and speaking skills in explaining technical information to end users, managers, CIOs and executives
