Daniel Herndon's Summary

Interested in Coffee, Music, Business, Marketing | CEO innovation marketing agency @redwalllive | Co-Author Facebook AIO For Dummies.

A little history:

1998-2002 Selling college dorm furniture nationwide. Sold to Yale, UCONN, University of Jamaica and more.

2003-2006 Developing, marketing, managing, performing in "Liptocoal"
During that time I sold copiers, traded commodity metals, washed windows and then went full time in the music business.

2006-2008 Started redwall MUSIC. Marketing and Developing bands and their records. I thought I would go after brands to sponsor artists and events as a marketing effort. This idea birthed redwall LIVE.

2008-Present, redwall LIVE is an Indianapolis Marketing Innovation Company that provides Campaigns, Strategies and Tools for Successful Marketing. redwall provides Creative services, Strategic planning and systems to make businesses better.
