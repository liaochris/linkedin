H+A International, Inc. the MarCom Pros' Summary

H+A International specializes in working with organizations around the world to help them take their brand communications to the next level. We have a very impressive track record of helping organizations of all sizes enhance their awareness, sales, and ROI.

We understand how the entire marketing communications puzzle fits together - not just the individual components. Our team of skilled professionals has vast experience in creating multifaceted marketing communications programs for various domestic and global organizations in a variety of fields. We work closely with our clients to determine what mix of marketing tactics including publicity, advertising, direct mail and special events will most effectively achieve their specific objectives.
