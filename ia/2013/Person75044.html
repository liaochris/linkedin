Kevin Manthei's Summary

Kevin Manthei scores music for film, television and games. In television, Kevin scored the cult-hit animated series Invader Zim as well as Generator Rex for Cartoon Network, Xiaolin Showdown & Johnny Test for Warner Brothers and Brandy and Mr. Whiskers for Disney. Kevin has also composed additional music for many feature films (Resident Evil, Scream 3, Scary Movie 2) as well as scoring independent features and animated features (Batman: Gotham Knight and Justice League: The New Frontier). Kevin has composed music for nearly 60 game titles and been nominated for 2 Annie Awards.

Currently (Winter 2013) Kevin is scoring the 2nd season of Marvel's newest animated series Ultimate Spider-Man as well working on the MMO Neverwinter for Cryptic Studios/Perfect World.
