Cena Block's Summary

My philosophy is Clarity + Focused Action = Sanity! Sane Spaces, LLC allows me to do what I love in a way that serves moms in business and small business owners who struggle to balance their desire for business results with a life that works. I am a coach, radio show host, author, facilitator and mentor to mom entrepreneurs around the globe.

Coaching: I primarily help small businesses and solo entrepreneurs (mompreneurs) to organize, systematize and delegate non-essential tasks so they can save time and make more money. My ideal clients are smart achievers who have enjoyed professional success yet severed corporate identities to create a life-style businesses. They want more clients and more money while having time for themselves and their busy families. My clients take action, and HATE waiting for results. They are quick decision-makers who tend to become overwhelmed with details and avoid the routine tasks necessary for business success. Because they are so capable, often delegating doesn't occur so they try to do it all themselves. They need to clarify, prioritize, systematize, create processes, delegate, and reclaim their time so they can focus on what matters most!

I provide 1 on 1 customized coaching, group programs and VIP days that create a "sane space" to get clear and establish step by step actions to achieve the results you desire.

Workshops: Time management, productivity, communication, teamwork, workflow and process improvement, leading meetings, and networking. Some workshops are available as "done-for-you" downloads that can be co-branded and modularized for deployment with your staff or team.

25 years of corporate experience in HR, Training & Performance informs my practice.

* Business Coach & Mompreneur Mentor
* Facilitator & Trainer
* Speaker & Author

If you're curious about how I can help you, PLEASE contact me today and let's discuss your needs.
