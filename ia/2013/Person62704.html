Calliste Jr, is a proven leader, lateral thinker, tech investor, C-suite exec, mgmnt consultant. For 30+ years he recruited successful teams, developed and partnered music co's and sold an est. 5.0M units, generating an est. $25M in global sales and grew sales at venues by 350%. His peers say he’s “an authority on music marketing, licensing, and independent talent development.” and "...a natural at identifying new trends and opportunities related to start ups and strategic business.” He studied dual majors in IT & MIS at Ross School of Mgmnt and Leadership at Franklin U. He's fluent in English, light Spanish, learning Mandarin, Arabic, French.

Since '11 he is COO & President at cloud start up Simplestreamtech.com. In 2009 he’s MD at Callistecomm. Since '01 he serves as Chairman & CEO, Bassmint Music Inc. He volunteers on Economist's EIU Opinions Leaders Panel.

Created online curriculum for 2 internships at 12 accredited universities OSU, OU, Capital U, UofM (FL) and others spending 4.5K hrs supervising 30+ interns.

In ‘00 he promoted a VIP bday for The Duchess of York Sarah Ferguson, Columbus Monthly, Charles Penzone, Guess?, FTV.com.

His music appears on 150+ definitive Electro and Hip Hop music comps with Gwen Steffani, Daft Punk, Missy Elliott, David Guetta, DJ QBert, Timbaland and Magoo, Run DMC, Beastie Boys, Common feat. Pharrell, Jay-Z, Eminem, 50 Cent and others. Video games GTA: Vice City, NBA Shootout 2004, DJ Hero: Renegade Edition and Traktor Scratch Pro II, FIFA 2013 (see Hashim Music). He received RIAA Gold awards for 2 In A Room “Wiggle It” and Ahmad “Back in the Day”.
