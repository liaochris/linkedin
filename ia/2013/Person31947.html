David Leonard's Summary

David currently serves as an advisor and legal counsel to energy sector owners, developers and investors leveraging business, legal, technical, government and community affairs, RFP, PUC, M&A, environmental, permitting, regulatory, EPC, and construction relationships and experience as an energy executive, general counsel and engineer in the U.S. and international locations.

He specializes in assisting clients to strategically optimize the opportunities and navigate the unique challenges of the energy sector for project and business development, acquisitions, and collaboration in the downstream petroleum, LNG, alternative fuel, biomass, wind, solar, energy efficiency, power, public utility, jet fuel consortium, and venture capital sectors.

David has served in legal roles at Chevron, BHP Billiton, Imperium Renewables and Tesoro and as an advisor and legal counsel to an extensive client base that has included Private Equity, Venture Capital and Hedge Funds, Public Utilities, Airline Jet Fuel Consortiums and Operators, International Airport Authorities, Alternate Fuel and Biochemical Start-up Companies, Wind and Solar Energy Developers, and Petrochemical Refining and Marketing Companies.
