IMS Pro A/V's Summary

IMS Pro A/V is a recognized industry leader since 1979, providing products, integration services and expert advice on computer hardware and software, pro audio & video equipment applications.

We have designed, sold, rented, installed and serviced equipment worldwide for recording studios, houses of worship, schools, live venues, military, government, broadcast and commercial facilities.

We are authorized for over 400 product lines many with academic/government pricing and we offer flexible financing plans. 

To see additional information about our company, please visit our web site: http://imsproav.com.
