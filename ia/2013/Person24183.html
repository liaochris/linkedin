Christine Kirk's Summary

Christine Kirk is Founder & CEO of Social Muse Communications, advising luxury travel, restaurant, technology, and lifestyle brands on social media marketing, traditional public relations campaigns, and online branding. She has amassed over 30,000 engaged followers on Twitter (find her @LuxuryPRGal), and is a guest blogger and speaker on various social media, PR and luxury subjects. Christine was declared a “Klout Star” by the source measuring online influence. http://corp.klout.com/blog/2012/03/klout-star-christine-kirk/

Christine also has experience in traditional PR campaigns and media relations and has landed influential placements for clients in local and national television, radio and print media including: USA Today, The Wall Street Journal, The Los Angeles Times, The Hollywood Reporter, CNN, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Good Day LA, etc.

Christine specializes in online communications and social media marketing campaigns for food & beverage, restaurant/nightclub, luxury travel, luxury real estate, consumer lifestyle products, consumer electronics, wireless, consumer tech, and automotive clients.

Additionally, Christine is founder of the popular live Twitter chat #luxchat - an online discussion about the intersection of luxury brands and social media. Prestigious guests include, Lexus, The Ritz-Carlton Hotels, Four Seasons Hotels, Exclusive Resorts, Bergdorf Goodman, and more.

Christine is also the LA Correspondent for Forbes Travel Guide, covering food, restaurants, and luxury travel on Startle.com

Christine Kirk is a Luxury Award Contest Winner for Top "Decadent PR Pro" of 2010.

Specialties: Social Media Marketing, Media and Blogger Relations, Company Branding and Brand Management, Product/Company Launch, Crisis Management, and Event Planning
