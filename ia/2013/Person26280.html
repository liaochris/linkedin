Colin Cameron, CA's Summary

Founder of Profits for Partners, Management Consulting Inc. Colin provides strategic profit-focused advice based on over 25 years of executive management and consulting experience with law and professional service firms. He is a management consultant, chartered accountant and former COO of Clark Wilson LLP, a major Vancouver, Canada law firm.

Colin advises small and midsize law firms in the following key areas related to profitability:

Profitability improvement, strategic planning, firm governance, partner compensation, financial management and operations management.

    Law firm financial dashboards should focus management and partners on those key performance indicators (KPIs) that are critical to the success of the firm. As with a car dashboard, too many gauges simply distract and lessen their value—and warning lights may be ignored until it's too late.
