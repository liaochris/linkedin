Jeanette Eleff's Summary

I am delighted to be a certified success coach, speaker, and author who inspires people to have more success with less stress - building your business, thriving in your career, and living your life does not have to be a struggle.

I really understand the demands of a woman in a leadership position because I have served as a leader in healthcare, municipal government, cable television, Chamber of Commerce, and not-for-profits. (whew!) I know what it means to have many demands on my time, to at times feel unappreciated, and to wish for something better. By incorporating both my stress-management-instructor and coaching experience in my own life, I was able to exchange my stress and struggle for a fuller and richer life and business.

Several years ago I chose to transform my life when I took the leap from being a busy healthcare leader to a coach entrepreneur. I have never regretted this leap and love using the powerful tool of coaching to help enhance the lives of my clients. I am now living my dream!

My additional background as a registered nurse, health educator, and stress management instructor combined with my life coaching skills makes me the ideal coach to help clients transform stress and struggle into balanced and fulfilled lives.

I love to write and am also a contributing author in the book The Spirit of Women Entrepreneurs, where I give women leaders a concrete formula for staying organized while building a gratifying business.

With my business Focused Vitality, I help my clients stay focused and organized on creating a fulfilling and energetic business or career.

Specialties

Executive coaching, small business strategies, personal coaching, team development, leadership development, creative workshops, mastermind groups, group facilitation, stress management, keynote speeches, fun & adventure!
