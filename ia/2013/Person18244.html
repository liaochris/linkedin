Bruce G. Barnes' Summary

•I am an entrepreneur. I make every effort to find new ways to conduct my brokerage that is beneficial to both my clients and me.
•The internet has dramatically changed the landscape in the way residential property is marketed and sold. It has also changed how information is delivered and exchanged.
•These changes affect agents, brokers, sellers and buyers.
•When it comes to real estate commissions, I just do things differently than almost all agents and brokers.
•I have developed a business model that affords both sellers and buyers substantial savings without sacrificing services.
•BG Barnes Real Estate is demonstrating that it can serve clients well and operate profitably at the same time.
•The effect of the internet has given me the opportunity to offer service to sellers at rates much less than others and to also give back to buyers a percentage of my commission.
•Before the internet both buyers and sellers did not readily have access to properties for sale on the (mls). Now they do.
•Because of the internet properties for sale are exposed to the market in a way they never have been.
•This is not a discount brokerage or limited services brokerage. Full service is provided to all clients.
•Selling and buying residential property is not just about commissions.
•Selling or buying residential real estate in the State of California can be an arduous and complicated task. Real Estate laws and procedures must be followed.
•Marketing property for a seller and showing property to a buyer is the easiest part in the whole process.
•When a seller receives an offer or a buyer makes an offer, that is when a Broker or Agent really goes to work. It’s when the rubber meets the road.
