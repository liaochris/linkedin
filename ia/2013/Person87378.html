Marquette Jones' Summary

Marquette Jones is an alumna of New York University’s graduate film program. Prior to film school, she was an active public interest attorney and social entrepreneur in Oakland, California.

With several award-winning short films under her belt as a director and producer, Marquette creates creative content that focuses on strong and complex female characters for the entertainment of her expanding and reflective audience. Her most recent film project as producer, “Jackie”,s began its festival run at the Academy Award-qualifying festivals, Atlanta Film Festival and Cleveland International Film Festival. Marquette’s producing talents will next be used to create a public service announcement in conjunction with the non-profit organization, Women in Film, in the Fall of 2012.

Marquette’s work as a director focuses on storytelling from the feminine perspective and seeks to reflect the realities faced by her audience in nuanced and imaginative ways.

When she is not busy writing, producing, or directing, Marquette indulges her obsession with color and nail polish through her blog, www.swagpolish.com.

Specialties: Film Production, Line Producing, Production Budgets, Contracts and Negotiations, Film Editing, Social Media Management
