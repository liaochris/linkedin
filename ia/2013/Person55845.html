Ioannis (Yannis) Roussos' Summary

As a software engineer, I specialize in the areas of requirement analysis, design and development of large information systems. I believe that the future of information management lies in the web and mobile environments, so my focus during the last years has been on the process of design, development and deployment of web information systems and mobile applications that access them.

Specialties: Analysis, design and architecture of Information Systems.
Software product definition, architecture, development
