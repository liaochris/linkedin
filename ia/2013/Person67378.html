Jonathan Andrew's Summary

Currently working as the Technology Lead at The Hive Group, I'm responsible for leading a talented team of developers and software engineers in their creation of iPad, iPhone, Android and Web Apps. We work on a variety of projects from games, to websites, to augmented reality, for the worlds leading pharmaceutical firms.

My technology background is as an 'all encompassing Interface Development Specialist' , with ten years of industry experience in working with AS3, HTML5, CSS3 / LESS, Objective-C, Cordova (PhoneGap) and JavaScript - using knockout.js, node.js, ember, backbone.js and grunt.js.

Although specialising in front-end technologies, I'm well versed in PHP, MySQL, SQLlite, Python, and various MVC frameworks - such as Django and CodeIgniter.

Notable clients include: Sony Ericsson, 3Mobile, L'Oreal, Vichy, GSK, AstraZenica, Yamaha, MTV, Nickelodian, FIFA, Activision, Sony.
