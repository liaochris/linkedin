Ca` Dien Christie's Summary

A very humbled individual who accepted and appreciated the growing accolades such as the: "HYPE TV 2009 AWARDS - Music Video Editor of the Year 2008”, "Editor of the "2010 EME AWARDS - Music Video of the Year" and “2010 CARIBVIBEZ AWARDS - Director Avant-Garde Nominee”.
