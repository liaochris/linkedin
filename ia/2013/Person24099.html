Christina Phillips' Summary

Knowledgeable, discreet, honest and hardworking are qualities one will find when working with Christina. In 2012, Christina alone assisted buyers and sellers in over 18 million dollars worth of real estate transactions doubling sales from 2011. Christina was also recently voted a 5 star agent by New Jersey Monthly Magazine.

Christina™s background is in the performing arts. She hails from NJ but left at a very young age to pursue a career in classical ballet. Achieving much success, she performed all over the world with the critically acclaimed Miami City Ballet, nationally with the world famous Metropolitan Opera and Cirque du Soleil, and on the Broadway stage with the hit show The Phantom of the Opera. That high standard of excellence and professionalism is now carried forward to a passion for real estate.

Forever a real estate aficionado she enjoys the challenge of finding buyers homes that they love and helping sellers achieve a successful sale. Widely traveled, Princeton is one of her favorite places and she would love to share it with you. Not only will she show you the town but she will give you an insider view of what life is like for a Princetonian. She believes that one is not just buying a home but a lifestyle and community.

Christina returned to Princeton from Brooklyn in 2002 after retiring from the stage. Along with her husband, she has renovated and/or built 8 homes in the Princeton area in the past 6 years. She currently resides in Princeton Township with her family and is involved in many community activities. Please don™t hesitate to call Christina whether you are thinking about renting, buying or selling anywhere in the Greater Princeton area.
