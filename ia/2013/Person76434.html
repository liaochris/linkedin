Kristine Ranger's Summary

Driven by a competitive spirit, marketing mindset and inquisitive nature, I provide integrated performance improvement systems, consulting, and coaching to help organizations achieve their value proposition in the marketplace.

As a "Knowledge Navigator", I use a wide variety of tools and approaches, including the Everything DiSC library, to overcome dysfunctional teams, provide guidance to leaders, and develop internal operations for organizations.

Along with strategic assessments, I use a unique blend of skills and experiences to transform people, teams and organizations to talent-driven, knowledge-based providers. I am a "self contained innovation team" who can generate new ideas, advance and refine them, then execute to completion. In every situation, I bring wisdom and wit, knowledge and know-how, and experience and expertise!

Specialties: Group facilitation, performance measurement and management, program/project evaluation, leadership and organizational development, coaching, employee engagement, creative writing/copywrite, public speaking, business development and team innovation.
