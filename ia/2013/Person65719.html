Joe Santoro's Summary

Founder, Personal Property Managers; www.personalpropertymanagers.com. Personal Property Managers services all forms of residential and multi-family property management. This includes: Senior Real Estate Life Style Transition & downsizing services, estate sales, Emergency Response Services for property emergencies, Absentee Home Watch Services for extended stays away from home or relocation, Property Rentals, Residential Property Management, Multi-Family Property Management, and full service Real Estate buy/sell services. Joe is a certified Senior Real Estate Specialist (SRES) and a licensed Realtor in Pennsylvania and New Jersey. Prior to founding Personal Property Managers, Joe was a senior manager at Anheuser-Busch, PepsiCo, Keebler and NutriSystems. Joe has had extensive experience in sales, operations, marketing, organizational change, business expansion, business start-up and training. Joe is an expert in building businesses via strategic alliances and partnering. In addition, Joe is passionately involved in volunteer work with wounded veterans and the Marine Corps and is the co-founder and executive director of the Veterans Owners Business Alliance.

Specialties: Personal Property Managers specializes in providing a single-point-of-contact and a one-stop solution for all property management needs (residential, multi-family). In addition, PPM provides senior Real Estate life style transition services (for those who are transitioning from independent living to assisted living), emergency response services, relocation services, extended stay home watch services, rentals and full Real Estate services. Joe specializes in 1031 exchanges and innovative mortgage financing for his clients. With Personal Property Managers one call does it all.
