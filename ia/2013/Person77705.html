Kyle Cassano, MBA's Summary

Kyle Cassano is a Sacramento native with an educational background in public relations, and professional background digital marketing. Currently, he owns Capital City Social Media, co-owns Sacramento Marketing Labs, and is lead organizer for Sacramento's #1 social media networking community, SacTweetUp.

His main goal is to help small to medium-sized businesses and entrepreneurs grow their companies with the use of digital marketing and other proven Internet marketing strategies.

Capital City Social Media:

Capital City Social Media relieves business owners of the day-to-day burdens associated with managing social media, and enables them to focus on their core business and customer service. We effortlessly take social media management off your shoulders. www.capcitysocial.com

Sacramento Marketing Labs:

Sacramento Marketing Labs is Sacramento's first social media agency helping local small businesses better leverage proven internet marketing strategies with social media to grow their businesses. The company's core focus is to work 1-on-1 with business owners and entrepreneurs in small workshops. We provide free and paid workshops for everyone on a variety of topics relating to social media. www.sacramentomarketinglabs.com

SacTweetUp:

The SacTweetUp has held 30 events to date. The community is the largest of its kind in Sacramento, and is one of the most attended monthly social media events at any time in the area. We like to say these events are 2-parts social and 1-part networking. www.sactweetup.com

Specialties: Social Media Management, Social Media Strategy, Custom Facebook Design, Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
