Dan Parsons' Projects

  • Short-Term & Emergency Use Aerodrome Standards - BARS Implementation Guide Vol II

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Dan Parsons, Cameron Ross

    To assist BARS Member Organisation with the assessment and development of aerodromes in support of short-term exploratory mining operation or for emergency use only during production operations, we produced a set of clear and targeted standards for operators to follow. Based on industry best-practice and in line with the BARS risk approach, this guidance material gives resource companies a sense of confidence in the safety of their aerodrome.

  • Authorisation Holder Performance Indicator

    • September 2011 to June 2012
    Team Members: Dan Parsons, Michael Burgess, Marlies de Geus Verschoor

    Led the development of a questionnaire-based tool to assist regulatory inspectors in assessing the oversight posture to be applied to any Civil Aviation Authorisation Holder (AOC, COA, Aerodrome, etc.). This tool allows CASA's certificate management teams to consider a number of organisational and performance factors for each authorisation holder in a consistent manner across offices, states and operating environments. The result is an indication of the level of surveillance to be applied to the authorisation holder.
