Ed Barkley's Summary

Download my resume and recommendations summary below in "Ed Barkley's Files"

I’m a true "End-to-End" BA, meaning I have experience from the very beginning of a project (business case), into requirements gathering, then development (facilitating understanding of the specifications), test (test plans, scripts, users stories, etc.), UAT (write and guide testing) and onto production support (known issues, work-arounds, training, etc.). I can even help with improvement requests that are submitted later in an application’s life cycle.

As a BA, I proactively and passionately facilitate successful software development projects by:

• Helping the stakeholders and IT communicate effectively, and improving both the final product and processes we use.

• Applying my knowledge in best practices, organizational analysis, and change management.

Skills:
• Requirements – Waterfall, Agile and beyond.
• Test Management
• SDLC, user stories, iterative development, issue management
• Process improvement, change management and team mentoring
