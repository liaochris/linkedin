Amy Lyndon's Summary

Lyndon has 40+ films to her credit including; “And They’re Off” opposite Sean Astin, "The House That Jack Built” opposite Joe Mantegna, and "The Poughkeepsie Tapes" for MGM. She can be seen in 5 LionsGate Feature Films: "B.T.K," "Bram Stoker's Dracula's Guest", "Chicago Massacre: Richard Speck", "Ed Gein: The Butcher Of Plainfield", and "Are You Scared?"

On TV, she Guest Starred in over 30+ TV shows: “Adopting Terror” for Lifetime, “Law and Order: LA,” “Eagleheart” w/ Chris Elliot, “NCIS,” "Ugly Betty," "Entourage," "NYPD Blue," "JAG," Recurring in “Days Of Our Lives,” “The Bold and the Beautiful," "General Hospital," & "Young and The Restless."

She's a Multi-Award Winning Director/Writer/Producer. "Odessa" starring the late Yolanda King won top awards on the Festival circuit and the screenplay is HOT. As a Writer, Lyndon won the "Spirit of Moondance Award" for Best Family Screenplay in The Moondance Film Festival and as a Director her World Premiere play "The Parasites" was produced by The Met Theatre to sold out audiences. “Award-Winning,” a film that Lyndon Directed and Produced won Major Awards on the Festival Circuit along with a Directing and Audience Choice Award for the film “The Whiskey Girls” from The NY International Film Festival. Her Comedy “I Wanna Marry Larry,” that she Wrote, Produced and Directed won a Merit of Excellence and she’s currently directing several episodes of “The Whiskey Girls” inspired by the Award Winning Film. http://iamenoughproductions.com

Lyndon also owned and operated a Management Co. for 9 years and has been an International Booking Coach for close to 20 and created The Lyndon Technique: A 15 Guideline Map To Booking Technique and has helped 1000’s of actors learn how to book jobs and run their business. Look for The Lyndon Technique “The15 Guideline Map To Booking” Handbook and Lecture CD’s at Amazon.com or on her website http://thelyndontechnique.com/ and check out http://TLTaccess.com Amy Lyndon’s ACTOR BUSINESS SITE.

CEO

The Lyndon Technique

January 1992 – Present (21 years 7 months)

The Lyndon Technique is a revolutionary "15 Guideline Map To Booking" Technique that provides a practical approach to auditioning and booking the job. This way of working is for any actor at any level in their career who wishes to learn the art of competition. By learning this technique you will know how to break down a script properly and always know what you are doing in your audition. This technique can take you from the bottom 99% to the top 1% of actors booking jobs.

"The Lyndon Technique" has helped thousands of students learn how to book acting jobs, run successful businesses and consistently earn their living as actors. Amy Lyndon just launched http://tltaccess.com to help actors build their acting business from online educational information.
