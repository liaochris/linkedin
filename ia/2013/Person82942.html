    The homepage that groups together all the entertainment products of Globo Organizations including the sites of Globo TV programs, and other channels such as Globosat, Multshow and GNT, as well as the magazine sites for Brazilian Vogue and Marie Claire, radio sites, Receitas.com, Globo Teatro and others.     Team Members: Luciana Albuquerque

    A research about the usability of the Kindle app for iPad. Through techniques of interface evaluation and following the premises of User Experience, the research begins with an analysis to become familiar with similar products, and then, collect data about the habits of using of tablets, followed by a cooperative evaluation (UT).

  • G1 Local News

    • December 2011 to June 2012

The full project involves 3 templates (city, region and the country), the first of which is generated automatically for the aproximatelly 6000 cities in Brazil, all filterad using semantic content. Throughout the project, we used techniques like interviewing the journalists, personas and scenarios, paper prototyping and usability testing.
