John Webber's Summary

I have 10 years experience founding, building and managing internet related companies. I built my first website for my footbag company, Dragonfly Footbags, when I was 15 (2001) and learned e-commerce, web design and online marketing while I built that company. I have since built Dragonfly into one of the best known (and best selling) footbag brands online and offline.

In 2004 I started iTech Resources with my dad. At the time, I had my CCNA and A+ certifications so I offered computer service and network support.

In 2006 I went to work for a local web design company and did project management and interface design for them. I spent a year with them and learned a lot about business management and growth. I worked with a great team of people and had a great boss who really taught me a lot.

In 2007 I founded Ad Stream with a focus on web design and online marketing (SEO & PPC) for local businesses. Using all that I learned from my past experiences, I have built Ad Stream into a very successful (and very profitable) web design business. We are a small team with a sharp focus on web design and e-commerce for small businesses. Thanks to the strengths of each member, we are able to launch usable, powerful websites consistently on-time and on-budget.

In 2008, I founded StrataShops with a few other partners. StrataShops builds online brands who sell indoor & outdoor furniture. We currently have 7 strong brands with plans for many more over the coming years. Our focus all day, every day, is on customer service. Our people actually care about your order and we don't outsource anything.

In June 2011, I founded Carved. We make and sell beautiful, real wood iPhone/iPad skins and cases. We offer custom skins and cases and sell our products through our website and specialty retailers. Check out all our products at www.carved.com.

Specialties

Business creation, sustainable growth and profitability, Website design, E-Commerce, project management, search engine optimization, internet strategies, internet marketing techniques, designing for usability
