Ben Bosher's Summary

For over 16 years I have been working in the employee benefits arena. Particular expertise with Health Savings Accounts (HSA's) and Health Reimbursement Arrangements (HRA's). I take an analytic approach to employee benefits to enable benefit managers to quickly assess the insurance products offering the greatest value to their company. With the detailed proposals prepared by BDS, our clients are able to save valuable time and money.
Once a decision is made, BDS takes care of all the details ensuring the smooth implementation of the new or replacement benefit(s).
The efficiency of our service throughout the year will become clearly evident whenever questions or concerns arise. BDS provides same-day response to phone and email inquiries from benefit managers and employees.

Over the last three years I have developed an expertise in Medicare and Supplemental plans including MediGap policies, Part D prescription drug plans and a less-expensive but comprehensive Medicare Advantage. The last product incorporates both supplemental medical and creditable Part D drug coverage all into one health plan. Let me help you sort through the complexities of Medicare and commercial ancillary products.

Specialties: Employer-sponsored benefits, flex plan document preparation, evaluation of group
disability policies, self-funded dental plans.

Medicare and the supplemental products offered by commercial insurers.

Consulting services connected with the Vermont Benefit Exchange starting in October,
2013 to assist Vermont residents and businesses to purchase individual/group helath
insurance.
