Anup Soans' Summary

Are you in the pharmaceutical industry?

I can help you in areas of pharmaceutical sales training for pharmaceutical representatives and managers.

I work in the area of field force excellence for pharmaceutical industry and medical devices industry.

I began my career as a pharmaceutical representative. I also worked as an oncology product specialist, frontline manager in pharmaceutical and medical devices industry.

I conduct skill development and Field Force engagement programs that bring an alignment between employee aspirations and organizational objectives.

Keynote Speaker at pharmaceutical industry conferences and pharmaceutical industry forums for senior professionals.

Specialties: Motivational Speaker with High Quality Audience Engagement Skills.

MedicinMan is an effort to empower pharma field force people to excel in their personal and professional life - http://medicinman.net

I am the editor of MedicinMan, which is sent free to more than 60,000 professionals in Pharma, Medical Devices, Diagnostics and Surgical sectors.

I wrote “HardKnocks for the GreenHorn” based on my own experience of being pushed into field sales without any training or orientation. HardKnocks for the GreenHorn is an ideal starter-kit for any Medical Rep when he just joins the profession or the company. That’s the time he is most receptive to learning.

The same is true about Front-line Managers. They need to immerse in learning about their new role, which has nothing to do with what got them promoted. I wrote “SuperVision for the SuperWiser Front-line Manager” for first time managers. It is an ideal learning and development resource for any aspiring Medical Rep or newly promoted manager to prepare for his future role.

Repeat Rx is an eBook on Amazon.Com for professionals in Sales Training /HR/SFE managers and other customer-facing roles. Repeat Rx provides a 4-step development process - Calling>Connecting>Consulting>Collaborating to transform Amateurs into Achievers.
