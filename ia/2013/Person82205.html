Matt Davidson's Summary

After concluding a first career, I was fortunate to find a new one 20 years ago that offered me the chance to use my training, skills, and experience. I graduated from a seminary in 1973 that pioneered work in pastoral care and psychology of religion. I use each day the skills I developed there as well as in ten years as a pastor.
In 2010, realizing the need to grow LOGO Dynamics I started using e-mail marketing and online surveys by Constant Contact. I was so impressed with the results I took the training to become a Partner with Constant Contact. I was asked to be part of the Constant Contact Local Experts program in 2012. I am available to teach seminars on both these topics. See my blog, www.growyourbusinesswithcc.com for details.

Matt Davidson's Projects

  • Masters of Marketing

    • August 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Matt Davidson, Angela Edwards

    Today's solo entrepreneur and small business owner with up to 12-15 employees are an underserved market when it comes to marketing training. They are constantly being presented with ideas on how to market their businesses and given little advice on what really works. Masters of Marketing is an attempt to change that situation. It will feature a pyramid of commitment in which people can choose their level of interest. "Community" (little or no commitment) to "Learning Center" to "Committed Member" to "Partner" (highest level) are the levels offered. Engagement marketing, effective use of video, and creating "Wow!" moments are elements of this effort by people who are not just talking about it, but doing it. Stay tuned for more information.
