I am an allround international health management expert, certified massage therapist, dancer, instructor, writer, coach, mother, artist....well...a very blessed and creative multi-tasker!

Everybody can become a healthier and happier person.

"Live your life to the Fullest!".

Don't take your health for granted. Take an active role in managing your health.

2. Corporate health program
