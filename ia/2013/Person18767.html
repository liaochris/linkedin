Barbara Truman's Summary

Recognized innovator with a record of intellectual leadership and strategic vision working at the nexus of research and practice toward learner-centric, technology-enhanced learning required to advance sustainable attainment and success of higher education.

Curious and collaborative pioneer exploring the application of emerging media, technologies and systems in the support of human performance and intellect, individually, collaboratively, and through augmented cognition. Partnership builder expanding communication and outreach efforts across academia, industry, government, and military. Representative for national and international professional associations in pursuit of rigorous scholarship surrounding the application of learning technologies to inform policy, practice, and support of diverse learners.

Topical areas of inquiry include digital, collaborative, and creative citizenship, augmented, virtual reality, and simulation, game-based learning, intelligent and adaptive systems, visualization, mobile learning and computing, and universal design for learning.
