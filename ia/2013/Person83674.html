Lyndi Thompson's Summary

Who is Lyndi? I am your fire hose of fresh ideas, your positive attitude, “life is a daring adventure,” dreams in data online marketer.

Why Me? You don’t need a rockstar, guru or an expert; you need someone who believes in the “we are great,” team-oriented philosophy with a solid foundation in marketing. And, that’s only the beginning.

Who You Are: You take BIG ideas and empower those around you to make them into a reality. You are a leader, mentor, listener and aren't afraid to take a chance, laugh or have ice cream for lunch.

What Inspires Me: Teams excited to take on the next challenge knowing they are surrounded by talented, capable people whose potential is truly unlimited.

What Do I Do? Listen, learn, engage, and measure. I listen to customers, teams, leaders and my own gut. I learn from the best, seek out mentorship, take continuing education classes, do customer research and ask a lot of questions. Communities grow when I engage as a friendly brand voice online and in person. The land of data is one I love exploring. From landing pages to web analytics and conversion optimization, I love it all and am always hungry to learn more.

My Focus: Leveraging technology to improve the world by supporting big idea, action taking entrepreneurs. It just so happens to be that this mission is a magnet for technology start-ups.

My Experience: The full stack marketer is often the role I get thrown into due to my “can-do” attitude. However, when strategically placed, I am a compliment to teams as a leader who can strategize, manage and implement.
