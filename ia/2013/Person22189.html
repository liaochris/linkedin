Charles Newman's Summary

During my more than 40 years experience, I have refined the steps that bring people together to make alliances work and have become an internationally recognized expert on negotiations.

For Ford and my other clients, I successfully negotiated a variety of joint ventures, licensing agreements, acquisitions and divestitures around the world. In 2009, I published “Beyond the Chicken Dance” to share some of my experiences. Since then I have appeared on Detroit’s WVSN radio where I am recognized as the “go to guy when a deal is to be done.” I also have appeared on the National Entrepreneurs TV Network and I provide training seminars on the “art” of negotiation.

By the end of 2012 my second book, "Getting from You and Me to WE" will be published.

Specialties

Strategic & Business Planning, Building Business Alliances, Licensing Arrangements, Joint Ventures, Acquisitions, Divestitures
