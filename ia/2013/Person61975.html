Jennifer Sarpong's Summary

Strategist, consultant, and serial entrepreneur working to improve the lives of people worldwide.

The common thread through all of my work is my desire to deliver value that helps others achieve their maximum potential. I have an innate ability to help others cut through the clutter and produce meaningful action that gets results.

Sarpong Global Business Consulting - my firm effectively connects buyers and sellers worldwide in a way the produces win-win agreements and promotes international development (www.SarpongGlobalConsulting.com) .

Strategic Life & Business Coaching - helping aspiring entrepreneurs and young Gamechangers become successful in their professional and personal lives. I am watching first-hand the powerful transformation that takes place when people have order, accountability, a solid plan of action, and someone to encourage them to live their dream.

Global Empowerment Initiatives

I am also the founder of the top-ranked website, www.Help-My-Self-Esteem.com and author of the book Get R.E.A.L! About Low Self-Esteem. Another passion of mine is to see women and girls everywhere live out their lives from a place of worth and value. Through originally authored books, monthly newsletters, inspiring content, and live empowerment seminars, I have had the privilege to do just that. My web readership includes thousands of girls and women from the U.S. to China, from India to Egypt.
