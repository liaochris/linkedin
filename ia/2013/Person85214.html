Marco Loguercio's Summary

Marco Loguercio is a search marketing and digital marketing consultant, founder and CEO of the search marketing agency I Search.
Loguercio is recognized in Italy as a pioneer in SEO, working in this field since 1996, and as a leading expert in search marketing and digital marketing.
In 2002 Loguercio founded SEMS, a leading and award winning italian search marketing agency. Prior to found SEMS, Loguercio leaded the SEM division for important italian web and media agencies, developing and managing search marketing strategies for several top tier clients.

Loguercio is a frequent speaker at industry events around the country including Search Engine Strategies, IAB Forum, Search Marketing Forum. He has written bylined articles for numerous italian publications and is the author of the book "Le nuove vie del marketing digitale" (www.marketing-digitale.com) in 2001.
He served on the european working group of SEMPO, the Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization (www.sempo.org).

Specializzazioni: search marketing, search engine optimization, search advertising, web analytics, web marketing, online advertising, social media marketing.
