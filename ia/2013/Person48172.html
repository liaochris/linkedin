Gerard Tannam's Summary

Originally from Dublin, I've always been fascinated with brands and how they work, rest and play.

This fascination has taken me from my philosophy studies in Dublin to Asia where I worked with brands including the Mandarin Oriental Hotel, Remy Martin, Cathay Pacific, Prudential, MTV and Chanel.

More recently, on returning to Ireland, I set up Islandbridge where my clients include Topaz Energy, Maldron Hotels, Antaris Consulting, Venture Network and Aussie Ice.

I'm Brand Expert on Newstalk Radio's Kick-Start Your Business, and Mentor to Enterprise Ireland's High Potential Start-Ups (as they gear up to export around the world), and to Dublin City University Invent's Incubation Clients.

I'll publish my book I'll Buy That (A Business-Owner's Guide To Brand-Building) in 2013.

Specialties: Brand building (helping my clients become the natural choice of their customers)
