Bonnie Engel Lee, Ph.D., CCC-SLP's Summary

Speech/language pathologist in a variety of settings: private practice, outpatient clinics, hospitals, skilled nursing facilities, rehab agencies and now in an elementary school setting. While working of rehab company, I served as Administrator of a Medicare Certified Rehabilitation Agency and supervised rehab departments at multiple skilled nursing facilities in several states.

Present voiceover training includes progress series courses at Edge Studio subsequent to initial training with Such A Voice company and auditioning through Voice123 and other sties.

Specialties

Speech and voice coaching, accent reduction for native and non-native speakers of English
