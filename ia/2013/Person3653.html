Alex Amsel's Summary

Alex Amsel is an award winning video games developer, Bitcoin and cryptocurrency enthusiast, digital media consultant, and BAFTA member. An industry veteran of nearly 20 years, he has worked across many interactive platforms, utilising both internationally renowned brands and innovative concepts.

Alex's current focus is triple award winning and BAFTA nominated Eufloria, Apple's Game of 2012 Runner-Up on iPad. Eufloria is a collaboration with Rudolf Kremers and Alex May, and has sold over 500,000 copies to date. Alex is also working with stop-motion artist Anthony ‘Cletus Clay’ Flack.

He has worked with brands and titles as varied as Family Guy, Eufloria, Alien Hominid, Puzzler, and even the classic Asteroids. As well as producing over 70 video game projects, he’s also worked with TV broadcasters and production companies in fields as varied as Twitter strategy (Big Brother/Channel 4 UK) to innovative real-time animation (CBBC’s Strange Hill High/Yoshimi & Katoi). .

Alex has also spoken at and participated in a variety of events such as Develop, DocFest, GameHotel Switzerland, Zurich School for Design, Crossover, Game Republic, keynote speaker at GDC New Zealand and GDC San Francisco.
