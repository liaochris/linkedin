Bella Guzman's Summary

A customer-focused graphic designer with more than fifteen years experience in graphic design and art direction, Bella has worked with Fortune 100 clients, growing start-ups, entrepreneurs and small business owners in the entertainment, manufacturing, real estate (commercial and residential),retail, coaching/consulting, publishing, marketing and nonprofit industries.

She enjoys partnering with clients and supporting their marketing goals throughout the life of their business. Whether it’s developing or updating a company’s identity or designing a project from concept to finish, Bella’s customer-centered approach and keen business sense deliver results that ensure her clients stand out from the crowd.

Specialties:
• Dedicated to understanding and communicating the client’s vision
• Self-motivated and committed to superior work that generates results
• A passion for working with and understanding entrepreneurs
• A diverse artistic background and a comprehensive “toolbox”
• Flexible, easy-going, client-driven nature
• Works well independently or collaboratively with a team
• A love for the work and a desire to grow professionally and personally
