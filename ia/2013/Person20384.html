Back to Earth Belize

September 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Belize

Belize is a jewel of a country on the Atlantic coast of Central America. A former British colony the official language is English. The country is almost totally unspoilt, large areas still do not have paved roads. From the coast, which has the second largest barrier reef in the world to the rainforest in the western highlands there is beauty wherever you look.

I designed these retreats to combine my two passions, yoga and the natural beauty that I am surrounded by in this wonderful little country. So if you are looking for a vacation which is more than another excersize in consuming stuff, this might just be what you are looking for.

To find out more go to www.leavesplace.com and www.thelodgeatbigfalls.com/specials.php.
