Greg Sicotte's Summary

PUGET SERVICES
Since 1998 we have delivered comprehensive home maintenance solutions to residential and mid-size commercial clients. Our goal has always been to deliver a predictable level of quality in every service we offer, thus providing our customers with a safe, reliable experience — every time.

Our committed team of experts specialize in a handful of basic home maintenance needs from window cleaning and gutter cleaning, to pressure washing, custom Christmas lighting and seasonal maintenance schedules. Our extended network of pre-screened professionals round out the rest of our service offerings with everything from kitchen remodeling, painting and staining, heating and cooling, home inspections and appraisers, electrical, moving and storage and much more.

We want to make hiring quality home maintenance as easy as ordering your favorite pizza. As a fellow homeowner, I understand the biggest step in any project is always the first one – selecting your contractor. Our educational, straight-forward, no-pressure approach keeps customers coming back time after time. We’re here to help make your decision (and maintaining your home) easy, enjoyable and fun.


LINKEDIQ
I am the founding Principal at the interactive marketing agency - LinkedIQ. Motivated by the potential of technologies to facilitate innovation, social participation, and community development, I believe that the present online social media revolution is reminiscent of the Industrial Revolution, where attention has become the precious commodity. My portfolio consistently models and promotes the effective use of web-based tools and methods to advance community development and online engagement. From complex multi-user website design and deployment to technical optimization, analytics reporting and growth management strategy, I have established an authority as someone who leverages clever solutions and can deliver complex business ideas to their market.
