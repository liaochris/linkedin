Tired of knowing all the answers for your clients but not yourself?

Not sure if you're at the right firm, in the right job, or realizing your potential?

Questioning whether you made the right choice to become a lawyer?

Struggling to manage your job stress while balancing family life?

I can help.

I'm Dr. Leah Jackman-Wheitner, an executive coach and consultant who specializes in helping lawyers find answers and greater balance. After 10 years of clinical practice as a Ph.D. psychologist, I began my coaching business 11 years ago.

My clients...

Gain insight into what they really want to be doing, why they became lawyers, and how they can regain control of their careers so they can serve the clients that inspired them to become lawyers in the first place.
Create stronger family bonds, enjoy more time with their family and friends, and feel that they are back in control while learning to maintain balance.
Reduce their stress levels and improve overall health by removing the root causes or shifting their perspective, resulting in more insight into their old patterns and an improved approach to repeated situations.
Increase their productivity while become more effective leaders and performing at the top of their game.

My website http://happierlawyer.com has more information. All consultations are done by phone, email, or Skype and are always completely confidential.

If you’re interested in finding out more about my services or if you would like to schedule a free introductory call, please contact me. I would be happy to see if I can be of assistance.
