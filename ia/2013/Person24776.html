Chuck Castle's Experience

Asst. Vice President - Key Accounts

Tap Packaging Solutions

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Packaging and Containers industry

July 1983 – Present (30 years 2 months)

Typically I work with companies who are frustrated that their packaging is not helping their profit margins as much as it should. They work with me as they’re not happy with their current packaging, and feel they are losing market share or profits because of it. They also like the design and construction ideas that TAP has to offer.

Another area they are concerned with is the time they waste on keeping up with inventory of their packaging. They want help with this so they can spend more time working on their core business, and not dealing with the hassles of keeping up with their inventories.

Customers I work with come from photo, confectionary, retail packaging, and many other industries. Photo packaging, folding cartons, and rigid set up boxes are some of the items common to these companies.
