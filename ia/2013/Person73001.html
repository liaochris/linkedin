Kathy Wheeler's Summary

A passion for making other people's authorship dreams come true. Eighty percent of people want to write a book. Very few of them accomplish this goal. Some just don't know where to start and others just didn't finish it for whatever reason. Kathy consults with people to help them accomplish their book writing goals including self-publishing and book marketing if they desire.

As Editor-in-Chief of Book Writing Magazine she know the market and how to coach you through it.

Do you want to have a book of your own, but don't want the hassle of writing it? She can also ghostwrite and publish for professionals that want to position themselves as an expert in their field.

With her passion for helping others and writing good copy, Kathy also provides copy writing services to small and medium size businesses. Visit www.wheelerwriting.com.
