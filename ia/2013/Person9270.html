April Finnen's Summary

Building communication strategies from the ground and laying the tactical foundation is my specialty. I’ve done this successfully for three companies, including a biotech. I thrive on the challenge of doing things the company has never tried, and producing results beyond the expected. Researching projects, sifting through large amounts of data and figuring out what it means—and how we can use the information to grow our business—is one of the things I’m best at.

One of my favorite professional activities is curating strategic digital marketing content. It’s the best way I’ve found to put a personal face on what could easily be dry business-to-business content, and connect with prospective clients, partners and employees in a no-pressure environment ... and it generates results. DynPort’s email newsletter maintained click-through rates 23.8% above the industry average for two years, and we grew opt-in subscribers by more than 31% in one year. More importantly, these connections led to business partnerships and enhanced recruiting efforts--two of the strategic business goals behind the content marketing strategy.

On a personal level, I am interested in food and nutrition, particularly healthy eating and community-supported agriculture (CSA). I have written content and produced weekly newsletters for the largest CSA in Frederick County, Maryland for three years, totaling more than 70 editions. In 2010, I also began managing the farm’s newsletter distribution list, and redesigned the format using MailChimp.

I am available for contract/project work in: science/health communications, digital marketing, public relations, social media, business writing, website management, content management, editing, trade shows, community outreach, internal communication, management, strategic planning

Industries: biotech / biopharmaceutical, science, health, technology, non-profit, food / agriculture
