Robert Coppinger's Summary

I have worked in the real estate and financial services industries for the past 12 years. I have worked for large banking organizations in the northeast in the commercial sector extending credit for real estate projects.

I have been professionally credit trained by JPMorgan Chase Bank for Commercial Lending.

After leaving the banking sector, I founded and successfully transitioned out of a successful commercial mortgage brokerage shop, and founded a Private Lending Fund (Hard Money) - W.A.D. Capital, LLC. I also was partners in a real estate development and finance company, Reliance Realty Partners, which is based out of Stamford CT.

In addition to my real estate initiatives, I have successfully co-founded a legal services business, and a distressed asset acquisition fund.

I am currently Managing Member of The W.A.D. Group LLC, a full services alternative lender. We specialize in hard money real estate lending, plaintiff funding against pending lawsuits, and alternative business financing solutions.

I look forward to speaking with anyone regarding their projects financing need, or their interest in putting capital to work in one of our development projects or business plans.
