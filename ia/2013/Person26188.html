David Connor's Summary

My personal goal has been to push CSR / Sustainability practice beyond its current niche to mainstream businesses across all sectors and sizes.

Recently we have been exploring redesigning Coethica to act as values driven platform for inspiring, educating, supporting and promoting better business, especially at smaller businesses.

Specialties: CSR / social enterprise / campaigning / strategy development and implementation / relationship management / stakeholder engagement / communications / training.
