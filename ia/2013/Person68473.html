Jo Saunders' Summary

Are you an experienced professional, who is Perth's best kept secret online? If you are not being found by your prospects and contacted by having a consistent, complete online presence, I can help. I am a social media marketing consultant who loves to help clients leverage the power of LinkedIn, other social media platforms and email marketing to get you fired up and found.

My mission is to help business owners and professionals connect and communicate by leveraging LinkedIn as part of their social media strategy. It is the ultimate positioning tool to showcase your expertise and experience.

As Director of Wildfire Social Marketing, I bring over 15 years’ experience in event management and marketing to the business. I specialise in online marketing strategy, training and support, and offer group training, 1-1 coaching and webinars in LinkedIn and online marketing tools, especially for professionals over 40, small to medium businesses and organisations in Perth, Western Australia and beyond.

My first business came from ‘old school’ social media (letter writing). I set up Correspondamania Penpal Club, in the early 90s to help people around the world connect which I ran for 7 years. Then in 2004 I set up a design and marketing business which became Wildfire Social Marketing in 2011.

Before getting started with any social media, know what you want to achieve, who your target market is and what their problems are. Then we can look at creating the right online marketing strategy with the right tools, in order to grow your business.
