Claudia Loens' Summary

Claudia Loens is a Writer! As the founder and Chief Wordflirt Officer at Wordflirt, a Social Media Content and Writing company, she works with businesses to write and produce content to grow their business. Wordflirt provides consultation, strategy and professional writing services.

Wordflirt also designs and writes blogs and Wordpress websites. Examples can be found at her website at www.wordflirtsocialmedia.com/websiteportfolio.

Claudia is a published author of romance and nonfiction. Romance novel, "Believe in Love" is now available on Amazon. In March of 2013, Claudia published "How to Become a Social Media Content Machine - Wordflirt's Guide to Cranking out Killer Content to Grow Your Business". And in October 2013, she released "How to Become a Social Media Content Machine for REAL ESTATE" Available on Amazon.com.

Personally, Claudia is a mother of four daughters, an avid reader, traveler and beach bum. She enjoys experimenting with new cooking recipes and will someday live in Maui!

Specialties: Web Design, Blogging, Ghost Writing, Social Recruiting Strategy, Social Media Marketing, strategy, consultation, blog writing, Mobile Marketing, professional writing, Social Recruiting Content, Social Media Set-up and Maintenance, Marketing, business writing, newsletters, Wordpress and social media content
