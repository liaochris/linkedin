Cathy Sletten's Summary

Freelance graphic designer / production artist / photo retoucher

Creative concepting and production with a passion for good design, clean production, and a talent for organization and solving complex problems in fast-paced environments. Experienced with all facets of print campaigns from initial client consultation; preparing cost estimates; design, production (Adobe Creative Suite 6) and presenting concepts; to project management and working with vendors.

Specialties:
