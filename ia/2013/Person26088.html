Roberto Cobianchi's Summary

I work to bring Internet technologies inside organizations, both to improve internal process management, internal relations and communications, both to develop a different approach to market and brand online presence.
I design and coordinate the implementation of corporate intranets and build online strategies for companies.
In the previous I worked for 10 years in the software, initially as a programmer for applications in banking sector, then as an analyst and products manager. Then another 3 years of experience in organizational consulting in Quality Systems, Privacy and Business Process Rengineering to land to the design of corporate intranets, as the strategic use of new technologies for greater efficiency of internal processes and organizational improvement

Specialties: Foursquare e GeoLocal Marketing, Web Strategy, Social Media, Intranet Design and Management, Process Reingeneering
