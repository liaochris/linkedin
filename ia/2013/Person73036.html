Kathleen Vaughan's Summary

A fifth generation native of Scranton, Pennsylvania and graduate of Mercyhurst College in Erie. I followed my great uncles west and became the first of my family to settle in the great state of Idaho. I found employment with the Hewlett-Packard Company in Boise, Idaho. There I worked in the laser printer business where I designed service solutions for international multi use customers.
A friend introduced me to Garden Valley and it was love at first site. I knew I had to make this wonderful place my home. I simply could not resist the majestic views, the outdoor life style and the friendly atmosphere. The proximity to the city made it possible for me to continue working at Hewlett-Packard.
The more I fell in love with Garden Valley the more I realized I needed to participate actively in my new home town. I started a property management business, renting cabins to vacationers and maintaining vacant homes for the part time residents. With my broad knowledge and love of the area, experience in property management and services background it seemed obvious that real estate would be a good fit for me. Leaving my job in the city was a risk that has paid off in many ways. I have earned an Associate Broker license and a GRI designation. This year I plan to add additional education and designations to my list of credentials. Over the years I have been privileged to participate in the Garden Valley Hospital Board, the Chamber of Commerce, the Crouch Ambulance Auxiliary and the Ican mentoring Program. Most importantly I have enjoyed helping others make their dreams come true as I have done for myself.
To learn more about Garden Valley, Idaho visit me at www.gardenvalleycurrents.com.
