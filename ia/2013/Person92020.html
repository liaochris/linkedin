Mike Altieri's Summary

As a child Mike studied Acting at Frohman Academy in Carmel, California. He continued his 22 years of training as an Actor in Los Angeles with professional scene study and improvisation instruction.

Mike also studied Producing at the American Film Institute. After finishing at AFI Mike went on to star in his own independent films. In 1999 he starred in the short film "On Duty" which won Best Short Film at The New York International Film Festival in 2000. He went on to star in the Toronto Student Short Film Festival's Official Selection, "Til Undeath Do Us Part" in 2006. In 2007 Mike starred in "Revolver", Official Selection at the SoCal Indy Film Festival in Orange County, and Official Selection at Festivus Film Festival in Denver, CO.

Currently, Mike is in development attached to star in both features AMERICAN RUNAWAY and THE TROPHY CASE trilogy.
