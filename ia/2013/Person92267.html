Michael D. Haberman, SPHR's Summary

Experienced HR consultant, advisor, speaker and instructor. I work with primarily small to mid-size organizations on all HR issues. My main focus is consulting in employment compliance and supervisory training. I also help smaller businesses understand the importance of the alignment of HR with the overall strategic objective of the organization. I have been teaching the SHRM certification preparation course for approximately 15 years. I also teach HR Fundamentals. Additionally I author a blog, HR Observations, which can be found at our company website. I also write for Blogging4Jobs and several other blogs.
Specialties: Consulting in employment regulations compliance, FLSA, employee handbooks, employee relations, performance, labor. wage and hour issues, social media use in human resources, executive advisement.
