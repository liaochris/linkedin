Charles (Chuck) Moyer's Summary

Chuck is the founder and president of Fat Margins, a company that helps businesses get their customers to buy more, pay more, and refer more.

He is the author of Profit Margin Magic, a comprehensive course that teaches business owners how to substantially increase their companies' profit margins.

He has advised business owners for nearly 40 years and routinely speaks to leading business groups throughout the United States. Prior to founding Fat Margins, Chuck spent 20 years running his own CPA practice, developing expertise in a range of industries, including automotive parts, aircraft dealerships, manufacturers, multi-partner professional service firms, and nationally distributed wineries.

He is the creator of Profit Margin Magic, a self-study course that helps business owners increase their companies' profit margins using a three part process; targeting the right customers, developing products and services that defy comparison, and implementing more effective pricing skills.

Chuck has successfully negotiated business acquisitions and raised both bank and private-placement funding for clients. An adjunct college professor, he has a solid academic foundation that supports his broad-based experience.

Chuck holds a Professional Pricing Certificate from the Professional Pricing Society and a Certificate in Personal Financial Planning Certificate, with distinction, from the University of California, Berkeley
