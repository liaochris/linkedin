JL Jordan III's Summary

Previously, I served as COO of Nicole ND Durr’s (ND) network of companies & projects, which include Havana Nights Show, & ND’s Fuego Nightclub at Harrah’s Rio Hotel & Casino.

During my time with the company, I negotiated several four-wall deals for ND at several casino/hotel properties in LV. My work with ND also encompassed assisting with company television project involvements.

Prior to accepting my post with ND, I spent several years honing my extensive marketing, pr & operational skills for companies in myriad industries, such as the Nevada Test Site Historical Foundation & the Atomic Testing Museum; IDC, a philanthropic fundraising firm headquartered in Las Vegas, Left Right Marketing Technology's (LRMK), CrazyGrazer.com, Hall Communications, & Carepanion/CareTouch, Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Kaiser Foundation Hospitals. Further, I worked to develop brands for a diverse line of products and companies including GM, Prudential, Michelin, BC& B, Hershey, Ambi & Volkswagen.

I also provide independent marketing, operations, pr, & entertainment consulting. Former satisfied clients include Krave Nightclub in Las Vegas; Alternative Wellness Solutions in Chicago, Illinois; 1-2-1 Philanthropy Services in Henderson, Nevada; & SFS Entertainment & Back East Bar & Grill in Las Vegas, Nevada.

I studied Industrial Marketing & Management at Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, PA. I hold a Certificate of Leadership Training from the University of Notre Dame & have National Certification in Mediation from The Pittsburgh Mediation Center in Pennsylvania.

Community service work includes turns as past Chair of the Public Service Committee & Executive Director of the Greater Las Vegas Advertising Federation. I also served on the Board of Directors of Family Support Services in the Bay Area.

I was a featured celebrity/gossip columnist in the highly successful monthly publication QVegas Magazine & on the web site, RoxanneMagazine.com.
