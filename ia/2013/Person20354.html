Carol Hayse's Summary

Ron's Carpets has been a full service floor covering store for 21 years. We offer the latest in carpet, ceramic, hardwood, & resilient flooring. We have a friendly, professional service staff with emphasis on SERVICE. We can bid on any job Commercial or Residential to your specifications, and because of our knowledge of materials and applications, we can also assist you with preparing a finish schedule and specifications for bid. Over the years, our flexibility in meeting even the tightest schedules has helped us to build an impressive list of clients.
Our mission at Ron's Carpets is to provide products and services which meet our customers needs and expectations.
Our goal is total customer satisfaction!

Specialties: Ron's Carpets became Certified as a Women's Business Enterprise with WBENC in 2007. Ron's Carpets works directly with contractors and architects to specify products for the Builder/Residential, Commercial, and Hospitality market segment.
