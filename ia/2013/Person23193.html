Owned a Commercial Finance Company.

Owned a Manufacturing Plant.

Previously on the Boards of TMA, ACG, Angel Investment Forum, Palm Beach Resource Center and Children & Family Connection of Palm Beach | http://cfcpbc.org

Specialties:

Bankers Advocate specializes in designing successful Exit Strategies for Entrepreneurs. The Exit could be via a financial or strategic buyer we find or a controlled liquidity event/transfer to key employees or next generation. We also have a very active Targeted Search program were our Individual, Corporate and Private Equity Clients entrust us to find them businesses to purchase in the "Hidden Market". www.bankersadvocate.com

Paragon Financial provides working capital and cash flow to non and under bankable companies through Invoice Factoring, Purchase Order Funding and Vendor Guarantees. Our typical client has sales between $500,000 to $20,000,000 per annum. www.paragonfinancial.net
