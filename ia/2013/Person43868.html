Fabien Catteau's Summary

Software developer, entrepreneur, trainer, podcaster, technology enthusiast.

I run a small consultancy company called Mingalar. This is Julien and me (so far), and that is because we share the same values: master the technology, learn and teach. It's also about taking the time to understand our customers by learning their domain and their business. That's the only way we can serve well!

I offer development in Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Javascript and other web technologies. I've got a solid background in Ruby language, Linux systems administration and video processing (and know how to plug these things together, by the way).

I like to share experiences with others. I host a French speaking podcast called "Parlons Ruby" and I do all my best to promote Ruby conferences and meetups in France.
