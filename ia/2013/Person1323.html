Adam Cohen's Summary

I am a Partner in Fleishman-Hillard's Boston office and SVP of Digital and Social Media. In August, 2012, I took on a leadership role driving social media services in the East Region of FH. I have more than 17 years of experience leveraging technology to drive and sustain business value and have worked with Fortune 1000 companies across the spectrum of marketing, social media, eCommerce and technology and strategic consulting. Currently I am leading the social media strategy for AT&T's Business Solutions organization, and in the East Region of Fleishman I lead a talented FH digital team of community managers, digital strategists, social analysts and content producers.

Previously I was a partner in Rosetta’s Consumer Product and Retail practice, as an account executive leading transformational interactive marketing and eCommerce efforts for clients such as Coach, philosophy, Maidenform, Church & Dwight, Citizen's Bank and others. I also served as a digital strategist focused on integrating social media to impact and inform all of the component parts of the marketing mix for Rosetta's clients, building programs that treat social media tactics as informed strategies leveraging deeper understanding of the brand’s most valuable customers and prospects through Rosetta's Personality®-based segmentation. I founded Rosetta's Social Media practice in 2008 and have spoken on behalf of the agency at industry events with partners such as BazaarVoice and IBM.

I have previously served on Forrester Research’s Technology Marketing Executive Council, and have contributed articles on marketing and social technologies to PR Week, AdAge, PharmaVoice and other industry journals.

Prior to Rosetta I spent nearly twelve years at Accenture leading transformational technology programs for clients including Boston Scientific, Kodak and EMC.

I like to share thoughts and discuss the evolution of social media and interactive marketing at adamhcohen.com and on Twitter @adamcohen.
