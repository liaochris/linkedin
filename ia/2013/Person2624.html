Aimee Edlund Maescher's Experience

Owner/Principal

Nisse Designs

October 2002 – Present (11 years 2 months) Norwell, MA

As an experienced marketing professional since 1989, Aimee has extensive experience in the brand building process and graphic design needs for her clients. Design experience includes producing an array of brand identities and complementing print and electronic collateral including brochures and annual reports, traditional identity collateral, newsletters, client alerts, e-mail marketing campaigns, event planning materials as well as other unique promotional pieces. With a clear understanding of clients' business goals and target audiences, Nisse Designs specializes in designing the critical "start up" or next generation (brand/image) promotional package. Designs range from the unusual and innovative to the more conservative and classic - all "appropriately differentiating" - and created with the clients' cultures, business goals, industries and budgets in mind.
