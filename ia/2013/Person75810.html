Kim Beckers' Summary

Successful business creator and owner with extensive experience in overcoming personal stumbling blocks. Kim has over a decade of experience in online business creation and brings a unique combination of experience and insight to the job, with systems, marketing skills and a deep understanding of the mechanics of business on and offline. She specializes in helping you identify your issues that are hindering your business.

She has more than 6 years of experience in online business management as well as certification as an Online Business Manager (OBM) through the International Association of Online Business Managers (IAOBM) where she closely worked with Tina Forsyth as her mentor/coach.

She started out as an employee of family owned accounting firm when she was 15, and kept the books for that business and others through college. In college, she turned a sharp eye for detail and a keen business sense toward math and computer science, graduating in the top of my class and taking my first position with a web firm as both their bookkeeper and their web support. In this position, she expanded her role to include setting up websites, design and marketing for the firm.

Eventually she brought these talents to bear on her own online, plus-size lingerie shop, My Plus Lingerie, which she designed and promoted from the ground up before selling it as a fully functional business and, based on the success of that website and similar endeavors, moving my focus onto consulting and now as a transformational success coach.

Kim sees her own success as being tied directly to her clients’ level of success.

She specializes in helping you identify your issues that are hindering your business.
