Jon Brennan's Summary

For over 10 years, Jon has been writing music and designing sound for film, advertising and live theater.

Jon has an MFA in Music Composition for the Screen from Columbia College in Chicago. At Columbia Jon studied with Disney Music Supervisor Andy Hill, and veteran film composers, Gary Chang, Hummie Mann and David McHugh.

Prior to graduate school, Jon managed all audio services at Curtis Inc., a production powerhouse in Cincinnati.

Jon’s music has been featured in award winning independent films and animations at Film Festivals from Los Angeles to Zlin, Czech Republic. He has written music for national brands including, GE Aviation, Mentos, Airheads, ConAgra, Pitney Bowes, NCR, AON and AirIT.

Jon resides in Cincinnati Ohio with his wife Katy and daughter Evelyn. As an Adjunct Professor Jon enjoys sharing his passion for audio and music with his students at Northern Kentucky University.
