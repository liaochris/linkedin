James Qualtrough's Summary

I've worked in marketing since leaving university and have a real passion for internet marketing. Always been a sucker for gadgets and technology so I guess this was always going to be inevitable. Since first attending the Search Engine Strategies conference in 2006 I have been blown away by the opportunities online and am now on a life-long learning curve to make the most out of this channel for my own projects and for clients.

I've been blogging since 2007 and run a growing online news website focusing on the Isle of Man, Jersey and Guernsey. As an extension of my online interest I've built a number of websites for both personal projects and for clients.

In terms of goals and aspirations I'm always looking for the opportunity to do something better than I've ever done before and my recent move to work at Home Strategic will certainly offer me this challenge.
