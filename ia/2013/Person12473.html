Beatrice Schultz's Summary

Principal for a financial planning business which offers independent personal financial analysis, planning and advice to families and small businesses.

Licensed College Planning Relief specialist. The cost of a college education can be one of the largest wealth leaks in a parent's lifetime. We educate parents on how to be highly efficient with their money as they enter the most expensive time period of their lives, sending a child to college.

Through the use of detailed financial modeling we optimize financial strategies for college spending, retirement saving, retirement spending, personal and small business self-financing, and investment portfolio management.
