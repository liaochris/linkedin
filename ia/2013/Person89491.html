Matt Mulder's Summary

As a creative professional, I bring more than 17 years of experience to an art department and provides creative, strategic solutions to the publishing business.

Specialties: Highly qualified print design expert. Experienced, project manager and problem-solver who enjoys getting things done and creating sustainable revenue streams.
