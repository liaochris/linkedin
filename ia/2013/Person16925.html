Brent Irvine's Summary

My passion is helping SMEs put MORE money back in THEIR pockets by unlocking cash trapped inside the business.

Typically I find up to 10% of annual revenue is locked up in inefficient systems (high debtors, low stock turn, high expenses etc.). We refer to this trapped cash as BI dollars (that’s BI for Business Improvement) and I love to see business owners/CEO’s smile when they see how much money is sitting on the table.

I focus my energy on the major but forgotten and neglected sector that most grows the economy.

Micro companies have political clout because there are lots of them and they’re vocal. And the large companies have enough resources to look after themselves.

The neglected sector are those caught in the middle – mid-sized businesses (“M”) or those aspiring to become one. Typically their annual revenue has broken the $1 million milestone and they have long term growth aspirations. They face challenges similar to larger businesses, but without the same resources. Whilst their need for assistance is largely ignored by politicians.

With just minimal support and some focused expertise everyone has the potential to thrive, as they are mature businesses just waiting for their growth to be unlocked. The missing ingredients are the ideas and support to go to the next level, to become the champions of the future.

As part of my commitment to aspiring M Businesses, I have helped transform Attaché from a software company to a business improvement thought leader. I have helped develop the tools and the delivery mechanisms to convert business improvement tactics into action. , all at no cost to the M Business.
