Ash Singh's Summary

Ash has accumulated over 15 years of diverse experience in Sales, Marketing, Business Development, Business Model Innovation and General Management. He has led organizations through turn-around, start-up and high growth situations. His success in the field was noted by Business Week in 2009 as one of "Asia's Top Young Entrepreneurs" and reiterated by the Economic Times in 2012 for having "built a solid reputation as one of the most successful serial entrepreneurs in Southeast Asia"

Over the past 15 years, Ash has focused on creating and bringing to market, applications and services in the mobile, trans-media, digital and Internet spaces.

While still a student at Chinese University in Hong Kong, he conceived and launched SNBS, a corporate social networking company which was acquired when he was just 22 years old.

In his next role, Ash served as the Chief Technology Officer for At-Life, which created Singapore’s largest customer loyalty rewards program with 500,000 members, for McDonald's in all of their 125 restaurants.

Ash is the founder Interactive SG - Asia's leading digital media and new business accelerator based in Singapore. Interactive.SG has already collaborated on new media projects with established companies from such diverse industries as entertainment, technology, real estate, and education.

His project "The Angel's Gate" is Asia's first 360 TV platform for entrepreneurs that was aired prime-time on MediaCorp's Channel NewsAsia and YouTube.

Singh is also committed to nurturing the next generation of entrepreneurs. He is a speaker and honorary judge for business venture competitions at the INSEAD, SMU, NUS, NTU, Synergy Business School (Russia) and his alma mater, The Ivey Business School.

As a basketball fan, he is a co-owner and "super fan" of the Singapore Slingers professional basketball team.

In his free time, Ash promotes his favourite hobby, turbans, through his mobile app "Turbanizer" which is on track for 1MM downloads this year.
