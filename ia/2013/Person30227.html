Danny DeMichele's Summary

I have been building and managing online businesses for the last 17 years. In 2010, my company eVisibility.com was Inc Magazine's 169th fastest growing company in the US. I sold the company in late 2010 to LSF Network, Inc., a top 5 private digital agency.

Incubate.com was founded in early 2011 as a different spin on the digital agency experience. Transparency is our cornerstone. Most of our staff or senior level entrepreneurs. Why does this matter? We market for companies from a business-owners mindset, not an employee mentality. We have 30 staff members in our Carlsbad office and specialize in Usability/CRO, SEO, PPC, Social Media and Magento build-outs.
