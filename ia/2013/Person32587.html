David Garland's Summary

Hi! I’m David Siteman Garland. Here are 3 fun facts about me:

1. I’m the creator/host of one of the Internet’s most popular, successful and longest running web shows, blog & resources The Rise To The Top: THE #1 Badass Show, Resource & Community For Mediapreneurs. To date, The Rise To The Top web TV show & podcast has been viewed over 6,000,000 times in over 100 countries. The Rise To The Top is all about helping aspiring/rising mediapreneurs (peeps who create online media, experts, authors, thought leaders, personal brands, coaches, Internet marketers/information marketers) grow their biz like a weed & dominate online.

2. I’m super passionate about helping mediapreneurs generate revenue (the bling bling…the right way!), build their online platform & grow their audience like a weed through my products and programs including:

Create Awesome Online Courses: The proven step-by-step system for creating, promoting & profiting from your own online course.

Create Awesome Interviews the #1 program to help folks create their own interview-based web TV show with hundreds of graduates creating shows on everything from inspired travel to car racing to healthy living

3. To date, I’ve had conversations with well over 300 diverse, awesome people on the show with a special focus on interviewing experts, thought leaders and & personal brands about how they build their biz. Some of my favorites include The Millionaire Matchmaker Patti Stanger, Two-time New York Times Best Selling Author Tim Ferriss and creator of Wine Library TV Gary Vaynerchuk.

4. If I had to choose my very favorite thing in the world (next to his wife/family/friends/recreational sports), it would be rocking the camera. I’ve been lucky enough to be involved in a variety of cool on camera roles besides my own show including a correspondent for Great Day St. Louis, on air personality for Google, and a variety of other projects including emceeing one of the world’s largest business conferences for women.
