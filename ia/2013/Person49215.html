Joe Palmer's Experience

Sole Owner & General Manager

Green Light Safety (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Oil & Energy industry

April 2012 – Present (1 year 6 months) Houston, Texas Area

Formed and Initiated Company for the purpose of Gas Detection Services and Rentals. We provide Safety Equipment Sales to include Calibration Gas, Gas Detection (Portable and Fixed), Detector Tubes, Spare Parts, and a variety of Industrial Safety Equipment on a needed basis. We also perform other Safety Services as a convenience for existing customers.

We perform OnSite Calibration and Preventative Maintenance saving you downtime and shipping costs. We also execute Marine Vessel OnBoard Fixed and Portable Gas Detection Annual Inspections & Calibrations as well as Expedited Repairs as needed.We offer a wide variety of Rental Products including Confined Space Meters, Airline Breather Boxes, PIDs (VOC Meters), Inert Monitoring (Cannonball 3), and Exotic Toxic Monitors (Chlorine, Ammonia, etc).

Our Customers range from Shipyards, Chemical Plants, TankWash and Repair Facilities, Marine Chemists, and Marine Vessel Agencies...even a few Safety Companies.

We would love to include you in our list of Satisfied Customers...We Give Safety a Green Light!
