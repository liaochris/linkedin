Catherine Ulrey's Summary

Licensed real estate broker serving the residential community since 1997 in Salem, Silverton, Aumsville, Turner, Stayton, Jefferson, Keizer, Dallas and surrounding areas. My goals are to listen to the needs of my clients, offer multiple solutions to their concerns, find the right home for them while making sure they don't overpay. In 2008 I spent a year developing my website, HomesWithHorseSense.com to serve the needs of horse property buyers and sellers. It has been the most rewarding venture yet!

Specialties

Assist buyers and sellers of residential, small acreage and equestrian property in the valley area. Horse property is my specialty!
