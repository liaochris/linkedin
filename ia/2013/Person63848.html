Jim Edholm's Summary

In addition to owning BBI Benefits I am also the Benefits Columnist for Mass Lawyers Weekly. They, like my customers, trust my advice about group health, dental, life and disability plans because I have more than 30 years in the benefits business and have proven to them -- by way of results - that I can:

* find creative design solutions to their (and your) group health plans cost problems.

* design Dental plans that meet employee needs

* implement and design Group Long and Short Term Disability plans cost effectively

* even help them find International Medical plans (benefits for overseas employees)

What's more, I and my associates at BBI constantly work to find new ways to help the smaller company, particularly those with fewer than 100 employees, deal with the fact that, while they're held to the same Federal and State regulations as their larger competitors, they lack the HR muscle to do everything they want to.

Most recently we have introduced an HR Portal that help put these smaller companies on a competitive footing with the giants that they compete against-- and through BBI, it's FREE to every client who needs it.

As regards plan design, unlike many brokers, we at BBI go beyond expectations to find the solution that's perfect for any given client.

As the VP Operating Policy of Eaton Apothecary said, "(Jim) was able to learn our company, objectively present a myriad of possibilities and execute our selections to a superior level of performance... all in the span of seven weeks. ... our experience of working with him and his team, as well as saving $130,000, has been worth every second, worth dropping our long-time health insurer and worth changing brokers."

Reach us at LinkedInInfo@bbibenefits.com to get your free copy of "Three Strategies Smart Businesses Use to Control Health Care Costs." See how you can benefit, too.
