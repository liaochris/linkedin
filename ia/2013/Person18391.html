Bruno Taylor's Summary

Bruno is a designer and social entrepreneur with a strong background in service design, user insights and social innovation. A passionate and creative individual with visionary ideas and the discipline and experience to implement them. He brings a user-centred design approach when taking new services and products to market.

Drawn to a start-up culture and can-do attitude he is currently dedicated to tackling youth unemployment with digital technology as the founder and director of Flip Yourself - a social venture building groundbreaking software for young people, educators and youth organisations.

Prior to this Bruno co-founded and led commonground - a design consultancy working with public and third sector organisations to develop services that transform people's lives. He won industry challenges and led design teams working with a range of public and third sector organisations.

Bruno has extensive industry experience spanning a decade and covering a broad range of disciplines including service design, design research, product and exhibition design. He has taught at a range of business and design universities including Central Saint Martins, Brunel University, Warwick Business School and Laurea University, Finland.

Bruno believes a deep level of empathy is at the core of every good designer and has an overwhelming drive to make things work.
