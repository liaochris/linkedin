Eric Barron's Summary

Eric is the CEO of Keller Williams NYC and is responsible for overseeing the Manhattan Brokerage. Keller Williams Realty International (KWRI) is the largest residential brokerage in the United States with more than 700 offices and 80,000 agents in North America. Since launching in Manhattan in 2011, Keller Williams NYC has grown to more than 275 agents, with plans to triple in size over the next few years.

Eric is the true definition of a serial entrepreneur. He’s owned and operated mortgage companies, trained thousands of real estate professionals, consulted for some of Manhattan’s biggest real estate firms, delivered professional development seminars to dozens of universities across the US focused on Leadership, Networking, and Personal Branding, and is now responsible for expanding North America’s number one real estate firm throughout Manhattan.

"With Eric's vast experience as a real estate educator, we believe he is the ideal person to lead Keller Williams NYC, which is built on the Keller Williams coaching and training model that supports over 80,000 agents," states Chris Heller, president of Keller Williams’ International Division.

Eric is a native New Yorker who graduated from Washington University in St. Louis. He’s married with two wonderful children, Jacqueline and Zachary.

Specialties: Personal Branding, Communication & People Skills, Motivation & Humor, Real Estate, Mortgage Industry, Generation Y, DiSC Behavioral Styles, Coaching and Training
