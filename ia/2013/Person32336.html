David Niles White's Summary

I've been a producer and production executive for over 20 years. I've produced the computer animated television series "South Park" for Comedy Central as well as being vice president of digital animation for Film Roman, the company that produces "The Simpsons" and "King of the HIll". I enjoy the tough challenges of merging art and science - images and technology.

I recently completed work at lynda.com where I produced 23 documentary films for their "Creative Inspirations" series (lynda.com/inspiration). It was a great gig working with a very talented team. And, maintaining my dual life, I'm a licensed EMT working as an Emergency Trauma Technician at the UCLA Emergency Medicine Center. Never a dull moment.

Specialties: documentary production, computer animation, visual effects, digital post production, live action production, and commercial production.
