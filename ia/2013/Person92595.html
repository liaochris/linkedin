Michael Kleiner's Summary

Just by our name -- Michael Kleiner Public Relations and Web Site Design -- you can see how we believe public relations and web sites are integrated and integral parts of your business. We provide one-stop shopping, keeping your professional image and brand identity consistent.. We work primarily with authors, small businesses and excel in milestone and special events publicity with an excellent record in media placement. Michael Kleiner is an award-winning author himself, of a memoir, Beyond the Cold: An American's Warm Portrait of Norway, so he brings his experience and reputation in PR and being an author to the table.

We offer blogs to e-mail marketing to e-commerce options to web sites formatted for mobile devices and more. And all priced not to break your budget. Public relations and promotions are not a cookie-cutter field. It takes creativity, imagination and thinking outside the box to know what angle will interest which reporters, whom to target, and what cross-promotions can be utilized. Research and marketing plans are utilized. We strive to live up to our tagline: "Making the Unknown Known."
