Matthew McCauley's Summary

Member

*Actos ESI Committee Member - responsible for Analytical Search Protocol

*Member of Science & Discovery Commitees for Fosamax (Alendronate Sodium) Products Liability Litigation MDL No. 2243

* Plaintiffs Steering Committee and Co-Chair Science & Expert Sub-Committee for In re: Levaquin Mass Tort Litigation, Case No. 286, Superior Court of New Jersey, Law Division: Atlantic County

* Science and Discovery Sub-Committees for Yasmin & Yaz (Drospirenone) Marketing, Sales Practices & Products Liability MDL No. 2100

* Plaintiffs Steering Committee Gadolinium Based Contrast Agents Product Liability Agents Multidistrict Litigation No. 1909

* Discovery Sub-Committee for In Re Bayer Corp. Combination Aspirin Products Marketing Sales Practices Litigations 09 MD 2023 (USDC-EDNY).

Prior to joining PWA, I completed a clerkship in the United States Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit. After leaving the Second Circuit I defended manufacturers of pharmaceuticals and medical devices for next seven years. I am also currently a Prosecutor for the Town of Mamaroneck and have been a NYS Paramedic for more than 20 years. Prior to practicing law, I was a New York City Police Officer and during law school I was selected for an externship with the Terrorism Prosecution Division of United States Attorney's Office for the Southern District of New York.

Education: Iona College (B.S.); Pace University School of Law (J.D.); New York City Police Academy; NYS Paramedic.

Reported Cases: Rudenko v. Costello, 322 F. 3d 168 (2d Cir 2003); Estate of LaMontagne v. Bristol-Myers Squibb, 127 Wash.App. 335, 111 P.3d 857 (Wash.App. Div.1 Apr 04, 2005).

Specialties

Practice Areas: Trials, Mass Torts, Defective Drug/Pharmaceutical Liability, Defective Medical Devices, Toxic Torts, Products Liability, Class Actions, Complex Litigation and Electronic Discovery.
