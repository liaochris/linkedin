Harry Snedden's Summary

President of ScoreCrafters,LLC. Harry specializes in Credit Bureau Correction,Credit Consulting and Education. Harry has over 17 years experience as a Corporate Trainer and Speaker in the Finance/ Credit Bureau Consulting arena and is an expert in the credit industry. He has performed hundreds of personal credit consultations and has educated thousands of consumers.

ScoreCrafters' specialty is to optimize and correct a consumer's credit file along with providing education for an ongoing healthy credit report. Harry understands the unfortunate life events that can cause negative credit and also coaches on behaviors that create negative credit.

Harry has been interviewed and featured on many of the talk radio shows and TV stations within the Southeast U.S. about Credit Education and related subjects such as Short Sales, Foreclosures,Collections, Post-Bankruptcy and Credit Restoration.

CEO of Snedden Enterprises Inc.( A Coaching and Business Development Firm) with focus on professional business consulting for organizations. Special interests in entrepreneurial education and marketing. Public speaking seminars on business start-up education and creating valuable business relationships.

Harry's belief is......"The life you create and the level of success you have is directly proportionate to the action you take!"
