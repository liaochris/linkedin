Martin Brossman's Summary

I am committed to economic growth from the ground up through small and micro businesses.

I am a success coach, speaker, trainer and author incorporating social media training to accelerate growth for entrepreneurs and small businesses. I have originated in-person and on-line networking groups which have facilitated valuable business connections among members. My latest book with Anora McGaha is called Social Media for Business, focused on understanding Social Media and other free on-line resources for business. The book can be found on ProNetworkingOnLine.com and Amazon (see Websites above).

My IBM background and computer skills supports my ability to create and teach crucial new Internet communications, such as blogging, podcasting and on-line networking.

My own podcast show can be heard at www.lnquireOnLine.info . I offer consulting on how to generate profitable alliances by maximizing face-to-face and on-line presence and managing "the conversation of you" on the Web.

My 20 years of professional experience includes 7 years with IBM, where I received the "IBM Means Service" top customer service award, and 13 years developing and operating small businesses. Coaching since 1995, I developed a certification / mentoring program for Coaches in 2003. I am a native of Washington, D.C., I hold a BA in Math / Computer Science from St. Andrews College in Laurinburg, N.C., and currently reside in North Raleigh.

Paying member of Linkedin. Promoting the value and utility of Linkedin; the business tool for solving tasks that allows local and global connections.

Specialties

Personal branding, conflict resolution training, social media business integration, business networking training, group facilitation, sales coaching & training . I offer Social Media Coaching and Training. Some of the specific Social Networking areas and topics I speak and train in are: Social Media, Reputation Management, Personal Branding, Professional Branding, Linkedin for Business, Facebook for Business, Twitter for Business, Podcasting, Video / Youtube, Poken and Blogging for Business.
