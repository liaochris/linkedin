Angela de la Cruz's Summary

I am passionate about people, creativity and cooking. I'm a wannabe adventurer who questions everything but will inevitably say yes to just about any challenge you throw at me. Competition drives me, my sense of loyalty grounds me, and new information consumes me. These qualities, combined with my obsessive-compulsive need for organization and planning, make me a meticulous mediator for clients and the team of writers, designers and strategists I manage.

Since starting my career, I have served as an Account Manager at a social media engagement agency, the Marketing Specialist facilitating the re-branding of GunAuction.com - one of the world’s largest firearm auction websites - managed the social media and online reputation of several small businesses, and worked as copywriter for an online marketing company.

Graduating with a degree in Communications from Brigham Young University, I quickly rose to leadership positions and became manager of the BYUadlab while still a full-time student. There I was able to work with advertising agencies such as McCann, DraftFCB and Translation, and manage a large group of peers on numerous projects ranging from small businesses to multimillion dollar brands.


Specialties: Account Management, Production, Writing, Client Relations, Contract Negotiations, Branding, Media Buying, Social Media, Online Marketing.
