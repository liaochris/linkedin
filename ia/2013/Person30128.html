Dan Lovejoy's Summary

NOTE TO RECRUITERS: I am always happy to connect on LinkedIn, but I am not interested in pursuing other career opportunities at this time.

--- --- --- --- ---

I'm a User Interface/User Experience architect working for Oklahoma's largest utility. While I excel at facilitating requirements and producing documents, my greatest strength is advocating for users' needs and enabling teams to build excellent user experiences. I accomplish this through UX evangelism and by direct contribution to create user scenarios, personas, user process flows, wireframes, prototypes, artifacts, usability test plans, and by facilitating usability testing.

I am particularly skilled at teaching user experience concepts and skills in group or individual settings. I excel at connecting technical professionals, business users, and subject matter experts to discover needs and design solutions. At OGE, this means producing excellent business requirements, functional requirements, use cases, and other Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) documentation.

Finally, I guide OGE's UX practice by producing architectural documents, heuristic analyses, and lots of white papers. I'm currently work on integrating UX best practices with the existing OGE SDLC.

I LOVE MY JOB.

I also write regularly on workplace issues for Blogging4Jobs.
