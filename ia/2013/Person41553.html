Endrit Kromidha's Summary

Dr Endrit Kromidha has been professionally involved in banking, leasing, NGOs and think-tank projects. Currently he is a Teaching Fellow at Royal Holloway University of London — School of Management.

His professional interest and involvements are in Multi-Actor Networks, Project Management, E-Government, Research and Innovation in E-learning, Sustainability Information Systems, ICT for Development.
