Leigh Hilton's Summary

I have over 12 years of legal experience in the practice areas of Estate Planning and Probate. I routinely prepares simple estate plans as well as very complicated Wills and Trusts for high wealth individuals. I also practice in the areas of Real Estate, Business Law and Contracts. I would welcome the opportunity to meet with you to discuss your estate planning needs.

I was born in Dallas and grew up in Denton. I attended the University of North Texas where I earned a Bachelor of Science, graduating with honors in 1989. I earned my law degree at the South Texas College of Law in Houston, TX, where I graduated Summa Cum Laude in 1992. I have been married for 20 years and am the mother of two little boys. I am very active in civic events, am a frequent speaker to various organizations and strive to be a positive influence in the Denton community.

Specialties

simple estate plans as well as very complicated Wills and Trusts for high wealth individuals in Denton, Dallas, Tarrant and Grayson counties. Also in Denton, Lewisville, Sanger, Plano, Frisco, Dallas, Fort Worth, Aubrey and Krum
