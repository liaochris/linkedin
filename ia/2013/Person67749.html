Jon Atkinson's Summary

I'm a web developer, with a fairly long and varied history in high-end technical delivery and infrastructure management. I'm particularly interested in agile development methodologies, rapid application development, and scalability challenges .

I'm the Technical Director at FARM Digital., and editor of Django Weekly. I'm a regular speaker at various tech conferences (and unconferences) throughout the UK.

Specialties

Python, Django, Ruby, Rails, PHP, MySQL, PostgreSQL, XHTML ,CSS, JQuery, Debian (and Debian-derivative) administration, Xen, Email administration (mainly Postfix, but some Exim and sendmail, too), accessibility, branding and web marketing.
