Jim Kinghorn's Summary

What I do is similar to a recruiter, but with a different outcome. I match individuals to franchise opportunities that have the potential to replace a corporate salary while building equity in a business you own and control. Franchise ownership is a viable alternative to corporate employment or augment your corporate career with a semi-absentee model where you manage a manager.

I educate and guide individuals through a discovery process to achieve the dream of small business ownership. My experience as a business owner and seasoned small business placement coach allows me to understand the strong desire to chart a new career direction in self employment. Corporate is no longer the safe bet especially if you are 50+ and are struggling to stay in a job with diminishing opportunities; whereas small business ownership can provide you an executable plan (blueprint) to replace a corporate salary as well as achieve lifestyle goals. No matter if you are just beginning your research or have been looking for some time, I can help narrow the possibilities and avoid the obstacles that often prevent success. If at all interested in a conversation to discuss the process in more depth, feel free to contact me.
