Brad Pickering's Summary

I founded Innovate Advisory in 2009 to help executives in the mining, automotive, manufacturing, technology, and other industry sectors drive accelerated, profitable, and sustainable business improvements.

Throughout my career as an Engineer and Management Consultant, I have refined and defined a series of processes and tools to help organizations increase profit margins from existing and new operations. Using my proven SES Strategic Planning Process, Profit Driver Solution, Enterprise Risk Management Solution and YPYP Executive Coaching Model, and my clients are able to make great strides in achieving their organizational goals.

I specialize in Strategic Planning, Enterprise Risk Management, Operational Profitability and Sustainable Development at both the operational and business planning levels.

On my website, InnovateAdvisory.com, I publish weekly ideas, best practices, advice and insights on both the challenges and opportunities facing your business today.
