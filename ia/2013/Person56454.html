Jack Eyer's Summary

In my career, I have had the privilege to serve over 1,500 individuals & families from all walks and in all stages of life. For the last five years of my career, its has been my distinct pleasure to exclusively serve as a philanthropic advisor designing strategies to assist in non-cash gift cultivation while aiding families seeking credible strategies to provide, protect and preserve their estate. Our service and technical solutions bring clarity to complicated family, elder care and tax matters.

Presently, through a partnership with the Lifestyle Giving Alliance, Crescendo Interactive, and Salesforce Foundation our team of industry experts and philanthropic advisors are able to provide a specialized set of solutions for cultivating charitable commitments through our core offerings:

GVmarketer | Print, Digital and Social Media Production and Distribution
GVmessenger | Professional Presentation and Representation for Gift Planning Program
GVmemorar | Personalized Memorandums, Gift Proposals and Estate Designs
GVmanager | Customized Platform for 360 Degree Program Management
GVmentor | Professional Development Program for Staff, Stakeholders and Supporters

Our unique gift and estate planning service model provides supporters of charitable organizations an unbiased, third-party opinion related to their financial situation at no cost with no hidden agendas. Since our service and compensation model is directly tied to cultivating non-cash gifts for the charities we serve, supporters of such charities are able to obtain our counsel in the comfort of their own home or privacy of their business without concern of cost nor commitment to future obligation(s).

To learn more about our work please visit gvgift.org
