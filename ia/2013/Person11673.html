Barbara Giamanco's Summary

Argument Over. Social Selling Works!

My recent Social Media and Sales Quota report proves the monetary ROI of using social media for selling.

- In 2012, 72.6% of sales people using social media as part of their sales process outperformed their sales peers!

- Of the 54% of sellers who tracked social media usage back to closed deals, 40% have closed more than one deal (2-5). Another 10.8% closed more than 5 deals!

For the past 10 years, my team and I have helped customers with:

- Learning to use social media and new technology strategically as part of the sales process.
- Busting sales habits that lose deals.
- Filling the funnel with more prospects and moving those connections to close more quickly.

Client Results Speak For Themselves

- Using one approach learned during a program, a rep closed a $500,000 deal in less than 30-days after being stonewalled for 3 years.

- While monitoring social channels, the seller heard a prospect complain about their provider. She called the C-level executive directly and offered to answer his questions. He was impressed with her initiative and a contract was signed that day.

The first 20 years of my corporate career were spent selling technology solutions and managing sales teams who supported enterprise, channel, small-medium business and retail customers. I know what it’s like to carry a quota. As a sales leader and coach, my teams consistently exceeded their sales objectives. And through experience, I know that there are no quick fixes!

Training Won’t Solve the Problem

A request for training means that something in the sales process is broken or you have the wrong people or both.

Stop wasting budget on one off training. The 3 Pillars: Strategy, Skills and Execution are needed. Invest in experience and someone who wrote the book The New Handshake: Sales Meets Social Media. Social Selling is bigger than one technology or approach. We aren't tied to either. We are tied to your results!

Barbara Giamanco's Publications

  • The New Handshake: Sales Meets Social Media

    • Praeger Publishing an imprint of ABC-CLIO, LLC
    • August 15, 2010
    Authors: Barbara Giamanco

    After reading my book, The New Handshake: Sales Meets Social Media, you'll understand how sales has evolved and WHY you and your sales teams need to be leveraging a Social Selling approach to close more business more quickly. You will benefit from HOW TO tactical tips and case studies from companies who are using social media to drive sales. The final chapters describe the major social network players and encourage you to take the 30-day Social Selling challenge, which helps you put theory into practical application.

  • Tweet Me, Friend Me, Make Me Buy

    • Harvard Business Review
    • July 3, 2012
    Authors: Barbara Giamanco

    Are your sales reps using social media to drive revenue and pipeline through smarter approaches to networking, prospecting, lead generation and pre-sales call research? Read my article to learn why your sales teams must use social selling approaches to get today buyers or risk losing out on opportunities without even knowing it.

    Understanding why and how to engage with today’s social business environment starts with recognizing the changes in buyers’ behavior. We know that customers are online and that they use the web to investigate purchases they’re considering. The shift from a “push” to a “pull” world of commercial messaging has been thoroughly documented by now. It’s not just that buyers start the sales process without you; research has shown that today they typically complete most of the purchase journey before having any contact with sales. And by that point they are far more informed about your business than you are about theirs.
