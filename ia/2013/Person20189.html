Carlton Burroughs' Summary

Carlton is a Senior HR Strategist (SPHR certified) and Community Workforce Leader with over 15 years of progressive HR & Business Leadership experience ranging from Staffing/Recruiting, to HR & Compensation Management, to acting as HR Leader on major Outsourcing engagements with annual combined revenue exceeding $1 Billion.

In his current position, he is Managing Director & Founder of MyHRVP.com, an HR & Operations consulting service focused on small/medium sized businesses. Prior to launching MyHRVP.com, Carlton's most recent corporate role was with Hewlett-Packard, and included total compensation oversight for a 15,000-employee organization spanning across the US, Latin America and Canada. In addition, he currently serves as Chairperson (since 2006) for Fulton County's Workforce Investment Board of Directors.

As a complement to his deep HR background, Carlton's experience is also heavily weighted in the Client/Account Management arena, including the management of major, complex client relationships.
