Annetta Ribken's Summary

My job experiences include employment with a major medical insurance company, a urologist; several stints in management in the retail and food industry; a memorable summer job as a dancing hamburger. But my real passion is for fiction and story.

I am a freelance editor of over 70 indie novels, countless shorter works, and a writer with an extensive portfolio of non-fiction articles on a wide array of subjects. I have also self-published a collection of flash fiction, a first novel in the Aegean Trilogy titled "Athena's Promise", and "The Trailer Park Tiara and the Goad Incident", a hilarious romp with a most unusual young woman.

I am currently editing several works in progress for some amazingly talented authors. Their releases are updated regularly on the Editing Work page of my website. I'm also working on "Athena's Chains", second in the Aegean Trilogy. If that wasn't enough, I'm also editing an anthology of 22 writers based on the 22 Major Arcana cards of the Tarot, titled ALLEGORIES OF THE TAROT. The release date for this is October 31`, 2013.

I have over 50 years experience with the written word and absolutely love what I do.

I am a Story Doctor with a PhD in the School of Hard Knocks.

Specialties: I'm adaptable, flexible, and quick to learn.

I'm an ideas person, a trouble-shooter, I think outside the box.

  • Athena's Promise

    • Word Webber Press
    • October 28, 2011
    Authors: Annetta Ribken

    As the front desk manager of a hotel on the edge of Zombietown, Pallas is used to dealing with angry centaurs, surly trolls, and zombie housekeepers. The trouble really starts when one of her guests ends up dead. But that's not her only problem.

    A cop with an attitude – can he be trusted to be more than just a pain in her ass or does he have a more sinister agenda?

    A new Guest Services Manager, out for her job and ready to sacrifice anyone in his way – what does he really want?

    The attractive maintenance guy, endangering the promise she made out of necessity to the Goddess Athena – does he know more than he's telling?

    A mermaid diva, whose show at the Sparkling Butterfly must go on – or else.

    Pallas needs to find the killer, and fast, or she'll lose her job, her home, and the ragtag family she's adopted out of her crew of "critters".

    In the course of the investigation Pallas uncovers connections to a nasty Oddities dealer deep in the heart of Zombietown, forcing her to expose a trauma from her past which could threaten her future. With everyone and everything she loves in danger, the promise made to the Goddess Athena may well damn her if she breaks it, but she is bound and determined to save her friends, her home, and everything she's built.

    No matter what it takes.

    "The thing you gotta remember is the Queen of the Trailer Park title has been held by the womenfolk of my family for three decades. So for me, Sally Mae Riddley, the pressure was on. And I was really motivated, see, because of the goat.

    She gets to that and a whole lot more. Sally Mae Riddley is running into all kinds of problems. First, she has to secure the title and tiara not only to uphold the family tradition and keep her mama happy, but she has to deal with an unusual goat and Mad Hattie, the swamp witch. What seems like a simple matter becomes quite complicated in this hilarious novella about a unique young woman.
