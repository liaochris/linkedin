John Pearson's Summary

Possessing over 20 years of channel management experience, I am a results driven manager accomplished in formulating channel strategies, designing the channel programs to implement the strategy, and managing the operational processes to support the program. I have successfully managed all aspects of Channel Management policies and processes at Photovoltaic Solar, Networking, and Enterprise Software companies.

Specifically, I have served as a key leader on Channel Management teams that designed Channel Partner Programs that helped drive annual revenues at companies such as SunPower Corporation, Sun Microsystems, Netscape Communications and Bay Networks/Synoptics.

Related closely to my channel management expertise, I have Sales and Marketing Operations experience in driving programs and processes to increase the sales productivity of both direct and indirect sales organizations. I have a record of excellence in the design and implementation sales and marketing operations processes and programs that drive revenue, reduce costs, and optimize market coverage.

I believe that well executed channel programs and sales and marketing operational processes, all working together, can give an organization a competitive edge in the market place.

Specialties: Channel Partner Program Management/Channel Partner Operations/Partner Experience/ Team Organization Design and Leadership/Sales and Marketing Operational Process Design/Sales and Marketing Tools Development /Change Management - Project Management
