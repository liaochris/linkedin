Jacques Oury's Summary

As a writer and associate creative director with Ogilvy & Mather, I led teams on integrated digital campaigns for IBM and the global rebrand of Tyco Inc's safety division.

To broaden my skill set, I joined social@Ogilvy, the world's largest network of social media professionals. In addition to supporting digital strategy for brands like Molson Coors, The Lincoln Motor Co., Darden Food Group and BP, I built the Social Media Toolkit for InterContinental Hotels Group, and regularly develop Visual Storytelling trainings for social@Ogilvy.

Clients have included IBM, UPS, The Lincoln Motor Co., Molson Coors, British Airways, BP, Coca-Cola, Hilton Head Island, Orvis, and the good people at Camp Make-a-Dream.

I ride trails on my bike, I actively suck at golf, and you could say I'm in between Ironmans.
