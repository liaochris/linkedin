Carlos J. Gomez de Llarena's Summary

I design physical/digital experiences that shape social interactions and perceptions of space. I call this practice "Media Architecture" and my goal is to create a body of work that establishes these ideas and transforms our relationships with built environments.

I am also an award-winning interaction designer with more than ten years of experience for clients in telecommunications, advertising, design, fashion, retail, culture and government. I provide UX and strategy across web, mobile, events and installations among other capabilities.

I have led/mentored teams of designers, connected the dots between business goals and good experience design, and even created a few patents along the road.

Currently I run my own interaction design consultancy in New York.

Feel free to contact me if you have questions or comments.

Carlos J. Gomez de Llarena's Patents

  • System and method for selectively displaying data

    • United States Patent 7,549,753
    • Issued June 23, 2009
    Inventors: Carlos J. Gomez de Llarena

    An apparatus and method for selectively displaying data includes an aerial support mechanism, such as a helium balloon, adapted to be positioned at a desired location, which may be changed based on a user preference. The apparatus also includes a processor, coupled to the aerial support mechanism, which accesses, retrieves and stores display data. A projection module is coupled to the processing module and displays portions of the data, as a function of the processor. The projection module displays the portions of the data on a surface such as the ground, side of a building or screen. User-input commands determine the portions of the data displayed by the projection module and the projection module displays different data over a predetermined period of time. Also, a mobile display, mounted on a bus or taxi, can be used to display data based on the location of the mobile display.

  • Communication Services and Application Launch Tool

    • Issued November 24, 2010
    Inventors: Carlos J. Gomez de Llarena, Ryan Rowe, Brett Berkowitz, Gopi Krishna Kancharla, Ravi K. Vankayala

    Resources in a communication system are associated based on a query by receiving the query from a client device, generating requests based on the query, each request being formatted in a different message format, transmitting each of the plurality of requests to at least one corresponding source, and receiving at least one result associated with the query from the at least one corresponding source, where each result is associated with a communications application.
