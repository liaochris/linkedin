Leigh White's Summary

At Natural Family Health, we believe the body has the innate ability to heal and repair itself.

When symptoms occur in the body - they indicate that something is out of balance in the body's physiology. These symptoms are messages for us to listen to and act upon.

From addressing common colds to allergies, insomnia, hormonal imbalance and acute or chronic conditions - Dr.White will provide you with the education, guidance and support you need to empower your body to balance itself - as it is designed to do.

Specialties

Leigh White's Experience

Naturopathic Physician

Natural Family Health, LLC

November 2008 – Present (5 years 1 month)

Naturopathic doctor, Leigh White, specializes in natural family health care: assisting and supporting families in creating and maintaining health, and, in healing from everyday health issues, both acute and chronic.

Naturopathic medicine is a distinct system of health care – it is the art, science, philosophy and practice of not only diagnosing, treating and preventing disease, but also enhancing health by stimulating the body to do what it is designed to do to keep itself in balance.

Dr. Leigh White provides personalized attention to each individual. You will experience a holistic approach that encompasses the whole person - that time is taken to understand who you are, your lifestyle, your concerns and your current health. Each treatment plan is created to encourage the prevention of illness by strengthening and supporting the body's natural intelligence and removing obstacles to the patient's well being.

As a doctor, Leigh encourages people to be empowered and active in their health process, to ask questions, to be curious and engaged. She firmly believes her role as a doctor is to be an educator, providing you with the tools you need to feel healthy and well!

November 2008 – Present (5 years 1 month)
