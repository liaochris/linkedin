Kristine Meldrum Denholm's Summary

I'm an award-winning independent journalist and columnist, whose bylined pieces have appeared in magazines, best-selling anthologies, daily newspapers, online publications, websites, blogs, employee publications and newsletters.

I write on family/parenting, psychology, social issues and mental health, and law enforcement and crime stories. I help you tell the story that needs to be told, which will add value to your publication/site, as I produce easily readable, credible and relevant copy for your audience. Well-written copy and content builds trust with readers and interaction with your brand.


Specialties:
***Writing quality feature articles, covering psychological/societal issues, and health and wellness
***creating, developing and producing stories with colorful anecdotes and quotes based on:

***excellent interviewing skills (validation and compassion with sources) and research
***building network of expert sources (government and law enforcement, authors, psychologists, therapists, teachers, professors, physicians, hospital/medical PR pros, etc.)
***two decades of writing experience; member of ASJA (American Society of Journalists&Authors)
***journalism degree, magna cum laude (psychology minor)
***excellent proofreading and editing skills
***social media, promotions, blogging, public relations savvy

*Excellent understanding of Federal law enforcement, from working 10 years in an intense press office of ATF (Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco and Firearms) in Washington, DC. Special experience in crisis communications (dealt with international media in Oklahoma City and Waco, TX incidents.)
