Berk Ott's Summary

CDI/MDM Architect with 8 years of experience creating Siperian/Informatica MDM implementations. Main objective is to bring rigor and quality focus to each CDI implementation so that a quality architecture and quality infrastructure is delivered. The quality of the implementation is supported by understanding of the client’s vision, goals, limitations, data sources and timeline. Performance of logical and physical data modeling is seen as the key activity in producing the foundation of the CDI architecture. A modeling tool is utilized to capture logical and physical data model. Model is physically implemented in oracle and then imported into MDM. This allows rapid and rigorous implementation of the data model in Siperian for POC or actual development. Extensive Informatica MDM internal knowledge is applied in the design of the processes which wrap the hub. Process automation, control and monitoring is thought about and designed from the outset. Similar architectural considerations, features and experiences are applied around the inbound and outbound ETL and SOA components.

Specialties

Customer Data Integration: Informatica MRM / Siperian, Informatica Data Director, Hierarchy Manager. High quality and repeatable integration techniques using ETL technologies at hand, publication, and process automation. Problem solving.
