Laura Jones' Summary

Animal Science Specialist with expertise in innovative planning, programming, conceptual design, construction, process improvement, project management and training development. Certified Manager Animal Resources (CMAR) with a vast knowledge acquired from matrixed environments within Amgen, a Fortune 500 Company and UCSF, a world renowned University system. Experienced manager of large scale animal facilities and programs including agriculture, BSL-3, discovery research, SPF, biotechnology, preclinical drug development, GLP and cGMP.

I have dedicated my career to science by providing excellent and humane animal care through leadership and training. As a vivarium manager and global team leader I have led design teams through 25 capital projects representing nearly 2,000,000 gross square feet of space ranging from tenant improvement remodels to construction and occupancy of new Research buildings. As an animal and client advocate it is my goal to share this knowledge and passion across the animal science industry to bring forth state-of-the-art and sustainable work environments.

Managing large operations has provided me with competency in project management, operational logistics, regulatory compliance, occupational health and safety, training development, technical writing and team building.

Promotion of teaching, learning and working in teams is the heart of our business. Building alliances with industry leaders for the advancement of animal science and care is our goal. We welcome the opportunity to add value to any organization looking to achieve higher standards!

Specialties:
Training Development, Project Management, Planning, Process Improvement, Technical Writing, Regulatory Compliance, Design Development, Construction Oversight
