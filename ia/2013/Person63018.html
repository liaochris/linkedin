Jessica Read's Summary

Jessica most recently served as a Brand Consultant and Digital Communications Consultant at Evviva Brands. She had demonstrated success in both strategy and creative execution in marketing, branding, digital (social media and web), and communication to align brand image and messaging for clients. She has the ability to effectively lead internal and external teams and clients again tight project deadlines. Project management, maturity, and work ethic recognized by clients resulting in stretch role opportunities to lead engagements.

Her portfolio of clients include some of the world's most renowned brand and industry leaders such as BlackRock, HSBC, Marriott International, The Ritz-Carlton, and Xilinx. Jessica has worked on projects which have been well-received in publications such as the The Wall Street Journal, MSNBC, and USA Today.

She has served in a leadership capacity on the Board of Directors for the IABC DC/Metro chapter as Portfolio Director for the New Communicators SIG. She has also held memberships with the D.C. Chapter of the American Marketing Association as well as DCWW (DC Web Women).
