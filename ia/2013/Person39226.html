Julie Meyer, RD's Summary

Merging strategic food and nutrition communications with global expertise, Julie Meyer, RD, leads Eat Well Global, Inc., with enthusiasm, dedication and attention to detail. Eat Well Global helps food and nutrition-related companies bring their products and programming to a global audience through health professional outreach, consumer marketing and translation services in more than 70 countries. Using a dedicated team of Eat Well Global registered dietitian consultants, Eat Well Global has helped some of the world's largest food and beverage companies conduct market research in 15 countries, translate and localize health-professional websites and create specialized HP outreach materials for US-based ethnic groups.

Along with consulting services, Eat Well Global also markets travel apps written by members of the Eat Well Global network including Eat Well China, Eat Well Mexico and Eat Well Brazil, as well as the compilation app, Eat Well for Business Travel. To complement the app, Eat Well Global has an ongoing travel blog in The Huffington Post, featuring the "best of" global cities from nutritionists around the world.

Before starting Eat Well Global, Julie Meyer, RD was a nutrition communications specialist, working with organizations such as the National Dairy Council, PepsiCo, Unilever, Dannon and The Coca-Cola Company. She is also a journalist and the author of Eat Well Shanghai: Your Guide to Eating Well at Home and On the Go, a guide for expats living in Shanghai, China, where she lived for two years. She is the fundraising chair of the American Overseas Dietetic Association and received their 2012 Emerging Dietetic Leader of the Year award. She lives and eats well in beautiful Brooklyn, NY, with her husband and two school-aged daughters.

Specialties: nutrition communications, global nutrition consulting, nutrition journalism, global nutrition
