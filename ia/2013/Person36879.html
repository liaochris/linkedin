Don Phin's Summary

Owner of The Employer Advisors Network, Inc.:

Our main product is HR That Works, a human resources program with processes, strategies, and tools focused on companies with 15-500 employees. We work with a wide range of partners, including individual companies, insurance agencies, and HR consultants, who share in helping these entrepreneurial driven companies grow their bottom line by inspiring great HR practices.

At HR That Works we are passionate about building the HR department and career. Our human resource program will show you how to find and hire the best employees, retain the best workforce, increase productivity, reduce and eliminate workplace dramas, reduce employee claims, build teams and the company culture.

To learn how HR That Works can help you and your company, go to www.hrthatworks.com. You can try 30 days access for free. Contact me directly if you need HR or legal advice. (800) 234-3304

Vistage Speaker:

I've been a top-rated speaker for Vistage International since 1997, where I travel the United States and Canada speaking to CEOs and business owners about the opportunity represented by great HR practices.

HR Consultant:

As an HR Consultant, I provide HR advice, support and coaching in all human resources disciplines including: compliance, culture, change management, employee training, EPLI, employment practices/policies, team building, time management, and much more.

Author:

I have authored several books and eBooks including: Victims, Villains and Heroes: Managing Emotions in the Workplace; Lawsuit Free! How to Prevent Employee Lawsuits; Building Powerful Employment Relationships; Job Security; From Chaos to Order: How to Protect Your Entrepreneurial Business from Today's Crazy Economy; and Time Management, Making Mitsakes and more.

  • Victims, Villains and Heroes: Managing Emotions in the Workplace

    • San Diego, CA
    • 2002
    Authors: Don Phin, Loy Young

    Owners, managers, employees, customers, and clients are all part of the constant, swirling emotional drama. Rejection, sabotage, lack of appreciation, miscommunication, lawsuits and overwork with few rewards are all symptoms.

    • IRMI.com
    Authors: Don Phin, Bob Bregman

    Employment practices liability is a complex and multidisciplinary exposure that requires comprehensive risk management solutions. Because it addresses the legal, regulatory, human resources (HR), and insurance issues in one easy-to-read periodical, the quarterly Employment Practices Liability Consultant (EPLiC) provides the tools you need to develop leading-edge risk management and insurance solutions for these daunting loss exposures.
