Joe Calhoon's Summary

JOE CALHOON, CSP, is a highly creative and resourceful business growth strategist, serial author and keynote speaker who works with business leaders and business owners who aspire to develop higher performing organizations. An experienced and trustworthy voice in the marketplace, Joe has worked with hundreds of small to mid-sized businesses, delivering sound business growth strategies while developing leaders organically, nurturing talent from within and instilling a winning attitude into the corporate culture. He is the President of Priority Advantage™ and Co-Creator of 1-Hour-2-Plan.com, instruments that produce measureable, game-changing and GUARANTEED business results.

In the ‘90s, Joe was one the most requested and highest-rated speakers and consultants at Franklin Covey. Ultimately, he moved on to owning his own business and delivered double-digit growth for business owners in a wide variety of industries and markets. Drawing upon his background in personal and professional development, he provides strategic direction for business owners in growth-oriented entrepreneurial ventures. Those who leverage Joe’s talent for catalyzing growth can expect increased profitability, enhanced employee engagement and improved customer satisfaction. He excels in situations where he can assess skills, diagnose barriers to productivity and define the appropriate strategies that position companies for growth.

Known for his authenticity, positive energy and ability to build trust and rapport quickly, Joe is supremely committed to the welfare of his clients and invests in their success through comprehensive services. He creates an environment of accountability and thrives in situations where he can bring clarity, isolate opportunities for growth and stimulate action through collaboration. Companies that retain Joe are able to assemble a solid ROI framework for revenue generation, chart an upward growth trajectory and operate in complete alignment with their objectives.
