Ben Larson, CSM's Summary

I am a very driven and experienced project manager and builder of products, community, and culture. I’m process-oriented, deadline-focused, and achieve a high level of customer satisfaction. I excel by understanding people, valuing consensus, and fostering strong relationships. In addition to an obsessive work ethic, I enjoy a very active lifestyle and constantly challenge myself to grow intellectually.

In my "down time," you can find me training for various races as I run, cycle, and swim around the City; testing out (and photographing) the various culinary offerings in the greater Bay Area; or exploring every nook and cranny in this beautiful state of California. My friends know that if there's room on my calendar, I'm available to meetup for food, drinks, or a little friendly competition, so feel free to contact me!
