Lexi Lewtan's Summary

At first glance betaworks seems to be a loose organization of people that do many things -- but in reality we are building products that when combined together form a strong network, and eventually a media company for this century.

I wear many hats at betaworks, but I mostly split my time between coordinating our studio product launches, interacting with our vibrant community and acting as the Product Manager of Openbeta.

My goal with Openbeta to create a more open ecosystem around betaworks and our community of users. We believe we’re better at what we do — building and operating companies — because of the networks around us.

If you'd like to build, test and invest with us, join Openbeta here: http://betaworks.com/openbeta/
