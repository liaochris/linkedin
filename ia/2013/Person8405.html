Anne Tammel's Summary

Turning intelligent visions into realities, principal and founder Anne Tammel has devoted over two decades to coaching executives for leadership development and placing professionals with top-ranking business schools. Tammel began her career at the Silicon Valley's highest-ranking public relations firm, Cunningham Communication, then went on to apply her positioning and branding expertise in the high-tech, medical, and financial fields, crafting executive branding strategies, leadership development tools, and promotional portfolios for Fortune 500 executives. While pursuing her master of fine arts degree, Tammel was approached by an influx of executives, each seeking her guidance at entering the nation's top business school programs. Within two years, without having sought an applicant, Tammel had competitively, successfully placed each of her clients with top-tier business schools. This unmatched success led to the inception of MBA Expertise: a highly-personalized, results-based, boutique consultancy devoted to your professional advancement. Tammel guides business school applicants through the admissions process and remains their consultant as they continue on their path from MBA alum to rising executive to CEO. An author frequently called upon as a judge for literary arts organizations, Tammel also runs Poets and Dreamers, leading the multi-faceted author's network that was featured in CBS Los Angeles, and which serves over one thousand writers throughout Southern California. Tammel's articles, essays, fiction, and poems have been published in numerous publications. Tammel earned her MFA in creative writing at California State University, San Diego, and her BA in English literature / career writing at California State University, San Jose.

Specialties: executive branding and positioning, leadership strategy, business school consultation, academic and executive advancement, professional and corporate communications
