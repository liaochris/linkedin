Adam McGowan's Summary

Whether acting as a collaborator, consultant or connector, I am always looking to help like-minded entrepreneurs in any way I can. My diverse background in product, management, development, fundraising and finance allows me to offer a unique perspective to the tasks I undertake and the people with whom I work.

Regardless of my venture or role, I strive to be a productivity driver, an influential communicator, a respected contributor, and a leader by example. I design intuitive products, improve inefficient processes and inspire cohesive teams.

I own and operate Firefield, a venture that helps entrepreneurs efficiently translate their concepts into interactive products. I serve as Head of Product or CTO for a number of Firefield's clients and partners.

When not propelling ideas or solving problems, I'm usually found cooking, collecting sneakers, skiing, or playing lacrosse.
