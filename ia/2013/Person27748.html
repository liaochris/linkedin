Eliza Alys Young's Summary

I am a creative professional who has been running my own business for the past 20 years. I prefer to work independently with a team that I can pull in as needed but previously I have creative directed an in-house team of eight people, working on projects up to a half a million dollars.

For the past eight years I operated my business in the Dominican Republic. My clients were principally European luxury hotels who were investing in the Caribbean and Latin America.

I work with technology every day but I would consider myself more of a creative than a techie. To me, technology is a tool, a powerful tool that enables the creative process.

In addition to graphic design I explore other creative pursuits on my own time: fiber arts, photography, poetry, gourmet cooking and my blog: AmorYSabor.com

Specialties

Branding, market visibility, creative consulting -- specializing in companies with luxury products or services
