Thedford Hitaffer's Summary

Founding Partner, Hitaffer & Hitaffer, PLLC

Extensive experience in intellectual property matters, including preparation and prosecution of patent and trademark applications, conducting examiner interviews, preparing briefs and arguments for appeals before the Board of Patent Appeals and Interferences, managing foreign filings, and participating in post-grant proceedings, including re-examination and reissue proceedings

Counsel clients in various intellectual property matters and assist in managing intellectual property portfolios

Conduct legal studies and provide product clearance, invalidity and non-infringement opinions

Experience in licensing patents and technology

Monitor competitor patent activities

Investigate controversies and aid in the enforcement of intellectual property rights

Comprehensive experience working with automotive technologies, including interior component parts, frames, axles, drive shafts, transmissions, braking and occupant safety systems; building materials, including insulation and roofing systems, composites solutions and asphalt products, as well as processes, machines, and tools for making the same; metal-cutting products, including drills, insert and saws; and medical devices and disability products, including wheelchairs, walking aids, beds, respiratory products and daily living aids

Experience with various aspects of automobile restoration and building construction, and intimately familiar with a myriad of mechanical and electro-mechanical tools, devices and equipment

Admitted in Michigan, Ohio and Virginia and registered to practice before the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office

Specialties

Preparing, filing and prosecuting US and foreign patent applications in the mechanical and electro-mechanical arts, as well as for software and business methods
