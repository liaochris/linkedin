Editor-in-chief

Get Lost Magazine (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Publishing industry

1999 – 2012 (13 years)

Founder of this online travel magazine, I recruit and support the international pool of writers and photographers, write features and editorial column, photograph and illustrate articles, ride herd on the overall branding and gestalt of the publication. On the technical side I apply SEO to content, maintain and monetize content archive, partner media sales with Powell's and Amazon.

Our tiny technical team is currently very excited to be migrating the archive of wonderful content into a new WordPress site, which includes reader feedback and the ability to contribute articles from an iPhone in a bus in the middle of an I-5 traffic jam.

In July of 2012 the domain name GetLostMagazine was sold to the Australian print magazine and all the original content was moved to LostIntelligencer.com.

Job captain, Store design

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; SBUX; Retail industry

My favorite store project is at 316 N. 67th Street, Seattle, WA. We retained the garage door storefront, though later it was replaced with a knee-wall and accordion windows to block pigeons and mooching dogs.
