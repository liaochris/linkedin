Julián Amorrich's Summary

After getting a Degree in Tourism at Universidad de Alcalá (Spain), I moved to Finland where I was working since 2003 to the end of 2010 as a Sales Manager of Wild North, a DMC located Rovaniemi and specialized in Lapland as a destination for all kind of groups, leisure or incentives. From FIT's to series of charters.

Currently I combine my passion in Tourism with Internet and Social Media in my own company, Via North, in Rovaniemi.

In Rovaniemi I work as well in Lapland Safaris, where I have developed their Digital Marketing strategy for the last year.

Beside that, I'm a partner at iClicko Technologies & Communities, a company based in Spain and specialized in the creation of niche social networks and communities. We have launched projects such as Erasmoos.com (winner of the II Edition of BBVA Open Talent), Bolsa.com (for a third party), Estrenos.com (for the movie lovers) and Libros.com (for the book lovers).
