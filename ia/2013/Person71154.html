Justine Curtis - Virtual Assistant's Summary

Justine Curtis is the director of successful virtual assistant business My Virtual Assistant Limited http://www.my-va.com and is currently building a team of franchisees, details available at http://www.myvirtualassistant.co.uk.

Justine is the founder of The UK Association of Virtual Assistants (UKAVA) which offers free resources and information to its subscribers, sign up for free at http://www.ukava.co.uk. She is also the author of Setting Yourself Up As A Virtual Assistant and is passes on the benefits of her vast experience of the VA role to aspiring and progressive virtual PAs as a co-founder of the VA Success Group. If you are thinking about starting a virtual assistant business, visit http://vasuccessgroup.co.uk

Justine Curtis is a co-founder of Academy for Online Business, a company devoted to helping self-employed professionals and entrepreneurs to build their own online business empires. If you would like practical and informative online business training products to help you create your perfect online business, visit http://academyforonlinebusiness.com

For more information about the businesses and projects Justine is involved in visit: http://www.justinecurtis.com
