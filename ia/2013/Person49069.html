Glenn Remoreras' Summary

Glenn is a technology and business process expert with international and hands-on management experience in a senior IT management role. He is experienced in leading innovation and change that drive operational excellence in global business processes. He currently heads Corporate IT and Supply Chain Process Evolution at CEMEX in the US. He spearheads IT strategic planning sessions and conducts workshops on business relationship management. He specializes in using technology in enabling business processes to deliver customer and shareholder value with maximum efficiency.

Prior to joining CEMEX USA, Glenn was IT Regional Director of CEMEX in Asia. He also managed post-merger integration projects for CEMEX in the Asia Pacific, Europe, Mexico and the United States. In those projects, he directed major enterprise-wide projects that included global process standardization and enterprise solution implementation using SAP. He published articles on major publications and presented in conferences on topics ranging from IT, business process management and social media. Glenn recently joined Business Relationship Management Institute as Founding Member and Vice-chair of Advisory Committee.
