Chuck Chugumlung's Summary

Chuck Chugumlung is a Creative Director with ample experience in various digital media design ranging from interactive design to motion graphics animation. In 2009 He created Annexx Design Studio. As Principal, he is focused on providing outstanding creative digital media services.

After graduating from Art Center College of Design, Chuck started his career in packaging for Gaming and then later into Film Advertising. Wanting to pursue Interactive Design inspired him to learn coding for flash and HTML. After reaching his interactive design career goals at the height of the market, Chuck continues to think outside the box by incorporating 3d content into his work while at the time integrated 3d interactive content was fairly new. He later continued his study in 3d animation and design which lead him to become a Motion Graphics Designer and an Art Director for broadcast animation.

He continues to pursue his creative interests now with focus on interactive, mobile application, information graphics, web media, and advertising. With more than 18 years of creative experience under his belt, Chuck is capable of tackling a wide range of obstacles. He is currently exploring new technology and mediums, including explorations of 3d game engines to enhance user interactivity in communication design. He has also adopted open source development and fullfilling sustainable design practices.

Creativity is his passion and continues to be his priority on into the future.
