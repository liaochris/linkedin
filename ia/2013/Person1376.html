Alfonso Amelio's Summary

I’ve worked passionately as a conceptual art director in the advertising industry — always searching to reveal the true character of a brand, that essence that will organically appeal to the consumer or business.

Which is why I consistently integrate marketing and graphic design, in order to craft targeted messages that take into account both online and offline media.

As a result, I’ve produced extremely strategic, as well as creatively compelling ad campaigns for businesses the likes of Coke, Citibank, Steve Madden, IBM, Victoria’s Secret, AOL, IKEA, Banana Republic and numerous others

Specialties
