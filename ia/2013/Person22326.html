Charlotte Francis' Summary

Freelance writer with experience of international publishing, marketing, not-for-profits and fundraising. I specialise in writing grants, acquittal reports, newsletters and donor communication pieces for not-for-profit organisations and charities.

I have also written a book - SLOWING DOWN IN THE FAST LANE - which is now out on submission to publishers and I also write feature articles on a variety of subjects that interest me. Covering the arts, environment and spirituality, my travel, human interest and lifestyle features have been published in newspapers and magazines in Australia, the UK, Hong Kong, Canada and the USA.

Specialties: Fundraising: Grant-writing and research, acquittal reports, newsletters and donor communication pieces.
Journalism: Features on travel, human interest, wellbeing, spirituality and lifestyle
