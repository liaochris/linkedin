Erica Peitler, CPCC's Summary

C-Level Executive Leadership Coach (Professionally Certified through Coach Training Institute and International Coaching Federation), Speaker/Facilitator, Leadership Consultant and Author. Recently named Chairman of CEO board in NJ for Vistage International, the largest CEO membership organization in the world. In addition to facilitating monthly business meetings where current issues/challenges are debated/discussed and organizing speaker workshops, Erica provides Executive Leadership Coaching each month to 15-18 CEOs.

Erica Peitler has over 20 years of Consumer Packaged Goods/Healthcare experience in major corporations at Sr. Executive levels including Divisional Leadership at Executive VP/GM level, Global Strategic Planning, Marketing and Sales, Global R+D leadership and consumer brand management.

Having lead organizations and teams through transformational change and growth initiatives, Erica is now working with CEO's, and aspiring young high potentials through one on one and group coaching. She also develops custom workshops and executive retreats focussed on growth and change through experiential learning. Her first book, "Open Up and Say aaah!" was published in July 2008 and is now available on amazon.com and barnesandnoble.com. The book is a personal leadership journey where you explore your own growth and change process. Included in the book is a diagnostic, personal scorecard and experiential exercises. It is self-directed, self-help for those who are ready, able and willing to reach their full potential!
