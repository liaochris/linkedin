Eric Grossman, CGA's Summary

I introduce quick cash flow financing to businesses and bankers. We are the lowest-cost alternative to bank financing.

Today's strict credit qualification requirements can make it difficult for business owners to get traditional forms of financing that they need to maintain and grow their businesses. Qualifying for factoring or purchase order financing is easier to obtain than traditional financing because qualification is based on the creditworthiness of a business's customers…not the business's credit rating or financial history. Ready and flexible cash flow is key if a business is to thrive in a competitive market.

We offer factoring, purchase order financing for finished goods, and accounts receivable management solutions to help businesses across Canada and the US keep their cash flow healthy.

Our Credit Track program links our customers to lending bankers and puts them on track to obtain bank financing.
