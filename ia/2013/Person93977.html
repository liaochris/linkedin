Mike Berard's Summary

I'm an award winning magazine editor who has worked in the editorial, social media, and advertising arenas for the past 10 years and offers a diverse media and communications skill set that includes print, online, photography and copywriting. Big ideas and precise execution drive me to create media that goes above and beyond the mediocre middle. If it's worth doing, it's worth doing excellently.

I am a positive, passionate person with a love for the creative and the exciting. I love life and this reflects in my work and in the way I interact with partners.

Specialties: Content development, editing, writing, copywriting, custom publishing, SEO copywriting, social media, blog management, photography, marketing, advertising and communication.

"Nowhere is a journal of literary travel writing—which to us means narrative with a strong sense of place, character or time. We’ve run stories about the longest sea voyage in history, living on a farm in Tuscany and kidnappings in Libya. We don’t publish reviews of spas or shopping centers or "Top 10 Vacation Getaways." The magazine is published digitally so we can distribute globally, across borders and cultures, instantaneously. We are reader-supported, meaning your donation helps pay to produce the magazine. Imagine public radio in print: You are the reader and the publisher."
