Ben Hernández's Summary

Designer of fine pixeled experiences large and small. I have an affinity for simple, clean interfaces and get there by way of sketching, pixel-pushing and plenty of coffee.

As mobile/web designer, user experience expert, aspiring product strategist, and a "know enough to be dangerous" front-end developer I'm inspired by technology and where it's going.

I have worked on a variety of projects a for an equally varying array of clients including: National Geographic, Microsoft, Red Hot Organization, GameStop, and NBC-Universal.

Specialties: mobile ui design, web ui design, user experience, information architecture, CSS3, HTML5, responsive web design
