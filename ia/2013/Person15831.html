Murray Falconer's Summary

Hailing from the Highlands of Scotland, Murray grew up surrounded by mountains, rivers and fields ... and sheep. Lots of sheep. Somehow this all led to a fascination with design, which in turn led to a degree in Industrial Design from Napier University, Edinburgh.

Three years later, he followed that up with a degree in Multimedia Design (back when it was called Multimedia). From there he ran his own interactive design studio for four years before he and his wife packed it all up and moved to Australia.

Although once again surrounded by his beloved sheep, Murray had a serious snowboarding itch that needed scratching. So it was off to Vancouver to continue his career. The move proved a good one, garnering recognition and awards from the FWA, Webbys, Applied Arts, CommArts and more.

Today, Murray plays an integral role at Tribal DDB creating global interactive campaigns via web, mobile, digital environments and online advertising.

He is still friends with many sheep on Facebook.
