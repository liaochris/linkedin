  • LegalCrystal

    • February 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Lakshman Srikanth D, Sattvik Chakravarthy, Ashish Prasad, Harish Raddi, Anil Muppalla, Adarsh Patil

    LegalCrystal is an Indian Law Search Engine. Search can be performed to obtain pertinent High Court and Supreme Court judgments with Citations and Casenotes. You can choose to search using keywords or sentences pertaining to either Acts or Casenotes/Headnotes.


    We received the feedback of several advocates who were of the opinion that the existing online products and CDs /DVDs used by them to browse through judgments and Acts were not user friendly, complicated, confusing and time consuming. To tackle these issues, our core development team developed an innovative search engine which is user friendly, unique, effective and efficient. We understand that many advocates are hesitant to use technology despite their love for it, due to the common perception that the procedures are complicated and require some effort to learn. LegalCrystal promises you a simple, easy and effortless way to search for not only precedents but also Bare-Acts. We offer you a sophisticated Indian law search service which opens the door to great opportunities with just a few clicks!
