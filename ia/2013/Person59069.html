After years of working for someone I am now working with someone. This career change has allowed me to fully utilize the decade of skills I have developed as a promoter of social media integration and as a business analyst who turns data into knowledge. Let's see how much I can accomplish.

At ATCS Inc. I am growing consulting and development offerings to adapt to the evolving digital world. Where we once captured data from traditionally transaction systems, we now seek marketing data inputs directly from the customer via digital channels such as iPads, mobile applications and email services. Where we once developed websites that looked clean and were functional, we now ensure they are fully mobile and social media optimized. Where we used Flash for dashboard development, we now code in HTML5 so an executive can check their latest Key Performance Indicators on an iPad while riding the train to work in the morning.
