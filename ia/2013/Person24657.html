Christy Pregont's Summary

When I was in 8th grade I took one of those lame career aptitude tests (that everyone has to take) and the number one career for me was Advertising Account Executive.

True story.

Luckily (& apologies to the account folk who may read this), I stumbled upon the creative path and was fortunate enough to gain some amazing experience through portfolio school and traveling the world. I ended up in Boulder, CO, and came across a gig that combined my love of advertising with my passion for social media.

Three years ago I answered a Craigslist ad, interviewed at a coffee shop for something that I wasn't entirely sure was a real job, and started working in the living room of the Movement Strategy co-founders.

Since then, Movement Strategy has expanded to have offices in Boulder and NYC, and has worked with clients including Nickelodeon, NBA, St. Louis Rams, New York Knicks, New York Rangers, Whole Foods, Adidas and Bonnier Corporation.

Specialties

Social Media, Community Management, UX, Design, Interactive Design, Facebook Applications, Web Design, UI, Mobile Design
