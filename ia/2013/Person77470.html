Kunal Chowdhury's Summary

Kunal is a Microsoft Client Development MVP & Insider, Telerik MVP & Insider, Nokia Developer Champion, former Code Project MVP, Valued Windows Phone Blogger and a good speaker in various Microsoft Events. He received several Awards during his professional career for innovation, quality and customer satisfaction. He received MVP awards from both Microsoft, Telerik and CodeProject for developer community activities during his off time.

He posts his findings in his technical blog. He is a Platinum Author of CodeProject. Many of his articles were highlighted as "Article of the Day" in Microsoft sites.

He has 6+ years of professional experience in all stages of Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) involving requirement analysis, design, development, testing, deployment, documentation and maintenance to diverse business domains including Media and Health Care.

Kunal has a strong technical expertise in coding standards, usability, UI design, OOPs and refactoring of code. He is more passionate on Silverlight, WPF, Windows Phone, Windows Store application development. During his off-time, he built and published many applications in Windows Phone Store and Windows 8 Store.

He has good hands on experience in .NET, C#, XAML. He is a good team player, proactive learner and mentor with excellent analytical, verbal and writing skills. He published 600+ articles including Tutorials on Silverlight, Windows Phone and Windows Store application development in his blog. He is very active in various Microsoft User Groups like PUG, KolkataGeeks, KolkataNet and delivered various sessions in Microsoft events and Hands-on-Labs.
