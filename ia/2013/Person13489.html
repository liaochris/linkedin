President

Fresh Coat

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Executive Office industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months)

Fresh Coat uses an unbelievably effective marketing program supported by proven high-tech management tools and systems. By following the Fresh Coat process, franchise owners can tap into the most profitable markets and build an extremely high-volume business with no seasonality.

Our technology lets franchisees schedule clients and employees with certainty, which virtually eliminates cost overruns and "down time" between jobs. Because there are few gaps in the schedule, we attract the very best painting professionals in the business. Franchisees are paid when jobs are completed, so they see positive cash flow and few accounts receivable.

With Fresh Coat, franchise owners can focus on the most lucrative aspects of their business for a solid return on investment in the shortest time with minimized risk – all without ever getting on a ladder or picking up a paint brush.
