David Chiu's Summary

A digital commerce visionary with 15 years of experience turning ideas into results for Fortune 500 companies. Proven success designing and delivering ecommerce products trusted by leading internet retailers. An award-winning product manager, usability expert, and thought leader with a portfolio spanning global brands such as American Express, FTD Group, General Motors, IBM, and Time Inc.

Specialties: Ecommerce strategy and technology; Mobile commerce; Software, cloud, and API product management; Consumer marketing and loyalty insights; Digital product monetization; Market and feature requirements; Usability and interaction design; Online operations and governance; Cross-functional team leadership
