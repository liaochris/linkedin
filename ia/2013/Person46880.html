Danny Garcia's Experience

Owner/Principal Digital Evidence Examiner

DG Digital Services, LLC

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Photography industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 6 months) Miami/Fort Lauderdale Area

Mr. Garcia is a Certified Forensic Computer Examiner who honorably served within the largest police department in the Southeastern United States for nearly 20 years as a full time law enforcement officer. His final seven years in investigative work was dedicated to conducting and managing digital forensic examinations of computers, cellular telephones and other electronic devices.

Mr. Garcia left the department as the Sergeant of the Digital Forensics Section where he managed, supervised and reviewed examinations conducted by full time examiners. He was responsible for the overall operations for criminal and administrative investigations involving digital evidence and was often called upon for public speaking situations. Sensitive investigations included covert and overt operations, internal affairs cases, as well as critical incident/first responder situations.

Upon leaving the police department, Mr. Garcia decided to specialize in the forensic acquisition, examination and analysis of cellular telephones, GPS units, digital cameras, and other small scale digital devices.

Other services offered include:

- Technical instructor and speaker for computer and mobile phone forensics software, methodologies, and discussions

- Data recovery services (hard drive failure recovery)

- Strategic consulting on digital evidence cases

Senior Instructor (Contract)

AccesData Mobile Training/Mobile Forensics, Inc.

July 2008 – Present (5 years 5 months) Worldwide

Mr. Garcia teaches basic, advanced, and numerous specialty courses which introduce students to legal issues, information on mobile service providers and requirements for searching mobile handsets. Delivers hands-on instruction (labs) covering the acquisition and analysis of GSM, CDMA and iDEN mobile phones and SIM cards, using multiple types of software from numerous vendors. In addition, Mr. Garcia teaches courses where students learn how to recover and analyze data from Apple iOS (iPhone, iPad, iPod), Android, and Windows Mobile devices.

Advanced level courses include learning how traditional software and hardware performs data acquisitions and also utilizing additional tools to obtain data that is non-recoverable with traditional "logical" software. Mr. Garcia also teaches where most of date and times are located within handset files to include SMS/MMS messages. He teaches students how to carve evidence from physical memory dumps recovered from handsets, how to find deleted data and more.

Specialty courses include: Call Detail Records, GPS Devices, Cellular Artifacts, Android and iOS Forensics, as well as software-specific topics to include AccessData's Mobile Phone Examiner Plus (MPE+), Oxygen Forensic Suite, BitPim, Cellebrite UFED and Physical Analyzer, Susteen's SecureView for Forensics, Project-a-Phone, Fernico's ZRT, BK Forensics Cell Phone Analyzer, Sanderson Forensics ReVEnge and PM Explorer among others.
