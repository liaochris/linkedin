Islamic Civic Engagement Project

May 2007 – Present (6 years 9 months) Greater Seattle Area

The Islamic Civic Engagement Project disseminates information on current issues where then the recipients are able to stay informed on up to date policy changes at many levels. ICEP offers educational information where WA State residents are then able to determine their next steps on their concerns. "Your Voices Form The Laws" * ICEP initiated In 2007 Muslim leaders attending Martin Luther King Day at the capitol in Olympia for the first time. The issues that the Muslims presented all passed over the next three years time. Housing, Health Care for all Children, PayDayLoan regulation. The Martin Luther King Day "Muslims at the Capitol" continues today. For educational civic brochure contact:
