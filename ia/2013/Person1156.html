Amanda Coolong's Summary

Amanda is a digital media strategist with deep expertise engaging audiences across multiple platforms. She is skilled at adapting and extending content to second and third screens, and uses the latest social applications to empower audiences to participate in a deeper experience with your brand.

Amanda believes in bringing context back to storytelling via layered, non-linear stories that create a framework for genuinely understanding an issue.

Specialties: New media producer and host focusing on emerging technology. Consult entertainment and media brands on digital media strategies.
