Designing a social media community

African fashion

December 2011 – July 2013 (1 year 8 months) Copenhagen/Africa

“A digital community that makes it easy for designers all around the world to get in contact with unique African manufacturers. This community aims at making it easy for designers and African manufacturers to interact and have their work elegantly presented.”

My motivation for designing a digital community for the African fashion industry comes from my own experience working out of Zanzibar for five years, where I founded and managed a clothing workshop called Malkia. I often wonder had Malkia stood a better chance in the global fashion industry had we had a communication vehicle designed for our business.

For this concept I'm focussing on social media and how it interaction with mobile phones, with an eye on visual branding. To gain a full understanding of the users needs I work with a user-oriented method with in concept development.

Please feel welcome to send me your thoughts on www.iddi.dk/logbook.
Thank you very much.
