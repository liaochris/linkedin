Laura-Leigh Palmer's Summary

Extremely proficient in Adobe Illustrator, QuarkXPress, Adobe Photoshop, Dreamweaver, and Flash and my goal is to stay that way. Proficient in FileMaker, Microsoft PowerPoint, HTML code and Microsoft Word. And out of necessity experienced with system maintenance and trouble shooting software and hardware problems on the Macintosh.

Involved in the execution of a variety of graphics work in the Washington DC area including: books, catalogs, brochures, event promotions, letterhead, business cards, logo designs, presentation materials including power point, tradeshow graphics, illustration, web design/graphics, web site creaton and Flash animations as well as event photography.

Specialties

Creation of photo collages, maps, affordable logo design and flash animations. In-house large format archival giclee print and poster services.
