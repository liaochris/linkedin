Frank Catalano's Summary

Frank Catalano is a marketing, brand and product strategy executive, industry analyst, speaker and author.

He is currently Chief Marketing Officer for Professional Examination Service, the most experienced organization in professional certification and licensure services.

Frank has worked with a variety of companies primarily in digital learning and education technology through his Intrinsic Strategy consulting practice. In addition, he has been a consulting Senior Analyst for MDR's EdNET Insight market research service.

Earlier, he was senior vice president of marketing for Pearson's U.S. testing and K-12 education businesses during Pearson's print-to-digital transition. He has a long background as a consultant to, and executive for, consumer and business-to-business tech firms.

Frank's also a professional speaker and writer. He's a founding analysis and commentary columnist for the tech news site GeekWire, and a regular contributor to the NPR/KQED education site MindShift and the edtech news site EdSurge. He's the co-author of two of the "Dummies" books on digital marketing. And he's written hundreds of published essays, columns and articles. He's a former news broadcaster and science fiction writer.

Frank currently tweets on education and crossover consumer technology @FrankCatalano.

Specialties: Marketing strategy, brand strategy, product strategy, industry trend analysis, marketing and business planning
