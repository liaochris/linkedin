Jason Wiedel's Summary

My purpose is to grow and develop the people of my community by serving those who have needs, growing leaders, and facilitating spiritual devlopment.

As a project manager for Habitat for Humanity Virginia I direct the Sussex-Surry chapter of Habitat. This chapter was established in 2012 in an effort to serve people in un-represented, rural areas of Virginia.

I also blog at JasonWiedel.com about challenging spiritual ideas and important life issues.
