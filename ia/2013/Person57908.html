Jamey French's Summary

If your organization is struggling with core issues affecting client care, service delivery, revenue, and long-term growth, we should talk. I am dedicated to helping healthcare organizations tell their stories through marketing, sales, and service initiatives while energizing and inspiring employees to work toward a common goal. Areas of expertise include:

Training & Coaching / People Development
===============================
By combining my training and speaking skills with a strong marketing and organizational development background, I have created a unique approach to helping organizations develop their people into dedicated and passionate employees that come to work every day with a “can-do” attitude.

Core training topics include:
Sales, Customer Service, Teamwork, Leadership, Personal Effectiveness, Communication, Public Speaking, Conflict Management, Change Management, Time Management, Diversity, and more.

Sales & Marketing Consulting
======================
I work with healthcare organizations, nonprofit organizations, and small businesses to identify strengths and opportunities, analyze challenges, develop winning strategies, and create real-life simulations of high-performance environments.

My professional experience is complemented by my work with Toastmasters International. A Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM) and 3-time Divisional Champion in the International Speech Contest, I expanded my marketing skills by serving as Lieutenant Governor of Marketing for a 4-state district with 3,000 members and 160 clubs. In this role, I built 20+ new clubs, generated 50+ club leads, increased membership by 500+, and earned the Excellence in Marketing Award.
