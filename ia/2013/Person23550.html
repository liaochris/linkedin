Each day, you receive an email with two meal options. Click the one you want and voila – the order is placed. Don't want a meal today? Ignore the email. Want to see more options? Click through to the website. Ordering food now takes 30 seconds instead of 10 minutes, and healthy, tasty, freshly prepared meals are for the first time more accessible and convenient than their unhealthy alternatives.
