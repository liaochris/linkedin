Mattice Haynes' Summary

My mission is to help you create democratic spaces where the diverse voices of everyday people are harnessed to address public challenges and community issues. I have a successful track record, across the state of Georgia and beyond, for designing and facilitating town meetings, focus groups, stakeholder summits, and community forums.

I'm a professional facilitator and community consultant who's passionate about ensuring "we the people" have a voice and meaningful influence over decisions that impact our lives, families, and communities.

I work with neighborhoods, nonprofits, governments, foundations, and others on a wide range of policy areas including transportation, child and family services, economic and community development, health, education, regional planning, and the environment.
