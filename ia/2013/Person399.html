Aaron Beashel's Summary

Just two loves: Marketing & Surfing.

With a wealth of knowledge and passion bordering on obsession, my career to date has spanned multiple B2B marketing roles, from some of Australia's hottest young startups to larger corporate gigs.

Often regarded as a 'Growth Hacker' or 'Lean Marketer', my approach is to focus on growth rather than just marketing. Based on data driven insights, I adopt an agile approach to customer acquisition that spans not just marketing, but all aspects of the funnel including sales, product development, UX design, customer service and more. With a strong technical knowledge, I am consistently looking at how I can apply technical solutions to marketing challenges.

In my latest role, I am bringing this kind of lean marketing thinking to a large multi-national organisation. I am tasked with re-invigorating their marketing from the ground up, with the ultimate goal of 'taking the market' across key global advertising markets, including NYC and London.

When I'm not in the office, I consult on startups & marketing for a number of clients and spend as much time surfing & snowboarding as I can.

Specialties:
- Marketing Development (Positioning, UVP, Branding, etc.)
- Customer Acquisition and Retention (AARRR Metrics)
- Growth Hacking
- Web Design & Conversion Optimisation
- Inbound Marketing (Content, Search, Social, Email)
- User Interface Design
- Graphic Design

Aaron Beashel's Experience

Global Director of Marketing

Dubsat

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

March 2013 – Present (6 months) Sydney Area, Australia

Dubsat is a global advertising workflow and delivery application that enables ad agencies, production houses & brands to validate, book, upload, deliver, manage, track and repurpose broadcast & print advertising via simple web and/or desktop apps.

As Global Director of Marketing, I have been tasked with re-building the marketing and customer acquisition initiatives from the ground up. Taking a lean marketing approach, this includes:

- Conducting in-depth customer research, defining market segments and building detailed buyer personas.
- Designing and implementing a comprehensive analytics and business intelligence system that spans and instruments the full customer lifecycle.
- Optimising the customer acquisition process and aligning it with the identified buying cycles, roles and key messages for each segment (including acquisition channels, websites, onboarding & user flows, lead nurturing, lifecycle emails and more).
- Scaling acquisition channels proven to return a positive ROI.

Marketing Lead

OneSaas

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

November 2012 – March 2013 (5 months) Sydney Area, Australia

Widely regarded as one of Australia's hottest startups, OneSaas enables you to integrate the applications you use to run your business, saving small business owners hours each week.

Common use cases include integrating your eCommerce store with your accounting program, or your CRM and your email marketing software.

As Marketing Director, I was responsible for the creation and execution of their customer acquisition and retention strategy. Although my tenure was short, I was instrumental in the adoption of a data-driven, agile marketing approach and personally designed the analytics & business intelligence system they use to run the business. This lean marketing thinking, combined with the analytics system and optimisation tools I implemented, is helping them realise the true growth potential of the business.

Director

Realise Media

August 2008 – June 2010 (1 year 11 months)

Realise Media is a digital production company I started during my university years to help fund my education. I took it from a sole-trader entity to an organisation with some multi-million dollar clients and up to 7 people (mainly freelancers) working for the brand.

Realise Media and some of it's team (including me) were merged into Launchpad6 in 2010 and continue to be a critical part of the company going forward.

These days Realise Media operates as a 'digital co-operative', an organisation made up of highly talented and experienced digital natives who by day work for some of the biggest brands in the country, and by night rent this collective knowledge and ability to whoever needs it.

Throughout this time, my role at Realise Media has primarily involved:

- Driving new business, primarily through Telesales, Networking, SEM & SMM.
- Co-ordinating team members and 3rd parties to produce web applications, marketing videos, websites & marketing material.
- Design & build of client websites, marketing collateral & more.
