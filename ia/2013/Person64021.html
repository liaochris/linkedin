Missional Money workshops are is based on the book, Make Your Money Count. The book, the workshop, and The Blueprint for Financial Success are designed to serve four types of people:

Jim Munchbach's Publications

  • Make Your Money Count

    • Baxter Press
    • February 10, 2007
    Authors: Jim Munchbach, Matt Chandler, Patrick Springle

    Make Your Money Count identifies four types of people:

    • Those who are "buried in debt" but continue to spend even though they're consumed with anxiety about money,
    • Those who live "barely above water" and are always "one paycheck from disaster,",
    • Those who have "bucks in the bank" but are still worried about their finances, and
    • Those whose lives are full of purpose and contentment and who connect their resources to what matters most.

    With gripping and heart-warming stories, Jim helps each of the first three types of people take courageous steps to become people who are full of purpose and contentment.

    We long to make sense of our finances, but many of us don't know where to turn for help. In this book, Jim puts the pieces together so that we clearly understand...

    • the necessity of having a clear, compelling purpose in life,
    • ways you can connect all of our resources to our purpose,
    • how to write a strategic and simple "Blueprint for Financial Success,
    • ways we can maximize our savings and investments, and
    • a strategy for passing principles of good financial management to our children.

  • Allied for Success

    Authors: Jim Munchbach, Dr. Laurence Barton

    This book addresses the benefits, processes and barriers to building professional relationships with allied professionals for more effective client service. Alliances with allied professionals offer financial planners amazing opportunities for growth, encouragement and referrals, and this book will give you the tools to create them.

  • What Matters Most

    • Baxter Press
    • March 17, 2004
    Authors: Jim Munchbach

    This book outlines a process and a plan for building trust with clients using "The Blueprint for Financial Success."


    The Blueprint for Financial Success is designed to take you and your client through the step-by-step process of developing a successful financial strategy. It will help you communicate with clarity and precision, and it will help your clients understand the overall strategy you are using.

    The front of the Blueprint uses the metaphor of building a house to illustrate the process of developing a successful financial strategy. The three phases are the foundation, the framing, and the finish work. This metaphor is explained in detail in Chapter 6, “Build a Dream House.” The skill exercise at the end of the chapter, “Mastering the Three-Meeting Process,” outlines the process.

    The back of the Blueprint contains two distinct parts. On the left are the Essential Elements of a Financial Strategy, with brief descriptions of each key element. The rest of the back part of the Blueprint will be used to gather and organize the clients’ information and provide a template for designing the presentation.

    An instruction sheet takes you through the Blueprint step by step.

    You’ll be able to use this incredibly helpful tool immediately!

    To order copies of the Blueprint for Financial Success, go to http://moneybible.org/
