Carla Henry's Summary

Carla Henry is an experienced organisational change consultant & coach who is based in Singapore, originally from the UK.

Supporting organisations and their senior teams to lead change and increase engagement in the change journey is really important to Carla.

Carla is a member of the British Psychological Association, International Coach Federation and a founding member of the Division of Coaching Psychology (BPS). She is also a trained Co-Active Coach (www.coaching-courses.com)

Previous clients have included:

Healthcare & Pharma: UK NHS (Acute & Primary Care, including General Practice Management Development), Roche/Genentec
Telecoms & Technology: BT, Vodafone, Tiscali, Fujitsu
Central & Local Government: UK Cabinet Office, Teaching Training Agency, Local Authorities, City & Borough councils
Other: DHL, British Airways, Abbey Life Insurance, BPP Professional Education & SAB Miller

Specialties

Organisation Development & Design
Change Programme Design & Implementation
Change Leadership Coaching & Development
Group Coaching, Training and Facilitation
Communications & Stakeholder Engagement
