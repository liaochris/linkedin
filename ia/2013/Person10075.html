Art Wilson, Realtor,Real Estate Broker/Agent's Summary

Art Wilson and his wife Michele are successful full time Realtors on the North Shore of Chicago IL. Art has more than 13 years of successful real estate experience. Covering a wide geographic area of expertise, Michele is based in the Coldwell Banker office in Lake Forest, the number one office in that community and Art is in the Winnetka Coldwell Banker office, the number one real estate office on all of the North Shore. Art and Michele specialize in meeting the real estate needs of buyers, sellers and investors in the following communities: Lake Forest, Lake Bluff, Highland Park, Lincolnshire, Deerfield, Northbrook, Vernon Hills, Libertyville, Winnetka, Wilmette, Glencoe, Kenilworth, Glenview, and the surrounding communities. They take pride in providing superior and caring service which results in becoming their client's trusted advisors and Realtors for life. The success of their business is largely the result of referrals from satisfied clients, family, and friends.

Art’s knowledge of the communities and schools is invaluable to his clients. He is your trusted resource enhanced by his background as a 30 year North Shore resident and his active involvement in the community. Art can help you become acquainted with not only the properties you are considering buying but also with the neighborhoods and towns as well. Whether you are a first-time buyer, relocating, or a seasoned North Shore resident he assesses your needs and lifestyle requirements and finds you the home that meets or exceeds your expectations. He listens to what is important to you and makes every effort educate you so that the process is simplified, organized, easy, and enjoyable. His extensive experience selling homes and managing the real estate process, translates into successful smooth transactions for his clients.
