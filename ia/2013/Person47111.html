Gary Greenfield's Summary

As a speaker, author, trainer and business coach/consultant I help people and their organizations “Profit Through Performance.” I specialize in success principles, time management and strategic communication skills that drive performance improvement and inspirational leadership.

My services include expert solutions for eight business challenges:

Time Management
Keys to Success
Coaching Training
Presentation Skills
Sales Skills
Personal Coaching
Strategic Planning Facilitation
Highly Customized Training Solutions

I had three decades of experience in the financial services industry when I made the leap from the corporate world to the life of professional speaking. Now the real life skills learned—while rising from an insurance agent to an Executive Vice President managing thousands of sales managers, sales representatives and a multi-million dollar budget—are brought to bear in helping many clients reach their performance goals.

As an author, I'm very excited about the impact of my book, Life’s Ride or Fall…You Make the Call. The book is a series of thoughts, stories, lessons learned and actionable ideas to help people create the ride of their life. It provides a focused, multi-dimensional approach to help people searching for motivation and direction so they can connect where they have been and where they are to where they want to go in life. It won Honorable Mention at the 2009 New York Book Festival.

Affiliations include professional member of the National Speakers Association, The Florida Speakers Association and the Global Speakers Federation. I am a Past President of the Florida Speakers Association.

Specialties

Time management; sales and sales management; coaching; training; presentations skills; leadership
