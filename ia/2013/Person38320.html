Sean Alexander, Ph.D.'s Experience

International Financial Consultant

Alexander International, LLC.

January 1987 – Present (26 years 8 months) Worldwide

Sean Alexander, Ph.D., is an International Financial Consultant and professional speaker who has served with many of the world's largest Fortune 500 and Fortune 1000 corporations for over three decades. His focus in the areas of mining, agriculture, biotechnology, and energy combined with working with small, mid-sized and large businesses in gaining access to the funding they need in order to grow their businesses.

Sean operates from an experienced-based perspective in all of his client partnerships. He believes it is vital to treat his clients with dignity and respect in every aspect of the client relationship.

Sean has created innovative techniques and strategies that help his clients get massive results in record time.

Sean has been blessed to be trusted by some of the world’s largest Fortune 500 and Fortune 1000 corporations and healthcare practices, including Baptist/St. Vincent Hospital, Agency for Persons with Disabilities (APD), U.S. Veterans Administration Hospital, US Army, US Air Force, IBM, Barnett Bank, Burger King Corporation, Southeast Toyota, Wachovia Bank, Puerto Rico Telephone Company, and American Express, in their strategic and organizational development needs.

His background in design, development and implementation in the areas of finance, leadership, sales & marketing, customer service, team building and information technology combined with high-performance coaching and business growth expertise equips Sean to understand fully his client' needs and assess their current strengths, identify challenge areas and move into solution based results quickly and decisively.
