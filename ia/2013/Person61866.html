Jennifer Hill's Summary

I have spent the past 10 years as a professional recruiter and career expert. I began recruiting in 2003, and have recruited for top tier law firms and corporations throughout the country. I recently finished my first book "Stop Hoping...Start Hunting!" A Job Seeker's Guide to Finding their Job, which will officially be published in Fall of this year. It is currently available for pre-sale on Amazon:

http://www.amazon.com/Stop-Hoping-Start-Hunting-Seekers/dp/0986041602

In the past two years, I have been interviewed by NBC, ABC, FOX, & BBC News as an expert in the field of recruiting and job hunting, in addition to being a featured speaker for groups such as UCLA, PIHRA, LAPA, International Management Consultants, Pink Slip Mixers and the International Association of Administrative Professionals.

What differentiates me from others in my profession is that I take the time necessary to understand my candidates and clients needs to ensure a symbiotic match is created. I have invested thousands of hours preparing job seekers of all levels for challenging interviews with top national law firms and Fortune 500 companies, and have placed hundreds of job seekers into their dream positions.

I enjoy educating both my clients and candidates on the benefits of using technology and social media in recruiting and job hunting. I often lead Social Media 101 classes to a variety of professionals on how to effectively use blogs and websites such as Facebook, Twitter and Linkedin for professional purposes.

I graduated high school early and went on to earn a B.A. in Psychology from the University of California Irvine. I am very passionate about contributing to my community, and I spend my spare time volunteering for organizations such as the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society (LLS), Pink Slip Mixers and the SOS Mentor. I was nominated for Woman of the Year 2009 by the LLS and was elected Chairperson of the American Wine Society in Los Angeles for 2007, 2008, 2009, 2011 & 2012.

Specialties

I specialize in working with law firms and corporations in Orange County and Los Angeles. We occasionally recruit in other areas too. I evaluate my client's needs and partner with my clients to facilitate the hiring of the ideal candidate for their position. I recruit human resources professionals, paralegals, attorneys, legal secretaries, administrative professionals, and a variety of other legal and corporate positions. To find out more please go to: www.jhccs.com
