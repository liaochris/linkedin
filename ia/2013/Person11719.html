Barbara Neisendorf's Summary

Barbara Neisendorf is President of Neisendorf & Associates, Inc., a consulting firm that specializes in organization and leadership development.

PROFESSIONAL HIGHLIGHTS

• Served as Vice President of Worldwide Learning and Development for Levi Strauss & Co. and MasterCard International. Held senior leadership positions with Arthur Andersen & Co. and Nestlé, USA.

• Partnered with executive management to identify corporate values, vision and culture that support the business strategy; led successful change management initiative; designed leadership development programs to create sustainable change.

• Led research study sponsored by Heidrick & Struggles to identify what boards need from the succession process to fulfill governance requirements. Published by The Conference Board and presented at a series of seminars: “Trends in Corporate Governance… The Evolving Role of the Board in CEO and ‘Back Bench’ Senior Executive Succession”.

• Serves her clients as expert facilitator and executive coach for Neisendorf & Associates proprietary leadership and career development programs:
**Strategic Leadership: Leading in Alignment with Your Organization’s Values, Vision, Brand and Strategy
**Bring Out the Best in Your Team: Coaching Skills for Leaders
**Authentic Leadership: Building and Sustaining Leadership Resilience
**Career Window®: Achieving the Career Success You Deserve
**Career Serendipity: Creating Your Own Career Good Luck

Barbara is a frequent speaker on strategic human resources issues with a focus on leadership development, diversity and inclusion, succession management and career development. She has been published by The Conference Board, The Center for Creative Leadership and several professional journals.

Specialties

Barbara has distinguished herself with innovation and accomplishment with cutting-edge global corporations for more than 28 years. She has consulted in the areas of strategic planning, organization effectiveness, organization design, culture change, leadership development, talent management, career development, executive coaching, team development activities and process facilitation. Clients have included Microsoft, Epson, Bechtel, Ericsson, Nestlé, Brown-Forman and McKesson.
