Bill Bliss' Summary

For the past 15+ years, our company has helped organizations of all kinds increase their profits through improved leadership, teamwork and organizational health. A trusted adviser with over 25 years of experience coaching and developing CEOs, "C" suite executives, business owners, Presidents, family business owners, next generation leaders and other senior leaders at Fortune 500 as well as mid-sized companies. Extensive experience in manufacturing, pharmaceutical, retail, publishing, technology, financial services, transportation services, real estate, construction and other industries. Certified coach, trainer and speaker and Founding Partner of the John Maxwell Team, as well as certified in delivering and interpreting the Caliper Profile and Walton Culture Compass. Co-Founder of Proven Leaders Pipeline.

Specialties: Executive Coaching for CEO & VP levels to enhance leadership skills. Driving organizational health solutions to improve senior leadership trust, collaboration and mutual accountability. Helping organizations have the right people on the bus, in the right role at the right time. Meeting facilitation for strategic planning and team building; performance and change management. I am most effective in helping organizations retain their best talent and overcoming leadership dysfunction.
