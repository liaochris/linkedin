Bob Andrews' Summary

For over 30 years I've been helping people from many walks of life and a variety of industries put their success in writing....turning clumsy prose into organized, clear, concise and persuasive copy for individual and business clients……composing results-getting complaint letters to businesses and government agencies..... writing, editing and formatting letters, reports, forms and other documents for the insurance industry.....and creating dynamic, targeted resumes and cover letters.

With 14 years "in the trenches" providing editorial support in the commercial insurance market, and extensive experience with workers' compensation and liability claims, I understand the editorial challenges of the commercial insurance professional.
