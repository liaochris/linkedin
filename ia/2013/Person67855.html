Jonathon Jones' Summary

Currently living and freelancing in Melbourne. I design for a large range of clients as well as being a keen illustrator, animator, and photographer.

I have collaborated with and designed for numerous international brands including, Fosters, Matilda Bay Brewing Company, Dubai Airports, GIO Investment Group, Medibank Private, Disney-Pixar, Universal, FOX, Paramount, Dreamworks, Lend Lease, FIBA, Marvel Entertainment, Mercedes, agIdeas, Pluna Airlines, Honda, Wolf Blass, Qantas, BenQ and many more. Design is my passion and I approach every job with energy, enthusiasm and commitment.

Specialties: Branding, packaging design, print-ready artwork, hand drawn and digital illustration, website design, photography, marketing, art direction and team management.
