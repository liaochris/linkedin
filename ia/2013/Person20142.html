Carlos Riveroll's Summary

In a Nutshell:

I'm an Associate Creative Director with over a decade of experience creating integrated advertising and branding campaigns for all types of clients; as well as leading both Copywriters and Art Directors to produce the highest level work achievable and always on strategy.

Category Experience:

Automotive, Alcoholic Beverages, Finance, Public Health, Packaged Goods, Travel/Tourism, Technology, Telecom, Entertainment, Retail, Government and Non Profit. I can adapt to new categories at the drop of a hat, so if there's a category you don't see listed here, chances are it will appear here soon.

The Skills to Pay the Bills:

I excel at developing strategies, concepts and executions for integrated, digital, broadcast, print, OOH, direct and whatever else is thrown at me. I do whatever it takes to solve a problem, even if it involves inventing a new product, a new brand or a new medium. I'm completely bicultural and as such, I'm able to work for General market or Hispanic market.
The longer I work in this industry, the blurrier the line between copywriter and art director becomes and that's probably why I can easily wear both hats without skipping a beat. Although my first love and weapon of choice is art direction; I am equally obsessive about the craft when it comes to writing. This applies to both, English and Spanish, since I'm fluent in both languages at native levels. Having said that, I play very well with my creative partners, regardless of what discipline they come from. Just ask them.
