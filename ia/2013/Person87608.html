Martine Cantrel, PMP's Summary

Martine created GLADSTO International in Paris, France in 1994, and incorporated it as a Limited Liability Company (llc.) in the USA in 1999.

Through GLADSTO, she has helped big and small businesses gain international exposure for their goods and services ranging from the cosmetics and the perfumes to the steel and the plastics industries.

Contracted as a multilingual and skillful import/export project manager – she is fluent in French, English, Spanish and Portuguese, and she is PMP certified, she has travelled extensively in the US, Canada, Europe and Latin America to represent her clients' products on international trade shows and at seminars.

She also acted as interpreter travelling with teams of engineers to audit manufacturers and to find best sources for supplies.

Fascinated with the Internet technologies, she wrote and taught many Web related classes, such as Cyber Marketing, How to Build an Online Forum and The Legal Aspects of the Internet, for institutions such as the Chamber of Commerce of Paris, Learning Tree International Inc. and Fairfax Families4Kids.

Recently, she has added social media and SEO to her set of tools. She is currently developing virtual classrooms hosted on the Edmodo.com and the Moodle.com platforms.
