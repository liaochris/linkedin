David Wittlinger's Summary

Hmmm... let's see, how would I summarize myself? I suppose professionally I would use words like 'Wordpress web designer" - "social media marketing consultant" - and "copywriter."

In the realm of Web Design, I see my job as taking a client's vision and goals for their online marketing channels and building the tools needed to engage their customers. This includes using proven principles of design and incorporating the best open-source tools available.

As a Social Media Strategist, I see myself as the bridge between "marketing" and genuine customer interaction. My background includes developing programs that have gained my clients market share, brand equity, increased revenue and stronger resonance within their respective communities.

I believe that Colomark Media has a distinct advantage when it comes to looking at all sides of the marketing matrix. I live in Steamboat Springs Colorado with my wife and two children.

Specialties: social media manager, Wordpress web design, copywriting
