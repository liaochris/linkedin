Michael Papanek's Summary

Michael Papanek specializes in providing leaders with practical strategies, tools, and skills to implement change. His experience combines 20-plus years with a wide array of clients in high-tech, retail, health care, and financial services — as well as local, state, and federal governments and non-profit organizations.

Michael has lead transformational change initiatives in the areas of product/service innovation, IT transformation and new go-to-market strategies.

Using an “action learning model”, he combines keen insight, a strategic mind, high energy, and the right level of humor to help leaders and teams improve relationships, gain skills, and achieve exciting new outcomes. To that end, Michael functions as equal parts leadership consultant, strategist, team facilitator, executive coach and tool-giver.

Michael’s clients include:

Agilent, Amgen, Apple Computer, Charles Schwab, Cisco Systems, Clorox, eBay, EA, GE, Google, HP, kCura, Kaiser, Microsoft, Moody’s/KMV, Net App, SGI, Southwestern Energy, Sprint, SRI International, State Fund, StubHub, US Forest Service, US Steel, SONGS, Telefonica, Visa, Wells Fargo Bank, Yahoo!.

Michael’s experience includes 15 years at Interaction Associates (IA), a global consulting and performance improvement firm. Michael had senior management roles at IA including company strategy, product innovation and business development as well as delivering learning solutions in leadership, teamwork, facilitation, strategic thinking and change management.

Prior to joining Interaction Associates, Michael was a systems engineer and account manager at Electronic Data Systems (EDS).

Michael also has a long-term commitment to working with non-profit organizations.

Not-for-profit clients include:

Earth Justice, Head Start, The Oakland Unified School District, The Presidio Trust.
