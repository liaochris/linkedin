George Polzer (YourBizzWizz.com)'s Publications

  • Life Settlement Investment Indexation & Best Practices Guidelines

    • International Society of Life Settlement Professionals
    • August 8, 2011
    Authors: George Polzer (YourBizzWizz.com), Jim Aspinwall, Wai-Keung Tang

    In this paper we do not discuss management decisions with regard to risk management which are a part of the management problem. For example the two main problems in Life Settlement pools are longevity risk and liquidity risk. Longevity risk is a model risk. Calculation accurate runoffs is 80% or more of the risk in managing a pool of Life Settlements. However in order to keep the pool in-force premiums have to be paid on time. The management of this risk is primarily a management decision. What should the size of the reserve be? Should I offset short term liquidity risk with an annuity structure, short term viaticals, convertible term or a number of other hedges that can be used to compensate for liquidity risk? There are quite a number of ways to hedge for liquidity risk but that can be covered under a separate heading. For now we concentrate on modelling and model risk.
