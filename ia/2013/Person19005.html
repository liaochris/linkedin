Ian Jarvis' Summary

A commercially minded professional with a strong background is marketing and sales.

With over 15 years' experience, I have conceived, deployed and managed marketing and sales campaigns, both goods and services here in the UK and globally.

Physicool:

Physicool manufacture and supply a range of innovative, reusable cooling bandages. The product’s combine instant cooling, compression and support.

Physicool is used by surgeons, physical and other rehabilitation therapists, team physio’s and sports enthusiasts to reduce swelling and inflammation, combat pain and speed up recovery.

Physicool has been clinically proven to be significantly more effective than many other cold therapies in reducing swelling and pain – speeding up mobility. The bandage’s come pregnated with a coolant which when applied to a limb or injured body part will draw heat out via rapid evaporation.

I’m interested in connecting with anyone or businesses involved in the medical and sport markets.

The following is a short video of Physicool and Simon Garrett BM MRCS FRCS, Consultant Orthopaedic & Trauma Surgeon at Dorset County Hospital. Simon talks about the clinical trials he conducted using Physicool post–Total Knee Arthroplastry to reduce swelling and inflammation, and mobilise patients quicker. Simon has no financial involvement with Physicool.
