John Lancaster's Summary

John understands the fears and anxieties small business owners experience when employing staff or thinking about employing staff for the first time.

Taking people on should be one of the most rewarding decisions you make and John is determined to allay those fears and anxieties by supporting you with a no-nonsense, practical, professional and affordable service.

He has over 25 years’ experience delivering hands-on HR and personnel support to small and medium sized businesses. He is a qualified Trainer and Work Based Behaviour Assessor and has also gained qualifications in Business Advice, Marketing, and Health & Safety.

What you see is what you get. John has a very open and honest style and passionately believes success for anyone owes a lot to the ability to build relationships based on trust and confidence.

John Lancaster's Experience

HR Consultant

John Lancaster HR

September 2011 – Present (2 years) Darlington, United Kingdom

John Lancaster HR provides a wide range of practical, professional and affordable HR services to businesses that have neither the time or the expertise to deal with their employment and people management issues.

Services are customer driven; designed and delivered to meet the needs of each business and include:

- HR health checks
- Recruitment
- Contracts of employment
- Policies and procedures
- Staff handbooks
- Payroll services
- Employment documentation
- Training
- Managing performance
- Dealing with discipline and grievance
- Absence management
- Handling redundancies

Conveniently based in Darlington, John Lancaster HR works with businesses across the North of England.

Business Adviser specialising in delivering HR services and supporting business start ups

South Durham Enterprise Agency

August 2003 – August 2011 (8 years 1 month) North East

South Durham Enterprise Agency is a not-for-profit social enterprise and award winning economic development agency. The Agency supports local people starting up in business, works with existing businesses improving performance and sustainability and provides a wide range of specialist support, including HR.
