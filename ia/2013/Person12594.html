Becky Sansbury's Experience

Creator

After the Shock

2012 – Present (1 year) Raleigh-Durham, North Carolina Area

When people have been rocked by shock, nothing is the same. After the Shock: Moving from Reaction to Resilience is a process that helps people find their new normal.

This simple, but powerful process is designed for businesses and organizations who want to prepare ahead of time to decrease the effects of a crisis, or to regain stability as quickly as possible in the aftermath of a shock.

Delivered through keynotes, training, and consulting, After the Shock focuses on calming fears, focusing energy, marshaling resources and promoting growth during challenging times. More than just crisis management, After the Shock promotes individual and organizational resilience.
