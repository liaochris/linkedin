Mar Omega (Marlin Littau)'s Summary

Mar Omega is a modern renaissance artist / designer / entertainer.

His artwork has been exhibited all over Manhattan, Brooklyn, Washington DC, New Jersey and Texas.

Mar Omega worked in television at MSNBC and CNBC from 2004 to 2009. He moved to Texas in June of 2009 and was a graphics operator at the CBS affiliate in Austin. He is currently living in eastern Pennsylvania and Northern New Jersey working on private commissions.

Specialties: Camera operations, robotics operations, steadicam utility, web programming, traditional painting techniques, digital painting and imaging, industrial design, acting, stunts
