Brent Madison's Summary

Business and entrepreneurship weigh heavily on what I do in everyday life, which is why I believe in honest hard work and entrepreneurial drive. My father and grandfather have always owned and created their own opportunities, a trait I have tried to follow. I have cofounded three startup companies in entertainment, two of which are still operational. All Night Pictures, which is an Illinois corporation with independent government contracting specialties, and American Black Market, which is a new international digital media venture.

I began my career with three years in IT and network development. As an Eagle Scout, I have dedicated many years to volunteering for national organizations such as the Boy Scouts of America and have personally organized campaigns for candidates in government elections.

I truly love what I do, producing in film combines my hobbies and ultimately my passion. I owe many thanks to great friends, my supporting family, and humbling business partners. Originally a native to St. Louis and its suburbs, I am a Columbia College Chicago alum, and a resident of the city of Los Angeles. I am actively seeking new endeavors and partnerships and I am always willing to collaborate on good ideas, regardless of the risks or consequences.

My experience in the film industry includes: producing, intellectual property development, production, film sales, distribution, and consulting.

Notable Clients: Starwood Hotels, W Hotels, Young Money, The Miss World Organization, CrossFit Hollywood, Milwaukee Tourism
