Faith Wood's Summary

Would you like to know how…..?
• To leverage your collective intelligence by asking better questions and showing up differently?
• To understand how to take your ideas and conversations to a whole new level of influence?
• To re-engineer systems that are not working and replace them with fresh implementable strategies?
• To stop doing, thinking and believing in ways that are no longer serving you?
• To leave behind the smoke and mirrors and start implementing real change for real gain?

Perhaps then it’s time you met, Faith Wood. Faith believes that every day we get to choose who we’ll be and the person we choose most often frequently becomes our legacy.

Faith works with hard working, driven professionals helping them to discover how to show up, on purpose, by strategic design, and think out of the box (or outside the crowd if you prefer).

So, if what you are doing & thinking has become too predictable and no longer appears to be serving you, perhaps it is time to ask yourself ….now what? Are You Ready to Unleash the Power of Faith?

Using hypnosis, facilitation skills, NLP strategies, performance coaching, speaking, training and interviewing skills (learned on the front line of community patrol work), Faith has developed a system of navigating conflict and de-escalating crisis with proven results.

Where others see only complexity, Faith’s greatest strength is in her ability to read an audience and detect communication patterns quickly, adapting her style to seamlessly match the needs of the group or the individual.

Specialties: Professional Speaker, Trainer and Coach in interpersonal relationships - primarily communication, conflict reduction, influence and reading body language

    • Airdrie, Alberta

    Authors: Faith Wood

    The Graduating Bully follows schoolyard bullies from the benign comfort of their daily lives to the secretive environment of the workplace. By understanding the progression from the schoolyard to the office, you’ll learn how to diffuse the underhanded techniques employed by the workplace bully.

  • Taking the Bully by the Horns

    • Infinity Publishing
    • July 2011
    Authors: Faith Wood

    Taking the Bully by the Horns by Faith Wood is the first in a three-part series that offers parents, students and all victims of bullying a down and dirty look at the pervasive and escalating problem of bullying in our school systems. Parents who suspect their child is enduring the torment of a bully will recognize some or, perhaps, all of the signals commonly displayed by a young bully victim, as well as how to confront, respond to and dodge a bully’s physical or psychological bullets.

    Parents who suspect their child may be a bully will also learn and understand many of the precipitating factors that cause children to choose bullying as a means of acceptance within their peer groups. But it’s not enough to understand. Parents of bully victims, as well as parents of youth bullies, will learn how to accept, not deny, the existence of this insidious behavior. And, what to do about it.
