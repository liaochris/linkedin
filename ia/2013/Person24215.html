Christine Osborne's Summary

What's an Encore Navigator. Are you a coach? Kind of. A career transition counselor? Sort of. A reference librarian? Let me look that up for you. A writer, always.

I'm a little of lots of titles in the work I do with Discovering What's Next, a Newton nonprofit I've been part of since 2005, and now with ReServe Greater Boston. We serve people 50+ in the search for meaningful work in the second half of life.

Specialties: Creative Director was a title I had in advertising. Then, the job was to think up ideas that would make products stand out and get bought. Now I help people stand out and get bought. I work with job seekers who want to
. explore career change, especially encore careers
. identify transferable skills
. articulate a personal brand
. investigate online and community resources
. create effective social media communications
. plot a course leading to next steps
. discover what's next
