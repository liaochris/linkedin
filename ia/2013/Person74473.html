Ken Reimer's Experience

Director, Centre for Enhanced Teaching & Learning

University of New Brunswick

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

2005 – Present (8 years)

CETL promotes teaching excellence and innovation in all faculties at the University of New Brunswick - Fredericton campus. Three units include Media Services, Teaching & Learning Services, and Classroom Technology & Services, enabling our team to support teaching from faculty development, to the learning environment, learning technologies, and the development of educational media and video production. Our group supports the Desire2Learn learning management system for the campus.

Director, UNB Online

University of New Brunswick

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

2008 – Present (5 years)

UNB Online offers graduate programs, credit undergraduate courses, and workplace programs for working professionals. The UNB Online unit develops new programs, and delivers and supports 1000's of students around the world.

President & CEO

Reimer Consulting Group

2005 – Present (8 years)

Consulting services around online learning development, program evaluation. Consulting experience includes establishing new programs, making the case for going online, building a value proposition, identifying a market niche, and developing a sustainable business model.

2010 – Present (3 years)

President & CEO
