Jane Vita's Summary

I'm a dynamic and versatile person, always ready to face new challenges and learn new things. Keeping myself well informed is high priority on my list, as is making sure the knowledge I have nowadays is current and valuable.

I enjoy team work, specially multi discipline teams, because they can make your daily job more pleasant and productive. Well informed opinions are always important, and need to be respected.

When problems arise in my my projects, I keep myself calm and controlled, and look very hard for the best solutions. Difficult work is challenging, but a rewarding one. It is the time I enjoy showing my best qualities and potential.

My current objective is to work in projects related to Interaction Design, User Experience, Web Design, Transmedia, New Media, Cross-platform, Games and Gamification.

My specialties are: Information architecture, usability, wireframes, sitemaps, personas, scenarios, prototypes, heuristic inspection, competitive analysis, analysis of log of access, content inventory, interface guidelines, interaction design, user experience, management of people and projects, methodology and quality of software, web standards, design patterns, futures thinking, design research, UI design, SEO, social interaction design and gamification.
