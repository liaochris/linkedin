Dawn Quesnel's Summary

Dawn Quesnel, CPCC, PCC, aka, Coach DQ is the President of Quesnel Companies, a contributing author, an award-winning career coach, speaker, and radio show host, who for the last 10 years has been providing coaching, training and consulting programs to marketing, advertising and creative entrepreneurs who want to accelerate their career so they can love their life! Specializing in coaching top performers with job/career changes, raises, and promotions. Known for helping clients to increase revenues/salaries, confidence, the opportunity development pipeline, and career advancement.

Her work has been recognized by industry leaders including being named by the International Coach Federation New England “Career Coach of the Year” in June 2011.

In the Spring of 2011, Dawn contributed to her first book “101 Ways to Enhance Your Career” (Self Improvement Online, Inc. Publishing) and most recently did an interview series on her BRIDGE Method for Job Seekers, featured in the book for ABC, Channel 40. DQ is currently working a on comprehensive version of the BRIDGE Method for Creative Entrepreneurs.

Dawn has given and conducted thousands of interviews; radio, TV, and recruiting combined. A few favorite: Michael E Gerber, David Rock, Dan Pink, Alan Weiss, Julio Olalla, Loretta LaRoche. DQ co-host’s “BrainTalk Radio” the 1st Wednesday of the month at 12pm EST. for more information or to listen to archives go to http://www.facebook.com/BrainTalkRadio.

She has been published on the cover of “One Women’s Place Magazine”, on-line, and in various publications throughout New England. She also appears regularly as a speaker at professional associations and conducts webinars and workshops for companies who are looking to bridge the career life balance gap in the workplace.

To find out more about DQ’s Coaching Programs and Services, please visit us online at http://www.CoachDQ.com or www.CareerLIfeBalance.net

Specialties

Coach marketing, advertising and creative professionals to accelerate their career so they can love their life! Customized programs are available for individuals and teams in areas such as: advancing or starting your business, cold calling, pipeline development, changing jobs, career advancement, career change/transition, salary negotiation, resume & job search strategies, time/stress management, life transitions and helping you create more career life balance!
