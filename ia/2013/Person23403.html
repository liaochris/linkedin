Chris Jann, MBA's Summary

Medicus Solutions is a company founded on the premise that healthcare organizations need an IT support company who knows and caters to the unique needs of each and every practice. Medicus does just that by helping practices procure equipment, setup & install, and by providing "best in class" ongoing support of their IT infrastructures. We know healthcare and “We Do IT Right”, the first time. With over 20 years of Healthcare Informatics experience, the Medicus team can help alleviate common bumps when moving to an EMR.

We know healthcare providers, practices, and most of all what they need in regards to technology to run as efficiently as possible while watching the checkbook. We have practices running a wide variety of PM & EMR applications; over 40 today (Practice Management (PM) and Electronic Medical Records (EMR) / Electronic Health Records (EHR) systems). We specialize in the healthcare and small to medium sized businesses. Contact us today to find out how we can help your practice or business!

Specialties: Healthcare Practices, Small to Medium Sized Businesses
