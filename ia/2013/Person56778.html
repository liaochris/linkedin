Jacob Weiss' Summary

Jacob Weiss, PhD, is a social entrepreneur who believes that we can do the most good by being good at what we do, and doing what we love.

He is the director of Playing By Air Productions, an entertainment company that bridges corporate events and social responsibility by donating performances to local causes with each event appearance. He launched Creativity Moves Nashville to inspire, empower, and connect local individuals and organizations to make a difference through a spirit of creativity, entrepreneurship, and giving back to the community.

As a consultant and coach, he works with creative professionals and organizations to transform their careers and brands through new approaches to giving, listening, and community partnerships.

Jacob Weiss' Experience

Director

Creativity Moves

January 2012 – Present (1 year 10 months)

Creativity Moves brings together creative professionals and community leaders around a vision of giving back through creativity. Members include creative professionals, social entrepreneurs, artists, students, event planners, community leaders, philanthropists, and non-profits.

Creativity Moves is an initiative of Playing By Air Productions and High-Wired Communities, in partnership with local leaders.

Director

Playing By Air Productions

January 2009 – Present (4 years 10 months)

Playing By Air Productions offers full-length stage productions, variety acts, and high-energy, creative entertainment for corporate meetings and special events. Event attendees and local communities are given an opportunity to join together through an experience filled with creativity, collaboration, and fun!

With every corporate event performance, Playing By Air donates an appearance to a cancer support non-profit organization. Playing By Air represents the power of collaboration between the arts and medicine, between corporate and non-profit partners, and among community leaders.

Companies, associations, and event planners use Playing By Air's experience and expertise to convey their message and motivate their audiences. Perfect for headline shows, conferences, trade-show exhibits, product launches, special events, promotions, experiential marketing, and team building. The creative team customizes each performance to create a memorable experience for each audience.

High-Wired Communities brings together individuals and organizations who connect, engage, and impact their communities.

Who are our members and partners?

Catch It Early

January 2009 – Present (4 years 10 months)

Catch It Early uses juggling and circus arts to create awareness for the importance of cancer prevention and taking charge of one's own health and life.

Programs include cancer awareness web videos, inspiring performances at half-time shows and public events, juggling + running in local cancer awareness races, and circus workshops for cancer support groups.
