Tamás Horváth's Summary

I am a medical doctor and ENT specialist born in Szombathely, Hungary, now living in Budapest. After graduating on Semmelweis University in 2002, I started my medical career at the Department of Otorhinolaryngology of the Hungarian National Health Center (former University for Postgraduate Medical Training), now I work at the Department of Otorhinolaryngology of Bajcsy Kórház. Middle ear and sinus surgery are my favorite parts in ENT, but inner ear pathophysiology is also in my field of interrest thanks to the molecular inner ear researches that I am taking part since 2005 at the Hungarian Academy of Sciences – Institute for Experimental Medicine.

As the co-founder of the otorhinolaryngology based private clinic ENT House Budapest, my job was to create the online presence for our institute. Since I am a web 2.0 enthusiast, I have built up the brand of ENT House Budapest by using social media tools. I write a professional ENT blog in Hungarian, and run an otorhinolaryngology microblog on Twitter. I am a regular speaker on Hungarian healthcare marketing and communication conferences about social media in healthcare. I won the special prize of Webicina’s “Social Media Story” contest entitling me to a prized speaker spot on Doctors 2.0 & You conference in Paris, May 2012.

I live with my wife and became a proud father of a boy at the end of 2011.

Specialties

Inner ear patho-physiology, middle ear surgery, chronic sinusitis & sinus surgery
