Gary O Shelton's Summary

In 1998 the Rainbow/PUSH Coalition through the Citizenship Education Fund launched the Automotive Project to ensure and promote full and equal participation in the economic growth of the global automotive industry for people of color and women. At the 12th Annual Auto Industry Summit Gary O. Shelton Consulting, after a national search, was recruited to develop strategy for Rainbow PUSH’s new initiative to help Tier 1 suppliers improve their minority supplier development programs.

Gary O. Shelton provides support to major national companies in the area of supplier excellence-diversity. Mr. Shelton works very closely with his clients to customize the development of the program to fit the client’s unique needs. Also, Mr. Shelton has the ability to present the business case of why supplier diversity makes sense. These services can be used to start a new program or expand an existing program. A supplier excellence-diversity program is a multi-faceted platform that helps to create and capture value through a results-oriented approach to supplier development and diversity. The program is flexible, and can be tailored to fit the unique culture and business model of the sponsoring organization.
The program is designed to:
1. Assist the sponsoring agency to increase spend with diverse companies;
2. Expand the base of well-qualified suppliers
3. Expand the base of well-qualified suppliers familiar with emerging technologies, such as Solar
4. Support the efforts of prime contractors to find well-qualified subs
5. Support community small business outreach as part of a
corporate responsibility plan
Gary O. Shelton is a serial entrepreneur; recently formed a collaboration with PerformanceAscent and Coastline Community College Institute for Economic Development to help corporations reduce cost, increase cash flow, margins, speed to market, and profit by improving supplier performance.

Specialties

Leadership--Applying the Highest Levels of Integrity, Innovations and Economic-Trend Insight to the Fulfillment of Core-Focus Objectives that include-Defining/Originating Business Plans and Financing Strategies; Raising Early-Stage Working Capital; Recruiting/Motivating Sales and Management Teams; Allocating Resources; Driving Project Direction and Cohesion; Negotiating Domestic/International Sales and Marketing Agreements; Obtaining Regulatory Clearances; Forecasting/Archiving Profitability.

Gary O Shelton's Experience

Gary O. Shelton Consulting

January 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months)

Gary O. Shelton Consulting (GOSC) is an experienced business strategist, helping entrepreneurs develop a comprehensive approach to major sales strategy that’s a proven winner. Knowing how to get to the decision maker, deal with competition, understand psychology, and service the client--are the keys to success in nailing down the complex sale--that is where you have a long sales cycle (taking several calls to close); it requires a large commitment by the buyer; it has more than one decision maker; and it often involves a long-term relationship between the companies.

GOSC also is an experienced financial planner, helping entrepreneurs perform the task of determining how their business will afford to achieve its strategic goals and objectives. Performing Financial Planning is critical to the success of any organization. It provides the Business Plan with rigor, by confirming that the objectives set are achievable from a financial point of view. It also helps the CEO to set financial targets for the organization.

GOSC is an expert at helping entrepreneurs and CEOs raise capital to pursue their vision by getting them the right money at the right time, on the right terms. After more than four decades of being an entrepreneur, serving as a strategic adviser to entrepreneurs and growing companies, and speaking on entrepreneurial finance, GOSC has found one recurrent theme running through all those businesses: Capital is the lifeblood of a growing business.
