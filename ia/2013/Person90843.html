  • Celebrating Five Years of the Stanford Arts Initiative

    • June 2011 to October 2011
    Team Members: Megan Miller

    The Stanford Arts Initiative celebrated its fifth year with a print and interactive retrospective report. The report featured highlighted stories and developments in the main focus areas of the initiative – strengthening departments, bridging disciplines, fostering student creativity, building new arts facilities, and giving to the arts.

    The print piece features an innovative fold-out format, highlighting story content on one side, and a timeline view of the five years of the initiative on the back side. The interactive version features added video content and a horizontal scrolling interface.

    See more here: http://meganerinmiller.com/portfolio/celebrating-five-years/

  • SiCa Annual Report 2009-2010

    • June 2010 to September 2010
    Team Members: Megan Miller

    The SiCa Annual Report 2009-10 was an unorthodox design, combining a fold-out poster and an interactive website in displaying an interactive infographics report of events, programs, people, and projects from 2009-10. 3,500 copies of the printed poster were delivered to faculty, students, staff, alumni, and donors at Stanford during September 2010.

    See more here: http://meganerinmiller.com/portfolio/webdesign/sica-annual-report-2009-2010/

  • The Arts Guide

    • January 2006 to Present
    Team Members: Megan Miller

    The Arts Guide is a multimedia guide to the arts at Stanford. It is sent to incoming and prospective freshmen each year through the Admissions Office. Each year I coordinate updating the Guide to reflect the 120+ arts organizations on campus. In the beginning, the project was a Flash application sent out on DVD. Then I migrated it to an online database with a custom updater application that allows groups to update their information online. In 2012 I re-built the front-end of the Arts Guide to use WordPress and re-themed to match the new Stanford web design guidelines.
