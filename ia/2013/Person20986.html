Catherine Simpson's Summary

Offering fast, affordable and practical marketing solutions for small businesses, self-employed professionals and start-up companies in the East Midlands through Speed Marketing. Designed to help you win more customers quickly, we have three services to make marketing accessible and effective for your small business:

1. The Marketing Inspector. Like The Hotel Inspector, only for marketing......... and a good starting point to trigger new ideas if you are feeling stuck. A quick-in, quick-out look at your marketing activities and their effectiveness, followed by lots of practical suggestions. Typically done in just half a day for a fixed fee of £100.00

Need marketing advice quickly? Try a one hour Mini Marketing Inspector session by Skype, phone or email. You may have a niggling aspect of your marketing that you are not sure about…..maybe your website isn’t producing results .... perhaps you can't get a brochure right or you may want quick-fire ideas on how to get started with social media? A Mini Marketing Inspector session is just £25.00 and can be arranged to suit you.

2. Your own Virtual Marketing Executive: When you need fast and flexible marketing support, try a Virtual Marketing Executive on a "pay as you go" basis. Have your own dedicated marketing professional for a few hours a week or a day or two a month. Our hourly rate is £25.00 and our day rate is just £200.00.

3. Speed Marketing Plans: Need a Marketing Plan quickly? For just three hours of your time and a fixed fee of £299.00, you get a bespoke Speed Marketing Plan specific to your business containing SMART new marketing ideas and a detailed Action Plan to implement yourself.
