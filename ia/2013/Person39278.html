eBooks 2go's Summary

ebooks2go makes online publishing easy and affordable for independent authors and publishers. If you have a manuscript ready, all you do is simply upload it on our site and we'll take care of the rest. We will convert your manuscript into an eBook (ePUB or Mobi Pocket formats) that can be read on a variety of eReader devices such as Amazon Kindle, Nook, Kobo or iPad.

If you'd like to add audio, video or animation to your book, we have that covered as well. With our expertise in ePUB 3.0, HTML5, Flash and a host of other tools that make enhanced eBooks a cinch, we will take your manuscript to a whole new level with the aforesaid add-on features. If you are looking for eBook apps to make your e-book interactive and further amplified, we can build a superlative eBook app for you. Whether you are simply looking for eBook conversion services or a partner to help you right from conversion to worldwide distribution of your ebook through our retail partners, our dedicated team will leave no stone unturned in making it happen for you.

Your bestseller is merely a click away. Get in touch with us for a comprehensive action plan to help create and distribute your e-book today!
