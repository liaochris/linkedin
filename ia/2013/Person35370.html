Dick Boren's Summary

After 25 years of highly successful technology sales, sales leadership and executive management, I elected to make a career change and enter the Real Estate field.

In a few short years I have established my self as a top producer, leader, and expert in the field. I am a licensed Real Estate Broker in the state of California and I am a Realtor, a Graduate of the Real Estate Institute (GRI), an EPro, an Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR), and a Buffini Certified Mentor.

While with the Select Group, in addition to my responsibilities as a Branch Leader, I drove the implementation of the Select Group's internet marketing platform, "AIMS" (Agent Internet Marketing Platform), as well as driving other key technology driven business development projects and business practices.

In 2009 Melissa and I started the Boren Group and have established a highly successful practice serving a select group of clients who are looking for the highest possible level of professional, yet personal services.

Specialties

Real Estate Technology Evangelist, Realtor, Residential Real Estate Services, Online MLS searches, Real Estate Investment. First Time Home buyers, First Time Real Estate Investors, Short Sales, Foreclosures, Bank Owned, REO
