Dr. Michael Lange, Optometrist's Summary

Fortifeye Vitamins
Dr. Michael Lange, optometrist believes strongly in nutritional intervention, having long understood the nutritional connection to healthy eyesight. As a result he has developed nutrient-based vitamin formulas (Fortifeye Vitamins) that are currently being used throughout the world by eye doctors. These formulas are a combination of synergistically blended whole foods, phyto-nutrients, vitamins, enzymes and essential fatty acids. When combined with proper diet and exercise, these vitamins can help.

"These vitamins help to slow down free radical damage and thus the disease process in the eye and body," Michael P. Lange, an optometrist with Lange Eye Care in Ocala stated. "These vitamins help with total body wellness. And if you give the body the nutrients it requires through supplement and proper diet, it will slow down degenerative diseases in the eye."

Michael Lange OD, founder and chief optometric physician at Lange Eye Care, travels throughout the world to speak with eye care providers and the public about the nutritional importance for healthy eyesight individually ,in groups and at large conferences. In 2010 Michael Lange Optometrist spent weeks in Norway researching omega three fatty acids from squid, krill and different species of fish, as a result of this research trip and many other studies he formulated Fortifeye Super Omega the latest in triglyceride form omega three fish oil supplements. Dr. Lange then started an omega index study utilizing the new formula he has created . This omega index blood study has proven that two per day dosing of Fortifeye Super Omega will bring the average Americans omega index over 9.1%, this level has true positive health benefits. Michael Lange OD,CNS is developing a zinc free eye vitamin for macular degeneration in response to a recent study proving that patients with specific genotypes may be harmed by zinc. Zinc free Fortifeye out Late September 2013. Michael Lange OD, CNS.

Specialties

Dr. Michael Lange optometrist with Lange Eye Care in ocala Florida is an expert in Nutritional eye care , primary eye care, ocular disease diagnosis and treatment, advanced contact lens fitting and design, corneal refractive therapy, nutritional intervention and research for macular degeneration, development of ocular and whole body vitamins, low vison and vision rehabilitation, pre and post operative mangement of Lasik surgery, cataract surgery and cosmetic facial surgery.
