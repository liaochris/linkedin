Lea Richards' Summary

Building strong relationships with customers, key members of the press, and other businesses with similar goals is one of Lea's greatest accomplishments and continues to lie at the forefront of her goals. Hoping to build even stronger relationships going forward, she has already successfully partnered with Good Morning America, The Rachel Ray Show, Gilt Taste, Fab.com and Food and Wine magazine.

Founding and executing marketing initiatives and public relations outreach, she has both created companies and led them to new and exciting levels of sales and growth with her out of the box creative thinking that produces results on a shoe string budget.

Understanding the importance of a strong online presence in the business development stages, Lea has successfully helped create, build, and contribute to the social media efforts for numerous organizations, including her own national gourmet foods company. She continues to find innovative ways of reaching consumers and teaching businesses how to effectively reach their target audience through social media and internet marketing techniques.
