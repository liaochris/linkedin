Nic Meliones' Honors and Awards

  • Winner AT&T and GM Connected Car Challenge

    AT&T and GM
    • January 2013

    Received 1st place in AT&T and GM's Connected Car Challenge at the AT&T Developer's Conference on January 6, 2013, for QuickTrip. QuickTrip is an app for busy families that connects their phones with their car's smart system and uses a "Heads Up Display" unit to project driving directions on the front windshield of your car. The driver's eyes stay on the road while the GPS updates dynamically based on new locations added by the family.

    We built on GM's SDK, AT&T's Call Management API, and Windows Azure Mobile Services.

  • Winner Best Mobile Commerce App at Sprint Developer's Conference Hackathon

    Sprint and Sequent
    • October 2012

    Received 1st place in Best Mobile Commerce App Challenge at the Sprint Developer's Conference Hackathon on October 24, 2012 for purchACE. The purchACE android app used NFC technology to scan Kovio NFC tags on items in a store and add them to your shopping cart.

  • Winner Bitcoin 2013 Hackathon

    Boost VC
    • May 2013

    Received 1st place in the Bitcoin 2013 Hackathon hosted by Boost VC on May 19, 2013, for BitWall. Micropayments from traditional payment methods are unprofitable due to high transaction fees. BitWall allows digital content providers to easily monetize their content through Bitcoin micropayments.

    We built on the Coinbase API.

    Read more details about BitWall on the Coinbase Blog here: http://blog.coinbase.com/post/50890389277/bitwall-winner-of-the-2013-bitcoin-conference

  • Winner NewAer Inside Proximity Challenge at TechCrunch Disrupt 2013 Hackathon

    TechCrunch and NewAer
    • September 2013

    Won 1st place in NewAer's Inside Proximity Challenge at TechCrunch Disrupt Hackathon on September 8, 2013 for Style Stalk. Style Stalk is the easiest way to find and purchase great items that someone nearby is wearing, in real-time through your phone. Through the NewAer Inside Proximity Platform, Style Stalk let's users quickly identify a nearby fashion guru. The ShopSense API allows the user to easily purchase those items, giving the fashion guru a referral commission for the purchase.

    Technology:
    -NewAer's Inside Proximity Platform SDK
    -ShopStyle API (POPSUGAR)

    More info on Style Stalk: https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/techcrunch-disrupt-sf-2013/hacks/style-stalk
