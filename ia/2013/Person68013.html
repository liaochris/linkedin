Jonny Allbut's Summary

I am a true multi-disciplined designer with over 15 years experience of working with industry standard software. I have the proven skills to deliver what you and your clients demand – the highest quality output, within budget and on time.

As of November 2011, I have taken the role of Director and Head of Digital/Creative at Tanc Design based in Birmingham. I've created work for some of the most recognised brands in the UK and welcome the opportunity of discussing your next project.

I have also led presentations at various conferences since 2008 on web design, WordPress and freelance business practices, including a number of times at WordCamp - the UK WordPress conference, The Digital Barn, Multipack and various smaller scale web development meetups.

Specialties: Creative design for branding, print and the Internet. Website coding and bespoke WordPress theme and plugins.
