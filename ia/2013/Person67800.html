Jon Clements' Summary

I am a Chartered B2B PR, corporate and marketing communications consultant with more than 16 years' PR agency experience, based in Manchester, North West England, and am the founder of Metamorphic PR.

My sector specialisms include B2B PR and corporate/marketing communications for companies and professional member organisations in insurance, health and safety, financial services, commercial motor, petrochemical and chemical industries, engineering, manufacturing, business and consumer technology.

I've been responsible for the B2B public relations and corporate/marketing communications activities of numerous market leading blue chip companies based both nationally and with operations in the North West, including Aviva, RAC, Cunningham Lindsey, the Chartered Insurance Institute, Shell, Tata Chemicals Europe and Hertel.

My main areas of expertise include:

- Public relations and marketing communications
- Corporate reputation building and protection
- Journalist and media relations
- Devising communications strategies
- Generating creative campaign ideas
- Editorial content creation both online and offline
- Executive media training
- Social media management, training and guidance
- Mentoring and developing junior PR executives
- News, feature and comment writing
- Local community relations and crisis communications
- Internal communications
- Blog post writing and optimising online content
