Hector Quiroga's Summary

Hector works with his wife, Casey Quiroga, in Spokane Valley, Washington. Together, they founded the Quiroga Law Office, PLLC. They practice bankruptcy, family law, personal injury, real estate, and estate planning. Hector also practices immigration law.

Hector seeks amicable resolution of all legal disputes to avoid costly legal bills for his clients. However, if an agreement cannot be reached, he will take the matter to trial and the court of appeals if necessary.

Specialties

The Washington State Bar Association do not allow attorneys to claim "specialties" if they do not have an advance certification on those areas. Linkedin has not change this for legal practitioners. Below are Hectors areas of practice (not specialties):

- Bankruptcy and Family Law Litigator
- Insurance negotiation and arbitration for Coverage, Total Loss, and Injury Claims
- Immigration adviser for Refugee resettlement, Parolee Status, and Citizenship and Naturalization Process
