Ash Tanasiychuk's Summary

As an artist, I love expression and the freedom to do so however I wish.
As a designer, I love communication and the principles that enable me to do so effectively.

With these loves as my focus,
I aim to unite fine arts with media production.

Specialties

DRAWING: Charcoal and graphite. Realism and abstract.

PHOTOGRAPHY: Urban scenes. Macro. Natural light. Movement.

GRAPHIC DESIGN: Proficient in both Mac and PC.
Extensive experience with Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash, Premiere Pro.

WRITING: Nonfiction and fiction.

GENERAL: I am meticulous; an excellent editor, both written and visual (quickly spot errors/discrepancies).
I am eager to inject vitality and originality into every project I work on.

April 2007 – Present (6 years 6 months) Vancouver, Canada Area

Custom workshops in Adobe Flash, Illustrator, and other graphic design applications.

The intention of these workshops is to introduce artists to the creative possibilities of various design applications such as Flash. In Flash, one can create animated movies from scratch, or import graphics, photos, video, and audio created with other programs and use Flash to animate them. The participants will learn about Flash painting and drawing tools (and how they differ from other programs), creating symbols (the three types, and their characteristics), interactivity (including an intro to Actionscript, Flash's programming language), publishing for web, and more.
