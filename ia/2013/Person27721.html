Jeff Colburn's Experience

Owner - Jeff Colburn Fine Art Prints and Stock Photos Arizona

Stock Photos Arizona

January 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months)

Hello, my name is Jeff Colburn, a professional photographer for over 35 years. At JeffColburn.com you'll find Fine Art prints of Arizona, showing the beauty and wonder of this amazing state. These prints are made with the finest paper and dyes, and can last up to 98 years when displayed under glass. Up to 200 years when stored in an album. Head on over and take a look, http://www.JeffColburn.com

At http://www.StockPhotosArizona.com I have many stock photographs of Arizona for you to use. These images include Indian Ruins, Plants, Flagstaff, Jerome, Sedona, Leaves Changing Colors, Rainbows in the Red Rocks of Sedona and much more. Find the stock images you need at http://www.StockPhotosArizona.com

Have Fun,
Jeff

* Ran my own stock photography agency, StockPhotosArizona.com, which features images from Arizona. And Fine Art prints through JeffColburn.com
* Coordinated studio and location photography projects to meet the needs of marketing departments and advertising agencies.
* Evaluated portfolios for several photographers, which allowed them to acquire more photography assignments.
* Wrote, and published, several articles on photography for print and online publications.
* I've been interviewed and quoted by several writers working on photography articles.
