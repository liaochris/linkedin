Katie McCartney's Summary

My name is Katie McCartney and I have been a real estate consultant for more than five years. Prior to that, I was in business and IT for more than seven years. I know there is a lot to navigating the process of buying or selling your home and my passion is helping others understand the financial, legal and logistical details. I also have a passion for Columbus small businesses growth.

I know you are busier than ever, so I take full advantage of all the resources available to real estates professionals today including digital media and minute-by-minute updated listings. I am active in the Columbus community and enjoy getting to meet new people through TechColumbus, the Worthington Camber and other community groups. I enjoy building on those relationships and establishing trust - as I really become a close part of a person’s life when they consider the important decision of buying or selling a home.

I also am the easiest person to get in touch with so just send smoke signals, connect with me on any social media platform or visit KatieMcCartney.com.

Specialties: Real Estate, Consulting, Marketing, Branding, Technology, Project Management, Communication, Negotiations
