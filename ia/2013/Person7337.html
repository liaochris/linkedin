Andy Aldridge BSc(Hons), MBA, DipM, ICIOB's Summary

With over 25 years experience in construction and manufacturing industries I have been priviledged to assist 100's of Client organisations achieve maximum results, for the ultimate goal of improving profitability. I have a passion for business improvement and creating profitable growth for my Clients.

Experienced in running projects upto £25M in value I have been a Business Owner for over 19 years. Not only am I a well seasoned Business Improvement Consultant, Coach & Mentor but have been an Investors in People Practitioner (Assessor/Adviser) and Midlands Business Excellence Assessor but am passionate about construction and manufacturing. The buzz that comes from actually building or making something is a feeling like no other ! Having studied at the European Centre for Total Quality Management (TQM), I am an advocate of the TQM philosophy which reflects in everything that I do. I now divide my business time between three principle interests ....... business consultancy, construction and developing a commercial and residential property portfolio.

Specialties: Business Planning, Strategy, Business Improvement, KPI's, Construction, Constructing Excellence, Low Carbon Construction, Development, Manufacturing, Consultancy, Advisory, E-Procurement, Project Management, Tendering & Bid Writing, Winning Work, Doing Work Efficiently, Making Profit, Leadership & Management, Growth, Non-Exec Director,

Rider (Domestic)

V-Sprint Track Cycling Team

September 2011 – Present (2 years)

Check us out at:- www.v-sprintracing.com

Having riden the Velodrome for the first time in October 2010 with no experience of cycle racing, I was hooked within an hour ! I knew racing around my parents lawn on my push bike when I was a kid would come in handy one day - shame I left it over 35 years to find out ! After achieving track rider accreditation with my daughter Lucy in April 2011 we spent Friday nights in the summer developing our skills with the help of 4 great coaches - John, Jeff, Sue and Paul. I was then invited by Mark Thomson to join the newly formed V-Sprint track cycling team as their 2nd Domestic Rider in September 2011 and competing for them in the Manchester Regional Friday Night Track League in the Autumn of 2011. With a surprising top 15 place finish out 100+ riders in my first half season riding at the December 2011 break, I was asked to compete in the World Masters Track Championships - October 2012 in Manchester !

Joke, it was not ! I will be riding Man 3 for V-Sprint, in the Team Sprint with Mark Thomson and Jeff Winstanley who have been a great help and support to me in the last 12 months. Clearly, for us and our families Manchester 2012 rather than London will be our Olympics ! With the support and help from V-Sprint Pro-Racing Riders such as Dave Daniell and Pete Mitchell (who ride for GB) we are confident upon achieving a good result in October 2012. The experiences of riding, training and competing, meeting new friends in 2011 has been brilliant - however 2012 is likely to prove to be awesome !

Without doubt Track Cycling has become my passion - but what's even better I am more physically and mentally fit than I have ever been !
