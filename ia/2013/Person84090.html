Tara Clapper's Summary

I remember the first time I saw my work in print. It was a letter to the editor, and once I got over the fact that my name and opinion were being read by hundreds of others, I discovered why I loved publishing: I was educating others with the quality of my content.

Today, I write, edit, and prepare everything from how-to articles to sonnets, and my goal has never changed. I want to help others, whether that means providing information or improving moods.

-Noted for engaging interviews with music and TV personalities such as Cesar Millan, James McCartney, and Paige Davis.
-Proficient writer and copyeditor in AP, APA, and MLA styles.
-Myers-Briggs Personality Type: INFJ
-Interest in creating and maintaining progressive, eco-friendly, community-supportive, ethical business environment.
-Typing speed of 116 WPM.
-Working knowledge of current world events, history, and literature.
-Ability to bridge the concrete and the abstract, making publishing products tangible.
-Served as developmental editor in a freelance capacity for over a dozen independently authored novels.

Specialties: Editing, copyediting, proofreading, data entry, customer service, process document creation, training, management, project management, business contracts, magazine writing, tutoring, SEO optimization, marketing, adcopy, online content, developmental editing, WordPress, content management, Blogger
