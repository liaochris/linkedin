Mark Kern's Summary

I have had the opportunity to work in several industries in different types of organizations.

My background includes employment in Logistics with Kmart Corporation which led to a position as a management consultant supporting middle market manufacturers and distribution companies. Supporting these companies allowed me to strengthen my technology skills; allowing me to transition to a role supporting businesses from an IT perspective. My final contract as a IT contractor with Advantagetech allowed me the opportunity to transition to a permanent position with Sprint. What a great time to be involved with product development and finance at a telecommunications company; experiencing the growth and maturing of the mobile industry! But all good things must come to an end.

The last couple of years I have been working for the IRS and learning more about taxes then I ever thought I needed to know. It is a great opportunity to work in public service and to interact with my fellow taxpayers on a daily basis.

Always one to enjoy new challenges, I have been learning web development. Over the last 7 years I have created websites for both myself and clients. I started developing in MS Frontpage many years ago and transitioned to other Microsoft technologies such as .Net. Recently, I have concentrated on creating sites in WordPress. By utilizing my HTML and CSS skills, I am able to create unique, search engine friendly sites personalized to convey the brand image of the business or organization that I am supporting. By implementing social media strategies and managing my Google AdWords account, I have been able to increase engagement and drive traffic to my sites.

To make a long story short, you could describe my skills in 3 separate areas:

1) Web Development
2) Analysis and Project Management
3) Finance

I'd love to hear about new opportunities or just get back in touch with those that I have worked with in the past.
