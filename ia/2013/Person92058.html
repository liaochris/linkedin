Michael Baker's Summary

I'm a polymath. I have many and varied professional interests. These include Joint Programme Director with the Centre for Sustainability and Environmental Management where I also teach a courses on Life Cycle Assessment, Disaster Management & Preparedness, Management of Oil & Gas Exploration, and Energy Policy and Management in Business and manage the Centre's website. I have previous experience as a software engineer and project manager.

I am currently available for short term consultancy and advisory roles on Environmental Sustainability projects.

Specialties

Environmental Sustainability, Life Cycle Assessment (LCA), Consultancy, Teaching, Project Management, Management Systems, Energy Research, Civil Engineering, Town Planning
