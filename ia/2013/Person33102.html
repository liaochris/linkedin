Chris Turner's Summary

Twenty successful years as a reporter, sports editor, overseas correspondent, media relations manager and director of communications. Golfer, mountain biker and Formula 1 racing junkie.

Goal: To help people and organizations communicate better. We do this by building relationships with our clients to determine their needs and then determine the most effective way to support their initiatives through unique communications strategies.

A full list of the services we offer can be found at www.dchristurner.com.

Specialties: Media Relations, Working with C-Level executives, Speech writing, Crisis Communications, Leading Generation Y employees, Writing in various styles, Media Training, Excellent people skills, Strategic planning, Social Media, Press releases, Podcasting, Strong verbal & presentation skills, Internal communications, Spanish Speaker
