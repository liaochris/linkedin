Aaron Neumann's Summary

NE Minneapolis Realtor with a background as a community and political organizer with substantial media, writing, and social networking experience. Listens and relates well to diverse groups of people from across all backgrounds and operates with integrity.

Interests:

Just started a new career as a Realtor with RE/MAX Results, with a focus on NE Mpls. Beforehand I worked as the Outreach Coordinator for the Holland Neighborhood Improvement Association (HNIA). My job required everything from organizing block clubs, meetings and events, working with local businesses and homeowners, to rolling up the sleeves in the community garden or tot-lot. Some of my political organizing experience includes working for Congressman Ellison (MN-05) in the District Office and being a key member of the successful Reich for Ward 1 Minneapolis City Council campaign. A true renaissance man, I'm also an artist and musician. My art is in prehistoric styles and techniques as an homage to the world's first art and artists. I create pictographs/petroglyphs on stone, instruments inspired by artifacts, and imagined music from the paleolithic and neolithic cultures. I also play a variety of music in modern genres.
