Alan Pugh's Summary

After various entry-level jobs, I began my career with Best Buy at their Sandusky, Ohio retail store in July of 2003. Since that time, I have worked in several positions related to sales and service and in several locations in Ohio, as well as time in Minnesota and in a traveling role as a member of a District Project Team. A large portion of the time was spent with the Geek Squad brand, servicing technology clients with setup and repair of computers, smartphones, networks and various electronics.

I became the Online Experience Planner for Exclusive Brands at Best Buy in September of 2011. In this role, I created strategy for the Insignia, Rocketfish, Dynex and Init websites and social properties. I focused on the importance of social media as the centerpiece of marketing and remaining relevant to core demographics, and I continue to seek opportunities to apply my passion and knowledge in this field.

Most recently, I have moved to Quicken Loans and returned to my roots in IT. As a desktop technician, I diagnose and repair issues related to computers, networks, A/V, wireless headsets and phone systems in a corporate environment. Myself and another technician handle support for six hundred employees in Cleveland and Pittsburgh on Windows 7, 8, Mac OS X, Android, iPhone, and Blackberry devices along with IP phone and VPN support.

Specialties: Client interaction, technical skills, social media, client escalation resolution, innovation, efficiency
