Marla Gunasegaram's Summary

Marla is a web designer, site developer, and interface expert currently living and working in North San Diego County, California. She is the Principal and Creative Lead of Marla G Web Design.

Marla has over 10 years of web design experience. Her career started with internships while enrolled at UCLA obtaining her masters degree in Information Science. At UCLA she specialized in Information Architecture, Interface Design and Usability Engineering. She has created web sites for a number of large and small educational institutions, small business, non-profits and professional independent consultants.

Marla creates beautiful, professional, and above all else, usable websites that comply with the latest web standards.
