Alli Worthington's Summary

I am a connector of great people and big ideas. I help people build things that matter.

Lover of bootstrapped startups, big hairy audacious goals (hat tip to Jim Collins), heart and hustle.

My Focus-
Helping entrepreneurs and business owners build successful businesses and live life with meaning.

My Startups-
In 2008 I founded Blissfully Domestic Magazine as well as co-founded the largest international women's blogging and social media conference- BlissDom Events.

Where I Advise-
I served on the Intel Social Media Advisory Board and serve as an advisor to the NGO Safeworld.

Where I Write-
1. AlliWorthington.com- On building a successful business.
2. This is Alli, Ranked #33 in the Top 100 Blogs list, is a column on the Disney site, Babble.com.
3. Business- My articles on the Citibank property, Women & Co. on personal finance, technology, productivity and career advice has been featured on the Huffington Post.


Who I Help-
Fortune 500 companies, international humanitarian organizations, small business owners and individual entrepreneurs are among those I've been lucky enough to help. Expertise in digital marketing, branding, digital strategy, experiential marketing and transmedia storytelling.

Speaking engagement topics range from business (social media best practices, building successful communities, social media marketing, starting your own business) to life development.

Specialties: Event Production, Social Media, Native Advertising, Content Marketing, Integrated Media Campaigns, Business Process Improvement, Blogging, Marketing to Women, Branding, Digital Strategy, Startups, Transmedia, Social Good Promotions, Visual Storytelling, Public Speaking, Digital Storytelling, Digital Trends, Email Marketing, Blogger Outreach, Entrepreneurial Coaching
