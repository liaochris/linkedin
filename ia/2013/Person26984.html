Corey Cook's Summary

Corey is a Director and Founder of 3lytics where he is responsible for strategy, implementation and software development. Over his career he has worked on a variety of areas including strategy, consulting, acquisition integration, valuation, software design and implementation. He has worked internationally in Australia, Canada, United States, and the United Kingdom in a variety of senior roles. Corey has extensive experience in capital markets and industry sector knowledge in metals and mining, oil and gas, agriculture, transport, industrials and technology.

Corey has a keen interest in conflict resolution within the finance industry and is one of the first CFA charterholders to also become an accredited mediator in 2011 with the Centre for Effective Dispute Resolution (“CEDR”). He has attended The Program on Negotiation at Harvard Law School and also holds an advanced certificate in negotiation from CEDR. Corey is a Trustee for the McGill University UK foundation, and a is committee member for Goodenough College and the CFA Society of the UK.
