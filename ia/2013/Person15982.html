Blake Puryear's Summary

I am currently a business analyst working at Acumen Brands, focused on user experience and agile software processes.

I have a varied array of skills that run the gamut from user experience, software architecture, social media planning and strategy, software development and client facing consulting.

I studied Computer Science at the University of Arkansas. I focused in business area applications of software, mobile application development and database applications.

My biggest passion lies in the realm of user experience and user interface design. I spend time daily in the UI/UX realm and keep up with current web, mobile and desktop design trends. With this UI/UX obsession comes an eye for industrial design and a critical outlook on product design in everyday life.

I am also passionate about Social Media and the impact that networking can have on the realm of technology. I am "wired in" to most popular social media networks and am frantically taking notes on who is doing things correctly and what users want out of their experience.
