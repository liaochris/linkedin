Joan Otto's Summary

I love what I do. I help a variety of organizations improve their digital presence and build community.

Primarily, I'm the project and community manager for Man Vs. Debt, an amazing personal finance and lifestyle design company run by one of my heroes, Adam Baker. Our flagship websites, www.manvsdebt.com and www.youvsdebt.com, help a thriving community of thousands improve their lives under the motto "Sell your crap. Pay off your debt. Do what you love."

I'm also the online editor for my hometown news provider, the York (Pa.) Daily Record/Sunday News (www.ydr.com). There, I bring more than 12 years of experience in community journalism, social media and streamlined content management to bear in a role that allows me to constantly drive new changes to our digital offerings across many platforms while engaging the community. I write daily about life in our town at my blog, Only in York County (www.yorkblog.com/onlyyork).

In both these positions, I help my team members succeed and I help my community share its stories. What could be better?
