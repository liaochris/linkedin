Mike J Quinn's Summary

I am a husband and father in a multi-national family. For over 6 years I have been trying to unite us all under the citizenship of my home country: the U.S.A. It's been a long, and difficult process, with no sign of a solution.

I have consulted several attorneys and have spent a great deal of time and money trying to keep my family together, in what really should be a simple and straight-forward procedure. I'm an American Citizen; I should be able to have the family of my own choosing.

This is the United States of America, and as a nation we actively support citizens rights in countries all over the world. Too bad we have been slow to do this for our own citizens, almost all of whom: are immigrants, were immigrants, or are descended from immigrants.

Aside from being born and raised in California, I've been a restaurant manager for over 25 years and have worked employing citizens from many different countries, with many different immigration statuses, and I'm familiar with all the forms and procedures regarding immigrant employee requirements, and corporate responsibility.

I've written a book titled, "America Needs A DREAM," about how the DREAM Act is good for America and disproves the myths being paraded around as facts regarding this subject.

I'm also finishing a novel titled, "The Dishwasher's Son." It's about a young Arizona border guard volunteer who accidentally gets deported to Mexico when the restaurant he works in gets raided by ICE. Since his photo and fingerprints now declare him to be a Mexican who has recently been deported, he must resort to getting back home using all the methods he has been learning to defend against as an Arizona Minuteman volunteer

With my background I feel I'm uniquely qualified to be writing a book about how the border between Mexico and the US affects a typical American family.
