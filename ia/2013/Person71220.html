Justin Hunter's Experience

Founder & CEO

Hexawise

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

February 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months)

I founded Hexawise, Inc. to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of software testing by making powerful test design techniques accessible and easy to use.

Software testers use the Hexawise software test design tool to identify tests that achieve higher coverage in fewer tests. Hexawise-generated tests are both much faster to create and far more effective at identifying defects than standard, manually-selected sets of tests.

Typical users of Hexawise are the people within IT organizations who are responsible for software test design and project management (e.g., generally the people who document and manage sets of test scripts). Additional Hexawise users include Software Testers, Business Analysts, Developers, and Senior-Level IT stakeholders.

Benefits generated by Hexawise include:

(1) Time and cost savings from faster test case design (because it typically takes about half as long to generate and document test cases using Hexawise as compared to selecting and documenting test cases by hand),

(2) Time and cost savings from faster test execution (because it often takes only about half as long to execute a smaller number of more powerful Hexawise-generated tests as compared to a less thorough set of manually selected set of tests), and

(3) Decreased costs from improved quality and the ability to find defects earlier in the development life cycle.

Hexawise is used for black box and grey box testing on applications with any kind of underlying code language, and is used during Functional Testing, User Acceptance Testing, Assembly Testing, Systems Integration Testing, Product Testing, Configuration Testing, etc.

Hexawise is used by testers in every major industry. Case studies of Hexawise are available at http://www.hexawise.com/case-studies
