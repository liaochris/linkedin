Garland Van Dyke's Summary

My efforts for you!

A caring, spiritual person, with years of experience in training, business practices, programming, writing, development, mediation, speaking, radio, and most importantly to me, preaching! I am about helping others achieve their goals through spiritual applications.

I have a Biblical message of: "It's takes an extraordinary mind change to produce an extraordinary life." Check out Romans 12:2! It's a great message of how to rise to the next level in life, business, and spiritual life. It's a message worth taking to heart!

My goal is to help as many people as I can improve their life by using Biblical techniques and helping folks achieve the proper place in the afterlife.

You can see my written material at www.garlandvandyke.com.

Specialties: Mediation, Public Speaking, Writing, Training, Development, Preaching!
