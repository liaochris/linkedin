Jonni Biaggini's Summary

I love what I do. Through marketing, I encourage and facilitate the process of moving a company from sedentary to trend setter, from ordinary to extraordinary. And when the extraordinary happens to a company, to a group, or to an individual, nothing beats it!

In my 30+ years in the tourism industry, I have provided marketing services for destinations as well as individual hotels. I honed my destination marketing skills in Snowmass Village, Colorado and then established and led the Visitors and Conference Bureau for the County of San Luis Obispo, California for 22 years. I've also developed and implemented marketing plans for hotels in Aspen, Snowmass Village and Vail, Colorado, Cambria, Pismo Beach and Morro Bay, California and Bandon, Oregon.

With a degree in marketing and through my memberships in organizations like HSMAI, MPI, CALTIA, and CALSAE, I have sharpened my skills in market analysis, media planning, social media networks, and website development
.
My marketing programs have resulted in recognition statewide, having received the "Best Overall Tourism Marketing" award from the California Office of Tourism and more recently awarded "Public Official of the Year" by the California Lodging Industry Association.

Specialties: strategic marketing plans, organizational plans, social media initiatives, website development, business model development, interactive marketing, public relations, budget management

Jonni Biaggini's Organizations

  • Morro Bay Harbor Festival

    Vice President, Board of Directors
    • January 2012 to Present

    MBHF is one of the oldest on-going special events in San Luis Obispo County. It's a fun and educational event that generates tourism to Morro Bay by bringing focus to the working fishing harbor and community. It also gives back - the many volunteer non-profit organizations that help to put the festival on receive money from working the event. It's a win-win!

  • California Travel Industry Association (CALTIA)

    Board of Director
    • June 2000 to June 2004

  • Morro Bay Business Forum

    Facilitator
    • July 2008 to June 2009

    Founded by Ed Biaggini III, the Morro Bay Business Forum is a totally volunteer business/economic advocacy group. Ed Biaggini wanted to create a gathering place for the business community, government employees, elected officials, and the community at large to openly discuss topics that affect the economic well being of Morro Bay. Meetings are held weekly with at least one guest speaker followed by open discussion guided by the Forum facilitator.

    Proudly, this organization is still going strong thanks to the many volunteers who believe in the same principles and goals and love for Morro Bay as Ed Biaggini III.
