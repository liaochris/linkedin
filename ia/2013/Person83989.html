Jim MacNee's Summary

Jim brings to his clients over ten years experience in retained executive search. Jim founded Maitland Partners Executive Search to be a premier retained executive search firm serving clients nationwide. Successfully serving a diverse client base, placements have been made in the medical device, hospitality, distribution, gaming, technology, manufacturing, financial services, tribal and aerospace industries.

Jim began his career in search in 1999 and has placed executives in leadership roles in finance, operations, general management, sales & marketing, distribution management, HR and engineering. Jim is particularly adept at understanding the cultural needs of the client and identifying the best-qualified candidates to lead the organization.

Prior to that Jim was President of Manchester Plastics, a manufacturer of extruded sheet products. General Manager of Bear Medical Systems, an international medical device company that designed, manufactured and distributed adult and infant critical care ventilators and Vice President, Human Resources and Corporate Officer for Allied Healthcare Products, Inc.

Jim started his career working for Chrysler Corporation in HR / labor relations.

Jim has an undergraduate degree in psychology from Albion College and earned a graduate degree from Washington University in St. Louis. Jim has taught Organizational Development for the University of Phoenix MBA Program and speaks on Leadership, Job Search Skills, Social Media & Change Management at conferences nationwide.

Jim has served as the PIHRA District 11 Careers Chair and has been involved in SHRM since the late 1980’s.

Jim and his wife have three adult children.
