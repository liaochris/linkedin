Dr. Dean Burke's Summary

Kenneth "Dean" Burke, MD, is currently serving his first term in the Georgia State Senate, District 11, after being elected in a special election in February 2013. He serves as Vice-Chairman of the Senate Retirement Committee. He also serves on the Health and Human Services, Agriculture and Consumer Affairs, and K-12 Education Committees.

He is currently employed by Memorial Hospital and Manor as it's Chief Medical Officer/Medical Director. His responsibilities include quality,utilization, medical staff affairs, and employed physicians and physician practices.

Prior to this position he served as a Co-owner of Burke and Robinson M.D. P.C.as a practicing gynecologist.

Dean is also an author "The Millionaire Nurse: A Proven Financial First-Aid Kit for Building Wealth and Achieving Freedom."

Dr Burke is a Fellow of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists.

He graduated Summa Cum Laude from Georgia Southwestern University and then graduated from Medical College of Georgia in Augusta, Georgia. He completed specialty training at Mercer University School of Medicine.

Kenneth Dean Burke, MD graduated Summa Cum Laude from Georgia Southwestern University and then graduated from Medical College of Georgia in Augusta, Georgia. He completed specialty training at Mercer University School of Medicine.

Dr. Burke is certified by the American Board of Obstetrics and Gynecology and a Fellow of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists. He is married and has two children. Dr. Burke has been in practice in Bainbridge since 1985. He has a special interest in hormonal management of menopause and skin care.

Choosing a Gynecologist is one of the most important and intimate decisions that a woman can ever make. Our trained staff will make you feel comfortable and will gladly answer any questions you may have.

You will find that we offer the best quality, professional, and friendly primary health care for women that you will encounter anywhere. Drs. Burke and Robinson, along with Claire Wells, PA, have a practice focused on Gynecology and the Primary Health Care of Women. Our physicians are indeed specialty trained in the health care of women. This applies to wellness care and health promotion, as well as expert care in the unique ways to assess and treat medical and surgical conditions in women. We are especially aware that women are not just variations of men, but that each woman is an individual. We must care for each woman, keeping in mind the woman's femininity and sexuality. From the time of the first menstrual period through the elder years, each of our patients can be assured that our goal is to maximize their full potential as healthy women and insure that problems are treated early and effectively. This is our pledge to you.
