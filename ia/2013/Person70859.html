Julie Penfold's Summary

Freelance journalist and copywriter specialising in everything health and wellbeing. I'm passionate about health writing, absolutely love what I do and have never missed a deadline in over ten years of being a scribe.

My editorial clients include consumer titles Natural Health, Women's Fitness, Benhealth, Your Healthy Living, Asda, LighterLife, Spirit & Destiny, Expert Beauty and Australian title Nature & Health. Additionally, I regularly write for several specialist healthcare titles including Primary Health Care, Practice Business, Nursing Children & Young People, Learning Disability Today and Mental Health Today.

Just drop me a line at julie@juliepenfold.co.uk for further information...

Specialties: Health Journalist, Health Writer, Freelance Journalist, Features, News, Writing, Copywriting, Editing

Julie Penfold's Experience

Freelance Journalist & Copywriter

Self - Employed

October 2008 – Present (4 years 10 months)

Writing on health, nutrition, wellbeing and lifestyle for a range of national consumer magazines. Additionally, I write on nursing and social care issues for several Royal College of Nursing titles and Pavilion titles such as Learning Disability Today.

My copywriting and commercial writing services include creating content from scratch, refreshing and editing existing content, producing copy for: case studies, charity and contract magazines, websites, newsletters, reports, advertising, blogs, leaflets and brochures. Tasks include features and advertorial writing, providing health content for charity publications, newsletter writing and copy rewriting. Clients include charities such as the British Lung Foundation, Alzheimer's Society, Depression Alliance and Diabetes UK.
