Bronwen Kennedy's Summary

Bronwen Kennedy is the Founder and Creative Director of Spring 13, a boutique creative marketing agency based in Washington, DC. With nearly two decades of agency experience, her role is not simply to oversee and lead the agency’s endeavors, but to energize and inspire its people to think and create in new ways.

Founded in 2006, Spring 13 was born out of Bronwen’s desire to deliver fresh ideas, top-notch talent, and flexible, scalable solutions to her clients. Her drive and desire to bring winning ideas to life is seen and experienced in all aspects of her work — from her creative team and her clients, to their unique brands and their targeted audiences.

Prior to Spring 13, Bronwen served as a lead creative at major agencies that include RTC Relationship Marketing, Young & Rubicam, and Earle Palmer Brown. She’s lent her expertise and creativity to a number of consumer and business-to-business Fortune 500 accounts including: Weight Watchers, FedEx, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Time Warner Cable's Road Runner HSO, GlaxoSmithKline, Bristol-Myers Squibb and Mead Johnson Nutritionals.

Specialties

Integrated national and local advertising and marketing campaigns that involve multiple media channels including video, print, direct mail, radio, and web. Overall marketing management of budget, staff and production. Identity development and naming. Brand positioning and strategy. Creative direction and graphic design. Strategic and tactical planning. Market research.
