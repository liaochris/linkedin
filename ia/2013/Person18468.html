Bryan Eaton's Summary

I have solid experience with technology and product project management, development lifecycles. Formally, my training includes in-depth Project Management Process & Certification training and CMM level 3 process training. I regularly define processes for software systems lifecycle project management, including CMM and PMI models for software process lifecycle management.

Throughout my career, I have built highly motivated and coordinated teams that have crossed the national as well as business group borders.

As project manager, maintaining project scope has included developing detailed project plans and tracking/managing projects to successful completion. My role as Project Manager involves conducting project kickoff meetings, documenting and tracking the progress of the project roadmap, regularly communicating issues, risks, and statuses to project stakeholders, planning, communication and issue resolution at all levels of a project from vice-presidents, operations and resource Management, product development and QA teams, as well as with all levels of management within the organization. In addition I have coordinated tasks and project information with external vendors and global stakeholders.

My tasks have included validating and managing project scope; developing and maintaining an accurate, detailed project plan including cost and schedule control, including budget maintenance. Other regular project management tasks have included ongoing project risk identification and mitigation which is critical to the ongoing schedule tracking to provide successful completion of projects. During projects, I provide a regularly scheduled status, issues and risks communication for the project stakeholders. I have regularly managed detail level activities with multiple business line management and teams, delivering well-defined projects (often simultaneous projects) within time and budget schedules.
