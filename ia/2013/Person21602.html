Chris Heinen's Summary

Chris Heinen, a Boston based developer, founded Heinen Creative in 2008. With a background in both design and development, Chris brings a unique perspective to any project. Over the years he has also developed a network of trusted associates and service providers. This allows him to manage a wide range of projects.

Chris got his start at the NYC design firm RWI, where he worked his way up to Art Director. There, he led and contributed on a variety of different print and digital projects for clients such as Colgate-Palmolive, Hertz, RIM and Foot Locker. Also in NYC, Chris taught classes and held seminars at Noble Desktop. He created the school’s first ever WordPress courses and published two workbooks.

In 2010, Chris moved to the Boston area and took a position at Tank Design. There he worked his way up to the role of Senior Developer and Technical Lead. In that role, he oversaw the production of WordPress, Drupal and Concrete5 CMS builds. Clients he worked with at Tank included FedEx, ServiceMaster and Rockport Shoes.

When he isn’t working, you can find Chris at 13,500 ft. He is an experienced skydiver, videographer, and coach with over 1,100 jumps. He also enjoys climbing, backpacking, snowboarding, fly fishing, kiteboarding and traveling.

Specialties: Open source CMS development, front-end development, responsive design and graphic design.
