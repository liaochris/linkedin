Jeff Harmon, ACC's Publications

  • The Anatomy of a Principled Leader: A Field Guide to Being the Type of Leader Everyone Dreams of Working fo

    • Amazon Createspace
    • October 3, 2012
    Authors: Jeff Harmon, ACC

    The Anatomy of a Principled Leader" is a step into a different brand of leadership. Leadership that is character driven and flips the power dynamic on its head to empower people and organizations to become the best they can be. The metaphor of the human body is used to symbolize the seven characteristics of the Principled Leader. The mind, heart, eyes, ears, mouth, hands and feet each represent a unique set of indispensable functions of a principled leader that operate at times at full health and at other times are in a state of dysfunction. All these characteristics are grounded in character and humility. The book is written in field guide form to be a practical, every day tool to enable the reader to reflect on themselves, the leaders they have observed in the past and the action steps they want to take to develop themselves as a Principled Leader.
