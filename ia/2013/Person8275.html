Anne Bachrach's Summary

After enjoying a successful career with IBM, Anne Bachrach started her own firm, A.M. Enterprises. For nearly a quarter of a century, business owners and entrepreneurs who have worked and currently do work with Anne make more money, work less, and enjoy even better work life balance.

While pursuing her own career goals, Anne began to realize the effect personal accountability was having on her own business. The systems and tools she used early in her career, along with her experiences, are the foundation of the program she continues to use with her clients today.

Anne Bachrach has dedicated her career to helping business people and entrepreneurs accelerate their results. Her coaching and training services are in high demand and she only works with clients who are truly serious about achieving all of their goals and living their dreams.

Her goal is to is to help people focus on their highest payoff activities in order to put them in the highest probability position so they can achieve all their goals (personal and professional) in the timeframe they set.

Anne is the author of the books, Excuses Don't Count; Results Rule!, and Live Life with No Regrets; How the Choices We Make Impact Our Lives, The Work Life Balance Emergency Kit, and No Excuses. She is a contributing author to the book, Roadmap to Success, with Stephen Covey and Ken Blanchard.

Specialties: Known as The Accountability Coach and The Results Accelerator. You will use proven business success systems for achieving all your goals so you can experience better work life balance.

The people who are best served by Anne Bachrach's services, programs, and products are business owners and entrepreneurs who really want to achieve all their personal and business goals, within the time frame they desire, so they can enjoy the life they always dreamed of having.
