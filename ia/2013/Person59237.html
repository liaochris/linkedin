Jason Heller's Summary

Official Bio:
Jason Heller is a digital marketing pioneer, recognized thought leader, speaker and author. He is CEO of Agiliti, a unique consultancy focused on digital marketing operations and strategy. Jason co-founded one of the first digital media agencies, which he later sold to Horizon Media, the largest independent media agency in the world. His efforts working with industry leading brands led to the development some of the best practices that are commonplace today. After leaving the agency world, Jason has trained thousands of digital and traditional media teams at the largest global agencies and leading brands. Today Jason works closely with marketing leadership to develop and hone their internal digital organization, capabilities, and strategies, as well as the processes to manage digital agency portfolios and performance.


The Untold Story:
An entrepreneur by nature, at the age of 18, Jason founded a recording studio, which spawned into a successful independent record label. In 1995, the necessity of utilizing alternative marketing vehicles helped him discover the power of the internet. Jason tapped into the potential of online global niche communities early on, and was truly inspired by its increasing potential. Then in 1997 he left the music industry to pursue a career in digital marketing. He launched one of the first digital media agencies, Mass Transit Interactive, and quickly made a name for himself as a new media pioneer. Working with many leading dot com and blue chip brands, Jason's leadership helped to develop top industry talent as well as spawn some of the best practices that are commonplace today.


Other Highlights:
Speaker at many industry events including OMMA, iMedia, Ad:Tech, IAA Global Marketing Summit, DigiDay, SMX, and many others.

Authored articles or quoted in NY Times, Wall Street Journal, CNN, Forbes, Fortune, Entrepreneur, FT, Fast Company, Ad Week, Advertising Age, OMMA, Corporate Board Member Magazine, and many others.
