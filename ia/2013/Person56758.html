Ken Jacobs' Summary

Jacobs Communications Consulting, LLC helps public relations and communications agencies grow business, and enhance staff performance, communications, and leadership skills.

We do so via consulting, training and coaching. Our services include advising on agency new business and marketing plans, marketing campaigns and new business proposals and presentations; consulting on agency structure and profitability; offering PR skills training workshops; leading workshops on expanding business from existing clients; designing agency training programs and facilitating idea-generation sessions.

Training programs include "Five-Star Client Service," "Managing Up," "Managing and Motivating Your Teams," "Managing Difficult Clients," "From Manager to Leader," "Introduction to Account Supervision," "Write Better, Right Now!," "PR Account Management," and "Constructive Confrontation: Having Difficult Conversations," to name a few.

In addition, we provide coaching to individuals and teams.

Specialties

Consulting, Training, Coaching, Business Development, Agency Management, Staff Retention, Professional Development, Agency Marketing, New Business, Agency Management and Improving Staff Performance in Client Service, Leadership, and Communications Skills
