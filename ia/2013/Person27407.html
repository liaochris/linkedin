Julie Robertson's Summary

At CQ inSight, we make all of our clients feel welcome. Small businesses, corporations, government enterprise, community groups and individuals all take advantage of our extensive range of services. We're passionate about our work and love what we do. Our creative graphic design services include business identity design, promotional design, publication design, along with copy writing and editing. Our photo editing services include photo enhancement, photo retouching and blending images. Our Adobe training services include training in Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator and Acrobat techniques; workplace training, private tutoring and public workshops; consulting on Adobe software and file setup to maximise productivity in the workplace.

Julie Robertson's Experience

Creative Director

CQ inSight

July 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) Yeppoon

I have many roles as Creative Director at CQ inSight, including graphic designer, trainer and manager of the business. I liaise with clients and suppliers, create unique designs, manipulate and enhance images and provide computer training in Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator and Acrobat. I also conduct local public workshops in these programs and help people learn to design and edit photos themselves.

Owner/Graphic Designer

Main Menus inSight

2010 – 2012 (2 years) Yeppoon

As graphic designer at Main Menus inSight, I created designs primarily for the hospitality and tourism industries including menus, brochures, guest compendiums and websites. I also liaised with clients and suppliers, and maintained the business website.

Graphic Designer and Adobe Trainer

Julie Robertson Graphic Design & Training

2003 – 2012 (9 years) Central Queensland

During my 9 years trading as Julie Robertson (Graphic Design & Training), I created many unique designs for clients including logos, business cards, brochures, calendars, publications, posters, concept illustrations and signage. I also provided workplace training services throughout the region in Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator and Acrobat. In 2011 I began offering public workshops in Rockhampton, teaching participants to use these programs effectively to create their own unique designs.

Graphic Design Teacher

CQ TAFE

2004 – 2010 (6 years) Rockhampton

I taught the Diploma of Graphic Design and associated short courses at CQ TAFE to countless students during my 7 years with the institute. In addition to the day-to-day delivery of classes and provision of guidance and direction to the students, I also prepared all learning content, assessments and course guides for the diploma for many years and actively worked to build the quality of the course delivery to the region.

Tutor

CQ University

2003 – 2004 (1 year) Rockhampton

As tutor at CQ University, I provided weekly guidance and learning support to students studying desktop publishing as part of their degree. This course taught Adobe InDesign and design principles to help students learn to create their own designs such as business cards, menus and brochures. I also prepared lessons and marked assessments each semester.

Julie Robertson's Education

Queensland College of Art - Griffith University

Bachelor of Design Studies, Graphic Design

1996 – 1998

During the first year of my Bachelor of Design Studies I studied a combination of graphic design, interior design and industrial design courses. I then chose to specialise in graphic design so the remaining two years were spent studying typography, computer graphics, advertising design, environmental design, corporate design, website design, signage design and the customary design theory subjects.
