Barbara Alevras, PMP's Summary

Barbara Alevras, PMP, uses her extensive experience in strategy, marketing, project, and operations management to provide strategy execution, marketing/social media, and professional productivity services to small businesses, non-profits, and self-employed professionals with big ideas and limited resources.
