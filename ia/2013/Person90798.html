Meera K's Summary

Co-founded Citizen Matters, an award winning media recognized as an pioneer of community journalism in Bangalore. Citizen Matters has been independently investigating the many realities of Bengaluru, exposing inconvenient truths, interpreting what's really going on and bringing it to citizens’ attention. We encourage citizens, professionals and journalists alike getting involved in city affairs, reporting, collaborating on, analyzing, and proposing ideas and solutions.

Founder Trustee at Oorvani Foundation, a non-profit trust for a free and independent media. This foundation develops and promotes deep-diving public interest journalism on governance, citizenship and society in India at city, state and national level, through Citizen Matters, India Together and other channels.

Director at Oorvani Media Pvt Ltd, that publishes Citizen Matters and India Together online newsmagazines, and guidebooks -- 'Living in Bengaluru' and 'Get to know Bengaluru better'.

Active in neighbourhood improvement initiatives and volunteer for projects related to transport, lake rejuvenation, organic kitchen gardening and solid waste management. Also RWA and Ward committee member.

Have volunteered for projects related to sustainable development issues with Association for India's Development (AID). Supported education and health related projects in urban and rural areas.

Expertise
- Publishing and Newsmedia: Operations, writing, editing, commissioning, marketing, technology, print and web production
- Technology: Product development, Mobile, Web, Project management, and Training
- Non-profit & community development: workshops, projects, communication and mentoring
