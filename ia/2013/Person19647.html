Candy Cohn's Summary

I help seniors find the most appropriate lifestyle and environment to meet their needs in their later years. There is a broad array of housing options available to seniors, from specialized facilities that provide round-the-clock care to independent living communities where people still drive on a daily basis. The choices can be overwhelming to seniors and to their adult children. I make it a priority to ease the process by offering Senior Placement services. I understand how daunting this process can be, having recently gone through it with my own parents. I also bring years of experience as a public relations and marketing professional in the healthcare industry.

Specialties

Senior placement, eldercare marketing, senior care, public relations, communications, special events, fundraising and sponsorships, training staff, public speaking, networking; and writing web content, blogs, brochures, newsletters, magazine articles and press releases.
