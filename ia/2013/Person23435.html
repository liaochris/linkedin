As a programming media strategist, consultant and market researcher, I distill market and customer information into company opportunity and profit while creating a product that satisfies both the customer (such as radio listeners) as well as those who create it (the company and its employees).

As a Program Director, I lead my team to help create entertaining and successful radio stations that emotionally connect with listeners while positively representing the local community and delivering profitable results for advertisers and stakeholders. Often, I serve those needing guidance due to extreme competitive situations and simply need valid answers, direction and leadership.

I work in the radio, TV/cable, new media (satellite radio), marketing/advertising and select retail (beer/banking/audio-video-electronics) industries...while also spotting emerging trends in the media.
