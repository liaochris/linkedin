Amy Mancuso's Summary

AMY MANCUSO is a Professional Wedding Consultant and a specialist in the Weddings and Events Management Industry. She has been coordinating elegant weddings and events in the Valley for over 15 years, and is one of the most innovative, creative professionals in the business today. Amy has been responsible for numerous local, national and international events ranging from 10, to over 10,000 participants, and she is a preferred vendor at most of the premier hotels and resorts in the Valley. Amy has been voted Wedding Consultant of the Year 2000-2005 and into the Wedding Coordinator Hall of Fame 2006-2013 by the Wedding Chronicle’s Reader’s Poll.

Amy has also been interviewed and appeared in InStyle Magazine, People Magazine, Bride’s Magazine, Modern Bride’s Magazine, Bridal Guide, The Arizona Republic, Mesa Tribune, Item Magazine, Desert Living Magazine, Phoenix Bride & Groom Magazine, Arizona Weddings Magazine, Arizona Bride Magazine, The Wedding Chronicle and a featured article appeared about her in Arizona Foothills Magazine.

Amy’s TV appearances include: InStyle Celebrity Weddings TV Special, The Pat McMahon Show, ABC News, Sonoran Living, Good Morning Arizona, Arizona Midday, and Phoenix Channel 11.

Specialties: Liaison, Mediator, Financial Planner, Contract Negotiations, Budget Analysis.
