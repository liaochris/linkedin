Chris Naylor, MA's Summary

Business coach serving as an advocate for healthy businesses, strong leaders and high-performance teams through offering a transformational business acceleration framework with a variety of proprietary programs for breakthrough results. Over 25 years of successfully working with large and small businesses, entrepreneurs, non-profits and educational institutions at local, regional and national levels. Successful at inspiring organizational growth through focusing on strategy, people, processes and accountability for maximum results. Coaching services include:

*Business Growth Coaching & Consulting for leaders, owners and teams.

*Strengths-based team building and strengths coaching for boosting employee engagement, creating winning teams and building stronger organizations.

*Culture Transformation Program for organizations seeking to break through to new levels of growth and success.

*Entrepreneurial Operation System (EOS) Facilitation & Consulting Program – Strategic growth planning; vision, mission, values facilitation; marketing planning; deep communications/PR experience; social networking resources; leadership development, hiring & performance management.

*Conflict Resolution Facilitation for leaders and teams.

*Career Coaching and Personal Branding for people who want more from their career.

*Transformational Workshops, speaking and facilitating for team-building programs, workshop
break-outs, retreats, special events and lunch-n-learns.

*Life Design Coaching for individuals seeking more happiness and success.

Chris is passionate about helping people in business who are motivated to reach new levels of growth and success through strategic business planning, team building and strong organizational cultures.
