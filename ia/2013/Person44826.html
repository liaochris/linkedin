Fireball Tim Lawrence's Summary

SUBSCRIBE to the Fireball Tim Blog for Hollywood Action News, Cool Cars and Crazy Episodes!
http://www.fireballtim.com

Fireball Tim is not only America's best TV Hosts, as a car designer, he's also responsible for over 400 designs of some of the most famous Hollywood Movie Cars in history.

As host, Fireball has done the gambit of Automotive TV Shows, live events at SEMA, The LA & San Diego Auto Shows and is the emcee for The World Class Automotive Film & Arts Festival.

Fireball (his legal name) has designed All Star Vehicles for the hit TV Show “Monster Garage,” the Award Winning 500+hp MINI Police Pursuit for Speed Channel's "Street Tuner Challenge" and was the host for Discovery Channel's "World's Most Expensive Rides" and "Extreme Rides." He also reviews new vehicles for many of the Automotive Manufacturers to his audience of 15 million.

Fireball's real talent goes beyond being a TV Host and into Children's Books where he just released "Fireball Tim's BIG BOOK of Wacky Rides" showcasing the wildest and funnest car concepts for kids. Available on AMAZON at http://www.tinyurl.com/fireballswackyrides

Fireball's Social internet prowess has become massive and his comprehensive network hits his audience through successful Online Shows like "5MINUTE DRIVE," "RIDE ALONG," "WACKY RIDES MONDAY" through The Auto Channel, Streetfire, Autoholics, Facebook & Youtube. No one has accomplished more online.

His design sense and passion for cars makes him America's #1 Automotive Design Expert and Host for Television according to AUTOWEEK MAGAZINE.
