Melanie Meadows' Summary

When it comes to creative work, I am a jack-of-all-trades. I have a degree in journalism but started working as a graphic designer in college. I am one of those rare breeds of creatives who can wear both the writing and design hats — and wear them well. In addition, I am a highly skilled project manager. I am organized, detail-oriented and pride myself on building lasting relationships with both my clients and my business partners. I have worked for both global and large regional corporations, as well as small start up firms. I set the highest standards for both my creative work and for my customer service.

Specialties

Copywriting, content development, print and web design, creative strategy, art direction, brand development, communications strategy. Software skills include: Adobe Creative Suite (PhotoShop, InDesign, Illustrator), Dreamweaver, Fireworks, all Microsoft Office applications and many others.
