Kate McCann's Summary

At McCann Chiropractic our goal is to partner with you in reaching your health goals. What sets us apart from other facilities is our state of the art technology – the Insight Subluxation Station. The Insight allows us to measure the true function of your health and wellbeing. Then we design a care plan that is tailored specifically for you and your goals. From there we are able to re-measure as you progress through care.

As a husband and wife team are patients become part of our chiropractic family. Getting to know you allows us to serve you better. We want you to feel at home.

We are extremely passionate about educating our community on true health and wellness. We host in house wellness workshops, send out weekly health newsletters, we have a website and a Facebook fan page which allows us to stay in constant contact with you. We even offer wellness workshops to the businesses in our area for no cost.
