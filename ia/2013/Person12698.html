Belinda Pruyne's Experience

Founder and CEO

Business Innovation Group

2007 – Present (6 years)

You don’t have to “tolerate” a career or business that is anything less than extraordinary.

In fact, you don’t have to settle for anything less than what you truly want in your life.

Have you noticed how many people feel like they are held hostage by their careers?

Held captive by their circumstances and doubts about what’s truly possible?

It’s that feeling that everything is “good enough” while knowing deep down there’s something more…

It’s that yearning for permission to dream big and stop playing nice at the expense of your dreams…

It’s that fear of looking back on your life always wondering what might have been…

A Universal Truth: It doesn’t matter if you’re a lifelong employee, a serial or first-time entrepreneur. It doesn’t matter how brilliant, talented, smart, or savvy you are, you can’t wait for someone to ask you to follow your dreams.

You’ve got to go out there and grab them.

Put another way: If you want to have an extraordinary life, you’ve got to stop acting ordinary.

We’re talking about creating a serious plan to help you accomplish your dreams.

But you don’t have to do it alone. (In fact, we don’t recommend it).

My name is Belinda Pruyne from the Business Innovation Group. I have spent 20+ years helping my clients uncover and amplify their natural talents and brilliance within themselves. I help give them the tools to re-envision what is possible, and then create the business plan to bring their dreams to life

In other words, I help you mange your inner journey while simultaneously developing an innovative plan to create the extraordinary business or career you’ve always wanted.

So that you’ll never have to tolerate an ordinary day again.
