Della Ratcliffe's Summary

Della Ratcliffe is a solution-focused life coach, business coach and business consultant with expertise in supply chain strategy and organizational optimization. She has worked in the non-profit sector, federal government and with Fortune 500 companies such as Procter & Gamble, Pillsbury and Pfizer.

Through her company Inner Journey Outfitters, she supports individuals in creating transformed and balanced lives using solution-focused coaching techniques plus other modalities such as Psych-K Balancing.

As an executive and business coach, she works with new business owners and entrepreneurs in the organizational and operational areas of their business, particularly in the areas of system and process development, metrics, lead generation, purchasing, supply chain, project management, sales and marketing.

Della is now a QuickBooks ProAdvisor and a Salesforce Consulting Partner.
