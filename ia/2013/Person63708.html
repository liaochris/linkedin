Jill Underwood's Summary

I love what I do! I have 30 years of experience helping folks with their home financing goals. I was not a participant in the 'sub-prime' lending mess because it is my belief people should have a good financial track record before they buy a home. Then the home will be a blessing to them, not a nightmare.

I am a professional mortgage banker, mortgage lender, and Senior Loan Officer.

Works with clients to help them make good financial decisions when purchasing or refinancing their home.

Specialties

Residential mortgage financing, Senior Loan Officer, mortgage banking, purchase and refinance transactions, can originate loans in 48 states. Works with buyers and homeowners, work with real estate agents and their clients.
