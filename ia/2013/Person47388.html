Principal Lecturer and Owner

Learn Resume Writing www.learnresumewriting.com

2006 – Present (7 years) Melbourne Area, Australia

Over the last two decades, I have worked tirelessly to refine my talents and grow my reputation across the resume-writing community.

I have gained distinction as being the most decorated resume writer in Career Directors International (a position I still enjoy) with more than 51 TORI (Toast of the Resume Industry) award nominations (28 prizes in all) and multiple certifications including Certified Master Resume Writer, Reach Certified Personal Branding Strategist, Master Career Director, Certified IT Resume Specialist, Certified Job Loss Recovery Coach (and more!).

A few years go, my mind went back to the early nineties when I first opened my resume writing business, Top Margin. How "green" I was! In those days, there were no training courses for resume writers, the Internet was only used by a select few, and resume writing as a career was almost unheard of. Everything I knew I had to learn the hard way; largely though trial and error and more than a few lessons learned! That was when the idea for LearnResumeWriting.com started to germinate. With my knowledge and experience validated and my business successful, it made sense to me that I should share what I have learned and help aspiring writers and entrepreneurs reach new heights in their profession.

✔ Do you love to write but need direction with strategy, formatting and questioning your clients?

✔ Are you an aspiring business owner who wants to get up-and-racing quickly and circumvent the school of hard knocks?

If the answer to either of those questions is yes, then my course (endorsed by leading resume writing association Career Directors International is for you! My methods work for me, have stood the test of time, and they'll work for you too.
