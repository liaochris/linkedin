Kevin Kunze's Summary

Kevin Kunze has had film screenings at the Director's Guild of America Theater in New York City, the Berkeley Art Museum / Pacific Film Archive, and the Casoria Contemporary Art Museum in Naples, Italy. Kunze's past and current film collaborators include: NASA Ames, Singularity University, Google / Android, U.S. National Park Service, Asics Onitsuka, Outside Lands, Dim Mak Records, and the University of San Francisco.
