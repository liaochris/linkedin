Green energy enterprises

January 2000 – Present (13 years 8 months) Port Orange, Florida

Our specific proposal is the vertically integrated development of large scale, pelletized, switchgrass
powered electrical generation. The Green-ee process is intrinsically carbon neutral because all CO2
produced is removed from the atmosphere during the growth of the switchgrass. Burning fossil fuels
to produce electricity releases billions of tons of CO2 into the atmosphere annually. To generate only
two percent our electricity the US burns one hundred fifty million barrels of oil annually adding pressure
on the price of oil. Green-ee's power plants will eliminate both the CO2 emissions and the pressure
on oil and natural gas prices.
Because of the coal industry's political grip on the economics of electric generation we propose as our
Level 1 replacing the use of oil and then natural gas but our ultimate or Level 2 goal is to eliminate
the use of coal as well. We intend to keep our Strategic National Reserve in the ground where it will
truly remain a reserve.
As our power plants come on line Green-ee's methodology will return dramatic, measurable benefits
to both the environment and the economy. These benefits will further the education of both the public
and governments. Eventually, as the newly enlightened consumer demands the recovery of the
true costs of unlimited CO2 emissions, a viable methodology for attaching those costs directly to the
emitters will emerge. At present the value for CERs and carbon credits from projects like the Regional
Greenhouse Gas Initiative stands around USD3.00 to 8.00 per tonne of CO2. The market, however, is
new, limited and unstable. It is also inherently open to manipulation by the entities it is supposedly
designed to tax. In the Appendix we propose an alternate to the carbon credit market.
