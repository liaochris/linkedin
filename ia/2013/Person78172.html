Lance Langenhoven, CCIM's Summary

I was a software analyst for about 20 years before becoming a commercial realtor in 2006. I used to work for Sungard, a $10 billion dollar conglomerate with 100's of international locations. The job started in Walton-on-Thames, Surrey , England & then moved to Houston in 2001.

In 2006 I decided to leave Sungard & became a licensed realtor. In 2012 I became a licensed real estate broker & formed my own brokerage called "The Commercial Professionals".

We are a commercial real estate operation offering the following:

- investments (syndicated or otherwise - accredited investors)
- investment analysis (NNN, industrial, retail or office deals)
- site location (multi-family, industrial, retail or office deals)
- development consultation (industrial, retail, multi-family, office)
- lease analysis (industrial, retail or office)
- buying / selling / leasing commercial real estate

With my partner David Matos, CPA, we also offer business brokerage services.

Lance has earned the coveted CCIM designation. This is the "MBA" of commercial real estate & differentiates the professional practitioners from the rest.

Specialties: My specialties are the following:

- Deal Analysis. If you don't do a good job at the front end of a deal, you can make some big mistakes. We turn down more deals than we close. That's the right ratio.

- Grocery Anchored Retail Shopping Center investments. We have a specific niche that we operate within. We co-invest with our investors.

- Buying / Selling industrial, retail or office properties.

- Buying / Selling businesses.
