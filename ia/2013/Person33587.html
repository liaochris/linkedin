Deborah L. Schuster, CPRW's Summary

What do I do best? I give professionals and executives a competitive advantage in this tough job market by writing strategic, high-impact resumes. As a published resume expert with over 18 years of experience, I've helped thousands of folks land their dream jobs.

I'm confident enough in results to offer a 30-day interview guarantee on all of my resumes. It makes my day when a client calls or emails to tell me they have been selected from among thousands for an interview. It’s even more exciting to hear they’ve been hired for the position -- at the salary they deserve!

Quick Bio: I'm a Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW), a member of the Professional Association of Resume Writers and Career Coaches (PARW/CC), and currently serve on their CPRW Certification Committee. Over 45 of my resumes and cover letters have been selected for publication in numerous "Best Resumes" books.

Specialties: I specialize in personal brand development, high-impact resumes, and personal marketing strategies for clients in a wide variety of fields, including: Executive Management, Manufacturing Managers, IT, Engineering, Sales/Marketing Management, Finance, Accounting, Medical, Education, HR…and more.
