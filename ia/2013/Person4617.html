Alison Roth, LMFT, MA's Summary

By way of introduction, during my MFT Internship at The Maple Counseling Center in Beverly Hills, I worked full time (again) at Jdate (the largest Jewish online dating site).

I’d long since been a veteran of the online dating industry dating back to 1999 having worked for Match.com, eHarmony, Date.com, Lavalife, The Relationship Exchange and Matchmaker to name a few. My expertise involved building compelling brands and designing intuitive websites that ensured a high conversion rate from visitor into subscriber. Additionally I developed and executed marketing strategies to drive qualified traffic to those sites. It was an extraordinary time, at what felt to us like the dawn of “Internet Interactivity”, making up the rules as we went along!

When I transitioned from the clinic to private practice, I co-founded www.thought-rocket.com, a consulting company with two Jdate colleagues catering to the same clientele. The money was liberating as was working from home, but as I became more involved with building my practice, I found myself gravitating towards my new community of shrinks, a welcome reprieve from the world of matchmaking.

After watching colleagues struggle trying to navigate the Internet to market their private practices’ and for the most part failing miserably—the proverbial penny dropped and I realized I could provide colleagues with a similar solution to what I provided for dating sites. My “secret sauce” lay in combining my online marketing expertise with my personal experience building a private practice and thus ShrinkWr@p was borne.

At ShrinkWr@p, we do more than just create and manage a uniquely-branded-referral-generating website for our clients, though we do provide that.

At ShrinkWr@p we equip savvy therapists with a dynamic and easy to manage online business platform to help you become a successful small business owner and live your dream as a financially abundant and sought-after therapist.

Specialties

Helping private practice therapist, counselors, psychologists and psychiatrists brand themselves online with dynamic websites that generate quality referrals so they can focus on what they do best--helping their clients.


Extensive experience in the online dating industry performing business development, product development, marketing strategy, PR and promotions, affiliate marketing, writing content and customer service optimization.
