DJ Bean's Summary

Why I am on LinkedIn:

Social Networking Media is the fastest growing medium for networking. I am a firm believer that there is more worth in who you know versus what you know. I want my tenants, clients, investors, vendors and prospects to know that I am someone who is connected and always looking for more connections.

I am a commercial real estate broker in Phoenix that is an expert in ALL phases of commercial real estate, including leasing, sales, marketing, management, investment, development and consultation.

Recognized as one of Phoenix's Top Producing Brokers, I average 125 lease/sale transactions every year. I currently represent over 1.5 million square feet of commercial property in Maricopa County.

What I am looking for:

I am looking for products and services to help me better serve my clients in their commercial real estate decisions. Architects, Developers, Environmental Specialists, Attorneys, Telephony, IT, Security, Signage, etc.

I am looking to represent small to medium sized business owners who lease and/or own their current business locations. Whether it is retail, office, or industrial, I can help them save time and money, as I perform all the research and negotiations in their behalf, with no cost to them as the landlord/seller pays my fees.

I am also looking for commercial real estate investors who currently own properties and are looking to expand their portfolios, change marketing efforts or even dispose of their current holdings.

Specialties

tenant representation, buyer representation, lanlord representation, lease negotiation, leasing consultation, investment sales, user sales, owner/user opportunities, development, marketing, management, research
