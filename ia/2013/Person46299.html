Robert Hernandez's Summary

At Frontier Insurance Agency, we specialize in all lines of insurance including home, auto, business, commercial, life insurance and group health insurance.
As insurance brokers, we can offer our clients in New Jersey and New York, many alternative companies to choose from. This allows us to meet our client's objective; to get the best insurance rate possible relative to their particular insurance needs.

Although we opened our doors in 2007, our CEO, Robert Hernandez has over 25 years experience in management, marketing and sales both at the corporate level and in the insurance industry.
