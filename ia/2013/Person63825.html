Jim David's Summary

CEO and Founder of Leapfrawg.com, a Social Media Management, Social Media Marketing, Social Media Strategy and Social Media Training Company. Jim's was an early adopter and Speaker about Social Media Marketing. Seeing Social Media Marketing expand exponentially he realized there was a lack of knowledge by many Small and Medium Sized Companies on how to use Social Media Marketing to its full advantage. So he started a Monthly Social Media Marketing program that helps manage the actual implementation, and 2-way engagement, of content across multiple Social Media Channels. Jim David is also available as a Speaker on various Social Media topics from the basic Social Media 101 to more advanced Social Media topics.

Jim holds an MBA from San Francisco State University. He is President Emeritus of the DFW American Marketing Association and Past EVP of Marketing. He's a member of the Dallas Society of Visual Communications, Print & Image Association of America, and the CMO (Chief Marketing Officer) Council. He's on the Board of the Texas Institute of Science as well as the Digital Imaging Advisory Council for Eastfield College and Advisory Committee for Creatives in DFW. After working in the Financial Industry for several years in Europe and Asia he returned to the U.S. in 2001.He's been responsible for 4 start-ups himself and continues to be an advisor for several others.
Leapfrawg.com is a Social Media Marketing company that maximises the benefit of Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, blogs, Google+, Pinterest and other emerging Social Media Marketing channels to drive sales, improve brand recognition and increase customer loyalty.
