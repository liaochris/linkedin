Catherine Birkelo's Summary

I show people whose fire has died how to relight that flame AT WILL and feel filled up with renewed intention and quiet presence.

I am a highly self-motivated, excellence-driven visual artist. Adept at rendering varied subject matter while excelling at illustrating human and other organic forms. Recognized for creativity, superb work ethic and consummate dedication to high caliber of craft.


Client Productions Include:

Myrddin Tales; Part 1 (in production), Untitled "Monster Rancher" Game (in production), Water Bomber, Rumors, Omnium Gatherum, Born Yesterday, The Uneasy Chair, All In The Timing, Over The Tavern, Rough Crossing, Seascape, Ambition Facing West, The Physicists, The Foreigner, The Man Who Came To Dinner, Lola Goes To Roma, The Spitfire Grill, Lives Of The Saints, Gunfighter, The Beard Of Avon, Real Women Have Curves, Enchanted April, Boy Gets Girl, Ghosts, Bat Boy, La Boheme, De Fledermaus, The Mikado, The Sound of Music, Grease, Willy Wonka & The Chocolate Factory, The Music Man, Peter Pan, San Marcos Drum Line Floor Cloth, Snoopy on Ice, Landscape Thematic Portals for Teva International, 1920's Chicago Streets for Private Party, Conny Island Theme for Private Party, Halloween Tombstones for Private Party, Canne Film Festival Posters for MOCA Fundraiser, Santa Barbara Landscape for Fiesta Celebration, Ballard School Fundraiser Signage, Los Olivos Elementary School Mural
