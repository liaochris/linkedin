Fran Simon's Summary

I am a consultant with a rich history of leadership and management experience in mid-sized organizations, companies, and membership associations in the early childhood education community. My educational background and heart are in early childhood education, but my creative soul and my experience are in technology leadership and business development. I am a global thinker with a knack for serious networking.

I am a contributing author for Child Care Information Exchange, and co-author of Digital Decisions: Choosing the Right Technology Tools for Early Childhood Education.

I founded Early Childhood Investigations Webinar series, an ongoing series of free webinars presented by national experts for early childhood administrators, and Co-Founded Early Childhood Technology Network, a website portal for teachers and administrators.

My core competencies include anything that includes educational technology, webinars, content development, management and leadership in early education and non-profit organizations.I'm a B2B, B2E, B2G woman.
.
My consulting business, Engagement Strategies, LLC, is different: Training, coaching a mentoring are often woven into contracts with my clients. I offer my clients the unique advantage to learn the strategies, techniques, and skills I use to execute their initiatives so they can sustain
them on their own. I work collaboratively with my clients to build internal competencies and skills they need to independently execute their technology implementation, social media, marketing, and webinar programs.

I am a frequent presenter at national conferences colleges, and for corporations on topics related to marketing, webinars, early childhood technology, and social media.
