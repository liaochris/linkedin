Cathy Ma's Summary

I am an independent consultant specialising in helping companies and organisations unlock the potential of simple, yet effective digital, content, social media and analytics strategies. If you can track what you do well (and not so well), you will get better at what you do. I am also the founder of http://lotsofyoga.com, a social news aggregating platform for all things yoga.

Previously I managed IPC Media's Social Media strategy and social insights that covered more than 60 iconic brands including Wallpaper*, MarieClaire and NME. My focus is on cultivating a culture of community empowerment and smart use of actionable insights. Prior to that, I was a sociologist on Wikipedia, a social user experience designer for Yahoo! and a social media consultant at Vodafone.

I speak internationally and have presented as conferences such as the MarieClaire International, World Newspaper Congress and the Social Media World forum on strategy and audience development.
