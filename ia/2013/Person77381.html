Kyle Snavely's Summary

I continually strive to produce the highest quality content for my clients.

Working in the industry since 2003, I have produced over 300 films, videos, websites and graphic designs. I have also had the pleasure of leading both the high school and college students to state and national championships in media design.

Under my guidance, students have been recognized at the local, state, national and international level. While graduates of my college program are now enjoying successful careers in the media industry, graduates of my high school program have continued their education at such prestigious colleges and universities as:

University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts
University of Central Florida School of Radio and Television Broadcast
Emerson College School for Digital Media
Western Carolina University Motion Picture Program
Seminole State College of Television & Digital Cinema

My personal accomplishments span all areas of media production including major motion pictures, national television, professional sporting events, local television, live event coverage, print and web content.
