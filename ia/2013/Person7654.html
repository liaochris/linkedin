Angie Glotzbach's Summary

Hi there! I'm an online social media manager who's naturally motivated to help others. One of my favorite things to do is help small businesses cut through the clutter and be more effective with their social media – Twitter, Facebook, YouTube and LinkedIn – to really expand their online presence, increase visibility and awareness. It’s about providing the steps, systems, guidance and encouragement. It’s about being authentic. And having fun.

My background is an eclectic one. I am a recovering corporate bot with over a decade of experience at a Fortune 50 company where I had been an account rep, presentation producer, and a senior admin assistant for a variety of executives. I was also trained as a court reporter, and my lightning-fast typing (“writing” in court reporter speak) helped me really excel. For a time I was a top producer at a staffing company, where I discovered a talent for interviewing and had an uncanny ability to match clients with the ideal candidates.

I’m a flexible, go-with-the-flow kind of gal. Certified in social media, I help small businesses get clear with their social media. With a smile.

Specialties: Social media strategy and consulting, training in Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, social network setup, link building, article and video submission, marketing videos, email marketing, screencast videos, PowerPoint.
