Brian DiSalvo's Summary

Partners Group is a global private markets investment management firm with more than USD 38 billion under management in private equity, private debt, private real estate and private infrastructure.

Fully discretionary private real estate funds for creative deal structuring up and down the capital stack including real estate secondaries, direct equity joint ventures, asset and portfolio recapitalizations, partnership replacements, preferred equity and senior-sub equity placement, mezzanine financing, and selective primary fund investing.

Global integrated approach to deal sourcing, deal development / management, valuation, negotiation and execution. Deal sourcing, business development, investor relations, capital raising, structured solutions and specialized deal teams.

Value-added or opportunistic strategy typically requiring re-positioning, re-development, lease-up, or another form of value creation at the asset level with a total basis below replacement costs. All global markets considered; typically primary locations in tier-2 cities or secondary locations in tier-1 cities. Multifamily, office, retail, industrial, condominium, hospitality, other asset classes selectively considered.
