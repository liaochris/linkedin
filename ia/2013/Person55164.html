Iñaki Unsain Romero's Summary

Consultant has 15 years of experience in the Spanish real estate sector. He is based in Barcelona, Spain, and since September 2009 has been operating an independent consulting practice focused on assisting banks in managing real estate assets in Spain repossessed from developers who have been unable to meet debt obligations. He is already working with several Spanish banks. Consulting services include land management, due diligence and securing of land development rights, project management, project Monitoring, construction management, and sales and after-sales services. Prior to this, he was Director Regional Cataluña y Baleares (June 2006-June 2009) at one of Spain's leading real estate developers Sacyr Vallehermoso, with responsibility for defining strategy and market objectives for the company. Prior to this, he held a similar role at Metrovacesa.
