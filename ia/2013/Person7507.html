Andrew McCabe's Summary

We're all in Sales, yet no one likes to be "sold."

No matter where I've worked, in whatever industry, I know that what I am selling is myself.

In order to make a choice to purchase, customers must FIRST trust THEMSELVES in making that choice with ME.
