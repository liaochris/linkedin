Gretchen Gary's Summary

Gretchen Gary is an accomplished marketing professional with proven success in product management, direct marketing and Web analytics.

• Excellent communication and influencing skills necessary for driving results in large, matrix organization structure

• Demonstrated ability to manage the conversion optimization plan for a Web-based business

• Advanced training in Google Analytics and Adobe Marketing Cloud tools, including SiteCatalyst and Test & Target

• First-hand experience in product development and go-to-market strategy with a leading technology firm

• Analytical skills in forecasting, accounting, ROI calculations, and product lifecycle metrics.

• Working knowledge of HTML, CSS, blog publishing platforms (i.e. WordPress), and email marketing tools

• DMA certified direct-response copywriter and published journalist

Currently, Gretchen holds the position of Future Product Manager at Hewlett-Packard, where she manages the online conversion funnel and Web analytics solutions for a high-profile start-up business in the inkjet printing division.

Prior to the MBA, Gretchen lived abroad and was the Marketing Manager for two large condo developers in Costa Rica. Her primary role was to prepare the marketing launch plan for the rental program of four different condo hotels, including Bahia Azul, La Paloma Blanca, Bahia Encantada and Jaco Bay Ramada.

Before moving abroad, Gretchen worked as a direct marketing specialist for a B2B firm in Los Angeles. She is trained in direct response copywriting, email marketing, graphic design, Web development, search engine optimization (SEO and SEM), public relations, journalism, print production and social media marketing. She has strong interpersonal skills across all management levels, and a demonstrated ability to produce high-return campaigns.

Gretchen now lives in San Diego, CA and completed the International MBA program at USD in January 2012.
