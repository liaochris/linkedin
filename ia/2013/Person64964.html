Joan Nowak's Summary

Joan Nowak is a results-oriented business coach, consultant, trainer and creator of the Hybrid Business Coaching System – helping small business owners build a sustainable, profitable business one step at a time. Joan’s practical, innovative approach to profit building comes from applying the business development, marketing, operations and team-building skills acquired while working in senior management positions with various Fortune 300 companies.

While clients span a variety of industries, Joan has proven results working with small businesses in the following areas: increasing sales, profit and cash flow; improving service delivery and operations; recruiting, hiring and building teams; creating and implementing marketing programs; and developing business systems and procedures.

With small business owners in mind, my hybrid approach and coaching services are:

Practical - Realistic, useful strategies for east execution, the key to taking action
Effective - Deliver results, with a focus on profit, quickly and consistently
Affordable - Pay for itself quickly through improved profit -- a great return on investment

Specialties: planning, sales, marketing, business development, team building, operations and service delivery, systems and procedures, financial mastery.

Industries: business services, professional services, medical practices, manufacturing, wholesale distribution, personal services, trades, general contractors.
