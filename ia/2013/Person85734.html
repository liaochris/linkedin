Mariana Lloyd, CRS, GRI, Hon. AIA, SRES's Experience

Broker Associate

Keller Williams Realty

Privately Held; 10,001+ employees; Real Estate industry

May 2002 – Present (11 years 6 months)

Mariana joined Keller Williams as the Team Leader for the Mariana Lloyd Team in 2003. With her diverse background in architecture, design, marketing, sales and management, Mariana brings an expertise into the real estate profession that is very unique. Prior to joining Keller Williams, Mariana was the Director of Marketing, Managing B roker and Director of Real Estate for Oak Tree Country Club in Edmond, one of Edmond's premier golf course communities. She also served as the Executive Director of the Central Oklahoma Chapter of the American Institute of Architects where she is an Honorary Member of both the Chapter and the National Association of the American Institute of Architects. In 2002, she was named "Business Woman of the Year" by the Edmond Women's Club. She is a past board member of the Edmond Chamber of Commerce, Leadership Oklahoma City and Leadership Edmond. She was recently appointed by the Mayor of Edmond to serve on the Edmond Historic Preservation Trust.
