Lloyd Princeton's Summary

iMatchDesigners
Owner - Through iMatchDesigners.com, I match home, business, and property owners with the most talented interior designers, architects and landscape designers - worldwide.

Design Management Company (DMC)
Owner - Through DMC, I assist interior designers, architects and landscape designers with managing all aspects of their business - including contract negotiation and business development.

“Marketing Interior Design”
Author - I wrote this book to help design professionals understand the "business side" of their work and show them how to operate it more efficiently. Because, while interior designers are wonderful artistically, many are not comfortable running and promoting their businesses.

Speaker
I am a regular speaker to the design industry on marketing, business management and development. My "Master's Class" is IDCEC certified for CEU credit.
