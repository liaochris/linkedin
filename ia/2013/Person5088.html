Kahai Tate's Summary

Staging is Lifestyle Merchandising and is the number one marketing tool in selling a home. It is not luck, it is about preparation.

We do NOT decorate, we prepare homes based on demographics, architectural style and current trends. We understand how to create "a feeling" that allows buyers to see themselves in the home. We also understand the impact that the internet can have on selling a property and have quality photographs taken that capture the essence of each room.

Our goal is to partner with Realtors and homeowners to sell their properties for the most amount of money in the shortest period of time.
