Chophouse Hijack

September 2009 – May 2011 (1 year 9 months)

Once a month, we "hijack" a restaurant in Quincy and take it over! We take over the space and use it however we want! We bring in artists to show their work, poets, musicians, comedians, board games, movies... or maybe nothing at all. And we ALWAYS hijack the stereo... sometimes there is a specific theme, but it's always fun.

We do it for fun, and to get people in seats and eating the great food that local restaurants have to offer. We don't get any money, we don't get free food, we don't exclude anyone and it's not a charity. It's just for fun and to support local eateries.

Whats the catch?
In order to hijack the space, we need at least 20 paying customers each night, so check the calendar for the next one, burn a disk, bring a board game, or just bring your appetite.
