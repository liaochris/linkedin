Melvin Royster's Summary

The Fitness Craze is an extraordinary fitness company that offers group fitness programs like never seen before. We also offer personal training from two of the top trainers in the fitness industry, Kim and Mel Royster, founders of The Fitness Craze. We have been in the fitness industry for over 25 years. Our success comes from the results we give our clients. Our goal is to take our concept of training to the highest level of professional standards possible and deliver it to you. Our system is like no other found in the fitness industry today. We have learned the secrets for success and want to share those secrets with you.

We don't believe in fad diets or any of the quick exercise gimmicks you see on TV. It takes hard work, dedication, determination and motivation. So if you have tried everything in the books and are still on the roller coaster ride, let us help you make the lifestyle change you are looking for. We have done all the research, refuted all the gimmicks and have come up with a system that works. This will take the guess work out for you so you don't have to waste your time. We guarantee you will get the results you deserve in the quickest time possible. Life is short, "There Are No Boundaries"

Our Mission: The Fitness Craze mission is to provide the required connection between mind, body and soul needed to achieve success that leads to living a healthier, fuller lifestyle. In addition, our goal is to help make our community a healthier place for adults and children of all ages.

Our Vision: The Fitness Craze is committed to offering safe, fun, effective, energetic, and goal oriented exercise programs for small groups, businesses, kids and individuals that caters to age, fitness level, physical limitations, time constraints as well as personal objectives.

Our Motto: "There Are No Boundaries"

Now that you know who we are, join us so we can help you achieve your goals. We have the secrets for your Health and Fitness success.
