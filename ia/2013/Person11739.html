Barbara Rubin-Greenberg, ACC's Summary

Barbara brings compassion, insight and integrity combined with years of experience as a leader, organizational consultant and business owner to her coaching practice. In her role as an executive coach and change navigator for executives, leaders and entrepreneurs, Barbara is known for guiding clients through a customized process of assessment, clarity, action and alignment of personal and professional goals to bring their vision to life.

Barbara is certified by the Hudson Institute of Santa Barbara as an Executive Leadership Coach. She also has certifications for EQ In Action profile (emotional intelligence) and the Meyers Briggs Personality Type Indicator (MBTI®). She is an Associate Certified Coach (ACC) with the International Coach Federation.

Living with purpose, passion and intention, Barbara balances her love of English riding (dressage), community service, family and business with her coaching practice.
