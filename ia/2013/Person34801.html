Mark M Deutsch, MBA's Summary

You can reach me directly at (804) 338-3987 or Mark@MarkDeutsch.com and my personal website is www.MarkDeutsch.com

I work with high-growth SMALL BUSINESSES to make sure they stay that way ... through EFFECTIVE MARKETING and sales.

Most people know me as the CEO of BNI (Business Network International) - Central Virginia, the largest and most successful business referral organization in Virginia. I also frequently give seminars and conduct training programs on sales, marketing, networking, and strategic planning.

I have experienced mature companies, acquisitions, rapid growth, and miserable failure. I'm a co-author of the best-seller "Building the Ultimate Network" and personally deliver over 100 PRESENTATIONS A YEAR. I have started, co-founded, or operated more than a dozen companies ranging from a medical billing software company (during the dot com boom) to a diagnostic imaging provider (MRI of Richmond). I currently advise multiple start-ups and rapid growth companies, and serve as a mentor for several dozen others.

I graduated from the the University of Central Florida (Go Knights!) and received an MBA from Averett University. I am married to Henly Dawson Deutsch, we have two very active young boys, Jack & Davis, and we live in the Richmond, Virginia (RVA) area.

FYI - My last name is pronounced DOYCH [I know that it's not one you see everyday]
