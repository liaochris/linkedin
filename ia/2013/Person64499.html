Jason S. Lewis' Summary

As a true farm boy, I learned the lesson of hard work and entrepreneurism at a very young age. Before graduating high school, I had the privilege of starting three businesses. One is still in operation today, one that was sold for profit in 2000 and one that was a complete loss. All three provided their own unique lessons on entrepreneurship.

My passion and focus since 2005 has been helping businesses make smart office leasing decisions. One of the most rewarding aspects has been helping over 300+ entrepreneurs and business owners in the leasing or acquisition of commercial office space.

When not improving the bottom line of mine or my clients' businesses, you’ll find me in the mountains, backpacking my way to 100+ countries (no exotic hotels for this guy), on my stand up paddle board or simply enjoying the wonderful life I've have been blessed.
