Brian Nelson's Summary

Brian is the co-founder of ArcticLlama, LLC a premier freelance writing and content generation firm. With experience as a Certified Financial Planner ( CFP ) and as an MCSE ( Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer) Brian writes professional, technical and detail oriented, articles, books, brochures, webpages, analysis and other publications for a wide variety of clients.

In addition to publishing freelance writing advice on the Arctic Llama blog at -- http://www.ArcticLlama.com/blog/ Brian also writes helpful financial advice and investing advice at -- http://FinanceGourmet.com

As a father of two, Brian also seeks to help new dads and experienced dads with his writings on parenting advice at -- http://www.UndefeatedDaddy.com

You can follow Brian at http://twitter.com/ArcticLlama

There is an Arctic Llama freelance writing business page on Facebook at http://www.facebook.com/ArcticLlama
