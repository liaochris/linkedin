Laura Canny's Summary

I have closed over 185 transactions since I began my real estate career in 2003. I have advanced my real estate education by obtaining my ePro certification, the GRI designation, becoming a Certified Loss Mitigation Consultant, a Certified Distressed Property Expert, and obtaining an International Real Estate Specialist certification. I really appreciate your trust & confidence that I will provide the highest standard of customer service. Thank you, thank you, thank you!!
