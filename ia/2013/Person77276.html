Klaus Ruhmer's Summary

Experienced Marketing, Sales and Business Development Executive with over 13 years in the semiconductor equipment industry and over 4 years in the Alternative Fuels & Bio-Energy industry.

Klaus has a strong technical background. He is a well rounded expert in business development, marketing & sales with an entrepreneurial vision. A highly skilled leader of global teams including management of internal team members and external representatives.

Klaus Ruhmer is Director of Sales - Lithography at Rudolph Technologies. He is responsible for global sales of Rudolph's JetStep Lithography Systems.
Prior to this position, Klaus led the Business Developoment efforts at Multitest after 4 years in the BioEnergy industry. He joined BDI - BioEnergy after 11 years at SUSS MicroTec, a global leader in the area of Semiconductor Equipment. Klaus managed global marketing and sales for one of SUSS' product lines. He also managed the US Southwest sales territory for SUSS.

In 2007, Klaus co-founded a very successful international package forwarding business called BONVU.COM. Besides many logistical services for individuals and small businesses, BONVU.COM offers people from all around the globe to shop online in USA and have the items consolidated and forwarded to their doorstep.

Klaus is a native Austrian and holds a BS in Electronics and Telecommunications Technology. He worked in software development before he moved to the US and joined the semiconductor industry in 1996.

Specialties:
* Semiconductor manufacturing processes including Lithography, Advanced Packaging, Bumping, WLCSP, 3D Packaging, 3D Integration, Wafer Bonding, Device Bonding, MEMS and Final Test.
* International logistics: Package Forwarding / Paketweiterleitung
* Biodiesel and Biogas
* Alternative Energy technologies

* Sales / marketing of capital goods
* Negotiation skills, contract negotiations
* International experience: US/Canada, Europe, Asia
* USA Country Manager
