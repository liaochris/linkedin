Alex Volk's Summary

Seasoned online marketing professional with over 10 years of post-MBA experience including marketing strategy, online advertising program and network development, and search engine marketing.

I currently lead Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Design, User Experience (UX) and site search efforts for Microsoft.com in the Central Marketing Group at Microsoft. We're focused on influencing the structure, content, and authority of the Microsoft.com to increase the quantity and quality of referrals from search engines to the site and delivering great user experiences across our properties.

Since founding this practice I have grown organic search referrals from roughly 12% to over 30% to Microsoft.com and deliver over 200M organic referrals per month to the site. I lead a team of experts that are actively involved in the search community (both inside and outside of Microsoft) and speak on in-house SEO and enterprise SEO topics at conferences and large organizations.

In my role as User Experience Director I partner with Product Marketing, IT, and my UX Designers to analyze and create compelling user experiences (based on their own needs) that drive high satisfaction. My approach includes UX strategy development, user insight analysis, storyboarding and wire framing, user testing and usability analysis, and a focus on continuous improvement.

Previously, I led a team focused on display advertising operations and marketing for Microsoft.com. We grew the advertising footprint on MIcrosoft.com by almost 100% and increased engagement with the ad engine by double-digit percentages.

Specialties: marketing strategy, search engine marketing, search engine optimization, display advertising, online marketing, SEO, SEA, SEM, online traffic acquisition, social search.
