Mark Hanf's Experience

Broker, President

Pacific Private Money, Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Financial Services industry

August 2008 – Present (5 years 5 months) Novato, CA

Mark founded Pacific Private Money in 2008, and in 4 years has built it into one of the most prolific and successful private loan originators in Northern California. With a staff of 8, Pacific is funding on average 15-20 loans to real estate investors each month.

The company specializes in originating real estate loans for experienced investors, and funds its loans through its client's savings and retirement plans. The company helps borrowers obtain scarce financing, and provides its investor-lenders with high, fixed-rate yields on their savings.

Fund Manager

Pacific Private Money Fund I, LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Financial Services industry

March 2013 – Present (10 months) San Francisco Bay Area

Pacific Private Money Fund I is a real estate mortgage pool fund that provides investors with high, above-average yields secured by real estate.

Radio Show Co-Host

Fox News Radio's "The Best of Investing"

August 2010 – Present (3 years 5 months) San Francisco Bay Area

The Best of Investing is a weekly 1-hour radio show on Fox News Radio AM910, highlighting traditional and non-traditional investing in today's market climate. Topics include alternative real estate-based investments. Weekly guests bring new topics of general interest. The show is rated #5 in the Bay Area in the investment category. Listen live at 1:00pm every Saturday.

Associate

Pacific Equity And Capital

January 2007 – July 2008 (1 year 7 months) San Rafael, CA

A real estate services and development company specializing in originating private money loans. Its products included short-term, high-yield Trust Deeds and equity interests in joint venture developments.

Vice President, President

Hanf Properties, Inc.

1982 – 2006 (24 years) San Rafael, CA

Founded by Mark's father Horst Hanf in 1965, this was a family-held real estate development company. Starting out building single-family homes in Marin County, the company progressed to larger multi-family and commercial projects such as apartments, condo's, office and retail buildings. Over the years, the company's portfolio included such properties as marinas, RV parks and motels.
