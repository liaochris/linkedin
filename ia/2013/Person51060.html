Guy McPhail CPA, PFS, CTC,CFP®'s Summary

Hello, my name is Guy McPhail and I am the President and CEO of The GM Financial Group LLC. My designations include CPA, PFS, CTC,and CFP®. I am also a best selling co-author and financial and income tax educator.

My firm has a unique and proprietary system and a step by step process for the small business owner and executive that enables me to determine which services and solutions my client needs in order to help them achieve all of their financial goals.

I most often find that business owners

•Pay too much in income taxes
•Has the wrong entity in place
•Has the wrong retirement plan
•Pays too much in investment advisory fees
•Has the wrong insurance in place
•Doesn't have an exit strategy
•Doesn't have time to put an effective plan in place

We help the small business owner gain control over their financial lives, save them thousands of dollars in missed opportunities and give them a road map to more freedom and financial independence.

We are a fee-ONLY no commission firm (not to be confused with fee-based) and a proud member of NAPFA (the organization of fee only financial planners who recognize the CFP® designation as the leader in financial planning).

We offer a no obligation mini audit of your small business to show you how we may rescue thousands of dollars for you and put them to work for your financial success.

Thank you
