Debra Vey Voda-Hamilton's Summary

I am a conflict coach and mediator of conflicts between people involving animals. I practice alternative animal conflict resolution, using mediation and collaborative practice, in Contract, Civil, Criminal, Commercial, Divorce, Special Education and Veterans Affairs.

I speak all over the country at State Bar Animal Law Committee Meetings, Pet Industry Conferences and Veterinary Conferences about the methods you can use to address conflicts before they become unwieldy, keep the relationship between the parties intact and acknowledge the parties desire to avoid costly and time consuming litigation.

I am a member of the NYCLA-Animal Law committee, ABA-TIPS-Animal Law Committee, Eastern Irish Setter Association, Irish Setter Club of America, NYSBA- Women in the Law Committee, ABA-LPM-Women Rainmakers Committee, Westchester Bar Association, New York City Bar Association-Animal Law Committee and the Westchester Women's Bar Association,

Specialties: Mediation and Conflict Coaching of disagreements involving people about animals.
