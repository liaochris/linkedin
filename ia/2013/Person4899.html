Allen Wyke's Summary

Startup, turnaround and critical launch technologist and digital executive working, hands on, wherever needed. Spent most of my career in fulltime roles, but also called upon to advise and drop in to bridge gaps. Its all about bringing smart solutions to complicated or complex business and technology problems, including the cultural or passionately emotional ones, which can be tough. I feel comfortable working to navigate those situations.

I have worked for Internet companies since 1995. Unlike many technologist making the leap from Enterprise, my entire career has been in digital. Generally brought in for quick movement: turn around, rebuild, transition, or startup. Whether its a resource/team issue, your platform is crashing, or you need to grow your traffic. I can help layout strategies as well as own the execution of those plans. Its up to you; whatever you feel most comfortable with.

I have direct operational experience in digital media, online advertising, software and eCommerce and have worked for or implementedsolutions at roughly 200 companies over my career. At my core I am a technologist, where I often architect and lead the buildout of technology and infrastructure platforms (CTO hat), but have spent a significant amount of time on the business side as well (CDO hat). So lead generation, business development, traffic acquisition, analytics, SEO, editorial, AdOps, etc. are also areas of expertise.

Additionally, I have worked on various Internet standards groups and written over a dozen books. These days, I find myself working for a number of Digital Media, eCommerce and Online Advertising companies.

If you have a problem, challenge, issue or opportunity, then please reach out and drop me a note. You will hear from me and hear from me quickly. And if I can't help, then I may know someone who can.

Specialties

Technology strategies, product strategies, contract negotiations, staffing strategies, building engineering teams, mergers and acquisitions, mobile advertising, online/interactive media, content management systems, open source, commercial software, Web/Internet standards.
