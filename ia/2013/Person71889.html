Kristian Andersen's Summary

I work with entrepreneurs and business leaders to design compelling brand experiences, create new business models, and innovate new products and services. In addition to my work at Kristian Andersen + Associates, I'm an active angel investor and co-founder of Gravity Ventures, a seed-stage venture fund that invests in tech-focused startups.

I've co-founded a few startups, including; TinderBox, Visible.vc, Lesson.ly, and Pathagility and advise a number of others. I sit on a few start-up and non-profit boards, co-founded The Speak Easy, and serve as a mentor at The Next Big Thing. I split my time between Indianapolis and Little Rock, where I mix it up with my amazing bride and 4 kids.

You can find out more at www.kristian.vc

Specialties

Brand Consulting, Corporate Identity Design, User Experience Design, Service Design, New Ventures, Angel Investing

Visible.vc

July 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Indianapolis & San Francisco

With offices in San Francisco and Indianapolis, Visible was founded in the spring of 2012 with a very clear mandate – to simplify the sharing, management, and reporting of data between startups and investors. Visible was launched by a group of founders and investors that had been struggling with the vexing, but all too common, problems of tracking investment performance and managing communications between startups and investors. Their response was to build a comprehensive platform that helps investors, of all types, track and report on the performance of their individual and aggregate investments (including angel, seed, VC, and private equity funds). Equally important, Visible was engineered to provide founders with tools that simplify investor communication, capitalization table management, and ongoing performance tracking.
