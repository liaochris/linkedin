Clark Gilder's Summary

CEO and founder of GSF, we provide THE newest and most innovative electronic payment products and services based on our patented paperless Check 21 technologies or Electronic Payment Orders (EPO) as defined in the 2009 Fed whitepaper. GSF's "Digitally Originated Checks" or DOCs provide a next generation payment product for multiple use cases: mobile, P2P, small business AP/AR among others based on existing Check 21 check image standards (X9.37, X9.180 etc.). GSF's broadly patented "electronic check" technologies are the foundation of the payment platform which runs our business. Building and designing GSF utilizes my executive level software skills developed during my 12 year tenure at Microsoft in Redmond WA, where award winning results were generated by working across the entire software eco-system, managing internal dev teams, evangelism to external ISVs and sales, marketing and support roles. Finally, GSF is angel funded and supported by a board of advisors who are world-class banking experts which gives GSF a head start in a complex, slow moving and heavily regulated banking and payments marketplace. GSF Go Checks(tm) - are the Check 2.0 (tm) solution!

Specialties: During my 25 year software career, I have developed expertise across the entire business including: general management, managing product teams, include leading product management, design, development, sales and marketing, working with industry analysts and PR firms, evangelism, managing 3rd party ISVs and VAR channels, product training, and customer support. I've utilized my extensive software business experience as a key member of ATA for due diligence as an angel / VC investor evaluating business models and investment opportunities and helping coach and mentor many startups.

Clark Gilder's Publications

  • US Patents 7,539,646

    • US Patent Office
    • December 1, 2008
    Authors: Clark Gilder

    Covers the fundamental aspects of creating paperless checks (or EPO's as the Fed Whitepaper describes them), these "virtual drafts" are not EFT like ACH debits but true UCC check payments in electronic Check 21 form without needing an original paper check. This process is superior to Remote Deposit Capture, i.e. taking a picture of a check with your phone for deposit, because the "payor" sends the check image to you and you skip the photograph step & go to automatic deposit. This groundbreaking patent covers creating any form of Check 21 file without an original paper check as well as conversion of ACH files into Check 21 items.
