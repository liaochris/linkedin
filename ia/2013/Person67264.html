John Worley's Summary

I have vast successful background in the marketing of multilines insurance and the recruitment and management of insurance agents, both new and experienced. I have managed agents in Virginia, Ohio, Maryland, West Virginia, New York, Maryland, Indiana, Delaware, and North Carolina while serving in different roles. I have negotiated several agency contracts to move(roll) entire books of business from competing carriers to my carrier to help increase market share. My new agent recruiting record has been outstanding and assisted in moving Virginia from last to first in weekly production with Countryway Insurance in less than 1 year. My background involves working with public schools serving as a board director for the Verona Community Association. I have experience creating and being the president of non profit corporations that specifically focus on helping Virginia high school athletes obtain college scholarships and grants toward funding their education. I have also coached a successful AAU boys traveling basketball team(Virginia Cobras(1998-2002) and am a winner of the Commonwealth Games of Virginia Gold Medal in Basketball. I also coached the first ever boys basketball team to represent Virginia in the inaugural State Games of America in 1999 where we finished 3rd. Fortune 500 Consultant Morris Schectman led several team building seminars I was active in and these helped shape my views and practices on managing accountability and change. I am currently focusing my total energy and efforts as Sales Development Leader with Horace Mann Insurance, a company formed by educators for educators.
