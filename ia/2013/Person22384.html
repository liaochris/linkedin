Charlie Carpenter's Summary

Educate Virtually is web based e-Learning for businesses, organizations, and individuals. The intention is to take our proven course offerings and make them available to anyone on their computer.

All of our courses have been proven in the field around the world.

We provide businesses, organizations, and individuals with outstanding, relevant, and high quality course selections that are worthwhile investments in continuing education.

We have taken proven live classroom courses that successfully transfer knowledge and skills, and developed impactful, self-paced e-learning offerings.

Our format is interactive with self-testing and provides formal certification upon course completion.

We also can bring training, coaching, and facilitation services to you.

Visit us http://www.educatevirtually.com

Our experience comprises 26 years in the Consumer, Financial, Industrial, and Pharmaceutical sectors. We implement programs that reduce costs, reduce cycle time, and minimize variation in business processes resulting in improved productivity and quality of products and service to customers.
