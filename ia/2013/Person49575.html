Misty Lackie's Summary

As chief executive officer for Go Smart Solutions, LLC, I oversee programming, web development, business operations and new developments for the company and its affiliated web sites.

I started my professional career working as a systems management supervisor for Alloy Inc./CCS, an action sports-focused apparel and gear company, charged with database management, as well as networking and programming. I also worked in networking, database and systems management with the Denmat Corporation, a dental products company located in Santa Maria, Calif.

In 2002, I combined my experience in database design and software development with my entrepreneurial spirit to form my own company, Go Smart Solutions, LLC, to develop SaaS applications. The company has since expanded to include online services, Advantly.com, FaceItPages.com, EmailFlyerAds.com and CallModo.com. Go Smart Solutions, LLC SaaS applications combined have grown to a user base of over 500,000 nationwide.

Specialties: Web development, advertising, project management, PHP, HTML5, CSS, XHTML, JavaScript, AJAX, XML, MySQL, MS SQL, SaaS, PPC
