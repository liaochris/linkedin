Each year, the Chamber presents the Small Business Leadership Awards to honor companies that continue to excel and grow. The emphasis of the recognition is on leadership in keeping with the Chamber’s principle of Leadership in Motion.

The South Metro Denver Substance Abuse Coalition is a Resource for the Community. We are comprised of and represented by Behavioral Specialists, the Mental Health Field, Recovery and Treatment Facilities, Education Professionals, Law Enforcement, the Judicial System, other Government Agencies, DC Youth Initiative, Parks and Recreation, 12 Step Fellowships, Parents and members of the Community. The Substance Abuse Coalition of Douglas County works to identify solutions and share resources to combat substance abuse from prevention through recovery across all ages.

5280 Drug Testing Company, LLC

September 2010 – Present (3 years) Centennial, CO

5280 Drug Testing Company, LLC is Colorado's premier location for pre-employment, post-accident and random drug screening for both private and corporate clients. We can provide a variety of drug testing solutions with paperless chain of custody and web based reporting. We are also part of the Quest Provider Network and are a Quest Preferred Provider, and we provide other lab based testing through a variety of SAMHSA approved laboratories. We also do testing for a variety of synthetic drugs like synthetic marijuana (K2/Spice) and synthetic cocaine (bath salts).

If you have any questions about any other the services we provide, please contact me directly at the office or by email.

If you need drug testing for any reason, we are your source. Call 303-953-2619, email tricia@5280drugtesting.com or visit www.5280drugtesting.com for more info.
