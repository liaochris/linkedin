Julie Jenkins' Summary

I help families spread their wings in a new home that better suits their needs. I take the time to listen so that I can discover what is important to them and ultimately find exactly what they need in the neighborhood they want. I take the stress out of moving by educating my clients and protecting them through the buying and selling process. I take the extra time and make it personal because I care.

By the way, I am never to busy for your referrals. Providing superior customer service to my clients is always my first priority. The finest compliment that I could ever receive is a referral from my friends and clients.

In addition to being a full time real estate sales representative, I am a founding member and the coordinator for Women in Networking Sarnia, a networking group for professional women. Email for information about WIN and find out how to get involved.

I am also a member of Lambton Toastmasters and our clubs current Vice President of Education and past President. I encourage you to visit us to see what Toastmasters can do for you. We meet each Thursday at noon at the Goodwill Centre at Wellington and Murphy.

I have been a home owner in Sherwood Village subdivision in Sarnia for over 18 years. I have planned and organized two annual community events in Sherwood with my colleague Shana. We had a very successful community garage sale in June and in October we collected over 3200 lbs of food for the Inn of the Good Shepherd.

Specialties: Sarnia real estate, Sherwood Village real estate, Sarnia home sales, public speaking
