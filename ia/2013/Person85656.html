Maria Cucinotta is an International Image Advisor, Speaker and specialist in personal branding, dedicated to improving client creditability and influence.

She was the first Image Consultant in Australia to be qualified as a Personal Branding Strategist with Reach International, the No.1 global Personal Branding company.

Maria works internationally with clients optimizing her client’s Image and style to their Personal Brand, focusing on the ABC’s of Image: Appearance, Behaviour and Communication. She has extensive international training in Business Etiquette, International Protocol and Leading with Influence.

She is a certified Master Results Coach and NLP Practitioner and Trainer, specialising in Rapport and Presentation Skills, Her clients have included Estee Lauder, 3M, and Myer. She has worked with a range of private and corporate companies and SME’s.

Working from the inside Maria transforms the individual on a level that is congruent with their values and image.
