Andrew Wood's Summary

Andrew Wood is the one of the world's leading marketing strategists and an expert on lead generation, golf, resort and real estate marketing, although his successes reach far beyond those industries.

He is the author of over 20 books on sales and marketing including, Cunningly Clever Marketing, Cunningly Clever Selling and The Golf Marketing Bible. Andrew speaks worldwide on sales and marketing topics and is in high demand as a copywriter and marketing consultant, He owns Legendary Marketing a Tampa/Orlando area advertising agency and is a principle in Legendary Golf & Resort Management.

He can be reached directly at (352) 266-2099 or andrew@LegendaryMarketing.com

Specialties: marketing strategy, sales, internet, advertising, golf, resorts, real estate, private clubs, martial arts, destinations, travel, leadership, personal development, copywritting,
