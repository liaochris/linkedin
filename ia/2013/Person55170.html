INCAE MBA Oath Club's Summary

The Club's actively support the global movement to professionalize the business management career and maximize the contribution of MBA GRADUATES to the sustainable prosperity of society utilizing the MBA OATH as an essential step forward.

Our Mission: We are a student-led organization promoting the use of ethical standards in business and management, by means of the MBA Oath.
