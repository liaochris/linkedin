Jeffrey A. Cohen's Summary

I graduated from Penn Law in 1988 and specialized in corporate and appellate litigation before going into business.

Since 1996, in partnership with my brother Marc, I've founded and sold four technology businesses to publicly traded companies.

Until April 2008, I served as co-CEO of Teleperformance Interactive, the automated technologies company my brother and I sold to Teleperformance in 2004. Teleperformance is the world’s leading customer relations management company with about 138,000 employees.

After selling Teleperformance Interactive, my brother and I founded Techstar Partners, LLC, through which we invest in technology companies and advise them.

My first novel, The Killing of Mindi Quintana, was published by Welcome Rain in 2010. I'm currently at work on my second.

I began traveling to China in 2011 and have lived in Beijing and Tianjin for just over a year now. I'll split my time between the US and China in 2013. I've been working on my book here, making good friends, and involving TechStar in the business and investment communities.
