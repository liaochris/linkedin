Eric Baze's Summary

Call me a hybrid, a unicorn, a generalist, a jack of all trades -- all would be accurate.

I have 14 years of experience creating and managing all aspects of online experiences. I specialize in visual design, front-end development, content development and social media within a marketing context for desktop and mobile device delivery.

Secondary skills related to this include user experience, information architecture, back-end development, and server administration. I also have experience in print design, photography, and digital audio/video production.

From researching and recommending technology stacks to brand positioning, email marketing, event promotion and CRM -- I've had a hand in it. I won't pretend I'm an expert at everything, but I know when to ask, when to read, and when to outsource.

As the industry matures, I am very interested in taking on projects to both focus on and enhance my visual design and user experience skills while maintaining technical proficiency.
