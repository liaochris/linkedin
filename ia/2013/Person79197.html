Laurie Bornstein's Summary

Hi, my name is Laurie Bornstein and my passion, the thing I love most, is helping you create a sense of comfort and balance in your life. Empowering YOU to find inspiration in your surroundings and success within yourself.

Our lives are full of twists and turns, many of us have had more than one career. Along that road we've gained LOTS of valuable experience. The goal isn't to leave our past behind, the goal is to design our lives from our life experiences, the experiences of others, books we’ve read, classes or workshops we’ve taken and the expert advice of the pros and mentors we’ve had the honor of learning from.

Creating a life & biz YOU love means looking honestly at what’s working and what isn’t working within your life, your way of working, and in most cases, your living environment.

It’s not about having enough money or not having enough money, or following the latest trend or guru. It’s about understanding until you change, your quality of life is going to remain the way it is.

My journey has taken me down many roads, each experience has given me the tools, knowledge and strategies that have uniquely combined and crafted who I am and what I do. From becoming a fashion buyer at 21, having twins when I was 27, to owning my own decorating business, becoming a feng shui consultant and teacher, CEO of the International Feng Shui Guild and Life & Biz Soul-utions coaching expert. It amazes me to see the trajectory and opportunities I've been blessed to have had.

I've had some pretty incredible experiences and absolutely amazing opportunities. I'd like to help you do the same. I can't wait to see what comes next!

Laurie Bornstein's Experience

President

Harmony Life, LLC

Privately Held; Myself Only; Design industry

July 2006 – Present (7 years 2 months)

Laurie is the President and CEO of Harmony Life, LLC., and the founder of Biz Soul-utions from the Inside Out™, a dynamic, transformative coaching program designed to help women entrepreneurs embrace success in all areas of their lives while building a powerful base for business success. Laurie is an internationally renowned mentor, coach and feng shui teacher. With over 17 years of experience, Laurie combines her unique blend of knowledge, training and skills into her coaching and feng shui training programs, creating powerful learning environments that combine the practical with the transcendental. As an inspiring leader and in-demand teacher and coach, Laurie has helped hundreds of entrepreneurs learn how to use their knowledge, skills and the environments around them as guideposts to achieve deeply personal and highly focused business and personal success.

Laurie is a Master Teacher and Educator of Interior Alignment®. She is one of the original Interior Alignment® board members, where she served for six years. Laurie is also active in the International Feng Shui Guild® where she served on the board for over seven years, first as the Education Director and then as CEO. Under her guidance, the IFSG® developed professional standards for practitioners and schools within the feng shui community, while expanding the visibility, viability and recognition of feng shui and feng shui professionals throughout the world.

Laurie has hosted a monthly Internet Radio Talk Show, written for The Examiner.com as the National Feng Shui Lifestyle Examiner and has been featured in magazines, TV and radio shows throughout the US.

Living Space Designs was the name of my company when we were based in Kansas. We specialized in Interior Alignment Feng Shui training, consultations, holistic design and life coaching.

Laurie Bornstein's Education

Other

Interior Design, Advanced Feng Shui, several schools

1995 – 2003

Laurie Bornstein is a licensed Master Teacher of Interior Alignment®. She is one of the few teachers in the world certified to teach beginning, advanced and teacher level training. Laurie is one of the original Interior Alignment Board members and is a current member of the Advisory Panel. During the 6 years she was a Foundation Council member, Laurie had the opportunity to work closely with Denise Linn in the development of the Interior Alignment training materials and various other aspects of the organization.

Laurie has been an active participant in the International Feng Shui Guild for over 10 years. She has served as committee chair for the Education Committee, and as the Education Director and as the CEO.

Laurie’s practice incorporates a synergistic blend of Feng Shui methods and techniques in combination with the elements of holistic design and the power of life coaching to create a sense of comfort and balance in all areas of her clients and students lives.
