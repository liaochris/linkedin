Josh Ross' Summary

With great experience as a Realtor servicing all areas in Montgomery County, MD as well as the surrounding counties in MD & into Northern Virginia, I help both buyers and sellers meet their real estate objectives. I have extensive knowledge of each of the communities located in and around Montgomery County MD and will work tirelessly on your behalf to make your next home buying or home selling experience a pleasant and successful one. With a strong focus on customer satisfaction & delivering seemless results to my clients by utilizing both the newest & best technology as well as the ever important personal touch & communication I continually strive to improve my service & results to ensure that each & every client receives the presonal attention they deserve & their real estate goals are met. With a strong focus on residential real estate as well as the brokerage of land for development of both residential & commercial communities, this knowledge & information allows me to provide more accurate information of the latest trends & growth areas to my clients.
