Joseph Langan, CSCP's Summary

Results oriented Supply Chain, Operations, Compliance and Logistics professional with over 20 years diversified experience in small to large union and non-union manufacturing businesses serving the United States Department of Defense, steel, automotive, heavy truck and hydraulic/pneumatic industries. Creative problem solver with a proven ability to develop cross-functional teams to increase net profit by improving and systematizing procurement, quality, safety, manufacturing and operations. A dependable, hands-on and self-motivated professional with steadfast attention to detail and the ability to organize enduring solutions to complex challenges in the metal, rubber, plastic and textile industries.

Specialties: • Profit & Loss Responsibility • Supply Chain Management • Lean Manufacturing • Sourcing and Procurement • Logistics • New product design and development • OSHA and Labor compliance • Conformance to standards: Military, SAE, AMS, ASTM, AWS • First Article Submission, PPAP, FMEA • Recruiting and team building • ISO 9001:2008 & AS9100 • Expansive knowledge of materials and processes including rubber, plastic, steel and aluminum, hose making, extrusion, compound mixing, CNC machining and manual & robotic welding
