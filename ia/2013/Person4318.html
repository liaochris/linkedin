Alex Trup's Summary

First and foremost I'm a lover of music across all genres, and while I lack musical talent, I have an ear and eye for artists that could be commercially successful given the right opportunity. That is why I decided to focus on applying my experience in marketing communications and knowledge of digital technologies to the music industry. Evocative Group Ltd. is the company that was born from that passion, with Evocative Records (www.EvocativeRecords.com) being my first foray into the music industry.
