Adam Lewkovitz, PMP's Summary

Adam Lewkovitz is a customer obsessed and results oriented Product Manager and Digital Strategist. He applies a flexible, efficient and analytical approach to all his work and has successfully guided products and projects in the financial services, retail, health care and hi-tech sectors for Global 2000 companies and industry leaders.

Adam has a proven record of driving new ideas through to realization, from research and design through development and launch, to the screens of tens of millions of happy users.
