Dimitri Cools' Summary

- I started my career as international truckdriver (throughout Europe). This job gave me the opportunity to explore my continent as well as to explore my own life.
- In 1999 I worked as account manager for Argentia nv (part of the Rethmann Photorecycling Company)
- Afterwards I started my own carreer as freelance graphic designer (since 2001). I worked for agencies such as Duval Guillaume Antwerp, Duval Guillaume Corporate, Duval Guillaume Dialog (CreAdvice), BZ Factory, Germaine BBDO, Alligence, CAST, Darwin BBDO, Rebound, De ideegraaf, WP Creaties, ... but I also worked for media: Knack, Trends, Elle Magazine, Sensa, BMW Magazine, Galeria, Focus, ...
- In 2005 I started my own agency called "Cools Concepts". Our main goal is to be a no-nonsence agency. In the meanwhile we work with 8 employees and a full range of subcontractors and specialists.

Specialties: Specialized in corporate and retail communication. We always want to create communication that works, applications that generate more sales. Design and marketing have a goal which is not only creating nice stuff. Nevertheless "details" as typography and our design-craftsmanship give our realisations that "final touch". Last but not least, our baseline is "always thinking for you".
