Bonni Titgemeyer CEBS, SPHR, CHRP, CMS's Summary

Bonni Titgemeyer is the Managing Director of The Employers’ Choice Inc. She has been in the human resources consulting field for more than twenty years, in both a Canadian and international context. With significant business acumen and an HR focus, she has highly-sought after experience which can effectively address a myriad of workplace issues. She is particularly adept at managing projects with cross-border HR issues.

Specialties:
HR Department structuring and restructuring
HR needs assessments
Compensation design and positioning (including executive compensation, incentive systems, and benchmarking)
HR role profiling and search
OD projects
Legislative compliance (pay equity, employment standards, diversity, health and safety)
HR project management
Best practice training


Extensive experience working with investors who buy and sell businesses, owner/entrpreneurial and large family-run businesses.

Extensive experience working on multi-national projects.

January 2003 – Present (10 years 7 months)

The Employers’ Choice Inc. (TEC) is a Toronto-based human resources consulting firm with highly qualified and experienced human resource professionals.

Originally founded by three lawyers, we provide employers with the tools, resources, and support they need to develop and optimize their key asset—their people.

TEC provides a full range of human resources services on both a consulting and outsourced basis. Our solutions are based upon best practices and the latest thinking in the field of human resources management.

We also offer interactive, educational, and fun training programs on a variety of human resources-related issues. Our training programs are custom-designed to improve the performance of your people and help achieve corporate excellence within your Company.
