Eric Fletcher's Summary

Eric is the Chief Marketing Officer of McGlinchey Stafford PLLC, a US law firm focused on providing legal counsel and assistance to business.

His professional focus is on strategic business development, communications and marketing. His experience spans virtually every aspect of these disciplines -- from award winning copy and production in the advertising and broadcasting arenas, to designing and successfully leading new business development efforts, including individual pursuits valued at more than $150,000,000 in the professional service arena. In addition, Eric taught advertising copy writing and production at the undergraduate level.

He has provided strategic planning and consulting to local entrepreneurs and Fortune 100 companies, BtoB and BtoC enterprises, and two of the world's largest professional service organizations.

As a law firm Chief Marketing Officer / CMO, this experience is employed with two primary objectives: to expand proactive business development strategies in a way that stimulates organic growth; and to protect and enhance the brand of the firm.

Over the past two decades his track record includes
-- successfully leading marketing and BD teams
-- major target pursuits
-- direction of key client team initiatives, and
-- the alignment of communication and BD strategy for international consulting organizations and, for the past dozen years, AmLaw 100 and NLJ 250 law firms.

He writes the Marketing Brain Fodder Blog, has published numerous white papers and marketing primers, was named a 2012 Top Private Company CMO, was named among the "50 Marketers Over 50 You Should Know" by The Global CMO Magazine, and is co-author of the October 2012 book "8 Mandates For Social Media Marketing Success."

Specialties
