Chad Boyd's Summary

I started my IT career in November of 1998 at TravelNow.com as a programmer. In the three years I worked there we went from a small privately held company to a publicly traded company on the NASDAQ and ultimately were purchased by Hotels.com.

I left TravelNow.com in 2001 and moved to St. Louis, MO to work for Ciber, a large consulting firm with locations all over the US and abroad. I was placed at client sites such as Parents as Teachers, Illinois Department of Human Services, Barnes-Jewish Hospital and Enterprise Rent-A-Car. I worked with many talented people at Ciber and quickly increased my expertise while working with them.

In 2003 I left Ciber and the consulting world to work for Interactive Hotel Solutions, a company that a former colleague from TravelNow.com and another friend started in Springfield, MO. I had the opportunity to work with many different technologies at IHS, and as Director of IT, I was responsible for building out and maintaining the core platform that enables customers to book hotel reservations through several different travel suppliers. Part of my work included communicating with multiple payment gateways for credit card transactions. During my employment at IHS, I completely revamped and streamlined practically all of the tools and processes used by the software team department on a daily basis.

Since leaving IHS, I've returned to the consulting world as an independent, using Ruby and Rails on a daily basis to help clients succeed in their business.

I'm also the founder of Txt2Give, a service that makes it extremely simple for organizations to receive donations via text message.

Specialties: Ruby/Rails, jQuery, CSS, RSpec, Cucumber, Passenger, Nginx, Git, Java, XML, AJAX, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Apache, JBoss, Tomcat, Subversion
