Lisa K. Ford's Experience

Owner of Invent Your Image, Certified Image Consultant and Creator of "Simple Style Solutions"

Invent Your Image, LLC

January 2007 – Present (6 years 10 months) Tampa/St. Petersburg, Florida Area

As a certified image consultant, Lisa offers clients a unique combination of expertise: corporate experience, vast knowledge regarding the fashion and beauty industries and years of presentation excellence. Through personal consultations and group presentations, Lisa works with professionals and non-professionals alike, guiding and educating them on how to enhance their appearance, use color to their advantage and dress to honor their personal style statement.

Lisa is a certified image consultant through Association of Image Consultants International the world’s most recognized image consultant organization and served as the VP of Communications for the Central Florida AICI Chapter. Lisa has trained and is certified from two internationally known schools-Sci/Art- color theory, significance of human coloration, and 12 tone personal color analysis and Fashion Feng Shui®-five element style.

Lisa has been featured and quoted various times in the Tampa Tribune from Fashion Feng Shui, what to wear for a courtroom appearance, 5 tips for mixing prints and patterns, Tampa fashion is a flop, Joe Madden’s hoddie controversy and more. Her energetic and interactive style makes her a sought-after training resource and speaker for organizations and individuals alike.

Lisa is also the Co-Founder of “Charity Chics” -women connecting for a cause. Charity Chics has financially assisted local Tampa Bay organizations like Dress for Success, Hillsborough Achievement and Resources Centers, P.A.C.E. for Girls, Bay Area Legal Services and Metropolitan Ministries, to name a few. Lisa enthusiastically supports, volunteers and contributes to Dress for Success Tampa Bay (past board member), Outreach Assisting Students in School, American Heart Association, HARC, Mabry Elementary and more.


For additional information about Invent Your Image, LLC contact Lisa K. Ford at info@inventyourimage.com or 813.766.8375.
