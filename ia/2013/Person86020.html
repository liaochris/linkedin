Marina Edelman, M.A., MFT's Summary

www.marinaedelman.com

"Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life" - thanks Confucius, I couldn't have said it better my self.

I eliminate psychobabble and facilitate clients awareness of their issues as well as guide them towards personal solutions. I am looking to expand my private practice through personal and professional recommendations.

As a psychotherapist in private practice I provide counseling to individual, couples, and families who want to solve problems, improve relationships, relieve anxiety/stress and depression, re-build self esteem and perhaps take a fresh look at their lives.

My office is conveniently located at The Landing in Westlake Village. However I also offer phone and video sessions to busy executives, care-givers, and those physically disabled.

Sliding fee scale offered on the honor system.
