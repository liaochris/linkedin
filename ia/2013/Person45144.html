Floris Vlasveld's Summary

Inspire develops innovative web applications for computer, tablet and mobile that make people smile. Our method is based on close cooperation with clients and allows for continuous improvement. We use the world's most advanced web technologies, such as Ruby on Rails and HTML5, and always take a test-driven development approach. Inspire websites and web applications are safe, extremely scalable and future proof. Visit http://www.inspire.nl for more information.

Before Inspire, I founded internet advisory company Eyos. My team and I supported organizations such as Mediq, TypeTopia, Bartiméus and Syntens with the development and optimization of internet activities. In 2009 our work for client TypeTopia was rewarded with the third place in Computable’s Best Business Case Award.

In 2011 I was involved with Deloitte's Emerging Solutions service line. Within Deloitte I worked on cloud computing and web application functional design for Canon Europe.

I completed an Information Science bachelors and a Business Informatics masters (thesis: e-business strategy development) at Utrecht University.

Specialties: Entrepreneurship, business/IT consultancy, Scrum, web technology.

Floris Vlasveld's Projects

  • Elsevier Journal Insights

    Team Members: Floris Vlasveld

    The Journal Insights web application offers academic authors the possibility to view important statistics about Elsevier’s approximately 1,800 academic journals.   • Kanker.nl

    Team Members: Floris Vlasveld

    Inspire was contracted by the Dutch Cancer Society, the Dutch Federation of Cancer Patient Organisations and the Integrated Cancer Centres to realise the www.kanker.nl website, a digital platform for everyone whose life is affected by cancer. We are currently working with a team of Ruby on Rails programmers, front-end developers and designers to carry forward the platform’s development.

  • AllNomads.com

    Team Members: Floris Vlasveld

    AllNomads.com allows people to easily collect and organize all the places they want to explore, visit and remember. Now that the first version of the website is online, AllNomads and Inspire are working on the next version of the platform, which will introduce sharing places with other users.

  • NVPO Relationship Management System

    Team Members: Floris Vlasveld

    The Dutch Society for Psychosocial Oncology (NVPO) uses the NVPO Relationship Management System to manage its relations, such as members and experts. Functions that the web application offers include automatically validating addresses, processing new members and creating and managing membership invoices.

  • Calibris Sterk Leerklimaat

    Team Members: Floris Vlasveld

    Inspire is currently developing a web application for knowledge institute Calibris that allows their advisors to measure and monitor the learning climate within organizations. It consists of a survey system for respondents and a comprehensive analysis and management environment.
