Victoria Cooper's Summary

I'm a Freelance marketing specialist providing flexible and affordable marketing services and support to UK based SMEs. With over 10 years Marketing Management experience working with various organisations, I can help you to achieve your business goals through strategic, targeted marketing planning and implementation. I can offer help with all aspects of marketing including planning, research, communications and PR, and am also able to offer a flexible in-house service whereby I can act as your company's very own Marketing Manager for as long as you require. I can work with you and your team whenever you need additional support and can always guarantee a practical, hands-on approach that achieves the results that you want.

Being a freelancer, I only work with a select number of clients who I tend to develop an ongoing relationship with. Since doing this, I have gained experience in a variety of sectors including retail and consumer finance, building services, catering and leisure. I provide support with all aspects of my client's marketing strategies including advertising (TV and radio), brochures, letters, articles, websites, social media, blogs, PPC, SEO, eMarketing and anything else that may be deemed relevant, to increase sales and further the position of the company.

Specialties: Marketing strategy and planning, digital marketing, marketing communications, advertising, PR, integrated marketing communications.
