Emily Foshee's Summary

The Internet has changed the way companies market themselves forever. Gone are the days of blasting a corporate message in the traditional way: TV, radio and newspaper, and hoping people see your message and buy your product.

Today, consumer engagement is the way to build a business. But what is consumer engagement, exactly? It's building business relationships by managing the buying process through giving prospects the information they need to make their buying decisions at each point along the sales cycle.

Consumer engagement is all about helping the prospect meet their business goals...it's not about selling! That comes later.

This takes research, strategic planning and execution. It also takes creating the best content you can to build trust with your prospects. Through this content, you establish yourself as an industry expert, build your brand, and are top of mind when your prospect needs to make a purchasing decision.

At Degrees of Engagement, I provide spot-on content that gets your prospect's attention. Jump on the engagement marketing bandwagon! I help you build that relationship through copy that educates the consumer, not sells to the consumer. I'll help you market your company through blogs, white papers, case studies, videos, and more.

I am a veteran public relations professional with more than 15 years of experience in corporate communications and public relations management.

I have managed communications for some of the country's best known corporations, and have gained an understanding of how successful communications initiatives work. Through the years I've developed a writing style that will get your message across to your target audience.



Check out my website at http://www.degreesofengagement.com

If you want to build profitable business relations that stand the test of time, engagement marketing is the way to success.

Contact me and let's talk about how I can help you reach your business goals.
