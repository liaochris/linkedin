Jenn Lumetta's Summary

I graduated from Grand Valley State University with a BFA in Graphic Design. Though my studies have presented me with the basic design principles and creative thought I need to succeed in this field, I believe an education is never truly complete. Whether it be formal education, discussion, or life experiences, I continue to seize every opportunity I get to be inspired and to continue learning. I believe in an ever-shifting design style that changes to fit each new project presented yet a loyalty to the almighty brand. Creative Problem Solving is my passion and my reason for choosing the design field.
