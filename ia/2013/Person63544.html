Jamie Heuyard's Summary

From servers and databases to support and marketing, I have over 12 years experience helping business and individuals accomplish their goals using Web Technology. I have dedicated the past 5+ years to mastering the open source CMS known as WordPress, which currently powers over 56 Million sites and counting.

Why WordPress? In a nutshell, it allows the development of websites that are affordable, speedy, Internet Marketing friendly, and easy for a non-developer to update. This free software is surrounded by an incredible community of designers, developers, marketers, and users that offer both free and premium themes, plugins, and support to enhance the platform even further.

I have worked hands-on with WP site owners to build, enhance, and manage their WP sites. This experience has provided a vast knowledge of all aspects WordPress. I am an active member of the WP community, keeping on top of the latest themes, plugins, trends, and best practices.

Do you currently operate a WordPress site or considering building one? Feel free to reach out to me with any questions or requests and I would be glad to help!
