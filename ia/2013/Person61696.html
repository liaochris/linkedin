Jenn Coonce's Summary

I am a seasoned UX professional with over 15 years of proven experience in developing customer experience and product strategies across multiple platforms. I help businesses get to know their customers and create digital experiences to optimize customer interactions.

Areas of expertise include:

- User research design and facilitation (usability testing, card modeling, brainstorms, etc.)
- Facilitation of group brainstorming and strategy workshops
- Project leadership of cross-functional and UX teams
- Analysis of user research
- User Experience and product strategy development
- Cickstream data and quantitative research analysis
- Agile process: Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO)
- Design of rich digital experiences
- User experience design, including wire frames, task flows, experience maps and other documentation

I tend to work on a freelance or consultant basis, and can build and lead project teams.

Clients have included Aetna, Ameritrade, AT&T, Boyds Collectibles, British Airways, Canyons Resort, Chase Bank, Cisco, Closer to Truth, Deloitte, Everyday Health, IBM, Insala, Intel, New Balance, Nokia, Polaroid, Rocket Fuel, Scholastic, Teleflora, Verizon and Volvo.

I have worked with agencies including Behavior Design, Big Spaceship, Euro RSCG, and McGarry Bowen.

Specialties: User experience, User research & analysis, Interaction Design, Product strategy, Information Architecture, Team management, Presentation, Creative Process, Content creation and strategy, Copywriting, Acquisition and Retention, Competitive and Heuristic Analysis

You can find me at http://www.jenncoonce.com.
