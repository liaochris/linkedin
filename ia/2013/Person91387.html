Mel Schwartz's Summary

Mel Schwartz, LCSW, M.Phil, is a psychotherapist, marriage counselor, executive coach, author and seminar leader.He wrote The Art of Intimacy, The Pleasure of Passion and writes the blog A Shift of Mind. He is completing his forthcoming book, A Shift of Mind.

Mel earned his graduate degree from Columbia University and has been a keynote speaker at Yale University. He has appeared on numerous television and radio shows and been interviewed by many major publications.

Mel is in practice in Westport, CT and NYC.He also works with individuals and organizations throughout the world, either by skype, webconforence or in person.

Specialties: My practice expertise is focused in the following areas:

Mel Schwartz's Experience

Melschwartz.com (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

February 1996 – Present (17 years 11 months)

I am a psychotherapist and marriage counselor working in Westport, CT. My book, The Art of Intimacy, The Pleasure of Passion has assisted many people in overcoming their relationship struggles. My work focuses on clarity and change. I'm not a believer in analysis or diagnosis, but in a co-participatory engagement with my clients to help them reach their goals.

I have developed a unique and transformative approach to therapy, whereby I utilize many of the remarkable discoveries of the emerging sciences and integrate them into a protocol for change and personal evolution. I teach this process--known as Emergent Thinking--locally and via webconferencing.

Visit my website, www.Melschwartz.com, to learn more.
