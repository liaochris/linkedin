Judy Strong's Summary

I am a writer and internet business owner at www.survive-strong.com. My business was created to identify myself to the public, to communicate my expertise in the field of grief and loss and to profile my books and other products. Past work experience: Preschool, Latchkey staff member, and substitute teaching K-12.
Working with children is one of my greatest joys. I recently completed two years of grief facilitating with New Song Center for Grieving Children, a program of Hospice of the Valley in Phoenix. I now lead a group I founded, called Flying Solo. It's purpose is comfort, support, and information for those living alone.
Business experience: Assistant Manager at Naturalizer Shoes, Bloomington MN. and Inventory and Quality Control at Hadley House, Bloomington, MN.
My business experience taught me the value of customer service.

My goals and purpose in life are to educate and inspire others to achieve life balance, to rebuild their lives following loss and adversity, and to add incomparable riches to their lives by going inside and discovering their own spirit.

Specialties: Teaching and facilitating grief and loss recovery, spiritual renewal,identifying personal issues, handling adversity, designing your life for balance and wholeness.
