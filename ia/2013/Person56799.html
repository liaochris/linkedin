Jacqueline Collins' Summary

Coach | Advisor | Speaker | Trainer

Your Business Doctor
Author of "The Communications Tool Set - Rx for Communication"

When was the last time an experienced business doctor gave your business a checkup? Just as regular medical checkups help us maintain good health, regular business checkups help companies sustain and thrive.

PFP provides businesses of all sizes and types with individualized, hands-on business checkups that help them preserve what they currently have, and grow. I work with business owners to adapt to current market conditions and change and improve as needed. Even when a business appears to be operating smoothly, a wellness check is a necessity to reach its full potential. Sole proprietors, businesses with over 100 employees, and those on the cusp of hiring, benefit from working with me.

Improved problem solving, communication, presentation to the marketplace, and collaboration among employees, are just a few of the benefits realized.

I encourage business owners to ask questions about the health of their business and to express their concerns. Together, a plan for sustainability and growth is developed.


Our coaching and consulting helps business owners and individuals assess their strengths so they can develop them to the fullest. These services are used by people who know that by hiring us, they’ll get assistance in turning their business into an even greater success.
