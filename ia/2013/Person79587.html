Leanne Roukema's Summary

As a packaging developer I want to be the link between concept and produceable product. My technical education and experience, combined with a creative background make that I have a good understanding of both sides. I will always work towards a solution that is a perfect mix of both worlds.

I am always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge and develop my skills in packaging technology.

If you have any questions or comments, please do not hesistate to contact me.

Specialties

- A good understanding of the possibilities of different packaging materials and processes
- The translation of design concepts in to produceable products
- The search for a balance between produceability and efficiency vs. design and marketing needs
