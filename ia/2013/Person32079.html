David K. Easlick's Summary

At present, I am spending most of my time putting together a designer dog boutique, Haute Diggity Dawg, in Beaufort and eventually on line. Spending time learning QuickBooks POS software, dealing with wholesalers and manufacturers, learning advertising techniques.

After a professional career starting as a big firm litigator and spending several years in non-profits as an executive and attorney, I have accepted an 18 month marketing and recruiting non-legal assignment with Economic Recovery Group, LLC. ERG is a business and consumer law firm dedicated to helping its clients navigate the BP Claims Process.Almost every type of business on the West coast of Florida was affected.

I have been a Real Estate Broker or sales agent since 1977. A long time REALTOR® I am a member of the Beaufort County Assoiation of Realtors®, and I am pleased to announce that I have joined Caldwell Banker Platinum Plus in Beaufort where I will specialize in Residential Properties.

Spanning a career of over twenty- five years, serving as chief executive officer, board member, and volunteer for international, national and local not-for-profit organizations, I also practice non-profit law in Michigan and consult with fundraising and development for various entities. I'm available to consult for short or long term campaigns or work on member development, communication and annual giving programs.

Specialties: History of developing and optimizing funding sources through exceptional events, public relations, and promotional messages. Seamlessly managing multiple projects, establishing strategic partnerships, improving operations, organizational visibility, and exceeding financial goals. Expert in analyzing existing processes and implementing powerful systems, strategies, and policies that enhance performance. Ethical and poised with contagious enthusiasm and persuasive communication skills.
