Hugo Cuevas-Mohr's Summary

Hugo Cuevas-Mohr has over 20 years experience in the money transfer and remittance business, both in Latin America and in the US.
Since 2001 Hugo has been a consultant for Companies and Banks on Money Transfer issues in Latin America, USA, Spain and the United Kingdom. He routinely gives lectures and seminars on subjects related to money transfer, remittances and financial inclusion.
Since 2010 he is the Director of IMTC Conferences, developing Conferences, Seminars, Trade Fairs on International Money Transfers, such as IMTC Miami, IMTC West, IMTC Mexico & IMTC Brasil.

Specialties: Market Development, Strategic Advice, Mergers & Acquisitions
