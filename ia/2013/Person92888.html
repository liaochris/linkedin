MICHAEL PROVENZA is a professional artist and creator of ThreeTrees Studio, LLC, creating contemporary landscape oil paintings and prints for national galleries, art dealers/buyers, interior designers and private art collectors.

ThreeTrees Studio is a fine art and visual communication company located in Perrysburg, Ohio, created by MICHAEL and SHELLEY PROVENZA in 2009.

Its fine art studio offers fresh, inspiring, surreal landscape oil paintings and prints by MICHAEL PROVENZA for galleries, art dealers and private art collectors.

Its visual communication studio, offers affordable, world-class graphic design services by SHELLEY PROVENZA for brand identity, interactive and collateral print design and whatever creative design project you can imagine.

MICHAEL PROVENZA is a contemporary landscape oil painter who has developed a distinctive pointillistic style and blending technique that exudes a realistic and dimensional quality, yet is surreal and magical. His work reflects many years of experimentation with various approaches to his subject, nature. PROVENZA'S recent original work captures Life’s Journey with the use of paths and stripes surrounded by earth’s ethereal beauty and glory.

Inspired by his artist grandfather, PROVENZA began painting at age nine and continues to diligently produce oil paintings as his life-long passion. This native of the California Bay Area has won several awards in local and regional art competitions and has exhibited in art galleries around the country.
