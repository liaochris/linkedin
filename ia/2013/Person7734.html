Angela Michanitzi's Summary

I am a Designer, Typographer, Visual Communicator and Concept Developer. I am the co-founder of AVMC Group, AVMC Studios, AVMC Training and a University of the Arts London Lecturer. I have experience working with many organisations and industries.

I conceptualise new ideas and bridge the gaps between technology and creativity. My skills and experience cover a wide variety of disciplines.
