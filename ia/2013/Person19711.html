Maurizio Caporali's Summary

Maurizio Caporali is Project Manager at AidiLab s.r.l (www.aidilab.com) and Assistant Professor at the University of Siena IDA Interaction Design Laboratory (www.discom.unisi.it/ida/).

Currently he is mainly involved in the UDOO (www.udoo.org) lifecycle management.

He has been working as a Product Manager and Interaction Designer at Softec spa, he worked as a Interaction Designer at University of Applied Science western Switzerland, he worked at Arsnova, Digital Arts & Sciences, and Infoblu - Autostrade SpA (www.infoblu.it), as technology and product innovation analyst and Web designer.
Maurizio Caporali is information technology, services consultant and service designer for mobile devices business. His research interests include physical computing, Embedded Systems, Internet of things, Web 2.0 services, social computing, interaction design and mobile services.
He holds a Masters Degree in Mathematics at University of Siena, specialization in computer science, and a Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technologies at Electronic Engineering Department of University of Florence, Italy (http://tsi.det.unifi.it) (April 2007), with a focus on computer-supported cooperative work, social computing, collaborative knowledge-based environments.

Specialties: Interaction Design, Digital MockUp, Mobile user experience design, Industrial Design, Embedded Systems, Android developer, interface design, Service Oriented Architecture, Web Service, XHTML, CSS, PHP
