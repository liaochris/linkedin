Karin Zeigler's Summary

Coming from military family, I have experience in relocating, purchase & selling homes or other real estate. I am a member of the RE/MAX Veterans First Program. The RE/MAX Veterans First Program was created by veterans with simple mission of providing both veterans and services members with the best home buyer and seller incentives in the industry combined with world Class Service. Whether you are active duty, a reservist or retired, call me on how I can save you money. Call me for help in buying residential homes, condos, new or resale, land or duplexes. If you are a first time home buyer, senior, investor, or are buying your second or third home I love to help you. I will look to save you money. I have a marketing plan that sells real estate. As a Certified Investor Agent Specialist (CIAS) , and a Distressed Property Expert (CDPE) I am committed to help people that are facing foreclosure by giving them options and helping them make informed decision. I will help investors find unbeatable deals on properties based on non-emotional analysis where you make a decision to acquire real estate that will cash flow and allow the renters to pay for the property. Cash strapped sellers are getting out of mortgages they cannot afford. Short sales are increasingly becoming viable options.

I am committed to delivering first class service throughout the selling, buying and moving process with honesty and integrity. I follow through with commitments and I pledge to be available to you, and responsive to your needs. I am looking out for your best interest. I will look and research to save you money, time and frustration.
