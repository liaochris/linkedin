Craig Stanford's Summary

An accomplished senior executive who has created multi-million dollar business units from standing starts. Driven sales, marketing and product development success for several organizations, including two start-ups, from their inceptions to multi-million dollar top and bottom lines. Recruited, developed and trained sales teams to accelerate revenues. Negotiated complex contracts with key c-suite and senior leadership executives at some of the most prominent F-500 corporations and institutions of higher learning across North America.

22 years of successful experience in: B2B Enterprise Software, Higher Education, Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Software, Technology, Software as a Service (SaaS), Professional Services, Heavy Construction Equipment and Commercial Petroleum Products.

Specialties: Start-ups, General Operations, Go-to-Market Strategy, Enterprise Sales Management, Marketing/Advertising, Product Management, Team Building and Personnel Management, Business Development, Direct Sales, Solution Selling, Channel Creation, Partner Development, M&A, P&L.
