Julia Angelen's Summary

I founded Z Group PR as consultancy after working within PR departments, marketing communications teams, and public relations agencies. I offer my clients many years of experience as a media relations expert and have a proven ability to secure media coverage – from local television to national news outlets. My poise, writing skills, special events planning, and social media training experience make me indispensable to clients and employers, as does my team focus and leadership skills.

My extensive business knowledge and strategic planning experience allows me to offer solutions that positively impact the organizations I work with, as well as the communities they support. Using measurable goals to tie communications programs to desired business results, my clients have available an advisor and a strategic partner in ensuring that communications objectives are surpassed.

Through my employment, contract work and consulting, I have served a variety of clients over the years, helping over 100 organizations expand their communications, define their brand, connect with audiences, and increase their business success. My firm’s tagline – Everything You Say And Everything You Do Is Public Relations – reflects Z Group PR’s comprehensive approach to meeting clients’ communications needs and my personal commitment to PR.

My skill set is broad and areas that I offer expertise include:

:: Establishing effective relationships with customers, government officials, and media representatives and effectively using these professional relationships to develop business opportunities;

:: Writing interesting, effective media releases, articles, blog posts, and social media content for specific audiences, and;

:: Identifying audiences and determining the best means to engage with them, while managing communications plans and budgets.

I would love to connect. Please feel free to contact me at julia@zgrouppr.com.
