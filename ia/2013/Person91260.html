Melissa Burkheimer's Summary

My intention is to help entrepreneurs like you realize that doesn't have to be hard. I believe it can and should be simple, effective and fun. Because I'm part marketing, part graphic design and all human being, I have an accidental tendency to lift the weight off my clients shoulders when it comes to marketing their business. Success takes practice and some dedicated strategy. In my world, consistency always gets the gold.

Welcome to my world.

Working with me means you get actual 1 on 1 time with someone who’s willing to listen to your successes, failures and identify your strengths when it comes to getting the right tools to market your business.

My strategies for upping your marketing game are uncomplicated, doable and more importantly, created custom fit for you. My are services designed specifically (no pun intended) for those want to work towards developing a consistent brand and help their customers find them online using social tools.

Notice I said work – because yes it does take work. Hard work. It takes consistency and effort to achieve success.

My journalism background gives me a secret skill of asking the right questions to help my clients discover the core elements of their story. You have to discover, accept and understand your story before you can start telling it. That is when all the good stuff happens.

Specialties: Graphic Design, Social Media Management, Client Relations, Marketing, Copywriting, Illustration, Social Media Graphic Design, Web Design, Blogging, Interactive Media, Event Promotion, Marketing Plan Development & Execution, Flyer Design, Logo Creation, Business Card Design, Invitations, Twitter Background Design, LinkedIn Photo Editing, Digital Portfolio Creation, E-Newsletter Graphics, Social Media Education, Brochure Design, Integrated Marketing Strategy
