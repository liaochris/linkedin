Fiona Ashe's Experience

Film Director / Screenwriter / Script Editor

FlasheForward Films

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Motion Pictures and Film industry

November 1994 – Present (18 years 11 months) Ireland

I am a multi-award-winning Director/Screenwriter with an extensive track record in the film, television and transmedia industries. I direct films which explore big moral dilemmas: terrorism, corruption, exploitation. I create flawed characters who struggle with the darkness in themselves.

I also provide script editing services to help other screenwriters elevate their screenplays. This makes their screenplays highly marketable and much more likely to secure production finance.

I have directed dramas, documentaries, television programmes and a viral hit video. FlasheForward Films is developing projects that converge cinema and social media, delivering a richer experience for the audience.

Script Reader

Northern Ireland Screen

Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Media Production industry

April 2013 – Present (6 months) Belfast

I read scripts - feature film, television drama, comedy, documentary, short films - and write reports on them for Northern Ireland Screen, the national screen agency for Northern Ireland.

I deliver insight into these screenplays, analysing every element of the scripts: tone, genre, style, premise, structure, plot, pace, action, character and dialogue.

I deliver reports on projects at various stages of development, including: synopses, beat outlines, step-outlines, treatments, and rough drafts. I identify unique screenwriting and new talent and recommend them for financial support.
