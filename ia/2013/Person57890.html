Jim Martin II's Summary

Since Early 2001, Jim has grown his reach from an independent software developer located in Sarasota, FL to the CEO of a globally recognized Internet marketing and advertising agency. His knowledge and expertise has allowed him the opportunity to interact with countless South West Florida Businesses in addition to such Fortune 500 / 1000 brands as General Motors, Wal-Mart, and Sprint, as well as aerospace & defense firms such as: Northrop Grumman, Lockheed Martin, Rolls Royce and NASA. In addition to the numerous for-profit companies listed, Jim is also a huge supporter of non-profits - sitting on the board of Sarasota's Community Youth Development (CYD) organization, as well as being a highly involved committee member of the Boys and Girls Clubs of Sarasota County. Whether your business caters to the client around the corner, or around the world - Jim has the skills and the tools needed to bring your brand to the masses in fun and engaging ways!
