Greg Swan's Summary

I'm passionate about SEO and about helping businesses to reach more clients through optimization and search engine marketing.

I enjoy SEO consulting and the optimization process, keyword research, competitive analysis and the challenge of staying informed and adjusting to the constantly changing SEO landscape.

In a down economy, it is critical for businesses to continue to market themselves. The impact of their marketing efforts during this time is going to be greater. The results of organic optimization do not happen instantly, it is a long term investment that builds equity along the way. The optimization strategy that is started today will pay increased dividends in the future and will help to position the business as an authority in their niche.

I have a successful track record in SEO management of building web equity in businesses through a dedicated optimization campaign and by drilling down to target market to their ideal clients. I enjoy the process and the results achieved by my clients, local and nationwide.

My experience also includes project management, team management, marketing strategy and analysis, and exceptional customer service.

Specialties: SEO Consulting, Organic Search Engine Optimization, Targeted Keyword Research, Competitive Analysis and Link Building Services. Online marketing, email marketing, video marketing, social media optimization, On-Page and External optimization.
