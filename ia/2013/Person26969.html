Nancy Wallace's Summary

Nancy is not a fan of fitness trends and fads. She does Pilates 2-4 times weekly by herself, as well as taking lessons from the instructors at Nancy Wallace Pilates rotating between mat, reformer, chair, and the wall-unit. Recently she has developed a new exercise program using Kangoo Jumps Rebounding IPSystem Boots called “Kangoolates.” The Kangoolates classes are offered exclusively at her studio and she’s invented the “Butt and Thigh Sculptor” to redefine your lines as shown in her Pilates Mat Exercise Video (to be released soon). Nancy is a visionary and has a heart for helping others find happiness through physical fitness!

Specialties: Pilates, Contrology, Core Strength, Pilates Exercise, Health & Wellness, Counseling, Networking

Nancy Wallace's Experience

Owner

Nancy Wallace Pilates

June 2008 – Present (5 years 7 months) 990 102ND NE Bellevue WA 98004

Since opening our doors in June 2008, Nancy Wallace Pilates, formerly known as Core Pilates of Bellevue WA, remains the first fully loaded Authentic Studio teaching the original principles on the Gratz Pilates equipment engineered by Joseph Pilates. We are a team of Pilates professionals showing clients the benefits the Pilates discipline has on life. Our team of highly qualified instructors works extensively with elite athletes, cross training runners, golf and tennis players; we also help re-establish the muscles of the abdominal region post pregnancy and work closely with rehab and injury prevention. Nancy Wallace Pilates takes pride in our strong community of Pilates Enthusiast. We understand that most thoughts about body image and health can result from impulsive “right-now” decisions. Because of this, Nancy Wallace Pilates continually empower clients to make conscious sound decisions about health and fitness. As the Greater Seattle’s premiere Pilates Studio we are dedicated to community and helping people to be “Pilates Strong” for the rest of their lives. Nancy Wallace Pilates of Bellevue is a family environment and every hour spent here should be an exceptional experience with results..
