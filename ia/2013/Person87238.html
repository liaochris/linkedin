Mark Nguyen's Summary

The Short Sale Buyers, also known as Velocity Group Investments (VGI) is a Real Estate Investment firm based out of Teaneck, NJ. At VGI, we work with homeowners to avoid foreclosure and mitigate its associated negative credit impacts. If a homeowner is over leveraged and needs to sell a property, we can still buy the property. For people that are currently behind on their mortgage payments or foresees that they will soon be unable to continue making payments, we offer them option and the best solution for their situation.


Our Mission

We take pride in what we do and the number of people we can help through the process. Through 2010, we will continue to introduce our Property Acquisition Program to homeowners and to professionals in our industry. With our Property Acquisition Program, we can expedite the process, negotiate the fairest outcome for the homeowner, provide full realtor’s commissions, and create a win-win for all the parties involved. As with most successful businesses, ours is built with referrals and we welcome the opportunity to reciprocate. If you would like to catapult your business or feel we can benefit you please reach out to us.

Our Competitive Advantage

Especially in today’s economy, we understand everyone has different circumstances that may lead to a short sale. As short sale situations are rising, homeowners are looking to real estate agents for advice but surprisingly not too many agents understand the full logistics in approaching and successfully completing a short sale. This comes from experience but some may say they have completed one or know how to do them because they have attended a seminar. In realty, the banks regulations changes constantly and what used to work, sometimes will not work the next month. Our outside the box process and paperwork has been approved by multiple attorneys as it provides total transparency for home owners, foreclosing lenders, real estate agents, attorneys, end buyers, and whoever else involved. The traditional way of doing Short Sales will only get you so far, as we have a way to catapult the process.

- We have aligned ourselves with top professionals in the field. Along our journey in helping our close friends, VGI became affiliated with a national mastermind group that specializes and focuses primarily in Short Sales. This group consists of real estate attorneys, brokers, title agents, bank loss mitigators trained by Freddy Mac and Fannie Mae, as well as other investors. Through our collaboration we complete 50+ short sales a month. Because of the volume that we do, we get first updates on regulation changes (which they often do), current trends, and solutions to approach lenders that negotiate Short Sales. This allows us to set the proper expectations for homeowners.

-Short sales can be approved quicker when there is a cash offer involved. The reason for this is because banks prefer not to deal with financing and the closing process is faster. We have direct access to private investors with cash funds in excess of 5 million dollars that allows us to put in an offer and close promptly.
