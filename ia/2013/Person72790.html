Kathie Condon's Summary

The resource to bring focus to your medical-legal case. Legal Nurse Consultant Certified (LNCC), Non Attorney Representative for Social Security Disability Cases. As a legal nurse consultant I can save you time and money by requesting medical records, prescreening your medically related cases for merit, missing notes and finding experts just to name a few of the services we offer.
