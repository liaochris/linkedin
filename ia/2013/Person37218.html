Hero Day Security offers premium information security consultation and standards compliance services to small and medium sized businesses throughout the state of New York. At Hero Day, we seek to make an impact at a time in which cyber crime has never been more prevalent.

The rate at which small and medium sized businesses are being targeted has increased exponentially over the past few years. For instance, 95% of all credit card thefts originate from cyber attacks directed at small businesses. Even more compelling, 50% of *all* targeted cyber attacks are directed at small and medium sized businesses, and this number continues to grow rapidly. So why is it the case that small and medium sized businesses are being targeted so heavily? The answer is simple. Small businesses lack the skill sets and the resources to defend against these cyber attacks, and therefore, cyber criminals view them as easy targets.

In response, Hero Day Security offers premium information security consultation services to small and medium sized businesses at an affordable price.

Hero Day Security was founded by Doug Crescenzi, a Senior Information Security Engineer/Scientist at The MITRE Corporation with years of experience defending the Department of Defense's (DoD) most critical network infrastructure. Doug Crescenzi has won a number of awards from MITRE and the DoD, and is best known for his subject matter expertise supporting the National Security Agency's (NSA) and the Defense Information System Agency's (DISA) classified information sharing programs.
