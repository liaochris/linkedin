Ilona Meagher's Summary

An independent Midwest-based digital journalist, photog, new media designer and author, I'm drawn to science, health and tech communication with positive social aspects and value.

Select highlights from recent years:

- 2011: Voted in as an online fitness community "dailymile Team" ambassador.

- 2010: Accepted into University of Wisconsin-Madison M.A., Communications program with a focus on science, health and technology. (studies deferred presently)

- 2009: Nominated for the Veterans & Military Families for Progress Public Service Award.

- 2007: Wrote Moving a Nation to Care: Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder and America's Returning Troops. Testified before Congress on suicide/combat PTSD in returning veterans.

- 2006: Featured in the book unConventional: Citizens, Focus and Action.

Specialties

Health communication, public speaking, online research, citizen journalism, new media content and web development, seo, graphic design, flash development, marketing collateral design, press release copywriting, military health advocate, combat ptsd expert
