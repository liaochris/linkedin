Bob Lay's Summary

Lay and Williams strives to provide unique service to individuals and groups shopping for medical insurance and Medicare products. In additional to traditional health insurance products, we offer a unique private health insurance exchange with over 20 health plan options for employees. For clients seeking additional benefits such as dental, life, vision and disability insurance, we have a wide array of employer paid and voluntary products. Our primary markets are in Fort Worth, Dallas and Houston, Texas as well as Arkansas.

Consumers confused and frustrated by the health insurance legislation and all the terms such as PPACA, ACA, Obamacare, Affordable Care, Qualified Plan, Subsidies and Healthcare Reform can shop through us for all group and individual products offered by all insurance companies as well as the federal exchange marketplace. If you think you qualify for subsidies, we can help you navigate the process at no additional cost. You do not need to deal with a long distance person on the telephone. Stay local and call us.

Specialties: Designing quality benefit health benefit plans for small groups and individuals in Fort Worth, Dallas and Houston. Our insurance companies include Aetna, Assurant, Blue Cross, Cigna, Humana, Golden Rule, Unicare and United Healthcare.
