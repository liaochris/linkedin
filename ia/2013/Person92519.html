Medical Device Sales Representative

Medtronic Vascular

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; MDT; Medical Devices industry

November 2003 – January 2005 (1 year 3 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

I sold both Coronary & Peripheral angioplasty products including stents, balloons, guide catheters, etc. in the Los Angeles market. I was fortunate to get to work with some amazing Interventional Cardiologists. I successfully turned around a grossly underperforming territory, which had done $67,000 in total revenue the prior year. This job was especially difficult because we had neither a Drug Eluting Stent or the Rapid Exchange platform. At that time, the DES penetration was 95% and my territory was roughly 85% Rapid Exchange/RX. Some how I still found a way to garnish some nice increases in market share! This position exposed me to the field of interventional medicine, and provided me a network of contacts that were instrumental in allowing me to transition into the field of medical device recruiting.
