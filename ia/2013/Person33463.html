Debbie Elmer's Summary

DSR Insurance Services is an independent insurance brokerage firm. We are not limited to offering you just one carrier. We shop all of the major carriers in California and provide you with the most competitive choices available.

Deborah L. Elmer, the firm's Founder, entered the life and health insurance business in 1982. She built DSR Insurance Services with one deep seeded motivation...to help individuals, families and small businesses protect themselves, their assets and their dignity through sound, conservative guidance, while focusing on the needs of each individual and small business.

Deciding on an insurance plan can be complicated and somewhat confusing. You want someone who will provide insight, service, and respect. You want a company that will always be there when you need them.

At DSR Insurance Services we will sit down with you, either in person, on the phone or via email, and help you determine the type of coverage that best fits your needs.

You can feel comfortable and confident that the people at DSR Insurance Services are here to help make your insurance experience as painless as possible. It's just plain easy!

Contact us today at (562) 420-7104.
