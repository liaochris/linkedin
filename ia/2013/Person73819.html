We recruit, train and educate new agents, "white-collar refuges," as well as professionals from affiliated industries.

The financial market is changing; technology and innovation are causing Creative Destruction - the transition of a traditional industry into something entirely new. Consider the impact of The railway on the stagecoach industry. One creatively destroyed the former.

What was previously reserved for the wealthy elite is now becoming available to middle class America.

Regardless of your financial situation, every person needs a strategic financial plan. Unless all of your financial accounts and insurance products have been updated in the past 18 months, it is likely you are not maximizing on the changes in the industry - either giving up potential gains or carrying an increased tax liability. Contact the Skyline Group to find out if we can offer meaningful assistance for your finances.
