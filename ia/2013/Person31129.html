Dave Ingland's Summary

Entrepreneurial drive, always focused on the big picture. Develop and implement processes to meet and/or exceed goals. Enjoy working in a team environment and look for opportunities to make those around me better. Appreciate a fast-paced environment that is process-driven and goal-oriented.

As a sales manager for both DCH Honda of Oxnard and Honda of Santa Monica, I strive to help people be the best that they can be. Always training and motivating sales teams to learn new things daily. Able to meet customers and close deals as well as structure deals to maximize gross profit and capture finance opportunities.

Developed and implemented strategies and processes at DCH Honda of Oxnard to rebuild the internet sales department. Increased sales from 30% to 53% of the overall total sales for the dealership within nine months. Closing ratios were improved from 9% to 13% within the same nine month period. Initiated a social media action plan and effectively engaged customers--and the community around us--through social media campaigns. Reduced monthly spend on internet lead providers while increasing sales volume and closing ratios.

Published contributing author to The Big Book of Social Media: Case Studies, Stories, Perspectives.

Served as an assistant pastor at Living Stone Community Church for three years prior to becoming a founding pastor and lead visionary for Revolution Church Sacramento.

Started USAsia Press from scratch to become a multi-million dollar corporation within its first three years. Focused on customer service and satisfaction with a sales approach based on building relationships rather than trying to compete solely on price. All clientele was derived from telephone or email cold calls and serviced from the home office.
