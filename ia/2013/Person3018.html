Alain van der Linden's Summary

Profile
Alain van der Linden is an experienced hands-on Business Process, AO/IC & SOx Consultant/Specialist. As a pro-active professional he's pragmatic, analytical, critical, customer-oriented and quality-focussed. He's also organization/culture sensitive, enthusiastic, honest and has strong communication skills (spoken/written in Dutch & English) at all organizational levels.

His Process & AO/IC (Re)design/Modelling, BPM and SOx/Audit/Test jobs alternate. This allows him to fathom various matters, interests, arguments and viewpoints of parties involved. He quickly manages to collect, analyze, understand and clarify lots of information/data about new/linking branches, working areas & processes. In a pleasant, tactical and effective way he easily penetrates to the heart of the matter.

Background
Over 10 years he worked at (Big 4) accounting firms (i.e. Deloitte). He gained lots of knowledge & experience regarding AO/IC, Administrative Information, finance, accounting, (set-up, perform, manage & report) financial/operational audits in various branches. Now he performs, as an independent Interim Manager & Business Consultant, interim jobs for 13 years. Each time with Dash & Brainpower and full customer satisfaction.

Specialties
Process & AO/IC (Re)design/Modelling, BPM, SOx, Audits, User Access, IT Security and Project Mgt..

With mgt. & employees/users he develops/implements (process/org.) changes/improvements and let them work. He's able to optimally involve people, (let) make decisions and overcome resistances.

With thorough, solid and smart descriptions, flowcharts & workinstructions he provides clear and/or simplified insight in new/changed/improved and complex ways of working, (organizational) structures, AO/IC & processes for various objectives/(user)groups.

He also advises management & board, tests new/changed ways of working, controls etc.. Organization/employees gets familiar with it through his coaching, workshops, presentations etc..
