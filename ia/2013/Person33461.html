Debe Maxwell, CRS's Summary

Debe Maxwell, CRS is a Charlotte Residential Real Estate Agent / Broker with Savvy + Company Real Estate. As Certified Residential, Neighborhood and Relocation Specialists, we can assist you with the purchase and/or sale of real estate in Charlotte NC. Savvy + Company's team of agents can also assist you with a move any place in the country by connecting you with a relocation professional in your destination of choice. We have created a team of professionals throughout the country to ensure that you enjoy a smooth transition to your new area. These professionals are experts in the field of relocation and can serve many purposes beyond a simple home search. The destination service is of no cost to you! Please visit www.iCharlotteRealEstate.com for your relocation and/or your local needs.

Specialties

Specializing in relocation to the Charlotte Metro area, Debe has years of experience in the area and is a Certified Neighborhood Specialist and able to find the neighborhood that fits your needs, then the home of your dreams! If you're looking for a Charlotte real estate agent, give Debe a call today.
