Andrew Schwint's Summary

I am a hands-on creative professional with over 10 years of branding, interactive, and advertising art direction and design experience. My work is strategy-driven, highly conceptual, and thoughtfully customer-focused.

Compelling experiences are what I'm passionate about.

My goal is to create, revitalize and grow brands that are meaningful parts of our everyday lives.

Specialties

Creative Direction, Conceptual Development, Brand Positioning & Strategy, Interface Design, Photoshoot Art Direction, User Experience Design, Logo & Identity Design, New Business Development, Client Management, Presentations & Consensus Building, Business & Interactive Strategy, Leadership & Mentoring
