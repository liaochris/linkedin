The purpose of these competitions were to provide gamers within the institutes of Pakistan a fun and healthy platform wherein they could compete in their respective games, and tried to earn a cash prize while they were at it. The event attracted gamers from all over Pakistan, all competed neck to neck for the title of top gamers. It was also being hoped that these events would further the name of gaming as a whole; since gaming had already become a valid profession in many parts of the world by 2008/2009, and we were hoping that it would evolve to that; both from gamers perspective, as well as Pakistani society as a whole.
