Janice Paulsen-Guazzo's Summary

I have over 10 years of marketing both large and small business. My goals are to help all small business act like big business and provide affordable solutions to make that happen! I've been in marketing and website development since 1998.

Growing up I worked for small businesses and always found myself working on and managing the creative projects. When I found myself in corporate America I landed in marketing services and web project management. During my time at those big insurance/banking companies I was working with Content Management in its infancy I am now proficient in Joomla, WordPress and can pick up most any other CMS in a quick minute.

I started working in social media in 2008 helping all my current clients use this free marketing to its fullest potential. Today, I use my marketing, web design and social media experience to help small businesses integrate new media strategies (blogs, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn) with traditional marketing and business development plans. My strength is to find the right new media match for business development goals. I strive to help small businesses start or improve their online businesses and grow their traditional ones with the newest technologies at a reasonable cost.

Specialties

web design, marketing, social networks, online marketing, social media, online communities, new media marketing, content management, CMS, logos, brand identity, Online Marketing, Search Engine Optimization
