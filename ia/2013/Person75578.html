National Gluten-Free Business Forum

November 2011 – Present (1 year 11 months) Portland, Oregon

The National Gluten-Free Business Forum has been established to serve as a support and information exchange institution for regional Gluten-Free businesses and its supply chain. The NGFBF is comprised mainly of Gluten-Free manufacturer’s, ingredient suppliers, restaurants, brokerages, distributors, foodservice, retailers, clinicians, nutritional specialists and small business start up’s. The goal is to provide effective, timely and business critical information so that each member is able to achieve their individual business goals while providing safe and innovate products to consumers. We welcome new members! You will find that being a member provides you with a great networking community as well as monthly informational meetings designed to actively engage and improve your business operations.

Kimberley Wilson, M.Ed.'s Publications

  • "Eat Better, Live Richer"

    • 2010
    Authors: Kimberley Wilson, M.Ed.

    This amazing cookbook teaches everything you need to know about cooking towards a healthier lifestyle using the CORE System. What is the CORE System? It is an easy way to remember the keys to making the changes needed for a healthier lifestyle –
    C- Create a health inventory
    O- Organize your food lists
    R- Read all food labels
    Not only does the Eat Better, Live Richer Cookbook have tons of information, it also has many easy-to-follow recipes, with pretty simple ingredients. Plus it includes suggestions on where to purchase the ingredients and which name brands to use.
