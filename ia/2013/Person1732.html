John Bushe', The White Wizard of Reuse's Summary

Hello, my name is John Bush. I am a catalyst for change, and most recently helped bring Zero Waste to the City of Dallas. I am a social entrepreneur who loves tinkering with complex systems and bending them in new ways that create momentum for others. I specialize in reuse market development, and design businesses that can decompose the waste stream according to the triple bottom line: people, planet, and profit. I do this by imitating nature.

I love to communicate through art and am proficient in multiple creative platforms such as Adobe Creative Suite and Final Cut Pro. After my experience in the Marines, I went to Southern Methodist University (SMU) and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Art Direction and Copy Writing from the Temerlin School of Advertising. I am proudly wrapping the latest incarnation of my innovation platform: Adbongo Inc, the first for-benefit business accelerator in the world. This is our 5th time at the drawing board, as the new economy is only discovered through trial and error.

I have experience in multiple industries but have most enjoyed my last six years as an entrepreneur. I started my company, Adbongo, Inc., in 2006. I encourage you to read the testimonials from my clients here on linked in to learn more . Over the years I have taken some beatings, as all entrepreneurs must, but I believe those tough times have only served as lessons that have helped evolve my approach to business. Currently I am swimming in multiple blue oceans of uncontested market space, and I work diligently to help other see these possibilities.

Contact me for a FREE resource efficiency audit, if you want to learn how I can accelerate your business in black and white.
