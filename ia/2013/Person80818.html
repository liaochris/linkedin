Mike Close's Summary

I studied film and digital media in school and, with the help of some patient mentors, taught myself how to write software in the years that followed. I'm currently the lead developer on the Chase Mobile iPhone application, providing millions of users with access to their bank account information and a suite of innovative consumer banking features, including Chase Person-to-Person QuickPay and Chase QuickDeposit.

I'm always looking for new opportunities to help small and medium-sized businesses advance their software development efforts, and help non-technical folks gain a greater understanding of what it takes to maintain a successful relationship with their engineers. I can augment an existing team, or lead you through the process of building a new one. I can provide architectural oversight, and I can write code. I can help you offshore your new project, and I can help you get out of your current offshore nightmare, keeping as much skin on your back as possible. Just message me to get the conversation started!
