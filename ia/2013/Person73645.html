Keith Holdt's Summary

Passionate about transforming business for the better since first entering into the commercial world, am motivated not just by the smart application of the latest thinking and in the creative use of information but in seeing it realised into working solutions, enabling businesses to see further, respond faster and achieve their goals more effectively.

Have provided innovative consultancy, strategies and practical solutions that have resulted in commercial success for over 20 years to SMEs, corporate and multi-national businesses. These range from making strategic alliances, establishing profitable and efficient operations, opening new pipelines and markets, to setting up and leading global teams across national boundaries.

Created and implemented strategies and models for business growth and increased company value, bringing added value, buy-in and excellent communication skills into the board room, client-facing relationships and internally across businesses.
