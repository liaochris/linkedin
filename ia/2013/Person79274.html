Lavinia Osbourne's Summary

I am the business owner of Butterfly Wealth Creation as well as an Internet Network Marketing Professional and Coach,specialising in Financial Education and Wealth Creation,

I have 9 years’ plus commercial and international experience as a PA, Client Services Executive, and Office Manager, in a wide variety of business areas, including Publishing, Banking, Recruitment, Finance and Business Advisory. I possess a flexible and adaptable attitude and can provide confidential and business-critical support to Partners, Directors, and their teams. I am IT-literate, with excellent project management skills; a first-class researcher; and a confident communicator.

By using my extensive experience as a PA, as well as my first-class organisational and research skills, I have propelled my learning of Internet and Network Marketing, and have so far acquired a new understanding and ability on how to use 21st Century marketing tools and strategies, such as SEO (Search Engine Optimisation), and Web 2:0 sites to market a business/organization and grow a client base.

My goal is to inspire and teach others on how to fulfill their potential, so that they may, too, develop their skills and broaden their opportunity to become financially free and independent.

Specialties

As an Internet & Network Marketing Professional: MLM Lead Generation, Financial Education, Wealth Creation, Networking, Marketing, SEO, Web 2:0, Internet Marketing, Article Writing and Copy Writing.

As an Executive Assistant: Implementation of office processes and procedures; administration, planning and supervising; problem solving; multitasking; relationship building; client relationship management; personality assessment and training (Thomas International Personal Profile Assessment).
