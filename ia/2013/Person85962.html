Bizzo

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

January 2011 – April 2012 (1 year 4 months) Rotterdam, Netherlands

With their large presence, Small and Medium sized Enterprises (SMEs) are the cornerstone of every economy. The average SME has had a hard time during the recession, but its day-to-day business is gradually picking up again. These SMEs could use some support.

Running a SME requires a manager to be a Jack-of-all-trades. It’s owner is spending too much time on secondary subjects and is often lacking knowledge in specific fields of expertise.
These enterprises need better options for marketing and leadgeneration in the digital world and want to participate in group-buying. They need to create their own effective digital footprints in stead of adding another website or web shop

Bizzo helps smaller businesses doing business!

Bizzo is changing this, by offering a support platform that makes a difference. Instead of just connecting people, Bizzo actually helps the SME do business, by connecting the things that matter: products and companies. Bizzo enables groups of businesses to reap the advantages of the bigger company without giving up any of their individual strengths.

The members present a digital company profile and connect it to numerous other companies. They generate leads by selling support and special offerings directly to B2B customers and opposite find special offers and reduce costs.
Bizzo creates a professional network of knowledge that helps the SME owner do business easier at lower cost. It allows them to create groups of entrepreneurs and collaborate and share knowledge.

In the same way Bizzo will also support the millions of Self-Employed that grease the labor market and finally it offers a great toolset for Businesses clubs.

Interaction Designer

Master of Arts in Interaction Design Graduation Project

January 2008 – September 2008 (9 months)

Individual Master of Arts in Interaction Design graduation project. In this period, I've developed two versions of mobile (iPhone) applications with location based services and user generated content; iTraveller and iTraveller 2.0.

Applications that provides you specific information on a specific location (or current position). Several web2.0 applications are embedded in iTraveller to merge it to one mobile web2.0 user generated content application. My Master thesis was about the impact on privacy with upcoming (always connected to the internet) mobile techniques and user generated content.
