James Tibbles' Summary

I've always had a keen interest in programming, gadgets and technology. I spent far too many of my teenage years messing around on my computer, developing games and learning new techniques.

After completing a BSC Hons degree in Multimedia Design I went on to develop websites back in early 2002. Shortly after I started working with Adobe Flash to create my first online web games.

By 2009 I was lead developer for websites and online games, advanced knowledge of many industry standard tools, including Actionscript 3 (Flash, Flex and Air), PHP (with Mysql), jQuery/javascript and many more. I also started developing mobile apps.

In 2010 I became project manager, leading a team of programmers and creatives in developing projects for large worldwide brands.

I learned techniques in Agile/Prince2 management and can confidently manage projects on a large scale.

I am currently for hire as a freelance web, mobile and/or games developer. Feel free to contact me if you would like to know more.
