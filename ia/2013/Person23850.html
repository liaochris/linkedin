Christiaan Stoudt's Summary

Christiaan Stoudt has spent his career breaking down the status quo and helping individuals and businesses look inward to evolve, rethink, and transform their existing paradigms into new personal growth opportunities, strategic agility potential, business service oriented models, and revenue/cost saving potential.

Experience includes -- empowering people to flourish by developing a strong personal growth mindset, leading large scale customer oriented business culture impacting projects, strategic transformations, new business model innovation, workflow and process revitalization, security and compliance integrity, and helping people and businesses embrace change as well as see the gift that is found within adversity.

Working with companies such as NBCUniversal, GE, Comcast, Cloudify, VMWare, BMC Software, EMC, HP, Microsoft, and Sophos, Christiaan's approach uses unique inward reflecting techniques and foundational methodologies to focus on the individual’s needs and uncover the root cause that must transform to cultivate a new expression of oneself. The new shift in mindset that is achieved results in increased potential, efficiency, and growth that can be modeled by individuals and businesses. He believes in overcoming complex demands by leveraging intuition and imagination while using a broad span of technical and business acumen to develop simple clear thought out strategic solutions and action plans.
