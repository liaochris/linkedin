Webmaster

Kristal University

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Primary/Secondary Education industry

February 2009 – January 2010 (1 year) Tirana

The University had an urgent need for a new website, as the existing one was getting obsolete very fast. So I hopped on board...

We sketched a few concepts to better improve the information architecture and make space for some new, cool ideas. From then, I started the design and developing with the target to get the website up and running in less than two months. The feedback was really positive, so we started improving it and in less than one month launched the international (English) version.

The routine tasks after the website completion was improving usability and accessibility, updating the content, working on SEO and improving SERP, promoting on social media, etc.

Web Developer

Eleico Albania

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

June 2008 – January 2009 (8 months) Tirana

Eleico was a subsidiary of Hale Communications, a Belgian company specialized in web applications and Content Management Systems.

The experience there was quite nice and informative. I had the chance to work with some very talented guys and learn a few tricks in programming, project and time management.

Officially I was a developer building custom Content Management Systems. I had to sharpen my design skills too in a few projects, clients' and internal ones.

Web Designer and Developer

Albania Online SP

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer & Network Security industry

June 2007 – February 2008 (9 months)

I was part of the web team, in charge of building customers' websites, as well as internal projects. Albania Online SP was merged with several other companies into Primo Communication.

The biggest challenge was creating a full-featured custom Content Management System in PHP and MySQL in just twelve days by myself. The system turned out to be quite usable and a very good choice for customers, but unfortunately it never had the chance to see the light.

Another interesting project was one consisting of a PHP application that interfaced with a telephony database. It authenticated the customer, parsed the required information from the database and returned formatted data (credits left, call history, etc) in a user-friendly layout.

Helped also in print design; mainly leaflets, business cards, logotypes and such.

Web Designer
