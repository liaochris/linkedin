Peter Beacom's Summary

I build software products. I also perform technical due diligence related to mergers & acquisitions as well as intellectual property analysis.

My passions are technology and business. My current technology stack of choice for building software products is SQLServer and/or RavenDB for data persistence, C# with .NET MVC or Web API for business logic, and HTML/CSS, JavaScript, KnockoutJS, & KendoUI for front end UX. Some of my current interests are using social media to streamline science & engineering recruiting and improving investment transparency for outbound investments from China to the United States.

I strive to maintain a well rounded background in technology, finance, and public policy. In addition to software expertise, I have experience lobbying at the state & federal levels, negotiating project work, administering employee benefits, and serving as a trade mission delegate.
