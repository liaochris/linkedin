Owner profikoucink.eu
Executive & Life Balance Coaching, Profi Sports Mental Coaching & Mental Training

I believe that every person is unique and deserves to be satisfied and happy. Each of us can be a winner in one's own life, only sometimes for a victory we need a "coach" who will lend us a helping hand and will help us to achieve our dream goals.

"We want to with clients not only achieve our goals, but also experience them in health and good shape."

In my team I cooperate with my husband the Olympic Games winner Robert Změlík and with mitochondrial and bioregulation medicine specialist, an expert in the field of food supplements MUDr. Michael Kučera and other professionals. Currently, I am cooperating with the company Principal Coaching s.r.o. and Bright Principal s.r.o.

Specialties: I specialize in maintaining life balance of individuals using a unique methodology Results Coaching Systems - NeuroLeadership Group, which is based on the findings of modern neuroscience. This is the approach by the brain "brain-based approach that coaching", which focuses on embedding the new behavior.

"As the first coach in the Czech Republic I bring a unique combination of professional coaching, sports and health."
