Joris Ketelaar's Summary

I'm a communicative sr. Interaction Designer (5+ years experience), who's convinced of the strong relationship between visual- and interaction design. Of course user-centered, but also with commercial targets in mind.

By using programs like Axure, Visio and Photoshop, I'm able to quickly turn concepts into prototypes, which are easier to judge and understand by clients.
