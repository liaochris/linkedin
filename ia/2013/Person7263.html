Andrew Urban's Summary

An experienced relationship-oriented technology professional with a focus on working with the nonprofit, NGO, educational institutions, and association markets. Over 20 years of sales, management and small business ownership experience with over 14 years within the nonprofit sector.

I seek to help companies and nonprofits have a balanced working relationship that focuses on a positive return on mission. My skills lie in helping companies with early stage go-to market strategies in this sector, building and training sales team to understand this market, and engagement of early adopters and key partnerships. I support nonprofit organizations with a methodology and consulting/training to help them in the often painful procurement processes in order that they are able to solve critical business issues in the manner that best supports their unique mission.
