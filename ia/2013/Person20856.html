Anthony Castelli | Attorney's Summary

I have been a practicing attorney for over 30 years in the fields of personal injury, workers compensation and social security disability.

Who I Help:

My clients include individuals or families of people killed or injured in car accidents, personal injury victims, motorcycle and truck accident victims. I also help workers compensation claimants.

How I Help Them:

I use my experience to help my clients get the money they deserve to make up for their injury and losses. I also help people trying to get social security disability because of injury or illness. My goal is to get my clients every penny they deserve.

How You Can Reach Me:

I've dedicated my life giving personal attention to my clients needs and getting results. My practice extends throughout Greater Cincinnati and the state of Ohio. Call 1-800-447-6549 or e-mail me at tony@castellilaw.com to schedule a free consultation.

Feel free to check out my website with great educational materials and free reports to educate injury victims about their rights: www.CastelliLaw.com

The initial consult he provides is always without charge, and he works exclusively on a contingency fee basis with all of his cases. This means if he accepts and works on an injury case, he only charges a fee when he successfully resolves the case; otherwise there is no charge for his legal services. Thus, there is no financial risk to the client.
