Adam Dustagheer's Projects

  • Labour on Youtube

    • January 2010 to October 2011
    Team Members: Adam Dustagheer, Tom Christian

    Helping establish the Labour Party brand on Youtube, with professional in house video production. Leading to over 2 million channel views

  • Digital manual for Pre-registration pharmacist manual

    • January 2012 to June 2012
    Team Members: Adam Dustagheer, David Taylor

    Replacing previous hard copy of the manual with a mobile optimized website.

  • People's Question Time with Gordon Brown

    • March 2010 to April 2010
    Team Members: Adam Dustagheer, Sue Macmillan

    Prime Minister Gordon Brown took questions from the public on MSN UK in a UK election first of launching an internet campaign to engage with voters.

  • Labour Leadership Election

    • May 2010 to September 2010
    Team Members: Adam Dustagheer, John Miles, Claire Hazelgrove, Tom Christian

    Creating and maintaining a 'one stop shop' for all candidates, supporters and Labour Party members for the 2010 Leadership elections.

    From initial nominations to delivery of the complete breakdown of results at the end of the process this site formed the basis to communicate everything about the contest.

    As part of this there was a major recruitment drive, which resulted in tens of thousands of new members. Thanks to an innovative social media strategy over 10,000 new members were recruited via Twitter and Facebook.

    Labour.org.uk was also adapted to incorporate live on-line broadcast of all the hustings, a major technical and logistical challenge delivered on a tight budget.

  • Labour Party Conference App

    • April 2010 to September 2010
    Team Members: Adam Dustagheer, Stuart Radforth

    Created the first Labour Party Conference app, which was a comprehensive guide to the country's largest political conference.

    It contains the full fringe and main talk listings included allowing users to plan their personal Conference agenda. Each event came with a useful reminder alert and a map, including bespoke floorplans for the exhibition area to help navigate to specific locations internally. It also provided all the latest news including a collection of tweeting MPs and recommendations on who to follow during conference.

  • Campaign Engine Room

    • January 2010 to September 2010
    Team Members: Adam Dustagheer

    A campaigns platform integrated with organisations single sign in, created in order to allow users to create, and share campaigns.
