Matthew Jeffreys' Summary

MJc overview:

Business/Product development consultant assisting companies and individuals turn ideas and designs into reality by combining innovation, practicality and efficiency.

A wide network of diverse industry contacts allows me to facilitate important connections, manage projects and enable clients to find solutions to their problems - either through engineering design or commercial development.
