Gary Fielder's Summary

Working for BT at senior management level leading large dispersed multi-disciplinary teams across the UK, shaped high end leadership and management capabilities alongside knowledge of the Telecoms security and Information Assurance specialisms.

But after 34 years with BT I decided to pursue my passion for photography into a business venture and established Fielder Photography in 2007. Up until then I had been shooting weddings, parties, christenings, etc as gifts to friends and family. Formal training in wedding, studio and commercial photography helped but the biggest challenge was probably setting up a small business!

Alongside this, my leaving BT enabled my return to academia to complete my counselling qualifications at Post Graduate / Masters level to become a Certified Practioner and also establish GF Counselling Services Ltd as a vehicle for my private practice, alongside my work as a volunteer counsellor.

My goals are to excel in all that I do whilst achieving a meaningful work/life balance, to be surrounded by satisfied customers and clients, to listen, understand and align my services to customer needs. To provide high quality services to professional standards and ethical codes. To be fair minded, accepting and genuine, working with integrity and honesty.

Specialties: Photography: Modern informal wedding photography, contemporary portraiture, party/event photography, commercial services.

Counselling: Person-Centred Existential Integrative approach to emotional and other difficulties in life, solution focused life coaching.
