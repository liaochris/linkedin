Mel Reyes' Summary

With over 20 years of technology experience, Mel Reyes operates across diverse business sectors that include: e-Commerce, fashion, travel, consumer goods, publishing, music, video, advertising, media and digital services.

Using a distinctive combination of technology arsenal, strategy, operations, and creative flair; he has revitalized the technology offering for a creative and events services agency, managed product development, deployed mobile and web applications, overseen technical and creative execution and developed products for a number of Fortune 500 companies.

Mel has extensive experience with mobile and cross platform desktop development, Open Source initiatives, authored Hacking Firefox, managed or developed database, e-Commerce, mobile, web, desktop software and IT solutions. He has consulted with multi-functional teams in the implementation, maintenance, and upgrade of business processes and operational efficiencies for diverse Fortune 50/500 clientele such as Pepsi, Bank of America, T-Mobile, Mercedes-Benz, Lowe’s, Sharp, Thomson Reuters and others.

A practical businessman, mentor, and product developer; Mel is internationally recognized by leading publications and media for his innovative software designs and utilities, including TechTV, Lifehacker.com, InformationWeek.com, PC World, PC Magazine, C*Net, Downloads.com, and others. As an independent developer, Mel has created eleven world-class Mozilla extensions with a total reach of over 875,000 active daily users. As well as desktop applications; creating and supporting 500k active members, 50M total members and 800k daily MP3 downloads.

Specialties: Content Management, Operations, Advertising, Marketing, UI|UX, Mobile Applications, Responsive/Adaptive Web, Change Management, Product Development, Technical lead, Executive Producer, Digital Engagement, Interactive Exhibits, Operational Metrics, Database Design, Business Development, Sales, Customer Service, Mentoring, Agile and Scrum.
