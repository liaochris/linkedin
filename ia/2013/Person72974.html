Kathleen Howard, M.B.A., M.Ed., MBTI Practitioner's Summary

Human Development Associates provides management development, staff training and one-to-one coaching solutions customized to clients' needs. Kathleen Howard, M.B.A., M.Ed., HDA's President and Adjunct Professor in Management for Keuka College, specializes in Communication and Interpersonal Skills, Customer Service and Conflict Resolution, Leadership, Coaching and Team Building, Myers Briggs Type Indicator® classes, one-to-one and on-line. With over twenty years in human resource work including training director, curriculum developer and instructor, affirmative action officer, personnel officer and placement counselor, Ms. Howard, a MBTI® Master Practitioner, is certified also in both FIRO-B and FIRO-Element B as well as other reliable self assessment instruments. She has administered the MBTI® to over 3,000 people and also offers executive coaching and lively interactive training in areas such as problem solving, critical thinking, preventing sexual harassment, motivation, delegation, interviewing, hiring, performance appraisal and discipline.

Specialties

MBTI (R) Master Practitioner and training consultant offering lively interactive training in areas outlined above as well as problem solving, critical thinking, preventing sexual harassment, motivation, delegation, interviewing, hiring, performance appraisal and discipline.
