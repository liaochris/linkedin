Andrew Raimist's Summary

I'm a professional architect licensed to practice in Missouri and Illinois with a special expertise in mid-century modern architecture, historic preservation, adaptive reuse and building rehabilitation.

As a teacher I've worked with students at the graduate and undergraduate levels down to primary school focused on architectural history, design, photography, and digital imaging.
