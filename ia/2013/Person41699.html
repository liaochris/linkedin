Harriett Flowers' Summary

Over a 30 year career as a software and SaaS vendor, I have sold, architected, managed, (and near the dawn of computing, wrote code for!) successful systems that have generated well over $1 Billion in revenue:
• Healthcare Contractual Allowance Modeling and Accounting
• Healthcare Point of Service Patient Cost Estimation
• Healthcare PPO Claims Administration
• Normative Physician Activity Reporting
• Data Center Scheduling, Tracking and Reports Distribution
• Master Limited Partnership Transaction Tax Reporting
• Business Bankruptcy Restructure Creditor Payment & Debtor Mgmt
• Propane Gas Market Deal Analysis and Transaction Accounting
• Retail Bank Sales Tools
• Academic Registration and Transcript Reporting

In 1990, I founded a Software-as-a-Service Healthcare Revenue Cycle Solutions company, IMaCS. Years of classic entrepreneurial struggles resulted in an older, wiser Harriett and a successful business exit. I am currently actively involved in the Healthcare and broader Software industries as a Consultant, advising entrepreneurs and investors in those sectors.

My alter-ego, the Enumerist’s motto, “Finite and Unbounded” speaks to my interest in the not-yet-practical possibilities before us. I’m working on a Grand Unified Being Theory. (When it’s done, I’m happy to share it.) Until then, I continue to do my research by reading subjects like natural language processing, brain infrastructure, foundational mathematics, subjective probability, and history.

Specialties: Process change: technology architecture in context of industry dynamics and workflow redesign
