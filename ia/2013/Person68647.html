Yosef (Joseph) Daskal, CSCP's Summary

With over 10 years of successful Procurement & Supply-Chain experience at large corporations, I have created fantastic value across diverse disciplines and driven the effective strategic vision which has ensured great financial returns.
My responsibilities as Director of Procurement for National HealthCare are to ensure the value-driven execution of all contracts and the effective agility of National’s demanding supply chain. Having negotiated advantageous contracts with service providers and suppliers, I successfully support the seamless operation of 39 Skilled Nursing and Rehabilitation centers located across 7 states. National HealthCare’s footprint extends across NY, NJ, CT, MA, ME, VT & NH. Along with my entire team, I have the privilege of extending exceptional customer service to all residents and employees of National HealthCare.

My passion to succeed and my ability to connect with people in creating lucrative business relationships have resulted in great accomplishments for National HealthCare. Some of my specialties include;
