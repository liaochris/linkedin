Gabriela Gruianu-Gambra's Summary

Passionate life re-design catalyst working with successful executive and entrepreneur women in transition times, to achieve balance and fulfillment, to live purposeful lives.

Specialties: balanced life, purposeful life, life mission & vision, self alignment.

Through life coaching and business coaching, I am a catalyst of change for successful women. I truly believe that each person can live the life he (she) wants.

My mission is to follow, to inspire and to coach people through their journey to live a purposeful life, to reach work-life balance, to build their own life-map, to find their personal compass and to point their own North.

In my life coaching programs I use NLP coaching, life-maps and a holistic and spiritual approach in order to help my clients to find their new life territories.

I organize personal coaching retreats, urban retreats and small group coaching.

Life coaching with me is a journey. So take your ticket now and visit the life you ever wanted!
