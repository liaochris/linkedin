Mark Ursino's Summary

Solution Architect, project technical lead, and Sitecore MVP with extensive experience in both Sitecore CEP and ASP.NET. My depth and breadth of Sitecore knowledge enables me to architect sophisticated CMS solutions for clients in many different industries and verticals.

I lead a team of developers as a mentor and also contribute to the development team as a whole as a strong advocate of development principles and best practices. I've also developed various coding practices and standards for efficient, defensive, and re-usable development.

As a client-facing technical liaison, I train clients and content editors for custom solutions, and I often train client developers in learning how to think and develop as Sitecore developers.

Specialties: Sitecore CEP, ASP.NET, C#, LINQ, SQL Server, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Web Services, SEO, CMS architecture, web architecture, technical documentation
