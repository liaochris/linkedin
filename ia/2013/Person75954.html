Kinshuk Sunil's Summary

I am an independent game developer. I have been making games since 2001, while I was still in school. Worked on many open source, community game projects, before taking the plunge and started my own game development company. Right now, struggling to keep things green, but having fun all along the way.

For the last 5 years, I have been actively involved with open source communities around the country, helping build and nurture them along with community initiatives to bring the best of them forward.

I have been actively organizing community engagement campaigns and events all across India with student communities and technology groups.

I have also been active on the Social domains; having blogged for a number of popular media, including What I Say, Make Use Of, and Hindustan Times. Interests in the domain include Reputation Management, Brand Creation, Social Graph and Metrics.

I am an amateur musician, actively working on publishing my first album as "Ctrl Alt Delhi", while work on the next one has already begun.

A strong people's person, I look forward to engaging work that requires me to work with people towards defining new perspectives and new technologies.
