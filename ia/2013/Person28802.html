Damien Leroy's Summary

Graduated from UCLouvain with a Master in Computer Science Engineering in 2006, I obtained a PhD in the same field in 2011. My research was about Computer Networks and Security.

In 2011, I joined the Keemotion project, which is now a spin-off from UCLouvain that aims at delivering automated production for the sport industry.

Since 2010, I am also highly involved in the organization of the Belgian Olympiad in Informatics and in the training of the Belgian delegation for the International competition.
