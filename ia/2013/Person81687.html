Lisa Saline's Summary

As a business owner or entrepreneur you are probably searching for ways to reduce the amount of time spent marketing your business and increase your conversion rates. I hear you. You want to sell more and work less.

Your social media profiles can be a positive asset to your business and can generate leads and referral partners if properly optimized and updated.

I will help you with the set up and content development of your social channels so that you experience:
✓ Greater visibility online
✓ Obtain positive feedback from your website and social media channel visitors
✓ Increase conversion rates due to the strategic placement of your content
✓ Reduction in technical frustration by allowing me to help you with the set up and posting of your valuable content
✓ Content development so you can go play golf, ski or lay on the beach.
✓ Freedom to network and build relationships that will bring you more sales and release the administrative burdens you are faced with at the end of a long day.

Not only can I provide done-for-you services I will train your staff, or you personally on:
✓ Setting up an attraction marketing campaign model
✓ Optimizing the social media profiles you are currently working on
✓ Connecting with your target audience/customer
✓ Teach tips on how to save time on your social media campaigns and marketing plans with a number of free tools.
✓ Provide video tutorials, checklists, podcasts, and other educational modalities that will increase efficiency in the daily work as well as improve your skillsets so you are constantly have that marketing mindset.
