Allie Wist's Summary

I am an art director, with speciality in food styling and magazine layout and design. I currently provide freelance art direction + graphic design for WHIRL Publishing.

I aim to develop beautiful, meaningful visual communication through styling, composition, and design. My artistic expertise is complimented with strong management and organizational skills.

I have a particular passion for food studies and sustainable food systems. My goal is to provide a beautiful, and at the same time deeply meaningful presentation of food, cuisine, farms and local food communities.

Specialties: Print Design, Magazine Design (print + optimized for web/mobile), Art Direction, Food Styling, Prop Styling, Digital Media Strategy, Print Project Management, Online Marketing stategies, Front-End Web Design

I am experienced in the CS5 Adobe Suite (particularly in Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Bridge and InDesign). I am very familiar with directing photoshoots and working with photographers. I am experienced with social media like Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, etc. and related web-based tracking tools.
