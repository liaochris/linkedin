Mike Spaans' Summary

A historian and philosopher by training and Information Technician by practice, my professional interests cover a wide range of topics, from Medieval Geography to Social Media Marketing and from text editing to management. Above all I love to share knowledge.

Looking for challenges where I can use my versatile nature to its full advantage, I am particular interested in combining my passion for history with my IT experience and my personality as communicative leader in a meaningful way.

-- WHY | Passion
I am passionate about o.a. the possibilities of combining content and narratives from the Humanities - Medieval History - with modern information technology. Thereby unlocking them in new ways and for new audiences. Being knowledgeable in both the academic field of humanities and in IT, I add value to projects by bringing these two together and explore new initiatives.

-- HOW | Skills & Personality
As early as my first jobs, I have always grown into positions as leader/process initiator.
I am driven by new opportunities - not in the last instance with the help of new technologies - and facilitating teams with tools and processes that make them more efficient.
This comes with a passion for teaching: using my qualities to empower people with new knowledge/skills.

-- WHAT | Experience
