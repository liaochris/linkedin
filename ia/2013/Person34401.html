Derek Hyde's Summary

Mr. Hyde coordinates the development of products, oversees the technical team and manages vendor relationships to deliver information technology solutions. Mr. Hyde has much experience and success in the management, development and support of software development, information databases, application interfaces, and enterprise search. With an undergraduate degree in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Southern California, Mr. Hyde has over 15 years in the technology space while starting his career as a consultant and analyst for the prestigious National Academy of Sciences in Washington, DC.
