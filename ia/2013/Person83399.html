Luke Bayes' Summary

Since early 1998, Luke Bayes has worked with companies such as America Online, Microsoft, Applied Materials, Entertainment Arts, Hewlett Packard, PowerBar, RockShox, Levi's, Macromedia and Adobe to deliver an expansive set of distributed applications, business tools, and instructional content.

He is the coauthor of the Certified Macromedia Flash MX Developer Study Guide and two Macromedia Flash Developer Certification Exams. He is a frequent speaker at industry events, has served on the steering committee of the San Francisco Macromedia User’s Forum and is cofounder of both AsUnit, and Project Sprouts.

Luke has been consulting with Ali Mills since 2003 architecting and implementing visually compelling, data driven, application frameworks for a variety of clients. During this time, they have focused intently on design patterns, code reuse, and agile processes in the context of ActionScript and Ruby on Rails development.

Most recently, Luke has been working full time to build the first ever secure, third-party, in-Flash payment platform for Social Gold.

Some Activities of Note:
* Co-Founder of AsUnit, the only open-source, ActionScript unit testing framework that works everywhere ActionScript works. This includes Flash Players 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Adobe AIR, Flex 2, 3, 4 and Flash Lite 2.0
* Co-Authored ActionScript Language Reference code examples for Flash Player 9 release
* Co-Authored ActionScript Language Reference code examples for Flash 8 release
* Co-Authored Certified Macromedia Flash MX Developer Study Guide
* Co-Authored the Macromedia Flash MX Certification Exam
* Technical Editor for Macromedia Authorized Flash 5 Study Guide
* Co-Authored the Macromedia Flash 5 Certification Exam
* Developed and sold Flash Components (The first commercial Flash components ever offered)
* Member of the San Francisco Macromedia User Forum (SFMMUF) Steering Committee from May, 1999 through 2006
* Hosted East Bay Flash User Group (EBFUG)

Specialties

ActionScript of any flavor, Flex, AIR, AsUnit, Project Sprouts, Test-Driven Development, Agile methodologies, Object-Oriented Programming, Design Patterns, Ruby, Rake, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML
