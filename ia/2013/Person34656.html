Andy Cauchie's Summary

I have been running my own Graphic Design/Illustration and web design business now for the last 22 years, the last 14+ in a full time role. In the main I have been pretty successful. Over the years I have spread my wings and got involved with the design and supply of Exhibition Graphics and Stands and all the promotional bits and pieces that go with it. I've also ventured into the world of website design and build. Who says you can't teach and old dog new tricks!! I've have a wide range of clients large and small in a wide range of businesses. I'm always on the look out for new clients so if anybody ....... !

Specialties

Technical, Medical and Product Illustration, Logo Design, All aspects of Graphic Design, Exhibition Graphics and Portable Exhibition stands.

Engineering Apprentice and Trainee/Qualified Technical Illustrator

Lansing Bagnall

1975 – 1983 (8 years) Basingstoke

I was an Engineering Apprentice spending a year in the apprentice school, followed by another year doing 6 week stints in the factory and another year doing similar in the office area. I got into the Technical Publications division and, through my love of drawing, I got involved in technical illustrating. I stayed in here throughout the rest of may apprenticeship. For the last 5 years I continued to work in Tech Pubs within the first 2 years (1 day a week) spent at Southampton Art college on a City in Guilds Technical Graphics course (I know I passed OK but with what grade ....). In my first 4 years as an engineering apprentice I spent 1 week in 4 at Basingstoke Technical college studying a City in Guilds Engineering Technicians course.
