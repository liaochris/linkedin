Jeff Burchett's Summary

Jeff Burchett has nearly a decade of marketing experience, having worked in the financial, electronics and gaming, and energy services industries. Honing his skills in marketing strategy, B2B and B2C communication, digital and social media marketing and advertising, and rich media content creation, he has learned what it takes to develop and run successful, impactful marketing campaigns. Under his guidance, Turtle Beach's social media has become a significant revenue generator for the brand, and has experienced phenomenal growth. He has led numerous successful co-branded social media marketing campaigns working with 2K Games, Activision, Electronic Arts, Dr. Pepper, Major League Gaming, Metal Blade Records, and numerous other companies. While at Turtle Beach, he has evolved the social media for a growing business from a US-only presence to a truly global one. His experience as a corporate spokesperson, on and off camera, make him a valuable asset to any company looking for someone who knows what it takes to reach and engage customers with a brand, and his deep expertise with the English language makes him a wonderful asset to any communications-based role.
