John Burrows' Projects

  • eWRECK - Electronic Witness Re-Creation Kit

    • 2009 to 2012
    Team Members: John Burrows

    Developed by me in Flash, this software allows users to recreate vehicle collisions and accident diagrams. It's main use was envisioned to be a demonstrative for lawyers to use during settlements or for displaying to a jury in court.

    The only thing I didn't make for this was the name.

    View demonstration video here: http://vimeo.com/46454766

  • I.L.I.E.D. - Individual Logging & Interactive Evaluation Display

    • August 2008 to August 2012
    Team Members: John Burrows, David Dorris, Jason Dorris

    My personal name for the project was Hours of Service Truck Logging (HOSTL). This software was developed in Flash. The purpose of this software was to take the complicated hours of service regulations and present them in an easy to understand visual interface.

    Inputting a truck driver's driving logs into the software then creates a digital version that automatically calculates when a driver has gone into violation.

    This project was first used by Swift trucking company resulting in a jury verdict of ten million dollars. It has since been used by multiple other companies all resulting in multimillion dollar settlements.

  • Diagnostic Slice Chooser

    • 2006 to 2012
    Team Members: John Burrows

    This product takes MRI/CT images and then presents those images in a 3D rendered client likeness illustrating the level at which brain and skull trauma occurred.

    I created the entire interface and functionality allowing for colorized films and labels to appear and for side by side comparison of any available dates within the presentation.

    I built this product as a template which allows for rapid release of customized presentations for multiple clients.
