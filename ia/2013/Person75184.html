Kevin Saunders' Summary

From tragedy to triumph, motivational speaker Kevin Saunders has risen to become one of the world’s greatest wheelchair athletes and top inspirational athletes, inspirational speakers and motivational speakers today. He has captivated audiences at thousands of schools, colleges and universities, not to mention businesses of every size.

With more than 5,000 speeches under his belt - including more than 1,000 to schools - motivational speaker Kevin Saunders is perhaps one of the most well-traveled professional speakers working today. From small groups of a couple dozen students, to stadiums filled with more than fifteen thousand executives, he's worked with almost every kind of audience across several continents and countless industries. But as impressive as those numbers are, they're only a small part of his professional background. Here are just a few of his achievements:

See how Kevin's inspiring success as a wheelchair athlete helped him become a motivational speaker, business safety speaker, corporate presenter, inspirational athlete, Kansas State football motivational coach and Paralympics World Champion, church speaker and was the first person with a disabilty and Paralympian appointed to the President's Council on Physical Fitness & Sports by President Bush and the only person reappionted by President Bill Clinton.
