Charlotte Greenman's Summary

I have worked in Telemarketing and Telesales for almost 20 years and absolutely love it. It is my passion which is why I set up Accredited Marketing Ltd. I have a vast amount of experience telemarketing a wide variety of businesses including financial services, printing, IT and Software, Websites and many more. Not only do I still carry out Telemarketing on a daily basis but also offer my experience and expertise to companies through Training and Consultancy. This can be to individuals or teams.

I would be delighted to hear from you if you need any help or advice regarding Telemarketing or Telesales.

Specialties: Accredited Marketing Ltd offers telemarketing services such as appointment making, lead generation, database cleansing, cold calling, marketing campaign support, keep in touch campaigns, marketing and promotion. We also offer training and consultancy covering Telemarketing, Telesales and Sales.
