Don "Buddy" LaRue's Summary

Buddy’s experience in both residential and commercial property, knowledge of the finance industry, and overall demeanor makes him a valuable asset to your real estate transaction. With his previous experience in the hospitality industry sellers and buyers feel welcome and relaxed, while they are being kept up to date about market conditions and escrows. Buddy’s business administration/advertising college background has been a key factor in his steady business model.

Don “Buddy” LaRue was first licensed in the state of California in August of1998, and was a real estate assistant in New Mexico and California for four year prior to his obtaining his license. He was the first newly licensed agent to be accepted at Classic Homes Inc. in Palm Springs (policy was to recruit only agents with at least five years experience, and the average agent had 15 years experience); within the first year of his license he became one of the top five agents for closed sales in the office of approximately 40 agents.

In 2000, The Palm Springs Desert Sun newspaper featured him with an elite list of 25 other agents as the metro area’s most productive, the Top 1%.

Since then Buddy has expanded his real estate activities. He is currently a licensed real estate agent in California, Texas and Florida where he now resides in the Fort Lauderdale area.

Specialties: Probate real estate sales, Investment real estate, buyer representation, seller representation, general real estate.
