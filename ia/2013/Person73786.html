Karen Keller, Ph.D.'s Summary

Lasting influence resides in becoming influential - creating an internal shift (not change) in your own beliefs, attitudes, emotions, and thought processes, leading to actions that give you power. Influence is NOT just about changing what you say or do to another person. Smart men and women know this. Thriving men and women live this!

That’s where I can help.

As a former clinical psychologist and current Master Certified Coach (ICF) I bring to the table 20 plus years of understanding, comprehending, and valuing the execution of influence.

Influence is your secret weapon! And finding your influence is the single most important factor in determining the amount and extent of your success.

During the course of my career I’ve coached hundreds of small business owners, transformational leaders, and corporate executives, like PepsiCo, Pizza Hut and American Express. I gave them a combination of methods, insight into human behavior, and planned strategies, all intended to increase their influence and get results.

Smart men and women know that being influential isn't just about what you say or do to another person. It's a shift that takes place within you. After years of research and development, we are almost ready to launch the answer to the questions you may not even know you've had about your own influence potential!
