Jeff Beals' Summary

Award-winning author of "Self Marketing Power: Branding Yourself as a Business of One," and "Selling Saturdays: Blue Chip Sales Tips from College Football," I deliver keynote speeches and training seminars for companies and associations on sales, marketing and personal branding.

Speech Topics:

“Self Marketing Power: Branding Yourself as a Business of One”
To land a new client or move up, you must promote your personal brand. This presentation shows how to become a celebrity in your own sphere of interest plus 3 philosophies of personal branding, 8 rules of marketing, how to develop an area of self marketing expertise and how to get results from networking and social media.

“Selling Saturdays: Blue Chip Sales Tips from College Football”
You won't believe the skills necessary to build a championship football team! This presentation exposes sales secrets of legendary college football coaches such as Tom Osborne, Barry Switzer, Hayden Fry, Phillip Fulmer, R.C. Slocum and others. You'll become effective at selling and building relationships by learning how famous coaches battle their competition in the race to sign the most prized athletes.

"How to Market & Sell in Brutally Competitive Environments"
Untold opportunity awaits savvy professionals but there has never been a market so full of competing distractions. This presentation shows you how to unearth what your clients truly value while building trusting, career-long relationships.

"Goal-Based Networking: How to Turn Your Social Life into Profitable Relationships"
Networking has never been more important, yet most people do it poorly. Goal-based networking focuses your efforts. You'll learn what to say, when to say it and to whom you should say it plus how to synthesize social media with traditional networking. Once you master networking, your colleagues will be left wondering how you became so effective at generating opportunities.
