Kyle Shields' Summary

Kyle Shields is an experienced filmmaker, editor and motion graphics designer, he has a passion for anything media related and loves being on set. He has worked on many projects with companies such as HSN, IZOD, Nexxus, John Frieda, Oxford Republic, etc. He has several years of experience using Final Cut Pro, Color, Compressor, Shake, Avid Media Composer, After Effects, Photoshop, Soundbooth, Audition, Premiere Pro, and 3DS Max. In his spare time he usually enjoys photography, working on his short films, gaming and game development.
