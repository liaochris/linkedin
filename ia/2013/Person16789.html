Brendan M. Hare's Summary

For over 35 years, Brendan has been involved in the full spectrum of commercial and legal issues that arise in the computer, communications, controls, and life safety industries, including performance disputes, antitrust, trade secrets, copyright and environmental. He has served as founder and managing partner in litigation firms that concentrated their practices on national-level commercial disputes.

Brendan was responsible, as chief litigation counsel for Honeywell Inc., for the defense of cases aggregating billions in claims and pioneered effective litigation management and cost control procedures. He has represented several Fortune 500 companies and provided litigation management services for prominent suppliers of high technology products and services. In addition to his role as a litigation manager, Brendan has, since 1989, served as a neutral in many large complex, commercial arbitrations.

He was one of the first lawyers selected as a Massachusetts Super Lawyer in the area of commercial litigation, an honor reserved for the top 5% of practitioners in a subject matter area.

He is also a member of the American Law and Economics Association. Prior to entering private practice, Mr. Hare was Associate General Counsel for litigation at Honeywell, Inc., a Staff Attorney to the Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, and taught economics at the college level.

He holds a master's degree in economics from Syracuse University and is a 1973 cum laude graduate of Suffolk University Law School. He is admitted to the bars of Massachusetts, New York, U.S. District Court for Massachusetts, and First and Fifth Circuit Courts of Appeals.

Brendan has recently refocused his practice to concentrate on the emerging field of Litigation Finance and Funding.
