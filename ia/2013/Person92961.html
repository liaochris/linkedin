Michael Sandnes' Summary

Meet Michael Sandnes:

Accomplished healthcare executive with extensive hospital and ambulatory care center operations experience. Recognized expert in the senior living care industry. Deep experience and talent for turning around ailing healthcare businesses and optimizing healthy organizations. Experienced with both tax-exempt and investor-owned healthcare organizations, including physician group practice management organizations, hospitals, integrated delivery systems, academic medical centers, medical schools, and long-term care providers. Called upon to direct the downsizing and closure of facilities and to help healthcare providers find alternative options for remaining in business.

• Revenue cycle management;

• Marketing and service line development, censes;
• Managed care;
• Board and medical staff relations; and
• Excellence in leadership and Board of Directors relations.

Michael has a proven track record of building and operating high-performance, high-quality organizations. He has also developed capable management teams that increased patient volume, improved profitability, and maximized cash flow.

Specialties: Strategic thinker, acknowledged troubleshooter, effective change agent, values driven and engaged, hands-on leader in working with.
