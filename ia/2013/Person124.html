Gavin Greenhalgh's Summary

Consultant / Director of 2G Recruitment Ltd, a company specialising in Asbestos, Occupational Hygiene / Safety and other Environmental sectors in the UK, Australia and New Zealand. I also run a website - www.asbestosjobsinaustralia.com - that compliments my main business.

Prior to working in recruitment, I held a marketing role for an international cinema chain and also spent a period teaching. I hold a BA (Hons) degree, a Post Graduate qualification (PGCE Cert. Ed.) and am at Fellowship level with the REC, the recruitment industries' representative body.

My goals are simple - to match the right individual to the right position, and vice versa, by listening to your requirements, understanding and acting upon them.

Specialties: My speciality is my approach. Personable and professional, I believe in 'quality not quantity' so I don't try to fit square pegs into round holes or take short cuts. I haven't lost sight of the human element of recruitment and don't have an over reliance on job boards, blanket advertising & passive methods. By far, the vast majority of my business comes from recommendations and referrals which endorses my approach.

Gavin Greenhalgh's Experience

Consultant / Director

2G Recruitment Ltd

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Human Resources industry

August 2002 – Present (11 years) UK & Australia

2G Recruitment specialises in Asbestos, Occupational Hygiene / Safety and other Environmental recruitment in the UK, Australia and New Zealand.

Clients like the way we do business. With an emphasis on consultation, we listen to the needs of the Client and pride ourselves on sourcing the right individual for the job. Our success and retention rates are second to none and satisfaction levels high.

Your role is first matched against our extensive resource pool of Candidates and, if a suitable individual is not found, we expand our search expertise to find them. We may advertise the vacancy but prefer to utilise our existing resources and network of contacts. Built up over ten years in the field, this is populated with trusted individuals whose personal and professional history is known to us ensuring you of a successful match.

Candidates like the way we work. We listen and act on your behalf, consulting with you on a one-to-one basis with an aim to finding you the right job not just any job. We can operate as quickly, or as methodically, as you require – plus a high number of our Candidates often register their CV speculatively to be alerted to any new positions that may arise or to be marketed to our ever-evolving Client base.

Your CV is critical to us and is put through our rigorous quality system so it goes out looking it’s best. CVs are always sent with an accompanying profile, covering career history, location and salary preferences, so the Client is well informed from the outset. Your CV is sometimes sent speculatively but never without invitation, in batches or to people we don’t know. A well-written and professionally presented CV is a critical opportunity to make a good first impression and reflects well on both on the Candidate and the recruiter who sent it.

Please email me at gavin@2Grecruitment.com or visit our website at www.2Grecruitment.com. You can also contact me directly on 01672 511190 (work), 07884 261746 (mob) or gavgre (Skype).
