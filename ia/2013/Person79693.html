Lee Dooley's Summary

Lee Dooley is a digital and marketing communications professional with extensive leadership and management experience skilled at bringing people and ideas together to create original marketing, business, creative, and technology solutions. Lee’s experience is focused on developing and leading integrated marketing campaigns and strategies utilizing the best mix of traditional, new, and rapidly changing media, marketing, and communications tools, techniques, and channels. He is highly skilled and specialized in branding and identity and has a proven track record transforming companies and tired brands as well as creating visionary brands from scratch. He has worked on Internet projects since 1994 and possesses a rare veteran knowledge on all things Internet as well as a hands-on capacity to produce Web solutions. Lee is also a media relations expert with mastery of the most innovative, globally reaching PR distribution techniques for broader and global market penetration. With over 15 years of marketing and new media communications experience, Mr. Dooley has strong project management, interpersonal, client, and entrepreneurial skill with a high-level educational background in business, marketing, media, science, technology and design.
