Gloria Ponziano's Summary

Gloria Ponziano, a powerfully Motivational Speaker, is a trail-blazer in the field of personal growth.

In 1995 she founded More than Fitness, Inc., a successful personal training and wellness business that maintained a "wait list" for more than 5 years. Her sincere passion to help men and women become healthy in spirit, soul and body inevitably led her from the gym to the public arena of radio and public speaking after more than16 years.

By the time Gloria's 4th book was published, she became a self-taught marketing and social media expert; promoting her books and speaking to a variety of organizations, in conjunction with the marketing provided by her publisher. This initiative landed her the opportunity to write a weekly newspaper column that quickly became the newspaper's most popular.
Journey to Love, (her 4th book) found it's way to Australia and into the hands of a General Manager for a radio station with 1.5 million listeners. Once again, doors opened and Gloria was offered the opportunity to do a remote radio show for an Australian station. A year later, the Talking With Gloria radio show debuted on WTMY 1280 in Southwest Florida and beyond.

In January of 2013, Gloria launched her "Tapping Your Inner Power" events which combines neuroscience with spirituality. Unbeknownst to her, Gloria had tapped the pulse of people everywhere with this amazing wisdom. Practically overnight, "Tapping Your Inner Power" was the buzz and the positive results people gleaned from her talks made Gloria a much 'sought-out' speaker and success.


Gloria incorporates 25 years of experience into a multi-faceted approach to teaching, speaking, and training. Whether she's speaking to an audience of thousands or a small intimate group, her experience, wisdom, compassion and wit make her a much sought-out Motivational Speaker and Life Coach.
