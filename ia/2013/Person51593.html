Hans Ludi, PhD's Summary

Hans Ludi has thirty years of experience in the life sciences and technology industries. During that time he has held a variety of technical and management positions in the areas of research and technology management. Later Hans Ludi has focused on commercializing advanced technology projects in partnership with corporations and VC's.
Hans has specific experience in diagnostic and medical device industries. He started as Project Manager with Ciba-Geigy that later became Chiron Diagnostics and then Bayer Diagnostics. Over that time, he rose to become a member of the Point-of-Care business unit management team at Bayer Diagnostics. Subsequently, Hans Ludi held several Chief Executive positions in small to mid-sized companies. He was CEO of Gnothis SA, a multinational start-up company. At Gnothis, he redirected their business strategy to focus on high value added projects and services, improving financial performance and increasing valuation. Hans Ludi also served as a COO of LMA Inc., a Nanotechnology and Materials R&D Company.
Hans Ludi is a Principal at Vectur LLC and an Affiliate at Goldense Group Inc..
Hans Ludi holds a Masters Diploma in Biotechnology and Microbiology from the Federal Institute of Technology in Zürich, Switzerland and a PhD in Biochemistry from the University of Bern, Switzerland.
Recently Hans has become interested in working with non-profit organization in the Health Care and Life Sciences Industry.
Hans Ludi can be reached at: hans.ludi@att.net

Specialties: Ludi Consulting Group supports companies in the development of an integrated product development process: i.e.
- Innovate in a systematic and sustainable way;
- Improve product development and commercialization effectiveness;
- Apply open innovation.

We help companies to grow into a workplace, where people continuously innovate, and where structured business processes result in effective and efficient integrated product development ultimately resulting in sustainable growth.
