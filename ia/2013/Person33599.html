Deborah Uhl's Experience

Fine Artist and Conservator

Creative Conservation Collective

February 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months)

Preservation of painted surfaces including ghost signs, theatre curtains, wall paintings, and decorative painting. Investigation into historical decoration and replication if the original painting cannot be recovered. Creation of new murals and decoration incorporating historical design elements and nouveau elements. Preservation of the oldest carvings and paintings in America, on petroglyph and pictograph sites.

Assistant Rock Imagery Conservator

Dean and Associates Conservation

Artist and Conservator

Ethereality

January 1995 – January 2012 (17 years 1 month)

Creation of easel paintings and murals depicting the aliveness of the elements of nature. Focused subjects included bristlecone pine trees, the oldest living things on earth. The produced work was created over airbrushed skies with oil painted or acrylic foregrounds.
