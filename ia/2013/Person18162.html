Brosch For Congress' Summary

Jack is the Precinct Organization Chair at Mecklenburg GOP and is running for Congress in the 12th District.

He has owned his own computer consulting business for 26 years specializing in data import/export and analysis, as well as sales force administration.

He is a trainer in online activism and grassroots organizing for American Majority, and has been serving as Chair of the MeckGOP Precinct Organization since summer of 2010.
