Johnny Brooks' Summary

For over 19 years Johnny Brooks has been fully committed to providing a level of service to his clients that consistently places him near the top of the real estate field. Having sold real estate in Davis, Dixon, Woodland and Sacramento, Johnny has satisfied buyers and sellers with exceptional service. It is Johnny's focused approach of analysis, communication, perseverance and patience that sets him above his peers. By listening to his clients, then using his extensive knowledge of inventory, financing and contacts, he enpowers his clients to make informed, satisfying decisions. It is his goal to provide a rewarding experience for home buyers or home sellers, to provide exemplary service... everyday.

Specialties

I specialize in Davis,Ca homes for sale, traditional sales and short sales. I work with both buyers and sellers in Davis, Woodland, Sacramento and Solano County.
