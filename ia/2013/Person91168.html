Melinda Brody MIRM's Summary

After setting records for many years as a top closer in Property Management, and serving as a National Sales Trainer, Melinda formed her own sales training company (Melinda Brody and Company) which provides "real world" sales seminars, video mystery shopping services, and extensive sales training tools (including sales coaching) for the home building industry.

She travels the country conducting seminars and inspiring sales representatives in the housing industry with her keynote speeches, sales seminars and workshops.. Additionally, her video mystery shopping services have resulted in evaluating over 25,000 salespeople nationwide! Melinda Brody and Company has proudly been providing these services to home builders for over 27 years!

Melinda has a unique benefit of incorporating actual video clips of the best practices caught on tape into her sales seminars and sales training products. Not only does she teach sales people how to deliver the best presentation possible, she is able to demonstrate real life examples from the field. It is considered an honor to be showcased by Melinda as one of the top performers in the nation.

Melinda is a MIRM (Member of the Institute of Residential Marketing), a 6 time MAME Award winner (Major Achievement in Marketing Excellence) and was named one of the "Top 25 businesses in Orlando" by the Greater Orlando Chamber of Commerce. She is a proud member of the National Speakers Association, National Home Builders Association and National Apartment Association. In addition, Melinda is a regular keynote speaker at the International Builders Show.

In July, 2009, Melinda opened "TheVideoShoppers.com" . This division of the company does video mystery shopping for a variety of industries including auto, insurance, medical, retail, etc.

Specialties: Sales coaching, training and evaluating salespeople. Video Mystery Shopping Services. Melinda Brody and Company is YOUR one stop video stop!
