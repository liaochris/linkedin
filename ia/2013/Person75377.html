Khadijah Roussi's Summary

I am a positive, adaptable, and passionate designer and user advocate, specializing in UI / UX design for web and mobile platforms. My demonstrated command of UX design heuristics is complimented by my expertise with visual communication, allowing me to assertively guide complex projects from start to finish. I deliver true value, which drives up engagement for small-scale entrepreneurs and enterprise clients alike.

- I translate complex concepts into engaging applications and presentations, using clear, stunning visuals, which let the content shine, and speak for itself.

- I draw on agile development methodology to tailor my approach to each specific project.

Along with my expert level skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, and other Adobe CS6 tools, I maintain an advanced knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3, as well as other front end development tools. This blend of skills helps develop forward thinking, user-centered design, which responds to the ever-changing advancements of technologies and code.

I synthesize input from large teams to create efficient, crisp design solutions to the complex business requirements of larger organizations, resulting in stronger branding and increased user base.
