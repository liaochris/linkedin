Brad Milne's Summary

My central career focus has been acting, producing, and filmmaking for over 20 years. As an accomplished actor, director, and acting coach I've empowered my career growth in the investigation and understanding what evokes top performances in preparation for and during TV, film, and stage production, pre-recorded and live. I have experienced success producing and directing, TV-pilots, short films / web-media, corporate videos, infomercials, commercials, and live-events. BOOK, The Series, produced by By The Book Films, Inc and the Milne Studio was nominated best dramatic pilot at the 2012 BANFF World Media Festival International Pilots Competition

With ancillary and in-depth career experience in innovation, business development, and sales, I look forward to growing Milne Studios vertical integration model of vocational education and training with in house creative and production services.
