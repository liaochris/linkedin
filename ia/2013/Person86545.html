Jennaro Villa's Summary

Jennaro Villa is senior manager of business development and marketing for the New York office of Landor Associates. He is responsible for developing and executing initiatives to help achieve the office’s overall business development objective for a focused, consistent, and scalable approach to strategic revenue growth.

Jennaro is a dyed-in-the-wool business developer whose career has focused on all aspects of business development as well as account management and marketing. He has worked for agencies that include Bartle Bogle Hegarty (BBH), Berlin Cameron United, and Merkley+Partners. While at Berlin Cameron, he played a crucial role in the creation of its business development infrastructure. Once the infrastructure was established, Jennaro helped the agency win key accounts, including Comcast Business Class and three Wyndham Hotel brands (Super 8, Travelodge, Howard Johnson).

Prior to his role at BBH, Jennaro helped build, launch, and manage mirren.org, a community website for business development professionals in marketing communications. He has also worked as a business development and marketing consultant for media behemoth MediaCom, as well as for Saffron Brand Consultants and Johannes Leonardo.

Jennaro received a BS in business administration with a minor in science from Rider University. He has been a speaker for Rider’s Marketing and Advertising Student Association and is a member of DAARSTOC (Developing Administrative Abilities and Resources through the Synergistic Training of Organizational Competencies), a selective executive training organization that improves interpersonal, interviewing, and management skills.

Born and raised in Monmouth County, New Jersey, Jennaro currently resides in Hoboken. In his leisure time, he enjoys snowboarding, health and fitness, wine, and beach volleyball.
