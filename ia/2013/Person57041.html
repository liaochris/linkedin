Jake Aull's Summary

Award Winning Creative and Digital Marketing Director, with 17 years’ experience collaborating and producing fresh, successful brands, websites and social media strategies. Enjoys people, and advancing knowledge. Inspired to use creative experience and strategic marketing knowledge to grow others and their companies (my personal mission statement). I love collaborating with, developing and learning from others. My thesis final project (April 2009) was on Social Media Promotions & Technologies.
