Kara Starkey's Summary

I am a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist in private practice in Santa Clara with over ten years of experience conducting psychotherapy with children, adolescents, adults, couples, families, and groups.

Specialties: Animal-assisted therapy, play therapy, treating children of divorce and abused children (especially foster children), domestic violence prevention and treatment, parenting groups, and treating depression, anxiety, and trauma.
