David Knauss' Summary

Growth in personal life and in the work environment comes when there is an agreement made inside you that leads to a deep conviction. Decisions are really only good intentions unless followed by action. Through coaching, I partner with you to bring clarity, confidence and clear forward movement from where you are now to where you want to be. I support, encourage, provide confidential thinking space and challenge you to be intentional and on purpose with your life and work.

I believe coaching to be one of the most significant investments a person or an organization can make because, put simply, people matter. You matter! and how you live your life and interact with those in your world - matters. Coaching offers an opportunity for you to learn how you perform at your best, gain clarity on what’s most important to you, and develop your next steps to achieve your personal and/or corporate goals. I partner with you in a creative process, that inspires you to maximize your potential. I believe you are the expert and can bring the very best of you to every opportunity!

I look forward to coaching with you - Lets start the conversation! www.KnaussExecutiveCoaching.com
