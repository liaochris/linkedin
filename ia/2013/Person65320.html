Joe Grady's Summary

Visionary and innovative leader who leverages entrepreneurial drive with solid management and community service experience to create and execute resourceful initiatives and campaigns that promote outreach and maximize sales. Extensive management responsibilities and national and regional strategic sales experience, resulting in significant sales and revenue growth in multiple organizations. Strong administrative, negotiation, and recruiting skills. Expert in consultative selling techniques and customer service life-cycle management.

Highly successful in building partnerships with leaders in business, government, community groups, and nonprofit organizations in order to achieve common goals. A hands-on leader adept in overcoming challenges, transforming comprehensive strategies into compelling programs and inspiring diverse constituencies into action, enhancing market presence and gaining competitive advantage.

I can summarize who I am in four words........Loyal, Integrity, Visionary, Ethical, These words combined form an acronym - LIVE - and this is how I truly live my life.

I have achieved many successes in my career because I have focused on customer relations and a win-win attitude. I have always sought out innovative solutions to challenging situations. Regardless of the task or challenge at hand, I establish benchmarks of high performance and standards of excellence both for myself and the organizations and companies with whom I associate. I thrive on challenges or situations that others would normally stay way from or deem impossible. No, never, and can't are not part of my vocabulary. My belief is that an organization must continually evolve, and adapt. I have delivered exceptional results and have a record of achievement and awards in most, if not all, my endeavors.

My personal philosophy is to live by the Golden Rule, not only treat people the way I would like to be treated but to be candid, sincere and caring in that process.
