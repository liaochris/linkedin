Janica Smith's Summary

As an Author Consultant I help strategize and navigate the path from idea to published book. I help you determine the best publishing method -- traditional publishing, self-publishing, or publishing with a small independent press -- to best meet your goals. My experience and knowledge help you avoid the pitfalls that can unnecessarily cost you time and money. I then work with you to establish a marketing plan for your book that fits with your other business goals.

As the CEO of AdminiSmith I lead a team of Virtual Assistants to help implement the strategies we develop together. The AdminiSmith team provides support to authors, speakers, coaches and other "infoprenuers". With specific training to support authors through all phases of book creation and marketing out team can help with the business aspects of developing the product and marketing of your book.

We also help busy entrepreneurs and small businesses solve their need for administrative services without hiring additional staff. Many entrepreneurs fail to realize their potential when they allow administrative details to steal the focus of growing their business. Still others fail when they allow things to fall through the cracks as they avoid those details. We provide professional services to handle the details so you can grow your business.

Janica Smith's Experience

Owner - CEO

AdminiSmith Virtual Assistants

June 2007 – Present (6 years 3 months)

I am the Owner and CEO of a team of highly qualified Virtual Assistants who specialize in providing services to Authors and Speakers. Most of our team have completed or are in the process of taking a training and certification course to become Professional Virtual Author Assistants. We provide a team approach to assisting authors in all phases of bringing their books from idea to manuscript to printed book. Whether you are planning to self-publish or seek a traditional publisher, our team of highly trained assistants can help you manage and execute the details needed to be successful. Our services don't stop with your published book. We can help with book marketing through traditional channels as well as social media. We offer affordable author website development, press kits, marketing materials and can coordinate virtual book tours and Amazon.com best seller campaigns. As the publishing world changes, we keep updated in the ways we can help support our clients to adapt.
