Dawson Loudon's Summary

Web developer by trade, mobile developer by ambition.

I started out tinkering with web in high school and then started contracting in college. A few years later I entered the professional side working mostly for marketing and digital agencies.

A few years ago I was introduced to PhoneGap and fell in love with the idea that I could use my current skills and transfer them to mobile application development. In the beginning hybrid mobile dev was not up to par with native but in the past year there have been many advancements.

I have found that you have to learn to think in both spaces equally to produce a winning experience. A web dev with no native knowledge or a native dev with no web knowledge will have a difficult time completing a full functioning hybrid app.

I have been fortunate enough to work for companies that allow me to work between those lines and have become very proficient with this development method.
