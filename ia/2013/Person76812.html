Lifelong learner, passionate entrepreneur and lover of life.

Encouraging others to see the positive in themselves and in their lives amid the grind of personal and professional living. We forget how amazing we are when we're trying to slog through the day and get work done, laundry done, and an occasional goal done.

Who has time for putting positive thinking back in our days? Well, that's my job. My purpose, passion and pursuit, really. And happily so.

I love creating positive products for your own enjoyment and for gift-giving. Check out my Web site, www.shortCHICK.com, for a rapidly growing collection of newly produced products and short original quotes throughout 2013 and beyond!

One of the many groups I love working with are entrepreneurs. Their energy and passion continue to fuel my own! It's an amazing road and a tough road. Unless we have the mindset of a healthy entrepreneur, there's no way we'll ever succeed with our business. There's no sense in learning the tangible how-to's of running a small business, unless and until we get a handle on the intangible qualities that drive us to persevere.

  • A Shot in the Arm and a Strong Spirit: How Healthcare Professionals Help Patients Persevere. A Lifelong Patient Opens Her Heart and Journal.

    • Strong Spirit Unlimited
    • June 4, 2011
    Authors: Kris Harty, shortCHICK with the Walking Stick

    Quick, easy read of my lifelong journey as a patient with Juvenile Rheumatoid Arthritis, and with caring for parents as cancer patients. Perfect for healthcare students as well as working professionals about the positive difference that healthcare providers have in a patient's life, often without realizing it. This book ties together all aspects of the patient experience as seen firsthand through the eyes of one individual from early childhood to middle-age.

    Each two-page spread is its own chapter, highlighting one episode of the healthcare experience. Questions are included for individual reflection or group discussion. Inspirational quote on each page. Easy, quick, conversational readability.

    Perfect for workplace breaks, workplace staff meetings, and for class discussion, and as a gift for your favorite nurse, physician, surgeon, physical therapist or any other healthcare provider.
