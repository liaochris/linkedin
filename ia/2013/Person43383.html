Evan Emolo's Summary

I am a web application designer and developer based in NYC. Working with people who truly care about the work they put into the world, especially in the technology field, is what excites me. Open-source projects, rapid prototyping, novel ideas, and a healthy serving of minimalism are what make me tick.

Having spent the last two years at the NYU Interactive Telecommunications Program, and working as a producer and video-editor before entering the program, I have been fortunate to combine my interest in media, design, and technology into great projects.

I can hold my own coding in JavaScript, and Python. I have built projects using node.js, MongoDB, and D3. I have also built projects to manipulate and analyze data in Python and R. I am competent in HTML, CSS, and jQuery. Additionally I have dabbled with Ruby and Sinatra, Django, Flask, Processing, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi. Node.js/MongoDB/Backbone.js is my stack of choice.
