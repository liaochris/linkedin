Ashley Nichole's Summary

I am an illustrator and colorist based out of LA. I can promise excellent communication and quality. As a production artist I enjoy visual development, concept art, and background painting. Contact me for your creative endeavor!

I have experience in book cover painting/illustration, interactive web background illustrations, character design, and logo design. Recent work includes coloring comics, cartoon illustrations and pin ups.
