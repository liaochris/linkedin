Steve Denning's Summary

Steve Denning is the president of ITResource, Inc., a firm that traces it information technology roots to 1983. His accounting firm was among the first to become authorized to sell and support what is now MAS90/MAS200 software. A CPA and Quickbooks ProAdvisor, he is an authorized reseller/partner for Cisco, Citrix, DELL, HP-Compaq, Intuit, Microsoft, Symantec, and other technology products. Whether general consulting, planning and design, application-specific training, or technical support, Steve has built his reputation on these core objectives: listen, respond, provide value, and empower the client. He demonstrates his belief in community service through Rotary International, Meals on Wheels, the Salvation Army, his church, and other organizations

Specialties

Accounting & Information Management software implementation/integration for Professional services, Construction, Dental, Franchises, Restaurants, Retail, more.
