ALICE SYDOW's Summary

As the premier Image Consultant of the Twin Cities, Alice Sydow, helps men and women look and feel their best by portraying confidence and success. 'I've Got Your Style' is a resource that provides people and companies with image tools and knowledge to put their best foot forward everyday by consulting them on their wardrobe and style.

Alice is a professional speaker on the importance image in the workplace. She has spent several years in corporate america and helps companies communicate the importance of professional dress in the workplace and the positive effects it can have on their employees. You can find out more information at www.ivegotyourstyle.com.

As a certified Image Consultant, she received valuable training on Style, Image, Color, & Personal Shopping, at the prestigious Image Resource Center in New York City. She is also a member of the Association of Image Consultants International (AICI), the largest professional association of corporate and personal image consultants worldwide.

Go to www.ivegotyourstyle.com for a description of services

Alice also has kept up her connections in the travel industry from when she was employed full-time with Maritz Travel Company from 2003-2008. In addition, she has traveled to 5 of the 7 continents and has worked in diverse international environments and executed corporate incentive trips and business meetings for Fortune 500 clients.
