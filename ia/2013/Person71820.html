Kalynn Amadio's Summary

The sole proprieter of ikalynn, llc, a small business marketing consulting firm that helps local business owners harness the power of the internet to compete in their local market through local search reputation management, mobile marketing and social media campaigns. I help small businesses, solo and entrepreneurs become more "findable" online and get their message out to the right audience. As a social media strategist I particularly enjoy working with business organizations to craft a strategy they can embrace and implement.

I help concerned, confused and even clueless small business owners market online simply, safely and successfully. I specialize in showing smart, savvy and motivated local business owners how to kick it up online; even if you’ve tried and been disappointed before or technology just isn’t your thing. Local business entities and experts with a story to tell are my particular specialties. The internet is the 21st century tool that allows every voice to be heard but first the audience must find you.

As a 3rd dan black belt and Instructor of the world's most popular martial art, tae kwon do; I endeavor to live a healthy lifestyle through my martial arts dedication while working toward the rank of Master.

I am the creator and webmaster of www.Taekwondo-Network.com where taekwondo enthusiasts from around the world come to learn more about their favorite topic, Tae Kwon Do.

My degree is in Architectural Engineering, I still practice Architectural Lighting Design on commercial projects in the New York City area.
