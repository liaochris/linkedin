Les Sandoval's Summary

Les W. Sandoval is an active member of the American Bar Association Family Law Section. He is also a member of the Albuquerque Bar Association. His background includes nine years in the accounting field, with a year and a half of public auditing, and eight years in private accounting before he decided to become a lawyer. He is a veteran and served in the United States Naval Reserve for almost nine years. He was a member of the New Mexico Law Review, and received the West Law Family Law Achievement Certificate for excellence in Domestic Relations Law. Mr. Sandoval serves as the New Mexico representative for the Law-in-Fifty Edition of the Family Law Quarterly. His practice includes all aspects of litigation and negotiation in all areas of family law, including divorce, pre and post nuptial agreements, custody, child support, child neglect and abuse issues, and valuation and division of assets. Mr. Sandoval is also an associate professor of Accounting and Paralegal Studies at Albuquerque TVI.
