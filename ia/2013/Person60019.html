Jay Wolcott's Summary

A forward-thinking executive and entrepreneur, Jason possesses an unparalleled knowledge of customer service operations and its application to new and emerging medias. These strategic insights led Jason to launch Digital Roots in 2007 with the purpose of helping Fortune 500 clients tap into digital communications channels, specifically social media. Digital Roots services include social media monitoring, reporting and, most importantly, facilitation of proactive engagement. The unrivaled processes and technologies of Digital Roots have already had a profound impact on the customer care and social media efforts of a number of Fortune 50 clients — creating efficiencies and improvements in the way brands communicate and interact with customers and prospects alike.

Specialties: Social Media Management, Digital CRM, Customer Relationship Management, Customer Experience Management, Contact Center Optimization, Vendor Management
