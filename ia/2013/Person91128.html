Melanie Saucier's Summary

Over the last 15 years, I have focused my talents on working in the electronics and consumer goods manufacturing industries. In 2005 I co-founded a design firm offering services in industrial design, graphic design and interior design. I left Quebec City in 2010, have worked as a freelance in the Toronto area and started an online shop of handmade kitchen accessories. I am now moved to California.
Visit this link to view a few projects I have worked on: http://melaniesaucier.com/portfolio/
