Christopher Dingman's Summary

As a writer, public speaker and coach, I’ve developed a simple formula for success. Pair first-class communications with top-tier training to move companies forward and create exceptional business leaders.

If you’re a CEO or a senior manager looking to grow professionally, I can help you communicate your vision, motivate employees and lead by example. You’ll inspire those around you to produce the best products and services available and improve your company's bottom line.

Experience

Through the years, I worked as a freelance writer and journalist, learning to dig deep for the facts which give writing its authenticity. I was fortunate to write for international, national and local publications including Advertising Age, Business Tokyo and Chief Executive magazines.

I also created promotional copy for ad agencies and PR firms in New York and Boston, plus major companies and small businesses, keeping in mind that the best copy rings true with readers. Working as a spokesman, I lectured on behalf of health organizations and worked with the press, enlisting public support to defeat debilitating diseases.

As my career progressed, I founded Trimount Communications, leveraging past accomplishments and exploring new opportunities for growth. Based on my professional experience, I developed leadership training programs in four key areas which I believe every successful executive needs to master: interpersonal communications, business writing, public speaking and media relations.

Building on Trimount's leadership platform, I launched TrimountExecutivePlus coaching as a bonus level of service for C-suite and senior managers in need of additional assistance.

Please visit trimountcommunications.com to learn more or call today to arrange a free consultation. I look forward to working with you.
