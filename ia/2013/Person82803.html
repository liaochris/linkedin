Luboš Louženský's Summary

My focus is to help customer to get what they need, by using my knowledge and expertise to their benefit. The big picture is to build or help create things that users would love to use.

I am involved in IT since I can even remember, from early times of playing with now old computers, through my education and during my career I am trying tu put my skills to best use.
I have started working in IT support department where I have learned the importance of using IT to benefit users and gained knowledge of what a IT architecture is, how to develop, manage and service services, and that development of software is the just the beginning of long run. Since then I was freelancing and contracting, working on various project from fairly small like coding web sites and e-commerce solutions to bigger projects such as new product development and marketing, and user oriented IT architecture.

Also I am consulting by nights to start-ups in areas of marketing, business and IT to give them mainly ideas about company economics, sales and marketing.

I currently manage big clients, in regards to performance marketing and internet marketing as whole, helping them to design and realise lead generating campaigns and solutions.

I love working as part of bigger teams where everybody can contribute their knowledge to create the best results and I am capable of leading them with limited oversight from project sponsors.

As a hobby I am leisure photographer with main focus on atelier photography (portrait, fashion, glamour). For sports I am quite good floorball goalie and love all team sports I can play in.

I have lived and studied in Ireland among more than 100 other Erasmus students, managing school projects with members from all around Europe.

I have experience with live and written translation from and to English. I am holding and FCE certificate from British Council.
