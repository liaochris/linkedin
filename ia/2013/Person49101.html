Glen Surnamer's Summary

A business builder who believes that the economic prosperity we seek as a country emanates from a collective re-commitment to entrepreneurship and education. Small businesses face great challenges to adapt and repurpose in a greatly evolving domestic, global, and technological landscape. Industries are facing that the ecosystems that supported their 20th century solutions are no longer viable. The answers to these challenges lay not within interest rate policy, but within our ability to develop and to evolve. I ascribe to the principles and purpose set forth in the Huffington Post’s What Is Working platform that jobs are most certainly created through bottom up grass roots initiatives to put the winds in the sails of small businesses, incentivize risk taking, and improve the odds of success for start ups.

Specialties

small business strategy, financial communications, networking, startups, resource allocation, financial projections, asset management, capital markets, investor relations, corporate development
