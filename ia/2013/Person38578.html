Daniel Trimble's Summary

Nonprofit & public sector entrepreneur. 20 years in related technologies, public affairs, and nonprofit development.

Marketing and policy geek. Political operative. Explorer. Published photographer. Tea drinker.

I speak the language of markets, government, and people. For most of the past 20 years, my work has concentrated at the nexus of the government, nonprofit causes, and technology. I help lead campaigns, products, and organizations that address a range of public-interest issues, with most of my work in strategic communications, product marketing, government affairs, and fundraising.

I'm also an intelligence officer and disaster management officer in the U.S. Coast Guard Reserve; I currently hold an active Top Secret clearance.
