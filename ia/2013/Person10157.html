Armando Santana's Summary

Armando is a passionate product manager in pursuit of creating "something very beautiful or something very useful." Obsessive over the user experience and overall customer experience. Loves working on products that are delightful to use and strive to make an impact.

Armando was a member of the Product Management Team at Amazon in Orange County where he was responsible for the Amazon Appstore on Kindle Fire tablets. His prior role was with the Qualcomm MEMS Technologies Product Management Team, responsible for defining and delivering on the roadmap of disruptive products. Armando was also part of the core product management team responsible for Mobile TV products and services at FLO TV / MediaFLO Technologies.

Armando has over sixteen years of experience in the technology and entertainment industry. Prior to Qualcomm, Armando had a proven track record in successful product development/management roles at Walt Disney Imagineering’s Research & Development group, the primary R&D arm of the Walt Disney Company. At Disney he worked on various strategic and confidential projects in the areas of Mobile, Wireless, Interactive TV, Internet & New Media, Advanced Animation Technologies, Real-Time 3D graphics, Theme Park concepts, Digital Imaging, Interactive Experiences and new Product Development.

His last role at Disney was as Principal Mobile Product Development for the Disney Parks Mobile Magic service. Armando was also responsible for various mobile strategic initiatives for Walt Disney Parks. Armando holds a BS in Computer Engineering & Computer Science from the University of Southern California.

Specialties: Product Management, Product Development, Mobile, Wireless, Entertainment Technology, Business Development, User Experience, Software Engineering, Internet & New Media, Ideation Process, Computer Graphics, Digital Imaging, Interactive Television
