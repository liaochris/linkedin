Karen Walker's Summary

Global Technology Marketing Leader with global perspective; have lived and worked in Europe, North American and Asia, serving developed and emerging markets, inclusive of B2C and B2B (SMB, Enterprise, SP and Public Sector). Deep experience with all elements of marketing life cycle: business and brand strategy, corporate marketing, product management, portfolio, segment and industry, partner and field marketing. Hardware, software, services and solutions marketing leadership. Collaborative executive credited with transforming marketing to deliver profitable business impact.

Recognized industry authority on both marketing and technology topics. Board member of the I.T. Services Marketing Association (ITSMA), member of the CMO Council and Marketers that Matter Council, and frequent lecturer on business and marketing thought leadership at UC Berkeley, Haas Business School.
