Guy Carmeli's Summary

An Art Director with a passion for interdisciplinary fields of work, and a flair for style and perfection.

In the past 15 years I have gained massive experience in the various fields of design and media types such as print, photography, 3D & animation, web & software GUI design, post-production and video editing.

I am passionate about music, film, and games. I am keen to involve myself in new projects and am constantly working to expand my body of work.

Specialties
