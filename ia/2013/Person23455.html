Chris Koeleman's Summary

Textile and garment technician, with experience in practical quality control and assurance in the Netherlands and 4 years abroad. I offer companies: assistance with product development, quality manuals, production control, and product inspection (AQL or other).

Also I teach textile knowledge and quality management. Next to my aim to enrich the students' knowledge, I am always working on my own knowledge and (teaching) skills. One important aim of my company is to strengthen the moderately organised clothing industry, for both the buyers and the vendors.

I have set up a textile/quality blog, where I try to share as much as possible knowledge and news about fashion. It's meant as a kind of 'textile and quality knowledge centre', offering information about all kinds of practical subjects for fashion professional, such as AQL, phenolic yellowing, pilling&snagging, water repellency, etc. And the nr. of subjects is growing. Come and visit the site soon and/or leave a comment...
