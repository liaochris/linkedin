Marilyn Fenn's Summary

Artist and visual designer with great creativity, innovative ideas and attention to detail, specializing in design for other creatives, individuals and small to mid-size businesses and organizations.

Extensive professional web design, graphic design and illustration experience for print and online marketing and product information. Direct experience working with the industries of healthcare, software, educational publishing, fine art, music, and charity organizations.

Specialties

Web design, graphic design, illustration, technical illustration, fine art painting and drawing; also desktop publishing, fine art bookbinding, and photography.

Web design skills include protoyping in PhotoShop and coding in Dreamweaver using currently accepted industry standards, focusing on great-looking, appropriate design, and clear and consistent navigation and usability.

Specific skills include: CSS-P, XHTML, WordPress, PhotoShop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, and Flash.
