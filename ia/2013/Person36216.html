Dmitri Siegel's Summary

Dmitri Siegel is currently the Vice President of Global E-Commerce and Executive Creative Director for the outdoor clothing and gear company Patagonia. Reporting the CEO, Dmitri oversees all digital strategy and operations for Patagonia including full P&L responsibility for Global E-commerce. He sets the strategic vision for digital retail and marketing and oversees contributing functional areas including technology, marketing, CRM, creative, merchandising, and customer care. Patagonia.com operates in over fifty countries and five languages.

In his role as Executive Creative Director Dmitri oversees the visual image of the brand in all media. He leads the Graphic Design, Photography, Video and Web Creative teams in their work creating the global brand image for Patagonia.

Dmitri was formerly the Executive Director of Marketing for Urban Outfitters. Reporting to the President, he oversaw the Direct-to-Consumer business as well as Marketing and Creative departments. Dmitri had full P&L responsibility for Urban Outfitters Direct. He oversaw all Creative for the brand including catalog, web and retail art department. He launched numerous innovative retail and marketing programs at Urban Outfitters including mobile, social, and CRM.

Dmitri has published widely on the topics of Design, technology and digital culture. He is a contributing writer for Design Observer and his writing has been featured in Dot Dot Dot, Émigré, and Creative Review among others. He recently published his first book "Green Patriot Posters: Images for a new Activism" with co-editor Edward Morris.

Dmitri's work has been recognized by the AIGA, the Cooper Hewitt National Design Museum, Promax/BDA, and the International Biennale of Graphic Design. He has lectured at Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), Maryland Institute College of Art, and Art Center Center College of Design among others.

Dmtiri earned his MFA in Graphic Design from Yale University.
