Patrick Baynes' Summary

I'm a midwest guy from Wisconsin, raised in the south, and educated in upstate New York.

Working at LinkedIn was my first job out of school and the perfect launch pad into the internet and software space. I was once told that if you sliced me open to see what I was made of, it would mostly be LinkedIn DNA; I'm ok with that.

PeopleLinx is a software dashboard companies give their employees to help them use LinkedIn and other social networks for business. We are an opt-in, gamified solution and our users compete against each other on a leaderboard to be the best on social, per their organizations custom configurations. Benefits of working with PeopleLinx include freakishly high service levels and partnership with a world-class team of social business professionals.

We help our clients drive better online branding, increased customer connectivity and strategic use of social networking sites for their employees.

You're welcome to contact me at pbaynes@peoplelinx.com
