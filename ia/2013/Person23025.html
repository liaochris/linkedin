Chris Anderson, CPA, CITP, CGMA's Summary

Professional history in management in accounting, IT, operations and administration supported by consulting experience in a variety of industries (including healthcare, entertainment, technology and private equity). Former "Big 4" Senior Manager in both audit and enterprise risk services. Investor, executive, advisor and mentor with a network of resources to support acquisition and disposition review/integration, internal and external fraud risk and internal control assessment and remediation, integrated process workflow design, deployment and restructuring, corporate governance and organizational change and process improvement.
