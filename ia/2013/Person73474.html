Keaton Tips' Summary

Most of my experience is in film, animation, & motion graphics. I have worked in many different facets of the film production pipeline as well as commercial and animation work. From grip & electric, stop-motion set building to traditional and 3D animation and 2D and 3D motion graphics, I've done just about all of it.

As well as film and animation, I have worked with metal and glass professionally and made jewelry.

My passions are story telling and being productive with my hands.
