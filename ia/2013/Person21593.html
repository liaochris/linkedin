CFA is passionate about ICT, digital media, mobile technology, social media, entrepreneurship, leadership, sales and marketing. In all these areas, he has respectively made his mark as an expert public speaker, consultant, author, facilitator, business & life coach.

Simply put his deep belief in God and his mantra: Conditioned for Accomplishments (CFA) have helped him and those who he works with to record a number of successes in life. As the CEO (Chief Encouragement Officer) of CFA Leverage; an ICT consulting & online marketing strategy company, he firmly believes and preaches that individuals and businesses can achieve their objectives & goals.

In fact, with his deep knowledge of the Internet and modern trends in technology, he has developed solutions that have helped many businesses to find a space in the cyber world. His main blog (http://www.cfagbata.com) reaches out to hundreds of thousands of people locally and internationally. With over a thousand enlightening articles to his credit, CFA has earned the coveted Ezinearticles.com’s Platinum Author.

Over the years, CFA has become a regular contributor on various platforms, presented by the following organizations:

• Enterprise Development Centre (EDC) of the Pan-African University
• Fate Foundation
• SME Toolkit
• Annual IT Leaders West African Summit
• Youth Enterprise with Innovation in Nigeria (YouWiN!)
• The Full Gospel Business Men’s Fellowship International (FGBMFI)

CFA is a columnist with The Punch (Nigeria’s most widely read newspaper) where he writes for the “ICT Clinic” page published on Sundays. Many of his readers now fancy calling him the Punch’s resident “ICT Doctor.” He is a regular guest on several television and radio stations and has featured on Channels Television’s highly respected “Sunrise Classic” show as an authority discussing “Cyber Crime."
