Kevin Poulsen's Summary

Kevin Poulsen is the Senior UI/UX Architect and Co-Founder of Storeplacer, LLC. With a strong background consisting of 15 years of software engineering, business analysis, and managing development teams, Kevin is now focused on producing the best UI/UX for Storeplacer's products by leading all aspects of the front-end web engineering efforts.

Founded in 2010 and based in Chicago, Storeplacer has developed an innovative unified data platform featuring a Big Data backend and secure REST API layer. In 2011, Storeplacer raised a Series A round backed by successful internet entrepreneurs to capitalize on converging trends in eCommerce, advertising, and cloud technologies.

Before joining Storeplacer, Kevin enjoyed a successful career at Spencer Stuart and Associates for nearly 10 years. During his time there, he held roles including Senior Web Developer, Product Advocate, and Director of Software Development. During this time he introduced web standards to the development team, initiated and coordinated CSS training, created technology prototypes, built applications using an agile development style, and delivered presentations to executive audiences.

Kevin also worked as a Senior Consultant for Software Architects, Inc. (now Sogeti) for five years during which time he developed applications including payroll systems, advertising and billing systems, and websites for multiple national and international clients.

A 1997 graduate of the University of Wisconsin, Parkside in Kenosha, Wisconsin, Kevin holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Management with a concentration in MIS, and was named to the Dean’s List in 1996 and 1997.

Specialties: CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Google Web Toolkit & UiBinder, Google App Engine, Java, HTML5, (X)HTML, DHTML, C#, .NET, SQL, Data Modeling, XML, XSLT, SOA, Actionscript, Ruby, Software Architecture, Information Architecture, Interface Design, Systems Design, Product Management, Communication & Presentations
