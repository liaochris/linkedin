David Shirey's Summary

IT professional and Project Manager with strong business and EDI skills. Have spent the last fifteen years split between being an ERP oriented Business Consultant (with special emphasis on the PRMS ERP package), an EDI consultant who does both initial set up and ongoing support for the GXS TLi package, and as a skilled IBM i technical specialist with programming skills in RPG ILE, RPG /Free, SQL, and PHP.

Specialties

Project Management - Skilled in managing both IT and business projects using waterfall or Agile/Scrum. Good written and verbal communication. Focused and organized.

ERP - Experienced in solving business and technical problems in a variety of business environments.
Manufacturing - MRPII, Lean, Kanban, Short Time Frame, and Shipment Driven.
Distribution - traditional warehouse, High Volume Distribution, and Consignment-VMI
type warehouses.
Financial - AP, AR, GL, and the cost interface to the manufacturing and distribution areas.
Technology - Familiar with on site ERP systems, Cloud based environments, and SAAS.

PRMS - The ERP system that I am most familiar with is PRMS which runs on the IBM i platform. I am familiar with all modules, and all releases up to and including 10.1. Also very familiar with the data base and with making modifications or enhancements.

EDI - Planning, installation, and ongoing support of EDI featuring the Inovis Product Suite. Experience includes x.12, EDIFACT, XML, a variety of comm scenarios, and the integration of EDI into the ERP system.

Technical - IBM i (AS/400) platform, RPG ILE, /Free, DDS, CL, DDL, SQL, IT Audits, Security Audits, Bar Code / Scan applications, HA/DR Planning.

For more information, visit my company website, http://www.shireyllc.com or my LinkedIn company page for Shirey Consulting Services or contact me at dave@shireyllc.com (616 304 2466).
