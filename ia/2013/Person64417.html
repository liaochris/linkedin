Jack Klingert's Summary

Jack Klingert has over twenty years of experience leading high technology companies in the global marketplace. His core strengths include his deep technology background coupled with a drive to close business transactions that deliver high value commercial and consumer products and services. He is known for his hands on leadership and his ability to deliver operational goals.

Jack has international experience leading teams to generate significant revenue streams through new product launches, business development, intellectual property licensing, mergers and acquisitions, joint venture formation and divestiture. His leadership skills include substantial experience in successfully launching organizations and restructuring operations for maximum shareholder return.

He is an active member of the Licensing Executives Society. He is a Certified Licensing Professional (CLP).

Specialties: business development, commercialization , contract negotiation , cross-functional teams, digital media, divestiture, due diligence, emerging technologies, global transactions, high technology, intellectual property, joint development, licensing, litigation, mergers and acquisitions, new business development, patent monitezation, product development, product marketing, research and development, strategic planning, technology licensing, technology transfer, valuation
