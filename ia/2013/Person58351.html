Jane Vora's Summary

I have been building websites for a long time. I started learning to build websites back when everyone was using tables in the golden days of Geocities. I started pursuing web design professionally when I realized how everyone I knew needed help with their online presence. I am passionate about design, typography (ooh I love a good slab serif), well crafted content, and clean code.

I come from a family of entrepreneurs. I started my first business when I was 5, selling origami at the counter of my parent's old print shop. Ok, I only sold one origami flower for 50 cents, but since then I've learned a ton about doing business.

Recently, I graduated from the Flatiron School, where I learned to build Ruby on Rails applications. Check out my Github to see what I've been working on: http://github.com/janeeats
