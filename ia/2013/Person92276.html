Michael Nicholson's Summary

Do you need a website? Do you need marketing materials? Do you need letterheads, business cards, and/or flyers? Are you creating a site, or is someone creating one for you but does not possess the means of producing an eye-catching final product? Well look no further.

MDN graphics can provide solutions to these questions and more. You have a current website up, but you feel that it is outdated...we can assist you by creating a site that will be head and shoulders above the rest. You need a brilliant design and layout for your company brochure, manual, financial
report etc....we can help you with that. You need a sign for your newly established or current business...we can design it for you! Check out all of the services in which we provide in the services section of this site.

MDN graphics is a professional graphic design and marketing business that creates business solutions, creatively utilizing both design and technology.The services that we provide include web development, brand management, print design, and interactive media.

Graphic Design Logos, Letterheads, Collateral Material, Corporate Identity, Custom Design, Illustration, Brochures, Architectural Signage, Vehicular Signage, and more.
