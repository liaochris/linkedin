Eyran Katsenelenbogen's Experience

Owner

Eyran Records

January 1998 – Present (15 years 8 months)

Company Description:
Eyran Records is dedicated to the production, distribution and preservations of master pianist Eyran Katsenelenbogen. Established in 1998 and operated by Eyran Katsenelenbogen Management, Eyran Records specializes in solo piano music, with special interest in jazz, tonal and free improvisation, renditions of folk and popular songs, contemporary classical music and original compositions.

Eyran Records’ catalogue is distributed via CD Baby and its digital distribution program, which includes Apple iTunes, Rhapsody, eMusic, Amazon, Verizon V-Cast and Napster. Eyran records’ CDs have been enthusiastically reviewed by well noted jazz publications and websites, such as All About Jazz, eJazzNews, Jazz Police, Jazz Review, Jazz Times and All Music Guide.
