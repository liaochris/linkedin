Jake Nonnemaker's Summary

I am the CEO of AXICOM, Inc. in Westlake Village, CA which provides computer and technical services to businesses. I started AXICOM in 1996 to help local businesses leverage technology to make them more efficient and profitable.

Previously, I received my B.S. in Engineering from CSUN in 1992, worked briefly for the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers then at the L.A. County Department of Public Works.

My main goal is to continue to grow the company and bring technology to businesses to help their growth while developing my business and leadership skills. It is also very important to me to maintain a balance between my faith, my family and business.

I believe values are very important. AXICOM's core values are communication, integrity, respect, teamwork and efficiency. We strive to deliver a special experience with our customers and exceed their expectations.

I have been married to Christa since 1995 and we have two young boys. I am active in my church and I am also a Cub Scout den leader.

Specialties

Microsoft Windows server, Exchange server, and SQL server. HP and Dell server solutions. Various SAN and NAS storage solutions. SonicWall firewall and VPN solutions.
