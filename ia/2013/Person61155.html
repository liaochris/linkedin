Jeffrey Blais' Summary

Strategy, design, thought leadership and business transforming experience design solutions across the mega-channel landscape, pushing the boundaries on how users interact with and within environments.

A seasoned experience design leader, driving business transforming and brand-forward solutions within the Omni-Channel landscape; including channel strategy, the underlying experience architecture, to the design, development and delivery of related experience digital channels and environments.
