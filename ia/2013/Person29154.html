Dan Carbone's Summary

Founder of Virteom. Many people ask me what does Virteom mean? It simply means 'Virtuous Neon Cranium' translated into 'Upward Bright Thinkers'.

Who are we?
At our core we are a web and mobile development firm.

What do we do?
1. Virteom services companies that need web sites or mobile applications created.
2. Virteom acts as the back-end technical infrastructure to a handful of marketing firms located in Cleveland and Detroit.

Why are we different?
If a web design firm promises you an award winning website, run the other way! If you feel like every time you launch a website its not very effective then you need to talk to us. We are all about rhythmically growing your business after website launch.

Simply put, Virteom is a company that creates a Rhythmic marketing plan that helps you create awareness and gain new business through it's lead generation tools.

My Past History...
In the past I have been the lead engineer and architect that has developed and delivered websites and mobile applications for corporations / organizations with revenues between 1 and 100 million annually. I have lead amazing developers who have backed the technology and implementations.

My development teams have created strategies with both C-Level and upper management teams in developing compelling online and mobile applications.
