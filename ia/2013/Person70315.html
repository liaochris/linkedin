Judi Knight's Summary

Business Strategy Coach, WordPress Website Developer and Graphic Artist. My team works with small businesses and entrepreneurs to create an online presence that reflects their business brand and converts visitors into sales. Not only does New Tricks set up creative, useable WordPress websites, we train and coach our clients to use new technologies and social media tools to reach their business goals.

We offer full service solutions; business marketing consultation, graphic design and web development to help grow business and relationships online and in real life.

Specialties

My background as a Clinical Psychologist, CEO of a Software Company, and Artist give me the ability to work with a client on branding through website development with training and on-going social media and business coaching.

I am the Coordinator the Atlanta WordPress Users Meetup Group with over 1100 members and organized WordCamp Atlanta 2012.

I am available to speak on Social Media, Website Strategy and Email Marketing.
