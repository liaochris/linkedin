John van Oossanen's Summary

I have been in Banking/Finance from 1974 to 1990 and in the International Trading business as a Finance Manager from 1991 to 2009. I am a "Generalist with an eye for detail" who then decided to be an advisor to (small) businesses and entrepreneurs in my fields of expertise.
