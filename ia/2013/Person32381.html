David Parker's Summary

David Parker is an estate planning attorney specializing in helping seniors and their families preserve their assets from the devastating costs of nursing home care. Medicaid Planning is critical for every middle class family, whether they have $50,000 or $1 million.

I will be speaking monthly on the subject of estate planning, how to use the living trust, how to avoid Probate, how to use special trusts to have Uncle Sam pay for nursing home costs and how to minimize or completely avoid federal estate taxes.

Specialties

Estate Planning, Elder Law, Trusts, Mediciad Planning, Real Estate, Creative Real Estate
