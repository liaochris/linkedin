Lance Bachmann's Summary

As the founder and President of 1SEO.com back in 2009, Lance Bachmann knew what it took to provide the best search engine optimization (SEO), pay per click (PPC), social media optimization (SMO), and website design services that clients and businesses around the world would appreciate. Throughout the years of 1SEO.com’s existence the company has been able to grow their clientele to 400+ organizations both locally and for Fortune 500 companies, providing them their entire search engine marketing needs.

Prior to 1SEO.com, Mr. Bachmann successfully founded YellowPages.com premise division of AT&T and became their first principle employee. Through hard work, dedication and passion, his strong business strategies are what helped take the company from no revenue to $157 million. Also he had worked for Verizon Yellow Pages as the District Sales Manager, Account Executive and Account Representative.

Graduate of Temple University in Philadelphia, PA, Bachmann obtained a Bachelors of Science in Education and also his Executive Master of Business Administration at the Fox School of Business and Management.
