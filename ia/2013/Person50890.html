Danny Lehrman's Summary

Recently, I gave a local student a guitar lesson by webcam, using Skype. It may have been the most effective lesson I have taught. My intentions are to bring guitar lessons by webcam to local students. I don't live in a large town, but think about it. No travel time is involved, therefore no gasoline is used, environmental damage is incurred, or travel time wasted. Something about webcam lessons commands more attention from my students. I also offer private guitar lessons in my guitar shed, and will travel to your home to teach you if you request it.

As a guitar teacher, my job includes teaching everything guitar. From re-stringing ones instrument, to making adjustments to the action. Perhaps you need some help learning how to use an amplifier, or effects pedals, my lessons include all things guitar.

Specialties: Repair, upgrade, setup guitars.
