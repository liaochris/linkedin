Daissy Linares' Summary

Daissy is a professional Visual Communicator focused on the development of "one-of-a-kind" branding solutions that combine unique media techniques rising above the conventional.

She approaches every design project by involving in-depth research about her clients and their customer needs. Her process concentrates on conceptualizing first and then delivering high quality work with strategic value. Daissy enjoys collaborating with other creatives and partnering with her clients in delivering comprehensive marketing campaigns that include business strategy, branding evolution, and customer focused results.

Specialties: Creative Direction + Branding + Interaction Design
