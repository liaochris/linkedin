John Parkinson's Summary

A successful senior executive leader and general manager with international experience in business & technology management, I have been a COO, CIO and CTO and spent over 20 years as a consultant on business strategy, business operations, technology strategy and portfolio management.

I was a board member for TeleGuam LLC., the private holding company that provided telecommunications services on the island of Guam and I am currently an external director at Visible Spectrum Inc. and US Micro Corp. and an advisory board member for Morningstar Inc.

In 2003, I was named by Global Consulting News as one of the world’s 25 most influential consultants. In January 2005 I was named as one of the Computerworld 100 Leaders in IT.

I have written or edited five books on the process of information systems development and have contributed over 40 papers to conferences and journals. I write a regular column and blog – “Strong Signals” – for CIO Insight and have contributed online content to the Wall Street Journal and Harvard Business Review.

Specialties: IT organization leadership; business and technology strategy; organizational change management; organizational design; business and technology architecture; technology operations; information security.
