Jim Garcia Real Estate Agent's Summary

My background includes 20+ years as CEO/COO of 4 high growth businesses in Colorado and actively working as a entrepreneur – In High Tech & Low Tech. Over the years I have worked in Fortune 100 C-Level Sales, Finance, Detailed Contract Negotiations, Mergers and Acquisitions, and developing effective deal structures.

I specialize in Castle Rock and Castle Pines, and have lived here for a number of years. I am aware of many issues/concerns which may arise during a real estate transaction. Why do taxes & HOA fees vary significantly? What are the various pros and cons of the different subdivisions? What about new construction, prices, and floor plans? Some brokers not familiar with this area my not be familiar with our local market. Denver is a very different market from Castle Rock/Pines. (I know I lived in Denver for 15 years).

YES - You should always be represented by a Buyer Agent in Colorado.
YES - You should have a broker if you are buying from a builder.

Buyers: Sellers pay for Buyer Agent fees to represent you as a Buyer in a transaction in Colorado. And typically you cannot negotiate a better price by representing yourself. For more information, see the home page on my website.
