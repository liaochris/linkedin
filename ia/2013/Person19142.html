COL (Ret) Byron L. Cherry, PhD's Summary

Succeed to Lead, LLC (STL) was established in 2008. Succeed to Lead is a SBA Certified 8(a) Program Participant (Effective: 21 April 2011), Certified Small Disadvantaged, VA Certified Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small, Virginia SWaM (Small Woman and Minority Owned), and VDOT Certified DBE (Disadvantaged Business Enterprise). We are committed to serving as a force for integrity and good sense. We have effectively built and developed solid relationships with our clients (e.g. DHS, USSOCOM, Marine Corps Systems Command, TRADOC HQs, Small Business Administration, Commonwealth of Virginia, CGI Inc., and The Ravens Group. We deliver high-quality and best value to our clients through exceptional creativity, ingenuity, analysis, communication and leadership skills.

STL (www.succeedtolead.com) provides a broad range of consulting services in Financial Management to include all areas of Budget Analysis and Budget Execution, Information Technology, IT Portfolio Management, Program/Project Management, Organizational Leadership and Human Resource Management to the Department of Defense, other federal agencies and state and local governments. Your success is our success!

Our knowledge of financial management regulatory requirements and industry best practices make us a leader in assessing, designing, and implementing your organization's overall financial management outlook. We have experience in providing expert Planning, Programming, Budgeting, and Execution (PPBE) advice to Government agencies, Program Executive Offices and program managers.

We are a member of NaVOBA (National Veteran Owned Business Association), AFCEA (Armed Forces Communications and Electronics Association) and ASMC (American Society of Military Comptrollers). We are also certified by the Virginia Department of Minority Business Enterprise. Succeed to Lead’s SWaM/DBE Certification Number is 664092.

We are a Top Secret Level Cleared Facility.

“Your Success is our Success!”
