James Hedlund's Summary

I currently work as a Staff Manager in the Collection Department for a law firm in Buffalo Grove which mainly deals in condominium association law. I supervise 15 employees and am involved in the day-to-day workflow and ensure adherence to quality standards, performance management goals, and proper procedures.
We have become very technology driven over the past several years and I have been involved with managing projects to move us forward in that direction. In fact, a few of the most recent positions I have held had been newly created so I have had to not only manage certain systems, but in some cases had to design them and/or their processes from scratch.
Some of my recent accomplishments are acting as co-chair on two separate paperless committees and a new software committee; designing, implementing, and managing a quality assurance program; and creating and running reports for performance goal monitoring for the entire department. On the committees, while also providing insight and ideas on the processes, I was in charge of managing project timelines and creating workflows using MS Excel and Visio.
Another one of my main functions is providing management with monthly, quarterly, and annual reports and analysis on various firm functions, ranging from billing to revenue to timeliness goals, etc. While many reports I run are the same from month to month, I am often asked for specialized reports that I have to create with SQL queries on the various databases. I am also regarded as the firm expert on the various software systems we use and have the most knowledge on the underlying SQL tables and how they relate to each other.
