Jon Dufau's Summary

Jon is a local boy – born, raised and working in Southern California. An Art Director and Designer hell-bent on developing creative and concept-driven visual solutions, he is dedicated to kicking ass and chewing gum – and he’s all out of gum. From his first doodles of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and Spider-Man to graduating from CSU Long Beach’s prestigious Graphic Design BFA program, Jon has developed and honed a keen sense of design over the years.

When he’s not trying to achieve world domination through design, Jon enjoys working on personal design projects, following sports - especially the NFL and MLB, game nights with friends and knocking back a good craft brew.
