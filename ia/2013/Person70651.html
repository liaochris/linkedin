Julia Williams, ASID's Summary

Color unifies spaces, leading the eye through a room or visually emphasizing an area. It can help mask an awkward architectural element or emphasize a beautiful feature within a room. It can impact emotionally and physically, boosting a dreary mood or providing a calm, cool oasis. When a room needs help to become the space you love to use, color can be the ideal solution!

Space planning is one of the most important elements to good design. A room can be filled with the most beautiful furnishings available, but without giving them space to be comfortably viewed, used and enjoyed, a room will never feel quite "right". Space planning incorporates the placement of furnishings and an awareness of where best to let people navigate within the space.

Lighting is often-neglected, but plays a major role in maximizing the visual impact of a space. Picture a room with your favorite wall color, ideal furniture and accessories. Now imagine sitting in that room with the lights off at dusk in the middle of winter. Is it still as inviting, warm and exciting as you first imagined? Lighting is an integral component of interior design, and should be given the same level of consideration as any other element.

Furniture and accessories set the style and mood of a room, affecting users' physical comfort. Knowing that may make choosing a new piece (or a whole houseful) of furniture a little overwhelming! It can be daunting trying to balance size, style, color, pattern, shape, comfort, features/accessories, not to mention time and budget. With professional guidance, choosing new furnishings can be fun and rewarding.

AutoCAD drafting helps to provide construction drawings that are clearer, faster, and more easily adjusted than traditional hand-drawings. With AutoCAD, I provide my clients with multiple design options, furniture layouts, and precisely dimensioned drawing sets that can be used by contractors. I can also coordinate with architectural and engineering professionals.

Specialties

My design services range from two-hour "brainstorming" sessions, to employing AutoCAD drawings, professional contractors, and the latest products to create customized plans for your space.

I use only the finest resources in the Portland Metro area to help my clients create the home or office of their dreams.

I approach every design project as a chance to create something unique. I don't start with any preconceived notions of what "should" be done with a space, but instead work to get to know my clients and how they choose to live and work. I help my clients identify the design elements that will provide a sense of comfort and tranquility within their space.
