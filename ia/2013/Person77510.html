Kurt Gies' Summary

Certified Mortgage Planners - Responsible for originating residential and commercial loans using all the current major product lines, including, VA loans, USDA loans, FHA loans, Conventional loans and HARP refis.

American Way Real Estate - Broker/Owner for a full service real estate brokerage company specializing in distressed residential assets in Central Florida.

RHEMA Solutions is a non-profit organization designed to help distressed homeowners. Inspired by real estate and financial professionals who have come together to offer HOPE to homeowners by arming them with the knowledge, information and resources necessary to fully understand their current mortgage situation. The only organization of its kind, RHEMA Solutions understands that simply providing the resources to learn about loan modifications, short sales, and the impacts of bankruptcy or foreclosure is not enough. A complete plan is laid out addressing personal and professional expectations and a specific course of action the homeowner has elected to pursue. Then, by partnering with one of our affiliate-specialists who share our same core values, homeowners are properly guided through the execution stage - the final step to ending their mortgage anxiety.

RHEMA Solutions is both an acronym (Remain Home and End Mortgage Anxiety) and a word with Christian context. A “rhema” is and act of service inspired by the Holy Spirit thru reading a passage of Scripture, which in our case has called us to use our skills to help our neighbors find a way out of this mortgage mess.
