Dale Halterman's Summary

My background as a professional IT management consultant and program leader has allowed me to advise some of the world's best bio-tech companies - McKesson, Gilead Sciences, Pacific Biosciences - and other world-class organizations like Hewlett-Packard (HP), Safeway, Electronic Arts (EA), Chevron, Agilent Technologies, REI, Accenture, Novellus Systems, Booz-Allen, Vivitar, Genetech, Tata and Honeywell.

Specialties: Late-stage deployment activities surrounding SAP implementations and upgrades; engagement and professional services delivery management; recovery of technical programs and projects which are 'at risk'; PMP; MBA.
