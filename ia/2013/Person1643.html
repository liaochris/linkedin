Adam Stairs' Summary

I specialize in fully accessible web and graphic design. The stigma exists that because a web site is accessible then it has to be dull and void of any aesthetic qualities; this just simply isn't true and I can show you why!

I have worked in a variety of industries from the private sector to government contracts, as well as non-profit organizations. The sites I have created range from a fully flash site to a multi-tier; 200+ page layout utilizing everything from HTML5 and JQuery, to PHP and Flash .

I can also compete in a strictly graphics oriented environment as well as a strictly code-driven web system.

Specialties

Web design, web development, graphic design, accessible design, life-cycle development.
