Bob Cameron, PhD's Summary

I have worked in the public service sector for the past 30 years (25 of those in the criminal justice field), and completed my doctorate in Human Services Administration, with a specialization in Criminal Justice, through Walden University in 2011.

I'm also an adjunct professor and teach a variety of criminal justice and general education courses.

Specialties: Criminal justice, coaching, leadership, human motivation, and online teaching and learning.
