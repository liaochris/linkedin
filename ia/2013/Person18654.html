Bryce Howitson's Summary

I design Products, Services & Experiences for a wide range of business and processes. Its my passion to make products both usable and useful while maintaining a strong aesthetic. I have over 10 years of experience with all aspects of interactive technology and leading teams to produce powerful features rooted in sound business strategy. I love building technology platforms and have experience in everything from a SaaS accounting & legal tool to digital courseware to smart energy meter trends.

In the past I've described myself as an interactive designer and as such I have an extensive experience with both user interface design and front-end development. Basically I'm usually capable of building the front-end of what I design.

I believe very strongly in life-long learning and frequently spend time teaching courses related to advertising/design/development/technology at the University of Minnesota and the Miami Ad School. I also understand that everyone has skills to share and to that end I'm working to build tools to empower organic learning.
