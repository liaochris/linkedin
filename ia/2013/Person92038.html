Michael Arnold Mages' Summary

I currently run a design consultancy specializing in strategically oriented design practice: research, communications analysis and planning, as well as UI design and development for web, mobile and web-based applications.

In the past I have taught research-informed design practice to students in business, computer science and fine arts. Past projects have included a Systems Design and Branding project for Case Logic, a Systems Audit and Redesign for the Denver Regional Transportation District, and an extensive Experience Audit and redesign of all customer-facing business processes for the Denver District Attorney's office.

This past year, I served as visiting faculty at the University of Colorado, Denver in the Digital Design program.

Specialties

Experience Design, Interface Design, Research, Design Theory, Education, Digital Media, Digital Art, Physical Computing, Conference Programming, Branding and Identity, User Testing, HTML, JavaScript, usability, CSS
