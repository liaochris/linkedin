MC Casal's Summary

Managing Partner of the digital agency Relax In The Air, MC is Working there as a UX Strategist and Designer.

MC prototypes and designs user experiences for the digital world.

Her main task is to bring clarity and simplicity to complex constraints on a human scale. She steps into the front and back user’s shoes to provide to most efficient tools, websites, applications and business processes through experience design.
By analyzing the vision, the context and the business goals of her clients, she is able to understand the opportunities to implement sustainable solutions for a changing digital future.
With her business partner, Sabine, she helps define and manage digital ecosystems to understand where and how to engage with customers.

Her expertise in the digital world also led MC to be a successful lecturer in several Swiss communication and marketing schools like CREA Genève, CREA Digital, the SAWI, Polycom, ESM and Haute Ecole de Gestion de Genève.
MC is also columnist for Cominmag.

  • Secteur public et numérique: le défi de la gestion du changement

    • Relax in The Air
    • February 5, 2013
    Authors: MC Casal, Sabine Dufaux

    L’intégration des technologies numériques et de l’engagement social donnent l’opportunité aux institutions publiques de mieux servir et interagir avec leurs usagers. La mise en place de cet écosystème digital implique cependant des changements structurels, organisationnels et culturels. Au travers de l’exemple des Musées d’art et d’histoire de Genève, Relax In The Air montre comment ce processus de gestion du changement centré sur l’utilisateur (publics et équipes des musées) a été mis en place.

    Conférence donnée par Sabine Dufaux (Strategist) et MC Casal (UX Strategist / Designer) à l'occasion de la 22ème Journée de Rencontre de l’Observatoire Technologique à l'Etat de Genève. La journée avait pour thème: Transformation des organisations (http://www.ot-lab.ch/?p=3687).

  • "We created a monster" #6 Uncomfortable Talk in Vienna [LHBS]

    • Relax In The Air
    • October 2011
    Authors: MC Casal, Sabine Dufaux

    Uncomfortable Talks are provocative, inspirational talks and points of view about marketing & innovation hosted and sponsored by LHBS in Vienna.
    The 27th of October, Relax In The Air held a talk with the title “We Created a Monster”.
    By focusing on markets, clients, campaigns, stats, profits and on the seemingly miraculous effects of social media, we, agencies and marketers have neglected our responsibility for the newly created users. By focusing on classic interruption we have lost focus on the end user experience and continue to bombard people with classic marketing gimmicks but in new digital platforms. Is there an ‘ethical’ issue in how we do marketing online and keep the user experience and benefits in mind?
