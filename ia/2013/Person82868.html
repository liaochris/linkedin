Patrick (Pat) Murphy's Summary

We are a full service security consulting firm, providing expert witness services, security consulting, assessments, and training. We also conduct internal theft and fraud investigations.

Forensic Expert Witness for litigation matters involving security issues such as:

* Standard of care and foreseeability within claims of inadequate security/security negligence.
* Premises liability in apartments, malls, parking areas, other venues.
* Security Guard and Bar Bouncer issues such as conduct resulting in serious injury or death, negligent hiring, breach of contract, inadequate training.
* Retail Loss Prevention involving use of force, inadequate training.
Both plaintiff and defendant.

Consultant to businesses regarding
* Apartment and other Multi-Family properties through our Project CARE: Community Awareness, Response & Education program.
* Physical security assessments
* Retail Loss Prevention and Operations
* Threat analysis
* Training.Seminars

Contributing Author: Retail Crime, Security, and Loss Prevention. An Encyclopedic Reference. Published 2008. Butterworth-Heinemann (Chuck Sennewald/John Christman)

Specialties

Litigation forensic security expert, loss prevention, investigations, apartment security management, retail operations, physical security, school and campus security, employee theft and fraud investigations.
