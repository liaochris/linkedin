Thomas Williams' Summary

From our parent company CityWide-TrashOuts:

We are Charlotte's first and only mattress recycling company ! Started in 2012 we have grown into one of the largest mattress recycling programs in North Carolina. We service Hotels, Residents, Apartments, Schools & many more companies. This is a free service that we offer to keep mattresses out of the landfills & off the street. We will pick up one or a hundred if you need them removed. If you need one or more removed please contact us at 704-689-8836

Why recycle them?

Over 30 million mattresses are thrown in the landfills every year!

The wood, foam, cotton and metal springs that make up most mattresses are all recyclable. This saves valuable space at the landfills.​
It takes over ten years for some of the materials in a mattress to decompose! With the help we give and other recycling companies we can reduce the amount that goes to the landfills.

We start off by getting the mattress from companies and people like you.
We break down the mattress by taking off the soft material and separating the foam and cotton to be recycled.​
We transport the 20 to 40 pound springs to local recycle companies.​
The wood frames are also recycled and can be used again. ​

Mattress Recycling

CityWide-TrashOuts

August 2012 – Present (1 year 2 months) Charlotte, North Carolina Area

Our goal at CityWide Mattress Recycling is to bring people and companies together & keep mattresses out of the landfills. It only takes one phone call from a hotel or donation center to keep hundreds of mattresses from clogging up the landfills.

We will do the whole process for you, saving you money and time. We will pick up the mattress and recycle and no time or money wasted for you. Plus you feel a little better knowing your mattress is going to good use.

WHAT MATERIALS:

>Quilting and foam can be turned into carpet underlay.
> Wood is recycled into biofuel or other recycled wood products
>Plastic is recycled by plastic recyclers
>Steel from the box-springs are recycled into new metal products
>Cotton and felt can be recycled into new felt and insulation

If you own a business and would like to get involved please contact us. It takes only one company to make a difference but it takes a few to change how we look at things. If a few can get together it will show that we are all together to make a better Charlotte. Contact us on how we can work together and make a difference. This is a great way to work with other companies and show your support for the Charlotte communities. Also we will provide your links on our website that you showed support.
