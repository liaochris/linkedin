Alexander Minidis' Summary

Highly creative and innovative project and team leader with more than a decade experience of pre-clinical drug development.

Excited by any form of cross-over research and applications - leading, drug-design, information analysis, IT programming and testing, applications such as drug-delivery, or robotics and tele-medicines, consulting, etc., thus open to new challenges, and easily adopt to new projects.

My experiences cover extensive collaborations across multiple disciplines (within drug-development), joint ventures/collaborations with external partners, leadership during change, continuous improvement, strategies in patenting & writing and software testing.

Selected achievements include: 
* Main contributor to design and synthesis of small molecule pain-target inhibitor AZD2066, which reached phase II clinical trials, delivering a proof of concept
* Leading teams during organizational changes
* (Co)Inventor on a number of patent applications
* (Co)Author on a number of scientific publications on synthetic chemistry and medicinal chemistry
