      VISION
      WAHMs WIN is an empowering women's business network for experts and for emerging businesses. We are also a business education advocacy organization that promotes training and mentoring of digitally disadvantaged mom entrepreneurs by the community stakeholders who are already serving them.

      PURPOSE
      Goals and Expected Outcomes
      Our aim is to help connect our network of experts, who are joining our online network and local chapters, with disadvantaged, minority mom entrepreneurs who are ready to learn and to be mentored by them. The end goal is for these moms' businesses to be launched with the proper foundation for continued growth and prosperity for their families and communities. Ultimately, their local economy will also improve over time, as a result of having more mothers who are prospering in their businesses.
