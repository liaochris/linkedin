Dr. Judith Wright's Summary

A media favorite, sought-after inspirational speaker, respected leader, peerless educator, lifestyles expert, bestselling author, world-class coach, and corporate consultant, Dr. Judith Wright (www.judithwright.com) wrote There Must Be More Than This (Random House/Broadway Books), The One Decision, and The Soft Addiction Solution (Penguin/Tarcher) to share her proven personal transformation methodologies, and the release of her new book Transformed! co-authored with Dr. Bob Wright. She is a sought-after speaker and expert in areas like women’s training and development, relationships, leadership, wellness and lifestyle, transformation, career fulfillment, spirituality, and more. She has been a keynote speaker for many events, conferences, and corporations.

In addition, Dr. Judith Wright has appeared as a featured lifestyle expert and coach on ABC’s 20/20, Oprah, Good Morning America, the Today show and hundreds of radio and television shows. Called the “world’s ultimate expert,” her work has appeared in Marie Claire, Fitness Magazine, Health, Better Homes and Gardens, Shape, The New York Daily News, The Chicago Tribune, The Boston Herald, and The San Francisco Chronicle who calls Judith “one of the most sought after self-help gurus” in the country.

A pioneer in the field of human development and social and emotional intelligence, Dr. Judith Wright first rose to national prominence by developing innovative education programs for those with developmental disabilities. Dr. Judith Wright then applied the insights she discovered to the general population, spurring people from all walks of life to have significant success in personal transformation, leadership development, and personal goal fulfillment. She co-founded Wright — a cutting edge coaching and training institute in Chicago— and the Wright Graduate Institute for the Realization of Human Potential, offering Masters and Doctoral degrees.

Dr. Judith Wright's Publications

  • There Must Be MORE Than This

    • Broadway Books
    • 2003
    Authors: Dr. Judith Wright

    We all have an innate desire to lead emotionally fulfilling lives, but too often we are tripped up by our dependence on seemingly harmless habits--shopping, watching TV, gossiping, surfing the Net--routines that can insulate us from our thoughts and feelings, and from other people. These seemingly harmless habits are soft addictions, and they form a powerful net that traps us, preventing us from living more consciously or fully.

    Soft addictions satisfy surface needs and ignore deeper needs. They numb feelings and help us avoid anxieties, substituting a superficial high or sense of genuine purpose and fulfillment. In a very real sense, we become hooked on these habitual behaviors. But unlike drugs or alcohol, our soft addictions arrive in the guise of a "normal," socially acceptable activity.

    There Must Be More Than This helps identify the deeper needs behind soft addictions. Educator and life coach Judith Wright has created a complete, eight-step program that has been used by hundreds of her students to overcome soft addictions and open up their lived to a greater life purpose and happiness. There Must Be More Than This shares her methods, along with real-life inspirational stories of success.

    Her promis is a life in which every day advances your goals and your vision; in which you live more purposefully and feel more intensely; in which you stop asking the small questions and start asking the big ones; in which your days are structured around meaningful activities and your soft addictions fall away.

  • The Soft Addiction Solution: Break Free of the Seemingly Harmless Habits That Keep You from the Life You Want

    • Penguin Group
    • 2006
    Authors: Dr. Judith Wright

    Do you wish you had more time, money, or energy? Would you like to carve more purpose and meaning out of your crowded days? The answer is simple: cut out the "soft addictions." As Judith Wright reveals in this revised and expanded edition of her classic self-help book There Must Be More Than This, many of us are trapped by seemingly harmless and social sanctioned habits such as shopping, watching TV, and surfing the Internet. These seductive habits not only rob us of our time and money, but also numb our feelings, zap our energy, and cloud our clarity of mind. Offering only a superficial high that works to mask our deeper desires, they prevent us from achieving our goals, our ambitions, and our dreams.

    According to Wright, we are easily hooked because, unlike drugs or alcohol, soft addictions are perceived as "normal" or even desirable behavior, when in fact they keep us from reaping all of life's benefits. In this groundbreaking book, she draws on her vast experience in the world of personal and professional development to provide a unique, practical, and inspirit program that will help readers escape the trivial routines that fill their days and help them discover more passion, love, commitment, and meaning in their lives.

  • The One Decision: Make The Single Choice That Will Lead You To A Life of MORE

    • Penguin Group
    • 2007
    Authors: Dr. Judith Wright

    The exciting, powerful, and inspiring life you were born to live is only One Decision away. Judith Wright, lifestyles expert and cofounder of the Wright Institute, inspires us by this simple yet powerful truth that has transformed her life and the lives of thousands of others.

    A decision has power. We can want, wish, and hope for a life that brings us more satisfaction, fulfillment, time, money, energy, and meaning. But according to Judith Wright, even with all our external successes and goals achieved, we will not live the life we were born to live until we make our One Decision.

    This revolutionary books demonstrates how to make and live a One Decision and how to allow this simple profound choice to become the guiding force in everything you do. With vivid insight, divine inspiration, real-life stories, and a "30 Days to Your One Decision" program, Judith show you how you can begin living your One Decision, you life of greatness, today.

  • Transformed! The Science of Spectacular Living

    • Turner Publishing Company
    Authors: Dr. Judith Wright, Dr. Robert (Bob) Wright

    A groundbreaking guide to making extraordinary improvements in life, featuring innovative research from leadership experts Dr. Judith Wright and Dr. Bob Wright.

    In this radical new book Transformed!, bestselling authors Dr. Judith Wright and Dr. Bob Wright explore how individuals can achieve lifelong transformation--in their careers, their relationships, and their lives. Transformed! integrates cutting-edge findings across many fields with groundbreaking research about the highest performing students at the Wright's internationally recognized leadership training institute, who didn't just make exceptional improvements in their lives but took remarkable leaps forward. This study, combines with over 20 years of feedback and program development, is crafted into an effective and compelling six-phase process for growth and transformation, the heart of which is a person's deepest yearning and the pathway to finding it.

    Providing a powerful breakthrough to understanding why most self-help and personal development programs fall short, Transformed! profiles individuals who have attained deep fulfillment in every aspect of life and illustrates how anyone, by following this proven process, can achieve lasting, revolutionary transformation.
