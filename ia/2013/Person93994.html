Mike Bombon's Summary

I guess it all started when my father, an IT engineer, taught me how to play the Atari 2600 when I was 4 years old. A little later my mother, a graphic designer, taught me Photoshop and Illustrator in the 8th grade.

I'm currently a visual designer at Cisco, creating user interfaces for collaborative endpoints. I bring a handful of experience building mobile entertainment apps, and am learning all about collaboration platforms.

My main mission is innovation.
