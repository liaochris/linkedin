Danielle Primiceri's Summary

Born and raised in New York City, my love for art and design started in the third grade, when I won an award for the best colored map of the United States. Fast forward thirteen years, I’m graduating from FIT with a BFA in Graphic Design.

Immediately after graduating college, I started working as a print designer for a small publisher designing children's books. Wanting to broaden my horizons and gain more experience, I left publishing after three years to start designing for the web.

My approach in my 17 years of professional experience has always been to take each new assignment with the same idea in mind: It should be the best work I've ever done and I should have fun doing it. The enjoyment I get comes from working through the challenges of the whole design process and arriving at a solution that makes the client – as well as myself – proud.

Specialties: Creative Direction, Web Design, Mobile Design, App Design, Print Design, Producing (Film)
