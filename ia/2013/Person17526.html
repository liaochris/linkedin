Brian Lambert, PhD's Summary

Brian helps sales, marketing, and product teams design, implement, and optimize initiatives that reduce the friction caused by changing selling strategies. He specializes in developing techniques to help knowledge workers unlearn old habits while creating programs to develop and then reinforce new behaviors required to efficiently and effectively execute changing business strategies.
