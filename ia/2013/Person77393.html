Kevin Stokes' Summary

I am currently responsible for approximately 350 engineers in 4 countries. Our team designs, implements and validates mass transit solutions globally, ensuring the safety and integrity of our solutions.

Prior to this position, I was General Manager of an electronics contract manufacturing facility with annual sales of approximately $150M. I responsible for all facets of this organization, including: finance, engineering, production, materials, etc.

Recently, I lead all technical shared services for a $750M Electronics Manufacturing company; Engineering (Manufacturing, Design and Tester development), Systems/IT, Global Supply Chain, Quote, Continuous Improvement (Lean/Six Sigma) and Quality & Reliability.

I have started multiple Reliability groups in my career. Typically, these start ups include establishing a Reliability Engineering team to act as consultants and help design teams with the different stages of their New Product Introduction process (design review, FMEA's, etc.). Additionally, typically it includes the creation of an accelerated test lab to test OEM equipment to look for design flaws and develop the reliability of the product. These tests include: vibration, thermal cycling, HALT, HASS, EMI/EMC, etc.

Additionally, I am a certified Black Belt at two of the companies I have worked at (including Kimball Electronics). I have a strong working knowledge of the Six Sigma tools as well as other statistical analysis tools, including: SPC, DOE, ANOVA, GR&R, Weibull analysis, Life Data analysis, Reliability predictions, etc.
