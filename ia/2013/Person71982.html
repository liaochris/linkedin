Milan Karasek's Summary

Milan works as a Senior Researcher with Moravia IT a.s., his focus is on XML processing, XML standards development (founding member of OASIS XLIFF TC). Milan graduated in 1997 as Master of Computer Science (Ing.) from the Brno University of Technology (VUT Brno), Department of Computer Science. His technical skills comprise programming in .NET framework, VBA, Java Script, ASP (.NET), HTML 4 and 5, XML.

Milan programmed several custom applications for Localization processes that are in production use in Moravia. Milan has been also extensively working on commercial and open source Machine Translation (MT) platforms evaluations and production rollouts. His major expertise in the field of TM (Translation Memory) and MT is edit distance measurement and other automated metrics for MT evaluation.
