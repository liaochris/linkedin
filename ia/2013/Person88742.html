Small Tree Communications

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Networking industry

May 2007 – April 2013 (6 years)

Small Tree is a solutions research and development company focused on developing and engineering solutions for the Video and Media Production Industry using Apple Hardware, and Ethernet networks for the basis of their network backbones. Small Tree understands Unix based systems, as well as Windows and Linux environments.

Small Tree's core focuses in the industry are Device Driver Engineering, Hardware Solutions Supply, Brand Integrations, and Full Technology Support for it's product sold to End Users and it's Partner Channel.

Small Tree offers a wide array of solutions being utilized to achieve Mac OS X Ethernet connectivity. From Ethernet Standard solutions supporting 1GbE, 10GbE, and other converging networks, to support for Low Latency Video Networking, to connecting with Apple Thunderbolt offering Intel's best in class chipset support under Mac OS X.

In addition to standard type of network connectivity, Small Tree is also a very well established industry specialist offering Real Time Video Networked Shared Storage solutions that bring the users best in class quality, performance, and long term support of their Video and Media Production Team environments.

Small Tree prides itself on having the proper knowledge and experience to understand in detail what is required of Real Time solutions to support the different types of I/O moving across the network and back and forth over Server / Storage solutions.

Small Tree knows what it means to create a standard Ethernet network and the difference in the ways to approach deployments and customize a shared network to support and sustain the the end users requirements with both software and hardware from end to end.
