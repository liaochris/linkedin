Celina Shands Gradijan's Projects

  • Workforce Leadership Council

    • March 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Celina Shands Gradijan, Josh Copus, ron painter, Tim Aldinger, Tom Kavanagh, Frank Gradijan, CLSSBB

    The Workforce Leadership Council is a learning exchange of workforce investment boards interested in growing their capacity for leadership. The NAWB Leadership Assessment provides the starting point for boards to better understand organizational strengths and areas of opportunity. With more than 200 responses analyzed to date, the survey results are becoming a valuable tool for NAWB to determine commonalities in developing leadership capabilities, and provide participants a way to compare their results with a growing pool of national data. Participating boards will learn from colleagues through peer-to-peer discussions, webinars from leaders in the field, and technical assistance from subject matter experts. Boards have used the results to inform their strategic planning processes, improve operations and start new initiatives in their region. Join us in growing the leadership capacity of the system!

  • Maryland Statewide Workforce Brand Strategy

    • August 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Celina Shands Gradijan, Ellen Flowers-Fields, Susan Kaliush

    This collaborative process between state and local workforce stakeholders in Maryland resulted in a statewide brand called Maryland Jobs Now, and an ongoing storytelling website to showcase the value and success of the workforce investment system.

  • One In Three Campaign

    • September 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Celina Shands Gradijan, Kimberly Alleyne

    The Association of Enterprise Opportunity (AEO) is a national nonprofit and business trade association that represents the U.S. microbusiness industry. Its One in Three Campaign has the goals of reaching one million entrepreneurs with new capital and services to create 500,000 jobs within the next three to five years. FCM developed the campaign website, fact sheet and video to support the national public-education effort which is a “sister campaign” to the National Assn. of Workforce Board's Just Add One Initiative.

Celina Shands Gradijan's Publications

  • Website Benchmark Study for Nonprofit Workforce Organizations

    • Full Capacity Marketing
    • March 12, 2012
    Authors: Celina Shands Gradijan

    FCM launched the first-ever Workforce Website Benchmark Study to examine how workforce organizations utilize the web for outreach to job seekers, businesses, youth and key stakeholders. FCM worked with more than 37 workforce organizations who allowed FCM access to their website statistics and completed an extensive survey about their online publishing behaviors.

  • National Economic Impact Scorecard Initiative

    • Full Capacity Marketing
    • March 12, 2012
    Authors: Celina Shands Gradijan

    FCM assisted the National Association of Workforce Boards (NAWB) and its partners in designing a national workforce scorecard model that reports the benefits and costs of Workforce Investment Act (WIA) programs, along with the unique economic impacts of Workforce Investment Board (WIB) operations on regional income and jobs.
