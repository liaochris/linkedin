Larry Rowan's Summary

Larry Rowan is the Broker/Manager for the Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage Schaumburg office which services Cook, DuPage, Kane and McHenry counties. Larry’s conscientious, experienced and innovative approach to real estate has made him a leader in the industry.

Throughout his 30+ years in real estate Larry has experienced many changes to the business first hand. He owned a successful real estate company, is very familiar with changing markets. Overcoming today's challenges is one of Larry's strengths and he provides results oriented solutions to his clients and agents pressing needs.

Larry’s real estate experience includes teaching licensing on the staff of Harper College and Coldwell Banker School of Real Estate. Larry has shared his leadership skills as a Director of the Realtor Association of Northwest Chicagoland (formerly Northwest Association of Realtors) and has served on the Professional Standards committee and is the Chairman of the Realtor Lawyer Committee.

Larry holds membership in the Realtor Association of Northwest Chicagoland, Mainstreet Association of Realtors and Northshore Barrington Association of Realtors.

Larry joined Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage in 1982, has managed 5 offices for Coldwell Banker and in past years has been recognized by earning the Coldwell Banker International President’s Council award five years in a row which is given to managers in the top 1% of Coldwell Banker offices internationally.

Larry has a deep sense of community and patriotism which stems from his service in Viet Nam, serving on the presidential commissioned Viet Nam Veteran Leadership Commission and volunteers for Willow Creek Community Church and the Salvation Army.

Whether buying, selling or looking for a career in real estate Larry will provide you with Conscientious, Experienced, Innovative leadership to help you succeed in all your endeavors.

Larry J. Rowan, a Real Estate Broker licensed in Illinois began his career in the Chicago area in 1975.

Larry owned and operated ERA Executive Realty in Buffalo Grove until 1982, when he joined a fast growing company, Coldwell Banker. Coldwell Banker entered the Chicago marketplace during a deep real estate recession when the majority of real estate offices were either downsizing or going out of business. In spite of the economy, Coldwell Banker grew and built a strong group of of 80 offices in Chicagoland and remains the #1 Real Estate Company.

Larry’s assignments with Coldwell Banker have included building and expanding various branch offices, recruiting and increasing office productivity and market share.

Larry is the the Managing Broker of the Coldwell Banker Schaumburg Office of 106 Top Producing agents and was honored as a recipient of the National President’s Council Manager Award 5 years in a row.
Additionally, Larry holds an Illinois Real Estate License Instructor license and has taught Real Estate at Harper College and for Coldwell Banker School of Real Estate. Larry also holds a Continuing Education License and is certified to teach the Illinois Broker Management courses.

Larry uses his experience in having been trained in the Brian Buffini Coaching program to help his agents move their production to a higher level.

The use of new technologies in real estate is one of Larry’s passions and has been asked to be a pilot office of the newly developed Homebase tm transaction management system.
