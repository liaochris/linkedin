Simon Cooke's Summary

My goal:
* Make life more fun for everyone.
* Work hard, doing fun things.
* Work with people I trust, enabling them to go further and do better, more awesome things.

I bring a small can of spray-on infectious enthusiasm wherever I go. I can be quite polarizing, but I don't have a malicious bone in my body. I seem like I'm too nice, but really I'm just friendly, courteous and polite. There is a difference. But give me the resources, and I'll happily bring the people along for the ride.

I've been a freelance journalist, graphic designer, engineer and more. Most recently, I've been getting involved more on the business side of the video game industry. Right now, I'm part of Microsoft's Advanced Technology Group - we make games run faster and better, and help you to make the best games you possibly can for our platforms.

What do I want to do right now?

I'm very happy where I am, but some things that might sway me:
* Directing feature films.
* Writing position on a sci-fi TV show.
* Writing screenplays for movies.
* Producing feature films.
* Producing video games.
* Designing video games.
* Running a video game publisher.
* Running a video game studio.

I'm interested in the business side of video games (both publishing and development), the creative side, and the engineering side. My ideal position would be a mix of all of the above. (That's why I'm looking more for capital these days to start a venture, or for well-funded ground-floor opportunities).
