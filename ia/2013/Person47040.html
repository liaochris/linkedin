Gary Barber's Summary

Gary is a multi-talented independent (User) Experience consultant, with a flare for visual and interactive design, having gained a large body of experience in the information industry since 1990.

His focus is in providing a greater ROI for businesses while providing an enhanced customer experience by being highly focused on research, analysis and interaction design.

Gary's personal goal is to make the world easier to use for all the interfaces and services we encounter.

He often works with government, education, NGOs, not-for-profits, logistical, startups on short to long-term contracts.

Gary has a wealth of experience in the application of best practices, requirements gathering, process documentation, holistic usability and inclusive accessibility with the application of business analysis and UX techniques within a front end development framework for all of his projects.
