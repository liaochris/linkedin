Catherine Cleare's Summary

Your home, your style, your story.
Catherine Cleare is an interior designer with a singular focus: Translating your aesthetic into functional, beautiful design reality, one gorgeous detail at a time. Experience and artistry inform her abilities, but it is her commitment to honoring your goals that sets her apart.
Designing your home should be an exhilarating experience. Catherine Cleare’s approach incorporates your vision, your budget and your time frame. She supports you every step of the way, allowing you to focus on the fun part: Watching your house transform into your home.

Interests:

Artistic inspiration is derived from life experience. Whether I’m shopping for rare antiques or taking in the Connecticut coastline from my perch on the back of my husband’s Harley Davidson, I’m constantly storing away moments of beauty that I can bring to my work. Like people, every home is unique. And each has a story to tell. Few things give me more satisfaction than illustrating these stories, detail by personalized detail.
