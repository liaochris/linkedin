Sarah Hilton's Summary

Do you consider yourself a speaker? Well think, do you speak to your co workers, your managers, do you make presentations to audiences of 1 or 1000, and finally do you sell your product or service by speaking about it? Absolutely, if you are in business you are in speaking. Through the World Class Speaking Program you will pick up the very tools that will take you to knew success just by speaking. As a World Class Speaking Coach, I will coach, train and speak on Business Speaking Skills to help you develop your speaking success.

How did I get here, I started and continue to speak out myself on Mental Health in the Workplace. My passion to stop silence, stigma and suicide. How do I do this? I train, speak and coach businesses on how to stop the silence. Through a customized program your company can reduce absenteeism, decrease WSIB claims, increase productivity and ultimately bring back the drive that all employees loose after built up workplace stress. Speak out on mental health, speak for success.
