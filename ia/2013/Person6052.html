Amy Hile's Summary

I have owned and managed several very successful design/build companies and am looking forward to furthering my career as an award-winning interior designer. Projects that I have spearheaded include everything from simple color changes to working directly with the client from the beginning stages of designing the floor plans for their homes. Budgets for these projects can vary greatly, ranging from a few thousand dollars to multi-million dollar homes.

I am looking to enhance an existing design team and/or take on the role as team-leader, as my experience and knowledge will allow, and am actively seeking full time employment.

Specialties: I am experienced in reading full sets of blueprints and am skilled at conversing with contractors, subcontractors, and clients. I am as comfortable on the jobsite as I am at the drafting table or workroom. I consider myself to be an expert at color choices and room layout, as well as space planning.

I'm voracious writer and have written a 250 page manuscript (memoir), and have received amazing reviews from readers. I am currently shopping for a publisher.
