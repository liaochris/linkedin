Diego Beristain's Summary

Born in San Diego CA, Graphic Designer and Brand Consultant with more than 7 years of experience in Branding, Marketing and Strategic Design. He has worked closely with various agencies and key customers to develop successful brands in the market; generating desire, providing trust and creating value. Has been worked on the creation, positioning and strategic design of various brands for some of the TOP companies of consumer products, such as Nestle, Dannon, Pepsi-Co, Unilever, P&G, Kraft Foods, Henkel & Sigma Alimentos.

He has served as a speaker, lecturer and teacher on various topics of the discipline, such as Brand Identity, Packaging Design and Consumer Insights in severe educational institutions and business organizations in Mexico, achieving remarkable results.

Specialties: His specialties include:

• Audits and branding opportunities.
• Consumer insights, brand research, segmentation strategy.
• Brand revitalization, positioning, concepting.
• Portfolio management.
• Brand architecture strategy.
• Innovation programs development.
• Brand implementation, delivery, alignment.
• Internal and external brand experience.
• New products, extensions and brand creation.
• Strategic packaging design.
• Naming, verbal branding.
