    Editorial:

    Hello everyone!

    As President for Latino Styles Magazine, I am very happy to see how this new year has brought new changes to the magazine. A new and exiting page with vibrant colors. A new "Tag" line. A new challenge...not only to show the Latino community around us... but all around the Latino Places in the world! How do we do that? Well, let's just say that we are a "BIG" family. The Latino culture is embraced to the love and unity of families and close friends who we "adopt" in our hearts. We Struggle together, we Celebrate together, and we Succeed-TOGETHER!



    Latino Styles strives to bring to our readers the images and articles of those who deserve to be recognized for their care and time spent in our community, making progress and helping others succeed as well. Those who don't expect nothing more than to see a change for the better future for our children, our teenagers, our parents and our seniors.



    Let us learn, grow and maintain to teach our Latin tradition, our cultural history and how proud we are of our birthplace! We welcome our friends to join us while bringing the best of both worlds: Ours and yours! Let's begin with ourselves! Let's make a change! Let's do it LATINO STYLE!



    -- Luz Méndez- President for Latino Styles Magazine
