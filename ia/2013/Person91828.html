Mish Handwerker's Summary

I have been training in the martial arts since 1990 with a major focus on pressure points under the Dillman Method of Pressure Point Fighting. In addition I have also studied Modern Arnis, Kenjutsu, Small Circle Jujitsu™ and a little bit of several other arts. I was able to incorporate this experience into the security industry which I have been in full time since the beginning of 2004 working in several different aspects of the industry including that of bar security.

I teach my own flavor of martial art which is a culmination of everything that I have learned from the many instructors I have studied with. I also teach what I have learned from my martial arts / security training and experience along with my business partner Bill Collins through our company Matador Academy. We provide online courses as well as live certification seminars directed toward bar security (bouncers).

As Matador Academy, we believe in turning the idea of the thug bouncer into the Bar Security Professional. This can be achieved through continual training in good customer service and effective hands on techniques.
