Dr. Liz Alexander's Publications

  • #Thought Leadership Tweet: 140 Prompts for Designing and Executing an Effective Thought Leadership Campaign

    • United States and India
    • October 20, 2012
    Authors: Dr. Liz Alexander, Craig Badings

    Winner of 2013 WPP Atticus Award (category: Public Relations and Public Affairs). This book covers all the questions aspiring organizational thought leaders needs to ask themselves before embarking on a campaign to differentiate themselves and enhance their value to clients and customers. Includes a five-step thought leadership blueprint for successfully executing your unique "white space" point of view.

  • Transform Tomorrow: Awakening the Super Saver in Pursuit of Retirement Readiness

    • John Wiley & Sons
    • January 9, 2012
    Authors: Dr. Liz Alexander, Stig Nybo, AIF

    Transform Tomorrow investigates why so many Americans are at risk of out-living their savings. The authors draw inspiration from successful behavior change campaigns to identify the drivers of change—context and beliefs—and how they can be successfully employed to boost retirement savings rates. While the retirement savings industry increasingly embraces the contextual drivers of behavior, very little is being done to shape our beliefs to start saving smarter and sooner. The book suggests a retirement readiness campaign to inspire and enlist the support of individuals, employers, industry, government, and the media.
