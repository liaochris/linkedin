Kathleen Stimmel's Summary

Adapting to Constant Change
With new ideas continually being introduced and applications constantly being upgraded, it’s no easy task to keep up-to-date with all the new trends and applications in design and marketing.

As a skilled web and graphic designer my passion has always been in pursuit of continuing education. I love to challenge my skill set and receive educational training in my ongoing commitment to be the best creative designer you will ever work with.

Diverse Clientele
With more than 20 years of experience in conceptual design and development, I have had the privilege of working with a diverse group of clients that includes Hewlett Packard, Kenworth, Blue Cross of Washington and Alaska and Kenmore Air, to name a few. I consider this the best of circumstances as I have learned to create websites and marketing materials artistically different but with the same goal in mind—to help your business succeed!

Memorable Branding
My commitment is to help small to large businesses stand out and gain instant brand recognition through detailed, creative design and persuasive content. I take the time to listen to a client’s goals and combine my skills and expertise to create a design as unique as my clients.

Ready to tell the world about your business? Email kathleen@fittingimages.com to learn more.

Freelance Graphic Designer

Floathe Johnson Advertising
