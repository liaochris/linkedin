Leo Grinsteiner, CSP's Experience

Sr. Safety Manager SSHO Level-6

Clark Construction

Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Construction industry

November 2011 – Present (1 year 4 months) Camp Pendleton California

Leading the high-profile safety efforts for Clark-McCarthy a Joint Venture (CMJV) at the new $500mm Naval Hospital Replacement Project at Camp Pendleton Ca. Providing direction, instruction and energy into the heavy demands of EM385 and NavFac’s safety ROICCs to bring smart compliance and safety to over 700 tradesman on the project daily. Point man for the joint venture team's integrated safety program, leading the weekly safety committee, daily stand-ups, subcontractor meetings and the monthly all-hands events speaking directly to all workers on site. Performing daily field presence and safety audits, enforcement and coaching, problem solving, regulation decisions, risk management and compliance to EM385. Enhanced safety orientation process with visual guide books and custom video. Achieving a daily effective response to the needs of the joint venture project managers and superintendents, subcontractors and Navy ROICCs, engineers and officers to allow $1mm of work in place each day without any lost time injuries and recordable rate 1/3 the national average. Direct the on-site activities of two full-time CMJV safety managers and two full-time paramedics.
