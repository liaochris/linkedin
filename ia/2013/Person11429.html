Jeff Wade's Summary

Back Stage Technologies - A Special Effects Company established in 1997, was founded by Jeff Wade, President of the Orlando operation. Formerly a Senior Special Effects Designer for Walt Disney Imagineering, as well as such notable show producers in the Themed Entertainment industry and a variety of Major Road shows. BST's Special Effects knowledge and experience of theme park, movie special effects and theatrical productions across the globe, stemming from North America, Europe, and Asia has brought a wealth of experience from design, fabrication, integration, and installation.
Already a Special FX force around the world, BST, based in Winter Garden, Florida , became a truly International company in 2001 with the formation of BSTE, a European division located in Wiltshire, England . To head up the European operation Jeff brought onboard as his co-director, John Coller who brought with him an extensive knowledge of pyrotechnics to add to the formidable catalogue of services already offered by BST. Jeff will be heading up the conceptual design team for the project and reviewing all portions of the project processes.
