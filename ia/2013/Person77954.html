Lachlan Sloan's Summary

Lachlan began his career in technology, starting his own dotcom business straight out of university. This experience at such a young age meant that he gained a solid understanding of sales and operations at an early point in his career, before taking these skills and knowledge to a boutique technology recruitment firm in his home town of Auckland, New Zealand.

Always interested in Asia, he joined ConnectedGroup in 2001, an IT recruitment firm based in Hong Kong. He was quickly promoted to head of the firm’s technology division. He later participated in a joint management buyout of the firm and this led to a new role as business development manager for the firm’s human resources outsourcing solutions business.

In 2003, Lachlan joined Hong Kong recruitment firm Wall Street Associates serving first as Chief Operating Officer and then as Chief Executive Officer. This firm was acquired by Talent2 in June 2005, after which Lachlan continued to manage the business in his capacity as Chief Executive Officer for another two years. In July 2007 Lachlan then moved into the Managed Services business unit of Talent2 Asia.

In 2008 he decided to take his skills in Recruitment, RPO, Management and Solution Selling and work in private equity with the IRI Group where he was Managing Director of Group Operations where he helped to establish 7 recruitment brands as well as work on M&A deals with ConnectedGroup and Inspire Search & Selection. Lachlan has since moved into a Non Executive Director role on the IRI Board.

In 2009 Lachlan was one of the founding members of Compass Offices working the majority of his time on this business whilst continuing to support IRI Group. In January 2012 Lachlan moved full time to Compass Offices initially as the Chief Operating Officer and now as a Board Director and Vice President of Corporate Development.
