Holly Hoffman's Summary

As a professional, Holly has dedicated the past six years to expanding her knowledge base and gaining experience in multiple areas in the media and marketing fields.

She is the cofounder and chief inspiration officer of Neovia Solutions, an interactive agency known for its teaching component. After graduating from Rollins College with honors in philosophy and art (read: the right-and-left brain degree), she moved to Corpus Christi, TX to be near her family & to be a part of a community on the tipping point, in 2004.

Holly brings nearly 10 years of media experience to Neovia, ranging from radio to print to online, as well as three years of media marketing experience. Blessed with a knack for creativity and strategic logic in one brain, she has contributed her social media marketing skills to a range of industries, including personal services, consulting and media, and will speak on social media to whoever will listen, including the Texas Economic Development Council, Texas Association of Continuing Education, the Texas Certified Public Manager program, and the Corpus Christi Hispanic Chamber. She has even been invited by the Swedish government to speak to European Union prime ministers about emerging technologies and e-government.

She is the founder of Social Media Club Corpus Christi and is an active participant in regional social media goings-on, including SXSW Interactive conferences. She was named a 2009 40 Under 40 recipient by the Corpus Christi Chamber of Commerce. She is an avid blogger and has been featured in the Wall Street Journal.

Holly's greatest strengths include a passion for continuous learning, relentless curiosity, integrity and an eye for detail.
