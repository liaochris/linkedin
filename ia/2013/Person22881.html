Chip Herr's Summary

Chip Herr works as a Professional Race Driver the last 13 years.
Chip has raced for Mazda, Acura, BMW, Ford, Toyota, Lamborghini, and VW in the World Challenge Touring Car series and he also drove for DP7 Racing in a Lamborghini Gallardo GTR, Chevrolet Corvette ZO6 in World Challenge GT. Herr has raced in many different series and won many races and championships over the last 13 years. Chip also had done TV hosting on SportsCar Revolution on SPEED (2004-2007) and raced in The Grand Am Series for more experience in endurance racing. Chip has had the opportunity to race in the Domincan Republic in the DTS class. in his EX World Challenge car. Chip was born the same night his mother was drag racing and on her last run to win the ladies category, she had pains and till this day we think Chip had his first victory celebration before he even was on this earth! Family is very supportive and that means a lot when you are on the road. Chip started in Spec RX7 to learn momentum and ended up driving for Pro teams in only 3 short years and teaming up with people he has always looked up to. Chip knows how tough it is to continue a professional driving career but racing has been a great career choice and he is determined to win many more races and create new contacts during his journey!
