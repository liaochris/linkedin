Cynthia Cummins' Summary

I’ve been happily serving sellers and buyers in San Francisco since 1987. A McGuire Partner and a perennial Top Producer, I list and sell homes, condos and small residential buildings throughout the city.

My commitment to my sellers and buyers has resulted in a satisfied clientele who call on me any time they need help with real estate. I always put my customers’ needs and wishes before my own. I listen attentively, answer truthfully and bring my experience and intuition to every interaction.

Yes, throughout my career I’ve been one of the top agents in San Francisco, but that tells only part of my story. Asked to speak about my attributes, my clients will tell you of the rewards they’ve reaped because of my counsel and skill set. But they will also speak highly of my trustworthiness, kindness and affability. Their good opinion is what I value most in my busy career.

I love my job!
