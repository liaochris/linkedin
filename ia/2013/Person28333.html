Curtis Marolt's Summary

Curtis Marolt is an experienced composer/producer and a emerging star in the music industry. His work with network and cable television, feature film, mobile, interactive, games, and casino gaming have earned him a great deal of recognition from Disney/Pixar, Dreamworks, NBC, CBS, AMC, MTV, Fox, CW, TLC, ABC Family, Sesame Street, McDonald's, Ubisoft, Dimension Films, Oxygen, Nick and many others. He is currently senior composer with Incredible Technologies, adjunct faculty with Columbia College, a contributing composer for many successful music libraries, as well as a freelance professional.

His work with Disney/Pixar has included scoring original music for interactive titles such as: Wall-E, Toy Story, The Incredibles, Cars, Monsters Inc., The Little Einsteins, and Cinderella just to name a few. The products on which he has worked have kept children entertained while the story comes alive as well as enhancing reading skills

As a producer, Curtis has taken his “hook driven” chops to the pop arena for artists Sarah Leichtenberg, MId Coast All Stars, Keith Masters, Sherrie Adams, David James Jr., and many others. With pop hits featured on all of the major TV networks, national ad campaigns and most cable networks, Curtis is sought after for his ability to generate Pop classics in any genre. Visit the curtismarolt.com to experience some of his tracks.

In addition, Curtis has taken his memorable melodies to the online gaming arena. His compositions are the foundation for the unique environments in McDonald's "McWorld". Visit happymeal.com and check it out!

Curtis currently lives and works in and around the great city of Chicago, IL
