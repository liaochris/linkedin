1) Click away, you've heard it all before. I know what it’s like being an adviser and being bombarded with messages with ulterior motives. Classify my message in the same category and justify it with thoughts like ‘my clients aren’t on Social Media.’ ‘My partners will never go for it.’ ‘The leadership on my company won’t let me.’ ‘The compliance issues are too great.’ Or the best of all ‘I don’t have time.’

But that is just the start, it will no longer be sufficient to just be vanilla as an adviser. We are going to create a new world where the spotlight shines on Advisers of Influence where those advisers who have a message, who have commitment and passion can reach a vast audience of ideologically matched ‘perfect clients’.

As confronting as it may be, we will also make sure that those advisers and companies that have made choice 1 no longer have the means to remain in the business of advising clients. This is my vision, passion and commitment, are you with me? Are you ready to become Social Advisers or run Social Businesses in support of those who are?
