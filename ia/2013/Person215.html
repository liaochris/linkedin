The Mission of CNCF is to

1. Encourage and support research to reveal causes and discover effective treatments of neuroblastoma.
2. Provide services to children with neuroblastoma and their families.
3. Educate the public and healthcare providers about the disease.
4. To provide a forum for patients and family members to share information concerning medical treatment, palliative care, hospice services, bereavement groups and emotional support.
5. To foster, promote, support, develop and operate such other educational programs, entities and activities which will support those purposes.

It is the belief of the Board that funding of research projects at hospitals and institutions across the country that specialize in neuroblastoma and other solid tumor cancers will best serve the purposes of our foundation in hopes of finding a cure.
