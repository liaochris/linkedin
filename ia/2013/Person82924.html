Lisa Synalovski's Summary

L. Synalovski C.B.C ., Inc. is a Building Contractor Firm that started with one woman’s passion for the environment.


Lisa Synalovski founded our company with a dream of constructing sustainable or “green” homes for families that would be healthier to live in and cause less damage to the environment. From the design phase we look at the house as a whole building envelope and create a plan that incorporates significant energy and water efficiency as well as healthy indoor air quality for our home buyers. Our team of building sub contractors share our passion for “green” sustainable construction. They utilize Best Practices relating to their area of expertise throughout the project which ensures compliance with our Certified Green Standards. We partner with Florida Power and Light as an FPL Buildsmart Builder affirming that our homes will be at least 30% more energy efficient than the current Building Code requires. In fact our Prototype "Green Dream Home" is 45% more energy efficient than the National Energy code. Our commitment to the environment is assured by each home being Certified by Energy Star as well as The NAHB National Association of Home Builders and The FGBC Florida Green Building Coalition.
