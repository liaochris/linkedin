David Petersen's Experience

Omaha SharePoint User Group

January 2012 – Present (2 years) Greater Omaha Area

In 2012, Corey Erkes and I decided to revive the Omaha SharePoint Users Group. We began with a hand-full of people and have grown to an average of 55 people attending a presentation and lunch every month. We hold frequent SharePints at a local pub for socializing and this year we are hosting Omaha's first SharePoint Saturday. Enabling people to collaborate and share knowledge is one of my passions and the SharePoint Users Group is one way I can live out my passion. We meet for lunch on the first Thursday of each month at the Microsoft office. Please register at http://omahamtg.com to receive our communications.
