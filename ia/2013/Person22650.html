Cheryl Burgess' Summary

Cheryl Burgess, CEO and CMO - Blue Focus Marketing® (@BlueFocus), author, The Social Employee (McGraw-Hill summer 2013) is a creative and marketing technologist with expertise in B2B marketing, social business and social media. She blogs at Blue Focus Marketing's blog. Also, she is an expert blogger for AT&T Networking Exchange on social media. She was awarded Wharton Future of Advertising's MVP and praised as a "brilliant strategic thinker in the social media space". She was awarded a Huffington Post "Passionista" for "great business expertise and timeless blog posts". She was listed as "Top 100 Business, Leadership and Technology Twitter Accounts You Must Follow" on Huffington Post. She was featured in Fast Company for "Pitching & Storytelling in the Digital World". Cheryl was a speaker on "Expanding Your Social Influence" at AT&T's Networking Leaders Academy Annual Conference. She is the winner of the 2012, 2011 and 2010 Twitter Shorty Award in Marketing [The New York Times hails this as the Oscar of Twitter], named Top 75 Twitter Women, and a 100 Top Marketer on Twitter. She is co-author of Ad Agencies Winning New Business 360, which has sold in 25 + countries worldwide. Also was also a speaker at Morgan Stanley headquarters in NYC on the power of social media during a crisis. Cheryl is a syndicated blogger for B2Community, Uprising, B2B Marketing Zone, B2B Informer and Crowdshifter. She is the co-founder of #Nifty50 Top Twitter Women and #Nifty50 Top Twitter Men. Cheryl holds a BA in Journalism from the University of Pittsburgh.

Specialties: Leadership, social business, strategic planning , implementation, integrated social media websites/content, design and development, advertising new business development, interactive /web marketing, social media, advertising, marketing communications, direct marketing, new product development, marketing research, e-commerce, and SEO.

Cheryl Burgess' Experience

CEO and CMO (@BlueFocus)

Blue Focus Marketing® (@BlueFocus)

July 2010 – Present (3 years 4 months)

Blue Focus Marketing® LLC (@BlueFocus) is a social brand consulting firm focused on strategy development, assessments, customized training and social media strategy. We help build brands from the inside out. We help brands extend offline media into social media. We create a strategic social media marketing blueprint for brands to help connect with social consumers, build relationships with influencers and reach a wider community. Our goal is to help brands engage and deepen relationships and to get their marketing efforts up to social speed. Our community building approach is based on many years of real-world experience in Internet and digital marketing, social media, education, marketing strategy and communications at corporations and ad agencies. The brand's strategic blueprint becomes the basis for executing social media channels with the best potential to meet business goals. We believe that the brand lives in the connections. We help solve one of today's biggest marketing challenges -- the strategic integration of all marketing communications touch points we call: Brand Choreography.
