Kevin French's Summary

Greetings, my name is Kevin Patrick French, and I am a passionate web designer who has an interesting background in illustration, graphic design, and user-centered web development.

I have experience gathering requirements from diverse users, from marketing teams to business owners, working on both customer-facing and internal-facing websites.

I'm currently the Senior UI/UX designer for Adknowledge where I work with the development team to design and implement changes to multiple sites and applications. Soon I will be moving over the our marketing department to take on more customer facing projects.

Previously a Front-end UI/UX designer for Weissman Costumes. I was the link between the Marketing and Development teams coordinating, designing, and implementing all changes to their four e-commerce sites as well as their small portal pages.

Prior to Weissman I worked for Network Solutions as a web designer. I played both designer and developer for each client, and regularly interfaced with Account Reps and marketing teams to refine technical specifications, user requirements and desires. At Network Solutions, I implemented a systematized change management policy to ease upgrades across websites, a process which included developing a standardized framework and leading cross-functional team meetings to update and train employees.

I possess a deep understanding of SEO practices, semantic markup, typography, grid systems, color stories, proof of concept mark ups, wire-frames, and best practices for user experience improvement in every project I endeavor.

While I am self-taught in code, I continue to expand my knowledge with the latest techniques and practices. My ideal work environment would be creative, open, and collaborative. I've been pegged as a perfectionist who is truly passionate about my work, and naturally assumes the role of leader quite often.
