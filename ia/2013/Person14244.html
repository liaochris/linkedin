Bill Dallas' Summary

Real Estate agent in Tampa, Tampa Realtor, Certified Distress Property Expert, Pre Foreclosure Consultant

Bill Dallas, Lead Listing Agent
Bill has been an active Realtor for 6 years, where he has doubled his sales production every year. Bill had a successful career in corporate sales, selling capitol equipment for manufacturing plants, where he exceeded his yearly sales goals. Bill decided to bring his sales experience back to the community of Tampa where he has resided for 20 years. Bill is actively involved with his son's school and sports interests.

As a long time Real Estate investor, Bill has an extensive background in real estate. He added his GRI designation and a graduate of the Realtor Institute in 2006. He is actively coached by the Mike Ferry organization, the premier real estate coaching and consulting company in the country. In 2009 he recieved his Certified Distressed Property Expert designation to help troubled homeowner's out of the current mortgage crisis in the Tampa area.

Bill graduated from Drexel University in 1988 with a Bachelor of Science with a degree in Marketing and Finance. Bill has continued his education by taking seminars in business coaching, which includes Karrass Negotiating Skills, Tom Hopkins Boot Camp, and SPIN selling and is also a certified DISC personality instructor.

"Do you want your home for sale or sold?" is my motto. I work very hard every day to exceed my client's expectations. Put me to work for you and let me get your house SOLD.
