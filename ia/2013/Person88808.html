Matthew Borkowski's Publications

  • Integrating LCA and thermodynamic analysis for sustainability assessment of algal biofuels: Comparison of renewable diesel vs. biodiesel

    • IEEE Proceedings
    • May 2012
    Authors: Matthew Borkowski, Vikas Khanna, George Gregory Zaimes

    Developed a well-to-pump life cycle assesment model for algae-derived Renewable Diesel II fuel and comparing it with the corresponding pathway from algal biomass to biodiesel. Particular attention was paid to primary energy use, fossil energy ratio (FER), and greenhouse gas emissions. While hydrotreating is less than half as energy-intensive as transesterification, the overall life-cycle energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions are found to be nearly equal for renewable diesel and biodiesel. The biofuel production system is only found to be net energy positive for scenarios with reduced burdens from both CO2 sourcing and biomass drying.

  • Life cycle environmental impacts of biofuels and coproducts.

    • Biofuel Technologies: Recent Developments / Springer-Verlag Berlin Heidelberg
    • 2013
    Authors: Matthew Borkowski, George Gregory Zaimes, Vikas Khanna
