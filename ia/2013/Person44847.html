First Choice Books' Summary

First Choice Books now occupies 4500 square feet just north of downtown Victoria. Today we have a staff of 10, in-house digital printing in black & white and colour, design services, and a full bindery operation that can do soft cover, hard cover, book repair and custom work. We are developing our own lines of printed product, including calendars, magnetic lists, and notebooks which reflect local unique culture.
