Andrew Hess' Summary

With over twenty years of experience in print and publishing, both as designer and illustrator, Andrew brings talent, vision, and leadership to all of his work.
• Projects, spanning a wide range of media, include: books, toys, posters, newsletters, t-shirts, game-boards, vans, identity programs, logos, and many more.
• Experienced and proficient with InDesign, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, FreeHand, Quark, PageMaker, Word, Excel, and Constant Contact.
• Hands-on familiar with BBEdit, Illustrator, and PowerPoint.

Specialties

Graphic design, 4-color process, pre-print, page production, image manipulation, production art, project and staff management, budget adherence, team leadership, team player.
