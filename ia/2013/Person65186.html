Jody Weiss' Summary

TCHG Executive Search, with locations in San Antonio and Atlanta, is a uniquely different and fresh approach to Retained and Executive Search and Recruiting,

Committed to exploring new concepts and approaches to executive search, Weiss has developed a methodology for talent selection that accomplishes a significant portion of the hiring manager’s work prior to client presentation in record time (in as little as 65 days from start to "offer") compared to industry standard of six month for a retained "C"-level position.

All the service you would expect from a retained firm within a cost effective business model.

Weiss' diverse professional experience ranges from city govt to prof services at Tatum, LLC.

Specialties: An innovative and fresh approach to Retained / Executive Search and Recruiting. We place leaders and C level execs capable of delivering long term, sustainable results, primarily in the areas of Accounting, Finance, Technology, Sales and Operations.
