Erica Drake's Summary

BRIDGING MAIN STREET TO WALL STREET - LEADERSHIP, ENTREPRENEURIAL TRAINING & INVESTMENT BANKING: Pragmatic leader with over 20 years of executive experience; board member for 3 different companies; founded, designed and managed 3 different companies. In an executive consultant capacity, assisted and advised business clients in their capital-raising quests; help clients procure than $100M in terms sheets in the past 3 years.

AUTHOR: IN THE TRENCHES: FINDING INVESTORS TO FUND YOUR BUSINESS - TheMaverickApproach.com and MaverickEntrepreneurs.com - Veteran entrepreneur and leading business coach, Erica Drake takes you through a two-part proven approach to the process of raising capital for your privately held company. In Part I of her Guide, she teaches you the Maverick Approach -- a step-by-step method of systematically approaching the daunting task of raising capital. These steps include the preparation phase: how to prepare, plan and create perception. Drake then teaches you the intricacies and language of the investment landscape, including how to understand corporate structuring, develop strategies to protect your control, all about debt, equity and bootstrapping capital, and then how to formulate your own corporate valuation. In the final phase of Part I, Drake then teaches you to how to find, meet with and close a deal with investors. In Part II of the Guide, theory turns into action! Drake gives you a total start-up kit for building your battle plan. Complete with electronic templates to build your business plan, financial projections and investor presentation, this section is invaluable to your success. Drake explains in a simple method how to create each element using maverick strategies that attract investor’s attention and close a deal. Drake then gives you a full-length business plan example to teach you how to properly present your total battle plan in a powerful format .

Maverick Management Consultants, LLC

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

July 2006 – April 2013 (6 years 10 months)

Maverick Management Consultants, LLC (“Maverick”) is a successful executive boutique consulting firm that advises and assist entrepreneurs and their companies with the process of raising capital (debt, equity and bootstrapped capital) for their corporate growth. We are not an investment company, broker, dealer or finder. We don’t raise the capital for you. What we do is teach you how to do it yourself – the right way! Using a proven method, Maverick becomes an extension of its client’s executive management team, coaching and guiding them through the entire capital-raising process required to find, present to and close investors. Maverick has a very simple philosophy with its clients: If you want to close funding for your company, then do it right – or don’t do it at all!

THEMAVERICKAPPROACH.COM : Maverick teach entrepreneurs how to prepare, plan and create perception, and then prospect, pitch, present and persevere to a successful close. Along with these 7 steps, Maverick teaches its clients to truly understand the investment community, how to communicate and set-up win/win situations that close investors. Additionally, Maverick teaches its clients to strategically prepare their battle plan to protect their control, develop a strong but clean corporate structure that attracts investors, and creates the ‘sweet spots’ that closes deals. Maverick’s proven method is based upon real-life practical experiences of entrepreneurs who have successfully closed investors at all stages of development. Over the past 18 years, Maverick has developed an enviable worldwide network of hundreds of investor contacts (including: Institutional, Private Equity, Venture Capital and Angel Investors). These investors are looking for the right companies to work with, fund and grow – but their standards are high!
