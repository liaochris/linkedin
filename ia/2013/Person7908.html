Owner/Photographer

Angie McMonigal Photography

2005 – Present (8 years) Greater Chicago Area

I'm a Chicago-based photographer specializing in Fine Art and Commissioned Photography. My work features architectural and urban themes, oftentimes with a minimalistic or abstract perspective. I am currently working on projects 'A Thousand Points of Light' and 'Hometown'. As well as ongoing series 'Architectural Street', 'Architectural Abstracts', 'Nature & Man' and 'Streetlamps'.

My work has been awarded and exhibited nationally and tends to focus on urban, architectural elements and geometric patterns, most often in my current hometown of Chicago. When I do take a break from the urban environment, I enjoy making images in nature and travel, and strive to find minimalistic, geometric patterns there as well.

I have been commissioned by individual art buyers as well as companies to create artwork for their homes, offices and products.

To view my work, please visit my website or Facebook page. I greatly appreciate you taking the time to view my photos and hope you enjoy what you see!
