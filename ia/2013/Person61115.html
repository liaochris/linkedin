Jeff Price's Summary

Author, Educator, Consultant and leading aviation industry Trainer

Jeff Price is an associate professor of aviation at the Metropolitan State University of Denver, the owner of Leading Edge Strategies, an aviation management training & consulting company and a member of the National Speakers Association. His company offers numerous training courses related to airport ops, security and management, & expert witness services.

He is the lead author of Practical Aviation Security: Predicting and Preventing Future Threats, the primary textbook for universities on the subject and an essential desk reference.

He has been a professional aviation management trainer for the past decade teaching and authoring both AAAE’s ACE-Security program and the Certified Member program. He also teaches courses in airport operations and emergency management. Jeff is a FranklinCovey certified trainer of the 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, Presentation Advantage and the Leadership Series.

He is presently working on "What I've Learned, 50 books in 50 weeks", a blog that features the key life lessons from reading 50 books in one year. www.achievementprice.com He is also available for keynote speaking engagements.

He is frequently called upon by the media to provide insights on aviation issues His comments have been featured on CNN, ABCNews, Bloomberg, MSN, AP and USAToday.

With 25+ years experience in airport management, Jeff began his career in the U.S. Coast Guard as an intelligence officer. He moved to airport ops at Stapleton International Airport in Denver, CO and was the Assistant Security Director at Denver International Airport. He managed the Jefferson County Airport and previously served as the Director of Public Relations and Marketing. He is also a civilian pilot.

Specialties: Aviation security, airport operations, airport safety, public relations, community relations and marketing. Airport management. Training. Keynote speaking.
