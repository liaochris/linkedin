Brent Elliott's Summary

As a top CEO, I drive business initiatives and increase stake holder value by utilizing marketing, operations and planning strategies to inspire change in medium and large sized companies. I turn around and grow organizations to profitability and increase market share in public and privately held multinational manufacturing businesses.

Professional Expertise

I have proven ability in balancing business initiatives that drive shareholder value. Strengths include strategic planning, marketing, operations and inspiring change in medium and large sized companies. Extensive experience with public and privately held multinational manufacturing and service focused businesses.

What I Do

I ensure an organization has a clear vision and enable the company to meet that future vision. I build, grow, transform and revitalize companies. I rewrite the future, solve problems, and achieve breakthrough performance.

Benefits to You

• Transferable skill sets across multiple industries that provide sustainable results
• Customer Centric, Market Facing executive who builds high performance teams
• Deep operational and supply chain skills to lower cost and raise productivity
• A leader who can inspire people to meet a Vision
• Communicate and create excitement about the future direction and vision.

How I Work

I work with the leadership team to develop a vision, a strategy and a strategic roadmap for future growth based on solid understanding of the company’s own strengths, our customers and the competitors. Then I lead the team and company to ensure we achieve our commitments.
