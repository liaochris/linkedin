Kenneth Cleary's Summary

I am currently the Archivist and Librarian for the Historical Society of Haddonfield, as well as a Processing Assistant for the New Jersey State Archives. I have also been an Archives Assistant for Rutgers University Archives, a summer archives intern at the New-York Historical Society and completed a one year processing project for Rutgers University Special Collections. In addition, I worked as a volunteer archivist at the New Brunswick Free Public Library for two and a half years. Before all of this, I worked in technical support and customer service.

Proficiencies: Historical Research, Reference Services, Archival Appraisal, Processing and Description, Collection Management, Preservation, Digital Curation, Digital Photography and Digitization, Archivist Toolkit, EAD, EAC-CPF, DACS, MARC/AMC, Dublin Core, MODS, XHTML, CSS, MySQL, Oxygen XML, Web 2.0, Event Planning, Project Management, Past Perfect, Microsoft Office, PC Hardware, and Windows Operating Systems.
