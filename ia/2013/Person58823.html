Dating Coach Jarod Oner's Summary

Want to Uncover How I Helped Men All Over the World Have Choice When It Comes to Their Dating Lives?

My name is Jarod Oner and I’ve been lucky enough to be a speaker at some of the largest seminars in the world for seduction and attraction. People seek me out because I live what I teach, I’m not some dating coach that sits in his office giving advice, I am out every week meeting women and going on dates. Whether it’s from approaching women or meeting them online, I’m doing it.

Here’s how you too can become a more “well rounded” dater.

Go out three days a week to socialize and meet women. Approach 10 women MINIMUM! Now if you want to get better and faster go out four nights a week.

In your “down time” do ONLINE DATING! Not only will it keep your blade sharp with women but you’ll also be talking to MORE WOMEN! Which again means… more phone numbers and more numbers means... More dates!

Grab my free eBook entitled: Texting Girls Dissected

In this eBook I took real life text message conversations from guys and broke them down one by one. You can get this downloadable eBook by signing up for my newsletter at ManOfChoice.com - You will need it since you’re approaching so many women.
