Manny Goldman's Summary

I'm married to the woman of my dreams and together we have two beautiful and amazing kids that brighten our lives and those they touch everyday.

I’m a man on a mission with a huge purpose and passion and tenacity to pull it off.
I’ve dedicated my life to the evolution of humanity.

That includes you, me and everyone – living a life they love through connection and collaboration. No longer do we have to feel alone, lost or frustrated when pursuing our goals and dreams. When we all serve, we all win.

I wasn’t always committed to helping myself and others. Actually, it was quite the opposite. For the first 25 years of my life, I was completely self-destructive and I hurt just about anyone and anything in my path.

At the end of these long and painful years, I was at my rock bottom. I was obese, clinically depressed, $100,000 in debt, a college dropout, arrested 6 times, failed in 5 romantic relationships, failed in more than 10 businesses and even failed at multiple suicide attempts.

Just as I was ready to give up and commit suicide for the last time, I found what I was looking for. I was introduced to Personal Growth. I attended a 4 day weekend seminar that changed my life forever.

My efforts really paid off – in just 11 short months I released 50 lbs, was passionate about my life, became financially stable, created the romantic relationship of my dreams, discovered a vision for my life and so much more.

What I’ve gone through and what I’m currently experiencing fuels the actions I take every day to realize my ultimate vision: to assist 2.2 billion people to create lives they love through connection and collaboration with each other. No longer will people feel alone, lost or frustrated while pursuing their goals and dreams.

I wish you love, compassion and abundance on your life’s journey. Please let me know what your goals and dreams are and how I can help you create them.
