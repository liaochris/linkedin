Brad Chacos' Summary

Professional dork i.e., full-time technology scribe. I'm a senior writer for PCWorld, with news analysis and Windows as my primary focus areas (though I'm really a jack of all trades).

Before taking the full-time plunge with PCWorld, I was a longtime freelancer, with my circuitry-related work appearing in PCWorld, LAPTOP magazine, ComputerShopper, Maximum PC, DigitalTrends, the Intuit Small Business Blog, Wired, Maclife.com, Gizmodo, PC Gamer, MSNBC.com, Fox News, CIO.com MacWorld, ComputerWorld, ITWorld, the Webroot website and many, many more.
