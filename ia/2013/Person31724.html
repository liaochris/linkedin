David Ealson's Summary

Cornerstone International Group
Cornerstone Search New Zealand Ltd has been selected as the New Zealand partner firm of Cornerstone International Group, a global executive recruiting firm with over 100 offices globally. The unique concept of Cornerstone's continued growth and success is their commitment to carefully select the "best in class" recruiting firms into an international consulting organisation without losing the attention and boutique style of service typically found in smaller, entrepreneurial executive recruiting firms. This expanded capability now allows us to support both national and international recruitment, development, retention and transition of your key employees and leaders. We are leaders and innovators in executive search solutions across senior appointment in technical, leadership and C level positions. Our insight and strategic view of industries and our maintenance of Talent communities across a broad number of industries, helps us in the delivery of effective results for clients.
