Jillian Myers' Summary

Certified Pet Tech Instructor focused on "improving the quality of pets' lives, one pet owner at a time." My goal is to put pet health care knowledge back in the hands of pet owners and pet care professionals, encouraging them to take a proactive stance in the care of their (and their client's) pets. As a volunteer for Los Angeles Animal Services, I see firsthand the mistreatment and neglect that many of our city's animal suffer at the hands of uneducated owners. Through my company's animal rescue fundraising program, I hope to partner with rescue groups and other animal-related organizations to raise awareness of these issues as well as much needed funds.
