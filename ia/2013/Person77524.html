Kurt Jensen's Summary

Executive with significant expertise in Program and Project Management, Technical Service Delivery, Operations Management and related disciplines in IT, telecommunications and other technology areas. A strong leader and strategic thinker with exceptional analytical skills. Leads by example and applies high levels of energy, engagement and commitment to drive significant results through a structured, goal-oriented approach. International business experience from North America, Europe, Australia and South East Asia contributes to a unique and well-rounded blend of leadership skills and experience. Examples of accomplishments are:

• Project Management and Program Management: Developed an ISO 9000-certified Project Management Center of Excellence for Brüel & Kjær, for the delivery of complex technology system solutions up to $2M throughout South East Asia and Pacific Rim.
• Service Delivery Management / Vendor Management: Managed transition and steady-state phases of the strategic outsourcing of Store Technical Services to IBM and it’s sub-contractors.
• Strategic Business Management: Developed 5-year business strategy for the IT and telecommunications infrastructure for a 35-site organization. Negotiated contract that yielded annual operating cost savings of 10% with vastly improved service levels.
• Technology Deployment and Infrastructure Upgrade: Planned and managed implementation of IT and Call Center facilities allowing a telecommunications carrier to significantly increase the subscriber base and reduce customer churn.

• Contract Specification and Negotiation: Developed specifications for a $300M contract for the Australian Federal Government including the development of improved alternative solutions and strategies that resulted in over 28,000 customers having their annual telecommunication costs reduced by $40M while receiving greatly improved voice and data services.

Specialties

Strategy development, strategic business planning, contract negotiation, contract management, program management, project management, vendor management, strategic outsourcing, change agent, team building, International experience, technology, telecommunications, IT, engineering, cost saving, technology deployment
