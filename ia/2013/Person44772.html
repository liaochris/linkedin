Next Big Thing, LLC

January 2009 – Present (4 years 11 months) New York, NY

Next Big Thing grows the social media presence for a variety of different businesses. You probably know how important it is to use Twitter to connect with fans, but knowing how to navigate through social media can be daunting. And even companies who understand the importance & have started tweeting are often only connecting with a small handful of loyal followers, rather than monetizing the platform to expand & source new business. That's where Next Big Thing comes in- we work hand in hand with your business to aggressively build a highly targeted following. Next Big Thing works across all different industries, representing everyone from doctors to grocery store chains, film makers to national radio stations, authors to large casinos & more.

One of the first things we do is develop a strategy around the type of content that goes out from your feed, the aspirational story your brand tells. Tweeting haphazard company info has some benefit in conveying information, but it doesn't craft the story that gives the true vibe of your business and it doesn't hold attention long-term. Additionally- and most importantly- we focus on attacking the scope of the account. You may have great information & great content- but if you're only reaching a couple hundred people, what's the point? We don't purchase fake twitter accounts just for numbers' sake- you may have seen politicians in the news lately doing just that. We target actual people pre-vetted to have the greatest interest in your brand- your most likely customers that are currently on twitter. We'll sit down with your team at our kickoff meeting to determine the particular verticals we want to source, whether it's people talking live time about your industry, people with relevant bio data, or even people following competitor brands.

Reach out if YOU'RE ready to be the NEXT BIG THING!
