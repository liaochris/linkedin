Ryan McAbee's Summary

____________________________/¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

My name is Ryan McAbee. I persuade folks to take action. Whether through a presentation, technical paper, screencast, e-book, webinar, speech, blog post, or tweet, my purpose is to arm you with the exact information needed to do something. Some call this “inbound marketing” others call this “selling”, but I view it through the lens of active listening and giving in abundance. To be successful, you have to know people. Over the course of a day I might talk strategy with the C-suite, decode geek speak with IT, swap war stories with production, and so forth. More often than not, I become the bridge to ensure that each party understands the other.

Technology had me at hello… well at least the Macintosh did. Currently, I write about the influence of technology on our communications landscape at M-bossed.com. I provide information and guidance for marketers and managers on the why, where, when, and how technology is affecting media. The site is also my testing laboratory to implement new digital and social marketing techniques — SEO, SEM, analytics, e-mail marketing, etc.

As the saying goes, “all work and no play makes Jack a dull boy.” I love going to new places (to-date NZ is my favorite & Lauterbrunnen, Switzerland is the most dramatic). I devour business and marketing books (dull for most, entertainment for me). I have been a puppeteer. I’m also a fan of Tarheel basketball, Clemson football, radlers, caffiene, Asheville, NC.
