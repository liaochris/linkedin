Mark Preston's Summary

I have been self employed now for over 10 years working within the internet marketing industry. I am a big believer in keeping up to date with the very latest techniques and doing in-depth research. When it comes to internet marketing, SEO and inbound marketing services it is a must for me to provide a quality service and top results so all my clients receive a very good return on investment.

Over the past few years I have gained top ranking results for major key terms such as 'kitchens', 'property for sale', 'video productions' and 'business ideas' to name a few. I always like a challenge and nothing is impossible if you follow the ethical route using inbound marketing. It just comes down to time.

Before establishing 195 SEO Ltd I was the owner of one of the largest white label web and marketing companies within the UK providing web design and SEO UK services to other web and marketing agencies throughout the UK. Things was going very well but the only issue was that other people and companies was getting the recognition for the results I had personally achieved so I decided to setup 195 SEO Ltd to promote my own skills directly and establish my name as one of the UK's top SEO UK Consultants.
