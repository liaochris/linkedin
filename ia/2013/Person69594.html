Jessica Paterik's Summary

I have production experience in video, print, web media, and enjoy helping teams develop larger projects. I understand the timelines and processes that are needed to create successful animations, videos [and video shoots], websites and printed materials.

The best part of a project? Figuring out the 'why' behind it, so that only the most meaningful work is done. Problem solving and doing something new energize my creativity.
