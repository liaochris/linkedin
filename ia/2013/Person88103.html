Mary Henry's Summary

Senior Professional in Human Resource certified through the Society of Human Resource Management with 10+ years of diversified HR experience. I am the President and Founder of HR on Demand LLC.

HR on Demand LLC is in business to provide human resource services to who employers need to grow their business. We do this by assessing the company and its needs and providing the services needed right now. The demand for high performing individuals to meet business goals will only increase over the next decade. In addition to attracting talent, retention will continue to challenge employers, and we have proven methods for creating an environment in which people excel.

HR on Demand LLC is now partnered with TTI Performance Systems. We offer the opportunity to create custom job benchmarking. This benchmarking process is a powerful tool for recruiting superior talent and delivering the best results possible.

Do your employees understand how their functions impact revenue and managing costs? We can build a system that aligns results with goals and rewards.

Specialties: Performance Management, Business Results through Total Rewards, labor relations, mergers and acquisition, competency based interviewing and selection, leadership and management training.
