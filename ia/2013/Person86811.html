Vice President, Director

Square 1 Bank

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Banking industry

February 2009 – March 2012 (3 years 2 months)

Prior to joining Updater, Mark was a Vice President at Square 1 Bank, a $1.5 billion dollar venture bank. Mark was the Director of the bank's national early stage practice, called Square Roots. Under Mark's guidance, the program set records in new customer growth, division profitability, and financing received by the client base.

Mark was recruited by Square 1 to launch Square Roots in the major east coast markets of Boston and New York City before being promoted to manage the Division on a national level. Mark has worked with hundreds of early stage entrepreneurs, serving as an advisor and mentor in areas including strategy, hiring, operations, sales and fundraising. Shortly before departing, Mark was selected as a member of the Bank's President’s Club.

Mark has been profiled in Venture Studio and Square Roots has received recognition in several national publications including the Huffington Post.
