David Standen's Summary

I’m a creator, a creative and digital art director with 10 years of experience behind me with a driving enthusiasm to build better experiences to engage people in new and better ways.​ I have a branding, visual background and I spent the best part of my early years working in the youth / fashion / music industry. I've worked and partnered with other Designers, Art Directors, Creative Directors, Photographers, Copywriters, Creative Technologists, Interaction Designers, Directors and also worked independently on campaign, brand, creative, experiential, interactive and integrated briefs. My strengths are understanding how to make the best use of technology when making creative decisions and applying fundamental design principles to everything I do.

I'm a charismatic, curious, inspirational chap and I've been fortunate to have worked with some awesome people and pick up some awards a long the way. Think of me as a creative sponge whose trying to absorb as much as I can.
