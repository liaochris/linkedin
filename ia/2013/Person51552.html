Hanny Alshazly's Summary

A result oriented Marketing and Educational Technology Consultant with 17+ years of experience in both of these fields, outstanding credentials and a proven record of accomplishments in Operation and P&L Management, revenue building and organizational enhancements, Hanny Alshazly brings pragmatic insights into the online teaching and learning industry from both the corporate and academic perspectives. Having a great passion for online education and the perfect mix of interpersonal, relationship building and technical skills, Hanny takes every opportunity to transfer his enthusiasm to the decision makers and planners of higher education institutions and corporations across the globe, effectively communicating the pedagogical benefits of online learning technologies to organizational and ministry level provosts. Hanny has successfully worked with communities and clients in the US, Canada, Europe and the Middle East and is well known for his interactive keynote presentations having spoken at numerous conferences worldwide (including at events in the US, Canada, UK, Europe and throughout the Middle East and Africa) and was the Chairperson for The Current E-Learning Market in the Middle East forum at the 2004 Online Educa in Berlin.

Specialties

Marketing, Business development and planning, partnership negotiation, consulting, educational technologies, sales, training and teaching, marketing, operations management.
