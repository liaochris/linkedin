Kyle MacLean's Summary

I'm a lawyer at Advocates LLP in London, Ontario. I work in litigation, focusing on corporate/commercial, employment, estate and construction law.

I'm an LL.B. graduate of the University of Western Ontario. I also completed an M.Sc. in Biochemistry and an H. B.Sc. in Genetics at Western.

Apart from a focus on legal matters, my interests include world affairs, the outdoors, science, technology and the intersection of all of the above with individuals' rights. Hailing from Essex County, I'm a long-time resident of London, Ontario, together with my wife, son and 2 large, sleepy dogs.
