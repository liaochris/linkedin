Ann Rolfe's Summary

Mentoring Works helps you to create mentoring conversations, relationships and programs. We offer a comprehensive range of resources, including books, audio, online and face-to-face training for mentors and mentorees, based on extensive experience.

As an organisational strategy, mentoring attracts, retains and develops employees. The knowledge that I share is drawn from over fifteen years working with corporate and public sector clients to design and implement mentoring programs that support people as they engage in relationships for personal and professional development and from my interaction with participants from many different professions and industries.

Specialties

Mentoring programs, mentor training, career development, training, adult learning, professional development, learning and development
