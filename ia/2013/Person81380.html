Shawn Noratel's Summary

Shawn Noratel is currently the Founding Partner and Creative Director at Liquified Creative, a digital marketing and design firm based in Annapolis, Maryland. His firm offers brand awareness and integration on a cross-media level for both small and international clients alike.

Shawn is also President of Holy Nativity Lutheran Church, a community-centric and outreach-focused church located in Baltimore, Maryland. In addition, He also assists start-ups as a speaker through local organizations on topics geared toward marketing and brand development planning.

Awards included: National Addys, 2009 Creativity Awards and numerous Print Quality/Design Awards from the PGAMA.
