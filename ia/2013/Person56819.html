Jacqueline Miller's Summary

I have always loved art and drawing, so I set my goals to be a graphic designer.
I have ten years of experience using many graphic suites as well as working with HTML and CSS. I hold two Diplomas in Computer Art and Web Page Designer, which I earned in 1999 and 2004. I now have an Associate Degree in Graphic Design and Multimedia from TSBI. Some of the software I can use is Adobe Photoshop, Corel Photo Paint, FrontPage, Dreamweaver, as well as beginning to use Adobe Flash. I have also had a design put into print; it is the cover of the Camden, NJ resource guide. I am so able to work under pressure; I as the convention chair, with twenty others under me put together a three day event called Erie Anime Experience, which is a convention that supports Japanese Animation as well as World Culture and History, with only three months of planning. I am good at multitasking, which in my life has been a must have, as I live on a farm with my family as well as working on friends websites, working the convention and going to school. .

Specialties: Adobe Illustrator, CorelDraw, Adobe PhotoShop, Corel, PhotoPaint, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe InDesign, HTML, CSS, Adobe Dreamweaver, MS Word, Microsoft Certified in PowerPoint, Adobe Flash
