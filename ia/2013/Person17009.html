Bret Kruse's Summary

When people ask me what I do, I always struggle to sum it up. I honestly do most everything, I have crafted my skills to be a self sufficient machine if need be. I create high quality designs, I build the front end, I create the database, I code the backend and I deploy.

I also love working as part of a team where I may focus on only one of those aspects.

My skills also extend beyond the web development world. I model in Cinema 4d and animate in either After Effects, Flash, Cinema 4d, etc. If it involves creative, especially in regards to interactive, I either have experience with it, or would love to learn it.

Getting a new media degree means that I understand all media. I obviously have my areas of focus but I can hold a conversation with people of all media backgrounds and we all speak the same language. The learning is never done though. In this industry, if you stop absorbing, you get left behind.

So, what do I do? Whatever you need me to.

Specialties

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Flash, PHP, mySQL, Design, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, 3D, Cinema 4D.
