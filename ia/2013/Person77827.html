Kyle Samani's Summary

If I had to describe myself in one word, I'd describe myself as infectious.

I am crazy. No, insane. And I'm going to change medicine using some of the most incredible technologies in the world. I bust my ass everyday in the pursuit of awesome, and I'm always looking for people who want to join in that mission.

I'm Founder and CEO of Pristine, a company in Austin, TX that's delivering the next generation of telemedicine, teaching and training, communication, and process control solutions at the point of care through Google Glass.

When I'm not busy taking over the world, breaking things, or playing ultimate frisbee, I write for a few blogs:

HIStalk - most influential blog in healthcare IT
TechZulu - a prominent technology blog
Svbtle - a new style of online magazine

All of my writing is consolidated and reproduced on my personal blog at kylesamani.com. I tend to write about: business models, challenges, and opportunities in health IT; strategic analysis of technology markets; my entrepreneurial learnings; personal life hacks.

Before Pristine, I spent years developing, selling, and implementing electronic health records (EHRs) into hospitals at VersaSuite, an EHR vendor that caters to rural hospitals. I've learned the ins and outs of EHRs, CPOE, meaningful use, ICD10, medical billing and accounting, and information systems for every ancillary department in modern hospitals and LTACHs (ED, ICU, OR, radiology, pharmacy, lab, pathology, etc).

Contact me at kyle@pristine.io if you want to partake in some of the most ridiculous, exciting adventures of your life.
