Kristen Christian's Summary

When I was outraged by the actions of several corporate for-profit banks, I reached out in the language every Millennial is fluent in— Facebook. Consumers responded en masse, resulting in nearly 6M Americans changing the way they bank in the first year alone. In the year since, numerous similar movements have been founded in countries across the world.

While continuing to advocate for consumer empowerment & economic sustainability, I offer lectures and consultations to help organizations connect with their communities through organic social strategies.
