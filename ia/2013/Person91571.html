Merrill Peters' Summary

Thirty years of diverse experience in sales, design and project management of material handling systems. Utilizing cubic space for faster through-put, time savings and manpower efficiency implementing designs for increased storage capacity. Seismic designs, local building permits and project management services available for turn-key systems. Sixteen years ago I established and still operate a local branch of a national company headquartered in St. Louis, MO.
