Karim Varela's Summary

Karim Varela, the head of Android at Tinder, has almost ten years of mobile application development experience. Karim is currently responsible for building Tinder's Android application from the ground up.

Karim is also leading SocialTagg, a startup that is redefining how people check into and network at events (http://socialtagg.com).

He was a tech reviewer for the book Pro Android 4 and is studying towards his MBA from the University of Florida.
