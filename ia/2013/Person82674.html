Louis M. Balenko's Summary

Louis is a Silicon Valley, CA based professional with 12+ years of global experience across start-ups, Fortune 500 corporations and management consulting firms. He currently leads a multi-million dollar portfolio of online media properties with P&L responsibility, developing strategies to drive revenue and traffic growth. Louis brings the soul and innovation of a startup, the discipline and operational efficiency of a Fortune 500, the problem solving and analytical expertise of a consultant and the best practices and insights gleaned from a diverse background of managing increasing scope and responsibility across complimentary industries. As an experienced people manager, Louis is committed to continuous learning for himself and his teams.

Louis is a graduate of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), where he received his Masters of Business Administration as a Sloan Fellow, while also managing a holding company for his portfolio of independent entrepreneurial investments (Real Estate, software, e-commerce). Louis also holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree (Cum Laude and Beta Gamma Sigma) with a major in Marketing from the John Molson School of Business.
