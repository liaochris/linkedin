Frank Baker, CSP, CFPS, ALCM's Summary

Career safety professional, published author, frequent concurrent session presenter at national and regional Professional Development Conferences, with over 25 years of combined multi-line and WC mono-line insurance loss control / risk management experience. Strong focus on customer service to assist with control of identified risk factors as well as recognition and mitigation of potential loss sources; and serving as an invaluable technical resource for our clients, underwriters and agents aimed at solidifying those relationships and retaining desirable business.

Specialties

Authorized 10/30 Hour OSHA Outreach Training Program Instructor for General Industry and Construction. Municipal entity risk management and safety consulting including fire service operations.
