Art Yeaman's Summary

Preserving, Celebrating, and Showcasing American Heritage --- One-Story-At-A-Time. Art is "Your KEY To Applied Heritage" he involves, individuals, families, organizations, and communities with patriotism, innovation, education, and entertainment.

Specialties: American Heritage (No Hyphen Required). Art has a passion for our American Heritage – he’s an observer of nature; exalts indigenous respect; and honors our Nation’s Founding Fathers. Promotes the arts, science, free-enterprise and personal/civic responsibility.

He's available to speak at your Luncheon/Dinner, Festival, School Programs, Seminars and Conferences also a Chautauqua style presenter.

Topics/Services Include:

U.S. Constitutional Studies: Teaching, Facilitating Discussion Groups.

Tour Guide Services: St. Johns River, Clay County, Florida, and specifically Green Cove Springs.

Meet Ben Franklin (1706-1790): Saving Money "A Heritage of Why Saving Matters," Life and Times: “Ben’s Story,” Natural Philosophy: “Let’s Go Fly a Kite” – Art & Science -- Ben tames lightning,
Writer and Publisher: “Poor Richards Almanack,” The Way to Wealth: “Father Abraham,” Liberty and Independence: “Join or Die” from vision to declaration – A Revolution, U.S. Constitution – “Yours If You Can Keep It,” Innovation: "America’s 1st Compatriot Innovator"

Meet William "Billy" Bartram (1739-1823): Life and Times “Billy’s Story,” Travels of William Bartram: From Philadelphia to Florida, Nature Connect: America’s 1st Spiritual Naturalist

Meet Henry Clay (1777-1850): Life and Times - The Great Compromise.

Current Projects: U.S. Constitution classes, Local Heritage Sign Marker Program, Development of local Archives, Creation of Ben Abram (1798-1890), Secondary Education Text Book Review.

Creator of the H.I.T.M.A. formula… a Hall of Fame approach what matters.

United States Marine Corps

Government Agency; 10,001+ employees; Military industry

Serving and defending our Nation – a republic – for more than 20 year, 1st, 2nd & 3rd Marine Air Wing as a CH-53 Helicopter mechanic and crew chief. He is an honor graduate of the Staff Non-Commissioned Officer Academy, a decorated Marine, who participated in numerous Humanitarian operations during the post Vietnam era.

His last tour of duty was serving members of the Armed Forces and their families as a substance abuse counselor. He commenced his speaking career as an “Outreach Prevention” facilitator and Marine liaison at Naval Air Station Jacksonville, FL.
