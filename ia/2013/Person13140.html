Ben Levin's Summary

Through a combination of quantitative and qualitative research, and strategic analysis, my clients receive human-centered, research-driven Experience Design. I provide a holistic view of the intersection between business goals and customer desires, as it is mediated by technology and human-to-human interaction.

I have provided consulting, design and measurement strategies to global businesses for the past 15 years, with particularly deep experience in Financial Services, Pharmaceuticals and Consumer Packaged Goods.

Specialties include: Rapid concepting and prototyping, experience design, business strategy, competitive analysis, information architecture and interaction design, customer experience management, site metrics and analytics, and digital strategy.
