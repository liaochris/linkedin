Mark Haugstad's Summary

Welcome to WaterMark Design! I have been designing since 2008 - In May 2011, I launched WaterMark Design, started in Fargo, ND and now in Elk River, MN. I have a degree in Graphic Communications in Multimedia, Minnesota State University of Moorhead. I am forward thinker who stays up-to-date on the latest technology and trends.

I have a passion for the arts, an eye for detail and a broad range of multimedia knowledge. I am dedicated to my clients. I will put forth my best effort to get the job done right and go above the standard.

In my spare time, I love to spend time with my wife and family. In the summer you might find me camping, biking, spending time at the lake, fishing or playing frisbee golf. I'm a diehard Minnesota Timberwolves fan and love to get on the court myself. I'm also involved at my church and enjoy volunteering in the community.

I enjoy traveling and have gone on three camping road trips (10-14 days a time) to the west coast, east coast and central. I've also been to Canada, Mexico and Nicaragua.
