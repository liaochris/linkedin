Jeremiah Stanghini, MBA's Publications

  • Nurturant Parenting, Religiosity, Moral Judgments, and Political Affiliation: A Test of Lakoff's Model.

    • Association for Psychological Science
    • May 23, 2008
    Authors: Jeremiah Stanghini, MBA, Eric DeVos, Maria Schell

    We tested Lakoff’s (2002, 2006) suggestions that conceptual frameworks representing “the nation as family” (i.e., Strict Father versus Nurturant Parent) are related to parenting styles, moral reasoning, and political attitudes. Some predictions based on Lakoff were supported, others were not. To complement Lakoff's psycholinguistic view, we revisit sociological ideas concerning working-class authoritarianism.

    *Note: This was published with Professor Eric De Vos and colleague Maria Schell.
