Anthony Barker's Summary

An experienced Graphic Designer with the confidence and enthusiasm to take responsibility for all aspects of a design project.

A proven track record for successfully managing and motivating design teams as well as building and maintaining excellent client relationships.

With 15 years of creative industry experience – my award winning designs cross a wide range of disciplines including brand identity, corporate/annual reports, staff magazines, advertising/marketing campaigns and web design.

A keen desire to be involved in generating creative concepts and design solutions and thriving under pressure especially when faced with tight deadline.
