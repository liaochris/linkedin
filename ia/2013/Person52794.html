Henry Hopking's Summary

T-CUP. - "Thinking Correctly Under Pressure".

I work as a coach / consultant specialising in human mental performance. However, this is not from a psychological perspective. Rather it is based on a unique system of neurological training I have developed over the last decade.

The application is wide reaching and the work varied...

For private coaching my individual clients include top executives, entrepreneurs and World Class / Olympic level athletes.

For organisations I provide consultation services and tailored solutions to high performance sports teams, government agencies, private security companies and other high pressure environments such as air traffic control.

They all need to improve their mental performance. That is what I help them achieve.
