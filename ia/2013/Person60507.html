Jeanette Bent's Summary

I've been in NYC for two years choreographing, teaching and performing various dance, burlesque and circus pieces at Silhouette Dance Studio, SHOCKra Studio and Trapeze School New York. I came from two years working as the choreographer/director of two different resorts, Club Med and Club Maeva, from multiple locations in Mexico, the Turks & Caicos Islands and Florida.

Prior to this, I was finishing my bachelor's degree in dance, journalism, French and international studies at California State University, Long Beach. I was accepted as an intern to Dance magazine my senior year, jumped on a plane and flew across the country two days after graduation.

I started working as the copy and production editor for Movmnt Magazine, while writing two books that I would later publish, “Vital Energy” and Flicker. I was also freelancing intermittently for Beautiful/Decay, Ehow, E.L.M., L.A. Splash, Move, Suite 101 and Valley Scene magazines.

While preparing for graduate life, I was the managing editor for the campus magazine called DIG, and DJing a radio show on campus called L’Ambiance under the alias Jazzerina Jaye. That year, I was also the choreographer for an American Basketball Association team, The Buzz’s dance team, the Honeybees.

This was after I had spent a busy year writing, dancing and soul-searching in Aix-en-Provence, France. I published articles in French for IEFEEXpress and Ladies Room magazines before moving to Italy for a dance intensive with ProDanza Italia/USA.

Before I lived in Europe, I was the city editor for the CSULB's newspaper, The Daily Forty-Niner, while teaching dance class every night at 24 Hour Fitness locations all over Southern California. I also interned at Fox News Channel in L.A. to better understand broadcast media.

I danced in SuperBowl XXXVII’s Pregame and Half-time shows, as well as a runway fashion show for L.A. Models while still in high school. At this time, I was also writing and editing for my school’s newspaper, The Gangplank.

Specialties

commercial dance and circus choreography/production, magazine writing and editing, fiction writing

Jeanette Bent's Publications

  • Flicker

    • Xlibris Publishing
    • October 2009
    Authors: Jeanette Bent, Jeanette Prather

    The world is like a twilight zone for the brain; people all thrive within their own planes and write their own stories. They need to constantly shed their own sparkling histories in order to welcome momentary and fleeting infinite possibilities with open arms, because it is in these moments where the beauty in love and life lie. Jeanette Prather encourages readers to live life to the fullest with Flicker.

    Readers will witness the exploits of Paige, a young woman who experiences life’s harsh realities at a tender age. She gets pregnant but loses the child in her womb. She enjoys life to the hilt, cultivates intimate relationships with whomever she likes, and runs away to escape from her mistakes.

    A reflection of reality, Flicker unfolds the life of the savage girl within Paige and teaches readers that in this world, there are neither repercussions nor absolutes. People create their own destiny, decide on their fate, and make their own rules. Author Prather invites readers to follow Paige’s adventures as the savage girl finally gets unleashed from the chains of Paige’s past and runs free.

  • Vital Energy

    • Xlibris Publishing
    • May 2009
    Authors: Jeanette Bent, Jeanette Prather

    Each person has his or her own way of expressing oneself. Some choose the canvas, manipulating designs and colors to create vibrant masterpieces. Others choose the clever play of words and rhymes to convey their thoughts into prose. Witness how Jeanette Prather transforms pure emotion into art through Vital Energy.

    Be it negative or positive intent, this wonderful poetry anthology is the culmination of the raw inspiration that Prather has drawn from humanity and life. See how the author views the world in this universally contagious plague of poetic thoughts. Discover her perception on humanity during the dark and light periods of human life.

    Uncover the truth of unity and the vast differences that exist only in the people’s minds. Through words and emotions, Prather paints vivid images that will pull at our sentiments and remind us that the world is pulsing with Vital Energy.
