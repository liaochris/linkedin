Jabari Johnson's Summary

Jabari Johnson is an entertainment media personality, producer, and director. As the creator and host of Jabari Presents, as well as the host of Face To Face on BET.com, Jabari has interviewed over 100 of the most influential celebrities including Justin Bieber, Nicki Minaj, Russell Simmons, and Floyd Mayweather. To date, his work has earned him more than 5 million views across the web and a growing legion of supporters.

In addition to his interview series, Jabari has directed
web commercials for TY KU and several music videos
including a recent collaboration with Trae Tha Truth and
Wiz Khalifa for the “Getting Paid” single. And due to to
his status as a trendspotter and influencer among the
18-34 demographic, Jabari has represented numerous
brands and organizations including Heineken, Diesel,
Amp Energy, LRG and Rock the Vote.

Specialties

Film, branding, advertising, digital.
