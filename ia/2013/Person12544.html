Becki DeVall's Summary

My career began in the world of banking as a teller. I was then asked to utilize my skills as a trainer of new tellers, and I eventually ended up as the customer service manager of new accounts. Of course, this didn't happen overnight, but I truly enjoyed the journey!

I am passionate about helping and supporting others! My career has taken me from basic support personnel to the role of Executive Director of a non-profit organization, and forward to the virtual support of a diverse clientele with businesses that cover a myriad of industries.

I have been recognized for outstanding job performance, reliability, multi-tasking skills, efficiency, accuracy, speed, and timely completion of assignments, as well as efficient and organized office management.

Currently, along with my Virtual Assistant business, my husband and I own a business that is designed to help others reach their full potential in whatever business arena they choose. Our goal is to empower individuals to reach their own financial success, whatever that may look like to you.
