Daniel Taub's Summary

Daniel Taub has been an editor and reporter at Bloomberg News for more than a decade. He has received awards from the Society of American Business Editors and Writers, the National Association of Real Estate Editors and the Los Angeles Press Club, and was a finalist in the Public Interest category of the 2008 National Magazine Awards. Before joining Bloomberg in 1999, Taub worked at the Los Angeles Business Journal, the Antelope Valley Press and the Los Angeles Daily News. He has a bachelor's degree in journalism and a master of arts in humanities from California State University, Northridge. Taub contributed to "The Billy Bob Tapes: A Cave Full of Ghosts," by Billy Bob Thornton and Kinky Friedman (HarperCollins, 2012).
