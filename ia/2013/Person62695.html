Gerald Bricker's Summary

Are you building a career in leadership or building a business that you can be proud of? Both a successful career and a successful business require a clear strategy, effective leadership and strong client loyalty.

Our purpose is fostering growth leadership. Our clients experience high growth of leadership, incomes, client loyalty and revenues with our help. We are "Miracle Grow" for business.

We work with executives, Owners, CEOs and Presidents to strengthen their leadership and improve the results of their careers and their businesses. Most are running small to mid-sized professional service companies in financial/insurance, legal, technical, personal/medical, sales, marketing communications and promotional/printing businesses, among others.

We are unique, with: demonstrated results growing business in companies with annual revenues ranging from under $ 1 million to $1 billion; an holistic approach to planning and executing improvements; a "risk free" money back guarantee.

Our President built and led successful sales and management teams in North America, South America, Europe and Asia, leading teams in sales, engineering and customer service, and management committees responsible for strategic planning and business development. He led management and leadership teams that grew business by an average of more than ten per cent per year over a thirty year period.

He has held the positions of Vice President, OEM Sales, Vice President and General Sales Manager and Vice President, Products Group.

Specialties: Growing revenues, training management, organization development, leadership development, building customer relationships, customer loyalty, management coaching, strategic planning, professional assessment, executive coaching, goal planning and achievement and change management.
