Michael Borre's Summary

Mechanical Engineering Consultant specializing in structural and performance analysis of aerospace hydraulic system components.

30 yrs total in the aerospace hydraulic component design and manufacturing field

• 20 yrs in mechanical engineering design of new products (aerospace hydraulic valve and actuator components)

• 10 yrs in manufacturing of close tolerance valve components (CNC mill and lathe programming)

Select clients and services provided:
• Eaton Aerospace, Los Angeles, CA - valve design, mechanical packaging, performance analysis, structural analysis, planetary gear train analysis
• PneuDraulics, Rancho Cucamonga, CA - structural analysis
• Crane/Hydro-Aire, Burbank, CA - valve design, mechanical packaging
• L-3/Sonoma EO (formerly Wescam, Healdsburg), Santa Rosa, CA - electro-mechanical packaging of EO/IR camera systems, structural analysis
• General Dynamics/OTS (formerly Versatron), Healdsburg, CA - mechanical packaging for high-G environment, structural analysis

Specialties

stress and vibration analysis using ANSYS and ProMechanica FEA software, dynamic system analysis using Matlab/Simulink simulation software, gear train and bearing life analysis per AGMA and ABMA standards, mechanical design using ProEngineer
