Landon Cox's Summary

29 years software development, embedded, and computer aided engineering. Experience with a myriad of platforms, technologies, and programming languages.

Exceptional hands-on technical manager and individual contributor. It's important to me, my clients and company to maintain a business outlook rooted in a deep understanding of technology.

If you need someone who can fluidly and competently move between multiple disciplines of engineering, manufacturing, and business, I fit that description.

Technology depth includes multiple software layers (enterprise, application, middleware, firmware), as well as microcontroller hardware design, mechanical and computer aided engineering (CAE / CAD).

Work ethic: Self-motivated - remote developer since ’92 for major technology corporations like IBM/Lotus, Macromedia (now part of Adobe), and 3Com.

IT Specialties: Development of Linux-based network and host security architectures including deployment of security sensors (Snort / OSSEC), aggregated logging (Splunk), and custom statistical analysis (R). Linux high availability and failover architecture and deployment with DRBD on Linux.

I consider myself technically wide and deep. I'm curious, innovative and as likely to be interested in material science as I am organizational management and network protocols. I'm not just a geek and have excellent writing and communication skills often applied to technical blogging and proposal writing.

Personally own and use digital fabrication tools for parts production in my own commercial designs - tools include CNC router, 3D printer, and laser cutter.

Specialties: Linux, embedded development, sensor design, dsPIC, AVR, XMOS parallel programming, Android, XMega, DSP, GPS, GIS, OS X, Web, Flash, Propeller, Ruby, Rails, MySQL, PostreSQL, DRBD, Apache, PHP, RIA, Eagle CAD, Alibre CAD, R stats, TouchOSC, TCP/IP network protocol analysis, deep open source expertise.

Languages: C, C++, Ruby, Java, Groovy, among many others.
