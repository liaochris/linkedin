Anita Smith's Summary

At a glance it’s obvious that Anita Smith loves her job as a full-time Real Estate professional. Her passion is teaching others and helping them grow to become more successful.

Anita has been selling real estate in Santa Clarita Valley for over ten years. She began her career as an expert in the Custom Home market, specializing in ranch style homes in Sand Canyon. As the market changed Anita was wise enough to change her specialization. She has extensive experience in the short sale process and handled varied negotiations for many struggling sellers, repeatedly resulting in enormous success for them.

She is also very committed to continuing education and will shortly have her Broker’s license. Her concern is always to better serve her clients. Anita works with a team of full time Realtors and Associates, where performance is measured by her clients reaching their goals.

Anita truly enjoys helping clients navigate through our current volatile market. So, whether you are looking to buy or sell, Anita Smith wants to earn your trust and help you make your Real Estate dreams come true!  

Specialties: Seller's representation of high-end properties, specializing in Short Sales, Working with Investors, Guiding First Time Home Buyers, and helping Homeowner's who are in financial difficulties.
