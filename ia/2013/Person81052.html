Linda Caldwell's Summary

Linda Caldwell is an extremely creative luxury home marketing professional. Her "out-of-the-box" approach offers clients a unique experience packed with high service. A background in Economics as well as Public Relations have been a critical foundation to the success of her business. Linda is known for offering her clients the most comprehensive luxury home marketing programs and buyer services in the South Charlotte, North Carolina market and nearby luxury home communities Myers Park, Ballantyne and Union county.
