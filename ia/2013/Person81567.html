VP of Marketing and Agent Development

Merchant Service Sales

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; BAC; Banking industry

September 2008 – Present (5 years 4 months)

Continuing marketing responsibilities, but was promoted to V.P. of Marketing and Agent Development.

Researched the merchant services industry needs of a more transparent and accountable pricing structure along with agents needs to streamline sales procedures and developed ClearView fixed flat rate merchant processing. ClearView Fixed flat rate merchant account pricing structure is the most transparent, accountable, easy to understand, and has enabled agents to easily and quickly quote an account the absolute lowest price without hidden fees and surcharges.

This has eliminated a lot of customer service issues and complaints stemming from more convoluted pricing structures. The end result has been the ability to increase customer base and customer attrition rates to increase exponentially while allowing increased sales productivity.

Agents have been able to easily educate their customers so they fully understand their statements and rates they pay creating more satisfied customers.

Developed and implemented a strategic plan for nationwide agent sales channels designed to drive agent recruiting, sales and recurring revenue.

Expanded marketing strategy to include agent sales channel and recruited agents on a national level for selling merchant processing services to all types of businesses.

Developed agent standards, training and marketing tools to ensure a consistent and streamlined process for agents to effectively sale and promote our products and services.

Worked with computer programmers in developing a back end portal solution for agents to easily gain access to their accounts reporting, training, support services, and marketing materials at their demand.

Exceeded estimated sales and revenue targets by 175% two months after ClearView fixed flat rate merchant accounts and the new agent channel launched .
