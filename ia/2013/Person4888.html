Allen Starrett's Summary

I founded Starrett Law Firm to pursue intimate and lifelong relationships as trusted legal counsel to families and business owners in Ballantyne and throughout the greater Charlotte area.

At Starrett Law Firm, we are passionate about providing guidance, protection, and peace of mind in matters such as the following:

ESTATE and LEGACY PLANNING for minor children, disabilities, blended families, family legacies, special needs, probate avoidance, business successions, transfer taxes, planned giving, digital assets, pets, etc.

PROBATE, ESTATE, and TRUST ADMINISTRATION services including filing the will and applying for letters; handling the notice to creditors within the time frame allowed; completing and filing the estate's inventory; locating, collecting, and managing assets of the estate; paying off debts and satisfying claims against the estate; distributing assets to the estate's beneficiaries; and closing the estate.

BUSINESS LAW counseling and services including entity selection, trademarks, formation, governance, bylaws and operating agreements, shareholder and partnership agreements, employees and independent contractors, confidentiality agreements, service contracts and agreements, commercial leases, taxation, and succession planning.

If I can be of service or you simply have questions, please don't hesitate to contact me directly at ars@starrettlawfirm.com or (704) 887-4944.
