Danielle Butler's Summary

A graphic designer and marketing consultant with over 11 years graphic design experience and over 6 years experience as a creative director. Formally owner of Graphic Design Studio Sass Design which I started in 2005 and sold in 2012. Now working as graphic designer and marketing consultant for Web Ninja Pty Ltd based in Surfers Paradise on the Gold Coast.

I am a person who communicates well with clients, co-workers and suppliers.

I have experience with large publishing companies, running a design business and working with other small - medium businesses.

I am most experienced and passionate about design, magazine production fashion and photography.

I have the skills and experience to lead design teams, complete any design briefs from initial concept to a highly developed end product and the passion and creativity to lead others to do the same.

I also have extensive experience dealing with clients, pressure and deadlines.

I am passionate about travel, culture, meeting new people and experiencing new things and helping people.
