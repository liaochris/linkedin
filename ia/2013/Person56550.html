Jack Lugar's Summary

Jack Lugar is a Real Estate Broker on The Lugar Real Estate Team at Century 21 Scheetz serving commercial and residential buyers and sellers in Central Indiana.

As a licensed attorney, he has an adept skill in the art of negotiation and understanding the intricacies of real estate contracts. He is deeply committed to serving his clients by providing them excellent service, leading-edge technology, deep community knowledge, and financial counsel so they can explore options and make informed decisions. Jack is strongly committed to his community and stepped up to run for office when he was asked, which furthered his knowledge of and commitment to his community.

Prior to real estate Jack worked in the entertainment industry and as an attorney. He wrote the humor book The Starving Artist's Diet, wrote for FOX's Wanda At Large, and is the creator of The Sitcom Lab. He continues to write and speak to audiences on screenwriting and working in Hollywood.

Lugar currently sits on the Concentrics Institutional Review Board and has just finished coaching his son's hockey team at The Forum and basketball team through S.P.O.R.T.S. in Fishers.

If you know of someone who has real estate questions or is relocating to Fishers, Carmel, Geist, or Indianapolis, have them reach out to Jack at 317-572-5033.

Specialties: Public Speaking, Business Law, Entertainment Law, Employment Law, Real Estate Law, Residential and Commercial Real Estate Negotiation, Employment Litigation, Business Development & Management, Sitcom Writing, Film and Television Production
