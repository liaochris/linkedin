Efiong Etuk's Summary

Dr. Efiong Etuk is the founding director of the Global Creativity Network, a worldwide community of concerned individuals dedicated to the idea of a world in which everybody can be effective and successful. Proponent of "Global Creativity-Consciousness," "The Right to Be Creative," "The Age of Creativity," "Mass Creativity," and the "Global Creativity 'Marshall Plan,'" Dr. Etuk speaks and writes extensively on strategies for nurturing and engaging everybody's unique set of abilities in the "Great Work" of building a viable and sustainable global civilization that is worthy of our generation and a fitting legacy to future generations.

Specialties

Creative Problem Solving; Policy Research; Executive Training; Project Design;..Organizational Development.
