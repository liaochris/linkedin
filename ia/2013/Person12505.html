Sarah Sawyer's Summary

At Beautiful Ama Acupuncture, we believe that everyone has a beautiful, healthy person within themselves. We help your body access its inner wisdom to heal through acupuncture, herbal medicine, and food therapy. Our unique program is designed to balance the body and the emotions naturally, so you can be the person you always knew you could be. We offer daytime and evening hours at our St. Paul acupuncture clinic.

We specialize in sports medicine acupuncture for athletes who want to improve performance by increasing strength, stamina, endurance, post-workout recovery and healing from musculoskeletal injuries. We also specialize in treating emotional issues like stress, irritability, depression, anxiety, insomnia, tobacco addiction, seasonal affective disorder (SAD), obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and sensory disorders. We treat all different kinds of pain including headaches, migraines, carpal tunnel, back pain, neck pain, shoulder pain, hip pain, knee pain, joint pain, and sports injuries. Other common symptoms we can address include weight issues, menstrual issues, fatigue, constipation, diarrhea, bloating, nausea, pregnancy discomfort, or anything else that is making your life less beautiful.
