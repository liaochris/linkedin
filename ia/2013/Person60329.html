Justin Hong's Summary

My passion lies in entrepreneurship and building businesses. Currently, my focus is on building Highly Relevant, an Internet marketing company, with my ambitious and talented team. We also have another company, Highly Relevant Brands, which is our portfolio of websites and businesses, which currently consists of FixieBikes.com, VeniceFixies.com, and ConcertTickets.com.

My current entrepreneurial pursuits are in the Internet & Digital Media space. I aspire to become an angel investor within the next 3-5 years, and my long-term goal (personal BHAG, if you will) is to be the owner of my beloved Dallas Mavericks one day.

Throughout my career, I've launched various Internet ventures as well my own Internet marketing consultancy called The Small Business MBA. Prior to my entrepreneurial career, I worked as a consultant in IBM’s Financial Management division and management trainee with the Cintas Corporation.

While I have learned a lot about entrepreneurship and business in general, I know that I still have a long way to go. I share my thoughts and some of the things that I've learned about entrepreneurship and becoming a better entrepreneur on my blog, JustinHong.com.

Thanks for checking out my profile, and I look forward to connecting with you.

Specialties: Entrepreneurship, business operations, corporate infrastructure, business development, and internet marketing.

The Small Business MBA was a small business Internet marketing consultancy that I launched while I was in business school at USC.

I developed and implemented customized marketing solutions for my clients. In addition, I educated small business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs about Internet-based technology platforms and how they can be utilized to help grow small businesses.

Initially, my idea for The Small Business MBA was a bit different: I wanted to create a membership site that distributed valuable content that could help small business owners survive tough economics times and increase the value of their businesses.

While I was in the process of creating my program, however, I discovered that many small business owners simply didn't know how to harness the power of the Internet to market their businesses in a cost-effective way - and because of my on-going interest in Internet marketing, I decided to create a consulting company instead.
