Marissa Karolyi's Summary

My name is Marissa and I have 5 years experience in Digital & Graphic Design as well as a growing portfolio of diverse projects.

I established the quirky company Mitzi Designs with the aim of combining Form with Function so as to create beautiful and cleaver designs which fulfill a brief, inspire action and/or promote a positive message to society.

Digital Design is a growing passion and responsive websites are created to be captivating on all standard devices.
