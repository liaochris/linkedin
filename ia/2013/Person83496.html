Luke Westerman's Experience

Chairman and CEO

Solomon Global Holdings INC.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

2010 – Present (3 years)

Solomon Global Holdings is a conglomerate holding company dually housed in Columbus, Ohio and New York, New York, that oversees and manages a number of subsidiary companies across various industries.

Solomon Global Holdings acquires early stage and lower middle market companies poised for significant growth. Solomon subsequently owns a diverse portfolio of synergistic businesses in a variety of industries including consumer goods, IT, telecom, channel marketing, immigration services, intelligence, real estate and strategic consulting.

The goal of Solomon Global Holdings is to acquire businesses with strong growth potential, an experienced management team, a thorough and extensive business plan, high barriers to entry, and a visible exit strategy. Solomon then looks to expedite growth, foster creativity, minimize business risk, dramatically increase profitability, increase overall valuation and then exit the business at a significant premium.
