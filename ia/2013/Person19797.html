Carel Redelinghuys' Summary

An intensely curious mind and passion for progress have seen me work in many different industries and roles – usually busy implementing large projects or complex programs of work. These experiences developed me as a Jack of many trades, and I became a master of some. I have successfully moved through the ranks from Programmer to Head of IT; on-wards to Management Consultant (at partner level), Business Strategist and Organisational Change Director, (even doing a stint as heading up HR for a large Bank!).

One thing that I am really good at is directing and driving to success, technology and complex organisational programs of work.

Some of the large programs of work that I have had the pleasure of seeing through to completion include: New model introductions in the Motor manufacturing industry; Design, development and implementation of a new Data Centre for an Insurance Company; Development of a new suite of core transaction processing systems for an Insurance company; Organisational right-sizing of a financial company; Merger-integration of a Bank; Redevelopment of a Short-term insurance system.

My specialties include: Directing / Managing complex projects; Building exceptional teams; Communicating compellingly, clearly and concisely; Influencing and aligning stakeholders; Resolving ambiguities & clarifying objectives; Directing effort.

Academically, I have covered the bases with an MBA in Project Management and Strategic Change and have supplemented my people management experience with extensive studies in NLP.

Do you have a challenge in aligning senior stakeholders, generating organisational commitment, shaping and managing a complex program of work that includes technology and organisational change elements? Do you need someone that can work across all organisational levels and business units? Contact me on carel@gtg.co.nz.
