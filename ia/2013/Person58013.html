Jamie Yost, M.A.Ed.'s Summary

Welcome to my LinkedIn profile. Thanks for visiting!

I've worn a diverse array of hats in my career - writer, editor, researcher, manager, marketer, and strategist, just to name a few - and yet, at the end of the day, I've always been two things: an educator and a leader.

I can't help it; I've been teaching since I was 3. Back when most other girls my age were pretending to be princesses, I was in my playroom, standing in front of my little blackboard-on-wheels, teaching a "classroom" full of imaginary students all about letters and numbers.

Jumping ahead a few decades, I'm now a highly skilled educator with more than seven years of experience creating and delivering instruction to learners of all ages, from pre-schoolers to seasoned executives. I'm also a highly strategic thinker who can look at a big-picture challenge, develop a vision for the future, and manage tactical projects to achieve that vision.

When I'm working on solving educational challenges, I apply learning theories like Constructivism and Gardner's theory of multiple intelligences (two of my favorites) to design curriculum that engages learners and helps them quickly apply new information in a relevant and concrete way.

I've accomplished a number of things in my career of which I'm quite proud, including:

★ Leading the brand development, positioning, and market launch of a $20M corporate division
★ Adding more than $1M to company revenue by creating and implementing effective education programs
★ Leading my high school biology students to a 96% pass rate on mandatory state exams

There's one more thing you should know about me: I bring wit, empathy, and honesty to everything I do. I believe it's important to have fun while working hard, to identify as well as you can with the people you serve, and to be transparent and truthful.
