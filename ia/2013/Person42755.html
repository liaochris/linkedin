Erin Lunde, MT-BC's Summary

I am a board-certified music therapist who owns a private practice, Sound Matters Music Therapy, LLC. I provide music therapy services to individuals with developmental disabilities in the greater Minneapolis and St. Paul area. I travel to clients' homes and contract with Chaska Music Studios, Growing With Music, and Music Medicine. My primary instruments are voice and violin, and currently I work with clients who have developmental disabilities and neurotypical children. Follow my blog posts at http://soundmattersmusictherapy.com for more information.

Specialties: Vocal performance and voice techniques, violin improvisation and performance, and public speaking
