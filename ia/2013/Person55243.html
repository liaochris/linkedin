Indra Kumaran's Summary

Experienced product management professional with over 10 years in the web content, services, and e-commerce space. Skilled at shaping product strategy to meet company goals, and passionate about building products that exceed customer expectations. Proven ability to lead cross-functional teams through all phases of the product development lifecycle, from concept, to requirements definition, through to design, development, release, and optimization. Successfully launched a broad range of complex product initiatives including web and mobile e-commerce purchase flows, new payment systems, video players, content management systems, social tool integrations, partner facing APIs, and more.

Specialties: Product management; Product roadmap development and prioritization; Web and mobile product strategy development; Resource planning and project management; Opportunity assessments; e-Commerce; Mcommerce; Requirements definition, Functional specification documentation; Purchase funnel optimization; Partner product integrations; Product release planning, Web analytics analysis, Consumer behavior; Product development methods (Waterfall/Agile), Cross-functional team leadership; Creative problem solving
