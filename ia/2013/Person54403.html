Ian Krass' Summary

I want to help people by using media to educate, entertain, and inspire. My focus has primarily been on video because I see it as the ultimate synthesis of all art forms- image, motion, form, sound, and music. With the expressive and informative power of video and the increasingly accessible digital distribution platforms the Internet has to offer, I believe I can use my skills to create real and positive change in the minds and lives of others. I am seeking opportunities to which I can bring my knowledge and experience of digital and social media, and that will help me learn and grow as a person, creator, and member of society.
