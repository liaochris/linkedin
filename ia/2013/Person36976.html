Dorian Quispe's Summary

Sr. Director of Online Media and Marketing, Dorian E. Quispe oversees all LegalZoom online marketing efforts including search, display, lead generation, landing page optimization, and cross-channel attribution tracking.

Prior to joining LegalZoom, Dorian served as director of online marketing for Oversee.net, one of the largest online performance marketing networks in the world, connecting advertisers with over 250 million unique users per month. He previously managed search marketing for LowerMyBills.com, a lead generation powerhouse which was sold to Experian for $400 million to create the 15th largest internet company in the US.

Quispe managed and cross-sold top tier clients at Overture.com prior to being purchased by Yahoo! for $1.63 billion and held numerous roles at Yahoo! corporate in Silicon Valley dating back to 1999.

Dorian holds a bachelor of science degree in marketing from California State University at Northridge.

Specialties: marketing strategy, online media buying, search engine marketing, e-commerce, lead generation, email marketing, analytics, a/b and multivariate testing, affiliate marketing, organizational development
