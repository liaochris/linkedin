Ted Behr's Summary

I work with adults with ADD who are tired of being disorganized and unproductive and with Boomers who are unhappy in their work and want a meaningful and fulfilling new career.

ADD COACHING

In my coaching with adults with ADD, I provide a supportive, practical, concrete process in which I work with my clients to identify and pursue their goals. The primary purpose of ADD coaching is to develop the structures necessary for the client to function effectively and to teach practical approaches to the challenges of daily life. As someone with ADD myself, I know how important this is.

In this process, I provide encouragement, recommendations, feedback and practical techniques such as reminders, questions and calendar monitoring. Together we develop strategies to address issues of time management, eliminating clutter, and becoming more effective in one's personal and professional life.

CAREER COACHING FOR BOOMERS

There are many reasons that people want to change careers. They are burnt out, stressed, bored, feel trapped or wondering “Is this all there is?” If this is you, there is hope.

I have spent 16 years helping clients find careers and jobs that excite them, provide meaning and fulfillment and give them balance in their lives. I have developed a 7-step process called “Discover Your Dream Career” which leads my clients to finding their ideal next career.

It took me 40 years, 7 career changes and 14 jobs to create the life that fits me perfectly today, and I now help others do the same in much less time.

WHO AM I?

My name is Ted Behr, AKA Coach Ted. I apply my intelligence, wit and wisdom to energize and inspire the experienced, intelligent professionals I serve. By creating a safe space, I help clients see the mazes they get stuck in – and how to get out. I help my clients sort, structure and re-frame their perspectives, gain new insights and formulate effective action plans so that they can be successful in creating a life of passion, purpose and joy.
