Asha Phillips' Summary

I'm an experienced journalist and social media editor with extensive knowledge of news gathering, broadcast and social media platforms.

I currently head up social media newswire Storyful's Asia Pacific editorial operations and business development. Originally from Sydney, I have lived and worked in Australia (ABC, Nine, Sky News), Vietnam (CNN, ABC, Storyful), Hong Kong (Storyful) and Thailand (Storyful) as a journalist. My role at Storyful sees me mining social media to find news as it happens in real time as well as viral content and crucially, find people closest to the action, and help newsrooms better embrace social media as an avenue for news gathering.

I'm also a Social Media Editor & Communications Manager at Dragon Industries Asia, where I assist companies including those in the shipbuilding and engineering industries in utilising social media.

I regularly present at journalism and social media conferences, forums, panels etc. throughout Asia, and I consult and train both newsrooms (ABC, WSJ Asia, CNN etc.) and corporations on social media policies and strategies.

I'm active in the media community throughout Southeast Asia and Australia and a member of the Asian American Journalists Association (AAJA).

I have an understanding of web, video and music copyright and licensing agreements and a solid news sense for both traditional news content and viral content.

Beyond social media, I'm proficient in TV and radio broadcasting, having experience as an Executive Producer and Producer in national and international newsrooms. I can work news room software and content management systems including: iNews, ENPS, Avid, Final Cut, Netia among others. I have knowledge of web publishing tools, photoshop and editing software.

You can see me on ABC News 24's social media show 'News Exchange' every week, where I talk about the latest trends and buzz from the social web: http://www.abc.net.au/news/programs/news-exchange/.
