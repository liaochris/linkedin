Mary Anne Dorward, PCC, Author + Speaker + Coach's Summary

Mary Anne Dorward, PCC, is an author, award winning radio host, coach and cancer survivor. A graduate of the University of California, Berkeley, she s a veteran of the National Speakers Association and has been a professional actress on Broadway, television and radio since the age of 17 .

As a professional speaking coach, Mary Anne Dorward’s clients have included CEO’s , Politicians, Sports Celebrities and Humanitarians in the US and internationally.

Her commitment to making the world a better place through both her writing and her company, My Real Voice , has inspired, motivated and enabled hundreds of individuals to speak both strategically, successfully and authentically to their many different audiences.

She is currently working on a series of books, of which Words to Thrive By is the first, that will continue her work on inspiring and helping people to become more successful and inspired in both their personal and professional lives.
