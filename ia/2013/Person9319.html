Apurv Kulkarni's Summary

I am a software engineer.

I was introduced to programming when I was 10. Falling in love with it instantly, I decided at an early age to pursue this as my career. However, as I matured into developing more real applications, I realized that being a programmer is not enough. I believe in being the full rounded software engineer.

I have worked for over 4 years in the various phases of software development. Being impartial to front-end,back-end or database development, I became a generalist.

I am a software engineer with over 4 years of experience in developing leading edge applications for companies like SAP and Infosys with a focus on delivering an exceptional user experience.
I have also demonstrated my application design and development talent in building and maintaining software for RIT.

Aspiring to be a software architect, I matured to be a generalist to all software development tools like programming languages, frameworks, databases. Although most of my experience is in Java and JavaEE, my philosophy is to choose the tool according to the problem. This enabled me to deliver a XAMP based solution to the Student Learning, Support and Assessment unit of the Rochester Institute of Technology.

I am not just a programmer.
I am a software engineer.
