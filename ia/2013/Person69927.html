    • February 2009 to February 2009
    Team Members: Joachim Schlosser, Guido Sandmann

    Magazine article:

    Autosar is by far not as frightening as it sometimes seems. While system architects need deeper knowledge about "Autosar behind the curtain", the function development engineer should not be bothered with that level of detail. For sure, additional capabilities and scenarios can be thought of. The next releases of Autosar will demonstrate the ability of the standard to address the complexity issues in the industry. In the end the engineers have to be able to solve their challenges, and tool suppliers need to support them as much as possible.
