Antonio Zenzola's Summary

As Technical and Solution Architect, first for many IT companies (1990 – 1997) and then for Oracle Consulting in Italy (from 1998), I offer about 20 years of IT projects development experiences, varying from accounting and fiscal applications for Small Medium Enterprises (about 70 applications for low-medium italian company) to high-end e*commerce, transactional and service-oriented web portal applications based on J2EE technologies stack. From 2003 to 2007 I was involved in mobile online and offline application design using industry-standard development framework based on J2EE / J2ME and Oracle Mobile Server product.
As a Mobile Tech Architect, in 2006, I designed and developed SmartSync extension for Oracle Mobile Server product, providing dramatic performance improvements and new synchronization models, compliants to multi-country and multi company enterprises. The SmartSync product is now in production in the biggest italian pharmaceutical company and in the most important banking and assurance italian company (about 10000 total users).
After having acquired ITILv3 and Lead Auditor ISO/IEC 27001 certification, I was involved in several ICT Security projects doing ISO 27001 assessment and audit, Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing.
