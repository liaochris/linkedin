Alison Bentley's Summary

Freelance senior creative in digital and print media with over 20 years experience, the last 16 years of which working in award-winning agencies.

I create integrated campaigns which take your brand through online and print media. I design and build eMarketing HTML eDM emails, websites and landing pages (front end development), social media themes, banner adverts and email signatures. Exceptionally strong in print production I design and artwork brand development, advertising, brochures, literature, annual reports, stationery, large format exhibition stands and image retouching.

Specialties: Executing high end design and artwork to best practice for on and offline media. On brief, within brand guidelines, to tight deadlines. Taking concepts through to reality.

A stickler for accuracy, precision and layout - I can spot a typo a mile away. Uber organised, methodical, conscientious, forward-thinking, logical and creative.

Alison Bentley's Projects

    • October 2012 to December 2012
    Team Members: Alison Bentley, Tariq Rasul, Nicola Batchelor

    Kemsleigh was engaged by one of the large Global Pharmaceutical companies to develop an interactive eLearning Programme for Pharmacists within NHS Ayr & Arran.
