Jen O'Sullivan's Summary

I have realized that my reason for why I do anything I do boils down to one thing: The Power of Possibility. Growing up I was always told, "You can do anything as long as you put your mind to it and work hard." I truly believed that if I wanted to be a doctor I could and equally if I wanted to be a trashman I could. All my parents wanted was that I was happy in whatever life brought me. Looking back I see how blessed I was as a child. For me, your wedding day encompasses everything I was brought up to believe in: POSSIBILITY. I believe that people who are in love and are at the beginning of their life together are at the beginning of possibility. You are inspired and overjoyed with that possibility. I understand what an important time in your life this is and every time I am blessed to work with a new bride and groom, I am able to relive that feeling with every new vow I hear, and every first kiss I see. I love it! I love love. I love my life and the opportunity to be a part of the beginning of possibility over and over again.

My name is Jen O’Sullivan, a boutique wedding photographer who specializes in portrait journalism. I love what I do. I am blessed. Beyond what I ever could have imagined! I have some of the best clients on the planet, the most supportive and loving husband, and the absolute cutest son ever! Check out his latest photo editorial on my Facebook profile. http://www.facebook.com/jenosullivan

To read more about my background, please visit my website: http://www.jenosullivan.com

Specialties

Wedding Photography, Portrait Journalism, Business Coaching, Book Design, Bookbinding, Lighting

An Excerpt from the Book:

Understanding marketing is one thing; being successful at it is entirely another. To become great at it you must practice. Problem solving, like any area of learning, is a process that takes time and energy to get good at. Don’t give up – ever. Not for anyone or any reason. If owning your own business perfectly matches you and you are the quintessential entrepreneur, then don’t ever let anyone tell you that you can’t do it. Just keep going. Continue in the direction you hope to go. Write those goals out so you can see your map. Keep going!

Be true to yourself. It truly boggles my mind how different we all are. We all do business differently. There is no set way. There is no right or wrong way. There is only your way. Don’t be someone you are not. Your marketing must reflect your brand and your brand must reflect you. Your way is the way that will attract the customers who are most like you. They will become customers for life because you get each other.

If you think something is too hard, it is probably worth it. It will be hard. It will be really hard sometimes, but in the end the pros always outweigh the cons. I want you to own your success and own your failures. In marketing, your best learning technique comes from evaluating and analyzing your failures. Solve the problem and you will be using the right muscles in your brain to get better at marketing. I wish you much success in your marketing endeavors.
