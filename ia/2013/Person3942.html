Alex Danovitch's Summary

I work with businesses, institutions, nonprofit organizations, and entrepreneurs to conceptualize and implement a model of environmentally sustainable success by utilizing my expertise in business development and sustainability. This triple-bottom line model values people, planet and profits. It balances economic, social and environmental benefits as a strategy for long term growth in the face of climate change, globalization and a rapidly changing economy.

Sustainability experience includes; zero waste (recycling, composting, reuse, prevention), sustainable transportation and local economies.

Specific business start-up experience (business planning, proforma projections, financing, management) includes:

• ReGo Electric Car Conversion Company; Co-founded a company that has innovated a process for converting vehicles to plug-in electric, customized for cold weather performance. After 2 years of R&D, the company successfully launched services in July of 2010 in Minneapolis.

• Municipal Curbside Recycling Contracts; 10 city contracts serving over 150,000 households with collection and/or processing services.

• Institutional Resource Management Contracting; including contracts with colleges, county buildings, public school districts, manufacturers and office buildings.

• Zero Waste Event Services (event recycling).

• Reclaimed wood manufacturing business.

• Online Residential Materials Exchange; 6 County Contracts serving 1,000,000 households in Minnesota as well as a contract with the State of Rhode Island.

• Restaurant Composting; Launched a commercial composting collection route for restaurants.

Specialties

Zero waste and sustainability planning, sustainable business planning, proforma development, strategic direction setting, composting, carbon quantification and footprint development, financing, contract negotiations, recycling operations, waste reduction, prevention, reuse, sustainable transportation, development and presentation of proposals, relationship building.
