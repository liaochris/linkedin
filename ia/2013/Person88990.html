Matthew Weaver, PMP, CSM, ITIL's Summary

Matthew Weaver specializes in project management, best practices, methodology definition, and process improvement. He has more than 30 years of experience that began with enlistment in the Navy, progressed through work for a variety of companies, and several forays into starting and running his own companies. Matthew’s experience involves all aspects of project, product, and development life cycles. In recent years he has shifted focus to teaching project management while still continuing to do project and proposal-related consulting work. Matthew will publish "A Project Methodology" in 2013.
