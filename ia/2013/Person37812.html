Andrew McGuinness' Summary

My focus is on meeting the client's objectives in the most effective, efficient, and timely manner possible under all of the circumstances. Having run a small business before attending law school, I take pains not to lose the business objective of my commercial clients. I am aggressive when it makes sense to be so, but always professional. I have found that this approach--and the judgment I have developed through law school, my First Circuit clerkship, and years of sophisticated commercial practice--enable to me to be the most effective advocate for my clients.
