Einar Ingebrigtsen's Summary

Passionate software developer with professional experience since 1994. Worked with everything ranging from games development on Playstation, PC and Xbox to enterprise LOB applications since 2002.

I'm passionate about code quality and highly scalable and decoupled systems.

Great experience with being team leader and architect, which has been my role for the most part since 1997, although my primary focus has been to do actual development. My goal is to never stop writing code, but rather lead by example.

Got awarded MVP; Microsoft Most Valued Professional for the DirectX / Xna product group in fall 2008. Later in 2009, Silverlight MVP.

Experience with what used to be known as Microsoft Surface, now PixelSense and general touch development.

Expert knowledge within Microsoft Azure and general cloud development, and also continuous deployment on AppHarbor, Heroku and now the latest Azure WebSite support

Runs several open source projects that I'm really passionate about :
Balder, 3D engine for Silverlight++ : http://balder.codeplex.com
Bifrost, enterprise library with focus on CQRS and MVVM : http://github.com/dolittle/bifrost
