Dan Evans' Experience

Partner

Site Altitude

April 2013 – Present (7 months) Beverly Hills, CA

Site Altitude was started by two Pepperdine Graduates a couple of years after they finished college. After spending some time in “Corporate America” and dealing with small and medium sized businesses, they realized that this was the direction of the future. The best days were the ones when they would interact with smaller business that concentrated on a niche product or service. That's when they saw an opportunity to facilitate the growing number of entrepreneurs and assist in their digital needs through web design and web development. Some words of advice that stuck with them from their mentors were “concentrate on what you are good at” and “less is more.”

Site Altitude was built on those words of advice. Many people think the opposite and attempt to be good at everything; however, when you look at most successful businesses, they are experts in their specialized fields. The digital space is Site Altitudes forte and they are always striving to help young and growing companies with their online needs.
