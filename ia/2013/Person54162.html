Hyer Bercaw's Summary

Avid evangelist for the customer voice and for data grounded decision making. Co-Founder and CEO of TrueModus. TrueModus is the only market research firm capable of delivering insights from consumers who are actively shopping and making real -- not hypothetical -- product decisions. We’re able to get these insights by helping consumers find the best product that meets their specific needs. We deliver insights that have helped our clients optimize pricing, design products, balance product lines, and develop messaging that resonates with customers. Our capabilities include advanced techniques such as conjoint analysis. TrueModus is able to deliver actionable results in as little as one month and at lower costs than other research providers.

A strong believer in giving back to the community via volunteer efforts. An active supporter of entrepreneurship with the Northwest Entrepreneurial Network and other Seattle area business organizations. Hyer supports the enjoyment and safety of the outdoors through the Washington Trails Association and Everett Mountain Rescue.
