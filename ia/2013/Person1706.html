Adam Zak's Summary

I'm Adam Zak, the Lean Executive Recruiter. For the last 20 years or so I've been helping organizations find and recruit exceptional leaders who can do a better job of running their operations. My clients have included small entrepreneurial companies (think: "Lean Start-Up"), mid-market and privately held organizations, and a good number of the Fortune 500.

The talented people I've recruited are the key, hands-on leaders you'd find in operations, supply chain, operational excellence and general management. The common link is my concentration on recruiting individuals who play a critical role in creating and building a culture of excellence in their organizations, based on principles of Lean (Toyota Production System, Operational Excellence/OpEx). It's about how they drive continuous improvement and strengthen the company's ability to execute more effectively across the whole enterprise.

Titles and labels are often confusing. But whether you call it Lean, OpEx, Continuous Improvement, Lean Six Sigma or, well, you get the picture, I know how this stuff works and I know how to help you identify, evaluate and attract exactly the right person for your organization.

During the last 24 months alone I've recruited people into roles as diverse as President to Plant Manager, VP Supply Chain to VP Strategy, Director Lean Six Sigma to VP Lean Operational Excellence, and more... Each was a leader who could size up the current state, create and communicate a future vision, align resources with objectives, and then execute to deliver successful outcomes.

I wrote a book too. Making complex things simple is often the first step in making a huge difference. Check it out, see if it helps you.

"Simple Excellence: Organizing and Aligning the Management Team in a Lean Transformation" (CRC Press, 2011), explains the critical roles senior managers must play in successfully achieving a company’s transformation to a Lean, operational excellence organization.

Specialties

I recruit executives on a retained basis, and I work personally and directly with each and every client. I'm really good at identifying and recruiting leaders who help transform companies based on the principles of "respect for people" and "continuous improvement."
