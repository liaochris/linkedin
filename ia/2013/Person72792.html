Kathie Nitz, CSA's Experience

Life Strategist

Strive to Thrive

2005 – Present (8 years)

Professionally-trained life & relationship coach Kathie Nitz helps her clients establish an unwavering sense of self so they can thrive in any relationship or environment. Kathie is passionate about helping others break patterns that no longer serve them and empowering them to live a life of choice. In addition to providing individual and family coaching, she delivers group coaching to corporations, small businesses and non-profit organizations.

As a caregiver to a mom with dementia, Kathie is honored to support fellow caregivers. By leveraging her coaching skills and personal experiences, she is able to introduce concepts that teach caregivers how to give care from a stronger, healthier and more capable place. This enables them to minimize the emotional drain, understand their role and recognize their choices. This helps them embrace the journey and find the many gifts within the challenges.

Being a Certified Senior Advisor allows Kathie to support families navigating the challenges of aging. Her forte is guiding and educating adult caregivers involved in eldercare. Kathie also encourages them to put their own life and financial planning in place at the same time. She helps her clients create a vision of inspired aging so they gain peace of mind and savor the rewards of advance, pro-active planning.

Kathie Nitz, CSA's Publications

  • Caring Cards®

    • Dr. Amy, Inc.
    • December 2010
    Authors: Kathie Nitz, CSA

    Inspired by her own caregiver journey, Kathie created Caring Cards® as a tool to promote meaningful connection with her aging mom and provide an opportunity to preserve her legacy. It was so effective that Kathie knew she had to share the tool with other caregivers. She teamed with Dr. Amy d'Aprix (who has exclusive licensing rights) to produce them.

    When caregivers are at their best while caring for another person they do more than just take care of physical needs and daily tasks. They engage their mind and lift their heart. Caring Cards® make it easy to explore people’s memories and wisdom -- and have more positive and meaningful conversation. Everyone can use some conversation inspiration! Caring Cards® come in a handy tuck box with more than 50 questions on a wide range of topics. Questions are open-ended to avoid simple ‘yes’ or ‘no’ answers.

    Caring Cards® have been very well received by family caregivers in addition to those who serve this community such as assisted living and skilled nursing communities, home health care agencies, financial institutions, scouting troops who visit the elderly and support groups to name a few. They’re great for people of all ages!

    They provide a great fund raising opportunity for non-profits. Contact Kathie directly if you would like more information on any of the above.
