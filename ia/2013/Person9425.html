Debbie Davis' Summary

My Castle Rock "Area Newsletters" are mailed monthly directly to almost 15,000 home in 12 major neighborhoods in town. Each ranging from 20-28 pages, they are a valuable tool for residents, and are well-read because they contain useful local information such as community events, classifieds, business opportunities, childcare/babysitter listing, and is supported by advertising from amazing local businesses wanting to reach the great people of Castle Rock, Colorado.

Ads are offered in a variety of sizes, formats and prices, such as Display Ads, Inserts, Advertorials/Articles, Business News write-ups and the very affordable "Homebase" section for people who work from home. Advertisers may choose to be in one, two or all (3) "Areas". There are no long-term contracts or commitments. Deadline: 10th of each month. Mails: around the 1st of each month.
Email me for more information: debbie@areanewsletters.com
Sign up to receive my "e-Newsletter":
http://www.areanewsletters.com/more/edelivery-areanewsletters.html

Specialties: Local advertising. I help businesses get the best local exposure within their budget so they may survive and thrive in this economy.
