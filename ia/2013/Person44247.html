Lauren Young's Summary

Whose motto is “Just do it”? Which company is represented by a red bullseye? How do others identify your business?

I love to help companies become memorable through brand marketing strategy and creative writing.

You’ll know it’s the right time to refer me when you hear:

• How much time should I put into social media to generate business?
• What’s the most effective way to spend my marketing dollars?
• Why do I continue to attract the wrong clients?

As a marketing resource, author and professional speaker, I share the secret to effective marketing on a small budget. Ideal clients come from the hospitality, food & beverage and event management industries.

You deliver the business challenge, and I will deliver a sweet reward!

The print and interactive collateral that is conceptualized by FBC will result in the following business gains:
