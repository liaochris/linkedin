Claire Bronson's Summary

Does your work have you bringing together diverse groups of people to learn, create shared futures, plan for them or have important conversations? Do you struggle with:

• How to use creative processes with your group to spur engagement and innovative thinking?
• Building teams that are focused, satisfying and whose members are committed to follow through?
• Capturing the key ideas that emerge in a meeting, workshop or conference and keeping people engaged and moving them forward?

Now imagine that your next event is celebrated as seminal, memorable and inspiring for your attendees and that they eagerly anticipate the next one. Or that your team comes together quickly, people are engaged and committed to getting the work done over the long haul.

If you share these goals, then I can help you reach them. I specialize in applying the power of visual thinking and creative engagement to support groups in seeing the big picture, making connections and creating innovative solutions. Teaming with internal and external consultants, I bring my unique background to collaborate with you so we can co-create remarkable experiences.

Seeing is believing. Visit my website to see how other clients have used the power of visual thinking and let's talk about your unique needs. We can meet in person or online and I'll help you see how this can work for your group.
