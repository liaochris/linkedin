Marcus Turner's Summary

Okay, so "think tank" was honestly the best one of the categories that LinkedIn had to describe what we do at Atomic Axis. We are the 20% of the 80/20 rule ... the 20% that is the first one to work and the last one to leave. The 20% that does 80% of the work. The 20% that innovates and continues to challenge the industry norms.

I have always been passionate about process and technology innovation. To this end, we have trademarked and are in the process of patenting our customer engagement --> delivery excellence process, Adaptive Action.

Adaptive Action is achieved when a business is capable of rapid and cost effective response to unanticipated challenges, events and opportunities. In order to achieve this level of agility, businesses need processes that facilitate speed and change while enabling greater levels of transparency. Instead of just reacting to change, we anticipate and plan ahead for new input and conditions. Understanding elements that influence change enables our team to make “on the fly adjustments” with minimal disruption.

Our processes and methodologies provide a comprehensive view of engagement and operational performance. When you’re armed with this agility and transparency, it’s much easier to keep each initiative closely aligned with the stated goals and overall business direction.
