Gregoire Gentil's Summary

Grégoire Gentil is a serial entrepreneur with five start-ups in fifteen years, he is an innovator at heart with a nothing-is-impossible attitude. Grégoire is currently the founder and CEO of Always Innovating, one of the most innovative design companies in the consumer electronics space with multiple breakthrough innovations. Prior to that, he co-founded Twingo Systems, which was introducing the concept of security by virtualization and was eventually acquired by Cisco Systems. Grégoire graduated from Ecole Polytechnique in France, from Stanford in USA, and is an alumni from McKinsey&Company.

Specialties

Engineering (software and hardware), Management.

Gregoire Gentil's Experience

Founder

Always Innovating, Inc.

June 2008 – Present (5 years 5 months)

Always Innovating is one of the most innovative consumer electronics design companies. The company has released revolutionary versatile new devices such as the Touch Book and the Smart Book which can be used as a netbook, a tablet, and a MID (Mobile Internet Device) at the same time. Those innovations have shaped the consumer electronics trends and are a reference in the industry. The company currently have several breakthrough innovations in its lab, protected by multiple pending patents. Discover more at http://www.alwaysinnovating.com.

Founder

Zonbu

February 2006 – Present (7 years 9 months)

Grégoire launched and continues to oversee Zonbu, the first environmentally-responsible computer designed for consumers. Zonbu hardware was the first computer to get the EPEAT gold rating back in 2006. Additionally, Zonbu software was the first consumer-friendly operating system with a cloud-based storage system - think it like Google ChromeOS but five years ago. Discover more at http://www.zonbu.com.

Senior Manager

Cisco Systems

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; CSCO; Computer Networking industry

April 2004 – April 2006 (2 years 1 month)

Grégoire was leading the integration of the Twingo Systems technology into the Cisco offering including IOS, firewall and routers.

CEO / co-Founder

Twingo Systems

September 2001 – April 2004 (2 years 8 months)

Grégoire co-founded Twingo, which was introducing the concept of security by virtualization. This innovation led to the main security defense mechanisms used by most anti-virus solutions against zero-day attacks. Twingo Systems was acquired by Cisco Systems in 2004 and the technology was integrated into the main Cisco firewall and router product lines.

Co-Founder

Edena SA

September 1999 – June 2002 (2 years 10 months)

Associate

McKinsey & Company

Partnership; 10,001+ employees; Management Consulting industry

April 1998 – January 2000 (1 year 10 months)

Grégoire participated to strategy consulting engagements for developing product strategy, growth strategy, operations transformation, operations and supply chain strategy, and post-merger management. Grégoire got experience in multiple industries such as high-tech, consumer goods, and banking.

Founder

Pilot PLUS

July 1997 – May 1998 (11 months)

Grégoire was the founder of Pilot PLUS, the editor of top selling applications for the PalmPilot. Pilot PLUS was acquired by Hands-High, another software leader for the Palm family. Pilot PLUS was started by Grégoire to pay his Stanford tuitions (!) and ended up as a big Palm software story.

Project Manager

Liris Interactive

June 1996 – June 1997 (1 year 1 month)

Project Manager

French Museum Laboratory

February 1995 – April 1996 (1 year 3 months)

Grégoire worked at Le Louvre in the French Museum Laboratory. He was the co-author of a CD-ROM (yes, remember that before Internet was mainstream!!!) on Camille Corot. This CD-ROM was published for the retrospective of the 19th century pre-impressionist painter in Paris (Louvre), New York (MET), and Ottawa (National Gallery).

For this CD-ROM, Grégoire adapted a laser system to create and show in 3D the pictorial layer of a painting. This innovation was a first worldwide breakthrough, was recognized by the curator establishment, and received a special award.
