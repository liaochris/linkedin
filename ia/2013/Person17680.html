Brian Powers' Summary

Full time Realtor with Jeff Glover & Associates at Keller Williams Macomb St.Clair. Our team is an aggressive, results driven group of Realtors that was recently ranked one of the top 100 Real Estate Teams in the United States by the Wall Street Journal. We were the #1 Keller Williams team in Michigan for homes sold in 2012. Our team sold 456 homes in 2012.

In addition to my own business endeavors, I sit on the Agent Leadership Council (ALC) at our Macomb-St.Clair Market Center. I am also Past Chairman of the Board of Directors at the Anchor Bay Chamber of Commerce and a current Board Member.
