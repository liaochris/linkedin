Ed Lefkowicz's Summary

Compelling images for businesses, non-profits and the media that help my clients tell their stories, engage their clients and make them money. I’m easy and fun to work with, from concept through completion, and I understand time and budgetary constraints. I have a digital sense with analog sensibility, I seldom run with scissors, and channel Rembrandt and MacGyver. I travel well, and don’t wrinkle in transit.
