John Birkenhead's Summary

Senior independent property/casualty (non-life) insurance actuary.

Acting as an independent consulting actuary to captives, brokers and UK local authorities on risk retention, risk management and risk communication.

Partner-level specialist in insurance, risk and financial management. Advice on insurance program design, insurance schemes, insurance pricing, data and MI.

Previously Partner at Mercer - developed and implemented 'greenfield' site business plan, for new consultancy practice. Acquired almost 100 new clients in 3 years.

Have been lead actuarial advisor for over 200 projects.

Specialties

Property/casualty (non-life) insurance. All lines of business : EL/PL/Warranty/Property/Motor/Med Mal/PA/Off-shore/On-shore. Data management, data analysis, risk retention analysis, loss reserving, actuarial sign-off, broking assistance. Analysing and maximising profitability and loyalty of insurance customers. Industry experience includes telecommunication companies, high-street retailers, motor manufacturers, public sector and governmental agencies
