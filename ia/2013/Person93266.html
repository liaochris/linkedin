Michel Devos' Summary

Independant Business Consultant ready to help you improving your production performances:
- Collect and model end-users requirements, using requirement engineering methodology
- Building your strategic plan in the context of Manufacturing Execution Performances covering domains such as Organization, Business Processes, CyberSecurity and Industrial IT Architecture.
- Preparing a request for proposal, describing features required for your Production Management (MES/MOM) including production planning and scheduling , SCADA and/or PLC applications
- Valorisation of the benefits expected for the project (R.O.I.)
- Follow-up of implementation and rolling out according to end user specifications

Initiative for better energy performance of your production assets
- Creating the Production Process Map
- Specifying initiatives for improvements in energy performance
- Coupling with LEAN Manufacturing and 6-SIGMA initiatives

MESA Authorized Instructor in the context of MESA Global Educational Program (http://www.mesa.org). Courses in French or English. AREVA (Paris) is the first to host a program in French.

30 years of experience in industrial software products (SCADA, DCS, MES) development and implementation for manufacturers and producers in various industries such as metals and food, is the key factor of success.

More on http://www.mesconsult.be

Michel Devos' Projects

  • Strategic plan organisation and industrial IT for a chemical plant (Belgium)

    • June 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Michel Devos

    The objective of the mission (supported by the Walloon region) is to elaborate a strategic plan covering domains such as organization, business processes and industrial IT architecture in order to improve the operational excellence.
    The first step is today to analyse the current situation.
    Depending on the goals defined during the requirement engineering phase, the mission will recommend implementation of solutions (Scheduling, MES activities according to ISA 95 activity model).

  • MESA - ROI Guidebook working group

    • October 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Michel Devos, Darren Riley

    In 2010, a team was initiated to develop a MESA ROI Guidebook to explore investment metrics and models and the steps to building and presenting a business case to secure investment funding for manufacturing IT and enterprise solutions projects.

  • Strategic plan industrial IT and organization for water utility major

    • January 2012 to September 2012
    Team Members: Michel Devos, Valentijn de Leeuw, Jean Vieille, Bertrand Ricque

    In collaboration with members from the Control Chain Group, we made a strategic plan for industrial IT and automation, business process improvement, improvement of the governance process, and an organizational architecture. PARTICEPS took care of organization, governance and participated in redefining business processes. MES consult worked on IT architecture and Business processes.

  • Opportunity Study in a textile company (Belgium)

    • December 2012 to June 2013
    Team Members: Michel Devos

    The goal of the mission is to evaluate opportunities to improve manufacturing process in the context of continuous improvement initiative. Mission recommendations were related to modifications in the organisation of the team managing customers' orders and implementation of MES features to support changes in the business processes and reports of new KPI.

  • Club-MES : content working group

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Michel Devos, Philippe Allot, Omer AKDENIZ

    The Content working group intends to create documentation (written in French) on M.E.S. that will be available to industrials who are preparing projects in their manufacturing facilities.
