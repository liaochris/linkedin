Mark Herring's Summary

I’m a highly experienced copywriter who can do everything from a snappy line to a suitable tone of voice to a seamless integrated campaign.

I understand requirements quickly, work efficiently and get the job done with a minimum of fuss whether it's print, digital, experiential, social or, even better, everything at the same time.

Big ideas for brand activation? Check. Exceptional experiences? No problem. Not to mention the award-winning web sites and online campaigns. Ads for TV, cinema, radio and plenty of press. Tone of voice development. Brand film scripts. Tons - literally - of shopper marketing, DM and brochures (the chances are you’ve ‘recycled’ something I’ve worked on). Football and business journalism. Film reviews. Editing, planning content, ensuring compliance... and that's just before lunch.

RECENT WORK INCLUDES:

- Tone of voice development, web site and brochures for Bentley

- Tone of voice, UK & US web sites, brochures and trade comms for Exact Online business software

- A range of digital experiential projects for the new Range Rover Sport and Range Rover Evoque

- Tone of voice, brand story, Place Book and web site for the transformational development project at Wembley Park

- White papers, guides and web copy on Bluetooth certification, device interoperability and NFC technology for UL Verification Services

- B2B emails, landing pages and case studies for Google Apps for Business

- New tone of voice and new 2014 brochure for Amber Lounge, the ultra-exclusive, global F1 Grand Prix party for drivers, movie stars and royalty (but not copywriters)

- Bags of shopper marketing, brand activation, ads, scripts, key visuals, tool kits and "stuff" for a variety of Diageo and other FMCG brands: Pepsi, Tanqueray, Gordon's, Sky, Cathedral City, TK Maxx...

- The Sky Studios experience at the O2 Arena

- Online Corporate Social Responsibility review for Standard Chartered Bank
