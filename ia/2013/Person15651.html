Gaston Bonaudi's Summary

Meet Us Today.

Bonaudi, is an award-winning industrial design and manufacturing firm that offers a unique approach to add value to your enterprise, based on our flexibility to provide you a plan that can fit your budget, regardless of how big or small your firm is.

We have designed over 400 products, from concept to shelf, in a variety of product categories.
We offer a high quality affordable integrated design service. Our services cover research, strategic planning, creative solutions, industrial design, mechanical engineering, electronics design, interface design, prototyping and manufacturing supervision.

We are a design consultancy with professional and enthusiastic product design team.
A multidisciplinary, multi-sectored product design, development and manufacturing consultancy that can be the missing piece of the puzzle in your product launching plans.

We have a successful history of helping clients to achieve their objectives in markets across the globe, including US, Europe, South America and Asia.

We can assist you from concept to product launch. Our experience enables to help companies access to new markets that are impossible to reach without high-quality, specifically tailored product design.
