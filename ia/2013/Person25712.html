Cliff Pavlovic's Projects

  • Comparative study of technology transfer units

    • April 2010 to November 2010
    Team Members: Cliff Pavlovic

    Mandated to perform a study comparing technology transfer between Quebec (Canada) and Flanders (Belgium), specifically between 2 technology transfer units. Goal was to understand what allows for efficient and successful transfer of technology from the academic world to the market.

    The report was presented to various technology transfer organizations as well as government ministries (such as the MDEIE in Quebec).

    The study was commissioned by Gestion Univalor and sponsored by Gestion Univalor, K.U.Leuven Research & Development and the MDEIE.
