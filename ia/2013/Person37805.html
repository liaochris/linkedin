Drew Litton's Summary

I own my own creative services company, Littoonz Studios LLC which is dedicated to creating graphic content in new and exciting ways for an ever changing media climate.
I'm also a nationally syndicated cartoonist with Universal Press Syndicate and a weekly contributor specializing in sports cartoons for the Chicago Tribune. I am a frequent contributor to ESPN.com, Mile High Sports magazine, as well as various other print and online clients throughout the United
States. I specialize in all kinds of Comic Art, Animation, Humorous Illustration, Cartooning, Character Design and Story Boarding. My clients range from the Colorado Rockies Baseball Club to the School of Rock.
I find the most joy in creating content for multiple platforms including broadcast, internet and mobile technology, illustration for children's books, greeting cards, graphic novels and character design and love making my clients happy.
My sports editorial cartoons can be seen at http://www.drewlitton as well as weekly in the Chicago Tribune. I served as the the sports editorial cartoonist for the Rocky Mountain News in Denver Colorado from 1982 until the paper ceased publication in 2009. In 1993, I won the National Cartoonists Society award for sports cartooning.

I am also the creator of Transitory Flight about the Art of Reinvention (www.transitoryflight.com) Creators Incubator (http:www.creatorsincubator.wordpress.com) about the process of creativity. and DrewLitton.com where You can see my sports cartoons.

I currently reside in the Dallas Ft. Worth area.

Specialties

Animation, Cartoonist, Illustrator, Adobe CS3 Photo shop, Flash, Illustrator, Soundtrack Pro, After Effects, caricature, illustration, greeting cards, Children's books, character design, toy design, cartoon maps, sports and editorial cartoons.
