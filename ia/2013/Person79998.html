Lelia Marcus' Summary

My passion is helping you to meet your fitness goals with a custom-designed, flexible and effective program that suits your needs and schedule. Sessions can be arranged at your gym, home or office. Drawing on 15+ years as an AFAA certified personal trainer, I have worked with clients of all ages, supporting a wide range of goals: to lose or manage weight, pre/post natal fitness, toning up, and post physical therapy maintenance. My approach is to start gently and gradually increase the intensity without injury--especially to your motivation. My goal is to help you make permanent healthy changes in order to improve your level of fitness for the rest of your life.

Specialties

Weight Loss, Weight Management, Executive Fitness Training, Post Rehabilitation Workouts, Prenatal Workouts, Postnatal Workouts, Pregnancy Exercise, Geriatric Fitness, Toning Up, Health Improvement, Personal Training
