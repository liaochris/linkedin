Larry Compton's Summary

Larry Compton, FVS' Operations Manager and Certified Forensic Video Analyst, is a former New York State Peace Officer and Municipal Police Instructor. Mr. Compton is widely recognized as an industry leading expert in the acquisition of Digital Multimedia Evidence.

Larry helped to establish one of the first law enforcement digital evidence labs in New York State. He is a former member of the FBI Southern Tier Cyber Predator Task Force, and the NYS Digital Multimedia Evidence Technical Working Group.

During Operations Desert Shield and Desert Storm (Gulf War) Larry served with the 82nd Airborne Division, and was later named as Team Chief of the 82nd's Emergency Operations Communications Center. He has over twenty years of experience designing, installing and maintaining secure voice, data, and video networks.

In July, 2013 Larry started his own consulting business, and he is transitioning to work full-time for the new business effective January 1, 2014. He will remain full-time with FVS throughout 2013, and is honored to say FVS has offered to keep him on board through a consulting and/or retainer arrangement thereafter.
