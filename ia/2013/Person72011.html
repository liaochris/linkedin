Karel Mořický's Summary

Starting as "Operation Flashpoint" community modder, I was hired by title's developer Bohemia Interactive and participated on creating following games every since.

While I still enjoy doing some level and game design myself, I prefer to support the team by sharing skills, providing ready-to-use solutions and helping everyone to reach required level of quality.

'Game Development' leads many to focus solely on making the 'Game' better, forgetting to improve also the 'Development' bit. My constant goal is to make sure both parts receives same attention.

Specialities:
• Implementation of end-to-end design solutions
• Sandbox game design
• Technical documentation
