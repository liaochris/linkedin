Juliet McEwen Johnson's Summary

In addition to preparing homes for sale, Juliet also shared her burgeoning expertise on all things web 2.0 as social media sites arrived to augment blogging, forum participation and traditional SEO with exciting results: like #1 on Google's Page 1, +

✔ 3 yrs as a professional voice-over artist - in New York City - voicing promos for CNN World, Sirius Satellite Channel 21 (First Wave), ABC Radio Networks, the Cartoon Network's 'Adult Swim' (which were always very rude) and many more, +

✔ 15 yrs of corporate even planning and meeting planning at trade shows, corporate events, (e.g. picnics for 14,000 - 25,000 people) and street fairs into the online visibility arena
