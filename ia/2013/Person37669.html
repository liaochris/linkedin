Dr. David L. Lawrence, RFC, AIF's Summary

Dr. David Lawrence, RFC®, AIF® has over twenty-seven years experience in the financial services profession and 37+ years of business experience, including successful ownership of six different businesses during that time.

He spent 18 years with a major financial planning firm as a Senior Financial Advisor, Training Manager and District Manager. He has also worked for two large independent financial planning and asset management companies in senior management positions.

One of the most respected efficiency experts in America, Mr. Lawrence has been recognized for his efforts by numerous organizations such as The U.S. Housing and Urban Development (HUD), The Volunteer Center for the United Way, The Internal Revenue Service, The Securities Industry & Financial Markets Association (SIFMA), The International Association for Registered Financial Consultants (IARFC), The National Displaced Homemaker Network and The Financial Planning Association (FPA), to name a few. He has been frequently quoted by such national publications as Barron's, Financial Planning Interactive, USA Today, and The Wall Street Journal Online among others. He has also made frequent appearances on NBC and FOX television affiliates.

He holds the coveted designations of Registered Financial Consultant and Accredited Investment Fiduciary™. Conversant in five languages, David currently writes a monthly column on business efficiency for Financial Advisor Magazine (www.FA-Mag.com). He is a past Chair of the FPA’s National Leadership Council.

David is the Founder and President of EfficientPractice.com, an operational efficiency consulting firm with clients all across America and other parts of the world. He hosts the Efficient Practice Advisor Network, a subscription-based archives of thousands of resources for financial advisory firms. (www.efficientpractice.com).

Specialties

Operational Efficiency Consulting in four distinct areas of business operations:
1.) Management - which includes Coaching and strategic planning (Blue Ocean, Six Sigma, etc.) and employee management.
2.) Technology Consulting
3.) Process Development and training
4.) Design - which includes efficient office space utilization, harmonious office envrionment and branding.
