Human Capital... Real Intelligence

Our team maintains over a 90% success rate on offers by understanding both our clients' needs and the needs of the talent we represent. By focusing on making the right placement every time, our clients have been able to retain much of the talent we helped them find over the years.

What sets us apart from other agencies:

2) We partner with our clients, we aren't just a vendor. By setting expectations together, we help you make smart decisions regarding project-based and full-time talent acquisition.

3) We work within our core strengths, not over extending ourselves to more business than we can handle. Thus, we are the top supplier for our clients, with relationships going back decades.
