Jose Colmenares' Summary

Accomplished Human Resources Executive with over 20 years of Leadership coaching, process improvement, and Customer Service delivery experience.

As a member of the Southwest Airlines People Department for 11 years, Jose lead the recruiting and on-boarding efforts for several departments in the company including the Flight Attendant and Ground Operations groups as well as several Headquarters departments. During his last four years at Southwest Airlines he created and lead the "People Service Center" that provides Human Resources Customer support to all Southwest Airlines Employees nationwide.

After Southwest Airlines Jose spent 9 years with AutoNation - America's largest automotive retailer. During his first 5 years he lead all the Human Resources functions for the Texas Region as Regional Director of Human Resources. He then took on the task of creating a nation wide recruiting and on-boarding program for the company as the Director of Recruiting and On-boarding based in Fort Lauderdale Florida.

Jose relocated back to Texas, attained his Professional Business Coach certification and started a consulting practice to coach executives and business owners in all aspects of operations’ Leadership as well as providing other strategic Human Resources solutions.

Jose then joined Conifer Health Solutions as a Human Resources Director. Conifer Health is one of the most experienced business process management providers in healthcare with more than 30 years of healthcare operations management experience.
