Colt Cooper's Summary

A technical consultant with deep proficiency with Navitaire's New Skies platform (www.navitaire.com). While most of my work is done in and around SkySales, I also have experience extending Navitaire applications such as SkySpeed and SkyUtilities (SkyManager) and writing custom applications based on both the raw and published Navitaire APIs.

I've worked with New Skies from release 1.2 through the current 3.x release. If you have need of New Skies conversion or customization help, please contact me directly at: contact@coltcooper.com.

Specialties

Navitaire New Skies Platform (Airline Reservation System)

I was brought in to lead a team of entry-level developers to build the SkyBus SkySales-based website. A fair amount of ingenuity

I also built a custom low-fare calendar for SkyBus to meet their corporate vision of how availability searches should happen on the web. Each of these items had a significant effect on SkyBus revenue, earning/saving the airline millions.
