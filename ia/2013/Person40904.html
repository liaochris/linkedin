Elliott Hauser's Summary

I figure things out.

I teach programming, research collaborative systems like Github, and founded Coursefork to help educators like me share and collaborate on open course materials. I advise other startup founders informally and through UNC's Launching the Venture program.

My research and teaching are funded by a Royster Fellowship. I completed a M.S. in Information Science from UNC in May 2012. I studied Art History at Duke as an undergrad, and my undergraduate research on the economics of art auctions was published in 2006.
