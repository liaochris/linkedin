Charlie Sharp's Summary

Tough times require focus, tenacity, and new skills. If you or your team need to know more about salesforce.com or become a certified Administrator, Developer or Consultant, you need help from someone who has combined technology, experience, and common sense.

As a salesforce.com Instructor and Consultant with 25 years of sales experience, I have helped companies survive hard times and flourish in good times.

My past sales process improvement, Product Management, program facilitation and coaching have given me even greater insight into today’s sales challenges and strategies to overcome them. I have seen what has failed and succeeded. My background helps the technical Administrator Developer or Consultant better understand salesforce.com to make their job more rewarding and beneficial to their customers.

I can also enrich the end user learning experience to best utilize the power of saleforce.com.
If you plan on becoming certified in any area of saleforce.com, my Company, Stony Point, Inc. can help.

Eight years ago I changed my life. I lost 110 pounds and have kept it all off. The result is a leader with 25 years of experience and the energy level of a 25 year old.

To stay ahead of the technology curve I continue my education including a course in social media as well as further studies and certifications with salesforce.com. Be it Linkedin, facebook, Twitter or blogs, I am doing it all.

Volunteerism has been an important part of my life. As a member of two Boards of Education I helped shape educational policy. As a volunteer EMT I responded to the World Trade disaster on 9/11. Animal rescue is now tops on my list.

Specialties: Training, Sales, Managing accounts, creating and uncovering opportunities, and closing sales.
