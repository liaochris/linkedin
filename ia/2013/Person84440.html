Talk Radio is what Malcolm calls the 'Truth Meter of America'... it is a unique medium that encourages involvement and allows for a free-flowing dialogue and exchange of ideas. Malcolm Out Loud brings his unique perspective to this changing medium in areas that encompass the headlines of the day. News, politics, society, culture are all areas that Malcolm weighs in on.
