Keith Smiley's Summary

I am currently an attorney at Tadlock Law Firm. My practice incudes both plaintiff and defense side in patent litigation, trademark opposition in front of the TTAB, and business dispute collections in Federal Court.

I began practicing in 2009 with the Bertrand Law firm. While there I had the opportunity to practice in various areas of law including family law, probate, criminal law, juvenile law, contracts, debt settlement, landlord tenant law and client negotiations.

In a pro bono matter for The Human Rights Initiative, I also successfully defended a Christian Pakistani family in their application for asylum to avoid religious persecution in their home country.
