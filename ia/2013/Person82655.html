Lou Hughes' Summary

Lou Hughes is Principal and Chief Marketing Officer of Moving Minds®, a strategic marketing and web consultancy.

Serving as a CMO On Demand to a diverse group of clients ranging from early stage start-ups to established Fortune 500 companies, Hughes leads mission-critical, strategic marketing engagements, demand generation programs and digital marketing initiatives that consistently increase market valuation, revenue and demand.

Hughes is highly sought after for his ability to drive transformation and innovation across large, complex global organizations in the areas of branding & repositioning, digital enablement, e-commerce, marketing systems and supply chain.

Current and former clients include MasterCard, Siemens, HealthSouth, Caremark Rx, Fannie Mae and Baptist Health.
