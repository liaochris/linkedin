Ron Martin's Summary

Acoustic Design was founded by Christopher Sterle and Ron Martin nearly 10 years ago. Together, Christopher and Ron have over 35 years of experience in the Audio, Video and Automation Industry. They are active members of CEDIA, Crave, CEA, TechHome Builder, Southern Nevada Home Builders Association, the Better Business Bureau and are THX Installer Certified. With Ron being a born and raised Las Vegas native and Christopher having been in the valley for over 13 years, they both bring a unique and professional service to our local community. The entire Acoustic Design Team love what they do and are constantly improving their knowledge by attending industry courses to stay on the leading edge of products and technology.

Acoustic Design's goal is to turn every “job” into a lifelong relationship. No matter the size of the job, everyone is treated with the same level of respect and customer service. We take pride in the way we treat our clients, and our long list of satisfied client testimonials proves it. http://www.acousticdesignlv.com/testimonials.html It is for this reason that the majority of our business is either from client referral or return business.

With Acoustic Design’s team of experts, we are able to take a project from conception through to completion. We strive to meet and exceed our client’s expectations by engineering reliable Integrated Audio, Video and Automation Systems that are both simple to use and perform impressively, no matter the size. Using our collective abilities and experience, along with a proven line-up of electronics manufacturers, our engineered systems provide repeatable reliability, lasting value and impressive performance. We are continually investigating the latest innovations in the industry to ensure that our clients are getting the most out of their investment. We pre-build all of our engineered systems in-house to ensure that when they are integrated into our client’s project they perform perfectly.
