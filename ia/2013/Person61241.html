Jeffrey Liss' Summary

Whether as a management consultant or corporate executive, my work has centered on leading strategic ventures in complex business environments, with my current focus being in the rapidly changing world of omnichannel retail and customer experience initiatives.

At Ascena Retail Group, Inc., a leading retail holding company, I lead Digital Services, a shared services organization whose mission is to accelerate Ascena’s retail brands toward achieving omnichannel pre-eminence in the women’s retail apparel sector. It includes a digital marketing center of expertise, omnichannel initiative leadership and customer insights .

As a strategic retail leader, I share the excitement of what achieving omnichannel excellence can deliver for our customers as well as the company, and enjoy tackling the strategic and technical challenges this journey entails. I have spoken on this topic at various forums, including Argyle Executive Forums, Retail Marketing Association and the National Retail Federation. I also serve on the Research Committee for shop.org, the eCommerce division of the National Retail Federation.

In my prior role, I led e-commerce operations across three brands, including customer care, fulfillment, merchandise planning and finance. I also launched a customer insights function focusing on actionable business-oriented insights via qualitative and quantitative research, analysis of customer data, and a psychographic segmentation study of the plus-size women’s apparel market that was an important component in a brand turnaround and revenue generation for a multi-brand retail organization.

Other work included launching two new retail chains, acquiring and divesting businesses, leading a multi-million dollar overhead transformation program, and overseeing the strategic planning for three large retail chains.

Previous experience was as a management consultant, leading large initiatives for clients in various industries.
