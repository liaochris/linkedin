Duke Marr's Summary

Broad-based ecommerce and social media leader with fifteen years experience in marketing, product management, merchandising, technology, user experience, strategy and brand development. Successful management of multi-million dollar budgets against strict revenue goals for $1B+ ecommerce companies. Established track record of increasing conversion and revenue through innovative product and marketing initiatives.

Dynamic manager with superb communication skills, having led diverse teams ranging from brand presidents to product directors and creative directors to marketing managers and user experience architects. Eight years experience as a VP/Director/Manager, including hiring, mentoring, and realigning teams. Passionate about building multi-disciplinary high performance teams, both within and across business units and brands.

Specialties

Ecommerce, Product Management, User Experience, Social Media, Vendor Management, Merchandising, Searchandising, Funnel Optimization, A/B Testing, Usability, Web Analytics, Rich Media, Content Management.
