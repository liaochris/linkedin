Kristian Norling's Publications

  • Enterprise Search and Findability Report 2012

    • Findwise
    • June 30, 2012
    Authors: Kristian Norling, Maria Johansson, Caroline Abrahamsson, Mattias Ellison

    This report is based on an open web survey conducted March to May 2012. 170 respondents (all practitioners) have provided unique responses. Represented are many organisations (66%) with more than 1,000 employees from 28 countries globally, primarily from Europe and North America. This report provides a unique insight into how search is currently being managed, or rather is not being managed, in the best interest of the organisation.

  • Enterprise Search and Findability Survey Report

    • Findwise
    • June 28, 2013
    Authors: Kristian Norling, Frida Linghammar

    The second annual Enterprise Search and Findability Survey is aimed at investigating how search is being managed and used globally. Over a hundred Enterprise Search practitioners (mainly from Europe and North America) have responded, providing unique insight into how search is currently being managed. In this survey we focus on the practitioner view of Enterprise Search, hence search vendors and integrators have been excluded, resulting in 101 unique responses from 18 countries.
