Chase Jones' Summary

I am a self-taught, web, mobile, and social media site designer. It all started in the basement of my parents house in Liberty, Utah when my dad needed help getting online. Needless to say, I have spent countless hours reading books, scimming online forums, watching YouTube videos, and messing with computer design programs to be able to provide services that not only do I love, but services that I see are valuable and irreplaceable. Not later after designing some websites for my dad and his business partners, I created CBJ Marketing and Design, a company dedicated to helping companies create an online identity.

Being the owner of CBJ Marketing and Design has it's perks. I have been blessed with the opportunity to travel the world while working with some outstanding individuals and companies. I started out in the industry designing custom Facebook fan pages for people such as Brad Barton, Doug Nielsen, and Dan Clark, world-renown speakers. I then began designing web sites for companies based out of Utah such as VEO Naturals and Empowered Naturally. During this time, I was flying out of the country to give seminars (in different languages) on my experience and expertise in the industry to young entrepreneurs and college students. After working for a year with local companies, I began to pick up accounts throughout the United States such as ACE Hardware, John Kim Consulting, Club Photo Booth, and Attorney Journal of San Diego. Since then, I have landed jobs for companies throughout the world.

A big shout out to those people that have helped me become who I am today and for those amazing individuals and companies that have trusted me with their online identity.
