qShift Travel Nurses' Summary

OUR MISSION:
qShift Travel Nurses is one of the leading travel nurse companies. We offer competive pay, great benefits, nationwide job placement, and are among the most highly ranked nursing job agencies. It is our goal to be your only choice.

ABOUT US
Travel Nursing offers unmatched opportunity to the nursing profession. New nurses are bringing their services to travel nursing immediately after they have met the industry requirements of experience. Experienced Health Care Professionals are consistently moving into the travel profession as well realizing the benefit of travel, locations, compensation and a personal commitment to them providing unparalleled satisfaction.

At q Shift we offer outstanding locations with a personal emphasis on YOU. Many talk personal commitment, few follow through. q Shift is dedicated to advocacy. Our action on behalf of you and our client facilities is immediate and critical to your and our success.

Thank you for your interest and please know, when you travel with q Shift Travel Nurses, you have made a decision & chosen the leader in advocacy, support, and total compensation focusing on you personally, professionally and financially.

Specialties

We offer RN, LPN, CNA, Tech, and Radiology professionals great pay, excellent travel nurising opportunities, and some of the best benefits in the industry. Because we are a nurse-owned company, we know full well the what is important to you and your career. Let q Shift provide you with the respect you deserve, the compensation rates that you have earned, and the freedom that comes with being a travel nurse.
