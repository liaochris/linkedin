Accounting

Bernco Distributors

1991 – 1997 (6 years) Greater Salt Lake City Area

Straight out of high school I was asked by my father to take over accounting. At the time that was accounts payable, accounts receivable, and invoicing clients.

When I started we did this all by hand. Hand written ledgers, invoices, checks etc. And I knew that had to change.

Our first attempt to bring computers into the company was with a software package called CYMA, a professional accounting software. We worked with that platform for a while, but quickly grew out of it.

Next we brought in Touchstone/2000, . This was an amazing shift for us as we were now able to track and order inventory, and automate many proceses and therefore save time and money. We also launched cloud computing in our entire business using Citrix, and set up our distribution location as well as our retail location to be able to tie into the same real time database. This made a night and day difference for our company!
