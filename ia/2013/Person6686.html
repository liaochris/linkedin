Andre Morton's Summary

Brooklyn born and bred with a background in sports and music, André Morton is a film producer who specializes in full production services. As a cinematographer, André has shot narratives, documentaries, music videos and commercial spots.

From high-end productions with large budgets to time-crunched, micro-budget shorts, AMP consistently delivers story-driven content with striking imagery.

Our client list includes:

The Lifetime Network, BET, Jay -Z, Roc Nation, OK Go, VH-1, MTV, 4th Row Films, Random House, Crown Royal, Clockwise Productions, Mount Sinai and Astra Zeneca.

Specialties

Content Creation, Production Services, Cinematography and Editorial Services.
