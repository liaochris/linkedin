Loren Overholt's Summary

I am experienced graphic designer with over 10 years of experience in advertising, print and logo design. My background comes from a solid foundation of concept and composition from my education in fine art. And I have extensive experience in a production environment. These two traits have provided me with a well rounded foundation of creativity balanced with a strong sense of being detail orientated.

My strength is being highly flexible and resourceful with experience working on every aspect of a project from planning, designing and managing. As well as, working on a project from start to finish and working closely with clients. I believe customer service is an important factor in graphic design. And I make an effort to better understand a clients business and needs.

My goal as a graphic designer is to provide the best possible idea and create a design that is effective.
