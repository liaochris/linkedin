Darla Pompilio's Summary

Darla Pompilio is a professional organizer and founder of Your Tasks - Our Time, LLC located in Bucks County, PA. Your Tasks - Our Time offers a wide range of Professional Organizing services designed to increase your peace of mind, reduce your stress and bring harmony to your home, office and life. We reduce the clutter and implement organizational systems designed for the way you live and work. We give home and business owners, in Bucks, Montgomery and Philadelphia counties the freedom to focus on their priorities.

Darla founded Your Tasks-Our Time in 2008 as a way to help others and fully utilize her innate and corporate polished organizing skills. "With 20+ years of experience in corporate and customer services she had the skills and knowledge to make it work, but more importantly she had the passion and the personality. When a client works with us they know they are in good hands, they are being cared for and the job will be done right the first time."

Your Tasks - Our Time is fully insured to guarantee the value and security of our clients' personal belongings. We offer a wide variety of organizing services for the home and office.

Residential services include de-cluttering and organizing your entire home or individual rooms including closets, laundry rooms, basements, attics, and storage units.

Moving services including de-cluttering and organizing your home before it's placed on the market. Sorting, downsizing and packing after the sale of the home and new home set-up which consists of unpacking and organizing your new home.

Business organizing services include paper and time management, electronic file management, space management and workflow systems.
