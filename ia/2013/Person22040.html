Charlene Kingston's Summary

I can help your business grow!

Online Business Presence

Does your business need a strong online presence? I explain how the world of online business works, identify the strengths and weaknesses of your current presence, and help you get from where you are to where you want to be.

I can help you with websites, blogs, email lists, social media, business photography, mobile, and more.

I can show you how to attract people who love what you do! You can reach more people by expanding your company's products to include information products like ebooks, webinars, courses, autoresponders, membership sites, and more.

Content marketing is about educating people. It's the most effective way to bring new people into your online business community because it's about helping not selling.

Social Media

Confused by the social media options? Not getting the results you want? Let me cut through the noise to give you the straight story on social media, what it can and cannot do, and how to use it for your business.

I can help you find the tools that showcase your business strengths to your target audience. I'll help you find the conversation topics that turn casual visitors into clients and fans.

Get my free ebook, Getting Started With Social Media (http://socialmediadiyworkshop.com/subscribe/).
