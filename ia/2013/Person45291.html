Spencer Ford's Summary

I am a Graphic Designer living in London, Ontario. I graduated after three years of Graphic Design study at Fanshawe College and have since been working as a graphic designer on a multitude of projects. As a well-rounded designer I design a range of mediums including brand identities, promotional pieces, web design and production, brief multimedia and package design.

Along with working as a Graphic Designer, I have pursued multiple unique opportunities to compliment my career. At an early age, I took on the massive responsibility of guarding lives and later delved into the most intense customer service challenges you can take on at a young age: call center technical support. From there, I have worked as a bartender for the amazing experience in teamwork and the intense pace of the service industry.

All of these experiences have aided my goal of becoming what a great designer should be: a detail-oriented, driven, reliable, pace-focused designer working strong within a team.
