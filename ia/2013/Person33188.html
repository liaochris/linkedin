Joshua R. Beal*, MBA's Summary

Schwarz Financial Services LLC* is the nation's oldest, deaf-owned financial services firm providing registered investment advisory services, tax preparation, financial planning and education for the Deaf community.

Our tax preparation and planning service, which is branded under DeafTax.com, has been serving the U.S. deaf community since 1971. In 1983, the firm became a registered investment advisory firm and began managing investment portfolios for individuals and organizations. We provide discretionary investment services for clients who have IRA, Roth IRA, Trusts or personal Investment accounts.

In addition to our consumer financial services, we offer several additional services:
-Federal Benefits & Retirement Seminar for Deaf federal employees
-Financial Management training for ASL interpreters (e-Book, CEU workshop)
-Non Profit Set-up, Fundraising and Consulting service


*Securities offered through Royal Alliance Associates, Inc.
Member FINRA/SIPC Investment Advisory Services offered through Schwarz Financial Services LLC, a registered investment adviser not affiliated with Royal Alliance Associates, Inc.
