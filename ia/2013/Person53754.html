Lance Howard's Summary

I am an Investment Advisor with extensive experience in all aspects of retirement planning, wealth management and volatility control. I have direct knowledge with asset protection, financial needs analysis, risk management and insurance planning. My interests lie in working with retirees and pre-retirees to formulate a strategy for secure retirement.

Furthermore, I am committed to developing local professional relationships with tax professionals and estate planning attorneys to supplement the needs of my clientele.

Additionally, I host "The Asset Protection Hour", Saturdays at 7 a.m. on KGEO - SmartTalk AM 1230 and again, Sundays at 12 noon on KERN AM 1180.

Specialties: Financial advising, retirement and estate planning, risk management and insurance planning, income riders, financial needs analysis, asset allocation and protection, investment management
