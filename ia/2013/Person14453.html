Bill Shulman's Summary

Bill Shulman is a successful high tech business owner, manager, consultant, and public speaker. An avid NY Met, Jet, Knicks, and small business fan; Bill more often than not can be found rooting for the underdog.

Co-founder and current Chairperson for the Executive Leadership Symposium which for over 10 years has produced conferences and other programs related to Technology, Information Security & Corporate Governance, as well as a long-running series for women and diverse business leaders. Bill considers each conference an opportunity to advance a cause; often leverages these world class connections to advance non-profit organizations he personally supports, or to help his clients build their profile.

Insperity, a trusted advisor to America’s best businesses for more than 25 years, provides an array of human resources and business solutions designed to help improve business performance. Serving more than 100,000 businesses with over 2 million employees, Insperity’s mission is to help businesses succeed so communities prosper. We provide a wide range of business performance solutions, enabling business owners to be more systematic and strategic about the role people play in the success of a company. Whether you have ten employees or 5,000, Insperity’s business solutions are tailored to helping you run your business more efficiently.
