Galina Espinoza's Summary

I'm an editorial executive with 15+ years of experience at some of the world's leading media companies (Time Inc., Condé Nast, Hearst), and with a proven track record in delivering innovative, strategic content that drives brand growth and engages audiences across print/digital/social media/video platforms. I've efficiently managed budgets >$1 million, motivated teams of more than 20 creatives, and expertly collaborated with multiple stakeholders.

But that's just my day job! I'm also a sought-after, on-air expert on celebrity and pop culture, appearing regularly on TV and radio programs including the Today show, Joy Behar: Say Anything!, and NPR's Tell Me More. I am also one of the industry's preeminent experts on the U.S. Hispanic market, speaking regularly at conferences and on panels.
