Mike Arvela's Summary

Software has always been to me a source of inspiration and a thing of beauty. It fascinates me how there is always more to learn.

In the last ten years I have built web and mobile applications with various technologies, developing for backend as well as frontend. I have an eye for detail and enjoy perfecting the user experience.

I believe in simplicity, openness, and making things happen. I want to be the guy that inspires people and makes a project better just being there. Versatility and being quick at picking up things is my biggest asset.
