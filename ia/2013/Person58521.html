Jan Mundo's Summary

I work in several domains.

I help clients design and achieve lives of health, happiness, and meaning through in-depth, transformational somatic coaching, bodywork, and self-care practices. I work with people in all walks of life and professions, especially those who are plagued by trauma and chronic pain.

In 1970 I created a hands-on therapy and in 1993 a body-mind coaching program for headache and migraine sufferers. My clients learn to build the life skills they need to overcome their chronic pain cycles naturally without drugs or side effects. I offer private sessions, classes, workshops, lectures, tele classes, and professional training.

I am also a copy editor and writer of nonfiction and educational articles, essays, and books. I'm currently writing a book about the Mundo Method for headache and migraine, which will be published by Book Publishing Company.
