Evan Eggers' Summary

As vice president of marketing for Monument Construction, a construction prime contractor to the federal government founded in 2008, Evan manages outreach to federal contracting officers, facility managers, and other constituencies to further accelerate the firm's growth curve.

As co-founder of the SureCruise® Internet Cruise Superstore (GoDifferent LLC), Evan grew SureCruise into one of the top 1% of cruise sellers nationwide.

Evan previously led marketing, sales and software engineering efforts at travel, transportation, and technology firms, including as Director of Marketing & Sales at travel powerhouse Sabre. This ideal background enabled Evan to succeed in the cut-throat and low-margin online travel sales business, ensuring SureCruise’s high-service, high-expertise, high-technology model carved a successful niche against the Goliath order-takers of online travel and generated enviable per-unit premium cruise sales.

Outside of work, Evan is an avid amateur triathlete, having completed, along with wife Jana, five full Ironman 140.6-mile events between 2008 and 2011.
