Justin Lacche (La'Kay), PMP, MBA's Summary

My portfolio: http://lacche-library.blogspot.com

I build and coach top global winning teams, using three core pillars: social media, program/project management and brand development.

Social media
I lead the award-winning social media and business development teams for Oregon’s professional affiliate of the World Champion, San Francisco Giants. I have created and implemented a series of social media and brand development projects that have made the Salem-Keizer Volcanoes one of the strongest sports brands in Oregon.

This is proved by the 72,000+ unique readers to our site www.volcanoesfan.com and a lean, relevant and customer-driven Facebook page, both of which successfully monetize our brand.

Program/project management
I have a documented and successful track record running projects for Nike, Intel, as well as, the cable and professional sports industry.

My methods are based on math and science, and also leverage the fact that projects are run by people leveraging technology, not the other way around. I'm a very strong believer that applied mathematics and audience awareness gets hard projects completed.

Results, not best efforts, are how my projects are defined and my focused and collaborative approach to team execution is supported by the dozens on LinkedIn endorsements from fellow highly-skilled, highly-educated, highly-motivated people who continue to want to work with me.

Brand development
Where better to evolve brand development practices than Nike, Intel and the San Francisco Giants organization? My brand portfolio ranges from designing a sports apparel line to hardware/software product lines.

I deliver brands to market that have the support of our customers pre-release, allowing for successful planning, forecasting, and the ability to scale.

Summary
I am the product of many wonderful mentors, colleagues and direct reports. What I offer and deliver starting “day one” is a skilled math and people based approach to leadership.
