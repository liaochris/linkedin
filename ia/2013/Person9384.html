Jim Heilbronner's Summary

Founder and President of Architectural Dimensions. Architectural Dimensions (AD) is a full service architecture and design firm that tackles complex projects in multiple sectors, including retail, hospitality, restaurants, shopping centers, office, industrial and historic renovations. Founded in 1982 by James Heilbronner, the Oakland Based firm's design approach is to "work hard at responding ti the natural surroundings and public attitudes toward a project."
Project Management is at the core of AD's work, including the development or sophisticated systems to track and manage large projects. A well rounded knowledge of land planning, building codes, development, engineering and construction, enables AD to manage all facets of large projects.

Jim Heilbronner's Projects

  • Rotunda Building

    • 1999 to 2001
    Team Members: Jim Heilbronner, Phillip Tagami, Pankow Construction

    This historical restoration project is a grand demonstration of urban mixed use redevelopment at its best with 2 floors (60,000 sq. ft.) of retail and 5 floors (200,000 sq. ft.) of office space. The Beaux Arts building features an elliptical Rotunda which rises from the basement floor up to the seventh story. Besides restoration, the building is being seismically upgraded and brought code compliant.

  • Historic Oakland Fox Theater

    • 2006 to 2009
    Team Members: Jim Heilbronner, Phillip Tagami, Patricia Dedekian, Dan R. Wheeler, Jeffrey Chew

    Renovation of Historic Fox Oakland Theater, serving as a live performance venue, restaurant and Oakland School for the Arts.

  • Hampton Inn and Suites

    • 1990 to Present
    Team Members: Jim Heilbronner, Jeffrey Mac Adam, Joe Vieira

    Preferred project architect and project manager for various Hampton Inn and Suites throughout Northern California.
