Kathy Leodler's Summary

Kathy Leodler, Founder and CEO of the Rampart Group LLC, Seattle, WA

Kathy's unique and broad reaching security expertise spans 28 year’s combined corporate security, federal law enforcement, and international liaison leadership roles.

Today, Kathy heads up the Rampart Group, a team of highly skilled global security professionals who have a passion for safeguarding people, products, properties, information and business reputations.

The Rampart Group offers a full portfolio of domestic and international security solutions for businesses of all sizes, as well as government agencies, educational institutions and NGOs. Their flagship service, The Virtual Security Director Program, offers Fortune 500 caliber security coverage to small and mid-sized businesses.

Kathy Leodler possesses an unwavering work ethic and represents a high caliber of domestic and international security expertise dedicated to uncompromising service excellence for all her clients.
