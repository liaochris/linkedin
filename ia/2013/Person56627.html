Jack Tatar's Summary

As a leading market researcher, Jack is the owner of GEM Research Solutions which conducts research on various topics, primarily financial services, technology and consumer products.

Jack's work in the Retirement space has led him to be known as "America's Safe Retirement Coach". He's recently written 3 books on retirement: His latest is "Having The Talk: The Four Keys to Your Parents's Safe Retirement" which focuses on the vital discussion between adult children and their parents about retirement and later life issues. His bestseller and first book, "Safe 4 Retirement:The 4 Keys to a Safe Retirement" takes a holistic approach to retirement for pre-retirees and retirees. Rather than just focusing on financial matters for retirement, this book explores the 4 Keys to a Safe Retirement: Financial Preparedness, Health & Wellness, Mental Attitude and Staying Involved!

Jack has worked extensively with many start up businesses including highschoogle.com, which was recently named the #4 best college planning site on the internet, and Terracycle, which is the leading company in "up cycling" and was named "Coolest Start Up in America" by Inc. Magazine. He is currently working with a company that is creating a revolutionary shopping model.

Extensive executive experience with businesses such as JP Morgan, Avon Products and Merrill Lynch allows me to provide my own successful business, GEM Research Solutions, with the business knowledge of a client with the expertise of a research provider. I speak with over 1000 employees, clients and consumers a year through our core qualitative research business where we conduct focus groups, executive interviews and manage online panels and forums.

Specialties: qualitative research, quantitative research, focus groups, executive interviews, consulting services, Second Life, market research, retirement planning, startups

  • Having The Talk: The Four Keys to Your Parents' Safe Retirement

    • People Tested Publications
    • January 22, 2013
    Authors: Jack Tatar

    Have you had “the talk” yet?
    No, this isn’t the one you have with your kids about sex, hoping you actually know more than they do.
    This talk is the one adult children have with their parents about their parents’ retirement and aging and such concerns as medical proxies, healthcare and estate planning, wills, trusts, and the fact that 70% of people over age 65 will require some form of long-term care.
    This book focuses on the talk and incorporates Jack Tatar’s innovative Four Keys approach to a safe retirement. By following Jack’s game plan laid out in his book, you will understand
    • What the talk is and why it’s important to have it earlier, rather than later.
    • How to start the talk with your parents and use talking points to keep them engaged.
    • What to discuss during the talk, ensuring that all the important topics and details are covered.
    • How to create, maintain, and review a financial plan for retirement.
    • The latest up-to-date information on health matters pertaining to retirees.
    • How to maintain a positive attitude in retirement.
    • How to create an effective social structure and network for retirees.
    The talk is often not a single discussion, but rather something that’s ongoing and detailed. It will help create a safe retirement for your parents and peace of mind for your entire family. It will also provide adult children with the understanding they’ll need when considering their own retirement and an appreciation of the value and importance of having this talk with their own children.
    Like Jack’s previous books, Safe 4 Retirement: The Four Keys to a Safe Retirement and The 10 Joys of a Safe Retirement, this new book will change the way you think about retirement.
    The talk will bring families together so that they stay together.
