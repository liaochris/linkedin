Ian Mackenzie's Summary

I make ads. I've been quoted in the Guardian UK, profiled in Communication Arts, and been a contributing writer on advertising for Applied Arts. My portfolio is a mix of traditional, digital, social and integrated creative for brands such as McDonald's, Yum!, Honda, Labatt and Cadbury.

LP Tremblay (CD), Derek Blais (AD) and myself (CW) were the core creative team behind the internationally recognized "Our Food. Your Questions." radical transparency platform for McDonald's Canada.

Awards include: Cannes Lions, One Show, Webby, CLIO, Marketing, ADCC and Bill Bernbach.

I like cats.
