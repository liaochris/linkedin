Dave Roberts, PhD's Summary

I am a "professional communicator", giving a "voice" to my customers as a voiceover artist, writer, and producer. I also give a "voice" to my clients' customers as a consumer researcher. It's a synergy based on a wide range of life experiences--radio personality (San Bernardino to San Francisco to Syndication), radio programmer, commercial voiceover, marketing researcher, focus group moderator (over 4,000), corporate officer (RKO and CBS), business owner (researcher and voiceover), consultant, Marine, college instructor, voiceover coach.

Specialties

Voiceover: Radio/TV Commericials, Radio/TV Imaging, Corporate Narration, Events, On Hold Messaging, Pod/Vod-casting, Syndicated Programming, Voiceover Coach.

Consumer Researcher: Survey design, focus group moderating, qualitative research consultant, quantitative research analysis, marketing consultant. Also, radio programming and voiceover.
