Max Cameron's Summary

Five areas of expertise that best represent my core talents.

•Creative Direction
I am an award-winning designer, an artist in multiple mediums, an impelling copy writer, and I manage both process and personnel with equal effectiveness. I pride myself not only in my ability to articulate my own creative vision and execute it myself, but also in my ability to recognize and utilize the talents and contributions of the people I work along side. I have seen firsthand the positive by-products that come from guiding others in their creative growth and encouraging them to take pride in their contributions — a more pleasant workplace and ultimately higher revenues for the company.

•Account Representation
I maintain several accounts that require specialized attention when their needs lean more to the creative side of the business. Instrumental in the procurement of many new business clients, I am a primary contact in establishing a solid working relationship.

•New Business
Beyond being behind the conception and design of presentations and promotional materials, I also have been a key deliverer. Whether it is an up close and personal meeting, giving a presentation to a multitude, or being a trade show spokesperson, I excel in conveying the message at hand with proven results.

•Internal Business
I am a big picture thinker with the ability to focus on the small details. The companies with whom I have worked and consulted have benefitted from my input on matters ranging from business strategy to client and employee relations.

•Public Relations
With my knack for being a quick-on-the-draw thinker and my ability to articulate those thoughts clearly, I am often the go-to guy for public speaking, and I have provided several interviews for printed articles and promotional videos. I am often chosen to be a company representative for industry-related conferences and other events.
