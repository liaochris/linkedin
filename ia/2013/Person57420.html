James Fales' Experience

Co-Founder

V Capital

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Commercial Real Estate industry

August 2010 – Present (3 years 3 months)

The team at V Capital has been buying debt from banks, the FDIC, and other institutional holders of notes for huge discounts since the Savings and Loans crisis and the days of the RTC. With the downturn in the economy over the last 5 years, V Capital's team has participated in the purchase of over $1.8 Billion in loans. Banks do not typically sell their notes to borrowers, but rather use loan sales advisors to sell loans that have defaulted due to maturity or monetary issues. These clearing houses sell the loans exclusively to their list of approved buyers like V Capital. Since January 2011 our focus has been multifamily but have closed on several office and retail assets this year.
