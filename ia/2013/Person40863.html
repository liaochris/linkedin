Ellerton Whitney, MBA's Summary

Ellerton is a heavy-hitting internet marketer that gets results. He has extensive experience managing multi-million dollar direct-response Campaigns, achieving and exceeding client and company goals. He has managed projects ranging from the Fortune 50 all the way down to small business, on both the agency and client-side. His diverse background makes him exceedingly qualified to succeed at e-commerce, lead generation, and branding. Ellerton has helped build multi-million dollar businesses, and believes strongly in the power of search and contextual targeting. He uses his marketing expertise combined with a virtual PhD in data analysis to drive strategy that converts. In other words, Ellerton makes money fall from the sky.

"Everyone should watch Ellerton do Excel, it's like art." -A coworker.

Ellerton has his MBA in marketing from San Diego State University. He also received his Bachelor of Arts from University of California, San Diego. He is currently rapidly growing a large B2C website in the Compete Top 250.
