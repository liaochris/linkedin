KC Shashidhar's Summary

My work broadly concerns reliability of software and systems, with a particular focus on cyber-physical systems. I'm primarily interested in solving verification and validation problems that arise in model-based software and systems engineering. My other interests include, but are not limited to, design automation of embedded systems, and formal logics, models and semantics.

Specialties

- Formal Methods for Software and Systems Engineering
- Software and Systems Analysis, Verification and Testing
- Methods and Tools for Control Software Engineering
- Embedded Systems Design and Development
- Formal Verification of Software and Hardware
- Electronic Design Automation
