Roman Markovski's Publications

  • Professionalisering: een naturalistische analyse van een familiebedrijf in groei

    • Erasmus University Rotterdam
    • March 5, 2005
    Authors: Roman Markovski

    This is my graduation thesis for Management of Change, a specialization in the field of Business Administration. The thesis is based on observations during a 10 month period in a big consumer electronics manufacturer. Markovski researches the way this family organization was started, has been growing for several years and how the people working in the company responded to the changes.

    I use a naturalistic method of research, which encompasses observing the subject in its proper context, attention to details and (inter)subjective social gathering of data. Diving deep into the social and formal relations, the thesis holds a mirror for the organization and visualises the clusters of behavior lead that lead to other clusters of consequences.

    The relational map is used to advise a follow-up research and ways that the company can keep monitoring the dynamics between the people in the workplace.
