John Allsopp's Summary

I try to double my clients' online business year on year through Internet marketing.

I look at traffic through search engine optimisation (SEO) and social media mainly, but also through very targeted advertising (PPC), traditional marketing and affiliates.

That traffic needs to convert into sales, so conversion is next. I'm strong on sales psychology, usability, website testing and basically organising your website so that people get what they want and start buying things from you.

You may have customers, but outright fans and advocates take things to a whole new level. I help encourage that, and make it easier for people to share their great experiences online so we can share the good news.

I combine almost two decades of PR and marketing experience with a first class degree in Internet Computing and around 700 hours (really) of study into Internet marketing techniques.

Everything I do is aimed at giving customers a better experience. They should be able to find you when they need what you sell, be convinced you're right when they do find you, and be so happy with your product or service that they need to tell their friends.

Please do connect here, just let me know what possibility or problem is in your head so I know what you're working on.

I'm on Twitter, feel free to follow me here: http://twitter.com/JohnAllsoppIM

I've three main online businesses:

If you want me to bring my views, advice, suggestions, passions and ideas to your business (and perhaps implement them), http://www.johnallsopp.co.uk is wholly me.

If you are looking for a more usual provider of Internet marketing services and web development by a professional team of specialists and experts (ie. not just me), http://www.surgemarketing.co.uk is where you want to be.

If you want to be mentored, or you are looking for an ongoing continuous online marketing improvement service or a virtual marketing manager http://www.amilliontweaks.co.uk is probably it.

Specialties

Traffic: SEO, social media, PPC, PR
Conversion: usability, photography, persuasive writing, accessibility, Information architecture, psychology
Lifetime customer value: making life easier and better for your customers (sometimes through technology) and building engagement
I can build sites from scratch in PHP/HTML/CSS/mySQL/JavaScript/AJAX and also in (mainly) Drupal but also Wordpress.
I've worked on consumer (kitchens, insurance, interiors) as well as engineering & business to business sites.
