Andy Carle's Summary

Usability scientist, interaction designer, hacker. Recently finished my PhD in Computer Science (emphasis in Human-Computer Interaction) at UC Berkeley. Currently User Experience Architect for the Kinoma Software Platform team at Marvell Semiconductor.

I have a strong background in UX/interaction design, educational and instructional technologies, qualitative evaluation methods, and formative evaluation techniques.
