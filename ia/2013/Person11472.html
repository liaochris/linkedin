Ziv Baida's Summary

I am the Customs Solutions Lead in IBM´s Global Center of Competence for the Government.

Global Industry Consultant for the Customs industry, operating globally.

Experienced Customs consultant with broad knowledge of customs strategy, processes and information systems (import, export, Customs Risk Management, AEO, Single Window and more).

Management consultant and IT strategist focusing on business model development and innovation, Business Architecture, Business-IT Alignment, Business Process Management.

Conceptual thinker. Thought leader. Result-driven. Perfectionist. Strong cross-cultural communication skills. Experience in working in challenging, multi-national and cross-cultural environments. Mobile.
