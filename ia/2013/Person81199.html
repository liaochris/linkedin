Lindsay Maines' Summary

I've been on many sides of the digital equation, as content producer, advisory board member, new venture lead, and agency- those diverse roles help me create value for all stakeholders in the event trifecta: Attendees, Sponsors, and Event Producers.

I consult for brands in the following industries: Automotive, CPG, retail, Private Label, and online flash sale sites.

Additionally. I have an interest in growth ventures.

I maintain category exclusivity for one year post-contract per industry vertical.
