Bill Richardson's Experience

Head of Business Development & Sales

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

2011 – Present (2 years)

Our easy-to-use scheduling, billing and marketing tools help teachers with administrative tasks so they can spend more time with students. The TakeLessons product suite is complemented by TakeLessons.com, the largest online service marketplace dedicated to matching students with pre-screened arts and education instructors.

We provide value to students (and parents of students) by taking all the guesswork, hassles and time out of finding and hiring the most qualified music teacher for them.

For teachers, our web-based software handles back office operations including scheduling, billing and payments as well as tools for lesson journals, goal setting and more. We also provide front end marketing (www.takelessons.com) that brings them more students.

The Business Development group partners with retailers, manufacturers, publishers, community organizations and corporations. The company has a strategic partnership with Best Buy and is the recommended service when you buy an instrument on Amazon.com. Our corporate music lessons wellness/discount partners include Google, Zappos, Pandora, Red Bull North America, Electronic Arts, Universal Music Group, and others.
