Lillian Coury's Summary

Lillian enhances the impact, influence and productivity for the best and brightest business professionals across the U.S. The coaching relationship sharpens leadership style and presence through authentic self-expression. A caring and challenging coach, Lillian works ‘One-on-One’ and combines group with individual coaching for maximum return on investment. Her work is grounded in increasing employee engagement and business optimization.

Lillian is an executive coach, speaker, author, presenter, and workshop facilitator specializing in transformational leadership development. She founded her own coaching firm called Pegasus Pathways, Inc. early in 2001, and sold her printing company in December, 2001. Since then, she has been inspiring and challenging her clients throughout the country to create meaningful success in life and business.

Lillian maintains her credentials as a PCC (Professional Certified Coach with International Coach Federation) and CIC (Certified Integral Coach with New Ventures West Coaching School, CA). Since 2010, Pegasus Pathways is certified as a WBE (Woman Owned Business Enterprise) by WBENC West. In 2011, Lillian was trained and licensed to use the CVI (Core Values Index) assessment, a powerful tool utilized in coaching programs and business optimization programs which enhance the engagement of employees and directly improve the profitability of teams and their organizations.

A highly energetic and experienced businesswoman, Lillian brings 15 years in sales and 13 years as an owner of a NYC printing company to her coaching -- there is nothing she hasn't experienced first hand in the business world!
