Laurie Barkman's Summary

Laurie Barkman is the President and Chief Executive Officer of GENCO Marketplace, one of America's largest merchandise liquidators. GMP is a majority owned subsidiary of GENCO, a Product Lifecycle Logistics company. GENCO is North America's 2nd largest and a global Top 25 third-party logistics company and the recognized leader in reverse logistics.

GENCO Marketplace customers range from small online sellers who purchase single pallets for eBay inventory on GENCOMarketplace.com to other merchandise liquidators who regularly purchase multiple truckloads. GMP also operates NoBetterDeal.com-- a web store for bargain shoppers to purchase high-quality surplus inventory, retail returns and premium dotcom products at aggressively discounted prices.
