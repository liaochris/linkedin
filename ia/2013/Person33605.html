Deborah Ward's Summary

Deborah Ward, Realtor is an Ireland native, and owned and operated several successful businesses with her husband before they won a Visa lottery, allowing them to move to the U.S. to spread their wings in the Sunshine State. Having been in the real estate business for many years, Deborah firmly believe that customers benefit most from a customer service oriented team format, and is very happy to be with a company that is so supportive of this concept. Deborah is a multi-million dollar producing agent, and works as her team's Lead Listing Specialist – ensuring that Deborah Ward & Associates sellers are kept informed and that their property is kept at the forefront of the market. Having benefited from the training of real estate greats, Deborah bring a variety of business expertise and real estate wisdom to the team and her clients.

Specialties

Deborah Ward & Associates is a team of dedicated experts specifically trained to handle every part of the real estate process. It is our goal to ensure that you are making educated and informed decisions from start to finish, so that you can rest easy. We offer experience, guidance, expertise, and understanding from consultation through closing.
Deborah Ward & Associates believes our customers DESERVE EXCELLENCE from their realtors. Customer satisfaction is the highest priority in our team.
