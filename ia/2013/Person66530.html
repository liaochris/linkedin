John Nail's Summary

Mr. Nail, a 35+ year benefits industry veteran and internet entrepreneur, is currently the founder and “newsmaster” of The Industry Radar, the premier knowledge source for employee benefits, healthcare and health insurance and all things healthcare reform (PPACA) related.

Current client projects include the sale or lease of the ultimate industry brand, benefits.com™ and an online video production of a series on the Affordable Care Act, "Explaining Obamacare"©.

Mr. Nail’s passion is “information logistics” - or creating transactional, information and communication efficiencies between the various constituents in the “employee benefits/health insurance supply chain” – employees, employers, brokers and benefits/healthcare insurers.

Mr. Nail, a Mustang and Shelby car buff and avid golfer, is a Cum Laude graduate of Williams College.

John Nail's Experience

Principal/Founder

The Industry Radar / Bentelligence

Market Research industry

January 2006 – Present (8 years) Greater Atlanta Area

Today we provide consulting, industry knowledge and actionable marketplace data to our employee benefits, technology and healthcare industry clients.

Current client projects include the sale or lease of the ultimate industry brand, benefits.com™ and an online video production of a series on the Affordable Care Act, "Explaining Obamacare"©.

The Industry Radar (www.theindustryradar.com) is an digital media business focused solely on meeting the real time information needs of human resources, insurance, healthcare and employee benefits professionals. Its 500+ RSS news feeds and 2 email newsletters reach well over 100,000 industry professionals daily and serve over 4 million article views monthly.

Bentelligence our proprietary healthcare and benefits industry marketplace knowledge and analytics platform was rolled out in 2008 and currently is used by insurance brokers, national insurers and numerous Fortune 500 pharmaceutical companies for strategic planning, marketplace analysis and targeted sales and marketing activities.

Combining multiple industry data sources with 5500 benefit plan data we have detailed knowledge on over 135,000 American businesses and their health and welfare plans, brokers and insurers.

Other services provided to clients include HIPAASecure 360, our HIPAA/HITECH compliant email encryption solution powered by industry leader Zix, which we sell to insurance brokers, TPA's and other healthcare industry service providers all over the country.
