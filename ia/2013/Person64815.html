Joachim Coppens' Summary

My portfolio >> http://www.joachimcoppens.be

Hi there! I'm an environment artist and I recently graduated with high honors from Digital Arts & Entertainment. I have a passion for making and playing video games, and love to learn new things.

I still have a lot to learn but I love what I'm doing and do my best with every task I'm given or challenge I undertake.

Working on game related projects these past few years has been amazing so far. I'm really into 3D environment modeling and level design. I've also done quite some coding and tend to be the planner and manager of group projects. It would be a dream come true to continue on this path.

Specialties: 3D Modeling in Maya, 3Ds Max and Mudbox. Programming in C#, C++, ActionScript 3.0, UnrealScript, HLSL, ASP.NET, XHTML & CSS, and JavaScript.
