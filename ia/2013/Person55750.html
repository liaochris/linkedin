Ira M. Hendon, MBA, PMP, CSM's Summary

Welcome to my Linked In profile page.

As a 30 year IBM retiree, and with 7 years of experience as the President and CEO of Hendon Group, I have extensive experience delivering results for critical and complex initiatives with Fortune 500 companies, governments and small / medium sized businesses. My experience spans a variety of industries including: aerospace and defense, manufacturing, distribution, retail, government, insurance, health care, telecommunications and electronics.

I have helped organizations implement complex IT strategies, take corrective action on existing programs / projects (turning around troubled projects), led new, first of a kind, business initiatives, led business development projects, and have conducted reviews for complex program and projects. I am passionate about helping organizations successfully implement their strategic initiatives while protecting and leveraging all available intellectual property (IP).

I have lectured at the Project Management Institute’s (PMI®) Regional Professional Development Conferences and have developed, and delivered, custom curriculum / training, including “Complex Systems Integration Bid Development” and “Implementing Earned Value Management”.

I have given back to the program / project management professional community through the PMI Chicagoland Chapter in a variety of leadership and coaching roles (e.g., Director of Programs; Strategic Events; Professional Development; and mentor.)

I have also authored “E-business: Think Big, Start Small, Act Quickly”, the Next Century of Flight” Aviation Week & Space Technology, August 28, 2000.
