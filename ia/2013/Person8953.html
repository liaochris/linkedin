Antonio Fournier's Summary

Antonio is an experienced consultant in corporate intelligence, public affairs, strategic management and communication. His areas of expertise are in a wide range of sectors such as energy, defense, construction, infrastructures, shipping, tourism, consumer goods and luxury industries.

He serves in several boards or advisory boards, among them Spain’s leading strategic communication management consultancy, Llorente & Cuenca, the nº1 network in PR, Lobbying and Public Affairs in Latin America. As non-executive Vice-President of LLorente y Cuenca, Antonio works closely with the founding partner on the strategic development of the firm as well as some of their most relevant clients.

He also represents in Spain ESL & Network Group and is a member of the Supervisory board of the Group.

After leaving the Paris Graduate School of Commerce (ESCP 91) I worked closely for two years with Andree Putman, the worldwide known French designer. In 1994 I started International Communication & Marketing (ICM) and Globally, two strategic marketing consultancies. ICM became Spain’s leading agency in the fashion, luxury and cosmetic businesses, working for more than 8 years with clients such as Procter & Gamble (Vidal Sassoon, Hugo Boss, Pantene, Laura Biagiotti, Giorgio Beverly Hills, Max Factor, etc.), Beiersdorf BDF Nivea, Bourjois, Estee Lauder, Vivienne Westwood, LVMH-Parfums Givenchy, Madrid Fashion Week Pasarela Cibeles, Factory Outlet Stores, The Walt Disney Company, etc.

Antonio Fournier's Projects

  • INTELCORP Activity

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Antonio Fournier

    The essence of our activity can be summarized as consultants in Corporate Intelligence and Corporate Diplomacy.

    We offer an overall control and supervision covering all aspects of gathering key decision-making information and the main layers of influence providing you with the understanding of a leading local high-end experienced expert.

    That’s our business: to look inside, to look on the side, to look more ahead, better and further. We go after what is not said, showed, given or known. That is corporate intelligence.

    Some examples: once we discouraged an important investment in renewable energy to take place because we knew that a law that was in its initial stages of the making would jeopardize profitability, but also because the real reasons why the seller was willing to sell were other than the ones exposed. Both would entail a considerable loss to our client.

    We often become the local and present eyes of our overseas clients in their investments or interests (or just monitoring competition), allowing them to have a full comprehension of what is exactly happening at all times. We offer a confidential, discrete and low-profile local and full time watch. Some of our clients have already, as advisors, local and/or international top lawyers, blue-chip auditing firms, corporate or investment banks on fees, even their local representatives or middle-men on success-fees or commissions. However, key elements of analysis are not always included or said, or they are simply weak, incomplete or, even sometimes, misleading or responding to their personal interests, not the client’s, thus not providing a clear assessment and a risk analysis of the situation. That’s our call…
