Kelly Stroda's Summary

I like to say that I have a "Swiss Army" approach to work.

Early on in my career, I began to understand and appreciate the importance of having a versatile skill set. Utility is a major asset.

A Swiss Army knife has knives, a screwdriver, corkscrew and more. Each of these has a different function, and together they create an incredibly effective tool. My career thus far has allowed me experience in design, writing, reporting, management, HTML, social media and more.

As a result of this background, I pride myself on being a hybrid employee who can work to help a company or organization through creative thinking.
