Liz Borod Wright's Summary

Liz Borod Wright (@Travelogged) is the editor/publisher ofTravelogged.com, an award-winning travel blog, and a social media consultant. She first became passionate about social media through the need to drive traffic to Travelogged.com without any kind of advertising budget. Before branching out on her own, Liz held editorial staff positions at major websites, including Zagat.com,ABCNews.com and Fortune.com. An alumna of Columbia’s Graduate School of Journalism, Liz is an adjunct for Prof. Sree Sreenivasan’s Advanced Social Media Workshop at the J-School and a social media instructor at Mediabistro. She is also a freelance writer on a wide range of subjects including travel, food and business.

Specialties

writing, editing, copywriting, blogging, photography, photo editing, Social Media (Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, StumbleUpon, Foursquare)
