Louis B. Cady, M.D.'s Summary

My overarching goal, and that of Cady Wellness Institute, is that of “integrating mind and body for peak performance.” It is my and my staff’s desire to transform the lives of our patients, enhance the health of our community, and positively impact people’s lives touched by my books or presentations.

As founder of the Cady Wellness Institute and the Cady Wellness TMS Center in Evansville, Indiana my mission is to bridge the two worlds of classic “allopathic” medicine and psychiatry and that of the contrasting, integrated functional-medicine based model. Toward these ends, we use everything from the latest cutting edge technique in psychiatry – the treatment of depression with Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation - to a step-by-step process of rebuilding a patient nutritionally, hormonally, behaviorally and psychologically.

I am board certified by the American Bd. of Psychiatry and Neurology in adult psychiatry, and a Fellow of the American Psychiatric Association. Having trained at the Mayo Clinic, I was exposed to cutting-edge psychiatry concepts and skills. Since opening my own institute, the importance of adopting the latest practice techniques remains relevant. In fall, 2011, we expanded our treatment options to include the utilization of Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS).

TMS is an FDA-cleared treatment for those that suffer from medication resistant depression and anxiety, or for those whom medication is less desired (pregnancy / breast feeding) or less tolerable. We have seen tremendous results with those who had previously achieved only limited improvement from medications.

Patients and colleagues have recognized my passion for treating patients and utilizing the latest of the medical literature and scientific advances. I eagerly research new proven treatment options to better serve my patients and my collaborating medical colleagues. This approach as well as collaboration with our patients allows those we serve to achieve their potential.

Program Development - NeuroVitality Breakthough

Cady Wellness Institute

The overarching goal of Cady Wellness Institute is that of “integrating mind and body for peak performance.” It is my and my staff’s desire to transform the lives of our patients, enhance the health of our community, and positively impact people’s lives touched by my books or presentations.

The Cady Wellness Institute’s healing interaction with our patients is built upon a unique six step process known as the NeuroVitality Breakthrough®. The core of this process is that we orchestrate a comprehensive realignment in mental and physical performance by combining unmatched medical investigation, leading advancements in neuroscience and technology, and a focus on collaboration in patient care. We emphasize collaboration between our two therapists, our naturopath, and me on complex cases.

Here is our proprietary six step process:

• Step 1: We determine the patient’s needs and whether or not our services are complementary and therapeutic. Background information is completed prior to the first intake appointment.

• Step 2: We conduct a thorough investigation of the patient’s background information, reviewing medical, neuropsychiatric, family genetics, educational and biopsychosocial information.

• Step 3: We then develop an understanding into the neurodiagnostic, optimal mind-body-health balance, hormonal deregulation/inadequacy, and clinical pathways that may be affecting health or mental performance.

• Step 4: Collaboration is emphasized between doctors, other treating clinicians, and our patients. Together we will identify the best strategy that will result in total mind/body wellness.

• Step 5: We carefully monitor mental and physical health as they improve and make adjustments as necessary.

• Step 6: When stabilization has been achieved, we work together as a team by making Wellness Alliance™ appointments so that continued support can be given.
