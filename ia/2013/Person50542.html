Jerome Sevilla's Summary

At one point I was a graphic designer doing graphic design stuff. Nowadays graphic design is just another part of my skill set. I never got any of the jobs I wanted, so I just stopped looking and started doing my own thing.

These days you can find me filling sketchbooks or selling knitted hats on etsy. I suppose being a graphic designer has helped me groom my online presence, so I suppose that part of me hasn't changed. Chances are it never will.
