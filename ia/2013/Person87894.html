Marty Stanley's Summary

As an author, speaker, facilitator and executive coach since 2000, I help people and organizations change from where they are now to where they want to be.

When people and organizations are clear about what they want and align thoughts, words and actions, they get different outcomes. Communication improves, productivity and profitability increase and people have a greater sense of hope and accomplishment.

As an HR executive for 20 years, my experience was in 3 dramatically different cultures: An entrepreneurial organization that grew from 250 people to 1700, an established, traditional company and an international business that transformed their industry. These experiences taught me how to help leaders plan, implement and communicate organizational changes that are aligned with strategic objectives.

Specialties

Facilitation, speaking and coaching on: Strategic planning; organizational change and change management; leadership and management development; instilling accountability; aligning HR practices with organizational objectives.
High impact conference keynotes and breakout sessions.
