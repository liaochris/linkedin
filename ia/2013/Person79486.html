Linda J. Whitehead's Summary

Are you finding that cold calling and traditional prospecting is slow and costly? Attract targeted leads to your business instead. Zuz Marketing focuses on providing inbound marketing and lead generation services to small and medium sized businesses, including:

- Strategic consulting and development of your customized inbound marketing plan
- Generate increased traffic to your website through content marketing and social sharing
- Create targeted followup campaigns to convert leads to customers

Or if you have an exciting new product or brand you want to launch, but your internal marketing team doesn't have the resources to plan and execute, Zuz Marketing has extensive experience with successfully conceiving, planning and launching new products for both B2B and B2C markets.

As principal of Zuz Marketing, I am a very passionate senior marketing professional with more than 20 years diverse experience in B2B and B2C marketing - on both client and agency sides. I really love the variety of working on projects and assignments for different companies and industries, applying my skills and expertise to help companies achieve their marketing goals and business objectives.

Specialties

Online marketing, inbound and outbound marketing, content marketing, email marketing, marketing planning (both strategy and tactical implementation), new brand/product launches

Linda J. Whitehead's Projects

  • The Lab blog

    • May 2011 to July 2012
    Team Members: Linda J. Whitehead, Christine T. Andrews, Charles Truelson

    I was acting as a consultant on inbound lead generation for Agency Access, a one-stop promotional service that takes care of direct marketing for commercial photographers, illustrators, artist reps and stock agencies. The objective was to help artists, who are generally solopreneurs in need of advice, by answering their questions on business, creativity and marketing, establishing Agency Access as the leading industry go-to educational resource. My idea for The Lab was to have artists submit their questions and we would procure the right expert to answer their questions. I managed this project from conception through to planning and implementation. We worked with over 40 different expert authors, published daily from launch in May 2011, and built up a following of thousands of artists.
