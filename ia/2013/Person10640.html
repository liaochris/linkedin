REG ATHWAL's Summary

Reg Athwal is an Entrepreneur and Executive Advisor to many owners of Family-Operated Businesses (FOB's) in the Middle East, Africa and South Asia (MEASA) region. He is also an Investor, Bestselling Author, Professional Speaker and Media Presenter.

Reg Athwal founded RAW GROUP (RAW LTD and RAW LLC) in 2002, with offices now in the UK and UAE, with the headquarters based in Dubai. RAW GROUP has 8 expanding service divisions and owns the brands: RAW Talent Scouts, RAW Media, UnleashYourDNA, Chase Hamilton & FamilyMBA, and has made other seed capital investments in start-ups and new concepts.

In 2012, RAW Group became part of TAO-Inc, a private investment holding group.

Reg Athwal as a professional presenter has inspired millions of people with his 'Unleash Your DNA' inspirational business speeches and transformed the lives and businesses of over 30,000 CEOs and 500,000 managers in 49 countries since 2002.

Reg is a highly rated Livewire Conference Chairman and Moderator for large conferences. Visit www.rawltd.com for indepth information, free articles, speech videos, free books, speech menus, photos, testimonials and much more.

Reg Athwal is also the Founder and an Executive Director of RAW Media / OneTVO FZ LLC (www.onetvo.com) - a global television show format creator that produces, broadcasts and markets TV Shows in 103 Countries, which was established in 2005 in Abu Dhabi and Dubai in the UAE. In 2011 OneTVO successfully produced the Ask-Ali TV Show for Etihad Airways, viewable now by 8+ million passengers.

Reg is an elected member of the Global Speakers Federation for Professional Speakers (GSF) and Professional Speaking Association of the United Kingdom (PSA-UK) and President for 2006-2007. Reg is also the Founding Chairman of the Professional Speakers Association of the Middle East (PSA-ME).

REG ATHWAL's Experience

Executive Director & Founding Partner

RAW Talent Scouts

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Human Resources industry

September 2012 – Present (1 year) Dubai, UAE

RAW Talent Scouts (RTS) is Division of RAW LLC and Member of RAW Group, and was officially launched on 30th September 2012, based on the high demand and requests from its trusted network of clients.

RTS is the first company of its type in Africa & Middle East to provide superior talent scouting, succession planning and strategic recruitment outsourcing solutions to predominately family-based business groups and conglomerates.

RTS has a similar DNA to that of a top sports/entertainment agency which scouts for talent, but specifically for the business world and for large family business groups.

RTS is not a recruitment agency or traditional recruitment process outsourcing company. We combine cutting-edge technology and tools with a global network of RTS Partners and have created a unique proposition to serve clients, who want the right talent at the right time without paying high fees or compromising on their need to hire A players.

RTS operates on a partnership model, with no full-time employees. Our partners are based around the world and service clients only in Africa and the Middle East.

RTS is looking to expand into 22 countries by 2022 by attracting new RTS Partners and servicing hundreds of clients in the region. RTS works strategically with RAW Advisory Services, which has served many clients for over a decade throughout Africa and the Middle East. We have significant on-the-ground experience in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Uganda, Zambia, Egypt, Morocco, United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Lebanon, India and Sri Lanka.

RTS's prime purpose, is to proactively support clients in hiring A-Players to build their high-performing dream-teams, by utilizing our superior talent scouting solutions, that will dramatically reduce both their cost- and time-to-hire top talent.
