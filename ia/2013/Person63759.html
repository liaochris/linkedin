Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist

Envoy Mortgage

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Financial Services industry

June 2012 – Present (1 year 4 months) Phoenix, Arizona Area

As the strategic lending partner to many organizations, Jim Barnett’s comprehensive approach to financing has proven to be invaluable. His business goal is to instill a level of service, trust and value so his clients have the WOW experience. He is truly passionate about making a difference in his client’s financial portfolios. By educating clients on the loan process, helping clients understand the products available and how to manage their debt portfolio, and honoring his commitments to his referral partners, Jim has built his business 100% by referral. Jim is truly viewed by his clients and partners as a trusted advisor and is regularly consulted on matters relating to housing and financial strategies. He is an advocate of educating consumers and advises his clients on how to best structure their home loans to accomplish all of their short and long term financial goals. As a result, Jim’s valued clients become enthusiasts of his business and actively promote their positive experience to others. His unique approach to cultivating relationships using Purpose, Impact, Passion and Inspiration as his key ingredients, has blessed him with the finest business professionals to be included in his Circle of Advisors. Jim considers himself a relationship specialist and has spent his career successfully building and sustaining strong client, REALTOR®, and other professional relationships. Jim has a fun‐loving, charismatic spirit that brings fresh perspective to the often serious subjects of finance and life design. His insightful, evocative and inspiring addresses with many local audiences have received outstanding reviews. Jim uses his own experiences to energize new ways to enhance personal performance, relationships, and to bust stress. Most importantly he is known as a vibrant example of life balance and one who places values before profit.

Owner

J. Barnett, Inc. - Voice Talent Services (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Entertainment industry

August 2005 – Present (8 years 2 months)

For more than twenty years, Jim has been an identifiable voice throughout the valley of the sun, having served as the Public Address Announcer for many professional sports franchises in the greater Phoenix area. Jim has held the position for the Phoenix Suns of the NBA, the Arizona Cardinals of the NFL, Phoenix Mercury of the WNBA, Arizona Rattlers of the AFL, Arizona Sting of the NLL and several sports for Arizona State University of the NCAA. He has also served as the In‐Arena Host for the Phoenix Coyotes of the NHL at Jobing.com Arena in Glendale and remains the voice of the Arizona Interscholastic Association State Championships held at Jobing.com Arena and University of Phoenix Stadium in Glendale, among other venues. He is also a professional voice actor with more than a thousand credits to his resume within the television, radio, internet and video game industries.
