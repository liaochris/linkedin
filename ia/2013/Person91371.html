Melody Hensley's Summary

I am the Executive Director of the Center for Inquiry's Washington, DC branch hosting 8-10 events per month for the local secular and skeptic community. I am also the organizer of the annual CFI sponsored Women in Secularism national conference.

I have a background in volunteering and community building. I have been involved with church-state separation and secular activism, as well as reproductive rights and progressive issues, in the DC area for over a decade. Prior to becoming Executive Director, I was the CFI–DC Event and Volunteer Coordinator. I am also on the advisory council for Secular Woman, Inc.

Melody Hensley's Projects

  • Women in Secularism – A Center for Inquiry Conference

    • May 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Melody Hensley

    Organized the CFI sponsored national conference Women in Secularism, devoted to the issues of the oppressive nature of religion in women's lives, gender equality in the secular movement, and to celebrate the great contributions women non-theists have made to the secular movement past and present. This was the first conference of its kind, the speaker line-up made up of exclusively non-theist women. The conference was a huge success and before the conference was over, we had announced plans for another Women in Secularism conference in 2013.

  • Women in Secularism 2 - A Center for Inquiry Conference

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Melody Hensley

    Organized the CFI sponsored national conference Women in Secularism, devoted to the issues of the oppressive nature of religion in women's lives, gender equality in the secular movement, and to celebrate the great contributions women non-theists have made to the secular movement past and present.
