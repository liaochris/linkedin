Bunches Direct's Summary

Welcome to BunchesDirect! We are your premier source for wedding flowers in the United States, Canada and Australia.

BunchesDirect specializes in the best-quality flowers at wholesale prices. Whether you're looking for flowers for your wedding, a corporate event, or any type of party, BunchesDirect delivers only the finest. Do-it-yourself with our amazing variety of flower options, or enjoy our gorgeous pre-made bouquets.

With our access to an incredible variety of flowers and our award-winning designers, the only limit is your imagination.

Whether you’re planning a party for 50, your wedding, or need 2000 roses for a fundraising event, we’ll be happy to assist you in making your event even more special. You can buy directly from our website, fill out a quote request, chat with a customer service representative or give us a call - we’re here for you!

Specialties: Floral delivery, custom floral arrangements, wholesale flowers, bulk flowers, pre-made wedding flowers, floral tips, floral DIY projects, wedding inspiration
