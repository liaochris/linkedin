Johan Brand's Summary

Born in Oslo, Norway, and now based in London, Johan has over a decade's experience merging creativity, innovation and technology. His work has included high profile projects for brands including Mercedes, BBC, HP, and Unilever, as well as startups in London and abroad.

His professional career started in 2000 as a consultant on the revolutionary MagCom smartphone, backed by leading firms including Insight Capital Partners, Telenor Venture and Anders Wilhelmsen AS. He later went on to study Fine Art Photography in Edinburgh and Entrepreneurship in Boston, working in the City's high tech startup environment.

In 2008 Johan was part of the founding management team of award-winning creative company, Playgroup. In 2010 he left his position as Strategic Creative Director to set up what has become We Are Human, a lean co-creative company, with global impact. In addition to working with some of the world's most recognised brands, We Are Human also incubates and develops its own IP, including the innovative EdTech platform Kahoot!

Most recently, Johan launched The Nordic Connection, an entrepreneurial network based out of London's TechCity, with a focus on creating global opportunities and connections for Nordic startups.

Johan is a regular speaker and lecturer at universities and events including SXSW, OECD, WOW, Helsinki Design Week, Service Design in Tourism, Pecha Kucha, UCL and UiO.
