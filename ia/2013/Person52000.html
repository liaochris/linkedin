Steve Hawk's Summary

I'm an Experienced Sales & Marketing Professional with over 25 years of successful experience in Financial Services, Call Center Solutions, Mortgages, Independent Direct Sales & Internet Marketing.

Following a more conventional career path, I started No Hype Marketing in 2009 & Top Tier Marketing Pro in 2012. I use the home based business direct sales/network marketing model to create income and recommend it to others since it's so affordable to get started and very lucrative IF you know what you're doing.

I'm a believer in using multiple marketing strategies to build your home business, both online and offline, and that's exactly what I teach my team members in order to get them to a minimum six figure annual income. Some of these strategies include attraction marketing, social media, voice broadcasting, postcards, and more.

The home based business or network marketing industry tends to get a bad name...mostly because people do not get the training that they need to succeed. That's where I come in! If you're someone looking to "start over" or re-invent yourself... I'm here to help!
