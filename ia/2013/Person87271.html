Mark Zmarzly's Summary

We are defined by how we impact others with the resources we cultivate. In my life I have developed the ability to story tell, to sell, to learn, to build networks, to connect deeply, to deliver passionate presentations, and to make others laugh.

I’ve used these skills to better the lives of my customers, my friends, my family, and myself. This is what life is truly about!

These are consistent elements of my life, but my professional roles continue to evolve. I've been a leadership consultant, waiter, student, teacher, writer, creative director, marketer, entrepreneur, and in sales. I’ve learned immense lessons about both life and business with each endeavor, and I always challenge myself to grow more personally and professionally. Here’s more:


IN MY PROFESSION

As the SVP at ACTON Marketing, I consult with banks and credit unions to uncover and solve their growth problems. I also founded Hip Pocket and regularly speak within the industry, including for the ABA, BAI Retail Delivery, and JD Power & Associates.

ON THE INTERNET

I’m at www.markzmarzly.com, www.ihelpbanks.com, @BankMarketing, @MarkZmarzly, and www.slideshare.net/mzmarzly.

IN MY LIFE

With my beautiful and loving wife of 11 years, I’ve been able to create an amazing life. We have two darling kids who never fail to put smiles on my face and gray in my hair. My bucket overflows.

IN MY FUTURE

My future involves more of what I'm passionate about: public speaking, learning, leadership, innovation, entrepreneurship, and making a beautiful life. Find my TEDX Talk here: http://tinyurl.com/SayYesTEDTalk

IN MY CIRCLE

If you are in my tribe, I’ll do anything for you. If I’m in yours, I’ll learn from you and connect on a deep level. If you want to connect, tell me why and ping me at markzmarzly@gmail(dot).com. I am waiting.

These are the areas where I have excelled in life and where I will continue to learn and grow. I welcome all who share these passions and who want to walk with me on this path.

      TEDxLincoln is an organization that seeks to provide a number of opportunities in Nebraska for people to share ideas. We were created in the spirit of TED’s mission, “ideas worth spreading.” Our events are planned and coordinated by a team of volunteers with support from individuals and organizations from across Nebraska.

      TEDx is a program of local self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Since the first TEDx in 2008, this popular local oriented TED concept has grown to reach every continent and millions of people through nearly 6,000 events. In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDxLincoln is a local, self- organized event focused on bringing people of Nebraska together to share ideas. At TEDxLincoln (Nebraska) we combine live local and recorded TED speakers to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. TEDxLincoln includes youth and adult events featuring people sharing ideas from Nebraska as well as opportunities for Nebraskans to participate in TED events from around the world.

      Talks are then made available, free, at www.ted.com.
