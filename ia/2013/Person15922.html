Peter O'Mahony's Summary

I established Boxer Motor Company, Maidenhead in 1982 to offer servicing and repairs at competitive pricing for renault vehicles.

Over the last 29 years I have grown the company from renault specialists to providing servicing, repair, mot, diagnostic testing and more to all makes and models of vehicles.

My aim has always been to offer the highest standard of service and to reinvest in the business. In March 2011, I invested in a new MOT lane which enables my technicians to carry out MOTs for cars or commercial vehicles from light commercial vans up to large vans, pick up trucks, tippers, horseboxes, etc.

For me, service starts the minute a customer calls the Boxer Motor Company number or walks through the door. Whatever the problem the aim is always to get them back on the road as quickly and as cost effectively as possible.

Specialties

Renault Specialists - the only renault specialist in Maidenhead, capable of carrying out service, repair and comprehensive diagnostics testing on all renault makes and models
