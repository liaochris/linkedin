Joe Moore 714-655-0116's Summary

Joseph Moore is the branch manager of the Southern California regional office of Mountain West Financial. This California based retail/wholesale lender is a leading provider of down payment assistance funding for first time homebuyers and an industry leader in both the purchase and refinance markets for FHA, VA, Conventional and Jumbo Loans. Mountain West Financial has recently been awarded the Master Servicer Contract for CalSTRS, Teacher Retirement Fund. The new loan program for educators will be launched in September 2013.

Mr. Moore brings twenty plus years of lending and management experience to Mountain West Financial. This alignment provides Joe the opportunity to recruit top lending professional to expand the company’s market share throughout Southern California, while continuing to provide exceptional service to his customers and realtor partners in the local community.

Joseph was formally the founder of More2Lend Financial in 2001. He successfully built a consumer direct lending platform specializing in FHA government lending. Prior to opening More2Lend Financial, Mr. Moore was Vice President of Sales & Marketing with Loans Direct. The company merged with E*TRADE Financial in 2001 and became E*TRADE Mortgage. LoansDirect.com distinguished itself as a technology leader and remained one of the most profitable and successful lenders of that time.

Mr. Moore is a graduate of California State University-Long Beach (1992) with a degree in Economics. He has been married to his wife Kathleen for 16 years and they have two children. Reagan is 15 years old in the 9th grade at Mater Dei High School and Brenden is 12 years old attending 7th grade at Saint Bonaventure. Joe and Kathleen are very active in the community and have both lived in Huntington Beach for the past 20 years.

Specialties: Consumer Direct & B2B Marketing, financial modeling , executive management and recruiting
