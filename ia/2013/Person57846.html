James Towey's Summary

Legal advisor and advocate for all professions regulated by the Virginia Department of Criminal Justice Services, including private security, private investigators, bail bondsmen, bail enforcement agents, electronic security, alarm respondents, armored car personnel, personal protection specialists, locksmiths, detector canine handlers, and security canine handlers.

James Towey Consulting provides experienced legal representation to the businesses and professionals regulated by the Virginia Department of Criminal Justice Services. This includes practical and cost-effective solutions on all legal and regulatory matters affecting your business, including regulatory compliance, licensure and disciplinary actions, employment law, contract disputes, legislative affairs, and advocacy.

Specialties

As Senior Assistant Attorney General, James Towey served as counsel to DCJS and, as a result, possesses a detailed, working knowledge of the statutes and regulations governing each profession that falls under DCJS oversight. James offers practical and cost-effective solutions on all legal and regulatory matters affecting DCJS regulated businesses and professionals.
