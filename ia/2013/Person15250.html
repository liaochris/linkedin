Bob Bossert's Summary

Mr. Bossert owns and operates an Executive Consulting Practice that has provided services for Xerox, Lexmark, Welmont Hospitals, n-Tara, and Enterprise Financial Group in a variety of areas: e-Commerce, ERP, CRM, SharePoint, ATS, and Business Process Optimization.

Before going in to consulting launched Medical Technologies Group (MTG) after spending months developing the operating plan for MTG. He launched a innovative integration of the medical practice, HIPAA Compliance and ground breaking Electronic Medical Records Software, that allowed doctor’s to continue focusing on patients while streamlining their back office operations.

Prior to joining MTG, Mr. Bossert was Chief Information Officer and Chief Technology Officer for Protocol Communications, a leading provider of marketing services, call center and material logistics. His responsibilities included business strategy, product development, consulting, marketing, sales, operations and general management.

Mr. Bossert previously was CTO of Modem Media, one of the largest online advertising agencies in the industry. At Modem Media he directed all aspects of commercial database design, oversight of needs analyses, as well as system and development of e-commerce solutions for fortune 500 clients.

He worked at Prodigy Services where he established The Future Media Lab. This leading edge lab did all of the early work in Cable based high speed network and content delivery, Interactive Television. The Lab was a frequent stop for industry analysts and reports alike, the lab was featured in a Documentary on The Death of TV, along with MIT, Microsoft, and other visionaries.

Mr. Bossert received his Masters Degree from the prestigious Interactive Telecommunications Department at New York University. He received a BS in Marketing with honors from the University of Cincinnati.
