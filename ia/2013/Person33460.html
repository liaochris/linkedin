Debe Fennell's Summary

Crowdfunding isn't a very new concept. It's been around a long time, but now you can use social media, email and word of mouth to raise capital for new businesses and projects. Marketing through social media is key to crowdfunding.

Knowing how to get connected with your crowd can be time consuming and confusing, but I can create a simple plan and help you execute it on your social media sites. If you don't currently participate in social media, no worries, I can get you connected!

If you have an idea and a business plan and you need seed money, my business experience can help your business get started.
