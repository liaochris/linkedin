Michael Longo III's Summary

Michael Longo III is a seasoned Business Continuity Advisor at Lootok. Michael’s career in Business Continuity Management began in the Financial Services industry in 2007. His beginning entailed much more than just Business Continuity, also providing Information Security and Supplier Risk Management services in Investment Bank Operations and Corporate Banking. Michael has a track record of performing in-depth risk assessments and business impact analyses; developing and implementing recovery strategies; creating business continuity plans; conducting full-scale exercises to test business continuity plans; organizing and coordinating disaster recovery testing; and analyzing business partners’ business recovery strategies/plans to ensure resiliency.
