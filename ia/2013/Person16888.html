Brent Carnduf, MBA's Summary

I have a MBA in Financial Planning with specialized studies in search engine optimization (SEO) and social media marketing from California Lutheran University.

I am the founder and owner of Echelon SEO (and Echelon Business Solutions). Although I work with businesses in many fields, my educational background has made my expertise an especially good fit for Financial Services firms.

Echelon SEO is a search marketing firm with offices in Sun Valley, ID, and Boise, ID.
Our services include:

• Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

• Pay Per Click Advertising
• Blogging and Content Creation
• Social Media Marketing
• Video Marketing
• Lead Generation Optimization
• Email Marketing
• Analytics

We also have partnerships with HubSpot, Smarsh, WP Engine, Constant Contact, and Aweber.

In addition to my blog, I contribute to the California Institute of Finance blog, Steamfeed, B2C Community, and Social Media Today.
