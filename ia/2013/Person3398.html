Albizu García's Summary

Albizu García is a digital media professional and entrepreneur with over 10 years in the digital and technology space. In 2007 he founded OVOPhone, the first independent VoIP telephony service to be launched in Puerto Rico. Later that same year, García founded Kaput Media, the first interactive agency in Puerto Rico to specialize in online media strategy, planning and buying. Under his guidance Kaput Media launched .FOX Networks in Puerto Rico as its exclusive representative in this territory and pioneered the integrated use of online media in successful campaigns. In 2010, he merged Kaput Media with Nobox Marketing Group in Miami, FL. García has executed successful online media strategies for global brands and provided online media planning and execution for international brands such as Heineken, Honda, McDonald’s, Bacardi, Mercedes-Benz, Claro, Coca Cola, AT&T, Copa Airlines and Mozilla Firefox, and global advertising agency groups like MediaCom, Mindshare, MEC, Starcom, Mediavest, DraftFCB, JWT, Grey, Y&R and Saatchi & Saatchi. He has vast experience in the Financial, Travel, Health Care, Automotive and Consumer Packaged Goods industries. In 2012 he founded Big Propeller, a technology focused marketing company that blends marketing strategies and proprietary technologies to help maximize the potential of e-commerce companies.
