Pranav Sharma's Summary

A subject matter expert capable of taking any SharePoint project from concept definition to execution and beyond. Over the course of five years, Pranav has successfully advised his portfolio of 15 clients at varying stages of a SharePoint project; ranging from Business Analysis to Solution Governance. He specializes in the Architecture & Development of custom solutions on the SharePoint platform. In 2012, Pranav co-founded Almond Labs (@AlmondLabs) with the goal of creating reusable solutions to help organizations get the most out of their SharePoint assets by improving user adoption, engagement, and knowledge-worker productivity.

Pranav has a diverse background that allows him to approach technology and business challenges from multiple perspectives and he works hard to understand his clients' true business needs before proposing a technology solution. Pranav has successfully worked for prestigious firms including Accenture Technology Consulting, and Portal Solutions, a boutique SharePoint provider. He is also a Microsoft Certified SharePoint Solution Developer (MCTS), SharePoint IT Professional (MCITP) and Certified Trainer (MCT). Pranav is an alumnus of the University of Maryland with degrees in Computer Engineering and Business Management.
