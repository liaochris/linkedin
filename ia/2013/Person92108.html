Mike Kirby's Summary

As a systems integrator and information technology company, General Communications provides communications products and services to a wide variety of customers both nationally and internationally.

These solutions include things such as audio and video systems, control systems, assistive listening devices (ADA compliance), conferencing, video teleconferencing, digital signage, IP telephony, and many other A/V products.

General Communications has provided thousands of communications solutions to customers in every state in the United States of America, in every province in Canada, and in numerous countries around the world. We are also a proud GSA member.
