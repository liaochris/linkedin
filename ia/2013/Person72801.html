Kathi Minsky's Summary

Bestselling Author, Speaker, Trainer, Network Marketer.
I have a passion to help people who are struggling to figure out how to live comfortably in retirement without relying on their family, friends or the government.
For the past 15 years, I have made a 6 figure passive residual income. So many start with that dream in network marketing and never achieve it.
My goal is simple:
Help you understand HOW we make money and the skills you will need to succeed.
Help you choose a products or services that will drive your passion to succeed.
Help you choose the right company that will support your goals.
What to do next?
My book, Eliminate Your Retirement Gap is designed to take you through the process so that you can determine if this profession is right for you.
Read the book. Take the test. If you choose Network marketing, you can go forward with confidence.
