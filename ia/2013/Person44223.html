Fawzi Rahal's Summary

I have over thirteen years of experience in the digital field in the Middle East.

Founder and Managing partner at gamutt, a strategic digital consultancy working with clients, brands, agencies, startups and content owners to devise and optimize their digital strategy and presence across web mobile, social media, content and design

Over the last nine years while heading digital at Grey Middle East and North Africa, I have helped launch, rebrand and manage online channels for the world's leading brands including Nokia, Visa, Danone and Pantene. As a key member of the new business team, I focused on integrated marketing and communications strategies to help optimize client budget and maximize campaign exposure. I have a strong creative background and solid strategic and marketing experience in telecom, blue chip, nutrition and FMCG.

A typographer and designer by training, I have helped clients establish online identities and strategies across web, mobile and social media.

Started my career at Leo Burnett in 2000 by creating and launching the first online Arabic presence for clients like Nokia, General Motors, Kellogg’s and Philip Morris before joining Fortune Promoseven.

Moved to Grey Dubai in 2004 and started building a team which handled all of Nokia MEA's digital platforms and CRM programs until 2009. Relocated to Grey Beirut early 2010 to head digital in the region reporting to the CDO. Handled digital for the entire portfolio of Grey (Pantene, Fairy, Pringles, Dubai Airports, GSK, BAT) in addition to digital-only clients including Visa CEMEA, Pfizer, and Danone.

Also had the role of Regional Communications Director, as part of the new business team, with a focus on strategy, integration, creative conceptualization, digital activation and digital identities.

I'm a hardcore geek with love for anything creative. Founded The Next Web Middle East and was its editor until mid-2011.
