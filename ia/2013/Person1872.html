Adlene Ezzekmi, PA's Summary

Adlene spent more than ten years in the hospitality industry and six years in business, tourism and economic studies, Real Estate came to be the perfect fit for his career. Going to both George Washington University in Washington DC and George Mason University in Northern Virginia, thought him a great deal of discipline and competitiveness.

Excellence in Real estate requires all, and not less than market and community knowledge, trust, integrity, efficiency and effectiveness, negotiation skills, and professionalism. Adlene has been living in Miami for more than 9 years, has earned strong product knowledge and a wide web of strategic networks. His motto is to put his clients first, keeping himself accessible, and providing efficient delivery.

By getting in touch with him, you are one step closer to fulfilling your needs in Real Estate. Whether you want to rent, buy, sell or just in need of information, he is at your complete disposal.



Adlene provides the following Specialties: Real Estate sales, purchase, income properties, land aquisition, residential rentals, commercial leases, and property monitoring.
