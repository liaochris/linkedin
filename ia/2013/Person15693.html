Bonnie Giller's Summary

My name is Bonnie R. Giller and I help people who are tired of struggling with dieting or health conditions like diabetes and IBS take back control so they can get the healthy body they love and live their lives symptom-free.

I help you put these 3 pieces of the puzzle together. Completing this puzzle is the answer you have been looking for to finally get off the diet roller coaster and into a life and body you love and feel great in.

Are you someone who has battled your weight and has gone on and off every new diet that hits the market without reaching your goal?

If you’ve ever tried to lose weight by restricting foods or by trying to follow strict diets, you know firsthand that diets don't work. Not only are they impossible to stick to long term, but you find yourself with a stronger food craving than when you started.

It is time to try a different approach; an approach that will give you the tools for lifelong success…the Intuitive Eating approach.

Simply put, intuitive eating is eating when you are hungry and stopping when you are comfortably full.

Intuitive eating will help you create a healthy relationship with food, mind, and body. You will learn how to distinguish between physical and emotional hunger and focus on recognizing and responding to your inner body signals which may be disguised by emotional factors and years of dieting and food myths. Intuitive eating is a process of making peace with food so you no longer have to worry about eating a “bad” or “fattening” food. You will be able to achieve a healthy weight and never have to worry about dieting again!

Health Solutions Strategist

BRG Health Solutions, a division of BRG Dietetics & Nutrition, P.C. - Owner

Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

November 1987 – Present (26 years 2 months)

If you are suffering with other medical conditions, such as irritable bowel syndrome, reflux, diverticulosis, high cholesterol, high blood pressure, heart disease, or fatigue, you would benefit from a customized health solution through nutrition therapy.

Medical Nutrition Therapy involves the assessment of your nutritional status, a review and analysis of your medical and diet history, laboratory values, and anthropometric measurements (height, weight, percent ideal body weight, BMI (body mass index), waist/hip ratio, body measurements).

Based on this assessment, nutrition intervention most appropriate to manage your condition is chosen.

The application of medical nutrition therapy and lifestyle counseling as a part of the nutrition care process is an integral component of the medical treatment for management of specific disease states and conditions and should be the initial step in the management of these situations.

Better yet, nutrition should be used to prevent these conditions.
