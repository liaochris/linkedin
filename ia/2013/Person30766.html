Darren Fell's Summary

Darren is a disruptor. Having changed the email marketing industry ten years ago with a cost effective, anti-spam solution, he is now challenging the crusty old world of accountancy – and traditional accountants hate him for it!

An experienced entrepreneur, Darren’s latest venture Crunch.co.uk is the UK's first totally integrated online accountancy service providing each customer with a team of accountants at the end of the phone. Crunch enables unrivalled levels of customer service at a much lower cost, and was specifically created to fulfil the needs of freelancers, contractors and small business owners.

It has featured in national newspapers, appeared on Radio 2’s Drivetime show, as well as winning the ‘Innovation in Business’ accolade at the Brighton and Hove Business Awards (BAHBAs). High profile online entrepreneurs - Chairman of Skype Michael van Swaaij and co-Founder of Bebo Paul Birch - are investors in the company.

FreelanceAdvisor.co.uk was launched by Darren back in early 2008. It now has around 30,000 readers every month. On the back of this success, Darren launched ContractorAdvisor.co.uk to support the needs of the UK’s contractor workforce.
Prior to founding Crunch, Darren built a top UK digital marketing agency from scratch with only £100k investment. Pure now employs over 60 people and has over 2,000 clients. Darren took the company to the point of a multi-million dollar sale in 2008.

Pure was named eleventh fastest growing new media company in the UK by investment bank GP Bullhound’s Media Momentum Top 50 2007 league table, and Best Small Business at the Sussex Business Awards 2006/07. Darren won Entrepreneur of the Year at the same ceremony in 2007/08.

Darren now helps many other entrepreneurs for free and is passionate about the flourishing freelancing and small business community. He is often found speaking at seminars and large shows giving advice to small businesses and helping implement sales regimes that really make businesses work.
