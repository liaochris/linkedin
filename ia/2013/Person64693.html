John Migueis' Summary

I am a licensed therapist with 15 years of experience in the field as a Clinician, Supervisor and Administrator.

I have been working with individuals, couples and families since 1998. The majority of my work has been with adolescents and adults who struggle with a variety of psychiatric, emotional and behavioral issues in individual and group settings. Much of my work has also involved family and couples therapy which can be critical components to individual treatment. Currently I maintain a private practice and am a therapist within an Adult and Adolescent PC/IOP program.

I also have extensive experience with Program Management, Budgeting, Grant Writing and Quality Improvement activities. I am very knowledgeable about technology and for a time owned a small web development firm.

Specialties: Trauma, Anxiety, Adolescent Issues, Depression, Anger Management, Family Issues, Relationship Issues, Parenting, Impulse Control Disorders and Personality Disorders
