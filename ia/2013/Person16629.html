Brant Bukowsky's Summary

Since 1997, my brother and I have started companies in various industries.

In 2005, our first company, ShowMe Tickets, was acquired by industry leader TicketsNow (who was later acquired by Ticketsmaster). In 2007 our vacation rental company, LakeRentals.com, was acquired by The Weather Channel. Three of our companies received the Inc 500 award, for being one of fastest growing private companies.

Without question, the greatest reason for growth of any of these companies is attributed to the fact that we work with an unbelievable group of people. It is inspiring to be part of such an awesome team and they amaze me daily.

My focus is to work with our great team to help Veterans United Home Loans achieve long term success and fulfill the company mission of "Enhancing Lives".
