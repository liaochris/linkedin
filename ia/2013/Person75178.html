Kevin Rutherford's Summary

I am an experienced software developer, project leader and coach with over 30 years' experience in hands-on software delivery. I am a Chartered Engineer and have been Managing Director of a successful consulting business and architect of several million-pound enterprise solutions. In recent years I have coached numerous software teams to more than double their speed and quality of delivery.

I am author of the book "Refactoring in Ruby" and the Reek code quality tool for Ruby.

As a software developer I rely on agile principles, with strong emphasis on rapid, sustained, incremental delivery of tangible results and the creation of defect-free software.

Specialties: Agile project leadership, coaching, mentoring, project rescue, extreme programming, test-driven development, object-oriented design, refactoring
