Jonathan Bollag, MBA.'s Summary

I have long immersed myself in the study and practice of career and life coaching. Through CareerAssessmentSite.com I provide career, personality, and interest inventory assessments. I also offer focused one-on-one career coaching sessions, career counseling, resume building support, and job search guidance with highly personalized interpretations.

I excel at translating an individual’s specific interests, preferences, and needs into a rich and fulfilling work and personal life, and in providing direction for those seeking a new professional career.

My fields of study have included clinical psychology, business management, and executive coaching.

GOALS

To continue to build and expand CareerAssessmentSite.com by making the leading assessment inventories -- the Myers Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), the Fundamental Interpersonal Relations Orientation-Behavior (Firo-B), the Thomas-Kilmann Conflict Mode Instrument (TKI), and the Strong Interest Inventory -- and other invaluable career- and life-enriching tools available and affordable to people from all economic, social, and geographical backgrounds.

Specialties

Career assessments such as the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator and Strong Interest Inventory, career coaching, personality profiling, executive coaching, and human resource management.

Jonathan Bollag, MBA.'s Experience

Owner

Career Assessment Site

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Consumer Services industry

January 2007 – Present (7 years) Goleta California

Career Assessment Site is a career assessment and interpretation website geared toward helping individuals find the career that best suits them, making their lives more fulfilling and successful.

Careerassessmentsite.com offers the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), a personality assessment based on Carl Jung's theory of Psychology; the Strong Interest Inventory (Strong), an interest inventory based on Holland's theory of vocational choice; and the FIRO-B, a powerful interrelationship assessment that explores how the respondent prefers to relate to others and vice-versa. The site also offers the Thomas-Kilmann Conflict Mode Instrument (TKI), a thorough assessment on how an individual or a corporate team handles and manages conflict.

Other specialized variations of these four assessment types are included, such as college reports for students, team and leadership reports for businesses and/or individuals, and work styles reports for employees.

Jonathan has earned his B.A., MBA , and Executive Coaching Certificate. He is licensed and certified to administer MBTI, Strong Interest Inventory, and Fundamental Interpersonal Relations Orientation-Behavior (Firo-B) assessements. Personal consultations, evaluations, and interpretations are available.

Jonathan can be contacted via email at info@careerassessmentsite.com and by telephone at 1-805-729-6433.
