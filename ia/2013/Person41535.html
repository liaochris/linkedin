Why we are different

Encore Direct Marketing will look after your marketing as if we were your internal marketing department. All of our clients get to choose if the want call tracking to track call responses, they also get call reporting as well. We have dedicated analysts who will look, watch, and analyze calls on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis. By doing this, we can be proactive as to changes in the industry and by having this information we can be as efficient as we need to be. We don't market your company with our eyes closed.

Bundle of Services

Encore Direct Marketing has the option to bundle your services. You can get as basic as just mailing out a custom direct mail campaign, or you can add value services to help you be a better company. We have partnered up with multiple companies to help us out with technology so that we can streamline our bundled services. Not only can we send direct mail for you, we can also track where your mail is, watch your calls, listen to your calls, generate call reports, generate campaign responses, and most importantly, we can give you this data so you can make strategic marketing decisions.
