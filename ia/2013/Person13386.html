Jason Bequette's Summary

I consider myself a visual craftsman, working in the hybrid space of digital and physical realities. I love the tactile feel of paper, but also live comfortably in the digital theater. I look at my process as storytelling, connecting with audiences through a vision journey, with intent and levity. This happens through a focus on well-crafted, elegantly simple design and direction.

By day I work at TD Ameritrade, crafting fine learning experiences and communications. I also freelance under the moniker Ocean Tied Creative Company to further my artistic ventures.

Specialties: Instructional & communication design, print, creative direction, corporate video production, brand implementation, and ideation.
