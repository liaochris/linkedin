Jeff Ross' Summary

I've been working as a creative artist since I was about 5 years old, when I figured out the magical properties of crayon and paper when combined with my brain. Ever since then, the blank space on the back of my school work was never safe again.

After a childhood influenced by Saturday morning cartoons and Cocoa Puffs, I vowed to use my creativity for the betterment of all mankind. I've worked in the Treasure Valley since 2004, and have had the pleasure working with companies like HP, Disney, Random House and Idaho Department of Health and Welfare. I'm now working as an Art Director at True Science, a pet health and wellness company.

Specialties: Flash animation, pen & ink/digital illustration, web/UI design, front-end web development (xhtml/css), identity/brand design, collateral/print design
