Laurie Guest, CSP's Experience

Owner

Solutions Are Brewing

2001 – Present (12 years) DeKalb, IL

During my 17 year employment at the Hauser-Ross Eye Institute my primary focus was creating and managing programs that attracted and maintain new patients. This include a very successful referral network program, public outreach programs, and a successful patient based volunteer program.

Our organization was known for it's customer service and physicians from miles around would ask what about our secrets. Now as a professional speaker I share the best kept secrets of healthcare service.

After eleven years as the owner of Solutions Are Brewing, professional speaking and training company I have found that all industries, not just healthcare clients, are interested in these secrets. Today I speak full time nationwide on how to attract and keep new customers and develop a team that can deliver the best service possible.

Certified Speaking Professional

National Speakers Association

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

1997 – Present (16 years)

This is a national association of professional speakers based in Tempe, AZ. Through this association I earned the honor of Certified Speaking Professional (CSP) a designation held by less than 10% of speakers worldwide. (Less than 500 of which are women.)

This designation is important because it can only be earned after several successful benchmarks are met including client volume, income level, testimonials in regard to the quality of the work. It cannot be earned without significant dedication and time in the speaking industry.
