John Schonegevel's Projects

  • Leadership Development

    Team Members: John Schonegevel, Susan Scott

    The CEO of a financial regulator identified the need for senior managers to add more value to the organisation and to be sufficiently skilled to be promoted, when vacancies arose. We created and ran a bespoke behavioural change programme for these senior managers which included internal communications, individual development coaching, four team workshops and several psychometric assessments.

    20 managers attended; leading to new ways of working, improved internal communications and resource sharing, which is resulting in better regulation of the finance industry.

  • Leadership Development - Financial Services

    Team Members: John Schonegevel, Susan Scott

    The CEO of this specialist insurance provider was concerned that he had more business opportunities than suitably qualified executives to deliver them. We were tasked with developing a bespoke Executive Development Programme for senior executives in UK, Europe and Australasia (45).

    During the four years we ran it, the programme had a significant impact on business and personal performance. One new product was launched (projected revenue is £14.5M per annum) as a direct result of the project based approach we took throughout the programme and several managers promoted to major leadership roles across the world.

  • Business Development - Making a difference to sales

    • March 2011 to Present
    Team Members: John Schonegevel, Susan Scott

    One of our clients recently described how they were having their best ever year for winning new clients and new contracts and were on course for 7.6% revenue growth and a total of £103 million of net secured business.

    We've been working with them throughout this period; challenging and changing the behaviours of their business development teams, transforming the dialogue they enter into with their clients and enabling them to develop innovative new solutions to more effectively meet market needs and create more value.

  • Leading a start up venture

    • January 2009 to June 2009
    Team Members: John Schonegevel

    The Board of this specialist data and services provider wished to better develop its brand in consumer and business markets. It identified a need to establish a new Limited company start up. I led a project team of 8, including employees and external consultants, to set this up ensuring that it was aligned to corporate values and stakeholder requirements, as well as having a credible customer focus, a realistic product development programme and an agreed business plan. The company was launched in May 2009
