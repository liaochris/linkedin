Mark Barnett, MBA, Ph.D., CPP Master's Summary

Mark is an execution-focused practitioner with over 25 years of external and internal consulting experience in Business Process Management (BPM) and Process Improvement (PI), Customer Experience Management, and Outside In. He has developed transformative business solutions for R&D / product development, process design and deployment, and product and service delivery operations in the Oil and Gas / Energy, Financial Services, Insurance, Telecommunications and Aerospace / Aviation industries.

Recognized for consistent delivery of outstanding outcomes, Mark achieves this through deep, direct and ongoing involvement from strategy definition through execution of the steps needed to achieve outstanding results that benefit customers, stakeholders and shareholders. His foremost goal is always the delivery of outcomes that enable increased revenue and service quality while simultaneously reducing operating costs, to achieve breakthrough levels of business performance.

• Mark defines and drives all aspects of end-to-end change (strategy, process, culture / behavior, systems, data and work flow, metrics and measures, business rules, and organizational design), influencing key stakeholders, aligning IT and Business resources, and improving identification, prioritization, and execution of company-critical programs and projects.

• He has delivered successful transformation at all scales, from tactical to enterprise-wide change, encompassing the people, process and technology aspects of the business in every initiative and across the entire change portfolio. Mark identifies and delivers the tools and systems that support the target state.

• Mark possesses exceptional interpersonal, coaching, mentoring and facilitation skills contributing to significant success in building high-performance teams, improving cross-functional collaboration, breaking down barriers between departments and organizations, and enhancing alignment, cohesion and system thinking.
