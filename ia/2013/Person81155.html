Lindi Wheaton's Summary

I am a focused and ambitious web / graphic artist with experience in layout, design and maintaining client relationships. I am effective in strong communication skills while staying organized and remaining creatively active with projects. I want to grow personal and professional relations and experiences in the graphic design world by working and networking with people from all over the world. I am a reliable, motivated, organized individual who has interests in social networking, ui design, web design, dashboards and admin panels.

At the present moment I’m a full time web / graphic designer at Channel-Fusion in Hiawatha, Iowa. Since 2008 I have been a dedicated web designer for my freelance company, The-Pixel. I had the pleasure of landed a rewarding job position as a Level 1 Technician at JaguarPC, which allowed me to work remotely.
