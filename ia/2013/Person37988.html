    • Leadership Team Advisor and Host: Annual Greater San Diego Regional Track Meet

      Special Olympics Southern California - San Diego County
      • Children
      April 1992 – present (21 years 6 months)

      Point Loma Nazarene University has hosted this event for over 20 years as an opportunity for our students, faculty and staff to serve and as an outreach to our community and specifically to the disabled community of San Diego. It serves as a stellar event for the PLNU community and broader community in sharing and supporting the needs of those in Special Olympics and provides a venue for the broader San Diego community to experience and see the values and service mission of PLNU.

      The Event is co-sponsored by a cross section of Departments, Schools and Student Development.     • Leadership Team Advisor and Host: Annual DSA Easter Egg Hunt and Parent Reception

      Down Syndrome Action (DSA) - Rady's Children's Hospital
      • Children
      April 2010 – present (3 years 6 months)

      Point Loma Nazarene University has hosted this event the past three years with great success. Each year we host the Easter Egg Hunt for children with Down Syndrome and the reception for parents and family members. PLNU students, community service organizations, and community volunteers provide games, songs, and entertainment for the children and families - including the annual visit of the Easter Bunny.

      This has become a highly supported annual event that supports and nurtures our relationship with these families and the disabled community of San Diego County every year. This event raises awareness about special needs in our community while giving our students that lead and volunteer the opportunity to reach out, with the love of Christ, to the greater San Diego community, specifically the special needs community. All benefits are in support of the Down Syndrome Center at Rady Children's Hospital through the work of the Down Syndrome Action organization http://www.downsyndromeaction.org/
