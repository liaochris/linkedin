Alan Ram's Summary

Upon being honorably discharged from the United States Marine Corps, Alan began a career in the Automotive industry at what would later become the nation's second highest volume Nissan dealership. Alan was promoted through every position on the front end of the dealership, up to General Sales Manager by age 25. In 1991, after realizing the tremendous need for high-quality automotive sales & management training, Alan launched Proactive Training Solutions, Inc.

Not only does Proactive Training Solutions work with premier automotive groups nationwide, Alan's workshop was officially the #1 attended workshop at the 2012 NADA Convention.

Alan is recognized by most as the best in the automotive business at increasing individual, as well as dealership productivity through effective utilization of the telephone, Internet and client base management. He has worked and continues to work with tens of thousands of salespeople, managers, business development and Internet staff from coast to coast, increasing individual and group productivity.

Alan was also selected as a training partner by NADA University because of his proven ability to increase individual as well as dealership productivity through effective utilization of the telephone, Internet and client-base management. His 'Management by Fire' workshops are now endorsed and promoted by NCM Associates.

Apart from business, Alan is an experienced private pilot as well as an avid fan of professional football, tennis and hockey. As a proud sponsor of the Children's First Academy in Phoenix, Arizona, Alan enjoys giving back to the community through his personal charitable foundation, The Janeen Barber Foundation. "Sometimes all anyone needs is one opportunity. Through Children's First Academy, I know I am helping to provide that chance to as many children as possible," said Alan.
