Doug Richards' Summary

My passion drives everything I involve myself in. I am fiercely independent and relentlessly driven to change the world.

I love to teach. I love to create. I am obsessively in pursuit of technology that will leave a legacy. Getting rich is easy - I'd rather be remembered.

I lead my management consulting firm, B2i Ventures, with single minded purpose. Our mission is simple; make it count. We believe in individuals and their ability to create change, so we serve individuals inside organizations using Gamification, Technology Strategy, Training and Creative Catalyst services. Through B2i Ventures I manage early-stage to series B start-ups as an owner/operator with markets ranging from a few million annual hyperlocal deployments to multi-billion global innovators.

In addition I lead Alinare; a Cohen-Esrey company. Alinare is a cloud-based loan management system serving community banks, credit unions and economic development councils across the USA. I am also the acting CEO for Your State ID, and the Chief Innovation Officer for the Innovation Incubator.

I'm lucky that people like to listen to what I think, so I occasionally travel the world speaking. My latest book; Artificial Business Intelligence - <Hack Your Company> - is available 10.18.13
