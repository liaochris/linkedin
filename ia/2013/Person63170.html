Jessica Samph's Publications

  • "Serving the Millennial Generation: Advertising Appeals and Consumer Attitudes towards Services"

    • 3rd International Conference Proceedings: "An Enterprise Odyssey: Integration or Disintegration" Zagreb, Croatia, pp.239-241.
    • May 2006
    Authors: Jessica Samph

    A test of reactions of young American consumers to ad appeals designed for various services. Three types of appeals (individualistic, collectivist, and functional) that were designed for different product type categories used in the FCB grid (informative, feeling, habit-forming, and self-satisfaction goods) were tested to address this objective with a convenience sample of young Americans. The results of the study show that responses to ads do not depend on the appeal type but rather on the service type that is presented in the ad. Implications of this study and future research suggestions are discussed in the paper.
