Erica Palgon's Summary

ABOUT ERICA PALGON

CASTING

Casting Director for ALL types of commercials (on-camera casting and voice over casting), indie and studio film casting, theater, and real people casting. SAG and Non Union casting.

Focuses on in-depth casting, and exploring new ways of discovering unique talent. Going BEYOND! Erica’s passion and eye for discovering new talent stands out in all of the projects she has cast.


DIRECTING

Erica has watched many directors over the years (among them John Cameron Mitchell, Tom DiCillo, Jesse Peretz, Matt Aselton, Phil Morrison, Jeff Preiss, David Gordon Green, Pam Thomas, Kinka Usher, Frank Samuel, Stacy Wall, Morgan J Freeman), and has taken notes on different styles of directing. This combined with the many casting directors she has worked with have allowed her to develop her own natural techniques in directing, inventive casting ideas and teaching methods.

Actors find her energy contagious. Clients feel safe and comfortable with her, even in the most pressured situations. Her lightness and sense of humor allows for all “parties” to have fun and be creative.

COMEDY CASTING

One of Erica Palgon specialties is comedy casting. She searches for up and coming comedic talent for projects as well as coach actors who want to perfect their comedic performances or are just breaking into the comedy/improv world. Erica also recently threw herself even further in the trenches of the comedy world, taking improv classes at The PIT (The People’s Improv Theater), to get an up close and personal understanding of the ins and outs of this challenging form of performing. She is now moving onto Level 3 Improv.


COACHING

Erica’s company also covers on-camera acting and business of acting classes, private one on one career counseling and on-camera coaching for actors of all ages.

Specialties

Strong background in all areas of casting. Specialties are comedy/improv as well as real people casting. Often referred for challenging casting searches.

Freelance Casting Director for Grey Advertising.
