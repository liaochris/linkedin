Elizabeth Bacon's Summary

Today I'm both an entrepreneur (founder & president of Find Wellness) and a user experience designer (principal of Devise Consulting). I apply my customer research, design and management skills to identify market opportunities and define innovative and meaningful solutions.

Generally I'm a highly strategic designer and consultant who specializes in delivering novel yet feasible solutions for complex systems. My expertise spans the full breadth of the user experience design fields, with very strong skills in understanding user needs, defining innovative products & services, and communicating well with cross-disciplinary stakeholders.

I'm extremely passionate about improving the state of our healthcare & medical systems. I have successfully addressed the critical needs of patients, providers and payors throughout my career. I understand the highly-regulated medical device domain, and am familiar with both U.S. and international human factors and regulatory guidance documents. I can provide high-level process guidance as well as design and usability support in this area.

One of my principal life goals is to make the world a better place. To that end, I help advance the profession of interaction design, and served for 3 years on the Board of Directors of the Interaction Design Association (IxDA). In 2010, I was the lead designer, creative director & project manager for the organization's website (http://www.ixda.org). Today, my efforts to improve people's lives with brilliant technology continues with Find Wellness.

Specialties: user experience design (especially interaction design & information design); design research (especially personas); strategic consulting (especially defining UX process & providing training); product management (especially for healthcare); and public speaking. I'm also crazy about high-performance driving, and I'm a nationally-certified high-speed driving instructor. Aussi, je parle Français!

Elizabeth Bacon's Experience

President & Founder

Find Wellness

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

July 2012 – Present (1 year 6 months) Portland, Oregon Area

Find Wellness is like OpenTable meets WebMD—for holistic care. We offer a qualified wellness provider director for consumers where people can find the right provider for their needs and make connections online. We also deliver customer relationship management tools for natural health & wellness practitioners that help them grow their businesses. Additionally, we're a smart data play, operating in digital health at the intersection of tools for consumer engagement and personal health tracking. The western medical system needs to be transformed, and we aim to promote powerful holistic approaches to health and wellness in America.

As the interim Director of Product, I defined the user experience, positioned the product in the marketplace, supported development of the website and related social media properties, and generally helped to address the breadth of customer-centric concerns needing to be tackled for this new business to get underway.

Sr. Human Factors Design Engineer

St. Jude Medical

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; STJ; Medical Devices industry

July 2002 – May 2007 (4 years 11 months)

I led the creation of user-centered software solutions for St. Jude Medical's Cardiac Rhythm Management Division, collaborating closely with cross-disciplinary team members. By re-orienting the key clinical product to work well for nurses, I helped the company move from a distant 3rd in the market to a close 2nd. I also formalized a powerful system for medical product design by combining interaction design principles and best practices with classic Human Factors methods. My processes included ethnographic research, mockup and prototype development, and requirements definition. I also led usability testing of proposed solutions and participated in detailed design reviews throughout the requirements, implementation and validation phases to ensure that the final product met customers' expectations.
