Amanda Abella's Summary

Amanda Abella is a writer, speaker and life coach based out of Miami. Recently featured on Forbes.com, HuffPost Live and The Consumerist Amanda combines her passion for lifestyle design with career development to help ambitious young professional women feel confident, find their purpose and take major action toward creating a career (and a life) they truly desire.

For more information and access to Amanda's Set Fire to Your Goals Workshop visit http://www.amandaabella.com

Services: workshops, writing, speaking, private coaching

Past speaking engagements: American Student Assistance, Local Levo Miami, Women of Tomorrow, Pure Barre Miami
