Doreen M. McCullough, Esq., R.N.,MBA's Summary

I am an attorney with a bachelor degree in nursing and a master's
degree in business with a concentration in pharmaceutical-chemical studies. I worked for 10-years as a registered nurse in a large medical center in the New York metropolitan area. In the late eighties I entered the pharmaceutical industry and subsequently obtained a position with Ortho-Biotech, Inc. a division of Johnson & Johnson. I am very familiar with drug development and the FDA process. I co-authored the Orphan Drug application for Hairy Cell Leukemia for Leustatin and also was one of the co-authors for an article on myelodysplasia in the British Journal of Hematology. I did a summer externship for a NJ Superior Court Criminal Judge in Hackensack, NJ during law school; after graduation I worked as an associate in a New York Medical Malpractice Defense firm and also was an associate in a NY Firm that was national supervisory counsel for Pepsi Bottling Group, LLC

Specialties

Pharmacuetical Products Liability, Medical Malpractice, Personal
Injury, Estate Planning, Elder Law, Guardianships, Special Needs Trusts for children with autism and other special needs as well as adults with chronic illnesses such as multiple sclerosis, ALS, dementia, stroke
