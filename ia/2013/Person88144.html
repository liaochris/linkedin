Mary Liu | Inspirational Speaker

Empowered Women Speakers (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

January 2012 – Present (1 year 9 months) San Francisco, CA

As an inspirational speaker, I share my story of overcoming my personal challenges of shame, low self-esteem, and feelings of not being worthy stemmed from my facial birth defect and how that affected my personal and professional life. It was not until I had the courage to speak my truth that my life took on a new direction.

One of my many stories I share is how I overcame the fear of public speaking to addressing an audience of 18,000 people at an international convention within two years.

I share my story through keynote speeches, workshops, and in my book. Audiences love my ability to take them on an emotional roller coaster ride throughout my storytelling.

I have delivered motivational talks on stage in front of 3,000 to 18,000 entrepreneurs at international conventions and have appeared numerous times on international television on the topic of sales & marketing.

My vision is to create a worldwide movement to empower women to find their voice and to step up and stand out to be bold, to be brilliant, and to be seen in all their greatness!

I speak at conferences, conventions, women’s organizations, corporations, networking events, radio shows, and on television. I am also available for interviews for teleseminars, webinars, and podcasts.
