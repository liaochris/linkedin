Angel Palomino's Summary

Independent consultant to the hospitality industry and hotel investors fully committed to assist his clients in the fulfillment of their objectives in the most efficient way and in the shortest possible term.

Since 1991 and up to his resignation, at the end of 2006, Angel Palomino served as Executive Vice President and member of Sol Melia‘s (today Melia Hotels International) Executive Board.

In July 1998 he became EVP for Global Development of Sol Meliá, driving the continued growth of the company and developing new business concepts and company Divisions such as the Sol Meliá Vacation Network, the Asset Management Division and several Joint Ventures and alliances with companies such as Hard Rock and Cendant.

Has been a speaker at several industry forums, including the International Hotel Investment Forum, European Hotel Industry Investment Conference, the American Bar Association / International Bar Association, the Madrid Summer University, in El Escorial, the Spain/China Forum, the ESADE Business School…

Among his clients are the Hotetur/Blue Bay, Parquesol, Compagnia Fondiaria Nazionale, Roma Studios, Real Estate Services Poland, Avenue Capital Group, Monitor Group, TPG, Jumeirah, Liberbank....

He is as well advising on specific projects to a select number of private investors, hotel owners and developers, architects, financing institutions and law firms.

General Secretary for the Industry at the II and III China-Spain Tourism Congress.

Member of the Advisory Board of Master in Hospitality & Tourism Management ESCP Europe Business School.

Member of the International Researchers Guild, Toledo

(A summary of his most recent advisory activities is included in the hotelexper.com section)

Specialties: Global and multilingual consultant with the highest experience in the hotel management, marketing, investment and development activities.
