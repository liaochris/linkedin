Paul Furey's Summary

I uploaded my first "website" in 1997. It was actually a web page (my account page on www.indigo.ie) just after Netscape had just made things easier with website browsing!

My first client website was launched 10/1/1998 and consisted of 17 madly named htm web pages and 45 image files, no home page text or keywords! It always amused the client whenever he received an enquiry from places like Japan! There was no concept of website Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) in thos days!

In 2006 I was introduced to the science of SEO and search engine optimised my entertainment website so it appears continuosly in the top three in Google organic search results. I build and maintain Search Engine Friendly websites and manage their page rankings/Adwords campaigns once these sites have been uploaded.

I also started semi professionally in the entertainment world in June 1992 where I was part of a team that developed children's entertainment for corporate functions and for private events. I have performed in corporate and private events that have been both secured through my own resources and through various agencies.
