Lisa J. Jackson's Summary

As a writer and copyeditor, I enjoy crafting a new piece of writing of any length or revitalizing and updating what already exists, to have it say what you want it to say in an appealing and concise manner.

That's how I developed my company's motto: Your words, only better.

Every business and individual has a unique 'voice' - it's what makes you stand apart from the crowd. I can help you make a positive, lasting impression on your audience while keeping your voice intact.

As a business content writer and business blogger, I assist businesses in extending their market reach and showcasing their area(s) of expertise.

I have over twenty-five years of combined writing and editing experience. I work with large and small businesses across the globe. I'm also a registered writing and editing contractor with the federal government.

I'm able to assist you with several types of content including: business profiles, case studies, white papers, success stories, business articles with SEO, ad copy, Web page content, process manuals, technical guides, brochures, marketing collateral, and more.

As a writer and regional journalist, I enjoy writing about anything related to New England - businesses, destinations, people, and events that can show the variety and passion that exists in this part of the country.

Specialties: Small businesses, New Hampshire, Article writing, Freelance editing, Book editing, Copy editing, Journalistic writing on the New England region, Community journalism, Business content writing, Business blogging, business case studies,
Business profile writing, Editing e-learning courseware, Technical writing, Process guide writing, Marketing communications editing, Non-fiction editing, Small business communication, Retaining company or individual 'voice'
