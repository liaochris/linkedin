Ed de la Fuente's Summary

Over 15 years of experience leading the development and launch of consumer technology products with organizations ranging from start-up to market-leading Fortune 100. I’m passionate about gaining deep insights into consumer needs and buying behavior to develop successful go-to-market and e-commerce strategies. I specialize in leading marketing and product teams through planning and execution of multi-platform launch programs. Enjoy creating profitable growth by fostering collaborative relationships both within and around the organization. Colleagues would describe me as the person that defines the path, gets the team on-board, then moves everyone towards common goals and success.
