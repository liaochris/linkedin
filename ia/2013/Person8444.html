ANNE vanschothorst's Experience

composer/producer & rec. harpist

harpandsoul.com

August 2007 – Present (6 years 1 month)

Anne has been described as 'an extraordinary musician' 'such a cool artist' and 'a highly emotive harpist'. Music critics characterize her work as 'musical innovative' 'original minimal' 'ambient' 'lyrical' and 'very touching emotional music'. She is one of the few creative and edgy harpists in the world who records original, contemporary classical, crossover + instant music for MultiMedia &ARTprojects.

Compositions have been published, CDs are international for sale and the audiorecordings can be licensed via 2 major US music agencies. Recent work has been placed at Discovery Communications (Discovery Channel / Animal planet / TlC a.o.) and the popular American PBS series called Closer to Truth (2012).

The videoclip DANCE ! MARIE DANCE! has been awarded in the ART ROCKS ! contest with a prof. video clip &audio recording + Anne had the rare opportunity to play live harp music at museum Boijmans van Beuningen next to La Petite Danseuse in room 23.

Anne is the initiator of the international and groundbreaking online (SoundCloud/Youtube) music competition : A BIRD CONTEST 2012

HARPmusic

harpandsoul.com

January 1989 – Present (24 years 8 months)

I have played background music (classical/pop/celtic/jazz melodies) as a harpist for several years @ weddings/ceremonies etc.

from age 9 untill age 20 i have played - the regular harp repertoire - and then because of fybromialgia i had to take a break from my harp playing (almost 15 years!)

and yet ... the harp has been always a part of me so in 2007 I decided to play only original music and discovered the freedom of improv. / composition & ART fusion projects

and that is why I have chosen a different format to share my music: cd/film/installations and my style has become my own.

very occasionly I organize/perform live @ music/art/film/poetry concepts
