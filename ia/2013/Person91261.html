Melissa Kuzma's Summary

After two decades in the pharmaceutical and software development industries, Melissa combined her love of technology and training and started Kuzma Technical Enterprises. Her vision is to help organizations bring their training to life on the web. Implementing the techniques and technologies unique to the eLearning industry, Melissa and her team deliver outstanding eLearning initiatives. Her commitment to quality, attention to detail, and the firm belief that customer service really does matter, form the foundation for Melissa’s continued success.

If you'd like to find out more about Melissa or Kuzma Technical Enterprises call 443.674.0544 or send an email to Melissa@KuzmaTechnical.com.

Specialties: eLearning, technology integration, web-based training design, database management, Articulate, Camtasia, Captivate, PowerPoint
