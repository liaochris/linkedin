Eva Velasco's Summary

Seasoned professional with extensive experience in product management and brand development for pharmaceutical and consumer brands. Broad international experience, my assignments have taken me from my native Spain to Europe and the US, where I worked for six years as Global Marketing Manager for Alcon Laboratories, driving the strategy and implementation for my brands around the world: pricing, patient modeling, market research, KOL development, CME and marketing programs to drive demand.

In my current role as Managing Director Spain of digital marketing agency Signature Pharma/Across Health, I help pharma brands seize their opportunities in the online space, developing programs involving healthcare professionals, patients and consumers always with a go-to market approach, ensuring they match the brand's business objectives and ultimately provide ROI.

Specialties

Fusion marketing (online and offline), multichannel campaigns, strategic planning and execution, online digital strategy, brand development, product management, global marketing, digital strategy, social media, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, business planning, market research, medical education, sales, remote detailing
