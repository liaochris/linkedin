Fredrik Häthén's Projects

  • FORCED

    • March 2012 to Present

    FORCED is a brand new gladiator combat-game, based on tactical cooperative 2-4 player gameplay either locally or online (also includes full single player game). Enslaved and forced to fight in mysterious arenas, the players must survive trial after trial, or face the consequences of their new power mongering master. The more they survive the more they learn about the dreadful purpose of the arenas. Their only ally is their mysterious mentor whose spirit is trapped in an orb. He has no empathy for the weak, yet he often holds the key to success and spiritual growth.

  • The Horror at MS Aurora

    • February 2011 to Present

    The Horror at MS Aurora is a modern horror adventure drawing inspiration from H.P Lovecraft and Stephen King. With AAA-production values, filmic visuals, and cinematic gameplay, it's built to be an intense and immersive experience, no longer than you average friday-night horror flick.

  • A Common Pulse

    • May 2009 to Present

    A Common Pulse is a game about the primitive joy of rhythm and music. There are few activities that makes us as happy as dancing! Bringing the aesthetics from the 'French Touch' genre, A Common Pulse creates a synesthetic experience where shapes and colors and rhythm comes together and reinforce each other. There are no sound effects, only music. As you progress throughout the level the music advances. The better you play, the more intense the music gets.

Fredrik Häthén's Publications

  • StarCaptain Original Soundtrack

    • Fredrik Häthén Music
    • April 16, 2013
    Authors: Fredrik Häthén

    StarCaptain (available on iOS and Android platforms) is a game about bouncing on stars. Why? Because Gloof - the StarCaptain himself - needs fuel for his spaceship and bouncing on stars is the only way to get it! The game started out as a concept created by Tobias Kärrman and was later developed into the finished product. For the music, the only real guideline was "cutesy and memorable" - something that has hopefully been accomplished!

  • Home - Original Soundtrack

    • Fredrik Häthén Music
    • January 24, 2013
    Authors: Fredrik Häthén

    "Home" is a small game that was created at Nordic Game Jam 2013 by Tobias Kärrman and Karin Bruér. The game is short and, like many game jam games, not quite finished. It was made with a lot of heart, however. The music from it received some praise and now it can be found as a digital download at fredrikhathen.bandcamp.com.

  • Different Beats / A Common Pulse

    • Fredrik Häthén Music
    • May 18, 2012
    Authors: Fredrik Häthén, Calle Leppäjoki

    "A Common Pulse" was a game made by Kian Bashiri, of "You Have To Burn The Rope" fame, and his friend Daniel Remle. It was essentially a remake of an older game called "Accelerando", which won a game concept challenge in Sweden. Not entirely happy with the look and feel of that game, Kian and Daniel went to work on a new version which they would later submit to the Swedish Game Awards competition. Gameplay was centered around a tube and a character that ran on its own along its surface. The player needed to jump, move left and right, collect items and not collide with obstacles.

    More info on the game can be found on Kian's site: mazapan.se/games/aCommonPulse.php

    The music people - Fredrik Häthén and his friend Calle Leppäjoki - had worked on "Accelerando" and they were happy to return for the next version. "A Common Pulse" was never released to the public, but this soundtrack is still there! The composers also remixed some of their music for this little mini-album. The idea for the album name was Kian's. Sure, there are different beats in the different tracks, but they all share the same pulse.
