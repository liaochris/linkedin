Jim Rosenberg's Summary

A results-driven yet collaborative strategist and leader with a demonstrated record of high-impact, global and national engagement and thought leadership programs leveraging knowledge, digital media, traditional media, live events and one-to-one interactions in the fields of microfinance, mobile telecom, banking, and international development. Skilled at integrating multimedia and social media with legacy communications. Award-winning public radio journalist for NPR affiliate WAMU-FM, Marketplace Radio, and Australian Broadcasting. Master’s in digital & broadcast from Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism.

AREAS OF EXPERTISE
Communications and issue management. Crisis communications, rapid response, influencer outreach and engagement. Stakeholder relations with private sector, governments, activists, bloggers, and journalists.

Digital media. Conceptualizing and executing a multiplatform content strategy. Global campaigns. Skilled and able to mentor others. Able to adapt to new trends, as well as advise leadership on the suitability of such trends in the operational context.

Event/speaker thought leadership. Obtaining high profile speaking engagements for leadership at global events. Event management and strategy. High impact speechwriting, talking points and presentations.

Staffing and resource management. Project and budget management, leading and mentoring direct reports as well as virtual teams.
