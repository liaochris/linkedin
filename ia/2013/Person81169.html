Lindsay Stenovec, MS, RD's Summary

As a registered dietitian, I understand that each individual approaches nutrition, weight maintenance and general health in a unique fashion. I believe that for many, the first step to weight maintenance or weight loss is forgetting about weight all together and focusing on an even more important goal- long-term, all encompassing health.
