Eileen Huang's Summary

A pianist and soprano, I've performed at Carnegie Hall, Lincoln Center, Symphony Hall, the Aspen and Tanglewood music festivals, and Fenway Park. For several years I worked in graphic design and marketing management. I have an MIT degree in Chemistry, two patents, and a small fraction of a Grammy Award. Getting things done makes me happy.
