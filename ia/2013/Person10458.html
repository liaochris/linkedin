Tammy Manners' Summary

A prim and proper gal. Elegant, glib and regal; Tammy has a knack for giving timely advice on all of life’s pressing problems. A yet discovered television and movie star, Tammy was de-pom-pommed as a college cheerleader, impeached as the university student body president, de-throwned as the homecoming queen and had her beauty agreement terminated with Mary Kay, Inc. A widow at the early age of 40 and mother of a precious little dog, "Jenny Craig." Tammy is the epitome of bouncing back. As she always says, “With a glib tongue and a ready smile, the world is your oyster!”

Specialties: The ability to make people feel at ease and comfortable. The gift of laughter.

A virtuoso in marketing and branding. Civil service and giving back to the community all that has been given to her. Be good to Tammy and Tammy will be good to you. Tammy regularly writes for an online magazine doing restaurant, theatrical and venue reviews. A published author and soon to be reality television star.
