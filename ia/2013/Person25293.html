Claire Rolston's Summary

Partner turned sole practitioner in July 2010...and never looked back! If you're looking for a down to earth, plain talking lawyer to sort out your employment problems, look no further. My ethos is simple - to make employment law clear for employers who have complex legal and HR issues thereby offering security and peace of mind.

Specialties

employment law, workplace disputes, unfair dismissal, discrimination, redundancy, employment tribunals, compromise agreements, employment contracts

I run a boutique law practice specialising in employment law. I advise employers of all sizes across a range of sectors on strategic planning involving staff, implementing best practice and procedure and defending employment claims in the employment tribunal. I also provide training to companies and HR Consultants on all aspects of employment law. My ethos is to give clear and practical advice/guidance to enable employers to make the right decisions for their business.
