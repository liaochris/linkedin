Maria Diaconu's Summary

Being passionate about software world and entrepreneurship, my focus is on supporting teams in these two areas in their way to deliver high quality results and maximize business value.

From Software Developer to Agile Lean Practitioner & Coach, and later on, entrepreneur, I'm supporting quality software, software craftsmanship and people passionate about their craft.

After many years coaching teams in agile and lean software development, now I have more focus on business agility and lean start-up methodology. Helping founders and managers to achieve excellence keeps me on a steep learning curve, which is extremely beneficial for my own development.

My volunteering work is dedicated from 2007/2008 to Romanian Agile Lean community, AgileWorks and later, Agile Lean European community. I'm also an active promoter of the Software Craftsmanship movement in Romania.

Personal values: Continuous Learning & Self Improving • Honesty & Integrity • Passionate • Highly adaptable • Constructive

Specialties:
