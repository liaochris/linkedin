IAN TARRANT's Summary

A proven achiever, Ian has improved results, built powerful teams through strong leadership, engaged clients and has added-value through service excellence across multiple lines of business.

He currently leads a group of sales professionals within the Mortgage Solutions Team at MCAP, and is charged with retaining and expanding the existing asset base of borrowers across Canada, while contributing to the strategic leadership of Single Family Servicing.

Before MCAP Ian led Direct Energy's contact centre operations, covering sales/service activities in Canada. His achievements included increased sales of protection plans, and cost cutting through well-executed vendor consolidation initiatives. He championed the development/launch of a ‘Quality Management System’, improving satisfaction scores and creating comfort for customers. He also led the ‘Employee Council’ and ‘Customer Council’ initiatives during his tenure.

At Maritz Canada his team improved fulfillment operations, and built a new contact centre that was critical to the launch of the Loyalty sector within the business in 2005. He was the first Canadian employee to participate in the company’s leadership program in the US.

His time at Canada Bread (Maple Leaf Foods) began in sales leading a team of franchise dealers in the London area, then was promoted to senior regional sales/operations positions throughout Ontario. He accepted a national role in 2000 where he directed cross-functional efforts to improve the fresh bakery supply chain and reduce costs across Canada. He took part in the ‘Leadership Academy’ at the Richard Ivey School of Business, and also trained as a Six Sigma ‘Black Belt’ while with the organization.

Ian has won sales awards in both B2B and B2C areas, been recognized for leadership and coaching skills over his career and is currently serving as President and Chairman on the Board of Directors for the Homer Watson House Foundation.

He is who you go to when you need to get things done!
