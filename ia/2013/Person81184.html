Lindsay LaVine's Summary

I'm a freelance writer with a number of articles that have appeared in various publications, including Entrepreneur.com, BlogHer.com, American Express Knowledge Center, Chicago Magazine, the Media Law Resource Center's Media Law newsletter and the Illinois State Bar Association's Intellectual Property newsletter.

I also contributed an essay to the book, "Reaching the Bar: Stories of Women at All Stages of Their Law Careers."

I frequently speak to and educate groups of lawyers, bloggers and performers about legal issues including defamation, copyright, trademark and right of publicity issues.

Prior to attending law school, I was a Media Coordinator at CNN and a writer/associate producer at NBC 17 in Raleigh, North Carolina. Oh, and I do standup for fun!
