Karen Stone, CCIM, MSW, LCC's Summary

As a Coach, I specialize in assisting people to reach their highest potential by sharing their unique gifts with others. Whether you are in transition, or ready to take the next logical step in your life or career, I provide support, creative energy and accountability so that you not only set goals, but reach them. As a speaker and trainer, I motivate and inspire by offering insights and tools that will serve you on your path. When you connect with your purpose and passion, the journey is fun and enlightening! Specialties: Equine assisted coaching, learning, and teambuilding, mastering uncertainty, mentoring young professionals, training and supervising coaches; developing and delivering customized trainings; motivating, inspiring and grounding energy into productive action.

As a Consultant, my goal is to become a valuable and seamless part of your team - adding my 35 years of experience to your endeavors. With my broad experience, I am able to focus on details while keeping my eye on the big picture. Specialties: Acquisition and due diligence activities for retail and multi-use investment properties,
