Kylie Ryan's Summary

Do you want to lose weight but are struggling with the motivation to eat well and exercise?

I have been there, I too struggled with 30kgs of extra weight. No diet or exercise program worked for me until I changed my mindset. When I did shift my thoughts and beliefs I easily dropped the extra weight, and have kept it off for over 8 years. Now I would like to help you do the same. I am an expert in guiding you to make those mindset changes that will give you the freedom, choice and self love that you truly deserve so you can finally be comfortable in your own skin.

I specialize in helping women to break through their mental and emotional barriers to becoming slim, You can overcome yo-yo dieting, emotional eating, excuses and self-sabotage and become slim, sexy and empowered.

Read my free coaching articles here: http://mymindcoach.com.au/blog/ and see some amazing results here: http://mymindcoach.com.au/it-works/

You can find me at my private practice in Bondi Junction, Sydney, Australia or at www.MyMindCoach.com.au. I offer Private Coaching, 2 day Transformation Sessions, Workshops/ Seminars and Audio Products

I also run Goal Setting, Motivation & Mindset Workshops for gyms, nutritionists, personal trainers and fitness studios.
