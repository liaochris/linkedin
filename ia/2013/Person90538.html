    A fresh start with a new feel, Gallery Artisans is located at 71 London Street, Hamilton, in town, next to Machina Cafe, opposite the Fonterra building.

    Gallery Artisans stocks an extensive range of established and emerging New Zealand Artists. Including paintings, ceramics, sculpture in stone, wood, copper and bronze, art glass and jewellery, New Zealand limited edition prints, reproduction art prints.Gallery Artisans has an onsite custom framing workshop with a New Zealand Guild Qualified professional framer. We can frame anything from, paintings, prints, certificates, photos, tapestries and needlework, tapa cloths, rugby jerseys, mirrors to canvas stretching.

    Steve Oldfield (Guild Qualified Framer) is at Gallery Artisans as our framer. Steve specialises in custom and conservation framing of the highest quality.


    Quality is an important criteria for the works exhibited in our gallery, along with integrity. We believe that art enhances life and is an expression of being.

    At Gallery Artisans we continue to offer the best range of artwork in the Waikato.
