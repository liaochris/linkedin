George M. Gilmer, Esq.'s Summary

George M. Gilmer, Esq. has made over one thousand Court appearances since being admitted to practice law (including arguing before the United States Court of Appeals). He is admitted in New York State, the Eastern and Southern Districts of New York (Federal Court) and the United States Court of Appeals (2nd Circuit). He has vast experience in Chapter 7 and Chapter 11 Bankruptcy cases.

My Pledge to You, my Client

My pledge to you is that I will strive to dedicate my skill and expertise in solving your legal issue. I will keep myself competent, by taking advantage of legal education and by keeping abreast of the latest developments in the law so I can properly serve you. I will do my best to provide great client service and give you honest legal opinions and advice based upon good judgment. I will treat you with courtesy and respect.

I will respect your financial situation and do my best to work with your resources. I offer discounted legal services in Immigration, Divorce and Bankruptcy. Click on the links below to find out about my Current Offers.

If you have any questions, please give me a call for a free phone consultation.

Specialties
