David Riggleman's Projects

  • Cedarville Campus Compass Mobile Application

    • August 2011 to May 2012
    Team Members: David Riggleman, Nathan Brown, Dane Baldwin

    The Campus Compass is a mobile application, built on Appcelerator's Titanium platform to introduce prospective students to Cedarville University. A single JavaScript code base supports both the Android and iPhone versions of this application.

    Support was provided by a number of campus departments including Admissions, Marketing, Creative Services, and Information Technology. Throughout the project, we strove to maintain open communication with all stakeholders and ensure the project remained relevant and up-to-date with respect to our client's expectations.

    The three elements of this app include a Maps section, allowing the user to navigate the campus using a Google Maps interface, view an interactive map of campus, or navigate dynamically using an augmented reality engine built on the camera and a unique labeling system.

    The user can also sift through the faculty, majors, and academic buildings at Cedarville University to find the people, places, and majors that are right for him or her. Information is presented in a friendly and intuitive interface. Student life information is also presented in a similar manner.

    In addition, students can view available classes to visit, allowing them to make a quick and informed decision that makes the best use of their campus visit.
