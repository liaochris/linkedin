Donald Mullen's Summary

Don Mullen is the Co-Founder of Ronin Solutions. A digital advertising industry careerist, Don spent six years with the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel’s online team before establishing the automotive-focused digital rep firm, Ronin Solutions, in 2009. Over the last 10 years, he's been directly responsible for bringing the services of large national online vendors — including Cars.com, Monster.com, Kbb.com and Digital Air Strike — to the local level. Don has applied his online automotive marketing background to building, selling and executing a highly successful Digital Ad Package proprietary to Ronin Solutions. Don and his business partner have since sold Ronin Solutions to PureCars in order to capitalize on the hyper-relevancy that PureCars data brings to Digital Advertising . Don’s primary goal as the Vice President of the PureCars Ronin Division continues to be helping automotive clients grow their business through cutting edge advertising technologies.
