Dr. Daniel Turo's Summary

For over 10 years now, I have been dedicated to learning the universal laws of health and healing. I practice the philosophy, art, and science of chiropractic, which involves adjusting the spinal segments to restore optimal nerve function and allow healing from within, the way God intended. In all of my studies, I have come to understand that chiropractic provides the greatest foundation for healing to occur. From the time of conception to birth (approximately 9 months) we go from a single microscopic cell to a 70 trillion cell organism and member of humanity. The intelligence it takes to run life is well beyond our comprehension. My vision for the world is that we all will respect that intelligence. Imagine a world where every person respects the Spirit of life!

Specialties
