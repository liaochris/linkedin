Ashley Kasarjian's Summary

I am an attorney at Snell and Wilmer L.L.P. in Phoenix, Arizona. I have experience in employment and commercial litigation, employment counseling/advice, and internal investigations.

My litigation practice includes representing companies in a broad range of matters at the administrative, state and federal levels, including claims of workplace discrimination, harassment, and retaliation. I have defended employers against claims brought pursuant to the ADA, Title VII, FLSA, ADEA/OWBPA, FMLA, GINA, Section 1981, OSHA, MSHA, and numerous other state and federal laws. I have also represented places of public accommodation in lawsuits regarding accessibility and compliance issues pursuant to Title III of the ADA and state law.

In addition to my litigation practice, I routinely advise and counsel employers regarding employee hiring, discipline, leave and termination; reductions in force; personnel policies and handbooks; noncompetition, nonsolicitation and confidentiality agreements; employment contracts and best employment practices.

I assist clients with complex internal investigations-- including allegations of corporate misconduct, accounting fraud, executive improprieties and employment issues relating to private and publicly traded companies.

I am the author of the legal blog, Employment and the Law, which was previously named the Top Employment Law Blog in the Nation by LexisNexis.

I am a Phoenix native and a graduate of the Barrett Honors College at Arizona State University. I attended Vanderbilt University Law School as an Honor Scholar. I am a member of the Board of Directors of the Maricopa Community Colleges Foundation and the Editorial Board of Arizona Attorney Magazine, published by the State Bar of Arizona. I also serve on the Executive Council and as the Chair of the CLE Committee of the Labor and Employment Section of the State Bar of Arizona.
