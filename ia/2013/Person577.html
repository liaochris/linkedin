Aaron Millar's Summary

I have been working as a location scout/manager since 2005,and have worked on over one hundred projects in the locations dept.I Within the past few years,I have taken assignments in production coordinating/management on both music video's and indie films.

During the course of the past several years,I have worked as the producer/executive producer, and creator, of an interactive museum project titled"The history of interactive video-gaming 1957-present" for "The Paley Center For Media" in Beverly Hills, during which I successfully negotiated with Brookhaven lab for the use of "Tennis for Two", and with Activision, for the pro-bono use of the the Activision video game catalogue. I am currently producing a film titled "Buddha Eyes" which stars Eric Roberts, and starts filming mid-2013.

My skills include budgeting (setting up and maintaining during production),contracts,script breakdowns, coordinating, photography,location scouting/management,community relations, locating and understanding tax incentives related and unrelated to film,union negotiation,film finance, and making deals with crew and suppliers.I can also write incredible treatments and proposals.

Productions I have worked on include documentaries, feature films, shorts,commercials,music videos,P.S.A's and television(long form,M.O.W's and half hour series)

I am very creative,and resourceful and,I have an excellent sense for what works and what does not.This helps to save time and money while not compromising either the creativity or integrity of any particular show I am working on.

I am a member of The Location Managers Guild Of America, Teamsters 399, The Academy of Television Arts and Sciences(New York Chapter), and The Producers Guild Of America
