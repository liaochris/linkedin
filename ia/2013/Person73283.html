About us:

Kawader

January 2008 – Present (5 years 10 months)

Established in 2008 by Mr. Mohammed Khalaf Al-Mohannadi as a subsidiary of Business Zone, Kawader is a leading Qatar based Recruitment Company that takes pride in initiating connections and building relationships between talented individuals and focused organizations.


We work closely with candidates and clients alike, and are always happy to offer advice on any resourcing campaign or provide assistance to candidates looking for their career move.

We are the experts in recruiting qualified professionals and skilled people across a wide range of specialized industries and professions.

Our value lies in our ability to provide you with the right candidate for the right job at the right time.

We strongly believe in long-term relationships. Kawader's experienced multilingual consultants commit to offer you the best guaranteed quality service.

To discuss any requirements your company has or if you are a candidate looking for your next dream job, please contact a member of our team. We are always happy to hear from you!
