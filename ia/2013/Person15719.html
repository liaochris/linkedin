Bonnie Tomek's Summary

Marketing consultant, social media consultant, branding expert, web design.SEO friendly copywriting and photography.

I work with individuals, start-ups, and small to medium size companies.

For branding and design portfolio, writing examples and VO MP3s go to http://www.bonnietomek.com

I have extensive hands on marketing and print/web design experience in the business and retail sector. I am a Google Adwords consultant and a social media consultant.

I have written business plans, started and run an overseas import business, and managed retail operations. I have been a consultant for 10 years and counting for small to medium size businesses and individuals for a variety of projects.

I also like to use my fine art background in drawing, painting, and sculpture when thinking about web design.

Specialties

Branding, marketing, SEO advice, copywriting, social media consulting, Google adwords consulting, email and direct mail campaigns, web page/site design with Senior IT support, hand held device campaigns, public relations.

I work through the discovery process, the design process and the deployment process with my clients. We have a lot of fun along the way in creating their branding.
