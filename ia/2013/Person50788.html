Eric Guess' Summary

Hey! Thanks for checking me out! I'm not currently seeking employment, but there's a good read below:

I'm a User Experience Designer (or Architect, Engineer, etc.) who loves a good challenge, though that's not what has brought me into this field; it's my hard-wired deep empathy for people.

I harness my years of experience and education in the web industry and couple that with my passion for simply wanting to help others.

I've seen so many disjointed experiences online and off over the years, and I love to collaborate and create solutions to allow for people to take less effort and gain more enjoyment in whatever they're doing.

Just because my career has been mainly web-based doesn't mean I'm not aware of experiences that go awry in the physical world. While I don't have a background in industrial design, or even relevant higher education experience, I am human.

I'm a person who simply wants to help other people live their lives without technology getting in their way. If I can do that, and bring a sense of joy to people, then I know I've done my job.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

With all of that said, if you are looking for someone to bring outdated or disjointed interfaces and branding back together, you may have just found him.

I enjoy the intangible, higher-level thinking and processes of UX Design, as well as helping craft the tangibles through wireframing, prototyping, visual design, content curation; and the list could go on.

So, if you read this far, please feel free to contact me, as I'd love to talk. Thanks!
