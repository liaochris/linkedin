Chris Crosby's Summary

As CEO of nPowered Social and Managing Director of SociallyActive, I'm responsible for product development, business strategy and growth. I also have the privilege of working with an amazing team of people that care deeply about helping parents help their kids.

I cofounded my first company, Latigent, in 2002 with $1,000 and a vision to remake business intelligence in call centers. As CEO, I drove all aspects of internal operations and grew the company into a widely recognized industry leader. Latigent was acquired by Cisco Systems, Inc. in 2007.

After the acquisition, I oversaw the successful integration of Latigent products into Cisco and continued to drive our product to explosive growth. I also led business development, global sales enablement, product strategy and various go-to-market activities while at Cisco.

In 2011, seeing the privacy challenges being created for teens and parents with the rise of social networks, I cofounded SociallyActive, LLC.
