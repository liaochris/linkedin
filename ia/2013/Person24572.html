Christopher Sturm's Summary

Chris is an entrepreneur filled with the passion to build successful businesses. His primary firm, GovCollect Software, is the leader of all-in-one affordable municipal tax collection. Chris also owns InstrumentLife which protects musical equipment in one online secure location.
