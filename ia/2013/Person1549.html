Adam Mokan's Summary

I'm a right-brain problem solver that has been able to leverage my experience working in many environments from manufacturing and public utilities to the telecom industry. Combined with 14+ years of professional software development experience, there are few problems I've not faced.

Most recent experience in technologies such as Ruby, Node.js, Erlang, Python, Backbone.js, AngularJS, Riak, MongoDB, Redis, and others.

I also have nearly a decade of professional experience in mobile technologies in both consumer and industrial markets. This includes low-level development on RF-based handheld devices for industrial use back before the movement to 802.11x.

Aside from technical skills, I have previously worked in the print/media industry doing both editorial and copy layout.

Lastly, I have over 15 years of personal and professional experience in audio engineering, sound design, and electronics. Everything from experience in a professional recording studio, running live sound, building custom synthesizers for music, to PLC design and development for industrial use.

Specialties: JavaScript, CoffeeScript, Backbone.js, Backbone.Marionette, Handlebars.js, Node.js, RabbitMQ/AMQP, Ruby, Erlang, C#, ASP.NET MVC, WCF, Objective-C, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, OOP & functional programming, system architecture, MongoDB, CouchDB, Redis, Riak (and other key-value data stores), Agile/Scrum methodologies, TDD and BDD paradigms and approaches, continuous integration, strong people skills (both written and verbal), strong presentation skills
