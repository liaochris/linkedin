Cpt. Michael Metzler Sr.'s Summary

A respected officer and leader who is always willing to help those who want help succeeding online and offline.

Army Helicopter Test Pilot and former Marine of 7 years. I guess you'd say that I'm a professional soldier. My military career started the day after Thanksgiving in November of 1977, when I departed for boot camp to become a U. S. Marine. Destination, "Marine Corps Recruit Depot" San Diego, Ca. (Hollywood Marine). Approximately five months after being Honorably Discharged from the Marines, my Army aviation career was just starting (Feb. 85). I departed for 67N Aviation Mechanic school at Ft. Rucker, AL. "Headquarters of Army Aviation" in June 1985, and graduated in August 1985. "Cinderella Man Story" - In August 1986 God blessed me and I was accepted to Flight School..Eleven months later I graduated Flight School (July 1987). My career skyrocketed when I was accepted to the Maintenance Manager/Maintenance Test Pilot Course in Ft. Eustis, VA. in Mar. 88. While still attending school, I was contacted by Colonel Paul D. Alford "State Aviation Officer", and was given a full time position as a Quality Control Supervisor/Test Pilot for the Louisiana National Guard. My career as a Helicopter Test Pilot has spanned for over 27 years now, and I'm hoping to continue for another ten years or so, God willing.

My passion for the last five years has been Network Marketing, where I've been working as an Online Business & Marketing Coach. I love helping as many people as I can to succeed on the internet, while working from the comfort of their own home. It's NO surprise that the economy is plummeting, and in turn has forced many people to pursue different avenues of making money to provide for their families.

I own and operate 4 Paid Advertising sites and 4 SOLO AD Exchanges that send out Super Solo ads to over 50 sites. My Value Ad Network provides quality advertising for internet entrepreneurs of various niches, to advertise their business(es) on the internet.

Specialties
