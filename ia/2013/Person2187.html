Alan Schwanke's Summary

I am a Freelance Scenic Designer for theatre & performance. I am also resident scenic design assistant for the Oregon Shakespeare Festival. I am always interested in developing new collaborative relationships, working on challenging material and telling great stories. Please feel free to have a look at my website for more information.

Specialties: Scenic Design

Alan Schwanke's Experience

Freelance Scenic Designer

Alan Edward Schwanke Designs

January 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months)

I am a freelance Theatrical Scenic Designer working both in the Pacific Northwest and the Midwest. I am a passionate storyteller and collaborator. I provide scenic design services for Theatre & Performance as well as providing graphic and event design services for both corporate/business and entertainment industries.

Resident Scenic Design Assistant

Oregon Shakespeare Festival

Nonprofit; 201-500 employees; Performing Arts industry

September 2010 – Present (3 years)

As resident design assistant my main duty is to assist the resident and visiting designers in realizing their visions for the scenic designs of the mainstage shows. I try to support their artistic visions in any way I can. This includes visual research, drafting, drawing/sketching, model building and painting elevations. I also prepare graphic design materials for artistic presentations and provide necessary build information to the technical director and lead carpenters.
Some designers I have provided design assistance to (at varying levels) while at the Festival include: Richard Hay, Michael Ganio, Christopher Acebo, Sibyl Wickersheimer, Todd Rosenthal, Dan Ostling, Michael Locher, Jo Winiarski, Rachel Hauck, David Jenkins, David Gallo, Clint Ramos and more.
