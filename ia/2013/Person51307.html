  • Stempl - mobile loyalty application

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Håkon Harberg, Adam Holtrop, Daniel Tandberg-Johansen

    Stempl revolutionises the global loyalty concept of stamp cards, and gives merchants a unique tool to overview, interact and reward their most loyal customers. The merchant solution is simple to use and requires no technology investment to implement. There is no need for high threshold technical implementation in point of sale systems or any other hardware in store.

    Now consumers only need to pay attention to one app for all their loyalty cards and can independently choose who is more important by toggling in-app communications from merchants. The Stempl app is available for Android and IOS and is ready for download from Google Play and Appstore.

    About the Norwegian Launch

    Launched in Norway together with Amedia provides Stempl with an incredible local sales force together with the strength of Amedia channels for nationwide promotion of the service. While the traditional news media industry is struggling in the face of declining print revenues, forward leaning media groups are adjusting to new media technologies and rapidly advancing in new directions. Mobile represents massive opportunity towards long lasting consumer retail relationship business and Amedia has wasted no time taking full advantage.

    For sales enquiries in Norway call Amedia at 22009000 or visit www.amedia.com
    For international stempl enquiries see www.vidamo.no

  • Online claims system for AIG

    • October 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Håkon Harberg, Dan Richard Isdahl-Engh, Adam Holtrop

    Vidamo fills a gap in the insurance technical landscape, providing an online claims solution for AIG independently of their core platform. Our focus is on the user experience, providing an elegant, stress relieving claims process to customers with the use of a responsive java UI designed for phones, tablets and desktop computers.
    AIG benefit from customer satisfaction and an internal cost saving from the digital delivery of claim applications.
