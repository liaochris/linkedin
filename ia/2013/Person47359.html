Gavin Richardson's Summary

A veteran youth worker for over 16 years. Seeing many young adults go into ordained profession, short & long term mission, local justice causes, and other Christian leadership.

He's been published by the UpperRoom, United Methodist Publishing House, Circuit Rider, YMToday, YouthWorker Movement, United Methodist Reporter, Patheos, Devozine, and more.

Gavin has been considered ahead of the curve in digital community building. He's been listed as a top 10 blog for youth ministries by YouthSpecialties, featured on UMC.org, and currently a featured blogger on Patheos. He also consults and builds web presence & communications plans for churches and ministries as part of the Proof Branding team.

The new project is YouthWorker Circuit a membership site for Wesleyan yotuhworkers to resource themselves to enable & challenge their ministries for lasting impact.

writing, digital community building, public speaking, technical training, buzz master, web site development, communications strategy, relational leadership, truth speaking, misfit church leader
