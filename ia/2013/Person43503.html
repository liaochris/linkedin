Evelyn Axelrad-Espinosa's Summary

My friends call me an “adventure junkie.” I love a challenge.

My most favorite vacation was a 15 day trek crossing the Indonesian island of Ceram, plucking leeches off my legs, building banana leaf shelters against torrential downpours, and meeting people ascribed with magical powers.

This adventure spirit has also led me professionally. Be it as a Project Manager in my newest position or previously, working as an independent Business Consultant I love the challenge of finding solid and workable solutions and implementing them with agility and reliability to ease my clients’ pain points.

As a co-founder and COO of my Internet Start-up every day was an adventure - from fundraising and evangelizing a concept that was way ahead of its times to scaling the company’s infrastructure to 136 employees to support Fortune 500 clients 24/7 in their anti-piracy and marketing efforts.

I have a passion for technology and software that make your life easier. I am not afraid to delve into difficult situations, identify the issues and translate the analysis into actions that delight clients.

Specialties: Business Operations, Customer Success, Customer Advocate, Implementations, Project Manager in High-Tech, SaaS and Start-Up. Certified ScrumMaster.

Client Account, Advocate and Success Management, Sales, Marketing, Business Development, Human Resources and Staff Development, Business Operations Management, Program and System Implementations, Team Building, Training, Collaborative Approach to Problem Solving, Organizational Change Management, Process Identification and Re-engineering.
