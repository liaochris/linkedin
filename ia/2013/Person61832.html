Jennifer Ball's Summary

After ten years in corporate marketing, Jennifer understands how to sell. She brings a wealth of new ideas and proven strategies to her Pacific Sotheby's office, and with her high energy style of service, she strives to leave every client completely satisfied.

Jennifer has spent the past two years helping Californians through the complicated tasks of buying and selling “short sales.” Having managed a team of short sale realtor/negotiators, she understands all the details of how different lenders work, and can help you to negotiate the many tasks involved. Whether you are a homeowner who is “upside down” with your mortgage, or a buyer who has fallen in love with a home in a short sale, Jennifer can navigate your sale or purchase successfully.

So, if you need traditional sales services, or are involved in any stage of the foreclosure process, Jennifer has the expertise to serve you. Her innovative ideas, coupled with Pacific Sotheby's established methods, will provide the personal, customized care that you deserve.
