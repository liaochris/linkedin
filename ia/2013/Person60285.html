Jérôme Cordiez's Experience

UX Architect / iOS Developer

This is Lovely!

January 2008 – Present (6 years) Brussels

This is Lovely! is a one-man UX consulting firm based in Brussels, Belgium. 
Activities vary from user research to front-end development (iOS mainly), through functional analysis, requirements capture, wireframing, prototyping, information architecture, interaction and user interface design.

Most recent projects include an iPad application prototype for BNP Paribas (Paris), the design of a Google Adwords monitoring/analytics web application, a financial reporting iPad app for investment funds, and a mobile app for Vodafone UK.

Founder

Lovely Charts

Privately Held; Myself Only; Information Technology and Services industry

February 2007 – Present (6 years 11 months) Brussels Area, Belgium

"Lovely Charts (http://www.lovelycharts.com) is a diagramming application that allows you to create professional looking diagrams, such as flowcharts, sitemaps, organisation charts, wireframes, and many
more... For free."

Founder of this bootstrapped service, available on the web, desktop, and iPad, my role entails pretty much everything from software develoment to graphic design and copywriting, marketing and strategy.

Couple achievements:
- TechCrunch Europas 2009 Finalist
- Ranked #7 in LifeHacker's "Top 10 Underhyped Webapps of 2009"
- 250K registered users
- Featured on TechCrunch, Mashable, LifeHacker, DownloadSquad...

UX Consultant

Adobe Systems

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; ADBE; Computer Software industry

July 2007 – January 2013 (5 years 7 months) EMEA

Adobe Consulting was a Professional Services organization within Adobe Systems, delivering custom applications to strategic customers, such as NATO, Adobe, Morgan Stanley, Rabobank, Nike, F1, Vodafone, Philips... As a User Experience consultant and Design Technologist, my job was both to define user experiences for custom, high-profile, Rich Internet Applications, and to bridge the gap between the UX and Technology practices. Activities involved user research, analysis, requirements capture, interaction design and prototyping.
While at Adobe, I notably worked on a Mission Support application for NATO’s AWACS aircrafts, a trading platform for Morgan Stanley, an eCommerce platform for Philips, eBanking vision demos, internal apps for Adobe and a couple more things I’m afraid I can’t talk about :)
Collaboration was initially on a fulltime basis, then project based.

Co-founder

Appside

2010 – 2012 (2 years) Brussels Area, Belgium

Appside edited a "web to print" product called Mindy: a web application running on top of Indesign Server, allowing clients to generate high-quality custom print documents.
Mindy was notably used rather intensively by one of Belgium's leading supermarket chain, generating thousands of documents every month.
