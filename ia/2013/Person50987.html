Gustav Schmiege's Summary

For the past 20 years, this Dallas/NY based photographer has worked with some of the country’s best known commercial clients, including Neiman Marcus, Oxxford Clothes, Horchow, Stanley Korshak, Rosewood Hotels, Pavestone, Pier One, Bombay Kids, Brinker International, the Jan Barboglio Collection, Red Bull and DaKine. His editorial credits include publications such as Esquire, Men's Journal, Men's Vogue, Redbook, Veranda, Traditional Home, The Land Report, Better Homes and Gardens Publications, American Way and D Magazine. A graduate of Southern Methodist University, Schmiege is equally at home in his Dallas studio or on in extreme locations such as the coast of Brazil, Barbados, Turks & Caicos, and the back country of Utah shooting for kiteboarding magazines.
