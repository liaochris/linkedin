Geoff Laughton's Summary

Geoff Laughton is a Relationships Coach & Mentor who began Coaching clients in 1997, after spending 16 years in a very unsatisfying Corporate Management career in the Bay Area. He helps people either achieve and/or regain the relationships and lives of their dreams...the potential that they started out with and somehow lost track of. He helps people master all their relationships, both personal & professional, achieving results that typical couples counseling fails to reach.

Geoff helps people be as authentic, empowered, at peace, and as driven by their Spirit's purpose as they can possibly be. To bring the transformation he helps his clients achieve, Geoff uses his depth of compassion, a fierce advocacy for wholeness & well-being, acute intuitive insights into matters both spiritual and worldly, and his mastery of communication techniques developed through his journey as father of two and husband of 29 years. Geoff’s work has included 7 years with the Mankind Project, a position as a Founding Spiritual Director of the Cornerstones Community Foundation in California, and now counseling individual men and leading men's groups in the Boulder, Colorado area.

Geoff would like to connect with:

- Couples wishing to re-ignite or rescue their relationships from ennui and/or divorce.

- Middle aged to senior men ready to shift their life from a focus on producing and wealth accumulation into an individualized gift of service to life

- Individuals yearning for a life where they're not asking "Is this all there is?"

To find out more about one-on-one opportunities for working with Geoff, or his workshops, email Geoff at info@geofflaughton.com - or, to receive a complimentary 40- minute call with Geoff to begin reNEWing your relationship now, visit http://www.renewingyourrelationship.com/
