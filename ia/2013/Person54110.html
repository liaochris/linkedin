Ray Hutchins' Summary

Ray’s deep entrepreneurial business experience, combined with a commanding grasp of web technologies, makes him a powerful ally of enterprises large and small – whether they are interested in dominating their markets and increasing revenues – or protecting and defending their IT assets with leading-edge security solutions.

Ray's company, Denver Cyber Security (DCS), is a partner/reseller of Solutionary, which is recognized with such companies as IBM, AT&T and Dell SecureWorks as a Gartner Magic Quadrant MSSP (managed security service provider). DCS is also a partner/reseller of Wombat Security Technologies' software, which is the leading online security awareness training platform in the industry. We provide IT security consulting and custom solutions for enterprise and medium-sized firms in Colorado.

Ray's other company, Denver Web Services, specializes in web development and search marketing that creates an overwhelming competitive edge for clients. To do this, Ray has built and manages a top-notch offshore technical team, which allows the company to achieve high search rankings for a fraction of the cost charged by competitors.

Additionally, Ray serves as President of TiE Rockies, the international organization for entrepreneurs, and drives the growth of this highly successful organization comprised of a large, diverse network of professionals from all over Colorado. Ray is also an Executive Board member of the Denver chapter of the Information Systems Security Association (ISSA).
