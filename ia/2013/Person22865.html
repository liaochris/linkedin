Chip Beale's Summary

Chip is an innovative leader with a passion for solving complex business challenges. He excels at building go-to market business plans that maximize the full profit potential of any product portfolio. Whether it is improving on a conventional idea or creating cutting-edge solutions, his creativity and energy have resulted in continuous new business development success.

His extensive experience guiding teams through dynamic industry changes has made him adept at developing long-term strategies that last beyond the current climate. These successful teams were built by identifying self-motivated, talented people with the drive to learn new ideas and then providing the opportunity and environment in which to excel.
