Jane Peterson, PhD's Summary

Jane Peterson, PhD is an International trainer, consultant, speaker and workshop facilitator, in the field of Systemic Constellation work and the developer of SocialSoma theory. The Human Systems Institute, Inc. offers customized organizational consulting, training and coaching for executives and business leaders and family businesses.

See our websites for more information:
Jane Peterson at http://wwwJanePetersonPhD.com
Human Systems Institute, Inc. at http://www.human-systems-institute.com
Organizational Constellations at http://www.human-systems-institute.com/OrganizationalConstellations.php.

Specialties: Getting to the heart of the issue quickly. Mobilizing you and your teams' resources and deeper wisdom to generate emergent, sustainable solutions. We help businesses thrive and families flourish.

Family, Business & Organizational Constellation trainings, workshops & seminars. Family business advising & business consulting. Executive coaching and leadership development.

Director

Human Systems Institute™

1999 – Present (14 years) Portland, Oregon USA

M. Jane Peterson is the founder and director of the Human Systems Institute, Inc. The Institute is internationally respected for the quality trainings it offers in the field of systemic constellation work, including organizational constellations. Jane has trained systemic constellation work in Asia, S. America, Mexico, Canada, Europe and the U.S. In 2005, she organized and sponsored the first U.S. Conference in the constellation work. The institute is currently offering it's eighth year of training programs. Jane started her professional career as an engineer and manager in a high tech firm. She has also been a professional ceramic artist, founded and operated a sculpture studio, and was an officer in a local cooperative. She is a master practitioner of Neuro-linguistic Programming, a Certified Clinical Hypnotherapist and a Certified Professional Coach.
