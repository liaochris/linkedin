Brooke Manino's Summary

Brooke is the Vice-President of Enterprise Technology Services for John Wiley and Sons, leading a global team of Information Technology professionals. She is also the founder and President of Advanced Leadership Performance Strategies, LLC, a coaching and leadership development practice focused on transforming individuals, small businesses and leadership teams to achieve superior strategic business outcomes.

Brooke is a seasoned leader with demonstrated success in building high performing teams to deliver upon critical business objectives. Key strengths include her ability to identify strategic organizational needs and design team structures and business processes to achieve superior business results. Brooke’s leadership skills, strategic thinking and change management capabilities enable her to form collaborative partnerships across organizations to successfully implement transformational initiatives.

This expertise, combined with her training at the FastTrack Coaching Academy, enables her to leverage strategies for excelling in leadership along with practical application of essential tools and techniques to help her clients achieve their desired success.

Prior to her current role, Brooke spent 20 years with D&B and American Express in a variety of senior global leadership positions delivering critical business results through diverse team structures. Brooke holds a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration and French from William Jewell College and a Master’s degree in International Management from the Thunderbird School of Global Management. She currently serves as the Treasurer of the New Jersey chapter of the International Coach Federation. Brooke is also the President of the Board of Trustee for Child and Family Resources, a non-profit organization in Morris County, NJ.
