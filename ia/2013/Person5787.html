  • Brain Gym® and recent Neurological Studies

    • http://www.free-articles-zone.com
    • April 2, 2008
    Authors: Amnon Reiss

    Brain Gym®* is a method designated to enhance cognitive, mental, emotional and physical performance. Despite, a substantial empirical success the mode of action of Brain gym remains obscure. However, recent Neurophysiologic studies in the field of Brain plasticity suggest a surprising novel explanation for the role of the balancing process, the core of the Brain Gym® program.
