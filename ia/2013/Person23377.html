Chris Holzwarth's Summary

Chris Holzwarth, Director of Beyond the Applause offers specialized education, college admissions and career development planning services for students in grades 8 through 12. Our services are specific to the needs of students and families. Services include but are not limited to the following:

- Curriculum and activity planning
- Summer enrichment programs
- Building a targeted college list
- Scholarship planning and financial aid clarification
- Creating testing and application time lines
- Resume and cover letter guidance
- Essay process and development
- Parent workshops for schools, businesses and
organizations (upon request)

Chris Holzwarth, was awarded a certificate in College Admissions Counseling UCLA, 2008 and has been working in the field of education for the past 20 years. She continues to visit colleges across the US on a yearly basis and attends conferences and groups focused on student match, college admissions and affordability.

Specialties

Experience working with performing, visual, design and communication arts students. Advise on portfolio and audition requirements to complete college application.
Major and career planning services for college students.
