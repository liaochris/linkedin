Jason Lehr's Summary

Creatively driven, business-structured individual focused on developing great stories through digital and traditional advertising content. I have had the pleasure of working in the advertising and marketing world on both the client side and agency side, and have a complete understanding of how the two synonymously work together. I spent several years in marketing and business development for several Major League Baseball affiliate teams where I was responsible for campaign development and management at the team and league level. I have had some great budgets to work with and some really tight budgets to work with. I pride myself as someone who understands brands, and that helps me in the post-production world to best execute the messaging and deliverables for our clients. I have managed teams for video and film production, live sound and studio music and live events, including budgeting, scheduling, project management, rights management and cost analysis. I am seasoned in the newest media trends, including web, social media and app development. I work independently with a great group of designers, programmers and industry experts. I also have PR experience in the motorsports industry, representing NHRA Sportsman race teams.

I am an entrepreneur at heart always searching for ways to work with very talented people and high level clients. I also have a strong passion for cars. I grew up around automotive technology, and in 2013 I launched a side project called Rockin L Customs with a long-time friend and master-mechanic. Rockin L Customs provides custom fabrication and restoration for our clients looking to restore, freshen up or buy and sell classic muscle cars.

Specialties: PR,Brand Marketing, Retail Marketing, Event Marketing, Product Marketing, Merchandising, Sales and Business Development, Website Design, Executive Producer, Producer, Director, Video Production, Video Editing, and a lot more.
