Erik Attkisson's Experience

March 2006 – Present (7 years 10 months) Chicago

Satellite Office is an artist management and creative marketing agency. We have a diverse roster of some of the world's most distinguished creative talents spanning the fields of illustration, graphic & interactive design, film, and digital media.

We employ an inverted agency model, where the artists are the stars, and we provide strategy, creative and business support. Working with agencies and clients, our goal is to curate the most outstanding creative team to fit the project.
