DeRone Bell's Summary

• Strong work ethic; a people person that is highly motivated, as well as a self-starter dedicated to service, quality and leadership by example

• Strong background in customer service, technical support, and sales, 9+ years experience, Renewed Certification for Data Entry and Customer Service Training (BlueCross BlueShield of SC, 2008)

• Working computer knowledge includes Windows based PCs along with MAC OS X and later OS versions, including hardware and software, Strong knowledge in MS Office: Excel, PowerPoint, Access Database

• Graphic Designer with experience in design programs such as Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, and QuarkXPress, Basic IT Networking, and Data Entry programs such as Siebel and Customer Desktop.

I received my Associates in Science Degree in Graphic Design from South University in September 2009. There I learned to have a good command on Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. My education also led me to be more attentive to my skills and to meet deadlines for clients.

Being from a small town, graphic design was something you did in your spare time and wasn’t considered a career. It wasn’t until I made some handbills for a local politician, which allowed her to reach a broader public and win her election, that my community realized it was a needed profession.

With 6 years experience in the graphic design field, I have grown to understand that being creative is not just what is shown in the final design, but it’s also the process.

My desire is to work in advertising and publish print media for top end retailers. I strive to show and exhibit designs based on, what I would say, "the creative season", giving my clients up to date, trendy designs that not only boosts sales but expresses their business in a new light.
