Holly Harris | LION's Summary

I am a technology-focused staffing and recruiting professional currently based in Austin, TX with a background in psychology and customer service/hospitality. At Phoenix Staff, I work to find the best people for the best jobs in Las Vegas, Phoenix, Denver, Austin, Dallas-Fort Worth/Plano, and San Antonio. I pursue what is "right for the client, right for the candidate - every time!"

I believe honesty and communication are cornerstones in every relationship including professional ones such as the candidate-recruiter relationship. I continually work to keep my industry knowledge up to date through ongoing training and education, and studying market trends and technology. At Phoenix Staff, we support educational and charitable initiatives, and offer ongoing internal training as well.

At Phoenix Staff, I have collaborated on writing and editing training manuals, as well as creating processes and procedures to ensure proper documentation and professional communication. I serve on academic and technology councils, and participate within my IT markets' communities through volunteerism, participating in and providing networking opportunities.

My specialties include Information Technology (IT) staffing and recruitment in gaming & hospitality, gaming manufacturing, financial & banking, education, healthcare, utilities, eCommerce and mobile technology.

I lend my professional skills and expertise to my candidates by assisting them in crafting and maintaining effective resumes and a professional online social presence, as well as interview coaching and preparation. One of my professional goals is to ensure that the education I provide through Phoenix Staff sets my candidates up for future successes regardless of whether or not I am able to place them in a new professional role immediately.

We Do Things Differently

We do things differently at Phoenix Staff. A recognized leader in professional placement since 2002, we specialize in identifying, evaluating and placing the most highly skilled, qualified and professional candidates available. We do things differently from others in our industry because we focus on establishing, building and nurturing a relationship with every client and candidate—a relationship designed to be mutually beneficial, reliant, above all else, one built on mutual respect and trust.

When it comes to identifying the right candidates, we believe that experience and process is everything. Given the challenges in today's volatile work environment, we know that your next career move is very important to you and your family. We develop a relationship. We learn about each other and we constantly listen. We can then begin to discuss your situation, your needs and under what circumstances, and in what conditions, you succeed.

Do you know what you want your career to look like in five years? Three? A year from now? If so, we'll help you get there. If not, we'll help you develop the vision you'll need to get there. And if your company is looking for just the right person to meet a critical need (and, really, aren't they all eventually critical?), the relationship we build with you will allow us to present candidates who have not only the technical skills but also the competencies you desire.

    BarCode Network (BCN) was founded by Phoenix Staff’s Regional Director and Lead Recruiter, Holly Harris, in Las Vegas in August of 2011. The name BarCode Network was conceived by Phoenix Staff’s Regional Director, Keith Bailly, under the premise that everything we as IT professionals do relates back to programming code. We also host our events at local pubs conveniently located for our members. Membership to BCN is free and by invitation only.

    So many networking events take place each month in our markets, and most of them have a greater degree of structure or focus on a specialization with IT such a project management, network security or software development. Phoenix Staff sought to create a business casual, neutral environment for all members within the gametes of IT to come together and exchange ideas, talk about trends in technology & hiring, and learn about opportunities. We also think we know some of the coolest IT folks around and we like to introduce them to other professionals looking to network and build new connections in the local IT community.

  • CIO Breakfast

    Sponsor
    • December 2010 to Present

    The CIO Breakfast is an event designed to educate and promote conversations with local technology leaders. Feedback on the events has consistently been favorable due to the fact that NO VENDORS are in attendance – this gives you, as a leader, the opportunity to speak freely and share your stories in a peer setting. As an additional consequence, we have found that these events serve tremendously well for us – we are able to educate our candidates (your future employees) on the direction of the local technology community. We are able to provide direct feedback to the questions we receive every day from the people we work with and represent. The value proposition is high for you and for us.

    Who attends: This is a vendor free environment. We ask technology leaders only – zeroing in on the highest level within each organization and offering to them to bring their direct reports if they feel the topic is relevant and important for them to also hear.

    What you can expect: You can expect to meet your peers, learn from their own challenges and successes and also learn market and industry trends. The Q&A portion of these events is the most beneficial as it elicits great conversations and collaboration among the attendees.

    For more information, see http://www.phoenixstaff.com/cio-breakfast/
