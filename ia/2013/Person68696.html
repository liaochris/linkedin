Josephine Rotolo's Summary

Type J Coaching helps people unlock their potential to achieve the life they want both personally and professionally.

The career coaching addresses long term career development as well as transitioning between jobs. The life coaching focuses on how to lead a more fulfilling life.

My ideal client:
Working Professionals who want to be happier on the job. Those wishing to change jobs/careers. Those looking for career direction and ways to establish themselves in an existing job and field.

Companies going through a management change, a merger or acquisition. Corporate teams that now consist of the "old staff" and "new staff" who are looking for ways to motivate the team and get them working on the same page.

Benefits of working with me:
Josephine has over 15 years experience within the Entertainment industry specifically in advertising and promotion, marketing and production. She has translated her skills surrounding television brands to helping individuals and companies develop and leverage their own personal brand.

Services/Products:
One-on-one coaching, group coaching and customized corporate workshops.

Her Coaching Communications Corporate Training guides staff on how to better communicate with each other, and in turn, on how to better communicate with clients and patients. This training has been administered to media companies and healthcare facilities.

Josephine is booked for speaking engagements on a variety of subjects that draw on her background in television and her coaching expertise.
