Jodi Hersh's Summary

Jodi’s passion for art dictates her attitude toward life — her devotion to her work, her thriving and award-winning studio. Jodi has over 20 years of experience as a designer and earned her BFA in graphic design and photography from the University of Michigan School of Art & Design.

After graduating, Jodi returned to her hometown of Miami where she worked as part of team designing signage and graphics for Universal Studios and Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines. Jodi broadened her range of experience by art directing for a printing company where she gained valuable working knowledge of developing technologies and processes.

Since starting Orange Star in 1993, Jodi has worked on and led projects for clients as large as ALLTEL, Centers for Disease Control (CDC), The American Cancer Society, and Styx... and as varied as environmental graphics, logos & corp. identities, concert/tour merchandise, event graphics, publications, and interactive new media to complex database driven websites.

Jodi believes form follows function and good design is about more than great aesthetics. Her approach has always been to partner with her clients and other creatives to realize the best possible solutions.

Specialties

Logos & Corporate Identity design, Website Design, Music CD/DVD design, Merchandise/Promotional Items design, Brochures & Publications design, Event Graphics & Signage design, Environmental Graphics design

Founded in 1993 by Jodi Hersh, Orange Star has established a reputation for providing high quality creative solutions to a very diverse clientele ranging from small businesses to mega corps to rock stars. We partner with our clients to realize the best possbile solutions using intellectual and visual inquiry of the highest quality. Our creative skills apply to both print and other interactive media such as website design & development, logo & corp. identity, annual reports, brochures, posters, music cd packaging, tour/concert merchandise, event graphics, signage, desktop software applications and more. In addition to our experience and expertise, we also believe in providing a high level of client support and pride ourselves at delivering effective solutions.

Our studio is small by design so that we may provide personal attention to each and every project and client. We have strategic partnerships with other creatives on an as-needed basis such as writers, editors, illustrators to ensure that each project is handled in the best possible way, yielding the highest quality results.

The bottom line is... we love what we do and we do it very well. To learn more about our creative solutions or to schedule a no-cost consultation, please contact us.

Jodi Hersh's Projects

  • Char-Broil Europe Website

    • July 2012 to August 2012
    Team Members: Jodi Hersh, Mary Eitel, Bukie Opanuga, Michelle (Giroux) Zeller

    I had the pleasure of working with Mary Eitel of MKE Communications, Bukie Opanuga of Modavate, and Char-broil to create a website for Charbroil Europe.

    Char-Broil®, America's oldest and best selling barbecue brand, is entering the European market and needed a short-term stand-alone website to be used as a marketing vehicle to get their name into the marketplace in time to support their presence at the SPOGA 2012 trade show. The objectives were to attract positive attention, demonstrate innovation and reinforce the idea that Char-Broil understands and can meet the needs of the European market. The site design conforms to their brand guidelines and complements the look and feel of the existing U.S. Char-Broil website. The site was developed with Wordpress in both English and German, and features an online product catalog.

    Check it out at www.charbroil.eu
