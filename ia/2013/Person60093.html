James Bielefeldt's Summary

Elvis Presley with his extravagant “TCB” ring (Taking Care of Business) and Larry The Cable Guy with his addictive “Get ‘er done.” have it right; actions are what’s important. My approach to work is delivering results with immutable efficiency and effectiveness.

After college, I opened a commercial photography studio. Photography was my life until I was introduced to the Internet and this changed my focus forever. I began my new career began with training as a Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer (MCSD). As a developer with experience in graphics, marketing and a mix of things related to digital communication, I quickly became immersed in variety of projects encompassing rich media, e-commerce, content management systems and custom applications.

When I found myself managing teams more frequently, I earned certification as a Project Management Professional (PMP) with the Project Management Institute (PMI) in 2006. Combined with over ten years of project management experience, this certification has strengthened my management skills and helps me consistently deliver exceptional results to clients.

Although I still write some code, as a consultant my effort is now directed towards understanding business processes and objectives, defining problems, crafting solutions, and providing guidance through careful analysis and oversight of execution.
