Angela Ferraro-Fanning's Summary

I specialize in working with successful entrepreneurs to design identities that accurately reflect their business and speak to their intended customer. But whether it be a new business in need of an image or an organization that's looking to rebrand, I can help. What I do goes beyond just designing a logo or a brochure; I keep your dream customer in mind, work to understand or establish your brand positioning and weave marketing materials into promotional campaigns. Oh, and I'm an eco-friendly designer too.

I firmly believe that remarkable graphic design stems from unobstructed, one-on-one relationships between designer and client. Because 1331 Design LLC is a one-person show, I work with you and your teams directly; your vision is never mediated and your intent is always understood. By working together, we develop phenomenal identity pieces and websites that reinforce your brand and make a statement.


Who I Work With

Businesses Who Appreciate Branding
I work with new business owners to create and position their brand. This includes identity design and all of the promotional materials that follow. I also help businesses who have been in existence for a while tighten up their identity or rebrand themselves entirely.

Marketing Departments
I work with marketing directors and coordinators who don’t have the luxury of working with an in-house design department. Helping them to execute their marketing campaigns and develop their promotional materials is what I do.

In-house Design Staff
Too much work and need to farm some of it out? I’m happy to help in-house design departments with their work load. Just let me know what needs to be done!

Contact me at design@1331design.com to discuss your project, pricing and timelines.

Specialties: logo design, stationery design, corporate branding, advertising, self-promotion, business cards, brochure design, print collateral, web design, website programming, wedding invitation and collateral design

Angela Ferraro-Fanning's Projects

  • 1331 Design Self-Promotion: Valentine's Day Cards

    • December 2011 to February 2012
    Team Members: Angela Ferraro-Fanning, Printing by Todd Thyberg of Angel Bomb Design + Lettepress, Label Printing by Rob Bosworth of Graphic Design Inc.

    For the first time ever I created custom Valentine’s Day cards for my clients. The idea is that the cards are graphic or web design related so as to promote what I do. I wrote the sayings myself and designed them with letterpress printing in mind. Each client will receive a package with three cards in it for their own use. In keeping with being green, my thought is that the clients can reuse the cards and share the love.

    The cards each have their own saying relating to web or graphic design such as, “You’re just my type,” “Let’s tighten the kerning between us,” and “You make me happy when skies are #CCCCCC.”
