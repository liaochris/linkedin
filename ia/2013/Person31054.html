Dave Duarte's Experience

CEO

Treeshake

February 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Cape Town Area, South Africa

Treeshake was established to provide management and investment for the various companies and projects that I get involved with. The name Treeshake is inspired by Nelson Mandela’s birth name, Rolihlahla, which means “pulling the branch of the tree" - the organization’s goal is to productively disrupt inefficient practices through education. Our focus is on working with Leaders as we believe this has the largest impact on culture and society.
