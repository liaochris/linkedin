Louis Farah, Jr.'s Summary

Fortunate enough to have been raised in the media capital of the world, Louis began his website design career in 1998, during the internet's infancy. Gradually building his skill set by working on websites for friends and family members, he eventually began growing a steady list of clients. A few years later, Backhouse Media was born and business has been busy ever since.

Today, BHM boasts a wide range of clients, from platinum selling musical artists and political candidates to small businesses and service organizations.

Louis found early on that an intelligent and affordable web presence didn't have to be so difficult. Often times, clients are intentionally baffled with complex terms and high price tags by developers. The mission statement of BHM has always concentrated on providing top level customer service at an extremely affordable rate.

The internet is here to stay and everyone should make their online presence known.
