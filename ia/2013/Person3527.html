Alejandro Recio's Summary

Mechatronic Engineer, based out of Miami, founder of AR Solutions an internet web design and internet marketing company, that has worked in web design, web marketing, social network management and search engine optimization for artists and companies such as Universal Music Latino, Juanes, Luis Fonsi, Live Nation, Jennifer Lopez, Marc Anthony and Nelly Furtado among many others. I founded the company with my brother Andres Recio, who has worked as road manager for artists such as Juanes, Ricky Martin, Julio Iglesias and Nelly Furtado, together they found the need to provide the artists with services and technologies that really helped the artists reach their fans and market their music more directly thru the internet, reflecting thru their websites what they are musically, artistically and creatively.

We have offices in Miami, Bogota and Toronto, I love to travel to feed and learn from the team of engineers, designers, web-architects, bloggers and project marketing managers.

We were awarded with the Golden "@", in 2006. We are constantly searching for new technologies and ways to market music and artists thru the internet. I believe that constant research and an understanding of the globality of the music business mixed with the simplicity of what you want to see, hear and feel as a normal person at home, is the key to a successful website, campaign or design.

Specialties

Service, Design, make things done.
