OKIZU to all of you (Native American Sioux for "Unity"),

As a human tribe, it is our commitment to each other that heals us emotionally and physically. Many of your have had a family member touched by cancer or have struggled with this diagnosis yourself. It is a brave fight for everyone involved...

The specific purpose of this corporation is to operate peer support programs for children and families affected by childhood cancer, many of which are based on the community-forming nature of a residential camp experience.

he Marin Women's Hall of Fame has honored more than 116 highly accomplished women since its founding in 1987. Our mission is to identify and honor LEADERS, record their stories for posterity, EDUCATE the public about these positive role models and offer dynamic multi-cultural and intergenerational programs for MENTORING and LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT.
