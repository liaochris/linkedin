Dan Moore's Summary

I have been an educator since 1998 and an entrepreneur most of my life. I have been designing and leading programming at EverGreen Escapes since its inception in 2007. EverGreen Escapes specializes in crafting the perfect outdoor or culinary experiences for our clients to all reaches of Cascadia and abroad. Environmental and social sustainability is at the foundation of our approach to travel and corporate events.

In my time at EverGreen Escapes, we have become the cutting edge in corporate team-building and retreats. Drawing upon a background combining Environmental Education, outdoor-guiding, and leadership training, I have created a fresh concept within a crowded field. There is no gadget or apparatus that defines an our programs. Instead, each program is built upon a carefully crafted backstory, and weaves in, adventure activities, and team building concepts. I immediately noticed clients who thought they had “done everything” were thriving in this new concept.

I love designing and leading programing for EverGreen Escapes because, through observation, I get to learn right along with the group.

Specialties: Diplomacy, marketing, natural history, communication

Terra Forma Education

February 2002 – Present (11 years 10 months)

Terra-Forma Education offers many unique programs based on a model that incorporates the concepts of mythic journey and magical realism with environmental education. In these programs, participants of all ages interact with characters in a complex story that is forming around them. The seemingly spontaneous storyline leads them through the challenges of self-awareness and group dynamics, as decisions are made that shape the future of the planet, just as it happens in our daily lives. Unlike many traditional education models, these participants are exposed to real life situations, and forced to make real life decisions, that help them become responsible adults. This all happens in the moment, without forethought, as students are involved and actively seeking knowledge, some for the first time.
