Clare Fazackerley's Summary

I am a Certified Public Accountant & Accredited Retirement Advisor and have been preparing individual and small business taxes professionally for twenty years.

Prior to that, I had a twenty year career in banking and regulation, as a National Bank Examiner for the U.S. Treasury Department, a bank consultant and as an employee of several commercial banks. I enjoy working with numbers and the intricacies of the tax code and regulations, but mostly love making my clients’ financial lives simpler and more satisfying.

I worked happily for seven years for a local tax preparation firm, but went out on my own when that company was sold to a large national firm, knowing I couldn’t continue with my very personalized service in that environment.

I hold certifications in retirement planning & financial counseling and love to help clients to make good decisions on a broad spectrum of financial questions.

Specialties: Tax law & planning, Financial Counseling, General Financial & Business Knowledge
