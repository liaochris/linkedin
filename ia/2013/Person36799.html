Donna Obstfeld - "We do HR"'s Experience

Managing Director / HR Specialist

D O H R Ltd

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Human Resources industry

April 2007 – Present (6 years 4 months) London / Hertfordshire

Established DOHR Ltd in 2007. An experienced HR practitioner with over 15 years HR management experience.

Donna’s expertise has been gained in interim, in-house and consultancy roles. Sector experience spans retail, transport, education, finance, pharmaceutical, technology and professional services. Past employers include Dixons Stores Group, TK Maxx, Metronet SSL, West Herts College and Hewitt Associates

Roles have included HR management, recruitment, project management, compensation & benefits and consultant.

Key projects have included recruitment & selection initiatives, HR systems, pay and bonus reviews, job evaluation frameworks, design and delivery of benefits and communications, the establishment of HR departments, redundancy and TUPE and strategic policy & procedure development.

Donna is a Chartered Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (FCIPD) and a member of the Civil Mediation Council.
