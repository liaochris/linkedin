Chuck Lobaugh's Summary

• Director of Technology The Troy, OH Daily News - Took this company from the old school days of no computers to a unified network of newspaper production. Additional successes included the development of large community Internet portal, the design and implementation of the initial network and workflow, and implementation of all systems.

• Advancement soon led to the position of Vice President and Chief Financial Officer in Troy. Major successes included the computerization of the operations and the integration of all related systems.

• After the sale of the TDN to Pulitzer, advancement lead to the position of Vice President of Operations with responsibility for all operations at the company's remote printing plant and commercial printing sales and management. Major success included the increase of sales volume by over one million dollars within the first year.

• In 2000, my wife Marianne and I made an acquisition of an Ohio printing company. Major successes included significant sales growth, computerization of the operations, and updating the press and production equipment and capacity. In 2006, we successfully sold the operation and returned to the media business in Florida.

• Named Special Projects Manager at The Villages Media, this capacity included responsibility for the research, analysis, recommendation, installation, and integration of all new technologies into the operation.

• In 2008 named, Information Systems Manager at The Villages Media. Major successes included the implementation of systems including, help desk, asset tagging, redundant backup, color automation, advertising, circulation, pre-flight, workflow, newsprint inventory, video playout and software inventory and compliance.

• In late 2010, another business acquisition opportunity brought my family to Hilton Head Island where we reside now. We have been blessed by a truly outstanding business opportunity in what has already proved to be a wonderful place to live and raise a family.
