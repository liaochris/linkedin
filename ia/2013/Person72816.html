Kathleen Deggelman's Summary

Kathleen Deggelman enjoys the success of her business today because she followed her passion for people, wellness, and financial empowerment. In 2004, she left her career as a top producing real estate agent in San Francisco to become a full-time investor, acquiring properties throughout the United States. She was highly successful as an investor yet, as she began to recognize shifts in the economy, Kathleen realized she needed a Plan B.

It was at that point she discovered the network marketing industry, and her career sky-rocketed. Kathleen was the first and only single woman to reach the top ranks of her company at the time, building a multi-million dollar networking distributorship in less than two years and helping thousands achieve financial success. Kathleen is now building partnerships worldwide, and lives her dream of helping people create financial freedom and wealth with their own part-time home-based business.

Kathleen is also a professional speaker, and is available to speak for audiences on the topics of growing a network marketing business, financial strategy, and wealth building.

Specialties

Kathleen is committed to mentoring others to live the life they dream about and to live with passion. She teaches others to look at wealth building with a new perspective, and actively helps them achieve their personal and financial success goals.
