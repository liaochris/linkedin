Cláudio Vilarinho's Experience

Owner

Cláudio Vilarinho - Arquitectura e Design, Lda.

Privately Held; Myself Only; Architecture & Planning industry

May 2005 – Present (8 years 4 months) Oporto, Portugal

We seek to start each project from:

1. one idea, one concept, one powerful image, the elements that will structure all the design;
2. creative solutions, that will translate the solution in a singular project;
3. from a contemporary language, because in this way we can “survive” in the future;
4. the implementation of minimalism, because we are identified with this concept;
5. from the flexibility theme, in order to generate different program values in relationship with the client wills;
6. from sustainability, as working theme and as we are concerned with the world;
7. from the furniture design inclusion (when previously accepted by the client), interpreting the Design as a complement to the Architecture Project.
