Barbara Jones' Summary

Helping businesses and business owners work smarter, not just harder, is my passion. I love working with Infusionsoft because it helps small businesses simplify and standardize their processes, put all their information in one place so they can find it, and get on with the growing their business. I enjoy it so much that I started a subsidiary of my training company - Small Business Automation - so I could concentrate more on Infusionsoft.

I have spent years in designing technical training – training that involves a specific field of knowledge or a work process. Those years prepared me well to be an Infusionsoft Certified Consultant. Infusionsoft helps small businesses convert more leads, get repeat sales, and grow without adding staff.

Specialties: Infusionsoft implementation consulting and user training; business process coaching; design and development of customized technical training programs.
