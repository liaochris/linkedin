Gail McMeekin's Summary

I am an executive/career/creativity/life choices coach in Boston and I am the Passionate Mentor to Creative Women. I help you to activate your innate creative gifts and then leverage them in your business and your life. There is creative gold in you which will lead you to prosperity. I work with people internationally by phone and email, or in-person in Boston. I am the author of the best selling book The 12 Secrets of Highly Creative Women, and two new books The 12 Secrets of Highly Successful Women, The 12 Secrets of Highly Creative Women Journal, as well as The Power of Positive Choices, two e-books and the Creativity Courage Cards all of which are on my site at http://www.creativesuccess.com . I have a background as a licensed psychotherapist, trainer, and consultant. I have an MSW from Boston University, a certificate in Human Resource Management from Bentley College and completed the training at the Coaches Training Institute. I have had my own business since 1982. I also offer LIfe Purpose Work using the potent tool of Scientific Hand Analysis. Isn't it time you step into your life purpose and create the life that you want?

Specialties

coaching and mentoring creative women entrepreneurs and professionals, Mastermind Creative Focus Groups, assessments including career testing and life purpose analysis, expansive thinking about executive and leadership challenges, stress resolution using my Positive Choices model, Outplacement, creativity and success in the arts and business, life planning and design
