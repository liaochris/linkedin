Donna Donohue's Summary

My company KidoImages has been creating affordable web sites for small businesses since 1999. I focus on web sites that are search engine friendly, with good clean code. I can create an e-commerce web site using a variety of shopping cart solutions that are easy to use to keep the products updated.

With the teaching skills I have gained over the years, I also create web videos for my clients in using various things that I may use on their site such as a ecommerce catalog of products, or a content/text management system.

A new area that I am specializing in, is custom WordPress themes. You can add a WordPress blog to your current web site, or have one in your new web site, that will match your site design. No more using the default themes. If you are a blogger, or podcaster who wants a unique look, to stand out from all the others, a custom theme is what you need. WordPress can also be used as a site-wide content management system, so that you can edit the entire web site yourself. Gotta love WordPress. Having a business blog on your site is an excellent way to feed the search engines new content, which keeps them coming back more often.
