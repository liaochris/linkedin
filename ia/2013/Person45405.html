Bryan Fowler's Summary

I am currently seeking full-time employment in the DC/MD/VA area. I am also freelancing in the mean time.

I have over 7 years professional design experience, 2 years of which stem from launching my own web design studio. Having my own business allowed me to feed my creativity by choosing projects that contributed meaningful art to my portfolio. It also taught me how to manage a SEO campaign, direct-mail initiative and work with people with varied web-proficiency and experiences.

I graduated Stevenson University with a BS in Computer Information Systems last December. I decided to go back to school to learn about languages and to understand what goes on behind the user interface. This understanding has made me a better designer.
