Dave Sawyer's Summary

I recently exited the full-time world of accounting to flex my entreprenuerial spirit, co-founding an Internet company. We plan to launch in 2012. Check back for more later as we begin to nail down the details and solidify our product.

In the interim, I've taken on a part-time consulting role serving as the lead finance person for two progressive science and technology companies.

Before my most recent venture, I was part of the audit team at Schechter Dokken Kanter for +6 years. During that time, I was primarily responsible for supervising various assurance and attestations services for clients in a number of different industries (see below), and I also developed some serious expertise in Excel. Beyond the normal audit duties, I was responsible for resolving the major IT concerns of the audit department and managed to build a solid foundation of general business and personal tax knowledge.

If you asked my former work colleagues (i.e., friends) or clients, you'd likely find that I've developed a reputation for cultivating personable working relationships and a penchant for embracing technology to enhance efficiencies and effectiveness whenever possible and appropriate.
