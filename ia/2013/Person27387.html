Chris Post's Summary

It’s no accident that Chris Post runs a technology company aimed at helping small business better market themselves online. These subjects have been lifelong passions.

He came by his small business expertise early, growing up in a family of entrepreneurs and launching his first business at age 8.

Chris has always been a technology early adopter and the go-to tech geek for his friends and family. He earned spending money in college by fixing computers and building websites.

After earning a Business & Marketing Degree from Cal State Chico, Chris began a successful foray into commercial real estate, ultimately earning his CoStar Power Broker status and setting sales records.

In 2009, the California State Treasure’s Office tapped Chris to manage their online education and training efforts. But the entrepreneurial life kept calling.

Chris launched Post Modern Marketing in 2010 to help Sacramento businesses connect with online customers. As a small business owner himself, and a fourth-generation Sacramentan, Chris is finely attuned to the special needs and challenges Sacramento business owners face. Chris and his business have been profiled in the Sacramento Business Journal, and he is a sought-out speaker on the topic of SEO and Digital Marketing.

Specialties

website design, search engine optimization (seo), local seo, paid per click advertising (ppc), emai marketing, social media marketing, graphic design, consulting, branding
