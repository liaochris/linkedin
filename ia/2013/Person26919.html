Owen Richason IV's Summary

Owen Richason, the founder of Copywriting Services Pro, is an online copywriter, SEO aggregator and social media promoter. Owen is a journeyman freelancer, making his way from hard-copy broadsheet into the digital age, growing with the Internet as it developed.
