Deron Dalton's Summary

I'm a Missouri School of Journalism alumnus with an undeniable passion for blogging, writing, social sharing and viral curating digital media focused on diversity, social issues and pop culture. For the past seven and a half years I garnered a variety of skills (listed in my skills section) -- making me an emerging media specialist.

I used my duty as a journalist to cover pop culture and issues about people of color, gender and sexual minorities, and celebrities fairly and accurately in a mainstream presence. This is a duty I strive to continue doing throughout my career.

The plan is to advance my career as a diversity and pop culture writer, and a social media curator to a media entrepreneur, and then, a media mogul.
