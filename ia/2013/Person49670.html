November 1979 – Present (34 years 2 months) United Kingdom

I am a Fashion Designer and a Stylist. Sole Owner of Grace Bardin and Collection Beau Monde.
While Grace Bardin Fashion provides bespoke tailoring for ladies and gentlemen, Red Carpet Haute Couture, bridal gowns and evening wear, Collection Beau Monde is dedicated to only natural and eco-friendly fashion, including house, spa and hotel collections.
Fashion, accessories and anything which is part of the Collection Beau Monde, has to be hand made and eco-friendly and natural, as well health supporting for human beings and in an absolute harmony with the Earth and Ancient Wisdom.
We are also professional, personal and corporate stylists, including media, film and advertising as well as weddings.

At the moment we are looking into possiblities of finding sponsors/partners as our plan is to expand globally our Collection Beau Monde which we wish to combine with a trust/charity.
