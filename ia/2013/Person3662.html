Alexander Bereznyak's Summary

Lead Technical Artist / Art Director in video games

Just happily shipped Metro: Last Light as Lead technical artist.
I have focused on improving existing tech and creating new tech for the game. This includes character, environment and special shaders; screen FX such as gasmask moist, breaking, wiping, blood etc; particle effects for weapons and special game events. I have also directed several game sequences, such as ghost crowd on Red Square, D6 destructing level, and other. Another part of my job was creating high quality materials for product presentation.

My past art direction experience includes two unreleased projects (PS3, Xbox360, PC, PSN), one released project (Wii), and about 20 projects for different platforms which I led as outsourcing AD. My experience as Lead artist adds about 30 more outsourcing projects of various complexity and size. Been around for 10 years, huh.

Another part of my job is devising technologies for art creation and art team management; establishing efficient pipelines for game design, art, programming teams.
