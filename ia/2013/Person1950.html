Adrian Buerki's Summary

In my professional life I started out as a civil engineer, but quickly decided to move into computer science. I have been developing Java software professionally since 2001. I have worked for companies in the United States and in Europe, in the industrial, financial and insurance sector, always within the Java technology space. Since December 2011 I am with the IT Service Centre for the Federal Department of Justice and Police, where I am part of the software architecture team.

Specialties

Apart from working with established Java EE tools/frameworks I like to tinker with cutting edge technologies. I am the project owner of a GWT (Google Web Toolkit) add-on library and give talks about web technology every once in a while.
