Kevin Seifert's Summary

My fascination with photography began in the fifth grade when my middle school offered a basic black & white photography elective using Holga cameras. I followed my heart and my vision to pursue a career in photojournalism where I successfully grew through education, mentors, workshops and, especially, through the people I photographed. I am a visual storyteller and people-person.

A camera has granted me access to some amazing opportunities to create images and freeze timeless moments in an instant. Hardwork, patience, timing and technical training show in my work. I bring a collaborative, passionate approach to the table.

Since graduating college with honors in my profession, I have been a full-time, working photographer serving editorial, corporate, and private clients with a camera-in-hand nearly every day. I have been on tight deadlines and delivered, I've been in remote locations and delivered, and I have been responsible to capture sentinel moments in people's lives and delivered. I love what I do and hope you will find my recommendations helpful in your decision to chose the photographer that will exemplify your brand, fit your style, and make you positively memorable.

Specialties: Wedding Photography, Wedding Photojournalism, Corporate and Executive Portraits, On-location lighting, Photoshop, deadline editing, illustrations, conceptual photography
