Logan Diaz's Summary

Logan Alexander Diaz is an advertiser, designer and business owner raised in Burbank and now residing in Anaheim Hills, California. Logan studied Interactive Media at the Art Institute in Santa Monica and business at the University of Phoenix.

Logan began providing design and web services independently in 2000 after realizing its long term potential. Shortly thereafter he became involved in the entertainment industry. At only 21 years old he took on the role as director for a show called On The Scene, which was an entertainment variety show about LA hotspots. He eventually was in talks with Viacom to absorb that property for their MTV brand, but after realizing the emerging Internet marketplace for entertainment he decided to retain ownership of On The Scene. Shortly thereafter he started his own company iGem Productions where he grew the On The Scene brand and simultaneously began providing design and advertising services under the iGem banner. In its peak, iGem occupied a 15,000-square foot office in Woodland Hills and maintained 12 full-time employees which included a busy design studio.

Prior to iGem Productions Logan served as the Director of Business Relations for a leading nationwide wireless company. There he created the business sales materials, protocols and defined the sales territories for new franchisees.

After iGem Logan became the Director of Marketing for Environmental Managers and started his second advertising agency called Lexgen Design.
