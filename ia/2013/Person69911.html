Janine Sarna-Jones, CPO®'s Summary

I am the catalyst for the change you seek. I work with individuals looking to effect change in their lives either internally—by becoming more productive on a daily basis in their home office or at work—or externally—by moving from one home to another. The core services I provide are:

- Action Plans & Accountability Coaching
- Productivity and Time Management Coaching
- Home Office and Office Organization
- Moving and Relocation Organization and Management
- Project Management

In each case, I act as a Change Agent and help my clients obtain the resources and tools required to achieve their goals, both personal and professional.
