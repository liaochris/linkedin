Andrea Bayles' Summary

Thank you for viewing my profile. I want to connect with people who are as passionate about their health and well-being as I am. Nutritional Therapy is an excellent method of health and lifestyle assessment.

The benefits of a Nutritional Therapy Consultation are:
* Increase your energy
* Eliminate health problems
* Learn healthier eating habits
* Feel fitter and stronger
* Improve chronic or long-term health conditions

By reviewing your health history, your family history and assessing your current health status, Nutritional Therapy can help to support and enhance your health and well-being. Nutritional Therapists are able to offer a variety of functional tests to look at how well your body is working. For example, stool testing can identify dysfunctions within the digestive system, saliva testing can highlight stress hormone levels. This allows us to provide a strategic and specific programme of recommendations. We do not diagnose medical conditions.

Each person is treated as an individual and recommendations are made for a personalized nutrition and lifestyle programme.

I am passionate about food and health. I want to help people get the best from their diet and lifestyles and achieve peak performance in their daily lives, whether at work, at home or at leisure. Knowing how to eat well is very important if you want to feel well, vibrant, energetic and have a zest for living! Additionally, good nutrition and diet will enhance your performance at work.
