Senior Business Development Manager

Dell, Inc.

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; DELL; Information Technology and Services industry

November 1998 – May 2007 (8 years 7 months)

From 1998-2007, Amy Baer was a Marketing Manager at Dell, Inc. where she served in a variety of marketing roles. She was recruited to Dell in 1998 as the Outbound Product Manager for the Enterprise Server Division, launching worlwide the Dell PowerEdge Servers 4th, 5th and 6th generation servers. In this role, she functioned as the worldwide product and solutions expert internally and externally. Her duties included product marketing to customers and partners as well as press interviews for product reviews, etc. Amy then served as the Manager of Enterprise Strategy and Communication for the AP/J region. After returning from her time overseas, Amy joined the Dell/Microsoft Alliance Marketing team, where she served as Partner Alliance Manager for the corporate sales divisions of Dell and Microsoft, managing a $12M annual sales and customer incentive programs budget covering both small-to-medium business markets, large corporations and the public/government space.
