Craig Amiot's Summary

Nobody likes to think about car accidents. Rest assured, if one happens to you, our ASE, I-car Certified Technicians will ease your mind with free estimates, a quick turnaround, cooperation with your insurance company, and a lifetime written warranty on all paint and body work.

Our state-of-the-art body repair shop is home to honest technicians with a combined 60 years of experience and a passion for what they do. I am proud of everyone who works for me because of their great history of friendly service. We are so concerned about your satisfaction that we have drawn up the 5-Star Collision & Glass Center Customer Bill of Rights. Ask to see it!

We provide an environmentally friendly shop featuring PPG Envirobase high performance waterborne paint. This cutting-edge technology reduces volatile emissions by as much as 80%, improving the air quality for our technicians, our community, and the environment. It also ensures a superb digital color match and carries a lifetime warranty for as long as you own your vehicle.

Insurance companies trust us to take care of their clients. We will work with your own insurance company to save you time and paperwork. All insurance estimates are welcome from anywhere.

We have rental cars available for use during your repair. Just ask!

Whether you own a car or a fleet, the next time your vehicle needs work, you can trust the experts at 5-Star Collision & Glass Center. After all, you will drive home our reputation.

We're open between 7:30 am and 5 pm, Monday through Friday. Call us at 701.775.3454 or drop by 1239 Dyke Ave., in Grand Forks for your free estimate or to make an appointment.

Craig Amiot's Experience

Autbody Shop Owner

Five Star Collision & Glass Center

August 2000 – Present (13 years) 1239 Dyke Avenue, Grand forks, N.D, 58203

I started 5-Star Collision & Glass Center with the idea of providing the best service and highest quality work to make sure every customer is 100% satisfied.

Over the years, we have kept current with technology and improvements to be able to continue offering our customers the best in auto body services.

In 2011, we installed the Miracle System for dent removal in ultra-high-strength steel vehicles. This high-tech system, unique in the Grand Forks market, allows us to repair more types of vehicles.

In 2012, we introduced waterborne paint that produces better color matches with more vibrant colors and improved paint adhesion. In addition, the paint is environmentally safe and provides a higher air quality for our workers.

Insurance companies trust us to care for their clients and their vehicles. We will work with your insurance company to save you time.

As always, our cycle time remains excellent. We will deliver your vehicle back to you as soon as humanly and technologically possible.

Autobody Technician

Wilcox & Malm

January 1985 – December 1995 (11 years) Grand Forks, N.D.

I repaired auto bodies and frames and painted vehicles as one of four technicians.

By doing a lot of heavy work on frames, I gained a deeper understanding of cars. I took car repair to the point where lots of people wouldn't have, pulling motors and replacing everything from the windshield forward.

The experience taught me much of what I needed to know to start my own business.
