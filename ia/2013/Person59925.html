Jayne JohnsonRecruitment's Summary

My company provides recruitment services to companies looking to source permanent staff for management, sales and office roles.

Having experience of recruiting for teams, coaching candidates and clients on recruitment policies and applicant attraction it seemed a natural step to become a Talent Dynamics Flow Consultant in August 2012. This qualification means that I can work with individuals, employers and candidates alike - to help them gain understanding of when they are In Flow how they can increase their own performance and how to create and build more effective teams.

Gaining an understanding of each clients business and what they are really looking for means that we can tailor our service to meet the needs of each client – not a “one size fits all service”
Sourcing staff for GP Surgeries, dental practices, marketing companies, accountants, plumbing, design and architects to name a few, has elicited excellent feedback from my clients. Recent comment-
"I used Better People to recruit a medical records clerk position in our busy reception team. Jayne sent 4 excellent candidates for interview, all of whom adhered closely to our requirements. I felt Jayne had really listened and understood what our practice needed and she dealt with the recruitment process efficiently and within specified timescale. I will use Better People for future staffing needs and would recommend them to other practices."

Another client commented -
“ I wish we had engaged Better People when we first set out on this recruitment project 8 mnths ago. Jayne had the brief for a short time & sourced 3 excellent candidates for me to interview. My new chap is doing well and we are all really happy”

"At every stage Jayne has provided an extremely efficient and personal service. I cannot recommend her highly enough as her perception of individual’s needs & requirements appears to be extremely intuitive".
