Bernardo Javalquinto, Economist's Summary

Bernardo Javalquinto is Chairman of the proposed Centro de Innovación y Desarrollo Tecnológico Latinoamericano & Social Business Center (CIDTEL), a Partner and Executive Director at JMLC International Consulting, a Chile based independent consulting firm. Mr. Javalquinto is knowledgeable in business development and provides advisory services to companies that want to do business in Chile. Mr. Javalquinto has been associated (former employee) with companies like the World Bank, IFC and Embassy of Chile in the USA. Henkel-Loctite. He is a Former Director at the Engineering and Business School (EBS) of Universidad de Aconcagua - VI Region. He is also a Lecturer of Economics, and Co-chairman MBA in Social Business, awarded (2 Times) by the Argentinean Congress as Leader for development in Latin America. Mr. Javalquinto 1997-2006 member of the Grameen Bank Dialogue founded by Peace Nobel Prize winner Prof. Yunus. Member Royal Economic Society, Founded 1890 UK

Specialties: Social Business - R+D+i+E - Merger& Acquisitions - Crisis Management - International Project Investment - Company Liquidation, Doing Business in Chile, Innovation Enterpreneurship, Technolgy Bioceanic Corridors, Future trends in the Southern Hemisphere
