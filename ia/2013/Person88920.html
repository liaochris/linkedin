Matthew Freyer's Summary

Matthew Freyer is an Award-Winning Visual Artist, Owner and Creative Director at Matthew Freyer Productions. Matthew specializes in dark artistry for the Halloween, Horror and Entertainment industries. His work has been featured in all forms of media, from magazines and films to clothing and skateboards – a testament to his wide-ranging creativity and interests. Matthew embraces his unique style, which may not fit the social norm, and presents it in a way that earns as much recognition as mainstream fine artists.

Matthew utilizes elements of photography, freehand drawing, and digital design to produce artwork that is both emotionally haunting and commercially viable. The creative process is what drives Matthew – spending hours conceiving and arranging elements to end up with something where there once was nothing. The most satisfying part of his work is being able to talk with an individual, understand their vision, and bring that vision to life beyond all expectations.

Specialties

Custom Artwork, Graphic Design, Illustration, Website Design, Logo Design & Brand Identity, Advertising & Online Marketing, Promotional Collateral
