Kimberly Metz Director + Photographer's Summary

Work has appeared in every major magazine from Vogue to Wallpaper.

What sets Kimberly apart is her versatility which spans multiple genre's stemming from life experience and an early introduction to photography by her high school photo teacher. Nothing short of an expert who bubbles with ideas, her fun sociable personality, quick thinking and off the cuff wit, she is a well rounded asset to any production.

After being in front of the camera having traveled the world exposed to different cultures and challenges, she returned to her ultimate creative passion behind the lens while working in Europe.

Recently just directed 6 PSA spots for American Red Cross charity as well as 6 other spots for fashion.

Attributes: Highly motivated creative resourceful individual with the ability to problem solve and work well in a team under pressure. Managed studio for over 12 years. Strong marketing skills. Creates from concept to completion layout posters, catalogs, graphics, advertising campaigns including storyboard, production, hiring of entire creative teams. Has the resources to provide complete production on location or studio; local/international.
