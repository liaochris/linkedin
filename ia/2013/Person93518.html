Michelle Manning-Kogler's Summary

Michelle Manning-Kogler is the author of “Quantum Soul Clearing – Healing the Scars Life Leaves on the Soul,” and a contributing author to “Pearls of Wisdom: 30 Inspirational Ideas to Live Your Best Life Now,” with Jack Canfield, Marci Shimoff, Chris and Janet Attwood, et al. She is also a co-contributor in "Ready, Aim, Captivate," with Deepak Chopra, Jim Stovall, et at

She is the founder of “The Quantum Soul Clearing Process.” She has been in the alternative health and wellness field for over 25 years. She is an internationally renowned Life Coach, Distance Energy Healer, Medical Intuitive, and Speaker.

Refusing to accept a devastating diagnosis of crippling rheumatoid arthritis, Michelle went in search of the answers to optimal health. That search included years of research, education, training, self-discovery, and has equipped her with the necessary knowledge and skills to show others how diet, life-style modification, and alternative healing modalities can repair and heal the body. She now creates customized whole-life health and wellness programs to help others achieve their optimal health goals.

Michelle’s Infinite Potentials Health and Wellness practice serves people around the world. By focusing on strengthening the four foundations for optimal health – emotional, mental, physical and spiritual – her clients experience vibrant health and vitality in every aspect of their lives.

Specialties: Distance Healing, Spiritual Life Coach, Bio-Energetic Practitioner, Soul Healer
