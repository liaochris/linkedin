Arie van Baarle's Summary

User Advocate develops brand concepts and propositions and helps organization develop based on meaningful relationships and interactions with stakeholders. Relationships and interactions that create value for all stakeholders.

All touch points of the brand play a role in this, both online and offline. An integrated approach in which the user always is the principle of all thoughts and actions.

The central question: How can you have the organization or the brand act on the brand promises?


This creates:

Brands that achieve great return on investment and contribute to society.
Organizations and brands where the expectations of customers are exceeded due to relevant services and added value.
Organizations where employees like to work and where they thrive.
The brand concepts, service propositions and resources developed by User Advocate can also be called on in the emotional sense in communication. So-called Rational Branding.




Specialties:
Strategy, Concept development, Business Model Innovation, Human Centered Design, Interaction concepts, Service Design, Experience branding, Social Media- and Experience design.
