Carol Shaddix's Summary

Imagine Golfing There Travel focuses on helping clients create great memories at stunning golf courses with Luxury Included options for everyone - even those who don't love to play the game. We also know that those who are passionate about improving their swing make have family and friends that want to travel with them that don't share their love affair with the game.

Here are some benefits our clients experience:

• Being able to enjoy planning your next vacation, knowing that your preferences are taken care of by a trained professional who will sort out the best resorts, room categories and dates to fit your travel time frame and budget.

• Being taken care of every step of the way, and knowing that everything’s included in the price – no surprises or extras. (You really can leave your wallet in the safe.)

• Someone else to take care of all the travel details for your group. No need to wonder if you copied down the correct legal name, birth date, correct credit card information for everyone. Once you’ve decided to travel together, you can leave all the other details to us!

• Exclusive experiences and special surprise touches you won’t get booking it on your own.

• A Give Back program that makes donations to your favorite cause or charity.
