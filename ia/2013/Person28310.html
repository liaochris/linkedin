Curtis D. Kuttnauer's Summary

Curtis is a creative entrepreneur that is focused on providing the best professional services for his clients. He currently runs two companies providing executive recruiting and financial improvement services for clients nationally. Curtis is the founder and President of Kuttnauer Search Group, Inc. (KSG), which specializes in the placement of senior sales, sales management and technical professionals in high technology firms. Clients include enterprise software companies, systems integrators, VARs and consulting organizations. Curtis consults clients with the audit & design of on sales compensation plans so they are aligned to business strategies and plans.

In addition, Curtis is a Principal of Alliance Cost Containment (ACC), where he brings many years of P&L management experience to assist clients in delivering financial improvements through expense reduction and audit & recovery reducing, while seeking new profit enhancement strategies. Clients include both for-profit and non-profit organizations, across many industries.

Curtis has a broad sales and sales management background, with over 16 years experience with IBM. While at IBM, he worked with both large and small organizations across multiple industries. Curtis spent a couple years at IBM headquarters as a Financial Consultant advising clients and interfacing with industry analysts. During his IBM career, he earned 10 100% Clubs, 1 Golden Circle and numerous sales leadership awards, including Regional, Branch and Industry recognition. Curtis worked in the client organization, IBM Server & Storage brands and IBM Software Group. In his last assignment, he managed a $1 billion software sales team.
