Boris Kushkuley's Summary

With a broad international background and fluency in three languages, Boris brings more than 20 years of senior management experience in leading providers of state-of-the-art digital, marketing, and e-CRM solutions exclusively focusing on healthcare and pharmaceutical industry. Boris is known for building, growing, and leading energetic and innovative marketing, service, agency and technology organizations. He has a strong understanding of how business and marketing interweaves with innovation and technology. Boris' years of multichannel and digital strategy are complemented with extensive hands-on technology and IT experience of architecting and building e-marketing solutions for Fortune 500 companies.

After earning a PhD in biophysics from the Sackler Institute of Molecular Medicine, Boris started his career at the Weizmann Institute of Science, designing and developing groundbreaking online interfaces to biological databases. Later, he joined SoftWatch—a pioneer of e-CRM and disease management solutions to the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries.

Eleven years ago, Boris joined Ogilvy CommonHealth as Chief Technology Officer. As President, he led Ogilvy CommonHealth's Interactive Agency and has recently been promoted to Chief Digital Officer overseeing Ogilvy CommonHealth's Digital and Relationship Marketing practice.
