My sister Sonna and I felt that the industry needed ungoing design trends and product information that would help consumers make smart buying decisions.We also felt if we showed better merchandise it would help retailers sell their products. She asked if I would stay on to help them develop new ideas and ways to market the magazine through social media.

Of course we are all missing Sonna, her wit and ideas but we're excited about the magazine and giving it added direction. We are offering training using the magazine as a format and will be visable at many design events.
