Bridget Jurgens' Summary

DOG & PONY is an award-winning graphic design agency. We create strategic advertising and marketing deliverables for entertainment, film and brand clients. DOG & PONY has over 20 years of combined experience in graphic design, account management, and project management.

At DOG & PONY, you get the best creative with none of the drama. Our witty no-nonsense approach to art delivers results with the satisfaction of individual client attention. We love to entertain, but still keep our feet on the ground and run with your strategy. Our boutique positioning utilizing low overhead allows us to custom build teams of the best ad industry professionals to fit each of your unique projects.

In July 2013, DOG & PONY became certified as Women's Business Enterprise (WBE) by WBENC and currently has an application pending to become federally certified as a Women Owned Small Business (WOSB).

CLIENTS:
Some of DOG & PONY's current clients include: Mattel, Inc., (Brands: Barbie, Monster High) Breakthrough Entertainment, Epic Pictures Group, Phase 4 Films, KOAN Inc., Instrum International, Camelot Entertainment Group, Inc., MonteCristo International, Instrum International, Blanc/Biehn Productions, Tica Productions, Mirrortree Productions, Photographer Peter Konerko, James Kendall Salon, Super Troop Entertainment, Gold Cap Films, Surgery Room Films, Anoushka Fashion Designs, Laundry Room Productions, Intricacy Films, Victory of the People Productions, Ruya Media, WorkingBug Media, Audacity Entertainment

BRAND EXPERIENCE:
