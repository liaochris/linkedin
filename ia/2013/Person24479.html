Christopher MacFarlane's Summary

My name is Christopher, but you can call me Chris. I'm from a small town in New Jersey near NYC. I graduated from The Rhode Island School of Design in Graphic Design. When I first sit down to design, I have to choose my color palette, or else everything turns out black and white. If I get stuck, I sleep on it and when I’m finished, I immediately find a spelling error.

Specialties: Computer
Fluent in Adobe InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop as well as Microsoft Office on both Macintosh and PC with a working knowledge of AfterEffects and Dreamweaver.

Design
Experienced at branding, identity, print production, color theory, web design, layout and composition using grids, hierarchy, vectors and typography. Proficient at sketching and bindery techniques.

Personal
Organized, resourceful, and self disciplined with collaborative, interpersonal and management experience.
