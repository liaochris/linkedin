Francisco Polo's Summary

“President of the World”. That’s what I wanted to be when I was a child. The reason was simple: I could see lots of injustices happening so someone had to stand up and make decisions to fix them. I was aware that that could take some time so I started to do things in the meantime to “save the world”: I started planting trees, putting empty bottles into the toilet to save water and even making my own recycled paper! (The paper thing used to drive my mom crazy)

At the age of 29 I put together my three passions (my will for social change, the potential of the internet and my relentless determination to make things happen) and became an entrepreneur who founded the fastest-growing startup ever in Spain, growing it from 40 users to 2.5 million in 18 months only.

Today, I still dedicate all my talent to empower people, to push the boundaries of what is possible on the internet and manage a team to multiply our impact, grow our platform, ensure great reengagement rates, promote our brand and keep increasing our revenue.

We made of Change.org the largest petition platform in the world and we are honored to help millions of users to start campaigns on the issues they care about while also helping our clients to connect with hundreds of thousands of people to support their mission.

I love making new professional acquaintances. Reach out if you want to talk about technology, business, empowerment, entrepreneurship or videogames.
