Izzy Kalman's Summary

Israel (Izzy) C. Kalman, MS, Director of Bullies to Buddies, Inc, is is a Nationally Certified School Psychologist and psychotherapist with 35 years experience. He has developed a uniquely fun and effective method of teaching children and adults how to solve their interpersonal problems through the practical application of the Golden Rule. He has produced numerous publications and materials for dealing with bullying in schools and organizations, taking a radically different approach to bullying than te orthodox one which hasn't been working. Over the past decade, he has presented more than 1000 full-day seminars to professionals throughout the US, and presented at numerous professional conferences.

Specialties: Training schools and agencies
Keynote speeches at conferences
Counseling individuals who are struggling with bullying or relationship problems
Creating harmony in the workplace
Production and publishing of books, audio CDs and videos on bullying, racism/sexism, and relationship problems

Izzy Kalman's Publications

  • Bullies to Buddies: How to turn your enemies into friends.

    • The Wisdom Pages, Inc.
    • June 1, 2005
    Authors: Izzy Kalman, Lola Kalman, Cover Illustration - Steve Ferchaud, Cover Design -Dunn + Associates

    This book gives kids a basic understanding of human nature and shows how to use that understanding to stop anyone from bullying them–and even turn their bullies into buddies. Kids–and their parents–are amazed at how quick and easy it is to stop being bullied once they understand the simple dynamics of bullying and the simple solution. It also teaches kids how to get along better with their parents, teachers and siblings. Even adults have dramatically improved their relationships by reading this book.

    The book is appropriate for grade 5 and up. Younger kids can also be helped if they are strong readers, or if their parents read it with them.

  • Comic for Victims of Bullying: Super-Dren–the De-Victimizer

    • The Wisdom Pages, Inc.
    • January 1, 2009
    Authors: Izzy Kalman, Chari Pere, Lola Kalman

    This beautifully illustrated comic book teaches kids how to stop being bullied by using the simple-but not commonly understood–wisdom of the Golden Rule. While most people think the Golden Rule means that we should be nice to people, the real purpose is to instruct that we need to be nice to people even when they are mean to us. It is hard for people to treat us like enemies when we treat them like friends.

    The protagonist is Super-Dren (Dren is nerd spelled backwords) who goes around saving victims of bullying by bringing them to an alternate universe in which they have turned their bullies into friends by using the Golden Rule.

    It is appropriate for children grades 2 and higher.

  • How to Stop Being Teased and Bullied Without Really Trying

    • The Wisdom Pages, Inc.
    • January 1, 1999
    Authors: Izzy Kalman, Lola Kalman

    This audio CD program includes all eleven of Izzy Kalman’s website lessons for victims of teasing and bullying, beautifully read by his older daughter, Lola, on two one-hour CDs. For kids (or adults!) who are being tormented by their peers or family members, this program will quickly teach them to solve their bullying problems and possibly save hundreds or thousands of dollars of expensive counseling or psychotherapy. It also has a recording of Izzy playing his entertaining “game” with a teenager. This game shows kids the “optical illusions” that cause the problem of bullying, and how to make the bullying stop.

  • A Revolutionary Guide to Reducing Aggression

    • The Wisdom Pages, Inc.
    • January 1, 2003
    Authors: Izzy Kalman

    Are you a parent or a teacher? Has the fighting and bickering between kids brought you to your wits’ end? The simple advice in this manual can reduce their aggression by 70, 80, or even 90%! And it will require less effort on your part! Imagine what you can do with all the time and energy you’ll save! In the process, the independence, resilience, and emotional maturity of the children in your care will be enhanced.

    Usually, when their is a lot of hostility among kids, it is unwittingly being caused by the adults. The adults are subject to a simple illusion. They see the kids fighting and they go about trying to get them to stop fighting. This manual explains in great detail how the truth is really the other way around: the reason the kids are fighting so hard is BECAUSE the adults are trying to stop them. Parents and teachers are amazed to discover how quick and easy it is to make the situation better.

  • Bullies to Buddies: Victim-Proof Your School

    • The Wisdom Pages, Inc.
    • January 1, 2008
    Authors: Izzy Kalman, Lola Kalman was the producer

    Victim-Proof Your School is a whole school bullying/violence prevention program that teaches kids the skills they need to deal with hostility on their own. It enhances children's independence, resilience, emotional maturity and popularity. Teachers lives also become easier, as they are able to spend their teaching academics instead of dealing with bullying problems. The program is consistent with all major principles of wisdom and psychology and can be used from elementary school through high school.

    "In my 15 years as a school counselor, I have never seen a more effective social skills program. This one is pure genius." — Jeannie Brewer, School Counselor, Snyder Elementary, Las Vegas, Nevada

    Victim-Proof Your School program includes:

    -DVD (100 minutes) teaches students to handle bullying on their own.
    -DVD (60 minutes) teaches school staff to respond to bullying in a manner that reduces bullying while enhancing kids’ emotional development, as well as how to handle been bullied by students and parents.
    -A Trainer’s Manual has detailed instructions for counselors or other guidance staff on using role-playing to teach people how to deal with all the typical bullying situations.
    -An Online survey to measure bullying before and after implementation of the program.

  • Turning Workplace Hostility into Harmony

    • The Wisdom Pages, Inc.
    • January 1, 2012
    Authors: Izzy Kalman, Lola Kalman

    The secret to harmony has been known for thousands of years, and it is called The Golden Rule. Whether it is your boss, your employees or your colleagues that are driving you to your wits’ end, this ancient rule can help you turn almost any hostile situation into a win/win. Your job will become more pleasant and the company will be more successful when people treat each other like friends rather than enemies!

    "If a picture is worth a thousand words, then a video is worth a trillion. There's deep neuroscience behind Izzy's seemingly simple methods. A masterpiece of emotional intelligence!" – Barbara Oakley, author of Evil Genes: Why Rome Fell, Hitler Rose, Enron Failed, and My Sister Stole My Mother’s Boyfriend

    This entertaining DVD program, 100 minutes in length, featuring Bullies to Buddies™ creator and Director, Izzy Kalman, will have you laughing while you learn to apply the Golden Rule to solve typical interpersonal problems at work. Your workday will become easier and more pleasant, and the company or organization will function more smoothly and efficiently. As a result, everyone at work will be happier!

    "Having been in the staffing business for 25 years and the consulting business for 20, I've interviewed over 10,000 people. People leave jobs because they don't like their boss or their coworkers. All of us will work for (or with) a jerk sometime in our lives. This DVD is both educational and entertaining.
