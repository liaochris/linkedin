Michele Newhouse's Summary

As a TRAINER and CONSULTANT, I love empowering others to define and reach their goals. I do research, analyze and organize information, and then share what I find! Many describe my personality as energetic … with a splash of zany.

I have a passion for communication – verbal, written, and online – as evidenced by the following experience.

■ Facilitated over 800 web events and delivered hundreds of in-person events for business professionals across North America. Designed and delivered numerous train-the-trainer sessions for peers.
■ Authored over 250 how-to and nonfiction articles.
■ Developed online content. Trained others to use social media for personal business purposes.

Since 2003, I have been with Right Management as a Career Management Consultant where I have created website content, led the redesign of event presentations used weekly across North America, and delivered training on topics such as these.

■ Negotiating
■ Business Networking
■ LinkedIn / Social Media
■ Starting a Consulting Business
■ Maintaining Optimism

Previously, I spent years running a small firm providing training, administration, and management services to associations and nonprofits. My prior business background includes work for large and small corporations in marketing, communications, office management, and analyst roles.

As for education, I have a Bachelor of Business Administration degree, various certifications, and hundreds of hours of training on subjects such as marketing, sales and new technologies. Still, my best learning has come from working with others through the years. I embrace that truth of Proverbs 27:17 -- “As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another.”

You’ll find I prefer plan-driven organizations with colleagues who have a sense of humor and strong moral character. And I thrive in roles with varied tasks that allow me opportunities to influence others, make decisions and be creative.
