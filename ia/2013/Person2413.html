Alex Greenwood's Summary

I'm a 20-year Public Relations veteran successful in PR strategy, media relations, crowdfunding, e-publishing and content marketing in the social media sphere.

I own a firm in Kansas City that's part of a national PR collective, working with national, regional and local brands. I'm also open to talk to companies and organizations about long- and short-term employment and contracting.

In 2012, I ran a successful national grassroots Kickstarter campaign to fund my latest mystery novel, then monetized that Kickstarter experience with a top-selling how-to ebook and seminar for my fellow entrepreneurs.

As an advocate for indie publishing, I founded Caroline Street Press, LLC, an independent book publisher and author services consulting firm.

I'm available for booking as a speaker and trainer on the subject of media relations, e-publishing, social media strategy and crowdfunding.

I've made presentations to numerous business and civic groups including KC SmartPort, KC Online Community, KC Ad Club, the Greater KC Chamber of Commerce, United Way of Wyandotte County, Topeka Community Foundation, Philanthropy Midwest and the Kauffman Foundation. I've had the privilege of serving three times as MC for the Arthritis Foundation Kansas City's "Art for Arthritis" event.


Specialties: Public/media relations consultant; Social Media Strategist; Content Marketing pro,; national, regional & local media placement expert; marketing; crisis communications expert; internal communications, special events; columnist, business development, on-camera press interviews; develop sound bites and media skills; message development; create talking points; strategic planning; public speaking, speech writing; copywriting; web content; political strategy and lobbying; digital photography; book reviews; blogging; podcasts & voice-overs, ebooks.
