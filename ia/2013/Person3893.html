Alex Boatman's Summary

I'm an over-achieving, visual bad-ass with a whole arsenal of creative skills ranging from illustration, 2D design, modeling, texturing, rigging, animation, lighting, special effects, and rendering and compositing.

While I enjoy the entire animation production pipeline, what I really love is the process of turning pencil lines into vertices (a.k.a. modeling).

I have a my BFA in Computer Animation from the Ringling College of Art + Design.
