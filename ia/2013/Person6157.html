Anand Pillai's Summary

Anand Pillai is the Sr. Exec Vice President & Chief Learning Officer at Reliance Industries Ltd. www.ril.com With more than 30 years of rich experience in the corporate world, Anand has handled challenging assignments in general management & as head of sales operations in companies such as Honeywell, Tata Unisys, Hughes Network Systems & Bay Networks. Anand has been a much sought after speaker in many national & international forums. He has been the distinguished speaker at the recent Fortune Innovation Forum in NY, USA (the only non American speaker to be invited) & also at the Fortune Global Forum in New Delhi, He also has given guest lectures at premier institutes both International like Harvard Business School, London Business School, MIT-Sloan and India's premier institutes such as IIM-Ahmadabad, SPJIMR, IMT, etc.
His leadership & strong vision has succeeded in bringing many accolades & recognition to the department, like “Excellence in Training” for 07-08 & 08-09,at the Asia Pacific HR Excellence Awards “Contribution to the Field of Global Training &Development” & Outstanding contribution to the field of Education, at the Global HR Excellence Awards-2009&2010, Award for Talent management, Innovation in Employee retention & Career management, Excellence in Training & HR Leadership award at the employer branding awards. He has also been recognized as “HR Professional of the Year” 2008-09&also given the HR Leadership award at the Global HR Excellence Awards & "Pride of the Industry" at the Star of India awards. Anand has been featured in the book “Leading with Wisdom” (along with 30 other leaders Steven Covey, Abdul Kalam to name a few) & the book “The Open Source Leader”
