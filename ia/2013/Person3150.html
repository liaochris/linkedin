Alan Kotok's Publications

  • Cienfuegos Rainbow: Observations of a 21st Century Cuba

    • Technology News and Literature
    • April 22, 2013
    Authors: Alan Kotok, Sharon Kotok

    An eight-day study tour of Cuba became an eye-opening experience that highlighted a country struggling to feed and house its population in the 21st century, while stuck in a confrontational cold war past. Alan and Sharon Kotok describe their experiences as part of a group of University of Iowa alumni who visited Cuba in January 2013.

  • ebXML: The New Global Standard for Doing Business on The Internet

    • New Riders Publishing
    • August 23, 2001
    Authors: Alan Kotok, David R.R. Webber

    Businesses have come to realize that information resources and technologies are key corporate assets and decisions on these matters need to be made at the highest level in an enterprise. As a result, when new standards come along with the potential for expanding collaborative e-business relationships that open new opportunities, improve cash flow, and reduce costs, business people need to know about them. That is the purpose of the book, ebXML: the New Global Standard for doing Business On the Internet. The book describes this new set of specifications not only in terms of the technology, but also in terms of their impact on the way business really work.
