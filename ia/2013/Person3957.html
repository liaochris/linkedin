Alex Dolan's Summary

Marketing is the art of convincing others to support you. Social marketing is the art of changing human behavior. I have spent a career doing both—helping mission-driven organizations reach their business goals through communications, and creating initiatives that drive positive social change.

Working in both the private and public sectors, I have proven successes in developing strategies based on research and deep analysis of organizations' specific challenges.

Specialties: Strategic planning, branding & positioning, messaging, partnerships, writing and editorial, project management, leadership, events, sponsorships, digital content strategies, social media, creative problem solving.
