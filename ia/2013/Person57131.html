NeoConsult ApS

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry

January 2001 – Present (12 years 10 months)

Partner and co-founder of NeoConsult ApS. Today I primarily work with Business Development.

NeoConsult is an international software and service company which specializes in developing, implementing, and maintaining SOA solutions based on solid business and industry knowledge for telecom incumbents, service providers, MVNOs and MVNEs.

Since 2001, we have handled advanced IT projects for both international and local mobile companies. Based on a well-founded understanding of business cases as well as the requirements of our customers, we provide scalable, reliable and flexible software that works.

Our main product NeoConsult Chayn BSS, is an industry proven business support system, scaling from the needs of small service providers to large incumbents. Chayn BSS includes billing, provisioning, CRM and Self Service.

The scalable and robust platform NeoConsult Chayn M2M is designed specifically for the handling of all types of machine to machine communication. Thus, the NeoConsult Chayn M2M enables 2-way communication in terms of data flow among people, devices, and systems.
