Jason Darby's Summary

Jason Darby has worked in public relations and marketing in the higher education sector for more than 15 years, and has nearly 20 years experience as a journalist.

Darby presently works as the Marketing and Communications Director for Palmetto College, a new online bachelor's degree completion program offered by the University of South Carolina. He also serves as Managing Editor of How We Live Magazine, a national publication that showcases homes, style, decor and careers.

Darby earned degrees in English and Journalism from SC State University and the University of South Carolina, respectively. He is also a Riley Institute Diversity Fellow (Furman University) and a certified Lean Six Sigma Green Belt.

Darby specializes in strategic and crisis communications, media relations, speechwriting and graphic design. He is also the co-author of a study on outdoor advertising effectiveness that is cited in a leading college advertising textbook.
