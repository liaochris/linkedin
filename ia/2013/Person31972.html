David Harris' Summary

My firm focuses on helping individuals and families who have have suffered serious loss, challenge, or tragedy, due to serious and catastrophic personal injury or wrongful death caused by auto accidents, semi truck accidents, and motorcycle crashes, among other forms of personal injury claims.

The firm's guiding principle is to provide each client with the highest level of advocacy marked by personal service, drive, and the highest ethical standards. We seek to help those in need to overcome their challenges caused by others' wrongful conduct, and enable them to move toward stability, security, and hope for the future.
