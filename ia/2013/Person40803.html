  • AICPA Peer Review Certificate of Recognition

    AICPA

    Bushnell & Company has received a certificate of recognition for each peer review since the firm's establishment in 1996. The peer review program is one way to reassure the public and regulators that the accounting profession is serious about maintaining and enhancing the quality of audit and accounting services. In 1988 the membership of the AICPA made the periodic quality review of public accounting firms a mandated requirement. Firms that are members of the AICPA’s Division for CPA Firms satisfy this requirement through a peer review every three years, a program which provides “an effective means of assuring the public that the firm has performed its accounting and audit engagements at a satisfactory level of professional achievement.”
