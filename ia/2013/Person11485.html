The app also features a loan calculator allowing staff to give customers an indication of how much they can afford and what their repayments might be.

  • MTF Android app

    • August 2012 to March 2013
    Team Members: John Bain, Joshua Lindsay, Ashleigh Whittaker, James Ing, Sara White, Karl Windle, Ian Timperley, Karl Bray

    We built the app from the ground up, taking the time to understand how an Android version would look and the unique differences of the platform. MTF for Android has the same great features as the iPhone version and a look and feel, that while similar to the iPhone app, fits into the Android ecosystem perfectly.

    At launch, the MTF Android app reached the top 30 in the Google Play Top New Free list.
