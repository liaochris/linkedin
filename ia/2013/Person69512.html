Joyce Saint- Cyr BA MHA's Summary

Formally educated in Accounting and having approximately 25 years in accounting. My purpose is to be the Community Partner proving marketing strategies. Marketing consist of Social Media usage, Press Release, Email Marketing (Constant Contact), and Affiliate Marketing Opportunities. Many times we wonder how to start. I realized there was no simple guide detailing what’s needed to start a business. Therefore, I wrote the book called, “Starting a Small Business - The Real Deal”. This book is an easy read step-by-step guide detailing what's needed to start a business. If you want to start or have started a business, this is a MUST have. This book will prepare you for the path to success! Joyce will develop or help develop your BUSINESS PLAN.
The key to personal development consist of many functions. An important factor ensuring an individual’s development; is understanding what is required. Social media is a growing trend, which requires professionals to seek and create positive relationships.
Joyce is a speaker requested to speak about social media, individual’s development and motivate. Owner/Operator of a coffee shop with live entertainment, business coach and host of Diversity Talks has demonstrated her passion is being able to help those seeking what “one” it. Currently she is working on her second book and inviting other professional to become published. Every entrepreneur has a story and she wants your story told.
