Heather Braden's Summary

Heather Braden has been a photographer and production expert for over 7 years Internationally based in New York City.

Beginning her career as a 1st photography assistant to renowned fashion, advertising and catalog photographers, Heather was responsible for overseeing all technical details, all cameras, gear and lighting, execution of digital and classic film techniques. Heather went on to serve as the lead producer and a photographer for editorials, ads, catalogs internationally and has been credited with creative solutions, smooth productions, avoiding disasters, taming wild crews, reducing expenses, and overseeing the creative direction with a smile. Whether on set, in studio or in an exotic location on 3 continents Heather earned recognition as single handedly leading some of the most successful productions internationally for a variety of respected clients.

She has also worked side by side with today’s leading fashion catalog photographers and directors on numerous projects from Ad campaigns to respected catalogs, fashion editorials, and personally oversaw photo shoots with celebrity talents like Jennifer Lopez, Angelina Jolie, Ricky Martin, Meg Ryan and others.

Heather now resides in NYC and continues to develop her personal style.

Specialties

Photography & Productions in fashion, commercial, catalogs, advertising, people, portraits, beauty, celebrities, travel, & international locations.
