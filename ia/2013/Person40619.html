Eliud Gautier's Publications

  • Establishing Roots with Your ITIN

    • Eliud Gautier
    • January 15, 2013
    Authors: Eliud Gautier

    This book is designed to help buyers and realtors alike with the process of buying a home with an Individual Tax Identification number issued by the IRS to families in transition with their immigration status. This is a unique and uncharted area for both the purchasing and real estate communities. Therefore, this book will help navigate through the treacherous waters of buying a home with an ITIN.

    The book is in English and Spanish so that both the non Spanish speaking realtor and Spanish speaking family could work through the process together. It will also give the Spanish speaking family the opportunity to learn about the real estate process in both languages.
