Chris Colston's Summary

Buying a new car is one of the most exciting days in a person's life. And it truly is an important decision, because your car becomes a member of your family; it becomes your best friend. Who else reliably gets you to work on schedule? Who else makes sure you get to the beach or the mountains safely? Who else do you spend time with each and every day?

Other than your workspace, you probably spend more waking hours in your car than any other room in your life (especially in Northern Virginia!) So yes, it's an important decision, and I'm here to guide you through it professionally so that you get a great deal, award-winning service, and most of all, so that you're HAPPY.

I've got a great company behind me: Toyota has the best cars on the road, and Ourisman Fairfax Toyota has an award-winning service department. So if you're looking for a great new vehicle, message me. I'll help you make a great decision.

And yes, I write novels, too. I AM THE WOLF MAN is currently available at Amazon.com. My new novel, THE HONOR TAP, will debut in time for the holidays.
