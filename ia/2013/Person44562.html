Fernando Rivero's Summary

With 17 years of experience as a marketing and online consultant, in the analysis, design, development and implementation of channel optimization strategies, online and 2.0 strategies, Internet as a sales channel, market research, marketing plans, customer loyalty plans, etc. has led him to be a leader in this field.

Their career is backed by the experience of having led and participated in numerous projects in this field for organizations of various sectors with clients such as AEDE-Association of Spanish Newspaper Publishers, American Express, Banco Popular, Bankoa, HSBC Bank, Iberia Cards, ING Direct, Insurance Caser, EOI Business School, Ministry of Tourism of El Salvador, Praxair Spain, Santander Bank among others.

Fernando Rivero is Co-author of the books "Customer Experience: A multidimensional view of Marketing Experience" (2012), "Keys to innovate in Marketing and Sales" (2009) as well as "MK Consultant for Commercial and Marketing" (2007).

He's currently Professor of Strategy and Marketing Management in CUNEF (College for Financial Studies) and Professor of New marketing and Digital Marketing in IEP-CEU.

He is also Director, member of the Steering Committee, responsible of the Online Strategy Committee and Coordinator of the Marketing financial and insurance sector Commission on Marketing Association of Spain, and Director of the Marketing Blogosphere Observatory.

Is Master’s degree in Marketing Management from ESADE Business School. He studied Business Administration with specialization in Marketing from the Universidad Complutense (CESSJ Ramon Carande) and Marketing for e-commerce by UC Berkeley. He has taken several refresher courses in ESADE, IE and ESIC.

He has participated as a lecturer and speaker at various courses, seminars and conferences and also given many training courses in company for departments and companies of all sizes in his areas of specialty. It's a regular presenter and speaker of the National Congress of Marketing and Sales.

Specialties

Analysis, design, development and implementation of channel optimization strategies, online and 2.0 strategies, Internet as a sales channel, multi-channel strategies, marketing plans, market research, customer loyalty plans, etc.

Fernando Rivero's Publications

  • Keys to innovate in Marketing and Sales

    • Wolters Kluwer España
    • May 19, 2009
    Authors: Fernando Rivero, Luis Asenjo Perez, Pedro Martínez Jover

    This book shows the relationship between innovation and marketing and sales.

    It covers therefore a vacuum as almost always books on innovation show the other sides, when the key is generating wealth and prosperity, and this requires to close its cycle with the sale of the product.

    Innovation is the most important professional challenge we have, due to the increasing ease of copying our differential values ​​and increasing difficulties we face in a rapidly changing business environment.

    As Einstein said, problems can not be solved at the same level of thinking we were at when we created them. We need radical change that introduces innovation ("the electricity was not invented by a manufacturer of wax candles.")

    Innovation today is reinventing business models, processes or working methods, rather than launching new products or use the last technological advances is to build new markets where none existed before. We have to select and implement the best ideas and put them into the market in record time. We have to move to a creative culture.

  • Consultant for the Commercial and Marketing Management

    • Wolters Kluwer España
    • July 1, 2007
    Authors: Fernando Rivero

    The book Consultant for the Commercial and Marketing is a reference work, essentially practical, containing all the necessary concepts for the development of commercial and marketing activity in a company.

    In this book, you'll find Spanish market trends and international strategies for success in the commercial field, the most proven and efficient management techniques, and case studies and examples of large and small companies in many different sectors.

  • Customer Experience: a multidimensional vision of Marketing Experience

    Chapter summary: we must be aware that for our customers our company is the same thing regardless of the channel they use, and that their experience with our brand is shaped by the whole story of their dealings with us.

    A real innovative approach, to the Customer Experience practice. A book in which are grouped and summarized the amount of knowledge and experience of recognized professionals.
