Lori Hanson's Experience

Founder & CEO

Learn2Balance

July 2008 – Present (5 years 4 months)

Life balance strategist and founder of Learn2Balance, Lori Hanson knows personally what it takes to create balance in life. Her battle with bulimia started at the young age of fourteen and continued into her forties. Her low self-esteem propelled her into becoming a stressed out “Type A” corporate workaholic. As she struggled to find balance and happiness for almost 30 years, Lori an amateur body-builder established a deep understanding of diet, nutrition, exercise and alternative medicine and their effect on human behavior.

Lori tells her story and shares tips for managing stress, improving nutrition and balancing lifestyle in her award-winning books It Started with Pop-Tarts®, Teen Secrets to Surviving & THRIVING and Stress Survival Kit for College Students, as well as through membership clubs and personal coaching.

Lori has appeared on radio and television programs internationally and has been featured in Essence, New Living Magazine, AFLV Connections, monthly newspaper columns, Wellsphere.com, Limitless Living.org, and numerous other publications, bringing awareness to the epidemic of stress, eating disorders and the risks of overloaded living.

Sharing her inspirational personal story Lori has spoken at conferences, corporations, college campuses and events nationwide—wherever people are seeking intelligent, practical, and entertaining ways to find balance in their lives.

To learn more about Lori Hanson visit http://Learn2Balance.com

    • May 2013 to September 2013
    Team Members: Lori Hanson, Leysa Noone, Mara Purl, Theresa Byrne, Mary Ann Plocher, Anne Rojo, Dr. Caroline Smith, Beth Kuper, Shannon McAndrews

    First annual Learn2Balance Professional Women's Retreat. The mission of the retreat is to give overwhelmed professional women and women who seek a higher quality of life, tools to be F.R.E.E. (Find Relaxation, Energy and Empowerment).

    "Women who are balanced, relaxed and have pro-active tools for dealing with stress are better executives, employees, business owners and members of the community. We are thrilled to hold our first retreat which was four years in the making. Session topics include improving quality of life, engaging creativity and passion for success, improving relationships, techniques for relaxation and more. We've created the program to be interactive and fun!"

    The featured speakers include Colorado based business leaders Lori Hanson (Founder of Learn2Balance), Mara Purl (Award-winning author of Milford Haven novels and actor), Leysa Noone (Owner of VedaLuz LLC), Mary Ann Plocher (Owner of Rishi's Crowwing Yoga), Anne Rojo (Private Practic e Hellerwork), Dr. Caroline Smith (Owner of SportsSense Co.), Theresa Byrne (Owner of United Martial Arts) and Beth Kuper (Owner of Path of Chi Feng Shui).

    Sponsors for the event include KeyBank, CitiWide Banks, The Green Herb and Aimee Cohen Executive Coach for Women.

    A portion of the event proceeds will be donated to SafeHouse Denver to provide emergency shelter for victims of domestic abuse.
