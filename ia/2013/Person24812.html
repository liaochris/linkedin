Chuck Parker's Experience

Chief Revenue Officer

Unicorn Media, Inc.

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry

June 2013 – Present (3 months) Greater Los Angeles Area

Responsible for driving revenue growth at Unicorn Media through the marketing, sales and partnership functions. The digital video industry is about to experience hyper growth in the ad-supported segment as technology readiness for creating a "TV-like" video viewing experiences reaches a market that is inundated with a massive proliferation of smart phones and tablets. TV executives in every segment are looking for a way to increase their CPMs to offset the steady decline in analog TV ratings and to combat disruptives industry approaches from companies like Aereo, while at the same time trying to monetize the market phenomena that is "2nd Screen". Unicorn Media has the technology to enable multi-screen and connected device video ad insertion which significantly improves existing video ad completion rates and creates new ad inventory potential for publishers, brands and ad networks.
