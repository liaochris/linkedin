Kristie Finnan's Summary

Kristie is a Registered Dietitian/Licensed Nutritionist in private practice in Doylestown, PA.
KLF Nutrition at
Eat Right Bucks
875 N. Easton Rd- Suite 6B
Doylestown, PA 18902 Phone: 215-230-1900

As a Nutrition Counselor & Educator she helps teach people healthy eating & wellness strategies by eating Real Food!

After an accomplished Career in the Pharma/Biotech industry, where she spent most of her career working in Immunology, Kristie is now using that background to help others via nutrition counseling and education. Areas of interest include: food allergies and non-IgE food sensitivities, Celiac's, GI Disorders such as IBS, migraines, fibromyalgia, oncology and other auto-immune and inflammatory diseases. Kristie is also very passionate and skilled working with kids (and families) who need help with weight management, picky eating, behavioral and neurological disorders. Whether it's a picky eater, over-eater, or food allergy/intolerance, Registered Dietitian and Mom- Kristie L. Finnan- establishes a special connection to help tackle the problem.

She is also a speaker for the PA Academy of Pediatrics working with the EPIC® program- (Pediatric Obesity: evaluation, treatment and prevention in community settings) teaching other health providers (primary care physicians, nurses and school nurses) about Childhood Obesity& Strategies for Prevention.

As a Kids Eat Right Campaign volunteer (and 2x Grant Recipient), she has been doing education in schools and in the community via assemblies, school classroom visits and teen athlete & coach workshops to help kids, teachers, parents and coaches learn about new foods and develop healthy eating habits for maximum performance. She was also awarded the first ever- Sports Illustrated Kids- Sports Parent of the Year award and is in the December 2012 SIKIDS magazine!

More about Kristie: http://Kristiefinnan.com or http://EatRightBucks.com
