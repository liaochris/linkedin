Brandon Munson's Summary

I am the founder of Fulcrum Design, LLC, a strategic consulting and technology firm specializing in the formulation, development and implementation of interactive technology solutions.

Fulcrum Design Interactive specializes in the development of interactive mobile technology solutions ranging from the latest in cutting edge SMS geo location marketing programs such as Frugaldoo to our virtual waiting line management systems for the restaurant and higher education sectors i.e. Mobile Matradee and Student Q. Fulcrum Design Interactive clients include ESPN, New York Jets, Dave & Busters, Adams & Knight, Max Restaurant Group and Gwinnett Technical College to name a few.

I have also been a health care executive since 1995. I am a Fellow of the American College of Health Care Executives. I have extensive network development and post acute care leadership experience. I have been the technology consultant to the Meriden / Wallingford IPA for over 15 years. In my capacity with the IPA I am currently leading the electronic medical record (EMR) adoption initiative as a Direct Assistance Contractor to Connecticut's Regional Extension Center. The goal is to get the IPA's physician members to achieve "meaningful use" with an electronic health record by 2015.

In my spare time I am on the faculty at the University of New Haven and Charter Oak State College where I teach health care policy and planning, organizational theory and design and health care economics.

Specialties

Interactive technology development and programming including geo location marketing solutions, queue management applications, SMS mobile marketing, QR codes, text marketing, apps, website design, Web 2.O applications, instant messenger bots mobile ticketing and redemption solutions.
Health care strategic planning and consulting, EMR selection and meaningful use adoption and nursing home culture change transformation.
