Mark Herbert's Summary

In the new reality an intimate relationship with your stakeholders has to begin before you offer a product, service, or solution. Creating this relationship with your internal and external stakeholders is what high performing organizations do brilliantly. The competition for customers and talent is fierce.

We NEED a paradigm shift!

Our goal is to help our clients make that transition.....

My goal is to build "cults" for my client organizations. There is such a degree of alignment around values and value there in no "line" for employees and customers where we stop and they begin.
They are committed- physically, pychologically, and emotionally impelled. They gladly give up other choices....

You will never have higher engagement from your customers than you have from your employees!


Specialties: My company and I have been recognized by LinkedIn, Focus.com and other organizations as leading experts in the development of employee engagement strategies and integrated employment brands, organizational development and meaningful change management and organizational development strategies.
We consistently deliver organization centric solutions and results because of our comprehensive, integrated approach!
