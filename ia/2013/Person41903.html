Eric Allen's Summary

I currently lead my own consulting firm, making improvements to manufacturing organizations both large and small.

For over 20 years I worked in manufacturing management for the Folger Coffee Company, first as a subsidiary of Procter & Gamble, and later as a part of the JM Smucker Co. During my years with Folgers, I held numerous positions, gaining both operational and technical expertise in the process of manufacturing. I led operations as large as 90 direct reports and led several significant start-up operations. I managed capital projects of up to $10 million and annual operating budgets of $10 million. I led organizational change, implementing self-directed teams in a union factory, leading a number of plant pillars in Total Productive Maintenance, as well as delivering results through the systemic use of automated data-driven metrics. I am technically adept at packaging machinery design and troubleshooting. I have made numerous technical contributions, utilizing expertise in Control Loop Tuning, Web Control Theory, programming of Programmable Logic Controllers, Human Machine Interfaces, and set-up of virtual computers.

During off hours of my full-time job, I have assisted my wife's business, Marketsmartz, Inc. by personally executing accounting and technology functions. I completed all bookkeeping, payroll, and tax returns for the corporation for the past ten years and the predecessor businesses dating back to the mid-1990's. I managed the hardware, software, and networking of the business computers of employees, transitioning moves between 5 physical locations. In recent years, more effort has been to devoted to email and web site development.

Prior to these efforts, I gained experience in college as a Physics lab instructor and serving as President of my 75 in-house member fraternity. During high school and continuing into college summers I worked 5 years at an amusement park, managing up to 50 teen-agers.

Specialties

Statistical Process Control, Control Loop Health, Web Handling, Front End Engineering, Lean Manufacturing, Total Quality Management, Ergonomics, 5S, Reliability Engineering;

Experienced pillar owner for Autonomous Maintenance, Focused Improvement, Education and Training, Leadership, Initiative Management;

Allen-Bradley and Siemens PLC, Wonderware, GE Proficy Plant Applications and Historian, Computer Networking, Computer Virtualization, CAD, Wordpress, and Microsoft Office programs
