    • AAJ Convention 2008
    • 2008
    Authors: Barry Walker

    This racial reasoning and perception also permeates the insurance industry. Historically, insurance companies have been able to discriminate by way of redlining entire communities. My opinions come mainly from my personal experience and a study conducted by Tom Baker and Karen McElrath at the University of Miami after Hurricane Andrew ravaged south Florida.
