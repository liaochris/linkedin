Claus Østergaard's Summary

During my Ph.D-studies I focus on the visitors' and guests' user-experiences at attractions like theme parks, at concerts and festivals, the airport, shopping center and so on, when they are using location specific mobile application (such as smartphone applications, mobilesites etc.).

In my work as a project coordinator and consultant for ApEx (Centre for Applied Experience Economy) I've been involved in a variety of different projects. Most of the projects focus on developing new experiences for the users by develop traditional experiences (such as visiting art museums, amusement parks, libraries etc.) by integrating mobile technology that actively involve users in new and engaging experiences - via mobile media content.
Some of the partners in the different projects include KUNSTEN - Museum of Modern Art Aalborg, Aalborg Public Library, Fårup Sommerland, Aalborg Zoo, Skallerup Klit Feriecenter, Nordsøen Oceanarium, Responsfabrikken and More Mobile Relation.

During my MA in communications I specialized in network-driven branding for start-ups and small businesses. Network-driven branding is primarily based on (1) the entrepreneurs' or business' networks (online as well as physical), (2) user-driven innovation and (3) a loosely structured organizational structure, where employees work independent of location/place and time.

I started my first business in early 2006 with an associate, focusing on marketing for one-man businesses and small start-ups.

I'm interested in experience economy, user-driven innovation, network-driven branding, entrepreneurship, academic work as well as photography, video editing and digital artwork.

Specialties

Project coordination and management, user involvement, user experience, innovation, concept development, network-driven branding, marketing, communications.
