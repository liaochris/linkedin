Jeremy Osborn's Summary

Multifaceted designer, educator and writer who emphasizes user-centered design and specifically user experience. Author of best-selling books on web technologies on topics that include HTML5 and Web Design. Experienced veteran of developing e-learning content and measuring student performance in an online environment. Producer and author of numerous educational videos on topics as far-ranging as Prototyping to HTML5 and Responsive Design.

I have strong experience in managing teams of creative people, both in the corporate world and education. I am also a well-seasoned educator and presenter and have consulted and/or led trainings across the US for clients such as Google, Microsoft, Sony, Canon, T. Rowe Price, Staples,Time/Warner and far too many software companies to list here.
