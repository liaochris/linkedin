Jennifer Shelton's Experience

Director Small Business Development Center

Western Washington University

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months)

As a Certified Business Advisor, Jennifer provides no-fee, confidential advising to small business. She assists clients on developing loan application packages, forecasting, marketing strategy, written business plans, analyzing financial statements to improve profitability, and other topics driven by the client's goals.

In 2010 Jennifer was recognized as the WSBDC state star performer and later that year awarded the State Star for Washington award at the national conference of the Association of Small Business Development Centers.

She has taught and written curriculum on business planning and technology called 21st Century Business Planning which is currently being used for the Self Employment Assistance Program in Snohomish County to help new entrepreneurs learn to run a business successfully.

Small Business Development Center Director

Edmonds Community College

Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Higher Education industry

2005 – January 2010 (5 years)

Jennifer Shelton is a Certified Business Development Advisor with the Washington Small Business Development Center Organization (WSBDC).

She brings over 15 years business development experience in corporate, non-profit and service business areas including starting up and managing a retail coffee house.

Areas of specialization are developing powerful sales and marketing strategies, business plans and loan packages, and analyzing financial statements to improve profitability.

She is passionate about supporting the entrepreneurial spirit and providing access to information & tools to create and sustain successful business ventures.
