Christopher Livemore's Summary

Chris is a Director at Ibex Earth, a not for profit organisation that he established in 2008 to harness support from businesses and individuals to help preserve global biodiversity and protect threatened habitats and endangered species.

Qualifying as a solicitor (specialising in environmental law) in early 2008, Chris has since gone on to gain extensive experience within both the low-carbon and environmental sectors.

Through Ibex Earth, Chris provides businesses with the opportunity to support some inspirational environmental and conservation projects, enhance their corporate responsibility programmes, and position themselves as both a sustainable and environmentally responsible business in the process.

Additional details about the work that Chris is involved in can be found at www.ibexearth.com

Specialities: corporate responsibility; brand management/development; film; digital media; environmental law;

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Environmental Services industry

March 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months) London, United Kingdom

What we do:

We create unique and innovative projects that are designed to raise awareness about global conservation, whilst galvanising businesses and encouraging individuals into taking positive action to safeguard our planet’s environment.

We run an engaging events programme to support our initiatives and provide a series of services to businesses looking to help protect threatened habitats and endangered species.

Who we work with:

We work directly with over forty charities to help deliver our projects and ensure that they all achieve the maximum environmental benefit possible. Each charity has been chosen due to their wealth of experience and proven track record in delivering successful conservation projects across the world.

What we offer businesses:

We run a series of services - corporate responsibility, brand, digital media and film - to provide businesses with the opportunity to support some incredible environmental and conservation projects, enhance their corporate responsibility programmes and position themselves as both a sustainable and environmental responsible organisation.

Rainforest Life Funding

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Environmental Services industry

January 2011 – Present (2 years 10 months)

RLF is a benefit of the community society established in January 2011 with the specific purpose of maintaining and protecting rainforests, preserving bio-diversity, developing sustainable forestry techniques and promoting carbon sequestration projects around the world to mitigate climate change.

RLF will also assist local populations to use and develop land and land based resources in a sustainable manner through the sharing of expertise, education and benefits. RLF will use and support the development of carbon credit mechanisms which may generate additional revenue streams.
