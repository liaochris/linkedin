Lance helps sales managers get off of bridges where they hang with the names of salespeople on their chests ...

LANCE has installed new systems: people, processes (sales plans, activity management, face-to-face skills), tools, and skills to help small entrepreneurial and billion dollar companies achieve sales goals.
