Loreto Weir's Summary

Loreto is a Freelance Educational Technologist & Graphic Designer.

After completing her third level education many moons ago, Loreto enrolled and successfully completed a 2 year course in Graphic Design at the Crawford school of Art in Cork. Her place of work closed in 2004 she returned to full time education in 2005 at the Tralee Community College doing a PLC (post leaving cert) in Multimedia Productions (11 Distinctions). This developed an interest in Multimedia and her lectures encouraged her to enrol at the IT Tralee

Loreto graduated from the IT Tralee (2009) with a 1st Class Honours in Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Interactive Multimedia and in 2010 with a 2nd Class Honours in Masters of Science in Educational Technology. Since she left college Loreto have worn many hats, in that she has many different roles and tasks performed. Loreto is always open to new possibilities, It keeps things interesting. Loreto has recently completed her Train the Trainer course with the Open College in 2013.

Services she provide

- 1:1 tutoring in basic/intermediate computer skills and related services in Tralee & North Kerry.

- grinds to students by request at the Institute of Technology Tralee in Dreamweaver/Photoshop etc.

- proof-read, edit and transcribe a students dissertation or project and offer help and advice that will save time and money in the long run.

- She am available to create quality websites to suit your budget, or teach you how to create a quality website that you can edit and update regularly. Of course she is more than happy to assist you with any queries you may have along the way.

- creating RLO’s ( examples below) and elearning courses.

- customising your new laptop or PC to your specifications with full anti virus and Office Suites

In my spare time she volunteers as a literacy tutor.
