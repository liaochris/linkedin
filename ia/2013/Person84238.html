Magnus Møller Petersen's Summary

I've been working with software since the early 90's. Back then, of course, I was just a kid playing with Assembly and Pascal, and it was only for fun, but it turned out to be much more. I've held different positions during my professional career the last 10-15 years. This has given me great experience and insight into different industries and technologies; both theoretical and practical. I'm both curious and like a good challenge, combined with being very creative and pragmatic. I'm a good good communicator with fantastic technical and commercial understanding. And that can be topped of with a great sense of humor and customer focus.

I have a extensive technology background, and have been particularly focused on all things mobile intelligence and mobile technologies. Such as mobile devices, convergence, OTT, CEM, business intelligence, data discovery, big data, business analytics, device centric business analytics, process optimization, product innovation, mobile strategies, operator requirements, technical sales, experimentation, playing with different technologies, the mobile network (all the way from low level signaling and to high level app development), messaging, LTE, RCS, the big vendors, integration with social networks and app development.

I have a both broad and in-depth knowledge of how to build great products, and how to get them in the hands of the customers, and how to help the customer. I'm very much focused on the entire value chain.

As the CSO, I direct the sales staff and activities, and plans, develops, and establishes policies and objectives of sales function in Mobilethink in accordance with the strategic objectives of organization. I also device sales strategies to optimize the sales function and have a high focus on efficiency, automation, customer relations, networking and alignment across the company. I'm very much involved in driving innovation, performing risk analysis, long term strategy planning, business/sales/lead development.
