Ellen Blum Barish (MSJ)'s Summary

My book of essays, Views from the Home Office Window: On Motherhood, Family and Life, was published in 2007 and my nonfiction writing has appeared in national publications and on WBEZ/Chicago Public Radio. You can find some of my currently published works under Publications on this page.

I teach writing at Northwestern University and StoryStudio Chicago and have spoken on writing-related topics at Northwestern, DePaul and Columbia College and have facilitated writing workshops at Ragdale, Off Campus Writer’s Workshop, New Trier Extension and Writers Workspace. I also work privately with writers on creative, academic and business writing.

When I'm not teaching or coaching, I write personal essays and tell stories. In the past year, my work has appeared in Literary Mama and I a storyteller at Story Sessions Chicago.
