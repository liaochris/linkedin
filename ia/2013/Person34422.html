Derek Godfrey's Summary

With well-honed expertise in strategic planning and business development, Derek Godfrey is Director of Strategic Planning and Business Development for Micro Matic USA Inc., where he has used his visionary thinking and entrepreneurial skills to develop and execute a five-year strategic plan doubling revenue of the North American manufacturing, engineering and distribution business.

Helping solidify the company as an authority leader in the market, Godfrey also served as Director of Marketing, e-Business and Information Technology, bolstering brand awareness and revenue, and Director of e-Business, where he brought the $250 million, 1,700 employee worldwide company online globally. As part of the executive team, he touches every aspect of the business through managing cross-divisional issues and evaluating new technology and business.

Previously, Godfrey founded a Los Angeles-based internet start-up e-commerce business supplying beverage dispensing equipment to the U.S. market, which has since been acquired by Micro Matic and evolved into the largest and premier leading North American site for beverage dispensing equipment. Likewise, he was founder and President of a supply chain outsourcing and services firm in Seattle, managing $35 million in inventory for four U.S. distribution centers representing thousands of SKU locations.

Godfrey brings enterprise sales experience from Yahoo! Inc., where he sold million-dollar corporate My Yahoo! portal solutions to city governments, and further operation and supply logistics management improvements in the U.S. and Canadian brewery industry.

Recognized as an entrepreneur, visionary thinker, expert in business platform building for rapid growth and true leader in organizational change, Godfrey holds an economics degree from McMaster University and an associate degree in Marketing and Advertising from Algonquin College of Applied Arts and Technology, both in Ontario, Canada.

He regularly blogs at www.DerekGodfrey.com.
