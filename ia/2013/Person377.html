Nick Aalerud's Summary

An established entrepreneur, speaker, & owner of several businesses, Nick prides himself on sharing with individuals his challenges, his successes, and his ongoing education in everything he does.

AA Real Estate has 3 main parts:

1. AA Real Estate Properties / Rapid Property Relief

In this branch, we provide real solutions to complex real estate problems. Whether it be divorce, foreclosure, death, retirement, and other sudden life changes, to as simple as fixing a balance sheet, we find solutions through a number of different methods.

Our second mission is to provide quality, affordable housing to our buyers & tenant communities. Through prudent acquisition & effective management, we are able to provide excellent housing to families & individuals from all facets of life.

2. AA Real Estate Partners

This is our investment branch. In this, we strive to offer first-class investment opportunities to our exclusive network of private investors, with controlled risks, and couple that with unparalleled customer service.

We have developed our own strategies & techniques that allow us to locate, negotiate, & acquire both residential & multi-family investment property in targeted areas throughout the country, and either hold for cashflow or rehab & resell for a shorter term. By consistently evaluating and testing new markets, it enables us to bring the best opportunities to the table and instill effective management techniques that no other real estate firm can bring to the table.

3. AA Real Estate Mentor

As Nick transformed his own financial future through finding opportunities in real estate, he found many people were yearning to do the same, but that they wanted a local mentor instead of those bigger, national names.

This branch focuses on individually taking students through their own fears & challenges, establishing goals with them, and then taking them by the hand to ensure they implement their plan, using real estate as their vehicle to creating wealth.

Nick Aalerud's Experience

Managing Member

Short Sale Mitigation

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 6 months)

Having worked with multiple 3rd party negotiation services for our short sales, we ran into 2 recurring problems:

1. Lack of consistent communication & follow through with our files, and

2. Lack of aggressive "negotiation" with the lenders' loss mitigation departments. (Many were "Approval getters", not "Negotiators".)

We took these two complaints we had, and established our own in-house negotiation company to service both our own short sale acquisitions, as well as for other Realtors and investors.

We NEVER charge a fee to the real estate agent or the homeowner, and value professionalism, integrity, and client relationships at all times.

We are associated with a full time legal firm to assist us in keeping in compliance with all changes to disclosures & regulations in the industry, and are a completely transparent, full-disclosure company.

Due to FTC-MARS regulations, we are no longer allowed to specify our approval success rate, but I can assure you it's exceedingly higher than many you've worked with. We are based in New England, but are able to handle files in many states throughout the country. If you have a submission, let us know, and we can work to ensure compliance with state laws.

If you are an investor or real estate agent looking to hire an aggressive negotiation firm to work with the lender on a short sale transaction, feel free to contact us at Info@ShortSaleMitigation.net for details on how to submit your file.

AGENTS - special commission rates may be offered, inquire when you submit your file!!

Managing Member

Real Estate industry

January 2010 – Present (3 years 11 months)

Rapid Property Relief was formed with its primary goal to assist distressed homeowners who are facing a pending foreclosure by their lending institution, with a focus in Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and Maine.

Homeowners DO have options when it comes to working with their lender, and our job is to show them their options, discuss what their best route is, and should it be in their best interests to sell their home, we work with them to purchase it ourselves, and utilizing our in-house negotiation and bank contacts, work to negotiate away deficiencies, promissory notes, and keep a foreclosure off their credit report.

With a network of attorneys, accountants, real estate agents and investors, we have the team and the resources to bring them out of a stressful situation, and to start fresh.

We also form partnerships with real estate agents in the area - if YOU are an agent that has a short sale listing, and can't get it sold, keep a buyer interested, or are afraid the bank will slash your commissions... bring your client to us.

We will treat them with the utmost professionalism, keep everyone in the loop, take over ALL negotiations and operations, AND... we guarantee the agent 8% commissions, split between our purchase and resale.
