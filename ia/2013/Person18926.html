Doug Burger's Summary

My entire career, after college, has been in the real estate business in Thurston County Washington. Over the years, since 1978, I was an independent real estate agent at both RE/MAX and a few locally based companies. Prior to retiring from working directly with customers, I achieved the number one position for RE/MAX for Washington State, their four State region, and the top 100 for volume in the United States. Starting in late 2007, I shifted to mentoring a small team of brokers and opened Burger Professionals, where I am still operating.

In 2009, I formed Micro-Social Communities, dba WEBaBUZZ, to formalize the handling and development of a neighborhood and community Web site project I had been working on since about 2004. We are currently close to launching the beta version, locally, to test out our product WEBaBUZZ/neighbors. We are super excited about the potential of this product, and will keep you all posted!

Personally, in my younger days, I spent most of my time getting speeding tickets on motorcycles. Three of the tickets were from the same cop! Anyway, did some motocross, hill climbs, enduros, etc. and had a great time. Later in life, with my two awesome boys and beautiful wife, we did quite a bit of traveling in our motorhome, jet skiing, snowmobiling, and riding quads.
