Erik Peterson's Summary

I have 7+ years of design experience in agency and corporate environments. I have worked from concept to completion on all forms of print media and marketing campaigns for regional and national clients.

I have followed and managed brand guidelines for Subway, ATT, HP and Commercial Metals Company and helped develop brands for regional clients.

My get your hands dirty attitude allows me to have a high learning ability and great work ethic for meeting deadlines or client expectations.

If I don't have copy I'll write it, If I don't have photography I'll find it, If I need it I'll get it, If I don't know it, I'll learn it.

I love laughing, enjoyment is the best nutrient for creativity.

Specialties: design, advertising design, branding, identities, marketing, campaigning, ideation, communication.
