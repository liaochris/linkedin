Michael Burton AIA NCARB's Summary

Mr. Burton’s family history as builders and architects in Baltimore dates back five generations. Structures of note are the Roland Park Water Tower, St Paul’s Church, (now “St. Francis Xavier Parish”) etc., and many of the homes in the Barclay Greenmount local historic district.

As a founding principal of Urban Design Group LLC, Mr. Burton manages the firm’s personnel to create innovative designs that meet the client’s budget, program, and schedule. He has 17 years of experience in historic preservation,Land-Use, Urban Planning, Commercial/Retail Architecture and Multi Family Architecture for projects located in Central Maryland Region. He has been engaged in multiple projects ranging from Historic house renovations to large scale mixed use projects seeking Historic Designation status, Rezoning, ect. Before founding Urban Design Group, LLC, Mr. Burton worked for several local firms including RTKL, CSD, and Design Collective Inc.

Specialties

Land-Use, Urban Planning, Historic Preservation, Adaptive Reuse, Multi-Family Architecture, Commercial/ Retail Architecture
