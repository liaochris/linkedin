  • Grocery Gateway Mobile App

    Team Members: Carla Alexander, Madeline Marovino, David Grguric, Chris Bryson, Amr Hiram, , Justin Howlett, Marc Faucher

    Implementation of award-winning Grocery Gateway mobile app, the first Canadian mobile app to support grocery delivery.

    Features include:

    - Select a delivery window prior to or upon completion of order creation
    - Seamless integration between mobile app and grocerygateway.com: start an order from app and finish on website, or vice versa.
    - Safe and secure credit card payment, including the introduction of a wallet concept allowing users to checkout without having to capture credit card data for every order.
