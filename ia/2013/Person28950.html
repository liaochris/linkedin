Damien ROUX's Summary

Currently working for both IGS Group and C&D, two privately owned education groups (22 schools, executive education, apprenticeship and career services located in 20 campuses in France and abroad), I am in charge of the global marketing & communications strategy, as well as digital strategy and associated services.

Previously, I was working for EMLYON Business School where I was successively in charge of corporate communications & branding, international business development before assuming the position of director of marketing, recruitment, and careers services for the school’s graduate programmes & alumni.

In addition, I was a consultant on digital strategies and social media and have spoken at conferences about branding and recruitment policy in the digital age.

Before entering the arena of business education, I held marketing, communications & sales positions in IT services companies.
