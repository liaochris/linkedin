Jeff Rhoden's Summary

My involvement in the Telecom industry began in 1995 when Scott Hansen (still one of my current business partners and lifelong friend) and I started a telecom brokerage and consulting firm, GCW Communication Consultants.

Initially we contracted with LD carriers to resell their services and later evolved the business to include contracts for operator services, cellular and local dial tone. In 2000, we partnered with Rio Communications, a regional ISP turned telecom company, to help build out their Southern Oregon network and customer base - our first venture into the emerging world of the competitive local exchange carrier (CLEC).

In 2003, having gained a passion for the industry, we joined together with third business partner, Chad Cota - also a lifelong friend - and purchased InfoStructure, a longstanding and reputable ISP in S.Oregon and began its transformation into a CLEC. Since then we have focused on building our client base and service offerings which now include traditional Local & LD phone service, Web & Conference Calling, Hosted PBX and SIP Trunking. InfoStructure also provides business internet services like DSL, Cable Modem, T-1, DS3, Fiber & Copper Ethernet, Private Line, MPLS, Web Hosting and Email.

Fast forward to July of 2013 and we finally completed the purchase of the company we once worked for - Rio Networks. The purchase doubles our employee size and nearly triples our customer base and revenue. Rio has had its struggles over the past 10 years but has a great network and we know our drive and vision will be what makes it flourish once again under our InfoStructure brand.

Additionally, Scott and I took an interest in the drive-thru specialty coffee industry after witnessing its huge growth in Southern Oregon. We formed a new company and purchased the franchise rights for the Human Bean Coffee Drive-Thru in Reno, NV.
