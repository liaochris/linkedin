Donna Quanrud's Summary

Donna Quanrud’s first priority when meeting a new client is to find out how she can best help them. She takes the time to understand their unique situations and establish a personal connection with them. The insight she gains helps her to more accurately address their wants and needs.

While she embraces technology as a tool to help her communicate with clients and market her listings, Donna also believes in the basics like answering her phone, listening carefully, and being responsive to her clients. Donna uses the power of 700+ local websites to market her listings and attract buyers – a proven marketing plan that is comprehensive and very effective.

Next she provides the information clients need to move through the process of buying or selling a home with little stress or uncertainty. Donna’s belief in educating her clients about today’s market leads her clients to making good decisions. When clients know what to expect along the way, it makes the entire transaction more enjoyable for everyone.

A true “people person,” Donna’s favorite part of being a REALTOR® is the opportunity to meet so many interesting people and help them fulfill their real estate goals. She understands that this isn’t like any other purchase – it’s home - where you live and raise your family.

Donna’s sense of empathy for the emotion that goes into buying or selling a home could be the reason why so many of her former clients consider her a friend and refer others to her.

Donna started her real estate career more than ten years ago. Along the way, she has owned and managed investment properties and also worked in the mortgage industry.

Specialties

Experienced in selling expired listings, luxury homes, foreclosures, short sales with the help of attorney negotiators, investment properties, listing and buying real estate in Minneapolis/ St. Paul and the surrounding suburbs.
