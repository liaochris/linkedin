"UpStream - Are you ready to turn your business around?"

June 2012 – Present (1 year 4 months)

Making changes (in a business) is tough! It’s like swimming or paddling against the current — UpStream.

This book is a great read for people who would like to see their business grow and feel that their organizations could and should be doing better. This book takes you through each step that will help your business grow; from sharpening listening skills, using today’s most effective leadership styles, creating a marketing strategy, eliminating workplace tension to using social media and business networking. There are also a few eye-opening stories that may cause you to wonder how a business like this can survive.
"UpStream" is for those who connect the dots, know how to listen and possess the desire to modify their behavior. “Are YOU ready for the challenge to turn YOUR business around?”
