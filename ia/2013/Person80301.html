Psychology

Advanced Studies, Psychology

2003 – 2013

I started studying psychology to gain a better understanding of my own behavior which led me to conduct many experiments and hacks on myself. It is an interesting field of study which continues to evolve and unfold as more and more people like myself unlock the mysteries of human behavior.

Hypnosis Motivation Institute

Certified, Hypnosis

2008 – 2009

I was cynical about the effectiveness of hypnosis as a tool for helping people break down and break through mental and emotional barriers. As I advanced through my studies I began to see some practical applications for the use of hypnosis as one of many tools for coaching my clients to reach that next level in their life and/or profession. One interesting conclusion that I didn't expect to discover is how frequently people ( all people ) go in and out of trance like states simply from external stimulus like radio, sunsets, background conversation, etc.

Sandler Sales Training

Trainers Training, Sales Psychology

This field of study blew my mind and anchored my soul. I learned more about the human condition and human behavior while receiving this education than in any other field of study to date. Sandler incorporated aspects of socratic questioning, NLP, hypnosis, gestalt therapy and psychology to unlock many of the mysteries of human interactions and relations. As a coach I use many aspects of my training in sales psychology to advance the selling - communicating - relating abilities of my clients.

Wordpress and HTML5 and CSS3

1998 – 2013

I'm fascinated and intrigued by web development and design and have used my education to build and maintain my own websites and blogs. I remain a student year after year to keep up with the evolution and advances taking place in web development and design.
