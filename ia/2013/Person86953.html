Mark Baer's Summary

I have been serving the greater Phoenix area real estate markets since 2007, I strive to make buying and selling real estate a positive experience. I have been a consistent million dollar producer and ranked #12 for sales in our Regional Branch for 2011. Starting with my clients needs first I like to find creative solutions to help clients achieve their goals.

I hold the designations, CNE-Negotiations Expert and CSSPE-Short Sale Property Expert. I'm also a member of both The West Valley Association of Realtors and National Association of Realtors.

With an entrepreneurial spirit, I enjoy using all forms of media and technology for both buyers and sellers.Staying up-to-date with technology, using such sites as You-Tube,Craigslist,Facebook,Twitter and exposing my listing to over 200 other real estate sites & search engines is an important part of my service.
