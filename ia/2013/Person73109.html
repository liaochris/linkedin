katie moffat's Summary

I help organisations use social media & PR more effectively, through training, strategy development, content creation and ongoing support. If you need help developing a coherent plan for your social media activity, training your team or understanding how to engage your online audiences, I can help.

I have been running training workshops on online PR & social media since 2008 and over the years have worked with many organisations across the public and private sector (examples: Econsultancy, the Arts Marketing Association, UKTI, Mediacontact.ie, plus a large number of PR, Marketing and digital agencies). I have also spoken at several leading conferences, on the topic of social media/future trends.

I write regularly, on social, tech & digital topics, for many websites and publications including .net magazine and creativebloq. More recently I have contributed, as a writer and editor, to the AMA's Culturehive website, providing new case studies and how-to articles on various digital marketing topics.

I am also a shareholder at Pyjama Drama, a fantastic company which provides drama, movement and creative play classes for babies, toddlers and children up to seven year olds. The entire programme is linked to the UK National Curriculum and supported by original music and songs. Currently 28 franchises across the UK.

Specialties: online PR, social media, SEO PR, copywriting, content creation, analytics, digital marketing
