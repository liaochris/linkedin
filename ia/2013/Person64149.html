Jim Ryan's Summary

Having personal experience in purchasing properties, it is only natural that Jim Ryan found his niche working with investors and buyers in the DFW Metroplex. He has formed a specialized team of realtors and support staff known as the "The TexInvestors Team" an extension of the "Ryan Real Estate Group". The team currently has members located across the DFW metroplex. This concept allows the team to cover a larger area for their buyers and sellers than most realtors can handle alone. The team members are personally mentored and trained by Jim Ryan, and are experienced professionals, who are leaders in working with discount properties such as, foreclosure and HUD homes.

The Ryan Real Estate Group and TexInvestors are a full service Real Estate brokerage under RE/MAX, that can handle all real estate buying and selling transactions

I have an extensive background in working with investment and discounted properties, both professionally and personally. I am able to utilize my excellent negotiating skills when purchasing such properties as; HUD, VA and bank forclosures, as well as any other property on the market.

I'm a Texas native having been born and raised here. My youth was filled with extra-curricular activities and organizations centered around school, church and athletics. I graduated from University of Texas with a degree in Marketing. Current hobbies of mine include spending time with the family, traveling, reading, and volunteering with various organizations. I believe my career in Real Estate is a hobby as well. I enjoy interacting with my clients and spending time with them to find the perfect investment for the client.
