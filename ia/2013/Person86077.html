Mario Fortunato's Summary

Wild Forest Products & MarDona Specialty Foods

Partner & CFO

Manage the day-to-day operation of the company in all aspects from sales to bookkeeping.

Oversee Sales staff of over one dozen independent sales contractors. Work closely with the production and shipping departments.

Design and market our products that consist of:
Truffle Infused Olive Oils, Flavored Olive Oils, Dried Porcini, Whole & Sliced truffles, truffle infused sea salts and truffle flavored honey.

Specialties

We specialize in manufacturing the finest White and Black Truffle Olive Oil all from Italy, as well as gourmet items such as Prickly Pear Grilling sauces, infused olive oil sprays. We are known for the Wild Forest Balsamic Condimenti and the MarDona Balsamic Glaze, used by many noted restaurants and chefs. We are also known for our private label production through our sister company The Olive Oil Factory. Visit our websites for more information.
