Joshua Wilson, CPA's Summary

Certified Public Accountant with experience working with small business owners. I help small business owners set up accounting systems to prepare financials necessary for tax return preparation, assistance with loans and budgets. I not only prepare all tax returns that a small business will need to file but also do tax planning to minimize the amount of tax due.

With 14 years of experience in the public accounting and tax industry, I enjoy helping my clients with all of their accounting and tax needs. I enjoy getting to know my clients interests and goals. My goal is to have my clients best interest in mind and to make sure that they are educated on all of their possible deductions, liabilities, and risks so that they can make the best financials decisions possible for their business and individual futures.

Specialties: Tax Planning, Tax Preparation (Business & Individual), Financial Statement Preparation (Compilations & Reviews), Payroll, IRS and GA tax problems, QuickBooks and Accounting Training.
