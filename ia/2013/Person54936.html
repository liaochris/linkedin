Iliyana Stareva's Projects

  • 'The Impact of Social Media on the PR Industry' - 11,000 words disseration

    • January 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Iliyana Stareva

    The research investigated how social networking and online platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn etc. have changed Public Relations.
    The study examined the dynamic of the PR discipline before and after the rise of social media and focused on how it affected the communications practice for organisational purposes. I also looked at the way modern PROs use social media tools in their normal day-to-day work activities as well as for personal reasons.
    For the research I conducted a survey as well as seven interviews with PR practitioners.

  • 'The Power of Social Media as a Communications Channel for Creating Business Sustainability Value: a Support Tool or Menace?' - 14,000 words dissertation

    • May 2013 to July 2013
    Team Members: Iliyana Stareva

    The thesis aimed to analyse and explain social media’s impact on sustainability communications and offer practical guidance for companies and professionals interested in exploring these new opportunities. I examined the power of social media as a communications channel for creating business sustainability value with the main research objective to discover whether social media can be a support tool or a menace for corporate sustainability. This included researching the importance and the current state of social media for sustainability communications and the opportunities and the risks of social media comms. As a result, I developed a best practice guide for utilising the power of social media and avoiding potential pitfalls in the form of an infographic.
