Marc Harrison's Summary

I'm a business guy who enjoys thinking, learning, listening, talking, helping, and having fun.

I am currently in the process of building a startup team at listium.com.


I spent the first 15 years of my working life in the corporate world, with the majority of that time in the funds management business with JBWere Goldman Sachs. I managed a team of analysts and portfolio managers, and together we managed a couple of billion dollars invested in the Australian share market for institutional and retail investors.

The last few years I have spent my time helping small business owners with finance, marketing and business management related issues.

More recently I have focused on the tech startup space which I love. I have formally and informally advised several startups, am a director to one funded startup, and regularly mentor at startup events and programs.

I often help startups with finance related issues, including forecasting and fundraising, and increasingly have focused more time on understanding marketing and sales as well as helping startups improve the way they pitch to investors and customers.

In a normal week I spend my time between York Butter Factory in Melbourne, Inspire9 in Richmond, and time at home with my wonderful 2 year old daughter.

I also spend a lot of time reading (http://www.goodreads.com/marcharrison) and enjoy kitesurfing, stand-up paddle boarding, and watching AFL team Richmond play each weekend.
