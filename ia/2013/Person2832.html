Anita Kilkenny's Summary

You know how running your own business can be stressful and time consuming? Don't spend valuable time on tasks that you can outsource, such as Social Media and Email Marketing. Make time to focus on your own business requirements and have the freedom to grow your own client base.

Enjoy the flexibility of having your own Virtual PA. You can have peace of mind that your work is being done by an accredited office professional with many years experience in the secretarial field - over 25 years to be exact and over 14 as a business owner.

AKA Virtual PA is your one-stop-shop for all of your secretarial and administrative requirements, enabling you to save time and money and receive 100% productivity, 100% of the time.

Specialties: Virtual solutions for Associations (Secretariat), Event Co-ordination, Social Media Support and Set Up ... for anyone who needs remote assistance without the stress of employing someone full or part time.
