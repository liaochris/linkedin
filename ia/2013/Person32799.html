David Zook's Summary

I believe that a simple message of grace and truth will radically change your life and your city for the better.

I want to help you get there because it will bring peace, purpose, and joy to your life.

Lived out, this message will strengthen your marriage, make you a better steward of your time, talent, and treasure, bring lasting direction and purpose to your life, and you will impact the lives of many.

Consider hanging out with me for a while, meet some like-minded people, and let me help you live out this timeless message.

For those of you who live in the Phoenix area, I teach Christians how to live out the Gospel at work without being obnoxious or judgmental. Groups have formed in the government, media, the arts, and public education. For the past three years I have been teaching these principles to Christian groups at Intel, American Express, the State Capitol, and some public schools.

I have started care, prayer, share in a local public school with the faculty and staff and are looking to expand them to three other campuses this fall.

I have also helped create a coalition of eight local neighborhood churches to serve the nine schools in the Creighton School District and our neighbors. This coalition has helped clean up and spruce up seven of the schools as well as Camelback High. We have also worked with the city of Phoenix to help clean up homes of code violators in our area. We have also brought together all eight congregations on two separate occasions to pray together and worship together.

I also connect Phoenicians to the deep truth which will bring them the peace, purpose, and joy that they are seeking in some really great venues.

Click on the visio dei link above to discover more about this unique and exciting ministry.

Specialties: church strategy and development, discipleship, personal coaching, evangelism, public speaking, mentoring, staff training, community development
