Christina Motley's Summary

Christina is a strategic, integrated marketer, compelling content developer and published author. Throughout her career, she has worked with agencies, small businesses and Fortune 500 companies, leaving a lasting footprint by building brand loyalty, fostering customer engagement and driving new business. She has won more than 200 awards for her creativity, leadership, copywriting and client results.

Today, Christina bridges the gap for companies and agencies with limited resources, tight deadlines and work overload. For the third time she’s established her own business – Christina Motley, llc – where she serves as a Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) for hire.

She holds a position on the Board of Advisors for Career Pro, Inc., and from 2012-13 she served the Triangle Chapter of the American Marketing Association (AMA) as the VP of Marketing and Communications. Christina is also a member of the Alumni Advisory Council for Virginia Tech's Department of Communication, where she taught courses, as well as earned a dual degree in English and Communications - Journalism.

► Company Information:

Christina Motley, llc delivers on point marketing strategy when and where clients need it. As a strategic partner, Christina Motley, llc serves as a CMO-for-hire, providing C-level counsel to executives, developing marketing strategies and offering interim project help as needed.

The company partners with agencies, advertising/marketing firms and public relations companies, allowing them to accept new business, enhance their capabilities and support their staff to strike a balance between client needs, tight deadlines and work loads. Christina Motley, llc also provides comprehensive strategic, integrated marketing and communication services and implementation for private-equity / venture capital-backed firms and small to medium businesses (SMBs).
