Jane Baxter Lynn's Summary

Jane Baxter Lynn is an experienced strategic planner, facilitator and communicator with extensive international experience across a broad range of activities and businesses. In particular, she has an in-depth knowledge of the Travel & Tourism, Wine, Green Building/Sustainability, franchising and non-profit sectors. Jane has developed a reputation for providing employers and clients with effective strategic counsel; developing results-oriented solutions, and bringing together diverse groups to achieve common goals. She has lived and worked in Europe, South Africa and the United States, where she now resides. Her work has included: working with organizations to develop and implement strategic plans; establishing sustainable structures for non-profit organizations; building and raising awareness for consumer brands; initiating successful internal and external communications campaigns, and handling crisis and issues management. A past National Board member of the Association for Women in Communications, Jane continues to serve on AWC's National Marketing & Communications committee, as well as provides mentoring services for the AWC Austin Chapter. She also serves on the Advisory Councils of the U.S. Green Building Council's Central Texas-Balcones Chapter and of the Wine & Food Foundation of Texas. Her goal is to help people and organizations to clearly identify who they are and the desired impact/outcomes they want to have. Then to work with them to have the information and strategy necessary to move forward in an efficient, effective, impactful and sustainable way While the principles she uses are applicable generally, her main areas of experience are in the Travel & Tourism, Wine, Green Building and Sustainability fields.

Specialties

Specializes in strategic planning, facilitation, corporate and marketing communications, and non-profit management. Her focus is on organizational management, clarity of positioning and messaging, and alliance building and partnerships within the context of clients’ strategic plans.
