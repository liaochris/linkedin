AIIM EduSys Pvt. Ltd.'s Experience

Training Academy

AIIM EduSys Pvt. Ltd.

Educational Institution; 11-50 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry

June 2010 – Present (3 years 2 months)

Managed by a team of professionals with years of rich experience in the corporate world, AIIM EduSys Pvt. Ltd. (AIIM) is dedicated to promoting high standards of performance in the industry.

As a training academy, our objective is to enhance through our career programs the knowledge, skills and competence of our trainees and prepare them for the numerous career opportunities in the industry. We also conduct Executive Programs for the benefit of management students who wish to learn more about specific domains. Our endeavour will always be to equip our students better to take up higher responsibilities in the roles they commit to.

As an industry service provider, we have activities that range from Corporate Training Programs, Training Process Outsourcing to Consultancy Services to be able to serve corporates.

The management team is supported by a robust Advisory Board, whose members are renowned names from the corporate world and education fields. With their technical and professional expertise combined with valuable experience, they have made significant contributions in enriching our offerings.

Check out more about us on our website www.aiim.in
