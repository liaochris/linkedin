Tony Fiorino's Summary

I provide consulting services to biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies, investors in those companies, and others requiring insight or expertise related to the biopharmaceutical industry. My unique perspective as been shaped by medical and scientific education and training at top academic institutions, analytical and financial tools and an investor’s perspective earned in a decade spent on Wall Street, and tangible operational know-how gained by running a clinical-stage biotechnology start-up company for more than three years. Engagements have including due diligence on biopharmaceutical assets and companies, outlicensing of clinical stage assets, clinical development planning, and expert witness projects.

Among my achievements are founding a biotechnology start-up, completing IND-enabling preclinical studies for a biologic agent, writing several phase 1 and 2 clinical trial protocols, filing a pair of INDs, successfully running two clinical trials enrolling 50 gout patients, and finally serlling the phase 2-ready asset to 3SBio. As an investor, I have managed long-only and long-short portfolios of biotechnology and pharmaceuticals stocks ranging from $25 to $125 million, with a focus on companies with upcoming clinical, regulatory and commercial catalysts. I have published extensively and have twice been invited to make presentations on drug development to the FDA.

Specialties: Drug development, in-depth due diligence on drugs and portfolios of drugs at all stages of development, market and financial modelling, scientific and medical writing, business development, biotechnology and pharmaceutical investing, portfolio management, equity research
