Dave McMurtry's Experience

Senior Vice President, Strategy

Habitat for Humanity International

Nonprofit; 501-1000 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

December 2009 – Present (3 years 9 months) Atlanta GA, Bangkok Thailand, San Jose CR, Pretoria SA, Bratislava SK

Habitat is the 6th largest private homebuilder in the US and the leading Shelter-focused NGO in over 80 countries. Its volunteers and employees have built over 600,000 houses providing more than 3 million people in 3,000 communities with safe, decent, affordable shelter. Habitat is not a giveaway program. In addition to a down payment and monthly mortgage payments, homeowners invest hundreds of hours of their own labor (sweat equity) into building their Habitat house and the houses of others. Habitat houses are sold to partner families at no profit and financed with affordable loans and the homeowners’ monthly mortgage payments are used to build still more Habitat houses.

Dave's Responsibilities:

* Executive leadership of the Strategic Realization Team responsible for the creation, implementation, and monitoring of the 5-year Strategic Plan for the $1.5B operation in 90+ countries
* Executive leadership of Global Village, a $10M+ per year program sending 10K+ volunteers, from North America and Europe to 50+ developing countries, as fundraisers and advocates for Habitat and poverty shelter issues
* Executive leadership of Habitat's initiative to create an online crowdsourcing platform of donors, volunteers, and advocates
