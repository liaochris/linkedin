Ben Summers' Summary

A creative UX and visual designer with over 10 years of agency and client-side digital experience, I specialise in creating engaging, intuitive user experiences across multiple devices and online touch points.

My skills range from pitching and producing visual designs for web and mobile device interfaces through to online brand development, long-term client liaison and off-site team management. I’m just as comfortable producing concept sketches and interaction prototypes as I am defining the guidelines for large, responsive web builds with complex data requirements.

I have experience of both Agile (Scrum, Kanban) and process-driven working environments within the telecoms, finance, travel and retail sectors, and am equally at ease leading work independently or taking direction as part of a larger team.
