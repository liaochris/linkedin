Scottsdale, AZ-based Maintenance Made Simple (MMS) is an international franchise organization specializing in residential and commercial property maintenance services. Handling everything from minor repairs and maintenance to property improvements, MMS fills an important gap between simple handyman services and behemoth remodeling companies. MMS develops long-term relationships with customers to ensure that their ongoing property upkeep is handled professionally and reliably. By utilizing MMS, clients receive peace of mind that allows them to focus on more important things – such as being more productive or simply relaxing and enjoying life. Named by Entrepreneur Magazine as one of the Top 50 Franchises for 2007, Maintenance Made Simple operates in more than forty locations throughout the United States, Canada, and the Middle East.
