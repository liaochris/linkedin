Julie Sackman's Summary

Online Retailer of Custom, Handcrafted Western style gifts, Sterling Silver Jewelry, Handmade Bits, Ropes, Classic Women's Apparel, Cowboy Hats, Caps, T-Shirts & Sweatshirts, Hand Tooled Leather Journals and Handbags & More!

Courage, commitment, passion and dedication define a special attitude, a mental firmness, a quality and strength that is “spirit”, that is cowgirl. We believe there is a promise that lies within each of us to find that spirit and live with passion, whether you are sitting at a desk in the city or driving cattle on the range.

Horsemanship and fly-fishing are so seemingly different and yet so very much alike. Both are detailed, yet vast, simple yet complicated. The key to each is timing, feel communication, and technique. Success in each is defined individually.

Cowgirl Up & Fly ™ brings them together for you in a weekend get-away. Our Women’s Ride & Fly program is geared for the beginner fishermen and the seasoned trail rider. Now you can experience both and see the magic in each. Small groups makes our Ride & Fly program a relaxing and inspiring weekend. Bring your horse and yourself and we’ll take care of the rest.
• Peaceful camps tucked away in the trees yet steps away from high mountain lakes,streams and rivers
• The ultimate in horse camping & trail- riding set against the beauty of different Northern California locations
• Learn the basics of Fly Fishing ~ the knots ~the cast ~the bugs ~the catch
• Gourmet meals, evening campfires
• Easy going and relaxed setting
• Bring your horse and yourself, we’ll take care of the rest!
