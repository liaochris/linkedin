Donna Bigda's Summary

Donna has been a life long resident of the New Haven area and a successful full-time REALTOR® since 1997. She has earned her Accredited Buyer Representative, Certified Distressed Property Expert, Senior Real Estate Specialist, Accredited Staging Professional Realtor® and e-PRO certifications and is a founding member of The Institute For Luxury Home Marketing. Together with the combination of Donna's knowledge of real estate transactions and her insight into our area's real estate market along with her understanding of information technology and the Internet provides you with an outstanding level of service. This unique blending of diverse talents sets her apart from others.

As your Personal Real Estate Consultant For Life Donna strives to deliver first-class service to her clients using 100% of her efforts to meet their real estate needs both during and after the transaction. The finest compliment she can receive is when past clients keep coming back and when they continue to refer new buyers and sellers to her. Donna is interested in building strong, lasting lifelong relationships, one person at a time by making business decisions based on the long-term benefits of her clients, not based on "making the sale....starting with you.
