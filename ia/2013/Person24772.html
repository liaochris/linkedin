Chuck Brown's Summary

I left my full-time gig (at a video production company) in 2001 as a result of an economic downturn, after amassing ten wonderful years there. However, the decision to do so was the best move I've ever made. I've been set free to pursue other ideas and passions, and I take great joy in just about everything I do these days. My current responsibilities include providing voiceover services to well over 100 clients, designing web sites for my own interest and creating music for my own creative fulfillment. I currently have several albums of my original music for sale (HealingPiano.com), have a web site for children built around my readings of freely downloadable audio stories (LightUpYourBrain.com), and am the host of a daily Bible-reading podcast (HideYourWord.com).

Chuck Brown's Experience

Professional Voiceover Talent

Chuck Brown

July 2001 – Present (12 years 3 months)

I have decades of experience as a professional voice talent, and work daily for clients located in the U.S. and worldwide. I maintain a basic home studio where I handle the bulk of my voiceover work, but I also have local connections with studios equipped with ISDN for the occasion when that need arises. Most projects can be completed within 24 hours.

I'm also happy to read a short section of your script and send it to you, to help you determine whether my delivery is a good fit for your project.

Specialties: My particular speciality is voiceover for e-learning/training, but am also well-versed in broadcast commercials, telephony (on-hold and voice mail), web audio, corporate image voice over, select audio book projects and the occasional character voice.
