Auke van den Hout's Experience

Founder

Consentu

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry

September 2012 – Present (11 months)

Consentu offers solutions for online privacy management. Focussed on publishers, advertisers and online shops. Privacy solutions characterised by an integral approach of commercial interests, changing consumer perceptions and legislation and new technology.

Founder

Adatus

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

2010 – Present (3 years)

Adatus is a data exchange for exclusive anonymous online profiles. Classified in geodemographic information, interest and purchase intent. Providers of data generate additional revenue streams without any investment. Advertisers, media agencies, publishers and adnetworks can buy real time audiences. And the consumer gets to see relevant ads.

Board Member

IAB Netherlands

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry

2010 – Present (3 years)

The Internet Advertising Bureau (IAB) is the trade association for online and mobile advertising. With over 170 Dutch member companies, IAB promotes growth of these channels, develops best practices for advertisers, agencies and publishers, and aims to put digital advertising high on the agenda of every marketer in the Netherlands.
