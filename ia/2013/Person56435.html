Jack Chapple's Summary

I am an experienced, results-oriented web developer and designer with over 10 years’ experience. I build bespoke web software solutions, taking a modular approach with development to ensure a cohesive product is supplied that is scalable, efficient and easily maintainable.

I possess a strong programming and development background – familiar with all aspects of overseeing and developing projects, including initial design, programming, implementation and user support. I encourage the integration of existing open software and services through the use of API’s when appropriate.

I have extensive experience in online / social marketing, e-commerce and analytics. I employ a creative approach, combined with strategic thinking to provide commercially focused, conversion optimised web and mobile applications. I am passionate about harnessing the power of the social web to inspire meaningful communication between brand and consumer, driving customer acquisition / retention and increasing brand advocacy.

I have worked with sole traders, SME's, to the largest of brands with turnovers in excess of £100million.

I am also an experienced and versatile composer specialising in music for TV, film and video games. I have recently worked to provide the score for ITV's "Survival with Ray Mears" and currently have music being used in ITV's “Tales From The Wild” series.

I am able to digitally produce world class quality recordings from my studio, including fully orchestral cues when desired. I strive to produce music that is in keeping with contemporary industry trends. I focus on production values to ensure that I deliver recordings that exceed expectations. I use the latest sample libraries and music production software, combined with aural intuition to achieve this.

I am a panel endorsed member of the Screened Music Network - celebrating excellence in British film & television composition.
