Product Manager

KISSmetrics

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry

March 2012 – June 2013 (1 year 4 months) San Francisco Bay Area

I helped KISSmetrics build a great customer-focused product using Lean principles and a passion for customer happiness.

During my time running product we:
- Rebuilt our Live feature to be the best debugging and real time activity view on the analytics market.
- Overhauled our People Search and Person details feature, the core of the KISSmetrics, people-driven tracking system.
- Improved our onboarding conversion rate by 68% through many A/B testing experiments.
- Developed and handed off to sales a Power Report upsell program, taking customers from small plans (<$200/mo) to enterprise level plans ($499+).
- Overhauled our Daily Metrics Email based on customer feedback, which improved adoption within customer teams and open rates.
- Helped prototype our mobile app, MyAnalytics, which is currently still in limited release.
- Led development of a SaaS Revenue Report to help companies calculate the most important metrics for a SaaS business (churn, MRR, LTV, etc), which is also in limited release.

I evolved our product processes as we went from 3 engineers to over 10 and formed and led a cross functional team that wrote our company values in the Summer of 2012.

Most importantly, I built a customer driven culture throughout the product team and company through development and utilization of new and existing channels to connect with customers, teaching team members customer development tactics, and using data and the voice of the customer at every opportunity.
