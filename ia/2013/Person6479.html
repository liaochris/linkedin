Andrea Rigon's Summary

I focus on digital marketing, defining and executing the online strategy of my company.

My mission? Keep large and small scale digital projects on time and on budget, with a smile.

Since January 2013 I'm Digital Marketing Manager in EOS Solutions, Italian leader in business software solutions based on Microsoft Dynamics ERP and CRM. In the previous five years I was in Microsoft as Online Marketing Manager in the Small & Medium Business division. In my previous career stages I managed various web projects, including the municipialities of Milan and Lecco (web portal), Banca Popolare di Milano and BNL (internet banking), Henkel (brand community), SAP (corporate site), Vodafone (web portal). I also managed web and communication in a tlc company.
