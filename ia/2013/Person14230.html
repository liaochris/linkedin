Byungyol Chun, MD, FACOG's Summary

Dr. Bill Chun is a board-certified OB/GYN with a private practice in Lawrence, Massachusetts. He is a leading provider of robotic surgery in New England, and performs minimally invasive gynecologic surgery at several hospitals in Massachusetts and New Hampshire. A prominent figure in the community, he has written articles for several regional newspapers and appeared on local radio programs. In 2012 he published Don't Give a Damn: How to Cope with the Fears, Frustrations, and Challenges of Daily Life, in which he shares his personal philosophy for living a more productive and fulfilled life.

Dr. Chun maintains a medical profile site and blog at www.billchunmd.com where he
provides information on his medical practice, the benefits of robotic surgery, and his weight loss programs. He blogs about a wide variety of personal and social issues and offers advice on navigating the world of medical care. In addition to his website, Dr. Chun can be found on twitter where he has a large following.

An experienced entrepreneur, Dr. Chun is constantly looking for new and better ways of serving the needs of women. All of his ventures are informed by a holistic approach to healthcare and a belief that women are their own best health advocates.
