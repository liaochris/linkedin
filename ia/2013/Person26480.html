leslie moore's Summary

As an Interior Design Consultant for Color and Light Interiors I specialize in architectural color consulting for interiors and exteriors, redesign, home makeovers, custom window treatments, staging and visual merchandising for residences and businesses.

One to two hour makeovers to complete remodel projects are in my expertise for commercial and residential design projects.

Branding with color is another area I specialize in. I will help you create the feeling, mood and image you want for your home or business with color.

USA Made Wholesale is a business I began in 2010 bringing USA Made artisan and unique culinary, bath & body, jewelry and artists work to the retail market. Currently have active sales representation in Texas and Northern California.
Consult with artists helping them develop a wholesale line featuring the beauty and uniqueness of their art so it is successful in the retail market.

Specialties: Color consulting, space planning, floorplan layout, redesign, branding, marketing, sales, event coordination, wholesale consulting, visual merchandising, and interior design.
