Darrell Cole's Summary

Darrell Cole is a 23 year “veteran of the trenches”. He came up in the Commercial/ Industrial Mechanical Industry beginning his Apprenticeship in the early 90’s, returning to the Construction Industry, where he started while in high school after a 3/3 contract in the U.S. Army. Having acquired a valuable field perception, he has the fortunate insight for conveying in layman terms, the technical complexities of Plumbing and HVAC Systems, in detailing deficiencies and problems associated with them. This results in him becoming a solution catalyst for the customer. He also has a keen understanding of relevant building codes.
Darrell's true passions are helping others; B2B Networking, Sales, Marketing and Business Development but he is well rounded with additional experience in Customer Service; Quality Control, Project Management, Purchasing, Estimating, Contract Negotiation, Company Leadership, Team Building, Safety Training/ Coordinating, and in addressing HR challenges.

Goals: LEED-Associate Professional, Existing Buildings Operations & Maintenance (AP- EBOM) persuit.

Specialties: Commercial & Industrial (MEP) Service Solutions; Special Projects, Performance Contracting, Preventive Maintenance Programs, always looking for Hot Water, Conditioned Air and Electrical Power Opportunities.
