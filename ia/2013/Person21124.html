Carlos Victor Costa's Summary

A multicultural, result-oriented professional, with over 20 year´s experience spanning the full corporate communications and marketing communications spectrum, with leadership positions in large corporations (Dell, Petrobras, Praxair) in complex competitive environments. Also, sound experiences in PR and marketing agencies and communications consultancies (Territorio creativo, Ogivly&Mather, Llorente&Cuenca, Reputation Institute) as account director and planner for important B2C and B2c accounts.

Core competencies in marketing and corporate communications, innovative strategy formulation, reputation management, crisis communication, digital strategy, brand positioning, team building, and coaching, with relevant experiences both in the Business-to-Business and the Business-to-Consumer segments.

A pioneer on the development of the Internet as a marketing and communication medium, working with large-scale online programs since 1996, and since 2008 working with social media.

Portuguese and Brazilian national, living in Madrid since 2007.

Guest speaker in many executive seminars, I also started an academic career, as lecturer for two of the best Brazilian business schools (ESPM, and FGV), and for two business schools in Madrid, Spain (ESIC-Business and Marketing School and EOI-Escuela de Negocios). Main subjects: online reputation management, crisis management, social media strategy, crisis and risk management, corporate communications, marketing communications, branding.

Blogger since 2008. Current blogger http://www.carlosvictorcosta.com on online reputation and crisis management on the Internet.

Specialties: Marketing Strategy, Corporate Reputation, Crisis Management, Integrated Marketing Communications, Social Corporate Responsability, Online Communications, Social Media Marketing, Internal Communications, Brand Management.
