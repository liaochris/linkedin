Jeffrey LeFevre's Summary

Jeffrey LeFevre is a PR and marketing professional with experience working among the industry’s top-ranked global communications networks, including FTI Consulting, MSLGROUP (Publicis Groupe), GolinHarris (Interpublic Group) and Edelman. He specializes in professional services marketing and business development, with a niche background in corporate marketing, thought leadership, insights creation, graphic layout/design, content creation and digital strategy.

Prior to joining FTI Consulting as Americas marketing coordinator of the firm's Strategic Communications segment, Jeffrey was a business development coordinator at the top-five ranked, global communications and engagement agency, MSLGROUP. As part of the MSLGROUP Midwest region, he partnered with the leadership team and global network on new business and marketing for MSL Chicago and MSL Detroit, and had served as an interim department lead there. A strong growth rate and top-tier client development has earned the Midwest region recognition across the company’s network, contributing to a global agency growth of 13.4 percent year over year and global ranking of four (The Holmes Report 2012).

In addition, Jeffrey has served as a project manager and consultant under the name, @jeffreyplefevre Consulting, working with small businesses and entrepreneurs on PR, marketing and digital strategy.

Jeffrey holds a B.A. with Honors in advertising and public relations from Loyola University Chicago, with minors in marketing and bioethics, and sits as a board member on the university’s GOLD alumni board. He also remains active on the PRSA Chicago board of directors and as a co-chair of the communications committee.
