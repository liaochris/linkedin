Michael Maranda's Summary

Michael consults on movement building and information strategies for the social benefit sector and engages in capacity building for the Commons. As co-founder of the Chicago Digital Access Alliance he architected the 10 Principles for Digital Excellence, a holistic perspective on Digital Inclusion and Community Building. Michael is past president of the Association for Community Networking and CTCNet Chicago.

Personal mission: "remake fields with tools and provisioned spaces; open the path to a more fluid, functional and open society; design tools and services that integrate the field - making us visible to each other in value and success."

Specialties

Movement strategies, field-building, capacity-building.
Non-profit start-up, governance.
Community Technology Planning.
