Meghan McFadyen's Summary

Meghan is currently seeking full time work in the educational field.

Meghan earned her California multiple subjects K-8th grade teaching credential at CalState TEACH. She has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Liberal Studies: Teaching Pathway with an emphasis in Visual and Public Art from California State University, Monterey Bay. She has over ten years of experience working with children and school aged youth from an array of backgrounds through volunteer experience, student teaching and past positions.

Meghan recently completed her student teaching at Hubert H. Bancroft Elementary School in the Sacramento City Unified School District during the 2012/2013 school year, where she taught both 2nd and 5th grade. While teaching 5th grade, Meghan created and implemented an integrated unit on public art. Students designed and painted a class mural on the front wall of their classroom as apart of the unit. The project was such a success that Meghan was asked to come back to teach the same unit and several other teachers have decided to implement their own class mural projects in the coming school year. She has volunteered for the Sacramento County of Education in a 4th through 6th special education classroom as well as in 2nd grade classroom at Noralto Elementary School in the Twin Rivers School District.

Besides her passion for education, Meghan loves being apart of the growing Sacramento community. She enjoys yoga, staying up on the latest social media trends, TED talks, attending local events, writing, working with youth outside of the classroom, fashion, and public art.

In addition to her work in education, Meghan has seven years of experience in fast-paced business and sales oriented environments. Her specific areas of expertise include social media marketing, event planning, special project management, and executive level support.
