Michael Koski's Summary

Currently working with health care facilities to quickly, discretely and effectively exclude bats from their buildings.

I use LinkedIn to network and offer services in these three areas:

My Company - GET BATS OUT

We offer commercial and residential bat exclusion along with consultation on reducing tenant risk and liability. We have experience negotiating with public health departments, the Joint Commission and other regulatory bodies when necessary.

My Company - TECHNICAL LINGUISTICS

We have developed an app to speed up the check in and triage process for non-English speaking patients and improve Medicare/Medicaid customer satisfaction survey results.

THE MODIFIED ATKINS DIET FOR SEIZURES

After an incredibly successful experience involving the modified atkins diet, Johns Hopkins and my daughter's seizures, I decided to write a book on the subject. I also maintain the website and forum www.AtkinsForSeizures.com.

I have a twofold mission in regard to this cause.

1) Provide information and resources for people who are managing epilepsy with diet.

2) Get the word out as far and wide as possible that it is possible to manage seizures with diet. If you are in the medical field, a dietician, a nutritionist, an epileptic, or the care giver of someone with epilepsy please connect with me.

I am an author, father, and happy husband. My goal in life is to never stop learning and teaching. I have a passion for both. I gain tremendous satisfaction on a personal, family, spiritual and business level from these activities. I hope to learn and grow from my connection to you.

Specialties: commercial bat exclusion, public speaking, writing, diet for seizure management
