Dustin Pearlman's Summary

Cinematography reel and resume can be viewed on my website:
http://dustinpearlman.com/

I've earned a reputation for my bold compositions and expressive lighting while crafting short films, commercials, and music videos. Based in Los Angeles, I arrive to set with a meticulous eye for detail and a passion for filmmaking. With five features under my belt, long form narrative is my forum of choice. The challenges of independent film keeps me on my toes and the creative solutions that must be made on the fly are what keeps me sharp and excited to come back to work. Aside from my dedication to cinema, I am an outdoor enthusiast and avid environmentalist.

As a freelance cinematographer, I'm constantly looking for the next script to bring to life through the medium of film. No matter the budget or format, please don't hesitate to contact me about your upcoming project.

I also own a fully loaded Canon 5D Mk3 camera package. Rental details are here: http://dustinpearlman.com/gear/

Specialties: Lighting, Framing/Composition, Group/Crew Leadership, Hand Held Operating, Focus Pulling while Operating, 3D Cinematography
