Joy Phillips' Summary

I find great joy in helping smart n’ savvy women reconnect to their passion, purpose, and highest potential. And I’d love to help YOU make your dream a reality by guiding you in a fascinating journey through the world of archetypal patterns where you will discover the 12 archetypes that make up who YOU are at the deepest level and why you feel, think, act and react the way you do.

The overwhelming feeling of being stuck in frustration, dissatisfaction and restlessness, that you experience a little bit more with each passing day, is your Soul’s way of gently nudging you to wake up — to free yourself from the self-imposed barriers and thoughts that bind you so that you can become everything you know you were born to be.

Once you understand and embrace the light and shadow sides of your archetypes, you will become unstoppable in pursuing and living your dream. Having this knowledge makes life flow so much easier!

As a “creative techie” with a passion for helping you awaken your entrepreneurial bliss, I go beyond the traditional coaching relationship in offering both life and business solutions, including private coaching sessions, self-guided programs, and group workshops. I also offer creative virtual services + web design to help you brand your business, showcase your expertise and attract YOUR ideal clients.

BIO
Joy Phillips is the founder of Awaken Bliss Center for Inspired Living, Archetypal Consultant, Expert in Business Branding & Design, Creator and Facilitator of the Adventures in Enlightenment Journaling Book Club for Women, and contributing author to A Cup of Comfort for Parents of Children with Special Needs. Before realizing her dream as a solopreneur, Joy worked 20+ years at the executive level in corporate marketing, strategic business planning, and event planning. She holds a Masters in Metaphysical Science and has earned a PhD specializing in Holistic Life Counseling.
