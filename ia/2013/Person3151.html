Alan Krawitz's Summary

Hybrid, veteran independent NYC journalist and communications specialist.


Journalism experience highlights:
More than 15 years experience in writing/reporting/editing/multimedia for variety of media outlets including Newsday, NY Daily News, NY Press as well as magazines and numerous regional, local weekly and hyperlocal outlets in the NY metropolitan area. I'm a strong interviewer and tenacious reporter capable of cultivating sources quickly and efficiently and uncovering breaking news.

Specialties include all types of features (politics, education, immigration, transportation, land-use/zoning, cops/courts), business and community profiles, spot hard news coverage and longer, more investigative-type pieces.

Communications work: Industries include nonprofits (Tourette Syndrome Assoc., United Spinal Assoc., Paraprofessional Healthcare Inst.) consumer (Nabisco, Nissan, Huffy, American Plastics Council, Sporting Goods Manufacturers Assoc., Guidance Group educational publishers), finance/tech (Paragon Federal Credit Union, Arbitron, IEEE, SSL, Avid, Bexel, Chyron) public sector (NYPD, Port Washington School Dist., Manhasset School Dist.)

Adept at maximizing PR opportunities via traditional media relations outreach, press kit development from releases and advisories to bios/backgrounders, executive speeches, marketing/copywriting/collateral materials, social media campaigns, media training, blog/web copy and public affairs work.

Specialties: Freelance journalist whose work has appeared in a variety of media outlets including Newsday, New York Daily News, New York Press, Ourtown, Downtown, West Side Spirit, Queens Chronicle, Times-Ledger, AM New York and others.

•Creative and highly versatile writer/editor adept at producing jargon-free, press materials/correspondence.

•Ability to interpret complex information and relate to lay audiences.

•Strong knowledge of NYC media, government, community affairs.

Alan Krawitz's Projects

  • Operation Reachout Military Suicide Prevention App

    • January 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Alan Krawitz, John T. Barlowe, MBA, Dr. Lawrence Shapiro

    Operation Reachout is a private-sector effort to help stem the rising number of suicides by active duty service members and veterans of conflicts including Iraq and Afghanistan.

    The effort is centered around a free smartphone app that connects depressed or suicidal service members (or members of their families) with immediate help from counselors who are specially trained to deal with emotional problems such as trauma, PTSD and other combat-related issues.

    My involvement with Operation Reachout has been media relations and public affairs outreach. To date, news of the app has been carried widely by traditional news organizations including Newsday, AP and the Wall Street Journal as well as through social media.
