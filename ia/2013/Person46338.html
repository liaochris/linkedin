Ian Howard's Summary

As President of FISST.org, GofFederationOfAmerica.com, and IH Enterprises, LLC, produced Mattech in Miami Beach, FL – a material handling, logistics trade show. For seven years prior, was President of National Mall Media, Inc. producing Health, Home & Family Expos in over 250 malls nationwide. Career responsibilities included overseeing all sales, marketing and office and contractor management, establishing and implementing a budget successfully, and ensuring profitable results.

In my personal time, spent time writing two books, am a fitness enthusiast, was an actor and consultant. My outlook on life is positive and he believes if you make a commitment every day to be the best person you can be, you’ll get the most out of life that you can.
