Mike McAnally's Summary

I am Co-Founder and Partner at NordicClick Interactive which was founded in 2006 by 3 industry veterans. NordicClick is a full service Digital Marketing firm located in Excelsior, MN with our offices on beautiful Lake Minnetonka.

We help companies build their online brand and presence by creating engaging campaigns that drive and convert traffic.

In 1996 I jumped into the first gold rush of Online Marketing and developed a passion for driving traffic including Paid, Organic (SEO) Pay for Performance and Local search. I have experience working with Sports & Fitness, Retail and Software companies.

Specialties: Search Engine Marketing (SEO & PPC), E-mail Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, Web Strategy, Local Search Management, E-Business Management, Landing Page Optimization, Mobile Technologies, Linking Strategies, Social Media Optimization (SMO), Web Metrics & Analytics, Lead Generation & Nurturing
