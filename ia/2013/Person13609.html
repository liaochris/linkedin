Jonathan Duarte's Summary

  • Internet Recruiting / Job Applicant - Source of Hire Protocol Author

    • Jonathan Duarte
    • January 4, 2004
    Authors: Jonathan Duarte

    In 2003, the tracking and reporting of job applicant sources was almost non-existent.
    Most employers were accepting resumes via a single email address, therefore loosing any type of advertising tracking abilities.

    As one of the first internet employment classifieds ad networks, I built the first email based response tracking system, such that each job posting was given a unique alias email address for resume applications, per job board, thus allowing tracking on a per job board, per job posting basis.

    Own tracking system allow us to consult with our clients and present usable data to help them become more efficient with the Internet job board spending.

    I then released this as a standard that was later implemented by many of the top job boards.
