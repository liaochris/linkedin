Kathy Baughman's Summary

Content supply chain and social business expert, with expertise in b-to-b, b-to-c and employee programs. Considered a visionary who looks at challenges in unique ways that result in innovative solutions. Continuous learner who contributes thought leadership to the industry. Author of two eBooks: Content Supply Chain and Alchemy of Content. Co-founded HLB Communications over 30 years ago to offer strategic counsel and incubate a variety of other businesses. In 2011, merged the firm with ComBlu Inc. a leading content strategy and social business firm, which specializes in helping companies maximize engagement to influence decisions and build brand advocacy.

Specialties: Content strategy, influencer marketing, online communities, social media, branding, advocate identification and activation, customer and employee engagement, social media measurement, and customer experience. Industry expertise includes: retail, consumer products, technology, financial services, professional services, healthcare, among others.

ComBlu specializes in content strategy, influencer marketing and analytics. Our sweet spot is helping companies engage at the confluence of content, community and conversation in order to influence decisions and drive brand advocacy. We structure content strategies that delivers the right content in the right place and time; connect organizations with advocates and influencers and facilitate engagement tied to business results; and provide actionable insights by using groups of metrics to tell a brand's value story.We create and amplify influence along the stakeholder decision journey and maximize the voice of our clients' customers across the cloud. We focus on building online and offline communities, engaging influencers, socializing content, activating brand advocates and executing engagement strategies, and then measuring the impact against business objectives. ComBlu also has a robust Content Supply Chain practice.
