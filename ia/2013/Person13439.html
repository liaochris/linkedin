Branch Manager

DecisionOne

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

May 1991 – January 1999 (7 years 9 months)

I was first hired by Idea Servcom. We were purchased by Decision Data and became Decision Servcom. Decision Servcom purchased Bell Atlantic Business Systems Services and became DecisionOne.

I was a Branch Manager with DecisionOne covering the South, Central, West Texas, and New Mexico. My organization included three Sales Representatives, three Service Delivery Managers, 85 employees, as well as a network of independent sub-contractors. Branch revenues were over $13 million per year. DecisionOne is national information technology services company. We specialized in Helpdesk Services, Outsourcing, LAN Support, Rollouts/Installs/Upgrades, and break fix on: PC's, LAN's, various printers, 3270 Devices, 3X Systems, RISC Systems, IBM Mainframe Systems, and OEM Warranty Services.

Duties included complete management of a field service organization. I was responsible for: staffing, sales support, inventory management, national accounts management, project management, service/support plan design & implementation, and P&L accountability.

Region Manager

October 1977 – May 1991 (13 years 8 months)

I was first hired by Lanier Business Products. This company was then purchased by Harris Corporation in May 1988. My division of Harris, Lanier BSD, (Harris Customer Service Division), was purchased by Syntrex in January of 1990.

Duties include complete management of a field service/support organization covering Texas, New Mexico, Colorado, Oklahoma and Louisiana, including: staffing, sales support, network implementation management, key accounts management, parts management, and Annual Operating Plan Objectives.
