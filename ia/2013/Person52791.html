I am a seasoned leader and accomplished strategist who thrives in demanding and challenging situations where performance is critical. My advanced education in business (an MBA and Master of Marketing degree achieved through three years of study at the University of Newcastle in Australia), Project Management Professional certification (PMP), and my recent Digital Marketing Mini – MBA provides me with a comprehensive understanding of management theory and practice. I continue to apply this education in my various roles both in local and global organizations. I have coordinated and lead cross-functional professionals from over 30 countries to ensure a cohesive, cooperative approach to the business needs. I have been recognized by my superiors for having strong leadership skills and have been selected for my organization’s Chairman’s Award and People Leadership Program. My leadership qualities, combined with my ability to adapt to change, have been pivotal to my success and would be valuable attributes in working with organizations around the world.

I'm known as a collaborative, integrative and critical thinker, masterful at making rapid assessments of business challenges and for developing and leading the resulting action plans. I have an exceptional ability to develop & maintain corporate relationships towards the attainment of strategic goals and innovation, resulting in bottom line profits and organizational excellence.

I believe that dedicated, skilled and motivated individuals who are passionate about their work can and do make a difference in the world. I am inquisitive, willing to try new things, enjoy learning, and view challenges as opportunities for professional and personal growth. My highly organized, hands-on style means I can make an immediate contribution to your team. Simply put, I intend to achieve results.

    • Founder

      Operation HoHoHo
      • Children
      January 1997 – present (16 years 10 months)

      Operation Ho, Ho, Ho is a toy drive that we hold every December to help bring smiles to the families staying at the Ronald McDonald House in Long Branch during the holiday season as a member of their family fights to see their next Christmas.

      For the past 12 years, the Operation Ho, Ho, Ho elves have been hard at work continually growing this simple concept of collecting unwrapped toys at their work, among their various social organizations and during their holiday parties. The idea of bringing smiles to the families staying at the Ronald McDonald House started with a simple idea of including a note about the toy drive in holiday cards that were sent out to customers of the former Hansch Landscaping and collecting all of the gifts by the night of a large holiday celebration held by the elves.

      Additionally, for years the elves inevitably received gifts from their family and friends that came to their holiday celebrations. Though the bottles of wine and trinkets were greatly appreciated, the elves decided to include a simple little note in their invitation to instead bring an unwrapped toy to bring a smile to a unknowing toddler’s face...

      Please spread the word about Operation Ho, Ho, Ho and we are always looking for new elves to join the sleigh to collect even more toys every year. Please give Henry Hansch III a call/email with any questions you may have or with ideas on how we can continue to expand this idea to continually ensure that we bring a little holiday cheer to the families staying at the Ronald McDonald house.
