WHAT IS INNEX?

Innex, Inc. is the worldwide leader and your next partner for video game, toys, mobile and CE accessories. Since 2004, Innex has distributed millions of units of merchandise to tens of thousands of global clients. With facilities in the United States and Asia, Innex has the logistical and shipping expertise to deliver its products on time and under budget.

From its headquarters in Southern California, Innex hosts a team of dedicated, multi-lingual sales representatives and a state-of-the-art purchasing website.

Featuring online inventory stock status, printable UPC codes, and a customizable, rapid ordering system, www.innexinc.com is your one-stop destination for all your video game accessories, toys, mobile and CE accessories.

With over 100 years of combined industry experience, Innex provides service and product strategies to both start-ups and well-established businesses. Innex is a proud member of the Consumer Electronics Association, in addition to serving as a member of the National Association of College Stores and the Better Business Bureau’s Reliability Program. As a 4-time honoree on INC.’s prestigious Inc. 5000 List. Innex’s professional success is matched only by its generosity. Partnering with Kids In Need—a charity providing free school supplies to underprivileged children—and the Vietnam Veterans of America, Innex donates a portion of its sales and inventory to these important causes.

With clients including brick & mortar stores, high-traffic online merchants, domestic and international retailers and distributors, and order fulfillment brokers, Innex is YOUR global solution.

We help companies grow their business and build their brand names globally through something we call the INNEX EXPERIENCE.
