Joan M. Medeiros' Experience

Attorney at Law

Law Office of Joan Medeiros

January 1996 – Present (17 years 10 months) Sacramento, California Area

Joan Medeiros devotes her Sacramento legal practice to estate planning, Medi-Cal planning, elder law, Special Needs Planning, probate and conservatorships. Her primary focus is protecting individuals and families from the confusing and devastating legal effects of failing to plan for death and disability. Through proper estate planning, such as wills, trusts, special needs trusts, Medi-Cal and Veterans Benefits planning, and elder life planning, her clients can protect their assets from the unnecessary expense and delay of court proceedings and unwanted government and administrative oversight.
