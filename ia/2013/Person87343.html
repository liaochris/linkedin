  • The Athletic Scholarship Eligibility Coach: A How-To Guide for the Eligibility Certification Game

    • December 2012
    Authors: Marlynn R. Jones, Esquire

    Knowing that one simple mistake can turn a scholarship celebration into a frantic search for $15,000 - $60,000 to finance the first year of college, Marlynn "The Eligibility Coach" Jones serves as a personal coach through the maze of legislation involved in acquiring a college athletic scholarship.

    The Athletic $cholarship Eligibility Coach: A How-to Guide for the Eligibility Certification Game is an action guide for parents, high school coaches, youth coaches and guidance counselors to explain the rules you will need to understand to be confident guiding your prospective student-athlete to Win a College Athletic $cholarship.

    The book addresses the Top 21 Common Mistakes made during the Certification Process, and answers these important questions:
    --- What is Amateurism Certification
    --- What core courses are required for Certification?
    --- how does the sliding scale work?
    --- How to transfer between schools and be eligible to compete?
    --- Are there additional requirements for International Students?
