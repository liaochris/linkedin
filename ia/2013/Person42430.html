Eric Ungs' Summary

Social media marketing, online marketing strategist and social business advocate.

Consumers are changing and businesses are transforming. It's an amazing time to be in love with technology and connectivity.

The question is no longer who owns social media within an organization. It's now how can everyone within the organization use social media. From internal collaboration to social media cross functional hybrid employees; Marketing, PR, R&D, Customer Service, HR, etc. all will play a role.

There are many components, tools and tactics that go into a digital presence. But the core to that presence in creating a lasting and genuine community of users is dissecting, then defining, the intersection between your brand and user; connecting purpose and lifestyle.

The online, social and technology space is fascinating and is constantly evolving. I thoroughly enjoy learning something new each day; connecting, discovering and experiencing.

My true escapes are running, reading and writing. It's an opportunity for me to just think. It's peaceful and extremely motivating. It's my Zen-like experience.

If you love coffee and the internet as much as I do, let's take this offline (or to Twitter) and chat. Feel free to send me a note (ericungs@gmail.com) or find me on Twitter (@EricUngs) to chat and talk all things marketing and technology.

Specialties: Marketing Communication Design, Marketing Strategy, Branding & Identity, Digital Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Visual Concepts, Graphic Design. Marketing and Communications Planning, Email Marketing, Content Marketing, Spredfast, Wordpress, Creative Suite, Microsoft, Google, Social Media, Hootsuite, SEO, Mailchimp, Unbounce.
