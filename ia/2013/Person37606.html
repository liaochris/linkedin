Dr. Brandon Lemuel's Summary

I graduated from the Harvard of chiropractic schools, Palmer College of Chiropractic, Feb. 2006. Since then I have been helping individuals and families optimize their health potential naturally with wellness chiropractic care. I take their care to a new level. My goal is not only to get people well through chiropractic care but also to keep them well through wellness lifestyle education. An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure.

I love to "edu-tain" audiences of all kinds about health and wellness. From graduating as one of the top chiropractic leaders in our school, to organizing lobby efforts on Capitol Hill to expand chiropractic benefits to millions of people or speaking at the American Chiropractic Assocation's Annual Meetings, I am ready to serve the world and make it better than what I found it.
