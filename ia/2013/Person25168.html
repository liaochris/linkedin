Cathryn Jakobson Ramin's Summary

I have been a journalist since I was 18, or maybe 12, depending how you want to look at it. I've written for most of the magazines that have graced our newstands over the last 30 years, and enjoyed almost every minute of it. I wrote Carved In Sand between 2003-2006, published it in 2007, and was blessed with an 18-month long speaking tour that brought me up-close-and-personal with my readership. My new book, The Fragile Column, is about my journey inside the back pain industry. And quite an industry it is -- a whole lot bigger and more rapacious than I thought when I began this journey

Specialties

Healthcare reform, neuroscience, mechanisms of pharmacology, anything to do with the back or the spine, sociology, religion, travel, anthropology -- especially medical anthropology.
