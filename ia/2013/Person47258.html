Gary Wolter (CRS)'s Summary

Award winning Realtor since 2001-Sales Volume Leader at Iowa Realty, Marion, IA office. Over 350 successful transactions. Leader of a 3 person team, with Melynda Wolter and Duane Johnson.

I am a Certified Residential Specialist (CRS) This is the highest Designation awarded to sales associates in the residential sales field. The CRS Designation recognizes professional accomplishments in both experience and education. Currently, there are only 30,000 active CRS Designees nationwide.

Because we had the best 12 months ever, selling more homes than ever before in our 11 years, Iowa Realty recruited us, equipped us with even more marketing power and believes we are going to the next level. If Iowa's largest real estate company believes in us...we hope you will too! :) Let us help you and your friends!
