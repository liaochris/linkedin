Barb Murphy's Summary

I’m confident working with high profile brands, large scale promotions and campaigns. I have an eye for detail and maintain a high personal standard when it comes to consistency, quality and focusing on the importance of adhering to brand guidelines. I have worked as a freelance graphic designer for the last 10 years, collaborating with creative teams within an agency environment as well as working independently with my own clients.

I have fine tuned my creative/design skills working with brand and standards creation, branded environments, packaging, corporate communications and advertising. I’m as comfortable collaborating with advertising agencies and publishers as I am working with local startups and non-profits. I've had the opportunity to work on brands like SABIAN Cymbals, Royale Tissue, and Tourism New Brunswick.

This past year I completed a certificate in Multimedia Studies from The University of British Columbia acquiring skills in HTML, CSS, Flash, Dreamweaver and video editing.

Specialties: • Print and Web Design
• Producing and Managing Complex Design Projects
• Maintaining Sense of Humour
