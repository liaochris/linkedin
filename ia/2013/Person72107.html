Karen Kefauver's Summary

Karen Kefauver conducts popular social media marketing workshops, staff trainings and webinars in Santa Cruz, California, the Bay Area and beyond. She teaches business executives how to effectively use Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Google Plus and blogging for business and promotion. She also offers private social media coaching.

With a roster of long-time public relations clients, and a 15+ year background in journalism (freelance contributor to the San Jose Mercury News and the San Francisco Chronicle, among others) Karen has the inside scoop on how to get media attention.

Karen writes and photographs endurance sports and adventure travel and writes a bicycling column, Spin City, published monthly in the Santa Cruz Sentinel.
