Jeff Herzer's Summary

A background in interactive media for the architecture/engineering and transportation industries. Preparing to complete a Master’s degree in GIS in 2014. Pursuing interests in geospatial data visualization (including ESRI CityEngine), LiDAR and 3D GIS.

I've been the voice on your radio, a face on the 6 o’clock news, the content architect behind presentations for huge public works projects, the help on the phone when you call 911. My interest in tall buildings has landed me on television documentaries, in supertall skyscrapers from Chicago to China, and in the interviewer's chair opposite some of the world’s most prominent architects and engineers -- quite literally, the people guys like Donald Trump call when they need a skyscraper.

As a freelancer, I’ve created dynamic web sites, web content applications and databases, audio and video content for small- to medium-sized businesses and professional organizations. I take pride in my versatility and my ability to get from point A to point B. I love technology and empowering others by training them.

I enjoy playing in professional ensembles and I can solo, too. I don’t like “cookie cutter”. But I’ve worn a uniform for many years in public safety and earned the Missouri Governor’s Award for Quality and Productivity. I don’t get into things to be mediocre. I am always looking to make things happen.

Let's get to work.
