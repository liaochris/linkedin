Francesco Di Maio's Summary

Francesco's life can be characterized by an intricate mosaic of various languages, cultures, educational backgrounds, and work experiences of nearly 25 years in 30 countries.

Focusing on international development, Francesco launched his career in Education, establishing language faculties and institutes throughout the Far East. He then turned his attention to international business in Eastern Europe, specializing in publishing, marketing, and small business development, such as launching a chain of clothing stores.

Currently Francesco is the owner and editor of e-magazine, Men's Fashion by Francesco, and publisher of Uomo Moderno, the 1st and only all Italian men's interest magazine.

Outside work, Francesco enjoys swimming and still competes from time to time in Master’s competitions. He also loves cooking, dancing, foreign films, and all genres of music.

Francesco's specialties include International Sales, Marketing, Social Media Marketing, SEO, Foreign Languages, Copyrighting, Editing, Branding, and Menswear.
