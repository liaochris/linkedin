Lauren N MacLeod's Summary

Lauren MacLeod is a Visual Communications and Events Designer with over eight years of combined photography, graphic design, event coordination, marketing, and management experience. Lauren's specialty involves creating visual displays that communicate a common message to a user group by being able to accurately define and target demographics. Lauren has extensive experience in using advanced communications based technologies such as Adobe CS5 with a focus on Adobe Photoshop. Lauren’s experience with planning events such as Rochester's Art and Apples Festival as well as her time spent as Events Coordinator for the Oakland University Alumni Association demonstrates her ability to effectively target demographics and mange resources to produce a successful event. Lauren's excellent organizational, problems-solving, communications, management, and design skills enable her to complete projects with the successful results that are desired by all customers.
