David Newton's Summary

David Newton is a producer of quality Internet video for websites and e-learning platforms. After two decades of pursuing combined passions of music production and the world of wine, David began producing a series of educational videos designed to teach the wine novice how to live their life at a higher level through wine and food. The video e-learning concept of TeachingWineOnline.com began to take shape in 2010 and had gained traction in the wine community by 2012.

During this time David began to have a clear vision of the future of Internet video. He recognized that online branding and education was in its infancy stages and would inevitably have the power to change the world. David became the co-founder of Storyboard Videos LLC and then segued into the Atlanta based company Realview TV as Executive Producer.

David is currently an independent freelancer producer of internet video and e-learning platforms. Additionally he continues to develop the TeachingWineOnline.com platform and consults for wine programs combining video wine education with hands on training.
