Cathy Archer's Summary

Cathy Archer is a writer who was previously engaged in Organizational Development Consulting for nine years and worked with a number of companies providing valuable insights to help resolve their organizational challenges.

She conducted many assessments and offered solutions born out of her experiences in all areas of her background. However, she credits her management experience as most valuable in that it allowed her to sit in the executive’s chair and discover ways to exceed performance targets while effectively managing a diverse work force. The solutions that she developed and offered were not just restricted to her consulting clients, but was offered to the general public in her previous radio show entitled, “Success Is Yours,” which focused on effective organizational practices and personal development; and her newspaper column entitled, “Performance Issues? Ask Cathy!”


PHILOSOPHY OF PRACTICE

Ms. Archer felt that the organizational challenges she explored went beyond the ‘presenting problems.’ She saw a commonality among many of her clients that led her to believe that many of the organizational challenges, while presenting in varying ways, had similar causes, one being the ‘seeming’ incongruence of executive and employee goals. So she asked herself, “How can I get executives to meet the needs of their employees while meeting their business needs as well?” This led her to develop a modality that has a basis in what she calls a ‘New Consciousness’ mindset.

Ms. Archer has developed a new management approach that helps executives meet their organizational objectives while helping employees to fulfill their needs. She has succeeded in identifying ways in which executives' business goals and employees' goals can be mutually inclusive and presents it in her book entitled, "A Different Path A Different Result: A New Consciousness Model for Businesses" (By Cathy A. Archer)
