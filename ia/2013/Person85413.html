Marcus Nygren's Summary

What's the difference between dream and reality? It's actually not that complicated: action!

I'm a passionate 21-year-old entrepreneur and creative professional, driven by curiosity and purpose. My goal is to combine and use my three biggest passions/talents; media, technology and entrepreneurship; to create opportunities for people and organizations to grow.

Since 2010 I'm studying Media Technology (M.S.) at Linköping University. In September 2012, I was headhunted for an Digital Media/Webmaster internship in Washington DC. Since March 2013, I combine my studies with innovating the Spotify Street Team (student ambassador's program), working from the HQ in Stockholm for 2 days a week. Another dream come true, combining incredibly fun challenges and opportunities with a truly remarkable team of entrepreneurs and programmers.

From having built web platforms to live conferences, lectured youths about entrepreneurship to helping talented students land their dream job at Spotify, my speciality is making ideas happen.
