Mark Morse's Summary

My name is Mark Morse and I'm a graphic design, web design, branding professional who specializes in bringing life to marketing campaigns.

Imagine employing a graphic designer or web designer who not only knows all the vagaries of good graphic design for both print and the web, but also possesses the marketing experience to know what creative approach will best fit the company's marketing and branding campaigns.

I have worked in both the B2B and B2C market place acquiring invaluable experience navigating those spaces. While working in retail promotion, I acquired the skill of working within concrete deadlines. My tenure with a retail startup taught me the skills of creating professional results under the constraint of very tight budgets.

While working at a global telecommunications firm, I worked both in the information technology and corporate marketing departments. I became the conduit between the two departments and greatly aided their communication with each other as I'm as equally comfortable conversing in marketing terms as I am in working with back-end programmers and engineers to build marketing solutions.

I have a proven track record that is reflected in the following achievements:

Redesigned public web site for 3 billion dollar EMS manufacturer. Coded an maintained 2 other public sites for the company. Producing and maintaining the sites in house saved the company over $150,000.

Set up and ran a digital graphic department for a retail franchise company that resulted in production cost savings of 40% on all store promotions without sacrificing quality

Serviced over 1500 retail stores controlling an annual budget of $2,000,000

Provided 4 color P.O.P. materials and sales promotions for 31 retail stores as well as retail ads and direct mail pieces with an annual budget of less then $200,000

My online portfolio can be viewed at www.mindandmatterdesign.com. I can be reached at: mark@mindandmatterdesign.com, markmorse2@cox.net and 949-766-6560.
