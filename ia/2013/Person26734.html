Connie Roberts' Summary

I am a professional blogger who writes about social issues, family and travel. As an advocate I feature social causes related to children, education, women's issues and poverty. As a product review writer, I write original reviews, use my own photos whenever possible to share features that are important to my readers.

My social media strategies include growing, maintaining and connecting with others on Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest, Instagram and Google +. Promotion is key to campaigns and these skills do a great deal to help get as many eyes on my writing as possible.

Specialties: Professional writing and editing, blogging, social media, online sales, business marketing, networking, promotion of non-profits
