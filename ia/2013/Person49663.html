Michael Madill's Summary

Music by Michael Madill
Madsound | Los Angeles

Producer, Composer, Sound Designer.

I compose and record music for video games, television & web commercials, movies, pilots, DJs, and even for fun. EDM, folk, solo cello, rock, dubstep, ukulele, orchestral... you name it.

I also create non-musical audio: sound effects and character audio design for video games, commercials, and animation of all types. Please view some of my work at Madsound.com.

Corporate clients include Marvel Comics, Ford, McDonald's UK, Chevrolet, DC Comics, Sony Online Entertainment, BMG/Chrysalis, Nokia, Virgin Mobile UK, Honda, Jeep, Volkswagen, VH1, Diesel, Saab, Bank of America, Comcast, Radio Shack, National Football League, Microsoft and many more.
