Andrew Arroyo (Easy Move)'s Summary

Andrew Arroyo started the concierge service at Easy Move Real Estate in 2010 with one goal in mind. That goal was to "reinvent real estate". Specifically, Mr. Arroyo desired to re-create the way the traditional real estate transaction was being handled. After 15 years in the business and being part of over 1000 homes sold, it was evident to him that a significant change was desperately needed. In Mr. Arroyo's view, consumers and agents were fed up with the highly stressful environment created by buying and selling real estate. On top of that, the consumer typically felt ripped off by paying thousands of dollars in high commissions for a service that sometimes took less than a few hours to perform. Andrew was determined to deliver something new, fresh and innovative that would change the industry forever and leave a positive footprint for the consumer.

Somehow, Mr. Arroyo wanted the everyday Joe to be able to be experience a stress free deal and be excited about moving, not dreading the thought and cost of a move. Mr. Arroyo wanted to bring back the joy and happiness to buying a new home and delivering a smooth, stress free environment for the consumer from start to finish. Could that be possible? Everyone knows “moving” stinks. And real estate transactions are not “easy”. Plus, the commissions to move really add up. Easy Move’s business model is the solution to these problems and the success stories are compounding daily.

Easy Move has evolved today into the "ONLY WAY" to buy and sell a home today. Ask any homeowner that has used the service and they will tell you immediately that they don’t know how they did it before working with Easy Move. You have heard the good news and yes it’s possible to move stress free and not pay high commissions. Why go through a stressful move the old traditional way and pay high commissions when you can hire Easy Move and relax? Call today and find out how.

Easy As 1-2-3

1. Sell your home TODAY for a total commission rate as low as 1.95% - 4.95%

2. Save time, money, and stress with our all inclusive packages.

3. Receive your own personal concierge and butler from start to finish until your home is SOLD!

Easy Move is reinventing real estate, by recreating the traditional real estate transaction. Instead of the typical six percent commission rate that a homeowner pays to sell their home (three percent for both the buying and selling agents), Easy Move has rates ranging from as low as 1.95 to 4.95 percent – with all inclusive packages, depending on the level of benefits – that save time, money and stress for local homeowners.

“The key way we are able to offer this deal boils down to one word: technology,” explains the founder. “With today’s technology there is no reason to have to pay a six percent commission rate. The internet has put the power in the hands of the homeowner; many end up finding their own home 75 percent of the time. This, along with digital signatures, enables agents to spend much less time on the process.”

Through Easy Move, homeowners will receive their own personal concierge and butler to take care of the operational details of the sale and move, such as home staging, scheduling inspection appointments and packing. These luxury attendants are program benefits no matter the size of the home being sold. Packages include all traditional realtor services, such as advertising, marketing, contracts, and negotiation performed by a licensed real estate agent, as well as complimentary moving services*, home staging*, home repairs*, termite inspection, home inspection and broker price opinion/appraisal (*certain restrictions apply).
