Fabiola Price's Summary

Glow Meetings & Events is a boutique event planning firm specializing in corporate and social events in the San Francisco Bay Area.

As an independent event planner, my focus is on the customer. I provide personal attention to every project and see it through from start to finish. My goal is to help my clients maximize their allocated budgets, ensure that their message is getting across, and make certain that their events always have an impact on the attendees.
