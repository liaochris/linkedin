Jan Zika's Summary

I am an experienced creator and designer of media projects with a strong knowledge of television and video production. I am comfortable behind the scenes and on-air. At Czech Television, the national broadcaster, I present the news for the CT1 and CT24 channels and previously lead the development of motion grahics.

I designed and implemented the programming strategy and a system of automated on-air graphics for Z1, a dedicated business channel, where I also presented business and finance news, and hosted discussion programs.

I also train CEOs and executives to perform comfortably and capably in front of the media.

Specialties: Television news, programming development, strategy, training, project management, on-air graphics, finance, broadcast media, television production, new media, online video
