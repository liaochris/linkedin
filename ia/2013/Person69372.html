Josh Yeyni, CPA, CFP®'s Summary

We provide financial planning and investment management. Along with our affiliated CPA firm, we also provide tax planning, tax preparation and IRS problem resolution.

We help our clients KEEP more of their money; GROW their net worth; PROTECT their family and income; ENJOY a stream of income in retirement; and TRANSFER the wealth efficiently to the beneficiaries.

We focus mainly on serving the following:
● Income and Estate tax planning, Portfolio managment and advice for retirees and high net-worth Individuals.
● Tax planning for executives of high-tech companies with specific issues such as stock options, Stock Awards and Employee Stock Purchase Plans.
● Multi-national individual clients mainly from Israel and Canada. These clients often face challenges in coordinating taxation in the US and the other taxing country.
