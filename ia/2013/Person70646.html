Julia Swanwick, MBA's Summary

My professional experience and passions have led me to a career in Organizational Development and Program Management within The Walt Disney Company. I continue to learn and develop fundamental HR skills as I advance in this career path. My professional development plan focuses on fine-tuning facilitation skills and building leadership qualities.

I am a graduate of the MBA program at the University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business. I graduated with honors from Emerson College in Boston, where I received a Bachelor degree in Integrated Marketing Communications.

I am a devoted fan and supporter of the performing arts. My childhood was defined by my time on-stage with community theater groups. I am so proud to have founded and to serve as President of the new Burbank Community Theater. Our mission is to build our community through a mutual love of theater, including mentorship among all ages and all levels of experience. I regularly attend musicals and plays at large and small theaters, productions from the L.A. Opera and other opera companies, and concerts of all kinds. I am also an avid museum-goer, my favorite L.A. museum being the Getty Villa in Pacific Palisades. My hobbies are baking, watching documentaries, travelling, and experiencing Disney Parks and Resorts around the world.

Specialties: Organizational Development, Analysis, Project Management, Leadership, Talent Development and Management, Corporate Training, Facilitation, Instructional Design, Public Speaking, Team Building, Leading Effective Teams, Arts Advocacy and Theatrical Production.
