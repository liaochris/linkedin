Tanya Knight's Summary

I am the author of the book: Who Says You Can't Go to College? This resource gives you everything you need to know about the higher education process. Through this comprehensive resource, I guide students, adult learners, parents and professionals looking to advance their career from applications to graduation—covering important topics like degree types, coursework, financial aid, and job placement.

I speak at conferences on the topic of preparing and successfully graduating from college, and host regular webinars on continuing education and college preparation.

If you would like to learn more about either my book, speaking engagements, or webinars please go to www.tanyaknight.com to learn more.

Tanya Knight's Experience

President and CEO (Education Coach & Consultant) | Motivational Speaker

Tanya Knight International, Inc.

January 2008 – Present (6 years 1 month) Orlando, Florida Area

Inspiring. Enlightening. Passionate. Encouraging. These are all words that people have used to describe Tanya Knight’s incredible speaking abilities during her frequent workshops, tele-courses, and private coaching sessions.

As an expert on distance education and non-traditional students, Tanya Knight spends her time spreading the encouraging and exciting message that anyone, regardless of background, can get an education and succeed.

As someone who did not excel academically as a child and grew to become a Ed.D. Candidate in Educational Leadership, Tanya knows firsthand the struggles and the pain that comes with people not believing in you. Thus, Tanya has made it her purpose, her ultimate passion in life, to encourage others to pursue their educational dreams.

There is a reason Tanya is named “America’s Education Coach.” Her flagship organization, Tanya Knight International, helps students of all ages through the higher education process. She has also been a part of many prestigious organizations, most recently as a partner with Colin Powell’s educational initiatives, American Promise Alliance.

People love her incredible personality and amicable nature. That’s why she’s been chosen to be a regular contributor for Education.com, Jacksonville.com, Hopeforwomenmag.com, Yahoo.com and The College World Reporter Magazine. She was also named as one of “17 Distance Learning Experts Worth Knowing About” by The Knowledge Maven. And, as if those successes weren’t impressive enough, she’s also a published author. Her motivational book Who Says You Can’t Go To College? gives parents and students the inspiration and resources necessary for college matriculation.

Ultimately, Tanya is a fighter, and her intense passion comes across in every speaking engagement. Booking a session with her will leave you, your students, or your organization excited, exhilarated, and feeling as thought they can conquer the world.

Learn more about her services at www.theeducationcoach.com.
