Desamuda Villas Bali's Summary

Is One of Bali’s most idyllic resort. Ideal for both short and long stay Desamuda Village captures the spirit of rural Bali consisting with a collection of 26 private villas set within a tropically landscaped garden. It is a retreat for the senses where exotic greenery and endless blue skies invite travellers to momentarily escape the pressures of modern living. Impeccable hospitality ensures that every stay is a memorable experience.

Leisure needs at Desamuda Village are addressed with two communal swimming pools with ample space to socialise or soak up the sun. The garden is dotted with an array of traditional Balinese pavilions providing a shady niche for some quiet time alone.

There is an internet facility at the lobby as well as a small library of reading materials. Day excursions to explore the sights and sounds of Bali can be arranged on request. We even have a selection of traditional costumes for a unique photo opportunity.
