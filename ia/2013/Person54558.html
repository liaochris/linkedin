IBS Emea's Summary

IBS is an Egyptian organization for integrated business services which strives to provide a uniquely specialized standard of services while aspiring to offer the right solutions for any corporation, whatever its business activities are, to satisfy its needs in the fields of training, information technology solutions, and business administration consultancy.

In attaining such an end, we aim to provide our services by recruiting a team of experts and professionals in different technical and administrative fields. Such a team enjoys the experience and the academic knowledge which enable them to provide our clients with not only solutions or traditional services but also a whole realm of innovation and creativity leaving a powerful imprint for their organizations.
Mission Statement: Our mission is to develop and enhance the productivity of individuals and Organizations not only by providing them with the updated knowledge and skills of any specialty needed in the market but also by putting them on the right track.

We strive to enhance our employee’s competencies, develop a teamwork environment that ensures the efficiency of the services that meet our clients’ and stakeholders’ expectations.
