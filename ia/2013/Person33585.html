Deborah Sakelaris' Summary

Deborah Sakelaris, Professional Certified Career and Life Coach, is the President and Owner of Rodas Coaching, LLC, a Career and Life Coaching firm. Deborah provides career and life coaching, career management consulting, resume review, and interview skill development.

Deborah has coached over 1000 successful professionals on career and life transitions in designing and living extraordinary lives. With 15 years of progressive experience in career coaching and training, Deborah will empower you with the focus to gain clarity on what you want in your career and in your life. She spent 11 years as the National Operations Manager at Randstad Staffing Services, where she managed, trained and coached 47 franchise owners and their staffs in all areas of the industry.

Deborah is an experienced, interpersonal and results oriented leader. She holds a Bachelors Degree in Public Relations and a Masters Degree in Business from Ball State University. She has also completed 3 coach training and certification programs with the Coaches Training Institute, iPEC, Institute for Professional Excellence in Coaching and the International Coach Federation.

Deborah developed her passion for giving excellent customer service through her training and work experience at the Walt Disney Company in Orlando, Florida where she worked as the Assistant to the President and Guest Relations Associate.

Deborah has presented and coached individuals and groups on career and life transitions, recruiting, sales, and training and development for staff members, management teams and corporate leaders across the country

Specialties: Career Coaching certified, Life Coaching certified, Energy Leadership Index Assessment certified. Professionals in LIfe Transitions. Presentations/Workshops and seminars.
