Brian Mueller's Summary

Lexstop Law Firm LLC is a full service law firm that I founded which can provide a variety of legal services to the good people and businesses of Missouri and Illinois. A lot of people are confused by the name. Lex is Latin for law and the stop is self explanatory. Hence, the name simply means law stop. I didn't want to have a name like Mueller and Associates or Traffic Ticket Law Firm LLC because every law firm I know of has a name just like it. Plus, how many people in Saint Louis do you know named Mueller? I have gotten and continue to get calls for different Brian Muellers. While I try to help point them to the right person, it is sometimes difficult. Lexstop strives to be different by offering the highest quality legal services and customer support to clients. Further, by choosing a name like Lexstop Law Firm as opposed to Mueller and Associates, people know that no one person, not even the founder, is bigger than the firm's mission of helping people. Finally, we are so sure that once you know of the quality of our work and how you will be treated on a simple matter, you will come back in the future for other legal services. To help differentiate ourselves, I came up with this unique name so the client knows when they hire lexstop they know exactly what they will be getting. A law firm firmly committed to helping as many good people and businesses as possible.
