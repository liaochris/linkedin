Joan Miller's Summary

Our emphasis is on enhancing the window decor in your home and office. We do that by going to your location with samples and professional expertise. We work to address light control, insulation, privacy, and style.

We specialize in challenging windows. Our main objective is to provide a solution for you that fits your personal taste and style. Above all we want to exceed your expectations with excellent service.

Specialties

Custom Window Decor of all types including plantation shutters, blinds, roman shades, roller shades, honeycomb shades, sun screens, draperies, top treatments, bedding, pillows and cushions. We specialize in windows with a challenge.
