David Horat's Projects

  • European Grid Initiative

    • April 2010 to March 2011
    Team Members: David Horat, Laurence Field, Steven Newhouse, , Wojciech Lapka, Emir Imamagic, Sergio Andreozzi, Tiziana Ferrari

    The European Grid Infrastructure enables access to computing resources for European researchers from all fields of science, from High Energy Physics to Humanities.

  • EGEE: Enabling Grids for E-sciencE

    • March 2009 to April 2010
    Team Members: David Horat, Laurence Field, , Wojciech Lapka

    The distributed computing infrastructure built and nurtured by the projects DataGrid (2002-2004), EGEE-I, -II and -III (2004-2010) is now supported by the European Grid Infrastructure. This long-term organisation coordinates National Grid Initiatives, which form the country-wide building blocks of the pan-European Grid. Transitioning the care of the infrastructure from EGEE to EGI is part of the process for ensuring a vibrant and sustained European research community.

  • CLOUDOPENDATA

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: David Horat, Luis Guirado Fuentes

    The project aim and objective is to develop an Open Source Middleware, that through the use of open standards, allows the development of interoperable services for access and distribution of data between “cloud computing” service providers in a transparent way, using intelligent, self-operating procedures (techniques) of elasticity control and guaranteeing security to the data access as well as achieving simplicity on the development of data mashups.
