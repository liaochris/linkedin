Jeff Ashcroft's Summary

Over twenty years of logistics experience from warehouse floor to boardrooms of major retail and third party logistics organizations has given me a solid base of capabilities and practical approach to helping clients develop logistics solutions that work.

Specialist in major retail logistics, systems, strategy and effective execution especially in the unique Canadian marketplace. I have helped some of the world's largest retailers successfully enter the Canadian market. Have developed world leading Retailer/Vendor collaborative logistics process solutions for a number of Canadian retailers.

Connect with me and I'd be happy to share some of the growing factors making now one of the best times ever for retailers to enter Canada with both retail e-commerce and bricks & mortar stores. A number of opportunities/challenges related to technology and sustainability have potential to turn current retail distribution models upside down. Those who understand and prepare will reap significant competitive advantage as others struggle to reset their networks in response. Happy to connect with retailers both here in Canada and around the world to discuss these issues and opportunities.

Specialties

Logistics and supply chain operations, strategy, optimization, transportation, warehousing, distribution, systems, negotiation, integration, process redesign, micro / macro change management, pilot projects, rollout definition and facilitation, project definition and management, sales, marketing and business development, web/internet and communications strategy, automatic identification and systems integration, data cleansing and data synchronization, professional writing and presentation skills
