Jeff Fox's Summary

J. M. Fox Associates opened it's doors in Norristown PA, in February of 1990. The initial focus of the Advertising agency was to establish it's self as the leader in advertising, Marketing and web site design. For over 22 years, J.M.Fox Associates Inc., has grown to become a full service Advertising and Marketing Agency that specializes in Broadcast, Cable Campaigns, Radio and Creative Marketing, as well as Website Development, Hosting and Internet solutions. We attribute our stability and growth as much to a constant pursuit of professional excellence as to a strong commitment to our clients and our proven ability to respond to the ever-changing market place. We specialize in helping businesses grow, not just for the short term, but by developing and taking care of all advertising, marketing and Internet solutions over the long term. We currently work with over 175 clients throughout the Greater Philadelphia, South Jersey, Delaware area and beyond. 86% of the businesses with which we work are family-run, most in their second and third generation.

Specialties

Broadcast, Cable Campaigns, Radio, Print Media, and Creative Marketing, as well as, Web Site Development, Design, Hosting, Search Engine Optimization, E-commerce, Blog development and Complete Internet Solutions.
