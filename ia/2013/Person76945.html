Kristen Light's Summary

I am passionate about working with physicians and healthcare professionals utilizing Motivational Interviewing techniques to promote health behavior change and health education, and improve health literacy among diverse populations. I am very interested in opportunities related to improving the relationship between physicians and patients and physicians and medical staff. I have more than five years of experience in health communications and I am trained in Motivational Interviewing by a leading expert from the University of Michigan, Dr. Ken Resnicow.

Specialties: Needs assessment; tailored health communications; multicultural health education material production; qualitative research; program development and evaluation; behavior change theories; motivational interviewing to promote health behavior change.
