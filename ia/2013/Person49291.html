Glenn O'Bannon's Projects

  • Disney/Pixar's Cars

    • October 2003 to June 2006
    Team Members: Glenn O'Bannon, Jordan Itkowitz, Ken George, Leslie Carrera-Keys, Karen Waller, Dave Lowmiller, Chris Baranowski, Eric Grajo, Brent Ashe

    #1 best-selling kids’ game and #2 best-selling game overall in 2006.
