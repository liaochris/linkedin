Gregory Jenkins' Summary

The internet is not what it was in the 1990's, the days of websites acting as online brochures are coming to a close. Online users aren't interested in what happened last month or last year - they want a web experience that's all about what's going on right now.

My clients understand this shift from the online brochure of yesteryear's internet to the evolving ebb and flow of the dynamic webspace of today. They want to be able to manage their content in an efficient, simple way - they want to be in control.

I build sites that are visually rich and functionally sound. This allows the client to use the site and use it often. Hire me to build your website and you will have a tool that you can use and your online patrons can use as well.

Specialties

Designing and building visually rich and functionally sound websites with an emphasis on dynamic content. Additional specialties include custom wordpress themes, branding and identity, user interface design, intuitive interaction, and a blend of creativity and problem solving that is essential for the web development process.
