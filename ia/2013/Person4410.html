Alfred Pong's Summary

Alfred has over 20 years experience in Technnology, Investment, and Executive Search. He brings a wealth of knowledge as a serial entrepreneur, involved in the start up of several successful technology ventures in the Silicon Valley and Asia.

Before founding TierOnes, Alfred was the Managing Director of The Strategic Executive Search Group, a leading senior level retained executive search firm in Asia. Alfred led the firm’s Private Equity Practice and had successfully placed many senior level positions for top PE and VC firms as well as for their portfolio companies. Prior to that, Alfred was an Investment Partner at Cybernaut Venture Capital, where he focused on Information Technology and Life Sciences investments. Alfred was also Vice President of Product Management and Development at 99Bill Corporation, the leading electronic payment company in China, where he led the development and launch of several successful online and mobile payment services.

Alfred started his career in the Silicon Valley, where he was the founding member of several successful technology companies, including Expert Edge, Future Labs and WebEx Communications (a NASDAQ listed, global leader in web conferencing services, acquired by Cisco in 2007 for US$3.2B). In 1995, Alfred founded 3W Concepts – one of the very first Internet consulting firms in the world. During this time, Alfred assisted many Silicon Valley technology companies in developing their very first corporate websites. Alfred also founded in 1997, one of the first online real estate portals in Asia - Malaysia New Homes Online.
