Henk Boorsma's Summary

Mede oprichter van Riege Software International BV voor de Benelux markt.

Dè gehoste (SAAS) oplossing voor zeevracht, luchtvracht, douane (Sagitta/ AGS/ Export/ NCTS/ Csum) en 3PL warehousing.
In 2 jaar al 18 zeer tevreden klanten in Nederland.

Hosted software solution for seafreight, airfreight, Customs, Warehousing.

Riege Software International GMBH, headquartered in Meerbusch near Düsseldorf, is a leading supplier of smart software solutions for air and sea freight forwarding as well as handling and customs applications.With around 80 employees, over 300 international customers and offices in Europe, Asia, North America and Australia, Riege has made a name for itself as an experienced partner for freightforwarders, carriers and manufacturing businesses since 1985.

Highly experienced in implementing freight forwarding systems globally and have extended domain knowledge on air and ocean im- and exports, Customs, EDI, warehousing, various financial administration software packages and interfaces.

In 1990 I founded Westlake Systems BV (softwarehouse for freight forwarding software) in the Netherlands together with 3 partners. After 14 years the company, which at that time was called CargoMate International BV, was sold to an Indian public listed software house.

Specialties: Very detailed in-depth knowledge of the Freight Forwarding domain.

Riege Software International

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry

September 2010 – Present (2 years 11 months) NL

Riege Software International strengthens its presence in Europe by opening a new branch in the Benelux. Since it's foundation in 1985, the family owned company provides innovative software solutions for the global freight industry and is one of the leading software vendors for the freight industry.

Located in Europe, Asia, Australia and North America, the company has provided 25 years of dedicated services to forwarders in over 100 countries with more than 850 installations. The reliability of the software is proven by customer’s loyalty. We greatly value the many long-term relationships we have developed with our customers.

By opening a Benelux branch office, Riege Software International is specifically able to serve the SME Benelux forwarding market with native speaking forwarding experts, each of them with extended knowledge of the market and business for more than 20 years.

Scope, the next-generation freight forwarding application built and designed by Riege, is being localized for the Dutch market. Modular components like a direct customs link for Sagitta import, export, NCTS, Type C/sum customs warehouse, DGVS and an interface to external accounting software will be available.

The result is a multifunctional software solution allowing a free selection of modules for almost all operational needs including air and sea freight operations, customs and handling agent activities.
