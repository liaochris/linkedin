Andrew Playford's Summary

Goals:
To build beautiful, awesome products with great people.

Professional Experience:
For the last decade Andrew has worked in the internet technology sector in San Francisco, New York and Chicago. Currently Andrew is working from his native South Africa for a British company - Remitix. Remitix offers digital solutions in the International Money Transfer space.

Andrew is also a founder and major shareholder of Glinnt.com which is an e-commerce platform for jewelry designers in the USA to profile their collections and sell direct to consumers.

Previously Andrew founded Signature Days (Chicago), an online retailer of lifestyle gifts and co-founded SmartRay Networks (New York), a platform for real time mobile alerting.

Andrew previously held a senior executive role at Think New Ideas, a small-cap public integrated marketing services firm headquartered in New York. He started his career with 5 years at Price Waterhouse Coopers in London where he specialized in business process re-engineering in the Entertainment and Media Sector.

Andrew holds a B. Bus. Science from the University of Cape Town, South Africa, and is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants for England & Wales.

Specialties: Strong experience with early stage growth companies and strong operations and product management expertise in consumer web and mobile application development.
