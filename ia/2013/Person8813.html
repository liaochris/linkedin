Anthony M. Rael's Summary

Anthony Rael is a Denver Colorado Native offering a high-degree of personal accountability, technical/marketing expertise and a sincere passion for delivering outstanding real estate services throughout the Denver Metropolitan area.

I'm a "high tech - high touch" Realtor who's ready, willing and able to text, tweet & facebook, but I also value the old-school face-to-face approach when serving my clients.

If honesty, integrity and a real estate agent's reputation are important factors to you when deciding to purchase or sell a home - Anthony Rael is the agent for you. "Ants" will look out for your best interests every step of the way and promise you a first-class experience. For trustworthy real estate advice & counseling, call (303) 520-3179 or visit www.anthonyrael.com
