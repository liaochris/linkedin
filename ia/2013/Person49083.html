Glenn Todd's Summary

With a background in design, Glenn is passionate about empowering people to use technology to solve important problems.

Glenn is the founder and director of Dvize Creative – a Melbourne-based web design and development agency established in 2000.

Dvize specialises in producing engaging and dynamic websites for corporate, small-to-medium business, and non-profit community organisations.

With a reputation as a teacher, Glenn is known for simplifying the often very technical aspects of the web so that his clients are empowered to manage their websites independently.

Inspired by biomimicry and sustainable design, Glenn is the founder of Bindarri, an online magazine and directory that supports and promotes not only the arts, design and other creative industries, but also how they can foster positive social and environmental change.

Glenn’s commitment to positive environmental and social change sees that one third of his time is spent sharing his skills and expertise to projects like Bindarri, non-profits and organisations committed to a more just and ecologically sound future.
