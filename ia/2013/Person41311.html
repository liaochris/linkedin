Emily Rogan's Summary

I am an award-winning writer who covers health & wellness, education and lifestyle topics. I've written for Cooking Light, Newsday, Woman's Day, womansday.com as well as several other national and regional publications. Two of my features, "What's for Lunch?" and "What Quality Childcare Looks Like" won awards from the Press Club of Long Island.

Specialties

Health & Wellness, Education, and General Lifestyle.
