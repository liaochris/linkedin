Brad Taylor | CEO - LHM's Experience

President | CEO

Luxury Home Magazine (LHM) | LuxuryHomeMagazine.com

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 2001 – Present (13 years) Local | National | International

With over 30+ years in Marketing/Advertising, Taylor’s background has always involved innovative and creative marketing strategies where the customer comes first and where relationships with your customers are critical in the success of your future business even in today's digital industry.

Taylor’s career began in the deep discount drug industry in the 1970’s. Throughout the 1980's and early 1990’s Mr. Taylor was co-owner of several deep discount drug stores working alongside some of the most respected and well known leaders in the retail deep discount drug store industry.

In 1994 Taylor formed Sunshine Publications, Inc. an advertising and marketing company. In 2003 SPI purchased PV Luxury Homes and PVLuxuryGuide.com out of Portland, OR, that same year SPI changed the name of PV Luxury Homes to Luxury Home Magazine ® (LHM) and PVLuxuryGuide.com to LuxuryHomeMagazine.com. As part of the transition SPI changed the format of LHM to a “coffee table/tabloid size” know today as “The Coffee Table Magazine”. In 2005 SPI took Luxury Home Magazine national, opening distributorships and company owned magazines throughout North America while registering Luxury Home Magazine®.

Throughout his career, Taylor’s philosophy has always retained the same formula, work hard, do what you say you will do and NEVER ask someone to do something you’re not willing to do yourself. With over 30 years of experience in advertising, marketing & building customer relationships, Taylor is one of the most respected individuals in advertising, marketing and digital media.

Favorite Quote: "Don’t be intimidated by anything. Work harder than everyone else. Never quit something you start” – Bill Cowher, former NFL head coach

Brad Taylor | CEO - LHM's Summary

The success of Luxury Home Magazine (Niche Publication) is due to our commitment to work with the best of the best. As you turn the pages of Luxury Home Magazine or search on our multiple web platforms at LuxuryHomeMagazine.com you'll see the nation's top real estate agents representing some of the world's finest homes, estates and properties with beautiful photography displayed in a "coffee table magazine” easy to read oversized format. What you don't see are the successful and talented Distributors, Publishers and Corporate Management that are the driving forces behind Luxury Home Magazine's around the country. These individuals are talented, passionate and entrepreneurial minded that enjoy the challenges and rewards of representing Luxury Home Magazine in their market specific areas.

Our goal at Luxury Home Magazine is ultimately to be a resource to not just our Publishers, but to our Clients and their Clients as well. Our Team, our Vision, our Values and our Focus are the driving forces as we continue opening magazines throughout North America.
