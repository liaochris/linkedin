Kenneth Overman's Summary

An international business development and export trade specialist with an MBA. Experience in the US and abroad in business incubation, training, marketing, strategic planning, international distributor expansion, and promotional/creative writing. Fifteen years overseas residence in five European countries. Additional twenty-five years of multinational business and development endeavors in Europe, Asia, Latin America, North Africa, and Oceania. Thrives in cross-cultural environments and is effective in areas with security risks.
