JC Cancelleri's Summary

As a business lawyer and business coach I help clients grow and succeed by encouraging them to focus on three areas referred to as SUPer. The S stands for strategy; the U stands for urgency and the P for passion. When a business owner is committed to making strategic changes with a sense of urgency and from a place of passion the sky is the limit as what he/she can accomplish.

I am particularly interested in working with business owners who believe their faith is relevant to building a successful business.

Goals: 1) To help your business become and remain exceptional; 2) To help you become and remain an extraordinary leader; 3) to develop a network of collaborative professionals who are also committed to helping businesses grow; and 4) to have fun doing 1, 2 and 3.

Specialties

Change Agent; Business Advisor and Lawyer; Life and Executive Coach; Financial Advisor related to getting out of debt; and along with my bride of 20+ years marriage and relationship coach and mentor.
