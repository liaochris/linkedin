Mike Corak mikecorak@gmail.com's Summary

I am the EVP of Strategy and the Managing Director of the Phoenix office of ethology, a Tallwave company. I enjoy diving into audience research and data, and developing integrated digital marketing programs that produce meaningful results for ethology's clients. I am also responsible for architecting the agency's go to market strategy and operational design. Besides a love for all things digital, I thrive on expanding client and agency business alike through smart, creative, and resourceful collaboration. consumerist + integrationist = ethologist

Prior to joining ethology, I served as the Director of Interactive Services, Strategy at Mighty Interactive, a Division of Off Madison Ave. Notable clients included Nike, Fujitsu, Cars.com, Apartments.com, and Leslie's Swimming Pool Supplies.

Before Off Madison Ave, I was employed as Senior Strategic Planner at iCrossing where I was responsible for designing effective programs for Disney, Coca-Cola, FedEx, and ConAgra Foods among others, involving regular interaction with resources ranging up to C-level on both the client and agency side, and coordination across multiple disciplines and time zones. I also played a prominent role in organic and new business development.

Beyond the day-to-day, I participate in a wide variety of industry groups, and blog and speak on related topics. Recent gigs include:
