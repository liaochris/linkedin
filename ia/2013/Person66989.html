John Rountree's Summary

I am currently involved in two areas: residential architecture and solar design/innovation.

As Principal of Rountree Architects, I specialize in the design of energy efficient, green new homes and additions in Connecticut and New York. My design approach is to integrate the client's needs and budget with informed decisions about healthy, environmentally sound construction.

As Principal and co-owner of Westport Solar Consultants, I work closely with other architects and building owners in the design of Photovoltaic (solar electric) and solar hot water systems. My specialty is in “building-integrated photovoltaics (BIPV)” which uses solar panels as the weathering skin of the building.

Combining my passion for architecture and renewable solar energy, I am currently designing a new innovative solar product that will combine solar electricity, solar hot water and solar air heating in one roof-integrated system. Called the “Energy Roof”, it will solve two of the biggest problems with conventional solar systems: cost and aesthetics. I have applied for three grants and am planning to build the 1st working prototype in early 2012.

Specialties

As an architect, I specialize in the design of energy-efficient, green new homes and additions. As a solar consultant, I specialize in the design of solar photovoltaic (PV) systems and solar hot water systems (solar thermal) for buildings. My main area of expertise is in Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV), which is the architectural integration of solar into a building’s skin.

John Rountree's Projects

  • Rountree Residence

    • 2005 to 2006
    Team Members: John Rountree

    This house was originally built in 1910 as a farm house. It was purchased by the Rountree family in 1996. In 2006 the house underwent a major renovation to enlarge it and bring it up to date with state-of-the-art materials and equipment. Mr. Rountree, an architect and solar consultant, designed the house to be a showcase for green materials, energy efficient construction and cutting edge solar technologies.
