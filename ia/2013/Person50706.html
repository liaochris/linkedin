David Cohen's Summary

GSIG LLC is a privately owned and operated company specializing in listing, managing, marketing, and the selling of corporate owned assets throughout Southeast Florida in Palm Beach, Broward, and Miami Dade counties.
We use an intraoffice REO property management application named REO Maestro to effectively manage all aspects of our properties such as marketing, listings, contracts, and closings. In order to stay environmentally conscious we operate a Green Office and are entirely paperless. All of our self generated files, documents, transactions, offers, HUDs, etc, are kept on a secure server, in PDF format for easy office access, and organization, with a RAID mirroring system and offsite backup in case of hard drive failure.
