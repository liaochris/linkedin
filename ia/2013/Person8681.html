Anthony Buccino's Summary

Seasoned business news editor, writer and proofreader with real-time online news expertise backed by web site content editing and design, and published writing.

As a freelance editor for business news website TheStreet.com and two subsidiary websites, RealMoney.com and RealMoneyPro.com, I produce and edit news and commentary using a variety of systems and protocols. That means being ready for anything while maintaining a focus on fact-checking, grammar, spelling and newsworthiness.

An award-winning blogger earning the SPJ NJ Chapter 2010 and 2011 EXCELLENCE IN JOURNALISM awards for Media Affiliated Blog, Buccino is a sociable and talented wordsmith.

Specialties: Reading, writing, improving writing, AP Style, desktop publishing, web design, business news editing, mentoring new writers, Certified Internet Developer, Corporate Webmaster, Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations.
