Colin Daymude 12,000+ Business Development's Summary

Currently Colin is recognized as an accomplished speaker and Business Development expert with numerous industry specific white papers and two personal finance books under his belt ("What Really Affects Mortgage Rates" and "You Deserve all the Credit").

Colin has been a featured speaker for the MBA, Florida Bankers Association, New Jersey Bankers Association, The Mortgage Management Conference and a host of sales conferences and a popular radio show, Entrepreneur 101.

In addition, Colin produced his own Marketing Conference as a virtual unknown yet still attracted over 200 individuals from the lending industry. With a Human Resources background, the topic of the conference focused on personal development, taking action, business development and marketing vs. lending practices. The Mortgage Marketing Review Conference featured notable speakers and organizations such as the Institute for Health and Human Potential and Allyson Lewis, Financial Planner and author of The Seven Minute Difference.

In the past Colin has been in charge of recruiting and training teams as large as 100 and has personally or through his team coordinated the recruiting and training of over 50,000 professionals in his career.

In 2005 Colin started his own Training Academy from scratch and wrote the company's Certified Mortgage Planner Course as well as the Summit Certified Mortgage Planner Course for experienced financial professionals. Espleez quickly rose to the top of the competitive ranks in the lending industry, competing successfully against companies with 20+ years experience. Colin was extremely successful building alliances with industry association groups, synergistic training organizations and large wholesale and retail mortgage operations.

Specialties: business alliance development, affiliate programs, coaching, training, speaking, talent acquisition, assessment, team building, recruiting, Human Resources, performance based compensation planning, CRM, marketing automation.
