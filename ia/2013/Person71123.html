Justin Bilyj's Summary

I come to the table with my prospective client and craft a solution that will protect their Business, Family and Employees' needs.

My job is to help the client find out if there are any "holes" in their business and personal financial life and to shield them and their loved ones from any potential risk.

We will then work together to determine an appropriate strategy to handle those holes. If it's something that I can handle for them, I will ask that they give me the opportunity to do so. If it's not, I will find an appropriate person/company to help them complete their business and personal financial picture.

When all risk is controlled, we will then take a look at their retirement plan to see if it meets up to date current regulations, maximizing all allowable credits and deductions. The aim is to cover the income gap which is a combination of pensions, savings, and social security to cover living standards for retirement.

After Risk and Retirement I finally take a look at estate issues. Are they disposing their assets in a tax efficient manner or do they have enough liquidity to cover any taxes, obligations or fees that comes with passing on a legacy? Whether its personal wealth or disposing of your business interest to a partner, key employee or younger generation, you can rest easy knowing your family, employees, and business is secure, protected, and ready for any obstacle that will come your way.
