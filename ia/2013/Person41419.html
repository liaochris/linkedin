Emma Stanton's Summary

Dr Emma Stanton, BM MRCPsych MBA is a Psychiatrist and CEO of Beacon UK and Europe.

Former roles include Commonwealth Fund Harkness Fellow at Harvard Business School, Clinical Advisor to the Chief Medical Officer at the Department of Health and Advisor for Bupa Health Dialog.

Co-founder of the clinical leadership social enterprise: Diagnosis, Emma co-edited “Clinical Leadership: Bridging the divide” (2009) and co-authored ‘MBA for Medics’ (2010).
