Meg Jones Wall's Summary

After graduating from my MLA Gastronomy program in 2011, I traveled to Europe for several months, blogging and photographing my way through several countries. Now back in the States, I do freelance food photography and styling, and am seeking a full time position in food media or photography in general. I'm always available for recipe development, photography, and writing opportunities.

Specialties:
-Excellent writing and editing skills, strong eye for detail, good organizational and leadership abilities.
-Creative, flexible, able to work independently or in groups, learns quickly.
-Loves to try new things, appreciates a challenge.
-Passionate about social media. Comfortable with a number of platforms, including Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, Flickr, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Wordpress, and TweetDeck.
