Darwin Tat's Summary

Accomplished graphic designer with 5 years experience working in a high tech corporate environment. Proficient in the process of executing compelling and cost effective consumer and professional product packaging from concept to final production. Detail and deadline oriented individual, well versed in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Acrobat with strong typographical, communication, and project management skills. Worked closely with in-house design team, industrial design engineers, product/campaign managers, executives, external vendors and international partners to create packaging, print collateral, web graphics, software ui, and more.

Specialties: Packaging, visual design, print communications, technical illustration, vector graphics, desktop publishing, brand and identity, logo design, web design, apparel graphics, pre-press, voice acting, and more.
