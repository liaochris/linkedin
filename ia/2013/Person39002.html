Dylan Barrell's Summary

I have almost 20 years of experience in many areas of taking technology to market - development, technical sales, product management, product marketing and corporate development. I have lived and worked on three continents and speak 3 languages.

I founded a startup in 1989 that was acquired. Went public with Open Text and helped grow revenues to over $400 million. Turned around another troubling startup which was subsequently acquired. I led the effort to create a digital center within a Borders store and to begin the transition from purely physical products to digital and physical product mix delivered in a cross channel online and in-store way.

In addition to my work experience I have an MBA with high distinction from the University of Michigan and a BS from the University of the Witwatersrand.

I lead through action to create value for shareholders and the community.

Specialties

Marketing, Product Marketing, Product Management, Corporate Development, Business Development, Funding, Retail, Digital Music, Digital Audio Books, eBooks, Video, Electronics
