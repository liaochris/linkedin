Jany Jacob's Summary

Jany K. Jacob, Esq. practices in the areas of business law, real estate and environmental matters, employment law and immigration law. Jany speaks French, Spanish, Hindi and Malayalam (India) and is a member of the Washington State Peace Corps Association and the South Asian Bar Association of Washington. She has also been a columnist for the Seattle Daily Journal of Commerce, a legal correspondent for KING 5 -TV NEWS STATION, and serves as a Network Attorney for the Microsoft Corporation. Ms. Jacob frequently speaks on toxic mold and indoor air quality issues to groups and conducts training sessions for construction companies. She has authored numerous articles and co-authored a book on construction law. Ms. Jacob was on the Board of Directors of National Public Radio from 2006-2008 (KUOW 94.9 FM), and is also the founder of a charity benefitting children in India and Africa that can be found at www.muchgrace.org and the founder and CEO of two companies, www.travelersgoods.com and www.christianreel.com
