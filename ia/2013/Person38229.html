Peter "Dr. Pete" Meyers' Summary

As Non-resident Resident Marketing Scientist at SEOmoz, my job is to make data cool. I've always believed that the best ideas and the best data are useless unless you can communicate them, and so it's my mission to explore strange new data and boldly blog where no one has blogged before.

Prior to SEOmoz, I've been a nearly lifelong coder, cognitive psychologist, and reluctant entrepreneur. I've worn many hats, but never a beret. I’ve always been fascinated by putting the right tools to work for people, whether it’s technology or the human mind.

Specialties

content marketing, search engine optimization, data science
