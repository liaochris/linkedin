"Cycle Evolution Theory" is a significant work that totally changes how cycle analysis is used, interpreted, and applied to modern economics, the stock market, and trading. Martha Stokes CMT's Experience

Master Rated Technical Analyst for TechniTrader®

Decisions Unlimited, Inc.

Educational Institution; 11-50 employees; E-Learning industry

June 1998 – Present (15 years 7 months)

I study the financial markets, the stock market, and the economy to determine what students need to know and learn. I determine how new methods and theories need to be applied to the current stock market with changes to market activity due to changes in regulations or new technologies. We listen to our students to find out how they want to trade or invest, and what concerns they have about trading or investing.

I write up an outline for the course, create a syllabus and begin to assign areas of work for my guidance team. Extensive research and study is done at this point to define and clarify the course content and direction of study. We do not back test, instead we forward test using the rules, tactics, strategies, and methodology in the live market to determine how each will behave in each market condition. Testing must confirm a consistent 80% or better success rate using the methodology of the course over an extended period of time in various market conditions often many years. Once all of the testing has been completed, the analysis and summaries are applied and prepared.

The outline is expanded to detail all aspects of the training. Auxiliary course materials including manuals, chart layouts, chart analysis sheets, homework assignments, tests and answers, flash cards, course documentation, work sheets, and market journals are created and developed. I write scans, develop chart settings, and indicator layouts. This can take several months as all scans must be tested against live market conditions.

The new course is presented to the staff to make sure every aspect of the training is not only the finest education with in-depth content but also easy to understand and apply. Then we film, edit and format in our High Definition Studio production facility. Attention to detail and our commitment to excellence for course content sets TechniTrader® apart from all other stock market training courses.

June 1998 – Present (15 years 7 months)

A Chartered Market Technician is a professional certification for professional technical analysts administered by the Market Technicians Association. It is the ONLY certification for technical analysts approved and recognized by the SEC, NYSE, and the NASDAQ, and qualifies the awarded individual with a series 86 SEC status.
