Karen Rowe's Summary

I grew up on the Canadian Prairie, which means that it gets dark in the winter by 5 p.m. I got a paper route when I was nine years old and spent a lot of time walking my neighborhood delivering papers, and telling stories to keep myself entertained. I was sure I was going to have my first novel published by the time I was 12.

Today, I still get to tell these stories, although these days, I get to write them for you. My passion is creating work that is smart, honest and successful. I love helping you uncover your own unique and powerful story that will create an instant connection with your marketplace.

You no longer have to settle for taking years to finish your book.

My books work. Let’s talk.

Send an email to frontroweseat@gmail.com to find out how you can get free up hundreds of hours of your time to get your book into the hands of the people who matter to you.
