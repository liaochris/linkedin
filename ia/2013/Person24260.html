Christi Halls Robinson's Summary

I'm currently the co-owner of a small software and business intelligence consulting company, PBD Consulting LLC. As an entrepreneurial-minded individual with a passion for analytics and data, I understand and appreciate the importance of being an "IT-smart" organization as well as minimizing organizational technical debt. I believe strongly in data integrity because I understand that the quality of resulting business intelligence and analytics are only as good as the quality of the inputs.

I love to self-educate through reading, solve problems, and to ponder questions with no black or white answers. When I'm not in my office, you'll find me hiking, lifting weights at the gym, or enjoying my garden.

Specialties: analytics, business intelligence, financial analysis, business analysis, econometrics, budgeting, planning, project management, process automation, portfolio modeling, Excel, VBA, SAS, R, SQL, PowerPivot, SharePoint, ASP.net design, web design

Christi Halls Robinson's Experience

Senior Financial and Business Analyst

PBD Consulting LLC

April 2006 – Present (7 years 9 months)

PBD Consulting is my personal software development and business intelligence contracting company.

At PBD, we specialize in the design, architecture, and implementation of small business solutions ranging from e-commerce to financial modeling. We utilize a staged delivery method, adequate planning during the upstream development phase using Agile methodologies, and testing driven development to achieve highly functional and flexible software solutions.

My current functions include financial analysis and accounting, business intelligence and analysis and software planning, ASP.NET website architect and designer.

Senior Financial Analyst

eBay Inc

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; eBay; Internet industry

January 2011 – March 2013 (2 years 3 months)

On the GCS Finance Analytics team, I perform various functions. A cross-over position between business and financial analyst, I am responsible for managing the global customer services operational scorecard software implementation and testing from a business perspective. This requires that I fully understand the underlying data model and how to create ways to test the database and front-end clients. I have also developed a SharePoint site to facilitate the management of project tasks. On top of the technical tasks, I use econometrics to perform analysis on any given aspect of the business. This allows me to develop forecasting models or to deepen the understanding of business drivers.

AVP, Senior Financial Analyst

Merrick Bank

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Banking industry

September 2001 – April 2010 (8 years 8 months)

I was originally hired as a Financial Analyst I and earned promotion to Senior Financial Analyst and AVP within four years. As the bank grew from a small institution, I developed advanced forecasting models in Excel to help management forecast credit performance and to determine effective risk-based product pricing. These model also allowed management to examine the profitability of potential partnership deals and how to optimize the profit sharing structure. I also created and maintained the corporate profitability forecast compiled of individual credit card portfolios and other business units. Creating ad-hoc models, presenting results, and making recommendations allowed me the opportunity to work with all levels of management.

Developer / Interface Designer

June 2000 – September 2001 (1 year 4 months)

I was originally hired as a designer for SunGard's financial software interface but was soon promoted to the financial calculation development team. Using Java, I assisted in the development of the data layer between our calculation layer and database. I also implemented unit testing the layers using a Java-based testing engine. Coordination with other teams ensured that calculations were in compliance with company financial standards and helped to identify problems within software and implement solutions.
