Alan Trench's Summary

Alan Trench brings more than 20 years of experience as an executive in global corporations and as a consultant.

Throughout Mr. Trench’s professional career, he has held managerial and executive positions in such varied areas as finance, project management, business strategy, and human resources with TOTAL, a major oil & gas multi-national corporation, and Schlumberger, the technology services giant. As a consultant with McKinsey & Co., he served Boards, CEOs, and executives in the areas of strategy, operations and organization.

In his current role as a Principal with Partners in Performance, Mr. Trench supports clients in business improvement, project management, operational performance improvement, and process engineering. His clients include international and local companies in the energy, oil & gas, renewable energies, utilities and mining industries. Mr. Trench’s work spans globally, having served clients in Asia, Middle East, Europe and the Americas.

Specialties:
