Johnny Haru's Summary

I’m a graphic designer and musician from New Zealand and have been based in cities throughout Asia for the last 7 years working in the photo and design industry. I have created work for several major international companies around the world.

I’m passionate about strategic, functional and innovative design which helps you or your company enhance your presence through vibrant, interesting and concise visual material.
When I’m not working on projects I’m a scratch DJ and a percussionist for Funk, groove and folk bands in Shanghai.
