Mikal Belicove's Summary

Mikal E. Belicove is an Entrepreneur magazine contributing writer, a Forbes Magazine contributing blogger, a Today's Campus magazine columnist, a NewHope360.com contributing writer, a member of the Board of Trustees at Keystone College, and a business strategist specializing in content development and integrated marketing communications. Mikal's latest co-authored book -- The Complete Idiot’s Guide to Facebook, 3rd Edition -- was published in the Fall of 2012.

Previously, Mikal was the founder and CEO of an Internet company specializing in the development and delivery of B2B products and services for the outdoor education and adventure recreation industries. Under his guidance, Unified Communications published The Outdoor Network and the Outdoor Education & Recreation Law Quarterly, developed and managed The Outdoor Network website, and ran the award-winning employment service Outdoor JobNet.

Mikal worked on the 'For Dummies' series (Wiley Publishing) where he was responsible for finding & signing the best author candidates to write new books. There, he developed new titles; conceptualized content; negotiated authors' contracts; directed authors’ efforts; and researched markets for revenue-generating ideas. Prior to working for Wiley, Mikal did the same thing for Penguin's 'The Complete Idiot's Guide' series (now published by Penguin Random House).

Mikal's specialties include: Integrated marketing communications, business and corporate blogging, public relations training, branding, product development, strategic planning, writing for publication, employee management & stewardship, contract negotiation & monitoring, setting up strategic marketing alliances, and conference/seminar development.
