Christina Weber's Summary

In my professional career I help people, heal, grow, and become the person who God wants them to be. They become leaders whose impact makes a powerful, positive, rippling effect in the larger culture.

I accomplish this goal by providing high quality products and services in my faith-based therapy practice, my Catholic Coaching practice, my information product business, or my mentoring efforts. Each campaign is founded on a common philosophy but targets different populations with different approaches, methods, and services.

Specialties

I specialize in helping people understand, apply, and reap rich benefits from sound mental health principles, stystems theory, positive psychology and the vast teachings and resources of the Roman Catholic Church.
