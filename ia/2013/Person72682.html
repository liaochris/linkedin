Kate Neaverth's Summary

Kate started out in healthcare at the age of 16 and over the next 18 years worked in many levels of operations: Dietary aide in a nursing home, a teacher with developmentally disabled youth, direct care giver in a group home. She ran the front office of a primary care medical office and managed the staff in reception. She stepped out of an office role to care for her son in an in-home daycare until he went to kindergarten. Finally on to Amherst Media. Kate was hired based on her personality and work ethic. She loves her job with Amherst Media. She is always excited to network for (e)book selling, book printing and like-minded folks in the publishing industry. Kate is always on the lookout for the latest trends and tips for success!
