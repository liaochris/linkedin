Cathy Burrell's Summary

My name is Cathy Burrell and I am a Retail Consultant and Social Media Educator. My clients are business owners/companies who like most people, know very little about Social Media, but are curious how they can use it to grow their business, while educating themselves and their employees.My company is Cavern Retail Consulting. I help people understand Social Media by explaining what it is, and how to use it effectively. I teach Social Media to Boomers through White Rock Leisure Services and UBC Continuing Education programs such as 'Ageless Pursuits' and at Tapestry on the UBC Campus at Westbrook Village. You can find a current list of the workshops I am teaching by visiting my website at: www.cavernretailconsulting.com and clicking on the 'WORKSHOPS' tab. I also enjoy my volunteer work as a mentor with The Women's Enterprise Centre in Vancouver.

Specialties: Social Media, Retail, Speaking, Mentoring, Education, Writing.

Twitter:Basics Workshop Instructor

Cavern Retail Consulting

April 2013 – Present (5 months) Tapestry-UBC-Westbrook Mall Vancouver BC

Why Twitter? Twitter is a much misunderstood Social Media platform. If all you think people do on twitter is leave banal comments on what they had for breakfast...this workshop is for you. Twitter allows people and businesses to broadcast information immediately. The example of the recent Boston Marathon bombings allowed individuals,law enforcement officials,first responders,and many others to communicate information quickly and effectively, the way a phone call or an e-mail or a facebook status update could not. Learn Twitter from someone who uses it every day. I will show you how to create an account, send a tweet, and decide who to follow. The rest is up to you.
