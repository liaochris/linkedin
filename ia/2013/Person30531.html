Daphne Foreman's Summary

At mid-career, I am on a mission to help women and men achieve financial balance, and the sooner, the better. Focusing on financial organization and efficiency – and assessing protection, assets, liabilities, and cash flow – together we examine the individual's current situation, identify strategies for maximizing wealth building, and work to help secure the personal and/or business financial future that individual desires.

If the past few years have shown us anything, it is that traditional financial thinking has failed many consumers, leading to a reliance on risk above other strategies and on the acquisition of unsafe financial products. As a Financial Representative of the Guardian, I am committed to helping my clients organize – and optimize – their total financial picture.
