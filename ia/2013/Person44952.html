Francis Laleman | लालमान's Summary

Francis is a scholar in India Studies, a senior Business & Management Consultant, a first class Instructional Design Specialist, a seasoned Corporate Learning & Development Expert and an acclaimed Public Speaker, with an extensive track record in Europe, the Middle East & the Arab World, South Asia and the Far East. Unique in blending thorough academic insight in the subject-matter with a practical, hands-on global business and management experience, Francis has worked with a great number of corporate and non-corporate clients in ICT, manufacturing, engineering, pharma, public services and performing arts.

An acclaimed international training expert, Francis is available for tailor-made instructional design and competence development projects, training management assignments and train-the-trainer programs with a difference. In whatever he takes on, his richly filled international track-record guarantees excellent intercultural awareness and a rare, ethically sound client-oriented approach.

Hire Francis for outstanding training assignments, solid training and competence development consultancy results, incomparable intercultural competence workshops, and lavishly worded public speaker’s addresses.
