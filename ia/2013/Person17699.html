Brian Reiser's Summary

Brian's objective is to help business professionals build their careers and shape their business by helping them improve their ability to speak, write and listen. His coaching teaches his clients What to say and How to say it. Everyone needs a competitive advantage, especially in this economy and having the ability to communicate your important messages provides you that advantage.
He has over twenty years experience in the television and teaching profession.
He has worked as a television producer, anchor, reporter and play by play person.
Brian managed and directed advertising campaigns, marketing initiatives, new business development strategies and negotiated major sponsorships in support of his various television productions.
He taught television performance classes at the Connecticut School of Broadcasting.

Specialties

Brian specializes in helping people improve their communication skills such as vocal techniques, body language, listening skills and writing skills.
After improving the fundamentals his coaching helps you apply the techniques to all genres of communications such as; public speaking, sales presentations, sales negotiations, media skills, group skills, interpersonal skills and interviewing skills.
He is also highly skilled in the art of meditation and relaxation techniques.

2009 – Present (4 years) Stoughton, Ma

Timeless Communications provides our clients the game plan needed for success. We deliver results for business professionals by transforming them into dynamic communicators. We educate people on the importance of communictions and how it effects all aspects of your professional and personal life. Our coaching begins by improving your vocal technique, body language, listening skills and writing skills. Once you have improved your fundamentals we'll help you apply your new skills to all genre's of speech communications such as; public speaking, sales presentations, sales negotiations, media training, interpersonal skills, group skills and interviewing skills.
We provide individual and team coaching as well as classroom style sessions and seminars. Our mission is to improve your productivity at work and help build confidence in your communication skills in any setting.
