Benedict Mitchell's Summary

Benedict A. Mitchell Jr. began his investment career in 1996 as a Financial Advisor in Tampa, Florida with Morgan Stanley. Early in his career, Ben focused his efforts on working with small to medium sized businesses to make sure each organization had the best qualified retirement plan the market could offer as well as assisting those employees to exceed their unique individual investing goals. From 1998 until 2005, Ben worked for John Hancock as a Regional Vice President and sold hundreds of qualified retirement plans throughout the State of Florida. In 2005, Ben went on to join Nationwide Financial as a Regional Vice President in charge of all qualified retirement plans of Nationwide Financial in the southern half of Florida. Over the years Ben has been accountable for nearly 1,000 qualified retirement plans and approximately $1,000,000,000 in assets. Ben has achieved the Accredited Pension Representative (APR) designation from the National Institute of Pension Administrators (N.I.P.A.) as well as the PLANSPONSOR Retirement Professional (PRP) designation from the PLANSPONSOR Institute. In 2009, Ben started Florida Benefit Specialists to better serve the needs of his clients. Ben has been named as a "Top 100 Retirement Plan Advisor" by Asset International Inc., the Publisher of Plan Sponsor Magazine in 2010, 2012 and 2013.

Specialties: administration, banking, benefits administration, bonds, books, business management, cash flow, customer relations, features, financial, financial planning, funds, insurance, legal, marketing, meeting facilitation, networking, payroll processing, profit, public speaking, real estate, sales, scheduling, seminars, tax planning,
