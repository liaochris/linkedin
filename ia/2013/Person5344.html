Amanda Devine's Summary

My work experience and interests have covered a diverse range of industries and subjects, which at first seem unrelated and random, but in retrospect they all contribute to the establishment of a unique and useful skill set, which enables me to translate fundamental business practices and knowledge in a way that creative-minded entrepreneurs can relate to and ultimately adopt.

For approx 15 years I was a florist/floral designer in London. My passion for flowers led me to open my own business where I learned first-hand the skills and knowledge needed to start and operate a profitable business. Before long, a second shop was planned and added as well as a Floristry School and delivery service, generating a thriving business entity – a sale-able asset.

I returned to South Africa permanently & took a position with the V&A Waterfront, specifically in the Small Business department - a unique incubator that assists the growth of viable craft and art enterprise. This was a challenging, but highly rewarding role that exposed first-hand the difficulties faced by creative people in business, and my ability to help address them because of my own experiences.
The V&A has a diverse and unique portfolio of assets and I gained a broad knowledge in all areas (and qualified for my Certificate in Shopping Centre Management) but am still drawn to the SMME sector so I began to focus on services that could be of value such as online marketing & PR.

Currently I provide a tailored selection of services to each of my clients based on their individual needs, that range from website administrator to brand ambassador to social media manager.
