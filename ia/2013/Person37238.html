Douglas Grant's Summary

We assist owners,investors and management to buy,develop and asset manage hotels and leisure assets

COMMON ISSUES AND CHALLENGES
• To maximise value, hotel owners need market knowledge and the ability to influence management
• Value, linked to trading performance, needs focus on the detail of individual businesses
• In a tough trading and financing market, effective portfolio management can still be achieved through using specialist skills, a team approach and a realistic time horizon
• Most sector professionals provide ‘standard’ solutions and services-brokerage, feasibility and valuation -but not the flexible bespoke input required to move projects to the next stage
• Some international companies need strategic projects progressed in Europe but do not want the costs of a London based office infrastructure

THE BENEFITS of using DGA
Independent, flexible input capable of moving a range of projects forward
In depth experience of the hotel business
Extensive sector contacts across a wide range of disciplines
Able to work closely with your management team and external advisers
Willing to represent companies on a flexible project or time basis
Language ability in Portuguese and Spanish

GOALS /SERVICES INCLUDE:
Ongoing representation of owners through the provision of business development services including asset management, selection of brand/management companies, market and financial feasibility and helping to implement strategic long term projects

We also represent foreign based companies in Europe by providing bespoke services without the need to set up an expensive infrastructure in London.

CREDENTIALS
Roles with international organisations including Grand Metropolitan /InterContinental Hotels, Forte Hotels, Gleneagles Hotels, Nomura International, and Horwath Consulting. International experience and language ability in Portuguese and Spanish

Specialties

Owner Representation, Market and Financial Analysis; Business Planning;Feasibility;Selection of Management Company or Franchise; Asset Management;Project Management
