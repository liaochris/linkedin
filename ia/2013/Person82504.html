Lori Krolik's Summary

Professional Organizer and Time Management Consultant providing services to residential and small business clients. I help people de-clutter, create an organized space that is customized to their needs and personality, and teach them tools they need to manage their time more efficiently to maximize productivity.

Specialties

My clients range from those that are slightly disorganized to one that are severely challenged in the area of organization, including people with ADD.
