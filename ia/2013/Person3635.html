Andrew Lewis' Summary

I am a technology entrepreneur skilled at bringing truly innovative solutions to market, from fighter aircraft to digital publishing systems, to cloud and mobile solutions. I especially love the adventure of the startup, engaging with founders and their teams, helping them see different and execute better. Highly intuitive, my greatest strength is acting as a catalyst, helping teams discover their best path to growth with distinct vision and strategies. I codify these paths into structured plans that are elegantly simple and straight forward to execute.

I first learned innovation as a young engineer being mentored by a veteran team of cold-war engineers at the F-16 fighter "skunk works". Here we worked on special projects with NASA and USAF. After business school, I did my first startup as founder and CEO of a computer company, DayStar Digital, Inc. There, my amazing team built it into one of the top rated brands in the Apple publishing market. We developed new image processing tools with Kodak, and then with IBM, and under a clone license from Apple, the world's first multiprocessor desktop computers. Following these successes, I founded Mediatools Inc., the first of it's kind web site for uploading and playing home consumer videos. I have also developed entertainment products as a writer and creative producer. Today, I help build companies innovate through mentorship, strategy, marketing based on lean startup methodologies which apply to organizations of any size.
