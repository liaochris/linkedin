Korre Johnson's Summary

Throughout my career, I have been deeply involved in developing and integrating digital marketing strategies that leverage both traditional and emerging online marketing channels.

I became the Digital Marketing Strategist for Children's Hospital of Wisconsin in June 2011. In this role, I serve as an online marketing expert for the organization, developing web and social media strategies to acquire new patients, referring physicians, donors, community partners and volunteers.

At Versant, I led the development of branding and strategic communications solutions for clients such as Thomson Reuters, Xerox, Shopko, the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee and Kohl’s Department Stores.
