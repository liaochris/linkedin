Giuseppe Musci's Summary

Media executive with a vast amount of experience in the sector, currently working as Head of Channel Management at Sky TG24 (SKY Italia’s all-news channel).
Worked as Channels Editor (Head of Content, Commission Editor) at Dahlia TV, digital terrestrial pay tv, securing all original and 3rd party content for the platform’s channels.
Started his professional career in a local Italian channel moving rapidly to an international media company (FBC) with activities in Italy, UK, India and U.S.A., operating on an international level and acquiring wide-spread knowledge in the adaptation and localization of TV formats and the production of original programming.
Responsible for the adaptation of a series of original TV formats for the Italian market including Queer Eye for the Straight Guy (NBC) and Kicked Out (Buena Vista).
Achieved high standards of operational execution working within diverse environments, demonstrating high management ability in all levels of production, including rights and talent management, and deal making.
Closely involved in the production of a range of TV programs for the start-up and launch of SKYTG24. commissioned to FBC. Also executive-produced television programs, talk shows, documentaries, infotainment for leading Italian TV channels including terrestrial channel La7, state television RAI and satellite platform SKY Italia.
Specialized in video streaming & cross media content: acted as videostreaming manager of “Miaeconomia”, one of the pioneers of the web-based TV in Italy, partner of Virgilio (Telecom Italia / La7); also recently, worked in consultancy for the development of audio-visual contents for the most popular Italian news portal “Repubblica.it”.
Designed and executed communication strategies with on line video-based elements for a range of Italian and international clients including American Express, H3G, ING, the Italian Ministry of Finance and a number of high-profile political candidates.

Specialties

Start up of TV projects, Digital channel launch.
Editorial Supervision, Production Supervision, Budget and Cost Control.
Strategic Vision and Project Planning, Team Building and Staff Leadership, Complex deal structuring and execution,
