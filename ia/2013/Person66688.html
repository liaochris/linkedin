John Cohen, CPA's Summary

John Cohen has been a Controller and CFO for multiple companies across a wide variety of industries and Project Manager on many process improvement projects, including Sarbanes-Oxley Act efforts. He has been a Partner of a national consulting firm and has been hired on multiple occasions by Boards of Directors and Senior Management as a Subject Matter Expert (SME).

He has also served the Nonprofit Community as an interim CFO/Controller, Process Improvement Consultant, and Auditor. He has performed many audits for Nonprofit Organizations, including audits under OMB A-133 requirements, and has been responsible for compliance requirements such as Form 990 preparation.

As a practicing CPA, he has been an audit manager at a Big Four accounting firm and a partner in a regional CPA firm, where he was Partner in Charge of all Audit and Nonprofit client services.

Specialties

Design and execute rapid operational improvements, improve financial reporting, reduce accounting costs, remediate internal control deficiencies.
