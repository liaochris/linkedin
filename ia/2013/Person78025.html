Lainie Kartoon's Summary

Lainie Kartoon brings over a decade of entertainment industry experience in several areas including talent representation, content creation and production. After graduating from UC Berkeley, Kartoon launched her career in the agent training program at the William Morris Agency. At WMA, Kartoon built a solid foundation in the motion picture packaging business, while interfacing with high-profile actors, directors, writers and executives. Kartoon followed up WMA with a stint working for A-List Talent Manager Rick Yorn.

In addition to her entertainment industry experience, Kartoon served as a Financial Consultant at Citigroup, overseeing 150 million in client assets. After Citigroup, Kartoon reunited with a former William Morris colleague, Glenn Rigberg, owner of Rigberg Entertainment Group, and launched a business development department.

As head of business development and strategy at Rigberg Entertainment Group, Kartoon evaluated new business models, advised management and served as a consultant to several digital media startups. Kartoon was charged with developing strategic partnerships by leveraging her relationships at studios, networks and various media and technology companies.

Kartoon currently owns her own talent and literary management & production company, LBK Global, LLC, which specializes in developing strategic partnerships and generating new revenue for film, television, music and new media clients.
