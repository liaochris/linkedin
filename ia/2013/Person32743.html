David W Fischer's Summary

I’m a marketing professional with a track record of helping small businesses raise their profile in the market by:
• Providing sound, practical and achievable advice on how best to market their company. From strategy to messaging and marketing tool selection, the goal is to present my client's company to the market in a way that it becomes irresistible to prospects.
• Identify, create and manage marketing tools that are effective at attracting prospects to drive new and repeat business. Websites, printed material, email newsletters, graphic design, copywriting, social media management, search engine optimization and pay-per-click advertising are among the tools we create for our clients.

Bottom line: We create cost-effective and results-driven professional marketing for small businesses that leads to increased sales.

Our clients are quite good at what they do. What sometimes happens is that marketing becomes a challenge because there are so many options (do I need to be on Facebook?), there are technical challenges (how do I get a logo designed?) and there is limited time (I wish my days had 30 hours!). When you work with Solutions for Growth you get a marketing firm that specializes in small business marketing. We understand your business and will create marketing tools that are affordable and effective.
