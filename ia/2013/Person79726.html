Lee Hyder's Summary

Lee Hyder and Associates is a successful financial planning and Wealth management firm serving the Cleveland and Akron areas. With over twenty three years of experience, Lee uses his depth of knowledge and expertise to assist, educate and empower his clients to achieve financial success. He has been featured on several guest radio and TV shows and is in the process of writing his third book on Retirement and Estate Planning. Lee believes in developing real relationships with his clients which he does through hosting numerous client activities and events. He takes the guessing out of investing and puts the power in the hands of his clients by teaching them how to understand where returns really come from and how to avoid all the BS pushed down our throats by TV, radio, and the internet.
Having grown up in the home of a successful CPA, Lee quickly learned the importance of finance and tax management. From a young age he learned that successful asset management is not simply about numbers on paper. Regardless of the numbers on the tax return, Lee is focused and motivated to assist each and every client in the same financial opportunities. His passionate approach to helping others achieve financial success continues to grow as he assists and teaches those approaching retirement or those who are currently enjoying it.
