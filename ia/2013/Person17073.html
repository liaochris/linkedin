Brett Leonhardt's Experience

Director of Design & Development

Benchmark Real Estate Group

December 2012 – Present (9 months) Greater New York City Area

As Director of Design & Development for Benchmark, Brett supports the team in overseeing all aspects of the company's building and development activities - from design and planning, to construction and project management. His supervision includes architects, designers, LEED consultants, leasing, marketing, legal council, building management, and construction personnel to ensure coordination and quality control. These endeavors take place during all phases of real estate from acquisition through to repositioning or sale. His diverse background in architecture and education in real estate finance/investment make him a versatile asset to the team.

Sustainability Manager / Architectural Designer
