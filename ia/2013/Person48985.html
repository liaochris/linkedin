Glen Findlay's Summary

South Africa has it all, abundant wildlife, amazing game lodges, year round sunshine.I own and run Go Safari and will arrange your dream safari, to start please visit www.safarinsouthafrica.com
We specialize in families, couples and small groups so with us your are not just a number you are a special guest.We do not have scheduled departures but will be at your service from the fist communication until your return home.
Experience the magic of Southern Africa!

Specialties

Go Safari will arrange your personalised dream safari to Southern Africa form start to finish. Kruger Park Safari, Pilanesberg day & overnight safari, Madikwe Safari using Private Safari Lodges, Victoria Falls and Botswana Safaris and the rest of South Africa including Cape Town.Luxury Trains, road transfers and flights .
