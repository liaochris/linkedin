Adam McCabe's Summary

Adam McCabe is a modern renaissance man.

At age 26, Adam has not only worked alongside some of the most acclaimed companies in the world (Coca Cola, Hewlett Packard, Disney, Universal, Paramount, The Discovery Channel, AT&T…to name a few), but he is now one of the most sought after digital artists in Central Florida.

His work with the Orlando International Fringe Theatre Festival helped usher in the most successful marketing campaign in the festival’s history, and his art is regularly circulating throughout the state of Florida. He now sits passionately as the Creative Director of Orlando Weekly, an award-winning publication available in one thousand locations around Orlando, Florida.

On top of all of this, he is an avid singer, actor, and guitarist in Orlando’s local entertainment scene, and has spearheaded two popular blogs from the Orlando Weekly website: “Gorelando” (local horror news, reviews, entertainment) and “Gamelando” (video game news, reviews, entertainment).

He continues to be inspired by everything around him, and looks forward to seeing where this journey takes him from here.
