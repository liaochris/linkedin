Laura Borja's Summary

I have been in mortgage lending for over 20 years. I started my career as a JR loan processor working my way up to an underwriting position before making the transition to a mortgage consultant. My focus has always been and continues to be providing excellent service to the buyer, agent and all other parties involved in each transaction.

Now that I am a part of the Primary Residential Mortgage team, I have the best of both worlds, the resources that come with a large financial institution and the flexibility of a local lender focused on meeting the needs of the local market.

My team and I are committed to providing all of our referral partners with all the support, tools and resources needed to close as many transactions as possible. Whether your client is purchasing his first home, or is an experienced investor, you can be confident that you will be working with an experienced mortgage professional who is focused on your timeline and your client's financial needs.

Specialties

Creative thinking to fully utilize the products currently available to tailor the perfect lending solution for your situation.
VA, FHA, Jumbo, Conforming and Affordable Home Loan solutions. Approved with City of San Diego, City of La Mesa and CALHFA to participate in their down payment assistance loans.
