John Darer CLU ChFC CSSC RSP CLTC's Summary

John Darer is a Registered Settlement Planner who provides financial consulting services and settlement solutions in conjunction with the settlement and resolution of all types of claims and litigation as part of a community of practice. Settlement services include comprehensive pre & post settlement planning, mediation/negotiation strategies, structured settlement broker, structured Medicare Set Aside arrangements, implementation of annuity & non annuity alternative periodic payment recovery management solutions and New York CPLR 50B Judgment calculations, analysis and projections. I believe that regardless of where you stand, by utilizing our services and tapping us as your centralized information resource you can save time and energy.

Related services include life insurance, joint and survivor (second to die) life insurance, disability insurance, long term care and Medigap coverage
