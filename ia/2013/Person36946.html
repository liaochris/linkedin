Da-Woon Chung's Projects

  • Granin Keittiö - 365 Wellbeing: Rethinking Villa Breda

    • January 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Da-Woon Chung, Mia Pei, Outi Martikainen, Mikko Vuoto

    We are one of the 7 teams involved in Design Strategy and Innovation(D.S.I.) course of Industrial and Strategy Design Programme in Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture. The course is part of World Design Capital 2012 and is under the big theme of ’365 Well-Being.’ Based on this big theme, we are running a project in Kauniainen, with its specific theme of ‘Re-thinking Villa Breda,’ where Villa Breda is one of the facilities for elderly people in Kauniainen. Each team involved in the course are trying to find various factors in the city that can be improved in order to enhance well-being life of the citizens.
    We are team 6 of the project, and our theme is food. Food is something we have to face everyday, and we believe that it has potential to improve everyone’s life. For more information, please visit graninkeittio.com.
