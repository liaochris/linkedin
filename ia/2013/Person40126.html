Eileen Boyle's Summary

Life & Business Coach & Consultant Eileen Boyle helps midlife women in significant personal & professional transitions. Health & wellness, menopause, divorce, death of loved one, empty nest, elder care & downsizing, career changes, business start-up & expansion, marketing including social media.

Eileen understands the stresses of juggling family and career. She knows what it's like to "wear all the hats" at work including administrative, sales, human resources, marketing & facilities management. On the home-front as the only parent she was nurturer, disciplinarian, nurse, supporter, motivator, maintenance & repair, budgeting, planning & recreation.

Having owned & managed a graphic arts company, she has acquired in-depth knowledge & extensive hands-on experience relating to a wide range of business concerns such as planning & formation, legal, accounting & insurance, employee management, equipment & facility lease negotiation, customer acquisition, retention & vendor relations. Eileen has studied online marketing & social media extensively, & is very committed to helping midlife women utilize these powerful tools in launching, building or expanding their businesses.

Eileen's diverse business experience provides her with a unique, knowledgeable & very personal perspective. Her strong commitment & desire is to assist women in launching their own businesses, growing ones they already own, & advising them on integrating & utilizing online technology & social media to capture market share within their niche market.

Known for her high energy & direct, but compassionate nature, Eileen coaches her clients via one-on-one phone sessions, in group teleseminars & webinars & during specially designed workshops & retreats. In addition to coaching services, she is available to consult with clients who require more than just coaching support.
