Michael Vignery's Experience

Senior Consultant, Client Selection Services

The Barrett Group

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry

May 2010 – Present (3 years 7 months)

As a Senior Consultant, Client Selection Services, I represent The Barrett Group which has been in business since 1990. Having recently joined this company in May of 2010 I have been truly amazed by our effectiveness, dedication to excellence and the commitment we make to each and every client.

We are truly career consultants providing services to our executive clients. Our projects, campaigns and initiatives are intense and they all focus on helping our clients with their unique situations. Of course we help our clients market themselves effectively when they are looking to make a career change, but we also have clients hire us to help them with existing employers and business partners too.

We have many clients who hire us to assist them with navigating the nuances and challenges facing them such as politics, mergers and acquisitions, supervisor changes, abusive environments, reductions in force, and underemployment as well as just about any situation that could possibly occur in the workplace.

Being part of a close knit, personalized company, with a complete team of dedicated consultants, researchers and writers, allows me to give the personalized attention and professional guidance to our clients in an ethical environment where integrity and truthfulness permeate.
