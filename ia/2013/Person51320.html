Haleh Alemzadeh Niroo's Summary

Haleh Alemzadeh Niroo, Principal of Haleh Design Inc., has been practicing high-end interior design for the past two decades in Atlanta, Georgia and Potomac, Maryland. Her work is inspired by her travels across Europe and her interest in art history. Throughout the years, she has been blessed with the ability to create luxurious, graceful and lively spaces to exceed her client’s expectations. Haleh and her team are dedicated to helping make your home distinctly your own, from pre-construction design to final fine details.

Haleh’s goal for each project is to create an artistic and functional environment that is elegant and grounded in the rich history of European architecture. Drawing from her experience abroad and her design education, her work is inspired by the world of art and architecture. Her design is both creative and practical, and her attention to detail and enthusiasm for design give Haleh an edge over her competition. From charming, old-world style to clean, modern elegance, her designs will lend a classic yet unique touch to your space.

    Professionally and personally, David Niroo and his wife Haleh were a match from the outset. Meeting on a blind double-date 21 years ago, the native-Iranian duo clicked so well they married six months later. Despite their seemingly divergent career paths - Haleh spent her formative years in Nice and planned a career in fashion design, while David was in financial services - the couple found themselves inspired by their world travels, in particular by the capitals of Old Europe.   • Home Expert-Haleh Design, Inc.

    • Bethesda Magazine
    • August 9, 2010
    Authors: Haleh Alemzadeh Niroo, Valerie Portney

    Haleh Alemzadeh Niroo acquired her inspiration for high-end design through watching the exquisite taste and attention to detail that her father demonstrated as a successful executive director in the textile industry.

    Her passion for architecture grew while living in France, where she admired the country's classical historic buildings and exquisite homes. Haleh was fascinated by everything from the exterior- facade and landscaping to every square inch of the interior. "The details were always an important aspect that remained in my mind," she says.

    Haleh's interior design firm emphasizes classical values and timeless elegance. Haleh Design's talented and seasoned professionals have one goal: "Exceed the client's expectations from pre-construction to the finest finishing touches."

    Haleh and her designers first discern their clients' needs. Then they create an artistic and functional environment that is stylish and grounded in the rich history of European architecture. Utilizing the finest hand-craftsmanship and fine materials from around the world, Haleh Design transforms the client's vision into reality. Each project is one-of-a-kind, respecting the principles of classical designs while providing the benefits of the new age state-of-the-art technologies.

    In addition to interior design, Haleh works in partnership with her husband's company, Niroo Masterpieces, to design the facade of homes and any other exterior requirements. Together, Haleh and David Niroo design and create some of the country's finest high-end homes.

    Haleh remains inspired by her father words: "As long as you do what you love, and do it with passion, you will succeed."
