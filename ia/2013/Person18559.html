Bryan M. Fish's Summary

As an aspiring hotel entrepreneur, I focused a great deal of my early age reading and learning about as many aspects of entrepreneurship in the hospitality industry as I could. I have taken, and continue to take, every opportunity and challenge presented to me to learn and better myself within the hotel industry. Acquiring a business major, emphasizing entrepreneurship, from Wichita State University allowed me to begin pursuing my hotel aspirations shortly after graduation.

In the spring of 2011 I was offered the opportunity to advance my hospitality career as assistant general manager at a limited service hotel located in River Falls, Wisconsin. With the support and independence given to me from the management company I have been working for, I have gained strength in multiple areas including hotel customer service improvement, creativity in sales and marketing and revenue management. These along with the responsibilities and initiatives I have completed through my work at this property have contributed to the knowledge and growth I am eager to bring into my next position.
My goal for the future includes furthering my career in a management position at a full service hotel and eventually moving into director of operations for a corporate hotel management company.

Specialties

Improving Guest Experience, Revenue Management, Creative Sales and Marketing
