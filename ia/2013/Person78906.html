Laura Ramos' Summary

Follow me on Twitter: @lauraramos
Read my blogs: http://blogs.forrester.com/laura_ramos and www.b2bmarketingpost.com

As Vice President and Principal Analyst at Forrester Research, I write for and advise Chief Marketing Officers. I am a leading expert in business-to-business (B2B) marketing with hands-on senior management experience in corporate, industry and product marketing, demand management, social media, and customer relationship management. I have worked as a marketing practitioner and expert for more than 20 years – primarily in B2B roles at high technology companies.

I place a high premium on developing and executing marketing programs that generate measurable sales pipeline results. Core skills include developing integrated marketing programs (including direct, online, and social media) improving marketing effectiveness, brand and product positioning, public speaking, creative/analytic writing, and sales collaboration that drive customer consideration and engagement.

Prior to initiating Forrester’s B2B coverage, I advised clients in healthcare and life sciences industry. I also researched the enterprise portal and search technology markets and wrote about how businesses invest in these technologies to drive business change and collaboration.
