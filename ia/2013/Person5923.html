Amy Derr's Summary

Are you sick and tired of doing everything yourself and find that things still don't get done?!?

Who you are
You’re totally passionate about your business, you make good money through steady revenue streams and you are ready to leverage your business to take it to the next level. You are a dedicated entrepreneur with a clearly defined and proven business model, but you struggle with a full schedule and it seems that you can’t find the right amount of support you need. As a result, you have too much on your plate and you’re probably feeling that there is no light at the end of the tunnel.

What your issues tend to be
Although business is good and you’re making good money, you’re working a lot; you’re tired and overwhelmed because your to-do list is a mile long. You’re the bottleneck in the business because you have too many projects and team members to manage. Your stress level might be high and you just aren’t finding the time you need to focus on what only you can uniquely do best in your business – marketing and product development.

What you need most right now
It’s time to change how your business is managed and get you out of your own way. The focus for you right now needs to be on reducing overwhelm and creating systems and processes so things can run more smoothly. Delegation and automation will be key at this point in your business. It’s about multiplying your personal productivity and letting go of the day-to-day tasks of running your business. It’s crucial for you to let go of some of the control and get the support you need. Then, the focus needs to shift to creating more passive and leveraged income so you can serve more people than you’re currently serving, you make even more, while finally having time to develop marketing plans and new products and also having time to take care of yourself.

If this sounds like you, then I would like to invite you to connect with me for a free 30-minute assessment to find out if my services are a fit for you.
