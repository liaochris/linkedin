Chesna Closs, MSW's Summary

Chesna Closs, The Fit Esteem Expert, is a holistic fitness coach for women and girls and the creator of Fit Esteem Kids. Chesna is certified by the American Council on Exercise and has 15 years of experience as a personal trainer and fitness advocate. In 2009, she was appointed by then-Governor Jon S. Corzine to the New Jersey Council on Physical Fitness and Sports for a three-year term (2010 - 2013).

Chesna brings a unique perspective to her work in fitness, health and wellness. Prior to founding Fit Esteem LLC, Chesna spent 14 years in social services working with pregnant and parenting teens, the developmentally disabled, the economically-disadvantaged and children and their families via the Head Start Program. She received her Master’s degree in Social Work from Rutgers University in New Brunswick, New Jersey.

Chesna has a southern background and grew up singing in a gospel choir. She has experience acting, writing and producing. Her love for the performing arts, passion for empowering others and expertise as a fitness professional have allowed her to approach the world of fitness from a different angle.

In 2013, Chesna introduced her first children’s character, Jubilee Washington. Jubilee helps to teach younger children about the importance of a healthy lifestyle.
