Chris Márquez's Experience

front-end designer

Freelance UI/UX design

Sector de Graphic Design

diciembre de 2013 – Presente (2 meses) San Francisco - Los Angeles

The front-end is the layer of the system(s) interact with the user. My passion is to help create beautiful and useful products that delight the users by doing what they suppose to do, when they suppose to do it.

My strengths are in the visual design realm and even-though some people call me "artist" or "photographer" I consider myself a designer who's job is to help get the communication flowing back and forth between the product's creators and the product's users. I bring design thinking to a table and help move things forward and get them done and ready for production or testing.

I'm good at working directly with developers and engineers to get the design process to a happy development and follow up with them to make sure the design decisions are being implemented as they were intended. I can work together with executives and seniors to understand what matters to them in regards to the product and the user experience and make sure we account for what matters most to the users.

I work where Design and Engineering connect.

Many people in the tech industry talk about the sweet spot where art and technology converge; Steve Jobs, Walter Isacson, Jack Dorsey among others highlited the magic where these two intersect.

I'm the kind of designer who likes to code, I prefer being in front of a computer than being in lots of meetings and I usually get along better with Engineers than Marketeers. I understand the value of Marketing but my focus is on agile, responsive and responsible product development that goes beyond the style.css

- Make good design decisions or they will haunt you down
