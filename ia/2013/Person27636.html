Craig Watson's Summary

My 18 years+ experience in the commercial recruitment industry has allowed me to form deep and successful networks. Until now the Rec2Rec industry has failed to deliver adequately to its clients. The simple solution is that I will respond to your specific business requirements by understanding them. My philosophy is less Rec2Rec and more Rec4Rec.
In my Training and HR business I have worked with some of the largest organisations in Asia Pacific - providing HR strategic consulting aligning people and culture needs with commercial requirements.

I have a proven background in Rec2Rec, HR Strategic Management, Career Training and Recruitment, Senior Management (National Level) with extensive People Management and Training/Mentoring experience.
Over a career spanning 15+ Years I have gained my experience at HMS (UK), Adecco, Hallis, Drake, Accel and most recently my own venture - HeadStart.
I am at cutting edge of Rec2Rec, Training, HR and Recruitment best practice - with additional experience in Organisational Development, Retention, Reward and Recognition Initiatives.
In the current recruitment industry landscape of various cultures, expectations and job descriptions, allow me to help you in navigating your next career move.
I have consulted to leading organisations as to HR solutions strategy across a breadth of industries - aligning expectation with reality.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry

May 2010 – Present (3 years 8 months)

Scott Recruitment Services was the first Recruitment-to-Recruitment company in Australia. The business began in Sydney in 1992 and since then has opened branches in Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Canberra with associates in New Zealand and the UK.

Our approach is to be Talent Managers. For our clients this means not only locating the most suitable individuals to fill their vacancies but also providing a range of related services to optimise their performance. This includes business advice, staff training and selling and/or acquiring businesses.

With our candidates our approach is to manage your career from the beginning to end, not only looking at your immediate needs but providing advice that will build your career over a sustained period.

To raise the profile and maintain the standards of the industry, Scott Recruitment Services is a corporate member of the RCSA and takes an active role in its operation and development. Rosemary Scott, our Managing Director, is also an Honorary Life Member of the RCSA, a testament to her dedication and service to the industry.

All of our consultants have worked directly in the recruitment industry and with over 100 years of combined local experience in the Australian marketplace we are able to provide you not only with a sound recruitment service but an enormous amount of market place knowledge.

If you are interested in finding out how partnering with Scott Recruitment Services can be of assistance to you, please contact us.
