Personal Property Broker | International Business Consultant

Exchange Pro, LLC (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; International Trade and Development industry

September 2008 – Present (5 years 4 months) Baltimore, Maryland Area

Exchange Pro, LLC is an international personal property brokerage based in Baltimore, MD, USA. We serve the entire U.S., including Alaska and Hawaii as well as a moderate level of international clients. Our goal is to help consumers, businesses, and government agencies perform capital transactions with other parties in a fast, convenient, profitable, and efficient manner. We deal with a wide array of consumer goods and property including cars, trucks, boats, aircraft, heavy machinery, furniture, artwork, antiques and collectibles, appliances, and domain names. As the founder, I endeavored to (1) bridge the gap between surplus goods and surplus buyers in the marketplace, (2) help expedite the flow of goods to improve the national economy, and (3) facilitate personal property transactions between buyers and sellers to provide each with a convenient way to perform valuable transactions with other parties. Exchange Pro has proven that its service is not just valuable, but has the potential to impact the world in a positive way, simply by coordinating trade between businesses, governments, and individuals throughout the world.

Global Developer

Global Innovations and Development (GI&D)

February 2001 – Present (12 years 11 months) Baltimore, Maryland Area

Global Innovations and Development is a company committed to wealth accumulation, and the development of international communities through the deployment of financial, human, and other resources. In a nut shell, GI&D is the parent company for all the individual projects that I, and my associates operate. This includes such subsidiaries as DGN Coaching, Exchange Pro LLC, and Children's Bread. GI&D, therefore, is essentially a framework for developing profitable businesses, and using a portion of the proceeds from those profitable businesses to reach out to people and communities in need.

The business relies on the principles of faith and Christianity as the foundations of our global strategy. The goal is ultimately to minister to the world spiritually and materially, as a faith-based business, geared toward Kingdom advancement.
