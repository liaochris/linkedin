Bruce Arbit's Summary

Founded in 2006 by Bruce Arbit, Melarbit Partners, Inc. is an independent fundraising consulting and community investing firm. We use our experience in fundraising and innovations in philanthropy and community investing to help mission-based enterprises, entrepreneurs and investors leverage the catalytic role of private philanthropy in order to create opportunity, change lives and build community.

My work is influenced by over 25+ years of professional fundraising, marketing and organizational capacity building experiences in the private and not-for-profit sectors and is continuously refined through consulting engagements, civic and personal commitments. Current affiliations and associations include: New York University’s Catherine B. Reynolds Foundation Program in Social Entrepreneurship; The Center for Excellence in Governance, Leadership and Philanthropy at Fairleigh Dickinson University; The Community Investing Network of NJ; The Prospect Finder; and Ventureneer.

Specialties:
1. Strategy Development and Fundraising Business Planning
2. Marketing and Organizational Positioning
3. Raise/Attract/Leverage Capital
4. Convene Funders/Investors/Thought Leaders
