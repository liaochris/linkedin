Michael Shelton's Summary

Michael has 20 years as Product Manager of manufactured goods, international sourcing, with the proven ability to work with all areas within a distribution channel. His professional character and customer service are highlighted by his technical ability to understand varying products along with his experience with global factories and North American customers. He has lead divisions in the implementation of new processes and record keeping systems to improve productivity as well as instrumental in the launch of multiple product categories. Interested in program/product development, technical sales, supplier management and purchasing.
