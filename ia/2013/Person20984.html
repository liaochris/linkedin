Catherine Sherwood's Summary

Catherine Sherwood is a consultant specializing in social media, strategic marketing and common-sense search engine optimization. Her insights and expertise are backed by 20+ years of business experience at the senior executive level.

Catherine’s combined executive and consulting experience includes analyzing markets for new product entry, specifying market requirements, developing marketing messages and sales strategies, building key partnerships, and creating marketing and client retention programs. She has launched dozens of new products and new business lines for start-ups, mid-sized and large companies, both public and privately held.

She is a strong proponent of using social media to transform how businesses approach marketing, and committed to helping her clients engagge successfully with the social media navigate this rapidly growing, often chaotic world. Catherine integrates the new social media paradigm with her solid executive experience to help her clients make better products, improve their support and delivery systems, grow their brands, and increase their sales.

Catherine has also developed and implemented social media programs for political candidates that reinforce political messaging, create a more personal connection between voters and the candidate, improve volunteer recruitment, and support an overall GOTV strategy.
