Dr. Daniel S. Smith, D.C.'s Summary

Genesa is creating a new paradigm of Philanthropy meets Entrepreneurship. Genesa Living Foundation, which educates about sustainable agriculture and micronutrient solutions is funded by Genesa Living, which provides the best amino acid product in the marketplace: Total Amino Solution™

We’re in the midst of a huge and growing paradigm shift in our approach to health, with growing interest in taking control of our own wellness and prevention, and extending longevity.

Dr. Dan believes that education is critical.

He believes consumers, families and communities can and should revolutionize their own wellness by educating themselves about how they fuel their bodies ... from fresh, local, seasonal offerings to micronutrient supplements.

Dr. Dan believes in educating and empowering all of us so we can take responsibility for our own wellness.

He is clearly representative of the shifting health paradigm, one that encourages individuals and communities to be uniquely interested and qualified to extend the quantity and quality of their lives.
