Owner & Founder

X-Change Studio

Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Architecture & Planning industry

January 2010 – August 2012 (2 years 8 months)

The X-Change Studio is an online design studio created to provide a community-based network for firms and individuals related to the Architectural, Design and Construction industries. Within this network community, we provide the necessary tools for the “x-change” of ideas, resources, services, and knowledge to encourage and aid the collaboration of design and production talent with Architectural/Design firms on architectural and interior design projects of all sizes. The X-Change Studio provides the framework for creating a virtual team of online design and production talent that can collectively work on projects.

The X-Change Studio‘s open, collaborative environment allows the pairing of any specific needs required to round out a project team, or even the ability to assemble an entire virtual, distributed project team of independent Architects, Designers, CAD/Production experts, Engineers, Graphic Artists, Renderers/3D Modelers, Project/Construction Managers, and even Administrative Support and financial management for any size project… No matter where the project and individual team members are located!
