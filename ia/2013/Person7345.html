Andy Bagley's Summary

I am a highly professional consultant, facilitator, trainer and author with more than twenty years experience in my field.

My passion lies in understanding the way that individuals and organisations behave, and using customised management methods to influence these behaviours, create positive change and improve performance. This means understanding each organisation as a unique entity and applying improvement techniques in unique ways - ways that work in the Real World.

Originally from the public sector, my consulting experience spans public, private and voluntary sector organisations. My clients include local and central government, housing associations, police, the health sector, third sector organisations and private companies of many kinds.

Until 2008, I was a Director of TEAL Consulting Ltd, and I retain an association with the company as well as doing other freelance work. Real-Improvement gives me the chance to work in my own right, and with selected colleagues who share my beliefs and approach.

Specialties

I specialise in:
1) Performance Management
- Whole-organisation models including Balanced Scorecard & EFQM Excellence Model
- 'Making it happen' - planning and practical implementation frameworks
2) Performance Measurement & Evaluation
"What gets measured gets done" - performance measures that actively drive change and inspire improvement as well as track progress.
3) Social Return on Investment (SROI)
I also work with leadership, people and process techniques that support organisational change.
