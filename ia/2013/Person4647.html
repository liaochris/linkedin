Alison Kelly's Summary

At Delano Designs our mission is to:
Create beautiful, healthy, and functional home and workspaces that make our clients feel good;
Create these interiors while paying close attention to the budget and to options for materials that are eco-friendly, healthy and sustainable;
Create these interiors in a spirit of collaboration and open communication.

More specifically, we offer a variety of services from an hour or two of consultation regarding design and materials, to designing a complete remodel or new construction.

We now make organic crib bedding as well, so that parents can create a beautiful, fun and healthy nursery for their baby!

Specialties

We specialize in green design and staying current in this area. We can provide you with a variety of options for healthier, eco-friendly materials.

We also can help you redesign tired furniture or design new furniture and cabinetry to fit your spaces perfectly.

We make organic crib bedding and will customize if you have something particular in mind!
