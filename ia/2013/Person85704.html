Maria Lago's Summary

Maria Lago recieved a B.A. in Communications from St. John's University and is currently the president and owner of 3PR, a public relations agency specializing in social media marketing, beauty, fashion and lifestyle brands.

A passionate advocate for animal rights, Maria volunteers for the SPCA of Westchester, a local animal shelter as well having created an event to benefit the organization. With community business participation she raised funds and awareness for the no-kill shelter. In addition, she now heads the Twitter and Instagram accounts for the organization.

Maria mixes traditional PR practices with new media to create her campaigns bringing her clients' message to the forefront. Specializing in Beauty and Fashion PR, Maria's knowledge of the industries parallel her expertiese in the field of public relations.
