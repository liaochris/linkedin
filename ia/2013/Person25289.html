The Deep Water Leaf Society

2008 – 2012 (4 years)

Grief Support and Coaching Services.

I provide a gentle, creative pathway through grief into healing using dreamwork, expressive arts and journaling to explore and heal the many emotions experienced after the loss of a loved one.

My approach is based on my own personal experience with grief and healing after my son died of a drug overdose in 2004. You can read about my journey in my award-winning book, The Deep Water Leaf Society: Harnessing the Transformative Power of Grief (Intuitive Journey Press 2008).

My techniques are grounded in professional training: I am a certified Creative Journal Expressive Arts Instructor (trained by Lucia Capacchione, PhD, ATR), Transformational Life Coach (Southwest Institute of Healing Arts) and Level II Dream Teacher (trained by Robert Moss).
