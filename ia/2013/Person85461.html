Marc Whitehead's Summary

Disability attorney. Founding partner of Marc Whitehead & Associates, established in 1992 in Houston, Texas. We help individuals with Long Term Disability & ERISA, Bad Faith Insurance, Social Security (SSI) and Veterans Disability law.

Originally from Memphis, Tennessee, and raised in Normangee, Texas. Graduated from Normangee High School as class valedictorian.

Attended Texas A&M University and graduated in 1989 with a Bachelors of Business Administration in Finance, then attended the University of Houston Law Center and received his law degree (J.D.) in 1992, graduating in the top quarter of his class. Admitted to the State Bar of Texas in 1992 and is admitted to practice before all U.S. District Courts in Texas, the United States Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims and the United States Court of Appeals-Fifth Circuit.

Served as President of the Houston Trial Lawyers Association (2009-2010), member of the Board of Directors of the Texas Trial Lawyers Association, and the American Association of Justice. Member of the Texas Aggie Bar Association, the Texas Association of Civil Trial and Appellate Specialists and the Houston Volunteer Lawyers Association. Past Chairman of the Houston Bar Association Social Security Section and is a frequent lecturer on the topic of Social Security and Disability Law.

Specialties

Long Term Disability & ERISA, Bad Faith Insurance, Social Security (SSI) and Veterans Disability law.
