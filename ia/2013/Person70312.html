Judi Glova's Summary

Judi Glova brings more than 20 years of experience in the corporate world to her firm, Pinnacle Coaching & Consulting, LLC (a woman owned business). Working at a senior level for some large corporations in the United States she has led, managed, mentored and coached teams and individuals to achieve award-winning outcomes.

Judi has parlayed her unique skill set to create a successful coaching, consulting and training business and segued into working with other hi-tech industries as well. With core competencies in problem solving, strategic agility, emotional intelligence, effective communication, personal branding and executive transitioning, Judi has helped clients accelerate their success and produce more satisfying results in their careers. These strengths combined with her high energy, direct and forward-looking approach to strategic career development make Judi a distinctive specialist sought out by innovative companies that value human potential and entrepreneurs who want to excel.
