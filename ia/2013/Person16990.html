Brentwood SoberLiving's Summary

Brentwood Sober Living for Men was designed to create the most conducive, and exclusive environment for those transitioning from residential inpatient treatment back into their new, addiction-free lives. Our experienced, well trained staff will coordinate and work closely with our guests’ primary treatment professionals and staff, to facilitate each guests’ transition during this critical phase of their development. From our vast experience in the field of recovery we found the need to design a stress-free environment where privacy, serenity, and anonymity are at a premium. Brentwood Sober Living for Men works with only six guests at one time, ensuring that each guest will receive the upmost in time, attention, and direction required to confidently embark on the spiritual voyage of an addiction-free life.

At Brentwood Sober Living, our primary goal is to assist all our guests in embracing this wonderful journey in sobriety. We specialize in providing a safe and sober environment for guests who seek the importance of privacy. Even though guests leaving treatment face an uphill battle in the fight against relapse, sober living offers recovering individuals a better chance at long-term recovery from their addiction. The experienced staff at Brentwood Sober Living offers everything needed to make rehabilitation successful and sobriety matter. By emphasizing luxury sober living at every turn we aim to make recovery a celebration of life.

After leaving treatment, the continued care at Brentwood Sober Living for Men helps guests to facilitate treatment goals to create a successful life in sobriety. Individual strategies of support and treatment plans are customized and enforced by a wide range of treatment professionals allowing our residents to address any obstacles they face and embrace the healing process. Please feel free to contact us any time for information regarding the admission process, availability and professional referrals. Brentwood Sober Living for Men is a private pay facility. Fees include lodging, food, local transportation, cleaning services and gym membership. Specialized clinical and medical services are considered ancillary and will be billed separately. Although we are not a licensed facility, we do have affiliations with a wide range of local professionals for referral should any specific needs arise during a guest’s stay at Brentwood Sober Living for Men.
