Amelia Barrett's Summary

Amelia Barrett is the owner/broker of Barrett Property Management. She is a licensed California Real Estate Broker, California Real Estate Appraiser, and a Certified Real Property Tax Appraiser. With her vast experience in the real estate industry, Amelia has become a successful business owner. Her passion for property management exuberates through the superior services she delivers to tenants/lessees, property owners, investors, and industry associates alike. At Barrett Property Management, Amelia focuses ONLY on property management services so that she can deliver the most superior services to her clients. She specializes in commercial property management and residential property management and placement services.

Prior to forming Barrett Property Management, Amelia worked for the County of Nevada appraising commercial and residential real estate properties for appeals hearings and property tax purposes.

Amelia began her career in the real estate industry in 2005 by providing “a new wave” of real estate support services to local top producing Realtors such as Mark Weyman. She introduced the internet-era of catchy websites, syndicated online listings, and top-notch promotional print and web advertising services to not only her customers, but to the Nevada County real estate industry as a whole. Shortly thereafter, she obtained her real estate salesperson license, and then later obtained her real estate appraisal license to better her knowledge of the real estate industry.

Amelia attended colleges in Bend, Oregon and Sacramento, California studying Business Administration and Construction Management. She grew up in Nevada County and graduated from Nevada Union High School.
