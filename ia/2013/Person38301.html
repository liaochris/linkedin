Education:
Received Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from New York University College of Dentistry in 1979 after earning B.A. from State University of New York at Albany.

Completed a general practice residency at Albany Medical Center Hospital while also serving as assistant instructor in the Department of Surgery.

Instructor at New York University College of Dentistry while practicing in New York and served on staff at Maimonides and Southside Hospitals.

Ongoing continuing education including a number of surgical and prosthetic training programs on Osseo integrated implants, among them a 330 hour maxi course provided by the Medical College of Georgia and the American Academy of Implant Dentistry. Extensively trained and experienced in esthetic dentistry, Dr. Schwartz has been veneering teeth since 1979.
