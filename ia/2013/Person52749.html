Henrik Hylleberg Luxhøj's Summary

Henrik H. Luxhøj (Country Manager) is responsible for the Dutch Mat & Washroom Business Line and member of the Board of Directors in Berendsen Netherlands. Berendsen Netherlands is part of Berendsen plc, which is Europe's leading textile service company and noted on London Stock Exchange.

I joined Berendsen in 2006, and I have held management positions in three different countries (The Netherlands, Germany and Norway) as well as in Berendsen’s International HQ. I also participated in formulating the Berendsen Group strategy in 2010.

I am responsible for 140 service minded employees, and together we take pride in providing the entire country with high quality mat and washroom services from our plants in Ede, Zaandam and a number of depots throughout the country.
In my current position I execute a strategy of profitable growth, and I am determined to make Berendsen the leader on the Dutch mat and washroom service market. In alignment with my ambition, Berendsen in November 2012 acquired Groene Team. I give the lead in the careful integration of the Groene Team organisation into Berendsen.

I have proven knowledge of acquisition negotiation, post acquisition integration, turnaround management, formulating Group strategy, and executing a profitable growth strategy locally.

I hold two master degrees. One from Copenhagen Business School and one from CEMS. My specialties are Strategy and International Management.

Accomplished results:
- The Dutch organisation has under my leadership delivered double digit organic growth in both top and bottomline.
- Closed two acquisitions in 2012.
- Closed a partnership agreement with the market leading facility service company in the Netherlands.
- I received the honor of being awarded one of the top 100 business talents in Denmark by Berlingske Nyhedsmagazin in 2010.
