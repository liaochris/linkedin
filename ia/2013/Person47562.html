Mehran MD's Summary

GEESOO® AESTHETIC is one of the world's most experienced skin care and hair restoration clinics, successfully treating men and women with skin care and hair loss issues since 1998. The staff at Geesoo® is devoted full time to performing the latest proven techniques in skin and hair care.

GEESOO® SKINCARE At Geesoo® skincare, the latest proven technologies are implemented using the expertise and experience of a seasoned staff to give you the most desirable results. Each person and skin is unique and requires individual evaluation and attention, using only the most proven treatments for you. In your individual session, we take the time to thoroughly evaluate your skin type, your needs, and your desire for outcome. Geesoo® treatments are implemented to meet your satisfaction. Once in the Geesoo® care family, your concerns and questions are always promptly addressed. Geesoo® skincare services include: Skin cleansing treatments, Skin rejuvenation treatments, Revitalizing skin peals, Microdermabrasion, IPL (Intense Pulse Light), Facial massage, Thermage treatments, Hair removal, Botox®, Dermal fillers (Juvederm, Restylane, Radiesse), and much, much more.

GEESOO® HAIR RESTORATION is performed using Ultra Refined Follicular Unit Hair Transplantation (UR-FUT) Surgery, the gold standard. UR-FUT involves relocating (transplanting) “permanent” hair follicles from the back of the head to the balding areas on the top and front of the head. This process is effective and will last a lifetime because the hair follicles taken from the back of the head are genetically resistant to baldness, no matter where they are transplanted. These transplanted hair follicles will continue to grow hair for a life time. Our exclusive technique provides aesthetically pleasing, superior, and natural looking results. We have performed hundreds of undetectable procedures with excellent results. Many performed on well-known and public personalities.
