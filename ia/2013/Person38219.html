Paul Cullen's Summary

I am a counsellor and psychotherapist and the director of Real People Counselling, a Melbourne based counselling service.

I also have a private practice in the Melbourne CBD where people consult me for a variety of reasons, including:

*General support
*Mental health issues (e.g. anxiety and depression)
*Trauma
*Grief
*Anger management
*Relationship difficulties (family members, friends, colleagues)
*Assistance in finding ways to live a more satisfying life
*Self development

If you are interested in undertaking counselling or psychotherapy please don't hesitate to contact me by phone or email.
