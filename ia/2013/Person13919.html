Brenda Ezell's Summary

Practice includes representation of: landowners and developers in the purchase and sale of land for commercial, retail and residential development; landlords and tenants in complex commercial leasing matters; borrowers and lenders in commercial real estate and other asset-based commercial lending transactions, including financing for the establishment and rehabilitation of elderly, low-income and affordable multifamily housing; owners and developers in land use and zoning and other county and local government regulatory matters. Also providing services involving title insurance, escrow and closing, business law and corporate law (including formation, acquisition, dissolution and compliance), and probate and estate planning.

Specialties: Real estate transactions including acquisition, disposition, leasing, lending, securitization and property rights, including easements, encroachments, and covenants and restrictions.
