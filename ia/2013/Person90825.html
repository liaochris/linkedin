Matthew Hall's Patents

  • Methods And Systems For Detecting Suspected Data Leakage Using Traffic Samples

    • United States Patent Application PCT/US2010/054131
    • Filed 2010
    Inventors: Matthew Hall, Reinoud Koornstra

    Covers novel algorithms for uncovering leaked data using traffic sampling as part of a DLP (data loss prevention) strategy for an enterprise LAN with sFlow or another traffic sampling technology.

  • In-Kernel Network Traffic Sampling

    • United States Patent Application PCT/US2011/028043
    • Filed 2011
    Inventors: Matthew Hall, Reinoud Koornstra, Kevin Worth

    Covering the use of loadable modules in the source and/or destination of network packets to accomplish network traffic sampling without the need for sampling support from the routers and switches. By conducting sampling within the
    kernel, a network administrator can gain visibility into encrypted traffic and traffic within virtualized environments that would not otherwise be visible through switch-based sampling.

  • Identifying Potential Account Numbers in a Single Pass of a List of Digits

    • United States Patent Application PCT/US2011/022709
    • Filed 2011
    Inventors: Matthew Hall, Reinoud Koornstra, Miranda Mowbray

    Covers a novel streaming version of the Luhn algorithm for efficiently
    identifying Credit Card numbers and Bank Account numbers within arbitrary binary data more efficiently than using regular expressions or other more popular methods.

    Presented at the Joint IFIP TC6 and TC11 Conference on Communications and Multimedia Security - CMS 2011.
