Frank J. Davies, CHS-IV, CIPS,CVI's Projects

  • School Dangers - Crowdfunding project to bring Safety & Security awareness to K-12

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Frank J. Davies, CHS-IV, CIPS,CVI

    U.S. Dept. of Education reports over 1.8 million criminal incidents occur in K-12 schools every year (30x higher than higher ed).

    Surprised? You hear news about security improvements, awareness and accountability at colleges and universities. Unfortunately, the laws that make this happen (like the Clery Act) do not apply to K-12 and recent Presidential directives do little to change this course.

    *** The Rush To "Do Something" ***
    As a result of press exposure from recent horrific criminal incidents, most of the schools we see are in a rush to "do something". Unfortunately, there is little oversight and lots of opinions. This results in wasted time & money on efforts that don’t work because they are applied incorrectly or insufficiently.

    *** What Can The Average Person Do? ***
    Looking at the past 23 years, shows us the path to securing the 42 million children in K-12 is a program of consistent awareness and accountability (just like what we have in colleges and universities).

    So we looked at what we could do to make a change & we are in the process of building the following:

    1. Provide a free mobile application that empowers everyone to report
    when they see something wrong at school.

    2. Let parents, students, teachers & officials see what are types of incidents
    are being reported. (While protecting sensitive information)

    3. Once a month, deliver a summary of reported incidents to state officials
    who can & should do something about it.

    This simple program allows the average person to point out the safety and security shortcomings that still exist in our nation’s schools. If we can't measure it, then we can't fix it.

    *** With Your Help We Will Build It ***
    The principals of Aella Consulting Group have launched a crowdfunding campaign to develop this program.

    http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/school-dangers-mobile-app/x/3252692

    Donations are appreciated but spreading the word is just as important. Please contact me to get involved.
