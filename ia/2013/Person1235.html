Adam Cuzzort's Projects

  • Synthesis Collaboration Platform

    • June 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Adam Cuzzort, Dino Carlos, Josh Hurst, Brandon Fischer, Ryan Ricci

    Synthesis is a cutting-edge, multi-touch collaboration platform that enables the highest levels of visualization of information, storytelling of complex material, flexibility of content and connectivity to remote colleagues.

    Using Synthesis, people are able to connect and work on information-heavy projects at the speed of thought. Synthesis provides a large-scale multitouch environment in which users can interact with their data and documents, make annotations on that data, then share it with hundreds of colleagues around the world through integrated video-conferencing and Synthesis' proprietary connectivity between endpoints.

    Synthesis is developed in-house at Anacore and is one of our core product offerings. I was the lead architect and developer on Synthesis and continue to help shape it into a product that provides high value to our clients.

  • MediaStation Digital Signage Platform

    • February 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Adam Cuzzort, Dino Carlos, Josh Hurst, Ryan Ricci, Brandon Fischer, James Gilchrist

    MediaStation is a feature-rich, easy-to-use digital signage platform that gives organizations the ability to display a wide variety of digital content on distributed screens. At Anacore, our focus is always on simple, beautiful software that communicates information in the most effective ways. With MediaStation, we have created a platform to do just that--communicate through beautiful simplicity.

    Also, with our strong background in custom development, we can easily customize the MediaStation platform to integrate with other business apps and data source, as well as create unique interactive or visual experiences to compliment our out-of-the-box functionality.

    I served as lead architect and developer for MediaStation and continue to help shape it into an industry-leading digital communication platform.

  • Triage Assessment Engine

    • May 2008 to Present
    Team Members: Adam Cuzzort, Brandon Fischer, James Gilchrist

    Triage is an intelligent assessment / survey platform that allows for easy creation of dynamic, intelligent surveys. It includes a decision tree engine that allows for surveys to tailor themselves to the user, providing increased accuracy and shortening the overall time required to take the survey.

    Triage is very media-rich and contains green-screen videos of presenters that walk through the survey content. This engages audiences and helps them move through the survey while receiving feedback that is relevant to their responses.

    Triage is part of Anacore's catalog of products that helps people better communicate and learn. I lead the architecture and development for Triage and am also a co-applicant on a US patent for our work on the decision engine that powers it.
