Shane Gramling's Summary

Shane Gramling has had a knack for art since as far back as he can remember, and he still recalls being fascinated specifically with modern architecture and sculpture during his childhood. During fifth grade he was exclusively chosen to attend a class of his choice at the Flint Institute of Arts by art instructor Patricia Morello. Morello also selected one of Shane’s paintings to be featured in a gallery at the Bower Theater. As you can imagine, this kind of recognition gave great hope to a shy kid growing up in a low-income, single-parent home on the east side of Flint.

Today, Shane provides a whole host of services from product photography and copywriting, to annual reports, website development, brochure design, printing solutions, branding, and more.

Shane's business approach is no-nonsense, while his creative approach comes by positioning himself as uninfluenced by current trends in popular culture. He looks past trends, and focuses on target audience interaction, timeless design, and solutions that deliver results.
