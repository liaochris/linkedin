Wolfgang Bühn's Summary

MEVAS is specialized in Heavy Equipment inspections. With a network of European engineers we perform appraisals for used machinery such as earthmovers, cranes, crushers, tractors and trucks.

With top-level inspections we reduce the risk for buyers and importers of machiery. We support sales activities of machinery suppliers with an unbiased expertise of machine condition.
