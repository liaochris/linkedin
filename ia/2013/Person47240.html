Gary Jennings' Summary

Result driven, proficient and highly motivated International Sales, Marketing and Managing Director with over 18 years experience in the international arena.

A dynamic business leader and key sales team member, he has a proven ability to deliver significantglobal sales growth through leading field sales teams and distributors. Develops and implements sales and marketing strategies to identify and capitalise current and future business opportunities. Utilises resources and liaises with stakeholders to support the strategy, meet KPI’s and drive a strong P&L. Builds productive customer, colleague and KOL relationships at all levels and cultures. Energetic, confident, with professional communication, negotiation and presentation skills.


KEY SKILLS AND COMPETENCIES

Formulate, execute and manage robust sales and marketing strategies, by customer, segment and territory, establish KPI’s and drive scalable, sustainable, profitable EMEA sales to plan.
Manage and monitor key accounts, operational plans, pricing strategies by segment, budgets and P&L.
Manage, coach, direct and monitor field sales and in-territory managers and teams against performance targets.
Select, appoint, manage and drive sales through international commercial agents and distributors.
Exploit product USP’s, innovation and resources; feedback market, customer and competitor intelligence to drive advantage.
EMEA market development; market entry, product launches and product life cycle management.


PERSONAL SKILLS

Customer oriented and widely travelled; understands, adapts and works with global cultures.
Innovative and ambitious with a consultative approach; quick to understand product and market dynamics.
Leadership with a high emotional intelligence; enjoys being a team player, comfortable managing from the front.
Growth mindset; Business Unit/Board Level experience and commercial acumen to deliver against plan.
