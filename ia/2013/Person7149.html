Andrew Wilcox's Summary

A passionate and committed senior executive and leader with detailed knowledge of delivering IT services and solutions and a proven track of delivering results. Well versed in directing, motivating and supporting sales activities and possessing a rare ability to understand and operate within both the technical and commercial domains. Leads from the front and an excellent team player with detailed knowledge of public sector and blue light including procurement policy, methods and approaches.

Key achievements:

• Defined a marketing strategy and execution plan, together with a five year growth projection for a newly formed division focusing on public sector business that won contracts delivering 80% revenue growth in two years.

• Built and lead a highly successful and motivated team that delivered against budgetary targets in excess of £5M in year one whilst maintaining excellent customer service through delivery and support functions, gaining repeat business.

• Built strategic relationships with a number of public sector bodies and tier one primes that delivered an opportunity pipeline exceeding £20M in revenues.

• Gained multiple new customers within the public sector including several direct relationships and partnerships with tier one primes and delivered new business in excess of £10M.

• Redefined the approach to pre-sales and proposals creating a proposal capability encompassing a Shipley based house style, a qualification and production process, and training resources, delivering a win rate in excess of 40% and a short listing rate exceeding 90%.


Specialties: Strategy, public sector, procurement, government frameworks, relationship development, software and service delivery, team building, telecommunications.
