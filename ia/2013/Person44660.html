Jeff Passantino's Summary

After Jeff’s initial visit to Sanibel Island over 20 years ago he knew there was something special about the beaches and ongoing preservation that only Sanibel and SWFL can offer. In 2004, he took a leap of faith and relocated from Kansas City to Southwest Florida.

Jeff graduated from Kansas State University with a Bachelor of Architecture. After college Jeff started his architectural career in the health care industry, designing over 24 elder care facilities throughout the midwest. He was not only the lead designer, but also managed oversight of the land acquisitions and building construction for the new facilities.

In 2004, Jeff and his wife, Elizabeth, and their three children, Jeffrey, Cassie, and Ella were ready for year-round subtropical weather, and life near the beach. Sanibel Island proved to be the destination of choice! Jeff immediately entered into the real estate market, and utilized his knowledge of Architecture/Construction/Land Acquisition to help other folks make informed decisions when purchasing their piece of paradise. Jeff quickly became successful in the real estate business in Southwest Florida, and was most recently honored by RE/MAX International as a member of the Executive Club. This award recognizes agents who exemplify professionalism and provide top quality service.

Jeff’s past clients have described Jeff as: responsive, accessible, knowledgeable about the market conditions, and always hard working. Jeff’s design and development background coupled with his communication skills are a winning combination for a successful and smooth transaction.
