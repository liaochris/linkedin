Michele Perry's Summary

Published author. Fine Artist, Photographer and Illustrator. Executive Coach. Corporate Creativity Consultant. Serial Starter of New Things. Unabashed dream wrangler. Paradigm Iconoclast.

After almost 7 years of pioneering a work in South Sudan that cares for and assists hundreds of at risk and vulnerable women and children, I have successfully transitioned the project's leadership to its established leadership team. I continue to serve as a consultant on an as needed basis. {http://irissouthsudan.org}.

There is nothing I love more than to see the people around me succeed… wildly. I love it so much I launched my own consulting firm {http://edgecreativeconsulting.com} to help the amazing leaders, creatives, artists and innovators I meet do just that.

My intuitive visual coaching approach blends the practical know-how of a skilled business consultant with the creativity of an artist and the perception and acuity of a personal coach. It is particularly tailored for executives, C-Level leaders and those who would like to develop a legacy of leadership that embraces authenticity, sustainability, innovation and creativity as core values.

Drawing on a broad base of understanding in both for-profit and not-for-profit sectors grounded in over 15 years of cross-cultural experience, unique creative expertise, as well as my signature LifeCanvas™ Visual Coaching method, I am pioneering original, art-based, applied learning techniques that equip and engage leaders and their teams to excel in times of change.

Redefining possible. Everyday.

Specialties: Executive & Creativity Coaching, Writing, Creativity & Innovation, Leadership Development, Photography, Cross Cultural Communication & Conflict Resolution, Sustainable business models that create wealth and ownership in developing world contexts, Copy writing, Branding strategy & development, Motivational speaking, Art/Illustration, Entrepreneurship/Start-up Development
