Founder

Your Virtual Sherpa (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Information Services industry

May 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months) Fairhaven MA

Many small businesses are realizing that they must transfer a lot of their business management to computer and internet tools, but if your expertise is selling properties, meeting law clients, designing landscapes, or fixing people’s plumbing, you probably find the thought of learning this new technology overwhelming.

And it often may be impractical to hire a full-time IT person or relying on your nephew who’s in high school to put such an important system together. There are times when you need to add special expertise to your staff to get you off on the right foot. This is where a specialized virtual assistant services can come in very handy. Based in southeastern Massachusetts, Your Virtual Sherpa is not limited by geography to assist you in finding the right software to handle the critical aspects of your business operations: business management software, customer relationship management (CRM), content creation and management for your website, SEO (search engine optimization), social media campaigns, taxonomy development for content management systems, and many other virtual tasks. Additionally, Your Virtual Sherpa has expertise in Quickbooks and nonprofit finance, nonprofit governance and meeting IRS filing requirements.

Why use virtual assistant services?

*You save yourself the time and costs involved in a new hire that you are not certain you will need long-term
*You save yourself the costs of providing competitive benefits
*You save yourself the payroll tax expense you normally share with an employee
*You have access to an individual who has breadth and depth of general knowledge in many types of organizations, from public employment, private corporations, and not-for-profits
*You have a guide on your side to evaluate the different options available so you don’t have to rely solely on a salesperson’s pitch to make a decision.

When facing mountains rapidly changing technology that need to be conquered, think “Your Virtual Sherpa”.
