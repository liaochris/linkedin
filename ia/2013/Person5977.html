Amy Kraft's Summary

Seasoned Producer, Writer, and Game Designer with over twelve years experience making fun, interactive, educational experiences for children ages 1 to 10. Recent work includes Magic School Bus: Oceans and I Spy Super Challenger on the Leapster Explorer, the NOOK Kids line of electronic picture books, eBook scriptwriting, front-end development on the Scholastic Parents website, Wii game design, and a line of math workbooks. Previous award-winning work has included web game design, Leap Frog platforms, CD-ROMs, interactive DVDs, audio CDs, a board game, books and other print media, Nintendo handhelds, and classroom experiences. Extensive track record of meeting schedules, ranging from 1 week to 14 months, and meeting budgets, ranging from $5K to $1.4M.

Specialties

Game design, creative brainstorming, application of curriculum and brand to various media, document and script writing, vendor relationships, creating and maintaining budgets and schedules, product presentations, industry trends and research
