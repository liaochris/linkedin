Helena Bouchez's Summary

Helena Bouchez is a business storyteller and communications consultant known for her ability to help clients clearly define their point of view and articulate with a distinct and compelling voice what makes them truly unique.

She has helped dozens of clients uncover and activate a unique point of view that enables more natural and effective business conversations and consistent creation of content that is interesting, insightful, and relevant to their ideal clients.

An expert editor and writing coach, Helena helps clients create a strategic, sustainable content strategy; create engaging, well-written content such as articles, blog posts, resumes and cover letters; identify speaking opportunities at industry events and prepare winning proposals; and initiate key relationships via Twitter, LinkedIn and other social networks.

Helena specializes in communications coaching and consulting for creative professional services firms including (but not limited to) marketing, branding, design and digital technology.

Rather than acting as your intermediary, Helena helps you hone your internal capabilities and maximize use of bandwidth so that you can connect more consistently with those in a position to help you build your business.

Helena holds a bachelor of arts in Art from Wayne State University and studied industrial design at Center for Creative Studies, in Detroit. She also earned a certificate in business administration, emphasis on small business strategic planning, from the Institute for Entrepreneurial Studies at the University of Illinois at Chicago. She is currently undertaking professional coach training through ICF accredited coach training program, Mentor Coach and expects to become certified in the spring of 2014.

Specialties

Coaching, consulting, mentoring, communications coaching, content marketing, communications coaching and consulting for professional services firms, creative firms, consulting and internal training. Profile raising, thought leadership. Expertise positioning, expertise PR, public relations, PR coaching, social media, media relations, bylined articles, book editing, website design, website search optimization, project management, speaking programs, speaker placement, proposals and awards.
