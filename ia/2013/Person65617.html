Joe Menth's Projects

  • Whidbey GeoDome Project

    • January 2012 to April 2012
    Team Members: Joe Menth, Matt McDowell

    The Whidbey GeoDome is an immersive learning environment designed to explore and discover what it takes to increase the health of our communities, our bioregions, and our planet. It inspires participants to ask questions about humanity's place in the cosmos, as well as our role in sustaining life on Earth.

    I designed and developed a site to effectively reach K-12 and college-level educators and students, corporate donors and leadership development coaches.

    The site features search functionality, compelling information that can be easily shared with others, and an easily-browsed listing of WGP program offerings. Online donations can be collected, and links from the site to social networking accounts enable visitors to share content easily.

    An important goal for the site was to translate some of the immersive and transformational experience of the GeoDome to a website design that is visually compelling and easily navigable. This was accomplished in part through the use of stunning universe and Earth imagery.
