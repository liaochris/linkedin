Jason Ling's Summary

Jason has a rich and varied background in all things digital and currently leads E-commerce operations across APAC for Premier Farnell, a world-leading distributor of electronic products. He has previously started up and headed the E-commerce business of Eu Yan Sang, a leading healthcare company in Asia. He has also launched the Singapore and Hong Kong offices of AD2ONE, a global UK-based digital media agency. When he was 18, he founded one of Singapore’s most popular online communities SgForums.com. Outside of work, he enjoys salsa dancing and playing the guitar.
