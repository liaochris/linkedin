Alex Matthews' Summary

Alex has over 15 years of experience in Information and Communications Technology [ICT] and in business, working in a diverse range of countries around the world and in a variety of large multi-national enterprises.

Alex has a professional history of award winning performance in positions ranging from Information and Communications Technology technical expert to strategic business leader.

Alex has an outstanding record as an enterprise architect, strategic planner, operational manager, including staff of 50+.

Alex has formal training and wide-ranging experience in enterprise architecture, strategic planning, human resource management, budget / resource / logistic control, training / competency / performance management and planning, fleet / facility management and business continuity planning.

As a project manager Alex has a strong track record of excellence in delivering multi-million dollar, high-complexity projects on time, on scope and within quality and resource constraints.

Alex’s distinguished record has been achieved through a sharp focus on business need and a careful attention to creating value. This, combined with an extremely diverse grounding in technical appointments, positions Alex uniquely to devise and evaluate solutions from both business and technical perspective.

Specialties: Now primarily an enterprise architect, management consultant, strategic planner and complex technical project management advisor.
