L. Gustavo C. Barbato's Summary

Gustavo Barbato, Ph.D. and M.Sc., is a member of Dell's Global Information Security team, Security Professor at UNISINOS University in Brazil and founder of POASEC.org project. He has worked in security projects for the Brazilian Government and Security Companies for many years involving software programming, systems hardening, network security, application and network penetration testing, software security assessments, code review, malware analysis, intrusion detection, forensics analysis and others activities. Gustavo has been working as information security professor at college and postgraduate level since 2004. He is the Porto Alegre (Brazil) OWASP Chapter Founder/Leader and member of OWASP Global Chapter Committee, where he is responsible for helping Latin America Chapters, especially, brazilian ones. He is Bachelor in Computer Science and nowadays is taking MBA (Business Management) classes at UNISINOS.
