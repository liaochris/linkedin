Graphics Coordinator

Chase Studio, Inc.

July 1995 – Present (18 years 6 months)

I'm the graphics coordinator at one of the world's leading firms specializing in museum design. Collaborating with clients and creative teams across several disciplines, I oversee the production of all 2-dimensional design used in our natural history and environmental exhibits worldwide.

I specialize in combining cutting-edge digital processes with traditional exhibit techniques. Analytical thinking, project, staff, and resource management, as well as creative problem-solving are a few of the skills I've acquired.

Owner

Allan Burch Illustration

January 1994 – Present (20 years)

I am a portrait artist and illustrator, creating award-winning visual solutions for editorial, publishing, advertising, and institutional clients.

My illustrations are rooted in realism with a personal raw energy and flowing movement. Concepts employ subtlety and use the components of the painting -- value, light, texture, color, and cropping -- to help tell the story. They work great for any project that has a need for contemporary visuals that are straightforward, accessible to a wide audience, and display a personal edge. They also work perfectly for any and all portrait needs.
