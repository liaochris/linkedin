Jonathan Barnett-Neefs' Summary

A commercially aware IT Director with over 20 years of proven delivery of value-based IT in the UK and world-wide. Skills include driving change management in culture, process, technology and people in both start-ups and in rapid-growth phases, and selecting, leading and motivating cross-border multi-disciplinary teams, I lead and deliver successful transformation initiatives, especially in the areas of compliance and corporate governance. I am an effective communicator and successfully bridge the traditional technical and non-technical divide. Where the usual approaches to IT are often unsuccessful at aligning with Business goals and values, my style is far more customer-focused and drives IT as an enabler of Business: not simply an overhead cost but a value-add and investment.
