Antoinette Ransom's Summary

In 2001, the Kansas City native moved to Los Angeles, CA. Although she came from a family that supports diversity, nothing could prepare her for the subcultures embraced in Los Angeles. She starred in a few films, several commercials, music videos, and guest starred on daytime soap.

Starting at the Art Institute California-Hollywood campus; allowed her the opportunity to be “uniquely” creative.

She later became the president of F.B.I (Fashion Bureau Incorporated) Student Chapter. Francis Harder, owner of F.B.I became her mentor while attending AI. She managed to get Costco and UPS to sponsor the fashion and marketing students. Thanks to the donations, the students were able to participate in the Magic Trade Show in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Antoinette participated in several fashion shows as a designer/stylist/coordinator. She was more than flattered to be a part of Tyra Banks, “Project Runway” experiment on the “Tyra Banks Show”. She continued to intern for IMG for three years, handling the PR/ PA duties for Mercedes Benz Fashion Week in Los Angeles, CA.

After graduating from the Art Institute in 2007; she started her own clothing line, "LoveNote Couture", and continued producing her own shows.

In 2010/2011, she started her own production company called, Ambush Events. She had her premiere event, “Exhibit Ambush” at the luxurious Horton Plaza. She produced the first multi-sensory event in San Diego, and the event sold out. Her goal is to open doors artists of all genres that are unique, risk takers, inspire, and think outside of the box.

In Exhibit Ambush, she created a women’s collection dedicated to the late Alexander McQueen. The garments were made from recycled cordouroy fabric that was donated.

Antoinette has been featured in several publications including the Apparel News, MODA, LA Weekly, Pacific San Diego Magazine, AOL News, Bottomline Magazine, Discover SD, Dot Magazine, ArtRocks Radio and the Los Angeles Times.

Specialties

merchandising, marketing, patternmaking, trend forecast, fashion show production, and styling...
