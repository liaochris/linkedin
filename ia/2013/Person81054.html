Linda Leviton's Summary

Linda Leviton attended the University of Cincinnati School of Design, Architecture and Art and graduated Summa Cum Laude.

She was a graphic designer in Chicago, providing design and art direction to corporations and design firms. She was an assistant art director for Cuisine Magazine.

While working as Art Director for Baxter, Linda began creating artwork from materials for their corporate art collection. She continued this interest by developing a series of wall hangings using textiles combined with metal and paint to create art quilts.

Her background as a graphic designer and corporate art director has prepared her to work effectively with a variety of clients by meeting their project, timeline and contractual requirements.

Since 1993, Linda has exhibited nationally and internationally. and is installed in over 130 hospitals, universities, Fortune 500 companies, libraries, public and private settings nation wide.
