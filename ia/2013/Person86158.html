Marisa Villarin's Summary

My ultimate goal is to create long-term relationships with all my clients and in order to achieve this, I give the best in every transaction.I make sure their experiences in both buying and selling will be stress-free and pleasant.I also provide a "one-stop" shop since I have the Accredited Staging Professional designation and I am also a Mortgage Broker.

Some of the services are provide :

For Sellers:
-comparative market analysis to determine the ideal listing price
-comprehensive research of the property and neighbourhood to effectively market the property
- free staging consultation using my training as an Accredited Staging Professional
-professional virtual tours posted in http://www.marisavhomes.com and company websites aside from the MLS; this will also be posted in all my social media networks
-Open House as the need arises
-professional photography and full coloured print brochures
-mortgage pre-approval for direct prospective Buyers

For Buyers:
-mortgage pre-qualification using my training and knowledge as a Mortgage Broker
-step-by-step home buying process which includes overview of neighbourhoods that match their criteria, closing costs, history of the property they are purchasing and review of all documents in the transaction
-provide comprehensive market analysis of the property being purchased in order to come up with the best price
-provide list of other professionals needed in the buying process such as home inspectors, appraiser, insurance brokers,lawyers, handymen, etc.

Specialties: Aside from being a Real estate and Mortgage Broker, I have obtained my designation as an Accredited Staging Professional (ASP) and Accredited Senior Agent (ASA). Please visit my real estate website at http://www.marisavhomes.com. and mortgage website at http://www.marisavmortgage.com.

I use technology in my business and continuously learn in order to service my clients the best way I can.
