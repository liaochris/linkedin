Grant Hogarth's Summary

I am an experienced professional communicator, information developer, and team leader, with a proven ability to effectively communicate both business and technical knowledge to diverse audiences. I create award-winning documents (both technical and non-technical) and products for companies of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. I bring a wide range of knowledge and experience to my work, and the ability to see the immediate details and place them in a larger context.

My experience includes 10+ years managing and working with both creative and technical personnel, as well as 20+ years as a technical writer. I have a history of successfully employing risk/mitigation analysis to identify potential problems and opportunities. In two managerial positions, I increased revenues by 105% ($0.7M), and 45% ($0.11M). I have a strong knowledge of the disciplines supporting product development, (configuration management, change control, engineering, QA, localization, etc.), as well as a working knowledge of databases, spreadsheets, and project-management tools such as MS Project.

My skills include effective team leadership, clear writing, strong (and context-sensitive) editing, information architecture, document architecture, publication design, usability, desktop publishing, conceptual illustration, and writing: User Manuals, RFP responses, Online Help, API/SDKs, and for the Web.
