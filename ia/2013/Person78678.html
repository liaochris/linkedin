LaTosha Johnson's Summary

LaTosha Johnson is a Marketing consultant who specializes in providing marketing and administrative support to companies. Her broad based marketing experience with special emphasis on research, planning, design, and implementation enables her to provide companies with the necessary techniques to differentiate themselves from the competition.

She realizes that many businesses have limited funds and often fall into the trap of trying to do everything on their own. Therefore, if your goal is to devote more time to your business by recruiting affordable talent to assist in your endeavor to support and expand your brand…then look no further!

LaTosha enjoys assisting companies in how to maintain the delicate balance of simultaneously attracting and retaining customers. It is her desire to make sure that when people question an organization's identity that the marketing strategies she creates will ensure that your clients and you both share the same answer.

Specialties: Marketing, marketing research, customer insights, client insights, social media marketing, article marketing, blogging, copywriting, ghostwriting, analysis, brand repositioning, writing, editing, proofreading, virtual assistant, content marketing, event planning, event promotion,
