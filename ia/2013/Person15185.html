Brent Nims' Projects

  • Beyond Suffering IBook

    • February 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Brent Nims, Bill Denzel

    We recently completed the Beyond Suffering iBook Project for International disability ministry, Joni & Friends. What was originally published as two books and a double DVD package has been condensed into this single iBook including almost two hours of video content and multiple interactive iBooks widgets.

  • One Million Children

    • July 2007 to October 2007
    Team Members: Brent Nims, Jenn Nims, Marc Stein

    When Filmmakers Brent and Jenn Nims traveled to China, India and South Africa to document the real life stories of children around the world, they really did not know what to expect.

    They visited humble homes, orphanages, townships and churches filming children telling their own stories, some of which broke their hearts. Despite their bleak circumstances all found faith, hope and love.

    Journey of the Heart focuses on the significance and far reaching consequences of bringing God’s Word to children around the world. This is their story. This is their movie.

    Ted Baehr of MovieGuide calls it, “A lot of fun and a convicting inspiration.”

  • All You Can Arcade

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Brent Nims, Seth Peterson

    A rental platform for vintage arcade video games.
