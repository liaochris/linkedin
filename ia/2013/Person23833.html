Christen Rochon's Summary

I’m Christen – 10+ media aficionado, freelance writer & publishing entrepreneur. As a former radio executive now navigating the field of new media publishing, I enjoy sharing my outlook on everything from fashion, entertainment, gadgets and more on the award-winning website DivasandDorks.com. Featured as one of the ‘Blogosphere’s Best in 2012′ by BlackEnterprise.com and one of the ‘Top Black Bloggers You Should Know’ by TheRoot.com – I am the self proclaimed Diva behind Divas And Dorks, LLC - (which includes urban & international travel site, DivasOnDestinations.com as well as the web's first automotive lifestyle site for women, DivasDriveInHeels.com.)
