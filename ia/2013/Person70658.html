Julieanna Davis Jablonski's Summary

Hello, I'm a professional photographer based in the Northern Front Range of Colorado.

My styles of photography are photojournalistic, creative editorial & fine art portraiture. I strive to capture emotion and meaning in my photographs.

I absolutely love to take photos.

Photography isn't what I do as much as it's who I am. Capturing images is one of my great passions in life. I even take pictures in my dreams﻿. I would love to bring my passion for photography to your wedding, event or portrait session.

Specialties: Action photography, Sports, Portraits, Engagements, Weddings, Children, Pets, Landscapes, Events, Commercial etc.

My kit includes a Nikon D700 & D300s, two external flash units, many different lenses, diffusers, and filters, low apertures for shallow depth of field, tools for artistic shots; Lensbaby, Tilt Shift, Fisheye, and colored gels.

Every customer receives a CD of their images so that they can make as many copies of their photos as they wish, and use them on social networking sites. I can professionally enhance your favorite pictures in Adobe Lightroom 4 and Photoshop CS6.

Portrait packages start at $100. Wedding packages start at $2000 with Engagement shoot included. Event photography starts at $75 an hour. I'm willing to barter services and I love to travel. Please contact me if you're interested in some interesting photos;

Julieanna Davis Jablonski's Publications

  • Dave Graham's Battle Gaming: The New American Sport

    • Battle Games Sports & Hobbies llc
    • December 2010
    Authors: Julieanna Davis Jablonski, Dave Graham, Amy Hutcheson

    Battle Gaming: The New American Sport is the first authoritative book that fully describes the uniquely American sport of battle gaming. The comprehensive book includes a description of the sport, rules of play, game scenarios, safety guidelines, battle field strategies, combat techniques, how to make a new player kit, history of the sport, and organization profiles. Players are taught the Warrior Code Open Battle Gaming System. From this system players can easily make the transition into other battle gaming systems like Amtgard, Belegarth, Dagorhir, Darkon, and the High Fantasy Society. Appendices include battle game system comparison tables and guidelines for battle game weapons.
