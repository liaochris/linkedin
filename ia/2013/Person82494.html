Lori Wilson's Summary

Currently, I am a marketing coordinator at an architectural and engineering firm. I market our aviation services, which means I have gotten quite familiar with funny acronyms like NAVAIDS and IROPS while refining my proposal creation, technical editing and social media skills.

I also have a background in event-planning, public relations and sales with a record of successful direct mail and e-mail projects. The combination of graphic design and stamps (okay, we don't usually get to use "real" stamps with direct mail or e-mail) makes me happy.

In my spare time, I do odd-job graphic design projects, which means I pretty much live in the Adobe Creative Suite at work and home. It's a treat to stretch my creativity outside of my job, though.

Specialties: Graphic design, marketing communications, proposals, event-planning and direct mail campaigns
