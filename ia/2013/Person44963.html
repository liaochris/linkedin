Melanie Summer's Summary

Melanie Summer was born and raised in Germany. She left home at 16, continued her education working to put herself through school and then trained for 3 years to get a diploma in Real Estate and Property Management. At the age of 24, she left Germany with her then partner, to sail to the Caribbean – a journey that ended up lasting for three years and around the world. Returning to Germany, she married her partner and they chose to permanently leave Germany and call Australia home.

After separating from her husband, she needed to reorganise her life as a single mum. She started a small book keeping company specialising in helping small business in the Whitsundays, north Queensland. However, after three years she decided to return to the Sunshine Coast, where she took the opportunity to establish Flash Fotos.

Her dramatic learning curve over the next three years saw her contemplate bankruptcy and then turn her business into the success that it is today. As she says, "I always knew I’d land on my feet but I just wasn’t sure if I’d be wearing high heels or running shoes".

Melanie won the National Telstra Small Business Award 2011. Melanie is an inspirational story and a very engaging speaker and writer. She now helps small business owners to "grow out of their business" by transforming their business into an independently operating enterprise while living life to their fullest potential.
