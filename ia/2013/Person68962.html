Josh Jablon's Summary

University Painters Founder Josh D. Jablon

Jablon was in college in 1986 when he started house painting as a summer job in the Philadelphia area. During this time he worked as a student owner of a franchised house painting business while attending college.

Upon graduating from college, Jablon established the University Painters trade name and trademark and began franchising painting businesses under this name across the United States.

In about 1999, University Painters adopted a new business approach, using permanent non-student business owners who license the University Painters trade name and trademark.

At present, the University Painters owners continue to use this career professional painting crew system which aids the year round operation of their businesses.

Jablon attended George Washington University and Graduated from Temple University in Philadelphia with a degree in Finance.

Specialties

Franchising & Trademark Licensing, Rendering Technical Assistance in the establishment of Painting & Other Home Improvement Businesses
