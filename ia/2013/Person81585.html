Lisa Lipton's Summary

More than ever young girls are struggling to feel good about themselves. Low self-esteem, self-doubt and feelings of insecurity can lead to negative behavior and unhealthy choices.

If your daughter struggles with self-doubt and low self-esteem, she might be more vulnerable to negative outside influences, despite her doing well in school and at home.

My name is Lisa Lipton and I’m a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist in Mission Viejo, California.

Through my business, Girl Spirit, and my private counseling and psychotherapy practice, I help pre-adolescent girls, adolescent girls, young adult women and their families learn better ways to manage the various stresses that are part of these challenging developmental stages by providing adolescent and teen counseling, parent counseling and seminars, and family counseling and workshops.

You’ve already taken the first step by recognizing that your daughter’s mental and emotional health is vital to her present and future success and happiness. The next step is to contact me at Girl Spirit. Together we can help your daughter realize her own true worth.

For more information about how Girl Spirit and the various counseling services and workshops I provide can help your daughter and your family, I encourage you to visit http://www.girlspirit.us or contact me at (818) 913-5556 or lisalipton@girlspirit.us.
