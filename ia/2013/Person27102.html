Cornell Green's Summary

For more than twenty years, I have worked with the technologies that now drive today's Internet economy. Since 2009 I have worked with Small Business owners, operators and employees, showing them how they can use the hardware, software and communication channels that drive the 21st Century economy to expand their customer base and grow their businesses.

The "post-crash" economy provides the most level playing field the Small Business market will see in a lifetime. Combined with unprecedented access to affordable hi-tech products and programs, solo enterprises, startups and local Small Businesses can now develop and utilize the same integrated processes and practices that drive the world's major corporations to continued success and profit.

"Come with me if you want to live"... with a well-managed, prosperous business set to improve, expand and face the challenges of the online economy.

Specialties: Small Business computer support, Small Business infrastructure design, Small Business website design, Social Media marketing strategy, Educational Content creator, Seminar and Webinar presenter, eLearning website design and implementation.
