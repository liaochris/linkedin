My name is Elijah Gartin and I am an Information Technology Professional/Entrepreneur, Student, Soldier, and Baseball Player. 2013 has already started off on the right foot. While I will be drawing things down with my company, Thunder Rock, I will still be busy as ever with my new job at KP. Hopefully within the next ten years I will have a MSIS (Masters in Information Systems) and a MBA (Masters of Business Administration). I’m fully excited to go through this year, and I’m really happy that all the Mayan apocalypse nonsense never came to fruition.

    This certification program has been designed for graduating seniors from 4 year undergraduate Information Systems degree programs, especially for those universities following the Information Systems Model Curriculum (ACM, AITP, and AIS sponsored).
