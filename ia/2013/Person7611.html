Andy Ward's Summary

Andy Ward is a UK based illustrator. He studied painting at the Norwich School of Art before moving to London, spending eight years freelancing for editorial and advertising clients. His focus has since broadened to include toy design, jewellery and fashion.

His illustration work has appeared on Airplanes, on Catamarans, sneakers, on the backs of buses, on billboards, in shop windows, on balloons, in magazines, on bikinis, in newspapers, on jackets, in books, on stickers, on cd's, as record covers, on handbags, on coats, on t-shirts, on skirts, blown up for exhibition displays, hand sewn in sequins, miniaturized for mobile phones, on the TV, on the catwalk, in the cinema, and online.

He currently has three weekly slots in the UK's Daily Mail and has held regular weekly or monthly slots in The Times, The Independent, The Telegraph, and the Observer national UK newspapers. His animation work has been shown on TV, cinema, and more recently at London's IMAX cinema.

He has worked regularly with Spanish fashion label Custo Barcelona since their SS 2006 collection and has collaborated on over 300 pieces.

He is also the exclusive illustrator for California based fashion label Tookata People.
