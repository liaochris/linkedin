Kevin Corti's Summary

I am a digital media professional with broad experience spanning digital/game design, project and product management. I have a strong commercial outlook derived from founding three studios and winning and delivering over 100 projects over the course of fifteen years. I have a strong digital product focus (social, web and mobile) and I am comfortable working at any stage of a project from early concept creation through to post-launch and on-going optimisation. I like to work with/in dynamic and talented teams that have an entrepreneurial spirit where I can have a strong influence over the product vision and/or day to day evolution of social/mobile products and services that have significant commercial potential.

Key experience with:

• Mobile, social & browser games product development lifecycle
• Mobile/social market dynamics, platforms, vendors & technologies
• F2P business models and product design/management
• Game design (UI wireframe, user stories/use cases, sim modelling, virtual economy)
• 'Serious games' design and development
• Game data analysis to drive iterative design & product optimisation
• Complex game logic/data models
• Social media / community management
• Digital music services / discovery
• Agile project management (running daily scrums, sprint planning, product backlog etc)
• Continuous development/integration & running Games as a Service (GaaS)
• Building & leading high-performing teams with challenging budgets & time frames
• Selecting & leading remote/outsourced teams
• Strong written communication & presentation skills
• Managing company/product P&L
