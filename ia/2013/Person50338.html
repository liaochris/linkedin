Gregory Margolis' Experience

Sales Engineer

Keyence Corporation of America

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Industrial Automation industry

June 2013 – Present (7 months) Greater Boston Area

I am a Sales Engineer in the Micro-Analysis Group (MAG) working with Keyence's high end measurement microscopes and macroscopes. I currently cover all of New England but am based out of the Boston area. The two main products series I work with are the VK-X100/X200 and the VR-3000.

The VK-X100/X200 series is our laser scanning confocal microscope. This scope is great for scanning samples in 3D at the nanometer level. It is particularly excellent when it comes to scanning for line and surface roughness.

The VR-3000 series is our white light LED, one-shot, measurement macroscope. This scope is great for scanning larger areas in 3D at the micron level. This system was designed to measure 3D profiles of any sample as well as providing the ability to view and analyze flatness and curvature over a large area.
