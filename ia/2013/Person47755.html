Geoffrey Timmerman's Projects

  • Reporting database

    • 2005 to Present
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman

    Development of a PHP/MySQL web-application as a reportingsystem for the maintenance, quality and production department to report breakdowns and problems associated with building engines for Volvo Cars.

  • Tooling and nutrunner module

    • 2006 to 2006
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman

    Development of a tooling and nutrunner module in addition to the reporting system. The module makes it able to manage and control nutrunners and other measurement driven tooling meeting the Volvo standard.

  • Stock and warehouse application

    • 2007 to 2008
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman

    Development of a stock and warehouse application to manage the maintenance warehouse. The application makes it possible for the maintenance people to easily manage their stock with a PDA (with built-in barcode scanner), a desktop computer and even a forklift terminal.

  • Rebuilding the platform for the reporting database

    • 2009 to 2009
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman

    Rebuilt of the server and platform that holds the Reporting system.
    Migration to Ubuntu 8.04LTS and later on in 2010 upgraded to 10.04LTS

  • Making Siemens PLC logs available in webbrowser

    • 2011 to 2011
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman, Koen K. Cocquyt

    Development of a PHP, XHTML and Ajax based webapplication that makes it possible for everyone to read the actual alarmlogs of our production lines in the Engine Center using a webbrowser.
    A Kiosk system was also built for the maintenance and production coach office to display the current production status.

  • Breakdown Analyse application for Siemens PLC logs

    • July 2011 to November 2011
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman, Koen K. Cocquyt

    Development of a breakdown analyse application that creates detailed statistics and graphs from the data found in the PLC alarmlogs. The user can generate the breakdown analyse after selecting a start and end date. Previous our coach needed about 1 day to gather all statistics and graphs which now only takes up about 5 minutes.

  • Cognex vision checker online pictures

    • November 2011 to December 2011
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman

    Making the images of a Cognex vision checker available through a web browser so the maintenance technicians can check from any place within the Volvo network if something goes wrong on the vision checker station.

  • Renewal of copier machine park

    • May 2012 to September 2012
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman

    Roll out of new copy machines to replace old machines. Research and implementation of machine configuration and LDAP scan to mail function.

  • Digital workplace PC Renewal

    • October 2012 to March 2013
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman, Peter Aerts, Philip Bauwens, Ronald Modde

    Hardware and software renewal of +/- 1200 computers, migrating them to Microsoft Windows 7 and solving specific problems that come with the migration.

  • Virtualisation of ABB Sattline production systems

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman, Philip Bauwens

    Working out a virtualisation solution to keep the older ABB Sattline production systems available for the next number of years.

  • In and outgoing goods registration

    • July 2013 to August 2013
    Team Members: Geoffrey Timmerman, jose reinaldo
