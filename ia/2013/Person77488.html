Richard L Kuper's Summary

Richard is a seasoned business and technology professional and a Certified SIx Sigma YB. In addition to his current daytime job as Team Lead - Technical Communications with the Investment Services division of Fiserv (formerly CheckFree Investment Services), Richard is President, CEO, CIO, and Chairman of the Board of NY SPIN, manages a variety of websites, and is available to provide business and technology consulting services.

Anyone can take immediate advantage of the useful websites Richard has created, such as http://ThisIsMyStore.com , where you will find many of your favorite stores (and some that may become your favorites) in one convenient location -- along with sales and coupons and such. Richard also writes for and publishes The KUPER Report (http://TheKuperReport.com), an online newsletter containing Business and Technology related articles of interest to C-Level Managers, Business Managers, IT Managers, and the general public.
