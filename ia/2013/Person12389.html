Burkhard Daniel's Summary

Software Technologist with 19 years of experience in engineering, architecting, and managing software projects.

I develop industrial-strength embedded software, highly specialized desktop applications, custom kernel drivers on Windows, Linux and UNIX, and build complex distributed business solutions tailored specifically to my clients' domains.

I specialize in low-level system software engineering and high-performance software architectures for Windows, Linux and embedded platforms. I am an expert in C++, C, and C# and have in-depth knowledge of Javascript, Java, Assembly, and many other programming languages. I work best in an agile setting and enjoy doing SCRUM.

Having worked as a contractor for many years, I am customer-focused and work especially well in a dedicated team. I feel at home both in project-based business and in product development, and constantly keep close tabs on the markets my solutions are being used in by engaging with end users of my products and maintaining close relationships with key market players.

In recent projects and positions I have had comprehensive exposure to applications from the high-voltage industry, and have gained a deep understanding of partial discharge phenomena and their measurement and analysis.

My projects routinely incorporate the latest web technologies.

In my current position, I act as a product owner and lead a team of dedicated software development professionals. We are working with C++ to deliver best-of-breed measurement solutions.

Though now based in Berlin, Germany, I have in the past lived in the US and have done business in many parts of the world. I continue to operate internationally today.

Specialties: System Software including firmware, kernel drivers, high-performance software architectures, C++, C, Qt, Boost, C#, Javascript, HTML, CSS, RESTful services, distributed software, project management, entrepreneurship, Partial Discharge Measurement and Analysis

Burkhard Daniel's Projects

  • MPD Software

    • September 2002 to Present
    Team Members: Burkhard Daniel

    Software for measuring partial discharges using OMICRON's MPD range of products. It performs advanced signal analysis, extracts measurement values from the stream of data received by the MPD device(s), and visualizes the results in diverse and convenient ways. It also controls the measurement device.
    The software is used both in diagnostic and monitoring environments. It has a full-featured yet easy-to-use UI and also provides an automation interface for remote control and integration into larger industrial-scale setups.
    This software includes a windows driver and an embedded firmware component. The associated hardware is based on USB 2 and was one of the first devices on the market to successfully utilize isochronous high-speed streaming.
    Together, the software and the associated measurement hardware set the technological benchmark in its domain, and have done so for the better part of a decade.
