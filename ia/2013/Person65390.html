Joel Ballezza's Summary

Joel Christian Ballezza is an endurance runner, digital marketer and blogger. Ballezza enjoys inspiring people to build businesses, live healthier lives and help others.

Ballezza's background in marketing includes working with start-ups, government agencies and corporations including Brooks, IBM, University of Washington, and US Federal Judiciary, among others.

When not working, Ballezza enjoys exploring the outdoors. He blogs about running and marketing.

Specialties

Digital and social media marketing, crisis communications, public speaking, partnership development, consultant services, user interface design, HTML, Drupal, Wordpress, AdWords, project management, running, endurance sports
