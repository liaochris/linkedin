Chris Shattuck's Summary

Chris Shattuck is an Atlanta-based journalist, blogger and photographer with a strong background in nonprofit public relations and media management & marketing.

A rising senior at Georgia State University, he lead the marketing of last year's highly successful redesign of the university's award-winning student newspaper, The Signal. As the paper's new editor-in-chief, he remains dedicated to maintaining The Signal's expansion and push toward a daily digital-first publishing strategy, launching two new apps for iOS users in the fall.

Outside of the newspaper, Chris serves as the webmaster for the Georgia State chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists and previously worked as the vice president of public relations for GSU's American Marketing Association chapter from 2012-2013.

Chris has also interned in the Public Information Office of the Carter Center and freelanced for GlobalAtlanta, which covers daily international business in Atlanta from a global perspective. With an expected 2014 degree in journalism and marketing, he hopes to build on this experience as a senior public relations professional for a U.S.-based international corporation or nonprofit.

Specialties: Media Branding, Social Media Promotion, Networking, Journalism, Editing, InDesign, Photoshop, Wordpress, HTML, Google AdSense, Google Analytics, Search Engine Optimization, Rebranding, Blogging
