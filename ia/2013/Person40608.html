Robert W.H. Price, LCPC, LPC, NCC's Experience

Founder

Elite Minds, LLC

August 2000 – Present (13 years 2 months)

Elite Minds, LLC is a sport psychology consulting service that promotes athletes exceeding their potential on and off the field. This is accomplished through proven mental skill techniques that will lead the athlete to more consistent performances even in the face of adversity. Robert W.H. Price founded Elite Minds in 2000 as a graduate student and has been creating successful inroads since.

Robert has counseled collegiate and professional kickers, amateur tennis players, golfers of all levels, basketball players, gymnasts, hockey players, lacrosse, soccer, ice skating, field hockey, track, and many other recreational athletes. His most recent work has been with the TEST Sports Clubs, Chicago Bears, New York Giants, New Jersey Nets, Montgomery County Department of Recreation, and Advanced Spine and Wellness Center. He has also been recently employed as a Sport Psychology Consultant for Career Consultants Inc. and Pro Sports Consultants, LLC.

Provide resources and training as needed for providers on how to create and sustain an environment that promotes collaborative decision making in all placement and service delivery decisions.

Responsible for developing supervisory policies that make supervision case-driven and clinically focused for improved services throughout the child serving system.
