Faith Erickson's Summary

My goal is to help my clients sell their homes in the shortest amount of time for the highest price. By teaming with the home owner and their Realtor, key home renovation and repair priorities are established and addressed. Staging is then implemented in order to create that model home feel.

Staging a home well means that the home will appeal to the broadest number of buyers and will allow the features and focal points of a home to shine through. Successful staging evokes positive emotional responses which, in turn, yield sales!

Remember - an investment in staging is ALWAYS less than the cost of that first price reduction!
