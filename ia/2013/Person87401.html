Marsha Egan, CPCU, PCC's Summary

Certified business leader coach, celebrated email productivity guru, speaker, facilitator, and author, Marsha Egan brings over 25 years of outstanding leadership experience in both corporate & volunteer America to any individual, organization or audience.

An International Coach Federation-certified professional coach, Marsha currently serves as CEO of her executive coaching firm, InboxDetox.com a division of The Egan Group, Inc.

Marsha was named one of Pennsylvania's 50 Best Women in Business, and is CEO of “The Egan Group, Inc,” a Reading, PA based executive coaching firm. Her focus: to inspire her clients to maximize their competitive edge through leveraging their strengths.

In addition to speaking all over the world, she has appeared on ABC Nightly News, Fox and Friends and numerous other international and regional TV and radio shows. Her articles and interviews have been featured in national press such as "The Chicago Tribune," The New York Post," "The Washington Post," "Shape," and was on the front page of "USA Today." Her e-newsletters, "the Signal, and "Email Savvy" have circulations of over 20,000 subscribers.

She enjoys helping her individual and business clients realize their life and business goals through interpersonal skill mastery, self management and action.

Not only is Marsha a twenty five year veteran of corporate America, she has served as president or chairman of over 30 volunteer and charity organizations, including the Junior League of Reading and national CPCU Society, a 30,000 member organization that promotes excellence in the insurance industry.
