Startup Effect

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Education Management industry

July 2012 – Present (1 year 6 months) Greater Detroit Area

Startup Effect is an enrichment education program in transitioning American cities designed to help middle school students gain confidence and skills through action-oriented, entrepreneurial activities and challenges. Our mission is to build entrepreneurship into the culture of American cities by empowering youth to think independently, proactively, and creatively. Students in the program will join the Startup Effect network to provide them with the mentorship, resources, and peer group necessary to create value in their communities.

Research Associate

Earthwatch Institute

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Research industry

June 2011 – August 2011 (3 months)

The Earthwatch Institute supports scientific field research and education by offering volunteers the opportunity to join research teams around the world to collect field data in the areas such as wildlife conservation, rainforest ecology, marine science and archaeology. I conducted two independent research projects: (1) statistically analyzed how different program factors affected their measures of success scores and (2) evaluated market opportunities for Earthwatch to develop online citizen science programs. I also assessed field reports to determine program impacts on ecological, socio-cultural, and economic systems, and on national and international policies.
