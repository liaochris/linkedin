Jesse Meijers' Summary

Envisioning and creating innovative solutions that inspire our customers to think differently. That’s my drive.

I started as an entrepreneur early on in my degree in Software Engineering at the VU University Amsterdam. Together with my former business associate, I built a highly advanced Point of Sale system and first generation web applications. In all our software, user friendliness was key.

In 2007, I started a new company, together with Mark Hulshof, based on a shared passion for developing innovative web-based concepts. With Nubiko we focus on realising web applications that really strengthen our customers’ business in the examination industry.

Nevertheless, on a daily basis we were seeing organisations struggle with long and expensive IT projects: consuming considerable amounts of time and money, and often not even delivering. We envisioned and designed a solution, and that’s why we founded Triggre. The Triggre ‘strategic agility system’ enables organizations to quickly and easily translate ideas, strategies and business concepts into innovative and adaptable applications.

In my role as a CTO at Nubiko and Triggre, I lead, coach and challenge our development team to design, develop and implement flexible, user-friendly and innovative solutions for our customers. Solutions that inspire our customers to think differently and that allow them to get ahead.
