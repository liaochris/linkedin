Dyshann Anderson's Summary

Dyshann Anderson is responsible for Aquastone Graphic Arts & Print overall operation management and growth, directing strategic business planning, project planning, budgeting, Designing and Art Direction.

Dyshann has more than a decade of business experience working with print media and communications. Before owning Aquastone, he was an recent graduate of Savannah College of Art and Design and a Broadcast Designer for ESPN in Bristol, Connecticut. At ESPN, he was responsible for producing digital graphics under short turnarounds for several sports programs.

Recently, Dyshann held a solo showing at the Pump House gallery, Hartford CT and has produced brand management for over 80 businesses local and national. As an Designer, he conducted marketing audits, designed and produced campaigns to reduce business and financial risk.

In addition to dis responsibilities as owner, Dyshann is a fine artist—Painting in Acrylic, Ink, Graphite on canvas and fine papers from around the world—subject matters that are thought provoking and supports Aquastone’s talent and clients as the company continues to grow.
