Ilja Theuwissen's Projects

  • SafeCam on Rock Werchter

    • July 2011 to August 2011
    Team Members: Ilja Theuwissen

    SafeCam implemented a temporary CCTV solution for the largest and most famous festival in Belgium, which was awarded "best festival in the world" already a couple of times.

    We installed about 20 camera's to monitor both the perimeter (during the event build-up and breakdown) and for crowd control.

    We collaborated with event operational management, technical firms security firms, local and national police.

    Electricity is an issue when you have an even this size, so we designed battery packs that powered the camera's an wireless equipment. We rolled out a wireless network covering the whole festival area which was used for the CCTV, but also other applications such as message bord, cash registers, etc...

    The cameras were monitored at more then 10 locations, both on- and off site, each of them using different means of communication (fiber, UTP, wireless, 3G, VDSL), and each of them with their own permission set. We had a couple of people which used the mobile client to watch the CCTV streams.
