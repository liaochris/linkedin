Jeff Etzin's Summary

Jeffrey Etzin's life experience, prepared him for an innovative approach to client service as an accountant and business advisor. He grew up in a family of entrepreneurs and this upbringing taught him to see the world from the perspective of a business owner.

Jeffrey received his accounting degree from Rider University, one of the premier business schools on the East Coast, and was awarded membership in the Omicron Delta Kappa National Leadership Honor Society.

Jeffrey entered the world of business first working in small, then regional as well as international public accounting firms which provided extensive experience and exposure to businesses in a wide variety of industries. He was admitted as a Certified Public Accountant in the State of New Jersey and became a member of the New Jersey Society of CPA's and the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants.

The accounting firm, Jeffrey Etzin, CPA was founded in 1989 on the concept of being able to provide guidance to clients in many different aspects of their financial lives from a business owner's perspective.

Jeffrey has been recognized by being admitted to Who's Who in Finance & Industry and serves on the Boards of both business and civic organizations. He brings this world of experience to every client relationship.
