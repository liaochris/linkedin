Michael "Mickey" Klein's Summary

Radio broadcast professional with experience in network programming, audio production, marketing and advertising. With a career beginning in 1980, I have developed promotions for broadcasters and advertisers in every major market in all 50 states.

Specialties

Ozark Internet Radio Network, LLC. combines the power of Internet radio with terrestrial radio, expanding on the influence of an advertisement, while reducing overall marketing expense. Specialty campaigns and promotions for business and performing artists. Broadcaster services include programming, promotions and marketing, and audio production.
