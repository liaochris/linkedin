Bernard Vanderlande's Summary

Bernard Vanderlande leads both the search teams of Tula International and Roxboro Executive Search.
Tula International is specialized on Renewable Energy, Cleantech and Advanced Manufacturing.
Clients include energy firms, venture portfolio companies, wind and solar companies, manufacturers, global engineering and waste water treatment companies, as well as international supply chain providers.

Roxboro Executive Search has its focus on Atlanta based clients who are working in the consumer goods, logistics, software application, medical and the transaction processing arena. Vanderlande and his team help clients fill Leadership positions, such as CEOs, CFOs, Board members, Chief Sales Officers, Marketing Directors, and senior innovation professionals.

Both firms are members of IRC Global Executive Search Partners, ready to serve clients around the globe with consistent world class levels of service and results.
