Iddo Hadar's Summary

Iddo Hadar is a versatile and accomplished Strategic Planning Executive with global mindset and proven expertise in corporate development and strategic marketing.

As an M&A transaction integration consultant with Ernst & Young, he works with major technology companies to rapidly implement complex deals, allowing clients to capture the greatest return on their investments. He is also on the faculty of Stanford University's Continuing Studies Program, teaching a popular business analytics/modeling class.

Earlier, as interim CEO of an early-stage digital publisher, successfully turned around and refocused an acquired content provider.

Previously he was the president of Strat Man Consulting, a management consultancy focused on industries undergoing fundamental change, such as clean tech and digital content.

Prior to founding Strat Man, he held a variety of strategy, corporate development, marketing, and technology roles at Applied Materials; including head of Corporate Strategy, Chief Marketing Officer and Chief Technology Officer. In these roles, Mr. Hadar has worked on a broad range of strategic, economic, and technological initiatives for the semiconductor and electronics industries. He played a key role in driving the company's entry into the clean energy space. He has been a leading spokesperson for the industry in the areas of economics, productivity, and technological change, including the economics of RD&E investment.

Previously, he was a management consultant with Booz, Allen & Hamilton in its strategy practice, serving clients in the aerospace, defense, and electronics industries. Earlier he was a product manager with Microsoft and an independent marketing consultant.

Mr. Hadar holds an MBA from Stanford University and is a trained programmer and economist. He also was trained in biotechnology business issues at UC Berkeley.
