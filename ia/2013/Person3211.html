Alan Rutter's Summary

Following 11 years in HM Royal Navy (CPO), I Started commercial life as Service Manager for Omicron Electronics before moving into flight training and simulation as both an On Site Engineer and later as Regional Customer Liaison Manager. I then became a Project Manager - Civil Aircraft with British Aerospace Ltd on the BAe 146 aircraft until the downturn in '91. The next 16 years were in Aviation information management, OEM & aftermarket spares, logistics and procurement with the top three aviation B2B services, ILS, Cordiem & Partsbase, looking after the Europe, Middle East and Africa region. On return to the UK I took up a position with AQD as Global Account Director looking after aviation safety management systems within the airlines. Following the buy out of AQD in June 2012, I have moved into a consultative role within the aerospace market.
I am an experienced business development, sales and marketing professional with a good understanding of engineering principles and IT infrastructures.

Specialties: 18 years Sales and Marketing in information database products
Ability to converse in Italian, French and Spanish
Representation at International Trade Shows
2 years of Project Management on 2 concurrent programs
10 years of flight training for major OEM’s, Airlines and Military including Braniff, Boeing, American Airlines, CAE and Alitalia.
Establishment of a Customer Liaison Department within a new business unit.
Customer Support & Liaison as an integral part of career progression.
