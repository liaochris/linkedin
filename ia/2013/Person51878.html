Harry Turner's Summary

I am currently studying MSc Video Games Development at Gamer Camp (Birmingham City University), working in teams to develop games for PC, iPad and PS3.

Prior to this I studied at Aston University in Birmingham, where I achieved a first class honours degree in BSc Multimedia Computing. At my graduation, I received an award for achieving the highest overall mark on my course.

I'm available for employment once my MSc course finishes in August 2013. My most recent experience is as a C++ programmer on projects for PS3, iPad and PC. I have also worked using UnrealScript in UDK, JavaScript in Unity and ActionScript 3 on Flash projects.
