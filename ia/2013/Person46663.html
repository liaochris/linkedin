Michael Gaddis, J.D.'s Summary

Michael Gaddis, California Real Estate Attorney, Broker and short sale and loan modification expert has years of experience helping clients to get their proverbial heads above water. With over 600 successful loan modifications and counting, under his belt, his track record speaks to his successful negotiation and loss mitigation skills. Over the years he's dealt with hundreds of lenders and by experience has gained intimate knowledge of the process nuances needed to successfully navigate these heavily regulated transactions.

As a Real Estate Broker and California Licensed Attorney, clients can trust Michael’s expertise far beyond that of any real estate sales agent or broker. His in-depth understanding of legal repercussions, as well as real estate law put his clients in the best possible hands in the rocky and unknown waters of mortgage modification red tape and short sale bank negotiations.

Michael owns and operates three Real Estate companies. Frontier Consultation Services specializes in California Loan Modifications, where Dream House Realty, Inc. is a full service real estate company specializing in short sales and helping clients with distressed properties. Lastly Frontier Loan Group, Inc. is the financial arm of the trio helping hopeful home hunters to finance their home loans.

Dedicated to helping people with their real estate and financial goals, he shares his experiences as a resource to those in similar situations through his blogs. He posts the problems he encounters as well as solutions he implements to complete successful loan modifications and short sales. As it's well known that these are difficult transactions to realize, he also shares misses and why they occurred and how to avoid them.
