David Jasse's Summary

Watch Emmy winning "Born to Explore" as seen on ABC every Saturday morning, featured episodes are cut by our lead editor Jason Katz. DMJ is also responsible for producing the new show open, and graphics for the show.

Contact DMJ Digital Media's team and utilize our passion and expertise as well as my professional experience garnered at CNN, MTV, CBS, FOX, and working for corporate clients like Redken and Bristol Meyers Squibb Co., to produce great films with Stunning Results.

A large part of my companies work is showing non-profit organizations how to take advantage of the benefits of video, internet and social media. Recently as creative director and producer my boutique production company produced print, web and video materials to help facilitate a record breaking 2 million dollar fund raising campaign for SINAI special needs school. In addition to producing great short films, the teams I head up offer media solutions and integration, not just great productions.

Goals...producing meaningful uplifting feature films and educational programming. I Look forward to collaborating with you, call or email jasse@dmjdigital.com or 516 3001500

Specialties: Exceptional skills when it comes to interviewing & directing real people, especially when dealing with highly sensitive issues. Over the past 15 years we've documented numerous cancer patients, special needs students, and a host of real life sensitive stories. In addition I'm skilled at working with heads of companies getting them to be great on camera. Being a season broadcast video editor at the start of my career is a huge asset to my overall story telling abilities.
