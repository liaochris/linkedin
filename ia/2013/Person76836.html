Kris LaFavor's Summary

I founded Design Ahead to provide graphic design to nonprofit organizations. Today, I lead and manage comprehensive communication projects for both the for-profit and nonprofit sectors.

Depending on the client need, I work as a creative director, graphic designer, project manager, and project lead. Through my professional network and my membership at CoCo, I draw on specialized talent to conceive, plan, and implement projects.

Every significant change begins with moving hearts and minds. My ideal clients are launching new initiatives and working towards transformational change. I am passionate about learning, collaborating, and designing solutions that give a visual voice to new ideas.


BUSINESS CLIENTS: Navitor • Elhers: Leaders in Public Finance • Buyer at Large • Suncor • Idea Food • Dalton Sherman • Fredrikson & Byron • Regency Beauty Institute • Voice Hive • Zel Skincare
