Damon Comerio's Summary

Comerio Corporation is a diversified general contracting company with a wide range of real estate services focused on Single Family New Home Construction, Residential & Commercial Remodeling. Comerio Corporation is also an authorized Dealer / Installer of the DuPont StormRoom with Kevlar.

Management within the company has over nineteen years of start up, owner operated contracting & maintenance business experience, with a track record of progressive business growth, project performance and contracting cost reduction skills.

Currently Comerio Corporation provides general contracting services to over 30 property management companies at 100 different apartment complexes in Kansas City, which range in size up to 800 units.

The company’s approach to customer service and customer retention has become the driving force with one goal in mind, customer satisfaction through excellence in quality and workmanship.

A pool of vendor resources and a team of skilled employees with over 40 years of construction and maintenance experience, has allowed it to build a niche with quick response, performance and quality results.
