Josh Frank's Summary

I am an independent professional and member of Design Cloud, a collaborative design startup changing the way creative work is done. I work in strategy, user experience (UX), and web design.

After 10+ years of part-time freelance experience designing for print and designing and building for the web, I took my business full time in August 2010. In the past two years, my work has grown from local independent professionals, small businesses, and non-profits to clients on the national scale.

As a member of Design Cloud, I am able to offer a full range of services to clients looking for strategic design services from inception to market and beyond.

Specialties

Web Design, User Experience, UX, Strategy, WordPress, HTML, CSS, social media, community management
