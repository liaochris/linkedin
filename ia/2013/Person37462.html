Dynamic Painting's Summary

I love this business. I love painting.Painting & Remodeling is what I, and my crews do everyday. I've been lucky enough to stay busy through the hard times because of my dedication to doing a great job even if it does not earn me the money to be rich. I am not in this to get rich. But do think I know enough to earn the money I need to exist, and still afford a fast food lunch sometimes! Honestly I'm happy making just what I need to live, comfortably, and this often is less than what the other really high end painters in the area demand. I strive to be the most professional painter & businessman you will find. When we are done and while we are doing it. I keep many customer and trade references all the time. I will do quality painting. Always. And I can prove it!
