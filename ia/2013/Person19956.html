Carl Cummings' Summary

At Carl-Designs building a solid brand is essential. Our creative team believes that a strong brand has the power to change the way people think about the purchases and decisions they make every day. Carl-Designs is a forward-thinking creative firm that helps a client to grow and thrive by building a brand, by designing communications elements that support the brand, and by helping to maintain the brand so that both the client and Carl-Designs can grow and thrive together.

In addition to building brands that extend across the full range of organizational communications, Carl-Designs produces top quality communication elements that fit the mission and budget of any client, no matter the size. These elements include print advertising, social media, packaging, interactive vehicles, collateral materials and more. Currently based in Wisconsin, Carl-Designs boasts a global client list that includes Microsoft, Boeing, Starbucks and Weyerhaeuser.

Specialties

Logo and identity system development, marketing materials, packaging design, web design and development, brand development.

Preflight Tech./Network Manager

Cenveo

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; CVO; Printing industry

2004 – 2006 (2 years) Greater Seattle Area

CHAPTER THREE | Curious about various facets of the design process, I stepped into preflight and hit the production fast track. Here I was responsible for reviewing and managing all graphic files before they entered the print process. I flagged submissions unable to meet the standards for high quality print, and I often had to reconstruct them from low-resolution art files while meeting crucial deadlines. It was the design version of battlefield triage. My main objective was to work with clients to handle concerns early, before they became costly problems, thus cutting waste and increasing profits. I’m happy to report that I was extremely successful, and I emerged a better, more nimble designer.
