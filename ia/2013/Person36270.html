Dominik Mueller's Summary

I am a current MSc Finance student at Imperial College Business School in London. My core courses include financial econometrics, portfolio management and asset pricing, and my electives are international finance, advanced options theory and fixed-income securities. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in business studies with a specialization in finance from the University of Hamburg.

I previously worked in asset management at Berenberg Bank in Hamburg during the first half of 2012. Most of my time was spent in the currency overlay team, where I monitored the exposure of client portfolios to foreign exchange risk and assisted in the development of quantitative trading models. In 2011, I interned in private banking at HSBC Trinkaus & Burkhardt in Hamburg. In the years 2009 and 2010, I was an intern in the equity research and risk management departments of DZ Bank in Frankfurt and London respectively as well as an intern in economic research at HSH Nordbank in Hamburg.

From 2003 through 2007, I was in the business of trading domain names. During that time, I successfully sold or purchased many high-priced premium domains on behalf of my clients (average transaction value: >$70,000). My track record includes the sale, acquisition or development of Credits.com, Kid.net, USACreditCard.com, Manhattan.info, NewYork.info, Filez.com, GVG.com, Science.info, WineStore.com, YG.com, Lilies.com, Trusted.com, ZUM.com, WeddingSong.com, BookSellers.com, Xmas.com and several other keyword domains, most of which have been subject to non-disclosure agreements. In addition to that, I provided domain evaluation and consulting services to a wide range of clients.

Specialties: Asset Management, Currency Management, Programming
