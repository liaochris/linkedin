Gerald Lucas' Summary

Associate Professor of English in the Department of Media, Culture, and the Arts at Middle Georgia State College. Ph.D. and M.A. earned from the University of South Florida in 2002 and 1997, respectively. Areas of interest include new media, issues in technoculture, contemporary literature and theory, computer-assisted teaching and learning, and epic poetry.
