Jay Kshatri's Summary

I am the author of the book - Think Smarter in a Digitally Enabled World.

This book is a call to action – it is about realizing our potential, both individually, and as a collaborative whole.

Today, with the availability of the world’s collective knowledge at our fingertips through the Internet, we have an unprecedented opportunity to have a deep understanding of any conceivable topic. However, at the dawn of the 21st century – despite our technological advancements and wealth – we continue to encounter significant challenges: in our politics, culture, educational systems, economy, environment, healthcare, conflicts domestically and abroad, and more.

I, however, don’t believe the situation is as dire as it seems. It can be fixed – and relatively quickly if we take the right steps. First we need to harness our technological tools to achieve a more thoughtful understanding of those topics. Next, using some basic frameworks and models on how to think through the related issues, problems and opportunities, we can then consciously and proactively drive toward meaningful solutions for all, not just some.

In effect, we need to upgrade our mental operating system to better manage our highly complex and dynamic world. We can no longer outsource our future to political and corporate leaders. Instead we, the people, must become more conscious and enlightened citizens and take back control of the ship.
