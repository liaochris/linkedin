Guy Sie's Summary

I'm a marketer with a research background, but technology is my passion. Prior to getting my MSc in communication studies I endeavored to become an electrical engineer and a computer scientist, but neither study included the social elements I craved in my education. Now I have the best of both worlds.

My niche is the overlap between these two worlds, the point where technology and marketing meets. Combining my education in marketing with my love of technology, I have a natural disposition for online and social media marketing. My ability to see the business perspective, yet understand programming and design aspects, makes me the perfect link between coders, creatives and management. I'm a marketing jack-of-all-trades that can be deployed anywhere.
