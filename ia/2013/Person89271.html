Matt Hirschfelt's Summary

I am an attorney, open to opportunities to join a firm as an associate, admitted to practice in all State Courts in Ohio and the United States District Court, Northern District of Ohio. I have experience in personal injury, medical malpractice, and labor and employment law matters. I also have significant successful appellate experience, experience performing complex legal research (including issues of first impression in Ohio), performing litigation functions (discovery, motion practice, and trial preparation), and managing clients and their case files.

Additionally, I am the Community / Social Media Manager for an indie gaming company, Blue Frog Gaming, in Akron, Ohio. My current position involves daily interaction with individuals from all over the world (through Facebook, Twitter, company run Forums, and Skype), processing their feedback, and communicating it effectively to an internal development team. I am also responsible for communicating changes from the development to our users, managing beta testers, and providing QA testing.
