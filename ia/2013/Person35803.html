Dirk Owen's Experience

co-owner

Applause Productions

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Events Services industry

January 1985 – Present (28 years 7 months)

Producers of the Annual "Partyfest Extrazaganza" tradeshow and publishers of the "DFW Party & Event Planner's Sourcebook" since 1988. "Where Great Parties Begin"

The 26th Annual Partyfest "Spring Showcase" is coming up January 8th, 2014 at Dallas Market Hall. Partyfest is the largest one-day exhibition & tradeshow of its kind held in the Southwest... a spectacular one-day event featuring over 250 exhibits by the top organizations in the meeting & party industry. Food & beverage samplings, new & exciting products & demonstrations on all kinds of party & event topics will be available to help you plan your next event.

Who is Invited?
D/FW's top producing businesses, corporate meeting planners, social party planners, concierges, wedding planners, association planners, industry affiliated organizations including, MPI, ISES, NACE, TxACOM, DCVB, HSMAI and many others. Invitations are also extended to anyone who's planning a party or special event in the D/FW area. This is D/FW's premier showcase for planning the perfect event!
Who is Exhibiting?
Caterers, Entertainers, Hotels, Party Suppliers, Beverage Distributors, Interactive Game Co.s, Balloons, Ad-Specialties & Gift Items, Florists, Restaurants, Audio Visual Co.s , Photographers, Unique Party Facilities, Decorations/Props, Sound & Lighting, Transportation/ Limos, etc.

PROUD SPONSORS:
Texas Meetings + Events Magazine, Dallas Convention & Visitors Bureau, D/FW Chapter MPI, ISES Dallas Chapter, NACE D/FW Chapter, TxACOM, Texas Jewish Post, OnStage Systems, Fun Factory Events, Superior Exposition Services, HSMAI D/FW Chapter, PeaPod Group, Security Reconnaissance Team Inc. & Dallas HD Films

For more information call 214 652-4300
or visit www.ApplauseProductions.com

The 25th Annual Edition of the "DFW Party & Event Planner's Sourcebook" is now available.
