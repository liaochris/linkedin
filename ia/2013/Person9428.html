Amanda Reilly's Summary

Well for starters my name is Amanda Reilly. My life began in a small town called Monroe, NY. At a young age I wreaked havoc, getting into trouble for exploring everything around me. I often wondered off, so curious with my surroundings. That curiosity stayed with me and feed my creativity. I always wanted to know more and create things that I imagined in my head. As soon as I got to high school I began to do what I loved. After graduating with a concentration in Fine Art I made my way to SUNY Rockland. There I gained a better understanding of who I was and where I wanted to go. I graduated with an Associates of Fine Arts and moved to the big city, New York City that is. I spent the past few years living and working in Brooklyn, NY until recently decided to move out west to San Francisco. I love all the opportunities i've been given and am always looking to broaden my horizons and take on new challenges.

Specialties: textile and t-shirt design, branding and identity, editorial, poster design, packaging, silkscreening, printmaking, letterpress, web design
