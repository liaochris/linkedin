Jennifer Hugon's Summary

I am a graphic artist with 10 years experience creating logos, brochures, web sites, and all manner of printed collateral. I am also a painter and illustrator, creating art for private collectors and clients.

I aim to provide my clients with a reliable source for high-impact, creative design within a real-world budget. It is my goal to provide you and your company with dedicated customer service and unique, business-focused design solutions.

Specialties

branding and corporate identity, photography art direction, illustration, marketing and advertising
