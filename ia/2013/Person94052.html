Mike Clayton's Summary

I am an Business Speaker, a Trainer, and a Facilitator, with a background of integrating complex change in major national and multi-national organisations. I offer keynotes and seminars to larger organisations, conferences and events, and provide in-house and open workshops and seminars.

I am also the author of seven published books, with two further books due for publication in June and September of 2012.
______________________________________________________________________________

If you need an insightful presentation or seminar on managing or handling the effects of change, project or risk management, influencing or presenting powerfully, or personal effectiveness, then give me a call on 08444 141349. I also offer tailored presentation skills workshops and coaching.

My style is informal and you will find I have a way of explaining the complex and subtle situations that the real world generates in simple and easy-to-action terms, that produce excellent business results.

I have an extensive seminar tour planned for 2012 with open dates available, speaking about project and risk management, management and leadership, and time management. The dates are on my website at www.mikeclayton.co.uk
