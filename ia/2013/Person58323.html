Janet Reese, MA, APR, DTM's Summary

I enable individuals, businesses and organizations to succeed through effective communications and public relations. My services include writing, PR consulting, public speaking, presentation coaching, teaching, social media, media relations and publicity.

On assignment, I write articles and marketing copy about inspiring people, health, fitness, outdoor adventure, business, travel, better living and consumer tips. See a few of my published articles at www.RinPR.com, and my videos on YouTube channel: ReeseJanet.

I'm also an adjunct professor of public speaking at Arapahoe Community College.
