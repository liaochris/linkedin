Barbara J. Cormack's Summary

Barbara J. Cormack combines her extensive background and experience with a keen insight into the demands of balancing the personal and the professional. Her career started in a more traditional accounting role as an assistant Auditor, followed by various accounting roles, financial analysis; before moving into the IT profession and working in various roles including business process engineering, IT consulting, IT programme/project management, and IT functional training. Barbara also works with organisations and individuals as a Business Coach, Business Mentor, and IT and Personal Development Trainer.

Barbara is a fully trained IT Professional, Master Coach (NMC) and an Accredited Fellow Coach (IIC); with over 25-years’ experience of working with organisations, individuals, teams of people, goals, implementations, aspirations, dreams, desires and successful outcomes.

Barbara has spent her working life in various parts of the world; born abroad, educated abroad and in the UK. She gained her business experience while living and working in the UK and abroad and through working with a large range of industries. Barbara has a strong knowledge of international business practices, while balancing the personal and professional, and incorporating the excitement of emigration. She works with various clients in a number of roles. As a professional in the software industry for over 25 years, Barbara fills a wide variety of roles and takes on a considerable breadth of functional responsibilities. As a professional in the coaching industry, Barbara works with a wide variety of clients achieving a variety of differing goals. As a member of a faculty, Barbara held the role as Head of Support, Mentoring, and Final Assessment. As a Technical Editor, Barbara has been providing the technical editing and support role for an online publication.

Clients hold Barbara in high esteem. Her style of working makes each role she has held an achievement for the client.
