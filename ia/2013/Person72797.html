Kathleen (Kathi) Joy's Summary

Kathleen has always operated on two tracks. One is as a business consultant. For 25+ years she’s acted as an executive coach, speaker and leadership consultant to senior leaders in Fortune 500, mid-size and start-up companies in the USA and Europe. She’s worked in a variety of industries including healthcare, finance, high tech, and bio tech. She specializes in working with high powered, smart, and driven performers.

The second track Kathleen has followed is more esoteric. As early as childhood Kathleen has had sensitivity to others and could sum up situations quickly. Her strong empathetic sense and ability to see a bit beyond the ordinary is a quality that has benefited many. Kathleen has an ability to deeply tune into and listen to her clients so that she can offer insights, feedback and observations that go beyond the standard fare.

The combination of both a strong analytical and intuitive mind, plus international business experience allows Kathleen to offer her clients fresh insights and profound clarity. Her clients use their new knowledge to take smart, practical actions and get fulfilling results. In addition to empowering the leaders themselves, she also helps them harness the power of the teams and people around them.

Ms. Joy holds a MA in Organizational Development from The California Institute of Integral Studies and a BA in Public Relations from the University of Michigan. She completed graduate-level work in Cross-Cultural Studies at the University de Lyon while working in France for several years. Kathleen is a Master Practitioner in Neuro-Linguistic Programming, a certified practitioner in The McKay Method of Energy Healing, and earned credentials with the International Coaching Federation. She has the pleasure of living in the San Francisco area with her two beautiful daughters and a 50 pound dog ‘lap dog’ named Coco.


Specialties: Change management, Leadership development, executive coaching
