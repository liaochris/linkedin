Maurine Patten's Summary

♦ Transform your Encore Years into the most rewarding, meaningful & joy-filled time of your life. Tap into your retirement dreams of freedom. ♦

Even if you have the financial part of your Retirement Puzzle worked out, there are other important decisions to make that can leave you feeling uncertain and concerned about your future. To actively engage in meaningful pursuits and know you matter, you need to also know the answers to the questions that are at the heart of solving your Retirement Puzzle:

✔ Identify — WHO are you at this time in your life?
✔ Imagine — WHAT are your possibilities?
✔ Innovate — WHICH direction are you going?
✔ Implement — HOW are you going to move forward?

Once you have solved the Retirement Puzzle, you will:
✔ Identify and pursue options that leverage your strengths.
✔ Make better choices with confidence.
✔ Do things that matter most to you.
✔ Be more motivated and have more energy.
✔ Enhance the relationships that mean the most to you.
✔ Live a joy-filled and grateful life during your Encore Years.

I believe planning for your Encore Years is not a problem to be solved but an opportunity to be created. I would love to help you create the retirement you really want rather than take the one that just shows up.
