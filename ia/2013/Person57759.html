Ray Massie's Summary

Hi -- my name is Ray and I am a Graphic Designer. I have nearly 20 years experience in graphic communications, from publishing to multi-channel marketing in both the print and digital realms. I like clean, effective design and well-crafted user experiences. Grilled cheese sandwiches are pretty awesome, too.

Some quick points:
• Designed and produced "Bona Fide Blackjack" for iPhone, available for FREE in the App store.
• Created a UI/UX concept for an online magazine which resulted in 400% increase in both page views and click-thrus.
• Created and implemented processes and best practices for start-up teams.
• Comfortable working in agile environments.
• Team oriented, detailed, conscientious, self-starter.

Specialties

Primary: design, typography, production, wireframes, concepts, strategy, UI/UX, Adobe Creative Suite, Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Adobe Digital Publishing Suite

Secondary: HTML, CSS, jQuery, Dreamweaver, AppleScript
