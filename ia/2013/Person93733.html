  • Lord of the Rings: War in the North

    • September 2008 to July 2011
    Team Members: Mic Marvin, Dustin DeVoe, Brent Barrett, Branden Drake, Ian McCoy, Adin Clark, RJ Martin, Jason Booth, Thomas Johnson, James Ross, TJ Perillo, TJ Stamm, Shea Vos, Sam Robinson, Ralph Lewis, Mike Beach, Matthew Holdener, Lee Rosenberg, Steve Chiavelli, Damon Thompson, Naomi Steele, Ruth Tomandl, Charles Kim, Kim Marlis, Joe Janca, Dacey Willoughby

    A 3rd-Person, Action-RPG set in the "Lord of the Rings" universe with a focus on three-player cooperative gameplay. Released for the Xbox 360, PS3, and PC.

    The title features, in both narrative and gameplay, a "fourth member" to the 3-player co-op experience in the form of a giant eagle named "Beleram". Players are able to summon Beleram to aid in the battle by either directing him to a specific target or allowing him to choose his victim. Once summoned, Beleram swoops to the field, grabbing and tearing at the opponents in his path.
