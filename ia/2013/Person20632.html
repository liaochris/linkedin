Carrie Wrigley's Summary

Has worked as a counselor for twenty years, specializing in treating depression, relationship problems, and trauma resolution. Has done many presentations and talks on topics related to emotional wellness and strengthening relationships. Has written and performed hundreds of songs putting these healing concepts to music. Early background was in the performing arts (acting, singing, songwriting.) Recently called upon to oversee website development for several counseling organizations. Loves using the arts and technology to promote and facilitate the healing process.

Specialties

Providing counseling for depression, anxiety, marital conflict, grief/loss issues, sexual addiction, and resolution of abuse trauma. Presenting multi-media presentations on emotional wellness and relationship enhancement.
