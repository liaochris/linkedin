Janette Dywasuk's Summary

Experienced, passionate marketer with 16 years in the business. I work to help companies become better at marketing through effective storytelling using the best channels available. Paid to think, I work with teams to create marketing strategies that produce results. I drive development of visionary, revenue-driving marketing plans and strategies, brand development and integrated marketing and advertising campaigns.

I strive to make connections and build relationships with colleagues and customers that are based on trust and respect. I am typically well-regarded for my ability to make a positive impact within organizations at all levels and contribute to the bottom line.

Direct career experience in marketing is extensive, with passion driving my success at every level. I have relevant hands-on experience in managing budgets and building top-notch marketing plans and teams for both national and international companies.

Specific skills include: Marketing plans & strategies, project management, print, email campaigns, website design and development, content creation and management, website analytics, paid and organic seo, internet marketing techniques, social media strategy and execution, photography, graphics and video.

Specialties: Website design and development, SEO and PPC, social media tools and strategies, internet marketing, video, photography, & Photoshop.
