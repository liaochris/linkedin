Brian Pate's Summary

I have been in the real estate business since 1993 as an agent, instructor, manager and coach.
Currently, I am the President Elect of the North Carolina Real Estate Educators Association.

Specialties

My real estate sales expertise is in the Triangle of North Carolina with a focus in North Raleigh, Wake Forest and Rolesville.
Along with that, I am a certified by the NC Real Estate Commission to teach pre-licensing, post license and continuing education classes throughout the state.
