Technical Lead

Senior Programmer Analyst

JDA Software

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; JDAS; Computer Software industry

Senior Programmer Analyst working on several different projects. JDA is an international software company that specializes in POS (point of sale) software for Tier 2 and Tier 3 retailers. JDA is recognized as the leader in the industry with their software installed on more cash registers than any other company in the world. The software I work on is their WinDSS product line. My division is Consulting Services Group for In Store Systems (CSG-ISS). The CSG division mostly works on enhancements and modifications for clients.
Acted as project leader on a Phase 3 installation for a client with over 500 stores. In charge of managing source code and supervising three programmers. Wrote dozens of technicals and worked closely with project manager as well as client. Personally coded several complex portions of Phase 3. Project was ahead of schedule. Completed successful on-site implementation of Phase 2 in October 1998 for same client at their corporate headquarters. Client happy with Phase 2 implementation and says automated coupon module I personally wrote will save them over one million dollars the first year.
Part of team for Y2K compliance. Worked on both Clarion for DOS products and Clarion for Windows products.

Partner and CIO

Paladin Telecom

October 1994 – January 1997 (2 years 4 months)

Started by advising purchase of debit card switch and writing scripts for special features. Quickly switched to better hardware and personally rewrote entire switch from scratch using Clarion for DOS. Responsibilities included writing all software to start and maintain a debit card company which sold MexiCall cards as well as other telecard brands.

Also responsible for directing marketing and promotional material. Reviewed and selected hardware. Supervised hardware upgrade and provided specifications then directed C++ contractor in writing switching software for OS/2. Created import utilities to continue using other applications. Wrote billing software using Clarion for DOS 3.1 to handle customers of international phone cards in ten countries for major Philippine client, World Communications, Inc.

Designed and wrote software to automate the Least Cost Routing based on carrier, country and time of day. Program analyzed each carrier's rates (including time of day) and created a database of optimal routes for any given time of day for any given country. Debit card switch used this database for routing all calls. Involved in extensive research and implementation in contracting, installing and maintaining T1 digital circuits, channel banks and multi-plexers.
