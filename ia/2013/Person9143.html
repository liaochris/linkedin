Darrel Dickson's Summary

PCMIGroup and it's partners have been buying apartment buildings since the early 1990's. I got started purchasing apartment buildings in the middle of the Savings and Loan crisis at that time. Currently PCMIGroup owns 1,577 apartment units in 15 apartment communities throughout the Western United States . My staff only operates apartment buildings in which I am the managing partner and have significant ownership in the apartment complex. Over the years I have developed the ability to search out undervalued and poorly managed apartment complexes and other underperforming assets, and purchase them from financial institutions or other entities.

As an owner of apartment buildings I have developed a talented group of associates who are able to turn around underperforming apartment buildings and other real estate assets which has resulted in signficant cash flow and capital appreciation. We are also licensed contractors and are able to complete development, and major rehab projects ourselves.

PCMIGroup is able to procure long term fixed rate financing on our transactions, that result in principal reduction, when you add that component to the cash flow and appreciation our yields over the year have been predictable and safe.

Specialties: Licensed Real Estate Broker in Washington and California.
Graduated with Bachelor of Science from BYU in Financial and Estate Planning. Specialize in tax, insurance, funding, refinancing,1031 exchanges, and syndication.
Owner of ApartmentsforSale.com. (Advertising company)
