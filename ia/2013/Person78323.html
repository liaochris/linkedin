Matt LaPrairie's Experience

Co-Founder & CEO

420 Science

Partnership; 11-50 employees; Retail industry

2004 – Present (9 years) Austin, TX

420 Science - The creators of 420 Jars, the 420 Scope, 420 Wipes, Smoke Soap, 420 Preserve and RezBlock. Our mission is to maximize your smoking enjoyment. Gary and I started this company in our spare time when I was 24. After a year it grew to the size that demanded my full-time attention, so I left my job in corporate america. Today we are a team of 10 and together we've built an international brand that's focused on the needs of our customers.

Software Developer

Ticketmaster

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; TKTM; Entertainment industry

October 2000 – May 2005 (4 years 8 months) Phoenix, AZ

I was a programmer in the PCI department at TicketMaster. We wrote the software that the call center agents use to sell tickets. I've done work for professional sports teams, Broadway performances, the 2004 Olympics in Greece and numerous musical acts. However, the highlight of my time there was when I won the 1st annual Ticketmaster Phoenix Open - an indoor miniature golf course setup inside the office.

DSL Support

Earthlink

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; ELNK; Telecommunications industry

January 2000 – October 2000 (10 months) Phoenix, AZ

I worked on DSL Support team in the Phoenix location. The company was just getting into broadband and had fewer than 1,000 DSL customers at the time. I left the company due to an increasingly corporate culture and an opportunity to be a software developer at Ticketmaster.

Dialup Support

Retail Sales
