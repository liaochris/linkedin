Lauron Buys' Summary

I am an experienced international executive coach and have helped numerous successful leaders and senior managers become even more effective by consistently and effectively getting the best out of their people, their teams and themselves.
I have more than 20 years’ experience in Leadership, Executive and Strategic roles in law, commerce, education and sport.
I have been practising as a fulltime Executive Coach since 2001, in South Africa, the UK and New Zealand. During that time, I have worked successfully in various market sectors, including the Banking, Insurance, Retail, Healthcare, Mining and Automotive industries. My clients provide me with numerous referrals and more than half of my 60 corporate clients have provided me with repeat mandates after my initial successes.
I believe that much of this success is founded on my ability to build safe, trusting relationships relatively quickly, and to raise the self- and social awareness of my clients. My aim is to help my clients leave lasting legacies to the generations that follow them thereby perpetuating their new-found effectiveness.

I hold a Diploma in Professional Coaching (Coaching Centre, Cape Town) and the internationally recognised Workplace Coach Training programme (Results Coaching). I am currently completing a further internationally renowned programme with Integral Coaching Canada. I am a founder member of Comensa (Coaching and Mentoring South Africa) and a member of the International Coach Federation (ICF) and I abide by their respective codes of ethics and conduct.

I am the author of three books, Management by Coaching – 7 Basic Keys (2007), High-Performance Coaching for Managers (2010) and Shooting the Lights Out: A Guide to Consistent Effective Management. I am also the author of some 180 articles that have been published in the daily press and professional online magazines.
