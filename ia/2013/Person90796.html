Haiko Meelis' Summary

Catalyst for organizational change. Management professional with over 14 years of experience as management consultant in helping businesses to translate their strategy into a simple plan that everyone can understand and help to implement it. So: Organizing for success, action and results. He worked mainly in telecoms and internet environments. Industry experience in telecoms, media, entertainment, electronics and banking. His direct experience ranges from start-up companies to corporate environments.

Haiko is quite obsessed with customer delight and particularly interested in the definition and implementation of new concepts, products and services that use today’s technology to realize previously unthought-of opportunities. On top of that Haiko is interested in how you need to organize your organization to do all that. Keywords: radical management, agile, lean.

Knowledgeable person, professional attitude, very driven, pragmatic, entrepreneurial and able to communicate on different levels (from work floor to boardroom). Able to draw a realistic plan from imperfect or scattered information in an uncertain environment (expert intuition).

Strength: activator, maximizer, relator, strategic, self-assurance

Married and 3 children. Based in the Netherlands. Lived and worked in Europe, Asia and Africa.

Holding a MSc in Computer Science of the University of Leiden, a MSc in Business of the Nyenrode Business University. Currently studying Philosophy at Birkbeck College, University of London.

Specialties: - Change Management
- Radical Management: End-to-end Agile Way of Working
- Program & Project Management
- Governance
- Contract & Vendor Management
- Innovation & Portfolio Management
- Risk Management
- Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)
