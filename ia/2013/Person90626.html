Matt Newman's Summary

Matt is an MBA student at the University of Michigan focusing on e-commerce, digital media, and consumer products. He is currently a Business Manager Intern at Amazon. Matt also spent six weeks, during the summer of 2012, as an intern at Totsy, a mid-stage e-commerce startup.

He is also the leader of the Frankel Commercialization Fund’s Consumer Team. The Frankel Commercialization Fund is a pre-seed investment fund led by University of Michigan students.

While in school, Matt helps out at RPM Ventures, an early-stage venture capital firm based in Ann Arbor, MI.

Matt worked in corporate finance at Steamboat Ventures, the VC arm of Disney, for 3 years before starting his MBA. At Steamboat, Matt was responsible for reporting and analyzing the firm’s portfolio companies’ financial results, analyzing term-sheets for upcoming investments, reviewing legal documents, and creating models for the firm’s current portfolio. Matt was also responsible for the management of Steamboat’s financial statements. Prior to joining Steamboat, Matt earned his CPA license while serving as an auditor at Porter & Company. Matt holds a B.S. in Business, with an emphasis in finance, from Liberty University.

Specialties

Advising seed and early-stage companies on financing and strategic decisions.
