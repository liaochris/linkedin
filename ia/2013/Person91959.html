Micah Gast's Summary

For me as a designer, I enjoy this field because you're not limited to what you can create because the possibilities are endless. Each client I work with brings something different to the table with what they're expecting.

I'm proficient in website design, using my knowledge in Dreamweaver, Illustrator, and Photoshop to help bring my ideas to concept. Then taking that through use of CSS, Javascripts, and Flash animations to have an end product that easy to maintain, navigate and appealing to the viewer.

With that, branding, page layout, print design has been equally important to me. Within in Richmond alone, I've worked on signs for local business, service vehicles, menus for various restaurants, and promotion material for local colleges.

Specialties

Website design and hosting, print design and production, logo recreating and creation.
