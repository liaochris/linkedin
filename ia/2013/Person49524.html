Gordon James Bovéll's Summary

James Bovéll is the director and Maximum Performance Coach of The Mushin Project. He is a certified 2nd degree black belt martial arts instructor. In the past decade, as an executive coach, personal trainer, and kickboxing instructor, James has personally trained and coached over 1,000 clients. Several are top executives from companies such as Coca-Cola, Bellsouth, Cisco Systems, Summit National Bank, CNN, Diaz Foods, Oracle, IBM and many more.

Specialties

Business development, leadership development, brand/graphics designer, martial arts and combat instructor, strength and fitness coach
