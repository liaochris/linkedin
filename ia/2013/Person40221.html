Ekaterina Walter's Summary

As a Social Media Strategist for Intel, I work at the intersection of high tech and integrated marketing—driving campaigns and training colleagues to leverage new media in support of corporate goals. I’m aggressive in pursuing corporate challenges and in transcending cultural boundaries along the way. I’ve detailed my track record in these areas below.

The summary of my work history and achievements presents an incomplete picture of who I am. At heart, I am enthusiastic, innately curious, and can be fearless in pursuit of my goals. I am a passionate student of social media, marketing, and brand management. Throughout my career (from my studies earning 2 Bachelor’s degrees to my recent MBA) I have worked to translate theoretical concepts into practice – from my volunteer work with budding entrepreneurs in Alaska to my work customizing and rolling out integrated marketing strategies for Intel Developer Forum, a tradeshow held on three continents. Along the way, I learned the value of market research and of sharing consumer opinions.

So, in order to describe myself, I asked my colleagues for insight through a voluntary and confidential professional assessment. Forty respondants chose the following descriptors to depict my style: enthusiastic, energetic, outgoing, competitive, intelligent, self-motivated and “international.”

I take an interdisciplinary approach to my work drawing upon best practices in operations, event management, and integrated marketing. My colleagues recognize my ability to manage projects (I recently became a certified project manager), exceed success targets, and my thirst for professional development and big picture thinking. The number one strength they identified was my ability to think strategically and globally. To borrow a phrase from the Seven Habits of Highly Effective People, I believe in beginning projects with the “end in mind” – give me a goal and I’ll work tirelessly to achieve it.

Specialties

Social Media and digital marketing; Global integrated marketing; Global demand creation and go-to-market activities; Channel and reseller marketing; Event marketing and trade show management; Customer and field training; Project management; International relations; Business process improvement
