Colleen Madden's Summary

Colleen Madden has been a full time real estate agent since 2003. During this time she has been awarded in Recognition of Superior Performance the Gold and Silver Certificates of Achievement. She has sold homes though out Marin ranging from the low hundred thousands to several million dollars, giving each client the same level of respect, energy and enthusiasm. Her success is based on her ethics, values and strong negotiating skills creating high client satisfaction, long standing relationships and repeat business.
She is a graduate of San Francisco State University earning a Bachelor of Science degree in Business with an emphasis in Marketing. Colleen has been in Sales and Marketing for over 25 years.
Colleen is a third generation Marin resident. She lives in Mill Valley with her family, enjoys the outdoors, loves abalone diving, swimming, running and mountain biking.

Specialties: Real Estate Sales in Marin.
