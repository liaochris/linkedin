Linda Bellisario's Summary

Joined Windermere in 1984 as a Sales Associate.. In 1989 asked to open the first Pierce County office. Managed 30 agents & was asked to become Vice President of Windermere Relocation. Served corporate relocation clients until 1997 when I returned to listing & selling residential properties....I missed working with actual Sellers and Buyers! Am now a top producing Managing Broker and specialize in King, Pierce and Kitsap Counties.
My goal is a smooth move for YOU and raving fans as clients. I do that with integrity, efficiency and care!

Specialties: Accredited Residential Specialist for Seniors (SRES), Corporate relocation, Assist companies with Recruiting, marketing, negotiating, customer needs analysis, assisting clients with financing, & much more!
