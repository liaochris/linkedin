Anil Chhikara's Summary

It has been an eventful journey - especially so since I turned an entrepreneur!

I founded 24x7 Learning (www.24x7learning.com) in 2001. It today is India's largest eLearning implementation company. Some of 24x7 Learning’s marquee clients include: Aditya Birla Group, Essar, Accenture, Wipro, GMR, Barclays Bank, L&T, Dell, Samsung, Vodafone etc. End of 2007, we raised $4m from Network18, India's leading full play media conglomerate that controls India’s leading television channels - CNBC TV18, CNN IBN, MTV, Nick, VH1 etc.

After spending more than 12 years building 24x7 Learning, now moving on to create something new, something fresh!

A Mechanical Engineer, I have more than 24 years of experience in the IT industry with companies like HCL-Hewlett Packard, Wipro, Parametric Technology Corporation, USA, Patni and Cimatron. Before founding 24x7 Learning, I successfully set-up PTC's office in India and then the operations for Cimatron. From an unknown brand, we were able to establish Cimatron as a major player in its target segment within a year of commencing operations.

Having been an avid sculptor and artist and having won many national level prizes while in college, I have now got back in touch with my old passion - sculpting... an amazing stress buster!

I love to connect with people from different cultures and industries as each such encounter leaves me (and hopefully others) enriched! Now working on my next venture...

Specialties

Demand creation, international partnerships, setting up distribution network, acquiring marquee customers and high-value solution selling in the software industry. Having been involved in successfully eLearning implementation at some of India's largest corporates. Love to mentor new start-up teams and helping with key-account acquisition, business development and channel sales strategies
