Judith Gordon's Summary

As a long time business owner and resident of Howard County, my knowledge of the community and strong attention to detail has proven invaluable to my clients.

Prior to my career in Real Estate, I owned and operated four local Jerry´s Subs & Pizza restaurants and the retail sports establishment, Simply Soccer in Columbia, Maryland while raising my children. With over 29 years of customer service experience, I am well equipped with the professionalism, motivation, ethics and fairness you deserve while buying or selling your home.

Above all, I realize the true meaning of home. Home is our sanctuary. Home is where we raise our families, entertain our friends, and find comfort at the end of each day. I consider it an honor and privilege to represent you while buying or selling your home. I take this responsibility very seriously and look forward to having the opportunity to work with you to ensure that your real estate experience is a pleasant and rewarding one.

I am a multi-million dollar producer!
