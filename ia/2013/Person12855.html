Ben Eisenstein's Summary

Mr. Eisenstein has extensive experience in both database administration and data architecture of complex state-of-the-art information systems. He possesses a wide range of software and hardware experience including: Data Warehousing, Data Modeling, Client-Server, Mainframe and Scalable Solutions. His experience includes work as a DBA, DA and developer in the financial, telecommunications, retail, and petroleum industries.
