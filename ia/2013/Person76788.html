Kris Black's Summary

I started my career as a professional graphic designer 2000. I've grown and expanded my knowledge, practice, and skills as a web designer. I've worked for two Fortune 500 companies.

I've had various titles throughout my career including: graphic designer, art director, freelance web designer, customer support specialist, and senior front-end web developer. In short I like to think my most current titles are designer, author, and cartoonist.

I've written two For Dummies books, both about Squarespace. I specialize in building and designing websites using Squarespace.

As a partner at black&hue, LLC, I help individuals, small businesses and non-profits establish a broad and engaging reach online through their personal and business Web sites, social media, and email campaigns.

Specialties: Squarespace; HTML; CSS; mobile web design; user interface design; user experience; usability; Mac OS X; advanced knowledge of Adobe Creative CLoud; active blogging and podcasting experience; cartooning; and comic book lettering.
