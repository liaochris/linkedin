Ben Renegar's Summary

Ben has been involved in all manner of enterprise IT delivery projects, across a range of industries, performing primarily in roles such as User Experience Consultant/Designer, Business Analyst, Information Architect, Development Lead, UI Designer, and Web Application Developer (both "front" and "back" end). User-centered Design philosophy has consistently played an integral part in all roles he has performed.

He is passionate about the web and about creating great user experiences that increasingly span multiple channels. He enjoys showing technical leadership, pushing things to the boundary and staying in front of what's next, serving as a mentor to junior employees, collaborating with and engaging others along the way.

Ben has solid oral and written communication skills and is a confident and articulate presenter which has seen him successfully facilitate and lead numerous workshops and training programmes, present at industry conferences, and corporate events.

He has a broad skill-set ranging from technical to business to creative and visual design and is also a qualified secondary and adult-education teacher. He is equally comfortable performing in highly technical, creative, business and people-oriented positions.
