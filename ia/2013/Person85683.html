Maria Guida's Summary

I help savvy business professionals and executives enhance their credibility and generate business by speaking with poise, passion, and persuasive power. I bring Broadway to business, coaching executives in speaking strategies rooted in theatrical performance. As a Broadway, film, & TV actor, I've worked with Paul Newman, James Earl Jones, and Kevin Kline. Actors know how to move audiences.

Every time you speak for business, you are on stage; acting techniques & performance skills are fundamental to the business of building credibility & selling any idea, product, or service. As a speaking strategist at Fortune 500 companies, I have adapted acting techniques to help business executives deliver powerhouse performances. I have also coached celebrities, TV newscasters, professional speakers, attorneys, and business owners. I will help you project star quality and the image you desire: in the office, boardroom, courtroom, and media.

I’ll show you how to influence your business listeners with authority and authenticity. Each program is completely custom-designed; private, semi-private, and group sessions are available. For ongoing tips on great business speaking, subscribe to my VideoBlog at http://successfulspeakerinc.com/blog/

Specialties: Specific areas of expertise:
Cultivating "presence; Creative problem-solving; Thinking/speaking quickly on one's feet; Maintaining speaker power with PowerPoint; Acting improvisation and rehearsal techniques that help you appear completely spontaneous. Writing with persuasive power. All communication skills for the foreign-born executive, including foreign accent reduction.

Maria Guida's Experience

President and Founder

Successful Speaker, Inc.

Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Professional Training & Coaching industry

January 2002 – Present (12 years 1 month)

I have coached hundreds of individuals in all industries/professions: executives & corporate professionals at all levels, attorneys, business owners, doctors, and TV newscasters, etc. I have helped them speak both formally and informally in a wide variety of situations: from speaking to the SEC to speaking to clients/prospects with or without PowerPoint; from speaking on television to delivering voicemail messages that get results -- and even helping foreign-born professionals project their best image when communicating in the US. I use sound pedagogical methods, some of which are traditional and some are experiential techniques from the theater that help make exciting transformations in the speaking style of my business clients.

Adjunct Professor

New York University

Educational Institution; 10,001+ employees; Higher Education industry

September 1988 – May 2002 (13 years 9 months)

Four fourteen years, I was adjunct assistant professor at NYU's School of Continuing & Professional Studies, where I taught all communications skills to business professionals and MBA, doctoral, and JD candidates from the US and abroad -- helping them move out of their comfort zones of mediocre speaking to the persuasive power of confident, passionate, and focused communication. I taught Presentation Skills, Business Writing, Speech, and Theater classes, as well as all communication skills for foreign-born business professionals (including foreign accent reduction). I conducted teacher-training seminars for Hunter College, the Multifunctional Resource Center, Long Island University, Marymount College NY Adult Basic Education Conference, Jersey City State College Conference on the Role of Imagination in Learning, and the International and NY State TESOL Conferences.

Actor on Broadway, TV & Film

Maria as Spokesperson & Actor

September 1976 – December 1996 (20 years 4 months)

As an actor, I have worked with some of the greats, including Paul Newman, James Earl Jones, and Kevin Kline. Along with my performances on Broadway (including the Tony Award-winning musical, Pirates of Penzance), I played principle roles in Shakespeare and other classic plays and on the soap operas "As the World Turns" and "One Life to Live". I also appeared in the film "Fort Apache the Bronx" and on "Saturday Night Live", the "Jerry Lewis Telethon", and dozens of television commercials.
