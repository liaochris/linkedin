Cyril Moshkow's Summary

Publisher and editor of Russian niche music magazine and Web portal, with broad experience in printed media (20 years in the field) and online media (15 years in the field.) Extensive knowledge of music history, solid knowledge of music theory, music technology, and music industry, exceptional skills in writing on music, music events reporting, and music industry research. 10+ years of experience in teaching music journalism. Skilled in building niche online community, offline readership community, and successful targeting niche businesses on niche audiences. Serious training in radio presenting, including rich experience on nationwide radio channels and TV. Interested in broadening the magazine's and Web portal's business contacts and liasions, helping to bring new services, products, and businesses to Russia's steadily growing market, and targeting them to highly educated and generally well-to-do niche audiences. Also interested in broad international contacts with new writers and other types of contributors for the music-oriented media.

Specialties: Media management, media editing, music writing, radio presenting, music industry research

Cyril Moshkow's Publications

  • Jazz Industry in America

    • Planet of Music
    • August 1, 2008
    Authors: Cyril Moshkow

    'Jazz Industry in America' was first published in Russian
    by Planeta Muzyki in 2008. The book was based on a collection of more than 40 in-person and several more phone and email interviews with American jazz educators, club owners, festival organizers, scholars, radio presenters, record label executives, producers, sound engineers and others who create and support jazz. The first edition sold well, and revealed demand for the second, enhanced edition, titled Jazz Industry in America, 21st Century. About 15 interviews were added, and several chapters were totally rewritten, as the music industry experienced dramatic shifts in the past five years. Scheduled for release in July, 2013.
