L H's Summary

Lou Huskey, Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW) and Job Interview / Career Coach, served over 25 years in creating effective resumes and coaching candidates to improve their interviewing techniques. As a professional recruiter, first with a private employment agency, then with Management Recruiters, Inc., and eventually as owner of her own personnel recruiting and consulting firm, she has prepared thousands of resumes, CVs and letter packages and federal documents for candidates at all levels within a wide variety of companies over the span of her career.

Being prepared for an interview is a critical part of a candidate's job search. As a Job Interview Coach, Lou assists candidates in being thoroughly prepared for telephone and face-to-face interviews.

The main focus of this service is to make sure crucial facets of your career and educational history stand out when preparing resumes as well as helping clients become better prepared for interviews with potential employers.

Lou has a solid understanding of precisely what prospective employers are looking for in a resume. She is adept with crafting resumes, CVs, federal resumes, KSAs, ECQs, PTQs, cover letters, follow-up, and thank you letters that effectively "sell" an individual's background. Her experience also includes extensive knowledge of interview strategies and career counseling, which has proven to be of great value in enabling individuals to perform confidently and successfully during face-to-face interviews.

An effective resume is the fundamental tool you will use to make your initial impression with potential companies or institutions. As such, the document’s main focus should relay to the reader the key accomplishments you have made during your academic and professional career. We strive to provide exceptional customer service as we endeavor to craft documents that portray your unique experience in a way that is both engaging and compelling within the current job market.
