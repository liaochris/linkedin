  • GEO-Pictures

    • March 2010 to February 2011
    Team Members: Alexandra Dammann, Harald Skinnemoen, Harm Bastian Harms, Håkon Eggemoen, Roberto Aroso Cardoso, Alois A. Hirschmugl

    GEO-Pictures is an integrated geo-service platform for management of disasters and emergencies in the fields of global humanitarian and environmental operations. It supports collecting field observations (geo-referenced high quality photos, video, text, sound, various sensors) and making relevant information (rapid mapping results, expert advice, etc.) available rapidly in the field. GEO-Pictures is an all-in-one solution for field staff (assessment experts, USAR, etc.) and deploys micro-drones for aerial assessment. All this using new protocols for data transfer, thus reducing bandwidth and saving money.
