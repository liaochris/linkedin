Dan Prothero's Projects

  • ExSponge plugin for ExpressionEngine

    • March 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Dan Prothero

    ExSponge is a real-time HTML filter and WYSIWYG / Rich Text editor cleanup plugin for ExpressionEngine, which has been in active use and under ongoing development for several years. I've recently made it available to other developers as an open source GitHub project. Written in PHP.

  • ExpressionEngine syntax highlighting

    • May 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Dan Prothero

    I completely rewrote Sublime Text's grammar definition for ExpressionEngine, upgrading its almost unusably primitive support for EE to what is now the most comprehensive syntax highlighting and hinting available in any editor. EE developers inevitably leave the control panels behind and work in an external code editor for the entire development process; this no longer needs to be a compromise.

  • Echocardiogram Evaluator

    Team Members: Dan Prothero

    While in high school, I developed a computer assisted echocardiogram evaluator for a local cardiologist. Built with an Apple II computer (plus third-party hardware) and programmed from scratch in Applesoft BASIC and 6502 Assembler, the system provided many of the functions of Hewlett-Packard's at 9% of the cost. It remained in use in his private practice for ten years and saved him more than $100,000.

  • Music Packaging

    Team Members: Dan Prothero

    Working as a graphic designer of music packaging (personally creating over 100 LP and CD packages for major and independent record labels) has deepened my appreciation for clear communication through elegant design, appropriate typography, and the elimination of clutter -- all of which broadens my perspective as a developer.
