Gale Twersky's Summary

Began as English and speech arts high school teacher and Individual Events speech coach in Wheeling, IL. and was also Director of Speech Activities for WHS in 1971-72. In 1984-1999, worked as Public Speaking coach and Image Consultant (President, Glassner Associates Speech and Image Consultants) Since March, 1999, HypnoCounselor and President of Glassner Associates Hypnosis for Personal Growth and Wellness, HypnoCounselor/hypnotherapist, featured motivational speaker for personal growth and wellness using hypnosis as the main modality. Recording artist, writer (Hypnosis CDs and book). First Goal: To teach self-empowerment skills to empower people to lead happier, healthier and more meaningful lives through proven Mind/Body/Spirit techniques... Second Goal: To help people triumph over their challenging life issues...Third Goal: To use all my years of experience, expertise, intelligence, wisdom, intuition and spiritual understanding to accomplish Goals #One and #Two.

Specialties: Creator/instructor of certification program for hypnosis specialty, Reprogramming Hypnosis; Writer (In 2010 completed updated and greatly expanded book edition of Reprogram Your Subconscious: How to Use Hypnosis to Get What You Really Want); Authored Nightingale-Conant CD program; Editor for articles, books; CD recording artist (first project was Relax, Release and Dream On), hypnotherapist/HypnoCounselor w/ specialties in Post Traumatic Stress, Self-esteem, Relationships, Anxiety, Fears, Surgery Prep, Positive Thinking and Grief
