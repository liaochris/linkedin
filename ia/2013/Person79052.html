Lauren Eastman's Summary

Lauren is a seasoned public relations executive with more than 12 years of experience creating and implementing PR campaigns for clients in hospitality, food & wine, lifestyle and design. She has also worked with numerous Fortune 500 companies and international governments. She launched Issima! Public Relations in 2009 and represents luxury, hospitality, food, wine and lifestyle companies. Lauren's fresh approach and ability to tell a story combined with her ability to adapt to the ever-changing dynamic of the public relations field sets her apart in the field. Specialties include Lifestyle, Hospitality, Food, Wine, Interior Design, International Relations, writing, branding, media relations, website development, strategic counsel.
