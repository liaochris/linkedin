Jason Gregory's Summary

Jason Gregory has over 12 years of global, start up, commercial and corporate experience within the digital industry. Jason has built a successful career creating and leading innovative commercial and technology solutions in digital marketing, search and competitive intelligence. As an early employee of Hitwise Inc (now part of Experian plc), he was integral in growing the business to be one of the most recognised global Internet measurement brands. Jason currently sits on the global product and technology senior leadership team for Experian Hitwise, leading employees across North America, United Kingdom, Malaysia and Asia Pacific.

Jason has a strong track record of improving productivity and scale to drive growth globally, with a focus on Product Management, Strategy and Innovation. Jason is passionate, driven, and is inspired by working with and leading multi-disciplinary and diverse global teams in the digital sector.

Jason holds a Bachelor of Business (Marketing), and is an active member of the Product Management industry presenting and hosting Product Camps and attending Agile Development and Product Management conferences.
