Heather Ozur's Summary

Heather Ozur & The Ozur Group have built a solid reputation as the REALTORS® who “Bring Buyers and Sellers Together”. This reputation stems from a combination of extensive sales and business management experience, shrewd negotiation skills, creative marketing strategies and polished communication acumen. Heather and The Ozur Group count good old fashioned, down-to-earth professionalism and personalization as their most valuable team trait.

They credit much of their success to their ability to interact with a broad spectrum of people and develop marketing plans that suit their individual needs. This unique personal attention comes natural for The Ozur Group who knows that a move across town or anywhere in the country is an emotional experience. They offer their clients more than knowledge of the real estate market; they provide their clients with understanding, compassion and honest representation that enables their clients to feel at home in their new community.

Through expert negotiation skills, excellence in service and utmost integrity in business, The Ozur Group have earned the reputation as the most trusted name for Real Estate in the Coachella Valley. Desert residents place their faith in this Team and their staff of professionals knowing that The Ozur Group will exceed their expectation and protect their interest always. The Ozur Group clients are clients for life.

Serving the areas of:
Palm Springs, Cathedral City, Rancho Mirage, Palm Desert, Indian Wells, La Quinta, Indio, Coachella and surrounding Coachella Valley Cities.
