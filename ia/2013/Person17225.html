Brian Burridge's Summary

Brian is an entrepreneur developer with over 16 years experience in building web applications. Brian provides Ruby on Rails development services and IT consulting, specializing in working with startups, through his company Agile Nomads.

In both 2009 and 2010, Brian led teams of four to compete in the global 48 hour hackathon, Rails Rumble. In 2009 his team created PeepNote (peepnote.com) which finished 8th out of 237 teams worldwide and was named by Mashable as one of the '11 New Apps We Love'.

In 2010, Brian's team created CommendableKids.com, which finished in 4th place out of 180 teams, won the Appearance Category, was named a runner up from Chargify as Most Potential to Monetize, was the #1 team from the U.S. and listed as a Tech Crunch Top 5.

Brian is an experienced leader in the production of web sites and Internet based software with varying scopes from those with multi-million dollar revenue to major celebrities, to local leaders in advertising and marketing. He has led large and small size teams using varying sets of Internet technologies, with a focus on Java, Ruby on Rails, AJAX, and relational databases. Brian has served as Creative Director on many web sites and served as a coordinator between all the skill sets needed to produce a web application from concept through to the final product, including marketing, architecture and development, graphical and user interface design.

During Brian's extensive career he has worked for many high profile clients with heavy usage and performance requirements, using both his Java/J2EE and Ruby on Rails development skills. Clients include the hit star Miley Cyrus, Authentic Jobs, TourWrist, Intercontinental Hotels, IBM, Brighthouse, Syniverse, and ValPak.

Specialties: Ruby on Rails, Thinking Sphinx, DelayedJob, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Ajax, jQuery, HTML/CSS, Heroku, Amazon S3, startups.
