Delno Ebie SAG/AFTRA's Experience

Partner

Fire And Water Entertainment, LLC

June 2012 – Present (1 year 4 months) Wichita, Kansas Area

Fire And Water Entertainment, LLC was created by Delno Ebie and Suz Carr to develop and protect the intellectual properties that they create together as writers and producers. Combined they have over 40 years experience in the entertainment industry and have been involved in over 125 productions from Theatre, Film (Feature & Short), Television, Industrial and Training videos for companies like Hilti and the FAA, Educational CD Roms, Commercials and Infomercials, Music videos and Voiceover.

Some of the projects they have been involved in are: Walt Disney Pictures “The Haunted Mansion”, “Intolerable Cruelty”, “Mattie”, “Ambrose Bierce: Civil War Stories”, “ER”, “The District”, “Deadly Obsession”, “Craig Morgan’s International Harvester”, “Designing Spaces”, “Pilate”, “Dancing with the Devil”, “The Be All End All”, and “I Am Soccer” to name a few.

Stage productions include: “West Side Story”, “The Woman In Black”, “Heaven Can Wait”, “Hamlet”, “Romeo & Juliet”, “Seven Brides For Seven Brothers”, “A Christmas Carol”, “The Dresser”, “Little Women”, “The Wizard of Oz”, “The Simon Peter Passion Play”, “At The Foot of Black Mountain”, “The Best Christmas Pageant Ever”,”Annie Get Your Gun”, “Carnival” and many more.

Currently they are writing several feature film and television pilots, and getting ready to assist in several feature films in development. For more information go to www.delnoebie.net or Google Delno Ebie or Suz Carr. Fire And Water Entertainment website is currently under construction - www.fireandwaterentertainment.com.
