Michael N. Mercurio's Summary

Attorney that serves as outside general counsel to clients on matters related to corporate and business law, mergers and acquisitions, commercial transactions and real estate. Regularly counsels entrepreneurial individuals, government contractors, family firms and assorted other enterprises on all aspects of business and commerce including (i) formation and structure; (ii) ownership, management and control issues; (iii) financing and capital; (iv)expansion and acquisition; and (v) contraction and dissolution. Well versed in the various issues and challenges companies of all sizes and industries face in the business life cycle including start-up, maturation and end stage considerations.

Specialties: Business and commerce, mergers and acquisitions, real estate, contract, ownership succession planning and exit strategy, government contracting matters and structures and family business issues and considerations.
