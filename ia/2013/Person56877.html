How it all began...

nHarmony

July 1970 – June 2012 (42 years)

For most of my life, I felt like I was constantly searching for that elusive 'something' that would make me feel better, something that would make me feel whole.

When my partner of 15 years died overnight, I found myself in the awkward position of having to deal with unwanted emotions and the effects of sexual abuse.

I was told I needed to feel my emotions - but nobody told me EXACTLY how to do it. Due to this lack of knowledge I wallowed in my emotions and depressed myself for two years! Good advice without exact instructions will always exacerbate your problem instead of actually helping you.

Unable to find the complete answer from other people, I formulated techniques which helped me to feel better on a consistent level. I formulated programs and began teaching my methods.

After being hit by a drunk driver and left for dead, my mentor Paul had developed a unique switching technique which enabled him to completely heal his body and mind - which is amazing considering he lost his memory for two years and was told he would never walk again.

When Paul and I met, we discovered similarities in our teachings. However, Paul was able to provide me with the reason WHY my techniques usually worked (and why sometimes they didn't.) He then taught me the missing link which bridged the gap to guarantee success and eliminate failure forever. Paul explained to me HOW OUR BRAIN WORKS in the most simplest of layman's terms.

From his life experiences, Paul reasoned that most of our problems stem from a misplacement between our conscious mind and our subconscious mind and with the correct information these problems can easily be rectified - but when we have incorrect or incomplete information, we are unable to formulate a correct solution.

This unique method is 100% effective for healing all sexual, emotional and relationship issues and is unavailable elsewhere. It's simple to assimilate and integrate into your life - so you can end these problems forever.
