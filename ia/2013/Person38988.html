Dan Nguyen, ESQ.'s Summary

I am an international business attorney that provides as corporate counsel legal services for small to medium sized businesses. Whether they want to expand their market into Asia or into the United States, or just want counsel in navigating the legal minefields in their business, I can assist you or put you in contact with people who can.


PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE
- Act as general outside counsel to home-health care agencies with respect to a variety of business issues, including immigration, corporate maintenance, contracts, employment, new ventures, and taxes.
- Hands-on experience in civil litigation including drafting complaints and answers, propounding and answering discovery, drafting motions, court appearances.
- Draft and review various forms of contracts for businesses, including independent contractor agreements for software company and a residential rental agreement.
- Assist in large copyright infringement litigation where photos of products were being used on competitor's websites.

Specialties: International business, general corporate & business law, business & subsidiary structuring, real estate transactions, trademarks & copyrights, business negotiations, general business consulting.
