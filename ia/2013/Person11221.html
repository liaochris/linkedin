Per Axbom's Summary

I help companies and individuals reach their digital potential. The web scene has been my playground since the mid-nineties and I've helped small startups as well as global corporations with everything from strategy workshops to interaction design to coaching of the entire web team.

People hire me to inspire, teach, optimize and get to grips with challenges in managing business critical websites. More often than not I visualize problems and get everyone on board to work with the solution. Sometimes I just ask the right questions.

Since 2010 I run Axbom Innovation with a passion in everything from interaction design to corporate education. I get my clients in shape to give their customers a journey worth remembering.

Specialties

Creating insights from research, generating relevant ideas and solving the right problem with design. Oh yeah, and having fun doing it.
