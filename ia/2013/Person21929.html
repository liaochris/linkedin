Chandra Knee's Summary

Chandra Knee is the President of Dandelion PR, a public relations agency specializing in strategic planning and communications, media relations, brand development, and industry relations with a strong focus on hospitality brands including boutique & luxury hotels, fine dining, wine & spirits and luxury products. A hospitality industry and media relations expert, Knee focuses on the principle that PR campaigns should be a relationship-based marriage of client needs strategically crafted into story angles the media are interested in covering.

Knee has built lasting relationships equally with high-profile journalists and clients and currently represents Paso Robles-based hospitality clients Hotel Cheval, Il Cortile Ristorante, RE:Find Distillery, Arroyo Grande-based real estate development Las Ventanas Ranch at Talley Farms, and the National Rhone Wine Association the Rhone Rangers.

A native Californian, Knee has more than 10 years of public relations and marketing experience as well as a BA in Communications with an emphasis in Public Relations and Hospitality Management from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. With experience both in-house as well as with many respected hospitality-focused agencies, Knee best serves her clients by utilizing the experience she’s garnered with such recognized hospitality brands as Walt Disney Resorts & Hotels, Starwood Hotels & Resorts Worldwide, Pebble Beach Resorts, La Quinta Resort & Club and PGA West, Barton Creek Resort & Spa, The Historic Mission Inn Hotel & Spa, and destinations including Paso Robles, Marina del Rey and the Republic of Palau. During her career, Knee has developed an extensive knowledge of the hospitality industry and traveled throughout Europe, Asia, Mexico, Canada, the vast majority of the world's most traveled tropical islands and all 50 U.S. states.
