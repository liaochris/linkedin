Joan Muschamp (Fagnani)'s Summary

I am a marketing and social media strategist with over 35 years experience in marketing strategy and planning for small business. A well integrated marketing strategy and understanding of basic marketing concepts is essential for business owners so they are better able to delegate and manage their marketing plans, once we create a solid marketing strategy.

I give my clients access to the wealth of my marketing & business knowledge—including the “extra somethings” that came with years of marketing and social media trial, error, and learning that will help them implement a strategy driven tactical marketing plan to deliver results.

Leverage my strategic marketing experience and passion to grow your business!

My career has given me many opportunities to experience hands-on marketing and sales. I’ve successfully planned, executed, and attended marketing exhibits at over 350 trade shows and conferences, as well as individual marketing seminars, webinars, and other events.

I’ve managed Customer Service for a manufacturer, gaining insight into processes and their critical importance to success. I’ve sold advertising—yellow pages, newspaper, and magazine. A marketing promotional products expert, I’ve helped clients execute marketing and branding campaigns, merchandise programs and enhance company branding.

Ten years in technology firms taught me to embrace new technologies for marketing, digital marketing and social media strategies to improve marketing results as well as open new markets.
