Rocco Menzel's Projects

  • Man Machines - the unique robot act from Metroccolis

    • 2008 to Present

    Unmistakable with their white faces, white skin and elegantly dressed in black, the Man Machines from Metroccolis showcase a modern and unique performance: living robots float through the space switching their direction and dynamics from high-speed to slow-motion. The crowd wonders whether "human or machine?" and it seems impossible to take the eyes off these charismatic entities. Finally they set up a stage to reveal a nonverbal show full of electrifying dances and bizzare little stories rife with moments of pure mime poetry.

    "Look out for these silent robots from Germany - they may seem to be made of metal - but they will melt your hearts! Masters of silent mime Metroccolis live and breathe their characters, mesmerizing the audience with a silent art form well beyond antiquated mime clichés." - Preston City Guild Festival

  • Love Duet - a corporeal mime story

    • 2012 to Present

    A story as old as mankind. A story that is being told anew millions of times each and every day. A story that is repeating itself over and over again. Yet still, it's a story as unique as the people that are going through it.

    Silently, we are now about to tell you ours.

    Choreography & performance: Katja Tannert & Rocco Menzel
    Duration: 10 minutes
