Karen Murri's Summary

I specialize in consulting on performance support needs, especially as they relate to procedure and process documentation. I'm skilled at facilitating best practice discovery and in designing performance support solutions that are sharply focused on usabilty, functionality, and readability, all based on the audience's abilities and needs.

I currently manage the documentation web site for Wells Fargo's Institutional Retirement and support my internal clients by helping to identify needs and craft useful documentation solutions.

I have over 10 years experience in documentation consulting, technical writing, editing, and instructional design. I've completed projects for General Mills, Starbucks, 3M, Cardinal Health, Minute Maid, Miller Brewing, and many others. I usually have a lot of fun doing it, too.

Specialties

Information design. Document design. Clear writing. Documentation usability. Instructional design. Performance improvement. Consultation on knowledge capture and transfer issues. MS Word 2003. Vignette.
