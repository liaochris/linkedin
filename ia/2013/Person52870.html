Kathy Viskocil's Summary

Our family has been serving the Chicagoland area for over 75 years. Grandma and Grandpa Bicek started Bicek's House of Flowers in 1925 and we bought Heritage House Florist from the Nelson Family 18 years ago. We are dedicated to offering the personal touches you expect from a real family owned florist.
Our design staff is professional, experienced and award winning. We are ready to create not only traditional designed arrangements but contemporary or exotic arrangements to suit your floral needs. We have long been known for our quality and long lasting flowers, our spectacular wedding & party work, beautiful and affordable funeral pieces. We design custom silk arrangements for your home or office and have a large selection of green plants, blooming plants and english gardens.

We hope we can provide the service you'd expect and more from a family owned florist. We are located in Downtown Downers Grove, you can send your order online, call us on the phone to customize any order or stop into our store at 5109 Main Street.
