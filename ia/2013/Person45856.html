Fran Piggott's Summary

As a successful marketing strategist and sales executive in my own right, I went on to found four companies of my own. Experience in corporate new business development and as a business owner stretches from 1967 until the present. For the past 10 years, I have been coaching or mentoring small business owners to grow their businesses, both offline and online, by using proven tools and techniques to increase their sales.

I now put back as much of my experience to those who want to learn from my success … and also my mistakes!

If you need help with redefining your marketing strategy or taking your business to a higher level and improve your bottom line fast, you're welcome to contact me via email.

Specialties: Business Strategies; Marketing Strategy and Planning; Marketing Plans; Strategic Planning; Plan Implementation; Marketing Management; Product Development; Marketing Campaigns; Marketing Awareness; Redefining Targeting Segmentation; Target Pipeline Management; Sales Analysis; Product Analysis; Customer Analysis, Business Mentoring; Marketing Coach.
