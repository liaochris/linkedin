Candy Adams~The Booth Mom®~Tradeshow Exhibit Project Mgt's Summary

NOW ACCEPTING NEW CLIENT FOR EXHIBIT/EVENT MANAGEMENT! CALL ME TO DISCUSS YOUR NEEDS!

Call me crazy, but I LOVE trade show exhibiting. I am a "trade show junkie".

Since 1991, I've been project managing B2B trade show exhibits, corporate events and exhibitions, either as an employee of exhibiting companies (Optigraphics, ENCAD and Intel). I now spend the majority of my time as a 3rd party (freelance/independent) exhibit project manager for companies who don't have experienced exhibits/events staff to manage their exhibit program, need interim help or need to outsource a segment of their event marketing program.

My passion is teaching exhibitors best practices based on the "real world" of trade shows. I pair one-on-one with industry "rookies" to co-manage their first season of shows and teach them the ropes to drastically cut their learning curve (and their company's future budgets) by shadowing them through their initial shows. I then continue to mentor them throughout their careers.

I also work with companies who have multiple exhibit managers to train them on industry best practices.

On the exhibition side of the business, I work with associations who wish to help their exhibitors improve their exhibit programs, by offering custom articles, Webinars/Podcasts, live training, or providing the criteria and exhibit judging for "Best-of-Show" contests on the show floor.

Specialties:

- Hands-on exhibit project management and consulting resource for companies without a full-time exhibit manager -- both domestic and international, inbound and outbound -- turnkey or a la carte.

- Working with start-up companies to build their exhibit programs from the ground up... and getting it right the first time without costly trial and error!

- One-on-one training of rookie exhibitors.

- Writing exhibiting "best practices" articles/blogs aimed at exhibitors.

- Humorous exhibit staff training through The Hill Group ("Boothmanship meets Improv")

    Trade Show Training is sales and marketing training for face-to-face conversations in an exhibit booth. For over 24 years, The Hill Group's Trade Show Selling Skills Workshop has done this. The result? More quality conversations. More qualified leads. More sales.Our training is customized, engaging, and entertaining.

    Even your people who don't want to be trained and think they're experts at working at trade shows already will actually enjoy our training. And they'll thank you for bringing us in.

    Here are the kinds or results you can expect from your exhibit staffs after we've trained and coached them:
    1. They will have more conversations with more highly qualified visitors.
    2. Their behavior will be polite and professional; they'll want to be in your booth, they'll be approachable (they won't stand in closed circles), and they'll initiate conversations with the visitors to your booth.
    3. They'll be prepared with good opening questions, brief overviews of your products and services (elevator answers), and they'll be ready to handle just about any type of question.
    4. They'll know how to politely disengage from a conversation, how to not make anyone wait to talk to them, and how to manage their time with visitors depending upon how busy your booth is.
    5. Qualified lead generation should double (our client's average increase is 250%)
