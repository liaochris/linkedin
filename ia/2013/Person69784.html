Key Data

Jonathan Roberts established his own consulting business in 2009. Previously he led the local government, transport, planning and infrastructure practices at Grayling and at Citigate Public Affairs.

Jonathan has 40 years’ experience as public policy consultant for local authorities, transport businesses, major projects and planning proposals.

He has advised local and regional government, transport public sector and plcs, promoters and developers. His clients have included trade associations, partnerships, and stakeholder groups seeking adoption of projects and business development wins.

His work includes:
• strategy and delivery of campaigns for clients facing public policy issues or changes in government funding and laws
• opinion forming for development proposals and schemes requiring community involvement
• stakeholder consultation for public and private sector clients and for partnerships
• political and specialist advice for public bodies, businesses and trade associations, for topics that involve political decisions or a public inquiry.
