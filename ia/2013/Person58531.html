Jannemiek Starkenburg's Summary

My current position might have given this away somewhat, but I specialize in public relations and communication advice on both a strategic and an executive level for online business and tech firms. Mostly, I like to think, with an intrapreneurial attitude. And, I'm a firm believer of online PR, SEO, social and content strategies. No really, I am.

Besides all this, I dabble in storytelling. Speaking of which, we should focus at least an equal amount of time (if not more) on storythinking as we do on the telling part.

Before we continue, a small confession: being a former journalist who happily crossed the line to the dark (PR) side, I do still write (freelance) about new media, e-business, theatre and/ or film. You're welcome to dip into a selection at starkenburg.contently.com.

Asking and answering questions are two things I truly love to do. So, if you have an appropriate, or out of the ordinary question that falls within my professional field, I'd be more than willing to discuss with you.

TL;DR: I be smitten with stories. Do surprise me with yours.
