John Pearce's Projects

  • iAbacus - interactive tool for evaluation and planning

    • December 2012 to Present
    Team Members: John Pearce, Daniel O'Brien

    We are really excited about launching The iAbacus™ It's like no other self-evaluation tool we know. It mimics a real abacus with sliding beads and then adds drag-n-drop analysis with interactive action planning. This kinaesthetic approach is so much more engaging than form-filling, box ticking and number-crunching. For more go to www.iabacus.co.uk

  • Aspiring Leaders of Education

    • June 2012 to Present
    Team Members: John Pearce

    An exciting, new year long programme, commissioned for Nottingham and district senior leaders by QUAD C Leading Edge Partnership and Nottingham City Schools’ Partnership. This programme is for colleagues who wish to consolidate their senior leadership position, or are considering progression to headship.

  • Leadership for Learning (L4L)

    • June 2011 to June 2012
    Team Members: John Pearce

    Leadership for Learning” (L4L) is an exciting new continuing professional development programme for Middle and Senior Leaders in Schools. The pilot programme was organised through the QuadC Leading Edge Partnership in Nottingham City schools.

    L4L is designed, in consultation with School Senior Leadership Teams, to meet the specific needs of participating schools, each individual leader and to have a positive impact on improving the enjoyment and achievement of young people. Involvement in the programme requires close collaboration between Senior Leaders in each school, each participant and the programme tutor.

    In essence it is an Action Research based programme with taught elements added, as and when required. The whole focus, as the title suggests, is what leaders do to improve the quality of learning in classrooms. The programme is now available for all schools. Contact John for evaluations of the pilot, sample materials and further information.
