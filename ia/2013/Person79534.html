Leah Howard's Summary

At JMMC I provide strategic-planning and branding experience to our leadership team. My expertise with local,national and international client varying from retail, B2B and corporate marketing help guide the development and growth of our account management group. I work closely with both clients and agency leaders to develop strategic, results-driven marketing plans that incorporate JMMC's “Spheres of Influence” philosophy.

Since becoming Vice President, I've focused on streamlining the processes that allow JMMC to serve as a full-service firm for clients seeking to access multiple research, media, creative and business channels. My client experience includes such wide-ranging industries as Howard Hughes Corporation (The Woodlands Development Co. & Bridgeland Development), Petrobras, Hospital Corporation of America-Gulf Coast, and various business ventures in Guatemala. Graduated from Baylor University with a BBA in Marketing.


Big Picture:
Most importantly, I understand the big picture, and can adapt to new trends quickly. Over the years we have seen a lot of fads in advertising and marketing. We make judgments about new media technologies based on proven marketing fundamentals: Which technologies are best for driving interest? Which technologies are best for responding to interest? Which technologies are most cost effective and how do they fit into your overall marketing objectives?

Specialties

Brand Management, Web Presence, and Social Media Development. Strategic Online Marketing Strategy, Print Materials, TV & Radio, and new media. We are a full service agency so we handle every aspect of a campaign including the media planning, account services, creative and client training if needed.

Tools:
Sales Force, Microsoft Office, BaseCamp, Network Solutions/Monster Commerce, ReachLocal Platform, Social Networks (i.e. Facebook FBML), Web Edit, Dreamweaver, Garage Band

My core expertise lies in research and sorting vast amounts of publicly available information, and objectively analyzing the information to help make smarter decisions about online marketing, emerging technologies and e-business.
