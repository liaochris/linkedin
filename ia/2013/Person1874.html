Jonathan Adlerstein's Summary

Jonathan has a strong background in information-technology, marketing and business development. He successfully founded and managed an independent design agency in NYC before moving into business development in the financial arena. Jonathan’s technical background began in the non-profit sector, where he performed systems administration duty in addition to his other responsibilities. He successfully leveraged this combination into his current position as Chief Information Officer at Plymouth Rock Energy where he uses his technical skills and business-savvy to guide the technology and data management in synergy with the strategic growth of the company.

Jonathan Adlerstein's Experience

CIO

Plymouth Rock Energy

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Oil & Energy industry

May 2010 – Present (3 years 9 months) Woodmere, NY

Jonathan is designing and deploying a custom ERP: cloud-based deregulated energy billing system comprising Customer Information System (CIS), Customer Relationship Manager (CRM), General Ledger (GL), Business Intelligence (BI) and Unified Communication (UC) fully integrated with multiple sales channels, customer and vendor portals and full mobility.

The new system includes a unified data set for forecasts, sales and general ledger, unified UI for internal users, customer care, supply, executives, outside sales reps and customers and is integrated with the company's advanced ACD VOIP system.

- Implemented disaster recover and backup
