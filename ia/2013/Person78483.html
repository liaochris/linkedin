Larry Prevost's Summary

Larry Prevost is a sales trainer, communication coach, IT administrator, Web administrator, and SEO consultant.

Larry has been involved with technology throughout his career. He has served in a variety of roles including designing enterprise storage systems, creating test procedures for high performance computing platforms, providing field and phone technical support for enterprise level computing environments, and working with major account sales teams selling enterprise class data storage systems.

Over the last few years, Larry Prevost has worked extensively with small businesses, understanding their issues and addressing their challenges through training and coaching. He has sold and taught sales, communication and leadership training solutions to the SMB community in Ohio, Indiana and Kentucky.

Prevost has also spent the last 15 years coaching business professionals to identify and achieve their performance goals, communicate effectively with their customers, and develop flexibility and versatility in addressing their challenges.

Currently, Prevost works with several Dale Carnegie franchises and small businesses, providing technical support, website administration, search marketing consulting, and social media consulting to support their sales and marketing efforts.

Activities include:

● Search Engine Optimization (SEO) for web properties.
● Email marketing.
● Leveraging SalesForce.com to supplement online marketing methods.
● Performing communication and leadership training for small business owners, technical consultants, and sales people.
● Performing social media, online reputation management, and technical training for sales people.
● Coaching individuals to identify and reach their performance goals.
● Mapping Dale Carnegie human relations principles into social media interactions.
