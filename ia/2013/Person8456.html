Ann Halloran's Summary

My early career was as a HR/Learning and Development Manager in British Telecom (UK), CR Bard (now Medtronic, Galway) and Arthur Andersen Consultants (Dublin).

In 1989, I established my own learning and development consultancy, now called In-Tuition Learning and Development Limited.

Since then I have been fortunate to work across all sectors and organisation sizes, assisting with learning strategy and management, learning material design and delivery of leadership and management programmes.

In 2009, I researched and created an online business through http://www.practical-management-skills.com. This website provides concise, practical tips for people managers and offers management workbooks, monthly ezines and regular management webinars. It was the result of years of observing what works (and doesn't work) in business in terms of managing people and yourself.

I was fascinated by online marketing and how to sell online and wanted to help others learn the basics.

In 2011, I commenced the delivery of FETAC accredited online business building programmes for individuals and small business owners.Our flagship 10 day Build an Online Business programme allows individuals to gain a FETAC Award in Digital Marketing.

I am also hired as a public speaker in the area of online business building, people management skills and worklife balance.

Specialities: learning and development strategy, learning material design, and delivery of programmes on leadership and management, train the trainer, digital marketing, website building, ecommerce and social media.
