Jamie Garantziotis' Summary

Current:   

I'm a PR and communication professional currently working as a Senior Account Executive in the digital team at Edelman Melbourne - a role that incorporates activation, strategy and community management for our clients.

My professional interests include corporate communication and reputation management, social media, community management, content marketing, media relations, blogging, corporate and public affairs, media, CSR, crisis communication and issues management.

Prior:  

I began my communication career as PR Manager- Regional for Southern Cross Austereo, where I managed both internal and external communication across the group's regional radio network brands, as well as their Southern Cross Ten, Southern Cross Television, ONE and channel ELEVEN television brands, and corporate social media.

More recently, in 2011-2012 I sat on the board of IABC Victoria as the Member Communications Chair - coordinating and working across the association's website, social media accounts and community management.

In July 2011 I began writing about PR, communication and media as a blogger at Shonali Burke's 'Waxing UnLyrical' blog, as well as my own blog - JamieGarantziotis.com.

From February - June 2011 I was the Australian correspondent for Engage TV- interviewing professionals about social media, public relations and communication.

While studying my Masters in Public Relations and Corporate Communication, I concurrently undertook the role of Program Coordinator in Communication at Bond University’s Centre for Executive Education.

I additionally completed internships and work experience roles with the BBS Communications Group, Professional Public Relations and Undertow Media.

I earlier graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Media and Communication from The University of Melbourne in 2007.

Specialties: Skills and interests:

Writing, editing, research, branding, blogging, social media and digital communication.
