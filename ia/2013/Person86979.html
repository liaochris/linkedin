Mark Prasuhn's Summary

Over 20+ years in C-suite roles in media organizations, I've applied visionary, entrepreneurial leadership to deliver triple digit % growth in audience ratings, revenue & profits.

I've developed, financed, commissioned & produced thousands of hours of TV programming, movies & digital media content; and acquired & sold thousands more.

Startups and companies in transition have benefited from my diverse experience and fresh perspectives. I've lived and worked in several cities in Canada and the U.S.

My unique breadth of experience includes Programming, Marketing, Production, Business Affairs/Rights Management, Regulatory, Sales/Business Development, Finance, Underwriting, Legal, HR, Engineering, Strategy & Government Relations. I've worked with, and served on, the Boards of many organizations.

I'm a creative, driven, innovative problem solver and brand builder with financial & legal acumen, operational expertise, analytical proficiency, superb interpersonal skills & a calm manner.

Through professional development, networking, voracious reading & consulting assignments, I keep current with industry developments. In addition to broadcast and content financing/production, my knowledge base includes digital media, online video, content curation/discovery, mobile, social media, and online education.

A champion of the business case for diversity, I've heightened employee skills & built robust management teams. With innovative approaches & a stellar reputation for integrity and reliability, I've worked with & mentored companies large & small - both for-profit and not-for-profit charities - to overcome challenges, seize opportunities, build new business lines & profitability.

I'm a highly effective negotiator & spokesperson, architect of excellent customer & stakeholder relationships, and a "glass half full" person.

I am guided in what I do by something Harry Truman said: "it is amazing what you can accomplish if you do not care who gets the credit."
