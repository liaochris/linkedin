Adam Veaner's Summary

I am Adam Veaner, an audio engineer from Boston, Massachusetts. I am an alumnus from Webster University with a B.A. in Audio Production.

After working as a freelance engineer for the last two years, I have made the step into small business ownership. Adam Veaner Audio is a one stop shop for all of your audio needs. Specializing in studio production, recording, and consulting. I most recently mixed a sixty minute special for WGBH, Boston.

I have also worked extensively in the live sound and event industry. I have worked along side almost every major company in Boston, engineering and setting up events of all kinds. This year, I was chosen as one of two engineers to construct and operate three large P.A. systems and an L.E.D. video wall at the Burning Man Festival in Black Rock City, Nevada.
