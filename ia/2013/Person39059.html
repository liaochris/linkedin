Sean Hogue's Summary

Working in the offshore industry with DP technology has always been my chosen career path. I find the work fulfilling as it is technically challenging yet still retains the traditional nautical aspects which drew me to a career at sea in the first place.

My focus is primarily on DP operations including pipe lay, heavy lift construction, semi-submersible operations, & dive support. Additionally I am experienced in shore side project management & continue working to expand this knowledge base.

I moderate a Facebook group for persons working in the DP field which offers a forum for conversation, networking, & job opportunities. The success there led me to create DynamicPositioningNews.com, a portal for all the news, articles & events that relate to the DP Industry. I closed the site down midway through 2013 due to increasing time constraints from my family (another child!) and career. I learned a lot from the running of that site and met lots of great people through it.

I have also worked with gCaptain, publishing a number of articles that relate to the DP field. Additionally my writing has been featured in the IDPOAs newsletter - 6 Degrees
