Angel L. Perez, MSHRM, PHR's Summary

Results-oriented bilingual HR professional with diverse experiences in the global, private, non-profit, and academic sectors in the following industries: Hospitality, Healthcare, Banking/Financial Services, Consumer Products, Airline, Manufacturing, Academia, and Health Insurance. Extensive experience in a full spectrum of human resource functions. Articulate, self-motivated, and problem solver with exceptional leadership skills. Highly effective communicator with strong ability to build relationships at all organizational levels. Proven track record in the design and administration of Human Resources and Payroll operations.

Focus is on People Development and Performance Management that improves and sustains performance. Value added to an organization includes but not limited to superior customer service, motivating others, and continually increasing productivity, which adds to the “bottom line”. Top three goals are to get more involved in community based organizations, advance my education, and attain additional professional certifications. My motto….”"Serve the employees, Lead by example, and Bring out the BEST in others”.
