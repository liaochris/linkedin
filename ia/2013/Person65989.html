John Abong's Experience

Owner

PrecisionPC Repair

May 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months) Newport, RI

I love fixing things and seeing the look on customers' faces when I make their computer problem disappear. I have over 20 years of hands-on experience building and maintaining computerized systems for manufacturing, controls industry, office and home/entertainment use. I started PrecisionPC Repair to offer that proven skill set to support your personal or home office computing needs. This experience can only be gained through thousands of hands-on hours. My main intent is to prevent or reduce your down-time (and to see your smiling face).

Quality Specialist

International Manufacturing Services, Inc

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry

June 1995 – Present (18 years 7 months) Portsmouth, RI

I devise, setup, maintain, and operate auditing and testing stations for quality control, manufacturing, and reliability testing of thick-film electronic components. I started the digital drafting department by implementing AutoCadLT (self-taught). I converted IMS's one-of-a-kind, automated parts sorting and marking workstations. The seven station conversion was from VB3 on Windows 3.1 to Java(self-taught) for cross-platform. It's a mix-mash of motors, electrometers, cameras and solenoids. I also design, fabricate and maintain custom hand-probe sets for other standard and specialized audit workstations. I enjoy being part of a team effort, developing solutions to acheive company goals.

Controls Specialist

Ryan Iron Works

1992 – 1995 (3 years) Rayham, Ma.

I was part of a 3 man team that designed, drafted, installed, tested, and debugged electro-mechanical, pneumatically driven, security door/gate control systems for prison facilities in MA, CT, RI. We also provided emergency services to these facilities with zero tolerance for lock /door failure. PLC Programming was done in ladder logic.
