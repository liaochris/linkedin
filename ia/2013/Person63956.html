Jim Klotzle, MA, LMFT's Summary

My work experience is varied; I've pursued a career in emergency services as an emergency medical technician, a city firefighter and a wildland firefighter. I've risen to the top of the toughest training academies in the Los Angeles area and proven myself as a valuable team member and leader in a wide variety of stressful environments.

Working in the construction industry, I learned basic plumbing and electrical work, landscaping and did some finish carpentry. Although I enjoyed both emergency services and construction, my true calling all along was to build helping relationships with other people.

I have volunteered for years with a humanitarian aid organization called World Relief, working with social and economic outcasts in Haiti, Mexico and the U.S. I have also spent some time in the mission field, working at an orphanage in Mexico.

Much of my interest and expertise in stress management, communication issues and relationships has sprung directly from these experiences. I have become passionate about creating and repairing systems that meet human needs.

Goals for the future include building a business based on my passion for creating positive systems and growing a private family therapy practice based on my passion for facilitating healing environments for broken systems.

Specialties: Children's Mental Health, Family Behavioral Health, Family Systems Therapy, Communication issues, Relationship issues, Stress issues, Anxiety, Depression, Psychotherapy.

Jim Klotzle, MA, LMFT's Experience

Outpatient Therapist

Private Psychotherapy Practice

February 2012 – Present (1 year 11 months) Champlin, MN

I am a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist providing mental health and relationship counseling services to individuals, couples and families. Addressing issues such as: Anxiety, Depression, Anger, ADHD, Adolescent issues, Couples/Marriage conflict, Divorce, Parenting and Sexual Concerns. Also providing life coaching and mediation (conflict resolution) services on fee-for-service basis. My passion in counseling is discovering "what works" for my clients.

In counseling and therapy I specialize in working with men from 10 years old and older on relationships, anxiety, depression, substance abuse/dependency, trauma/abuse, breakups, ADHD and anger management. I have also had remarkable success counseling women 16 and older who are ready for my refreshingly direct, caring and proactive approach to therapy.

My therapy office is in Champlin, Minnesota, convenient to Hennepin County, Anoka County and Ramsey County. Champlin Minnesota is a convenient drive from Anoka, Maple Grove, Ramsey, Brooklyn Park, Brooklyn Center, Osseo, Coon Rapids, Plymouth, Minneapolis and Rogers and other parts of Hennepin County and Anoka County. My clients regularly drive from these cities for counseling, life coaching and therapy sessions.
