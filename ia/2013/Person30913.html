Daryl Mulvihill's Summary

dmau – was founded in 2012 by Daryl Mulvihill, an architect and urban designer with a MA in documentary film. This diversity of experience brings me into contact with a lot of different organisations and people. My work focuses on projects – designs and documentaries – that improve the public realm, with an emphasis on work that has a positive social or environmental impact. Alongside this I delve deeper into the history of urbanism through documentary based research.

In 2012 the “Into the Wild” playground design was awarded first place in the Richard Krajicek Foundation sponsored sustainable playground design competition. “Into the Wild” was warmly received and is currently being developed as a playground concept in a number of locations.
