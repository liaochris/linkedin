George Smith's Summary

George G Smith Jr is the head of digital marketing for Rémy Cointreau USA. As a marketing leader, he has a proven track record running digital, social media, and interactive marketing programs for Fortune 50 companies and iconic brands such as Pepsi, Walmart, and Rémy Martin. As an industry leader, he has appeared as a speaker on numerous occasions on the conference circuit: from SxSW, Ad:Tech, Blog World Expo, and Brand Innovators.

A graduate of the University of Massachusetts - Amherst, George was recognized in 2012 as one of the Distinguished Young Alumni and continues his support of the University through student and alumni mentorships.

George currently works and lives in the New York City Metro area.
