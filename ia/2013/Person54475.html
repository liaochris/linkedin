Ian Rubin's Summary

Ian Rubin MA CPT CHC is a Food Relationship Coach and Master Personal Trainer who empowers his clients to overcome their food, fitness and weight hurdles to look great, feel fit and eat happy.

As a coach helping clients with their relationship with food, a dynamic workshop instructor, and a passionate speaker, Ian’s philosophy is rooted in his personal journey. Joining his first gym at 13 to “buff up” (emulating the physique of Schwarzenegger and Stallone), recovering from a back injury, and becoming gluten, egg, and sugar-free have all contributed to Ian’s unique message about health and wellness, delivered with warmth, caring and a sense of humor.

Ian helps his clients regain a happy and healthy relationship with food by teaching them how to re-write their “food story” in one-on-one sessions, in small groups and through speaking engagements. His method focuses on how and why we eat, rather than on what we eat. This approach releases his clients from the guilt they feel over eating foods they enjoy, the shame they feel about their weight, and the blame they lay on themselves for lack of “willpower.”

Ian emphasizes humor, fun, and close attention to form in his work as a Personal Trainer. Ian has been excited to work with all kinds of people; different ages, backgrounds, and those with injuries or disabilities. To see his clients feeling more confident in their physical performance and overall fitness is a great source of gratification for Ian.

Ian brings over 12 years of experience as a Certified Personal Trainer & Food Coach, a Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do and a Masters degree in Health Arts & Sciences to his coaching and training. His background gives Ian a unique perspective to help clients achieve the relationship with health and well-being that they desire. Ian also enjoys sci-fi movies and novels, cooking and eating unusual and exciting recipes, and traveling with his wife of 13 years.
