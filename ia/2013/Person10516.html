Brian Lockrey|Digital Forensics|Data Recovery's Summary

Data Recovery; Computer Forensics; Data Conversion;

At Assist Data Recovery, we assist people in getting data back from computers, hard drives, removeable media, USB drives, CD's, DVD's, diskettes, tapes, digital cameras, cell phones, and other electronic devices and media.

Assist Data Recovery is owned and operated by a College Professor that teaches Data Recovery, Computer Forensics and Computer Security courses at a major University.

If your computer has been struck by lightning, attacked by a Virus or invaded by Spyware, we can often get most, if not all, of your data back for you. We can even get your files back if you have erased them, wiped out your hard disk, or reloaded your Operating System over the top of your old data!

We can assist you and your business with Computer Forensics and Investigations. Our clients include families, businesses, law firms, law enforcement and government agencies.

Past Computer Forensics cases involve investigations for Child Custody, Divorce, Marital Disputes, Intellectual Property, Information Theft, Computer Hacking and Security violations.

Assist Data Recovery provides free analysis of your media and give you an honest quote before recovering your data. Unlike other data recovery and computer forensics firms, We are able to recover and only charge you for that data that you need. We do not charge you to recover data that you don't need and can restore from CD's and backup tapes.

Specialties

Data Recovery. Computer Forensics, Web Data Mining.
