MyPaydayPlan is revolutionary. Our exclusive Date Selection Software (DSS) enables the consumer to synchronize payday payments with their monthly payment dates. GrandSlam Alley will assist entrepreneurs at all levels of the entrepreneurial process. We will start with finding idea people, programmers, operations personnel, sales support personnel, driving products to market staffed by full time and part time multipreneurs mentoring the businesses in the accelerator. From idea to funding, the main goal will be to ferret out great ideas, bring the proper people together to bring the idea to market, grow and ultimately sell the business. GrandSlam Alley will ultimately push a company out on their own once they are operational and profitable (maybe earlier) but will continue to mentor the business through to independence and with a focus on the sale of the company.
