Jay Harrill's Summary

Jay Harrill of JH3 Technology Solutions offers over 14 years of experience in providing computer, server, and network services offering support and solutions for small business and home computer users.

JH3 Technology Solutions is a Microsoft, Dell, GFI Software, and Rackspace partner.

Mission Statement

To provide dependable, quality computer services to the Lake Norman area and to treat each relationship with honesty and integrity. You can trust JH3TS to only do the work promised and to let you know if there are other issues that can become problems in the future.

I have worked at the corporate level and supported many small businesses and home users. Through all of these experiences I have developed excellent trouble-shooting skills and knowledge of many different kinds of issues. I am sure I can help you with your computer problems.

Specialties

Virus/Spyware removal, small office and home networks, server administration, data backup, general computer troubleshooting.
