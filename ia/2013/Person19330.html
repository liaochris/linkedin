Caleb Adam Haye's Summary

Caleb Adam Haye is a software architect and entrepreneur who has been developing software professionally since 1997.

In 2005, Caleb founded Fire, Inc -- a software consulting company.

In 2012, Fire, Inc. released the Presskit Platform -- a mobile application platform for artists and content creators.

In his spare time Caleb is an inventor, poet, traveler, farmer, and a tangential blogger (caleb.org). Most importantly, Caleb is the proud father of his beloved daughter.
