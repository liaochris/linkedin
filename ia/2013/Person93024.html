Michael Manley's Summary

I am currently the Director of Technology at Elevate Studios, a web and interactive design shop in Chicago.

I have 15 years of experience developing and deploying online solutions and managing software development and operations teams in the publishing and online marketing industries. I have written software for embedded microcontrollers, desktop operating systems and web-based applications, and I'm looking forward to starting development on mobile platforms.

I prefer to work under the precepts outlined in The Pragmatic Programmer. I believe that the needs of the customer should shape software functionality, not vice versa.

Specialties

Web application architecture & development at all scales, from small business sites to large multi-server deployments; content management strategies; technical due diligence and vendor evaluation; software engineering management; Agile software development practices; Open Source technology; PHP application development and operations.
