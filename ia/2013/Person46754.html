Gail Zelitzky's Summary

Creative, collaborative business advisor with hands-on experience owning and running her own multi-million dollar companies. Exclusively coaches business owners to ThinkSales and increase their revenue and profits by creating new revenue strategies, honing their leadership skills and streamlining their operations. I speak and present skill-based workshops and lunch and learns for small business on subjects such as Double Your Sales with Half the Effort, Networking Nirvana, Boost Your Sales by Differentiating Your Style and TamingTime. I am a Trainer and I facilitate mastermind round tables for owners. Senior instructor for E200 - an initiative of the SBA.

I am passionate about helping small business owners attain success as they reach clarity, define focus and implement practical solutions to achieve the results they expect for themselves and their business.

Personal Goal: completing my Masters of Arts in Applied Professional Studies at De Paul University in order to help small business owners create foundations that allow for innovation leading to sustained growth.

Specialties

SOAR Formula for Success; ThinkSales! Approach to Growth; Skill-based Workshops on branding and identity, taming time, effective networking, boosting sales and more; Mastermind RoundTables; Inspirational Keynotes for Women
