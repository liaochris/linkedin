John Gray's Summary

My career has combined marketing/business development/CRM work within law firms and practising as a lawyer. This skillset and experience is now put to use as Director of John Gray Marketing, a specialist professional services marketing consultancy with particular expertise in law firm marketing.

I'm committed to breaking the "advice without implementation" consultant mould. A genuine, successful law firm marketing program requires strategy, planning and implementation. With my team of consultants and hand picked suppliers my clients receive as much help or support as they need every step of the way.

Specialties

Marketing, business development and content management for small to mid-size professional service firms, particularly law firms, but relying on large firm experience and expertise.
