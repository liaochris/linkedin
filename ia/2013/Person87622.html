Martin Evans' Projects

    • September 1999 to Present

I've made a huge number of enhancements to DBD::ODBC over the years including updating to ODBC 3 and adding Unix Unicode support but these are just the tip of the iceberg as DBD::ODBC is a very popular module.

    • November 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Martin Evans, John Scoles, Tim Bunce, Yanick Champoux

Recently I've made TAF support work but my contributions can be found at http://www.ohloh.net/p/5976.
