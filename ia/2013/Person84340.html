Maisie Knowles' Summary

Maisie Knowles is the co-founder, Vice President and Secretary of Kinoli, Inc.

In 2005, her and her husband launched the highly successful website FreeShipping.org which led to other money saving websites such as GiftCardGranny.com, CouponSherpa.com and the billion dollar shopping holiday, Free Shipping Day.

Maisie currently spends most of her time at home with her two young girls and continues to be involved in business operations and development for Kinoli Inc. in Fort Collins, Colo.

Maisie earned a B.A. in Communications from the University of Colorado in 2003.

Learn more about Maisie at www.maisieknowles.com

Specialties: Entrepreneurship, Internet and email marketing, and small-business operations. Also project management for web and print design, web development and publishing.

Maisie Knowles' Projects

  • FreeShipping.org

    Team Members: Maisie Knowles, Luke Knowles

    Founded in 2007, this popular website provides free shipping offers to over 4,000 merchants. FreeShipping.org has been featured on CNN, ShopSmart, Consumer Reports and many others media outlets.

  • Free Shipping Day

    Team Members: Maisie Knowles, Luke Knowles

    Free Shipping Day was coined by entrepreneurs Luke and Maisie Knowles just weeks before the event in 2008. This shopping holiday is one day in December when consumers can purchase gifts with free shipping and guaranteed delivery by Christmas Eve. Since 2011 Free Shipping Day has produced more than $1 billion in sales.

  • Coupon Sherpa

    Team Members: Maisie Knowles, Luke Knowles

    Coupon Sherpa's free mobile app puts digital coupons into the hands of shoppers. Coupon Sherpa was named an iTunes Staff Favorite in 2009 and has been seen in The New York Times, CBS, ABC and more.

  • Gift Card Granny

    Team Members: Maisie Knowles, Luke Knowles

    Who knew saving money on everyday purchases could be that easy? Gift Card Granny puts discounted gift cards in the hands of shoppers who can save up to 50% on their purchases.

  • Ink Jet Willy

    Team Members: Maisie Knowles, Luke Knowles

    Did you know that a colored HP ink cartridge costs over 7 times more per milliliter than a 1985 Dom Perignon? Ink Jet Willy fighting the injustice of high ink prices and showing consumers where to get them for less.

  • Mr. Free Stuff

    Team Members: Maisie Knowles, Luke Knowles

    Who doesn't like free? Mr. Free Stuff is a source for free services and complimentary goods. He's also got the inside scoop on things like restaurant freebies and free things to do.
