Duaine Owings' Summary

Duaine R. Owings, is an insurance & financial advisor who holds multi-state insurance licenses to offer insurance products in the U.S. and internationally.

He specializes in advising his clients on such subjects as; how to preserve their assets and reduce their taxes, how to diversity their portfolios for growth and stability, and how to create more income for retirement.

Duaine is committed to the financial security of this clients and he is proud to provide exemplary customer service, while displaying the highest standards of ethics and professional knowledge.

He is also an expert in international health, life, disability and emergency evacuation coverage’s.

Duaine is also a member of the National Association of Professional Agents and the President of Plan To Win Insurance Agency and Missionary-Services.

Specialties: General Agency specializing in Health, Life, Disability placement for our clients. We operate on a Worldwide basis covering the international as well as our U.S. clients.
