Chris Ferrara's Summary

Chris Ferrara is an interactive web designer, UI/UX developer, front end developer, graphic designer, art director, and content manager who has designed, developed and produced successful marketing and communication packages for the entertainment and corporate community for over 20 years.

Prominence Design has been created to accomplish the marketing, design, and promotional challenges that face small to medium size businesses, start - ups, and independent artists, and to elevate their visual presence in an increasingly cluttered communications landscape.

Specialties: interactive design and development, ecommerce, content management, social networking, logo design, music packaging, print media, email management and marketing.
