G.W. Linhart's Summary

ICS is a full service private investigation agency. ICS employs cutting edge techniques and technology to solve cases in the private, industrial, and commercial worlds. ICS was founded in 1967. ICS has a global mission to provide investigations throughout the world. ICS currently serves domestic and international clients.

ICS of Colorado is the premier private investigation agency in Colorado, conducting professional and discreet investigations across the state. No matter what your case may entail, whether it be catching a cheating spouse, finding a birthparent or long lost friend, collecting evidence for child custody, support, or visitation hearings, or conducting a background check, ICS of Colorado has the expertise to handle your investigation. ICS strictly adheres to all federal, state, and local laws, so rest assured that any ICS private investigator assigned to your case will be fully qualified and meets or exceeds all industry standards.

Colorado is one of the few states in the country that does not require private investigators to be licensed through any state governmental agencies. Colorado is in fact now a Voluntary License State, which Grant and ICS are a part of. ICS of Colorado is owned and operated by Grant Linhart, and only employs qualified, legitimate private detectives that adhere to the highest industry standards. Mr. Linhart is licensed as a private investigator in the State of Colorado & California and has years of investigative experience. Many private investigators in Colorado come from military or police backgrounds.

Individuals and Companies Hire ICS for the following reasons:

-45 year history in the private investigations field, since 1967

-Nationwide and International coverage to serve you

-An experience and service level that is unmatched due to the long standing traditions and brand of ICS
