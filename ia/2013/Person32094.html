David Khazai's Summary

I have been directly involved for most of my life in the Oriental rug industry, from design to production, distribution and wholesale and retail sales. My family has been in that business for five generations.

My other key interest, which has become a professional pursuit as well, is the development of software and the application of web-based technology to help the small to mid-size retailer compete with the big companies.

I am an avid reader, especially books having to do with changing consumer and marketplace dynamics and how they affect the sales environment. Based on my experience and reading, I have written my own book on the subject, and from time to time I provide consultation and assistance to small and mid-sized retailers on subjects ranging from starting a new business to saving a failing one.

Specialties

Retail management consultation, sales training, retail software development, and consumer behavior/trends.
