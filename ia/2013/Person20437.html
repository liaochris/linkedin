Carol J Carter's Summary

My goal is to help America be competitive as a global force in the upcoming years through improving the education, opportunity and preparedness of our young people. LifeBound books, trainings, and coaching skills are designed to help teachers and students make successful transitions and understand the demands and complexities of our world so that they can create every advantage that life has to offer.

I am an author and an international speaker on educational trends, techniques and solutions. Prior to beginning my company, LifeBound, in 2001, I was an executive for Pearson Education; first as a Director of Marketing and later as VP and Publisher for Student Success. I have written or co-authored over twenty books on college, career and life success.

Specialties: I bring a business perspective to the way we teach students and train teachers. The "R" in Bill Gates' relevance is the foundation of my learning philosophy. If students don't understand the value and benefit of what they are learning, they are often unchallenged and disengaged. Our learning is in the context of their success in school, college, career and life. There is an applciation for all kinds of learning and our job as teachers and trainers is to bring that to life.

  • KEYS TO SUCCESS

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Research indicates that intelligence isn’t fixed: It can grow. Keys to Success is designed to build your intelligence and give you skills that will transfer to other courses, to work, and to life. Every chapter focuses on ways to develop analytical, creative, and practical thinking skills, the basic tools of successful intelligence. The skills you develop will help you realize your goals in college, career, and life.

  • THE CAREER TOOL KIT

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Coverage of important topics includes developing and maintaining a positive self image, managing time, budgeting and organizing finances, avoiding or reducing credit debt, and tracking down the resources available at school, in the community, and online. A focus on self-awareness teaches readers how to get to know themselves better so that they understand what kinds of jobs might best suit their unique talents and qualities, how to network and locate job opportunities, and how to present themselves in the best possible light on a resume as well as in an interview, and how to handle both failure and success. Additional topics discuss workplace communication with managers, co-workers, clients, and customers; qualities and skills that employers look for in their employees; rights on the job; critical thinking at work; improving and maintaining mental health and social intelligence; motivation, and more.

  • KEYS TO EFFECTIVE LEARNING

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    This book focuses on developing effective learning techniques to help readers excel in school, in their careers, and throughout their lives as lifelong learners. The authors outline getting ready to learn through self-awareness, goal setting and time management, as well as, critical and creative thinking, targeting success in school through reading and studying, listening and memory, taking notes and test taking, quantitative learning, researching and writing, gathering and communicating ideas, as well as, creating life success.

  • KEYS TO ONLINE LEARNING

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Keys to Online Learning addresses though the convenience of online education has enabled greater numbers of students’ access to degree programs, being successful in this environment requires specific skills and strategies. Every element can be fundamentally different for online students (i.e. the presentation of material, classmate and instructor interaction, assessments and activities). Students will learn how to effectively experience the online environment learning process as they decide what, how, and when course materials are accessed. Reading skills are required for print and onscreen content. Writing is the main form of communication, informally on discussion boards and formal writing skills in academic papers. Assignments require virtual collaboration, and developing peer relationships requires a different skill set than those used in face-to-face settings. Online education can make a student more anonymous, so motivation and time management is paramount. This resource will support online students to develop the aforementioned skills: reading, writing, assignments, and more, infusing learning styles and critical thinking throughout.

  • KEYS TO COLLEGE STUDYING

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    This proven student success author team has updated its study skills text to extend the depth of core coverage while increasing the relevance and usefulness of the material. In addition to a comprehensive treatment of study skills, Keys to College Studying integrates a definitive theme of active thinking throughout the text and exercises. This theme builds students' ability to develop skills, strengthen the will to learn, and monitor and manage their progress. End-of-chapter exercises, as well as new in-chapter exercises, link to and reinforce the theme.

  • KEYS TO CAREER SUCCESS

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    With an emphasis on developing real-world success skills, Keys to Career Success shows readers how to understand themselves, their interests and their future goals. This internet-based sourcebook helps readers build success skills that will help them in whatever career they decide to pursue. This book reflects the same philosophy and approach as the best selling student success book: Keys to Success. The book covers all aspects of success in the 21st century including learning and flexibility, recognizing success, strategizing for achievement, assessing your SWOT (strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats), transforming hopes into realities and researching and ranking companies. For anyone trying to build a successful career.

  • KEYS TO ENGINEERING SUCCESS

    Authors: Carol J Carter

Keys to Engineering Success offers an upfront description of what engineering is, skills needed to succeed, and information about what career opportunities are available upon graduation. Profiles of engineering professionals and personal stories, offer inspiration and encouragement in pursuing academic and professional excellence.

  • KEYS TO SCIENCE SUCCESS

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Proficiency in the sciences and an understanding of technological advances has become a must for career success. This resource explores all aspects of careers in science, from career planning and preparation to networking. Discusses how to study in the sciences—how science books differ from other books and how to approach them, understanding inquiry-based learning, and conducting research. Emphasizes communication skills through various group projects and written work. For anyone interested in pursuing or changing to a career in the life sciences or physical sciences, nursing, engineering, computer science, or mathematics.

  • KEYS TO NURSING SUCCESS

    • Pearson
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Nursing is the largest health occupation in the world and with the current shortage, qualified nurses are more in demand than ever. Keys to Nursing Success is designed to promote a realistic understanding of nursing, explore the exceptional opportunities nursing careers offer, and help students prepare for the rigors of nursing school. Emphasis is placed on how to study the sciences, how to develop critical thinking skills, and the importance of liberal arts courses in building a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

  • SUCCESS IN MIDDLE SCHOOL: A TRANSITION ROADMAP

    • LifeBound
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Students in America perform on par with their counterparts in other developed nations until they get to sixth grade. Once our students enter middle school, their scores start to slip, and by the time students are in high school, American students on average fall behind their global counterparts in math, science, and other academic measurements. Success in Middle School helps transitioning students adjust to a new school culture, handle increasing academic demands, manage relationships, learn to take measured risks, and much more.

By following the strategies in Gifts & Talents for Teenagers, students identify their strengths, learn to manage their weaknesses, and see how both of these relate to their learning styles.

  • PEOPLE SMARTS FOR TEENAGERS

This title helps middle school students develop specific skills for learning about themselves and their peers. By helping students understand their emotions, People Smarts for Teenagers strengthens teens' ability to make friends, cope with adversity, and manage stress – which makes for more focused, happy, and successful students.

  • MAKING THE MOST OF HIGH SCHOOL: SUCCESS SECRETS FOR FRESHMEN

    Authors: Carol J Carter

This title includes anecdotes from real high school students about how to navigate the choppy waters of adolescence. Using humor and real-world instruction, these stories help readers discover how to get the most out of high school, whether they are at-risk, average, or high-achieving students. Making the Most of High School is a must read for entering high school freshmen and a fantastic resource for freshman seminar or advisory classes.

Sophomore Guide to College and Career inspires high school students to dream, conduct a self-analysis, learn critical-thinking skills, manage resources, match abilities and interests to career fields, understand the global landscape, rebound from setbacks, and prepare for the real world of work. This title helps students plan for their college education, learn about financial aid, choose the right school, and plan for different career choices.

  • JUNIOR GUIDE TO SENIOR YEAR SUCCESS: BECOMING A GLOBAL CITIZEN

    Authors: Carol J Carter

This book helps students make the most of their senior year of high school and introduces them to global perspectives with essays from inspiring leaders from around the world. A special chapter on financial aid is included to help high school students learn to finance their college education.

  • MAJORING IN THE REST OF YOUR LIFE

    • LifeBound
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    College students who truly want to succeed after graduation must start preparing as soon as they begin their post-high school education. Majoring in the Rest of Your Life helps high school seniors gain skills vital to college success, including defining interests and goals, balancing academic and personal lives, aiming for useful extracurricular activities and part-time jobs, finding internships, and, eventually, sending out resumes and going to interviews.

  • STUDY SKILLS FOR HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Studying is a skill that requires practice, fine-tuning, and experimentation. Study Skills for High School Students equips students with the keys for effective study skills: goal setting, learning style assessments, active note taking, preparing for tests, and critical thinking skills. Make studying smart a habit now and reap the benefits through high school and beyond.

Each chapter presents students the opportunity to evaluate and develop their own leadership skills. This book is a perfect companion to Critical and Creative Thinking, and serves as a compliment to core curriculum presented in highschool classrooms.

  • CRITICAL AND CREATIVE THINKING FOR TEENAGERS

    • LifeBound
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Why ask why? What’s a powerful question? When should I take a risk? With today's focus on test scores and graduation rates, many students leave school without learning how to think critically or creatively. To solve anything from today's biggest problems to the everyday query, students must develop an inquisitive mind, strong investigation skills, the ability to ask powerful questions, and the skills to imagine, dream, and create. Critical and Creative teaches teens these must-have skills in one unique and interactive book.

  • DOLLARS & SENSE: HOW TO BE SMART ABOUT MONEY

    • LifeBound
    Authors: Carol J Carter

    Get students ready to make educated financial decisions today with Dollars & Sense: How to Be Smart About Money. This book is mapped directly to the National Standards for Financial Literacy, established by the Jump$tart Coalition to improve the financial literacy of youth from kindergarten through college. Teens will categorize their spending in terms of wants and needs, learn the risks and rewards of taking out loans, start saving for life beyond high school, and gain an understanding of how smart money choices can effect their lives today and in the future. Dollars & Sense approaches the important topic of financial literacy in a colorful, creative, and fun way with many real stories, online activities, and opportunities to engage with peers and family over money dos and don'ts.
