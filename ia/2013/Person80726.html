  • Magic Cube

    • October 2010 to December 2010
    Team Members: Libo Su, Stephanie Ahn

    Magic Cube introduces an Augmented Reality based tangible interaction tool the cube that supports primary interaction behaviors to control applications. The cube is the only control device that operates every function in Magic Cube and the location and the movement of the cube is matched to the appropriate functions. Magic Cube incorporates free movements of the cube, including turning, tilting, rotating, shaking, and straight and curved movement, which mimics the natural hand gesture. Magic Cube supports not only 2D controls but 3D controls such as sphere movements or 3D special view. The movement of the cube is captured through the webcam, so users can track their movements. The webcam screen also generates images according to the actions via Augmented Reality.

  • C.A.R.E

    • March 2011 to May 2011
    Team Members: Libo Su, Lan Wang, Esther Yu Xian Goh, Eric Chng

    C.A.R.E stands for Chinese Augmented Reality Education. It is a new assistant learning method to help children improve their Chinese language proficiency. By holding a character card to the webcam, a child can obtain the meaning, stroke and pronunciation of the character via Augmented Reality. This system also employs 3 games to help children learn for fun:
    1. Monster Challenge is a quiz game which helps children to remembering the shape of Chinese characters by fighting with a monster.
    2. Four Tones helps children practice their skills of identifying the four tones of Chinese language.
    3. Radical Puzzle is designed to enchance children's understanding of the structure of Chinese characters.

  • The Mind Garden

    • October 2010 to January 2011
    Team Members: Libo Su

    The Mind Garden is a novel game based on Brain Computer Inteface. Supported by Neurosky Mindset, an EEG device which can analyze people's brain waves and interpret them into quantitative data. This is a farmer type game, a player can grow his/her plant by concentrating attention and change the environment of the garden by keeping relax.

    Neurosky Mindset connects to computer via Bluetooth. Four dry sensors are implemented to gather the brain wave data. The development environment is Flex 4 and Flash Professional. In this project, I am trying to add an additional dimension in game control and discover new interaction experience.

  • Hungry Bird

    • January 2011 to January 2011
    Team Members: Libo Su

    Hungry Bird is a physics game. The worlds in this game can be full simulations of the real physical world or customized world. The main goal of this game is simple: to lead the bird to eat his food. However, the process is not that easy. User can draw free form lines as the path to lead the bird to the target and take the gravity and inertia of the lines into consideration.
    This game is still under development. In the current four stages, gravity, collision, friction and other key physical features are employed in the world. Also, water, wind, additional gravity field and static and moving obstacles are created to increase the difficulty of the game.
    This game is developed using Flash Professional and Box2d physical engine.

  • MyCourier

    • August 2010 to October 2010
    Team Members: Libo Su

    MyCourier is my version of the Microsoft Courier tablet's. Courier was a prototype of a two-screen digital journal, allowing the user to take notes using digital ink, embed content from the web and other sources into journal pages, and process and interpret ink strokes in some interesting ways. In this application, the left panel is an area for computer-generated information which holds some widgets like mini web browser, address book, image browser, calendar and calculator. The right pannel contains a notepad to for user-generated information like free-form drawing and writing, word typing, and primitive shape generation. Also, user can create new notes, save notes, delete notes and view each note page. This application is developed using Java Swing.
