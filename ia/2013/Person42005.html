Eric Champion's Summary

Hello, my name is Eric Champion.

I'm an Illustrator, Graphic Designer and 3D Animator. I have a Bachelor's in Graphic Art and Design from Nossi College of Art. Art is my passion and has been since I was a child. Now as a young professional I'm looking for an opportunity to show off my talents. Please feel free to contact me with any questions and/or opportunities. Please visit my website www.ecdesign1.com and I hope you enjoy my work as much as I enjoyed creating it.

Thank you for your interest.

Eric Champion's Experience

Freelance Graphic Designer

Eric Champion Design

2010 – Present (3 years) Lebanon, TN

EC Design is my personal Freelance business offering Graphic Design, Illustration, & other artistic and design services (Web Design, Packaging, ect.). EC Design has allowed me to better understand the business and what costumers want. It has also allowed my creativity to grow and learn as a professional while working with such clients as:

-Therapeutic Interventions, Incorporated (TII)
Logo and Web Designer, My job was to help rebrand TII. Therapeutic Interventions, Incorporated (TII) is an innovative program focused on helping today’s children, youth and young adults achieve and maintain healthy relationships and productive lives in their communities.

-New Horizons
Program Designer, My job was to create a program for this special Music and Auction event called Guitars at Grassmere at The Nashville zoo. The event included talents such as Melinda Doolittle from American Idol, Kelly Sutton from Fox 17 News, and Rich Karg. Proceeds benefit the Wellness Program at New Horizons Corporation, a non-profit organization that empowers adults with developmental disabilities to live and work in our community.

-American Advertising Federation (AAF)
Poster Designer, My job was to create a promotional poster for the 2011 Student Addy Awards. The AAF Student ADDY Awards Competition is a unique national awards program designed specially for college students.
