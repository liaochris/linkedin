Dana Harris' Summary

I have an extensive background in management, technology and real estate that covers almost 30 years. Early in my career I worked for several Fortune 500 technology companies here in California including Nortel, SGS-Thomson, NexGen, AMD and AMCC. I also conceived, developed and ran my own business located in Jackson Hole, Wyoming, where I lived for over 8 years. I have been a licensed Realtor in California, Idaho and Wyoming since 2003 and have been involved in many different types of transactions from single-family homes, condos, large land parcels, light commercial and 1031 exchanges. My extensive business background gives me an excellent perspective of what it takes to get results while providing unparalleled service.

Being actively involved in the community has always been important to me. I have served on the boards of the Fairbanks Highlands Homeowners Association, Creekside Education Foundation and the Jackson Hole Music Experience. I’ve been a youth soccer coach, community volunteer and a volunteer at my children’s schools. I also volunteer for and/or financially support many local organizations including The Stephen Birch Aquarium, The Helen Woodward Animal Center, Del Mar Lifeguards, The Del Mar Foundation, the San Diego Chapter of The Surfrider Foundation, and 1% For The Planet. In my free time I enjoy photography, surfing, snowboarding, traveling, golfing and cycling.

I am a Realtor with Real Living Lifestyles, in the Del Mar Village office, and I specialize in the North County Coastal areas of San Diego County which includes Del Mar, Carmel Valley, Fairbanks Ranch, Rancho Santa Fe, Solana Beach, Cardiff, Encinitas, Leucadia.
