Bill Milburn's Summary

With over 20 years in the rail industry now, starting in the UK as a signals technician and currently a Specialist Reliability Engineer (Signals) in Australia. I have a varied career with operational, maintenance and project management experience in moving block ATP/ATO light rail, London Underground fixed block ATP/ATO and High Speed ATP.

After moving into consultancy in Brisbane in 2006 I have been involved in a wider variety of roles including type approval, condition monitoring strategies, annual certification, electrification, control centres and signal maintenance safety.

In 2011 I moved to Perth to join Rio Tinto as their Specialist Reliability Engineer (Signals) within their Asset Management group. The role has brought me back to my roots and allows me to work within the Rail Reliability team working on issues to improve the overall reliability of rail assets not just limited to signalling. The role includes a FIFO arrangement to allow me access to the railway in Karratha and Tom Price.

Specialties: Maintenance and Operations of metro, high speed and heavy haul railways
