Evan Oliff's Summary

I specialize in RE-IMAGING Commercial Real Estate. I conceptualize, develop, and lease projects in both urban and rural markets. I'm PREFERRED since 1992.

I'm a creative thinker and problem solver and have the ability to "size the situation up quickly". Due to my 27 years of experience in the real estate business as owner, developer and broker, I have an innate ability to synthesize an opportunity in very short order. My commercial projects lead to economic growth and job creation in the communities I'm passionate about.

On each development project, I bring a talented team of architects, civil engineers, and contractors that have many years of successful commercial developments behind them. My team has worked on developing over 45 free-standing Walgreens along. In addition, we've worked on other commercial developments, including projects for Wal-Mart, Chase, Bank of America, Kroger Foods and other tenants.

Preferred Development has also developed over 1mm square feet of retail, office, and industrial space for tenants including Starbucks, Five Guys Burgers, Jiffy Lube, AT&T, T-Mobile, GSA and the State of Indiana.

Recent projects include developing Kroger anchored shopping centers in Decatur, IN and Canton, IL. My work with Kroger includes installation of a fuel center and renovation of two existing shopping centers.

Our specialities include grocery anchored shopping centers, brokered CBD office buildings, multi-tenant industrial buildings and single tenant net lease properties throughout the country.

Contact me today at 312-953-1802 for your next commercial development opportunity.
