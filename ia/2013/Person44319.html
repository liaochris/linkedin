Corliss Jackson's Summary

FEDERAL / FEDERAL CONTRACTOR JOB SEARCH AND COACHING: Federal Job Results specializes in comprehensive and affordable Federal / Federal contractor job search services, resume writing, application support and interview preparation. As a former senior level manager at the GS-15 level and another decade as a HR senior consultant to Federal agencies, our passion is to connect people with Federal / Federal contracting job opportunities. Our mission is to structure your application materials -- to put you in the best possible light -- to gain the full attention of the HR staff so you can land the best possible Federal / Federal contractor position and to do it as fast as possible. We’ve done it for thousands of our clients and we can do it for you!

Federal Job Results is extremely proud of our staff certifications and backgrounds. We are Career Transition Specialists, Certified Federal Resume Writers, Certified Federal Career Counselors, Certified Federal Job Search Specialists, and Certified Global Career Development Facilitators (GCDF), providing exceptional Federal / Federal contractor job coaching services to our clients.

Specialties: Federal Resume Writer, Federal Contractor Resume and Cover Letter Writer, Certified Federal Career Counselor, Certified Federal Job Search Specialist, Certified Federal Resume Specialist, Certified Federal Job Search Trainer, KSA Writing, Federal Job Analyst, Federal / Federal Contractor Interview Coach, SES Resumes and ECQs, Keynote Speaker on Federal Job Application Process.
