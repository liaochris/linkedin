Brian Phelan's Summary

Brian has been with Asco since 1968 and holds a BSEE from Newark College of Engineering (NJIT) and a MBWA from his years within the Electrical Industry.

In his tenure with Asco, he has served as Industrial Control Product Manager and Director of Customer Engineering dealing with the development and marketing of Emergency Power Products and Systems. He is currently Director of Marketing Services, responsible for Strategic Planning, Marketing,Advertising and promotion of the company and the products for the electrical industry

An accomplished speaker, Brian has presented numerous tutorials on Emergency Power Distribution and Communication Systems throughout the electrical industry. He has also developed and presented comprehensive product and marketing plans for Emerson Corporate Planning Conferences

He is the recipient of the prestigious "Emerson Technology Award" and several exhibitors' awards from national trade shows serving the electrical industry.

He has pioneered the use of new technology in the development of multimedia and web-based applications for his company's marketing communication initiatives. He is the recipient of the 2000 Axiem Award for absolute excellence in electronic media

Brian Phelan's Projects

  • ASCO "Compare" Ad Campaign

    • June 2009 to January 2013
    Team Members: Brian Phelan, JW Huckfeldt, Ramon Sosa, Chrissie Stewart, james huckfeldt

    "ASCO invites you to compare..." was a print ad campaign created by First Team Marketing Communications and ASCO Power Technologies to better communicate to ASCO's target market by differentiating its Technology, Service and Support from other power switching and controls manufacturers. The project began in 2009 and lasted 30 days from concept to completion. ASCO executives decided on the first eight print ads and The "Compare" theme was so successful that ASCO continues to employ that theme today and has been featured in more than two dozen print ads.
