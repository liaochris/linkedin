Jerry Corley's Summary

Taking from the adage of "It's impossible to dislike someone who makes you laugh" I love traveling all over to provide customized comedy for companies that see the benefits of humor in the workplace. Whether it's a USO tour, a convention, or company picnic, laughter can motivate, unite and invigorate any team in any industry.

Jerry teaches classes on how to write comedy as well as how to be a stand up comedian in weekend seminars and 8-week classes from his comedy studio in Burbank, CA.

Specialties

Currently known by associates and colleagues as "The Joke Doctor" for the ability to fix or reconstruct routines or individual jokes to illicit a better audience response.
