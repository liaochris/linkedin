Bruno Bergher's Summary

I'm a product designer and design leader with 10 years experience, a generalist profile and a proportionally broad skill set. My core skills revolve around product thinking, interaction design and visual design, and I have valuable skills in design management, user research, product strategy, funnel optimization and programming (I can go full stack, from the server to the front-end, and dabble with Android apps).

I can wear so many hats because I have a great thirst for learning. I'm more interested in solving meaningful problems in an elegant way than any specific tool or medium. I'm also passionate about creating and nurturing design cultures in organizations, be it by leading a design team, company-wide education, or the best means of all: doing great work that matters.

Specialties: Mobile, Interaction Design, Visual Design, UX Research, Design Leadership, Prototyping.

Head of Product Design

Lookout

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer & Network Security industry

January 2012 – Present (2 years) San Francisco Bay Area

I lead a team of 4 product designers and a UX researcher at Lookout. We're responsible for the UX of all of the company's products – Android, iOS, Web and Business —, from user research to defining features to design and prototyping. I also fortunately get to design and write some code pretty regularly, on top of helping shape product strategy through design methods in a user-centered approach (which is not just a soundbite, it's actually quite cool).

Major accomplishments: built an incredible design team from the ground up, introduced UX research to the company and drove it to deeply shape product roadmaps, shipped dozens of news features, redesigns for 3 products, and 2 new enterprise products.
