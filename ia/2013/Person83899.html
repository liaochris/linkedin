Designed to deliver a strong and flexible adhesive layer on the wound surface.
Sure+Close® II™ High Viscosity Tissue Adhesive is a sterile, liquid topical skin adhesive containing a monomeric (2-octyl cyanoacrylate) formulation and the colorant D & C Violet #2 which allows the user to easily see where the adhesive has been applied.



Sure+Close® II™ is intended for topical application to hold closed easily approximated skin edges of wounds from surgical incisions, including punctures from minimally invasive surgery and simple thoroughly cleansed trauma-induced lacerations.

AS APPLIED

As applied to skin, Sure+Close® II™ is syrup-like in viscosity and polymerizes (dries) quickly (within two minutes if applied correctly). The increased viscosity of Sure+Close® II™ is intended to reduce the risk of unintended placement of the adhesive during application due to migration of the liquid adhesive from the wound site
