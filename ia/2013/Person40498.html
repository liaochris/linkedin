Elijah May™'s Summary

Over the past 15 years, I've developed an obsession with how companies can increase brand value through the articulation of, and adherence to, a core set of principles. As such, I place a great deal of emphasis on internal culture, external relevance, and open communications.

If you ask me about marketing or PR, I will probably talk about:

* being outrageous
* creating a unique experience
* using interactive media
* and just plain making people smile.

All-in-all, making a difference gets me up in the morning, but I do like to rock the boat a little.

In case you were wondering - and I know that you were - I've taken a minute to articulate my personal purpose, principles, and brand promise below:

PURPOSE

My purpose is to serve people that serve others

PRINCIPLES

- I believe that the only business worth being in is the business of making life better for other people.

- I believe that rules should be measure by the wisdom and compassion with which we make exceptions.

- I believe that sometimes you have to be unreasonable if you want to make the world a better place.

PROMISE

- Ask me to help you help others and we'll climb that mountain together.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Specialties:
- Defining Brands
- Connecting People
- Driving Meaningful Conversations
