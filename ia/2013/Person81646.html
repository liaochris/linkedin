Lisa Olson's Summary

I help businesses with their online marketing. Since transitioning from the medical device industry, I have turned my online marketing passion into a full-time business.

While creating our first couple of online products, segmented for the medical device industry in 2010, I was able to put my experience of project managing multi-million dollar clinical studies to use. Knowing first-hand the intricacies of a regulated industry and also incorporating the best user experience for adoption of web-based user access controlled products.

There are several areas that are important for companies with their online marketing. Knowing the customer and the unique selling proposition and then mapping this from a user experience is the first step in any website design and optimization.

The next area is creating the customized online system that will actually be able to be implemented in a fairly easy and quick basis. I subscribe to the school of strategic rapid application development. In the fast paced world of online marketing, there needs to be an urgency and speed that needs to be implemented along with the flexibility of the web-based technology to make changes quickly and easily, once the website analytics are analyzed.

I look forward to connecting with people that are interested in:

• Optimizing a new or existing website for a better user experience and outcome.

• Helping sales organizations through a customized customer relationship database system.

• Analysis for an online business strategy that is optimized for your unique customer and value proposition.

• Creating a collaborative marketplace, utilizing inbound and social media marketing plans and best practices for user experience strategies.
