Lori Maher (E-mail)'s Summary

Maher Group, LLC has successfully served a broad range of local and regional trade associations for over 15 years.

The traditional management by volunteers who may lack the skills, time, and capabilities to fulfill the needs of the organization can be counterproductive. Many trade associations do not have the capital to make the investment in the staff and facilities necessary for efficient operations. Maher Group, LLC offers an affordable and creative alternative.

Maher Group, LLC allows elected leadership of the association to focus on current "hot" issues and ongoing strategy. Volunteer leaders can focus on long-term goals and sustainability - not day to day management.
