Maggie Jackson's Summary

Award-winning Journalist and Author - on technology's impact on humanity, the science of attention and work-life balance in the Digital Age.

Author of "Distracted: The Erosion of Attention and the Coming Dark Age" (Prometheus Books) - the book that jumpstarted our global conversation on the fragmentation of attention.

Founding columnist of the popular Sunday Boston Globe column "Balancing Acts." Contributor to the Huffington Post, CNN.com, New York Times and publications worldwide.

Sought-after keynote speaker and media expert.
VP/Senior Fellow at Center for Talent Innovation, NYC.
