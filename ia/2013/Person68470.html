Joe Ortenzi's Summary

User eXperience Architect, Universal design advocate, Interaction designer, content strategist, with substantial experience and understanding across the entire project spectrum, from Strategic research through to reiterative testing.

Specialist in user-centred design, accessibility, compliance with web standards and a tested, user-advocate approach. Strategic planning, architecture and interaction design of Products, services and websites for private and public organisations including government agencies, charities, retail, manufacturing and entertainment, including mobile and multi-channel implementations.

Created and led teams of developers & designers in collaborative environments and experienced with team-based, iterative production environment. Interested in collaborative, agile work teams, and the horizon of mobile, social and integrated networks.

Specialties: User eXperience, strategy, scope, wireframes, information architecture, prototyping, user-centred design, interaction design, SEO skills and content strategy. PRINCE2 Registered Practitioner, iterative release cycles, team-building, knowledge sharing, procedural & tested accessibility.
Open-source advocate, web standards compliance, semantic markup, LAMP development environment, XHTML/HTML5, CSS, some JavaScript, DOM, WordPress environments and plug-ins.
