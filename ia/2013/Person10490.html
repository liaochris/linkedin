Peter B Nielsen's Experience

President

Aspetuck Antiques

January 2007 – Present (6 years 7 months) Fairfield County, CT

Aspetuck Antiques is located in Fairfield County, Connecticut. We are headquartered in the Aspetuck Valley area whose history goes back to the early seventeenth century when European settlers made first contact with the local Aspetuck native American tribe.

We are an online antiques sales and auction company that specializes in rare, unusual and designer antiques. Since 1997, our sales on eBay have earned us over 720 written testimonials to our credit and praise.

We are nearing completion of an online sales website of our own, which is targeted to appeal to upscale clientele in the retail, wholesale and designer markets. Our scope will be international and domestic. Expect the new website to have a grand opening and unveiling by January 2012. Our website is currently live, however, it will be a couple of weeks before we have the content and selection that is suitable for all audiences.

The auction arm of the business is currently in its development stage. Aspetuck Antiques is considering a wide range of options regarding its auction business. We will announce our news regarding this portion of the business by the beginning of 2012. Our goal is to run live auctions from both a physical location and online with an outfit such as Live Auctioneers.

We specialize in antique, vintage and mid-century merchandise of all types and styles, with a flair for the unusual and rare. We are fortunate to live and work in a part of the United States where exciting inventory is always bountiful. Our numerous sources include New England estates, peer to peer acquisitions, antiques shows and nearly every other source imaginable. Additionally; we expect to develop a strong presence in the categories of artwork, ancient and even prehistoric items.

Aspetuck Antiques is always enhancing its profile to include partnerships in the art & antiques marketplace worldwide.
