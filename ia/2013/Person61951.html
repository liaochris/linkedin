Jennifer Pender, CLTC's Summary

What insurance are you required to purchase in 2014? Where do you buy it? What is a Private Exchange, Federal Exchange/Marketplace, Private Market plan? Business owners/personal plan purchasers have asked these questions, but do you have answers? Health Reform Play or Pay provisions affect everyone! Pender & Associates is an insurance solutions organization solving employee benefit problems for small businesses and helping families with protection plan decisions. We understand the changes brought about by Health Care Reform and will help you solve non-compliance issues before they become tax penalties. Pender & Associates is a trusted insurance solutions partner serving Georgia and the regional southeast for over 40 years.
