Angelique Duffield's Summary

My social media marketing career actually began with my photography business. As a portrait and event photographer, I was looking to find more effective ways to market my business and connect with potential clients. Once I saw the power of what I was learning I decided that I needed to share all I know with other businesses and entrepreneurs to help them as well.

I discovered social media when it was still a relatively new form of online marketing, and committed to learning how to use it effectively to grow my business. I've attended social media and internet marketing conferences, workshops and seminars, and read business marketing books and blogs, by some of the world's leading experts including Mari Smith, Melonie Dodaro, Michael Stelzner, Scott Stratten, Amy Porterfield, among others.

I love connecting with business owners, entrepreneurs and professional consultants to teach them how to use the power of social media so they can thrive in today's marketplace. Businesses get better lead generation from social networks, and essentially make more money in their business with social media.

My training workshops and coaching programs show people how they can use Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter and YouTube to connect with prospective clients, get more sales leads, and increase their visibility online, build trust, and establish their status as an expert in their industry.

I do commercial photography or photograph corporate events for businesses, including head shots and staff photos, and create promotional videos, that companies can use on their websites, blogs, Facebook or LinkedIn profile to help them achieve better search results (SEO).
