Automotive Account Executive

Teletrac

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; TFC.L; Information Technology and Services industry

June 2013 – Present (3 months) United Kingdom

I manage and build customer relationships in the Northern part of the UK for Teletrac® Inc, a fast-growing, innovative software company with offices in Cranfield, UK and Southern California in the USA.

I provide information and support to Citroen Business Centres, as Teletrac hardware is installed in the majority of Citroen light commercial vehicles. This provides intelligent Sat-Nav functionality, including real-time traffic updates and alternative routing from thousands of traffic sensors across the UK. Typically, that will stop you wasting 5 hours of your life every month. However, the real power comes when the big data gathered from the telematics for a fleet of vehicles is unleashed using the cloud-based Fleet Director® platform.

Teletrac’s Fleet Director® is the most advanced and interactive fleet automation software on the market, equipped with easy-to-use navigation and safety capabilities. With innovative features that include on-board navigation, lane guidance and two-way messaging, it is the premier software for fleet tracking intelligence. Tracking more than 200,000 vehicles in 75 countries, Teletrac saves fleet owners time and money while reducing their carbon footprint. Teletrac customers report up to 30% lower fuel usage, an average of 15% less driver overtime, 12% higher productivity and less unauthorised vehicle use.

Teletrac focuses on fleet types of all sizes, working up and down the solutions curve from price-sensitive customers looking for basic GPS vehicle tracking, to supporting the needs of enterprise customers looking for service solutions, third-party data integrations or native business intelligence. Teletrac holds specific focus on servicing custom fleet needs in major industries including haulage, construction, property maintenance & plumbing, lawn & garden, transportation and service. Teletrac software currently processes over 25 billion data transactions daily through its cloud-based platform and real-time tracking capabilities.
