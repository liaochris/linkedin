Lynne Carr Columbus D.O.'s Summary

I am a board certified anesthesiologist specializing in interventional pain management. I specialize in injection therapies for back pain, neck pain, headaches, etc............that include epidural steroid injections, facet joint injections, botox injections, trigger point injections, occipital nerve blocks, radiofrequency neuroablation, and many other injection procedures. I believe that the most effective treatment of pain is a multidisciplinary approach to pain management. for that reason, I have established a multidisciplinary pain management clinic in Palm Harbor, Florida, that includes injection therapies, medical management, on-site physical or massage therapy, and mental health assessments and counseling.
In addition to in-office treatment of pain, we offer quality pain management products both in our office and through our online store at www.gulfcoastpain.com.
I enjoy sharing my expertise by lecturing to patients and fellow physicians regarding appropriate pain management as well as use and abuse of controlled substances.
I have had multiple guest appearances on local TV news programs regarding appropriate pain management and pain related topics

Gulf Coast Pain Management is a clinic in Palm Harbor, Florida in the Morton Plant Mease East Lake Outpatient Center
dedicated to properly diagnosing and treating people in pain.
We consider each patient a unique individual.
Our diagnoses, treatments and managements of pain are personal and assure our patients that their complaints have been heard, recognized, believed, and confirmed. This attitude, coupled with current medical expertise and treatment modalities, offers all of our patients relief.
Included are treatments for:
back and neck pain
headaches
arthritis
shingles
fibromyalgia
diabetic peripheral neuropathy
cancer pain
various other pain syndromes
Effective treatment modalities include fluoroscopically (x-ray) guided nerve blocks, ultrasound guided nerve blocks, medical management, on-site physical therapy or massage therapy, and mental health assessments with referral for counseling.
