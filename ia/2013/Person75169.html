Kevin O'Neill's Summary

As the CEO and Founder of O’Neill Consulting Group, I lead a global client-centered executive search firm that intersects clients’ need for talent with candidate aspirations. I have ownership of not only the firm’s strategic direction and aggressive growth, but also the ultimate satisfaction of every client. My team finds people who can have immediate impact – and staying power – in less time.

I try to lead with purpose, focusing on both success and significance. That means using my time and talent to make a real difference in people's lives and making this world of ours just a little better.

O’Neill Consulting Group’s approach is built on an understanding of the risks you face and the rewards you seek when hiring externally. That is why we devote the time and attention required to fully connect with your organization – internalizing your needs, your goals, your expectations, and your culture. Why you’ll always know what to expect from us: An engagement built around you. What we do is a business, with lots of complexity and big stakes. But it comes down to people. People we want to do our best for.

We build relationships with clients in our Consumer, Industrial & Manufacturing, Life Sciences, & Private Equity Practices. Other specialties include Business Services, Supply Chain, Legal, Insurance, and Not-for-Profit. Most often, we are engaged by client companies who are undergoing extraordinary transformations or expansions. We provide counsel for C-suite and Senior Executives including roles such as CEO, CFO, COO, CHRO, CTO/CIO, GM, EVP, SVP, VP, Director, etc.

More personally, I am the married father of four school-aged children, president of the parish council for Saint Thomas More Catholic Parish, and have been honored to serve on a number of not-for-profit boards. I have competed in three “Half-Ironman” triathlons and enjoy boating, recreational fishing, and skiing.

Client-Centered Executive Search
Built on an understanding of the risks you face. And the rewards you seek.

You know it, we know it: hiring people is a risky proposition. It’s expensive, it’s time consuming, and industry surveys show that more often than not it generates marginal long-term results at best. So why would anyone want to keep doing things the same old way?

We certainly don’t. We decided it was time to disrupt and transform a stagnant industry and completely change the standard paradigm. Which means looking at the situation through your eyes. Keeping our focus on your needs. Making ourselves answerable to you for our every action. And doing all we can to reduce risk and increase your odds of success.
