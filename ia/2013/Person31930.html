David is a proven Internet advertising, social media entrepreneur and startup executive.

In 1995, David was the very first VP of Sales and employee #5 at DoubleClick (later acquired by Google) where he helped pioneered the first cpm and cpc ad networks on the Internet. In 1998, David was recruited by MatchLogic (acquired by Excite@Home) helping to pioneer the first 3rd party ad serving platform with major brand advertisers General Motors and P&G. In 2000, David raised over $5.4 million in venture funding as CEO of Smartpoint (acquired by Aether Systems) a multi-channel alerts SaaS platform.

In 2007 David co-founded socialmedia.com (later acquired by LivingSocial), an early social application developer and the first social ad network supporting social platforms Facebook, MySpace, and Twitter. In 2008, with Microsoft as a strategic partner, David co-founded socialeyes.com the first social CRM platform enabling brands to optimize social presence across multiple social platforms Facebook, Twitter and MySpace. David drove the initial revenue for both of these startups.

David holds a BA in Film Composition from Berklee College of music and an MBA from Boston University and now resides in Austin, TX.
