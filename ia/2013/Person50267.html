Gregor Deticek's Summary

For more than twelve years, I have been involved in the web industry, either as Web Designer, Master, or Developer. Working in big multinational corporate offices or small three people startups. During that time, I learned the Ins and Outs of the internet and am still learning new things every day as things are changing rapidly as they have ever been.

During the past twelve plus years, I worked myself up from a Web Designer creating small banner ads for even smaller websites to a Web Project Manager overseeing the development of Web Applications involving designers, developers, and subject matter experts in various countries.

Working as a contractor, I helped local small businesses develop and employ online campaigns to improve their visibility. Looking for the right KPI, analyze ROI and optimize landing pages was always a challenging task to improve the business' future success. Nowadays, social media is the holy grail of pretty much everything and a must for every business, whether small or big. So, implementing a social media presence was a huge part of my contract work.

I usually have two to three of my own side project running to further develop my skills in the web industry. Be it a PPC / Affiliate site where I have to analyze the CPC and compare it to the ROI, or a website that survives completely through organic searches where finding the right back links is key along with optimized content (and trying not to be eaten alive by the penguin.)

I want to stay in the Web Industry because that's where may passion is. This industry never gets boring and new learning opportunities are around every corner. Learning new things is what keeps me wanting to go to work every day and I believe I can be a great asset to companies that offer challenging jobs.
