Rafael Morel's Publications

  • An introduction to Interpretation Equipment

    • Multilingual Computing
    • March 2007
    Authors: Rafael Morel

    Interpretation equipment is used to facilitate simultaneous interpretation from one language into another without interrupting the speakers or the listeners. This is achieved using a wireless signal — either FM or infrared — to transmit the interpreter's voice to headphones worn by those who do not understand the main language spoken. The key advantage of simultaneous interpretation over consecutive interpretation is that the interpretation is performed in real time; hence, it does not extend the time of the presentation or event.
