Carol Margolis' Summary

As founder of Smart Women Travelers, Inc. and Business Travel Success, Inc., Carol shows travelers, for business or pleasure, how to travel with less stress, increased productivity and more confidence.

Carol is the author of Business Travel Success...How to Reduce Stress, Be More Productive and Travel With Confidence!, is a speaker and consultant to companies and organizations with business travelers. She leads seminars to show business travelers how to live their life successfully while on the road, and consults with the travel industry on how to attract more business travelers.

Project Manager professional with over 20 years of global system implementations experience and 10 years of successful hands-on Oracle HCM implementations.

Specialties

Travel; Business Travel; Speaking, Consultant, Author, Project Management; Oracle HCM
