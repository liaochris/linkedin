Marilyn Suttle's Summary

Thanks for visiting my profile!

I'm known as a leading authority in the area of service excellence, turning around challenging customer service situations, business and personal relationships, and producing breakthrough results for individuals, teams and corporate cultures.

National press such as Ladies Home Journal, Woman’s World, Corp Magazine, Inc. Magazine, LA Times. Chicago Tribune & TV news have featured my advice on:customer service, communication & leadership.

After working with me my clients have won industry awards, raised customer satisfaction & get lasting results in reducing stress and experiencing greater success and self-fulfillment. Company leaders have made my presentations available to thousands of employees, improving their quality of life and passion to excel in their professions.

I specialize in creating Suttle Shifts in the way people think & act to produce massive results. With entertaining experiential group and individual activities, I change what people see as possible for themselves.

My bestselling book, “Who’s Your Gladys?” was initially published by AMACOM, NY in 2009. It's now in paperback. I also created the online course Customer Service Roadmap.

I train & consult Fortune 500 companies, universities, medical, mid-sized companies & entrepreneurs and have extensive experience with online community management.

I speak & host the Woman-to-Woman podcast series on iTunes sponsored Kimberly Clark Corporation's Poise, and speak regularly at Jack Canfield events.

Networkers, Sales Professionals & Entrepreneurs? I help bring out your compelling charismatic authentic self.
