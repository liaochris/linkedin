Amanda MacArthur's Summary

Deeply passionate about what makes people buy, click, read and share. I believe in the art of SEO and Social Media, and I'm a strong advocate of un-marketing.

I've made big companies more profitable, and little companies more famous. I know how to generate leads through useful content and loyalty through endless gratitude.

My research has been taught in workshops all over the world, and broadcasted on every medium - including international news! My face and article was recently on the cover of Digital Marketing Monthly's May 2013 issue. See below. It's crazy.

I'm intensely passionate about what I do and I love working with companies who are fiercely passionate about becoming better at connecting with their customers and with the world.

If you want to add me as a contact, my email is amanda@buzzfarmers.com.
