James Steer's Summary

I am the Operations Superintendent working in the Safety Department at SeaTec UK Ltd. I have a keen interest in improving safety and security within the shipping industry.

I am currently studying User Interaction Design as part of my degree and truly believe that by applying these fundamentals to our SMS's, SSP's and other manuals, we can encourage the seafarer to use them more and in turn reduce incidents, detentions and non-conformities.

I have an analytical mind and a drive for improvement of myself. Proven track record whilst operating in a high pressure environment and can be relied upon to deliver results.
