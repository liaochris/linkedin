President

Undergraduate Student Government of Baruch College

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Education Management industry

June 2010 – June 2011 (1 year 1 month)

Served as Undergraduate Student Government President, representing 12,000 students to the college and university administration, as well as local, state, and national government.

In this capacity I oversaw the expenditure and allocation of close to $1,000,000 and managed a team of 41 elected members. My responsibilities included serving on the Board of Directors for both the College Association and the Auxiliary Enterprises Board and sitting as an ex officio member of the Faculty Senate and General Faculty.

Our team accomplished several important initiatives during our term, including the implementation of a new campus-wide recycling program, the creation of a neighborhood discount network, and actions that led to the creation of the 25th Street public plaza and a forthcoming student center.
