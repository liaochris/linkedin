Ben Gribaudo's Summary

Combining a driving pursuit for excellence with a broad skill set and an observant, analytical aptitude, my goal is to provide technology solutions that help your organization achieve its strategic objectives. Currently based out of Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, I am also available to work remotely to assist with projects ranging in size from micro to multi-month and am able to work as a solo developer/project engineer or on a team.

---
Are you looking for a code writer or a software artisan?

The approach of “it doesn’t matter how it’s done, as long as it’s done quickly” lends to poor quality, resulting in a high total cost of ownership. The code produced is hard-to-maintain with numerous, hidden bugs.

Contrast the previous methodology to the thorough and efficient craftsmanship of the pragmatic software engineer. Clean code. Automated unit tests assuring stringent quality and allowing ruthless refactoring. Decoupled objects and a layered architecture making it easy to adapt and extend the code to meet the dictates of future requirements.

I look for an environment where contract developers of the latter caliber can thrive as they apply their skills for their client's benefit.

I like to put the methodical mindset and process analysis skills of a programmer to work outside the technology realm by coaching organizations towards strategic and operational excellence—in particular, helping nonprofits operate in a business savvy way.
