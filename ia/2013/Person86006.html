Marilyn O'Hearne MCC, Executive Leadership & Mentor Coach's Summary

Who will you be & what will you achieve as you fully step into your power?

““I believe in your ability to transform yourself and your organization!” Marilyn O’Hearne, Master Certified Coach, International Coach Federation. You are one step closer to transformation!

My clients say they are more:

* Prosperous

*Effective

*Confident

as a result of our coaching.

I delight in energizing, empowering, and developing leaders, and thus, their organizations. I quickly assess a situation and co-develop a plan with my clients to achieve and sustain their goals based on their vision, values, and purpose.

My clients adapt a more celebratory, fun, empowering approach which results in their positively impacting their organizational culture.

Sign up for my PEP for Leaders blog and receive a complimentary copy of my Flourish Magazine article, “Increase Your PEP!”, www.marilynoh.com

I have followed my calling, facilitating development for 20+ years including coaching; writing, training and mentoring coaches at ILCT (an International coach training program, since 2003) and the Kansas Leadership Center. How are you living out your calling?
