Dante Fazio's Summary

I am a post-production sound editor and audio mixer based in Los Angeles, California. I specialize in sound for feature films, television, and gaming. In addition to my freelance work I am also currently on staff at Warner Bros. Post Production in Burbank.

In 2006 I graduated from Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts, where I majored in Music Production and Engineering. In between semesters I interned at Avatar Recording Studios and Blast Digital in New York and Mix One Studios in Boston.

After graduating I relocated to Los Angeles and gained a position within Soundelux DMG. Soon after I was contacted by POP Sound and was offered an assistant mixer position, so I moved my services to Santa Monica. That work consisted primarily of commercial and documentary mixing, VO and ADR recording, and videogame dialogue editorial. While there I assisted in the mix of Academy Award nominated documentary 'The Witness', and was lead dialogue editor for 'Mafia II' developed by 2K Games.

In August of 2010 I began working for Warner Bros. Studios. I am also a sponsored artist with Fractal Audio Systems.
