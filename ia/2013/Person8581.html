Cathy DeWitt Dunn's Summary

Dallas Fort Worth Texas based Safe Money Talk Radio hosts, Cathy DeWitt Dunn and Matt Redding’s Growth & Income webinar provides invaluable information regarding the truth behind the “guaranteed growth” promises seen all over the internet. You’ll learn all about Growth and Income plans … and how they provide all the positive benefits of a fixed index annuity PLUS control, flexibility, AND growth.

If you are a client needing assistance, please email me directly at cathy@dewittanddunn.com.

Our new Growth & Income Plan is designed to show you how to translate your savings into guaranteed lifetime income PLUS an incredible growth component that really puts your money safely to work.

Join Cathy DeWitt Dunn and co-host Matt Redding every Sunday for Safe Money Talk Radio. With over forty years collective experience, Cathy and Matt are the retirement planning and annuity experts. They have helped thousands of individuals and families learn how to protect and grow retirement assets, create income that can’t be outlived, and provide a financial legacy for future generations – all while eliminating stock market risk.

Each week, Cathy and Matt share their experience and insight on meeting the retirement challenges faced by millions of Americans today.

Safe Money Talk Radio covers a wealth of topics such as:
•A review of the week’s stock market performance and the impact of market volatility on retirement portfolios
•Rethinking retirement planning in today’s rough economic environment
•The Baby Boomers’ changing investment focus: from wealth accumulation to income generation to get them through their retirement years
•Specifics on high quality fixed income products
•Interesting facts that put the importance of retirement income planning in perspective

Tune into Safe Money Talk Radio each week to learn more about how Cathy and Matt can help you strengthen your retirement future.
