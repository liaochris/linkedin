Evrim Kuran's Summary

She was born in the capital on a summer day.

She studied English Language & Literature at Hacettepe University and HR Management & Development at Marmara University. She received her Executive MBA degree at Sabancı University. She has trained people in various institutions such as Yeditepe University, Naval High School Command, and many others. Despite having the title of "filologist", she has fallen in love with the idea of becoming a salesperson. Her sales career is full of success and failure stories of many products from chemical fertilizer to blue jeans. She co-founded Dinamo Training & Consulting in 2005. Her areas of expertise include Generational Studies with particular focus on Generation Y, Organizational Attractiveness, Developing Effective Behavioural Patterns of New Age Employees. She is a "differentialist" and more than that, she is against collective mediocrity.

Evrim Kuran is also an Adler Certified Professional Coach (ACPC) and holds the Associate Certified Coach (ACC) designation from the International Coach Federation (ICF).

She keeps on observing, thinking, producing and learning.

She is married and she is Ali's supermom.
