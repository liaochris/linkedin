David Kachman's Summary

User experience is Dave's passion. We're all users in the end and every last one of us deserve better from our experiences, whether they're online or offline. To facilitate this, Dave collaborates with a broad range of people including designers, developers, stakeholders, business analysts, and most importantly, users, to create the best possible experience.

Dave conducts usability testing of all shapes and sizes: from wireframes to interactive mockups, live systems to mobile. Picking out key themes and findings from the results, he can then work with the stakeholders to ensure that the needs of the business and the user are met in the the best possible way to ensure a win-win situation.

Dave has a diverse background that includes working in both the Energy and GPS sectors as a software developer before finding his true passion in the web. He has experience as a User Experience Developer and Information Architect at the Habanero Consulting Group and now is a User Experience Designer at OpenRoad. He has a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Alberta.

Specialties: Usability Testing, Web Analytics, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery
