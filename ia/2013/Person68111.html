Jon Treon, CRS's Summary

I am a Natick Realtor specializing in residential real estate in the Natick, Wayland and Framingham, MA area. My objective is to listen closely to your needs and work diligently to assist you in meeting your real estate goals. When you are an informed buyer or seller, you'll make the best decisions for the most important purchase or sale in your lifetime. That's why one of my goals is to keep you informed on trends in the marketplace using the latest statistics in your local area. I firmly believe that real estate will be a sound investment now and in the future.

Over 70% of my business comes from repeat buyers and sellers and personal referrals. Satisfied clients are my primary goal.

As a full-time Realtor since 1993, I believe I have the necessary experience to negotiate the best possible price and terms for you in the purchase or sale of a home. Being a Certified Residential Specialist (CRS - less than 4% of all Realtor in the U.S. hold this designation) and a Graduate of the Realtor Institute (GRI) has provided me with the advanced marketing knowledge and skills required to make the purchase or sale of your house as smooth, pleasant, and rewarding as possible.

As a Natick resident, homeowner and parent of a child in the Public School system, I am personally committed to the community. The relationships I have built throughout the community and my extensive personal knowledge of the area will work to your advantage when you use me to represent you in the purchase or sale of your home.

Specialties

I am always looking to build new client relationships. I specialize in helping people buy and sell homes in the Metrowest Boston area. My expertise is real estate sales in Natick, Framingham, Wayland and surrounding Massachusetts towns.
