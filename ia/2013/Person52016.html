Andrea Haydon's Projects

  • Pregnant and Parenting Adolescent Support Services Year 2

    • June 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Andrea Haydon, Courtney Moore, Dustin Lynch, Helen Sanematsu

    The PPASS 2 Project involved implementing a refined journal prototype from the first PPASS Project. The journal was used by 52 pregnant and parenting adolescents in Indiana during the summer of 2012 thanks in large part to Empowering Teens as Parents, who implemented the journal within their program. The journal, its content, and the process of its use was evaluated by a research team from IUPUI. A photo voice component was also implemented to allow the adolescents to take photographs of their lives in addition to writing.

  • PPASS

    • June 2011 to August 2011
    Team Members: Andrea Haydon, Eric Gray, Courtney Moore, Dustin Lynch, Bridget Hawryluk, Brian Crain, Brandon Cockrum, Cheryl Harnishfeger, Ross Shafer

    Attic engaged in design research and prototype testing to develop a method of communication between pregnant and parenting adolescents and health organizations like the Indiana State Department of Health.
