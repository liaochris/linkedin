Lisa Silvershein's Summary

A certified master career coach focused on helping clients actively navigate their future by leveraging their strengths.

Career coaches individuals throughout their career lifecycle, including students, individuals in career transition and development, executives and people planning their active retirement.

Considered to be a secret weapon for her clients. Career coaching gives them the edge and confidence to achieve professional success.

Incorporates over 20 years of career coaching, human resources and management experience to her career coaching business. Has a BA in Psychology, an MS in Human Resources Management and is a Certified Master Coach through the Behavioral Coach Institute.

Specialties

Career coaching, behavioral coaching, retirement coaching, student coaching, job search skills, discovering and leveraging strengths, communication skills, time management, goal setting and achievement, moving people from ordinary to extraordinary.
