Heng Teng Teng's Summary

Executive Coach for Leaders in Behavioral Change, Career Coach for Professionals.

Teng Teng spent her formative years working closely with the line managers as a coach and HR business partner to develop a people-focused culture and good HR practices. She played an instrumental role in getting Hilti awarded the Best Employer in Singapore 2009. She has built a high level of trust within the organization and gained respect from the Senior Management and staff.

She believes leaders' behaviors have great impact on the team's performance and motivation. She spends great efforts in getting leaders to be mindful of their own behaviors on others, and coaches them on behavioral change. Her approach draws on the Stakeholder Centered Coaching methodology by Marshall Goldsmith. This approach supports a sustainable long term behavioral change of the leader.

Teng Teng is also passionate about getting people to enjoy and find meaning in their jobs. She coaches professionals on career planning and career transition at different life stages.

Specialties

Leadership Behavioral Coaching, Career Coaching for Professionals, Career Planning Workshops
