Russ Anderson's Summary

I am the owner of Anderson Homes, which provides high-end quality homes with modern amenities such as media rooms, wet bars and commercial-style kitchens at a price that is below market value.

I was born and raised in Columbia, Mo. During my senior year of high school, I joined the Army reserves and began drilling on weekends. During that time, I earned a promotion to E2 before I left for basic training. I served 8 years as a (91R) food inspector. After returning home from my army training, I enrolled at Kemper Military Academy. After attending classes for one year, I bought my first business, The Axle Shop. I owned and operated this business for seven years, before selling it to one of my employees in 2004.

I began building my first home in 2003, and has since then built more than 200 homes, commercial buildings and multi-family units. I pride myself as a family man; I have been married to my wife Andrea for more than 15 years. I am a proud father of two sons, Grant, 6, and Graden, 3. My hobbies include attending church at Grace Bible, spending time with my family, cooking, and golf.
