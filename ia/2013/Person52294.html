Heather Smith's Summary

Author of Xero for Dummies, Getting the Most out of Xero in a Day for Dummies, Mastering Australian Payroll in a Day for Dummies, Converting to Xero from MYOB in a Day for Dummies, Learn Small Business Start-Up in 7 Days and Learn MYOB in 7 Days.

I write for small business mags, blogs and corporate publications, including news.com.au, Flying Solo, Latte.

I would really love my own small business advice column in a suitable publication (print, online or radio!) – If you’re interested please get in touch!

As well as writing, I think it is important to be closely associated with what I write about. I work with micro and small business owners to assist them in implementing business management solutions that will allow them to generate timely and accurate financial reports. I implement, train, and support. I LOVE CLOUD TECHNOLOGY and what it has to offer.

I love empowering small businesses, if you think I can help please get in touch!

I also love sharing my knowledge through speaking. I’ve been told my talks are witty, warm, full of practical hands on, informative that engage, educate and entertain my audience. My talks are focused on starting, and growing a small business. They are brimming with useful information, inspirational stories and practical tips.
I would really love to speak at your next industry or franchise conference - – if you’re interested please ask your conference organiser to please get in touch!

I volunteer my time as a mentor at River City Labs (Brisbane) and Sunshine Coast Innovation Centre.

I'm happy to accept your invite to connect on LinkedIn, however this does not mean I want to accept money from the Nigerian kingdom, marry you, or join your email list. If you simply want to Pick My Brain about a small business issue visit: http://heathersmithsmallbusiness.com/shop/pick-my-brain/ to arrange some time with me.
