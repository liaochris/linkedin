Keith Johnston's Summary

The meetings industry and technology are strange bedfellows and it is my goal to show meeting and event professionals that they go together like chips and dip, dark and light, Batman and Robin.

I work with associations and corporations helping them develop plans to implement technologies throughout the event ecosystem from social media and content marketing to mobile apps and web design strategies. Whether you are looking to implement online registration for 100 people or need a hybrid event plan for 10,000 people, we will make you confident in the choices that you make.

When technology is used correctly, attendees are more engaged; meeting planners are more creative and confident.

In addition to my writing for PlannerWire, I have written articles for and been quoted in many industry magazines including Plan Your Meetings, Event Solutions, Meeting News, Connect, Corporate and Incentive Travel, PCMA Convene, Mexico Meetings Network and more. I got my full 15 minutes when I was featured on the cover of Midwest Meetings Magazine.

As a writer, publisher and speaker, I am not afraid to tell it like it is, using my meeting and event planning background I tackle not only technology but all of the issues that are facing our industry and in June of 2013, I was nominated for an Azbee Award with Plan Your Meetings and MeetingBoy for our article, "So You Say You Want a Revolution?".

Most recently, I have become the Co-founder, editor and publisher of The Meeting Pool, a technology website for the meeting and event industry that features articles, how-tos, editorial content, and the industries first technology decision engine that includes unbiased reviews of meeting industry technology solutions.
