John Pollard's Experience

Co-Owner

JM Pollard Building & Inspection Company

January 2000 – Present (13 years 10 months) Wilmington, North Carolina Area

JM Pollard Building & Inspection Company are 3rd generation Wilmington Custom Home Builders. John Jr. & John III are a father/son team that now run the day to day operations. John Sr. and John Jr. originally starting building in the greater Wilmington area in 1975 and John III joined the industry after college in 2000. JM Pollard has built numerous custom homes over the years in the areas finest communities. In 2005, JM Pollard expanded our services to include residential/commercial property inspections and building energy audits & testing. Please visit our company websites at www.pollardbuilding.com & www.inspectilm.com
