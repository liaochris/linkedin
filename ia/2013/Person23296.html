Chris Garrity's Summary

A highly creative developer with a thorough understanding of the design and the development process, over 10 years experience, working closely with universally recognisable brands such as Vodafone, Volvo and Salesforce.com, with a focus on B2B markets in Europe, designing, developing and delivering unique and innovative solutions on time and to cost.

A wide skill base across multiple disciplines, an understanding of the creative design process and a strong interest in the changing landscape of the web and emerging mobile environments, a desire to learn new technologies and methods, quickly picking up new programming languages and techniques.

Experience of team leadership and project management, an understanding of client relationships and interactions, a thinker who can provide new ideas and perspectives, readily adapting to new challenges and problems by offering new perspectives and relevant solutions, with a skill to describe these in a nontechnical and informative way.

An individual, who is committed, trusted and dedicated to providing the very best solutions and experiences to his clients and their users.


Development Skills:

HTML5 (video and canvas tags) and XHTML, responsive design techniques, extensive Actionscript 3 and 2 including development in both the FLEX and the Flash IDE delivered online or through the Adobe AIR platform, .net C# (including SOAP web services, third party APIs), CSS3, Javascript including use of frameworks (JQuery) and JSON data layer, Microsoft SQL server (including database design, creation and programming, e.g. T-SQL stored procedures), ASP.net, Classic ASP, Visual Basic 6, experienced in MVC development pattern.

Currently Researching: Objective C, Phonegap, mobile app development, CSS pre-processing (less and sass).


Design Skills:

Highly competent with Photoshop and the Adobe suite, experience in website and mobile UI design, creation of online and print marketing and advertising materials.
