Scott Carson's Summary

I am owner and managing member of Inverse Investments LLC, a Austin based, defaulted note buying company. I specialize in finding non performing notes on residential and commercial properties and purchasing these notes for our own portfolio.

I also teach Note Buying For Dummies, a 3 day workshop for investors on buying defaulted notes.

I was the previous Sr. Real Estate Coach for RealEstateProfitCoach.com and a Mortgage Banker and Vice President with JPMorgan Chase.

Specialties: Non Performing Notes, Raising Private Money, Short Sales, Defaulted Paper, Residential and Commercial Investing
