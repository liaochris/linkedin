Kristina Barnett's Summary

I am an independent project management consultant and a professional coach.

As a senior-level project manager, I manage UX design, research, web design/development, software design projects and more. I help folks estimate, budget, schedule, communicate, and track progress in a way that suits each team. In addition, I help small organizations improve project management and operational practices to help things run more smoothly.

As a coach, I use my business and project management experience as well as an evidence-based coaching framework to help clients achieve lasting changes they're looking for. My coaching is made up of strong challenges, equally strong support, and a dash of humor. I coach in person in Portland, OR or by phone or Skype with clients anywhere.
