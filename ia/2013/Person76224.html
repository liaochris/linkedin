Kevin Kovaleski's Summary

Kevin has a passion for bringing impactful ideas to life. He currently serves as the Director of Communications and Marketing Strategy at the College of Technology and Innovation for Arizona State University (aka Director of the Comm Shop).

Prior to relocating to the sunny southwest, Kevin was responsible for digital communications and government relations for the government of the District of Columbia. He led the social media strategy for Eastern Market on Capitol Hill and was a member of the team that brought those snazzy red Capital Bikeshare bikes to the nation’s Capital.

Kevin loves playing his vintage banjo, hiking, teaching his beloved dog Bandit sweet tricks and cooking for friends and family.

He does not refer to himself in the third person in real life.

Specialties: Communications, media relations, management, team-building, content development, social media, writing, copy editing, website management, marketing, program management, change management, relationship management, strategic planning, photography, design, optimism.
