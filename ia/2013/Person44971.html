Flavio Picchi's Summary

Complete corporate attorney with broad experience in Brazilian legal matters, both in-house and in law offices. For the last seven years, I have been assisting national and international clients, both individuals and companies in their needs related to the Brazilian business and legal environments.

I can provide legal support in Portuguese and English, but Italian and French are also working language with clients. My professional focus is on international investments, corporate law, contracts, public bids, tax planning, antitrust filings, corporate restructurings, wealth management, international and local arbitration.

Specialties

General matters of corporate law, international and investment law, contracts, real estate, intellectual property, administrative and antitrust law and public bids, tax and fiscal matters, international and local arbitration and litigation. For a broader explanation of services provided and clients assisted, please feel free to be in touch!
