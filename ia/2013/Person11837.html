Barkha Dattani's Summary

Barkha Dattani has made pioneering efforts by sharing her concepts and innovative ideas for value addition in brands for Reliance, Launch of Hyundai Getz, Kiran Exports, Sabio Eagle Group, Shaadi.com, Aptech, K Raheja through Exhibitions.

After her successful foray in Barkha’s Exhibits, with her expertise, she founded another company in the name and style of Barkha’s Brand clinic.

Barkha's has its specialization & immense expertise in ‘Logo Designing’.
We are passionate about creating identities.
We are passionate about meaningful logos.
We live and breathe logos.

Our company has designed Logos and Brand Strategies for various Brands like:
R Madhavan, Vijay Sales, Rashmi Housing, Sunshine Group, K Omega, Print It, DNA, Sejal Glass Ltd, G S Oils Ltd, Sabio Group, Rainbow Kids, Right Channel Constructions, Casa Amore, Scintillating Jewellery, Ajmera, Vasundhara Agro, Talwarkars, SOTC & many more.
www.barkhasbrandclinic.com

Specialties

Logo Designing, Brand Name Suggestion, Brand Tagline Suggestion and Strategy Planning.
