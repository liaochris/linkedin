Carl Von Luger Steak & Seafood's Summary

We offer fine dining at an affordable price. Serving USDA PRIME Steak and quality Seafood for Lunch and Dinner. Previous owner of Great Uncle Peter's Steakhouse. An age-old family tradition since 1887.

Looking for an approachable fine dining decor beyond the casual pub look? Join us at Carl Von Luger, Scranton's finest steak and seafood dining establishment. Dine inside or out, lunch or dinner, in our beautiful modern art deco atmosphere.
