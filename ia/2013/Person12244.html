Michael Dorshkind's Summary

I carry the highest rating (AV) given by Martindale-Hubbell, the premier legal directory and attorney rating system in the United States. The peer review process employed by Martindale-Hubbell measures two criteria: Legal Ability and Professional Ethics.

In terms of legal ability, I have been designated "Very High to Pre-eminent." In terms of professional ethics, I adhere to the highest standards of the Bar.

For over 30 years, my practice has been dedicated to representing individuals in Personal Injury and Medical Malpractice Claims. I do not represent the interests of the insurance industry.

I was a Deputy District Attorney in Alameda County, California, for three years. I then worked as an associate
attorney in a firm that represented the interests of medical and liability insurers for the next two and one half years.

I have been exclusively representing persons who are the victims of medical neglect and negligence since
1982.

Specialties

I specialize in Personal Injury, Medical Malpractice, Car Accidents, Motorcycle Accidents, Wrongful Death Claims, and Child Injuries.
