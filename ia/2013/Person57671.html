James M Gutierrez's Summary

Mr. James M. Gutierrez is President of Career Resources, Incorporated. A leader in affirmative action consulting, Affirmative Action Plans. Mr. Gutierrez is a recognized national speaker and subject matter expert and is highly regarded by many law firms.

Gutierrez joined Career Resources in 1995 and is the chief architect of the company’s strategy for leveraging it as national leader. He became President of Career Resources in 1998 and purchased it in 2003. Under Gutierrez’s leadership, the company has grown over 600% and now has clients in forty three states.

In 2010 he became the President of the San Diego Industry Liaison Group and was instrumental in partnering with the OFCCP to create the first “Construction Industry Liaison Group” in the nation, which is now being used as the template to create similar organizations in all 50 states.

CRI provides comprehensive employment management solutions that ensure the federal contractor compliance under federal Equal Employment Opportunity & Affirmative Action regulations.

Founded in 1979, CRI is minority owned & is an acknowledged industry leader having prepared thousands AAP’s for hundreds of loyal customers throughout the U.S. and its outlying territories. CRI receives many client referrals from law firms and works in the background for some law firms preparing their clients AAP’s. No other company can match the depth and breadth of CRI’s project experience. Clients trust the CRI team’s recommendations.

Among a diverse group of competitors, CRI is squarely situated as the Value Leader. We offer services available from large law firms – at a much lower cost. We differentiate ourselves from 2nd tier consultants and their “one-size-fits-all” solutions. Your investment in our partnership is the basis of a long term, cost effective compliance solution personalized for your specific requirements.

We look forward to having the opportunity to share our services and present our company as your future consultant.
