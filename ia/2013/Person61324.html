Jeff Griffin's Summary

Jeff is Quri's EVP of Sales and Business Development, helping grow the revenue functions of this crowd-sourced retail intelligence company that enables brands and retailers to maximize in-store product conditions, revenue, and profitability.

Prior to Quri, Jeff was recruited to become Bain Capital Ventures-funded HookLogic’s first President of Retail, helping e-commerce retailers drive new, media-based revenue streams, and helping brand marketers create targeted opportunities to influence shoppers at key points in their digital path to purchase.

Prior to joining HookLogic, Jeff was Shopper Science’s co-founder & GM, and an SVP of IPG Mediabrands. Shopper Sciences – in just its first year - delivered the primary insights work for Google’s ZMOT initiative, the Booz/GMA Shopper 5.0 study, and was the first shopper marketing agency (and youngest agency ever) to receive the Agency of the Year Award from Media Magazine.

Prior to Shopper Sciences, Jeff was SVP of Premier Retail Networks. where he helped create & grow the Digital Place-Based Advertising industry by building and monetizing advertising & merchandising networks in leading retailers worldwide.

Before PRN, Jeff worked in Product Management at the Kellogg’s Company, and before Kellogg's Jeff helped create, launch, and grow the Channel Marketing functions at Nabisco Foods’ Lifesavers and Planters companies.

Jeff is also a sales & sales management alumni of Nestle Foods, Coca-Cola Foods, and Procter & Gamble.

In addition to his work in the shopper marketing world, Jeff is an investor, advisor, and partner in startup & early-stage B2B & B2C eCommerce companies, focusing on the Customer Development Process and revenue generation.

Jeff is a graduate of the University of North Carolina’s School of Journalism & Mass Communication, and of the University of Michigan’s Ross School of Business and currently resides in Atlanta, GA with his wife and son, both of whom are excellent shoppers.
