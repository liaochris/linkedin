Mel Zabel's Projects

  • The Awareness Exercises

    • February 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Mel Zabel

    The Awareness Exercises is a course of learning Awareness.

    Awareness is little more than our higher conscious observation redefined as being aware.

    Staying in the place of the observer is a key to freedom of "it", whatever "it" may be.
    As we look at that which is not which we truly are, we become aware not only of
    that which is the perceived other, but of ourselves as being the observer simultaneously.

    The freedom within these practices is that which is this simple Awareness.
    With true Awareness, one is freed from the perception of being anything other than the
    observer of the perceived reality as that reality perceived.

    "I" is not even the body which shares these words. The true "I" is that which is above this,
    the observer, that which is ever present even at the times when unknown to us.
    We are observers living out a life experience within our own created realities, as we
    have created them and as we've chosen to perceive them.

    Most of the human life experience is illusion in this way.
    With the Awareness of the illusory quality of our life experience there is freedom from the feeling of being bound to it.

    Through the practice of the Awareness Exercises it is quite possible to attain acceptance of not only all that is, as it is, but more importantly, self acceptance that brings forth true love of self, life and the world you perceive around you.

    This is the result of stepping out of definition and engaging your life from a place of observance rather that place of the controller. This also also reveals the truth of your perfection.

    You are in fact perfect.

    Love what is.
    Engage that which you love.

    May my offerings serve you well.

    I wish for you peace and happiness within all of your life experience.

    ~ Mel Zabel
