During my early days with with CAIS, I designed and developed their first website and intranet site; in time these were reviewed and redesigned. The new intranet was implemented by utilising Microsoft SharePoint technology.

Performance Officer

Nonprofit; 5001-10,000 employees; Civic & Social Organization industry

February 2000 – February 2002 (2 years 1 month) Llandudno

Sure Start was a UK Government initiative, with the aim of "giving children the best possible start in life" through improvement of childcare, early education, health and family support, with an emphasis on outreach and community development. The programme was intended to support families from pregnancy until children were four years old and was an ambitious attempt at tackling extreme deprivation and the cycle of social exclusion.

I worked on the Sure Start Project shortly after its launch in Conwy and was involved in opening the first Children’s centre in the area as well as peripatetic services in the rural localities. Sure Start also commissioned partner agencies to provide services; my role included designing, implementing and monitoring performance information systems to commissioned projects, and providing a full administrative service.
