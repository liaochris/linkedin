Justin Hartman's Summary

Justin Hartman is the co-founder and CEO of Social Code and is a well respected entrepreneur in the industry. In 2007 he co-founded Afrigator which he later sold a stake to MIH Internet - a division of Naspers Limited.

In December 2008 Justin was voted on as one of eleven members to the Silicon Cape Steering Committee – a not-for-profit, community owned & driven initiative that serves to attract and bring together local and foreign investors, the brightest technical talent, and the most promising entrepreneurs and now sits on the advisory panel for the Initiative.
