Personal Information: I have always been proud of my identification as a “Denver native”. Growing up in Capitol Hill, attending Dora Moore, Morey, East and the University of Northern Colorado, as well as growing up in the Real Estate business has made me who I am today. I am dedicated to my community and neighborhood and encourage others to do the same. My parents were very influential in my career choice, with a Realtor mother, architect and city planner father, both of whom were immersed in the development of our neighborhood. As a SUN (Stapleton United Neighbors) board member, I feel I am following in their steps in creating and nurturing a community that I am excited to raise my two children in.

I have been in the Real Estate business for over 15 years and have developed relationships with City and Neighborhood planners, Private developers, Builders and many homeowners throughout the Denver Metro area. The market takes many turns, however, there has been one consistency that happens to be my favorite part of the job, handing someone the keys to their home.

I have been able to dominate and explore a niche that many realtors have no knowledge of; Affordable Housing. It is very satisfying to assist someone who never thought homeownership was a possibility.

The Knop Home Team offers a vast knowledge and expertise in Real Estate that will benefit you whether you are an investor, developer, home buyer or seller.

Specialties

Customer Service, Affordable Housing Expert, 2009 and 2010 Stapleton top selling agent, Denver native, First time homebuyers, On site sales, builder and developer knowledge and connections.

Outstanding and personal customer service is the strength of the KnopHomeTeam. Every client is different and every deal is different so I take pride in our ability to assess what needs to get done for each transaction and getting the job done. Sometimes it takes staging, painting, lender expertise, a great contact referral, or just meeting after hours to explain things thoroughly we will do what it takes.

As Vice President of Sales at Distinctive Properties I am hands on with the sales team with technology training, operations and helping agents succeed.

I also have an unusual specialty in being an expert in Denver's Affordable Housing Program. I have sold over 150 affordable homes in Stapleton and am well versed in the program all over the metro area. It is a niche market that is very rewarding because the program creates homes for people that may not be able to purchase otherwise. It is income based so it helps teachers, nurses and other folks.

At the end of day my favorite part of every transaction is when the keys are exchanged and there are smiles all around. It is an honor to have the responsibility of helping our clients in such an important transaction and transition in their lives.
