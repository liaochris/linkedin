Ellie Nieves, Esq.'s Summary

Fresh out of law school, Ellie Nieves cut her teeth in the high stakes game of New York City politics. Often the youngest, and many times the only woman, sitting at the table, she earned a reputation as a much sought-after strategist. Through her unrelenting work ethic and natural leadership ability, she was recruited for campaign leadership roles such as New York State Political Director and Campaign Manager for national and city-wide campaigns. Ellie eventually transitioned into the private sector and became an award-winning attorney for the Government Relations department at MetLife; later working as Chief of Staff to the President of MetLife International.

Today, Ellie is a Women's Leadership Speaker and Coach. She presents seminars and webinars for companies, organizations and associations that want to develop their emerging women leaders into effective managers and executives. She also coaches women who want more both personally and professionally.

Ellie's keynote presentations, seminars, webinars and retreats help emerging leaders to develop leadership skills, set strategic goals, bolster effectiveness and create a personal brand.

Topics include:

Developing Your Vision for Success

Show Up, Speak Up and Step Up: Leadership Strategies for Emerging Women Leaders

Stand Out: Developing Your Personal Brand

Building Powerful Partnerships and Networks

Faith at Work: Career Success Without Compromise

For more information: www.LeadershipStrategiesforWomen.com
