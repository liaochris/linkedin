Annetta Wilson's Summary

Clients, business and money head out the door straight to your competition if your messages and presentations don't hit the mark...not to mention the damage to your reputation.

I help clients leverage presentation skills and media to get more business.

Most communication problems stem from three major mistakes. Get our free report to find out if you're making them: http://www.SpeakWithEase.com

I've coached on-air journalists at CNN and have coached for Walt Disney World's Ambassador Program.

Clients include Tupperware, AAA and Orlando Health.

Individual and group trainings are available via telephone and in person. Behavioral and values-based assessments ('how' and 'why' you do things) are also incorporated in some sessions.

Specialties

Signature workshops: 'Help! The Media Are Here! ' (media training); 'They're In the Room. Now What?' (presentation skills/public speaking training); 'The Art of Networking' (how to work a room so it works for you); Dynamic Communication ('how' you show up can make or break your career); 'You've Got Less than 15 Seconds. Impress Me!' ('elevator speech' preparation). Certified Mastery Coach; Certified Trainer, former television news anchor, reporter, producer/talk show host.
