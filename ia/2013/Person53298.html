Howard Lubert's Summary

11 years as the principal of SafeHatch LLC, a technical due diligence and business acceleration consultancy that supports startup and early stage technology companies. SafeHatch typically takes a senior management role in the portfolio companies it supports and works with them until they are prepared to complete their management teams with full-time employees. Business Acceleration services may be provided for companies in almost any discipline but are likely to be focused in the Internet, e-Business, security, SaaS, infrastructure, or wireless space.

28+ years of technical due diligence for Safeguard Scientifics and its family of funds as well as other VC, Angel and investment organizations.
