Dawnmarie Hatfield's Summary

Working with people is exciting for me. Everyone has a story and the experiences you take from your relationships are far more important. In the avenue of Real Estate, I am a buyer's agent at heart always trying to find that perfact house or property. My background in legal real estate provides a confidence to the buyers and sellers that I will do what I set out to do making sure contractual due diligence is complied with.

In my legal real estate employment, I managed and distributed over 45 million dollars in commercial and residential real estate for Trust disbursements. Those transactions included inspections, zoning ordinances and deed searches. 1031 exchanges and transfer of title for all property. Commercial real estate managment of leases and tenants along with title transfers for Charitable Trust establishments.

I feel this background allows me to help whom I am working with to be aware of their contractual obligations and their rights as a consumer and to assist them in making the best decisions they need to in order to make it to a successful close and enjoy the new home ownership they've worked hard to have.
