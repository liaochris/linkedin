Daniel Minihan's Summary

Daniel is the Director of Wealth Management, a Partner in the Accounting and Taxation Services Division and Head of Private Clients at Moore Stephens Melbourne.

Daniel works with a broad spectrum of clients from small individuals to large private family groups. The advice Daniel and his team provides spans cash flow, debt structuring, personal protection and retirement planning for individuals through to asset allocation, portfolio construction and investment selection for investors small and large.

Daniel is also responsible for a portfolio of business and private clients where he provides strategic advice on accounting, taxation, structuring and commercial issues. These clients range from small to medium size businesses, through to medium and high net worth individuals and family offices. Daniel brings a unique ability to combine accounting, taxation and commercial advice together with unbiased investment, asset allocation and structuring advice to deliver group wide holistic solutions.

Daniel also authors an investment blog and is a regular speaker on the topic of investments and financial planning.

Daniel has been an Authorised Representative of Moore Stephens Consulting (Melb.) Pty Ltd (AFSL 236556) for over 12 years and has been with Moore Stephens for over 15 years.

Moore Stephens has held a financial services license for over 25 years and Daniel and his team have significant experience working in financial markets and in more recent times successfully steering clients through the Global Financial Crisis.

Daniel and his team of finance professionals provide advice and services encompassing:

Specialties: Daniel provides clients with unbiased advice to help them make smart choices about their money, putting their finances in order and keeping them that way...for life.
