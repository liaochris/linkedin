Dr Randi Lou Franklin's Summary

I have worked as a translator/interpreter for over 20 years. 2012 saw an year upswing in business! I look to continued growth in 2013. My love for language has always been part of who I am. I have taught from Pre-K through College levels Spanish and French. I have a gift for language and truly enjoy sharing it with others. 2013 is our 25th year in business and I look forward to sharing our success with our clients as we celebrate this pinnacle. My business has allowed me to grow from my languages of study to providing top level services in over 150 languages. In addition I work with many companies and groups providing them with cross-cultural training so that they are more successful in their endeavors. My professional experience runs the gamit from working as an interpreter at the G7 conference and translating technical manuals, to tutoring young children and translating children's books. In addition I have the good fortune of working with our college students increasing their linguistic and cultural knowledge.

Specialties: I specialize in legal, medical and technical translations as well as interpreting for community out-reach programs.
