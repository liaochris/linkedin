Edward Kemp's Experience

User Experience Lead

Webigence Ltd

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

August 2011 – Present (1 year 10 months) Wimbledon, London

Webigence specialise in high quality website and application development for larger and more technically complex client requirements such as online portals, booking engines, marketplaces, Ecommerce websites, admin systems, and mobile and tablet browser based applications.

My role as User Experience Lead begins with a strong new business and client focus whereby I will be involved in pitching to win new projects alongside our Technical lead.

Once a project has been won, I will then spend time listening to clients and key stakeholders, doing research, and gathering and prioritising information which I will compile together into a specification / requirements document. I then work from this spec document to design the page template wireframes and hook them up forming a prototype that visualises the page structure, layout and user journey of the new website / application.

At the point where the client is happy to sign off the wireframes / prototype, I then work closely with the visual designer by briefing them and ensuring the wireframes which have formed the foundation of the site, are translated and produced to a high standard.

I then continue to be the clients main point of contact, and help produce the overall project ensuring that the development team match the design exactly and we deliver the site to the highest standard possible for the client.
