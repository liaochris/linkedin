E. Martin Davidoff's Summary

I have over 30 years experience working as an accountant and attorney. I am the author of a column in CPA Magazine as the IRS Representation Advisor. I am the owner of accounting and law firms with a total of 15 employees dedicated primarily to taxes, tax advice and tax preparation. Our goal is to provide peace of mind to our clients. Please see my website for more information.

I am the CEO of the Make A Smile Foundation, Inc., which is a public charity dedicated to help children. See www.makeasmilefoundation.com .

I am on the Board of Directors of Bioclinica, Inc., a public company. See its website at www.bioclinica.com . During my tenure at BioClinica, the company grew from less than $24 million to over $98 million.

I am the proud father of two daughters and grandfather of two beautiful granddaughters.

On Wednesday nights you will find me on the Basketball Court!

Specialties: Representing Individuals and Companies before the IRS and State Tax Agencies. We provide expert representation services and provide our clients Peace of Mind.
