Jane Stretton's Summary

Life is all about finding your own way, isn't it?

and I am always interested in working collaboratively with other people who recognise and nurture this particular concept as a "route to success".

I had enough of spending my time doing things I did not believe in, that served no purpose, and held no satisfaction.

Finding a place where I feel grounded and able to be myself, has been hugely important for my 'being' - which is equally important as 'doing.' I urge everyone to take time to do this, whether metaphorically, temporarily or through finding a physical place, where they can "be".

After professionally accredited training in performance coaching, I work with business owners and managers, helping them to use their own core values, and real self, to best business effect.

I am professionally respected for my integrity, straight talk and generosity of spirit.

Understanding and using your personal selling point (PSP) can often be the key to unlocking business success. And it doesnt need to be unique - just delivered in a way that is personal to you.

Here at Dove Farm, we try to put these theories into practice, in the way that we run our own business enterprises. Our cottages and meeting/dining room provide a 'place in the country' for around 16-18 guests.Great for entertaining friends and family gatherings.

Dove Farm is a quirky venue for training events, workshops and small corporate away-days. Thre farm and country environment is rich in positive energy for coaching, healing and other personal development programmes and retreats.

I am a longstanding advocate and co-network leader for WiRE (women in rural enterprise.) Dove Farm is home to a WiRE networking group serving Derbyshire Dales and Staffordshire borders.
