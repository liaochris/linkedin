Current clients include Big Picture Learning, a national network of innovative high schools funded in part by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and Gateway to College, a national network of community colleges that offer a way for high school drop outs to get their high school diplomas while gaining community college credits. School Development Consultant/HS Principal

The Big Picture Company

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Education Management industry

August 2003 – June 2007 (3 years 11 months)

I directed all daily operations of the high school such as recruited, hired and trained the staff and built and tracked annual budget. I forecasted and analyzed student achievement data and made alterations to the program to meet needs of the school. With the team, I ensured that there was a 100% college acceptance rate for graduates of whom 75% were first generation to attend college.

Consultant

The Big Picture Company

Nonprofit; 11-50 employees; Education Management industry

2002 – 2007 (5 years)

During my time at Big Picture, I managed sales and project-execution for ten school accounts within the Big Picture national network, which is a project of the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation. I was responsible for designing over 45 instructional modules and delivering training to ensure the successful implementation of key aspects of the Big Picture design such as training volunteer mentors, individualized learning plans, project-based learning, advisory structure, and project management. I coordinated and presented at seven national conferences. Dennis Litky and Elliot Washor, the co-founders of the Big Picture, are amazing visionaries and I am grateful for the incredible opportunity I had and continue to have to assist with this very important work.

Trainer

The Boomerang Project

1996 – 2006 (10 years)

The Boomerang Project, owned by Carolyn Hill, Micah Jacobson and Marybeth Campbell, is the parent organization of Link Crew, a high school transition program. Phil Boyte is the original creator of Link Crew and when I met him in my 4th year of teaching, he transformed my understanding of what student empowerment and student engagement could be. Link Crew is a program where 12th graders mentor 9th graders so that they stay engaged with school. I brought the program to Harbor High, then to Aptos High, and then I started to work for Phil, recruiting and training teachers all around the country to do Link Crew in their schools. I trained over 350 adults to be Link Coordinators. I also presented the Orientation Assembly to numerous high schools, the largest audience being over 900 high school freshmen. (And it rocked!!!) Link Crew is one of the most outstanding programs in this country and it was an honor to be part of bringing it to so many schools. www.boomerangproject.com.

Principal Consultant

Mari Ruddy Consulting (Self-employed)

1998 – 2003 (5 years)

I launched, operated and marketed a successful business to create and conduct national workshops and seminars for schools and organizations on leadership, team-building, vision and mission creating, staff development, culture and climate building and motivation. During this time, I had a long term engagement with the University of California at Santa Cruz in the Educational Partnership Center. I coordinated, trained and recruited teams of teachers and college professors at six different school pipelines (Kinder - College) with the intent to work to level the playing field and make it possible for children from disadvantaged backgrounds to have an equal opportunity to attend the UC and CSU systems.

Lead Consultant

Collective Solutions

1999 – 2001 (2 years)

Kath Robinson and I did a collaborative project at Watsonville High School for two years with the Conflict Resolution Team. Kath taught the conflict resolution skills and I taught leadership and teambuilding. Together we were a dynamic and powerful duo, so we decided to take a chance and start our own company. We had many fantastic work experiences with non-profits and education organizations. During our work partnership, I deepened my facilitation skills and honed my presentation abilities.

Assistant Principal

Aptos High School

August 1994 – June 1998 (3 years 11 months)

During my first year, I oversaw the facilities and handled discipline issues. The following three years, I managed the Bilingual Advisory Committee, evaluated teachers and oversaw several pro-active student engagement programs including Link Crew and the Student Government. I used the medium of dances, proms, Homecoming and other traditional high school activities to teach project management, leadership, effective communication, time management and conflict resolution. At one point, at one time, over 150 students were enrolled in the various programs. What we were able to accomplish was amazing. Over the three year period, student suspensions dropped by over 70%, 9th grade failures were dramatically reduced and the overall feel of campus improved.

Associate

Odyssey Performance Enhancement Network

1992 – 1996 (4 years)

I assisted Bill, Lain and Scott (now at LearningChange) in running many high and low ropes courses. I went to work part-time with these amazing men after taking my high school students on several courses with them. I never overcame my fear of heights, but I did learn a tremendous amount about group dynamics, how to set-up teambuilding activities and how to guide a group through powerful learning experiences.

Spanish Teacher

Harbor High School

August 1988 – June 1994 (5 years 11 months)

During my six years at Harbor High, I was a Spanish language teacher, levels one through three. I also was the Activities Director and I was the advisor for the club Friday Night Live. I started the Link Crew program and I was the assistant director of the 24 Hour Relay Challenge. It was during my tenure at Harbor High that I came to understand how incredible it is to provide the opportunity and to give the skills necessary to young people to make a difference in their immediate community. The vision and hope of youth goes far when supported by caring and believing adults.
