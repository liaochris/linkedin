Gene Sasso's Summary

With more than 15 years of communications, public affairs, marketing and brand development experience, I have helped organizations create conversations and build relationships with stakeholders. More recently I have developed expertise as a professor of Public Relations, and higher education administrator.

As teacher and advisor to graduate students, I have excelled in the online and traditional classrooms, receiving superior student and peer evaluations. It has been especially rewarding to help students – American and international – achieve their academic potential, career goals, and life objectives.

In academic administrative roles, I developed and launched a remarkably successful master’s (Public Relations) program, delivered online. In the initial two years, more than 250 students enrolled, the retention rate exceeded 90%, with gross revenues of $6 million.

I am an action oriented visionary, a builder of departments and agile agency teams, with success as a servant leader and mentor. I also have considerable accomplishments as a consultant and in-house shop-of-one, advising and implementing stakeholder relations, brand support and advertising.

Responsive to the demands of organizational oversight, I am a trusted confidant to executives, a spokesperson, strategic thinker, and hands-on contributor. I have devised award winning and goal exceeding strategies and tactics that combine social media techniques with traditional print and broadcast media to maximize reach, resources and ROI.

An avid reader and quick learner, I stay current with business theory, technology, tools and practices, envisioning ways to make the new and emerging applicable and profitable.

Curious with strong interpersonal skills and eager to make connections, I have a robust network that includes personal and business associates across a spectrum of industries, as well as media, government, not-for-profit and public service professionals from around the world.
