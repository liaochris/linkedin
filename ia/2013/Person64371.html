John Zanetta's Summary

My architectural illustration career began in high school when I was introduced to the illustrator at Urban Design Associates, David Csont. I was invited to be a summer intern which then opened the doors to a two year internship. Since that time I have began my free lance career based in Pittsburgh, PA. Throughout my career I have gained valuable project experience creating concept level sketches, detailed presentation level illustrations, and everything in between to fit clients' needs. Project experience includes residential, commercial, industrial, mixed-use, transit-oriented, and many New Urbanist projects at a wide range of scales and styles throughout the United States, in the Middle East, Eastern Europe, the United Kingdom, Asia, and Central America.

Specialties

Watercolor, colored pencil, pencil, pen and ink, marker, digital, and hybrid illustrations.
