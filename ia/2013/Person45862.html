Frans Captijn's Summary

I believe and feel that this time needs a change of leadership and another employee ship. Oriental principles combined with western background provides a unique approach and contribution. Every person and every organization (as a team of people) is unique. They each have a unique set of talents and gifts to be able to fulfill their (life) mission. People yearn deeply for meaning, connection, fun and value in and from life. It inspires me to tell and to let people and organizations experience from living my ideal, how beautiful they are and where they actually all are capable of.

This leads to growth in development, performance, efficiency and satisfaction. I make an effort to do those things that I am good at with fun, to contribute for a better world. Integrity and authenticity are playing a major role in my life. My core qualities/strengths lie in my authenticity, sincere commitment and interest in the individual. Inspire, enthuse and motivate (challenge) people and positioning these people based on their qualities and (primal) talents.

I'm well over 30 active years active in the (top) management in the world of physical safety and crisis & disaster management. My volunteer mission, as chairman of the E.S.I.E.A Foundation, is to deploy personal competences from specialists, in a better and more specific way for humanitarian relief and assistance.

On first call, I am worldwide available as independent expert for the European Committee and VNG-International. Also for the International Red Cross Red Crescent as a FACT-team and ERU-Relief delegate.
I am the author of the (Dutch) book 'Speurtocht naar persoonlijk meesterschap' (On search for personal mastership) 'that appeared on June 29, 2010.

I like to share my extensive experience in the field of personal development, governmental and operational leadership, philosophy, acting, leadership development, talent management, change management, coaching, consulting and counseling with participants in Villa-Asia.

Specialties

Personal development and coaching, Crisis and disastermanagement, safety and security, general management, project management, operational management, humanitarian aid, international relations.
