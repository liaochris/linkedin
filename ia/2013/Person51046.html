Guy Farmer's Summary

I'm Guy Farmer and I'm a self-awareness guy, how to be happy guy, poet and joke writer who helps creative, open-minded people let go of the things that don't work and replace them with thoughts, emotions and behaviors that lead in a positive direction.

I'm an unconventional coach, workshop and retreat facilitator, and writer who believes in people's wonderful ability to change whenever they're ready and enjoy life instead of doing weird stuff. Visit me online at:
