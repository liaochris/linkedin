About Brandy and Vinca

Sometimes a chance encounter changes the course of your life. Such was the case for Brandy Mychals and Vinca Heart, born a world (and a wall) apart and destined to meet in a business training. Since then, these two successful entrepreneurs formed a partnership dedicated to inspiring confidence, attracting the right clients, creating high end sales and designing the life you have always dreamed of living.
The “Dynamic Duo”, as Brandy and Vinca are affectionately known, are passionate about initiating change and overcoming the odds in your business and personal life. They aren’t strangers to seemingly insurmountable challenges in their own lives and know exactly what it means to “achieve the impossible”.
Read more below about how Brandy overcame a head injury that left her unable to read for 7 years and Vinca grew up behind the Berlin Wall and then led the Underground Movement to bring it down.
Today, Brandy and Vinca are still leading movements and bringing down walls in the business, sales and entrepreneurial circles. Their virtual program “Confident: More of the Right Clients. Right Now.” combines their signature Character Code and Money Code Systems which has revolutionized how client attraction, sales, communication and marketing occurs in the business world.
Their extensive body of work became a Certified Coaching program (Client Coach) and Brandy’s Character Codes are being released by the prestigious McGraw-Hill Publishing House.
This “Dynamic Duo” are best known for their speaking, training and live events. Their down-to-earth “tell it how it is” style, incredible content, insightful innovation and huge hearts has moved thousands of clients worldwide.
Brandy and Vinca would be the first to tell you that if you can dream it, you can do it. When the naysayers discourage you, just use that as motivation to fuel you. Reach out to learn more about what it means to “inspire kick-ass confidence and 6 figure sales”. Yes, you can do it!
