Discovered in the early twentieth-century by Dr. Mikao Usui, Reiki is a Japanese relaxation and energy healing practice using a gentle touch (likened to a massage for the spirit) and is based on the idea that an unseen "life force energy" flows through us and is what causes us to be alive. The practice of Reiki has been proven to reduce stress, promote relaxation, increase energy and in many cases relieve pain.

Jonas also works with Akashic Records, a record of all that has happened since the beginning of time stored in the ether of the none physical plane of existence. Access to the Akashic field can be considered a sort of prayer or meditation. He uses the Records as a way to attune himself to a higher vibration to better serve his clients.

Counselor

Massachusetts Boys' and Girls' State

June 2001 – Present (12 years 4 months) United States

Since the founding of the American Legion Boys' State in 1935 and the American Legion Auxiliary Girls' State in 1937 the two programs have maintained a legacy of positively influencing the lives of young men and woman.

Every June juniors in high school live on a college campus for one week to learn about state and local government, and so much more. They participate in classes in law and economics, marketing and advertising, religion, media, military, philosophy, among others. They play sports, music and participate in debates. They are visited by local and national leaders such as Mayors, Auditors, Attorney Generals, Governors and U.S. Senators who come to speak with them. In short, the Boys' and Girls' State Program is a once on a lifetime experience.

Since 2001 I have volunteered my time with this very worthy program and consider it to be part of my most important work. Responsibilities include supervising and mentoring a group of up to thirty teenagers; directing instrumental and choral groups through rehearsals and performances; creating and executing lesson plans; maintaining a safe and welcoming environment; recruiting; and fundraising, among a myriad of other tasks.

Skills required include team work, patience, organization, time management and leadership.

Audiences will be treated to the legendary origins of playing cards, complete with genuine card readings, and demonstrations of the magic tricks that make them every magicians favorite tool. Set to be released in the Spring of 2013.
