Hemant Naidu's Summary

Hemant has been in the software industry for over 13 years where he has focused on enterprise application development. Whether it is leading a team of talented developers or getting in the trenches and writing code, Hemant utilizes Agile principles and eXtreme Programming methodologies to get the job done.

When he's not thinking software Hemant operates Naidu Photography, where he specializes in wedding and portrait photography. He strives to portray the beauty that EVERYONE possesses, especially when his clients don't feel it's there.

Hemant has a passion for his community, and is always looking for ways to influence social change. No matter how busy he is, Hemant is always eager to organize or join initiatives that promote equality, freedom, and civil rights.
