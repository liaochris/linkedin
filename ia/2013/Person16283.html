Brad Thomas' Summary

I've been a designer in some capacity since I was very young. I illustrated my first book when I was 4-years old. I drew on the insides of the brown paper shopping bags or on the back of my dad's gray bar paper reports he no longer needed.

Some kids go through the whole stage of trying to figure out what they want to do in life. I've always known that I'd be a designer in some capacity for a living.

It's just who I am.

From illustration to logo design, print or web design, I'm your guy. I think about it all day. I dream about it at night.

I've worked in positions where I've managed projects, managed people and managed expectations. I've led massive redesigns and reorganizations and made recommendations on vital pieces of software. I understand how to communicate and how to get my point across in a constructive manner. I also enjoy mentoring young designers and helping them find their way in the design world.

I'm fluent in HTML and CSS (LESS and SASS), and I understand how to use and write JavaScript. I keep up-to-date on the latest design and web trends through reading magazines and websites, as well as trial and error. I like pushing the envelope with my design, but I know when not to do that as well.

I am well versed in most everything Adobe, (Flash, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Premiere, InDesign and Illustrator for the most part) as well as most web content management systems. I'm familiar with PHP and .NET, as well as most web technologies that can be employed using those languages.

Specialties: graphic design, illustration, social media implementation, project management, web design, branding and identity design, content management systems (CMS), blogging etiquette
