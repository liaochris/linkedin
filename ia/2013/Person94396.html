Michael Mergenthaler's Summary

I’m a multi-disciplinary artist with many years of experience, who has worked with a wide variety of clients, including Coke, Nike, AB-Inbev, Wells Fargo and more. I have experience in package design, illustration, marketing, corporate branding/identity, publishing, web design, screenprinting, photography, pitch design, copy-writing, and much more.

In addition to my corporate design experience, I also have experience designing and screenprinting promotional posters for artists including, Snoop Dogg, Murder By Death, JD McPherson, Steel Panther, Ra-Ra-Riot, The Get Up Kids, Saves The Day, Streetlight Manifesto & more.

I also possess a strong background in music and journalism, having studied all three extensively at the college level. I have an intense passion for design and the creative arts in general. This is not a job for me. Creating is my life.

Currently, I'm open to consideration on just about any design opportunities. Please don't hesitate to contact me about any kind of project.
