Olaf Knechten's Summary

I live and work in London, UK and lived in Cologne, Germany for many years before moving to the United Kingdom. During my studies I spent long periods in France. I travel a lot for business, for leisure and to keep up with developments in the countries where my working languages are spoken. I have a keen interest in the arts, cooking and current affairs, nature, horticulture and the environment.

I translate from English and French into German. Other language combinations can be covered by translators within my network.

Translation by a native speaker of the target language, localization for the German market; I have extensive experience in the translation of websites (including SEO), print documents, subtitles and voice-over scripts.

I offer editing and proofreading services for translations and original copy. I can also deliver translations edited and proofread by experienced professionals.

I'll find snappy slogans and the right wording for your German advertising campaign and write informative texts in the language appropriate for your target audience.

I specialize in Literature as well as Marketing, Tourism, Entertainment, Linguistics and Current Affairs.

Tourism:
Brochures and websites for various international hotel chains and airlines.

Film & TV:
Subtitles for numerous feature films, TV series and documentaries.

Literature:
In March 2012 I started translating novels which I thoroughly enjoy. Overall feedback so far has been very positive.

Linguistics:
Collaboration on a dictionnary for learners of German as a foreign language.

Current Affairs:
Translation of newspaper articles for a major German broadsheet.
