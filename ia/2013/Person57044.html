Jake Beckman's Summary

Don't fire yourself!

My name is Jake Beckman, Chief Business Problem Solver at Art & Business Consulting. I work with Skilled independent professionals that are struggling with finances, tired of not understanding where all their money goes, confused about their taxes, and feeling embarrassed by their lack of knowledge about their business & personal finances. I work with you to teach you financial skills, and demystify the financial side of your business. I am the No BS Tax & Financial Literacy Expert because I restore your confidence in your ability to run your business, and achieve your financial goals & pursue dreams.

Bio
Tax expert, self-realized artist, renaissance woman and serial entrepreneur.

I am an Enrolled Agent (EA); EAs are America's tax specialists. I am a fellow of the National Tax Practice Institute, which is a high level distinction in the world or representing taxpayers before the IRS. I have been a small business specialist for more than 16 years. My practice has focused on small business services, taxation, accounting, bookkeeping, business planning & development, human resources and the other needs of micro/small companies and start-ups.

In addition to my business and financial services background, I also have been involved in technological research & development as well as pure science.

I have been an artist my entire life and my media has ranged from jewelry & sculpture to drawings & digital art. Currently I am an acrylic painter of magical realism and representational abstracts.

Specialties: Taxes & representation before the IRS, business planning & development, business start-ups, online marketing, social media, QuickBooks training, set-up & maintenance, web development, Arizona taxation, payroll taxes, human resource requirements for small businesses, science and technology, art, digital art, painting, digital image manipulation, and a partridge in a pear tree...
