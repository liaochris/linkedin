C. Enrique Ortiz's Summary

C. Enrique Ortiz (a.k.a. CEO) is an industry-recognized Mobile, Pervasive, Cloud & Telco technology leader & innovator. He brings passion to the equation, and the ultimate technical and operational balance across business, products and software development.

A hands-on Technologist, Advisor, Products-and Dev leader, and at times, coder, with a "roll-up your sleeves" attitude, he is co-author of a number of patents, author or co-author of dozens of articles and presentations, a couple of books on Mobile development, and a tech blogger.

He is organizer of various Austin high-tech groups & events including Mobile Monday Austin and Android Dev Austin. Founding member of the Austin Wireless Alliance. Founding-Trustee of the Austin Chapter of the Awesome Foundation. He is also co-organizer of the 2014 American Freestyle Open.

Enrique is a family man, and a mobile and space geek, whose favorite place is right by the Ocean. You can find him Frisbee jamming, at Zilker Park.

C. Enrique Ortiz's Patents

  • Mobile presentation system

    • United States Patent US20020147749
    Inventors: C. Enrique Ortiz

    A method can be used to generate information for a user. The method can determine the attribute(s) of the user's device and determine the appropriate grammar for the device. The method may access a grammar cache to determine if the appropriate grammar is in the grammar cache. If it is, the grammar can be sent to a servlet engine to generate the information in the appropriate presentation form for the user. If not, presentation information, business logic, a device profile, and a transformation rule can be used to generate the grammar that is sent to the servlet engine. An information handling system can be used to implement the method and send information to the user in a form that is more user friendly and compatible with the presentation component of the user's device.
