Kaitlyn Wells' Summary

"Great design is the product of great ideas" - Kaitlyn Wells

I am an enthusiastic graphic designer, taking pride in bringing a fresh and imaginative perspective to every project I take on. I strive to powerfully connect every message with it's audience helping your brand to reach its fullest potential.

Within the past two years I have worked with clients such as The U.S. Department of Defense, Discovery Channel, NASA Federal Credit Union, and The Portland Cement Association. I've produced award-winning work that has been featured on CNN, NBC, and CBS.

I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Arts and New Media with a concentration in Graphic Design from the State University of New York at Fredonia.

Specialties

I specialize in Print Design and Web / Interactive Design including, Brochures, Annual Reports, Marketing Materials, Newsletters, Posters, Business Papers, and Package Design; Identity Design including, Brand Identity, Graphic Identity Systems, Identity Use Guides, Logo Design, Stationery, and Symbol Development; Web & Mobile Site Design, Mobile App. Design, and User Interface Design.

I also have experience in Project Management, HTML / CSS Development, and Social Media for Businesses.
