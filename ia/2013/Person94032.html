Mike Carlucci's Summary

I am a writer and researcher of all things technology, planning, baseball, and copyright related. Currently I am a research associate working with municipalities in Eastern Massachusetts on economic, environmental, and developmental issues for PGC Associates.

I graduated from Providence College and Suffolk University, focusing on the impact of new media on politics and intellectual property, respectively.
