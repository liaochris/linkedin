Jenny Brock's Summary

I'm an accomplished copywriter with more than 20 years of experience in advertising, direct response and marketing communications. As principal of Brock's Copy Shop, I serve up communications and campaigns for clients throughout Ohio and the United States.

No matter how you take your copy - whether you're an advertising agency, design firm, small business or large organization - you'll benefit from using my professional copywriting services. At Brock's Copy Shop, the copy is always high-octane and satisfying. Full of flavor, and benefits too.

Want to meet up for copy? Contact me directly at jb@brockscopyshop.com

Specialties

I serve a rich blend of industries - from health care, beauty care, telecommunications, banking and insurance ... to restaurant, retail, real estate, technology and tourism. And there's no vehicle I can't handle - print ads, direct mail, websites, email blasts, brochures, collateral, radio, video scripts, outdoor, and much more.
