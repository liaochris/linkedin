Kimball Hill Homes

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Construction industry

May 2001 – September 2006 (5 years 5 months)

Served Kimball Hill Homes as their Director of IT. Homebuilding is not a strong technology sector, but by replacing many consultants with expert in house associates, we improved the internal departmental ranking from worst to best. The turnover rate for the department remained well below industry and national averages. During my tenure our team implemented a national Frame-Relay network and replaced that with a VOIP capable MPLS network. We also deployed a Sales Automation tool across the entire enterprise and helped the company double in size in 5 years. Other highlights include implementing a hand held construction scheduling solution across most of the company, moving to a dot-net architecture, and bringing in a revenue stream through a partnership with a major telecommunications company. My duties included representing KHH at an industry consortium, and with many outside vendors, and negotiating deals as well as managing the company technology budget.
