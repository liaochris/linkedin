Christine Smith's Summary

I own and operate the Motor Vessel David B in the San Juan Islands of Washington State, the Inside Passage of British Columbia, Canada and in Southeast Alaska.

Our cruises range from 3-12 days and we specialize in taking our passengers to remote locations where they can enjoy the peace and quiet of out-of-the-way harbors and coves.

Specialties

On board the David B I serve as the cook, naturalist, jr. engineer and deckhand. In the office I work as receptionist, passenger contact and bookkeeper. In the off season I work on boat maintenance and marketing.
