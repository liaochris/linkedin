Brandon "Juse One" Lewis' Summary

Juse One is a graffiti moniker that I have been using for nearly 2 decades. My primary work experience is in media production and visual arts. I hold a bachelor's degree in Media Studies from Hunter College, and I am a graduate student in the Film and Media Studies program at the University of Copenhagen. Currently, I freelance as an art director and multimedia artist, and event organizer for the Copenhagen BBoy BBQ, a free public hip hop event being held for the 3rd time this year. I have over 7 years experience as a freelance art instructor, and over 16 years experience as a graffiti artist.

Academic Work:

BA Media Studies - Hunter College
MA Media Studies - University of Copenhagen (in progress, Sept. 2013 - June 2015)

Creative Work:
Juse One has been my graffiti name for years, but it is also the name of my art, media and design company. My freelance work in Juse One Studios includes creating fine art for exhibition and private commissions, independently teaching street art and hip hop workshops, producing commercial mural productions and graphic design for digital and print media, as well as Wordpress website design.

I have just relaunched my website, http://juseone.com.

Specialties: Graffiti, portraiture, set painting, mural creation, aerosol art, digital art, graphic design, multimedia, basic web design, video editing, video post production, documentary video production, apparel design, curating, public speaking, teaching, event promotion

Owner, Curator

Gotham Gallery

October 2011 – April 2013 (1 year 7 months) Copenhagen, Denmark

Gotham Gallery was an urban art gallery established in 2011 in Copenhagen. It specialized in showing emerging and established graffiti and street artists as well as contemporary artists whose work engages urban themes and motifs. Gotham Gallery housed a 70+ sq meter exhbition and studio production space located in the heart of Nørrebro, Copenhagen. The gallery closed in April of 2013.

Art Director

StreetMekka

2010 – October 2012 (2 years) Copenhagen Area, Denmark

I have just been added to StreetMekka's team of staff as the art director. I am responsible for creating and maintaining StreetMekka's overall visual identity via their printed, audiovisual, web and social media. I am also the in-house artist and primary course instructor for StreetMekka's new street art education project called The Mekkanine School of Street Art. At Mekkanine, we are currently offering 3 unique arts courses:


Street Art 101 - stencil and printing techniques for outdoor art
Letter Science - design and production of graffiti art
Urban Film Crew - multimedia course with focus on DSLR video production

Brandon "Juse One" Lewis' Projects

    The BBoy BBQ is an international celebration of Hip Hop culture that unites the elements of dance, music, performance and visual art in free public block parties held annually in cities around the world.

    • November 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Brandon "Juse One" Lewis, Matthew "Blacc El" Thabane Muki, Kristian Mathiassen

    Mic Check is Denmark's Urban Talk Show - a grassroots production that serves as a forum for Danish urban art, music and culture. The show is hosted by hip hop journalist PTA, rapper Blacc El, with DJ KCL on the turntables. The show was established in 2012 and the concept was developed by Juse One, Kriswontwo, and PTA with support from Run For Cover.
