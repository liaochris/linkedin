Angela Bushman's Summary

Angela Bushman: Intuitive Empowerment Coach, Author, Speaker, and Artist at AngelaBushman.com

I enjoy inspiring others to step out of dysfunction, disconnection, depression, illness, disease, or lack of energy and step into a life of contentment, unconditional love, good health, joy, and abundance.

I help people to remember who they are and what they are here to learn. Consciously knowing this information assists people in understanding that they are creating the movie of their lives and puts them in the director's seat instead of remaining an unconscious actor.

I assist others in the process of stepping beyond fear and out of limiting beliefs and outdated programs and finding the courage to live a life that is true to a person's authentic self.

Through my own, brave journey of transformation, I have traveled the world, studied with healers, cultures, and systems of all types and have applied many systems of healing and growth to myself. From this journey, I have synergistically created methods of sharing the growth, healing, and expansion process with others.

I enjoy living a life of freedom, creativity, love, and abundance and teach others how to create the same through the sharing of my personal stories and examples.

I courageously share my journey of transformation in my first book, A Soul's Journey Home, now available worldwide on Amazon and BarnesandNoble.com.

I also offer an inspirational empowerment on-line audio course, as well as personal development workshops and lectures.

Painting has also become a way for me to create energy shifting tools to help others expand on so many levels. Check out my unique award winning inspirational abstract paintings, and my photography, on my website.

Please visit me at www.AngelaBushman.com  for more information on all that I can do for you.

Angela Bushman's Honors and Awards

  • Honorable Mention for "In the Flow" Inspirational Watercolor Painting

    Lawrence Seward, Juror of the 43rd Annual Aloha Show, Honolulu, Hawaii
    • March 2013

    Angela Bushman's inspirational abstract watercolor painting, "In the Flow," was given Honorable Mention at the 43rd Annual Aloha Show by Juror Lawrence Seward, Artist and University of Hawaii Art Department Lecturer. The inspirational piece by Angela was one of 119 pieces displayed by over 50 artists from March 5 to March 21, 2013 at Honolulu Hale. Angela's "In the Flow" painting is a wonderful representation of the bright color and free-flowing style of Angela's visual creations, that frequently evoke the response, "It just makes me happy," from viewers. Angela's passion and visual representation of freedom certainly shows through in this recent inspirational creation birthed while she was in the flow.
