Colleen Boisvert's Summary

Fitting the theme of big things coming out of small unexpected places, one of my most valuable and enlightening work experiences was obtained at a small weekly newspaper. While the paper derived from simple origins and was relatively small in size, it possessed a lot of soul and character. It also possessed a powerful brand message, which was it's devotion to the community. This theme was effectively conveyed through the paper's content, design, and overall tone. Within a short span of time, it became a hit with the community, with its charm and honesty. The paper covered stories that other local newspapers were missing, and it is this for this reason the small paper really endeared to the community it served.

Everything that I learned from this experience has carried over to the present day, as I continue to embark on professional endeavors. The job showed me how powerful clean, functional design can shape an individual’s experience with a brand.

Whenever I work with a new brand I carry that experience with me, and I will undergo the necessary research to gain a sound understanding of your brand and company and what makes it unique. For every project I devote myself to, I keep my sight on one main goal-to provide users an experience so memorable and engaging that they will be eager to return.
