Chad Luce's Summary

I became a chiropractor because of a very personal experience with chiropractic care as a young teenager. I was introduced to a chiropractor at 14 years old because I was experiencing back pain. While that, in itself, may not seem significant, it was the overall improvement of my health I achieved, above and beyond simply the relief from back pain, that truly impressed upon me the importance of chiropractic care. Not only did my back pain quickly disappear, but I never missed another day of school from sickness after continuing with chiropractic wellness maintenance. It dawned on me that chiropractic is a truly special form of health care.

Through all my focus and emphasis on chiropractic, health, nutrition, and lifestyle, I have had two other passions. The first passion has been with me since my childhood years: cars. I love cars and motorsports, particularly open wheel racing such as Formula 1 and IndyCar, and sports car racing such as American Le Mans and Grand Am Racing. I read car magazines and books voraciously. When I'm not talking chiropractic, I'm talking cars! My second passion, a more recent introduction into my life, is my family; my beautiful wife, Megan, and my absolutely gorgeous son, Ayrton, born in 2011. If you're a car nut, then you'll recognize the name "Ayrton..."

I bring my passion for everything health and wellness and family and cars each and every day, whether at work or play!

Our mission is to help as many families as possible reach their fullest health potential through the use of chiropractic and nutritional wellness care.

We understand people have many choices when it comes to deciding what type of healthcare to choose. At Luce Family Chiropractic, we recognize the need for individual attention and individual care. We empower our clients towards the healthiest lifestyles possible through our relentless education and leadership.

We practice what we preach, and we live by what was taught during Dr. Chad's tenure at Life University School of Chiropractic: to give for the sake of giving, to love for the sake of loving, and to serve for the sake of serving. By this guidance, we have been able to reach and help people of all ages in the Southwest Florida area, from newborn infants to active nonagenarians!

Our emphasis is family chiropractic wellness care, and we also specialize in the needs of athletes (spine and extremities), acute trauma care (i.e motor vehicle accident), and pain relief care. We believe in using chiropractic care not only for adult health, but also for pediatric development.

We use specific, gentle, and highly effective chiropractic care (Gonstead, Pettibon, and Diversified techniques), customized to the specific needs of the individual, to get the fastest results possible, then we emphasize long-term stabilization and maintenance through regular chiropractic care and nutritional guidance.

We encourage you to contact us with any questions you might have... we promise that you will feel at home in our office!

Associate Doctor

The Spine Center

October 2005 – November 2006 (1 year 2 months) Seffner, Florida

As an associate doctor at The Spine Center, under Dr. Scott Stoltz, I was responsible for processing new patients during their initial days of introduction to the clinic. Additionally, I was responsible for maintaining patient education, for training patients on rehabilitative exercises, and for administering chiropractic treatment. This was a large, family-oriented practice that emphasized involving the entire family in chiropractic wellness care.

My proudest achievement during my tenure here was organizing and conducting the national Kids Day America event in Seffner, Florida in 2006. This event drew over 300 participating kids, used 25 volunteers, and had 30 local and national companies and groups participate. Kids received fire and police safety training, free dental checkups, free spinal checkups, learned arts and crafts, and received goody bags full of donated items.
