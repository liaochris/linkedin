M. V. Walker's Summary

I am a Christian Speaker and Apologist as well as the Founder & President of Anchor Apologetics.

I have 8+ years of ministry experience and have previously served as the University Minister at First Euless in Euless, Texas and as the Youth Pastor at Real Life Fellowship in Corpus Christi, Texas.

Anchor's philosophy of ministry is missional in focus and encompases issues pertaining to Christianity, culture, worldviews, etc. Our practical emphasis works its way out in many ways. It ranges from live discussions within pubs to speaking/training events, from current posts/articles on the website to ebooks and other writing. To see our full philosophy of ministry and practical emphasis, please visit our website at www.AnchorApologetics.com.

Book Me to Speak, Teach or Train at your event by contacting me at mvwalker@anchorapologetics.com

Specialties: Christian Worldview, World Religions, Cultural Apologetics, and Spiritual Growth

M. V. Walker's Experience

Founder & President

Anchor Apologetics Ministries

September 2011 – Present (2 years) Fort Worth, Texas

Anchor Apologetics is a ministry aimed at 18 to 35 year-olds, yet open for anyone, who want to understand their and others' worldviews, and learn how to be a compassionate and thoughtful Christian voice within their culture.

We equip other followers of Christ with the tools to strengthen their faith as they are bombarded with difficult questions through multiple avenues, such as, media (video/audio), literature (hardcopy and ebooks), a strong website source, speaking & training events, public pub discussions over cultural issues, coffee house discussions over art and its influence, etc.
