Ariane Colenbrander's Summary

Ariane Colenbrander MGDC graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design from California State University, Northridge and continued her education with Design and Typography courses at Otis College of Art and Design in Los Angeles.

With work experience in the health and wellness, computer software and advertising industries, she founded Ahead of Steam Productions in San Francisco in 1990. Her talent was widely used in marketing campaigns for Sperling Sampson West, Levi's San Francisco, Vehicle SF, Fetzer Vineyards and the San Francisco and Amsterdam offices of Anderson + Lembke.

In 1997, she brought Ahead of Full Steam overseas to Western Europe, focusing on web concept and design for both mid-sized advertising agencies and Atos Origin, an international (Dutch/French) internet technology company.

Since 2005 she has been working in Vancouver, BC, as founder and owner of arianecdesign.com.

Her background and skills are extensive and cover a wide range of industries and work environments. She has worked in four creative markets to date, including Los Angeles, San Francisco, The Netherlands and Vancouver, BC.

Ariane is enrolled in Simon Fraser University's Certificate in Editing Program and offers copy editing and proofreading services in addition to creative direction, event coverage, and photography.

Specialties: Event Coverage, Photography, Writing, Design, Art Direction, Websites, Brand Identity, Typography, Strategic Branding, Brochures, Newsletters, Advertising, Marketing, Copy Editing, Proofreading, Blogging, Packaging, Direct Mail, Photography, Promotional Materials.
