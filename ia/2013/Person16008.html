Brad Barnett's Summary

My career started in investment banking in the late 80s. Since leaving that business and starting BFS in 1993, I have had the pleasure of building a national network of exceptionally focused, licensed professionals providing services to the executives and owners of day care centers, childcare centers, Montessori schools, private elementary schools, charter schools and latchkey programs. I and BFS have always focused on providing an investment banking level of service to companies that might not otherwise experience such treatment when buying, selling, financing or improving their own companies.
