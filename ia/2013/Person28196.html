Chandu Thota's Summary

Chandu Thota is an entrepreneur and an engineer. He has extensive software development experience in the Consumer, Enterprise and Web Services, Search and Information Retrieval areas. He is one of the most accomplished names in the web and local search and geo-spatial/mapping industries.

He is a Founder, CTO and VP of Product Development of The Dealmap where he was responsible for product strategy, development, building the engineering/executive teams and raising over $10 million in venture funding. In August of 2011 TheDealmap was acquired by Google where he is now leading Google Offers engineering efforts.

Previously he was at Microsoft Corporation as a Program Manager in the ASP .NET group working on the next generation web platform (Silverlight & Tools). Prior to that, Chandu Thota worked as a Software Development Lead for Microsoft Local Search and Mapping product: Virtual Earth now known as Bing Maps.

Chandu Thota has filed over 40 patents in the web and mapping and social technology areas. He is a published author (3 books) and a well known speaker in developer communities.
