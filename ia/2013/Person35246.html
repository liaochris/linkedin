Diane Esposito's Summary

If you would love to learn the art of creating healthy relationships and be inspired to “move to the heart of healing” with support, security and acceptance, Personal Harmony and Health will guide you through a step-by-step process for success. You will learn to live your best life with ease and grace even amidst challenges.

Diane Esposito is the founder of Personal Harmony and Health LLC and that's just what she helps you bring out in yourself. She has practiced the Healing Arts for nearly three decades and is a compassionate spiritual being having a human experience, perhaps just like you. She offers unconditional love and guidance to integrate the keys to stress release, relaxation, and rejuvenation.

As a certified Reflexologist, Usui Reiki Master, Angelspeake™ Facilitator and Holistic Coach, Diane shares the secrets to live your best life. She includes Aromatherapy as a complimentary option to personalize most in-person sessions. Diane’s degree in Communication Arts enhances her ability to share intuitive, transformational healing gifts with you.

You relax, rejuvenate, and create habits that heal with personalized, interactive, private or small group sessions. As the author of "Play, Heal, Love! The Art of creating Healthy Relationships" she guides you to become the master of your own Personal Harmony and Health.
