Daniel Weule's Summary

5D Consulting specializes where the elements of leadership, strategy and organizational systems intersect. If your needs require customized design of an approach to improve performance in any or all of these elements 5D can help. We have experience across many industries including Banking and Finance, Telecommunication, Mining, Heavy Manufacturing, Aviation, Not-for-Profit and state, Federal and Local Government.

5D can help you and your leaders formulate your strategy for success in your market while working with you to implement organisational change to enable you to execute against your strategic objectives.

Specialties

Strategic Development & Planning, Executive Team Development, Leadership Development, Individual & Team Profiling, Conference Design and Delivery, Change Management, Organizational Development

Daniel Weule's Projects

  • Global Innovation in HR - Rio Tinto

    • November 2010 to July 2011
    Team Members: Daniel Weule

    5D Consulting was engaged to lead the design and implementation of a global system for innovation development within the Rio Tinto HR community. Using a social media platform as the basis to connect practitioners around the planet, 5D engaged the support of innovators within the online community to develop and prototype new solutions to people related business issues. 5D designed and facilitated the global launch of the program in Brisbane, Perth, London, Paris, Johannesburg, Salt Lake City and Montreal. At transition over 1300 HR practitioners were connected through the social media platform and $9M worth of new value had been identified for consideration.

  • Global HR Leadership Forum - Rio Tinto

    • July 2010 to October 2010
    Team Members: Daniel Weule

    5D Consulting was engaged to design and deliver a HR Leadership Forum in Singapore for 140 HR executives from around the globe. The program was specially designed to focus on the challenge of supporting product groups with meeting their business objectives through identifying new and innovative solutions. Feedback from the participants was extremely positive about the program, delivery and the stimulus it provided.

  • National Strategy Development Dealer Network - Nissan Motor Company

    • November 2009 to July 2010
    Team Members: Daniel Weule

    5D was engaged by Nissan to design and deliver strategic development sessions for 210 dealerships around Australia. Sessions were design to be conducted in key locations around Australia in partnership with the Business Development teams within each state office. Sessions were purposefully designed to cater for business varying in experience levels and sophistication. Stronger alignment between corporate objectives and the network was achieved with significant gains in market share in some states.
