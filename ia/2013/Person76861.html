Kris Quinones, BA, LMT, YT's Summary

After pursuing a career in Western medicine for six years, I realized I didn't want to be a surgeon anymore. So I quit my job and began exploring other careers while earning an income in higher education. Eventually, I went back to school for massage therapy and have now been practicing massage and Ayurveda professionally since 2007.

As a practical and solution-based therapist, my treatment approach is interactive and goal-oriented. I use a combination of my knowledge in therapeutic massage and my previous experience in orthopedics, yoga, and competitive sports to assist you in reaching your goals. Whether they be pain management, an increased mind-body awareness, or professional consultation on how to incorporate a wellness program into your company.

By integrating my training in Western ideologies with Eastern philosophies, I offer a balanced approach - delivered with the highest standards of excellence in the profession. I live in Somerville, MA and am a former martial arts competitor in Tae Kwon Do (placing 4th in the 1999 Boston International Karate Championships for my division). Other hobbies include photography, music, world travel, Astanga yoga, and salsa dancing.
