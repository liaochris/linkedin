Jim Fitzpatrick's Publications

  • Advocating for Effective Regulation of the Tanzanian Cashew Sector

    • ANSAF
    • February 15, 2013
    Authors: Jim Fitzpatrick

    Resulting from an in depth study including over 100 interviews with a range of stakeholders ranging from farmers to senior industry professionals this study recommends policies to enhance the capability of the cashew nut sector in Tanzania and in particular improvements which will add more value for the small holders who grow the Tanzanian cashew crop.

  • Global Study on Cashew Nut Processing Equipment

    • Technoserve/ACi
    • March 1, 2011
    Authors: Jim Fitzpatrick

    A detailed assessment of the market in equipment and the options for processors of cashew nuts in procuring equipment. This study covers equipment from 34 countries including in depth studies from teams in Vietnam, India and Brazil. It also describes the cashew process as it is found in all the main processing countries.

  • Competitiveness in the African Cashew Sector

    • GIZ - African Cashew Initiative
    • March 1, 2011
    Authors: Jim Fitzpatrick

  • Prospects for Cambodia's Cashew nut sub Sector

    • International Finance Corporation
    • December 3, 2010
    Authors: Jim Fitzpatrick

    A study of the sector in Cambodia outlining a strategy for developing niche markets. Launch workshop widely reported in the media in Asia and the trade press.

  • Indonesia Cashew Sector Development Study

    • Smallholder Agribusiness Development Initiative Australia Indonesia Partnership
    • July 18, 2008
    Authors: Jim Fitzpatrick, Dr P.Jaeger
