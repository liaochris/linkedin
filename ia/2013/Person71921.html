Kan Wang, MBA, MSCSci's Projects

  • County of Riverside Enterprise Solutions for Property Taxation (CREST) Project

    • June 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Kan Wang, MBA, MSCSci

    Riverside County's current property tax system began in 1972. The system runs in a traditional mainframe computer environment based on an expansive hierarchical database. Numerous technological, operational, economic, and administrative business factors affect the County's use of this system. These factors mandate that the County replaces this aging property tax system.

    The CREST project is a joint effort between the Assessor-County Clerk-Recorder, Auditor-Controller, and Treasurer-Tax Collector offices to create a new integrated property tax management system with increased business functionality.
