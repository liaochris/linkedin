Chris "The Brain" Hoyt's Volunteer Experience & Causes

  • Volunteer Experience

    • Guiding Coalition and Big View

      The Julian Center
      • Poverty Alleviation
      October 2013 – present (3 months)

      Indianapolis is one of the most fiscally generous charity cities in the US. However, our remaining challenge is our cultural and sociological divide. In order for people to be empowered to work their way out of poverty, they need access to the communities and social networking resources we take for granted. Isolation, lack of positive relationships, and hopelessness are the true prisons of economic boundaries. No one succeeds alone.

      Because of this, The Julian Center is a champion of the Circles program which surrounds recovering individuals with the support and encouragement they needs. It is our hope to get more awareness and commitment from churches, businesses, and local organizations to participate in this effective program to overcome and eliminate poverty.

    • Community and Organizational Partnerships

      Horizon House
      • Poverty Alleviation
      October 2013 – present (3 months)

      Horizon House is an experienced and effective community resource for overcoming homelessness. They are fully equipped to provide homeless individuals the medical, psychological, and practical care they need during the day to find employment, stability, and residence.

      Our desire is to partner with churches, businesses, and local organizations to network the resources of community together to create opportunities and provide support for those coming out of homelessness.
