Luke Deady's Summary

Luke is a full-time freelance art director + designer — located in Columbus, Ohio. Backed with experience and the know how in the agency world and from Columbus College of Art & Design, where he graduated in 2009 with a BFA in Advertising and Graphic Design. His passion for branding shines through in every detail of his work on a day-to-day basis. He thrives on digging deeper into brands, figuring out the bigger picture of what needs to be communicated and successfully accomplishing it through design. Please email Luke with any inquiries or projects you may have. For professional freelance work samples please visit http://www.lukedeady.com
