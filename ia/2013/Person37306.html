Douglas Martin's Experience

Musician

Doug Martin Guitar

2000 – Present (13 years) Beijing, China

Currently based in Beijing, I am available for solo and group musical performances for small or large venues, corporate events, festivals, private parties and more. I am available to travel and I am a dual citizen of both the US and Italy, which means no additional work visa needed within the EU.

Stylistically my musical specialties are jazz and gypsy jazz guitar as well as Italian mandolin.

Additionally I am able to conduct business in both Mandarin, Chinese as well as English.

Aside from my projects in China and the US, I occasionally tour in the US and abroad as a member of the John Jorgenson Gypsy Jazz Quintet.

Among others, I have also performed with acclaimed gypsy guitarist, Lulo Reinhardt and his Latin Swing Project, the Hot Club of San Francisco, gypsy guitarist Mike Reinhardt and jazz luminaries Howard Alden and Mimi Fox.

www.dougmartinguitar.com
