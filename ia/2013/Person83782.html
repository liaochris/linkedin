Lynn Moore, Creator Inspirational Goaling's Summary

Have you been feeling unsettled lately?

The time in your life when you're realizing you’ve gone through the higher education process, got married, raised a family, had (or are nearing the end of) a good career but you are left with asking: Now what?

“What do I have to show for all my efforts? Surely this isn’t it?”
“And why do I feel like I shouldn’t be talking about this to anyone?”

Women I work with at this stage of their life often feel this way... they find themselves:
- Feeling bored …. and then feeling ashamed and guilty for feeling that way….

- Experiencing an emptiness that no amount of food or other “emotion-numbing” crutch can fill …

- Battling a deep, visceral level of loneliness …. and not getting any empathy or understanding from others….

Does this describe you too?

Firstly, you’ll be happy to know that you don’t have to struggle to reach a future and distant mountain top where someone or something may make you happy. That’s “goal setting” and its process is very flawed. However, there is a process where you will find all your answers …

It's called: Inspirational Goaling, an in-depth exploratory process I have created

One of the gifts that I was blessed with is a sense of intuition that can get you to the bottom line quickly and easily. Often my clients tell me how amazed they are at the things I instinctively know about them! Working with me, you will be guided to the discovery of your soul’s purpose and the realization it is the magnetic core keeping you grounded, centered and present at all times.

With me, you will:
Become Crystal Clear about where you are and where you are going
Discover more about yourself on a level you never knew existed
Learn the power and gift of personal responsibility
Finally discover your True Gift (Purpose) and Passion
Become empowered....

To help you move into the life you desire, you can copy/paste this link to apply for a no-cost conversation with me: http://www.inspirationalgoaling.com/application/
