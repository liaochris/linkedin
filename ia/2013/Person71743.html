Kaitlin Murray's Summary

I was very lucky to always have art in my life. When I was one year old my mother would sit me in a wooden chair, that my grandfather built, and let me scribble away. I am pretty sure I scribbled more on the chair than the paper, but I was thinking outside the box! My early interests in art were heightened, at age seven, when I attended a clay and drawing class at the DeCordova Museum. You might say that was when the spark was ignited. After my first Graphic Design class in High School I was hooked and never looked back.

I am a Graphic Designer who specializes in print and web design. Currently, I work for Backupify as a Graphic Designer. I also do freelance work on the side.

When I am not designing you can find me going to concerts, doing yoga, and taking pictures with my new DSLR camera.

Specialties: Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Bridge), Quark, Content Management System, Expert on Mac and PC Platforms, and Working Knowledge of Flash and Dreamweaver. In the process of learning HTML and CSS
