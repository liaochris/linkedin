    Awakened Leadership:Beyond Self-Mastery

    Your Story is My Story

    Mine begins in the small town of Corona, where I began to suspect that a first glance doesn’t reveal the whole picture. Living in California during the sixties and having a doting grandfather who told me stories about Socrates were part of my early wonder and curiosity about things unknown. A bite of the seeker’s bug quite early in life eventually led me to travels in Peru, India, and many other parts of the world. My journeys, however, were not only to different locations; they also included a career in the corporate financial world, advanced study in academia, years in the ashrams of India, and the convoluted, never-ending drama of relationships, money, and just plain living.

    Awakened Leadership has been written for you.

    My invitation to you is to imagine a reality in which you arise as the highest form of awareness no matter where you are in any given moment, and no matter what you are doing. No longer will it be necessary to seek out diagrams and models that are a distant reflection of another’s journey. You will be effortlessly reflected into every situation in a way that only you can be. Much like the gurus and sages who look to their own experience and report out to those that surround them, you too can expand into that perfect space, and become yourself the invitation to others to join you there. That is the promise of this book and the possibility contained within. Awakened Leadership is my heartfelt gift pointing to that outcome.

    You are already what you are seeking.

Alan E. Shelton's Summary

Great leadership requires that the leader be self aware, open to change and developmentally oriented creating a momentum that draws the team into great leadership as well. You cannot pretend that the attention to the nuts and bolts of fundamental business only; and the elimination of leadership expansion for budget concerns equates to inspired leadership.

Source Consulting offers you a process to quickly and successfully move yourself and your team into greater leadership and higher performance. We start with you manifesting a new way of seeing yourself, your creative leadership strengths and reactive challenges. You are where you are today because of the structure you have created. Now, these structures may no longer serve you. Our process establishes the transformation of structure such that a more expanded leadership style can emerge. The personal awareness work you engage automatically models a leadership style that inspires your managers to reflect on their leadership. We show you how to create from within establishing the center from which all creative leadership characteristics moves out. We show you a way to achieve what you want through clarity in your leadership.
