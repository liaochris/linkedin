Linda Knijnenburg's Summary

In my 15 years of experience in the ICT and Internet business, I’ve become a firm believer in the saying that technology is about people. Yes, there’s bits and bites and stuff. But in the end – or I should say in the beginning – it is about our need to be able to do something faster, more efficient or more fun. And it’s from these needs or latent needs that business opportunities arise to create a technology product of value.

As a Business Analyst, Information Analyst, User Experience Expert – I’ve worn many hats - I see it as my quest to form a bridge between the business vision and requirements, the end-user needs and technical possibilities. I’m curious by nature, and I just love to explore and understand complex business domains and to apply this knowledge to help create or improve applications or websites. I have the most fun when doing so together with people, who are each able to contribute their own expertise.

Specialties:
- Business process analysis
- Requirements analysis
- Functional specifications
- User Experience
- End-user profiling
- Interaction design
- Salesforce CRM
