Robert Bowling's Summary

President of the independent video game development studio Robotoki.

With nearly a decade’s experience managing the multi-billion dollar entertainment brand of Call of Duty, Robert has straddled the creative and business sides of the entertainment industry while building creative IPs from concept to over $4 billion dollars in global sales.

Robert's experience benefits from building not just original IPs but also several companies from the start-up phase to established studios responsible for billions in global sales and crafting $40 million dollar+ partnerships with major brands such as Pepsi, Co., Doritos, and JEEP.

However, business is merely a means to allow for the creative freedom for Mr. Bowling to concept and execute on original IPs and innovative new proprietary technology in which to build them.

Specialties: Creative direction, strategic partnerships, product development, launch strategy.
