Ted Doraisamy's Summary

What my clients say I do best is stretch their thinking and harness available knowledge. The facilitative processes I use with management teams encourages collaborative learning and results in the development of the very best and often innovative strategies to address issues or opportunities faced - in the pursuit of strategic goals. This always inspires the team’s whole-hearted commitment to the plan – so crucial to delivering success.

Specialties: Senior Team Facilitation, Strategic Thinking, Business Strategy, Marketing, Executive Coaching & Mentoring
