Andrew Kirke's Summary

Andrew is an associate in the Contract and Technology team at Tughans Solicitors in Belfast, and works with the rest of the team in advising on all aspects of Intellectual Property and IT law, as well as providing assistance to clients in a full range of commercial contract and procurement matters. Tughans is one of Northern Ireland's largest commercial law firms, and as a full service law firm provides corporate legal advice throughout the UK.

Andrew joined Tughans in July 2013, having completed his training contract with another leading Belfast firm, where he gained a year's experience working within the Corporate & Commercial Department. During this time he also gained a Post Graduate Diploma from the Institute of Professional Legal Studies, where he was awarded the Carson McDowell Prize for Legal Drafting.

After qualifying Andrew worked as a Corporate Finance lawyer in the Jersey head office of Appleby, the world's largest provider of offshore legal, fiduciary and administration services, before deciding to return to Northern Ireland.

Andrew was admitted to the roll of solicitors of the Northern Ireland Law Society in September 2012, and the law societies of the Republic of Ireland and England and Wales in January 2013.
