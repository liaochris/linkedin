Debbie Bowie's Experience

Speaker

NSA Virginia

2007 – Present (6 years) United States

Debbie Bowie's speaking goal is to provide participants with new information that has the power to change their lives very quickly. In her speeches she combines the practical principles of professional organizing with the timeless wisdom of Feng Shui. Because she weaves into her speeches practical and easy to apply information, participants often remark, "I can't wait to get back to my office to get started clearing my clutter!" It's common for participants to report back to Debbie that her speech got them started, helped them realize their organizing goals, and changed the way they view their stuff and their lives.

Owner, Feng Shui Practitioner, & Certified Professional Organizer

Simply Organized, Inc.

1998 – 2008 (10 years) Richmond, Virginia Area

Debbie Bowie is a professional speaker, a Certified Professional Organizer®, feng shui practitioner and the owner of the Rock Scissors Paper Institute, formerly Simply Organized, Inc. She established her company in 1998 and to help people clear a path to getting more of what they really want from life by clearing clutter and setting up their homes and offices for utter comfort and success.

Debbie has a B.A. and M.A. in Art History and a M.S. in counseling. She is the author of Rock Scissors Paper: Understanding How Environment Affects Your Performance on a Daily Basis.
