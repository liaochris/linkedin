I believe I am the business woman I am today because of one teacher Mrs. Jacinta Cox (Tullamore). Not because I was an excellent student in fact I struggled through high school. My mother was illiterate until I was 12. My father went to work at the age of 12 as both parents died when he was a young boy. Dad taught himself to read and write at the public library.

I believe teachers have a huge influence over the life of a child. They can encourage a child to find their inner greatness while teaching the assigned subject or just be a regular teacher and teach the subject at hand. Mrs. Jacinta Cox was one of those extraordinary teachers. She was my high school business organization and social studies teacher. When she walked into a room the room lit up, her students suddenly became energized, attentive and willing to open their minds to the possibility that one day they could be famous and run their own business. She ignited within me the spark to dream big and achieve a lot.
