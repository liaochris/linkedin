Melissa Osika's Summary

I am a broker associate with 21 years of real estate experience in Northwest Indiana. I am a life long resident of Porter County and am actively involved in my community.

95% of my business in 2012 was repeat or referral business from my sphere. I closed on 37 homes in 2012. I look forward to providing you, your family or friends, with the extraordinary level of customer service that I am known for. I am never too busy for your referrals!

I am a member of the following organizations:
Valparaiso Chamber of Commerce;
Director with Friends Assisting Women in Need, Inc.;
Past Vice-Chair, Housing Opportunities Board of Directors;
Past chair of the Ivy Tech's Capital Campaign, Community Division;
Chair, FAWN/Housing Opportunities 2012 & 2013 Dinner and Style Show committee;
President, Referral Organization of Indiana Network, Inc.-Valparaiso chapter.

In addition, I am a certified Mentor for the Brian Buffini 100 Days to Greatness and Peak Performers program.

Specialties

First time homebuyers, residential resale, land development and new construction in Lake, Porter and LaPorte counties in Northwest Indiana.
