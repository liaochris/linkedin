Jonathan Khorsandi's Summary

Strategy
There are many definitions and concepts of strategy and what I have learned and implemented over the years comes down to a very basic analogy: the strategy is the road map with major milestones defined to help you get from here to there. A good strategy helps any individual entrepreneur or any sized business realistically assess what “here” is and what exactly “there” looks like.
I help any business or entrepreneur define and refine the strategy that will realize the vision and goals of the stakeholders.

Advisor
I help leaders lead. I have been told by clients over the years that they immediately trust, confide in and connect with me. As a result, leaders don’t feel “so lonely” at the top and through ongoing advisory, coaching and consulting I help them steer clearly through difficult decisions with confidence. Whether it is the small mom and pop business in the neighborhood, the $100M annual revenue enterprise or the non-profit looking to bring about world peace, each leader confided in me the unique fears, doubts and challenges related to their business.

I oftentimes like to get involved with idea’s that I think are promising and make a difference. Most recently, I helped co-found and co-create the following entities:
