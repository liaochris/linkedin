Bill Geist's Summary

• Consultant to the Destination Marketing industry. Worked with over 170 Convention & Visitor Bureaus and State Tourism Offices since founding Zeitgeist in 1995.

• Author of the seminal book for DMO/CVB Boards, "Destination Leadership for Boards." Contributor to "Fundamentals of Destination Marketing" and "Tips from the T-List."

• Host of the innovative distance learning series DMOU.com (Destination Marketing Organization University.

• Popular speaker on consumer and travel trends, marketing, customer service and the internet.

• Intimately involved in several Statewide legislative initiatives and the public referendum battle to build the Frank Lloyd Wright-designed Monona Terrace Convention Center in Madison, WI

Specialties: Marketing, Political Advocacy, Board Governance, Organizational Management, Destination Assessments, Professional Speaker, Tourism, Technology, Internet Marketing, Trends, Tactics
