Floral Ordering, Prepping and Conditioning, Basic Floral Design, Floral Design Special Occasions, Floral Design Sympathy, Floral Design Weddings, Proms, Formals and Special Events

The original aim with going to floral school was so we - my birth Mom Caroline and I - could work at my cousin Sherry's then floral shop, Floral Tributes, in Solvay. 2nd and 3rd generation from a family of florists, gardeners and landscapers, we had the background knowledge and know-how, but lacked the schooling certifications. During our first floral design course, while putting together our personal Everson Museum's Fine Art and Flowers floral project - a floral fountain we called, "Fountain Of Youth" we had on display at the Everson Museum Spring 2005, the floral design instructor, Colleen Teachout, said that we had "the gift" and encouraged us to create our own floral business. Cousin Sherry cheered us on to go for it. Shortly after graduation, The Gardens Of Venus Florist was born
