Elliott Randall's Summary

I enjoy great challenges - being creative, solving problems, and 'the human interaction' - in all areas musical, visual, spatial, and technological.

I have broad-based experience and expertise in the entertainment, television, musical instrument and electronic technology sectors, and enjoy the synergy of all the above media. Bleeding edge technology is what I bring to the table!

Specialties

Composer, Music Producer (records, commercials and industrials), Multimedia Producer, Studio Musician, Recording Studio Design & Development, Web Design / Internet Consultation, Digital Recording Specialist / Consultant, Musical Instrument & Amplifier Design & Development, Software Design, Technical Writing & Education.

Elliott Randall's Experience

CEO

ELZ Music & Multimedia

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Music industry

1990 – Present (23 years) London, United Kingdom / Global

ELZ Music & Multimedia provide creative services including recording, music composition & production, multimedia presentation, recording studio design and development, website design, photography, graphic design, art direction and Internet consultation.

Since the advent of midi in the early 80s, Elliott has worked as independent consultant for a wide range of companies, including Akai, Roland, Korg, Yamaha and Electro-Harmonix, in the areas of musical instrument and amplifier development, recording & sampling technology, software design, and education.

ELZ Music & Multimedia also offers a wide range of inspirational educational packages, including lectures, masterclasses, consultations, workshops and seminars for both public & private sectors.

Consultant / Advisor

NuMuBu, Inc.

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Music industry

March 2011 – March 2013 (2 years 1 month) Montreal, Canada / Global

NuMuBu.com THE music industry network that brings together members from all aspects of the music industry in an industry-specific networking site.

NuMuBu offers a range of tools that enables users to develop comprehensive sales, marketing and networking tools to emulate the activities of conventional record & distribution labels from production and collaboration to research, marketing, distribution, promotion and sales, live video streaming and so much more. NuMuBu gives you the power to create your own micro business platform.

CEO

Elliott Randall Music, Inc.

1973 – 2002 (29 years) NYC / LA / Global

Recording services - world-renown studio musician, owner of three recording studios in New York City. Work included composing, producing and playing on myriad records, commercials, and industrials.

Commercial clients include: Coca-Cola, PepsiCo, Miller Beer, Budweiser, Cadillac, Ford, McDonalds, Burger King, Wendy's, CitiBank, General Mills, Nabisco, Proctor & Gamble, MTV, ESPN, CBS, ABC, BBC-TV.

As a musician, recorded and performed with artists as diverse as Steely Dan, The Doobie Brothers, Carly Simon, Seatrain, The Blues Brothers, Carl Wilson, Asia, Michael Kamen, Peter Wolf, Peter Frampton, James Galway, Richie Havens, The Rochester Philharmonic and The American Symphony Orchestra, among many others. See http://elliott-randall.com for more.

Consultant

Motorola

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; MMI; Consumer Electronics industry

1998 – 2001 (3 years)

Principal remit was to unify Motorola's web presence. Designed sections of Motorola's Internet, intranets; served as consultant on webdev body. Authored numerous hi-tech training manuals (hard copy and digital) for client-server apps.

Consultant

Res Rocket

1993 – 1997 (4 years) London UK / Global

Began consulting in 'beta'-days of ResRocket / Rocket Network - an interactive multi-client midi-Internet music application,. We were the first to appear on the 'net, and arguably the best. The company was able to transition from midi to full-frequency audio, & was ultimately acquired by AVID / DigiDesign.

Music Consultant

NBC

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; GE; Broadcast Media industry

1976 – 1981 (5 years)

Saturday Night Live - the seminal years! Worked with actors and comedy writers developing tele-plays with John Belushi, Dan Ackroyd, Bill Murray, Gilda Radner, et al. Also guest appearances with the Saturday Night Live Band, as well as accompanying guest artists including Judy Collins, Boz Scaggs, Kate Bush, others...

Freelance Musician and Entertainer

large

1962 – 1972 (10 years)

The formative years - 'learning the ropes' playing in nightclubs, at major social events (i.e. Peter Duchin Band et al), Broadway Theater... then breaking into the recording industry as a sideman, vocalist, bandleader, arranger, contractor. A good beginning.
