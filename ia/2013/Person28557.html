Cynthia L. Bischoff's Summary

My passion and purpose is to help individuals achieve greater life balance and meaning in their lives!

In 1998, after enjoying a successful 20+ year career in academia as a business communications coach, lecturer and published author, I began to design and lead my own programs based on heart-centered consciousness, equipping men and women with strategies critical for deep introspection and healing.

Trained in numerous energetic and therapeutic modalities, I have successfully facilitated the transformation of individuals, couples and teams in the private sector, Corporate America, Europe and Asia.
