Marshall Brown's Summary

I am all about helping people get clear, get focused and get ahead!
My personal brand attributes include: intuitive, connector, outgoing, sincere, honest and results oriented.

I am an executive and career coach with a passion for encouraging individuals and organizations to reach exceptional levels of performance. I am founder and CEO of Marshall Brown & Associates, an international coaching, training and leadership development company. My mantra is simple: Get clear, get focused, get ahead.

I am known for producing results, a critical strength in today’s world of work. Many of my clients have raved about my ability to help them to get unstuck and move forward. A recent client who landed a new association CEO position after working with me had this to say: "Marshall Brown was my coach, supporter and cheerleader through my recent search. He made me work. He made me examine what I truly wanted for the next success. He challenged the way I presented myself in cover letters and in interviews. Working with Marshall was absolutely the right move. If you want to be authentic, confident and a leading candidate in your next search, Marshall will get you there."

In addition, I have the ability to build strong teams and equip them to make a difference in the workplace. I provide employees with the training and development that improves their ability to understand their roles, to work more effectively together and show how they can best support their organization’s growth. I regularly share my expertise on a global scale in countries such as, South Africa, Ethiopia, Thailand, Kenya and Latin America.

As a personal brand strategist, I am committed to helping people identify their unique promise of value. I encourages people to discover and use their true talents and passions in life to achieve their goals and stand apart from their competition.
