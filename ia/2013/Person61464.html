Jeff Witters' Summary

Jeff is a creative director, designer, author, avid motorcyclist and pilot currently splitting time between Los Angeles and Washington DC.

Currently, I specialize in interface design (UI) and user experience (UX). Typically I work with funded startup consumer facing tech companies. I have also built enterprise level database administration applications for some of the largest gaming companies.

I've been fortunate to work with a wide breadth of clients over the past years. Some of those clients include: Fox, Fox Searchlight, Miramax, Universal, Saatchi and Saatchi, Warner Brothers, Lionsgate, FX Networks, Deutsch, MGM, Microsoft, eBay and many other smaller production studios.

I have been featured in various publications, from Web Designer Magazine to Web Designers Idea Book vol. 1. I have also been nominated various times for Key Art Awards, and Webby's and have also served on the judging committee.

Specialties

User Interface, User Experience, Interactive Design, Creative Direction

Jeff Witters' Projects

  • Roots x Douglas Coupland

    • October 2010 to November 2010
    Team Members: Jeff Witters

    We created this Webby nominated site in conjunction with Farstar Agency located in Frisco, Texas. The site was developed for the collaboration between iconic Canadian clothing brand Roots and Douglas Coupland. We wanted the site to have a connection between the clothing that was created by Douglas Coupland and the application of the designs and look to the Roots brand clothing. The site features calls to several social networks including the Roots YouTube Channel, Douglas Coupland personal twitter account and several other sites. We also included full size downloadable one sheets for visitors of the site as a takeaway.

  • InkMe

    • December 2011 to June 2012
    Team Members: Jeff Witters

    Inkme.com lets tattoo fans connect with tattoo artists. Artists can set up free profiles, upload galleries of their work, schedule new clients and sell their very own artwork online. Fans can interact, schedule appointments and connect to their favorite artists. Your one stop for finding booking, following and interacting with the top tattoo talent.

    Cartisien also was tasked with defining a creative style for the content of the site as well. We helped InkMe produce several videos that showcased the clientele of high end tattooing, the artist themselves and also periphery examples of clientele such as musicians actors and any other aficionados of the art.
