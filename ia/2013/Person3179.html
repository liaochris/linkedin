Alan Murrells - Project Manager (Taybray Ltd)'s Summary

Taybray Ltd provides low cost, high value temporary staff to businesses around the Essex and Greater London area - from school leavers/recent graduates through budding architects, music producers, reporters, environmentalists, etc. up to experienced project managers, business analysts and test managers. I have placed 9 of these people in the last year and my customers keep asking for more. These people learn fast and deliver with a "can do" attitude. These people are all known to and hand-picked by me or trusted colleagues and friends - not your normal agency staff then.

I am also available personally as a business analyst/project manager with strong communication & interpersonal skills, a business background in insurance and banking, and a technical background in programming & networking. I build teams from different organisations and countries with different cultures and languages. My projects have covered insurance, banking, accounting, automotive, retail & shipbuilding as well as the public & charity sectors. I have worked in much of Europe and the Far & Middle East. I have managed stakeholders up to chief executive level.

Specialties

BUSINESS EXPERIENCE - banking and insurance; IT EXPERIENCE - banking, insurance, automotive, retail, shipbuilding & public/charity sectors; Prince2/MSP practitioner; TECHNOLOGIES - Office/Project, C++, Websphere, XML, Notes, Siebel, SQL, Sybase, COBOL, DB2, BASIC, LAN, WAN, firewall, VOIP; Methods - Agile, SDM, PMI, 6Sigma; LANGUAGES - Italian, French, German and some Spanish
