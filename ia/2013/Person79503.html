Leah Bradbury's Summary

Over the course of 13 years, Leah Bradbury has developed into one of the industry’s most accomplished performance-based affiliate marketers. Leah has always had an entrepreneurial spirit. She graduated early from USC in 2000 while working 2 jobs to pay tuition and expenses. After graduating, she started her business development career in sales, where it was quickly realized that she had a talent for understanding the sales process and consistently exceeding her quotas and goals. Shortly after the dot-com boom, Leah got her feet wet in online advertising while working for Cox Interactive Media (a division of Cox Enterprises) and quickly became recognized as a top sales executive. Her successful experience at Cox served as a launch pad and enabled Leah to help several other companies design and implement their own affiliate programs. With her experience in developing these programs, Leah correctly identified that that the industry lacked an affiliate solution based on integrity and ethical business practices. Eager to fill that void, she borrowed $500 and launched Plasmid Media from out of her home. A short two years later, Plasmid Media was generating annual revenue in excess of $5 million dollars and was recognized as a top network within the affiliate and online marketing industry. Since her business launched over 10 years ago, Leah has helped hundreds of advertisers and generated over millions of consumer actions for her clients. She enjoys sharing her wealth of experience by helping companies develop and implement successful online advertising programs. When Leah isn’t working, she enjoys spending time with her husband and their two children in Idaho. She also enjoys yoga, cooking, skiing, hiking and tennis.
