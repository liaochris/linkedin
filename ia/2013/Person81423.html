Lisa Bhella's Summary

Served on the National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO) Phoenix Chapter Board of Directors as Social Media Director from 2011-2012 and as Secretary from 2008-2010. Also served as Newsletter Editor on the Board of the Entrepreneurial Mother's Association from 2008 - 2009.

Has a passion for helping businesses create their unique voice on social media with results always in mind. Enjoys delivering social media presentations and workshops to prospects, clients and organizations.

Teaching experience in England includes high school and adult education in the areas of IT and advanced business. Prior to teaching, worked on the trading floor of Barclays Bank, in London.

Work experience includes international finance, education and business owner.

Specialties

Creating results orientated social media strategies, email marketing, article writing and blogging for businesses. Coaches businesses on how to effectively set-up and implement their social media marketing strategy. Also providing content and maintenance of social media accounts.
