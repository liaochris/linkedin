March 2007 – Present (6 years 7 months) Topeka, Kansas

Brad Johnson, Vice President of Marketing for Advisors Excel, works by referral only and mentors a small group of the country’s most elite financial advisors. By leveraging the ideas of their most successful peers, advisors in this group grew their production by an average of over 36% over the past year – this during a time in which most producers have seen declining marketing results. Advisors in this mastermind group, consisting of the nation’s top financial professionals, count on Brad and his team to help enhance their businesses and drive their profits upward.

When Brad isn’t helping the best in the business get even better, you can find him enjoying time with his family at their home in rural Silver Lake, Kansas. Brad is married to his high school sweetheart, Sarah, who truly has her work cut out for her as she spends her days at home with the couple’s two young boys, Braun and Nash. Sarah also stays busy coaching high school volleyball and recently graduated with her Master’s Degree in Education which she plans to utilize when she returns to teaching. Brad, a former All-American football player, fills his free time staying physically active and recently became Level 1 certified to instruct CrossFit. He also finished his first Tough Mudder race late last year and plans to complete another in late 2013.

Advisors Excel is an independently owned, world-class insurance marketing organization focused on helping independent advisors increase their life insurance and annuity business. Their mission is simple and straightforward – they never forget that advisors are their clients. Advisors Excel is not designed to work with everyone so they don’t mass recruit and work exclusively with top producers. This allows for a personal focus on their clients and an allocation of the company's resources and support to drive maximum results. Each advisor receives proven prospecting systems, exclusive product offerings, access to advisor-led mentoring, and unmatched incentives.
