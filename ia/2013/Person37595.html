Dr. Bobbie Bennett's Summary

My work in the chiropractic field centers around optimal health. My goal is to educate as many individuals about the possibilities they have in maintaining and attaining optimal health. I work with patients of all ages, and hope to make those around me healthier. Available for industry ergonomic and education presentations.

Specialties: Additional training in prenatal, perinatal, and postnatal care for women, pediatrics, sports injury, and rehabilitation. Full body certified in Active Release Technique. Level III certified Medical Fascial Stretch Specialist. NASM-Certified Personal Trainer, teaching functional training principles to patients interested in maintaining the benefits they attain from regular chiropractic and stretch therapy care.
