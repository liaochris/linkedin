Eric Olson's Summary

I add value to the world through art.

As an artist, I see the world through a uniquely personal lens. Although trained originally as a painter, I now prefer include camera, computer and printer. My work is an ever-evolving flow of ideas that reflect my personality and world view. Many people note my work for its variety, composition and color. Others refer to them as paintings, although they are made with digital tools.

My artworks and photos are in the collections of private collectors, corporations, museums and nonprofit organizations. My work is exhibited and sold through art galleries, art consultants, and various affordable online sites.

In “Transformations,” my most recent work, original photographs are converted into highly abstracted new artworks that liberate and expand color, energy and feeling. These exciting new artworks give viewers a renewed sense of energy and place, even feelings of pure sensation. See: http://bit.ly/epgallery

Nancy and I enjoy talking about art, working with people, and traveling. We're also interested in the therapeutic art movement, and work to place beautiful art in hospitals and clinics to enhance the healing process.

Specialties: Fine digital art, site-specific large scale art for large spaces such as airports, cruise ships, building lobbies, exhibition venues and other spaces that require monumental abstract art.
