Craig Boshier's Summary

Craig focuses on applying human and behavioural factors to product, service and experience design. A key part of his work is also helping companies to design and develop their innovation management practices.

Before joining S&C, Craig performed a lead role in his firm's online services consulting team, researching, designing and delivering a patented software-as-a-service offer. Prior to this, Craig worked in a number of consulting roles with a wide range of telecoms, banking, financial services, media and public sector clients, in the UK, Australia and New Zealand.

He holds a BSc (Hons) Psychology, a MSc (Dist) Occupational Psychology, and a Graduate Certificate in Change Management from the Australian Graduate School of Management (AGSM).
