David L. Reed's Summary

I’m building a group for real estate professionals to me meet virtually on a monthly basis with each other and with me. The benefits will be to grow revenue, share best practices, and recognize emerging trends. I will lead the group and hold monthly meetings with all of you together and individually to help you achieve these goals.

Focus, listening, establishing priorities, positive self expectancy and intensity of will has brought me a great deal of success over the 30 years of my career. As a successful business owner, I now focus on helping other business owners and leaders draw out the wisdom within and the power of peer advisory groups to make the best possible decisions, and to be better leaders.

Today, I surround myself with life long learners, business executives who are focused on driving results and developing a business and a culture that will survive long after they are gone.

I have developed extensive experience in management and sales, and business development. I enjoy the challenge of growing revenue, being fully accountable for the P&L of a business, managing expenses to revenue while moving forward the long term objectives of the business.

In 2010, I began Reed Advisory Services and affiliated with Vistage International, the leading CEO Organization in the World. Through the Executive Advisory Group model, I help Business owners and leaders who are committed to challenging one another and excited by taking their companies to a higher level.

Specialties: My skills of business management, developing commercial real estate strategies, sales training, business development, and property management have proven to be of tremendous value to my clients.
