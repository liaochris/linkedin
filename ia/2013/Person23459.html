Chris Kossifos' Summary

Mr. Kossifos is a seasoned Information Technology Executive having worked in the private sector as both technical lead and principal executive for numerous high technology firms. He is also an investor in a variety of technology businesses and start-up ventures.

Mr. Kossifos has been at the forefront of developing technologies since the early 2000s, with a focus on network applications and services in the Hospitality and Construction verticals.

Paramount to all of his undertakings, commercial or altruistic, is the philosophy that technology betters economies, quality of life and the spirit of any modern society.

Chris Kossifos' Experience

Co-Founder and Chief Technology Officer

Wanderport Africa, LLC

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

October 2012 – Present (8 months) New York, NY

Wanderport Africa delivers cutting-edge, wireless communication services to under-developed countries throughout Africa. Through the use of low-frequency bands that are relatively unused at current, we are able to deliver an end-to-end, enterprise-grade communication infrastructure without the use of wires for "last-mile" connectivity. Our systems and processes are highly redundant, scalable and easily deployed.

Through the use of our technologies, we envision a connected Africa ready to face the business demands of a modern world. Paramount to this undertaking, is the delivery of internet to schools and public institutions for the greater good of the masses. A better educated Africa is a more prosperous one.

Managing Director

CK Technology Group, LLC

November 2005 – Present (7 years 7 months) New York, NY

CK Technology Group is a world-class provider of information technology driven solutions and services. Our clients include Fortune 500 firms, the real estate development and construction industries, hospitality, medical and law firms throughout New York City. In addition, CK Technology Group has successfully completed contract projects in numerous states and consulting work around the world.

Offering consulting services and solutions covering the complete IT lifecycle, CK Technology Group is a true "One-stop IT Shop" with divisions for Data Center and Network Design, Structured Cabling and Construction Services, Security, Access Control and Alarm systems, Life Safety, Software Design, Distributed Antenna Systems and Telecom/VoIP services.

Operating in the capacity of Managing Director, I oversee the daily operations of CK Technology Group, as well as the numerous technology firms under its umbrella.

Paramount to our success as a complete IT solutions provider is the strict delegation of our various service offerings to leading executives in the group's other business divisions. While operating as separate business units, there is absolute collaboration on projects.
