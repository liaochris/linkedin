Kathleen Ramsey, Allied ASID, CAPS's Summary

“Re-energize, inspire and comfort”. I believe your surroundings should do all three. My interior design practice includes space planning and layout, finish selection for new construction and remodeling, as well as furniture, fabric and accessories to make A house YOUR home. I have a degree in Interior Design, so I have the knowledge to know why something works. Many prospective clients ask me what my design “style” is. I appreciate all furniture periods, and feel confident using the motifs, colors and lines of them all. My natural tendency is toward adding color and signature items that reflect the client and their unique qualities. Whether you gravitate to “traditional” or “today”, I can help you add the color, texture and style to reflect your personality.

As an Allied Member of the American Society of Interior Designers, I am committed to the highest level of professionalism. My first priority is listening to you and identifying what your design problems and goals are. Ultimately, you will live in your space and it needs to reflect your personality - not mine. In addition, my background as a Registered Nurse makes me uniquely qualified to design for your long-term needs.

If you feel like you are headed on the right track with your interior, but just need a little reassurance or help with the next step? I particularly like to work with the "color commitment challenged". Adding color and texture can go a long way in creating atmosphere.

As a Certified Aging in Place Specialist, I am able to make recommendations for your existing home that will allow you to live in it longer. I call this Aging in Style!

Who I want to connect with:
* Small businesses that want their office space to reflect their brand and company values.
* People that want to update or renovate their current home instead of moving.
* Remodelers, Contractors, other craftspeople to partner on projects.
