Lucy Cripps' Summary

Years of writing experience comes as standard in this fully-specced model. Solid copywriting knowledge and excellent proofreading skills are neatly packed under the bonnet.

0 - 60 in ... barely any time at all! Written copy starts smoothly, accelerates powerfully and romps efficiently over the line, boasting about its accuracy and professionalism.

From a school essay that needs the once-over, to global business websites and projects, my pen and I are in your pit waiting to ensure your vehicle outperforms the rest.

Extended metaphor over!

I have an MA in Professional Writing, I teach writing (in English) at Salzburg University, and I write for several private clients who use my words daily, weekly, monthly and on the odd occasion. Let me help your words do exactly what you want them to do.

Luckily, I have borrowed Hermione's time-turner, so I am perfectly capable of doing in a day what it takes others to do in a week.

Specialties: Writing, proofreading, copyediting, subediting, webcopy and article submission

Oh words are pesky little things, aren't they? And if words weren't bad enough, you then have all that confounding punctuation.

What you need is a word chameleon that can find your voice and put it on the page, so your words represent you accurately and professionally when you can't be.

Guess what! I'm a word chameleon and can do just that; what are the odds?

Get in touch if you need your website written or rewritten, articles created for submission, a wedding speech or poem, a card for a bunch of flowers to your favourite person; heck, I'll even write your diary or memoires if you provide me with the memories!

With my MA in Professional Writing, I teach writing at Salzburg University, and I have been teaching English both in the UK and abroad since passing my PGCE in English in 1998.
