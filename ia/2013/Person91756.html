Michelle Curiel's Summary

Michelle Curiel is a web creative professional who has been designing and developing websites professionally for over nine years. She found her passion for designing websites c. 1998, when she taught herself everything she needed to know to create a multicultural web community. This community site focused on inspiring others to follow their dreams, encouraging creativity and bringing people from different cultures together. Within a year of launching the website, it received over 1 million visits and also received media attention. What started out as a hobby, became her career.

Nowadays, she has expanded on her web design experience, putting her talent, skills and passion to work, working both Freelance and full-time for great companies including Harbor Freight Tools, impreMedia Digital (La Opinion), Webmetro, AT&T Yellowpages, Playboy TV International and more. These companies have benefited from her creative nature, enthusiasm, aesthetic innovation, hard work and team attitude.
