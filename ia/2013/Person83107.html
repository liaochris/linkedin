Luciano Palma's Summary

Electronic Engineer, MBA, Community Manager, Professional Speaker,
Social Networks and New Media Consultant, Technology Consultant, Professor.

“Passionate about sharing Knowledge and building Effective Relationships, always applying Technology to the experience.”

At top companies like Intel and Microsoft, I’ve been leading relationships between companies and the Technical Community for around 7 years, establishing a new way to nurture collaboration, innovation, support and advocacy. This work is based on the concepts and dynamics of socialmedia channels like Facebook.
The vast experience implementing Technology in the Corporate world, from IT Infrastructure and Network Solutions to Advanced Digital Imaging Processing Software brought a deep understanding of Business Strategies and Processes, as well as strong knowledge about Project Management.
I lived for 4 years in England and Italy, working as a Software Developer. Besides the valuable technical experience acquired, the strong contact with different cultures got me to a higher level of professional and personal maturity.
My continuous activities as a Professor and Technical Instructor help to keep excellence regarding communications and presentation skills.
Communicative and expressive, I am a believer of continuous improvement through education, experimentation and collaboration.
Overall, I have more than 25 years of Professional Experience.
