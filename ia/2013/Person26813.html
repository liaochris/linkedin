Charlene Reed's Summary

I am the Owner of Responsible Business Services, where I create custom Excel spreadsheets, write Policy & Procedure Manuals, consult with business leaders, prepare sales tax returns and negotiate contracts on behalf of construction companies performing in the private and Federal Government Sector.

Previously, as the Practice Administrator for a physician medical group over a period of 14 years, I managed accounting, medical billing and the operation of both the medical practice for 5 full time physicians, an injury division and a Pharmaceutical Research Division, supervising 48 full time employees with providers who saw over 450 patients in 5 offices throughout the State of Arizona.

Later I became Operations Manager, responsible for all operations of a general commercial construction company and two subcontracting companies. In April 2000, I took a position with a mid-sized, 8(a) general commercial contractor performing Federal Government contracts in Washington, DC and 8 states. There, I was the Controller & Sr Contract Admin/Asst Project Manager on a 7 year, $21 million SABER contract at Davis Monthan AFB and many IDIQ, MATOC, BOA, and JOC contracts in the Federal Government.

After several years in Federal Government contracting, I began consulting, working with owners and contractors on both long term and short term assignments. During this time I had titles of Senior Accountant, Construction Manager, Operations Manager and Controller.

In 2005, I began writing the “Construction Administration Handbook”. The “Construction Administration Handbook”, now in its 2nd printing, covers all aspects of a construction company including accounting, project management and personnel, as well as other areas necessary for the profitability of any construction company.

I also write a construction blog covering construction and business related matters that can be found here:
MyConstructionOffice.Blogspot.com
