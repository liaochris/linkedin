Bevan Bird's Projects

  • Analysis of Survey Point Displacements Using Total Station Measurements

    • September 2007 to June 2009
    Team Members: Bevan Bird, John Ogundare, Don Thompson, Jack Clarke, Eddie Neumann, Azadeh Koohzare, David Martens, Colin Lawrence

    This technical report describes a software solution to the conversion, adjustment of raw survey data (3D total station measurements) and subsequent displacement analysis of networks by pairs of epochs. This is useful because sometimes when engineering structures are constructed, land deformation occurs, which can be dangerous and may cause serious damage. I made the software fast and it gives great control over relative weighting of observations so that observations of varying precisions can be adjusted together. Since completing the report for my BCIT degree, I have improved the software and I am still improving it. If you would like to experiment with it or use it in your own work, send me an e-mail at birdify [AT] gmail [DOT] com.
