  • GoCook app

    • June 2012 to October 2012
    Team Members: Jakob Mikkelsen, Simon Færch, Theis Othmar Frøhlich

    Become a better chef with the GoCook app for iPhone and Android

    Finally, there is an app for your phone that will help you become a better chef, in the comfort of your own kitchen. By utilizing easy-to-follow videos featuring professional chefs as well as over 100 recipes with step-by-step instructions and beautiful images, the GoCook app allows you to create savoury recipes while teaching you the basics of cooking.

    All the recipes included in the app can be automatically adjusted to fit into portion-sizes of 2, 4 and 6 persons and the built in cooking-timer helps you keep track of time, ensuring that your newly created masterpieces will taste just as good as expected.

    As if these features weren't enough the app also features a “cooking school” section in which you will find full-length video explaining methodically how to prepare the most common of the recipes.

    Finally, there is an extensive encyclopedia containing photos and explanations of the ingredients and the kitchenware you will find throughout the app.

    GoCook is a free app created by Danish co-op FDB and is available for both iPhone and Android phones. All the recipes can also be found on GoCook.dk
