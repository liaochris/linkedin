John Wilson's Summary

Life is only getting busier and busier​. Between work and family and whatever else we do throughout the day, it seems that sometimes we blow past the stuff that really matters in life. That's where my drive comes from: paying attention to what really matters.​

I specialize in three main areas:
Branding - Logos, stationary, static websites, etc.
Typography​ - Custom type, type setting, illustrative type, etc.
Illustration​ - Web icons, artwork, posters, books, etc.

​Over two years of freelance experience and over four years of design experience have lead to an understanding in what is important and what is not when it comes to communicating through design.

I am also backed by more than two years of in-house design experience as well as awards for both corporate branding and digital publishing. Graduating from Full Sail University with a Graphic Design BS Degree.

Specialties

branding and identity, illustration, typography, product packaging, print design
