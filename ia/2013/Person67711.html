Jonathan Weaver's Summary

For more than 20 years I have helped companies to drive top line sales and/or drive down costs towards better profitability. The successes with these companies can be attributed to the writing of a Sales & Marketing Business Plan that was

* Recognized by the team as new rules of the game
* Accepted by the the team as best practices not the ideology of management
* Carried out by the team on a daily basis as part of their new job descriptions

Since my involvement with Peak Performers I've had the pleasure of being exposed to literally hundreds of businesses and thousands of sales professionals who were seeking better results.

Currently through my company I am working with clients on the following projects

1) Helping a Canadian Drug Manufacturer to deliver to the marketplace a painless prescribed treatment that, in studies has reduced dental decay by as much as 87%

2) Helping a Canadian financial Institution to create a vehicle for Canadians with disabilities to access the Grants & Bonds available to them. Helping the same financial institution to create a Financial Literacy Program to take to the public so they can become aware that more options exist for them to get their NEST EGG to grow MORE SAFELY & MORE PREDICTABLY

3) Guest lecturer for an American client who has clients requiring sales education process

4) Helping a Canadian Barter Exchange to establish a liquidation model for corporations with excess inventory looking to avoid liquidation priced product in the marketplace cannibalizing their existing markets

If you know of an individual or organization looking for assistance or someone in the field of Professional/Personal development who is a Peak Performer please refer them to me.

Jonathan

Specialties: Working with companies to increase the productivity of their sales force via direct work with the Team or through a consultative approach / mentoring of the Sales Manager
