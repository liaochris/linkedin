Marta Spendowska's Summary

I illustrate, create patterns and design for web & print, with coffee or chai mug on the side.
I am a MULTIPOTENTIAL working as surface pattern designer, illustrator and web + print designer, design consultant specializing in Wordpress, logo design, web design, print design, social media, illustration (love fashion illustration) and more.

As a designer with a background in marketing (Masters in Management and Marketing), I seek to teach and inspire others through sharing my knowledge while running an online happy business.

1. I illustrate beauty and complicated women. I paint portraits, perfume bottles, surface patterns, creatures with watercolor and inks.

2. I design and develop websites that have nothing to do with "boring" using Wordpress (predominately), I build online strategy and work with print materials.
I also created a cool digital program Create Your Website Fast that helps entrepreneurs to build their own online presence.

3. I'm a strategic and intuitive designer. I prefer to work with small businesses, artists, coaches, solopreneurs, women, or anyone who is passionate about what they’re doing. There is just one condition — good match between you and me in terms of mindset and personal chemistry.

MY PAST LIFE IN 3rd PERSON:

1. Left (still communistic) Poland in 2005 with no plan. Feeling burnt out, quit her dead-end job in marketing (she received her Masters in Management and Marketing in 2004) and decided to leave her life behind. Having experienced America while completing an internship in Atlanta, Georgia her senior year at University, she knew it was the place to follow her dreams.

2. Enrolled in design programs, graduated with honors, interned for agencies, worked for agencies.

3. Realized she preferred to work independently. “I started getting requests from clients of the agency asking if I would freelance for them. We could cut out the middle-man.I could talk to them directly and get their true feelings and ideas.”

And so it began...

WHERE ALL THAT LOVE COMES FROM :

Every time I reach for a brush, paint, pencil, tablet I feel like this is a time to let go. Every time right before I start, there is this feeling of completeness, joy, a bit of confusion, if I can handle my emotions, and than the time stops. It literally stops. I don't care about physics anymore.
Some people don't know what to do with their free time. This feeling was always strange to me.
Abundance is everywhere. Feelings are everywhere, time stretches, if not disappears. With this stunning amount of emotional freedom to do whatever I wish, with feelings overflowing from joy that I can touch paper and create a face, an eye, a foot, I am what I supposed to be/mean.
