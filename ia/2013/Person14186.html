Bill Addison's Summary

Designer / Digital Lead for Dog & Pony Studios (Toronto).

I’ve worked in various positions throughout Australia, Japan, Canada and now from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Experiencing many worlds has proven essential to solve new challenges, be successful in different markets, and to master the art of managing local and remote teams.

Questions? Feel free to contact me, I'd love to chat.... or find out what makes me tick below:

My background is based in digital design; designing interfaces that are easy and practical to use. This is imperative to a great online experience. In recent years, I've moved from a design and interface role into project management and digital positioning for businesses of various sizes.

What fascinates me is how our expectations and understanding of the online world is evolving so rapidly. Users are embracing new technologies, smart phones are enabling a richer, more connected experience, while innovators are building new ways to harness these technologies. This is the world we live in and it's an amazing time to help businesses adapt to this space.

Specialties

Project Management, Content Architecture, Interface Design & Development, Wordpress, Social Media Integration, CSS, HTML, Javascript, PHP, MySQL.
