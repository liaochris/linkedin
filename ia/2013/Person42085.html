Eric Glasshofer's Summary

I have been in the Consumer Electronics industry for 25 years. I have held senior management positions with both public and private companies. I have experience in retail, commercial, marine and MDU sales and installation sectors. My passion for the inudustry has never diminished. I see our industry growing and evolving in so many ways.

I offer my peers and employers experience, passion and most importantly a work ethic and drive that delivers results. My experience working in larger public companies allowed me the expereince to work as a team lead and as a support player. My experience working with my partners in the private sector allowed me to learn how the leverage core competencies and resources to grow and maintain our market share.
