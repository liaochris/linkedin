Ian Cull's Summary

I'm a licensed environmental engineer specializing in indoor air quality. My time is divided between speaking around the world on the topic of indoor air quality and consulting for problem buildings mostly in the Chicagoland area.

I provide training through the Indoor Air Quality Association (IAQA) and through my company, Indoor Sciences, Inc.

I also maintain a blog dedicated to sharing information related to the indoor environment. The blog's theme comes from my first name being an ancronym: Indoor Air Nerd! Check it out at www.IndoorAirNerd.com.

Specialties: Indoor Air Quality, Mold, HVAC, Building Science
