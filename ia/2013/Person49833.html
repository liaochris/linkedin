Grant Norwood's Summary

Web and mobile developer, architect, project manager, product designer, social media nerd.

I’ve recently moved from the creative services and interactive marketing world into a full-time non-profit role with the Michael & Susan Dell Foundation. It’s wonderful that I’m able to spend more time contributing to a unique organization that is truly a leader in the philanthropic space, and comprised of an exceptionally talented team of individuals. My personal challenge is to bring my extensive experience in web design, software engineering, and creative crafting of online user experiences to the foundation as they work fiercely towards their mission goals.

I dig live music and photography is a passion, and until I break something you can find me biking some rocky trails on the weekends. Born and raised in Austin, I like to think that I bring a little weirdness up to the suburbs of Round Rock, which is just a short commute north of the State’s capital city and the “Live Music Capital of the World”.

I dabble in ... WordPress, PHP, MySQL, Linux, ASP.NET, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, mobile & responsive web design, XML, XPath, XSLT, web services, J2ME on BlackBerry, Windows apps, MS SQL Server, SSIS, DTS, MS Analysis Services, Reporting Services, Adobe AIR, Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, and Premiere.

Find me on Twitter at @grantnorwood, or visit my blog at http://grantnorwood.com. (I don't blog often, so when I do, it's worth the read!)
