Mark Dienger's Summary

I'm a music, art and popular culture addict passionate about product development and social media. With over a decade of my career invested in the entertainment industry, I have strategized and executed dozens of complete online solutions for top artists and brands. I adore metrics and love to use it to show off my success. My track record shows my keen eye for upcoming trends and know-how to get in front of the curve rather than follow the pack.

Specialties: Building innovative products, developing SMO strategies and translating them into web traffic, brand-building, word-of-mouth marketing, community management particularly on, Facebook and Twitter, fan outreach and activation, ethics and transparency in online marketing, social media, Web 2.0, and account management.
