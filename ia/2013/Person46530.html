Gabi Rose's Summary

Following a traumatic near death experience, Gabi Rose made a conscious decision to reclaim her life from obesity and become a positive role model for her husband and four young children. Gabi’s discipline, dedication and determination empowered her to be successful in her endeavor and she lost a dramatic 168 lbs! Proper nutritional counseling and exercise helped Gabi make a total lifestyle change for herself and the rest of her family. Gabi’s hard work and effort truly paid off when her dress size dropped from a hefty size 24 to a slender size 2.

Gabi has inspired millions of people to make an investment in their lives by getting healthy. Through all her years fighting the obesity epidemic Gabi has always believed, “The training of the body begins with the mind”! The Gabi Rose program is recognized exclusively as a thermodynamic weight loss break through. Her distinctive approach toward obesity increases the overall metabolic rate, improves energy, amplifies lean body mass and aids in rapid fat loss in her patients. Gabi has developed her own line of unique products and improved bariatric practice revenue for other doctors.

Gabi Rose is an international obesity and weight loss expert. She is the host and co-producer of the Get Fit Club, a one of its kind reality series seen on ABC local10.com throughout South Florida. Gabi is the CEO of Gabi Rose, LLC and creator of “Get Fit with Gabi Rose”, an exciting lifestyle methodology to health and wellness. Gabi has extensive training in the science of nutrition and weight loss. She is a business entrepreneur, television spokesperson, motivational speaker and fitness mentor. Gabi is a highly sought after media guest with appearances on ABC, NBC, FOX and CBS television. She has been featured in Ladies Home Journal, Woman’s World and First for Women magazines. Gabi has developed her own show, Get Fit with Gabi on WINZ, Miami with Philip Bell and Michael Woulfe on the Community News and Review Show .
