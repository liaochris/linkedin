Mary Beth Brennan's Summary

“Friendly, professional, considerate and thorough” these are words often used to describe Mary Beth by her clients. She has a reputation for being a strong advocate for homebuyers and sellers alike.

A native Oregonian, Mary Beth came to central Texas in 2008 and knew she had found “home” in Austin, Texas and Bend,Oregon. For more than 11 years, Mary Beth Brennan has been a leader in the residential real estate market.

Known and respected industry-wide, Mary Beth’s Multi-Million Dollar production is not a product of sales tactics but by being her authentic self. Yet her real estate expertise extends far beyond that of a Realtor®. Mary Beth provides invaluable market intelligence to a wide range of customers, from single homeowners to developers of multi-million dollar luxury buildings, and she is wholly committed to providing personalized service creating lasting relationships.

Mary Beth is not your average Realtor® she has an Internet Marketing Bachelors Degree that gives her an unlimited amount of creativity and innovation for getting the job done. She is a leading expert in personal branding, leadership, and risk management of which she shares by teaching Mandatory Continuing Education courses thru the Texas Real Estate Commission (TREC). Mary Beth is a natural Internet Marketing Evangelist, which is an advanced form of word of mouth marketing (WOMM).

The culmination of her professional development has uniquely positioned Mary Beth to work as a highly professional real estate Principal Broker. Despite all the demands of a successful real estate career, Mary Beth is committed to balancing work and family. Her love for the West Lake Hills is evident when you talk to her about Austin, Texas and the surrounding metropolitan area and the flip side she values the beauty Bend, OR has to offer it's residents.

Put her vast professional skills to work and you’ll like the results.
