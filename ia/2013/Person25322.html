Clara Guelbenzu's Summary

I have 20 years of internationally recognized professional interpreting experience (more than 3000 hours) in a variety of fields.

I am currently working as a staff interpreter and translator at the International Centre for Advanced Mediterranean Agronomic Studies (IAMZ-CIHEAM) where I interpret and translate all subjects which are directly or indirectly related to agriculture: genetics, nutrition, reproduction, crop production, biotechnology, ecology, economics, marketing, econometrics, market research, aquaculture, fisheries, forest fires, management, wine, pests and diseases, husbandry, water management, irrigation, soils, erosion, landscape ecology, agribusiness, climate change, etc.


Languages: Spanish (A), English (B) and French (C).

Specialties

- Conference interpreting in workshops, congresses, seminars, symposia, meetings, press-conferences, etc.
- Translation of all types of documents and coordination of translation projects.
- Proofreading and editing of books, academic papers, articles, etc.
- Coordination and organization of in-house training courses.
- Compilation and design of ad-hoc glossaries.
- Management of terminology database.
- Language consultancy.
