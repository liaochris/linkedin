Robert Layne's Summary

Robert N. Layne, CIPM is the Executive Director of the IFCPP. He is also Vice President of Operations for Layne Consultants International, coordinating safety & security training, planning, and design services for businesses and cultural facilities across the U.S. and abroad. Rob is a member of the American Society for Industrial Security’s Cultural Properties Council, and Past-President of Colorado Crime Stoppers and Safe2Tell, working with several governmental and public agencies on a groundbreaking youth violence prevention initiative. Rob graduated from the University of Colorado with a degree in International Affairs. He is responsible for the daily operations of the IFCPP and coordinates membership, educational, conference, and certification programs. Rob also coordinates operations for the newly re-formed Protection Alliance, offering comprehensive security and fire protection consulting services to organizations worldwide. Rob is proud to live and work in Denver, CO.

Specialties

Security Consulting, Corporate Administration, Non-Profit Management, Training Development
