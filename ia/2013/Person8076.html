Anjali Gupta's Summary

I love building ideas that can impact millions. In my work I seek a good combination of 'people, problem, & impact'.

As a Web entrepreneur, I have conceived and built products around e-commerce (B2C), Crowdsourcing, and software to tech-enable regional audiences (SME).

Previously worked with Amazon.com in Seattle, Netegrity Inc in Boston, Persistent in Pune;

I've presented my products at DEMO.com in California, at Proto India and Manthan South Asia, been featured by Entrepreneur, CNBC, and India Today, been a panelist at TiE-ISB and SiliconIndia, been a guest editor for Innovation at CSI DeskTalk. Co-founded Wharton's First India Immersion Program, and Pune's Open Coffee Club for Entrepreneurs (punestartups.org).

I'm closely associated with the innovation and startup eco-system in India - mentor at Startup Weekend, Google Women on Web, BITS Pilani Conquest, GSF Accelerator, and an advisor to a few startups.

Twitter me: @anjaligupta
Blog: AnjaliGupta.com

Specialties: Starting with blank canvas and building momentum; Creating new ideas and initiatives for business growth, Design Thinking across wide consumer expectations, Energizing a team to execute towards a goal, Planning across roles/people to jump-start the product cycle of - Acquisition, Conversion, Referral, Revenue and Retention. More simply, I enjoy these verbs - "innovate", "iterate", "grow", "market", "plan", and "detail".
