Chris Boggs' Summary

Digital marketing specialist with 13 years of experience of driving ROI with organic search engine optimization (SEO), paid search advertising (PPC) campaign creation and management, and use of Web analytics platforms and tools to improve integration between online marketing and branding performance.

Expert Columnist for Search Engine Watch Blog. ""SEO Rockstars," podcast co-host at WebmasterRadio.fm. Regular speaker at industry conferences including Search Engine Strategies (Advisory Board). Current Chair of the Board of Directors for the Search Engine Marketing Professionals Organization (SEMPO, Inc). Board member since 2006 serving as President 2010-2012.

Specialties: search engine optimization, search engine marketing, social media, web analytics, internet marketing, link development strategies, ethical seo, social media, Google+
