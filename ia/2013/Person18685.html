Bryson Payne, Ph.D.'s Experience

Department Head of Computer Science

University of North Georgia

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

January 2013 – Present (9 months)

First department head (interim) of the new Department of Computer Science (formerly part of Math & CS), orchestrating the development of a thriving, accredited CS and CIS degree program with over 360 majors on two campuses.
Selected achievements:
• Led the departmental effort to update the curriculum to match ABET and ACM standards for upcoming ABET accreditation application, and added relevant, current-interest courses.
• Removed barriers to student success by simplifying prerequisites for all upper-level computer science courses, improved section offerings of major and core courses.
• Collaborated with the College of Business’ IT/MIS faculty to cross-list relevant CSCI courses with ITEC to facilitate multiple pathways to graduation for CS and IT/IS majors.
• Established CS/IT Advisory Board to connect students and faculty with regional IT industry leaders and firms.
• Have begun building relationships with over 370 North Georgia computer science alumni from IBM, General Dynamics, Blizzard Games, Equifax, AT&T, Adobe, and more.

Associate Professor of Computer Science

University of North Georgia

Tenured Associate Professor of Computer Science in the College of Science & Mathematics. Graduate faculty member in the Mike Cottrell College of Business MBA program.
• Exceptional student ratings in Information Management, Web Programming, Computer Graphics, Java I & II, Artificial Intelligence, and Computer Animation.
• Maintaining research and publication qualifications for promotion and tenure while serving in a full-time, cabinet-level position as CIO.

Chief Information Officer

University of North Georgia (formerly North Georgia College & State University)

Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Higher Education industry

November 2006 – August 2012 (5 years 10 months)

Led university technology transformation in the consolidation of two institutions with a combined 15,000 students, 2,000 faculty and staff, on four campuses. Responsible for university IT strategy, innovation, technology evangelism, IT operations, information security/business continuity planning, and exceptional customer service.
Selected achievements:
• Successfully implemented a leading-edge, scientific approach to organizational structure to transform all North Georgia IT units into a cohesive, customer-oriented organization.
• Building the IT implementation plan for the consolidation of NGCSU and Gainesville State, consolidating 60 full-time IT staff with annual IT budgets of over $6 million.
• Delivered on-time and within-budget technology solutions for over $260 million in construction and renovations over six years.
• Worked with Student Government Association to secure funding and delivered 4-year lab & classroom computer and 6-year projector refresh cycle, Microsoft Student Option campus licensing, remote virtual desktop labs, wireless network expansion, and more.
• 2010-2011 Chair of the University System of Georgia’s CIO Advisory Council.
• Established the university’s first I.T. Advisory Board, connecting university leadership with regional industry IT executives.
• Implemented the first leadership training program for IT managers and staff.
