Paul Bragiel's Summary

I'm a managing partner and co-founder of i/o ventures, an early stage startup accelerator based in San Francisco. We focus heavily on a mentorship driven model.

Previously I have founded and served as the CEO of 3 companies including:

Lefora, a product bringing forums into the 21st century, proudly hosting over 100,000+ unique communities. Meetro, the first location based social network. Paragon Five, a game development studio specializing in console and mobile content with offices in Chicago and Krakow, Poland.

I get really excited about projects that involve travel, entertainment, and technologies that encourage communication and networking.

I speak regularly around the world about entrepreneurship, tech policy, early stage funding & incubators. I've also been know to serve as a liaison for foreign companies looking to establish operations in silicon valley.

I can also proudly say that I'm one of the authors of Game Programming Gems 4. Amazing considering I suck at coding nowadays. :)

Outside of work, I have toured Russia by train (starting in Siberia), bicycled across the United States (riding over 5,000 km), canoed the entire Mississippi River (paddling over 2,300 miles) and visited 75+ countries including the birth of a nation.

I like to surround myself with people from various backgrounds especially those with a "jack of all trades" streak in them. Besides that, I aspire to drive a taxi and work at a hot dog stand in New York when I retire.
