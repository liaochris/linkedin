Jessica Plotnick, PA-C, MMSc's Summary

After completing a B.A. at SUNY-Binghamton, I went to Emory University’s School of Medicine Physician Assistant Program, rated the number one P.A. program in the nation. I graduated amongst the top of my class and received a Physician Assistant degree along with a Masters in Medical Science. Following graduation in January 1996, I started practicing Emergency Medicine, which I continue to do today. After a few years in the E.R., it became apparent that my strength was with detail-oriented work. This realization guided me into the field of Sclerotherapy. I've been performing Sclerotherapy for many years and have gained recognition as a highly skilled, knowledgeable, and gentlest Sclerotherapists in the tri-state region. I've treated over a thousand men and women, of all skin colors, who remain thrilled with their amazing results.

With a dedicated patient population, my practice has expanded from performing Sclerotherapy to include many other invasive and non-invasive cosmetic procedures. These include laser hair removal, chemical peels, and many injectables including BOTOX® and JUVEDERM®. I take great pride and appreciate when patients tell me, "When you find someone you trust, you stick with them." I'm pleased to be that person for so many wonderful people.
