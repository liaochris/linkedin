Mary Groll's Summary

Over the last two years, there has been a seismic shift from traditional advertising to content marketing. The concept is to offer a steady stream of FREE, useful information to establish thought leadership in your field so that when potential clients need what you sell, they think of you. Give to get, you might say.

If you are too busy doing what you do to write about it or if writing is not your strong suit, PerfectWright, the writing division of Mary Groll Marketing, makes it easy for you to offer useful information. By creating blogs, e-blasts, white papers, newsletters, website copy, press releases, and other communications, it helps you establish your expertise.

Editing and proofreading services also available.

Specialties: Content marketing including well-crafted, keyword-enriched websites, blogs, e-blasts, white papers, newsletters, press releases, etc.; editing and proofing to ensure flawless text; strategic marketing options lists; public relations and advertising for Philadelphia area businesses, professional practices, and non-profits. For further information, visit www.PerfectWright.com or contact PerfectWright at 215-885-2803.
