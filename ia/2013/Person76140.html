Kitti Lakner's Summary

I’m a User Interface/User Experience (ui/ux) designer with 10 years of hands on experience. I specialize in interaction design (IxD) and human–computer interaction (HCI) for hosted and on-premise applications. I have expert level knowledge of interaction design processes and deliverable throughout the development lifecycle including requirements refinement, task flows, storyboards, wireframes, mock-ups, and prototypes. In addition, I am experienced in all phases of usability testing including user profiles and personas, task analysis, contextual research, test moderation, and reporting.

With all that said, my primary focus is to satisfy and exceed the needs and desires of the people who will use the products I design.

Specialties

Designing Graphical User Interfaces, User Interface Architecture, User Experience Testing, Wireframe/Prototyping, Experienced in the use of agile methodologies., Thorough understanding of cross browser compatibility, Advocate of Web Standards, WordPress Theme Development
