Marcelo Manucci's Summary

Psychologist. Doctorate in Communication. International consultant in organizations and companies. University professor of Graduate degree in Communication and Marketing in Universities of Argentina, Latin America and USA. Specialist in the application of Theory of the Chaos, Complex Thinking and System Thinking in strategic corporate processes.

Specialties

Has designed tools and models of training for the development of competitive teams in unstable contexts. His areas of work are: strategy, design of sectorial analysis, organizacional change and business units development.
