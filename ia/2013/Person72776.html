Katherine Ruppe's Summary

I'm a produced playwright and published essayist. Currently focused on writing and developing TV dramas, as well as feature films (two of which have been optioned).

TV PROJECTS:
SILVER STAR -- crime drama pilot; out to producers; garnered me an interview for the 2013 Humanitas New Voices Competition.

MAD MEN -- spec script; placed in the top 5% of applicants to the Warner Bros TV Writers' Workshop; Scriptapalooza Quarterfinalist.

MRS. SMITH GOES TO WASHINGTON -- Political comedy pilot.

FEATURE FILM PROJECTS:
LIVE BAIT (aka HOOKED) -- Edgy romantic comedy. Winner of UCLA MFA Screenwriting Showcase; Nicholl Fellowship Semifinalist. Currently available after being under option. Seeking attachments.

A KILLING FROST -- Suspense thriller; accepted into the Film Independent Screenwriters Lab and 2012 Fast Track program at the LA Film Festival; New American Theater Screenplay Competition Winner; Nicholl Semifinalist (top 30 scripts). Under option.

WHITE KNUCKLE RIDE -- Action-thriller assignment for High Treason Productions; in pre-production.

DIAMOND DOGS -- Action adventure; Nicholls Quarterfinalist; Sloan Fellowship Finalist.

ESSAY/ VIDEO/ THEATER:
A GUEST STAR IN HIS ROMANTIC DRAMA -- Comedic first-person essay.
- published in the The New York Times "Modern Love" column, Jan 18, 2009
- published as "Open Season" in the anthology "What Was I Thinking?: 58 Bad Boyfriend Stories" St. Martin's Press, 2009.
- READ IT on my websites link. It's fun, really.

WHAT WAS I THINKING?: GEEK LOVE -- writer/narrator of animated short based on my essay, "Open Season." Premiered on Slate V.
- WATCH IT on my websites link. It's short (and fun).

AMERICAN DARLINGS -- One-act comedic play; produced in the UCLA Francis Ford Coppola One-Act Festival, 2004.

I’m a former union camera operator on feature films/TV in New York, including 4 years on the hit ABC sitcom SPIN CITY. In features, I worked with such directors as Jonathan Demme, Spike Lee, and Robert Benton.
