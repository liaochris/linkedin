Matt Alder's Summary

I help companies get more value from their digital strategy by offering strategic advice, training and auditing based on my wide range of experience in the Recruitment and HR industries

Over the last fourteen years I've helped numerous employers connect to the talent they need via digital, social and mobile recruitment strategies and techniques

I'm also a highly regarded futurist who is fascinated by the way organisations are becoming more connected to create the future of the world of work.

My extensive experience of leading digital migration within large companies has given me a unique insight into the opportunities emerging technologies can offer both organisations and the people who work for them.

I now act as an independent business consultant via my company MetaShift. I'm a well read blogger, author and an accomplished public speaker as well as being a regular commentator on the emerging world of Social Recruiting
