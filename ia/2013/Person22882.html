Chip Jarvis' Summary

I have been a practicing attorney for almost 15 years.  During that time, I have actively handled cases in the three major areas that the 380th District Court hears -- family, criminal, and civil.  Not just a touch of experience, but I have personally tried and managed cases in each area of law. My career has taken me from a large medical malpractice/insurance defense firm in Dallas, Texas  TO starting my own law firm focusing primarily on criminal and family law (and we represented several area businesses) TO serving Our Country in the United States Army as an enlisted infantryman and JAG Officer (after Sept. 11 2001) TO prosecuting in the Collin County District Attorney's Office TO again starting my own firm focusing primarily on family law.  I believe that it is important that our citizens have a choice on election day - to vote for a candidate who has the experience and temperament necessary to respectfully, responsibly, and reasonably run the 380th Judicial District Court.
