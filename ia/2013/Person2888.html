Ann Keys-Spencer's Summary

Visual Marketing Mentors, LLC is a visual marketing company that specializes in working with businesses who attend live marketing events. (Tradeshows-Trade Fairs-Conventions-Conferences-Meetings-Symposiums-Networking )

Needs Assessments-Training/Coaching-Exhibits/Displays-Graphics-Promotional Items-Logistics are all done by VMM.

Visual Marketing Mentors, LLC, is an Eco-friendly corporation. We assist our clients who want to reduce their carbon foot print also. We work with companies who provide recyclable, sustainable, Eco-friendly and green displays/exhibits and graphics.

Visual Marketing Mentors, LLC has another division, Concierge Services Group which works on all the other ancillary needs for companies who attend live marketing events.

Concierge Services Group** helps our clients with seamless, integrate, one stop shopping for other items needed for their visual marketing needs. Our goal: One itemized bill for the project.

Ann Keys-Spencer (CEO/Founder/Owner of VMM), has worked with North America's leading manufacturers, exhibit houses and graphic production firms for 22 years. Visual Marketing Mentors, LLC works with companies who offer superior products, inventive solutions and quality services.

Every event that you exhibit at, using your marketing dollars, should give you good ROI's. That is our goal, to make your visual marketing components match your companies marketing message.
