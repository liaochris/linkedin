Chris Chambers DSA ADI's Summary

An experienced Driver Training professional, and also the Director / Operator of Chris Chambers School of Motoring - A dedicated provider of a variety of effective, informative and enjoyable driving lessons & solutions to valued clients in and around Sunderland, UK.

Chris Chambers School of Motoring is not the 'run-of-the-mill' training provider (all driving schools are not the same . . . some are different!). I provide a "pleasingly different", client centred learning environment which truly makes the learning experience a personal one.

My mission is to both teach and to act as a mentor figure, whilst developing the clients' skill-set to the highest possible standard, which, in turn increases the chances of a successful first time test pass and, of course, prepares the client for a life-long safe driving career on the UK's roads.

When it comes to the learning of an important life skill such as driving, I firmly believe that one should choose their training provider very carefully, and avoid making the decision based primarily on cost. For me the following is true -

"GOOD TUITION ISN'T CHEAP, BUT BAD TUITION COSTS MORE . . "



Chris Chambers School of Motoring currently provide the following driver training services:

• New Driver/ Partly Trained Driver courses (*Manual transmission vehicles only)
• Pass Plus
• Confidence sessions
• Refresher sessions
• Mock Test assessment
• Motorway tuition (*Full licence holders only)
• Driving Test Rescue
• Manoeuvre Improvement
• FREE On-line Theory & HP training and support
• Night Time Driving
• Adverse Weather Driving
• Automatic to Manual licence conversion
