Matt Leschuck's Summary

With a degree in both Digital Audio Engineering and Advanced Midi Production, I can confidently say that sound design is a true passion of mine.  I have worked on multiple projects including live band recording, sound design for film, voice for TV and radio and music for gaming.

My education started at the University of Washington where I studied and received a minor in Law, Society and Justice and a Bachelor’s in Sociology. I then pursued Audio Engineering & Advanced Midi Production at Shoreline Community College.  After two years of hard work, I left campus with an extensive amount of knowledge and hands on skill.

Today I am seeking opportunities to work on all things audio related and would love to join a team of other skilled engineers to produce the best work possible.
