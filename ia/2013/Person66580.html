John Lalone's Experience

Founder & Principal

The Lalone Group, LLC

October 2002 – Present (11 years 3 months)

The Lalone Group has established a solid reputation over 10+ years for providing trusted, experienced and innovative consulting solutions across many industries. We continue to add to our portfolio and can bring top-tier consulting solutions to the table for small and mid-sized emerging companies in need of sales and sales management expertise, as well as banks, thrifts and credit unions requiring senior (20+ years experience) project resources for mission-critical implementations.

Ex: Small and mid-sized business owners wear many hats and work tirelessly to develop their product or service, bring it to market and grow their companies – creating jobs and security for many. The downside to wearing all of the hats is that the entrepreneur/owner cannot be great at every function in the company.

One functional area that often ends up on the owner’s already full plate is Sales Management. By default, the owner, who may have little-to-no sales expertise, attempts to build and manage a sales team while taking away from his/her core skills and value to the company. Outsourcing this area can free the owner up to focus on what they do best.

Applying 20+ years of sales and sales management experience, we bring a set of operational templates and processes to your company on a temporary, retained basis. Our range of services includes assessing the current Sales team, hiring (if necessary) solid Sales professionals, compensating them properly and providing effective and efficient Sales operational structure and management for them to work within.

Your company gains the expertise of a veteran Sales Executive without the full loaded costs. When the business is stabilized or scaled to the point where a full-time Sales Manager/Director/VP is justified, we will work with you to assess, select and transition that player into your organization.
