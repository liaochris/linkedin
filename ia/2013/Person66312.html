Vice President, Business Development

LiveIntent, Inc.

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

May 2012 – September 2012 (5 months) Austin, Texas Area

LiveIntent is the solution that unites the power of display and the reach and personalization of email through a simple set of persistent ad tags. Using our predictive ad optimization technology, publishers can now present highly targeted ad content to newsletter, alert and notification subscribers without the hassle of segmentation or suppression prior to send, an advantage that makes email inventory more valuable, and importantly more sellable. Our platform also allows advertisers to target their campaigns using all of the same targeting criteria that they're used to with regular display buys, but adds the performance of email to really reach highly engaged subscribers.
