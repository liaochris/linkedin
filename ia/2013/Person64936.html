JoAnne Lenart-Weary's Summary

Thirty-five years in the decorating and staging industry have provided me a strong background in creating beautiful spaces as well as how to help others turn their love for decorating and staging into a profitable business. I consider myself a professional brainstormer as I love to create systematic ways to approach creating a beautiful room and am amazing business. I love to find new ways to market business, concepts, and people.

Specialties: Staging, Interior Design, Training, Career Coach, Business Coach, Color Expert, SpacePlanning, Marketing, Promotion, Sales and training
