Christina Milanowski's Summary

At Maccabee, I serve as the agency's director of social media, and lead client projects as account supervisor. I work hard and love to learn... I have more than six years of post-college experience developing PR, marketing and communications strategies, both in-house and agency. Outside of work, you'll find me at St. Thomas, where I'm pursuing a masters degree in business communications, with fellow public relations society members, as I co-chair the Membership Committee, or at my new home in Minneapolis!
