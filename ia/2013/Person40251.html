Ekta Daryanani's Summary

I am a human-centered UX and interaction designer with a strong drive to simplify and delight. I have a passion for understanding the aspirations and behaviors of real people in their everyday lives.
I enjoy working throughout the product development process, from conceptualizing the idea to interpreting metrics and evolving the product.
