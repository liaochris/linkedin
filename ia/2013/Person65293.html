Joseph Egan's Summary

My goal and passion is to help organizations define and execute their mobile strategy. A mobile strategy beyond just devices but focusing on how to use mobile to meet your business objectives.

There is an old adage “the right tool for the job”. In technology, this is not always the case.Decisions are made based on the tools organizations are familiar with. While these tools may get a task completed, often this approach can leave the people you are trying to connect with alienated because it does not meet their needs.

I have spent a large part of my career working with people solving business problems, matching up the technology to objectives. The key to this success is starting with an understanding of the goals, developing a roadmap to there and beyond and execution of the strategy.

Mobile strategy is rooted in the same beliefs, not starting with the device (tool) but understanding what is needed to be achieved. My career has had me working with large corporations, government agencies and multiple startup organizations including my own mobile platform, Jumyo. This experience in strategy, my deep understanding of the mobile space and access to an amazing team of people that deliver on solutions enables me to execute on the business vision.

Why it is so important to have a mobile strategy for your business?

❖ By 2015, it is projected that 788 million people will have mobile only internet access

❖ Today 70% of mobile searchers connect with the businesses, 52% called the business, 50% look the business up on a map

❖ 66% of mobile searchers visit the business, 48% visits the website, 36% made a purchase

❖ 46% of people are unlikely to return to a mobile site or application if it did not work properly the first time

❖ 74% of customers will wait 5 seconds for a page to load on their mobile device before leaving

You know you need a mobile strategy and you know there are a lot of questions. Let me help you execute – contact me at joe@kiehlnorthwest.com
