Joseph Ruiz, CPC, CTS's Summary

As a talent acquisition executive and strategic staffing partner, I am deeply committed to the success and well-being of my customers and stakeholders. This unshakable commitment has enabled me to serve as a trusted advisor and confidant to executives and professionals for the past 16 years. My core values of integrity, humility, trust, faith, and accountability drive my daily decisions, bringing lasting value to my well-established network.

After starting my career in the U.S. Army's Public Affairs Office and then working for global recruitment firms, I have established highly successful organizations in the staffing industry by earning the confidence and loyalty of top-tier employers, assembling world-class recruitment teams, and building a robust network of accomplished and emerging executives. As a staffing industry and job market expert, I have authored widely-distributed, monthly job market newsletters, online blogs, annual employment forecasts and salary guides, all of which have been distributed to countless hiring executives and job market enthusiasts. My team and I have provided job search and consultation services to thousands of executives and professionals, and we have been entrusted with a myriad of talent acquisition and organizational development initiatives.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master’s in Organizational Leadership degree at Gonzaga University. Earning this distinguished degree will enable me to continue providing innovative and customized talent acquisition and advisory solutions to top-tier employers that entrust me with their human capital and organizational development needs. The knowledge and skills acquired in this comprehensive program, augmented by my proven competence in talent development and management, will also enable me to continue providing sound career guidance, effective job search strategies, and inspirational coaching and mentoring services to those who rely on my leadership and expertise.
