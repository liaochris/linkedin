Howard Jackson's Summary

As a successful entrepreneur with more than 30 years’ experience providing construction services, I offer corporate management, executive boards and public clients the professional executive leadership services that ensure the success of any major construction project.

Highly qualified as a senior project executive, I have led projects of $100 million in total contract value. Each project's success has been built upon the foundation of proven systems/processes that include lean construction practices and the engagement of all stakeholders to ensure that the project remains on schedule and completed within budget.

I am accomplished at all levels of construction and facilities management. Key responsibilities include upholding full project authority/accountability; identifying/appointing qualified, effective management team members; and, ensuring that risks are tracked, managed and mitigated effectively. Team building, strategic partnering and achieving collaboration between all team members, has been the cornerstone of my success.

My commitment to excellence and utilizing best-business principles/practices has consistently delivered measurable bottom-line results. I am results-driven and relationship-oriented. I understand that all relationships should be handled with integrity, ethics and fair dealing.

I am a Certified Federal Contractor (SAM-registered) with extensive experience in the public sector. My company, CM/GC Group Inc., is a SDVOSB (Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business) and HUBZone-certified.

If you are looking for an experienced project leader in the disciplines of construction and facility management; project consulting and management, that consistently exceeds expectations - I'd love to connect and see how I can help you.
