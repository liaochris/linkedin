Bryan Luce's Summary

Reliance Capital Partners is a strategic, growth oriented private equity fund. The purpose of the fund is the strategic acquisition of mature private micro and small cap companies with strong and recurring cash flows. Facilitating integration, consolidation and growth through synergistic merger into portfolio companies with profitable exits within 5 years.

Reliance Funding Group, is a small regional boutique Lending and Consulting firm specializing in Financing Strategies, management consultng, Merger/ Acquisition / Disposition Services. We provide indepth consulting to help companies of all sizes and types, with a concentration in Agri-business and Energy related companies in the New England Region.

Specialties

Management Consulting, Mergers and aquisitions, asset, disposition, financing, due diligence, strategic plannning.
