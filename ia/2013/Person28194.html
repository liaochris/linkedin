Christiaan Alberdingk Thijm's Summary

Christiaan A. Alberdingk Thijm is founder and partner of bureau Brandeis, a Netherlands based law firm, specialized in complex litigation. The firm has a particularly strong background in internet and technology law.

As an attorney Alberdingk Thijm is well-known for his work in copyrights, privacy, e-commerce and the freedom of speech.

Mr Alberdingk Thijm has been recognized and credited as a key player within the law and technology field. In 2004 he was elected the best IP/IT attorney of the Netherlands. The reputable Chambers Guide credits him consistently:

2013: "Considered an expert in the field of new media and internet litigation. He also focuses on copyright, e-commerce, privacy and freedom of speech."
2012: “Bright and very reputable. A great guy, both on an intellectual and a personal level."
2011: “He is brilliant and able do the most difficult cases, because he understands the technology as well as the law. He is not easy to outsmart.”
2009: “A determined lawyer who is always challenging the relevant issues.”
2008: “He doesn’t accept every case, but when he does, he really goed for it. He’s very convincing and doesn’t stop until he reaches the finish.”
2007: "A “rising star” of the Dutch IT law scene. He shines in litigation and is an expert in data protection issues and digital work."
2006: "He is consistently on top of the game and has a good feeling for the ever-changing market."

Besides his work as an attorney, Mr Alberdingk Thijm teaches Intellectual Property and Information Law at the University of Amsterdam.

Mr Alberdingk Thijm is a partner at Peak Capital, a technology start-up investment fund that has received funding from the Dutch Ministry of Economic Affairs.
