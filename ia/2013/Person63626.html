Mark McIntosh's Summary

At Jiffy Auto Glass we know, we have all experienced or have known someone who has had to repair or replace the windshield of their car. Often all it takes is a small rock striking our windshield, starting with a circular chip that spreads to a spidery web of cracks. Luckily, most windshield replacements are covered by your car insurance, sometimes up to 100 percent of the cost, based on your coverage.

If it is determined by our technician that you do not need a replacement and you only need to have your windshield repaired, Jiffy Auto Glass professionals can fix most dings or chips. A simple repair can be done as long as the damage is smaller than the size of a silver dollar, including any cracks and is most importantly not be located in the driver's line of vision.
