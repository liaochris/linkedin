Gabriel Flesher's Summary

My entrepreneurial experiences began with my passion for graphic and web design which led me to Mexico almost immediately after graduating from Michigan State University. In the city of Taxco, Guerrero I quickly learned digital photography and began making a living by creating catalogs and websites for many different silver companies, This exposure to the sterling silver jewelry industry led me to start a dichroic glass and silver jewelry production business in Taxco with Jon Hunt. At the peak of our success we had 26 factory workers, a wholesale glass & jewelry store as well as a retail jewelry store in Mexico. Our innovative glass and sterling jewelry creations sold all over North and South America, in several European countries, Japan and Australia.

After 4 and a half years of living in Mexico I returned to the US and worked as a bartender in the evenings while running a graphic & web design company by the name of FLiPP Design during the day. Five years inconsistent earnings followed by marriage and a new family made my decision to make a career change into the world of third party logistics very easy.

In 2009 I helped my father, Greg Flesher, and his business partner, Roger Gumz, start Superior Logistics Solutions. Our company also is the Michigan franchise for QW Express, the third party logistics division of Quad Graphics. In our company I wear many hats; operations manager, IT technician, webmaster, internet marketing, inside sales and direct sales.
