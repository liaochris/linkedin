Derk Simonson's Summary

I know a lot about houses, I used to design and build them. That experience is useful at inspections whether they're La Jolla homes, other La Jolla properties or any homes, or with a Buyer who wants to re-model houses in La Jolla– interfacing with an architect and construction people on homes in La Jolla, or anywhere – looking out for my Client. Consequently, I’m comfortable with the old homes in Mission Hills, or the fancy updating of a La Jolla beachfront condo, or brand new homes in Carmel Valley. This background also comes in handy working with a Seller, getting their home up to speed to stage it.

I work with out-of-town, sophisticated, Buyers looking all over San Diego including homes in La Jolla, and other upscale areas, Pacific Beach, Mission Beach, Ocean Beach, Solana Beach, downtown San Diego condos, Del Mar, Mission Hills, Hillcrest, Rancho Santa Fe, Point Loma, Clairemont, or Carmel Valley. These discriminating Buyers have confidence in me, I’m their age and I’ve been doing this a long time. They instinctively trust me. Many agents are off the grid a lot, off traveling, doing things with their kids, or distracted by all manor of other responsibilities, and so are the agents they’ve asked to cover for them. NOT ME. I’m never off the grid, I’m here minding the store, all the time.

I answer the phone, respond immediately to email and text requests, and get questions answered right away. I’m resourceful, I’ve accumulated resources over ½ a lifetime, and my company, Sampson California Realty is totally service oriented.
