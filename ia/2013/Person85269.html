Marcos Moralez's Summary

I am a UI UX and Visual designer with over 12 years of experience in varied design environments. I have collaborated with project managers, design teams, and developers in startups as well as large corporations to ensure UI and business goals are met in a creative manner.

My experience and achievements include redesigning CBSSports.com from research/concept, wireframes, high fidelity mocks, to overseeing production. In addition, I also designed The Emmy Award-Winning March Madness On Demand application for The NCAA in 2008.

My Process:
Ideation > User Research > Competitive Analysis > Sketch Concepts > Wireframes > Wireframe Prototype > User Testing - Feedback > High Fidelity Mockups > HTML Prototype > Create Style Guide / Interaction Requirements > Oversee Development with an iterative approach

Focus:
User Interface Design, User Experience, and Visual Design.

Additional Specialties:
Lean Methodologies, Ideation, Product Development, User Research, Personas, Competitive Analysis, Usability Testing, Wireframes, Interaction Design, Mobile App Design, Logo Design, SEO, SEM, Social Marketing, Monetization, Growth Strategies, Digital Illustration, Photography, Typography, Audio & Video Editing, CSS, HTML, Analytics

Applications:
Photoshop, Illustrator, Axure, Balsalmiq, Github, Wordpress, Twitter Bootstrap.
