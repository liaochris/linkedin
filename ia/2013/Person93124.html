Michael Donovan's Summary

I am an experienced, passionate and creative UI designer/developer with pixel-perfect attention to detail looking to lead the development of visual design, interaction models, content structure, and help drive larger strategic goals. I love to create clean, engaging, and intuitive online experiences. With a mix of left and right brain, I strive to satiate a customer's desire for "eye candy" while satisfying the underlying business needs for results and revenue. I bring a breadth and depth of experience in both the non-profit and commercial environments that have allowed me to weave together unparalleled digital initiatives based on strong user empathy across varied demographics. I have an obsession with all things interactive that allows me to turn an inspiring visual design and sound strategy into a functional reality with code. I am fluent in HTML & CSS (with a working knowledge of JQuery) and I produce clean, usable, and valid coding frameworks. I keep my practices cutting-edge by attending UX and design conferences, pouring over the latest design blogs and publications, and embedding myself within the global design community. I want to push the web design, UX, and UI fields forward through inspiration and innovation.
