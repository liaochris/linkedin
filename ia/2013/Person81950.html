Liz Olivier's Summary

Based out of New York now, Liz is originally from South Africa where she worked in the Fashion & film industry.

With a love for Make-up & Hair, she followed her passion and has since gained reputable acknowledgement globally for her work over the past 10 years. She is known for her efficiency, creativity, versatility and good nature she brings to the set.

Having worked around the world, she has covered everything from Print to Runway and from Television to Feature Film. As a global artist, she draws her inspiration from these projects combined with her unique experience.

Liz also write Beauty articles on a freelance basis. As a Makeup Artist she works with an array of beauty products every day which gives her an unique perspective on the beauty world when writing.
