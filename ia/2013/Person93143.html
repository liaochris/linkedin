Michael Boyd's Summary

Specialising in Building Information Modelling implementation at a company and project level. A large part of my current role is focused on delivering value to clients with increased innovation through project technology. I am interested in information modelling in a practical sense, starting with the end in mind to produce purpose driven information models allowing clients and design team members to make informed decisions and realise added value on their projects.

In addition to project work I also have internally focussed position, mentoring teams utilising BIM processes and further developing WSP’s capabilities in this field. Assessing and focusing staff training needs and providing internal training where required.

I am also the co-founder of the Edinburgh Revit User Group, an open forum for BIM discussion and software use on construction projects.
