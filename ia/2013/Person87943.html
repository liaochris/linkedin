MARVIN SIEGEL's Summary

As President of Premier Professionals International LLC, MARVIN SIEGEL, CPA, functions as a Chief Liberation Officer for professional services firms who want to “liberate” their high level partners from non-revenue generating activities.

Marv is an experienced, articulate, and insightful financial consultant serving professional service firms, closely held businesses, and family enterprises. As the Founder and President of Premier Professionals International, LLC, he brings a wealth of specialized knowledge in Intellectual Property Law, finance, mergers and acquisitions, and administrative management, and has positioned himself as a trusted advisor for small to mid-sized businesses seeking profitability and growth. Marv is a dedicated student of the corporate culture, having resolved a wide range of operational and financial issues in a career punctuated by executive achievement.

Marv has been a fixture in the C-Suite of top accounting and law firms. Known for his thorough, hands-on approach in managing people and processes, he boasts a stellar history in enhancing organizational performance, increasing profitability while reducing costs, and solidifying key business relationships. He has gained a deep understanding of the factors that go into a client’s decision-making process. “My worth to a firm,” Marv explains, “is the experience I bring in addressing key issues in the day-to-day operations and developing the solution model.”

Marv has a deep understanding of Intellectual Property Law and has served this area with great focus. He commands an excellent working knowledge of patent and trademark prosecution and litigation, foreign considerations, expenses a firm can incur, and its involvement with patent and trademark renewals. Marv seamlessly coordinates all functions attendant to running professional service practices.

Legal and Accounting Professional Service Firms representing various industries.

Marvin laid the foundation for the consultation arm of his practice with the formation of this company. He marketed and delivered consulting services, including serving as an arbitrator in legal disputes, on behalf of a broad range of clients.

The consultancy was established in response to the economic concerns of progressive firms who are reluctant to incur the costs of a full-time executive, and wish to derive value from a comprehensive suite of services on a discrete, as-needed basis, or according to specific project demands.

On an assignment basis, companies wishing to leverage Marvin Siegel’s extensive C-Suite experience, analytical skills, and business acumen will be well-served on all organizational levels.
