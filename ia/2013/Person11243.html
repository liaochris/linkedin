Paul Souders' Summary

Human beings fascinate me. A former anthropologist, I turned my website-making hobby into a web design career nearly 20 years ago. I’ve been an illustrator, art director, designer, developer, information architect and user experience designer. I have worked extensively for academic and non-profit clients, most recently serving for six years as Mercy Corps’ sole web designer. One of my favorite quotes is “computers are like bicycles for the mind,” and not just because I like both computers and bicycles. I believe technology can extend human capacity and inspire joy.
