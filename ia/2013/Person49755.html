Graham Robertson's Summary

At Beloved Brands, we are only focused on only two things: making Brands better and making Brand Leaders better. Our motivation is that we love knowing we were part of helping someone to unleash their full potential. We promise to challenge you to Think Different. We believe the thinking that got you here, will not get you where you want to go. Beloved Brands has a growing reputation for finding growth where others couldn’t. We love to help on turnarounds, brand re-positioning, sustaining success and new launches. Client list includes NFL Players Inc, Earls restaurants, 3M, 649 Lottery, Carlsberg beer and Fluke.

Our President and Chief Marketing Officer, Graham Robertson is a brand leader at heart, who loves everything about brands. He comes with 20 years of experience at companies such as Johnson and Johnson, Pfizer Consumer, General Mills and Coke, where he was always able to find and drive growth. Graham has won numerous new product and advertising awards. Graham brings his experience to your table, strong on leadership and facilitation at very high levels and training of Brand Leaders around the world.

In terms of social media, follow my blog at beloved-brands.com or follow me on Twitter @grayrobertson1 To reach out directly, email me at graham.robertson@beloved-brands.com
