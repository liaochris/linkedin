David Feng (冯大辉)'s Summary

As CTO, Dahui Feng currently works for DXY.com , the largest Chinese medical community site.

Before June 2010, He worked for Alipay.com, the largest online payment service company in China. he managed database team of Alipay and responsible for database architecture designing, including performance tuning and high availability.

Feng is active in the Chinese technology community using the ID of "Fenng", and he's co-translated the book Troubleshooting Oracle Performance(TOP) and co-authored the book Oracle Database Performance Tuning (in Chinese).

In addition, Feng has great interest in open source technology and web architecture. He has wrote a lot of articles about that and help several Web 2.0 sites to sucessfully resolve performance or scalability problem.

He got Oracle ACE award at 2007 and got Oracle ACE Director award at 2009.

For more information, please visit his site: http://www.dbanotes.net

or follow his Twitter : http://www.twitter.com/Fenng , currently has over 26,000 followers on Twitter (@Fenng) and is one of the most influential twitter users from China.

Specialties: Growth Hacking, Technical Team Management, Web Architecture Design and Tuning, Database (Oracle , MySQL), Unix , Open Source, HA Architecture Design.
