Marlin Buckley's Summary

Forensic Analysis
&
Expert Witness



Marlin E. Buckley is well established among attorneys as an effective investigator and expert witness in building construction issues. Specific areas of expertise include plumbing, electric, carpentry, and many other general construction trades.



Analysis of . . .

• Costs
• Delays
• Inefficiencies
• Design deficiencies
• Construction deficiencies
• Personal injuries



A current record of testimony and expert work is available upon request.


I have over 35 years of experience in various positions within the construction industry, beginning with trade apprenticeships in Electric, Carpentry, and Plumbing. I hold active registrations as a Licensed Electrical Contractor / Registered Master Electrician, Registered Master Plumber, and Fire Sprinkler Worker, in Philadelphia, New Jersey, Delaware, and Wilmington. Registrations can be viewed on the various governmental licensing web sites.

Additionally, I am a journeyman carpenter, having specialized in millwork and architectural woodwork.

The tool boxes and bib overalls were retired some years ago, and I now provide experience based consulting services to the industry.

I have held managerial positions at several prominent construction firms, before entering into private consulting practice in 1997.

Specialties

Construction Cost Estimating & Bidding, Owner's Rep Construction Management, Forensic Analysis, Expert Witness, Project Management, CPM Scheduling.

Areas of Expert Witness: General Construction, Carpentry, Plumbing, Electric, HVAC, Construction Management, Construction Accidents

My Firm provides technical services to Builders, Specialty Contractors, Owners, Attorneys, Bankers, Insurance Adjusters, Surety Claims Managers, and others who need construction related technical support.
