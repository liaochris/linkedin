Cyndi Craven's Summary

Two things I love: creating beautifully designed websites that function brilliantly, and making people happy. I've been combining these two passions to "make the Web work" for my clients since 1996.

Today’s professionals and business owners need websites that are of exceptional quality; standards compliant; search engine optimized; able to be controlled and managed in-house; and realistically priced.

To meet this need, I design websites with WordPress, the world’s most powerful and popular content management system. I train and support site owners to quickly become expert managers of their own WordPress websites and blogs. I also manage sites for those who prefer to delegate to a pro, design and build 'regular' (non-WordPress) static HTML websites, and offer turnkey as well as a la carte services to enhance and maintain your online presence... no matter how simple or sophisticated your needs.

My services include:
* WordPress web site design and customization;
* WordPress blog design and customization,
* basic WordPress installation, setup and/or plugin installations (great for for DIY’ers),
* WordPress consulting, training and support,
* Custom web site design (non-CMS/non-WordPress),
* web site and blog maintenance and management,
* custom social media and business networking profiles (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc),
* ecommerce solutions with PayPal and 1ShoppingCart,
* email marketing with Constant Contact and MailChimp,
* audio and video editing and embedding,
* logo design,
* custom a la carte web graphics
* web site and blog hosting,
* domain registration... and more!

Contact me today so we can talk about how we can make the Web work for you and your business (and not the other way around)!
