Dov Hoffman's Summary

Over the last few years through an assortment of professional and volunteer related experiences, Dov has become a well-rounded professional in the Greater Baltimore business community. As a graduate from the College of Business and Economics at Towson University, Dov earned a double major in e-Business and Marketing.

Dov is currently working as a Marketing Specialist, responsible for all marketing activities in support of national operations for COLUMBIA Technologies, a global environmental services firm providing world-class innovative data collection, analyses, visualization, and mapping tools for soil and groundwater pollution.

Prior to his role with COLUMBIA Technologies, Dov participated in The Associate competition, Towson University’s adaptation of the NBC hit series “The Apprentice” and placed second. Throughout this competition Dov worked in a team setting as case challenges were presented on a weekly basis while vying for the title of the “Associate.”

Previous professional experiences have included the development and implementation of sophisticated marketing materials, strategic business initiatives, market research, and managing multiple details for training sessions. Dov has served in various leadership positions simultaneously throughout his collegiate career as he connected with students, motivated leadership, and planned events; overall helping these organizations become more cohesive.

Dov is a strong believer in the following quote from Dale Carnegie: "The person who gets the farthest is generally the one who is willing to do and dare. The sure-thing boat never gets far from shore.”
