Gert Van Duinen's Experience

Brand Identity Designer & Creative Director

Cresk Design

Design industry

January 2002 – Present (11 years 9 months)

Gert van Duinen is a Dutch graphic designer, iconographer and founder of cresk™, a Dutch design agency primarily focusing on exclusive logos and brand identity design.

He got involved into graphic design during the 90′s, as a graphic designer, sign maker, as well as a creative director, iconographer and logo designer. Over the course of his career, he has worked with several leading agencies for a number of globally recognized brands.

It is said that he had been scribbling signs and symbols before he could walk. The intense passion for logos and symbols has been the leading force behind every single design he made to date.

His work has been exposed and published in various media, ranging from logo design books and online design galleries to outdoor signage and design related blogs.

As a reliable self-employed brand identity designer and consultant, he thrives with the most important characteristics in regards to a successful logo and identity design, such as originality, distinctiveness, relevance and memorability. As Leonardo Da Vinci once said: “Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.”
