Joe Ward's Summary

I'm the co-founder and CEO of Viral Age Inc., creators of the Launch.forum platform, currently in alpha development.

My career has been entirely focused on Internet Technology and Marketing and I am a specialist in Web based software development and marketing optimization.

I can instantly evaluate and improve any website to analyze and optimize the business process, and marketing and sales funnel to boost conversions.

I designed a marketing and sales automated system responsible for generating millions in revenue, wrote an email that generated 15X daily sales volume, and wrote a single article that generated over 243,000 hits, leveraged as a vital lead warming article within the sales funnel.

I consistently write email headlines and content that convert, and I've used single email messages to cold leads that resulted in instant conversion of high value projects.

Within Social Media, I am highly effective at engagement. I use persuasive copywriting to network with industry targets, thought leaders, and journalists, for both networking and business goals.

Over 5 years I enhanced my skills in online Community management, once again leveraging my communications abilities, and I was consistently able to turn upset leads into customers and brand advocates.

My use of Marketing best practices, communications psychology, carefully integrated within my specialized understanding of the entire Web ecosystem, allows me to reliably repeat these successful results across many industries.

My focus is always on efficiency and conversion, and delivering maximum value for the customer.

Specialties
