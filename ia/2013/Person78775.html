Laura E. Peterson's Summary

Laura’s professional writing and marketing career spans 25 years. She's produced work for Fortune 500 companies, a worldwide media relations firm, health and technology leaders, and savvy-smart start-ups across all industries.

Her focus and passion with Five Spot Health Communications is to harness that strategic marketing and storytelling expertise into producing game-changing communications for health industry leaders.

Laura's clients today include universities, bio pharmaceutical companies, medical technology startups, health insurance leaders, and doctors who are trailblazers in their fields.
