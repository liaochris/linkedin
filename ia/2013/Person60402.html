JD GERSHBEIN's Summary

Seamlessly blending his background in marketing communications, sales, neuroscience, psychology, broadcast journalism and improvisational comedy, JD GERSHBEIN, CEO of OWLISH COMMUNICATIONS, is blazing a trail as one of the most original personalities in social media. Widely considered a top thought leader on LinkedIn, JD's game-changing insights into personal branding, social networking and communication strategy create true competitive advantage for the individuals and companies that retain him. His value-added process is geared toward enabling a LinkedIn success mindset, inspiring respect-based connecting, and converting LinkedIn activities into tangible, real world business results.

In stressing best practices and sound business etiquette, JD collaborates with opportunity-oriented professionals to build remarkable, fully-branded LinkedIn profiles and assemble deep, cohesive social networks. Deemed a trusted asset by executives and their firms, entrepreneurs, salespeople and professional service providers, he designs educational programs commensurate with learning style, personality, and social media tolerance. JD's strength is in getting people to define their rules of engagement on LinkedIn, set realistic goals in using the site and visualize their success. In so doing, they gain an enhanced feeling of self-confidence that propels them in the business world.

A globally acclaimed speaker characterized by a smooth, storytelling style, razor-sharp wit and wry sense of humor, JD is in high demand at trade shows, conferences, sales meetings, colleges and symposia. Audiences at JD’s talks leave energized, empowered and prepared to explore a wealth of possibilities on LinkedIn. His workshops, seminars and Webinars are considered the platinum standard in LinkedIn training. JD is also Adjunct Professor of Marketing Communications at the IIT Stuart Graduate School of Business in Chicago, where he is advancing social media marketing as an accredited field of study.
