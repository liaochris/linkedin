Jacob Christensen's Summary

I take unmanageable and intangible problems, give them shape and present them in an accessible way.

I know a lot about societies, organizations, change and development. I definitively don’t know everything in these fields, but put great effort into learning the essential facts about the issue I am dealing with.

I am thorough in my work, not afraid to take up new challenges, like to analyze things, am good at creating solutions to problems - and on a personal level I’m friendly and accommodating.

In the past, I have worked with research in social and employment policy, political parties and institutions and taught political and general social science at political science and social work programmes in Denmark and Sweden.
