Lauren Alberti's Summary

For nearly 30 years I have served the community in extensive capacities that include selling DE beach properties, serving on local, statewide and national Realtor committies, serving on community and commerce boards, advocating for stray animals and promoting the political process. It is all about... Lifestyle! My committment to the buyers and sellers that I represent encompases every day, living, breathing and promoting the process of protecting that lifestyle.

The beaches are slowly becoming a go to for year round living. The expanding housing selection, low taxes, clean beaches, tax free shopping, medical services, golf courses and the best of all, tennis are all part of the...lifestyle.

Join in...the lifestyle of the DE beaches, today!
