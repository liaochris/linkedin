Houssem Aoudi's Projects

  • TEDx: Ideas into Action

    • avril 2012 à Aujourd'hui
    Membres de l'équipe : Houssem Aoudi, Daniel Kinzer, Jason Hsu, Ryuta Aoki

    Together with an amazing team of TEDx organizers and curators from around the world, we focused our attention on the inspiring stories of positive change and action that stemmed from local TEDx events.
