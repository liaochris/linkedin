Les Bill Gates' Summary

Do you need an editor or proofreader?
Do you need an article written?
How about a short story?
Do you need a ghost writer?
Or, do you need some technical writing in the area of mathematics?

Whatever your writing need, I can help you.

I have
• A degree in mathematics from Oxford University.
• A post-graduate Certificate in Education.
• A Diploma in short story writing.
• A Certificate of proofreading and editing.

I have taught high school mathematics for more than thirty years and also have a good general
knowledge of the sciences. Together with my recent qualification in proofreading and editing, this potentially makes me an expert at editing and proofreading books or articles of a mathematical or scientific nature.

I have a good command of the English language and have recently finished writing my first
novel. ‘Windows on Our World, Part 1: Hope’, the first of a fantasy trilogy, written under the pen
name of Les Bill Gates, has just been published by Lulu. Visit the page ‘Hope’ to read a chapter
from my novel.

I therefore have the credentials to write, edit or proofread non-fiction or fiction materials, and of
any genre.

In my editing, I bring into play my command of the English language. Whilst always retaining the
original ideas, I can:
• Edit and proofread your documents for spelling, grammatical, and punctuation errors.
• Make style-related changes.
• Ensure accuracy, consistency, and appropriateness of style, format, syntax and spelling.
• Rewrite awkward sentences.
• Make ungrammatical writing readable.

For any kind of creative writing, editing, and proofreading, please don't hesitate to contact me. I
will always approach my chosen projects with enthusiasm, reliability and creativity.

Specialties

My editing and proofreading services go under the name of Editaddition, signifying both my expertise in mathematics and the quality of my service.
