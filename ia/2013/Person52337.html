Owner

Heaths Old Wares Collectables Antiques Bangalow

1990 – Present (23 years)

The catagories do not really allow for our genre of old wares, collectables and industrial antiques.
Heath's Old Wares & Collectables are known for a broad range including industrial antiques, tools and unusual treasures.

Originally established in Ballina, in 1990,
after starting with stalls on the local market circut, we relocated to
12 Station Street Bangalow, 2479, in 2007.

We have been producing a reference list called
'The Local Trail' for nearly 20 years.

'The Local Trail' is our list of like minded shops we are aware of from
Chinderah (near the Queensland border to Ullmara (30 minutes north of Grafton)
west to Casino and Kyogle.

The latest edition in of 'The Local Trail' and a printer friendly version is available by clicking
'The Local Trail' Tab at the top of the page.

Heath's Old Wares, Collectables and Industrial Antiques have been hiring props for movies, television shows, commercials, plays, weddings, photo shoots and displays for over 20 years.

Some of our films include:
The Thornbirds- the missing years,
DrJeckyll and Mr Hide,
Oscar & Lucinda, The Lost World,
East of Everything, Peter Pan,
The Great Raid, Roar
The Phantom, Returning Lilly,
20 Thousand Leagues Under The Sea
The Dragons Prow - Narnia

Set dressings for Paradise Country at Movieworld on the Gold Coast
