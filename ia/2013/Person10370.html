Ash Shepherd's Publications

  • Social Media Road Map: A Social Media Optimization Guide

    • Independant Publishing
    • April 29, 2012
    Authors: Ash Shepherd, Noland Hoshino, Zan McColloch-Lussier

    The Social Media Road Map is your simple guide to developing and implementing a custom social media strategy for your business, organization, or even yourself. With a useful structure, helpful questions and tons of examples it's designed to give you a practical plan to jump start your social media journey. Working through the Social Media Road Map you'll quickly and easily identify your goals, develop messages that motivate your audience and know how to use your time efficiently. What are you waiting for? Pick up the Social Media Road Map, plan your course and enjoy the journey.

  • Social Media Audit

    • Independent Publishing
    • September 1, 2011
    Authors: Ash Shepherd

    In a nutshell a social media audit is meant to be an assessment of your organizations performance in a number of key best practice areas related to social media. It can help identify strengths and weakness and hopefully priorities for moving things forward to a more efficient and effective state.
