Matt Stocking's Summary

Creative Artist and Designer. Visual Communicator. Problem Solver. Passionate about effective solutions and efficient execution.

Production Maven. Blatantly exploiting production resources for pure creative gain.

Though creatively motivated, I am consistently realistic in regards to production time, cost and value. Great execution may improve poor creative, but poor execution will surely kill any creative effort. All effective creative efforts must have a balance between great ideas and realistic, affordable execution.

Specialties: Connecting outstanding, effective creative with efficient, affordable production execution.
