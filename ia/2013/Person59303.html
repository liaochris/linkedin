Jason Koval's Summary

Jason Koval is a 20+ year veteran of the IT/Telecommunications field. As a senior level engineer, Mr. Koval provides his expertise to a wide range of customers in various industries including healthcare, education, government, banking, and manufacturing. He has hands-on, certified expertise with the full line of Nortel’s (Avaya Blue) Enterprise lineup. Mr. Koval’s experience spans project management, vendor management, contract management, and operations.

Specific areas of expertise include contact centers (16+ years designing, building, optimizing, and repairing), acquisitions and merger integrations, workflow and process improvements, audio and video conferencing selection and deployment, and RFPs for voice and data systems, services, and circuits.

Specialties

VoIP, ACD/Contact Centers, SIP, Audio/Video Conferencing, Data Networking, E911, Call Recording/Quality Monitoring, Workforce Management, RFPs, Assesments, Project Management, Unified Messaging, Unified Communications, System Procurements, IVR, Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity Planning.
