Glenn S. Petriello, PHR's Summary

Born and raised in the Bronx, NY, Glenn sought a Jesuit education at Fordham Prep and then studied Human Resources at the University of Scranton. He graduated Cum Laude with a Bachelor’s Degree in Human Resources Studies in 2007 and a Master’s Degree in Human Resources Administration in 2008. After harnessing the alumni network of the University of Scranton, Glenn obtained an internship with Hyperion Capital Management and after 2 years interning, became a full time HR Associate with Hyperion Brookfield Asset Management Inc. in November 2007.

After being a HR Associate with Brookfield Investment Management for 2 years, Glenn was promoted to Assistant Vice President, Human Resources in 2010. Since 2009, he has been the HR Generalist for Brookfield Asset Management’s US employees. On June 21, 2010, he successfully obtained his Professional in Human Resources (PHR) certification.

After attending the 2012 SHRM Annual Conference and connecting with other HR Professionals and HR Bloggers, Glenn became inspired to create his own voice in the HR industry and decided to launch his own HR / Social Media blog, The HR Social, on August 1st, 2012. Most recently, he has become more involved with the NYC SHRM’s Social Media Committee.

In addition to his passion for HR, he has a passion to give back. He is the Sponsorship Chair for the Big Brothers Big Sisters of NYC’s Young Professionals Committee where he leads the corporate sponsorship outreach for their biggest annual event, BIG Night Out.

On top of his career and charitable efforts, he is also slowly starting his own chocolate e-commerce company, Simply Euphoric Chocolate. After taking a few classes at the French Culinary Institute and meeting Jacques Torres in 2011, he knew that he had to share his passion and so on September 9, 2011, Simply Euphoric Chocolate was born. He has made chocolates for various charitable causes and looks to start selling chocolates on his website in the near future.
