Kathy Freeman Godfrey's Summary

Kathy Freeman-Godfrey has over 20 years of experience in identifying, assessing and recruiting top financial services talent. She founded her retained search firm in 1992, focusing her work on senior sales, marketing and client-facing executives exclusively for investment firms, or service providers within that market segment. Since that time, she has personally completed hundreds of assignments across the industry.

Before founding Kathy Freeman Company, she spent seven years with boutique firms in Los Angeles, where she held management roles and conducted searches for a broad spectrum of positions and industries, such as real estate development, manufacturing, financial services and hospitality.

A frequent speaker and writer on talent management issues, Kathy Freeman-Godfrey has been quoted in numerous industry publications such as “FundFire,” “Family Wealth Report” and “I Carry the Bag.” She has presented to the membership of the IAA, 100 Women in Hedge Funds and The Family Wealth Alliance. Kathy is an active member of the International Association for Corporate & Professional Recruitment (IACPR), where since 2000 she has collaborated with both corporate HR and retained search professionals to develop best practices in recruitment. She has also played a key role on the executive committee for IACPR’s Small Search Firm Roundtable, which brings innovative thought leadership to the organization’s boutique search firm members.

Kathy Freeman Godfrey holds a BS in Marketing from California State University, Chico. She lives in San Luis Obispo, California with her husband and two children.
