Michael Ross, PLS/PSM's Summary

Over 25 years of experience in professional surveying and mapping operations. Professionally licensed in New Jersey, Massachusetts and Florida and responsibilities include project management, new business development, financial oversight, employee oversight and operational procedures. Expertise includes topographic surveys; GIS data collection; boundary surveys; right-of-way surveys; machine control data preparation; and construction layout for a diverse group of public and private sector clients.

Member of the Florida Surveying and Mapping Society (FSMS), and former member of the Massachusetts Association of Land Surveyors, New Hampshire Land Surveyors Association, and the New Jersey Society of Professional Land Surveyors.

Technically proficient with Autodesk Civil 3D, MicroStation, Geopak, ESRI ArcGIS, Carlson Civil Suite and various tools for data processing and automated mapping. Currently certified as a Microsoft Systems Engineer and IBM Application Developer.

Recently published online with siteprepmag.com, in the magazine The Rochester Engineer, the construction magazine New England Construction, Cornerstone Magazine, and the journal for highway construction, Highway Builder Magazine discussing Automated Machine Guidance and the Future of Construction.
