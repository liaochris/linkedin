Christopher Jon's Summary

Christopher Jon is a synth programmer, sound designer, and recording/mix engineer originally from New York City. He has an extensive background with both analog and digital synthesis through his original music project I, Parasite. Other credits include synth programming for Cradle of Filth, various synth and mixing work with Android Lust, as well as remixes for Rosetta, Schuldt, Android Lust, Croc Shop and others.

In addition Chris has experience with graphic and web design, t-shirts and other merchandising, packaging, etc.
