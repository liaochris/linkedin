Overview about Igor’s methods

Igor Oliveira’s area of expertise is the analysis of beliefs and decision making process of his clients. When Igor share his perspective, the client will be able to see what are the patters affecting the flow of the his or her life in all kinds circumstances, independently in which area of life the client is facing challenges and/or issues, relationships, emotional, business related, spiritual and so one. Igor believe that external circumstances are created by one's beliefs and choices.Therefore by changing it, one can change the circumstances. It may be a hard concept to grasp at first, but this is one of subjects that Igor will discuss. Igor also work with clients going deep in their unconscious mind releasing unwanted beliefs and pain from past experiences.

To support the analysis of beliefs and decision making process, Igor has done extensive research about human behavior from different approaches and sources. From West to East, shamanic cultures or isolated monastics groups and individuals, humans have universal concerns, motivated for different regional customs, but all humans are in a quest for the same goals, those being well being and peace. This profound studies has given Igor the ability to recognize the patters humans behave in order to attain their goals. Furthermore Igor is a intuitive. Able to grasp what he calls Non-Local intelligence, he obtain precious information.

To guide the client toward change Igor integrated in his coaching an secret Ancient Wisdom that was found in different cultures and religions around the globe. This teachings are still today known and used within secret societies and many others groups and philosophies. Fortunately this Wisdom is not secret anymore and is starting to be recognized by its profound impact. Those teaching are the reintegration of fragmented understanding of the Self and Human Nature by Universal Laws that can help the client bring flow and harmony back to his or her life.
