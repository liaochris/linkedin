Ajit Chouhan's Summary

Ajit serves as Senior Unit Manager - HR Business Partner at Infosys Limited. He is responsible for managing and leading the Business Partner Human Resource (BPHR) team for Business Units to ensure effectiveness and growth, in alignment with the business and HR objectives for the Units (Energy, Services and Communication, Cloud and Business Platforms Groups). His oversight affects approximately 3000 Infosys employees in North America.

He is also serving as Advisor member of the Harvard Business Review Advisory Council, an opt-in research community of business professionals.

Ajit's core expertise includes Performance Management, Compensation and Benefit, Career Development, Talent engagement & Retention, Human Resource Information Systems, Employee communication & HR Branding.

Prior to joining Infosys Technologies Limited, Ajit served as HR Coordinator at FIITJEE Ltd.He was responsible for Human Resource and operations activities.

Ajit attended XLRI where he studied Human Resource Management and earned a Masters degree. He also attended Patna University where he studied PMIR and earned a Post Graduate degree.

Ajit was rated 44 in the list of 175 in list of Top HR online influence's list and also part of Global Top 100 HR trendspotters list. Ajit is a prominent blogger and has many blogs to his credits. His blog HR Funda is featured amongst the Career 100 - a list of the top 100 career related blogs globally.HR Funda also has various online communities which provides exciting platform for HR professionals and students from around the globe for interaction and sharing HR best practices and learning's.

He is also dedicated for the cause of his native state Bihar and blogs on Blissful Bihar, a small community of passionate Biharis who are working towards the Bihar branding and other social initiatives.Ajit is very passionate about Photography, Music, Social & Professional Networking, and Socio-cultural issues.
