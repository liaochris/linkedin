David Cauble, CFP®'s Summary

I specialize in busy people. Specifically, I help them become more effective in two areas:

First, I help people in their professional networking activities through Network Charlotte. I founded Network Charlotte on LinkedIn in 2009. Today we have more than 20,000 members and act as a point of contact for centers of influence in the Charlotte area.

Second, I help busy people find professional management for their investments through Transitions Wealth Management, which I also formed in 2009.

Securities, planning, and advisory services are offered through LPL Financial, a registered investment advisor. Member FINRA & SIPC. For hyperlinks to FINRA and SIPC, please refer to website section above.

• Over 25 years financial markets and industry experience.
• Former board chairman, president, and treasurer of Financial Planning Association of Charlotte (400+ member peer professional organization)
• Past president and treasurer of Charlotte North Rotary
• Former financial planning coordinator - EF Hutton - Charlotte offices
• CFP® licensee since 1985

Other community activities have included serving on UNC Charlotte's Certified Financial Planner® certificate program advisory board, volunteering as a seminar leader for Charlotte Saves, serving as unit commissioner for the Boy Scouts of America, and serving on the board of the Johnston Memorial YMCA.

Third party posts found on this profile do not reflect the views of LPL Financial and have not been reviewed by LPL Financial as to accuracy or completeness.

For a list of states in which I am/we are registered to do business, please visit http://www.transitionswealth.com.
