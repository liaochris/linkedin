Jean-Paul Wong's Summary

1996 to Present. Co-Principal/Owner of one of Kansas City's premier commercial office furniture/interiors firm. Business development, sales management, finance, operations, product design, space planning, & project management.

Current Board of Trustees: Kansas City Ballet (President), Arts Council of Metropolitan KC (Treasurer), March of Dimes-Kansas (Past-President).

Prior to 1996. 2.5 years of public accounting experience with Ernst & Young specializing in tax compliance for corporations and highly compensated individuals.

One year of contract negotiation experience for Payless Shoesource with emphasis on real estate lease analysis.
