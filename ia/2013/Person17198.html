Brian Bennett's Summary

I've had a wide range of experiences that give me a unique perspective on marketing - from large agencies to design shops, to corporate marketing management and as a small business owner. In every instance it has been with companies that are on the cutting edge of creativity and innovation. This enables me and my team to combine smart business strategy with imaginative, integrated and technically advanced messaging.

I get much enjoyment from showing clients the way to marketing success and building businesses by providing that spark of creativity. My goal is to build more relationships and to stay on the very front edge of the art and science of marketing.

Specialties: Heath Care, CPG, Food and Beverage, Retail, Automotive Aftermarket, Financial, Tourism, Industrial / Manufacturing.
