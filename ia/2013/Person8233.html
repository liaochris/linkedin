Anna Tauzin's Summary

When I say "I make the Web more interesting" I don't mean the kind only viewable on a large monitor while sitting at a desk. Users access content on a wide variety of devices throughout the morning/afternoon/night. I design experiences for all of those.

Lately I've been concentrating on ways to help restauranteurs come up with new ways to engage and market to diners - ones that don't involve two-for-one appetizers or a musical website. I believe that restaurants are a cornerstone of any community - where memories are made and life events celebrated. That may sound a little cheesy, but I love my job.

I'm a big fan of serendipity, keeping the logo small and letting stories tell themselves.

Specialties: user experience, social media, public speaking, information architecture, project management, content strategy, mobile application design, journalism, Photoshop, InDesign, video/audio editing, HTML, CSS, PHP, Drupal, Wordpress, Expression Engine, Kentico.

I'm also pretty handy with a dry erase marker and white board.
