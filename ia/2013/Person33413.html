Debbie Sherrie's Summary

I am a Career Coach and Job Search Strategist with expertise in helping job seekers identify and communicate what makes them unique.... memorable..... and marketable in their target market,

As a consultant with international talent development and outplacement companies for 15+ years, I coach my clients to communicate with confidence in writing, in person, in presentations and online:
- Resumes
- LinkedIn profiles
- Presentations, graphic resumes, portfolios
- Elevator speeches, "Tell me about yourself..."
- Audio resumes
- Answering Interview questions, negotiating job offers

With a highly personalized coaching style, I ask laser questions that draw out what you “meant” to say and inspire you to take the next step in your career with confidence. My sales and marketing background at IBM and my corporate communications experience at Wells Fargo have prepared me to find and communicate that "sizzle" behind every product and person!

I've been through my own career transitions, and I know that even the best career communications require a strategic marketing plan to connect to just the right job. My action-oriented plans keep you on track, positive and productive, while integrating the latest job search techniques including personal branding, networking and social media.

As a Certified Toastmaster and Reiki Practitioner, I believe in the power of self confidence and positive energy! ~"Self-confidence is the first requisite to great undertakings," Samuel Johnson ~
