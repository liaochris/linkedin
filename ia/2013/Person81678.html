Lisa Rosendahl's Summary

Lisa Rosendahl, SPHR I am an astute business leader with more than 18 years of professional human resources experience. I develop and lead human resource practices in public and private organizations and with the federal government and provide leadership and expertise to full-cycle strategic HR operations.

I am an active participant in a variety of social networks to build connections and impact change. I am the author of lisarosendahl.com, a blog dedicated to leadership, growth and human resources and the co-founder and editor of womenofhr.com, a community dedicated to women, human resources, leadership and business.

I am recognized as a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) by the Human Resource Certification Institute (HRCI).

I live in Minnesota with my husband and daughter and we hit the road to travel every chance we get.
