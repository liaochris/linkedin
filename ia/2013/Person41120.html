Melissa Wagner-Gens' Summary

Due to demand, I'm excited to announce Emerald Certified Seminars will become an online course to show event professionals everywhere how to offer eco-chic ideas to their clients in all aspects of their event. Being green is something I practice and deeply believe in so I am jazzed to be able to offer this certification to eco-curious event planners from Sea to shining saea and beyond.

We will launch Emerald Certified Seminars on May 1st, 2013 in order to give planners across the globe a solid background on the green movement & spread the word on innovative eco-chic ideas for all aspects of client events. Ask me how you can "Be Brilliantly Green;Be Emerald" today!!

January 2008 – Present (6 years)

Melissa is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) through the Project Management Institute (PMI) as well as a certified Wedding Coordinator through the International Institute of Weddings. She is a current member of both organizations.

Melissa is the resident eco-chic event expert for both EcoPartyTime.com & The Green Parent celebrity blog. She has written and contributed to numerous green articles on event and wedding planning for such magazines as PMI Today, Get Married, & Texas Weddings and Events. Her article "Green Weddings: Save the Date; Save the Planet – A guide to making your day eco-chic" was picked up by Reuters News Service as well as several other eco-friendly websites.

She has been highlighted by Figure Magazine as a female business owner who exemplifies green business practices. Melissa's own wedding was been highlighted in such eco-friendly venues as The Green Bride Guide & Turtle Love Company

Melissa Wagner has quickly established herself through her exquisite eco-chic taste, unique pairing of modern & traditional styles and outstanding attention to detail.
