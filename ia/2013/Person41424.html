Emma Walton's Summary

Over the past 14 years I have built the business to specialise in local businesses both established and start up, mobile technology clients, food and food events, the ethical/ charity sector, art and antiques.

We have taken a number of companies through start/set up to launch and continue to work with them, advising on their ongoing PR and Marketing strategies.

Walton PR is a small consultancy that specialises in companies who require a personal approach to the promotion of their business.

Our aim is to reduce any myths surrounding PR and how it works, helping those with small budgets to achieve their goals and increase the awareness of their business within their target market.

Specialties: Press release creation and distribution,
PR and marketing planning,
Copy writing; newsletters, features, promotional copy,
Media consultancy including social media campaigns
Event management,
Online PR and marketing,
Market research,
Advertising campaign management,
Corporate hospitality organisation,
Photography.
