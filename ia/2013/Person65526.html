Joel Morales' Summary

Prior to ext.54, I spent 10 years leading the marketing, branding and public relations efforts for nationally well-known lifestyle/luxury hotels and restaurants including launching The James Hotel, David Burke's Primehouse, Jbar, and W Hotels amongst others. I have activated innovative programs and events with Lollapalooza, Paul Frank, SmartWater, Audi USA, Mercedes Benz, GQ, Vanity Fair, Moby, Kanye West, GenArt, Sony BMG, Fashion Week NY, and many others.
