Lesley Palmiter's Summary

I've been selling in the neighborhoods of Boston and Metro West for twenty five years. I sell all types of properties at all price points- working with sellers, end users, investors and developers. I take great pleasure in selling New England's unique architectural styles, both historic and new construction.

Real estate transactions can be fraught with challenges and pitfalls. However, I make it my business to maintain positive relationships, stay educated, and deploy both proven and innovative marketing strategies. Bottom line, I provide my clients with a lifetime of experience, preparation, superior service and follow through.

If you are in the market for Real Estate Done Right, call me at 617-275-6240.
