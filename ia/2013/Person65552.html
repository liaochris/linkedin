Joel Zain Rivers' Summary

Illustrator with fine arts education and digital chops. Artist for Comic Books, self-publisher and Writer. I illustrated the children's book 'Beginning Of The Chumash' and helped produce the accompanying film in 2000. I then Published the Xeric-winning Along The Canadian in 2004-2005 with my label Obion Comics.

In 2011 I have joined up with Sillywood, an animation studio in California, as a concept artist and story guy. I am also a co-founder of Portland, Maine-based concept company Out For Justice.

Specialties

Pre-pressing, Illustration, Character Design, Storyboards, Animation, Layout, Writing, Grant Writing, Teaching (College & Elementary), Web Design, HTML, CSS, Photoshop & Illustrator, Flash, Watercolor, Graphic Design and Lettering.
