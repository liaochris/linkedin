Mary F. Hughes-Dado's Summary

Business Writer and Editor

Mary F. Dado is the founder and Editor-in-Chief of Business Writing, Ink. A Manhattan native, Mary is an award-winning writer who runs a dazzling team dedicated to supporting companies with their corporate communications, proposals, and custom copywriting and editing projects.

After over twenty years on Wall Street as a Technology Project Manager and Technical Writer, Mary launched this energetic enterprise that just celebrated its fifth anniversary as a Tampa-based operation.
