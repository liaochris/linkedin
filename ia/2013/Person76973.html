Kristen Stieffel's Summary

You know how embarrassing it is to have someone find a typo after you've printed a thousand copies of a brochure, or after your website has been live for months. My goal is to make you look good by giving your readers material that is clear, concise, and correct. If you're a non-writer, I can help get your ideas out of your head and into print. If you're a writer, I can polish your work till it shines.

Specialties

Writing, Editing, Publishing
