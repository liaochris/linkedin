Jared Miller's Summary

I have over 6 years of Salesforce.com experience and quickly became a Salesforce.com evangelist. My passion is using Salesforce.com to solve problems while bringing efficiencies within a company, increase sales, focus marketing, and stay on the front line of cloud computing and technology.

I also enjoy being very active in the Salesforce.com community. I co-founded the Tampa Salesforce.com User Group 5 years ago and have played an active role since. I presented 2 sessions at Dreamforce 2012 and I have also presented at many User Group meetings, including “Dreamforce To You Florida” events.

I am also the co-host of the ButtonClick Admin Podcast. This podcast has super smart guests who share valuable information about Salesforce. Including; user adoption, the AppExchange, Chatter, being socially engaged, the value of user groups, and tons of tips and tricks.
