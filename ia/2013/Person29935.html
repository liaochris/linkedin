Daniel Skrok's Summary

As a designer, I fully understand the importance of using design to actively engage consumers, rethink branding, integrate new technologies and evoke purpose in peoples’ minds. I have a strong passion for design and diverse skills in traditional, interaction and motion graphic design to bring compelling ideas to fruition.

Dedication, passion and drive have provided me with a Master of Fine Arts in Computer Graphics Design, from Rochester Institute of Technology and a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design, from Daemen College. I have over five years of real-world design experience, providing innovative designs for local, regional and national clients.

Specialties: Traditional Design, Interaction Design, Motion Graphic Design
