Dennis Helsel's Publications

  • The Unofficial Users Guide to ProUCL4

    • Practical Stats ebook
    • December 27, 2012
    Authors: Dennis Helsel, Edward Gilroy

    This Unofficial Users Guide to ProUCL4 is a concise, easy to read guide to USEPA's free software for estimating the UCL95, the statistic used in many regulatory applications. UUG4 is like a “home inspector” when purchasing a home. It looks for areas that are, and are not, performing well. It informs the reader of both so that you are able to best use this software with your data. To make this task intuitive, the book uses the ‘stoplight’ format, grading each menu option as either green (Go: in good working order), yellow (Caution), or red (Stop: not in working order). Most menu options in ProUCL4 are graded yellow, indicating that users will need a guide such as this one to get the most from their version 4 software, and avoid problem areas. UUG4 enables you to make sense of ProUCL4's output, and of your data.

  • Statistics for Censored Environmental Data (2nd ed)

    • Wiley
    • February 1, 2012
    Authors: Dennis Helsel

    How to interpret data with nondetects? Perform hypothesis tests, run regression models and trend analysis. Or just compute a UCL95. First edition was titled "Nondetects And Data Analysis".
