Hannu Hauki's Summary

I am running Qbitech's daily business as well as participating in most of our projects.

Specialties: ICT project management, business management, service conceptualization, software development, programming, usability evaluation, HCI, web design, technology research and studies.

I have been working in the ICT area since May 2003. Between 2003 and 2010, most of my work consisted of varying R&D projects for TeliaSonera Finland, focusing especially on user-generated content and web-based services. My area of work was e.g. writing technological studies, programming, server side configurations, service concept development, and usability evaluations.

In 2006, I wrote my Master's Thesis "Design and Evaluation of Mobile Services for User-Generated Content" for TeliaSonera, as well as achieved my Master of Science degree from Tampere University of Technology. In November 2006, together with my colleague John Bergström, I founded Qbitech Oy that provides ICT consulting and software development services.
