    • December 2008 to September 2010
    Team Members: Mary Closson, John Burke, CPA, Finance Committee

    As a result of economic downturn and closure of the Columbia Gorge Hotel, the Chamber was confronted with diminished revenues. To combat this, I took the following steps:

    2) Increased sponsorship participation; Developed new sponsorship categories for Harvest Fest, Blossom Fest, and annual dinner;
    3) Increased new memberships to an average of 8-10 new members per month;
    4) Developed sponsor categories for Forum Luncheons and monthly Government Affairs breakfasts;
    RESULT: Chamber ended 2009 fiscal year $25,000 in the black; Prior to my arrival in 2008, Chamber ended several of its fiscal years in the red.
