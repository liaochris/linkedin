David Allen's Summary

Property rehabbing contractor and Real Estate Investor based in Boston, Massachusetts.

Current focus is the acquisition of distressed properties for rehabbing and sale to create more access to Affordable work-force housing.

Specialties: Marketing, Home Performance (Energy Efficiency/Renewable Energy) Improvement, Investment Property rehab solutions

Experience as the General Manager of a 27-employee Construction Company, Managing Partner exposure at a successful real estate development company and qualifications and experience in marketing, guest relations and housing management.

Indeed, my experience spans varying exposures particularly in human relations, since graduating with a business degree in 1997. This included a Tax Examiner position at the Massachusetts Department of Revenue, a Front Desk Representative in the Hospitality Industry and a Customer Service Manager at a systems management company.

Similarly, my interest in community improvement has dictated my involvement in various grass root community organizing roles including local political campaigns, Urban League of Eastern Massachusetts etc., all requiring the effective execution of people relation skills. In addition, my Low Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) training and Certified Apartment Manager (CAM) studies are knowledge base that I found valuable to my affordable housing management interests.
