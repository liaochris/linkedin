Rose Ramirez's Summary

We provide:

Residential moves – state, national and international which includes:

* Packing services
* Truck loading and unloading
* Secure storage including climate controlled units for households and businesses
* Commercial moves – for offices of any size

What we will do for you:

* Treat your possessions with care
* Provide a free estimate
* Pack your household belongings
* Load them onto our trucks with care
* Secure them for the trip
* Unload your belongings in the right place at your new residence


Our Relocation Services:

* Free Estimates
* One of our sales representatives will come to your home and set up a moving plan and give you an estimated cost.

Packing

* We have our professional packers, pack up all of your belongings with the uttermost care and get them ready for your driver on loading day.
* Loading and Unloading
* Bekins will have an assigned professional driver for loading and unloading your belongings at the origin and destination. All vehicles are fully insured and equipped with the materials they need for relocating.

Unpacking

* Unpacking is optional at destination. If you choose, Bekins will unpack 1 dish pack box & up to four other early out boxes free of charge!

* If you would like a full unpack of boxes, charges do apply. Any boxes packed by customer (PBO) will not get unpacked. Furniture will be set in areas you want in your home.

Storage In Transit

* We can store your belongings in our climate controlled facility. Until you find your permanent residence. Then you can set up dates for delivery with your coordinator.
