Max Lautenschläger's Summary

Born in 1974, Max Lautenschläger is a professional photographer for magazines and companies. His specialty is portrait photography, a discipline that brings together his fascination with people, his love of lighting and the enjoyment he derives from creating excellent backdrops. In the mid-nineties, Max Lautenschläger moved to Berlin, where he began his career as a photojournalist. He lives in Berlin, with his wife, two childen, and a very old cat.

The pleasure he takes in the exchange of ideas, his fondness for portraits, and-not least-the fun he has setting up complex lighting schemes and creating interesting backdrops have broadened the range of Max Lautenschläger’s work. Today, in addition to journalistic photography, he helps companies develop a visual language to better communicate who they are and what they do. He is experienced in both creating and implementing campaigns.
Behind every successful image, you’ll find good communication: With clients, with editorial staff, with teams of stylists, assistants, and technicians. Longstanding relationships, built on trust, are crucial to producing the expert yet unaffected pictures that are Max Lautenschläger’s signature.
