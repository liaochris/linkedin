  • Therapeutic Education Direction and Intervention: TEDI: Learning Activities for the Special Needs Child

    • Amazon
    Authors: Leslie McKerns, Dorothy McKerns

    Therapeutic Education: Direction and Intervention: TEDI is a program that will bring the young child from the initial traumatic brain injury or acquired condition through the acute hospitalization, into a rehabilitation program, entry into the school system and beyond. The TEDI program allows for the setting of meaningful goals for children with severe disabilities across the domains of cognitive learning, communication and motor skills, and achieving them through a blend of therapy and learning activities.

    Therapeutic Education: Direction and Intervention: TEDI, Volume 2: Activity Kits for the Child with Special Needs: contains The Communication Kit, The Gross Motor Adapted Positioning Kit with specific activities developed for the child with special needs.

    • The Communication Kit: Curriculum Goal: To progress from receptive to spontaneous expressive “speech” or communication, using the most efficient access mode to communication that is possible to achieve.

    • Gross Motor and Adaptive Positioning Kit: Curriculum Goals: To develop body awareness, strength, control, and endurance. To develop and maintain range of motion while engaging in a cognitive and communication development curriculum.

    Each activity in every kit in the TEDI program covers the domains of learning behavior/cognitive, communication and motor (fine motor and gross motor). Important precursor skills and independent skills (such as understanding the nature of the task, following directions, grasp and release, isolated finger point) are developed through the activities. Curriculum goals are also set and progressively achieved through the activities.

    Therapeutic Education: Direction and Intervention TEDI is a six volume program based on classroom learning experiences combined with therapeutic intervention: physical, occupational, speech-language and cognitive therapy.

The TEDI program is a complete program blending rehabilitation with education, providing skill building lesson plans with progressive goals and a means to measure achievement in learning behavior/cognitive, communication and motor skill development. The TEDI program is based on age appropriate classroom learning experiences combined with physical, occupational, speech-language and cognitive therapeutic intervention.
