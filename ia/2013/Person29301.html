Dan Gregoras' Summary

Dan Gregoras is a Creative Director, Senior Visual Effects Artist, and Designer. For the past 12 years he has successfully worked on various high-profile advertising campaigns and commercials for clients such as IBM, Samsung, Microsoft, HP, Adidas, McDonald’s, Cartoon Network, MTV, and Google. Having both the skills of a creative director and technical director, Dan has led high-end visual effects teams in the development of projects from conception to completion and has worked for award winning New York based production studios such as 1stAveMachine, Psyop, Buck, and Aero Films.
