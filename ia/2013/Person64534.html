J Lonnee's Summary

I am a contract Concept Artist and Illustrator currently available for Contract and Full-time work.

The bulk of my work has been with the gaming industry, primarily tabletop and mobile.
Some of the titles I have worked on include Wizkids "Quarriors!" ( ALL card art and packaging ) and MageKnight ( cards and packaging ).
I've also done technical drawings used in the production of multi-part injected models ( Catalyst's Leviathans, Wells Expeditions Arcane Legions ).

My most recent contract was with AmazonLocal where I worked as a production artist.

My goal is to work in-house as a concept artist for game studio.

Specialties

I specialize in Fantasy, Sci-fi, and other geeky kid stuff.
I especially enjoy style development and world building as well as anything relating to miniatures.
