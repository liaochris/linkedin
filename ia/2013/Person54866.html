Iker Marcaide's Experience

Founder and Chief Executive Officer

peerTransfer

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

July 2009 – Present (4 years)

peerTransfer intends to become the lowest cost and most convenient way for individuals and businesses to send money and make payments internationally. Its first innovation is peerTransfer Education, a leading solution that helps international students save money compared to using their traditional bank (up to $2,000 on a $40,000 payment) when paying their tuition internationally. At the same time it helps universities and schools that offer this solution to streamline the reconciling and posting of these payments.

This technology enabled financial services business was born at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and now has offices in US and Europe, has raised nearly $9M from international investors (Spark Capital, Accel and Maveron) and:
- Featured in TechCrunch http://tcrn.ch/oYWdao
- Featured in the New York Times (http://nyti.ms/aW9AWm) and Wall Street Journal (http://bit.ly/aJwGXU).
- Named most promising start-up in the world at HIT Barcelona 2010 (http://bit.ly/d2C9QV)
- Named as one of the 5 big ideas in Boston (http://bit.ly/h0iMAn),
- Finovate Spring 2011 Finalist (http://www.finovate.com/spring2011/)
- Awarded as “Best Consumer Technology That Makes Life Easier” and “Innovative Business Model” at the Massachusetts Innovation & Technology Exchange Innovation Awards (June 2011)
