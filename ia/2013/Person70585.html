Julian Holmes' Summary

Julian has a proven track record in enhancing the software development capability of large and complex IT organisations. These skills stem from the experience of performing many different software development roles over his 20 years working within the industry, providing him recognition as a proven software development leader.

Julian adopted the Rational Unified Process as his approach of choice in 1999, becoming quickly regarded as a leader as to its successful use, and in its deployment for project teams and organisations alike. More recently Julian's work has been focused on helping his clients achieve greater "agility" through the use of Scrum, XP, and Disciplined Agile Delivery.

This agile transformation has been demonstrated most recently by Julian's work with systems integrator, government and financial services clients in the transformation of their organisations into reference sites for large-scale and distributed agile delivery projects, including clients who have had to learn how to collaborate with 3rd-party suppliers for their agile projects.

Julian has also developed his standing within the software development community through initiatives including:
- Presentations at many conference and seminar events
- The promotion and adoption of Disciplined Agile Delivery
- His honorary title as an IBM Champion for Agile
- Involvement in Agile community initiatives
- Articles in industry journals

Julian can bring many services to any organisation, complex, large or small, including his specialities of: mentoring, training, and strategic change planning for the use and adoption of software development practices including those from Agile methods, Iterative Project Management, Software Development Team Leadership, and the supporting delivery tools.
