Diane Keefe's Summary

Professional Geriatric Care Manager with Care Manager Certified (CMC) designation through the National Association of Professional Geriatric Care Managers. President of the Mid-East Area Agency on Aging Foundation. Author, speaker for the Alzheimer's Association. Former Board member of Breakthrough Coalition, former Social Workers in Long Term Care Association member, former chairperson for the Maryland Heights Senior Response Team; former hospice volunteer for Heartland Hospice. Graduated from Webster University with an MA in Gerontology. Speak on all topics related to seniors.

Specialties

Alzheimer's and dementia

Diane Keefe's Experience

President/Author/Speaker/Talk Show Host/Webinar Instructor/Geriatric Educator

Caring for Parents Together

May 2011 – Present (2 years 8 months) Ballwin, MO

Caring for Parents Together was formed for the purpose of providing education to family caregivers about issues, services and resources in the senior arena. As a professional Geriatric Care Manager, Diane Keefe hosts a talk show on BlogTalkRadio.com every Wed. at 4 p.m. with guests ranging from caregivers to professionals in the aging community. The website offers an e-newsletter, blog, and helpful links. Past show links can be accessed on the www.caringforparentstogether.com website and under the Archives of BlogTalkRadio. Podcasts are available via itunes.

Diane Keefe is President of the MEAAA Foundation and a speaker for the Alzheimer's Association in St. Louis. She has published a book entitled, "Blueprint for Care". A companion workbook is also available on the website which facilitates the tough conversations with parents about end of life matters. A DVD is also available: 'Tips From the Experts' featuring interviews with experts on 19 topics.

Diane Keefe's Projects

    • October 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Diane Keefe, Donna Beck, Mary Swan

    Ensuring continued delivery of Meals on Wheels from local Mid East Area Agency on Aging Senior Centers to homebound Seniors 60+ in four counties through sponsorships and donations by various local businesses and organizations. DONATIONS ARE ACCEPTED YEAR ROUND ON AN ONGOING BASIS and necessary due to current and projected Federal and State reductions in funding and growing need. MEAAA Meals on Wheels provides a support to keep Missouri Seniors in the Counties of St Louis, St Charles, Jefferson and Franklin, Independent and living longer in their own residences.
