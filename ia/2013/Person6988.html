Andrew Kable's Summary

Owner, Managing Director and Principal Consultant at H2Otalent, the global search & selection consultancy focusing exclusively on the high-growth, talent-short water sector.

Strategic thinker and business partner, with broad knowledge and a deep understanding of the talent market and business drivers across the full water sector; financiers, technology providers, process risk owners, designers & consultants, builders, operators and natural system managers.

Expertise in water management leadership, with a particular focus on/interest in the leadership implications of applying the complex adaptive system heuristic to integrated water management challenges

Specialties

Executive Search, recruitment and talent management for water & wastewater industry businesses in the Asia Pacific. Career management and job search support for water industry professionals.

We believe:
* that water management is the world's most complex challenge
* that the water industry needs passionate, visionary, innovative and inclusive leaders
* in bringing leaders together to make a difference

H2Otalent is the global water talent search leader

We have several points of difference:

Our primary business model is to enter into strategic partner arrangements with the leading firm in each water industry segment, and work exclusively with those partners to develop their talent based competitive advantage.

We actively maintain a database of up-and-coming talent in the industry, identified by attending conferences, monitoring publications and talking to our trust based network, which then becomes a talent pipeline for our strategic partners.

We are still able to take on strategic partners in some industry segments, so contact me if you are interested in building your talent based competitive advantage.
