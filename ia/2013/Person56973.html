Jaime Derringer's Summary

I launched Design Milk (design-milk.com) in 2006. Since then, Design Milk has received tens of millions of pageviews and has over millions of readers worldwide. Prior, I experimented with a subscription-based online magazine & also owned and operated a successful e-commerce shop and a consultancy for small businesses to assist in Internet marketing/PR, blogging, and new media exposure. In 2009, I launched a sister site, Dog Milk (dog-milk.com).

My experience as a project manager has enabled me to take an idea and organize, execute, and transform it into a success, whether an event, print piece, ad/marketing campaign, or website. I've managed budgets, written/edited articles, designed & produced print newsletters, posters, direct mail, advertisements, websites, logos, & banners. I have acted as a web producer, marketing director, event planner, and managing editor. I have a unique skill set that enables me to be both analytical & creative.
