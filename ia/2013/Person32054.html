David J Sullivan's Summary

Mr. Sullivan is a technology executive who has brought multiple new businesses into the marketplace. His philosophy is to deliver value to customers, partners and investors through relentless sales leadership and customer advocacy. Exceptional results and profitable revenue growth have been delivered via focused execution.

During his career David has achieved two IPOs and multiple acquisitions for emerging companies. In 2005 he launched negotiations that led to the acquisition of Nimcat Networks by Avaya, a $46M acquisition that netted investors a 9x return in 3 years. In 1999 he participated in the IPO of AudioCodes, one of VoIPs early pioneers helping to grow the company from $4M to over $100M. His leadership of global teams at multinational organizations provides diverse leadership experience.
