Hardy Smith's Summary

As a consultant, author, and speaker, my passion is to help maximize the performance of nonprofits and associations and their essential leadership teams.

Organizations benefit from my more than 30 years experience working in the high performance world of NASCAR racing. My in-depth understanding of the needs of nonprofits and associations comes from extensive involvement with nonprofit, volunteer based, and community groups nationwide.

My insights on improving organizational performance have been published in numerous leading publications such as GuideStar, BoardSource, Nonprofit Business Advisor, Canadian Fundraising & Philanthropy, Nonprofit World, The Practicing CPA, Investor's Business Daily, Supervision Magazine, Industry Week, Canadian Facility Management & Design Magazine, Building Women Magazine, The Real Estate Professional Magazine, and FSAE Source Magazine.

Specialties: •Improving performance of nonprofit & association boards
•Volunteer and staff leadership development
•Membership recruitment, activation, and retention
•Creating effective community outreach and marketing strategies
•Strategic planning

Topics for Keynotes and Workshops:

•Why Don’t Board Members Do What They’re Supposed To Do?
•Dealing with Personal and Professional Pressures Created By Change and Challenging Times
•Is Your Nonprofit or Association’s Leadership A Joke?
