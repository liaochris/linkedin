James Gagel, Esq.'s Experience

Owner

Gagel Law Firm - Immigration & Business Law: Coral Gables, Florida

February 1997 – Present (16 years 9 months)

Immigration Law Firm - We represent executives, entrepreneurs, employees of companies, fiances and families to obtain visas, lawful permanent residence, and citizenship in the United States. We also handle petitions for political asylum and applications for waivers before the U.S. Immigration Courts, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), Customs and Border Protection (CBP), United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS), the State Department, and at Embassies and Consulates worldwide. We have no geograhpical limit: we represent clients in all 50 ststes and in all countries worldwide.

Owner

Gagel Law Firm - Immigration & Business Law: Lima, Peru

February 1993 – January 1997 (4 years)

Represented Peruvian nationals as well as foreign investors, entrepreneurs, executives and their families with ties between the United Sates and Peru. Appeared before the U.S. Consulate in Lima, Peru, the United States Immigration Service in the U.S., Peru, and Mexico, as well as before Peruvian government agencies on behalf of U.S. and other foreign investors doing business in Peru. Handled a wide range of U.S. immigration matters, as well as corporate, commercial and cross border transactions on behalf of individual investors and multinational entities.
