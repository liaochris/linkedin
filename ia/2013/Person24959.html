Cindy Hillsey, CPC, ACC ~ Business Coach's Summary

You are in the right place if you have lots of ideas and ambition, are ready to attract your Ideal Clients, and want to grow your business.

I help women build successful businesses!

Entrepreneurs (like yourself) have hundreds of ideas every day. The challenge isn't in coming up with those ideas, but implementing them, right?

You need a coach who knows small business and can help you implement those ideas to grow your business. I enjoy working with women who have lots of ideas, love to try new things, are ambitious, and want to grow their business.

Are you ready to attract your Ideal Clients? Have more confidence in yourself and your business? Then you are in the right place.

Here is the impact I have on my clients:

√ More confidence in making decisions and taking steps toward positive change in your business and yourself

√ Better understanding of your strengths and how to use them to advance your business

√ Improved time management skills

√ More focused vision of the steps and tools needed to move your business forward

√ More clarity around your Ideal Client and WHY this is important to your business

√ More clarity and focus around your dreams and goals

√ Increased productivity and growth in your business

√ Turn those complicated, dense, and overwhelming projects into simple, manageable, and comprehensible steps

I will show you how to monetize what you do, and help you to put a strong business foundation in place.

My background in small business is a strength that I bring to my coaching business. I spent my career working in, and for, small business owners/solopreneurs. I know small business inside and out. I also have a B.S. in Management which couples nicely with my skillset.

Think it! Create it! Market it!™ That's what we'll do together.

Specialties

marketing, business foundation, entrepreneur, small business owner, solo practitioner, price setting, product roll out, setting up a new business, helping you discover your Ideal Client, business, coaches, virtual assistants, VAs
