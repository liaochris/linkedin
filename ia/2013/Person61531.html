Jeff Ello's Summary

Proven technology leadership, delivering elegant innovation for 25 years.

I have been asked to explain the value of IT without getting technical: Do you know the feeling you get when something difficult is unexpectedly made easy? Or the advantage you feel when you gain a capability no one else has? Or maybe you've found $50 in your pocket you didn't realize you had? This is how IT, done well, makes an organization feel.

If your organization doesn't feel like that, you should drop me a line.

ps... I'm also friendly... so drop me a line anyways.
