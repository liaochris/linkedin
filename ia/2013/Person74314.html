Kenneth "Andy" Constable's Summary

An adept and responsive professional within the commercial real estate practice. Skilled in Retail, Office, Industrial and Investment property transactions, development and management. The talent and versatility to manage client relationships ranging from CEO's/CFO's through Real Estate Mangers, independent owners/developers, entrepreneurial start ups, vendors and suppliers, as well as government officials, agencies and commissions. The facility to work cross functionally with an operational understanding of various other disciplines including Corporate Real Estate, Legal, Finance, Accounting, Construction, Operations, Management and Architecture. The ongoing ability to identify and analyze critical information, and to use that information to define strategy for both short and long term investment goals. Prior experience as a successful team-focused leader, utilizing goal setting, training and coaching to produce a motivated and productive leasing team.

Specialties:

* An inventive thinker and persuasive negotiator that excels at imaginative deal structuring, creative problem solving techniques, and closing.

* An articulate communicator and attentive listener that ambitiously seeks, develops and maintains a strong rapport with all clients, team members and staff, while exercising tact and diplomacy in dealing with difficult and/or sensitive situations.

* Exceptional Customer Relationship Management.

* Successful property and tenant assignments from as small as a thousand feet to over 1.2 million square feet.

* The ability to prioritize and re-prioritize activities based on the changing needs of the client.

* Possess a high level of expertise in the acquisition, development, merchandising, leasing, sales and management of commercial property ranging from Strip Centers and Garden Office, through Industrial/R&D Space, Class “A” Office and Super Regional Malls.

* A positive, self motivated, energetic, ethical, transparent, team player and leader.
