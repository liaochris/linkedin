Allan Fowler's Summary

Originally from a print, design and production background I have many years of experience in the Digital Media Industry. From the early days of 5.25 floppy discs through to current formats of Blu-ray and flashdrive.
My career developed in the Film, Games and Music industries, working with the leading companies in specialist packaging, replication, fulfilment, shipping and logistics.
I have worked closely with European age rating and trade organisations as well as sourcing specialist components globally.
I also have my own record label, Dark Angel Records, available for use by independant artists who don't have their own label or a contract.

Specialties

Consultancy and Service Provider for Disc and Digital Media manufacturing, all areas of print, packaging, artwork, design, localisation, fulfilment, sourcing suppliers, age rating, legislation, warehousing and shipping. Problem solving and solutions provision for all your print and packaging projects.
