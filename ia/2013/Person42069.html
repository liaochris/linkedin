Eric Lawrence Frazier, MBA's Summary

Mr. Eric Lawrence Frazier is the Host and Executive Producer of the Power Is Now Online Radio Program. The Power Is Now is a weekly online radio talk show for Real Estate Agents nationwide and is live on www.blogtalkradio.com/thepowerisnow and www.thepowerisnow.com every Thursday from 10:30 AM to 12:00 PM PST. He is a licensed California Real Estate Broker and a Mortgage Banker. He is also the President of the Orange County Realtist which is a chapter of the National Association of Real Estate Broker's also known as NAREB. A Director of the California Association of Real Estate Brokers and the Past President of the African American Association of REO Brokers a National Real Estate Association. His career history includes a total of 31 years in sales management and marketing in the Real Estate, Mortgage and Finance Industry. Mr. Frazier is a graduate of Redlands University and has a MBA with an emphasis in Finance and a BS in Business Administration and Management. Mr. Frazier has been married for 31 years and has four children. Three of which have MBA degrees. He is very involved in the community and is a director and/or member of numerous non-profit boards, and Realtor associations. If you are interested in contacting Eric Frazier his direct number is 714-361-2105.

Specialties: Expertise in real estate and loan sales management, operations, human resources, customer relations, developing loan consultants, business and marketing plans. Extensive knowledge in all types of mortgages – fixed to variable, purchase and refinance transactions, FHA and VA, city and government DPA Programs. I am an expert in real estate development & acquisitions, the development and implementation of winning sales strategies; establishing Realtor relationships, conducting investor and Realtor seminars and public speaking.
