David Christopher Loya's Summary

Directorial/Production Resume: http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0092526/

David Christopher Loya is an award-winning television and film director who has created hundreds of productions for national networks, Fortune 500 companies, international non-profits and prominent educational institutions. His unique "Conscious Filmmaker"® approach to production has successfully been used by educational institutions and international non-profits to raise over $1 billion in donated revenues. David's large body of work has also been recognized by twenty national Telly Awards as well as honors at the New York Film Festival and the Banff World Television Festival.

A partial list of past production clientele includes: Sony Pictures, The Walt Disney Company, CBS, NBC, ABC, MGM Interactive, Arts and Entertainment Network, The History Channel, PBS, TCI, Liberty Media, Ameritech, Gateway Computers, Harvard University, The Mayo Clinic, Bravo Channel, The Smithsonian Institute, Black Entertainment Television, National Medical Enterprises, Genesis Entertainment, Wordnet Productions, William M. Mercer, Inc., The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, Microsoft, University of California, Los Angeles, The Cleveland Clinic, Amnesty International, The Hallmark Channel, Sprint International, Beckman Coulter, Lockheed, Aramark, Sunkist, Prudential Insurance, Archdiocese of Los Angeles, Family Theater Productions, The Russ Reid Agency and The Salvation Army.

David is also President and a principal of Vision4Media Entertainment - a motion picture production company focused on creating movies with timeless themes and meaningful subtexts. Vision4Media produces films the enrich the soul of an audience, while providing solid ROI to its production partners.

David is now attached to several motion pictures in late development as both a director and producer. See his IMDB profile to learn more: http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0092526/

Specialties

Directing for film using the registered trademarked “Conscious Filmmaker®” approach to production.

Directing actors on-camera to achieve conscious and absolutely authentic on-camera performances.

Directing Interviews through an "Active Listening" process designed to elicit desired responses and soundbites.

Participates in and presents at the major international film festivals and markets including Berlinale (EFM), Festival de Cannes (Marché du Film), TIFF (Toronto) and AFM.

  • BEYOND THE MOONWALK - Documentary Motion Picture Promo and Mini Series Demo Pilot

    • June 2010 to Present
    Team Members: David Christopher Loya, Keith Myers, Coraly Babin, Geron Candidate, Daniel Andreas Pfisterer, Paula Moreno, Sarah Bojorquez, Jim Axl Siler, Brian Montes

    Beyond the Moonwalk is a must-see compelling, pop culture odyssey that reveals the untold story of the amazing, but un-credited street dancers who not only taught Michael Jackson "The Moonwalk" and other moves; but then went on to directly influence the greatest performers of our time. These are many of the same dancers appearing next to Michael on all his music videos. Today these artists remain largely mired in obscurity. However, their untold phenomenal influence on today's hip-hop, pop-music culture is unmistakable. Michael Jackson wasn't the only superstar whose performances were derived from the original dance moves by these unheralded artists. What's more, the legacy of these dancers can be traced to the evolution of a street movement that began as far back as 1970. Beyond the Moonwalk is a high energy, fun, cinematic quest that tells a four decade long story of a pop culture we thought we knew, but in reality was sculpted by the most unlikely of heroes. And, it's more than a single movie. Beyond the Moonwalk is a branded entertainment franchise that includes a docudrama motion picture, a documentary, a six part mini-series and ongoing ancillary properties .

  • BEYOND THE MOONWALK: A Dream to Dance (Theatrical Feature Film now in Pre-Production)

    • September 2011 to Present
    Team Members: David Christopher Loya, Paula Moreno, Brian Montes, Coraly Babin, Danijela Jasprica, Gary Bickal, Lisa A. Cervantes, Mike Doban, John Michaels, Darby Angel, Sarah Bojorquez, Keith Myers

    BEYOND THE MOONWALK: A DREAM TO DANCE is the true untold story of the street dancer who taught Michael Jackson and transformed pop culture history. BEYOND THE MOONWALK delivers the dynamic account of a fatherless, inner-city gifted teenager who earns money competing in nightclub dance contests during the late 1970's. Capturing the attention of a prominent music producer, the young man is thrust to the national forefront as a featured Soul Train dancer. His first performance in 1981 catches the eye of Michael Jackson. The next day he's hired to teach the prince of pop the moves that make him King - and that's only half the story...

    BEYOND THE MOONWALK is an explosive pop culture odyssey - a celebration of an era featuring some of the greatest music and dance the world has ever known. Just as the films Saturday Night Fever and Grease changed the cultural landscape of the seventies and reverberated for decades to come, BEYOND THE MOONWALK: A DREAM TO DANCE promises to be one of this generation's event movies, ushering in a new-found interest in the sounds, the moves and the people who laid the groundwork for today's soul, rock, pop and hip hop world.
