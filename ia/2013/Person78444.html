Larry Kramer's Summary

Larry Kramer brought 27 years of finance, accounting & ops experience in joining Foundation Management Associates as a Managing Partner in 2012. Prior, he was the $3Bil North Bridge Venture Partners & Growth Equity's Controller 2004-2012. Before that, Larry was VP-Finance & Controller for TWI Interactive, the $30Mil technology arm of the $1Bil sports and marketing giant IMG. He modeled and assisted in the buyout of the then majority external shareholders to allow TWIi to reintegrate with its parent IMG in NYC. Before TWIi, Larry spent three years as the Controller of NewsEdge Corp., a $70Mil public co. and leading provider of syndicated content solutions/services, which he assisted in its 2001 sale to and integration with Thomson down to Cary, NC. Before NewsEdge, Larry spent ten years at the $325Mil public mutual fund company The Pioneer Group in a variety of accounting and finance roles. He began his career with GE and GE Capital, completing GE’s Financial Management Program in upstate NY.

Larry earned an MBA with honors from Boston University and a B.A. with honors from Rutgers University. Outside of work, Larry served 2006-2009 on the Boston Chapter Board of Financial Executives International (FEI), an 80 year old national organization of senior financial professionals, ran its Career Services/Career Planning Committee to help others and remains an active committee member. Also outside of work, Larry is lead singer and fronts not one but two suburban Boston cover bands whose ten charity fundraising events since 2009 have raised over $87,000 for The Crohn's & Colitis Foundation (CCFA), Pediatric IBD Foundation, and Medfield, Ashland, Groton and Norton town public school fundraisers. His bands have also entertained 20 other paid and free concert audiences. Larry has worked with CCFA's June 2010-2012 Boston Take Steps Walk Organizing Committee on its $325K/1,000 person annual fundraising walk events.
