Mikaële Holzer's Summary

Mikaële is a Certified Hippocrates Health Educator, Health & Nutrition Coach, Feng Shui Consultant and Graphic Designer.

She is also building a huge successful business around the world through Lifemax and Mila with a fabulous team of independent distributors.

http:www.lifemax.net/mik

Co-Founder of Green Your SPIRIT, a global movement and lifestyle company focused on vibrant health, cleansing and empowerment for your inner AND outer environment.

http://www.greenyourspirit.com

Co-Founder of The POP Campaign with four primary focuses: keeping our voices and choices alive, keeping organic standards pure, keeping ourselves and others informed and educated and taking action to preserve organics from pasteurization, irradiation and more...

http://www.popcampaign.org

For over 11 years, Mikaële has worked with clients from New York to California and travelled the world studying with indigenous cultures and other teachers gathering tools for her self and her clients. Her passion is facilitating clients to detox, cleanse and rejuvenate their physical bodies and minds through live-nutrition, bodywork and whole life coaching,… and their homes and offices through feng shui and organizing.

As a graphic designer and creative director she loves working with start-ups and small businesses to create a corporate ID that helps their business shine. In her spare time, she is a singer/songwriter and member of SAG!

Mikaële guides people to have fun while manifesting their dreams. She invites you to step into the “New Earth Culture and Playground” that is emerging, where “Sustainable Radiance” and financial freedom is the reality!

Specialties

Nutrition Consulting and Cleansing, Branding and Corporate ID, Start-Up packages.

Helping entrepreneurs build their successful home based business in the health and wellness industry!
