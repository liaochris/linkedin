Carrie Stack, M.Ed.'s Summary

Carrie Stack, M.Ed. is a Certified Life Coach, author and motivational speaker with over 20 years of experience providing people with skills and tools to make positive changes in their lives. Founder of the Say Yes Institute (SYI), a company focused on building emotional intelligence skills through training and coaching, she has shared her “people skills” strategies with thousands looking to build more positive and powerful relationships at home and at work.

Carrie is the author of the newly released book, The Dream Boss: Powerful, Positive, Professional. She is also the author of, Conversations with the Future, and a featured columnist for Achiever magazine. She was named in “101 Women to Watch” by WE Magazine and featured on 7-NEWS, and FOX 25, as well as in the Boston Globe, Boston Literary Magazine, Lynn Daily Item, and North Shore Sunday. She is also founder of the nonprofit Courage2Know, co-founder of EO Your Life, and a contributor to EmpowermentRX.

Carrie Stack, M.Ed.'s Publications

  • The Dream Boss: Powerful, Positive, Professional

    • My Life List Publishing
    • October 18, 2012
    Authors: Carrie Stack, M.Ed.

    Being in charge takes a lot of energy and investment if you want to do it well, but it doesn’t have to be the endless source of stress and misery it seems for so many! The Dream Boss gives leaders concrete skills and tools focused on building emotional intelligence skills to help them successfully inspire teams to go to the next level. The Dream Boss activities and content grew out of workshops and trainings with thousands of people looking to have powerful, positive, and professional relationships in their world. The models in the book can be used with teams of all sizes, and applied in a business, a nonprofit agency, or a volunteer group. These skills/tools have worked for people leading for decades, as well as for new supervisors with only months on the job. The Dream Boss is in an easy-to-read, easy-to-use format, enabling leaders to start using the tools when they need them, now!
