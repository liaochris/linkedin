Erika Shannon-Hathaway's Summary

Erika Shannon is a passionate, New York-based dancer, choreographer, and coach. Her upbeat teaching style has earned her features in Glamour Magazine and Gals Magazine, and her followers bridge the gap between the dance studio, the stage, and the fitness club.

A seasoned performer and consummate student of life, Erika firmly believes that education is paramount. She holds a B.F.A. in Musical Theatre from The University of Michigan, where she first learned that practice and persistence unlock the door to success. Combining her education with her managerial experience, Erika co-founded MTCollegePrep.com, a coaching program for high school students who want to go to college for Musical Theatre. Now in its second year, MTCP is the only college prep program that connects clients directly to successful Broadway performers and nurtures each client not just to get into school, but to be prepared for life after college as well.

Erika is ready, willing, and able to teach your next master class, choreograph your next production. She conducts one-on-one coaching sessions virtually.
