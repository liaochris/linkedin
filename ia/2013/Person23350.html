Chris C. Hau's Summary

Celebrating 9 years in the Real Estate business!


Your Trusted Professional Real Estate Advisor

Chris C. Hau, Broker Associate | Partner with The Group, Inc. Real Estate, specializes in residential real estate sales and commercial real estate including the sale and leasing of investment, office, retail, warehouse, and industrial property. He is also experienced in 1031 Exchanges.

Chris is a member of several industry organizations including the National Association of Realtors, the Colorado Association of Realtor, the Fort Collins Board of Realtors, and the Northern Colorado Commercial Association of Realtors. He is enthusiastic about continually building his real estate knowledge and experience, and is pursuing several professional designations including Certified Residential Specialist (CRS), Graduate of the Realtor Institute (GRI), and Certified Commercial Investment Member (CCIM). Most recently Chris has become EcoBroker Certified and a Certified Negotiation Expert (CNE).

Chris has closed over 159 transactions exceeding $31 million since obtaining his real estate license in 2005. He is very knowledgeable in Internet based real estate technologies and data base systems.

Chris is a native of Fort Collins and graduated from Fort Collins High School in 2001 and attended Colorado State University. In 2010 Chris played hockey for the Colorado Eagles.

While holding myself to the highest professional, technical, and ethical standards, I will provide my customers with expertise, honesty, and the highest level of service. I will strive to help my customers make prudent real estate decisions and in the process prove worthy of their trust and friendship, so that I may become a long term friend and their trusted real estate advisor.
