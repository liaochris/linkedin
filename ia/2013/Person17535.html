Brian Lee White's Summary

Brian Lee White is an Oakland based producer, mixer and educator. As both an engineer and composer, Brian’s work has been experienced worldwide, on major label releases, billboard charts, network television, fortune 500 ad campaigns and in internationally acclaimed live acts. As a founding partner of Finishing Move Inc., and composer for Everstudio, Brian’s production work encompasses a broad range of clientele, from independent to major label artists, television and film companies, and a variety of international clients world wide. A prolific Lynda.com author, Brian shares real world industry experience and craft via engaging online training titles on a variety of audio production topics. As an Avid certified expert trainer, Brian teaches Pro Tools in the Commercial Music Program at Ohlone College in Fremont, CA.

Please visit brianleewhite.com and finishingmoveinc.com for more information

Invitations to connect: If I don't know you, please include a personal note with your invite. I do not accept un-recognized connection requests.

Specialties: music production, music composition for film/tv/ad/game, music technology, instruction, educational curriculum and content development, tech writing, mixing, artist development, song writing, pro tools.
