Charles Wolford's Summary

Experienced content executive with extensive training at top creative advertising, entertainment, and commercial production companies in the US and Europe/UK. Dedication and focus on creating provocative and effective content in all media.

Known for producing complicated and award winning work for US, pan-European, and worldwide campaigns on behalf of a diverse client list. Major shoot experience throughout the world in a diversity of locations and situations. Budgets range from smaller tactical shoots through multimillion dollar international brand projects.

Management experience includes building departments from the ground up in addition to isolating and developing talent into creative and well-trained Content staff.

Supervision of Content Production, Business and Legal Affairs, Interactive, Technology, UX, as well as in-house editorial, tactical production, and post facility groups. Specialties include broad knowledge of director, celebrity, and music licensing contracts.

As a Content Production Executive & Consultant, specialized in evaluation, design, implementation, and recruiting of key personnel for Integrated Production groups designed specifically for various communication environments.

Experience and analysis helping organizations deal with rapid growth and/or restructuring.

Extensive brand experience: sports, fashion, automotive, beverage, food, financial, government, entertainment, packaged goods, network, theme park, technology & public service. Skilled at developing and maintaining strong client relationships.

Born in CA, raised in small town Texas, having lived and worked all over the mainland US, with stints in Europe (Amsterdam) and Asia (Thailand).
