Mark C. Crowley's Summary

My Mission Is To Fundamentally Change How We Manage And Lead People In The Workplace. This is because traditional leadership practices are failing:

Conference Board research shows that worker happiness has been declining for 25 consecutive years, in good economic times and bad. Today, 53% of U.S. workers effectively hate their jobs. New Gallup studies not only validate that people feel worse about their work, bosses and organizations than ever before, they reveal an astounding 70% of Americans admit to not being engaged in their jobs – or to being actively dis-engaged.

So, what will it take to reverse this trend and fully restore employee engagement, loyalty and productivity? I’ve discovered that the path to inspiring 21st Century workers is through their hearts.

If an alarm is sounding inside of you right now, it’s likely because you’ve been influenced to believe bringing the heart into workplace leadership is an inherently weak approach to driving engagement and profit. But that’s simply not true. Recent scientific discoveries prove the opposite. THE HEART IS THE PRIMARY DRIVER OF OPTIMAL HUMAN PERFORMANCE. Feelings and emotions drive human engagement.

By no coincidence, Towers Watson research shows that the greatest driver of engagement WORLDWIDE is whether people FEEL their managers and organizations demonstrate genuine concern for their well-being. And, in their new best selling book, “The Athena Doctrine,” Pulitzer Prize winning author, Michael D’Antonio, and John Gerzema surveyed 64,000 people in 15 countries before concluding that effective leadership in the 21st Century requires a balance between masculine and feminine traits and values – equilibrium of mind and heart.

As a former senior executive (Leader of the Year) at one of the nation’s largest financial services companies, I’ve proved my thesis is a more far more productive leadership model for the 21st Century. I invite you to read this recent article to learn more: http://bit.ly/11wO3jF

    • Hay House

    Workplace leadership in the 21st Century is failing.

    Job satisfaction in America has steeply and steadily declined for over a generation. Today, an astonishing 71% of all workers admit to being un-engaged or actively dis-engaged in their jobs. Never in modern history have people been so unhappy with their work, bosses and organizations.

    What's needed is a new leadership model for a new age.

    The needs of employees (human beings) have greatly evolved since traditional leadership theory was established a century ago. Pay no longer ranks tops in importance to most people as motivation to do great work.

    In Lead From The Heart, I present compelling evidence that the path to re-engaging and re-inspiring workers is through their hearts. Profound new scientific and medical research proves it's the heart -- and not the mind -- that drives optimal human performance.

    For people to truly thrive in their jobs in the 21st Century, they need to be made to feel...valued, respected, developed and cared for. Their work has to matter.

    The results in business are clear. When we lead from the heart, people not only follow, they accomplish the most remarkable things.
