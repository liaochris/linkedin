Michelle Maskaly's Summary

I'm an East Coast-based multimedia journalist, social media specialist, editor, media consultant, lifestyle writer and pet lifestyle expert, who serves as the editor of Pet Age magazine. In this capacity I am responsible for all editorial content both digitally and in print, social media strategy, marketing and the overall production of the magazine.

With more than 13 years of journalism experience in newspapers, TV and web, I am also proficient in social media, as well as, public relations, marketing and general media. I also have some experience working on political campaigns. I've spoken at national and regional journalism conferences, frequently participate in social media panels, trained companies and small businesses on social media tools and has worked on regional and national social media campaigns.

My skills have let me work with a diverse group of industries, from chambers of commerce and non-profit travel events to sports and pet websites, as well as small businesses and law professionals.

I founded the nationally recognized pet lifestyle website My Tail Hurts From Wagging So Much and lifestyle, food and travel website, The Adirondack Chick.

Specialties: social media, multimedia, interviewing, street reporting, social networking, crime, talent, branding, writing, pet lifestyle, marketing, pet trends, freelance, pet products, journalism, brand ambassador, trade shows, expert,
