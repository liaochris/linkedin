Joel M. Leyden's Summary

Joel Leyden = Digital PR / social media pioneer, digital communications strategist, journalist, crisis communications, reputation management, integrated marketing, branding, B2B, B2C consultant, SEO - search engine optimization, viral marketing, public affairs.

Joel Leyden - Specialties: Social Media, digital PR, journalist, crisis communications, B2B, B2C consultant, branding, integrated marketing, SEO - search engine optimization, social media, reputation management, viral marketing, public affairs.

Joel Leyden's professional media, PR and public affairs work has been featured in TIME, CNN, The New York Times, FOX, AP, Washington Post, Reuters and several other leading, global media.

Joel Leyden has worked in North America, Europe and the Middle East. Leyden also serves as a media consultant / coach preparing clients to interact with local and international media.

Joel Leyden has served in uniform for the IDF Combat Engineers, MAGAV Anti-Terrorism Unit and as an officer in the IDF Spokesperson's Department. He has consulted the US State Department regarding digital media. Leyden has worn a police uniform in both the US and Israel and has worked closely with a wide varity of security organizations.

Joel Leyden is the proud father of three children and is involved in non-profit work for disaster relief and the homeless.
