James Haltiwanger, Jr.'s Projects

  • Disclosures and Their Effect on Consumers Intention to Trust

    Team Members: James Haltiwanger, Jr., Jennifer Siemens

    Individuals working in investment-related industries have come under increased scrutiny due to the recent financial market and economic turmoil. This has led to increased regulation and oversight from The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and the passing of legislation such as the Dodd-Frank Act targeting Individuals in positions of trust; such as Investment Advisory Representatives and Registered Brokers. As a result there has been an increased emphasis on disclosure from these Individuals that includes information about their customer disputes, disciplinary events and financial matters. The goal of this public disclosure is help individuals make informed decisions when choosing an Individual(s) to manage their investments. In the past Individuals seeking information could find it via online databases and by contacting a federal or state agency. With the passing of the Dodd-Frank Act Investment Advisory Representatives are now required to provide this information annually to their clients.
