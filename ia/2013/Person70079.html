Juliano Tagliaferro de Oliveira's Summary

I have been working as Quality Supervisor, coordinating the quality control department of a big Brazilian company, Windmill blades manufacturer with 10 manufacturing facilities, 4.500 workers and annual sales of US$350,000,000.00.
I manage the maintenance of company’s quality management system based on ISO 9001:2000, by internal and external audits, Germanischer Lloyd (GL) audits and British Petroleum (BP) audits.
Also I work as Project Manager using PMBOK concept from PMI (Project Management Institute) for internal projects.
As PM, I have been planning and coordinating Company’ strategic projects by executive board guidelines.
I worked as internal consultant for other departments’ planning projects.
As Green Belt I worked with continuous improvement projects.
I have great experience working six years as Production Manager with ATO (Assemble-to-Order) system, managing Production Control & Planning, Engineering and Product Development, Logistics and Supply Chain Departments.
I was the director of Industrial Management Group (IMG) in order to implement and maintain management programs as 5S, Kaizen, etc.
I also trained workers and team leaders for lead courses, quality management courses, IT courses and internal audit courses.
I coordinated technical application of Drum-Buffer-Rope methodology in operations management using Theory of Constraints (TOC).
I coordinated implementation of CAPP (Computer-Aided Process Planning) developing applicative for fusion of systems CAD (Computer-Aided Design), CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing) and ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning).
I also implemented and developed CIM (Computer Integrated Manufacturing) technology for production system integration by IT.

Specialties
