Lex McKee's Summary

I am a VIP: a Vital Ideas Person. That means I can find multiple ways to move you and your organisaiton forward towards your goals. All my enterprises are focused on one 'vital' outcome: to promote a 'life to the full' = to enjoy life more abundantly and to prosper more sustainably.

I believe that transformation is easily possible through the adoption of fresh patterns of thinking (Vital Ideas), backed up by a commitment to action, supported, in turn, by passionate resilience and perseverance. Inspiration is the first key - everything starts with an idea whose time has come.

My proprietary approach to Accelerated Learning is called Entertrainment® for good reason - we learn faster and at a deeper level when the emotional brain is engaged - you can learn longer while laughing.

The vision for my studio work is to be THE Producer that inspirational speakers make their first choice to work with for recording their audio, video, and online key messages. By bridging the worlds of Accelerated Learning AND Media Production, I am in an unique position to help you articulate your message in a way that will inspire and stick. Photography is one of my major passions, so I will work with you visually to help you capture the imagination of your audience.

Living Organisations® is my mission to transform the workplace. I think work should be somewhere you want to spend your time, don't you? I can work with you to make work a better place to be.

Specialties: Vital Ideas, Living Leadership, Creativity, Accelerated Learning (and it's applications), Audio Recording and Mastering, Video Production, After-Dinner and Conference Speaking, Brand Marketing and image development through photography and other visual arts.

Freelance Author

Homme de Plume

September 2013 – Present (2 months) Dorset

Homme de Plume is my fresh new venture, allowing you to release that book that been sitting on the back-burner for too long! I work with people who don't have the time to write their own book. The usual process is for me to record our conversations and then to put your words in print. In this way, it really is your book, your ideas, expressed in your way. I just remove all the hassle and time it takes to transform an idea into writing.
For those wanting to hit multiple markets I can also help with the production of audio and even video versions of the materials.
Non-fiction content can be crafted into training programmes to spread your unique message.
