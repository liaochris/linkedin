Invent Help's Summary

Ideas and inventions come from individuals in all walks of life. At InventHelp, we believe these individuals are a great source of creativity. In fact, it may surprise you to know that an overwhelming percentage of the 20th century’s greatest inventions came from individual inventors, not corporations.

As America’s leading invention firm, InventHelp has several value-added services to offer for individual inventors and for companies looking for new products.

InventHelp’s inventor services are geared toward attempting to submit clients’ inventions or new product ideas to companies for a good-faith review. We also refer clients to independent patent attorneys who may assist you in applying for a U.S. patent. Due to the volume of work that we refer to patent attorneys and agents, our inventor clients are able to obtain very low rates for patent services.

Additionally, each year InventHelp holds the Invention & New Product Exposition (INPEX), America’s largest invention trade show. INPEX is a resource for both inventors and companies. Inventors can exhibit their inventions and attempt to make contacts with companies interested in new product ideas; companies can search for new products or attend seminars on Open Innovation.

InventHelp can provide further support for companies looking to add to their existing product lines. Your company can sign up in our InventHelp Data Bank to receive invention ideas on an ongoing basis, and we can search our invention resources for specific products that meet your interests. If you happen to find something you like, the licensing professionals at our sister company, Intromark Incorporated, will step in to negotiate a possible licensing deal as well as manage the relationship between your company and the inventor.

For more information about InventHelp and the services we offer for companies and individual inventors, please visit www.InventHelp.com.
