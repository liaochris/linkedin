Mallory Bower's Summary

EMPLOYERS: Looking for fresh talent? I am the employer relations coordinator at SUNY Oswego Career Services. Connect with me here—let’s see if we can help each other out!

SUNY OSWEGO has more than 8,000 talented students who are ready to roll up their sleeves and get to work. It’s our goal to find them fulfilling internship, co-op, and full-time opportunities with organizations like yours.

STUDENTS who are job searching or trying to meet new people are encouraged to connect with our SUNY Oswego Career Services group on LinkedIn. Network with over 600 employer, staff, and alumni group members. Also feel free to connect with me—I would love to introduce you to some great folks!

MY SUMMARY: I am a university career coach and "friendraiser" with a penchant for finding out what makes people "tick." In my work, I believe that connection, curiosity, and storytelling are influential vehicles for career success.

My professional experiences include career counseling, college-level teaching, programming, and supervising roles. In my work, I teach students how to communicate effectively in business environments, create individualized career plans, and create positive online identities. On the flip side, I build meaningful relationships with employers and alumni who are interested in connecting talented students with exciting career opportunities.

Remember when I said that connections influence career success? Well, I would love to connect with you here. Please include a note with your request and let me know what I can do to help you make strides toward your goals.

Specialties: Employer Relations, Career Counseling, Resume and Business Writing, Teaching, Technology and Career Services, Social Media
