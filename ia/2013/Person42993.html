Simon Kemp's Summary

Hello, I'm Simon Kemp, Managing Director of We Are Social in Singapore. We are a leading global social media agency that helps brands to listen to, understand, and engage in conversations across the spectrum of social channels.

We work with clients from a variety of industries to:
• build vibrant social communities that deliver on-going value to both audiences and brands;
• identify key influencers, and build lasting relationships with them;
• promote brand advocacy and positive word of mouth; and
• understand the wider business and societal impacts of social media, and what these mean for organisations and brands.

You can find out more about us at http://wearesocial.sg

I'm a contributor to industry-leading publications like SocialMediaToday and The Wall, and I regularly speak about Social Media at conferences and corporate events

Apart from We Are Social, I'm alter-ego to @djeski, the world's biggest bedroom DJ, and I'm also a huge fan of street food. You'll find me as eskimon across most of the social web.
