Mark Graffagnini's Summary

Mark is an attorney licensed in Louisiana and California with a variety of legal and financial experience.

Mark is currently a member and principal of Maximum Generation, LLC, a leasing and consulting firm. While capable of leasing all types of equipment, Maximum Generation is focused on combined heat and power (CHP, also known as co-generation) and other types of distributed generation projects. Opportunities to provide equipment leasing services in a variety of areas, particularly CHP or co-generation are welcome.

Mark's legal experience has included representations in venture capital financings, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), debt collection, lender liability and creditor's rights, corporate governance, UCC and secured transactions law, leasing law, LLC formation and operation, and commercial litigation.

Mark has written and spoken on topics in the CHP industry as well as on topics in corporate finance and advanced materials companies. Opportunities to speak on or publish materials related to nanotechnology, venture capital/corporate finance, CHP, co-generation, environmental revenue streams or clean technology are welcome.
