Chris Baker, CPA's Experience

President

Baker DMM, LLC

May 2012 – Present (1 year 3 months) Atlanta, GA & Sarasota, FL

A "Daily Money Manager" (DMM) is a person who takes over the day-to-day financial tasks for those who would prefer someone else to handle their day to day financial matters. A variety of people employ DMMs ranging from elderly clients who are unable to perform these tasks on their own to busy professionals who don't have the time. Some tasks performed by DMMs include payment of bills, account reconciliations, and assistance with cash decisions.

For more information about the markets we serve, the services we offer, and pricing, please visit our website at www.bakerdmm.com.
