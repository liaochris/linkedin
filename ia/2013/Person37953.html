You are passionate about using your expertise to transform the world. As a Leader you are called on to innovate at the speed of life, continuously and consciously creating the future as you live it, hurtling through massive amounts of input and expectations, with new definitions of success and continuous demands for creativity, all the while developing numerous connections, hopefully honing some deep relationships, maintaining your equilibrium and your sense of humor!

If this sounds like a relationship that would support you, your leadership, and your innovative capabilities, reach out by sending me an in-mail. Let's start a conversation about how I might contribute my support and expertise to your leadership success!

Partnership Coaching and Training | Career Coaching

Big Picture Partnering

2003 – Present (10 years)

With my extensive background working with organizational systems, family systems, entrepreneurial /professional couples, professional / business partners and teams, one of my specialties is coaching people in the mindset, skills and tools that build rock-solid, long-lasting partnerships -- personal and professional.

I began developing my signature Big Picture Partnering ™ approach and coaching program in the mid-80’s, refined it through workshops and observational research in the 90’s, and published two books on the topic: Big Picture Partnering: 16 Weeks to a Rock-Solid Relationship (2004) and Romance Rehab: 10 Steps to Rescue Your Relationship (2010.) These, along with my well received Living Your Dreams Together: Conversation Cards for Couples (2004) may be found online.

While my emphasis is now on high-level executives and developing leaders, I continue to coach these folks and other highly motivated couples and business partners who wish to acquire the skills and tools for a healthy, long-lasting relationship, most often while running a 2 profession household or running a business together.

My background in interpersonal development, communications and marriage education is extensive having been trained in Developmental/Clinical Psychology (CSPP), negotiation through the Harvard Program on Negotiation (PON), mediation at Dispute Resolution Services (DRS Mn) and both Collaborative and Cooperative approaches through Collaborative Law Institute (CLI), the International Academy of Collaborative Professionals (IACP) and the Cooperative Practice Network Minnesota (CPN.)

I have also conducted numerous workshops for couples and partners, and have produced products and teleseminars for this audience that can be found online. I am frequently interviewed about this expertise in publications/magazines,and appear on radio and TV speaking on the topic of healthy relationships of all kinds.

Creative Alternatives, Jan Hoistad PhD, PA

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Individual & Family Services industry

1979 – Present (34 years)

Dr. Jan Hoistad has 30+ years experience working with individuals, couples, and families at all stages of life.

A seasoned veteran of personal and professional development, with an M.A. and Ph.D. in Developmental/Clinical Psychology from the California School of Professional Psychology, and extensive post-doctoral study in family systems, all forms of communication, negotiation and mediation, Collaborative and Cooperative approaches, in addition to the latest methods in achieving health and happiness, Dr. Jan has been a popular psychotherapist for many individuals, couples and families at all stages of the life cycle throughout her career.

An authority on personal and business communication and conflict resolution, she has also trained as a Neutral Coach in Collaborative and Cooperative Family Law, and functions as a reconciliation and divorce/beyond divorce specialist working with clients going through transition and along with other Alternative Dispute Resolution professionals.

    As much as we all might want it, there’s no set formula for the perfect relationship. There is, however, a solution for couples in conflict—a proven system that not only rejuvenates romance but also helps build a healthy relationship based on commitment, support, adventure, and joy.

    In Romance Rehab, 10 Steps to Rescue Your Relationship, (Sterling / January 2010), Dr. Jan Hoistad, who has been showing couples how to rehab their romance through Big Picture Partnering for more than thirty years, teaches you her innovative 10-Step approach to creating a relationship that will last longer and stay stronger because both partners are getting what they most want in life.     Take a step back and look at the Big Picture of their relationship—what they value, desire, and envision.

    Pinpoint their individual relationship style and explain how to focus on the positive aspects of their connection, identify common goals, and find enjoyable ways to stay committed.

    Apply their Big Picture aspirations to the daily details of life—how they manage everything from household chores and schedules to finances, sexuality, and minor disagreements.
