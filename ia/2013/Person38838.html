Dustin Hansen's Summary

I'm a designer with a passion for creating simple, usable, and memorable interactive interfaces and experiences for the web.

My chief strength is curiosity -- I have always been self-motivated and extremely eager to learn and try new things; to investigate, read, explore and discover. Because of this I have not been confined to one narrow field or skill set, but rather a diverse set of interests ranging from writing to painting to graphic design to web design to animation and even acting. I feel these pursuits culminate perfectly in my current field of user experience -- the essence of which is to communicate, both visually and with words. To tell a story. To create an entity that is easy to recognize yet hard to define, one which is greater than the sum of its parts...

UX design requires equal parts left brain and right brain -- it's half art, half science. I bring great creativity, strong instincts, curiosity, and a willingness to experiment -- balanced with an openness to change, a focus on user testing, feedback and data. Usability testing is essential, and always humbling -- instincts can be wrong. The challenge is deciding what to do with that data -- to get at the why and the how, and to determine the next course of action. This is the designer's job, and it is one that I love.
