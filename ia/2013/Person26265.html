Anthony Colfelt's Summary

As an Associate Director at the Customer Experience Company, I lead and deliver service design consulting projects. To do this I leverage my background as a researcher, an interaction designer, an experience architect, a graphic designer, a manager and a problem-solver.

I have worked to assist a wide variety of industries to build excellent customer experiences across a variety of touchpoints.

I have helped
- financial clients to create differentiated products and cross-channel service strategies
- media clients to make cutting-edge news and engaging online video experiences
- medical device manufacturers to create complex software that is simple to use
- telcos and banks to shape their new physical store designs

I approach problem-solving through design with a customer-centric approach. I help my clients create profitable products and services that are innovative, intuitive, engaging and meaningful to their customers.

Some highlights:
- Management of customer experience design teams (up to 30) as discipline lead and line-manager.
- Successful embedding of customer-centred design methodologies into large corporates and small consultancies e.g. The BBC, Classmates.com, MyFamily.com, Different Australia, The Customer Experience Company.
- Successful consulting engagements to create CX-led product and service strategies in Financial Services
- Creation of Agile development and User Experience design work practices (early naughties)
- Various articles and speaking engagements as a User Experience and Service Design thought leader.

At the intersection of management consulting and service design, The Customer Experience Company (CEC) helps large organisations to design and deliver great products and services for their customers, particularly where these pose a multi-faceted and complex challenge. From measurement of customer sentiment; research into customer behaviour, to co-designing solutions with staff and customers and then embedding them through systems implementation and change management, The CEC helps create great customer experiences end-to-end.
