Glenn Brown's Summary

Since entering the mortgage industry in 1986, Glenn is proud to have made a difference in the lives of many hundreds of clients whom he has served.

Glenn is recognized as a both a Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist and Certified Mortgage Planner. He has attended numerous professional seminars and conferences throughout North America to remain at the forefront of his profession. Glenn also has been an on-air contributor for KYW1060 News Radio in Philadelphia since 2008.

Prior to entering the mortgage industry, Glenn graduated Cum Laude as a Commonwealth Scholar from the University of Massachusetts in 1983 with a degree in Accounting.

Glenn resides in West Chester, PA with his wife Joyce.
Their son and daughter (twins), are both out of state college students.

Specialties

Mortgage Planning, Refinancing, Purchases, New Construction, First Time Homebuyers, Portfolio Lending, Jumbo, FHA, VA, Second Homes, Investment Property, Stated Income, Credit Challenges, Land, and Home Equity Lines of Credit
