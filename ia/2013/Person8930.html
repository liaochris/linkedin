Antonia Montoya's Summary

Bring out your best ... I can show you how. My clients focus on gratitude and learn from their successes in order to make the positive changes they want in their lives.

Change is Yours improves businesses by empowering employees to make lasting changes on their terms. This path to happy, health, focused employees has direct, positive effects in the workplace.

I am a strong believer in work/life balance and am passionate about playing upright bass, singing, painting, knitting and writing when I'm not working. I am a nationally certified health educator who has been conducting business seminars since 1995 and facilitating group and individual health education since 2001.
