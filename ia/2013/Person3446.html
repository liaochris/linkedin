Aldric Chang's Projects

  • Western Digital Digital Marketing Campaigns

    • January 2008 to Present
    Team Members: Aldric Chang

    I have been consulting for Western Digital's Asia Pacific office for years, advising them on digital marketing strategies and implementing digital marketing campaigns for them.

    I also led a team to create 3 mascots called Brent, Gus and Blake to personify and represent their hard drives, and following that I oversaw the production of 5 cartoon webisodes based on the mascots. The mascots were later adapted into various merchandise and were very well received worldwide.

  • The Travels of Wiglington and Wenks - An Award-winning Virtual World for Kids

    • January 2009 to January 2011
    Team Members: Aldric Chang, Chee Yong Ngo, Edrienne Torres

    The Travels of Wiglington and Wenks is a multi award-winning virtual world for kids which was once so popular amongst the kids that there was a tweet mentioning it almost every few minutes during the peak of its popularity.

    The Disney FamilyFun magazine described it as 'the virtual world which has found the perfect ratio between education and entertainment'.

    Leading a team of 15 programmers, graphics artists and animators, I was commissioned to conceptualize and spearhead the development of The Travels of Wiglington and Wenks Virtual World.

    Following this, I helped the virtual world garner 500,000 registered players from over 160 countries using internet marketing and social media strategies.

    I also helped the virtual world obtain over 200 positive reviews from parents, educators and kids and win several prestigious awards -
    Disney FamilyFun Top 10 Fun Websites
    Mom’s Choice Awards Gold Winner
    Nappa Awards Gold Winner
    Parents’ Choice Award Winner

    I am now lending my experience and consulting for virtual worlds who need help in various stages of its development, content creation, community building or in reaching out to a larger fan base.

  • Lamebo vs. Zombies

    • January 2011 to June 2011
    Team Members: Aldric Chang, Edrienne Torres, Chee Yong Ngo

    Lamebo vs. Zombies was Swag Soft's first mobile game and got pretty good traction due to its simple but addictive game play, polished artwork and whacky humour.

    It made it to the Top 20 most downloaded in many countries' iTunes stores and received many positive reviews from top game review sites and players.

    I was the Executive Producer for the game and was responsible for overseeing the overall creative direction, writing the whacky storyline and quality control.

    I also formulated a strategy that helped the game hit the top charts within a month of launch.

  • Little Space Heroes Virtual World

    Team Members: Aldric Chang

    Little Space Heroes is a safe and fun online virtual world for kids.

    I am consulting for Little Space Heroes and providing advice on kids engagement, building a community and bringing kids to the website.

    I've played and reviewed dozens of virtual worlds for kids and hundreds of parents and kids visit this website everyday for trusted reviews of these online playgrounds.

  • My Makeup Collection

    • June 2013 to November 2013
    Team Members: Aldric Chang, Ming Hui Chiew, Chee Yong Ngo, Rebecca Pedlow-Collins
