Carol S. Ritter's Projects

  • Room to Grow a project of the Bethlehem Area Public Library

    • June 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Carol S. Ritter, Janet Fricker, Mayor John Callahan, Marie Sterlein, Steve Samuelson, Lisa Boscola

    Room to Grow is a project to increase and improve the space for children at the Bethlehem Area Public Library. We have revitalized our South Side branch and will build a new children's room, teen room and cafe. To date our strategies and team has raised $2.2 million dollars and over 350 partners.
