Edouard Bourbon's Summary

I've been working in online media, publishing, entertainment industry and advertising for almost a decade.
Athomedia was founded in late 2007 when as a Marketing Manager at MySpace France I realized how much the new tools and ways of communicating brought by 2.0 were going to change the media landscape. How the behaviors of the consumers in these new communication playground were going to impose to advertisers new ways of thinking their marketing. They now need specialized agency as Athomedia, as everybody is a new media to convince, to build a relationship with.
