Jamal Khawaja's Summary

I am a visionary executive and entrepreneur with 15+ years of experience driving strategy for Fortune 500 companies. I deliver results in business process automation, infrastructure architecture, organizational change management, and business development. I have managed budgets of up to $350 million and projects of up to $50 million. I have held leadership positions in Technology, Sales and Marketing, R&D, and M&A. I have worked on multi-site global operations both on and off shore, including North America, the EU, and South Asia. My business acumen is unparalleled; the projects and institutions I have worked in have been the largest and most challenging environments in the world.

I have deep expertise with financial services. In addition to technology strategy, my concentrations include investment, retail, and mobile banking, hedge funds, risk, and capital markets. I have an outstanding track record for building out new organizations, managing the integration of acquisitions or divestitures, and streamlining overweight or struggling components of the business.

I have deep expertise with oil and gas companies as well. I have an intimate knowledge of their political infrastructure, the decision-making process, and selling products and services into these organizations. Functionally, I am conversant with geological modeling, the geomechanics of multilateral drilling, wellsite operations and completions, integrated workflows, reservoir stimulation, and extraction techniques.
