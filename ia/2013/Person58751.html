Jared Meyer's Summary

Jared Meyer is a creative, marketing professional with start-up experience who works with organizations on building unforgettable, long-term relationships in a very short-term world. He has been hired to contribute towards over 100 experiential marketing programs by companies like OgilvyAction, the largest brand activation agency on the planet. Jared has also worked for global brands like vitaminwater and Monster.com. Recently, he traveled the country speaking to small business owners and corporate sales staff on behalf of American Express OPEN. Jared is the author of "Making Friends: The Art of Social Networking in Life and Online" and nine other books.

Expertise: Communication, Marketing, Networking, Branding, Internet Marketing, Social Networking, Social Media, Community Engagement, Advertising and Relationships
