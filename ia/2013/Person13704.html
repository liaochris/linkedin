Beth Galleto, Copywriter and Editor's Summary

Attract attention to your business and differentiate it from your competition through clearly written, effective marketing materials. My goal is to construct well-written website and brochure copy, newsletters, blogs, and press releases that express your individual marketing message.

My experience as a community journalist and editor with Marin Scope Newspapers and as communications manager for the San Rafael Chamber of Commerce have given me a deep understanding of how to create business communications that get results. My chamber of commerce newsletters consistently won regional awards. My press releases get media attention because experience as a journalist taught me what reporters and editors want to see. My current focus is on blogging and LinkedIn because I believe they are a must for small businesses today.

The challenge in preparing quality marketing materials is to focus on how your clients benefit from using your products or services, and what makes you stand out among all those in your profession. Creating distinctive marketing materials requires problem-solving and creativity. These, plus my passionate interest in words and their correct usage, result in effective business communications.

For more information, please check out my website and blog: http://galletomedia.com, or email me at Galleto@pacbell.net.
