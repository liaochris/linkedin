Don Goodman Certified Executive Resume Writer's Summary

EXECUTIVE RESUME WRITER ☞ www.GotTheJob.com ✆ 800.909.0109

In a nutshell, I help people find jobs. I am a nationally recognized Career Expert, resume writer and President of About Jobs (www.GotTheJob.com) which provides resume writing and job search assistance for professionals and executives worldwide. An Expert Resume Writer, Career Management Coach (CCMC) and job search strategist (CJSS), I have helped thousands of people secure jobs for over 10 years.

My background is probably a bit different from other resume writers and career coaches. A graduate of the Wharton School of Business as well as Stanford University’s Executive Program, I have held a number of challenging executive roles at Fortune 500 and mid-sized firms where I led 4 turnarounds and over 6 startups to record levels of revenues, profits and market share.

An Expert Resume Writer and Featured Keynote Speaker at dozens of Job Fairs and Executive Conferences, I have been interviewed numerous times on radio and television and my work has been featured in over a dozen books for Best Resumes. I have also been quoted in hundreds of articles.

My resume writing and career coaching experience includes working with Career Services Departments of Johns Hopkins University, Fairleigh Dickinson and Clark University. Additionally, I partnered with Hewitt Associates, a global HR firm, to discuss Strategic Staffing with Fortune 500 companies.

So to answer your big question, “Don, how did you end up running a resume writing firm?”, the answer is simple: after leading a company to over 100% compounded annual growth over 5 years, I got tired of corporate politics and dealing with idiots...so I left to do what I love - helping people secure jobs and enjoy their lives.
