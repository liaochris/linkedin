Mike Duchen's Summary

Mike is currently building long-term relationships with various agency and direct partners at ReTargeter.

Before working at GlobalMojo, Mike was US employee #2 at TravelShark. Mike initiated and closed a portion of TravelShark's first large partnerships with various hotels, brands, and management groups in over 8 major domestic and international markets.
