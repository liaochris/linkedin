C.C. Chapman's Summary

Best selling author and agile, performance-based marketer with over ten years experience in marketing and emerging technologies.

Proven record of achievement in:
- Marketing strategy and execution
- Strategic planning
- Teaching and motivating learning
- Content Creation
- Keynote Speaking

Author of two best selling books, Content Rules (w/Ann Handley, Wiley 2010) and Amazing Things Will Happen (Wiley 2012)

Specialized skills in conference speaking, creative development, social media marketing, online direct response, working with large brands in helping them understand how to apply emerging technologies and social learnings to their marketing plans.

C.C. Chapman's Publications

  • Amazing Things Will Happen: A Real-World Guide on Achieving Success and Happiness

    • Wiley
    • November 27, 2012
    Authors: C.C. Chapman

    Amazing Things Will Happen: A Real-World Guide on Achieving Success and Happiness, explores how to create a successful business doing what one loves, responsibly and creatively, using real world example of people who made amazing things happen for themselves in all different walks of life. Through personal anecdotes from the author's life and interviews of successful individuals across industries, the book demonstrates how to achieve success, in all aspects of life, while Chapman’s direct and candid style enables readers to start putting together their own action plan before they even finish the book.

  • Content Rules: How to Create Killer Blogs, Podcasts, Videos, Ebooks, Webinars (and More) That Engage Customers and Ignite Your Business

    • Wiley
    • November 10, 2010
    Authors: C.C. Chapman, Ann Handley

    Content Rules demystifies the idea of organizations as publishers. It streamlines the process of creating remarkable blogs, podcasts, webinars, ebooks, and other web content that will lure would-be customers to you. It walks you through the fundamentals of how to create bold stories, videos, and blog posts. And then, once you’ve created the content, it tells you how to share it widely online to cultivate fans, arouse passion for your products or services, and ignite your business.

    Content Rules is suited for organizations of all sizes and stripes, whether you are a Fortune 500 corporation or a mom-and-pop on the corner, or anything in between: hospital, entrepreneur, nonprofit, sole practitioner or consultant, artist, government agency, church, school, political candidate, sports team, community group, or rock band or maybe a butcher, baker or candlestick maker.
