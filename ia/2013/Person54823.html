Wen-Tien Chang's Summary

Wen-Tien Chang (a.k.a. ihower) is a software developer, agile enthusiast, Rubyist, Ruby on Rails specialist&contributor, the author of "Ruby on Rails 實戰聖經" book (http://ihower.tw/rails3) and the founder&organizer of RubyConf Taiwan. I have been programming web services since 2002, using Ruby since 2006 and leading engineering team from 2011.

I'm also active at technical community in Taiwan. I like to spread out great ideas and have presented more than 40+ talks since 2007, including RubyConf China 2010 at Shanghai, RubyKaigi 2011 at Tokyo, RubyConf China 2013 at Beijing, OSDC.TW, COSCUP, WenConf.TW at Taipei and organize RubyConf Taiwan from 2010. All my talks are listed at http://ihower.tw/blog/talks. My well-known presentations includes:
