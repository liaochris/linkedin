CyberStudios

Ambitious endeavor early on in the proliferation of the WWW. Founded by ex Strategic Planning head of Warner Bros. Studios, Steven Koltai, I was the 2nd hire... Developed relationships with dozens of original content developers in an attempt to produce and market "revenue-generating original online content"... Despite some terrific productions, the world wasn't ready to support (or even understand) such a concept and so when all focus was shifted away from creative content to a single concept involving planning weddings online, I bailed.

Founder/CEO

Panolog Software

1990 – 1995 (5 years)

Co-founded Mac-based custom software company, teamed up with Apple, designed and built mission-critical database systems using the Mac as the front end clients for UNIX servers. Each solution featured hi-concept graphical user interfaces (GUI), something that "just wasn't done". Despite intense opposition from internal MIS departments coast to coast, Panolog built and/or consulted on major systems for Warner Bros., Paramount Pictures, MGM/UA, Universal Studios, Sony Music, HBO, Mattel Toys, MTV Networks, The Album Network, Steven Spielberg's "Survivors of the Shoah", AMGEN, ETC.
