Francesco Bertagnin's Experience

Owner and Managing Director

Foresight Consultancy Limited

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Accounting industry

February 2010 – Present (3 years 11 months) Gibraltar/Spain

Foresight Consultancy Ltd was created to offer specialist tax services and business services to Companies and high net worth individuals living in Gibraltar and Spain. In addition to offering international tax advice (as detailed in my previous position), I also help clients coming to Gibraltar obtain Category 2 residency, advise on local Gibraltar and Spanish personal and corporate tax issues including acting on behalf of clients in obtaining tax rulings (in Spain and Gibraltar). I continue giving lectures on tax issues as well as appearing as the tax expert for Gibraltar and Spain on a leading tax planning website.

Tax and office manager

Sotolaw Asesores S.L. (t/a Marrache & Co. Spain)

2007 – February 2010 (3 years) Sotogrande, Spain

I was employed to manage a new tax and legal office based in Sotogrande, Spain.
This office was set up to specialise in giving cross border tax and legal advice for high net worth individuals and companies. During my time there I advised on various international tax issues which included (amongst others), advising on permanent establishments, the international use of trusts, UK domiciliary issues, international residency issues (including Art 4 tie breaker clause), use of holding company structures etc.
I was also heavily involved in business development of the office and wrote articles on tax issues on a monthly basis in the local press and annually wrote the chapter on Gibraltar in the “Tax Directors Handbook”. I also spoke in a number of seminars on tax issues held by various organisations including private banks, IFA’s and the Spanish Chamber of Commerce in London. The location of the seminars I have spoken at included Switzerland, Gibraltar, Spain and UK.

International tax adviser

BDO Fidecs

2003 – 2006 (3 years) Gibraltar

Advising on the international tax issues arising between civil and common law jurisdictions, on double tax treaties and their effect, as well as advising on various personal and corporate tax issues mainly involving UK, Spain and Gibraltar.
I was also involved in various one off projects. These varied from producing tax/finance products to performing tax due diligence on businesses, (including perfoming the tax due diligence on the very first Gibraltar Company to be listed on the London Stock Exchange).
I was also involved in business development of the tax department including preparing tax flyers, speaking in seminars and updating the BDO “Doing business in Gibraltar” guide.

Tax Senior

Morgan Brown & Spofforth (later became Vantis MBS)

2001 – 2003 (2 years) London, City

Dealing with the UK tax affairs of high net worth individuals in the City. I dealt with a smaller allocation of clients (approximately 140) as they were of a much more complex nature. In addition to the usual high net worth individuals, my portfolio included Lloyd’s names, landed gentry, professional sports people etc. This exposed me to a much wider variety of issues such as dealing with tax residency issues, domicile, Lloyds cases, EIS and VCT claims, NI, CGT etc.

Tax senior

Sobell Rhodes LLP

Public Company; 11-50 employees; Accounting industry

1999 – 2001 (2 years) Pinner, Greater London

Dealing with personal tax allocation of clients of approximately 230 from various backgrounds including Solicitors, Doctor’s, IT contractors, Hotelier’s etc. I was also involved in the preparation and submission of partnership returns, trust returns, P11D forms, NI Class 4 deferments certificates, NI Class 2 exception forms, VAT registration forms, notification of Self-employment forms etc. I also dealt directly with HMRC on any tax investigations instigated on any clients in my allocation.

Company manger and then changed position to Tax assistant/Tax semi senior

Lester Associates

1997 – 1999 (2 years) London, Hammersmith

Initially employed as a Company manager and I was involved in completing various statutory forms and submitting them to Companies House for a large number of companies held under management by Lester Associates.
I was then moved to the tax department where I was trained in the preparation and submission of personal tax returns for a large portfolio of IT contractors.
