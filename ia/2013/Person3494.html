ALEEN BAYARD's Summary

I have worked in the areas of communication, business development and client services consulting since 1998. I specialize in communication [internal and external], leadership and team development and people strategy. I am on faculty at Northwestern University teaching several leadership courses as well as Lake Forest Graduate School of Management where I teach in the MBA program. My vision is to serve as a coach and facilitator of people to optimize their performance and create vibrant and transformative environments.

Specialties

Branding & corporate positioning, communications, client research, client service, change management, human capital infrastructure development.
