Darin Barney's Summary

Darin formed LedMonkey to utilize his experience in assisting clients with upgrading their marketing efforts and improving marketing ROI.

Darin worked with NutraCea, a public company based out of Phoenix, AZ for two years. In that time, he implemented SalesForce into the sales, marketing, customer service and accounting departments. He led the redesign all of NutraCea consumer packaging, marketing communications, and managed the resolution of various FTC and state agricultural packaging issues.

Prior to NutraCea, Darin was on the Executive Management Team as the Senior marketing executive for MiniCo, Inc. For seven years, he had led and had P&L responsibility for the marketing efforts that grew MiniCo from under $18M to over $38M. He directed the marketing to launch three self-storage operations in Hong Kong, cobranded efforts with various organizations including TransUnion and Eastern Seaboard Packaging, and was instrumental in the strategic planning processes of the organization.

From 1990 to 1999, Darin worked for Prudential starting as a Marketing Intern and then swiftly moving into functional and staffing management positions. He managed marketing initiatives supporting 28 agencies across the US and 2 call centers. He was responsible for all collateral design and production, trade show participation and logistics, and managing vendors. Darin began working for Prudential while he was attending ASU working on his BA in Communication.

Specialties
