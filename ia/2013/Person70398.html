Judy Karlov's Summary

Judy Karlov is a management consultant who advises the mid-market on reducing overhead expenses.
Clients who partner with Judy are focused on their core business strategies and do not have sufficient time or resources to lower their indirect expenses in order to maximize profits.
Judy's 30 years of experience in purchasing management and corporate real estate have enabled her to enhance her expertise in procurement, comparative financial analysis, project management and client relationships.

Specialties

Categories of savings include: electronic payables, energy, equipment maintenance, fleet management, freight, grounds maintenance, hotel rates, imaging diagnostics, insurance, janitorial/safety, lighting audits, MRO, messenger services, medical supplies including eldercare, merchant services, office supplies, parcel delivery, payroll services, pest control, printing/promotion, records management, rehabilitation services, security, telecommunications, temporary staffing and waste disposal.
