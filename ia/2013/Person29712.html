Danielle MacDonald's Summary

As CEO & President of MAC4 Communications, LLC, a company I founded 12 years ago, I manage a passionate team of professional women who assist me in providing internal, employee, HR and online communications and branding support to various clients.

MAC4 helps large and small businesses to market their products and services externally and provides employee, HR and change communications support to companies looking to provide clear, consise communications on change projects, issues and topics important to employees, managers and leaders.

MAC4 helps companies promote company news, happenings and events internally to employees and externally to prospects, and customers. As a full service communications firm, MAC4 also provides branding, advertising and Web design and development support.

MAC4 has provided communications and project support related to internal, change and HR communications to companies like Janssen Supply Chain (a Johnson & Johnson company), AstraZeneca, Abbott Laboratories, Shire Pharmaceuticals, and SAP North America, to name a few.

In additon, MAC4 provides branding and web support to various clients including: K HR Solutions, Kimhugginsspeaks.com, In-Development, Perpetual Leadership, Crompco, The Farm & The Fisherman and more.

Visit us on the Web for a complete client and project listing at: mac4comm.com.
