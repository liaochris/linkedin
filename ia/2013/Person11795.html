Barb Troy's Summary

Barb Troy brings over 35 years of business and leadership experience to every position. Her experience covers a wide range of activities from ownership of several highly successful businesses to professional speaker, trainer, business consultant, and senior executive coach.

Barb has developed and presented seminars and workshops on organization, communication, time and resource management, productivity, sales, marketing, team and personal development, and the operation of a successful business. She has consulted national and international companies of all sizes, both public and private, on a wide range of business issues. Barb is commonly asked to consult businesses during the start-up phase taking them through their successful and profitable first year of operation (and from a plateau to the next level of growth and success, or how to operate and grow during changing and sometimes uncertain economic conditions).
