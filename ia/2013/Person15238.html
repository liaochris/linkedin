Bobbin Beam's Summary

Voiceover industry pro, Bobbin Beam is a full-time voice actress in the business 20+ years. Audio is recorded broadcast-quality studio via ISDN , Source-Connect, skype phone patch and ftp.

Recent clients: St. Jude Children's Cancer Research Hospital, Disney, Nivea, Citgo, Kellogg's, Goodrich, ProFlowers, Discovery ID, Cricket Wireless, RFD TV Network, U.S. Army Reserves, Cox, Microsoft, Cisco Systems, ABC, Expedia.com, American Heart Association, plus numerous regional and local projects.

Production/turnaround: Usually within hours

Bobbin Beam brings a warm, confident voice to your commercials, in-store announcements, narration, e-learning, web presentations & explainer videos, IVR , radio and television imaging, network and affiliate promos.

Specialties: Voiceover recording: Radio and television commercials, corporate narration, web video, radio imaging, TV promos, documentaries, medical narration specialist, script writing, marketing, broadcast-quality ISDN recording studio, Source-Connect, phone patch, Adobe audition 3.0, Neumann U87ai , AT 2020, Rode NT1A microphones.
