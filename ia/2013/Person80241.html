Leo S. Primero III's Summary

I possess extensive UX/Web/UI Designing and Front End Developing experience in both Waterfall and Agile processes. With an MS in Professional and Technical Communication focused on User Centered Design from New Jersey Institute of Technology (with Honors), I have a full understanding of the whole life cycle of a desktop application and/or web based software/site development project. As an experienced certified ScrumMaster and certified Scrum Product Owner, I can work well in an Agile/Scrum oriented environment efficiently.

My primary technical skills include: Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Fireworks, Illustrator, InDesign, and Flash); Search Engine Optimization and efficient use of Web Analytics; Online marketing specialization; Strong typographic skills; Solid understanding of HTML5 and CSS3 to include cross-browser compatibility; Knowledge of industry best practices in web design, information design and usability; Comfortable working within a PHP environment; Extensive experience working with JavaScript and Ajax, PHP, ASPX, Java, Perl, and C++; MySQL and MSSQL databases; Strong working knowledge of WordPress customization and theming; Copywriting experience (headlines, banner copy, landing pages, etc.); Digital photography and digital video editing.
