Janis Borgueta's Summary

Janis Borgueta is a Licensed Real Estate Salesperson, living in the Town of Newburgh NY.
Selling Real Estate in my family has been an on going profession for some years. My father and brother had their license for many years...and after watching them I saw the value and joy in the profession. I spent my earlier years as a nurse with the US Navy...I definitely enjoyed the time, but needed to move on to pursue having a family. Nothing stays the same... and so after training... I followed in the family footsteps and started in home sales. I feel that in this profession I am able to meet my personal achievement goals as I find my clients and customers the joy of finding their new house to make their own... a home! Working with clients and listening to their needs I am able to give them the service that they deserve. I feel that it comes naturally to me to work in this profession

Specialties: I love working with first time home buyers. The process of educating them to the process and then guiding them along the way. I also do a great deal of new construction.... from finding land to working with buyers on what its like to buy a home that is just an idea. Find me at http://myhudsonvalleyproperties.com

Key Properties of the Hudson Valley

March 2005 – Present (8 years 9 months) 26 Faculty Road Cornwall on Hudson NY

Real Estate is my business. I am a full time agent in the Hudson Valley. Newburgh NY has been my home for the last 29 years, and I know the area and the market. Helping people buy and sell real estate is what I do.
A good deal of my business is selling new construction homes. I work with different builders in the area that have survived the downturn and have continued to produce a great product at an affordable price to consumers. These homes now include most everything and have become turnkey, reflecting the ever changing market. Included in the area are many smaller locations of beautiful new homes at a fraction of the sales price of five years ago. Times have changed with needs and wants of buyers, and builders are hearing their demands loud and clear with some builders providing GeoThermal solutions to energy prices.
