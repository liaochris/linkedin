Daniel Blakemore, CFRE's Summary

A Cleveland, Ohio native, a proud graduate of both New York University and Howard University and a tireless advocate for the fundraising profession and the non-profit sector, I am interested in making professional connections and building relationships with people in my field and related areas.

Based upon my professional experiences, I have a skill set that includes sharp research skills, strong oral and written communication skills, comfort dealing with people and a dedication to intellectually challenging work in individual fundraising.
