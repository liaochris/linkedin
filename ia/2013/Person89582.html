Matt Rudowski's Summary

What's up? This is Matt Rudowski’s Linkedin profile; here you will find a gateway to his proffesional information, and a little something about Matt himself.

Matt was born in New Orleans, but raised in Detroit. Even from a young age you could tell he would be a design geek, constantly drawing in the margins of his homework and making sure his art projects were tastefully minimalist. These days, he focuses on doing what he likes: making things that look good.

Matt generally spends his spare time learning the finer points of web design and geeking out to a variety of sci-fi show and electronic music.

Have look around and drop him a line if you see something you like. Matt is available for a variety of work, from print to branding, and everything in between.
