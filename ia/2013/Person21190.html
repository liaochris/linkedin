Chris Brooker's Summary

Chris Brooker believes in the power of technology to make the world a better place. An avid studier of Neuroscience with a passion for cognitive emergence and the mysteries of perception. He started building application in his early teens and moved on to build Loan origination and management systems for credit unions to help free them from the high cost of manually managing and vetting loan application making the process better for the community. Later moving to document imaging and management software that let municipalities, universities and corporations scan and index all their paper documents removing the limitations of paper based files. An active member of the Toronto tech and startup community and a long time tweeter and blogger, he’s brought all this experience and more to Epilogger and is excited about what the future holds.

Specialties: ASP.net MVC 3, C#, ASP.net web development, MS SQL, HTML, CSS, Web standards, Business Process Automation
