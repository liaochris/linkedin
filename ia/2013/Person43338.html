Eva Har -Even's Summary

As an executive and career coach for individuals and organizations, I help clients transform their lives into success stories of personal and professional accomplishment and fulfillment. I approach clients with respect and empathy, providing a dynamic and confidential environment to foster positive change in themselves and their organizations. Through strategic coaching, clients expand their awareness and experience reframed thinking. As a result, they are energized to take the steps needed to increase their effectiveness in their roles or, when appropriate, make new career choices that lead to a more meaningful and purpose-driven life.

I have designed and delivered programs in Leadership Development, Executive Coaching and Career Coaching to Fortune 500 companies as well as smaller, rapidly growing enterprises. My clients represent a broad range of industries, including pharmaceuticals, financial services, media, consumer products, insurance, telecommunications, advertising, law firms, consulting firms, and not-for-profits. I have helped mid-level to top executives alike define and then achieve their professional and personal goals.

Specialties:
* Executive Coaching and Leadership Development for individuals and teams
* Career Development for professionals and senior executives
* Career Coaching
* Talent Management
* Career Management and Assessment
* Team Coaching
* Cross-Cultural Coaching.
