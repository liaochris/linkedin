Bryce Welker's Summary

CPA Exam Website:

I founded CrushTheCPAexam.com to help people pass the CPA exam. After passing the exam myself, there were many things I wish someone would have told me BEFORE I had started that would have saved me a huge amount of time and headache. This website was started as a way for me to share my insights and experiences with others and has since grown into an active community.

CrushTheCPAexam.com covers the best tips, tricks, and strategies on how to study for and pass the CPA exam. It provides CPA candidates with all the information they need to know, from application to licensure, in one centrally located website.

Founder

Refresh Vending, Founder

2005 – 2009 (4 years)

While in college I took out a loan and bought 10 new vending machines. I solicited businesses throughout Orange County and negotiated locations in law firms, rehabilitation centers, Jiffy Lubes and office buildings without having to share any profits or pay for any utilities.

I ran into many obstacles and made a lot of mistakes along the way but I learned a lot of powerful lessons about life and business. Despite a few setbacks (like having a rat get stuck in the cooling fan and blowing up the refrigeration unit and a not so clever thief tip over and destroy my machine only to steal some candy bars and leave the cash), I still managed to be profitable for 4 straight years. I sold the assets off when I moved to San Diego and hope to use the skills and experience I learned to further my professional pursuits.
