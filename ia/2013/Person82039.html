David Llopis' Summary

My name is David Llopis and I am a 3D Character Animator based in Barcelona (Spain)

My main goal in my animation career is simply to create believable, funny characters and alway keep improving my skills.

I can do both, cartoon and realistic animations, I’m feeling more comfortable with cartoony style, but I can afford realistic animation with succes, for me, it’s always interesting to have challenges to improve my skills as an animator. I also have experience working with MoCap for movies and videogames.

I am available for freelance opportunities and contract work. If you feel you have a project that I could help with, I’d love to hear from you… so please, get in touch!

Also I’ll always be glad to hear what you think about my work…

► 12 plus years experience animating in different fields of moving image.
► Character animation and Character Rigging for feature films, commercials, TV series, shortmovies and videogames.
► Creative writing, high skill developing pipelines and timelines.

Specialties:
► Expert animation & rigging knowledge with Autodesk Maya.
► Advanced animation knowledge with Autodesk MotionBuilder and XSI.
► Advanced knowledge with Adobe Photoshop.
