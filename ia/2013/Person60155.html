Trey Brown, AINS's Summary

Trey is an I-CAR Platinum certified auto damage adjuster for GEICO Insurance where he inspects vehicle damages and writes estimates that comply with state regulations, using accepted industry practices for both first and third party claimants.

Trey has been in the insurance industry since 2004 and regularly attends industry seminars and classes that expand his knowledge in the insurance and auto damage field. He is also currently attending classes at Oklahoma City Community College and The Institutes that prepare him for exams through AICPCU/IIA to complete the Associate in General Insurance (AINS), Associate in Risk Management (ARM), Associate in Personal Insurance (API), Associate in Claims (AIC), and Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter (CPCU®). He is also actively working on completing classes to graduate with a Bachelor of Business Administration degree, with plans on earning a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree, both through the University of Oklahoma.
