Brett Polonsky's Summary

I have over 20 years of professional experience, the last 10 as creative manager and senior product design team lead. I am also the author of four books, have traveled North America as a seminar instructor, and taught classes in the Interactive Media Department at The Art Institute of Seattle.

I’ve worked on large projects with enterprise clients such as Microsoft, Amazon, HP, PTA, and others, where I’ve managed teams that include UI, IA, usability, graphic design and marketing – both in-house and vendor.

My diverse experience in interaction design, user-experience, information architecture and usability, along with a strong technical understanding, recent work in social media and mobile applications, allows me to work collaboratively across multidisciplinary internal teams as well as effectively communicate at all levels.
