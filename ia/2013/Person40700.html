Elizabeth Howell's Summary

I’m a multiple award-winning science and business journalist in Ottawa who writes, edits and tweets – and also travels in search of space museums and events.

My work has appeared in SPACE.com, Universe Today, Air & Space Smithsonian, SEN.com, SpaceRef, Physics Today, MSNBC, Wired.co.uk, Mashable, the Globe and Mail, Ottawa Business Journal, CTV, CBC and many other publications.

I've covered three shuttle launches and prominent gatherings such as the National Space Symposium. Additionally, in 2012 I completed an M.Sc. Space Studies, enhancing my policy and technical knowledge of space.

Specialties: Writing, editing, space exploration.
