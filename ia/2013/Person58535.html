Jann Fitzgerald's Summary

RESTORATION ... Stop paying to replace glass and start saving your hard earned cash by repair and restore.

Why Replace When You Can RENU?

RapidRenu: We remove graffiti from glass. Up until recently most people would simply replace their windows when hit by graffiti. I discovered a newly patented technology that revolutionized the entire process.

Our Glass Technicians and are working closely with property managers, building owners and store owners to remove vandalism from their windows -- faster, cleaner and cheaper than ever before.

Before forming RapidRenu, Jann served as president and CEO of New Century Alliance, Inc. a consultancy which specializes in strategic partnerships and alliances. Jann was key in driving strategic relationships for companies such as Sun Microsystems, The Dalai Lama Foundation, Intel Corporation, Adobe Corporation and Oracle Corporation. Jann was elected to the Board of Directors to the Danish-American Chamber of Commerce for a third year.

Jann Fitzgerald received her B.S. in Organizational Behavior and Computer Science from University of San Francisco.
