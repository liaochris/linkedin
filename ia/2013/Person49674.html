Grace Bylinka's Summary

Hello, my name is Grazyna Bylinka and I'm the Regional Manager for Oriflame Natural Swedish Cosmetics.

I joined Oriflame to get my favourite products cheaper having known and used them for over 12 years. I knew about the great discount on my own purchases and I wanted to earn extra income by showing the catalogue to my friends.
Soon after joining I realised that this company not only has great products but also gives people fantastic earning opportunities! Thanks to Oriflame I’ve been able to leave my full time job and become financially independent; it’s also given me the confidence to develop myself through trainings and support given all the way.

I've been with the company for over 5 years and I'm now running a team of 600 people!

Oriflame has been on the market for 45 years and trades in over 60 countries. What's unique about our products? They are made from natural ingredients and have never been tested on animals.

We offer fantastic range of products for all the family: skin care, body care, hair care, fragrances and aftershaves and make up.
My customers love advanced, high quality skin care and fragrances. We are comparison of Clarins or Clinique but better value for money!


I'm always looking for more people who will help me promote and sell our fantastic products so if you know anybody who will benefit from earning extra cash please get in touch!
