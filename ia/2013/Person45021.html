Terra L. Fletcher's Summary

Style. Substance. SEO.

***Who is Terra L. Fletcher?

Fletcher is a hard worker from a small town, the offspring of serial entrepreneurs. She gleans a business insight beyond her years by surrounding herself with great people and reading stacks of books. Fletcher is an opportunist, rarely saying no, living for an intellectual challenge. Fletcher has become a go-to for social media, search engine optimization, business writing, and website content. Fletcher is sought after for speaking and consulting.

***What Does She Do?

Fletcher is founder and CEO of Fletcher Freelance, a marketing agency specializing in internet marketing. Fletcher helps organizations - large and small - grow their business. She was awarded Entrepreneur of the Year in January 2013. Fletcher has spoken to businesses, trade associations, and chambers of commerce. She's presented to the American Counseling Association and the Wisconsin Guild of Chimney Service Specialists. Fletcher offers classes through Northeast Wisconsin Technical College and has spoken at Fox Valley Technical College.

***Who Should Contact Fletcher?

Fletcher won't turn down a cup of coffee. (Though she’s more of a tea drinker.) She loves meeting with entrepreneurs, people working on their website, those who want to learn about social media, and anyone serious about improving their company's marketing.

***Why Hire Fletcher?

Fletcher is an engaging speaker. Her presentations leave everyone with at least one or two ideas to implement in their business immediately. As a consultant, Fletcher provides her clients with analytics and direct access to their accounts. ROI is priority ONE. Fletcher is your go-to for marketing, especially social media.
