Micki Sievwright's Projects

  • Men's Journal Magazine Publicity

    • May 2011 to May 2011
    Team Members: Micki Sievwright

    Achieving a spot in the national spotlight takes persistence and dedication. And sometimes it is delivered to you. In this case, my client received an email requesting information directly from a Men’s Journal editor. From this point on, I coordinated key messages, request for the editor’s personal VAP Test and tracking of the story. The result was an important story about cholesterol and men’s health that referred to my client’s test as “a better cholesterol test.” The client was overjoyed, saying “We have never had a better article in a men’s magazine. Nice work.”

  • 'The Doctors' TV Show Publicity

    • February 2008 to December 2011
    Team Members: Micki Sievwright, Amanda Frank

    I worked on the producers at “The Doctors” TV show since its inception in 2008 to feature our client’s patented advanced cholesterol test. In December 2011, the show aired its “10 Ways to Live a Longer Life” episode and aimed the spotlight on the VAP Cholesterol Test and its many benefits.

    Dr. Travis Stork of “The Doctors” TV show interviewed Dr. Michael Cobble, chief medical officer at Atherotech Diagnostics Lab (my client), about a the VAP (Vertical Auto Profile) test. The test, which reports 22 components of cholesterol, provides a more accurate, individualized, and comprehensive picture of heart disease and diabetes risk.
