Michelle DeRepentigny's Summary

With 13+ years of real estate brokerage experience, I'm passionate about promoting affordable home ownership opportunities in the Athens, GA area. Offering both homeownership and career opportunities to the diverse popluation that Athens Georgia enjoys, is what I love best about my career, and I specialize in serving relocating clients.

I'm also a Georgia Certified Residential Appraiser (License #6547) and the co-owner of Diversified Property Services with my husband, David. Diversified Property Services specializes in complex residential appraisals and forensic appraisal reviews.

What's in it for you if you choose to do business with me? An extensive knowledge of the Athens, GA real estate market brought to the table through longevity in my real estate brokerage & real estate appraisal career.

I am also a native Athenian!
