Lynda Katz Wilner's Summary

Communication and Accent Trainer for workplace communication skills, including: voice improvement, accent modification, regional dialect modification, telephone, networking, writing and listening skills, public speaking.

Developed training materials for non-native English speakers in the business, financial, science, medical, engineering, technical, religious fields.

Created specialized communication training materials for international medical graduates and nurses. Experience as a speech and language pathologist in the medical setting helped design medical materials that are relevant to the needs of this profession.

Conduct on-site seminars, training, workshops, and lunch and learns for small, mid-size, and large groups.

Individual training addressing professional communication skills, coaching for presentations, foreign accent modification

Distance training via GoTo Meeting, web conferencing; Skype

Training conducted in Owings Mills, MD office or on-site.

Specialties: Foreign accent modification; professional communication skills; voice improvement; public speaking
