Keith Bell's Summary

Throughout my high school and college careers, I had a passion for working in politics. I volunteered on several campaigns and was employed by several more.

After leaving politics, I moved to Georgia & began working for Vision Equipment & Auction Company. After working there for 6 enjoyable years I left to start a new company with my father. Bell Auctioneers opened its doors on March 15th, 2010. Bell Auctioneers produces and conducts large food service equipment auctions in selected major cities across the United States on an annual or semi-annual basis. These auctions will provide consumers with the opportunity to purchase food service equipment by the auction method instead of paying retail prices for them.

We specialize in auctioning large quantities of reach-in cooler and freezers, ranges, fryers, griddles, char-grills, vent-hoods, seating, stainless steel tables and sinks, smallwares and other equipment related to the food service industry. The equipment we sell is all new "scratch and dent", overstocked, or obsolete restaurant equipment. These items are consigned by the leading names in the food service industry. If you are planning a new restaurant or need to replace some of your existing equipment, plan to attend one of our auctions.

Specialties

I am a very persuasive individual that can balance multiple tasks, and would excel in presentations or other forms of public speaking.

With over 7 years of knowledge within the restaurant and chain equipment market, I believe I would be a great purchasing-procurement agent for any supermarket or restaurant company.
