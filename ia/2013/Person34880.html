Brandon Harper's Summary

Talented, creative, and experienced Software Architect and Engineer who uses both academic principals and 14 years of professional experience with internet based applications to write JVM based software. Thorough understanding of internet based applications all the way from the UI to J2EE application servers down to the OSI model used to connect to them. Self-teaching and an early adopter/tire kicker for many applications and technologies.

I strive to work with the best people and best technology-- you won't find me settling for a "9 to 5" or anywhere that I am unable to make a solid contribution towards making things better. Although I'm very passionate about what I do and perhaps drip with sarcasm at times, I'm not a diva programmer. I work well in teams and love working with colleagues who can teach me new things and vice versa. I'm a fairly ego-free engineer, skeptical yet positive, and mostly just enjoy doing cutting edge work with awesome groups of people.

I believe in TDD with high amounts of code coverage, working towards the nirvana of continuous deployment to be truly agile, and leaving code in better shape than I found it in. I primarily focus on writing back-end systems which run on the JVM (mostly in Scala and Groovy these days, and sometimes Java), but also tinker with a lot of other technologies in my free time including Ruby, JRuby, Python, HTML5, Twitter Bootstrap, etc. I believe in open standards, am pragmatic about my tool and technology choices, and seek to build new software with a great team.

Lastly and possibly most important out of everything you have read about me, I have maintained a sweet programmers beard for over 6 years now which helps empower me to design scalable, elegant platforms and write clean, well tested code.
