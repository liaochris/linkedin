Janelle Brittain (Performance Management Prof)'s Summary

Janelle has 30 years of business experience focusing on Management, Leadership and Boards. Her career foundation was in hi-tech with Xerox, Control Data and healthcare organizations. She founded her own company, Dynamic Performance Institute, LLC in 1989. DPI builds leaders to strategically grow their people and companies. Janelle delivers leadership knowledge and insights via training, executive coaching, consulting and speaking to companies and associations across the U.S. and 6 other countries.

Speaker, Trainer, Consultant
Janelle has helped over 300 companies and organizations from Fortune 100 to entrepreneurial companies develop leadership, corporate turnarounds, team building and managing change. Janelle acquired the nickname “The Growth Guru” for her ability to help businesses manage growth. Clients bring Janelle in when their teams are struggling and need more collaboration.She has an Executive MBA and is a Certified Speaking Professional (CSP), the highest earned designation from the National Speakers Association.

Executive Coach
Her Executive Coaching helps executives meet their goals through their people by increasing their leadership skills, managing other’s performance, improving communications/presentations, directing change and strengthening teamwork. Her style has been described as profoundly insightful, filled with self-discovery, diplomatically direct and results focused

Board Work
She specializes in Board Assessments, Training, Strategic Planning, board conflict, Board Coaching and creating Advisory Boards.. She has served on 7 Non-profit Boards for over 27 years, 7 Corporate Advisory Boards and 1 Corporate Board (6 years).She is currently the Director of the Boardology Institute ® for Boardroom Bound®, an organization with the mission of increasing the number of women and minorities on corporate Boards.

Specialties: Leadership development, Board effectiveness,Communications,Business mangmt
