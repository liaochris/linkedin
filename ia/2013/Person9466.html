Ariana Lise Newcomer-Voice Coach's Summary

Discover the missing ingredient to success - your VOICE. I passionately believe in the power of the human voice, and our ability to grow, develop and succeed through developing our voices. Do you cringe when you hear your voice on a recording? Do you avoid speaking engagements? Do you know your voice is holding you back in life and business?

You CAN change your speaking voice. Some of the benefits of voice coaching with me are:
*When you speak, people will pay attention and understand.
*Command respect just by the sound of your voice.
*Improve your self-confidence, creativity and expressive ability.
*Communicate more effectively.
*Step on stage knowing you can count on your voice.
*Engage and inspire your audience.
*Improve business and personal relationships.
*Improve your client attraction and retention.
*Speak authentically from your heart.
*Fulfill your mission and make the money you deserve.

I offer online programs, in-person sessions and VIP days. My Reclaim Your Authentic Voice™ program is designed to take you to a whole new level in your speaking and presentation skills.
Bodywork for the Voice℠ helps in-person clients improve freedom & flexibility of the voice, and helps recovery from vocal injury!

My rich and varied background and experiences give me a great deal to draw on in working with you. I've worked with voices since 1994. I've been a Massage Therapist since 1984. I've been a professional modern dancer and opera singer. A voice injury ended my singing career, and my recovery has led me on an incredible personal growth journey and given me a deep toolbox to use with you. I love working with individuals who deeply value integrity, service, and collaboration.

Change your voice, change your life!

Specialties: Voice coaching, vocal coaching, speech, public speaking, presentations, fear of public speaking, engaging public speaker, vocal problems, personal growth, vocal rehabilitation, Bodywork for the Voice℠, recovery from vocal injury
