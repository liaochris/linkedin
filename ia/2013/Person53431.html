Hollis Miles' Summary

Hollis leads ONTILITY’s National Sales Team. Since joining the ONTILITY team as a Sales Manager in 2010, Hollis has worked with many of ONTILITY’s key accounts and has been responsible for more than 300 customers and $15 million in solar equipment sales over two years. As Vice President of Distribution Sales, Hollis is responsible for leading ONTILITY's national sales team, sales team support and overall sales strategy. His goal for ONTILITY is to create an environment that enables ONTILITY's team to best support and serve its valuable customer base.

In just three years, Ontility has grown from start-up to a $100M Solar Distribution, Solar Training and Solar Projects leader. Ontility supplies top quality PV panels, inverters, mounting hardware and other solar products to solar integrators and other qualified solar contracting firms. Additionally, Ontility is one of the nation’s largest solar training organizations, featuring NABCEP, DOE and IREC courses. To complement its distribution and training divisions, Ontility offers a full portfolio of solar project support services. Ontility is one of the largest solar players in the U.S. with a leading market share in many of the top solar markets.

Previously, Hollis was an International Account Representative at Smith & Associates, where he developed new markets for the 25 year old electronics distributor. Hollis is a class of 2007 graduate of Texas A&M University in College Station, TX.

Strengths include a positive outlook on life, a strong work ethic, a logical approach to getting things done, a willingness to learn and the ability to apply what I learn to my life and my work. I have more than five years of sales experience and four years of computer programming experience.
