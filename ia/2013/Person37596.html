Robert Flower's Summary

Discovered Natural Intelligence (N2L) a paradigm for understanding human potential and maximizing success. Works with individuals and businesses and trains counseling professionals to identify and overcome obstacles to achievement in both their personal and professional lives.

Authored three books on how Natural Intelligence can help you understand and access your potential "Decoding Potential", "A Revolution in Understanding" and "Your Exceptional Mind."

Developed the "Master Pattern" predictive model for investments and economics. The weekly "Master Pattern Report" on youtube highlights investment insights along with political and economic analysis. http://www.youtube.com/natiandyou

Hosts local cable shows on political awareness; frequent lecturer and radio /TV guest.

Founder and President of Robert J. Flower & Co., a diversified real estate company.

Specialties

Lectures on Natural Intelligence, Empowerment, Motivation, Achievement. Works with individuals and groups to maximize potential and success. Forecasts investment and economic trends. Real Estate services.
