Joyce McAndrews' Summary

Possessing a penchant for collaboration and talented in a wide array of artistic modes, Joyce is a multimedia director. In marketing and communications, with an emphasis on developing and supporting corporate brand identity, Joyce has designed original print and digital content for promotional collateral including websites, logos, style guides, icons, flyers, posters, banners, staff newsletters, member publications and enewsletters utilizing social media (viral videos, facebook, LinkedIn, Constant Contact, twitter and blogs) to convey corporate mission and strategic initiatives.

Joyce has developed and employed her illustrative and animation skills in the creation of the popular interactive children’s app "Dash & Ditto's Playground" for Android and iOS (featured as New and Noteworthy by iTunes). She has recently released "Touch World" a geography app - "old school geography basics with a modern twist", now available in the AppStore for the iPad. Both apps have been praised by top educational reviewers including Common Sense Media, Wired, Moms with Apps and Geek Dads.

Joyce is an art director in film (Sweet Crude) and video production (Microsoft and creativeLive) creating innovative and effective design solutions for over 100 photo and video productions.

Her artwork, published and sold internationally, is included in both private and corporate collections. Joyce’s original textile designs are currently available at the Stacy Logan custom carpet gallery in Seattle.


Clients include but are not limited to creativeLive, Microsoft, YMCA, Eddie Bauer, Art Publishing Group, Sweet Crude, Puget Sound Energy, Teatro Zinzanni, Providence Hospital, Reel Girls, Lifelong Aids Alliance, HRC, Neighborhood to Neighborhood for New Orleans, Giddon’s School, and Songbird Foundation.

Specialties: Art direction, marketing and communication, childrens apps, graphic design, illustration, logos, textile design, interior design, fundraising, painting, photography
