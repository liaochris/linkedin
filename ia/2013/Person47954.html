    GLU Consulting provided expertise and content to assist The Hoopis Performance Network and LIMRA in producing Trustworthy Selling which is designed to recapture the lost art of selling in the financial services industry. George Ludwig's Publications

  • Power Selling: Seven Strategies for Cracking the Sales Code

    • Kaplan.Dearborn Trade Publishing
    • July 1, 2004
    Authors: George Ludwig

    George Ludwig has made it his life’s work to study the selling DNA—the specific skills, thinking patterns, and actions—of the world’s greatest sales professionals. In doing so, he’s cracked the elusive code for success in sales.

    Ludwig shares his findings and distills them down to the seven simple strategies revealed in Power Selling. From Zig Zigler to Anthony Robbins, the most influential achievers among us, he says, all share the powers of reputation, real passion, research, rapport, resource management, resiliency, and relationships.

    Salespeople of all kinds—from beginners to corporate sales managers to entrepreneurs—will discover the Power Selling methods that lead to bigger sales, more loyal customers and ultimately, higher-powered sales success.

    In Power Selling, you’ll learn to:

     Overcome buyer suspicion and skepticism
     Leverage research to gain a competitive edge
     Establish trust that prompts buyer action
     Maximize emotional states for greater sales
     …and transform sales setbacks into sales comebacks

    The book also includes practical tools for success, including an online Power Selling Assessment Questionnaire and a list of “Power Boosters” that concludes each chapter and maps the steps that will transform you into a sales superstar.
