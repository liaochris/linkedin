James Martinez's Summary

People turn to me when they want a highly organized, dedicated, trustworthy and honest outlook on how best to manage their project, market research and meeting needs.

Clients value my direct, concise communication style and pragmatic problem solving approach developed from years of working in a variety of environments, learning what works and what doesn't.

I understand the importance of everything from the grunt work to the big picture. What I do is solve problems effectively by having honest discussions about what your needs, strengths and pains are, being highly flexible and intuitive and figuring out strategic, practical ways to organize your chaos and bring structure to your organization. Emphasis is on providing concise, pragmatic, detail oriented market research, meeting and administrative management to medical device, healthcare, creative and innovation driven companies.

My goal is to make you and your organization look good and deliver superior customer service to your employees, clients, vendors and partners.

Specialties: Project Management, Corporate / Non-Profit Meeting Planning, Focus Group, IDI and Market Research Recruiting and Logistics Management, Organizational and Administrative Support
