Grenville Hamlyn's Summary

Over twenty years broad experience of branding, design and corporate communications.

I work closely with design and technical team in the development of technical design briefs, as well as working with the account teams to help scope digital projects and design output.

I also research digital developments and help incorporate relevant areas to enhance the agency offer.

I am also responsible for designing creative corporate communications websites and digital experiences that meet measurable business objectives - helping organisations define and express themselves online, whether it's delivering services, supporting operations or strengthening communications.
