Dan Schuman's Summary

I have over 20 years experience in real estate sales and finance. Specifically, my wife Amy and I operate the Schuman Team at Ohio's #1 real estate company, Howard Hanna Real Estate Services.

Being a husband and wife team gives our clients two professionals each with specialized areas of expertise and unique personalities. We specialize in luxury homes and apply our unique training and experience to help our clients successfully buy or sell - no matter what the price point.

We provide a high level of hands-on service to our clients so that we can exceed their expectations. Our focus is always on our clients and looking out for their best interests.

Although we are business-minded, we do appreciate the personal side to our profession. We thoroughly enjoy helping our clients, especially those relocating to the Cleveland area, by being a good resource for them. Whether someone needs to find a doctor for their children, a good handyman, or just wants the name of a good local restaurant, we’ll be there to lend a helping hand. In fact, many of our clients become our friends, which is one of the greatest rewards of being in real estate.

Specialties

We specialize in luxury homes, home staging, and working with people who are relocating to Cleveland. Our use of technology helps us stand out from our competition.
