Lesley Carhart's Summary

I currently work as a security incident handler, dealing with any and every type of hacking, digital theft, compromise, misconfiguration, vulnerability, or infection which affects my customers on a given day. I specialize in digital forensics; it is my passion. I also have background in tactical communications, radio, and Cisco networking. I find the most enjoyable part of my work to be the critical thinking and intuition that goes into understanding the many technical and human elements in security investigations.

My specialties are incident response, log analysis, computer forensics, fighting evil, and common sense.
