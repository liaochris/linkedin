Bella Ikpasaja's Summary

I specialise in Business Development and enjoy working with organisations to grow their revenue, build client relationships and reputations.

I’ve spent the majority of the past 10 years generating millions in revenue for Branding and Communications Consultancies; Public Relations, Integrated and Research agencies who work with Private, Public and Third sector organisations. They include Unilever, Amazon, RBS, Barclays, the Cabinet Office and Transport for London. I have gained valuable insights into effective Brand Management; Corporate, Employee and Stakeholder communications using traditional and new media techniques.

At the end of 2007, I set up NBGB to provide strategic and tactical marketing and PR support to Creative agencies as well as Financial and Professional Service providers. This has led to a broad range of projects, where I have been able to add value to start-up businesses as well as long established Private Banks, such as Brown Shipley.

I am currently studying for a CIM Professional Diploma in Digital Marketing, as digital is evolving from a peripheral element of Marketing to one that is central to an organisation and its communication with customers and stakeholders.

As a personal project, I am keen to highlight progressive developments in Africa in order to begin to balance the perception of the continent. This inspired me to create ZeitgeistAfrica, an online platform to showcase social and economic developments in Sub-Saharan Africa. Also, to share some of the discussion themes I have with inspiring and creative individuals connected with Africa.
In this capacity I am learning valuable new skills, including how to use web hosting services / content management systems such as Wordpress..
