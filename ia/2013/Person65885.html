Jeroen Offerijns' Summary

“I have not failed. I’ve just found 10,000 ways that won’t work.” - Thomas A. Edison

As a Computer Science student, I am fortunate enough to have the opportunity to try out as much as possible, explore the world and learn about new things constantly. For example, recently I’ve been learning more about Big Data and Machine Learning; and as a Delegate of United Netherlands, I look into the role IT plays in our global society.

Being an entrepreneur is also a result of this drive to learn new things. I believe education is a field that could use improvement, which is why, in 2012, I joined the team of Net-Les, a startup providing online tutoring lessons to high school students. Later on, I founded Transvise together with Henk Bohlander, with whom we've set the goal of increasing the use of digital technologies in traditional learning environments.

Furthermore, I love meeting new people who have different perspectives and views than mine, which is why I am extremely grateful to be a Global Fellow of the Kairos Society.
