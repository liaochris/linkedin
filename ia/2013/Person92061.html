Michael Barany's Summary

For Michael, Software Development is a life occupation in the full sense of the term. He is constantly striving to push the limits of what can be done inside the browser. He is a believer in the migration toward cloud computing and is averse to deadlocking software development to the confines of a standalone machine.

Michael enjoys reading about the latest technologies and keeping up to date with new releases from companies like Google and Square. He is a strong believer in Open Source technologies and will gladly prefer an Android phone to an iPhone any day.
