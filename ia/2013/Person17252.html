Brian Schwartz's Summary

I am the proud owner and founder of Visibility Partners. My firm helps Collaborative Family Law attorneys grow their practices.

My goal is to help develop marketing strategies for family law firms, so they receive 2-5 new phone inquires from prospective clients each week.

Ideal Client
My ideal client is a Collaborative Family Law attorney, firm or practice group interested in increasing their web visibility

I work directly with my clients implementing a broad range of Internet marketing solutions including:

•Custom designed websites and blogs
•Search engine optimization
•Logo design
•Content and social media strategy
•Internet marketing workshops

Traffic is Key
I believe that when you build a website it must be designed from the beginning as a tool to draw visitors. The team I manage includes experts in search engine optimization (SEO), website design and social media technologies.

Customized Approach
I work one on one with our clients developing a customized marketing strategy for family law firms.

Collaborative Family Law Marketing (SEO)
I understand the value of Collaborative Family Law, enabling me to write more effective and search friendly content for our Collaborative clients. In total, the Collaborative sites that we have worked with attract over 50,000 new visitors each year.

Family Lawyer Workshops
As a professional speaker, I meet with family law groups across the country speaking on the benefits of social media and internet marketing.
