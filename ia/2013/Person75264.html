Kevin Warhus' Summary

Kevin Warhus shapes logic and creative thinking into a fresh new outlook when developing digital strategies for his clients. As a member of the StringCan team since the beginning in 2009, Kevin has worked to develop his passion for creating smart and effective solutions.

With a background in marketing, entrepreneurship, and small business, Kevin has worked with brands of all sizes such as Arizona State University, Drive Time, and the World Trade Centers Association. He applies his enthusiasm towards connecting the right ideas with the right implementation. As a frequent writer for StringCan, he has been featured in the Huffington Post. He has a deep understanding of the digital marketing landscape, measuring success through metrics, and is Google Analytics certified.

Kevin is a problem solver both externally and internally. In additional to strategy development, he plays the vital role of office Fun Czar, building and maintaining the culture here at StringCan. With his creativity and enthusiasm for fun, Kevin acts as a team builder, orchestrating the expansion of our brand.

Born and raised a desert rat in Arizona, Kevin currently lives in Tempe. He is a food fanatic, music aficionado, and a classic car lover – just look at his rebuilt 1966 Ford Mustang. Kevin can be reached at the contact info below if you want to discuss anything from digital strategy to the best happy hours in the valley.
