Maria Anderson AFAMI CPM's Summary

Maria Anderson is the founding director of Sustainable Marketing Services, a multi-award winning, Brisbane marketing agency, that delivers sustainable marketing services to growing technical, professional services and sustainability-focussed businesses.

Maria's commitment to growing businesses with strong brand stories and competitive advantages is demonstrated by Sustainable Marketing Services winning Best Small Business in 2012, 2011 and 2010, and Best Emerging Business in 2009 (Commonwealth Bank Redland Business Awards).

The five success factors to Maria's approach are 1) strong brand stories, 2) the right marketing strategies (traditional and digital), 3) effective marketing systems, 4) excellence in implementation and 5) continuous improvement through ongoing measurement,

Maria's approach helped one company introduce a new product range and grow the range to 20% of revenue in a few years. Within 12 months, two other companies have grown and are expanding their team. One client achieved a 23% growth in web traffic in 2012.

She is pasionate about sustainable business practices which improves the bottom line through optimisation of marketing resources and adds more value to the top line through the growth of stronger brands and more employee engagement.

Under Maria’s leadership, Sustainable Marketing Services was recognized as an ecoBiz Partner by the Department of Environment and Heritage Protection. In 2012, she completed a Carbon Accounting Course and is currently undertaking a Diploma of Sustainability.

Specialties:
* Marketing strategies (traditional and digital marketing)
* Brand stories and brand strategies
* Outsourced marketing team services.
