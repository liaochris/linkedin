Gayle Barton's Summary

I am an extremely fortunate agent who lives in a bustling suburb north of Atlanta while enjoying the luxury of owning a second home in the beautiful North GA mountain town of Blue Ridge.


SOUTH FORSYTH / CUMMING / SUWANEE/ JOHNS CREEK / MILTON / ROSWELL

As a full time real estate professional I specialize in residential properties representing both Buyers and Sellers.

My area of expertise encompasses the Fulton/Forsyth/Gwinnett triangle. Because this area is my home, I have an in-depth knowledge of the real estate market, school districts, and neighborhoods.

I am well known for my use of innovative technologies in successfully marketing properties and matching buyers with the properties that most closely fit their needs. But, despite my use of the most up to date high tech “gadgets”, I still believe that personal service is at the forefront on my success!

My honesty, integrity and enthusiasm guarantees that my clients are satisfied 100% of the time! Through my dedication to service I have earned the respect of my previous clients developing a rapport which has allowed me to build a business based strongly on referrals.

I feel confident that you will find no other agent more willing to put your needs first and provide the level of service you require. My goal is to provide the highest level of service possible without compromising honesty or integrity.


NORTH GA MOUNTAINS / BLUE RIDGE / CHERRY LOG / ELLIJAY

The experience I gained during those 7 years of ownership provides me the unique ability to locate properties that will not only make an excellent rental, but also provide my Buyer with the perfect spot for a little R & R.

For those with an investment or vacation cabin / rental property to sell, I have the experience and ability to market your cabin to display its full income potential.
