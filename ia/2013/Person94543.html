Mike Rundle's Summary

Hey there! I'm Mike Rundle and I'm a designer, software engineer, entrepreneur, product thinker and a tireless proponent of beautiful & smart user experiences. I started designing and building websites in the mid-90s and now I work across multiple platforms (web, desktop, mobile) to design, develop and evangelize amazing products.

SKILLS:

• Rich and detailed visual interface and icon design
• HTML5, CSS3 (animations, transitions), JavaScript, jQuery
• Native iOS development with Objective-C, Cocoa, UIKit
• PHP5, MySQL, Zend Framework, CodeIgniter
• User research, listening to others, incorporating great ideas
• Experimenting, prototyping in code, production development
• Making my 1 year old daughter laugh uncontrollably
