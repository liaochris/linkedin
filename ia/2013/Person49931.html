Keith Johnson's Summary

Hello - my name is Keith Johnson; I am a Senior Technical Writer/Software Trainer/Software Process Analyst who writes about desktop, web-based, and mobile applications within the Agile/Scrum software development framework. My two "mottos" are: (1) great documentation ensures the attainment of goals and milestones on a continuous basis at software development companies and (2) great training ensures an informed and enthusiastic end-user. This keeps a software business moving forward at all times. Here is how I can help your company:

Online Help Systems (Adobe Technical Communication Suite)
Project Management (Requirements and Process Documentation)
Hardware and Software Process Analysis (Microsoft Visio Diagrams)
Training Presentations (Microsoft Movie Maker)
Training Videos (Techsmith Camtasia Studio)
End-User Documentation Manuals (Microsoft Office)
Source Code Documentation (Doxygen, Sandcastle)
Programming Documentation: Currently learning Microsoft C#.NET
Quality Assurance Support: Test Scripts & Test Platform Documentation
Sales Support: Requests for Proposal & Information (RFPs, RFIs)
Language Support: 100%: English & Portuguese, 75%: Spanish
