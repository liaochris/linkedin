A. Lewis LCG-CFL, LLC's Summary

KINITRACO (kə-’nē-träh-cō) is the newest addition to LCG-CFL,LLC.

Specializing in training and exam preparation-Kinitraco is derived from an older Greek term kvinitra which translates to “motivated.” Mr. Lewis started Kinitraco for those who are motivated to prepare for their new skill but perhaps may not have the funds for some of the more expensive training programs.

Mr. Lewis founded LCG for the purpose of helping others to succeed. He has overcome many barriers in the different levels of his success. While at Seminole State College (SCC) in Sanford,FL, he served many positions of leadership on campus.

He concluded his final year as the Student Body President leading his staff to become "Runner-Up" for the Best SGA in the State of Florida for 2002. Mr.Lewis also contributed in placing SCC "on the map" with the Future Business Leaders of America(FBLA) by winning multiple speaking awards.

Mr.Lewis continued his venture for success at The University of South Florida where he later joined Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc.

As he is a great speaker, Mr.Lewis' contributions to his fraternity lead back-to-back successful State Leadership Conferences. While serving as the Conference Chair was successful, he also was selected to serve as Second Vice State Director and later First Vice State Director.

He was most recently employed as the Area Supervisor for a design company managing 24-50 varied employees from Oahu,Hawaii to Panama City,Florida.
