Joe Neill's Summary

Life long resident of NE Oklahoma
35 years in Cattle Business owning Neill Cattle Co, a 14000 head custom cattle feedlot and farm/ranch operation at Welch, OK.
15 years in Bank Ownership
35 years Licensed OK Real Estate Broker

Current goal is to help as many people as possible to achieve their real estate dream in the Grand Lake/NE OK area whether it be a waterfront home, golf course home, retirement home, commercial real estate, or farm/ranch real estate.

Specialties

Listing, Selling, Business Devlopment, Construction in the Grand Lake area including waterfront homes, golf course homes, retirement homes, weekend getaway homes, commercial real estate, and farm/ranch real estate.
