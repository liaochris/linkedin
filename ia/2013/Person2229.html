Mike Schulz's Summary

Since my early teen age years I have been always a hands on type of person. Instead of someone else fixing or building something for me I took the time to learn and do it myself. No challenge was to large. From building cars & motorcycles to electronics' trouble shooting and furniture building.

I have over 30 years experience in construction. My primary job was a roofing contractor for many years. I also have hands on experience in electrical , plumbing, framing, hvac, sheet rock, flooring.and trim work I have built two additions on my own home and did all the work myself except the masonry. I have built three garages from the ground up myself and helped others with theirs.

Through the years on new residential work it always troubled me when I see other sub contractors install components of there trade incorrectly. I would go to them or the builder to express my concerns and they where always grateful. In the early 90's when I was speaking with a builder about concerns of one of the trades he told me I should be a inspector. So in 94 I began a home Inspection business. I prided myself on being unbiased about my findings for the benefit of my clients.

Today I provide home inspections through out the triangle area. I also do seller inspections, phase inspections, warranty inspections and commercial inspections. I am also certified to perform septic inspections. Each year I go above the continuing education classes and attend seminars on housing related subjects. I keep up on current materials and trends. I use state of the art inspection software with digital pictures and provide online report access. If the clients desires I can have there security system inspected by reputable installer for free and they will provide them with a Limited 90 day home warranty at no cost to them.

Specialties

At Affordable Home Inspections, we strive to provide accurate reporting services to help protect our clients from potentially hazardous conditions in their environment.
