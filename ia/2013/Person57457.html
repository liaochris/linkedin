James Gehring's Summary

I am a highly motivated and seasoned business leader with strong technical savvy and proven experience helping businesses assimilate and leverage the new “As-a-Service” IT paradigm. I have extensive experience in all aspects of business operations, including; start-ups, R&D, product development and launch, product management, financials, program management, marketing, executive operations, sales and sales management. I have worked in a variety of industry verticals, including (but not limited to): technology, data center, retail, law, government, non-profit, education, medical, manufacturing and distribution.

A successful business leader, I deliver product and service solutions around clear operational disciplines that ultimately (and always) are grounded in the goals and objectives of the business. I craft the right message, materials and approach for the initial sales pitch and have designed assessment engagements (consulting) that bring immediate revenue as new sales begins to unfold. These assessment engagements deliver a long term IT solution for the customer that cements the ongoing relationship. My years of experience have matured my abilities to quickly adapt new solutions to a variety of IT arenas, including (but not limited to); core IT infrastructures, data center, server environments, virtualization, cloud, database systems, email systems, networks, wireless, voice. Technologies: VMware, Citrix, Oracle, Microsoft SQL Server, Cisco, Dell DaaS, Linux, Unix.

Over the course of my 25+ year career I have demonstrated an innate ability to align technologies and IT services with what businesses actually need to operate more efficiently and powerfully. In this rapidly changing IT landscape I have matured from building complex customized solutions into today’s “as-a-service” model that meets the expectations of this new paradigm.
