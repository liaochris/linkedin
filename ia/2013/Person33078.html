Daniel Castro's Summary

I have a proven track record of strategic, operational, and product design leadership. I think design, speak geek, and champion business.

Recently, I started and grew a game studio to over 25 employees (+offshore development in Argentina and Australia). We produced an amazing 3D social virtual world for kids as well as a line of toys and comic books which received astounding reviews from parents, teachers, kids, and organizations. ($5M in funding)

Previous to that I successfully headed and grew a UX department to over 15 UXA, UXD, and UIE's across U.S., India, and Brazil.

@Verizon I managed a team of Information Architects that lead the user experience for Verizon's online media convergence initiative which covered mobile, TV, and desktop.

Over 15 years working in e-commerce, enterprise, mobile, TV, niche markets, gaming, 3D, virtual worlds, kids, security, entertainment, professional services, and many many startups.

I have extensive experience tailoring distributed Agile processes to bind technical, design, and product management team collaboration.

Social projects: sustainability, youth, alcoholism & addiction, Latino community, education, and anti-bullying.
