Jim Lillig's Summary

Described as a Swiss Army knife of digital marketing, I have a creative passion and results oriented approach that produces millions in revenue for advertisers and publishers alike. Since 1996 I have excelled in bridging clients understanding of the benefits of investing in digital marketing and how it can dovetail with their existing efforts to create a higher level of engagement. I have led teams to successful lead generation & direct sale campaign executions in SEO/PPC/CPM/Social Media/Mobile Ads (includes SMS)/eMail/Video and B2B and B2C Webinar formats. I write professional copy for the web, whitepapers, marketing communications, trade shows, blogs, direct mail, email/newsletter, radio, call center scripts and presentations for live and webinar broadcast. I have provided end to end digital strategy and execution plans for clients from Fortune 500 to start-ups and middle market players. Innovative enough to lead designers and creative teams, technical savvy enough to lead developers and technicians in all aspects of campaign development. I enjoy connecting and directing disparate teams, getting everyone on the same page from the initial client buy-in to final approvals. Experience working with high volume advertisers has provided me analytic insights from big data that pinpoint customers’ behaviors and allow me to make calculated predictions on future behavior.

As a C Level Manager, I have always taken the approach of mentoring and sharing knowledge to encourage insights, creativity and an entrepreneurial spirit. In the Business Development role, I have achieved success by being able to quickly assess gaps and devise strategies that mutually benefit both parties and leverage each other’s strengths. I am able to juggle multiple campaigns and responsibilities and am a tireless learner. My appetite for new digital and mobile knowledge has a continual tapeworm, desiring more and more information and techniques to be able to unlock the next killer campaign.

Specialties

B2B/B2C Digital & Mobile Marketing Brand Strategy; Affiliate Marketing; Analysis, Strategy, Media Buying & Mgmt of SEO, SEM, CPA, retargeting, email & Social Media; Lead Gen Campaign Mgmt; Copywriting; Marketing Communications; Business Dev.; P&L Responsibility; Strategic Partner Dev.; eCommerce Strategy & CRM Development; Full Cycle Product Dev and Mgmt; Mobile Marketing; Web Analytics; Call Centers; Creative Campaign Development; Direct Mail; Word, Excel, PPT, HTML, Photoshop, Illustrator.
