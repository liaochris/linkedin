Abby Jochnowitz's Summary

Do you want to drive up to a house you love? Do you want an attractive space to store clutter? DesignAnts delivers smart, refreshing solutions to design challenges. Homeowners and businesses turn to them for projects ranging from clutter-busting, custom cabinetry, family room additions, to exterior and garden design. By listening to clients' needs and experiencing their lifestyle, they will craft the perfect solution to fit every moment of their lives. DesignAnts will coordinate the project team or can collaborate with a client's contractor or architect.

Contact Abby Jochnowitz to initiate the first steps towards a design you’ll love.
