Richard London's Summary

My mission is to personally touch the lives of 100,000 people with Parkinson’s and their caregivers within the next three years to help them live well with Parkinson’s disease and enjoy every day.

To read testimonials on how I’ve helped others go to http://AHandbookForLife.com/testimonials.

To see video segments of my speaking go to http://AHandbookForLife.com/seminar-video.


Please help me help someone you know.

Author (A Handbook for Life, A Handbook for Life: The Parkinson's Edition, A Handbook for Happiness, audio CDs and DVD), Speaker and Entrepreneur. Overcame cancer and living with Parkinson’s disease. Most importantly, I enjoy life and the people around me.
