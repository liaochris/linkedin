David Borish's Summary

I have been the founder of several trailblazing companies. The most recent is CrowdHut, the first full-service pre-and post-crowdfunding solutions provider. Prior to that I founded BP Wiz book publishing software that streamlines the process of writing and submitting book proposals. In 2008 I founded Midtown Capital and created the first securities-based loan product, Midtown Capital Was acquired in April 2011. Earlier in my career I founded Sell Your Stuff, the first eBay drop-off franchise concept in New York which I quickly grew to a national franchise concept. I am also currently the Editor of www.futuristtimes.com a blog dedicated to the future of technology.
