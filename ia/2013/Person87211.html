Mark Roberts' Summary

I live and breathe music. By day, I manage the Manhattan-based world music label, Evergreene Music. By night, I run Stars & Letters Records out of my Brooklyn-apartment and write music under the moniker, We Are Temporary. I also write for the music blog Cassette-Rewind, participate in music panels (APAP, CBGB, etc.), and organize shows in New York. In my spare time, I try to play as much tennis, swim, and bike as I can, whilst enjoying life with my amazing wife, Mary.

Specialties: DIY; Label management; publicity; PR; booking; A&R; digital distribution; social marketing; digital strategy; brand development; creative merchandising; public speaking; graphic design; web design; content generation (video, audio, photography); music composition; audio production; sync & licensing; contracts; negotiation; copy writing.
