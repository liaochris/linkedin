Aysh Banaysh's Summary

At the age of 16 I combined my love of music and blogging to create a music blog called Aysh Cream Entertainment. After a few months I realised that the music industry is the place for me and I left school to study Music Business. I have since had the opportunity to explore different aspects of the industry and though I still blog for both myself and now Link Up TV, I have also dabbled in artist management, digital marketing, organising video shoots and advising artists.
After studying the music business in general, I became particularly drawn to PR & digital marketing and I am now looking to develop my skills in this area.
