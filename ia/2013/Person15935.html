Boyd Martin's Summary

20-year veteran of the Portland blues music scene as a drummer, and maintain a robust national and international touring schedule.

I am an advocate of live music performance in all forms, believing it to be a cornerstone of any cultured society.

I have created or been a team member for several musician support groups, activities and enterprises--the latest being a definitive foray into a Web 2.0 application for musicians, supporting music education, apprenticeship, and professional performance on all levels.

Specialties

Drumming: All styles of blues, soul, and old school R&B, rock, swing, and funk.

Business: Music marketing, writing, networking, databasing, web development, trend identifying, and a good futurist.
