Jesse Walker's Summary

Jesse Walker is currently a User Experience/Human Factors Manager at Altisource Labs, as well as an independent design and accessibility consultant for various other organizations. He is as adept at pushing assumptions, vision, and thinking as he is at pushing pixels. Regardless of whether it is an overhaul of an entire e-commerce funnel, designing mobile apps from the ground-up, or helping build award-winning interactive advertising campaigns, you can always look to Jesse to move the needle.

Adopting a systems-design approach, he excels in the design of environments that support complex decision-making activities, or systems employed by specialized user populations.

A boy of the South, Jesse was born in Texas, grew up in Kentucky, and now resides with his wife and four dogs in Atlanta, Georgia.

Specialties: Interaction design, concept development, accessibility, user-centered design, web development, web standards, technology integration, human-computer interaction, product management, decision-modeling, decision support, systems design
