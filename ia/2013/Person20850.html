Cassie M. Chew's Summary

I am a media professional who stands at the crossroad between "shoe-leather" reporting, filmmaking and website development. I am fluent in three languages: text, photo and video.

Specialties

Business/policy reporter. Produce well-sourced, well-written, news and enterprise stories on complex public policy and industry topics in healthcare, education, technology and energy.

Visual Journalist. Capture storytelling photos and footage. Operate Mini-DV, DSLR cameras. Set lighting and audio. Edit with Final Cut and Premiere.

Web design. Build, update websites. CMS, Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Fireworks, Flash, ActionScript HTML, CSS.

Social media. Hootsuite, FB, Twitter, YouTube.
