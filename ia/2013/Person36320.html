Doc Gallagher's Summary

Known as “America's Money Doctor,” Doc Gallagher has earned a reputation for providing expert financial advice to senior citizens in his home state of Texas. W. Neil Gallagher, Ph.D., earned the moniker of Doc Gallagher for his ability to assist people with financial difficulties. Doc Gallagher hosts the weekly radio show, Your Family Matters, where he discusses lawsuit protection, estate planning, investing, and a number of other financial topics. Doc Gallagher aims to educate individuals about their financial options in order to help them maximize their wealth. At The Gallagher Group, Doc Gallagher works with individuals and families to navigate the financial system in matters related to aging, including preparing for retirement, understanding Medicare and Medicaid, and estate planning. The Gallagher Group’s website, www.docgallagher.com, includes a number of investment resources that simplify the complicated language that often accompanies financial planning, offering information about Revocable Living Trusts, a guide to important questions, a guide to important questions on caring for parents, and information about planning for long-term care for loved ones. Featured in Mature Texan Magazine, Doc Gallagher earned recognition as one of the 25 Best Services, Companies, and Organizations for Seniors in the state of Texas. Doc Gallagher's most recent book, The Money Doctor's Guide to Taking Care of Yourself When No One Else Will, guides readers through the steps necessary to maintain financial and health stability for a lifetime. Doc Gallagher has published articles in 17 publications, including World Vision Magazine, Charisma & Christian Life Magazine, Journal of Value Inquiry, and Philosophy and Phenomenological Research. A number of universities and seminaries across the United States have included Doc Gallagher's book, Don't Go Overseas until You’ve Read This Book, on their syllabi as a preparatory guide on the effects of culture shock.
