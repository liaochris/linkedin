Cindy Kunst's Summary

I am a photographer that specializes in working with people whether that be one on one or documenting a corporate event. I use a wide variety of photographic styles to create unique and intrinsic images for my clients. I work on location and can create my studio anywhere or work as fluidly with the environment as my background. My primary goal in any session is to beautifully represent the true personality of my subject and to completely satisfy the needs of my client regarding their images.

Specialties

High Resolution Digital Images, Wedding Photography, Event Photography, Concert Photography
