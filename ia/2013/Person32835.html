Davor Meersman's Summary

Hi! I'm Davor.

I head the EU research strategy at iLab.o, Europe's leading living lab. Focus areas are Smart Cities, Ambient Assisted Living, Future Internet, and Service/Platform Innovation.

My research is in Service Science & Domain-Driven Innovation. I have recentlyfinalised a PhD at Curtin University, Australia, where I've also lived for 4 years.

I am also the owner of POËSYS consulting, a boutique RDI management and acquisition firm.

Over the years, my collaborators and I have attracted more than EUR 25 million in research funding for our ideas. Having the funds, space, and time to play, learn, and improve the world is what it's all about for me.

I used to be a research project manager at Synergetics, where I managed European research projects under the FP6, FP7, Leonardo Da Vinci, IBBT and IWT programmes.

Before that I ran an advertising company whilst doing my Master in Business Communication at the Free University of Brussels. I may or may not have created the name and logo for one of the University's best known bars...
