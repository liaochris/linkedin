Martin Eriksson's Summary

Entrepreneurial, driven and innovative Product Management professional with over 15 years experience building world-class online products, in both corporate and start-up environments. Data driven with a passion for user experience, I have a proven track record of defining strategy, designing solutions and delivering them successfully. I focus on analysis, design, usability, technology and the integration of different demands into a well-implemented, cohesive, easy to use product serving the user’s and business’s needs while maximising ROI.

I also founded and run ProductTank, the preeminent monthly networking event for online product managers in London with over 1,000 members and 140+ attendees at each event, MindtheProduct.com - a blog and community site for product managers in London, and Mind the Product 2012 - an annual conference on product design, development and management.

Specialties

* Product Management and Marketing
* Interactive and Digital Strategy
* Building and Leading great teams
* Design and User Experience
* Agile development
* eCommerce and eBusiness
