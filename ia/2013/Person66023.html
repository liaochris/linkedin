John Angove's Summary

I’m a seasoned sales and product management executive, equally comfortable managing a team or “carrying the mail.” My specialties are money-handling and security solutions; and, new product evangelist in the banking, retail, grocery, amusement and restaurant space.

As a senior staff member who has run teams in both the U.S. and Canada, I’ve doubled the size of a small business in just 2-years while maintaining the bottom line, taken an account from $0 to a $1 million dollar budget in just 90 days; helped reverse in 18 months a 4-year sales downturn; and gotten so far outside the box that my concept created an industry!

I’m one of those people who love REALLY BIG challenges: Opening up a new vertical; turning around an account, territory or sales team; and, taking an underperforming product or situation from “impossible” to “Wow!”

My beliefs are simple:

In sales,
• The company owns the account; the salesperson is temporary steward.
• Be known as a “Trusted advisor.”
• In sales management, make your people successful; replace yourself.

In product development,
• Have your eyes in the sky, and your feet on the ground.
• Create the unexpected.
• Deliver measurable value.

In life,
• Your guiding principles should be obvious.
• You need others. They don’t need you.
• Family and friends must to be able to say, “I’m proud of you.”
