Devon Jordan's Summary

I have recently begun to consult in the field of Social Media. I knew that many people and companies didn't want to deal with all of the noise on the web, and often could not figure out on their own what social media marketing works and what doesn't in their particular niche. So my job is to help individuals and companies clarify their social media goals and then to develop and execute a plan that achieves success. I do more than just repeat all of the tips that everyone says work. I have tested all of these tips and strategies, and I know what does and does not achieve success. For clients, I listen attentively to what they want to achieve, and I develop a plan to fit their needs and achieve their goals
