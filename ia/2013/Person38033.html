Kathleen Nickerson, PhD's Summary

Dr. Kathy Nickerson, a relationship expert who helps couples reconnect and repair their marriages, has been quoted in Woman’s World, Forbes Woman, Woman’s Day, Glamour UK, and the LA Times....and that’s just last week.

Dr. Nickerson is a founding member of FeelBetterNetwork.com and—along with her passion for helping people—brings more than 20 years professional mental health counseling experience. As a licensed clinical psychologist, she has an extensive background in various areas including anxiety, addiction, domestic violence, and depression, but today she focuses mainly on relationship counseling through her California-based private practice and globally on FeelBetterNetwork.com. She also maintains a year-round schedule speaking at more than 150 conferences and broadcast media outlets, and has authored or co-authored more than 75 professional articles and books including:

•The Everything Book – Defiant Kids
•Speaking Up: How to Help Children Who Live In Abusive Homes
•Save The Date: A Curriculum for Safe & Healthy Teen Dating
•Be Your Own Shrink: 10 Steps to Keep You Happy and Healthy
•Let's Stay Together: The Essential Guide for Strengthening & Repairing Your Relationships

Kathleen Nickerson, PhD's Experience

Licensed Clinicial Psychologist

Dr Kathy & Company

March 2002 – Present (11 years 10 months)

Dr. Kathy Nickerson is an expert counselor who helps couples reconnect and repair their relationships.

Kathy is an accomplished writer with a passion for teaching people how to live their best lives. Most recently, Kathy's work has been featured in the LA Times, Glamour UK Magazine, OC Register, Cosmopolitan UK Magazine, Parenting Magazine, DAWN Magazine, OH Magazine and Bariatrics Today. Her first book, Speaking Up: How to Help Children who Live in Abusive Homes, is used in classrooms throughout the country to teach professionals how to recognize and report child abuse. Kathy is currently at work on her newest title: Let's Stay Together: The Essential Guide for Strengthening & Repairing Your Relationships.

As a highly qualified and dynamic public speaker who enjoys sharing her knowledge with others, Kathy has spoken at over 150 local and national conferences. These conferences include the Family Violence Conference, the Sexual Assault Institute’s International Family Violence Conference, and the Violence in the World of our Youth Conference. Kathy's dynamic and vibrant style makes her a favorite with local television programs and national call-in radio shows, including KFI, KOCE, WNYC, and WBGO.

Kathy is a strong believer in the value of education and prevention. During the last five years, she developed innovative curricula, classes, newsletters, and training seminars for the United States Department of Justice - Office of Victims of Crime. Kathy has also created training materials for a variety of violence reduction programs and has taught courses on anger management in secondary schools. In addition to working in private practice, Kathy teaches courses for mental health professionals at the California Institute for Professional Continuing Education.
