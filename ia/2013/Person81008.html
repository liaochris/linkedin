What is Holistic Coaching?

Most coaches work solely with the conscious mind. Holistic Coaching works at a very deep level by enlisting the support of the subconscious mind. How do we do this?

Hypnosis
Transformational Self-Talk
Neuro-Linguistic Programming also known as NLP
Core Transformation – release limiting core beliefs

Holistic Coaching also works with the human energy field using several simple but powerful tools:

Emotional Freedom Technique also known as EFT or “tapping.”
Psych-K – popularized by Bruce Lipton in his book The Biology of Belief
Brain Gym – simple movements that prime your neurology to learn with ease

During a Holistic Coaching session we address all levels of your Being – Mental, Emotional, Physical and Spiritual.

When I say “spiritual” I’m not speaking about religion, in fact I’ve worked with clients of every faith. I’ve also worked with agnostics. While I do affirm the existence of the human soul, Holistic Coaching promotes no particular belief.

Your first Holistic Coaching session is 3 hours. Follow-up sessions are usually 1 hour. Phone or Skype sessions available.
