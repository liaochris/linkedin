Colleen Kerwick's Summary

Colleen Kerwick is an intelligent, aggressive and results orientated attorney who holds professional degrees in Corporate Law (magna cum laude) and Law (magna cum laude) from the National University of Ireland Galway, which she selected for being bilingual - Colleen is a fluent Irish speaker. She also studied Communications Science with Trinity College Dublin; Comparative American Law at University College Dublin and Speech & Drama with the Leinster School of Music. She is admitted to the first department of the New York State Bar as well as the Federal Courts in New York and Connecticut and pro hac vice in a number of other jurisdictions. Over her ten years of practice, her victories have been reported in the New York Law Journal, New York Times, Cois Coirbe and by the Associated Press and she was elected as a Public Interest Attorney of the Year and to the Emerald Association. Her courtroom skills are complimented by her distinguished sideline as an actor – Colleen played the lead role in many feature length films, did a cameo for Martin Scorsese and freelanced with the agent for Christopher Walken and Dennis Hopper. This helps her performance in the courtroom on behalf of her clients.

Specialties: Law, Non-Profit, Trials, Arbitrations, Mediations, Aviation, Entertainment.
