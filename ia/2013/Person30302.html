Danny Woodruff's Summary

Professional multimedia career officially began in 1999 at Lockwood Broadcast Group's KTEN affiliate in Texas.  Was then promoted to Creative Director after 4 years of producing and marketing experience with Lockwood's ratings successful UPN affiliates in Richmond, Virginia.  Current responsibilities include management of marketing & branding for 4 broadcast stations as well as structuring the creative department for future Lockwood Broadcast acquisitions.  Marketing goals are attained through the development on-air promotions, print design, outdoor advertising, radio, and internet media. Goal is to maintain efficient workflows for all promotional media along with the management of an extremely talented team of producers.  Continuing to increase station ratings through creative marketing while remaining focused on the needs and efforts of LBG's sales teams and overall company operation.

Production and design requests by clients was the beginning of my own freelance production company, inSight Creative, in 2004. This provided me with a creative outlet and time for industry research outside of my career at Lockwood Broadcast Group. I continue to work on client based projects with a promise to deliver the same quality product they may receive at a larger production house. In all, I define myself as a Multimedia Producer with skill sets in hd cinematography, digital photography, video editing, storyboarding, script writing, print & logo design, and some web/social site publishing.  A variety of client production includes long form corporate videos, broadcast commercials, wedding cinema, and overall company image projects. I continue to have a desire to work on client projects that allow me to utilize my talents and sharpen my skills on evolving design & media tools.
