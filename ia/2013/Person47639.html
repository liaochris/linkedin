Gene Kohlmann's Summary

President and Principal Gene Kohlmann of The Business Owner's Consultant has been committed to helping business owners define and achieve their goals for over 20 years. Uniquely, Gene is often active as a team member to get things done because ideas are rarely enough.

Despite a rapidly changing business climate, Gene's passion for growing healthy companies is what gets him supercharged every day. A gifted leader, strategist and communicator, Gene's extensive situational awareness helps him assess businesses to bring about clarity and direction. He is an avid student of economics, business models and business execution. For a company that is struggling, in transition, or simply one who strives for excellence, Gene brings a fresh set of eyes and a unique breadth of experience to a company's circumstances and culture.

A few of Gene's nicknames include the 'crisis guy', the 'turn around guy', 'leader', 'team member'. To some business owners, he is your personal trainer. He comes alongside by deeply understanding your goals and working together with you and your team to achieve them. Gene has walked in your shoes and understands what is at stake by choosing a path of business ownership. With a reputation for over delivering great results and a deep sense of commitment to stick by your side, The Business Owner's Consultant will help your business to achieve the best possible outcome!
