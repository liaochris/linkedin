Dominic López's Summary

For the past 10 years, I have dedicated myself to working on my craft, and over the last year I have been working solely as a freelance director, writer, cinematographer and editor. In that short amount of time, I have worked on some incredible projects taking me to New York, Los Angeles, Las Vegas and more. My portfolio ranges from commercials, corporate videos, music videos, documentaries, and feature length and short films. I bring a strong sense of professionalism and a wealth of knowledge to the set and I will deliver my best every time. Beginning as a cameraman, and most recently a cinematographer, I have worked with a lot of cameras. I mean, a lot. From ENG broadcast Sonys, JVCs and Panasonics, to HDV, the beginning of SSD capture with the Panasonic HVX line, the whirlwind ride of the DSLR revolution and the advent of digital cinema cameras like the RED, I have had hands on experience with them all. The opportunity to work with these cameras has given me a great understanding off all of their qualities and limitations, and I know which tool is right for the job. As a cinematographer / director of photography, I have gained a wealth of knowledge of lighting for mood and tone, and how to acquire the perfect look for each individual project. And as a director and writer (my true passion in life), I strive to tell and capture the best story I possibly can for the project I am engaged in.
