I worked with Caroline when she was Cultural Regeneration Officer with Manchester City Council, Cultural Strategy Team. Caroline is an excellent project manager. Her expertise contributed to the development of an innovative approach in working with communities and individuals who would not usually have accessed opportunities to engage in their locality. Caroline was valued team member, bringing a breadth of experience and understanding in working with the arts and communities. Not only was she experienced in working with artists and the arts - she used the ways of working from this sector to engage with other agenda’s.
