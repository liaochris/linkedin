Gregory Lewis' Summary

As of the summer of 2012, we took on the rebuilding of a restaurant where the General Store Cafe was in downtown Pittsboro. We opened the Pittsboro Roadhouse & General Store in August 2012, with new jobs, a fresh look, new recipes and a great lineup of music four nights a week.

In 2000, we started Catering by Design and have grown it into an award-winning full service catering and event planning company serving the greater Raleigh area of North Carolina.We have developed a well-tuned team that excels in preparing, delivering and serving quality food for memorable events.

Specialty Training: I am a 1986 graduate of the Culinary Institute of America and have been in the culinary field for more than 30 years.

Family: I married my high school sweetheart Maria in 1993 and we have 3 beautiful daughters.

Specialties: Our specialty is catering to you: custom-designed catered events to meet all of your needs.

Gregory Lewis' Experience

Independent Business Owner

Pittsboro Roadhouse & General Store

August 2012 – Present (1 year 1 month) 39 West Street, Pittsboro Roadhouse

In January 2012, Pittsboro's eclectic community cafe and restaurant the General Store Cafe closed due to bankruptcy. After considerable discussion, as a family we decided to reopen a restaurant at 39 West Street, and on August 17, 2012, the Pittsboro Roadhouse & General Store opened.

It's exciting to be building a restaurant again, especially one that is such a community center, and one that has a lounge, bar and a stage, with live music events four times a week, and brunch once a month on Pittsboro First Sundays.

We provide some of the biggest and most attractive meeting, event and luncheon / dinner space in the area, perfect for half or full day retreats and presentations. Contact me to find out what we can do for your group, department, division or organization.

Executive Chef

The Hotel Hershey

November 1993 – January 1995 (1 year 3 months) Hershey, Pennsylvania

Lewis oversaw the opening of The Fountain Cafe, the first new restaurant to open a The Hotel Hershey in over 50 years. The Fountain Cafe opened to rave reviews.
