Leslie Tuchman's Summary

I have a long and successful work history in mental health clinics, public and private school settings, and substance abuse recovery centers and rehabilitation. In public and private schools I have dealt with students ranging from mentally gifted, learning disabled, as well as mentally challenged. As a school psychologist I trained interns from local universities in the field of educational psychology.

Specialties

My 25 years of educational and clinical experience includes providing psychotherapy services to groups, families, couples, individuals, adults, adolescents, and children from different ethnicities and various religious backgrounds.
