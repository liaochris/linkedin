Gilbert Velazquez's Summary

Please be sure to visit http://www.gvcerv.com to view our interactive gallery.

People ask me "what sets you apart" my answer is always this"...

As a designer it is imperative that I understand the product, the market, the challenges, and most importantly what is the thing that is going to "tickle" the emotion of the audience, so they take the next step. This is especially true in the medical communications field. My job is not to just develop a pretty picture or concept, but finding "what does it mean" to the specific audience based upon the objectives and the science.

Whether you are a health communications company, a small bio-tech firm or a top-ten pharmaceutical manufacturer, my extensive experience in healthcare can provide a plethora of creative services to generate a lasting impression in your marketing communications.

As creative director, I can design your medical communication needs to a level coupled with superb client services, utilizing print, web and multimedia to capture the attention of your professional or patient audiences.
The mission is not just to come up with the "single solution", but rather to support your  marketing message over time. A communication solution that ensures the longevity of your message is always the objective.

By combining the importance of design for the cohesion and structure of your message, communication that reaches your audience and a solution that supports your message, my experience offers a service based on attention to detail, uncompromising quality, and integrity.

Specialties: Design and development of supportive CME, clinical trial and general pharmaceutical product marketing communication tools.
