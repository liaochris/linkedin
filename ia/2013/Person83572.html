Luxury Texas Living's Summary

The Luxury Texas Living Group powered by Keller Williams Realty Cedar Park Leander brings a distinctive level of professionalism and provides unparalleled Real Estate service throughout Central Texas.

The Luxury Texas Living group are diverse women with an economics, marketing and communications background. Individually they have specialized Real Estate designations that combined offer you exceptional buying and selling assistance. From lots on which to build your dream home, new home construction, residential and luxury homes, these women will provide you expert guidance and outstanding, concierge level customer service.

Tracie Johnson is a member of the KW Agent Leadership Council, a select group of top producing agents who oversee the Market Center by setting the vision, direction, profitability and budget. She is newly elected to the Board of Directors of the Williamson County Association of REALTORS and is a Graduate of the REALTORS® Institute.

Stacey Rider is the 2013 President of the Williamson County Association of REALTORS® and serves on the Texas Association of REALTORS® International Committee. She was the Membership Committee Chair, Community Services Chair and served on the TREPAC Committee. Stacey was the WC REALTORS® Rookie of the Year 2008 and is a Certified Negotiation Expert. Both women serve their community with numerous volunteer hours and gifts.

Whether buying, selling or building Luxury Texas Living will provide you with everything you need from start to finish and beyond, fostering not only a business relationship but a lifelong friendship.
