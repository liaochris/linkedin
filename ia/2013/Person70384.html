judy franks' Publications

  • Media: From Chaos to Clarity. Five Global Truths That Make Sense of a Messy Media World

    • April 2011
    Authors: judy franks

    What happens when you apply old media thinking to a new media world? Absolutely nothing! You’re left with a pot of confusion. Is the sky falling? Are new technologies destroying old media? That would make for a great crime fiction novel, but that isn’t the case either. So, what’s left? We’re left with a mess!
    “Media: From Chaos to Clarity” strips away the bias of a bygone media era to reveal a new framework—the Five Global Truths that make sense of a messy media world. This isn’t rocket science or a new boutique media and message delivery paradigm. Rather, author Judy Franks draws upon common metaphors that exist in our everyday lives to make sense of the new media world. Ironically, the media aren’t so complicated after all. When you strip away the bias of old-school media thinking, the media actually hold the keys to a bright and optimistic future for anyone who is fortunate enough to work and to play in the space.

    Author Judy Franks takes the reader on a journey that departs the old, Newtonian media universe and enters a new world of media chaos where media work in exciting ways. The Five Global truths offer practical solutions that can guide more integrated outcomes in a fractured marketing world. For each truth, there’s a technology story, a pioneer, a lot of chaos and ultimately a truth—a new way to implement IMC.

    “Judy has explained the changes in media and communications in terms that we’ve never used before. She understands networks and systems and circles and synergy and interactions, where most media and marketing communications people still think in lines and arrows and outputs.” Don Schultz, Professor, Medill Northwestern University and Author “IMC: The Next Generation.”
