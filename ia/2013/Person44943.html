Fernando Labastida's Summary

I'm a passionate Strategic Communications professional, with an emphasis on Content Strategy, Content Marketing, Public Relations, Copywriting, Online Engagement & Conversions. and Community Management.

I help organizations:

1. Create a comprehensive content strategy to communicate their primary messages across all channels to their key personas
2. Help them achieve their objectives through the intersection of customer goals and strategic messaging
3. Manage, govern, create and analyze strategic content so it remains fresh, relevant and compliant
4. Educate customers and supporters through multi-media content such as online content, videos, podcasts, blogging, social media and printed collateral.

I'm completely bi-lingual in English and Spanish, and have a solid track record producing content in both languages that attracts and sells.

I help bridge the cultural and language gap between English-speaking and Spanish-speaking users, constituents, customers and organizations. I am a passionate believer in educational excellence, and the need to change and adapt to the modern needs of a connected world.
