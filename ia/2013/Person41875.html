Erica Collica's Summary

Stylish, sophisticated, insightful and driven. With a refreshing yet exacting approach to real estate, she is able to seamlessly incorporate her background in local real estate development with her experiences in San Francisco at the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising.

A passionate student of fashion, commerce and culture Erica is immersed in trends without being defined by them. Her attention to detail and eye for design give her the edge and confidence that make her work style fresh, exciting and bold but she never loses sight of the importance of staying approachable and having a sense of humor!

Erica doesn’t take herself too seriously. What she does take seriously are your needs, your goals, your deadlines and your expectations. She has an astute eye for the intricacies and realities, as well as the unique opportunities, of this market.

You have a right to high standards and expectations of great service. Let Erica and her panel of skilled professionals introduce you to real estate the way it should be. She knows the business, but most importantly she makes it her business to know you.

DEVELOPERS AND NEW CONSTRUCTION: Architectural Review, Site & Structure Analysis, Site Selection, Blueprint Approval, Model Placement. INVESTOR SERVICES: Property Search, Value Proposition & Analysis, Design Consultation, Remodel Management, SPECIALTY SERVICES: Luxury Estates, Short Sales & Foreclosures, Property Management
