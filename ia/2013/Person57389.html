Jim Sweeney's Summary

Creating global sports brands which both entertain and positively impact culture.

Founder of NVE or New Vision Entertainment - which owns 100% of the MIKE, KIX, DISKO and MIKE's Sports Comics sports intellectual properties. As a public speaker, Jim shares about being an entrepreneur and his journey in developing the MIKE intellectual sports property.

Creator of MIKE - a sports cartoon character:

MIKE - the sports world's ultimate talking head - is a witty, provocative and highly entertaining sports humorist. Watch MIKE's animated shorts, listen to the MIKE Minute audio monologues, read MIKE's Blogs, follow MIKE on Twitter and look for MIKE soon on television, a top 5 sports web site and Jumbotrons in arenas and stadiums.

Jim has written 26 sports comic books through the voice of MIKE. The MIKE books are available at www.theemike.com and at www.amazon.com/author/mikeraffone

MIKE is the perfect intellectual property for sports licensing, sports advertising and sports sponsorship opportunities. For more info: call 727-460-8932 or email jim@theemike.com.

Co-Creator of KIX - a sports cartoon character:

KIX - a sparkling Latina soccer ball, she embodies the world's biggest sport and is the perfect icon for sports licensing, sports advertising and sports sponsorship opportunities for the 2014 FIFA World Cup in Rio de Janiero, Brazil.

Co-Creator of DISKO - a sports cartoon character:

DISKO - born Andrei Diskorovic to Russian immigrants in Zamboni, MN, this sleek, durable vulcanized rubber disk, or hockey puck, is a scrappy yet highly endearing character, perfect for sports licensing, sports advertising and sports sponsorship opportunities for the 2014 Winter Olympic Games in Sochi, Russia.

Co-Creator of MIKE's Sports Comics:

MIKE's Sports Comics lampoon the ridiculous ways we view or report on sports. Watch for these hyperbolic sports comics to be syndicated on sports web sites as well as incorporated into the series of MIKE's Sports Books for Kids.
