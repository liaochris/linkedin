Kim Rullo's Experience

Partner, Conceptual Artist and Graphic Designer

Isaac Rullo Conceptual Artistry

October 2003 – Present (10 years)

Co-founder (with Brent Isaac) of this collaborative and creative company. We specialize in design and photography. Our highly recommended expertise and experience includes print design, website design, and artistic and commercial photography. Art from Chaos, Bees Knees Entertainment, Inc., the Duquesne University Tamburitzans, Harmonia House Publishing and Northway Mall are just some of our unique Pittsburgh based clients. In addition to our design services, our photography is in high demand for many who are looking for a unique style that often represents the definition of “outside of the box” thinking.

Our innovative and groundbreaking multimedia photography/design exhibition, The Pittsburgh Nude Project™, has been exhibited in The Andy Warhol Museum, the Blue Ruin Gallery and The Southside Works.

Decades™ (a modern take on the vintage pinup) premiered at the inaugural event of Pittsburgh's newest artistic performance space, The Meter Room. More recently Isaac Rullo and our pinup photography were asked to be the solo guest tribute of the Blue Ruin Gallery at a LUPEC (Ladies United for the Preservation of Endangered Cocktails) sponsored event. All of the above Isaac Rullo projects and exhibitions have been featured in the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, the Pittsburgh Tribune-Review, and the Pittsburgh City Paper.

Our most recent work, the short film "Circus of Self (Zircus des Zelbst)" debuted to rave reviews at Your Inner Vagabond in Lawrenceville last October at a Isaac Rullo circus/vaudeville themed benefit for the Hughes Syndrome Foundation and is now making its way to be presented on the festival circuit.

Graphic and Web Designer

Freelance Graphic Designer

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Graphic Design industry

January 2000 – Present (13 years 9 months)

From the administrative/production aspects to prepress graphic design — including research and execution of new and creative design ideas, to layout and design of both printed and web materials and photography — I've worked in just about every aspect of this industry. I strive to go above and beyond all of my client's expectations and this drives all the work I do.

Along with to co-founding Carousel Point Multimedia, my clients include or have included Auberle, Carson Publishing, Inc., Family Hospice and Palliative Care, SureLogic, Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church, Little Athens of Sewickley, The Animal Rescue League of Western Pennsylvania, Loud Mountain, and Architects of the Atmosphere. Their needs have ranged from simple layout and design of printed materials including magazines, newsletters, and art and promotional pieces, to branding, photography, and cd design, to Power Point Presentations and billboard design.

Over the last few years my expertise has expanded into the digital realm with web and IPhone application design. My work with both Verve X3 and Trail Behind have broadened my creative path and enhanced and honed my skills in this realm. Due in part to this experience, I have garnered a roster of clients both locally and nationally who are looking for efficient, simple yet beautiful site and application designs to bolster their idea or their company profile. Clients and projects include Dragonfly Pilates, LLC (website), Eggs R Us (website), Trail Behind (app and website), and Gaia GPS (app and website).

Production Manager | Administrative Director | Freelance Designer

Carson Publishing, Inc.

September 1996 – January 2006 (9 years 5 months)

Duties included design and layout of various materials for publication, copy reading and editing, coordinating production schedules, ordering supplies, billing and acting as the office’s computer technical advisor. The Greater Pittsburgh Region Metroguide was the company’s biggest project, for which I wrote, designed and laid out portions of the publication, as well as handled some of the aspects of sales and distribution.
