Jack Waley-Cohen's Summary

I joined Lingo24 as Director (and only 5th company employee) in 2003 and I looked after the operational, technical, financial and human side of the business to grow to 150+ employees on 5 continents and turnover of £6m (growing at 25%+ per year).

I've now moved onto new challenges, and I am focusing more of my time on startups.

I particularly enjoy working with a basic idea to turn it into something commercially viable, as well as developing a raw team into a crack squad of managers and doers. More generally - operations, finance, systems design, HR combining into effective and good natured management of small and medium-sized businesses.

I "get things" very quickly, see connections, and find pragmatic solutions.
