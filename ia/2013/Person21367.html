Cecilia Gorman's Summary

With over 20 years in the advertising industry, I have extensive experience in agency operations focused on creative management, recruiting, financial management, graphic production and workflow.

I believe the core responsibility of management is to develop the next generation and my deep passion for leading and teaching has become the foundation of all I do. My greatest strength lies in an ability to make effective decisions and empower my teams to deliver to the highest graphic standard.

My career has helped refine and strengthen key skills in these areas:

• Creative Operations
• Financial Management
• Talent Recruitment & Staffing Strategy
• Teaching, Training and Development
• Ad School and University Relations

In addition, I own a consultancy that guides the next generation of creative talent toward careers in advertising through leadership training, creative workshops and portfolio development.
