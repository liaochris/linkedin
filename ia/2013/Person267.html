Bill Nicol, DC's Summary

Dr. Nicol is Board certified by the National Board of Chiropractic Examiners, Texas Board of Chiropractic Examiners and Board of Examiners Council of Chiropractic Extremity Adjusting. He is a member of the American Chiropractic Association. Dr Nicol obtained his Doctor of Chiropractic degree in 1997 from the Texas Chiropractic College graduating with honors. In December 1998, he completed a postgraduate certification and became a Certified Chiropractic Extremity Practitioner. He has completed over 300 hours of postgraduate courses in natural medicine, and in December 2007 received his certification in Applied Clinical Nutrition.
