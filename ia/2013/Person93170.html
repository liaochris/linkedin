Michael Witham's Summary

Michael is an Evangelist for Traklight.com.

Traklight empowers small businesses, entrepreneurs and inventors with tools and resources for identifying Intellectual Property (IP) and allowing companies to protect and leverage their ideas. Traklight bridges the gap for businesses, entrepreneurs and innovators to become more educated about the strategies, costs and implications of IP.

Michael is an Arizona native who’s always looking for good discussions with interesting people. He recently replaced Craig Ventor with Richard Feynman as his favorite scientist. To him, the creation of business is one's way of testing assumptions against the world; the intersection of human behavior, economics and science.

After earning a BS in Management from the Arizona State University’s WP Carey School of Business, he founded a retail coffee shop in Tempe, AZ. He has a variety of professional experience in consumer web, online education and NGO’s. Make sure to give him your favorite book recommendations.

We’re only humans.
