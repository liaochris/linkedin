Barry Sauer's Summary

Barry Sauer is a Program Management Principal Leader with Computer Sciences Corporation, Inc. working for the Chief Information Officer (CIO) at the United States Southern Command under the Department of Defense. He is passionate about information systems that empower end-users with the software system tools to work in a productive and efficient manner. He is an organizational change agent providing imaginative insight and dynamic leadership to transform organizations into best practice, customer-focused environments.

He has empowered numerous organizations, including many Fortune 500 companies, designing, developing, and leading software enhancements, process re-engineering, and new project management. Barry has developed the first Knowledge Management system for Electric Power Research Institute (EPRI), wrote tax management software for Price Waterhouse, mobilized Progressive Insurance Total Loss units, lead start-ups Auctions.com and Apartments.com, re-engineered the Empirica credit scoring models for Trans Union, and architected project software for Southwestern Bell (SBC). More recently, he wrote payment processing and e-commerce software for Banc of America and TigerDirect.com, lead and developed ESRI recognized Geographic Information Systems for Miami-Dade and Florida municipalities, and received service coin and SPOT award recognition for work at the Department of Defense.

Barry Sauer received his undergraduate degrees in Finance from Northern Illinois University and Computer Science from Northeastern Illinois University, as well as, a certificate in Microsoft Foundation Classes and Visual C++ from DePaul University. Barry received his specialized Master's Degree from the College of Business Administration at Florida International University in Management Information Systems. He also has an Executive Education certificate from Harvard Business School in Leading Professional Service Firms.

Specialties

Barry is a Microsoft Certified Professional and Technology Specialist in SQL Server databases, ITIL®V3 & CompTia Security+ certified, member of the Project Management Institute, Agile Alliance, and Infraguard unit of the Federal Bureau of Investigations. He also has expertise in ITIL Service Management and Agile SCRUM development.

Microsoft Certified Professional MCP, SQL Server, .NET, OOD, GIS, ESRI ArcObjects, Project Management Institute Professional Member, CompTia Security + Professional
