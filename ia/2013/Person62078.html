Jennifer Gniadecki's Summary

More companies using social media means the only way for your business to stand out is through engaging, friendly, high-quality writing. That's just one reason you want to talk to me.

I am a freelance social media writer. My work history focuses on social media writing in the verticals of local businesses, food, beverage, and cosmetics. I've run Facebook pages, Twitter feeds, and maintained YouTube accounts. I enjoy making sure multiple social media channels are working in harmony across networks, but now I'm focusing more on the content. The catch-phrase used to be "content is king" but that needs to be updated to "quality content is king."

If your website does not incorporate high-quality writing, you will be passed over for a website that does. It's as simple as that.

I have been published online at Ebony.com, Walmart.com, At-A-Glance.com writing articles about staycations, how to fight the flu, and choosing the right calendar for your schedule and life. I have written brochures and corporate pieces with a professional - but friendly - tone. If you are looking for copy that is friendly, without being lowbrow or silly, you are looking for me.

Do you want a writer who will engage your clients?
Do you want a writer who listens to what you want done?
Do you want a writer who respects a deadline?

Email (jennydecki@beyondmom.com) or call (708-224-9560) today and we can talk about your project and I'll be happy to provide a quote!
