Keith Daulton's Summary

A creative technologist with 13+ years of experience focused on the creation of user interfaces, whether it be development or design. My passion lies at the intersection of the two.

On the development side, I architect and write elegant JavaScript as well as structure zen-garden-ish HTML and CSS. I have a deep understanding of cross-browser compatibility and web-standards. I've done UI work on several web application stacks including PHP, Java, Grails and .NET.

From a design perspective, I have produced simple sketches to mock-ups to final design. I take great care in ensuring that my designs are as user friendly as possible and at the same time, make technological sense.

I can speak geek and artist and can translate concepts from both so that end-users can understand them.

I love thinking up and developing new products and/or services that leverages my creative skills and satisfies the challenge junkie in me.
