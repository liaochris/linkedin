Dave Surber's Summary

• Award-Winning film/video maker with over twelve years of production experience including 9 years of production supervisory experience. Experience in electronic news gathering, mobile, and studio-style television productions. Consistent record for exceptional artistic creativity, while keeping projects on time and on budget. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

• Wrote, produced, and directed "Nananafreakinbooboo" a five-minute DV short that won grand prize in a nationwide filmmaker's contest entitled "Focus in Film" sponsored by J. Walter Thompson and Ford Motor Company. The winnings included a meeting with a major Hollywood development executive and a $15,000.00 cash prize.

• Named Arizona's new up-and-coming filmmaker by the Film Makers Forum at Scottsdale Center for the Arts in 2002.

• Directly involved on over 140 video productions for MCTV (Maricopa Colleges Television) within a period of 18 months during 2002-2004.
