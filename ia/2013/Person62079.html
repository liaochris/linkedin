Jenny DeVaughn's Summary

As the senior director of Employer Branding and Sourcing for ADP, Jenny DeVaughn is instrumental in driving the internal and external employment brand, mobile and social media strategies that set the company apart as an employer of choice. At Randstad Sourceright, DeVaughn developed and delivered strategic recruitment campaigns and innovative solutions to help clients around the world enhance their brand and engage even the most difficult to find talent as the Sr. Director, Employment Branding, Mobile and Social Media.

Previously, DeVaughn was the Social Media manager for at Waste Management where she was responsible for all internal and external communication strategies and tactics over of the company’s multiple social media platforms, including marketing, customer experience, and talent acquisition. Earlier in her career, DeVaughn was director of Social Strategy at Bernard Hodes Group. There she developed effective solutions to help organizations leverage digital tools to enhance their brands and gain a competitive recruiting advantage. During her tenure, she also served as the vice president of Social Media for the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) Atlanta chapter.

Consistently ranked as one of LinkedIn’s top ten most connected women, DeVaughn’s professional strategies are heavily rooted in the value of networking. An often sought-after industry expert, she has shared invaluable insight on social media strategies for recruiting at SXSW, mRecruitingcamp, SHRM Talent Management and Leadership Conferences, and at Facebook's headquarters. Her comments have also been featured in Wall Street Journal, the Atlanta Business Chronicle, and on NBC, among others. Jenny was recognized by Workforce Management magazine as a 2013 Game Changer and a distinguished HR professional under 40.

DeVaughn holds a Bachelor’s degree in Business Marketing from the University of Phoenix. Follow her on Twitter at @JennyDeVaughn - http://twitter.com/jennydevaughn.
