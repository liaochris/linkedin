Cannon Berrong's Summary

Through working with businesses and individuals, we understand complex financial issues, and how they can affect your life goals. We work with clients to fully understand their situation, and look to build a relationship that will serve their needs over time. Equipped with a team of specialists and access to a network of over 50 of America's top insurance companies and financial institutions, we provides innovative solutions and professional direction to help our clients achieve their objectives.

Specialties

Business owners- We specialize in efficiently helping clients find the most advantageous way to provide their employees with a generous benefit package for a low cost.

Individuals- We specialize in helping high net worth clients to develop tax-efficient financial plans, whether a client is focused on managing current success, or protecting and preserving assets over the long-term.
