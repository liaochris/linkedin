Grant Palmer's Summary

More than twenty years experience with start-up, early stage and established companies developing a wide variety of medical devices, including implantable devices from concept through regulatory approval and into post-market surveillance. Directed Regulatory Affairs, Clinical Research and Quality Assurance functions for manufacturers of Class II and III medical devices. Developed global regulatory and clinical strategies. Trained and directed cross-functional teams in the implementation of CE Mark and FDA Regulations. Coordinated regulatory submissions including IDE, PMA, 510(k), Certificates for Foreign Government, and product Technical Files for CE Marking. Acted as corporate liaison with regulatory agencies including FDA and European Notified Bodies. Performed internal QSR/GMP audits and vendor qualification audits. Implemented complaint handling, MDR and Medical Device Vigilance systems. Direct experience in interaction, negotiations and inspections by FDA and other regulatory bodies.
