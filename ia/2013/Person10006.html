Tara Reed's Summary

I have been licensing art since 2000, fully focusing my efforts since 2003. My creativity and artistic talents, coupled with a degree in Marketing and experience in sales, is proving to be an asset for me.

I am actively working to expand my licensing business through quality partnerships with manufactures who license artwork for their products. I understand that artwork facilitates product sales and enjoy collaborating with manufacturers to create successful designs.

In 2008, I began sharing my experience and teaching artists what art licensing really is, what the job looks like, and if they are interested -- how to license their art. For artists interested in more information, go to www.ArtLicensingInfo.com.

Tara Reed's Publications

  • How to Understand Art Licensing Contracts

    • eBook co-written with Artist Tara Reed and Maria Brophy
    • November 9, 2011
    Authors: Tara Reed, Maria Brophy

    The e-book on art licensing contracts for artists! Written by Maria Brophy and Tara Reed, this book will help artists gain confidence in their knowledge of art licensing, negotiating and how licensing deals work. There is no other resource out there like this one; it will save an artist years of experience!

  • How to Be a Press Friendly Artist

    • eBook
    • April 7, 2011
    Authors: Tara Reed

    The eBook “How to be a Press Friendly Artist” is to help artists understand how to write press releases, prepare press kits, build a media contact list and become a resource for members of the press.

    Practical steps are offered to help artists think through what they have to offer to the press and their readers and learn to write press releases that will get read and better yet – printed. Artists also learn how to create digital and physical press kits that explain who they are and what they do as well as how to make your website a resource for the press to find information, quotes and images when they need them quickly.

  • How to Get Started in Art Licensing - 2nd Edition

    • eBook
    • April 29, 2012
    Authors: Tara Reed

    Each artist comes to licensing from a different background and takes a different path. Learning from several people is a great way to discover what will work for you. As well as my experience, in this eBook I will interview another artist, an agent and a manufacturer so you learn from people involved in different aspects of the business.

  • How to Find an Art Licensing Agent

    • eBook
    • September 2, 2010
    Authors: Tara Reed

    The goal of this eBook is to help artists understand the business and become attractive to potential agents. They will prepare their mind, their pitch and their portfolio.

    Artists will learn to present themselves in a way that makes them stand out from the crowd and they will learn to present their art so they feel more confident in their search process.

    From what to say on the phone to how to write a compelling query letter or email, artists will find usable information to help them find an agent that is a good fit for their business.

  • How to Find, Interact and Work with Manufacturers Who License Art

    • eBook
    • October 8, 2008
    Authors: Tara Reed

    This eBook is for artists who want to promote their own work in the art licensing industry. It gives strategies for finding companies that license artwork and tips to organize information and efforts.

    Other topics include providing step-by-step sales techniques for contacting manufacturers and building rapport, tips on turning leads into clients and common sense methods of turning new clients into repeat clients.

  • The Goal Wheel for Artists

    • eBook
    • December 20, 2009
    Authors: Tara Reed

    If you are an artist and you want to be in business, not just working at a hobby, there are three areas in which you need to set goals in order to succeed: goals to CREATE your art, goals to PROMOTE your art and goals to RECHARGE yourself. Isn't it a wonderful coincidence that there are also 3 primary colors? (I thought you'd agree!) I'll teach you how to work around the wheel and keep your business rolling forward.

  • How to Maximize Your Time and Investment in Art Licensing Trade Shows

    • eBook
    • December 2, 2008
    Authors: Tara Reed

    Tara Reed offers practical tips to help you plan your booth, man your booth and follow-up like a pro - making the time and investment in your art licensing trade show pay off. Decrease your learning curve by implementing tips and tactics that are proven to succeed.

  • How to Create Repeat Patterns in Photoshop

    • eBook
    • August 8, 2008
    Authors: Tara Reed

    Repeat patterns are used in many aspects of art licensing - from fabric and textile design to gift wrap and more, having the ability to provide your art set up in repeat will give you a leg up in licensing. These two eBooks teach you how to put your art in repeat in Photoshop - without having to sift and sort through many other techniques to figure it out. I create repeat patterns all the time so I know this works.
