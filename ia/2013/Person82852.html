Larry Pauley's Experience

Owner

MLB Memories

2009 – Present (4 years) Springfield, Missouri area

MLB Memories is a baseball memorabilia and collectibles business that is proud to say it is "a place where cherished memories and baseball memorabilia meet!" We accomplish our goal of enjoying and spreading the enjoyment of the national pastime via various retail outlets in the Springfield, MO area and through our website MLBMemories.com.

Because we are about MORE than just selling baseball cards, memorabilia, autographs and the like, our website also includes photos, random quotes, baseball related articles, links to various baseball organizations and more. Additionally, we are proud to serve our area through the provision of professional appraisal of baseball collections.
