Founding Partner

The Morpheus

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

May 2009 – April 2011 (2 years) Bangalore, India

c.2009 and you know what was broken in the Indian venture industry? Large-ticket late-stage deals and a non-existent seed/early stage venture market. In civilized investing nations the venture money is split 50:50; large number of small-ticket deals and a small number of late-stage big-ticket deals. Every Indian investor talked about the virtues of early-stage but few deals were happening. Joined Morpheus as the 3rd partner when it was a scrappy advisory firm. Raised Morpheus's 1st fund. Upped the game of seed-stage investing in India with small investments of INR 5 Lakhs ($12,000) each in about 15 startups/year and created Morpheus the brand. Not just money, but gave 100% time to portfolio companies & any entrepreneur who walked through the door or called for a meeting.

Fast forward to 2011 after 500+ meetings with entrepreneurs, investors, partners and participants of the venture community in 2 years, Morpheus has become synonymous with seed-stage investing in India. Spoke/moderated/keynoted at 20+ events. Got profiled/interviewed/quoted in major business newspapers, magazines & blogs, thus becoming an integral part of the larger Indian venture community.

Today Morpheus has raised a larger fund and no story is complete without Morpheus's mention when the category "seed investor" or "incubator" is discussed in the Indian startup ecosystem. Loved every day of it, future stars-aligning, and maybe after few gigs, I'll be back in a similar role after gaining few more locks of silver on the head!

Advised/Invested in Emo2, Grexit, InterviewStreet, Jumadi, Phitesla, Practo, Radbox, Reach Accountant, RedQuanta, RobotsAlive, Sarga, 99 Tests, Akosha, Mmp, Commonfloor, Instamedia, Crederity, DeskAway/BrightPod and 4 others that have since shutdown.
