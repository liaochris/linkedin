Harry Blanchard's Summary

Highly experienced user interface designer in telecommunications and information technology industries. Experienced university teacher and well published in the professional field of human factors and UI design. Broad range of practical design experience in interactive voice response systems and messaging systems, speech technology systems, hardware design, telecom control systems UI design, graphical user interfaces, web design, and small device design (cell phone, PDA applications). Special experience in designing interfaces using advanced speech technologies, such as natural language, and knowledgeable about speech technologies, and their usefulness to customers and end users. Experienced with customer contact and sales, also experienced with some HTML coding, web design tools and other prototyping tools. Experienced systems engineer and proficent at writing requirements. Experienced with customer facing responsibilities and interview techniques

Specialties

User interface design for speech technology applications, human factors for web design, information technology hardware and software. Systems engineering and system test experience. Experienced university teacher and researcher in Human Factors and Experimental, Cognitive, & Perceptual Psychology.
