  • MYLAPS.com

    • oktober 2012 tot Huidig
    Teamleden: Koen Romers, Ivana Setiawan, Jeroen van Eck, Ruben Bos, Sander de Gans, Ivar van den Broek, Rick Meijer, Mike Yau, Viola Mashoed, Jeroen van Velthoven, Arjen Tebbenhof, Robert Leefmans, Marleen van den Brand

    MYLAPS creates solutions for professional timers, event organisers, track and club owners, federations, as well as individual racers and athletes. The product enhance the experience for all sports categories. MYLAPS asked Mangrove to create a strategy and a new online platform that shows the value of their solutions; big data. By showing data in a meaningful order, and making it able to share data, the experience before, during, and after an event is intensified. The new MYLAPS.com is a first step to a new online eco system which provides athletes and racers true insights to optimise their performance.
