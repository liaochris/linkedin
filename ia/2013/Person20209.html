Carl Winans' Summary

What I Do in 15 Seconds or Less
I plan, design and develop websites, start-ups and marketing strategies. I have a passion for great product design from physical products to web apps and user interfaces. But mostly, I just enjoy the process of making ideas happen.

Carl Who?
I’m a new media strategist, designer and technology junkie with a marketing background and more than a decade of experience. My background in interface design, usability and my deep understanding of new media, social networking and start-up trends allows me to make an impact across a wide variety of projects.

It All Started When
I began my career freelancing while attending Michigan State University, founded RIPE Interactive in early 1998 after graduating with a degree in advertising. In late 2003, I and four friends founded Elemental with nothing more than our talent, chemistry & teamwork. Elemental was an interactive firm that was hell-bent on retaining creative & technical talent in the midst of Michigan’s challenging economy. I have subsequently built a network of strategic alliances and clients throughout Detroit, Chicago and Los Angeles and launched Oceanvue in late 2008.

I'm also currently working on a new web start-up called refre.sh that will "refresh the way you look at the web" which was born out of Start-Up Weekend Detroit.

By The Way
My entrepreneurial drive asserted itself during my college career when, by day, I would skip classes to design, print and sell t-shirts out of my dorm room and, by night, attend the local comedy club to perform amateur stand-up. I found myself quickly returning to classes, however, when MSU sent me a letter stating I could not run a business out of my dormitory and the comedy club strongly advised me not to quit my day job.

Specialties: Web Design, Web Development, Online Social Network Development, New Media Strategy, Branding, Marketing Strategy, Interface Design, Website Usability, Product Development, Inventing, Start-Ups

Carl Winans' Experience

Founder

FocusMail.me

January 2013 – Present (9 months) Detroit, MI

One afternoon I walked outside to get the mail from the mailbox and there was nothing inside. About 30 minutes later, even though I knew the mail carrier had already come and I had no mail, I walked back and checked the mail again. Almost out of habit. And then I realized, I do the same thing with email. It made me start to think about email and what would the world would be like if it were more like snail mail. Out of that came a lot of thought around efficiencies, focus and how we use email. To me, email isn't broken, but how we use it is. www.FocusMail.me

Founder

Oceanvue

October 2008 – Present (5 years) Detroit, Michigan

Oceanvue is a small idea development and marketing firm where an idea is just an idea until someone makes it happen. When ideas just pile up, they build a wall around you that makes it hard to execute. The company was born out of our need to actually build new things from that pile and to move from idea generation to meaningful action. We've failed more than we've succeeded, but that's probably our greatest strength now.

Co-Founder

Beershorts

December 2010 – Present (2 years 10 months) East Lansing, MI

Years ago, my dad and I were having a beer one day and he had the idea of shorts with sewn-in insulated can koozies to keep two extra beers cold. A few years later, I found myself interested in how physical products are designed and manufactured and how you then sell that product starting from scratch. So I called up my dad and my best friend and the three of us set out to make the world's first pair of Beershorts. Long story short, it was an interesting road and we now have our shorts for sale online at www.beershorts.com!

Carl Winans' Projects

  • Refre.sh

    • January 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Carl Winans

    Born out of Start-Up Weekend Detroit, Refre.sh (formally LiveSnip) helps you clip and save live views of the web a single dashboard. The web is a fast and wild place. Refresh keeps up. Drag over part of a web page to create a view, then watch as it's updated in realtime on your dashboard. Whether you are following stock prices or just friends on Twitter, Refresh is always up to date.

  • Shirtmob

    • April 2012 to Present
    Team Members: Carl Winans

    I had an idea in the shower to combine t-shirts with coupons. A few days later, Shirtmob was hatched. The initial premise was to turn shirts into wearable, reusable coupons. We worked with Michigan State Students to get feedback on the idea from students and local businesses. While I think the idea has legs, I'm not sure this is the kind of company I want to build. At least in its current iteration.
