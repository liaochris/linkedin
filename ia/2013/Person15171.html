Bret Wagner's Summary

Since starting my career in 2003 as a field engineer for Schlumberger, I made a commitment to the oil and gas industry. I grew up in the oilfield, first in hydraulic fracturing offshore Gulf of Mexico, then helping commercialize water-treatment technology for the industry, and now running an oil & gas software company.

With ten years of oilfield and software experience, I returned to my roots and started SpudShare.com. One of the biggest challenges for oil & gas land and investor groups is collaborating on projects with mountains of paperwork and managing access to those projects. SpudShare helps facilitate collaboration through online workspaces and managed user access. We're deployed for document-intensive tasks like pulling title, buy-side and sell-side due diligence, and promoting private investment opportunities.

One more thing - I just started the Oil & Gas Investment Council. We provide complimentary speakers to any oil & gas industry trade association.

Our group includes:
* a top CPA who advises how to value prospects before a sale
* a top attorney who can discuss navigating divestitures
* an experienced underwriter who can discuss insurance
* an experienced leadership, training and presentation consultant 
* an information technology consultant experienced in leveraging technology to get deals done faster and more efficiently
* yours truly who can discuss the next generation of young investors - Gen X and Gen Y'ers who are in the oil industry.

If you need a complimentary speaker, please send me a message through LinkedIn or email me at bret@spudshare.com

I look forward to meeting you. In the meantime, please keep me in mind should you come across a particular challenge you feel I might help you address.
