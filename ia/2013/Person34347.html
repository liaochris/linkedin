Dennis Van Staalduinen's Summary

Dennis Van Staalduinen is the founder and principal brand strategist for Brandvelope Consulting - a boutique marketing consultancy specializing in positioning, messaging, and brand strategy.

> Training and public speaking
> Message development
> Whole brand analysis
> Competetive marketing research
> Re-branding consultation and project support

After 7 years at Brandvelope, and more than 13 years in Marketing and Communications, Mr. Van Staalduinen has helped many dozens of companies and organizations make better sense to their customers.

Specialties

messaging, positioning, brand strategy, branding and identity, high tech corporate brands, high tech product brand architecture, government program branding, retail marketing, competitive analysis
