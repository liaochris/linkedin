Sharon McWilliams' Summary

Sharon is a certified master retreat coach, intuitive life coach, woman's empowerment coach, speaker/performer, author, singer, songwriter, educator, Reiki master/teacher and radio show host of The Sharon McWilliams Show. A co-creator with wise women, Sharon shares her discoveries with you in her multifaceted experiential retreats and programs for the midlife and baby boomer woman!

Specialties: Sharon uses many modalities and tools in her coaching sessions, depending on the client's needs with energetic, sound and color healing. Sharon also offers her clients custom retreats, using the healing power of nature. Working with women to rediscover their wisdom within and their amazing intuitive processes especially those who are perimenopausal or menopausal. Life the second time around is much greater than you have been led to believe! Come take the walk of the wise woman with me.

And, Wise Woman, you are invited to listen in every Monday evening at 9 pm eastern time to the Sharon McWilliams Show. Take the walk and meet other Wise Women---listen to their wisdom and discover the tools they use to live their lives fully. Here is the site location: http://www.blogtalkradio.com/voaradio
