Eric Boggs' Summary

Hi - I'm Eric Boggs. I'm an entrepreneur and experienced SaaS executive based in Durham, NC.

I started Catfoxtail in 2013 to provide fractional sales/marketing executive services to early- and mid-stage SaaS companies and marketing agencies. We help companies develop sales and marketing strategy, build execution process and capacity, and ultimately drive revenue.

Previously, I was the Founder & CEO at Argyle Social - a social media management platform provider. Argyle powers social for thousands of users around the world at companies of all sizes, with a focus on agency, e-commerce, SaaS/technology, and retail/brand verticals.

Prior to Argyle, I was the first employee and part of the senior management team at Bronto - an email marketing software provider. I held sales, services, and product roles and helped lay foundations that have supported Bronto’s growth to an industry leader and 150+ employee powerhouse.

I completed my undergraduate studies at UNC Chapel Hill in 2002 and earned an MBA at UNC’s Kenan-Flagler Business school in 2009, where I was a Dean’s Fellow.

I volunteer my free time as a mentor for Triangle Startup Factory, a Board Director for The Southern Documentary Fund, and a casual advisor to several interesting people building interesting companies.
