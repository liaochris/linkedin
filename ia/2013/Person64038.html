Jimmy Ghazal's Summary

Jimmy Ghazal joined "Quantum Group" one of the leading communications groups in the region. "Quantum" is made up of several companies from the various industry disciplines: Advertising (“M&C Saatchi MENA”), Branding (“Brand Central”), Media (“Vertical”), Production (“Firehorse”), Digital and Content (“Mercury”).

His Journey in the group started in “BrandCentral” as a corporate identity and Arabic typography specialist where he had worked on several iconic regional brands ranging from government institutions to media, tourism, and telecom. His expertise lied in creating visual identities as well as being an Arabic typography expert developing customised award-winning typefaces.

In 2006 Jimmy moved into the advertising arena with "Saatchi & Saatchi" now "M&C Saatchi MENA" where he headed the digital and multimedia division leading the agency into the digital age. He has contributed in creating several award-winning digital campaigns for various local and regional clients.

Today, Jimmy has several roles in the group. He is heading the digital operations at “Quantum” where he is in charge of digital thinking and implementations for group companies. He also heading the digital operations at “Mercury” the specialised digital and content driven division of the group.

Jimmy Ghazal graduated as a graphic designer from Notre Dame University (NDU) in 2000. He was published and recognised several times for his work.

Specialties

Digital Communications, Strategic Branding, Arabic Typography, Multimedia Solutions, Social Media, Digital Strategy
