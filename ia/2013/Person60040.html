Laurie Lamson's Summary

Laurie loves helping tell great stories. She is highly versatile and creative, with a proven ability to complete projects on time and on budget.

She has a down-to-earth, collaborative approach - clients value her enthusiastic, encouraging nature as much as her work ethic. She is proud of her long-term client relationships.

Laurie has written feature screenplays on assignment for indie producers (2 moving toward production). She is the co-editor of Now Write! Screenwriting and is delighted to share her passion for screenwriting as the teleconference host for International Screenwriters Association.

She has written over 20 corporate and educational scripts. Many of the videos made from her scripts won multiple awards like the Aegis, the Telly and the Golden Cine Eagle. She also edits books and writes copy for clients.

Laurie earned a BFA in Film production from NYU Tisch School, which lead to freelance opportunities as a production assistant, script supervisor and production coordinator. She independently produced and directed short projects and worked full-time as a writer/associate producer overseeing video productions for clients such as Bank of America, Clairol, The Gap and VH-1.

Her own projects reflect a unique voice - edgy, witty, yet compassionate.
