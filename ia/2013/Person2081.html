Adrienne Graham's Summary

I help clients repair, scale and grow their business by customizing strategies & immediate implementation to improve processes, increase profits, plug revenue holes, optimize products & services, grow their teams, and sharpen their brands by leveraging what they've already got.

Yes, I'm the author of No, You Can't Pick My Brain on Forbes & the book.

I founded Empower Me! Corporation (www.empowerme.org), a business Growth Strategies consultancy with brand extensions in media, publishing and entrepreneurial education. I host Views From the Top Radio Show, and created Empower Me Institute, Empower Me! Magazine, Empowered for Growth TV & Next Level Business Strategies.

I've got 20 years in the Recruiting game, so I know more than a little bit about recruiting. I launched The Red Shoe Agency (www.theredshoeagency.com) to specialize in recruiting women in technology and engineering providing talent acquisition, talent development, talent management & employer branding services. We also provide career management services to women in technology and engineering. There are PLENTY of talented women in technology & engineering.

I'm a disrupter not afraid to aim high & never want to be the smartest person in the room.

I've been in the media as a Subject Matter Expert in recruiting, networking, diversity in technology, entrepreneurship, small business & career management including MSNBC, Entrepreneur Magazine, Fortune, Black Enterprise, Inc Magazine, Fox Small Business, Financial Times, Essence & Forbes. I've spoken on a variety of panels & delivered keynotes. I've written for Forbes.com.

My radio show was twice selected a Top 100 Heavy Hitter Radio Show for Small Business by Small Business Trends, & was quoted in Success Magazine, Fast Company Magazine and the Dylan Ratigan Show.

I'm a Mentor to startup companies of the Straight Shot Accelerator Program.

SPECIALTIES: Business Growth Strategies, Recruitment, Talent Acquisition, Employment Branding, Career Management

Adrienne Graham's Publications

  • No, You Can't Pick My Brain

    • Empower Me! Corporation/Empower Me! Publishing
    • January 23, 2012
    Authors: Adrienne Graham

    What started as a blog post on Forbes.com in March 2011, launched a worldwide conversation for weeks after. That conversation has been reignited in 2012 and people are more heated than ever. Many service professionals have no idea how to assign value to their knowledge and most don’t know exactly how or where to draw the line between free friendly advice and fee based consultations. They find themselves being taken advantage of by people looking to get maximum information for no cost. Some people will say you have to give away a lot of free to earn paying clients. But I say that’s the quickest way to train people to expect to always get free stuff from you.

    It’s time to teach people where the boundaries are and how to value our expertise.

  • The Business of Self-Publishing

    • Empower Me! Corporation/Empower Me! Publishing
    • July 2012
    Authors: Adrienne Graham

    So, you want to publish books. Writing a book is the easy part. But it doesn’t stop there. Did you know that self-publishing your own book is a business that goes way beyond writing it and putting it on Amazon? Even if it’s only part of your business strategy, it’s still serious business. Many authors step out for the first time without a clue how to manage the process, market and make sales.

    This self-study course focuses on:

    * Avoiding the pitfalls of a first time authors
    * Extending your book into other products, services & opportunities
    * Ways to maintain complete control over your content (and profits)
    * Maximizing author tools to keep your book front of mind
    * Ways to position your book to attract traditional publisher’s attention
    * Managing your book like a separate business entity
    * Using your book to build your brand

    *Disclaimer: This course is not for fiction writers. Although many of the techniques and tips can be adapted and tweaked for fiction writers.*

    This self-study course includes:

    * A 101 page guidebook
    * 12 audio mp3s
    * A Resource Guide packed with a variety of resources to ensure your publishing & business success (including what I’ve used to publish, print, distribute and market my own books)
    * A Glossary with key terms and definitions so you’re in the industry know
    * A 98 page Toolkit complete with sample documents and templates
    * Editable Excel Documents to keep track of your business finances
    * And templates of a variety of book layouts sized and ready to use.

    By the time you finish this course you will not only be ready to self-publish your book, but turn it into a thriving business or publishing arm to your company. This is not a course on how to write. It’s a course about managing the business of writing.
