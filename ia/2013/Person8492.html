Ann E (Annie) Oliveri's Summary

Ann E. Oliveri has a deep understanding of the social, emotional and learning needs of children. She holds a Masters Degree (Dist.) with a Special Education major. Her major passion in teaching is inclusion, which she views as the heart of education. Also holding a Diploma in Teaching English as a Second Language, from the Royal Society of Arts in London, Annie has spent 30 years teaching in Australia, Japan and Hong Kong. She was recently awarded the Dean’s Commendation for Outstanding Achievement (USC).

In 2005, Annie Oliveri turned her attention to helping more families and has established Raising Happy Kids. Co http://raisinghappykids.co a global community for parents, teachers and homeschoolers.

Specialties: Raising Happy Kids, Special Educational Needs including Giftedness, Literacy and Numeracy, Learning (dis)Abilities and English as a Second Language
