Mary Bourke's Summary

Mary Bourke was born in Ireland and raised in South Bend, Indiana. She began her professional career as an assistant to the Football Coaches at the University of Notre Dame. She has also held senior positions in the public and private sectors. She brings her vast experience to the brokerage business. She has been a highly successful real estate professional in the Northern Virginia and D.C. markets for nearly 23 years. Mary's personal goal is to simplify the process of buying and selling real estate, and to satisfy everyone's needs to the best of her ability. Her extensive experience and proven track record are factors you can count on when working with her. She uses state of the art technology to communicate with her clients. and assist her clients in marketing their homes.
