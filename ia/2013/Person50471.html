Greg Zlevor's Experience

President

Westwood International

1993 – Present (20 years) Global

Greg Zlevor is the President and Founder of Westwood International, a consulting collaborative that's reimagining what it means to lead wisely in a global age. Westwood's signature, global-scale action learning programs and change management initiatives have energized the vision of global brands like Johnson & Johnson, Volvo, Pfizer, and General Electric.

Westwood International's highly customized leadership solutions include:

• Global-scale action learning programs
• Worldwide change management initiatives.
• Executive and Team coaching
• Strategic leadership and long-term planning sessions
• Executive leadership development programs
• Teamwork and group facilitation

Greg has over 25 years of experience in executive development and education and has served as a coach, consultant, and facilitator for companies in Europe, Asia, North America, South America, and Australia.

Current and past Westwood International clients include: Johnson & Johnson, General Electric, Volvo, Harvard University, Singapore Government, Pfizer, US Army, Global Institute of Leadership Development, Janssen Diagnostics, Boston College, Arthur Anderson, Capital Region Complete Dental Care, Children First, DePuy Synthes, Family Health International, Kenilworth Dental, L3 Wescam, Leap Source, Massachusetts General Hospital, National Institutes of Health, Seattle Study Club, Singapore Government, Singapore Police, Think Better, and more.

Under Greg's leadership, Westwood International has a mission to give back, supporting not-for-profit organizations that help homeless children and at-risk youth across the United States, Mexico, Thailand, India, and South America.

Greg is also the founder of FOG (For Our Growth), an annual gathering of collaboration, and he is a frequent speaker and facilitator at the Global Institute of Leadership Development.
