Jay Gerland's Summary

Personally, I have been around computers since I was a kid and began working full time with computers while studying Computer Science at the University of Buffalo in the early 90's. In 1994 I was the first employee for After Five Technology, a local web design firm in Buffalo, NY. Shortly after that, I moved on to become the first Webmaster for Canisius College. Two years later I joined the startup Chek.com working on branded email solutions for companies such as Major League Baseball and Budweiser. Chek.com would eventually become Synacor, Inc. where I currently direct teams of developers building large-scale portals for the country's biggest Cable companies and ISPs including Charter, Centurylink, Road Runner, Verizon and Windstream.
