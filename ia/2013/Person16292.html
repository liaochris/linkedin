Brad Vetter's Summary

Brad is a General Management & Digital Executive with a successful track record of building businesses.

Brad has worked globally for venture capital funded start-ups, private equity backed enterprises and Fortune 500 companies. He has been a partner in building brands like Hyatt, Glade, J. Hilburn, Goodyear, Dell, and Orbitz by continually developing and improving the customer experience.

Previously, he had P&L responsibility to 400MM for an Internet Retailer Top 100 company and is currently building a new consumer business for a private equity funded enterprise in the automotive aftermarket.

Specialties: Building Businesses, E-Commerce, eBusiness Management, Global Commerce, Entrepreneurship, Business Development, Digital Marketing, Product Management, Product Development, Revitalizing Under-Performing Businesses, Program Management, Social Networking, Project Management, Outsourcing & BPO
