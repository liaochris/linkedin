Barry Spencer's Experience

co-founder

Motivate Generosity

January 2012 – Present (1 year 8 months) Alpharetta, GA

Our mission is to motivate a billion dollars in generosity through family and to causes with no regrets. The wealth of money, experience, and values is too often squandered to needless taxes, lost opportunities, and on purposes never intended leaving the generous person have regrets about the decisions made and decisions not made due to confusion and uncertainty. We show wealthy individuals and families how to partner with causes that matter to them so they can be more generous in ways that matter to them and help their legacy and family. We show charities how to make stronger connections with donors so they can be the charity of choice for interested and able donors.
