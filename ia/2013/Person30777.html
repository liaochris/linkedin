Darren O'Brien's Summary

Darren is a professional photographer and photojournalist with over 7 years experience working for newspapers and magazines.
He is the owner of Freedom Light Photography which offers photographic services for Businesses, ranging from corporate portraiture to advertising and promotion. Experienced in location photography, advertising, travel photography and fashion photography.

Freedom Light Photography has special offers for small start up businesses. Please contact Darren for more information.

Darren is a member of the SWPP and SIMMP professional bodies.

Specialties

Editorial, Press, Portraiture, Corporate Events, Advertising, promotional stills, marketing and PR stills, Modelling and Fashion stills. Adobe Photoshop CS5 and Lightroom 4.
