Adam Bonner's Summary

My passion is talking to people about technology, whether that is a lecture hall with 100 computing students or just somebody I've bumped into with the latest phone. I love hearing what people have to show me and in turn try either learning or teaching people something every day.

I completed my Masters in Computer Science (with Distinction) in January 2013. University provided me with a wealth of experience helping me promote myself, develop business contacts, work in partnership with world brands such as Microsoft. Most importantly, it give me a platform in which I get to talk to interested students about obscure things I've learned to help them with their studies

In 5 years’ time I want to be in a position where I am respected for my opinion on technological/platform decisions and at the same time come out of work every day having learned something new.
