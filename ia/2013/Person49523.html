Ian Gordon's Summary

I have been involved in developing and executing online marketing strategies and digital publishing since 1998, when converting a document to PDF was a really big deal and websites were nothing more than electronic yellow pages ads.

I have owned a small business in one form or another, for most of my adult life. I bring this business owner's mindset and more than 15 years of online marketing experience to every project I take on.

My company, Real Digital, helps businesses achieve their online goals. We do this by using the right combination of Website Strategy, Social Media Strategy, Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Pay Per Click Advertising (PPC), Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO), and Web Analytics Consulting.

Specialties: Website strategy including traffic and conversion optimization, social media consulting, content development, digital marketing, public speaking, training.
