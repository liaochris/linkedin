Chris Saribay's Experience

Front Office Guest Service Manager

Aulani, a Disney Resort and Spa

Public Company; 10,001+ employees; DIS; Entertainment industry

June 2011 – Present (2 years 4 months) Hawaiian Islands

I played an integral role in the opening of Disney's first mixed use resort outside of its theme park destinations. I developed, implemented and trained standard operating practices for Front Office operations, specifically focused on Front Desk arrival processes and room inventory operations. Following the opening of the resort, I led our Front Desk and Concierge teams on a daily basis. Based on these contributions, I was promoted into my current role of Front Office Guest Service Manager in July of 2012. I am currently responsible for the broader oversight of the collective Front Office, which includes Front Desk, Concierge, PBX, Vacation Planning, VIP Guest Services, Bell Services, Valet, Curbside Arrival Greeters, Bell/Valet Dispatch and our Cultural Greeters. Within these areas, beyond Guest Service and management responsibility, I am responsible for developing curated and targeted communications.

I also champion new and emerging technology and how it can be practically applied to our operations. I am widely regarded for my ability to leverage technology and develop tools in several ways not commonly used at other Disney resorts to support the organization's green standards and to enhance communication in operations, with some projects leading to widespread adoption across the organization as operational best practices. It is with this drive, knowledge and experience that I am frequently tasked with creating solutions for not only our own area, but areas around the resort and from the executive level.
