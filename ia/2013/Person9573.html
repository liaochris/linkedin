Ariq Azad's Summary

I am currently focusing on addressing health disparities through scalable mHealth and social enterprise interventions. My current work involves understanding how social inequality is embodied as health inequality and the role of mHealth at this interface. My long term interests include understanding social medicine and working towards structural change that tackles the root causes of health injustice.

A senior at Columbia University with formal training in biology. Much of my independent study focuses on theoretical understandings of the social determinants of health and the role of technology in this context. I am a software engineer by self-training.

Interests: Social medicine, global health, health disparities, mHealth, social enterprise, software engineering, social justice, philosophy, religion.
