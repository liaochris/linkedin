Lisa Crawford's Summary

Organizational/leadership development consultant/executive coach with more than 20 years of expertise in human resources, training and OD. Helps organizations implement The Leadership Architect(R) suite of tools which are the gold standard of research-based, experience-tested, talent managment tools. Specialty area is IMPLEMENTATION - not only teaching you HOW to use the tools for yourself, but also teaching you HOW TO IMPLEMENT them in your own organization and what you can expect to happen when you do.

Specialties

Current independent associate of KORN/FERRY Leadership and Talent Consulting - powered by LOMINGER. And I own my own consulting business - The Crawford Group. My consulting practice focuses on all phases of talent management including; succession planning, performance management, high potential identification, executive coaching, organization development, selection, and individual development. I offer certifications in San Diego on all of the Lominger Leadership Architect(R) tools.
