Brad Benson's Summary

In 2011 & 2012, I spent the summers in Healy, Alaska working as a Flight Controller/Office Manager/Pilot for Fly Denali.

In 2009 and 2010, I managed a Jeep tour business just outside Denali National Park in Alaska. Besides being a great hub for adventure and living in an outdoorsman's paradise, the logistics of managing a business in this environment provided some unique challenges.

Before my experiences in Alaska, I had been involved in movie theater operations since 1999, when my brother founded Movie Tavern (dinner, drinks, and movies) with the first locations in the Fort Worth, Texas area. From 1999 to 2008, we expanded to 14 stores in five states with 1500 employees, making us the nation's largest operator of cinema eateries (by screen count). Over those years, I held various positions within the company: General Manager, District Manager, Director of I.T., and Director of Real Estate and Development. In May 2008, the company was sold to a private equity firm which continues expansion of the brand.

I am now a partner in a cinema venture that began in 2008 after the sale of Movie Tavern. Cinergy Cinemas currently operates two new, state-of-the-art, cinema/family entertainment centers in Texas with a third location due to open in Midland, Texas in 2013.

In addition, I maintain active participation with two fire departments--Granbury, Texas and Healy, Alaska.

• Very proficient with computer hardware, software, networking, troubleshooting, and point of sale systems

• Enjoy fast-paced environments and management of challenging conditions
