John McMillan's Summary

John McMillan - "The Software Marketing Expert" - specialises in bringing software to market. He is the author of the Kindle books "How to Write Software for Sale" and “How to Market Your Business”. Bringing software to market is much more than marketing. Many organisations who want to sell software do not have experience of the software industry. Their offering needs to be user friendly and attractive. Quality and support systems have to be put in place.

He learned to develop, market and manage computer applications for computer giant ICL. Since then he has been a director of or adviser to some 20 software companies . He has worked with universities to turn their research into commercial products and has given seminars on business at Cambridge and other universities..

He chaired the Collaborate2Innovate Commercial group that encouraged high technology companies to increase opportunities through networking. He is President of the Sudbury Chamber of Commerce; at one point Sudbury was found to be the best performing town centre in the UK. As a Rotarian he was responsible for a campaign that raised £13,500 for the Asian tsunami disaster.

John is a highly experienced developer which means he can both "get his hands dirty" in technical matters and can help with strategy and marketing.

Achievements

John has numerous achievements, his favourites are:

* Created a software division for a company with no IT skills and lead them to £700K of software sales a year

* Re-designing the interface for a package and reducing user training was from three days to one hour

* Set up a support system that achieved ISO 9000 without change

Specialties: Bringing software to market, project delivery, analysis and design, software engineering and development, user interfaces, quality systems and internal procedures, design marketing messages, demonstrations, setting up help desks, Delphi, PHP.
