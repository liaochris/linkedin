Chud Wendle's Summary

After spending 16+ years (last 10 as President/Dealer) in the car business, I chose to leave the family business to start life all over again. Since August 2007, along with Stejer Development I have worked on constructing a 72,000 square foot shopping center. Specifically, I have worked on demolition, permitting, environmental, entitlement issues, leasing and financing. The project is currently 75% leased (Starbucks, Red Lobster, DSW Shoes, Ulta Beauty, Honey Baked Hams, Dress Barn, STCU and Honey Baked Hams).

The skills that I developed in the automotive industry (developing relationships, leadership, marketing, networking, and communication) are skills that will greatly assist me in real estate. The experience that I have had with developing the shopping center will assist me on the commercial real estate side.

With the experience and skills I developed at the dealership and through the development of our shopping center, I fell in love with real estate. Negotiations, communication and organization have created a foundation for commercial and residential real estate in Pullman, WA.

Specialties

Negotiations, marketing, credibility and communication
