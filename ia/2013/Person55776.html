1982 – Present (31 years)

I design visual communication materials that make a difference and that exceed client expectations. I specialize in working with clients that are service organizations concerned with improving quality of life, supporting sustainability, and promoting a better world for everyone. I enjoy collaborating with people who are also good at what they do.

I am a good listener, a dynamic problem solver, and an organized project manager. Most of my work comes from repeat business and referrals. I am always interested in new collaborations and new assignments to keep me inspired.

I offer full service design capabilities and I put together the right team appropriate to each project. One of my favorite testimonials is from Kaiser Permanente, a client for over twenty years: “Ms. Nelson’s artistic creativity and ability to translate our Annual Reports into beautiful works that deliver our key messages is unparalleled. She has always delivered our projects on time and under budget. Most importantly, she has done so with grace, humor, and total professionalism.”
