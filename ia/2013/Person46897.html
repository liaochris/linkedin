Garet Bedrosian, LCSW, CIRT, CBT, CET's Summary

Licensed Psychotherapist focused on providing individual and couples counseling services and workshops. Specializing in Imago couples and individual therapy, Bioenergetic therapy, Expressive Arts therapy and EMDR. Areas of interest are sexuality, addictions, mood disorders and grief.

I am also available for consultation.

Garet Bedrosian, LCSW, CIRT, CBT, CET's Experience

Owner

Garet Bedrosian, LCSW, CIRT, CBT, CET

Mental Health Care industry

1983 – Present (30 years)

I am a psychotherapist in private practice, workshop presenter and speaker. I facilitate Imago "Getting The Love You Want" workshops for couples, "Keeping The Love You Find" workshops for individuals and "Recovering Our Connection" for couples with addictions. I so co-facilitate Equus workshops for couples and individuals combining the genius of working with horses with the other modalities in which I'm trained.

The various treatment modalities I use are Imago Relationship Theory, Bioenergetic Analysis, Expressive Arts and EMDR. I see individuals, couples, families and groups as well as speaking, consulting and training.

I believe the most important aspect of therapy or training is safety. My primary objective is to create a safe, healing environment.
