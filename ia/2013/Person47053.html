Gary Boodhoo's Summary

For over two decades, I've been fortunate to work with some of the most talented people in the game industry meeting the unique and constantly-changing challenges of interaction design for this medium. From projects like Madden NFL and The Sims to product concepts for real-life tactical and biometric applications, I use storytelling, role playing, sketching and prototyping to share testable ideas at all levels of a company, in whatever medium is needed.
