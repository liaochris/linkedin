Jamey Tippens' Summary

I work as a licensed home inspector in Chapel Hill, Durham and the surrounding areas of North Carolina. Home inspections generally happen when houses are bought and sold. I spend several hours in the house with my clients, examining all of the systems and components, and answering questions. After the actual inspection I write a report that is delivered to the client within 24 hours. The report has many photographs and describes any problems with the home and suggests what type of contractor could solve the problems.

Specialties: Expert in all types and ages of North Carolina residential construction. Especially interested in historic homes and sustainability.
