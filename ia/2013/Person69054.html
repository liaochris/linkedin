Josh Raborn's Experience

Owner & Managing Partner

High Desert Medical Solutions

2013 – Present (less than a year) Albuquerque, NM

High Desert Medical Solutions is a specialty distributor and manufacturer's representative company based in Albuquerque, NM. Our primary goal is to provide manufacturers with a high quality and experienced sales force that has a strong history of clinical success and relationships with physicians, health care providers, hospital and clinical decision makers. Our team demonstrates an intimate knowledge of the growing needs and demands in healthcare by providing solutions designed to benefit patients and providers.

Our mission is to remain specialized in providing health care professionals and hospitals unparalleled customer service, and clinical and technical excellence through the offering of superior medical solutions for their patients. We strive to provide a value proposition for our customers and their patients to increase the quality of healthcare. We are a New Mexican owned and operated company that understands and serves the needs of our great state.

The primary focus of energy is spent in orthopedics, podiatry, pain management, spine and hospitals, clinics and outpatient surgery centers. Areas of surgery include total joints, pediatric trauma and deformity, trauma, sports medicine, spine and indications related to the musculoskeletal system. Other non-surgical specialties include podiatry, wound care, pain management, neurology, and oncology.
