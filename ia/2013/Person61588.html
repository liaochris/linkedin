Jeneane Sessum's Summary

As the words and voice behind dozens of Fortune 500/1000 companies over the last 20 years— including AMS, AT&T, Comcast, Deloitte, Delta Air Lines, Equifax, IBM, and The Home Depot — Jeneane has given personality to corporations by providing creative, targeted communications that deliver compelling messages.

During her five years in Ketchum's Technology practice, Jeneane contributed to the branding, launch, and positioning of more than 50 clients. As Senior E-Business writer, she was responsible for concepting and writing virtually every type of communication — websites, brochures, ads, white papers, articles, scripts, marketing plans, speeches, launch strategies, and more — for every imaginable audience and market.

From 1994-1998, Jeneane was Director of Corporate Communications for Systems Techniques, a healthcare data warehousing company later acquired by IBM. There she executed marketing and public relations programs that built the company's brand and led to its acquisition.

Prior to moving to Atlanta from Rochester, NY, Jeneane spent five years with Eastman Kodak Company managing the Technical Publications Group at Kodak's Edicon Systems Division.

Jeneane is a well-known "weblogger" on her personal blog, allied, social media blog Jeneane.net, and is a business blogging pioneer who started blogging in 2001. She founded the first women's team blog, Blog Sisters, that same year. She has been an Advisory Board Member and Contributing Editor to the high-profile women's community, BlogHer. Jeneane and her work have been featured in such publications as Business Week, The New York Times, Time Magazine and The Times Online.

Jeneane has helped several companies with their social media and blogging strategies and implementations— including AT&T, Cox Communications, Deloitte, IBM, and StyleFeeder. She serves on a number of advisory boards.

Specialties

Tech marketing and PR, social media, blogging, content generation and copy writing, technology and other complex business subjects, the Internet,and hamster care. Find THAT combination anyplace else. ;-)
