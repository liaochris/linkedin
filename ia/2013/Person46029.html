Fred Hackney, MD's Summary

Dr. Hackney has years of experience establishing a pattern of excellence you can trust and he is one of the few surgeons in the country double board certified in Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery and Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery. Dr. Hackney's dedication to a very demanding area of medicine has allowed him to provide his patients artistic, individualized and technically advanced cosmetic procedures and thereby offer his patients superior care and exceptional results.

Dr. Hackney has helped patients seeking cosmetic surgery in the Dallas Fort Worth area, including Frisco, McKinney, and Plano. Patients from over 70 different cites across Texas, Oklahoma and Louisiana, have had cosmetic procedures performed by our Dallas plastic surgery clinic.
