Jeff Kart's Summary

Old School journalist, New School environmental reporter and writer.

Interviewer, blogger, radio personality.

Big thinker, consultant, social media captain of the virtual networking ship.

Master's degree holder, husband, dad, camper, music lover, consumer of fine (and borderline) spirits. Spirited editor, fanatical with copy, but constructively critical.

Wrangler of words and two daughters. Working in Michigan, the Great Lakes state, and beyond.

I've been trained by the best:
- Metcalf Institute for Marine and Environmental Reporting
- Great Waters Institute, Institutes for Journalism and Natural Resources
- Scripps Howard Institute on the Environment
- Knight Center for Environmental Journalism
- Great Lakes Environmental Journalism Training Institute
