Blaine Graboyes' Summary

I work with entrepreneurs and executives to launch startups and create new products including Facebook games, virtual worlds, social and mobile apps, entertainment properties, and fan communities. I develop Business Plans, Financial Models, Game Design or Product Requirement Documents, and Pitch Decks for innovative projects.

I am an award-winning Executive Producer and Game Designer with over fifteen years’ experience developing digital and transmedia entertainment. I have produced over 4,000 projects for world-renowned entertainment, corporate and fine arts clients, including DreamWorks, Mattel, 4mm Games, Sony Pictures, Disney, WeeWorld, Nickelodeon, Rinksters, LeapFrog, DIRECTV, Warner Bros., The Guggenheim and Whitney Museums, Nam June Paik, and Yoko Ono.

I have the flexibility to work as a consultant or as a full-time member of an executive team. I can also provide the resources to fill key roles, direct an existing team, or fully staff the project for my clients. I have been a founder of multiple successful startups where I have contributed to raising over $20MM.

Over the years, my work has been recognized with numerous awards including: RIAA Platinum Sales (Melissa Etheridge’s Live...and Alone), IGN Editor’s Choice Award (The DC Video Deluxe Edition UMD) plus recognition for best new software product (ActiveDVD™), and best game play (Madagascar Animal Trivia DVD Game).

I am an active member of the Producers Guild Of America (PGA), the International Game Developers Association NYC Chapter (IGDA NY), the International Digital Media Alliance (IDMA), and the Casual Games Association (CGA).

Specialties

Create Successful & Disruptive Products, Manage & Inspire Teams, Games and Apps for Facebook, Social & Mobile, Transmedia (I think?), Making Cool Stuff
