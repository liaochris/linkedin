Diane Jordan-Grizzard, M.Ed.'s Summary

My desire to make a difference in the world took me to culturally-rich Liberia where I lived from 1979-1984, owned a business, served as a midwife, and witnessed the 1980 coup. The integration of these experiences with my formal studies inspires the framework I use to coach, design and facilitate learning opportunities that encourage individuals to unlock their purpose, passion, and power.

After more than 20 years of executive nonprofit experience where I led organizations, people, and change, I launched Thembi Speaks LLC in to be a catalyst in bringing about an alternate future in personal and organizational life. Our business approach is to inspire individuals and teams to lead from aspiration in order to increase accountability and decision-making, and deliver sustainable results. We facilitate change, strategic planning, leadership and development, and provide executive coaching.

My recent release, Are Your Riding in the Back Seat of Your Own Dream? was preceded by Free Soil (2010), a novel based on my experiences in Liberia.

I participated in the 2012 United Nations Commission on the Status of Women, 56th Session in NYC, where I interviewed women worldwide for a book that will publish in 2014.

  • Are You Riding in the Back Seat of Your Own Dream?

    • HeartStudios
    • August 23, 2013
    Authors: Diane Jordan-Grizzard, M.Ed.

    Are You Riding in the Back Seat of Your Own Dream? is a metaphor for feeling stuck and not moving forward with your dreams and aspirations. This book brings together personal interviews of women from diverse occupations and lifestyles, providing insightful lessons that will inspire you to pick up your dream, brush it off, push against fear, and get back into the driver’s seat. The book is for anyone who still believes in dreams coming true but may have been dissuaded into believing it is too late, and for those who still have passion and just need encouragement to go ahead and live their dreams.

    After you’ve read the book, subscribe to the blog on my website, or send me a note about how you returned to the driver’s seat of your dream.
