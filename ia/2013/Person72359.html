KARLA ILARDE's Summary

This Is Who I Am:

If you're like me, I love the process of taking a product to market. After 20 years working in sales and marketing in the consumer products industry, I decided to leave my corporate career to follow my lifelong dream and put my skills to the test as an entrepreneur.

Only a year into my consulting business, I helped a client launch a toy company that made almost $3MM in revenue in less than 2 years.

Now I'm dedicated to using my expertise to help inventors, indie designers, small batch producers and manufacturing business owners become well-positioned so they can launch & grow profitable brands.

This Is Who We Help:

Our highly experienced network works with manufacturing business owners who are looking for a key partner to help get products to a larger audience effectively.

If you have a new or existing product line that you'd like to take to the next level...and you want a partner who can guide you how to become well-positioned for retail success, I invite you to check us out at www.kimarketingintl.com or email me at karla@kimarketingintl.com so we can schedule a complimentary 20-min strategy session. You can also sign up on our website to get our FREE Resource Kit.

This Is How We Can Help Inventors & Manufacturers:

We offer consulting and management services, training programs and products that can help get your consumer brands to market successfully.

Using a proven process, we show you exactly what steps to take and what to AVOID when manufacturing and marketing your consumer products. We guide you how to become well-positioned so you can confidently pitch your products to investors, strategic alliance partners and key buyers and place your products at retail, ensuring your brands remain PROFITABLE.

We begin by doing a product assessment. Next, we develop an action plan that ensures you're ready for retail. And lastly we get your products manufactured, delivered and if necessary, distributed to the appropriate channels of distribution.
