Joe Zente's Summary

President/CEO -- Z3 Performance Development, Inc
Managing Director -- The Alternative Board, Austin, TX

Z3 Performance Development, Inc helps private owners grow their Sales, strengthen their businesses and enjoy their lives. Z3 specializes in the development of High-Performance Sales and Operational Teams and effective Strategic Business and Life Plans. 25+ years of executive management experience with international companies involved in a diverse variety of products and programs. Led six successful companies, founded four, and sold two. Developed the UnCommon Sense© Sales Process and Formula For Success, used currently by thousands of successful sales executives. Facilitated over 400 CEO Peer Advisory Board Meetings. Designed and implemented additional training programs in Sales Development, Sales Management, Sales Recruiting, Sales Assessment, Effective Communication, Managing for High Performance, Open-Book Management and Principle-Centered Leadership. Certified in Strategic Business Leadership®.
