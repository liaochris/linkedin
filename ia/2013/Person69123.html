Joshua Metzger's Summary

Since graduating from The Ohio State University in 2004, with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Landscape Architecture, I have had great opportunities to gain valuable work and career experience. I have been fortunate to work on multiple master planned communities, resorts, waterparks, and public parks, creating design development packages, construction documents, and performing site observation and construction administration.

I have currently taken, and completed, 3 sections of the Landscape Registration Exam, A, B, and D. And plan to take the final sections soon.

Specialties

Landscape Design, Landscape Renderings, Sections, Planting Plans, Plant Lists, Construction Drawings, Design Development Drawings, Schematic Drawings, Concepts, Details
