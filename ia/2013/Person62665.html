Jerome Ryckborst's Summary

I am an experienced usability analyst, information architect, and interaction designer who specializes in designing user interfaces for software and websites. That means I help companies make products and sites that naturally guide their audiences to find information and complete tasks.

My projects range from designing large and mission-critical business software to concise and mobile apps. I work with project managers and development teams to ensure project goals and user needs are met. I have experience gathering user requirements, interviewing users and stakeholders, developing personas, wireframing and specifying designs, and conducting usability tests.

I have a graduate degree in Business Administration as well as a science degree in human ecology—an interdisciplinary field that combines sociology, science, and business. I focused on communication technology.

Specialties: Organizing information on a website so your audience can find things easily. Migrating key features from your large software system or SaaS site into a task-focused mobile app. Usability testing both prototypes and released products. Influencing the development process to help team members understand and rely on the contributions of usability analysts, information architects, and interface designers.
