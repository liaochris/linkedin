Elisabeth Lugar's Summary

I am a Real Estate Professional on The Lugar Real Estate Team at Century 21 Scheetz. Our team serves Home Buyers & Sellers in the Greater Indianapolis Area - Hamilton, Hancock, Boone and Marion Counties. I work 100% on referral and help clients invest and own homes, Residential or New Construction, as well as, help clients who would like to purchase or lease commercial property. I work on the relocation team at Century 21 Scheetz and would be happy to help you with any local real estate questions.

After graduating from graduate school, I worked in marketing and originally worked in Distribution & Affiliate Relations at The WB Television Network. I make sure our clients have access to the most current technology to both sell your home and provide a focused search to help you find the home of your dreams. I am a problem solver who is a strategic thinker and planner and use this skill to help make your dream of home ownership as easy as possible.

I stay active in my community and have enjoyed speaking in local high schools on interpersonal and life skills topics. I love being a mom, serving on the PTO, and most recently as Assistant Coach for my son's 5th Grade Basketball Team through S.P.O.R.T.S. My son tells me I have cool moves.

It would be an honor to serve you or any of your referrals. Here's to the good life!
