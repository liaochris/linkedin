Debra Trappen's Summary

My personal passion statement:
Everyday I seek to ignite passion, infuse purpose, and inspire progress by connecting, engaging, elevating and empowering the amazing relationships and ideas in my world.

My d11 mission:
Help brands engage customers and build relationships across social media channels.

Whether you are a friend/colleague trying to find the right words to explain what I do to a referral… or a new connection curious about how we can work together, here it is – in a nutshell!

I CONSULT brands I am passionate about on how to connect with customers and build relationships on social media channels. The process starts with an in-depth social media audit, followed by strategy and consulting. I wrangle a team of social media firecrackers who manage social media for brands that don’t have a marketing department yet. This d11 team does NOT do social media for individuals. Period. Please don’t ask.

I SPEAK at conferences. My target events focus on entrepreneurs, the wine and real estate industries; and I always have a passion for elevating and empowering women in business. My messages are always customized to the audience!

I PRESENT a custom, 6-hour social media business workshop designed to kick entrepreneurs into HIGH GEAR. I call it Socially Sparked Sessions.
