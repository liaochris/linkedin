Mike Marco's Summary

I am a project management consultant. I deliver projects and train people how to do that. It is not easy, but I like what I do…since childhood I have been called organizer… My strong point is that I am result driven person…I get things done…I like success…my week point is my lack of “soft skills”… I try to improve my “soft” side, but it is not easy…For the last 15+ years I have managed projects on four different continents… trained close to 3000 project managers…at least 40% of them have had very limited knowledge of the math, statistics, and finance…some of them have never used spreadsheet software or PM software while managing their projects… I do not blame them for that for two reasons:
1. That is why I have a job…I teach them how to be better project managers, and
2. It is our work environment that allows people with limited skills to get to the PM positions… and stay at the same knowledge level for years… No wonder jobs are going to India and China…we lost our competitive edge…printing money is not enough to bring economy back. We need EXPERTS. I salute those project managers who try to get things done through continuous improvement and learning…without the challenges…we would be still riding donkeys…Specialties: PMO deployment, agile/project management consulting and training.
