Bec Pfeifer's Summary

I am an experienced leader who thrives on working with others to build organizations where people are able to do their best work.

My focus has always been on learning and change. Early on, this focus was through the lens of technology and business process redesign. Later in my career, my work expanded to include the organizational systems and processes that directly support people.

I am a creative, conceptual thinker and versatile change agent who builds trust, engages others, and aligns human capital systems, business processes, and technology to accelerate and improve individual and organizational performance.

Specialties: executive coaching, performance management, change management, succession planning, talent management, leadership development, business process improvement, strategic planning, team development.

Bec Pfeifer's Experience

Executive Coach | Leadership Consultant

Living Possibilities LLC

July 2009 – Present (4 years 3 months) Greater Minneapolis-St. Paul Area

Living Possibilities, LLC is a coaching and consulting practice founded on the belief that each one of us has enormous capacity to lead a rich and fulfilling life – one in which we feel confident and capable, and have a sense of joy and passion for what we do and the impact we can create, professionally and personally.

My clients engage me to help them achieve a variety of goals, including:
- Accelerate a successful transition to a new or expanded role at work;
- Develop skills that enhance performance and satisfaction in a current role;
- Reduce stress from the demands of a career, family and other commitments;
- Create and sustain change in new ways versus relying on old strategies that have become less effective;
- Rekindle energy, confidence and passion for work and life.

Through a variety of services including one-on-one coaching, group coaching, and workshops, my clients learn to develop greater awareness and competence in addressing key challenges and creating meaningful results. As a result, clients experience increased confidence and satisfaction in achieving their immediate goals and develop greater versatility in dealing with future challenges as well.
