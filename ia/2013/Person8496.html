Annie Presley's Summary

An accomplished fundraising and political consultant in the for-profit and non-profit arenas, Annie has enjoyed the privilege of spearheading many successful and fascinating campaigns. She offers clients advanced strategies designed and perfected by her unique business esperiences throughout the U.S., particularly in Missouri, Kansas, Washington D.C. and on Wall Street.

In 1983, AP started Legal Assistance Inc., Kansas City's first temporary paralegal service agency which still thrives today under a different name. After its sale, she joined Kit Bond as Finance Director for his first re-election to the U. S. Senate in 1992. After his win, she opened The McKellar Group and introduced the political model of fundraising to non-profits. An early client, Swope Parkway Health Center was a believer and invited Lady Margaret Thatcher to launch their inaugural "Great Leaders Award" raising more than $500,000 and drawing more than 1500 people. The success of this event positioned McKellar as the leading event fundraising firm for area non-profits and political figures.

As a result, in April 1999, Annie was summoned to Austin, Tx, where she joined the fledgling Bush for President campaign as National Deputy Director of Finance. Working through the January 2011 Inaugural festivities, she then realized a dream and took a high profile political position in NYC at UBS Paine Webber as VP and Director of Governmental Affairs and Charitable Giving. After the terrorist attacks of 9/11, Annie fashioned a way home, reviving McKellar as a consultancy with new political strategy skills and knowledge of complex financial insturments in her toolbox. In August 2011 she joined the Bryan Cave Public Policy Group, lauching its KC office to "straighten the crooked path" for the firm's clients. See www.BryanCave.com/anniepresley/

Specialties: The Art & Science of Fundraising and political consulting called "Strategery"

McKellar Consulting is a fundraising consultancy serving non-profit organizations and political figures. We offer our clients advanced strategies designed and perfected by Annie Presley and her unique business experiences while working throughout the United States particularly in Missouri, Kansas, Washington DC and on Wall Street. As a fundraising devotee and developer of The Art and Science of Fundraising, Annie is pleased to have helped McKellar clients raise more than $700 million for charities and political figures over the last 30 years.

The Art and Science of Fundraising is a highly customized system created to provide clients a plan to reach their fundraising goals. The Science behind our fundraising method was discovered by the knowledge attained through 30 years of success. When we apply the Science to our clients’ plans, their specific goals become clearer, and the process a solvable equation. The Art entails stylizing what clients naturally bring to the table. We help them recognize their innate skills, build courage and confidence, and assist in personalizing the ways in which they can achieve their goals. When we help them discover and apply the Art, clients will have developed their own fundraising style, at their own comfort level. We specialize in fundraising efforts including annual fund campaigns, capital campaigns, cause-related marketing, feasibility studies, in-house development, partnerships with for-profits, public finance, sponsorship, strategery, and sustainable revenue.

As a result of Annie’s extensive political contacts, McKellar Consulting also offers advice and counsel at the Federal, state, and municipal levels of government. We help each client build meaningful relationships with elected officials and teach clients how to leverage those relationships into successful appropriations pursuit, grassroots development, issue positioning, legislative change, lobbying outreach, strategic counsel, and tax credit application.
