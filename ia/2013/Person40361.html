Eleanor Yearwood's Summary

Your organisation and its people, needs constant adjustment and development to deal with the ever changing internal and external environment if it’s not going to be left behind.

I have over 12 years experience as an Executive Coach working with global organisations. My skills, knowledge and experience have enabled me to build strong coaching relationships with senior leaders as they steer their teams through massive change.

Over the last 12 years, I have had the pleasure of working with some very talented people in businesses across Europe and the US . Here are a handful of the businesses that I have been associated with. ABN Amro, Royal Bank of Scotland, American Express, Diageo, NHS Trusts, Diageo, Apple, Dell, Logica CMG, EMC, Ricardo, Therma Fisher, and many more.

I founded Key Talent Partners Ltd in 2001 having qualified as an ICF coach with International Teaching Seminars (ITS) the previous summer.

As a ‘Strengths focused’ coach, I have a wealth of experience, plus a large tool-kit of models and techniques available to share with my clients to enable them to grow and develop their skills.

    This is one of my favourite tools for individual and team development.

    Developed by Isabel Myers and Katharine C Briggs more than 50 years ago, it is a powerful indicator of personality type.
