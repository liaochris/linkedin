Jason Horsley's Summary

WeChangeLivesDaily.com is a team of certified nutrition coaches, fitness professionals, dietitians, and wellness coaches providing nutritional coaching and solutions in a 100% virtual manner as an affordable, convenient alternative to conventional, expensive nutritionists or personal training.

Founded with the sole purpose of reaching clients who might typically not have the means to work with a nutritionist due to costs, location, schedule, etc. Jason's vision was to provide a program that allowed clients to work with their personal coach from anywhere in the world, at any time. Seeing this work for professional athletes and movie stars, Jason believed there was no reason this could not work for everyone.

With the use of the World Wide Web, WeChangeLivesDaily.com has created a proven means of helping anyone, anywhere, control their weight, get in shape and live longer, healthier lives.

With 24/7 access to their personal coach through the use of email, the internet, even text messaging, the personal attention Elite Lifestyle Solutions clients receive, creates a level of comfort and trust not typically seen in today's society due to the use of technology.

Elite Lifestyle Solutions has found a way to bring personal attention back, through the use of the World Wide Web and help those looking for a total wellness solution.

With a long list of healthier, satisfied customers now in place, Jason and Elite Lifestyle Solutions Team are convinced that the use of virtual coaches and solutions will, in fact, be "The Future of Fitness!"
