Mariano Pinal's Summary

Insulated Packaging Systems is family owned and operated and has been in business for over 30 years. We have extended experience and serve many different areas of the country. We are proud to offer products that are environmentally friendly.

We specialize in custom-design foam packaging, packing peanuts, and floating fiberglass docks. We offer all types of different foam. Our many years of experience have allowed us to become quite effective in our delivery and the ability to give competitive pricing. Call us today for all your packaging needs!
