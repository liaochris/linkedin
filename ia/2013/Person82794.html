Lou Roepke's Summary

I am accredited by the Institute for Independent Business. With an extensive business background, including ownership of a sales and service business for over 20 years, Chief Financial Officer, Controller, Accountant and Department Manager, as well as a part-time college Economics instructor, I bring a wealth of experience to business.

A member of the Rotary Club of Knoxville, TN, previous member of the Rotary Club of Gilroy, CA and the Rotary Club of Willows, CA. A long-term Rotarian, I enjoy community service and other volunteer work.

My Business Philosophy is to partner with clients to find areas for improvement, reduce costs and optimize opportunities for improved profit.

My approach is don't just control costs, reduce them. My Financial Health Assessment program spotlights opportunities for greater profit for independent businesses.
