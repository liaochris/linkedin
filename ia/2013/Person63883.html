James R. Garrett, Jr. MCR's Summary

Jim Garrett is a seasoned commercial real estate professional with more than 25 years in the industry. Well versed in the dealings of commercial real estate brokerage, corporate real estate, operations and training, Jim is known throughout the U.S. and beyond as a problem solver and thought leader.

As the Chief Operating Officer for the Ohio operation of Colliers International, Jim is responsible for implementing strategic plans, spearheading operational success and efficiencies, and managing training and best practices throughout the state.

Prior to joining Colliers, Jim spent 14 years at NAI Global where he was key player in a significant member expansion and increasing network transaction revenue by 20 percent annually. This was accomplished by managing and aggregating membership data, developing best practices models and fostering continuing education. While at NAI, he became a staunch supporter of new media strategies including social networking and on-demand content delivery to distribute research, best practices and training.

Jim worked his way through the ranks at NAI Global, joining first as a Regional Director of Brokerage Services in 1997 to eventually becoming the Executive Vice President of Network Operations in 2009.

He also has significant experience in corporate real estate, spending six years at Nationwide Insurance as a Real Estate Manager. While there, he completd over 350 transactions.

Jim also spent time in the trenches of real estate, working as a commercial real estate agent at Donald R. Kenney & Company in Columbus, Ohio. Because of his time as a real estate advisor, Jim has a unique management style that sales teams and executive leadership find both refreshing and effective.

Jim was inducted into Midwest Commercial Real Estate Magazine’s Hall of Fame. In addition, Jim is currently the Chair of Colliers International's Manager's Steering Committee.

Specialties

editing, instruction, real estate, six sigma, total quality management, trainer, corporate solutions, integrator and connector.
