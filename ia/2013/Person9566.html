Ariniko O'Meara's Summary

In October of 2010 I started a project called City Saunter where I walk every street in Lansing, MI. As I walk I photograph interesting, exciting, possibly hidden “gems” throughout my hometown. I started this in hopes that I could create a showcase of all the wonderful things Lansing has within its borders. Lansing is lush with parks, well kept homes, unique small businesses, river access, architecture, landscaping and people. It’s a photographer’s gold mine, if you’re willing to take the time to see it. There is a lot of energy in our city and I plan to expend just as much, equal energy to share what I find with anyone who wants to see it.
