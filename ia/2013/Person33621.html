Debra Brown's Summary

Published writer and award-winning marketing professional with extensive experience in project & product branding, multiple project & national account management, social media, public relations, event planning, and more. Personal strengths include continual learning, excellent organizational skills, ability to work under pressure, and great attention to details and deadlines. Writing skills, creativity, and innovative approaches set my projects, events and performance apart from the competition.

Published in the creative nonfiction genre.

In the marketing arena, my current goal is to make social media easier and more effective for clients and their content more engaging.

In creative nonfiction, I want to make readers laugh or to be inspired.


Specialties: Marketing, Public Relations, Writing, Branding, Coaching, Project Management, and Creative Design.
