Alejandro Barros' Summary

Alejandro holds Master in Computer Science in University of Chile, Bachelor in Computer Science in University of Chile.

His consulting work focuses on IT Governamental Policies, Public eProcurement, eGov and IT Design.

He has advised governments and firms in the Chile, Argentina, Peru, Paraguay, Central America, USA and Spain, and has experience in the IT, telecommunications, retail, and mining sectors.

He has several technical publications

Author of book on digital development public policy polisDigital -www.polisDigital.infol
