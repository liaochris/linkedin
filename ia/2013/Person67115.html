Ben Johnston's Summary

I'm the founder and director of London Studio Limited. A creative design agency that works with businesses in the fast moving consumer goods industry who have great products to sell but have huge competition from other established brands. Businesses that have products that need to stand out and grab the attention of consumers. We design product packaging that improves their sales and do this with a team of professionals with meticulous attention to detail and the experience to craft a brand.
