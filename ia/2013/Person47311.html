Sunny Sethi's Summary

I have been working in the insurance industry for past 10 years. My company, Matrix Insurance Agency is a business-to-consumer health insurance agency that delivers Web-based, insurance information to individuals, families and small businesses who are in need. Our integrated suite of online services facilitates a comprehensive search and review of leading insurance companies.

Our core business has been setting up group benefits for Small and Large employers in Northern CA. With our expertise in the field along with prompt & personalized customer service my clients are guaranteed to get the best available coverage for the most affordable rates.

Specialties

Group Benefits for Small and Large size business. Individual Health Insurance.
Visitors coverage and Travel insurance for individuals and groups.
