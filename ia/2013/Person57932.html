Jamie Ashe's Summary

Jamie Ashe is the product of a solid education and computer network launching family. Her father was an integral part of building and launching the 1990's online network, "Prodigy", so the apple did not fall far from the cyber tree.

Jamie graduated from high school at the age of 15. Receiving numerous awards and scholarships including the prestigious 'National Merit Honor Scholar'. Quickly educated. Jamie earned her B. S. In Computer Science, and a B.A. In Journalism and then her MBA by the age of 21. She then went on to receive her Ph.D. just three years later at the age of 25.

It was in 2001 she decided to join the media giant AOL Time Warner. During her tenure at AOL, she was the number one sales and advertising executive. Recognized for outstanding sales achievements, she was award "Top Sales Person of the Year" and the prized 'Managers Over Achiever Award' for both years, and in her last year, Jamie was nominated for AOL Time Warner's "Samurai of the Year Award" for overall contributions to the company.

Determined to be an industry leader and pioneer she continued to contribute to the worlds most prestigious advertising companies such as ValueClick and Vendare Media.

In 2005 she joined forces with Medium21, an advertising and marketing agency in which she was recognized as one of the most powerful female executives in the advertising industry with her strength and abilities to ink lucrative celebrity endorsement deals as well as award winning marketing campaigns for her clients.

Today, she continues to be one of the “Industries Movers & Shakers” sited by Media Post, and most recently was inducted to the Presidential Who’s Who for her media expertise.
