Jason Salas' Summary

I'm an idea guy and a storyteller. I'm passionate about products - conceiving them, building them, leading discussions about them, and getting people excited about them.

As an experienced product manager, software developer, marketer, broadcaster, sportswriter, author and filmmaker, I fell in love with the Web the moment I first got online in 1994, seeing it as the world’s biggest toy, and began helping people embrace the Internet before shifting my focus to content development and ultimately to designing data-driven systems. More recently, I've been concentrating on Google Glass, Google Hangout apps/extensions, HTML5 games, audio-animatronic-style robotics, Chromecast apps, and evolving Google+ as a proper venue for sports conversations.

I'm currently authoring a book on Google Glass and producing a documentary film. I also intend to pursue a Ph.D in consumer psychology.
