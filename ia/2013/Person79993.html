Leland Dake's Summary

Mr. Dake is a Partner with ArchPoint Consulting and is the President of Strategic Sourcing Partners. Leland Dake specializes in the supermarket industry but also works with many different business industries.

SSP is a leading grocery industry consulting firm since October 2009. He has consulted with Fortune 100 CPG companies which include GMills, Nestle Purina and Unilever. Mr. Dake also has consulted for grocery retailers like A and P as well as Northgate Markets, a Hispanic format chain in Southern California.

Dake continues to stay close to the grocery industry by tracking the in- store merchandising and strategies of Wal-Mart, Target, Kroger, Safeway and Supervalu. Previously, Mr. Dake was the Group VP of Merchandising at Supervalu. He set strategic direction and was responsible for Merchandising, Category Management, In-store execution and Procurement.

Mr. Dake's leadership crossed over from Wholesale Supply Chain to Corporate Retail Banner stores. He led many departments including Category Management, Center Store (Grocery, Frozen, Dairy, GM and HBC) as well as Fresh (Meat & Seafood, Produce, Bakery and Deli). Mr. Dake was also responsible for Strategic Pricing, Strategic Sourcing, Store Supplies, Hispanic Merchandising and Private Label at Supervalu.

Dake also has experience with Group Purchasing Organizations (GPO) by combining several different companies buying power and leveraging this volume for better cost of goods (COG's), service contracts and Supply Chain logistics.

Dake's Sourcing and Procurement expertise is not limited to the Grocery industry. He has worked in many different industries improving companies procurement processes and buyers skill sets.
