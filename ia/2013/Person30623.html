Dario Diament's Summary

Technology and Media specialist. Kickstarted my career in 1999 as a Software Developer, working for start-ups of the first generation of internet companies, understanding the back-end of e-commerce, auction, publishing and CRM systems.

In 2007, I moved to the Digital Marketing & Advertising industry where I developed my understanding of the up-and-coming Digital Brand Marketing scenario, working on client challenges for international Fortune 500 companies.

I grew from SEO Specialist into Integrated Marketing Director covering the whole digital ecosystem, including Display Media buying and optimization, Search Engine Marketing, Social Media and Digital Analytics. Founded the Social Media and DSP team and businesses.

SEO: I've built personal sites that receives more than 5,000 relevant visits per day and created content and optimization strategies that were adopted by Fortune 500 companies.

Search Engine Marketing: Managed multi-million dollar campaigns and developed a strategy framework that has been adopted by clients and employees. In 2008, I won the first local Google Agency Competition, and was awarded with a trip to Mountain View's Google HQ.

Social Media: Guided communication and marketing executives into adopting Social Business strategies, when the conversation was around "Why aren't you proposing a Hi5 strategy?". Founded the Social Media business in Latin3, now +15 analyst strong. In 2008, I coded and built a Social Network event platform and in 2010, I launched an Politics and Influence monitoring platforms that is still used by communication departments.

Media and Analytics: Managed the planning and buying for multi-million budgets. Accelerated the adoption of new technologies and methodologies. Managed at first hand Tag Management, Ad Serving, Planning and Programmatic Platforms.

Programmatic Media Buying: Founded and managed the DSP/Real Time Bidding (RTB) and DMP business for the agency.
