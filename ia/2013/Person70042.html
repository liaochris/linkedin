tabeso

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry

March 2013 – Present (7 months) las vegas, nevada area

We have all-encompassing apps for keeping up with friends and colleagues, news and nonsense, blogs, photos, restaurants, and random comments from your favorite celebrities. There’s a one-stop shop for searching and commenting on everything imaginable, except the happenings that define our daily lives and become our most indelible memories: events.

Launched on March 1st, 2013 in the App Store, Tabeso is filling that void with free, intuitive tools for knowing and sharing what’s up in the places we travel and call home. Download the app, create a profile and start discovering, whether you enter a range of dates or miles from a location, or names of favorite artists and venues. Tabeso helps you follow it all with a mobile interface of event data and a social layer for interacting with that data in new ways.

Tabeso enables you to follow people, places, and organizations—even your family—because birthdays and book clubs matter almost as much as that dream pilgrimage to the Super Bowl. The activity stream filters to fit your needs, and additional social connect capabilities let you share what you’re into with your friends as well. Tabeso’s “Tab” feature gives options to attend, share, calendar, log, or comment on must-do events, and it lets event organizers know you’re interested, which could lead to exclusive offers and perks. You can even buy tickets on Tabeso. It’s a handheld antidote to boredom and a guarantee, as Herrera says, that “date night will never suck again.”
