Charlie Thomason's Summary

Charlie Thomason is a Front-end Web Developer, Web Designer, and Internet Marketing expert from Chicago. His specialties include CSS3, jQuery, WordPress, Drupal, cross-browser compatibility, and responsive design layouts. Charlie is currently employed as a full-time Web Developer at Razorfish.

Charlie is also a Studio Artist and Photographer and has had work appear in both group and solo exhibits in Chicago, IL and Lexington, KY. His first photography book, "Open Range: From Monument Valley to the Mojave Desert" was published in January 2012.
