Jo Rosen's Experience

Founder & President

Parkinson's Resource Organization

Nonprofit; 1-10 employees; Individual & Family Services industry

1990 – Present (23 years) Palm Desert, CA

I am the visionary of the first Emotional and educational support (psycho-social) organization in the Parkinson's world. Prior to our organization, the focus in the Parkinson's world was only on research. We work so no one is isolated because of Parkinson's and we continue doing this until a cure is found. We have support groups throughout Southern California, A monthly newsletter that goes throughout the world and an interactive website, in addition to doing one-on-one coaching to caregivers and People with Parkinson's.
