Michael Bertoni's Summary

I am an art director and designer specializing in graphic design, visual design, user experience, web design, video editing, marketing and illustration.

I have sustained a creative professional career for over two decades, generating web, brand and design solutions for the following industries: TeleCom, Gaming & Audio Product Accessories, Action Sports, Kid’s Sports Accessories, Sportswear Apparel, Music, Art, Education, Healthcare and Publishing.
