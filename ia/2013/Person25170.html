CJ Robinson, CFRE's Summary

I am a relationship builder and I solve problems.

As an accomplished entrepreneur, entertainment and revenue development professional, I excel in identifying, understanding and offering solutions to meet the unique needs of my clients. This client-centered approach yields the long-term, loyal and trusting relationships necessary to support the sustained revenue growth I realize for the organizations I represent.

Through years of successful experience, extensive education and training, I've honed my skills as a listener, negotiator and closer. I have acquired the tools essential to overcome objections and the resilience to never give up. Further, the expansive professional network that I've established and maintain throughout the region offers me excellent access and important introductions to key decision-makers.

My entrepreneurial spirit, creativity and strategic approach keeps me focused on the big picture; while my practical experience in the trenches as a small business owner, nonprofit and community leader have provided me the perspective necessary to recognize and address the essential details along the way.

As my professional journey continues, I seek adventure in leading a talented team down the path of sustained revenue growth through a client-centered commitment to selling solutions and adding value.

Specialties

Entrepreneurship, business/financial development, relationship development, marketing and community/public relations, public speaking, strategic planning, team-building, fundraising planning, direct solicitation, planned giving, government relations, strategic partnership development, sales management, stewardship, campaign implementation, entertainment, negotiation, special event planning, annual giving, direct mail/phone appeals, coaching, and talent acquisition/development/evaluation.
