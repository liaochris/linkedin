Brandi Kamenar Image Design Studio

Complete Image, Brand, Marketing & Media Relations Management

We are in the business of image, providing complete image, brand, marketing and media relations management for CEO’s, professionals, talent, celebrities and high profile individuals. Image Design Studio brings your ultimate vision to life, by developing a strategic roadmap to your success.

Public perception is everything. Our team of experts will provide, build and manage the right one for you and your business through a unique blend of:

Exposure:
media, red carpet, events, appearances – we get you in front of the right people, at the right place, at the right time. But we won’t do that without…

Communications Services:
we manage your publicity, public and media placement; create a marketing blueprint for your image as a business tool; we provide public speaking and media coaching; we are web and social media architects that will help you grab, and maintain, buzz. For that you have to have…

Style:
we’ll give you the right look from head-to-toe so you feel confident whether you’re walking down the grocery aisle or walking down the red carpet. But you can’t feel like a million bucks without your…

Health:
You can’t do any of this if you’re not taking care of yourself! From fitness and nutrition, to stress relief, our lifestyle and wellness experts will give you the energy boost you’ll need.

Your brand is more than just a logo or slogan. Image Design Studio will help you create an impression that is simply unforgettable.
