Mike Klein's Summary

Accomplished journalist, major projects producer, writer and instructor whose background includes positions with national, international and major local media companies including CNN, several print media companies, a television live sports programming entity, public broadcasting and an adjunct teaching position at the University of Georgia Grady College of Communications. Currently, Editor at the Georgia Public Policy Foundation since March 2010 to assist as the Foundation transitions from policy research and white papers into a more interactive media environment. Service includes board positions with The Commerce Club of Atlanta, the Atlanta Press Club and the Global Soap Project. Experience supervising small-to-large staffs and responsibility for multi-million dollar budgets.
