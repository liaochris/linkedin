Daniel Casciato's Summary

As a freelance copywriter and ghostwriter from Pittsburgh, I help small businesses and entrepreneurs write better copy for their websites, marketing collateral, blog posts, and other social media channels. My goal is to make sure your copy flows easily, is engaging, and is interesting to read.

Contact me at writer@danielcasciato.com if you'd like to learn more about how I can help you.

I'm also an experienced freelance writer and author. I've written over 3,000 articles for trade and consumer magazines. Clients and editors love working with me because I beat deadlines and turn in clean, concise, and easy-to-read copy.

Additionally, I have 10 years of professional experience in public relations, advertising, and marketing. I have done work for several for profit and non-profit organizations, including Carnegie Science Center, Small Business Development Center at Duquesne University, the Pittsburgh Three Rivers Arts Festival, the Pittsburgh chapter of the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation Race for the Cure, the Pittsburgh chapter of the American Heart Association and the Allegheny County Bar Association.

Through my volunteer work, I co-founded the Pittsburgh Sports League, which offers year-round, co-ed sports for college students and adults in the Pittsburgh region. I was also actively involved with the Pittsburgh Urban Magnet Project (PUMP), the Duquesne University Alumni Association and the Pittsburgh Technology Council.

Specialties: Newsletters, Brochures, Case Studies, White Papers, Websites, Social Media Writing, Press Releases, Bylined Articles and Features, Biographies, Corporate Profiles
