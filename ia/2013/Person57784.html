James Richard's Summary

I am a graduate of the Rochester Institute of Technology with a degree in Applied Networking and Systems Administration. I am currently working at Google Cambridge as a Corporate Operations Engineer. I also spent two years as an inaugural member of the Internal Technology Residency Program at Google in Mountain View, CA. I regularly provide exemplary IT support to Googlers via our walk up help desks called "Techstops", Tickets, and Chat. My position at Google also gives me the opportunity to work on many special projects, including live video streaming and networking.

I am a motivated self starter who lives, breathes, and sleeps technology. Throughout my education I have worked as a freelance technician for small business and residential customers, doing computer, network, and A/V support and installation.

My experience at Gemini Telemanagement Systems and Single Digits has provided me with extensive experience installing and maintaing HSIA (High Speed Internet Access) platforms at hotels across the country. These HSIA platforms included DSL and WiFi based connectivity in high utilization public environments. This position gave me an appreciation for properly documenting these systems during installation. Without extensive and accurate documentation the task of remote support becomes trivial at best.

I have built my customer service experience throughout college by working for the RIT Finance and Administration department, supporting IT systems for the business functions of the institute. This involved supporting a large number of users, both via phone using remote access technologies and in-person. This position also enforced the importance properly documenting issues via a ticketing system and following up with users to ensure their issues are resolved.
