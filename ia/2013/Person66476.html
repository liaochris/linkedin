John Horrigan's Summary

Experienced Program Manager with diversified exposure in manufacturing, new product development, planning, business operations, supply chain management and quality assurance with demonstrated success in on-time, on-budget delivery, team leadership and mentoring, forecasting, resource allocation, market analysis, risk mitigation, process improvement and regulatory compliance. John's planning expertise, photographic memory and unique marketing insight provide him with an over-the-horizon view of the medical device industry landscape.

Specialties: A talented project manager and motivator with polished presentation skills and a sharp mathematical mind, John keeps his teams focused by driving schedules through the timely closure of actions and deliverables while identifying and eliminating program risks. He achieves this through shared knowledge, process refinement, meticulous program reviews and real-time communication. John's mission is to service his customer before someone else does. His mantra is "consistency breeds excellence". Amongst John's other talents, he is a professional sports announcer with the Boston Bruins Alumni and the New England Patriots Alumni, a professional historian (author/lecturer) and the host of a two-time Boston/New England Emmy® Award-winning (and five-time Emmy nominee) historical television show entitled "The Folklorist".
