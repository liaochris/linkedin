Alison Gilbert's Summary

Strategic digital media professional. Expertise in marketing, organizational infrastructure, project management, operational workflow. Passionate about team building.

Was the fourth person to join Tasting Table, a daily email publication about food and drink backed by Pilot Group--the firm behind DailyCandy and Thrillist.
