Annette Raveneau's Summary

As a fully bilingual and multicultural Latina living in the U.S., I serve as Director of Communications for the Know Your Care campaign in Nevada for Ramirez Group. I handle a broad variety of tasks for this client. I collaborate with other advocacy groups and government agencies (local, state and federal) to inform Nevadans about the Affordable Care Act (ObamaCare), including Nevada Health Link, Nevada's health insurance exchange.

Prior to working at Ramirez Group, I served the community as a television journalist for the Univision Nevada station and prior to that as a reporter and producer for national Univision Network shows in Miami Florida and Los Angeles California.

I have freelanced for ESPN International in English as well as Spanish and I have co-hosted the Latin GRAMMYs for LatinGRAMMY.com with Kike Posada.

I have a global awareness having done a fellowship with Radio in the American Sector (RIAS) Berlin Commission in Germany, worked on cultural stories during the FIFA world cup in South Korea, and fulfilled other reporting assignments in Mexico, Panama, Argentina, the Caribbean and Spain. I have been immersed in the diverse Caribbean and Latino cultures most of my life. Being originally from Panama with Afro-Caribbean roots; has given me a unique cultural perspective.

I have a B.A. in marketing from Florida International University and an. A.A. in Business from Miami Dade College both in Miami Florida.

I have to honor to serve on the Board of Directors of FirstMed Health and Wellness Center, a non-profit clinic that serves the vulnerable population in Southern Nevada.

I am also a founding member of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists (NAHJ) - Nevada Chapter.

I am a true advocate for healthy communities and healthy living. I enjoy running, training Capoeira (Brazilian martial arts) & photography.
