Michael G Connor's Summary

My name is Mike Connor. I am based out of Porltand, OR - a recent transplant after 17 years in Brooklyn, NY. I am a creative front-end developer - with a specialty in Flash Action-script coding for developing complex yet flexible applications. My current tools set is HMTL 5, Javascript, CSS for building mobile-first designs. I dabble in other technologies such as Python, Ruby/Rails, Node.js, unix, and motorcyle maintenance.

I have a strong interest in the processes that any project goes through and look for ways to get things done as efficiently as possible.

Before hopping into digital production in the mid 90s, I worked at NPR as an editor - cutting and mixing quarter-inch audio tape for deadline.

Aside form coding - I’ve worked on a number of teams on large scale art projects. Through these experiences I’ve honed problem solving and communication skills - overcame technical, environmental, and team cohesion/communication challenges - and was instrumental in dragging projects past the finish line.

I have been an avid Cross-fitter for three years and became a professional rafting guide in 2013. I’ve learned to widen his gaze, push myself to acceptable and recoverable levels of exhaustion, and engage new people and situations.

I am located in Portland, OR but I’m open to re-locating for the right projects.
