  • Word 2010 Tips, Tricks & Techniques for Long Documents

    • The Software Pro-Denver, Colorado
    • January 2013
    Authors: Dawn Buzbee, MCT

    Longer documents present bigger challenges to business applications. The more pages there are, the more details you have to keep track of, and the greater the chance specific pages, or even chapters, require separate formatting. Add in frequent edits, complex layout needs, and long document standards such as tables of contents and cross-references, and your need to amp up your Word 2010 talents and productivity is even stronger.

    In this learning guide, dive into intermediate and advanced Word 2010 topics with ease. You will explore:
    * Document navigation tips and tactics
    * Understanding formatting choices in Word
    * Inserting and editing document headers and footers
    * Mastering sections in complex documents
    * Tips for automating large documents
    * Formatting with styles and themes
    * Adding and formatting a table of contents
    * Inserting an index
    * ...and much more!

    Whether you want to finally master section breaks and styles, would like to stop building a table of contents manually, or just wish to add great tips and tricks to your Word 2010 skills, this guide explains topics in easy to follow steps you can use immediately. And you don’t have to start from the beginning as each chapter is designed so you can move directly to the Word skills that are most important to you!
