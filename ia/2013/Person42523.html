Erika Kerekes' Summary

The Kerekes Group provides social media marketing, content marketing, social media advertising and other marketing services for small businesses, medium-sized businesses and enterprise companies.

We use today's marketing channels to build meaningful relationships with customers, prospects, influencers, and media.

Some of the things we do:

* Write marketing plans
* Develop social media strategies, including sample social content, promotion themes, and content calendars
* Run online influencer and blogger outreach programs
* Create and manage social media presence Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+, Pinterest, Instagram, YouTube and more
* Write blogs, articles and press releases
* Manage social advertising
* Train internal marketing teams

About Erika Kerekes:

I've been a pioneer in social web product development since 1992, before the Internet had pictures and before the term "social media" was coined.

I've conceived, built and managed social media and online community products and programs for a wide range of companies, both B2B and consumer-facing. The technology people use to find and talk to each other online has changed over the years, but the principles have not.

My award-winning recipe blog In Erika's Kitchen (http://www.inerikaskitchen.com/) serves as my social media R&D lab. I have built a large, passionate community around my personal brand on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Google+, and in real life as co-founder of Food Bloggers Los Angeles, a networking group that meets monthly for education, community service, delicious potlucks and the occasional raucous party.

Specialties: social media, social media marketing, social media campaigns, blogger campaigns, community outreach, content marketing, product development, product management, online community, user-generated content, food writing, blogging, recipes
