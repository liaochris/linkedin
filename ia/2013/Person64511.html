Jakub Linowski's Summary

I'm a user interface designer who very much enjoys sketching, evaluating, detailing, prototyping, and discussing web based interfaces and interactions. I also share my ideas over at the @wireframes blog as well as the www.goodui.org project.

Specialties: UI sketching and solving fuzzy/wicked/undefined problems is my bread and butter. I can move through a design space quickly all the way from exploratory concepting, to detailed visual design to prototyping. Finally, I also have an eye for conversion optimization - after all, we can only improve what we measure. :)

Jakub Linowski's Projects

  • GoodUI.org

    • April 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jakub Linowski

    Good User Interface is a running list of tips for making your UI easy to use and increasing conversion rates. I believe that a good UI is nice to both the business side as well as the people using it.

  • Interactive Sketching Notation

    • April 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Jakub Linowski

    The Interactive Sketching Notation is an emerging visual language which intends to enable designers to tell more powerful stories of interaction. It is a sketching style which merges wireframes, flow diagrams, and user stories into one deliverable and allows us to explore ideas quickly. The project has solidified itself as an Adobe Illustrator template that is constantly updated and available for purchase.

  • MicroPersonas

    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Jakub Linowski

    MicroPersonas is an icon set for building and using quick persona like characters in your interaction design deliverables. The set is founded on the belief that personas should be generated rapidly with only those characteristics that can be used to influence or inspire design action.

  • Wireframes Magazine

    • April 2009 to Present
    Team Members: Jakub Linowski

    Wireframes Magazine started as a blog to collect and share peoples' wireframe samples. The intention was to learn about various styles and techniques of representing user interfaces and digital products. Ever since then, the resource has grown from a collection of samples to also encompass interesting tools, templates and inspirations from the field of interaction design.
