Elizabeth "Bitsy" Jennings' Summary

Despite my chosen profession, and the fact that rejection is simply a part of it, I am an extremely positive person.

Originally from Boston, I moved to Pennsylvania after graduating with my Bachelor's Degree in English. I soon joined software company Bentley Systems and then ATX, a telecom company where I worked in marketing communications for ten years.

However, after realizing my proximity to QVC in West Chester, PA, I realized that I really wanted to pursue hosting and acting opportunities in the area. I grew up doing theater and continued through college, and I was looking for the thrill and creativity that acting has to offer. And thus my career change happened.

I have now been representing vendors on QVC since 2004 and have appeared on QVC 195 times. In addition, I hosted a PBS travel and tourism series for four seasons which took me all over the state and earned me an Emmy nomination!

Currently, I host an online video series called The Frugalicious Show & Bitsy's Quick Tips which has over 600K views on YouTube. Plus, I volunteer each week as a game show host at The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia.

Since I am most fulfilled being myself on TV, I am actively pursuing more Hosting opportunities; but I'm always looking to work/volunteer with non-profit organizations and would be happy to discuss how my marketing, communications and graphic design skills might help the right agency.
