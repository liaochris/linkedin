Matt Zampella's Summary

Mr. Zampella brings a wealth of skill & professionalism with extensive experience in real estate, acquisition, development & management; he has worked closely with owners, city planners, architects, contractors & designers.

Mr. Zampella has extensive experience in planning, marketing, contract negotiation & sales. Mr. Zampella’s strong analytical capabilities & straightforward approach to business have earned him the trust and confidence of his clients.

Mr. Zampella strives to help his clients make informed decisions with as much objectivity as possible. He believes that his job is to “Be the Guide.”

Mr. Zampella has represented Sellers & Buyers in Saratoga, Los Gatos, Monte Sereno, Los Altos/Los Altos Hills, Menlo Park, Palo Alto & San Jose. Clients choose to work with Mr. Zampella because he consistently demonstrates the core competencies that they expect:

• Honesty, Integrity, Knowledge & Experience
• Marketing: Strategy, Resources, Results
• Sales: Negotiating & Closing
• Service: Preparation, Feedback, Follow-up

On a personal note, Mr. Zampella graduated from Sacred Heart School in Saratoga; then Saint Francis High School in Mountain View. He then ventured out & attended Seattle University graduating with a Degree in Communications with a second emphasis in Sociology & Psychology.

While in Seattle, Mr. Zampella started his own Real Estate & Property Management Firm, which he still owns today and has expanded to the Bay Area. Mr. Zampella’s passion for life is expressed through real estate, particularly through the homes he builds, in both Silicon Valley & in Seattle which are unique in design with the utmost attention to quality. He was married in 2005 at which point he and his wife decided to move back to CA.

Mr. Zampella is very proud of the many referrals he receives from clients. He is hopeful to continue to grow & build a significant client base in the coming years as one of The Valley’s youngest, most aggressive & ambitious Real Estate Agents!

Specialties
