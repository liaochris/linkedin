Charles Burch's Summary

Seasoned senior sales manager with a proven 15+ year track record of success. Demonstrated ability to deliver immediate and sustained revenue results in a sales organization. Accomplished gains in highly competitive markets with leadership experience in team building with strong analytical skills, exceptional problem solving and sound strategic & tactical planning.

Currently building a field sales team in the Greater Seattle area for AmazonLocal, the newest division of Amazon.com. Working with all types of SMB's to create an offline experience for our loyal online customers.

Recent experience includes developing the orthopedic and spine markets for minimally invasive surgical products, biologics (stem cell technology), external bone growth stimulation and a full line of bracing. The partnership between Orthofix and Breg is a symbiotic one and the two companies are highly respected and leaders in their field.

Former General Manager for Verizon Information Services and Idearc Media in Hawaii and Mainland U.S. Markets.

Further experience with ReferAloha.com, a Hawaii-based website dedicated to helping people find qualified and reliable companies to work with. We pre-screened every business in ten different areas prior to placing them on the website. This provided consumers with the security of knowing the company had received "The Stamp of Aloha" from us and feel comfortable working with them.
