Judy Brizendine's Summary

Independent publisher committed to harnessing the power of books to effect change. The mission of BennettKnepp Publishing is to produce and promote books that engage, inform, delight and transform people--to make a real difference in their lives.

Nonfiction author whose award-winning debut books, STUNNED by Grief: Remapping your life when loss changes everything, and the STUNNED by Grief Journal, represent a unique approach within this genre--and a new way to consider and deal with grief recovery.

Mission: to educate, encourage, inspire, and motivate anyone facing grief and loss to step into the process that empowers them to move from pain to living expectantly again

Judy Brizendine's Publications

  • Stunned By Grief: Remapping Your Life When Loss Changes Everything

    • BennettKnepp Publishing
    • October 15, 2011
    Authors: Judy Brizendine

    STUNNED by Grief is a straightforward, personal look at grief that highlights issues critical to healing. Grievers will relate to the authentic reality of grief, find tips and tools for healing, and gain hope that they can work through their grief and live a satisfying life again.

  • Stunned By Grief Journal

    • BennettKnepp Publishing
    • October 15, 2011
    Authors: Judy Brizendine

    STUNNED by Grief Journal is a recovery tool to help anyone cope with grief’s effects by providing a simple way for grievers to explore their thoughts and feelings by writing about them. The Journal guides folks through the grieving process naturally while helping them understand themselves and take action.

  • Stunned By Grief Blog

    Authors: Judy Brizendine

    Stunned By Grief Blog is an ongoing collection of articles written by Judy Brizendine that focuses on a wide range of topics related to questions, concerns, fears, misunderstandings, and down-to-earth information to help anyone cope with grief.
