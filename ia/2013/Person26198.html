Ben Cohen's Summary

As a professional insurance broker and advisor I help people become more efficient with their money. If you know me, then you know what I'm talking about. If you don't know me, then I can teach you.

My company utilizes dozens of insurance and financial product markets for the procurement of many types of insurance products. My expertise is in personal insurance, risk free wealth accumulation, and tax-free wealth distribution strategies.

My goal is to not only protect my clients from risk, but also to help them achieve their financial goals efficiently by legally avoiding taxation. I have been in the insurance/advising business since graduating college; I love what I do, and couldn't imagine doing anything else.
