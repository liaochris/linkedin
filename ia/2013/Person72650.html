Kate Hopper's Summary

I'm the author of Ready for Air: A Journey Through Premature Motherhood (2013, University of Minnesota Press) and Use Your Words: A Writing Guide for Mothers (2012, Viva Editions/Cleis Press). I'm a freelance writer and editor, and I teach creative writing online and at The Loft Literary Center in Minneapolis. My writing has appeared in a number of journals and magazines, including Brevity, Connect!, Literary Mama, The New York Times online, and Poets & Writers. I write about reading, writing, teaching and motherhood on my blog, Motherhood & Words. I'm Literary Reflections co-editor at Literary Mama.

Specialties: writing, editing, teaching, creative writing, mentoring
