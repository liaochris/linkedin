Margie Dana's Summary

- Launched Margiedana.com, my new content strategy business, in 2013. My mission is to help clients create & shape the most effective content across many channels to help them engage and attract their ideal prospects.
- Founder of Print Buyers International, premier professional organization that catered to professional print & media buyers, including designers & marketers.
- Founder of Boston Print Buyers, a member-based professional organization that offered education and networking to those who work with printing and the related arts.
- Popular public speaker at conferences, meetings and other events for audience of printers and print customers.
- Blogger/author of Margie's Print Tips, weekly enewsletter read by 15,000+ the world over (since 1999)
- Created the customer-only Print Buyers International LinkedIn Group
- Blogger and columnist for Printing Impressions at www.piworld.com and their monthly magazine

My specialties are broad:

*Expert strategist in the printer/print buyer relationship.
*Leading authority on print buyer issues, trends, challenges, and practices.
*Avid social media user who creates content to build clients' brands
*Email marketing expert, having published Print Tips enewsletter weekly since 1999
*Public speaker, including preparing presentations, delivery tips and coaching, repurposing content
*Developer of clients' newsletter strategy, from soup to nuts
*Web site content strategist, from providing new content to auditing and improving existing content so that it attracts ideal market

Margie Dana's Publications

  • Print Buying Made Simple

    • LuLu.com
    • November 1, 2010
    Authors: Margie Dana

    Practical tips & everyday advice for professionals who source printing, including print buyers and designers. Perfect guidebook for those who've recently started their careers. This edited collection of nearly 40 Print Tips include never-before-published articles by the author of "Margie's Print Tips."

  • Print Selling Made Simple

    • January 2011
    Authors: Margie Dana

    Special collection of Margie’s Print Tips - advice and insights to help print sales and service reps as well as print company owners who want insider information about working more effectively with customers. Unique publication that has its eye on helping printers thrive.

  • View from 30,000 Feet: Key Trends & Sourcing Practices of Print Buyers

    • (c) 2013 Margie Dana
    • February 6, 2013
    Authors: Margie Dana

    This research report by Margie Dana is the result of a survey of 162 print-buying professionals conducted in December 2012. Respondents answered 20 questions, covering the basics of how much their annual print spend is, how much experience they have and where they work within their organizations, what their preferred value-added services are, and much more. This market research combines meaningful data as well as pertinent comments by the author.
