Dale Bowman, CML, CJIL, CPP's Summary

I’m a key system custodian – basically a janitor for key systems. Most businesses have key systems that are a mess. Rarely does the key system meet the security goals, or the operational goals of the company. Lost keys, unaccounted for keys, duplicate keys, and mismanaged key records can lead to slower response rates, increases in operational expenses, and a loss of productivity. The key to profit is productivity. How much profit does your key system cost you?

What I do is clean up the mess. I help companies with their key system design in order for them to improve their security, re-establish accountability, increase their productivity, and help them deliver an exceptionally safe and productive work experience to each and every employee and customer.

I spend my time evangelizing the importance of high security, key control, and digital key systems through the DC metropolitan area. Your key system does not have to be a nightmare.

Specialties

Key system design, Key system management, High Security Lock design, Key Control Systems, Digital Key System Design, Training, Lock Nerd... I mean savant.
