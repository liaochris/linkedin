Chris Sier, PCC, BCC's Summary

Email: Chris@ExecutivePotentialPlus.com or call me at: (586) 786-1044

“I am a better team builder through my new ability to connect with people in a different way that allows me to bring together the right mix of people from sales support, delivery, and senior account people to create a high performance and highly successful team contributing to the win of multiple sales pursuits totaling $200M+.” Aage Edvardsen, Managing Director, Scandinavia

Life is so much easier when one can live and work at full potential.

My mission is to help clients tap into their unused potential and expand their thinking and energy by using a brain-based coaching methodology.

As an Executive Coach, I assist my clients to discover and act on their potential to achieve desired results. This process is based on aligning values, strengths, and vision. In partnership, we collaborate on a plan, remove any obstacles, establish measurements of success, and implement strategies and actions.

As clients expand and apply their new thinking to everyday situations and experiences, life becomes more effortless and more enjoyable.

Some areas we will explore:

► How do others perceive your effectiveness in the specific areas you want to master?
► What are your distinctive strengths?
► Are you using your strengths powerfully or are you under utilizing them?
► What are you over-doing that may be inhibiting your success?
► How will you know you have reached your goals? What will be different?
► Is there a particular benchmark or target to aim for to make your goal more tangible?
► What step do you think you would take to achieve your desired level?
► What have you learned about yourself and how can you apply it moving forward?

I bring a diverse perspective to coaching based on my experience as a Manager, Process Improvement Consultant, Trainer, and Coach. In addition, my own life experience of “walking through fear” to get what I want inspires my clients and informs my coaching.
