Jim Tunney, Ed.D's Summary

Drawing from 30 years experience as an educator and 31 years as an NFL referee, Jim Tunney is the celebrity speaker for team building, leadership, peak performance, motivation, customer service and wellness. He is currently employed by the NFL as an Observer attending a game each week.

Recognized as a speaker who educates, motivates and entertains, Tunney works with Fortune 500 companies and international organizations teaching Leadership to improve productivity. His credentials rank him in the top 1% of professional speakers working today.

Tunney’s book, Impartial Judgment, “The Dean of NFL Referees” Calls Pro Football As He Sees It chronicles his career in the NFL. Other recent titles include Speaking Secrets of the Masters, The Winning Spirit, You Can Do It, Chicken Soup for the Sports Fan's Soul, and It's the Will, Not the Skill, outlining principles and philosophies of success.
Currently in process is a book to be titled: The Best of The TUNNEYSIDE of SPORTS, a collection of the top stories he has published in a local newspaper - The Monterey County Herald - and sent to subscribers each Monday, TUNNEYSIDE takes issues from the world of sports and transforms them into positive messages for everyday living. Look for his book y the end of 2013!

Specialties: Keynote Speaker, Author, Former NFL Referee and currently NFL Observer
