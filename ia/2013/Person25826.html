Carl Loeb's Summary

Thank you for checking out my profile on LinkedIn. If you and I haven’t been officially introduced, let’s do something about that. Soon.

A little about me: I’m a creative director, copywriter, brand strategist, idea-thinker and problem-solver. “Ad guy” for short, even if that’s only part of the story.

Our company, Grand, crafts ideas that shape how people feel about products, services, brands and social causes. We create brand identities, strategies, ad campaigns, events, web sites, digital tools, original content, and things that don’t fit into neat categories, but do attract attention.

Often, our work makes a brand feel smarter, more special, more of whatever is good and true at the brand’s core.

I hope our next grand idea is for you.
