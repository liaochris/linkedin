Matthew Hunt's Summary

Today, I work in the Orlando, FL area, with clients based in Central Florida, Philadelphia, Tallahassee, Atlanta, and New York City.

Over the past 6 years, I have built successful brands for clients, contracted with a number of agencies and worked on hundreds of website projects.

I have a great deal of experience working with many different kinds of agencies over the years through freelance opportunities and full-time employment. I work with advertising agencies, brand agencies, marketing agencies and interactive agencies all over the country. I understand their needs and I am familiar with common processes throughout any given project.

From 2008 – 2011 I was a Project Manager, Lead Front-end Developer, Drupal Theme Developer and Application User Interface Designer DLC Solutions in Philadelphia, PA. I created branding and user interface design for the EthosCE Learning Management System, which runs on two open source systems: Drupal and Moodle. As lead front-end developer, Drupal themer and UI designer I worked on websites for large organizations such as UPenn, United Healthcare, Cochlear and the American Society of Anesthesiologists. Today, I continue to support the growth of the EthosCE system by working closely with DLC designing promotional and marketing materials.

I have a strong focus and desire to make designs usable and accessible. The sites I create are optimized for performance and search engine placement. Clients find me easy to work with, strategic, obsessively organized, responsive and sharply focused on their project and budget requirements.

I hold a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Graphic Design. I exercise continuing education and persistent practice to achieve the level of mastery that enables me to contribute to the success of others' businesses and organizations. I am constantly following and implementing the latest techniques, tools, trends, best practices and efficiencies in this rapidly-changing industry.
