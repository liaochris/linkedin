Jaime Ordonez Victoria's Summary

Jaime Ordonez Victoria is a Freelance Art Director NYC Marketing Hybrid ³. That is, a combination of Hybrid Marketing & Hybrid Creative, combined. While the latest buzz-word in Marketing has become 'Hybrid Marketing,' Jaime takes this to another level as a new breed of emerging marketeers. Hybrid Marketing ³ has spawned from the convolution of media & marketing over the last decade. "Now everyone *must* wear all the hats!" Today, it is as essential that the Hybrid Creative Director be hands-on expert in Marketing, as much as the Hybrid Marketing Director needs to be hands-on expert in Creative—the new Paradigm.

As a Hybrid Inbound Marketing & Art Director, Jaime has spent the last 8 years directing & implementing online campaigns through PPC, Email Marketing, Copywriting & content management, SMM, SEO, SEO-Copywriting, White Papers, Newsletters, UX, eCommerce, WordPress, Blogs,—all tracked & tested through Google Analytics. His White Paper, "Be Kind to Robots, Write to Them Sincerely," can be found on his website at http://www.CosmicWisdom.com.

As a Hybrid Creative, this NYC Freelance Art Director / Producer brings a diverse skill-set to each project, from print, to web, video, animation, UX/UI and conceptual think-tanking. Additionally, Jaime is not shy to go 'hands-on' with his, or others', production-work in order to meet deadlines and assure quality. In fact, all the pieces in his portfolio have been exclusively created by Jaime from concept through final execution, for what he calls, "An accurate picture." He comes with many years of experience in Advertising agencies, Home Entertainment, and High-tech marketing. From effective Copywriting & Art Direction, Animation, CSS / HTML coding & User Experience Design, to a diverse print background Jaime has successfully lead many projects & teams over the last 20+ years.
