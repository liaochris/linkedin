JOHN AVOLA, MBA's Summary

Highlights: Experience in developing social media and mobile strategies focused on awareness, engagement and monetization. Developed a simple 5-step approach used to connect brands with the right target audience. Recently recognized for leading St. Jude to having the "Most Loyal Fans on Facebook" of more than 15,000 pages per LoudDoor (Feb. 2013).

Currently, I work for St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital as a Senior Liaison in the interactive marketing department specializing in social media and mobile. My goal is to use the power of social media to help support fundraising initiatives so that no family ever pays St. Jude for anything.

Prior to moving to Memphis, I worked as a Marketing Manager for Produce for Kids, a marketing organization focused on healthy eating while raising funds for local children’s non-profit organizations. At Produce for Kids, I focused on developing multi-channel strategies that contributed to raising nearly $3 million in donations in just two years.

Some of personal achievements include graduating summa cum laude with an MBA and receiving multiple National American Advertising Federation awards. In my spare time, I enjoy running and spending time with my wife, Kim, and two dogs, Molly and Zoe.
