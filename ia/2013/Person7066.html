Andrew McAllister's Summary

Andrew has been dissecting and building computers since he was 11 years old. With a comprehensive understanding of network and system architecture, he designs network infrastructure while meeting the productivity needs of user and client base alike.

Currently Andrew is on the network architecture team at LiveVox building high available network infrastructure for datacenters. He maintains a broad scope of IT technologies from business critical applications such as Exchange and Virtualization to Storage and Network Core technologies. Working closely with network and systems teams with an innate understanding that good networks are often best complimented with great design.

Born in Teagu, South Korea, and raise in SF bay area, Scotland and Idaho, Andrew began his ventures in high school running a small home business building custom computers and residential networks. He further explored his cultural and professional interests by completing an A.A.S degree in Computer Information Technologies and catching the next 1-way airplane ticket to the SF Bay area.

Since then, Andrew has worked for several companies in and around San Francisco and looks forward to what new things and people he may work with in the future. Technology is a form of art that Andrew is very enthusiastic about. Enabling people to do more; using technology as a means for improving collaboration and innovation in a creative way.
