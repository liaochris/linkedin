Sandra Adomatis, SRA, LEED GA's Summary

I have experience in appraising high-performance (green) residential properties and recently earned my LEED GA. I developed a four-hour seminar and an eight-hour course for the Appraisal Institute involving green properties.

Appraisal Institute activities include Vice Chair of National Education Committee from 2010 tthrough 2012, instruct residential courses and seminars, develop courses, 2013-2014 appointed to serve on the ADQC Committee, and serve as reviewer for various seminars and courses.

My professional experience includes testifying in court on eminent domain, divorce property settlement cases, and appraisal fraud-related cases.

Specialties: Appraising Residential High-Performance or Green houses; Quality Control; Fraud related research; Appraiser Educator; Complex Residential Appraisal Problems; and Small Commercial Properties.
