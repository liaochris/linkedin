Jorge A. Quintana's Summary

Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC is a full-service frame, door and hardware supplier that has been providing quality metal doors and security door hardware for over 20 years. Serving the South Florida area since 1985, we not only know the products we provide to our customers, but we know exactly how to supply it. At Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC we pride ourselves on doing business with knowledge, professionalism and integrity.

When you call Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC to purchase our products, our friendly and qualified sales associates will help you find the right metal doors, security door hardware, steel doors and frames to fit your project and your budget. Whether you are looking for 100 hollow metal doors or one steel door and frame, Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC has it. All products offered by Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC are tested and pass our personal quality control satisfaction evaluation.

With customer satisfaction as our number one goal, we know that your purchase of metal doors through Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC will be a rewarding experience and we are confident that you will be pleased with your purchase.

Our Products

All metal doors and security door hardware provided by Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC comply with Miami Dade County product approval codes and regulations. We ensure that each component sold by our company has been tested and re-tested – right down to the bolt!

We currently offer our customer’s metal doors, security door hardware, steel doors and frames as well as hollow metal doors for both commercial and residential use. All warranties provided by the manufacturer are provided at the maximum to you. At Amazing Doors & Hardware, LLC, we want to be your one-stop provider for metal doors, locks, hardware and accessories.

Specialties: Steel Doors, Frames and Architectural Hardware (standard and custom) galvanized steel. Security Hardware and more...
