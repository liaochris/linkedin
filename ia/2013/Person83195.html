Lucy Shenouda, CPCC, ACC, ORSCC, NLPMP's Summary

FosterEssence is a coaching and consulting business specializing in Change Leadership. Our programs are designed within an experiential learning environment to facilitate collaborative learning and collective intelligence. At FosterEssence, we believe in cutting through the clutter to get deeper into essence, what needs to emerge and change, to facilitate an environment of innovation, collaboration and creativity.

FosterEssence is a Global Alliance and Co-Founder of LeadingChangeSpecialists. We work with leaders and teams during change transitions, new team assignments, matrix team leadership, new team acquisition, mergers & integration.
