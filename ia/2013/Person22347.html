Ken Poindexter's Summary

Charlotte & Lake Norman Social Media & Internet Video Marketing Consultant. We help our clients get a massive web footprint in conjunction with the latest technology tools and strategies to create visibility, leads and more customers. These include content rich informative and promotional videos placed on hundreds of the most important websites, cross channel mobile marketing systems and many more of the latest tools. Our clients show up on the web where their clients are everyday, building relationships and being a part of the relevant conversations on the web. We bring our clients out from behind the advertising and get them recognized as the experts in their industries and geographic areas. Through the utilization of our systems they are able to retain their existing customers, increase their frequency of patronization and attract new customers.

I am a former Vice President/Technology Services and managed technology projects for several business areas at one of the largest financial institutions in the country.

I have more than twelve years of corporate technology experience as a programmer, systems analyst and project manager. I spent time at Harvard University working with companies from around the world on how to use the internet as a successful business tool.

I also have many years of experience in sales & marketing utilizing my technology background coupled with successful marketing strategies which made me an annual multi-million dollar sales producer.
