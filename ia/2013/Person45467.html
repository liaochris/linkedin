Francesco De Angelis' Summary

I am a research associate at the School of Science and Technology – Computer Science Division, University of Camerino. I received a PhD in "Computer Science and Complex Systems" from the University of Camerino in 2009. For my PhD dissertation, I make research about web service orchestrations and verification of web services compositions. My research interests are in the field of Software Engineering focusing on Software Testing and Service-Oriented Architecture in complex scenarios.

From 2004 I am a member of the research group coordinated by Prof. Flavio Corradini.

Since the academic year 2005-2006 I am also involved in didactics activities of University of Camerino. I am an adjunct professor for "Algorithms and Data Structures" and "Computer Architecture".

From 2010 I am also an adjunct professor in the University of Macerata for the course "basic computer skills".

Since 2007 I have been involved in the activities of the spin-off "e-Linking online systems" (http://www.e-lios.eu) of which I am co-founder. The company operates in the research and development field, in order to find specific and practical solutions for real problems, by using the knowledge coming from research and new technologies.

Specialties: Software Engineering, UML, Object Oriented Analysis and Design, Design Patterns, Software testing. Service Oriented Applications, Web Services.
