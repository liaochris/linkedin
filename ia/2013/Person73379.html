Kyle Blohn's Summary

I'm a tenacious worker with a passion for changing lives. Whether it's working with a kid to pick out a case for their new iPod, or helping design AdWords campaigns for major corporations, I always strive to make my clients be the best that they can be.

I have a deep background in B2B negotiations, and a wealth of experience dealing with the technology sector. I've helped to launch new projects that innovated Apple's approach to helping customers in their world-famous retail stores, and designed a test program for Best Buy's home theater departments that completely changed how the company approached large sales. I also was a launch member of Best Buy's "Twelpforce" initiative, the first major effort by a Fortune 500 company to use Twitter as a means of customer support.

At Dex, I've gotten even more experience by working on a team that was named Google's #1 AdWords partner world-wide, as well as a strategic partner of Bing and Yahoo. I've learned the intricacies of geo-targeting SEM campaigns, as well as how wide the gulf is between doing "just enough to get by" and going all out for clients in online marketing. All this has given me a very real appreciation for the power of social media, SEO and SEM; especially where it involves converting customers that traditionally have shied away from that type of advertising.


Specialties: Client negotiations, rapid training, organizing and managing multiple teams, client presentations, creative technology deployments, presentation delivery, Business metric analysis
