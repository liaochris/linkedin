Gail Finnegan's Summary

I have been a Realtor since 1988 and have helped many families and individuals buy, sell and relocate to the beautiful Augusta County area of the Shenandoah Valley in Virginia. I enjoy meeting people and showing the many special features of the area from the scenic Blue Ridge Parkway and the George Washington National Park to the pristine Victorian inspired town of Historic Staunton with it's many speciality shops, restaurants, Shakespeare Theater and Art Galleries. I have experience in new construction and have represented several builders over the years. Through continuing Education, I have earned the Certified New Home Specialist designation
I am fortunate in that a large part of my business is referral business from past clients and/or repeat business from past clients. I have earned the Certified Residential Specialist Designation and well as the Accredited Buyer Agent Designation.

Specialties

New Homes and New Developments, Residential Properties ranging from Starter Homes to Executive Homes from Historic to New Construction
Licensed to sell real estate in the Common Wealth of VA
