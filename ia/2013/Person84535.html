Vimal Patel's Summary

I provide hospitality executives with hotel management software that expedites operations information. This data plays a key role in central planning and decision-making processes, which enables hotels to realize greater profitability and efficiency.

Mystic Solutions creates custom-programmed applications by teams of hotel management developers with over 10 years of experience. The technology is designed to fulfill hotel management needs ranging from centralizing data from a network of hotels to comparing financial costs and revenue.

Currently, I oversee 5 hotels in Southeast Louisiana under the hotel management business, Cajun Lodging. In addition, I build hotel management web applications that manage the behind-the-scenes action, iCorp (www.icorp.net), and sales action, iSalesonCloud.
