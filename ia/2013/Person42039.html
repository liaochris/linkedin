Eric Dose's Summary

It's not everybody's cup of tea, I suppose--but I do enjoy identifying and explaining new and neglected technological opportunities, seeing upper management grasp what had been missed, and then working with them to set up the first tests.

I enjoy working with lead scientists to move from strategies to demonstrated leads, and all that that entails. I throw energy into all the learning, coordination, and persuasive groundwork it takes to get there--most recently for very advanced measurements in biology.

--------------------------

Expert in aligning scientists' enthusiasm with business needs, and in clearly explaining technical results and their business import to other company leaders, no matter what their technical fluency.

Consistently identifies and hires first-rate corporate scientists at technician through Ph.D. and manager levels; integrates their talents into exemplary teams.

Extensive experience in difficult measurements. Expert in: keeping product development on technically sound bases; anticipating future measurement needs; coordinating projects linking R&D to other departments.

---------------------------

Specialties: Design and implementation of (often difficult) new measurements. Design and apply effective performance metrics for product candidates. Set strategy for measurements in chemistry and molecular biology. Assemble tasks and projects into understandable, efficient, and realistic portfolios.

Identifies, hires, and mentors superior technical talent. Ensures they (1) keep putting their energies into useful directions , (2) have what they need to go fast, (3) work famously well together, (4) enjoy doing it.
