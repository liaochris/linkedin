James DonVito's Summary

Since 1995 James has been providing inspiring concept art and design to the industries of Video Games, Television Animation, Toy Design, and Theme Park Design. His credits also include 3 published video game titles.

Has worked with studios including: Walt Disney Imagineering, Marvel Animation, Warner Brothers Animation, Sony TV Animation, Universal Studios Theme Parks, Nintendo, Mattel, Hasbro, Nike, Spark Unlimited, and 2k Games.

Skills include concept painting and sketching, knowledge of Maya, 3D Studio Max, Rhino, Sketch Up, Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator.

Specialties: Illustration and design of:
- Environments, Backgrounds, and Sets
- Characters and Creatures
- Vehicles and Props
