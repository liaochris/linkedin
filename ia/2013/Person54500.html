Ian Todreas' Summary

Vice President with extensive experience developing climate change, transportation, and sustainability consulting practice areas serving local, state, regional, and federal agencies and nonprofit organizations. Manage multiple and multi disciplinary teams on wide array of complex projects focusing on reducing greenhouse gas emissions, developing communications and executing outreach strategies and stakeholder engagement initiatives, and increasing efficiencies in the energy and transportation sectors. Excel at creating sustainability strategies for organizations and programs, large and small. Widely respected for overall communications skill, creativity, and organizational/management style.
