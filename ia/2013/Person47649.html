Gene Morton's Summary

Consulting in leadership and organization development, in private and public sector construction and healthcare organizations focusing on leadership structure, reorganizations, performance turnarounds, and merger implementations.

Specialties:
-Partnering sessions for construction projects.
-Design and implementation of leadership structure.
-Presentations and seminars: Leading organization transformation and change, leader development and outcome leadership.

Goal: Develop value-added solutions leaders can employ to grow and sustain greater rate of return from their investments in operation and market share.

Gene Morton's Publications

  • Leaders First: Six Bold Steps to Sustain Breakthroughs in Construction

    • Accelerated Solutions, Ld.
    • June 30, 2012
    Authors: Gene Morton

    Read Leaders First and learn how you can connect your leaders' power to success.

    Synopsis: One day, after suffering under a huge load of frustrating customer project problems and wasteful rework, a group of ordinary senior managers awakened their entrepreneurial spirit and committed to make their company an industry leader. With their president, Harvey, they completed six growth-enhancing, capacity building and value-added steps in a matter of days, all while defending their company from the dual swinging swords of competitive market pressures and bleak economic times. In addition, they had resolved, not without some anxiety, many internal antagonisms, confusion and violated expectations. By leading together, they ultimately designed their company's first organized leadership structure and unique strategic vision—guides to stellar performance for many years to come.

    Intended Audiences:
    Innovative and entrepreneurial construction industry leaders, board members, as well as architects and engineers, plus construction management students. Systemic oriented consultants and organization development professionals. Leaders in all industries who want to understand the basic structural dynamics that makes outstanding leadership possible.
