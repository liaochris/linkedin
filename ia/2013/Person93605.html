Michelle Tripp's Summary

I'm a Marketing Strategist and Creative Director based in New York.

For 10+ years I was a Creative Director and copywriter, winning 200+ creative and marketing awards, including 16 Tellys, an IECF Astrolabium (Japan), The Gold CASE Award, New York Globals, ADDYs, and more. During that time I was also the agency's Brand Strategist and developed a proprietary core value brand positioning process, leading workshops and execution in more than a dozen industries, also winning Best of Shows for campaign creative.

I have a lifelong passion for technology and product ideation. The best marketing solves more than just messaging. It uncovers deeper consumer needs and creates a path to better products and stronger interaction with the brand. I've been pushing the integration of digital marketing for clients since 1996, and "social" media since 2004. I have a fascination with the bond between brand and consumer and love how technology has the ability to elevate brand marketing to something higher.

I've worked with Fortune 500 and publicly-traded companies, small and medium-sized businesses, ad agencies, and tech start-ups. My digital experience includes web presence, social strategy, user experience, experiential branding, team training and management, and community architecture at the enterprise level.

I write about social culture, emerging technologies, and the evolution of branding at BrandForward. I'm also a contributor at Adweek. When I'm not on site at client locations in Manhattan, I travel across the U.S. helping agencies integrate social into their service offerings, leading in pitches, and helping B2B and B2C clients build stronger brands.
