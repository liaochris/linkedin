Chris Titze's Summary

Chris Titze runs a successful photo illustration and retouching studio based in Dallas, Texas. Chris also teaches Photoshop workshops in the Dallas area as an adjunct professor. Previously, Chris worked as a Digital Effects Artist for one of the ad industry's hottest shops - Crispin Porter + Bogusky in Miami. While at CP+B Chris had the opportunity to work on an amazing list of clients like VW, Burger King and Microsoft. Chris Titze graduated from the Art Institute of Dallas.


Working at different agencies, Chris Titze had the privilege to work on a wide variety of clients:
