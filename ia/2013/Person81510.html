Lisa Frasier's Summary

At present, I spend most of my free time as a Clay Artisan perfecting my work for art shows and galleries, and part of my time as a freelance artist and illustrator for print and web.

For most of my career I spent over 20 years as a full time news artist creating conceptual and dynamic designs and illustrations for editorial pages of major metropolitan newspapers and magazines. I've also done headline and slogan writing, book illustration, art directing photo shoots, proofreading, research, and production for advertising, editorial/marketing campaigns, and web design.

I have an excellent ability to communicate, mentor and work well with others, as well as work both independently and in a team environment to multi-task with strict deadlines and minimal supervision.

I also have 6 years experience with teaching college courses on Portfolio, Digital Publishing, Digital Imaging, Illustration, and Informational Graphics.

Specific areas of expertise:

• Clay Artisan with experience and knowledge of clay bodies, glazing, hand building, sculpting, using tools and creating forms, greenware, and how to use kilns for bisque and glaze firing.

• Over 20 years experience in editorial design and illustration with major metropolitan newspaper and magazine publications.

• Proficient in color theory and principles of design.

• Proficient in using MAC and PC platforms.

• Proficient with Adobe Creative Suite, Quark XPress, Microsoft Office.

Specific specialties for print and online include: Annual reports, book covers, corporate branding, illustration, photo imaging (color correction, manipulation and retouching), information graphics (data visualization), logos, magazines and newspaper design, posters, production (prepress and online); and other marketing materials such as ads, billboards, booklets/pamphlets, brochures, business cards, handouts, invitations, maps, postcards, posters, power point presentations, product displays, rack cards, and slogan writing.
