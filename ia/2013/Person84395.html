Arno Koch's Experience

Sensei - Author

Makigami.Info

Partnership; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry

February 2009 – Present (4 years 7 months)

Makigami.Info is a knowledge platform and network organisation for Systemic Process Improvement, rooted in the Japanese 'Harmony Model' manufacturing strategies such as TPS on one hand and in the European psychological models starting with Jung on the other hand.

After more than 20 years 'classic -Japanese- improvement strategies', we aim to explore the knowledge field and find new ways, techniques and combinations to make sustainable improvement possible.
