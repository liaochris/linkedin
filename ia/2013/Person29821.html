Daniel Nüst's Summary

I am a researcher with 52°North. Is use my background in the field of geoinformatics and my experience in geographic information systems (GIS), Java, and R to design and develop applications for the Sensor Web and online geoprocessing.

I am interested in using my education to advance interdisciplinary geographical applications for spatio-temporal data with computer science methods utilizing mobile devices, advanced visualisation techniques, and the Sensor Web.

Specialties: Sensor Web, Geoprocessing, Open Source, Citizen Science, Java, R, XML, Web development, Android
