Mathew Smart's Summary

For over 23 years Ace has provided personalised clothing and promotional items for small and large firms, schools, colleges, clubs, private groups and event organisers.

I can find unusual promotion items for product launches, recycled and environmentally friendly sales promotion items from reusable bags, pens made from recycled plastic bottles and getting the entire kit together for football, hockey and sports teams. I enjoy helping organisers of golf days, suggesting a range of giveaways starting at budget prices to luxury personalised goods.

Specialties

Personalisation and branding; screen printing, embroidery, Cad Transfer Printing, Heatseal Transfer & Stickers, Sublimation Full Colour Printing, Vinyl Stickers, Decal Domed Adhesive Badges
