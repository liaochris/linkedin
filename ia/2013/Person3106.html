Alane Pearce's Summary

Alane helps writers create high-quality and professional books and eBooks to meet their goals as authors. Additionally, Alane helps her author/clients develop a comprehensive, step-by-step, proven and effective book promotion plan for book sales.

As an author and freelancer, Alane writes and/or edits web copy, business plans, book proposals, e-newsletters, e-marketing blasts, traditional newsletters, brochures, marketing letters, books, press releases, articles, blog copy, marketing plans, and more; her copy is hailed for its on-target message and often called "best I've seen" by customers.
