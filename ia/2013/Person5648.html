American Passport Now's Summary

My professional experience is in service, customer care, marketing, and sales. For more than 15 years of service to the American public, I have refined my ability to create solutions for time-critical projects based on my superior customer service, planning logistics, implementation, and execution of my mission work.

My strengths are in communications, sales and business development, and account management. I am constantly under pressure to meet customer expectations, to develop new strategies to grow the business, and perform daily conflict resolution involving the issuance of urgent documents for cross-border transactions or business travel deadlines.

My early professional experiences began while serving as a support staff member in the House of Representatives, the Inter-American Development Bank, the Organization of American States, and Freedom House. I learned the dynamics of how things get accomplished in politics, at non-government organizations, think-tanks, and economic institutions. Understanding the importance and relevancy of laws and policy, research and development, public relations, and business development/marketing while on the job working with senior level management, helped me a lot to develop the wisdom and maturity of how to set goals, benchmarks, and create a plan for success.
