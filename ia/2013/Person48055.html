Georgia Daniels, J.D.'s Summary

I offer family and divorce mediation in Pasadena, CA. After thirteen years of law practice, I decided to focus exclusively on mediation. I have trained and mediated in a wide range of settings, from a juvenile justice diversion program, to a public school, and private practice. In addition to divorce mediation, I am available to mediate conflicts involving family-owned businesses and elder care and estate issues. Evening and Saturday appointments are available.

On a personal note, as a divorced single mother, I successfully raised my son to young adulthood. For many years I have been drawn to a spiritual path in life, which leads me to listen to clients with care and compassion.

Specialties

1. Divorce mediation; modifications of existing divorce agreements; pre-marital (pre-nuptial) agreements and living-together agreements.

2. Family issues related to elders and aging, including driving, living arrangements, finances, health care decisions, estate planning and conservatorship (or avoidance of conservatorship).

3. Small business or family-owned business conflict, where the parties will likely continue to be in relationship with each other.
