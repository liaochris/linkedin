Cari-Lee Stevens' Summary

Cari-Lee has been involved in business for over 20 years and is always expanding her entrepreneurial pursuits. She is a health & wellness coach, trainer of trainers and savvy real estate investor. Her fitness company CLS Fitness Inc. is one of the leading and most established providers of contract fitness programs in the province of British Columbia. In real estate, Cari-Lee continues to build on her initial success of a multi-million dollar portfolio within her first two years and is now building towards her goal of 60 additional properties within the next 3 years.

Within the fitness industry, Cari-Lee has worn many hats. She started in the field as a teacher of dance, yoga and myofascial ball and acquired multiple certifications in Pilates and personal training and has since established herself as a sought after trainer of trainers. Cari-Lee also has a successful coaching practise and works with clients internationally. Always a problem solver, when she noticed a lack of qualified instructors in the Pilates field, she designed the Canadian Pilates Institute course, wrote both manuals and regularly facilitates the teacher training.

As a member of High Output Business Networking, she has served as a facilitator and also served on the board of directors of Business Network International (BNI). In 2008 she was the recipient of the Master Networker of the Year award for BNI. She has also been a member and served on the board of the International Fitness Network Association and member of the Vancouver Board of Trade. She obtained her BA in French and Psychology at UBC and the Université of Jean Moulin in France. Cari-Lee’s sessions are fun and laughter filled. She loves to educate and to help people make life long, lasting changes whether it be in their physical health, mental health or both. Her clients have been government organizations, universities and corporations.
