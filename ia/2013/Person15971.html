Brian McCullough's Summary

Over the past eight years, Mr. McCullough has consulted for a wide variety of businesses, including two Hollywood studios, a number of high profile business executives, a national magazine and an apparel company. Brian earned his bachelor of science degree in American government from Oral Roberts University and immediately started his own consulting agency. After successfully selling that company, Brian created Interloop Media Inc., a firm designed to help businesses drive their SEO.

Today Brian offers his expertise as a founding principal at pr loop in Tulsa, Oklahoma.
