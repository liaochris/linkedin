Bob Levin's Summary

Questions about social media marketing? How to help your company get noticed? This is what I am the most passionate about. After running a wholesale home furnishings wholesaler for 17 years constantly put me in situations where I need to make quick decisions. At times they were not profitable. But what I learned is that the company that markets their company with consistency and spends a majority of their time on core competencies (essential and unique to their business) is the company that creates the most cash flow. After all isn't that why we are in business?
Problem – Legal Marketing Company is not able to execute all day-to-day marketing activities keeping on a limited budget.
Action – The President contacts TOI to working as their “marketing assistant” – posting to their Social Media Channels, creating email marketing campaigns, public relation articles, blog submissions
Result – They are now able to cost effective publicize their business so President can focus on contact existing and potential customers to grow their business.
Problem – Website that markets listing services to lawyers is seeking a company to cost-effectively market his company
Actions – They hire TOI to handle all their marketing needs- from writing 30 blog articles a month, email drip campaign, design graphics for their newsletter and manage all social media channels
Results – Owner can now focus all of his attention to hiring staff to contact lawyers all over the country to offer his service – his social media and SEO efforts have been greatly enhanced by our cost-effective online marketing

Specialties: Business Social Media Marketing, Email Drip Campaigns, PR Releases and Submissions, Blog Creation and Submissions, Social Media Creation, Facebook Advertising, Social Media Management
