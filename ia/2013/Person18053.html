Brock Petrie's Summary

Brock Petrie is a versatile Creative Director and Interactive Product Strategist based out of Minneapolis, MN. He has a decade of experience designing and developing brand-sensible web campaigns for a growing array of brands including 3M, Adobe, Allianz, Kodak, Microsoft, Symantec and Target.

Brock specializes in both the design and development of unique, interactive websites and web applications with a focus on web standards compliance, scalability and user accessibility. His areas of expertise extend beyond the browser to mobile design, print design, motion design & animation, identity design, social media and audio production.

Specialties: Brock practices the interactive design, development, user experience strategy, branding, marketing and creative product strategy of web products & projects for start-ups in the interactive space.
