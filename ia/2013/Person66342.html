John Fielder's Summary

John's professional portfolio includes career transition management, executive & management coaching, strategic career development coaching, NLP training, management development, psychometric assessment, top team building and stress management.

John is an accredited Master IIC & NLP Coach, (4000+ accredited coaching hours), Certified Trainer & Master Practitioner of NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), a knowledgeable Career Performance and Executive Coach, Chartered CIPD, Fellow of the Chartered Management Institute, and Charter Member of the Association of Career Professionals International with over 35 years experience of working with individuals, teams and organisations, within a diverse range of companies spanning a wide range of market sectors.

John is an enthusiastic innovator and change agent, who uses experiential approaches, practical and theoretical knowledge, plus NLP techniques to align organisational values, thereby increasing the performance, self-esteem and effectiveness of individuals, teams and organisations. He has coached and trained many thousands of individuals at all organisational levels in their career development, and successfully used psychometrics for the management of change.

John is an international presenter, trainer and speaker on the uses of NLP for career and performance coaching, and has published articles on stress management, performance coaching and the use of the MBTI and NLP for personal and organisational development.

John has worked extensively with HM Forces ~ Royal Navy ~ Army ~ Royal Air Force, delivering NLP Communications Trainings and is on the academic faculty and Final Coach Assessor with Noble Manhattan Coaching.

Specialties
