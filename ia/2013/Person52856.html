Herbjeet Bal's Summary

There is an inherent conflict between software quality and software delivery. Quality improvements and software testing can be done forever, however, we must deliver product for our business to succeed. I excel in bridging this conflict by using my skills to deliver software on time for our internal and external stakeholders, on quality for our customers, and on budget for our business.

I continually strive to raise the bar in software testing by getting constant feedback and improving processes as well as fighting for, encouraging, and expanding the knowledge and skill-set for the department.

Specialties

Virtualization technologies, software testing strategies and techniques, Windows domain knowledge, networking domain knowledge, disaster recovery business space
