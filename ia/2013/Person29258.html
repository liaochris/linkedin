  • Classic For Kids

    • January 2007 to Present
    Team Members: Daniel Fein, Julia Smith, Michelle Halpin White, Vanessa Miller, Carl Thomas

    The Classic for Kids is an annual charity golf tournament held in Napa, California to raise funds for the Solano Kids Insurance Program(SKIP). SKIP enables eligible Solano County children to receive health and dental care insurance. Since 2007, the Classic for Kids Golf Tournament has raised over $1,260,000 for this worthwhile cause. These children typically do not qualify for government sponsored programs such as Medi-Cal and come from families who can not afford health insurance for their children.
