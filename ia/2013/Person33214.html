Dean Brady's Summary

I have more than 20 years of experience delivering consumer technology products to the global market. My experience encompasses the entire product lifecycle from ideation to end of life. As the founder of the Intuitive Customer Experience team at 3Com's consumer division, I focused on creating continuity and valuable experiences with 3Com's consumer products.

My key areas of interest are technology-enabled products and applications that can create life-changing experiences.
