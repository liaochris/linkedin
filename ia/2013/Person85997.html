Marilyn Larese's Experience

President and Founder

MJL Performance Solutions, LLC

1990 – Present (23 years)

MJL Performance Solutions, LLC provides custom-designed solutions for improving personal and workplace performance. Marilyn Larese, president and founder, has 22 years experience creating and delivering results-focused organizational programs. Her areas of expertise include management and leadership development, communication, team building, and business acumen. She also has extensive experience developing technical training materials, including business systems and health and safety. Marilyn has assisted manufacturing companies, service organizations, health care, dental and advanced educational institutions with their organizational and professional development needs.

Clients describe Marilyn as an expert in her field, a person with high integrity, and a valued partner providing great results.

MJL Performance Solutions is a certified Women’s Business Enterprise and an authorized distributor of Inscape Publishing, the world's leading provider of DiSC solutions.
