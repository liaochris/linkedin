Josh O'Neal's Summary

I'm currently a mobile software engineer, formerly a web developer. I began my career writing PHP frameworks for web-based applications, but around 2009, I became unable to resist the allure of a mobile platform, and began developing some iOS applications on the side, as a hobby. A few short years later, I now find myself a full time mobile developer, leading the Android efforts at 004 Technologies USA, as well as assisting in the iOS based applications we create.

During my time at Parkland College, I was also a pseudo-TA for their brand new Android Development course.

I have experiences in mobile development with Objective-C (iOS) & Java (Android), some C++, and a whole bunch of web goodness: PHP, MySQL, CSS, HTML, Javascript, jQuery
