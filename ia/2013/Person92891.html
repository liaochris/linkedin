Michael Stone's Summary

A successful and passionate web developer, looking to make a difference not only for our end users, but within whatever team I'm on.

Primarily focused on the front end and middle tier while using ColdFusion, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and jQuery. Well versed in Oracle SQL using packages, views, T-SQL and query optimization.

I take pride in being a leader and someone who's very easy to talk to and is always willing to lend a hand to help.

To me, a great web development team is a family. You have to be open minded, patient, willing to talk about your problems and have the will to be successful. If you can acquire this, you're going to be doing great things together.
