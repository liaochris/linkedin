Grzegorz Szczytowski's Summary

Networking expert with 11 years' experience in mobile telecommunication industry, including 6 years' in UMTS/GPRS Mobile Packet Core (SGSN,GGSN,RNC/BSC) and Datacom (Routers,Switches, Loadbalancers, Firewalls) network design, dimensioning, acceptance testing and optimization and 5 years' in NSS elements (BSC,MSC,HLR,TMSC,VMS) development, configuration, integration and troubleshooting.

Technical expert on Nokia and Huawei Mobile Packet Core solutions (2G/3G-SGSN, GGSN, CG) with strong Starent GGSN knowledge focusing on end to end service design and testing of brand new GGSN hardware platform and software versions (NE80E/V900R007, Dual chassis FISN/4.0 CD2, ST40/8.0) including traffic classification and content billing functionality (DPI techniques), online prepaid billing interface (Diameter CCA and OSA/Parley) and user end to end bandwidth limitation and Fair use policy implementation (AAA – Radius based, Diameter Gx – PCRF based).

Deep knowledge about various IP VAS platforms (AAA, WAPGW, MMSC, Video-Streaming) and mobile data optimization, acceleration and adaptation platforms from Bytemobile (Unison) and Flashnetworks (Nettgain).

Excellent working knowledge of Cisco (6500,4500,4900,3500,7200,7600), Juniper (M-series,J-series) and Huawei (8500,6500,5700,NE80E,NE40E) flagship datacom platforms and range of Operating System environments (Linux,Solaris,FreeBSD,IPSO,MacOS,HP Unix) together with most popular network application and daemons with standalone and cluster based configurations.

Very strong experience on network security policy design and implementation using two main telco-grade Firewall and VPN (IPSEC and SSL) solutions based on Checkpoint ( Firewall-1 NGX/GX on Nokia IPSO and Checkpoint SPLAT) and Juniper (Netscreen 500,SSG) implemented in Active/Active and Active/Standby mode.

Packet data content billing frauds mitigation audits using protocol fuzzing techniques and own proof of concept techniques and developed tools.
