Michele Broad NP-C's Summary

My professional experience is in the medical filed in the area of women's health care. I owned and operated a medical office for women for the past 8 years and now I am the owner of the new online community at www.wellwomennetwork.com. My passion is helping women to understand their health better, to be an advocate for better health care for women, and to teach women how to be financially healthy through owning their own business . I also speak professionally on a variety of topics that concern women's health.

Well Women Network well empower and show women all over the globe how to maximize their health, wealth and life! Support that can be counted on with clear answers to the latest health issues that face women today. Our mission is to inspire and teach women how to create innovative solutions and make informed choices that not only improve the quality of their health but of their whole lives. We guide, motivate, and educate women to achieve their own optimal state of health and professional well-being as defined by them. We view ourselves as partners with our women clients and stand firm to the belief that together we bring out the best in each other.

My goal is to become an international speaker on women's health issues.

Specialties: I specialize in women's health and adult medicine and I sub specialize in menopause and bio-identical hormone replacement therapy and adrenal fatigue. I am a coach/consultant and educator for women on how to optimize their health and wealth.
