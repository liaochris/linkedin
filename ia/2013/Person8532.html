Ann Marie Monteiro's Summary

Experienced Multimillion Dollar Producing Real Estate Agent 2005 - Present.

I obtained my Real Estate license in 2000 and worked part-time at Re/Max of Princeton as an assistant to top producing agents while attending Rutgers University. Since graduating with a BA in Economics I have worked for the leading residential real estate agency in Manhattan - The Corcoran Group. As a project coordinator for the Marketing Division, a premier provider of consulting, marketing, sales and leasing services to New York’s most distinguished residential real estate developers, I learned the process in taking new residential buildings from pre-development to sales and leasing.

My transformation in 2005 from major development projects in Manhattan to single family homes in Central New Jersey was perpetuated by my desire to bring my knowledge and experience to consumers on a more personal level and to work in the market I know best around my hometown of Princeton, NJ.

Specialties

Represent buyers and sellers of luxury homes, new construction, condominiums, investment properties and rentals throughout Mercer, Middlesex & Somerset Counties.
