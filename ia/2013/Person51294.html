Ph.D. Thesis, "Video-Based Patrolling Using a Mobile Platform": The main goal of my PhD work is to match two videos of the same environment recorded by mobile platforms following unknown trajectories at different instances. In this work, we explore more complex imaging models for solving the video registration task and propose a framework that accomplishes spatiotemporal registration and change detection.

Master of Science Thesis, "Real Time Turkish Sign Language Recognition System from Video Using Hidden Markov Models": I developed a vision based Turkish Sign Language (TSL) recognition system.
