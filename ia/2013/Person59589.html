Jason Wiser's Summary

Jason is a web professional. He is a church website designer, web master, and social media consultant. To him, church websites and social media are both ways to promote the Gospel by helping to create a platform for ministries to gain visibility and to be more effective in their calling and missions work. It is a ministry, not a business. All funds raised from website sales are donated to the ministry work in Ukraine.

Jason and Valeriia Wiser are Christian missionary's working in Ukraine and have been in ministry since 2006. Their ministry has been steadily growing and have branched out into nearby countries as well. Jason is passionate about presenting Christ centered training curriculum for anyone working with children in crisis, and helping churches, missionaries, and para-church organizations by building church websites and providing Social media consultation to help missionary fundraising.

The Committee for Child Protection of Ukraine has significant priority. The primary function is to provide training material to people who work directly with children in crisis. The long term vision is an international training center where groups from all over Eastern Europe can gather to study social work principles which are founded on the Word of God.

Jason and Valeriia are partnering with the Church of the Living God and the Romani (gypsy) community alongside Pastor Brugosh in Uzhgorod, Ukraine.

His passion is to watch people grow in their faith and become transformed by the power of God.

Valeriia assists in translating materials from English to Russian for training materials.

Specialties

Church website design, Web Master, Social Media consultation, Christian missions, conference coordination, training material promotion, Wordpress, HTML,CSS, PHP. Missionary Websites, Para Church Websites, Christian Websites, Email Marketing, social networks, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Picassa, Google products, any many more resources pertaining to the web.
