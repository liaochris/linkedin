Lesley Lambert's Summary

I am entering my 25th year in the real estate field. I have worked as a salesperson, manager, trainer, speaker, publicist and media front in this field. I am dedicated to making every sale a professional experience for my client.

My background in management means that I am organized, knowledgeable and serious about the field. My experience in public relations gives me the ability to inventively promote my listings to the public. My experiences as a home buyer and seller myself give me objectivity and empathy for my client.

I love using social media to market myself and my properties and have spent the last few years creating a marketing plan that has created lots of new opportunities for me. I consult on the use of social media and have trained on the topic around the country.

My moldering degree in English has been dusted off for the blogs that I contribute to on a regular basis. I write for my own blogs as well as a national online magazine for real estate agents and a a local online magazine.

Specialties: Historic homes, new construction, residential sales, investment property.

Social media,video blogging, blogging, twitter, facebook.
