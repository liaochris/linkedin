Ian Stirling's Summary

Business Growth through Planning and Action - Hands on support. Growth Accelerator Registered and Approved Coach. Call my office 01603 920227 or 07879 402887 at any reasonable UK time Skype ianstirling100 ian.s@objectivesreached.co.uk

My experience and success is in both assisting businesses to use their marketing budget to the best effect and turning opportunities into a business.

I listen for businesses saying “We need some Marketing (or eMarketing)”. I have found some of these businesses do indeed need to improve their marketing; however, many have other issues holding them back. This is the reason we offer a free business or marketing review – we say 2 hours although in reality many are nearer a day by the time I have thought about the business and written up the report.

I specialise in family run businesses of all sized, private companies and Not for profit (special rates apply). As I understand their decision making processes.

I have become an expert on eMarketing – we are quite different to many of those we compete with. We set targets in terms of sales and leads not vague targets such as “we will get you to number 1 on Google for the term of “whatever”.

Training is still the part of the role I like best and produces great outcomes. The range inc. Decision making – practical course that gives people a number of tools they can apply to a problem or situation. How to run a meeting – practical steps to stop wasting hours of time by going around in circles. At the moment eMarketing is the one people keep coming back for more – from ‘how to profit from social networking’, through ‘emails that generate sales’ to ‘make money from your website’.
