Joshua McVey's Summary

I strive to create beautiful and clear interactive experiences for users of all types. This includes developing software, plugins, and games across a variety of platforms. I try to keep my limitations low, and not be confined to one OS or one method of thought.

I've been behind the curtain at software development companies and become adept at working with clients of many types, and in many ways. Whether through email, phone, CRM, or face to face communications, I have increased the positive experiences of clients. In cases where communication was lacking, I have implemented new ways to get a clear message across, including training seminars, single page product outlines, and tutorials.

Specialties
