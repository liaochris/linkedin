Jeff Rowley's Summary

Jeff Rowley is an Australian Big Wave Surfer and Adventure Athlete who travels the world in search of the biggest and best waves.

Jeff specialises in paddle-in surfing the biggest waves in the world. Jeff travels the world at a moments notice to chase monster swells that develop on the weather map.

From giant 'Cloudbreak' in Fiji, monstrous 'Jaws' in Maui to deadly 'Teahupoo' in Tahiti, Jeff's colours can be seen loud and clear.

Jeff is best known for being part of a small group of the best big wave surfers in the world dedicated to paddle-in surfing and ditching jet ski assistance to tow into waves.

Jeff has a specialised year-round training program to prepare for riding gigantic waves which consist of a pool based breath training and gym weight resistance training.

Jeff's sponsors are Channel Islands Surfboards, Go Natural, Strapper Surf, Xcel Wetsuits and Caribee. Jeff's official suppliers are Justin's and Bomboras
