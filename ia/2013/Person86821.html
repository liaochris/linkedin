Mark Lusher's Summary

My background starts in Italy, where I spent four glorious years in the Air Force. It was a time of learning and growth. Not only was I working in the electronics field, I took the time to study the Italian language and culture.

After that I spent many years in corporate America, working first in electronics but quickly moving into the information technology field. Metro Atlanta offered many opportunities to further my career.

I am an avid skier and hiker, so in 2007 I moved to beautiful Boulder,Co. to take advantage of the outdoor opportunities available here.

Having started my own business a couple years before I reestablished myself here in the Front Range. Currently supporting dozens of small businesses in many capacities.

I have gained a broad base of knowledge, having worked with hundreds of small businesses through the years. My goal is to help your business succeed.

In addition to my consulting and technical skills, I cofounded a coworking space in Boulder and starting a brewery. I don't like to limit myself and put boundaries on things I'm able to do.
