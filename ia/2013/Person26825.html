Stefan Witthalm's Summary

SWM Consulting is a consultancy firm that the Mission has to change the world by changing business of customers.

The world is changing faster than ever. The political, economic, social, technological. For better or for worse. We believe in entrepreneurship and innovation as positive force. Transformation is yourself, your company or your industry substantially change by inn ... ... ovation. Innovation is in our view anything other than cutting, outsource, cut off, although companies reduce or bends there also not always escape. Innovation is new, create sustainable value through a unique new combination to find products, markets, technologies, processes or distribution channels. That brings real innovation for customers, employees, suppliers, but also to society in General. Not so much out of idealism, but from a long-term perspective on value creation in the 21st century. The company actively changes the world instead of to adapt to a changing world. Making it a positive factor in a sometimes difficult to indicate environment. There are three conditions: unique strategic movements, the successful launch of required implementation processes and ' natural ' leadership.

SWM Consulting helps its clients to think about the direction by both, as well as on the road up to it, that the required support and leadership there.

Specialties: Project management, Technical engineer-manager, Quality manager, etc..
