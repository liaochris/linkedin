Jeffrey Cutter, CPA, PFS, MBA, MS's Summary

Jeffrey Cutter graduated from the Massachusetts Maritime Academy. After a successful career as an Officer in both the US Merchant Marine & the US Naval Reserves, he earned an MS in Accounting & an MBA, with distinction, from the Executive Program at the Graduate School of Professional Accounting at Northeastern University. He began his financial career in 1994 at KPMG Peat Marwick in both Boston, MA and Providence, RI as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA).

In 2002, with a passion to help others with tactical & strategic private wealth and financial planning, Jeff Cutter opened Cutter Financial Group, LLC in Mansfield, MA. Seeing the need for help within the conservative and retirement communities, Jeffrey expanded his business to Cape Cod in 2006. Cutter Financial Group, LLC is a family run company specializing in comprehensive wealth and retirement planning, investments, & insurance specifically for those who appreciate safe, secure investments & who don’t want to risk moving backwards financially. As retirement & investment specialists, they focus on creating value, security, & peace of mind for clients and their families. Jeffrey specializes in protecting clients’ life savings from market volatility, creating family legacies, income tax reduction, and income planning to assist in retirement. Their strategies focus on low risk and low volatility, coupled with high probability of success.

Jeffrey’s background & education allows him to objectively evaluate everything from retirement plans & educational funding to investments & estate planning. In the past, he has created training platforms to teach tactical agents tax-free retirement planning, health & life insurance strategies, & safe & secure investing strategies. His focus on education also earned him the designation of becoming part of an exclusive group, as an Ed Slott Elite IRA Advisor. Ed Slott, America’s IRA expert, has trained Jeff to grow & protect his clients’ money in these turbulent times.
