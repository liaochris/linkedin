Marc Puddy's Summary

Prior to entering the Insurance Industry, Marc worked in the Financial Services Industry for the world’s largest multi-national securities firm where he was responsible for institutional client transactions in the High Yield and Provincial Bond markets. After a wave of consolidation, he elected to join Jones Brown Inc.

In 2003 Marc was appointed Partner with responsibilities for the Sports Division. His interest and knowledge in this specialized area assisted in building an impressive list of Canadian and international clients in both insurance and risk management transactions. He was instrumental in the development of the sports related divisions within Jones Brown Inc., including Motorsport and Golf. In 2007 Marc was chosen for by the firm for secondment to London, England, working with the largest independent Lloyd’s of London brokerage, Miller.

In June 2011 Marc purchased his own insurance brokerage, Chris Steer Insurance Brokers, specializing in House Hold Goods, Moving and Storage. By transfering this unique knowledge and skill sets and combinging insurance company relationships developed of 50 years into new areas of focus, Chris Steer Insurance Brokers is implementing an aggressive growth strategy.

Marc resides in Toronto and Medonte Township. His interests include running, golf, skiing and squash. Marc has been a licensed private pilot since 1992 and is active in Federal politics and several charitable organizations.
