Ed Berliner's Summary

I have been part of the broadcast media industry for over 25 years, both in front of and behind the camera/microphone. At present, I have put my experience to work as a Media Trainer/Head Coach at "The Undefeated Image".

The key words here are "Image Management". No matter what your business, career goals or social status, there is nothing more important that your image.

Nothing.

More than just media training, I work with broadcast and non-broadcast clients in crafting and maintaining a perfect image in both professional and personal life. My client list ranges from major University athletic teams, professional sports leagues and administrations to CEO's and sales forces for some of the Nation's biggest companies.

I also work with a large number of public relations firms, non-profit corporations, politicians, legal firms, medical professionals and law enforcement agencies. "The Undefeated Image" instructs their clients and employees on learning lessons from media examples in how to deal with the general public and co-workers.

I am also available for TV and radio guest appearances as an expert commentator on broadcast and social media issues. Please contact me as well for guest speaking engagements at conventions, trade shows and private corporate events.

Please learn more about "The Undefeated Image" at http://theundefeatedimage.com and read my daily comments on current media appearances and issues. Then please take a few minutes to contact me and let's explore how we can work together and bring "The Undefeated Image" to your business.

Specialties: TV/Film production, broadcast programming, media marketing, program creation and inception for broadcast and Internet delivery, media consulting, media training, remote production services, TV/radio/film talent
