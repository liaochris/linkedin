Gary Bamberger's Summary

Professional and personal leadership coach with a proven track record of working with people and organizations to align efforts with goals in order to attain desired results. I also partner with organizations to determine the vision / mission, target markets, goals and objectives, and implementation strategy necessary to move the organization through their transition period.
