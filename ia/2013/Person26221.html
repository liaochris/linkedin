Colby James M Smith's Summary

James Smith is a highly skilled Enterprise Architect with a broad range of knowledge across technology and business practices. With more than twenty years of experience in software development and over ten years in architecture and design of enterprise level systems, he possesses a depth of experience not easily found amongst technology professionals. James has a rare ability to bridge the communication gap between technology and business professionals while also providing invaluable foresight and vision to a project.

Throughout his career, James has demonstrated an ability to lead small and large teams to success in very diverse and challenging environments. In delivering these successes, he has accepted a wide range of roles; VP of Engineering, Product Manager, Architect, Technology Director, Business Process Consultant and Program/Project Manager are just a few examples. It is this broad skill set combined with the deep domain knowledge that is the most valuable assets that James brings to a team.
