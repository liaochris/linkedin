Matt Hall's Summary

Matthew Hall was hired with Universal Forest Products as an Industrial Specialist Engineer to work at the newly acquired facility in Florida. Universal Forest Products Inc. and its subsidiaries design, manufacture and market wood and wood alternative products to several different markets. He is the liaison between sales and production. His main area of focus was working in the industrial sales department to increase margins on industrial products using value engineering while assisting in the sales, operations and production departments in different scopes of work. Please read the experience section for more information about his duties and achievements working with Universal Forest Products Inc.
