David O'Brien's Summary

Internationally recognized author, consultant, speaker, coach and trainer with 25 years of experience in helping leaders and teams maximize performance potential. Proven track record of exceeding client expectations in a wide range of corporate, non-profit and public sector environments.

Strong commitment to community service and active board member of Mental Health Association of CT, National Association of Asian American Professionals (NAAAP) and leadership advisor to Association of Latino Professionals in Finance and Accounting (ALPFA)

Adjunct Faculty: CT Nonprofits, Center for Professional Development, Hartford, CT

Guest Lecturer: UConn School of Business
