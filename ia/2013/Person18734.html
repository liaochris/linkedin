Bob Stolzberg's Summary

I am an extremely passionate, positive, successful leader who is known as a visionary and entrepreneur with over 15 years of deep enterprise IT experience focused on UNIX and cloud computing. I'm also a family-man and a Freemason. My career background includes team and senior leadership positions, senior engineer/architect, consultant, and operations management roles. I have owned or been a key partner in numerous small businesses which focused on IT hosting, consulting, platform-as-a-service, and software-as-a-service offerings. I successfully scale business by sharing knowledge through collaboration and enabling teams.

In my current role as a Senior Solutions Design Engineer I am responsible for designing complex enterprise cloud, network, colocation, and managed hosting solutions using a broad mix of Savvis offerings and professional services.
