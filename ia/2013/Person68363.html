IE Business School EMBA International Senior Executive, Oxford University educated, bilingual Spanish/English & working knowledge of Italian and Portuguese, 20+ years of Executive responsibilities across different geographies (Europe and Latin America), background in multinational corporations (tobacco and automotive) and in multinational consultancy.
