Michell Zappa's Summary

Tinkerer, thinker, builder, breaker, designer, hacker. Born in Stockholm & raised around the world (currently in São Paulo). Previous lives as ad-man, video-editor, art-director & web-designer. Owns more books than vinyl but fewer trousers than computers. Sci-fi admirer, photo buff, music lover, future shocked & loving it.

Perpetually fascinated by tech. Studies the nature of tech, the ethics of progress and its effects on society. Founding executive director of the Envisioning Technology Research Foundation in Brazil. Wants to tell the world what he believes.

Believes we are smarter than tech. That predicting probable technological change is reassuringly simple and should be accessible to all. That society dictates tech's direction but tech dictates the pace. That we need better dialogue around the nature of progress. That education can be unbroken. Believes he can help others think about tech & progress this way.
