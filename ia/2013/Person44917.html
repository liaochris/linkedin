These major brands trust me with their reputation in Ireland. Over the past 17 years, they have all appointed my company, Fixtronix Ltd. as their exclusive Service Partner in this market.

We only work with manufacturers and distributors who take the Irish market seriously and we offer them a mutually beneficial relationship which strengthens their brand and market share.

We component level fault find and repair digital and analogue circuitry in professional audio equipment used in commercial broadcast, recording, production, editing & live performance.

We are also the exclusive support centre in Ireland for ODEON Group cinemas, looking after their sites throughout Ireland, including Northern Ireland.

We are a full line service centre, and we can and do repair all makes and models of professional audio from a diverse range of manufacturers.

I have extensive experience in component level fault finding and repair (digital and analogue circuits), with attention to detail & client satisfaction. This includes full SMT rework facilities for all packages of Surface Mount Devices.

In Ireland, you won't find a better qualified and experienced person to offer you solutions for repair, service and modification to professional audio products.
