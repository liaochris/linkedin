Brandon Karratti's Summary

Who am I? I'm a designer, a producer, a communicator, a writer, and a creator. I love to craft and develop interesting experiences for others, and I use my talents to that end. My goal on every project that I take on is to create the greatest experience possible for the end user, in the most efficient and entertaining way possible.

Producer (Game) & Scrum Master

University of Utah - Entertainment Arts & Engineering

Educational Institution; 10,001+ employees; Higher Education industry

May 2011 – May 2013 (2 years 1 month) Salt Lake City

I am a graduate of the University of Utah's top-ranked Entertainment Arts & Engineering program, where I studied game production and design in multidisciplinary teams.

During that time, I worked on multiple projects and prototypes, as well as two published projects, including Aquaducks for the Utah Natural History Museum, and Race of the Zodiac, which is a published title for Android as well as Kinect/PC.

The friends, contacts and experience that I gained from this program have been invaluable in my career.

President / Founder

Press Start Career Club

August 2012 – January 2013 (6 months) Salt Lake City, Utah

Press Start is an organization dedicated to helping individuals in and around the Univerisity of Utah to better prepare themselves to seek and acquire employment in the video game industry. The primary focus of the group is understanding personal brand awareness, how to interview and present yourself, resume work, and networking skills.

I founded this club with Sterling Pierce in 2012, and am currently President-Emeritus since graduation.

Media Designer

PoleVault Media

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

May 2012 – August 2012 (4 months) Greater Salt Lake City Area

In the summer between my grad school semesters, I worked as a designer at PoleVault Media, working on everything from web site redesigns to media campaigns to even designing concepts for a portable mercury testing lab. It was a varied, interesting, and versatile environment with a variety of challenges, and I was grateful for the time that I spent there.

Producer (Game) & Scrum Master

Aquaducks

October 2011 – November 2011 (2 months) Salt Lake City

I designed and developed this game with a talented team of artists and engineers for the Utah Natural History Museum, in an effort to engage children in learning about water conservation in Utah. I was responsible for the design, presentation, and management of the project.

The game is still on display as of March 2012.

Producer (Film)

Below The Line - Pilot

December 2008 – April 2010 (1 year 5 months) Orem, UT

Below The Line was an independent television pilot exploring those on a television set who are often overlooked - the grips, the electrics, the producer's assistants, the camera guys, etc. The idea was to have a show within a show, as the story played out during the filming of the hit procedural drama, Sugar and Spies.

I was one of the original creators of the show, along with Weston Critchfield and Chris Clark, and during filming was responsible for unit production, scheduling, and day-to-day operations management of this independent television sitcom pilot submitted to ITVFest in 2010.

Student Lead Producer (Broadcast)

I scheduled and managed crew for live broadcast of multiple collegiate sports broadcasts, and acted as liason between management and talent, crew, and advertising representatives. This required the ability to thrive in a quick-thinking environment, and being able to deftly diffuse high-stress situations.
