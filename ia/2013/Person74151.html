    • January 2013 to Present
    Team Members: Kendall Morgan, Sean Payne, Joe Czubiak, Tatsuya Matsubara

    We are creating an online application that interfaces with Facebook and Twitter APIs. This allows psychics to create readings online. When a student makes a status update or tweet, the program analyzes the word choice and determines the student's mood. It then sends them a relevant reading based on that mood. Students can like or dislike the reading to filter the psychics they enjoy. There is also a psychic dashboard, where psychics can monitor metrics about their fortunes applications and level of success.
