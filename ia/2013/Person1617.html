Adam Schweigert's Summary

I love building things and connecting dots.

I'm an accomplished design-oriented digital strategist with experience helping organizations from small nonprofits to Fortune 500 companies.

Interested in using technology to connect people, build stronger communities and make the world a better place.

These days I'm most passionate about user-experience design and social technologies, but historically I'm a big thinker about nonprofits, technology, journalism and particularly the intersection of the three.

I've also (at various times) written a symphony, built and programmed robots, designed interactive sound installations and created original music and sound design for theater and modern dance.

I'm equal parts right and left brained, a lover and creator of well-designed user experiences and compelling stories, effectively told and an all-around (occasionally wannabe) geek.

Local food lover, runner, climber, coffee drinker, photographer, bike rider, writer (and occasional blogger), lover of the outdoors, dog person.

Specialties

All of the above, plus leading and managing organizational change and innovation, thinking differently, delivering thoughtful solutions to difficult problems and operating a red pen (with a special focus on buzzword removal).
