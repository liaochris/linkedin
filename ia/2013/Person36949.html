Dora Tarver's Summary

Ms. Tarver is a Senior Program/Project Manager and member of PMI with extensive experience leading and training teams of professional consultants and employees, including external resources, to successfully deliver technology solutions. Expertise in systems and application development, change management, resource management, risk management, team development, client management, time & cost analysis, business analysis, and project schedule/project plan development. Also skilled at working directly with CTOs, VPs, Directors, Business Managers, Technical Leads, Developers, driving design, development, testing and implementation of new products and services using Agile and Waterfall methodologies. Over 20 years experience in SDLC. Have published papers and spoken across the country for PMI, including at the PMI Annual Seminars & Symposium. Most recently spoke at SharePoint Saturday in NYC on how to quickly set up SharePoint to successfully manage projects.
