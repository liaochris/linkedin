In addition to the iOS Objective-C iPad and iPhone apps I also work with an advanced CEP/ESP engine on the back end to detect complex time based patterns in claim and other data in order to feed alerts to our end users. I also built a MongoDB based caching and archival service for our jasper reports, enabling enhanced performance as well as completely new capabilities requiring access to historical reports.
