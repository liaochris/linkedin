Dennis Major's Summary

During my career, I have designed and implemented systems and networks for a group of travel companies operating multiple call centers and retail locations. These systems support a complex exchange of data between branches, online reservations systems and Head Office. Other implementations include a turnkey insurance application environment used by major US and Canadian property and casualty insurance companies for processing large volume transactions.

Currently, I am a Network Manager for a chemicals manufacturing company. As a global leader, their technology and patents are used by many companies across the world. I have implemented a MPLS network as well as VoIP, a VMware and Hyper-V virtualization environment across all locations complete with a comprehensive data backup and redundancy solution allowing any site to provide hot backup capabilities including a complete disaster recovery plan. There is an extensive network that includes company locations as well as customer locations that are linked in to utilize their technology.

I was previously, Network Operations Manager, for a Mobile Solutions software application developer. This environment was constructed using Microsoft solutions, processes data from mobile CE devices connecting through telecom lines and the Internet. A complete network solution with redundant site locations provides this company with a complete self maintained Application Hosting Solution using Microsoft technology. Active Directory (Server 2003), SQL, Exchange, RDA, Checkpoint, Cisco equipment, IBM servers, VPN's, VoIP are just a few technologies used in this 24x7 high availability environment, along with Internet and telephone technology used for handheld communications. This solution processes more than half of Hallmark Cards business.


I have earned numerous industry certifications that demonstrate my technical abilities with experience in Managing, Designing, and Implementation of solutions.
