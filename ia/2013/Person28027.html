Joseph Cruise, M.D.'s Summary

Cruise Plastic Surgery is dedicated to patient education and satisfaction. We strive to make sure that from the very first contact you have with us, to the surgery and post operative care you will always feel comfortable and welcome in our office. Our goal is to provide you with the best of what plastic surgery has to offer with uncompromised safety.

Dr. Cruise dedicated his practice entirely to cosmetic plastic surgery. This includes all aspects of cosmetic surgery including facial plastic surgery, aesthetic breast surgery, body contouring, skin care, and soft tissue fillers.

Our elegant office is conveniently located within beautiful Fashion Island in Newport Beach. Your consultation typically takes between 1 to 1.5 hours. During this time we put forth a significant amount of effort to make sure we understand your goals and desires. We listen to how dramatic or subtle you would like your results to be. It is very important to us that when you leave our consultation you have a full understanding of the surgical procedure, recovery time and expectations.

Specialties

For nose, neck and chin surgery we typically do digital imaging. For our breast augmentation patients we get engaged in bra sizing to make sure that when you leave the office, you know what size implant you will go with, what profile and type. We also discuss the desired shape and cleavage. In general, the more educated and prepared you are before the surgery the easier the recovery. We are here to help every step out of your way.
