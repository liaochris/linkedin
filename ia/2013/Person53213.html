Daniel Hires' Summary

Daniel Hires is a communications and marketing expert, with a profound affinity towards sustainability, start-ups and social business. His core interest lies in using contemporary technology and real experiences to design purposeful communication strategies for a better world.

As a creative freelancer for innovative eco-social projects and businesses in Berlin, Daniel is an innovator and highly resourceful catalyst who can accelerate projects in different capacities due to his broad skill set. He often draws upon his natural literacy of Web 2.0 as a digital native as well as his background in managing a campus-wide concert series in the USA.

Trained at the University of Michigan where leadership is a core value, Daniel has years of experience in leading teams, especially motivating and coordinating volunteers. Daniel’s international upbringing as a ‘Third Culture Kid' (USA/Germany) in addition to being a 'Korean Adoptee' lends him a high degree of cultural and social competence, which paired with his bilingual communication skills, make him an adaptable and versatile networker anywhere in the world. His corporate experience in designing and selling B2B climate neutral services sharpens his strategic focus on customer-centric marketing and a result-oriented working style. Daniel’s profile is completed by a US-influenced entrepreneurial mindset, and a personality that can inspire and motivate others.
