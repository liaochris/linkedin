Andrea Herrst's Summary

Chiropractic physician with experience in treating business professionals that need help with repetitive stress injuries and posture while still performing above expectations on the job.
I also enjoy working with expectant moms and kids.

Experienced in treating common spinal complaints of pregnancy, as well as low back pain, headaches, plantar fascitis and more.

Staff physician in physical therapy department for Oregon Ballet theatre, with past experience as team physician for ORSU Jesters Men's rugby team.

Active member of Oregon Chiropractic Association, American Chiropractic Assocation, American Pregnancy Association, National Assocation of Professional Women.

Member of Oregon Board of Chiropractic Examiners Examinations, Tests, Substances, Devices & Procedures (ETSDP) Committee.

Lobbied in Washington, DC and Salem, OR on numerous occasions for patient rights, provider parity and public health initiatives.

Specialties

Soft tissue injuries, repetitive motion injuries, instrument assisted soft tissue therapy (Graston technique), Kinesiotape, Pregnancy chiropractic care, pediatric chiropractic, dragonboat / paddling injuries and rehab,
