James Christie's Summary

In College, James was named Team Captain and MVP. He also was named Mid A... and played at the Under 20 National Team. He has played with and been coached by some of the greatest names in North American Soccer History.

After knee surgeries and nagging injuries cut his playing career short, James needed something to take the place of his passion for Soccer. He started to lift weights and became a Personal Trainer helping adults and youth improve their lifestyles and fitness levels. James has been a Personal Trainer for the last nine years and currently still trains at the prestigious Union League Club in New York City.
