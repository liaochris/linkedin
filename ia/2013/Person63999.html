Jim McKean's Summary

Jim likes to call himself a "portfolio entrepreneur." He is a management consultant, an expert at collaboration, a technology project manager, and a career consultant. He knows how to help groups of people work better together, has a deep understanding of business strategy, and a special talent for applying strategies and branding to small to mid-sized businesses and individual careers.

As a consultant, he has a reputation for grasping business models quickly and identifying the sources of true added value. He is known for his ability to help clients find their way through the complexities of strategy development, technology projects, and new capability development. He uses these skills to identify how to create more value for his clients.

A skilled teacher and facilitator, Jim is capable of leading and collaborating with diverse groups, including senior executives, cross functional teams, managers and individual contributors.

He is curious and always learning.

Specialties: Curiosity, collaboration, project management methodologies, group interaction protocols, professional learning communities, thought leadership, business alignment, process modeling, data modeling, Linux, Open Source, Six Sigma and Lean tools, team development, understanding complexity, finding deep structure, writing and talking well, figuring out what matters, stubbornness
