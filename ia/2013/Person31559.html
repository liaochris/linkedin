    • February 2012 to March 2012
    Team Members: David Bruggink

    This is an infographic video I did in After Effects to illustrate the issue of HIV/AIDS in China. Though it isn't brought up very often in the context of the global HIV/AIDS epidemic, the situation of people affected by HIV in China is serious and demands our attention. Societal stigmatization, lack of effective government intervention and access to health care, and an unwillingness to admit to and discuss the problem have made the prospect of living long-term with the virus in China very difficult.
