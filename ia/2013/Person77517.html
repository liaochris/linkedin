Kurt Hunter's Summary

My first marketing win happened at the age of 7. Tired of answering the same question – “How did your basketball game go last week?”- from the retired neighbor, I convinced her to pay me $7.00/month for a monthly update on not only my sports accomplishments but also the neighborhood kids’ accomplishments (added value). Not bad considering my monthly allowance for vacuuming the house was $2.50.

Today, I still use careful listening skills to identify opportunities in the marketplace. My ability to listen, observe, and create solutions has allowed me to co-own and sell two businesses for a combined $500,000+.

I’m passionate about creating marketing campaigns and designing customer experiences that lead to increased sales and loyal customers.

I love making new connections. Please feel free to reach out if you’re interested in discussing baseball (St. Louis Cardinals), basketball (KU Jayhawks), business development, marketing campaigns/marketing strategy, or if you like to make things happen and want to kick around ideas!

Specialties: Client Acquisition, Client Retention, Branding, Consumer Psychology, Difficult Conversations, Identifying Additional Revenue Streams, Marketing Strategy, Maximizing the Customer Experience
