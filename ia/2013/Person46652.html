Gabi Syberg-Olsen's Summary

I’m a staffing professional with 6 years of experience hiring across an array of industries. I have successfully filled Retail and Fashion roles for well-known Luxury Retailers in Toronto, Montreal, Vancouver and New York, as well as Digital Design, Marketing, UX and Front-End Web Development roles for global Advertising Agencies in both TO and NY.
As well, I have staffed various roles for Cosmetics Co's, Manufacturers, Technology Co's and other great clients.

I also provide Career Coaching, including interview prep and resume assistance to candidates from entry level to executive.

I take a holistic approach to Recruiting.
I see people as being multi-layered, and as such, that there are several things to take into account when matching them to a job or career, aside from merely looking at their skills/experience.
I depend equally on my own trusted intuition, a skill and experience assessment, as well as extensive research, to truly understand both the nuances of any given role and the unique culture of the company. I believe that this approach can help to improve the chances of hiring the right candidate for any role, leading to better retention. Yay!

Retail:

Marketing:
