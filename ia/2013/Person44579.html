Martin Ferro-Thomsen's Summary

[Please let me know why we should connect when sending a request. Thanks!]

Currently: Building Conferize to reinvent conferences for the 21st century.

I am also a Co-Founder of the leading digital publishing service Issuu, a Time.com best website, twice a SXSW finalist that today serves 3.6 billion monthly pages & 57 million monthly users. I'm no longer actively involved with Issuu but continue as a shareholder and con amore evangelist.

The past couple of years I've also done quite a few keynotes and panels for interesting conferences and events. My favorite topics includes new media & tech, entrepreneurship, community management, and (digtial) publishing.

Before Issuu, I've worked with communication, project making and -management, and brand creation on a multitude of levels. I've facilitated large-scale international projects, including everything from concept to dissemination. I have written and edited in just about any genre out there; for journalistic, academic, creative and marketing purposes. I'm also a closet organizational theory & practice geek.

In general I keep an open mind towards learning within adjacent fields of practice, and my strongest characteristic is probably this: Entering a new field of practice; researching, rapid learning - and finally doing, always striving for flawless execution.

Specialties

Entrepreneurship, leadership, social interaction design, conferences, publishing, culture and technology, branding and identity, communications and community.
