Gabriel Prero's Summary

Awake or not, I design. Though the awake stuff tends to make more sense. No matter the task at hand, be it a salsa bottle or a walking aid for Parkinson's patients, each project brings new challenges that make my job as much play as work.
My goal is to broaden my design horizons, soak in all the information, and produce better designs with every passing day.

My unique strengths: Strong technical ability, MacGyver-esque problem solving skills, thorough thinking all the way through, and presentation.

Specialties

Photoshop, Illustrator, Solidworks, Rhino, Computer rendering, Sketching, Understanding of Engineering and manufacturing, presentation, vendor relations

Gabriel Prero's Experience

Industrial Designer

Home Products International Inc.

Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Consumer Goods industry

July 2012 – Present (1 year 5 months) Greater Chicago Area

HPI is an international consumer products company specializing in the design, marketing and manufacture of quality, innovative products.

HPI has a broad product portfolio that includes storage containers, laundry management products, bath and shower organizers, hooks, hangers and home and closet organizers.

I design home storage and organization solutions for all of HPI's various product lines, under their Homz name as well as private label. My responsibilities include market research, trend and market analysis, concept generation, CAD modeling and rendering, design for manufacturing, presentation, in-house FDM prototyping and machine maintenance, concept research and analysis, and consulting through the production process.

Independent Industrial Designer

Prero Design, LLC

May 2009 – Present (4 years 7 months) Chicago

Need help with a design solution? I can help from full product development consulting from soup-to-nuts, to something as small as a round of concepts or CAD modeling. I have experience in furniture, packaging, ceramics, plastics, office supply, medical supply, juvenile products, home decor and more.

I specialize in helping "the little guys" and startups get their big idea from concept to manufacturable reality, keeping all aspects of product development in mind.

I'm always looking for what's new and different, and can't wait for the next challenge.

Industrial Designer

TricorBraun

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Packaging and Containers industry

May 2010 – November 2011 (1 year 7 months) Oakbook, Il

Lead and collaborated on custom bottle and packaging design projects for TB’s range of international clients. Process included initial sketch ideation, detailed Solidworks modeling, FDM and Objet prototyping, customer presentations, vendor relations and followup, and quality control throughout the sampling, unit cavity, and full cavity production processes. While at TB, I successfully launched over 20 custom packaging designs into production, have been awarded 4 patents, and have another 2 patents pending.
