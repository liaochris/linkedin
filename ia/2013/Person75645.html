Kimberly S. Brown's Summary

I'm currently working full-time for Active Interest Media (AIM) and I am not taking private clients, but feel free to contact me if you are looking to enter, or expand, your business in the equine or veterinary markets. My position with AIM means our team might have the resources to help you even more!

With more than 30 years of experience, I can put my thousands of global connections and decades of business expertise to work for your company, as well as bring to bear all the Active Interest Media resources for the right clients.

My award-winning equine journalism background means I know how to get your message into mainstream print and digital outlets. I have assisted the industry's top companies and start-ups with business development, marketing, sales, digital and social media, and idea generation that puts money on the bottom line.

As Global Marketing Manager of Kentucky Equine Research, in three years I led the development of the Equinews.com website that won a bronze Addy for design, swept the American Horse Publications awards for the top two digital newsletters in their first year, was recognized for award-winning content, and scripted/produced KER's award-winning corporate videos.

Prior to that my career spanned nearly 30 years at Blood-Horse Publications, where for the last 15 years I led the development of The Horse brand. The Horse magazine and website became the leading general equine health and news information sources for horse owners and veterinarians.

I currently am the Associate Publisher/Editor for the EquiManagement brand for equine veterinarians and the Stable Management brand focused on farm/stable owners and managers. We have a lot to offer in print and digitally, so contact me about your potential involvement!

Specialties

Consulting, marketing, business development, public relations, journalism, innovative thought leader, strategic planning, writing, editing, brand development, sales campaigns, project oversight, public speaking, coaching, assisting with project development and creative expansion of current projects, developing options, transition journalism from print to online, social and digital media.
