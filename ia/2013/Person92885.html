Mike Pritchard's Summary

We are Goodwill Denver – we translate the good in the local community to programs and resources that change lives. While you might know us through our Denver thrift stores, we're more than just a place for great deals – we have programs that improve our community and inspire hope, one person at a time.

Goodwill’s retail division is comprised of a network of 23 retail stores, 1 Deja Blue boutique, 3 outlet worlds and 16 donation centers throughout metro Denver and northern Colorado. Profits from these retail stores, as well as donor contributions, fund Goodwill’s highly successful programs in workforce development. We help people find the tools to success at work and in life.

In 2012, Goodwill served over 21,000 individuals in our community:
• We provide career development programs to more than 18,000 teenagers annually.
• We help more than 3,300 adults on government assistance get the training they need to get off welfare.
• We provide meaningful employment for 1,300 people throughout our community.
• We provide work opportunities for 320 disabled adults.

Specialties: I have significant experience in the following areas: development, volunteer engagement, strategic planning, managing staff, creating and managing budgets, annual campaigns, direct mail, e-mail marketing, major gifts, planned giving, endowments, website development, social media strategy, donor database administration, public relations, communications, marketing, branding, special events, and grant writing and reporting.
