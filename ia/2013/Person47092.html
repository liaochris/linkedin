Gary Tomlinson's Summary

I've been a serial entrepreneur and business owner for over 35 years. I started my career as an Operating Room Technician and Surgical Assistant in Richmond Virginia after graduating from Randolph-Macon College in 1974. In 1975 I began my first business venture as an independent sales rep that pioneered US Surgical Stapling Instruments. In 1978 I moved to Raleigh, NC and co-founded a medical home care company that pioneered infant monitoring to prevent crib death, volume ventilators in the home and oxygen concentrators. My partner and I sold the company in August of 1980. Today that company in known as Apria Healthcare. I started a second home care company in September of 1980 and sold it in September of 1981. In 1982 I founded Med Covers a manufacturer of protective covers, carrying cases and therapeutic bed surfaces for the medical industry. After 18 years I sold my shares in November of 1999.

In November of 1999 I started Tomlinson & Associates, a business consulting firm working with senior level executives. In 2007 I co-founded Women's Edge Magazine which was a publication for professional women in the greater Raleigh-Durham area.

For over 25 years I've been a regular lecturer at NC State University in the College of Management, specifically in the Entrepreneurship Education Initiative. In 2008 I began lecturing regularly at Elon University in their Business College. In 2012 I began lecturing at NC Central University, UNC (Chapel Hill) and Duke University. Sharing business education with college students has been a passion and I feel honored to have had the privilege of doing so for all these years.

Today, I'm a motivational speaker and business consultant specializing in business execution. I work with CEOs to help them establish execution management as an organizational competency. An organization that knows how to plan and execute can overcome any obstacle and reach greatness. Unfortunately, very few do!

Specialties

As a motivational speaker my favorite topics are the "Art of Telling" which is professional speaking education for professionals in everyday use; "Making Change When Change is Hard" and "Top Ten Messages from Ten Top Books."

As a strexecutionist I work with CEOs to help them manage execution down to an individual level. Knowing how to plan and execute is the most foundational capability an organization can have!
