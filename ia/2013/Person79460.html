In short, we help make you the news!

For Journalists....
Need a source over the weekend or a holiday? Our service allows you to make such a request (when everything else is closed) allowing you to receive pitches from our experts in order to meet your deadline.

Found your source? Simply close your request early.

Don't want your pitches emailed? You can even turn off the email function and view pitches online, keeping your inbox clutter-free.

Forgot to specify something in your request? Edit it, and recalculate the pitch rating automatically.

Could finding a quality source be any easier?

For Experts...
Just getting into the PR groove? PitchRate helps you connect with the media. Find the requests that fit your expertise, make pitches, and then receive the media attention you've dreamed - for free!

Connecting with the media has never been easier!
