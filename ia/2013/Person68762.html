J. Patrick Kennedy's Summary

For 10 years I've been serving communities internationally and domestically, through thoughtful community outreach and engagement that facilitates human connection points for cause based missions. I’ve served in five countries: England, Mauritania, Gambia, Bolivia, and Mexico. I have organized community and fundraising events with several international and local organizations such as: Denver's Road Home, Habitat for Humanity, Extreme Community Makeover, Global Refuge International, Invisible Children, and Love 146.

I love ideas and facilitating great ideas to fruition. I develop thoughtfull plans for program, outreach, marketing, and fundraising goals that lead to positive results. I am a creative thinker and I enjoy the creative process in problem solving and planning programs, events, and campaigns that utilize print, digital, and social media forums to engage with constituents and the community. I’m a big-picture strategic thinker and a maximizer, who looks to make every detail fit for the most efficient and productive path.
