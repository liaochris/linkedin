Eddie Godshalk's Summary

Real estate builders, developers, and investors rely on various data sets to determine where and when to buy or sell property. These data are supplemented by their personal knowledge of local conditions. Investors in these segments seek the latest information about market conditions that provide them with a competitive edge in knowing in which areas to buy and sell property.
In addition, real estate investors need an accurate predictive model of changes in market conditions that affect price.

The Solution:
Whiz Maps expands the data available to investors by integrating little-known public and private data sets that provide more detailed information about which local markets are growing or declining. These datasets provide information at the Block, Tract, or Zip Code levels on changes in income, unemployment rates, and migration, so that investors and real estate professionals can more completely assess local real estate market risks.

Whiz Maps delivers this information to subscribers in a proprietary user-defined and dynamic mapping platform.

This new service reduces risks by giving developers, builders, and investors local information in a visual, easy to understand format that has, until now not been easily accessible online.

In addition, Whiz Maps has developed a highly accurate 24-month predictive model (“Future Value Model” or “FVM”) based on these private and public data sets that is available to premium subscribers. FVM applies proven GIS (Geographic Information System), hedonic regression, and hyper-local analytics to deliver the most comprehensive base of private and public attributes ever assembled.

Utility patent filed in 2012. Envisioned and developed a real-time rendering real estate software at, with current data and information like no other.

After collecting more than four years’ worth of unique micro block data, Eddie Godshalk back-tested countless expert systems (rules) and mathematical techniques.

Specialties

While most online tools provide numbers at the County or ZIP code level, Local-Insights gets you down to the block level with neighborhood by neighborhood numbers and forecasts. And we do it using data that’s refreshed monthly – and even, in some cases, daily – while others update their demographic and economic data only yearly.
