Business Development Director

Special Counsel

Public Company; 201-500 employees; MPS; Legal Services industry

June 2010 – October 2012 (2 years 5 months) Greater Nashville Area

Chelsey Johnson is the Business Development Director for Special Counsel. In this role Chelsey coordinates with clients and potential clients to ensure that their legal staffing needs are satisfied in a timely, effective and efficient manner. She primarily works with law firm and corporate in-house legal department clients whose staffing needs include temporary placement of legal secretaries, paralegals, attorneys, and litigation support. In addition, she also helps those clients who seek experienced, licensed attorneys for large scale document review projects and direct hire paralegals, legal support staff and litigation support professionals.

Her unique combination of both legal and marketing experience makes her an excellent resource for Special Counsel’s clients. Chelsey is a graduate of Hamline University School of Law located in St Paul, MN and received her undergraduate degree in public relations and marketing from Oklahoma State University. Prior to attending law school she served as the Marketing and Events Coordinator for the Minnesota Army National Guard where she supported statewide recruiting and retention efforts. Chelsey’s collective experience allows her to understand and effectively fulfill staffing needs while building long-term relationships with Special Counsel’s clients.

Chelsey is licensed to practice law in the state of Minnesota and is an active participant in the legal community. She also sits on the board of directors for Meredith’s Miracles Colon Cancer Foundation; a national foundation that provides grants to young adults battling colon cancer.
