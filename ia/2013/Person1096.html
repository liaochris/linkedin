Keith B. Jones' Summary

Keith B. Jones is the Managing Principal of Ace Parking. Keith has led the corporate strategy and operations for the company since 2004, including Ace Parking’s expansion and emergence as the nation’s premier parking company. In his role, Keith is responsible for the strategic and creative operational vision of the company, along with ensuring the company’s growth and profitability and is deeply involved with the company’s new business initiatives. Keith has been involved with Ace Parking from a very young age and has worked countless hours at Ace Parking’s many locations. His hands-on approach is proving to be a valued asset at all levels of Ace Parking’s organization. Keith adopted the strong work ethic from his father, Scott, and his grandfather - Ace Parking’s founder Evan V. Jones, and is committed to preserving and improving his family’s business. Keith has instilled his father’s values of quality, customer focus, teamwork and ethics into the Company.

Keith believes strongly in giving back to his community and when he is not engaged in the family business, he invests his time in charitable work and serving in various distinguished local organizations. Keith serves as the Chairman of the Downtown San Diego Partnership, on the Executive Committee & Board of the San Diego Regional Chamber of Commerce, on the Board of Directors of St. Vincent de Paul Village in San Diego, and as a Regional Executive Committee member of GenNext (an exclusive organization of successful business professionals whose mission is to tackle tough issues facing future generations). Keith is also a member of Young Presidents Organization (YPO), is the co-founder of the Herb Klein Leadership Roundtable, and serves on the Board of Directors for the San Diego Police Foundation. Keith holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Philosophy and a Minor in Business from New York University. Keith and his wife, Megan, reside in La Jolla, California.
