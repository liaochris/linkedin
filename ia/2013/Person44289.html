Frank DeBlasi's Summary

I am often asked, "You must love your career." My usual reply to that is, "I like to have fun and take on challenges." That's just how I view my career, fun and challenging. I have a passion for what I do, and enjoy doing it.

For over 15 years I have been a part of the web and digital media world, and have had my work featured in the media nationwide.

In 2008, in the midst of a down economy, I cofounded and created www.hoopladoopla.com, one of the best online money-saving sites on the web.

A team player both in and out of the workplace, I see when good minds come together, great things happen.
