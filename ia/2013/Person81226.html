Lindsey Haas' Summary

I'm a Multi-Million Dollar producing Realtor in the Northwest Atlanta Metro area. I work with buyers, sellers and many investors for all their real estate needs, and service Cobb, Cherokee, Paulding, Forsyth, Bartow and N Fulton Counties.

I also have personal experience in flipping homes. I know what is a good investment for flipping or purchasing for a primary residence... and what is not.

I look out for the interest of every client I work with as if it were my own decision. This has proven to be a successful business strategy for me, as I receive tons of referrals from past clients, and my clients always stay in touch.

I can assist anyone all over the nation in their real estate needs as well. My mother was a real estate agent and broker for 15 years and I grew up in the business. I know what it takes to do well and my strongest attribute is my negotiation skills. I have developed lifelong friends through real estate and enjoy nothing more than helping people with finding a home.

Call or email me for some advice as I would be happy to help! Buying real estate is the largest financial decision that any of us make. Be sure you have the right advice!!
