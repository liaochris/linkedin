Kevin Chan's Summary

I picked up photography in 2010 when I purchased my first SLR camera with the help of a friend, and have enjoyed it since then. If it weren't for my friend, I would not have discovered my passion for photography. I would like to help those who may be interested in photography but do not know where to start, therefore my purpose is to share with you my experience. I will also share the few tricks I learned in the past few years, and show off my latest photos on a weekly basis and explain how I take the shots so every can emulate. What sets me apart from the other photographers online is that, as a "novice," I know how someone without formal training may find photography daunting, and I would provide answers in a way that is easier for non-photographers to understand should they have any questions regarding photography. If you would like to, you may visit my website to view the photos or the blog posts that I posted in the past.

I am also a full time employee at Arvato Digital Services in the capacity of Quality Assurance analyst. I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Nevada, Reno and am working on my MBA degree at the same school. Aside from English, I also speak fluent Manderin Chinese.
