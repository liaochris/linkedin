Patty Herrera's Summary

I am a Fayetteville native and a military brat. I am very familiar with our market and trials and tribulations involved with a military move. I am a PROUD supporter of our troops and it is my goal to provide a smooth transition for our military families!
I have a team called the Patty Herrera Team, consisting of 4 licensed Brokers who specialize in residential home sales, new construction home sales, and Relocation. We pride ourselves in providing excellent customer service, a competitive edge on the newest technology, and a strong web presence. We have a reputation of being dedicated, thorough, hardworking and honest. We give the good old fashioned customer service and care that our buyers and sellers deserve. Our main goal is to give you the information needed and the guidance that will aide you in making a good decision when selling or buying a home. We will guide you through a seamless transaction from start to finish and you will see that the home buying or selling process is easy when you hire the right team!
