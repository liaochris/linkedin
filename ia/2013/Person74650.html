Kerry Gatlin's Summary

I graduated college from East Tennessee State University in May 2008 with a degree in graphic design and graduated Tennessee Real Estate Education System in June 2008 with my Affiliate Broker license. I have been working as a full-time Realtor since July 2008, but have been around the Real Estate business most of my life as my mother, Shirley Davidson, Principal Broker/Owner of Exit Realty Mountains and Lakes, has been marketing and selling Real Estate for nearly 20 years. I am able to use my skills as a graphic designer to better market the properties I have listed.

Specialties

I specialize in working with First Time Buyers and Young Professionals in Jefferson County, TN. Being a young homeowner myself helps me relate to my clients and better understand your needs. I purchased my first home in March 2009, received the $8,000.00 First Time Buyer Tax Credit and want to help others in doing so. I think it is important to understand all the tools that are available when purchasing a home. My goal is to make all of that information available to you.
