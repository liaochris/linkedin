Julia Bonem's Summary

Nonprofit expert with years of hands-on, in-depth experience--14+ years as a fundraiser and 10+ years as a retained recruiter of nonprofit professionals for premier organizations.

Worked for top higher education institutions--raising millions of dollars in restricted and unrestricted support for MIT, Johns Hopkins-SAIS, Barnard College, and the University of Pennsylvania.

Currently, I am: (1) Senior Counsel at Graham-Pelton Consulting, Inc; (2) an interim professional in the major gifts, parents fund, and annual giving programs at Barnard; and (3) a coach helping professionals break into nonprofits and those already in the field advance in their careers.

I am skilled at:

--Cultivation, solicitation, and stewardship of prospects;
--Managing high-level volunteers;
--Crafting written proposals and solicitations;
--Strategic planning;
--Coaching, resume, and LinkedIn strategies;
--Developing new business.
