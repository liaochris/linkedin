Jenni Proctor's Summary

Jenni Proctor is a Career Coach, with special interest in assisting older workers. This includes the usual career management support including Leadership Development, but also expands to helping older workers create a Laptop Lifestyle as an alternative to a traditional retirement.

Jenni assists career decision making using a holistic approach which recognizes that your career is an important part of your life but must be congruent with your lifestyle goals.

Her work in career development reflects her deep belief that every person brings a unique genius to their work; a genius that is often only revealed when they are doing work they love in an environment that allows them to shine. This has lead to a deep interest in empowering individuals to embrace their personal leadership and older workers to recognize the special contributions they can make.

We specialize in services for both INDIVIDUALS and ORGANISATIONS.
- Career management strategies for career change and transitions
- Career and lifestyle business options for older workers.
- Leadership development for future transformational leaders
- Talent management services (in conjunction with Taylor Protocols)

For each INDIVIDUAL CLIENT we focus on providing these four benefits:
- Clarity of career direction and personal understanding
- Recognition of your unique package and brand that defines you in your career
- Effective strategies to achieve your goals, including career management strategies, leadership strategies and online business strategies
- Motivation and confidence to take action

For ORGANISATIONS we focus on providing:
- Empowering coaching for your executives, future leaders and other staff
- Engaging workshops on career management topics
- Illuminating workshops for older workers about their options for retirement lifestyle planning
- Keynote presentations
- Recruitment and talent management solutions that will change the way your business views your people.

Jenni Proctor's Publications

  • New Jobs for Older Workers: A practical guide to rewarding career change

    • Clarity Connections Pty Ltd
    • October 12, 2012
    Authors: Jenni Proctor

    Many older workers fear making a career change, or having a career change thrust upon them. They worry that they are too old to be marketable; that their experience and skills won't be valued; that they don't know how to get a job in this digital world.

    "New Jobs for Older Workers: A Practical Guide to Rewarding Career Change" guides the reader through the process of determining the best career direction for them to take, then provides practical contemporary strategies to get the job they want. It is supported by a free workbook, available online, and is based on years of professional experience assisting clients to make successful and rewarding career changes.
