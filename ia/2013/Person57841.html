Jim Calhoun's Summary

Jim Calhoun helps his clients resolve disputes and restore relationships in families, in the work place, in churches and in the community.

Particular emphasis is place on building skills to effectively manage the under lying causes of conflict such as stress, anger, anxiety, guilt and depression.

Clients have included high profile pastors, professional athletes, national and local law enforcement, physicians and corporate executives.

Specialties

* High conflict people
* Anger management coaching for executives and professionals
