Gary Michels' Summary

Gary Michels, leading motivational expert, has impacted thousands worldwide through his remarkable techniques for balancing career success and thriving personal lives. With more than 25 years of success in selling, managing, and motivating, Gary’s proven insights will inspire a brand new level of personal and career achievement!

Gary began successfully selling young for a well-known reference book distributor. Pushing himself relentlessly, he finished in the top 10 from more than 3,000 students earning over $100,000 in three short summers! Gary’s phenomenal career success now spans a breadth of experience, from retail sales to network marketing to real estate. Gary spent 19 successful years as a sales rep and manager for a national fundraising company, finishing #1 in sales for 9 of his last 10 years, ahead of over 350 sales representatives as well as leading some of the top teams in the country.

Nearly 1 million people have been motivated to achieve their highest potential in their work & personal life from Gary's seminars, leadership and training. Gary has spent multiple hours observing and interviewing sales pro's in a variety of industries such as financial services, advertising, technology, electronics, pharmaceutical, and many others, giving him amazing creative techniques that he shares with his audiences.

Gary writes frequently for various magazines on sales and leadership excellence and is the author of Gettin’ In and Gettin’ Out, How to Get In Every Prospect’s Door and Get Out with a Sale and Turn It Up A Notch Weekly.

Gary currently serves as one of the Co Founders of the motivational sales training company Southwestern Consulting. He conducts public seminars and sales conferences across the country, as well as individual sales training, sales coaching, and consulting.

http://speakers.southwesternconsulting.com/Gary-Michels.aspx

Specialties: Positive Mental Attitude, Self Motivation, Process, Habits, Self Management, Goal Setting, Life Planning.
