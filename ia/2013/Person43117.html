Estia Zafeiraki's Summary

By defining the ecology of sustainability and injecting design thinking into places it never existed before, unmet human needs are met, businesses build more value, and society’s quality of life is improved as a whole.

Discover, define, design and deliver messages and products by uncovering innovation opportunities and take new leaps. Direct the problem solving process through service design methodologies, concept visualization, marketing strategies and design implementation for international to local businesses including banks, multi-national food corporations, attorneys, financial services, investment groups, community associations, and start ups.

Estia Zafeiraki's Experience

Communications Designer & Brand Strategist

Wholesome Creations

2010 – 2011 (1 year) New York, NY

Wholesome Creations is a family oriented business passionate about delivering 100% natural, vegan and gluten-free products. Dedicated to sharing highly nutritious dietary alternatives, their products are locally sourced and manufactured in an allergen free facility.

• Designed the brand vision, brand naming, marketing strategy and product definition for a food start-up that successfully landed the product in Whole Foods Market

• Created the package design with an eye catching visual system and recognizable hand drawn illustrations allowing consumers to quickly identify the products’ natural ingredients

Research Scholar & Communications Consultant

Pratt Center for Sustainable Design Studies and Research

2009 – 2010 (1 year) Brooklyn, NY

In 2008 Pratt Institute was awarded the FIPSE Grant to support innovative projects and research that respond to issues of global significance around sustainable urban development. The Center for Sustainable Design Studies (CSDS) was established to create an incubator for thought leadership, and provide accessible resources to students, faculty and alumni.

• Developed the strategic vision for CSDS to establish itself as a collaborative hub for sustainable design education

• Designed a distinct visual identity system, unifying all internal and external communications from print, web, and signage, to exhibitions, workshops and presentations

• Created a life cycle assessment tool that evaluates material properties and measures their human-environmental impact through a visual rating system

• In two weeks, worked with a small team to transform a storage unit into a physical collaborative space, successfully launching CSDS’s inaugural opening at Green Week 2009

Corporate Identity Designer

The Karka Group

2005 – 2010 (5 years) New York, NY

The KARKA Group is a financial services company that develops personalized investment strategies for private and non-institutional investors by identifying unseen risks in the marketplace and leveraging partnerships in real estate, law and insurance.

• In 2005 created, designed and managed a new corporate identity for Rahmanan & Asher, that represented the partner’s union for four years

• Redesigned the business identity in 2009 by integrating two companies under one visual system to retain client base and brand recognition

• Discovered a new business model by visually mapping a comprehensive diagrammatic explanation of services delivering a clear message with a solid communications strategy
