Alberto D'Ottavi's Summary

I work at the intersection of technology, Internet and media (esp. journalism), studying its social and cultural impact. Having a long-time experience of how things work in digital environments - eg social networks, mobile apps, etc. - I help established companies to move in the digital innovation space or new initiatives to grow creating digital projects, products and/or digital strategies.

I have a focus on Web 2.0 and Social Media since 2006. In 2010 Edelman included me among the Top 1.000 Twitter Tech Influencers worldwide (http://infoservi.it/patpv).

I help foster the idea of a new and open entrepreneurship participating in a number of initiatives for startups, and I'm also building one: http://blomming.com, among the first european companies working on Social Commerce. We have been included in the "Cool Vendor in E-Commerce 2012" report by Gartner, Inc., and won the Premio Nazionale per l'Innovazione in June 2012.

Specialties:
- Visioning, trends scouting
- Concept design and launch of new digital ventures
- Digital strategy
- Management

Alberto D'Ottavi's Experience

Co-founder, Chief Marketing Officer

Blomming

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

September 2009 – Present (4 years 1 month)

Blomming is a Social Commerce solution that allows people and businesses to sell on their blogs, websites, Facebook Pages and smartphones.

I co-founded the company, working on the original idea with the founder and developing the brand, marketing and communication strategy, that has been heavily based on digital tools.
During the bootstrap we had almost no budget, so we developed a strong presence on Social Media: blogs, Facebook, Twitter and other social networks.
Then, I worked on all the customer development issues using different means (digital, corporate communication, media PR, e-mail marketing, content and community, Social Media, etc).
In the actual third phase, after the early stage financing, we are working on optimizing all the metrics measurements (SMM, SEO, others) for accelerating the growth on international markets.
