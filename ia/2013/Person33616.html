Deborah Phillips' Summary

I just built a website for my own company, Deb Phillips Web Design. I published it on July 28, 2011. I needed my own website, since I have grown over the last 10 years. I still display my websites on the ANS website.

I have been affiliated with ANS Business Forms & Systems, since 1986. I have done graphic/web design for both ANS & their customers. I am still a sub contractor with ANS.

I am now affiliated with Odyssey Management Group, as their Executive Secretary/Web Designer. I have 5 or more slideshows on the Odyssey Website. The most recent slideshow is the installation of a 20kW wind turbine that we installed in Palmerton PA. I take all of the photos, edit them, and prepare the slideshows.

I have about 25 published websites.

Specialties

Search Engine Optimization (SEO). I am able to get my clients websites in both GOOGLE & Yahoo's top pages. I get this page ranking by building Meta Tags & checking them in a few online websites, which rate the Meta Tags, & allow me to edit the tags to get them at 85% & above, which helps my client’s website rankings. I also do photo editing with Photoshop, which allows me to optimize the images for quick download time. I can also restore old photos that are cracked ripped, or damaged.
