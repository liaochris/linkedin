Michael Johnson's Summary

Michael Johnson is the Founder and Owner of Waterstone Realty, a premier real estate solutions company in Central Florida. Even before Waterstone Realty's creation, we have been helping homeowners and working with communities in every location we work in. Through our extensive network of buyers, resources, years of experience, education, knowledge, and expertise, we have been able to assist homeowners in a large variety of situations to create a Win-Win while still creating a steady return for our investors.

Services include procuring various forms of real estate opportunities. Our investment portfolio includes notes, properties purchased at auctions, equity deals, and distressed retail properties from $10,000 to Million. Waterstone Realty also lends out privately to local expert investor borrowers. We provide accurate timely underwriting on equity based deals and service the deals during their income production. We work closely with our investors to match their lending criteria to appropriate ventures that produce a predetermined passive annual return of 7-12% from 3 months to 5 years. More aggressive Lenders have seen much higher yields. We do not Loan to Owner Occupants, but we do have a plan designed for Lease-to Own for the correct tenants.

Our Waterstone Realty Investor transactions are each secured by specific individual properties. No funds are ever sent to us directly. Closings are organized by our attorneys. Properties are evaluated and insured. We are able to fractionalize notes to ensure our investors funds constantly perform. These are low risk and high performing reliable returns. There are numerous opportunities we can offer any investor TODAY.

If you are interested in Private Lending or Borrowing, please visit http://www.Waterstone-Realty.com/ or email us at info@ Waterstone-Realty.com.

Specialties

Evaluation, acquisition, stabilization, and income production of distressed REO properties, short sales, and private equity sales. Lending of Private funds via asset based lending.
