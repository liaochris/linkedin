Giuseppe Vinci's Summary

Since I started working in teams at a very young age, I learned to constantly relate and perform well in many very high pressure situations and understand very well the importance of delivering good results at the right time.
I understand hard work pays back and in my life I was able to experience the truth of this statement especially since volunteering at several different companies paid me back with experiences that I evaluate as priceless.

I also have been lucky enough to participate in several volleyball competitions around the world as a coach and data analyst. These experiences served me well in order to develop a great understanding of human resources and of techniques and theories that can help teams and individuals to work better together and achieve important goals while under extreme pressure. Also, having traveled to over 30 different countries helped me become even more self-motivated and to grow a strong desire to keep innovating the processes I am part of.

When working in groups I always have a positive attitude and I am easy to work with while still being assertive and straight forward when needed.
My experience dealing with big amount of data and evaluating performances allows me to be comfortable when looking through numbers trying to find trends and relevant information.

I enjoy time with friends, conversations, traveling and brainstorming about work and life with people from different cultural and professional backgrounds.

VolleyMetrics already helps elite volleyball programs around the world achieving their goals and changing the way they approach training and and the way the think of the game of volleyball.
VolleyMetrics keeps thriving to become the most important tool for volleyball coaches to make better choices when it goes down to data analysis.
