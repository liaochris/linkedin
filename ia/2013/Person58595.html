Jan Siderius' Summary

Negometrix is highly specialized in (strategic) Purchasing software, tools, intelligence and consultancy. In 2000, together with 2 other senior industry executives, I launched a unique and powerful internet-based concept (backed by US Internet Capital Group) for hospital purchasing called eumediX. In 2001, we renamed it to Negometrix reflecting our growth outside healthcare; Serving private company customers such as KLM, Reed Business Information, HBG, Imtech, Martinair, and HEMA in addition to Public Institutions such as the Ministries of Finance, Defense, and Internal Affairs, all Universities, the largest Dutch cities, and semi-public Housing groups. The majority of all Dutch hospitals have been relying on our strategic purchasing solution to intelligently and efficiently contract suppliers. The Negometrix organization and staff has been growing in parallel with our growth. While substantial progress has been made, I believe the best is yet to come!

Previously, I held senior management positions in the Hospital Supply Industry for Fresenius and Baxter. My field of study (and professional interest) is bringing efficiency in Healthcare delivery combining a University Degree in Medicine (Groningen, NL) with Management (MIT Sloan US).

Specialties

electronic purchasing, sourcing, (reverse) auctioning, tendering, contracting, multi-criteria decision making, group-buying, internet
