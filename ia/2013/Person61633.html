Jennifer Kane's Summary

I am principal of Kane Consulting (www.kaneconsulting.biz) and a communications strategist with more than 15 years of experience working with B2B and B2C companies and professional associations.

In addition to consulting, I do public speaking, teaching, training, blogging and radio interviews about topics related to social media, content marketing and digital communications. Additionally, I have served as adjunct faculty for Minneapolis College of Art and Design and Concordia University, St. Paul, MN.

I am a two time IABC Bronze Quill award-winner for strategic planning, a frequent contributor to the Next Stage Business Radio Network, and host a popular business book club in Minneapolis that is free and open to the public.

Specialties

Social media marketing | digital communications | strategic consulting | social media content creation and curation | digital reputation management | online community engagement | social media training and presenting
