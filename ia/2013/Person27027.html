Corey Olin's Summary

Having advanced 110+ brands and satisfied Clients, Corey Olin serves more than 10 years experience in creative direction, brand strategy, digital and marketing design, editorial print production, publishing and illustration.

Corey believes that every each new project, big or small, should hail an improvement upon the last. More than aiming to please, Olin aims to exceed your brand.

Corey Olin is an awarded and published artist whose visual styles are adaptable to target any audience, taking full advantage of tested marketing trends. Olin is always on the lookout for efficient, cost-saving routes without sacrificing esteemed brand value. Articulate, friendly, fast and professional, Olin is always on the lookout for newly rewarding collaborations.

Client include VANS, Heineken, Rockefeller Center, BFW Publishing. His experience extends to fashion labels, public figures, publications, foodies, gamers, retailers, eco and educational businesses.
