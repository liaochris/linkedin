Michelle Leivan's Summary

Artist, Author and Entrepreneur, Michelle Leivan was educated at Fort hays State University. She has been distinguished as the featured visual artist at the Kansas River Valley Art Fair, Topeka Jazz Festival, and the Aaron Douglas Art Fair, and has been nominated for the Kansas Governor’s Art Award in the visual arts category. Her work has been selected to hang in the Kansas State Capital building and in the U.S Embassy Residence of the Ambassador in Asunción, Paraguay.

Michelle’s work has been featured in solo shows and exhibits at the Manhattan Arts Center, Arts Council of Topeka and Topeka Performing Arts Center. She also has exhibited in several juried internet competitions and in Soho, New York. Her work is represented in the Ellis County Historical Archives, State of the Art | Kansas a fine art book and in several corporate and private collections.

With a passion for helping her fellow artists Michelle has established ArtPrintExpress.com which offers fine art giclée prints to fellow artists and photographers. As well as ArtMarketingOnline.com blog where she draws from more than 20 years of experience in marketing and gallery management, which has led to writing “Art Marketing Online” which shares the basic concepts behind internet tools that makes marketing art easier for the artist. Michelle has also shared her creative passion with an entrepreneurial fundraising concept for the non-profit organization ARTSConnect Topeka where she is sharing the profit of her conception the “Stroke of Genius Game” which is a play at home creative drawing game available to order online and an android application for download.

Stroke of Genius Game (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Myself Only; Entertainment industry

2009 – Present (4 years) Topeka, Kansas Area

Stroke of Genius is a creative drawing card game that challenges you to take a single drawn Stroke and transform it into an imaginative picture that reflects the Spark Word, please the Juror and collect the Spark Word cards to win the game.

In collaboration with D2Coding and Arts Connect Topeka, Stroke of Genius online incorporates the hard copy of the game as a social media interactive game utilizing Facebook and Flash. This version is currently in development an planned full implementation is August 2010.

This game is the result of the creative work of Michelle Leivan. She has conceived, designed, illustrated, published and hand assembled the game and packaging. She also reserves the creative copyright for this game.
