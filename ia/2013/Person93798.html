Mignonne Pollard's Summary

My career objective is to champion programs and policies that increase cultural competencies and promote intercultural awareness in organizations.

My work experience includes:
Lecturer, multicultural education strategies; Senior Equity Specialist providing training in areas of cultural understanding: gender equity (Title IX), disability rights, considerations when working with English Language Learners. Additionally, I was a researcher for television production company. I've worked with city, state, and federal government agencies as well as several nonprofit organizations in the U.S. and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) abroad.

Specialties

Training in cultural awareness and understanding, cross-cultural travel preparation and training, international exchanges, program management, volunteer development, understanding issues around risk management and youth programming.
