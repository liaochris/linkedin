WHY YOU SHOULD HIRE ANDREW POLIN AS YOUR CONTENT MARKETER?
I am a veteran journalist with extensive writing and editing experience. Since I ran media companies, I bring an understanding to the challenges that businesses face. I have a proven track record in marketing, both traditional and online. I work with entrepreneurs to align their business goals with their marketing campaigns. I guide them to develop a marketing strategy that can offer a mix of traditional advertising, as well as social media strategies that include Facebook, LinkedIn and other platforms. The marketing strategy will emphasize content that will drive traffic to your website and increase revenue. Marketing platforms come and go but the one constant is the need for awesome content. My clients have included business organizations, entrepreneurs and small businesses.

WHAT DO I DO?
I work with business people and professionals to market their services online in a manner which supports their companies’ goals and achieves results.

MISSION:
I founded E-Marketers of Sarasota-Bradenton because I saw business people and professionals who needed to learn how to market online to stay competitive. My goal is to help all people who need guidance with their online and/or traditional marketing needs.

WHAT IS IN IT FOR YOU?
1. Work with a marketing professional who comprehends the entire spectrum of your business, including budget constraints and marketing needs.
2. Work with a marketing professional who has 30 years of proven track record of creating exciting and interesting content.
3. Work with a marketing professional who will show you how to sustain your marketing and online strategies beyond the roll-out.
