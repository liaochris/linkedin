Jeremy Stunt's Summary

At CFT Asia we help businesses perform at higher levels. If you want the people in your company to enhance their leadership, communication and productivity, we can help.

We use robust methodologies to support transformational change so people are more resourceful and resilient in the face of a constant flow of challenges. Our work is grounded in ontological coaching theory and solid business leadership experience.

Our guiding principles are:
• Developing authentic leadership that is grounded in a genuine respect for others
• Precise business and communication interactions as key to organisational productivity
• Recognising that people are a complex mix of thoughts, feelings and actions
• Equipping people to be adept at advancing their own learning and development

Jeremy is an executive coach and training facilitator who helps executives to be resourceful and resilient in a world of change and uncertainty. He combines a unique blend of ontological coaching and neuroscience with 20 years of personal leadership experience in demanding settings.

Jeremy is an ontological coach working with deep levels of learning to produce major shifts in perception and behaviour. He particularly enjoys delivering transformational change over extended periods where outcomes are not always easy to predict.

Jeremy has facilitated 200 courses in management and leadership effectiveness to 3,000 delegates in Hong Kong, China, Singapore, Japan, India, Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, UAE and Saudi Arabia. He grounds his work in neuroscience to provide additional rigour especially for emotional regulation, emotional intelligence or mindfulness training. Jeremy's leadership experience includes having held regional COO and CFO roles in periods of great change. Based in Hong Kong, he has lived in Asia since 1997.

Specialties: executive coaching, personal coaching, management and leadership training, PRISM Brain Mapping, Everything DiSC, in-person and virtual-instructor-led training.

Jeremy Stunt's Education

Newfield Institute

Ontological Coaching

2008 – 2010

This Diploma has been described as the most in-depth and comprehensive coach-training program currently available in the Asia Pacific region. Graduating from the program fulfils the coach training component for Master Certified Coach accreditation with the International Coach Federation. Studied under Alan Sieler, who is recognised as the world’s leading authority on Ontological Coaching.

The Diploma is conducted over an 18 month period with frequent face-to-face learning supplemented by a very comprehensive programme including:
• A total of 29 intensive and experiential workshop days with programme leaders.
• Participation in study groups conducted on a fortnightly basis.
• Individual assignment work.
• Participation in at least 50 supervised coaching conversations throughout the programme.

Middlesex University

Neuroscience of Leadership

2010 – 2011

The goal of this course is for the student to gain insight into the observable aspects of neuroscience (behavior, memory, emotions), thus enabling them to modulate their interventions in the workplace to improve leadership, learning and change initiatives, and ultimately teach NeuroLeadership principles to others.

The course is a 12 month program with 4 modules:

1: Decision Making and Problem Solving
2: Emotional Regulation
3: Collaborating with Others
4: Facilitating Change

The University of Hong Kong

Executive Diploma, Corporate Coaching

2008 – 2008

This was an intensive course of 120 hours of classroom teaching and practical work plus additional written and practical assignments. The diploma was led by two experienced professional coaches and provides a superb, comprehensive grounding in traditional coaching methodologies to complement professional experience as a manager and leader.
