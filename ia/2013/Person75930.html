King Pui Leong's Summary

Since 2007, King Pui has immersed himself in digital B2B media through his work in Vance Publishing. He has supercharged prominent brands including Modern Salon, Cattle Network, The Packer, Dairy Herd, and Woodworking Network. King is passionate about leadership, video, and equipping others to tell stories. Today he is product manager for all things video in Vance Publishing. He earned a bachelor's degree from the University of Central Missouri in 2007.
