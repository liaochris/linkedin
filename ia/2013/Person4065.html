Alexis Brion's Summary

I worked for a leading European online shopping portal, for business software consultancies and in the research area. I worked in South America and Germany; in 2009 co-founded the Munich chapter of the IxDA, the Interaction Design Association. Since 2011 I manage the IxDA Europe Leader Network.

In June 2011 I joined the amiando GmbH team in Munich.

Specialties: User experience, interaction design, innovation, websites, mobile, prototypes, models, human computer interaction, usability, Adobe, Illustrator, Photoshop.
