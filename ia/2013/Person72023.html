Karen Swinger, MBA's Summary

People ask me all the time, 'What do you do?' I help people use businesses to solve social issues. My clients (usually ministry leaders, business owners and entrepreneurs) are interested in finding innovative ways to improve peoples lives, and make a profit doing it.

Whatever your core business/program focus is, I work with you to use the platform of your business to create real social value - connecting supply with demand - in a way that benefits your business too. Whether the cause be education, health care, global development, hunger, employment, or affordable housing, I guide you to own an outcome (a change in behavior, condition, or status that results from a social intervention or strategy) instead of an issue (a broad, big problem) and make an impact in your sphere of influence.

I’ve worn many hats in my career – business manager of two multi-million dollar corporations, executive coordinator, office administrator, project manager, compensation and benefits analyst, author and Realtor®. I've been an international missionary having traveled to Ghana, West Africa organizing free health clinics and construction projects. As a result, I have an ability to grasp vision, manage minute details, navigate complex challenges, and execute BIG ideas. I'm also a wildly ambitious, highly optimistic, creative thinker who is inspired by the uniqueness of individual projects.

If your business is not doing the type of work that is inherent to who you are, or if you have a vision for 'more' but you don't have a plan to get you there, I can help position you for the growth you desire. Contact me... I'd love to hear from you!

Have an amazing day!

We help you come up with fresh, innovative strategies that are specifically designed to create business value through social change.
