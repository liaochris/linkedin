Jim Davidson's Experience

Founder & CEO

UpTyme Solutions Group

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Networking industry

May 2009 – Present (4 years 8 months) Bluffton / Hilton Head Island / Savannah

UpTyme represents the SE US's newest and most advanced commercial Data and Business Continuity Center facility. The Data Center is a Tier-IV class facility and is SSAE-16 (replaces SAS-70), ISO2700, and Fed RAMP certified. Located at the crossroads of I-26 and I-85 in the SC upstate, the data center is over 900 feet above sea level and 275 miles inland from the Savannah, GA

UpTyme is a privately-held firm with local decision making, supported by a large multinational company with significant financial, human, and technical resources. Data centers offer clients a way to expand their IT footprint without the capital expense associated with building a facility. Customers utilize our facility for applications hosting, server colocation, cloud computing and virtualization, terminal server solutions, and hot / warm / cold site recovery and business continuity.

The facility not only offers Tier-IV class physical-plant resources, but a menu of specialized services that allow businesses to increase profitability, gain a competitive advantage, or mitigate risk. In our world, we are "home" to some (or all) of our client's most critical IT and to their most valuable business asset - their data.

In 2012, we entered into an alliance with 6Fusion to become an iNode on their global network and "home" to clients utilizing their public cloud offerings. With 6Fusion's metering technology, clients have impacted their cost of moving to / from the cloud significantly. In fact, VMWare has announced support for 6Fusion’s “kWAC” unit of measure ... legitimizing it overnight.

As costs plummet, users are now paying for ONLY those resources (kWAC’s) they use - the “electric utility” model has been realized.
