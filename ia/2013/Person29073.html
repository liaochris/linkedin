Dana Newman's Summary

I'm an independent literary agent representing authors of practical and narrative non-fiction books.

I'm also a transactional attorney with substantial in-house legal experience at companies involved in entertainment and communications technologies. I focus on business and legal advising, intellectual property (copyrights and trademarks), technology and literary law, and corporate law (formation and business transactions).

I've negotiated and drafted complex, sophisticated contracts involving technology licensing, joint ventures, development, and publication, as well as asset purchases and real estate purchase agreements and leases. I've also managed an extensive intellectual property portfolio, and handled the prosecution of domestic and international trademarks, software licensing, domain name enforcement, and copyright registrations.

Specialties

Contract drafting, review and negotiation; technology transactions (licensing, development, distribution); trademarks and copyrights; publishing law and literary agent representation; purchase and sale of businesses, formation, and start-ups; writing.
