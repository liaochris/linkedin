Frances Palaschuk's Summary

Frances is an innovative, strategic thinker who has a passion for marketing businesses online. Specializing in digital marketing management, content creation, SEO (Search Engine Optimization), and web analytics. With close to 20 years of customer focused, results driven business experience Frances knows how to acquire and treat customers like Rockstars!

Offering both an extensive technical perspective of programming, web development and databases to a deep understanding of marketing websites online through search engines and other web 2.0 strategies, her goal is to work in an environment that complements her skills, education and passion for online marketing.

"I have always had a passion for technology, so it was natural for me to adapt so well to the use of social media to market businesses online.”

Plus, the progression to become a programmer analyst/web developer complements her previous experience and education in search engine optimization and online marketing.

"Today, I am able to setup an entire digital marketing strategy. I have become an avid fan of everything Google. I use Google Apps, Google Webmaster Tools, Google Analytics, Google+ – and I know how each application plays into one another for maximum results."

"I remember the first blog post I ever wrote - it was as scary for me as public speaking is for most people. I was really nervous. Thankfully, it all worked out for me - I love writing."

"My audience is a mixture of people from online business owners, those looking to learn how to start a blog, those wanting to learn how to use WordPress, to some of my current and potential clients."

I started a fun blog about blogging. You can join me there at http://thebloglaunch.com.

"My passion for technology has enabled me to focus on what I love doing – developing digital marketing strategies and hanging out in all the cool social media spots and of course, keeping up with everything Google!"
