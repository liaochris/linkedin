Brian Milne's Summary

I’ve been working online since the mid-90s, but also spent a decade in the newspaper business as a senior writer and web editor for McClatchy Newspapers.

Along with the professional writing background, and that daily newspaper deadline experience, I've developed content, blogs, SEO, social media, email and other online marketing campaigns for four Top 1,000 websites – About.com, Examiner.com, Experts-Exchange.com and HP.com. More recently, I was Marketing Manager at Shopatron, a global eCommerce company.

I used all of those above experiences to start Meadows Interactive, which provides web design and marketing to both local and national clients.

Along with the online marketing background, I am a WorkTraits certified trainer and reseller if you are interested in improving business communication at your office.

I've also founded popular online communities such as BallHyped.com and have had my web work showcased on ProBlogger.net, SEOMoz.org, SI.com, NYTimes.com, Philly.com and the Yahoo! homepage.

Along with writing more than 5,000+ blogs and online articles, I've written and edited numerous books, including a pair of outdoors guides, Top Trails: California Central Coast and Fishing Central California, along with The Best Sports Blogs of 2010 and 2012 books.

Specialties: Writing, online marketing (including social media, email, content and affiliate marketing), content development, Drupal and WordPress CMS development, Marketo marketing automation, Constant Contact and MailChimp email design/management, SalesForce/Sugar CRM integration/management, Magento eCommerce, SaaS, SEO and Android/Apple mobile development.

Brian Milne's Publications

  • Top Trails: California Central Coast

    • Wilderness Press
    • June 3, 2008
    Authors: Brian Milne

    Halfway between San Francisco and Los Angeles, California’s Central Coast is a treasure of beaches, poppy-dotted meadows, and countless lakes and streams. This region offers a nearby destination for adventurers coming from both Northern and Southern California. Outdoor writer and lifelong Central Coast native Brian Milne has selected his favorite 46 trips for hiking, running, horseback riding, and mountain biking. From easy beach strolls to inland treks, trails range from a half mile to 18 miles, covering the mainland and the Channel Islands. At-a-glance information shows which trips have the best fishing, bird-watching, camping, running, and other activities.
