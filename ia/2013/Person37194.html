Doug Krug's Summary

The essence of Doug’s work is built around the premise that the core of what makes an effective and inspiring leader cannot be taught, not in the traditional sense - it has to be brought out from within.

Through “enter-trainment,” Doug provides inspiration and information, giving participants the opportunity to participate in their own discoveries. Through such engagement, valuable leadership insights occur that result in organizational tools which become immediately applicable.

Doug’s primary focus is helping top executive teams - in both corporate and governmental arenas - generate and sustain the transformation in their thinking essential to successfully deal with today’s most pressing leadership challenges. With a diverse career as an entrepreneur and management consultant, Doug has proven skills for effectively and quickly creating powerful organizational results.

In the area of government, Doug has worked with the top executive teams of the FBI, NASA, Medicare/Medicaid, the IRS, EPA, Secret Service, U.S. Marshals, NOAA, and all 50 Admirals in the U.S. Coast Guard, as well as three Governor’s Cabinets and the leadership teams of numerous law enforcement and security agencies.

A few of his larger corporate clients include GE, Georgia-Pacific, Ericsson, GM, Sprint, Avaya, NASDAQ, The Hartford, and Verizon.

Doug is the author of "The Missing Piece in Leadership: How to Create the Future You Want" and co-author of two other books, "Enlightened Leadership: Getting to the Heart of Change" and "Leadership Made Simple." “Enlightened Leadership” is in its 31st printing.

His latest book, “The Missing Piece in Leadership: How to Create the Future You Want,” was released in November of 2011 and is now in its second printing. This book serves as the centerpiece of his consulting work. It is specifically designed to be entertaining and interactive.
