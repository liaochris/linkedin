Kristin Noell's Summary

I am a Graduate of the Tyler School of Art, Philadelphia, PA. (BFA in Metals/Jewelry/CAD-CAM)

I specialize in CAD-CAM, 3-D modeling and 3-D printing, and the self-directed projects I have worked on tend to draw thematically from fairy and folk tale sources.

I have worked a freelance CAD modeling/rendering position long-distance for B.You toys, aiding to move the product along in the production process.
