Mark Giovanniello's Summary

I am a US Coast Guard trained digital communications specialist and licensed Captain, specializing in design, application and operation of high capability automated command and control systems for Transportation, Utility, Industrial and Commercial Marine applications, and also provide automation hardware and software solutions as a Microsoft Partner Network member.

Specialties: Giovanniello Integrator specializes in the design and implementation of Water Treatment, Transfer and Storage Systems, Infrastructure Security, Shipboard Command and Control Systems that primarily utilize industrial controllers (PLC'S) & Variable Frequency Drives (VFD'S) combined with a variety of mechanical devices and measurement instruments connected by hardened communication networks.
Our typical clients develop sustaining relationships with our staff enabling us to provide lasting value.
