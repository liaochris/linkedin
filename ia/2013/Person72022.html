Karen Gallagher's Summary

In my career my greatest achievement is always seeing outcomes delivered. Strategy is only valuable when executed.

I’ve worked within my own business The Bees Knees and for corporations including; NEC, Victoria Government, AFL, Carat, Cadbury Schweppes, Fontera, On The Move, amongst others.

I’ve managed multi-million dollar budgets and the cash flow of small businesses, developed brands and products and re-engineered departments. I’ve worked on community initiatives and commercial projects with multiple stakeholders. I have conducted research, written business plans and budgets, rolled out marketing initiatives, change programs and learning and development plans.

Outside of work, I run events for women to foster their self-belief and drive equality from within.

Industries I have worked with include FMCG, Media, Sports, Construction, Government, Not-for-Profit, Technology, Finance, Retail and Utility.

About me:

What I like doing most is building big pictures.

I see the big picture clearly, finding the logic in complex interrelated concepts and then I write the plan and manage the execution to deliver the goal down to the last detail.

People like working with me, because delivering results feels good. Together we solve problems and harvest opportunity to create better work places, products, service and community.

Specialties: Business strategy, marketing strategy, sales strategy, audience research, communication strategy, training, learning and development, change management, project management, product development, persuasive writing.
