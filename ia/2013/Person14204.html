•Bosco v. Regan, et. al.,102 Conn. App. 686 (2007): Plaintiff’s verdict in assault claim at Defendant’s bar for $264,000; successfully preserved verdict on Appeal

•Smith v. Country Scents Candle, Inc., 01-CBAR-1197; November 27, 2001, Sfrerrazza, J.: Motion to Strike granted on behalf of property owner sued for defective premises where plaintiff suffered amputated foot as a result of motorcycle accident
