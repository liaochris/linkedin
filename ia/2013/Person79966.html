Leilla Blackwell's Summary

As a Strategic Interventionist, Leilla Blackwell specializes in Relationship Constructs for Singles, Marriage Education and Divorce Prevention. She received her training through Robbins-Madanes Center for Strategic Intervention with Tony Robbins, Cloe Madanes and Mark Peysha. Leilla celebrates a 23 year relationship with her husband; they share their life with their three children... a teen, a tween and a toddler.

Leilla has contributed to the growth and development of children and families since 2005 through public speaking, coaching and teaching as well as training professionals in schools. She is skilled at using her wealth of experience, knowledge and training to develop and strengthen the family unit, and inspire others to contribute to the happiness, wellness, success and fulfillment of many in their own way.

Leilla Blackwell's Experience

Personal Relationship Coach

Leilla Blackwell is the Relationship Revolutionary. She guides, supports and coaches her clients on their journey to building personal relationships that are mutually fulfilling in all areas; leading to a life filled with greater joy, love and passion. On the path to ultimate fulfillment, challenges are turned into triumphs, destructive patterns are identified and replaced with empowering action, and loving bonds are nurtured and expanded to create a new story for your life.
