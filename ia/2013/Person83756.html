Lynn Freer's Summary

As an Enrolled Agent, owned a tax practice specializing in planning and preparation. Worked with employee stock options and physicians who were employees or partners in large HMO practices.
Began writing and lecturing on Calfiornia tax law in 1987. Currently an owner and President of Spidell Publishing, Inc. based out of Anaheim, CA.

Spidell is the premier provider of tax updates in California with over 16,000 tax professionals attending our federal and California tax update each year. We also provide California research information to CPAs, EAs, attorneys, and tax preparers.

Specialties: Called Ms. California Tax. Work with state tax agencies to recommend new policies and procedures and liason between the practitioner community and the tax agencies to promote fairness in taxation.
