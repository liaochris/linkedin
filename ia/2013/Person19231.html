Helge Brettschneider's Summary

Engineering Life:
Helge started using Autodesk Software in 1986 while working as a engineer for portal cranes and conveyer design in factory planing and mining. In his engineering days he spend 1 years in the Automotive supplier field and designed plastic parts and moulds, for instance in the Emission vacuum control of Ford engines.

IT Life:
During 20 Years in the Autodesk biosphere Helge collected lots of experiences, he worked as Trainer at ATC's in Germany, he spend 5 years as a training and support manager at an Autodesk Channel Partner, he trained Autodesk Partners on new products and versions, he worked in planing and managing content of events like CADCAMP's in CE or a Design Academy in England.

Helge is highly passioned making knowledge about CAD available to the users in EMEA and US, regardless where he has to run a class, for the Autodesk channel, at ATC's, Universities, Autodesk Events or in a workshop on the client side.

When Helge is not teaching, he works as technical Journalist and Video reporter for the CADRelations news portal. Over the years another essential part of of his work life is localization and Subject Matter Expert services for localizations of UI, Content and Videos.

Helge is a team player that has the ability to lead and support his team members in a Project or a department. This is also very important in his work as a pedagogical assessor.

Engineering, design, education and professional video production this is world of Helge Brettschneider. Meet him and you will see it makes sense :-)
