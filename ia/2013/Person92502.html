Michael Jacoby Brown's Summary

Michael Jacoby Brown

Organizer, Trainer and Consultant: strategic planning, organizational development, curriculum development, board development and training, fund raising training, meeting facilitation, volunteer recruitment, leadership coaching and mentoring, campaign planning, social entrepreneurship

I have trained and mentored hundreds of people in community organizing skills, many of whom have gone on to lead organizations
I have organized campaigns that resulted in maintaining millions of dollars worth of affordable housing in a gentrifying neighborhood, created community organizing programs within community development organizations, trained dozens of community health center and public health workers in community involvement methods, and organized and structured community involvement in a $6 million public health initiative.

I founded and led for ten years a community and labor organizing training program where I recruited hundreds of volunteer trainers and teachers and dozens of community and labor organizations and raised and leveraged millions of dollars.
I organized hundreds of clergy and congregational leaders to press elected officials to raise millions of dollars to maintain town services and improve police-community relations.

I wrote a book, Building Powerful Community Organizations, which has sold over 14,000 copies and is used as a text in courses in dozens of colleges.

I have been the featured speaker at dozens of conferences throughout the US and Australia. I have been a trainer at hundreds of workshops on a wide variety of topics related to community organizing, leadership and organizational development, fund-raising, volunteer recruitment and motivation, and civic engagement

Specialties

Author of a new book on community organizing, community organizing trainer and consultant, non-profit manager and executive, board development, fund raising, campaign planning, strategic planning, curriculum development. Certified to deliver the Myers Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) Assessment instrument..
