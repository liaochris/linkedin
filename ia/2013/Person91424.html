Nathan Mendel's Summary

I started Mendel and Company Construction, Inc. in 1997. The focus of the company is to bring the highest level of professionalism to all of our projects, from smaller office and retail spaces to multi-million dollar ground-up buildings. Over the past 15 years, Mendel and Company Construction has had steady, controlled growth. With attention to detail and personalized attention to every client. Mendel and Company is one of Denver’s most trusted and well respected names in commercial tenant finish and small ground-up construction.

Mendel and Company expanded from a home base in Denver to covering the entire state of Colorado, as well as parts of Utah, Idaho and Wyoming, with projects upcoming in Massachusetts, Washington, and Texas. Mendel has completed over 150 dental offices, hundreds of retail and restaurant locations, warehouse and manufacturing spaces, places of worship, dance studios, and much more.

Specialties: Dental & Medical, Retail, Restaurant, Tenant Finish and Small Ground-Up.
