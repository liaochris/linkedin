Jan Olsen's Summary

For the last 10 years I have helped small and medium sized companies with Internet Marketing consulting services through my own company, Agilitor. I have direct experience with e-business, search engine optimization, Google AdWords and email marketing.

At the moment our clients are also asking for inbound marketing services and content marketing in which I am personally involved.

Before starting my own company I have more than 10 years experience from sales and management roles in the IT-industry, (HP and CSC).

For many years I have also been writing about wine on VinForum.dk - a great hobby!
