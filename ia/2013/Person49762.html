Graham Tuttle's Summary

I have now worked in the Third Sector with Norfolk Community Foundation since April 2009. I am responsible for the growth of the Foundation in terms of donor development and for its grant making activity which now exceeds £3,000,000 per annum.

Previous to that I worked for 30 years in the Insurance industry with Sedgwick Ltd and more recently Marsh Ltd.

I was a Managing Director with responsibility for the Marine and Energy Client Service team of 50 staff and the Marsh global claims management company called RECLAIM.

Budget responsibility for over £20,000,000 per annum.
