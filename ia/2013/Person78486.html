Larry Ramsey's Summary

Mr. Larry Ramsey serves as the Vice President of Business Development for XPEC Global Consortium. With over 25 years of experience in telecommunications, Mr. Ramsey’s responsibilities is to identity and grow revenue generating opportunities through his vast industry contacts, vendors and channel partners.

Prior to joining XPEC, Mr. Ramsey served as President of xGen Communications, LLC, a telecommunications consulting and Master Agent Company. Mr. Ramsey has extensive experience in creating and managing start-up operations within Channel Partner programs and is credited with creating Qwest Communications (formally known as SP Telecom) first channel partners program.

Additionally, Mr. Ramsey has held various positions at companies such as MCI, Qwest, Express Tel, NETtel , and AireSpring. Further, he has served in senior executive level positions with a number of start-up telecommunication companies including VP Sales, Wholesale and Alternate Channels, NxGen Networks, President, WTA On-line, and VP, Sales and marketing, MC2 (a regional CLEC/IXC).
