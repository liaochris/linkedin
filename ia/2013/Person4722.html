A. Litsa's Projects

  • Bazaarvoice Handbook for Mobile Design

    • August 2012 to Present
    Team Members: A. Litsa

    I'm currently writing a handbook on mobile design and strategy. It's not done, but I figure it can't hurt to get feedback along the way. Here is a high-level outline for the finished product:


    ## Strategy Development
    Depending on your business and its objectives, it may or may not be appropriate to build an app, a mobile website, an in-store kiosk... Mobile investment should be prioritized according to pain-points or opportunities you've identified in your own customers' journey as they interact with your brand.

    ## Website Design
    Though modern browsers are extremely capable, website, web app, and native app design are differentiated by the objectives they serve. A browser is an application whose purpose is to deliver content. A website is that content which, if well designed, is extremely accessible. Information architecture, good copy writing, and smart visual layout are all components of this accessibility.

    ## Application Design
    Applications are so called because they have a specific application to the user. In well designed applications, all components serve a central purpose.

    ## Email
    To smartphone owners, email is a communications app with push notifications just like their Message app or even Facebook. Roughly 40% of email is read on mobile devices, making email is a critical bridge between businesses and their mobile audiences.
