Mattias Christoffer Hardrup's Summary

Lead on the development of websites and online databases/systems with primary focus on architecture, design / layout, technical parameters, and general project management with specialized designers, developers, consultants and of course the client. Taking part in writing public procurements and other types of offers to our customers on a regular basis.

Skills: Primary focus on Drupal CMS setup and architecture, theming and coding (PHP/HTML5/CSS3), wireframing (Azure), Adobe Flash designing and programming - several other areas of experience like interactive PDF's (Zmags), TYPO3 / Sitecore / Sharepoint and Joomla CMS workflow, Adobe Creative Suite / Cloud software (skilled user), setup and support of hardware/server/printer/NAS and professional network setups (wired and wireless), user-support on PC and Mac platforms etc.

Specialties: Project management and architecture, web- and graphic design, website/database theming and development, online solutions, corporate branding and identity, marketing and communication etc.

Some clients that I've worked for:
Maersk Oil, Novo Nordisk, Velux, Danske Bank, VKR Holding, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Denmark, TryghedsGruppen, City of Copenhagen, Copenhagen Resource Institute and many more.

Operate IT A/S is a subsidiary of Operate A/S, Denmarks largest PR and communication consultancy, located in the heart of Copenhagen.
