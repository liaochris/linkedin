Lisa Allen's Summary

I believe in the power of story; yours and mine, those we've already lived and the ones we're still creating.

I believe that stories are richer when shared, celebrated and documented.

I know that sometimes the stories get stuck between experience and expression, and I bring voice to those stories. I do that by writing books, blogs and marketing materials.
