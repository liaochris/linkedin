Dwighd Delgado, CQE's Summary

Manufacturing operations must become strategic weapons to compete in today’s global marketplace. Startups and turnarounds are faced with the need for leaner, more innovative equipment, processes, and people to stay ahead of the curve.

My job is to find solutions that cut across industries and borders.

√ Challenge: Startups must meet performance metrics that meet stakeholders’ expectations.
►Solution: I focus on the delivery, quality, and cost drivers that yield raving customers and positive ROI.

√ Challenge: Turnarounds face key issues that threaten survival and hinder next level growth.
►Solution: I develop strategic roadmaps to navigate complex market barriers. I launch well-executed plans incorporating customers, cost, and culture.


BRING ME IN WHEN…

►Transferring technology from one location to another – overseas or down the street
►Starting up a new business offshore and are facing cultural challenges
►Customers are threatening to leave because you’re not delivering
►Experiencing unplanned warranty costs and you don’t understand why
►Getting field failures that are costing your customers a lot of money
►Sole source supplier just announced they are going out of business
►Something is broken and you need it fixed!
