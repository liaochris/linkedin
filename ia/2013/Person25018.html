Cindy Wawrzyniak's Summary

I have 20+ years experience in the mortgage lending industry. I have had the great pleasure of helping many loan officers develope their careers over most of this period. I have also been fortunate to have the best customers and referral sources in the world. My customers have been known to tell me, "You just make it easy."
