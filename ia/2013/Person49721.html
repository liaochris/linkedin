Graham Baumann, AIA, NCARB's Summary

I take pride in my integrity, imagination and using my career to make a positive impact. I enjoy spending my free time with found often found laughing at my own jokes.

With my time in the professional design industry, I have been able to gain valuable experience which will be an essential foundation for my career as an architect. My focus is on the design of educational and municipal facilities in and outside Texas including the following:
-Public and Private Schools (Pre-K through 12)
-Administration Buildings
-Maintenance Facilities
-Sports Facilities
-CATE Technology Centers
-Existing Facilities Renovations/Additions
-City Park Structures

I have been able to participate in group and individual projects while continuing to increase my responsibility. The various roles in my career have allowed me a diverse range of assignments including: leading projects with budgets ranging from 50K to 20M, coordination of engineering systems, code/regulation review (accessibility, zoning, life-safety), as well as project management, project scheduling, budgeting, design proposals, construction administration and building relationships with clients, engineers, consultants and general contractors.

Specialties include: Extensive experience with Autodesk Revit Architecture: project start-up, BIM model management, rendering, and coordination of BIM teams. I also have experience with ArchiOffice Database, Google Sketchup, Microsoft Office Suite, AutoCAD, comCheck, Illumion, and Adobe Suite Products (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
