John Ramey's Summary

I'm a disruptor entrepreneur at heart, meaning I thrive when "throwing stones at thrones." I follow two principles as a founder: 1) speak the truth, even if your voice shakes and 2) if your work doesn't make anyone mad, then you're not doing something worthy enough.

Working on my third business, meeting great new people, trying to shake things up, and learning a metric ton every day. I'm lucky enough to work with amazing people in a role/industry that rewards relentless stubbornness and delusions masked as vision.

Youngest entrepreneur in the world to raise their first VC round in 2009. Have since raised $12M in VC from top tier investors. Honored by the White House & US Chamber of Commerce as one of the top entrepreneurs under 30 in the US (2012).

I respectfully only accept friend requests on LI from folks I know. Dear recruiters: Do not add me unless we're friends or you've placed someone with me before. My social graph is not available for you to spam.

Specialties

Creative problem solving, market and trend analysis, big picture thinking, web tech, strategy, advertising, living outside the box, challenging assumptions, programming / building cool stuff, product spec and design.
