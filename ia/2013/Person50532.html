Grgur Grisogono's Summary

I'm a helpless technology addict, implementing the freshest of ideas in managing web application development services at VA-based Modus Create Inc.

Globally active, I exploit the passion for acquiring and sharing knowledge as a trainer, student, book author, blogger, reader, public speaker, listener, conference organizer, and a devoted team member.

Although I have worked with a 100 brands (many of which are Tier 1) and half a dozen governments, I'm yet to start giving back enough - just as some of the world's best have invested in tutoring me.

As a constant fiddler, I best follow the process of making processes better. Scrum and Kanban are heavily integrated in my everyday routine so feel free to drop me a story.
