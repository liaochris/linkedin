Chip Parrish's Summary

Chip Parrish is the founder/CEO of Urban Life 5280. In 2012, Chip was recognized as a Platnium Award winner for the Denver Metro Association of Realtors, with over $24M in annual team production, placing Urban Life 5280 in the top 1% of Denver REALTORS.

With the #1 core value of World Class Customer Service, Chip & the Urban Life 5280 team, serves the diverse real estate needs of clients throughout many of Denver’s finest neighborhoods, including Montclair, Mayfair, Park Hill, Congress and Cheesman Parks, Capitol Hill and Cherry Creek.

Parrish studied political science at the University of Colorado (Boulder) and agricultural economics at Texas A&M University. His professional background includes serving eight years as an investment banker, including five years in industrial real estate. Chip was a custom home builder for six years in Texas, Colorado and Wyoming, and has been a licensed real estate agent in Colorado since 2006.

Chip is a participant in the XTERRA off-road triathlon series, and was a sprint finisher in the 2007 Buffalo Creek XTERRA event and a full course finisher in the 2009 Mountain Cup race in Beaver Creek. He enjoys backpacking, fly fishing, running and creative writing.

For more information on real estat services from Chip Parrish and Urban Life 5280, please call 303.395.5611 or email: chip@UL5280.com
