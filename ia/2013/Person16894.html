Brent Cohen's Summary

Hello, I am Brent Cohen, Managing Director and General Manager of Ybrant Digital’s China and Asia Pacific region. I am based in Beijing where we consult with global and local brands and dozens of advertising agencies across the region to deliver digital media campaigns across all relevant markets/channels. We address communications challenges by deploying performance-based and creative cross-media solutions.

I came to Ybrant with extensive general management, marketing, sales and digital experience developed over nearly 30 years in tech, advertising and PR. As a founder/officer at over a dozen companies, I guided one Internet business to IPO, achieved profitability for others, revitalized 2 non-profits and even had to wind down a massively financed but unprofitable ecommerce company.

A results-driven leader of cross-functional teams of creatives and account managers, I produced over 600 TV ads and nearly 1000 radio spots while working for candidates in 38 states during my award-winning decade as political media strategist. During that time, I worked for 3 U.S. Presidents and the Pope. Today, I apply my skills and experience overseeing the expansion of our company across Asia.

DETAILS

Put simply: I am an entrepreneurial leader and business strategist with the talent, motivation and passion to make an immediate and lasting impact. I was a media strategist and producer in DC for a decade. I moved to LA in '95 and helped launch the digital entertainment and technology sector there.

As Co-Founder of eHobbies, I raised $32 million from VCs as the business grew to 300+ employees. After 8 months, the company was selected as one of the “Top 10 Best Companies to Work For” by Interactive Week magazine while the company’s retail sales grew 800%—topping out at nearly $24 million annually.

I joined nonprofit PlanitJewish.com in 2005 and spun off CircleBuilder as a for-profit in 2006. I am now GM & Managing Director of Ybrant's China & Asia Pacific operations.
