Diego Nei, MBA, PMP®'s Summary

Project Manager. PMP Certified, Project Management MBA, MS Project 2010 domain. Fluent in English, Intermediate Spanish skills. Participated in small and medium projects on various areas, mainly project management training and methodology development and implementation. Availability for travelling and relocation.

What can I do for you and your company?

*** Project Management Methodology Deployment: Develop and deploy a Project management methodology based on the best practices found in the PMBoK Guide, using as a basis the existing processes being used by the company, in order to deliver the benefits of project management generating the lowest negative impact, rework and change resistance possible amongst the company’s employees.

*** Project management best practices coating: Internally develop talent within the company’s employees so that they can perform the role of project management, providing them with support as to how to plan and execute the projects and applies best practices, adding value to the professional and lowering hiring costs for the company.

*** Project Management Office Deployment: Evaluate the Project management maturity level of the company and based on that, suggest, plan and deploy a Project Management Office that will be in charge of addressing the company’s needs in as well as provide support for the Project managers and handle information/knowledge management – providing data for strategic decision making.

What benefits can you expect from my services?

*** Processes optimized and focused on the deliverables;
*** Fast paced Project development;
*** Clearer and more efficient communications;
*** Better understanding of the work to be performed by the team;
*** Knowledge Transfer;
