Kelsey Libert's Summary

Kelsey's work with viral content has led to featured placements with over 1,200 publishers. Notable publishers include Upworthy, Adweek, Fast Company, CNN, The Huffington Post, The Motley Fool, Mint.com, ESPN, Forbes, Business Insider, Mashable, New York Daily News, Daily Mail, MSN, CBS News, AOL, Mirror, Fox, Yahoo!, Examiner, The Telegraph, Cosmopolitan, Babble and many more.

In addition to Kelsey’s work, she often writes and speaks about viral content promotion for several highly regarded industry publishers. In 2013, she'll be speaking at Pubcon (Regional) New Orleans, MozCon Seattle and Pubcon (National) Vegas.
