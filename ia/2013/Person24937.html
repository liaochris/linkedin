Cindy Branson's Summary

WHY MONTAGE SHOW PRODUCTIONS

Specializing in Video Productions for Business we combine 40 plus years of experience in Marketing, Videography, Photography and Audio Engineering to bring a unique, well-balanced, creative and useful solution to your business marketing and/or sales and training videos.

Choosing a video company is an important decision. You will need to trust your video producer to create your vision from start to finish so you can have very little involvement or so you are comfortable working closely with them from pre-production to finalizing your video. Having a partner relationship is the most beneficial type for both of these scenarios.

We work with businesses and corporations of all sizes including Non-Profits, producing Web based videos of Product or Service Commercials, Client Testimonials, Sales Presentations, Business Profiles, Training, Fundraising, Real Estate and much more.

Montage Show Productions LLC has designed a complete, custom mobile studio with back drops and green screen for your convenience and comfort. Access to studio space throughout the Tri-State area is also available. We assist you after your videos have been produced by guiding you or your staff on setting up Video Channels, Embedding videos on your website and using them on your Laptop, Tablet, Smart Phone, Print Advertising and other Marketing materials.
