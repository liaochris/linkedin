Max Hahne,'s Summary

Max Hahne: C.I.P.S. /I.R.E.S. /A.C.R.E
Broker | Collingwood/Muskoka Region

As a member of the Toronto Real Estate Board, Muskoka Haliburton Orillia Real Estate Board and the Georgian Triangle, Collingwood Real Estate Board, Max truly believes that his friends, clients, and customers deserve the very best that life has to offer. He also firmly believes that if you are going to achieve successes, then everyone involved had better start with the right tools, knowledge and resources.

Max has worked in Real Estate and fine home building for 30 + years and has lived in Collingwood just as long. He has accomplished a consistently high record of achievement on the Georgian Triangle Real Estate Board and his passion and specialty are dealing with waterfront property, whether it's luxury living or three season cottages.

Max specializes in the communities of Ontario, Canada, the Caribbean Islands and Central America. Passions include boating, food and fine wine, high definition photography and video, building, computer technology, and K-9 companions.

Max is a respected member of the National Association of Realtors in the USA as well as a Member of FIABCI: The International Real Estate Federation of Agents and Brokers.
