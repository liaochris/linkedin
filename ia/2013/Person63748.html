Jimar Garcia's Summary

We all get into routines, it’s a part of human nature. Routines are meant to optimize our actions, shortening or even eliminating the decision-making process. Routines become the intuition that drives our individual lives and are manifest in how we do business. Businesses operate in a similar manner. Standard operating procedures, whether official or organic, become the default ways businesses interact with customers, build products or educate employees.

The problem with routines is when left unchallenged, they can leave us leading lives that are — at best, stale — at worst, destructive. This is especially apparent for businesses, large and small.

HatchWork Solutions will help you and the people in your organization refresh your perspective and challenge yourself to create something special.
