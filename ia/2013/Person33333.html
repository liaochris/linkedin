David Deaver Brown's Summary

Content Acquisition and Sales, with emphasis on working with small and medium publishers and up and coming authors as well as selling our key accounts Simply audiobooks, ebooks, and consumer software.

Set up web site to sell physical CDs directly to consumers which has evolved into digital downloads as well as working through major download companies such as audible.com (Amazon) and iTunes through audible for audiobooks and digitalriver.com for software as well as Kindle and iBookstore for eBooks and hybrid retailers such as Walmart and Staples through gift card companies.

A major responsibility is to sell major physical, online, and hybrid retailers by getting their title, boxed assortment, and display ideas to leverage our mutual sales. As Head Listener, their ideas have been the best we have received since they have horizontal across market knowledge and ours is more vertically limited to our own market.

Specialties: Media content development in audiobooks, ebooks, software, and web based. Content acquisition, licensing, and sale. Company acquisition, licensing, and sale. Turnaround work in functional areas, divisions, or a company as a whole.
