Michael Pollok's Summary

Attorney Michael Pollok focuses his practice of law on state and federal Criminal Defense matters and on Sports and Entertainment Law including contract, negotiations and intellectual property issues.

Over the years, Michael has tried a wide variety cases in Federal, State and local criminal courts in New York and several other jurisdictions. Michael also has extensive experience handling appeals and post-conviction motions including writs of habeas corpus and motions to set aside convictions and sentences.

In the field of Sports and Entertainment law, he has represented athletes, musicians, actors, producers, directors, choreographers, costume designers, set designers, crew, craftspeople and artists in their day-to-day dealings with employers, unions and other artists. He also handles cases involving copyright, trademark and other forms of intellectual property cases in the sports and entertainment industry. Michael tends to assist artists in the independent film and theatre sector and is an advocate for the arts in education.

Michael has the highest ("AV") peer review rating in Martindale Hubbell:

http://www.martindale.com/Michael-S-Pollok/158367755-lawyer.htm?view=cr

Michael provides the highest possible level of attention to each of his clients by limiting his case load and being available to his clients day and night. Unlike many attorneys, he does not maintain a "volume practice" and selects his cases carefully to ensure that his clients have time to ask as many questions as necessary, fully understand all of the possible options and outcomes, and receive the best legal representation available.

Specialties: Practice limited to Sports/Entertainment Law & Criminal Law and Procedure.
