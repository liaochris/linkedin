Aubrey Beach's Summary

I have worked with technology since the age of 14 when my extracurricular activities turned into an actual business venture. Originally offering technical support solutions for neighbors in my local community I was one day asked to help setup a private network in a new office building for over 30+ employees at the time. After completing this project, I worked exclusively with this company as a part-time network administrator after my high school classes. This experience really helped me develop my interpersonal skills when engaging with employees to help identify problems and efficiently implement solutions.

Once I started college I was determined to still work with local businesses and clients; however, I was over 300 miles away at school. It was at this point I setup my first VPN access in order to remotely manage the network from out of the area.

Over the next year I began learning how to put together websites with Adobe Dreamweaver and basic SEO methodology. After launching my first website for a local business I became increasingly interested in how I could generate more dynamic content and functionality for businesses using the Internet.

In 2009 I formed WhizKid Online, LLC with the mission of providing small businesses an affordable solution to both technical support and website development services. I managed two part-time employees over the next year and really learned all the different aspects of running a business. It was through this experience that I had to demonstrate leadership skills and effectively manage company resources including marketing, accounting, and customer service operations.

In 2011 I formed Beach Stant, LLC with my business partner Sebastian Stant in an effort to expand our service selection and combine our business resources. We now maintain our own hosting server and manage over 200+ domains for various clients around the United States.

Specialties

Website Design, Network Security, Technical Support, Search Engine Optimization, Social Marketing, iPhone Development, Print Marketing, Graphic Design
