Lou Huang's Summary

I am an urban planner and designer based in San Francisco, improving the use of information technology in the process of city planning and architecture. The future of cities, public places, transportation, economic development and housing will be very dependent on design professionals being able to make intelligent decisions very quickly, collaboratively, and with less overhead. This means building a peer-to-peer infrastructure for knowledge sharing, creating open standards for data interoperability, streamlining the feedback loop between practitioners, community members, and government officials, and creating better places using a lower-cost, higher-impact iterative process rather than with top-down, big-developer megaprojects.
