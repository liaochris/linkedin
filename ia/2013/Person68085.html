    Authors: Jon Samsel

    • Allworth Press
    • January 1, 1999
    Authors: Jon Samsel

    Novice writers can depend on this straightforward guide to tell them exactly how to create and move their book ideas from proposal stage to published page.

  • Writing for Interactive Media: The Complete Guide

    • Allworth Press
    • December 1, 1998
    Authors: Jon Samsel

    The complexities of interactive writing are explained in this lucid guide covering Internet soap operas, educational kiosks, web-based training, and global gaming narratives.

  • How to Write Articles That Sell

    • Allworth Press
    • May 1, 1999
    Authors: Jon Samsel

    This handbook guides aspiring writers as well as seasoned pros through the mysteries of researching, writing, and selling freelance articles for magazines, newspapers, and Internet zines. It gives a complete overview of various types of articles and their potential markets, and explains how to write and pitch articles to editors in each market successfully.

  • Interactive Music Handbook

    • Watson-Guptill Publications
    • May 1, 1998
    Authors: Jon Samsel

    The definitive resource that teaches you everything you need to know about creating an interactive music title; from working with a record company, to designing, and distributing your own product.

  • Interactive Writer's Handbook

    • Carronade Group
    • January 15, 1996
    Authors: Jon Samsel

    The definitive guide for writers of multimedia stories, edutainment, interactive fiction, and business interaction design.

  • Going Global: Marketing and Distribution of Multimedia

    • Carronade Group / Multimedia Development Group (MDG)
    • September 3, 1995
    Authors: Jon Samsel

    This research report is a comprehensive study of global opportunities for multimedia development, localization, marketing and distribution, covering multimedia markets and technology trends in over 25 countries across Europe, Asia, Africa and North America. It is designed to be used as a contact and statistical resource for developers, investors and marketers interested in selling multimedia products and services overseas.
