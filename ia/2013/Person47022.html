Tom Gartner, CFP®'s Summary

What gives Tom the right to be trusted by his clients? Integrity. It is this foundational principle that makes Tom a no nonsense, “no bull,” CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional.

Consistently, Tom is a true advocate for his clients, repeatedly looking out for their best interests.
Tom’s innate honesty and goodness was instilled by his upbringing and reinforced by his solid, high quality education from The University of San Diego, where he earned his degree in finance and a minor in psychology. With this concrete background as a foundation, Tom has the knowledge and experience needed to do the best for his clients. Truly, Tom is dedicated to providing impeccable service to secure the financial futures of his clients.

Instinctively, Tom knows how to treat his clients’ right, as he is respected for offering straight forward financial advice and is rewarded with long-term relationships because of this ability and dedication. This has been Tom's philosophy for more than a decade now: “My confidence and passion in dealing with financial markets, allows me to comfortably work exclusively with clients on a fiduciary basis. That means I am held to a standard of only representing the needs of the clients, not my own or the company’s needs.”

As a CFP® professional, Tom takes his work and clients seriously, which has enabled Tom to continue his education and passion for finance. Currently, Tom is a candidate to earn his Master of Security Analysis, and Portfolio Management credential from Creighton University, with an expected 2014 completion date.
