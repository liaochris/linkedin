Andrew Buckley's Summary

VuePoint-Connect where Information Technology meets Audio Visual.

I have over 28 years IT and over 10 years AV experience from development to management with a variety of software and hardware technologies including collaboration, conferencing, control system and cloud technologies.

I consider myself a hybrid who has always been interested in hardware and software technologies and how these can be explored to create new technology solutions. My past has given me exposure to both with a variety of projects under my belt that have delivered new technologies as per the clients’ scope of works as well as my own ideas for new business opportunities.

With my previous company "Virtual Entertainment", I created and developed social and entertainment solutions during a very exciting time where we were just discovering new possibilities. Since 2001 I have been involved in the AV industry from system design to control system development with the goal to introduce the AV industry to IT. For the last 5 years I have been involved in technology in the Education sector and how both IT and AV can merge to create new solutions for both Teachers and Students as well as the overall institution.

Now with VuePoint-Connect we finally have solutions that truly join the 2.

We live in very exciting times with technical requirements and new technologies generating new business opportunities almost on a daily basis and my passion is to fully explore this and be at the forefront.

Specialties

Working with merging IT and AV as a technology.
Collaboration, conferencing, sharing and cloud solutions.

Creating specialty IT solutions and technologies to improve on current business operations and generate new business opportunities through designing and developing new technologies.

VuePoint-Connect where "Information Technology" meets "Audio Visual".

VuePoint-Connect specialise in developing collaboration, conferencing, cloud and control system software for the Education, Corporate and Audio Visual industries.

Providing people with the ability to connect more meaningfully and collaborate has become a significantly more important part of today's boardrooms and classroom exchanges given an ever-increasing range and type of multi-media content.

The team at VuePoint-Connect have extensive experience with video conferencing, control systems, mobile event driven touch enabled software and cloud technologies that blend together to deliver an overall collaboration solution.

We currently have our Education solution running at Queensland University of Technology and was on show at the 2012 AETM.

Personally, I have over 4 years experience with collaboration technologies and how these fit with collaborative group learning spaces in a variety of universities across Australia.

Founder CEO

Virtual Entertainment Pty Ltd

January 1999 – June 2000 (1 year 6 months) Sydney Area, Australia

Virtual Entertainment started with an idea I had in 1996 to stream Audio and Video over the Internet. The Technology evolved enough in 1999 for me to leave my job as an AutoCad designer and develop a prototype Hardware/Software solution to deliver media, play local media, Cable and Terrestrial Television, Internet and more delivered to a standard Television set. The software design of the system even took into consideration that colours had to match the curtains as well.

This was for both domestic and commercial application. We secured $4m Venture Capital and partnered with Australian Cable and Telephony to roll out to the Novatel Hotel chain. The commercial application was server based with both having a back end database to capture usage data for marketing reports. The commercial application also allowed companies to sell advertising space to their suppliers and automatically charge their clients on useage.

Like many start ups of the time, the tech crash also took us down with it. Interestingly, I didn't see anything remotely like it for a number of years after. Maybe ahead of out time perhaps.

I can say that I thought it and I did it though.
