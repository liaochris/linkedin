Jim Dorsey's Summary

Are you looking to put together a t-shirt campaign for your team, organization or event?

Do you have special requirements for custom labeling, glow in the dark, specialty inks or global shipping needs?

Do you want the best quality of t-shirt and wearable's printing?

Then you have found the perfect person to help you get your project done on time and on budget.

For over 25 years I have helped many corporate and non-profit clients understand the various options available in t-shirt screen printing. From product selection, to the best printing techniques, (direct to garment vs traditional screen printing) ink types, and more. My unique approach has always been to function as my clients project manager, engaged during the entire process as if I was a member of your team. This gives clients the peace of mind that their work is in trusted hands from start to delivery.

Understanding that I work with very busy professionals with heavy workloads and responsibilities is what motivates me to make their lives easy by taking on a bigger and person role in the success of the t-shirt projects

Call Jim Dorsey for additional information and details on how to make your t-shirt printing project a smooth success.

Non Profits: ONE.org / American Rivers / Oceana / Rails to Trails / Zynga.org

And many, many more.

This extensive experience has helped me understand that each project has dedicated needs and challenges. Every project requires individual attention to make certain that the entire process is smooth, pleasant and valuable for my clients.

My job is not just to sell our services, as the quality and capacity that we provide is unquestionable. What makes me better and different from other vendors is my ability to act as part of my client’s team(s). I function as a project manager to help my clients understand the printing processes, see the balance between the opportunities and limitations of the various printing techniques, and anticipate issues in order to prevent problems.

This level of engagement and involvement is rare in the screen printing industry and has been one of my cornerstones for gaining the trust of so many valuable clients.
