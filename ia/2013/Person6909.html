Andrew Gardner's Summary

For the past 13 years I have worked as a visual design and UI specialist and in recent years as a UX/UI consultant leading solutions across a wide range of technologies. My focus has been on taking the UI from the initial concept phase, through branding and user experience, to UI coding and development.

I have merged a strong background in visual design with prototyping, Information Architecture, UX requirements definition/gathering, and strong technical skills. This ensures an innovative approach which is possible within the confines of the chosen technology and a solution that meets client needs.

Within the past few years I have been increasingly client facing and feel at home leading regular client workshops to drive out requirements and ultimately achieve agreement from key stake holders. In conjunction with this I have added UX and other front end services to large bids and provided pre-sales support in multiple arenas.

Specialties

• 13 + yrs in Graphic Design for websites / intranets / interactive media / print.

• 5 + yrs experience in high pressure consulting, engaging with medium to large clients.

• Pre-sales support and communicating key differentiators to a varied client base.

• Capable in the field of analysis, design and implementation, including: Strategy, prototyping / wire-frame creation, graphic design and conceptualisation, (X)HTML coding, CSS, accessibility compliance, Usability, solution delivery.
