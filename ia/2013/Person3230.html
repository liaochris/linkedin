Alan Strachan's Summary

Alan Strachan Personal Training has long been established as the best in class for personal training and fitness services in South Lanarkshire and the Greater Glasgow area. With over 16 years of personal training experience under our belts, and having tailored and delivered fitness, weight loss, muscle toning and lifestyle programmes for literally thousands of people, with resounding success, you can rest assured that we know how to deliver results.

Your One-to-One fitness sessions at Alan Strachan Personal Training is carried out in our stunning new Aszurra Fitness Club. Training can take place with one of the Alan Strachan Trainers, ensuring that all clients are working one-on-one whilst in the club. This productive and inspiring setting allows you and your Trainer to focus on one thing, ..your results!

Personal Training at Alan Strachan Personal Training integrates a wide range of training disciplines and methods to ensure that your interest is high and your body is always being challenged in a wide range of ways. Following your initial Physical Analysis sessions, your Trainer will develop your bespoke plan of Training. Designed to be progressive and challenging, the plan will take you through all facets of fitness development, making sure that you are not just leaner, but also stronger, more flexible, more stable and agile.

Through diligent planning and implementation we aim to make you feel like yourself again, only better!

For further information, please call 07930 176 788.
