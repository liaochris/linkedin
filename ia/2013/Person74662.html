Founded by Kerry Martin, Hope Xchange Nonprofit is privately funded and instills hope in the lives of abused and neglected children and LGBQT teens through innovative collaborative programs and initiatives.

Hope Xchange accomplishes this primarily by working with for-profit and not-for-profit organizations to help them integrate social cause business practices & leverage the Internet for on-line fundraising.

Hope Xchange's long-term initiative is to build a safe haven and temporary refuge for LGBTQ teens on a vineyard where Mother Nature's healing hands can instill in them a sense of hope for their future, a sense of courage to get them through the BS bullying and ridicule, a sense of purpose to draw upon to make a difference, and a sense of self-importance because they too are people regardless of their sexual orientation and gender transformation 'choices' or fate. (A vision inspired by our founder's own personal plight and the inspiring journey of her little brother and amazing parents and siblings ... and a vision that will be achieved for her angel Sara and for all the LGBQT teens who have taken their own life.)
