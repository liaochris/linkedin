Alexandra Annala's Summary

I have a deep and abiding interest in planning, preparation, simulation, and support for medical teams treating combat and civilian personnel in mass casualty environments. I am deeply concerned about the provision of sensitive healthcare for victims of trauma, torture, and rape perpetrated for personal, ethnic, social, political, institutional, or national purposes. I believe we must provide universal access to culturally competent healthcare for underserved populations.
