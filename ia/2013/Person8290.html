Anne Driscoll's Summary

Anne Driscoll is a marketer, retail strategist and the founder of Build Media Marketing, a social media marketing company specializing in building brand recognition, team member education and collaborative marketing opportunities for entrepreneurs and small business owners. She currently oversees and manages over twenty-five business blogs, and is in the process of opening her first retail store in Oakhurst, CA. When not working, Anne enjoys spending time with her adorable family and nurturing her slight obsession for zombie shows and dystopian novels.
Contact Anne @ anne@buildmediamarketing.com
