Brett Andrews, CWS®'s Summary

My mission is to build and protect Financial Independence. I believe that Financial Independence begins with a strong foundation which requires four equally important cornerstones.

* Accumulation/Growth
* Risk Management/Asset Protection
* Structural Efficiency/Tax Reduction
* Succession/Legacy Planning

Also, a strong foundation requires a Financial Advocate which is one of the most important pieces often missing in financial plans. I realized this while growing up in a family of entrepreneurs as I witnessed the issues that come with owning a business. I learned many things that only life experience can teach those that have made the decision to become their own boss. As a business owner, I understand the responsibility and commitment it takes to build and maintain a successful practice. That is why my passion is to work as an Advocate for those that would rather spend their time growing their business or enjoying the fruits of their labor. As my client’s Advocate I evaluate each situation to discover inefficiencies. Then, I coordinate all efforts and work with all of a client’s trusted advisors. Next, I propose solutions to resolve any gaps and create savings over a client’s current structure. These solutions result in Increased Cash Flow, Lower Taxes, and personal Asset Protection. Finally, I implement and maintain the custom designed plan that is constructed based on each client’s objectives. In essence, my business is to improve a client’s situation and that is what motivates me to show up to work every day. I am the point of contact for all of my client's financial needs.

Finally, with over a decade of experience as a portfolio manager, I have far more to offer than many advisors that have been salesmen for their entire careers. This experience provides me with the ability to apply a unique technical overlay to investment allocations as well as the capability to interview and converse with portfolio managers in their language on your behalf.
