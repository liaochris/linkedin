Bonnie Carter's Summary

Since 1994, Adworks has been offering marketing, advertising and design to regional, national, and international clients. As an aggressive and agile firm, we believe in the fundamentals of communication strategies and have developed a reputation for effective, on-target marketing, building and positioning brands and maximizing exposure.

Investing ourselves in our clients’ success breeds a pride in our work that can’t be synthesized. We’re accountable, and we maintain accountability by operating with modest goals: not to over-promise; not to over-build our business with respect to load vs. capability, and not to let our egos drive us. Our size allows us to remain focused on this goal and allows us to provide our clients with top agency talent and experience in a leaner, results-orientated agency model insuring our clients receive the greatest return on their advertising investment.

Through this vision our small but unparalleled team has worked with many world-renowned companies including: AT&T, Big Lots, CIT, Citrix, Cunard Cruise Lines, DirecTV, Embassy Suites, Food Lion, Fleet BankBoston, Harley-Davidson, Ingersoll Rand, JDS Uniphase, Kraft General Foods, Motorola, MGMA, Siemens, Osram Slyvania and Toshiba.

Specialties

Brand Development, Consulting, GUI Design, Infomercials, Interactive, Marketing, Media Planning and Placement, Print, Public Relations,Research, Social Media, SEO Copywriting, Strategic Planning, Content Management System Websites, Web and Smart Phone Apps
