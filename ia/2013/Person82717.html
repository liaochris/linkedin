Louise Mercieca's Summary

The Health Kick is a sensible, holistic approach to fitness and nutrition - weight loss is not just about what the scales say and healthy eating isn't about counting calories. At the Health Kick we offer exercise and nutrition plans to fit in with busy lifestyles and make feeling fit, strong and healthy a way of life not a temporary fix.

For individuals Personal Training can be a rewarding yet challenging way to achieve your goals. Personal Training is bespoke and flexible. The Health Kick offers Personal Training at Equilesse Pilates Studio, here you get the benefit and privacy of a private studio! Personal Training is also available at your home or out and about if running is more your thing!

For businesses, looking after the health and well-being of employees is vital, check out the Healthy Businesses page for an overview of the Corporate offer. The Health Kick offers fun, informative and practical interactive workshops on health and nutrition.
