Caitlin M. Sparks' Summary

I am a current student at the University of Missouri located in Columbia, MO, dual majoring in Accountancy and Economics. I will graduate in 2014 with undergraduate degrees in Accountancy and Economics, as well as a Masters in Accountancy.

In addition to my academic studies, I have been actively involved in various leadership roles throughout the Trulaske College of Business including the following: Cornell Leadership Program, Trulaske College of Business Ambassador, Trulaske College of Business Student Council, Accounting Excellence Mentor, Sam Walton Scholar, Professional Development Program Student Advisory Board, and Relay for Life of Mizzou. I currently serve as the President of Student Council in the Trulaske College of Business. Throughout my time at Mizzou, I have been honored to receive the Top 14 Freshmen Award and induction into Beta Gamma Psi.

In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my family in Sedalia, MO. I also spend time travelling around the world, including both Costa Rica and Chile throughout past summers.
