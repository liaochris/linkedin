Johan Gozali's Summary

Johan Gozali is Head of Engineering at mig33 where he is responsible for growing and leading the software engineering team, spanning quality assurance, localization, and application development (Mobile, Web, and Server) with teams in Singapore, Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia), and Jakarta (Indonesia). With his hands-on technical leadership, the team has successfully launched the mig33 miniblog, as well as the mig33 social games platform.

Prior to joining mig33, Johan was a Technical Program Manager with Linden Lab (makers of Second Life) where he lead a geographically distributed cross-functional team of developers, QA, content creators and UX designers to build quality Second Life web experiences. He has also been an Academic Staff at Republic Polytechnic where he taught Enterprise Java Programming, and Web Services Development.

Johan holds a Masters of Computer Science and a Bachelors of Computing (1st Class Honors) from the National University of Singapore.

Specialties:
Software Architecture, Project/Program Management, Internationalization/Localization, Curriculum Design/Training, Team Building / Hiring.

Interests:
Web Development, Metrics/Analytics, Big Data & Hadoop, Java, Scala, Python, PHP, Scalable Distributed Storage, High Performance Systems, Cloud-based Infrastructure/Services, Agile/Scrum.
