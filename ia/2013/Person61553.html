Jenae Hommes' Summary

I currently work as a Production Coordinator & Graphic Designer in the Ad Production Department for Gannett in Des Moines, IA. As a Production Coordinator, my main duty is to provide excellent support to internal and external clients as well as artists through effective communication by taking/placing phone calls and communicating electronically through email and Skype. I take whatever measures possible to assure quality and timely production. I also serve as a resource and mentor to Graphic Designers in the event that they have any questions or concerns when understanding customer instruction, problem solving, or are needing assistance in Adobe programs.

As a Graphic Designer my responsibilities include interpreting customer instruction and designing creative and quality ads in a timely manner while paying attention to detail and accuracy.

I am a 2011 graduate of Trinity Christian College where I earned a BA in Studio Art with an emphasis in Graphic Design and Photography. At Trinity I was very involved in the arts on campus by being the Yearbook Editor my junior and senior years. While attending school I was also venturing into professional work off campus. During my time at Trinity I was hired as a Graphic Artist intern at The Sun in Ames, IA, where I was in charge of designing the advertisements for the paper and preparing the paper for press. I also interned at Today's Chicago Woman Magazine, where I directly assisted the Art Director in designing and formatting 5 issues of the magazine, assisted in photo shoots, designed high end ads, and assisted in preparing the magazine for print and web viewing.

I specialize in Advertising, Typography, Logos, Layout, Print, and Portrait Photography. I have experience working on many diverse projects that include the following: Brochures, Inserts, CD Covers, T-Shirts, and other Promotional Materials. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, Microsoft Publishing System, Social Media and Wordpress.
