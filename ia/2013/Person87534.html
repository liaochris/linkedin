Martijn Van Der Woud's Summary

My main interest is the development of systems and instruments that do valuable work. Having a masters degree in organization psychology, the organizational, communicative and social aspects of (web) development have fascinated me since I started my R&D work at Orga-Toolkit in 2004.

I get a kick from solving problems related to building flexible, extensible and maintainable systems. I like to bend my mind in uncomfortable ways, but I have also found that in many cases it is best to learn from the experience of those who have solved similar problems before me. Originality is often overrated!

Specialties

ColdFusion development, Software development, Web development, Statistical data analysis, Psychological test construction
