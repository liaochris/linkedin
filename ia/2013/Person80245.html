Three Achievements:

o Got the first "yes" from potential customers to actually deploy the system in their institutions: a geriatric hospital, a Parkinson Physiotherapy Clinic and an leading company in the Physiotherapy industry.

Leo Ruffini's Publications

  • Conversations on Presence

    • Starlab
    • 2009
    Authors: Leo Ruffini

    Presence research focuses on understanding, generating and achieving control over the cognitive experience of being somewhere, or someone. The objective of the field is to produce "real" experiences through sensorial replacement, sensorial augmentation and interaction with "bits" (full or partial immersion in simulated reality).

    As simple measure of success for Presence technologies is this: If it feels real then it is Presence.

    "Conversations on Presence" is a series of interviews with key players in the field.
