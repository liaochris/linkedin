Michelle Brubaker's Summary

Michelle Brubaker is a successful Northwest-based entrepreneur who helps businesses “Create a Profitable Internet Presence” through innovative web marketing, consulting, and speaking. Michelle has a degree in eBusiness Administration.

Michelle is also dynamic professional trainer with outstanding testimonials and reviews from participants of her seminars and workshop series. She consults with small businesses creating marketing strategies that deliver results. In addition, she contracts her services and expertise to five Community Colleges in Oregon/Washington and the Small Business Development Commission.

Author of the eBook “10 WAYS TO BE YOUR OWN INTERNET PUBLICIST” and the soon to be released “ADD A FLOOD OF NEW CUSTOMERS THROUGH FREE INTERNET ADVERTISING” workbook/manual (based on the popular 2010 seminar/workshop series).

Specialties: Web design, web development, internet marketing, web marketing, seo, branding, web video, videography, web training, trainer, teacher, speaker, instructor, seminars, ACT job profiler
