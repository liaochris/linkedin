CEO & Chief Blogger

Cooperative Intelligence Source

June 2008 – Present (5 years 8 months)

We train individuals and companies how to be cooperative detectives, that is have people provide you with the knowledge you seek by being a giver. Collaboration insinutates you give and expect in return. Cooperative means you give to give. As you develop your cooperative intelligence skills, you will become a generous leader, an altruistic connector and an effective communicator who listens and is heard.

Cooperative Intelligence puts people in the center. Its foundation is giving attitudes and practices which encourage openness, sharing and trust. Trust begins with communication, listening, telling the truth, and doing what is good for people and the organization. When people trust you, they often will do what you ask them to do since they want to. Check out our blog http://cooperativeintelligenceblog.com
