Mohammed (MIDHAL) Dhalech's Summary

• Developed and implemented international programmes and training Related to intercultural learning and dialogue, Religious dialogue, Human Rights Education, Non Formal education and international youth work.
• Developed the use of Social media in disseminating key messages and the use of Social Media in an Equality and Human Rights context and digital inclusion of minority groups.
• Developed a range of community engagement initiatives including Independent Advisory Groups and Youth Participation initiatives both in the public and voluntary (NGO) sectors.
• Experience of working in the Public and the Voluntary sectors.
• Have successfully developed a programme introducing Muslims and BME communities to the countryside and outdoors since the 1980’s and latterly engaged with The Campaign for National Parks MOSAIC project.
• Project managed and delivered various projects and managed large budget projects in the voluntary and public sectors
• Successfully developed work or Rural Race Equality in the UK and Europe and published various work on rural racism in the UK and Europe.
• Have successfully represented International Non-Governmental Youth Organisations at Intergovernmental steering group on Racism Anti-Semitism and Xenophobia and intolerance campaign of the Council of Europe.
• Have successfully delivered training to youth organisations, leaders, young people and institutions and policy makers at an international level
• Served on the Council of Europe Inter Governmental steering group (representing international Youth organisations) of the “All Different All Equal” Campaign (1994) and Home Secretary’s Race Relations Forum.
• Common Purpose Graduate
