Jacob Fletcher's Summary

Process:
My approach is to, research all aspects of the project including client goals, target audience, budget, timeline etc. From here I observe all previous creative efforts, campaign demographics and target statistics. By assembling all research from the previous stages I am able to resolve identified concerns and avoid potential problems.

As solutions are defined I begin to formulate defined concepts. Once a defined direction is chosen I roll up my sleeves and begin the implementation of the creative concept while assessing if each goal is met successfully. When all is said and done, I evaluate the project as a whole, considering the goals and the process through which it came to fruition.


Story:
My name is Jacob Fletcher. I received my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from the Art Institute of Boston at Lesley University.

I'm 25 years young. I am old enough to know what I am doing but young enough to know the emerging trends and understand the current market. I am married. I have a dog. I appreciate the opportunity to meet new people and cherish my ability to learn new things. I enjoy sketching on napkins, eating clam chowder, coffee talks and arbitrary dialogue with strangers. I consider my ability to communicate a power. I advocate for sustainable energy and recycle almost everything. Discovering a solution to a problem is the greatest triumph one can have. I love fixing problems.

I have explored many avenues in the design world, freelance, studio ownership and internal design. In all of these directions I was expected to guide a team to a successful deliverable.
