Jyoti Srivastava's Summary

Dr. Jyoti Srivastava is one of New York's top prosthodontists and one of the few female specialists of this order to also run her own practice, EastSide Dental Medicine, along with Dr. Robert Castracane.

As her friends might say, Dr. Srivastava was “born to do this job”. With her eye for perfection and passion for excellence, Dr. Srivastava would gladly admit she's found her true calling.

It came by way of her seemingly unlimited aptitude for all things design, from the wide expanses of architecture to the inner spaces of molecular science. Before becoming a prosthodontist, a pinnacle of dentistry, she stopped to get her Masters of Science in Biophysics.

Dr. Srivastava’s practice is a reflection of her calm. Her patients embrace her personal approach and holistic perspective. There is a tangible sense of trust between Dr. Srivastava and her patients and with her there is a knowing that you’re receiving the very finest dental treatment.

Her ability to restore good oral health and proper biomechanical functionality is matched only by her talent for creating beautiful dental aesthetics.

As a leading academic prosthodontist at NYU College of Dentistry, she is on the cutting edge of advancements in the field. NYU’s program is one of the foremost centers for dental technology in the world and Dr. Srivastava is helping to shape that debate.

She has a rare talent for putting together a comprehensive treatment plan for her patients. Achieving good dental health is the foundation of her work alongside her knack for creating beautiful smiles and great aesthetics. Her sensitive touch and calming presence are a relief to each and every patient that sits in her chair.

Specialties

Dr. Srivastava loves to teach and is currently Associate Professor, Department of Postgraduate Prosthodontics at NYU College of Dentistry Graduate Program, where she trains future prosthodontists in the specialty.
