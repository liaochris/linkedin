Andy Bruce's Summary

With over 12 years experience in the advertising, consumer goods and technology industries, I have developed a passion for real-time data, context marketing and exceptional marketing experiences.

As a mobile marketing strategist and technologist I work with retailers, agencies and brand marketing teams to develop innovative solutions for getting their message to the right people when and where it matters most.

The Mobile Experience Company (Mobile X Co.)

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Toronto, ON

Inspired by the age-old sentiment that, “half the money spent on advertising is wasted; we just don’t know which half," The Mobile Experience Company specialize in delivering and measuring the effectiveness of brand content across media channels to determine which marketing activities work best.

With a suite of technology solutions and proven methodology, Mobile X Co. measures consumer behaviour in real-time and over time so marketers can maximize the return on their investments.

Public Company; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

The Location Based Marketing Association is a international group dedicated to fostering research, education and collaborative innovation at the intersection of people, places and media. Our goal is simple; To educate, share best practices, establish guidelines for growth and to promote the services of member companies to brands and other content-related providers.

Members of the LBMA include retailers, agencies, advertisers, media buyers, software and services providers, and wireless companies. Simply put, we want to help those engaging location-based services be as successful as possible.
