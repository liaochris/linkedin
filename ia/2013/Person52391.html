Heidi Alexandra Pollard Company Culture and CSR Coach's Summary

Our Vision: UQ Power's vision is to provide the best corporate culture support for organisations and government agencies worldwide so they can power up their workforce, improve engagement, keep top talent and grow new leaders.

We do this by transforming managers into Expander Leaders who empower their people and create teams that are innovative, agile, healthy and engaged. We know that people join a company but leave their manager and happy work forces grow profitable businesses that give back to the community, so we are out to change the world one leader at a time.

Background and Skills: Leadership & Communication Training. International Speaking. Corporate Culture Organisational Culture Change and Corporate Social Involvement Expert.

I use my experience as a successful corporate communicator, body language expert, certified business coach and NLP practitioner to guide companies and their leaders to gain a competitive edge through the way they communicate with their workforce, stakeholders and industry,

I believe companies can greatly contribute to society when they have a strong company culture with a clear vision, belief in their value, and commitment to sustainable growth.

Through my support companies find they:
- IMPROVE workforce engagement, performance and safety
- INCREASE stakeholder and community influence
- POSITION their company as an industry leader.
