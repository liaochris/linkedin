Beeay Interactive Services' Summary

Beeay is an interactive full-service development company from Buenos Aires - Argentina.

We partner with Interactive and Digital companies to build result-driven and cost-effective projects including web, mobile and social applications.
Whether you are looking to outsource the development of large scale solutions, smaller applications or simply need to increase your design and development bandwidth to complete projects, Beeay is the right partner for you.

Desde comienzos de 2010 las platafomas Moviles y Tablets nos sumaron un gran objetivo, y era borrar la barrera de la uni-plataforma Web, y pensar unicamente en el alcance global del proyecto, aplicable a todos los medios necesarios.

Hoy esto es una realidad, demostrada en proyectos exitosos.
