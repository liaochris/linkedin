Des Walsh's Summary

“Trends in technology, society, and work converge to create enormous opportunity for businesses to become more effective, efficient, transparent, and agile. But navigating the challenges that these emerging trends present demands a willingness to embrace change and a fundamentally different approach to how business gets done. ' Social Business Design, The Dachis Group

Des Walsh helps business owners and executives engage effectively with this new world of social business and achieve the knowledge, insight and adaptive ability to create their own advantage.

He brings to that task a unique blend of high level coaching skills, a deep, strategic understanding of the social business environment, and practical business wisdom, to deliver readily understandable and action-oriented guidance for his clients.

Business coach, blogger, social media strategist, LinkedIn expert, author and international speaker, Des has been delivering communication, training and coaching services for businesses, not-for-profits and government organisations, for over 20 years.

    • November 2012 to Present
