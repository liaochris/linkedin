Craig Manson's Summary

Founding his first company at the age of 20 (later sold to Diageo) and named as a “trailblazer” by Revolution magazine, Craig is a deeply driven and successful entrepreneurial leader with a wide range of public, private and third sector experience and exceptional track record of innovative delivery. Educated at both Glasgow University and London Business School, he combines a passion for all forms of digital media with a degree in law and a pragmatic commercial approach to business challenges. He is comfortable and adept at engaging at all organisational levels and has a network of relationships at the highest level worldwide. As an independent consultant he has been retained by a variety of blue chip clients from Mitsui Corp to the UK Government.
