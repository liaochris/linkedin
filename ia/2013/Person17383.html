Brian Geyser, APRN, MSN-BC's Summary

I am a clinician, consultant, educator, blogger, speaker, and serial entrepreneur with over 25 years experience in health care, pediatrics, geriatrics, and psychiatry. Since 2008, I've been helping organizations around the country leverage digital communications technology to connect with and engage their audiences.

Currently, I run CareNetworks.com, a digital agency that helps health care, senior living, LTC, hospice, and homecare providers and other care-related organizations by offering strategy, design, social media, content, analytics, and search solutions for a digital world - specializing in web, social, and mobile technologies.

In addition to running CareNetworks, I practice Pediatric Emergency Psychiatry at Yale-New Haven Hospital, teach for Yale Schools of Medicine and Nursing, lecture on digital media best practices throughout the U.S. and Canada, and provide clinical and digital consultation to health care companies, senior living communities, home care agencies, long-term care companies, and hospice organizations around the country.

Specialties: Communications, digital infrastructure, websites, blogs, mobile, social media, email marketing, graphic design, online presence strategy & management, content creation & aggregation, SEO, analytics, landing pages, branding.
