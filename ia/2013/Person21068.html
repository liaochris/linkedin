Cathy McDaniel's Summary

A 22 year veteran in the real estate and mortgage arena. Author of over 60 books and training manuals to include "A Road Map To Saving Your Home" currently available on the National Association of Realtors bookshelf. Serves as one of the top educators at the American Real Estate University and have met over 45,000 real estate professionals throughout 17 hardest hit states. Cathy recently opened her own school, My C.E. Class, LLC. She was an endorsed Instructor in 2007 for HUD - PEMCO, Ltd., Established relationships with real estate boards, associations, government agencies and non-profit organizations. Received the 2008 "Educator of the Year" awarded by the Georgia Real Educators Association. Organize and host numerous industry conferences and events.

Specialties

REO, and Short Sales transactions, foreclosure prevention, loss mitigation, property manangement, pre and post home counseling, budgeting and credit restoration, FHA, Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, VA, USDA products and policies. Event planning, public speaking and foreclosure prevention community events.
