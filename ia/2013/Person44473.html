Ryan Sumner's Summary

Named "Charlotte's Best Photographer," by Elevate Magazine, Fenix Fotography specializes in editorial fashion, advertising images, and artful headshot portraits of executives.

Our goal is to use photography to help you attract, multiply, and retain clients by making a great first impression, outshining your competitors, and providing creative pragmatic solutions for your visual communication needs.

We focus on understanding your company and the challenges you face in these extraordinary times. We believe in individual attention, a customized product, and taking a team approach to realize your vision and be consistent with your brand.

We use state-of-the art cameras and lighting equipment, as well as lenses from the highest-grade optical glass, and we have the creative vision and technical knowledge of how to use them to their fullest capacity in making stunning images for your business.

It's important not to risk your business with poor-quality images that will infer poor quality on your services and products. And hiring a specialist is often less expensive than attempting to create your corporate photography in house--especially since many business owners don't take into account the expenses of time and of business lost when employees are diverted onto projects outside their areas of primary talent and training. The results are almost always lackluster and have to be recreated professionally.

Good photography is an important investment in your business. After all, if you don't invest in your business, you can't rightly expect your prospective clients to.

Ryan "Fenix" Sumner
