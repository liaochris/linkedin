Kristen Daukas' Summary

Atlantic Social Media Group (a division of Atlantic Webworks) is the only full-service Social Media Marketing agency in the area. As Partner and Director of Social Media Services I work closely with our clients to develop a strategy and create social media plans that help them brand their companies in a cohesive way to ensure that they achieve maximum results. We work with companies from all areas of business - hospitality, food and beverage, manufacturing, retail, medical and more. Every company is unique in their approach but similar in their desire to reach their target markets while engaging and developing new ones. Our number one focus at Atlantic Social Media Group is to make sure these goals are met in an unintimidating way.

I am passionate about technology, social media, blogging, and community involvement. As a professional speaker, I bring energy, enthusiasm and new ideas to groups on topics such as social media, branding, sales and customer service.

As a long-time blogger and self-proclaimed "geek", I have honed my craft through personal use and experience combined with aggressive study and research as social media has risen to the forefront of both business and (general) interpersonal relationships. In addition to the work I do for my clients, I also work with parents to help them understand technology and social media.

Specialties: Social Media, Branding, Marketing, sales, e-marketing, public speaking, customer service, networking, start-ups, blogger outreach, research and development, public relations, trade shows, special event marketing
