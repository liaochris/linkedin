Amy Poinsett's Summary

An alumnus of the University of Denver, Amy has trained and led technology development teams in software, application design, and research analysis across various business sectors within financial services, luxury tourism, and medical technology, resulting in high revenue returns and stable corporate growth. Amy is a talented manager with the unique ability to simultaneously manage and direct multiple high priority tasks. A successful entrepreneur, Amy has founded, directed, managed, served as COO, and served as CEO of various high revenue earning technology start-up companies in the point of sale and e-commerce spaces.
