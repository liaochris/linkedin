Gary Jesch's Summary

I'm excited about ways to help large companies connect with their customers, using leading (and sometimes bleeding) edge technologies. Right now, I'm into sharing information about marketing automation, lead nurturing, Sales 2.0, webinars and online events, and of course, my live animation at tradeshows, special events and corporate meetings.

I also find myself giving back to the community, with pleasure. My current project is called the "Kids Talking Wall," a place where parents, children and community leaders can interact for everyone's benefit. It's sponsored by Partnership Carson City, a group organized to combat alcohol and drug abuse by children. For more info - http://kidstalkingwall.com

My background is in journalism, graphic design, marketing, sales, video production, 3D animation.

Currently proficient in real time 3D animation art called performance animation, using computer systems I build and use myself.

I created my first virtual character in 1993, and have been working in the business ever since.

My goal is to see virtual computer-generated characters contribute to society and to the well-being of people.

Specialties

Writing, photography, videography, editing, marketing, website design and production, live animation, tradeshow exhibiting, stage show production, lighting and projection design, acting, audio staging, business management
