Kathleen Hockey's Summary

Kathleen Hockey is an award-winning author, speaker, and counselor, whose clinical specialty is the prevention and treatment of depressive conditions.

She works at her private counseling practice (www.kathleenphockey.com). She also works part-time at New Mexico Solutions, Albuquerque, NM as a school-based community therapist.
