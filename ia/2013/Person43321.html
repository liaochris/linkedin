Eva Amsen's Summary

I'm Outreach Director for F1000Research, a new Open Access post-publication peer review journal in the life sciences. I'm also one of the founders of F1000 Specialists, which is a programme for fans and power users of F1000 products to share their knowledge with others.

Prior to this I was in charge of the Node - a community website for and by developmental biologists, run by the journal Development.

I've also worked as a freelance science and medical writer, and I researched the cell biology of mammalian skin pigmentation for my PhD in Biochemistry.

I'm interested in channels of communication between scientists, ranging from the traditional peer review system to web 2.0 tools.
