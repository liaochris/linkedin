Carl Bradford's Summary

I help Corporate Recruiters, Talent Acquisition Professionals and Hiring Managers hire top performing employees and contractors for their teams. I use Performance-based Hiring methods that include showcasing client career opportunities in fresh new ways and then building a compelling win/win rationale for top candidates to say yes to employment offers.

My motto and Trademark is: Better People. Better Teams. Better Business.

You’ll find me actively recruiting a variety of hard-to-find professionals every day for clients both large and small. My goal? Partner with incredibly crazy-busy Recruiters and Hiring Managers to help them hire top employees every time.

Another part of every week for me involves certifying Corporate Recruiting Teams in Lou Adler’s Performance-based Hiring methods. This includes personally coaching recruiters from some of the largest US employers and helping them solve difficult recruiting problems. Something I’ve been doing for over 15 years.

I started my recruiting career on the HR side in technology recruitment, and then after 15 years I left my role as Director of Human Resources and moved into independent recruiting. When I started my consultancy, the initial focus was on national recruiting. About 15 years ago I became an early adopter of Performance-based Hiring and added additional services helping clients upgrade their recruiting processes and training.

Among the many, many clients that have engaged me to fill critical positions and/or helped become certified in Performance-based Hiring include: IBM, McKinsey & Company, Bauch & Lomb, Verizon, American Express and Intel. Plus, I also work with numerous mid-size companies who are focused on recruiting top employees.

I can help starting today.
