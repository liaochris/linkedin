Jim Dodson's Summary

The Law Office of James W. Dodson is a personal injury law firm focused exclusively on representing the rights of those who have been seriously or fatally injured due to the negligence of others in car and motor vehicle accidents, pedestrian/crosswalk and bicycle accidents, slip and falls, motorcycle crashes, dog bites, child injuries, and wrongful death and medical malpractice claims. Attorney Dodson is committed to leveling the playing field for accident victims against aggressive insurance companies.

James W. Dodson is the author of many books offered free to Fl residents @jimdodsonlaw.com:

*The Five Mistakes That Can Wreck Your Florida Accident Case
*Dangerous Trips To Avoid in Your Fall Case
*Buying Car Insurance
*Survivors Guide to Florida Wrongful Death Claims
*When Kids Suffer Big Injuries
*Florida Pedestrian Accident Guide*
*Florida Bicycle Accident Handbook
*Uninsured Motorist Insurance: Don't Leave Home Without it
He has also offers an extensive list of accident and injury related videos, articles and free reports.

Jim Dodson Law has been built, not on large advertising campaigns, but rather on referrals from other lawyers and professionals, and those from former clients. He is a member of the prestigous Million Dollar Advocates Forum, has achieved the highest possible AV rating by his peers for legal abilities and ethics from Martindale-Hubbell, was awarded a 10.0 Superb Rating from AVVO.

Jim is President of the Mid-County Bar Assn., former Pres. of the Clearwater Bar Assn, member of the Clearwater and St. Petersburg Bar Assn., the American Justice Association and the Florida Justice Association. He was interviewed on Bay News 9 regarding Sixth Judicial Circuit elections. He has serves on the Board of Directors of Isaiah's Inn, a ministry dedicated to transitioning families from homelessness to independence and is on the Advisory Board of MADD. Jim is active in his church; is a 2nd generation native Floridian and is married with 2 children.

Specialties

Florida Bar rules do not allow the use of specialties or specialist in this listing, however Jim Dodson limits his practice exclusively to representation of those injured in vehicle collisions, pedestrian injuries, slips/trips/falls and unsafe premises, all child injuries, wrongful death and fatal injury, medical negligence and other injury claims.
