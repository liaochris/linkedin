Kenneth Lanneskog's Summary

Freelancing Art Director, based in Stockholm, Sweden.
My focus is currently on screen media. Since 1996, I have held positions as Art Director at various agencies - both inhouse and as a consultant. My experience ranges from print to web. In 1996 I studied Interactive Media Design at one of Sweden's foremost institutions for contemporary communication disciplines, RMI-Berghs (now Berghs School of Communication).
