Brett Elizabeth Larkin's Summary

My two passions are wellness and emerging technology. My company, ReferBright, is the first comprehensive digital marketing platform for independent health practitioners (physical therapists, occupational therapists, fitness trainers, nutritionists, acupuncturists etc) who want more referrals but don't know how to get them. We help 2.1 million health practitioners market themselves online and walk them through the process of qualifying for clinical referrals which varies by discipline and state and requires renewal annually.

Health practitioners, let us supercharge your online presence https://www.referbright.com/

Companies, clinicians, and payers, get access to our vetted referral directory https://www.referbright.com/directory
