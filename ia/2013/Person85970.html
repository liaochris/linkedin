Photographer / Owner

Marike Photography

November 2009 – Present (3 years 10 months) Eldoraigne, Centurion, Gauteng

Marike Photography is owned by professional photographer Marike Roelofse. Her life as a photographer started in 2005 when she enrolled at TUT’s photo school. She spent 4 years there, and left in 2008 with a b-tech degree and some published work. Marike Photography was found in 2009, and today she specializes in Corporate, Life&Love and Documentary photography. Some of her recent clients include Beaulah Vari Design, KPMG, Deloitte, Ehlers Attorneys, Save the Children, Mi Mind Educational Products, Sangari South Africa, The Department of Trade & Industry, Laser Adventures, The University of Johannesburg and IST. Marike is based in Centurion, South Africa.
