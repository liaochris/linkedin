I worked for one term as an intern with the Attorney Grievance Commission - and that was nearly twenty years ago! Interns at that time helped primarily with attorney misconduct investigations. The one case that sticks out in my mind is when an attorney decided to walk out on his practice, leaving only a note to his secretary on his desk. The AGC took possession of his files, reviewed them and contacted the clients. It was a very large project that I believe was still ongoing when I graduated.
