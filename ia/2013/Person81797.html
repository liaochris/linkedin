manager

August 1996 – July 1997 (1 year)

I spent a year working at the Achray which is a small (9 room), busy hotel on the shore of Loch Earn in the small village of St Fillans. I loved working here and relished the opportunity to put my newly honed degree to practical use. The hotel was full of personalities with whom I learned how to operate as a small business manager. Amongst the plethora of hospitality tasks carried out on a day to day basis in small hotels I was tasked with implementing health and safety policies, carrying out risk analysis, running the restaurant, and dealing with the day to day, making on the spot decisions and learning to put the hours in.
