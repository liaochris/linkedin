Lance Darcy's Summary

Lance has been working in events and TV for over twelve years. His industrial credits include numerous weddings, TV up-fronts, fashion shows, product launches, press conferences, and after parties.

For The Lighting Design Group, Lance is a Lighting Director for Al Jazeera America, where he works on "Real Money With Ali Velshi" and "Consider This," in addition to various special events throughout New York City.

Lance Darcy also writes the mostly monthly column LD On The DL for Live Design. He also wrote The I-Havior Manual, which is an attempt at a Unified Theory of Human Behavior. Additionally he is the Editor of The Field Guide to Difficult Clients.

In his spare time he enjoys cooking, aviation, video editing, photography, and swimming.

Specialties: Event Lighting Design, Event Production Design, Event Production Management, Project Management, ETC Consoles, TV Lighting Design & Direction, Vectorworks, Rendering, Writing, Adobe CS 6, Photography, Director of Photography, Writing, Lightroom 4, and Getting Stuff Done
