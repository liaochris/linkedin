Chris Hilton's Summary

I'm a London Based, creative driven Motion Graphic Designer, Animator and Director.

I graduated From The University of Creative Arts (Epsom) with a BA (Hons) in Graphic Design: New Media. Starting my career in the Broadcast industry I now have over 7 years experience creating work for the Music, Film, Publishing and Advertising industries (public and B2B) working both client side and as part of larger creative teams.

During my career creating Motion Graphics and Animation I've also expanded my creative outlook to include many aspects of film production, including Direction, Cinematography, Lighting, VFX, Editing and Grading having created multiply music videos and book trailers. I'm also currently in the process of writing several short films to hopefully be made by the end of 2013.

As a sideline to my motion work, over the last 3 years I have also been very fortunate to work as a photographer for Universal Music Group covering some of the UK's largest rock festivals, taking pictures of some of the biggest bands in the world. As a result I have now also started taking on work as a portrait photographer for up and coming bands as well as unsigned local bands. For some examples of my work please check out my photography page and 500px account, unfortunately I am unable to showcase some of my music photography online, but I'll be more then happy to show you them over coffee and biscuits.

During my career I have been fortunate enough to work with clients such as Universal Music Group (acts including The Rolling Stones, Paul McCartney, Black Sabbath, The Who etc), Nokia, Simon & Schuster, Harper Collins, Red Bull, Sony, Ubuntu & Burberry to name a few.

As well as some of the biggest and best creative agencies in London including: Imagination, the Team, Saint (now RKCR/Y&R), Duke & Earl, RPM & Iris to name a few.

Software I use: Adobe After Effects, Premiere, Photoshop, Illustrator, 3D studio Max, Cinema 4D, final cut, Da Vinci Resolve & Colour (final cut studio)
