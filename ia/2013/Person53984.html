Hugo Gelissen's Summary

Loyality, decisivenesss and teamspirit. These are important values that I strive for in my business life.
With more than twenty years of experience in various organizations and sectors I am a projectmanager that gets results in a complex environment. My work area is more generic than specific and I am not specialized in one product or package, but widely deployable with a preference for the sectors industry, technology and logistics.
I integrate in an organization easily and I am able to connect on all levels with internal and external parties. Hereby I have a good sense for political relations.
I use best practices as Prince2, MSP en IPMA and besides these qualifications I have a lot of experiences in managing teams. With attention for people, their environment and culture I know how to build a good team.

Services
You can hire me as a project-, program- or interim manager to provide the following services:
- Translation of your strategic goals in tangible projects on operational and tactical level
- Management of your project in accordance with market standards and specific requests in your company
- Temporary execution of line functions

Specialties

Project management, Program management, Interim managent, Logistics, Industry, High Tech, IT Infrastructure, Prince2 practitioner, IPMA-B
