TEDxSantaMonica

March 2011 – Present (2 years 6 months) Santa Monica, CA

My co-organizer and I develop forums for discussion, inspiration and change through ongoing TEDx events in the Santa Monica community. We find local trailblazers to speak about their inspirations, endeavors and contributions to the greater good. Our primary focus isn’t necessarily to find the best speakers, but rather to find people with passion, commitment and an inspiring story to share. In particular, we seek women and those with diverse ethnic backgrounds who are sparking creativity in their community. As we strive to make our events as local and green as possible, we partner with innovative local businesses and vendors who support our efforts. We mentor a team of high school students who participate in all facets of the year long preparation for our youth events. Our mission for our youth events is to reach children who are underserved and underrepresented and may not otherwise get the opportunity to be a part of a global movement. This is a 100% volunteer/labor of love position to promote the TED mission of spreading great ideas!
