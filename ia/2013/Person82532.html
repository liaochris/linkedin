Lori Pasulka's Summary

CREATIVE HARVEST: "Plant. Grow. Harvest."

Reap abundant harvests for your business with creative collateral and image solutions. Ensure the seeds you sow today cultivate your future success.

Think of your brand as the sustenance that will enable your company to thrive and grow. Your image must be consciously cultivated, not merely left to the elements. Your investment in strategic collateral establishes integrity, generates vitality and enhances professionalism — ultimately benefiting your company’s well-being.

CREATIVE HARVEST emerged from the concept "reap what you sow." Just as an acorn holds the promise of a mighty oak tree, so Creative Harvest possesses the vision for nurturing inspiration into a successful image for your organization. Lori works in tandem with clients to invest, cultivate and establish their brand.
