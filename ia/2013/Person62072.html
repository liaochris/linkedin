Jenny Brown's Summary

I am a financial adviser and director of JBS Financial Strategists a boutique financial planning company in Melbourne, specialising in SMSF’s and personal Risk Insurance.

After being retrenched from a major advertising company in late 1990, I started my foray into sales and learned to love listening to motivational and inspirational tapes on how to believe anything is possible, I answered an ad to sell insurance, got the job and started to study again!

I love building great relationships that often turn into friendships and am passionate about helping people. I am involved in several business advisory groups where I discuss with small business the issues they face in running their respective enterprises. I also sit on a couple of Business Advisory Boards helping women in business, as well as being an avid networker and Social Media advocate!

At JBS we have an awesome client focused team who are the engine driving JBS’s success. I delight in investing in the professional development of our staff, we are all highly qualified. When my team is happy and working at their optimum, our clients are happy and the rewards are universal. This is the legacy I want to leave JBS. We are a thriving, client driven practice that will flourish long after my retirement.

If I could do nothing else, I'd be happy networking and talking to people all day long about what's important to them in life!

My Motto: Love what you do and Smile it costs nothing and makes you and those around you feel better!

At JBS we are committed to forming a mutually-beneficial, ongoing, long-term relationship with our clients. We don’t want to be another number in your phone book, and you will hear from us regularly as we work towards effectively managing your wealth.

We recognise that each client is unique, so when providing strategic advice and our range of services, we focus on you and your family, and tailor all of our advice to your personal situation. No two pieces of advice are the same.

We focus on clients’ ‘lifestyle relevance’ over the long term, project managing and accommodating our clients with financial services, portfolio design, lending facilities, legal and accounting contacts, health services and lifestyle planning tools.

We implement sensible, easy to understand solutions which are comprehensive and meet our clients’ goals. We are able to provide you with a range of financial services to suit your personal needs and when appropriate arrange introductions to specialists who are highly qualified in their field of expertise.

We take several measures to ensure we are at the forefront of financial service provision and advice; regularly engaging clients for detailed feedback, as well being held accountable by our independent Advisory Board, which consists of an Accountant, our Business Coach, and two key clients.

I was a finalist for the AFA Adviser of the Year for 2012 and was awarded runner up Financial Planner of the Year for 2010 by Professional Investment Services.

I am an Authorised Representative of PIS and part of Professional Private Advisers and my concentration is in the creation of long-term wealth and strategies for all of our clients by helping create and manage wealth strategies for high net worth, small business owners, business executives, and Gen Y clients, and am the key contact at JBS.

Director & Owner

JBS Financial Strategists

Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Financial Services industry

1992 – Present (21 years) Melbourne Area, Australia

JBS is a leading Melbourne based boutique financial planning business focused on creating and managing wealth strategies for a range of clients, including individuals, families, small business owners, business executives, and Gen Ys. JBS concentrates on wealth creation and preservation by providing personalised financial planning strategies for all situations. These tailored plans all reinforce our three key ideals: to create long-term wealth, to protect yourself, your family and your investments, and to position you to be able to enjoy your success.

JBS specialise in the following areas:
* Financial Planning
* Superannuation
* Self-Managed Super Funds
* Investment Advice
* Risk Insurance
* Finance & Loan Structuring

JBS is licensed through Professional Investment Services (PIS). PIS is well known and respected as a leading provider of financial services to the professional and business community throughout Australia and Internationally. JBS is proud to be a member of the Professional Private Advisers group within PIS (Top 30 Practices nationally).

JBS focuses on fulfilling our client’s life goals and providing peace of mind by ensuring we consider their key ideals and beliefs when tailoring a strategy. Our communication and consulting process is customised to your goals, needs and desires, encompassing all relevant lifestyle factors. We ensure absolute confidentiality when providing any advice, and aim to foster ongoing relationships through providing strategic solutions and education.

JBS was established in 1992, and built on key alliances with like-minded professional associates adding value to our business, and in-turn, our clients. Our team are friendly, approachable, open-minded, technically skilled, great communicators and enjoy being able to meet all of our client’s needs. Our clients are loyal, open, attentive and reactive. They welcome our values, have our trust, and believe our business is instrumental in their future.
