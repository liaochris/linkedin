Juliette Robertson's Summary

I find that new leaders are often promoted because of their technical expertise with little training or guidance in how to manage their new team. Often their immediate response is to position themselves as the go-to technical expert to direct their team to success however their people skills are soon exhausted and they become disillusioned.

We need to show them there is a better way to lead. My passion and business focus is to set your emerging leaders up for success early in their careers by giving them the people skills and tools to develop the talent and initiative in their teams while managing day to day processes.

I do that through assessing their leadership competencies, helping them set an inspiring vision of the type of leader they want to be and providing the people skills training and support coaching they need to become confident and competent leaders long term.

If your emerging talent works in an office environment, I can help. Face to face, on-line and phone training and coaching programs and products are available to those who are searching for a better way to lead.
