John Przepadlo Strott's Summary

John is a User Experience Consultant focused on creating exceptional products. John has a future friendly approach to design, implementing mobile first and responsive designs in a lean UX environment. His work has been featured on Yahoo!, CNBC, large social networks and e-commerce platforms.

John's past experiences include Senior Designer for UX at StockTwits, User Experience Lead/Creative Director at HomeSublime (Blindsgalore.com); Senior Designer at AutoAnything; Co-Founder of AppleKrisper, Inc.; Co-Founder of West Coast Sunsets, Inc.; and he also provides design leadership through jlanedesign.com.

Specialties: User Experience, Visual Design, User Interface Design, Mobile First Design, Application Design, Responsive Design, Creative Leadership, Design Consulting, Start-ups
