David Farkas' Summary

David is an Interaction Designer out of Philadelphia, PA. He has worked with consultancies and in-house design teams and has acted as a consultant with a variety of financial, medical, and industrial clients.

He is no stranger to failure and the need to be aware of the characteristic of different verticals. A lover of workflow, the ideal situation is a stack of post-its and sharpies, a blank whiteboard, and open critique. Process happens through transparency and conversation, not a rote recipe.

David graduated Carnegie Mellon with degrees in Design and HCI, worked with PNC Bank, Electronic Ink, Bresslergroup and is currently with Tamman Technologies.
