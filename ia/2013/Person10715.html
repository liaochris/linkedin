Julie Courtney's Summary

A highly successful Consultant and Coach specialising in resilience building for people and organisations.   My passion is in the field of personal and organisational development for companies and individuals in the financial sector.    

MY BUSINESS 
 Founder of Atrium Synergies – a Consultancy which works to unlock the barriers to superior performance, health, wellbeing and productivity - developing the ability to sustain peak performance without burnout – we focus on making your performance, businesses and lives a success.    

THE RESULTS I GET
  I believe that every individual, team and organisation can achieve higher potential – the methods are effective, easy to implement and get the desired results!    

WHO I WORK WITH?   
Organisations and individuals seeking to remove barriers to engagement, health, wellbeing and productivity – and who are ready to build resilience in order to transform this into sustainable and profitable results.    

Already a high achiever? We equip you with further tools to maximize performance, health and vitality – taking you to the next level.    

Are you in a period of change or transition? – engage in powerful coaching to ensure effective performance and resilience during change - be it organisational or personal.

    DO YOU WANT TO KNOW MORE?   

If you would like to know more contact me for an initial consultation – this can be delivered either face to face or via Skype.    

For corporate clients we run a number of programmes including high performance and personal resilience strategy sessions during times of growth, change and business transformation. We also develop and run informative employee lunch and learn sessions as part of Wellbeing programmes and strategy.

Accelerated programmes – For those with busy schedules and who want to get results fast half-day programmes take you through 3 key steps to re-energise and refocus. 

Specialties

Coaching – Maximise your performance, clarity and focus

Change Management – Build personal resilience during periods of change personally or professionally

Leadership and Team Development - Design and implementation of programmes featuring Resilience and Emotional Intelligence leading to a potential 25% in improved performance.
