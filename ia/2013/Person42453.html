Eric Walker's Summary

Everything is narrative. Our lives are shaped by story. The stories we tell each other become the stories we tell ourselves. I dream up ideas and experiences that live within these storylines.

I have conceived and executed work across all media for such iconic brands as Apple, Lexus, Nissan, Toyota, Pedigree, Infiniti, Starbucks, Porsche, Verizon Wireless, Honda, America West Airlines, Ford, Hilton Hotels, and the legendary Smokey the Bear.

With any luck, those old narratives will inspire many new ones. At least that's the story I'm telling myself.
