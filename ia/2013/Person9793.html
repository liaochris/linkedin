Arnold Weil, MD, MBA's Summary

CEO of Non-Surgical Orthopaedics PC, specializing in non-surgical orthopaedic care and interventional pain management. We treat a variety of Orthopaedic injuries, including Georgia Workers' Comp, auto, and personal injury. We provide non-surgical treatment including interventional spine injections.

Established Clinical Research site conducting phase 2,3,and 4 clinical trials. We have been active in clinical research for over 13 years and have a terrific staff of four full time coordinators.

Professional medical business consultant and speaker. I provide practice management to small-medium sized medical practices. I also travel nationally speaking on Pain Management and non-surgical orthopaedic care.

Specialties

Orthopaedics, Pain Management, Medical Business Management, Business Consultation, Professional Speaking
