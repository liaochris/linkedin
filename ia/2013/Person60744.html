Jeff Linda Brandt's Summary

Experienced and cutting-edge Realtors providing innovative housing solutions to clients!

We are Jeff & Linda Brandt, full time Realtors & Desert Residents for over 22 years. We have extensive experience in successfully marketing & selling homes here in the beautiful Desert Area. Having raised our four sons in the Desert, we can help you find the best neighborhoods and schools, along with the latest market conditions, local economy and other pertinent information.

We will guide you through the complexities of buying and/or selling a home, eliminating the hassles and stress that can surface by using inexperienced real estate agents. If you are from another state or country we will patiently explain the buying process and the many legal disclosures that are required when purchasing property in California. We will do the leg work and keep you up-to-date with new listings and conditions as they impact the market. As a standard business practice we preview homes as they become available and keep you informed about market conditions as they evolve.

When buying a home we will use all of our networking skills throughout the mortgage industry to get you the best financing rates possible. We will provide you with the advantage of our personal one-on-one attention, as well as the use of our dynamic team of 5 licensed Realtors and 3 assistants and a marketing specialist.

We are resident members of Rancho La Quinta Country Club. We specialize in golf communities, country clubs, family neighborhoods & multimillion dollar dream homes. We are top negotiators and love what we do! Remember you will be treated as our only client in the real estate transaction.

Jeff is a native of California and graduated from Fairfax High School in Los Angeles. From there he attended California State University in Northridge graduating with a degree in Business. Linda is a native New Yorker and was born and raised in Brooklyn & Long Island. She graduated from Oceanside High School on Long Island and then attended the University of South Florida in Tampa where she studied Special Education. She moved to California for an opportunity to work in Los Angeles. Upon his graduation, Jeff started a business where he specialized in supplying paper goods to various businesses, which included restaurants and retirement hotels. From there he collaborated with his father, built, and operated Encino Hills Retirement Hotel until 1989 when he sold the business & decided to begin his professional sales career in Real Estate. Jeff & Linda`s 20+ year Real Estate career began with Mike Glickman and Associates in Woodland Hills California. In 1991 Jeff & Linda with their four young sons moved to Palm Desert California where they both joined Prudential California Real Estate. After numerous years with both Prudential California Real Estate and Dyson & Dyson, they found the perfect partner with Windermere Real Estate, having become the market leaders in the Desert Cities. Their extensive professional training, high ethical standards, attention to detail and phenomenal negotiating skills, along with their tenacious efforts have led them to the top of their field. Jeff and Linda have promoted their continued success in the luxury home market, family neighborhoods and golf course communities. Consistently ranked as two of the top agents in the Desert. As leaders in their field and consistent multi million-dollar producers, their client base reaches everywhere throughout the country & Canada consistently earning them repeat & referral business.
