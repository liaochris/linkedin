Barbara T. Nelles' Summary

* Multimedia journalist and editor

* Web content creator and strategist

* Oversaw development of a carefully designed news site with goal of getting it accepted by Google News

* Producer of search-optimized content & perpetual student of SEO

* Experienced reporter in the manufacturing and retail sectors

* Adept and obsessive researcher

* Fluent in WordPress, the CMS with 52% market share

* A blogger since 2006 with a local seo'd food blog and boring identity blog

* HTML fluent, CSS competent [JavaScript next]--thanks to Codecademy.

* Videographer who is proficient in Final Cut Pro X editing software

* Multiskilled writer of lovely marketing copy or straight-up news. Both a long-form journalist and a blogger. Can explain complex stuff in layman's terms or translate simple stuff into tortured jargon ;)

* Honing my public-speaking skills at Toastmasters

* Was a fashion editor in the day-glo 80s with a national consumer publication

* Have lived and worked in New York, Chicago, Los Angeles.

* Spent summer 2012 in that utopian experiment called San Francisco where I ate, walked, people-watched to my heart's content.

* I didn't "lean in" in my 30s. I lay in bed eating bonbons while my children ran amok in northern N.J. ;) But I sure lean in now.
