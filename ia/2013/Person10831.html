Audra Gorgiev's Summary

I am a professional freelance editor (copy, line, technical, and substantive editing) and proofreader offering a wealth of experience editing a wide variety of written material (including textbooks and K-12/postsecondary e-learning material; scientific, medical, and technical journal articles; academic documents, marketing materials; ESL documents; as well as other nonfiction work). My references will attest to my determination to accept nothing short of perfection in my work. I strive for complete accuracy and the highest degree of professionalism in the work I complete, and utilize APA, Chicago, AMA, AP, New Hart’s Rules, and CSE (formerly CBE) manuals of style as required. In addition to editing in MS Word, Adobe Acrobat, and MS PowerPoint, I am also well-versed in authoring tools and content management systems (such as Delta, SharePoint, and LaTeX), and very comfortable working with HTML.

My editing and proofreading skills extend across many subjects and business needs—you could say that I am an “editing savant”—and have always been the hidden accomplice behind any successful document. Grammar, punctuation, clarity, formatting, and rapid turnaround are my specialties, whatever the document may be; and I work electronically or with hard copy. I am a passionate perfectionist when it comes to a succinct, well-written, and mistake-free document—that oh-so-important first impression. My time is very flexible and I am on call six days a week for long- or short-term projects; my computer is fired up at 9 a.m. and is on until 10:30 p.m. MST.

My rates vary on the level of editing and turnaround time; rate structure and billing method can be tailored to the project. Please note that I do strive to work within my clients’ budgets.

Specialties

Copy editing areas of expertise: textbook manuscripts/proofs, professional journal articles for submissions, magazines, ESL documents/articles, broad science/medical expertise, fiction manuscripts, marketing and advertising materials/documents, bio/autobiographies.

Styles utilized include Chicago, APA, AMA, MLA, AP, CP; competent editing in U.S., U.K., and Canadian English.
