Jeff Lunderstadt, Realtor®, ABR, e-Pro – Marketing Director & Buyer’s Agent, became a Realtor® after having worked in magazine production, being the creative director in a downtown Internet design studio. He launched his own successful design studio, having served corporate clients such as Grubb & Ellis, The Hillman Company and UPMC. He has won national awards for magazine design and illustration, and is here to showcase your home and serve your needs as a dedicated Realtor® as well.
In 1997, Jeff developed Move2Pittsburgh.com, exclusively for Arlene (his wife), one of the very first feature-packed agent websites in Western Pennsylvania, to serve her listing clients the very best way.
Jeff recently added PittsburghLuxuryHomes.com to expertly serve his residential real estate clientele. Jeff realized, with his background he could best serve the residential real estate market with his creative and marketing skills as well as genuine care for his clients and their dreams.
