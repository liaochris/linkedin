Brandon Compagna, MBA's Summary

I am an eCommerce ace and toys and games expert who creates innovative solutions to business problems. I find exceptional new solutions to existing problems using a unique approach that will save you time and increase your bottom line. I can see opportunities where others do not, which is how my two eCommerce businesses came to be. One tracks the most popular toys at the major online retailers while the other is an online store which sells popular hard to find toys, video games, and collectibles. With my collective experience, I always have my finger on the pulse of the latest in the toy marketplace. While I have a great deal of desirable attributes, my most notable is that I can be depended on to get the job done, while exceeding expectations, every time.

Through my businesses, I have developed a strong background in B2C eCommerce, online retail marketing, and social media (Facebook, Twitter, blogging (Wordpress), and Pinterest). I have also spent a lot of time on eCommerce strategy and development, SEO, PPC (Google AdWords, Facebook Ads, and Microsoft adCenter), as well as Email and Affiliate Marketing. I always keep an eye out for new technologies that will allow me to streamline my operations and allow me to operate more efficiently in the ever expanding, always competitive, consumer and toy marketplaces.

I specialize in building eCommerce and marketing brands with a strong focus on consumer satisfaction. I’ve worked in both B2B and B2C environments as well as in large and very small companies. Though B2B and B2C have different customer bases, fundamentally the customers have the same goal - they want to be completely satisfied. Making sure consumers and customers are completely satisfied is something that I personally strive to do, which leads to customer loyalty year after year.

I challenge you to give me a goal and a blank piece of paper. I will not only create a unique vision, but a strategy to fully carry it out and execute it timely and efficiently.
