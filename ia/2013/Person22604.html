I make creative things, I teach creative people, I nourish whimsical beauty.


ART > PRODUCT > MARKET

My passion is art. I believe that beautiful things can happen when form and function embodies concept, color and texture.

Founder & Art Licensor

ArtOfLicensing.com

July 2008 – Present (5 years 3 months)

The Art of Licensing group on ArtOfLicensing.com helps bring those in the business of art licensing together in a supportive and cooperative format. It is the largest due-free online networking group for an international art licensing community with a presence on LinkedIn, Twitter and Facebook.

Members including licensors (artists, illustrators, designers, and licensing agents), licensees (product manufacturers, producers, corporations) as well as supporting roles such as attorneys, technical professionals, and media members. Members also include many international art licensing professionals which promotes avenues of communication for cross-cultural ideas.

Members exchange helpful ideas, news items, resources, report on trade shows, trends, and share information on marketing, copyrighting and licensing processes.

For information on participating in this group, please visit: http://ArtOfLicensing.com
