Michael Brandt's Summary

Specializing in mortgage origination in Austin, Texas for FHA, Conventinal, VA, Jumbo, Construction, Lots, TXVET, MCC, USDA, and Texas DPA loans. Purchase and Refinance transactions.

I am a loan officer for United Lending. I started on the ground floor of the mortgage business in 2000 as a loan officer assistant, and from 2001 to 2005 worked in a variety of roles as an assistant branch manager and as a loan officer. Since deciding to originate home loans full time, I have helped over 500 families obtain financing for their new homes. My business is 100% by referral, due to the strong relationships I have grown and maintained with my business partners and clients.
