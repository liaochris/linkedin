Marc Preston's Summary

I am a veteran Voice & Film Actor primarily working out of Los Angeles and New Orleans (the new "Hollywood South"). Depending on the project, you might see me acting on camera, or hear me as the voice promoting some of your favorite network programming. For over twenty years, I've been fortunate to have worked in front of the microphone and camera with some of the most creative people in the industry.

My voice can currently be heard around the world as the image/promo voice of a variety of broadcast networks and stations. I've also narrated numerous corporate, commercial and broadcast projects for clients such as Bacardi, Bell Canada, The National Baseball Hall of Fame, NASCAR and many more. I've been the "in-show" announcer/narrator for projects airing on networks such as Comedy Central, Speed Channel and ReelzChannel, where I'm currently heard in over 60 million homes in the US as the voice of the network.

I am also a former broadcaster and have worked as a major market and syndicated radio host on some of America's marquee radio stations, as well as the ABC Radio Network.

All of my production work is done through my company, Marc Preston Productions, LLC. Audio and video samples of my work can be found at on my LinkedIn page, or via my YouTube channel (www.youtube.com/airpreston). You can also learn more by heading to www.marcpreston.com.

Specialties: Film Actor | Image voice for broadcast networks and stations | Narrations for documentaries, industrial/corporate projects and new media | Commercial voice overs | Digital production | Copywriting | Directing | Ad campaign development for traditional and new media | Voice over coaching
