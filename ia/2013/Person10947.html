Austin Gardner-Smith's Summary

Austin currently holds the title of Senior Service Design Lead at Hill Holliday, where he spends his time designing systems and experiences that create lasting impressions for brands both big and small. Combining strong systems- and design-thinking skills with practical experience in digital product design and development, Austin works across nearly all of Hill Holliday’s existing accounts and is a frequent participant in new business pitches involving high-profile brands.

Prior to his current role, Austin joined Hill Holliday as an Editor in its then newly-formed Content Department, leading strategy and implementation of content marketing programs for clients including Bank of America and Merrill Edge, Staples, Cigna, Liberty Mutual, Major League Baseball and Puma.

A natural leader with a high risk-tolerance and soft spot for violently disruptive ideas, Austin loves working in environments where challenges are myriad and frequent. He has a history of rapidly acquiring new skills as necessary, from Photoshop to Javascript, and feels equally at home in front of an audience or behind a terminal window.

Specialties: Service Design, System Design, Content Strategy, Digital Media Strategy, Digital Product Design, Web Design, Web Development
