Barbara Mencer's Summary

As a marketing director in law firms I developed an expertise in helping lawyers grow their practices. I saw that many lawyers were out of balance – focused on work at the expense of other areas of their life. So, I started my own coaching & consulting business and created a step-by-step program to help you build your business while being balanced, healthy, fit and happy.

As a business development coach, nutrition consultant and competitive physique-sculpting athlete I believe in a holistic approach to life. My passion is helping my clients reach their goals while enjoying the journey.

I work with high-achieving, high-performing people who want to grow their business or reach other goals while living a balanced life. My clients understand that to live their personal peak performance lifestyle they need to address all the pieces: nutrition, physical fitness, relationships, career, balance & self-care, and structure.

Many of my clients are:

✓ LAWYERS & OTHER PROFESSIONALS (accountants, financial advisors, etc.) who want to get more clients easily without burning out.

✓ BUSINESS OWNERS & ENTREPRENEURS that want to grow their business while keeping balanced.

✓ PROFESSIONAL CONSULTANTS & COACHES who want to reach their goals while keeping balanced, happy & healthy.

✓ PROFESSIONAL OR COMPETITIVE ATHLETES that want to reach their next level without sacrificing themselves or their relationships.

✓ EVENT PLANNERS & ASSOCIATIONS looking for a great keynote speaker (business development and peak performance lifestyle topics).

►If you’re interested in building your business while creating your personal peak performance lifestyle email me at Barbara@BusinessBreakThroughInstitute.com.
