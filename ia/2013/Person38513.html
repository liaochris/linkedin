Liz Lyons' Summary

dsigns is a signage, printing and branding company based in County Kerry, Ireland. We are leaders in the design, manufacture and installation of outstanding signage solutions and display systems. Trusted by many well known brands, we deliver the dsigns advantage - a client focused, innovative and competitive service that is second to none. From concept to completion, our project management is tailored to achieve your objectives. So whether your company is a small or large business, we work in partnership with you to deliver exactly what you need on time and naturally, on budget. dsigns success is founded on a total commitment to exceptional service and quality. We get your business noticed with our exterior signs, Banners, Vehicle Signage, Illuminated Signage and Pavement Boards. Our interior signs are tailor-made for your business, from functional Directional Signs to eyecatching Illuminated Displays. We also provide Health & Safety Signage, Exhibition Display Systems, Labels and Stickers and promotional merchandise.
