David Riggle's Summary

For over 25 years I have been helping individuals and business owners build "Great Futures".

My practice focuses on investments, insurance, retirement planning, financial planning, estate planning, business planning and business succession planning.

I am married to my wife, Debi who I am happy to say also works with me at Colorado Financial Planning Group, Inc.

When we are not working, we enjoy skiing in the winter, riding motorcycles and camping in the summer and traveling several times a year.

Colorado Financial Planning Group, Inc. is located at 5251 DTC Parkway, Suite 290, Greenwood Village, CO 80111.

Securities and investment advisory services are offered through Cetera Advisors LLC, Member FINRA/SIPC. Exit planning services are offered through Colorado Financial Planning Group, Inc. Colorado Financial Planning Group, Inc. and Cetera Advisors LLC are not affiliated.

Specialties: Investments and Investment Advisory Services, Retirement Planning, Financial Planning, Estate Planning, Life Disability and Long-Term Care Planning, Business Planning, Exit Planning for Business Owners, Qualified Plan Design and Service, Stock Options and Executive Benefits.
