Jared Redick's Summary

PURPOSE ... CONTENT ... DESIGN

That's the résumé writing approach that compelled a client to say his experience was better than therapy. It's also why a colleague said our work was a "perfect blend of artistry, strategy, and utility."

A high-tech recruiter told one of my clients: "That writer is a sculptor of words." And a global marketing VP said: "Had I not gone through your process, I would have failed the first interview."

I'm Jared Redick, and I specialize in hard-to-write documents for mid-career Fortune 50 professionals and beyond. For 16 years, I’ve taken people on journeys of self-discovery, using résumé writing as a tool for professional self-assessment.

My background in retained executive search, teaching, and nonprofit management has been key to writing on behalf of a lot of fascinating people. But there's also a touch of curiosity and stubbornness at play when I'm chipping away at the details until a client's professional brand starts coming to light.
