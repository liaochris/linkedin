Mandy Shaw's Summary

Mandy Shaw’s wide experience spans development, infrastructure, pre- and post-sales support, quality management, software product management, technical project management, technology strategy, and solutions architecture. Her main areas of interest are the Data Centre, SOA, unified communications, and information management. Throughout her career she has been fascinated by the challenges of making one system or product talk to another in a low-risk way.

She has extensive knowledge and experience of the IBM i platform (previously AS/400, iSeries) as systems engineer, developer, integrator, communications specialist, educator, and technology champion. Since 2000 she has chaired user groups for large UK IBM i users.

Her goal as a strategic architect is to assist the customer in deriving maximum business value from his/her information systems and technology by establishing a 'trusted advisor' relationship, giving unbiased strategic and tactical advice, and interpreting IT to the business and the business to IT. She has been responsible for end-to-end architecture for projects across a wide range of applications and technology areas.
