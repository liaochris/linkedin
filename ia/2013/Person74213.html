Kenneth ("kengon") Gonzalez, CSMP's Summary

I have been providing systems/software design and delivery experience at all organizational levels from individual contributor to executive management since 1984 and bring significant technical & business leadership skills to customers and colleagues.

I provide advisory and technical consulting services that are focused on that area where Service Management, Personal/Organizational Development, and Data Center/Technology initiatives converge. This specific focus helps ensure that client organizations and their personnel are better able to have successful projects and programs by:
• Operating from a customer-centric view;
• Enabling better identification and management of customer requirements;
• Avoiding "Big Bang" projects and focusing on key results;
• Reallocating resources to value producing activities, reducing the need the need for wholesale cuts.

I use proprietary and licensed materials, specific bodies of knowledge, as well as my skills in communication, leadership, cultural change and process design to help drive innovation, business-IT alignment and tangible results/value for customers -- public, private and non-profit. I have experience doing this as both a consultant and internal change agent.

I have been recognized an industry expert and leader in Outside-In Thinking, Lean and Service Management and is regularly invited to write and give presentations internationally. My perspective is valued because of my ability to help practitioners and customers’ alike better understand and apply these principles to deliver value and tangible results.

Specialties

Application of Service Management, Outside-In & Lean Methods; Delivery of Advisory Consulting Services; Intellectual Property Capture and Packaging; Service and Methodology Development; Communication, Leadership and Management Development for Individuals and Organizations; Large Scale Software/Systems Development; Service Desk and Service Catalog; Business Continuity; Disaster Recovery; Governance, Risk and Compliance; Data Center Migration; NIMS, ICS, Mission/Incident Management;
