Amy Woolf's Summary

Amy Woolf is an architectural color consultant, working with both residential and commercial clients on interior and exterior color plans, materials selection and interior design. She graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in Economics and has studied art at Maryland Institute College of Art, Ringling College of Art and Design and Boston Architectural College. Following a a major house renovation, she began her studies with the International Association of Color Consultants - North America in 2006 and has since consulted both nationally and locally. Rather than simply "prescribing" color, Amy's specialty is teaching clients and thereby empowering them to use color more confidently. She currently lives in Northampton, Massachusetts.
