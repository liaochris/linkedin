Benjamin Gaw's Summary

My entry into the web design industry came at an era where there were no study courses about it and connecting to the internet meant that you needed a modem and a phone line. The year was 1996 and I was working as a graphic designer for a small advertising agency specializing in print design. I immediately realized how big the web would be one day so I studied HTML by looking at the page source. I created websites for family and friends and not long after that, was employed full-time at a web design firm.

From HTML to XHTML and now to HTML 5, I have been through it all. I still code my HTML by hand and do it according to the latest web standards and SEO best practices. I have knowledge in several technologies through the years as the web is ever evolving – some of these include classic ASP, Javascript (jquery in particular), XML, Flash and PHP. I also have working experience in Human-to-Computer Interaction (HCI) analysis through multivariate testing.

Apart from that, I’m knowledgeable in audio/video editing using Adobe Premiere. And have used SVN for project development. I also have used Smarty/PHP templates and worked with the Kohana framework in the past. I have working knowledge of how to theme in both Drupal and Wordpress CMS platforms as well.

Specialties

HTML/CSS coding, graphic design and layout, drupal and wordpress themes customization.
