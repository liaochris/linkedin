Joshua Kolbe's Summary

My business has thrived on being willing to do what is necessary to help a client reach a particular goal with very cost-effective solutions. I always tell potential clients that I am willing to be as involved as they want me to be. With some clients I manage every aspect of their technology, being involved in administrative meetings and making plans for keeping their technology running as smooth as possible. Other clients simply call me when something breaks and we still have a wonderful relationship. I always strive to maintain strong relationships with my clients, to the point of friendship with many of them.
