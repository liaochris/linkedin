Kara Bartoy's Summary

For more than a decade, Kara has been a small business owner/operator with a simple passion for developing services that meet her client's interests while investing in the education that meets their ever-changing skin care needs... naturally.

CUSTOM & SPECIALTY BOTANICAL FACIALS: Kara uses and sells Epicuren products which contain pure, natural ingredients; non-chemically derived oxygen, botanicals, and vitamins.

SKIN CARE MAKEUP: In-Studio or On-Location, Kara is a certified jane iredale partner. Known as "The Skin Care Makeup", the jane iredale line is so safe and beneficial that it is recommended by plastic surgeons and dermatologists worldwide.

AIRBRUSH TANNING: Kara uses Vani-T products which offers totally unique 99% natural and organic based spa formula without artificial fragrances, preservatives or other harsh chemicals which can irritate the skin.

NATURAL NAILS - Manicures & Pedicures: Kara uses and sells Zoya Nail Polish which is formulated to deliver the longest wear possible for natural nails. Zoya products are free of toluene, formaldehyde, DBP (phthalates) and camphor.

WAXING: Kara uses both strip and stripless waxes, depending upon the hair and the skin. For your safety, applicators are disposed of after each use ( NO DOUBLE DIPPING ).
