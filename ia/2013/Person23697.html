About NextiraOne...

NextiraOne helps its customers to transform their organisations, making the complex simple by using its expertise in leading-edge communications, including Data Centres, Contact Centres, Unified Communications, Secure Network Infrastructures and International Managed Services. We offer a full range of lifecycle services for advanced communications and IT technology for over 60,000 customers across Europe. NextiraOne has a direct sales and service presence in 16 countries with more than 4,300 employees across Europe and the USA, with revenues of over €1bln.
