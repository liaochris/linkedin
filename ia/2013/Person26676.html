Alison Bickford's Summary

I am Director and Principal Consultant of Connect Thinking Pty Ltd, a proactive and flexible e-learning, learning managememnt system and social media consultancy. Our aim is to:

1. Help orgaisations ask the right questions of themselves and of e-learning and LMS providers
2. Support trainers to design, develop and implement quality e-learning
3. Provide practical advice about e-learning and social media for formal and informal learning

I am also the e-coach for the Connect Thinking E-Learning Academy. The purpose of the Academy is to provide learning professionals who are new to e-learning with practical information, instruction and online support.

I have 12 years experience in organisational e-learning, and have been an independent e-learning consultant for six years. I am an expert in e-learning strategy, e-learning project management and blended learning design. I have expertise in implementing LMS and social media platforms.

Past consulting engagements include: PwC, Cochlear, Water Corporation, AHM, CSC, BAE Systems, BMS, Sanofi, AstraZeneca, Relationships Australia, HCF, CSIRO, Baxter, NSW Trade & Investment, PWCS, Weight Watchers, REINSW, ICAA and others.

Most commonly I am engaged on project work for a few days to several months, helping to implement e-learning strategy, and support learning practitioners in e-learning skill and knowledge development. My workshops are also popular.

Specialties: Analysis, e-learning strategy development, LMS selection & implementation, e-learning governance, blended learning design, social media for learning.

Workshops in: E-Learning strategy, E-Learning design & development, Social media for learning, Virtual Classroom skills, Articulate Studio 09.

I have a Master of Arts in e-Learning.
I am completing a Doctor of Education, thesis title "An examination of virtual learning spaces within a corporate context: Design and practice implications."
