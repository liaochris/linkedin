The Bridge Young Adults Ministry (Volunteer Leader / Minister)

Kenneth Copeland Ministries

Nonprofit; 501-1000 employees; Nonprofit Organization Management industry

August 2011 – August 2012 (1 year 1 month) Fort Worth, Texas

My wife and I launched a successful young adult ministry at Eagle Mountain Church called The Bridge (www.thebridgedfw.com). Before officially holding meetings, we jump started the ministry by creating a leadership team of young adults willing to build from the ground up who were already involved in the church. After about a month or so of meeting with the leadership team, brainstorming effective vision, creative meetings, leadership building activities, and diving into the word together - we launched The Bridge on September 11, 2011.

The Bridge began meeting only once a month holding a service for young adults, but also held activities and groups throughout the month. As the ministry grew, so did the leadership. We watched vision be birthed in the hearts of our leaders and pushed them to run with it. In this process, we adopted a saying from our friend Jack Black and School of Rock… “NO MORE SECRET SONGS”! In other words, we encouraged our team to make The Bridge look like the visions they had in their hearts. This created a team of true leaders and empowered them to really make this ministry run itself.

Our services began with a 2 hour time block. Due to our culture where community needed to be fostered and encouraged rather than occurring naturally, we built specific time into our services for fellowship. I know that sounds strange… but you would have to understand that culture to fully get it. The first hour of service had worship, announcements, and a message. We cut the “fat” that sometimes gets in the process of a service to allow time for community. The second hour was “hang time”. Since fellowship for our culture didn’t just happen - we created an atmosphere where it was encouraged to thrive. Think club - but minus the smoke, alcohol, and dating scene. We used cool lighting, live DJs for ambiance, a free espresso and treat bar, and relaxed seating (couches, benches, cafe style chairs).
