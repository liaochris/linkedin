Damien Franscoise's Summary

I am a driven, ambitious and enthousiastic person and working hard on being a professional photographer. Photography is the perfect combination for me of creativity, technical toys, working with people, being outdoors and entrepreneurship. Being an outdoor person I love to be outside and let nature surprise me with her most beautiful locations and epic light moments. I try to combine that in my wedding- portrait- and travel photography.

Before this I used to work as the manager webshop at the travel bookstore Stanley & Livingstone. In this position it was my task to look for new opportunities for expanding the webshop, maximizing the profit and optimizing the website and it's content.

Previously I worked as a manager of the grouptours at the touroperator Intertrek Hiking and Trekking. I used to handle all the communication with the clients, local agents, airlines and tourleaders. Also I organised the tours and made sure the necessary suplies for the tours were packed. Before that I was a salesman in several outdoor stores where it was my main responsibility to inform clients about the outdoor products they needed for their vacations or expeditions.

I have studied Tourism Management at the NHTV Internationale Hogeschool Breda and graduaded as an entrepreneur with my own learning company.

You can contact me by sending your message to damien@tweemetervijf.nl or you can check out my website at www.tweemetervijf.nl

Specialties

Nature photography, travel photography, wedding photography, portrait photography,
