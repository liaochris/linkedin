Sr. Manager

Silver Lining Capital Group

May 2012 – Present (1 year 7 months) Richmond, Virginia Area

This is the investment arm of Silver Lining Properties. We work with individual Debt and Equity investors to get the most out of their investment in Real Estate Assets.
I match private investors with the right real estate investment. I think of Silver Lining Capital Group as a matchmaking company. I have relationships with real estate investors around the country, who focus on various properties from single family flipping to large commercial and multi-family cash flow. Because I think that matching personalities is important for the private investor/real estate investor relationship we not only work to provide the best property match but put investors together as well.
Our goal is to not only educate the private equity or debt investor about the different kinds of real estate investments but to give them the best investment experience the will ultimately provide them with their desired returns.

Sr. Managing Partner

Silver Lining Properties, LLC

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry

April 2010 – Present (3 years 8 months) Henrico, VA 23229

We are a boutique Real Estate Investment Company.
We purchase distressed single family, multi family and commercial properties,rehab or repair these properties, reposition them or rent to tenants, sell or lease them.
We analyze, structure deals and manage undervalued and under utilized residential and commercial real estate assets so that our investor partners can achieve maximum returns that are not only above average- but sustainable and consistent.
We leverage our relationships and experience so that our investor partners don't have to spend the massive amount of time needed to be successful.
In other words- We put your money to work so that you don't have to.

With the help of our investor partners we are able to help sellers and buyers....so everyone wins!
