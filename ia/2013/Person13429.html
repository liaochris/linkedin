Bernadette Boas' Summary

There is nothing more rewarding than contributing to the growth and success of a business, their people and CEO. For 30+ years I have done just that; for small to corporate size companies in Retail, Technology, Manufacturing, Financial Services, and others, with my exceptional business growth management consulting, leadership coaching, and training expertise.

The main goal of my clients is to drive significant profit, revenue, and organizational improvements by optimizing their people, processes and products. With my out of the box strategies and ball of fire approach to execution, my clients are kept on their toes while we create a challenging, collaborative, and get it done environment for achieving results.

I am also an author; Shedding the Corporate Bitch, Shifting from Bitch to Rich in Life and Business; my apology to the road kill I left behind in corporate, leveraging my own story to change the trajectory of women in business, and to eliminate the excuses, blame game, and good old boy mentalities existing in individuals and company mindsets today.
