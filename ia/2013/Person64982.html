JoAn Risdon's Summary

JoAn Risdon is the dynamic force behind Smart Image Media, a leading marketing and brand strategy firm with its home base in Phoenix, Arizona.

JoAn’s 30 years of experience in business, marketing management, and graphic design enable her to strategically position business and personal brands for measurable growth. She thrives on creating emotional connections between brands and their customers, whether they are individuals, corporate, or non-profit organizations. She is committed to delivering a consistent experience, and her expertise in building trust enables her to bring results forward.

JoAn and her talented team create effective and clear brand messages through strong design and writing for all media. Her team is a unique mix of highly experienced designers, artists, writers, photographers, videographers, and web developers. Staying at the pulse of the latest trends in the industry and customizing work process for each client. Having a distinct understanding of the clients' needs and expectations. Smart Image Media's number one goal is to form long lasting mutually beneficial relationships with their clients. Our company culture is based on innovation, creativity and fun. We are passionate about what we do!

A sought-after speaker, JoAn is passionate about connecting entrepreneurs with their big ideas, which can lead to big results!

Specialties: Developing brand synergy between business and personal brands to create greater momentum and propel your success. We do this by developing authentic brand experience and trust.

Personal Brand + Business Brand = Synergy that propels YOUR success!
