Cyndie Shaffstall's Summary

Spider Trainers is the sum of all the [favorite] parts of my career: entrepreneur, author, marketer, marketing-data analyst, and business manager.

I have been an entrepreneur for more than 30 years, and much of that has been spent in the sales funnel — converting prospects to customers. With the unprecedented visibility offered today through marketing analytics, I’m enjoying the pursuit of data more than ever.

I thrive on implementing marketing initiatives based upon fully automated drip and nurture campaigns and analyzing the metrics that measure their effectiveness in developing stages, trust, and revenue.

If you're interested in automating your marketing, please connect, or visit http://www.spidertrainers.com/resources-2 for white papers and eBooks on the topic.
