Deb Payne's Summary

I have been an instructor/teacher from the elementary grade level to the college level and beyond for 30+ years. I have also been on the other side of textbooks and owned and operated several businesses (entrepreneurs rarely have just one!) as well as a coach/mentor for businesses of all kinds.

My goal is to help others help themselves to become productive, self-sufficient people in a "shaky" world with them knowing that they are working with a woman of integrity and someone who has an enthusiasm for a fulfilling life.


My Personality and Strengths

My personality is fun, engaging, inspirational, and entertaining. I create a fun, learning atmosphere by using humor and asking questions that help clients think deeply and creatively about the desired direction of their business journey.

I utilize many tools to assist clients to learn more about who they are and what drives them. Below you will learn more about what drives me!

Enneagram Personality Profile:

Type: 7, “The Enthusiast” - Visionary, optimistic, and able to channel my energy into creative visions for the future. I love to inspire friends, clients, and strangers to get the most out of life and enjoy every moment.


Gallup StrengthsFinder:

Adaptability- Woo (Entice) - Winning Others Over - Futuristic - Ideation -Includer (See my website for how I use these if you are not familiar with this tool).

I would like to be remembered as a woman who inspired people to courageously face challenges by trusting in their beliefs to create change, transform their lives and reach their destination.


Specialties: According to my students and clients, I am a great listener, visionary, and coach/cheerleader. We Clear the Clutter, Ignite Creativity and Transform Ideas Into Income!!
