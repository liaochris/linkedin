Dr. Kim Kimpel's Summary

Prior to receiving her Doctorate Degree, Dr. Kimpel served in many capacities twice as the Wedding Director of Crystal Cathedral Ministries in Garden Grove California where she personally supervised the marriages of over Four-Hundred Couples. In the year 2000, she was given the position of Executive Assistant to the Ministries Creative Director, Mr. Jim Coleman. And finally from 2003 to 2006, Kim had the pleasure of handling travel planning and logistics for the Founder and Pastor of Crystal Cathedral Ministries, Dr. Robert H. Schuller. During her tenure with Dr. Schuller Kim was in charge of purchasing, logistics, shipping, travel, office management and damage control; you name it, and she's done it successfully. She returned to the Crystal Cathedral after leaving to start and run her own business fulfilling a lifelong dream.

She has also been very effective with business development activities such as fund raising and cultivating solid vendor relationships with many different companies on behalf of her former employers. Corporations such as Hyatt, Allstate/North Brook Insurance and Hilton Hotels have all utilized Kim's poise, energy and multi-tasking skills.

Dr. Kim Kimpel's Experience

Relationship Coach

Marriage Matters More

April 2009 – Present (4 years 9 months) Corona, California

Does your relationship with your significant other feel strained and just not right somehow? Are family members from either side butting in and allowing their non-solicited opinions to be heard? Do you feel a deep dull pain in the pit of your stomach everytime your mate says or does something to criticize or put you down?

Well if you've answered yes to one or more of these questions, then you are suffering from what Dr. Kim Kimpel calls "Red Flags". Many people around the world are afflicted with this self-debilitaing phenomenom. Within the pages of her latest book, "Red Flags" Five Common Warning Signs That Should Make You Pause Before Saying "I Do",
Dr. Kim Kimpel spells out in clear, concise, easy to understand language, the warning signs of "The Red Fag Syndrome" and how you can best avoid it all together.
With class, humour and good old common sense Dr. Kim Kimpel will take you a journey of a lifetime that just might save you and your potential mate from many years of hurt feelings and despair.

You can only make sound descisions if you know what you're dealing with, and Dr. Kim Kimpel gives you plenty to think about in "Red Flags", because it truly is
"Marriage that Matters More".
