Ming Lau’s Summary

With over 20 years of experience in high-tech and software marketing, I have promoted technology for Adobe Systems, and produced marketing communication for Acer, Apple, Cisco, Levis, Intel, Kodak, Macromedia, Microsoft, NEC, SGI, Sony, and CNet, with my own creative marketing company.

With a focus on the new developments in interactive technologies and global marketing, I am looking work with a cutting-edge team that is passionate about new media communication and using interactive technologies to transform business process.

As a Creative Director, I have produced print, motion, video and interactive presentation for many types of corporate communication. Working in high technology drives me to develop new applications and new ways to utilize technology. I have also spent 14 years running and managing a design consulting business so I understand the requirements of corporate decision-making, as well as the need to be profitable.

We live in the most exciting time where knowledge and technology is transforming how we work, live and play. If you are looking for a leader that have a sense of urgency and excitement, that can lead by example, is creative and resourceful, is passionate about inventions and excellence, who will challenge the status quo and reinvent the way, and is driven to achieve world-class results, please contact me.
