Annie Logue’s Summary

I'm a freelance writer and consulting analyst who is fascinated by business and technology. I've written for Barron’s, The New York Times, Newsweek Japan, Compliance Week, and the International Monetary Fund. My current career follows 12 years of experience as an investment analyst.
