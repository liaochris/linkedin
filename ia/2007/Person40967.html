Jet Toh’s Summary

Mr. Jet Toh is the current Director and Founder of Venture Consortium Holdings Pte Ltd. His foray into corporate world started when he was selected from a pool of a few hundred candidates for a key finance position in a large Singapore-based MNC. Within two years, the MNC promoted him to be the Financial Controller of its subsidiary.

He was also part of the MNC's IPO Working Group, responsible for listing the vehicle worth more than 1 billion US dollars. His position requires him to liaise with institutions like UBS Walburg, Morgan Stanley, and Temasek Holdings. At the peak of his corporate career, Jet left his high paying position to start his first stint as an entrepreneur. He joined WBG Holdings, a top-notch company dealing with biotechnology food products. He is remembered as the builder of a marketing team that generated more than US$20 million turnover within the first few years. He also took the company regional and subsequently target to list the company on The Singapore Exchange (SGX) in 2008.

Mr. Jet Toh is also a shareholder and global trainer with GSS Training Academy. To date, this training entity has helped trained more than 10,000 entrepreneurs worldwide. Among the profiles of trainees are businessmen from Japan, Korea, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Hong Kong and China. Jet believes in helping others unleash their potential to the fullest, so that more can be achieved in their life time.

Mr. Jet Toh graduated with honors degree in B.Acc and was short listed in the privileged Dean's List. In his National Service days, he was voted Best Tank Commander during the Tank Commander Course. He got his early education from the reputable Chinese High School in Singapore. Mr. Jet Toh believes strongly in his alma mater's credo, which is win-win. Plus his strong learning capability, all these have helped him perform well in whichever industry Venture Consortium Holdings dwells into.
