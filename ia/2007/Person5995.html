Andy Dunn’s Summary

My passion is for making people happier, smarter or better off. I gravitate to consumer businesses - and have been active in the consumer world as an entrepreneur, a wannabe venture capitalist, a private equity chimmel and a data crunching consultant.

I am most excited about business model or technological innovations which create lasting value. In May, I became the Co-Founder of Bonobos. In July of 2007, I became the CEO. Bonobos is at the intersections of several of my passions - consumer, technology, creativity, and the internet. (Of course, I'd rather be playing second base for the Cubs.)
