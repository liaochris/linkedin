Raj Abhyanker’s Summary

Raj Abhyanker is a U.S. patent attorney focusing on U.S. patent preparation, prosecution, and due diligence litigation support. Raj has been registered with the United States patent bar since November 1999.

Raj has drafted and prosecuted United States patent applications for companies including Texas Instruments, Apple Computer, Inc., eBay, Adobe Systems, Ario Data Networks, Aesthera, Intel, Redback Networks, SonicWALL, Openwave Communications, Ariba, Ask Jeeves, and numerous startups.
