Cristina Melo’s Summary

After 5 years in www.activamultimedia.com I have recently joined Plot 4 (www.plot4.com) as partner and Strategic Planning.

Plot4 is a Strategic and Creative Consultancy Agency that works with Brands, Agencies, Broadcasters, Telephone & Broadband Operators and Technology Companies.

Plot 4 Limited was funded in London in October 2003, today it is based in London, Madrid, Barcelona and Milan.

Plot 4 Mission: Develop Strategies and Ideas to succeed in the Digital World

We are focused into media convergence, mainly in DTV - iTV, integrating also mobile & personal computer providing integrated communication strategies.

Plot4 provides research and strategy through out Europe. Major clients include: Astra (Brussels HQ, Belgium), Sky (UK & Italy), Orange (UK), RTVE (Spain), Digital Plus (Spain)

We believe in the power of ideas rooted in great consumer insights
Our philosophy is that Everything is Possible
