Brian Brown’s Summary

Started Identity Developments in 2003 as a web standards based web design company to serve the needs of small businesses. From the beginning, the foundation has always been about more than just web design. I believe that a website is an extension of the business and brand identity. The growth into Search Marketing, especially with a focus on Search Engine Optimization (SEO), has been a natural fit.

I manage all aspects of the business including client management, business development, web design and search engine optimization.

Identity Developments has always been about a holistic web presence solution. Today, most projects pull from all areas of focus, providing clients with a custom solution for their needs based on a SEO focused web design.

Brian Brown’s Specialties:

SEO focused, XHTML / CSS web design. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Marketing. Internet marketing and consulting. Keyword research.

New product development, product management, product marketing.
