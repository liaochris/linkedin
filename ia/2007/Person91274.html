I work with VCs, BoDs, and CXOs to recruit the top 5% in Executive Decision Makers, Revenue Drivers, and IP Builders. I recruit CXOs, VPs, Directors, and Principal Architects for wireless and mobile content vendors who are startups and high-growth companies in:
• Product Management

• Executive Management Consulting
• High-Level Sales, Business Development, and Product Marketing

I recruit in North America, Europe, and Asia in the following vertical markets:
• wireless infrastructure - CDMA, GSM, WiMax, smart antenna, mesh networks
• wireless voice and data - VoIP, Bluetooth, PoC, enterprise data
• fixed mobile convergence - IMS
• mobile handset chipsets and embedded software applications
• mobile content - audio, video, IPTV, MMS

Potential Candidates:
If you are an exceptional talent with more than 10 years experience combined with a proven track record of accomplishments in the wireless industry, AND your peers consider you to be among the top 5% in the industry, perhaps we can talk.

Hiring Managers: I work on only 1 to 2 C-level and retained level searches at a time. You receieve ONLY the top 5% - which means 2-3 candidates that are ideal - no flypaper.

Confidentiality and Trust – on both sides of the fence. I don't reveal the candidate name or contact info to a client nor will I reveal the company name to a candidate until I am absolutely sure both parties are interested in each other’s capabilities, track record, and opportunity..

I've been fortunate to be interviewed for several publications, including RCR Wireless, Big Biller, IT World, Computer World, CNET, Wireless Review, Mobile Commerce Times, and M-Business Daily.
