Brian Bowman’s Summary

A skilled general manager with more than 16 years of experience in online product strategy, product management and marketing, Bowman has held executive-level positions at several leading online consumer companies including Yahoo, Match.com and The Walt Disney Company.

He is currently the EVP of Marketing and Business Development for Reply.com. He is responsible for establishing Reply.com as a consumer brand, overseeing the expansion of new vertical businesses and helming online customer acquisition and channel development.

Prior to Reply, Bowman served as VP Community for Yahoo.com, where he led global planning, positioning and product development. While at Yahoo, he was responsible for all aspects associated with Yahoo’s online community applications (360, Groups, Photos and Avatars) and platforms (Blogs, Ratings & Reviews, Reputation, Message Boards). He is one of very few people to have created more than one multi-million member online community, and has spent the past 10 years developing and running products based around member interaction and social networking.

Previously, Bowman was VP of Marketing & Product Management for InfoSpace.com where he led product vision, planning and marketing for the local, web and mobile search products. Before that, he oversaw Match.com’s product strategy and led the company to significant revenue and registration increases. Prior to his tenure with Match.com, he served as VP and General Manager for The Walt Disney Company’s ABC Television Network and as Director of New Media for the company’s Disney Channel. Bowman has also overseen the development of many award-winning websites, including: Oscars.com, ZoogDisney.com, Moviso.com and "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire.”

Brian graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Fine Art from Purdue University, sits on advisory boards for AffinityCircles.com and CircleBuilder.com and resides in the San Francisco Bay Area.
