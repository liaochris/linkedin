Alan Hoshor’s Summary

Alan Hoshor has worked in the computer industry for much of his life. He started manufacturing computer components, advanced to programming, then assembly of computers, design of computers, networking, management of software development, and finally information systems management. Alan has worked in large industry, most recently: Engineering Services Manager at Motorola, Senior Development and Operations management at Microsoft. 10 years ago, Alan created Hoshor Systems Consulting to service small businesses in Snohomish County, Washington; but now lives and works in Jefferson County. To keep fit, Alan still runs about 20 miles a week. He also tries to keep up-to-date with all things computer....an impossible task. He is excited about the possibilities for SBS, the best product Microsoft has ever provided to small businesses at a very reasonable price.
