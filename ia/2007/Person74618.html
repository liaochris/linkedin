Roy Kelley’s Summary

Professional real estate services in the Maryland suburbs of Washington DC. Exclusive representation for buyers and sellers of residential real estate. Advising owner-occupant and investor purchasers and sellers. Licensed since 1960. Experienced in all aspects of residential real estate. Rainmaker for Roy Kelley & Associates.

Client services include free email links to the monthly Realty Times newsletter and free weekly lists of pre-foreclosures and REO properties in Montgomery County and Frederick County, MD. Expert advice on Maryland trustee sales transactions and foreclosures. We track all of the trustee sales in Montgomery County. We email monthly real real estate activity reports on six counties in our market area.

We welcome requests for referrals to lenders and title companies that provide the best service in our market.
