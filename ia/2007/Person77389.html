Scott Kiere’s Summary

Scott Kiere is Co-Founder and Vice-Chair of MECA Communications, Inc. (www.meca.com). MECA is an incubation and marketing company, reinventing itself through optimized marketing strategies as applied through merger and aquisition to build ever increasing returns for shareholders and investors.

Scott sees real-time communication, new educational models and arts & culture as the essential vehicles to express his vision of connecting people world-wide through a three bottom-line philosophy of business (People, Planet and Profit). Scott is considered a true Spiritual Entrepreneur mixing ancient wisdom with pragmatic business acumen and is often referred to as a Visionary Pragmatist.

To this end, Scott serves as a member the Board of Advisors for Social Venture Network, (www.svn.org) a not-for-profit organization with a mission to inspire a community of business and social leaders to build a just economy and sustainable planet.

Scott is also Founder, Chairman and President of Liberation, Interdependence, Freedom and Empowerment (L-I-F-E) Foundation, Inc., (www.OneLIFE.Us) a Not-for-Profit 501(c)3 Corporation which raises money for children’s educational scholarships through artistic and cultural projects and events.

"When a true journeyman has heart visions of what is over that mountain, through that porthole, and under the crying sky. Then what he expresses are foundation maps weaved of hope, love and magic."
