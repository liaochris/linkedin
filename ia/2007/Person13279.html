Kimberly Silk’s Summary

Kim has been working and playing online since 1990, and has over fifteen years of product management and marketing experience in the Interactive Media industry. She has a particular passion for digital collections and online products and services. As a librarian, she has a unique understanding of how to provide the right information to the right audience at the right time, with a creative flair that makes a lasting impact.

Prior to launching BrightSail, Kim spent more than three years with Rogers Communications, most recently as Director, Strategic Internet Development at Rogers Media. At Rogers, Kim worked with the New Media and Publishing divisions to develop Internet ventures from concept to market.

Prior to joining Rogers, Kim was Webmaster for Discovery Channel Canada. As the first Canadian specialty channel to launch an interactive online component, Discovery pioneered the use of the Internet as a unique way to offer compelling online content, video and interactive games and forums to engage the television audience.

Kim earned her M.L.S. (Library Science) from the University of Toronto, and her B.A. in English Literature from the University of Waterloo.
