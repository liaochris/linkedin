Leilani Shimoda’s Summary

I am currently freelancing for Modern Amusement, helping create tech packs for the men's Holiday line. I will be be designing into the women's line come Spring 2008.

I just left Lucky Brand as a designer in contemporary women's cut-and-sew knits and graphics. Previous experience with junior intimates and loungewear line, Honeydew Intimates. I design, souce fabric and trim, import and domestic, design all graphics, create prints and all over repeats, develop dye techniques, proficient in CAD Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

Strength and passion for contemporary women's wear, knit fashion tops and bottoms, intimates and loungewear and graphics.

Leilani Shimoda’s Specialties:

Illustrator, Photoshop, Mac/PC, technical, specs, CAD and hand drawn flats and Illustration, import/domestic, tech packs, graphic design, storyboards, trend reports. I love fashion and can find inspiration in any story. I'm clever and creative and always have ideas. I am also nice and a good team member.
