Rüdiger Paschotta’s Summary

Dr. Rüdiger Paschotta has an international reputation as an expert in research areas such as lasers and amplifiers, nonlinear optics, fiber technology, ultrashort pulses, and noise in optics. He is author or coauthor of around 100 articles in scientific journals, several book chapters and a large number of other publications. Also, he is the author of the well known Encyclopedia of Laser Physics and Technology.

Despite a successful academic career, he decided to start a consulting company, offering helpful services to companies in the photonics industry. Since mid 2005, he is fully focusing on this business, serving companies mostly in Europe and in the United States. He is still actively involved in research in order to stay fully up to date, but is enjoying to work mainly on industrial projects.
