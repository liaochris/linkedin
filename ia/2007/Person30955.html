Gary Parker’s Summary

Based in Montreal, Canada, the Airline Revenue Management Training Group provides practical, results-oriented revenue management (RM) training to professionals working for airlines and other travel-related industries around the world.

We design and deliver interactive RM training programs, ranging from half-day workshops to five-day courses and distance learning, covering a wide range of topics. In partnership with the International Air Transport Association (IATA) through its Aviation Training & Development Institute (ATDI), we present RM training courses, as listed in the IATA course calendar. In addition, we can create a custom-tailored RM training program that meets the needs of an individual company, and then present it in-house.

We constantly revise our training materials, so that they reflect the most up-to-date developments in the RM field.

We have already instructed hundreds of travel-industry professionals - from middle managers to chief executive officers - who have benefited from learning how to apply RM theory to achieve results.

Our lecturers are experienced practitioners who are passionate about transmitting their knowledge of RM to others. Gary is a frequently invited speaker at industry conferences and workshops.

You can reach us at: info@rmtraininggroup.com
