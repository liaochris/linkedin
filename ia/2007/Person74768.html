Raj Shahani’s Summary

I've spent the 10 years studying technology, human motivation, psychology, and marketing and 5 years working in technical, marketing, and sales roles at startups and Fortune 500 companies.

I've designed wireless networks, written code, created sales and marketing plans, presented to small and large audiences, and have picked up the phone and turned strangers thousands of miles away into friends, and friends into loyal customers.

It didn't take me long to figure out that that working for someone else is like driving a Ferrari on a small suburban street. I was born to be an entrepreneur.

My primary line of business now is online traffic brokering. I design and implement sophisticated traffic generation campaigns that bring the right people to the products and services online using 1) search engine marketing (I used to specialize in this at Yahoo) and 2) my ability to write hard-hitting, ass-grabbing, hypnotic sales copy that causes people to excitedly and uncontrollably start buying.

I also enjoy information marketing. I consult with experienced professionals that have specialized knowledge in their field and help turn what they know into an online empire. We create digitally-delivered informational products that generate an automated profit channel and market our partners as subject matter authorities. My next availability is Spring 2008.

I am also passionate about professional speaking, social entrepreneurship, and philanthropy. I have a vision for a multinational social enterprise that helps talented people in under served communities by empowering them with the knowledge, life skills, and tools to create extraordinary lives that make a difference in the world.

This is still in early research and development, and I welcome committed individuals to my network that have similar goals.
