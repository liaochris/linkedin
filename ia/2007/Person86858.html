Tommy Oddo’s Summary

A hands-on creative leader and new media pioneer with over 15 years of professional design experience-- from full-time, agency-side employment by 3 "Communication Arts Magazine" featured design firms; to owning and running a graphic design firm for 9 consecutive years; to full-time, client-side employment by a national, high-tech, consumer brand.

I utilize a user-centered design philosophy that relies on predefined strategic objectives and success metrics to create relevant, attractive, and highly-functional designs that take advantage of all that the web has to offer.

My work has been featured in several books, including "Shockwave Studio: Designing Multimedia for the Web", "Easy Lingo for Macromedia Director", "Print's Best Booklets and Brochures", and others.
