Petra Keinonen’s Summary

IMS Talent (www.imstalent.com)
We offer a wide scope of HR solutions including management audits, coaching, in-house hr managers, hr in m&a, and most importantly from my point of view: search (to those unfamiliar with the term: search=headhunting).

IMS Talent - Search
We focus on finding the most suitable individuals for positions defined by our client companies. We are specialized in executive search, management search and key specialist search. Our candidates are the kind of individuals who drive success and our clients are the kind of people in organizations who can appreciate that.

Search vs ad-based recruitment
The benefit of Search in contrast to ad-based recruitment is that all resources are focused on proactively finding a candidate whose competence matches the job-profile and whose character matches the work-environment.
