Andrew Doak’s Summary

A month before I earned my BFA in Advertising Photography (with a concentration in Digital Media) from Rochester Institute of Technology, I was offered a position at Swales Aerospace as Head Photographer. I had interned at Swales the Winter break prior and made a solid impression. My responsibilities were not limited to photography, they also included print design, interactive multimedia, website design & development, emerging technologies and client relationships. Swales main contract was Goddard Space Flight Center (NASA). I received multiple awards from NASA for my works on multiple projects.

With the desire to expande my online experience, I transitioned to a .com agency in Baltimore: e.magination. I spent a year doing web programming, flash design and development, email campaigns, site design, and digital architecture.

I then moved to Digex, a high-end hosting provider with a client list including Martha Stewart.com & Ford.com. I was hired as Senior Creative to aid in portal design, internal branding, prototype application design and usability. I received the Tier1 award for usability and design function beating competition the likes of Microsoft, LoudCloud & RackSpace.

Following Digex I transitioned to H&R Block as Head Interactive Design. I was responsible for all main online properties including hrblock.com & taxcut.com. I worked with a fantastic team. We made dreams happen.

I left Block to be Vice President of Interactive for Two West, a Marketing & Branding agency here in town...
