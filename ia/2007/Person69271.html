Prashant Kothari’s Summary

I am the CEO & Founder of String Real Estate Information Services (www.stringinfo.com).

String is the only offshoring firm focused exclusively on the mortgage and settlement service industries in North America. This singular focus is aimed at delivering value to every client through extraordinary execution and commitment, as attested by String’s client roster and growth -- String is the fastest growing firm in its space.

HQ: Washington, DC
Operations Centers: India.

As CEO, I'm focused on overall direction and strategy with a specific focus on marketing and client relationship.

I've spoken at several industry conferences, including the MBA (mbaa.org), the ALTA (alta.org), the RESPRO (respro.org) and numerous local conferences. I'm fairly well known within the mortgage industry as (ahem) an outsourcing expert.

I've also given briefings on this subject to congressional staffers on the Hill, at the National Press Club in Washington, DC, at various think tanks in the US and in India, and at other fora, and been featured in articles in the regular press (Washington Post and other mainstream media) as well as trade publications.

Before String, I worked for 13 years in the American mortgage industry in various roles – on Wall Street’s #1 mortgage trading desk and in the Office of the President at Freddie Mac.

I started my career in the diamond and jewelry business back in 1988, when I set up India’s first modern jewelry manufacturing factory.

Little-known factoid: Over 80% of the wholesale diamond trade globally passes through a handful of trading firms from my hometown.

My passion, apart from String, is teaching and writing. I've taught or spoken at American University's Kogod School of Business, the University of Virginia, the University of Madras and at the Great Lakes School of Management.

I'm also an amateur economist and writer, and a founder of the #1 blog on the Indian Economy (www.indianeconomy.org).
