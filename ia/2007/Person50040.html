W. Brandon Phillips <lashbackllc@aim.com>’s Summary

After leaving a position of Chief Sales Officer at 321Studios in 2002, I founded the online advertising company Rdestiny LLC. We quickly received a seed round investment from Flywheel Ventures in Santa Fe, MN. Purchases back sole ownership of Rdestiny in 2003.

Launched LashBack, an anti-spam organization with an emphasis on building "Reputation" services and technologies that analyzes junk email and build CAN-SPAM compliance profiles on all online advertisers. Currently LashBack has a patent pending with several claims to unique processes to identify advertisers. In addition, we have developed the only proven method to safely manage and automate the unsubscribe process.
