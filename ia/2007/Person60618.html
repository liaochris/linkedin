Matthew Milan’s Summary

Manic thinking at the convergence of spatial, digital, social and physical environments. Focused on developing compelling user experiences that leverage these frameworks and create new emergent environments.

Ignoring the goofy boilerplate, I like working in small teams of people who aren't afraid to break the rules when that's what's needed.

My current area of interest is the development of strategic design tools that help practitioners understand the complex adaptive systems that frame the development of large scale web environments.
