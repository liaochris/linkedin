Ken Hicks’s Summary

“Get Ken!” That’s what my employers and colleagues have tended to say when a significant business opportunity or difficult problem has arisen.

Perhaps it’s because I have a reputation as an accomplished internal management consultant and change agent…turning around a challenging situation…or inspiring a greater team effort. Possibly it’s because I am quick to see opportunities and solutions.

I've had responsibility for many high-impact process improvement projects that span from analysis and process design, through project implementation and support.

I have analyzed and improved functions including accounts receivable, accounts payable, call centers, procurement, sales, back-office processing, human resources, labor scheduling, collections, sales commissions, customer service, information technology, operations, facilities, manufacturing, and compliance.

I have experience working with the Clarify customer relationship management (CRM) application suite, Microsoft Visio and Micrografx Optima for workflow documentation and simulation, Serena TeamTrack and ExtraView for change management, and technologies including ACD/VRU, CTI, and predictive dialers.

My portfolio of accomplishments includes Staffing Models, Process Workflows, Business Cases, Feasibility Studies, Gap Analysis, Strength and Weakness Assessments, Pattern Identification, and Controls Design. I have successfully conducted Benchmark Studies, Metrics, Key Performance Indicators, Workflow Simulations, and Economic Justifications.

I am a hard-charging, innovative internal management consultant and process improvement professional. I learn quickly and direct, motivate, and coordinate others to maximum effort.

I promise to interest you with my ideas and suggestions, which could be profitable for you.
