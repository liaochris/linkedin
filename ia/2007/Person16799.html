Christan Zantboer’s Summary

During the last twenty years Christan Zantboer (44) made a successful career in all kinds of Customer Service related activities and processes, as well as in Lean and Six Sigma in the transactional environment. He started with a big chemical american business. There he became Customer Services manager with the challenge to centralize CS activities in the Netherlands. Because of his experience Christan was asked to join the implementation of Six Sigma in '96, followed by a leading role in the implementation of a new ERP-system. Analyzing and improving workflows / pricing standards / efficiency are a few of his special qualifications.

Looking for new challenges Christan switched for a few years to a small consulting agency where he looked into the kitchen of various other businesses. In daily practice he found out that the environment of bigger organizations, suits him much better. There he was able to develop his own capacities like coaching and motivating employees and staff members, ao in the Commercial Excellence environment. With results like: better use of the ERP solutions, more insight in the sales activities, use Voice of the Customer, Customer Dashboarding and Lean Six Sigma project approach.

People-management as well as the soft-skills parts of projects are Christans' favourite tasks within his profession. By nature he likes to guide companies through critical phases, making changes that matter. He is experienced with directly as well as indirectly leading departments of organizations as well is being member of the management team.

Nowadays he is responsible for parts of Lean Six Sigma, related to implementing this methodology, training Greenbelts, guiding projects, and the setup of Customer Service and Distribution department at a fast growing JV in the food industry 50/50 American/Dutch.
