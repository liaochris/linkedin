Mitch Skyer’s Summary

Mitch Skyer has been a sales professional since 1993. During that time his career has included direct business to business sales, national sales management, sales operations development, and strategic planning. His experience spans the telecom, high tech, management consulting, project management, security, and transportation industries. He’s worked for organizations on Fortune’s 100 Largest Companies list, as well as small startup operations. He’s sold to, and managed relationships with, organizations such as Coca Cola, BlueCross BlueShield, BellSouth (now AT&T), Farmland Industries, Pfizer, Lincoln Properties, Georgia Institute of Technology, The University of Alabama, Koch Industries, Georgia State University, the University at Buffalo, the Robert W. Woodruff Library, and North Carolina State University.

Mitch is the owner and president of Solstice Business Communications (www.solsticebci.com). His organization works to Build Salesforce Greatness by providing Salesforce Management Expertise for small and mid-sized B2B service based companies. They focus on diagnosing and repairing challenges within an organization’s sales processes. Mitch earned his Bachelor of Arts degree in History and Law & Society from Binghamton University, in Binghamton, NY (SUNY), and his MBA, concentrating in Marketing Management, from the University at Buffalo, in Buffalo, NY (SUNY). Mitch and his family currently reside in Atlanta, GA. Mitch is on the Customer Advisory Board for ACT! (Sage Software), and is a proud member of the National Association of Sales Professionals. Solstice Business Communications follows all NASP established Standards of Professional Conduct.
