Marco Donati’s Summary

Marco Donati is graduated in Architectural Design, and has completed three years of studies in Information Science at Milan Study University.

He launched his career in new media at Mediola (video games), before becoming Art Director (managing a team of 15) in Datanord Multimedia (since renamed Fullsix, and now floated on the Italian stock exchange). He was also a Researcher at Milan University's Engineering Polytechnic.

In 1999, Marco Donati founded MADD, an innovative Milanese web agency with a workforce of 40 professionals. He was Art Director and General Manager of the company.

After this experience, he has focused on providing consultancy services on creative design, marketing and project management [as freelance and for company as E-One S.r.l.].
Over the past decade, Marco Donati has managed a range of innovative projects in the fields of web design, product design and advertising. Now works in his two studios in Milan and London
