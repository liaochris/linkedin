Justin Tomlinson’s Summary

I am an executive with sixteen years experience in business management and IT based change delivery. My grounding is in technical project and programme management, which has afforded me the opportunity to work in several industry sectors and many different cultures. Through these experiences I have gained a broad insight into both operational and change management within product and service based companies.

Recently I have enjoyed the opportunity to build and lead large IT operations and delivery teams responsible for the implementation and management of multiple high profile projects. These positions have afforded me the ability to work closely with my peers in Sales, Marketing, Finance, HR and Customer Services resulting in a more holistic approach to change management. In this context I enjoy the chance to define and implement strategy that compliments and enables the goals of the business whilst delivering measurable benefits. I have a strong interest in delivery methodology and have become an advocate of value driven practices such as Agile development and release methods. Implemented diligently, I believe these methods can be extremely effective and beneficial to the business.

My drive comes from a passion for constant self-improvement both in and out of work. I thrive on the satisfaction of delivery and relish the challenge of creating and leading effective teams. I also gain satisfaction from implementing processes and technology that support the need for control but also allow room for creativity.

It is vital to me to work in a motivated, positive environment and to play an active role in the definition of company and implementation strategy.
