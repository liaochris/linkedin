Nigel Hall’s Summary

I specialize in developing marketing white papers for vendors of enterprise storage solutions. My clients include Hitachi Data Systems, Symantec (Veritas), McDATA, EMC, and a host of smaller companies. I write exclusively about storage technologies and I believe the service I offer is unique in the industry.

I approach writing white papers from a perspective that few other writers can match. For 12 years, I worked in enterprise IT and I am intimately familiar with the target market and the recipients of storage vendor marketing messages. In addition to a technical background, I have several years experience as a product marketer working for vendors of enterprise IT technology. My ability to blend marketing messages and technical content into a cohesive, credible, and readable story is a result of this combined technical and marketing experience. I have been writing storage white papers full-time for six years and I can guarantee the successful delivery of quality marketing materials that clearly communicate your message to readers.

Visit http://www.parallelprose.com to view examples of the work I provide.
