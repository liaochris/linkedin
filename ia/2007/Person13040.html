Brian Walker’s Summary

Wide ranging experience focused on direct to consumer eCommerce customer experience, merchandising, marketing, and technologies. In the past few years I have focused delivering simple, effective, and innovative customer experience through web-services based solutions. I have enjoyed playing roles both as client and as vendor of these services, both in sales-related roles, as well as in defining the requirements and negotiating large relationships with vendors representing a variety of eCommerce related technologies and services. I believe that in large complex and matrixed organizations simple and clear processes with wide participation lead to the best results, such as a highly collaborative customer experience project where bringing many disciplines and points-of-view together and then developing and testing alternatives will typically lead to the best result.
