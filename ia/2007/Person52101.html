Lori Dernavich’s Summary

We're looking for trouble!

Companies call Step Ahead Consulting when their executives or teams are struggling. Specializing in executive coaching to develop management and leadership skills, improve communication, establish accountability, set professional growth plans in motion, etc.

Background: Master’s degree in Counseling Psychology, has traveled extensively as a corporate leadership coach and trainer for the UNICCO Service company, and spent several years serving as a senior recruiting consultant in the high tech industry. Lori is a Certified Professional Behavior Analyst and specializes in the administration and interpretation of the DISC and PIAV assessments.

Partial list of clients includes Absolut Spirits Co., Legal Sea Foods, Brigham & Women’s Hospital, Dover Electronics, Pillar Financial, Vectron International, IBM (MRO Software), Slade Gorton & Co., EMC, JNPhillips Auto Glass, Ver-Tex Construction Specialists, and Lewtan Technologies.

- Founding Member of 1000 Women for Mentoring of Mentoring.org
- Member of the Boston College Chief Executives’ Forum
- Member of Step Up Women's Network
- Member of Cosmetic Executive Women, Inc.
- Member of the Northeast Human Resources Association
- Former Board Director and the VP of Membership for NESAP (New England Society for Applied Psychology)
- Published in SmartMoney magazine, MassHighTech, Women's Business Boston, and HR Executive Magazine.
