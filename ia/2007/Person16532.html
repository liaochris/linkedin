Chris Houchens’s Summary

Chris Houchens is a marketing speaker, writer, blogger, and owner of Shotgun Concepts, a marketing firm he founded in 1997 to provide guidance on marketing strategy to today's forward thinking companies.

Chris has a long history of working in both media and marketing in the broadcasting, online, print, and healthcare fields. He currently focuses his energy in delivering marketing keynotes and other presentations nationwide to conferences, meetings, and private corporate events.

Chris was the reviewer and wrote the foreword for the 2006 book, “A Marketer’s Guide to HIPAA: Resources for Creating Effective and Compliant Marketing” and he is a co-author of “Achieving Customer Mindshare through Advertising”, which was released in the summer of 2007.

Chris’ blog, the Shotgun Marketing Blog, is ranked on Advertising Age's Power 150 of the top marketing blogs in the world.
