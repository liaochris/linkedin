Tom Heruska’s Summary

IT Management Consultant and Solutions Architect with over 15 years of leadership experience in advanced technology software organizations. Specialize in bridging the gap beteween business unit stakeholders and IT, working closely with executives to ensure business goals are achieved. Have led numerous projects to success at organizations such as Sprint, Beal Bank, the Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas, CSG Investments, Frito Lay, AdvancePCS, and ClubCorp. Significant experience in the financial services sector, having led multiple system selection and implementation initiatives in banking and capital markets environments.
