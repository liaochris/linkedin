Michael Perry Ret. USAF’s Summary

I am an experienced manager, who's expertise has been establishing new, or revamping non-performing organizations requiring adherence to US Military, US Federal and International Policies, Regulations and Guidelines. I’ve often been sent in with little or no support to establish operational organizations in regions and countries where communications was limited and logistical support was non-existent. My forte has been bringing together “Tip of the Spear” teams of professionals, engineers, technicians and support personnel into cohesive units that are mission oriented and extremely competitive with their more comfortable counterparts back home.

I understand “Tech-Speak” “Militareze” and “Business-Speak” Terminologies and I know the Balance Factors associated between Risk and Opportunity, whether in Combat conditions or Business Strategy. I am as comfortable speaking with executives, managers, audit or regulatory members as I am with professionals, engineers, technicians and support personnel.

I haven't had a lot of jobs like many; but those I have had, sent me many different places - to which I was more than willing to go. My childhood dream was to roam the world, and still is.

I have visited and/or lived in many different countries and am a quick study on cultures and foreign business. I bring a network of associates and peers with whom I interact and keep in contact; Investment Banking, Military, IT Security and an array of others throughout the world.

I am not rooted in any spot and am available to travel or be posted wherever the business needs me. I am single and while I would prefer a USA position, I will be where the company needs me most.

Most of my technical training was accomplished On-The-Job and thru self study courses, with a few formal certification courses. I am a quick study on all IT technologies and continue research in my industry.
