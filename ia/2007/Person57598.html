Dr. Sarah Meharg’s Summary

Post-Conflict Reconstruction expert with Ph.D. focused on reconstructing culture after armed conflict. Specialization in working with the military on reconstruction and stabilization operations in places like Afghanistan. Seeking consulting contracts with military, humanitarian organizations, and development agencies.

Dr. Sarah Meharg’s Specialties:

Cultural heritage preservation during pre-and post-conflict periods; landscape architecture; university professor; research analyst; civilian-military liaison; Balkan expert; post-war reconstruction project manager.
