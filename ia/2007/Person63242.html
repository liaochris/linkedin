Niandong Wang’s Summary

Niandong is Chief Marketing Officer of United ITV, Inc (UiTV), a leading internet media company invested by top-tier US venture capital firms,. In this position, he oversees marketing, online video ads sales, and strategic planning and brings over 10 years of working experience in IT, Internet and online media to the company.

Most recently, he was with Microsoft headquarter as a product manager in one of the fastest-growing businesses at Microsoft, the Security Business Unit. He was managing business partnerships and driving global marketing strategies for emerging security technologies.

Prior to that, he spent a year as a senior business development manager for Chinadotcom, the very first Chinese internet company listed at NASDAQ.

Before that, he was with IBM China for six years. During his tenure, Niandong was instrumental in introducing, building, leading and growing software marketing and e-business services business in China.

Niandong holds an MBA degree from Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley, and a BSEE with honor from Gifted Youth Program at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

When he's not staring at LCD monitor, Niandong enjoys writing, hiking, travelling and checking out great concerts and decent restraurants with his family and friends.
