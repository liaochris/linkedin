Jeffrey L. Taylor’s Summary

Barnes & Thornburg is one of the 100 largest law firms in the United States offering clients virtually all areas of concentration one would expect in a large, full-service firm. We have more than 50 dedicated practice and industry areas to serve clients – nationally and internationally.

The firm includes a full bipartisan U.S. federal government relations lobbying group which offers clients legislative and regulatory representation before the U.S. Congress and the Executive branch departments and regulatory agencies. We have advised governors, senators, and congressmen from both political parties on policy and political matters and we offer insights as to how the U.S. government operates and what committees, departments, agencies and people are key to solving problems.

We represent businesses (U.S. and international), corporations, state and local governments, trade associations, charitable foundations, international companies, and foreign governments (Embassy).

In International Affairs, we assist foreign governments (Embassies) and companies interact with the U.S. Departments of State, Defense, Commerce, U.S. Trade Representatives Office and The White House.

Other practice areas include Corporate Law (Business Transactions and Counseling), Energy, Telecommunications and Utilities, Environmental, Global Services (International Business and compliance matters), Intellectual Property (patents, trademarks, copyrights, PCT), Life Sciences, Technology, Litigation, Healthcare, Contracts, Labor / Employment Law, Entrepreneurial Services, Financial Institutions, Tax, Immigration and Sports / Entertainment Law.
