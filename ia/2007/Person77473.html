Scott Place LION splace@maverickendeavors.com’s Summary

Our company has successfully produced over 750 sales and marketing programs for clients in the Southeastern US and the United Kingdom. We have extensive experience with early stage and rapidly growing companies in several industries including software, hardware and life science.

Maverick was deveveloped to offer companies strategic marketing and sales operations services that need results, but don't need full-time employees. We were recognized as a market leader in 2004 by our selection to Seth Godin's Bull Market Directory of 400 companies globally that make things happen in a changing world.

My goal for Maverick is not to be the largest consulting company or agency, but to be the best at what we do, the fastest at what we do and the most effective at what we do.

I am also serving on the Grants Board for NC IDEA (www.ncidea.org) as an advisor to the fund's management. The Grants Board evaluates very early stage companies for grant funding as assistance to help them get to seed or series A funding.

When I'm not working, I am either having fun with my very signficant other and 3 soon to be 5 kids. I also try to recapture my youth as a soccer goalie in a recreation league and pretend to be Lance Armstrong on my bike.
