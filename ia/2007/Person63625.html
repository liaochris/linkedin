Nicole Paustian’s Summary

I joined BCG Copenhagen in February 2003 as an Associate Consultant. In October 2004, I was promoted to Senior Consultant (ahead of schedule). In October 2006 I was promoted to Project Leader (my current position).

In BCG I have mostly worked for clients within consumer goods and retail industries. However, I have also worked for clients in the public sector and industrial companies. The projects have had varying focuses. Below is a short list with examples of some of the projects I have done:

- Fundraising strategy for one of Denmarks largest non-profit organization
- Strategic due diligence projects with assessments of target companies
- Growth strategy project for large international clothing manufacturer
- Organisation project for large Nordic retailer
- Logistics benchmarking project for international consumer goods company
- Production optimization project for international consumer goods company
- Value based management project for leading industrial company
- Profit improvement project for large Nordic retailer
- Sales and Marketing project for international consumer goods company
