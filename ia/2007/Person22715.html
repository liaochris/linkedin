Deirdre Antes Spencer’s Summary

With fifteen years’ experience in designing print and electronic marketing collateral in both staff and freelance positions, I have designed for a wide range of businesses and clients, including visual effects companies, new media start-ups, museums and other non-profit organizations, semiconductor corporations, and boutique web design studios. I am equally proficient in both print design (from concept to prepress) and web design (from site architecture to XHTML and CSS coding).

Please visit my portfolio website at deirdrespencer.com to see examples of my work and to access a print-friendly detailed chronological résumé and samples booklet.

Deirdre Antes Spencer’s Specialties:

Website design and printed marketing collateral: site architecture, wire frames, XHTML, CSS, film output, pre-press. Software: Mac OSX and Windows platforms; Photoshop, ImageReady, Freehand, Fireworks, Acrobat, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, HTML editors, QuarkXPress, InDesign, and more.
