Founder & President, MassingPR, LLC, 2002-Present

MassingPR, LLC offers high-quality, personalized and cost-effective Public Relations and Marketing Services to companies seeking to establish or enhance their market position, launch new products, work more beneficially with partners or promote their thought leadership credentials.

Unlike other PR firms and marketing agencies, MassingPR combines its extensive in-house expertise with a "Virtual Team" of professionals (when required), including talented writers, designers and strategy consultants. All client contact is handled by MassingPR’s company director, Jennifer Massing, ensuring personalized service.

Since inception MassingPR, LLC has worked with its clients’ both internally with executive management team for strategic planning down to the clients’ middle and lower management for training of marketing functionality and internal project management while concurrently executing the strategies externally using multiple tactics and set forth to Publishers, Editors/reporters, Analysts, Ad Sales Reps, Conference Organizers, Industry Associations and workgroups, design vendors, etc.
