The Distributor Value Program, LLC provides wireless services as well as time-saving, profit-increasing communications tools to the direct sales industry.

The DVP is a spin-off of the successful national-discount Employee Value Program of NationLink Wireless, which has enjoyed success with employees in well-known companies such as Coca-Cola, Federal Express, and Disney. It provides wireless discounts to employees in over 33,000 companies nationwide.

The DVP extends the benefits to the direct sales industry. The DVP also integrates the BlackBerry with a unique application and unlimited high-speed Internet data connectivity that enables sales reps and affiliates to run their sales business no matter where they are.

The DVP is available to any business that has independent sales representatives. For more information on how you can offer this program to your sales people, go to http://www.distributorvalueprogram.com
