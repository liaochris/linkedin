    MicroDraft Technologies

    (Design industry)

    January 1986 — Present (22 years 1 month)

    Many people in the Real Estate Business choose being a Realtor as a second career. My early years of employment were in the Design & Drafting field. Spent a few years as a drafter in civil engineering creating maps and roadway drawings. From there I moved on the mechanical instrumentation and at the infancy of personal computers became heavily involved with AutoCAD. Initially most of my AutoCAD work involved training drafters in the use of the software but quickly grew into custom programming and drafting services for many of the companies in the Greater New Haven area.
