Terry Green’s Summary

It's time to free yourself of those everyday administrative tasks that sap you of your energy and chip away at your precious time. Certified Master Virtual Assistant Terry L. Green has helped countless speakers, coaches and entrepreneurs maximize their free time and profit potential... and she can do the same for you, too. Established in 1991, Fastype specializes in providing administrative support and proofing/editing services to, professional speakers and coaches with a desire to save time and money! Our clients span the globe from across the United States to as far away as New Zealand, Antigua and Singapore. Backed by Terry’s more than 25 years of experience, Fastype and its team of qualified associates provide the skill and experience you need to assist you in successfully managing and growing your business. As virtual professionals we produce virtual "work that matters" for our clients using the latest technology and expertise, and welcome the opportunity to partner with professional speakers, coaches and service-oriented business professionals who refuse to settle for less than the finest.

Partnering with a Virtual Assistant allows you to manage your daily operations and get your back office under control so you can focus on the projects that yield the biggest return on your investment. The Fastype team works with coaches, speakers, authors, entrepreneurs... and anyone else who believes in thinking bigger, working smarter, and reaching out for the life of abundance, fulfillment and personal freedom that we all deserve to have!

No matter what your location; around the corner, in another state, or on the other side of the world, we can help. Our role as your partner in success is to be expert in every facet of your office support requirements. We spend our time and money on the equipment, software, and training required so that you don't have to. That means you're free to spend your time doing what you do best.
