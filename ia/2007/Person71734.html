Christian Reinboth’s Summary

Freelance Consultant with a degree in both Computer Science and Economics, providing valuable and cost effective solutions for all kinds of online and offline market research and consumer/client polling. Special focus on generating representative online samples for empirical research. Also working as an e-coach for students and professionals in need of help with their statistical/empirical research.

Co-Founder of HarzOptics GmbH, a photonics think tank specialized on optical measurement technology, research and development of optical components and effective photonics education programmes. HarzOptics is also the exclusive producer and distributor of the OPTOTEACH POF-WDM lab system.

Christian Reinboth’s Specialties:

Traditional market research and online research, design of online (and offline) questionnaires, multivariate analysis methods, webdesign, quantitative and qualitative research methodology, exploratory and descriptive data analysis
