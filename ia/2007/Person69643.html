Paula Skaper’s Summary

President and Sr. eStrategist of Kinetix Media Communications, Paula Skaper helps companies improve customer loyalty and retention through effective Internet marketing and communications programs. Paula is an ebusiness expert with over 15 years of Internet marketing and media experience.

An active member of the Vancouver business community, Paula holds the position of CIMBS Chair on the board of the International Internet Marketing Association, is a member of the Vancouver Board of Trade and the BCDMA and teaches ecommerce at BCIT. She is also the publisher of DigitalConversations.net, a portal devoted to the digital direct marketing. community

Paula Skaper’s Specialties:

Effective lead generation, customer loyalty and retention strategies that build profitable digital conversations to optimize the communications power of the Internet.
