Griffin de Luce’s Summary

Since 1992 I've consulted to startups, corporations, universities and governments worldwide. At Digital (DEC) I helped to shape the future of the Internet. At JP Morgan I honed my mission critical project management skills.

By 1995 I founded The Gryphon Group (TGG.com http://web.archive.org/web/19970623194915/www.tgg.com/indexx.html ) which became web agency to dozens of clients including IBM, MIT, SONY, Nielsen, American Express, the head of the US Securities Exchange Commission and the CEO of MasterCard Corporation.

By 1999 TGG & JWT (largest ad agency in the world) won Netscape's business after which JWT absorbed certain key accounts and assets. I moved to California where I married my wife (founder of Critical Path, Inc.) and began to incubate a VR meta-agency AxisVR; a Bioinformatics company (General Genomics) and a Social Media startup. (Tribecast Inc.)

My passion springs from the intersection of emerging technologies and the global marketplace. I invest in and/or consult to startups in Silicon Valley through my incubation firm Lucence LLC. I blog occasionally on TiVo, Apple and VR.

I maintain press credentials in the US and abroad and I have worked on international film productions ( http://us.vdc.imdb.com/name/nm1812143/ ) and TV interviews of luminaries like President Mikhail Gorbachev, General Colin Powell, John Sculley, George Soros and Bill Gates. I've advised producers, executives and talent in print and broadcast media outlets such as the BBC, CBS, PBS, Middle East Broadcast Center, The New York Times, Newsweek, Fortune, The Wall Street Journal, and the Charlie Rose show.
