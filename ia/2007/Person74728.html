Raquel R Sánchez’s Summary

Raquel R. Sanchez has been on the designing front lines for years. Her designs have been hailed as “clean, fresh, and wonderfully unique” by such global clients as: GE, The NFL and Kerri Chandler. It is no wonder that alphabet city design + marketing has become a primary consideration for a wide range of businesses, including record labels, corporations, party organizations, and respected artists in the community and beyond.

Raquel’s artwork has appeared in Big Daddy Magazine (UK), The Source, and Dime Magazine. With previous clients such as Jellybean Benitez,Target, Kay Slay and so many others, Sanchez hopes to become “the extraordinary and magnificent designer that everybody comes to.”
