Christian Rimkus’s Summary

After having graduated as Naval Engineer in 1990 from University of Hamburg, Institute for Naval Architecture, I worked for the Swedish software house Kockums Computer Systems (KCS) as a Software Engineer and Project Manager. I was responsible for a software package for preliminary design, hydrostatic and stability calculations for all kind of vessels and a Production Planning Application based on PL/I, C++, Tcl/TK and the ACIS 3D kernel from Spatial. When KCS closed its German office in December 1999 I started working for Atlantec Enter-prise Solutions, a PLM Software Development Company in Hamburg and Annapolis (USA). I was involved in the development of PLM integration tools for shipbuilding CAD/CAM software including Tribon and Catia V5, based on Java, Python, Catia V5 CAA/C++, CORBA and LDAP Services.

Since the first of April 2003, I am working for CIMPA, supporting the DMU Data Quality Team of Airbus as Project Manager for transnational teams.

After working as Software Engineer for many years my present goal is to use my experience for the management of IT projects and to encourage my project management know-how with learning by doing and attending seminars and trainings.
