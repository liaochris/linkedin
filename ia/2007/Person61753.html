johannes muecke’s Summary

Computer games are a great medium to create new worlds, fantastic settings and inovative, unique environments. My greatest interest has always focused on this and during my studies of Architecture I developed a strong expertise in this field.
I experienced, that a vision evolves from the broadest possible range of influences and inspiration. My practice as a life drawing teacher, as assisant at an Architecture school and my routine cooperation with production studios has influenced my work and proven the importance of an interdisciplinary discourse.
During my time at Rockstar Vienna and now Sproing I experienced, that a compelling visual and artistic vision of a game is an integral part of story and gameplay and vice versa. Therein lies the difference between fine art and concept art. As a concept artist I enjoy the challenge of reinventing myself and my vision with every new project.
