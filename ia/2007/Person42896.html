John Gilbertson’s Summary

Bitemark philosophy

We’re not successful unless you’re successful. It’s the simplest philosophy we know. We only drive energy into a product that works exactly the way our clients want.

You see, our designers are perfectionists. We can’t stop them from giving 100% to their projects. We find them in the mornings working in the dark, cast in the eerie glow of their computer monitors (they’re a strange – yet brilliant – breed). We can, however, stop them from creating something that doesn’t fit a client’s needs.

Bitemark clients get the exact product they require because we’re committed to building professional, friendly relationships. We work closely with our clients to create design solutions that fit their company vision.

It all comes down to asking the right questions.
We have no formulas, no templates. Bitemark celebrates your company’s uniqueness. It’s our driving force behind designs that leave more than a mark. They leave a bite mark.

What we can do for you?

Our strength lies in interpreting your vision and transforming it into a design that takes you beyond the boundaries of expectations. If our design doesn’t surprise you with an unexpected and effective communication of your vision, we haven’t done our job.

Our talented designers have their fingers on the pulse of design elements that trigger positive, inspirational reactions in your clients. Bitemark designs are different, fresh and put your company a cut above your peers.
Bitemark designers don’t ride the trends. They anticipate and lead them.

We’ve brought together an effective blend of artistic vision and consumer insight that only comes from years of experience in the design industry.
