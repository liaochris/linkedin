Fintan Palmer’s Summary

As a software professional with 17 years-worth (so far) of experience I believe that software development can and should be better, both in terms of code quality and as a process. Working for a variety of companies from small start-ups to large multi-nationals across many different industries, I've experienced some of the best and worst of it.

My goal is to make software and software development better. I intend to do that by teaching those around me what I’ve learned, encouraging an interest in technology and practices such as agile methodologies and inspire the same desire in others to Do Things Better.

Fintan Palmer’s Specialties:

Agile methodologies (specifically XP); Java & C++ OO design and development; Project management and team leadership.
