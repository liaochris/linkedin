Andrew Kao’s Summary

I am currently a sophomore, although first-year, student studying Hospitality Management at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. Born and raised in Burbank, CA – where the hospitality industry was not prominent, I excelled in computers, web technologies, math and science. However, I also took officer positions in a variety of organizations and decided that the combination of my experiences would give me an innovative edge in the hospitality industry.

I started my hospitality education at UNLV during the summer of 2006 – as my high school summers have always been filled with college courses. Also, I will be finishing the typical 4-year BS degree in Hotel Administration in about 2.5 years; continuing on with a second major in Meetings & Events Management, a minor in Japanese (in addition to my fluent Mandarin & Taiwanese), a dual MBA/MS in Hotel Administration/Business Administration and perhaps an Doctor of Philosophy after that.

Employment with Soleil Management supplements my UNLV hospitality education. I am currently working (almost full time) at Front Desk on the Club de Soleil property, with mentors throughout the property’s management team. I also participate in many hospitality organizations, such as AH&LA, IAEE, NACE, NSMH, F.I.T., and the LV Hospitality Association. These organizations further expand my hospitality education as they allow me to meet established professionals and learn the trends of the respective industry.
