Marcin Werla’s Summary

I am a Software Team Leader with 4 years of experience in professional software development and an expert in the field of digital libraries. Currently I am a leader of a digital libraries R&D team in Poznan Supercomputing and Networking Center. During my leadership the dLibra project (my current project) has developed from small R&D project into the leading digital library software in Poland with several deployments in the entire country. My specialty are various aspects of architecture of large scale information management systems. I would like to continue my career as a project/team leader and software architect and reach a PhD degree (up-to-date list of my scientific publications can be found here: http://marcin.werla.pl/pubs/).
