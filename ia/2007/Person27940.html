    Headr.com

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    January 2007 — Present (11 months)

    The internet is getting clouded with information and people find it increasingly hard to find the information they are looking for. Its all out there, the trick is to make it relevant for the user.

    Headr delivers personal relevance by combining information from current webflows, similar users and traditional search metrics to deliver recommendations for pages within the current website and related websites. With no user action required.

    Headr comes in three flavours.

    Use the headr toolbar to get recommendations for related pages and websites direct from the browser.

    Add headr widgets to a site or blog to help visitors find related information or proactively show related products.

    Become a subject expert and earn money with little or no effort. Add your knowledge to your personal search engine built on top of Google, MSN Live Search, Yahoo or headr’s api.

    Most important; start finding what you are looking for.
