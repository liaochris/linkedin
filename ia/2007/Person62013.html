Yvonne Li’s Summary

MyAdTV, Inc. (www.myadtv.com) is a software service provider dedicated to bringing digital signage solutions to the masses. MyAdTV provides a complete web based solution to in-store advertising and on-location ad sales.

As the founder of MyAdTV, I thoroughly enjoy the experience of being an entrepreneur. Internet technologies have given many the opportunities to sharp ideas into reality within their financial means. MySpace and YouTube are prime examples of how one can turn an idea into a phenom with limited resources. Because of their success, the industry now focuses largely on social networking. However, my personal belief is that the next big wave of Internet related businesses will be in open market trading. To my surprise, even with the success of eBay 5 years ago, few companies capture the fact that open market trading can generate staggering direct incomes and auction is not the only form. This business model can be applied to all industries.

In 2005 I authored a patent related to an open market ad trading system where people can trade ad space on digital signage screens installed in places such as stores, airports and shopping malls. The system consists of technologies that enable a fully automated ad trading process. Ad buyers can create and submit ads and ad sellers can review and approve ads online, the rest is handled by the system which includes content generation, aggregation, routing, distribution, display and trade term enforcement. MyAdTV is also a full feature digital advertising solution for store owners to display their own ads on any Internet connected PCs. I am interested in talking to anyone who wants to engage in the on-location ad sales business using this ad trading platform.
