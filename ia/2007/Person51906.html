Loc Nguyen’s Summary

Loc is a software developer with a broad set of skills. His background in web and graphic design provides insight into building GUIs that improve human-computer interaction. His previous work administrating Windows and UNIX servers compliments his growing experience with application development in the corporate space. Having interacting with clients and end-users in past and current jobs, Loc is a well-rounded individual with knowledge at all levels of the development life cycle.

Professionally, Loc delivers solutions based on Java, Spring, Struts, iBATIS, and Oracle. His own time is spent exploring open source technology and how to leverage it for commercial applications. He's determined to be on the cutting edge and develops on his own with Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, and Adobe Flex.

Lastly, his mind is always restless trying to think of the internet's "next big thing."
