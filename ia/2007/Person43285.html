John Warrington’s Summary

Experienced broadcaster focussing on football providing technical and editiorial broadcast soloutions to football clubs throughout the pyramid.

John Warrington’s Specialties:

Started working life as a footballer before moving into media as a broadcaster commentating on football. Moved behind the camera to work as a producer, executive producer and finally head of sport. By progressively gaining a knowledge of how the programmes I made reached an audience and coupled an entrepeneurial spirit, I find myself where I am to day. Que Sera Sera!
