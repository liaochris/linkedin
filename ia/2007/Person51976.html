Lon Adams’s Summary

During the last two years as CEO of a venture capital funded software company, I restructured the company, hired a new management team, focused our strategy and product offering and we increased sales by over 35% last year. As a result of the heavy travel demands (spending two weeks in Irvine, California followed by two weeks in Paris, France plus other domestic and international travel), I recently decided it was time to make a change and promoted the COO into my former position as CEO. I remain Chairman of the company but am now in a position to consider a new opportunity.

Citilog is a software company headquartered in Paris, France with regional offices in Philadelphia and Hong Kong and doing business worldwide. The company’s software adds intelligence to video surveillance systems by monitoring the video streams and automatically generating alerts from predefined triggering events.

In previous positions at Fortune 100 companies, I delivered highly profitable results as Vice President, Strategy and then heading an E-Business Product Management group. As a CEO I have experience working with those in executive management and managing budgets and staff. My MBA is from a top school and I have both a Project Management Professional (PMP) certification from the Project Management Institute (PMI) and a Six Sigma Green Belt certification. Having proven myself in both tough-minded entrepreneurial environments and Fortune 100 companies, I am confident that I can deliver the leadership, project and general management experience that your company needs.
