  • Product and Marketing Manager

    Flycast Communications

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Online Media industry)

    April 1996 — November 1997 (1 year 8 months)

As a part of the product management team we launched five web advertising buying and selling tools. In my role as marketing manager I was a part of the initial market launch and two subsequent corporate identity and customer acquisition campaigns. I am proud to have been a significant part of this important time and segment of the Internet’s growth.
