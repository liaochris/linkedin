Jeremy Geelan’s Summary

Domain expert on "the future of the future," with 25 years of continuous media experience from BBC-tv, BBC Radio, and national newspapers in the UK to worldwide book publishing then online publishing in the US (SYS-CON Media). Exponent of "helping the Web be all it can," special interest in social computing/social software and its impact on social, political, economic, cultural, educational, and recreational behavior.
