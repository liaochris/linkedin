Vanae Tran’s Summary

* Founded the non-profit org, Rock Your Awareness, a young Bay Area organization that is putting the juice back into progressive advocacy and innovative peer outreach for social change. RYA advocates important issues such as HIV/AIDS awareness, preventative education, Darfur awareness, connecting low-income seniors and more. The organization has expanded to 10 coordinators, 300+ volunteers, 12+ community partnerships and 10+ successful fundraising/awareness events.
