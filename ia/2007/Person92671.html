Zecki Dossal’s Summary

Zecki Dossal is a Vice President at Gerson Lehrman Group, responsible for managing Global Research for the Technology, Media & Telecommunications (TMT) Practice. He joined the firm in September 2002 as a Research Analyst, responsible for recruiting and development for the GLG Councils, a global network in TMT. Zecki earned a B.A. in Economics from Columbia University, after initially starting undergraduate work at Johns Hopkins.
