Xris Thomas’s Summary

I currently play a diverse roll as an Account Manager/Graphic Designer/CS manager with a DC-based technology company. Founded in 2004, Distributive Networks is a mobile content and technology firm that provides mobile marketing solutions to partners that range from sports teams to leading brands to marketing agencies. With more than two million consumers of our subscription text alert services, and a growing roster of prestigious clients around the world, Distributive Networks is one of the most successful privately-held companies in the wireless industry.

Distributive Networks provides strategy, creative consultation, execution and detailed analytics across a range of interactive mobile services -- including WAP development, real-time voting/polling, surveys, coupons, text to screen, sweepstakes and white-label text content. With its proven proprietary content, billing and subscription management system, and its established relationships and connections with all major US wireless carriers, Distributive Networks offers its clients a truly comprehensive suite of mobile marketing capabilities. For additional information, please visit www.distributivenetworks.com.
