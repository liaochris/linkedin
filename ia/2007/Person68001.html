Paul Guest’s Summary

Paul has delivered consultancy and training to some of Britain’s largest organisations including Orange, Nationwide Building Society and the National School of Government. Highlighted skills include:

• Selection and implementation of ISO/IEC 17799 & 27001, COBIT and PCI DSS control objectives;
• Delivering security and technical training courses;
• Installation of server environments using both Microsoft and Linux solutions;
• Deployment of secure workstation configurations;
• Design and implementation of Active Directory in single and multi-domain forests;
• Design and installation of encryption solutions, including VPNs and PKI;
• Design and installation of secure wireless LANs;
• Design and installation of Enterprise anti-malware solutions;
• Configuration and management of firewalls and routers.

Paul is a Member of the Institution of Engineering and Technology (MIET) and is also a founder Associate of the Institute of Information Security Professionals (IISP).
