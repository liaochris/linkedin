Daniel Azzopardi’s Summary

Daniel Azzopardi is a technical design consultant, with eleven years experience of the UK Telecom industry. He successfully held technical and team leader positions in mobile telecom companies where he gained experience in GSM, GPRS, 3G (WCDMA) and VoIP/WiFi product development and Prince 2 project management techniques. He is an ideal candidate for technical consultant, solution design or technical lead roles in Telecoms or IT related industry.

Daniel is studying for his Graduate diploma in Law at Kingston University, the first step towards becoming a qualified solicitor.

Daniel Azzopardi’s Specialties:

GSM, GPRS, 3G, IMS, SIP, WiFi, VoIP, Converged products, product and service design, location services, m-commerce, speech recognition, network architecture, network evolution roadmaps, RFI, RFQ, Vendor evaluation, contracts for supply of telco services and network equipment, Data protection, Data Security policies
