Glenn letham’s Summary

Founder, SymbianOne.com & Spatial Media LLC -- With a background in Geography I'm somewhat of a "Jack of all trades" albeit with a geospatial, mobile, and Web2.0 focus. Most people think of me as a technology editor, however, I'm also a skilled writer, moderator, GIS professional, and technology evangelist. My areas of expertise is mainly in GIS, mobile positioning, location-based services, and development for the Symbian Mobile operating system. Founding several web properties has also provided me with experience in developing for WEB 2.0, marketing, public relations, writing, and "shmoozing" - people recognize me as "the guy who travels all the time", this comes about from my passion for blogging and moblogging while I'm attending events. I've attended more than 75 industry trade shows, conferences, and tech. events over the past 8 years taking me to cool places like Orlando, N'Orleans, Vancouver, Helsinki, Paris, London, Munich, and Monaco.

Some of the coolest places I've been are:
- circumnavigating the Queen Charlotte Islands on a research vessel
- heli skiing the Purcel Mountains in the Canadian Rockies (B.C)
- the great barrier reef & Great Keppel Island (Australia)
- Long Beach, North Island, New Zealand
- Fiji during typhoon season!

Places on my list of things to do:
- Visit New York and ride the Staten Island Ferry
- Boston and go to Wrigley field to see a Sox/Yankees series
- Moscow... I've always wanted to go there!
- Dubai... why wouldn't anyone want to go there?
- Fiji again but with my wife this time (no kids!)

I also would like to get my Masters degree one day, perhaps get the GISP designation, and write a book.

Glenn letham’s Experience

  • Co-founder, Managing Editor - Geo Evangelist!

    Spatial Media LLC / GISuser.com, LBSzone, SymbianOne

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 2003 — Present (4 years 3 months)

    Have developed from scratch several leading web resources including GISuser.com (mapping and GIS), LBSzone.com (location-based services), SymbianOne.com (symbian OS and smartphone developers), and MAPuser.com. GISuser has rapidly become an industry leader in the GIS and geospatial arena. The online publication and community resource has a Global reach, publishing news, articles, job announcements every day and supported by a 3x a week electronic newswire billed as GISuser Today. GISuser has struck numerous deals with industry leaders Autodesk, ESRI, Thales, Trimble, LizardTech, GITA, Safe Software, and countless others over the years. GISuser.com is a leading online news resource and community web portal for geographers and GIS, Mapping professionals. the focus of the site is on GIS, mapping, GPS, location-based services, and geospatial technologies and the site is complemented by Glenn's weblog hosted at AnyGeo.com.

  • Founder, Managing Editor

    SymbianOne, GISuser.com & LBSzone.com

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    August 2003 — Present (4 years 4 months)

    In 2003 I developed and launched SymbianOne.com in order to fill a large void in the online wireless developer space. building on where I left off from wirelessdevnet, SymbianOne was designed to focus explilcitly on the Symbian OS and mobile application development related to devices that support the Symbian OS. Partnered with New Zealand-based Richard Bloor, SymbianOne has rapidly become the leadinng online technology resource focused on the Symbian OS, Series 60 (S60), UIQ, and related discliplines. SymbianOne publishes daily industry news updates, original editorial and articles, conference reports and reviews, and provides community resources for mobile technology professionals. In my position at the helm of SymbianOne I have been instrumental in establishing strategic partnerships with a number of industry leaders in the mobile space including: Forum Nokia, Handango, tradepubs.com, CTIA, WINBC, and many others.

  • Managing Editor

    GeoCommunity, WirelessDevnet

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Publishing industry)

    July 1999 — August 2003 (4 years 2 months)

    In 1998 I founded online publication and technology e-zine SpatialNews.com. After rapidly gaining acceptance in the GIS industry as a must-read resource I sold the publication to Florida-based GeoComm International Corp. At that time SpatialNews was rolled into The GeoCommunity we portal. I continued on as Managing Editor, charged with developing ALL content and editorial for the company's online publications. In 2000 I expanded my role with the company by also taking on the role as Managing Editor for Wireless Developer Network (Wirelessdevnet.com). At WDN I was acharged with website content development, establishing business leads and additional revenue streams, creating mutually beneficial partnerships and affiliations. This position entailed communicating with executives from many of the leading names in wireless technologies (Sony Ericsson, Nokia, Handango, Sybase, Symbian, Qualcomm, and many others).

  • GIS Consultant

    AXYS Environmental Consulting Ltd.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1994 — June 1999 (5 years 6 months)

    I spent 5 awesome years working at Sydney, BC-based AXYS, working my way up from a GIS Technician to GIS Consultant/Analyst. During that time I was charged with working hands-on with many environmental-focused GIS projects including Arctic sailing directions, coastal information Atlas projects, shellfish inventories, pipeline EIA projects, habitat suitability models, and many others. Projects were conducted mostly for government agencies (all levels), forestry companies, and the oil & gas industry. In addition to working as a GIS Analyst I was also charged with conducting GIS and Database training. Software platforms I was working with included PAMAP GIS, ArcInfo, AutoCAD MAP, SPANS, Genamap, MapInfo, QuikMap, and many others.

    CAD/survey technician responsible for digital input, output of legal survey plans. Required 2D and 3D input of data using AutoCAD and related products (surfer)

  • GIS Analyst
