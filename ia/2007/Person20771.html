Darren Bowie’s Summary

I manage the consumer protection regulatory group in the AOL Legal Department, which advises on privacy, data security, advertising, payment systems, accessibility, and other regulatory matters.

Darren Bowie’s Specialties:

Compliance with federal and state consumer protection and privacy laws and regulations, including the FTC Act, Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act, Fair Credit Reporting Act, Electronic Fund Transfer Act, CAN-SPAM Act, and the Telemarketing Sales Rule.
