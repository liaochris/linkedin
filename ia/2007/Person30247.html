Fred McIntyre’s Summary

Accomplished in senior leadership roles working with top consumer brands in successful start-up ventures as well as at a leading consumer Internet portal.

Strong track record of mapping the strategy, developing the partnerships, building, operating and growing the products behind category leading internet media businesses.
