Krzysztof Klincewicz’s Summary

10+ years of experience in international IT and consulting industry in Poland, Finland, UK and Japan. Certified chartered marketer of the Chartered Institute of Management. Professional and academic career focused on strategies of technology companies and project management. Good command of several foreign languages, including German, French and Japanese. Currently focused on exploring business opportunities in technology sector in Asia.
