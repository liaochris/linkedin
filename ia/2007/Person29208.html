Jonathan Feuchtwang’s Summary

I am an in-house, intellectual property attorney based in San Mateo, California. My practice focuses on providing clients with practical, proactive, and preventative services in the areas of competitive analysis and monitoring, application prosecution, and portfolio management through each stage of the product development cycle. My practice is focussed on medical devices, but I have extensive software experience (I was a programmer for 5 years).
