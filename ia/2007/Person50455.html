Josh Dorfman’s Summary

An environmental entrepreneur interested in creating stylish, innovative, and market-based approaches to environmental challenges that make it easy for everyone to be part of the solution.

My book, The Lazy Environmentalist: Your Guide to Easy, Stylish, Green Living is now available from Stewart, Tabori & Chang.

Josh Dorfman’s Experience

  • Author

    The Lazy Environmentalist: Your Guide to Easy, Stylish, Green Living

    (Publishing industry)

    May 2007 — Present (6 months)

    We can’t all camp out in old-growth forests, lying down in front of the bulldozers. And it’s not only that we’re too busy: Some of us just don’t want our fabulous threads to get caked with mud.

    But that doesn’t mean we don’t care passionately about the environment. Luckily, the days when becoming environmentally aware entailed eating bread that tasted like dirt, wearing clothes that looked like frayed burlap sacks, and spending summer vacations assailing whaling ships with Greenpeace are passing away. It is now perfectly possible (and increasingly easy) to be well fed, well coiffed, well dressed, and well traveled while remaining deeply committed to an ecologically sustainable lifestyle.

    In The Lazy Environmentalist, Josh Dorfman—host of the Sirius Satellite Radio program of the same name—provides comprehensive guidance to fashion-forward consumers who are as concerned about the long-term health of our planet as they are about the design of their bathroom fixtures.

  • Founder & CEO

    Modern Green Living

    (Real Estate industry)

    April 2006 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    Modern Green Living is an online resource that helps individuals easily find eco-friendly apartments, condos, and housing neighborhoods and locate green architects, builders, interior designers, realtors, and consultants in their area.

  • Creator, Producer & Host

    The Lazy Environmentalist radio show

    (Entertainment industry)

    June 2005 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    The Lazy Environmentalist is a radio show broadcast throughout North America on the Lime Channel of Sirius Satellite Radio and Lime.com that informs listeners about environmental trends and innovation and cutting-edge eco-friendly products and services. Guests are environmental experts from every industry that touches the lives of consumers and make it easy and attractive for consumers to green their lifestyles.
