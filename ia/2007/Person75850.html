Samantha Murphy’s Summary

I'm an artist first and foremost, but I'm also an early adopter of podcasting. With my show SMtv, I feature a different artist from a different city and a different venue each week as I tour.

I've been performing professionally since I was six years old and have had the great pleasure of working with Willie Nelson, Dolly Parton, Five for Fighting, Erikah Badu, Michael McDonald and many others. I released my first album, somewhere between starving & stardom, independently April 1, 2005.

SMtv live debuts on your computer Sundays at 2PM PST beginning August 12.

I've lived and worked in Australia, the UK, Canada and the U.S.

Samantha Murphy’s Specialties:

Talent and the ability to find, promote and sell it. I also write, record, produce and perform music. In my spare time, I'm a photographer, blogger and videographer.

  • Singer Songwriter

    www.samanthamurphy.com (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry)

    October 1974 — Present (32 years 10 months)

    My parents met on the TV show Australian Bandstand. My mom was a dancer and my dad was making an appearance as a singer and musician.

    Years later, after growing tired of traveling with our parents and only playing video games, one night my older sister and I decided we would rather be on stage. We were eight and six years old when we sang "You and Me Against the World" while wearing Little House on the Prairie dresses and staring at the floor.

    The next seven years would see us travel the world opening for artists like Willie Nelson and Dolly Parton. We performed at fairs, concert halls and clubs across America over 300 days a year.

    Last year I released my first album, somewhere between starving & stardom. Since then, I've been traveling across the US playing solo shows and spreading the word from LA to NYC and Nashville.
