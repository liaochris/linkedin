Micah Schmiedeskamp’s Summary

As a 24 year old, I feel like I've experienced a lot in the confines of Nebraska. I've experienced the gamut of the industry from concepting campaigns to printing physical pieces at my own shop. I have a strong desire to get back to design as my main field and I honestly feel that it is where my gifts lie.

Micah Schmiedeskamp’s Specialties:

Print design, screen printing, posters, music composition, branding, advertising, marketing, communication, and beer advocacy.

Micah Schmiedeskamp’s Experience

  • Production / Art Department

    Ink Tank Merch

    (Printing industry)

    October 2006 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Primary hire in start-up screen printing shop / tour merchandising service provider. The shop was created by Saddle Creek Records in an effort to expand their ever-growing empire. I act as one half of Ink Tank's daily operations. My business card says "Art Department / Production" but the reality is that I handle each job from art files in my inbox to printed shirts sent off in a UPS truck.

  • Art Director

    Sacco

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2006 — October 2006 (8 months)

    At Sacco, I adjusted to a smaller organization and the more hands-on role of Art Director. Though my time there was short, I gained valuable experience and inspiration from a few exciting colleagues. I worked heavily in concepting, web assistance, general design and production work.

  • Graphic Designer / Intern

    Bailey Lauerman

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 2004 — March 2006 (1 year 10 months)

    I snuck on staff at Bailey Lauerman immediately after graduation as an Intern that slowly morphed in to a Designer. BLA is an incredible organization that truly formed my view of what an Ad Agency should and could be. I worked for a wide variety clients ranging from gigantic insurance agencies and jewelry retail to professional lawn mowing and over-the-road trucking. I worked heavily in production work, prepress, graphic design and concepting.

Micah Schmiedeskamp’s Education

  • The Creative Center

    Double Associates, Graphic Design, 2003 — 2004

    My first (bittersweet?) taste of the design world in the form of two-year Omaha, NE trade school The Creative Center. Although I left with a heap of debt and severe lack of instruction, TCC immersed me in design for my immediate post-high school years. School also introduced me to some of my most valued professional assets in the form of the students at my side and future outside colleagues.
