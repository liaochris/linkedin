Vivek Garg’s Summary

I am currently working on search technologies at Microsoft under "Live" Umbrella. I believe that search is only getting started and game will become much more interesting once we have deep web search enabled using books, newspapers and all other data islands that are password protected or not crawled. With Microsoft, I have helped ship live book search (books.live.com). Before live, I worked with Office 2007 for couple of years.

I developed internet software for telecom domain before I went for my masters. During masters, among other things, I worked on a campus startup to develop a sales CRM and worked with economic research lab developing games for economic equilibriums. With Microsoft Office, I got my experience with desktop software before giving in to the agility and fast pace development enviroment of Live Internet services.
