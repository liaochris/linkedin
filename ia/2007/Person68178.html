Philippe Mora’s Summary

I recently joined Telegent Systems as Director of Business Development. Telegent is an incredibly successful little company that is making history with its rather refreshing "breaking the established rules" attitude !

As Director of Marketing and Business development at PortalPlayer as well as NVIDIA as part of PortalPlayer's acquisition, I have been working on various assignments ranging from product management and marketing strategy for product specific platforms to leading and directing the work of marketing, business development, marketing communications, design and development resources across global regions: US, Asia and Europe.

Prior to joining PortalPlayer, as Senior Program Manager at Spatializer Audio Laboratories, I was responsible for building/growing an engineering team, identifying business opportunities globally and expanding product and market reach to portable devices including Cellular Handsets, PMPs and PNDs for key accounts such as Sony, Toshiba, Panasonic, Samsung, Apple, LG, JVC, NTT Docomo, and Sanyo. We climbed to multimillion dollars revenue before selling the company and its extensive IP portfolio to DTS.

I kicked off my career in DSP firmware engineering and management at Digital Video Systems and C-Cube. DVS, my first startup, introduced digital video to China and went public to perform the best IPO of 1996. C-Cube pioneered the digital video revolution by introducing the first successful MPEG decoders for the DVD-player industry. C-Cube was ultimately acquired by LSI Logic.

An entrepreneur at heart, I have been able to create a unique mix of technical and business skills. This background, my extensive network of professional friends throughout the ecosystem and finally my passion for consumer electronics have always helped me stay on the cutting edge of the industry to the benefit of my employers and clients
