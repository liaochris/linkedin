Anol Bhattacharya’s Summary

For last 5+ years I am consulting clients (Cisco Systems Inc, IDC Asia Pacific, HP, Singapore Management University etc.) on behalf of GetIT Singapore, in the field of organizational E-Learning, Knowledge Management, Blog & Virtual Communities, Learning Design, User Experience Design & New Media Marketing.

I joined GetIT in November 2002 and was appointed as COO in 2006. You can find more about my job scope at GetIT at www.getitcomms.com

In my previous life I worked as IBM AS/400 programmer and information architect for web media.

I am passionate about harnessing the power of internet as an enabler, connecting people and ideas and driving innovation. My ideas, likes and dislikes you can find at my blog www.soulsoup.net
