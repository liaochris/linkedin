Paul Plvan’s Summary

Paul J. Plvan, Principal
Paul Plvan has over 15 years of experience as a successful entrepreneur and 20 years total in business management. Prior to founding Mercury Leads, Paul spent 10 years with full-service national direct marketing firms where he was responsible for Fortune 500 marketing programs. Paul was an executive with Excite and Matchlogic, their data warehousing partner, where he constructed fully integrated data solutions for some of the largest databases in the world. He built some of the original database marketing applications for data mining of his client's customer base, enabling them to maximize their ROI and target more effectively.
