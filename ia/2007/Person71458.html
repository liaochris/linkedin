Ron Dollete’s Summary

I am part of a sales team that helps find solutions for companies looking to test their high speed communications network. While the majority of customers in the Southern California territory are primarily interested in Triple Play (the convergence of data, voice and video), I have also worked extensively in Layer 2 and 3 traffic generation and measurement, wireless LANs, conformance analysis, throughput testing with Chariot, and also automation via Tcl and other test harnesses.

I have worked primarily in embedded systems, with a focus on FPGAs used in high-speed networks, interfacing both the link (fiber/copper) as well as the user (CPU). The speeds have ranged from OC48 to 10GigEthernet, and applications have included traffic generation and analysis as well as security preprocessing and load module infrastructure.
