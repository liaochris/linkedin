Steve Rosenberg’s Summary

Grew up in Sweden and got an MS-EE from Chalmers University of Technology, Goteborg (Gothenburg), Sweden. Worked at Ericsson and did Naval Military duty after school. Started Sern consulting firm with 3 partners. Moved to the US in 1982 to start subsidiary to Sern. Sold Sern to Swedish consulting firm in 1986. Ran own consulting until 1989.

Started at FEI 1989 and worked with the FIB500D and FIB600, designed the first digital pattern generator,(DSPG) and the ion mill software in DOS.

Started the SW department which enabled the move to Windows OS and UI. Worked on the FIB820 and the first DualBeam FIB620 in cooperation with Philips Electron Optics, spent 4 months in Eindhoven-Netherlands during this effort. Left FEI 1998 to start Deschutes. Deschutes was acquired by FEI 2001. Have been with FEI as Director of Enginereing since 2001.

Steve Rosenberg’s Specialties:

System Engineering. Electrical Engineering. System Architecture. Project Management and Functional Management of cross fucntional teams.
