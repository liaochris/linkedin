Angela Liew’s Summary

Accounting Information Systems: e.g. analysing and charting systems flows, internal control and audit of accounting systems, payroll processing and expenditure cycle, XBRL.

Business Analyst, Application Developer, Analyst Programmer, and Database Administrator in a wide variety of business applications. Particularly interested in relational database design using SQL Server and MS Access, and object-oriented database design using JADE. Co-authored a university textbook on Data Modelling: The Foundation of Information Systems, which is widely used in various courses at the Information Systems department at the University of Auckland, including Graduate Programmes.

Special research interests in Decision Making and Decision Systems, and applying such new framework and architecture to the One-Dimensional Cutting Stock domain. Has published and presented this work at the top-tier tier-1 international conference (HICSS'05).
