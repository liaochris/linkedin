Michael Chinnici’s Summary

I enjoy exciting challenges... turning dreams into realities... or bringing the next great idea to the world.

In 1982, I founded "Chinnici Direct" , a Direct Marketing Ad Agency to service the growing demands of the industry. My objective was to establish CD as the most creative and strategic agency in the business.

In 1996, I founded both "Icon Digital Photography" and "Brave New Media" to service the new world of Digital. I continue to be very involved in Icon Digital today.

In 1999, I co-founded "M2 Design Group" to service the Graphic Design needs of the Music and Fashion Industries.

In 2000, I sold a majority interest of Chinnici Direct, and stayed on as CEO until 2005 when I stepped down and left the agency.

In 2006... My desire to "change the face of the ad agency" led to the birth of "Scuderia O2" ...Italian for "Team Oxygen".

Scuderia O2 is a new kind of animal.

A Hybrid of sorts. A cross between the aerodynamic and fuel efficient creative boutiques and the gas-guzzling Goliath's of the “so-called” integrated holding companies.

It’s the brainpower of a mega-agency, the intensity of a boutique, the can-do chemistry of respectful pros having a great time working together.

And by providing a “Best-in-Class” worldwide network of seasoned Creative and Strategic talent supported by an exceptional and powerful Account Services team, it’s a WIN-WIN-WIN for everyone. The client, the agency and the customer.

So... FASTEN YOUR SEATBELTS!
We're sending SHOCK WAVES throughout the agency world.
