Jedi Wright’s Summary

Current professional experience:

Founder, principle and CEO of GreenGurus.net - Green Solutions For the Home & Business. Currently growing my team of consulting gurus for a wide range of roles to meet the growing needs of the sustainability movement. Developing companies processes, products and services; managing corporate affairs, business development, marketing, etc, etc.

I'm also currently the President and CEO of Distinctivefabric.com, Inc. where I've filled virtually every role since not long after it's startup in early 2004, by founding Chairman Adam Wiggins. I'm currently developing a sustainable fabrics division as well as our e-commerce site's software, into a highly customizable, off-the-shelf product.

In addition, I work as a production artist w/The Do LaB on a number of events, including their annual Green arts & music festival: Lightning In A Bottle, where I'm now serving as part of the core Green production team.

Goals:

Long-term: the protection and cultivation of all things in nature. Short-term: growing my dot-com ventures with the help of new partners/investors for my two biggest ventures: Distinctivefabric.com & GreenGurus.net, greening the Internet, expanding my global network, etc.

Jedi Wright’s Specialties:

Business processes & development; product creation & sales; customer & equipment transpo; tour design; materials & product sourcing; fulfillment systems; marketing/PR; web dev, branding/corp identity, ecommerce & social network architecturing; CRM, brand marriage management; event production: set, prop & costume design; sustainability consulting, fair trade materials/sourcing, carbon footprinting, alternative waste management solutions, team/staff awareness education & support.
