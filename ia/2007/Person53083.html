Gareth Madge’s Summary

Lavish creates and produces marketing and promotional materials for clients and agencies.

We specialise in creative production, design, retouching, project management, 3D/CGI visualisations and animations, online, print management and photography.

We work for large and small clients and charity organisations, producing advertising, direct mail, brochures, websites, packaging, banner adverts, logo design, corporate identity and video production.

Our team have worked with some of the biggest global and UK brands, inlcuding Lloyds TSB, Morgan Stanley, BT, 02, National Portrait Gallery, BBC, Shell and others.
