Allen Ackerman’s Summary

As a former IT consultant, Allen Ackerman has gained great experience in navigating both the peaks and valleys of working in the consulting world. After working for several years in this field, he recognized a need in the industry to provide solutions for both talent and corporate communities. Mr. Ackerman founded Indigo Technology Group in 1997 to fill this need by servicing both technical talent and clients. He made it his business to assist candidates with building careers, not just making placements, and to assist companies in building teams. In 2003 Mr. Ackerman launch Immerge L.L.C., a progressive recruiting agency with a streamlined structure, to better service an ever changing industry.
