Eric Newman’s Summary

I enjoy tackling complex problems and developing creative, and cost-effective solutions. My involvement in a wide variety of product, technology, and business development efforts throughout my career has given me an extremely diverse background in engineering, data modeling, and software development. Projects ranging from tiny embedded system controllers, to massively scaled AOL-sized publishing, data management, content management, feeds and syndication, and related infrastructure components. I am always eager to tackle bleeding edge new technologies, and find creative ways to extend my company's capabilities. Most recently have been developing working prototypes of small iPhone applications, using SOAP, AJAX, and LAMP.

Eric Newman’s Specialties:

HTML, AJAX, JSON, PHP, Asset Management, Content Management (CMS), scalable web delivery systems, contextual Ad serving, web traffic analytics, database development, and editorial workflow systems.

Have studied UI Design, performance tuning, and code optimization. Recently completed Java certificate-level courses. Awaiting approval for Patent on dynamic content generation and recirculation using taxonomy and classification systems to target small form factor, iPhone-style internet devices.
