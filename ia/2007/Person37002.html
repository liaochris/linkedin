Ivan Magrin-Chagnolleau’s Summary

I worked in scientific research for 12 years (Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering) before changing career and becoming a performing artist. I changed career in 2003. I studied for three years at the Actors Studio Drama School in New York (M.F.A. in Acting). And I am now an actor, singer, director, and filmmaker/editor. I also teach acting and coach actors. I work on the side as a life coach (B.S. in Psychology), a web designer and developer, an English teacher (bilingual French-English), and a translator.
