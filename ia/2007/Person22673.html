Chris Garrigues’s Summary

I've been in the IT field for most of my life, starting in the mid-70s as the "store teenager" at a computer store, programming my way through college and moving on to profession IT positions in a variety of environments from sole proprietorships to multi-nationals.

I've been working on the Internet since it's early days as the ARPAnet I'm highly experienced in a wide range of network administration issues including: IP routing, email management, VPNs, Unix and Linux server administration and security issues. I have relocated the IT infrastructure for many different entities including relocating a software R&D laboratory from Palo Alto, CA to Austin, TX.

I've leveraged this experience to create a strategic vision for efficient IT support and to develop and deploy an IT management system which provides reliable IT services at an affordable price.

I'm currently President and CEO of Trinsic Solutions, a proactive Managed IT Systems Provider in Austin TX.
