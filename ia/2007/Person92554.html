Zahid H Javali’s Summary

I have been a journalist for the last 12 years and done just about everything under the publication sun. From working in a weekly tabloid to a monthly magazine to a fortnightly tourist guide to a quarterly investment journal to a daily newspaper, it's been one good ride.

After being the City Editor of MiD DAY, a daily tabloid in Bangalore, I now freelance as editor/writer/photographer. I have just completed one travel book and finished editing the other. I regularly contribute to a couple of American portals on digital photography and computers.

I am a regular movie columnist for the in-flight magazine of Paramount Airways besides being on the advisory board of a newly-launched magazine on the South Indian film industry. I also contribute technology/gadgets pieces to Mint, the business newspaper of The Wall Street Journal and The Hindustan Times. That apart, I am in the throes of writing two film scripts and bringing out my book of short stories.

If you have some interesting projects for me, feel free to mail me at zahidjavali[at]gmail[dot]com.
