Ann Greene’s Summary

DO YOU EVER FEEL THAT YOUR TEAM IS NOT AS EFFECTIVE AS YOU WOULD LIKE IT TO BE?

Maybe members of the team don't combine their collective strengths, there is conflict between them, or maybe the team has no clear purpose. Maybe you sometimes wonder whether you even really need to be a team, or whether you are just a group of people doing similar work...meaning that you spend more time working out problems than you do getting things done.

At Allagi we specialise in building high performing teams.

The teams we work with vary in size and requirements, and include:

- small start-up teams aiming to define purpose, vision and values
- project teams who want a kick-off meeting
- teams experiencing conflict issues
- functional leadership teams wishing to improve their collective performance
- teams who want to get to know one another better through some fun teambuilding exercises and games
- individual leaders who want some coaching in defining their team structure and roles, or simply getting the best from their teams.

If you are a leader who wants to transform the performance of your organisation and you are committed to undertaking a journey of development with us, please get in touch.

ABOUT ME

I'm an author, speaker and team development specialist. I teach senior leadership teams my 8-step model to building and maintaining high performing teams - achieving more, more easily.

My blog (www.allagi.ie/blog) was a finalist in the 'Best Business Blog' category of the 2007 Irish Blog Awards. I also produce a monthly newsletter full of articles and tips, which you can sign up to on www.allagi.ie with no obligation.

Each week I co-host a podcast with Krishna De of Oneocean, where we discuss issues around leadership, teams and branding, amongst others . You can download it from www.todayswomeninbusiness.com or you can subscribe on iTunes.

You can find out more about me at www.allagi.ie
