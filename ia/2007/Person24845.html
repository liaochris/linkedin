David Portabella Clotet’s Summary

Currently: Phd
--------------
I am doing a PhD in Artificial Intelligence at EPFL-LIA under the supervision of Dr. Martin Rajman on the topic of Electronic Catalogs.


Start-up
---------------
After the PhD which I will finish in December 2006, we plan to start a company on the field of public open databases. Our aim is to promote the use of affordable open databases to end-users and small businesses. By databases we refer to the content (list of books, movies, restaurants, jobs...) rather than the software.

Employment opportunities: Project-based contracts on the following domains:
* Databases
* Machine learning
* Website implementation and design
* Generic projects using C/C++, Java and python

If you are interested in joining our team, please send your Resume and Motivation Letter to: david.portabella@gmail.com


The field of Electronic Catalogs
--------------------------------
How to design more useful (web) catalog systems
(for instance, how to help a user to find his/her best second-hand car in a catalog of one million cars)

Questions:
-Which type of analysis (e.g. trade-off analysis, prospective analysis) is more useful to the user?
-Does it help the user to feel more confident about his final selection?
-What is the impact of the different distributed cognition elements here?
