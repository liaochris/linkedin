Philippe RECHSTEINER’s Summary

I am a business manager with an extensive experience in wireless and software start-ups.
I began my carrier in 1995 by joining the Paribas' trading teams. After having started on derivatives (options and futurs), I had the opportunity in 1997 to extend my skills on European and US stock markets. Rapidly I specialized myself on techno stocks.
In 2000, I seized the opportunity of joining the private equity investment holding Remote Reward SAS. I am still working there as the Investment Manager.
This new professional experience allowed me improving not only my abilities in Finance and Legal matters, but also acquiring strong management skills for directing multifunctional teams.
I can assimilate complex information and I process quickly for delivering a strategic logical and integrated solution by identifying organizational opportunities, and developing more efficient alignment, processes and execution.
These several years' experience gave me the capacity to lead a small to medium business over a complete development cycle.
