Bill Deen’s Summary

Bill Deen, Solution Delivery Consultant/Senior Technical Recruiter of Elite IT Services has a strong background in Information Technology Products, Services and Solutions. His current role with Elite IT Services is to solve problems, deliver solutions and find talented/passionate people to support our clients consulting and staffing initiatives. Bill’s strong technical background combined with his unique ability to manage relationships has made him extremely successful in his current role. Bill provides a personal touch to recruiting and works hard to build relationships with potential candidates, always looking out for their best interests.

Bill also remains current on new technologies and changes in the IT Industry. He has a unique background and level of expertise in Storage Solutions, High Performance Computing, Wintel Architecture and Technical Account Management. Bill’s technical expertise is attributed to his prior role at Compaq Computers, where he worked with many large clients to support major infrastructure and application enhancements.

Bill started his IT career as a Programmer/Analyst at an Insurance company. He advanced his career rapidly and consistently moved into roles with more responsibility. Bill began his IT vendor career with Honeywell, working on state government accounts. He then moved on to Tandem Computers and then to Compaq in 1999. Bill left Compaq in 2002 to join Elite IT Services and was instrumental in the inception of Elite, which has grown into a Premier provider of project staffing and consulting services.
