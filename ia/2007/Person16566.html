Christopher (Chris) Kennedy’s Summary

As a media strategist, consultant and market researcher, I distill market and customer information into company opportunity and profit while creating a product that satisfies both those who consume the product (the customer, such as radio listeners) as well as those who create it (the company and its employees). Often, I serve those needing guidance due to extreme or near-impossible competitive situations and simply need valid answers, direction and leadership. I work in the radio, TV/cable, new media (satellite radio), marketing/advertising and select retail (beer/banking/audio-video-electronics) industries...while also spotting emerging trends in the media.

Management; Vision; People and presentation/Speaking skills; Closing sales and serving client needs, exceeding expectations within budget; Bringing strategic ideas to successful completion; Influencing others to move forward together as a cohesive team; Consulting/Advising; Advanced market research/Segmentation analysis and presentation; Launching new radio formats; Talent assessment and training; Protector of proprietary information; Ability to distill large quantities of information quickly.
