Steven Bennett-Day’s Summary

Responsible for the creative services of Green Cathedral in his role as Creative Director. Day to day responsibility of managing a creative team that consists of and has board responsibility for the development of the creative and technical services offered by Green Cathedral. Part of the key management team that achieved 13th place in the NMA Top 100 awards for Green Cathedral. Prior to his Creative Director role, Steven was Head of Design for Green Cathedral and in this position was instrumental in changing the company from a technical to a marketing and creative proposition.

Whilst at Green Cathedral Steven has worked extensively with The Eden Project, The Open University, The FIA, Cadbury, Wonderbra, Playtex, PC World, Which?, Daihatsu and The Chartered Society of Designers of which he is a member.

Steven has 16 years experience in conceptual design and advertising spanning the creative departments of many agencies and consultancies. Steven has experience in working across many different forms of media and specialised in digital in 1999.

Prior to joining Green Cathedral he was a founder member of a respected creative digital media studio. Where he developed a client list that included Marks and Spencer, Amerada Hess, The Juice Company, Teddy Smith, Full Circle, Anglia Railways and Granada Media.
