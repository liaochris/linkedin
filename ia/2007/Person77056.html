Shannon Bullard’s Summary

A little about me: I graduated magna cum laude with my B.F.A. in Writing, Literature, and Publishing from Emerson College in 2005. Since I graduated, I've been working full-time in the North End, networking all around Boston and actively socializing on the web. I'm familiar (and in some cases very, very active) with influential & trend-creating websites such as TripAdvisor, Yelp, Going, You Tube, De.licio.us, Flickr, Livejournal, Facebook, Myspace, Consumating, Digg, Stumbleupon, and Linked In. I recently attended the SMX Social Media conference in New York City where I continued my education on how to further "harness the power of consumer engagement."

My Marketing Analyst position at Smart Destinations, a web-based travel and tourism company, has provided me with valuable insight into the world of Online Marketing. In this role, I am not only responsible with supporting the Communications Director with all Search Engine Optimization, Advertising, & Affiliate Marketing efforts but, I'm also responsible for leveraging several Social Media sites daily by creating and maintaining online relationships with potential (and current) customers via blogs, forums, and online communities.
