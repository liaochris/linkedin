Marc Wright’s Summary

Currently founder and principal at Martinez & Wright Homeownership Partners, Marc is developing privately financed solutions for the problem of declining affordability of housing for essential workers (e.g., teachers, law enforcement) and for middle-income workers generally. These solutions include innovative ownership structures and mortgage programs made possible by advanced IT and original analytical methods. Marc has advised policymakers, elected officials and lenders on middle-income housing affordability and has been an invited speaker for hosts including Fannie Mae and the Milken Institute.

Previously, Marc helped build two business intelligence companies. Marketing Arsenal, which he founded in 1994, developed techniques for precision marketing, supply chain intelligence and forecasting for a multi-industry, Fortune 500 clientele. That caught the attention of decision support software company MicroStrategy (MSTR), which recruited him in 1997 to help build the business solutions consulting practice ahead of their successful IPO the following year.

Marc gained his finance and real estate expertise with Atlanta-area developers McDonald Development Company and the Myrick Company where he was involved in development of nearly 2 million square feet of commercial and industrial space for large corporate tenants and buyers. He handled development financing, acquisitions and sales, entitlements, and developed market research and analysis operations.
