Chris Blask’s Summary

Chris is a seasoned security technology professional with more than 15 years of experience in engineering and marketing information technologies. He has been involved in almost every aspect of the infosec business at several successful startups and Cisco Systems and is currently on faculty at the Institute for Applied Network Security, writing and acting as an advisor to information security startups.

In 2005 Chris founded Lofty Perch Inc. - today a recognized world leader in Critical Infrastructure Cyber Security. Lofty Perch provides leadership, expertise and training to public and private entities to help secure infrastructure via the process control systems that run them.

A member of the four-person founding team behind Protego Networks (acquired by Cisco Systems), Chris filled VP Marketing, Sales and Business Development roles.

While at BorderWare II as VP Marketing and BD, he successfully realigned the existing mail security software as the MXtreme Mail Firewall appliance and established partnerships with Cisco and Sun as well as arranging OEMs with anti-virus and anti-spam vendors and chairing the JAMSpam anti-spam coalition.

During his time with Cisco Systems as Firewall Product Line Manager, Chris led the PIX Firewall team from a planned end-of-life to a multi-billion dollar position of global leadership, started the security partners ecosystem program and was influential in shaping the corporate security strategy.

Chris conceived the BorderWare Firewall Server - one of the first commercial firewall products - and provided product management, channel development and marketing to the effort. BorderWare was acquired by Secure Computing for ~$200M in 1996.

Chris Blask’s Specialties:

Market analysis, business development, public speaking, team leadership, information security technologies and applications.
