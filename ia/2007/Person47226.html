Ken Kaplan’s Summary

Today I blend mainstream media skills with new and social media approaches to help Intel participate better with many different audiences. I'm an experienced writer and producer of creative, informative, trend-related stories for broadcast and online audio/video audiences.

Over 16 years of public relations work, including eight years at the NBC affiliate in a top five U.S. TV market.

More than seven years at Intel Corporation now managing integrated broadcast and new/social media strategies for sharing technology leadership stories, new products trends and uses, and corporate and co-marketing activities.

Work directly with national and international TV/Radio professionals, news producers and reporters, online media producers and bloggers.

Initiated strategic podcasting efforts for the corporate and North America PR teams in 2005. In 2007, helped create guidelines and course to encourage employees to participate with social media.

My media training and production skills help top executives and marketing professionals to become on-air experts who are passionate, engaging storytellers.
