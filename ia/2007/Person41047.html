Joshua S. Freeman’s Summary

While I started out my career as an orchestra conductor I have been on the Internet since 1987 when I arrived at CMU for graduate school. I made the transition from music to IT just as the web was taking off and have happily acquired a very respectable amount of technical, project management and strategic management skills in all areas of Information Technology.

I am deeply committed to using Open Source solutions wherever it makes sense, even for 'high-stakes' enterprise solutions when possible.

I have supervised Oracle Financials upgrades, managed the creation, implementation and operation of multi-million dollar e-commerce companies with complex warehouse logistics operations, designed data/cabling infrastructure for new scientific research facilities and overseen the conversion of legacy DB3 and DB4 databases to web-based LAMP solutions for libraries.

I plan to get an Exec. MBA within the next few years and eventually land a big-time CIO job in the private sector, government or higher education.
