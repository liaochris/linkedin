Rodrigo Sepúlveda Schulz’s Summary

Rodrigo Sepúlveda Schulz, 35, is the co-founder and CEO of vpod.tv, an innovative Video Publishing On Demand service, aiming to simplify the technical barriers to working with video online and to enable consumers and corporations to create their personal online and mobile TV channels.

He has just raised a $5.1m series A from Innovacom, the investment arm affiliated with FT Group Orange. Recent awards for his company include being named among the 12 top innovators at Supernova 2006 in San Francisco, a global Innovator at Innovate!Europe ’06 in Zaragoza, earning the 2nd place in the best B2C mobile application at the Global Mobile Monday Summit in Helsinki, and winning the Best Demo award at Capital-IT in Paris.
He is also a sought-after speaker at media events such as the recent DigitalHollywood conference in San José, or the upcoming Monaco Media Forum.

Previously, he was the co-founder and managing partner of RISC Partners, a seed-stage investment boutique, where he was instrumental in the successful launch of glowria.fr, a leading online DVD rental and VOD service, serving as a board member for two years and later as interim CEO where he helped raise the first $12m in financing. In 2004, he also led the mobile strategy team for AOL Europe, redefining the group’s online and mobile presence. Previously, Rodrigo was a principal with Gemini Consulting, and a manager (founder of the e-business practice) at KPMG Consulting France, leading large scale European e-commerce and media projects. He started his career as a consultant to the European operations of Intel and Novell.

His tongue-in-cheek video blog http://sepulveda.net entered the top global 500 blogs list on Feedster in November 2005. He can be reached at rodrigo@vpod.tv.
