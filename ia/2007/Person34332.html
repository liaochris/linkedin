Harvey Meharry’s Summary

I am very well rounded in my aviation experience. I am currently the Program Manager for Executive AirShare in Texas and President of Peregrine Aircraft Management, LLC.

In the past, I worked as a Chief Pilot/General Manager of an FBO in Walla Walla, Washington, Airport Manager and FBO owner in The Dalles, Oregon, Captain in single pilot operations, and Chief Pilot/Flight Department Manager managing corporate flight departments.

I am also very experienced in having flown many types of aircraft. I have over 14,300 hours flight experience and hold several type ratings. They are as follows: the historic Beech Starship, Beechjet, Diamondjet, King Air 300/350, Raytheon Premier I and the Learjet 40/45. I am currently an ATP rated pilot holding a second class flight physical.

Harvey Meharry’s Specialties:

Aircraft acquisitions, aircraft management, aircraft maintenance oversight, consulting current and future aircraft owners in aircraft selection.
