My First Modem was a 300bps brick, that i picked up, whilst throwing the 8 inch floppy disks down the street ;)

Since these early days: I've focused on becoming a competent business manager, developer and technologist.

This experience has also provided me a great deal of opportunity for learning faculties assocated to the development, commercialisation and sustainability of product commercialisation and business operations.
