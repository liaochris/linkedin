David Drake’s Summary

Drake Life is a diversified lifestyle company that specializes in providing a wide range of real estate, entertainment, travel, and security services. Services include individual and corporate apartment rentals worldwide, corporate relocation, real estate brokerage, real estate investment trust, real estate marketing and communications, home and business security, corporate and residential cleaning, and entertainment and luxury travel services worldwide.

David Drake, founder and president of Drake Life, has been involved in the NY metropolitan real estate market for over 10 years. He developed the numerous aspects of the business to compliment the existing services provided and to provide a level of quality and service he saw lacking in other areas. Today Drake Life services over 10,000 happy clients a year through its numerous companies.

The future for Drake Life is bright and sees expansion into yet new areas including real estate development and social and business networking.

Over the last few years, David has transformed Drake Life into a conglomerate that has over 15 divisions housed in two corporate office locations on Park Avenue and Fifth Avenue, a third opening up on Madison Avenue 2006 and a satellite office in Florida. The divisions of Drake Life entail Finance, Entertainment, Technology, Service and Real Estate Industries.

We are currently developers of a hotel next to Setai in South Beach. Brokeraging energy deals of M100 and D2 machine oil.
