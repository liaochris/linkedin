Julie Collins’s Summary

We are a performance Improvement company that specializes in personalized project management and business analysis training, since 1959.

Just call or walk into Systemation and you’ll feel a difference. In an era where thick tension pervades many organizations, we promote a culture where joy and humor are as important as hard work, integrity and delivering beyond clients’ expectations.

We view clients, employees and suppliers as partners—helping each other succeed, pushing quality into the details, constructively challenging mediocrity and continually focusing on new possibilities.

Our ideal Clients are organizations that are interested in increasing revenue and decreasing expenses by improving the quality of projects.

Some of our current clients include: Washington Mutual, JP Morgan Chase, Verizon Wireless, National Instruments, Whirlpool Corporation, WellPoint Health Networks, the State of Oregon, ... and many other Fortune 1000 companies.

I am always happy to discuss training and consulting needs, whether it be for an individual that would like to become PMP certified, a small group of PMPs that need PDUs or an entire organization that would like a corporate assessment so that they may create career development plans for a variety of teams. My goal is to become a resource for PM and BA training needs.

Please feel free to contact me directly to discuss PM or BA, I will always do my best to point people in the right direction, offer referrals or share knowledge. Networking is a wonderful way to build win - win relationships and create success.

One of our most popular programs is the Rapid PMP Certification program. We offer this both on-site, for groups, and as Public Workshops, for individuals, to attend.

Additional Certificate Programs include the Practitioner Certificate in PM, Advanced Certificate in PM and Analysis Certificate. These programs are available on-site and may be tailored to fit the specific needs of our clients.
