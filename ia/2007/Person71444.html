Ramesh Dewangan’s Summary

Ramesh has 20+ years of experience in the EDA industry in the areas of Engineering, Application Engineering and Product marketing related to RTL to GDSII design automation.

Currently at Atrenta, Ramesh manages a product line related to timing products at Atrenta Inc as Product Marketing Director. Prior to this, he has held key positions at Cadence Design Systems, Tera Systems, Synopsys and Texas Instruments.

His objective is to utilize proven EDA product management skills as part of Product Marketing, Application Engineering or Higher Management of an EDA company, especially in a multi-site environment.

Ramesh Dewangan’s Specialties:

IC Design Automation, Semiconductor Design, Product Management, International Operations Management, People management, Customer relationship management
