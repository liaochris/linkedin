Bruce Weiner’s Summary

Bruce Weiner is Managing Director of LogicSourcing, a subsidiary of Novantas focused on bringing the Novantas tools people and approaches to other service industries. Its particular specialty is in understanding the evolving structures of service industries and helping clients map their partnership/alliance strategies within it. During his career, spanning 20 years, Bruce has been a consulting partner, a business executive and a start-up entrepreneur. Before re-joining Novantas Bruce served as managing director, Strategic Sourcing for United Airlines and as CIO of United.com. Bruce’s work has been published in the American Banker and Citibank Today Magazine. He has been a featured speaker at global industry conferences and forums such as TravEx, EDS Leadership, Executives Club of Chicago, Bank Marketing Association, Direct Marketing Day NY, Institute of International Research and BAI.
