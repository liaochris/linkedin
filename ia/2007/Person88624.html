Wahyd Vannoni’s Summary

Responsible for Corporate Communications, Media Training and Investor Relations
I) Assist in presentation preparation
1. External presentations: media interaction, sales pitches, public speaking, industry conferences, etc.

Goals: To create clear and memorable presentations, make the presenter appear in-control and save preparation time.

How?
• Highlight fundamental aspects of human communication
• Analyze the communications requirements of different audiences in different settings
• Understand the effects of stress during important presentations
• Develop main themes of the presentation
• Organize these themes and control the pace of ideas for maximum impact to make them memorable to the audience
• Create Visual-Aids to support the presentation
• Teach how to use Visual-Aids in a manner that enhances the speaker’s personality
• Understand the threats and opportunities of a Q&A session
• Practice delivery
• Practice Q&A

2. Internal presentations: board meetings, company-wide communications, strategy sessions, etc.

Goals: Maximize use of time for internal meetings; develop a framework to ensure participants stick to key themes and prevent repeat meetings on the same subject.

II) Media Training

Goals: To understand specificities of various media (television, radio and print-media). To prepare the firm’s representatives for media interviews to give a positive and memorable impression of the company.

How?
• Highlight the unique qualities of each medium: television, radio and print-media
• Explain how audiences gather information through different media
• Signal potential pitfalls to avoid, examine various interview techniques
• Select themes, ideas and supporting data to communicate
• Teach skills to make the interviewee appear confident and in control
• Teach key interview techniques such as how to insert the firm’s ideas within an interview and how to turn a negative question or story into a more favorable one
