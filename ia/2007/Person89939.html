Vijay Mohan’s Summary

Right after college I joined my family owned home textile manufacturing unit to learn international export, marketing and textile manufacturing. After gaining experience in textiles, I started off working as an information technology consultant, providing customer relationship management (CRM) solutions for fortune 500.

After gaining enough international exposure, process knowledge, work culture from these companies, I decided to go back to my family owned business. Currently, I'm involved in marketing Granites (sculptures, tombstones & building materials) and home textiles. I'm looking after the marketing arm here in New York tri-state area. The merchandise is manufactured in our family owned factories in India and exported to the US.

I'm looking forward to increase the sales of granites and home textiles to the wholesalers, department chains and big retailers in the NY/NJ/PA areas.

I'm also interested in trading HMS steel scrap (ISRI 210/211) mainly importing it to a known steel rolling mill in India
