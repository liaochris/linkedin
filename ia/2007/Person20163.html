Danielle Reed’s Summary

I have been in both inside and outside sales for several years and in a variety of different sales areas. I joined the Online marketing industry 2 years ago as an affiliate manager, and I have worked with both advertisers and publishers finding the most effective ways to increase revenue streams, and build effective marketing campaigns. I love working in this industry and have had the most memorable career experiences that cannot be compared to anything else I have done. What has made me a great Affiliate Manager is the wonderful business relationships I have attained with seasoned professionals, and the trust I have built with my affiliates and publishers. It is because of those relationships that I learned what was needed to be successful in our industry. More importantly, I have learned what my affiliates and partners need to be successful by truly understanding the way they do business, and providing them solutions to their needs. Building trust with your partners and affiliates, is the key in this business, and that trusting relationship is simply priceless. If you were to ask anyone who has worked with me, I know they will find that I am one the most hardworking, trusted Affiliate Managers out there. The Interactive Marketing industry is full of possibilities, and opportunities; it's truly exciting to be a part of it.
