Georges van Hoegaerden’s Summary

Georges leverages his extensive operational experience in sales, marketing, business development and finance to identify and evaluate business opportunities for consumer, media and enterprise software companies.

Georges has more than 20 years of technical and business management experience in the software industry. At The Venture Company, he provides guidance to a variety of Silicon Valley CEO’s enabling their companies to achieve early revenues and profitability. Georges played a pivotal role in the optimization of business and product strategies for Symantec, HP, Ericsson, Oracle as well a variety of early stage companies.

Mr. van Hoegaerden created the early business direction and value that set OuterBay Technologies up for profitability and funding in 2001. Georges incubated the founding executive team, helped raise $11M series A and served as a board member to the company that was sold to HP in 2006.

From 1993-1998, Georges was the Worldwide Director of Product Marketing, Internet and Media products for a $490M product division at Oracle Corporation at its Redwood City, CA headquarters.

Georges van Hoegaerden’s Specialties:

Company executive with more than 20 years of hands-on management, finance, operating, marketing and sales experience in enterprise software and media markets. Board member and Venture Partner.
