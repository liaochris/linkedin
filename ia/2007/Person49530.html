Katherine Willhoite’s Summary

Editorial/production manager with exceptional breadth and depth in dynamic business environments and proven track record in strategy, editorial, design, production, operations, and distribution of print and online media. Leadership and recruiting abilities include embracing new visions and motivating teams to achieve near-impossible goals.

My unusual combination of attributes comprises: (1) sharp editorial skills, from conceptualizing to very detailed editing, and deep professional knowledge of design and editorial production; (2) ability to develop and implement complex processes and to improve and change them continually for maximum efficiency and flexibility; and (3) strong people skills, including the ability to collaborate productively with people in every part of an organization (plus vendors and clients) and an almost flawless record of employee hiring, mentoring, and developing.

Katherine Willhoite’s Specialties:

Editorial operations and management; people/team management; process development and implementation; project management; content, style, and design quality control; writing, editorial development, line editing, copy editing, and proofreading; development and analysis of online surveys
