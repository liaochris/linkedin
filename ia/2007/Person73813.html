Rodney Chua’s Summary

Rodney is a highly capable IT professional with a broad range of work experience in the telecoms industry.

In his six years with LogicaCMG, Rodney’s aptitude and drive resulted in exceptional career progression. He started as a consultant, went on to become regional Operations Director, and after that was entrusted to join the newly-formed Asia CTO Office as Principal Architect.

Exposure to this wide range of roles has given him a balanced view of the business from different key areas such as sales, marketing, people management, operations, and corporate governance.

Rodney has proven client interaction and problem resolution skills, having been frequently committed to assignments that were particularly challenging due to technical complexity or client sensitivities.

Technically, Rodney has a rare combination of expertise in both Internet/IP and traditional telecoms networks. He has consulted with clients and partners to design proposals for a next-generation countrywide signaling infrastructure, as well as optimization and evolution of legacy networks to next-generation SS7-over-IP.

In the telecoms messaging and services domain, Rodney is regarded as a leading technical expert among his co-workers and clients. He has first hand experience consulting with clients and partners, the efforts of which have enabled the deployment of several of the most ambitious pioneer 3G services platforms in Asia.

Rodney also has a keen interest in technology and media. As a student, he helped to conceptualize and produce the first movie in Asia to be shot, edited and screened digitally - the success of which led to him co-founding nuStudios, a digital video production studio based in the National University of Singapore.

Rodney Chua’s Specialties:

Internet / IP networking, LAN, WAN, telecoms networks, messaging platforms, new media related technologies, computer systems
