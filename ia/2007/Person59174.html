Micky Long’s Summary

An award-winning senior marketing strategist and change agent with over 20 years proven results with leading companies in business to business environments. A specialist in helping companies maximize marketing effectiveness by 1) creating the right internal and external team, 2) developing the right positioning to promote brand success and 3) implementing the right strategic approach to generate measurable and sustainable results. Demonstrated results have been achieved with small fast-growth companies (Hayes, Conner)as well as large multi-national organizations (IBM, Siemens, Tech Data, MAPICS, Indus)
