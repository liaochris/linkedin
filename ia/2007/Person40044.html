Jaythan Elam’s Summary

Jaythan is a strong design leader with an adept visual understanding of intricate branding systems. His strong management, communication and leadership skills have led to the completion of countless design projects and conceptual developments. He has expert knowledge of Adobe Design software, Mac OS environments, web development software, and is just as comfortable designing for the web as he is for print. Jaythan has extensive experience spanning marketing and product development platforms – including but not limited to, corporate identity, print advertising, packaging and interactive design. His aesthetics reveal a love for all things typographic, incorporating clean, thought provoking design solutions.
