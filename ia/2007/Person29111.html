Hao Yu Feng’s Summary

He was born in Guangzhou, China in 1976. He met his artistic friends in affiliate High School of Academy of Fine Art and spent half day painting and half day playing. He studied industrial design at Guangzhou Academy of Fine Art, where he met his future wife Xiong Dan.

Between 1999 and 2001 in China, as a designer of Honeycomb product design, he worked with 4 U.S product design firms on several projects for leading Chinese companies such as Midea, Founder and Lenovo. He followed with a master course with his wife together at Academy of Art University and San Francisco State University in U.S.

In 2004, he started as a Speck intern after meeting with Martin Leaugers and Shawn Hanna in IDSA mixer, and then he joined Speck design team with his desire and passion.

He had a wide range of work that included graphic, web and illustration, furniture and consumer product design. His projects have been recognized with numerous awards and presses.
