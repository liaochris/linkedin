Thomas J. Darcy’s Summary

Thomas J. Darcy, President & CEO of Mangold International Inc. ( Atlanta, Georgia ) is currently setting up the US subsidiary of Mangold International GmbH in Atlanta, Georgia. Mangold International based at Arnstorf, Germany is a leading provider for behavior research and usability solutions used by clients in more than 70 countries.

As President and CEO of the newly founded North American subsidiary of Mangold International he is tasked to establish the NAFTA operations for the leading software company in the sector of behavior research and analysis solutions. Develops the indirect sales channel impacting NAFTA business and driving growth and market share. Recruits, qualifies and develops NAFTA resellers and technology partners (ie. sensor and simulation solution providers).

From the beginning of his career, he has been recognized for his leadership and management abilities, outstanding communications skills and skill for establishing operations excellence. Tom Darcy maintains a proven track record selling IT solutions (predominantly software licenses and software based lab solutions) through and with partners (resellers). He is highly experienced in building up indirect sales channels.

Most recently, he completed the successful turnaround of a startup recycling company and raised a round of private equity. He has held numerous leadership positions including COO, CEO and Board of Directors member. As co-founder and partner in various companies in the sector of renewable industry, recycling and business consulting worldwide, Thomas has created an international network of contacts in the software industry, renewable energy, industry and the government / defense sector.

Software, Hardware and Systems Integration in Usability and Human Computer Interface (HCI), Behavior Research, behavior analysis, Military Simulation, Neurosciences, International software marketing and business development,
