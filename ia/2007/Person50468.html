Lara Bersano’s Summary

Communications and Public Relations specialist with experience in the fields of public policy, e-government, international trade agreements, editorial, internet, non-profits and e-learning.

As a Public Policy graduate I understand that there is no isolated action with no direct on indirect consequences in a company's lifecycle.

As a journalist I always get all the "angles" of a business situation, condition, influencers, players and situations.

Our business approach includes working close with the executive teams of our clients and identifying every single piece of important (or irrelevant) information. And particularly:

1.- Identify the company's needs, the members' needs and the community's needs.
2.- Talk with our clients constantly.
3.- Listen to the market, and what it has to tell us about our client.
4.- Consider every single person in a company as a potential spokesperson.
5.- Create a Public Relations plan that includes every valuable asset in the company.
6.- There are no boundaries to business. Include every potential market and every language.
7.- Plan for the short, medium, long and very short term.
8.- Evaluate and reconsider positions.

As Vice President of Grupo Empresarios, I am generally the first person to talk with the client and identify their needs.

Preferred business partners:

1.- Technology o internet-based companies doing business or interested in starting business in the USA or Latin America.
2.- Individuals that want to position themselves as industry leaders in any field, and want to start an international campaign.
3.- Social and political leaders.

In every case, we work only with individuals and companies with a deep sense for the community and social involvement. That is our "must".
