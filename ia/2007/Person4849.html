Abhijit Nadgouda’s Summary

I am a good friend at heart and a good engineer at work. I strongly believe that the only way one can excel is to do what one really likes to do. I do music for hobby and computers for my job. I love computers and software technology, and what it can do to solve problems and improve the world. I am turned on by a systematic approach to any problem and believe that all problems can be solved, to think of it, I believe in "Impossible is Nothing".

Thought the best way to do this was to start something on my own - its name is iface Consulting. You can know more about me and my establishment on my blog (http://iface.wordpress.com).

I have worked extensively on different flavours of C/C++ on Windows, Linux and Solaris. I have tried to make myself independent of tools and technologies by understanding the abstraction of programming. Since then I have worked on Java, Perl, PHP, Python and hope to work on ...

I tried to understand the software business by working in India and US, want to learn more from the customer's point of view.

I have taken to writing recently and all of them are available on my blog (http://iface.wordpress.com/). Some of them are also puslibhed on ManojKhatri.com (http://www.manojkhatri.com/articles/index.php?author_name=anadgouda). Feel free to check them out and comment on them. I am going to try and continue to pen down my understandings in the software world.
