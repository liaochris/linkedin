Terry Johnson’s Summary

Professional Cost Center & Profit Center manager in building product industry including resposibilities as President & COO, Division Manager & Plant Manager. Entrepreneurial experience as Founder, Managing Director, General Partner & Chief of Product/Technology development. Raised capital and organized corporation, limited partnership and limited liability companies. Licensed patents to Keller Industries and later sold / transferred proprietary technology & patents to Jeld-Wen, Inc.

Issued & sold three US Patents based on composite framing technology for building products. Issued one US Patent with three foriegn patents pending based on Phase Change Technology approach to suppression of heat transfer in building product.

Goals include licensing/sale of remaining patents and resume operational managment activities in building product manufacturing field
