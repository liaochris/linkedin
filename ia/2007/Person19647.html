Donna Harris’s Summary

Whether you are buying or selling your home, as your Dallas area REALTOR®, I'll be with you every step of the way. I provide full time services that include listening to your needs and wants, and working with you through your entire buying or selling process. I will do my best to make your experience a memorable one! Dallas Real Estate is changing everyday, so let's figure it out together!

I have been in the Dallas Metroplex for over 22 years. I have a BA in Psychology which helps me everyday with my clients. I started Real Estate in 2000, and I'm a Top Producing Agent in my office. I'm a member of the Metro-Tex Association of REALTORS®, Texas Association of REALTORS®, and the National Association of REALTORS®. Being a member of all these associations makes me a "REALTOR®" which sets me apart from the average licensed real estate agent who does not abide by a set of Ethics that I do.

I just recently completed my ASP, Accredited Staging Professional. Boy was that fun! I use it to help my sellers Stage® their homes better to help them sell faster and for higher dollar. I have also completed the required training to be on the registry for the Comptroller's office for the State of Texas for Arbitration of property tax values. This is a new program for Texas that started in 2005 and it's the step a home owner takes after they are still not happy with what the Appraisal Review Board says about their tax value.
