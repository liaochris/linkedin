Robert M. Cornish’s Summary

Founder of Cornish Equity, LLC as Chief Executive Officer. A private equity firm that seeks investment opportunities in privately owned companies for the purpose of implementing practical systems to improve operations and profitability for long term growth. Our Strategy focuses around buying and holding companies in our portfolio that we feel we can greatly improve value.

Founder and CEO of Cornish + Company, LLC. A M&A Management Operations and Advisory firm. We provide services to companies that are about to sell or are currently for sale to improve the operations and profitability in order to reduce sale time and secure a premium price for the owner.

Managing Director of Anchor Point Capital Group, LLC a private investment fund focused on raising private capital for the use of investment into privately owned companies to attain strong ROI for our investors of the fund based on the companies performance. The companies are managed and run by Cornish Equity, LLC which oversee all operations and expansion activities and focuses on increased profitability.

Owned/Founded Corol Acquisitions, LLC as Chairman/CEO since 2004, A private investment real estate firm located in Phoenix, AZ. Featured in AZ Business Magazine and Arizona Republic newspaper.

Licensed consultant since 2004.
