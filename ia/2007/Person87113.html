Tony Papaleo’s Summary

I facilitate creation of functional and non-functional business requirements by gathering and completing use cases and design documentation.

As your BA, I support the completion of analysis and design, I assist in the planning, development and implementation of the finished projects.

If you are not looking for an honest opinion and a clear take on the problems you are trying to solve, we'll probably go our separate ways.

I think the best BA should:
Conduct business requirement definition and design analysis

Provide functional marketing expertise to developers during the technical design and construction phases of the project

Conduct research and analysis to gather information on business value, consumer experience, conversion rates to lead and grow product in the market.

Keep abreast of new and diverse technologies and how they would apply to product set

Identify and escalate issues/risks impacting project deliverables to the Project Manager

Classify, prioritize and model requirements

Participate in integration testing

Complete functional analysis and documents the functional design and changes required to meet customer business needs in sufficient depth to enable the technical staff to develop design specifications

Coordinate with Technical Leads to insure project meets established business objectives

Facilitate customer meetings to present, define, and gather specific business requirements

Ability and willingness to accept responsibility and the desire and capability of motivating and directing the activities of others including a persistence and willingness to take calculated risk and the ability to stimulate new ideas within the organization
