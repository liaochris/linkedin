Sharona Gottlieb’s Summary

I began my career as a program assistant for the Women Presidents' Organization, a national peer advisory group for entrepreneurs based in NYC. I was promoted from within to the role of Director of Communications. I was responsible for both internal and external membership communications and served as editor of the newsletter, producer of annual directory and initiating new member outreach.

My talents are in attention to detail, seeing the forest for the trees and reaching out to others.

I have since relocated to San Francisco, CA where I am looking to apply my organizational skills to use in the creative sector; design, production, photography, media/web and the like.
