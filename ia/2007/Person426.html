Aaron Davis’s Summary

I have 4 years of experience working in production, planning, and reservoir engineering. I worked Permian Basin Waterfloods and CO2 floods for 2 yrs as both a production and reservoir engineer. Followed by working as a planning/reservoir engineer for Russia, Pakistan, and Yemen for 1.5 yrs. Currently, I am working for a privately owned company as a reservoir engineer for our "Central Region" which includes properties in Utah, Southeast Colorado, Nebraska, Kansas, and Illinois. These properties contain fields that are under waterflood, nitrogen flood and primary production.
