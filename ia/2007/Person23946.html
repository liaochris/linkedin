David Atkinson’s Summary

Dr. David J. Atkinson is presently Deputy Program Executive for Lunar Precursor and Robotic Exploration, NASA Headquarters. In this position, he aids NASA's Associate Administrator for Exploration Systems Mission Directorate in the planning, assessment and guidance of the program. Previously, Atkinson was Program Manager of JPL Exploration Systems Engineering. NASA's Constellation Program includes all of the projects to return to the Moon and future human exploration of Mars. Previously, Dr. Atkinson was Deputy Manager of the Information Technologies and Software Systems Division at NASA’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory,California Institute of Technology. As deputy manager of JPL’s lead organization for computing,software engineering and IT, he was responsible for oversight of the development,delivery and operations of major information systems and software for JPL’s flight projects and Deep Space Network as well as advanced computing and information systems for DoD and other agencies. Previously at JPL, Dr. Atkinson headed the Section responsible for development of key software subsystems of the AMMOS multi-mission system. As a Section Manager, he also oversaw the development of both mission-specific and multi-mission science data systems, such as the Topex SDS and the Planetary Data System. Dr. Atkinson is a member of the IEEE, AAAI, and ACM, and has over 45 publications. He serves as a Member of the Industrial Relations Board for California State University at San LuisObispo (CalPoly) Computer Engineering Program. An entrepreneur, he is also a Director and former CEO of PCNAlert.com. Dr. Atkinson holds a Ph.D in Computer Systems Engineering from Chalmers University of Technology, Gøteborg, Sweden the M.S. and M. Phil degrees in Computer Science fromYale University and the B.A in Psychology from the University of Michigan.
