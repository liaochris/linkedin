A summary...

37 years ago my mother was taking me with her to pick some wild greens in the near by American College of Greece...

After several visits, I probably became 6 years old. It was then that the big idea came in my mind: Many coca-cola empty bottles were hanging free here and there...

I begun to pick them and exchange them for one drachma each at the local mini market...

The security guards of the college were a problem, yet nobody could acuse such a young boy...

I was spending the money buying icecreams to my richer friends...

And as a british used to say to me: '' It's one of them days George...''

Is this enough for a summary? Or should I add some more..bottles ?

The coca-cola affair...Deree College, Athens-Greece, 1972-1977... many bottles, many icecreams there...
