Wim Tanudjaja’s Summary

After High School, I was teaching basic computer programming, Flowcharting, Database management system, Dbase Programming at Budi Mulia Office Training Center from 1986 to 1990. Joining in Team Design for Hospital accounting and financial information system for small and medium Catholic Mission Hospital at several places in Java island and Borneo island in Indonesia.

I moved to Central Asia Insurance, worked as Internal Auditor to audit 12 branches at different area in Indonesia. As internal Auditor I work to make sure every branch has accounting and finance policy and it had applied properly and making sure They have an excellent claim management system. I worked for Central Asia Insurance 1990 to 1995.

When Salim group took over the several food companies (mostly Noodle manufacturing companies) in indonesia and merged them in PT Indofood Sukses Makmur, I moved to Indofood and worked in Central Purchasing, to manage all raw material logistic operations for 12 noodle factories and several units for seasoning, candy and snack factories, from 1995 to 1996 and mid 1996 I concentrated to manage raw material logistic as Central Purchasing manager for 12 Noodle Factories and 2 Flexible Packaging Factories. Dealing with Quality conrol team and product research and development, Solving problem about Logistic problem and issues with production team in each factory, making sure all logistic processing for all factory operation ran smoothly.

September 2000, I moved to Virginia, USA, I managed and Owned Satay Sarinah Indonesia Restaurant under Shetland Green Incorporation from 2001 to 2002.

Currently, I work for Peace Corps as International Travel voucher Processor for Africa Region from Sept 2003 I am reviewing and processing all International Travel claims for Peace Corps staff. From 2005, I am processing Internation Travel Claim from Africa region and some part of Inter America Pasific Region.
