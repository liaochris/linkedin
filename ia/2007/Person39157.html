J. Brian O'Day’s Summary

POLITICAL PARTY EXPERT – National Democratic Institute for International Affairs

Developed and conducted seminars on party building and candidate elections; Wrote The Political Campaign Planning Manual: A Step By Step Guide to Winning Elections which has been translated into Russian and Arabic and used by NDI in various regions around the world; Consulted with political leaders and candidates to strengthen the various democratic political parties and prepare for elections.

Iraq – March 2004 – August 2007 – Worked with political parties and candidates in Baghdad and the Kurdish region in advance of elections. Lead an Iraqi study mission to Canada to observe the 2004 parliamentary elections. Managed the regional office in the Kurdish region of northern Iraq.

Mauritania - November 2003 - Member of the seven person team sent to Mauritania during the 2003 Presidential elections to assess the political and civic situation in the country.

Morocco – January – October 2003 - Worked with Moroccan political parties to develop their message and tested it with Moroccan focus groups; conducted over 40 focus groups for political and civic groups.

Yemen – March - December 2002 - Worked with the ruling party, the Socialists and the Islamists political parties in the seven major cities of Yemen to develop strategy and prepare for the parliamentary elections.

Ukraine – January – February 2002 - Conducted candidate consultations and seminars to assist the democratic parties in advance of the 2002 parliamentary elections.

Azerbaijan - 2001 – 2002 - Worked with activists from the ruling party in Azerbaijan.

Kosovo - September – November 2001 - Worked with Serb political parties in preparation for local elections in Kosovo.

Russia and the countries of the former Soviet Union - 1997 – 2000 - Consulted with political leaders and candidates in Russia, Georgia, Armenia, Kyrgyzstan, and Ukraine.
