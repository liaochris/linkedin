Helder dos Santos’s Summary

Account Director of a Portuguese Telecoms group with interests in both wireless and wireline operations, supplying half of its Wireless Infrastructure of both 2nd and 3rd generations networks, Cell Planning & Optimization services as well as Network Rollout and Integration & Commissioning services. In the convergence space, supplying the Push-to-X server.
Previously worked as Technical Director of a Portuguese emerging landline operator, reporting directly to the CEO and in-charge of the Engineering, the Network Planning & Deployment, the Interconnection Agreements, the SLAs, the Customer premises installations, the Billing Platform and the Operations, for services ranging from voice centric least cost routing to data packet routing as an ISP, ending in the integration and convergence of both services and networks.
Also led the Installation & Commissioning Teams of Philips and Alcatel Large Corporate Communications Solutions.
