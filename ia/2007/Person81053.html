Stephen Pierzchala’s Summary

Being a Canadian who lives in the US, my sanity is questioned everyday. What is not questioned is my dedication to the concepts of Web performance excellence. I work for the leading Web performance measurement firm, and went so far as to design my own experimental test-bed to better understand the complexity of a global measurement system.

I am also beginning a voyage of re-discovery with my artistic nature. I write (like an angst-filled teenager), draw (like a drunken hamster), and take and manipulate digital images. Balance has been lacking, until now.

I am deeply interested in contemporary architecture, beautiful photography, art, reading, and living a life of curiosity and discovery.
