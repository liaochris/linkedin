Adam Phillips’s Summary

Have almost 14 years animation experience. In 1993 began employment with the Walt Disney 2D animation studio in Sydney Australia. 8 years were spent in Special Effects, and 3 of those were as the Effects director.

Since resigning from Disney in Dec 2004 to pursue my own interests, I have worked to build a reputation as a storyteller and animation artist of high quality. With numerous international awards and a large fan base, my work and website continue to gain momentum.

Graphically, I work almost entirely with Flash, but I have extensive 3D experience with Lightwave, Cinema4D, Carrara and Maya in modeling, animation, some character rigging and dynamics.

Currently:
-writing books and articles on Flash motion graphics
-speaking at local digital media conferences and Universities on Flash and animation.
-maintaining biteycastle.com
-developing a Brackenwood feature film treatment
