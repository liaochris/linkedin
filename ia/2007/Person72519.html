Rich Kneece’s Summary

Rich Kneece is CEO of Massachusetts Technology Corporation (see http://www.masstechnology.com). MTC’s happy customers include top organizations such as Harvard University, Partners Healthcare, Boston Scientific, Verisign, Fidelity, Amgen and Pfizer.

He's been featured and/or sought as an expert by outlets such as CNN, The New York Times, The Boston Globe, Pharmaceutical Executive, The Scientist, NurseWeek, Mass High Tech, Boston Business Journal and the San Francisco Business Times, and has made speaking appearances for organizations such as Harvard School of Public Health and Babson College.

Rich has significant experience in business development, technology management, training/e-learning, and internet recruiting, and is a recognized expert in the use of technology, process and people to solve business challenges.

His career began with IBM, and he was a top graduate of Aetna’s leading executive sales training program, The Group School.

Rich is a former competitive bicycle racer and has raced on a national level. He is a member of the American Society for Training & Development and Marquis Who's Who, and is involved in community activities such as Boston Cares, the ASPCA and Junior Achievement.
