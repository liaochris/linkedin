Alan Warms’s Summary

Currently General Manager, Yahoo! News, after selling BuzzTracker.com and associated technologies to Yahoo! in September 2007. BuzzTracker was entirely self-funded. All Participate Media employees are now employed by Yahoo!.

Current angel investments include AdGooRoo and Moving Station. Previous angel investments include Wallstrip (sold to CBS).

In January 2007, successfully exited investment I led in RealClearPolitics (6.5x in one year's time) and stepped down as Publisher as part of transaction. Summary of deal can be found here: http://www.participatemedia.com/2007/03/13/realclearpolitics/

Sold last company, Participate Systems, to Outstart in November 2004 after founding it 1997.
