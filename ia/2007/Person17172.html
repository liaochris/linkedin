Christopher Knight’s Summary

A former software developer and graduate of Simon Fraser University, Chris Knight is currently a LL.B. Candidate at the University of Western Ontario. From June 2007, Chris will be articling at the law firm of Fraser Milner Casgrain LLP in Calgary, Alberta. He is also Managing Director of Acutus Trading Ltd, a provider of licensing services for ISVs and micro-ISVs (http://AcutusTrading.com/).

Chris’s legal interests are focused on the international licensing of intellectual property (IP) assets, especially where relating to small and medium-sized enterprises (SME) and electronic commerce transactions. Negotiating the legal and regulatory minefield of a single jurisdiction can often be troubling enough, but e-commerce exposes businesses to legal risk in dozens, if not hundreds, of jurisdictions worldwide. Chris is especially interested in helping entrepreneurs mitigate this risk.

For approximately a decade, Chris has worked with a number of software startups, both as a software developer and as a project manager. Chris has also successfully marketed a number of shareware software titles for and on behalf of several ISVs. He is an avid world traveller and has gained firsthand experience living and working outside of Canada, primarily in France and England. His professional and personal contacts extend around the globe to Canada, the United Stated, the United Kingdom, England, France, Australia and Germany.

International commerical law, taxation of e-commerce, software license agreements, non-disclosure agreements, software product design, software marketing, online software distribution, shareware software distribution, retail software distribution, international business and commerce,
