Dave Van Dyke’s Summary

Media analyst and corporate executive.

Established and operate two media companies: Radio Mentor, Inc., a management consulting firm for the broadcast industry providing strategic business and personal senior level advisement.

Bridge Ratings LLC was founded in 2002. The company provides media consumer behavioral research to its clients which include Emmis, CBS Radio and Clear Channel Communications and media investment firms around the world.

Clientelligence, Inc., a subsidiary of Bridge Ratings, provide client and customer profile and behavioral analyses to companies seeking to better understand their clientelle and who are eager to increase the ROCV (Return on Customer Visits).

Previously, I successfully established an organizational structure to accommodate 7 divisions and 21 departments to service corporate verticles at a private equity firm in Los Angeles.

Invented new revenue generation paradigms for Viacom, Inc.,which allowed company to achieve expansive sales goals.

Turned around poorly achieving radio property in Los Angeles for CBS Radio. Co-developed new rock format to establish foothold in marketplace and catapulted station from 21st to 4th 25-54 Adults and from $10 Million to $40 million in sales.

Joined ABC Radio Networks as Vice President of Affiliate Relations in October of 2007. Responsible for optimizing the relationship between ABC Radio and its 4400 national affiliates.

Dave Van Dyke’s Specialties:

Total operations expertise, radio programming, marketing, research, sales. Full P&L responsibility. Organizational establishment and process improvements. Turn around expert. Internet sales development. Employee retention. Business development. Branding and Positioning. Media analysis.
