Jon Reed’s Summary

I run a print and digital publishing consultancy that develops market-focused content and offers practical advice.

I use a very wide definition of publishing - anyone who produces original, branded content for print, web or audio is a publisher!

We research, develop, produce, brand and market our clients' content, and help them take advantage of new publishing opportunities offered by the digital age.

In addition to consultancy work for clients, I also develop my own commercial websites.
