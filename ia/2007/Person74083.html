Rolf Wiegand Storgaard’s Summary

Why isn't computer software as easy to use as driving a car?

My primary goal is to make computer software easy to use. To hide the complexity of the software beneath a system and to focus 100% on the task that a used has to solve.

In 2003 I formed the company NorthTech together with two of my friends - Jon Gregor Hemmingsen and Jes Wulfsberg Nielsen. The goal of the company is to form cosy lounge where we can focus on how to make the best software possible.

We currently have a nice office in Nørregade in the middle of Copenhagen and we meet every friday to discuss system architecture among other things of the system we are working on.
