Don MacLeod’s Summary

Effective Marketer specializing in radio, entertainment and small business marketing. Over the past ten years I have become proficient in online marketing including: SEO, PPC, viral marketing, email marketing and more.

I am the Senior Marketing Executive for Greater Media Philadelphia, working with radio stations, WMMR,WMGK,WBEN, WJJZ and Sports Radio 950. I also own Ideas4Rent Marketing - http://www.ideas4rent.com

Prior to my current position(s) I was the Marketing Director for WKTU-FM and Power 105.1 until February 2006. I joined Clear Channel New York in 1997 and I developed internationally known events garnering multi million dollar sales. I am a highly effective manager and promoter, best known for my unique guerrilla marketing techniques during key ratings periods.

I was born in Detroit and worked in the Motor City where I was the Director of Marketing and Promotion at WYCD-FM, “Young Country” and WKQI-FM.until 1997. I graduated from Central Michigan University, and I worked at several radio stations in the Midwest as a sports reporter, on-air personality, talk show host and program director.

I was named the 2004 Marketing/Promotion Director of the year for urban radio. I also have been nominated for Marketing Director of the Year by R&R, Gavin Magazine, Radio Music Awards and many others. I live in Chatham, New Jersey with my wife Marylynn and son Andy.

Goals:
