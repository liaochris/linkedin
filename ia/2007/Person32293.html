Deborah Kayatani at GKnot.net’s Summary

Grassroots Organizing, Community Building

GKnot Foundation is a Private Foundation that began as a Web Resource Innovation Center to provide a Safety Net for Communities in all 50 States in 1996 that inspired MoveOn.org and many United States grassroots organizations.

GKnot Foundation grew from a need to re-connect the Internet Community of Kids and Adults Concerned that created a dis-connected Community. MAcGruff Crime Dog and other Community Watch Dog Organizations weren't enough to fill the void of People going to their next door neighbor and saying hello, finding out if they're okay, providig a community of Well Being and a Social Safety Net.

GKnot Foundation believes and knows for a fact that Earth's Nature is Abundance. A Well Earth, a GKnot Production, will provide guidance to ways Communities can create Self-Sustaining Business Environment that automatically reinforces Well Being.

GKnot Foundation's A Well Earth aims to:
- Build Native Ancestral Rennaisance in every Community;
- Create the forums for Community to speak to Businesses and;
- Teach Leadership and Professional Communitication Skills that Kids need to Communicate Professionally;
- Assist Community Members in creating a Tightly Knit Politically Engaged Community to Prevent Exploitation.
