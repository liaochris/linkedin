John Weathington, PMP’s Summary

For over 15 years, I have been working as an independent consultant to Fortune 500 organizations in High-Tech industries. My experience ranges from designing, building, and managing an enterprise data warehouse, to leading the development of a decision support web application integrating J2EE technologies and Business Objects. Recently, my work has involved managing government related projects for Sun Microsystems, including a SOX Audit effort, and an enterprise-scale data warehouse for GSA maintenance and auditing.

I back my consulting experience with a BS in Computer Science, a Project Management Professional (PMP) Certification, a Six Sigma Black Belt ( from Motorola ), and an Oracle Certified Professional certification.
