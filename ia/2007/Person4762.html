Amy Jackson’s Summary

"Life is a journey not a destination" - I believe in finding some way, however small, to enjoy life and have fun every day!

"Decide to choose. Create your life."

I am an inspirational speaker, coach and trainer - I spend my days using our ever-increasing understanding of how the brain works to help young professionals to achieve their potential.

What do I do?
As a speaker I inspire audiences with my personal stories and understanding of psychology, neuroscience and behaviour change to create their own energy, fun and abundance in their lives. I use fun and highly interactive training with teams and brain-based coaching with individuals to help them become outstanding communicators and leaders.

Amy Jackson’s Specialties:

Speaker:
- Decide to choose. Create your life. Take responsibility for choosing your own behaviour and life every day.
- Use your brain more effectively to achieve more and play more

Coach:
- Use your brain to change the simple things that make a big difference
- Turning 30 - make it work for you

Trainer:
- Communicate and connect - with teams and the bigger picture
- Present with confidence - even when your knees are shaking.
- Psychology of Body Language - let your body speak!
