Imran Mohamed’s Summary

Imran Mohamed, a Manager with Alvarez & Marsal Business Consulting, LLC, specializes in delivering information technology (IT) strategy to corporate clients. Mr. Mohamed has deep expertise is leveraging IT functions to drive operational efficiencies in mission-critical business processes, as well as to facilitate top-line revenue growth through technology-centric opportunities.

Over the past eight years, Mr. Mohamed has led and advised numerous technology engagements across a broad spectrum of industries, including high-speed discrete manufacturing, telecommunications, and transportation and logistics. Most recently, Mr. Mohamed led efforts to implement real-time process monitoring solutions for leading aluminum can and packaging manufacturers.

Prior to joining A&M, Mr. Mohamed founded Second Millennium Consulting, a Chicago-based Internet consulting firm. As Director of Operations, Mr. Mohamed successfully built the firm into a multi-million dollar consultancy, which was subsequently acquired by a publicly traded telecommunications firm.

Mr. Mohamed holds a bachelor's degree in computer science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
