Aaron Cooper’s Summary

With over 10 years of interaction design experience, I help teams create rich, targeted interactive experiences that bolster client brands. Alignment of business needs and users' goals is always at the forefront of my mind. I have experience with a wide range of clients within industries ranging from architecture and product design to music and entertainment. I help clients stand out from the crowd and accurately convey value and uniqueness while collaborating with their customers and partners. Clients and user-customers experience success when direction is guided by savvy determination of business needs and research of users' goals.

// ADDITIONAL DETAILS

- I have a passion for iterative, two-way brand experiences that effectively communicate via a range of channels to engage with and learn from interaction with people.

- I have strong team management skills developed through years of experience working within creative groups to design, develop, deploy, and refine designs that achieve client goals and champion the user experience

- I offer a direct, strategic, effective, efficient communication style conducive to design solutions that enhance client brand experiences via print, interactive experiences, and product design.

- I offer effective business needs assessment skills developed and honed through freelance and full-time experience within a wide range of industries.

- I have a natural and nurtured sense of balance, cleanliness and intuitive information design coupled with a constant desire to encourage learning, growth and knowledge-sharing within the creative environment.
