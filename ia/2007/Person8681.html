Aurélie Batmalle’s Summary

I am usually in charge of B2C/B2B websites management and software development. I am used to dealing with analysts, designers, developers, engineers, architects and different types of clients (IT, Marketing department...)

My last major project was the development of a highly innovative photo product: a flash application allowing customers to upload their pictures online and create photo products such as DVD’s, photo books, etc. When customers buy those products, DVDs and photo books productions are automated through industrial printers or burners (.Net platform.)

My approach to product/project management is based on communication and teamwork. Through friendship, I usually get a personal and true involvement from each of my co-workers which allows’ the delivery of high quality and great products. As a matter of fact, I have studied and worked on projects requiring various web related expertise and that is why I am used to having the team’s members collaboration, either with artistic people (art school, music, Photoshop, Flash, Illustrator knowledge …) or technical people (C.S. : database, programming languages, network knowledge)

The client relationship management I favour relies on my ability to listen and to make technical concepts accessible. Also, my academic background in marketing/business allows me to have a good understanding of the market as well as a relevant product vision. Indeed, I first studied media advertising, then internet communication in general, and finally I specialized in e-business and e-marketing.

I’ve had the chance to experience various development methodologies from waterfall methods for websites, to agile ones for software (scrum). I was involved in the whole product life cycle from staff acquisition, to first product releases. It also implied activities such as specification gathering, design, development, QA, implementation, UAT etc.

I’m also PMP certificied (Project Management Professional) since february 2007.
