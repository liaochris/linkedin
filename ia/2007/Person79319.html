Scott Comber’s Summary

I specialize in the areas of leadership development, conflict resolution, team relationships, and personal change. My current work centers on creating leadership development initiatives aimed at enhancing leaders' capacity to lead.

Working with organizations, I have led initiatives to increase quality of work life, developed networks and structures to support leaders through change, leadership development and enhancement programs for leaders and organizations. I am passionate about my belief in people and their capacity.

Scott Comber’s Specialties:

I have a Ph.D. in Human and Organizational Systems, and am an Associate Faculty member and teacher in both the Master of Leadership and Training and MBA programs at Royal Roads University and I am also a Faculty member of the Canadian Medical Association - Physician Management Institute. I am certified in Coaching, Conflict Resolution and Mediation and Emotional Intelligence. My MBA (Finance) and MA (Human Development) further complement my work.
