Suzan St Maur’s Summary

I write books to help you - and make you laugh. In fact some of my books do both. (Yes, intentionally.)

In addition, my background in business/marketing communications and a range of other nonfiction work means I can help you to create books, speeches, online content, articles and other communications … by writing them for you, editing/refining them, and/or helping you write them yourself.

So, I'm an author, writer/ghostwriter, editor and writing coach.

I'm also a 2 x cancer warrior, with the scars to prove it. If you have a connection with cancer you might like to take a look at my weblog, CancerComicStrip:

http://www.cancercomicstrip.blogspot.com
