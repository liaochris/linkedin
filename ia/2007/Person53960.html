Marcelo Sant'Iago’s Summary

Marcelo Sant’Iago is working with interactive media in Brazil since 1996. He has given lectures about interactive media in Argentina, Brazil, China, Hong Kong, Mexico, USA and Middle East countries. Marcelo was the President of IAB Brasil - Interactive Advertising Bureau between 2002-2006, and now is Chairman of the Advisory Board. He also serves as Board Member at Fédération Internationale des Associations de Multimédia , based in Canada, and as Board Member of the World Summit Award, an United Nations supported initiative based in Salzburg, Austria. Sant’Iago blogs about interactive media and search engine marketing , writes for Caderno de Propaganda e Marketing, Webinsider.com.br and has articles and interviews published in Brazil and abroad.
Currently Marcelo holds the title of Business Development Director at MídiaClick, a performance based marketing agency.
