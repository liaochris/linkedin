Marc Leith’s Summary

Marc is a co-owner and Vice President at Maksim Consulting and currently is under contract with Equitable Life of Canada as an Implementation Business Analyst focused on implementing and upgrading their Quotes & Renewals system.

With principal partners having over 40 years of development, project management and consulting experience, Maksim Consulting is seeking to expand it's market presence into the areas of CRM and Customer Analysis.

Marc has extensive experience analyzing, designing, developing and implementing software solutions as well as 10 years of progressive management experience. In addition, he has over 20 years of technical experience with pc's, unix & mainframes - including network design, installation and support.

Prior to starting Maksim Consulting, Marc was a Product Manager at IN3 Healthsystems (aka Incubed) responsible for the Quotes & Renewals, Medical Underwritng and Security modules of IN3's Benefit Management Suite - a windows- based Group Insurance Administration system. In his 5 years at IN3, he also held positions of Senior Team Leader and Application Archictect and was responsible for designing and architecting major portions of BMS.

Before joining IN3, Marc was Vice President, Software Development at Compusearch/Equifax where he was managed multiple teams of systems personnel and the overall design, development, and testing of in-house, custom
solutions as well as shrink-wrapped and hosted GIS & Business Intelligence solution.

He rose to his position as Vice President following nearly 10 years experience within the Compusearch corporate family primarily at Prospects Unlimited, where Marc spent many years as a Programmer/Analyst, Manager, Technical Services and finally as Director, Internal & Data Development. Marc had a great mentor in Nancy Sprague (then President of Prospect Unlimited) and gained excellent knowledge of Direct Marketing as well as solid management skills.
