Robert Freeborn’s Summary

Rob is currently an Account Manager with ShoreGroup focusing on the CaseSentry and ShorePatrol product lines. ShoreGroup is focused on the development of these solutions for the delivery of next generation support capabilities to the converged networks being deployed today. The CaseSentry and ShorePatrol solutions are delivered as a subscription service, aka SaaS. In difference to many of the competing solutions on the market the CaseSentry and ShorePatrol solutions come bundled as a suite of service - from element management to operational management to service management and finally business level management. In addition, this solution is delivered in a unique fashion in that the ShoreGroup team supports the solution 100% yet the solution is housed within the firewall of the enterprise deploying it.

Prior to becoming an Account Manager with ShoreGroup, Rob worked in a Sales Engineering role supporting the sales team in a variety of verticals including CaseSentry and ShorePatrol as well as IPT and IPCC solutions.

In his life before ShoreGroup Rob worked for Science Applications International Corporation (SAIC). At SAIC Rob worked in the Cisco Center of Excellence where he supported company wide Cisco initiatives ranging from security to VoIP to wireless. In addition to that Rob was the first person at SAIC to organize the entire companies Cisco certified engineers - regardless of line organization and geography. In the building of this virtual organization Rob amassed a team of several hundred engineers in locations across the globe.

Before SAIC Rob ran an internet start-up and had his first job in the technology field when he joined IBM in their Finance Industry Consulting Group fresh out of his MBA at the University of Miami.
