Kate Tulenko, MD, MPH, MPhil’s Summary

Physician and global health industry specialist with broad experience in developed and developing country settings. Currently representing the World Bank in Africa and working with clients to provide technical assistance and financing for projects on health systems efficiency, human resources for health, public-private partnerships, and water and sanitation,etc.

Main current project: working with African countries to help solve the global health worker shortage. Goals include expanding new worker training (especially via the establishment of new private training schools), increasing worker retention in under-served areas, and maximizing worker productivity.

Founder and Medical Director of Living Solutions
Living Solutions is a franchise provider of everything needed to make the home environment more safe, comfortable, and supportive for seniors. Services include home assessements and planning, home modifications (ADA bathrooms, ramps, stairlifts, doorway widening, grab bars, etc), durable medical equipment and assistive devices.
