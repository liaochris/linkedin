Jeanine Tanner "JT" O'Donnell’s Summary

Workplace consultant and career development specialist with 15+ years of experience in coaching, managing and training employees on a wide variety of work-related topics. Author of "Find Your Career Path," a how-to guide for individuals looking to improve their career satisfaction, co-writer of the nationally syndicated career advice column, "J.T. & Dale Talk Jobs," which appears in more than 130 US papers weekly, and career subject-matter expert who has been quoted in the NY Times, Boston Globe and other major publications.
