Cassie Maas’s Summary

http://www.apr.com/cmaas

I have lived in Los Gatos for over twelve years. My time was been divided between downtown Los Gatos when I lived on Massol Ave and raised my daughter, Kim and the Santa Cruz Mountains where I have lived off the grid for the past eight years raising my young son, Chris. I am a graduate of Leadership Los Gatos, a member of the Mountain Art Guild Board, and Director of the Board for Only Kindness, a unique and inclusive daycare provider. I am also the originator of the www.hiway17.com community portal that has been in existence since 1996 and has been serving the Los Gatos and mountain community ever since.

I have lived in the Santa Clara County since the age of nine and have watched it transform itself from acres of fruit trees to the Silicon Valley it is today. I have seen home prices go from the low to mid twenties (as in $24,000.00 for a 4/2 in Santa Clara) to the impossibly high reaching low one hundred thousands (as in $120,000.00 for a beautiful 4/2 with a large lot, pool, hot tub, mountain views, state of the art kitchen, etc) to the market as we know it today. And every step along the way, someone said, that home prices could not possibly go any higher. And yet they climbed still higher.

Home ownership does not have to be a dream or something that only others can achieve. You can own a home and enjoy the same benefits that others enjoy, including the satisfaction of making it your own by transforming the grounds, painting the walls, remodeling, and taking advantage of the tax benefits of home ownership. Let me help you achieve the dream. And if you already own your own home, Ican assist you in finding your next home or your next investment.

Take the first step. Call me and let me help you find the house you will want to call home!
