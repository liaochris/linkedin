Doug Vanisky’s Summary

I specialize in creating interactive content and web strategies aimed at global consumer audiences. My writing background includes fiction, marketing collateral, business communications, editorials, artist interviews, content strategy, advertising campaigns, and editorial management.

Additionally, I am a California Licensed Acupuncturist and operate my own private practice. My experience is shaped by internships with Robert Levine, L.Ac. and the California Pacific Medical Center. I've also collaborated with leaders from the UCSF School of Medicine to create educational programs that promote integrative medicine in medical schools.

I offer unique medical and writing insights. My client experience includes Goodie Goodie Productions, Trouvé Media, Inc., Seagate Technology, iMedia Reports LLC, Pharmaceutical Care Network, The California Pharmacists Association, ACTCM, LifeScan, Kyphon, Ethicon-Endo Surgery, and the Diabetes Care Coalition.

Doug Vanisky’s Specialties:

Creative writing strategies. Web concepts. Interactive content development. Brand naming and verbal identity solutions. Integrative Medicine. Pulse diagnosis.
