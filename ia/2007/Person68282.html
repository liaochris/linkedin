Phil Parr’s Summary

about twentyfive

twentyfive is a Branding agency. We help organisations to increase their profitability by helping them to understand the true value of strategic branding and its internal and external applications.

After more than twenty years trading, twentyfive has established itself as a highly respected agency. Our strong focus on brand, and all that it entails, enables us to offer a fully integrated solution to our clients.

This deep understanding of what brand means to an organisation helps us to deliver highly relevant, top-down solutions that, when implemented, build sustained trust, market share and profitability.

In short, we will help you to leverage all of your brand assets to create both strategic and competetive advantage.

about me

After 20 years in the Financial services sector working in IT software development for large corporates, I took voluntary redundancy in 2001 to undertake some charity work which I did for 6 months.

Through Networking, I was introduced to ABA Partners (the old name for twentyfive) and came on board to help build and develop their fledgeling Web Development team.

Last year, I was promoted to the board as Director with responsibility for Business development. Our first task was to take stock of where we were as a company, looking at our heritage, core skills, values and aspirations. The result was a complete re-brand!

9 months on, twentyfive has established itself as an authority in the branding arena and our unique ability to bridge the gap between brand strategy and brand implementation sets us apart as an agency.

I have been happily married for 23 years to Dawn and have two wonderful daughters, Rachael who was married last year to Owen, and Rebekah who is busy studying Psychology at Swansea - so I now enjoy the benefits of a quiet house (When I see it!).
